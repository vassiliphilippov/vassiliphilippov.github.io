melodion

noun
keyboard wind instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈloʊdiən / (AmE), / məˈləʊdiən / (BrE)
a small keyboard wind instrument, similar to a melodica, that produces sound by blowing air through reeds

Examples

A melodion instrument
He played a cheerful tune on his melodion during the music class.
The musician carried a melodion to the park for an impromptu performance.
Children enjoyed learning simple songs on the melodion in their first music lessons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accordion - a portable musical instrument with keys and bellows
  • synonym: melodica - a small keyboard wind instrument played by blowing air through a mouthpiece
