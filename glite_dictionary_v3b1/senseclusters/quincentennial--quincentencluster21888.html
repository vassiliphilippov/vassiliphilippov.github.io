quincentennial

noun
500th anniversary or its celebration
Pronunciation: / kwɪnˌsɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / kwɪnˌsɛnˈtɛnɪəl / (BrE)
The 500th anniversary of an event or the celebration of such an anniversary.

Examples

The quincentennial of the city
Many books were published to celebrate the quincentennial of the university's founding.
Scholars gathered to commemorate the quincentennial of the explorer's voyage.
The city hosted a grand parade in honor of its quincentennial this year.
