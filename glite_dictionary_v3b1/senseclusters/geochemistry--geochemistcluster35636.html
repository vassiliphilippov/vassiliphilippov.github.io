geochemistry

noun
the chemistry of the Earth's crust
5.2 6.0
Pronunciation: /dʒiː.oʊ.ˈkem.ɪ.striː/ (AmE), /dʒiː.əʊ.ˈkem.ɪ.striː/ (BrE)
