Rhizobium

noun
genus of nitrogen-fixing bacteria in plant roots
9.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / raɪˈzoʊbiəm / (AmE), / raɪˈzəʊbɪəm /; / rɪˈzəʊbiəm / (BrE)
a genus of bacteria that live in the root nodules of leguminous plants and are able to fix nitrogen from the air
