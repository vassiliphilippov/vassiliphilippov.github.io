Arnica cordifolia

noun
North American wildflower with heart-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈniːkə kɔːr.dɪˈfoʊ.li.ə / (AmE), / ɑːˈniːkə kɔː.dɪˈfəʊ.li.ə / (BrE)
A perennial wildflower of alpine areas in western North America, with heart-shaped leaves and broad yellow flower heads.
