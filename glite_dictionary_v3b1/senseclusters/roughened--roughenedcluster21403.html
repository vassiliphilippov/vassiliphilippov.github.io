roughened

adjective
made rough due to exposure
1.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌf.ənd/ (AmE), /ˈrʌf.ənd/ (BrE)
(of skin or a surface) made rough, often as a result of exposure to cold, wind, or other environmental factors.

Examples

Roughened skin
After days in the mountains, her hands became roughened and dry.
Repeated washing left his knuckles painfully roughened and red.
The wind had roughened his cheeks over the winter.

Related Terms
