temporomandibular joint

noun
jaw joint connecting mandible and skull
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛmpəroʊˌmændɪˈbjʊlər dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˌtɛmpərəʊˌmændɪˈbjuːlə dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the joint connecting the lower jawbone (mandible) to the temporal bone of the skull, enabling movement of the jaw
