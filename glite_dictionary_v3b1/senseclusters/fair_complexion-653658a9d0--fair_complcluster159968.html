fair complexion

noun
light or pale skin tone
2.8
Pronunciation: / fɛr kəmˈplɛkʃən / (AmE), / feə kəmˈplɛkʃən / (BrE)
a light or pale skin tone, especially as regarded in contrast to darker skin tones

Examples

A fair complexion
He wore a hat to protect his fair complexion from sunburn.
People with a fair complexion are often more sensitive to sunlight.
She inherited her mother's fair complexion and delicate features.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dark complexion - a comparatively darker skin tone
  • synonym: pale - lacking color in the skin
