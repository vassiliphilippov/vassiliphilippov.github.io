torque

verb
to apply twisting force
6.7 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / t…îrk / (AmE), / t…îÀêk / (BrE)
to apply a twisting force to something, especially to tighten or turn it

Examples

Synonyms

  • twist
    1.7 20/m
    twist means to turn or wind something with a rotating motion, while torque is the twisting force used to turn or tighten.
  • tighten
    1.5 14/m
    tighten means to make something more firm by reducing looseness, while torque is the twisting force used to cause that tightening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loosen - to make something less tight
  • synonym: twist - to turn something in a circular motion
