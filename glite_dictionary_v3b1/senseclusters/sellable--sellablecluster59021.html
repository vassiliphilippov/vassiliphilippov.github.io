sellable

adjective
fit to be offered for sale
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛləbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛləbəl / (BrE)
Able or suitable to be offered for sale; marketable.

Examples

A sellable product
Fresh produce must be undamaged and clean to be sellable at the farmers' market.
Some of the artwork was not considered sellable due to its poor condition.
The company is working hard to make the product more sellable in overseas markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marketable - able or fit to be sold or marketed
  • antonym: unsellable - not fit to be sold
