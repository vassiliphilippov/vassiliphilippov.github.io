talkee

noun
old word for talk
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːlki / (AmE), / ˈtɔːlki / (BrE)
an old-fashioned or dialectal word meaning speech or conversation

Examples

Evening talkee
In the old story, the villagers gathered for a friendly talkee by the fire.
The character's use of talkee made the dialogue sound quaint and old-fashioned.
He preferred a quiet evening to a long talkee with strangers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: talk - the act of speaking or having a conversation
