photocoagulator

noun
surgical laser instrument for photocoagulation
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊkoʊˈæɡjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊkəʊˈæɡjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a surgical instrument that uses a laser to perform photocoagulation, typically used in medical treatments such as repairing retinal tears

Examples

A photocoagulator device
Advancements in photocoagulator technology have improved outcomes for diabetic retinopathy surgery.
The ophthalmologist used a photocoagulator to treat the patient's retinal tear.
Training is required to operate a photocoagulator safely and effectively during eye procedures.
