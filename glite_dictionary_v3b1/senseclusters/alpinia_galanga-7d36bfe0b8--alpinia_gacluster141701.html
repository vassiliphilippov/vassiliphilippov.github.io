Alpinia galanga

noun
tropical plant; aromatic root used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ælˈpɪniə ɡəˈlæŋɡə / (AmE), / ælˈpɪniə ɡəˈlæŋɡə / (BrE)
A tropical Southeast Asian plant of the ginger family, with aromatic rhizomes used in cooking and medicine, also known as greater galangal.

Examples
