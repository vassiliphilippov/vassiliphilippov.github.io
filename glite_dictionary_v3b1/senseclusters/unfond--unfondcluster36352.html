unfond

adjective
without affection
8.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɑnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɒnd / (BrE)
not having affection or liking for something or someone

Examples

Be unfond of food
She was unfond of spicy food and always chose milder dishes.
Many people are unfond of early morning meetings at work.
He remained unfond of social gatherings, preferring to spend time alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dislike - to not like something or someone
  • antonym: fond - having affection or liking for something or someone
