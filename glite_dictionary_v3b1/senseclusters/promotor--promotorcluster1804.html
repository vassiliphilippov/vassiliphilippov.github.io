promotor

noun
organizer or encourager
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / prəˈməʊtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that encourages or helps something to happen or develop

Examples

Promotor of change
The promotor of the concert secured several popular bands to perform.
A good promotor can make a small event attract a large audience.
She acted as the main promotor of the new community project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opponent - a person who opposes or resists something
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or coordinates an event or activity
  • synonym: promoter - a person or thing that promotes something
