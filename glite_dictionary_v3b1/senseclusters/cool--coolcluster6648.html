cool

noun
calmness under pressure
Pronunciation: / kul / (AmE), / kuːl / (BrE)
calmness and self-control, especially in difficult or stressful situations

Examples

Keep your cool
Despite the chaos, Maria managed to keep her cool and solve the problem.
He maintained his cool, even when everyone else was panicking.
If you lose your cool during negotiations, you might give away your position.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composure - calmness and self-control
  • antonym: panic - sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety
