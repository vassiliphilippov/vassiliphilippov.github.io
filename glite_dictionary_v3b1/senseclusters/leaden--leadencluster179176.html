leaden

adjective
dull or lacking liveliness
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛd.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɛd.ən / (BrE)
dull, lacking lightness or liveliness; oppressively heavy in mood or character.

Examples

Leaden mood
Her leaden response to the news disappointed her friends.
The meeting had a leaden atmosphere that made everyone feel tired.
They struggled through a leaden conversation, unable to find any excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement; boring
  • synonym: heavy - weighing a lot; having great weight
  • antonym: lively - full of life and energy; spirited
