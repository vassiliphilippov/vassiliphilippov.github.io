Sputnik

proper noun
Soviet satellites
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌtnɪk / (AmE), / ˈspʊtnɪk / (BrE)
the name of a series of Soviet artificial satellites launched into space, beginning with the first in 1957

Examples

Sputnik satellite series
The launch of Sputnik in 1957 shocked the world and started the space race.
Many students learned about Sputnik when studying the history of space exploration.
The success of Sputnik inspired new interest in science and technology around the globe.
