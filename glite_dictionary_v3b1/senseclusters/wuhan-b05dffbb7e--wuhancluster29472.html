Wuhan

proper noun
city in central China
Pronunciation: / wuˈhɑn / (AmE), / wuˈhæn / (BrE)
A major city in central China, located on the Yangtze River, known as the region's commercial and industrial center.

Examples

Wuhan city
Many international flights to China stop in Wuhan before continuing to other destinations.
The Yangtze and Han rivers meet in Wuhan, contributing to the city's strategic importance.
The outbreak of COVID-19 in 2019 brought Wuhan to the attention of the global community.
