machine

noun
efficient, organized group or system
9.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
an organized group or system that works efficiently to achieve its goals, especially in business or politics

Examples

Political machine
The company's marketing machine rolled out the new product across the country.
The military machine mobilized quickly in response to the crisis.
The political machine controlled every aspect of city government.
Candidates who go against the established machine often struggle to win primaries.
She had strong support from the party machine during the election campaign.
The old political machine lost much of its influence after the reforms.
