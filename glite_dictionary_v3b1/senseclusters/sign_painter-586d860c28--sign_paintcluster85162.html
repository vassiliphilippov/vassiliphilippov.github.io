sign painter

noun
person who paints signs and billboards
2.3
Pronunciation: / saɪn ˈpeɪntər / (AmE), / saɪn ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to paint signs and billboards by hand

Examples

A sign painter at work
A sign painter spent hours carefully lettering the store's new window display.
Before digital printing, every shop relied on a sign painter for their advertisements.
The old theater hired a skilled sign painter to create a vintage marquee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signwriter - person who writes or paints signs, especially in Britain
