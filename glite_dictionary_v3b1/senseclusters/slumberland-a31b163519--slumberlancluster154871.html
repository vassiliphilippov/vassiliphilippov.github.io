Slumberland

noun
imaginary world of sleep
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌmbɚˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈslʌmbəlænd / (BrE)
a fanciful or playful name for the state or world of sleep and dreams

Examples

Fall into Slumberland
After a long day at school, the children quickly entered Slumberland as soon as their heads hit the pillow.
She wished him goodnight and told him to enjoy his adventures in Slumberland.
The storybook described a magical journey through Slumberland filled with fantastic creatures and dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamland - an imaginary place where one goes while dreaming
  • synonym: sleep - the natural state of rest in which you are unconscious
