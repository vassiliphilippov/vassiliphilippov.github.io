newcomer

noun
recent arrival; new member
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnuˌkʌmər /; / ˈnuˌkʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːˌkʌmə / (BrE)
a person who has recently arrived in a place, joined a group, or started an activity

Examples

A newcomer to the team
As a newcomer to the company, Maria quickly adapted to her team.
It's never easy being a newcomer in a close-knit community.
The city offers special programs to help newcomers adjust to life here.
As a newcomer to the city, she was eager to make new friends.
At first, the newcomer felt nervous joining the weekly book club.
The company organizes an orientation session for every newcomer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beginner - a person new to a subject or activity
  • antonym: veteran - a person with long experience in a place or activity
