Cynara

noun
genus of plants including the artichoke
Pronunciation: / saɪˈnɛrə /; / ˈsaɪnərə / (AmE), / sɪˈnɑːrə /; / ˈsɪnərə / (BrE)
a genus of thistle-like plants in the family Asteraceae, including the artichoke and cardoon
