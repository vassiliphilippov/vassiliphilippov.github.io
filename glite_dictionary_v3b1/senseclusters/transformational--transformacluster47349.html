transformational

adjective
causing major change
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænsfərˈmeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌtrænsfɔːˈmeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
causing or resulting in a major change or improvement in someone or something

Examples

Transformational change
The new technology had a transformational effect on the way people communicate.
Her transformational leadership style inspired the entire team to achieve more.
Attending the workshop was a transformational experience for many participants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revolutionary - causing or relating to a complete or dramatic change
  • antonym: static - not changing or lacking movement or development
