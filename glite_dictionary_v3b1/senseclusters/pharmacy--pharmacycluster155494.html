pharmacy

noun
science of preparing and dispensing medicines
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːrməsi / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməsi / (BrE)
the science and practice of preparing, dispensing, and reviewing drugs and medicines

Examples

Pharmacy degree
After studying pharmacy, she began working at a large hospital.
He chose to pursue a career in pharmacy because he wanted to help people.
Modern pharmacy requires knowledge of chemistry and biology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharmaceutics - the discipline of pharmacy that deals with the process of turning a new chemical entity into a medication
  • synonym: pharmacology - the branch of medicine concerned with the uses, effects, and modes of action of drugs
