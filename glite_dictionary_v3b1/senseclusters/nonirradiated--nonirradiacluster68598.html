nonirradiated

adjective
not exposed to radiation
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd / (BrE)
not exposed to radiation, especially for purposes such as preservation or sterilization

Examples

Nonirradiated herbs
Many consumers choose nonirradiated herbs to avoid any possible changes in flavor.
The laboratory compared the effects of nonirradiated and irradiated samples on bacterial growth.
Some health food stores only stock nonirradiated spices for their customers.
