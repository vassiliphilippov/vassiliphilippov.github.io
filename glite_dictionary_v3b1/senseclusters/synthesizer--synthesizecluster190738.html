synthesizer

noun
electronic musical instrument
8.6 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪn.θə.saɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈsɪn.θə.saɪ.zə/ (BrE)
An electronic musical instrument, usually played with a keyboard, that generates and modifies sounds electronically and can imitate a variety of other instruments.

Examples

Modern synthesizers imitate instruments
Modern synthesizers can accurately imitate pianos, strings, and many other traditional instruments.
She composed all the music on her synthesizer for the electronic album.
The band added a synthesizer to create a richer sound during live performances.
He played a catchy melody on the synthesiser during the concert.
Many pop songs from the 1980s feature the synthesiser as a prominent instrument.
With her new synthesiser, she explored creating unique electronic sounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keyboard - a musical instrument played using a keyboard, such as a piano or organ
  • degree lower: piano - a large keyboard musical instrument
