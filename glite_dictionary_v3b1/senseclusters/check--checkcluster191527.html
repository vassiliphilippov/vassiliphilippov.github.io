check

noun
chess: threat to the king
5.7
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
(in chess) a condition in which a player's king is under direct attack and must be protected on the next move.

Examples

King in check
After his queen moved, she put my king in check.
He announced check with a swift knight move.
You must defend against check or you will lose the game.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: checkmate - a move in chess that directly ends the game by attacking the king with no escape
