gearshift

noun
device for changing gears in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪrˌʃɪft / (AmE), / ˈɡɪəʃɪft / (BrE)
a mechanical device in a vehicle used to engage and disengage different gears

Examples

A gearshift lever
A broken gearshift can make it impossible to change gears smoothly.
Please make sure the gearshift is in neutral before starting the engine.
She quickly moved the gearshift into second as the car began to climb the hill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gear lever - British term for a device that changes gears in a vehicle
