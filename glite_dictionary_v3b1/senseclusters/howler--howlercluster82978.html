howler

noun
loud cry or shout
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊlər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊlə / (BrE)
a loud, strong cry or shout, especially one made by an animal or person

Examples

A loud howler
A sudden howler echoed through the quiet forest, startling the campers.
The baby let out a howler when she was hungry in the middle of the night.
We heard a howler from the crowd as the team scored the winning goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scream - a loud, high-pitched cry
  • antonym: whisper - a very quiet way of speaking
  • synonym: yell - a loud, sharp cry or shout
