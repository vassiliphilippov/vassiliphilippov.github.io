turnaround

noun
sudden improvement
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnəraʊnd / (BrE)
a sudden or significant change that leads to improvement, especially in performance or results

Examples

Business turnaround
The company achieved a remarkable turnaround after hiring a new CEO.
After a slow start, the team's turnaround surprised everyone in the league.
The project saw a complete turnaround once new strategies were implemented.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - a gradual and continuous loss of strength or quality
  • synonym: recovery - a return to a better state or condition
