ungarnished

adjective
without decoration
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɑrnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɑːnɪʃt / (BrE)

Examples

He preferred his salad ungarnished, with no dressing or toppings.
