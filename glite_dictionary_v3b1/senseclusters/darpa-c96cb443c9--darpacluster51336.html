DARPA

proper noun
US agency for defense technology research
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑr.pə / (AmE), / ˈdɑː.pə / (BrE)
an agency of the US Department of Defense responsible for research and development of new technologies, especially in defense and surveillance

Examples

DARPA research projects
DARPA has funded many breakthrough technologies, including the early foundations of the Internet.
Researchers submitted their proposal to DARPA in hopes of advancing artificial intelligence.
Since its establishment, DARPA has played a critical role in US defense innovation.
