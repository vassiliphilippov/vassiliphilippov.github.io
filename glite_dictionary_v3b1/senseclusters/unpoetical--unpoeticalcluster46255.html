unpoetical

adjective
not poetic
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpoʊˈɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnpəʊˈɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
lacking poetic qualities, beauty, or imagination

Examples

An unpoetical explanation
His unpoetical explanation of the sunset focused only on the science behind the colors.
The novel's unpoetical language made the story feel flat and uninspired.
She found the technical manual too unpoetical to enjoy reading for pleasure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poetic - having qualities of poetry; beautiful or imaginative
  • synonym: prosaic - lacking poetic beauty; ordinary or dull
