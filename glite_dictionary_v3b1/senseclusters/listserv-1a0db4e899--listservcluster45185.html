LISTSERV

noun
email group service
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪstˌsɜrv / (AmE), / ˈlɪstˌsɜːv / (BrE)
a system or service that manages and sends email messages to a group of people (an electronic mailing list)

Examples

A LISTSERV system
I subscribed to a LISTSERV to receive updates about the university events.
The club uses a LISTSERV to send announcements to all its members.
Many professional groups communicate through a LISTSERV to share information and resources.
I joined the university LISTSERV to stay informed about upcoming events and announcements.
The club uses a LISTSERV to send important updates to all its members at once.
If you want to receive notifications, sign up for the department's LISTSERV online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mailing list - a list of email addresses used to send messages to multiple people
  • synonym: newsletter - a regular email sent to subscribers with news or updates
