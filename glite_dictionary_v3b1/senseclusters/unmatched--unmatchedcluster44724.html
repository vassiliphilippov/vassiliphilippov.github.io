unmatched

adjective
without equal or rival; unparalleled
1.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmætʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmætʃt / (BrE)
so good or impressive that no one or nothing else is equal to it; without an equal or rival; unparalleled

Examples

Unmatched talent
Her unmatched dedication to the project ensured its success.
That athlete's speed and skill are simply unmatched in the world of sports.
The chef is renowned for an unmatched sense of flavor pairing.
