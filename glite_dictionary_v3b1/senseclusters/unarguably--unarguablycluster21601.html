unarguably

adverb
undeniably, without dispute
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑrɡjuəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːɡjuəbli / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be disputed or reasonably argued; undeniably.

Examples

Is unarguably true
His contribution to the team’s success is unarguably significant.
It is unarguably true that climate change poses a serious threat to our planet.
The novel is unarguably one of the most influential works of the 20th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indisputably - impossible to question or dispute
  • synonym: undeniably - impossible to deny; clearly true
