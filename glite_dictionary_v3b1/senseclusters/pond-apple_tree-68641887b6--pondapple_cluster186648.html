pond-apple tree

noun
tropical American evergreen tree with edible fruit
6.0
Pronunciation: / pɑnd ˈæpəl tri / (AmE), / pɒnd ˈæpl triː / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree of tropical America, valued for its edible fruit and primarily used as grafting stock.

Examples

The pond-apple tree fruit
Because of its tolerance to wet conditions, the pond-apple tree is common in marshy areas.
The local farmers often use the pond-apple tree as rootstock for other fruit trees.
We spotted a pond-apple tree by the river, its fruit hanging just above the water.
