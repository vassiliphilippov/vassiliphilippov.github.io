nano

x
prefix for one billionth
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnænəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning one billionth (10^-9), or describing something as extremely small, especially at the nanometer scale

Examples

Nano prefix
A nanometer is one billionth of a meter, used to measure very small things.
Scientists use the nano prefix to describe extremely tiny particles and measurements.
The medicine was delivered in nanograms to ensure precise dosing.
Scientists are developing nano materials for use in electronics.
The new drug uses nano particles to deliver medicine directly to cells.
Advances in nano technology are changing the way we design computers.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: mini - smaller than usual or normal
  • degree lower: pico - prefix meaning one trillionth (10^-12)
