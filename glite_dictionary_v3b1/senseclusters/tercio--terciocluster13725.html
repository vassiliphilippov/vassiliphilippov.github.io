tercio

noun
bullfight stage
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrsiˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɛːsɪəʊ / (BrE)
one of the three traditional stages in a Spanish bullfight, each with a specific purpose and activities

Examples

First tercio of bullfight
The first tercio of the bullfight is known as the tercio de varas.
During the second tercio, the matador observes the bull's behavior closely.
Each tercio in a bullfight has its own traditions and rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullfight - a traditional Spanish event involving a matador and a bull
