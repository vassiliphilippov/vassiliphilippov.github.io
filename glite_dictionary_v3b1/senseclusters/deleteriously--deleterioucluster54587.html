deleteriously

adverb
harmfully
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛlɪˈtɪriəsli / (AmE), / ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəsli / (BrE)
in a way that causes harm or damage

Examples

To deleteriously affect
The new policy could deleteriously affect small businesses in the area.
Using too much fertilizer may deleteriously impact the local water supply.
Some medications interact deleteriously with certain foods or drinks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beneficially - in a way that produces good or helpful results
  • synonym: harmfully - in a way that causes harm
