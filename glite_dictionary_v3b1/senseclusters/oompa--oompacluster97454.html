oompa

noun
lively musical rhythm
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈuːmpɑː / (AmE), / ˈuːmpɑː / (BrE)
a lively, rhythmic sound or style often used in cheerful or upbeat music, especially in brass bands or folk tunes

Examples

Lively oompa music
The band played a cheerful oompa that got everyone clapping along.
You can hear the classic oompa rhythm in many traditional German folk songs.
The parade featured musicians performing an energetic oompa on their brass instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: march - a piece of music with a strong, regular rhythm
  • synonym: polka - a lively dance and musical style with a strong rhythm
