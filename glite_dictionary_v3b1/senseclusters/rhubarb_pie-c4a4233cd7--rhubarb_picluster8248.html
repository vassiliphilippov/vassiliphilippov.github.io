rhubarb pie

noun
pie with rhubarb filling
Pronunciation: / ˈruˌbɑrb paɪ / (AmE), / ˈruːbɑːb paɪ / (BrE)
A pie filled with diced rhubarb and a substantial amount of sugar, often served as a dessert.

Examples

A rhubarb pie dessert
I had never tried rhubarb pie before visiting England last year.
My grandmother bakes the most delicious rhubarb pie every summer.
They served warm rhubarb pie with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for dessert.
