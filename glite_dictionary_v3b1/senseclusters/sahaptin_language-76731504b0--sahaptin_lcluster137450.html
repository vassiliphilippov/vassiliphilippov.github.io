Sahaptin language

noun
a Native American language of the Pacific Northwest
Pronunciation: / səˈhæptɪn ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / səˈhæptɪn ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Native American language of the Penutian family spoken by the Sahaptin peoples of the Pacific Northwest.
