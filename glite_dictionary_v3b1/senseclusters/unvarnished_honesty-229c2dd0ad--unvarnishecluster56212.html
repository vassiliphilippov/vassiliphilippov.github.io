unvarnished honesty

noun
direct, blunt truthfulness
7.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɑrnɪʃt ˈɑnəsti / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɑːnɪʃt ˈɒnəsti / (BrE)
the quality of speaking or expressing the truth in a direct, blunt, and unembellished manner

Examples

Unvarnished honesty in speech
Her unvarnished honesty can be refreshing, but sometimes it catches people off guard.
Instead of sugarcoating the news, she delivered it with unvarnished honesty.
The journalist was admired for his unvarnished honesty in reporting difficult truths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candor - the quality of being open and honest; frankness
  • antonym: diplomacy - the art of managing negotiations and people tactfully; often involving less blunt honesty
  • synonym: frankness - the quality of being open, honest, and direct
