pusher

noun
small wheeled baby carriage
6.7 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃər / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃə / (BrE)
a small vehicle with four wheels in which a baby or small child is pushed around, especially by an adult

Examples
