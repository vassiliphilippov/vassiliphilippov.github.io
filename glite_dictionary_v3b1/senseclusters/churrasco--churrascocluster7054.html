churrasco

noun
South American barbecue
Pronunciation: / tʃuˈrɑskoʊ / (AmE), / tʃʊəˈrɑːskəʊ / (BrE)
a social gathering or meal where grilled meat, especially beef, is served, popular in South American countries

Examples

A churrasco party
We invited our friends to a churrasco in the backyard last Saturday.
The smell of grilled meat filled the air during the family churrasco.
At the beach, they organized a traditional churrasco with music and dancing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecue - an outdoor meal where food is cooked over a fire or grill
  • synonym: picnic - an outdoor meal, usually informal and social
