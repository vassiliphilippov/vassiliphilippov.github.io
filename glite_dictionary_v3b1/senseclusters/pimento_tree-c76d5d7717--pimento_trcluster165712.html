pimento tree

noun
aromatic West Indian tree producing allspice berries
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪˈmɛn.toʊ tri / (AmE), / pɪˈmɛn.təʊ triː / (BrE)
an aromatic tree native to the West Indies that produces allspice berries used as a spice

Examples

Locals in Jamaica harvest the berries of the pimento tree every summer for export.
The pimento tree thrives in warm, humid climates and is well known for its culinary value.
