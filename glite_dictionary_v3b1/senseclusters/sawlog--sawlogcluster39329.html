sawlog

noun
log for lumber
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌlɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌlɒɡ / (BrE)
a log of suitable size and quality to be sawn into lumber

Examples

A large sawlog
The lumberjack selected the largest sawlog to cut into planks at the mill.
After the storm, several fallen trees were turned into valuable sawlogs for construction.
Each sawlog must meet strict standards before it can be processed into lumber.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firewood - wood used as fuel for burning
  • synonym: timber - wood prepared for use in building and carpentry
