sublimed

adjective
having passed directly from solid to gas or vice versa
Pronunciation: / səˈblaɪmd / (AmE), / səˈblaɪmd / (BrE)
(of a substance) having passed directly from the solid to the gaseous state, or vice versa, without becoming liquid.

Examples

Sublimed ice
In certain conditions, snow may be sublimed without ever turning into liquid water.
Iodine crystals can be sublimed in the laboratory without melting.
The sublimed material collects on the cool surface above the heat source.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sublime - to cause a substance to pass from solid to gas without becoming liquid
