preoccupied with oneself and one's own interests or needs, often disregarding or ignoring the needs and feelings of others.

Examples

Self-centered attitude
His self-centered attitude made it difficult for him to maintain close friendships.
Many people view celebrities as self-centered, but some are surprisingly humble.
She was so self-centered that she rarely considered anyone else's feelings before making decisions.
His self-centred attitude made collaboration with others very difficult.

She was accused of being self-centred for ignoring her friend's problems.

Synonyms

    self-absorbed focuses on being lost in your own thoughts and feelings, while self-centered means paying little attention to others.
  • self-involved
    7.0 0.39/m
    self-involved takes up all your interest in your own life and plans, while self-centered ignores others' needs.
  • egocentric
    7.4 0.92/m
    egocentric sees the world only from your own view and ignores others, while self-centered means a strong personal focus.
  • egoistic
    7.4 0.47/m
    egoistic acts for what helps you most and dislikes sharing, while self-centered means being mainly focused on yourself.
  • egoistical
    7.6 0.02/m
    egoistical puts personal gain first in thinking and choices, while self-centered means being chiefly occupied with yourself.
  • narcissistic
    7.8 3.36/m
    narcissistic loves admiration and sees oneself as superior, while self-centered is simply focused on personal needs and interests.
  • self-serving
