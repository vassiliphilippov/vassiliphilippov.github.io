blouse

verb
to puff out loosely
8.1
Pronunciation: / blaʊs / (AmE), / blaʊz / (BrE)
to puff out or hang loosely, especially of clothing gathered at the waist or tucked in

Examples

Shirt blouses out
Her shirt bloused over the waistband, giving her outfit a relaxed look.
The wind caught his jacket and it bloused out behind him as he walked.
After tucking in the sweater, it naturally bloused above her belt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: billow - to swell or puff out in a flowing shape
  • antonym: cling - to fit closely to the body
