malposed tooth

noun
tooth grown in abnormal position
Pronunciation: / ˌmælˌpoʊzd ˈtuθ / (AmE), / ˌmælˌpəʊzd ˈtuːθ / (BrE)
A tooth that has grown or erupted in an abnormal or faulty position within the mouth.

Examples

A malposed tooth
A malposed tooth can make it difficult to clean neighboring teeth thoroughly.
Orthodontic treatment is often required to correct a malposed tooth.
The dentist explained that his malposed tooth could cause problems with his bite.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misaligned tooth - a tooth that is not in its correct position
