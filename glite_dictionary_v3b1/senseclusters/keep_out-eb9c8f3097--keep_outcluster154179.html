keep out

verb
remain outside; not enter
1.0 1.49/m
Pronunciation: / kip a ät / (AmE), / kiÀêp a ät / (BrE)
to remain outside a particular place or not enter it

Examples

To keep out of the yard

Visitors are required to keep out of restricted areas at all times.
