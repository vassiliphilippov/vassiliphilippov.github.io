inoperable

adjective
not suitable for surgery
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɑːpərəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈɒpərəbəl / (BrE)
(of a medical condition or tumor) not suitable for surgical operation.

Examples

Inoperable tumor
After several tests, they concluded that her heart condition was inoperable.
The doctors determined that the tumor was inoperable due to its location near vital organs.
Unfortunately, the cancer was discovered at an inoperable stage.
