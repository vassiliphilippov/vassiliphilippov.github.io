small

adjective
young; not fully grown (children, animals)
Pronunciation: / smɔl / (AmE), / smɔːl / (BrE)
(Of children and animals) young; not fully grown or mature.

Examples

Small children
Farmers protect the small chicks from predators until they grow.
Many small puppies need extra care in their first few weeks.
The kindergarten is only for small children.
