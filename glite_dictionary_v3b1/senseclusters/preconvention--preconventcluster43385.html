preconvention

adjective
before a convention
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkənˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːkənˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
happening or existing before a convention, especially referring to activities or conditions leading up to a main event or meeting

Examples

Preconvention planning session
The committee held several preconvention meetings to finalize the agenda.
Many important decisions are made during preconvention discussions among the delegates.
She was busy with preconvention planning in the weeks leading up to the event.
