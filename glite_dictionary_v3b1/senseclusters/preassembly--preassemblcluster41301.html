preassembly

noun
prefabricated unit
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːəˈsɛmbli / (AmE), / ˌpriːəˈsɛmbli / (BrE)
a set of parts that have been put together in advance to form a unit ready for installation or further assembly

Examples

A preassembly unit
The factory shipped the preassembly to the construction site for quick installation.
Using a preassembly can save time and reduce errors during the final build.
Each preassembly is carefully inspected before being sent to the customer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: component - a part or element of a larger whole
  • synonym: subassembly - a unit assembled separately to be incorporated into a larger product
