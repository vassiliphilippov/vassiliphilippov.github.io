interim

noun
period between two events or phases
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.tə.rɪm / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tər.ɪm / (BrE)
the period of time between one event, process, or period and another; an intervening interval

Examples

In the interim
Many students took part-time jobs in the interim before finding permanent employment.
Renovations will start in June, but in the interim the library will remain open.
The committee appointed an acting manager to lead the company during the interim.
