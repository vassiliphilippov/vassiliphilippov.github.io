microarchitecture

noun
processor internal design
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃər / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌɑːkɪˈtɛktʃə / (BrE)
the detailed internal design and organization of a computer processor or system, including how its components interact and execute instructions
