snout

verb
to inform to authorities
Pronunciation: / snaʊt / (AmE), / snaʊt / (BrE)
to inform on someone to the police or other authority, especially by betraying them with information.

Examples

To snout to police
He snouted his colleagues to the police before the union hearing began.
The gang was caught after one member snouted to the authorities.
Nobody trusted him after he snouted about their secret meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betray - to give information about someone that harms them
  • synonym: inform - to give information to authorities about someone's wrongdoing
  • antonym: protect - to keep someone safe from harm or danger
