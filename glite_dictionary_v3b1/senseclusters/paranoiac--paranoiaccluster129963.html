paranoiac

noun
person afflicted with paranoia
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈnoʊiæk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈnɔɪæk / (BrE)
a person who suffers from paranoia, experiencing persistent delusions of persecution or suspicion

Examples

A paranoiac person
She became increasingly isolated and behaved like a paranoiac, suspecting everyone around her.
Stories about government conspiracies can sometimes feed the obsessions of a paranoiac.
The doctor explained that a paranoiac might perceive threats where none exist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paranoid - person with irrational distrust or suspicion
  • degree higher: psychotic - person with a severe mental disorder involving loss of contact with reality
