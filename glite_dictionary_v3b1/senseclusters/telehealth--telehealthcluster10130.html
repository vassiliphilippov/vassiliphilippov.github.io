telehealth

adjective
remote healthcare
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛliˌhɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈtɛliˌhɛlθ / (BrE)
relating to healthcare services or activities delivered remotely using telecommunications technology.

Examples

Telehealth services
Many hospitals now offer telehealth consultations to reach patients in rural areas.
The new telehealth platform allows doctors to monitor patients' conditions from afar.
Some insurance plans now cover telehealth services as part of their benefits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-person - done with physical presence
  • synonym: remote - carried out from a distance
