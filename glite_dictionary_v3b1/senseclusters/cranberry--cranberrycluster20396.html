cranberry

noun
a shrub producing small, tart red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈkrænˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈkrænˌbəri / (BrE)
a small, evergreen shrub of the genus Vaccinium that produces small, tart, red berries commonly used in cooking and juice-making

Examples

Cranberry bush
Commercial growers often flood fields to collect cranberries during the harvest season.
The cranberry thrives in acidic, boggy soil and is harvested in the fall.
You can plant a cranberry bush in your garden if you have the right conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueberry - shrub of the genus Vaccinium bearing blue edible berries
  • synonym: lingonberry - shrub of the genus Vaccinium bearing small red edible berries
