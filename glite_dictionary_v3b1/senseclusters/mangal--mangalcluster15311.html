mangal

noun
charcoal grill
8.7
Pronunciation: / mɑnˈɡɑl / (AmE), / mænˈɡæl / (BrE)
a portable grill or metal brazier used for cooking food over charcoal, especially in Turkish and Middle Eastern cuisine

Examples

Turkish mangal grill
They gathered around the mangal to enjoy freshly grilled kebabs and vegetables.
A traditional Turkish mangal is perfect for outdoor family meals in the summer.
He bought a new mangal for their weekend barbecue in the park.
