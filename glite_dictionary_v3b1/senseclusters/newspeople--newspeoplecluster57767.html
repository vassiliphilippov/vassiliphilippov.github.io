newspeople

noun
journalists and reporters
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌpipəl / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpiːpəl / (BrE)
people who work in gathering, reporting, or presenting news for newspapers, television, radio, or online media

Examples

Newspeople report stories
Newspeople gathered outside the courthouse to cover the trial.
Many newspeople work long hours to deliver breaking stories to the public.
The conference room was filled with newspeople eager to ask questions at the press event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: audience - people who watch, read, or listen to news
  • synonym: journalists - people who write or report news for media
  • synonym: reporters - people who gather and report news stories
