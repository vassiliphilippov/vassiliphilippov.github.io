light

adverb
carrying very little
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
carrying very little; with few burdens, especially when traveling.

Examples

To travel light
For weekend trips, I always try to light so I don't have to check any bags.
Hiking through the mountains is much easier if you pack light.
She prefers to travel light, bringing only the essentials.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heavily - carrying a lot; with many burdens
