nutso

adjective
informal, crazy
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnʌtsəʊ / (BrE)
informal, pejorative: mentally unstable or behaving in a wildly irrational or crazy way

Examples

A nutso idea
Everyone thought he was a bit nutso after he painted his entire house bright orange.
She went completely nutso when she heard the shocking news.
Calling someone nutso can be hurtful and is considered offensive in many situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy - mentally deranged or behaving in a wild way
  • synonym: insane - seriously mentally ill or extremely foolish
  • antonym: sane - having a healthy mind; not mentally ill
