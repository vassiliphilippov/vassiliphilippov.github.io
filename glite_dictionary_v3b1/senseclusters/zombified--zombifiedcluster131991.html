zombified

adjective
infected by malware
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈzɑːm.bɪ.faɪd/ /; / /ˈzɑːm.biː.faɪd/ (AmE), /ˈzɒm.bɪ.faɪd/ /; / /ˈzɒm.biː.faɪd/ (BrE)
infected or taken over by malware so that a device or system can be controlled remotely, often as part of a botnet

Examples

Zombified computer
The company's network was zombified after hackers installed malware on several computers.
A zombified device can be used to send spam emails without the owner's knowledge.
Security experts warn that zombified systems are often part of large-scale cyberattacks.
