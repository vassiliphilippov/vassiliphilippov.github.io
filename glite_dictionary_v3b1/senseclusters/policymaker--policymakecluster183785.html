policymaker

noun
person making rules
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑləsiˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɒləsiˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is responsible for creating important rules, laws, or policies, especially in government or organizations

Examples

A policymaker in government
The policymaker introduced a new bill to improve public transportation in the city.
Many policymakers are working together to address climate change at the international summit.
As a policymaker, she must consider the needs of all citizens before making decisions.
A policymaker must consider the needs of all citizens when drafting new laws.
The conference brought together policymakers from around the world to discuss climate change.
As a policymaker, she worked to improve education standards in her country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - a person responsible for managing an organization or system
  • synonym: legislator - a person who makes or enacts laws
