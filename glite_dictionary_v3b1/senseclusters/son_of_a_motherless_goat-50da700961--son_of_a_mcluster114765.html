son of a motherless goat

noun
insult for a contemptible or foolish person
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌn əv ə ˈmʌðərləs ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn əv ə ˈmʌðələs ɡəʊt / (BrE)
A humorous or mocking insult for an unpleasant, contemptible, or foolish person.

Examples

A son of a motherless goat insult
During the game, one fan shouted, "You son of a motherless goat!" at the referee.

When the deal fell through, she muttered, "That son of a motherless goat tricked me!"
