prod

verb
urge or prompt to take action
3.6 0.9/m
Pronunciation: / prÉ‘d / (AmE), / prÉ’d / (BrE)
to encourage or urge someone to take action, often by reminding them or applying gentle pressure

Examples

To prod someone to act
He needed to be prodded into finishing his homework before dinner.
Her friends tried to prod her into applying for the new job.
Sometimes a gentle prod is all it takes to get someone moving.

Video Examples

Synonyms

  • nudge
    4.1 2/m
    nudge gently prompts or reminds someone to act by soft persuasion, while prod suggests firmer urging or push to get action.
  • press
    3.9 18/m
    press insists or urges someone strongly and often repeatedly, while prod is more like a gentle reminder or light pressure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discourage - persuade someone not to do something
  • synonym: encourage - give support, confidence, or hope to someone
  • synonym: prompt - to cause someone to act
