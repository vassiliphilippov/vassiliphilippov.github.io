bankrupt

adjective
financially ruined
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkˌrʌpt / (AmE), / ˈbaŋ.krʌpt / (BrE)
Unable to pay debts; declared as legally insolvent or financially ruined.

Examples

A bankrupt business
Many investors lost everything when the company was officially bankrupt.
She was left virtually bankrupt by her ex-husband's debts.
The once-thriving retailer was declared bankrupt after several years of declining sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insolvent - unable to pay debts owed
  • antonym: solvent - able to pay debts owed
