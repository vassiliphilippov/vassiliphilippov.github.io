unsustainably

adverb
in a harmful way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnsəˈsteɪnəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be continued over time without causing harm or using up resources.

Examples

Grow unsustainably fast
The company was growing unsustainably, putting too much pressure on its workers and resources.
If we continue to fish unsustainably, many species may disappear from the ocean.
The city expanded unsustainably, leading to traffic problems and pollution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sustainably - in a way that can be continued without causing harm
