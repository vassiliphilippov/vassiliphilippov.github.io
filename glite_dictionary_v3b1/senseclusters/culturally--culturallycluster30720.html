culturally

adverb
in relation to culture
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃɚli / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society.

Examples

To be culturally different
Children in the school are culturally integrated through language and shared activities.
The company values diversity and hires people from culturally diverse backgrounds.
The festival was an opportunity for the city to become culturally enriched.

Related Terms

  • synonym: socially - in a way that relates to society
