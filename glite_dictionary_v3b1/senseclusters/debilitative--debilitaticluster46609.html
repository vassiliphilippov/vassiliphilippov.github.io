debilitative

adjective
causing weakness or debilitation
6.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbɪlətətɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈbɪlɪtətɪv / (BrE)
causing weakening or a loss of strength; leading to debilitation.

Examples

Debilitative side effects
Some drugs can have debilitative side effects that must be monitored.
The illness had a debilitative effect on her overall health.
The team's performance was hindered by a debilitative sense of fatigue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debilitating - causing serious weakness; making someone weak
  • antonym: invigorating - making someone feel stronger or more energetic
