CRT

noun
Critical Race Theory (academic framework)
Pronunciation: / si ɑr ti / (AmE), / si ɑː tiː / (BrE)
Critical Race Theory: an academic framework for examining how race, law, and power intersect and shape social structures.

Examples

CRT in education
Debates over CRT in schools have become highly politicized across the country.
She assigned an article explaining the history of CRT to introduce the law seminar.
Many parents asked the board to ban classes that mention CRT from the curriculum.
