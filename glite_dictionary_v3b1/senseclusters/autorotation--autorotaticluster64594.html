autorotation

noun
spinning seed descent
7.7
Pronunciation: /ɑː.tə.roʊ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɔː.tə.roʊ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɔː.tə.rəʊ.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the spinning descent of a seed or winged fruit that slows its fall and aids in dispersal

Examples

Maple seed autorotation
The autorotation of maple seeds allows them to travel farther from the parent tree.
Scientists observed the autorotation of winged fruits to study their dispersal patterns.
Children watched as the autorotation of sycamore seeds made them spin gently to the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glide - to move smoothly and continuously through air or on a surface
