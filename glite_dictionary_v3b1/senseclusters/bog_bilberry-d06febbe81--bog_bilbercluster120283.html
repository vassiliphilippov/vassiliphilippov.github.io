bog bilberry

noun
evergreen shrub found in boggy soils
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /bɑːg ˈbɪl.bər.iː/ /; / /bɔːg ˈbɪl.bər.iː/ (AmE), /bɒg ˈbɪl.bə.riː/ (BrE)
