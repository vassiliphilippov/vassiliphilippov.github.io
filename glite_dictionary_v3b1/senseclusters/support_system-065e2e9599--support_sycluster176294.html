support system

noun
technology platform for customer support
3.4
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A technology-based set of tools, software, databases, and procedures used to provide customer or user support, manage inquiries, and track issues.

Examples

Customer support system
The startup built a new support system to handle live chat, tickets, and knowledge-base articles.
After hours of waiting, the customer praised the company's improved support system for faster responses.
IT staff integrated the ticketing platform into the support system to track issues across teams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: help desk - a service (often staffed) that provides assistance and troubleshooting for customers or users
  • synonym: ticketing system - software that records, assigns, and tracks customer support requests and incidents
