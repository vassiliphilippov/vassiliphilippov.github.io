mump

noun
beggar or vagrant
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌmp / (AmE), / mʌmp / (BrE)
an old or dialect word for a beggar or vagrant, especially one who asks for food or money

Examples

A poor mump
They found a hungry mump sheltering in the doorway and offered him bread.
In the old village, a mump would sometimes appear, asking for coins from passersby.
The story described a lonely mump wandering the countryside in search of kindness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beggar - a person who asks others for money or food
  • synonym: vagrant - a person without a settled home or regular work
