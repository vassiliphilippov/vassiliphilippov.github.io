disappropriation

noun
removal from rightful owner
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˌprəʊpriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of taking something away from its rightful owner or from its intended use

Examples

Disappropriation of land
The government launched an inquiry into the disappropriation of community land.
Allegations of disappropriation led to a thorough audit of the charity's finances.
The company faced legal action over the disappropriation of intellectual property.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appropriation - the act of taking something for one's own use, often without permission
  • synonym: misappropriation - the wrongful or dishonest use of something, especially money
