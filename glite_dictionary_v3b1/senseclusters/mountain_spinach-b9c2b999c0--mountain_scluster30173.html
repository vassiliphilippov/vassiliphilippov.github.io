mountain spinach

noun
edible leafy plant similar to spinach
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən ˈspɪnɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈspɪnɪtʃ / (BrE)
an Asiatic plant (Atriplex hortensis) with edible leaves, often used as a leafy vegetable similar to spinach, naturalized in Europe and North America.

Examples

Mountain spinach leaves
In some regions, mountain spinach is a popular substitute for regular spinach in cooked dishes.
The chef added mountain spinach to the salad for a burst of color and flavor.
We harvested fresh mountain spinach from the garden to use in tonight's dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orach - another name for mountain spinach (Atriplex hortensis)
