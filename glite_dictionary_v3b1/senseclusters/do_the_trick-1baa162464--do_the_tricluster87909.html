do the trick

verb
produce the desired result
Pronunciation: / du ðə trɪk / (AmE), / də ðə trɪk / (BrE)
to achieve the desired result or have the intended effect

Examples

To do the trick well
If the stain won't come out with soap and water, a bit of vinegar should do the trick.
Sometimes a simple thank-you card will do the trick to show your appreciation.
Switching to a different internet browser might do the trick if you're having trouble loading the page.

  • synonym: work - to be effective in achieving something
