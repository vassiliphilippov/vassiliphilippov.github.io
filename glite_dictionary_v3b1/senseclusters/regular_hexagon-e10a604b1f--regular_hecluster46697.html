regular hexagon

noun
hexagon with equal sides and angles
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr ˈhɛɡsɑːɡən / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjələ ˈhɛɡsəɡən / (BrE)
A hexagon in which all six sides and all six angles are equal.

Examples

A regular hexagon shape
A regular hexagon can be divided into six equilateral triangles.
Each panel of the dome was shaped like a regular hexagon.
The snowflake resembled a perfect regular hexagon under the microscope.
