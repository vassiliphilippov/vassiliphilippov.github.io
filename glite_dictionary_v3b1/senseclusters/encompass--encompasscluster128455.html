Examples

Synonyms

  • comprehend
    3.9 0.45/m
    comprehend includes something within a clear scope, while encompass suggests surrounding or covering a wide and varied range.
  • comprise
    4.4 20/m
    comprise refers to parts that make up a whole, while encompass focuses on including or surrounding many things or ideas.
  • embrace
    3.8 9.40/m

  • cover
    1.9 68/m
