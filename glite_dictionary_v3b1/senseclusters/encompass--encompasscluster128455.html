encompass

verb
include within a scope; surround
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkʌm.pəs/ /; / /ɛn.ˈkʌm.pəs/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkʌm.pəs/ /; / /ɛn.ˈkʌm.pəs/ (BrE)
to include a wide range of things, ideas, or people; to surround or cover something completely

Examples

To encompass many ideas

The festival encompasses music, film, and art from around the world.
The law encompasses all forms of discrimination.
