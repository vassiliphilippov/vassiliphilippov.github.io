pseudopodial

adjective
like a pseudopod
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈpoʊdiəl / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəˈpəʊdiəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a pseudopod, a temporary projection of a cell used for movement or feeding
