Brussels sprout

noun
green vegetable, like small cabbages
4.7 2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌsəlz spraʊt / (AmE), / ˈbrʌsəlz spraʊt / (BrE)
A vegetable plant of the cabbage family, grown for its edible small green heads that resemble miniature cabbages, produced along a stout stalk.

Examples

A Brussels sprout plant
Each Brussels sprout grows on the stalk and can be picked individually.
Many children dislike the taste of Brussels sprouts, but they are very nutritious.
She roasted the Brussels sprouts with olive oil and garlic for dinner.
