summary

adjective
giving the main points briefly
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌm.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈsʌm.əri / (BrE)
Giving only the main points or basic information, stated briefly and clearly.

Examples

Summary statement
In the meeting, he made a summary statement to keep things moving.
She offered a summary explanation of the findings in the introduction.
The report gave a summary account of the year's main achievements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detailed - giving a lot of information with many details
  • synonym: succinct - brief and clearly expressed
