bondservant

noun
legally bound servant
9.9
Pronunciation: /bɑːnd ˈsɜːr.vənt/ /; / /bɔːnd ˈsɜːr.vənt/ (AmE), /bɒnd ˈsɜː.vənt/ (BrE)
a person who is legally or formally bound to serve another, especially as a servant or slave in the past

Examples

Ancient bondservant
In ancient times, a bondservant was often required to work for many years to pay off a debt.
The novel tells the story of a bondservant who dreams of freedom.
Historical records show that a bondservant had very few rights compared to free citizens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: servant - a person employed to perform domestic duties for others
  • synonym: slave - a person who is the legal property of another and forced to work for them
