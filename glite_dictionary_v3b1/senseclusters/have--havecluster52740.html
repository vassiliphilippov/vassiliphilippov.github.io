have

verb
to possess or own
Pronunciation: / hæv / (AmE), / hæv /; / həv / (BrE)
to own, possess, hold, or include something as a feature, quality, or characteristic; to employ or maintain a relationship; to achieve a score or result

Examples

I have no idea how to solve this problem.
She has a wonderful singing voice.
They have several important meetings today.
Do you have any idea where my keys are?
She has a beautiful garden behind her house.
They have three dogs and two cats at home.
At halftime, our team had 14 points but still trailed behind.
By the end of the game, he had 27 goals for the season.
Serena had 6 aces in the second set of the match.
