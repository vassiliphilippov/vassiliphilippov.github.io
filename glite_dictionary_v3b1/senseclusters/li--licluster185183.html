li

noun
Chinese unit of distance, 0.5 km
9.9
Pronunciation: / liː / (AmE), / liː / (BrE)
a traditional Chinese unit of distance, equal to approximately 0.5 kilometers (about one-third of a mile)

Examples

Ten li walk
He claimed he could walk ten li without stopping for a break.
The ancient road stretched for a hundred li across the countryside.
Villages separated by several li communicated mostly by messenger.
