Synonyms

  • remunerated
    6.1 0.00/m
    remunerated is a formal word for being paid for work or services, while compensated can also mean eligible to receive payment.
  • paid
    1.0 0.02/m
    paid means already receiving money for work or services, while compensated can also mean eligible to receive payment later.
  • stipendiary
    8.4 0.01/m
    stipendiary describes someone who gets a fixed stipend as part of a role, while compensated is a general term for receiving payment.
