cut of meat

noun
a piece of meat from an animal carcass
Pronunciation: / kʌt əv mit / (AmE), / kʌt əv miːt / (BrE)
a piece of meat that is separated from an animal's carcass, usually named according to the part of the animal it comes from

Examples

A cut of meat
Different animals offer distinct cuts of meat, each with unique flavors and textures.
She asked the butcher for the leanest cut of meat available.
The chef recommended a tender cut of meat for the special dish tonight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joint - a large piece of meat for roasting, often with a bone
  • synonym: steak - a slice of meat, usually beef, for frying or grilling
