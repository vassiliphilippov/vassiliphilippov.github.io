Bernie bro

noun
male supporter of Bernie Sanders
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːni broʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːni brəʊ / (BrE)
A (typically male) supporter of Bernie Sanders, especially one who is outspoken or active online.

Examples

Bernie bro supporter
During the primaries, the term Bernie bro often appeared in political commentary.
Many online debates became heated when a Bernie bro joined the discussion.
She was accused of being a Bernie bro despite her obvious qualifications.
