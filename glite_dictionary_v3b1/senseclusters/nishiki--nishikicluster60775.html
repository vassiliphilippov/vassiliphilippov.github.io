nishiki

noun
Japanese brocade or carp
Pronunciation: / niːˈʃiːki / (AmE), / nɪˈʃiːki / (BrE)
a richly patterned Japanese brocade fabric, or a type of ornamental carp with colorful markings

Examples

Nishiki fabric
The kimono was made from beautiful nishiki fabric, shimmering with gold and silver threads.
In the garden pond, several nishiki swam gracefully among the water lilies.
Collectors prize nishiki for their vibrant colors and intricate patterns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brocade - a rich fabric woven with a raised pattern
  • synonym: koi - a colorful variety of ornamental carp
