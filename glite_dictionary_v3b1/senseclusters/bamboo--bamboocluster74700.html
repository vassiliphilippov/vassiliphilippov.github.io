bamboo

noun
woody tropical grass with hollow stems
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbu / (AmE), / bæmˈbuː / (BrE)
A tropical woody grass with hollow stems, used for building, furniture, and crafts.

Examples

Bamboo forest
He planted a grove of bamboo in his backyard to create a natural screen.
Pandas rely on bamboo as their primary source of food.
The chairs on the patio are made from bamboo and are both sturdy and light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reed - a tall, slender-leaved plant of the grass family
  • degree higher: wood - the hard, fibrous material forming the trunk and branches of trees and shrubs
