persevering

adjective
persistently determined and careful
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsəˈvɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsɪˈvɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
continuing in a determined way despite difficulties, especially with careful attention to detail or exactness

Examples

A persevering effort
Her persevering efforts eventually paid off with a promotion.
The persevering scientist continued her research despite several setbacks.
We admire his persevering attention to every small detail of the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diligent - showing care and effort in one's work or duties
  • synonym: patient - able to accept or tolerate delays or difficulties
  • synonym: persistent - continuing firmly or obstinately in a course of action
