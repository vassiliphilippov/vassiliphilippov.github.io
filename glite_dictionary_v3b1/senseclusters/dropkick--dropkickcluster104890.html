dropkick

verb
score with a dropkick after a touchdown
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpˌkɪk / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌkɪk / (BrE)
to drop a ball and kick it as it touches the ground, especially in sports like rugby or football
