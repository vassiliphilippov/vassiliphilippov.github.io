rock concert

noun
performance of rock music before an audience
Pronunciation: / rɑk ˈkɑn.sɚt / (AmE), / rɒk ˈkɒn.sət / (BrE)
a public performance where rock music is played, typically by one or more bands before an audience

Examples

A rock concert event
My parents were surprised when I invited them to a rock concert.
The band played their biggest hits during their first rock concert overseas.
Thousands of fans attended the rock concert in the city park last Saturday night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gig - a live performance of popular music
