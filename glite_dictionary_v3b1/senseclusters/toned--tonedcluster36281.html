toned

adjective
firm muscles
Pronunciation: / toʊnd / (AmE), / təʊnd / (BrE)
having firm and well-defined muscles, especially as a result of exercise

Examples

Toned muscles
After months of exercise, his arms became toned and strong.
She works out regularly to keep her body toned and healthy.
The fitness trainer admired his client's toned physique after weeks of hard work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flabby - soft and lacking firmness in muscles
  • synonym: muscular - having well-developed muscles
