push

verb
encourage or urge someone to act
1.1
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ / (AmE), / pʊʃ / (BrE)
to encourage or urge (someone) to do something or to achieve a goal, especially with determination or insistence

Examples

To push her to succeed
Her parents always pushed her to do her best in school.
My friends pushed me to apply for the scholarship even though I was unsure.
The coach pushed the team to practice harder than ever before.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discourage - to try to prevent someone from taking action; to deter
  • synonym: urge - to strongly encourage or try to persuade someone to do something
