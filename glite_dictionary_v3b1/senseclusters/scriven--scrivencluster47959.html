scriven

verb
to write documents
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪvən / (AmE), / ˈskrɪvən / (BrE)
to write or copy documents, especially as a professional scribe or clerk.

Examples

To scriven documents
She spent the afternoon scrivening contracts for the new business partners to sign.
In the old library, monks were scrivening ancient manuscripts by candlelight.
He made a living by scrivening legal documents for the town's officials.
