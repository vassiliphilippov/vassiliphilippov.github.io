remarkably

adverb
to a striking or exceptional degree
2.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɑrkəbli / (AmE), / rɪˈmɑːkəbli / (BrE)
to a striking or exceptional degree; in an unusual or noteworthy way

Examples

A remarkably strong plant
Bamboo is remarkably strong yet incredibly lightweight.
She handled the difficult situation remarkably well, showing great composure.
The weather this spring has been remarkably warm for the region.
