oenomel

noun
wine mixed with honey
Pronunciation: / ˈiːnəˌmɛl / (AmE), / ˈiːnəmɛl / (BrE)
A drink made from wine mixed with honey, especially known in ancient Greece.

Examples

A oenomel drink
In ancient Greece, oenomel was believed to have medicinal properties.
She offered her guests oenomel as a sweet after-dinner drink.
The recipe for oenomel called for equal parts of honey and red wine.
