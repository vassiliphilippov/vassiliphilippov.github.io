pendant

noun
ornate ceiling light fixture
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.dənt / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.dənt / (BrE)
a branched and often ornate lighting fixture that hangs from the ceiling

Examples

Pendant light
An ornate glass pendant graced the entryway of the old mansion.
The antique pendant in the dining room casts a warm glow over the table.
They chose a modern pendant to hang above the kitchen island for both style and light.
