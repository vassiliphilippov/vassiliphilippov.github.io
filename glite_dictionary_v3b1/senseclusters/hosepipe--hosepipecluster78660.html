hosepipe

noun
flexible tube for fluids or gas
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊzˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈhəʊzˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a flexible tube used for conveying water, gas, or other fluids from one place to another

Examples

A hosepipe ban
A hosepipe ban was introduced due to the drought.
He watered the garden with a hosepipe every evening during the summer.
The hosepipe burst, flooding the garage with water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hose - a flexible tube conveying fluids; often used as synonym in American English
