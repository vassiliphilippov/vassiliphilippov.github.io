Psittacula krameri

noun
species of African/South Asian parakeet
Pronunciation: / sɪˌtækjələ ˈkrɑːmɛri / (AmE), / sɪˌtækjʊlə ˈkrɑːmɛri / (BrE)
A species of African and South Asian parakeet, also known as the ring-necked parakeet, noted for its green plumage and distinctive ring around the neck of adult males.
