below decks

adverb
under main deck
Pronunciation: / bɪˌloʊ ˈdɛks / (AmE), / bɪˌləʊ ˈdɛks / (BrE)
in the area or space beneath the main deck of a ship or boat

Examples

Crew below decks
During the storm, the crew took shelter below decks to stay safe.
Meals were often served below decks in the ship's mess hall.
The passengers explored the cabins below decks after boarding the vessel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: above deck - on or over the main deck of a ship
  • synonym: belowdecks - in the area beneath the main deck of a ship
