chare

noun
narrow alleyway
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɛr / (AmE), / tʃɛə / (BrE)
a narrow street or alley, especially in northern English dialects

Examples

A narrow chare
The old city was full of winding chares between the buildings.
We walked down a narrow chare to reach the market square.
Many historic chares in Newcastle are now popular tourist attractions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alley - a narrow passageway between or behind buildings
  • antonym: boulevard - a wide street in a city, often with trees along the sides
  • synonym: lane - a narrow road or path, especially in a rural area
