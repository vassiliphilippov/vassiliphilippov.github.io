muroid

noun
rodent superfamily member
Pronunciation: / ˈmjʊrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈmjʊərɔɪd / (BrE)
a rodent belonging to the superfamily Muroidea, which includes mice, rats, gerbils, and hamsters

Examples

A muroid rodent
The scientist studied a muroid to understand its nesting behavior in the wild.
Many common pets, such as hamsters, are classified as muroids by biologists.
A muroid infestation can cause significant damage to stored crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: murine - relating to or resembling mice or rats
  • synonym: rodent - a small mammal with continuously growing front teeth
