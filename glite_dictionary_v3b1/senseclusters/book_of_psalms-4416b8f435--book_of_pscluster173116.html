Book of Psalms

noun
a book of the Bible containing psalms
4.6 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / b äk …ôv Ààs…ëÀêmz / (AmE), / b äk …ôv Ààs…ëÀêmz / (BrE)
the collection of religious songs, prayers, and poems included as a book in the Old Testament of the Bible, traditionally used in Jewish and Christian worship

Examples

The Book of Psalms in worship

Synonyms

  • Psalms
    7.9 0.01/m
    Psalms are the individual religious songs and poems, while the Book of Psalms is the biblical book that collects them for worship.
  • Psalter
    9.9 0.26/m
    Psalter is a book arranged for prayer and song with the Psalms set for services, while the Book of Psalms is the biblical collection itself.
