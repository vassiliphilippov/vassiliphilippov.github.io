unsprayed

adjective
not chemically treated
3.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspreɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈspreɪd / (BrE)

Examples

The unsprayed apples
The apples from this farm are unsprayed and safe to eat without washing.
Many gardeners prefer unsprayed vegetables for their natural taste.
Look for unsprayed produce at the local farmers' market if you want fewer chemicals.
