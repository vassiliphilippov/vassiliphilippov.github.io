turn of the century

noun
period around the start of a new century
5.9
Pronunciation: / tɝn əv ðə ˈsɛn.tʃə.ri / (AmE), / tɜːn əv ðə ˈsɛn.tʃʊ.ri / (BrE)
the period extending roughly ten years before and after the beginning of a new century, especially used to refer to the transition between centuries.

Examples

The turn of the century period
At the turn of the century, many new inventions changed daily life.
Many buildings from the turn of the century still line the city’s main street.
There was a dramatic shift in fashion at the turn of the century.
