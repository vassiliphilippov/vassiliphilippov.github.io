drogue

noun
parachute for decelerating an object
9.9
Pronunciation: / droʊɡ / (AmE), / drəʊɡ / (BrE)
a small parachute used to slow down or stabilize a rapidly moving object, such as an aircraft or a vehicle

Examples

Parachute drogue
Racing cars sometimes use a drogue to help decelerate quickly at the finish line.
The pilot deployed the drogue to slow the jet after landing.
The spacecraft released a drogue to stabilize its descent before landing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parachute - a device for slowing the motion of an object through the air
