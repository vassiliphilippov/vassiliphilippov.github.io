rosette

noun
circular cluster of leaves or petals
5.7 4.4
Pronunciation: / roʊˈzɛt / (AmE), / rəʊˈzɛt / (BrE)
a circular arrangement of leaves or petals growing closely together around a central point, usually near the ground

Examples

Leaf rosette formation
At the center of each rosette, a tall flowering stalk may emerge.
Succulent plants often grow in a rosette pattern to conserve water.
The dandelion produces a distinctive rosette of leaves close to the ground.
