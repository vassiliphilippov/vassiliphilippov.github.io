subconjunctival

adjective
beneath conjunctiva
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbkənˌdʒʌŋkˈtaɪvəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbkɒnˌdʒʌŋkˈtɪvəl / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath the conjunctiva, the clear membrane covering the white part of the eye

Examples

Subconjunctival hemorrhage
A subconjunctival hemorrhage often looks alarming but is usually harmless.
The doctor noted a subconjunctival injection during the eye examination.
Treatment for a subconjunctival bleed is rarely necessary unless there are other symptoms.
