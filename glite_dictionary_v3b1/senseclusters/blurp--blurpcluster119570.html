blurp

noun
soft bubbling sound
8.6
Pronunciation: /blɜːrp/ (AmE), /blɜːp/ (BrE)
a soft bubbling or popping sound made by a liquid or a small air bubble

Examples

A liquid blurp
A tiny blurp of air rose to the surface as she stirred the stew slowly.
He heard a blurp from the bottle as the last drop of soda escaped.
The fish made a blurp as it broke the surface of the pond.
