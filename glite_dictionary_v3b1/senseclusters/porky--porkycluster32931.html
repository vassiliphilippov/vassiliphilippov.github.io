porky

adjective
slightly fat
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrki / (AmE), / ˈpɔːki / (BrE)
slightly fat or plump, often used informally and sometimes considered offensive

Examples

Porky child
He felt a bit porky after eating too much cake at the party.
Some people find the word porky offensive when describing someone's appearance.
After the holidays, she noticed her jeans felt tight and joked that she was getting porky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chubby - slightly overweight in a way that looks soft and round
  • antonym: slim - thin in an attractive or healthy way
