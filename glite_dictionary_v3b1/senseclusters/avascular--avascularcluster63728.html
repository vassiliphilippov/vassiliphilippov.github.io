avascular

adjective
without blood vessels
Pronunciation: / eɪˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / eɪˈvæskjʊlə / (BrE)
(of tissue) lacking blood vessels.

Examples

Avascular tissue
An avascular tissue relies on diffusion to receive nutrients.
Cartilage is typically avascular, which contributes to its slow healing process.
Certain eye structures are naturally avascular to maintain their transparency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vascular - having blood vessels
