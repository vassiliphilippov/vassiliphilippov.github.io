Republic of Peru

proper noun
country in western South America
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv pəˈru / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv pəˈruː / (BrE)
A country in western South America, formerly the center of the Inca Empire, which achieved independence from Spain in 1821.
