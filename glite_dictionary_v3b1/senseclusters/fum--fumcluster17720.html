fum

noun
puff of smoke
9.9
Pronunciation: / fʌm / (AmE), / fʌm / (BrE)
a puff or cloud of smoke or vapor, especially in an archaic or poetic context

Examples

A fum of smoke
A thin fum rose from the chimney as dawn broke over the village.
The poet described the morning mist as a gentle fum drifting over the fields.
From the candle, a faint fum curled upward in the dimly lit room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloud - a visible mass of condensed vapor or smoke
  • synonym: whiff - a brief or small puff of air, smoke, or scent
