hold

verb
organize or be responsible for (an event)
Pronunciation: / hoʊld / (AmE), / həʊld / (BrE)
to organize or be responsible for an event, meeting, or activity

Examples

To hold a meeting
The organization held a fundraising gala last weekend.
The university will hold a conference on climate change next year.
We decided to hold a surprise party for her birthday.

  • synonym: host - to act as the host for an event or gathering
