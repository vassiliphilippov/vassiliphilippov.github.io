bisexual

noun
person attracted to both sexes
Pronunciation: / baɪˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / baɪˈsɛksjʊəl / (BrE)
a person who is sexually attracted to both men and women

Examples

A bisexual person
He identifies as bisexual and is active in the LGBTQ+ community.
More people are openly describing themselves as bisexual today than in previous generations.
Sarah came out as bisexual during her first year at university.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heterosexual - a person sexually attracted to people of the opposite sex
  • antonym: homosexual - a person sexually attracted to people of the same sex
  • synonym: pansexual - a person who is attracted to people regardless of gender
