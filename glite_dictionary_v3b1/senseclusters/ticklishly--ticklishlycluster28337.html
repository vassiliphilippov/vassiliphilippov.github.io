ticklishly

adverb
delicately; awkwardly
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪklɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈtɪklɪʃli / (BrE)
in a way that is delicate, awkward, or requires careful handling to avoid causing trouble or offense

Examples

Handles ticklishly
She ticklishly navigated the conversation, careful not to offend anyone present.
The manager ticklishly addressed the team's recent mistakes during the meeting.
He ticklishly suggested changes to the plan, aware of the group's strong opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a way that is difficult or uncomfortable
  • antonym: boldly - in a confident and courageous way, without hesitation
  • synonym: delicately - in a careful or sensitive manner
