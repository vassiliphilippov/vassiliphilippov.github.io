conjunction

noun
thing or state that joins or connects
6.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈdʒʌŋk.ʃən/ (AmE), /kən.ˈdʒʌŋk.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or state of joining or combining things or states together

Examples

Conjunction of ideas
A solar eclipse occurs at the conjunction of the sun and the moon.

The discovery was made in conjunction with a team of international scientists.
Their efforts, working in close conjunction, led to a successful outcome.
