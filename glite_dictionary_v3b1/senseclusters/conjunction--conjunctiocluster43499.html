conjunction

noun
thing or state that joins or connects
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʒʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / kənˈdʒʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of joining or combining things or states together

Examples

Conjunction of ideas
A solar eclipse occurs at the conjunction of the sun and the moon.
Good teamwork requires the conjunction of different talents and skills.
The successful conjunction of the two rivers created a powerful new waterway.
Economic growth and environmental protection must go hand in conjunction.
The discovery was made in conjunction with a team of international scientists.
Their efforts, working in close conjunction, led to a successful outcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combination - a joining or merging of different parts or qualities in which the component elements are individually distinct
  • synonym: connection - a relationship in which a person or thing is linked or associated with something else
  • antonym: separation - the action or state of moving or being moved apart
