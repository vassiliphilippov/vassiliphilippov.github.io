wordsmith

noun
a skilled writer or user of words
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrdˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who has skill and proficiency in using words, especially as a writer

Examples

A skilled wordsmith
As a wordsmith, she crafted speeches that captivated audiences across the country.
Critics praised the novelist as a true wordsmith for his elegant and vivid prose.
The editor is a talented wordsmith who can transform ordinary copy into engaging stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: writer - someone who writes, especially as a profession or skill
