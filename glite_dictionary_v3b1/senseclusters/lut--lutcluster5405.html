lut

noun
sealing paste
9.9
Pronunciation: /lʌt/ /; / /luːt/ (AmE), /lʌt/ /; / /lɒt/ (BrE)
a clay or paste material used by artists or potters for sealing, shaping, or molding objects

Examples

Potter's lut
The potter applied lut to the edges of the lid to ensure a tight seal.
Before firing the ceramic vessel, they filled the cracks with lut to prevent leaks.
Traditional kilns often use lut to join clay pipes and make them watertight.
The sculptor carefully molded the lut into a graceful figurine.
Before firing the pottery, she made sure the lut was smooth and even.
Many artists prefer using lut because it is easy to shape and work with.
