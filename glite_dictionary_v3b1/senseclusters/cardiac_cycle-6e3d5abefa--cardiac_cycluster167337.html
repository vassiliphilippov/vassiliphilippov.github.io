cardiac cycle

noun
one complete heartbeat, including contraction and relaxation
3.5 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.dɪ.æk ˈsaɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈkɑːr.dɪ.æk ˈsaɪ.kl/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.dɪ.æk ˈsaɪ.kəl/ /; / /ˈkɑː.dɪ.æk ˈsaɪ.kl/ (BrE)
the sequence of events in the heart from the start of one heartbeat to the beginning of the next, including contraction and relaxation of the atria and ventricles
