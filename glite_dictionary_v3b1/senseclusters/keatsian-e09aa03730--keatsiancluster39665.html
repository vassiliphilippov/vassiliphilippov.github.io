Keatsian

noun
follower of Keats
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːtsiən / (AmE), / ˈkiːtsiən / (BrE)
a person who admires, imitates, or follows the style or ideas of the poet John Keats

Examples

A true Keatsian
As a passionate Keatsian, he often quoted lines from 'Ode to a Nightingale' in conversation.
The literary club attracted several Keatsians who admired the poet's romantic style.
Her essays revealed her as a true Keatsian, deeply influenced by his themes and imagery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Romanticist - a person who supports or admires the Romantic movement in literature or art
