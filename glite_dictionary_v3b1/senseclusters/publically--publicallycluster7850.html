publically

adverb
in a manner open to the public
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪkli / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪkli / (BrE)
(less common) In a manner accessible to or observable by the public; openly.

Examples

To speak publically
He supported the initiative publically, despite his private doubts.
She apologized publically for her mistake during the television broadcast.
The mayor was publically criticized for his handling of the crisis.

  • synonym: publicly - In a manner accessible to the public; openly
