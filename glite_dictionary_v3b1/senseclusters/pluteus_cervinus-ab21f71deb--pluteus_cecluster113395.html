Pluteus cervinus

noun
a small edible mushroom found on hardwoods
9.9
Pronunciation: / pluːˈtiːəs sərˈvaɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈpluːtiːəs səˈvɪnəs / (BrE)
A small edible mushroom with a slender stalk, usually found growing on rotting hardwoods; commonly known as the deer mushroom.

Examples

The Pluteus cervinus mushroom
