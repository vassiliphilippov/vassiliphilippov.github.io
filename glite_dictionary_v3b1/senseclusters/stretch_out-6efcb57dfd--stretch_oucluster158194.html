stretch out

verb
lie down with body extended
Pronunciation: / strɛtʃ aʊt / (AmE), / strɛtʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to lie down with your body fully extended, especially to rest or relax

Examples

To stretch out on the bed
After a long hike, she decided to stretch out on the sofa for a while.
He loves to stretch out in bed and read a book before sleeping.
The children stretched out on their blankets to watch the clouds drift by.
After a long day, she stretched out on the sofa to relax.
I love to stretch out in bed with a good book on Sunday mornings.
The children stretched out on the sand and watched the clouds drift by.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curl up - to lie or sit with legs and arms close to the body, often in comfort
  • synonym: lie down - to put oneself in a horizontal position on a surface to rest or sleep
  • synonym: recline - to lean or lie back in a relaxed position
