goatlike

adjective
resembling a goat
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having characteristics of a goat, especially in appearance or movement

Examples

Goatlike movement
The climber displayed a goatlike ability to scale the steep, rocky cliffs.
Her goatlike curiosity led her to explore every corner of the old barn.
With his goatlike beard and lively eyes, he looked just like a character from a fairy tale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caprine - relating to or resembling goats
