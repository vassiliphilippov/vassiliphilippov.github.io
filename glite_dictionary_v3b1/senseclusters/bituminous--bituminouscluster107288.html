bituminous

adjective
containing or resembling bitumen
Pronunciation: / bɪˈtuːmɪnəs / (AmE), / bɪˈtjuːmɪnəs / (BrE)
containing or resembling bitumen; having the characteristics of bitumen, especially in being dark, sticky, or tar-like.

Examples

Bituminous coal
Many power plants still burn bituminous coal for energy production.
The contractor recommended a bituminous coating to waterproof the foundation.
The road was resurfaced with bituminous material to withstand heavy traffic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asphaltic - containing or resembling asphalt, a substance related to bitumen
