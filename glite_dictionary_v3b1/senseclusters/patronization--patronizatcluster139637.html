patronization

noun
regular customer support
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪtrənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpætrənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of regularly supporting a business, shop, or service as a customer

Examples

Patronization of a store
The restaurant relies on the patronization of local residents to stay open.
After launching a new menu, the bakery noticed increased patronization from students.
Small businesses appreciate the patronization they receive from loyal customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custom - regular business or patronage from customers
  • synonym: support - giving help or encouragement to a business or cause
