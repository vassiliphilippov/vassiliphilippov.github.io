timelessly

adverb
without aging
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmləsli / (BrE)
in a way that is not affected by the passage of time or does not go out of style

Examples

To timelessly stay relevant
The classic novel remains timelessly relevant to readers of every generation.
Her style is timelessly elegant, never going out of fashion.
The melody drifted timelessly through the quiet hall, enchanting everyone present.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eternally - in a way that lasts forever
  • antonym: temporarily - for a limited period of time
