cytoplasmic

adjective
relating to cytoplasm
1.6
Pronunciation: / saɪtəˈplæzmɪk / (AmE), / saɪtəˈplæzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or found in the cytoplasm of a cell

Examples

Cytoplasmic inclusions
Abnormal cytoplasmic inclusions can be a sign of disease.
Certain proteins are localized within the cytoplasmic space of the cell.
The researchers observed a change in cytoplasmic activity after the treatment.
