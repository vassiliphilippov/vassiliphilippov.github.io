magnanimousness

noun
quality of being magnanimous; generosity
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnænəməsˌnəs / (AmE), / mægˈnænɪməsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being magnanimous; generosity and nobility of spirit, especially towards someone less powerful.

Examples

His magnanimousness showed
Despite the fierce competition, his magnanimousness impressed his rivals.
She handled the accusation with a rare magnanimousness, choosing forgiveness over anger.
The leader's magnanimousness set a powerful example for the entire organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magnanimity - quality of being generous and forgiving, especially toward a rival or less powerful person
