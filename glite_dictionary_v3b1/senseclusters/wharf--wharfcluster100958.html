wharf

noun
structure for docking ships at shore
6.7 2.59/m
Pronunciation: / w…îrf / (AmE), / w…îÀêf / (BrE)
a structure built along or out from the shore beside which ships may dock to load and unload cargo or passengers

Examples

Busy wharf area
A group of workers repaired the old wooden wharf after the storm.
The cargo was unloaded from the ship onto the wharf early this morning.
Tourists strolled along the wharf, watching the fishing boats come in.
