spasmolytic

noun
drug that relieves muscle spasms
9.3 8.4
Pronunciation: /spæz.mə.ˈlɪt.ɪk/ (AmE), /spæz.mə.ˈlɪt.ɪk/ (BrE)
a drug or agent that relieves or prevents muscle spasms, especially in smooth muscles

Examples

A spasmolytic drug
He was given a spasmolytic after experiencing severe muscle spasms in his stomach.
Some spasmolytic medications are used to treat irritable bowel syndrome and similar conditions.
The doctor prescribed a spasmolytic to help ease her abdominal cramps.
