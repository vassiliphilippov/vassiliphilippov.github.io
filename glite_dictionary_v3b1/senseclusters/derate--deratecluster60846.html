derate

verb
reduce rated capacity of equipment
9.1 7.6
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈreɪt/ /; / /diː.ˈreɪt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈreɪt/ /; / /diː.ˈreɪt/ (BrE)
to lower the maximum rated capacity or capability of electrical or mechanical equipment for safety or performance reasons

Examples

To derate equipment
Manufacturers often derate electronic components when they are used in harsh environments.
To prevent overheating, engineers decided to derate the transformer during peak summer months.
You must derate the generator if you plan to operate it at high altitudes.
