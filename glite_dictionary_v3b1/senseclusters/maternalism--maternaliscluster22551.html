maternalism

noun
motherly care or behavior
7.9 6.3
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈtɜːr.nəl.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /mə.ˈtɜː.nəl.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
behavior, attitudes, or qualities of caring, nurturing, and protecting like a mother

Examples
