information theory

noun
study of quantifying and processing information
3.7 4.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn.fɚˈmeɪ.ʃən ˌθɪr.i / (AmE), / ɪn.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən ˌθɪə.ri / (BrE)
The branch of mathematics and computer science that studies the quantification, storage, and communication of information, often focusing on limits and efficiency of information processing.
