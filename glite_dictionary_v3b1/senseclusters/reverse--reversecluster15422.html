reverse

noun
the exact opposite; direct opposition
2.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrs / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs / (BrE)
the exact opposite of something; a situation, fact, or condition that is completely different or contrary to what is expected or stated

Examples

The reverse is true

Sue expected praise, but received quite the reverse from her boss.
The actual outcome was just the reverse of what we had anticipated.
