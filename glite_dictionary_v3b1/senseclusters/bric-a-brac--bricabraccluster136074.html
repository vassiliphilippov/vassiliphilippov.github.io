bric-a-brac

noun
miscellaneous small decorative objects
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪk.əˌbræk / (AmE), / ˈbrɪk.əˌbræk / (BrE)
small decorative objects or miscellaneous curios, especially those collected for their sentimental or ornamental value rather than function

Examples

Small bric-a-brac items
He dusted the bric-a-brac on the mantelpiece every Saturday.
Her shelves were filled with bric-a-brac collected during her travels.
The shop window was crowded with vintage bric-a-brac and antiques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knick-knack - a small decorative object, especially in a home
