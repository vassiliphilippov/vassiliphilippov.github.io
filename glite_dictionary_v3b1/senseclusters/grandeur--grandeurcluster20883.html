grandeur

noun
impressive greatness of character or ideals
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrændʒər / (AmE), / ˈɡrændjə / (BrE)
the quality of being impressive or great in character, ideals, or conduct

Examples

Moral grandeur
He approached every challenge with a moral grandeur that earned him respect.
The grandeur of her vision inspired everyone in the room.
The speech was marked by a certain grandeur of thought and purpose.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humility - the quality of having a modest or low view of one's importance
  • synonym: majesty - the quality of being stately, dignified, and grand
