log

noun
official written record of a journey
3.7
Pronunciation: / lɑɡ / (AmE), / lɒɡ / (BrE)
an official or systematic written record of events, activities, or messages

Examples

According to the flight log, the plane left on time and arrived early.

Please remember to enter each transaction in the daily log before you leave.
The ship's captain maintained a detailed log throughout the voyage.
The technician checked the log for any record of errors during the system update.
