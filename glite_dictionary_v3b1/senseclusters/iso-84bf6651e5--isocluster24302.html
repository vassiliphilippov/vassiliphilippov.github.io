ISO

proper noun
international standards body
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌɛsˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌɛsˌəʊ / (BrE)
an independent international organization that develops and publishes technical standards for products, services, and systems

Examples

ISO standards organization
Many companies seek ISO certification to demonstrate their commitment to quality management.
The new safety guidelines comply with ISO standards for workplace environments.
Our products are manufactured according to ISO requirements to ensure international compatibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ANSI - American National Standards Institute, a US standards organization
  • synonym: IEC - International Electrotechnical Commission, another standards organization
