malm

noun
Upper Jurassic rocks
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑlm / (AmE), / mɑːm / (BrE)
a geological term for the Upper Jurassic rock formations or the time period of that age in parts of Europe

Examples

Malm formation
The fossils found in the malm helped scientists understand the environment of the Upper Jurassic.
Many quarries in Europe extract limestone from the malm for building materials.
Geologists studied the malm to learn more about Earth's history during the Jurassic period.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Cretaceous - the geological period following the Jurassic
  • synonym: Jurassic - the geological period from about 201 to 145 million years ago
  • degree lower: Lias - the lower part of the Jurassic system of rocks
