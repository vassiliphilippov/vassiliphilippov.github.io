supply

noun
amount of something available for use
1.8
Pronunciation: / səˈplaɪ / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ / (BrE)
an amount of something that is available to be used when needed

Examples

A supply of food
Our food supply will only last another week if deliveries don't arrive soon.
The hospital was running low on its supply of medical equipment.
They kept a constant supply of fresh water for the campers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demand - the need or desire for a particular good or service
  • synonym: provision - the act of providing or supplying something for use
