healthcare

noun
the provision of medical services and treatment
1.0 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhelθ.ker/ (AmE), /ˈhelθ.keə/ (BrE)
The organized provision of medical services to maintain or improve people's mental and physical health, especially through the prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and management of illness.

Examples

Public healthcare system
Governments are investing more in public healthcare to improve access to medical services.
She chose a career in healthcare because she wanted to help people stay healthy.
The rising cost of healthcare is a major concern for many families.

  • synonym: treatment - medical care given to a patient for an illness or injury
