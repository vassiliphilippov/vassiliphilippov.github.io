annoyance

noun
source of irritation or trouble
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔɪ.əns /; / əˈnɔɪəns / (AmE), / əˈnɔɪ.əns /; / əˈnɔɪəns / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes irritation or is bothersome

Examples

A daily annoyance
Having to reset my password every week has become an annoyance I could do without.
Her little brother can sometimes be an annoyance, but she still loves him.
The constant noise from the construction site is a real annoyance to everyone in the neighborhood.
I know my little brother can be an annoyance at times, but he means well.
It's hard to ignore such an annoyance when you're trying to concentrate.
There was a real annoyance sitting behind me, talking loudly through the entire movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritation - the state of being annoyed or something that annoys
  • synonym: nuisance - a person, thing, or circumstance causing inconvenience or annoyance
  • antonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment; opposite of annoyance
