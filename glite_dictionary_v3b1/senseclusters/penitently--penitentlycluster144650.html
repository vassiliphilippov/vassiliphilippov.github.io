penitently

adverb
in a remorseful or regretful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnɪtəntli / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪtəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows remorse or regret for having done wrong; with penitence.

Examples

To act penitently
He hung his head penitently after realizing his mistake.
She apologized penitently for her thoughtless remark.
The student waited penitently outside the principal's office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiantly - in a manner showing resistance or bold disobedience
  • synonym: remorsefully - in a manner showing remorse or regret
