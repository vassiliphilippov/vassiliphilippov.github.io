caravaneer

noun
caravan traveler
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəvəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌkærəvəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who travels for leisure in a caravan and lives in it while on holiday

Examples

Holiday caravaneer
The experienced caravaneer planned a route through several national parks for the summer.
As a caravaneer, she enjoys the freedom of exploring new places while bringing her home along.
Many caravaneers gather at campsites to share stories and travel tips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camper - a person who stays in a tent or recreational vehicle for leisure
  • synonym: tourist - a person who travels for pleasure
