living

noun
people who are still alive
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the people who are alive, especially contrasted with those who have died; used mainly with 'the'

Examples

The living and the dead
After the disaster, the survivors joined the ranks of the living once more.
The ceremony honored both the living and the dead.
We must do more to improve the lives of the living among us.
