pack rat

noun
person who compulsively collects things
Pronunciation: / pæk ræt / (AmE), / pæk ræt / (BrE)
A person who collects or hoards unnecessary or discarded items.

Examples

A pack rat keeps everything
Despite her reputation as a pack rat, Lisa finally cleaned out her overflowing attic.
He became a pack rat, unwilling to throw away anything, no matter how useless it seemed.
My grandmother is a real pack rat and keeps old magazines from decades ago.
