dunce

noun
a person considered stupid
5.6
Pronunciation: / dʌns / (AmE), / dʌns / (BrE)
A person who is considered to be slow to learn or lacking intelligence.

Examples

A dunce hat
Calling someone a dunce is both unkind and outdated.
In the old days, students who struggled were sometimes forced to wear a dunce cap.
They treated him like a dunce just because he made a simple mistake in class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - an exceptionally intelligent or talented person
  • synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
