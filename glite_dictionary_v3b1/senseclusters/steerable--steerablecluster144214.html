steerable

adjective
capable of being steered
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈstɪərəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be steered or directed along a particular course or path.

Examples

Steerable wheels
The steerable wheels on the front of the cart made it easy to navigate tight corners.
They invested in a steerable antenna to track satellites as they move across the sky.
This boat is highly steerable, even in strong currents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unsteerable - not capable of being steered
