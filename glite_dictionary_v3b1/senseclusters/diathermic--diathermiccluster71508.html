diathermic

adjective
allows heat through
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈθɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈθɜːmɪk / (BrE)
allowing heat to pass through easily; relating to the transmission of heat

Examples

Diathermic wall
A diathermic wall permits heat to flow between two substances.
The experiment required a diathermic container to ensure proper heat transfer.
Unlike an insulated cup, a diathermic vessel does not prevent heat loss.
