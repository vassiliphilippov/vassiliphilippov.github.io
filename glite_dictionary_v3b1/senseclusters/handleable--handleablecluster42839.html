handleable

adjective
easy to control
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhændələbəl / (AmE), / ˈhændləbəl / (BrE)
easy to control, steer, or manipulate, especially referring to vehicles, tools, or animals

Examples

A handleable car
The new sports car was remarkably handleable on the narrow mountain roads.
She found the large dog surprisingly handleable during their daily walks in the park.
This lightweight suitcase is much more handleable than my old one when traveling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manageable - easy to control or deal with
  • antonym: unmanageable - difficult or impossible to control
