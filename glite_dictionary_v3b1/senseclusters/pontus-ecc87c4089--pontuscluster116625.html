Pontus

proper noun
ancient region in northern Asia Minor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑn.təs / (AmE), / ˈpɒn.təs / (BrE)
an ancient region in northern Asia Minor, situated along the Black Sea, which reached its height under Mithridates VI before being incorporated into the Roman Empire.

Examples
