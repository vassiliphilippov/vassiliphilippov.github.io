capsule

noun
dry seed vessel or spore case in plants
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.səl / (AmE), / ˈkæp.sjuːl / (BrE)
a dry, usually dehiscent fruit or seed vessel that splits open when ripe to release its seeds; also, the spore-containing structure in mosses and similar plants

Examples

A seed capsule
Each capsule splits open at maturity, dispersing its seeds to the surrounding soil.
The poppy plant produces a capsule that contains dozens of tiny seeds.
When the capsule of the moss matures, it releases spores into the wind.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cone - the seed-bearing structure in conifers
  • synonym: pod - a seed vessel in plants, typically elongated
