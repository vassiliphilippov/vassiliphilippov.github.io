subjectify

verb
to treat as subject
9.3
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈdʒek.tɪ.faɪ/ /; / /ˈsʌb.dʒek.tɪ.faɪ/ (AmE), /səb.ˈdʒek.tɪ.faɪ/ /; / /ˈsʌb.dʒek.tɪ.faɪ/ (BrE)
to treat or regard something as a subject, especially in a philosophical or psychological sense

Examples

To subjectify reality
The philosopher aimed to subjectify the concept of reality in his writings.
Modern art often tries to subjectify the viewer's experience rather than present a clear message.
Therapists sometimes subjectify emotions to help patients understand their feelings.

Related Terms
