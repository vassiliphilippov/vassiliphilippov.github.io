tweetable

adjective
suitable for Twitter
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtwiːtəbəl / (BrE)
suitable or likely to be posted or shared on Twitter, especially because it is brief, catchy, or appropriate

The marketing team focused on creating tweetable slogans for the new campaign.
He always tries to make his speeches more tweetable to reach a wider audience online.
