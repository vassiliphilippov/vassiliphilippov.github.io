breadcrumb path

noun
series of markers to retrace steps
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdˌkrʌm pæθ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdˌkrʌm pɑːθ / (BrE)
a series of clues, steps, or markers used to help retrace one's steps or find the way back to a starting point

Examples

A breadcrumb path on website
Many websites use a breadcrumb path at the top to help users navigate.
She left a breadcrumb path of notes to guide her friend through the maze.
The hikers marked a breadcrumb path so they could find their way back to camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trail - a mark or series of marks left by something that has passed
