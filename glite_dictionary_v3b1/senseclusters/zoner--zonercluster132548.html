zoner

noun
person who zones out
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈzoʊ.nər/ (AmE), /ˈzəʊ.nə/ (BrE)
a person who often loses focus and becomes mentally absent or distracted, especially by daydreaming

Examples

A real zoner in class
During long lectures, he turns into a complete zoner and misses important information.
Her friends teased her for being a zoner whenever she drifted off during conversations.
If you are a zoner at work, you might forget key details from meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absent-minded - often forgetful or inattentive
  • synonym: daydreamer - a person who is often lost in thought or fantasy
  • antonym: focused - paying close attention and not distracted
