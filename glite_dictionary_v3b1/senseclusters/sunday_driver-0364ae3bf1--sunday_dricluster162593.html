Sunday driver

noun
a slow or inexperienced driver
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌn.deɪ ˈdraɪ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ ˈdraɪ.və / (BrE)
a person who drives slowly and casually, often causing annoyance to other drivers; typically refers to an inexperienced or overly cautious driver

Examples

A Sunday driver on road
Don't be such a Sunday driver; pick up the pace a little.
He complained about being stuck behind a Sunday driver on his way to work.
My grandmother admits she's a bit of a Sunday driver these days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slowpoke - a person who moves or acts slowly
  • antonym: speedster - a person who drives very fast
