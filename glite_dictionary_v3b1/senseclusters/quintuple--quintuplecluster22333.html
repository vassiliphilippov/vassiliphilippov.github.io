quintuple

noun
set of five similar things
Pronunciation: / kwɪnˈtuːpəl / (AmE), / kwɪnˈtjuːpəl / (BrE)
A set or group of five similar things considered as a single unit.

Examples

A quintuple of drinks
He ordered a quintuple of espresso shots to help him stay awake.
The award ceremony ended with each quintuple being honored on stage.
The museum displayed a quintuple of rare coins from the same period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quintet - a group of five people or things, especially musicians
