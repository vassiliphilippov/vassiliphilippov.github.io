feature

noun
part of a person's face
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
a distinctive part of someone's face, such as the eyes, nose, mouth, or chin.

Examples

Facial features
Her delicate features gave her a striking appearance.
Strong features often run in their family.
The artist expertly captured the features of his subject's face.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspect - the appearance of a person's face expressing a particular emotion
  • synonym: lineament - a distinctive shape, contour, or line, especially of the face
