public nudity

noun
being naked in public
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈnuːdəti / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈnjuːdəti / (BrE)
the state of being naked in a place where others can see you, especially in public settings

Examples

Public nudity laws
He was arrested for public nudity after swimming in the fountain downtown.
Some artists use public nudity as a form of political protest.
The city enforces strict laws against public nudity in its parks and streets.
