chewer

noun
person who chews, especially tobacco
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃuːər / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːə / (BrE)
a person or animal that chews something, such as food, gum, or tobacco

Examples

A chewer of gum
A chewer must be careful not to swallow gum accidentally.
He was a habitual chewer of tobacco, even during meetings.
The young chewer struggled to resist the temptation to snack between meals.
The rabbit is a constant chewer of vegetables in the garden.
As a slow chewer, he always finished his meals after everyone else.
Some animals are natural chewers and need tough food to keep their teeth healthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chew - to bite and grind food with the teeth
  • synonym: eater - a person or animal that eats food
  • antonym: swallower - a person or animal that swallows food
