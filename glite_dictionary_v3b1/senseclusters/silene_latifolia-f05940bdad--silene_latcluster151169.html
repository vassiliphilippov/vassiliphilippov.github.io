Silene latifolia

noun
herbaceous plant with white evening flowers
9.6
Pronunciation: / saɪˈliːni læˈtɪfoliə / (AmE), / sɪˈliːni læˈtɪfəlɪə / (BrE)
A bluish-green herbaceous plant with sticky stems and clusters of large, white, evening-blooming flowers, belonging to the carnation family.

Botanists often study Silene latifolia for its unique evening-flowering patterns.
