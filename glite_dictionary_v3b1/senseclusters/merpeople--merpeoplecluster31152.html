merpeople

noun
mythical sea beings
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrˌpipəl / (AmE), / ˈmɜːˌpiːpl̩ / (BrE)
mythical beings with the upper body of a human and the tail of a fish, often found in folklore and legends

Examples

Merpeople in legends
Ancient sailors believed that merpeople could be seen swimming near rocky shores.
Stories about merpeople have fascinated children and adults for centuries.
In the fantasy novel, the kingdom of merpeople lies deep beneath the ocean waves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: merman - a mythical male being with a human upper body and a fish tail
