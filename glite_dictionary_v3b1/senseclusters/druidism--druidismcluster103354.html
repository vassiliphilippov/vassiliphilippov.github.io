druidism

noun
religion and philosophy of the ancient Druids
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdruːɪˌdɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdruːɪdɪzəm / (BrE)
the system of religion and philosophy practiced by the ancient Druids, including their beliefs, rites, and ceremonies

Examples

Ancient druidism beliefs
Historians study druidism to better understand Celtic religious beliefs and practices.
Many people associate druidism with mystical rituals performed in ancient forests.
Modern groups sometimes revive aspects of druidism as part of contemporary pagan traditions.

Related Terms
