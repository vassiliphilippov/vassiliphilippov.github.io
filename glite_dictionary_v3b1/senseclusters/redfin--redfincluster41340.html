redfin

noun
small fish with red fins
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌfɪn / (BrE)
a small freshwater fish with reddish fins, found in rivers and lakes in various regions

Examples

A redfin fish
We spotted a redfin swimming near the riverbank.
The children caught a small redfin while fishing in the lake.
Birds often feed on redfin in shallow streams during the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minnow - a small freshwater fish commonly found in streams and ponds
  • synonym: perch - a type of freshwater fish often found in rivers and lakes
