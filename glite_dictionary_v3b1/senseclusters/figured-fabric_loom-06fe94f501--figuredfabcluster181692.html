figured-fabric loom

noun
loom for decorative patterned fabrics
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚd ˈfæbrɪk luːm / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjəd ˈfæbrɪk luːm / (BrE)
a type of loom designed for weaving fabrics with decorative patterns or figures

Examples

A figured-fabric loom
A figured-fabric loom allows for the production of complex woven patterns unavailable on simpler looms.
Many historic tapestries were made using a figured-fabric loom in traditional textile workshops.
The artisan operated the figured-fabric loom to create intricate designs for the upholstery.
