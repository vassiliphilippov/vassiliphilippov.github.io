bohemia

noun
unconventional artistic community
Pronunciation: / boʊˈhiːmiə / (AmE), / bəʊˈhiːmiə / (BrE)
A community or group of artists, writers, or intellectuals, often with unconventional lifestyles and interests outside the mainstream.

Examples

The bohemia lifestyle
Despite their wealth, they longed for the excitement and rebellion of a true bohemia.
Many writers of the 1920s found acceptance in the vibrant bohemia of New York’s Greenwich Village.
The city's bohemia attracted free spirits and outcasts seeking creative freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bohemian - a person belonging to or associated with a bohemia
