teletherapy

noun
remote therapy sessions
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
therapy or counseling provided to a patient remotely using telecommunications such as video calls or telephone.

Examples

Online teletherapy session
Many people found teletherapy helpful during the pandemic when in-person visits were not possible.
Her doctor recommended teletherapy as a convenient way to continue treatment from home.
With teletherapy, patients can access mental health support regardless of their location.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-person therapy - therapy conducted face-to-face in the same location
  • synonym: online therapy - therapy conducted over the internet
