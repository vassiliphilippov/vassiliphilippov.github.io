unreceived

adjective
not received
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈsiːvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈsiːvd / (BrE)
not received or not having been accepted or taken in

Examples

An unreceived payment
The payment remained unreceived even after the deadline had passed.
Her letter went unreceived because the address was incorrect.
Several invitations were left unreceived due to a mailing error.

Related Terms

  • antonym: received - having been accepted or taken in
  • synonym: unaccepted - not accepted or approved
