yourselves

pronoun
plural reflexive pronoun
1.0
Pronunciation: / jʊrˈsɛlvz / (AmE), / jɔːˈsɛlvz / (BrE)
the reflexive form of 'you' used when talking to more than one person, referring back to the people being spoken to

Examples

Make yourselves comfortable
Please make yourselves comfortable while you wait in the lobby.
Did you all enjoy yourselves at the party last night?
Remember to take care of yourselves during the cold winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ourselves - the reflexive form of 'we'
  • synonym: themselves - the reflexive form of 'they'
  • degree lower: yourself - the reflexive form of 'you' for one person
