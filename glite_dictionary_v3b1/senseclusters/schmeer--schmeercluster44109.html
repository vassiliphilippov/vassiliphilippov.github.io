schmeer

noun
soft food spread
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʃmɪr / (AmE), / ʃmɪə / (BrE)
a spread or layer of a soft food, especially cream cheese, applied to bread or bagels

Examples

A schmeer of cream cheese
He likes a thick schmeer of cream cheese on his bagel.
She added a generous schmeer of butter to her warm toast.
Would you like a schmeer of hummus on your sandwich?

Related Terms

  • synonym: spread - a soft food that is spread on bread or crackers
  • synonym: topping - a food placed on top of another food
