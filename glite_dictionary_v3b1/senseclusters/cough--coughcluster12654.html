cough

noun
sudden expulsion of air from lungs
Pronunciation: / kɑf / (AmE), / kɒf / (BrE)
A sudden, forceful expulsion of air from the lungs, usually to clear the air passages.

Examples

A bad cough
His persistent cough kept him awake all night.
She covered her mouth to muffle a sudden cough during the meeting.
The doctor listened to her cough before suggesting any treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hack - a dry, harsh cough
