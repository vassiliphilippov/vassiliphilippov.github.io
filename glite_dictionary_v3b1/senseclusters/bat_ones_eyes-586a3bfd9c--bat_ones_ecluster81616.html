bat one's eyes

verb
to flutter eyelashes flirtatiously
6.9
Pronunciation: / bæt wʌnz aɪz / (AmE), / bæt wʌnz aɪz / (BrE)
to flutter one's eyelashes rapidly, often in a playful or flirtatious manner

Examples

To bat one's eyes

She batted her eyes and asked for a little more time to finish the project.
Whenever she wanted something, she would bat her eyes at her parents.
