Pneumocystis carinii

noun
fungus causing pneumonia
Pronunciation: / ˌnjuːmoʊˈsɪstɪs kəˈraɪniaɪ /; / ˌnuːmoʊˈsɪstɪs kəˈriːniː / (AmE), / ˌnjuːməˈsɪstɪs kəˈriːniːaɪ /; / ˌnjuːməˈsɪstɪs kəˈriːniː / (BrE)
a species of fungus that can cause pneumonia, especially in people with weakened immune systems
