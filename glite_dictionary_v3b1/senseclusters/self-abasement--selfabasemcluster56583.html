self-abasement

noun
act of humiliating or punishing oneself
Pronunciation: / sɛlf əˈbeɪsmənt / (AmE), / sɛlf əˈbeɪsmənt / (BrE)
the act of humiliating or punishing oneself, often as a way of expressing remorse or seeking atonement for wrongdoing

Examples

Self-abasement in public
During the ceremony, she displayed self-abasement by confessing her faults in public.
His constant self-abasement made others uncomfortable, as he always blamed himself for any failure.
The monk practiced self-abasement as a form of penance for his past mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-humiliation - deliberate humiliation of oneself
  • antonym: self-respect - a sense of pride and confidence in oneself
