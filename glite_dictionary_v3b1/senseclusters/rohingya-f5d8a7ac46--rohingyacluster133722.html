Rohingya

proper noun
Myanmar ethnic group
8.9
Pronunciation: / roʊˈhɪndʒə / (AmE), / rəʊˈhɪndʒə / (BrE)
a Muslim ethnic minority group from Myanmar, known for facing persecution and displacement

Examples

Rohingya people
Thousands of Rohingya have fled Myanmar due to violence and discrimination.
The international community has called for protection of the Rohingya people.
Many Rohingya families now live in refugee camps in Bangladesh.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Bamar - the majority ethnic group in Myanmar
  • synonym: Bengali - an ethnic group from the Bengal region of South Asia
