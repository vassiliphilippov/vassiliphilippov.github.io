sassafras leaf

noun
leaf of the sassafras tree used as flavoring
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsæsəˌfræs lif / (AmE), / ˈsasəˌfras liːf / (BrE)
a leaf from the sassafras tree, used as a flavoring in cooking and herbal drinks
