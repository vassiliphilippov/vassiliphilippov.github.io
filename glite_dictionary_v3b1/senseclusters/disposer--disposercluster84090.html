disposer

noun
waste remover
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspoʊzər / (AmE), / dɪˈspəʊzə / (BrE)
a person or device that gets rid of something, especially waste or unwanted material (such as a kitchen appliance for food scraps)

Examples

Kitchen disposer
The kitchen disposer quickly grinds up food scraps after meals.
A professional disposer was hired to remove the hazardous materials safely.
Many homes now have a disposer installed under the sink for convenience.
The kitchen was equipped with a modern disposer to handle food scraps efficiently.
As a professional disposer of hazardous materials, she followed strict safety protocols.
Many households rely on a disposer to keep their sinks free of waste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collector - a person or device that gathers or accumulates things
  • synonym: eliminator - a person or thing that removes or gets rid of something
  • antonym: retainer - a person or thing that keeps or holds something
