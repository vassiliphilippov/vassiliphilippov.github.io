stem

noun
front part of a vessel or aircraft
Pronunciation: / stɛm / (AmE), / stɛm / (BrE)
the front part of a vessel or aircraft, especially where the sides meet at the bow

Examples

Ship's stem
A sharp collision damaged the stem of the ferry.
The sailor stood at the stem, scanning the horizon for land.
Waves broke against the stem as the ship moved forward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow - the front section of a ship or boat
  • antonym: stern - the rear part of a ship or boat
