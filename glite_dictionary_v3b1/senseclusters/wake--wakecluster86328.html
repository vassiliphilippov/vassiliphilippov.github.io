wake

verb
make someone stop sleeping
Pronunciation: / weɪk / (AmE), / weɪk / (BrE)
to stop sleeping or to cause someone to stop sleeping; to become or make someone awake

Examples

To wake a person
Please wake me up before you leave the house.
She gently woke her daughter for school.
The loud noise from the street woke the entire neighborhood.
Every morning, I wake at sunrise to go for a run.
It can be hard to wake up early on cold winter days.
The baby woke suddenly when the phone rang.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arouse - to cause someone to stop sleeping or to be alert
  • synonym: awake - to stop sleeping; to become aware or conscious again
  • synonym: awaken - to cause someone to wake up
  • antonym: sleep - to be in a state of sleep
