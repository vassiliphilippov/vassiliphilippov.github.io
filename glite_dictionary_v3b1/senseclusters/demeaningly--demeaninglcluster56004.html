demeaningly

adverb
in a humiliating or degrading manner
5.6 6.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmiːnɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈmiːnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes someone to lose dignity or feel degraded; in a humiliating way.

Examples

To speak demeaningly
He spoke to his assistant demeaningly, which upset everyone in the office.
His remarks about the staff were demeaningly condescending.
She refused to be treated demeaningly by her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humiliatingly - in a way that causes embarrassment or shame
