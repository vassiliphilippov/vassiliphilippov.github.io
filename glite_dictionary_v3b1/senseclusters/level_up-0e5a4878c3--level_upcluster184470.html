level up

verb
improve a character in a game
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl ʌp / (BrE)
(in video games) to increase a character's abilities, stats, or rank by gaining enough experience or meeting certain criteria.

Examples

To level up a character
After defeating the boss, her character was able to level up and unlock new skills.
He spent all weekend leveling up his mage to prepare for the next quest.
It takes a lot of practice and patience to level up quickly in this game.
