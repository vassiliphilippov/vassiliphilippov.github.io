tapper

noun
person who wiretaps phone or telegraph lines
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpər / (AmE), / ˈtæpə / (BrE)
a person who intercepts telephone or telegraph communications by secretly attaching a device to the line

Examples

Phone line tapper
During the investigation, it was revealed that a tapper had recorded the politician's private conversations.
Security experts warn companies to check for a tapper if they suspect corporate espionage.
The police arrested the tapper after discovering unauthorized devices on the telephone line.
