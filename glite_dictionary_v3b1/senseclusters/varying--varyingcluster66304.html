varying

adjective
characterized by diversity or change
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɛːrɪɪŋ / (BrE)
differing in size, amount, degree, or nature; marked by diversity or change.

Examples

Varying styles
His art is known for its varying styles and use of color.
The prices of vegetables are varying from week to week depending on supply.
The team faced varying levels of difficulty during the competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: constant - not changing or varying; uniform
  • synonym: variable - liable to change; not consistent
