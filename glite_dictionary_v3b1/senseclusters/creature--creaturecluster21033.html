creature

noun
a living being, usually animal
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːtʃər /; / ˈkriːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkriːtʃə / (BrE)
a living being, especially an animal or imaginary being, as distinct from a plant or object

Examples

A wild creature
She believes that every living creature deserves kindness and respect.
The forest is home to many mysterious creatures that are rarely seen by humans.
The legend describes a terrifying creature that appeared during the full moon.
The children were delighted to see a strange creature crawling in the garden.
The forest is home to many fascinating creatures that are rarely seen by humans.
Under the microscope, the pond revealed hundreds of tiny creatures swimming about.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: animal - a living organism that feeds on organic matter
  • synonym: being - a living creature; existence
  • degree higher: person - a human being
  • antonym: plant - a living organism lacking specialized sense organs and voluntary movement
