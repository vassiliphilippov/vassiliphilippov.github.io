unapprehended

adjective
not understood
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌæprɪˈhɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌæprɪˈhɛndɪd / (BrE)
not understood, noticed, or mentally grasped; not recognized or realized.

Examples

Some subtle cultural differences are often unapprehended by visitors.
