millennium

noun
period of 1,000 years
Pronunciation: / məˈlɛniəm / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛnɪəm / (BrE)
A period of a thousand years, especially when calculated from a particular date.

Examples

A millennium passes
Changes in technology have accelerated dramatically over the last millennium.
Historians debate how civilizations developed over the past millennium.
The year 2000 marked the beginning of a new millennium.
