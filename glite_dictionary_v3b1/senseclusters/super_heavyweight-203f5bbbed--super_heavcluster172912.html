super heavyweight

noun
heaviest division in combat sports
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpɚ ˈhɛviˌweɪt /; / ˌsuːpər ˈhɛviˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpə ˈhɛviweɪt /; / ˌsuːpə ˈhɛviˌweɪt / (BrE)
