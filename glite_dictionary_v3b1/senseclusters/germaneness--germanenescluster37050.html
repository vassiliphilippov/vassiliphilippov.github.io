germaneness

noun
relevance or pertinence to a subject
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɜrˈmeɪn.nɪs / (AmE), / dʒɜːˈmeɪn.nɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Lawyers often debate the germaneness of certain pieces of evidence.
The judge questioned the germaneness of the witness's testimony to the case.
