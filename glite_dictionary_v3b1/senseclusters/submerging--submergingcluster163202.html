submerging

noun
the act of being completely covered by water
5.3
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɝːdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / səbˈmɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of sinking until completely covered with water

Examples

The submerging of land
After hours of steady rainfall, the submerging of the roadway became inevitable.
The captain tried to prevent the submerging of the vessel by sealing the hull.
The documentary showed the gradual submerging of coastal villages due to rising sea levels.
