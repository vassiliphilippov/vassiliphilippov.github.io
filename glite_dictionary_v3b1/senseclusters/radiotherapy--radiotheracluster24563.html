radiotherapy

noun
treatment of disease with radiation
3.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /reɪ.dɪ.oʊ.ˈθer.ə.piː/ /; / /reɪ.diː.oʊ.ˈθer.ə.piː/ (AmE), /reɪ.dɪ.əʊ.ˈθer.ə.piː/ /; / /reɪ.diː.əʊ.ˈθer.ə.piː/ (BrE)
the treatment of disease, especially cancer, using controlled exposure to radioactive substances or radiation.

Examples
