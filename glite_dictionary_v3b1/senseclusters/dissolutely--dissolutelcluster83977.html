dissolutely

adverb
in a morally unrestrained, licentious way
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˈluːtli / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈluːtli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of moral restraint or indulges in sensual pleasures; in a licentious or dissolute manner.

Examples

Behave dissolutely at party
He behaved dissolutely at the party, disregarding all sense of propriety.
She accused him of spending his youth dissolutely rather than pursuing an education.
The king was known to live dissolutely, squandering wealth on pleasure and vice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissolute - lacking in moral restraint; licentious
