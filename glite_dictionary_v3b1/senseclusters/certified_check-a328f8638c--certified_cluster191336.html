certified check

noun
bank-guaranteed check
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.t̬əˌfaɪd tʃɛk /; / sərˈtɪfaɪd tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tɪˌfaɪd tʃɛk /; / səˈtɪfʌɪd tʃɛk / (BrE)
a check guaranteed by a bank, certifying that the drawer has sufficient funds on deposit to cover the payment

Examples

A certified check
Many landlords require a certified check for the security deposit.
The bank issued a certified check to guarantee payment to the seller.
To complete the transaction, she brought a certified check as proof of funds.
He went to the bank to obtain a certified cheque before closing the deal.
She requested a certified cheque to pay the deposit on the new apartment.
The car dealership only accepted payment by certified cheque or wire transfer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bank draft - a cheque drawn by a bank on its own funds and signed by its cashier
  • synonym: cashier's check - a check written by a bank on its own funds, guaranteed by the bank
  • antonym: personal cheque - a cheque written by a personal account holder without bank certification
