nonreportable

adjective
not required to report
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈpɔrtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈpɔːtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not required or suitable to be officially reported or disclosed

Examples

Nonreportable incident
The minor accident was considered nonreportable and did not need to be included in the official records.
Certain medical conditions are nonreportable unless they pose a public health risk.
Because the error was nonreportable, the team handled it internally without notifying authorities.
