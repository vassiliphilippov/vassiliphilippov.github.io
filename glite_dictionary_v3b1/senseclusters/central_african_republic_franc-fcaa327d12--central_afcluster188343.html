Central African Republic franc

noun
currency of the Central African Republic
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛntrəl ˈæfrɪkən rɪˈpʌblɪk fræŋk / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəl ˈæfrɪkən rɪˈpʌblɪk fræŋk / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in the Central African Republic, equal in value to the CFA franc used by several central African countries

Examples

The Central African Republic franc currency
He exchanged his dollars for Central African Republic francs at the airport.
The Central African Republic franc is part of the CFA franc currency system used by several countries.
The price of bread in Bangui is usually given in Central African Republic francs.
