Synonyms

  • employee turnover
    3.3 0.04/m
    employee turnover describes staff leaving and replacement in a company, while turnover is a broader term used in different contexts.
  • turnover rate
    4.0 0.2/m
    turnover rate emphasizes the measured percentage of staff change in a period, while turnover names the general phenomenon of staff change.
  • churn
    5.1 0.1/m
    churn mainly names customers or users leaving a service and appears in business contexts, while turnover refers to employees leaving jobs.
