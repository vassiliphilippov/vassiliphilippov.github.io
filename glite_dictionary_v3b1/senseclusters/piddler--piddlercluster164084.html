piddler

noun
time-waster
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdlər / (AmE), / ˈpɪdlə / (BrE)
a person who wastes time on trivial or unimportant activities instead of doing useful work

Examples

Lazy piddler
Instead of helping with the project, he acted like a piddler and wasted everyone's time.
The manager warned the piddler in the team to focus on important tasks.
She was tired of being called a piddler and decided to become more productive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawdler - a person who moves or acts too slowly
  • antonym: hardworker - a person who works with energy and commitment
  • synonym: idler - a person who avoids work or spends time doing nothing
