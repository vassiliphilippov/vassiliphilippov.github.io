right

adjective
outer side of cloth or clothing
3.4
Pronunciation: / raɪt / (AmE), / raɪt / (BrE)
(of a piece of cloth or clothing) facing or meant to face outward; the side intended to be seen.

Examples

Right side of fabric
Before sewing, check that the right side of the fabric is facing up.
He accidentally wore his sweater with the right side inside.
The pattern was much brighter on the right side of the material.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wrong - (of the side of cloth or clothing) not meant to face outward
