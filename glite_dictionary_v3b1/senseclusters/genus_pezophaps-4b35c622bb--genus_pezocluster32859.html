genus Pezophaps

noun
genus of extinct bird (solitaire)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌpɛz.oʊˈfæps / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌpɛz.əʊˈfæps / (BrE)
A genus of extinct birds, consisting solely of the solitaire, which was native to Rodrigues Island in the Indian Ocean.
