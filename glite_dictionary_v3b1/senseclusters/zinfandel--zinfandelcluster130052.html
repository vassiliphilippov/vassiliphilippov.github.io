zinfandel

noun
type of Californian red wine or grape
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪn.fənˌdɛl / (AmE), / ˈzɪn.fən.dəl / (BrE)
A variety of red wine grape grown in California, or the wine made from it, typically producing a dry, fruity red wine.

Examples

A zinfandel wine
This winery is known for its award-winning zinfandel and beautiful vineyard views.
We opened a bottle of zinfandel to go with our steak dinner.
Zinfandels from California often have a rich, berry-like flavor profile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabernet sauvignon - a type of full-bodied red wine, or the grape itself
  • synonym: merlot - a type of soft, dry red wine, or the grape it comes from
