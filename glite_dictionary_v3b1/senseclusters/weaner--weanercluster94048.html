weaner

noun
recently weaned animal
6.8 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàwiÀên…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀên…ô / (BrE)
a young animal (or sometimes child) that has recently stopped feeding on its mother's milk

Examples

After a few weeks, the weaner lambs adapted well to eating solid food.

The farmer separated the weaners from their mothers to help them adjust to solid food.
After months of breastfeeding, she held her sleepy weaner close and smiled.
At the daycare, each weaner was given a special cup to encourage independent drinking.
