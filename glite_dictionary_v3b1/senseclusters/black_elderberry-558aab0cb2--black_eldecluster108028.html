black elderberry

noun
a shrub with edible purple-black berries
4.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /blæk ˈel.dər.ber.iː/ (AmE), /blæk ˈel.də.be.riː/ (BrE)
A shrub native to central and eastern North America, known for its clusters of purple-black berries used in wines and jellies.
