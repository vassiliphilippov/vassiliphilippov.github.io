Stalinist

noun
supporter of Stalinism
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːlɪnɪst / (AmE), / ˈstælɪnɪst / (BrE)
a supporter or follower of Joseph Stalin or the principles of Stalinism, especially relating to Soviet politics and ideology

Examples

A Stalinist leader
As a committed Stalinist, he defended the purges of the 1930s.
Historians debate how many true Stalinists remained after his death.
The party split when the Stalinists clashed with those who favored reform.
