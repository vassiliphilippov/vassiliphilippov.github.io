Examples

Synonyms

    3.5 8.74/m
    labor is the process by which a woman gives birth from contractions to delivery, while confinement names that whole period.
  • childbirth
    2.3 5.04/m
    childbirth is the process of giving birth that covers labor and delivery, while confinement refers specifically to the labor period.
  • parturiency
    9.9 0.00/m
    parturiency is a formal medical term for being in labor, while confinement is the everyday word for the same labor period.
  • birthing
    3.3 1.23/m
    birthing is an informal word for the act of giving birth, while confinement is the formal term for the labor period.
  • delivery
    3.0 5.20/m
    delivery is the act of the baby coming out during birth, while confinement means the whole labor period leading to birth.
  • childbed
    8.6 0.01/m
    childbed refers to the time of giving birth and the short postnatal period, while confinement names only the labor period.
  • lying-in
    9.9 0.05/m
    lying-in can mean childbirth and the long bed rest after birth, while confinement describes the labor period before delivery.
