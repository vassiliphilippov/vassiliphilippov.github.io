non-technological

adjective
not using technology
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌtɛknəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌtɛknəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving, using, or related to technology or technological methods

Examples

A non-technological solution
The team decided to use a non-technological solution that relied on simple tools.
Many traditional crafts are completely non-technological and require only basic materials.
They preferred a non-technological approach to teaching, focusing on face-to-face interaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manual - done by hand or without machines
  • antonym: technological - involving or related to technology
