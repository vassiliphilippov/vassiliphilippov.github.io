unclosed

adjective
missing closing symbol
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkloʊzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkləʊzd / (BrE)
not terminated or finished with a required closing symbol, tag, or boundary, especially in writing, code, or markup

Examples

Unclosed bracket
The error message indicated there was an unclosed parenthesis in the formula.
Be careful not to leave any unclosed HTML tags in your web page code.
The script failed because of an unclosed quotation mark in the input file.

Related Terms

  • synonym: open - not closed or finished; lacking a closing element
