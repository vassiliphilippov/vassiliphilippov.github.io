Czerny

proper noun
Austrian pianist and composer
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrni / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːni / (BrE)
(1791–1857) An Austrian pianist and composer renowned for his piano studies and as a student of Beethoven and teacher of Liszt.

Examples

Czerny piano studies
Czerny was a bridge between the classical and romantic eras in piano music.
