keep one's ear to the ground

verb
stay alert to news or rumors
1.0 6.5
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz ɪr tə ðə ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz ɪə tə ðə ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
