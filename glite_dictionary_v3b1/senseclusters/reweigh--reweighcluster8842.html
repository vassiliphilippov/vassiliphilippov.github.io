reweigh

verb
to weigh again
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈweɪ / (BrE)
to weigh something again in order to check or confirm its weight

Examples

To reweigh the bag
The airline asked us to reweigh our luggage before boarding the plane.
After adjusting the scale, the technician decided to reweigh the sample for accuracy.
You should reweigh the ingredients if you think there was a mistake the first time.
