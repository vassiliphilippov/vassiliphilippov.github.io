swinish

adjective
coarse or ill-mannered; pig-like
8.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswaɪ.nɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈswʌɪ.nɪʃ /; / ˈswaɪ.nɪʃ / (BrE)
coarse, greedy, or unpleasant in behavior or appearance, like a pig

Examples

A swinish attitude
His swinish attitudes made him unpopular among his colleagues.
Some critics described the crowd's response as downright swinish.
The guests were appalled by his swinish behavior at the dinner table.
His swinish behavior at the buffet embarrassed everyone at the table.
She found his swinish attitude towards money both rude and distasteful.
The guests attacked the feast with almost swinish enthusiasm, ignoring manners.
