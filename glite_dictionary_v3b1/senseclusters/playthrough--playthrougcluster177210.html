playthrough

noun
test run or demo
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌθruː / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌθruː / (BrE)
an instance of performing or testing something, such as a game, plan, or procedure, from start to finish to check how it works

Examples

Software playthrough test
The team did a playthrough of the new software to find any bugs before launch.
During the playthrough of the emergency plan, several issues were discovered and fixed.
He recorded his first playthrough of the video game and shared it online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstration - an act of showing how something works
  • synonym: trial run - a test of something to see how it works in practice
