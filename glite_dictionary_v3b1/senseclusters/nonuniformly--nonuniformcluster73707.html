nonuniformly

adverb
not evenly
6.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈjuː.nə.fɔːr.liː/ /; / /nən.ˈjuː.nə.fɔːr.liː/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈjuː.nɪ.fɔːm.liː/ /; / /nə.ˈjuː.nɪ.fɔːm.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is not uniform or not the same throughout

Examples

Spread nonuniformly across
The fertilizer was spread nonuniformly across the field, causing some plants to grow better than others.
If the heat is applied nonuniformly, the material may warp or crack.
The students were seated nonuniformly around the classroom, leaving some areas empty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unevenly - in an irregular or not equal way
  • antonym: uniformly - in a way that is the same everywhere or at all times
