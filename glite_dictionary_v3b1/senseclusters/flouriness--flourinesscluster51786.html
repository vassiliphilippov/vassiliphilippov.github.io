flouriness

noun
fine, powdery texture; flour-like quality
3.5 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈflaʊ.rɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈflaʊ.rə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈflaʊ.rɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈflaʊ.rə.nəs/ (BrE)
The quality or condition of being like flour, having a fine, soft, powdery texture.

Examples

The flouriness of powder
She checked the flouriness of the powder to ensure it would mix evenly into the sauce.
Some people dislike the flouriness found in processed potatoes compared to waxier types.
The flouriness of the cake's surface was a result of the excess dusting before baking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: grittiness - the quality of being gritty; having small hard particles
  • synonym: powderiness - the quality of being like powder
