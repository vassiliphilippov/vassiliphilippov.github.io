endophytic

adjective
inside a plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈfaɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈfɪtɪk / (BrE)
living inside a plant, especially describing microorganisms or fungi that inhabit plant tissues

Examples

endophytic fungi
Many endophytic fungi form beneficial relationships with their host plants.
Researchers study endophytic bacteria to understand how they protect crops from disease.
The endophytic microorganisms were isolated from the roots of several tree species.

Related Terms
