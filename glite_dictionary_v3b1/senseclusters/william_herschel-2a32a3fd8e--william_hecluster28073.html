William Herschel

proper noun
English astronomer; discovered Uranus and infrared radiation
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪl.jəm ˈhɜːr.ʃəl/ (AmE), /ˈwɪl.jəm ˈhɜː.ʃəl/ (BrE)
