Lord's Resistance Army

proper noun
Ugandan rebel group noted for violence and abductions
Pronunciation: / lɔrdz rɪˈzɪstəns ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / lɔːdz rɪˈzɪstəns ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a Ugandan rebel group founded in the late 1980s, known for violent acts including terrorizing civilians, abducting children, and committing human rights abuses
