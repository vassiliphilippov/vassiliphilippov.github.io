diffractive

adjective
wave bending
9.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfræktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈfræktɪv / (BrE)
relating to or caused by the bending and spreading of waves, especially light or sound, around obstacles

Examples

A diffractive grating
A diffractive grating splits light into its component colors for scientific analysis.
The diffractive properties of the material made the sound waves bend around the barrier.
Engineers studied the diffractive effects to improve the design of the optical device.
