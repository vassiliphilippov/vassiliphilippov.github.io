hotfix

verb
to quickly fix software
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌfɪks / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌfɪks / (BrE)
to make a quick and temporary change to software in order to fix a fault or bug immediately

Examples

To hotfix a bug
The development team had to hotfix the app after users reported a critical error.
We will hotfix the login issue tonight to restore access for everyone.
After the update caused problems, they quickly hotfixed the software to resolve the bug.

Related Terms

  • antonym: break - to cause something to stop working
  • synonym: patch - to apply a small update to fix a software problem
  • synonym: repair - to fix something that is broken or not working
