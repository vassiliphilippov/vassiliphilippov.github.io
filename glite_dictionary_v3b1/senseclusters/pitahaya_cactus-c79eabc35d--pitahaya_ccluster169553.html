pitahaya cactus

noun
cactus with edible fruit from the Southwest US and Mexico
4.3
Pronunciation: / pɪtəˈhaɪ.ə ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / pɪtəˈhaɪ.ə ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
