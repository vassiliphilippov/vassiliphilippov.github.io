gerfalcon

noun
large rare Arctic falcon
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːˌfælkən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːˌfɔːlkən / (BrE)
a large and rare species of Arctic falcon, known for its white and dark plumage phases

Examples

A gerfalcon bird
A white gerfalcon flew low over the icy cliffs in search of prey.
Medieval falconers prized the rare gerfalcon for its strength and beauty.
The Arctic tundra is home to the magnificent gerfalcon during the breeding season.
