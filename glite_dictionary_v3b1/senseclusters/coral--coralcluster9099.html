coral

noun
unfertilized lobster roe used in cooking
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl / (BrE)
the unfertilized eggs (roe) of a lobster, which turn reddish when cooked and are used as a garnish or to color sauces

Examples

Lobster coral
Fresh coral from the lobster can be incorporated into rich butter sauces.
She reserved the coral to use as a garnish for the seafood platter.
The chef sprinkled coral over the dish to add both color and flavor.
