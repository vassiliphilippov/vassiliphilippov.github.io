last stand

noun
final effort to resist or defend
4.4
Pronunciation: / læst stænd / (AmE), / lɑːst stænd / (BrE)
a final, determined effort to resist or defend, especially when defeat seems inevitable

Examples

A last stand effort
She organized a last stand against the new policy changes at work.
The defenders made their last stand at the city walls, refusing to surrender.
The film tells the story of a regiment's last stand during a historic battle.
