sing

verb
emit a whining or ringing sound (of objects)
Pronunciation: / sɪŋ / (AmE), / sɪŋ / (BrE)
(of objects) to make a whining, ringing, or high-pitched whistling sound.

Examples

Kettle sings loudly
As the water reached boiling point, the kettle began to sing on the stove.
In the silent workshop, the saw sings as it slices through the wood.
The bullet sang through the air, barely missing his head.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: hum - make a low continuous sound
  • synonym: whistle - emit a high-pitched sound by blowing or moving rapidly
