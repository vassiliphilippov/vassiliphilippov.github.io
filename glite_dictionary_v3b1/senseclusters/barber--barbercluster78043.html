barber

verb
cut hair or beard professionally
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbə / (BrE)
to cut, trim, or style someone's hair or beard as a barber does

Examples

To barber a client
He used to barber for extra income when he was a student.
She learned how to barber at a young age from her father.
Some soldiers will barber their comrades between duties.
He has been barbering in the same shop for over twenty years.
After training, she started barbering professionally in a busy city salon.
During the festival, volunteers were barbering for free to help the community.
