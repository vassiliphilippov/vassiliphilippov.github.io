beck

noun
beckoning gesture to signal someone
5.7
Pronunciation: / bɛk / (AmE), / bɛk / (BrE)
a gesture made to summon or signal someone, typically by nodding or waving

Examples

A beck and call
He responded to the queen's slightest beck, never daring to delay.
She came at his beck without hesitation, always ready to help.
With a simple beck, the waiter approached their table.
