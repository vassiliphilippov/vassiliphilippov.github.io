foofaraw

noun
fuss over nothing
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːfəˌrɔ / (AmE), / ˈfuːfəˌrɔː /; / ˈfuːfəˌrɑː / (BrE)
a big fuss or a showy, unnecessary display about something unimportant
