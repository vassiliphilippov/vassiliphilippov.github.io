pulper

noun
machine for making pulp
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlpər / (AmE), / ˈpʌlpə / (BrE)
a machine or person that crushes or removes pulp from material, such as paper, fruit, or coffee cherries

Examples

A pulper machine
The factory uses a pulper to process recycled paper into new products.
Fruit is placed in the pulper to separate the juice from the seeds and skin.
A skilled worker operated the pulper to prepare the raw materials for papermaking.
The farm bought a new pulper to improve the efficiency of their coffee production.
After harvesting, the coffee cherries are fed into a pulper to remove the outer layers.
A well-maintained pulper can process thousands of cherries in a single day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crusher - a machine or person that crushes material into smaller pieces
  • synonym: de-pulper - a machine that removes pulp from fruit or seeds
  • synonym: grinder - a machine or person that grinds material into smaller pieces
  • synonym: huller - a machine that removes the outer covering from seeds or grains
