tinkly

adjective
making a light, high ringing sound
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋkli / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋkli / (BrE)
having a light, high, ringing sound like that of small bells or a gentle chime.

Examples

A tinkly bell
A tinkly bell signaled that the shop door had opened.
The music box played a tinkly tune that reminded me of childhood.
We heard the tinkly laughter of children in the garden.
