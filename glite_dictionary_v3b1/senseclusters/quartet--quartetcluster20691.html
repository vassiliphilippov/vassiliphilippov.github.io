quartet

noun
group of four people
8.3
Pronunciation: / kwɔrˈtɛt /; / kwɑrˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɔːˈtɛt /; / kwɑːˈtɛt / (BrE)
a group of four musicians, singers, or performers who work or perform together
