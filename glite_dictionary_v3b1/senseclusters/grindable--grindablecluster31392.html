grindable

adjective
suitable for grinding
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndəbəl / (BrE)
suitable for performing a grind trick, especially on a rail, ledge, or similar surface in skateboarding or snowboarding

Examples

Grindable rail
The new rail in the park is completely grindable for both skateboarders and inline skaters.
He searched for a grindable ledge to practice his tricks after school.
Not every surface is grindable, so always check before attempting a trick.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rough - having an uneven or coarse surface
  • synonym: slippery - smooth and easy to slide on
