forasmuch

conjunction
insofar as
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrəzˈmʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːrəzˈmʌtʃ / (BrE)
used to introduce a clause that limits or qualifies a statement, meaning 'insofar as' or 'to the extent that'.

Examples

Forasmuch as he could
He was forgiven, forasmuch as he had acted out of kindness.
The law applies, forasmuch as the circumstances are similar to previous cases.
She was grateful, forasmuch as her friends supported her during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: because - for the reason that; since
  • synonym: insofar as - to the extent that; as far as
