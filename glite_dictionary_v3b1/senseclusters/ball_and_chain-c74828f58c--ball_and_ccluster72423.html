ball and chain

noun
heavy iron restraint for prisoners
Pronunciation: / bɔl ənd tʃeɪn / (AmE), / bɔːl ənd tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a heavy iron ball attached to a prisoner's leg by a chain, used as a restraint or punishment

Examples

A ball and chain restraint
He learned about the ball and chain in his history class on nineteenth-century prisons.
In old movies, convicts are often depicted wearing a ball and chain as they work.
The prisoner shuffled along the corridor, dragging his ball and chain behind him.
