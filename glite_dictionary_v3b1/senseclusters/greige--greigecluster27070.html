greige

noun
unfinished fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪʒ / (AmE), / ɡreɪʒ / (BrE)
cloth or fabric in its natural, unbleached and unfinished state before any processing such as dyeing or finishing

Examples

A bolt of greige
The factory received several bolts of greige to be dyed in various colors.
Before printing patterns, the textile must be processed from its greige state.
She inspected the greige fabric for any flaws before sending it for finishing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: finished - processed and ready for use
  • synonym: raw - not processed or finished
