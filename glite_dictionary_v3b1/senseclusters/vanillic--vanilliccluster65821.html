vanillic

adjective
vanilla-like flavor
6.8
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɪlɪk / (AmE), / vəˈnɪlɪk / (BrE)
having the taste or aroma of vanilla; resembling vanilla in flavor or scent

Examples

Vanillic flavor
The chef added a vanillic note to the sauce for a subtle sweetness.
Her perfume had a soft, vanillic scent that lingered in the air.
We enjoyed the vanillic undertones in the freshly baked cookies.
