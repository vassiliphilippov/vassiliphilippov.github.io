surface noise

noun
background noise on vinyl records
2.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.fəs nɔɪz/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.fɪs nɔɪz/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.fəs nɔɪz/ /; / /ˈsɜː.fɪs nɔɪz/ (BrE)
