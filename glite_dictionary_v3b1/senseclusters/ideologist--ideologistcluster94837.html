ideologist

noun
a proponent of an ideology
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪdiˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who strongly supports or advocates a particular ideology, especially in politics or society

Examples

A ideologist in politics
As a committed ideologist, she regularly spoke at political rallies.
He is regarded as the chief ideologist of the party's reform movement.
The government's policy shift was driven by a group of young ideologists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ideologue - a person who zealously advocates an ideology
