olive oil

noun
oil made from pressed olives
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑlɪv ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil obtained by pressing olives, used in cooking, cosmetics, and as a salad dressing

Examples

Extra virgin olive oil
He drizzled extra virgin olive oil over the salad before serving.
Many Mediterranean dishes use olive oil as a key ingredient.
She prefers to cook her vegetables in olive oil for a healthier option.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: vegetable oil - any oil obtained from plant sources, especially seeds or nuts, used in cooking
