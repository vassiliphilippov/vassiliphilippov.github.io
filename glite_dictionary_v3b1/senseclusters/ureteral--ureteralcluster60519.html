ureteral

adjective
of the ureter
8.0
Pronunciation: / jʊˈriːtərəl / (AmE), / jʊəˈriːtərəl / (BrE)
relating to the ureter, the tube that carries urine from the kidney to the bladder

Examples

Ureteral stone
The doctor identified a ureteral stone that was blocking the flow of urine.
A ureteral stent was placed to help the patient recover after surgery.
Symptoms of a ureteral obstruction can include severe pain and difficulty urinating.
