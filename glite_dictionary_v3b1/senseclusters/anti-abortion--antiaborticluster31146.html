anti-abortion

noun
abortion opponent
Pronunciation: / ˌænti əˈbɔrʃən / (AmE), / ˌænti əˈbɔːʃən / (BrE)
a person who opposes abortion, especially someone who is active in campaigns or protests against it

Examples

An anti-abortion protester
The anti-abortion spoke passionately at the rally outside the courthouse.
Several anti-abortion activists organized a peaceful protest in the city center.
She debated an anti-abortion on television about women's reproductive rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-choice - supporting the right of women to choose whether to have an abortion
  • synonym: pro-life - supporting the view that abortion should be illegal or restricted
