Tungus

noun
member of a Tungusic Siberian people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
A member of a nomadic people of eastern Siberia who speak Tungusic languages and are related to the Manchu.

Examples

A Tungus person
A Tungus shaman performed rituals believed to heal the sick.
Many Tungus communities still maintain their unique cultural practices today.
The Tungus have traditionally lived by hunting and reindeer herding in Siberia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Manchu - A Tungusic people from northeast China, related to the Tungus
