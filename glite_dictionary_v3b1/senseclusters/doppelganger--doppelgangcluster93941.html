doppelganger

noun
look-alike person
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːpəlˌɡæŋər /; / ˈdɑpəlˌɡæŋər / (AmE), / ˈdɒpəlˌɡæŋə / (BrE)
a person who looks almost exactly like another person but is not related to them
