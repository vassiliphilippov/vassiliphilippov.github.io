up your own ass

adjective
extremely self-absorbed or self-important
6.9
Pronunciation: / ʌp jʊr oʊn æs / (AmE), / ʌp jɔːr əʊn ɑːs / (BrE)
(of a person) extremely self-absorbed or self-important, often to an annoying or arrogant degree.

Examples

Up his own ass attitude
He is so up his own ass that he can't see anyone else's point of view.
The manager's always up her own ass and never acknowledges the team's efforts.
Try not to be up your own ass during meetings—it puts people off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-absorbed - preoccupied with one's own feelings and interests
