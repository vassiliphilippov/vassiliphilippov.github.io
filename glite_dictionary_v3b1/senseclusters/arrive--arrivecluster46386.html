arrive

verb
achieve success or recognition
3.1
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪv / (AmE), / əˈraɪv / (BrE)
(informal) To achieve success or recognition; to reach the top in one's field.

Examples

Arrive in business
After years of hard work, she finally arrived in the business world.
He truly felt he had arrived when he received the industry award.
Young musicians often dream of the moment they will arrive on the international stage.
