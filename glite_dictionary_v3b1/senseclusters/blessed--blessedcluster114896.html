blessed

adjective
regarded as holy or sacred
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛsɪd / (AmE), / ˈblɛsɪd / (BrE)
regarded as holy or worthy of worship, especially in religious contexts; declared blessed by the church or enjoying heavenly bliss

Examples
