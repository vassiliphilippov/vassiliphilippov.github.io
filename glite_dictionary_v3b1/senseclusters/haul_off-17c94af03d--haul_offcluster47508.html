Synonyms

  • haul away
    3.4 0.45/m

  • cart away
    4.1 0.13/m

  • cart off
    5.9 0.27/m

  • carry off
    4.4 0.52/m
    carry off can mean removing something or succeeding at a task, while haul off mainly means taking things away by vehicle.
