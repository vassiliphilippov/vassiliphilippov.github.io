keyfob

noun
small ornament or device on key ring
4.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkiÀê f…ëb / (AmE), / ÀàkiÀê f…íb / (BrE)
A small ornament or device attached to a key ring or key chain, often for decorative or functional purposes, and designed to be easily handled.

Examples

A keyfob on keys
He attached his new keyfob to his house keys for easier identification.
She bought a souvenir keyfob shaped like the Eiffel Tower.
The car uses a remote keyfob to unlock the doors automatically.
