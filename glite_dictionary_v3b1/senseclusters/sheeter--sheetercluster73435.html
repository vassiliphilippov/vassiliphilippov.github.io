sheeter

noun
machine making sheets
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːtər / (AmE), / ˈʃiːtə / (BrE)
a machine or person that produces or forms material into sheets, such as paper or dough

Examples

A sheeter machine
The bakery uses a sheeter to roll out the dough evenly for pastries.
A paper sheeter cuts large rolls into individual sheets for packaging.
The new sheeter increased production speed in the factory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press - a machine that applies pressure to shape or form materials
  • synonym: roller - a machine or tool used to flatten or shape material
