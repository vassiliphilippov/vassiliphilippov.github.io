Little Dog

proper noun
constellation with Procyon (Canis Minor)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl dɒɡ / (BrE)
A constellation in the northern sky to the east of Orion, containing the star Procyon; also known as Canis Minor.

Examples

Little Dog constellation
Ancient astronomers named Little Dog for its proximity to the larger Canis Major.
Procyon is the brightest star in the Little Dog constellation.
The constellation Little Dog is best seen in the winter night sky.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Big Dog - the constellation Canis Major, adjacent to Little Dog
  • synonym: Canis Minor - the Latin name for the constellation Little Dog
