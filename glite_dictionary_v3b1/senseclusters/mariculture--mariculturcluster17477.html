mariculture

noun
marine farming
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.rɪ.kʌl.tʃər/ /; / /ˈmæ.rə.kʌl.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.rɪ.kʌl.tʃə/ /; / /ˈmæ.rə.kʌl.tʃə/ (BrE)

Examples

She is researching how mariculture can help meet the world's growing demand for seafood.
