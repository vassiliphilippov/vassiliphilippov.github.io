superiorly

adverb
in a better way
Pronunciation: / suːˈpɪriərli / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriəli / (BrE)
in a way that is better, more effective, or to a higher standard than others

Examples

Performed superiorly
The team played superiorly throughout the tournament and won the championship.
Her project was completed superiorly, impressing both her teachers and classmates.
He handled the difficult situation superiorly, showing great skill and confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excellently - in an outstanding or extremely good way
  • antonym: poorly - in a bad or unsatisfactory way
