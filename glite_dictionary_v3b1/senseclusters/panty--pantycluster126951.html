panty

noun
short underpants for women or children
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn.ti / (AmE), / ˈpæn.ti / (BrE)
a pair of short close-fitting underpants, typically worn by women or children

Examples

A panty set

That store sells every color and style of panty imaginable.
The little girl wore a pink panty under her dress.
