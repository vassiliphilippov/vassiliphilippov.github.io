ministate

noun
small self-governing body
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪn.ɪ.steɪt/ /; / /ˈmɪn.ə.steɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmɪn.ɪ.steɪt/ /; / /ˈmɪn.ə.steɪt/ (BrE)
a very small organization or institution that governs itself and controls many aspects of its members' lives, often used figuratively.

Examples

Club ministate
The club operated like a ministate, setting its own rules and regulations for members.
Some companies become a ministate by controlling every detail of their employees' work lives.
The boarding school was described as a ministate due to its strict self-governance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microstate - a very small independent country or state
  • degree higher: nation - a large group of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular country or territory
