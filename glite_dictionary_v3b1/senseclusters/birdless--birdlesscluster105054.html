birdless

adjective
without birds
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdləs / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdləs / (BrE)
having no birds present in a place or area

Examples

A birdless garden
The garden was strangely birdless after the storm had passed.
We walked through a birdless forest, noticing only the sound of the wind.
A birdless morning made the park feel unusually silent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bird-filled - full of birds; with many birds present
  • synonym: empty - containing nothing; not occupied
