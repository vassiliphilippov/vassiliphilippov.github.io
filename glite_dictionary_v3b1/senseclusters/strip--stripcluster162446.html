strip

noun
sequence of drawings telling a story
Pronunciation: / strɪp / (AmE), / strɪp / (BrE)
a sequence of drawings, often arranged in panels, that tells a story or conveys a joke, typically appearing in newspapers or comic books

Examples

A comic strip
As a child, she loved reading the Sunday strip with her father.
He created a popular strip about the adventures of a talking cat.
The newspaper features a daily strip that readers look forward to each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comic strip - a sequence of drawings in boxes that tell an amusing story, often printed in newspapers
