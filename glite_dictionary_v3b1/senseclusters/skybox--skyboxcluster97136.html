skybox

noun
cube-mapped background in 3D graphics
7.5
Pronunciation: / c8ska6accb51ks / (AmE), / c8ska6accb52ks / (BrE)
(in 3D computer graphics) a textured cube or set of images placed around a scene to represent distant sky or environment and create the illusion of vast surroundings.

Examples

3D skybox background
The engine renders a skybox to simulate distant mountains beyond the horizon.
To save performance, the team used a low-resolution skybox with blurred clouds and light.
When the camera moves, the skybox must remain fixed to preserve the illusion of distant scenery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cube map - a texture mapping technique using six square images to form the faces of a cube for environment mapping.
  • synonym: skydome - a dome-shaped background used in rendering to simulate a sky or distant environment.
