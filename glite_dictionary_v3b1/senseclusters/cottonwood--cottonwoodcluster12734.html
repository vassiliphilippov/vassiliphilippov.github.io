cottonwood

noun
tree (Allegheny region), American basswood
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑtnˌwʊd /; / ˈkɑːtnˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɒtnˌwʊd / (BrE)
a large North American tree, often with soft wood or seeds with cottony hairs

Examples

A large cottonwood tree

The cottonwood trees along the river provide valuable shade for hikers.
