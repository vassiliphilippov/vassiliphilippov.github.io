uncompetitive

adjective
unable to compete
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmˈpɛtətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈpɛtətɪv / (BrE)
not able to compete successfully or effectively in a particular situation or market

Examples

Uncompetitive prices
The company's outdated products made it uncompetitive in the market.
If our prices remain high, we will become uncompetitive compared to other brands.
The team was uncompetitive this season due to a lack of experienced players.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitive - able to compete successfully
  • synonym: ineffective - not producing the desired result
