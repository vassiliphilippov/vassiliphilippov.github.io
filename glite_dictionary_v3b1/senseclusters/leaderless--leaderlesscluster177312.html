leaderless

adjective
without a leader
Pronunciation: / ˈliːdərləs / (AmE), / ˈliːdələs / (BrE)
without a person in charge or guiding a group or organization

Examples

A leaderless team
After the sudden resignation, the team was left leaderless and uncertain about the next steps.
A leaderless movement can sometimes struggle to make decisions quickly.
The club remained leaderless until a new president was elected.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chaotic - in a state of complete disorder or confusion
  • antonym: organized - arranged or structured with clear leadership
  • synonym: unled - not being led or directed by anyone
