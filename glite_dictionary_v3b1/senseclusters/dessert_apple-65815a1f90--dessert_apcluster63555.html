dessert apple

noun
apple eaten raw, not cooked
8.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɝt ˌæpəl / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːt ˌæpəl / (BrE)
An apple variety grown and selected to be eaten raw, rather than used for cooking or cider.

Examples

Sweet dessert apples
Only dessert apples are selected for the fruit bowls in the hotel rooms.
She prefers a sweet dessert apple to a tart cooking apple when packing lunches.
This orchard is famous for its crisp dessert apples that are perfect for snacking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooking apple - an apple variety used primarily for cooking
