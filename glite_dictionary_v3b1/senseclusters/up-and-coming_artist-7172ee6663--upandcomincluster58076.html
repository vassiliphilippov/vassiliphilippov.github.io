up-and-coming artist

noun
emerging talent in the arts
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌp ən ˈkʌmɪŋ ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌp ən ˈkʌmɪŋ ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
