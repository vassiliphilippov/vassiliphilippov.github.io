autonomically

adverb
in a self-regulating way
7.4
Pronunciation: /ɑː.tə.ˈnɑː.mɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ɔː.tə.ˈnɑː.mɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /ɔː.tə.ˈnɒm.ɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /ɔː.tə.ˈnɒm.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
in a way that is self-managing or self-regulating, operating automatically without external control.

Examples

Modern cars can autonomically apply the brakes in emergency situations.
The software updates autonomically without needing user approval.
