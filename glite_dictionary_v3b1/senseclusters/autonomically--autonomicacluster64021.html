autonomically

adverb
in a self-regulating way
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈnɑmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is self-managing or self-regulating, operating automatically without external control.

Examples

System runs autonomically
The heating system adjusts autonomically to changes in the weather.
Modern cars can autonomically apply the brakes in emergency situations.
The software updates autonomically without needing user approval.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatically - in a way that happens by itself without human intervention
  • antonym: manually - in a way that requires human control or intervention
