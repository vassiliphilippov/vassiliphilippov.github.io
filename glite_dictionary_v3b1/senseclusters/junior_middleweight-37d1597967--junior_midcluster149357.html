junior middleweight

noun
boxing weight class up to 154 pounds
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːniər ˈmɪdəlˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːniə ˈmɪdəlweɪt / (BrE)
A boxer or wrestler in a weight division with an upper limit of 154 pounds (70 kg).

Examples

A junior middleweight boxer
After moving up from welterweight, he became a junior middleweight champion.
Many talented fighters compete in the junior middleweight division.
The boxer is planning to defend his junior middleweight title in Las Vegas next month.
