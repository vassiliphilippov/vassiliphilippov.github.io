Synonyms

  • distributor
    3.5 3/m
    distributor is the common modern spelling for a company that supplies shops and businesses, while distributer is a less common variant.
  • wholesaler
    3.5 1/m
    wholesaler buys large quantities from producers and sells to retailers, while distributer often refers to a firm that delivers goods to shops.
  • middleman
    3.4 0.6/m
    middleman buys and resells goods between producers and retailers for profit, while distributer usually means a company that regularly supplies shops.
  • jobber
    7.8 0.07/m
    jobber buys large batches from producers and supplies small retailers quickly, while distributer describes any company that supplies shops or businesses.
  • supplier
    2.7 10/m
    supplier provides goods or services to businesses or customers, while distributer usually means a firm that supplies shops specifically.
  • reseller
    3.7 0.5/m
    reseller buys goods to sell to others, sometimes adding support or services, while distributer mainly supplies shops and other businesses.
