lengthy

adjective
long in duration; prolonged
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛŋθi / (AmE), / ˈlɛŋθi / (BrE)
lasting for a long time, especially longer than is necessary; prolonged or tediously long.

Examples

A lengthy explanation
Despite his lengthy explanation, many people remained unconvinced.
She wrote a lengthy letter explaining her side of the story.
The meeting turned out to be quite a lengthy affair, lasting over three hours.
