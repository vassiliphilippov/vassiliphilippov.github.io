craze

verb
to make someone insane or frantic
4.6
Pronunciation: / kreɪz / (AmE), / kreɪz / (BrE)
to make (someone) insane or very agitated; to cause to lose one's mind

Examples

Jealousy crazes him
Jealousy can craze a person if left unchecked.
The endless noise would craze even the calmest person after a while.
The horrifying experience nearly crazed her with fear.
