Indic

adjective
of Indian subcontinent
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪk / (BrE)
relating to the languages, peoples, or cultures of the Indian subcontinent, especially those of Indo-Aryan origin

Examples

Indic culture
Sanskrit is considered an ancient Indic language with a rich literary tradition.
Many Indic scripts, such as Devanagari, are used across South Asia.
The Indic cultural influence can be seen in art, music, and literature throughout the region.
