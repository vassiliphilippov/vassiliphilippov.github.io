El Iskandriyah

proper noun
Arabic name for Alexandria, Egypt
Pronunciation: / ɛl ɪsˌkændɪˈriːjə / (AmE), / ɛl ɪsˌkændɪˈriːjə / (BrE)
The Arabic name for Alexandria, the principal port city of Egypt, situated on the Mediterranean coast and founded by Alexander the Great.
