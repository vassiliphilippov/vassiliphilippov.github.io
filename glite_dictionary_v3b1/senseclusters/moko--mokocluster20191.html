moko

noun
banana plant disease
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.koʊ/ /; / /ˈmɔː.koʊ/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.kəʊ/ /; / /ˈmɔː.kəʊ/ (BrE)
