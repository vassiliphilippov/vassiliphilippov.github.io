moko

noun
banana plant disease
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊkəʊ / (BrE)
a bacterial disease affecting banana and plantain plants, causing wilting, yellowing, and often plant death

Examples

Moko disease in bananas
The spread of moko devastated banana plantations across the region.
Farmers are learning new techniques to prevent moko from infecting their crops.
Once moko appears in a field, it can quickly destroy entire banana harvests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panama disease - a fungal disease that affects banana plants
  • synonym: wilt - a plant disease causing drooping and loss of firmness
