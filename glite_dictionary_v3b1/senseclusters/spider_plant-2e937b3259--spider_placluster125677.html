spider plant

noun
decorative plant with narrow leaves
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdɚ plænt / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant with long, narrow leaves and clusters of small white or pink-purple flowers, commonly grown as a houseplant.

Examples

A spider plant flower
After a few weeks, my spider plant started producing baby plants on long stems.
Many offices use the spider plant to improve air quality and add greenery to the workspace.
The spider plant is a popular choice for hanging baskets due to its graceful leaves.
