microclimate

noun
group atmosphere
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.klaɪ.mət/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.klaɪ.mət/ (BrE)
a set of social, cultural, or organizational conditions in a small group or place that influences behavior or attitudes

Examples

Office microclimate
The company’s unique microclimate encouraged creativity and open communication among employees.
A negative microclimate in the classroom can make students feel unwelcome or anxious.
Leaders should pay attention to the microclimate within their teams to promote a positive work environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: environment - the conditions that surround and affect a group or place
