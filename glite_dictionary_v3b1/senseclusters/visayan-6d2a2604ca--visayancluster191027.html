Visayan

noun
member of a major ethnic group in the Philippines
Pronunciation: / viˈsaɪən /; / bɪˈsaɪ.ən / (AmE), / vɪˈsaɪ.ən /; / bɪˈsaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a member of the largest indigenous ethnolinguistic group in the Philippines, originating from the Visayas region and also found in parts of Mindanao and Luzon.

Her family is Visayan, tracing their roots to the island of Panay.
Many Visayan people speak Cebuano as their first language.
