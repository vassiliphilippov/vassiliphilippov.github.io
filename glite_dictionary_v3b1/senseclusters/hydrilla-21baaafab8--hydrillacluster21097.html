Hydrilla

noun
aquatic plant genus, includes Hydrilla verticillata
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɪlə / (AmE), / hɪˈdrɪlə / (BrE)
a genus of aquatic plants in the frog's-bit family, with slender stems and whorls of narrow leaves, including the species Hydrilla verticillata
