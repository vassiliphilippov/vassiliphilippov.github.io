co-inventor

noun
joint inventor
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ ɪnˈvɛntər / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ ɪnˈvɛntə / (BrE)
a person who invents something together with one or more other people

Examples

The co-inventor of device
He was named as a co-inventor on the new medical device patent.
Each co-inventor contributed unique ideas to the final product.
The company recognized both scientists as co-inventors of the breakthrough technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaborator - a person who works with others on a project
  • synonym: inventor - a person who creates or designs something new
