stereotaxy

noun
3D surgical targeting
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛriəˌtæksi / (AmE), / ˈstɪəriəˌtæksi / (BrE)
a surgical or research technique that uses a three-dimensional coordinate system to precisely locate or manipulate targets within the brain
