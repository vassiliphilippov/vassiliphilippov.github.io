Sison amomum

noun
a Mediterranean aromatic herb with parsley-like leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪsən əˈmoʊməm / (AmE), / ˈsɪsən əˈməʊməm / (BrE)
a slender herb native to western Europe and the Mediterranean, with parsley-like leaves, white flowers, and aromatic seeds
