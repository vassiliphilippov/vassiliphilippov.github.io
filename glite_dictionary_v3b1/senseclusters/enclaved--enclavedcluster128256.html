enclaved

adjective
completely surrounded
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈkleɪvd / (AmE), / ɛnˈkleɪvd / (BrE)
completely surrounded by a different area, group, or environment

Examples

A enclaved village
The village is enclaved by mountains, making access difficult.
Several ethnic groups remain enclaved within the borders of the larger country.
The park is enclaved in the middle of a busy urban area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclosed - surrounded or closed off on all sides
  • synonym: isolated - separated from others or alone
