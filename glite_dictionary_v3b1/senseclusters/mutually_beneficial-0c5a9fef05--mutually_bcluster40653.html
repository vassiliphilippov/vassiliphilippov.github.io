mutually beneficial

adjective
advantageous to both sides
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːtʃuəli bɛˈnɪfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtjuəli bɪˈnɪfɪʃəl / (BrE)
(of an arrangement, relationship, or agreement) advantageous or helpful to all parties involved; providing benefits for each side.
