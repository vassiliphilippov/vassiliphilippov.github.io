hedge one's bets

verb
protect against risk by multiple options
8.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /hedʒ wʌnz bets/ /; / /hedʒ wʌns bets/ (AmE), /hedʒ wʌnz bets/ /; / /hedʒ wʌns bets/ (BrE)
