Synonyms

  • Erastianism
    9.9 0.01/m
    Erastianism says the state runs church affairs through law and courts, while Caesaropapism usually means the ruler also serves as church head.
  • Byzantinism
    9.9 0.04/m
    Byzantinism describes the historical practice of state control in the Byzantine Empire, while Caesaropapism names general ruler control over church matters.
