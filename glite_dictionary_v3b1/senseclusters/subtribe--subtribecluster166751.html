subtribe

noun
smaller tribal group
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌtraɪb / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌtraɪb / (BrE)
a smaller group within a tribe, often having its own leader or customs

Examples

A subtribe leader
The subtribe developed unique rituals that differed from those of the main tribe.
Each subtribe elected its own leader to represent their interests at the council.
Anthropologists studied how the subtribe maintained its identity within the larger tribal community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clan - a group of families within a tribe sharing a common ancestor
  • degree higher: tribe - a large social group made up of families or communities
