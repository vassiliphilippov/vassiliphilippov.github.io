Plesiosauria

noun
order of extinct marine reptiles
9.9
Pronunciation: /pliː.si.ə.ˈsɑːr.i.ə/ /; / /pliː.si.ə.ˈsɔːr.i.ə/ (AmE), /pliː.si.ə.ˈsɔːr.i.ə/ (BrE)
