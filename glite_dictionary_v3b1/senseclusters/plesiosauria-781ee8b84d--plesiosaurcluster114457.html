Plesiosauria

noun
order of extinct marine reptiles
9.9
Pronunciation: / pliːzi.oʊˈsɔːri.ə /; / pliːsi.oʊˈsɔːri.ə / (AmE), / pliːzɪəˈsɔːrɪə /; / pliːsɪ.əˈsɔːri.ə / (BrE)
an order of extinct large marine reptiles with long necks, broad bodies, and flippers that lived during the Mesozoic Era

The fossils of the Plesiosauria reveal much about ancient marine life.
