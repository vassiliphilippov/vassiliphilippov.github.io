duiker

noun
water channel under road
a pipe or small channel that allows water to pass under a road, railway, or embankment; a culvert

Examples

Road duiker
The heavy rain flooded the field because the duiker beneath the road was blocked by debris.
Engineers inspected the duiker to ensure water could flow freely under the railway.
A new duiker was installed to prevent flooding during the rainy season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: culvert - a structure that allows water to flow under a road or railway
  • synonym: drain - a pipe or channel that carries away water
