spiral bandage

noun
bandage wrapped in overlapping spirals
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪrəl ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəl ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
A type of bandage applied so that each turn overlaps the previous turn in a spiral fashion, used to cover cylindrical body parts.

Examples

A spiral bandage wrap
A spiral bandage is ideal for wrapping areas like the forearm or calf.
The doctor demonstrated the proper way to use a spiral bandage during first aid training.
The nurse skillfully applied a spiral bandage to the patient's injured arm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandage - a strip of material used to bind a wound or protect an injured part of the body
