pepper family

noun
family of aromatic tropical plants (Piperaceae)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpɚ ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə ˈfæməli / (BrE)
A family of tropical woody vines and herbaceous plants, known for aromatic herbage and small flowers in spikelets, including species like black pepper.
