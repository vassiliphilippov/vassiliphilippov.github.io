ronde

noun
archaic song form
Pronunciation: / rɒnd / (AmE), / rɒnd / (BrE)
an old type of short song or poem with a repeating refrain, often rustic or simple in style

Examples

Medieval ronde song
The minstrel sang a cheerful ronde that everyone joined, clapping to the refrain.
In medieval times, villagers would gather to perform a lively ronde at festivals.
The poet composed a ronde to celebrate the arrival of spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballad - a narrative poem or song
  • synonym: roundelay - a short simple song with a recurring refrain
