bald

noun
treeless mountain area
Pronunciation: / bɔld / (AmE), / bɔːld / (BrE)
a treeless, grassy area on the summit or upper slopes of a mountain, especially in the southern United States

Examples

A mountain bald
The hikers stopped to rest on a bald with a clear view of the valley below.
Wildflowers covered the bald at the top of the mountain in early summer.
From the bald, we could see for miles in every direction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forest - a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth
  • synonym: meadow - an open field of grass, often used for hay or pasture
