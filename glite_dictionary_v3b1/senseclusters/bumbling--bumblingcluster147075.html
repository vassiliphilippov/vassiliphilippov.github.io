bumbling

noun
clumsy actions
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʌmblɪŋ / (BrE)
clumsy or awkward behavior that often leads to mistakes or confusion

Examples

His bumbling in class
His constant bumbling in the kitchen resulted in a big mess every time he cooked.
The team's bumbling during the game cost them several easy points.
Despite her bumbling, everyone found her attempts to help endearing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blundering - making careless or foolish mistakes
  • antonym: gracefulness - elegant and smooth movement or behavior
