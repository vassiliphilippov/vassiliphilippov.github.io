potently

adverb
in a powerful or influential manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊtəntli / (AmE), / ˈpəʊtəntli / (BrE)
In a way that has a powerful effect or influence.

Examples

To potently affect
His arguments were potently persuasive, changing many minds in the room.
She spoke potently about her experiences, leaving a lasting impression on the audience.
The new drug acts potently to reduce inflammation in patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: powerfully - in a strong or forceful manner
  • antonym: weakly - in a manner lacking strength or power
