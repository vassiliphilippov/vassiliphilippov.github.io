ovulation method of family planning

noun
fertility awareness by tracking cervical mucus
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvjəˈleɪʃən ˈmɛθəd əv ˈfæməli ˈplænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvjʊˈleɪʃn ˈmɛθəd ɒv ˈfæməli ˈplænɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of natural family planning in which a woman's fertile period is identified by observing changes in cervical mucus, which becomes clearer and more slippery around ovulation
