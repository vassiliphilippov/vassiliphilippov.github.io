horse

noun
framework for holding wood while sawing
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɔrs / (AmE), / hɔːs / (BrE)
a framework for supporting wood or other material that is being cut or worked on, especially by sawing; a sawhorse.

Examples

Wood horse
A horse is essential equipment for any woodworking project.
He placed the wooden plank on the horse before starting to saw.
The carpenter set up two horses to support the beams during cutting.
