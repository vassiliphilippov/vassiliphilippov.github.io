lustily

adverb
with vigor and healthy energy
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌstɪli / (AmE), / ˈlʌstɪli / (BrE)
in a vigorous and healthy way; with robust energy and enthusiasm.

Examples

To sing lustily
The campfire crackled as the scouts ate lustily after a long hike.
The children sang lustily and brightened up the whole room.
The young plants grew lustily in the warm spring sunshine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vigorously - with force and energy
  • antonym: weakly - in a weak manner
