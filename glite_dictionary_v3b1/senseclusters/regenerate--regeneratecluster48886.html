regenerate

verb
restore strength or energy
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt /; / rɪˈdʒɛnəreɪt / (BrE)
to restore or renew strength, energy, or vitality (physically or mentally)

Examples

To regenerate your energy
A good night's sleep can regenerate your energy after a long week.
Fresh air and healthy food helped to regenerate the exhausted hiker.
The spa promised to regenerate both body and mind through relaxation treatments.
A good night's sleep can help you regenerate after a stressful day.
Regular exercise gives me the energy to regenerate and stay healthy.
She went on vacation to regenerate her mind and body.
