channeling

noun
imitating another
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃænəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃænəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of intentionally imitating or expressing the style, behavior, or qualities of another person, character, or era

He was channeling the energy of a 1980s rock star with his bold outfit.
In her speech, the politician’s channeling of past leaders energized the crowd.
