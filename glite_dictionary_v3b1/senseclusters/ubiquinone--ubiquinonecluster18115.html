ubiquinone

noun
cellular respiration coenzyme (quinone)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːbɪˈkwɪnoʊn / (AmE), / ˌjuːbɪˈkwɪnəʊn / (BrE)
a quinone compound found in living cells, acting as a coenzyme in cellular respiration by transferring electrons between molecules
