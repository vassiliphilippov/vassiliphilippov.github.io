scutch

noun
fiber-separating tool
Pronunciation: / skʌtʃ / (AmE), / skʌtʃ / (BrE)
a tool or process used to separate fibers from plant stems or to break up stone in certain industries

Examples

A scutch tool
The worker used a scutch to prepare the flax for spinning.
After retting, the fibers were separated with a scutch before weaving.
In stone masonry, a scutch is used to chip away rough surfaces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break - a tool or process for breaking flax stems
  • synonym: hackle - a tool for combing and separating fibers
