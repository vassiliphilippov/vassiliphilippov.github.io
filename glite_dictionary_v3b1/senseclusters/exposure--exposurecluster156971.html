Examples

Exposure of secrets
After the exposure of the company's illegal activities, several employees were fired.
The exposure of the politician's financial records shocked the public.
They were worried that the exposure of their plans would ruin the deal.
His sudden exposure as the author of the anonymous letters surprised the entire community.

Synonyms

  • disclosure
    4.1 13/m
    disclosure is formally telling private facts to others, while exposure means making something hidden visible to the public, often suddenly.
  • revelation
    5.0 11/m
    revelation is the sudden sharing of surprising or new facts, while exposure is the general act of making hidden things known.
