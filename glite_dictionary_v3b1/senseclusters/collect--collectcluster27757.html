collect

verb
gather items or bring together
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛkt / (AmE), / kəˈlɛkt / (BrE)
to gather or accumulate items as a hobby or systematically, such as coins, stamps, or signatures

Examples

To collect stamps

We should collect our thoughts before making a decision.
He collected signatures for the petition after the meeting.
She loves to collect antique coins from around the world.
The museum plans to collect paintings from local artists for the new exhibition.
