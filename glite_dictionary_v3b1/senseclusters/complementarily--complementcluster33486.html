complementarily

adverb
in a completing way
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmpləˌmɛnˈtɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌkɒmplɪˌmɛntəˈreɪli / (BrE)

Examples

Work complementarily together

Each artist contributed complementarily to the mural, making it a true collaboration.
The flavors in the dish work complementarily, creating a balanced and delicious meal.
