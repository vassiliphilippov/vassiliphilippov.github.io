jumby tree

noun
West Indian tree with purple flowers and spotted seeds
9.9 9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌm.bi tri / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌm.bi triː / (BrE)
A small tree native to the West Indies and northeastern Venezuela, with large oblong pointed leaflets, panicles of purple flowers, and black or scarlet seeds with black spots.
