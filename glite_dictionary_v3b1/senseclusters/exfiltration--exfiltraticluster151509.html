exfiltration

noun
secret data transfer
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛksfɪlˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛksfɪlˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of secretly transferring data or information out of a secure area or system

Examples

Exfiltration of data
The security team discovered exfiltration of confidential documents during their investigation.
Cybercriminals often use malware to enable the exfiltration of sensitive data from corporate networks.
Strict monitoring can help prevent the exfiltration of valuable information from secure systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extraction - the act of removing something, especially by force
  • antonym: infiltration - the act of secretly entering or gaining access to a place or system
