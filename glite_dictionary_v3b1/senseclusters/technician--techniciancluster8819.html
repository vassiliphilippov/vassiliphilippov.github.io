technician

noun
person skilled in a technique
Pronunciation: / tɛkˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / tɛkˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled in a particular intellectual or artistic technique

Examples

A skilled technician
Although he is a great artist, critics say he is more of a technician than a visionary.
She is a technician at the piano, impressing audiences with her flawless performances.
The laboratory hired a technician known for his skill in delicate procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - a person who engages in a pursuit, especially a sport, on an unpaid basis or without professional skill
  • synonym: expert - a person who has a comprehensive and authoritative knowledge of or skill in a particular area
