deadfall

noun
animal trap with falling weight
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈdɛdˌfɔːl / (BrE)
a trap for catching animals that uses a heavy object to fall and trap or kill them

Examples

Animal deadfall trap
The hunter checked the deadfall he had set the night before.
Building a simple deadfall requires only a few sticks and a heavy rock.
Many trappers use a deadfall to catch small animals in the wild.
The hunter set a deadfall near the animal trail to catch small game.
Old survival guides often describe how to build a simple deadfall using logs and sticks.
After a night in the forest, he found a rabbit caught in his deadfall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitfall - a concealed hole used as a trap for animals
  • synonym: snare - a trap that catches animals by a noose
  • synonym: trap - a device for catching animals
