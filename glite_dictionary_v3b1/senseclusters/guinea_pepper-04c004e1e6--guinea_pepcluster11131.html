Guinea pepper

noun
West African plant and spice
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪni ˌpɛpər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪni ˌpɛpə / (BrE)
a West African plant (Aframomum melegueta) whose pungent seeds are used as a spice and in traditional medicine
