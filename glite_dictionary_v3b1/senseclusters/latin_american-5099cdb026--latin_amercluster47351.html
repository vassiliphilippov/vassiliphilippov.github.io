Latin American

noun
a person from Latin America
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌlætn əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌlatɪn əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
a person who was born in, or comes from, Latin America.

Examples

A Latin American person

There has been a rise in the number of Latin Americans living in the city.
