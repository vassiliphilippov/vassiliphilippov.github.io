buttress

verb
support or reinforce with a buttress
9.9 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtrəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtrəs / (BrE)
to support or reinforce a structure with a projecting support built against its wall

Examples

To buttress a building
Engineers decided to buttress the bridge to prevent collapse during storms.
Massive stone supports were added to buttress the cathedral walls.
They had to buttress the old library to ensure its structural safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: support - to bear the weight of or uphold
  • antonym: undermine - to weaken or damage, often gradually
