offertory

noun
part of a church service for offering bread and wine
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfɚˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈɒfətəri / (BrE)
the part of a Christian service, especially the Eucharist, during which offerings of bread and wine are presented to God

Examples

Offertory of bread and wine
During the offertory, choir members sang a hymn as the bread and wine were placed on the altar.
Some churches collect donations from parishioners at the offertory.
The priest explained the significance of the offertory to the congregation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eucharist - the Christian ceremony commemorating the Last Supper
