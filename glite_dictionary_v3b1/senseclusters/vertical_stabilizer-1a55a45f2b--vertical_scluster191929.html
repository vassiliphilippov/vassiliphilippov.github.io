vertical stabilizer

noun
upright tail fin of an airplane
5.0 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝː.tɪ.kəl ˈsteɪ.bə.laɪ.zɚ /; / ˈvɜːrtɪkəl ˈsteɪbɪˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tɪ.kəl ˈsteɪ.bɪ.laɪ.zə /; / ˈvɜːtɪkəl ˈstæbɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
the upright part of an airplane's tail that helps to keep the aircraft stable and maintain its direction during flight
