Synonyms

  • vertical fin
    4.3 0.02/m
    vertical fin is the vertical surface on an airplane's tail that keeps direction steady, while vertical stabilizer is the common technical name used by engineers.
  • tail fin
    4.2 0.06/m
    tail fin is the vertical surface at the back of a plane that helps keep it steady in flight, while vertical stabilizer is the formal technical term.
  • vertical tail
    6.0 0.02/m
    vertical tail is the whole vertical part of a plane's tail assembly and can include the rudder, while vertical stabilizer names the specific stabilizing surface.
