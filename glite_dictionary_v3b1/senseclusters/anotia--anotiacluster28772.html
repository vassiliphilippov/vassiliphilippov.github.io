anotia

noun
absence of external ear
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnoʊʃə / (AmE), / əˈnəʊtiə / (BrE)
a birth defect in which the external ear is completely absent

Examples

Anotia birth defect
The newborn was diagnosed with anotia after doctors noticed the absence of both external ears.
Surgical reconstruction is sometimes considered for children born with anotia to improve appearance and hearing.
Genetic counseling may be recommended for families with a history of anotia or other ear malformations.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: microtia - a birth defect where the external ear is underdeveloped
