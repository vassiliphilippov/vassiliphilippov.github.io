femtovolt

noun
one quadrillionth of a volt
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛmtoʊˌvoʊlt / (AmE), / ˈfɛm.təʊˌvəʊlt / (BrE)
A unit of electric potential equal to one quadrillionth (10⁻¹⁵) of a volt.

Examples

A femtovolt unit
In certain sensitive experiments, voltages as small as a few femtovolts can be significant.
Noise measurements sometimes require instrumentation capable of resolving fractions of a femtovolt.
The device is designed to detect changes as low as one femtovolt.
