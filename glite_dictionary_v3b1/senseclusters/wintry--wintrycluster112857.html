wintry

adjective
cold or unfriendly in manner
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntri / (AmE), / ˈwɪntri / (BrE)
(of a manner, expression, or atmosphere) lacking warmth or friendliness; cold or unfriendly.

Examples

Wintry smile
Despite her wintry manner, she agreed to help with the project.
His wintry tone made it clear he was not pleased with the news.
She greeted him with a wintry smile that revealed her true feelings.
