numerate

adjective
able to understand and use numbers
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.məˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.məˌreɪt / (BrE)
able to understand and work with numbers, especially in terms of performing basic mathematical calculations and reasoning

Examples

To be numerate
Employers today expect new recruits to be numerate and to have strong problem-solving skills.
She became more numerate after taking extra math classes during the summer.
The school focuses on making children both literate and numerate by the time they are seven years old.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innumerate - unable to understand or use numbers
