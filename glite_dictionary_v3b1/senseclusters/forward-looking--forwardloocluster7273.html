forward-looking

adjective
thinking/planning for the future
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərd ˌlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwəd ˌlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
thinking about and planning for the future with an emphasis on innovation and progress; ahead of the times.

Examples

Forward-looking leader
Government policies should be more forward-looking to address future challenges.
She is known for her forward-looking ideas in the field of education.
The company adopted a forward-looking strategy to stay ahead of competitors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - holding to traditional values, resistant to change
  • synonym: progressive - favoring or implementing change, progress, or reform
