cast

verb
stream media wirelessly to another device
3.9
Pronunciation: /kæst/ (AmE), /kɑːst/ (BrE)
to send or stream audio or video wirelessly from one device (such as a phone or computer) to another device (such as a TV or speaker) for immediate playback.

Examples

To cast a video
She cast the video from her phone to the living room TV an hour ago.
During the meeting he began casting his laptop screen to the projector for everyone to see.
If the speaker is compatible, you can cast audio from the app to any nearby smart speaker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mirror - to display the screen of one device on another in real time
  • synonym: stream - to transmit audio or video data over a network so it can be played immediately
