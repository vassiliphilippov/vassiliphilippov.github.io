dispatchable

adjective
ready for execution
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspætʃəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈspætʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be scheduled or assigned for execution, especially by a computer system or process scheduler.

Examples

Dispatchable process
The operating system marked the process as dispatchable once all its resources were available.
Only dispatchable tasks are considered by the scheduler for immediate execution.
After resolving its dependencies, the thread became dispatchable and was quickly run.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pending - awaiting action or decision
  • synonym: schedulable - able to be scheduled for execution
