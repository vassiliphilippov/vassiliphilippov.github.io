psychographically

adverb
relating to psychography
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to psychography, especially automatic writing produced by a medium or the unconscious mind

Examples

Psychographically written message
The message appeared psychographically on the page as the medium entered a trance.
She claimed to have written the letter psychographically, with no conscious awareness of the words.
During the session, the words were produced psychographically through her hand's movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatically - in a way that happens without conscious thought
