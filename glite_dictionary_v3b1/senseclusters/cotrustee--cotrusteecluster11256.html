cotrustee

noun
joint trust manager
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊtrʌˈstiː / (AmE), / ˌkəʊtrʌˈstiː / (BrE)
a person who shares the responsibility of managing a trust with one or more other trustees

Examples

Each cotrustee agrees
Each cotrustee must agree before making changes to the trust.
The will named two siblings as cotrustees to oversee the family estate.
If a cotrustee resigns, the remaining trustees continue to manage the trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trustee - a person or organization that manages a trust
