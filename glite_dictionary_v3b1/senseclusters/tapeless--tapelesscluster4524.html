tapeless

adjective
without adhesive tape
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈteɪpləs / (BrE)
not using or requiring adhesive tape to fasten, seal, or attach something

Examples

Tapeless bandages
The clinic switched to tapeless dressings to make changing bandages easier for patients.
Many modern diapers are now tapeless, using fasteners instead of adhesive tape.
He preferred tapeless packaging because it was quicker to open and recycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-adhesive - able to stick to surfaces without additional glue or tape
  • antonym: taped - fastened or sealed using adhesive tape
