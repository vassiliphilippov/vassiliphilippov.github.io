persuasibility

noun
easily convinced
Pronunciation: / pərˌsweɪzəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / pəˌsweɪzəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easily convinced or influenced by others

Examples

High persuasibility
Her persuasibility made her open to new ideas and suggestions from her colleagues.
The advertisement targeted people with high persuasibility to increase sales.
Because of his persuasibility, he often changed his mind after hearing different opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influence - the power to affect how someone thinks or behaves
  • antonym: resistance - the ability to refuse to be influenced or changed
  • synonym: susceptibility - the state of being easily affected or influenced
