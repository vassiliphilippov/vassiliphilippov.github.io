hairsplitting

adjective
paying undue attention to fine distinctions
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌsplɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌsplɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
concerned with unreasonably or excessively fine details or distinctions, especially in argument or analysis.

Examples

Hairsplitting details
Legal arguments can sometimes become unnecessarily hairsplitting and pedantic.
She criticized his hairsplitting analysis as missing the main point.
The debate quickly descended into hairsplitting over trivial definitions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nitpicking - giving too much attention to unimportant details; overly critical attention to minor details
  • synonym: pedantic - overly concerned with minute details or formalisms, especially in teaching
