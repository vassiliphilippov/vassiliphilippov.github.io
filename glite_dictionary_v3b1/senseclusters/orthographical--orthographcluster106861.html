orthographical

adjective
about correct spelling
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the correct or conventional spelling of words

Examples

Orthographical error
The teacher corrected all the orthographical errors in the students' essays.
Her report was well-written but had a few orthographical inconsistencies.
Proofreading helps to eliminate orthographical mistakes before submitting your work.

Related Terms
