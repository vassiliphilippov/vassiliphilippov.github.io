Clintonia

noun
a genus of plants in the lily family
Pronunciation: / klɪnˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / klɪnˈtəʊnɪə / (BrE)
A genus of perennial herbaceous plants in the lily family, native to North America and eastern Asia, known for their broad leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers.
