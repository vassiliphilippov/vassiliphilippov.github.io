gum

noun
wood from a gum tree
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm / (AmE), / ɡʌm / (BrE)
wood or timber obtained from any of various gum trees, especially the sweet gum.

Examples

Gum wood furniture
After the storm, several logs of gum were delivered to the sawmill.
Many furniture makers prefer gum due to its durability and appealing grain.
The cabinets were made from polished gum for a smooth and attractive finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweet gum - deciduous North American tree valued for its hardwood and resin
