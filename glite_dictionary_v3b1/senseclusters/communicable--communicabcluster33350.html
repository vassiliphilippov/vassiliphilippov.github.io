communicable

adjective
easily transmitted or shared
5.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪkəbəl / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkəbl / (BrE)
(of ideas, information, or qualities) able to be easily transmitted, shared, or imparted to others.

Examples

Communicable ideas
Clear language ensures that your message is communicable across cultures.
Her enthusiasm made even abstract concepts communicable to the audience.
The professor presented complex theories in a highly communicable manner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incommunicable - not able to be communicated or shared
