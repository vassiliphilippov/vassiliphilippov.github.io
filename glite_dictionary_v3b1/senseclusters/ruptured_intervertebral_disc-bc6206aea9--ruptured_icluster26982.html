ruptured intervertebral disc

noun
tear of spinal disc causing pain
6.1 5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌptʃɚd ˌɪntɚˈvɝːtəbrəl dɪsk / (AmE), / ˈrʌptʃəd ˌɪntəˈvɜːtɪbrəl dɪsk / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the disc between the vertebrae of the spine tears, often resulting in back pain and nerve problems

Examples

A ruptured intervertebral disc pain
A ruptured intervertebral disc can cause numbness or weakness in the legs if it presses on the nerves.
After surgery for his ruptured intervertebral disc, she was finally able to walk without discomfort.
He was diagnosed with a ruptured intervertebral disc after experiencing severe lower back pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herniated disc - a similar condition where the spinal disc bulges or herniates
  • synonym: slipped disc - another term for herniated or ruptured disc
