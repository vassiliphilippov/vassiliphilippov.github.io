run

noun
a small natural stream
8.8
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
a small natural stream, especially one that flows quickly through a narrow channel

Examples

A mountain run
Many trout can be found in the clear waters of this mountain run.
The children played by the run that flowed behind their house.
We set up our picnic near a quiet run in the woods.
