pail

noun
a round container with a handle; a bucket
4.8
Pronunciation: / peɪl / (AmE), / peɪl / (BrE)
A round, usually metal or plastic container with high sides and a handle, used for carrying liquids or other materials; a bucket.

Examples

A metal pail
Please rinse the mop in the pail before using it again.
She filled the pail with water from the well.
The children carried sand in a pail to build their sandcastle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bucket - an open container with a handle, typically used for carrying liquids or other substances
