sockpuppet

verb
to use fake identity online
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑkˌpʌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒkˌpʌpɪt / (BrE)
to create or use a false online identity to deceive or manipulate others, especially in discussions or forums

Examples

To sockpuppet a forum
Some users tried to sockpuppet the forum to make their opinions seem more popular.
It is against the rules to sockpuppet during online voting or discussions.
The moderators quickly discovered that he was attempting to sockpuppet the conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deceive - to make someone believe something that is not true
  • antonym: expose - to reveal the true identity or facts
  • synonym: impersonate - to pretend to be someone else
