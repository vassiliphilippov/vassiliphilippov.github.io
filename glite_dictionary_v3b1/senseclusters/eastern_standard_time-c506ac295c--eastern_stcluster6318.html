Eastern Standard Time

noun
time zone for eastern US (GMT-5)
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstɚn ˈstændɚd taɪm / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈstændəd taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time of the eastern part of the United States, five hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-5)

Examples
