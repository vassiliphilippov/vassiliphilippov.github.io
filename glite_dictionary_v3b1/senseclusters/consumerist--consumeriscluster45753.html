consumerist

noun
consumer rights advocate
5.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈsuːmərɪst / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmərɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or promotes the protection of consumers' rights and interests

Examples

A consumerist protects rights
The consumerist spoke at the conference about the need for stricter product safety regulations.
As a dedicated consumerist, she regularly reviews companies' practices to ensure fairness for buyers.
A well-known consumerist launched a campaign to improve food labeling standards.

  • synonym: consumer advocate - a person who works to protect consumer rights
