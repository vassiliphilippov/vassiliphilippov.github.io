strip

noun
airfield with minimal facilities
6.7
Pronunciation: / strɪp / (AmE), / strɪp / (BrE)
an airfield with minimal or no airport facilities, often used for temporary or emergency landing and takeoff

Examples

A landing strip
Several old planes were parked along the makeshift strip near the village.
The military constructed a temporary strip to support airborne operations.
The pilot made an emergency landing on a remote strip in the desert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airfield - an area of land used for the takeoff, landing, and maintenance of aircraft
  • degree higher: airport - a complex for the arrival, departure, and maintenance of aircraft with facilities for passengers
