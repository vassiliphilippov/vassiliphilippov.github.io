culture shock

noun
feeling of disorientation in a new culture
2.4 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.tʃər ʃɑːk/ /; / /ˈkʌl.tʃər ʃɔːk/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.tʃə ʃɒk/ (BrE)
the feeling of confusion and anxiety experienced by someone who is suddenly exposed to a different culture or way of life

Examples

Experience culture shock
After moving to Japan, Maria experienced intense culture shock during her first few weeks.
His struggle with culture shock affected both his studies and social life.
Many exchange students go through culture shock when they arrive in a new country.
