duty period

noun
period a person is officially at work
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈduːti ˈpɪriəd / (AmE), / ˈdjuːti ˈpɪəriəd / (BrE)
the span of time during which a person is officially on duty at work

Examples

The duty period at work
During my duty period, I am responsible for monitoring patient care.
Strict regulations limit the maximum length of a truck driver's duty period.
The pilot's duty period was extended due to flight delays.
