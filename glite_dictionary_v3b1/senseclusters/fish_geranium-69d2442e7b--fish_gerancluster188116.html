fish geranium

noun
upright geranium with zoned leaves and colorful flowers
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ dʒəˈreɪniəm / (AmE), / fɪʃ dʒəˈreɪnɪəm / (BrE)
an upright geranium with scalloped leaves marked by a broad color zone and bearing white, pink, or red flowers.

Examples

A fish geranium plant
My grandmother grows a beautiful fish geranium in her garden every spring.
The fish geranium is prized for its colorful leaves and vibrant flowers.
We planted several fish geraniums along the walkway to brighten up the path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zonal geranium - type of geranium with zones or bands of color on the leaves
