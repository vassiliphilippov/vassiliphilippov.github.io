neuroblast

noun
precursor cell for a nerve cell
Pronunciation: / ˈnʊrəˌblæst / (AmE), / ˈnjʊərəʊˌblɑːst / (BrE)
a cell in the early stages of development from which a mature nerve cell arises

Examples

A neuroblast cell
A tumor formed from neuroblast cells is known as a neuroblastoma.
Scientists study how a neuroblast differentiates into a functioning neuron.
The neuroblast divides rapidly during embryonic development.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: neuron - a specialized cell transmitting nerve impulses; a nerve cell
