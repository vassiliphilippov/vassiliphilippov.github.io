leukemia

noun
cancer of blood-forming tissues
1.8 3.8
Pronunciation: /luː.ˈkiː.miː.ə/ /; / /luː.ˈkiː.mɪ.ə/ (AmE), /luː.ˈkiː.miː.ə/ /; / /luː.ˈkiː.mɪ.ə/ (BrE)
