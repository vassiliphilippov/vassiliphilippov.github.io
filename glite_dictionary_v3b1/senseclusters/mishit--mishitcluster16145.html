mishit

noun
badly hit shot
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈhɪt / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈhɪt / (BrE)
a shot, strike, or blow that is made incorrectly or poorly, especially in sports.

Examples

A tennis mishit
The tennis player's mishit sent the ball flying out of bounds.
A mishit in golf can ruin an otherwise perfect round.
She was frustrated after her mishit cost the team a crucial point.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miss - a failure to hit the target
  • antonym: strike - a successful hit or blow
