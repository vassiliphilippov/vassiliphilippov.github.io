armor-plated

adjective
covered with heavy protective armor
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmɚ ˌpleɪtɪd /; / ˈɑːrmər ˌpleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmə ˌpleɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or object) covered with heavy steel or other armor for protection against attack.

Examples

Armor-plated vehicle
During the conflict, many trucks were hastily armor-plated to withstand attacks.
Rhinos have naturally armor-plated hides that protect them from predators.
The bank was protected by an armor-plated security van parked outside.
The bank van was armour-plated to resist any attempted robberies.
The military deployed several armour-plated vehicles to protect its personnel.
Their new truck is armour-plated and nearly impossible to penetrate.
