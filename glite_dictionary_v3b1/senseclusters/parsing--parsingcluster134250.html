parsing

noun
interpretation or analysis
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of analyzing the structure or meaning of language, text, or data (especially in language or computing)

Examples

Careful parsing of text
Her careful parsing of the poem revealed hidden meanings that others had missed.
The lawyer's parsing of the legal text changed the outcome of the case.
Accurate parsing of data is essential for drawing correct conclusions in research.
Accurate parsing is essential for understanding complex sentences.
The software's parsing of the data allowed for quick error detection.
Linguists often study parsing to understand how people process language.
