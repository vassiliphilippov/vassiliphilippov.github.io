primitivist

adjective
simple or early style
9.1
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈmɪt.ɪ.vɪst/ /; / /prɪ.ˈmɪ.tɪ.vɪst/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈmɪt.ɪ.vɪst/ /; / /prɪ.ˈmɪ.tɪ.vɪst/ (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of primitivism, especially showing simple, naive, or early cultural forms or advocating a return to a basic way of life.

Examples

Primitivist art style
The artist's primitivist approach was inspired by ancient cave paintings and folk art.
Some architects favor a primitivist design that emphasizes natural materials and simple forms.
Her primitivist philosophy led her to live off the land and avoid modern technology.

  • synonym: primitive - relating to an early stage of development or culture
