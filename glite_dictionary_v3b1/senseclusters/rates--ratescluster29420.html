rates

noun
property tax levied by local councils
1.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /reɪts/ (AmE), /reɪts/ (BrE)
a tax levied by local authorities on property, typically used to fund local public services; formerly a main method of local taxation in Britain

Examples

Local rates tax

The council increased the rates this year to pay for improved rubbish collection services.
