raptly

adverb
with full attention
Pronunciation: / ˈræptli / (AmE), / ˈræptli / (BrE)
in a way that shows complete absorption, fascination, or deep interest

Examples

Watch raptly the show
The audience watched the performance raptly, not missing a single movement on stage.
She listened raptly as her grandfather told stories from his childhood.
The students gazed raptly at the scientist as he demonstrated the experiment.
The children listened raptly as the storyteller described magical lands and brave heroes.
He gazed raptly at the painting, completely absorbed by its vibrant colors.
She watched the fireworks raptly, her eyes wide with wonder and excitement.
