Viola rostrata

noun
North American violet species
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈoʊlə rɒˈstreɪtə / (AmE), / vɪˈəʊlə rɒsˈtrɑːtə / (BrE)
A species of violet native to eastern North America, known for its lilac-purple flowers with a long, slender spur.
