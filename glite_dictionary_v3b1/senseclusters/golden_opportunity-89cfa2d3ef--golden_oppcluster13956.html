golden opportunity

noun
rare valuable chance for success
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˌɑpərˈtunɪti / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˌɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
a rare and valuable chance to achieve something significant or to succeed

Examples

A golden opportunity
He saw a golden opportunity to broaden his skills and took full advantage of it.
Landing an interview at that prestigious firm is a golden opportunity you shouldn't waste.
The launch of the new product presents a golden opportunity for the company to expand its market.

Related Terms
