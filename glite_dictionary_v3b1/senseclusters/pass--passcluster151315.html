pass

noun
in sports, transferring the ball to a teammate
3.2 1.0
Pronunciation: / pæs / (AmE), / pɑːs / (BrE)
an act of throwing, kicking, or sending the ball to a teammate in sports, or an advance to first base in baseball

Examples

A football pass
He made a brilliant pass across the field to his teammate.
She practiced her passes before the basketball game.
The quarterback's pass was intercepted by the opposing team.
An interception ended their drive on what could have been a crucial pass.
During the third quarter, they scored their first touchdown with a 40-yard pass.
The quarterback completed a spectacular pass to the wide receiver in the end zone.
He made a brilliant pass to his teammate, leading to a touchdown.
Practicing how to receive a pass is essential in basketball.
The quarterback's long pass was intercepted by the opposing team.

  • synonym: pass play - an American football play involving a thrown ball
