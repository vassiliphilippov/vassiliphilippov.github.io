scout troop

noun
group unit within scouting organizations
2.5 0.37/m
Pronunciation: / ska ät truÀêp / (AmE), / ska ät truÀêp / (BrE)
a group of Girl or Boy Scouts that forms a single organizational unit within scouting associations

Examples

A scout troop meeting
Our local scout troop volunteered to plant trees in the community park last weekend.
She became the leader of her daughter's scout troop after volunteering for several years.
The scout troop meets every Thursday evening in the church basement for their regular activities.
