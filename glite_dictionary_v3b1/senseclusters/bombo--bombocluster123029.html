bombo

noun
raffle drawing container
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɒmbəʊ / (BrE)
a cylindrical or spherical drum-shaped container used to mix and draw tickets, names, or lots in raffles and lotteries

Examples

Lottery bombo
The winning ticket was drawn from the bombo at the end of the school fundraiser.
Volunteers spun the bombo several times before selecting a name for the prize.
Each participant placed their entry into the bombo before the raffle began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lottery drum - a rotating container used to mix and draw lottery tickets
  • synonym: raffle box - a box used to hold and mix tickets for a raffle
