arm pad

noun
protective sports pad for the arm
2.0
Pronunciation: / ɑrm pæd / (AmE), / ɑːm pad / (BrE)
a protective pad worn on the arm by football players, hockey goalkeepers, or participants in other contact sports to prevent injury

Examples

Protective arm pads
Football players rely on their arm pads to reduce the risk of bruises and fractures.
He forgot his arm pad at home and couldn't play in the match.
The hockey goalie slipped new arm pads on before heading onto the ice.
