self-propelled vehicle

noun
vehicle that moves by its own power
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld ˈviː.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf prəˈpɛld ˈviː.ɪ.kl̩ / (BrE)
A vehicle with wheels that moves by means of its own power rather than being pulled or pushed.

Examples

A self-propelled vehicle moves
After years of research, engineers unveiled a new type of self-propelled vehicle designed for rough terrain.
Each self-propelled vehicle must be registered with the state transportation department.
The farm was equipped with a modern self-propelled vehicle for harvesting crops.
