sloe

noun
small, tart, dark purple fruit of a wild plum tree
Pronunciation: / sloʊ / (AmE), / sləʊ / (BrE)
a small, dark purple, sour fruit from the blackthorn bush, often used to make sloe gin or preserves

Examples

Sloe gin
Blackthorn bushes produce sloe berries, which are often used to make sloe gin.
The tartness of a fresh sloe makes it unsuitable for eating raw.
We gathered a basket of sloes during our walk through the countryside.
In autumn, we gather sloes to make homemade jelly.
She bit into a sloe and made a face at its tartness.
The hedgerows were dotted with ripe sloes ready to be picked for gin making.
