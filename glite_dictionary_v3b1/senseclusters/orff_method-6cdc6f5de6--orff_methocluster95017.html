Orff method

noun
music teaching approach
6.7
Pronunciation: /ɔːrf ˈmeθ.əd/ (AmE), /ɔːf ˈmeθ.əd/ (BrE)
a teaching approach for children that combines music, movement, and play to encourage learning and creativity

Examples

The Orff method teaching

Teachers trained in the Orff method often use instruments and movement in their lessons.
Many educators believe the Orff method helps children develop creativity and a love for music.
