defenselessly

adverb
without protection or defense
3.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛnslɪsli /; / dɪˈfɛnsləsli / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnslɪsli /; / dɪˈfɛnsləsli / (BrE)
In a way that is without protection or defense; lacking any means of resisting harm or attack.

Examples

Left defenselessly exposed
Critics argued that the company left personal data defenselessly exposed to hackers.
She lay defenselessly on the ground, unable to shield herself from the rain.
The soldiers surrendered defenselessly after running out of ammunition.
She defencelessly accepted the criticism, unable to argue back.
The small animal lay defencelessly before its predator.
They found themselves defencelessly exposed to the harsh weather.
