manic-depressive

noun
person with bipolar disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv / (AmE), / ˌmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv / (BrE)
a person who suffers from manic-depressive illness (bipolar disorder), experiencing alternating periods of mania and depression

Examples

A manic-depressive person
Living with a manic-depressive can be emotionally demanding for family members.
She wrote a memoir about her experiences as a manic-depressive in a society that misunderstood her condition.
The manic-depressive was prescribed medication to help stabilize his moods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bipolar disorder - a mental illness with episodes of mania and depression
