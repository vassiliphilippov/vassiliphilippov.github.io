Cynocephalidae

noun
family of gliding mammals (colugos)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnoʊsəˈfælɪdiː /; / saɪˌnoʊsəˈfælɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəʊsɪˈfælıdiː /; / saɪˌnəʊsɪˈfælɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of gliding mammals (colugos or flying lemurs) in the order Dermoptera, native to Southeast Asia
