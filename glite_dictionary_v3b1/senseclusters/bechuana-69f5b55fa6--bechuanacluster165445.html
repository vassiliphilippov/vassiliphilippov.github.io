Bechuana

noun
member of a Bantu people of Botswana and South Africa
Pronunciation: / bəˈtʃwɑːnə / (AmE), / bɪˈtʃwɑːnə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu people living mainly in Botswana and western South Africa
