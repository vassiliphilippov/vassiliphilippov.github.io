photoproduction

noun
particle creation by photons
9.9
Pronunciation: /foʊ.toʊ.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ /; / /foʊ.tə.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (AmE), /fəʊ.təʊ.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ /; / /fəʊ.tə.prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (BrE)
the creation of subatomic particles or reaction products when high-energy photons interact with atomic nuclei or nucleons

Examples

Photoproduction of particles

Scientists observed unexpected results in the photoproduction of strange particles during the test.
