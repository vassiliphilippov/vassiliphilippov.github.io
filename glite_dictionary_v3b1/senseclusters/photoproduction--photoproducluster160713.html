photoproduction

noun
particle creation by photons
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊprəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊprəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the creation of subatomic particles or reaction products when high-energy photons interact with atomic nuclei or nucleons

Examples

Photoproduction of particles
The experiment measured the photoproduction of pions using a high-intensity photon beam.
Advances in photoproduction research have improved our understanding of nuclear structure.
Scientists observed unexpected results in the photoproduction of strange particles during the test.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pair production - the creation of a particle and its antiparticle from a photon
  • synonym: photodisintegration - the process where a nucleus breaks apart due to photon absorption
