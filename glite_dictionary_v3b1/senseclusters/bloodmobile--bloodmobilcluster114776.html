bloodmobile

noun
vehicle for blood donation collection
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdmoʊˌbiːl / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.məʊˌbiːl / (BrE)
a vehicle equipped for collecting blood donations from donors at various locations

Examples

A bloodmobile vehicle
Every month, the community center hosts a bloodmobile visit to encourage local blood drives.
Nurses prepared the bloodmobile and welcomed donors as they arrived.
The bloodmobile will be parked outside the school all afternoon for donations.
