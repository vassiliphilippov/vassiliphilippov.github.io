shotgun microphone

noun
a highly directional microphone for capturing distant sound
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑtˌɡʌn ˈmaɪkrəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃɒtɡʌn ˈmaɪkrəfəʊn / (BrE)
a highly directional microphone with a long, narrow barrel, designed to capture sound from a focused area or distance

Examples

A shotgun microphone outdoors
Because of its narrow pickup pattern, a shotgun microphone is ideal for filming outdoors.
She mounted a shotgun microphone on her camera to capture clearer audio during the interview.
The sound crew used a shotgun microphone to record dialogue from across the street.
