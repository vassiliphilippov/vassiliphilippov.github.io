Hitchiti

noun
a Muskogean Native American language
Pronunciation: / hɪˈtʃɪti / (AmE), / hɪˈtʃɪti / (BrE)
the Muskogean language formerly spoken by the Hitchiti people of Georgia, USA.

Examples

Hitchiti language speaker
Few people today can still speak Hitchiti fluently due to a decline in native speakers.
Linguists have documented stories and legends in Hitchiti to preserve the language.
The Hitchiti language is closely related to other members of the Muskogean family.
