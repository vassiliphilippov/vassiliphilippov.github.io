Luba

noun
member of a Bantu ethnic group in Congo
Pronunciation: / ˈluːbə / (AmE), / ˈluːbə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu ethnic group living in southeastern Congo

Examples

Luba people
A traditional Luba ceremony celebrates the beginning of the planting season.
Many members of the Luba migrated to urban centers during the 20th century.
The Luba have a rich artistic heritage, especially in wood carving and sculpture.

Related Terms
