hardstand

noun
paved parking area
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌstænd / (BrE)
a paved, durable surface where vehicles or aircraft can be parked, loaded, or serviced

Examples

Airport hardstand
The airport built a new hardstand to accommodate more cargo planes during peak season.
Maintenance crews serviced the trucks on the hardstand outside the warehouse.
Each helicopter was parked on a separate hardstand to allow easy access for refueling.
The maintenance crew moved the jet to the hardstand for routine checks.
Several trucks were parked on the hardstand near the warehouse.
After landing, the pilot taxied the plane to the hardstand for unloading.
