beater

noun
person who drives out game for hunters
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtər / (AmE), / ˈbiːtə / (BrE)
a person who drives wild animals out of hiding so that they can be hunted

Examples

A beater in hunting
A group of beaters accompanied the hunters on the annual grouse shoot.
The beater walked through the undergrowth, flushing pheasants towards the waiting hunters.
Without a skilled beater, it would be much harder to find the game in dense cover.
