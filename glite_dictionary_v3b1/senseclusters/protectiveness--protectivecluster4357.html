protectiveness

noun
quality of wishing to protect
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktɪvnəs / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or feeling of wanting to protect someone or something

Examples

Protectiveness towards family
His protectiveness towards his younger sister was clear whenever they walked home from school together.
She appreciated her friend's protectiveness when things became difficult at work.
Some parents show too much protectiveness, not allowing their children to learn from mistakes.
His protectiveness sometimes made his friends feel stifled.
Scientists studied the protectiveness of different sunscreen formulas against UV rays.
The mother’s protectiveness over her children was evident to everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protection - the act of protecting or the state of being protected
  • synonym: protective - having the quality of protecting
  • antonym: vulnerability - the quality of being easily hurt or attacked
