janitress

noun
female caretaker
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒænɪtrɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒænɪtrɪs / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a woman employed to clean and take care of a building

Examples

The janitress cleans
The janitress swept the school corridors every morning before the students arrived.
In the old apartment building, the janitress was known for her kindness to the tenants.
The story described a hardworking janitress who took pride in keeping the place spotless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caretaker - someone who looks after a building or property
  • synonym: janitor - a person employed to clean and maintain a building
