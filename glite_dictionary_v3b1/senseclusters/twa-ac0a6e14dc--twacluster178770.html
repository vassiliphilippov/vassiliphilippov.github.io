Twa

proper noun
Central African people
Pronunciation: / twɑː / (AmE), / twɑː / (BrE)
a member of certain indigenous hunter-gatherer peoples of Central Africa, traditionally living in forest regions

Examples

Twa people
The Twa have a rich tradition of music and dance passed down through generations.
Researchers visited a Twa community to learn about their sustainable hunting practices.
Many Twa people continue to face challenges related to land rights and cultural preservation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Batwa - another name for the Twa people of Central Africa
  • synonym: Pygmy - a general term for various small-statured indigenous peoples of Central Africa
