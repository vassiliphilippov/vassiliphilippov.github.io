handstand

noun
inverted balance on hands
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈhændˌstænd / (BrE)
An act of balancing with the body inverted and supported by the hands, typically with the legs straight in the air.

Examples

A handstand balance
After months of practice, she could finally hold a handstand for several seconds.
Children at the playground often try to do a handstand on the grass.
He performed a perfect handstand during the gymnastics competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartwheel - gymnastic move involving a sideways rotary movement of the body
  • synonym: somersault - an acrobatic movement where the body rolls forward or backward in a complete revolution
