Stoppard

proper noun
British playwright and dramatist
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːpɑrd / (AmE), / ˈstɒpɑːd / (BrE)
Tom Stoppard (born 1937), British playwright noted for his witty, intellectual plays such as 'Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Dead.'
