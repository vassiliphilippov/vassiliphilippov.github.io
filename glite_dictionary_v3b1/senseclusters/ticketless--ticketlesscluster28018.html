ticketless

adjective
without a ticket
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪtləs / (BrE)
not requiring a physical or printed ticket for entry, access, or use

Examples

Ticketless entry
Many modern concerts now offer ticketless entry using digital passes on your phone.
The ticketless system made boarding the train much faster and more convenient for everyone.
Airlines are increasingly moving toward ticketless travel to reduce paper waste and streamline check-in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paperless - not using paper, especially for documents or tickets
  • antonym: ticketed - requiring a ticket for entry or use
