factory farm

noun
large industrialized livestock farm
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ ˌfɑrm / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tə ˌfɑːm / (BrE)
A large farm where animals are bred and raised using industrial methods, often in large numbers and confined spaces.

Examples

A factory farm
Many people are concerned about animal welfare on factory farms.
Some consumers prefer to buy meat that is not produced on a factory farm.
The pollution from the factory farm affected the nearby river.
