terroristic

adjective
relating to terrorism
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
using violence or threats to frighten people or influence government actions, especially in relation to terrorism

Examples

Terroristic activities
The government responded quickly to the terroristic activities in the region.
He was arrested for making terroristic threats against the school.
The film depicted several terroristic acts that shocked the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: peaceful - not involving violence or threats
  • synonym: terrorist - involving or relating to terrorism
