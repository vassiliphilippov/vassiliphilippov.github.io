running

adjective
measured along the length
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a measurement) taken along the continuous length of something, especially in terms such as 'running foot' or 'running meter'.

Examples
