running

adjective
measured along the length
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a measurement) taken along the continuous length of something, especially in terms such as 'running foot' or 'running meter'.

Examples

Running meter
Lumber is often sold by the running foot rather than by the piece.
The price is ten dollars per running meter of cable installed.
They calculated the total material needed by measuring each running length along the wall.
