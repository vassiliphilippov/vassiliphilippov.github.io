nonimportation

noun
refusal to import goods
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪmpɔrˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪmpɔːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or policy of refusing to import goods from another country, often as a form of protest

Examples

A nonimportation policy
The merchants agreed to a nonimportation policy to pressure the government to change its laws.
During the protest, nonimportation of British goods became a powerful tool for the colonists.
The committee discussed the impact of nonimportation on the local economy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boycott - an act of refusing to buy, use, or participate as a protest
  • antonym: importation - the act of bringing goods into a country
