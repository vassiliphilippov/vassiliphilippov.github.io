arrowhead

noun
the pointed tip of an arrow
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæroʊˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈarəʊˌhɛd / (BrE)
the pointed tip or head at the front of an arrow, designed to strike or penetrate a target

Examples

A arrowhead tip

The museum displayed a rare stone arrowhead from the Bronze Age.
They found several broken arrowheads while excavating the ancient campsite.
