physiographically

adverb
by physical geography
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪziəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfɪziəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the physical features or geography of a region

Examples

Regions physiographically separated
The two regions are physiographically separated by a range of mountains.
This island is physiographically unique due to its volcanic origin.
The country can be divided physiographically into coastal and inland areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geographically - in a way that relates to the geography of a place
