unsharp

adjective
not clear or sharp
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɑrp / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɑːp / (BrE)
not having a sharp edge, point, or image; lacking clarity or distinctness

Examples

Unsharp photo
The photograph was unsharp because the camera was not held steady.
He noticed the knife was too unsharp to cut the bread properly.
The text on the screen appeared unsharp after the resolution changed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blurry - not clear or distinct in outline or detail
  • synonym: fuzzy - not clear or sharp in appearance or sound
  • antonym: sharp - having a clear, distinct edge or point
