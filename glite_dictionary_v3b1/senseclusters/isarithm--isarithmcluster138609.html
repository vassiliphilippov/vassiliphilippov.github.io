isarithm

noun
line connecting equal values on a map
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈrɪθəm / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈrɪθəm / (BrE)
A line drawn on a map connecting points that have the same numerical value of a particular variable, such as temperature or elevation.

Examples

An isarithm line
Each isarithm on the chart shows locations sharing the same atmospheric pressure.
Meteorologists use isarithms to indicate areas of equal temperature on weather maps.
The student drew multiple isarithms to represent changes in population density across the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isoline - a line on a map connecting points of equal value
