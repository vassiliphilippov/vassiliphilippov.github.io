European hop

noun
climbing plant used to flavor beer
5.4
Pronunciation: / jʊˈɹoʊpiən hɑp / (AmE), / jʊəˈɹəʊpiən hɒp / (BrE)
A climbing plant native to Europe, whose flowers are chiefly used to flavor beer and other malt liquors; also cultivated in other regions.

Examples
