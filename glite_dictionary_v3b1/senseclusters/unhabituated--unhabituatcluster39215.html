unhabituated

adjective
not accustomed
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnhəˈbɪtʃueɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnhəˈbɪtjueɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having become accustomed to or less responsive to a repeated stimulus, especially in psychology or physiology

Examples

Unhabituated response
The animal remained unhabituated to the sound, reacting strongly each time it was played.
Because the children were unhabituated to the new routine, they found it difficult to adjust.
Researchers observed that the subject was still unhabituated after several days of repeated exposure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: habituated - having become accustomed to something through repeated exposure
  • synonym: unaccustomed - not familiar with something; not used to something
