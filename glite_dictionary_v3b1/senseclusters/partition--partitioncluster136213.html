partition

noun
the act of dividing or separating
1.7
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈtɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the act of dividing or separating something into parts, especially by creating a boundary or barrier

Examples

The partition of a country
After the partition of the country, families found themselves on different sides of the border.
The partition of the room into two offices made better use of the space.
The legal partition of assets between the two businesses was finalized last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: division - the act or process of dividing
  • antonym: unification - the process of making into a single unit; the act of uniting
