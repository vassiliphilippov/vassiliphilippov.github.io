mouthless

adjective
having no mouth
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊθ.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθ.ləs / (BrE)
Having no mouth or mouthlike opening.

Examples

A mouthless mask
Her mouthless mask made her expression impossible to read.
Some species of eel are born mouthless and absorb nutrients through their skin.
The strange, mouthless creature moved silently through the water, feeding in an unusual way.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mouthy - talkative or given to speaking a lot
