Examples

The iris diaphragm control
In microscopy, the iris diaphragm allows fine control over the illumination of the specimen.
The photographer adjusted the iris diaphragm to let in more light for the shot.
Turning the ring changes the opening of the iris diaphragm in the camera lens.

Synonyms

  • iris
    7.1 0.3/m
    iris is the short name for the thin overlapping plate device seen in cameras and microscopes, while iris diaphragm is the full technical name.
  • diaphragm
    7.1 0.02/m
    diaphragm is a general mechanical part that changes a lens opening to control light, while iris diaphragm is the specific plate based model.
  • paraphragm
    9.9 0.00/m
