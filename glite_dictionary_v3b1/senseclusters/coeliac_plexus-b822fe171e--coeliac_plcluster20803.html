coeliac plexus

noun
network of nerves behind the stomach
8.6 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.lɪ.æk ˈplek.səs/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.lɪ.æk ˈplek.səs/ (BrE)
a large network of sympathetic nerves in the abdomen located behind the stomach, involved in the innervation of abdominal organs

Examples
