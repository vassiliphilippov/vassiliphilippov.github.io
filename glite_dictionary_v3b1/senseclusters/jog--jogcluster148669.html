jog

noun
a sharp change in direction
Pronunciation: / dʒɑɡ / (AmE), / dʒɒɡ / (BrE)
a sharp or sudden change in direction, especially in a road, path, or line

Examples

A jog in the road
The fence follows the field's edge but makes a slight jog around the pond.
The trail took a sudden jog to the left near the old oak tree.
We almost missed the sign at the jog in the road.
