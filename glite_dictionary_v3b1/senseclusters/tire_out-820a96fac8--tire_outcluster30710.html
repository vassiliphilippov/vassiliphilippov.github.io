tire out

verb
exhaust or deplete energy
4.1 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪ.ər aʊt/ /; / /taɪr aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪ.ə aʊt/ (BrE)

Examples

She worked so hard that she completely tired out before dinner.
