Examples

Synonyms

  • radio receiver
    3.9 0.21/m
    radio receiver detects and converts radio signals into clear sound for audio or data, while radio is the general listening device.
  • radio set
    2.3 0.10/m
    radio set receives and plays broadcast stations for home listening, while radio is the common word for that listening device.
  • receiving set
    6.1 0.02/m
    receiving set picks up transmitted signals for radio or television equipment, while radio refers specifically to the listening device for broadcasts.
  • wireless
    5.3 0.55/m
    wireless originally names early radio receivers and sounds old-fashioned, while radio is the modern common name for the listening device.
