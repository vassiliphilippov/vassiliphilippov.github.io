Tamiflu

noun
antiviral flu medicine
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmɪfluː / (AmE), / ˈtæmɪfluː / (BrE)
a brand name for an antiviral medication used to treat and prevent influenza (the flu)

Examples

Many people buy Tamiflu during flu season as a precaution.
