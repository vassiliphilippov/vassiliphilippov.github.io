protostome

noun
animal mouth develops first
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtəˌstoʊm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəstəʊm / (BrE)
an animal whose mouth develops before its anus during early embryonic development

Examples

A protostome animal
Earthworms are a common example of a protostome found in soil ecosystems.
Scientists study the differences between protostome and deuterostome development in biology classes.
Most insects, such as beetles and flies, are classified as protostome animals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deuterostome - an animal whose anus develops before its mouth during early development
