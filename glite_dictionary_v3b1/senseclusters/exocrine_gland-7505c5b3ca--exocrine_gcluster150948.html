A gland that produces and secretes substances through a duct to an external or internal surface of the body.

Examples

Exocrine gland secretion
Damage to an exocrine gland can disrupt normal bodily functions by affecting secretion processes.
Sweat glands are a type of exocrine gland that help regulate body temperature.
The pancreas acts as an exocrine gland by releasing digestive enzymes into the small intestine.
