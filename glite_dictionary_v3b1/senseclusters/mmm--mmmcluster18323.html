mmm

interjection
hesitation sound
9.5
Pronunciation: / m̩ / (AmE), / m̩ / (BrE)
used to express that the speaker is thinking, hesitating, or considering something

Examples

Mmm a question
Mmm, let me think about that for a moment before I answer.
She paused and said, 'mmm, I suppose that could work, but I'm not sure.'
When asked about his plans, he replied, 'mmm, I haven't decided yet.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: er - an interjection expressing hesitation or uncertainty
  • synonym: um - a sound used to show hesitation or uncertainty
