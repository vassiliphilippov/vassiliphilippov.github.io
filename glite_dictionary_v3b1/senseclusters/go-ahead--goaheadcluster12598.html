go-ahead

noun
permission or signal to proceed
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ əˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ əˌhɛd / (BrE)
an official signal, permission, or instruction to begin or continue something

Examples

Official go-ahead
She gave me the go-ahead to start the new marketing campaign.
The project finally received the go-ahead from the board.
We can't start construction until we get the official go-ahead.
