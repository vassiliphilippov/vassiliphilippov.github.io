Brahmaputra

proper noun
major river in South Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɑːməˈpuːtrə / (AmE), / ˌbrɑːməˈpuːtrə / (BrE)
a major river in South Asia, rising in Tibet and flowing through India and Bangladesh into the Bay of Bengal

Examples
