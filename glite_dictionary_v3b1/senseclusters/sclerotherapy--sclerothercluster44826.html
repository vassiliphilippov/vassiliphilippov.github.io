sclerotherapy

noun
vein treatment procedure
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsklɛroʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌsklɛrəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a medical procedure for treating blood vessels or varicose veins by injecting a solution to make them shrink or disappear

Examples

Sclerotherapy treatment
After several sessions of sclerotherapy, her varicose veins became much less visible.
The doctor explained that sclerotherapy is a minimally invasive way to treat spider veins.
Many patients choose sclerotherapy because it does not require surgery or a long recovery time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laser therapy - a medical treatment using focused light to treat veins or tissues
  • antonym: surgery - a medical procedure involving an operation to treat veins or tissues
