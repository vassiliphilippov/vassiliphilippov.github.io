witching

adjective
relating to witchcraft or magic
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Having qualities or powers characteristic of witchcraft or the supernatural; enchanting or magical.

Examples

Witching hour
It was said to be the witching hour, when spells and enchantments were most powerful.
The novel describes the witching atmosphere of the old, abandoned house.
Under the witching moonlight, the forest seemed alive with mysterious magic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charming - delightfully enchanting, sometimes with a magical quality
  • synonym: magical - relating to magic or having supernatural qualities
