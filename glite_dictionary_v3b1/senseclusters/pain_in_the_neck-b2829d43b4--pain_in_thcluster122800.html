pain in the neck

noun
annoying or troublesome person or thing
Pronunciation: / peɪn ɪn ðə nɛk / (AmE), / peɪn ɪn ðə nɛk / (BrE)
a person or thing that is very annoying, troublesome, or inconvenient

Examples

A pain in the neck problem

Trying to find parking downtown during rush hour is a pain in the neck.
Filling out tax forms every year is such a pain in the neck.
Her younger brother can be a real pain in the neck when she's trying to study.
That old computer has become a pain in the neck to use lately.
