shagginess

noun
unkempt state of hair or fur
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæɡi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡ.i.nəs /; / ˈʃaɡɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being covered with long, thick, rough, or untidy hair, fur, or fibers

Examples

Wild shagginess of hair
After months without a haircut, his hair grew to a wild shagginess.
The shagginess of the dog's coat made grooming a challenge.
The child's shagginess gave him a charming, untamed look.
She admired the shagginess of the new wool rug in the living room.
The shagginess of the old dog's coat made him look even larger than he was.
The bear's shagginess helped it stay warm during the cold winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hairiness - the quality of being covered with hair
  • synonym: shaggy - having long, thick, and untidy hair or fur
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being smooth and lacking roughness or irregularity
