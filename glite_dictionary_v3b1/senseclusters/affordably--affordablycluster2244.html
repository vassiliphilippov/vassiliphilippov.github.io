affordably

adverb
inexpensively
Pronunciation: / əˈfɔrdəbli / (AmE), / əˈfɔːdəbli / (BrE)
in a way that does not cost too much money or is within one's financial means

Examples

To affordably buy food
The new restaurant offers delicious meals affordably to attract more customers.
Many students want to travel affordably during their summer break.
She managed to decorate her apartment affordably by shopping at secondhand stores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheaply - at a low price or cost
  • antonym: expensively - in a way that costs a lot of money
