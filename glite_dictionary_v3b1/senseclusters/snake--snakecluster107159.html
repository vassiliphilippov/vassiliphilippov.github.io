snake

noun
elongate, limbless reptile
1.0
Pronunciation: / sneɪk / (AmE), / sneɪk / (BrE)
a long, limbless reptile with a scaly skin, some of which are venomous

Examples

A snake slithers
A snake slithered across the forest path and disappeared into the undergrowth.
Some species of snake are highly venomous and can pose a danger to humans.
The child was fascinated by the snake coiled up in its glass enclosure at the zoo.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lizard - a reptile similar to a snake but with limbs
  • synonym: serpent - another word for a snake, especially used in literature or mythology
