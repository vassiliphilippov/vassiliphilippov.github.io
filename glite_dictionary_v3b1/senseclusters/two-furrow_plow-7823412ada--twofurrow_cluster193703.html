two-furrow plow

noun
plow with two moldboards
5.2 6.4
Pronunciation: /tuː ˈfɜːr.oʊ plaʊ/ /; / /tuː ˈfʌr.oʊ plaʊ/ (AmE), /tuː ˈfɜː.rəʊ plaʊ/ /; / /tuː ˈfʌ.rəʊ plaʊ/ (BrE)
A plow designed with two moldboards, allowing it to turn over two furrows of soil simultaneously when drawn by a tractor or horses.
