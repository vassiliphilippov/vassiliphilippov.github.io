LPM

sym
low power mode
Pronunciation: / ˌɛl piː ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌɛl piː ˈɛm / (BrE)
an electronic device setting that reduces power consumption to extend battery life, also known as low power mode

Examples

Phone LPM on
When my phone battery is low, I always turn on LPM to make it last longer.
Some laptops automatically activate LPM when unplugged from the charger.
You can enable LPM in the settings to reduce energy use during travel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battery saver - a feature that helps extend battery life
  • synonym: low power mode - a setting that reduces device power usage
