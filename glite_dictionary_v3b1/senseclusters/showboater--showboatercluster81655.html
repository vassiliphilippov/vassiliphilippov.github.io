showboater

noun
riverboat entertainer
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌboʊtər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌbəʊtə / (BrE)
a performer or entertainer who appeared on a traveling river showboat, especially in the past

Examples

River showboater
The famous showboater captivated audiences as the boat traveled from town to town along the river.
In the early 1900s, a showboater was a star attraction in many small communities.
Many aspiring actors dreamed of becoming a showboater and performing on the Mississippi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entertainer - a person who performs to amuse an audience
  • synonym: performer - someone who acts, sings, or entertains for an audience
