mordacious

adjective
sharply critical; biting
Pronunciation: / mɔrˈdeɪʃəs /; / mɔːrˈdeɪʃəs / (AmE), / mɔːˈdeɪʃəs /; / mɔːˈdeɪ.ʃəs / (BrE)
sharply critical or biting in manner, speech, or writing; caustic or cutting

Examples

Mordacious wit
Her mordacious wit left her colleagues at a loss for words.
His review was so mordacious that the author refused to comment.
The article was filled with mordacious remarks about the political system.
Her mordacious wit left everyone slightly unsettled at the dinner table.
Some animals become more mordacious when they feel threatened.
The horse turned suddenly unruly and mordacious, making it difficult to control.

  • synonym: caustic - severely critical, especially in speech or writing
  • antonym: gentle - kind or mild in tone
  • synonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt
