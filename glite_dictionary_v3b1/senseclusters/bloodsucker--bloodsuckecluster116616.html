bloodsucker

noun
exploiter or parasite
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌsʌkə / (BrE)
a person or animal that exploits others for personal gain, or an animal that feeds on blood

Examples

A greedy bloodsucker
The company was accused of being a bloodsucker that profited from its workers' hard labor.
A mosquito is a common bloodsucker found in many parts of the world.
He called the landlord a bloodsucker for raising the rent every year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benefactor - a person who helps others, especially by giving money
  • synonym: leech - a bloodsucking worm or a person who exploits others
  • synonym: parasite - an organism or person that lives off another
