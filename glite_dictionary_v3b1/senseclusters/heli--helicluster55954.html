sun-related prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːli / (AmE), / ˈhiːli / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'sun' or 'sunlight', used in scientific and technical terms

Examples

Heli prefix in science
The prefix heli appears in words like 'heliocentric' and 'heliotherapy'.
Scientists use heli to form terms related to the sun in astronomy and medicine.
In 'heliograph', the heli part refers to sunlight used for signaling.
