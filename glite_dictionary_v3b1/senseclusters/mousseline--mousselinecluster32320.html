mousseline

noun
sheer lightweight fabric
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmuːsəˈliːn / (AmE), / ˌmuːsəˈliːn / (BrE)
a sheer, lightweight fabric similar to muslin, often used for dresses, curtains, or veils

Examples

Mousseline dress fabric
The designer chose mousseline for the evening gown to create a delicate, flowing effect.
Light curtains made of mousseline let the sunlight gently filter into the room.
She admired the vintage scarf crafted from soft, white mousseline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muslin - a plain-woven cotton fabric
