harrier

noun
persistent attacker or annoyer
Pronunciation: / ˈhæriər / (AmE), / ˈhæriə / (BrE)
a person or thing that persistently attacks or annoys others

Examples

An online harrier
Online, he acted as a harrier, always criticizing and finding faults in others.
She became known as a relentless harrier of corrupt officials.
The team played the role of harrier, constantly pressuring their opponents through the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defender - someone who protects rather than attacks
  • synonym: harasser - someone who persistently annoys or attacks others
