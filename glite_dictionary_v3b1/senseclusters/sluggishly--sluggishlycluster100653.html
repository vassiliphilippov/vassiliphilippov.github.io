sluggishly

adverb
in a slow or inactive way
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌɡɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈslʌɡɪʃli / (BrE)
In a slow, lazy, or lacking energy manner; not briskly.

Examples

To move sluggishly
He responded sluggishly to the doctor's questions due to exhaustion.
The old machine started up sluggishly after months of disuse.
Traffic moved sluggishly through the city after the heavy snowfall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lethargically - in a sluggish and apathetic manner
  • antonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
