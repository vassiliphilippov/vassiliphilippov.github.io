Fall

noun
humanity's lapse into sin (the Fall)
9.7 5.60/m
Pronunciation: / f…îl / (AmE), / f…îÀêl / (BrE)
(the Fall) In Judeo-Christian tradition, the event in which humanity became sinful as a result of Adam and Eve's disobedience to God.

Examples

The Fall of man
Many theologians debate the consequences of the Fall for humanity.
Some artworks vividly depict the Fall as the expulsion from paradise.
The story of the Fall is told in the Book of Genesis.

Synonyms

  • Fall of Man
    8.9 0.18/m
