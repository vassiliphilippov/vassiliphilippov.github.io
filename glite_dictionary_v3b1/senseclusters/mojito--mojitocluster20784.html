mojito

noun
rum and mint cocktail
4.6
Pronunciation: / moʊˈhiːtoʊ / (AmE), / məʊˈhiːtəʊ / (BrE)
a cocktail made with rum, lime juice, mint, sugar, and soda water, served over ice

Examples

A cold mojito
On a hot summer day, nothing is more refreshing than a cold mojito with fresh mint.
He learned how to make a classic mojito during his trip to Havana.
The bartender garnished her mojito with a sprig of mint and a slice of lime.
