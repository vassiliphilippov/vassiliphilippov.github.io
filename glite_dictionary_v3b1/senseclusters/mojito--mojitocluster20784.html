mojito

noun
rum and mint cocktail
4.6
Pronunciation: /moʊ.ˈhiː.toʊ/ (AmE), /məʊ.ˈhiː.təʊ/ (BrE)
a cocktail made with rum, lime juice, mint, sugar, and soda water, served over ice

Examples

A cold mojito

The bartender garnished her mojito with a sprig of mint and a slice of lime.
