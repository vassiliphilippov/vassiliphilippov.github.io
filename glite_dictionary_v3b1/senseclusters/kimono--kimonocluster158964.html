kimono

verb
to put on kimono
8.4
Pronunciation: / kɪˈmoʊnoʊ / (AmE), / kɪˈməʊnəʊ / (BrE)
to dress someone in a kimono or to put a kimono on oneself or another person

Examples

To kimono a child
Before the ceremony, her mother carefully kimonoed her, adjusting the obi and sleeves.
The children were kimonoed for the festival by their grandmother.
He kimonoed himself quickly before stepping onto the stage for the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress - to put clothes on someone
  • antonym: undress - to remove clothes from someone
