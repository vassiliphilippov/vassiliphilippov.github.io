feignedly

adverb
in a pretended way
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪndli / (AmE), / ˈfeɪndli / (BrE)
in a way that is not genuine or is pretended

Examples

To feign feelings
He laughed feignedly at the joke, though he did not find it funny.
The student listened feignedly interested during the long lecture.
She greeted her rival feignedly as if they were old friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falsely - in a way that is not true or real
  • antonym: genuinely - in a real or sincere way
