unwealthy

adjective
not rich
3.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛlθi / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlθi / (BrE)
not having much money, property, or valuable possessions; not rich

Examples

An unwealthy family
The unwealthy family struggled to pay their bills each month.
Many scholarships are designed to help unwealthy students attend college.
Despite being unwealthy, she always found ways to help others in need.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poor - having little money or few possessions
  • antonym: rich - having a lot of money or valuable possessions
