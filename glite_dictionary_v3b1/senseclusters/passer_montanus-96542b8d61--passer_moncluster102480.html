Passer montanus

noun
Eurasian tree sparrow species
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsər mɒnˈteɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈpasə mɒnˈteɪnəs / (BrE)
A small Eurasian sparrow, also known as the Eurasian tree sparrow, distinguished by its chestnut crown and smaller size compared to the house sparrow.

Examples

The Passer montanus bird
A pair of Passer montanus built their nest in the old tree behind our cottage.
The Passer montanus is easily identified by its rich chestnut crown and black cheek spot.
Unlike the house sparrow, the Passer montanus prefers rural woodlands and farmlands.
