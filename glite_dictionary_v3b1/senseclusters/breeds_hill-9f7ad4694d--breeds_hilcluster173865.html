Breed's Hill

proper noun
hill in Boston; site of major Revolutionary War battle
7.4
Pronunciation: /briːdz hɪl/ (AmE), /briːdz hɪl/ (BrE)
a hill in Charlestown, Boston, known as the actual site of the Battle of Bunker Hill during the American Revolution in 1775

Examples

Breed's Hill in Boston
Many tourists visit Breed's Hill to learn about its role in the American Revolution.
Standing on Breed's Hill, you can see the markers commemorating the historic battle.
The Battle of Bunker Hill was largely fought on Breed's Hill rather than Bunker Hill itself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bunker Hill - the commonly named site of the 1775 Revolutionary War battle, actually fought mostly on Breed's Hill
