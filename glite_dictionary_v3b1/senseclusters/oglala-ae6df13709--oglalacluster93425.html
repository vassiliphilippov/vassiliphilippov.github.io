Oglala

noun
Siouan language of the Oglala people
Pronunciation: / oʊˈɡlɑːlə / (AmE), / əʊˈɡlɑːlə / (BrE)
the Siouan language spoken by the Oglala people, a subgroup of the Lakota

Examples

Oglala language
Linguists are working to preserve Oglala for future generations.
She learned to introduce herself in Oglala during her cultural studies class.
The Oglala language is still spoken by many members of the tribe today.
