food coloring

noun
a substance for coloring food
1.4 0.93/m
Pronunciation: / fuÀêd Ààk ål…ö…™≈ã / (AmE), / fuÀêd Ààk ål…ô…™≈ã / (BrE)
A substance added to food or drink to give it color, often for decorative or identification purposes and usually without affecting the taste.

Examples

Artificial food coloring
Many candies owe their bright shades to artificial food coloring.
Natural food coloring is often derived from fruits and vegetables.
She added a drop of food coloring to the frosting to make it pink.
Many bakers use food colouring to make cakes look more appealing.
Natural food colouring can be made from vegetables like beets or carrots.
Some parents prefer to avoid artificial food colouring in their children's snacks.

Synonyms

  • coloring
    1.0 1.59/m
    coloring is an edible dye used to change or brighten food and drink, while food coloring is the common term for such additives.
  • food color
    2.3 0.01/m
    food color is a tested, approved dye used to add or restore color in food and drink, while food coloring is the everyday name for these additives.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: food additive - a substance added to food to preserve its flavor or enhance its taste and appearance
  • synonym: food dye - a substance used to color food
