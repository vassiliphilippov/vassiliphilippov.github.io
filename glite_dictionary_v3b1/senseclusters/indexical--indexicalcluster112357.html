indexical

adjective
relating to or indicating by an index
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛksɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛksɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or indicating something, especially by serving as an index; denoting something by direct reference.

Examples

Indexical signs
Photographs are often considered indexical signs that point to real events or objects.
The catalog used an indexical numbering system for quick reference.
The word 'this' is an indexical expression because it refers to something specific in context.
