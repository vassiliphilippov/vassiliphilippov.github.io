avoider

noun
one who avoids duties
7.4
Pronunciation: / əˈvɔɪdər / (AmE), / əˈvɔɪdə / (BrE)
a person or organization that reduces or avoids paying taxes or legal duties, often by using legal strategies or loopholes

Examples

Tax avoider
The company became known as an avoider after using complex tax strategies.
Some people see a skilled avoider as clever, while others see them as unethical.
Authorities are working to close loopholes that benefit the typical avoider.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evader - a person who illegally avoids paying taxes or duties
  • antonym: payer - a person who pays taxes or duties as required
