luge

noun
winter sled sport
7.7
Pronunciation: / luːʒ / (AmE), / luːʒ / (BrE)
a winter sport in which a person rides a small sled feet-first down an icy track at high speed

Examples

Luge competition
He dreamed of winning a medal in the luge at the Winter Olympics.
The luge requires both courage and precise control to navigate the icy curves.
After watching the luge competition, she wanted to try the sport herself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bobsleigh - a winter sport where teams ride a large sled down an ice track
  • synonym: skeleton - a winter sport where a person rides a small sled head-first down an ice track
