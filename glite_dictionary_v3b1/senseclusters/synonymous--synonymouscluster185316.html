synonymous

adjective
having the same or nearly the same meaning
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɑːnɪməs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒnɪməs / (BrE)
(of words or expressions) having the same or nearly the same meaning as another word or expression.

Examples

Synonymous words
Fame is synonymous with success for many people.
In this context, 'happy' is not exactly synonymous with 'content'.
The words 'end' and 'finish' are often considered synonymous in everyday usage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antonymous - having the opposite meaning; antonyms
  • degree higher: identical - exactly the same
