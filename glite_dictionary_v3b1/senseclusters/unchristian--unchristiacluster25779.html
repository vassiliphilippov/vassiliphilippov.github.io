unchristian

adjective
not of the Christian religion
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
Not relating to or characteristic of the Christian faith; not of a Christian religion.

Examples

Unchristian beliefs
He grew up in an unchristian household and only learned about Christianity in adulthood.
Some critics labeled the festival's customs as unchristian and contrary to church teachings.
The village was considered unchristian by the missionaries due to its traditional beliefs.
