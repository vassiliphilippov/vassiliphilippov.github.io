hydroxy

adjective
containing a hydroxyl group
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɑksi / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒksi / (BrE)
(of a molecule or compound) being or containing a hydroxyl group (–OH).

Examples

Hydroxy acid
Many cosmetic products contain hydroxy acids to help exfoliate the skin.
Researchers are studying new hydroxy compounds for potential medical applications.
The addition of a hydroxy group alters the chemical properties of the molecule.
