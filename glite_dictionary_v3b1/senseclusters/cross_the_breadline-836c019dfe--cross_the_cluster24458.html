cross the breadline

verb
fall below the minimum income level
Pronunciation: / krɔs ðə ˈbrɛd.laɪn / (AmE), / krɒs ðə ˈbrɛd.laɪn / (BrE)
to fall below the minimum income level required for basic living needs

Examples

To cross the breadline during recession
During the recession, thousands of families crossed the breadline despite government support.
If food prices keep rising, more elderly people may cross the breadline this year.
She worried her part-time wages would soon have her crossing the breadline.

Related Terms
