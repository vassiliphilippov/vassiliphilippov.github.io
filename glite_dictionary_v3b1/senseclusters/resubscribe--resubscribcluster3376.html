resubscribe

verb
to sign up again
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːsəbˈskraɪb / (AmE), / ˌriːsəbˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to sign up again for a service, publication, or membership after a previous subscription has ended or been canceled

After her membership expired, she decided to resubscribe to the online course.
Many users resubscribe to the newsletter each year for the latest updates.

After a year away, she decided to resubscribe to the online streaming service.
If you want to receive updates again, simply resubscribe to our newsletter.
Many users choose to resubscribe after seeing the new features added to the app.
