layered

adjective
arranged in layers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈleɪəd / (BrE)
arranged in or consisting of layers; with one layer on top of another

Examples

A layered dress
She wore a layered dress that flowed beautifully as she twirled.
The dessert was perfectly layered, with cream and fruit stacked alternately.
The rock formation is layered, showing millions of years of geological activity.
