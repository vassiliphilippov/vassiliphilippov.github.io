left to one's own devices

x
left alone to act independently
Pronunciation: / lɛft tə wʌnz oʊn dɪˈvaɪsɪz / (AmE), / lɛft tə wʌnz əʊn dɪˈvaɪsɪz / (BrE)
to be left alone without help or supervision, so that one must rely on one's own resources and abilities

Examples

Left to one's own devices
After her parents left her to her own devices, she learned to solve problems by herself.
If you leave teenagers to their own devices, they may spend hours on social media.
When employees are left to their own devices, creativity often flourishes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independently - without outside help or influence
  • antonym: supervised - watched or guided by someone in authority
