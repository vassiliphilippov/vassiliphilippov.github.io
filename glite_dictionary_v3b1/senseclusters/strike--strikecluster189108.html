strike

noun
notable or conspicuous success
Pronunciation: / straɪk / (AmE), / straɪk / (BrE)
a notable or conspicuous success, especially one that has an impressive impact or draws significant attention

Examples

A big strike
Her new novel was a strike and received rave reviews from critics everywhere.
Launching a viral marketing campaign was a real strike for the small start-up.
The movie became an unexpected strike at the box office, earning millions in its first week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flop - a complete failure, especially in performance or entertainment
  • synonym: hit - a conspicuous success, especially in music or entertainment
