front-projection TV

noun
home projector television system
2.7 4.9
Pronunciation: /frʌnt prə.ˈdʒek.ʃən tiː.ˈviː/ (AmE), /frʌnt prə.ˈdʒek.ʃən tiː.ˈviː/ (BrE)
A television system in which images are projected onto a screen from the front, typically using a projector designed for home use.

Examples

A front-projection TV system
Many movie enthusiasts prefer a front-projection TV system over traditional flat-screen televisions.
The picture quality on his front-projection TV is impressive, especially in a darkened room.
We decided to invest in a front-projection TV for our home theater to get a bigger screen experience.
