herbal tea

noun
drink made from infused herbs, not true tea
4.3 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈɜːr.bəl tiː/ /; / /ˈhɜːr.bəl tiː/ (AmE), /ˈhɜː.bəl tiː/ /; / /ˈɜː.bəl tiː/ (BrE)
