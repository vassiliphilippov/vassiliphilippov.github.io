herbal tea

noun
drink made from infused herbs, not true tea
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːbəl ti /; / ˈhɝːbəl ˈtiː / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəl tiː /; / ˈhɜːbəl ˈtiː / (BrE)
a hot drink made by infusing herbs or other plant materials in hot water, not containing true tea leaves

Examples

She offered her guests several types of herbal tea after dinner.
Some people drink herbal tea as a caffeine-free alternative to black tea.

  • degree higher: tea - a hot drink made by infusing dried tea leaves in boiling water
