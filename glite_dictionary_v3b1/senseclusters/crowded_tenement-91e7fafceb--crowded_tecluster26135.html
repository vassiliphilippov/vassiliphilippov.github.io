crowded tenement

noun
densely populated apartment building
Pronunciation: / ˈkraʊdɪd ˈtɛnəmənt / (AmE), / ˈkraʊdɪd ˈtɛnəmənt / (BrE)
An apartment building with a large number of occupants, often resulting in cramped living conditions.

Examples

A crowded tenement building
Growing up in a crowded tenement shaped her view of city life.
Inspectors found that the crowded tenement lacked proper ventilation and sanitation.
Many immigrant families lived in crowded tenements during the early 20th century.
