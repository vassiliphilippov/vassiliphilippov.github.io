armless

adjective
without arms
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmləs / (AmE), / ˈɑːmləs / (BrE)
lacking arms, or without armrests or sleeves

Examples

An armless chair
She wore an armless dress to stay cool in the summer heat.
The museum displayed an armless statue from ancient Greece.
We bought an armless chair to save space in the living room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: armrest - a support for the arm on a chair
  • synonym: sleeveless - without sleeves
