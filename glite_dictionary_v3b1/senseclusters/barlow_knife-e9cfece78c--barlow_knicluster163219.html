Barlow knife

noun
sturdy pocketknife
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrloʊ naɪf / (AmE), / ˈbɑːləʊ naɪf / (BrE)
a pocketknife with a long, oval handle and a single blade, known for its sturdy design

Examples

A Barlow knife pocketknife

With his trusty Barlow knife, he quickly opened the package in the field.
