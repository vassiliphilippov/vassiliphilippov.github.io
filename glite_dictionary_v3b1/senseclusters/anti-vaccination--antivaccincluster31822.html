anti-vaccination

noun
opposition to vaccines
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌænti væksɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌænti væksɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
opposition to vaccination, or the belief that vaccines are unnecessary or harmful

Examples

Anti-vaccination movement
The rise of anti-vaccination sentiment has led to outbreaks of preventable diseases in some regions.
Public health campaigns aim to counter anti-vaccination misinformation spread online.
Some parents joined anti-vaccination groups after reading misleading articles about vaccine safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-vaccination - support for vaccination and immunization
  • synonym: vaccine hesitancy - reluctance or refusal to be vaccinated
