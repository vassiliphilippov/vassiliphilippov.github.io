importunateness

noun
persistent or insistent demanding
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɔrtʃuˈneɪt.nəs / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɔː.tjuˈneɪt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being persistently demanding or insistent in making requests, often to the point of annoyance

Examples

Persistent importunateness
Despite his importunateness, she refused to change her mind about the decision.
She was admired for her persistence but criticized for the importunateness of her demands.
The importunateness of his appeals finally persuaded the manager to give him another chance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: importunity - persistent request or demand
  • antonym: politeness - the quality of being respectful and considerate
