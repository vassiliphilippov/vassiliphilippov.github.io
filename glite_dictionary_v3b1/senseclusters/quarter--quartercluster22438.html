quarter

noun
one of four main compass directions
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
one of the four principal directions on the compass: north, south, east, or west, or the general direction corresponding to it.

Examples

Eastern quarter
Sailors watched the clouds gathering in the eastern quarter.
The weather is clear in every quarter of the horizon.
The wind shifted to the north and began to blow steadily from that quarter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: direction - the course along which someone or something moves
