corp

noun
abbreviation for 'corporation' (business firm)
Pronunciation: / kɔrp / (AmE), / kɔːp / (BrE)
an abbreviated form of 'corporation', used especially to refer to a business firm whose articles of incorporation have been approved in some state

Examples

Global corps
Many global corps are expanding their operations into Asia.
She landed a job at a large investment corp downtown.
The tech corp announced a major merger with its rival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporation - a large business or company legally authorized as a single entity
