thus

adverb
therefore; as a result
Pronunciation: / ðʌs / (AmE), / ðʌs / (BrE)
as a result of the fact or reason just mentioned; therefore; consequently.

Examples

Thus the result
He forgot to set his alarm and thus was late for work.
She trained hard every day and thus improved her performance significantly.
The evidence was incomplete; thus, the case was dismissed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: because - for the reason that
  • synonym: hence - for this reason; from this fact or circumstance
  • synonym: so - and for this reason; therefore
  • synonym: therefore - for that reason; as a result
