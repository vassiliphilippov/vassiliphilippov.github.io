somberness

A state of partial or total darkness; the quality of being shadowy or dimly lit.

Examples

Nighttime somberness
A thick fog settled over the village, cloaking it in complete somberness until dawn.
The somberness of the room made it difficult to see anything clearly.
The artist captured the somberness of the old cellar in his painting.
At dusk, a feeling of sombreness settled over the quiet street.
The sombreness of the room made it difficult to see anything without a lamp.
The old castle was filled with sombreness, making it seem mysterious and unwelcoming.
