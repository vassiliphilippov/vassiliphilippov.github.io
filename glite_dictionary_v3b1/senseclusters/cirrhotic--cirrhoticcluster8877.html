cirrhotic

adjective
affected by cirrhosis
Pronunciation: / səˈrɑːtɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by cirrhosis, especially of the liver

Examples

Cirrhotic liver disease
The doctor explained that a cirrhotic liver cannot function as well as a healthy one.
Patients with cirrhotic complications often require specialized medical care.
A cirrhotic condition may develop after years of chronic alcohol abuse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cirrhosis - a chronic disease of the liver marked by degeneration of cells
