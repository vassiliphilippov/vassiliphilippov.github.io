unleisurely

adjective
not relaxed
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈliːʒərli / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɛʒəli / (BrE)
not relaxed or unhurried; done in a rushed or busy manner

Examples

An unleisurely pace
She walked at an unleisurely pace, clearly eager to reach her destination.
The meeting was conducted in an unleisurely manner, with little time for discussion.
His unleisurely approach to lunch made it clear he was pressed for time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hurried - done quickly or with haste
  • antonym: leisurely - relaxed and unhurried
