northeasterly

adjective
coming from the northeast
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˈistɚli / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːstəli / (BrE)
(especially of wind or direction) coming from or directed toward the northeast.

Examples

Northeasterly wind
Strong northeasterly winds battered the coastline for hours.
The boat struggled against the northeasterly current as it sailed toward the harbor.
Tomorrow's forecast predicts a shift to northeasterly breezes in the afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: northwesterly - coming from the northwest
  • antonym: southwesterly - coming from the southwest
