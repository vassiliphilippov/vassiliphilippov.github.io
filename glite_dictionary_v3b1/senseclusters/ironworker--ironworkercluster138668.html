ironworker

noun
a person who works with iron
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.ərnˌwɜr.kər / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ənˌwɜː.kə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to make or shape articles from iron.

Examples

Traditional ironworker shapes metal
Many bridges were built by skilled ironworkers during the early 20th century.
She apprenticed with a ironworker to learn the art of metalworking.
The ironworker spent hours carefully crafting the ornate railings for the new building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - a person who forges and shapes iron, especially horseshoes
