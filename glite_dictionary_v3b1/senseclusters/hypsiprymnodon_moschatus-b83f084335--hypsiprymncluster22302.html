Hypsiprymnodon moschatus

noun
small kangaroo species of northeastern Australia
Pronunciation: / haɪpsɪˌprɪmnədɒn mɒˈskeɪtəs / (AmE), / hɪpsɪˌprɪmnədɒn mɒsˈkætəs / (BrE)
