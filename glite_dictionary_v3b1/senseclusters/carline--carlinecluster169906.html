carline

noun
supporting roof beam
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɑːˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a horizontal structural beam in a roof or ship that supports other elements such as rafters or deck planks

Examples

Roof carline
The carpenter replaced the damaged carline in the old barn's roof.
During the restoration, a new carline was installed to support the ship's deck.
Without a strong carline, the roof structure could become unstable over time.
