amobarbital

noun
barbiturate drug; sedative and anticonvulsant
Pronunciation: / ˌæmoʊˈbɑːrbɪtɔl / (AmE), / ˌaməʊˈbɑːbɪtɒl / (BrE)
a barbiturate drug used as a sedative and hypnotic to treat insomnia and as an anticonvulsant.

Examples

Amobarbital drug
Because of its sedative properties, amobarbital must be administered under strict medical supervision.
Researchers studied the effects of amobarbital on sleep patterns in laboratory animals.
The doctor prescribed amobarbital to help control the patient's seizures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbiturate - any of a class of drugs derived from barbituric acid, used as sedatives and hypnotics
