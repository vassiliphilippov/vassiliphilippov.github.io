multicasting

noun
sending data to many
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌl.tɪ.kæs.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.kæs.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmʌl.tɪ.kɑːs.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.kɑːs.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of sending data to multiple recipients at the same time over a computer network

Examples

Multicasting data packets
Many online courses use multicasting to deliver lectures to students in different locations.
The company improved its network efficiency by implementing multicasting for software updates.
Live sports events are often distributed using multicasting to reach large audiences simultaneously.

Related Terms
