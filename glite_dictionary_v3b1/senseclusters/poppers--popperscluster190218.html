poppers

noun
slang inhalant drug
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpərz / (AmE), / ˈpɒpəz / (BrE)
a slang term for small bottles of chemicals, usually alkyl nitrites, inhaled for a brief feeling of euphoria or relaxation

Examples

To inhale poppers
He brought a bottle of poppers to the club to share with friends.
Some people use poppers at parties for a quick rush.
The effects of poppers are short-lived but can be intense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhalant - a substance people breathe in to get high
  • synonym: stimulant - a drug that increases alertness or energy
