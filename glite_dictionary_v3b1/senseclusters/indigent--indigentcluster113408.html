indigent

noun
very poor person
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who is extremely poor and lacks the basic resources needed for life

Examples

Shelter for indigents
The city opened a new shelter to help indigents during the cold winter months.
Charities often provide meals and clothing to indigents in urban areas.
The government created a program to support indigents who cannot afford basic healthcare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destitute - a person lacking the basic necessities of life
  • synonym: pauper - a very poor person with little or no money
  • antonym: wealthy - a person who has a lot of money and resources
