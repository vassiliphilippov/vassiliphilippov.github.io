Tineola

noun
genus of clothes moths
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪˈnoʊlə /; / taɪˈniː.ələ / (AmE), / tɪˈniːələ /; / tɪˈnɪə.lə / (BrE)
a genus of small moths (clothes moths) whose larvae feed on animal fibers and fabrics
