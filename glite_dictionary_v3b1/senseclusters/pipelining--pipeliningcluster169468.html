pipelining

noun
sequential task processing
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpaɪp.laɪ.nɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpaɪp.lə.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpaɪp.laɪ.nɪŋ/ /; / /ˈpaɪp.lə.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
