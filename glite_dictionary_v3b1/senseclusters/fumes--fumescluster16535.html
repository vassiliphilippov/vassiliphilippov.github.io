fumes

noun
waste gases or vapors, especially from engines
Pronunciation: / fjuːmz / (AmE), / fjuːmz / (BrE)
gases or vapors, often harmful or unpleasant, released as waste, especially from an engine or machine.

Examples

Car fumes
The car's engine emitted thick fumes that made it hard to breathe.
Traffic congestion in the city often leads to a build-up of fumes in the air.
Workers were advised to wear masks to avoid inhaling the toxic fumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhaust - waste gases from an engine
