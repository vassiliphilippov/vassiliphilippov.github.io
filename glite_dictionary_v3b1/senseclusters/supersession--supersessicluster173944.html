supersession

noun
the act of replacing by a superior alternative
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pərˈseʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈseʃ.ən / (BrE)
the act of replacing someone or something with another considered superior; the process of being replaced.
