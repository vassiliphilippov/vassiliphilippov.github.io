supporter

noun
elastic band to hold up stocking or sleeve
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔɹɾɚ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
a band, usually elastic, worn around the leg to hold up a stocking, or around the arm to hold up a sleeve

Examples

An arm supporter
In the early 20th century, men commonly used an arm supporter to keep their shirt sleeves from slipping down.
She adjusted her supporter to keep her stockings in place throughout the performance.
The vintage dress came with a matching supporter for the sleeves.
