wine lover

noun
person who enjoys and knows about wine
3.0
Pronunciation: / waɪn ˈlʌvər / (AmE), / waɪn ˈlʌvə / (BrE)
A person who enjoys and has a strong interest in wine, often appreciating fine wines and sometimes possessing knowledge about them.

Examples

wine lover enjoys tastings
As a wine lover, she enjoys hosting tastings for her friends.
He considers himself a wine lover and travels to vineyards around the world.
The restaurant's wine list was designed to impress even the most discerning wine lover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oenophile - a person who loves and appreciates wine
