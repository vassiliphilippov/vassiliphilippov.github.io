psychologist

noun
expert in psychology
1.0
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who is trained and qualified in the study of psychology, especially one who studies mental processes and behavior

Examples

A psychologist studies behavior
After several sessions with a psychologist, she started to notice improvements in her mental health.
He decided to consult a psychologist to help him deal with his anxiety.
The psychologist conducted research on how children develop language skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counselor - professional who gives advice about personal problems
  • antonym: psychiatrist - medical doctor specializing in mental illness
