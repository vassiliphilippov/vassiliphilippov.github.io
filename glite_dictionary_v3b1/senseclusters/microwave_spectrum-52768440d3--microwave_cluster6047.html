microwave spectrum

noun
part of the electromagnetic spectrum for microwaves
5.8 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.krə.weɪv ˈspek.trəm/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krə.weɪv ˈspek.trəm/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.weɪv ˈspek.trəm/ (BrE)
the portion of the electromagnetic spectrum that corresponds to the range of frequencies used by microwaves, typically from about 1 GHz to 300 GHz

Examples
