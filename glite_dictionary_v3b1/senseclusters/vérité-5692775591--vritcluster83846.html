vérité

noun
realistic filmmaking style
8.6
Pronunciation: /veɪ.rə.ˈteɪ/ /; / /veɪ.rɪ.ˈteɪ/ (AmE), /ve.ri.ˈteɪ/ /; / /veɪ.rɪ.ˈteɪ/ (BrE)
a style or quality in art, film, or reporting that shows things realistically and truthfully, without artificiality

Examples

Vérité style film
The director's use of vérité gave the film an authentic and raw feeling.
Many modern documentaries employ vérité techniques to immerse viewers in real-life situations.
Critics praised the vérité style for capturing the genuine emotions of the participants.
The documentary's vérité style made the story feel authentic and unfiltered.
Critics praised the film for its emotional vérité and honest portrayal of family life.
Her writing is known for its vérité, capturing the struggles of everyday people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cinéma vérité - a style of documentary filmmaking that emphasizes realism and naturalism
  • synonym: documentary - a film or television program presenting factual information
  • antonym: fiction - a narrative that is invented or imagined, not factual
  • synonym: naturalism - a style in art or literature that seeks to depict the natural world accurately
  • synonym: realism - the practice of representing things as they are in real life
  • antonym: romanticism - a style that emphasizes imagination and emotion over realism
