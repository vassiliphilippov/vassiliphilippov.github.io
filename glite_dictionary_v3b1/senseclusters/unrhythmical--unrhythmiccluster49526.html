unrhythmical

adjective
not having a regular rhythm
3.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪð.mɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪð.mɪ.kəl / (BrE)
not having a regular rhythm; lacking a steady beat or pattern

Examples

Unrhythmical footsteps
Her unrhythmical footsteps echoed through the quiet hallways.
The music was oddly unrhythmical and difficult to dance to.
The poem has a deliberately unrhythmical structure to disrupt readers' expectations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rhythmic - having a regular pattern of beats or accents
