voter

noun
person legally entitled to vote
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈvəʊtə / (BrE)
a person who has the legal right to vote in an election

Examples

An eligible voter
Every eligible voter should participate in the upcoming election.
She registered as a voter as soon as she turned eighteen.
The new law aims to protect the rights of every voter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elector - a person entitled to vote, especially in a political election
  • antonym: nonvoter - a person who does not vote or is not entitled to vote
