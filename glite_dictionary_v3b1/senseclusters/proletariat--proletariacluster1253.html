Synonyms

  • working class
    2.8 7.63/m
    working class is the everyday term for manual or industrial wage workers, while proletariat is a more political, Marxist term emphasizing lack of capital.
  • labor
    2.3 44/m
    labor refers to workers as a social or political force, often in unions, while proletariat focuses on wage workers without property in Marxist thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bourgeoisie - the middle/upper class, especially as the capitalist class in Marxist theory
  • synonym: working class - the social group consisting of people who are employed for wages, especially in manual or industrial work
