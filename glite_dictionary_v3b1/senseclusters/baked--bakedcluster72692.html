Synonyms

  • sunbaked
    4.8 0.10/m
    sunbaked is dried and hardened by strong sunlight on surfaces outside, while baked covers drying or hardening by any heat or sunlight.
  • sun-dried
    3.4 0.38/m
    sun-dried is dried by direct sunlight, often to preserve food, while baked can mean drying or hardening by any heat.
  • hard-baked
    4.4 0.00/m
    hard-baked is baked until firm or very hard, usually by oven heat, while baked can include milder drying by sun.
  • scorched
    4.6 0.15/m
    scorched is burned and often blackened by very intense heat or fire, while baked implies drying or hardening without burning.
  • parched
    4.7 1.15/m
    parched is extremely dry, usually of land or plants after long sun and no water, while baked suggests drying by heat.
