Synonyms

  • step-up
    3.6 0.63/m
    step-up is a planned or measured increase in amount or intensity, while rise is a general word for upward change.
  • boost
    2.7 9.93/m
    boost is a helpful increase that often improves something, while rise simply means an increase in amount or level.
  • advance
    4.9 4.28/m
    advance is a formal increase in price or value seen in markets, while rise is a more general increase in level or amount.
  • hike
    3.6 4.19/m
    hike is a sudden, often large increase in costs or wages, while rise can be slow or small in amount or level.
  • upsurge
    5.0 1.14/m
    upsurge is a sudden strong jump, often in numbers or feeling, while rise is a normal term for any increase in amount.
