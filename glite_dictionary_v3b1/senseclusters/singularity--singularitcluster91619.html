singularity

noun

9.1
Pronunciation: /sɪŋ.gjə.ˈlær.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /sɪŋ.gjə.ˈlær.ə.tɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Black hole singularity
In physics, a black hole contains a singularity where gravity becomes infinite.
The mathematician explained how a singularity can cause equations to break down.
Some scientists predict a technological singularity when artificial intelligence surpasses human intelligence.
