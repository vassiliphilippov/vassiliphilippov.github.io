sapless

adjective
lacking sap; dry or lifeless
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpləs / (AmE), / ˈsæplɪs /; / ˈsæpləs / (BrE)
lacking life, energy, or vitality; dry, weak, or lifeless

Examples

A sapless tree
After decades of neglect, the old trees stood sapless and lifeless in the abandoned orchard.
The fire burned quickly through the sapless wood, which had dried out over the years.
The hot sun left the plants completely sapless and brown.
After the long illness, he felt sapless and needed help to stand.
She spoke in a sapless voice that betrayed her exhaustion.
The sapless branches broke easily under the weight of the snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry - free from moisture or liquid; not wet
  • synonym: feeble - lacking physical strength, especially as a result of age or illness
  • synonym: juiceless - lacking juice or moisture
  • antonym: vigorous - strong, healthy, and full of energy
