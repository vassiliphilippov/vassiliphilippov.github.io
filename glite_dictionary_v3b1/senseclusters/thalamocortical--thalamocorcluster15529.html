thalamocortical

adjective
relating to the thalamus and cortex
9.9
Pronunciation: / θəˌlæmoʊˈkɔrtɪkəl / (AmE), / θəˌlaməʊˈkɔːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or connecting the thalamus and the cerebral cortex of the brain

Examples

Thalamocortical pathways
A disruption in thalamocortical pathways can lead to neurological disorders.
Researchers study thalamocortical circuits to understand sensory processing in the brain.
The thalamocortical system plays a crucial role in regulating consciousness and sleep.
