Rocky Marciano

proper noun
American heavyweight boxing champion (1924–1969)
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑki mɑrˈsiˌɑnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɒki mɑːˈtʃɑːnɪəʊ / (BrE)
(1924–1969) An American professional boxer who held the world heavyweight championship from 1952 to 1956 and remains the only heavyweight champion to retire undefeated.
