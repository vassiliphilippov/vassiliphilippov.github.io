baby's dummy

noun
rubber or plastic soother for infants
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiz ˈdʌmi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbɪz ˈdʌmi / (BrE)
a rubber or plastic device for infants to suck or bite on, used to soothe them

Examples

A baby's dummy clip
She attached the baby's dummy to a clip so it wouldn't get lost.
Some parents worry about using a baby's dummy too much.
The mother calmed her crying child with a baby's dummy.
