crossbar

verb
to secure with crossbars
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɑːs.bɑːr/ /; / /ˈkrɔːs.bɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈkrɒs.bɑː/ (BrE)
to fasten, strengthen, or block something by adding one or more crossbars across it

Examples

To crossbar a door
They crossbarred the warehouse doors each night to prevent break-ins.
After the storm, workers crossbarred the windows to keep them from blowing open.
The old gate was crossbarred to make sure no one could enter the property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bar - to block or secure with a bar
  • antonym: unfasten - to undo or remove something that is fastened
