sportsman

noun
male athlete or participant in sports
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrts.mən /; / ˈspɔrtsmən / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts.mən /; / ˈspɔːtsmən / (BrE)
a person (usually a man) who plays sports, especially in a skilled, professional, or competitive way

Examples

A skilled sportsman
As a successful sportsman, he was admired for his dedication and fairness.
Several young sportsmen competed in the regional swimming championship.
The young sportsman trained daily to improve his performance on the track.
The sportsman accepted his victory with humility and respect for his opponents.
As a dedicated sportsman, he trains every morning before work.
Many young people look up to a successful sportsman as a role model.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amateur - a person who engages in a sport for pleasure rather than professionally
  • synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and physical exercise
  • antonym: sportswoman - a woman who engages in sports
