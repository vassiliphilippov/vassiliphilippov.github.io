eavesdropper

noun
one who secretly listens to conversations
Pronunciation: / ˈivzˌdrɑpər / (AmE), / ˈiːvzˌdrɒpə / (BrE)
a person who secretly listens to other people's private conversations

Examples

A secret eavesdropper
She felt uneasy, suspecting there might be an eavesdropper outside her office.
The eavesdropper hid behind the curtain, listening to every word they said.
The manager warned that any eavesdropper would face disciplinary action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snoop - someone who secretly tries to find out information about others
  • synonym: spy - a person who tries to get secret information about others
