high five

verb
to slap raised hands
2.7
Pronunciation: / haɪ faɪv / (AmE), / haɪ faɪv / (BrE)
to touch or slap another person's raised hand with your own as a gesture of celebration or greeting

Examples

To high five a friend
After scoring the winning goal, the teammates high fived each other in celebration.
She reached out to high five her friend after they finished the project together.
The coach encouraged the players to high five after every good play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fist bump - to touch fists together as a greeting or celebration
  • synonym: handshake - to grasp and shake someone's hand as a greeting or agreement
