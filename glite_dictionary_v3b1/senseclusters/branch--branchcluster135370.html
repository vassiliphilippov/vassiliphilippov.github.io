branch

verb
divide into branches or sections
3.7 2.3
Pronunciation: /bræntʃ/ (AmE), /brɑːntʃ/ /; / /bræntʃ/ (BrE)
to divide or split into two or more parts or sections

Examples

The path branches
As you hike deeper into the forest, the trail branches in several directions.
At the old mill, the river branches into two separate streams.
The main road branches off to the left just beyond the gas station.
As the river flows downstream, it will branch into several smaller streams.
Certain blood vessels branch off from the main artery.
The old oak tree began to branch widely after several years of growth.
