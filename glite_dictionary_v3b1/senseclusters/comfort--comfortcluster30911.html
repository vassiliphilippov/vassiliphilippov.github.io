comfort

noun
material ease; state of financial security
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmfərt / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfət / (BrE)
things that contribute to wealth, ease, or freedom from financial worry; the state of having a pleasant or easy life due to material possessions or financial security.

Examples

Live in comfort
After years of hard work, he finally lived in comfort and security.
Despite her success, she never forgot the struggles she faced before achieving comfort.
Many strive to provide their families with the comfort that comes from financial stability.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardship - severe suffering or deprivation, especially from lack of money
  • synonym: luxury - a state of great comfort and extravagant living
