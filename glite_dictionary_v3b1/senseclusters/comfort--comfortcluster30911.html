comfort

noun
material ease; state of financial security
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌm.fərt/ (AmE), /ˈkʌm.fət/ (BrE)
things that contribute to wealth, ease, or freedom from financial worry; the state of having a pleasant or easy life due to material possessions or financial security.

Examples

Many strive to provide their families with the comfort that comes from financial stability.
