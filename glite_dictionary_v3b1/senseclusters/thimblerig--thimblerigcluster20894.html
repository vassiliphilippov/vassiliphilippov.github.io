thimblerig

noun
swindling gambling game with cups and a pellet
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪmbəlˌrɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈθɪmbəlrɪɡ / (BrE)
a sleight-of-hand gambling game in which a small object is quickly hidden under one of three cups or shells, and the player wagers on its location, often used to cheat the unwary

Examples

The thimblerig game
Many have lost money to the classic thimblerig con over the years.
Police arrested a man running a thimblerig scam at the market.
The street hustler lured tourists into playing thimblerig on the corner.
