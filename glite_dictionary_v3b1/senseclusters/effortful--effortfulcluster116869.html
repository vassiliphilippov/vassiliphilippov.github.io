effortful

adjective
requiring great effort
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛfərtˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈɛfətfʊl / (BrE)
requiring great physical or mental effort; involving considerable exertion or difficulty.

Examples

An effortful task
Completing the marathon was a truly effortful achievement for her.
His effortful climb up the steep hill left him exhausted.
Learning to play the piano can be an effortful process for beginners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: difficult - not easy to do or achieve
  • antonym: effortless - requiring no physical or mental exertion
