cost-efficient

adjective
producing good results for the cost
2.4
Pronunciation: / kɑst ɪˈfɪʃənt / (AmE), / kɒst ɪˈfɪʃənt / (BrE)
producing good results without costing a lot of money; effective or productive in relation to the costs involved

Examples

A cost-efficient method
Choosing more cost-efficient suppliers allowed the business to increase its profits.
Solar energy has become increasingly cost-efficient as technology improves and prices decrease.
The new manufacturing process is cost-efficient and has helped the company save thousands of dollars each month.
