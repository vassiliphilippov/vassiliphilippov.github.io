verso

noun
reverse side of a coin or medal
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈvɜːr.soʊ/ (AmE), /ˈvɜː.səʊ/ (BrE)
the side of a coin or medal that does not bear the principal design, usually opposite the obverse

Examples

Verso of a coin
Collectors sometimes prefer the verso of the medal for its rare markings.
The verso of the coin was carefully examined for authenticity.
