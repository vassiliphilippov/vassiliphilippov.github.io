lower division

noun
a lower tier of a sports league
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.ɚ dɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ə dɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a lower level or tier in a sports league, typically with less competitive teams compared to the top division

Examples

The lower division team
After their poor performance last season, the football club ended up in the lower division.
Fans continued to support the team even after they moved to a lower division.
Several promising players were promoted from the lower division to the main team.
