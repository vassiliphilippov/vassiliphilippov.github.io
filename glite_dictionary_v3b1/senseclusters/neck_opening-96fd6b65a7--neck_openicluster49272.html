neck opening

noun
opening in a garment for the neck
4.5 3.2
Pronunciation: / nɛk ˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / nɛk ˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
an opening in a garment that allows the neck to pass through or a part of the garment surrounding the wearer's neck
