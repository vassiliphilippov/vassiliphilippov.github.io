neck opening

noun
opening in a garment for the neck
4.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /nek ˈoʊ.pə.nɪŋ/ /; / /nek ˈoʊ.pən.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /nek ˈəʊ.pə.nɪŋ/ /; / /nek ˈəʊ.pən.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
