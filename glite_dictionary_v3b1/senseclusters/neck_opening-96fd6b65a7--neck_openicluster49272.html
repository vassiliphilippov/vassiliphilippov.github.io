neck opening

noun
opening in a garment for the neck
4.5
Pronunciation: / nɛk ˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / nɛk ˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
an opening in a garment that allows the neck to pass through or a part of the garment surrounding the wearer's neck

Examples

The neck opening of a shirt
Check if the neck opening is too tight before buying the shirt.
She prefers dresses with a wide neck opening for greater comfort.
The neck opening of this sweater is stretchy, making it easy to put on.
