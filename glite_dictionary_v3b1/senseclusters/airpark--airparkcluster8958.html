airpark

noun
residential aviation community
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpɑrk / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpɑːk / (BrE)
a residential area or small airport where people can keep and use private airplanes, often with homes near a shared airstrip

Examples

A home airpark
After buying a small plane, they decided to move to an airpark for easier access to flying.
The airpark community hosts an annual open house for aviation enthusiasts.
Living in an airpark allows pilots to taxi their planes directly from their homes to the runway.
He keeps his plane at the local airpark just outside town.
Many pilots choose to live in a airpark so they can fly whenever they want.
The new airpark offers hangars and homes for aviation enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airfield - an area of land set aside for the takeoff and landing of aircraft
  • synonym: airport - a place where aircraft regularly take off and land
