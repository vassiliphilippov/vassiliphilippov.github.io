defeatist

noun
person who expects or accepts defeat
9.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfiː.tɪst/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfiː.tɪst/ (BrE)
a person who expects or accepts failure and does not attempt to find solutions or remain optimistic

Examples

A defeatist attitude
Despite the setbacks, their leader tried to eliminate any defeatist attitudes among the team.
He accused the critics of being defeatists who never offered constructive ideas.
Rather than accept that they would fail, she refused to become a defeatist.
