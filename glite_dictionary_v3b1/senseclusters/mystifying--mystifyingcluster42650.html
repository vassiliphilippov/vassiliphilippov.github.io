mystifying

adjective
hard to explain or understand
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɪstəˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Difficult to explain or understand; making someone feel confused or puzzled.

Examples

A mystifying event
Her disappearance was a mystifying event that the police struggled to explain.
The instructions were so mystifying that nobody could assemble the furniture properly.
The magician performed a mystifying trick that left the audience speechless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to understand
  • synonym: puzzling - difficult to understand or solve
