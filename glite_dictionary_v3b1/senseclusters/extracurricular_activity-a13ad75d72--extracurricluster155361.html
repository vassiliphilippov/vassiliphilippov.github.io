extracurricular activity

noun
activity outside regular curriculum
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjələr ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjʊlə ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
An activity at a school or college that is not part of the regular course of study, such as sports, clubs, or music.

Examples

A extracurricular activity club
Debate club is a popular extracurricular activity at their college.
Her involvement in extracurricular activities helped her develop leadership skills.
Many students participate in at least one extracurricular activity during high school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: co-curricular activity - an activity linked to the curriculum, supporting academic learning
  • antonym: curriculum - the subjects or courses offered by a school
