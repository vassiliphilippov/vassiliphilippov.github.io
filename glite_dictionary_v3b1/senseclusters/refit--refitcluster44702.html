refit

noun
act of repairing or equipping a ship again
4.0 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌfɪt / (AmE), / ˈriːfɪt / (BrE)
the process of repairing or equipping a ship, boat, or other vehicle again by replacing or fixing its parts

Examples

The cruise liner is in dry dock for a major refit before the next season.
The yacht needed a complete refit after years of neglect and harsh weather.
