bind off

verb
finish last row in knitting
6.3 6.2
Pronunciation: / baɪnd ɔf / (AmE), / baɪnd ɒf / (BrE)
To finish the last row of knitting by securing the stitches so that the work does not unravel.

Examples

To bind off knitting
After completing the pattern, she bound off the last row to finish her scarf.
He is binding off the sleeves of the sweater right now.
Make sure you bind off loosely or the edge will be too tight.
