hypervigilant

adjective
extremely alert
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈvɪdʒələnt / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈvɪdʒɪlənt / (BrE)
extremely alert and watchful, often as a result of anxiety, stress, or trauma

Examples

Be hypervigilant after trauma
After the break-in, she became hypervigilant and checked the locks several times each night.
Children who have experienced trauma may appear hypervigilant in new or unfamiliar situations.
His hypervigilant behavior made it difficult for him to relax in crowded places.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alert - quick to notice and respond to danger or problems
  • antonym: relaxed - free from tension and anxiety
  • degree lower: vigilant - carefully watching for possible danger or difficulties
