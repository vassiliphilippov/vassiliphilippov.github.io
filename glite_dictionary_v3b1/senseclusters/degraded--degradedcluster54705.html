degraded

adjective
lacking moral restraint
Pronunciation: / dɪˈɡreɪdɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈɡreɪdɪd / (BrE)
Having lost moral restraint; lacking the standards of proper behavior or morality.

Examples

Degraded morals
His degraded behavior shocked everyone at the dinner party.
She feared her brother would fall into a degraded lifestyle after losing his job.
The once respectable politician became degraded through years of corruption and vice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debauched - unrestrained by morals; indulging in sensual pleasures
  • synonym: dissolute - lacking moral restraint; licentious
  • antonym: virtuous - having or showing high moral standards
