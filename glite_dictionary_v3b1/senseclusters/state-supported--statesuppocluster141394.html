state-supported

adjective
funded and run by the government
Pronunciation: / steɪt səˈpɔrtɪd / (AmE), / steɪt səˈpɔːtɪd / (BrE)
funded and operated by the government of a particular state or country

Examples

A state-supported university
Many students choose to attend a state-supported university due to lower tuition fees.
Public broadcasting channels are often state-supported in many countries.
The museum relies on both private donations and state-supported funding to operate.
