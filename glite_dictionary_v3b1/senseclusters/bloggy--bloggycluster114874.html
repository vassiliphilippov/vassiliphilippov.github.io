bloggy

adjective
like a blog
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːɡi / (AmE), / ˈblɒɡi / (BrE)
informal, personal, or having the style typical of a blog

Examples

A bloggy style
The article's bloggy style made it feel more like a personal story than a news report.
Readers enjoy her bloggy approach, which makes complex topics easy to understand.
His emails became more bloggy after he started writing online regularly.
Her writing style is very bloggy, making the articles easy and fun to read.
The website's new layout feels more bloggy and less like a traditional news site.
He prefers a bloggy approach to sharing updates, using casual language and personal stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conversational - having a relaxed, informal style like spoken language
  • antonym: formal - following strict or official style and rules
