crawl

noun
a swimming stroke using alternating arms
3.4
Pronunciation: / krɔl / (AmE), / krɔːl / (BrE)
A swimming stroke in which the arms are alternately moved overhead while accompanied by a flutter kick.

Examples

The crawl stroke
He won the race using the crawl, which is his fastest stroke.
Many beginners find the breathing technique in the crawl challenging at first.
The crawl is commonly used in freestyle swimming competitions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freestyle - a swimming race in which competitors may use any stroke, typically the crawl
