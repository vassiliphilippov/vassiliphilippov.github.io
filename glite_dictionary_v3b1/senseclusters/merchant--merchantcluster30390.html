merchant

noun
person engaged in buying and selling goods
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.tʃənt / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.tʃənt / (BrE)
a person or company involved in the buying and selling of goods, especially one engaged in trade on a large scale or retail business

Examples

The village merchant sold everything from spices to silks.
