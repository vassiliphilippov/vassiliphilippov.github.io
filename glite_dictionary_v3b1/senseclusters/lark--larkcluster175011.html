lark

noun
carefree or mischievous adventure
Pronunciation: / lɑrk / (AmE), / lɑːk / (BrE)
an activity done for fun or amusement; a carefree or mischievous adventure

Examples

A harmless lark
Stealing the mascot was supposed to be a harmless lark, but it got out of hand.
The whole trip to Paris was just a lark; we planned nothing in advance.
We decided to take a midnight drive on a lark, with no destination in mind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventure - an unusual and exciting, often hazardous, experience or activity
  • synonym: prank - a mischievous act or trick played for fun
