noooooo

noun
dramatic refusal
9.8
Pronunciation: / noʊˈoʊoʊoʊoʊ / (AmE), / nəʊˈəʊəʊəʊəʊ / (BrE)
an extended or dramatic utterance of 'no' used to express strong refusal, disappointment, or despair, especially in informal writing or online communication

Examples

A loud noooooo
When she saw her favorite team lose in the final seconds, she let out a loud noooooo.
His text reply was just a dramatic noooooo after hearing the bad news.
The movie character fell to his knees and screamed noooooo in despair.
