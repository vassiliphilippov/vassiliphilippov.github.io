Sir Francis Drake

proper noun
English explorer and admiral (1540–1596)
Pronunciation: / sɜr ˈfrænsɪs ˈdreɪk / (AmE), / sə ˈfrænsɪs ˈdreɪk / (BrE)
(1540–1596) English explorer and admiral, the first Englishman to circumnavigate the globe, and a key figure in the defeat of the Spanish Armada.

Examples

Sir Francis Drake explorer
Sir Francis Drake is credited with being the first Englishman to sail around the world.

The daring exploits of Sir Francis Drake have inspired countless adventure stories.
