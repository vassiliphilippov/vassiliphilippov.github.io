tallowy

adjective
smelling of tallow
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtæloʊi / (AmE), / ˈtæləʊi / (BrE)
having the appearance, texture, color, or smell of tallow (animal fat or grease)

Examples

Tallowy smell
The old candle left a tallowy residue on the table after burning all night.
He wrinkled his nose at the tallowy odor coming from the kitchen.
The soap had a tallowy texture that made it feel greasy in her hands.
The old lamp cast a tallowy light across the dusty table.
His skin had a pale, tallowy look after weeks indoors.
She noticed the tallowy texture of the candle as it melted.
