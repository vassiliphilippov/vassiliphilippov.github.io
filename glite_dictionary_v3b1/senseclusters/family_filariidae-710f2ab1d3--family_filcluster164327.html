family Filariidae

noun
a family of threadlike parasitic roundworms
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli fɪˌlɛriˈaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli fɪˌlɛərɪˈaɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of threadlike parasitic roundworms that can infect humans and other vertebrates, some species causing diseases such as filariasis
