nineteenth

noun
nineteenth in a sequence
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪnˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˌnaɪnˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to the number 19 in a sequence; the position of someone or something that is number 19 in a series.

Examples

The nineteenth runner
He was the nineteenth person to sign the petition.
Her nineteenth birthday was a memorable celebration for the whole family.
The runner finished in nineteenth place out of fifty participants.
