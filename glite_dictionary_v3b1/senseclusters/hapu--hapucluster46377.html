hapu

noun
Māori subtribe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːpuː / (AmE), / ˈhɑːpuː / (BrE)
a Māori social group or subtribe, smaller than an iwi, with its own traditions and leadership

Examples

A tribal hapu
Each hapu has its own traditions and leadership within Māori society.
The festival brought together members from every hapu in the region.
She is proud to represent her hapu at the national gathering.
