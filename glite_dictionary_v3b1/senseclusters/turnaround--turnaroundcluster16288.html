turnaround

noun
reversal of an earlier decision
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnəˌraʊnd / (BrE)
a change of decision or policy to the opposite of what was previously decided

Examples

Policy turnaround
The company announced a turnaround in its merger decision after pressure from shareholders.
The policy's sudden turnaround caught everyone off guard.
Their surprising turnaround restored confidence among the employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: U-turn - a sudden or complete reversal in policy, attitude, or course of action
  • synonym: reversal - a change to an opposite direction, position, or course of action
