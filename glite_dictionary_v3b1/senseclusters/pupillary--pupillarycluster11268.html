pupillary

adjective
of the pupil of the eye
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuː.pə.ˌlɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.pɪ.ləri / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the pupil of the eye

Examples

Abnormal pupillary reactions
Abnormal pupillary reactions can indicate neurological problems.
Strong pupillary constriction occurred in response to the bright light.
The doctor tested her pupillary reflexes during the eye examination.
