impromptu

noun
an unprepared speech or remark
9.9 5.7
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɑmptu / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɒmptjuː / (BrE)
a speech or remark made without previous preparation; an extemporaneous statement

Examples

An impromptu toast
His wedding toast was an impromptu that left everyone laughing.
She delivered a clever impromptu at the end of the meeting.
The committee appreciated his thoughtful impromptu in response to the question.
