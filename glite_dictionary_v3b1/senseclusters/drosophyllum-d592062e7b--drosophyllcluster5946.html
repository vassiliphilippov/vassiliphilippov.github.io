Drosophyllum

noun
genus of Mediterranean carnivorous plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / droʊˈsɒfɪləm / (AmE), / drɒˈsɒfɪləm / (BrE)
a genus of carnivorous plants native to the western Mediterranean region, known for its sticky leaves that trap insects and its preference for dry, sandy soils
