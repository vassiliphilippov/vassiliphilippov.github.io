probe

noun
medical exploration with a flexible instrument
7.2
Pronunciation: / proʊb / (AmE), / prəʊb / (BrE)
a medical investigation in which a flexible instrument is used to explore an injury or body cavity

Examples

Medical probe
A probe can help locate foreign objects inside a wound without causing further damage.
During the surgery, the doctor performed a probe of the abdominal cavity.
The use of a probe was crucial to assess the extent of the internal injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploration - the act of carefully examining an injury or body cavity in medicine
