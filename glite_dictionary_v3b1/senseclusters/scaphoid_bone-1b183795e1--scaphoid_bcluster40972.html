scaphoid bone

noun
largest carpal bone on thumb side of wrist
Pronunciation: / ˈskæfˌɔɪd boʊn / (AmE), / ˈskæfɔɪd bəʊn / (BrE)
the largest carpal bone in the wrist, located on the thumb side and commonly involved in wrist injuries

Examples

The scaphoid bone fracture
A break in the scaphoid bone can cause persistent pain and swelling in the wrist.
Proper immobilization is crucial for healing a scaphoid bone fracture.
The doctor suspected a fracture of the scaphoid bone after he fell on his outstretched hand.
