pathologize

verb
to label as illness
9.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈθɑlədʒaɪz / (AmE), / pəˈθɒlədʒaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or consider a normal behavior or feeling as a medical or psychological problem

Examples

To pathologize normal feelings
Some experts warn not to pathologize everyday stress as a mental disorder.
It is important not to pathologize cultural differences in behavior.
Teachers should avoid pathologizing normal childhood energy as a problem.
Some people pathologize shyness, seeing it as a disorder rather than a personality trait.
The article warns against attempts to pathologize everyday stress and sadness.
Critics argue that schools should not pathologize normal childhood behaviors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medicalize - to treat something as a medical issue
  • antonym: normalize - to treat as normal or typical
