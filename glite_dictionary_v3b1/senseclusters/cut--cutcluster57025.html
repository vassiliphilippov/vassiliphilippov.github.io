  • cut through
    3.0 3.41/m
    cut through means going directly through or breaking into something, while cut can also mean many types of quick crossing.
  • slice through
    4.1 1.06/m
    slice through describes moving quickly and smoothly through material, while cut is more general and may not imply smooth motion.
  • knife
    7.4 0.21/m

  • transect
