blondish

adjective
somewhat blonde
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːndɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈblɒndɪʃ / (BrE)
having hair or color that is somewhat or almost blonde

Examples

Blondish hair color
Her hair turned a blondish color after spending the summer in the sun.
He wore a blondish wig for the costume party last night.
The puppy's fur was a soft, blondish shade that everyone admired.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: brownish - somewhat or slightly brown in color
