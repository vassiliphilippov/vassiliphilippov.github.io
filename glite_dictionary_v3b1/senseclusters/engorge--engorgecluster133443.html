engorge

verb
eat or fill oneself excessively
7.1 6.1
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈɡɔrdʒ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡɔːdʒ / (BrE)
to eat or fill oneself with food to excess; to consume immoderately

Examples

To engorge on food
After skipping lunch, he engorged himself on pizza when he got home.
During the feast, everyone engorged themselves without restraint.
The children quickly engorged on the candy at the birthday party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstain - to deliberately avoid eating or refrain from excess
  • synonym: gorge - to eat greedily; to fill oneself with food
  • synonym: overeat - to eat more food than needed or healthy
