unnourished

adjective
lacking nourishment
7.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàn…ù…™ Ét / (AmE), /  ånÀàn år…™ Ét / (BrE)
Not provided with the food or nutrients necessary for growth, health, or good condition.

Examples

An unnourished child
After weeks of inadequate meals, the children appeared thin and unnourished.
Plants left in the dark for too long often become weak and unnourished.
The unnourished soil could not support the crops for another season.

Synonyms

  • undernourished
    4.4 0.11/m
    undernourished means lacking enough food or nutrients for proper growth, while unnourished names not being given needed nourishment for health.
  • malnourished
    5.6 0.19/m
    malnourished describes poor health from wrong or missing nutrients, while unnourished focuses on simply not receiving needed food or nutrients.
  • underfed
    3.4 0.18/m
    underfed means not given enough food to eat, often in daily meals, while unnourished refers to lacking required nutrients for health.
  • ill-fed
    5.5 0.03/m
    ill-fed means poorly fed or cared for, often sounding old-fashioned, while unnourished is the neutral term for lacking proper nutrition.
