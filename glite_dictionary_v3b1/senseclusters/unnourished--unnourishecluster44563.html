unnourished

adjective
lacking nourishment
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnɝɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈnʌrɪʃt / (BrE)
Not provided with the food or nutrients necessary for growth, health, or good condition.

Examples

An unnourished child
After weeks of inadequate meals, the children appeared thin and unnourished.
Plants left in the dark for too long often become weak and unnourished.
The unnourished soil could not support the crops for another season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnourished - suffering from lack of proper nutrition
