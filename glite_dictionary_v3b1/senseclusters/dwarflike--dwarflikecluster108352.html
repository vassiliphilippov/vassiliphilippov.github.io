dwarflike

adjective
resembling a dwarf
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdwɔrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdwɔːfˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a dwarf, especially in being small in size or stature

Examples

Dwarflike small shrubs
The dwarflike shrubs lined the path, never growing taller than a few inches.
He admired the dwarflike features of the ancient figurine in the museum.
The mountain village was known for its dwarflike houses with low, sloping roofs.
