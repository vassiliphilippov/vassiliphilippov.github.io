Rhamnus purshianus

noun
Pacific coastal tree yielding cascara sagrada
9.9
Pronunciation: / ræmnəs pɜrʃiˈænəs / (AmE), / ræmnəs pɜːʃiˈænəs / (BrE)
A shrubby tree native to the Pacific coast of the United States, yielding cascara sagrada, a natural laxative.

Examples

Rhamnus purshianus tree
Rhamnus purshianus can be found growing wild along the Pacific coast.
Many herbalists use Rhamnus purshianus to produce natural laxatives.
The bark of Rhamnus purshianus is harvested for its medicinal properties.
