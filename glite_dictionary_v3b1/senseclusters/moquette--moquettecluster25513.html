moquette

noun
velvety upholstery and carpet fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / moʊˈkɛt / (AmE), / məʊˈkɛt / (BrE)
a thick, soft, velvety fabric, often made from synthetic fibers, used especially for carpets and upholstery

Examples

A moquette carpet
He chose a deep red moquette for the living room carpet.
Many vintage theaters still retain the original moquette on their chairs.
The seats on the old train were covered in moquette with colorful geometric patterns.
