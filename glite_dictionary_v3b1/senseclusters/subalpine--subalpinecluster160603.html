subalpine

adjective
of or relating to high-altitude regions below the tree line
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈæl.paɪn / (AmE), / sʌbˈal.pʌɪn / (BrE)
(of plants, animals, or regions) growing or occurring at high altitudes just below the tree line, especially on mountains.

Examples

Subalpine meadows
Hiking through the subalpine meadows in summer is a breathtaking experience.
Many subalpine plants are adapted to cold and windy conditions.
The subalpine forests are home to many rare species of birds.
