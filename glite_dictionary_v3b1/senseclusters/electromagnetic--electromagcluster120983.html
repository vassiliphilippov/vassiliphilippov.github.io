electromagnetic

adjective
relating to magnetism from moving electric charges
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or exhibiting magnetism that is produced by an electric charge in motion

Examples

Electromagnetic radiation
Cell phones use electromagnetic radiation to transmit information over long distances.
The invention of the electromagnetic telegraph revolutionized long-distance communication.
The scientist studied electromagnetic waves to understand how light travels through space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrical - relating to electricity
  • synonym: magnetic - relating to or exhibiting magnetism
