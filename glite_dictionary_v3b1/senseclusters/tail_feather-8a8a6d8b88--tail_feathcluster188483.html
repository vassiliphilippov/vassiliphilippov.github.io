tail feather

noun
a feather on a bird's tail
2.5
Pronunciation: / teɪl ˈfɛðər / (AmE), / teɪl ˈfɛðə / (BrE)
a feather that grows from the tail (uropygium) of a bird

Examples

A tail feather
She found a brightly colored tail feather on the ground during her walk.
Some birds lose their tail feathers during molting seasons.
The peacock spreads its tail feathers to impress potential mates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plume - a feather, especially a large or showy one
