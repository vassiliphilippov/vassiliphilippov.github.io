countian

noun
county resident
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntiən / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntiən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from a particular county

Examples

Local countian
As a proud countian, he always attended the annual county fair.
The new mayor promised to listen to every countian in her district.
Many countians gathered to celebrate the county's 200th anniversary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: local - a person from a particular area
