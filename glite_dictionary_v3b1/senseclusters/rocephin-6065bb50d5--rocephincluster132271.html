Rocephin

noun
brand name for an injectable antibiotic
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.sə.fɪn/ /; / /ˈroʊ.se.fɪn/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.sə.fɪn/ /; / /ˈrəʊ.se.fɪn/ (BrE)
A brand name for ceftriaxone, a cephalosporin antibiotic administered by injection to treat serious bacterial infections of the lungs, throat, ears, or urinary tract.

Examples

Rocephin is often given intravenously in hospitals for serious bacterial illnesses.
