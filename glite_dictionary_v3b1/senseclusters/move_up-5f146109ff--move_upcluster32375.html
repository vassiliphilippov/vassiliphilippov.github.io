move up

verb
rise to a higher position
Pronunciation: / muːv ʌp / (AmE), / muːv ʌp / (BrE)
to go or rise to a higher position or level

Examples

Move up a level
Prices are expected to move up as demand increases during the holiday season.
The elevator will move up to the top floor without stopping.
The manager asked us to move up a few steps so everyone could fit in the photo.
