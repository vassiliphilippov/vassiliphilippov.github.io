tubbing

verb
to bathe in a tub
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtʌ.bɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtʌ.bɪŋ/ (BrE)
to wash or bathe someone or something in a tub, especially by giving a bath.

Examples

To tub a child
She spent the morning tubbing the puppy until its fur was completely clean.
After a long hike, he enjoyed tubbing his tired feet in warm water.
The nurse was tubbing the patient gently to help them feel comfortable.
