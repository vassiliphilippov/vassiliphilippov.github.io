beatdown

noun
violent physical attack
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbiːtˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a violent physical attack in which someone is severely beaten

Examples

A brutal beatdown
The gang gave him a brutal beatdown in the alley after the argument.
After losing the game, the team felt like they had suffered a real beatdown.
Witnesses described the beatdown as quick and extremely violent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assault - a physical attack on someone
  • synonym: thrashing - a severe beating or defeat
  • antonym: victory - an act of defeating an opponent
