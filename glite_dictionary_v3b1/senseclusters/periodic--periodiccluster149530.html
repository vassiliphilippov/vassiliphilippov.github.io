periodic

adjective
recurring at regular intervals
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪriˈɑdɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪk / (BrE)
happening or recurring at regular intervals

Examples

A periodic event
Meteor showers are often periodic events that occur at predictable times each year.
She suffers from periodic migraines that interrupt her daily life.
The maintenance team conducts periodic checks on the equipment to ensure safety.
Earthquakes in this region are not periodic, but some natural phenomena elsewhere are.
The committee holds periodic meetings to review progress on the project.
The school schedules periodic fire drills to ensure everyone knows what to do in an emergency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuous - occurring without interruption
  • antonym: irregular - not happening at regular intervals; lacking pattern
  • synonym: regular - occurring at fixed intervals
