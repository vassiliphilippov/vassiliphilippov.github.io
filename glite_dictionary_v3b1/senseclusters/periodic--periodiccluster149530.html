Synonyms

  • periodical
    4.4 0.6/m
    periodical is used for things that come out at fixed times, while periodic describes events that repeat at regular intervals.
  • recurring
    3.3 0.00/m
    recurring means something appears again over time, while periodic emphasizes a fixed regular interval between each occurrence.
  • recurrent
    4.7 3/m
    recurrent describes events that happen again and often, while periodic stresses they come at steady, regular intervals.
  • cyclical
    5.1 3/m
    cyclical refers to processes that move in cycles like seasons, while periodic simply means they repeat at set intervals.
  • cyclic
    4.8 1/m
    cyclic describes a repeating pattern with a clear loop, while periodic focuses on timing of each repetition.
  • regular
    1.0 70/m
    regular means happening at fixed or usual times or in a usual way, while periodic stresses repeated intervals.
