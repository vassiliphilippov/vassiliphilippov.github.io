Siamese

adjective
relating to the Thai language(s)
Pronunciation: / saɪəˈmiz / (AmE), / saɪəˈmiːz / (BrE)
of or relating to the language or languages of the Thai people, especially as spoken in Thailand; Thai.

Examples

Siamese language
He wrote a paper comparing Siamese and Lao linguistic features.
Linguists have studied the phonetic structure of the Siamese language in depth.
The old grammar books often refer to Thai as Siamese.
