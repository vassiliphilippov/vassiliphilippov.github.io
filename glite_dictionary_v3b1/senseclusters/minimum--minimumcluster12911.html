minimum

noun
smallest possible amount or number
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪməm / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməm / (BrE)
the smallest amount or number that is possible or allowed

Examples

A minimum amount
Five dollars is the minimum you can spend to get free shipping.
The temperature fell to its minimum during the night.
You need at least the minimum amount of experience to qualify for the job.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maximum - the greatest possible quantity
  • degree higher: minimum wage - the lowest wage permitted by law or a special agreement
