conference

verb
to hold discussions
4.0 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀênf…ôr…ôns / (AmE), / Ààk…ínf…ôr…ôns / (BrE)
to consult or discuss with others, especially in a formal meeting, to exchange ideas or make decisions

Examples

To conference with colleagues
The managers spent the morning conferencing about the new project proposal.
We will be conferencing with our partners overseas via video call tomorrow.
After conferencing for hours, the team finally reached a decision.
