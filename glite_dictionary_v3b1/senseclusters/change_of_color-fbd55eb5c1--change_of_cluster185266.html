change of color

noun
an act or process causing a different color
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ ɒv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
an act or process in which something takes on a different color, usually as a result of a change in the light it reflects

Examples

A change of color in solution
A change of color in the solution indicated a chemical reaction.
Chameleons can perform a rapid change of color to blend into their surroundings.
The autumn leaves undergo a remarkable change of color every year.
