rego

verb
to register vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈriːɡəʊ / (BrE)
to register a vehicle or renew its official registration, especially by paying the required fee.

Examples

To rego a car
You need to rego your car before the current registration expires.
He forgot to rego his van and received a fine from the authorities.
Many people rego their vehicles online to save time at the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deregister - to remove something from an official register
  • synonym: register - to officially record or enroll something, such as a vehicle
