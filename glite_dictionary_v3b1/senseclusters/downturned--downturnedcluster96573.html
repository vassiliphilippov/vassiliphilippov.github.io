downturned

adjective
facing downward
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌtɝnd / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌtɜːnd / (BrE)
pointing or facing downward, often suggesting sadness, decline, or a negative change

Examples

A downturned mouth
His downturned mouth made him look as if he were always sad.
The artist painted a downturned flower to symbolize loss and disappointment.
She noticed his downturned eyes as he spoke quietly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drooping - hanging or bending downward
  • antonym: upturned - pointing or facing upward
