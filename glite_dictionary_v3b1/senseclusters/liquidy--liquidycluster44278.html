liquidy

adjective
smooth and flowing
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪdi / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪdi / (BrE)
having a smooth, flowing, or fluid quality, often used to describe sound, movement, or style.

Examples

Liquidy movement
The pianist played with a liquidy grace that captivated the audience.
Her liquidy laughter echoed through the quiet room, light and effortless.
The artist's brushstrokes had a liquidy quality, blending colors seamlessly across the canvas.
