linga

noun
grammatical gender
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡə / (BrE)
the grammatical category of gender, such as masculine, feminine, or neuter, in Sanskrit and related languages

Examples

Noun linga in Sanskrit
In Sanskrit grammar, every noun has a linga that determines its form and agreement.
The teacher explained how to identify the linga of different words in the text.
Understanding linga is essential for correctly forming sentences in Sanskrit.
