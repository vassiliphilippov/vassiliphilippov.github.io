barrel cactus

noun
large, barrel-shaped cactus
Pronunciation: / ˈbærəl ˌkæktəs /; / ˈbærəl ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈbærəl ˌkæktəs /; / ˈbærəl ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
a large, round, ribbed, and very spiny cactus found in deserts, known for its barrel shape
