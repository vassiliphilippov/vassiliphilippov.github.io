proleg

noun
larval abdominal leg
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊˌlɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊˌlɛɡ / (BrE)
a fleshy, stubby leg on the abdomen of some insect larvae, used for gripping surfaces

Examples

Insect prolegs
The caterpillar clung to the branch with its strong prolegs.
Scientists observed how the proleg helps larvae move across rough surfaces.
Without its prolegs, the insect larva would struggle to hold onto leaves.
