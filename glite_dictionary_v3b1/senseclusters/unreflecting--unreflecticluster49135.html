unreflecting

adjective
not thoughtful
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
not thinking carefully or considering things before acting or making decisions

Examples

Unreflecting decision
Her unreflecting response surprised everyone at the meeting.
He made an unreflecting choice that he later regretted.
The unreflecting actions of the group led to confusion and mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impulsive - acting without thinking carefully
  • antonym: reflective - thinking carefully about things
  • synonym: thoughtless - not considering the possible effects of something
