tent

noun
web resembling a tent or carpet (entomology)
Pronunciation: / tɛnt / (AmE), / tɛnt / (BrE)
a web spun by certain insects, such as caterpillars, that resembles a tent or carpet in shape

Examples

A caterpillar tent
After a few days, the tent had grown to cover much of the shrub.
The tent of webs protected the larvae from predators.
The caterpillars surrounded the tree branches with a thick tent spin.
