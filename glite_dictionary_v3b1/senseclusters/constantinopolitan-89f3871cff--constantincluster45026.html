Constantinopolitan

noun
person from Constantinople
Pronunciation: / kənˌstæntɪˌnoʊpəˈlɪtən / (AmE), / kɒnˌstæntɪˌnɒpəˈlɪtən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Constantinople, the historical city now known as Istanbul

Examples

A Constantinopolitan citizen
The famous Constantinopolitan was known for his knowledge of the city's ancient history.
Merchants from distant lands often traded goods with a Constantinopolitan at the bustling port.
As a proud Constantinopolitan, she shared stories about the city's rich cultural heritage.
