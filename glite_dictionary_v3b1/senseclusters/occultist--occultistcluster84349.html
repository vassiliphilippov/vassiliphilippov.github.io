occultist

noun
person versed in occultism
9.1
Pronunciation: / əˈkʌltɪst / (AmE), / əˈkʌltɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in or practices occultism, or is knowledgeable in occult arts

Examples

Occultists study magic
Many 19th-century occultists wrote books on alchemy and mystical symbols.
She considered herself an occultist, believing she could communicate with spirits.
The renowned occultist was invited to speak about ancient rituals and secret societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mystic - a person who seeks spiritual knowledge or enlightenment
  • antonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions beliefs or claims
