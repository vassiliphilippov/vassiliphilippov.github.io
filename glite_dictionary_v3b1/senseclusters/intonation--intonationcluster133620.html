The act of singing or speaking in a continuous, monotonous tone without variation in pitch.

Examples

Monotonous intonation
His intonation while chanting was flat and lacked variety.
Meditation practices sometimes include intonation of mantras in a steady tone.
The intonation of the choir was criticized for being dull and repetitive.

Synonyms

  • monotone
    5.3 0.91/m
    monotone describes speech or song with no change in pitch and a dull sound, while intonation means performing that continuous monotonous tone.
  • chanting
    3.4 0.25/m
    chanting is repeated, rhythmic singing or speech often in a steady monotone, while intonation here means speaking or singing with no pitch change.
