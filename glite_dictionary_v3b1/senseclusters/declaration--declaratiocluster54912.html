declaration

noun
statement of taxable goods or dutiable property
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an official statement listing taxable goods or dutiable property, especially for customs or tax purposes

Examples

Customs declaration
Failure to provide a declaration of taxable goods can result in a fine at customs.
He made a false declaration about the value of the imported property.
You must submit a declaration of all items you are bringing into the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: statement - a formal or official expression of fact or opinion
