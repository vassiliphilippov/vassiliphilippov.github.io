Mithridates VI

proper noun
king of Pontus, opponent of Rome
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪθrɪˈdeɪtiz sɪks / (AmE), / ˌmɪθrɪˈdeɪtiːz sɪks / (BrE)
(c. 132–63 BC) Ancient king of Pontus who expanded his kingdom and fought against Rome, eventually being defeated by Pompey.
