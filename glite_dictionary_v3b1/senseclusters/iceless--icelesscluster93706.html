iceless

adjective
without ice
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈaɪsləs / (BrE)
not containing, covered with, or having any ice

Examples

Iceless soda
She ordered an iceless soda because she did not like cold drinks.
The lake remained iceless throughout the unusually warm winter.
He served the juice iceless to preserve its natural flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chilled - cooled, often by ice or refrigeration
  • antonym: frosty - very cold or covered with frost or ice
