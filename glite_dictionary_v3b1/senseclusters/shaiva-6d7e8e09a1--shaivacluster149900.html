Shaiva

adjective
of Shiva worship
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪvə / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪvə / (BrE)
relating to Shaivism or the worship, beliefs, or practices associated with the Hindu god Shiva

Examples

Shaiva temple art
The temple is famous for its Shaiva architecture and ancient stone carvings.
Many festivals in South India have strong Shaiva traditions and rituals.
Art historians study Shaiva iconography to understand the evolution of Hindu religious art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Shaivite - a follower of Shaivism or relating to Shaivism
  • antonym: Vaishnava - relating to the worship of the Hindu god Vishnu
