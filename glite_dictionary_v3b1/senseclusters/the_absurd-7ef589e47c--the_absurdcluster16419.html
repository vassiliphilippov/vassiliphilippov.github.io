the absurd

noun
philosophical concept: life's irrationality/meaninglessness
3.1 7.8
Pronunciation: /ðə əb.ˈsɜːrd/ /; / /ðiː əb.ˈsɜːrd/ (AmE), /ðə əb.ˈsɜːd/ /; / /ðiː əb.ˈsɜːd/ (BrE)
