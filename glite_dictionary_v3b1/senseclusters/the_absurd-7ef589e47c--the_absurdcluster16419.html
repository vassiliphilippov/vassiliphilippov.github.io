the absurd

noun
philosophical concept: life's irrationality/meaninglessness
3.1
Pronunciation: / ði əbˈsɜrd / (AmE), / ði əbˈsɜːd / (BrE)
the belief or state that human existence is irrational and meaningless, often referring to the philosophical idea that attempts to find inherent value or meaning in life will ultimately fail.

Examples

The absurd belief
For Camus, confronting the absurd leads to a deeper understanding of freedom.
His writing is marked by a continual reflection on the absurd nature of human life.
Philosophers often debate how humans should respond to the absurd realities of existence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absurdity - the quality or state of being ridiculous or wildly unreasonable
