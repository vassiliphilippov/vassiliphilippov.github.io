rear-view mirror

noun
mirror for seeing behind a vehicle
4.5 2.1
Pronunciation: /rɪər vjuː ˈmɪr.ər/ (AmE), /rɪə vjuː ˈmɪr.ə/ (BrE)
a mirror attached to the inside of a vehicle, allowing the driver to see the area behind the vehicle

Examples

Adjust the rear-view mirror
He adjusted the rear-view mirror to reduce the glare from headlights behind him.
Remember to glance at your rear-view mirror before reversing out of a parking space.
She noticed a police car following her in the rear-view mirror.
