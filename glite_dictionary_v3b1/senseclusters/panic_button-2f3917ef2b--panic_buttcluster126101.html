panic button

noun
emergency button to call for help
Pronunciation: / ˈpænɪk ˌbʌtən / (AmE), / ˈpænɪk ˌbʌtən / (BrE)
a button that can be pressed to summon immediate help, especially in an emergency situation

Examples

A panic button at home
Many elderly people have a panic button at home to call for assistance if needed.
She pressed the panic button when she heard an intruder in her house.
The bank has a panic button under the counter for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alarm - a device that warns or signals danger
