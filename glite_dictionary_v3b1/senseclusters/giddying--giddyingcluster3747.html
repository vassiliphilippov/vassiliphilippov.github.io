giddying

adjective
frivolous or flighty
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪdiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪdiɪŋ / (BrE)
causing or showing a lack of seriousness, often in a frivolous or flighty manner

Examples

Giddying jokes
Her giddying remarks made everyone laugh, but distracted from the main topic.
The giddying atmosphere at the party encouraged people to act more playfully than usual.
He found her giddying behavior charming, though it sometimes made serious conversations difficult.
