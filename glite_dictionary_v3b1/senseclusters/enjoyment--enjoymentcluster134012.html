enjoyment

noun
pleasure or satisfaction
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt / (BrE)
the feeling or state of taking pleasure or satisfaction from something

Examples

Simple enjoyment
Reading is one of my greatest sources of enjoyment.
She could barely contain her enjoyment as the concert began.
They watched the children playing with obvious enjoyment.
People find enjoyment in different hobbies, from painting to hiking.
She gained great enjoyment from reading her favorite novels.
The children's laughter was a clear sign of their enjoyment at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment
