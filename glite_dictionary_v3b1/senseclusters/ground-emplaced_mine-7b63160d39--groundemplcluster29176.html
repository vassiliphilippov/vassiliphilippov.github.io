ground-emplaced mine

noun
explosive device hidden underground
5.6 7.2
Pronunciation: /graʊnd.ɪm.ˈpleɪst maɪn/ /; / /graʊnd ɪm.ˈpleɪst maɪn/ (AmE), /graʊnd.ɪm.ˈpleɪst maɪn/ /; / /graʊnd ɪm.ˈpleɪst maɪn/ (BrE)
