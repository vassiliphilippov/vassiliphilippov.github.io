producer gas

noun
mixture of gases used as fuel
Pronunciation: / prəˈdusɚ ɡæs / (AmE), / prəˈdjuːsə ɡas / (BrE)
a gas mixture of carbon monoxide, hydrogen, and nitrogen, obtained by passing air over hot coke and used as a fuel

Examples

Producer gas fuel
Early automobiles sometimes ran on producer gas when petrol was scarce.
Engineers improved the efficiency of producer gas for industrial heating applications.
The factory used producer gas as an energy source during the power outage.
