reception line

noun
line of greeters at a formal event
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛpʃən laɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛpʃən laɪn / (BrE)
a line of hosts and guests of honor who greet incoming guests at a reception or formal party

Examples

The reception line greets guests
Due to the size of the crowd, the reception line moved slowly but gave everyone a chance to greet the hosts.
Each guest shook hands with everyone standing in the reception line as they entered the ballroom.
The wedding coordinator instructed the newlyweds to join their parents in the reception line.
