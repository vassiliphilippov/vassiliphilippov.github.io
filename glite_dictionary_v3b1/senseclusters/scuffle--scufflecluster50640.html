scuffle

noun
disorderly physical struggle
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌf.əl /; / ˈskʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈskʌf.əl /; / ˈskʌf(ə)l / (BrE)
a short, confused, and disorderly fight or struggle, usually not serious

Examples

A scuffle breaks out
A scuffle broke out between the two boys over the last slice of pizza.
She lost her keys during a scuffle with the mugger.
There was a brief scuffle outside the stadium before the police arrived.
A scuffle broke out between the protesters and the police outside the courthouse.
She hurt her knee during the scuffle and had to see the nurse.
The boys got into a brief scuffle on the playground but quickly made up.

  • synonym: melee - a confused, noisy fight involving many people
