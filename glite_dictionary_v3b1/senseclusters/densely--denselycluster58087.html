densely

adverb
in a compact or crowded manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛnsli / (AmE), / ˈdɛnsli / (BrE)
in a concentrated or compact manner, with things or people closely packed together

Examples

Fog densely filled
Fog densely filled the valley, making it difficult to see more than a few meters ahead.
The city is densely populated, making housing expensive and public spaces crowded.
The plants were densely clustered together, leaving little room for sunlight to reach the soil.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sparsely - in a scattered or thin manner
  • synonym: thickly - in a thick or dense manner
