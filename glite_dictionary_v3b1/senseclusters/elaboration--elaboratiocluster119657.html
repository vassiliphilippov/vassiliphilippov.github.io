elaboration

noun
result of improving something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlæbəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌlæbəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of adding more detail, explanation, or complexity to something

Examples

An elaboration of details
Engineers presented an elaboration of the existing plan to increase efficiency.
Her research offers a new elaboration on traditional theories of learning.
The latest elaboration of the design includes several innovative features.
Her proposal lacked elaboration, so it was hard to understand the specifics.
The architect's elaboration of the design resulted in a beautiful, detailed building.
The chef's elaboration of the dish impressed all the guests with its complexity.
Her elaboration of the topic helped everyone understand the complex issues involved.
In his lecture, the professor offered a detailed elaboration of the theory.
The committee requested an elaboration on his original proposal before making a decision.

  • synonym: explanation - a statement that makes something clear
  • synonym: refinement - the improvement or clarification of something by making small changes
  • antonym: simplicity - the quality of being simple or easy to understand
  • antonym: simplification - the process of making something simpler
