pearlized

verb
to give pearly finish
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːlaɪzd / (BrE)
treated or coated to give a pearly or iridescent finish, especially to a surface or material.

Examples

To pearlize a vase
The artist pearlized the vase to make it shimmer in the sunlight.
Many luxury cars feature pearlized paint for a unique and elegant appearance.
She chose pearlized nail polish for a subtle, shiny look at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iridescent - showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
  • antonym: matte - having a dull or non-shiny surface
