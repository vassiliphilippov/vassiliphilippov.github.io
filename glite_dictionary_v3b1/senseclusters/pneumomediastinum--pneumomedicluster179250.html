pneumomediastinum

noun
air in mediastinum
9.9
Pronunciation: /nuː.moʊ.miː.di.ə.ˈstaɪ.nəm/ /; / /nuː.moʊ.mɪ.di.ə.ˈstaɪ.nəm/ (AmE), /njuː.məʊ.miː.di.ə.ˈstaɪ.nəm/ /; / /njuː.məʊ.mɪ.di.ə.ˈstaɪ.nəm/ (BrE)
the presence of air or gas in the mediastinum, the central space in the chest between the lungs

Examples

A pneumomediastinum diagnosis
The doctor diagnosed a pneumomediastinum after reviewing the patient's chest X-ray.
A sudden injury to the chest can sometimes cause pneumomediastinum to develop.
Symptoms of pneumomediastinum may include chest pain and difficulty breathing.
