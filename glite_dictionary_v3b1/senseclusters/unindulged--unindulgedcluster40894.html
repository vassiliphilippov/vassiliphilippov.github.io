unindulged

adjective
not satisfied
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdʌldʒd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdʌldʒd / (BrE)
not allowed to have what you want or not spoiled by too much generosity or pampering

Examples

An unindulged child
The child's unindulged curiosity made her even more eager to learn.
Years of unindulged ambition finally drove him to start his own business.
She felt restless, her creative impulses remaining unindulged for too long.
He grew up in an unindulged environment where discipline was valued over luxury.
The children remained unindulged despite their parents' ability to provide more.
Her unindulged childhood taught her the importance of hard work and self-reliance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indulged - having been allowed to satisfy a desire or wish
  • antonym: pampered - given a lot of attention and comfort
  • synonym: unsatisfied - not having had a need or desire fulfilled
