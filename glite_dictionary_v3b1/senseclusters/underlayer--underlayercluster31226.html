underlayer

noun
layer beneath another
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌleɪə / (BrE)
a layer of material that is positioned beneath another layer, often serving as a base or support

Examples

A waterproof underlayer is essential when installing new flooring in the bathroom.
The cake had a thin underlayer of jam beneath the icing.
