masked shrew

noun
a small North American shrew species
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmæskt ˌʃru / (AmE), / ˈmɑːskt ˌʃruː / (BrE)
a small, common shrew (Sorex cinereus) found in moist habitats of North America, noted for the darker coloring on its head and back

Examples

The masked shrew
Despite its small size, the masked shrew plays an important role in the ecosystem.

The masked shrew is frequently seen darting among the wet leaves in northern forests.
