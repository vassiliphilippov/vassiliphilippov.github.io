moldable

adjective
able to be shaped or formed
3.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊl.də.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈməʊl.də.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be shaped or formed, especially by being pressed or worked when soft

Examples

Moldable clay
Clay is highly moldable, making it ideal for sculptors and potters.
Many modern plastics are moldable when heated, allowing for easy manufacturing.
The children's modeling compound stays soft and moldable for hours of creative play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pliable - easily bent or shaped
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be changed or shaped; stiff
