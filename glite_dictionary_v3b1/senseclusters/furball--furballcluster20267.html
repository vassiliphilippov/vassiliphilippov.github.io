furball

noun
fluffy young animal
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːˌbɔːl / (BrE)
an informal term for a small, fluffy animal, especially a kitten or puppy, that is covered in soft fur

Examples

A fluffy furball
The tiny furball curled up in my lap and fell asleep instantly.
Each furball at the animal shelter was adopted within a week.
She posted a photo of her new furball playing with a ball of yarn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kitten - a young cat
  • synonym: puppy - a young dog
