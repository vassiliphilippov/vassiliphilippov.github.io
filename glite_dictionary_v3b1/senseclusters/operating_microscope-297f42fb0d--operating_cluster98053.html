operating microscope

noun
binocular microscope used in surgery
2.8 5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.pəˌreɪ.t̬ɪŋ ˈmaɪ.krəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ər.eɪ.tɪŋ ˈmaɪ.krə.skəʊp / (BrE)
a binocular microscope designed for use in surgery, providing a clear and magnified view of small or inaccessible parts of the body

Examples

An operating microscope in surgery
Advances in the operating microscope have revolutionized delicate procedures such as eye and ear surgery.
Proper use of an operating microscope requires specialized training for optimal results during microsurgery.
The surgeon adjusted the operating microscope to get a better view of the tiny blood vessels.
