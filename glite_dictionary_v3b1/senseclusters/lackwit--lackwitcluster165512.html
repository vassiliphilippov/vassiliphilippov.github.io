lackwit

noun
foolish person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlæk.wɪt/ (AmE), /ˈlæk.wɪt/ (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a person considered foolish or lacking intelligence

Examples

The lackwit fool
In the old play, the village lackwit provided comic relief with his silly remarks.
She called her brother a lackwit after he forgot his own birthday.
The teacher refused to label any student a lackwit, believing everyone could learn.
