postmortem

adjective
occurring or done after death
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈmɔrtəm / (AmE), / pəʊstˈmɔːtəm / (BrE)
occurring or done after death, especially relating to an examination to determine the cause of death

Examples

Postmortem examination
Investigators relied on postmortem findings to clarify what happened.
Some changes in body tissues are only visible during a postmortem inspection.
The pathologist conducted a postmortem examination to establish the cause of death.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antemortem - done before death
