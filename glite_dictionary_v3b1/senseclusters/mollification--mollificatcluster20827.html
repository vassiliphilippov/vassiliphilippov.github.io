Examples

Synonyms

  • pacification
    7.7 0.01/m
    pacification is calming people or places to restore peace after trouble, while mollification focuses on reducing a person's anger.
  • placation
    8.5 0.00/m
    placation is soothing someone by concessions or kind words to stop their anger, while mollification is calming hostility.
  • appeasement
    7.4 1.47/m
    appeasement is giving in to demands to avoid conflict, while mollification aims to calm an angry person.
