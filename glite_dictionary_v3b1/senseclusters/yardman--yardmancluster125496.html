yardman

noun
male worker in a railway yard
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdˌman / (BrE)
A man employed to work in a railway yard, often handling trains, switches, or freight.

Examples

Railway yardman
After many years as a yardman, he knew every track and switch in the depot.
Becoming a yardman required a strong understanding of railway safety procedures.
The yardman coordinated the arrangement of freight cars in the busy railway yard.
