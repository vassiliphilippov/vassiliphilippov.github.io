jousting

noun
argument contest
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
a contest or aggressive exchange of arguments or positions, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Political jousting
The political candidates engaged in lively jousting during the televised debate.
There was constant jousting between the two lawyers as they argued their cases.
The meeting turned into verbal jousting as each side defended its position.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreement - a situation in which people have the same opinion
  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion or argument on a particular topic
  • synonym: sparring - an exchange of arguments or verbal attacks
