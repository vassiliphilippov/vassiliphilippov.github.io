flakey

adjective
eccentric; unconventional; odd
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪki / (AmE), / ˈfleɪki / (BrE)
odd, eccentric, or unconventional, especially in a noticeable or strange way

Examples

Flakey person
He wore a flakey outfit to the formal dinner, complete with a feathered hat and mismatched shoes.
Her ideas were often dismissed as flakey by her more conservative colleagues.
The artist’s flakey behavior made him both fascinating and unpredictable.
