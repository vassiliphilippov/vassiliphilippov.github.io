still

noun
apparatus for distilling liquids
6.3
Pronunciation: / stɪl / (AmE), / stɪl / (BrE)
an apparatus or place for distilling liquids, especially for making alcoholic drinks

Examples

An illegal still
Authorities seized an illegal still hidden deep in the woods.
She studied the process of purification by observing the operation of a laboratory still.
The old whiskey was made using a copper still in the distillery.
During Prohibition, many stills were secretly operated in the countryside.
He built his own still to produce small batches of whiskey at home.
The police raided the farmhouse and discovered an illegal still hidden in the barn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distill - to purify a liquid by boiling and condensing its vapor
  • synonym: distillery - a building or place where distilling, especially alcoholic liquors, is done
