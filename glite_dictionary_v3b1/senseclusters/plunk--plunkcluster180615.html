plunk

noun
a hollow twanging sound
3.0
Pronunciation: / plʌŋk / (AmE), / plʌŋk / (BrE)
a short, hollow, twanging sound, typically produced by plucking a string or striking a similar object

Examples

A plunk of a string
A sudden plunk rang out as she released the guitar string.
Every time the ball hit the metal bucket, it made a distinct plunk.
We heard the plunk of a pebble dropping into the pond.

Related Terms

  • synonym: twang - a sharp, vibrating sound like that of a plucked string
