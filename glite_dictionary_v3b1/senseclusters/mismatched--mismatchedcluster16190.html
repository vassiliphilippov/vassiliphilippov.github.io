mismatched

adjective
not matching or suitably paired
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈmætʃt / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈmætʃt / (BrE)
not matching or not suitably paired; not corresponding appropriately with something else

Examples

Mismatched socks

The designer intentionally created a room with mismatched furniture for a quirky look.
Their skills were mismatched, making teamwork difficult.
