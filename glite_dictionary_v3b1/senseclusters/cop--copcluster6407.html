cop

verb
to receive criticism
Pronunciation: / kɑp / (AmE), / kɒp / (BrE)
to receive or endure something unpleasant, such as punishment, criticism, or a blow.

Examples

To cop criticism
After the match he was copping heavy criticism from fans and the press.
She knew she would be copping a lot of blame for the mistake.
The player copped a harsh penalty for breaking the rules.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoid - to keep away from or not experience something
  • synonym: receive - to be given or experience something
