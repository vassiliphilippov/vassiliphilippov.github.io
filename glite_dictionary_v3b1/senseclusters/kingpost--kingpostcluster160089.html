kingpost

noun
vertical support post
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋpəʊst / (BrE)
a strong vertical post on a ship or dock used to support cargo-handling equipment such as pulleys or booms

Examples

Ship kingpost
The crew attached the crane to the kingpost before moving the cargo.
A sturdy kingpost is essential for safely lifting heavy loads on the dock.
Inspect the kingpost regularly to ensure it can support the ship's equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beam - a long, sturdy piece of wood or metal used to support weight
  • synonym: mast - a tall vertical pole on a ship that supports sails or equipment
