unproductively

adverb
in an unproductive manner
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prəˈdʌk.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈdʌk.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that does not achieve useful results or produce desired outcomes.

Examples

Work unproductively
If you handle your time unproductively, you may miss important deadlines.
She spent the afternoon unproductively, scrolling through social media instead of working.
The team was criticized for using their resources unproductively during the project.
