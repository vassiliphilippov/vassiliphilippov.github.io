Synonyms

  • mythical
    6.0 4.62/m
    mythical is used for things from old stories and myths, while mythic describes qualities like those of legend or myth.
  • mythological
    6.8 2.12/m
    mythological refers to traditional myths and their characters in stories, while mythic describes the legendary quality seen in people or events.
  • fabled
    7.2 1.79/m
    fabled is famous through old stories and often exaggerated, while mythic focuses on being like a myth or legendary tale.
  • legendary
    4.2 14/m
    legendary is very famous, often because of stories or achievements, while mythic highlights qualities that come from myth or legend.
  • fabulous
    6.2 0.47/m
    fabulous means wonderful or like a fable, often used in casual speech, while mythic describes things from myths or legends.
