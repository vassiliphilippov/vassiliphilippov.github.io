tootle

verb
play music casually (wind instrument)
7.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtuː.təl/ (AmE), /ˈtuː.təl/ (BrE)
to play a musical instrument, especially a wind instrument, in a light, casual, or aimless manner

Examples

To tootle on a flute
In the quiet afternoon, he would tootle on his clarinet by the open window.
She likes to tootle a few cheerful notes on her flute before dinner.
The band tootled happily as the parade passed through town.
