sandwichwoman

noun
female advertising board carrier
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsænwɪdʒˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈsænwɪdʒˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who advertises products or events by wearing advertising boards suspended from her shoulders, usually in public places

Examples

A sandwichwoman advertises
During the event, a sandwichwoman handed out flyers while walking with her advertising boards.
The sandwichwoman marched through the busy streets, drawing attention to the new cafe opening.
Years ago, it was common to see a sandwichwoman promoting local theaters outside the stations.
