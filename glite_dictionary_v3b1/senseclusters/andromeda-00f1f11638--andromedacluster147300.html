Andromeda

proper noun
spiral galaxy
7.7
Pronunciation: / ænˈdrɑːmədə / (AmE), / ænˈdrɒmɪdə / (BrE)
a large spiral galaxy near the Milky Way, visible from Earth and also known as Messier 31

Examples

Andromeda galaxy
Astronomers study Andromeda to learn more about the structure of spiral galaxies.
The Andromeda galaxy is expected to collide with the Milky Way in several billion years.
On a clear night, you can sometimes see Andromeda with the naked eye.

Related Terms
