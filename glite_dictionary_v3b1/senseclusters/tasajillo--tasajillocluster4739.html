tasajillo

noun
small desert cactus
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɑːsɑːˈxiːjoʊ / (AmE), / tæsəˈhiːjəʊ / (BrE)
a small cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, known for its slender, jointed stems and spines
