democratizer

noun
promoter of democracy
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːkrəˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒkrəˌtaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who promotes or helps establish democratic government, institutions, or practices

Examples

Political democratizer
As a leading democratizer, he supported free elections in several countries.
The organization acted as a democratizer by encouraging open government and citizen participation.
Many historians consider her a key democratizer in the nation's political transformation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: autocrat - a ruler with absolute power, not supporting democracy
  • synonym: reformer - a person who works to improve or change a system
