hospitalman

noun
Navy medical rank
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːspɪtəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtəlˌmæn / (BrE)
an enlisted medical rank in the United States Navy responsible for providing basic healthcare services

Examples

Navy hospitalman care
The hospitalman quickly responded to the injured sailor during the training exercise.
After completing his training, he was promoted to hospitalman in the Navy.
A hospitalman is often the first to provide medical care on a Navy ship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corpsman - a medical specialist in the military who provides healthcare to service members
  • synonym: medic - a person trained to provide medical care, especially in the military
