Anoa mindorensis

noun
small wild buffalo of Mindoro, Philippines
Pronunciation: / ˌænoʊ.ə mɪnˌdɔːˈrɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌænəʊ.ə mɪnˌdɒˈrɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a small, endangered wild buffalo native to the island of Mindoro in the Philippines, also called the Mindoro dwarf buffalo or tamaraw
