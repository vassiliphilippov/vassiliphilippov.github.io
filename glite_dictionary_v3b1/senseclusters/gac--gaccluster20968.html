gac

noun
Asian climbing plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡæk / (AmE), / ɡæk / (BrE)
a climbing plant of the gourd family, Momordica cochinchinensis, that produces an orange-red fruit used in Southeast Asian cuisine
