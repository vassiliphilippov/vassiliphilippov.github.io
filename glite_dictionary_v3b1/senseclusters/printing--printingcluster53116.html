printing

noun
handwriting in print style
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
text that is handwritten to resemble printed type, rather than written in cursive or script

Examples

Neat printing
He prefers printing over cursive because it is easier to read.
Her printing is so neat that it almost looks typed.
The teacher asked the students to use printing so their handwriting would be clear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cursive - handwritten text in joined, flowing style
