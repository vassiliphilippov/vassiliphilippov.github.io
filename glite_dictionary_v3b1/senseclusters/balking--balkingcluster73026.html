balking

adjective
refusing to proceed or cooperate
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
unwilling or refusing to proceed, act, or cooperate

Examples

Balking horse
After much coaxing, the balking horse finally began to cross the stream.
Negotiations stalled due to a balking representative who objected to every proposal.
The balking employee refused to complete the assigned tasks without further explanation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooperative - willing to work or act together with others
