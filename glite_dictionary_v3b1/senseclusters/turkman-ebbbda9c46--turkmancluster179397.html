Turkman

proper noun
Turkic ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrkmæn / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkmən / (BrE)
a member of a Turkic ethnic group living mainly in Central Asia and the Middle East

Examples

Turkman ethnic group
The Turkman people have a rich tradition of weaving carpets.
Many Turkman communities can be found in Turkmenistan and northern Iran.
A Turkman festival was held to celebrate their cultural heritage.
