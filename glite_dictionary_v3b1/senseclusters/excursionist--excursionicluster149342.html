excursionist

noun
person taking a short pleasure trip
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskɜrʒənɪst / (AmE), / ɪkˈskɜːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who goes on an excursion, especially a short journey for pleasure or sightseeing

Examples

Group of excursionists
Groups of excursionists visited the museum as part of their day trip.
The excursionist boarded the bus early to see the famous landmarks of the city.
Unlike a tourist who stays overnight, an excursionist usually returns on the same day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tourist - a person who is traveling for pleasure
