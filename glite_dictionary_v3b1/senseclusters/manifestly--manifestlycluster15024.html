manifestly

adverb
clearly and obviously
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌfɛstli / (AmE), / ˈmanɪfɛstli / (BrE)
In a way that is clear and obvious; unmistakably.

Examples

Is manifestly clear
His explanation was manifestly false, as the facts did not support it.
She was manifestly relieved when the project was finally completed.
The new policy is manifestly unfair to small businesses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: covertly - in a way that is not openly acknowledged or displayed; secretly
  • synonym: evidently - in a way that is clearly seen or understood; obviously
  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily perceived or understood; clearly
