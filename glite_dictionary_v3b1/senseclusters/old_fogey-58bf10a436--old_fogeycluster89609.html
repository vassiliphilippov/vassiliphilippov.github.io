old fogey

noun
an old-fashioned or out-of-touch person
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈfoʊɡi / (AmE), / əʊld ˈfəʊɡi / (BrE)
a person who is old-fashioned or has outdated views, especially one who is overly conservative or resistant to change

Examples

An old fogey attitude
My grandfather can be an old fogey about new technology.
Stop acting like an old fogey and join us on the dance floor!
The club was full of old fogeys stuck in their ways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuddy-duddy - a person with old-fashioned ideas
