Synonyms

  • clash
    3.2 0.8/m
    clash describes a sharp, obvious opposition between people or ideas, while conflict is a wider state of disagreement or incompatibility.
  • collide
    4.4 2/m
    collide implies a sudden or forceful meeting of ideas or interests, while conflict is any opposition or lack of agreement.
  • disagree
    1.6 1/m
    disagree means having different opinions between people in a calm way, while conflict suggests stronger, active opposition or clash.
