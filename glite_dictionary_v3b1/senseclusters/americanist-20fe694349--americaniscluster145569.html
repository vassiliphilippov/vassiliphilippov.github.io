Americanist

noun
supporter of Americanism
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkənɪst / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or studies Americanism, especially the promotion of American cultural, political, or economic influence

Examples

A political Americanist
The historian is a well-known Americanist who has published several books on U.S. foreign policy.
As an Americanist, she often speaks about the influence of American culture around the world.
The conference brought together many Americanists to discuss the spread of American values.

Related Terms

  • antonym: internationalist - a person who supports greater cooperation among nations
  • synonym: nationalist - a person who strongly identifies with and supports their own nation
  • synonym: patriot - a person who loves and strongly supports their country
