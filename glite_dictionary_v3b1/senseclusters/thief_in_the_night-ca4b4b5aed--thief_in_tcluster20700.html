thief in the night

noun
secretly or unexpectedly, without warning
3.2 6.8
Pronunciation: /θiː ɪn ðə naɪt/ /; / /θiː ɪn ðiː naɪt/ (AmE), /θiː ɪn ðə naɪt/ /; / /θiː ɪn ðiː naɪt/ (BrE)
If something happens or someone acts like a thief in the night, it occurs suddenly and without warning, usually in a secretive or stealthy way.

Examples

Like a thief in the night
He vanished from the town like a thief in the night, leaving no trace behind.
The disease struck the population like a thief in the night, taking everyone by surprise.
The rumors spread through the company like a thief in the night, unnoticed until it was too late.

  • synonym: stealthy - acting in a secretive or sneaky way
