propositionally

adverb
in a proposing manner
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːpəˈzɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌprɒpəˈzɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or expresses a proposal, suggestion, or proposition.

Examples

Propositionally suggest ideas
The manager spoke propositionally, offering several ideas for the team to consider.
During the meeting, she presented her arguments propositionally rather than as final decisions.
He always communicates propositionally, inviting feedback and discussion from others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assertively - in a way that states something confidently
  • synonym: suggestively - in a way that suggests or proposes something
