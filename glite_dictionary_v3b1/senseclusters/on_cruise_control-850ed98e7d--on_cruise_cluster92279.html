on cruise control

adverb
continuing with minimal effort or attention
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɑn kruːz kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ɒn kruːz kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
operating or continuing with minimal effort or oversight, often after achieving a certain level of progress or stability

Examples

Set on cruise control

Once her team was trained, she managed the department on cruise control for the rest of the year.
Since the company became profitable, things have been running on cruise control.
