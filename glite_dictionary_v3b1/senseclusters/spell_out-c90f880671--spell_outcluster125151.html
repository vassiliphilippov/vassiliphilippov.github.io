spell out

verb
say each letter in a word or name
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: / spɛl aʊt / (AmE), / spɛl aʊt / (BrE)
to say or write each letter of a word or name one by one, usually to make the spelling clear

Examples

To spell out a name
Could you please spell out your last name for me?
He had to spell out the address over the phone to make sure they understood.
The teacher asked each student to spell out their assigned word aloud.
Please spell out your last name so I can write it down correctly.
She patiently spelled out each word for the child.
When filling out official forms, you must spell out abbreviations.
