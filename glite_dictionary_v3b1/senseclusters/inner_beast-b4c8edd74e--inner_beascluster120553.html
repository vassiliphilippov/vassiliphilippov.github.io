inner beast

noun
primal or aggressive instincts
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnɚ biːst / (AmE), / ˈɪnə biːst / (BrE)
a person's primal instincts or aggressive nature, especially when typically kept under control

Examples

Yoga helps many people tame their inner beast and find inner peace.
