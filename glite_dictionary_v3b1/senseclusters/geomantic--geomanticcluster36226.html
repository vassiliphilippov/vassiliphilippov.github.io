geomantic

adjective
earth-based divination
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈmæntɪk / (BrE)
relating to divination or mystical practices that interpret markings or patterns in the earth or ground

Examples

Geomantic reading
The ancient ritual involved a geomantic reading of patterns in the sand.
She studied geomantic symbols to understand the spiritual energy of the land.
A geomantic approach was used to select the site for the new temple.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astrological - relating to the study of the movements of celestial bodies for divination
  • synonym: divinatory - relating to the practice of seeking knowledge of the future
