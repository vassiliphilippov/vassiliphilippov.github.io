Wandala

proper noun
Chadic language of Cameroon with two vowels
Pronunciation: / wænˈdɑːlə / (AmE), / wænˈdɑːlə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken in the Mandara mountains of Cameroon, notable for having only two vowels

Examples

Wandala language
Linguists have studied Wandala to understand its phonological structure.
