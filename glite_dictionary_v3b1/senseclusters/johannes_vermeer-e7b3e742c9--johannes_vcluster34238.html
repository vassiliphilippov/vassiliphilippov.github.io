Johannes Vermeer

proper noun
Dutch painter renowned for light and interior scenes
8.6
Pronunciation: / joʊˈhænəs vərˈmɪr / (AmE), / jəʊˈhanəs vəˈmɪə / (BrE)
A Dutch painter (1632–1675), celebrated for his masterful use of light and detailed domestic interior scenes.
