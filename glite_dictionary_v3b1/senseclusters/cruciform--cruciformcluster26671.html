cruciform

adjective
shaped like a cross
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːsɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈkruːsɪfɔːm / (BrE)

Examples

Cruciform shape

Under the microscope, the crystals appeared cruciform in structure.
