unconsulted

adjective
not asked for input
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsʌltɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsʌltɪd / (BrE)
not asked for advice or opinion before a decision is made

Examples

Remain unconsulted
The committee made the decision while several key members remained unconsulted.
She felt frustrated after being left unconsulted about the changes to her project.
Many residents were unconsulted before the city approved the new development plan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consulted - asked for advice or opinion
  • synonym: ignored - not given attention or consideration
