investing

noun
the act of committing money or capital for profit
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛstɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛstɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of committing money or capital to an enterprise with the expectation of obtaining profit.

Examples

To invest money
Her passion for investing led her to a successful career in finance.
Many people start investing in the stock market to grow their savings.
Responsible investing requires research and understanding of potential risks.
