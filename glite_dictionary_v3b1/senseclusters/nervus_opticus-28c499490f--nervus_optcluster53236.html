nervus opticus

noun
the optic nerve (cranial nerve II)
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnɜːr.vəs ˈɑːp.tɪ.kəs/ (AmE), /ˈnɜː.vəs ˈɒp.tɪ.kəs/ (BrE)
the second cranial nerve, responsible for transmitting visual information from the retina to the brain; the optic nerve
