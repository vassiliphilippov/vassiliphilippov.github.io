item

verb
to list individually
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
to list or record something as an individual item, especially in a document or inventory

Examples

To item each entry

After the meeting, she itemed all the action points in the minutes.
Every expense was carefully itemed on the monthly financial report.
