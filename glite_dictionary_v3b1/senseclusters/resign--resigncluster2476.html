Synonyms

  • quit
    1.2 28/m
    quit is an informal, general way to leave a job, while resign is a formal, official act given with notice.
  • step down
    3.3 5.20/m
    step down is used when someone leaves an important post to let another take over, while resign is general official leaving.
  • vacate
    4.5 2.65/m
    vacate means to leave and make a place or position empty, while resign focuses on formally giving up a job.
  • bow out
    4.9 0.79/m
    bow out is to withdraw politely or with dignity from an activity, while resign is a formal, official job departure.
  • stand down
    5.1 2.59/m
    stand down tells someone to stop duties or action, often by order, while resign is a voluntary formal leaving of a post.
