Synonyms

  • go into
    1.0 47/m
    go into is used for moving through a door or starting a new state, while enter is the general word for coming inside.
  • go in
    1.0 52/m
    go in often describes walking into a room or place, while enter is the neutral, more formal word for coming inside.
  • get into
    2.0 15/m
    get into often suggests moving inside or starting to take part in something, while enter is more neutral and formal.
  • get in
    1.0 26/m
    get in is common for entering cars or small spaces and sounds informal, while enter is the standard and more formal term.
  • come in
    1.0 99/m
    come in often means moving toward the speaker when entering a room or building, while enter is neutral about direction.
