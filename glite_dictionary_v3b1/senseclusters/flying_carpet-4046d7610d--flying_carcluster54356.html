flying carpet

noun
magical carpet that flies
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkɑːrpɪt / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈkɑːpɪt / (BrE)
A magical carpet in Asian folklore and stories, believed to transport people instantly to distant places by flying.

Examples

A flying carpet story
As a child, she dreamed of riding a flying carpet over the mountains and seas.
He described the sensation as if he were gliding on a flying carpet above the city.
The old tale tells of a flying carpet that could grant any wish for travel.
