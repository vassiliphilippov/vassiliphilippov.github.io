jock

noun
athlete, especially in school or college
7.4 2.37/m
Pronunciation: / dÊ’É‘k / (AmE), / dÊ’É’k / (BrE)
an athlete, especially one who is enthusiastic about sports or regularly competes in sports, often used informally for male athletes in schools or colleges

Examples

High school jock
Although he's known as a jock, he excels in his studies too.
He was a typical jock in high school, playing football and basketball every season.
The jocks and the chess club rarely mixed at our school.

Synonyms

  • athlete
    2.0 56/m
    athlete is trained and often competes in organized events, while jock is an informal name for a sports enthusiast student, usually male.
  • sportsman
    2.8 1.07/m
    sportsman is someone who plays or competes in sports, often as a skilled or professional, while jock is an informal student athlete.

Related Terms

  • synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise
