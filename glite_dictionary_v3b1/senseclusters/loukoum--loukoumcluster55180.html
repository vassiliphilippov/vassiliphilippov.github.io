loukoum

noun
Turkish sweet
9.3
Pronunciation: / luːˈkuːm / (AmE), / luːˈkuːm / (BrE)
a sweet, chewy confection made from sugar and starch, often flavored and dusted with powdered sugar, also called Turkish delight

Examples

A piece of loukoum
She offered me a piece of rose-flavored loukoum with my tea.
At the market, we sampled pistachio loukoum dusted with powdered sugar.
He brought back a box of assorted loukoum from his trip to Istanbul.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candy - a sweet food made with sugar or syrup
