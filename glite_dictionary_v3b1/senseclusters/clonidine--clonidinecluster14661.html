clonidine

noun
antihypertensive medication
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈklɑː.nɪ.diːn/ /; / /ˈklɑː.nə.diːn/ (AmE), /ˈklɒ.nɪ.diːn/ /; / /ˈklɒ.nə.diːn/ (BrE)

Examples
