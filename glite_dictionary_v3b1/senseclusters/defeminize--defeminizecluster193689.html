defeminize

verb
remove the ovaries of a female mammal
9.1 8.7
Pronunciation: /diː.ˈfem.ɪ.naɪz/ /; / /də.ˈfem.ɪ.naɪz/ (AmE), /diː.ˈfem.ɪ.naɪz/ (BrE)
to remove the ovaries of a female mammal, making it unable to reproduce and making the animal biologically or behaviorally less female.
