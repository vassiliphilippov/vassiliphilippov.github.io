defeminize

verb
remove the ovaries of a female mammal
Pronunciation: / diˈfɛmɪˌnaɪz /; / dɪˈfɛmɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / diˈfɛmɪnaɪz /; / dɪˈfiːmɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to remove the ovaries of a female mammal, making it unable to reproduce and making the animal biologically or behaviorally less female.

Examples

To defeminize animals
After being defeminized, the animals showed different behavioral patterns during mating season.
The procedure to defeminize livestock is commonly done to control the population.
The veterinarian will defeminize the cat to prevent her from going into heat.
After being defeminised, the animal showed a change in hormone levels.
Researchers often defeminise lab rats as part of reproductive studies.
The veterinarian will defeminise the cat to prevent it from having kittens.
