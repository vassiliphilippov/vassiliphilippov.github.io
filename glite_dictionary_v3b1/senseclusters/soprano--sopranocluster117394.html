soprano

noun
female singer with the highest vocal range
3.9 3.7
Pronunciation: / səˈprɑːnoʊ /; / səˈprænoʊ / (AmE), / səˈprɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
the highest female singing voice, or a singer (female or boy before puberty) with this voice; also the highest part in choral music
