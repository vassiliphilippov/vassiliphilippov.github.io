xanthophore

noun
yellow pigment cell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzænθəˌfɔr / (AmE), / ˈzænθəˌfɔː / (BrE)
a pigment cell containing yellow coloring, found in the skin of some animals such as fish and amphibians
