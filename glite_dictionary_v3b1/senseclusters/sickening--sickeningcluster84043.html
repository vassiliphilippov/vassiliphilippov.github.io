sickening

adjective
causing nausea
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a strong feeling of nausea or disgust; making someone feel sick or revolted

Examples

A sickening feeling
After eating the spoiled seafood, he experienced a sickening wave of nausea.
The garbage emitted a sickening odor that made everyone feel ill.
There was a sickening stench coming from the abandoned fridge.
The sickening sight of the spoiled food made her lose her appetite.
He was overwhelmed by a sickening feeling after hearing the terrible news.
A sickening odor filled the hallway, forcing everyone to cover their noses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disgusting - extremely unpleasant or revolting
  • synonym: nauseating - causing nausea or disgust
  • antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment
  • synonym: queasy - causing or experiencing uneasiness or nausea
