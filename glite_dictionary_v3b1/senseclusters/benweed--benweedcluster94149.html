benweed

noun
European weed with yellow daisylike flowers
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈbɛnˌwiːd / (BrE)
A widespread European weed with yellow, daisylike flowers, known to be toxic to cattle if eaten in large amounts.

Examples

Yellow benweed flowers
Cattle farmers must be cautious, as large quantities of benweed can be harmful to their livestock.
Gardeners often struggle to control benweed because of its rapid spread and toxicity.
The fields were overrun with benweed, threatening the health of the grazing cattle.
