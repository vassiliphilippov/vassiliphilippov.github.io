bell ringer

noun
a resounding success; outstanding achievement
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛl ˌrɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈbɛl ˌrɪŋə / (BrE)
An event or achievement that is highly successful or exactly achieves its intended purpose.

Examples

A real bell ringer
Everyone agreed that her suggestion at the meeting was a bell ringer.
The launch of their latest smartphone was a real bell ringer in the tech industry.
Their new marketing strategy turned out to be a bell ringer for the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bull's eye - something that hits the exact target or is extremely successful
  • synonym: home run - an outstanding success, especially in American English
