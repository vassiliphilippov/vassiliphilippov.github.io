Thomas Stearns Eliot

proper noun
British poet and Nobel laureate
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑː.məs stɜːrnz ˈe.lɪ.ət/ (AmE), /ˈtɒ.məs stɜːnz ˈe.lɪ.ət/ (BrE)
(1888–1965) British poet, playwright, and critic, born in the United States, known for influential modernist works such as 'The Waste Land' and for winning the Nobel Prize in Literature.
