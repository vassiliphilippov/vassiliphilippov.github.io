stinking horehound

noun
ill-smelling European herb
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˈhɔrˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˈhɔːˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
an ill-smelling European herb with wrinkled leaves and whorls of dark purple flowers, commonly known for its pungent odor
