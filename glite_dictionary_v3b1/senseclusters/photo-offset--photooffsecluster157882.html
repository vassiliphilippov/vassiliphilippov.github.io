photo-offset

noun
printing using photomechanical plates
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈɔfsɛt / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈɒfset / (BrE)
a method of offset printing that uses photomechanical plates to reproduce images or text

Examples

Photo-offset printing method
Advancements in photo-offset made mass publication of books much easier.

The magazine was produced using photo-offset printing for higher image quality.
