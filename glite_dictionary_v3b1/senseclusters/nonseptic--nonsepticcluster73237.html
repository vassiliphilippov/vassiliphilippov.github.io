nonseptic

adjective
not infected
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛptɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛptɪk / (BrE)
not involving or caused by infection with harmful microorganisms

Examples

Nonseptic wound
The doctor confirmed that the injury was nonseptic and did not require antibiotics.
After surgery, the patient showed nonseptic inflammation, which is not caused by infection.
Proper cleaning helped keep the wound nonseptic during the healing process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aseptic - free from infection or contamination
  • antonym: septic - infected with harmful microorganisms
