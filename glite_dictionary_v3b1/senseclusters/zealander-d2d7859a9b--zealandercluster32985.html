Zealander

noun
person from Zealand (usually Denmark)
Pronunciation: / ˈziː.lænd.ər / (AmE), / ˈziː.lənd.ə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Zealand, especially the island in Denmark.

Examples

A Zealander from Zealand
