bake sale

noun
fundraising event selling baked goods
5.3
Pronunciation: / beɪk seɪl / (AmE), / beɪk seɪl / (BrE)
an event in which people sell baked goods, such as cakes and cookies, to raise money for a specific cause or charity

Examples

A bake sale event
Emma brought homemade brownies to the bake sale last Saturday.
Our school organized a bake sale to help raise money for new equipment.
The community center hosts a bake sale every spring to support local charities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fundraiser - an event intended to raise money for a cause
