raise

verb
build or erect (a structure)
Pronunciation: / reɪz / (AmE), / reɪz / (BrE)
to construct or build (a structure), especially by assembling materials over a period of time

Examples

To raise a building
Skilled workers were brought in to raise the steel framework of the bridge.
The community came together to raise a new barn after the fire.
They plan to raise several cabins along the river over the summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demolish - to completely destroy (a building or structure)
  • synonym: erect - to build or put up (a structure)
