charlatanism

noun
dishonest behavior of a charlatan
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrlətənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːlətənɪzəm / (BrE)
practices or behavior typical of a charlatan; dishonest or fraudulent pretensions to knowledge or skill

Examples

Blatant charlatanism
Such blatant charlatanism has no place in scientific research.
The medical board condemned his charlatanism and revoked his license.
Victims of charlatanism often lose both money and hope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraudulence - dishonest behavior intended to deceive
  • antonym: honesty - the quality of being truthful or sincere
