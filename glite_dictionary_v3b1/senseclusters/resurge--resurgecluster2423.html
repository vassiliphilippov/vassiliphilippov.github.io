resurge

verb
rise again; become active again
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɜrdʒ / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to rise or become active again after a period of inactivity or decline

Examples

To resurge quickly
After a brief period of calm, hostilities began to resurge in the region.
Interest in vintage clothing has started to resurge among young people.
The company's profits resurged after the successful product launch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - to decrease or become less active
  • synonym: rebound - recover in strength or position
