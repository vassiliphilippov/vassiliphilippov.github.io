sheeted

adjective
covered in sheets
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃiːtɪd / (BrE)
covered with or formed into sheets or layers

Examples

Sheeted in white cloth
The statue was sheeted in white cloth for protection.
After the storm, the fields were sheeted with a thin layer of ice.
The mountain was sheeted in snow, making it look completely white.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - not covered or clothed
  • synonym: layered - arranged in or formed of layers
