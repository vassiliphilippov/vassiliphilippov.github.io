otherworldliness

noun
quality of spiritual or mystical focus
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌðərˈwɜrldli.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌðəˈwɜːldlinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being concerned with spiritual, mystical, or supernatural matters rather than worldly affairs

Examples

Many poets seek otherworldliness in their work, drawing inspiration from dreams and visions.
The painting's otherworldliness lent the gallery an air of mystery and serenity.
