unlocalized

adjective
not limited to one area
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈləʊkəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not confined to or identifiable in a particular place; diffuse or widespread, especially referring to pain, symptoms, or processes

Examples

Unlocalized pain
The doctor noted that the patient's unlocalized pain made diagnosis more challenging.
Symptoms were unlocalized, affecting several parts of the body at once.
He described an unlocalized discomfort that seemed to move from place to place.

Related Terms

  • antonym: localized - confined to a particular place or area
