white supremacist

noun
person who believes in white racial supremacy
Pronunciation: / waɪt səˈprɛməsɪst / (AmE), / waɪt sʊˈprɛməsɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that white people are superior to people of other races and should dominate society

Examples

White supremacist group
He was arrested after spreading white supremacist propaganda online.
Many people spoke out against the views held by the white supremacist during the rally.
The group was founded by a notorious white supremacist in the 1960s.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-racist - a person opposed to racism and discrimination
  • synonym: racist - a person who believes one race is superior to others
