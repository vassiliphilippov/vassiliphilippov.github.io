hag

verb
to torment persistently
9.9
Pronunciation: / hæɡ / (AmE), / hæɡ / (BrE)
to harass or torment someone persistently, as if by a witch or evil spirit

Examples

To hag someone
He felt hagged by constant worries and sleepless nights.
The villagers believed they were hagged by an evil spirit haunting their dreams.
She was so hagged by guilt that she could not find peace.
