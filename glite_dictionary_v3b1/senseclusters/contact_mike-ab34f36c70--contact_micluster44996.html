contact mike

noun
microphone for direct-contact audio pickup
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tækt maɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt maɪk / (BrE)
A microphone designed to pick up sound through direct contact with a sound source or resonating surface, rather than from the air.

Examples

A contact mike device
She experimented with a contact mike on a window to record environmental sounds.
The musician attached a contact mike to the guitar to capture subtle body vibrations.
Using a contact mike, engineers could detect hidden mechanical faults in the machine.
