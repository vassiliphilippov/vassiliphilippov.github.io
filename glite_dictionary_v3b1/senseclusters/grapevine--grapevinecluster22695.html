grapevine

noun
rumor passed by word of mouth
3.7 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgreɪp.vaɪn/ (AmE), /ˈgreɪp.vaɪn/ (BrE)
rumor or unofficial information spread from person to person, especially through casual conversation rather than formal means

Examples

Heard on the grapevine
I heard through the grapevine that the company is planning layoffs next month.
Rumors about their relationship have been circulating on the grapevine for weeks.
The news soon spread along the grapevine, reaching people all over town.
