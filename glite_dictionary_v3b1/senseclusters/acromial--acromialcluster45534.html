acromial

adjective
of the shoulder acromion
Pronunciation: / əˈkroʊmiəl / (AmE), / əˈkrəʊmiəl / (BrE)
relating to the acromion, the bony process on the top of the shoulder blade

Examples

Acromial process
The doctor examined the acromial area for any signs of swelling or tenderness.
An acromial fracture can cause significant pain and limit shoulder movement.
Physical therapy may help restore strength after an acromial injury.
