heart

noun
playing card suit marked with a heart
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɑrt / (AmE), / hɑːt / (BrE)
A playing card belonging to the suit marked with red heart-shaped symbols; one of the four suits in a standard deck of cards.

Examples

A heart card
He collected all the hearts in the game.
She played the queen of hearts to win the trick.
The next card turned up was a heart, making hearts the trump suit.
