affreightment

noun
shipping contract
Pronunciation: / əˈfreɪtmənt / (AmE), / əˈfreɪtmənt / (BrE)
a contract or the act of hiring a ship or cargo space to transport goods by sea

Examples

An affreightment contract
The exporter signed an affreightment to ensure the goods would be shipped on time.
Under the affreightment, the shipping company was responsible for delivering the cargo safely.
Legal disputes sometimes arise over the terms of an affreightment agreement.
The company signed an affreightment agreement to ship its products overseas.
