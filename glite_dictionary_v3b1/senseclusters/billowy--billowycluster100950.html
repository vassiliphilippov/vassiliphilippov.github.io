billowy

adjective
rising and falling in waves or surges
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪloʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.əʊ.i / (BrE)
rising and falling in large waves or surges; resembling a billow or filled with billows

Examples

The billowy clouds
She admired the billowy clouds drifting lazily across the summer sky.
The model walked down the runway in a billowy silk gown that fluttered with each step.
White curtains took on a billowy appearance as the breeze swept through the open window.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - having a level and even surface; not rising or falling
  • synonym: wavy - having a form or edge that smoothly curves in and out; like waves
