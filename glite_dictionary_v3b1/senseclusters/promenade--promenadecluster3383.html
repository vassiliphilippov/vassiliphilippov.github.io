promenade

noun
square dance figure; couples march in a circle
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːməˈneɪd, ˈprɑːməˌneɪd /; / ˌprɑː.məˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌprɒməˈnɑːd, ˈprɒməˌnɑːd /; / ˌprɒm.əˈnɑːd / (BrE)
a formal march or dance figure in which people move in a circle or procession, especially at a dance or ball

Examples

The promenade signaled the official commencement of the senior prom.
