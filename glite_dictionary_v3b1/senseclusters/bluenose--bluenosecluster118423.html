bluenose

noun
strict moralist
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌnəʊz / (BrE)
a person who is very strict or conservative about morals or proper behavior, often disapproving of others

Examples

A strict bluenose
The new principal was considered a bluenose by students because of her strict rules.
Some bluenoses in the community protested the art exhibit for being too provocative.
He was called a bluenose for criticizing the comedian's jokes as inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: libertine - a person who behaves without moral principles or responsibility
  • synonym: prude - a person who is easily shocked by matters relating to sex or nudity
  • synonym: puritan - a person with strict moral beliefs, especially about pleasure
