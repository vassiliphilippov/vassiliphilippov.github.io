hadj

noun
Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca
4.5 7.0
Pronunciation: /hædʒ/ /; / /hɑːdʒ/ (AmE), /hædʒ/ /; / /hɑːdʒ/ (BrE)
the Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca that every Muslim is expected to undertake at least once in their lifetime if able; one of the Five Pillars of Islam
