mushy peas

noun
soft-boiled peas dish (UK)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃi piːz / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃi piːz / (BrE)
A dish of marrowfat peas that have been soaked overnight, boiled until soft, and often served with fish and chips.

Examples

A plate of mushy peas
