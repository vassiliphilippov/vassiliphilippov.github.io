cape

noun
narrow land projecting into water
9.9 2.7
Pronunciation: /keɪp/ (AmE), /keɪp/ (BrE)
a narrow piece of land that extends into a sea, ocean, or other large body of water

Examples

The cape juts out
Cape Cod is a famous cape on the east coast of the United States.
Explorers often named new capes they discovered during their voyages.
The ship sailed around the cape before heading north.

Related Terms
