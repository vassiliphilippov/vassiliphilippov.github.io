inkslinger

noun
printing press operator
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkˌslɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌslɪŋə / (BrE)
a person who operates a printing press or works in commercial printing, applying ink to produce printed material

Examples

Printing inkslinger
The old inkslinger took pride in every page he printed for the local newspaper.
As a skilled inkslinger, she could fix any problem with the press in minutes.
They relied on the expertise of their trusted inkslinger to produce high-quality posters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: printer - a person whose job is to operate a printing press
  • synonym: typesetter - a person who arranges type for printing
