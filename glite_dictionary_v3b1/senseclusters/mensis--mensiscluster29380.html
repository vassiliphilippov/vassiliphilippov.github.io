mensis

noun
month; menstrual cycle
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a month, especially used in medical or scientific contexts to refer to a menstrual cycle

Examples

The mensis cycle
The doctor noted that the patient's symptoms appeared at the start of each mensis.
In some medical texts, a menstrual cycle is referred to as a mensis.
The duration of one mensis can vary from person to person.
