at least

adverb
minimum or consolation phrase
Pronunciation: / ət ˈlist / (AmE), / ət ˈliːst / (BrE)
used to introduce a consolation, clarification, or positive aspect when the situation is not ideal

Examples

At least it worked
The concert was disappointing, but at least the tickets were cheap.
We need at least two more volunteers to start the project.
You should apologize, or at least explain what happened.
He forgot my birthday, but at least he called to apologize later.
It may not be much, but at least it’s a start.
The weather was terrible, but at least we arrived safely.
