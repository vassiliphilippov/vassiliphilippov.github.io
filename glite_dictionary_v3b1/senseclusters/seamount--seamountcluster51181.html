seamount

noun
underwater mountain in the ocean
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌmaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌmaʊnt / (BrE)
An underwater mountain that rises from the ocean floor but does not reach the water's surface.

Examples

A seamount underwater
A seamount can create diverse habitats that support a variety of ocean species.
Scientists discovered a new seamount teeming with unique marine life.
The ship's sonar detected a large seamount just west of the island.

  • synonym: submarine volcano - a volcano beneath the ocean surface
