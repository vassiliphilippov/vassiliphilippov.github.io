box kite

noun
kite shaped like an open-ended box
3.2
Pronunciation: / bɑks kaɪt / (AmE), / bɒks kaɪt / (BrE)
a kite with a box-like structure, open at both ends, usually consisting of two or more connected rectangular cells

Examples

A box kite flying
Children raced to see whose box kite could soar the highest.
He built a box kite to fly on the windy beach.
The museum had an old box kite on display from the early 1900s.
