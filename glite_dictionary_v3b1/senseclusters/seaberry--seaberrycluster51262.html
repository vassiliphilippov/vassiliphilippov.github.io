seaberry

noun
thorny berry shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a thorny shrub, also known as sea buckthorn, that produces orange berries used for food and hedges

Examples

Seaberry shrub
The seaberry is valued for its nutritious fruit and ability to grow in poor soils.
Birds often feed on the bright orange berries of the seaberry during winter.
They made a tart juice from freshly picked seaberry fruit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea buckthorn - a shrub with orange berries, also called seaberry
