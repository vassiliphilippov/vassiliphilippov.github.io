Ceratotherium simum

noun
white rhinoceros species
Pronunciation: / sɛrətoʊˈθɪəriəm ˈsaɪməm / (AmE), / sɛrətəˈθɪəriəm ˈsɪməm / (BrE)
a large light-grey African rhinoceros species with two horns, commonly known as the white rhinoceros; endangered in the wild

Examples

The Ceratotherium simum rhinoceros
Due to poaching, the population of Ceratotherium simum has dramatically declined in recent decades.
The Ceratotherium simum is the largest species of rhinoceros in the world.
Wildlife reserves in Africa are working to protect the remaining Ceratotherium simum from extinction.
