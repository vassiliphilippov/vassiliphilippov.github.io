Preclassic

noun
era before classical
9.2
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈklæs.ɪk/ /; / /prɪ.ˈklæs.ɪk/ (AmE), /priː.ˈklæs.ɪk/ /; / /prɪ.ˈklæs.ɪk/ (BrE)
the historical period immediately before the classical era in a particular culture, especially in Mesoamerican archaeology

Examples

The Preclassic period
The Olmec civilization flourished during the Preclassic in Mesoamerica.
Many ancient cities were first established in the Preclassic period.
Archaeologists uncovered artifacts from the Preclassic at the excavation site.
