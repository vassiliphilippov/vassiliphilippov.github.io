reidentify

verb
to identify again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to determine the identity of a person or entity from data that was previously anonymized or de-identified

Examples

To reidentify anonymized data
It is possible to reidentify individuals in a dataset if enough external information is available.
The company took extra steps to ensure that no one could reidentify users from the anonymized records.
After new techniques emerged, researchers managed to reidentify patients from old medical data.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anonymize - to remove identifying information from data
  • synonym: identify - to recognize or establish the identity of someone or something
