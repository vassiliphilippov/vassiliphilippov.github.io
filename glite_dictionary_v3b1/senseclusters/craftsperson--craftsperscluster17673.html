craftsperson

noun
a skilled and expert worker in a craft
3.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkræft.spɜːr.sən/ (AmE), /ˈkrɑːft.spɜː.sən/ (BrE)
A person who is highly skilled in a particular craft and consistently produces work of high quality.

Examples

A skilled craftsperson
Every craftsperson in the guild takes pride in their work.
She has become a respected craftsperson in the world of jewelry design.
The exhibition features the work of a renowned craftsperson known for exquisite detail.

  • synonym: artisan - a worker skilled in a trade, especially one that involves making things by hand
  • synonym: craftsman - a man who is skilled in a particular craft
