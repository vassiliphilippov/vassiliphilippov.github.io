extraneity

noun
state of being nonessential or irrelevant

Pronunciation: /ɪk.strə.ˈniː.ə.ti/ /; / /ɪk.ˈstreɪ.ni.ə.ti/ (AmE), /ɪk.strə.ˈniː.ə.ti/ /; / /ɪk.ˈstreɪ.ni.ə.ti/ (BrE)
