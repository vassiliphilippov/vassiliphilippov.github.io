crash

noun
loud, resonant, repeating noise
1.0
Pronunciation: / kræʃ / (AmE), / kræʃ / (BrE)
a loud, resonant, and often repeating noise, such as of metal being struck or objects colliding

Examples

A loud crash
A sudden crash from the kitchen startled everyone in the house.
He jumped at the constant crash of the waves against the rocky shore.
The crash of cymbals echoed through the concert hall after the song ended.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
