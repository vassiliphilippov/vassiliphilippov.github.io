lighthouse keeper

noun
person in charge of a lighthouse
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪthaʊs ˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪthaʊs ˌkiːpə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to maintain and operate a lighthouse, ensuring its light functions as a navigational aid for ships.

Examples

lighthouse keeper on duty
Generations of his family had served as lighthouse keepers along the rocky coast.
She read a memoir by a former lighthouse keeper about the solitude and challenges of the job.
The lighthouse keeper climbed the tower every evening to light the lamp.
