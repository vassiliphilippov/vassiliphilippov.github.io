Niger

proper noun
major river in West Africa

Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈniːdʒə / (BrE)
A major river in West Africa that flows through several countries and empties into the Gulf of Guinea in the South Atlantic Ocean.
