townie

noun
a person from the same town; local resident
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊni / (AmE), / ˈtaʊni / (BrE)
a person who lives in a town, especially as distinct from students or outsiders

Examples

A townie resident
I grew up here, so I'm a townie through and through.
She made friends with a townie who showed her all the local spots.
The university students often keep to themselves, separate from the townies.
During the college festival, tensions sometimes arise between townies and students.
Many townies have lived in the area for generations, well before the university was founded.
The local diner is a popular spot for both townies and college students to grab a late-night meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: local - a person from a particular area, especially one living there as opposed to visitors
  • antonym: student - person formally enrolled in a college or university
