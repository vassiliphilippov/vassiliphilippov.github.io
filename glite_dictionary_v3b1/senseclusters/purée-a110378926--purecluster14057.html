purée

noun
smooth food paste
Pronunciation: / pjʊˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəreɪ / (BrE)
a smooth, thick paste made by crushing or blending cooked or raw food, often used as a sauce or ingredient

Examples

A carrot purée
The chef prepared a carrot purée to serve with the roasted chicken.
For the baby, she made a fresh apple purée every morning.
Add a spoonful of tomato purée to the soup for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chunk - a thick, solid piece of something
  • synonym: mash - food that has been crushed into a soft mass
  • synonym: paste - a thick, soft, moist substance made by crushing or mixing
