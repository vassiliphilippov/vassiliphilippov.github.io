barbeque

noun
meat cooked with spiced sauce on a grill
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː / (BrE)
Meat that has been cooked slowly on a grill or over an open fire, typically seasoned with a highly spiced sauce.

Examples

A plate of barbeque
He ordered a plate of barbeque with a side of coleslaw and beans.
Nothing beats homemade barbeque at a summer picnic.
The barbeque was tender, smoky, and full of flavor.
