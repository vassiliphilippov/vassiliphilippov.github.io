barbeque

noun
meat cooked with spiced sauce on a grill
3.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː/ /; / /ˈbɑːr.bə.kjuː/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː/ /; / /ˈbɑː.bə.kjuː/ (BrE)
