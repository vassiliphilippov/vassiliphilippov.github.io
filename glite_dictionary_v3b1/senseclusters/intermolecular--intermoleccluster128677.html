intermolecular

adjective
existing or occurring between molecules
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərməˈlɛkjələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntəməˈlɛkjʊlə / (BrE)
existing or occurring between molecules, especially with reference to forces or interactions

Examples

Intermolecular forces
Hydrogen bonds are a type of intermolecular interaction important in water.
The scientist studied the intermolecular attractions responsible for crystal formation.
The strength of intermolecular forces affects the boiling point of a substance.
