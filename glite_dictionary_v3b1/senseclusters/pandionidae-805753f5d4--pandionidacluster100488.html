Pandionidae

noun
osprey bird family (raptors)
9.9
Pronunciation: /pæn.dɪ.ə.ˈnaɪ.diː/ /; / /pæn.dɪ.oʊ.ˈnaɪ.diː/ (AmE), /pæn.dɪ.ə.ˈnaɪ.diː/ (BrE)
A family of birds of prey consisting solely of the ospreys, large raptors found worldwide near water.

Examples

The Pandionidae family
Much research on Pandionidae has focused on breeding habits and migration patterns.
The Pandionidae are distinguished from other raptors by their reversible outer toes.
Unlike most bird families, Pandionidae contains only one living species, the osprey.
