pardonably

adverb
in an excusable or forgivable manner
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrdənəbli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdənəbli / (BrE)
In a way that can be forgiven or excused; to a degree that does not deserve blame.

Examples

To pardonably arrive late
He arrived pardonably late due to the heavy traffic.
It is pardonably optimistic to hope the repairs will finish ahead of schedule.
She was pardonably irritated after waiting for hours in the cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excusable - capable of being excused or forgiven
  • antonym: unpardonably - in an unforgivable manner
