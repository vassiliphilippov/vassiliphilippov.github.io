sloping trough

noun
an inclined channel for descending objects
9.8 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsloʊ.pɪŋ trɔːf/ /; / /ˈsloʊ.pɪŋ trɑːf/ (AmE), /ˈsləʊ.pɪŋ trɒf/ /; / /ˈsləʊ.pɪŋ trɔːf/ (BrE)
