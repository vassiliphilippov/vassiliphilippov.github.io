carpenter's square

noun
L-shaped carpentry measuring tool
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrpəntərz skwɛr / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpəntəz skweə / (BrE)
a large, flat, L-shaped steel tool used by carpenters for measuring and marking right angles

Examples

A carpenter's square tool
A carpenter's square is essential for marking out accurate corners in woodworking projects.
He used a carpenter's square to ensure the table legs were perfectly perpendicular.
The blueprint specified measurements that she checked with her carpenter's square.
