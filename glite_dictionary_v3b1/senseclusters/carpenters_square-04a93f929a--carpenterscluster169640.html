carpenter's square

noun
L-shaped carpentry measuring tool
4.4 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêrp…ônt…ôrz skw…õr / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêp…ônt…ôz skwe…ô / (BrE)

Examples
