Toltec

noun
member of ancient Mexican people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈtɒl.tɛk / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous Nahuatl-speaking people who inhabited central and southern Mexico before the rise of the Aztec empire

Examples

Ancient Toltec people
According to legend, the Toltec were skilled artists and artisans.
Archaeologists have uncovered many impressive structures built by the Toltec civilization in central Mexico.
Some scholars believe the Toltec influenced later cultures such as the Aztecs.
