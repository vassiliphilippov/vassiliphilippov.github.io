Bissau-Guinean franc

noun
former currency of Guinea-Bissau
9.8
Pronunciation: / bɪˌsaʊ ɡɪˈniːən ˈfræŋk / (AmE), / bɪˌsaʊ ɡɪˈniːən ˈfræŋk / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Guinea-Bissau before it was replaced by the West African CFA franc

Examples

The Bissau-Guinean franc currency
Historical prices are often given in Bissau-Guinean francs in documents from the 1970s.
The Bissau-Guinean franc was replaced by the West African CFA franc in 1997.
Travelers had to exchange their Bissau-Guinean francs after the currency change.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: West African CFA franc - currency currently used in Guinea-Bissau and other West African countries
