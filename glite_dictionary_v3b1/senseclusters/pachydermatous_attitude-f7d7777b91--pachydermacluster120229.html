pachydermatous attitude

noun
emotionally insensitive manner
9.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpækɪˈdɝːmətəs ˈæt̬ɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˌpækɪˈdɜːmətəs ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
An insensitive or emotionally thick-skinned manner or way of behaving, showing little empathy for others' feelings.

Examples

Pachydermatous attitude to feelings
Despite criticism from her peers, her pachydermatous attitude kept her unfazed and focused on her work.
His pachydermatous attitude was evident when he dismissed his friend's concerns without a second thought.
Many admired her resilience, but others found her pachydermatous attitude rather cold.

  • synonym: thick-skinned - not easily affected by criticism or hurt
