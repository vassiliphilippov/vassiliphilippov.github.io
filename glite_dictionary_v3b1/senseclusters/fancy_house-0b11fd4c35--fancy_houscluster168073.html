fancy house

noun
elaborate or luxurious home
Pronunciation: / ˈfænsi haʊs / (AmE), / ˈfænsi haʊs / (BrE)
an elaborate or luxurious home, often featuring expensive or ornate details

Examples

A fancy house
She decorated her apartment as if it were a fancy house from a magazine.
The television show featured a different fancy house in each episode.
They spent years saving up to buy their dream fancy house by the lake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cottage - a small, modest house, often in the countryside
  • synonym: mansion - a large, impressive house
