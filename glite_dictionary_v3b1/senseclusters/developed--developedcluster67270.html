developed

adjective
having advanced economic and industrial development
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈvel.əpt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈvel.əpt/ (BrE)
(of a country, society, etc.) having a high level of industrialization, economic growth, and standard of living.

Examples

Developed country
