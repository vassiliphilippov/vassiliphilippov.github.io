developed

adjective
having advanced economic and industrial development
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpt / (BrE)
(of a country, society, etc.) having a high level of industrialization, economic growth, and standard of living.

Examples

Developed country
Global trade agreements often differentiate between developed and developing nations.
Japan is considered a developed country with a high standard of living.
Many international organizations provide aid to less developed regions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: developing - a country or region with a lower level of industrialization and standard of living
