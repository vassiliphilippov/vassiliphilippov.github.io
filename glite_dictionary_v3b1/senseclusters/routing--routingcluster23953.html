routing

noun
path selection process
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈruːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of determining or directing the path that something takes, especially in technology, transportation, or communication

Examples

Network routing
The routing of internet traffic is managed by specialized network devices.
Efficient routing is essential for quick delivery of packages in logistics companies.
Modern email systems use automatic routing to direct messages to the correct inbox.
The new software improved the routing of packages across the country.
Efficient routing is essential for reducing delivery times in logistics companies.
Network engineers discussed the routing of data through different servers.

  • synonym: navigation - the process of planning and following a route
