seminomadic

adjective
partly nomadic
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛminoʊˈmædɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɛminəʊˈmædɪk / (BrE)
partly moving from place to place and partly living in one location

Examples

A seminomadic community
The community is seminomadic, spending part of the year in fixed villages and the rest moving with their herds.
Many seminomadic groups return to the same region each season after traveling.
Their seminomadic lifestyle allows them to adapt to changing weather and food availability.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: nomadic - moving from place to place without a permanent home
  • antonym: sedentary - living in one place and not moving around
