pina cloth

noun
fine fabric made from pineapple fibers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnə klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈpiːnə klɒθ / (BrE)
a fine, lightweight fabric made from the fibers of pineapple leaves, traditionally handwoven in the Philippines

Examples

Fine pina cloth
Artisans weave pina cloth using fibers extracted from pineapple leaves.
She admired the translucent texture of the pina cloth scarf in the boutique.
The traditional Filipino dress was made of delicate pina cloth embroidered by hand.
