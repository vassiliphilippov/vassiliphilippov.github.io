lightly-armed

adjective
equipped with light weapons
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtli ɑrmd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtli ɑːmd / (BrE)
Equipped with only light weapons or lacking heavy arms.

Examples

A lightly-armed unit
As a lightly-armed unit, they relied on speed and stealth rather than firepower.
Due to budget constraints, many soldiers were sent to the area lightly-armed.
The patrol was lightly-armed and avoided direct confrontation with enemy forces.

  • antonym: heavily-armed - equipped with heavy weapons
