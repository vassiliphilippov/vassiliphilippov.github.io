libertinage

noun
lack of restraint
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪbərtɪˈnɑːʒ / (AmE), / ˌlɪbətɪˈnɑːʒ / (BrE)
a lifestyle or behavior marked by a lack of moral or sexual restraint

Examples

Life of libertinage
The novel explores the consequences of libertinage in eighteenth-century society.
His reputation for libertinage made him a controversial figure among his peers.
Critics accused the artist of promoting libertinage through his provocative works.
