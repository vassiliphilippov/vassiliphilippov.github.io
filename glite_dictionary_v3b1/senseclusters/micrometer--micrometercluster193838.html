micrometer

noun
one millionth of a meter
1.0 4.8
Pronunciation: / maɪˈkrɑːmɪtər /; / ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtər / (AmE), / maɪˈkrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter (0.000001 m), commonly used in science and engineering

Examples

A micrometer in size
A bacterium typically measures about one micrometer in length.
Fibers with diameters less than one micrometer are used in specialized filters.
The tool can detect particles as small as a few micrometers.
Bacteria are often just a few micrometres in size.
Scientists measured the particle diameter to within one micrometre.
The width of a human hair is typically about seventy micrometres.

Related Terms

  • synonym: micron - another name for a micrometer as a unit of length
  • degree higher: millimeter - a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
