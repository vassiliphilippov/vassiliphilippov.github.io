uncoachable

adjective
not open to coaching
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkoʊtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkəʊtʃəbəl / (BrE)
unable or unwilling to accept coaching, guidance, or instruction

Examples

An uncoachable player
Despite his talent, the athlete was labeled uncoachable by every team he joined.
Her stubborn attitude made her seem uncoachable to the new manager.
Some students are not uncoachable; they just need a different teaching style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: receptive - willing to consider or accept new suggestions and ideas
  • antonym: trainable - able to be taught or trained
