insensibility

noun
lack of emotion or compassion
1.0 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.sen.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.sen.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪn.sen.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.sen.sɪ.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking emotion, compassion, or sensitivity; hardness of heart.
