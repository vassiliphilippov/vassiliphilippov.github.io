in hand

adverb
under control or managed
Pronunciation: / ɪn hænd / (AmE), / ɪn hand / (BrE)
under control; being dealt with or managed successfully

Examples

Repairs in hand
Once the repairs are in hand, we'll reopen the building.
The teacher assured us that the class was in hand despite the commotion.
With the new security measures, the situation is finally in hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of control - no longer under control; chaotic or unmanageable
