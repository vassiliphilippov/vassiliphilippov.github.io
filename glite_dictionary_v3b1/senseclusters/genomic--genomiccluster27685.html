genomic

adjective
relating to genomes
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnoʊmɪk / (AmE), / dʒɪˈnəʊmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the complete set of genes or genetic material present in an organism

Examples

Genomic analysis
The team conducted a genomic analysis to identify mutations linked to the disease.
Advances in genomic research have improved our understanding of inherited disorders.
Doctors use genomic information to personalize treatment plans for patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetic - relating to genes or heredity
