photoperiod

noun
daily light duration
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊˌpɪriəd / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌpɪəriəd / (BrE)
the length of time each day that an organism is exposed to light, especially as it affects growth or behavior
