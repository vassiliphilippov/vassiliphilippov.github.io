get a tuit

verb
(humorous) delay doing something; play on 'get to it'
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ə tuɪt / (AmE), / ɡɛt ə tjuːɪt / (BrE)
(humorous) Used to delay doing something, as a play on words for 'get to it'; refers to postponing an action until 'eventually' or 'someday'.

Examples
