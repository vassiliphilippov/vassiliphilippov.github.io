Sambre

proper noun
river in France and Belgium
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsæm.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈsæm.brə / (BrE)
a river in western Europe that rises in northern France and flows generally east into Belgium, where it joins the Meuse at Namur

Examples
