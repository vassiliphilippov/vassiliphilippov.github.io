sluice down

verb
flow or pour forcefully like from a sluice
9.4
Pronunciation: / sluːs daʊn / (AmE), / sluːs daʊn / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to pour or flow forcefully, as if from a sluice.

Examples

To sluice down water
A surge of rainwater sluiced down the city streets after the storm.
Sweat sluiced down his face as he finished the marathon.
The water sluiced down the hillside, carrying mud and debris into the valley.
