Damaliscus

noun
genus of African antelopes (sassabies)
Pronunciation: / dəˈmælɪskəs /; / ˌdæməˈlɪskəs / (AmE), / dəˈmalɪskəs /; / ˌdaməˈlɪskəs / (BrE)
a genus of African antelopes in the family Bovidae, including sassabies and topis, known for their long legs and distinctive horns
