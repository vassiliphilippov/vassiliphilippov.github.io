WHOIS

noun
domain lookup service
Pronunciation: / ˈhuː ɪz / (AmE), / ˈhuː ɪz / (BrE)
an internet service or protocol used to look up information about domain names or IP addresses

Examples

WHOIS lookup tool
You can use WHOIS to find out who owns a particular website.
The WHOIS record showed the registration details for the domain.
Many cybersecurity experts rely on WHOIS information to investigate suspicious sites.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DNS - the system that translates domain names into IP addresses
  • synonym: lookup - the act of searching for information in a database
