strip lighting

noun
lighting with long tubes instead of bulbs
4.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /strɪp ˈlaɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /strɪp ˈlaɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
Lighting that consists of long, narrow tubes, typically fluorescent or LED, used instead of bulbs to provide illumination.

Examples

Strip lighting tubes
Museums often use strip lighting for even illumination along display cases.
The kitchen was fitted with modern strip lighting to create a bright workspace.
They replaced the old bulbs with energy-efficient strip lighting in the office.
