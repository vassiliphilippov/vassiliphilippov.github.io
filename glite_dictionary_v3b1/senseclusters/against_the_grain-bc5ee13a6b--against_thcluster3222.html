against the grain

adverb
contrary to what is usual or expected
7.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈgenst ðə greɪn/ /; / /ə.ˈgeɪnst ðə greɪn/ (AmE), /ə.ˈgeɪnst ðə greɪn/ /; / /ə.ˈgenst ðə greɪn/ (BrE)
