steer

noun
advice or suggestion about an opportunity
Pronunciation: / stɪr / (AmE), / stɪə / (BrE)
an indication or piece of advice that suggests a possible opportunity or course of action

Examples

A helpful steer
If you want a good restaurant, I can give you a steer.
She gave me a steer about which companies were hiring this month.
Thanks for the steer on where to find affordable apartments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lead - information that may help discover an opportunity or find out something valuable
  • synonym: tip - a helpful piece of information or advice, especially about a job or opportunity
