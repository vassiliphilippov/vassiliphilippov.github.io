turnback

noun
folded-back part
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnˌbæk / (BrE)
a part of something, especially clothing or material, that is folded or turned back on itself

Examples

Turnback on a sleeve
The tailor adjusted the turnback on the jacket's cuffs for a better fit.
She admired the decorative turnback on the collar of her new coat.
The curtain's turnback added a stylish touch to the living room window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuff - the end part of a sleeve, often turned back
  • synonym: hem - the edge of a piece of cloth that is folded and sewn down
