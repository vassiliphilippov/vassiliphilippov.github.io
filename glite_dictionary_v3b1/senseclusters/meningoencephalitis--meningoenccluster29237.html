meningoencephalitis

noun
inflammation of brain, spinal cord, and meninges
Pronunciation: / məˌnɪŋɡoʊɛnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / mɪˌnɪŋɡəʊɛnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition in which both the brain and spinal cord, along with their protective membranes (meninges), become inflamed.

Examples

Meningoencephalitis infection
Early diagnosis of meningoencephalitis is crucial for effective treatment and patient recovery.
Symptoms of meningoencephalitis may include severe headache, fever, and neurological deficits.
The hospital reported several new cases of viral meningoencephalitis after the outbreak.
