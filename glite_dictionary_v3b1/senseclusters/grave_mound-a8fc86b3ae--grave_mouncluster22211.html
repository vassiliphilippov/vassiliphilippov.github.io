Synonyms

  • burial mound
    7.1 0.21/m
    a burial mound is the common name for an earth heap over graves, while grave mound is a less common variant used for prehistoric tombs.
  • barrow
    9.5 0.10/m
    a barrow is a large ancient earth or stone mound over a tomb, while grave mound is any artificial heap over a grave, often prehistoric.
  • tumulus
    9.9 0.05/m
    a tumulus is the archaeological term for a stone and earth burial mound, while a grave mound is a general, less technical name.
