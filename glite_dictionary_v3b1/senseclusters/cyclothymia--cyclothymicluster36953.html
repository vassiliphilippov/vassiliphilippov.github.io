A mild form of bipolar disorder characterized by chronic fluctuating moods involving periods of mild depression and hypomania.

Examples

Mild cyclothymia disorder
She was diagnosed with cyclothymia after experiencing mood swings over several years.
Treatment for cyclothymia often involves therapy and sometimes medication to manage the symptoms.
Unlike severe bipolar disorder, cyclothymia tends to cause less extreme mood changes.
