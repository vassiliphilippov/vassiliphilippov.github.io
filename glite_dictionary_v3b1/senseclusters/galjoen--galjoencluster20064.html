galjoen

noun
South African fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / xɑlˈjuːn / (AmE), / xælˈjuːn / (BrE)
a species of edible fish native to the coastal waters of southern Africa, especially South Africa

Examples

The galjoen fish
The galjoen is considered the national fish of South Africa.
Fishermen often catch galjoen near the rocky shores along the southern African coast.
Grilled galjoen is a popular dish in many seaside restaurants in South Africa.
