churchly

adjective
resembling or appropriate to a church
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɜrtʃli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃli / (BrE)
resembling, suggestive of, or appropriate to a church, especially in appearance, atmosphere, or character

Examples

The old cathedral had a churchly atmosphere that inspired awe.
