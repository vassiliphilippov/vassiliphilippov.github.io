Examples

A baseboard on wall

Synonyms

  • skirting board
    4.8 0.01/m
    skirting board is a narrow board fixed to the bottom of an interior wall to hide the joint, while baseboard names that same board.
  • mopboard
    8.7 0.01/m
    mopboard is a narrow molding that covers the wall-floor joint and protects the wall from knocks and dirt, while baseboard is the general board.
