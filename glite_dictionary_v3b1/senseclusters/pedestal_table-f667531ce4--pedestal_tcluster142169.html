pedestal table

noun
table with a single central support
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdəˌstɛl ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɛdɪstl ˈteɪbl / (BrE)
A table supported by a single central column instead of four legs.

Examples

A pedestal table
A pedestal table allows for more legroom than a traditional four-legged design.
She placed the vase in the center of the round pedestal table.
The antique pedestal table in the dining room is made of mahogany.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dining table - a table at which meals are eaten
