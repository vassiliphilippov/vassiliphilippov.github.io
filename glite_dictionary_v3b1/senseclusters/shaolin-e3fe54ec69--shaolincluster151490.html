Shaolin

noun
Chinese martial arts school
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʃaʊˈlɪn / (AmE), / ʃaʊˈlɪn / (BrE)
a Chinese style or school of martial arts originating from the Shaolin temple, known for striking techniques, acrobatics, and disciplined training

Examples

Study Shaolin style
He traveled to China to study Shaolin and learn its ancient martial arts techniques.
The movie features a hero who uses Shaolin skills to defend his village.
Many people admire the discipline and philosophy behind Shaolin training.

Related Terms

  • antonym: karate - a Japanese martial art focusing on striking techniques
  • synonym: kung fu - Chinese martial arts emphasizing skill and discipline
