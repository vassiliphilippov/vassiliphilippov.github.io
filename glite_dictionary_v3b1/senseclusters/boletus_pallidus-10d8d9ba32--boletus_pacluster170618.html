Boletus pallidus

noun

A species of bolete mushroom with an off-white to dingy brown cap, a similarly colored stalk, and pale greenish yellow tubes under the cap.

Examples

Mushroom foragers often seek the Boletus pallidus in deciduous woodlands during summer.
