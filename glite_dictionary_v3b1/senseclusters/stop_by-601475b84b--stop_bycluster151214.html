stop by

verb
make a brief visit
2.5 13/m
Pronunciation: / stÉ‘p baÉª / (AmE), / stÉ’p baÉª / (BrE)
to make a short visit to a place or person, usually briefly or casually

Examples

To stop by a store
If you have time, please stop by the store and pick up some bread.
She decided to stop by her friend's house on the way home from work.
Would you like to stop by my office after lunch for a quick chat?
