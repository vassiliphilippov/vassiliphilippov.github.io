unwrinkle

verb
to remove wrinkles
3.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪŋkəl / (BrE)
to make something smooth by removing wrinkles or creases, especially from fabric or material

Examples

She used a hot iron to unwrinkle her dress before the party.
It can be difficult to unwrinkle clothes that have been left in the dryer too long.
He tried to unwrinkle the paper by pressing it flat on the table.
