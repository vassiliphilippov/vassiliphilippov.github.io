foggy

adjective
indistinct or hazy in outline
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑɡi / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡi / (BrE)
Not clear or distinct; appearing hazy or blurred as if covered by fog.

Examples

Foggy image
A foggy outline of a mountain appeared on the distant horizon.
His recollection of the event was rather foggy after all these years.
The photograph was so foggy that I could hardly make out any faces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blurry - not clear or sharply defined; indistinct
  • antonym: clear - easy to perceive, see, or understand
