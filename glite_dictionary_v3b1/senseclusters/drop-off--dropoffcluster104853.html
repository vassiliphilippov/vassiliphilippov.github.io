drop-off

noun
steep vertical face of rock or land
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpˌɒf / (BrE)
a very steep or vertical face of rock or land, especially one forming an abrupt descent

Examples

A steep drop-off
Be careful near the drop-off; the ground falls away sharply just past the trees.
Divers love to explore the coral reef's drop-off, where marine life is abundant.
The hiking trail follows the ridge, skirting a dramatic drop-off with stunning views below.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cliff - a steep, high rock face, especially at the edge of the sea
  • synonym: precipice - a very steep or overhanging place
