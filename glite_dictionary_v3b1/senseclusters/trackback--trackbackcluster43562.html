trackback

noun
blog link notification
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈtrækˌbæk / (BrE)
a notification or system used by blogs to inform a website that another site has linked to its content

Examples

Blog trackback notification
After publishing her blog post, she received several trackbacks from other writers discussing the same topic.
The website displayed a list of trackbacks to show which blogs had linked to its articles.
He checked the trackbacks to see who was referencing his latest review online.
The blog received a trackback from another site that discussed the same topic.
You can see all the trackbacks listed below the article for further reading.
After publishing her review, she noticed a trackback from the author's official website.
