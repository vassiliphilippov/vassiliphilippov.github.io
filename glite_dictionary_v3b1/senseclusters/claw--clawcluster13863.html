claw

noun
mechanical gripping or pulling device
2.1
Pronunciation: / klɔ / (AmE), / klɔː / (BrE)
a mechanical device with a curved or hooked part, used to grip, hold, or pull something

Examples

Hammer claw
He used the hammer's claw to pull out the bent nail.
The crane's claw lifted the debris from the construction site.
The vending machine's claw failed to grab the toy.
