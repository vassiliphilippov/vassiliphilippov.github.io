raver

noun
someone who speaks loudly or violently
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪvər / (AmE), / ˈreɪvə / (BrE)
a person who speaks loudly or violently, often in an uncontrolled or angry manner

Examples

An angry raver
Despite his reputation as a raver, he was calm and thoughtful in private.
The neighbors complained that the raver next door kept shouting all night.
When he lost his temper, he turned into a real raver, impossible to reason with.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ranter - a person who speaks or complains loudly and angrily
