lozenge

noun
medicated tablet for mouth or throat
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːzɪndʒ /; / ˈlɑzəndʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɒzɪndʒ / (BrE)
a small, sweet or medicated tablet that dissolves slowly in the mouth to soothe the throat or mouth
