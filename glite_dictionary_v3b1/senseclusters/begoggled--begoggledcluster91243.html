begoggled

adjective
wide-eyed with surprise
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡɑɡəld / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɒɡəld / (BrE)
having wide, protruding, or staring eyes, often due to surprise or amazement

Examples

Begoggled with surprise
The children looked begoggled as the fireworks exploded in the night sky.
He stood begoggled at the unexpected results of the experiment.
Her begoggled expression made everyone laugh during the surprise party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goggle-eyed - having eyes that stick out or are opened wide in surprise
  • synonym: wide-eyed - having eyes opened wide, often from surprise or innocence
