multiplatform

adjective
usable on many systems
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈplætˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈplætfɔːm / (BrE)
able to work on or be used with more than one type of system, device, or platform

Examples

Multiplatform software
The new app is multiplatform, so you can use it on your phone, tablet, or computer.
Developers prefer multiplatform tools to reach a wider audience with less effort.
Our website is fully multiplatform and works smoothly on all major browsers.
The company launched a multiplatform app that works on both smartphones and desktop computers.
Their multiplatform marketing campaign included television ads, social media posts, and online videos.
A multiplatform approach helps reach a wider audience by using different types of media.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-platform - able to run on different operating systems or devices
  • antonym: single-platform - designed to work on only one type of system or device
