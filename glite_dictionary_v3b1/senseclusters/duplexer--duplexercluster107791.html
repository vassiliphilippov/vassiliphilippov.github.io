duplexer

noun
signal separation device
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈduːplɛksər / (AmE), / ˈdjuːplɛksə / (BrE)
a device that enables simultaneous transmission and reception of signals on a single communication channel

Examples

Radio duplexer
The technician installed a duplexer to allow the radio to send and receive signals at the same time.
A duplexer is essential in systems where one antenna is used for both transmitting and receiving.
Without a duplexer, the equipment would need separate channels for each direction of communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repeater - a device that receives and retransmits signals to extend range
  • synonym: transceiver - a device that can both transmit and receive communications
