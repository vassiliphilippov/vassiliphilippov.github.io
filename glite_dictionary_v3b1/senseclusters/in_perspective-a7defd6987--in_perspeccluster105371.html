in perspective

adverb
considered in relation to other things
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɪn pərˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / ɪn pəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
considered in relation to other things, so as to see the true importance or proportion of something

Examples

Look in perspective
It's important to look at the statistics in perspective before drawing conclusions.
The coach reminded the team to keep the loss in perspective and focus on future games.
When you put your problems in perspective, they may not seem as overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of perspective - not properly considered in relation to other things
