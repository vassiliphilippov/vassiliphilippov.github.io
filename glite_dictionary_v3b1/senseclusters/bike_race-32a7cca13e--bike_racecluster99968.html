bike race

noun
competition involving bicycle riding
Pronunciation: / baɪk reɪs / (AmE), / baɪk reɪs / (BrE)
a competition in which riders race against each other on bicycles

Examples

A bike race event
He trained for months to win the annual bike race through the city.
Hundreds of cyclists participated in the challenging mountain bike race last weekend.
The children organized a spontaneous bike race in the neighborhood park.
