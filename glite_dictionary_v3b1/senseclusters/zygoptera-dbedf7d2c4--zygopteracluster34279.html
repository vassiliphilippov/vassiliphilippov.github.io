Zygoptera

noun
suborder of insects: damselflies
9.9
Pronunciation: / zaɪˈɡɑptərə / (AmE), / zaɪˈɡɒptərə / (BrE)
the suborder of insects comprising the damselflies, characterized by slender bodies and wings of equal size and shape
