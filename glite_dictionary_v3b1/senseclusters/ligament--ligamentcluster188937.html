ligament

noun
unifying bond or connection
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪɡəmənt / (AmE), / ˈlɪɡəmənt / (BrE)
a thing that unites people or things; a bond or connection that brings them together

Examples

A ligament of friendship
Shared values are the ligament binding members of the organization to its mission.
The treaty provided a ligament between the two formerly opposing countries.
Trust can act as the ligament that holds a community together.
