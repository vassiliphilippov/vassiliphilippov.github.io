matured

adjective
fully developed; at the height of bloom
4.8
Pronunciation: / məˈtʃʊrd, məˈtʃɜrd / (AmE), / məˈtjʊəd, məˈtjʊrd / (BrE)
Having reached full natural growth or development; fully ripe or at the height of bloom.

Examples

A matured fruit
Each matured rose in the garden was at its most fragrant.
Only matured cheese develops a deep, complex flavor.
The matured apples fell from the tree when the wind blew.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immature - not fully developed or ripe
  • synonym: ripe - fully developed or matured, suitable for harvesting or eating
