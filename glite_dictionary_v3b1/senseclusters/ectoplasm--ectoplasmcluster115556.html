ectoplasm

noun
spiritualist substance from medium's body
6.5 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈek.tə.plæ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈek.tə.plæ.zəm/ (BrE)
(In spiritualism) a substance believed to emanate from the body of a medium during a spiritualist trance, said to form materializations of spirits.

Examples

Ghostly ectoplasm
During the séance, the medium claimed that ectoplasm was flowing from her hands.
Many skeptics regard ectoplasm as a trick rather than genuine paranormal activity.
The appearance of ectoplasm was once considered proof of spirit communication.
