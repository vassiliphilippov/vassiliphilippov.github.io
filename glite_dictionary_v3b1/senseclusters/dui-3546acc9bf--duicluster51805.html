DUI

noun
drunk driving offense
Pronunciation: / diː juː aɪ / (AmE), / diː juː aɪ / (BrE)
the crime of driving a vehicle while under the influence of alcohol or drugs

Examples

DUI arrest
He was arrested for DUI after being stopped at a police checkpoint.
A conviction for DUI can result in heavy fines and loss of your license.
The lawyer specializes in defending clients charged with DUI offenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DWI - driving while intoxicated
  • antonym: sobriety - the state of not being affected by alcohol or drugs
