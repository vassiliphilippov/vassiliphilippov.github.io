pedi

noun
informal for pedicure
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdi / (AmE), / ˈpɛdi / (BrE)
an informal term for a cosmetic treatment of the feet and toenails, short for pedicure

Examples

A spa pedi
She treated herself to a relaxing pedi at the spa after a long week.
Before the wedding, all the bridesmaids went out for a pedi together.
He surprised his partner with a gift card for a pedi at their favorite salon.
