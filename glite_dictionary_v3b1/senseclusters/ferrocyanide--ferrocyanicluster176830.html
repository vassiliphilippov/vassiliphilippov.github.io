ferrocyanide

noun
a salt of ferrocyanic acid
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛroʊˈsaɪəˌnaɪd / (AmE), / ˌfɛərəʊˈsaɪənaɪd / (BrE)
a salt formed by the combination of ferrocyanic acid and a base, usually prepared by reacting a cyanide with iron(II) sulfate
