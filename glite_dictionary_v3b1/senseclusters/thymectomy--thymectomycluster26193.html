thymectomy

noun
removal of thymus
8.6
Pronunciation: / θaɪˈmɛktəmi / (AmE), / θaɪˈmɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove the thymus gland

Examples

A thymectomy operation
The surgeon performed a thymectomy to help treat the patient's autoimmune disorder.
After her thymectomy, she needed to stay in the hospital for several days.
A thymectomy is sometimes recommended for patients with myasthenia gravis.

Related Terms
