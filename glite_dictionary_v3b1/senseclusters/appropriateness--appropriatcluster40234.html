appropriateness

noun
quality of being suitable or proper
Pronunciation: / əˌproʊpriˈeɪtnəs / (AmE), / əˌprəʊpriˈeɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable or proper for a particular situation or purpose

Examples

The appropriateness of comments
He questioned the appropriateness of her comments during the meeting.
Teachers often discuss the appropriateness of various topics for their students.
The appropriateness of the outfit depends on the event you are attending.
She questioned the appropriateness of using humor in such a serious situation.
The appropriateness of his comments was appreciated by all who attended the meeting.
There was some debate about the appropriateness of wearing casual clothes to the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inappropriateness - the quality of not being suitable or proper
  • synonym: suitability - the quality of being right or appropriate for a particular purpose or occasion
