point

verb
indicate, be a sign of
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt / (AmE), / pɔɪnt / (BrE)
to indicate, show, or direct attention to something (with a finger, object, or metaphorically)

Examples

To point at the board
All signs point to a possible change in management.
His symptoms point to an underlying health issue.
Rising prices point to increased demand in the market.
He points out historical landmarks during the tour.
She pointed to the sign at the end of the street.
The teacher pointed in the direction of the library.

  • synonym: signal - to convey a warning or information
