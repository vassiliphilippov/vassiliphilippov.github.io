Felis catus

noun
scientific name for domestic cat
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlɪs ˈkeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪs ˈkatəs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the domestic cat, a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal kept as a pet or for catching rodents.

Examples

The Felis catus species
Researchers study Felis catus to understand the domestication of animals.
The scientific classification for the household pet is Felis catus.
Veterinarians often use the name Felis catus when discussing feline health.
