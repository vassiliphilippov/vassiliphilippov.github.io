landlocked

adjective
entirely surrounded by land
6.1 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.lɑːkt/ /; / /ˈlænd.lɔːkt/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.lɒkt/ (BrE)
(of a country or region) entirely or almost entirely surrounded by land and having no direct access to the sea.

Examples

A landlocked country
A landlocked lake cannot be reached by boats from the open sea.
Switzerland is a landlocked country in central Europe.
The region remained landlocked until the construction of the new canal.
