flextime

adjective
flexible work hours
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛkstaɪm / (AmE), / ˈflɛkstaɪm / (BrE)
relating to a work schedule that allows employees to choose their start and end times within set limits

Examples

Many workers appreciate the flextime arrangement because it reduces stress from commuting.
Our department's flextime schedule allows staff to start work between 7 and 10 a.m.
