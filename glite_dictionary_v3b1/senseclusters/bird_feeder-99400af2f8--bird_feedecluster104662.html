bird feeder

noun
outdoor device that supplies food for birds
Pronunciation: / bɝd ˈfidɚ / (AmE), / bɜːd ˈfiːdə / (BrE)
A container or structure placed outdoors to provide food for wild birds.

Examples

A bird feeder station
A squirrel managed to steal seeds from the bird feeder again yesterday.
The bird feeder needed to be refilled every morning during the winter.
We hung a new bird feeder in the backyard to attract sparrows and finches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bird table - a platform for feeding wild birds outdoors
