blame it on

verb
attribute responsibility for a fault or problem
Pronunciation: / bleɪm ɪt ɑn / (AmE), / bleɪm ɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to say or think that someone or something is responsible for a mistake or problem

Examples

To blame it on someone
Don't blame it on me if you forgot your homework again.
She blamed it on the weather when she was late.
Whenever something goes wrong, he tries to blame it on someone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blame - to hold someone responsible for something bad
