amigo

noun
friend; companion (informal)
Pronunciation: / əˈmiːɡoʊ / (AmE), / əˈmiːɡəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a friend or companion, especially one with whom one shares a close relationship

Examples

Old amigo
Carlos greeted his old amigo with a big hug.
Thanks for helping me move, amigo!
We met a group of amigos at the party who showed us around.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed or hostile
  • synonym: friend - a person with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
