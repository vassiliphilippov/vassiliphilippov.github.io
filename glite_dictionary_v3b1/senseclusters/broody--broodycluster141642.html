broody

adjective
deeply thoughtful; melancholic
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdi / (AmE), / ˈbruːdi / (BrE)
deeply or seriously thoughtful, often showing signs of melancholy or introspection

Examples

Broody teenager

His broody silence at the dinner table worried his friends.
She stared out the window with a broody expression, lost in her own thoughts.
