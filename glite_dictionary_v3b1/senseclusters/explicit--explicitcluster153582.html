explicit

noun
graphic content
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsplɪsɪt / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɪsɪt / (BrE)
material, statements, or content that is clearly and openly expressed, often referring to sexual or graphic subject matter

Examples

Explicit content warning
The teacher warned that the book contains several explicits not suitable for younger readers.
Some streaming services restrict access to movies with explicits to protect children.
Parents should be aware of any explicits in the music their kids listen to.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graphic - describing content with clear and sometimes shocking detail
  • antonym: implicit - not directly stated or expressed
