spike

verb
produce a flower spike
4.8
Pronunciation: / spaɪk / (AmE), / spaɪk / (BrE)
(of a plant) to produce or develop a spike or spikes, especially referring to the flowering stalk.

Examples

Plants spike in spring
If you provide the right care, your hyacinth will spike beautifully each season.
Many orchids only spike once a year, displaying tall stalks of flowers.
The lilies in the garden are beginning to spike this spring.
