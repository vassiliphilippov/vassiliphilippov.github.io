tamale

noun
Mexican dish of cornmeal dough and filling
6.7 4.6
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈmɑː.leɪ/ /; / /tə.ˈmɑː.liː/ (AmE), /tə.ˈmɑː.leɪ/ /; / /tə.ˈmɑː.liː/ (BrE)
A traditional Latin American (especially Mexican) dish made of cornmeal dough stuffed with meat or other fillings, wrapped in corn husks, and steamed or boiled.

Examples

A tamale dish
His grandmother taught him how to make a traditional pork tamale during the holidays.
The plate came with rice, beans, and a delicious cheese tamale wrapped in a corn husk.
We ordered a chicken tamale with spicy salsa from the food truck downtown.
He ordered a spicy tamal filled with chicken for lunch.
The street vendor sells delicious homemade tamales every morning.
Wrapping each tamal in a corn husk is a family tradition during the holidays.
