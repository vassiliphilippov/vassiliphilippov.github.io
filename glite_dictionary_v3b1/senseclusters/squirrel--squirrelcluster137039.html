squirrel

noun
small tree-dwelling rodent
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːrəl / (AmE), / ˈskwɪr.əl / (BrE)
a small rodent with a long bushy tail, typically living in trees and feeding on nuts and seeds

Examples

A squirrel climbs trees
A squirrel darted across the lawn and disappeared up an oak tree.
Red squirrels are less common than their gray counterparts in some areas.
We left some nuts outside, and a curious squirrel came to investigate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chipmunk - a small striped rodent related to the squirrel
