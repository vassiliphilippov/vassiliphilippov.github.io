Kirgizia

proper noun
former name of Kyrgyzstan
9.6
Pronunciation: / kɪrˈɡɪziə / (AmE), / kɪəˈɡɪziə / (BrE)
A former name for Kyrgyzstan, a landlocked country in central Asia which became independent from the Soviet Union in 1991.

Examples

Kirgizia independence
After 1991, Kirgizia declared its independence and adopted the name Kyrgyzstan.
Many Soviet-era maps refer to Kyrgyzstan as Kirgizia.
The region now known as Kyrgyzstan was once officially called Kirgizia during the Soviet period.
