watch

noun
small portable timepiece
1.0 27/m
Pronunciation: / wÉ‘tÊƒ / (AmE), / wÉ’tÊƒ / (BrE)
A small, portable device for keeping and telling the time, worn typically on the wrist or carried in a pocket.

Examples

A wrist watch
He glanced at his watch to check the time before the meeting started.
I forgot to wear my watch today and kept asking others for the time.
She received a gold watch as a retirement gift from her colleagues.

Synonyms

  • wristwatch
    1.0 1.48/m
    a wristwatch is worn on the wrist for everyday use, while a watch can also mean a pocket or general small timepiece.
  • ticker
    6.8 0.05/m
    a ticker is an informal, old-fashioned name for a small portable watch people carry or wear, while a watch is more neutral.
  • timepiece
    4.5 1.21/m
    a timepiece is a formal, broader term for any clock or watch used to mark time, while a watch is usually a small personal device.

Related Terms
