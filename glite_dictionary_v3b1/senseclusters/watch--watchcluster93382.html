watch

noun
small portable timepiece
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ / (AmE), / wɒtʃ / (BrE)
A small, portable device for keeping and telling the time, worn typically on the wrist or carried in a pocket.

Examples

A wrist watch
He glanced at his watch to check the time before the meeting started.
I forgot to wear my watch today and kept asking others for the time.
She received a gold watch as a retirement gift from her colleagues.
