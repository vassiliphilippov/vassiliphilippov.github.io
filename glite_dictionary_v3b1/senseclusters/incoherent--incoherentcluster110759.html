incoherent

adjective
lacking clarity or logical connection
3.7 5.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.koʊ.ˈhɪr.ənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.kəʊ.ˈhɪə.rənt/ (BrE)
Not expressed in a clear, logical, or understandable way; lacking connection or consistency in thought or structure.

Examples

Incoherent speech
Her speech became increasingly incoherent as she grew more nervous.
His explanation was incoherent, leaving everyone confused about what actually happened.
The essay was so incoherent that the main argument was difficult to follow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coherent - logical and consistent, clear and understandable
  • synonym: illogical - lacking sense or clear, sound reasoning
