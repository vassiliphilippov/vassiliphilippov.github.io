Habu

proper noun
SR-71 nickname

Pronunciation: /ˈhɑː.buː/ /; / /ˈhæ.buː/ (AmE), /ˈhɑː.buː/ (BrE)
an informal nickname used by aircrew for the Lockheed SR-71 Blackbird reconnaissance aircraft, especially among units based in Okinawa

Examples

The Habu jet
Many pilots at Kadena Air Base affectionately called the SR-71 the Habu during their missions.
The nickname Habu became popular among aircrew stationed in Okinawa in the 1970s.
Collectors often seek memorabilia featuring the Habu emblem from the reconnaissance squadrons.
