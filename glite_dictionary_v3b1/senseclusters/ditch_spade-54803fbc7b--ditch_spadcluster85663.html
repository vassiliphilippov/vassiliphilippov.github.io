ditch spade

noun
long-handled spade for digging ditches
Pronunciation: / dɪtʃ speɪd / (AmE), / dɪtʃ speɪd / (BrE)
A spade with a long handle used for digging narrow ditches.

Examples

A ditch spade tool
A ditch spade is ideal when you need to create a deep, narrow hole in the ground.
He loaned his ditch spade to the neighbor, who was working on a drainage channel.
The gardener grabbed a ditch spade to start digging the narrow trench for the irrigation system.
