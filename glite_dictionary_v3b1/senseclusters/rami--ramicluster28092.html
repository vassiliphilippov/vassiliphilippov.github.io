rami

noun
fiber-producing plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈrɑːmi / (BrE)
a tropical plant whose fibers or wood are used for making furniture, crafts, and textiles

Examples

Rami fiber cloth
The durable fibers of rami are often used to make high-quality textiles.
Local artisans weave baskets from the stems of rami plants.
Furniture made from rami is both lightweight and strong.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemp - a plant used for its strong fibers in textiles and rope
  • synonym: jute - a plant whose fibers are used to make rope and cloth
