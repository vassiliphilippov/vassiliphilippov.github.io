Dialeurodes citri

noun
a whitefly species affecting citrus
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪ.əˌlʊr.oʊˈdiːz ˈsɪːtraɪ / (AmE), / daɪ.əˌljʊə.rəʊˈdiːz ˈsɪtrɪ / (BrE)
A species of whitefly that infests and damages citrus trees.

Examples

Dialeurodes citri whitefly pest
Dialeurodes citri can cause significant damage to orange and lemon crops if not controlled.
Symptoms of infestation by Dialeurodes citri include a sticky residue and leaf yellowing.
The spread of Dialeurodes citri has made citrus farming more challenging in affected regions.
