isopteran

adjective
relating to termites (order Isoptera)
8.0
Pronunciation: / aɪˈsɑptərən / (AmE), / aɪˈsɒptərən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of insects of the order Isoptera (now typically called termites).

Examples

Isopteran insects
Researchers studied the complex social behaviors present in isopteran species.
The isopteran colonies play a vital role in decomposing plant material in tropical forests.
Unlike ants, isopteran insects have soft bodies and straight antennae.

Related Terms

  • synonym: termite - a social insect of the order Isoptera
