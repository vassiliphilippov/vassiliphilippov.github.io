nonassertiveness

noun
lack of confidence
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əˈsɝː.tɪv.nəs /; / ˌnɑnəˈsɜrtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈsɜː.tɪv.nəs /; / ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of not expressing your opinions, needs, or rights confidently or forcefully

Examples

His nonassertiveness problem

The lawyer warned that nonassertiveness could weaken their position in negotiations.
In some cultures, nonassertiveness is seen as a sign of respect rather than weakness.
