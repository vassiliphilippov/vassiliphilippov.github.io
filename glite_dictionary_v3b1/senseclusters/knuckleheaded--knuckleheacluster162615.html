knuckleheaded

adjective
silly or foolish
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəlˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
behaving in a silly, foolish, or thoughtless way

Examples

A knuckleheaded mistake
It was a knuckleheaded mistake to leave the keys in the car overnight.
His knuckleheaded comments made everyone in the meeting roll their eyes.
She realized her knuckleheaded decision had caused more problems than it solved.
