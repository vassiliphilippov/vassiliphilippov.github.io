polytheist

noun
person believing in multiple gods
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑː.li.θi.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɒl.ɪ.θi.ɪst / (BrE)
A person who believes in or worships more than one god.

Examples

A polytheist believer
As a committed polytheist, she honored several deities in her daily prayers.
In ancient Rome, most citizens were polytheists who participated in elaborate religious festivals.
The novel explores the experiences of a polytheist living in a predominantly monotheistic society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monotheist - person who believes in only one god
