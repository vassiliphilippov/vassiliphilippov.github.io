interorganizational

adjective
between organizations
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between two or more organizations

Examples

An interorganizational agreement

They developed an interorganizational strategy to address industry-wide challenges.
