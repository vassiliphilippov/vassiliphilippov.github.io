Synonyms

  • incapacitating
    7.0 0.06/m
    incapacitating is a formal clinical word for making someone unable to function, while crippling feels stronger and more general.
  • disabling
    5.6 0.01/m
    disabling makes a person unable to do certain tasks or activities, while crippling implies extreme or lasting loss of function.
  • paralyzing
    5.1 0.00/m
    paralyzing stops movement or action completely in a moment, while crippling means causing severe damage over time.
  • debilitating
    7.5 2.63/m
    debilitating makes someone weak and low on energy so they struggle with daily tasks, while crippling causes major incapacity.
