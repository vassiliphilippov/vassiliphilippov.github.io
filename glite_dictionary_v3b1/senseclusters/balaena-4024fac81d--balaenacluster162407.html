Balaena

noun
genus of large baleen whales
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈliːnə / (AmE), / bəˈliːnə / (BrE)
a genus of large baleen whales in the family Balaenidae, including species such as the bowhead whale and Greenland right whale
