navigate life

verb
deal with life's challenges
Pronunciation: / ˈnævəˌɡeɪt laɪf / (AmE), / ˈnævɪɡeɪt lʌɪf / (BrE)
to manage or deal with the difficulties and challenges encountered in day-to-day living

Examples

To navigate life challenges
She has learned to navigate life independently after moving to a new country.
The story follows a young man trying to navigate life with a chronic illness.
Young adults often face challenges as they navigate life after graduation.
