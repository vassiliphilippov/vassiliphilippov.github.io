nonpsychiatric

adjective
not psychiatric
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌsaɪkiˈætrɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌsaɪkiˈætrɪk / (BrE)
not related to psychiatry or the treatment of mental health conditions

Examples

Nonpsychiatric hospital ward
The hospital has both psychiatric and nonpsychiatric wards for different types of patients.
She was referred to a nonpsychiatric specialist for her physical symptoms.
Most of his experience is in treating nonpsychiatric conditions such as diabetes and heart disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medical - relating to the science or practice of medicine
  • antonym: psychiatric - relating to psychiatry or mental health treatment
