snowshoe

verb
travel on snowshoes
6.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsnoʊ.ʃuː/ (AmE), /ˈsnəʊ.ʃuː/ (BrE)
to travel or walk over snow using snowshoes strapped to your feet

Examples
