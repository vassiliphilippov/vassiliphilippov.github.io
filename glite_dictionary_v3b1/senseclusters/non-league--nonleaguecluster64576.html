non-league

noun
team outside main league
4.9
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈliːɡ / (AmE), / nɒn ˈliːɡ / (BrE)
a sports team or club that does not belong to the main professional league, especially in football (soccer)

Examples

A non-league club
The small non-league club surprised everyone by reaching the third round of the tournament.
He started his career playing for a non-league team before moving to the professional division.
Many non-league sides dream of facing top clubs in the national cup competition.
