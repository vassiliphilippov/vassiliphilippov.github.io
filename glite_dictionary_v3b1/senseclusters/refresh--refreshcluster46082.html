refresh

verb
remind; renew memory of something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɛʃ / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃ / (BrE)
to help someone remember something they may have forgotten; to remind oneself of information

Examples

To refresh your memory
Before the exam, she quickly refreshed her knowledge of the main concepts.
Could you refresh my memory about how that system works?
He needed to refresh his memory before the meeting to recall all the details.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember
  • synonym: remind - to cause someone to remember something
