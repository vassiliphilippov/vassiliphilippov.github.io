recreate

verb
form anew in the imagination
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.kriˈeɪt / (AmE), / ˌriː.krɪˈeɪt / (BrE)
to form again in the imagination; to recollect and construct anew in the mind

Examples

To recreate in memory
After reading the description, I could recreate the landscape in my mind as if I had seen it myself.
The artist tried to recreate the bustling street scene from memory in his new painting.
Through meditation, she can recreate the memories of her childhood home in vivid detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagine - to form a mental image or concept
  • synonym: remember - to recall something to mind
