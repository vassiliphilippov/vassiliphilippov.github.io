intraorbital

adjective
within eye socket
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈɔrbɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈɔːbɪtəl / (BrE)
located or occurring within the eye socket

Examples

Intraorbital tumor
The surgeon carefully removed the intraorbital tumor without damaging the surrounding tissue.
Swelling in the intraorbital area can affect a person's vision.
An intraorbital infection may require immediate medical attention to prevent complications.
