Synonyms

    7.5 0.3/m
    tarry is an older, more formal word for delaying leaving on purpose, while linger is a common, neutral way to stay longer.
  • hover
    3.4 0.5/m
    hover means staying close and uncertain, often moving slightly, while linger means staying longer usually because you do not want to leave.
  • remain
    1.0 190/m
    remain means to continue in the same place, state, or condition, while linger means staying longer than expected for emotional or slow reasons.
  • stay put
    2.0 6/m
