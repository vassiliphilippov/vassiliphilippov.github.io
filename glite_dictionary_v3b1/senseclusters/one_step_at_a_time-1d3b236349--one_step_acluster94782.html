one step at a time

adverb
gradually; step by step
2.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /wʌn step ə taɪm/ /; / /wən step ə taɪm/ (AmE), /wʌn step ə taɪm/ /; / /wɒn step ə taɪm/ (BrE)
