cablegram

noun
telegram sent via submarine cable
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪbəɫˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈkeɪbəlˌɡræm / (BrE)
a message sent by telegraph over a submarine cable, especially to another country

Examples

A cablegram message
He received a cablegram informing him of the successful arrival of the shipment.
In 1910, sending a cablegram was the fastest way to communicate overseas.
The news of her promotion came by cablegram from London.
