guignol

noun
foolish person
Pronunciation: / giːnˈjɔl / (AmE), / giːnˈjɒl / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a silly or clownish way; a buffoon or fool

Examples

He is a guignol
The new intern acted like a guignol during the meeting, making everyone laugh at his jokes.
She was embarrassed when her brother played the guignol at her birthday party.
Instead of taking the ceremony seriously, he behaved as a complete guignol in front of the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffoon - a person who acts foolishly to make people laugh
  • synonym: clown - a person who amuses others by silly behavior
  • antonym: serious person - someone who behaves in a thoughtful or solemn way
