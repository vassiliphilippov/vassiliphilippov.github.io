plainclothes

noun
ordinary clothing
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
ordinary clothing worn instead of a uniform, especially by police officers to avoid being recognized as such

Examples

Worked in plainclothes
The detective worked in plainclothes to avoid drawing attention during the investigation.
Several plainclothes officers were present at the crowded event for security.
He changed into plainclothes before meeting the informant at the café.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civilian clothes - clothing not associated with a uniform or official role
  • antonym: uniform - official clothing worn by members of an organization
