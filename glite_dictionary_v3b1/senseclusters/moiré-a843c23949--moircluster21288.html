moiré

noun
wavy-patterned fabric
9.6
Pronunciation: /mwɑː.ˈreɪ/ /; / /mɔː.ˈreɪ/ (AmE), /mwɑː.ˈreɪ/ /; / /mɔː.ˈreɪ/ (BrE)
a fabric, especially silk, with a glossy, wavy, or 'watered' surface pattern

Examples

Designers often choose moiré for its unique, wavy appearance in luxury garments.
