neocolonialist

noun
supporter of neocolonialism
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåniÀêo äk…ôÀàlo äni…ôl…™st / (AmE), / ÀåniÀê…ô äk…ôÀàl…ô äni…ôl…™st / (BrE)
a person, government, or organization that supports or practices neocolonialism by indirectly controlling or exploiting other countries, especially for economic benefit

Examples

A neocolonialist company
The multinational corporation was criticized as a neocolonialist for its influence over the country's resources.
Some historians argue that modern trade agreements can empower neocolonialist interests in developing nations.
Activists protested against the neocolonialist policies that kept local industries dependent on foreign companies.

Synonyms

  • neocolonial
    9.9 0.01/m
    neocolonial describes a policy or power that uses indirect foreign control, while neocolonialist names a person or group that practices it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-imperialist - a person who opposes imperialism or colonial control
  • synonym: imperialist - a person or group supporting the extension of a country's power through colonization or force
