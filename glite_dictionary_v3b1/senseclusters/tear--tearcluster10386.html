tear

noun
a drop of salty liquid from the eye
Pronunciation: / tɪr / (AmE), / tɪə / (BrE)
A drop of salty liquid produced by the lacrimal glands, usually as a result of emotion or irritation of the eyes.

Examples

A single tear
A single tear rolled down her cheek as she said goodbye.
She wiped away a tear before anyone could see her crying.
The bright light made his eyes fill with tears.
