conspecific

noun
organism of the same species
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsəˈspɪsɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsɪˈspɪsɪk / (BrE)
an organism belonging to the same species as another organism

Examples

A conspecific individual
Aggressive interactions between conspecific individuals were recorded during the study.
Male birds often compete more fiercely with conspecifics than with members of other species.
The researchers observed grooming behavior among conspecifics in the primate group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heterospecific - an organism belonging to a different species
