inflorescence

noun
process of budding and blossoming flowers
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfloʊˈrɛsəns / (AmE), / ˌɪn.fləˈrɛs.əns / (BrE)
the period and process during which flowers bud and open on a plant

Examples

Inflorescence period
Gardeners observed the inflorescence period to record the flower development stages.
Prolonged rainfall can affect the inflorescence of certain crops, delaying their bloom.
The inflorescence of the magnolia tree is a spectacular sight each spring.
