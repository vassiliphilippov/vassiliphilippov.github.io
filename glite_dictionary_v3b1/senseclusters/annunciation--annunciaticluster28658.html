annunciation

noun
formal announcement
8.8
Pronunciation: /ə.nʌn.si.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ə.nʌn.si.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a formal announcement or the act of announcing something important, especially in a formal or religious context

Examples

The annunciation of news
The annunciation of the new policy was met with applause.
The mayor's annunciation of the results brought relief to the community.
A formal annunciation was made to inform the public of the upcoming changes.
The annunciation of the new policy was made at the annual meeting.
During the ceremony, the mayor's annunciation of the award winners was met with applause.
In many churches, the Annunciation is celebrated as a significant religious event.
