dispersible

adjective
able to be spread out
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɜrsəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈspɜːsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be distributed or spread out, especially when mixed with a liquid

Examples

Dispersible tablet
This medication comes in a dispersible tablet that dissolves quickly in water.
The paint contains dispersible pigments for easy mixing and smooth application.
Scientists developed a dispersible powder to improve the delivery of nutrients in drinks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insoluble - not able to be dissolved in a liquid
  • synonym: soluble - able to be dissolved in a liquid
