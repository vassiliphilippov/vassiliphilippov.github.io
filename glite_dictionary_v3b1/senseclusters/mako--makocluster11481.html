mako

noun
large fast mackerel shark
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkəʊ / (BrE)
A large, swift mackerel shark of the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, known for its speed and power.

Examples

A mako shark
Divers often spot a mako near the surface during summer months.
Researchers tracked a mako migrating across the Atlantic Ocean.
The speed of the mako shark makes it a formidable predator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shark - a large fish with a cartilaginous skeleton and sharp teeth
