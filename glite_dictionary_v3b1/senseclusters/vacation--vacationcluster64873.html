vacation

noun
time off for rest or pleasure
1.0
Pronunciation: / veɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A period of time spent away from work or study for rest, travel, or pleasure.

Examples

A summer vacation
Her family went on a skiing vacation in December.
I am planning to take a vacation to Greece this summer.
Many employees save their vacation days for the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiday - a period when work or school is suspended for rest or celebration
