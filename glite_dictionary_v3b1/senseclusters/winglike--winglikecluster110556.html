winglike

adjective
resembling a wing
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a wing in shape or position

Examples

Winglike shape

The artist created a sculpture with winglike projections extending from its sides.
The seed has a winglike extension that helps it float on the wind.
