intra-abdominal

adjective
within the abdomen
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrə əbˈdɑmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrə æbˈdɒmɪnəl / (BrE)

Examples

Intra-abdominal mass

Laparoscopic surgery is often used to treat intra-abdominal conditions.
