nightstand

noun
bedside table
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌstænd / (BrE)
a small table placed beside a bed for holding items such as a lamp, books, or a clock

Examples

A nightstand table
She placed her glasses on the nightstand before going to sleep.
A small lamp sat on his nightstand, lighting up the room softly.
He keeps a stack of books on his nightstand for late-night reading.
She keeps a glass of water on her nightstand in case she gets thirsty at night.
The alarm clock on his nightstand woke him up every morning at six.
He placed his phone and book on the nightstand before turning off the light.
