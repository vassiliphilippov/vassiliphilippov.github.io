streptococci

noun
type of chain-forming bacteria
7.1 6.8
Pronunciation: /strep.tə.ˈkɑːk.aɪ/ (AmE), /strep.tə.ˈkɒk.aɪ/ (BrE)
Spherical Gram-positive bacteria that occur in pairs or chains and can cause illnesses such as scarlet fever and tonsillitis.

Examples

Some species of streptococci can lead to serious complications if not treated promptly.
