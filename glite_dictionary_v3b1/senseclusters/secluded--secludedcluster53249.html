secluded

adjective
hidden from general view or use
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkludɪd /; / sɪˈklu.dɪd / (AmE), / sɪˈkluːdɪd /; / sɪˈkluː.dɪd / (BrE)
(of a place) hidden, private, or not seen or visited by many people

Examples

A secluded place
The resort offers secluded villas surrounded by lush gardens.
They enjoyed a picnic in a secluded corner of the park.
We found a secluded beach that was perfect for a quiet swim.
Her favorite reading spot is a secluded corner of the library.
The small cabin was located in a secluded forest far from the main road.
They enjoyed a picnic in a secluded garden, away from the noise of the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exposed - in a position where something is not sheltered or hidden
  • synonym: isolated - far away from other places or people
  • synonym: private - not accessible to the public; intended for or restricted to the use of a particular person, group, or class
  • antonym: public - open to or shared by all people
