bastille

noun
a harsh or tyrannical prison
Pronunciation: / bæˈstil / (AmE), / bæˈstiːl / (BrE)
a jail or prison, especially one operated in a harsh or tyrannical manner

Examples

The bastille prison
He described the run-down facility as a modern-day bastille ruled by fear.
Many stories evoke the image of a bastille to symbolize oppressive government power.
The political prisoners were locked away in the infamous bastille for years without trial.
