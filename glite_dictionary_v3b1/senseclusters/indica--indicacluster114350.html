indica

adjective
from India (scientific)
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪkə / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkə / (BrE)
used in scientific names to indicate that a species is from or associated with India

Examples

Indica species name
The plant species Oryza indica is widely cultivated in South Asia.
In scientific classification, indica often shows that the organism originates from India.
The butterfly Papilio indica was first described by a British naturalist in the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicus - Latin masculine form meaning 'of India', used in species names
