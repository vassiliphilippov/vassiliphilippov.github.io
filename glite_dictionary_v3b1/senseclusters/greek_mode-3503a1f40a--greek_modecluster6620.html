Greek mode

noun
musical scale from classical Greece
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɡrik moʊd / (AmE), / ɡriːk məʊd / (BrE)
any of the descending diatonic scales used in the music of classical Greece, each with a distinct sequence of intervals.
