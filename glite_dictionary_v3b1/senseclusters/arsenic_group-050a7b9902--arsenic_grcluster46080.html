arsenic group

noun
univalent chemical group from arsine
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.sə.nɪk gruːp/ /; / /ˈɑːr.se.nɪk gruːp/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.sə.nɪk gruːp/ /; / /ˈɑː.se.nɪk gruːp/ (BrE)
