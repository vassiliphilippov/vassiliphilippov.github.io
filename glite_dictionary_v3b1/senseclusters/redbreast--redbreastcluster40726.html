redbreast

noun
small songbird with a reddish breast
6.0 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈred.brest/ (AmE), /ˈred.brest/ (BrE)
A small Old World songbird with a reddish breast, especially the European robin.

Examples

The redbreast bird
A redbreast sang cheerfully in the frosty garden every morning.
In English folklore, the redbreast is often seen as a symbol of good luck.
The children watched a bright redbreast hop across the lawn in search of worms.
