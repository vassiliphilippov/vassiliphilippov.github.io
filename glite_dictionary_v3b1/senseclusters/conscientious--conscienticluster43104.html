conscientious

adjective
showing care and attention to detail
6.0 4.8
Pronunciation: /kɑːn.ʃiː.ˈen.ʃəs/ /; / /kən.ʃiː.ˈen.ʃəs/ (AmE), /kɒn.ʃɪ.ˈen.ʃəs/ /; / /kən.ʃɪ.ˈen.ʃəs/ (BrE)
wishing to do what is right and doing your work or duty carefully and thoroughly

Examples

Conscientious worker
She is a conscientious worker who always double-checks her tasks for errors.
The surgeon was praised for his conscientious attention to every detail during the operation.
We need someone who is conscientious and reliable for this challenging job.
He made a conscientious effort to recycle and reduce waste.
She is a conscientious student who always completes her assignments on time.
The company praised its employees for being conscientious about following safety procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: careful - giving attention to avoiding harm or errors
  • antonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
  • antonym: negligent - not taking proper care or showing sufficient attention
