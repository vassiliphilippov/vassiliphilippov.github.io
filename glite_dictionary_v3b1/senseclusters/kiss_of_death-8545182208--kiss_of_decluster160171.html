kiss of death

noun
something that leads to inevitable failure
Pronunciation: / kɪs əv dɛθ / (AmE), / kɪs ɒv dɛθ /; / kɪs əv dɛθ / (BrE)
an event, action, or circumstance that causes inevitable failure or ruin

Examples

The kiss of death
Failing to meet the deadline could be the kiss of death for the project.
Poor customer service has been the kiss of death for many businesses.
The scandal was the kiss of death for his celebrity career.
Delays in responding to customer complaints proved to be the kiss of death for the restaurant.
For many companies, ignoring new technology can be the kiss of death.
Her lack of preparation was the kiss of death in the final interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: death blow - a decisive, finishing, or destructive event
  • antonym: saving grace - a redeeming quality or factor that prevents failure
