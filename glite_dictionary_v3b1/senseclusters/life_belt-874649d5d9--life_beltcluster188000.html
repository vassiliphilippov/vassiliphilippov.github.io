life belt

noun

A circular device made of buoyant material, worn around the body to keep a person afloat in water; a life preserver in the form of a ring.

Examples

Swimming life belt
All passengers must wear a life belt during the boat drill.
He grabbed a life belt and threw it to the struggling swimmer.
The safety instructions included how to properly secure a life belt.

  • lifesaver
