unacademic

adjective
not scholarly
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌækəˈdɛmɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌakəˈdɛmɪk / (BrE)
not interested in, suited to, or effective at academic study or scholarly activities

Examples

Unacademic student

Her interests were mostly unacademic, focusing on art and hands-on projects instead of textbooks.
The club welcomes both academic and unacademic members who want to learn in different ways.
