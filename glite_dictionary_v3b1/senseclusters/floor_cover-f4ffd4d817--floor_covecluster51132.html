floor cover

noun
material covering a floor
Pronunciation: / flɔr ˈkʌvər / (AmE), / flɔː ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
a material or product used to cover the surface of a floor for protection or decoration

Examples

A thick floor cover
A thick floor cover can help insulate a cold, tiled basement.
The old floor cover was replaced with modern laminate flooring.
We chose a light-colored floor cover to brighten up the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpet - a thick, woven floor covering
  • synonym: linoleum - a type of floor covering made from linseed oil and other materials
