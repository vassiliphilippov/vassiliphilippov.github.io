multipoint

noun
multi-connection device
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltipoɪnt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltipoɪnt / (BrE)
a connector or device that allows several electrical or signal connections from a single port or location

Examples

Multipoint connector device
The technician attached the cable to the multipoint on the circuit board.
A multipoint makes it easier to connect several devices to a single port.
Modern conference systems often use a multipoint to link multiple microphones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hub - a central device connecting multiple devices in a network
  • antonym: single-point - a connector or device with only one connection
