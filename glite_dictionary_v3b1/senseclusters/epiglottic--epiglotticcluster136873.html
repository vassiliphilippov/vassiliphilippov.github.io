epiglottic

adjective
of the epiglottis
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡlɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈɡlɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the epiglottis, the flap of tissue in the throat that covers the windpipe during swallowing

Examples

The epiglottic flap
The surgeon carefully avoided the epiglottic region during the procedure.
Swelling in the epiglottic area can make breathing difficult.
The doctor noted an abnormality in the patient's epiglottic tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laryngeal - relating to the larynx (voice box)
