unbusinesslike

adjective
lacking professionalism
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈbɪznɪslaɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈbɪznɪslaɪk / (BrE)
not showing the efficiency, organization, or professionalism expected in business situations

Examples

An unbusinesslike attitude
Her unbusinesslike approach led to confusion during the project meeting.
The report was written in an unbusinesslike manner, lacking clear structure and detail.
Arriving late and unprepared was considered very unbusinesslike by his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: businesslike - showing efficiency, organization, and professionalism
  • synonym: unprofessional - not behaving in a professional or competent way
