ferry driver

noun
person who operates a ferry
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfɛri ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who operates or drives a ferry, transporting people or vehicles across water

Examples

A ferry driver operates
After years of working as a ferry driver, he knew every current and hazard on the river.
During the storm, the experienced ferry driver navigated safely to the opposite shore.
The ferry driver waved as the last passenger boarded before departure.
