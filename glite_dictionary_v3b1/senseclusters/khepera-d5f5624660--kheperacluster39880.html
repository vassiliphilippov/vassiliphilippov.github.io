Khepera

proper noun
Egyptian god of the morning sun
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈpɛrə / (AmE), / kɛˈpɛərə / (BrE)
In ancient Egyptian mythology, the god of the morning sun, often considered a creator deity.

Examples

Khepera god
Ancient Egyptians worshipped Khepera as the god who rolled the sun across the sky each day.
Legends say that Khepera created himself out of primeval chaos at the dawn of time.
The scarab beetle was a symbol of Khepera in Egyptian religion and art.
