agitation

noun
intense emotional disturbance
7.4 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a state of emotional disturbance, anxiety, or nervous excitement

Examples

Nervous agitation
His constant agitation made it impossible for him to concentrate on his work.
She paced the room in growing agitation as she waited for the phone call.
The news of the accident caused great agitation among the family members.
His voice trembled with agitation as he spoke to the crowd.
News of the delay caused agitation among the waiting passengers.
She paced the room in visible agitation before the interview began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of tranquility or absence of agitation
  • synonym: unrest - a state of disturbance or turmoil, especially emotional
