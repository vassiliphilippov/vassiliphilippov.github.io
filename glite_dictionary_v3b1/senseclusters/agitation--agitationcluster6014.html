Examples

Nervous agitation

Synonyms

  • anxiety
    3.5 39/m
    anxiety is a strong, often long-lasting worry that focuses the mind, while agitation is a restless, excited emotional state.
  • disturbance
    4.1 2.30/m
    disturbance is a broad state of mental trouble or disorder, while agitation is a more active, nervous excitement.
  • unease
    4.4 2.07/m
    unease is a quiet feeling of discomfort and worry, while agitation is a louder, restless form of emotional trouble.
  • edginess
    6.0 0.07/m
    edginess is a tense, easily irritated mood often shown in small actions, while agitation is a more shaken, restless state.
  • fretfulness
    7.7 0.04/m
    fretfulness is a touch of irritability and worry, often short and petty, while agitation is stronger and more unsettled.
