family Varanidae

noun
the monitor lizard family
Pronunciation: / ˈværəˌnaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈværənɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, carnivorous lizards known as monitor lizards, found in Africa, Asia, and Australia.

Examples

Family Varanidae lizards
Members of the family Varanidae are known for their intelligence and predatory skills.
Researchers discovered a new species within the family Varanidae in Southeast Asia.
The family Varanidae includes the Komodo dragon, one of the largest living lizards.
