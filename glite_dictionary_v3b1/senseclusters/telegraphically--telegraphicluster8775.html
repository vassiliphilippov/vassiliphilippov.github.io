telegraphically

adverb
in a brief, concise manner
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
In a concise and brief manner, similar to the style of a telegraph message.

Examples

To speak telegraphically
He conveyed his disappointment telegraphically, with just a few sharp words.
The report was written telegraphically, omitting all unnecessary details for clarity.
The witness answered the lawyer's questions telegraphically, offering only the essential facts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: succinctly - in a clear and concise manner
  • synonym: tersely - in a brief and direct manner
