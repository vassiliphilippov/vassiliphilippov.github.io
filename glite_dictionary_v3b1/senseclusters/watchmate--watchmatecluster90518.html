watchmate

noun
person sharing a watch
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who shares a duty period or watch with another, especially on a ship

Examples

Ship watchmate
During the long night shift, he relied on his watchmate for company and support.
Each watchmate took turns checking the equipment to ensure everything was running smoothly.
She became close friends with her watchmate after months of working together at sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crewmate - a fellow member of a ship's crew
  • synonym: shipmate - a person who serves on the same ship as another
