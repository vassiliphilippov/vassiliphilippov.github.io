to the point

adjective
directly relevant; concise
Pronunciation: / tə ðə pɔɪnt / (AmE), / tə ðə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
relevant and expressed without unnecessary detail; directly addressing the subject.

Examples

To the point comments
Her comments were always to the point and never wasted anyone's time.
His response was clear, concise, and to the point.
Try to keep your explanations to the point during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off topic - not directly related to the main subject
  • synonym: relevant - having a direct bearing on the matter at hand
