regionalization

noun

Synonyms

  • districting
    6.2 0.16/m
    districting divides an area into electoral or administrative districts for voting or local government, while regionalization organizes areas into larger functional regions.
  • territorialization
    9.9 0.01/m
    territorialization marks or claims land as a territory for control or identity, while regionalization arranges areas into administrative or functional regions.
  • sectionalization
    8.8 0.00/m
