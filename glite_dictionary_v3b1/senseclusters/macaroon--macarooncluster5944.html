macaroon

noun
small chewy almond or coconut biscuit
Pronunciation: / ˌmækəˈruːn / (AmE), / ˌmækəˈruːn / (BrE)
A small, chewy biscuit made with ground almonds or coconut, sugar, and egg white.

Examples

A coconut macaroon
I love the chewy texture of a fresh macaroon with my coffee.
She baked a batch of macaroons for the holiday party.
The bakery offers both almond and coconut macaroons every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: macaron - a delicate French meringue-based confection, often confused with 'macaroon'
