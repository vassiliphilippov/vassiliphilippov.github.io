iris

noun
a plant with showy, colorful flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪs / (BrE)
a plant with long, narrow leaves and tall stems bearing brightly colored flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals

Examples

A blooming iris
She planted a row of irises along the walkway to add color in spring.
The delicate petals of the iris glistened with morning dew.
The garden was full of blooming iris in shades of purple, yellow, and white.
