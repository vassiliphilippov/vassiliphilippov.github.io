schisandra

noun
medicinal climbing plant
Pronunciation: / skɪˈsændrə / (AmE), / skɪˈsændrə / (BrE)
a climbing plant of the genus Schisandra, whose berries are used in traditional medicine, especially in East Asia

Examples

Researchers are studying the effects of schisandra on stress and fatigue.
