order Guttiferales

noun
former plant order in botany
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ˌɡʌtəfəˈreɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌɡʌtɪfəˈrɑːliːz / (BrE)
a former order of flowering plants in some botanical classifications, coextensive with Parietales

Examples

Order Guttiferales classification
Some taxonomists no longer recognize order Guttiferales, incorporating its genera into Malpighiales.
The family Clusiaceae was traditionally classified under the order Guttiferales in older botanical systems.
Though not widely used today, order Guttiferales appears in some historical botanical literature.
