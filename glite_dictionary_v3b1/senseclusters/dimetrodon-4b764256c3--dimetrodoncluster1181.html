Dimetrodon

noun
extinct reptile genus with a sail-like fin
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˈmɛtrədɒn / (AmE), / daɪˈmiːtrədɒn / (BrE)
A genus of extinct carnivorous synapsid reptiles from the Permian period, characterized by a large sail-like fin on its back.

Examples

A Dimetrodon model
A model of Dimetrodon stands in the entrance hall of the natural history museum.
Paleontologists believe that the Dimetrodon used its sail for thermoregulation.
The fossil of a Dimetrodon reveals the impressive sail that ran along its spine.
