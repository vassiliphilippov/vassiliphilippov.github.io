LaserJet

noun
laser printer
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪzərˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈleɪzəˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
a laser printer, especially used generically to refer to any brand of laser printer

Examples

Office LaserJet printer
The office replaced its old machine with a new LaserJet for faster and clearer printing.
Many people use the term LaserJet to refer to any laser printer, regardless of the brand.
She printed her entire report in color using the LaserJet in the library.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inkjet - a type of printer that sprays ink onto paper
  • synonym: printer - a machine that produces printed copies of documents or images
