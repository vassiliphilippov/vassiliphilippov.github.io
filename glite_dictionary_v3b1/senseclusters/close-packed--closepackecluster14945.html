close-packed

adjective
packed tightly together
7.4
Pronunciation: / kloʊs ˈpækt / (AmE), / kləʊs ˈpakt / (BrE)
packed together very tightly; having little or no space between items

Examples

A close-packed group
A close-packed arrangement of books on the shelf left no room for new ones.
The audience sat in the close-packed seats of the theater, barely an inch apart.
The minerals form a close-packed structure at the atomic level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dense - closely compacted in substance
  • antonym: spacious - having ample space; not tightly packed
