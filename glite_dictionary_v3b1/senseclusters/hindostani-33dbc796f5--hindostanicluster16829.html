Hindostani

noun
form of Hindi spoken in northern India
4.5 9.1
Pronunciation: /hɪn.də.ˈstɑː.niː/ /; / /hɪn.dʊ.ˈstɑː.niː/ (AmE), /hɪn.də.ˈstɑː.niː/ /; / /hɪn.dɒ.ˈstɑː.niː/ (BrE)
a historical form of Hindi spoken mainly around Delhi and used as a lingua franca in northern India

Examples

Hindostani language
Hindostani was commonly spoken in the markets of Delhi during the 19th century.
Some classic songs from old Indian films were sung in Hindostani rather than modern Hindi.
The poet wrote in Hindostani to reach a wider audience across northern India.
