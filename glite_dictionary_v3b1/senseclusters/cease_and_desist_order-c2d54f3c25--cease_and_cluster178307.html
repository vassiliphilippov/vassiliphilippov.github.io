cease and desist order

noun
formal legal order to stop an activity
6.4
Pronunciation: / siːs ənd dɪˈsɪst ˈɔːrdər / (AmE), / siːs ənd dɪˈsɪst ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a formal legal order requiring someone to stop doing or to refrain from continuing a specific activity

Examples

A cease and desist order
A judge issued a cease and desist order against the protest group for blocking traffic.
The company received a cease and desist order to stop using the patented technology.
Without a cease and desist order, the infringement would likely have continued.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injunction - a court order requiring a person to do or cease doing a specific action
