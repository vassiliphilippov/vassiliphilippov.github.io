Virgilian

noun
admirer of Virgil
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɜrˈdʒɪliən / (AmE), / vɜːˈdʒɪliən / (BrE)
a person who writes in the style of, or strongly admires, the Roman poet Virgil

Examples

A Virgilian scholar
The professor was known as a Virgilian for his deep knowledge of Roman poetry.
Many aspiring poets looked up to the Virgilian in their literary society.
Her writing style was so classical that critics called her a modern Virgilian.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classicist - a person who studies or admires classical literature
