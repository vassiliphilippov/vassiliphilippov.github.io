weightlessly

adverb
in a light, floating way
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈweɪtlɪsli / (BrE)
in a way that is light, delicate, or as if not affected by gravity

Examples

To move weightlessly
The dancer moved weightlessly across the stage, captivating the audience with her elegance.
Leaves drifted weightlessly to the ground in the gentle autumn breeze.
He floated weightlessly in the pool, staring up at the sky.
The feather drifted weightlessly through the air before landing on the ground.
She spun weightlessly in the water, feeling as if she could float forever.
The dancers seemed to move weightlessly across the stage, barely touching the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant way
  • antonym: heavily - in a way that seems to have a lot of weight
  • synonym: lightly - in a way that is not heavy or with little weight
