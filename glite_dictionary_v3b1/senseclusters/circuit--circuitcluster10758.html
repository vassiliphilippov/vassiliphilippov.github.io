circuit

noun
a series of regular events or venues
5.8 3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.kɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kɪt / (BrE)
a regular route, journey, or series of events or places that people visit or travel around, often returning to the starting point

Examples

Lecture circuit
Many authors spend years traveling the literary circuit giving readings and talks.
She became well known on the jazz circuit in New York.
The Formula 1 racing circuit attracts drivers from all over the world.
On our vacation, we took a guided circuit of the island's historical sites.
The bike ride included a challenging circuit around the lake.
They completed a full circuit of the city before stopping for lunch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detour - a longer route that is taken to avoid a problem or to visit somewhere along the way
  • synonym: excursion - a short journey or trip, especially one taken for leisure
  • synonym: tour - a journey for pleasure in which several places are visited
