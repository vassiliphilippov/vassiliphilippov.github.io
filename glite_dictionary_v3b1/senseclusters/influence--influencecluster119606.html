influence

noun
person or thing affecting others
2.4 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnfluəns / (AmE), / ˈɪnfluəns / (BrE)
a person or thing that has the power to affect or change how someone thinks or behaves

Examples

A strong influence
My grandfather was a major influence in shaping my career path.
She has always been a positive influence on her friends.
The teacher warned the student not to be a bad influence on his classmates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authority - a person with power to give orders or make decisions
  • synonym: effect - a change that is a result of an action or other cause
