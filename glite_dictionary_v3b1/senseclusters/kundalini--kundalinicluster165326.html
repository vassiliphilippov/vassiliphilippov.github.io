kundalini

noun
spiritual energy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkʊndəˈlini / (AmE), / ˌkʊndəˈliːni / (BrE)
a spiritual energy believed in some Eastern traditions to lie dormant at the base of the spine

Examples

Kundalini energy awakening
Many yoga practitioners seek to awaken their kundalini through meditation and breathing exercises.
The teacher explained that kundalini is often described as a coiled serpent at the base of the spine.
Some people report intense experiences when their kundalini energy is activated during spiritual practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chi - life energy in Chinese philosophy
  • synonym: prana - vital life force in Hindu philosophy
