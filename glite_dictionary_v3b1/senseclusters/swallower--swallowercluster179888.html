swallower

noun
thing that consumes
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːlɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈswɒləɹə / (BrE)
an object, machine, or natural feature that takes in or consumes things or substances, often unexpectedly or completely

Examples

A coin swallower
The old vending machine was a notorious swallower of coins, rarely giving out snacks.
That river bend is known as a swallower of boats during the rainy season.
Be careful with that shredder; it's a real swallower of important documents if you're not paying attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eater - something that eats or consumes
