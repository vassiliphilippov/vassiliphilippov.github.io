Gentoo

proper noun
customizable Linux distribution
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛntuː / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntuː / (BrE)
a Linux distribution known for its high customizability and source-based package management system called Portage

Examples

Gentoo Linux distribution
Many advanced users prefer Gentoo because it allows them to optimize their systems for performance.
Installing Gentoo can take several hours since all packages are compiled from source.
She learned a lot about Linux internals while setting up Gentoo on her computer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arch Linux - a lightweight and flexible Linux distribution for advanced users
  • synonym: Ubuntu - a popular user-friendly Linux distribution
