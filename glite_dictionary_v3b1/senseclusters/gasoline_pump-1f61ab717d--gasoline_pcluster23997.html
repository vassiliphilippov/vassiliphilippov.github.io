gasoline pump

noun
machine for dispensing gasoline
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsəliːn pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈɡæsəliːn pʌmp / (BrE)
A machine at a service station used for dispensing gasoline into vehicles.
