pouf

verb
to make puffy
8.1
Pronunciation: / puːf / (AmE), / puːf / (BrE)
to shape or arrange hair, fabric, or other material into a rounded, puffed form

Examples

To pouf hair
The stylist poufed her hair to give it extra volume for the party.
He poufs the curtains every morning to let in more light.
She carefully poufed the dress sleeves to create a dramatic effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flatten - to make something flat or less puffy
  • synonym: tease - to comb hair to make it look fuller or puffier
