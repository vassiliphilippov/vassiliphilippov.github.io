immortal

adjective
not subject to death
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔrtəl / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔːtəl / (BrE)
Living forever; not subject to death.

Examples

Immortal life
Some people dream of achieving immortal fame through their works.
The legend tells of an immortal being who has watched centuries pass.
Vampires are often portrayed as immortal creatures in literature.
