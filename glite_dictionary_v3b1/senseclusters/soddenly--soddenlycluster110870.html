soddenly

adverb
dully or heavily
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑdənli / (AmE), / ˈsɒdənli / (BrE)
in a dull, heavy, or stupefied manner, often as a result of drink, sleep, or strong emotion

Examples

He looked soddenly tired
He stared soddenly at the wall, lost in his own thoughts after the argument.
After staying up all night, she moved soddenly through the quiet house.
The team sat soddenly in the locker room after their unexpected defeat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brightly - in a lively or energetic manner
  • synonym: dully - in a dull or listless manner
  • synonym: heavily - in a slow or weighted manner
