haute

adjective
high-class or elegant
Pronunciation: / oʊt / (AmE), / əʊt / (BrE)
describing something as high-class, elegant, or fashionable, especially in style or cuisine

Examples

Haute cuisine
The restaurant is known for its haute cuisine and impeccable service.
She wore a haute outfit that turned heads at the fashion show.
Many celebrities prefer haute brands for red carpet events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegant - graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • synonym: fashionable - popular or in style at a particular time
  • antonym: plain - simple and not decorated or elaborate
