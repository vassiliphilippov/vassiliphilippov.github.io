rest home

noun
institution caring for the elderly
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛst hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈrɛst həʊm / (BrE)
an institution where people, especially the elderly, are cared for and live together, usually when they cannot care for themselves at home

Examples

A rest home for elderly
After her surgery, Margaret decided to stay in a rest home for a few months to recover.
Many families visit their relatives in the rest home every weekend to keep them company.
The town built a new rest home to provide better care for its elderly residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nursing home - an institution providing medical and personal care, often for the elderly
