litho

noun
lithographic print
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪθoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɪθəʊ / (BrE)
a print made using the process of lithography, or the process itself

Examples

A litho artwork
He bought a limited edition litho from the gallery.
The artist signed each litho before the exhibition opened to the public.
Collectors often seek out rare lithos from famous printmakers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: etching - a print made from a metal plate with a design cut by acid
  • synonym: lithograph - a print made by lithography
