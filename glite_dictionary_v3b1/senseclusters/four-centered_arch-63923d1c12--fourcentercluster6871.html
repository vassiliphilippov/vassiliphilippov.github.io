four-centered arch

noun
type of elliptical or pointed arch
5.1 8.1
Pronunciation: /fɔːr ˈsen.tərd ɑːrtʃ/ /; / /fɔːr ˈsen.tərd ɑːrk/ (AmE), /fɔː ˈsen.təd ɑːtʃ/ /; / /fɔː ˈsen.təd ɑːk/ (BrE)
