spring

noun
elasticity; ability to return to shape
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ / (BrE)
the ability of a material or object to return to its original shape after being stretched, compressed, or deformed

Examples

Spring of the mat
Athletes often test the spring of a surface before jumping.
The spring in this fabric allows it to stretch without losing its shape.
The old mattress lost its spring after years of use.
