off the bat

adverb
immediately; without delay
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɔf ðə bæt / (AmE), / ɒf ðə bæt / (BrE)
immediately or without any delay; at once

Examples

Off the bat immediately
I knew off the bat that his story wasn’t true.
She answered the question off the bat without thinking.
They hired him off the bat because of his impressive résumé.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eventually - at some later time; not immediately
  • synonym: right away - immediately; without delay
  • synonym: straight away - immediately; without delay (chiefly British)
