uninformatively

adverb
in an uninformative manner
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪnˈfɔr.mə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪnˈfɔː.mə.tɪv.li / (BrE)

Examples
