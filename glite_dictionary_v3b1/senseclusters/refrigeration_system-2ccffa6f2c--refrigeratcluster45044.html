refrigeration system

noun
system for chilling or freezing, often for preservation
Pronunciation: / rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system designed to cool or freeze objects or spaces, typically to preserve food or other perishable items
