tweet

noun
a small bird's chirping sound
Pronunciation: / twit / (AmE), / twiːt / (BrE)
a weak, high-pitched chirping sound, especially one made by a small bird

Examples

A bird's tweet
The tweet of a sparrow filled the early morning air.
The forest was quiet except for the occasional tweet from a nest nearby.
We heard the faint tweet of chicks hidden in the bushes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chirp - a short, sharp sound made by small birds or insects
