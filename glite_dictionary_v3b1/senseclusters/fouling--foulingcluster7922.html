fouling

noun
rule-breaking in sports
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfaʊ.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfaʊ.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
an action in a sport that breaks the rules, especially illegal contact or conduct resulting in a penalty

Examples

Repeated fouling in football
The referee stopped the game due to repeated fouling by both teams.
Players risk being sent off for serious fouling during a match.
Strict rules are in place to prevent fouling and ensure fair competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fair play - playing according to the rules and in a sportsmanlike manner
  • synonym: foul - an action in sports that is against the rules
