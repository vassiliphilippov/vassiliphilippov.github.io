dorsal

adjective
of or on the back or upper side
5.9 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdɔːr.səl/ (AmE), /ˈdɔː.səl/ (BrE)
relating to or located on the back or upper side of an animal or organ

Examples

Dorsal surface
In many animals, the dorsal side is darker than the ventral side for camouflage.
The examiner noted a bruise on the dorsal aspect of the patient's hand.
The shark's dorsal fin was clearly visible above the water's surface.
Damage to the dorsal side of the leaf was visible after the storm.
Many fish have a prominent dorsal stripe that runs along their backs.
The shark's dorsal fin could be seen cutting through the water.
