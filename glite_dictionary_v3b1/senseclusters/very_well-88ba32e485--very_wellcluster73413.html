very well

adverb
expression of agreement or acceptance
2.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈve.rɪ wel/ /; / /ˈve.rɪ wɪl/ (AmE), /ˈve.ri wel/ /; / /ˈve.ri wiːl/ (BrE)
