mad cow disease

noun
fatal cattle disease affecting the nervous system
2.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /mæd kaʊ dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /mæd kaʊ də.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /mæd kaʊ dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /mæd kaʊ də.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
a fatal disease of cattle (bovine spongiform encephalopathy, BSE) that affects the brain and nervous system, and can pose health risks to humans if contaminated food is eaten

Examples
