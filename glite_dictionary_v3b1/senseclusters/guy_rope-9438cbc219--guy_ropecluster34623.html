guy rope

noun
rope or cable for stabilizing a structure
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪ roʊp / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪ rəʊp / (BrE)
a rope, wire, or cable used to support and stabilize a structure, especially a tent or pole, by anchoring it to the ground or another object

Examples

A guy rope anchor
A sudden storm snapped one guy rope, causing the entire structure to sway.
Be sure to tighten each guy rope before raising the tent.
The mast was held in place by several strong guy ropes anchored to the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guy wire - a wire used to brace a structure
