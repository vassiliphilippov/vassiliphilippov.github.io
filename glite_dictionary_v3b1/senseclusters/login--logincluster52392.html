login

verb
to sign in digitally
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːg ɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɒg ɪn / (BrE)
to enter your credentials to access a computer system, website, or application

Examples

To login to a website
You must login before you can view your personal information on the website.
After forgetting her password, she was unable to login to her account.
Please login using your email address and password to continue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: log in - to sign in to a computer system or website
  • antonym: log out - to exit a computer system or website after use
  • synonym: sign in - to enter credentials to access an account or system
  • antonym: sign out - to leave or exit an account or system
