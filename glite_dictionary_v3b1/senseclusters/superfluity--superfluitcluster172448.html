superfluity

noun
an excessive or unnecessary amount
9.9
Pronunciation: / suː.pɚˈfluː.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈfluː.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
an excessive amount; more than what is necessary or needed

Examples

A superfluity of things
Her wardrobe contained a superfluity of shoes she never wore.
The banquet table groaned under a superfluity of desserts.
The project suffered from a superfluity of unnecessary features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excess - an amount of something that is more than necessary
  • antonym: scarcity - the state of being in short supply; shortage
