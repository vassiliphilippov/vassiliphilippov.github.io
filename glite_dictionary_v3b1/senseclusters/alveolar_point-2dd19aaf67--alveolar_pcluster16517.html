alveolar point

noun
craniometric point on the upper jaw
Pronunciation: / ælˈvioʊlər pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ælˈviːələ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the most forward (anterior) point along the midline on the alveolar process of the maxilla, used as a reference in craniometry

Examples

The alveolar point location
During the examination, the surgeon identified the alveolar point to ensure accuracy in the procedure.
The alveolar point is an important landmark in dental and craniofacial measurements.
Variation in the position of the alveolar point can influence orthodontic treatment planning.
