scratchingly

adverb
with a scratching sound
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that produces or resembles a scratching sound or effect

Examples

Scratchingly loud music
The old record played scratchingly on the dusty turntable, filling the room with static.
She wrote scratchingly with her pencil, the sound echoing in the quiet classroom.
The dog pawed scratchingly at the door, eager to be let outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noisily - in a loud or disruptive manner
  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without roughness
