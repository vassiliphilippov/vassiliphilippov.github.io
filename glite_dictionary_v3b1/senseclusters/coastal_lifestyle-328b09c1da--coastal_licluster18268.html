coastal lifestyle

noun
way of living by the coast
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊstl̩ ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊstl ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
a way of living characterized by activities, attitudes, and culture associated with living near the coast

Examples

A coastal lifestyle choice
Many retirees choose to adopt a coastal lifestyle after years of city living.
She loved the relaxed pace and outdoor activities of the coastal lifestyle.
The coastal lifestyle often includes surfing, boating, and fresh seafood meals.
