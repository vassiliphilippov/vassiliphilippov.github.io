mainstreamy

adjective
conforming to mainstream trends
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnˌstriːmi / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnˌstriːmi / (BrE)
(informal) Conforming to or characteristic of mainstream trends, especially in culture, media, or entertainment.

Examples

A mainstreamy style
His music used to be unique, but lately it's become surprisingly mainstreamy.
She tends to avoid mainstreamy films and prefers indie productions.
The director's latest movie felt a bit too mainstreamy for fans of his earlier, edgier work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mainstream - the ideas, attitudes, or activities regarded as normal or conventional
