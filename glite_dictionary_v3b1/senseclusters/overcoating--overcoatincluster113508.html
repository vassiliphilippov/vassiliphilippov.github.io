overcoating

noun
additional protective coating
3.5 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

The wooden fence needed an overcoating to protect it from the harsh winter weather.
