Orcus

proper noun
demon prince of undeath (fantasy)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.kəs/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.kəs/ (BrE)
(in modern fantasy and role-playing games) a powerful demon prince of undeath; an undead demon-lord figure often depicted ruling legions of the dead.

Examples

Demon lord Orcus
In many D&D campaigns, Orcus is portrayed as a bloated demon prince ruling over legions of undead.
The players feared that Orcus would return to the world and corrupt the kingdom with undeath.
A fan painted a terrifying mural of Orcus clutching a skull and surrounded by necromantic runes.
