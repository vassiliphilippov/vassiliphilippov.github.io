Synonyms

  • fall over
    1.0 3.06/m
    fall over means losing balance and tipping onto the ground, while fall down is a general sudden drop from an upright position.
  • fall
    1.0 164/m
    fall is the broad verb for moving downward by gravity, while fall down shows losing upright balance and hitting the ground.
  • drop down
    1.8 2.73/m
    drop down describes moving from high to low, sometimes by choice or slowly, while fall down is sudden loss of upright balance.
  • tumble
    2.9 2.78/m
    tumble is a roll or quick acrobatic fall with turning, while fall down is simply losing upright balance and hitting the ground.
  • topple
    4.1 7.56/m
    topple often means to tip over, especially for objects or when pushed, while fall down focuses on a person losing upright balance.
