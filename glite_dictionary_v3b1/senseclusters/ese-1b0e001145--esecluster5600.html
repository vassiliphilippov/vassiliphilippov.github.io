ESE

noun
compass point between east and southeast
6.6
Pronunciation: / i ɛs i / (AmE), / iː ɛs iː / (BrE)
The compass point that lies midway between east and southeast, corresponding to 112.5° on a compass.

Examples

Direction ESE
He checked the map and found the village lying almost directly ESE of the city.
The ship adjusted its course to ESE to avoid the storm.
The trail continues ESE for several miles before turning south.
