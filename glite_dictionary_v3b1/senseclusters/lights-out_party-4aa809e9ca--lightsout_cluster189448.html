lights-out party

noun
event where all lights are turned off
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪts aʊt ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈlaɪts aʊt ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
an event in which all the lights are deliberately turned off, often to raise awareness about energy consumption or for a special occasion

Examples

A lights-out party
A lights-out party can be a fun way to teach children about saving energy.
During Earth Hour, our community hosted a lights-out party to support environmental awareness.
The students organized a lights-out party to experience one evening without electricity.
