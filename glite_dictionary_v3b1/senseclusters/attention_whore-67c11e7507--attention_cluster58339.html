attention whore

noun
person who seeks excessive attention
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛn.ʃən wɔːr / (AmE), / əˈtɛn.ʃən wɔː / (BrE)
A person who seeks attention from others in an excessive or undignified manner, often for social validation.

Examples

Attention whore online
Calling someone an attention whore is rude and can be quite offensive.
He acts like an attention whore at every social gathering, always trying to be in the spotlight.
Some people accused her of being an attention whore because of her constant posting on social media.
