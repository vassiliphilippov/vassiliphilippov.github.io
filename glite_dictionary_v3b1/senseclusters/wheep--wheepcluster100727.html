wheep

noun
bird whistling sound
9.7
Pronunciation: / wiːp / (AmE), / wiːp / (BrE)
a high-pitched, whistling sound, especially one made by certain birds

Examples

A bird's wheep
The sandpiper let out a sharp wheep as it flew over the water.
Early in the morning, we heard the distinctive wheep of a bird in the marsh.
The guide pointed out the wheep that signaled the presence of a rare species nearby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chirp - a short, sharp sound made by a small bird or insect
  • synonym: whistle - a clear, high-pitched sound made by blowing air or by birds
