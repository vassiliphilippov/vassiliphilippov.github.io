naiant

adjective
depicted horizontally (heraldry, esp. fish)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈneɪənt / (BrE)
(Heraldry) Depicted or shown horizontally, typically said of a fish pictured swimming across a shield or emblem.

Examples

A naiant fish
In heraldic art, a fish shown naiant appears to be swimming across the shield.
The emblem features three trout naiant as a symbol of abundance.
The family crest displayed a salmon naiant on a blue background.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hauriant - (Heraldry) Depicted upright with the head up (typically of a fish)
