sip

noun
a small drink
Pronunciation: / sɪp / (AmE), / sɪp / (BrE)
a small amount of liquid taken into the mouth and swallowed

Examples

A sip of tea
After a sip of water, she continued her run.
He took a cautious sip of the hot coffee before speaking.
Would you like to have a sip of my juice?

Related Terms

  • antonym: gulp - a large and hurried swallow of liquid
