dollop of cream

noun
a small quantity of cream
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːləp əv kriːm / (AmE), / ˈdɒləp əv kriːm / (BrE)
a small, shapeless amount of cream, typically added as a topping

Examples

A large dollop of cream
A warm scone is delicious with a dollop of cream and strawberry jam.
He finished his pie with a generous dollop of cream on top.
She topped the strawberries with a dollop of cream for extra richness.
