pipefitter

noun
pipe installation worker
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpˌfɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpˌfɪtə / (BrE)
a worker who installs, assembles, and repairs pipes for heating, cooling, or other mechanical systems

Examples

The pipefitter installs pipes
The pipefitter spent the morning installing new pipes in the factory.
After the inspection, a pipefitter was called to repair the damaged steam line.
Becoming a skilled pipefitter requires both training and experience with complex systems.
