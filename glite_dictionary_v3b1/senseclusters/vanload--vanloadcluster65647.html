vanload

verb
to load a van
Pronunciation: / ˈvænˌloʊd / (AmE), / ˈvænləʊd / (BrE)
to load goods or items into a van, especially for transportation.

Examples

To vanload furniture
The movers arrived early to vanload all the boxes before the rain started.
We need to vanload the equipment quickly so we can reach the venue on time.
After the event, volunteers helped vanload the leftover supplies for storage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pack - to fill a container or vehicle with items for transport
  • antonym: unload - to remove goods from a vehicle or container
