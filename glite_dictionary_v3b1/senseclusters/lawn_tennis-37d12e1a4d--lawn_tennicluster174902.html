lawn tennis

noun
tennis played on a court with a net
2.3 2.4
Pronunciation: / lɔn ˈtɛnɪs / (AmE), / lɔːn ˈtɛnɪs / (BrE)
A game played on a rectangular court divided by a net, in which two or four players use rackets to hit a ball back and forth over the net.

Examples
