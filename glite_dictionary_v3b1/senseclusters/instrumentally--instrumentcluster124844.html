instrumentally

adverb
by musical instruments
5.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.strə.ˈmen.təl.i/ (AmE), /ɪn.strʊ.ˈmen.təl.i/ (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is performed by musical instruments, often without vocals.

Examples

Instrumentally played music
The song was performed instrumentally, with no singers on stage.
Many film soundtracks are composed instrumentally to create a specific mood.
The band is known for playing both instrumentally and with vocals at their concerts.
