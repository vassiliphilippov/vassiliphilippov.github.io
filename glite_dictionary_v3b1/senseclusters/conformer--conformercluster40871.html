conformer

noun
person who follows norms
7.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàf…îrm…ôr / (AmE), / k…ônÀàf…îÀêm…ô / (BrE)
a person who behaves or thinks in the same way as most other people, following accepted rules or customs

Examples

Social conformer
As a conformer, she rarely challenged the opinions of her colleagues.
The school encouraged individuality, but some students remained conformers to fit in.
He was seen as a conformer because he always followed the latest trends.

  • complier

  • rule-follower
