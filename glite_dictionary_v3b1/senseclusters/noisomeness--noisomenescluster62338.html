noisomeness

noun
the quality of being harmful or offensive
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɪsəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔɪsəmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being noxious, harmful, or offensive, especially to the senses

Examples

The noisomeness of plants
Despite its beauty, the garden was plagued by the noisomeness of decaying plants.
Residents complained about the noisomeness emanating from the factory at night.
The noisomeness of the polluted river made it unbearable to go near.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmlessness - the absence of harm; lack of danger or offensiveness
  • synonym: noisome - harmful or offensive, especially to the senses
