African gray

noun
species of intelligent, grey African parrot
Pronunciation: / ˌæfrɪkən ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˌaf.rɪ.kən ˈɡreɪ /; / ˌæfrɪkən ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
a species of medium-sized, grey-colored parrot native to equatorial Africa, known for its intelligence and ability to mimic human speech
