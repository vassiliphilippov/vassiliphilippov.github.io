green apple aphid

noun
a bright green aphid infesting apple trees
Pronunciation: / ɡrin ˈæpəl ˈeɪfɪd / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈæpəl ˈeɪfɪd / (BrE)
a small, bright green aphid that feeds on apple trees, often causing their leaves to curl

Examples

The green apple aphid pest
Gardeners often use natural predators to control green apple aphid populations.

The green apple aphid can quickly multiply and damage young apple trees.
