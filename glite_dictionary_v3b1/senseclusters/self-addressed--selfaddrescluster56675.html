self-addressed

adjective
addressed to oneself
4.5 3.5
Pronunciation: / sɛlf əˈdrɛst / (AmE), / sɛlf əˈdrɛst / (BrE)
(of an envelope or package) having the name and address of the sender written on it, usually so it can be returned or replied to.

Examples
