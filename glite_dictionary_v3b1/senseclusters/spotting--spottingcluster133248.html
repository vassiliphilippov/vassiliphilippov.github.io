spotting

noun
the act of making spots or stains
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of causing spots or stains to appear on something

Examples

Shirt spotting
After the science experiment, there was significant spotting on the lab coat.
She noticed some spotting on the tablecloth after the dinner party.
The old photographs showed spotting due to water damage over the years.
