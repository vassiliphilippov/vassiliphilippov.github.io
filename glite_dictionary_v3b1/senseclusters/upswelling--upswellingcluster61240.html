upswelling

noun
rising to surface
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌswɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌswɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
a rising of water, emotion, or another substance toward the surface, often bringing something from below

Examples

Ocean upswelling
The upswelling of cold ocean water supports a rich variety of marine life.
She felt an upswelling of emotion as she watched the moving speech.
An upswelling of lava threatened the nearby village after the volcanic eruption.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subsidence - the sinking or downward movement of something
  • synonym: surge - a sudden and powerful upward movement
