crack shot

noun
someone highly skilled at shooting
Pronunciation: / kræk ʃɑt / (AmE), / kræk ʃɒt / (BrE)
a person who is extremely skilled at shooting, especially with firearms

Examples

A crack shot shooter
Despite the windy conditions, the crack shot managed to hit the bullseye every time.
Everyone knew that Jack was a crack shot and could hit any target he aimed at.
The army recruited her because she proved to be a crack shot during training.

  • synonym: sharpshooter - an expert shooter
