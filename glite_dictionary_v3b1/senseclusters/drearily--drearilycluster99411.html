drearily

adverb
in a cheerless or gloomy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪrəli / (AmE), / ˈdrɪərɪli / (BrE)
In a dull, cheerless, or gloomy manner.

Examples

To drearily walk home
He drearily walked home, weighed down by the disappointing news.
The meeting dragged on drearily, with little enthusiasm from anyone in the room.
The rain fell drearily against the window as we waited inside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy or lively way
  • synonym: dismally - in a depressing or gloomy way
