academization

noun
conversion to academy status
8.0
Pronunciation: / əˌkædəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌkædəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting a school or group of schools into an academy, especially making them independent of local authority control in the UK

Examples

Academization of schools
The academization of the school system has changed how education is managed in many regions.
Parents expressed concerns about the rapid academization of their local schools.
Debates over academization often focus on the loss of local authority control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: academisation - the British spelling of academization
  • antonym: nationalization - the process of bringing institutions under government control
