censoriousness

noun
tendency to criticize
9.1
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈsɔriəsnəs /; / sɛnˈsɔːriəsnəs / (AmE), / sɛnˈsɔːriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very critical and quick to find fault with others
