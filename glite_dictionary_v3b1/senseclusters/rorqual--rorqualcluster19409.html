rorqual

noun
a type of baleen whale
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔrˌkwɑl / (AmE), / ˈrɔː.kwəl / (BrE)
A large baleen whale of the family Balaenopteridae, characterized by longitudinal throat grooves and a small, pointed dorsal fin.

Examples

A rorqual whale
Scientists studied the feeding behavior of several rorquals in Antarctic waters.
The blue whale is the largest rorqual and indeed the largest animal ever known to have lived.
Unlike other whales, the rorqual has deep grooves along its throat, allowing its mouth to expand when feeding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blue whale - the largest animal on Earth, a species of rorqual
  • synonym: fin whale - a species of rorqual whale, second-largest animal
