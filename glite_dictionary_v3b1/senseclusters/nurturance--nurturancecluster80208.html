nurturance

noun
providing care and nourishment
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrtʃərəns / (AmE), / ˈnɜːtʃərəns / (BrE)
the act of providing physical and emotional care and nourishment, especially to support growth and development

Examples

Emotional nurturance
Children thrive when they receive nurturance from their caregivers.
Emotional nurturance is important for healthy relationships.
The teacher's nurturance helped students feel safe and confident in class.
