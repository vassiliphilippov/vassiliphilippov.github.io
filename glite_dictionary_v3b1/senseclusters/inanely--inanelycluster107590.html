inanely

adverb
in a silly or foolish way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈneɪnli / (AmE), / ɪˈneɪnli / (BrE)
in a silly, pointless, or foolish manner

Examples

To inanely giggle
She inanely giggled at every joke, no matter how unfunny it was.
The meeting dragged on as he inanely repeated the same points.
Their conversation drifted inanely from one trivial topic to another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foolishly - in a silly or unwise manner
  • antonym: sensibly - in a reasonable or wise manner
