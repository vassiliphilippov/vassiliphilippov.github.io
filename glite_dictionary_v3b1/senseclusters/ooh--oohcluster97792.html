ooh

noun
sound of admiration
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
a sound made to express surprise, delight, or admiration

Examples

A loud ooh
The crowd's excited ooh filled the theater as the fireworks exploded.
She let out a soft ooh when she saw the beautiful painting.
There was a collective ooh from the audience as the acrobat performed a daring flip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aah - a sound made to express pleasure or amazement
  • antonym: boo - a sound made to show disapproval or surprise
