prexy

noun
college or university president (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛksi / (AmE), / ˈprɛksi / (BrE)
an informal term for the head administrative officer of a college or university

Examples

The prexy of college
As prexy of the college, she introduced several student-friendly policies.
Everyone gathered in the auditorium to hear the prexy speak.
The new prexy made sweeping changes across the university.
