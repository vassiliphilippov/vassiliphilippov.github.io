garden lettuce

noun
cultivated lettuce used in salads
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
A type of lettuce (Lactuca sativa) cultivated in gardens, with succulent leaves commonly used in salads.

Examples

Fresh garden lettuce
After a week, the garden lettuce had grown large enough to harvest.
She planted garden lettuce in her backyard to have fresh salads all summer.
The chef prefers using garden lettuce for its crisp texture in his signature dishes.
