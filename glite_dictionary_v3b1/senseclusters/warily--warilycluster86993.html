warily

adverb
in a cautious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛrəli / (AmE), / ˈweərəli / (BrE)
In a cautious or watchful manner, showing or feeling caution about possible dangers or problems.

Examples

To warily watch
He answered the unexpected question warily, unsure of the interviewer's intentions.
She glanced warily over her shoulder, nervous about the noise behind her.
The fox approached the trap warily, sniffing the air for danger.
