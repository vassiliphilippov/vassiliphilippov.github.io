varnish tree

noun
Asian tree yielding lacquer sap
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑrnɪʃ tri / (AmE), / ˈvɑːnɪʃ triː / (BrE)
a small Asian tree that produces a poisonous sap used to make lacquer

Examples

Poisonous varnish tree
Contact with a varnish tree can cause severe skin irritation due to its toxic exudate.
The sap of the varnish tree is carefully collected by skilled artisans in Asia.
Traditional Japanese lacquerware is made using resin from the varnish tree.
