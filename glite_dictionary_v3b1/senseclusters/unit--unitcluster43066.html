unit

noun
single element of a larger whole
Pronunciation: / ˈjunɪt /; / ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪt /; / ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a single thing, person, or part that is part of a larger whole
