cocktail sauce

noun
spicy condiment for seafood
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːk.teɪl sɔs / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.teɪl sɔːs / (BrE)
a spicy sauce typically made from ketchup, horseradish, and lemon juice, often served with seafood

Examples

Cocktail sauce with shrimp
He dipped the shrimp into the cocktail sauce before taking a bite.
I always make my own cocktail sauce for seafood parties.
The waiter brought a small bowl of cocktail sauce with our oysters.
