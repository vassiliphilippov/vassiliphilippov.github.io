soundproofing

verb
to block sound
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
to make a room, building, or object resistant to the passage of sound, usually by adding special materials or structures

Examples

To soundproof a room
They are soundproofing the rehearsal room to reduce echo and outside noise.
The hotel is soundproofing its conference rooms to ensure privacy during meetings.
We spent the weekend soundproofing the garage so the band could practice without disturbing the neighbors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulate - to protect something by surrounding it with material that reduces the passage of heat, sound, or electricity
  • synonym: seal - to close tightly to prevent the passage of air, liquid, or sound
