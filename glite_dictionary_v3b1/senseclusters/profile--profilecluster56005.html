profile

noun
vertical section showing earth or soil layers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊfaɪl /; / ˈproʊ.faɪl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊfaɪl /; / ˈprəʊ.faɪl / (BrE)
an outline or side view of something, especially a face or a cross-section (such as of the earth or an object)

Examples

A profile view
A cross-sectional profile of the valley revealed distinct rock stratum.
The geologist studied the soil profile to identify different sediment layers.
The team drew a detailed profile of the excavation site to document the horizons.
He has a very distinctive profile with a strong jawline.
The artist captured her profile in a simple pencil sketch.
The mountain's profile was clearly visible against the evening sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: silhouette - the dark shape and outline of someone or something visible against a lighter background
