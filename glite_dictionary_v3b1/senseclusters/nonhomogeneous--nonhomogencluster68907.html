nonhomogeneous

adjective
not uniform
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnhəˈmoʊdʒəˌniəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnhəˈməʊdʒɪniəs / (BrE)
not uniform in structure or composition; consisting of different or diverse parts

Examples

Nonhomogeneous mixture
The soil sample was clearly nonhomogeneous, containing layers of sand, clay, and gravel.
A nonhomogeneous group of students can bring many perspectives to a discussion.
The solution remained nonhomogeneous even after several minutes of stirring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heterogeneous - consisting of different or diverse elements
  • antonym: homogeneous - uniform in structure or composition
