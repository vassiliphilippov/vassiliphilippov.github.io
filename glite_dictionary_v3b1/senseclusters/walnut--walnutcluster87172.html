walnut

noun
hard wood from walnut trees, used in furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔl.nət / (AmE), / ˈwɔːl.nʌt / (BrE)
the hard, dark-brown wood of a walnut tree, used especially for making furniture and paneling

Examples

Walnut furniture
An antique desk crafted from walnut adds elegance to the study.
The cabinet was made from polished walnut and had intricate carvings.
They chose walnut for the flooring because of its color and durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - hard reddish-brown wood used for furniture
