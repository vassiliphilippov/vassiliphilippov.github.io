gratefully

adverb
in a thankful or appreciative manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtfəli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows thanks or appreciation

Examples

To gratefully accept
He gratefully received their advice and put it into practice.
She smiled gratefully as she accepted the award from her mentor.
The community gratefully acknowledged the volunteers' hard work after the storm.
He accepted their help gratefully, knowing he couldn't have finished on his own.
She smiled gratefully when he offered her a seat on the crowded bus.
They listened gratefully to the volunteers' warm words after the long journey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: resentfully - in a manner showing resentment or bitterness
  • synonym: thankfully - in a thankful manner; with gratitude
  • antonym: ungratefully - in a manner that shows no gratitude
