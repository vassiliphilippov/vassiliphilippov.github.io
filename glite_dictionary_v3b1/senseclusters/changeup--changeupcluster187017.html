changeup

noun
player substitution
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒˌʌp / (BrE)
a substitution of a player or a change in a team's lineup during a game

Examples

A late changeup substitution
The coach made a late changeup to replace the injured striker before halftime.
A quick changeup in the lineup helped the team regain control of the match.
After the unexpected changeup, the team played with renewed energy and focus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lineup - the arrangement or list of players in a team for a game
  • synonym: substitution - the act of replacing one player with another during a game
