perplexingly

adverb
in a confusing way
7.9
Pronunciation: / pərˈplɛksɪŋli / (AmE), / pəˈplɛksɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes confusion or is difficult to understand

Examples

A perplexingly complex plot
The plot of the novel was perplexingly complex, leaving many readers puzzled.
He explained the rules perplexingly, making it hard for anyone to follow.
The directions were perplexingly written, so we got lost on our way.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to understand
  • synonym: confusingly - in a way that causes confusion
