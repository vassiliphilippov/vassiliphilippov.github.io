meat cutter

noun
tool for cutting meat
3.3
Pronunciation: /miːt ˈkʌt.ər/ (AmE), /miːt ˈkʌt.ə/ (BrE)
a machine or handheld tool used to cut meat into portions or slices, especially in shops or processing plants

Examples

Electric meat cutter
The supermarket uses a modern meat cutter to prepare fresh steaks every morning.
After cleaning the meat cutter, the worker started slicing pork for the deli counter.
A sharp meat cutter is essential for processing large quantities of beef efficiently.
