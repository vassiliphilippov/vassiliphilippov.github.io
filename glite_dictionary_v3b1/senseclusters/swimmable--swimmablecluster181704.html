swimmable

adjective
suitable for swimming
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪməbəl / (AmE), / ˈswɪməbəl / (BrE)
safe and suitable for swimming in, especially referring to water conditions

Examples

Swimmable water
The river is finally swimmable after months of cleanup efforts.
Officials tested the water to ensure it was swimmable before opening the beach.
During the dry season, the lake is not always swimmable due to low water levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: safe - not likely to cause harm or danger
  • antonym: unsuitable - not appropriate or safe for a particular purpose
