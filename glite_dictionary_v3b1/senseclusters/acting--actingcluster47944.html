acting

adjective
serving temporarily as substitute
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæktɪŋ / (BrE)
serving temporarily in place of another, especially as a substitute in a position or office

Examples

Acting manager
During the CEO's absence, the vice president served as acting director.
She was appointed acting manager until a permanent replacement could be found.
The acting chairman led the meeting with confidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permanent - lasting or intended to last or remain unchanged indefinitely
  • synonym: temporary - lasting for only a limited period of time; not permanent
