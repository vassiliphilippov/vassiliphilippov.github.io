nonsentient

adjective
lacking consciousness
9.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛnʃənt / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛnʃənt / (BrE)
not capable of feeling, perceiving, or having consciousness

Examples

Nonsentient objects
A computer is still considered nonsentient despite its ability to process information.
Many scientists debate whether certain animals are truly nonsentient or simply less aware.
Plants are nonsentient organisms, unable to experience pain or pleasure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insentient - not able to feel or perceive
  • antonym: sentient - able to feel or perceive; conscious
