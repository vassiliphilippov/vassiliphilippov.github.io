three-quarters

noun
three out of four equal parts
3.6
Pronunciation: / θri ˈkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / θri ˈkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
An amount equal to three out of four equal parts of a whole; three-fourths.

Examples

A three-quarters cup
He measured three-quarters of a cup of sugar for the recipe.
Nearly three-quarters of the students voted in favor of the proposal.
Their team won three-quarters of their games this season.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: half - one of two equal parts; 1/2
  • synonym: three-fourths - the fraction 3/4; three out of four equal parts
  • degree higher: whole - the entire unit; all parts combined; 1/1
