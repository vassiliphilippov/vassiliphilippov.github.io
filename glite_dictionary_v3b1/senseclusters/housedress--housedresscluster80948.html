housedress

noun
casual home dress
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌdrɛs / (BrE)
a simple, comfortable dress worn by women at home for everyday tasks or chores

Examples

A housedress at home
After coming home from work, she put on her favorite housedress to relax.
The old photograph showed her grandmother wearing a floral housedress while baking bread.
Many women in the 1950s wore a housedress while doing household chores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apron - a protective garment worn over clothes while working at home
  • antonym: nightgown - a loose dress worn for sleeping
