Synonyms

  • personal manner
    2.9 0.12/m
    personal manner is a person's typical, private way of acting in many situations, while manner is any way someone acts or does something.
  • behavior
    1.8 146/m
    behavior is how a person or animal acts, often in social situations, while manner is the general way someone does something.
  • conduct
    3.7 26/m
    conduct is a more formal word for how someone behaves in a particular situation, while manner is the general way of acting.
  • demeanor
    4.9 5.57/m
    demeanor is the outward look of how someone acts and shows their attitude, while manner is the simple way a person behaves.
