manner

noun
way of acting or behaving
Pronunciation: / ˈmænər / (AmE), / ˈmænə / (BrE)
a way in which a person acts, behaves, or does something

Examples

Polite manner
He spoke in a calm and respectful manner during the meeting.
Her manner of addressing the problem impressed her colleagues.
The teacher explained the rules in a clear manner so everyone could understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashion - a particular or current way of behaving, dressing, or living
  • synonym: method - a particular procedure for accomplishing or approaching something
  • synonym: style - a particular way in which something is done, created, or performed
