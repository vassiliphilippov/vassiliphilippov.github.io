palmlike

adjective
resembling a palm tree
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɑːmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a palm tree in form or appearance

Examples

A palmlike tree
A palmlike silhouette stood against the sunset on the tropical island.
Some houseplants have a palmlike appearance even though they are not true palms.
The plant had palmlike leaves but was actually a type of fern.
