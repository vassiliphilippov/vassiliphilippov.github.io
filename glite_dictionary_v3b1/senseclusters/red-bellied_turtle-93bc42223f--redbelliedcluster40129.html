red-bellied turtle

noun
freshwater turtle with a red underside
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd ˈbɛlid ˈtɝtəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈbɛlɪd ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
