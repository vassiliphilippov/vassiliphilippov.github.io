two-piece suit

noun
business suit with jacket and trousers or skirt
Pronunciation: / tu pis sut / (AmE), / tuː piːs suːt / (BrE)
A business suit consisting of a matching jacket and either a pair of trousers or a skirt.

Examples

A two-piece suit outfit
He wore a navy two-piece suit to the job interview.
Her two-piece suit was tailored perfectly for the business meeting.
Many professionals prefer a two-piece suit for everyday office wear.
