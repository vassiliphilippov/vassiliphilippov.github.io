moon blindness

noun
recurrent eye disease in horses
Pronunciation: / mun ˈblaɪnd.nəs / (AmE), / muːn ˈblaɪnd.nəs / (BrE)
A recurrent inflammation of the eye in horses that can lead to blindness.
