shepherd's pouch

noun
annual plant with triangular pods
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛpɚdz paʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛpədz paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a white-flowered annual plant (Capsella bursa-pastoris) with triangular, notched seed pods, widely distributed as a weed

Examples

The shepherd's pouch plant
Gardeners often remove shepherd's pouch because it can quickly spread and crowd out other plants.
The shepherd's pouch thrives in disturbed soils across much of the world.
The heart-shaped pods of the shepherd's pouch make it easy to identify in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shepherd's purse - an alternative name for Capsella bursa-pastoris, the same plant
