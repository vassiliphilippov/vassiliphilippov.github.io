ubiquity

noun
the quality of being everywhere at once
Pronunciation: / juˈbɪkwəti / (AmE), / juˈbɪkwɪti / (BrE)
the state of being present everywhere or seeming to be widespread at the same time

Examples

The ubiquity of coffee shops
He was struck by the ubiquity of coffee shops in the city center.
The ubiquity of smartphones has changed how we communicate and access information.
The ubiquity of social media makes it hard to escape online updates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: omnipresence - the state of being everywhere at once
