humorlessness

noun
lack of humor
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmərləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmələsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of lacking a sense of humor or the ability to appreciate jokes

Examples

Her humorlessness showed
Her humorlessness made it difficult for her to enjoy lighthearted conversations.
The film's humorlessness was criticized by many reviewers who expected a comedy.
Despite his humorlessness, he was respected for his dedication and hard work.
