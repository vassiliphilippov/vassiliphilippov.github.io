white campion

noun
a plant with sticky stems and white evening-opening flowers
8.6 7.4
Pronunciation: /waɪt ˈkæm.pɪ.ən/ /; / /hwaɪt ˈkæm.pɪ.ən/ (AmE), /waɪt ˈkæm.pɪ.ən/ (BrE)
