hydroxytetracycline

noun
a yellow antibiotic used to treat infections
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdrɑksiˌtɛtrəˈsaɪklin / (AmE), / haɪˌdrɒksɪˌtɛtrəˈsaɪkliːn / (BrE)
a yellow crystalline antibiotic, also known as Terramycin, used to treat a variety of bacterial and rickettsial infections
