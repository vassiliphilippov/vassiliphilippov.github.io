Lützen

proper noun
battle of the Thirty Years' War, 1632
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊt.sən / (AmE), / ˈlʏt.sən / (BrE)
a decisive battle in the Thirty Years' War (1632) where Swedish forces led by Gustavus Adolphus defeated the Holy Roman Empire under Wallenstein, resulting in the death of Gustavus Adolphus
