Carcharias taurus

noun
large shallow-water shark species, sand tiger shark
Pronunciation: / kɑrˌkɛəriəs ˈtɔːrəs / (AmE), / kɑːˌkɛəriəs ˈtɔːrəs / (BrE)
A species of large, predatory shark found in shallow coastal waters on both sides of the Atlantic, sometimes known as the sand tiger shark and known for its sharp, jagged teeth.
