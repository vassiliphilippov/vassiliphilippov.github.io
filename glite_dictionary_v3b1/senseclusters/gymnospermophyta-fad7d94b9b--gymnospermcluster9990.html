Gymnospermophyta

noun
division of plants with naked seeds
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɪm.nəˌspɜr.məˈfaɪ.tə /; / dʒɪmˌnoʊspɚˈmɑfɪtə / (AmE), / dʒɪm.nəˌspɜː.məˈfaɪ.tə /; / dʒɪmˌnɒspəˈmɒfɪtə / (BrE)
a division of plants (gymnosperms) that have seeds not enclosed in an ovary, including conifers, cycads, ginkgo, and gnetophytes
