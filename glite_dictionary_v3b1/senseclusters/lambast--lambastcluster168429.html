lambast

verb
to beat with a cane or strike repeatedly
Pronunciation: / læmˈbæst, læmˈbeɪst / (AmE), / ləmˈbɑːst, læmˈbeɪst / (BrE)
to beat or strike repeatedly, especially with a cane or similar object

Examples

To lambast with a stick
He was lambasted for his insolence, both with words and with a stick.
In the old days, some teachers would lambast misbehaving children with a cane.
The headmaster threatened to lambast any student caught cheating.
