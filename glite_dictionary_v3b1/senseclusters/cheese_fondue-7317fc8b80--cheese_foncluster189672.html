cheese fondue

noun
Swiss dish of melted cheese and wine for dipping
1.9
Pronunciation: / tʃiz fɑnˈdu / (AmE), / tʃiːz fɒnˈdjuː / (BrE)
A Swiss dish consisting of melted cheese and wine served in a communal pot for dipping bread, and sometimes fruits or vegetables.

Examples

Swiss cheese fondue
At the party, we all enjoyed dipping bread into the cheese fondue together.
Making cheese fondue at home is a fun and interactive meal for friends and family.
The restaurant offers a traditional Swiss cheese fondue served with assorted breads and vegetables.
