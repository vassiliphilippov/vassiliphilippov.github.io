meetup

noun
informal gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmiːtˌʌp / (BrE)
an informal or organized gathering where people with shared interests or purposes meet, often arranged in advance or online

Examples

A meetup event
We planned a meetup at the park to catch up with old friends.
The photography club holds a monthly meetup for its members.
After chatting online for weeks, they finally had their first meetup in person.
I joined a meetup for local photographers to share tips and experiences.
The tech meetup attracted people interested in the latest software trends.
After moving to the city, he found friends through a hiking meetup.
We organized a meetup for local artists to share their work and ideas.
The online meetup allowed people from different countries to connect and collaborate.
After joining the hiking group, she attended her first meetup in the city park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion
  • synonym: gathering - a meeting of people for a particular purpose
  • synonym: meeting - an event where people come together to discuss or decide something
  • degree higher: party - a social gathering for celebration or entertainment
