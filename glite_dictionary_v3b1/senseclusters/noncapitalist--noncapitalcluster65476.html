noncapitalist

noun
opponent of capitalism
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (AmE), / nɒnˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (BrE)
a person, group, or system that does not support, practice, or participate in capitalism

Examples

A noncapitalist leader
The new political party attracted many noncapitalists who wanted economic reform.
As a committed noncapitalist, he advocated for public ownership of major industries.
Some noncapitalists believe that alternative economic systems can create a fairer society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticapitalist - a person who is opposed to capitalism
  • antonym: capitalist - a person who supports or practices capitalism
