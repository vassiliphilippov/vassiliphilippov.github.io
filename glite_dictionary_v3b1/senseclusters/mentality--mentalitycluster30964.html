mentality

noun
mental ability; intelligence
Pronunciation: / mɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / mɛnˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the capacity for intelligent thought; intelligence or mental ability

Examples

Sharp mentality
Despite his young age, his mentality rivals that of experienced professionals.
Her sharp mentality helped her solve the complex puzzle in minutes.
The test is designed to measure the mentality of students in different age groups.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: dullness - lack of intelligence or mental ability
  • synonym: intelligence - the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills
