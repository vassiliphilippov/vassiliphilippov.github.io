Sistrurus catenatus

noun
a species of small rattlesnake
Pronunciation: / sɪˈstruːrəs kəˈteɪnətəs / (AmE), / sɪˈstrʊərəs kəˈteɪnətəs / (BrE)
A small species of rattlesnake found in moist habitats from the Great Lakes region to Mexico, feeding mainly on mice and small amphibians.
