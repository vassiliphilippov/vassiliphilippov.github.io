overbred

adjective
bred too much
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbrɛd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbrɛd / (BrE)
bred excessively, often resulting in undesirable traits, weakness, or health problems

Examples

Overbred dogs
The overbred horses showed signs of weakness and frequent illness.
Many experts warn that overbred animals are more likely to develop genetic disorders.
Some dog breeds have become overbred, leading to a range of health issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inbred - bred from closely related individuals, often causing genetic problems
  • degree lower: purebred - bred from parents of the same breed or variety
