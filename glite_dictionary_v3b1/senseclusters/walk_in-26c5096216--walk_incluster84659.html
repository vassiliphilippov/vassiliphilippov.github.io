walk in

verb
enter a place by walking
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɒk ɪn / (AmE), / wɔːk ɪn / (BrE)
to enter a place by walking, especially in a casual or unannounced manner

Examples

To walk in a room

Please don't walk in when the movie has already started.
She always walks in without knocking, surprising everyone in the room.
