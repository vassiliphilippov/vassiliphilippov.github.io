particle

noun
function word in phrasal verbs
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪkəl / (BrE)
A function word, typically an adverb or preposition, used with a verb to form a phrasal verb in English.

Examples

A phrasal verb particle
In the phrasal verb 'give up', 'up' is the particle that changes the meaning of the verb.
Learning to recognize the particle in phrasal verbs helps improve your understanding of English idioms.
Not every word that follows a verb is a particle; some are simply prepositions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adverb - a word that modifies a verb, adjective, or other adverb
  • synonym: preposition - a word placed before a noun to show relation
