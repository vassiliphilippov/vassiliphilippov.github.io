simulant

noun
person faking illness
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmjələnt / (AmE), / ˈsɪmjʊlənt / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be ill or injured in order to avoid work, duty, or responsibility

Examples

Army simulant
The army doctor quickly identified the recruit as a simulant trying to avoid training.
Hospitals sometimes struggle to distinguish a true patient from a simulant seeking time off work.
The manager warned that any simulant would face disciplinary action if caught faking illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faker - a person who pretends or deceives
  • antonym: invalid - a person who is genuinely ill or disabled
  • synonym: malingerer - a person who pretends to be sick to avoid work or duty
