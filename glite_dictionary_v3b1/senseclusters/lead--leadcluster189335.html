lead

noun
short wire for electrical connection
Pronunciation: / lid / (AmE), / liːd / (BrE)
a short piece of wire used to make an electrical connection, typically in electronic devices or circuits

Examples

A lead wire
Be careful not to let the lead wires touch each other during testing.
She attached a new lead to the electronic component to complete the connection.
The technician replaced the broken lead to restore the circuit's functionality.
