take the cure

verb
undergo medical treatment or remedy
3.9
Pronunciation: / teɪk ðə kjʊr / (AmE), / teɪk ðə kjʊə / (BrE)
to undergo a course of medical treatment, especially as a remedy for illness or addiction

Examples

To take the cure for illness
After years of struggling with his addiction, he finally decided to take the cure.
Many people came to the spa town specifically to take the cure.
She persuaded her brother to take the cure for his drinking problem.
