semiconsciously

adverb
partly aware
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈkɑnʃəsli / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈkɒnʃəsli / (BrE)
in a state that is partly but not fully aware or awake

Examples

To act semiconsciously
He responded semiconsciously to the nurse's questions after waking from surgery.
She walked semiconsciously to the kitchen, still half asleep.
The child semiconsciously mumbled a reply before drifting back to sleep.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: consciously - with full awareness or wakefulness
  • degree lower: unconsciously - without being aware or awake
