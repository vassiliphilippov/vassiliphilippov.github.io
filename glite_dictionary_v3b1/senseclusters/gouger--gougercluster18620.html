gouger

noun
a person who gouges (esp. the eye)
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaʊdʒər / (AmE), / ˈɡaʊdʒə / (BrE)
a person who attacks by gouging, especially one who gouges out another's eye

Examples

A violent gouger
In old wrestling matches, a gouger was considered the most dangerous competitor.
Stories of the brutal gouger spread quickly among the townspeople.
The infamous boxer was labeled a gouger after he was caught attacking his opponents' eyes.
