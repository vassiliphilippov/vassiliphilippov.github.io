dot

noun
short signal in Morse code
Pronunciation: / dɑt / (AmE), / dɒt / (BrE)
the shorter of the two signals used in Morse code, represented by a short sound or mark

Examples

Morse code dot
Each letter in Morse code consists of a unique sequence of dots and dashes.
She practiced her dot and dash patterns to improve her Morse code skills.
To send the letter 'E', you only need a single dot.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dash - the longer of the two Morse code signals
