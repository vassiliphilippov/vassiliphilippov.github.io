revenue stream

noun
source of regular income
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛvəˌnu strim / (AmE), / ˈrɛvɪˌnjuː striːm / (BrE)
a regular source of income, especially for a business or organization

Examples

Multiple revenue streams
Diversifying your revenue streams can help protect your business from market volatility.
Many startups struggle to identify their main revenue stream in the early stages.
The company developed a new revenue stream by launching an online subscription service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: income - money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or investments
  • synonym: profit - financial gain, especially the difference between amount earned and spent
