resistant

adjective
not affected or harmed by something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
Not affected or harmed by something; able to withstand the effects or influence of something.

Examples

Resistant to change
She remained resistant to all their arguments for change.
The bacteria are increasingly resistant to antibiotics, making them harder to treat.
This material is highly resistant to water and stains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: susceptible - likely or liable to be influenced or harmed by something
