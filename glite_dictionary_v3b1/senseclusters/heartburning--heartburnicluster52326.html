heartburning

noun
intense resentment or jealousy
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrtˌbɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
Intense resentment or jealousy, especially felt toward someone's success or good fortune.

Examples

Strong heartburning feelings
His promotion caused much heartburning among former colleagues.
The decision to award her the prize led to considerable heartburning in the department.
There was evident heartburning when the inheritance was divided among the siblings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jealousy - a feeling of envy toward someone's achievements or advantages
  • synonym: resentment - a feeling of anger or displeasure about someone or something unfair
