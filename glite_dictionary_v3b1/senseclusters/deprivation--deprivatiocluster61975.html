Synonyms

  • privation
    9.3 0.71/m
    privation means severe lack of basic things like food or warmth, while deprivation is the general state of missing necessary needs.
  • lack
    1.3 122/m
    lack describes not having something needed in general, while deprivation focuses on not having basic needs like food or shelter.
  • want
    4.3 2.11/m
    want means a need or shortage often used in phrases about poverty, while deprivation highlights lack of basic life necessities.
  • insufficiency
    6.5 0.36/m
    insufficiency means not having enough of something, often quantity or skill, while deprivation is about missing essential basic needs.
