deprivation

noun
the state of lacking necessities
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛprəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛprɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the state or process of not having something important or necessary, especially basic needs like food, shelter, or social contact

Examples

The deprivation of sleep over many nights can seriously impact your health.

Many children in the region suffer from nutritional deprivation.
Prolonged deprivation of sleep can seriously impact your health.
The citizens protested against the deprivation of their civil rights.
He grew up in an environment of poverty and deprivation.
Sleep deprivation can have negative effects on health and mood.
The long winter caused serious deprivation for many families.
