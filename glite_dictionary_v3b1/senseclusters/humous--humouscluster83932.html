humous

noun
Middle Eastern chickpea spread
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌməs / (AmE), / ˈhʌməs / (BrE)
a thick, creamy spread made from mashed chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic, often used as a dip or spread, originating from the Middle East

Examples

A bowl of humous
Her favorite snack is carrot sticks dipped in humous.
The restaurant makes its own humous with extra lemon and garlic.
We ordered a plate of humous and warm pita bread to start the meal.
