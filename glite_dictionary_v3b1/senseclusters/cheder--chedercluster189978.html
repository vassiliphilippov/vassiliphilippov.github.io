cheder

noun
small room or chamber
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪdər / (AmE), / ˈheɪdə / (BrE)
a room or small chamber, especially in a house, used for storage or other purposes

Examples

A storage cheder
They stored the old trunks in the cheder at the back of the flat.
The cheder was filled with boxes and forgotten furniture.
She found a quiet cheder to read in away from the busy kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chamber - a private room or small space in a house
  • antonym: hall - a large room or passage in a building
