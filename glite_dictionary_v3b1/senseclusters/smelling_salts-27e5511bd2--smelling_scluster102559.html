smelling salts

noun
aromatic stimulant to revive faintness
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɛlɪŋ sɔlts / (AmE), / ˈsmɛlɪŋ sɒlts / (BrE)
A pungent preparation containing ammonium carbonate and aromatic substances, inhaled to revive a person who has fainted or feels faint.

Examples

Smelling salts inhaled
Old-fashioned remedies like smelling salts are rarely seen in modern hospitals today.
She waved smelling salts under his nose, and he regained consciousness almost instantly.
The nurse quickly fetched the smelling salts to revive the fainted spectator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ammonia - a colorless gas with a pungent smell, used in smelling salts
