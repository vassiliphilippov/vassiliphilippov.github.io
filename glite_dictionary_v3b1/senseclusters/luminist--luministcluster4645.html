luminist

noun
artist focused on light
Pronunciation: / ˈluːmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈluːmɪnɪst / (BrE)
an artist who paints in a style that emphasizes the effects of light, especially in landscapes (luminism)

Examples

A luminist artist
The luminist captured the soft glow of sunset in every painting he created.
Many art historians admire the delicate use of light by a luminist in landscape scenes.
As a luminist, she focused on the changing effects of sunlight on water and sky.
The museum's new exhibition features several works by a renowned luminist from the Hudson River School.
As a luminist, she was fascinated by the way sunlight played across the landscape.
Art historians often study how a luminist captures subtle changes in natural light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impressionist - an artist who paints in the Impressionist style
  • antonym: realist - an artist who paints in the Realist style
