NIMS

proper noun
Indian medical institute
Pronunciation: / nɪmz / (AmE), / nɪmz / (BrE)
an abbreviation for several medical universities or teaching hospitals in India, such as Nizam's Institute of Medical Sciences and National Institute of Medical Sciences
