head lice

noun
parasitic insects infesting the scalp
3.0
Pronunciation: / hɛd laɪs / (AmE), / hɛd laɪs / (BrE)
Small parasitic insects that infest the hair and scalp, causing itching and irritation.

Examples

Head lice infestation
My daughter came home from school with head lice, and we had to treat her hair immediately.
Sharing hats or combs can easily spread head lice among children.
The nurse checked each student for head lice during the routine health inspection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: body lice - parasitic insects that infest clothing and body hair
  • synonym: nits - eggs of lice, often found in hair
