unhip

adjective
not trendy
7.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɪp / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɪp / (BrE)
not fashionable, trendy, or aware of current styles or popular culture; considered uncool or out of touch

Examples

An unhip style
He felt unhip when his friends talked about the latest music he had never heard of.
Wearing old-fashioned clothes made her seem unhip at the trendy party.
Some people proudly remain unhip and ignore passing fads.
Wearing socks with sandals is often seen as unhip among teenagers.
He worried that his taste in music made him seem unhip to his friends.
Despite being unhip, she was confident in her own unique style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outdated - no longer in fashion or current
  • synonym: uncool - not fashionable or popular
