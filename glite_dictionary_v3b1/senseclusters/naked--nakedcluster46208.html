naked

adjective
without protection or cover
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkəd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
having no protecting or concealing cover; exposed to harm or danger.

Examples

Naked to danger
The crops were naked to the harsh winter winds after the trees were cut down.
The island stood naked to the sea, with no cliffs or trees for shelter.
Without her armor, she felt naked before her opponents on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exposed - not protected or covered; vulnerable
  • antonym: protected - having protection or cover
