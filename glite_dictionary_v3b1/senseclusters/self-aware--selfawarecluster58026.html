self-aware

adjective
applied to machines: able to monitor and adjust itself

(in computing and AI) of a machine, program, or system: able to represent or monitor its own internal state and adjust its behaviour accordingly.

Examples

Self-aware robot
The updated virtual assistant seemed unexpectedly self-aware, apologizing when it misunderstood simple commands.
Engineers tested whether the autonomous car could adjust its route by being self-aware of sensor faults.
After the update, the monitoring software became more self-aware, rebooting services when anomalies appeared.
