world traveler

noun
someone who travels to many countries
2.9
Pronunciation: / wɜrld ˈtrævələr / (AmE), / wɜːld ˈtrævələ / (BrE)
a person who travels widely, especially to many countries around the world

Examples

A world traveler visits countries
As a world traveler, she has visited over fifty countries on six continents.
He dreams of becoming a world traveler and exploring new cultures every year.
Many world travelers keep blogs to share their adventures with friends and family.

Related Terms
