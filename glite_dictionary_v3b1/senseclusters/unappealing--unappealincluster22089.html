unappealing

adjective
not attractive or interesting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈpil.ɪŋ /; / ˌʌn.əˈpi.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈpiː.lɪŋ / (BrE)
not attractive, interesting, or pleasant

Examples

Unappealing food
He found the dish visually unappealing and decided not to try it.
The advertisement was so unappealing that it failed to attract any customers.
The movie was filled with unappealing characters that the audience struggled to care about.
His suggestion was so unappealing that nobody wanted to try it.
She found the color of the room unappealing and decided to repaint it.
The food looked unappealing after sitting out for hours.
