lory

noun
small Australasian parrot
9.5 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààl…îÀêri / (AmE), / Ààl…íri / (BrE)
A small, brightly colored parrot native to Australasia with a brush-tipped tongue for feeding on nectar and soft fruits.

Examples
