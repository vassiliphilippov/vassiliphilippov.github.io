coding

noun
writing computer programs
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of writing instructions for computers using programming languages

Examples

Computer coding
She spends her evenings coding new apps for her friends to use.
Many schools now offer classes in coding to help students learn computer skills.
He found a job that lets him do coding from home every day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decoding - the process of converting coded information back to its original form
  • synonym: programming - the act of creating computer software by writing code
