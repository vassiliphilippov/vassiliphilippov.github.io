echoic

adjective
like or characteristic of an echo
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɛˈkoʊɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈkəʊɪk / (BrE)
having the quality of or relating to an echo; resembling an echo in sound or effect.

Examples

Echoic voice
Her voice sounded echoic as it bounced off the stone walls.
The canyon’s echoic environment amplified every sound we made.
The large, empty hall gave the music an echoic quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: echo - a sound or series of sounds caused by the reflection of sound waves
