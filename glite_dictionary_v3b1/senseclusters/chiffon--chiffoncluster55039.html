chiffon

noun
light, sheer fabric
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈfɑn / (AmE), / ʃɪˈfɒn / (BrE)
A very light, sheer fabric made from silk, rayon, or other fibers, often used for dresses and scarves.

Examples

A chiffon dress
A pale pink chiffon scarf added elegance to her outfit.
Her wedding veil was made of delicate chiffon that floated in the breeze.
The designer chose chiffon for its lightweight and flowing texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: organza - a thin, plain weave, sheer fabric
  • antonym: satin - smooth, glossy fabric, usually silk
