misplacement

noun
placing something incorrectly
1.8
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / mɪsˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
The act or result of placing something in the wrong position or location.

Examples

Frequent misplacement of documents
Frequent misplacement of documents may indicate poor organizational skills.
Surgical errors can sometimes be traced back to the misplacement of equipment.
The misplacement of my keys caused me to be late for the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: displacement - the moving of something from its place or position
  • antonym: placement - the act of putting something in a particular place
