adjudicatory

adjective
relating to formal judgment or adjudication
9.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàd íuÀê.d…™.k…ôÀåt…îr.i / (AmE), / …ôÀàd íuÀê.d…™.k…ôÀåt…îÀê.ri / (BrE)
relating to the process of adjudicating or making a formal judgment or decision about a problem or disputed matter, especially in a legal context

Examples

An adjudicatory body
