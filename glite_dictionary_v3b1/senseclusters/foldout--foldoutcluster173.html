foldout

noun
an oversized page folded into a book or magazine
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊld.aʊt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊld.aʊt / (BrE)
an oversize page in a book or magazine that is folded and can be opened out for viewing

Examples

A foldout map
He carefully opened the foldout map to plan their route.
The art book includes a beautiful foldout of the artist's largest painting.
This magazine issue features a spectacular foldout poster of the winning team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insert - a slip or page added into a publication
