resynchronization

noun
restoring synchronization
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌsɪŋkrənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌsɪŋkrənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making things operate in the same timing or schedule again after they have become unsynchronized

Examples

System resynchronization
The engineer performed a resynchronization of the system clocks to fix the timing issue.
After the power outage, a resynchronization of all devices was necessary to restore normal operations.
The software update included an automatic resynchronization feature to keep data consistent across servers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desynchronization - the process of causing things to lose synchronization
  • synonym: synchronization - the process of causing things to happen at the same time
