outgrow one's fear

verb
stop being affected by a fear as one matures
1.0
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈɡroʊ wʌnz fɪr / (AmE), / aʊtˈɡrəʊ wʌnz fɪə / (BrE)
to become mature enough no longer to be affected by a particular fear or anxiety

Examples

To outgrow one's fear of the dark
As she got older, she managed to outgrow her fear of the dark.
Many children eventually outgrow their fear of speaking in front of a class.
With therapy, Jack was able to outgrow his fear of dogs.
