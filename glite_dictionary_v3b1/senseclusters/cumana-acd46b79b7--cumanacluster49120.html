Cumana

proper noun
port city in northeastern Venezuela
Pronunciation: / kuːˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / kjuːˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
A port city in northeastern Venezuela on the Caribbean Sea, recognized as the oldest European settlement in South America.
