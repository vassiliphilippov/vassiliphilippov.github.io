gunfight

noun
a fight involving guns
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnfaɪt / (BrE)
a fight between people using guns, typically over a short duration

Examples

There was a gunfight outside the bank that left several people injured.
