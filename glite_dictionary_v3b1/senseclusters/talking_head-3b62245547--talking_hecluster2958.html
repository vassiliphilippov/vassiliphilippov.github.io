talking head

noun
person who speaks directly to camera on TV
8.9 4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔkɪŋ hɛd /; / ˈtɔkɪŋ ˈhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ hɛd /; / ˈtɔːkɪŋ ˈhɛd / (BrE)
