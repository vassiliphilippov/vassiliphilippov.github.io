talking head

noun
person who speaks directly to camera on TV
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔkɪŋ hɛd /; / ˈtɔkɪŋ ˈhɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɔːkɪŋ hɛd /; / ˈtɔːkɪŋ ˈhɛd / (BrE)
a person, especially on television or video, who appears onscreen speaking directly to the audience, often giving opinions or commentary
