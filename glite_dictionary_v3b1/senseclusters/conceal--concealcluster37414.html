conceal

verb
hide; keep secret
3.7 20/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàsil /; / k…ônÀàsiÀêl / (AmE), / k…ônÀàsiÀêl / (BrE)
to hide something or keep it secret so that it cannot be seen or discovered by others

Examples

To conceal identity
He wore sunglasses to conceal his identity in public.
She managed to conceal her nervousness during the interview.
The suspect tried to conceal the evidence under the floorboards.
She tried to conceal her disappointment when she heard the news.
The magician managed to conceal the coin in his hand during the performance.
The thief concealed the stolen jewelry under the floorboards.
