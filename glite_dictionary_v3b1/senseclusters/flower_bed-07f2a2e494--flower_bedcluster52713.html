flower bed

noun
area for growing flowers
3.6 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊər bɛd /; / ˈflaʊ.ɚ bɛd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə bɛd /; / ˈflaʊ.ə bɛd / (BrE)
an area or piece of ground in a garden or park where flowers are planted and grown
