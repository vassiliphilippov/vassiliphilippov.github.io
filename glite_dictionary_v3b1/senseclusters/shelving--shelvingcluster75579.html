shelving

noun
storage shelves
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛlvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛlvɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Metal shelving
We installed new shelving in the kitchen to hold all the dishes and spices.
The store offers a wide variety of shelving for organizing books and files.
Metal shelving is often used in warehouses for storing heavy equipment.
