historical site

noun
site of historical importance
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔrɪkəl saɪt / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪkəl saɪt / (BrE)
a place that is important because of its history or has historical significance

Examples

An ancient historical site
Many tourists come to view the ancient temple, which is now a historical site.
Our school took a field trip to a historical site last spring.
The city council voted to preserve the historical site as a public park.
