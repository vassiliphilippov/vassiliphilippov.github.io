obsessor

noun
tormenting spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / əbˈsɛsər / (AmE), / əbˈsɛsə / (BrE)
an evil spirit or demon believed to harass, torment, or possess a person, especially in spiritual or religious contexts

Examples

An evil obsessor
The priest believed an obsessor was responsible for the strange behavior in the house.
Some spiritual traditions describe the obsessor as a force that feeds on negative emotions.
During the ritual, they tried to drive the obsessor away from the afflicted person.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - a benevolent spiritual being
  • synonym: demon - an evil supernatural being or spirit
  • synonym: spirit - a supernatural being, often invisible
