unmodeled

adjective
not worn in display
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɑːdəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɒdəld / (BrE)
not shown being worn or used by a person or mannequin, especially in photos or displays

Examples

Unmodeled dress photos
The catalog featured several unmodeled dresses laid flat on a white background.
Many online shops sell unmodeled accessories, making it hard to judge their size.
Customers sometimes prefer seeing clothes unmodeled to focus on the fabric and design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: displayed - shown or presented for viewing
  • antonym: modeled - worn or demonstrated by a person or mannequin
