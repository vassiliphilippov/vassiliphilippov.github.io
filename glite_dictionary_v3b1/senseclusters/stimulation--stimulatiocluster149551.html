stimulation

noun
something that arouses activity or interest
Pronunciation: / ˌstɪmjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌstɪmjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
something that encourages or arouses activity, interest, or enthusiasm.

Examples

Sensory stimulation
Children need a variety of sensory stimulation for proper development.
Daily mental stimulation is important for keeping your brain healthy.
The new project provided the stimulation he needed to stay motivated at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encouragement - the act of giving support or confidence
  • antonym: inhibition - a feeling that makes one self-conscious and unable to act in a relaxed and natural way
