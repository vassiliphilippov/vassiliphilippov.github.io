wild fig

noun
West Indies tree with fig-shaped fruit
3.7
Pronunciation: / waɪld fɪɡ / (AmE), / waɪld fɪɡ / (BrE)
a type of tropical tree found in the West Indies and Florida, known as 'wild fig'
