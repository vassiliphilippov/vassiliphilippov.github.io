soothing

adjective
giving relief from pain or discomfort
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːðɪŋ /; / ˈsuðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsuːðɪŋ / (BrE)
making pain, discomfort, or anxiety less severe; calming or giving relief

Examples

A soothing balm
He applied a soothing balm to relieve his sore muscles.
The soothing lotion quickly eased the irritation from the insect bite.
This cream has a soothing effect on sunburned skin.
He spoke in a soothing voice to calm the frightened child.
The soothing aroma of lavender filled the room, relieving everyone's anxiety.
The soothing music helped ease her nerves before the exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitating - causing anxiety or stirring up emotion
  • synonym: calming - making someone less anxious or upset
  • antonym: irritating - causing irritation or discomfort
