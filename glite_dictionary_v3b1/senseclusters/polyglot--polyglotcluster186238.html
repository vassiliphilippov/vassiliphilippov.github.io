polyglot

noun
person who speaks several languages
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːliˌɡlɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɒliˌɡlɒt / (BrE)

Examples

Maria is a true polyglot, fluent in Spanish, English, French, and Japanese.
The conference featured a polyglot who could interpret for nearly everyone present.
