light appetizer

noun
small, non-heavy starter before a meal
1.4
Pronunciation: / laɪt ˌæpəˈtaɪzər / (AmE), / laɪt ˈapɪˌtaɪzə / (BrE)
a small serving of food, typically not heavy, served before the main meal to stimulate the appetite

Examples

A light appetizer serving
A bowl of soup makes a perfect light appetizer on a cool evening.
At the party, guests enjoyed a light appetizer before the main courses were served.
We started our dinner with a light appetizer of cucumber salad.
