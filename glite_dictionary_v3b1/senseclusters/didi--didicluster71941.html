didi

noun
older sister term
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːdi / (AmE), / ˈdiːdi / (BrE)
a respectful term for an older sister or elder woman, especially used in South Asian English

Examples

My didi helps me
Whenever I need advice, I talk to my didi.
In our neighborhood, everyone calls the friendly shopkeeper didi out of respect.
My didi helped me with my homework when I was younger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auntie - a respectful term for an older woman
  • antonym: bhai - a respectful term for an older brother in South Asian English
  • synonym: sister - a female sibling
