blub

verb
cry noisily with snuffling
9.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / blÊŒb / (AmE), / blÊŒb / (BrE)
to cry noisily and in a snuffling manner; to weep uncontrollably, often with gasping or sniveling sounds

Examples

To blub loudly
She started to blub uncontrollably after reading the sad letter.
Stop blubbing and try to explain what happened.
The child blubbed all the way home after losing his toy.

Synonyms

  • blubber
    6.1 0.62/m
    blubber is to cry loudly and uncontrollably, sometimes speaking through tears, while blub adds snuffling, gasping, and sniveling sounds.
  • sob
    1.0 1.46/m
    sob is crying with sudden, sharp breaths from strong feeling, while blub often includes snuffling, gasping and sniveling sounds.
  • snivel
    7.4 0.35/m
    snivel is to cry or complain in a tearful, whining way with sniffing, while blub is louder and more uncontrolled with snuffling.
  • boohoo
    7.4 0.02/m
    boohoo is loud, often childish crying that sounds like boohoo, while blub is noisy but more snuffling and gasping.
  • bawl
    5.9 1.98/m
    bawl is to cry or shout very loudly without control, while blub is tearful with snuffling, gasping and sniveling sounds.
