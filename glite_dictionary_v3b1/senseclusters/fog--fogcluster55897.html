fog

noun
mental confusion; lack of clarity
1.9 4.8
Pronunciation: /fɑːg/ /; / /fɔːg/ (AmE), /fɒg/ (BrE)
A state of mental confusion or lack of clarity that makes it difficult to think or understand clearly.

Examples

Mind in a fog
After the accident, she was in a fog and couldn't remember what happened.
The medication left him in a mental fog for several hours.
Trying to work through my fog of exhaustion, I struggled to concentrate on the task.
