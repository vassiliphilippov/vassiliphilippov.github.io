Samaritan

noun
kind helper
Pronunciation: / səˈmærɪtən / (AmE), / səˈmærɪtən / (BrE)
a person who helps others in need, especially out of kindness or compassion

Examples

A kind Samaritan
A Samaritan stopped to help the stranded motorist on the side of the road.
She acted as a true Samaritan by bringing food to her sick neighbor.
The city honored a local Samaritan for his selfless acts of kindness.
A Samaritan offered his umbrella to the woman caught in the rain.
The lost child was comforted by a kind Samaritan until her parents arrived.
When his car broke down, a passing Samaritan stopped to help him fix it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruist - a person who cares about the welfare of others
  • synonym: benefactor - a person who gives help or support, especially financial
  • antonym: bystander - a person who is present but does not take part or help
