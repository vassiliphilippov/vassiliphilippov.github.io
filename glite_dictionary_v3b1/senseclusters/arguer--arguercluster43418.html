arguer

noun
person who argues or debates
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrɡjuər / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡjuə / (BrE)
a person who engages in argument or debate, often putting forward opinions or reasoning

Examples

A skilled arguer
As a skilled arguer, she often persuaded others to change their minds.
Even in friendly discussions, he could not resist acting as the arguer in the group.
The judge grew impatient with the persistent arguer disrupting the proceedings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debater - someone who takes part in formal debates
  • synonym: opponent - someone who opposes or argues against another
  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who tries to avoid or resolve arguments
