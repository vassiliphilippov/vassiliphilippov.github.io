Examples

To hire a car
It is common to hire extra equipment for big events rather than buying it.
We decided to hire a car for our holiday in Spain.
You can hire bicycles from the shop next to the station.
They are hiring a boat for the afternoon to explore the nearby islands.

Synonyms

  • rent
    1.8 16/m
    rent is paying money to use property or equipment under a contract, while hire also covers paying for people's services or short-term use.
  • lease
    3.3 1.84/m
    lease is a formal contract to use property for longer terms, while hire usually means short-term payment for use or services.

Related Terms
