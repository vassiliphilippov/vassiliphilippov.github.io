begum

noun
Muslim woman of high rank (India/Pakistan)
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪɡəm / (AmE), / ˈbiːɡəm / (BrE)
a Muslim woman of high rank or status in India or Pakistan, often used as a title of respect

Examples

The begum of India
Many stories have been told about the generosity of the late begum.
The begum attended the grand ceremony in a richly embroidered sari.
The begum played a significant role in advancing education for women in her region.
