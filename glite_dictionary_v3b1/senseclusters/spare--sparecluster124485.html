spare

verb
refrain from harming
5.0
Pronunciation: / spɛr / (AmE), / spɛə / (BrE)
to refrain from harming, injuring, or killing someone or something, especially when one has the power or opportunity to do so

Examples

To spare a life
Although angry, he managed to spare his rival in the final confrontation.
She begged the hunter to spare the injured animal.
The king decided to spare the prisoner despite his crimes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harm - to physically hurt or damage someone or something
  • synonym: save - to keep someone or something safe from harm or danger
