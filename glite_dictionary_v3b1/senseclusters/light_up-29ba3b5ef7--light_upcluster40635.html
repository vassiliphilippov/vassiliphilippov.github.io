light up

verb
become lively or cheerful
1.0
Pronunciation: / laɪt ʌp / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp / (BrE)
(of a place or situation) to become lively, cheerful, or full of positive energy, often due to the presence or actions of someone.

Examples

His jokes light up the party
His jokes made the room light up and everyone started to relax.
Whenever she enters, the entire room lights up with laughter and joy.
You could feel the room light up the moment the band started playing.
