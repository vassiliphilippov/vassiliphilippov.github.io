scrape by

verb
manage to survive with difficulty
7.3
Pronunciation: / skreɪp baɪ / (AmE), / skreɪp baɪ / (BrE)
to manage to survive or make a living with very little money or resources, often just barely

Examples

To scrape by on savings
Despite losing his job, he managed to scrape by on his savings for a few months.
Many college students scrape by on part-time jobs and cheap meals.
She had to scrape by after the divorce, doing whatever work she could find.

Related Terms
