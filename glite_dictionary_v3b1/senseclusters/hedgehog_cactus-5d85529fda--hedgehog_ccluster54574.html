hedgehog cactus

noun
a spiny cactus of the genus Echinocactus
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdʒˌhɑɡ ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʒˌhɒɡ ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
a cactus of the genus Echinocactus, characterized by stout, sharp spines often resembling the spines of a hedgehog
