meat thermometer

noun
cooking device for measuring meat temperature
Pronunciation: / mit θɚˈmɑmɪtɚ / (AmE), / miːt θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a thermometer designed to be inserted into meat to measure its internal temperature while cooking

Examples

A meat thermometer device
A digital meat thermometer can help you avoid undercooked chicken.
He checked the roast with a meat thermometer to make sure it was cooked properly.
She inserted a meat thermometer into the turkey before putting it in the oven.
