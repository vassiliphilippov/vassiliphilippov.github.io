bangs

noun
hair cut over forehead
Pronunciation: / bæŋz / (AmE), / bæŋz / (BrE)
a hairstyle in which the front part of the hair is cut straight across or over the forehead

Examples

Short bangs haircut
She decided to cut bangs for a new look before starting college.
His bangs kept falling into his eyes during the windy day.
Many celebrities have chosen bangs to change their appearance this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fringe - hair cut and styled to hang over the forehead
