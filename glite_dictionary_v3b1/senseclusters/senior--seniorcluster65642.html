senior

noun
final-year student (US)
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːniər / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə / (BrE)
A student in their final year of high school or college, especially in the United States.

Examples

A senior student
As a senior, she was preparing for graduation and applying to graduate schools.
Every senior is required to complete a final project before receiving their diploma.
The senior class planned a special trip to celebrate the end of the academic year.
