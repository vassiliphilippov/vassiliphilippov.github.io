take fire

verb
start to burn; burst into flames
7.2
Pronunciation: / teɪk ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / teɪk ˈfaɪə / (BrE)

Examples

To take fire quickly

The pile of dry leaves took fire after a spark landed on them.
