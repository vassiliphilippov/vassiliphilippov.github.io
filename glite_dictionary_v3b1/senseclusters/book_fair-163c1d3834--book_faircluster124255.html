book fair

noun
event for displaying and selling books
Pronunciation: / bʊk fɛr / (AmE), / bʊk feə / (BrE)
an event where books are displayed and sold, often to promote reading, literature, or raise funds

Examples

Local book fair
Many rare and discounted titles were available at the local book fair last weekend.
Publishers from around the world attended the annual book fair in Frankfurt.
The school hosted a book fair to encourage children to read more.
Many publishers debut new titles at the annual book fair in Frankfurt.
Our school held a book fair last week to encourage students to read more.
She volunteered at the local book fair to help raise money for the community library.

Related Terms

  • synonym: book sale - an event where books are sold, often second-hand or to raise funds
