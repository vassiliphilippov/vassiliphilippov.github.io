scleral veins

noun
veins draining the sclera of the eye
6.2 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsklɛrəl veɪnz / (AmE), / ˈsklɪərəl veɪnz / (BrE)
the small veins in the eye that drain blood from the sclera into the anterior ciliary veins

Examples

Scleral veins drain blood
Blockage in the scleral veins can affect blood drainage from the white part of the eye.
In certain eye diseases, the scleral veins may become more prominent and visible.
The ophthalmologist examined the scleral veins for any signs of inflammation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anterior ciliary vein - a vein that drains blood from the eye and receives the scleral veins
