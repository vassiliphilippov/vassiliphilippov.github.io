oligarchical

adjective
relating to an oligarchy
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: /ə.lɪ.ˈgɑːr.kɪ.kəl/ /; / /ɑː.lɪ.ˈgɑːr.kɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ɒ.lɪ.ˈgɑː.kɪ.kəl/ /; / /ə.lɪ.ˈgɑː.kɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
Relating to, supporting, or characteristic of an oligarchy—a form of government or organization in which power rests with a small number of people.

Examples

Oligarchical government system
He criticized the oligarchical structure of the organization, arguing it lacked representation.
Many citizens protested against the oligarchical policies favoring only a small group of wealthy families.
The country was governed by an oligarchical elite who controlled all major decisions.
