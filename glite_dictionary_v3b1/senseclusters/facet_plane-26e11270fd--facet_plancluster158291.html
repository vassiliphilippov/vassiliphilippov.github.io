facet plane

noun
plane forming a face of a geometric object
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsɪt pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪt pleɪn / (BrE)
the flat surface or plane that forms one of the faces of a geometric object, such as a cube or crystal

Examples

A facet plane of cube
In geometry, a cube has six facet planes, each corresponding to one of its faces.
Scientists studied the molecular arrangement along the facet plane of the crystal.
The diamond's brilliance is enhanced by the precise angles of each facet plane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: face - a flat surface that forms part of the outside of an object
