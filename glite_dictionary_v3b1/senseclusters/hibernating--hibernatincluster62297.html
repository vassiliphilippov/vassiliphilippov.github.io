hibernating

adjective
in a state of biological rest
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪbəˌneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal or plant) in a state of biological rest or suspended animation, typically during the winter.

Examples

Hibernating animals
Scientists studied the metabolic changes in hibernating squirrels during the winter.
The hibernating bear lay undisturbed in its den throughout the cold months.
The frog remained hibernating at the bottom of the pond until temperatures rose.

Related Terms

  • antonym: active - currently engaged in activity or movement
  • synonym: dormant - in a state of rest or inactivity
