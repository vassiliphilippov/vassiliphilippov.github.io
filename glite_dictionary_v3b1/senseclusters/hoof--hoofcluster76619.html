hoof

noun
hard covering on animal's foot
Pronunciation: / hʊf, huf /; / hʊf / (AmE), / hʊf, huːf /; / huːf / (BrE)
the hard, tough covering at the end of the foot of animals like horses, cows, or deer

Examples

A horse's hoof
After a long journey, the cow's hooves needed to be trimmed by the farmer.
The horse's hoof struck the ground with a resounding thud.
You could see the imprint of the deer’s hoof in the soft earth.
A sharp stone became lodged in the cow's hoof, causing it to limp.
Farmers must regularly trim the hooves of their livestock to keep them healthy.
The horse lifted its hoof and stamped on the ground impatiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: claw - a curved, pointed horny nail on the foot of an animal
  • synonym: paw - the foot of an animal having claws
