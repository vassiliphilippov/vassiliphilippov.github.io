hoof

noun
hard covering on animal's foot
6.0 1.3
Pronunciation: /huːf/ /; / /hʊf/ (AmE), /huːf/ /; / /hʊf/ (BrE)
the hard, tough covering at the end of the foot of animals like horses, cows, or deer

Examples

A horse's hoof

Farmers must regularly trim the hooves of their livestock to keep them healthy.
The horse lifted its hoof and stamped on the ground impatiently.
