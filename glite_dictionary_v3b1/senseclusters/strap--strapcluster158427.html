strap

verb
to fasten or tie with a strap
3.1 2.5
Pronunciation: /stræp/ (AmE), /stræp/ (BrE)
to fasten or secure something with a strap (including supporting a body part or holding something in place)

Examples

To strap a bag
Please strap the child into the car seat properly.
She carefully strapped her suitcase closed before leaving the hotel.
The workers strapped the crates onto the truck to keep them from falling off.
Before running, she made sure to strap her knee properly to prevent further injury.
He carefully strapped his wrist after he twisted it during basketball practice.
The nurse will strap your ankle to keep it stable while it heals.
