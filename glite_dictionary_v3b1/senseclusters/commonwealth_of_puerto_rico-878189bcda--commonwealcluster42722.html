Commonwealth of Puerto Rico

proper noun
self-governing U.S. territory in the Caribbean
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmənˌwɛlθ əv ˌpwɛrtoʊ ˈriːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmənˌwɛlθ əv ˌpwɛətəʊ ˈriːkəʊ / (BrE)
A self-governing territory associated with the United States, occupying the island of Puerto Rico.
