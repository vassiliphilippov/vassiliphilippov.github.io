evenfall

noun
the period just after sunset
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈivənˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈiːvənˌfɔːl / (BrE)
the time of day immediately following sunset; twilight or dusk.

Examples

At evenfall time
As evenfall approached, the city lights began to twinkle in the distance.
Birds grew silent at evenfall, marking the end of a busy day.
We walked through the quiet fields at evenfall, enjoying the cool evening air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dusk - the darker stage of twilight after sunset
  • synonym: twilight - the soft glowing light from the sky when the sun is below the horizon
