evenfall

noun
the period just after sunset

Pronunciation: / Ààiv…ônÀåf…îl / (AmE), / ÀàiÀêv…ônÀåf…îÀêl / (BrE)
the time of day immediately following sunset; twilight or dusk.

Examples
