offsite

noun
meeting away from office
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a meeting or event held away from the usual workplace or main site, often for planning or team-building purposes

Examples

A team offsite
The team attended an offsite to brainstorm new ideas for the upcoming project.
Our annual offsite helps employees connect outside the usual office environment.
Managers organized a two-day offsite to focus on long-term strategy and team building.

Related Terms

  • antonym: onsite - taking place at the main location or workplace
  • synonym: retreat - a period of group withdrawal for planning or reflection, often away from the workplace
