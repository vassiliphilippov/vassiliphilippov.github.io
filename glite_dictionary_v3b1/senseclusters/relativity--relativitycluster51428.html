Synonyms

  • theory of relativity
    6.7 0.8/m
    theory of relativity is the formal name used in physics texts and classrooms, while relativity is the shorter general term used in speech and writing.
  • relativity theory
    6.5 0.1/m
    relativity theory appears in scientific writing as a formal phrase, while relativity is the common single-word label people use.
  • Einstein's theory of relativity
    6.8 0.1/m
    Einstein's theory of relativity names Einstein as the founder, while relativity is the general theory name without the author's emphasis.
