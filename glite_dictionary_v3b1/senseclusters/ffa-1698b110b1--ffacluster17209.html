FFA

noun
uncontrolled competition
Pronunciation: / ˌɛf ɛf ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛf ɛf ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for free-for-all, meaning a situation or contest where everyone competes or fights without rules or control

Examples

FFA match
The online game mode was a chaotic FFA where every player fought for themselves.
After the referee left, the match turned into a complete FFA with no rules enforced.
In the debate, the discussion quickly became an FFA as everyone started talking at once.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competition - an event or situation in which people compete
  • synonym: free-for-all - a situation where everyone competes or fights without rules
  • antonym: order - a situation where rules or control are present
