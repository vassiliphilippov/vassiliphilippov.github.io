Synonyms

  • on the phone
    1.0 31/m
    on the phone simply says someone is talking on a telephone now, while on the horn is more informal and slightly old-fashioned.
  • make a call
    1.0 1.85/m
    make a call means you place or start a telephone call, while on the horn describes being in a phone conversation.
  • call up
    2.5 5.15/m
    call up means to telephone someone directly to reach them, while on the horn means you are currently talking on the phone.
  • ring
    1.0 13/m
    ring means to phone someone, often quickly, while on the horn describes being on a call and speaking with them now.
  • give a ring
    2.2 0.00/m
    give a ring is a casual way to say call someone on the phone, while on the horn focuses on being in that phone conversation.
  • telephone
    1.0 1.03/m
    telephone is a formal or neutral verb for phoning someone, while on the horn is an informal phrase meaning you are on the phone.
