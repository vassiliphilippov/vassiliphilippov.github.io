leg covering

noun
garment covering the leg
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / lɛɡ ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
a garment worn to cover the leg, typically extending from the knee to the ankle

Examples

A leg covering garment
A protective leg covering is recommended for hiking in rough terrain.
She prefers to wear a leg covering during colder months for extra warmth.
Traditional dancers often put on a decorative leg covering as part of their costume.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stocking - close-fitting garment covering the foot and leg
