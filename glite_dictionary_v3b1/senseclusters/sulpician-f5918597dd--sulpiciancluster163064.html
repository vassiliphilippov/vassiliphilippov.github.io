Sulpician

adjective
of Saint-Sulpice
9.9
Pronunciation: / sʌlˈpɪʃən / (AmE), / sʌlˈpɪsɪən / (BrE)
relating to the Society of Saint-Sulpice, its members, institutions, teachings, or traditions

Examples

Sulpician tradition
The Sulpician approach to education emphasizes both academic and spiritual development.
He attended a Sulpician seminary before becoming a parish priest.
Many Sulpician traditions are still observed in the community today.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
