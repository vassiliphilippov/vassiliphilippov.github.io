oxcart

noun
cart drawn by oxen
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌkɑːt / (BrE)
A cart typically pulled by one or more oxen, used for transporting goods or people.

Examples

An oxcart pulled by oxen
In rural areas, you can still see an oxcart rumbling down dusty roads.
The villagers loaded the harvest onto the oxcart for transport to the market.
They rode in an old oxcart through the countryside during the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cart - a vehicle with either two or four wheels that is pulled by an animal
