scrap

noun
a brief fight or struggle
6.7
Pronunciation: / skræp / (AmE), / skræp / (BrE)
a brief fight or struggle, especially a minor or informal one

Examples

A quick scrap
Despite their argument, it was just a small scrap and they made up quickly.
He ended up with a black eye from a scrap at the bar last night.
The boys got into a scrap in the playground after school.
