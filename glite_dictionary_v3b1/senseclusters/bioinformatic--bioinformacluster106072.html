bioinformatic

noun
computational biology specialist
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˌɪnfərˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˌɪnfəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
a person who specializes in using computational methods to analyze and interpret biological data

Examples

A bioinformatic expert
The research team hired a bioinformatic to help analyze the large sets of genetic data.
As a bioinformatic, she developed new algorithms for studying protein structures.
Many pharmaceutical companies now employ at least one bioinformatic to interpret complex biological information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bioinformatician - a specialist in bioinformatics
  • synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms
