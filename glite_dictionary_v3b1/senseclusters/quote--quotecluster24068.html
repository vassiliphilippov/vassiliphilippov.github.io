quote

verb
state the price of something
3.1
Pronunciation: / kwoʊt / (AmE), / kwəʊt / (BrE)
to state the price of something; to give a cost estimate for goods or services

Examples

To quote a price
Can you quote me a rate for a double room per night?
Several contractors quoted us different prices for the renovation.
The dealer will quote a price for the repairs before starting work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estimate - to give an approximate calculation of value or price
  • synonym: price - to assign or state a monetary value to something
