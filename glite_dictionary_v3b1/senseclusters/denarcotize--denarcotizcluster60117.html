denarcotize

verb
to remove narcotics
9.6
Pronunciation: / diˈnɑrkəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / diˈnɑːkətaɪz / (BrE)
to remove narcotic substances from something so that it no longer contains drugs

Examples

To denarcotize shipments
Authorities must denarcotize seized shipments before they can be safely stored in evidence rooms.
The laboratory technician was asked to denarcotize the sample before further analysis.
After the process to denarcotize the medication, it was safe for general use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contaminate - to make something impure by adding harmful substances
  • synonym: purify - to remove unwanted substances from something
