gratingly

adverb
in a harsh or jarring way
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a harsh and unpleasantly jarring manner.

Examples

To laugh gratingly
Her laughter echoed gratingly through the quiet room, making everyone uneasy.
The chair scraped gratingly across the tiled floor, drawing everyone's attention.
The teacher's criticism was delivered gratingly, stinging the student's confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant manner
  • antonym: smoothly - in a smooth and pleasant manner
