appallingly

adverb
to a shocking or horrible extent
4.8
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔlɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈpɔːlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes shock, horror, or dismay; to an extremely bad or unpleasant degree.

Examples

Treated appallingly
She was treated appallingly by her employers and eventually resigned.
The house was appallingly dirty after years of neglect.
The team's performance was appallingly poor throughout the tournament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awfully - very badly or unpleasantly
  • synonym: terribly - to a great or shocking extent
  • antonym: wonderfully - to a remarkable or pleasing extent (opposite sense)
