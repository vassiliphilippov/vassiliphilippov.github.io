appallingly

adverb
to a shocking or horrible extent
4.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpɔː.lɪŋ.liː/ /; / /ə.ˈpɑː.lɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpɔː.lɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that causes shock, horror, or dismay; to an extremely bad or unpleasant degree.

Examples

The house was appallingly dirty after years of neglect.
The team's performance was appallingly poor throughout the tournament.
