attack dog

noun
dog trained to attack on command
2.4
Pronunciation: / əˈtæk dɔg /; / əˈtæk dɔɡ / (AmE), / əˈtæk dɒg /; / əˈtæk dɒɡ / (BrE)
a dog that is specially trained to attack or protect on command, often used for security or protection

Examples
