Synonyms

  • discuss
    1.6 150/m
    discuss is the base verb for talking about a topic often in detail, while discussing describes the ongoing act of talking.
  • talk about
    1.3 600/m
    talk about means to mention or talk on a subject in ordinary speech, while discussing often implies deeper exchange of views.
  • confer
    4.9 2/m
    confer is a formal act of consulting with others to decide, while discussing is general talking to exchange ideas or opinions.
  • talk over
    3.2 3/m
    talk over is to discuss something carefully before deciding, while discussing can mean any exchanging of ideas, not only decision talk.
  • converse
    3.3 1/m
    converse is to talk informally and exchange ideas in a social way, while discussing focuses on talking to examine a topic.
