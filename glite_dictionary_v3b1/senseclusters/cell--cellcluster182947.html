cell

noun
small group forming a movement's core
5.1
Pronunciation: / sɛl / (AmE), / sɛl / (BrE)
a small group within a larger organization, especially one that operates secretly and forms the core or basis of a political movement

Examples

Secret cell
Each cell acted independently to avoid detection by authorities.
Police uncovered a terrorist cell planning an attack.
The revolutionary group operated as a secret cell within the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: network - an interconnected system of groups or people
  • degree higher: organization - a larger structured group with a particular purpose
