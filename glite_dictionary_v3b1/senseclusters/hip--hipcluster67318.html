hip

verb
to carry on hip
Pronunciation: / hɪp / (AmE), / hɪp / (BrE)
to move, push, or carry something or someone using the hip, especially by holding at the side of the body

Examples

To hip a child
She hipped the toddler up onto her side before leaving the room.
He hipped the heavy bag to carry it more comfortably across the yard.
The mother hips her baby while talking on the phone to keep her hands free.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoist - to lift or raise something, especially with effort
  • antonym: shoulder - to carry something on the shoulder
