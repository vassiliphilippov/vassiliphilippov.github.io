cost-prohibitive

adjective
too expensive to afford
7.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /kɑːst prə.ˈhɪb.ɪ.tɪv/ /; / /kɔːst prə.ˈhɪb.ɪ.tɪv/ (AmE), /kɒst prə.ˈhɪb.ɪ.tɪv/ (BrE)
