Marxist-Leninist

adjective
of or relating to Marxist-Leninist ideology
4.1 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːrks.ɪst ˈle.nɪn.ɪst/ /; / /ˈmɑːrks.əst ˈle.nɪn.ɪst/ (AmE), /ˈmɑːks.ɪst ˈle.nɪn.ɪst/ /; / /ˈmɑːks.əst ˈle.nə.nɪst/ (BrE)
relating to or supporting a communist ideology based on the theories of Karl Marx and expanded by Vladimir Lenin, especially as practiced in certain states and parties.

Examples

Marxist-Leninist ideology
Her dissertation analyzes the influence of Marxist-Leninist ideology in twentieth-century literature.
Members of the Marxist-Leninist movement organized a protest outside the government building.
The party adopted a strictly Marxist-Leninist approach in its economic policies.

  • synonym: communist - supporting or relating to communism
