workup

noun
laboratory procedures
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌʌp / (BrE)
a series of laboratory or chemical procedures performed after an experiment to isolate, purify, or prepare a product or sample

Examples

Chemical workup
The chemist performed a workup to separate the desired compound from the reaction mixture.
A complete workup is necessary before the results can be confirmed and published.
After the experiment, the team started the workup to purify the final product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analysis - detailed examination of the elements or structure of something
  • synonym: preparation - the process of making something ready for use
