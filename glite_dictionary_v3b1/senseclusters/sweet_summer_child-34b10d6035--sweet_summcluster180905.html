sweet summer child

noun
innocent, naive, or trusting person
Pronunciation: / swit ˈsʌmɚ tʃaɪld / (AmE), / swiːt ˈsʌmə tʃaɪld / (BrE)
a person who is innocent or naive, often displaying a trusting optimism about the world

Examples

A sweet summer child
As a sweet summer child, she never imagined her friends could betray her trust.
He looked at me, shook his head, and said, "Oh, you sweet summer child."
You really are a sweet summer child if you believe he’ll finish the project on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naive - having or showing a lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment
