necrological

adjective
relating to death
9.8
Pronunciation: /ne.krə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ /; / /nə.krə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ne.krə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl/ /; / /nə.krə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to death, obituaries, or the study and recording of deaths

Examples

necrological report
