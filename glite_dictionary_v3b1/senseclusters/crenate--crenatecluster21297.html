crenate

adjective
having rounded scalloped margins
9.9 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkriː.neɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkriː.neɪt/ (BrE)
(of a leaf or other organ) having a margin with rounded scallops or notches.

Examples

Crenate leaf margin
Some oak leaves are characterized by their crenate margins, creating a wavy appearance.
The crenate edges of the leaves help distinguish this plant from similar species.
Under the microscope, the cell membrane appeared crenate due to uneven shrinkage.
