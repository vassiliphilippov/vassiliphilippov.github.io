valorization

noun
assigning value
Pronunciation: / ˌvælərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvælərʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of increasing or assigning value to something, especially through official, economic, or policy measures

Examples

Government valorization of crops
The government's valorization of coffee helped stabilize prices for local farmers.
Policies aimed at the valorization of renewable energy can encourage investment in the sector.
Cultural valorization of traditional crafts has boosted tourism in the region.
Government policies can lead to the valorization of certain industries.
The valorization of local traditions helps preserve cultural heritage.
Efforts at valorization have made the region's products more competitive in international markets.

  • antonym: depreciation - a decrease in the value of something
