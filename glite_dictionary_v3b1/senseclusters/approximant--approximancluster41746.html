approximant

noun
approximate value or function
8.3
Pronunciation: / əˈprɑksɪmənt / (AmE), / əˈprɒksɪmənt / (BrE)
an approximate value, function, or object used to represent or estimate something more complex or exact

Examples

A mathematical approximant
The mathematician used an approximant to simplify the complex equation for easier calculation.
In physics, an approximant can help model systems that are too difficult to solve exactly.
The software generated an approximant to estimate the curve's behavior near the origin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approximation - a value or quantity that is nearly but not exactly correct
  • synonym: estimate - a rough calculation or judgment of value, number, or quantity
  • antonym: exact value - a value that is completely accurate and not approximate
