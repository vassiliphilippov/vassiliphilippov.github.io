piriform area

noun
brain region in olfactory system
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrɪˌfɔrm ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈpɪrɪfɔːm ˈeəriə / (BrE)
a pear-shaped region of the brain located in the rhinencephalon, involved in olfactory processing

Examples

The piriform area of brain
Damage to the piriform area can affect a person's sense of smell.
Researchers studied neural activity in the piriform area during odor recognition experiments.
The piriform area connects with other regions involved in olfactory processing.
