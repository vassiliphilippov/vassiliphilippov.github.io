corner

verb
go round a corner (vehicle/driver)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or driver) to go round a corner in a particular way, especially in terms of speed or control.

Examples

To corner quickly
Be careful on this road, as the truck doesn't corner easily.
Motorcycles corner differently from cars, so adjust your speed before turning.
The sports car corners smoothly even at high speeds.
