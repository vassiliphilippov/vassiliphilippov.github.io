ABN

sym
abbreviation; medical or business
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˌbiː ˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌbiː ˈɛn / (BrE)
an abbreviation that can mean 'abnormal' in medical contexts or 'Australian Business Number' in Australian business contexts

Examples

ABN in medical notes
The doctor wrote ABN next to several results on the patient's chart.
Every registered company in Australia must have an ABN for tax purposes.
The laboratory technician flagged the blood test as ABN due to unusual levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Australian Business Number - a unique identifier for businesses in Australia
  • synonym: abnormal - not typical or usual; deviating from the norm
