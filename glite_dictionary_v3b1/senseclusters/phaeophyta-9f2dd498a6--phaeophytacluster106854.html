Phaeophyta

noun
division of brown algae (taxonomy)
9.9
Pronunciation: / fiːˈɒfəɪtə / (AmE), / fiːˈɒfɪtə / (BrE)
A taxonomic division comprising brown algae, sometimes classified as synonymous with Phaeophyceae.

Examples

Phaeophyta brown algae
Scientists have discovered a new species within the Phaeophyta growing off the Pacific coast.
Textbooks often refer to kelp as a representative member of the Phaeophyta.
The Phaeophyta are notable for their brown pigments, which distinguish them from other types of algae.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Phaeophyceae - class of brown algae
