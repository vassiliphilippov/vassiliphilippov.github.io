dancehall

noun
Jamaican music style
Pronunciation: / ˈdænsˌhɔl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsˌhɔːl / (BrE)
a style of Jamaican popular music characterized by rhythmic beats and energetic vocals, often accompanied by dance

Examples

Dancehall music
The club played the latest dancehall hits, and everyone was on the dance floor.
She loves listening to dancehall because of its lively rhythms and catchy melodies.
Many artists from Jamaica have become famous for their unique dancehall sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reggae - a genre of Jamaican music with a strong rhythm and socially conscious lyrics
  • synonym: ska - a fast-paced Jamaican music genre that preceded reggae
