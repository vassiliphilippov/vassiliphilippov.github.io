laity

noun
non-clergy members (esp. in Christianity)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
People who are not members of the clergy, especially in a Christian church; lay people.

Examples

The laity members
Decisions made by the clergy can have a significant impact on the laity.
Members of the laity often serve as volunteers in various church ministries.
The church has always relied on the laity to support community activities and outreach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clergy - people ordained for religious duties
  • synonym: layperson - a person who is not a member of the clergy
