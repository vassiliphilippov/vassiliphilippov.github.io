boathouse

noun
shed for storing boats by water
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a small building by a river or lake used for storing boats

Examples

A boathouse by the lake
A row of colorful boathouses lined the riverbank, creating a picturesque scene.
Every summer, they stored their canoe in the family boathouse.
The old boathouse by the lake was recently renovated into a café.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boatyard - a place where boats are built, repaired, or kept
