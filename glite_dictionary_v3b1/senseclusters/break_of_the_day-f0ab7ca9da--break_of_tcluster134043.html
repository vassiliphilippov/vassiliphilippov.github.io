break of the day

noun
first light of morning; dawn
5.8
Pronunciation: / breɪk əv ðə deɪ / (AmE), / breɪk əv ðə deɪ / (BrE)
the time when the first light appears in the morning; dawn

Examples

The break of the day light
At the break of the day, the birds started to sing in the trees.
Fishermen often set out to sea at the break of the day to catch the earliest fish.
We began our hike at the break of the day, enjoying the cool morning air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawn - the first appearance of light in the sky before sunrise
  • antonym: night - the period of darkness between sunset and sunrise
  • degree higher: sunrise - the time when the sun appears above the horizon in the morning
