ring wall

noun
circular garden wall
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ wɔl / (AmE), / rɪŋ wɔːl / (BrE)
a low circular wall, often in a garden, used to retain soil or define a planting area

Examples

Garden ring wall
They built a stone ring wall around the flower bed to keep the soil from washing away.
The circular ring wall added a decorative touch to the center of the garden.
A low ring wall can help define different planting areas in your backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border - an edge or boundary, especially in a garden
  • synonym: retaining wall - a structure that holds back soil or water
