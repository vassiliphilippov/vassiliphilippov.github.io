tip

noun
suggestion of a potential opportunity
5.4
Pronunciation: / tɪp / (AmE), / tɪp / (BrE)
a piece of information that suggests a potential opportunity, especially one that is not widely known

Examples

A tip about stocks
He acted quickly after getting a tip regarding the upcoming property auction.
Thanks to a tip from a friend, she found a great new job.
The reporter received a tip about the mayor's secret meeting.
