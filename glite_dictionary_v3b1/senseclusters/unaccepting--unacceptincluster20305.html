unaccepting

adjective
not tolerant
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəkˈsɛptɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəkˈsɛptɪŋ / (BrE)
not willing to accept or tolerate something or someone

Examples

Unaccepting attitude
The organization remained unaccepting of any changes to its long-standing traditions.
Some people are unaccepting of opinions that differ from their own.
Her family was initially unaccepting of her decision to move abroad.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accepting - willing to accept or tolerate differences or new ideas
  • synonym: intolerant - not willing to accept views, beliefs, or behavior that differ from one's own
