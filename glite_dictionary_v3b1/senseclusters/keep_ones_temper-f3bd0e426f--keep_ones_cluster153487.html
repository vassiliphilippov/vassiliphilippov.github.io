keep one's temper

verb
remain calm and not get angry
1.2
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz ˈtɛmpɚ / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz ˈtɛmpə / (BrE)

Examples

It's not always easy to keep your temper when dealing with difficult customers.
She tried hard to keep her temper during the heated meeting.
