segmented

adjective
divided into distinct segments
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡ.mən.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈseɡ.mənt.ɪd / (BrE)
Having the body or structure divided into separate parts or segments, especially in reference to animals such as earthworms or lobsters.

Examples

A segmented body
Crustaceans like lobsters have a clearly segmented exoskeleton.
Insects show segmented bodies with distinct head, thorax, and abdomen.
The body of an earthworm is segmented, aiding its movement through soil.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuous - not divided into segments
  • synonym: sectioned - divided into sections
