spirits of ammonia

noun
solution of ammonium carbonate in ammonia water and alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪts əv əˈmoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪts əv əˈməʊniə / (BrE)
a solution of ammonium carbonate in ammonia water and alcohol, formerly used as a stimulant or smelling salt
