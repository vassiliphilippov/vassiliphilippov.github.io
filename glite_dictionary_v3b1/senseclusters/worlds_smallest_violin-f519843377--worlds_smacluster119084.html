world's smallest violin

noun
expression of mock sympathy
Pronunciation: / wɜːrldz ˈsmɔlɪst vaɪˈɔlɪn / (AmE), / wɜːldz ˈsmɒlɪst vaɪˈɒlɪn / (BrE)
used to express insincere or mock sympathy for someone, typically to suggest that their problems are trivial or not deserving of real concern

Examples

The world's smallest violin
After listening to his minor complaints, his coworker sarcastically referenced the world's smallest violin.
Every time you whine about long meetings, I have to resist playing the world's smallest violin.
When he complained about missing dessert, she said, "Here's the world's smallest violin playing for you."
