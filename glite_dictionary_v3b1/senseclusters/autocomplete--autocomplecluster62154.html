autocomplete

verb
to predict text
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtə.kəmˈpliːt / (AmE), / ˌɔːtə.kəmˈpliːt / (BrE)
to predict and automatically complete a word or phrase as someone types, usually using a computer program.

Examples

To autocomplete a word
When I start typing an email address, my computer will autocomplete it for me.
Many search engines autocomplete your query to help you find information faster.
If you enable the feature, your phone can autocomplete common words as you type.
