open-plan

adjective
layout with few or no internal walls
7.7 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈəʊ.pən plæn/ /; / /ˈoʊ.pən plæn/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.pən plæn/ (BrE)
(of a room or building) having a layout with few or no dividing walls or partitions, creating a large, open space.

Examples

Open-plan living room
Many modern homes feature open-plan living rooms and kitchens.
Some people find open-plan spaces to be too noisy for focused work.
The office was redesigned with an open-plan layout to encourage collaboration.
