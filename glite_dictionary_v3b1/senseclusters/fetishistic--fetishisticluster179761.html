fetishistic

adjective
relating to magical objects
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛtɪˈʃɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɛtɪˈʃɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the belief that certain objects have supernatural powers or are worshipped as magical.

Examples

Fetishistic worship
The tribe was known for its fetishistic rituals involving carved wooden figures.
Anthropologists studied the fetishistic beliefs surrounding sacred stones in the region.
Some early explorers misunderstood local customs as purely fetishistic practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rational - based on reason or logic, not superstition
  • synonym: superstitious - believing in supernatural influences or omens
