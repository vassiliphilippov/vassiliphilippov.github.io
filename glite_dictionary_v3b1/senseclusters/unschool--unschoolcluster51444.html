unschool

verb
to educate informally
8.7 0.05/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàskuÀêl / (AmE), /  ånÀàskuÀêl / (BrE)
to educate someone or oneself outside formal schools, using learner-directed and informal methods instead of a set curriculum

Examples

After years in the public system, they decided to unschool their son for a more flexible education.
Families who unschool often use everyday experiences as learning opportunities.
