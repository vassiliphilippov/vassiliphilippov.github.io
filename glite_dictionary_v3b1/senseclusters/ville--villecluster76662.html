ville

x
informal place suffix
Pronunciation: / vɪl / (AmE), / vɪl / (BrE)
an informal suffix added to words to create humorous or descriptive place names meaning 'town' or 'place'.

Examples

Welcome to Funville
After the concert, the area was jokingly called Partyville by the students.
With so many tech companies moving in, people started referring to the city as Startupville.
The neighborhood's many coffee shops earned it the nickname Caffeineville among locals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: town - a place where people live that is larger than a village but smaller than a city
