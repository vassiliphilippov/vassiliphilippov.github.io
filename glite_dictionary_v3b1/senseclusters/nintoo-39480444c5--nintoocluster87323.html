Nintoo

proper noun
Mesopotamian goddess, Lady of Birth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪn.tuː / (AmE), / ˈnɪn.tuː / (BrE)
A goddess in ancient Mesopotamian religion, worshipped as a form of Ninkhursag and known as the ‘Lady of Birth’.

Examples

Nintoo goddess
Archaeological texts often reference Nintoo as an aspect of the great mother goddess.
In ancient Sumerian mythology, Nintoo was revered as a goddess of birth and fertility.
Priests offered prayers to Nintoo to ensure safe childbirth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ninkhursag - Mother goddess in Sumerian mythology, associated with earth and fertility
