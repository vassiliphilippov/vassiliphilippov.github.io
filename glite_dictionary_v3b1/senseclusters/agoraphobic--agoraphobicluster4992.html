agoraphobic

adjective
fearful of open or public places
6.6
Pronunciation: / əˌɡɔrəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / əˌɡɒrəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Suffering from agoraphobia; having an abnormal fear of open or public places.

Examples

An agoraphobic person
As an agoraphobic, she found it extremely difficult to leave her house and go to crowded places.
Because he was agoraphobic, even a simple walk in the park felt overwhelming.
The agoraphobic patient described intense anxiety at the thought of being in a shopping mall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: claustrophobic - suffering from claustrophobia; abnormally afraid of confined spaces
