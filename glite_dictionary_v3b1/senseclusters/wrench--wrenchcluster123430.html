wrench

verb
twist or pull violently, often to remove
4.6
Pronunciation: / rɛntʃ / (AmE), / rɛntʃ / (BrE)
to twist or pull something violently or suddenly, especially to remove it or open it

Examples

She had to wrench the stuck drawer open with all her strength.

The sudden noise made her wrench the steering wheel to the left.
