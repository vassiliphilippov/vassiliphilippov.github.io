North Dravidian

noun
branch of Dravidian languages in eastern India
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ drəˈvɪdiən / (AmE), / nɔːθ drəˈvɪdiən / (BrE)
a branch of the Dravidian languages spoken primarily in eastern India, including languages such as Kurukh and Malto

Examples

North Dravidian languages
Kurukh, a North Dravidian language, has over two million speakers.
Linguists study the connections between North Dravidian and other Dravidian language groups.
The North Dravidian languages are primarily spoken in the states of Jharkhand and Odisha.
