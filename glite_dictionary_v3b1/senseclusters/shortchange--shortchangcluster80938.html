shortchange

noun
unfair treatment

an instance of unfair treatment in which someone does not receive the recognition, opportunities, or resources they deserve

Examples

A shortchange in awards
After the awards ceremony, several artists felt a shortchange because their work was not recognized.
The new policy led to a shortchange of resources for smaller departments.
Many students complained about a shortchange in opportunities compared to previous years.

Synonyms

  • inequity
    7.4 3.63/m
    inequity describes a general lack of fairness in systems, while shortchange names a single case of denied deserved opportunities.
