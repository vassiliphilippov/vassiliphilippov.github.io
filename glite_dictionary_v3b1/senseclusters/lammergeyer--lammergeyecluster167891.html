lammergeyer

noun
a large Eurasian vulture; bearded vulture
6.5 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlæ.mər.gaɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈlæ.mə.gaɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a large Old World vulture (Gypaetus barbatus), also called the bearded vulture, found in mountainous regions of Eurasia and Africa, notable for its long black 'beard' of feathers below the bill

Examples

The lammergeyer vulture
A lammergeyer can be identified by its distinctive black beard and massive wingspan.
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered lammergeyer in its native habitat.
The lammergeyer soared gracefully above the rugged cliffs of the mountain pass.
