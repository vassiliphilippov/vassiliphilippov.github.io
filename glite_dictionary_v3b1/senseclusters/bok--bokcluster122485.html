bok

noun
strong dark lager
9.3
Pronunciation: / bɑk / (AmE), / bɒk / (BrE)
a strong, dark lager beer, especially as known in Dutch or Afrikaans as 'bok' or 'bock' beer

Examples

A glass of bok
