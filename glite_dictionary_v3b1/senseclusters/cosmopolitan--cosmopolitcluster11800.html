cosmopolitan

adjective
international; familiar with many cultures
6.3 4.5
Pronunciation: /kɑːz.mə.ˈpɑːl.ɪ.tən/ (AmE), /kɒz.mə.ˈpɒl.ɪ.tən/ (BrE)
including or open to people, ideas, or influences from many different countries; having a global outlook

Examples

Cosmopolitan city life

It was a cosmopolitan conference, attracting experts from around the globe.
The city has a cosmopolitan atmosphere, with residents from nearly every continent.
