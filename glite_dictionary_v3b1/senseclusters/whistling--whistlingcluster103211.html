whistling

noun
the act of producing a tune by whistling
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪs.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪs.lɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of producing musical sounds with the lips, usually as a tune

Examples

Happy whistling
Her whistling filled the empty street with lively echoes in the early morning.
I heard his cheerful whistling coming from the kitchen as he prepared breakfast.
The whistling stopped abruptly when he saw someone approaching.
