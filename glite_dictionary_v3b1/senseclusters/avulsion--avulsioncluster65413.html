avulsion

noun
forcible body part tearing or separation
Pronunciation: / əˈvʌlʃən / (AmE), / əˈvʌlʃən / (BrE)
the forcible tearing away or surgical separation of a body part from its point of attachment

Examples

Tooth avulsion injury
In dentistry, tooth avulsion occurs when a tooth is completely displaced from its socket.
The patient suffered an avulsion of the toenail after the accident.
Treatment of a nerve avulsion can be complex and may require surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amputation - the removal of a limb or body part by surgery or trauma
  • synonym: laceration - a deep cut or tear in skin or flesh
