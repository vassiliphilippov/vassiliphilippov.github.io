allicin

noun
garlic compound
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈælɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˈælɪsɪn / (BrE)
a natural compound found in garlic that gives it its strong smell and is studied for possible health benefits

Examples

Natural allicin compound
Scientists are investigating how allicin might help fight certain bacteria.
The strong smell of crushed garlic comes from allicin being released.
Some people take supplements containing allicin for its possible health effects.
