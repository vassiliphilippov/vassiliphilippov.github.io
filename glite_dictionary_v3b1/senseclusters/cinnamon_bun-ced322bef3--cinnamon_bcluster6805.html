cinnamon bun

noun
sweet rolled pastry with cinnamon
2.3 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪn.ə.mən bʌn/ /; / /ˈsɪn.ə.mɪn bʌn/ (AmE), /ˈsɪn.ə.mən bʌn/ (BrE)
A sweet, rolled pastry made from dough spread with cinnamon and sugar, often with raisins, cut into slices and baked.

Examples

A warm cinnamon bun
Nothing beats a warm cinnamon bun with a cup of coffee on a cold morning.
She bought a freshly baked cinnamon bun from the bakery for breakfast.
The recipe calls for extra raisins in each cinnamon bun to make them sweeter.
