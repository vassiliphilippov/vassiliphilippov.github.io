zenith

noun
highest point
Pronunciation: / ˈziːnɪθ / (AmE), / ˈziːnɪθ / (BrE)
the highest point or most successful stage of something

Examples

Zenith of success
The athlete was at the zenith of his abilities during the championship season.
Many believe the empire reached its zenith in the 18th century.
Her popularity hit its zenith after the release of her bestselling novel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nadir - the lowest point of something
  • synonym: peak - the highest or best point of something
