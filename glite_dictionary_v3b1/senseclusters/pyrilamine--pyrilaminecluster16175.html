pyrilamine

noun
an antihistamine drug for allergies
8.4
Pronunciation: / paɪˈrɪləˌmiːn / (AmE), / pɪˈrɪl.ə.miːn / (BrE)
an antihistamine drug used to treat allergic conditions such as rhinitis and itching (pruritus)

Examples

pyrilamine allergy medicine
Some over-the-counter allergy remedies contain pyrilamine as an active ingredient.
The doctor prescribed pyrilamine to relieve her allergy symptoms.
You should not take pyrilamine with other sedative medications.

Related Terms
