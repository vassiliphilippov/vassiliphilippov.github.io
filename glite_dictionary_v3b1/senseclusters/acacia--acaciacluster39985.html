acacia

noun
spiny tree or shrub of the genus Acacia
Pronunciation: / əˈkeɪʃə / (AmE), / əˈkeɪsɪə / (BrE)
a tree or shrub of the genus Acacia, typically having spiny branches and compound leaves, found chiefly in warm regions

Examples

The acacia tree
In Africa, giraffes often feed on the leaves of the acacia tree.
The delicate scent of acacia blossoms filled the warm summer evening.
We planted an acacia in our garden for its beautiful yellow flowers.
