Pronunciation: /tɜːrn/ (AmE), /tɜːn/ (BrE)
to move or cause to move around a central point, to rotate, twist, or change direction; or the act of such movement or change in direction

The gymnast tried to turn several cartwheels in a row.
Every spring, the farmers turn the fields to prepare for new crops.
Some tractors are specially designed to turn heavy clay soils efficiently.
You must turn the soil before planting vegetables to promote healthy growth.
