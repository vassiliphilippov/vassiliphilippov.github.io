wide-open

adjective
completely open; fully open
Pronunciation: / waɪd ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / waɪd ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
completely open to the widest extent possible; not closed or partly closed

Examples

Wide-open mouth
His mouth was wide-open in amazement at the news.
She left the window wide-open to let in the fresh air.
The main gate stood wide-open after the parade.
