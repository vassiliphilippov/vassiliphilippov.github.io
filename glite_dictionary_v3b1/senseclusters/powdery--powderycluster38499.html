powdery

adjective
consisting of fine particles
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdəri / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdəri / (BrE)
consisting of, covered with, or looking like fine, loose powder

Examples

Powdery substance
His hands were covered in a powdery substance after working with chalk.
She dusted the cake with powdery sugar before serving it.
The freshly fallen snow had a powdery texture, perfect for skiing.
After walking through the old attic, his clothes were covered in a powdery layer of dust.
The artist chose a powdery blue for the mural, giving it a soft and delicate look.
The makeup has a powdery finish that leaves the skin looking smooth and matte.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dusty - covered with or resembling dust
  • antonym: granular - consisting of small grains or particles
