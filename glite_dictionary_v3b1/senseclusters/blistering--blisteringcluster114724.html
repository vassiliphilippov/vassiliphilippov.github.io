blistering

adjective
extremely hot or intense (of heat, sun, etc.)
6.2 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈblɪ.stə.rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈblɪ.stə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of heat) extremely hot, intense enough to burn or cause discomfort as if it could produce blisters.

Examples

Blistering heat
Athletes competed in the marathon despite the blistering afternoon temperatures.
The blistering sun made it impossible to walk barefoot on the sand.
We suffered through a blistering heatwave that lasted all week.
