antenna

noun
sensory appendage on insects or crustaceans
2.8 4.1
Pronunciation: /æn.ˈten.ə/ (AmE), /æn.ˈten.ə/ (BrE)
either of a pair of long, thin, movable sensory appendages on the head of insects, crustaceans, and some other arthropods, sensitive to touch and taste

Examples

Insect antenna
A lobster's long antennae swept the ocean floor in search of food.
After molting, the crab's new antennae appeared longer and more delicate than before.
The butterfly uses its antennae to sense changes in the air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feeler - a long, flexible organ for touch or sensing, as in insects; synonym for antenna
