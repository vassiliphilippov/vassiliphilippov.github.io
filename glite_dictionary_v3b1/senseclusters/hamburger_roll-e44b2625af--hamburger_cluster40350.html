hamburger roll

noun
round bun for hamburgers
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmˌbɚɡɚ roʊl / (AmE), / ˈhambəˌɡə rəʊl / (BrE)
a round, soft bread roll designed to hold a hamburger patty and its toppings
