ping pong

noun
table game like tennis, played with paddles and ball
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋ pɔŋ /; / ˈpɪŋ ˌpɑŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋ pɒŋ /; / ˈpɪŋ ˌpɒŋ / (BrE)
a game played on a table with a net, where players use small paddles to hit a lightweight ball back and forth
