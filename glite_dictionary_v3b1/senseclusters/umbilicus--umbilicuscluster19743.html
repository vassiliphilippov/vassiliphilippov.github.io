umbilicus

noun
the navel; scar where umbilical cord was attached
6.2
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəs / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɪlɪkəs / (BrE)
the small, round scar on the abdomen marking the spot where the umbilical cord was attached before birth; the navel or bellybutton

Examples

The umbilicus scar
Some people have piercings through their umbilicus as a form of body art.
The baby's umbilicus healed quickly after the cord fell off.
The surgeon made an incision just below the umbilicus for the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bellybutton - informal term for the navel
  • synonym: navel - the central scar on the abdomen where the umbilical cord was attached
