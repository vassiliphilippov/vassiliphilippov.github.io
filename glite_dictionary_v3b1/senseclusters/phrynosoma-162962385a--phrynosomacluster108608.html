Phrynosoma

noun
genus of horned lizards
Pronunciation: / fraɪˈnoʊsəmə / (AmE), / frɪˈnɒsəmə / (BrE)
A genus of lizards commonly known as horned lizards, characterized by short, squat bodies and spiny scales, native to North and Central America.
