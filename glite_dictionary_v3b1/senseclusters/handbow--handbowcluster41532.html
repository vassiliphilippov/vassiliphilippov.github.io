handbow

noun
bow drawn and released by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbəʊ / (BrE)
A bow that is drawn and released by hand, as opposed to a crossbow, which uses a mechanism to release the bolt.

Examples

A handbow weapon
Historical reenactments often feature the handbow as a primary weapon.
The archer preferred using a handbow for its speed and simplicity.
Unlike the crossbow, a handbow requires significant strength to draw.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crossbow - a bow fixed on a stock with a mechanism for shooting bolts
