Red China

proper noun
the People's Republic of China (historical/political term)
7.6
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / rɛd ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
A term formerly used, especially in Western contexts, to refer to the People's Republic of China under communist rule.

Examples

Red China in Cold War
During the Cold War, Western newspapers often referred to the country as Red China.
Many nations did not officially recognize Red China until the 1970s.
The term Red China fell out of common usage as diplomatic relations improved.
