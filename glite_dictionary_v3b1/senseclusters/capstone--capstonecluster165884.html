capstone

noun
top stone of a wall or building
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkæpstəʊn / (BrE)
a stone that forms the top or finishing piece of a wall or building

Examples

Stone capstone
During restoration, the missing capstone was carefully replaced to complete the structure.
The ancient temple's capstone was engraved with mysterious symbols.
The builders placed a large capstone on the finished wall to protect it from the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keystone - the central stone at the summit of an arch, locking the whole together
