Kryptonian

proper noun
Superman's species
Pronunciation: / krɪpˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / krɪpˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
a fictional being from the planet Krypton, especially in comic books such as Superman

Examples

Clark Kent is a Kryptonian
Clark Kent is actually a Kryptonian who was sent to Earth as a baby.
The powers of a Kryptonian increase under Earth's yellow sun.
In the comics, General Zod is another well-known Kryptonian character.
