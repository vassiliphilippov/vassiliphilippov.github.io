stereoscopic picture

noun
3D image from two angled photos
7.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ste.ri.ə.ˈskɑːp.ɪk ˈpɪk.tʃər/ (AmE), /ste.ri.ə.ˈskɒp.ɪk ˈpɪk.tʃə/ (BrE)
two photographs of the same subject taken from slightly different angles and viewed together to give a three-dimensional effect

Examples

Old stereoscopic pictures
He collects antique stereoscopic pictures from the nineteenth century.
I was fascinated by the stereoscopic picture of the mountains, which looked almost real.
Visitors viewed a stereoscopic picture using an old-fashioned stereoscope at the museum.
