profanity

noun
obscene or irreverent language/action
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈfæn.ə.ti / (AmE), / prəˈfan.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
rude, obscene, or irreverent language or actions, especially those which offend religious or moral sensibilities.

Examples

Use of profanity
His use of profanity during the meeting shocked everyone in the room.
She apologized for her profanity after realizing there were children present.
The film was criticized for its excessive profanity and explicit content.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blasphemy - speaking sacrilegiously about sacred things
  • synonym: obscenity - offensive or indecent language or content
  • antonym: reverence - deep respect for someone or something
