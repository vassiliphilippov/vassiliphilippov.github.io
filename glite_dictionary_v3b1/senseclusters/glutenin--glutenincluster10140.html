glutenin

noun
wheat storage protein
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːtənɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtənɪn / (BrE)
a protein (or group of proteins) found in wheat and related grains that helps form gluten and affects the texture and elasticity of bread and dough

Related Terms

  • synonym: gliadin - a type of protein in wheat that combines with glutenin to form gluten
  • synonym: gluten - a mixture of proteins found in wheat, formed from glutenin and gliadin
