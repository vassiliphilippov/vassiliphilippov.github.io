mousepox

noun
viral disease in mice
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsˌpɑks / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsˌpɒks / (BrE)
a viral disease affecting mice that is similar to smallpox in humans

Examples

Mousepox virus
Scientists observed the symptoms of mousepox in the infected laboratory mice.
The outbreak of mousepox led to strict quarantine measures in the research facility.
Researchers are developing vaccines to prevent mousepox in mouse populations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smallpox - a serious viral disease in humans causing fever and skin eruptions
