Tarascan

adjective
of the Tarascan people
Pronunciation: / təˈræskən / (AmE), / təˈræskən / (BrE)
relating to the Tarascan people, their language, culture, or historical state in western Mexico.

Examples

Tarascan culture
The Tarascan civilization was known for its advanced metalworking techniques.
Researchers studied Tarascan traditions to better understand pre-Columbian societies in Mexico.
Many Tarascan artifacts have been discovered in the state of Michoacán.
