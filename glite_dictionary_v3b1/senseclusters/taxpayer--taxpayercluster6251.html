taxpayer

noun
someone who pays taxes
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksˌpeɪər / (AmE), / ˈtæksˌpeɪə / (BrE)
a person or organization that pays taxes to a government

Examples

All taxpayers
All taxpayers must file their returns by April 15 each year.
As a taxpayer, she closely followed the government's spending decisions.
The taxpayer is entitled to know how public funds are being used.
