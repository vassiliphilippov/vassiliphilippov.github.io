sleep

noun
state of rest with suspended consciousness
1.0 63/m
Pronunciation: / sliÀêp / (AmE), / sliÀêp / (BrE)
a natural state of rest in which a person or animal is not conscious and the body recovers strength

Examples

A good sleep
After a long day, she enjoyed a peaceful sleep.
Getting enough sleep each night is important for good health.
The noise outside kept him from falling into a deep sleep.
After a long day at work, he needed a good night's sleep.
Doctors recommend at least eight hours of sleep each night for adults.
The baby finally fell into a deep sleep after crying for an hour.

  • antonym: wakefulness - the state of being awake
