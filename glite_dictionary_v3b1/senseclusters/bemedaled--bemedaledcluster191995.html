bemedaled

verb
to award medals
Pronunciation: / bɪˈmɛdəld / (AmE), / bɪˈmɛdəld / (BrE)
to decorate or honor someone by awarding them medals, especially for achievement or bravery

Examples

To bemedal a champion
The general bemedaled the soldiers who showed exceptional courage during the mission.
After the competition, the coach bemedaled each athlete for their outstanding performance.
The city bemedaled its firefighters in a ceremony recognizing their bravery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorate - to honor someone by giving them a medal or award
  • synonym: honor - to show respect or recognition for someone's achievements
