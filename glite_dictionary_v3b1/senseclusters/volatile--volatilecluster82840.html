volatile

adjective
readily evaporating substance (chemistry)
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑː.lə.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvɒl.ə.taɪl / (BrE)
(of a substance) evaporating readily at normal temperatures and pressures.

Examples

Volatile liquid
Gasoline is a volatile liquid that can easily catch fire if not handled properly.
Perfumes often contain volatile oils that disperse fragrance into the air quickly.
Some cleaning agents are made with volatile solvents that evaporate soon after application.
