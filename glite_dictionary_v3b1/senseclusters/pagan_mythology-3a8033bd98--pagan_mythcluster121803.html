pagan mythology

noun
myths and legends of pagan religions
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪɡən mɪˈθɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈpeɪɡən mɪˈθɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the body of myths, legends, and traditional stories belonging to ancient pagan religions, especially those before the advent of Christianity
