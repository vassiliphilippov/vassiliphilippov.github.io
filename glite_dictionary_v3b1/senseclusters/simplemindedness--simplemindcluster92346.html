simplemindedness

noun
naive innocence
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmpəlˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌsɪmpəlˈmaɪndɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being naive or lacking depth in thought; easily trusting or believing things without question

Some people mistake simplemindedness for honesty, but they are not the same.
