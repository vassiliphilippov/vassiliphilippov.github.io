bleeder

noun
person with hemophilia
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbliːdər / (AmE), / ˈbliːdə / (BrE)
a person who suffers from hemophilia and is prone to uncontrollable bleeding

Examples

A bleeder with hemophilia
