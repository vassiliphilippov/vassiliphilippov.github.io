choriocarcinoma

noun
rare uterine cancer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔriˌoʊˌkɑrsɪˈnoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌkɔːriəʊˌkɑːsɪˈnəʊmə / (BrE)
a rare, fast-growing cancer that develops from cells related to reproduction, often in the uterus, ovaries, or testes
