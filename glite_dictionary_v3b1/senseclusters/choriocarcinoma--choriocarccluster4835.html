choriocarcinoma

noun
rare uterine cancer
9.9
Pronunciation: /kɔːr.i.oʊ.kɑːr.sɪ.ˈnoʊ.mə/ (AmE), /kɔː.ri.əʊ.kɑː.sɪ.ˈnəʊ.mə/ (BrE)
