Tuileries

proper noun
former royal palace and gardens in Paris
9.9
Pronunciation: / twiːˈlɛriz / (AmE), / tɥi.lə.ʁi / (BrE)
the royal palace in Paris built for Catherine de Medici in 1564, largely destroyed in 1871, with only the formal gardens remaining today
