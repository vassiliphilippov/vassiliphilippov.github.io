American red squirrel

noun
small North American squirrel with reddish fur
8.0
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən rɛd ˈskwɝl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən rɛd ˈskwɪəl / (BrE)
a small tree-dwelling squirrel (Tamiasciurus hudsonicus) found in northern United States and Canada, known for its reddish fur and energetic behavior

Examples

The American red squirrel gathering cones
During our hike in the forest, we spotted an American red squirrel gathering pine cones.
The American red squirrel is well known for its rapid movements and loud chattering calls.
Unlike the larger gray squirrel, the American red squirrel prefers evergreen forests and builds its nest in tree branches.
