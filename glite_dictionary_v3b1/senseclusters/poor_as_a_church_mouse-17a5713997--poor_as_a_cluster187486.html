poor as a church mouse

adjective
extremely poor
7.3
Pronunciation: / pʊr æz ə tʃɝtʃ maʊs / (AmE), / pɔːr æz ə tʃɜːtʃ maʊs / (BrE)
extremely poor; having very little money or resources.

Examples

Poor as a church mouse man
After losing his job, Mark became poor as a church mouse and struggled to pay his bills.
He grew up poor as a church mouse, never having enough to eat.
When she first moved to the city, she was poor as a church mouse but determined to succeed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destitute - having no money and no means of support
  • synonym: impoverished - very poor; lacking money or resources
