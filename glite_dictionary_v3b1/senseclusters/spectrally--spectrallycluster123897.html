spectrally

adverb
in a ghostly way
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛktrəli / (AmE), / ˈspɛktrəli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles or relates to a ghost or spirit; in a ghostly manner

Examples

To appear spectrally
The abandoned hallway was spectrally illuminated by the pale light of dawn.
Mist moved spectrally across the graveyard, making the scene even more eerie.
Her voice echoed spectrally through the empty, darkened theater.
