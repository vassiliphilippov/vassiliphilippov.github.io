crocky

adjective
easily broken
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑki / (AmE), / ˈkrɒki / (BrE)
easily broken, brittle, or likely to crack, especially referring to pottery or similar materials

Examples

Crocky teacup
The old teacup was so crocky that it chipped when I washed it.
Be careful with that crocky plate; it might break if you drop it.
After years in the attic, the pottery became crocky and unsafe to use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brittle - hard but likely to break or crack
  • synonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
  • antonym: sturdy - strong and not easily broken
