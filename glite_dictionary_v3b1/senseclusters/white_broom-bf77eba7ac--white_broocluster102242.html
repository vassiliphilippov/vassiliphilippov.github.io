white broom

noun
low European shrub with pale flowers
3.0 8.7
Pronunciation: / waɪt bruːm / (AmE), / waɪt bruːm / (BrE)
a low European shrub with trifoliate leaves and yellowish-white flowers, belonging to the broom family

Examples

White broom shrub
Gardeners often plant white broom for its attractive pale flowers and compact shape.
The hillside was covered with blooming white broom in early summer.
Unlike common broom, white broom has lighter-colored blossoms and a milder scent.
