segmentation

noun
image partitioning into labeled regions

(in computer vision and image processing) the process of partitioning a digital image into meaningful regions or objects, often producing per-pixel labels (e.g., semantic or instance segmentation).

Examples

Image segmentation
Recent advances in segmentation algorithms enable robots to understand complex scenes and avoid obstacles.
The research group published a dataset to benchmark medical image segmentation models for tumor detection tasks.
During training, the neural network improved its pixel-wise segmentation accuracy after several epochs of fine-tuning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instance segmentation - detecting and separating individual object instances while also labeling them at the pixel level.
  • synonym: semantic segmentation - assigning a class label to every pixel in an image (identifies object categories but not separate instances).
