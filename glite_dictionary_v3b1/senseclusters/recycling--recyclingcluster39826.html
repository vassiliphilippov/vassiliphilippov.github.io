recycling

noun
processing of waste into new materials
1.1
Pronunciation: / riˈsaɪklɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈsaɪklɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of collecting and processing used or waste materials so that they can be made into new products

Examples

Recycling process
Our city encourages recycling to reduce landfill waste and protect the environment.
She started a recycling program at her school to collect paper and plastic.
The company uses recycling to turn old tires into playground surfaces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disposal - the act of getting rid of something
  • synonym: reuse - the action of using something again
