Asiatic black bear

noun
a bear species of central and eastern Asia
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪʒiˈætɪk ˈblæk ˌbɛr / (AmE), / ˌeɪzɪˈætɪk ˈblæk ˌbeə / (BrE)
a medium-sized bear (Ursus thibetanus) with a black coat and a white V-shaped patch on the chest, native to central and eastern Asia
