mod

verb
to customize software
5.9 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / mÉ‘d / (AmE), / mÉ’d / (BrE)
to change or customize a game, software, or device by adding new features or content

Examples

To mod a game
He loves to mod his favorite video games to add new characters and levels.
Many players spend hours learning how to mod software to improve their gaming experience.
After you mod the program, it will have extra features not found in the original version.
He modded his favorite video game to include new levels and characters.
Many players enjoy modding their games to improve graphics or add new features.
She learned how to mod her phone to unlock extra settings.

Synonyms

  • synonym: modify - to make changes to something, usually to improve it
  • antonym: restore - to return something to its original state
