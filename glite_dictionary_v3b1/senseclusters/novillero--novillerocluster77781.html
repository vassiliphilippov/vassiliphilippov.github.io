novillero

noun
bullfighter of young bulls
9.9
Pronunciation: / noʊ.viˈjeɾ.oʊ / (AmE), / nɒ.vɪˈjɛə.rəʊ / (BrE)
a bullfighter who fights young bulls, usually under four years old, especially one who has not yet reached full matador status

Examples

A novillero bullfighter
Before becoming a matador, he spent years as a novillero perfecting his skill.
The festival included both seasoned matadors and ambitious novilleros seeking recognition.
The young novillero impressed the judges with his daring moves in the ring.

  • degree higher: matador - the principal bullfighter who kills the bull in a bullfight
