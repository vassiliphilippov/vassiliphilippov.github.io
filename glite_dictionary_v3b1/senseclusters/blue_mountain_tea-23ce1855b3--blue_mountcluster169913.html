Blue Mountain tea

noun
goldenrod plant used for herbal tea
3.9
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈmaʊntən tiː / (AmE), / bluː ˈmaʊntɪn tiː / (BrE)
an aromatic goldenrod plant native to eastern North America, whose leaves are used to make a medicinal herbal tea

Examples

Blue Mountain tea leaves
Many indigenous communities traditionally brewed Blue Mountain tea for its soothing properties.
The herbalist recommended Blue Mountain tea to help with digestion.
You can find dried Blue Mountain tea leaves in some specialty health food stores.
