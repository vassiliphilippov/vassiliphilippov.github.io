choux

noun
light pastry dough
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʃu / (AmE), / ʃuː / (BrE)
a light pastry dough used to make desserts such as éclairs and cream puffs

Examples

Choux pastry dough
The chef piped the choux dough onto the baking tray to make cream puffs.
Learning to make perfect choux pastry takes practice and patience.
She filled the baked choux shells with vanilla custard for dessert.
