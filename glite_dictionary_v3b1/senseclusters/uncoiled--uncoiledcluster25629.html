uncoiled

adjective
no longer coiled or curled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɔɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔɪld / (BrE)
No longer coiled; having been straightened out from a coiled or curled form.

Examples

An uncoiled wire
After several turns, the wire was completely uncoiled.
She let the rope fall, and it uncoiled at her feet.
The snake lay uncoiled in the warmth of the afternoon sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coiled - curled or wound into a spiral or loop
