alley cat

noun
a homeless or stray cat
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈæli kæt / (AmE), / ˈæli kæt / (BrE)
a cat that has no home and lives in the streets, especially in urban areas

Examples

A alley cat roams
Every night, a chorus of alley cats would gather behind the restaurant.
She adopted an injured alley cat and gave it a loving home.
The alley cat scurried between the dumpsters searching for food.
