exhume the body

verb
dig up a buried body for investigation
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˈzum ðə ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzjuːm ðə ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
to dig up a buried body, typically in order to investigate the cause of death or gather evidence

Examples

To exhume the body carefully
After reviewing the case, investigators decided to exhume the body for further examination.
Medical experts often exhume the body when new evidence about the cause of death emerges.
The court granted permission to exhume the body despite the family's objections.
