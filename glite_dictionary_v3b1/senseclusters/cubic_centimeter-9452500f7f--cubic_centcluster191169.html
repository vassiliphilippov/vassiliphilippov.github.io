cubic centimeter

noun
metric unit of volume (1/1000 of a liter)
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈsɛn.təˌmiː.tɚ /; / ˌkjuː.bɪk ˈsɛn.tɪˌmiː.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈsɛn.tɪˌmiː.tə /; / ˌkjuː.bɪk ˈsɛn.tɪ.miː.tə / (BrE)
a metric unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a liter, often used in scientific and medical contexts

Examples
