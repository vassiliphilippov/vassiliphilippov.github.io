coatless

adjective
without outer covering
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtləs / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtləs / (BrE)
having no outer covering or protective layer, such as fur, feathers, paint, or a seed coat

Examples

Coatless animal
The coatless dog shivered in the cold wind after its fur was trimmed.
Some seeds are naturally coatless, making them more vulnerable to damage.
He arrived at the party coatless, despite the chilly evening air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered by any clothing or layer
  • antonym: covered - having a layer or covering over something
