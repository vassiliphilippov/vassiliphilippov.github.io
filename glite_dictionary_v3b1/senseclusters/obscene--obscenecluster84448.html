obscene

adjective
highly offensive or repugnant
4.7
Pronunciation: / əbˈsiːn / (AmE), / əbˈsiːn / (BrE)
extremely offensive, shocking, or morally repugnant, especially in a way that deeply offends accepted standards or sensibilities.

Examples

Obscene language
Many found the dictator's remarks to be downright obscene and deeply hurtful.
Some consider the enormous salaries of certain celebrities to be obscene in a world full of poverty.
The film depicted obscene acts of violence that shocked even the most seasoned viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abhorrent - inspiring disgust and loathing; repugnant
  • antonym: inoffensive - not likely to offend or shock
  • synonym: repugnant - extremely distasteful; unacceptable
