epicyclic gear

noun
gear revolving around sun gear in a gear train
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɪk ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈsɪklɪk ɡɪə / (BrE)
a gear in an epicyclic gear train that revolves around a central sun gear, commonly used in mechanical transmissions to alter speed or torque

Examples

An epicyclic gear train
Recent advancements in epicyclic gear design have improved the efficiency of wind turbines.
The epicyclic gear allows for smooth changes in speed and torque within the automatic transmission.
The mechanic replaced the worn epicyclic gear to restore proper function to the machinery.
