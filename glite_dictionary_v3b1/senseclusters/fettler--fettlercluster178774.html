fettler

noun
machinery repair worker
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛtlər / (AmE), / ˈfɛtlə / (BrE)
a worker who maintains, repairs, or cleans machinery, especially in railways or foundries

Examples

Railway fettler
The fettler carefully checked each machine before the factory started production for the day.
After the train derailed, a fettler was called to inspect and repair the damaged tracks.
In the foundry, the fettler removed excess metal from the castings to ensure a smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engineer - a person who designs, builds, or maintains engines or machines
  • synonym: mechanic - a person who repairs and maintains machinery
