applications programme

noun
software for user tasks on a computer
4.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /æp.lɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃənz ˈproʊ.græm/ (AmE), /æp.lɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃənz ˈprəʊ.græm/ (BrE)
