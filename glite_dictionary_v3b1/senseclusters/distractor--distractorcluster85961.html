distractor

noun
wrong test option
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstræktər / (AmE), / dɪˈstræktə / (BrE)
a wrong answer or option in a test or quiz designed to divert attention from the correct answer

Examples

Quiz distractor option
The teacher included several distractors in the quiz to test students' understanding.
A well-designed distractor can make multiple-choice questions more challenging.
Students should read each question carefully to avoid choosing a distractor.

  • synonym: decoy - something used to mislead or distract
