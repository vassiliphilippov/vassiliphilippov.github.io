acridly

adverb
sharply unpleasantly
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈækrɪdli / (AmE), / ˈækrɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner that is sharply unpleasant or irritating, especially to the senses of smell or taste

Examples

Smells acridly strong
The chemical fumes spread acridly through the laboratory, making everyone cough.
Smoke from the campfire drifted acridly into our tent, forcing us outside.
The onions were chopped so finely that their scent filled the kitchen acridly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mildly - in a gentle or not strong way
  • synonym: pungently - in a way that is strong and sharp in taste or smell
