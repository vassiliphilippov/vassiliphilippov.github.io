nutbag

noun
eccentric person
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈnʌtbæɡ / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a silly, eccentric, or crazy way

Examples

A real nutbag
Everyone thinks my uncle is a nutbag because of his wild stories.
She dressed up as a total nutbag for the costume party last night.
Sometimes you have to be a little bit of a nutbag to make people laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is crazy or behaves in a wild way
  • antonym: normal - a person who behaves in a typical or expected way
  • synonym: weirdo - a person who behaves in a strange or unusual way
