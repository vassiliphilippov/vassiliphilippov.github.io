bionic woman

noun
woman with electromechanical body parts
9.9
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɑnɪk ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / baɪˈɒnɪk ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose body is enhanced or replaced in whole or in part by electromechanical devices, especially in science fiction

Examples

The bionic woman character
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a bionic woman with enhanced strength and speed.
In the TV series, the bionic woman uses her superhuman abilities to fight crime.
Technology is advancing so quickly that the idea of a real bionic woman no longer seems far-fetched.

Related Terms
