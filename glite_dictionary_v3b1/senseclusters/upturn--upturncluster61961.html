upturn

noun
an upward trend, especially in business
4.0 0.49/m
Pronunciation: / Àà åpÀåt…ùn / (AmE), / Àà åpÀåt…úÀên / (BrE)
an upward movement or improvement, especially in economic or business activity

Examples

Economic upturn
After several months of decline, the economy finally showed signs of an upturn.
Analysts are optimistic about a potential upturn in the housing market this year.
There was a noticeable upturn in sales during the holiday season.
