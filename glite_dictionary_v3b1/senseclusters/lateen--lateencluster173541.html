lateen

noun
triangular sail, Mediterranean nautical term
Pronunciation: / ləˈtin / (AmE), / ləˈtiːn / (BrE)
a triangular sail set on a long yard mounted at an angle on the mast, used especially on boats in the Mediterranean region

Examples

Lateen sailboat
He admired the traditional design of the lateen on the old dhow in the harbor.
Navigating the narrow channels was easier with the maneuverable lateen sail.
The fishing boat was rigged with a single lateen sail that caught the afternoon breeze.
