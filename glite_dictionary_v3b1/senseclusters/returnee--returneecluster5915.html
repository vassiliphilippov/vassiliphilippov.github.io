returnee

noun

4.4
Pronunciation: /rɪ.tɜːr.ˈniː/ /; / /rə.tɜːr.ˈniː/ (AmE), /rɪ.tɜː.ˈniː/ /; / /rə.tɜː.ˈniː/ (BrE)
