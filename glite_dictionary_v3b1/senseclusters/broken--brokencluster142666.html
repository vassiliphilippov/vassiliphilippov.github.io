broken

adjective
thrown into disorder or confusion
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkən / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən / (BrE)
(of a group, arrangement, or state) thrown into disorder, confusion, or disarray; no longer organized or intact.

Examples

Broken team
After the argument, the broken team could not work together effectively.
Papers were scattered everywhere in a broken heap on the desk.
The general surveyed his broken ranks and tried to rally the fleeing soldiers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disordered - thrown into disorder or confusion
  • antonym: orderly - arranged in order or organization
