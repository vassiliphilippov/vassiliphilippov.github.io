Tadzhikistan

proper noun
country in Central Asia, north of Afghanistan
Pronunciation: / tɑːdˈʒik ɪˌstɑːn / (AmE), / tædˈʒiːk ɪstɑːn / (BrE)
A landlocked mountainous country in southeast Central Asia, north of Afghanistan, formerly part of the Soviet Union.
