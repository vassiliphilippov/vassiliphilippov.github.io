manage

verb
handle or control effectively
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒ / (BrE)
to handle, control, or deal with something (such as a task, situation, or object)

Examples

She was hired to manage a team of software developers for the new project.
