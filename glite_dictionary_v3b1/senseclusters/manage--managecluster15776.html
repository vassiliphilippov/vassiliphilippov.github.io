Synonyms

  • handle
    2.1 130/m
    handle is used for physically or simply dealing with objects or tasks, while manage covers control or bigger responsibilities.
  • deal
    1.6 180/m
    deal often pairs with 'with' to tackle a problem or person, while manage suggests broader responsibility and control.
  • take care of
    1.3 170/m
    take care of means looking after people or small tasks personally, while manage means organizing or controlling things more widely.
  • administer
    4.7 4/m
    administer is a formal term for running programs or systems in organizations, while manage is a more general everyday word for handling.
  • control
    1.7 30/m
    control means directing or operating a device or process tightly, while manage often means coping with or handling tasks more broadly.
