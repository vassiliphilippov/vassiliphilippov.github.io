headnote

noun
introductory summary note
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌnoʊt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdnəʊt /; / ˈhɛdˌnəʊt / (BrE)
a short note or summary at the beginning of a text, article, or legal decision that explains or summarizes its content
