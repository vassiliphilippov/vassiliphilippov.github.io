conspicuous consumption

noun
buying expensive things to show off wealth
5.0 6.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈspɪkjuəs kənˈsʌmpʃən / (AmE), / kənˈspɪkjʊəs kənˈsʌmpʃən / (BrE)
the act of buying and using expensive goods or services to publicly display one's wealth or status

Examples

Conspicuous consumption of luxury goods
During the holidays, many people fall into the trap of conspicuous consumption to impress friends and family.
He criticized the culture of conspicuous consumption that encourages people to spend beyond their means.
The billionaire's conspicuous consumption was evident in her collection of luxury cars and designer clothing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frugality - the quality of being sparing or economical with resources
  • synonym: ostentation - pretentious or showy display of wealth or luxury
