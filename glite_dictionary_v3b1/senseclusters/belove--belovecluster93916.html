belove

verb
to love deeply
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlʌv / (AmE), / bɪˈlʌv / (BrE)
to love greatly or hold very dear; now used only in archaic or poetic contexts

Examples

To belove a hero
In ancient tales, heroes would belove their country above all else.
She was said to belove her family with unwavering devotion.
Poets of old would often belove the beauty of nature in their verses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adore - to love and respect deeply
  • synonym: cherish - to hold something dear and care for it deeply
  • antonym: hate - to feel intense dislike for
