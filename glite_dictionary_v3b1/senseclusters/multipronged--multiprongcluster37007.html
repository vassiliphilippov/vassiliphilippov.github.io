multipronged

adjective
having several approaches
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈprɔŋd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈprɒŋd / (BrE)
involving several different parts, approaches, or methods at the same time

Examples

Multipronged strategy
The government launched a multipronged campaign to tackle unemployment and poverty.
Their multipronged strategy included education, healthcare, and economic reforms.
A multipronged effort is needed to solve such a complex problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comprehensive - including all or nearly all elements or aspects
  • antonym: single-minded - focused on only one aim or purpose
