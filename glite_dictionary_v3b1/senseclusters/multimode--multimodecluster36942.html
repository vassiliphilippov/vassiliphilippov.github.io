multimode

adjective
supporting multiple modes
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌmoʊd / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌməʊd / (BrE)
able to operate in or support more than one mode or method, especially in technology or devices

Examples

Multimode printer
The new printer is multimode, allowing both wireless and wired connections.
Engineers chose a multimode approach to ensure compatibility with different systems.
Our network uses multimode fiber cables to handle large amounts of data efficiently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-mode - supporting only one mode or method
