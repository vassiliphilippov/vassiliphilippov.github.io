intertropical

adjective
between the tropics
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈtrɑːpɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈtrɒpɪkəl / (BrE)
located in or relating to the region between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn (the intertropical zone)
