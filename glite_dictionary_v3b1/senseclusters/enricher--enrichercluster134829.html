enricher

noun
machine for concentration
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈrɪtʃər / (AmE), / ɪnˈrɪtʃə / (BrE)
a machine or industrial facility used to increase the concentration of a specific component, especially an isotope, in a material

Examples

Uranium enricher
The uranium enricher is a critical part of the nuclear fuel production process.
Engineers inspected the enricher to ensure it was operating safely and efficiently.
A new enricher was installed to increase the output of enriched material at the plant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diluter - a device that reduces the concentration of a component in a material
  • synonym: separator - a device that divides or removes components from a mixture
