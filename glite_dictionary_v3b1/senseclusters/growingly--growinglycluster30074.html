growingly

adverb
increasingly
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that increases or becomes greater over time

Examples

Become growingly frustrated
He became growingly frustrated with the lack of progress on the project.
The city is growingly popular among young professionals seeking new opportunities.
People are growingly aware of the importance of mental health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decreasingly - to a lesser degree over time
  • synonym: increasingly - to a greater degree over time
