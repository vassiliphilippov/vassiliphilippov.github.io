tonguing

noun
articulation with tongue
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of the tongue to articulate or separate speech sounds, especially to emphasize consonants in speaking or singing

Examples

Clear tonguing in speech
Good tonguing is essential for clear pronunciation in both singing and speaking.
The speech therapist taught her students exercises to improve their tonguing of difficult consonants.
Practicing tonguing helps wind instrument players produce crisp, distinct notes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: articulation - the clear and precise pronunciation of words
  • antonym: slurring - to pronounce words or sounds in a way that runs them together
