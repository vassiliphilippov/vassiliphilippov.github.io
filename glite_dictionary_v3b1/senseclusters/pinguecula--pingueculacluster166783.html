pinguecula

noun
yellowish eye conjunctival growth
7.8 9.9
Pronunciation: /pɪŋ.ˈgwe.kjʊ.lə/ /; / /pɪŋ.ˈgwe.kjə.lə/ (AmE), /pɪŋ.ˈgwe.kjʊ.lə/ /; / /pɪŋ.ˈgwe.kjə.lə/ (BrE)

Examples
