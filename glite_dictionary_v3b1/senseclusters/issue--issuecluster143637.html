issue

noun
a single publication in a series
Pronunciation: / ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈɪsju / (BrE)
a single copy of a newspaper, magazine, or journal that is published as part of a regular series

Examples

Latest issue of the magazine

This issue covers a wide range of topics relevant to modern science.
