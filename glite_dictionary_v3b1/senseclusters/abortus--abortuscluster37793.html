abortus

noun
fetus expelled prematurely (medical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈbɔrtəs / (AmE), / əˈbɔːtəs / (BrE)
a fetus weighing less than 0.5 kilogram that is prematurely expelled or removed from the uterus

Examples

A abortus fetus
Medical records indicated that the abortus weighed only 300 grams.
The study analyzed genetic material from each abortus to determine the cause of miscarriage.
The tissue sample was identified as coming from an abortus at fourteen weeks gestation.
