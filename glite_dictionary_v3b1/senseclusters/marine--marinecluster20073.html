marine

noun
naval infantry soldier
Pronunciation: / məˈrin / (AmE), / məˈriːn / (BrE)
a member of a military force trained to serve both on land and at sea, especially as part of a navy

Examples

A marine soldier
Dozens of marines boarded the ship for their next mission.
Every marine takes pride in completing the rigorous boot camp.
The marine was trained to handle both amphibious and land operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship as part of the crew
  • synonym: soldier - a person who serves in an army
