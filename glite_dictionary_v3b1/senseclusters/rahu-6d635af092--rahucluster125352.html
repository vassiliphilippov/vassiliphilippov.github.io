Rahu

noun
Hindu demon causing eclipses
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːhuː / (AmE), / ˈrɑːhuː / (BrE)
In Hindu mythology, a demon who swallows the sun or the moon, causing eclipses.

Examples

Rahu demon
During an eclipse, some people perform rituals to appease Rahu and lessen its negative effects.
In Hindu mythology, Rahu is the demon responsible for causing eclipses by swallowing the sun or the moon.
The story of Rahu and Ketu explains the origins of solar and lunar eclipses in Vedic tradition.
