bowsman

noun
person at boat's bow
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊzmən / (AmE), / ˈbaʊzmən / (BrE)
a person who sits at or works in the front (bow) of a boat, often responsible for lookout or navigation

Examples

The bowsman on boat
The bowsman scanned the river ahead for any hidden rocks.
During the race, the bowsman called out instructions to the rest of the crew.
As the fog thickened, the bowsman kept a careful watch at the front of the boat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowman - a person stationed at the bow of a boat
  • antonym: coxswain - the person in charge of steering and navigating a boat
