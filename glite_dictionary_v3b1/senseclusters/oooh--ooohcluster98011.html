oooh

interjection
pleasure exclamation
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express physical pleasure, sensual enjoyment, or delight

Examples

Oooh that feels good
She closed her eyes and sighed, 'oooh, that massage is amazing.'
When he tasted the dessert, he couldn't help but say 'oooh, this is delicious.'
The hot water hit her sore muscles and she let out a soft 'oooh'.
