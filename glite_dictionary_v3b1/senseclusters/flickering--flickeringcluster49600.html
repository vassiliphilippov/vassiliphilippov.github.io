flickering

adjective
shining unsteadily
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪkərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɪkərɪŋ / (BrE)
shining or burning with an unsteady or wavering light.

Examples

A flickering light
A flickering streetlight made the alley look eerie.
She watched the flickering flames in the fireplace, lost in thought.
The flickering candle cast strange shadows on the wall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steady - showing little or no change or variation; constant
