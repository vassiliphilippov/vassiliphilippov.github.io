merchandising

noun
activity of buying and selling goods
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrtʃənˌdaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːtʃəndaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the business activity of promoting, displaying, and selling goods, especially in retail or commercial settings

Examples

Retail merchandising

The company improved its merchandising to attract more customers to its stores.
