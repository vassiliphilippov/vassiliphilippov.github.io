noncontributing

adjective
not adding value
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈtrɪbjutɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
not providing, adding, or giving anything to a result, group, or process

Examples

Noncontributing team member
The report identified several noncontributing members who rarely attended meetings.
A noncontributing factor was excluded from the final analysis of the project.
He was removed from the team for being consistently noncontributing during group assignments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contributing - providing or adding to something
  • synonym: inactive - not participating or involved
