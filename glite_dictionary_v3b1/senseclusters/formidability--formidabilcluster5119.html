formidability

noun
the quality of being formidable
Pronunciation: / fɔrˌmɪdəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / fəˌmɪdəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being formidable; impressive difficulty or strength that inspires fear or respect

Examples

The formidability of armies
Historians often discuss the formidability of ancient Roman military tactics.
She was impressed by the formidability of the opposing team but determined to play her best.
The formidability of the mountain discouraged inexperienced climbers from attempting the ascent.
