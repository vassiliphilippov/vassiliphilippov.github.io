gob

noun
lump of slimy stuff
6.8
Pronunciation: / ɡɑb / (AmE), / ɡɒb / (BrE)
a lump or mass of slimy or viscous substance

Examples

A gob of glue
A thick gob of glue oozed out from under the paper.
He accidentally stepped in a gob of mud on the sidewalk.
She wiped a gob of jam off her chin with a napkin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wad - a small mass or lump of something soft
