pampering

noun
the act of indulging someone
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpæmpərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpæmpərɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or process of indulging someone's desires or giving them special treatment, often involving comfort or luxury

Examples

A pampering session
A weekend at a luxury spa is the ultimate pampering experience.
She enjoys a bit of pampering after a long week at work.
The hotel offers pampering services such as massages and gourmet meals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indulgence - the act of allowing oneself or another to enjoy pleasure
  • antonym: neglect - failure to care for or attend to someone
