inebriation

noun
state of intoxication from alcohol
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌniːbriˈeɪʃən /; / ɪˌniː.briˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌniːbrɪˈeɪʃən /; / ɪˌniː.brɪˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the state of being drunk or intoxicated from drinking alcohol

Examples

Signs of inebriation
After several drinks, his inebriation became apparent to everyone at the party.
Laws against public inebriation are strictly enforced in this city.
She regretted her actions during last night's inebriation.
After years of inebriation, he finally sought help to overcome his addiction.
Her reckless inebriation at social events often worried her friends.
The doctor explained that chronic inebriation can lead to serious health complications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intoxication - the state of being intoxicated, especially by alcohol
  • antonym: sobriety - the state of being sober, not affected by alcohol
