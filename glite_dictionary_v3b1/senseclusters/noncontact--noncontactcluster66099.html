noncontact

noun
absence of physical touch
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɑntækt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɒntækt / (BrE)
the state or practice of avoiding physical touch or direct contact, especially in sports, healthcare, or services

Examples

Strict noncontact rules
The new guidelines require noncontact between staff and visitors in the hospital.
Many sports introduced noncontact rules to protect players from injuries.
During the pandemic, businesses shifted to noncontact delivery options for safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contact - the act of touching or coming into direct physical connection
  • synonym: distancing - the practice of keeping space between people to avoid physical contact
