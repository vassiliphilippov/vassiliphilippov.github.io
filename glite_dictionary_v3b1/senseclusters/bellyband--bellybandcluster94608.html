bellyband

noun
cloth band worn around the waist
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛliˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌbænd / (BrE)
a cloth band worn around the waist, especially used for infants until the navel has healed

Examples

Infant bellyband
Many traditional cultures use a bellyband for infants during the first few weeks of life.
She bought a soft cotton bellyband for her daughter after the baby's birth.
The nurse wrapped a bellyband around the newborn baby to protect his navel.
