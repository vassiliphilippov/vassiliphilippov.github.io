Sabbath school

noun
Sunday religious instruction class
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsæbəθ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈsabəθ skuːl / (BrE)
A class or meeting, usually held on Sundays, for religious instruction, especially for children.

Examples

Sabbath school class
Every week, the children attend Sabbath school to learn about their faith.
Her favorite part of Sunday was singing hymns at Sabbath school.
The church organizes special activities for Sabbath school students during the holidays.
