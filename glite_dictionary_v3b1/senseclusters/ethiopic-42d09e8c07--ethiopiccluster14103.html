Ethiopic

noun
Geʽez script
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiːθiˈɑːpɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːθiˈɒpɪk / (BrE)
the script, also known as Geʽez, used to write several languages of Ethiopia and Eritrea

Examples

Ethiopic script
The ancient manuscript was written entirely in Ethiopic script.
Linguists study Ethiopic to understand the history of Ethiopian languages.
He learned to read Ethiopic so he could translate old religious texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Geʽez - the ancient script and language of Ethiopia
  • antonym: Latin alphabet - the script used for English and many other languages
