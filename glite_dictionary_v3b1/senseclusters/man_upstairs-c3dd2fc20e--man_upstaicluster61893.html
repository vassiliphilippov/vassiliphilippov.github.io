Man Upstairs

noun
colloquial term for God
Pronunciation: / mæn ʌpˈstɛɹz / (AmE), / mæn ʌpˈstɛəz / (BrE)
(informal) A colloquial term for God, used in informal speech to refer to a divine being watching over people.
