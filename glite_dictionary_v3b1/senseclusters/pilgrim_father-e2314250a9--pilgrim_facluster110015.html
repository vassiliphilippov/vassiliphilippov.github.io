Pilgrim Father

noun
early English settler in Plymouth Colony, 1620
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlɡrəm ˈfɑðɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɪlɡrɪm ˈfɑːðə / (BrE)
