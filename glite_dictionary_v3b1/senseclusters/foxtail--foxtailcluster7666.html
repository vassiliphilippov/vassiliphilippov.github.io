foxtail

noun
bristly grass with brushlike flowering spikes
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːksˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɒks.teɪl / (BrE)
Any of several grasses of the genera Alopecurus and Setaria, characterized by dense silky or bristly brushlike flowering spikes resembling a fox's tail.

Examples

Foxtail grass
Gardeners warned that foxtail grasses can be invasive and hard to control.
The bristly spikelets of foxtail can be dangerous to pets if ingested.
The field was covered in dense patches of foxtail swaying in the breeze.
