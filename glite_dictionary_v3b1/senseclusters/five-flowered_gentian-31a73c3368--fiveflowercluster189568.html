five-flowered gentian

noun
North American gentian with bristly blue flowers
Pronunciation: / faɪv ˈflaʊərd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / faɪv ˈflaʊəd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
A gentian native to eastern North America, distinguished by its clusters of bristly blue flowers.
