hackery

noun
low-quality work
Pronunciation: / ˈhækəri / (AmE), / ˈhækəri / (BrE)
writing, art, or journalism produced quickly for money and lacking originality or quality

Examples

Cheap hackery writing
The magazine was filled with hackery that failed to impress serious readers.
Critics dismissed the film as mere hackery with no creative vision.
He was tired of producing hackery just to pay the bills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hackwork - work done quickly for money with little care for quality
  • antonym: masterpiece - a work of outstanding artistry or skill
