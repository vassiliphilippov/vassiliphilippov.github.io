scrunch

verb
make a harsh engine noise
3.1
Pronunciation: / skrʌntʃ / (AmE), / skrʌntʃ / (BrE)
(of an engine or machinery) to make a harsh, grating noise, especially typical of parts lacking lubrication.

Examples

Engines scrunch loudly
If the engine starts to scrunch, you should check the oil level immediately.
The old tractor began to scrunch as we tried to start it on that cold morning.
We heard the gears scrunch when the transmission failed during the test drive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grind - to make a harsh, grating sound
