Fast of Tammuz

noun
Jewish fast day on 17 Tammuz
9.9
Pronunciation: / fæst əv təˈmuz / (AmE), / fɑːst ɒv təˈmuːz / (BrE)
(in Judaism) A minor fast day observed on the 17th of Tammuz, commemorating the breaching of the walls of Jerusalem.

Examples

Fast of Tammuz day
During the Fast of Tammuz, special prayers are recited in synagogues around the world.
Many Jews observe the Fast of Tammuz by abstaining from food and drink from dawn until dusk.
The Fast of Tammuz marks the day when the walls of Jerusalem were breached by the Babylonians.
