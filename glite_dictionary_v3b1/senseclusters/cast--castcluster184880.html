cast

noun
object formed in a mold
3.5 3.7
Pronunciation: /kæst/ (AmE), /kɑːst/ (BrE)
an object that has been shaped by pouring a liquid material into a mold, where it hardens or sets

Examples

A metal cast
Each unique cast takes on the exact detail of the mold used.
Metalworkers inspected the cast for imperfections before finishing the piece.
The bronze statue was a beautiful cast made from the original sculpture.
