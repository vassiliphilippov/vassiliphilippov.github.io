newsman

noun
male journalist or reporter
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who investigates, reports, or edits news stories as a profession

Examples

A newsman reports news
As a rookie newsman, he was eager to get the big scoop before anyone else.
The newsman asked tough questions during the press conference.
The veteran newsman covered the presidential election for more than three decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newsperson - a gender-neutral term for someone who reports or edits news stories
  • synonym: newswoman - a woman who investigates, reports, or edits news stories
