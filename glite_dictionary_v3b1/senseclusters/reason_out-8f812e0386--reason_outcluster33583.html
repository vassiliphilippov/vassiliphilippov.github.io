reason out

verb
solve or conclude by logical thinking
Pronunciation: / ˈriːzən aʊt / (AmE), / ˈriːzən aʊt / (BrE)
to solve or decide something by thinking carefully and logically

Examples

To reason out a problem
He finally reasoned out why the project had failed after looking at the data.
It took her a while to reason out the best solution to the problem.
They tried to reason out the cause of the engine failure together.

  • synonym: work out - to find an answer or solution by thinking
