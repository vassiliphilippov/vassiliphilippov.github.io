one-thousandth

noun
one part in a thousand; 1/1000
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
Each of one thousand equal parts into which something is divided; the ordinal number corresponding to one thousand in a sequence.

Examples

A one-thousandth part
Owning even a one-thousandth share in the company gives you voting rights.
She measured out exactly one one-thousandth of a liter of the solution.
The error margin was less than one-thousandth of an inch.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ten-thousandth - each of ten thousand equal parts
  • synonym: thousandth - each of one thousand equal parts; also the ordinal number
