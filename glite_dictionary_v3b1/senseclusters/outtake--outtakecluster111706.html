outtake

noun
unused film or recording scene
5.0 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊ.teɪk/ /; / /ˈaʊt.teɪk/ (AmE), /ˈaʊ.teɪk/ /; / /ˈaʊt.teɪk/ (BrE)

Examples
