outtake

noun
unused film or recording scene
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊt.teɪk / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.teɪk / (BrE)
a scene or part of a film, television program, or recording that is filmed or recorded but not included in the final version

Examples

Funny outtakes compilation
Each episode ends with a compilation of outtakes and funny moments.
Fans enjoyed watching the outtakes because they showed the actors making mistakes on set.
The DVD includes several hilarious outtakes that were cut from the movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blooper - an embarrassing error left out of the final cut (often shown as a joke)
