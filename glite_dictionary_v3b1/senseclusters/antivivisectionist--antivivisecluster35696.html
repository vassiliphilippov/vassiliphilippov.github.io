antivivisectionist

noun
opponent of animal experiments
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntivaɪvɪˈsɛkʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntivɪvɪˈsɛkʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who opposes or campaigns against the practice of performing operations on live animals for research purposes

Examples

An antivivisectionist protester
The antivivisectionist spoke passionately at the animal rights conference.
Many antivivisectionists believe that animal testing is both cruel and unnecessary.
As an antivivisectionist, she campaigns for alternatives to animal experimentation in science.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animal rights activist - a person who campaigns for the rights and welfare of animals
  • antonym: vivisectionist - a person who supports or performs operations on live animals for research
