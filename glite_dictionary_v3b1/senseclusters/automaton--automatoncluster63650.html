automaton

noun
a self-operating machine
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˈmeɪtən /; / ɔˈtɑməˌtɑn / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmeɪtən /; / ɔːˈtɒmətən / (BrE)
a machine or device that moves and operates automatically, often designed to imitate a human or animal
