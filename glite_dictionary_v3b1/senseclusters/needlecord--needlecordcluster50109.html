needlecord

noun
fine-ribbed fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəlˌkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈniːdəlˌkɔːd / (BrE)
a type of corduroy fabric with very narrow, closely spaced ribs, used especially for clothing
