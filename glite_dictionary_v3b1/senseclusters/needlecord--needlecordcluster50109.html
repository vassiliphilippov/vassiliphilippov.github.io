needlecord

noun
fine-ribbed fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəlˌkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈniːdəlˌkɔːd / (BrE)
a type of corduroy fabric with very narrow, closely spaced ribs, used especially for clothing

Examples

A needlecord skirt
She wore a needlecord skirt that was both stylish and comfortable.
The tailor recommended needlecord for making lightweight jackets in the spring.
His favorite trousers were made from soft brown needlecord.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corduroy - a durable fabric with raised ribs or ridges
  • antonym: velvet - a soft fabric with a dense pile
