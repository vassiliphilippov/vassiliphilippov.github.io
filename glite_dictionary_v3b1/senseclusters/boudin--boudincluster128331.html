boudin

noun
Cajun sausage
Pronunciation: / buːˈdæn / (AmE), / buːˈdæ̃ / (BrE)
a type of sausage, especially from Cajun or Creole cuisine, typically made with pork, rice, and spices

Examples

Cajun boudin sausage
We ordered spicy boudin at the Louisiana restaurant.
The chef prepared homemade boudin using a traditional Cajun recipe.
At the festival, people lined up to taste the famous boudin from local vendors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sausage - a food made of ground meat and spices in a casing
