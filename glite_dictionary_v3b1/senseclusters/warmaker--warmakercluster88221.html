warmaker

noun
one who promotes war
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person, group, or country that initiates or promotes war or conflict

Examples

Aggressive warmaker
The protesters marched against the warmaker responsible for starting the conflict.
Many historians debate whether the leader was truly a warmaker or simply defending his country.
The documentary explored the motives of several notorious warmakers throughout history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: peacemaker - a person who helps to prevent or end conflict
