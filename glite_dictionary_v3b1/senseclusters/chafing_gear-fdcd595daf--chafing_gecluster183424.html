chafing gear

noun
protective covering for ropes or spars
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ ɡɪə / (BrE)
Material, usually rope or canvas, used to cover a line or spar to protect it from friction and wear.

Examples

Chafing gear protection
Ensure the chafing gear is properly secured to prevent damage to the mooring lines.
The sailors added extra chafing gear to the ropes near the dock.
Worn chafing gear should be replaced to maintain the integrity of the equipment.
