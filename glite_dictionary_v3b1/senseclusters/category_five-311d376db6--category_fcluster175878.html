category five

noun
most severe hurricane classification
4.7 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˌɡɔri ˈfaɪv / (AmE), / ˌkatɪˌɡɒri ˈfaɪv / (BrE)
the highest classification on the hurricane scale, indicating a hurricane with sustained winds of 157 mph (252 km/h) or higher, signifying extreme intensity and catastrophic potential
