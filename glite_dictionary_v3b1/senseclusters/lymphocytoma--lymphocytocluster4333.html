lymphocytoma

noun
benign lymphocyte tumor
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪmfəsaɪˈtoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌlɪmfəsaɪˈtəʊmə / (BrE)
a benign tumor or swelling formed by an abnormal increase of lymphocytes in tissue

Examples

A lymphocytoma swelling
The dermatologist identified the swelling as a lymphocytoma after examining the skin biopsy.
A lymphocytoma is usually benign and can be treated with medication or minor surgery.
After noticing a lump, she visited her doctor, who diagnosed it as a lymphocytoma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lymphoma - a cancer of the lymphatic system involving lymphocytes
  • synonym: tumor - an abnormal mass of tissue
