solitaire

noun
extinct flightless bird related to the dodo
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːlɪˈtɛr / (AmE), / ˌsɒlɪˈteə / (BrE)
an extinct flightless bird, related to the dodo, that was native to Mauritius and Réunion

Examples

Extinct solitaire
Like the dodo, the solitaire could not fly and was driven to extinction by humans.
Researchers study fossils of the solitaire to learn about island evolution.
The Rodrigues solitaire was last seen on the island in the 18th century.
