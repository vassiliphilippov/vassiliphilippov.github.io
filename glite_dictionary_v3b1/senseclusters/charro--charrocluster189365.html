charro

noun
Mexican horseman
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional Mexican horseman known for elaborate clothing and skilled horsemanship, often participating in rodeo events

Examples

A Mexican charro
The charro rode proudly in the parade, wearing a beautifully embroidered suit.
At the festival, a skilled charro demonstrated traditional rope tricks for the crowd.
Many tourists admired the colorful outfits of each charro during the celebration.
The charro wore a beautifully decorated suit and a wide-brimmed hat during the festival.
Many tourists admired the skilled riding of the charros at the traditional Mexican rodeo.
He dreamed of becoming a charro and practicing the art of horsemanship like his grandfather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who herds cattle and performs skilled riding, especially in the US
  • synonym: vaquero - a Spanish or Latin American cattle herder or horseman
