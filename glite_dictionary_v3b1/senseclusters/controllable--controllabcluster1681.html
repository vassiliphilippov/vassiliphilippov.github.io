controllable

adjective
able to be controlled
3.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊləbəl / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊləbəl / (BrE)
capable of being controlled; able to be directed or kept within limits.

Examples

Easily controllable dog
Some dogs are not easily controllable without proper training.
The new safety features make the machine much more controllable during operation.
The situation remained controllable thanks to the quick actions of the staff.
