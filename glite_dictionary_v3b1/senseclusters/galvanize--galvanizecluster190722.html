galvanize

verb
coat metal with zinc
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡal.və.naɪz /; / ˈɡælvənaɪz / (BrE)
to coat metal (especially iron or steel) with a layer of zinc to protect it from rusting

Examples
