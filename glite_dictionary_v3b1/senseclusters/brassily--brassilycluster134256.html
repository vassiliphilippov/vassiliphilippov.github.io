brassily

adverb
loud or showy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsɪli / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsɪli / (BrE)
in a loud, showy, or harsh manner, like the sound or appearance of brass

Examples

Brassily laugh loudly
She laughed brassily at the comedian's joke, drawing everyone's attention.
The band played brassily, filling the hall with energetic, bold music.
He spoke brassily to impress the crowd, but some found it overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loudly - in a manner that produces a lot of noise
  • antonym: softly - in a quiet or gentle manner
