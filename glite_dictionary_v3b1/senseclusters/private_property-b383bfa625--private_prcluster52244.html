Synonyms

  • personal property
    3.1 1/m
    personal property is the everyday name for movable belongings, while private property is the legal term contrasting movable goods with land.
  • personal estate
    6.1 0.02/m
    personal estate refers to movable belongings a person owns, whereas private property names those movable possessions rather than land.
  • personalty
    9.9 0.01/m
    personalty is the older legal word for movable goods, while private property is the modern term for movable personal possessions.
  • personal chattel
    8.2 0.00/m
    personal chattel names a single physical movable item like a chair, while private property means all movable personal possessions.
