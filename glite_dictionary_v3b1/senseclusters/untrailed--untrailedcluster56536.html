untrailed

adjective
without a trail
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtreɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪld / (BrE)
not having a path, track, or trail, or not being followed by a trail

Examples

Untrailed forest
The hikers ventured into the untrailed wilderness, searching for untouched nature.
She preferred exploring untrailed areas where few people had ever walked.
The untrailed hillside was covered in wildflowers and tall grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pathless - without any path or track
  • antonym: tracked - having a visible trail or path
