weekday

noun
day of the week except Sunday (sometimes except Saturday)
1.0 6/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàwiÀêkÀåde…™ / (AmE), / ÀàwiÀêk.de…™ / (BrE)
any day of the week except Sunday (and sometimes except Saturday); typically a day when most people work or attend school.

Examples

Every weekday morning
She travels by train to work every weekday.
The museum is closed on Sundays and open every weekday.
We usually meet on a weekday when the office is less busy.

  • workday
    1.0 3/m
    workday describes a person's usual day of paid work or its hours, while weekday means any day outside the weekend or holidays.
