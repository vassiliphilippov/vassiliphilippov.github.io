aerophobic

adjective
fear of flying
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌeərəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
having a fear of flying in aircraft or being in the air

Examples

Aerophobic fear of flying
Because he is aerophobic, he always chooses to travel by train instead of by plane.
Her aerophobic reaction made the idea of a family vacation abroad very challenging.
The aerophobic passenger gripped the armrest tightly during takeoff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aviophobic - having a fear of flying in airplanes
  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not afraid
