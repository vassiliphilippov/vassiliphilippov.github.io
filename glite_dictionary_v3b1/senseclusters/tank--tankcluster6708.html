tank

verb
to fail badly
Pronunciation: / tæŋk / (AmE), / tæŋk / (BrE)
to fail badly or fall sharply in value, quality, or performance

Examples

Sales tanked quickly
After the negative reviews, the movie's box office numbers tanked within the first week.
His grades tanked after he stopped attending classes regularly.
The stock price tanked following the announcement of the scandal.
After the negative review, sales of the product tanked within a week.
The team's performance tanked after their star player was injured.
Investors were worried when the market suddenly tanked last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collapse - to fall down or fail suddenly and completely
  • synonym: crash - to suddenly lose value or fail completely
  • synonym: plummet - to fall or drop quickly in amount or value
  • antonym: rise - to increase in value, quality, or performance
  • antonym: succeed - to achieve the desired aim or result
