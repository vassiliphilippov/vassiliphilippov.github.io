peacekeeping mission

noun
operation to maintain or restore peace, often military
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpisˌkipɪŋ ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌkiːpɪŋ ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a military operation where forces are deployed to maintain or restore peace between hostile groups or countries, especially under international mandate

Examples

A peacekeeping mission deployed
After months of negotiation, a peacekeeping mission was deployed to enforce the ceasefire.
Many countries contributed troops to the peacekeeping mission in the aftermath of the civil war.
The United Nations sent a peacekeeping mission to stabilize the conflict-ridden region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: military intervention - military action in a foreign conflict or state
  • synonym: peacekeeping - the act of maintaining peace, often via military forces
  • antonym: war - armed conflict between nations or groups
