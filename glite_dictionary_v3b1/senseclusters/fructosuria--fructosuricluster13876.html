fructosuria

noun
presence of fructose in urine
Pronunciation: / ˌfrʌk.təˈsjʊr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˌfrʌk.tjʊˈsjʊə.ri.ə / (BrE)
the presence of fructose (levulose) in the urine, often considered a harmless condition

Examples

Fructosuria condition
Fructosuria is usually benign and does not require treatment in most cases.
Some metabolic disorders can lead to fructosuria in affected children.
The doctor diagnosed him with fructosuria after detecting sugar in his urine tests.
