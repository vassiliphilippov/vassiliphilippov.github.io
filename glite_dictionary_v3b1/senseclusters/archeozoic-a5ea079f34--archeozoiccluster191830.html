Synonyms

  • Archeozoic eon
    9.9 0.00/m
    Archeozoic eon explicitly names the interval as an eon, while Archeozoic is the shorter, general form for that same time.
  • Archean
    9.9 0.00/m
    Archean is used as the standard scientific name for that early era, while Archeozoic is a variant term describing the same eon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Archaean - the main alternative term for this early geological eon
  • synonym: Precambrian - the time in Earth's history before the Cambrian period, including the Archaeozoic and Proterozoic eons
  • degree higher: Proterozoic - the geological eon after the Archaeozoic, from 2,500 to 541 million years ago
