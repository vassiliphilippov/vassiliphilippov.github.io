polysilicon

adjective
containing polycrystalline silicon
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˈsɪlɪkən / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈsɪlɪkən / (BrE)
made of or relating to polycrystalline silicon, especially as used in electronics or solar panels

Examples

Polysilicon layer technology
The company invested in new polysilicon technology to improve solar panel efficiency.
Engineers selected a polysilicon layer for the microchip's gate material.
Modern photovoltaic cells often use polysilicon materials for better performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monocrystalline - made of a single crystal structure
  • synonym: silicon - a chemical element used in electronics and solar cells
