naturalistically

adverb
in a realistic manner
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnætʃɚəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnatʃərəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that imitates or represents nature or reality as it actually is

Examples

Actors perform naturalistically
The actors performed naturalistically, making the scene feel authentic and believable.
The documentary was filmed naturalistically to show wildlife in its true environment.
She described the events naturalistically, without exaggeration or embellishment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificially - in a way that is not natural or real
  • synonym: realistically - in a way that shows things as they really are
