rapturously

adverb
with great enthusiasm or delight
Pronunciation: / ˈræp.tʃɚ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈræp.tʃər.əs.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows intense pleasure, joy, or enthusiasm

Examples

To rapturously accept news
He accepted the news rapturously, unable to contain his excitement.
She spoke rapturously about her recent trip to Paris.
The audience applauded rapturously as the curtain fell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecstatically - in an extremely happy or enthusiastic manner
