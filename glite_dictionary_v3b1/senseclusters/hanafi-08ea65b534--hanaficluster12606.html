Hanafi

proper noun
Sunni legal school
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːnəfi / (AmE), / ˈhænəfi / (BrE)
a member of the largest Sunni school of Islamic law, or relating to that school

Examples

A Hanafi Muslim
Many Muslims in South Asia identify as Hanafi and follow its legal traditions.
The Hanafi school emphasizes the use of reason in interpreting Islamic law.
He studied the differences between Hanafi and other Sunni legal schools at university.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Hanbali - a member of a distinct Sunni school of Islamic law
  • antonym: Maliki - a member of a different Sunni school of Islamic law
  • antonym: Shafi'i - a member of another major Sunni school of Islamic law
