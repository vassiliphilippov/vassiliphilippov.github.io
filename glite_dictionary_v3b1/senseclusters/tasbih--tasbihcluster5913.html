tasbih

noun
Islamic devotional recitation
Pronunciation: / tæsˈbiː / (AmE), / tæsˈbiː / (BrE)
a form of Islamic prayer involving the repeated recitation of phrases glorifying God, often using prayer beads

Examples

Recite tasbih prayers
After the sermon, the congregation engaged in tasbih, quietly repeating phrases of praise.
She used her prayer beads to keep count during her daily tasbih.
Many Muslims perform tasbih as a way to remember and glorify God throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dhikr - the Islamic practice of remembering and mentioning God through repeated phrases
  • synonym: prayer - an act of communicating with a deity, especially as a religious ritual
