reascent

noun
renewed upward movement
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈsɛnt / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
a second or renewed upward movement or rise after a previous decline

Examples

A reascent in profits
After a period of stagnation, the company experienced a strong reascent in profits.
The climbers began their reascent of the mountain after resting at base camp.
The artist's popularity saw a reascent following the release of her new album.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recovery - a return to a normal or improved state after a decline
