adagietto

adverb
slightly faster than adagio
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːdɑːˈdʒɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌadadʒiˈɛtəʊ / (BrE)
in music, at a tempo slightly faster than adagio but slower than andante
