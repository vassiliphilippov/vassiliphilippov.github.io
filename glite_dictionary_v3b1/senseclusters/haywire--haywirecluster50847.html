haywire

adjective
(informal) mentally confused or erratic
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌwaɪə / (BrE)
(informal) Mentally confused or erratic; behaving in a wild or uncontrolled way.

Examples

Haywire thinking
After a week without sleep, his thoughts seemed to go completely haywire.
Sarah went haywire when she heard the unexpected news and started laughing uncontrollably.
The children went haywire during the birthday party, running and shouting everywhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crazy - mentally deranged; extremely foolish or wild
  • antonym: sane - mentally healthy and rational
