emic

noun
insider perspective
Pronunciation: / ˈiːmɪk / (AmE), / ˈiːmɪk / (BrE)
an account or perspective that reflects the viewpoint of someone within a particular culture or social group

Examples

An emic account
The anthropologist valued the emic provided by community members when studying local traditions.
Understanding the emic is essential for researchers who want to avoid misinterpreting cultural practices.
Her report offered an emic that revealed the true meaning behind the festival's rituals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: etic - an outsider's perspective or analysis of a culture
