alter ego

noun
a person's second self or identity
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑltɚ ˈiːgoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɔːltə ˈiːgəʊ / (BrE)
a second personality or identity of a person, often distinct from their ordinary personality

Examples

A secret alter ego
His alter ego is far more outgoing and adventurous than he is in real life.
The comedian performed as his alter ego, captivating the audience with a completely different style.
Writers often use an alter ego to explore aspects of their personalities in their work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: double - a person who looks or acts like another, especially as a substitute
