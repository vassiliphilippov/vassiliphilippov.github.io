duff

verb
to perform badly
9.4
Pronunciation: /dʌf/ (AmE), /dʌf/ (BrE)
to fail or perform badly at something, especially in a clumsy or awkward way

Examples

To duff a test

I really duffed my driving test and will have to try again next month.
She duffed the presentation by mixing up all the important facts.
