malambo

noun
Argentine folk dance
9.9
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈlɑːm.boʊ/ /; / /mə.ˈlæm.boʊ/ (AmE), /mə.ˈlɑːm.bəʊ/ /; / /mə.ˈlæm.bəʊ/ (BrE)
a traditional Argentine folk dance performed by men, known for fast, rhythmic footwork, and also the lively music style that accompanies it
