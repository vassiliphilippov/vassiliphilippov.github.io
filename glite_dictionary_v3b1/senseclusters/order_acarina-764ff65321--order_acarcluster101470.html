order Acarina

noun
taxonomic order of mites and ticks
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊrdər əˈkærɪnə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əˈkærɪnə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order that comprises mites and ticks, small arthropods found in diverse habitats.

Examples

Order Acarina mites
Mites belonging to the order Acarina can be found in soil, water, and even on plants.
Scientists recently discovered a new species within the order Acarina in South America.
Ticks, which are part of the order Acarina, can transmit various diseases to animals and humans.
