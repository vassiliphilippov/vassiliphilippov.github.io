Poecilogale albinucha

noun
small African striped weasel
Pronunciation: / poʊˌsɪlɪˈɡeɪl ælˌbɪˈnuːkə / (AmE), / pəʊˌsɪlɪˈɡɑːliː ælˌbɪˈnuːkə / (BrE)
A small, slender African weasel with a white patch on the top of its head, known for burrowing habits; also called the African striped weasel.
