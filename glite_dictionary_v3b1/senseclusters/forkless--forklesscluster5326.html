forkless

adjective
without branches
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrkləs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkləs / (BrE)
not divided into two or more branches; having no forks or splits.

Examples

Forkless road
The forkless road led straight through the forest without any turns.
A forkless branch is often used for making walking sticks.
The river remained forkless, flowing straight through the valley without splitting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: branched - having one or more branches or divisions
  • synonym: unbranched - not having any branches or divisions
