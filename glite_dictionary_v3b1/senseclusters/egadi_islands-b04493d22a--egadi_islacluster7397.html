Egadi Islands

proper noun
island group off western Sicily
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˈɡɑːdi ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˌɛˈɡɑːdi ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of islands off the west coast of Sicily in the Mediterranean Sea, known for their historical significance and natural beauty
