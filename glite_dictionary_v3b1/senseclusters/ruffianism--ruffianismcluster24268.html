ruffianism

noun
violent or lawless behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfiəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈrʌfiənɪzəm / (BrE)
violent or lawless behavior, especially that characteristic of a ruffian

Examples

Violent ruffianism
His involvement in ruffianism eventually led to his arrest.
The judge condemned the ruffianism that had taken hold of the streets.
Years of unchecked ruffianism undermined the city's sense of safety.

  • synonym: thuggery - violent or criminal behavior; brutality
