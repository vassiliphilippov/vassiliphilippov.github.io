give someone the brush-off

verb
dismiss or reject someone abruptly
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn ðə ˈbrʌʃ ɒf / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈsʌmwʌn ðə ˈbrʌʃ ɒf / (BrE)
to dismiss or reject someone in a brusque or unfriendly manner

When I tried to get advice from my professor, she just gave me the brush-off without any explanation.
