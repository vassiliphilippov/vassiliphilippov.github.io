nervous prostration

noun
extreme exhaustion from emotional stress
6.3 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈnɜːr.vəs prɑː.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈnɜːr.vəs prə.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈnɜː.vəs prɒ.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈnɜː.vəs prə.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A condition characterized by extreme mental and physical exhaustion, often caused by emotional stress, resulting in an inability to work or function normally.
