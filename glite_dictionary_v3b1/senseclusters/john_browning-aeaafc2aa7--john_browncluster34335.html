John Browning

proper noun
U.S. firearms inventor (1855–1926)
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
(1855–1926) A United States inventor renowned for developing firearms such as automatic pistols, repeating rifles, and machine guns, including the Peacemaker.
