lamellar

adjective
plate-like layers
Pronunciation: / ləˈmɛlər / (AmE), / ləˈmɛlə / (BrE)
having thin, plate-like layers or structures

Examples

Lamellar structure
The mineral has a lamellar structure visible under the microscope.
Scientists observed lamellar formations in the bone tissue samples.
The shell's lamellar layers provide it with extra strength and flexibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layered - arranged in layers
  • synonym: stratified - formed or arranged in layers
