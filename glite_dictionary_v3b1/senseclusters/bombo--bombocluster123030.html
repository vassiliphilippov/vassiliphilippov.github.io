bombo

noun
large bass drum
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɒmbəʊ / (BrE)
a large bass drum used in South American and Spanish music, especially in folk and traditional styles

Examples

Folk bombo drum
The musician struck the bombo to keep the rhythm during the parade.
In traditional Andean music, the bombo provides a deep, steady beat.
She learned to play the bombo as part of her folk music ensemble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bass drum - a large drum that produces a low, deep sound
  • antonym: snare drum - a small drum with a sharp, crisp sound
