fit in

verb
be accepted as part of a group
Pronunciation: / fɪt ɪn / (AmE), / fɪt ɪn / (BrE)
to be accepted as a member of a group or to blend harmoniously with others

Examples

Fit in at school
Despite his efforts, Mark never really managed to fit in at his new job.
She was nervous on her first day, hoping she would fit in with her new classmates.
Some people change their appearance in order to fit in with a group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend in - to look or seem the same as those around you and not be easily noticeable
