trick up

verb
dress or decorate elaborately
9.1 5.8
Pronunciation: /trɪk ʌp/ (AmE), /trɪk ʌp/ (BrE)
to dress or decorate (someone or something) in a way that is more elaborate or attractive than usual, often to impress others
