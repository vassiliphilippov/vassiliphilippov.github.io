trick up

verb
dress or decorate elaborately
9.1
Pronunciation: / trɪk ʌp / (AmE), / trɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to dress or decorate (someone or something) in a way that is more elaborate or attractive than usual, often to impress others

Examples

To trick up a car
He loves to trick up his car with custom accessories and flashy paint.
She decided to trick up the living room with new cushions before the guests arrived.
The children were tricked up in costumes for the school play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fancy up - to decorate or embellish fancifully
