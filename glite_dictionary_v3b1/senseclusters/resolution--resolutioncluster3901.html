resolution

noun
formal decision or statement by vote

a formal statement of a decision or opinion agreed on by a group, typically as a result of a vote at a meeting

Examples

Pass a resolution
A resolution condemning the act was approved by the assembly.
The board passed a resolution to adopt the new policy.
They debated the resolution for several hours before finally voting.

Synonyms

  • declaration
