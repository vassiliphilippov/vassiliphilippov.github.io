motif

noun
recurring theme in music
6.7 0.71/m
Pronunciation: / mo äÀàtif / (AmE), / m…ô äÀàtiÀêf / (BrE)
a short musical idea or theme that is repeated or developed throughout a piece of music

Examples

Musical motif
Each movement of the suite features a different motif that ties the work together.
The composer introduced a haunting motif that recurred throughout the symphony.
The film's soundtrack is unified by a simple piano motif that appears in several scenes.

Synonyms
