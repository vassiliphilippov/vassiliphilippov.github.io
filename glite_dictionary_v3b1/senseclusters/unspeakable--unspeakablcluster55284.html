unspeakable

adjective
too sacred to be spoken
3.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspiːkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈspiːkəbl / (BrE)
(of something, especially a name or word) too sacred or holy to be spoken aloud.

Examples

Unspeakable name
He shuddered at the thought of mentioning the unspeakable word in the sanctuary.
In her faith, speaking the unspeakable name was forbidden to all but the high priest.
The priests regarded the deity's unspeakable name as too holy to utter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ineffable - too great or sacred to be expressed in words
