undernutrition

noun
lack of nutrients
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərnuˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌʌndənjuːˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
a condition resulting from not getting enough food or essential nutrients for good health

Examples

Severe undernutrition
Children in some regions suffer from undernutrition due to food shortages.
Long-term undernutrition can weaken the immune system and increase the risk of disease.
Programs to prevent undernutrition focus on providing balanced meals to vulnerable populations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malnutrition - a condition caused by an unbalanced or insufficient diet
  • antonym: overnutrition - a condition caused by consuming too many nutrients or calories
