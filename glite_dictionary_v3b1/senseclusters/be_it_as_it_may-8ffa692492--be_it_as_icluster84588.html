be it as it may

x

2.9 6.3
Pronunciation: /biː ɪt æz ɪt meɪ/ /; / /bɪ ɪt əz ɪt meɪ/ (AmE), /biː ɪt æz ɪt meɪ/ /; / /bɪ ɪt əz ɪt meɪ/ (BrE)
Used to indicate that what has just been said may be true, but it does not affect what follows; regardless of the circumstances.

Examples

Be it as it may phrase
Be it as it may, the outcome is beyond our control.
Be it as it may, we have to make a decision by tomorrow.
He disagrees with the proposal, but be it as it may, the company will proceed.

Related Terms
