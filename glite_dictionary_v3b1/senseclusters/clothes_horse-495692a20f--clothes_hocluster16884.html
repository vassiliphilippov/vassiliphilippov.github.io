clothes horse

noun
fashion-conscious person
5.6
Pronunciation: / kloʊðz hɔrs / (AmE), / kləʊðz hɔːs / (BrE)
a person who is very interested in fashionable clothes and enjoys dressing stylishly

Examples

A stylish clothes horse
My sister is a real clothes horse and always knows the latest trends.
He became known as a clothes horse after appearing in several fashion magazines.
Although she is a clothes horse, she also values comfort in her outfits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashionista - a person who is very interested in fashion and style
  • antonym: slob - a person who is careless about their appearance or dress
  • synonym: trendsetter - someone who starts or popularizes new styles or trends
