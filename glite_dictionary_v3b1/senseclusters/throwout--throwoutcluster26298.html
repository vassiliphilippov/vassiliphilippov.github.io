throwout

noun
clutch release bearing
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈθroʊ.aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈθrəʊ.aʊt/ (BrE)
a clutch release bearing that presses against the pressure plate to disengage a vehicle's clutch

Examples

Throwout bearing replacement
The mechanic replaced the worn throwout to stop the clutch's grinding noise.
A faulty throwout can make it difficult to shift gears smoothly.
When the throwout fails, the clutch may not disengage properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clutch - a device for engaging and disengaging power transmission in vehicles
  • synonym: release bearing - a bearing that disengages the clutch in a vehicle
