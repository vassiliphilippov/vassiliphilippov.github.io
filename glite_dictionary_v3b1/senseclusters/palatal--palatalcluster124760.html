palatal

adjective
produced with the tongue near the hard palate
5.1 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpælət̬l / (AmE), / ˈpælətl / (BrE)
(of a speech sound) produced with the front or blade of the tongue near or touching the hard palate, as in the sounds 'y' in 'yes', 'ch' in 'chin', or 'j' in 'gin'.

Examples

Palatal sound 'ch'
Linguists classify 'ch' in 'chin' as a palatal affricate.
Some languages have more palatal sounds than English does.
The English 'y' is an example of a palatal consonant.

Related Terms
