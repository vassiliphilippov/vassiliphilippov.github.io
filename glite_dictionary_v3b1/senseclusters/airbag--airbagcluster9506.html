airbag

noun
protective packing bag
2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈer.bæɡ/ (AmE), /ˈeə.bæɡ/ (BrE)
a sealed bag filled with air used to cushion and protect items during packing and transport

Examples

Packing airbag protection
She wrapped the vase in an airbag and bubble wrap before sending it to the customer.
The fragile electronics were protected by a large airbag inside the shipping box.
Using an airbag can help prevent damage to delicate items during delivery.
