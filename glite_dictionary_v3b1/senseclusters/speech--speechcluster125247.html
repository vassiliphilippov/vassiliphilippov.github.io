speech

noun
manner of oral expression
1.0 9.96/m
Pronunciation: / spiÀêt É / (AmE), / spiÀêt É / (BrE)
the characteristic style or manner in which a person expresses themselves orally

Examples

Formal speech
Her speech was so formal that it surprised everyone at the casual gathering.
I admire the clarity of his speech when he gives presentations at work.
The therapist noted a significant improvement in the child's speech over several months.

Synonyms

  • manner of speaking
    3.8 0.33/m
    manner of speaking describes a person's usual way of talking, while speech refers to their overall oral style or expression.
  • delivery
    4.1 2.78/m
    delivery refers to how someone presents words in public, while speech means the person's general oral style or manner of speaking.
  • speech pattern
    3.8 0.41/m
    speech pattern is a specific accent, rhythm, or intonation people use, while speech is the broad style of their oral expression.

Related Terms

  • antonym: silence - the absence of speech or sound
  • synonym: speaking - the action of conveying information or expressing one's thoughts and feelings in spoken language
