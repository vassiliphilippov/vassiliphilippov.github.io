heroicity

noun
heroic quality
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪroʊˈɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌhɪərəʊˈɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being heroic or showing great courage and bravery

Examples

The heroicity of rescue
The heroicity of the rescue team was praised by everyone in the city.
Stories of heroicity can inspire people to act bravely in difficult situations.
Her heroicity during the crisis saved many lives and earned her a special award.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of courage to face danger or difficulty
  • synonym: heroism - great bravery or courageous behavior
