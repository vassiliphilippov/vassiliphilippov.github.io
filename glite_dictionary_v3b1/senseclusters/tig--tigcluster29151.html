tig

verb
to steal small items
9.9 0.01/m
to steal small items, especially in a sneaky or petty way; to pilfer (chiefly dialectal).

Examples

To tig sweets

The old stories tell of boys who used to tig sweets from the village shop.
She warned her son not to tig anything, even if it seemed unimportant.
