Apollyon

proper noun
Biblical destroying angel
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːliˌɑn / (AmE), / əˈpɒliˌɒn / (BrE)
a name for the destroying angel or demon in the Bible, often representing destruction or chaos

Examples

Apollyon in Revelation
In the ancient text, Apollyon is described as the leader of destructive forces.
The character in the novel was compared to Apollyon because of his ruthless actions.
Many scholars interpret Apollyon as a symbol of chaos and ruin in biblical literature.
