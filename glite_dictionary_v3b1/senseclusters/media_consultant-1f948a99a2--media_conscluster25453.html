media consultant

noun
professional advising on communication media use
3.7 0.40/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀêdi…ô k…ônÀàs ålt…ônt / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêdi…ô k…ônÀàs ålt…ônt / (BrE)

Examples

Media consultant advice
As a media consultant, she specializes in digital marketing strategies for small businesses.
