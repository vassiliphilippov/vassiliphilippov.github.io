two steps ahead

adverb
well prepared or advanced compared to others
Pronunciation: / tu stɛps əˈhɛd / (AmE), / tuː stɛps əˈhɛd / (BrE)
significantly more advanced in preparation, understanding, or progress than others

Examples

Successful investors stay two steps ahead by anticipating market trends.
