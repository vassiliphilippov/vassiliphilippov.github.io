patternmaking

noun
designing decorative motifs
Pronunciation: / ˈpætərnˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpætənˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or art of designing repeated decorative motifs or layouts, especially for fabrics, wallpaper, or similar surfaces

Examples

Patternmaking for designs
She studied patternmaking to create unique designs for her clothing line.
Modern patternmaking software allows artists to experiment with endless motif variations.
The company hired a specialist in patternmaking to refresh their wallpaper collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pattern design - the creation of decorative or functional patterns
  • synonym: textile design - the art of creating designs for woven, knitted, or printed fabrics
