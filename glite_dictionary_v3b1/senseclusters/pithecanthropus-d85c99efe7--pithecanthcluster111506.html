Pithecanthropus

noun
former genus name for Homo erectus
Pronunciation: / pɪˌθikænˈθroʊpəs / (AmE), / pɪˌθiːkænˈθrəʊpəs / (BrE)
a former genus name for certain extinct hominids, now classified as Homo erectus

Examples

Pithecanthropus fossil
Early textbooks often referred to Pithecanthropus as the 'Java man.'
Eugène Dubois discovered the remains of Pithecanthropus in Java in the late nineteenth century.
The name Pithecanthropus is no longer used in modern anthropology, being replaced by Homo erectus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Homo erectus - extinct species of hominid formerly called Pithecanthropus
