magnetic stripe

noun
magnetic data strip on a card
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnɛtɪk straɪp / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪk straɪp / (BrE)
a narrow strip of magnetic material on a card, storing data for electronic reading devices such as credit card readers

Examples

A magnetic stripe card
Most hotel key cards use a magnetic stripe to store room access information.
Please swipe your card so I can read the magnetic stripe and process your payment.
The magnetic stripe on my debit card stopped working after I kept it near my phone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magnetic tape - a tape coated with magnetic material for storing data or sound
