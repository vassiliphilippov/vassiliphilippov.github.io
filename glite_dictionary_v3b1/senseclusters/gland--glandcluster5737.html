gland

noun
organ that produces and secretes substances
2.2 3.0
Pronunciation: /glænd/ (AmE), /glænd/ (BrE)
An organ in the body that produces and releases substances such as hormones or other fluids, either into the bloodstream or through ducts.

Examples

Salivary gland
Damage to the salivary gland can affect digestion.
The sweat gland helps regulate body temperature.
The thyroid gland produces hormones that control metabolism.
