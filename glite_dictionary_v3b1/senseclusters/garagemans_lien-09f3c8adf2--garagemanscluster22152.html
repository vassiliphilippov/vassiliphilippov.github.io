garageman's lien

noun
legal right to retain a vehicle for unpaid work
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈreɪdʒˌmænz liːn / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɑːʒˌmænz liːn / (BrE)
a legal right allowing a garage owner to retain possession of a vehicle until payment for repairs or storage is made

Examples

Garageman's lien on vehicle
If you do not settle your account, a garageman's lien may be placed on your vehicle.
The repair shop exercised its garageman's lien when the customer failed to pay the bill.
Under the garageman's lien, the garage kept the car until all storage fees were settled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mechanic's lien - a legal claim against property by someone who performed work or supplied materials on it
