TPM

noun
security chip or maintenance method
7.6
Pronunciation: / ti pi ɛm / (AmE), / tiː piː ɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for either 'Trusted Platform Module', a computer security chip, or 'Total Productive Maintenance', a management approach in manufacturing

Examples

TPM chip installed
The IT department requires a TPM chip for all new computers to enhance security.
Our factory implemented TPM to reduce equipment breakdowns and improve productivity.
Before installing the software, check if your laptop has TPM enabled in the BIOS settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Total Productive Maintenance - a management system to improve manufacturing efficiency
  • synonym: Trusted Platform Module - a hardware chip for computer security
