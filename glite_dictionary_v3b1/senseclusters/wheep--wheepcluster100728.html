wheep

verb
to make a high-pitched cry
9.9
Pronunciation: /wiːp/ (AmE), /wiːp/ (BrE)
to make a short, high-pitched sound or cry, especially as made by small birds, animals, or young children

Examples

To wheep like chicks
The baby birds wheep loudly every morning when they are hungry.
As the kitten played, it would sometimes wheep in excitement.
We heard a child wheep with delight as she spotted the puppy.
