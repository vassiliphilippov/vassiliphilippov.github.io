bionic man

noun
man with electromechanical body parts
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɑnɪk mæn / (AmE), / baɪˈɒnɪk mæn / (BrE)
A man whose bodily functions have been supplemented or replaced by electromechanical devices, especially artificial limbs or organs.

Examples

The bionic man with limbs
After his accident, he became known as the bionic man due to his advanced prosthetic limbs.
She was fascinated by stories of the bionic man and dreamed of designing robotic limbs herself.
The idea of a bionic man once seemed far-fetched, but medical science is making it more of a reality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyborg - an organism with both biological and artificial components
