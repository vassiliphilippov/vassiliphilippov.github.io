anteriorly

adverb
toward the front (anatomy, medicine)
5.9
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪriɚli / (AmE), / ænˈtɪəriəli / (BrE)
In the direction toward the front, especially in anatomical or medical contexts.

Examples

Positioned anteriorly in body
In some animals, the heart is positioned anteriorly in the body cavity.
The incision was made anteriorly to access the frontal lobe.
The ribs curve anteriorly as they approach the sternum.

Related Terms

  • antonym: posteriorly - in the direction toward the back (anatomy, medicine)
