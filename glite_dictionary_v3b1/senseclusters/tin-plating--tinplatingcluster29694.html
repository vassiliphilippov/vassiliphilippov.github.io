tin-plating

noun
coating metal with a layer of tin
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪn ˌpleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪn ˌpleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of applying a thin layer of tin to the surface of a metal object, usually to prevent corrosion or improve appearance

Examples

Tin-plating metal sheets
Old-fashioned cans were made by tin-plating sheets of iron.
Proper tin-plating ensures the food inside remains uncontaminated.
The company specializes in tin-plating steel to prevent it from rusting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: galvanization - the process of applying a protective zinc coating to iron or steel
