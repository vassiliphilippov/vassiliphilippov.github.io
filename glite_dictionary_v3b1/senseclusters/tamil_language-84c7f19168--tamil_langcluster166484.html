Tamil language

noun
Dravidian language of southern India and Sri Lanka
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmɪl ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtæmɪl ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
