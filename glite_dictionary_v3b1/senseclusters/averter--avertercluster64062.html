averter

noun
one who prevents
Pronunciation: / əˈvɜrtər / (AmE), / əˈvɜːtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that turns something away or prevents it from happening

Examples

An averter of danger
The scarecrow acted as an averter of birds in the field.
Wearing a helmet is an important averter of head injuries while cycling.
The new policy was seen as an averter of potential financial crises.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cause - a person or thing that brings something about
  • synonym: preventer - a person or thing that stops something from happening
