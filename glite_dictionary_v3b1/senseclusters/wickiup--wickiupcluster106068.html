wickiup

noun
Native American brush hut
7.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪ.kɪ.ʌp/ /; / /ˈwɪ.kɪ.əp/ (AmE), /ˈwɪ.kɪ.ʌp/ /; / /ˈwɪ.kɪ.əp/ (BrE)
A domed hut or lodge made with a frame of poles covered with brush, matting, or grass, used traditionally by some Native American peoples of the southwestern United States.
