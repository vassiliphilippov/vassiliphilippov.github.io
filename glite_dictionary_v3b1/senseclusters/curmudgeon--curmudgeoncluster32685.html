curmudgeon

noun
bad-tempered, cantankerous old person
9.1
Pronunciation: / kɚˈmʌdʒən / (AmE), / kəˈmʌdʒən / (BrE)
an old person, especially a man, who is easily annoyed or bad-tempered and often stubborn

Examples

An old curmudgeon
Despite his reputation as a curmudgeon, he secretly enjoyed the company of his grandchildren.
Everyone in the neighborhood knew old Mr. Jenkins was a real curmudgeon who hated visitors.
She called her grandfather a curmudgeon whenever he grumbled about new technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grouch - a person who complains or grumbles frequently, especially habitually
  • synonym: grump - a person who is often sulky or in a bad mood
  • antonym: sweetheart - a person with a gentle, kind temperament
