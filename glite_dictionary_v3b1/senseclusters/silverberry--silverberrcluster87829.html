silverberry

noun
North American shrub with silvery leaves and berries
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəˌbɛri / (BrE)
A deciduous North American shrub with silvery leaves and fruits, commonly found in dry soils.

Examples

The silverberry shrub
Birds are drawn to the silverberry's fruit during the late summer.
The silverberry is often used in landscaping due to its attractive foliage.
We planted a silverberry hedge along the edge of our property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oleaster - a related shrub with similar silvery leaves and fruit
