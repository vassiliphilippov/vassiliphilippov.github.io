possum

noun
small Australian tree-dwelling marsupial
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːsəm / (AmE), / ˈpɒsəm / (BrE)
a small, furry, tree-dwelling marsupial with a long tail, found in Australia and the Americas

Examples

A possum in tree
A possum climbed across the power lines into the neighbor's yard last night.
The Australian possum uses its prehensile tail to grasp tree branches.
We installed a special box in the garden to provide shelter for the local possums.
I found a family of possums living in the old tree in our backyard.
We saw a possum climbing the fence last night.
When threatened, a possum may play dead to avoid predators.
