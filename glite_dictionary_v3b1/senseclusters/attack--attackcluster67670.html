Synonyms

  • assault
    4.7 4.21/m
    assault is a violent, often criminal attack on someone, while attack is a more general word for using force.
  • assail
    8.0 1.63/m
    assail means to strike or criticize with force in speech or action, while attack focuses on physical or military force.
  • set upon
    6.9 0.30/m
    set upon means to rush and attack someone suddenly from all sides, while attack is a general act of using force.
  • set on
    5.8 0.44/m
