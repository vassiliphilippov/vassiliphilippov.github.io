lyophilizer

noun
freeze-drying machine
9.9
Pronunciation: / laɪˈɑfɪˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / laɪˈɒfɪˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
a machine that removes water from substances by freeze-drying, often used to preserve biological or chemical samples

Examples

A lyophilizer machine
The scientist placed the samples in the lyophilizer to prepare them for long-term storage.
A lyophilizer is essential equipment in many pharmaceutical laboratories.
After running the lyophilizer overnight, the technician checked the dried specimens.

Related Terms
