dripping

noun
liquid falling in drops
4.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɪ.pɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdrɪ.pɪŋ/ (BrE)
A small amount of liquid that falls in drops, especially from something wet or leaking.

Examples

A dripping tap
After the storm, the steady dripping of rainwater could be heard everywhere.
There was a constant dripping from the leaky faucet in the kitchen.
Water dripping from the roof soaked the ground beneath the eaves.
