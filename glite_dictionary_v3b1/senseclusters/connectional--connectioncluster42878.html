connectional

adjective
church organizational system
8.8
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛkʃənəl / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the organizational system that links local churches and governing bodies within certain Christian denominations, especially Methodism

Examples

Connectional church system
The connectional structure of the church helps maintain unity among its members.
Methodist leaders discussed the benefits of their connectional approach during the annual conference.
A connectional system allows local congregations to work together on shared missions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independent - not connected to or governed by a larger organization
  • synonym: organizational - relating to the structure or arrangement of an organization
