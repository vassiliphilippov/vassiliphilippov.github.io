emo

adjective
emotional music style
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈiːmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈiːməʊ / (BrE)
relating to the emo music genre, style, or subculture characterized by emotional expression and distinctive fashion.

Examples

Emo fashion style
She wore an emo outfit with black eyeliner and band T-shirts to the concert.
The new album has a distinctly emo sound, with heartfelt lyrics and intense guitar riffs.
Many teenagers in the early 2000s identified with the emo subculture and its expressive style.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mainstream - conforming to the prevailing attitudes or practices
  • synonym: punk - relating to the punk music genre or subculture
