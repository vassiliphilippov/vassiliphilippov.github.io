bateau

adjective
boat-shaped neckline
8.8
Pronunciation: / bəˈtoʊ / (AmE), / baˈtəʊ / (BrE)
having a wide, shallow neckline that follows the curve of the collarbone, resembling the shape of a boat.

Examples

Bateau neckline dress
The dress featured a classic bateau neckline that elegantly framed her shoulders.
Designers often use a bateau cut to create a sophisticated and timeless look.
Her sweater's bateau neckline made it perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: V-neck - a neckline in the shape of a V at the front
  • synonym: boat neck - a wide neckline that runs horizontally across the collarbone
