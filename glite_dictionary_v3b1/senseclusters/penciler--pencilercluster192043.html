penciler

noun
person who uses pencil
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnsələr / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsələ /; / ˈpɛnsɪlə / (BrE)
a person who draws or sketches with a pencil, especially someone who prepares drafts, technical drawings, or comic book art by hand

Examples

Comic penciler sketches
The comic book penciler sketched the first version of each page before it was inked.
As a penciler, she carefully drafted architectural plans by hand for the design team.
The editor asked the penciler to revise several details in the technical drawing.
The penciler completed the rough sketches before the rest of the art team began their work.
As a talented penciler, she brought the comic book characters to life with her detailed drawings.
Many aspiring artists dream of becoming a penciler for major comic book publishers.
The comic book penciller finished the initial drawings before passing them to the inker.
As a penciller, she prefers to work with soft graphite for her detailed sketches.
The penciller made quick notes in the margins to outline his ideas for the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: colorist - an artist who adds color to comic book art
  • synonym: drafter - a person who prepares technical drawings or plans
  • synonym: illustrator - an artist who creates images for books or magazines
  • antonym: inker - a person who traces or outlines drawings in ink
  • synonym: sketcher - a person who makes sketches or rough drawings
  • synonym: writer - a person who writes texts or documents
