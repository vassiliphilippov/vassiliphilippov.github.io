tall order

noun
formidable or difficult task
8.3
Pronunciation: / tɔl ˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
a task or request that is very difficult or challenging to achieve

Examples

A tall order
Convincing him to change his mind will be a tall order after everything that's happened.
Finishing the project by Friday is a tall order, but the team is determined to try.
Scoring three goals in five minutes was a tall order, but the team almost managed it.
Convincing the board to change their decision will be a tall order.
Finishing the report by tomorrow is a tall order for such a small team.
Hosting a festival with just two weeks of planning is truly a tall order.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easy task - something simple or not challenging to do
  • antonym: piece of cake - something that is very easy to do
