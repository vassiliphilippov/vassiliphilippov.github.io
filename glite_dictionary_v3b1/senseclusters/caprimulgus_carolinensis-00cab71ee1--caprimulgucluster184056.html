Caprimulgus carolinensis

noun
large nocturnal bird of the southern U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˌkæprɪˈmʌlɡəs ˌkæroʊlɪˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkæprɪˈmʌlɡəs ˌkærəlɪˈnɛnsɪs / (BrE)
