reverberation

noun
indirect consequence or aftereffect
5.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˌvɜrbəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌvɜːbəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an effect or result that is indirectly produced by an earlier action, often one that continues for some time

Examples

Economic reverberation
Global financial markets still felt the reverberation of the economic crisis decades later.
Her resignation sent reverberations through the entire organization, prompting further departures.
The reverberations of the policy change affected the company for years after its introduction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consequence - a result or effect of an action or condition
  • synonym: repercussion - an indirect effect or result, often negative
