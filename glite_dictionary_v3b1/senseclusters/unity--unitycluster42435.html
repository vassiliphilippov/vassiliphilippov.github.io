unity

noun
the number one
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnəti / (AmE), / ˈjuːnəti / (BrE)
The number one; the quality or state of being one.

Examples

Mathematical unity
In mathematics, the multiplicative unity is simply the number one.
The ratio of any number to unity yields the number itself.
Unity is often represented by the digit '1' in equations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: one - the lowest cardinal number; a single unit
