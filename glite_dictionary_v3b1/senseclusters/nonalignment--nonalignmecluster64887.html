nonalignment

noun
policy of not aligning with major power blocs
7.0 6.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ə.ˈlaɪn.mənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ə.ˈlaɪn.mənt/ (BrE)
the policy or practice of not aligning with any major group, party, or country, especially in politics or international relations
