daffy

adjective
silly or foolish
Pronunciation: / ˈdæfi / (AmE), / ˈdafi / (BrE)
silly or foolish; slightly crazy, often in a harmless or amusing way.

Examples

A daffy joke
Her daffy sense of humor always makes me laugh.
Stop being daffy and focus on your work.
The daffy antics of the children lightened the mood of the room.
