bucktooth

noun
large projecting front tooth
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkˌtuθ / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.tuːθ / (BrE)
A large front tooth that projects noticeably outward, especially one of the upper incisors.

Examples

A bucktooth smile
He was self-conscious about his bucktooth as a child, but later embraced it.
The dentist examined the child's bucktooth and recommended braces.
The rabbit's bucktooth made it look even more adorable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overbite - the projection of the upper teeth over the lower teeth
