Huginn

proper noun
Odin's raven
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhuː.gɪn/ /; / /ˈhʌ.gɪn/ (AmE), /ˈhuː.gɪn/ /; / /ˈhʌ.gɪn/ (BrE)
in Norse mythology, one of Odin's two ravens who flies the world to bring him information

Examples

Huginn the raven
According to legend, Huginn flies across the world each day to gather news for Odin.
The statue depicted Huginn perched on Odin's shoulder, ready to take flight.
Stories often mention both Huginn and Muninn as Odin's trusted companions.
