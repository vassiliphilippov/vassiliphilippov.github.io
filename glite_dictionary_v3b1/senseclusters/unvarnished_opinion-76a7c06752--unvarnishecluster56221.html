unvarnished opinion

noun
honest, straightforward view or statement
6.0 6.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɑrnɪʃt əˈpɪnjən / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɑːnɪʃt əˈpɪnjən / (BrE)
an honest and direct statement of one's views, without any attempt to make them seem more pleasant or acceptable than they are
