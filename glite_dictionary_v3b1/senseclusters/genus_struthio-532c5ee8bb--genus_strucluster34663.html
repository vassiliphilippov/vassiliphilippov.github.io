genus Struthio

noun
genus of African ostriches
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈstruːθioʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈstruːθiəʊ / (BrE)
The genus of African ostriches, constituting the type genus of the family Struthionidae.

The genus Struthio is well known for its only living species, the common ostrich.
