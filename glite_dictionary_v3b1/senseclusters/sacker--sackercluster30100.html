sacker

noun
city plunderer
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsækər / (AmE), / ˈsækə / (BrE)
a person who attacks and plunders a town or city, often by force and looting

Examples

City sacker
The invading army's sacker marched through the city, seizing treasures and burning homes.
After the battle, the sackers looted the town and set many buildings on fire.
Legends tell of a notorious sacker who left entire cities in ruins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defender - a person who protects a place from attack
  • synonym: pillager - someone who violently steals from a place, especially during war
  • synonym: plunderer - a person who steals goods, especially during war or chaos
