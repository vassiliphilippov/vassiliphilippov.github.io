graphomaniacal

adjective
excessively writing
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræfoʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (AmE), / ˌɡræfəʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (BrE)
having an obsessive or uncontrollable urge to write excessively, often producing large amounts of writing
