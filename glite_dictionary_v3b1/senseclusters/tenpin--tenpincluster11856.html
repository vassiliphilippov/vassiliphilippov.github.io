tenpin

noun
bottle-shaped pin used in bowling
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.pɪn / (BrE)
one of the ten bottle-shaped pins used in tenpin bowling, knocked down by the ball

Examples

A tenpin pin
He set up each tenpin carefully before starting the match.
One tenpin remained standing, spoiling her perfect game.
The bowler knocked down every tenpin on his first try.
