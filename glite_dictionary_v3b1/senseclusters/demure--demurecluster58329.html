demure

adjective
affectedly modest or shy
5.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmjʊr / (AmE), / dɪˈmjʊə / (BrE)
quiet, modest, or shy in appearance or behavior (sometimes in a playful or affected way)

Examples

A demure smile
His comments made her laugh, but she quickly resumed a demure expression.
She gave him a demure smile that hinted at mischief.
Wearing a demure dress, she flirted quietly from across the room.
She wore a demure dress that covered her shoulders and knees.
His demure manner made him stand out in the lively group.
The student gave a demure response when praised for her work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bold - showing willingness to take risks; confident and courageous
  • synonym: modest - having or showing a moderate or humble estimate of one's merits
  • synonym: shy - nervous or timid in the company of others
