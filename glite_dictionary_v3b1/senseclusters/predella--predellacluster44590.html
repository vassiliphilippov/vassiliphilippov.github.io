predella

noun
altarpiece base panel
Pronunciation: / prəˈdɛlə / (AmE), / prɪˈdɛlə / (BrE)
a decorated horizontal panel or platform at the base of an altar or altarpiece, often painted or sculpted

Examples

A predella panel
The museum displayed a beautifully restored predella beneath the main Renaissance altarpiece.
Each panel of the predella illustrated a different scene from the life of the saint.
Art students studied the detailed figures painted on the predella for their class project.
The Renaissance painting was displayed above a beautifully painted predella.
The predella of the altar featured scenes from the life of the saint.
Restorers carefully cleaned the carved figures on the old church's predella.

  • synonym: altarpiece - a work of art placed behind or above an altar
  • synonym: plinth - a heavy base supporting a statue or vase
