handleable

adjective
able to be managed
Pronunciation: / ˈhændələbəl / (AmE), / ˈhændləbəl / (BrE)
able to be managed, dealt with, or handled without too much difficulty

Examples

A handleable problem
The new software update made the system much more handleable for beginners.
Despite the heavy traffic, the commute was still handleable for most drivers.
She found the responsibilities of her new job to be quite handleable after some training.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manageable - able to be controlled or dealt with easily
  • antonym: unmanageable - difficult or impossible to control or deal with
