Rene Magritte

proper noun
Belgian surrealist painter (1898–1967)
Pronunciation: / rəˈneɪ mæˈgriːt / (AmE), / reɪˈneɪ mæˈgriːt / (BrE)
(1898–1967) Belgian surrealist painter known for his witty and thought-provoking images that challenge observers’ perceptions of reality.
