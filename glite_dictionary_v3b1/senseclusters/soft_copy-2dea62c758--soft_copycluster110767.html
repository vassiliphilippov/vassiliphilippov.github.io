soft copy

noun
electronic version of a document
2.9 2.7
Pronunciation: / sɑft ˈkɑpi / (AmE), / sɒft ˈkɒpi / (BrE)
A version of a document or data that exists in electronic form and can be viewed on a computer screen, rather than as a physical printout.

Examples

A soft copy file
Please send me the soft copy of your presentation by email.
Students must submit a soft copy of their assignments through the online portal.
The manual is available in both soft copy and hard copy formats.
