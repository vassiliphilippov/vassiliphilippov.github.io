milkmaid

noun
woman who milks cows
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkmeɪd / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to milk cows and work in a dairy

Examples

A milkmaid milking cows
As a milkmaid, she rose early each morning to tend the cows.
In traditional stories, the milkmaid often appears as a hardworking village woman.
The old painting depicts a milkmaid carrying fresh milk in wooden pails.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dairymaid - a woman working in a dairy, especially one who milks cows
