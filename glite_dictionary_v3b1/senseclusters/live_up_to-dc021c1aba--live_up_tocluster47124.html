live up to

verb
fulfill expectations or standards
Pronunciation: / lɪv ʌp tu / (AmE), / lɪv ʌp tʊ / (BrE)

She felt pressure to live up to her parents' expectations.
The movie didn't quite live up to the hype surrounding its release.
