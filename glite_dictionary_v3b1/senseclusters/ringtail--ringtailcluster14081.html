ringtail

noun
immature golden eagle
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋ.teɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.teɪl / (BrE)
an immature golden eagle, especially before its full adult plumage develops

Examples

A young ringtail eagle
Birdwatchers often have trouble distinguishing a ringtail from similar-looking juvenile raptors.
During migration season, a keen eye can spot a ringtail gliding low over the fields.
The young ringtail soared above the canyon, distinct from the adults by its paler feathers.
