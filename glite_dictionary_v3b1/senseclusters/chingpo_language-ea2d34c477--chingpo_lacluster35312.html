Chingpo language

noun
Kachinic language (Jingpo, Myanmar region)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪŋpoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪŋpəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Kachinic language spoken primarily by the Jingpo (Kachin) people in parts of Myanmar and surrounding regions.

Examples

The Chingpo language
He is fluent in both Burmese and the Chingpo language.
Researchers are documenting the grammar and vocabulary of the Chingpo language.
The Chingpo language is spoken by many communities in northern Myanmar.
