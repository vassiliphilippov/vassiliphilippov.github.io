dreamy

adjective
pleasantly unreal
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmi / (AmE), / ˈdriːmi / (BrE)
having a pleasantly unreal, gentle, or romantic quality, like a dream

Examples

Dreamy atmosphere
The artist painted a dreamy landscape filled with soft colors and gentle light.
She spoke in a dreamy voice, as if lost in her own thoughts.
The movie's dreamy atmosphere made it feel almost magical.
The artist used soft colors to create a dreamy atmosphere in the painting.
She gazed out the window with a dreamy expression on her face.
The movie's music gave the whole scene a dreamy feeling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethereal - extremely delicate and light in a way that seems not of this world
  • synonym: imaginative - having or showing creativity or inventiveness
  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they are in real life
  • synonym: romantic - having an idealized or sentimental quality
