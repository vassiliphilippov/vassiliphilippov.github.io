prairie fire

noun
uncontrolled grassland fire
5.1 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛri ˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈpreəri ˌfaɪə / (BrE)
An uncontrolled fire that burns across a grassy area or prairie, often spreading rapidly due to wind and dry conditions.

Examples

A prairie fire spreads
A massive prairie fire swept across the plains, forcing ranchers to evacuate their cattle.
Firefighters battled the growing prairie fire as strong winds threatened nearby farms.
The dry summer made the region especially vulnerable to prairie fires starting from a single spark.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forest fire - an uncontrolled fire in a forest
  • synonym: wildfire - a large, destructive fire that spreads quickly over woodland or brush
