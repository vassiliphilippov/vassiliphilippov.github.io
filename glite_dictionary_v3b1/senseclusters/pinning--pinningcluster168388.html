pinning

noun
exclusive dating promise (US, historical)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
(historical, US) A mutual promise between a couple not to date anyone else, once especially signaled on college campuses by the giving of a fraternity pin to one's partner.

Examples

College pinning ceremony
After their pinning, everyone knew they were officially exclusive.
During the 1960s, pinning was a popular tradition among fraternity members at the university.
They announced their pinning with a small ceremony and invited their closest friends.
