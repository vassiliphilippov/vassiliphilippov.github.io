spoony

noun
sentimental lover
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːni / (AmE), / ˈspuːni / (BrE)
a person who is foolishly sentimental or overly in love, often in a silly or naive way

Examples

A spoony lover
Everyone could tell he was a spoony from the way he talked about his crush.
Her friends laughed at the spoony who wrote love poems every day.
In old novels, the spoony is often the character who falls in love too easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cynic - a person who does not believe in love or sentiment
  • synonym: romantic - a person who is very sentimental about love
