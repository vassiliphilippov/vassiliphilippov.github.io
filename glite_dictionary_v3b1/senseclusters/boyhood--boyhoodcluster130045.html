boyhood

noun
the period of being a boy
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ.hʊd / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.hʊd / (BrE)
the time in a person's life when they are a boy; the period of childhood for a male

Examples

His boyhood years
He spent his boyhood in a small seaside town.
Memories of boyhood adventures still brought a smile to his face.
The book describes the boyhood of the famous explorer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childhood - the state or period of being a child
  • antonym: manhood - the state or period of being a man
