duello

noun
duel code
9.9
Pronunciation: / duˈɛloʊ / (AmE), / djuˈɛləʊ / (BrE)
the set of rules or code that governs the conduct of a duel between two people

Examples

Duello rules
The gentlemen agreed to follow the duello before meeting at dawn for their duel.
Historians study the duello to understand how disputes were settled in the past.
He insisted that every step of the challenge comply with the traditional duello.

Related Terms

  • synonym: code duello - the formal set of rules for dueling
  • synonym: duel - a formal fight between two people with weapons
