cream

verb
defeat thoroughly (informal)
Pronunciation: / kɹim / (AmE), / kɹiːm / (BrE)
to defeat thoroughly and decisively in a competition or fight

Examples

To cream the competition
Our school creamed its rivals at the state championships this year.
The chess champion creamed his opponent in just twenty moves.
We creamed the visiting team in the final quarter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrash - to beat someone thoroughly, especially in a contest
  • synonym: trounce - to defeat heavily in a contest
