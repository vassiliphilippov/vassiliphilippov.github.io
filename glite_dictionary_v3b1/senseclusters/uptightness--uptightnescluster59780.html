uptightness

noun
state of tension
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈtaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ʌpˈtaɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being tense, nervous, or overly strict and unable to relax

Examples

His uptightness showed
His uptightness was obvious whenever he had to speak in public.
The uptightness in the office made it difficult for anyone to enjoy their work.
She tried yoga to help reduce her uptightness and feel more at ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nervousness - the state of being anxious or uneasy
  • antonym: relaxation - the state of being free from tension and anxiety
