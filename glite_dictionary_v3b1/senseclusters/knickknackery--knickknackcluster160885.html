knickknackery

noun
collection of small decorative objects
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪkˌnækəri / (AmE), / ˈnɪkˌnækəri / (BrE)
A collection or assortment of small decorative objects; knickknacks collectively.

Examples

A knickknackery collection
Her living room is filled with knickknackery collected from her travels around the world.
Modern interiors often avoid excessive knickknackery to create a clean, minimalist look.
The shop specialized in rare knickknackery and vintage curiosities.
