hunting

noun
careful search for something
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of searching carefully in order to find something or someone

Examples

Hunting for clues
After hours of hunting through old boxes, she finally found the missing photograph.
The detectives' hunting for clues led them to the abandoned warehouse.
We spent the afternoon hunting for the perfect birthday gift in the crowded mall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hiding - concealing oneself or something to avoid being found
  • synonym: searching - the act of trying to find something by looking carefully
