dispensary

noun
place where medicines are dispensed
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɛnsəri / (AmE), / dɪˈspɛnsəri / (BrE)
a place, such as in a hospital or clinic, where medicines or medical supplies are prepared and given out to patients

Examples

Hospital dispensary
After registering at the clinic, she was directed to the dispensary for her prescription.
Many rural areas rely on a local dispensary for essential drugs and basic healthcare.
The hospital's new dispensary provides free medication to low-income patients.
The new dispensary in town offers both traditional medicines and legal cannabis products.
She went to the dispensary to pick up her monthly prescription.
Many rural areas rely on a single dispensary for basic medical supplies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinic - a place where people receive medical treatment
  • synonym: pharmacy - a store where medicinal drugs are prepared and sold
