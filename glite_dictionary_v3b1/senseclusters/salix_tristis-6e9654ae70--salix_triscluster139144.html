Salix tristis

noun
species of willow shrub (eastern U.S.)
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlɪks ˈtraɪstɪs / (AmE), / ˈseɪlɪks ˈtrɪstɪs / (BrE)
A species of willow shrub native to dry areas in the eastern United States, with long, narrow leaves that are grayish beneath.
