petty officer

noun
Navy/Coast Guard noncommissioned officer
4.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.tɪ ˈɔː.fɪ.sər/ /; / /ˈpe.tɪ ˈɔː.fə.sər/ (AmE), /ˈpe.tɪ ˈɒf.ɪ.sə/ /; / /ˈpe.tɪ ˈɒf.ə.sə/ (BrE)
