wet rag

noun
an ineffectual or listless person
7.1
Pronunciation: / wɛt ræɡ / (AmE), / wɛt ræɡ / (BrE)
a person who is ineffective, lacking energy or enthusiasm, and fails to have a positive impact in social or professional situations

Examples

A wet rag person
Don't be such a wet rag—try to enjoy the party!
Everyone expected Tom to lead the project, but he turned out to be a wet rag.
His pessimistic attitude made him the wet rag of the group.

Related Terms
