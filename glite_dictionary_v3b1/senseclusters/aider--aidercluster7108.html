aider

noun
criminal assistant
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdər / (AmE), / ˈeɪdə / (BrE)
a person who assists or supports another in committing a crime or illegal act

Examples

A criminal aider
The police arrested the aider who provided the getaway car for the robbery.
In court, the prosecutor argued that the aider was just as guilty as the main offender.
A person can be charged as an aider even if they did not directly commit the crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accessory - someone who assists in a crime but is not the main offender
  • synonym: accomplice - a person who helps another commit a crime
  • antonym: principal - the main person responsible for committing a crime
