shareowner

noun
person owning company shares
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛrˌoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈʃeəˌəʊnə / (BrE)
A person who owns shares of stock in a corporation.

Examples

A shareowner of stock
As a shareowner, she receives dividends from the corporation's profits.
Every shareowner is entitled to vote at the annual general meeting.
The company sent out annual reports to each shareowner.
