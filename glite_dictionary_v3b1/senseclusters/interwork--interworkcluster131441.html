interwork

verb
to cooperate together
6.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈwɜːrk/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈwɜːk/ (BrE)
to cooperate or operate together, especially by connecting systems, organizations, or groups so they function jointly

Examples

To interwork systems
The two companies are interworking to develop a new software platform.
Successful projects often require different departments to interwork closely.
Engineers must ensure that the new devices can interwork with existing systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaborate - to work together with others to achieve a common goal
  • antonym: compete - to try to win or gain something against others
  • synonym: integrate - to combine parts so that they work together
