Arafat

proper noun
Islamic pilgrimage site
Pronunciation: / ˈærəˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈærəˌfæt / (BrE)
a hill near Mecca that is an important site for the Hajj pilgrimage in Islam

Examples

Arafat hill pilgrimage
Thousands of pilgrims gather at Arafat during the Hajj each year.
Standing on Arafat is considered a central part of the pilgrimage.
The plain of Arafat becomes crowded with worshippers on the Day of Arafah.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hajj - the annual Islamic pilgrimage to Mecca
  • synonym: Mecca - the holy city in Islam where the Hajj pilgrimage takes place
