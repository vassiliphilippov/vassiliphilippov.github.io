Eurotiales

noun
order of fungi with scattered asci
9.9
Pronunciation: / jʊəˌroʊtiˈeɪliz /; / ˌjʊəroʊˈtaɪəliːz / (AmE), / jʊəˌrɒtɪˈɑːliːz /; / ˌjʊərəʊˈtaɪəliːz / (BrE)
an order of fungi with a closed fruiting body (cleistothecium) and scattered asci, including many molds important in food and industry
