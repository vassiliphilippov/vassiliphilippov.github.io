1 Maccabees

proper noun
an Apocryphal book about the Jewish revolt
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈmækəˌbiz / (AmE), / wʌn ˈmækəˌbiːz / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book recounting the Jewish revolt against Seleucid rule, focusing on Judas Maccabaeus and events under Antiochus IV Epiphanes
