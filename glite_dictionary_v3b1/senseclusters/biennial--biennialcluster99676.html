biennial

adjective
occurring every two years
9.4
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɛniəl / (AmE), / baɪˈeniəl / (BrE)

Examples

Biennial event
Our club's biennial exhibition attracts artists from around the world every two years.
She attends a biennial family reunion that takes place in even-numbered years.
The committee holds a biennial conference to discuss major policy changes.
