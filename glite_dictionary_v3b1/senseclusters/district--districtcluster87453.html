district

noun
administrative area or region
1.5 100/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÉªstrÉªkt / (AmE), / ËˆdÉªstrÉªkt / (BrE)
an area of a country, city, or town that is marked off for administrative, governmental, or other official purposes

Examples

School district
Each district has its own local council and administrative offices.
The city was divided into several districts for the purpose of organizing emergency services.
The school is located in the financial district of the city.

Synonyms

  • administrative district
    3.9 0.07/m
    administrative district is set up by authorities for running local services, while district is a general area marked for official purposes.
  • territorial division
    5.4 0.06/m
    territorial division is a region defined by borders for government control, while district is any area marked for official purposes.
  • administrative division
    3.7 0.04/m
    administrative division is a larger unit set by government to organize services and regions, while district is a local area marked for official purposes.
  • city district
    2.8 0.10/m
    city district is an area inside a city used for local planning or services, while district can mean any marked official area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: region - a large area of land, usually without precise boundaries
  • synonym: zone - an area that is different from others in some way, often for particular use or administration
