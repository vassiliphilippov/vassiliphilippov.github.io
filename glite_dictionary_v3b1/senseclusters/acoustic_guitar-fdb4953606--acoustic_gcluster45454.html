acoustic guitar

noun
guitar without electrical amplification
3.2
Pronunciation: / əˈkuːstɪk ɡɪˈtɑr / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪk ɡɪˈtɑː /; / əˈkjuːstɪk ɡɪˈtɑː / (BrE)
a guitar that produces sound naturally through its hollow body, without electronic amplification
