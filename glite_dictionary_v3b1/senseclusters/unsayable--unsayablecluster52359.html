unsayable

adjective
cannot be retracted
8.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪəbl / (BrE)
impossible to retract or take back once it has been said

Examples

Unsayable words
Once the harsh words were spoken, they became unsayable and could not be taken back.
He regretted making an unsayable comment during the heated argument.
Some things, once revealed, are simply unsayable and change relationships forever.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreversible - impossible to change or undo
  • antonym: retractable - able to be withdrawn or taken back
