high

noun
state of intense elation or excitement
5.0
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
a feeling or state of intense excitement, happiness, or euphoria

Examples

On a high after news
After hearing the good news, he was on such a high that nothing could upset him.
The thrill of skydiving gives many people a natural high.
Winning the championship gave her an incredible high that lasted for days.
