duck off

verb
leave quickly; euphemistic command to go away
4.0 8.7
Pronunciation: /dʌk ɔːf/ /; / /dʌk ɑːf/ (AmE), /dʌk ɒf/ /; / /dʌk ɔːf/ (BrE)
