fatalize

verb
to make deadly
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to cause something to become fatal or to lead to death

Examples

To fatalize a mistake
A single misjudgment could fatalize the mission and cost the crew their lives.
The introduction of a new virus strain could fatalize what was once a mild illness.
Neglecting safety measures may fatalize an otherwise manageable situation.

Related Terms
