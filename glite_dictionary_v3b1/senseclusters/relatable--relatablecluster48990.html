relatable

adjective
easy to identify with or understand
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
easy for someone to understand or feel connected to; having qualities that people can relate to.

Examples

Relatable jokes
He always tells relatable jokes that everyone in the office enjoys.
Her story about growing up in a small town was incredibly relatable to many in the audience.
The main character's struggles made the film much more relatable to younger viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: understandable - able to be understood
  • antonym: unrelatable - difficult to relate to; not easy to identify with
