blether

verb
talk foolishly or aimlessly
9.4 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbleð.ər/ (AmE), /ˈbleð.ə/ (BrE)

Examples

Don’t mind him—he’s just blethering about the weather again.
She loves to blether on the phone for hours with her friends.
