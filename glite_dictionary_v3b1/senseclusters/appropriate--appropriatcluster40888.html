appropriate

adjective
suitable; proper for a situation
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊpriət / (AmE), / əˈprəʊpriət / (BrE)
suitable or proper for a particular situation, person, or purpose

Examples

An appropriate answer
Choose an appropriate response to each question in the exam.
It was not an appropriate time to ask for a raise.
Please wear appropriate clothing for the weather today.
It would not be appropriate to laugh during the ceremony.
Please wear appropriate clothing when visiting the museum.
The manager chose appropriate candidates for the new project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • synonym: suitable - right or appropriate for a particular person, purpose, or situation
