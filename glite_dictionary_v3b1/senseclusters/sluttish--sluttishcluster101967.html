sluttish

adjective
sexually promiscuous or disreputable (of a woman)
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌt.ɪʃ /; / ˈslʌtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈslʌt.ɪʃ /; / ˈslʌtɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a woman) behaving or appearing in a way that is considered sexually improper, promiscuous, or untidy

Examples

Sluttish behavior
Rumors spread quickly about her supposedly sluttish behavior.
She disliked being labeled as sluttish because of her lifestyle choices.
The novel depicted a sluttish character who ignored social norms.
Her sluttish behavior made her the subject of much gossip in the small town.
The critic described the character’s clothing as sluttish and in poor taste.
The landlord complained about the sluttish state in which the tenants left the apartment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chaste - abstaining from extramarital or all sexual intercourse
  • antonym: modest - behaving in a manner considered proper or decent
  • synonym: promiscuous - engaging in sexual activity with many partners
  • synonym: slovenly - untidy or careless in appearance or habits
