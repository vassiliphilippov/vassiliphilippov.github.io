dressed

adjective
wound treated and covered
3.1
Pronunciation: / drɛst / (AmE), / drɛst / (BrE)
(of a wound) treated with medications and covered with a protective dressing.

Examples

Dressed wound
All injuries must be properly dressed before transporting the casualty.
His arm was carefully dressed after the accident to keep it clean.
The nurse quickly had the patient's wound dressed to prevent infection.

  • synonym: bandaged - having a bandage applied
