perception

noun
an idea or view about something
1.0
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / pəˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
a particular way of understanding or interpreting something; an idea or view about a subject

Examples

Personal perception
Her perception of the situation was very different from mine.
Over time, his perception of the city changed completely.
The novel challenges our perceptions about good and evil.

Related Terms

  • antonym: misconception - an incorrect view or opinion
  • synonym: perspective - a particular attitude toward or way of regarding something
  • synonym: view - an opinion or way of thinking about something
