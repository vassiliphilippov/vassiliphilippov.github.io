mort

noun
death signal
Pronunciation: / mɔrt / (AmE), / mɔːt / (BrE)
an old word for death or a signal, such as a horn, announcing a death, especially after a hunt

Examples

The mort horn
The sound of the mort echoed through the village after the battle.
After the fox was killed, the huntsman blew the mort to signal the end of the hunt.
In old tales, a mort would be sounded to announce the passing of a noble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: death - the end of life
  • antonym: life - the state of being alive
