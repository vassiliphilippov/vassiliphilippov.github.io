magnate

noun
wealthy and influential businessperson
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡ.neɪt / (AmE), / ˈmæɡ.neɪt / (BrE)
a person of great wealth, influence, or power in business or industry.

Examples

A magnate in business
Many aspiring entrepreneurs dream of joining the ranks of the tech magnates.
She became a media magnate after acquiring several newspapers and television channels.
The shipping magnate donated millions of dollars to the local museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tycoon - a wealthy, powerful person in business or industry
