Hum-Vee

noun
military four-wheel drive vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌm.vi / (AmE), / ˈhʌm.vi / (BrE)
a high-mobility, multipurpose military vehicle with four-wheel drive, originally designed for the US military

Examples

A Hum-Vee vehicle
A convoy of Hum-Vees rolled into the base just before dawn.
After returning from service, he bought a civilian version of the Hum-Vee.
The soldiers drove a Hum-Vee through the desert terrain on their morning patrol.

Related Terms

  • synonym: HMMWV - acronym for High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle
  • degree lower: jeep - small, rugged four-wheel drive vehicle, military or civilian
