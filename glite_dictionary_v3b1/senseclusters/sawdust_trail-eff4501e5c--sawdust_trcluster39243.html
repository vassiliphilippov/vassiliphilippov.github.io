sawdust trail

noun
path marked with sawdust
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌdʌst treɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌdʌst treɪl / (BrE)
a path marked by a line of sawdust, typically leading to or indicating a route to a workshop or woodworking area

Examples

A sawdust trail path
A sawdust trail led from the main entrance to the carpenter's shop at the back.
The janitor swept away the sawdust trail after the carpenters finished for the day.
We followed the sawdust trail through the hallway to find the busy woodworkers.
