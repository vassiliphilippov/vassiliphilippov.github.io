overinvestment

noun
excessive commitment
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstmənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈvɛstmənt / (BrE)
excessive emotional or personal commitment to a person, project, or outcome, often leading to distress or poor judgment

Examples

Emotional overinvestment
Her overinvestment in the project made it difficult for her to accept any criticism.
He realized that his overinvestment in the outcome was causing unnecessary stress.
Therapists warn against overinvestment in relationships, as it can lead to disappointment and anxiety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detachment - a lack of emotional involvement or commitment
  • synonym: obsession - an excessive and persistent focus on something
