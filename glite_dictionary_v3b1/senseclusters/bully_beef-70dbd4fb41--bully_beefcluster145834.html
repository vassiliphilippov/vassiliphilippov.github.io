bully beef

noun
cured or pickled canned beef
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊli bif / (AmE), / ˈbʊli biːf / (BrE)
Canned beef that has been cured or pickled in brine, often found in a compressed or corned form.

Examples

Canned bully beef
During the war, soldiers often ate bully beef with hard biscuits.
He opened a can of bully beef for lunch during the camping trip.
The pantry was stocked with tins of bully beef and vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corned beef - salted and cured beef, often canned
