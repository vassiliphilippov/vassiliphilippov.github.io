mitogenic

adjective
stimulating cell division
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɪtəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
causing or stimulating cell division, especially by promoting mitosis

Examples

Mitogenic effect
The scientists observed a strong mitogenic response in the treated cells.
Certain growth factors are known for their mitogenic properties in tissue cultures.
Researchers tested whether the new drug had any mitogenic effects on human lymphocytes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antimitotic - inhibiting or preventing cell division by mitosis
  • synonym: proliferative - causing or relating to rapid cell growth or multiplication
