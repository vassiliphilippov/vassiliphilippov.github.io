orthodoxical

adjective
traditional beliefs
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrθəˈdɑːksɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈdɒksɪkəl / (BrE)

Examples

The committee favored orthodoxical solutions rather than experimenting with new ideas.
Despite his orthodoxical upbringing, he was open to alternative viewpoints.
