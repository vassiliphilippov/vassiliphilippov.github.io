Köppen

proper noun
climate classification system
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛpən / (AmE), / ˈkœpən / (BrE)
the name of a climate classification system that categorizes the world's climates based on temperature and precipitation patterns

Examples

Köppen climate system
The Köppen system is widely used in geography to describe different climate zones.
Researchers updated the Köppen classification to reflect recent climate data.
Many textbooks include maps based on the Köppen climate system.
