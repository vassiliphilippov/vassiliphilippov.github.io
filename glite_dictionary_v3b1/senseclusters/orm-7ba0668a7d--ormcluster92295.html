ORM

noun
risk management process
8.3
Pronunciation: / oʊ ɑr ɛm / (AmE), / əʊ ɑː ɛm / (BrE)
a systematic process for identifying, assessing, and controlling risks in operations, especially in military and aviation settings

Examples

ORM process
All personnel must complete ORM training before participating in field operations.
The pilot reviewed the ORM checklist to ensure all risks were addressed before takeoff.
Effective ORM can significantly reduce the likelihood of accidents during complex missions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hazard assessment - the process of evaluating potential dangers
  • synonym: risk management - the process of identifying and minimizing risks
