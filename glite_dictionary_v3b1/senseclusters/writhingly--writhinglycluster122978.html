writhingly

adverb
with visible discomfort
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪðɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈraɪðɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows extreme embarrassment, shame, or emotional discomfort.

Examples

Writhingly embarrassed
She smiled writhingly as everyone stared at the awkward gift he had given her.
He answered the question writhingly, clearly uncomfortable with the attention.
The student looked writhingly at the floor after making a mistake in front of the class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a way that shows discomfort or embarrassment
  • antonym: confidently - in a way that shows self-assurance and ease
