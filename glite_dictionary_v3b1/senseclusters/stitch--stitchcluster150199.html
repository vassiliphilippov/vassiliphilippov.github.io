Synonyms

  • sew together

    sew together is the act of joining fabric pieces by sewing along their edges, while stitch can mean a single needle loop.
  • sew
    1.0 12/m
    sew is the general activity of joining or making fabric by hand or machine, while stitch often means a small single join.
  • seam
    3.6 0.29/m
    seam is the visible line where two fabrics meet after sewing, while stitch is the single needle-made loop that makes that join.
