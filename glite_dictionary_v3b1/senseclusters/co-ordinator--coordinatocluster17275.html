co-ordinator

noun
a person who organizes people or activities
3.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ko äÀà…îrd…ôÀåne…™t…ôr / (AmE), / k…ô äÀà…îÀê.d…™.ne…™.t…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to organize people or activities so that they work together effectively and harmoniously

Examples

Project co-ordinator
As project co-ordinator, she ensures that every team member knows their tasks.
The event co-ordinator handled last-minute changes with remarkable calm.
The school hired a new co-ordinator to manage all after-school programs.

Synonyms

  • coordinator
    3.7 17/m
    coordinator is the common modern spelling used in writing to name someone who organizes teams, while co-ordinator is the hyphenated variant.
  • organizer
    2.8 11/m
    organizer plans and recruits people for events or groups, while co-ordinator focuses on making people and activities work together smoothly.
  • orchestrator
    6.4 0.09/m
    orchestrator manages many complex parts or resources like a conductor, while co-ordinator handles everyday organizing of people and tasks.
  • arranger
    3.2 0.09/m
    arranger sets details and order for an activity or plan, while co-ordinator works more with people to keep teams and tasks in harmony.
