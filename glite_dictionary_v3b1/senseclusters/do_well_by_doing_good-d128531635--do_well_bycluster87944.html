do well by doing good

verb
succeed through ethical or altruistic actions
Pronunciation: / du wɛl baɪ duːɪŋ ɡʊd / (AmE), / dəʊ wɛl baɪ duːɪŋ ɡʊd / (BrE)
to achieve success by acting ethically or helping others; to prosper through positive or altruistic actions

Examples

To do well by doing good in business
Many modern companies strive to do well by doing good, balancing profit with social responsibility.
Non-profit organizations often demonstrate that it's possible to do well by doing good.
She built her career on the principle that you can do well by doing good.
