railway system

noun
organization operating trains or rail network
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A commercial organization that operates a network for transporting passengers or goods by train.

Examples

The national railway system was modernized to improve both speed and capacity.
