railway system

noun
organization operating trains or rail network
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A commercial organization that operates a network for transporting passengers or goods by train.

Examples

European railway system
Many European countries have integrated their railway systems to allow seamless travel across borders.
The company invested heavily in upgrading its railway system for freight transportation.
The national railway system was modernized to improve both speed and capacity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bus system - organization operating networks of buses for transportation
  • synonym: railroad - commercial organization operating railway trains
