adventure

verb
to explore boldly
3.7
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃər / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃə / (BrE)
to go out and explore or try new and exciting experiences, often involving some risk or uncertainty

Examples

To adventure abroad
They decided to adventure through the rainforest despite the unpredictable weather.
Many young people dream of adventuring across different countries after graduation.
She loves to adventure into new hobbies whenever she has free time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoid - to stay away from something
  • synonym: explore - to travel through an unfamiliar area to learn about it
  • synonym: venture - to go somewhere that may be dangerous or unknown
