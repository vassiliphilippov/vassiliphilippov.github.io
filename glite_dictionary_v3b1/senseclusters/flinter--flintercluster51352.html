flinter

verb
to chip flint
9.9
to break or chip flint into flakes, especially for making stone tools

Examples

To flinter a stone
Early humans would flinter pieces of flint to create sharp blades for hunting.
Archaeologists demonstrated how to flinter a stone core to produce useful flakes.
He carefully flintered the rock, producing several thin flakes for toolmaking.
