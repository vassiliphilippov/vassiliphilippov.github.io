public opinion

noun
collective beliefs of the majority
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌblɪk əˈpɪnjən / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪk əˈpɪnjən / (BrE)
the collective beliefs, views, or attitudes held by most people in a society about a particular topic or issue

Examples

Public opinion polls

The results of the survey reflected a shift in public opinion about environmental issues.
Changes in public opinion can influence election outcomes.
The government often surveys public opinion before implementing new policies.
The media play a crucial role in shaping public opinion.
