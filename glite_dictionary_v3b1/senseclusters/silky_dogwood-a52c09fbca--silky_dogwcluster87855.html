silky dogwood

noun
shrub with purplish stems and blue fruit
7.9 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪl.kiː ˈdɔː.gwʊd/ /; / /ˈsɪl.kiː ˈdɑː.gwʊd/ (AmE), /ˈsɪl.kiː ˈdɒ.gwʊd/ (BrE)
a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America, with silky or purplish stems, blue fruit, and often found near water
