glooming

adjective
depressingly dark or shadowy
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
Dark or shadowy in a way that causes sadness or fear; depressingly dark.

Examples

The glooming streets
Fog drifted through the glooming streets as dusk fell.
The tale unfolds in a glooming castle, haunted by shadows and whispers.
We entered the glooming corridor and shivered at the silence inside.
