long horse

noun
elongated gymnastic vaulting apparatus
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ hɔrs / (AmE), / lɒŋ hɔːs / (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus without pommels, elongated at one end, used lengthwise for vaulting

Examples

The long horse apparatus
Coaches ensure that the long horse is stable before any athlete attempts a jump.
Modern competitions rarely use the long horse, having replaced it with a safer vaulting table.
The gymnast sprinted toward the long horse and performed a flawless handspring vault.
