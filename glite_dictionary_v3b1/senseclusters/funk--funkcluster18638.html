funk

noun
rhythmic music style blending jazz, blues, and soul
5.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /fʌŋk/ (AmE), /fʌŋk/ (BrE)
A style of music that blends jazz, blues, and soul, characterized by a strong rhythmic groove and heavy bass line, often emphasizing the first beat of each bar.

Examples

Funk music concert
Her love of funk music is reflected in her energetic dance moves.
The band's new album mixes traditional jazz with funk to create a modern sound.
They played a set full of classic funk tunes from the 1970s.
