uncoded

adjective
not marked by code
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkoʊdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkəʊdɪd / (BrE)
not marked or indicated by a specific code, symbol, or grammatical marker in language or communication

Examples

Uncoded language features
In this language, gender is often uncoded and must be inferred from context.
The message was left uncoded, making it easy for anyone to read.
Some social meanings remain uncoded in speech but are understood by native speakers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unmarked - not explicitly indicated or distinguished
