insertable

adjective
able to be inserted
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɜrtəbl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be put or fitted into something else

Examples

Insertable battery
The new battery is insertable and can be replaced easily by the user.
This type of filter is insertable into most standard water bottles.
The device comes with an insertable memory card for extra storage.
