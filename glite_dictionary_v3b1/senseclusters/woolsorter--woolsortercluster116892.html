woolsorter

noun
person who sorts wool by grade
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlˌsɔrtər / (AmE), / ˈwʊlˌsɔːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to separate wool into different grades based on quality and characteristics

Examples

A woolsorter at work
After years as a woolsorter, his expertise in grading fleeces was second to none.
Becoming a woolsorter required both experience and a keen eye for detail.
The mill employed several woolsorters to ensure only the finest wool was used for their yarns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wool-stapler - a dealer who buys and sells wool, sometimes grading it
