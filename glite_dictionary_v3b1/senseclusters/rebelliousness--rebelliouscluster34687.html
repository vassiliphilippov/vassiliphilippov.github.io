rebelliousness

noun
the state of being rebellious
1.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈbɛliəsnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈbɛliəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unwilling to obey authority, rules, or conventions; a tendency to resist control

Examples

Her rebelliousness behavior
Her rebelliousness often got her into trouble with teachers at school.
Overcoming his teenage rebelliousness was not easy for his parents.
The film explores the rebelliousness of youth during times of social change.
During her teenage years, her rebelliousness often clashed with her parents' strict rules.
His rebelliousness made him a difficult student for teachers who valued obedience.
The artist's rebelliousness is evident in her unconventional style and bold statements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defiance - open resistance or bold disobedience
  • antonym: obedience - the act of obeying authority or rules
  • synonym: rebellion - an act of violent or open resistance to authority
