rep

noun
informal short for reputation (standing)
7.9
Pronunciation: /rep/ (AmE), /rep/ (BrE)
Informal. A person's reputation or standing, especially their credibility within a group or online community.

Examples

Good rep
After selling rare cards honestly, he built a strong rep among collectors.
Her rep as a reliable coder helped her get invited to the open-source project.
On the forum, users with high rep often have more influence on decisions.
