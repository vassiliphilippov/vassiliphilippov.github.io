Swedish mile

noun
Swedish unit of length; 10 km
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswidɪʃ maɪl / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ maɪl / (BrE)
a unit of length used in Sweden, equal to 10 kilometers

Examples

A Swedish mile
A Swedish mile is ten kilometers, not the same as an English mile.
He ran five Swedish miles during his morning training session.
The marathon in Sweden covered more than four Swedish miles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kilometer - unit of length equal to 1,000 meters
  • synonym: mile - unit of distance equal to about 1,609 meters
