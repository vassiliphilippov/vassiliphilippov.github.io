wriggler

noun
mosquito larva
8.3 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪg.lər/ (AmE), /ˈrɪg.lə/ (BrE)
a small creature or larva, especially a mosquito larva, that moves with twisting or wriggling motions in water
