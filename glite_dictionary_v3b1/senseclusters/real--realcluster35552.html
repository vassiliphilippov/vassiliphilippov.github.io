real

adjective
relating to land and immovable property
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈriːəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl / (BrE)
(of property) consisting of land and buildings; immovable property as opposed to personal property.

Examples

Real estate property
After her grandfather died, she inherited several acres of real estate.
In legal terms, real property includes land and anything attached to it.
The government imposed taxes on both personal and real property.

Related Terms

  • antonym: personal - (as in personal property) moveable property, opposite of 'real' property
