emplacement

noun
act of placing something in position
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
the act of putting something in a particular place or position, especially for a specific purpose

Examples

Emplacement of equipment
Following the emplacement of the new computers, productivity increased noticeably.
The emplacement of artillery along the ridge was completed overnight.
The emplacement of the sculpture in the garden required careful planning.

  • synonym: placement - the action of putting something in a particular place
