kraft

noun
strong brown wrapping paper
7.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /kræft/ (AmE), /kræft/ (BrE)
A strong brown wrapping paper made from wood pulp that has been processed with a sulfur solution, commonly used for packaging and paper bags.

Examples

Brown kraft paper
He wrapped the gifts in plain kraft for a rustic look.
The books were carefully packed in kraft to prevent damage during shipping.
We use kraft for making sturdy paper bags at the grocery store.
