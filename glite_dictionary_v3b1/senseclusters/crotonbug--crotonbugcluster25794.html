crotonbug

noun
a small household cockroach pest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊtənˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊtənˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
A small, light-brown cockroach, originally brought to the United States from Europe, commonly found in households as a pest.
