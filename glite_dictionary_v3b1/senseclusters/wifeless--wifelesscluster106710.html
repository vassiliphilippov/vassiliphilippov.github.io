wifeless

adjective
without a wife
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪfləs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪfləs / (BrE)

The novel tells the story of a wifeless man searching for companionship.
He remained wifeless throughout his life, focusing on his career and hobbies.
