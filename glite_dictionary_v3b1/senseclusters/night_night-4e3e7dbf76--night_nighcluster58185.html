night night

interjection
informal goodnight, esp. to children
Pronunciation: / naɪt naɪt / (AmE), / naɪt naɪt / (BrE)
A casual and affectionate way of saying goodnight, especially to children, before sleep.

Examples

Say night night
Every evening, the twins cheerfully shout 'night night' to their grandparents on the phone.
She tucked her son in and quietly said, 'night night, sleep well.'
With a wave and a smile, he wished his friends 'night night' before heading upstairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goodnight - conventional expression used before going to bed or parting at night
