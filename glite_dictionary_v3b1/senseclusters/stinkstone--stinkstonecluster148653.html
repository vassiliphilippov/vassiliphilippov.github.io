stinkstone

noun
foul-smelling rock
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a type of rock that emits a foul odor, especially when struck or rubbed

Examples

A stinkstone rock
When the geologist broke the stinkstone, a strong odor filled the laboratory.
Collectors are often surprised by the smell released from stinkstone samples.
The museum displayed a piece of stinkstone alongside other unusual minerals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitumen - a black, sticky substance found in some rocks and used in road surfacing
  • synonym: limestone - a sedimentary rock mainly composed of calcium carbonate
