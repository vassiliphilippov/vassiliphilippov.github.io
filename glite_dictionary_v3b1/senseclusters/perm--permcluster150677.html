perm

noun
hair style with permanent waves or curls
6.5
Pronunciation: / pɜrm / (AmE), / pɜːm / (BrE)
a hairstyle created by chemically treating and setting the hair to produce permanent waves or curls

Examples

A curly perm
After having a perm, her straight hair became full of bouncy curls.
His mother wore her hair in a tight perm throughout the 1980s.
She decided to get a perm before her high school reunion.
