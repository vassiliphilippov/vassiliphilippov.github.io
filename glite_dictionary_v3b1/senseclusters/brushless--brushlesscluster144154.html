brushless

adjective
without brush
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃləs / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃləs / (BrE)
having no brush, shrubs, or undergrowth; cleared of brushy vegetation.

Examples

Brushless hillside
After hours of work, the hillside was finally brushless and ready for construction.
The park maintenance team keeps the walking trails brushless for visitors' safety.
Farmers prefer brushless fields to make planting and harvesting easier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clear - free from anything that makes movement or progress difficult
  • antonym: overgrown - covered with plants, especially in an uncontrolled way
