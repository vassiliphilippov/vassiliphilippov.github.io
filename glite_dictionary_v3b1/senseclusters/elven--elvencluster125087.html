elven

adjective
elf-like qualities
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlvən / (AmE), / ˈɛlvən / (BrE)
relating to elves or having qualities associated with elves, such as being small, delicate, graceful, or otherworldly (especially in fantasy)

Examples

Elven grace
The dancer moved with an elven grace that captivated the entire audience.
Her elven features made her look almost magical in the soft morning light.
He wore an elven costume to the fantasy-themed party, complete with pointed ears.
The elven city shimmered beneath the ancient trees.
She wore an elven cloak that seemed to blend into the forest.
Legends spoke of elven magic hidden deep within the mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elfin - resembling or characteristic of an elf
  • synonym: elfish - resembling or characteristic of an elf
  • antonym: human - having qualities typical of people, not elves
