Synonyms

  • refit
    3.9 0.1/m
    refit means replacing or repairing parts to make a ship or machine usable again, while retrofit adds new parts to update older equipment.
  • upgrade
    3.5 10/m
    upgrade usually means improving something to a newer version or quality, while retrofit focuses on adding new parts to older items.
  • modernize
    4.0 5/m
    modernize means making systems or buildings more current in style or function, while retrofit adds specific new parts to old equipment.
  • update
    2.8 30/m
    update often means adding new information or small improvements, while retrofit involves fitting new physical parts into older machines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: replace - to put something new or different in the place of something else
