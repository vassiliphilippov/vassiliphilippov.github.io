Boston terrier

noun
small American dog breed with short coat and flat face
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑstən ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈbɒstən ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a small breed of American terrier with a short, smooth brindle or black coat and distinctive white markings, known for its pug-like face

Examples

A Boston terrier dog
Many families choose the Boston terrier because of its gentle temperament and moderate size.
She adopted a Boston terrier puppy from a local animal shelter.
The Boston terrier is known for its friendly personality and tuxedo-like coat.
