clingfilm

noun
plastic film for wrapping food
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪŋˌfɪlm / (AmE), / ˈklɪŋˌfɪlm / (BrE)
a thin, transparent plastic film that sticks to itself, used for wrapping and covering food to keep it fresh

Examples

A roll of clingfilm
Cover the bowl with clingfilm to keep the salad fresh.
Do we have any clingfilm for sealing these sandwiches?
She wrapped the leftover cake in clingfilm before putting it in the fridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plastic wrap - thin clear plastic film for wrapping food (American English)
