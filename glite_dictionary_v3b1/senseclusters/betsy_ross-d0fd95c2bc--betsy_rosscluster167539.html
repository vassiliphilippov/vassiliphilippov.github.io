Betsy Ross

proper noun
legendary maker of the first American flag
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛtsi rɑs / (AmE), / ˈbɛtsi rɒs / (BrE)
(1752–1836) An American seamstress who, according to legend, made the first American flag at the request of George Washington.
