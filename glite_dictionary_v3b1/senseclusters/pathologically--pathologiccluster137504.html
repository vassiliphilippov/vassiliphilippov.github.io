pathologically

adverb
in a way relating to disease or pathology
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or caused by disease or pathology; (also) in a way that is extreme or abnormal.

Examples

To be pathologically obsessed
He was pathologically obsessed with cleanliness, washing his hands dozens of times a day.
The researchers found pathologically interesting results in the brain scans of the patients.
The tissue samples were pathologically examined for any signs of cancer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abnormally - in a way that is not normal; deviating from the typical
