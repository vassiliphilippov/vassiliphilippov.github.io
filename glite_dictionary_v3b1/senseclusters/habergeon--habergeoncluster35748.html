habergeon

noun
light sleeveless chain mail armor
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhæbərˌdʒən / (AmE), / ˈhabədʒən / (BrE)
A light, sleeveless coat of chain mail, worn under the hauberk as armor in the Middle Ages.

Examples

A habergeon coat

The knight wore a habergeon beneath his tunic for extra protection.
