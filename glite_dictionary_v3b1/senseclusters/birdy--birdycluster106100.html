birdy

adjective
like a bird
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdi / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a bird, especially in appearance, sound, or behavior

Examples

Birdy voice
Her voice had a birdy quality that was light and cheerful.
The child wore a birdy costume with feathers and wings for the school play.
He made a birdy gesture, flapping his arms and pretending to fly.
