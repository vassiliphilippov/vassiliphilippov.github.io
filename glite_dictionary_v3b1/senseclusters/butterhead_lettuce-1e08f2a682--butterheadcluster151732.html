butterhead lettuce

noun
lettuce variety with soft, loose leaves

Pronunciation: /ˈbʌt.ər.hed ˈlet.ɪs/ /; / /ˈbʌt.ər.hed ˈlet.əs/ (AmE), /ˈbʌt.ə.hed ˈlet.ɪs/ /; / /ˈbʌt.ə.hed ˈlet.əs/ (BrE)
