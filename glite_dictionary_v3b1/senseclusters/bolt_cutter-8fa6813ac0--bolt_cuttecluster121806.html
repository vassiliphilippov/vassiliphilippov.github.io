bolt cutter

noun
tool for cutting bolts or metal chains
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlt ˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlt ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a heavy-duty tool with long handles and strong jaws used for cutting through metal bolts, chains, or padlocks

Examples

Heavy bolt cutters

Security personnel used a bolt cutter to remove the old, rusted chain.
