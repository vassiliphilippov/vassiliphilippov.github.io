halfbeak

noun
a fish with a long lower jaw
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌbik / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌbiːk / (BrE)
a tropical or subtropical fish with an elongated body and a distinctive long, protruding lower jaw, found in marine and freshwater environments

Examples

A halfbeak fish
A halfbeak can be recognized by its extended lower jaw and slender body.
Scientists observed halfbeak populations thriving in both saltwater and freshwater habitats.
The aquarium had several brightly colored halfbeaks swimming near the surface.
