overburdensome

adjective
excessively demanding
7.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàb…úrdns…ôm / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàb…úÀêdns…ôm / (BrE)

Examples

An overburdensome task

Many students found the new homework policy overburdensome and stressful.
The cost of compliance was overburdensome for local businesses.
