overburdensome

adjective
excessively demanding
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbɜrdnsəm / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbɜːdnsəm / (BrE)
causing too much difficulty, trouble, or stress to be managed easily

Examples

An overburdensome task
The paperwork requirements became overburdensome for the small charity to handle.
Many students found the new homework policy overburdensome and stressful.
The cost of compliance was overburdensome for local businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: onerous - involving a great deal of effort or difficulty
  • synonym: oppressive - causing discomfort or hardship
