offeror

noun
person or party making an offer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfərər / (AmE), / ˈɒfərə / (BrE)
a person or party who makes an offer, especially in a legal or business context

Examples

The offeror makes an offer
An offeror must communicate the terms clearly to create a valid contract.
If the offeror dies before acceptance, the offer usually lapses.
The offeror has the right to withdraw the proposal before acceptance.
