forsakenness

noun
state of abandonment
9.9
Pronunciation: /fɔːr.ˈseɪk.nəs/ /; / /fər.ˈseɪk.nəs/ (AmE), /fə.ˈseɪk.nəs/ /; / /fɔː.ˈseɪk.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of being completely abandoned, especially by God or a higher power, leading to spiritual desolation

Examples

Spiritual forsakenness
