entorhinal

adjective
brain region; memory
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntoʊˈraɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌɛntəˈraɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to a region of the brain, the entorhinal cortex, which is involved in memory and navigation

Examples

Entorhinal cortex region
Damage to the entorhinal area can lead to problems with memory and spatial awareness.
Researchers studied the entorhinal cortex to understand how the brain processes navigation.
The entorhinal region is often affected in the early stages of Alzheimer's disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hippocampal - relating to the hippocampus, a brain region involved in memory
