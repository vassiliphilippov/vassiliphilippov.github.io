glissade

noun
gliding step in ballet
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ɡlɪ.ˈseɪd/ /; / /ɡlɪ.ˈsɑːd/ (AmE), /ɡlɪ.ˈsɑːd/ /; / /ɡlɪ.ˈseɪd/ (BrE)
a gliding or sliding step in ballet or dance, where one foot slides along the floor before both feet come together, or any smooth gliding movement in dance
