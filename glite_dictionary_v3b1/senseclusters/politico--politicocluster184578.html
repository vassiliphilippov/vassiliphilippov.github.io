politico

noun
person involved in party politics
9.4
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪtɪˌkoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a politician or someone actively involved in politics (sometimes used with a negative or critical tone)

Related Terms

  • synonym: statesman - an experienced and respected political leader
