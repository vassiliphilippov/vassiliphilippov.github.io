expander

noun
device for widening
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspændər / (AmE), / ɪkˈspændə / (BrE)
a device or tool used to make something larger or wider

Examples

Dental expander
The orthodontist placed an expander in the child's mouth to help widen the upper jaw.
A pipe expander is used to increase the diameter of metal tubes in plumbing.
The technician adjusted the expander to make sure the opening was the correct size.
The orthodontist placed an expander in the patient's mouth to help widen the upper jaw.
A chest expander is often used in physical therapy to improve breathing capacity.
Engineers used a pipe expander to increase the diameter of the metal tubing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compressor - a device that makes something smaller or more compact
  • synonym: stretcher - a device used to stretch or widen something
