well-informed

adjective
having thorough knowledge
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛl ɪnˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ɪnˈfɔːmd / (BrE)

Examples

A well-informed person

She consulted several sources to become well-informed before making her decision.
The well-informed speaker answered every question with confidence.
