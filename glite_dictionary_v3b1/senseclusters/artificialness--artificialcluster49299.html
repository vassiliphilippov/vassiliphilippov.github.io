artificialness

noun
lack of genuineness
7.3
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.tɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃəl.nəs/ /; / /ɑːr.tə.ˈfɪ.ʃəl.nəs/ (AmE), /ɑː.tɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃəl.nəs/ /; / /ɑː.tə.ˈfɪ.ʃəl.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of appearing insincere, affected, or not genuine in manner, speech, or style

Examples

Artificialness in her smile
Her polite tone had a certain artificialness that made her apology seem unconvincing.
The artificialness of the decorations gave the party a cold and impersonal atmosphere.
Critics noted the artificialness in his performance, saying it lacked true emotion.
