Lucite

noun
transparent acrylic resin brand (plastic)
Pronunciation: / ˈluː.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈluː.saɪt / (BrE)
a transparent, durable acrylic resin used in making plastic objects, notably for glass substitutes, jewelry, and other products

Examples

Colorful Lucite jewelry
Many vintage handbags from the 1950s were crafted from colorful Lucite.
The designer created a stunning table out of Lucite that seemed to float in the room.
Using Lucite panels allows light to pass through while providing structural support.

  • synonym: Plexiglas - a brand of similar transparent acrylic plastic
