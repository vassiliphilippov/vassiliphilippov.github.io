haemostasia

noun
surgical stopping of blood flow
6.6 9.0
Pronunciation: /hiː.mə.ˈsteɪ.zi.ə/ /; / /hiː.moʊ.ˈsteɪ.zi.ə/ (AmE), /hiː.mə.ˈsteɪ.zi.ə/ /; / /hiː.mə.ˈsteɪ.ʒə/ (BrE)
