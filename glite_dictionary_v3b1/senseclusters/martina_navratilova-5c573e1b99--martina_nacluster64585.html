Martina Navratilova

proper noun
Czech-American tennis player, Wimbledon record holder
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈtinə ˌnæv.rə.tɪˈloʊ.və / (AmE), / mɑːˈtiːnə ˌnav.rə.tɪˈləʊ.və / (BrE)
A Czech-American former tennis player, considered one of the greatest female tennis players of all time, who won a record nine Wimbledon singles titles.
