MPC

noun
central bank committee
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɛm pi si / (AmE), / ɛm piː siː / (BrE)
a group within a central bank responsible for setting interest rates and guiding monetary policy

Examples

Central bank MPC
The MPC meets regularly to decide whether to change the country's interest rates.
Analysts closely watch the MPC for signals about future economic policy.
After the announcement, the MPC faced questions from the media about its decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: FOMC - Federal Open Market Committee, the US central bank's policy-setting group
  • synonym: Monetary Policy Committee - a committee in a central bank that sets interest rates and monetary policy
