autobiographically

adverb
in a personal life story
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or is based on a person's own life story or experiences

Examples

Paint autobiographically
The artist painted autobiographically, using scenes from her childhood as inspiration.
He often writes autobiographically about his experiences growing up in a small town.
The film tells its story autobiographically, reflecting the director's own journey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fictionally - in a way that is invented or imagined
  • synonym: personally - in a way that relates to oneself
