frozen

adjective
not available for use (esp. assets/funds)
2.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfroʊ.zən/ (AmE), /ˈfrəʊ.zən/ (BrE)
(of assets, funds, etc.) not available for use or not convertible to cash, often because of legal restrictions or rulings.

Examples

Frozen bank accounts
Due to ongoing litigation, their bank accounts remain completely frozen.
She could not pay her bills because her accounts were frozen by the authorities.
The court order left the company's frozen assets inaccessible to its owners.

  • antonym: available - able to be used or obtained
