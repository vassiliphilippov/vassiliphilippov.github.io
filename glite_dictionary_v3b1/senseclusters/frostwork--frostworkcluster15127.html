frostwork

noun
delicate cave crystals
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstwɝk / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstwɜːk / (BrE)
a cave formation made of delicate, needle-like or feathery mineral crystals that resemble frost

Examples

Cave frostwork
The explorers marveled at the sparkling frostwork covering the walls of the hidden cave.
Delicate frostwork formed by aragonite crystals gave the cavern an otherworldly appearance.
Photographers traveled far to capture the beauty of the rare frostwork formations deep underground.
