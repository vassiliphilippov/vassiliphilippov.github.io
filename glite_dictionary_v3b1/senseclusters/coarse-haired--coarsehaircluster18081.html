coarse-haired

adjective
having rough, thick hair or fur
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrs hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːs heəd / (BrE)
having hair or fur that is rough and thick in texture

Examples

Coarse-haired rabbits
Certain breeds of rabbits are coarse-haired, making their fur less suitable for clothing.
She owned a coarse-haired dog that hardly shed any fur in the house.
The coarse-haired terrier is known for its wiry and dense coat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth-haired - having hair or fur that is smooth in texture
  • antonym: soft-haired - having hair or fur that is soft in texture
