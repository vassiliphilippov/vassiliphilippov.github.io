griper

noun
tool for gripping
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪpər / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪpə / (BrE)
a tool or device used for holding, grasping, or lifting objects securely

Examples

Metal griper
The mechanic reached for a griper to hold the pipe steady while he worked.
She used a griper to pick up the broken glass without cutting herself.
A specialized griper made it easy to lift the heavy lid safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clamp - a device used to hold objects tightly together
  • synonym: gripper - a tool or mechanism for grasping or holding
  • antonym: release - to let go or set free from being held
