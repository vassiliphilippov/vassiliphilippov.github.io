defluvium

noun
loss of fluids or hair
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfluːviəm / (AmE), / dɪˈfluːviəm / (BrE)
a flowing off or out, especially the loss of bodily fluids or hair from the body

Examples

Hair defluvium
After the high fever, the patient experienced defluvium and noticed significant hair loss.
Doctors monitored the defluvium of fluids to assess the severity of the illness.
Temporary defluvium can occur after stressful events or certain medical treatments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: retention - the act of keeping or holding onto something
  • synonym: shedding - the process of losing or discarding something, such as hair or skin
