articulate

verb
express clearly in words
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪkjəˌleɪt /; / ɑːrˈtɪkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to express ideas, feelings, or thoughts clearly and effectively in speech or writing

Examples

To articulate your ideas
It is important to articulate your goals when starting a new project.
She struggled to articulate her thoughts during the interview.
The professor articulated his vision for the new program clearly and confidently.
Despite being nervous, he articulated his ideas with confidence during the interview.
It is important for leaders to articulate their vision clearly to their teams.
She was able to articulate her concerns about the project in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: express - to communicate thoughts or feelings in words or by gestures and conduct
  • antonym: mumble - to speak unclearly so that words are difficult to understand
