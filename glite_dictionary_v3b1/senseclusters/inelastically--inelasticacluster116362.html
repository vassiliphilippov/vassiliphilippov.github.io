inelastically

adverb
without flexibility
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɪˈlæstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪˈlæstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that does not stretch, compress, or change easily in response to force or demand

Examples

Bends inelastically
The metal rod bent inelastically under the heavy load, showing no sign of returning to its original shape.
In economics, demand for basic necessities often responds inelastically to changes in price.
The fabric stretched inelastically, making it unsuitable for sportswear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elastically - in a way that stretches or changes easily in response to force
  • synonym: rigidly - in a stiff or inflexible manner
