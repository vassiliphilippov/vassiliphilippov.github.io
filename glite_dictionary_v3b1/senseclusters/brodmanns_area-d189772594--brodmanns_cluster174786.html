Brodmann's area

noun
region of the cerebral cortex defined by cellular structure
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑd.mənz ˈɛr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈbrɒd.mənz ˈeə.ri.ə / (BrE)
any of the regions of the cerebral cortex mapped and numbered by Korbinian Brodmann according to their cellular structure and organization

Examples

Brodmann's area 17
Damage to Brodmann's area 17 can severely affect visual processing.
Researchers often refer to Brodmann's area numbers to describe specific regions in brain studies.
The prefrontal cortex includes several Brodmann's areas involved in complex cognitive functions.
