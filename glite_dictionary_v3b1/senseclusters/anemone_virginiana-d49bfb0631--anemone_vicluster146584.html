A species of perennial flowering plant, commonly known as thimbleweed, native to central and eastern North America.

Examples

Anemone virginiana plant
Anemone virginiana is often found in woodlands and along roadsides in eastern North America.
Gardeners sometimes plant Anemone virginiana because of its attractive, thimble-shaped seed heads.
The delicate white flowers of Anemone virginiana bloom in early summer.
