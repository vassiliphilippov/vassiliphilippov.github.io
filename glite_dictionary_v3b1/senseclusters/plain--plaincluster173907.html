plain

adjective
not beautiful or attractive
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /pleɪn/ (AmE), /pleɪn/ (BrE)
(of a person or thing) not beautiful or attractive in appearance; lacking in physical beauty or pleasing proportion.

Examples

Plain face
Despite being a plain woman, she had a captivating personality that drew others to her.
He sometimes felt self-conscious about his plain appearance, but his confidence overcame it.
The house looked rather plain compared to the colorful buildings on the street.
