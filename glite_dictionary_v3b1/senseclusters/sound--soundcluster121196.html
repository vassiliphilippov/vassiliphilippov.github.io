sound

noun
an audible event or occurrence
2.0
Pronunciation: / saʊnd / (AmE), / saʊnd / (BrE)
something that can be heard; a noise, vibration, or auditory effect

Examples

A loud sound
A sudden sound rang out in the middle of the night.
She was awakened by the sound of thunder crashing overhead.
The loud sound outside startled everyone in the room.
Even in complete darkness, he could recognize the familiar sound of his dog's bark.
She awoke suddenly to the sound of footsteps outside her window.
The sound of laughter filled the room during the party.
She missed the comforting sound of her mother’s voice.
The sound of rain on the roof helped me fall asleep.
The forest was filled with the sound of birds singing at dawn.

  • degree higher: phoneme - the smallest distinctive sound unit in a language
