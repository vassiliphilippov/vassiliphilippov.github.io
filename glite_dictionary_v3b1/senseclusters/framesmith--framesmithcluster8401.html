framesmith

noun
frame maker or repairer
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪmˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈfreɪmˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs frames, such as for pictures or windows

Examples

The framesmith repairs frames
The framesmith carefully restored the antique picture frame.
If your window is broken, a skilled framesmith can repair the frame for you.
She apprenticed with a local framesmith to learn the art of making custom frames.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter - a person who builds or repairs wooden structures
  • synonym: framer - a person who constructs or assembles frames
