stipo

noun
Italian antique cabinet
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈstiːpəʊ / (BrE)
an antique Italian cabinet with drawers and compartments, often richly decorated and used for storing valuables

Collectors prize a well-preserved stipo for its intricate woodwork and historical value.
The ornate stipo in the corner was used to store important documents and jewelry.
