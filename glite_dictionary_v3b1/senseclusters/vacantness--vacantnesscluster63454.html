vacantness

noun
blankness of mind
6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈveɪ.kənt.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈveɪ.kənt.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of having no thoughts, awareness, or visible emotion; blankness of expression or mind
