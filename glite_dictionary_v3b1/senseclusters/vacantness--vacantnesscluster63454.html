vacantness

noun
blankness of mind
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪkəntnəs / (AmE), / ˈveɪkəntnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having no thoughts, awareness, or visible emotion; blankness of expression or mind

Examples

Vacantness in her eyes
There was a strange vacantness in her eyes as she stared out the window.
His vacantness during the meeting made it clear he was not paying attention.
The actor portrayed the character's vacantness with a blank expression and slow movements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awareness - the state of being conscious or attentive
  • synonym: blankness - the quality of lacking expression or content
  • synonym: emptiness - the state of containing nothing or lacking meaning
