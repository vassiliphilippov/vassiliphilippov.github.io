humblingly

adverb
in a modest way
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmblɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhʌmblɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that causes someone to feel less proud or important

Examples

To humblingly accept criticism
He accepted the criticism humblingly, determined to improve his work.
The team listened humblingly as their coach pointed out their mistakes.
She spoke humblingly about her achievements, always giving credit to others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modestly - in a way that is not boastful or proud
  • antonym: proudly - in a way that shows pride or self-importance
