publishing

noun
business of issuing printed materials
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or process of preparing and issuing books, magazines, or other printed material for sale or distribution

Examples

A publishing house
Many authors dream of signing a contract with a major publishing house.
She has worked in publishing for more than a decade.
The publishing industry is facing challenges from digital media.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broadcasting - the business of transmitting programs by radio or television
  • synonym: printing - the process of producing text and images on paper
