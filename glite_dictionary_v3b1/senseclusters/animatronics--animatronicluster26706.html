animatronics

noun
lifelike robotic figures for entertainment
8.6 6.3
Pronunciation: /æn.ɪ.mə.ˈtrɑːn.ɪks/ /; / /æn.ɪ.mə.ˈtrɔːn.ɪks/ (AmE), /æn.ɪ.mə.ˈtrɒn.ɪks/ (BrE)
the technique of making and operating lifelike robots, especially of animals or people, often used in entertainment settings such as films and theme parks

Examples
