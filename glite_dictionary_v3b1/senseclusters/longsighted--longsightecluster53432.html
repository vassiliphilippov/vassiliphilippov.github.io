longsighted

adjective
showing foresight, planning for the future
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
having the ability to consider and plan prudently for the future; showing foresight.

Examples

Longsighted planning
A longsighted strategy can bring lasting benefits to future generations.
Governments need longsighted leaders to address emerging global challenges.
Her longsighted approach helped the company avoid many financial pitfalls.
