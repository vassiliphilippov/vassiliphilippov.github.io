scatter rug

noun
small movable rug
4.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈskæt.ər rʌg/ (AmE), /ˈskæt.ə rʌg/ (BrE)
a small, movable rug used to cover a part of a floor, often one of several in a room

Examples

A scatter rug
Each child picked out a scatter rug for their own part of the playroom.
She placed a colorful scatter rug by the door to welcome guests.
The wooden floor was cold, so I added a few scatter rugs around the bed.

Related Terms
