wintertide

noun
poetic winter season
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntərˌtaɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɪntətaɪd / (BrE)
an old-fashioned or poetic word for the winter season

Examples

The wintertide season
The old poem describes the beauty of wintertide with snow covering the fields.
During wintertide, the village was quiet and blanketed in white.
They spent many evenings by the fire throughout the long wintertide.

Related Terms

  • antonym: summertime - the season of summer
  • synonym: winter - the coldest season of the year
