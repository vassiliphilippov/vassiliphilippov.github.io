administratrix

noun
female estate manager
9.6
Pronunciation: /əd.mɪn.ɪ.ˈstreɪ.trɪks/ /; / /æd.mɪn.ɪ.ˈstreɪ.trɪks/ (AmE), /əd.mɪn.ɪ.ˈstreɪ.trɪks/ /; / /æd.mɪn.ɪ.ˈstreɪ.trɪks/ (BrE)
a woman appointed by a court to manage the estate of someone who has died

Examples

The administratrix of estate
The court appointed her as the administratrix of her father's estate.
As administratrix, she was responsible for settling all outstanding debts.
The lawyer explained the duties of an administratrix to the family.
The court appointed an administratrix to handle the estate after the owner's death.
As the administratrix of the charity, she ensured all records were kept in order.
Historically, the term administratrix was used for women managing legal affairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - a person appointed by a court to manage the estate of someone who has died
  • synonym: executor - a person named in a will to carry out the instructions of the deceased
