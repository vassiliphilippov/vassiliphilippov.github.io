service department

noun
business division handling customer services
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝvɪs dɪˌpɑɹtmənt /; / ˈsɝːvɪs dɪˌpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs dɪˌpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the part of a business or organization that provides customer service, support, or repairs

Examples

The service department
He got a job in the service department of a large electronics retailer.
If your washing machine breaks down, contact the service department for repairs.
The service department handles all customer complaints and warranty issues.
Customers praised the professionalism of the service department at the dealership.
If your vehicle needs an oil change, please contact our service department.
The service department inspected my car and replaced the faulty brake pads.
