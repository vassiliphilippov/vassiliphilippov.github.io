grrrr

verb
to growl in anger
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɜr / (AmE), / ɡɜː / (BrE)
to make a low, growling sound to show anger, annoyance, or frustration, often imitating an animal.

Examples

To grrrr like a dog
When he saw the mess in the kitchen, he could not help but grrrr in frustration.
The dog began to grrrr when a stranger approached the gate.
She let out a quiet grrrr after losing the game for the third time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: growl - to make a low, threatening sound like an angry animal
  • synonym: snarl - to make an aggressive growling sound
