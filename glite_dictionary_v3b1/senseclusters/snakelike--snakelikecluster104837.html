snakelike

adjective
resembling a snake in form or movement
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsneɪkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsneɪk.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling a snake in shape or movement; long, slender, and curving like a serpent.

Examples

A snakelike trail
A snakelike trail wound its way along the forest floor.
Her snakelike movements made her an excellent dancer.
The river followed a snakelike course through the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serpentine - resembling a serpent, especially in form or movement
  • synonym: snaky - resembling or characteristic of a snake; sinuous
