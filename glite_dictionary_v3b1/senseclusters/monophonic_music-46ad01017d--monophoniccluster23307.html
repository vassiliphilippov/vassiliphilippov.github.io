monophonic music

noun
music with a single melodic line
9.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /mə.nə.ˈfɑː.nɪk ˈmjuː.zɪk/ /; / /mɑː.nə.ˈfɑː.nɪk ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (AmE), /mə.nə.ˈfɒn.ɪk ˈmjuː.zɪk/ /; / /mɒ.nə.ˈfɒn.ɪk ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (BrE)
