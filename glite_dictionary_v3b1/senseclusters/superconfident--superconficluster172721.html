superconfident

adjective
extremely certain
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈkɑːnfɪdənt / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈkɒnfɪdənt / (BrE)
extremely sure or certain of oneself or one's abilities

Examples

A superconfident person
He was superconfident about winning the competition, even though the other teams were strong.
Her superconfident attitude impressed the interviewers during the final round.
Despite the weak evidence, she was superconfident that the project would be a success.
He gave a superconfident presentation that impressed everyone in the room.
Despite being new to the team, she appeared superconfident during the meeting.
His superconfident attitude sometimes makes others feel intimidated.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • degree lower: confident - sure of oneself or one's abilities
  • antonym: insecure - lacking confidence or assurance
  • synonym: overconfident - too sure of oneself, often without enough reason
  • antonym: uncertain - not sure or confident
