shovel hat

noun
traditional stiff clerical hat (Britain)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvəl hæt / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvəl hæt / (BrE)
a stiff, broad-brimmed hat with a projecting front, traditionally worn by British clergymen or used for sun protection

Examples

She laughed when her brother tried on the oversized shovel hat at the costume shop.
