scream one's head off

verb
shout or scream very loudly
Pronunciation: / skrim wʌnz hɛd ɔf / (AmE), / skriːm wʌnz hɛd ɒf / (BrE)
to scream extremely loudly and for a prolonged period, often due to fear, excitement, or distress

Examples

To scream their heads off
Fans screamed their heads off as the band walked on stage.
She screamed her head off when she got the news.
The kids screamed their heads off during the scary movie.
