liquidate investment

verb
turn investments/assets into cash
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪkwɪˌdeɪt ɪnˈvɛstmənt / (AmE), / ˌlɪkwɪˌdeɪt ɪnˈvɛstmənt / (BrE)
to convert an investment or assets into cash, typically by selling them

Examples

To liquidate investment assets
He had to liquidate investment assets to pay for his children's college tuition.
If the market falls sharply, you may need to liquidate investment holdings quickly to preserve capital.
Many retirees choose to liquidate investment accounts as they transition into retirement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cash in - to convert an investment or asset into cash
  • antonym: invest - to put money into something to make a profit
