identical

adjective
(of twins) from a single egg
1.3 2.2
Pronunciation: /aɪ.ˈden.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /ɪ.ˈden.tɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈden.tɪ.kəl/ /; / /aɪ.ˈden.tɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
