identical

adjective
(of twins) from a single egg
1.3
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɛntɪkəl / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪkəl / (BrE)
(of twins) developed from a single fertilized egg and therefore genetically identical; monozygotic.

Examples

Identical twins
Identical twins share the same genetic makeup because they come from a single egg.
Scientists study identical twins to understand how genetics influence behavior.
The doctor explained that identical twins are always the same sex.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fraternal - (of twins) developed from two separate eggs; dizygotic
