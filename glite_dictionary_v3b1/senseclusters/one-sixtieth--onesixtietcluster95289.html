one-sixtieth

noun
one part in sixty; fraction 1/60
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈsɪkstiəθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈsɪkstiɪθ / (BrE)
One part out of sixty equal parts; the fraction 1/60.
