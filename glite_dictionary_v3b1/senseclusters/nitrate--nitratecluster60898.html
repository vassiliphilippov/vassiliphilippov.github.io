nitrate

verb
treat with nitric acid to form a nitrate
4.1 6.3
Pronunciation: /naɪ.ˈtreɪt/ (AmE), /naɪ.ˈtreɪt/ (BrE)
to treat or combine a substance with nitric acid or nitrogen compounds, especially to make fertilizers or explosives

Examples

Some companies nitrate organic materials to create fertilizers for agriculture.
The laboratory carefully nitrated the sample to study its chemical properties.
