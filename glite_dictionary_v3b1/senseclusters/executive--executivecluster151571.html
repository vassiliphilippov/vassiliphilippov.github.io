executive

noun
senior government manager or administrator
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv / (BrE)
a person in a senior position who manages or administers a government agency or department

Examples

Government executive
A government executive must ensure that all policies are implemented effectively.
As a senior executive, she oversees multiple agencies within the ministry.
The new executive took charge of the health department last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - person responsible for running an organization or department
