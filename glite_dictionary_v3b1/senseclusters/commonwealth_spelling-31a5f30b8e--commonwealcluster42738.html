Commonwealth spelling

noun
British and Commonwealth English spelling conventions
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.ənˌwɛlθ ˈspɛl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ənˌwɛlθ ˈspɛl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the system of English spelling conventions used in the United Kingdom, Ireland, and most Commonwealth countries, as distinguished from American spelling

Examples

Commonwealth spelling rules
