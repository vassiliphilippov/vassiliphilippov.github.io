brush aside

verb
dismiss or disregard; not consider
6.1
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ əˈsaɪd / (AmE), / brʌʃ əˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to dismiss or ignore something or someone, treating it as unimportant

Examples

To brush aside criticism
He brushed aside my suggestion without even listening to it.
She tried to explain her side, but he simply brushed aside her concerns.
The company brushed aside criticism from the media and continued with its plans.
He tried to brush aside her questions, but she insisted on an answer.
It's not wise to brush aside concerns about your health.
The manager chose to brush aside all criticisms and focus on the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismiss - refuse to consider; disregard
  • synonym: ignore - intentionally pay no attention to something
