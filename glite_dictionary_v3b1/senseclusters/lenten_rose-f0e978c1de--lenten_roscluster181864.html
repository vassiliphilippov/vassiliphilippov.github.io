lenten rose

noun
perennial plant with early spring flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛntən roʊz / (AmE), / ˈlɛntən rəʊz / (BrE)
A perennial garden plant (Helleborus orientalis) with deep green leathery leaves and purplish-green flowers, often blooming around early spring.

Examples

The lenten rose plant
Despite its name, the lenten rose is not related to true roses.
The lenten rose is cherished for bringing color to gardens in late winter.
We planted several lenten roses beneath the old oak tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: helleborus - genus of perennial flowering plants, including the lenten rose
