righter

adjective
more to the right
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈraɪtə / (BrE)
more to the right side or more conservative in position or politics than something else.

Examples

A righter position
Move the chair a bit righter so it lines up with the table.
His views became even righter after the election.
The arrow points righter than the one on the previous sign.
