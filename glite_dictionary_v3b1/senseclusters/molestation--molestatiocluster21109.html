molestation

noun
sexual harassment or abuse
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊlɛˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒlɛˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making unwanted or improper sexual advances or engaging in sexual activity with someone, especially women or children

Examples

Child molestation
Accusations of molestation were taken very seriously by the authorities.
Schools have implemented stricter policies to prevent molestation of students.
The organization was founded to support victims of molestation and help them recover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sexual assault - unwanted sexual contact or activity without consent
