Maltese

noun
small dog breed with long white coat
Pronunciation: / mɔlˈtiz / (AmE), / mælˈtiːz / (BrE)
A breed of small toy dogs characterized by a long, straight, silky white coat.

Examples

Maltese dog
Many families choose a Maltese because of its playful temperament and small size.
She brushed her Maltese daily to keep its white coat looking silky and clean.
The Maltese is known for its elegant appearance and affectionate nature.
