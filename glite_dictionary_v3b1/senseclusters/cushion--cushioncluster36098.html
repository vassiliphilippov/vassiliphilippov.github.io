cushion

noun
compact sponge pad with liquid foundation
Pronunciation: / \u02c8k\u028a\u0283\u0259n / (AmE), / \u02c8k\u028a\u0283.\u0259n / (BrE)
a compact cosmetic product consisting of a sponge-like pad soaked with liquid foundation or skincare, used to apply buildable, even coverage.

Examples

Cushion foundation compact
She touched up her makeup with a cushion foundation compact during lunch.
The makeup artist pressed the cushion onto her skin to build light, even coverage.
She always carries a small cushion in her bag for quick touch-ups after coffee breaks.

Related Terms
