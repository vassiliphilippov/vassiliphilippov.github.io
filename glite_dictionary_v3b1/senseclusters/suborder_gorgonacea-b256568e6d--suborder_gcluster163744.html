suborder Gorgonacea

noun
taxonomic suborder of red corals and sea fans
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈɔrdər ˌɡɔrɡəˈneɪʃiə / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈɔːdə ˌɡɔːɡəˈneɪsɪə / (BrE)
A taxonomic suborder of soft corals, including red corals and sea fans, characterized by their branching, tree-like colonies.
