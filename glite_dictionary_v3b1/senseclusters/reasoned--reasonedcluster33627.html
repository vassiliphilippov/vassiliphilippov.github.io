reasoned

adjective
based on careful thought or logic
2.6 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.zənd/ (AmE), /ˈriː.zənd/ (BrE)
(of an argument or statement) based on careful thought, logic, or reasoning; logically valid.

Examples

A reasoned decision
A reasoned decision is always preferable to a rushed one.

Their disagreement was settled through a reasoned discussion rather than an argument.
