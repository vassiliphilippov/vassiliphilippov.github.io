cloudwards

adverb
toward cloud computing
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdwədz / (BrE)
in the direction of cloud computing or remote servers, especially by moving data or services online

Examples

Moving cloudwards online
The company decided to move its entire infrastructure cloudwards to improve flexibility and reduce costs.
As technology advances, more businesses are heading cloudwards for their data storage needs.
Our IT team is planning to shift operations cloudwards over the next year.
