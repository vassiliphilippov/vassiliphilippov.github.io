annealer

noun
device for heat treatment
8.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈniː.lər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈniː.lə/ (BrE)
a person or device that heats and then slowly cools materials like glass or metal to make them stronger or remove internal stresses

Examples

Glass annealer
The technician placed the metal rods in the annealer to improve their strength.
A modern glass factory uses an automated annealer to ensure product quality.
After heating, the glassware must cool slowly in the annealer to avoid cracks.
The factory installed a new annealer to improve the quality of its glass products.
After passing through the annealer, the metal sheets became much less likely to crack.
A skilled technician operated the annealer to ensure the materials cooled at the correct rate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooler - a device used to cool materials or substances
  • synonym: furnace - a device for heating materials to high temperatures
