negrito

noun
Spanish nickname; sometimes offensive
Pronunciation: / neɪˈɡriːtoʊ / (AmE), / neɪˈɡriːtəʊ / (BrE)
a Spanish term meaning 'little black one', used as a familiar nickname or, in some contexts, as an offensive term for a dark-skinned person

Examples

To call someone negrito
His grandmother affectionately called him negrito when he was a child.
The use of negrito can be considered offensive depending on the context and relationship.
In some families, negrito is used as a term of endearment without any negative intent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negro - Spanish word for 'black', sometimes used as a racial term
