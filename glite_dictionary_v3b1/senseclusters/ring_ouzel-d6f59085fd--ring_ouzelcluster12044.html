ring ouzel

noun
a European thrush with a white neck band
8.6
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ ˈuːzəl / (AmE), / rɪŋ ˈaʊzəl / (BrE)
A European thrush found in rocky areas, the male of which has blackish plumage and a distinctive white band around its neck.

Examples

The ring ouzel bird
Conservationists are concerned about the declining population of ring ouzels in Britain.
The male ring ouzel is easily recognized by the striking white band around its neck.
We spotted a ring ouzel perched on the rocky hillside during our hike.
