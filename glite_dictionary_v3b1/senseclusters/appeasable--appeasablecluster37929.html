appeasable

adjective
able to be pacified
7.1 5.0
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpiː.zə.bəl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpiː.zə.bəl/ (BrE)
Capable of being calmed or pacified; able to be brought to a state of peace or satisfaction.

Examples

Appeasable anger
His anger was appeasable once he heard their sincere apology.
Some disputes are only appeasable by finding a fair compromise.
The crying baby was surprisingly appeasable with a gentle lullaby.
