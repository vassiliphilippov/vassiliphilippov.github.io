work out

verb
to happen as intended or result successfully
Pronunciation: / wɝk aʊt / (AmE), / wɜːk aʊt / (BrE)
to develop or happen in a particular way, often successfully; to have a particular result

Examples

If our plan doesn't work out, we'll have to try something else.
