predawn

noun
time before sunrise
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈdɔn / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈdɔːn / (BrE)
the period of time just before sunrise, when it is still dark or only beginning to get light

Examples

The predawn hours
The hikers set out in the predawn to reach the summit before noon.
A gentle mist covered the fields during the quiet predawn hours.
Birds began to sing softly in the predawn as the sky slowly brightened.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: dawn - the time when the sun rises and daylight begins
  • degree lower: night - the period of darkness between sunset and sunrise
