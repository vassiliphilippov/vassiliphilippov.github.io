Pictionary

proper noun
drawing guessing game
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈpɪkʃənəri / (BrE)
a game (played as a board game, TV show, or online) where players draw pictures to help others guess words or phrases
