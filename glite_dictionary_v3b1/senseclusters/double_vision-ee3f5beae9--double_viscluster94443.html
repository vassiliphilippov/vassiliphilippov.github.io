double vision

noun
condition of seeing two images of one object
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
A condition in which a single object is perceived as two images instead of one; diplopia.

Examples

Suffering from double vision
After his head injury, he started to experience double vision while reading.
If you suddenly notice double vision, it is important to seek medical help immediately.
Wearing an eye patch can temporarily relieve double vision caused by nerve problems.
