counterbattery

noun
artillery against artillery
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌbætəri / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəbætəri / (BrE)
artillery fire aimed at enemy artillery positions to neutralize or destroy them

Examples

Counterbattery fire
The commander ordered immediate counterbattery to suppress the enemy's heavy guns.
Effective counterbattery can protect friendly troops from incoming artillery fire.
Modern radar systems help locate targets for counterbattery operations quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artillery - large-caliber guns used in warfare
  • synonym: barrage - a concentrated artillery bombardment over a wide area
