sidlingly

adverb
indirectly or cautiously
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is sideways, indirect, or cautious, often to avoid attention

Examples

To move sidlingly forward
She approached the subject sidlingly, hoping not to upset anyone with her questions.
The cat moved sidlingly along the wall, trying not to be noticed.
He sidlingly suggested that there might be a better way to solve the problem.
He entered the crowded room sidlingly, hoping not to disturb anyone.
The cat moved sidlingly along the wall, stalking its prey.
She approached the conversation sidlingly, unsure if she was welcome.

  • synonym: obliquely - in a way that is not explicit or direct
