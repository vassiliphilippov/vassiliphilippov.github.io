Halacha

noun
Jewish religious law
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈlɑːxə / (AmE), / haˈlaxa / (BrE)
The collective body of Jewish religious laws derived from the Written and Oral Torah and the Talmud, governing religious and daily life.
