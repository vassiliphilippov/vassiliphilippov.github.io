freighter

noun
cargo ship or vessel
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfreɪtə / (BrE)
A large ship or vessel used for carrying goods, especially commercially.

Examples

A freighter ship
After the storm, the damaged freighter was towed to safety by tugboats.
Several tons of grain were loaded onto the freighter bound for Europe.
The giant freighter anchored in the harbor all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cargo ship - a ship that carries goods rather than passengers
  • degree lower: tanker - a ship for carrying liquid cargo
