unlonely

adjective
not lonely
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈloʊnli / (AmE), / ʌnˈləʊnli / (BrE)
not feeling lonely; having company or a sense of companionship

Examples

Feeling unlonely again
After moving in with her new roommates, she finally felt unlonely again.
The lively party made everyone feel unlonely and welcome.
He realized he was truly unlonely when surrounded by good friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connected - feeling close to others or part of a group
  • antonym: lonely - feeling sad because of being alone
