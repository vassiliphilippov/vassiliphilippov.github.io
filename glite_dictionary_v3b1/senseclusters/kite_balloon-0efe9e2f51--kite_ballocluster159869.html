kite balloon

noun
type of barrage balloon with stabilizing lobes
6.4
Pronunciation: / kaɪt bəˈluːn / (AmE), / kaɪt bəˈluːn / (BrE)
A type of barrage balloon designed with stabilizing lobes at one end to ensure it faces into the wind.

Examples

A kite balloon in wind
During World War I, many cities used kite balloons to protect against low-flying enemy aircraft.
Engineers improved the kite balloon to maximize its effectiveness in air defense.
The design of a kite balloon helps stabilize it so it always points into the wind.

Related Terms
