insatiability

noun
never being satisfied
8.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.seɪ.ʃə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.seɪ.ʃə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to satisfy or always wanting more

Examples

Her insatiability for adventure
Her insatiability for adventure led her to travel to every continent.
The company's insatiability for profit sometimes overshadowed ethical concerns.
He was known for his insatiability when it came to learning new languages.
