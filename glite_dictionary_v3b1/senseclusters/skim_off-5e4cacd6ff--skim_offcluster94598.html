skim off

verb
remove from the surface of a liquid

to remove a substance from the surface of a liquid, especially by using a utensil

Examples

To skim off the fat

She used a spoon to skim off the fat that had risen to the top of the soup.
The workers skimmed off the cream from the surface of the milk at the dairy.

Synonyms
