wedge

noun
type of shoe heel/sole
Pronunciation: / wɛdʒ / (AmE), / wɛdʒ / (BrE)
a type of shoe heel that is an extension of the sole, forming a continuous raised platform under the foot

Examples

Wedge shoes
Wedges are often more comfortable than high heels because they offer better support.
She bought a pair of stylish wedge sandals for the summer.
The shoes feature a cork wedge that adds height without sacrificing stability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: platform - a thick, raised sole of a shoe
