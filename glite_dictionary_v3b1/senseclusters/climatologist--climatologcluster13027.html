climatologist

noun
an expert in climate science
Pronunciation: / ˌklaɪ.məˈtɑː.lə.dʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌklaɪ.məˈtɒl.ə.dʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies climate and its patterns, especially over long periods

Examples

A climatologist studies climate
As a climatologist, Dr. Martinez studies the impact of greenhouse gases on Earth's climate.
Many governments consult a climatologist when developing policies for climate change mitigation.
The climatologist presented her research on global temperature trends at the international conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorologist - a scientist who studies weather and atmospheric phenomena
