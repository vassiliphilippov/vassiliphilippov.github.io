altiplano

noun
high mountain plateau
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæltɪˈplɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌæltiˈplɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a high, flat plateau region in the mountains, especially in the Andes of South America

Examples

The altiplano region
The altiplano of Bolivia is known for its breathtaking scenery.
Farmers on the altiplano must adapt to the region's high altitude and dry climate.
Many indigenous communities have lived on the altiplano for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plateau - an area of high, flat land
  • antonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
