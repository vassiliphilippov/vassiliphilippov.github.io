nonlethal

adjective
not deadly or fatal
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈliθəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈliːθəl / (BrE)
Not capable of causing death; not deadly or fatal.

Examples

Nonlethal weapons
Many police forces use nonlethal weapons to control crowds.
Researchers are developing nonlethal alternatives to traditional pest control methods.
The snake's bite is painful but considered nonlethal to humans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lethal - capable of causing death; deadly
  • synonym: nonfatal - not resulting in death
