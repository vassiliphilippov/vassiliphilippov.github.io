bestselling novel

noun
a novel that sells very well
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛstˈsɛlɪŋ ˈnɑvəl / (AmE), / ˌbɛstˈsɛlɪŋ ˈnɒvəl / (BrE)
A novel that sells in large quantities, often featuring on lists of top-selling books.

Examples

A bestselling novel list
He dreamed of writing a bestselling novel that would change his life.
Her new bestselling novel was adapted into a major film last year.
One of the surprises on the summer reading list was an unexpected bestselling novel.
