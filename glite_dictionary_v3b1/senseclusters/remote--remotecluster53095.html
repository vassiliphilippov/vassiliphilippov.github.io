remote

adjective
distant in relationship or relevance
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmoʊt / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊt / (BrE)
distant in relationship, relevance, or kinship; only slightly or indirectly connected.

Examples

Remote relative
She is a remote relative, descended from a different branch of the family.
The two companies have only a remote connection through their subsidiaries.
Their interests had only the most remote relevance to our discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: close - near in relationship or relevance
  • synonym: distant - far apart in relationship or relevance
