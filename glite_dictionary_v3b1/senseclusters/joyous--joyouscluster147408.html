joyous

adjective
full of joy; happy
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːjəs / (BrE)

Examples

Joyous smile
She had a joyous smile on her face when she received the good news.
The children's joyous laughter filled the playground on a sunny afternoon.
The holiday parade was a joyous celebration for the entire community.
