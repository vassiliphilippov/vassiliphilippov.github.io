misdeal

noun
an incorrect deal of cards
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈdiːl / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈdiːl / (BrE)
an act of dealing cards incorrectly according to the rules of a card game

Examples

A misdeal in poker
A misdeal was declared, and the cards had to be collected and reshuffled.
He apologized to the group for the unexpected misdeal.
If you spot a misdeal, let the dealer know before play begins.
