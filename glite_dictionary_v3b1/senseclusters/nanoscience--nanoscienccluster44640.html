nanoscience

noun
study of nanomaterials
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊˌsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈnanəʊˌsaɪəns / (BrE)
the scientific study of materials and phenomena at the scale of nanometers, typically involving structures and devices at the atomic or molecular level

Examples

Nanoscience research
Recent breakthroughs in nanoscience have enabled the development of more efficient solar panels.
Universities around the world are offering new courses in nanoscience to meet growing demand.
Her research in nanoscience focuses on creating materials that can repair themselves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biology - the scientific study of living organisms
  • synonym: nanotechnology - the application of scientific knowledge to create devices and materials at the nanoscale
  • synonym: physics - the science of matter, energy, and their interactions
