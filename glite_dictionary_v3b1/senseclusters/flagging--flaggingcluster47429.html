flagging

noun
marking content for review
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈflæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
(on online platforms) the act of marking posts, comments, images, or other content for attention, review, or removal by moderators or automated systems.

Examples

Flagging a comment
The platform's flagging feature lets users report hate speech quickly.
After multiple flagging reports, the comment was hidden pending moderator review.
We tracked flagging patterns across posts to understand abuse and improve moderation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: report - to notify a platform or authority about problematic or rule-breaking content
