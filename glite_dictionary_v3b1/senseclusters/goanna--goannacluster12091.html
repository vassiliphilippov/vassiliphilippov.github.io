goanna

noun
Australian monitor lizard
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈænə / (AmE), / ɡəʊˈænə / (BrE)
a large Australian lizard of the monitor family, often found in dry or semi-arid regions

Examples

A large goanna
A large goanna climbed the tree to escape the heat of the ground.
The children watched a goanna as it searched for insects near the campsite.
Farmers sometimes see a goanna sunning itself on fence posts in the outback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iguana - a large herbivorous lizard native to Central and South America
  • synonym: monitor lizard - a large lizard of the Varanidae family
