vanner

noun
ore separator
Pronunciation: / ˈvænər / (AmE), / ˈvænə / (BrE)
a person or machine that separates valuable ore from rock using water

Examples

A modern vanner can process large amounts of ore much faster than manual methods.
During the mining operation, the vanner played a crucial role in extracting valuable minerals.
