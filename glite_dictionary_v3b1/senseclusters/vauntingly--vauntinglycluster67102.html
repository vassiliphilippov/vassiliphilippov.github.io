vauntingly

adverb
in a boastful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔntɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈvɔːntɪŋli / (BrE)
In a boastful or bragging manner; with excessive pride.

Examples

To vaunt achievements
He spoke vauntingly of his many accomplishments during the meeting.
She vauntingly described her luxurious vacations to impress her friends.
The athlete vauntingly declared he would break the world record.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boastfully - in a boastful manner
  • antonym: modestly - in a modest or humble manner
