sawtimber

noun
logs for lumber
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌtɪmbər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌtɪmbə / (BrE)
trees or logs that are large and good enough to be cut into lumber for construction or manufacturing

Examples

Large sawtimber trees
The company purchased a large area of forest rich in sawtimber for their new mill.
Only trees that meet certain size requirements are classified as sawtimber.
After the storm, much of the fallen wood was not suitable for sawtimber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lumber - wood that has been processed for building
  • antonym: pulpwood - wood used for making paper, not for lumber
