cobweb

noun
a tangled spider web
8.2 1.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːb.web/ (AmE), /ˈkɒb.web/ (BrE)
a web made by a spider, especially when it is old, dusty, or tangled

Examples

Dusty cobweb in corners

The old attic was covered in cobwebs, making it look abandoned.
Ancient houses often have cobwebs hanging from the ceiling corners.
She brushed a cobweb aside as she entered the abandoned attic.
Sunlight revealed the delicate structure of a cobweb in the window frame.
