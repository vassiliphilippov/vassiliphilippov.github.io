simulative electronic deception

noun
simulating capabilities to mislead enemies electronically
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmjə.lə.tɪv ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk dɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / sɪˈmjuː.lə.tɪv ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk dɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
