beneficially

adverb
in a beneficial manner
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnəˈfɪʃəli / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃəli / (BrE)
In a way that produces a good or helpful result; advantageously.

Examples

Respond beneficially
Many plants respond beneficially to this type of fertilizer under ideal conditions.
Regular exercise acts beneficially on both mental and physical health.
The new policy is expected to affect the community beneficially over the coming years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advantageously - in a way that gives an advantage or is beneficial
  • antonym: harmfully - in a manner that causes harm
