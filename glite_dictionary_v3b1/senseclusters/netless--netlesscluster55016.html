netless

adjective
without internet connection
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtləs / (AmE), / ˈnɛtləs / (BrE)
not connected to the internet or to a computer network; offline

Examples

Netless connection
The remote cabin remained netless, so we couldn't upload photos until we returned.
During the power outage, our entire office was netless for several hours.
Some people prefer a netless vacation to truly disconnect from work and social media.
