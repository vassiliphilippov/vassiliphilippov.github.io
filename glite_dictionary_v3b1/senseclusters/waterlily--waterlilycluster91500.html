waterlily

noun
aquatic flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌlɪli / (BrE)
an aquatic plant with broad, flat floating leaves and large, often fragrant flowers that grow on the surface of water

Examples

A waterlily flower
A single waterlily bloomed in the middle of the pond.
The artist painted a beautiful scene of waterlilies floating on a calm lake.
Frogs often rest on the broad leaves of a waterlily in summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lotus - an aquatic plant with large, showy flowers, often confused with waterlilies
