rest your eyes

verb
take a break for your eyes
3.4
Pronunciation: / rɛst jɔr aɪz / (AmE), / rɛst jɔː aɪz / (BrE)
to stop using your eyes for a short time, especially to avoid strain or fatigue

Examples

To rest your eyes briefly
After reading for an hour, it's helpful to rest your eyes for a few minutes.
She closed her book to rest her eyes before starting another chapter.
You should rest your eyes regularly when working at a computer all day.
