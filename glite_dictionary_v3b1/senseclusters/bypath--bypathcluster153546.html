bypath

noun
a small, little-used side road
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌpɑːθ / (BrE)
A small, less-traveled road or path, especially in the countryside.

Examples

A bypath in countryside
Farmers sometimes use this bypath to move between their fields, as it's rarely used by others.
The old stone house is hidden away on a quiet bypath far from the main road.
We wandered down a narrow bypath that led us through the fields and woods.
