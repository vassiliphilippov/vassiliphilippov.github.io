social system

noun
organized structure of relationships in a society
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a group of people in a society regarded as an organized structure with established patterns of relationships and roles

Examples

Changes in technology can dramatically alter a country's social system over time.
Each tribe developed its own unique social system to govern roles and relationships within the group.
The healthcare social system in Sweden is often cited as one of the most effective in the world.
