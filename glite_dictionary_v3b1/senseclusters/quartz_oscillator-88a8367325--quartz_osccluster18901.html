quartz oscillator

noun
electronic oscillator using quartz crystal
5.6 4.9

An electronic oscillator that generates a precise frequency using the vibrations of a piezoelectric quartz crystal.

A faulty quartz oscillator can cause timing issues in electronic devices.
Engineers tested the quartz oscillator to make sure the circuit maintained a stable frequency.
