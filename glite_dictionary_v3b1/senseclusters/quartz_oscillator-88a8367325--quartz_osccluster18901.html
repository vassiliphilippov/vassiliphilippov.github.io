quartz oscillator

noun
electronic oscillator using quartz crystal
5.6
Pronunciation: / kwɔrts ˈɑːsɪˌleɪtər / (AmE), / kwɔːts ˈɒsɪleɪtə / (BrE)
An electronic oscillator that generates a precise frequency using the vibrations of a piezoelectric quartz crystal.

Examples

A quartz oscillator circuit
A faulty quartz oscillator can cause timing issues in electronic devices.
Engineers tested the quartz oscillator to make sure the circuit maintained a stable frequency.
The clock in your computer is usually regulated by a quartz oscillator for high accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crystal oscillator - oscillator that uses a crystal for frequency control
  • degree lower: resonator - device or system that naturally oscillates at certain frequencies
