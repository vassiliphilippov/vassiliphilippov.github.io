sweep away

verb
overwhelm emotionally
1.3
Pronunciation: / swip əˈweɪ / (AmE), / swiːp əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to overwhelm someone emotionally, so that they are carried away by strong feelings

Examples

Swept away by emotion
At the funeral, many people were swept away by emotion.
He found himself swept away by the beauty of the music.
She was completely swept away by his declaration of love.

  • synonym: overwhelm - to affect someone so strongly they cannot think clearly
