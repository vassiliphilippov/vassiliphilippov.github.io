intricateness

noun
complex detail
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntrɪkətnəs / (AmE), / ˈɪntrɪkətnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being complex and having many small, detailed parts or aspects

Examples

The intricateness of design
The intricateness of the ancient tapestry amazed the museum visitors.
She admired the intricateness of the puzzle, which took hours to solve.
The architect explained the intricateness of the building's unique design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complexity - the state of being complicated or difficult to understand
  • antonym: simplicity - the quality of being easy to understand or not complicated
