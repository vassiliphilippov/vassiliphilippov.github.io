crepe Suzette

noun
French pancake dessert with orange liqueur sauce
6.7
Pronunciation: / kreɪp suːˈzɛt / (AmE), / krep sjuːˈzɛt / (BrE)
A French dessert consisting of thin pancakes served with a sauce made of orange and lemon juice, sugar, butter, and liqueur, often flambéed at the table.

Examples

A crepe Suzette dessert
For dessert, she ordered a classic crepe Suzette at the French restaurant.
The chef recommends pairing crepe Suzette with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
The waiter prepared the crepe Suzette tableside, setting the sauce alight with a flourish.
