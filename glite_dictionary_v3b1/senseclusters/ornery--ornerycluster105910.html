ornery

adjective
bad-tempered or difficult to deal with
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrnəri / (AmE), / ˈɔːnəri / (BrE)
bad-tempered, easily annoyed, stubborn, or difficult to deal with

Examples

An ornery person
Despite her ornery attitude, her friends knew she had a kind heart.
My grandfather can be downright ornery when he doesn't get his morning coffee.
The ornery cat hissed at anyone who tried to come near.
My grandfather can be quite ornery when things don't go his way.
She's ornery in the mornings until she has her coffee.
The children grew ornery after missing their nap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cantankerous - bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative
  • antonym: good-natured - pleasant and easy to get along with
