Examples

Threaten the environment

Synonyms

  • menace
    4.6 1.68/m
    menace shows a clear and present danger that can harm people or places, while threaten often means a likely future risk.
  • endanger
    3.7 5.98/m
    endanger means actively put someone or something at risk of harm, while threaten often indicates a likely or possible danger.
  • imperil
    7.8 1.01/m
    imperil puts someone or something in serious danger, while threaten usually means to be likely to cause harm or risk.
  • jeopardize
    6.3 8.37/m
    jeopardize puts plans, chances, or status at risk of loss or failure, while threaten focuses on causing harm or danger.
