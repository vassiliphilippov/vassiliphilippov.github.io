threaten

verb
pose danger to; endanger
5.5 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈθre.tən/ (AmE), /ˈθre.tən/ (BrE)
to be likely to cause harm or something bad to happen; to pose a danger or risk

Examples

Threaten the environment
Rising sea levels threaten many coastal communities around the world.
The invasive species threatens native plants in the region.
Unchecked deforestation threatens the survival of several animal species.
A storm threatens to disrupt tomorrow's outdoor event.
Dark clouds threaten heavy rain for the afternoon.
Economic recession threatens several industries this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: menace - to seem likely to cause harm or danger
