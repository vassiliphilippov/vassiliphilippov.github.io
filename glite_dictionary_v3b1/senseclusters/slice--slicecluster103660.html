slice

verb
cut cleanly through
1.0
Pronunciation: / slaɪs / (AmE), / slaɪs / (BrE)
to cut something into thin, flat pieces, usually with a sharp instrument

Examples

To slice bread

She used a sharp knife to slice the bread for sandwiches.
The surgeon must slice through tissue with extreme precision.
He slices the cheese before serving it.
Please slice the bread thinly for sandwiches.
She carefully sliced the tomatoes for the salad.
