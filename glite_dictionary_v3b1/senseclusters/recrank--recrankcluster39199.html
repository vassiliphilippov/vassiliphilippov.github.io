recrank

noun
machine restart
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkræŋk / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkræŋk / (BrE)
an act of cranking a machine or engine again to restart it after it has stopped

Examples

A quick recrank
The mechanic performed a recrank after the engine unexpectedly stalled.
A quick recrank of the old tractor got it running again in the morning.
After the power outage, the technician initiated a recrank of the backup generator.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restart - an act of starting something again
  • antonym: shutdown - an act of stopping a machine or system
