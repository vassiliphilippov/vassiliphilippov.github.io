head

noun
pressure exerted by a fluid
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
a measurement of height or pressure (of liquid or gas), or a unit of length equal to the size of a head

Examples

Head of steam
A greater head of steam increases the locomotive's speed.
The engineer calculated the head required to power the water turbine.
We monitored the pressure gauge to ensure the head in the pipe was adequate.
In the final race, the champion won by a single head.
She stood two heads above her younger brother in the family photo.
The giraffe was at least three heads taller than the zebra next to it.
