Neisseria

proper noun
bacterial genus
Pronunciation: / naɪˈsɪəriə / (AmE), / naɪˈsɪəriə / (BrE)
a genus of bacteria, some species of which can cause infections in humans such as gonorrhea and meningitis

Examples
