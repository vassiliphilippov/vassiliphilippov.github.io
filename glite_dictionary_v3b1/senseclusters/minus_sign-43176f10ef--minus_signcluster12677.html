minus sign

noun
mathematical symbol for subtraction or negativity
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.nəs saɪn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.nəs saɪn / (BrE)
the symbol (−) used to indicate subtraction or a negative quantity in mathematics

Examples

The minus sign symbol
A negative number is always written with a minus sign in front of it.
Be sure not to confuse the minus sign with the dash on your keyboard.
To show subtraction, you place the minus sign between the numbers.
