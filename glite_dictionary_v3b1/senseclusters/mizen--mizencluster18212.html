mizen

noun
aft or third mast on some sailing ships
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪz.ən / (AmE), / ˈmɪz.ən / (BrE)
the after and usually shorter mast of a ship, such as a yawl, ketch, or dandy, typically the third mast from the bow in vessels with three or more masts

A ketch has a mizen located aft of the main mast.
