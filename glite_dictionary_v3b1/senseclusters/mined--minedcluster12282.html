mined

adjective
extracted from the earth by mining
4.6
Pronunciation: / maɪnd / (AmE), / maɪnd / (BrE)
(of minerals, resources, etc.) extracted from the earth or another source, especially by mining.

Examples

Mined gold
Much of the region's economy depends on mined copper and other minerals.
The gold was mined from deep within the mountains.
These mined resources are exported to various countries around the world.

Related Terms
