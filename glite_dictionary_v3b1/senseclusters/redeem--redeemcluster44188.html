redeem

verb
restore honor or worth
7.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdim / (AmE), / rɪˈdiːm / (BrE)
To restore the honor, reputation, or worth of someone or something.

Examples

To redeem a reputation
He worked hard to redeem his reputation after the scandal.
She made a generous donation to redeem her earlier mistakes.
The team's victory helped redeem their disappointing season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: debase - to reduce in quality or value
  • synonym: restore - to bring back to a former position or condition
