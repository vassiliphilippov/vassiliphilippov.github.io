sauteing

noun
frying food quickly in fat or oil
9.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑː.teɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈsɔː.teɪ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈsɔː.teɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈsəʊ.teɪ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
The method of cooking food quickly in a small amount of fat or oil in a pan over high heat.

Examples

Sauteing vegetables quickly
Sauteing the vegetables brings out their natural flavors and keeps them crisp.
Many recipes begin with sauteing onions and garlic to form the base of the dish.
Proper sauteing requires a hot pan and quick movement to prevent burning.
