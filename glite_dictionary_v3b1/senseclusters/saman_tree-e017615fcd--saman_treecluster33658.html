saman tree

noun
tropical shade tree with spreading crown
4.9
Pronunciation: / səˈmɑn ˌtri / (AmE), / səˈmæn ˌtri / (BrE)
a large tropical tree with a wide spreading crown, known for providing shade and bearing pink or white flowers

Examples

The saman tree shade
Many people gather under the saman tree to escape the midday sun.
The saman tree in the square creates a cool place to rest during the hot afternoon.
The park is famous for its ancient saman tree, which blooms beautifully every spring.
