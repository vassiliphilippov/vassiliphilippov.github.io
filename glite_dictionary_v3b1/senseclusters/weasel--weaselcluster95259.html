weasel

noun
a deceitful or sneaky person
Pronunciation: / ˈwizəl / (AmE), / ˈwiːzl̩ / (BrE)
a person who is regarded as treacherous or sneaky, especially one who is deceitful or evasive

Examples

A sneaky weasel
Don't be a weasel and try to get out of your responsibilities.
Everyone knew Tom was a weasel after he lied to cover up his mistakes.
She warned me not to trust him—he's a real weasel when it comes to business deals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rat - a person who betrays others, especially by informing
  • synonym: sneak - a person who acts secretly or treacherously
  • antonym: straight-shooter - a frank and honest person
