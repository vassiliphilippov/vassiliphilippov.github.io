proliferant

noun
weapons-seeking entity
Pronunciation: / prəˈlɪfərənt / (AmE), / prəˈlɪfərənt / (BrE)
a country or entity that seeks to acquire or spread nuclear or chemical weapons

Examples

Proliferant state
International agencies monitor proliferant states to prevent the spread of dangerous weapons.
The treaty aims to restrict the activities of any proliferant nation seeking nuclear technology.
Sanctions were imposed on the proliferant for attempting to acquire chemical weapons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonproliferant - a country or entity that does not seek to acquire or spread weapons
  • synonym: proliferator - an entity that causes something to increase or spread rapidly
