angulus

noun
corner or angle
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡjələs / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡjʊləs / (BrE)
a corner or angle in a physical object or space, especially in architecture, geography, or geometry

Examples

Building angulus
The architect designed a unique angulus at the entrance of the building.
In ancient maps, each angulus of the city walls was carefully marked.
The sculpture fit perfectly into the angulus formed by the two walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angle - the space between two intersecting lines or surfaces
  • synonym: corner - the point where two lines, edges, or sides meet
