hypesthesia

noun
decreased sensitivity to touch
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpɛsˈθiʒə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpɛsˈθiːzɪə / (BrE)
a decreased sensitivity to touch or tactile stimuli, often caused by nerve impairment or disease

Examples

Skin hypesthesia test
A neurologist tested for hypesthesia by gently touching different areas of the skin.
Diabetic neuropathy often leads to hypesthesia in the feet and hands.
The patient exhibited hypesthesia in her left arm after the accident.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: anesthesia - loss of sensation
  • degree higher: hyperesthesia - increased sensitivity to touch or stimuli
