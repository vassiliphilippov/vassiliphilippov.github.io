caution

noun
a warning or advice against danger or mistake
1.6 4.37/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…î É…ôn / (AmE), / Ààk…îÀê É…ôn / (BrE)
a warning or advice given to prevent possible dangers or misbehavior

Examples

A caution about danger
Before proceeding, the doctor gave a caution regarding potential side effects.
He ignored the caution from his supervisor and completed the risky task anyway.
The school issued a caution to students about the dangers of online scams.

Synonyms

  • warning
    1.0 0.00/m

  • admonition
    7.4 2.19/m
    admonition is a firm spoken warning to correct someone, while caution is general advice to prevent danger or misbehavior.
  • advisory
    4.3 12/m
    advisory is an official public notice about hazards or conditions, while caution is everyday advice to avoid danger or wrong acts.
  • caveat
    7.7 4.92/m
    caveat is a note that warns about limits or conditions, while caution is direct advice to prevent danger or misbehavior.
