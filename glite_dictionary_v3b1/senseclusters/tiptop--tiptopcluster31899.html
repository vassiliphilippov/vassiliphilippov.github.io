tiptop

noun
the very top or summit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈtɪpˌtɒp / (BrE)
the highest or uppermost point; the summit or extreme top of something

Examples

Tiptop of the mountain
From the tiptop of the tree, she could see the entire valley below.
He stood on the tiptop of the ladder to change the light bulb.
The climbers finally reached the tiptop of the mountain after hours of ascent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: base - the bottom or lowest part of something
  • synonym: summit - the highest point or top of a mountain or hill
