windowed

adjective
having windows
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊd / (BrE)
having one or more windows or window-like openings

Examples

Windowed room
The bright, windowed office made working there much more pleasant.
They admired the windowed facade of the modern building.
Her studio is a large, windowed space filled with natural light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: windowless - without any windows
