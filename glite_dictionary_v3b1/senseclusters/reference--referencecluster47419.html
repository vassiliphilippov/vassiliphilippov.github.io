reference

noun
formal recommendation for a job applicant
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛf.ɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˈrɛf.ər.əns / (BrE)
a formal statement from a previous employer or other professional contact, describing someone's abilities and character, often used when applying for a job

Examples

Job reference
Her former boss wrote a glowing reference praising her work ethic and leadership skills.
Please include at least two professional references with your job application.
The employer requested a reference from my previous manager before making a final decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recommendation - a letter or statement supporting someone's abilities or suitability
  • synonym: testimonial - a formal written statement about a person's character or qualifications
