probationary

adjective
relating to a trial or test period
2.7 7.3
Pronunciation: / proʊˈbeɪʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / prəʊˈbeɪʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to or denoting a period of testing or trial before a decision about permanent status is made

Examples

A probationary period

All new police officers must complete a probationary period before being fully appointed.
His probationary status means he is still being evaluated for the role.
