warm and inviting

adjective
friendly and comforting environment/atmosphere
1.4
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ənd ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / wɔːm ənd ɪnˈvaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a place or atmosphere) friendly, welcoming, and comforting, making people feel relaxed and at ease.

Examples

A warm and inviting room
Her warm and inviting smile made everyone feel instantly comfortable.
The café had a warm and inviting atmosphere that made us want to stay longer.
The living room is warm and inviting, perfect for entertaining friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cold and uninviting - unfriendly or not welcoming atmosphere
  • synonym: welcoming - friendly towards guests or strangers
