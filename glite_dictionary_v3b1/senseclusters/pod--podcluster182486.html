pod

noun
seed vessel of a plant
4.3
Pronunciation: / pɑd / (AmE), / pɒd / (BrE)
a long, narrow case or shell of some plants (like peas or beans) that contains the seeds

Examples

Soybeans grow in fuzzy pods that cling to the plant’s stems.
