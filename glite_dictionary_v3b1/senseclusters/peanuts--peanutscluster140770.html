peanuts

noun
a very small amount of money
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnʌts / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌts / (BrE)
A very small or insignificant amount of money; a trifling sum.

Examples

Paid peanuts
He worked long hours but got paid peanuts for his efforts.
She sold her vintage records for peanuts at the garage sale.
The offer was peanuts compared to what the property was worth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fortune - a large amount of money or assets
  • synonym: pittance - a very small or inadequate amount of money paid to someone as an allowance or wage
