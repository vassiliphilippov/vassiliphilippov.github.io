verb

to act in accordance with a rule, law, agreement, or set of instructions; to follow closely or obey

Examples

To adhere to rules
All participants must adhere to the safety guidelines during the experiment.
If you adhere to these instructions, you will avoid common mistakes.
It is important to adhere to the policies set by the organization.

Synonyms

  • comply
    3.8 35/m
    comply is a formal verb meaning act according to rules or orders, while adhere to is a phrasal verb meaning follow rules closely.
  • abide
    3.4 5.70/m
    abide is often used with by to accept or follow a decision or rule, while adhere to emphasizes close following.
  • follow
    1.0 56/m
    follow is a general word for acting according to advice or rules, while adhere to sounds more formal and focused.
  • observe
    3.4 7.98/m
    observe means respect or carry out a law or custom in practice, while adhere to focuses on strict close following.
  • obey
    1.0 20/m
    obey means do what an authority tells you to do, while adhere to can mean follow rules or instructions more generally.
  • stick to
    3.0 16/m
    stick to means continue with a plan or rule without changing, while adhere to is more formal and used for rules or agreements.
  • conform
    3.2 11/m
    conform means behave the same as others or fit social rules, while adhere to focuses on following explicit rules or instructions.
