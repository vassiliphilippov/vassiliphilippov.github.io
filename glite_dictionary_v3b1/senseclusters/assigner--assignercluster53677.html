assigner

noun
one who allocates
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪnər / (AmE), / əˈsaɪnə / (BrE)
a person, program, or device that allocates or sets values, resources, or identifiers, especially in technical or computing systems

Examples

Automatic assigner
The assigner in the software automatically distributes tasks to available workers.
In the network, the IP address assigner ensures each device has a unique identifier.
As the project assigner, she decided who would handle each responsibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allocator - a person or system that distributes resources or tasks
  • antonym: receiver - a person or system that receives something
