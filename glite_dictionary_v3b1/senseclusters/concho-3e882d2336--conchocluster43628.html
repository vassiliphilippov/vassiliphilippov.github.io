Concho

proper noun
Texas river name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːntʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒntʃəʊ / (BrE)
the name of a river in west-central Texas, often referring to the North and South Concho Rivers

Examples

Concho River, Texas
The Concho River flows through several towns in west-central Texas.
Many families enjoy fishing and boating on the Concho during the summer months.
San Angelo is located at the meeting point of the North and South Concho Rivers.
