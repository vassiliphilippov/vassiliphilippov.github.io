copyright infringement

noun
illegal violation of copyright
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpiraɪt ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiraɪt ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
the unauthorized use or violation of rights granted by copyright law, typically involving the copying, distributing, or displaying of protected works without permission

Examples

Copyright infringement case
The company was sued for copyright infringement after distributing unauthorized copies of the software.
To avoid copyright infringement, always seek permission before using someone else's music in your project.
Universities warn students that copyright infringement can lead to legal penalties and expulsion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copyright violation - another term for the unauthorized use of copyright-protected material
  • synonym: infringement - the action of breaking the terms of a law or agreement
