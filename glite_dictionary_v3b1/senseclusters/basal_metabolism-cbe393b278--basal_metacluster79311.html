basal metabolism

noun
energy used for basic body functions at rest
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.səl məˈtæb.əˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.səl məˈtæb.ɪl.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
the minimal amount of energy required to maintain vital bodily functions in a resting state

Examples

Basal metabolism rate
Regular exercise can increase your basal metabolism and help with weight management.
Scientists study basal metabolism to better understand how the body uses energy at rest.
Thyroid disorders can affect the body's basal metabolism and energy expenditure.
