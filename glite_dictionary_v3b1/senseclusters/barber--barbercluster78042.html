barber

noun
person who cuts men's hair and shaves beards
1.0 4.21/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêrb…ôr / (AmE), / Ààb…ëÀêb…ô / (BrE)
A person whose job is to cut men's hair and shave or trim beards.

Examples
