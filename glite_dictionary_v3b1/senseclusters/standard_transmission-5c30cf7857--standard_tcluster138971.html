standard transmission

noun
manual transmission in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɚd trænzmɪˈʃən / (AmE), / ˈstændəd trænzmɪˈʃən / (BrE)
a manual transmission system in a motor vehicle operated with a gear lever and clutch pedal

Examples

A standard transmission car
Driving a car with a standard transmission requires learning how to use a clutch pedal.
His first vehicle was a truck with a standard transmission, which took some time to master.
Many sports cars are still offered with a standard transmission for enthusiasts who enjoy the driving experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: automatic transmission - a transmission that shifts gears automatically
  • synonym: manual transmission - a transmission operated by the driver using a gear stick and clutch
