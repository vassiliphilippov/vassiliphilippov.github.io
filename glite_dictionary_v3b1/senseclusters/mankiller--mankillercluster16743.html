mankiller

noun
person who kills men
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌkɪlər / (AmE), / ˈmænˌkɪlə / (BrE)
a person who kills men, especially one responsible for the deaths of multiple males

Examples

A serial mankiller
The police believed the elusive mankiller had escaped across the border after the murders.
Legends spoke of a fearsome mankiller who haunted the old battlefield.
The detective was determined to catch the mankiller before he struck again.
