pose

noun
position and orientation in 3D (technical)
Pronunciation: / poʊz / (AmE), / pəʊz / (BrE)
(in robotics, computer vision, and related fields) the position and orientation of an object, sensor, or body in three-dimensional space, usually given relative to a reference frame.

Examples

Robot pose estimation
The robot estimated its end-effector pose relative to the base frame.
Researchers used multiple cameras to recover the subject's 3D pose during the motion capture session.
Before grasping, the system computed the object's pose and planned a safe approach trajectory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orientation - the direction an object faces or is rotated relative to a reference
  • synonym: position - the location of something in space, usually given by coordinates
