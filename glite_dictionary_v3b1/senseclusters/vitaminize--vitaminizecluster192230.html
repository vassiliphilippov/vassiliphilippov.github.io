vitaminize

verb
add vitamins to (something)
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.tə.məˌnaɪz /; / vaɪˈtæmənaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɪ.tə.mɪˌnaɪz /; / vɪˈtæmɪnaɪz / (BrE)
