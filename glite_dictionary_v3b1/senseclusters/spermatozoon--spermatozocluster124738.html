spermatozoon

noun
male reproductive cell; male gamete
7.9
Pronunciation: / spɝːˌmæt̬.əˈzoʊ.ən / (AmE), / spɜːˌmæt.əˈzəʊ.ɒn / (BrE)
A male reproductive cell or gamete, typically motile, that fertilizes the female egg cell.

Examples

A spermatozoon cell
Each spermatozoon is composed of a head, a midpiece, and a tail for movement.
The spermatozoon carries genetic material from the father to the offspring.
Under a microscope, a single spermatozoon can be seen swimming toward the egg.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ovum - female reproductive cell; female gamete
  • synonym: sperm - shorter, less formal term for spermatozoon
