Quai d'Orsay

proper noun
notable Paris street, metonym for French Foreign Ministry
9.5
Pronunciation: /keɪ dɔːr.ˈseɪ/ (AmE), /keɪ dɔː.ˈseɪ/ (BrE)
the street in Paris along the south bank of the Seine, known for housing French governmental ministries, especially the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; also used as a metonym for the French foreign ministry itself

Examples

Walk along Quai d'Orsay
Diplomatic negotiations often take place at the Quai d'Orsay in Paris.
In political discussions, 'the Quai d'Orsay' sometimes refers directly to France's Foreign Ministry.
Journalists gathered outside the Quai d'Orsay to await the official statement.
