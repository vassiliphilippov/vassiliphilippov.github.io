green mayonnaise

noun
mayonnaise with herbs and green vegetables
6.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrin ˌmeɪ.əˈneɪz / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˌmeɪ.əˈneɪz / (BrE)
mayonnaise flavored with herbs such as tarragon or dill and containing chopped watercress, spinach, or cucumber

Examples

A dollop of green mayonnaise
A dollop of green mayonnaise adds both flavor and color to any summer salad.
She served the fish with a spoonful of green mayonnaise on the side.
The chef taught us how to prepare green mayonnaise using fresh herbs from the garden.
