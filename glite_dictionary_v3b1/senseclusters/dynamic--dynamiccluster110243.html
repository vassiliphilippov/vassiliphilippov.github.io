dynamic

adjective
linguistics: expressing action or process
Pronunciation: / daɪˈnæmɪk / (AmE), / daɪˈnæmɪk / (BrE)
(Linguistics) describing a verb or adjective that expresses action or process, rather than a state of being.

Examples

Dynamic verbs in grammar
In grammar, dynamic verbs contrast with stative verbs, which describe states rather than actions.
Only dynamic verbs can be used in the present continuous tense.
The word 'running' in 'running water' is a dynamic participial adjective.
