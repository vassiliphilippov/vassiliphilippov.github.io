coulis

noun
pureed sauce
Pronunciation: / kuːˈli / (AmE), / kuːˈli / (BrE)
a smooth, thick sauce made from pureed fruits or vegetables, often used as a garnish or accompaniment in cooking

Examples

A coulis sauce
The dessert was topped with a strawberry coulis for extra flavor and color.
She drizzled a tomato coulis over the grilled fish to enhance its taste.
A raspberry coulis added a tangy touch to the rich chocolate cake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purée - food that has been blended into a smooth, thick paste
  • synonym: sauce - a liquid or semi-liquid substance served with food to add flavor
