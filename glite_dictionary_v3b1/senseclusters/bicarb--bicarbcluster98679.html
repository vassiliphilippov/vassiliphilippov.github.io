bicarb

noun
bicarbonate ion
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbaɪ.kɑːrb/ (AmE), /ˈbaɪ.kɑːb/ (BrE)
an informal term for the bicarbonate ion (HCO3−), which helps regulate acidity in the body or solutions

Examples

Bicarb level test
The nurse checked the patient's bicarb levels to monitor his acid–base balance.
Doctors may order a bicarb test if they suspect a problem with your blood's pH.
Low bicarb can indicate metabolic acidosis in medical diagnoses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bicarbonate - the bicarbonate ion (HCO3−), a buffer in the body
