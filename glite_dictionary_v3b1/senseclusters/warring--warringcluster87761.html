warring

adjective
involved in warfare or armed conflict
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɒrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of groups, nations, etc.) engaged in active war or conflict with each other.

Examples

Warring parties
Mediators attempted to reconcile the differences between the warring parties.
Several warring factions competed for control of the region.
The treaty brought an uneasy truce between the warring countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belligerent - engaged in war or conflict
  • antonym: peaceful - not involved in war or conflict
