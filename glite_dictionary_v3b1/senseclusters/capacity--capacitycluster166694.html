capacity

noun
position or role someone holds
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈpæsəti / (AmE), / kəˈpæsɪti / (BrE)
the position, function, or role in which someone serves or acts

Examples

Capacity as manager
He served the company in various capacities over the years.
She was invited to speak in her capacity as a senior scientist.
The president attended the meeting in a ceremonial capacity only.
