face towel

noun
small towel for face or hands
2.5
Pronunciation: / feɪs ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / feɪs ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
A small towel used for drying the face or hands.

Examples

A clean face towel
After exercising, he wiped his forehead with a fresh face towel.
Please use a separate face towel to dry your hands and face.
She keeps a clean face towel by the sink for her morning routine.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hand towel - a towel used to dry the hands, typically larger than a face towel
  • synonym: washcloth - a small cloth for washing the face or body
