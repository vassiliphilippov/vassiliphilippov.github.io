yellow nutgrass

noun
European sedge with edible tubers
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˈnʌtˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˈnʌtˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A European sedge plant (Cyperus esculentus) with small, edible, nutlike tubers.
