engagement

noun
promise to marry
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
a formal agreement by two people to marry each other

Examples

Engagement ring
After a year-long engagement, the couple finally set a wedding date.
Her diamond ring was a symbol of their engagement.
They announced their engagement at the family dinner last weekend.
