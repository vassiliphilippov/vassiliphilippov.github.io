to test drive

1. VERB

/ tɛst draɪv / (AmE), / tɛst draɪv / (BrE)
to drive a vehicle in order to evaluate its performance, typically before making a purchase

Examples

I test drove a few trucks last weekend.
Many buyers test drive several vehicles before choosing one.
She wants to test drive the car before making a decision.
