to choke under pressure

1. VERB

/ tʃoʊk ˌʌndər ˈprɛʃər / (AmE), / tʃəʊk ˌʌndə ˈprɛʃə / (BrE)
to fail to perform well because of stress or nervousness in a critical situation
fail due to stress or nerves

Examples

I'm worried I might choke under pressure in the final round of the competition.
Many athletes choke under pressure during important matches, missing easy shots they would normally make.
She tends to choke under pressure when delivering presentations to large audiences.

Synset Examples

Don't choke under pressure; just do your best.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choke - to fail at something because of nervousness or pressure
