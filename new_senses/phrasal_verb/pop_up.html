to pop up

1. VERB

/ pɑp ʌp / (AmE), / pɒp ʌp / (BrE)
to appear or occur suddenly and unexpectedly, often for a brief time
appear unexpectedly or suddenly

Examples

A message will pop up on your screen when the download is complete.
He tends to pop up whenever you least expect him.
Unexpected problems can pop up during any home renovation project.

Synset Examples

The farm popped into view as we turned the corner
He suddenly popped up out of nowhere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appear - to come into sight; become visible or noticeable
  • Antonym: vanish - to disappear suddenly and completely
