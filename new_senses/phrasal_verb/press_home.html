to press home

1. VERB

/ prɛs hoʊm / (AmE), / prɛs həʊm / (BrE)
to state or emphasize something forcefully and clearly in order to make someone understand or accept it
emphasize forcefully to convince

Examples

He pressed home his argument by citing several convincing statistics.
She repeatedly pressed home her point until everyone understood what she meant.
The minister tried to press home the importance of the new policy during his speech.

Synset Examples

drive home a point or an argument
I'm trying to drive home these basic ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drive home - to make something clearly understood by emphasizing it strongly

push the Home button to return to home screen

Examples

To exit the app quickly, simply press home once and wait for the launcher to appear.
She accidentally closed the game when she tried to press home during the intense level.
After changing the settings, he pressed home and returned to the main screen to check the widgets.
