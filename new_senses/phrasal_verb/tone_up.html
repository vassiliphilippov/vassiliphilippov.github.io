to tone up

1. VERB

/ toʊn ʌp / (AmE), / təʊn ʌp / (BrE)
to make the body or muscles firmer, stronger, and healthier, especially by exercise
make muscles firmer and healthier

Examples

She joined a fitness class because she wanted to tone up before the summer.
Swimming is a great way to tone up your whole body.
You can tone up your arms with regular weight training.

Synset Examples

Let's tone our muscles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strengthen - to make something stronger or more effective
  • Antonym: weaken - to make or become less strong

2. VERB NEW

/ toʊn ʌp / (AmE), / təʊn ʌp / (BrE)
(in cosmetics) to improve the appearance, evenness, or firmness of the skin or complexion, often by using skincare products or treatments.
improve skin tone or complexion

Examples

She uses a nightly serum to tone up her skin and reduce uneven pigmentation.
After two weeks of exfoliating and masks, his complexion began toning up noticeably.
The clinic promised clients they'd see their skin toned up and brighter in one month.
