to tap out

1. VERB

/ tæp aʊt / (AmE), / tæp aʊt / (BrE)
to produce a rhythm or series of beats by striking something lightly and repeatedly, especially with the fingers or an object
beat out a rhythm by tapping

Examples

He tapped out a lively tune on the tabletop during dinner.
She absentmindedly tapped out a rhythm with her pen while waiting.
The drummer tapped out the intro before the band joined in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drum - to make a rhythmic sound by beating repeatedly

2. VERB NEW

/ tæp aʊt / (AmE), / tæp aʊt / (BrE)
(informal) to type or compose a message on a phone, tablet, or keyboard by repeatedly tapping keys or the screen.
type a message by tapping

Examples

She quickly tapped out a short reply on her phone between meetings.
While waiting for the train, he tapped out a detailed work note on his tablet.
During the meeting she kept tapping out short messages and pretending to check notes.
