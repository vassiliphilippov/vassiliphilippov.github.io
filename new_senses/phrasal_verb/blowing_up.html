to blowing up

1. VERB NEW

/ bloʊ ʌp / (AmE), / bləʊ ʌp / (BrE)
(informal) to become suddenly very popular or widely shared, especially online; to go viral.
become viral; gain sudden popularity
informal

Examples

The teaser trailer is blowing up on social media, amassing millions of quick shares worldwide.
Her homemade cooking video blew up overnight, landing her invitations to several TV shows.
Indie artists hope a single hit will blow up and reach listeners beyond local scenes.
