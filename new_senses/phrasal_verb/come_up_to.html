to come up to

1. VERB

/ kʌm ʌp tu / (AmE), / kʌm ʌp tʊ / (BrE)
to approach someone, especially to speak to them
approach someone to speak to them

Examples

He didn't recognize me when I came up to him at the party.
If a stranger comes up to you on the street, be cautious.
She was too shy to come up to her teacher and ask a question.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approach - to move closer to someone or something
