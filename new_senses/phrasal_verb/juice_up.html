to juice up

1. VERB

/ dʒus ʌp / (AmE), / dʒuːs ʌp / (BrE)
to make something more interesting, lively, or exciting
make more lively or exciting
informal

Examples

They decided to juice up the event by hiring a live band.
You might need to juice up your presentation with some visuals.
