pear-shaped

1. ADJ NEW

/ ˈpɛr ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpeə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Informal (chiefly British). (of a plan, event, or situation) having gone wrong or failed; not turning out as intended.
gone wrong; failed
informal british

Examples

Our carefully planned picnic pear-shaped when it started pouring and half the food was ruined.
The merger talks went pear-shaped after a leaked document caused both sides to lose trust.
If the weather goes pear-shaped tomorrow, the race organizers will postpone the event until Sunday.
