Dutch oven

1. NOUN NEW

/ dʌtʃ ˈʌvən / (AmE), / dʌtʃ ˈʌvən / (BrE)
Informal slang: a prank in which someone traps another person under bedcovers and deliberately passes gas, creating an unpleasant smell.
prank of passing gas under covers
informal slang vulgar

Examples

He pulled a Dutch oven on his roommate during movie night.
At the sleepover, someone laughed after a Dutch oven left everyone complaining about the smell.
She warned him not to try a Dutch oven since it might seriously embarrass the other person.
