wrist pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪst ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈrɪst ˌpad / (BrE)
a protective pad worn on the wrist, especially by athletes to prevent injury
protective pad for the wrist

Examples

After spraining her wrist, she started using a wrist pad during workouts.
He always wears a wrist pad during football practice to protect his arm from injuries.
The new wrist pad design offers extra support for gymnasts and skaters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elbow pad - a protective pad worn on the elbow during sports

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈrɪst ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈrɪst ˌpæd / (BrE)
a cushioned pad placed on a desk in front of a keyboard to support the wrists while typing and help reduce strain.
cushion supporting wrists while typing

Examples

She placed a soft wrist pad in front of the keyboard to prevent soreness during long typing sessions.
Company IT provided ergonomic wrist pads to all employees after several reports of wrist pain.
She recommended using a gel wrist pad for support when editing documents for hours at work.
