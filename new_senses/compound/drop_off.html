drop off

1. NOUN NEW

/ drɑp ˈɔf / (AmE), / drɒp ˈɒf / (BrE)
a place where people or things are delivered and left; a designated point for unloading or leaving items or passengers.
designated unloading place

Examples

The mall's drop-off zone is clearly marked near the bus stop.
Please leave the fresh donations at the community center's drop-off desk before noon.
Parents should use the school drop-off lane to avoid blocking the main entrance during pickup.
