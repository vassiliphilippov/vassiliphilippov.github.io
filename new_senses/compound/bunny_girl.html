bunny girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌni ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈbʌni ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a young woman who works as a waitress or entertainer in a nightclub, typically wearing a costume with rabbit ears and tail, especially associated with Playboy Clubs
nightclub waitress in rabbit costume

Examples

Many people associate the bunny girl look with 1960s pop culture.
She dressed as a bunny girl for the costume party, complete with ears and a fluffy tail.
The club hired several bunny girls for its grand opening event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Playboy Bunny - waitress working in a Playboy Club dressed in a rabbit costume

Etymology

Formed from 'bunny' (slang for rabbit) and 'girl'; popularized in the 1960s by Playboy Clubs.
