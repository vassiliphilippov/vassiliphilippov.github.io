muscle-builder

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌsəl ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / ˈmʌsl̩ ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person who does special exercises to develop large and strong muscles
person developing large muscles through exercise

Examples

He worked hard to become a successful muscle-builder and entered several competitions.
Many muscle-builders spend hours in the gym each day.
The magazine featured tips for aspiring muscle-builders looking to improve their physique.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bodybuilder - a person who strengthens and enlarges the muscles of their body through strenuous exercise
