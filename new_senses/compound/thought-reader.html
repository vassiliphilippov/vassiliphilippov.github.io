thought-reader

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɔt ˌridər / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt ˌriːdə / (BrE)
A magician who appears to read people's thoughts, often by using subtle signals or tricks.
magician who appears to read thoughts

Examples

After the show, the thought-reader refused to reveal how he performed his tricks.
Many people were skeptical, suspecting that the thought-reader had assistance from someone in the crowd.
The thought-reader amazed the audience by revealing private details about volunteers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mentalist - a performer who claims to have psychic powers, especially related to mind-reading or prediction
