starting post

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrtɪŋ poʊst / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtɪŋ pəʊst / (BrE)
a post that marks the starting point of a race, especially in horse racing
post marking start of a race

Examples

Each competitor took their position at the starting post, waiting for the signal to begin.
Spectators gathered near the starting post to watch the excitement as the runners prepared.
The horses lined up at the starting post before the race began.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: finish line - line marking end point of a race
