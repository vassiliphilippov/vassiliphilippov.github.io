ping pong

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋ pɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋ pɒŋ / (BrE)
A game similar to tennis, played on a table divided by a net, with small paddles and a lightweight hollow ball.
table game like tennis, played with paddles and ball

Examples

She is the best ping pong player in our club.
The kids spent the afternoon playing ping pong during the rainy weather.
We set up the ping pong table in the basement for weekend tournaments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table tennis - The official name for the sport known as ping pong

Etymology

Originally a trademark, representing the sound of the ball; later adopted as the common name for table tennis.
