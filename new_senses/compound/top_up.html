top up

1. NOUN NEW

/ tɑp ʌp / (AmE), / tɒp ʌp / (BrE)
a prepaid amount, code, or voucher used to add credit or funds to a mobile phone, account, or prepaid service.
prepaid credit or voucher
british

Examples

I bought a top-up code at the shop so my phone would have data for the weekend.
The supermarket sells cheap top-ups for a range of prepaid mobile networks at the counter.
She scratched the card to reveal the PIN and entered the top-up on her phone quickly.
