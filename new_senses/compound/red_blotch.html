red blotch

1. NOUN

/ rɛd blɑtʃ / (AmE), / rɛd blɒtʃ / (BrE)
a red, irregular mark or patch on the skin, typically caused by irritation, rash, or other skin condition
red patch or mark on the skin

Examples

After hiking through the woods, she found a red blotch on her leg, possibly from poison ivy.
He became concerned when the red blotch started to itch and grow larger.
The doctor examined the red blotch that had appeared on my shoulder overnight.

Synset Examples

He noticed a red blotch on his arm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rash - an area of irritated or swollen skin, often with red patches
  • Synonym: blemish - a small mark or flaw on the skin

2. NOUN NEW

/ rɛd blɑtʃ / (AmE), / rɛd blɒtʃ / (BrE)
(in viticulture) a disease symptom of grapevines characterized by red patches on leaves or fruit, often caused by the Grapevine red blotch-associated virus (GRBaV).
grapevine disease symptom

Examples

Leaf samples showed a distinctive red blotch pattern that reduced photosynthesis in affected vines.
After diagnosis, growers removed the infected plant to slow the spread of the red blotch virus.
Scientists traced the cause of the sudden red blotch outbreaks to a newly identified grapevine virus.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grapevine red blotch disease - a viral disease of grapevines that produces red blotches on leaves and sometimes reduces fruit quality
