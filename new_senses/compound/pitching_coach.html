pitching coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪtʃɪŋ koʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtʃɪŋ kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an assistant coach in baseball responsible for training and managing the team's pitchers
assistant baseball coach for pitchers

Examples

After several poor games, the manager consulted the pitching coach for advice.
She aspires to become a pitching coach after her playing career ends.
The team's pitching coach worked closely with the new pitcher to refine his technique.
