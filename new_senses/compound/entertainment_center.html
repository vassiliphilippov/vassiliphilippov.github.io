entertainment center

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.tɚˈteɪn.mənt ˈsɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈteɪn.mənt ˈsɛn.tə / (BrE)
a large piece of furniture designed to house sound, television, and other multimedia systems, often including shelves for storage
furniture for housing multimedia systems

Examples

She organized the DVDs and game consoles on the shelves of the entertainment center.
The new television fit perfectly inside their entertainment center.
We bought an entertainment center to keep all our speakers, cables, and electronics in one place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: media console - a piece of furniture for holding audiovisual equipment
