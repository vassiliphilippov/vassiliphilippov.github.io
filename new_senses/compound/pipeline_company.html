pipeline company

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpˌlaɪn ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈpaɪplaɪn ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a company that builds, owns, or operates oil pipelines for transporting oil and gas
company operating oil or gas pipelines

Examples

Environmental regulations require every pipeline company to monitor for leaks and report incidents promptly.
The pipeline company announced plans to expand its network across three more states.
The local community protested against the new pipeline company building near their homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil company - a company involved in the exploration, production, or sale of oil
