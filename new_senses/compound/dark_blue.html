dark blue

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk bluː / (AmE), / dɑːk bluː / (BrE)
a deep and rich shade of the color blue, darker than standard blue
a deep, dark shade of blue

Examples

He wore a dark blue suit to the interview.
She chose dark blue paint for her bedroom walls.
The sky turned a beautiful dark blue just after sunset.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue, almost black
  • Degree Lower: blue - the color between green and violet in the visible spectrum
