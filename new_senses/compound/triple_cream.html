triple cream

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɪpəl ˈkrim / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpəl ˈkriːm / (BrE)
A soft, rich French cheese containing at least 72% butterfat in its dry matter, resulting in a very creamy texture and mild flavor.
rich French cheese with high fat content

Examples

Due to its high fat content, triple cream pairs well with sparkling wines.
She loves to spread triple cream on fresh baguette as a decadent treat.
The cheesemonger recommended a triple cream for the perfect addition to our cheese board.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: double cream - soft cheese with slightly less fat than triple cream
