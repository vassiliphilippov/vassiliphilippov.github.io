in quarantine

1. ADP

/ ɪn ˈkwɔːrənˌtin / (AmE), / ɪn ˈkwɒrənˌtiːn / (BrE)
in a state of isolation to prevent the spread of infectious disease
in isolation to prevent disease spread

Examples

After arriving from abroad, all travelers had to stay in quarantine for ten days.
During the outbreak, the pets were kept in quarantine at the animal shelter.
Many people felt lonely while they were in quarantine during the pandemic.

Synset Examples

She spent two weeks in quarantine before meeting family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quarantine - a period of isolation to prevent the spread of disease

Etymology

From Italian 'quarantina' (forty days), originally referring to the period ships were isolated to prevent the spread of plague.

(in computing) in a state where a file, email, or program is isolated and blocked from running or interacting with the system to prevent the spread of malware.
isolated and blocked (computing)

Examples

Suspicious email attachments are kept in quarantine until the security team completes a scan.
After detection, the antivirus placed the malicious executable in quarantine rather than deleting it.
The admin reviewed files currently in quarantine and decided which could be safely restored.
