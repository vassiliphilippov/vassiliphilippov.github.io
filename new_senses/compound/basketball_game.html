basketball game

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played between two opposing teams of five players each, aiming to score points by throwing a ball through an elevated hoop.
competitive basketball match

Examples

Last night's basketball game was extremely close and ended with a last-second shot.
She practices every day because she wants to play in the next basketball game.
Tickets for the basketball game sold out within minutes after they went on sale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basketball - a game played with a ball and a hoop
