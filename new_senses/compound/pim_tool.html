PIM tool

1. NOUN

/ piː aɪ ɛm tuːl / (AmE), / piː aɪ ɛm tʃuːl / (BrE)
A software application designed to organize, manage, and track personal information such as contacts, calendars, and tasks.
personal information management software

Examples

A good PIM tool can synchronize data across devices, making personal organization much easier.
Many professionals rely on a PIM tool to manage their daily schedules and contacts efficiently.
She installed a new PIM tool on her phone to keep her appointments and to-do lists organized.

Synset Examples

As an information management tool, a PIM tool's purpose is to facilitate the recording, tracking, and management of certain types of ‘personal information’.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal organizer - an electronic or paper tool to manage personal tasks and information
  • Degree Higher: productivity software - software designed to increase individual productivity, such as calendars, task lists, and reminders

Etymology

PIM is an acronym for Personal Information Manager; 'tool' refers to software application.

2. NOUN NEW

/ piː aɪ əm tuːl / (AmE), / piː aɪ əm tʃuːl / (BrE)
Software that centralizes, manages, enriches, and distributes product information (descriptions, attributes, images, pricing) for e-commerce and multichannel publishing.
product information management software

Examples

The retailer adopted a PIM tool to synchronize product data across online marketplaces.
Using a PIM tool, the brand enriched product descriptions and images before launching the new collection.
Their e-commerce team relies on PIM tools to ensure consistent attributes, pricing, and media across channels.

Synset Examples

The retailer adopted a PIM tool to synchronize product data across online marketplaces.
Using a PIM tool, the brand enriched product descriptions and images before launching the new collection.
Their e-commerce team relies on PIM tools to ensure consistent attributes, pricing, and media across channels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: PIM system - a software system for centralizing and managing product information for sales and publishing
  • Synonym: product information management - the practice and systems for organizing, enriching, and distributing product data across channels

Etymology

PIM is an acronym for Product Information Management; 'tool' refers to a software application.
