dog food

1. NOUN

/ dɔg fuːd / (AmE), / dɒg fuːd / (BrE)

food for dogs

Examples

Some owners prefer to cook homemade meals instead of giving their pets commercial dog food.
The pet store offers a wide selection of dog food for all breeds and ages.
