dank smell

1. NOUN

/ dæŋk smɛl / (AmE), / daŋk smɛl / (BrE)
a musty and unpleasant odor typically caused by dampness or humidity
musty odour from dampness

Examples

As she opened the cellar door, a dank smell wafted up, making her wrinkle her nose.
Rain-soaked carpets gave the old house a persistent dank smell.
The dank smell of the basement lingered for days after the flood.

Synset Examples

He loved the dank smell of the old library.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: musty smell - a stale, moldy, or damp odor
