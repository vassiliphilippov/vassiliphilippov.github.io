buttonology skill

1. NOUN

/ ˌbʌtnˈɑlədʒi skɪl / (AmE), / ˌbʌtnˈɒlədʒi skɪl / (BrE)
expertise in the art or technique of handling buttons, especially in tailoring and garment construction
skill in handling buttons and tailoring

Examples

Her buttonology skill allowed her to repair even the most intricate coats with ease.
His buttonology skill impressed the clients who needed delicate costume restorations.
The contest judged each participant's buttonology skill alongside their overall tailoring abilities.
