the Flood

1. PROPN

/ ðə flʌd / (AmE), / ðə flʌd / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the great deluge described in the Book of Genesis, said to have been sent by God to destroy all life on earth except for Noah, his family, and the animals in the ark.
the biblical deluge in Noah's time

Examples

During Sunday school, the children learned about Noah and the Flood.
Many scholars debate the historical basis for the Flood described in Genesis.
The story of the Flood has parallels in various ancient cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deluge - a great flood, especially one regarded as divine punishment

2. PROPN NEW

/ ðə flʌd / (AmE), / ðə flʌd / (BrE)
(in the Halo video-game series) a dangerous parasitic alien species that infects, assimilates, and transforms other life, causing widespread contagion and destruction.
parasitic alien species in Halo

Examples

In Halo lore, the Flood spread quickly, infecting entire colonies and overwhelming defenders.
Scientists in the story struggled to find a cure for the Flood before infection became unstoppable.
Players must destroy the Gravemind to stop the Flood consuming planets in later missions.
