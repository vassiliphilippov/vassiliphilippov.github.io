art gallery

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈɡæləri / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈɡæləri / (BrE)
a building or room where works of art are displayed for the public to view and often to purchase
a place for exhibiting artworks

Examples

Many artists hope to have their work displayed in a renowned art gallery.
The art gallery will host an exhibition of modern paintings next month.
We visited a new art gallery downtown featuring contemporary sculptures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: museum - a building where objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are exhibited

2. NOUN NEW

/ ɑrt ˈɡæləri / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈɡæləri / (BrE)
an online platform or virtual space where artworks are displayed to the public and often offered for sale.
online platform for exhibiting art

Examples

The artist sold limited prints through an online art gallery that reached collectors worldwide.
The museum partnered with a virtual art gallery to host a digital exhibition during the festival.
Students curated a themed show in a popular online art gallery to practice exhibiting and selling work.
