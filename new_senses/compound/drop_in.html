drop in

1. ADJ NEW

/ drɑp ɪn / (AmE), / drɒp ɪn / (BrE)
ready to be used, installed, or substituted without modification; designed to fit or work immediately in place of something else.
ready-to-use; easily substituted

Examples

The team chose a drop-in replacement that required no code changes before deployment.
We installed a drop-in module to upgrade the system without stopping production.
They advertised the batteries as a drop-in solution that fit all existing models.
