diaper changer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪpər ˌtʃeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəpə ˌtʃeɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person whose responsibility or activity is to change diapers, especially for babies or young children
someone who changes diapers
informal

Examples

After becoming a parent, he quickly mastered the role of diaper changer.
Every family gathering, his relatives joke about him being the expert diaper changer.
In their household, she is usually the designated diaper changer during the night.

Synset Examples

Her husband is the best diaper changer around.

As a new dad, he's now a professional diaper changer.
