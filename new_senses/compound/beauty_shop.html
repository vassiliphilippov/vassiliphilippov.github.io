beauty shop

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːti ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where hairdressers and beauticians provide hair styling and beauty treatments.
place for hair and beauty treatments

Examples

After work, they often meet at the beauty shop to catch up and get their nails done.
She made an appointment at the beauty shop for a haircut and facial.
The small town only has one beauty shop open on Sundays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty salon - a shop providing hair and beauty treatments
  • Degree Lower: barbershop - a shop where men's hair is cut and groomed

2. NOUN NEW

/ \u02c8bju\u02d0ti \u0283\u0251p / (AmE), / \u02c8bju\u02d0ti \u0283\u0252p / (BrE)
A retail shop that sells beauty and personal-care products such as cosmetics, skincare, haircare, and related accessories; does not necessarily offer hairdressing or salon services.
retail shop selling cosmetics and beauty products

Examples

She stopped by the beauty shop to buy a new waterproof mascara before the party.
Local entrepreneurs opened a small beauty shop selling organic skincare and boutique haircare products.
If you need styling products quickly, check the beauty shop on Main Street near the market.
