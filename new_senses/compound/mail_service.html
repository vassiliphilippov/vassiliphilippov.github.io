mail service

1. NOUN

/ meɪl ˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / meɪl ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
the official system for sending letters and parcels from one place to another, especially operated by a government or postal authority
official postal delivery system

Examples

Due to bad weather, the mail service was temporarily suspended in several towns.
She relies on the mail service to send important documents overseas.
The mail service delivers packages to rural areas every morning.

Synset Examples

the mail handles billions of items every day
he works for the United States mail service
in England they call mail ‘the post’

Related Terms

  • Synonym: postal service - the system of sending letters and parcels by post
  • Degree Higher: courier - a private service for delivering messages or parcels quickly

an online service or provider that sends, receives, stores, and manages electronic mail (email) for users.
online email provider

Examples

She switched to a new mail service offering encrypted, private email communications.
Many universities provide students with a campus mail service that supports large attachments.
After the outage, the company advised customers to use their backup mail service for urgent messages.
