control center

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtroʊl ˈsɛntɚ / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
a facility or location from which a group of related activities is monitored and directed
facility for monitoring and directing activities

Examples

A team of experts worked around the clock at the control center to coordinate the rescue mission.
All communications were routed through the control center during the emergency.
She monitored the satellite feeds from the main control center downtown.

Synset Examples

the general in command never left the control center

Related Terms

  • Synonym: command center - a center with similar function, especially for directing operations
  • Synonym: operations room - a room where operational control is exercised
