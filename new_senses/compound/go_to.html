go to

1. ADJ NEW

/ ɡoʊ tu / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tuː / (BrE)
Used before a noun: most reliable, trusted, or preferred option, person, or thing to use or consult in a particular situation.
most reliable or preferred option

Examples

My go-to coffee shop always has a table and friendly baristas on busy mornings.
When I need technical help, Jenna is my go-to person because she explains things clearly.
For quick dinner ideas, this cookbook is our go-to resource during the week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliable - consistently good in quality or performance; able to be trusted
  • Synonym: default - the option selected automatically or regarded as standard

Etymology

From the verb phrase 'go to' (to turn to someone or something for help); developed as an adjectival compound meaning 'something you turn to'.
