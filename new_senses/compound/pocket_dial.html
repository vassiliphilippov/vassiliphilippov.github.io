pocket dial

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːkət ˌdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˌdaɪəl / (BrE)
an accidental phone call made when a mobile phone's buttons are pressed unintentionally, typically while in a pocket or bag
accidental phone call from a mobile phone in a pocket
informal

Examples

He quickly apologized after his pocket dial interrupted the conference call.
She realized she had made a pocket dial to her friend during the meeting.
Sometimes a pocket dial can lead to funny surprises.

Synset Examples

He laughed about the awkward pocket dial during lunch.
My friend often pocket dials me while running.
I accidentally made a pocket dial to my boss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: butt dial - an accidental call made by unintentionally pressing a phone with one's body, especially the buttocks

2. VERB NEW

/ ˈpɑːkət ˌdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˌdaɪəl / (BrE)
to accidentally call someone by pressing a mobile phone's buttons while it is in one's pocket or bag.
accidentally call from pocket
informal

Examples

I accidentally pocket-dialed my boss while hurrying through the subway this morning.
She often pocket-dials her friends when her phone slides to the bottom of her bag.
He apologized after pocket-dialing his sister during the two-hour meeting at work.
