middle finger

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdəl ˌfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈmɪdəl ˌfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
the finger located between the index finger and the ring finger on the human hand
the finger between the index and ring fingers

Examples

He wears a silver ring on his middle finger.
She accidentally cut her middle finger while chopping vegetables.
The doctor asked her to extend her middle finger during the examination.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: index finger - the finger next to the thumb
  • Synonym: ring finger - the finger between the middle finger and the little finger
