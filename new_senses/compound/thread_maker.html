thread maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈθrɛd ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈθrɛd ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who spins fibers into threads by twisting them together
person who spins fibers into threads

Examples

Centuries ago, every village had its own thread maker to supply local weavers.
She apprenticed as a thread maker before opening her textile workshop.
The thread maker carefully spun wool into fine yarn for weaving.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinner - a person who spins fibers into thread or yarn
