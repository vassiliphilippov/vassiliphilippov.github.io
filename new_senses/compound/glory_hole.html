glory hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlɔri ˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri həʊl / (BrE)
a small storage locker at the stern of a boat or between decks of a ship, used for stowing equipment or supplies
nautical: small ship storage locker

Examples

Access to the glory hole was tricky due to its location between the decks.
Mark opened the glory hole to check for any supplies left from the last trip.
The old fishing boat had a glory hole where we stored extra rope and nets.
