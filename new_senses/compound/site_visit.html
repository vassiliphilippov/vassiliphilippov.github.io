site visit

1. NOUN

/ saɪt ˈvɪzɪt / (AmE), / saɪt ˈvɪzɪt / (BrE)
an official visit to a location to examine its suitability for a particular purpose or project
official visit to inspect a location

Examples

Before proceeding with construction, an engineer will conduct a site visit to evaluate the area.
During the site visit, officials took notes on environmental and safety concerns.
The project manager arranged a site visit to assess the new location for the factory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspection - careful examination, especially an official one

2. NOUN NEW

/ saɪt ˈvɪzɪt / (AmE), / saɪt ˈvɪzɪt / (BrE)
an instance of a user visiting a website; a single session or visit recorded and counted in web analytics.
website visit (analytics)

Examples

The digital team reported a rise in site visit numbers after the campaign.
Each site visit was recorded along with the user's country, duration, and device type.
Weekly reports show that mobile site visit duration increased after the redesign.
