eye dropper

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪ ˌdrɑpər / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌdrɒpə / (BrE)
a small tube with a rubber bulb at one end, used to draw up and dispense liquid in drops
device for dispensing liquid drop by drop

Examples

Please fill the eye dropper with the medicine and squeeze gently into the patient's eye.
She used an eye dropper to apply the solution carefully to the plant.
The laboratory technician measured the chemicals with an eye dropper for accuracy.

Synset Examples

she used an eye dropper to administer medication to the eyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pipette - small tube for transferring or measuring out small amounts of liquid

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈaɪ ˌdrɑpər / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌdrɒpə / (BrE)
(in image-editing or graphics software) a tool that samples or picks a color value from an image or screen so the same color can be used elsewhere.
color-sampling tool in graphics software

Examples

In Photoshop, use the eye dropper to pick the exact color from the image.
The designer used the eye dropper to match the button color across different files.
When recording the tutorial, she showed how the eye dropper reads hexadecimal color codes.
