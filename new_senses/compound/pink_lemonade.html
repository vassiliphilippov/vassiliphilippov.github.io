pink lemonade

1. NOUN

/ pɪŋk ˈlɛməˌneɪd / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈlɛmənˌeɪd / (BrE)
a sweet, pink-colored drink made from lemon juice (or lemon flavoring), sugar, water, and sometimes additional fruit or coloring
a sweet pink-colored lemon drink

Examples

I prefer the taste of pink lemonade over regular lemonade at picnics.
She poured a tall glass of pink lemonade to cool down after gardening.
The children sold pink lemonade at their charity stand during the festival.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
