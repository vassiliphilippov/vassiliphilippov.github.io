bhagwa color

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːɡwə ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡwə ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a deep saffron or orange color often symbolizing bravery and sacrifice, especially in the context of flags in South Asia
saffron color symbolizing bravery

Examples

Many religious ceremonies use cloth of bhagwa color as a symbol of purity and courage.
She chose to wear a scarf in bhagwa color during the festival.
The national flag features a prominent bhagwa color at its top band.

Synset Examples

The flag's bhagwa color symbolizes courage and sacrifice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saffron - a deep yellow-orange color
  • Degree Lower: orange - a color between red and yellow in the spectrum

Etymology

From Hindi 'bhagwā' (saffron-colored).
