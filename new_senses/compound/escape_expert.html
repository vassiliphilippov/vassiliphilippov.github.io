escape expert

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈskeɪp ˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪp ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
an entertainer who specializes in escaping from restraints, traps, or confined spaces as a performance
entertainer skilled in escaping acts

Examples

For years, he trained to become an escape expert like his childhood hero, Harry Houdini.
The famous escape expert amazed the audience by slipping out of locked chains underwater.
The magician hired an escape expert to consult on his latest stage illusion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: escapologist - an entertainer skilled in the art of escape
