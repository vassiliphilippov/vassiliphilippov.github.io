plus sign

1. NOUN

/ plʌs saɪn / (AmE), / plʌs saɪn / (BrE)
the symbol (+) used to indicate addition in mathematics or to show something positive
symbol (+) indicating addition

Examples

He carefully drew a plus sign on the paper to check his work.
In the equation, add the plus sign between the numbers to show addition.
The plus sign is often used to indicate something positive or additional.

Synset Examples

four plus three equals seven

Related Terms

  • Antonym: minus sign - the symbol (−) indicating subtraction in mathematics

2. NOUN NEW

/ plʌs saɪn / (AmE), / plʌs saɪn / (BrE)
A graphical symbol (usually a +) used in software interfaces as a button or icon to add or create a new item.
UI icon for add/create

Examples

In the app, tap the plus sign at the bottom to create a new note.
The designer chose a green plus sign icon to clearly indicate the add action.
When managing contacts, press the plus sign next to a group to add a member.

Synset Examples
