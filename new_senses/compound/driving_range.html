driving range

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪvɪŋ ˌreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvɪŋ ˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
an area or facility where golfers can practice hitting golf balls, typically with a series of marked distances
golf practice area

Examples

He spent every Saturday morning practicing his swing at the driving range.
Many beginners head to the driving range before playing a round on the course.
The town opened a new driving range next to the public golf course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golf course - a large area of land designed for playing the game of golf
