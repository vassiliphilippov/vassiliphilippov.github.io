book token

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈtoʊkən / (AmE), / bʊk ˈtəʊkən / (BrE)
a gift voucher that can be exchanged for books to the value indicated on the voucher
voucher exchangeable for books

Examples

She gave me a book token for my birthday, so I can choose whichever novel I like.
The prize for the essay competition was a £10 book token.
You can use this book token at most major bookstores across the country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift voucher - a certificate or card that can be exchanged for goods or services

2. NOUN NEW

/ bʊk ˈtoʊkən / (AmE), / bʊk ˈtəʊkən / (BrE)
an electronic voucher or digital code that can be redeemed to obtain an e-book or download a digital edition.
digital voucher for e-books

Examples

I redeemed a book token to download the new mystery novel to my e-reader.
The publisher emailed her a book token that unlocked the audiobook in the store.
Students received book tokens allowing free downloads of course readings for the semester.
