beats per minute

1. NOUN

/ bits pɚ ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / biːts pə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
The number of musical beats occurring in one minute, used to measure the tempo of a piece of music.
tempo measurement in music

Examples

Most running playlists feature tracks with around 120 beats per minute to keep a steady pace.
She set the metronome to 80 beats per minute to help her practice the piece slowly.
The song has a rapid tempo of 140 beats per minute, making it popular in dance clubs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tempo - the speed at which a musical piece is played

2. NOUN NEW

/ bits pə ˈmɪnət / (AmE), / biːts pə ˈmɪnət / (BrE)
The number of heartbeats occurring in one minute; a unit used to report a person's pulse or heart rate in medical and fitness contexts.
heart rate measurement
medical

Examples

The doctor measured the patient's beats per minute and noted a slightly elevated rate.
During cardio class she tracked her beats per minute to maintain an effective training zone.
A normal resting beats per minute for adults usually falls between 60 and 100.
