senior position

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːniɚ pəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə pəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a job or role at a high level within an organization, usually with significant responsibility or authority
high-ranking job role

Examples

After years of hard work, she was promoted to a senior position within the firm.
Holding a senior position often comes with more decision-making power and greater responsibility.
Many aspire to reach a senior position in their company before retirement.

Synset Examples

She held a senior position at the healthcare company.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: junior position - a job or role at a low level within an organization, usually with limited responsibility
  • Synonym: executive position - a job involving management responsibilities at the top level of an organization
