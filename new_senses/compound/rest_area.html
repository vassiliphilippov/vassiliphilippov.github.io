rest area

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛst ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈrɛst ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a designated area beside a main road or highway where vehicles can stop temporarily, often offering facilities such as toilets and picnic tables
highway stop with facilities for motorists

Examples

She checked the map for the nearest rest area before continuing their long drive.
The highway rest area was busy with families parked for lunch.
We decided to stop at a rest area to stretch our legs and use the restroom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: service area - area beside a highway offering fuel, food, and other services
