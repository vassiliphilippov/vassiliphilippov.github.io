parking lot

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrkɪŋ lɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ lɒt / (BrE)
an area of ground, usually outdoors, where vehicles can be parked temporarily
area for parking cars
american

Examples

He left his car in the parking lot overnight.
The new shopping center has a huge parking lot for customers.
We couldn't find a space in the parking lot so we had to park on the street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car park - an area for parking cars, especially in British English
