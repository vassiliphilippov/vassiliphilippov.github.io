token economy

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊkən ɪˈkɑnəmi / (AmE), / ˈtəʊkən ɪˈkɒnəmi / (BrE)
a method of behavior therapy in which individuals are rewarded with tokens for desired actions; tokens can later be exchanged for rewards
behavioral therapy using token rewards
medical

Examples

Children with behavioral challenges can benefit from a token economy at school, earning tokens for positive actions.
The psychiatric unit implemented a token economy to encourage patient participation in group activities.
Under the token economy system, patients exchanged their tokens for snacks or privileges each week.

A digital incentive system in which users earn tokens (often cryptocurrency or platform credits) for actions; tokens can be exchanged, traded, or used to influence behaviour, access features, or participate in governance.
digital incentive system using tokens

Examples

The app uses a token economy to reward contributors with tradable points and digital assets.
Many startups design a token economy to encourage network growth and long-term community participation.
Scholars debate whether the token economy of decentralized platforms creates sustainable user incentives.
