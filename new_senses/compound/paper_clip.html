paper clip

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər klɪp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə klɪp / (BrE)
a small piece of bent wire or plastic used for holding sheets of paper together
wire or plastic clip for holding papers

Examples

A stray paper clip was lying on the office floor.
He straightened a paper clip to reset the printer.
She attached the documents together with a paper clip.

Synset Examples

the paper clip was invented in 1900

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staple - a short metal wire used to fasten papers together

3. NOUN NEW

an icon or button (often shaped like a paper clip) in an email, messaging app, or other software used to attach one or more files to a message.
attach-file icon in software

Examples

To attach your resume, click the paper clip at the bottom of the email compose window.
On the messaging app, tap the paper clip and choose the photo you want to send.
She showed me how to press the paper clip button to add documents to the form.
