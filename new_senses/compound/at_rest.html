at rest

1. ADJ

/ æt rɛst / (AmE), / æt rɛst / (BrE)
in a state of quiet, stillness, or sleep; not active or moving
in a state of stillness or sleep

Examples

Machines that are at rest consume less energy than when they are running.
She looks so peaceful at rest, you can hardly tell she was ever stressed.
The child was finally at rest after a long day at the park.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asleep - in the state of sleep
  • Antonym: awake - not sleeping; conscious and alert

3. ADJ NEW

/ æt rɛst / (AmE), / ət rɛst / (BrE)
(in information technology and data security) describing data or information that is stored on a device or medium and is not currently being transmitted or actively processed.
stored data not being transmitted (IT)

Examples

All backups are encrypted to protect data at rest from unauthorized access.
Cloud providers must secure customer information at rest and during transmission to meet compliance standards.
The security team scanned the server to ensure sensitive files remained at rest and unreadable.

Synset Examples
