green groceries

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin ˈɡroʊsəriz / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈɡrəʊsəriːz / (BrE)
fresh fruits and vegetables grown for sale in the market; produce sold by a greengrocer
fresh fruits and vegetables for sale

Examples

He stopped at the corner shop to pick up some green groceries for dinner.
Many people prefer to buy their green groceries from local farmers' markets.
The outdoor market was overflowing with colorful green groceries early in the morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: produce - fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Synonym: greengrocer - a person who sells fresh fruit and vegetables

Etymology

From 'green' (referring to fresh produce) and 'groceries'; related to 'greengrocer,' a historical term for a retailer of vegetables and fruit.
