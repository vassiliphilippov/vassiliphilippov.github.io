mining company

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪ.nɪŋ ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.nɪŋ ˌkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a company that owns, operates, or manages mines for extracting minerals or other resources from the earth
company managing or operating mines

Examples

Local residents protested against the environmental practices of the large mining company nearby.
Several international mining companies attended the industry conference to discuss sustainable extraction methods.
The mining company invested millions in new equipment to increase production.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mine - an excavation for extracting minerals
  • Synonym: mining corporation - a corporation involved in mining activities
