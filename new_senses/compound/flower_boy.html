flower boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaʊ.ɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a young boy who carries flowers or scatters petals in a wedding procession
boy who carries flowers at weddings

Examples

At his cousin's wedding, Sam was thrilled to be chosen as the flower boy.
Some ceremonies include both a flower boy and a flower girl for added charm.
The flower boy walked down the aisle, scattering petals ahead of the bride.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower girl - a young girl who carries flowers in a wedding procession
