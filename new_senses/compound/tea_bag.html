tea bag

1. NOUN

/ ti bæɡ / (AmE), / tiː bæɡ / (BrE)
A small, usually porous, paper or fabric bag containing a portion of tea leaves, designed for single-use brewing.
small bag holding tea leaves for brewing

Examples

After brewing, he threw the used tea bag in the compost bin.
Many people find using a tea bag more convenient than loose tea leaves.
She dropped a tea bag into her mug and poured in hot water.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loose tea - tea leaves not contained in a bag, used for brewing

2. NOUN

/ ˈti bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtiː bæɡ / (BrE)
A small, porous bag containing a measured amount of tea leaves for making an individual serving of tea.
small bag containing tea leaves

Examples

After steeping the tea bag for a few minutes, he removed it and added some sugar.
I buy my favorite tea bags in bulk from the local store.
She dropped the tea bag into her cup and poured hot water over it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teapot - a container with a spout used for brewing tea
