non-player character

1. NOUN

/ nɑn ˈpleɪɚ ˈkærəktɚ / (AmE), / nɒn ˈpleɪə ˈkærəktə / (BrE)
a character in a video game or other role-playing game who is not controlled by a player but is instead managed by the game or its rules
game character not controlled by player

Examples

In the game, one non-player character provided a vital clue to solve the mystery.
Many non-player characters have detailed backstories and unique dialogue.
The shopkeeper was a non-player character who sold health potions and weapons.

Synset Examples

The non-player character offered valuable quests and insights.
He enjoyed interacting with the quirky non-player characters throughout the game.
In the game, the non-player characters added depth to the story.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NPC - abbreviation of 'non-player character'

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) + 'player character', the latter originating in tabletop and video gaming.

2. NOUN NEW

/ nɑn ˈpleɪər ˈkærəktər / (AmE), / nɒn ˈpleɪə ˈkærəktə / (BrE)
informal (often derogatory) a person who behaves in a predictable, scripted, or unthinking way, as if following a program rather than acting independently.
insult for an unthinking person
informal slang offensive

Examples

Many users angrily called him a non-player character for always repeating the same talking points.
She joked that her coworkers were non-player characters because they never questioned company decisions.
The influencer's followers treated him like a non-player character, copying his moves without thought.

Synset Examples

Many users angrily called him a non-player character for always repeating the same talking points.
She joked that her coworkers were non-player characters because they never questioned company decisions.
The influencer's followers treated him like a non-player character, copying his moves without thought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: NPC - abbreviation of 'non-player character', used the same way in speech and writing
  • Synonym: bot - an automated account or person behaving mechanically; often used similarly online
  • Antonym: individual - a person acting independently or showing original thought; opposite in meaning

Etymology

From the videogame term 'non-player character' (NPC); extended metaphorically to describe people perceived as lacking independent thought.
