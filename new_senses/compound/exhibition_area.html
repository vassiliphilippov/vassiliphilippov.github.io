exhibition area

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən ˈeəriə / (BrE)
a large indoor space or hall designed for holding exhibitions, displays, or trade shows
space for exhibitions or displays

Examples

Many companies set up their booths around the spacious exhibition area.
The exhibition area was filled with visitors eager to see the latest products.
The annual tech conference was held in the main exhibition area of the convention center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhibition hall - a large room or building used for exhibitions
  • Degree Lower: gallery - a room or building for the display of art, especially paintings
