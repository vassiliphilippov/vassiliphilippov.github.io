tech hub

1. NOUN

/ tɛk hʌb / (AmE), / tɛk hʌb / (BrE)
a place or region where technology companies, especially startups, are concentrated and innovation is encouraged
center for technology startups and innovation

Examples

Dozens of new companies have launched since the tech hub opened its doors last year.
Silicon Valley is considered the world's leading tech hub for startups and established technology firms.
The city is investing in infrastructure to become a major tech hub in the region.

Synset Examples

The tech-hub is where all the startups gather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: innovation hub - center for innovation and entrepreneurship

2. NOUN NEW

/ tɛk hʌb / (AmE), / tɛk hʌb / (BrE)
an online platform or virtual community that brings together technology professionals, startups, resources, events, and networking opportunities in one central place.
online platform for tech professionals

Examples

She joined the online tech hub to access startup resources and connect with experienced mentors.
Several tech hubs now host virtual pitch days where founders present to investors around the world.
The company listed its accelerator on a popular tech hub to attract developer talent and partners.
