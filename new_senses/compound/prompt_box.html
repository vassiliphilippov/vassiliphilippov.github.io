prompt box

1. NOUN

/ prɑːmpt bɑks / (AmE), / prɒmpt bɒks / (BrE)
a small booth projecting above the floor at the front of a stage, where the prompter sits and which opens toward the performers
booth for prompter at stage front

Examples

During rehearsals, the director often sat near the prompt box to give additional cues.
In traditional theatres, the prompt box was placed just below the lip of the stage.
The actor glanced discreetly at the prompt box to check his next line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prompter - person who assists actors by prompting forgotten lines

2. NOUN NEW

/ pr51d0mpt b51ks / (AmE), / pr52mpt b52ks / (BrE)
(in computing) a text-input field in a graphical user interface where a user types a prompt, question, or instruction for an AI assistant, chatbot, or other interactive system.
text input for AI/chatbot prompts

Examples

She typed a detailed instruction in the prompt box of the AI chat app.
The website designer labeled the prompt box to guide users in submitting clear requests.
During testing, the assistant failed to respond when the user left the prompt box empty.
