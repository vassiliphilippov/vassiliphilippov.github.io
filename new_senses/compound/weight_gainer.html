weight gainer

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪt ˌɡeɪnər / (AmE), / ˈweɪt ˌɡeɪnə / (BrE)
a person who gains weight, especially deliberately in order to improve health or physique
person who gains weight

Examples

After following a strict diet and workout plan, the weight gainer noticed a significant increase in muscle mass.
Not every weight gainer relies on supplements; some focus on natural foods and strength training.
The doctor advised the weight gainer to monitor his calorie intake carefully.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weight loser - a person who loses weight
