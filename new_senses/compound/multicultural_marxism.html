multicultural Marxism

/ ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl ˈmɑrksɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈmɑːksɪz(ə)m / (BrE)

theory blending multiculturalism with Marxism
