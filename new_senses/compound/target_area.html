target area

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrɡɪt ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˌeəriə / (BrE)
the specific area or location that is intended to be hit, reached, or affected, especially in military or sports contexts.
place intended to be hit or reached

Examples

During surgery, doctors must identify the exact target area to treat.
The coach set up cones to mark the target area for the goalkeepers' practice.
The pilot carefully located the target area before releasing the payload.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: target - an object or area aimed at
