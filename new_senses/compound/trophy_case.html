trophy case

1. NOUN

/ ˈtroʊfi keɪs / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊfi keɪs / (BrE)
a display case used to exhibit trophies, medals, or awards
display case for trophies or awards

Examples

He built a custom trophy case to showcase his achievements in chess.
The old trophy case in the hallway holds decades of athletic history.
The school's trophy case was filled with awards from various sports tournaments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: display case - a case used to display objects for viewing

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈtroʊfi keɪs / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊfi keɪs / (BrE)
a virtual collection or display, typically on a gaming platform or app, that shows a user's earned digital trophies, achievements, or badges.
virtual display of digital achievements

Examples

Her digital trophy case on Steam shows every achievement she earned since 2015.
Coaches checked the team's trophy case online to celebrate recent esports tournament wins.
Players compare their trophy case contents to plan trades and show off rare badges.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achievement - a digital award or badge earned in a game or app for completing specific tasks
  • Synonym: profile - a user's account page where personal information and accomplishments are shown

Etymology

Extended from the physical noun 'trophy case' (a case for displaying trophies) to a 21st-century figurative sense for digital displays of achievements.
