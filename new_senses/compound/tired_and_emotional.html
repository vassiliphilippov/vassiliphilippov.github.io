tired and emotional

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪərd ənd ɪˈmoʊʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəd ənd ɪˈməʊʃənl / (BrE)
feeling physically exhausted and emotionally sensitive, often as a result of strain or stress
exhausted and emotionally sensitive

Examples

After hearing the bad news, she felt tired and emotional and needed some time alone.
By the end of the marathon, everyone was tired and emotional but grateful for the experience.
He became tired and emotional following weeks of intense work without rest.

Synset Examples

He was tired and emotional after the breakup.
After the long meeting, she was tired and emotional.

used euphemistically (especially in British English) to suggest that someone is intoxicated and behaving emotionally, without saying they were drunk.
euphemism implying intoxicated
british

Examples

The tabloid described the celebrity as tired and emotional after leaving the party late.
At the hearing, witnesses said he seemed tired and emotional, implying he had been drinking earlier.
Reporters used the phrase tired and emotional to avoid explicitly saying the politician was intoxicated.
