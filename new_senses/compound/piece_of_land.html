piece of land

1. NOUN

/ pis əv lænd / (AmE), / piːs əv lænd / (BrE)
an area or portion of land, usually considered as a distinct entity
a specific area or portion of land

Examples

After years of saving, he finally bought a small piece of land near the river.
Farmers in the region each own a piece of land for growing crops.
The family purchased a piece of land on which they plan to build a house.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plot - a small piece of ground, especially used for a specific purpose
  • Synonym: parcel of land - a defined portion of land, especially one considered as property

2. NOUN NEW

/ pis əv lænd / (AmE), / piːs əv lænd / (BrE)
a unit of virtual real estate in an online world or blockchain-based metaverse platform, purchasable and tradable as a digital parcel and often representing ownership or usage rights within that virtual environment.
virtual real estate parcel

Examples

Investors bought a piece of land in a popular virtual world to host events.
She sold her piece of land as an NFT and used the money to fund a startup.
Players competed to own scarce pieces of land near central hubs in the metaverse game.
