cottage pink

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑt̬ɪdʒ pɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɒtɪdʒ pɪŋk / (BrE)
A European flowering plant (Dianthus plumarius) grown for its very fragrant pink or rosy flowers.
a fragrant European pink flower

Examples

Gardeners often grow cottage pink along borders for a touch of classic charm.
She filled a vase with fresh cottage pink from her cottage garden.
The cottage pink is admired for its delicate fragrance and lovely color.
