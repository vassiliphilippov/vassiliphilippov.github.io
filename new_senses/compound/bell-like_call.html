bell-like call

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛlˌlaɪk kɔl / (AmE), / ˈbɛlˌlaɪk kɔːl / (BrE)
a birdcall that resembles the tone of a bell, often melodious and clear
a birdcall resembling a bell's tone

Examples

Her favorite memory was waking up to the bell-like call of robins outside her window.
The thrush's bell-like call enchanted everyone in the quiet forest.
We listened for the bell-like call of the distant songbird at dawn.
