key ring

1. NOUN

/ ˈki ˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkiː ˌrɪŋ / (BrE)
a small circular metal ring used for holding keys together
metal ring for holding keys

Examples

I keep all my house keys together on a key ring so I don't lose them.
She attached a colorful tag to her key ring to find it more easily in her bag.
The souvenir shop sells a variety of key rings with local landmarks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keychain - a small chain, usually attached to a key ring, for carrying keys
