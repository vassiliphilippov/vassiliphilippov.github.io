day game

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ ˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
A sports game that is played during daylight hours, rather than at night.
sports game played during the day

Examples

The players said they prefer day games to avoid the colder temperatures at night.
We're going to the day game this Saturday because it's easier to bring the kids along.
