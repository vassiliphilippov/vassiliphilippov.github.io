real estate

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl ɪˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ɪˌsteɪt / (BrE)
property consisting of land and any buildings on it, including houses, offices, or other structures
property of land and buildings

Examples

Owning real estate can provide both stability and potential income.
She decided to invest in real estate instead of the stock market.
The real estate market has been booming in recent years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: property - something owned; a possession

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman *real* (relating to things) and English *estate* (property).
