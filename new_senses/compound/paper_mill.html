paper mill

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər mɪl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə mɪl / (BrE)
a factory where paper is manufactured from wood pulp or recycled material
factory where paper is made

Examples

Environmental regulations require paper mills to treat their waste water before releasing it.
He took a job at the local paper mill after graduating from high school.
The old paper mill by the river was converted into an art center.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: factory - a building or group of buildings where goods are manufactured
  • Degree Lower: sawmill - a factory where logs are cut into lumber
