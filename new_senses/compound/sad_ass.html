sad ass

1. NOUN

/ sæd ˌæs / (AmE), / sæd ˌɑːs / (BrE)
A disparaging or self-deprecating term for someone who is feeling down, dejected, or overly negative.
disparaging term for a dejected person
informal slang

Examples

His friends called him a sad ass for moping around all day.
She felt like a real sad ass after getting rejected.
Stop being such a sad ass and try to enjoy yourself.

Synset Examples

Feeling like a sad ass, she skipped the party.
Don't be a sad ass, let's go have fun!
He called himself a sad ass after missing the meeting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downer - A person who is unfailingly negative or depressing

2. ADJ NEW

/ sæd ˈæs / (AmE), / sæd ˈɑːs / (BrE)
Informal, pejorative intensifier used before a noun to describe something as pitifully bad, lame, or disappointing.
pejorative intensifier meaning 'pathetically bad'
informal slang

Examples

The indie film had a sad ass ending that left the audience confused and disappointed.
After the long buildup, the show delivered a sad ass finale with lazy writing and no payoff.
Critics called the sequel's plot a sad ass attempt to cash in on the original's success.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lame - informal term meaning weak, unimpressive, or not good
  • Synonym: pathetic - causing pity or contempt because of weakness or inadequacy

Etymology

From the compound of 'sad' + slang 'ass' (insult); an attributive use of the noun developed as a colloquial intensifier.
