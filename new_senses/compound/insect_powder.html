insect powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.sɛkt ˌpaʊ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sɛkt ˌpaʊ.də / (BrE)
a chemical substance in powdered form used to kill insects
powdered chemical used to kill insects

Examples

If you have ants in the kitchen, try sprinkling some insect powder where they enter.
Many old homes were treated with insect powder to prevent infestations.
The gardener applied insect powder to protect the rose bushes from aphids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insecticide - a substance used for killing insects
