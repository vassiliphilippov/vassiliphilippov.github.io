conversational partner

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːn.vərˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːrtnər / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.vəˈseɪ.ʃən.əl ˈpɑːtnə / (BrE)
a person who is engaged in a conversation with another
person in a conversation

Examples

She found her new conversational partner very engaging and thoughtful.
