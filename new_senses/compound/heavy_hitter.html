heavy hitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvi ˈhɪtər / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi ˈhɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is very influential or successful, especially in promoting causes or achieving goals
influential or successful person

Examples

He's known as a heavy hitter in environmental advocacy circles.
Several heavy hitters attended the charity gala to show their support.
The company brought in a heavy hitter to lead the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerhouse - a person or thing of great energy, strength, or power
  • Synonym: bigwig - an important or influential person
  • Antonym: lightweight - a person with little experience or influence, especially in a particular sphere

Etymology

Originally a baseball term for a powerful batter, extended metaphorically to influential people in other fields.
