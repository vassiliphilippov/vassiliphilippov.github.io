Golden State

1. PROPN

/ ˈɡoʊldən steɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən steɪt / (BrE)
A popular nickname for the state of California, especially referencing its history and natural resources.
nickname for California (U.S. state)

Examples

Earthquakes are a common occurrence in the Golden State.
Many people moved to the Golden State during the Gold Rush in search of fortune.
The Golden State is famous for its diverse landscapes and vibrant economy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: California - State in the western United States, also known as the Golden State

Etymology

Refers to the California Gold Rush and the state's golden poppies and sunsets.
