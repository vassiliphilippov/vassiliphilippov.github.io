trash bin

1. NOUN

/ træʃ bɪn / (AmE), / træʃ bɪn / (BrE)
A container for holding rubbish until it is collected and disposed of.
container for holding rubbish

Examples

City workers emptied every trash bin on the street this morning.
He accidentally threw away his keys in the trash bin.
Please take the full bag out of the trash bin before it starts to smell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustbin - British term for a bin that holds rubbish
  • Synonym: garbage can - North American term for a bin that holds rubbish

2. NOUN NEW

/ træʃ bɪn / (AmE), / træʃ bɪn / (BrE)
A virtual folder in an operating system or application that stores deleted files temporarily, allowing them to be restored or permanently removed later.
virtual folder for deleted files

Examples

I restored the file from the trash bin just before permanently deleting it.
The system warns that emptying the trash bin will permanently remove all files in that folder.
Her email's trash bin kept a copy of the message for thirty days before auto-deletion.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
