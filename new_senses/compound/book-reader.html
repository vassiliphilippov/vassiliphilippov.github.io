book-reader

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈriːdər / (AmE), / bʊk ˈriːdə / (BrE)
a person who regularly reads books, often as a hobby or habit
person who reads books regularly

Examples

As a passionate book-reader, she always carried a novel in her backpack.
Being a book-reader opened his mind to new ideas and cultures.
The club was formed by a group of enthusiastic book-readers from the neighborhood.

Synset Examples

Being a book-reader helps him escape reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reader - a person who reads
