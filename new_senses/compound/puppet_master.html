puppet master

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌpɪt ˌmæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪt ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who controls or manipulates others, especially in a covert or behind-the-scenes way
one who manipulates or controls others

Examples

Despite her quiet demeanor, she was the puppet master controlling the entire operation.
Many believe the real puppet master of the company remains hidden from view.
The politician was seen as a puppet master, orchestrating events from the shadows.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manipulator - a person who controls or influences others cleverly or unscrupulously

Etymology

From 'puppet' (a figure moved by strings or controls) + 'master' (one who controls or directs).

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈpʌpɪt ˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪt ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
(in information technology, especially system administration) the central server or controller in a Puppet deployment that stores configuration manifests and communicates with agent nodes to enforce system state.
central server in a Puppet deployment

Examples

During the outage, the puppet master failed to connect with dozens of agents across the network.
Our team updated the manifests on the puppet master before rolling changes to production servers.
The documentation explains how to migrate a legacy puppet master to the newer Puppet Server architecture.

Synset Examples

During the outage, the puppet master failed to connect with dozens of agents across the network.
Our team updated the manifests on the puppet master before rolling changes to production servers.
The documentation explains how to migrate a legacy puppet master to the newer Puppet Server architecture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Puppet Server - the modern server implementation that performs the same central controller role in Puppet deployments
  • Synonym: controller - a system or service that coordinates and manages the behavior of other devices or software

Etymology

From Puppet (the configuration-management software) + master; used to name the central controller in a Puppet deployment.
