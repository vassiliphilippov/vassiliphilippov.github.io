blue whale

1. NOUN

/ blu weɪl / (AmE), / bluː weɪl / (BrE)
a very large migratory marine mammal (Balaenoptera musculus), bluish-grey in color, and the largest animal known to have ever existed
the largest known whale species

Examples

The blue whale is considered the largest animal ever to have lived on Earth.
The tongue of a blue whale can weigh as much as an elephant.
We saw a blue whale breaching the surface during our boat tour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whale - a very large marine mammal

2. NOUN NEW

/ blu weəl / (AmE), / bluː weəl / (BrE)
an informal name for a dangerous online social-media challenge reported in the 2010s that encouraged participants to complete harmful tasks and was sometimes linked to self-harm.
dangerous online challenge
informal

Examples

Reports described the blue whale challenge spreading across social platforms among teens.
After schools warned parents, authorities investigated alleged blue whale posts encouraging self-harm.
The documentary examined how the blue whale phenomenon spread through private messaging apps.
