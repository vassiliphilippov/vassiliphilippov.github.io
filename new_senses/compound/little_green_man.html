little green man

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬l ɡrin mæn / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl ɡriːn man / (BrE)
a mythical extraterrestrial being or creature, typically depicted as small and green, often in the context of science fiction or popular culture
mythical small green alien

Examples

As a child, she was convinced a little green man lived under her bed.
The science fiction movie featured a little green man arriving from another planet.
Whenever he made a mistake, he would blame it on a mischievous little green man.

Synset Examples

He joked about seeing a little green man in the garden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alien - a being from another planet or world
  • Synonym: extraterrestrial - a hypothetical or fictional being from outside Earth

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈlɪtəl ˈɡriːn mæn / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl ˈɡriːn mæn / (BrE)
(informal, politics/military) an unmarked soldier or paramilitary fighter in green uniform, deployed so the sponsoring power can deny responsibility.
unmarked soldier used for deniable operations

Examples

During the crisis, locals reported sightings of unidentified little green men near the border checkpoints.
Western governments accused the country of deploying little green men to seize strategic buildings while denying involvement.
The photo showed a single masked little green man carrying a rifle and no insignia on his uniform.
