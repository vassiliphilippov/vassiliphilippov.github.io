oil painting

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
the art or technique of painting with pigments suspended in oil
art of painting with oil-based paints

Examples

He teaches oil painting to beginners at the local art studio.
She mastered the art of oil painting before exploring other techniques.
The museum is home to several famous oil paintings from the Renaissance.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: watercolor - the art of painting with water-based pigments

Etymology

From 'oil' (referring to the binder) and 'painting'. Oil painting as a technique dates back to early European art history.

2. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a picture created by applying oil-based paints to a surface, typically canvas or panel
picture made with oil paints

Examples

Many artists prefer oil painting for its rich colors and durability.
She spent months working on her first oil painting of the landscape.
The museum's collection includes a rare oil painting by Van Gogh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painting - a picture made using paint

3. NOUN NEW

/ ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a digital image or artwork created by software or artificial intelligence that imitates the visual style of a traditional oil painting, without using real oil-based paints.
digital artwork in oil style

Examples

The app turned my photo into an oil painting with dramatic brushstrokes and rich color.
She printed the AI-generated oil painting and framed it for her living room wall last week.
At the gallery, a digital oil painting attracted visitors who expected traditional canvas and real oil paint.
