doctor-fish

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑktər fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə fɪʃ / (BrE)
A surgeonfish found in the West Indies, known for its sharp spines and bright coloration.
surgeonfish of the West Indies

Examples

A school of doctor-fish swam past the coral reef, displaying vivid blue stripes.
Many divers in the Caribbean are fascinated by the brightly colored doctor-fish.
The doctor-fish uses its sharp spines for protection from predators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surgeonfish - any of several tropical fish of the family Acanthuridae, having knife-like spines on each side of the tail
