research chemical

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɜrtʃ ˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːtʃ ˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
a synthetic substance created primarily for scientific research and investigation, often not approved for medical use
synthetic substance for scientific studies

Examples

All research chemicals must be stored securely in a locked cabinet.
Before using any research chemical, you should review its safety data sheet.
The university ordered a new research chemical to test its effects on plant cells.

Synset Examples
