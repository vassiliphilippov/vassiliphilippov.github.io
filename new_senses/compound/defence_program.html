defence program

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / dɪˈfens ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
An organized plan or set of activities designed to protect a country from attack by its enemies.
plan to protect a country from attack

Examples

Funding for the national defence program was debated in parliament.
Many scientists are employed in research for the country's defence program.
The government announced a new defence program to upgrade its missile systems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defense program - American spelling of defence program

A software application or suite designed to protect computers and networks from cyber threats such as malware, viruses, or intrusions.
computer security software

Examples

The organization deployed a new defence program to shield its servers from cyber intrusions.
After the breach, the IT team installed a lightweight defence program on every workstation.
Consumers choose a reputable defence program when seeking protection against malware and phishing scams.
