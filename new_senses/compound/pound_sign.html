pound sign

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊnd saɪn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊnd saɪn / (BrE)
the symbol (£) representing the pound sterling or, in some contexts, other pound-based currency units
symbol (£) for the pound sterling

Examples

Please write the amount in the space next to the pound sign on the form.
The new keyboard has a dedicated key for the pound sign.
When traveling in the UK, you will see the pound sign (£) on many price tags.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dollar sign - the symbol ($) representing the dollar currency
  • Antonym: euro sign - the symbol (€) for the euro currency

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈpaʊnd saɪn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊnd saɪn / (BrE)
The symbol '#' (called the pound sign in American English) used to mark numbers or labels and, on social media, to create keywords or tags (hashtags).
symbol (#) for numbers or hashtags
american

Examples

To join the trend, she added the pound sign before the phrase to create a shared hashtag.
On many phones, press the pound sign after entering your extension to finish the call.
The social media manager instructed us to use the pound sign only for campaign-related tags.
