skunk-weed

1. NOUN

/ ˈskʌŋk ˌwid / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋk ˌwiːd / (BrE)
A tall herb native to the Rocky Mountains with sticky leaves and a strong, unpleasant smell.
malodorous Rocky Mountain herb

Examples

Hikers often avoid touching skunk-weed because of its sticky leaves and foul odor.
The distinctive smell of skunk-weed makes it easy to identify along Rocky Mountain trails.
We found patches of skunk-weed growing near the riverbank during our mountain trek.

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈskʌŋk ˌwid / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋk ˌwiːd / (BrE)
informal slang for marijuana, especially a strong or particularly pungent strain.
slang for strong marijuana
slang informal

Examples

At the campus party, several students were talking about skunk-weed and where to buy it.
Police seized a bag of skunk-weed that smelled strongly when officers searched the car.
Some strains of skunk-weed are known for their particularly strong, skunk-like odor and potency.
