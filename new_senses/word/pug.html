pug

1. NOUN

/ pʌɡ / (AmE), / pʌɡ / (BrE)
a small, sturdy dog breed of Asiatic origin, with a wrinkled flat face, short smooth coat, and tightly curled tail
small short-muzzled dog breed

Examples

Her family adopted a pug from the animal shelter last year.
Many people love the playful personality of a pug.
The pug snored softly as it slept on the sofa.

Etymology

Possibly from earlier English dialect 'pug' (a playful little devil or sprite), influenced by the dog's facial expression.

2. NOUN

/ pʌɡ / (AmE), / pʌɡ / (BrE)
a small, sturdy dog breed with a wrinkled, flat face, short coat, and tightly curled tail, originally from Asia
small dog breed with flat face and curled tail

Examples

Many people find the pug's tightly curled tail especially charming.
She adopted a playful pug from the animal shelter last week.
The pug's wrinkled face gives it a unique and expressive appearance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bulldog - muscular, flat-faced dog breed
  • Degree Lower: chihuahua - very small dog breed
  • Degree Higher: mastiff - large, powerful dog breed

Etymology

Possibly from earlier dialect pug (imp, mischievous spirit), of uncertain origin.

informal slang

Examples

Before the raid they formed a quick pug since their usual group was offline.
