marksman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrksmən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksmən / (BrE)
A person who is skilled at shooting, especially with a gun.
a person skilled at shooting

Examples

At the shooting competition, the best marksman won a gold medal.
She watched in awe as the marksman hit every target with precision.
The army recruited a marksman to train the new soldiers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharpshooter - a person skilled in shooting
  • Synonym: sniper - a person who shoots from a hidden position

Etymology

From Middle English mark man (man who marks or aims at a target).
