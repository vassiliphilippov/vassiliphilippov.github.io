inflater

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈfleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtə / (BrE)
a hand-operated device for pumping air to inflate items such as tires or inflatable toys
hand pump for inflating items

Examples

He used a small inflater to pump air into his bicycle tire.
Make sure the inflater is working before your long road trip.
The emergency kit included a portable inflater for flat tires.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pump - a device for moving liquids or gases by pressure or suction

Etymology

From 'inflate' + '-er', denoting a device that inflates.

2. NOUN NEW

/ ɪnˈfleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtər / (BrE)
(in computing) a software component or library module that decompresses data previously compressed (often by the 'deflate' algorithm), restoring the original uncompressed data.
software decompressor (deflate)

Examples

The ZIP library's inflater processed the compressed stream and returned the original file data.
Developers added multiple inflaters to handle different compression formats on the server.
When the archive was corrupted, the forensic tool's inflater still recovered several images.
