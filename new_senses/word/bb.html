BB

1. NOUN

/ ˈbiː ˈbiː / (AmE), / ˈbiː ˈbiː / (BrE)
a small round pellet, typically made of steel, fired from an air rifle or BB gun
small pellet for air rifles or BB guns

Examples

A stray BB cracked the window during target practice.
He loaded his air rifle with BBs before heading outside.
Please don’t shoot BBs at the birds in the yard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pellet - a small, rounded, compressed mass of substance

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'ball bearing'.

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈbiː ˈbiː / (AmE), / ˈbiː ˈbiː / (BrE)
Informal abbreviation used in messages or speech as a term of endearment for a loved one, short for 'baby' or 'babe'.
texting term of endearment
informal

Examples

She sent a late-night message to BB, asking if he was still awake for a call.
When he greeted his girlfriend he mouthed 'good morning' and texted BB a heart emoji.
Parents sometimes call their infant BB in private messages, an affectionate shorthand between partners.
