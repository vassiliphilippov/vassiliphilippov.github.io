tanga

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡə / (BrE)
a former monetary unit of Tajikistan, equal to one hundredth of a Tajikistani ruble
former Tajik currency subunit

Examples

At that time, 100 tanga were equal to one Tajikistani ruble.
Many old collectors still have some tanga from the Tajikistani ruble era.
The shopkeeper gave me change in small tanga coins.

Etymology

From Turkic *tanga* (coin, money), commonly used for currency units in Central Asia.

Examples
