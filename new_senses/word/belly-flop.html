to belly-flop

1. VERB

/ ˈbɛli flɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛli flɒp / (BrE)
to dive into water and land flat on one's stomach, usually causing discomfort or a loud splash
dive landing flat on the stomach

Examples

At the competition, you lose points if you belly-flop instead of entering smoothly.
Everyone winced as he tried to dive but ended up with a spectacular belly-flop.
She accidentally belly-flopped into the pool, leaving her stomach bright red.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dive - jumping or plunging headfirst into water

2. VERB NEW

/ ˈbɛli flɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛli flɒp / (BrE)
(informal) to fail completely and often embarrassingly; to be an unsuccessful or disastrous failure.
fail spectacularly
informal

Examples

The product launch was expected to belly-flop because marketing failed to create interest.
After the harsh reviews, the new play belly-flopped and closed after only two performances.
Critics warned the comedian would belly-flop if he relied on tired jokes and predictable gags.
