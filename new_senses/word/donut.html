donut

1. NOUN

/ ˈdoʊ.nət / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.nʌt / (BrE)
A small, usually sweet, fried cake that is shaped like a ring or filled.
small ring-shaped fried cake

Examples

At the fair, they handed out free donuts with every coffee purchase.
He made a donut run before the meeting, bringing a box for everyone.
She picked up a chocolate donut with colorful sprinkles for her breakfast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doughnut - small ring-shaped or round fried cake

Etymology

Variant spelling of 'doughnut', from 'dough' + 'nut'.
