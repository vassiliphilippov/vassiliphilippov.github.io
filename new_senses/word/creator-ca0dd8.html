creator

1. NOUN

/ kriˈeɪtər / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who makes, invents, or brings something into existence
person who creates things

Examples

As the creator of the sculpture, she received much acclaim.
Every invention begins with a creator and an idea.
He is the creator of several popular video games.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inventor - a person who invents something
  • Antonym: destroyer - a person who destroys something

Etymology

From Latin creare (to create) + -or (agent noun suffix).
