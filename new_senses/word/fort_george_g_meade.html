Fort George G. Meade

1. PROPN

/ fɔrt ˈʤɔrdʒ ʤi ˈmid / (AmE), / fɔːt ˈʤɔːdʒ ʤiː ˈmiːd / (BrE)
a United States Army base in Maryland, serving as the headquarters of the National Security Agency
U.S. Army base in Maryland

Examples

He was assigned to Fort George G. Meade after completing his training.
Many intelligence operations are conducted out of Fort George G. Meade.
The National Cryptologic Museum is located at Fort George G. Meade in Maryland.

Etymology
