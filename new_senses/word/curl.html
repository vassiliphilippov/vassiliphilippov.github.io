curl

1. NOUN

/ kɝl / (AmE), / kɜːl / (BrE)
a lock of hair that grows or has been shaped into a spiral or ringlet
spiral lock of hair

Examples

A single curl fell onto his forehead as he ran.
She brushed a golden curl away from her eyes before speaking.
The little girl had a curl at the end of each pigtail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ringlet - a small, tight curl or ring-shaped lock of hair

Etymology

From Middle English 'curlen', of Middle Dutch origin; related to the word for something coiled or looped.

Examples
