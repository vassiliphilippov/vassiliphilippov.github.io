2. ADJ

/ ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm / (BrE)
used to denote a quantity consisting of one thousand items or units, especially in financial and statistical contexts.
symbol for one thousand (1,000) units

Examples

He bought 5m shares at the opening price.
The company's revenue surpassed 50m dollars last year.
The population of the city reached 3m in 2020.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: k - used as a symbol to denote one thousand, especially in informal contexts

Etymology

Abbreviation of the Latin 'mille', meaning thousand.

3. NOUN

/ ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm / (BrE)
the symbol for the meter, the basic unit of length in the International System of Units (SI), equal to approximately 1.094 yards
symbol for meter (SI unit of length)

Examples

One m is approximately equal to 3.281 feet.
She ran a 100 m race in under 12 seconds.
The swimming pool is 50 m long and perfect for competitions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meter - the basic SI unit of length, equal to 100 centimeters
  • Degree Lower: cm - symbol for centimeter, a unit equal to one hundredth of a meter
  • Degree Higher: km - symbol for kilometer, a unit equal to 1000 meters

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'meter', from the French 'mètre', from Greek 'metron' (measure).
