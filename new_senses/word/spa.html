spa

1. NOUN

/ spɑ / (AmE), / spɑː / (BrE)
A place where people go to improve their health, relax, and often enjoy treatments such as massages or baths, typically located near a natural spring or at the seaside.
health resort offering treatments and relaxation

Examples

Many people visit the spa to enjoy therapeutic mud baths and massages.
She spent a relaxing weekend at a luxurious spa in the mountains.
The resort features a state-of-the-art spa offering a variety of treatments.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health resort - a place for enhancing health and relaxation

Etymology

From Spa, the name of a town in Belgium known for its mineral springs.
