solidity

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɪdədi / (AmE), / səˈlɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being solid, especially with respect to its firmness, density, or consistency
state or quality of being solid

Examples

Her sculpture impressed everyone with the solidity and strength of its materials.
Scientists measured the solidity of the substance to determine its density.
The solidity of the ice made it safe to walk across the pond.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firmness - the quality of being firm or stable
  • Antonym: softness - the quality of being soft or yielding

Etymology

From Latin soliditas, from solidus (solid).
