2. NOUN NEW

/ eɪ ɑr / (AmE), / eɪ ɑː / (BrE)
An abbreviation for augmented reality: technology that overlays digital information or images on the real world, viewed through devices such as smartphones, tablets, or AR glasses.
augmented reality (technology)

Examples

The museum app uses AR to display historical reconstructions over real exhibits.
We followed the museum tour and saw labels appear through AR on our phone screens.
The furniture store's site lets you preview sofas in your room using AR before buying.
