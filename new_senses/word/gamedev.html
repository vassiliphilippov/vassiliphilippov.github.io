gamedev

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡeɪmˌdɛv / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmˌdɛv / (BrE)
a person who develops video games, especially professionally or as a hobby
video game developer
informal slang

Examples

As a junior gamedev, he spent countless nights fixing bugs before release.
Many indie gamedevs share their progress on social media platforms.
She dreams of becoming a gamedev and designing her own worlds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: game developer - a person who creates video games

Etymology

Blend of 'game' and 'developer'.
