colorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlɚɪst / (AmE), / ˈkʌlərɪst / (BrE)
an artist or painter who is especially skilled in using color to achieve particular effects
artist skilled in use of color

Examples

As a colorist, he had an extraordinary sense for combining subtle hues.
Many critics praised the colorist for her vibrant and imaginative use of paint.
The renowned colorist used bold reds and blues to bring his landscapes to life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially painting or drawing

Etymology

From French 'coloriste', from 'couleur' (color).
