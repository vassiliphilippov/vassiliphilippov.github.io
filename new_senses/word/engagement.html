engagement

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of hiring or appointing someone to a job or position
act of hiring someone

Examples

His engagement as the company's financial advisor was confirmed yesterday.
The engagement of new staff will begin next month.
They announced the engagement of a new chief executive officer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: employment - the state of having paid work or the act of employing
  • Antonym: dismissal - the act of removing someone from a job

Etymology

From French *engagement*, from engager (to pledge, involve).
