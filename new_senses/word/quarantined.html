quarantined

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɔːr.ənˌtiːnd / (AmE), / ˈkwɒr.ənˌtiːnd / (BrE)
kept in enforced isolation to prevent the spread of infection or disease
kept in enforced isolation for health reasons

Examples

After being exposed to the virus, she remained quarantined in her apartment.
All new arrivals were quarantined for two weeks before joining the community.
The quarantined area was marked off with warning signs to prevent entry.

Synset Examples

a quarantined animal
isolated patients

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isolated - kept apart from others; separated

Etymology

From quarantine + -ed, formed in reference to being subject to quarantine.

Examples
