soutache

1. NOUN

/ suˈtæʃ / (AmE), / suˈtɑːʃ / (BrE)
a narrow, flat braid used as a decorative trimming on clothing or upholstery
narrow decorative braid

Examples

Antique furniture often features soutache as a decorative element on fabric surfaces.
She chose black soutache to embellish the edges of the velvet dress.
The jacket was adorned with intricate soutache along the collar and cuffs.

Etymology

From French 'soutache', possibly from Italian 'soutaccé', referring to a kind of braid.

2. NOUN NEW

/ suˈtæʃ / (AmE), / suˈtɑːʃ / (BrE)
a decorative technique and the jewellery or embroidered items made by sewing soutache braid around beads and other elements to form shapes and motifs.
jewellery/embroidery technique using soutache braid

Examples

She designs soutache necklaces by sewing colourful braids around beads to create floral motifs.
At the craft fair, visitors admired the delicate soutache earrings made from silk braid and seed beads.
She taught a weekend workshop on soutache techniques, focusing on bead placement and braid stitching.
