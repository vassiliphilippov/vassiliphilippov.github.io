sprint

1. NOUN

/ sprɪnt / (AmE), / sprɪnt / (BrE)
a short, fast run, especially at full speed over a short distance
short fast run

Examples

After jogging for several minutes, he broke into a sprint.
She finished the race with a final sprint to the line.
The team practiced their sprints to improve speed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dash - a short, fast run or movement
  • Antonym: marathon - a long-distance running race of 42.195 kilometers

Etymology

From Middle Dutch *sprenten* (to jump, leap).

2. VERB

/ sprɪnt / (AmE), / sprɪnt / (BrE)
to run at full speed over a short distance
run very fast for a short distance

Examples

After the whistle blew, all the players began to sprint toward the ball.
She decided to sprint the last hundred meters to win the race.
The dog loves to sprint across the open field every morning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dash - run or move quickly and suddenly
  • Degree Lower: jog - run at a steady, moderate pace

Etymology

From Middle English 'sprenten' (to spring, leap), related to 'spring.'

3. NOUN NEW

/ sprɪnt / (AmE), / sprɪnt / (BrE)
(in Agile software development and project management) a short, fixed period of time (usually one to four weeks) during which a team works to complete a set of planned tasks or backlog items.
time-boxed work period (Agile)

Examples

During the two-week sprint, developers focused on fixing bugs and improving performance.
The team completed the feature during the next sprint in our agile cycle.
Product managers plan which backlog items to include in the next sprint.

Synset Examples

During the two-week sprint, developers focused on fixing bugs and improving performance.
The team completed the feature during the next sprint in our agile cycle.
Product managers plan which backlog items to include in the next sprint.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iteration - a repeated development cycle in Agile; often used interchangeably with 'sprint'.
  • Synonym: time-box - a fixed period during which a particular task or set of tasks must be completed.

Etymology

Metaphorical extension of the noun 'sprint' (a short, fast run); adopted in Agile project management to mean a short, focused work period.
