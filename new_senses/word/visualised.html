visualised

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪʒuəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəlaɪzd / (BrE)
formed or seen in the mind as a mental image; imagined visually rather than physically present.
seen in the mind as a mental image

Examples

She visualised herself winning the race and crossing the finish line first.
The visualised landscape in his dream was more beautiful than anything he had seen in reality.
The architect carefully visualised each detail of the building before drawing the plans.

Synset Examples

the glory of his envisioned future
the snow-covered Alps pictured in her imagination
the visualized scene lacked the ugly details of real life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imagined - created or formed in the mind
  • Synonym: envisioned - seen or conceived in the mind
  • Antonym: real - actually existing or occurring in fact

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'visualise', from Latin 'visualis' (of sight).
