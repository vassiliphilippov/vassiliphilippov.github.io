2. NOUN NEW

/ juː ˈɛs / (AmE), / juː ˈɛs / (BrE)
(medical) abbreviation for ultrasound: a diagnostic imaging procedure that uses high-frequency sound waves to produce images of internal body structures.
medical abbreviation for ultrasound
medical

Examples

The obstetrician ordered a quick US to assess the fetus's growth this month.
During the emergency, she requested a quick US to check for internal bleeding.
The abdominal US showed no signs of gallstones or other visible problems.
