dank

1. ADJ

/ dæŋk / (AmE), / daŋk / (BrE)
(slang) Very high in quality or potency; especially used to describe marijuana.
high quality (esp. of marijuana)
slang

Examples

Everyone was talking about how dank that weed was at the party last night.
He brought some dank buds that impressed all of his friends.
The dispensary is known for selling only dank cannabis strains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: potent - having great power, influence, or effect; strong (as applied to drugs/substances)
  • Antonym: low-grade - of inferior quality; especially used for substances like marijuana

2. ADJ

/ dæŋk / (AmE), / dæŋk / (BrE)
(of an atmosphere or place) unpleasantly damp, cool, and often musty.
unpleasantly damp and chilly

Examples

After days of rain, a dank smell filled the old cottage.
The basement was cold and dank, making it uncomfortable to stay there for long.
We walked through a dank tunnel that dripped with moisture from above.

Related Terms

3. ADJ NEW

/ dæŋk / (AmE), / dæŋk / (BrE)
(slang, internet) (of memes or online content) exceptionally funny, ironic, surreal, or shareable; often intentionally absurd or provocative.
slang for highly ironic or viral online content
slang informal

Examples

That meme is dank, circulating quickly across social media and earning thousands of shares overnight.
He only posts dank images that are absurd, ironic, and oddly relatable to late-night forums.
The subreddit became famous for its dank humor that mixes nostalgia, irony, and surreal memes.
