connector

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛktɚ / (AmE), / kəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
A device or part used to join two or more things together, especially in electronics or machinery.
device that joins things together

Examples

He searched the toolbox for a connector that would fit the two cables.
Make sure the connector is securely fastened to prevent any signal loss.
The technician replaced the broken connector on the back of the computer.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connection - a link or joining between things

3. NOUN NEW

/ kəˈnɛktə / (AmE), / kəˈnɛktə / (BrE)
A software component or service that links two or more computer systems or applications to enable data exchange, synchronization, or API integration.
software component that links systems

Examples

Businesses often install a connector to synchronize customer data between their ERP and CRM systems overnight.
The cloud service provides a ready-made connector that moves orders from the online store to the fulfillment system.
Developers wrote custom connectors to transform data formats between legacy databases and modern APIs.
