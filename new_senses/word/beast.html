beast

1. NOUN

/ bist / (AmE), / biːst / (BrE)
A person who is extremely cruel, savage, or lacking in human feeling.
cruel or savage person

Examples

Despite his charming appearance, he could be a real beast when angered.
She called her captor a beast after seeing his cruel behavior.
The dictator was described as a beast for the atrocities he committed against his people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brute - a savagely violent person or animal

Etymology

From Old French *beste*, from Latin *bestia* (animal, beast).

2. NOUN NEW

/ bist / (AmE), / biːst / (BrE)
Informal: a person or thing that is extremely powerful, effective, or impressively skilled at a particular activity, especially in sports or performance.
informal: powerful, impressive person or thing
informal slang

Examples

The new striker was a beast on the field, scoring two goals in ten minutes.
After months of training she became the team's defensive beast, stopping every attacker.
That gaming laptop is an absolute beast, running triple‑A titles without any slowdown.
