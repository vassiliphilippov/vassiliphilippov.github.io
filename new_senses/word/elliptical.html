elliptical

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɪptɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪptɪkəl / (BrE)
using very few words and often so leaving out details or explanations; concise or ambiguous by deliberate omission.
concise and often ambiguous (language)

Examples

His elliptical answers frustrated reporters seeking direct explanations.
The film's plot is told in an elliptical manner, forcing viewers to interpret hidden meanings.
The writer's elliptical style leaves much to the reader's imagination.

Synset Examples

the dialogue is elliptic and full of dark hints
the explanation was concise, even elliptical to the verge of obscurity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concise - giving a lot of information clearly and in a few words
  • Antonym: explicit - stated clearly and in detail

Etymology

From Greek elliptikos (falling short, deficient), from elleipein (to fall short).

2. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɪptɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪptɪkəl / (BrE)
Having an oval shape; rounded like an egg.
oval-shaped; like an egg

Examples

She admired the smooth, elliptical stones along the riverbank.
The artist painted an elliptical mirror hanging above the fireplace.
Their garden featured several elliptical flowerbeds for a softer landscape design.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oval - having a rounded, egg-like shape

Etymology

From Greek elleiptikos, from elleipsis (a falling short; ellipse).

Source: WordNet

3. NOUN NEW

/ ɪˈlɪptɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪptɪkəl / (BrE)
A stationary exercise machine (short for 'elliptical trainer') on which a person moves their feet in an oval path for low-impact aerobic workouts.
exercise machine (elliptical trainer)

Examples

She spent thirty minutes on the elliptical before starting her weight session.
Many hotels now include an elliptical in the gym for guests who travel frequently.
He prefers the elliptical because it is gentler on his knees than running.

Synset Examples

She spent thirty minutes on the elliptical before starting her weight session.
Many hotels now include an elliptical in the gym for guests who travel frequently.
He prefers the elliptical because it is gentler on his knees than running.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elliptical trainer - a full name for the exercise machine that provides a low-impact cardio workout
  • Synonym: cross-trainer - another name for the same kind of exercise machine that combines motion similar to running and stair-climbing

Etymology

Short for 'elliptical trainer' (exercise machine), from the adjective 'elliptical'.
