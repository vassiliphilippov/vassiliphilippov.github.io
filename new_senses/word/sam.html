SAM

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛs ˈeɪ ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˈɛs ˈeɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
a guided missile launched from land or ship against an airborne target
surface-to-air missile
american

Examples

Military forces deployed several SAMs along the border for air defense.
The convoy was protected by mobile SAM launchers.
The enemy aircraft was brought down by a SAM.

US federal vendor registry (SAM.gov)
american

Examples

Before bidding on federal contracts, vendors must be registered in SAM.
I searched SAM to find open solicitations for small businesses last night.
Their company renewed its registration in SAM to keep eligibility for government contracts.
