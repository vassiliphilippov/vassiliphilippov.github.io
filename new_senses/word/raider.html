raider

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdɚ / (AmE), / ˈreɪdə / (BrE)
a person or company that attempts to take over another company by acquiring a substantial share of its stock, often with the intention of replacing management
person seeking company takeover

Examples

After the raider gained a controlling stake, the entire board was replaced.
In the 1980s, many famous raiders shook up Wall Street with their aggressive tactics.
The company was vulnerable to a raider looking to buy up its outstanding shares.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: takeover - an attempt to gain control of a company by buying its shares
  • Synonym: acquirer - an individual or company that buys another company

Etymology

From raid + -er, originally meaning one who raids; later applied in business contexts (mid-20th c.).

2. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.də / (BrE)
a person who enters a place, especially during a war, in order to steal or take property by force
person who steals by force, especially in war

Examples

During the Middle Ages, coastal towns often feared Viking raiders.
The raiders made off with valuable artifacts after breaking into the museum.
The village was attacked at dawn by a group of raiders seeking treasure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plunderer - someone who steals goods, especially during war or disorder
  • Synonym: robber - a person who steals by force or threat
  • Antonym: defender - a person who protects against attack or danger

Etymology

From raid + -er; 'raid' from Scottish, probably from Old English 'rād' (a riding, a raid).

3. NOUN NEW

/ ˈreɪdər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdə / (BrE)
(in online gaming) a player who takes part in an organised raid — a coordinated group attempt to defeat powerful enemies or bosses to gain rewards such as loot or achievements.
player who joins organised raids

Examples

The guild recruited a skilled raider to help defeat the dungeon's final boss this weekend.
Experienced raiders coordinate abilities and timing to ensure every team member survives the boss fight.
After practice, she became the group's primary raider for weekly raids and rare loot runs.
