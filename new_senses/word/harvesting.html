harvesting

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrvəstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of gathering mature crops from the fields
gathering of mature crops

Examples

Advances in technology have made harvesting more efficient for farmers.
After weeks of planting, the community came together for the annual harvesting festival.
The harvesting of wheat typically begins in late summer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harvest - the process or period of gathering in crops

Etymology

From 'harvest' (Old English 'hærfest') meaning 'autumn, harvest-time'.
