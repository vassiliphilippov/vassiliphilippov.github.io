clusterfuck

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌstɚˌfʌk / (AmE), / ˈklʌstəˌfʌk / (BrE)
A situation that is extremely chaotic, disorganized, or badly managed, often resulting in everything going wrong.
chaotic, badly managed situation (vulgar slang)
slang vulgar

Examples

After the new policy was implemented, the whole system became a clusterfuck.
That meeting was a complete clusterfuck, with arguments and confusion everywhere.
The project quickly turned into a clusterfuck after everyone missed the deadline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiasco - a complete failure, especially in a ludicrous or humiliating way
  • Synonym: shambles - a state of total disorder

Etymology

From cluster (group) + fuck (vulgar expletive), originally US military slang.

2. VERB NEW

/ ˈklʌstɚˌfʌk / (AmE), / ˈklʌstəˌfʌk / (BrE)
to cause or experience a complete, disastrously disorganized failure; to make something chaotic or turn it into a mess (vulgar, slang).
cause a chaotic failure (vulgar)
slang vulgar

Examples

If management ignores the warnings, the launch will clusterfuck the whole customer experience by Friday.
Their rushed decisions clusterfucked the project, leaving the team to fix critical errors overnight.
Everyone joked that the new policy is just clusterfucking our attempts to improve morale.
