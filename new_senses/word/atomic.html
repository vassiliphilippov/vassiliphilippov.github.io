atomic

/ əˈtɑːmɪk / (AmE), / əˈtɒmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of atoms; concerned with atoms or their properties

Examples

Scientists are studying the atomic structure of various elements.
The atomic model helps explain chemical reactions at a fundamental level.
