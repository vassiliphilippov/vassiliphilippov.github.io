microblog

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪkroʊˌblɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈmʌɪkrəʊˌblɒɡ / (BrE)
A blog or online platform where users post brief text updates or content, typically with a restriction on post length.
blog with short or limited posts

Examples

Many celebrities use microblogs to share news and connect with fans instantly.
The company's marketing strategy focused on engaging customers through daily microblog posts.
Twitter is a popular microblog that allows users to post short messages.

Synset Examples

A survey by the Data Center of China Internet from 2010 showed that Chinese microblog users most often pursued content created by friends, experts in a specific field or related to celebrities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blog - a regularly updated website or web page, typically run by an individual or small group

Etymology

Formed from 'micro-' (small) + 'blog' (short for web log).
