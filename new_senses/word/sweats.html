sweats

1. NOUN

/ swɛts / (AmE), / swɛts / (BrE)
Loose-fitting pants and a matching sweatshirt, typically worn for exercising or as casual clothing.
casual set of sweatpants and sweatshirt

Examples

He wore his favorite sweats to the gym this morning.
It's cold outside—don't forget to pack your sweats for the trip.
She changed into her sweats after coming home from work to relax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tracksuit - jacket and trousers worn for sports or exercise

2. NOUN NEW

/ swɛts / (AmE), / swɛts / (BrE)
informal, slang — (in online gaming) players who play extremely competitively or try very hard to win, often appearing intense or overly focused on performance.
very competitive gamers (slang)
informal slang

Examples

The ranked lobby was full of sweats who refused to back down.
She refused to team with sweats who complained about every small mistake during the match.
At the weekend tournament, the sweats always practiced for hours to perfect their plays.
