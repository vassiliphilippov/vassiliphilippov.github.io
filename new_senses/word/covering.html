covering

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌv.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of protecting something by placing or forming a cover over it
act of protecting by covering

Examples

A thin covering of snow protected the early spring flowers from the frost.
He used a tarp as a temporary covering for the broken window.
The wound needs a sterile covering to prevent infection.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protection - the act of protecting or being protected

Etymology

From Middle English coveringe, from Old French covrir (to cover).
