bbl

1. NOUN

/ biː biː ɛl / (AmE), / biː biː ɛl / (BrE)
A standard abbreviation for 'barrel', a unit of capacity used especially for measuring beer (31 gallons) and oil (42 gallons).
abbreviation for 'barrel' as a unit of capacity

Examples

Crude oil prices are often quoted per bbl on international markets.
The brewery produced over 10,000 bbl of beer last year.
The tanker was loaded with 100,000 bbl of petroleum for export.

Synset Examples

a barrel of beer is 31 gallons and a barrel of oil is 42 gallons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel - Unit of capacity, especially for beer and oil

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'barrel'.
