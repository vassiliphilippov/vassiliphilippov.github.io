dialogue

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪ.ə.lɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.ə.lɒɡ / (BrE)
a conversation between two or more people, especially as a feature of a book, play, or movie
conversation between people

Examples

In the play, much of the humor comes from the quick dialogue between the two sisters.
Teachers encouraged an open dialogue with students to discuss the issue.
The dialogue between the main characters revealed their hidden feelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conversation - informal talking between people

Etymology

From Greek 'dialogos', from 'dia' (through) + 'logos' (speech, reason).
