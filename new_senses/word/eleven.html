eleven

1. NUM

/ ɪˈlɛvən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvən / (BrE)
the cardinal number equal to ten plus one; 11
the number 11

Examples

She turned eleven years old this month and had a big birthday party.
The clock struck eleven, reminding him it was time to go to bed.
There are eleven players on a standard football team.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: twelve - the cardinal number equal to eleven plus one; 12
  • Degree Lower: ten - the cardinal number equal to one less than eleven; 10

Etymology

From Old English 'endleofan', from Proto-Germanic *ainalif.
