achievement

1. NOUN

/ əˈtʃivmənt / (AmE), / əˈtʃiːvmənt / (BrE)
the act of successfully completing something or reaching a particular goal, especially after effort
the act of accomplishing something

Examples

Completing the marathon was a significant achievement for him after months of training.
He felt a sense of achievement when he finished the complex project ahead of schedule.
Her academic achievement was celebrated at the graduation ceremony.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accomplishment - something that has been achieved successfully
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success in achieving something

Etymology

From Old French *achevement* (completion), from *achever* (to finish).
