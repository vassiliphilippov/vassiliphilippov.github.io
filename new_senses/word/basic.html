basic

1. ADJ

/ ˈbeɪsɪk / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪk / (BrE)
(Chemistry) of or relating to, characteristic of, or containing a base; having the properties of a base.
chemistry: of or relating to a base

Examples

Baking soda is a basic substance, not an acid.
Some cleaning agents are highly basic and can neutralize acids.
The solution turned litmus paper blue, indicating it was basic in nature.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: acidic - having the properties of an acid

Etymology

From base + -ic, indicating 'pertaining to'.
