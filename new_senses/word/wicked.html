wicked

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪkɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪd / (BrE)
Morally wrong and deliberately committing evil or unrighteous acts.
morally wrong; evil

Examples

The judge sentenced the wicked criminal to life in prison for his crimes.
Throughout the novel, the wicked queen is feared for her ruthless deeds.
