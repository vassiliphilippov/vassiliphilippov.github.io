2. ADJ NEW

/ siˈzɛriən / (AmE), / siˈzɛəriən / (BrE)
Relating to a caesarean (surgical) birth; performed by, or pertaining to, delivery of a baby through an incision in the mother's abdomen and uterus.
relating to caesarean birth
medical

Examples

After complications, the doctors performed a Caesarian to deliver the baby safely.
She opted for a scheduled Caesarian delivery because her previous birth had been difficult.
The hospital recorded the birth as a Caesarian delivery on the mother's medical chart.
