to expand

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈspænd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspand / (BrE)
to become larger in size, volume, or quantity; to increase in extent or scope
become larger in size or amount

Examples

Metal will expand when it is heated.
Our services have expanded dramatically over the last decade.
The company plans to expand into new markets next year.

Synset Examples

his business expanded rapidly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contract - become smaller or shorter

Etymology

From Latin expandere, from ex- (out) + pandere (to spread).
