buttonology skill

1. NOUN NEW

/ ˌbʌtənˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌbʌt(ə)nˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
(informal) the practice or study of designing and optimising on-screen buttons and controls to improve usability, conversions, or user interaction.
study of UI button design
informal

Examples

Many product teams now rely on buttonology to increase click-through rates on their signup forms.
She studied buttonology to learn where to place CTAs for better user engagement.
A/B tests helped the team apply buttonology and choose the most effective color and copy.
