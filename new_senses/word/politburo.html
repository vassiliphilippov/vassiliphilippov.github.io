politburo

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːlɪtbjʊˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪtbjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
The principal policymaking and executive committee of a Communist Party.
executive committee of a Communist Party

Examples

During the Soviet era, only members of the politburo held true political power.
She was the first woman appointed to the Chinese Communist Party's politburo.
The politburo met to discuss the country's economic strategy for the upcoming year.

Etymology

From Russian politbyuro, short for politicheskoye byuro (political bureau).
