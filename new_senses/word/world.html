world

1. ADJ

/ wɝːld / (AmE), / wɜːld / (BrE)
Involving or relating to the entire earth; not limited in scope to a particular place, nation, or region.
relating to the entire earth; global

Examples

Climate change is a world issue that requires cooperation from every country.
Her research has received world recognition for its impact.
The summit focused on finding solutions to a world crisis.

Synset Examples

neither national nor continental but planetary

of worldwide significance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: global - relating to or involving the whole world
  • Synonym: worldwide - extending or reaching throughout the world
