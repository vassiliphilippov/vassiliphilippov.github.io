prod

1. NOUN

/ prɑd / (AmE), / prɒd / (BrE)
a pointed stick or instrument used to poke, push, or drive animals or people into action
pointed instrument for poking

Examples

He gave the tangled machine a sharp prod with a stick to get it working again.
Her impatient prod with her umbrella hurried us along the crowded street.
The farmer used a prod to guide the cattle into the barn.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goad - a pointed stick used to drive cattle or other animals

Etymology

Perhaps from Middle English 'prodden' (to poke).

Examples
