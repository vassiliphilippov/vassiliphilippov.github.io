tagger

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæɡər / (AmE), / ˈtæɡə / (BrE)
a computer program that assigns grammatical labels to the words or other constituents within text
program that labels grammar in text

Examples

Researchers improved the accuracy of their tagger for analyzing social media texts.
The tagger automatically labeled each word in the sentence with its correct part of speech.
When training a new language model, it is important to select a reliable tagger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parser - a computer program that analyzes the grammatical structure of sentences

3. NOUN

/ ˈtæɡər / (AmE), / ˈtæɡə / (BrE)
a person or system that assigns grammatical labels or categories to words in a text, especially in computational linguistics or language processing

Examples

After running the document through a tagger, each word was labeled with its grammatical category.
Many natural language processing tools include a built-in tagger for text analysis.
The linguist trained a tagger to automatically identify parts of speech in English sentences.

Related Terms
