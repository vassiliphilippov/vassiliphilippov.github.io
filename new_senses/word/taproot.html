taproot

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæpˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈtapˌruːt / (BrE)
an important central source or origin from which growth or development springs, especially in a figurative sense
central source or origin (figurative)

Examples

A family’s values often form the taproot of a child’s character.
Her passion for music served as the taproot of her lifelong career.
The taproot of the company’s success lies in its commitment to innovation.

Synset Examples

the taproot of his resentment
genius and insanity spring from the same taproot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: root - the origin or basis of something

Etymology

From 'taproot', the main root of a plant, used figuratively for something that is a central source.

2. NOUN

/ ˈtæpˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈtapˌruːt / (BrE)
The main root of a plant that grows vertically downward and gives off smaller lateral roots.
main root growing downward (botany)
medical

Examples

Many desert plants develop a deep taproot to reach underground water sources.
The carrot is an example of a plant with a thick taproot.
When the taproot is damaged, the tree may not grow as tall.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fibrous root - root system with many thin roots, no main root

Etymology

Formed from 'tap' (plug, spout) + 'root', likening the main root to a tap or stopper.

Source: WordNet
WordNet ID: oewn-13146809-n
Synset POS: n

3. PROPN NEW

/ ˈtæpˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈtapˌruːt / (BrE)
A 2021 Bitcoin protocol upgrade (a backward-compatible soft fork) that improves privacy and scripting capabilities by enabling Schnorr signatures and more flexible smart-contract constructions.
Bitcoin protocol upgrade (2021)

Examples

Bitcoin's 2021 upgrade, Taproot, improved transaction privacy and enabled more flexible smart contracts.
Miners signalled support for Taproot before the soft fork activated on the Bitcoin network.
Developers praised Taproot for enabling Schnorr signatures that reduce transaction size and improve privacy.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft fork - a backward-compatible change to a blockchain protocol; the category of upgrade Taproot used
  • Synonym: Schnorr signature - a digital-signature scheme that Taproot enabled for Bitcoin to improve efficiency and aggregation

Etymology

Named after the botanical term 'taproot' (the main root of a plant), used metaphorically to suggest a fundamental root-level improvement.
