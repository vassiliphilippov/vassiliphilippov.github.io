modeller

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑːdələr / (AmE), / ˈmɒdələ / (BrE)
a person who makes or designs models of objects, often as a profession or hobby
person who makes models
british

Examples

As a professional modeller, she specializes in creating complex architectural prototypes.
Many hobbyists join clubs to share tips and ideas with fellow modellers.
The modeller spent hours crafting detailed miniature buildings for the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modeler - person who creates models (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'model' + '-er', denoting someone who models; chiefly British spelling of 'modeler'.
