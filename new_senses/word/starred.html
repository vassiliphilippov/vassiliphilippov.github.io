starred

1. ADJ

/ stɑrd / (AmE), / stɑːd / (BrE)
marked with an asterisk (*) to indicate importance or for special notice
marked with an asterisk

Examples

Only the starred questions on the form need to be answered.
Please pay attention to the starred notes at the end of the document.
The starred items in the menu indicate chef’s recommendations.

Synset Examples

the starred items

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asterisked - marked with one or more asterisks

2. ADJ NEW

/ stɑrd / (AmE), / stɑːd / (BrE)
(in online contexts) marked, saved, or favored by a user, typically by clicking a star icon to highlight or bookmark an item.
saved/favourited online

Examples

The project was starred by thousands of developers on GitHub.
She moved the important message to her starred folder to read later.
He starred the recipe on the cooking site so he could find it quickly.
