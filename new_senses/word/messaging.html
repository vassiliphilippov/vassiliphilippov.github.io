messaging

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛsɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the sending and receiving of electronic messages, especially by computer or mobile devices, such as email, text, or instant messages
sending and receiving electronic messages

Examples

He checked his messaging platform several times an hour for updates.
Many businesses rely on messaging systems to communicate with clients and colleagues.
The new app offers secure messaging for all users.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication - the exchanging of information by speaking, writing, or using some other medium

Etymology

From 'message' + '-ing', originally in computing contexts from the 1980s.
