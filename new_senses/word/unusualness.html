unusualness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈjuːʒuəlnɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈjuːʒuəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being not usual, rare, or uncommon
the state of being unusual or rare

Examples

Her fashion sense is defined by its unusualness and originality.
Scientists were attracted by the unusualness of the rock formation.
The unusualness of the weather startled the local residents.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rarity - the state of being rare, infrequent occurrence
  • Antonym: commonness - the state or quality of being common

Etymology

From the adjective 'unusual' + suffix '-ness', indicating a state or quality.
