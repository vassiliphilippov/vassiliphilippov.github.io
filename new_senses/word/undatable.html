undatable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdeɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈdeɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to assign a specific date to; not capable of being dated
not able to be given a date

Examples

Many undated manuscripts in the collection remain undatable due to the lack of contextual clues.
The ancient artifact was considered undatable because there was no reliable evidence of its origin.
Without carbon dating results, the fossil is currently undatable by modern scientific methods.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: datable - able to be given a date

Etymology

From un- (not) + datable (able to be dated).

2. ADJ NEW

/ ʌnˈdeɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdeɪtəbəl / (BrE)
(informal, slang) not considered suitable or attractive enough to date; unlikely to be chosen as a romantic partner.
unsuitable for dating
informal slang

Examples

On dating apps, many people quickly judge whether someone is undatable from their bio and photos.
He realized his jokes made him seem undatable after several matches stopped messaging him.
Calling a person undatable can be hurtful, so she tried to give gentler feedback instead.
