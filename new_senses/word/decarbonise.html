to decarbonise

1. VERB

/ dɪˈkɑːrbəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˌdiːˈkɑːbənʌɪz / (BrE)
to remove carbon deposits from the internal parts of an engine
remove carbon from an engine

Examples

After years of use, the cylinders were thoroughly decarbonised.
It's important to decarbonise old engines to prevent performance issues.
The mechanic needs to decarbonise the engine to improve its efficiency.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to decarbonize - American English variant of 'decarbonise'

Etymology

From de- (removal) + carbon + -ise (verb-forming suffix).
