mangle

1. VERB

/ ˈmæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmaŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
to smooth or press (laundry or cloth) using a mangle, a machine with rollers
press laundry with a mangle

Examples

In the old days, people would mangle their clothes to achieve a crisp finish.
My grandmother showed me how to mangle the sheets after washing them.
The workers used to mangle the linens at the hotel laundry room.

From Dutch 'mangel' (a laundry roller), from German 'Mangel'.
