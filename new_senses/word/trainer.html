trainer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnər / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnə / (BrE)
a machine on the ground used to simulate the conditions of flying an aircraft, for training pilots
flight simulator machine

Examples

Advanced flight trainers can simulate challenging weather and emergency situations.
Before taking real flight lessons, student pilots spent hours in the trainer.
The trainer uses realistic controls and displays to teach cockpit procedures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flight simulator - an apparatus that artificially reproduces aircraft flight and the environment for pilot training

2. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnər / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnə / (BrE)
a person who teaches skills or provides instruction, especially in a sport, profession, or for animals
person who teaches or instructs skills

Examples

She hopes to become a professional dog trainer after completing her course.
The trainer prepared the team for the upcoming championship.
The new fitness trainer helped me design a personalized workout routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - person who trains athletes or performers
  • Antonym: trainee - person who is being trained
  • Synonym: instructor - person who teaches a particular skill

Etymology

From 'train' + '-er', denoting someone who trains.
