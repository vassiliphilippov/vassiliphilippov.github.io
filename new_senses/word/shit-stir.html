2. NOUN NEW

/ ʃɪt stɜr / (AmE), / ʃɪt stɜː / (BrE)
An informal, often vulgar term for a person who deliberately provokes, incites, or spreads trouble or conflict, especially online or in group conversations.
person who provokes trouble
informal slang vulgar

Examples

The moderators banned a persistent shit-stir who kept starting arguments in the comment threads.
No one trusted the new member because he acted like a shit-stir from the first post.
Whenever politics came up, the group fell silent until a shit-stir injected chaos.
