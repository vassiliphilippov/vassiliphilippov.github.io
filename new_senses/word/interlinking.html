interlinking

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈlɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
connected or linked closely together, often in a way that makes separation difficult or forms an intricate whole.
closely connected or linked

Examples

The interlinking branches formed a dense canopy above the forest path.
The author explores the interlinking themes of love and loss throughout the novel.
These policies depend on a series of interlinking factors to function properly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connected - joined or linked together
  • Synonym: interconnected - having all parts or things connected or related

Etymology

Formed from 'interlink' (to connect together) + '-ing'.
