storefront

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔːrˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈstɔːˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
the front side of a store, typically facing the street and often featuring display windows
front of a shop with display windows

Examples

Graffiti was cleaned off the storefront before the shop opened for business.
Many small businesses decorate their storefront during the holidays to attract customers.
The new bakery has a beautiful storefront with large glass windows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopfront - the front side of a shop with windows facing the street
