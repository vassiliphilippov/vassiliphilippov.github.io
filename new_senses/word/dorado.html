Dorado

1. PROPN

/ dəˈrɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / dəˈrɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
a constellation in the southern sky near Reticulum and Pictor, containing most of the Large Magellanic Cloud
constellation in the southern sky

Examples

Astronomers study the Large Magellanic Cloud, which lies within Dorado.
The constellation Dorado is visible from the southern hemisphere during the summer months.
Unlike Orion, Dorado cannot be seen from most northern latitudes.

Etymology

From Spanish 'dorado' meaning 'gilded' or 'golden', referring to the dolphinfish (mahi-mahi), which the constellation represents.
