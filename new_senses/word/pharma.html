pharma

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑːrmə / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
a company that manufactures and sells pharmaceutical drugs
pharmaceutical company
informal

Examples

Many investors are watching the pharma sector for signs of new drug approvals.
Several major pharma companies announced plans to merge this year.
The government held a meeting with leading pharma executives to discuss vaccine distribution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmaceutical company - a company that develops and sells medicines

Etymology

Shortened form of 'pharmaceutical' or 'pharmaceutical company'.
