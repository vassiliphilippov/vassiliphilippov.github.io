mnemonic

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈmɑːnɪk / (AmE), / nɪˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)
a word, phrase, rhyme, or other device used to help remember something
memory aid or device

Examples

'ROY G. BIV' is a popular mnemonic for recalling the colors of the rainbow.
He invented a new mnemonic to help him memorize chemical formulas.
The teacher taught us a clever mnemonic to remember the planets' order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: memory aid - device that helps someone remember something

Etymology

From Greek mnēmonikos (of memory), from mnēmōn (mindful).

3. NOUN NEW

/ nɪˈmɑːnɪk / (AmE), / nɪˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)
(in computing, especially cryptocurrency) a sequence of words used as a seed phrase to back up and recover access to a digital wallet.
cryptocurrency seed phrase

Examples

Be sure to write down your twelve-word mnemonic and store it in a safe place.
The wallet app will display a new mnemonic when you create a backup for your account.
If you lose your mnemonic, you may permanently lose access to the funds in the wallet.
