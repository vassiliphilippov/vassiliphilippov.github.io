modeled

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːdld / (AmE), / ˈmɒdld / (BrE)
(of a surface or feature) shaped or fashioned in a way that resembles sculpture; having clear, well-defined contours or form.
shaped like sculpture

Examples

His face had a modeled quality, as if carved by an artist.
Sunlight highlighted the modeled muscles of the ancient statue.
The actress had modeled cheekbones that made her stand out on camera.

Synset Examples

her finely modeled features
rendered with … vivid sculptural effect
the sculpturesque beauty of the athletes' bodies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptural - having the qualities of sculpture; shaped like a sculpture

Etymology

From the verb 'model', ultimately from Italian 'modello', meaning 'a model or pattern'.

2. ADJ NEW

/ \u02c8m\u0251\u02d0dld / (AmE), / \u02c8m\u0252dld / (BrE)
(in computer graphics and CAD) created or represented as a three-dimensional digital model using modelling software; having an origin as a digital 3D model.
created as a 3D digital model

Examples

The game's protagonist was carefully modeled to capture realistic facial expressions and skin detail.
The design team presented a modeled prototype that allowed clients to inspect the product before manufacturing.
The architect showed a modeled version of the building so the committee could evaluate sunlight and shadows.
