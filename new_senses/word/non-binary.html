non-binary

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈbaɪˌnɛri / (AmE), / nɒnˈbaɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to or identifying as a gender identity that does not fit within the traditional categories of male or female
not identifying exclusively as male or female

Examples

Alex is non-binary and uses they/them pronouns.
Some workplaces are introducing policies to support non-binary employees.
The award category was open to both male, female, and non-binary artists.

Synset Examples

Being straighty, he struggles to relate to non-binary friends.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genderqueer - a person who does not conform to conventional gender distinctions
  • Antonym: binary - relating to or involving two things, especially male and female gender

Etymology

Formed from 'non-' (not) and 'binary' (having two parts); used in gender identity discourse since the late 20th century.
