Bi

1. NOUN

/ biː aɪ / (AmE), / biː aɪ / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for bismuth, a heavy, brittle, diamagnetic metallic element with atomic number 83
the symbol for bismuth (element)

Examples

Lead can often contain trace amounts of Bi after the refining process.
The element Bi has a wide range of industrial uses due to its unique properties.
The periodic table lists Bi as having the atomic number 83.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bismuth - a heavy, brittle chemical element, atomic number 83

Etymology

From New Latin bismuthum; 'Bi' is the chemical symbol standardized by IUPAC.
