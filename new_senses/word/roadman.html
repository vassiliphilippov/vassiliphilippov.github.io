roadman

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to repair or maintain roads
a man who repairs or maintains roads

Examples

The roadman worked tirelessly to fix the potholes after the storm.
The council hired another roadman to help with the increased repairs.
You could see a roadman laying asphalt along the main street early this morning.
