deliberation

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
The quality of being slow and careful in thought or action; the absence of haste.
slow and careful thought or action

Examples

His words were chosen with deliberation, avoiding any possibility of misunderstanding.
She moved across the stage with great deliberation, making each gesture carefully and thoughtfully.
The panel reached its decision after much deliberation and calm discussion.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: haste - excessive speed or urgency; hurriedness
  • Synonym: carefulness - the quality of being cautious and avoiding mistakes

Etymology

From Latin deliberatio (consideration), from deliberare (to weigh, consider).

Examples
