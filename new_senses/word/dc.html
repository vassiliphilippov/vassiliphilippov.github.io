DC

1. NOUN

/ ˌdi ˈsi / (AmE), / ˌdiː ˈsiː / (BrE)
the abbreviation for the District of Columbia, a federal district containing the city of Washington, which is the capital of the United States
abbreviation for the District of Columbia, U.S. capital
american

Examples

I have a meeting scheduled in DC next week.
Many politicians live and work in DC during the legislative session.
The cherry blossoms in DC attract thousands of visitors every spring.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: District of Columbia - the federal district containing the city of Washington, capital of the United States

Etymology

Short for District of Columbia, from the Latin name for Christopher Columbus, after whom the district is named.

2. NOUN

/ diː siː / (AmE), / diː siː / (BrE)
an electric current that flows in one direction only, as opposed to alternating current (AC)
direct electric current

Examples

Many electronic devices use DC power supplies instead of AC.
Solar panels generate DC electricity, which must be converted for home use.
The battery provides DC to the circuit, ensuring a steady voltage.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: AC - an electric current that reverses direction regularly

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'direct current'.
