coruscant

1. ADJ

/ kəˈrʌsənt / (AmE), / kəˈrʌskənt / (BrE)
Emitting brief flashes of bright light; sparkling or glittering.
flashing or sparkling with light

Examples

A coruscant trail of sparks followed the firework into the night sky.
Her jewelry was coruscant in the ballroom's brilliant lighting.
The coruscant surface of the lake caught the last rays of sunset.

2. PROPN NEW

/ kəˈrʌsənt / (AmE), / kəˈrʌskənt / (BrE)
A fictional planet in the Star Wars universe, known as the galactic capital and characterised by a vast, continuous urban cityscape covering its surface.
capital planet in Star Wars

Examples

Fans visited the exhibition to see a scale model of Coruscant used in the original films.
The Senate building on Coruscant dominated the skyline of the planet-city for centuries.
In the novel, the diplomat recalled arriving at Coruscant beneath endless skyscrapers and neon lights.
