friendzone

1. VERB

/ ˈfrɛndˌzoʊn / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndˌzəʊn / (BrE)
(informal, often slang) To treat (someone) as a friend and reject or not reciprocate their romantic or sexual interest.
treat someone as only a friend; reject romantic interest
informal slang

Examples

After they went on a few dates, he realized she had friendzoned him.
He worries that if he confesses his feelings, she might friendzone him.
She didn't want to friendzone him, but she just didn't feel the same way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reject - to refuse to accept, believe in, or agree with something

Etymology

Formed from 'friend' + 'zone'; popularized in 21st-century English, especially online.

Informal. the situation in which one person has romantic or sexual feelings for another but is seen only as a friend, so the feelings are not reciprocated.
state of unreciprocated romantic interest
informal slang

Examples

Being stuck in the friendzone made him avoid dating for months afterward.
They joked about the friendzone after she introduced him as a 'great friend'.
Therapy helped her accept life in the friendzone and move on to new relationships.
