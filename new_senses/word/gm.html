/ dʒi ɛm / (AmE), / dʒiː ɛm / (BrE)
Abbreviation for genetically modified; used before nouns to describe organisms, foods, or materials whose genetic material has been altered by genetic engineering.
abbreviation for genetically modified

Examples

Many consumers refuse to buy gm products because they worry about long-term health effects.
The supermarket introduced clear labeling for gm ingredients to help shoppers make informed choices.
Researchers compared yields of gm maize with conventional varieties across several regions.
