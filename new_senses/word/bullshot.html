bullshot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈbʊlʃɒt / (BrE)
A cocktail made with vodka and beef bouillon or consommé, often served hot or cold as an appetizer drink.
vodka and beef bouillon cocktail

Examples

He ordered a bullshot at the bar to warm him up on a cold night.
Some people prefer their bullshot served hot, especially during winter brunches.
The recipe for bullshot includes both vodka and beef broth, making it a savory drink.

misleading promotional game image
informal

Examples

The game's early trailers used a bullshot that made environments look unrealistically detailed.
