LLD

1. NOUN

/ ˌel ˌel ˈdi / (AmE), / ˌel ˌel ˈdiː / (BrE)
an honorary degree of Doctor of Laws awarded by a university
honorary Doctor of Laws degree

Examples

After many years of service to the community, she was awarded an LLD by her alma mater.
He proudly listed his honorary LLD among his academic achievements.
The university presented an LLD to the renowned human rights advocate during graduation ceremonies.
