printable

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of content or language) suitable for publication because it does not contain material that is morally or legally objectionable.
suitable for publication; not offensive

Examples

Please ensure that all comments on this forum are printable and appropriate for all readers.
Some of the jokes were not printable in a family newspaper.
The editor said that only printable language should be included in the report.

Synset Examples

printable language

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unprintable - not suitable for publication because of offensive content

Etymology

From 'print' + '-able', formed in English.

2. ADJ NEW

/ ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a digital model or design) able to be produced using a 3D printer; suitable for 3D printing without major modification.
suitable for 3D printing

Examples

The designer uploaded a printable STL file so hobbyists could make their own figure at home.
Before selling, she checked that the model was printable on common consumer printers without supports.
The repository shows which designs are printable and provides recommended settings for each printer.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: 3D-printable - able to be produced using 3D printing (synonymous term often used with files and models).

Etymology

From 'print' + '-able'; extended in the 21st century to refer to objects or files suitable for 3D printing.
