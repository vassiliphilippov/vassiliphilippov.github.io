gangsta

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæŋstə / (AmE), / ˈɡæŋstə / (BrE)
A member of a youth gang, especially as used in African American Vernacular English or hip-hop culture.
member of a youth gang (slang)
slang

Examples

He tried to act like a gangsta, but everyone could tell he wasn't really tough.
Many rap lyrics reflect the realities faced by a gangsta in the inner city.
The film tells the story of a young gangsta trying to escape a life of crime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gangster - member of a criminal gang

Etymology

Altered pronunciation and spelling of 'gangster', influenced by African American Vernacular English.
