toolbox

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːlˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈtuːlˌbɒks / (BrE)
a container, such as a box or chest, used for storing and carrying hand tools
container for holding hand tools

Examples

After buying new hammers and screwdrivers, she organized them neatly in her toolbox.
Every toolbox should include a tape measure, some pliers, and a screwdriver set.
The mechanic opened his toolbox to find the right wrench for the job.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool chest - a large chest for storing tools
