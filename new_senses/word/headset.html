headset

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌsɛt / (BrE)
A device worn over the head that consists of a pair of headphones, typically also including a microphone, used for listening to audio and/or speaking.
pair of headphones, often with microphone

Examples

Gamers often use a headset with a built-in microphone to talk to teammates.
She put on her headset and joined the online meeting.
The pilot wore a special headset to communicate with air traffic control.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headphones - A pair of small loudspeakers worn on or around the head over a user's ears.

Etymology

From 'head' + 'set', referring to a set of audio receivers worn on the head.

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈhɛdˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌsɛt / (BrE)
A head-mounted display device that presents immersive virtual-reality or augmented-reality images to the wearer, often including integrated audio, cameras, and motion-tracking sensors.
head-mounted display for VR/AR

Examples

He strapped on the headset to enter the immersive VR game.
The museum loaned us a lightweight headset that overlays historical information onto exhibits.
Developers tested several headsets to compare field of view and motion-tracking accuracy.

Synset Examples

He strapped on the headset to enter the immersive VR game.
The museum loaned us a lightweight headset that overlays historical information onto exhibits.
Developers tested several headsets to compare field of view and motion-tracking accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head-mounted display - A display device worn on the head that places images close to the eyes; often abbreviated HMD.
  • Synonym: headphones - Audio devices worn on or around the head; related but primarily for listening rather than displaying images.

Etymology

From 'head' + 'set' (a set of equipment worn on the head); extended to modern head-mounted displays.
