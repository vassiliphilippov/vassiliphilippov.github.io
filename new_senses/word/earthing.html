earthing

1. NOUN

/ ˈɝːθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɜːθɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of connecting electrical equipment to the ground for safety reasons
connecting electrical equipment to earth

Examples

Proper earthing is essential to prevent electric shocks in households.
The electrician checked the earthing before installing new appliances.
Without adequate earthing, electrical surges can damage sensitive devices.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grounding - connecting electrical equipment to the ground (mainly US English)

Etymology

From the noun 'earth' (meaning ground) with the suffix '-ing.'
