stonecutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈstoʊnˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut, shape, or carve stone, especially for building or sculpture
someone who cuts or carves stone

Examples

As a skilled stonecutter, he carved intricate patterns into the marble.
Many ancient temples were built by master stonecutters using traditional tools.
The stonecutter carefully shaped each block for the new cathedral.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mason - a person who works with stone, especially in building

Etymology

From 'stone' + 'cutter'; attested since the 15th century.
