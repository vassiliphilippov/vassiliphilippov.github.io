to gold-plate

1. VERB

/ ˈɡoʊldˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌpleɪt / (BrE)
to cover (an object) with a thin layer of gold, often by electrochemical means.
cover with a layer of gold

Examples

He learned how to gold-plate small pieces of copper wire in his chemistry class.
Many household items are gold-plated to give them a more luxurious appearance.
The jeweler will gold-plate the bracelet to restore its shine.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gild - to cover with a thin layer of gold or gold decoration
