far-right

1. ADJ

/ fɑr ˈraɪt / (AmE), / fɑː ˈraɪt / (BrE)
holding extreme conservative or reactionary political views, especially those strongly opposed to social or political liberalism or reform
extremely conservative in politics

Examples

Her speech was criticized as promoting a far-right agenda.
Many far-right groups have gained attention in recent years for their controversial policies.
The candidate is known for his far-right views on immigration and national identity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: far-left - holding extreme liberal or socialist political views

2. ADJ NEW

/ fɑr ˈraɪt / (AmE), / fɑː ˈraɪt / (BrE)
Situated at or toward the extreme right side of something (position rather than political orientation).
at the extreme right (positional)

Examples

Move the label to the far-right corner so it doesn't cover the main text.
Place the navigation menu on the far-right side of the header for better balance.
The teacher pointed to the far-right column where students should input their answers.
