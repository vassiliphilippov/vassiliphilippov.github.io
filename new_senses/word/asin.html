Asin

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːsɪn / (AmE), / ˈɑːsɪn / (BrE)
the seventh month of the Hindu calendar, typically falling between September and October in the Gregorian calendar.
seventh month of the Hindu calendar

Examples

Harvest time in northern India often coincides with the month of Asin.
Many rituals and celebrations are held during Asin every year.
The festival of Navaratri usually falls in Asin according to the Hindu calendar.

Etymology

From Sanskrit āśvina, relating to the Ashvins, divine horsemen in Hindu mythology.

A unique alphanumeric identifier assigned by Amazon to a product or item in its online catalog, used to find, track, and reference that product.
Amazon product identifier

Examples

She copied the ASIN from the Amazon listing to compare prices.
Enter the product's ASIN into the search box to find matching listings quickly.
The seller updated each item's database record with its correct ASIN last night.
