ASMR

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈɑr / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌɛs ˌɛm ˈɑː / (BrE)
a tingling, static-like sensation on the skin, usually triggered by specific auditory or visual stimuli, that induces relaxation or euphoria
tingling sensation from certain sounds or sights

Examples

Her first experience of ASMR was triggered by the sound of whispering voices.
Many people watch ASMR videos online to help them relax before bed.
The gentle tapping sounds caused a pleasant ASMR sensation down his spine.

Etymology

Acronym for Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response, coined in 2010s Internet communities.
