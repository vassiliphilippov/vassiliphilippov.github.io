goldilocks

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊl.diˌlɑks / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.diˌlɒks / (BrE)
An early-flowering perennial plant of southern and southeastern Europe, with yellow flower heads resembling those of goldenrod.
European perennial plant with yellow flowers

Examples

Botanists identified the yellow-flowered plant as a species of goldilocks.
Gardeners sometimes cultivate goldilocks for its cheerful, daisy-like blooms.
The meadow was dotted with bright goldilocks blooming in early spring.

Etymology

Named for the resemblance of its yellow flowers to golden or 'golden locks' (hair).
