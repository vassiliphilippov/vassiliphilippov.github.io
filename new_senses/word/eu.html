EU

1. NOUN

/ iː juː / (AmE), / iː juː / (BrE)
abbreviation for the European Union, an international organization of European countries formed to promote economic and political cooperation.
abbreviation for the European Union

Examples

Several countries hope to join the EU in the coming years.
The EU has implemented new regulations on environmental protection.
Trade agreements between the EU and other nations are often complex.

Synset Examples

he tried to take Britain into the European Union

Related Terms

  • Synonym: European Union - the full name for EU, an international organization of European countries

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'European Union'.

2. NOUN NEW

/ iː juː / (AmE), / iː juː / (BrE)
(in technology, online services, and commerce) a label for the Europe content/market or server region used to route users, price goods, or restrict content; may refer to the geographic region broadly rather than the political European Union.
tech/market label for the Europe region

Examples

The game's European servers will open in the EU region next Tuesday at noon.
Change your account country to view prices in the EU store before you check out.
For compliance we keep backups on servers physically located inside the EU, not elsewhere.
