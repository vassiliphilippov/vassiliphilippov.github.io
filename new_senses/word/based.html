based

/ beɪst / (AmE), / beɪst / (BrE)

3. ADJ NEW

/ beɪst / (AmE), / beɪst / (BrE)
slang (informal). Admirable or worthy of respect because someone or something is authentic, unapologetic, or true to their principles.
internet slang: admirable, unapologetic
slang informal

Examples

Her honest criticism was based, and many praised her for speaking the truth.
That political stance is based — it refuses to bend under popular pressure.
When he defended unpopular facts, people called his comment based rather than simply rude.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authentic - genuine; true to oneself
  • Synonym: unapologetic - not showing regret; standing firmly by one's beliefs or actions
  • Antonym: cringe - something embarrassing or worthy of contempt; often presented as the opposite of 'based' online

Etymology

From internet slang in the 2010s; popularized in online culture (notably by rapper Lil B) to mean being yourself or unapologetically authentic.
