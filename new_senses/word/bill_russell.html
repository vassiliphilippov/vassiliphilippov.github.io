Bill Russell

1. PROPN

/ bɪl ˈrʌsəl / (AmE), / bɪl ˈrʌsəl / (BrE)
(1934–2022) An American basketball player, widely regarded as one of the greatest centers in NBA history, known for his time with the Boston Celtics and his record of winning eleven NBA championships.
American basketball player and NBA legend

Examples

Bill Russell won eleven NBA championships with the Boston Celtics, making him one of the most successful players in basketball history.
In 2011, Bill Russell was awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom for his achievements both on and off the court.
Many regard Bill Russell as the greatest defensive player ever to play in the NBA.
