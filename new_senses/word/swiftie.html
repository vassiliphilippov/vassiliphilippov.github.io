swiftie

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪfti / (AmE), / ˈswɪfti / (BrE)
A fan of the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift.
fan of Taylor Swift
informal

Examples

A group of swifties gathered outside the arena to welcome Taylor Swift.
If you're a true swiftie, you probably know all the lyrics to her songs.
She proudly calls herself a swiftie and attends every concert she can.

Etymology

Formed from 'Swift' (Taylor Swift's surname) with diminutive '-ie' suffix, denoting an admirer.
