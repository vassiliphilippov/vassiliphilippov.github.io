production

1. NOUN

/ prəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the amount of something that is made or produced, especially within a particular period of time
amount produced in a period

Examples

Coffee production in Brazil reached a record high last year.
Oil production is expected to decline as reserves run out.
The factory has increased its production to meet rising demand.

Synset Examples

production was up in the second quarter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: output - the amount of something produced by a person, machine, or industry

Etymology

From Latin 'productio', meaning a bringing forth or producing.
