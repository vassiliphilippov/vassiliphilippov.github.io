Afro-Asian

1. ADJ

/ ˌæfroʊˈeɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌafrəʊˈeɪʒən / (BrE)
Relating to Africa and Asia or their peoples.
relating to Africa and Asia

Examples

2. NOUN NEW

/ \u02cc\u00e6fro\u028a\u02c8e\u026a\u0292\u0259n / (AmE), / \u02ccafr\u0259\u028a\u02c8e\u026a\u0292\u0259n / (BrE)
A person of mixed African and Asian heritage; someone whose ancestry or cultural identity includes both African and Asian roots.
person of mixed African and Asian descent

Examples

She identifies as an Afro-Asian and celebrates both her African and Asian roots.
Many Afro-Asians in the city organize cultural festivals to honor multiple family traditions.
Growing up, he felt caught between communities until he met other Afro-Asians who understood him.
