PM

1. NOUN

/ pi ɛm / (AmE), / piː em / (BrE)
the period of time between noon and midnight; used to indicate time after 12:00 midday and before 12:00 midnight.
the period from noon to midnight

Examples

He usually returns from work around 7PM every day.
The meeting is scheduled for 3PM tomorrow afternoon.
The store closes at 10PM on weekdays.

Synset Examples

AM to PM

Related Terms

  • Antonym: AM - the period between midnight and noon

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'post meridiem' meaning 'after midday'.
