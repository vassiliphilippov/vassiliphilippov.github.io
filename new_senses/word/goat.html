Goat

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊt / (BrE)
The tenth sign of the zodiac, corresponding to Capricorn, occurring when the sun is in this sign from about December 22 to January 19.
tenth sign of the zodiac (Capricorn)

Examples

Her horoscope said that the Goat would bring new opportunities this year.
In Chinese culture, the Goat and Capricorn are considered different zodiac signs.
People born under the sign of the Goat are said to be practical and ambitious.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Capricorn - the zodiac sign represented by a goat, the tenth sign

2. NOUN NEW

/ ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊt / (BrE)
Informal slang for a person widely regarded as the greatest of all time in a particular field (for example, sports, music, or entertainment).
greatest of all time (slang)
informal slang

Examples

Many fans regard Michael Jordan as the GOAT of basketball because of his six championships.
After three decades, critics still call Beyoncé the GOAT for her influence on pop music.
He joked that his grandfather was the family GOAT at storytelling and jokes.
