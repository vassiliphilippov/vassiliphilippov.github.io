baked

1. ADJ

/ beɪkt / (AmE), / beɪkt / (BrE)
(of food, especially bread and pastries) cooked by dry heat, especially in an oven.
cooked by dry heat in an oven

Examples

I bought fresh baked bread from the bakery this morning.
She prefers baked goods over fried snacks for a healthier diet.
The restaurant's baked pastries are famous all over the city.

Synset Examples

baked goods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roasted - (of food) cooked by exposing to dry heat, usually in an oven or over an open fire
  • Antonym: fried - (of food) cooked in hot fat or oil
  • Antonym: steamed - (of food) cooked by steam

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'bake,' from Old English 'bacan' (to bake).
