fiendish

1. ADJ

/ ˈfiːndɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfiːndɪʃ / (BrE)
Extremely evil or cruel; showing great cruelty or wickedness.
extremely evil or cruel

Examples

She regarded him with a fiendish grin, knowing he had lost the game.
The dictator was notorious for his fiendish cruelty toward his enemies.
The villain devised a fiendish plan to control the entire city.
