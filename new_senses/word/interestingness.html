interestingness

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntəˈrɛstɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˈɛstɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being interesting or capable of attracting and holding one’s attention
quality of being interesting

Examples

Researchers study the interestingness of different topics to predict trends in popular culture.
She was concerned about the interestingness of her presentation and worked hard to make it captivating.
The interestingness of the story kept the audience engaged until the very end.

Synset Examples

they said nothing of great interest
primary colors can add interest to a room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interest - quality of arousing curiosity or attention
  • Antonym: dullness - quality of lacking interest or excitement

Etymology

From 'interesting' + '-ness', forming nouns of quality or state.
