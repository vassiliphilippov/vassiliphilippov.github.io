T-shaped

1. ADJ

/ ˈtiː ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtiː ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of the letter T; shaped like a T.
shaped like the letter T

Examples

A T-shaped intersection requires drivers to either turn left or right.
The artist created a sculpture with a distinctive T-shaped design.
The house has a T-shaped floor plan with a central hallway and two wings.
