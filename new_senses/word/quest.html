quest

1. NOUN

/ kwɛst / (AmE), / kwɛst / (BrE)
a long or arduous search for something, often one involving pursuit or adventure
long or difficult search for something

Examples

Her lifelong quest for knowledge led her to travel the world.
The knight embarked on a quest to find the lost treasure.
They joined a quest to discover new sources of clean energy.

Synset Examples

a quest for diamonds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: search - the act of looking for something
  • Synonym: pursuit - the action of pursuing or striving for something

Etymology

From Old French *queste* (search), from Latin *quaerere* (to seek).
