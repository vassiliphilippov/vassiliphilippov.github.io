ABM

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ.biːˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.biːˈɛm / (BrE)
a missile designed to intercept and destroy incoming intercontinental ballistic missiles
anti-ballistic missile

Examples

During the treaty talks, the deployment of ABMs was a major point of negotiation.
Modern ABMs are capable of tracking and destroying multiple incoming threats simultaneously.
The country developed an advanced ABM system to defend against potential attacks.

Synset Examples

the Strategic Arms Limitation Talks placed limits on the deployment of ABMs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ICBM - intercontinental ballistic missile
  • Synonym: missile - a projectile weapon

Etymology

Acronym from 'anti-ballistic missile'.

Our marketing team measured conversion rates after launching a focused ABM campaign last quarter.
Agencies skilled in ABM often coordinate sales and marketing to close large corporate deals.
