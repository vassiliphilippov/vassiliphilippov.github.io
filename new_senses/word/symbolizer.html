symbolizer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmbəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbəˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in interpreting or representing symbols
person skilled in interpreting symbols

Examples

As a renowned symbolizer, she was often consulted on the interpretation of religious icons.
His reputation as a symbolizer grew after he deciphered the symbols on the mysterious artifact.
The symbolizer explained the hidden meanings within the ancient text.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interpreter - person who interprets meaning, especially of symbols or languages

Etymology

From 'symbolize' + '-er', meaning one who symbolizes or interprets symbols.
