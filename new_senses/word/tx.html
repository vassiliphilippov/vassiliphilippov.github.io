TX

1. PROPN

/ ti ɛks / (AmE), / tiː ɛks / (BrE)
Abbreviation for Texas, the second largest state in the U.S., located in the southwestern region, bordering the Gulf of Mexico.
abbreviation for the state of Texas
american

Examples

Her family moved to Austin, TX last summer.
The package was shipped from Houston, TX to New York.
The weather in TX can be extremely hot during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Texas - the second largest state in the United States

Etymology

Abbreviation formed from the initial letters of Texas.
