Sif

1. PROPN

/ sɪf / (AmE), / sɪf / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the wife of Thor, known as the guardian of the home and associated with fertility and family.
wife of Thor in Norse mythology

Examples

As the guardian of the home, Sif was often invoked for blessings of family and fertility.
In Norse legends, Sif is renowned for her golden hair and loyalty to Thor.
Many stories focus on how Loki cut off Sif's hair, leading to the creation of powerful artifacts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Thor - Norse god of thunder, husband of Sif

Etymology

From Old Norse Sif, meaning 'relation by marriage'.
