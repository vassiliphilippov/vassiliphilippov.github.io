Hela

1. PROPN

/ ˈheɪ.lə / (AmE), / ˈheɪ.lə / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the goddess of the dead and queen of the underworld.
goddess of the dead in Norse mythology

Examples

According to legend, Hela judges the souls of those who did not die bravely in battle.
In Norse mythology, Hela presides over the realm of the dead.
The comic book villain Hela is inspired by the mythological queen of the underworld.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hel - the realm of the dead in Norse mythology, ruled by Hela

Etymology

From Old Norse *Hel*, the name of the goddess and her realm.
