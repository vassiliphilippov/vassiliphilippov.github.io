brawler

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔlɚ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːlə / (BrE)
a person who engages in rough or noisy fights, especially brawls
person who participates in brawls

Examples

As a young man, he had a reputation for being a street brawler.
Professional wrestling often attracts fans of the classic brawler persona.
The notorious brawler started a fight at the bar last night.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fighter - someone who fights
  • Antonym: pacifist - someone who does not fight or avoids violence

Etymology

From 'brawl' (Middle English braul, quarrel) + -er suffix denoting agent.
