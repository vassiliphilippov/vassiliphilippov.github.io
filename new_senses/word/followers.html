followers

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑloʊɚz / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊəz / (BrE)
People who support, admire, or actively pay attention to a person, group, or cause, especially as a group.
supporters or enthusiasts as a group

Examples

Dictators often surround themselves with loyal followers who carry out their orders.
The celebrity thanked her followers for their constant support on social media.
The new movement quickly gained thousands of followers from around the world.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supporters - people who support someone or something
  • Antonym: leaders - people who lead a group or movement
