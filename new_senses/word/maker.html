maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkə / (BrE)
a company or business that manufactures a particular product
manufacturer; company that produces goods

Examples

Japanese electronics makers are known for their innovation.
She works for a furniture maker based in Italy.
The car maker announced a new electric vehicle this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manufacturer - a person or company that makes goods for sale

2. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that makes or produces something
person or entity that makes something

Examples

As a skilled maker, she crafts beautiful furniture by hand.
He is known as a problem maker, always coming up with inventive solutions.
The toy maker released a new line of educational products this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: producer - a person or company that creates or manufactures goods
  • Synonym: manufacturer - a person or company that makes goods in large quantities
  • Antonym: consumer - a person who buys and uses products

Etymology

From Old English *macere* (one who makes), from *macian* (to make).
