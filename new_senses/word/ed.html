ED

1. NOUN

/ iː diː / (AmE), / iː diː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for erectile dysfunction: the inability of a man to achieve or maintain an erection suitable for sexual intercourse.
erectile dysfunction (impotence)
medical

Examples

He was hesitant to discuss his ED with his doctor, but eventually sought help.
Many men experience ED at some point in their lives, especially as they age.
Treatments for ED have improved significantly in recent years, offering new hope to patients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erectile dysfunction - the inability to achieve or maintain an erection
  • Synonym: impotence - inability to achieve an erection or to father children

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'erectile dysfunction' (medical terminology).
