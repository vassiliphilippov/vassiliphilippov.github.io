learner

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɜːrnər / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnə / (BrE)
a person who is gaining knowledge or skill through study, experience, or instruction
a person who is learning

Examples

As a lifelong learner, he enjoys taking online courses and reading widely.
Every learner in the class was eager to participate in the science experiment.
She is a quick learner and picked up the new language in just a few months.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
  • Antonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school

Etymology

From Old English leornere, from leornian (to learn).
