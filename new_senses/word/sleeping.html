sleeping

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ / (BrE)
The state of being asleep, marked by a suspension of consciousness and a decrease in metabolic activity.
the state of being asleep

Examples

Sleeping is essential for good health and proper functioning of the body.
Babies spend most of their time sleeping in the first few months.
We heard a strange noise during the middle of our sleeping hours.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wakefulness - the state of being awake and conscious
  • Synonym: sleep - a natural periodic suspension of consciousness
