wacko

1. NOUN

/ ˈwækoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwakəʊ / (BrE)
An eccentric or mentally unstable person; someone whose behaviour is regarded as odd or crazy.
eccentric or crazy person
informal slang

Examples

Everyone at work thinks he's a bit of a wacko, but he's actually very kind.
She believes that only a wacko would choose to live completely off the grid.
The conspiracy theory was dismissed as the work of a lone wacko.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weirdo - a person who is very strange or eccentric
  • Synonym: lunatic - someone who is mentally ill or behaves extremely strangely
  • Antonym: normal - a person who is not eccentric or mentally unstable

Etymology

Probably from the slang 'wacky' meaning eccentric or crazy, plus an 'o' ending.

2. ADJ NEW

/ ˈwækoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwakəʊ / (BrE)
Informal. Crazy, absurd, or very strange; used to describe a person, idea, plan, or behaviour.
crazy; absurd (informal)
informal slang

Examples

That plan sounds wacko, like something only a movie villain would propose.
Her explanation was so wacko that nobody in the meeting took her seriously.
They laughed when he described his wacko scheme to earn money without leaving home.
