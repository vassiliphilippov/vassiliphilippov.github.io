DP

1. NOUN

/ di pi / (AmE), / diː piː / (BrE)
a person who has been forced to leave their home or country, especially because of war or persecution; displaced person
displaced person (abbreviation)

Examples

After the war, many DPs settled in new countries across Europe and America.
Her parents met in a DP camp in Germany after escaping their homeland.
The camp provided temporary housing for hundreds of DPs awaiting resettlement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refugee - a person who has been forced to leave their country to escape war, persecution, or disaster

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'displaced person', used especially following World War II.

2. NOUN NEW

/ diː piː / (AmE), / diː piː / (BrE)
Informal abbreviation for 'display picture': a digital photo or image a user sets as their profile picture on social media, messaging apps, or online platforms.
profile picture (display picture)
informal

Examples

She changed her DP on the app to a bright photo from last weekend.
After the breakup, he deleted all old DPs and chose a neutral landscape image.
Moderators asked users to upload a clear DP to verify their identity for the group.
