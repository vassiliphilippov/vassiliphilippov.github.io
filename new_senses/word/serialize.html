to serialize

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəlaɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or order in a series or sequence
arrange in a series or sequence

Examples

He was asked to serialize the files before archiving them.
Please serialize the results so we can review them step by step.
The program can serialize the data to ensure the correct order is maintained.

Synset Examples

Serialize the numbers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sequence - to arrange in a particular order or sequence

Etymology

Formed from 'serial' + '-ize', with 'serial' from Latin 'series' (row, chain, sequence).
