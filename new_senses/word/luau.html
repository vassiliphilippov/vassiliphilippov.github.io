luau

1. NOUN

/ ˈlu.aʊ / (AmE), / ˈluː.aʊ / (BrE)
A traditional Hawaiian feast or party, typically featuring local foods, music, and entertainment.
Hawaiian feast with food and entertainment

Examples

She wore a flower lei to the luau and sampled every Hawaiian dish.
The backyard luau included roasted pig and tropical drinks for the guests.
We attended a luau during our vacation in Hawaii and enjoyed traditional hula dancing.

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'lūʻau', originally referring to the taro leaves used in some traditional dishes.

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈlu.aʊ / (AmE), / ˈluː.aʊ / (BrE)
A scripting language developed by Roblox, used to write game logic and scripts for Roblox games; a dialect/variant of the Lua language.
Roblox scripting language

Examples

Developers write game logic in luau to run on Roblox.
She learned luau to add interactive features and improve player experience in her studio's games.
The tutorial explained how luau differs from Lua and why Roblox modified performance and type features.
