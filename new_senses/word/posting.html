posting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
a sign, notice, or advertisement displayed in a public place, especially to provide information or promote an event, product, or service
public notice or advertisement displayed

Examples

A new posting on the bulletin board announced the upcoming seminar.
She stopped to read a posting about job opportunities.
The concert was promoted through a large posting downtown.

Synset Examples

a poster advertised the coming attractions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poster - a large printed notice or picture used for advertising or decoration

Etymology

From the verb 'post', meaning 'to display (a notice)' in public, derived from Middle English 'posten'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
an entry or record made in a company's financial accounts
record in financial accounts

Examples

All incoming payments are recorded as a posting in the accounts ledger.
Late postings can lead to errors in the cash flow statement.
The auditor reviewed each posting for accuracy during the inspection.

Synset Examples

the posting was made in the cash account

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entry - a written or printed item in a list or record

3. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
the sending or transmission of a letter or other mail through the postal system
sending of a letter or mail

Examples

His job involves the posting of invoices to clients all over the country.
Please make sure posting is done before 5 p.m. to catch today's collection.
The posting of her letter was delayed due to the holiday.

Synset Examples

the postmark indicates the time of mailing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mailing - the act of sending something by mail
  • Antonym: receiving - the act of getting or accepting something sent

Etymology

From 'post', referring to mail delivery, and the suffix '-ing'.

4. NOUN NEW

/ ˈpoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
an item published on an online platform (such as a social network, blog, or forum), typically consisting of text, images, or links shared with other users.
online post on social media or forum

Examples

She made a new posting about the event on her social media page.
During the crisis, the moderator removed several postings that violated the community rules.
A thoughtful posting can spark hours of discussion among strangers online.
