sticker

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstɪkə / (BrE)
a small piece of paper, plastic, or similar material with an adhesive back, used for attaching to surfaces to provide information, decoration, or identification
an adhesive label

Examples

Children love collecting stickers to decorate their notebooks.
Each box was marked with a colored sticker for identification purposes.
She put a sticker on her laptop to show her favorite band.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: label - a piece of paper or other material that is attached to something and gives information about it

Etymology

From 'stick' (verb) + '-er' (agentive suffix), indicating something that sticks.
