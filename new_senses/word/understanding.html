/ ˌʌndɚˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
The ability to comprehend or grasp the meaning, significance, or nature of something.

Examples

Her understanding of the local customs helped her make friends quickly.
His understanding of the theory was impressive for someone so young.
We need to improve our understanding of climate change to respond effectively.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehension - the ability to understand something
  • Antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or understanding
