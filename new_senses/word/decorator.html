decorator

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛkəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to design and decorate the interiors of buildings
person specializing in interior decoration

Examples

After seeing her portfolio, I knew she was the right decorator for our project.
The decorator advised using lighter colors to make the room appear larger.
We hired a decorator to help renovate our old farmhouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior designer - a person professionally engaged in the design and decoration of interior spaces

Etymology

From Latin 'decorare' (to adorn) via French 'décorateur'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈdɛkəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛkəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to decorate rooms, homes, or buildings, especially by painting or adding decorative elements
person who decorates interiors or buildings

Examples

As a skilled decorator, she transformed ordinary spaces into beautiful environments.
The decorator suggested using lighter colors to make the room appear larger.
They hired a professional decorator to redesign the living room before the holidays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: designer - person who creates detailed plans for spaces or items
  • Antonym: builder - person who constructs buildings

Etymology

From Latin decorare (to adorn) via French décorateur.
