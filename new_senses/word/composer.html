composer

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpoʊzər / (AmE), / kəmˈpəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who writes music, especially professionally
person who writes music

Examples

Ludwig van Beethoven is a famous composer known for his symphonies and piano works.
Many aspiring musicians dream of becoming a professional composer one day.
The composer spent months working on the score for the new film.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: songwriter - person who writes songs
  • Antonym: performer - person who performs music or acts

Etymology

From French 'compositeur' (one who composes), from Latin 'componere' (to put together).
