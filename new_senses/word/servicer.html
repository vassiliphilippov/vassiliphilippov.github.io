servicer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːvɪsər / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪsə / (BrE)
a skilled worker whose job is to repair or maintain equipment or machinery
person who repairs or maintains things

Examples

A servicer regularly visits the building to maintain the elevator.
As an experienced servicer, she quickly identified the problem with the air conditioner.
The company sent a servicer to fix the broken washing machine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repairman - a person whose occupation is making repairs
  • Synonym: technician - a person skilled in the technique of an art or craft

Etymology

From 'service' + '-er', denoting a person who services.

company that manages loans

Examples

The mortgage servicer sent a statement showing the remaining balance and escrow changes.
