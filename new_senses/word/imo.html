IMO

1. PROPN

/ aɪ ɛm oʊ / (AmE), / aɪ ɛm əʊ / (BrE)
abbreviation for International Maritime Organization, the United Nations agency responsible for regulating shipping and maritime affairs.
UN agency for maritime regulation

Examples

Delegates from 150 countries attended the IMO conference in London.
New regulations adopted by the IMO aim to reduce pollution from maritime transport.
The IMO sets international safety standards for ships.

Etymology

Acronym of International Maritime Organization.

2. ADV NEW

/ aɪ ɛm oʊ / (AmE), / aɪ ɛm əʊ / (BrE)
informal online abbreviation for 'in my opinion', used as a discourse marker to indicate that the following statement expresses the speaker's personal view.
in my opinion (online abbreviation)
informal

Examples

On the group chat, IMO, the simplest solution is to postpone the meeting until Friday.
She prefaced her suggestion with IMO to make clear it was only her personal opinion.
In the thread, several people wrote IMO before offering quick fixes for the bug.
