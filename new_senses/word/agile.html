3. ADJ NEW

/ ˈædʒaɪl, ˈædʒəl / (AmE), / ˈædʒaɪl, ˈædʒəl / (BrE)
Relating to the Agile software development approach or similar ways of working that use iterative cycles (sprints), close collaboration, and quick adaptation to change.
relating to Agile software development

Examples

The team adopted an agile approach to software development with sprints and daily stand-ups.
Our company switched to agile practices so we could respond faster to customer feedback.
During the sprint review, the product owner praised the team's agile way of delivering features.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Agile methodology - a set of software development principles emphasizing iterative delivery, team collaboration, and responsiveness to change
  • Synonym: iterative development - a process of developing software by repeating short cycles of design, implementation, and review

Etymology

From Latin 'agilis' (nimble), adopted in the 21st century as the name of the software development approach.
