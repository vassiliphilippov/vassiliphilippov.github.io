steaminess

1. NOUN

/ ˈstiːminəs / (AmE), / ˈstiːminɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being filled with steam, mist, or vapor, causing cloudiness or haze
state of being filled with steam or haze

Examples

On summer mornings, the steaminess of the city made everything look slightly blurred.
The steaminess in the bathroom after a hot shower made the mirror fog up instantly.
The steaminess of the greenhouse created an ideal environment for the tropical plants.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mistiness - state of being misty or filled with mist
  • Antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to see through
