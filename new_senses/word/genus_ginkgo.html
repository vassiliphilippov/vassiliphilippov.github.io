genus Ginkgo

1. NOUN NEW

/ ˈɡɪŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
A dietary or herbal supplement made from the leaves of the Ginkgo biloba tree, often taken or sold for memory and cognitive support.
herbal memory supplement

Examples

She started taking Ginkgo every morning because her doctor recommended it for memory support.
The study found that regular Ginkgo use had mixed results for improving attention in older adults.
He bought a bottle of organic Ginkgo tablets at the health-food store last weekend.

Etymology

From Japanese ginkyō (silver apricot), via Latin; ultimately from Chinese yínxìng.
