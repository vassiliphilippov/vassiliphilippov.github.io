shipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person or company that sends goods by ship, train, plane, or truck
person or company sending goods

Examples

Each shipper is responsible for ensuring the goods are properly packaged before transportation.
The shipper prepared all the necessary documents for the overseas delivery.
The company has worked with the same shipper for more than a decade.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: consignee - the person or company to whom goods are shipped
  • Synonym: carrier - a company or vehicle that transports goods

Etymology

From 'ship' (to transport), plus agent suffix '-er'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpə / (BrE)
a company or person that sends or transports goods, especially by sea, land, or air, usually as a business
company or person that ships goods

Examples

Many retailers work closely with their shipper to ensure timely deliveries.
The shipper arranged for the goods to be transported by rail to the distribution center.
The shipper is responsible for preparing all export documentation for the cargo.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: consignee - the person or company to whom goods are shipped
  • Synonym: carrier - a company or person responsible for transporting goods

Etymology

From 'ship' (to transport by ship) + '-er' (agent suffix).

3. NOUN NEW

/ \u02c8\u0283\u026ap\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0283\u026ap\u0259 / (BrE)
informal (in fandom) a fan who supports, promotes, or roots for a romantic relationship between two (often fictional) characters.
fan supporting a fictional couple
informal slang

Examples

She proudly announced on Twitter that she was a devoted shipper of the new show's main couple.
Even though he criticized the plot, he remained a secret shipper of the childhood friends' romance.
The fan forum filled with art and theories created by enthusiastic shippers supporting different pairings.
