manipulator

1. NOUN

/ məˈnɪpjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / məˈnɪpjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who operates or controls equipment or machinery, especially with their hands
person controlling machinery or equipment

Examples

Each manipulator on the team was responsible for a different part of the manufacturing process.
The factory hired a skilled manipulator to control the assembly line robots.
Using precise movements, the manipulator adjusted the delicate instruments during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: operator - person who operates equipment or machinery

Etymology

From Latin 'manipulus' (a handful) and later 'manipulate', meaning to handle.

2. NOUN

a person or device that operates or controls a machine or apparatus
person or device operating machinery

Examples

Only a skilled manipulator can operate the controls of this complex machinery.
The manipulator deftly adjusted the robotic arm to assemble the delicate components.
The system requires the manipulator to monitor multiple dials during operation.
