vacay

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪˌkeɪ / (AmE), / ˈveɪkeɪ / (BrE)
(informal) A vacation; a period of time spent away from home or work for rest, travel, or recreation.
informal term for vacation
american informal

Examples

After months of hard work, a quick vacay was just what I needed.
She posted photos from her beach vacay on Instagram.
We're heading to Hawaii for a week-long vacay next month.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vacation - an extended period of leisure and recreation, especially away from home

Etymology

Shortened form of 'vacation', dating from late 20th century American English.
