lifehack

1. VERB

/ ˈlaɪfˌhæk / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌhæk / (BrE)
to find or implement clever methods or shortcuts to make daily tasks and activities more efficient or easier
improve efficiency in daily life via clever techniques
informal slang

Examples

He loves to lifehack his workflow with new apps and automation tools.
She managed to lifehack her morning routine by preparing breakfast the night before.
They lifehacked their travel packing process to save both space and time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: optimize - make as effective or functional as possible
  • Synonym: streamline - make more efficient by simplifying
  • Antonym: complicate - make more complicated or less efficient

Etymology

Blend of 'life' and 'hack' (in the sense of a clever solution, especially in computing slang).
