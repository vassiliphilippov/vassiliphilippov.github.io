flaming

/ ˈfleɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Used as an intensifier to express annoyance or emphasis, especially before adjectives and nouns.

/ ˈfleɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
