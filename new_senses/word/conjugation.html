conjugation

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːndʒəˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒndʒʊˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
the variation of the form of a verb to indicate tense, aspect, mood, person, or number
verb inflection

Examples

In French, the present tense conjugation varies depending on the subject pronoun.
Learning the conjugation of irregular verbs can be challenging for students.
Spanish verbs undergo conjugation for person, number, and tense.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: declension - the inflection of nouns, pronouns, or adjectives for case, number, and gender
