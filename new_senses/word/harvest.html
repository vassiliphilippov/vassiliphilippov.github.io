harvest

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrvɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪst / (BrE)
the process or period of gathering in crops that have ripened
gathering of a ripe crop

Examples

Farmers are preparing for the annual harvest of apples next week.
The wheat harvest began in late July after a long, hot season.
This year’s grape harvest was larger than expected due to favorable weather.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reaping - the cutting or gathering of a crop as a part of harvest

Etymology

From Old English 'hærfest' (autumn, harvest time), of Germanic origin.
