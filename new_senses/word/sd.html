/ cc5bs c8di / (AmE), / cc5bs c8did0 / (BrE)
a small removable flash memory card conforming to the Secure Digital (SD) standard, used to store digital data in cameras, phones, and other portable devices.
Secure Digital memory card

Examples

I bought a new SD card for my camera before our vacation next week.
She transferred the photos from her SD card to her laptop using a small card reader.
If the camera cannot read the SD, try reformatting it after backing up your images.
