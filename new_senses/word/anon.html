3. NOUN NEW

/ əˈnɑn / (AmE), / əˈnɒn / (BrE)
an anonymous person who posts or comments online without revealing their real identity; an anonymous user or poster.
anonymous online poster
informal slang

Examples

An anon posted a cryptic reply to the discussion and disappeared without explanation.
On imageboards, anons often adopt the same inside jokes to stay anonymous.
When the article leaked, an angry anon threatened legal action in the comments.
