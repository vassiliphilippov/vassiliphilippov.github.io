stand-up

1. ADJ

/ ˈstændˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈstandˌʌp / (BrE)
(of a bar, desk, or similar object) designed or intended to be used while standing rather than sitting.
designed for use while standing

Examples

The new office features a stand-up desk to encourage employees to move more during the day.
Using a stand-up counter makes it easier for people to mingle at parties.
We had drinks at a trendy stand-up bar in the city center.

2. NOUN NEW

/ ˈstændˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈstandˌʌp / (BrE)
a short meeting in which team members stand and give brief status updates, often held daily in agile or software teams.
brief daily team meeting

Examples

The team holds a quick stand-up every morning to review today's tasks.
During the remote stand-up, each developer gave a brief update on their progress.
They try to keep the stand-up under fifteen minutes so people stay focused.
