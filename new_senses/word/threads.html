2. NOUN NEW

/ θrɛdz / (AmE), / θrɛdz / (BrE)
A series of related messages or posts on an online platform (forum, social media, email) forming a discussion about a particular topic.
series of related online posts

Examples

I followed several threads on the forum to understand users' views about the update.
She bookmarked the favorite threads so she could return to important advice later.
The threads about the new policy drew hundreds of comments yesterday.

Etymology

From Old English 'þræð' (a thread); extended metaphorically in computing and online use to mean a connected line of messages.
