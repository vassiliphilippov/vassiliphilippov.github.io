typing

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of writing using a typewriter or computer keyboard
writing using a typewriter or keyboard

Examples

He took a typing course to improve his speed and accuracy.
My job involved a lot of typing and filing paperwork.
She spent the morning typing letters on her old typewriter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: handwriting - the act or style of writing done by hand

Etymology

From the verb 'type' + -ing, originally referring to typewriters.
