NCDC

1. NOUN

/ ɛn si di si / (AmE), / ɛn siː diː siː / (BrE)
the part of the U.S. National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) that maintains the world's largest active archive of weather data
U.S. weather data archive agency

Examples

Many meteorologists access the NCDC archive for accurate temperature data.
Researchers often rely on data from the NCDC for climate studies.
The NCDC plays a crucial role in preserving historical weather records.

Etymology

Acronym of National Climatic Data Center.

2. NOUN NEW

/ ən si di si / (AmE), / ən siː diː siː / (BrE)
the Nigeria Centre for Disease Control, the Nigerian government agency responsible for disease surveillance, prevention, and outbreak response.
Nigerian public-health agency

Examples

The NCDC issued an urgent travel advisory after several new cases were confirmed in the state.
Local hospitals coordinated with the NCDC to distribute vaccines and share testing data this morning.
During the outbreak, the minister praised the NCDC for its timely epidemiological reports and guidance.
