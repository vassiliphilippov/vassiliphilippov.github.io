nm

1. NOUN

/ ɛn ɛm / (AmE), / ɛn ɛm / (BrE)
abbreviation for nanometre, a metric unit of length equal to one billionth of a meter (10⁻⁹ m)
abbreviation for nanometre, metric length unit

Examples

DNA strands are about 2 nm in diameter, making them invisible to optical microscopes.
Modern semiconductor devices often use transistors sized at just a few nm.
The wavelength of visible light typically ranges from 400 to 700 nm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nanometre - a metric unit of length equal to one billionth of a meter

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'nanometre', from Greek 'nanos' (dwarf) + 'metron' (measure).
