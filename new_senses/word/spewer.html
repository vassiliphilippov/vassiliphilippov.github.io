spewer

1. NOUN

/ ˈspjuːər / (AmE), / ˈspjuːə / (BrE)
a person who vomits; someone who ejects the contents of their stomach through the mouth
a person who vomits

Examples

After the roller coaster ride, Tom became a notorious spewer among his friends.
He was embarrassed to be called the party's main spewer after feeling ill.
The nurse had to attend to another spewer during the outbreak of food poisoning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomiter - a person who vomits

Etymology

From Middle English 'spewen', from Old English 'spīwan', meaning 'to spit, vomit'.

2. NOUN NEW

/ \u02c8spju\u02d0\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8spju\u02d0\u0259 / (BrE)
Informal. a person who repeatedly emits angry, abusive, or provocative words or comments, especially online.
someone who emits abusive remarks
informal offensive

Examples

In the comments thread, a persistent spewer posted angry messages every hour.
She ignored the obvious spewer who tried to provoke readers with personal attacks.
The moderator banned the worst spewer after dozens of hateful comments appeared.
