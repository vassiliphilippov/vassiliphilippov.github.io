transmogrification

1. NOUN

/ trænzmɑˌɡrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / trænzmɒˌɡrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of changing into a different, often bizarre or grotesque, form or appearance
change into a bizarre or grotesque form

Examples

Science fiction often explores the transmogrification of humans into alien forms.
The artist's new exhibit depicts the transmogrification of everyday objects into something fantastical.
The wizard's spell caused an instant transmogrification of the frog into a prince.

Synset Examples

the transmogrification of the prince into a porcupine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transformation - a thorough or dramatic change in form or appearance

Etymology

From 'transmogrify', likely a humorous alteration of 'transfigure' or 'transmute'.

2. NOUN NEW

/ trænzm / (AmE), / trænsmɒˈɡrəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
(in video games) the action of changing the appearance of a weapon, armor, or other item without altering its gameplay statistics or functionality.
cosmetic change of game equipment

Examples

Many MMORPGs let players use transmogrification to make their armor look unique without changing stats.
She applied a rare skin in the game as a transmogrification to match her character's style.
The patch added a menu option for transmogrification, letting users swap appearances while keeping equipment power.
