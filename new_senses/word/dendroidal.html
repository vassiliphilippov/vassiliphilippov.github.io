dendroidal

1. ADJ

/ dɛnˈdrɔɪdəl / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrɔɪdəl / (BrE)
Resembling a tree in form or branching structure.
tree-like in form or branching

Examples

Certain types of coral exhibit a dendroidal growth form, branching intricately like trees.
The artist was inspired by the dendroidal shapes found in river systems and roots.
The chemical structure displayed a distinct dendroidal pattern under the microscope.

Synset Examples

arborescent coral found off the coast of Bermuda
dendriform sponges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arborescent - growing in the shape or form of a tree
  • Synonym: dendritic - having a branched, treelike structure

Etymology

From Greek dendroeidēs (tree-like), from dendron (tree) + -oid (form/likeness).
