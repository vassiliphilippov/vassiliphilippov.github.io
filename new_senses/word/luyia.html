Luyia

1. NOUN

/ luˈjiːə / (AmE), / luˈjiːə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in western Kenya
Bantu language of western Kenya

Examples

He learned to greet locals in Luyia during his visit to Kenya.
Many people in western Kenya speak Luyia as their first language.
Researchers are working to document the grammar of Luyia.

2. NOUN NEW

/ luc8jid059 / (AmE), / luc8jid059 / (BrE)
a group or cluster of closely related Bantu languages (a dialect continuum) spoken in western Kenya, sometimes treated as a single language for convenience.
Bantu dialect cluster (western Kenya)

Examples

Linguists consider Luyia a cluster of closely related languages rather than a single tongue.
Several dialects within Luyia are mutually intelligible but recognized as distinct languages.
The cultural festival featured songs performed in different varieties of Luyia spoken across the region.
