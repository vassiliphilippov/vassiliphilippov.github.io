kook

1. NOUN

/ kuk / (AmE), / kuːk / (BrE)
a person who is considered extremely eccentric, odd, or crazy
eccentric or crazy person
informal

Examples

Don't mind her wild ideas—she's a bit of a kook, but she's harmless.
Everyone thought he was a kook because of his strange inventions.
The small town had its fair share of kooks who believed in aliens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - a person with unconventional or odd behavior
  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill, extremely foolish, or irrational

Etymology

Possibly from the word 'cuckoo', meaning crazy or mad.

2. NOUN NEW

/ kuk / (AmE), / kuːk / (BrE)
(informal, surf slang) an inexperienced or unskilled surfer who ignores local rules or etiquette and is considered annoying or dangerous by other surfers.
inexperienced, inconsiderate surfer
informal slang

Examples

The new surfer cut me off again, a true kook on the waves.
Local surfers shouted at the kook who kept paddling into the lineup without looking.
We warned the visiting kooks that dropping in on others would get them shouted at.
