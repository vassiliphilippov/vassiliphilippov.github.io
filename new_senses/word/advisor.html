advisor

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvaɪzər / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzə / (BrE)
a person with specialized knowledge who gives advice in a particular field
person who gives expert advice

Examples

Before making any investments, it's wise to consult a financial advisor.
She served as an advisor to the environmental committee for over a decade.
The president's top advisor resigned amid growing political pressure.

Synset Examples

an adviser helped students select their courses
the United States sent military advisors to Guatemala

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adviser - a person who gives advice; an alternative spelling

Etymology

From Latin 'advisare' via Old French 'aviser', meaning 'to consider, to counsel'. Variant spelling of 'adviser'.
