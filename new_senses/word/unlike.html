unlike

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪk / (BrE)
different from each other in nature, quality, or appearance; not alike.
dissimilar; not alike

Examples

For siblings, they are surprisingly unlike in personality and interests.
Her reaction was so unlike her usual calm demeanor that everyone was shocked.
The two paintings are completely unlike in style and technique.

Synset Examples

for twins they are very unlike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: different - not the same as another or each other
  • Antonym: alike - very similar or the same

2. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪk / (BrE)
Not equal in amount or quantity; differing from each other in degree or size.
not equal in quantity or degree

Examples

Funds were allocated in unlike amounts to each department depending on their needs.
She noticed that the prizes given to the winners were unlike in value.
The two students received unlike grades on the test, reflecting their different performances.

Synset Examples

they distributed unlike (or unequal) sums to the various charities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unequal - not equal in amount, size, or value

3. VERB NEW

/ ʌnˈlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪk / (BrE)
To remove a previously given 'like' or other approval reaction from online content such as a post, comment, photo, or video.
remove a social-media like
informal

Examples

She decided to unlike the post after reading the misleading information.
I accidentally unliked his photo and then immediately liked it again.
Many users unlike old posts when they change their political views.

Synset Examples

She decided to unlike the post after reading the misleading information.
I accidentally unliked his photo and then immediately liked it again.
Many users unlike old posts when they change their political views.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remove - to take something away or off
  • Antonym: like - to express approval of online content (e.g., by clicking a 'like' button)

Etymology

From the prefix un- + the verb like; developed in the 21st century for social‑media use.
