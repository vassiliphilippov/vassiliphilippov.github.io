sevensome

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛvənˌsʌm / (AmE), / ˈsɛv(ə)nˌsʌm / (BrE)
A group of seven people considered as a single unit.
group of seven people

Examples

A sevensome is perfect for this board game, as it seats exactly seven players.
After meeting at the park, the sevensome walked to the movie theater as a group.
The sevensome enjoyed a lively dinner together at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: foursome - group of four people considered as a unit
  • Degree Higher: ninesome - group of nine people considered as a unit

Etymology

Formed by combining 'seven' and the suffix '-some', similar to 'foursome' and 'threesome'.
