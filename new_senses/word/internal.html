internal

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtɝːnəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːnəl / (BrE)
existing or taking place within a country; domestic rather than foreign.
within a country; domestic

Examples

Many of the company's problems stem from internal conflicts rather than outside competition.
The government increased spending to address internal security issues.
This new law is designed to regulate internal affairs, not international relations.

Synset Examples

the British Home Office has broader responsibilities than the United States Department of the Interior
the nation's internal politics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic - relating to or occurring within a particular country; not foreign or international

Etymology

From Latin internus meaning 'inward, internal'.

The developer documented why the internal API should not be used by third-party extensions.
