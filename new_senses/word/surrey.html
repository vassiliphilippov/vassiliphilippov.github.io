surrey

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜːri / (AmE), / ˈsʌri / (BrE)
a light four-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, often with two or four seats and a fringed top
light horse-drawn carriage

Examples

A horse-drawn surrey is on display at the local transportation museum.
The family toured the countryside in a bright yellow surrey with a fringed top.
They rented a vintage surrey for their wedding photoshoot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carriage - a wheeled vehicle for people, usually horse-drawn

Etymology

Originally named after Surrey, a county in England, where such carriages were first made.
