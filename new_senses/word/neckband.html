neckband

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛkˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈnɛkˌbænd / (BrE)
a band of material encircling the neck of a garment, typically forming or supporting the collar
band around neck of garment

Examples

Over time, the neckband of his t-shirt became loose and misshapen.
She sewed a contrasting neckband to give the sweater a stylish touch.
The shirt's neckband was made of soft, stretchy cotton for added comfort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collar - the part of a garment that encircles the neck

3. NOUN

/ ˈnɛkˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈnɛkˌbænd / (BrE)
A necklace or ornamental band that fits closely around the neck, especially as an article of jewelry.
tight-fitting necklace or ornamental band

Examples

Pearls strung on a neckband can add elegance to any dress.
She wore a delicate velvet neckband to complete her evening outfit.
The bride chose a diamond-studded neckband for her wedding day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: choker - a close-fitting necklace worn around the neck

Etymology

From 'neck' + 'band', meaning a band or strip worn around the neck.
