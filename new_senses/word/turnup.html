turnup

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrn.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈtɜːn.ʌp / (BrE)
a turned-up hem at the end of a sleeve or trouser leg, typically forming a visible cuff
a turned-back hem at a sleeve or trouser leg

Examples

His suit pants had a small turnup, giving them a slightly retro style.
She preferred shirts without any turnup on the sleeves for a cleaner look.
The tailor measured the length to add a turnup to his new trousers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cuff - a band of fabric at the end of a sleeve or trouser leg
