disgusting

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋ / (BrE)
causing strong dislike or revulsion; extremely unpleasant or offensive
causing strong revulsion or offense

Examples

She found the way he spoke to be absolutely disgusting.
The kitchen was in a disgusting state after the party.
There was a disgusting odor coming from the garbage bin.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revolting - causing intense disgust; extremely unpleasant
  • Synonym: repellent - causing disgust or aversion
  • Antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment

Etymology

From English 'disgust' + '-ing', with 'disgust' from French 'dégouster' (to have a distaste), from Latin 'gustare' (to taste).
