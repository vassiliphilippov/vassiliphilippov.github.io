undefined

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.dɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
not clearly or precisely limited, determined, or described; lacking definite or clear outlines or limits
not clearly defined or determined

Examples

She felt a sense of undefined anxiety before the exam began.
The future of the company remains undefined as the negotiations continue.
The term was left undefined in the contract, leading to confusion later.

Synset Examples

an undefined term
undefined authority
some undefined sense of excitement
vague feelings of sadness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vague - not clearly expressed, known, described, or decided
  • Antonym: definite - clearly defined or determined; not vague or general

Etymology

From un- (not) + defined, from Latin 'definire' (to limit, determine).
