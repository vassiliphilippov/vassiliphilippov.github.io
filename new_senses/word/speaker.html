Speaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːkɚ / (AmE), / ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
the person who presides over debates and proceedings in a legislative assembly, especially the House of Representatives or House of Commons
presiding officer of a legislative assembly

Examples

Nancy Pelosi served as Speaker of the House of Representatives in the United States.
The Speaker announced the results of the vote to the assembly.
The Speaker called for order after the heated debate began to escalate.

Synset Examples

the leader of the majority party is the Speaker of the House of Representatives

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chairperson - the person in charge of a meeting or organization
  • Degree Lower: member of parliament - an elected member of a country's legislature
