George Town

1. PROPN

/ ˈdʒɔrdʒ taʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ taʊn / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of the Cayman Islands, located on Grand Cayman Island.
capital of the Cayman Islands

Examples

Many tourists visit George Town to enjoy its beautiful beaches and duty-free shopping.
The government offices and financial institutions are located in George Town.
The population of George Town increases significantly during the cruise season.
