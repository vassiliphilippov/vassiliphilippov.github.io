kitchen

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn / (BrE)
a room in a house or other building where food is prepared and cooked
room for preparing food

Examples

She spent the afternoon baking cookies in the kitchen.
The kitchen was filled with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee.
They remodeled their kitchen to include more counter space and new appliances.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pantry - a small room or closet where food, dishes, and utensils are stored

Etymology

From Middle English *kichen*, from Old English *cycene*, from Latin *coquina* (kitchen).
