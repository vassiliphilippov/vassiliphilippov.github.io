introspection

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪntroʊˈspɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəʊˈspɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of examining your own thoughts, feelings, and motives
examining one’s own thoughts or feelings

Examples

Dedicating a few minutes each day to introspection can help improve your emotional awareness.
Many philosophers believe that introspection is essential for personal growth.
Through introspection, she gained insight into her true motivations and fears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-reflection - the process of thinking deeply about oneself
  • Synonym: contemplation - deep reflective thought, especially about one's own life or situation

Etymology

From Latin 'introspicere' (to look inside).
