photomechanics

1. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊtəʊməˈkænɪks / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊmɪˈkænɪks / (BrE)
the process of producing printing plates or cylinders using photographic techniques
printing plate production by photographic methods

Examples

Advances in photomechanics changed the way newspapers and magazines included images.
Studying photomechanics helped her understand the history of commercial printing.
The introduction of photomechanics reduced costs and improved the quality of illustrated books.

Synset Examples

photomechanics revolutionized the practice of printing

Etymology

From photo- (relating to light) and mechanics, reflecting the mechanical application of photographic processes.

2. NOUN

/ ˌfoʊtəʊməˈkænɪks / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊmɪˈkænɪks / (BrE)
a field of printing technology concerned with using photographic processes to create printing plates for reproducing images
use of photography for making printing plates

Examples

Advancements in digital printing have largely replaced traditional photomechanics methods in commercial presses.
Many scientific journals relied on photomechanics to accurately print detailed illustrations and photographs.
The early development of photomechanics revolutionized the publishing industry by enabling mass reproduction of images.

Etymology

From photo- (meaning 'light, photography') and mechanics (from Greek mēkhanikē, 'machine, device').

3. NOUN NEW

/ \u02ccfo\u028at\u0259\u028am\u0259\u02c8k\u00e6n\u026aks / (AmE), / \u02ccf\u0259\u028at\u0259\u028am\u026a\u02c8k\u00e6n\u026aks / (BrE)
The study or application of mechanical effects produced by light, especially light-induced motion, deformation, or actuation in photoresponsive materials.
light-induced mechanical motion

Examples

Researchers are using photomechanics to design light-driven soft actuators for robotics.
A course on photomechanics explained how light can bend tiny polymer beams in devices.
Recent papers in materials science demonstrated photomechanics that convert light into controlled mechanical motion.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photomechanical actuation - the process of producing controlled motion or force in a device using light-responsive materials
  • Synonym: photoactuation - activation of movement or mechanical response in materials by light
