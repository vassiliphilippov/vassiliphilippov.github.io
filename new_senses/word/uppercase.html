uppercase

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌpərˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈʌpəkeɪs / (BrE)
(of a letter) written or printed as a capital, as opposed to in small or lowercase letters.
written in capital letters

Examples

All the headings in the document are in uppercase.
If you write your name in uppercase, it will be easier to read.
Please enter your password using only uppercase letters.

Synset Examples

uppercase letters: X and Y and Z etc.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lowercase - (of a letter) written or printed in small letters, not capitals

Etymology

From printing, referring to the upper case in which capital letters were stored.

Source: WordNet

Synset POS: a

2. NOUN

/ ˈʌpərˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈʌpəkeɪs / (BrE)
a capital letter; a large alphabetic character used at the beginning of proper names and sometimes for emphasis
a capital (large) letter

Examples

Instructions often specify entering passwords in uppercase or lowercase.
Please write your name in uppercase letters on the form.
The company changed its logo to use only uppercase characters for better visibility.

Synset Examples

printers once kept the type for capitals and for small letters in separate cases; capitals were kept in the upper half of the type case and so became known as upper-case letters

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lowercase - a small (non-capital) letter
  • Synonym: capital letter - a large alphabetic character used at the beginning of sentences and proper names

Etymology

From the practice in early printing of keeping capital letters in the upper part of a type case.

3. VERB NEW

/ \u02c8\u028cp\u0259r\u02ccke\u026as / (AmE), / \u02c8\u028cp\u0259ke\u026as / (BrE)
to change letters in text so that they are all capital (large) letters; especially used in computing and typography to mean convert text to capitals.
convert text to capital letters

Examples

You can uppercase the selected text in the editor to standardize capitalization across the document.
Before exporting, she uppercased all headings so the PDF matched the brand guidelines.
The script automatically uppercases email fields to prevent case-sensitive matching errors.

Synset Examples

You can uppercase the selected text in the editor to standardize capitalization across the document.
Before exporting, she uppercased all headings so the PDF matched the brand guidelines.
The script automatically uppercases email fields to prevent case-sensitive matching errors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: capitalize - to make a letter or word begin with a capital letter; sometimes used interchangeably with making text uppercase.
  • Antonym: lowercase - to change letters so they are small (non-capital) letters; opposite of making text uppercase.

Etymology

From the noun/adjective 'uppercase' (from printing, where capital letters were kept in the upper part of a type case); extended to a verb in computing.
