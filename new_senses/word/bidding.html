bidding

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
an order, request, or command to do something or to be present, often used formally or in literature
command or request to act or attend

Examples

He changed jobs at the bidding of his mentor.
She attended the meeting at the chairman's bidding.
The king summoned his advisors at his bidding.

Synset Examples

they came at his bidding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: command - an authoritative order
  • Synonym: request - an act of asking for something

Etymology

From Old English *biddan* (to ask, pray).

2. NOUN

/ ˈbɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
In bridge, the process by which players state the number of tricks they are prepared to contract to win, determining the contract for the hand.
the process of stating a contract in bridge

Examples

Good bidding is essential to succeed in a competitive game of bridge.
He studied advanced bidding techniques to improve his bridge skills.
Her partner was impressed by her confident bidding during the bridge tournament.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bid - to offer a certain contract in bridge

3. NOUN

/ ˈbɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
an authoritative command or instruction, often given by a person with power or influence
authoritative command or instruction

Examples

He left the party early, doing his mother's bidding without complaint.
She only attended the meeting at her manager's bidding.
The knight acted at the king's bidding and rode into battle without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: command - an authoritative order
  • Synonym: order - a directive or instruction to do something
  • Degree Lower: request - an act of asking politely or formally for something (less forceful)

Etymology

From the verb 'bid,' Old English 'biddan' (to ask, order, command).

/ ˈbɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of making offers (bids) to buy an item or contract, especially at an auction or in online real-time bidding systems.
placing bids at an auction

Examples

At the charity auction, she won the painting after furious bidding from several collectors.
Online bidding by advertisers pushed the price for that keyword to an all-time high.
After hours of patient bidding, the antique vase finally sold well above estimate.
