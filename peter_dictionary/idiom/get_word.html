get word

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wɜrd / (AmE), / ɡɛt wɜːd / (BrE)
to receive information or news, especially indirectly or unexpectedly

Examples

He did n't tell Miss Jen, but she must have got word from the cook or nurse, who of course knew those Quinzaine nigs, and she really took a fit.

Synset Examples

I learned that she has two grown-up children
I see that you have been promoted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hear - become aware of by hearing (news, information)
  • Synonym: learn - find out or become informed about something
