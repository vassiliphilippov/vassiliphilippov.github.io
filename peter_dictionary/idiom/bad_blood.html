bad blood

1. NOUN

/ bæd blʌd / (AmE), / bæd blʌd / (BrE)
Feelings of animosity or long-standing hostility between people or groups.

Examples

There's been a lot of bad blood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animosity - strong hostility or dislike
  • Synonym: enmity - the state or feeling of being actively opposed or hostile to someone or something
  • Antonym: goodwill - friendly or kindly feelings
