raise the roof

1. VERB

/ reɪz ði ˈruːf / (AmE), / reɪz ði ˈruːf / (BrE)
To become very angry and show it with loud or vigorous protest.

Examples

Let her call Crosson if she wanted to, let Crosson raise the roof or even can him, he did n't care.

Synset Examples

He will raise the roof when he hears this

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lose one's temper - to suddenly become angry
  • Antonym: calm down - to become less angry or agitated
