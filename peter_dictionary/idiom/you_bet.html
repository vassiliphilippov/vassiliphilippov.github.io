you bet

1. ADV

/ juː bɛt / (AmE), / juː bɛt / (BrE)
Used to express emphatic agreement or confirmation.

Examples

Of course you can.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: of course - used to show agreement or confirmation
  • Synonym: certainly - used to indicate strong agreement or affirmation
  • Antonym: no way - used to strongly disagree or refuse
