you said it

1. ADV

/ ju səɪd ɪt / (AmE), / juː sɛd ɪt / (BrE)
Used to express strong or emphatic agreement with what someone has just said.

Examples

So be it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - completely agree; used to strongly affirm what someone says
  • Synonym: exactly - used to agree emphatically with what has been said
