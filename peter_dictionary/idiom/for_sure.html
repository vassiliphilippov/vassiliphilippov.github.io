for sure

1. ADV

/ fɔɹ ʃʊɹ / (AmE), / fə ʃʊə / (BrE)
Without any doubt; certainly.

Examples

An open field was better than a building, that was for sure, so he dismounted, turned off the horse, and plunged through the grass.

Synset Examples

she wanted to go; that was for sure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; for sure
  • Antonym: maybe - possibly; expressing uncertainty
