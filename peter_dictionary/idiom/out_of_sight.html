out of sight

1. ADV

/ aʊt əv saɪt / (AmE), / aʊt əv saɪt / (BrE)
Hidden from view; in a place where one cannot be seen.

Examples

His son watched until he got as far as the hall, almost out of sight, then hurried after.

Synset Examples

he lay doggo

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in view - visible; within sight
