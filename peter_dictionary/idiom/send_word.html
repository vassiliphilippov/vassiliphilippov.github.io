send word

1. VERB

/ sɛnd wɝd / (AmE), / sɛnd wɜːd / (BrE)
to send a message or information to someone, especially by a third party or indirect means

Examples

No casts are listed, but Lotte Lehmann sent word that the Negro soprano, Grace Bumbry, will sing Venus in "Tannhaeuser ".

Synset Examples

I advised him that the rent was due

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notify - formally inform someone of something
  • Synonym: inform - give information to someone
