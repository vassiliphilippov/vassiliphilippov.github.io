in the lead

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə lid / (AmE), / ɪn ðə liːd / (BrE)
in a position that is ahead of others in a competition, race, or ranking

Examples

Less assured than the tall, wide shouldered man in the lead, Cobb followed alertly, a hand on his gun butt.

Synset Examples

the horse was three lengths ahead going into the home stretch
ahead by two pawns
our candidate is in the lead in the polls
way out front in the race
the advertising campaign put them out front in sales

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ahead - at a position in front; further forward
  • Synonym: out in front - in a leading position, especially in a race or competition
  • Antonym: behind - at a position further back in a competition or race
