hot and bothered

1. ADJ

/ hɑt ənd bəˈðɜrd / (AmE), / hɒt ənd ˈbɒðəd / (BrE)
feeling agitated, anxious, or flustered, often as a result of excitement, nervousness, or confusion

Examples

Some contraband here, but nothing to get in a twist over.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flustered - nervous and confused
  • Antonym: calm - not agitated; free from anxiety
