mind's eye

1. NOUN

/ maɪndz aɪ / (AmE), / maɪndz aɪ / (BrE)
the ability to visualize or imagine pictures and scenes within one's mind

Examples

When painting, Mason's physical eyes are half closed, while his mind's eye is wide open, and this circumstance accounts in part for the impression he wishes to convey.

Synset Examples

I could see her clearly in my mind's eye

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imagination - the faculty of forming new ideas or images not present to the senses
