all the way

1. ADV

/ ɔl ðə weɪ / (AmE), / ɔːl ðə weɪ / (BrE)
to the full or complete extent; completely or thoroughly.

Examples

Because if we do this, we do it all the way, 100%, nothing half-assed.

Synset Examples

read the book clear to the end
slept clear through the night
there were open fields clear to the horizon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: completely - totally; entirely
  • Antonym: partially - not completely; in part
