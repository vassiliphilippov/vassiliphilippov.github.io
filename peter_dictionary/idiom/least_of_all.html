least of all

1. ADV

/ list əv ɔl / (AmE), / liːst əv ɔːl / (BrE)
Used to emphasize especially not or particularly not, introducing the most unlikely candidate or exception in a negative context.

Examples

Mrs. Meeker had n't struck him as ready to seek anyone's advise, least of all Garth's.

Synset Examples

nobody, least of all Joe, agreed with me
