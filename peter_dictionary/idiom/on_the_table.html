on the table

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə ˈteɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈteɪ.bəl / (BrE)
(of a proposal, offer, or issue) open for discussion or negotiation; available to be considered.

Examples

When I wake, if the money is on the table, I'’ll know I have a partner

Synset Examples

negotiable demands
the proposal is still on the table

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off the table - (of a proposal or option) no longer available for discussion or negotiation
