out of thin air

1. ADV

/ aʊt əv θɪn ɛr / (AmE), / aʊt əv θɪn eə / (BrE)
Without warning or apparent cause; suddenly or as if by magic.

Examples

I conjured him... out of thin air.

Synset Examples

your cousin arrived out of thin air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of nowhere - without warning or from an unknown place
