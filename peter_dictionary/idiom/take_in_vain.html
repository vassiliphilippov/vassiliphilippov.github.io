take in vain

1. VERB

/ teɪk ɪn veɪn / (AmE), / teɪk ɪn veɪn / (BrE)
to use a person or deity's name, especially God's, without proper respect or reverence; often used in the context of blasphemy or irreverence.

Examples

Sweet Jesus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blaspheme - to speak irreverently about God or sacred things
