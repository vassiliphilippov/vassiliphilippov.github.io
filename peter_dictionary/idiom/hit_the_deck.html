hit the deck

1. VERB

/ hɪt ðə dɛk / (AmE), / hɪt ðə dɛk / (BrE)
to quickly drop to the ground, usually to avoid danger or protect oneself

Examples

"Sweeney Blue, hit the deck.

Synset Examples

The soldiers hit the dirt when they heard gunfire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duck - to lower your head or body quickly, often to avoid being hit
  • Antonym: stand up - (in this context) to remain upright instead of dropping to avoid danger
