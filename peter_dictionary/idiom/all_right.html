all right

1. ADV

/ ɔl ˈraɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈraɪt / (BrE)
used to emphasize that there is no doubt about something; certainly.

Examples

The home listener is overpowered, all right, but the experience is a far from pleasant one.
"You have it, all right.
It was the call of the wild all right.
He was cold all right.
He was cold all right.

Synset Examples

it's expensive all right

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - used to emphasize a statement or response, meaning 'without doubt'
  • Synonym: definitely - without doubt; for sure
