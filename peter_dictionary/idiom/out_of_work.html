out of work

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv wɝk / (AmE), / aʊt əv wɜːk / (BrE)
not having a job; unemployed.

Examples

I'’ve been out of work... so just give me a little time.

Synset Examples

idle carpenters
jobless transients

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unemployed - without a paid job but available to work
  • Antonym: in work - having a job; employed
