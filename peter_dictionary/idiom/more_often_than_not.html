more often than not

1. ADV

/ mɔr ˈɑfən ðən nɑt / (AmE), / mɔː ˈɒfən ðən nɒt / (BrE)
In most cases; usually; as a general rule.

Examples

More often than not I have found easy excuse to leave my own work and stand at a respectable distance where I could watch this man transform raw nature into a composed, not imitative, painting.
Of such hidden meanings the patient himself is, more often than not, entirely unaware.

Synset Examples

by and large it doesn't rain much here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usually - in most cases; commonly
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; seldom
