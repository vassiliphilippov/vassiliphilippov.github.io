hold sway

1. VERB

/ hoʊld sweɪ / (AmE), / həʊld sweɪ / (BrE)
to have control or great influence over people or situations

Examples

It is not easy for the therapist to discern when, in the patient's communicating, an introject has appeared and is holding sway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reign - to rule as a monarch or to be dominant
  • Synonym: dominate - to have a commanding influence over
