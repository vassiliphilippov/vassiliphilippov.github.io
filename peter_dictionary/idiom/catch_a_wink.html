catch a wink

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ə wɪŋk / (AmE), / katʃ ə wɪŋk / (BrE)
To get a brief period of sleep, especially when it is difficult to do so.

Examples

Oso slept unconcernedly, his rifle cradled in his arms; I did n't catch a wink.

Synset Examples

She naps everyday after lunch for an hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nap - to sleep briefly, especially during the day
