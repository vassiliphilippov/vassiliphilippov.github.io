strike a chord

1. VERB

/ straɪk ə kɔrd / (AmE), / straɪk ə kɔːd / (BrE)
To cause someone to feel sympathy, emotion, or recognition; to elicit an emotional response.

Examples

Stories of the depravity of the guards at Globocnik's camps struck a chord of terror at the mere mention of their names.

Synset Examples

The music struck a chord with the listeners

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resonate - to produce a positive emotional effect; to evoke feelings
  • Antonym: leave cold - to fail to evoke an emotional response
