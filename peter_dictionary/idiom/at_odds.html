at odds

1. ADJ

/ ət ɑdz / (AmE), / ət ɒdz / (BrE)
in disagreement or in conflict with someone or something

Examples

Now that this is at odds with our meaning may be shown as follows.

Synset Examples

the figures are at odds with our findings
contradictory attributes of unjust justice and loving vindictiveness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in conflict - to be in a state of disagreement or opposition
  • Antonym: in agreement - in harmony or accordance with someone or something
