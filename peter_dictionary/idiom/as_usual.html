as usual

1. ADV

/ æz ˈjuʒuəl / (AmE), / æz ˈjuːʒuəl / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something happens in the same way as it normally does or as is typical.

Examples

Night drops as always, sir.

Synset Examples

as usual, she arrived late

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as always - in the same way as always; habitually
