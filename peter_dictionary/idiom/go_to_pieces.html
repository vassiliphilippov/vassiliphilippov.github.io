go to pieces

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə ˈpisɪz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ˈpiːsɪz / (BrE)
To lose control of one's emotions or mental composure; to become extremely upset or unable to function normally.

Examples

Jubal would not have minded it if service had not gone to pieces.

Synset Examples

She fell apart when her only child died

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fall apart - To break down emotionally or mentally; to lose composure
