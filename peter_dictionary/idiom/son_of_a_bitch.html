son of a bitch

1. NOUN

/ sʌn əv ə bɪtʃ / (AmE), / sʌn əv ə bɪtʃ / (BrE)
An offensive and vulgar insult used to refer to someone regarded as unpleasant, annoying, or detestable.

Examples

He'’s backing up those son of a bitches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - offensive insult for an unpleasant person
  • Synonym: jerk - informal insult for a foolish or contemptible person
