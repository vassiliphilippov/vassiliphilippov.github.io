after all

1. ADV

/ ˌæf.tɚ ˈɔl / (AmE), / ˌɑːf.tə ˈɔːl / (BrE)
Used to introduce a statement that explains or supports what has just been said, often emphasizing a relevant fact or consideration.

Examples

After all, we're not communists.

Synset Examples

after all, she is your boss, so invite her
he is, after all, our president
