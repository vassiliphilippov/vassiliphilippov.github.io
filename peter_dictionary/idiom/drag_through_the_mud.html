drag through the mud

1. VERB

/ dræɡ θru ðə mʌd / (AmE), / dræɡ θruː ðə mʌd / (BrE)
to publicly criticize or speak about someone in a way that damages their reputation

Examples

I was also publicly reprimanded, dragged through the mud by the radical press and made a figure of fun by such leftist publications as The New Republic, The New Yorker, Time and the Christian Science Monitor.

Synset Examples

She badmouths her husband everywhere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: badmouth - to criticize someone or something unfairly or maliciously
