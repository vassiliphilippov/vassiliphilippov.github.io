at a loss

1. ADJ

/ ət ə lɔs / (AmE), / ət ə lɒs / (BrE)
Unable to know what to do or say; bewildered or puzzled.

Examples

But then this theory confesses that it is completely at a loss as to how the image can possibly be received by the brain.

Synset Examples

at a loss to understand those remarks
puzzled that she left without saying goodbye

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puzzled - unable to understand or explain something
  • Synonym: bewildered - confused and uncertain what to do
