in the long run

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə lɔŋ rʌn / (AmE), / ɪn ðə lɒŋ rʌn / (BrE)
after a long period of time; eventually, as the final outcome of events or actions

Examples

Department stores were built on dry goods, especially ladies' fashions, and in this area, in the long run, the suburban branches will be hard put to compete against downtown.
Can God be mocked, ever, in the long run?

Synset Examples

she will succeed in the long run

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eventually - in the end, especially after a long delay
  • Synonym: ultimately - in the end; finally
