give it a whirl

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ɪt ə wɜːrl / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɪt ə wɜːl / (BrE)
To try doing something, especially for the first time or to see if it works or if you like it.

Examples

Find out what you like to do most and really give it a whirl.

Synset Examples

let's give it a whirl!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - make an attempt or effort to do something
