close at hand

1. ADJ

/ kloʊs æt hænd / (AmE), / kləʊs ət hand / (BrE)
near in place or time; within easy reach or imminent.

Examples

Calenda was not the sort who walked around without one of his "boys "close at hand.

Synset Examples

the town is close at hand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: near at hand - very near; close by
  • Antonym: distant - far away in space or time
