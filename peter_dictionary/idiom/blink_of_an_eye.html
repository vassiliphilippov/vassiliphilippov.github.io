blink of an eye

1. NOUN

/ blɪŋk əv ən aɪ / (AmE), / blɪŋk əv ən aɪ / (BrE)
an extremely brief moment; a very short period of time

Examples

Old life blown away in the blink of an eye.

Synset Examples

if I had the chance I'd do it in a flash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moment - a very short period of time
  • Synonym: instant - an extremely brief period of time
