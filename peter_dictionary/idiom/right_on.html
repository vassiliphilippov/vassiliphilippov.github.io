right on

1. INTJ

/ raɪt ɑn / (AmE), / raɪt ɒn / (BrE)
Used to express strong agreement or approval, especially in informal situations.

Examples

That's it, break it all, you spoiled Guinea brat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exactly - used to express agreement, as in 'that's right' or 'precisely'
