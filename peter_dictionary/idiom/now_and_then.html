now and then

1. ADV

/ naʊ ənd ðɛn / (AmE), / naʊ ənd ðɛn / (BrE)
from time to time; occasionally; at intervals, but not regularly or frequently.

Examples

Now and then he would disappear for several days.
"I do n't mind washing dishes now and then ", he said pleasantly.
Now and then, the glistening side of a half swamped object showed as it swept past.

Synset Examples

he was arrogant and occasionally callous
open areas are only occasionally interrupted by clumps of trees
they visit New York on occasion
now and again she would take her favorite book from the shelf and read to us
as we drove along, the beautiful scenery now and then attracted his attention

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - at infrequent or irregular intervals; now and then
  • Antonym: constantly - happening all the time; continuously; antonym of 'occasionally'
