closed book

1. NOUN

/ kloʊzd bʊk / (AmE), / kləʊzd bʊk / (BrE)
A subject or person that is difficult to understand or about which little is known; something mysterious or incomprehensible.

Examples

Like a closed book.

Synset Examples

how it got out is a mystery
it remains one of nature's secrets

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open book - someone or something easy to understand; opposite of 'closed book'
  • Synonym: mystery - something difficult or impossible to understand or explain
