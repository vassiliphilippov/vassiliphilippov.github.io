off and on

1. ADV

/ ɑf ənd ɑn / (AmE), / ɒf ənd ɒn / (BrE)
Occurring at irregular intervals; intermittently or not regularly.

Examples

Off and on, all day, someone would be wiping at the powdery gray dust that settled over everything.

Synset Examples

they phone each other off and on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on and off - intermittently; not regularly; variant of 'off and on'
  • Antonym: constantly - happening all the time without interruption; antonym of intermittent
