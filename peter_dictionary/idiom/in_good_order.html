in good order

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɡʊd ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ɪn ɡʊd ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
in a proper, correct, or well-organized manner; working or arranged as it should be

Examples

Then we have informed a large number of our crew that when they reach the United States, they will be punished but that in the meanwhile, they may run loose and are expected to perform their jobs in good order.

Synset Examples

please do your job properly!
can't you carry me decent?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: properly - in the correct or right way
  • Synonym: in order - arranged or working as desired
  • Antonym: out of order - not functioning or not arranged correctly
