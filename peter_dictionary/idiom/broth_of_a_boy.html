broth of a boy

1. NOUN

/ brɑːθ əv ə bɔɪ / (AmE), / brɒθ əv ə bɔɪ / (BrE)
A lively and outstanding boy, full of spirit and energy, often used with affectionate admiration, especially in Irish English.

Examples

This was a broth of a boy, our Felix, and nothing was more obvious than the joy he took in demonstrating how agile he was and how full of juice and spirit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broth of a girl - Spirited and outstanding girl (similar idiom, less common)

Etymology

From the comparison to a rich, savory broth—a concentrated, excellent result. Used especially in Ireland.
