the size of it

1. NOUN

/ ðə saɪz əv ɪt / (AmE), / ðə saɪz əv ɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate the true state of affairs or the main point of a situation.

Examples

Some people have luck and some have no luck and that, whatever people who prefer order say, is the size of it.

Synset Examples

that's the size of the situation
she hates me, that's about the size of it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: the gist - the essential part or main point of something
