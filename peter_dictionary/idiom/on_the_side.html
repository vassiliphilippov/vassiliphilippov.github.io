on the side

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə saɪd / (AmE), / ɒn ðə saɪd / (BrE)
in addition to one's main job or activity, often without official approval or as a means of earning extra money

Examples

Every man who dabbles in the market to make a little easy money on the side and suffers losses could at the time hardly face his wife who was wondering how her husband could be so dumb.

Synset Examples

he had made some money on the side

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonlighting - working a second job alongside a main one, often secretly
