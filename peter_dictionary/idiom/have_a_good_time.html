have a good time

1. VERB

/ hæv ə ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / hæv ə ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
to enjoy oneself greatly; to experience pleasure or happiness in an activity or event

Examples

Niggers are having a good time with our policy banks in Harlem.

Synset Examples

We had a ball at the party and didn't come home until 2 AM

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enjoy oneself - to take delight or pleasure in an activity
  • Synonym: have fun - to experience enjoyment or amusement
