cold fish

1. NOUN

/ koʊld fɪʃ / (AmE), / kəʊld fɪʃ / (BrE)
A person who is unemotional, distant, or unresponsive in social situations.

Examples

Rumor has it you're a real cold fish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ice queen - a woman regarded as cold and unfeeling
  • Antonym: warm - showing friendliness or affection
