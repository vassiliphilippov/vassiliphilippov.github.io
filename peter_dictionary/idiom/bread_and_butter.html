bread and butter

1. NOUN

/ brɛd ənd ˈbʌtɚ / (AmE), / brɛd ənd ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
A person's main source of income or means of earning a living.

Examples

Since ordinary breakfast-table conversation was impossible, it was at least something that they were able to offer Eugene the sugar bowl with their sugar in it, and the plate of bread and butter, and that Eugene could return the pitcher of hot milk to them handle first.

Synset Examples

each child was expected to pay for their keep
he applied to the state for support
he could no longer earn his own livelihood

Related Terms

  • Synonym: livelihood - means of securing the necessities of life
  • Synonym: income - money received for work or investment

Etymology

Refers metaphorically to basic sustenance, derived from the literal staple foods of bread and butter.
