in the air

1. ADV

/ ɪn ði ɛr / (AmE), / ɪn ði ɛə / (BrE)
(of a feeling, idea, etc.) prevalent or felt by many people; generally noticeable or being thought about by many at the same time.

Examples

I have not seen this charge made during my stay here, but apparently it is still in the air.
It was in the air.
But, at the beginning, this stayed just in the air.

Synset Examples

Christmas was in the air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in vogue - popular or fashionable at a particular time
  • Synonym: widespread - existing or happening in many places or among many people
