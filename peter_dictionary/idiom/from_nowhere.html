from nowhere

1. ADV

/ frəm ˈnoʊˌwɛr / (AmE), / frəm ˈnəʊˌwɛə / (BrE)
used to describe something that appears or happens suddenly and unexpectedly, without any apparent cause or origin

Examples

A new idea drifted in from nowhere.

Synset Examples

your cousin arrived out of thin air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out of thin air - used to indicate something happens or appears as if by magic, without visible cause
