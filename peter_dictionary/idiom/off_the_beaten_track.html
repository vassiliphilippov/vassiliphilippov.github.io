off the beaten track

1. ADJ

/ ɔf ðə ˈbitn̩ tɹæk / (AmE), / ɒf ðə ˈbiːtn ˈtræk / (BrE)
in a place that is remote or not often visited by many people; away from frequently traveled or popular areas

Examples

It's somewhat off the beaten track, to be sure, but therein lies its variety and charm.

Synset Examples

they found a quiet out-of-the-way resort

Related Terms

  • Synonym: out-of-the-way - in a remote or secluded location
