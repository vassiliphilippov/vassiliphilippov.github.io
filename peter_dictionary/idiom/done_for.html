done for

1. ADJ

/ dʌn fɔr / (AmE), / dʌn fɔː / (BrE)
Destroyed or doomed; in a situation where defeat, death, or ruin is inevitable.

Examples

The big shock everybody had when they found ol Slater and those others done for.

Synset Examples

we are gone geese

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finished - at an end; no longer able to continue
  • Synonym: ruined - destroyed or spoiled completely
  • Antonym: safe - free from harm or risk; not threatened
