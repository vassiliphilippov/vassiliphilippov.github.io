cut to ribbons

1. VERB

/ kʌt tə ˈrɪbənz / (AmE), / kʌt tə ˈrɪbənz / (BrE)
to defeat, criticize, or damage someone or something completely and severely

Examples

With her sharp tongue she 'd have cut his pompousness to ribbons.

Synset Examples

We must cut the other team to ribbons!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tear apart - to criticize or damage severely
  • Synonym: annihilate - to destroy or defeat utterly
