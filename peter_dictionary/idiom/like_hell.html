like hell

1. ADV

/ laɪk hɛl / (AmE), / laɪk hɛl / (BrE)
Used ironically to strongly deny or contradict what was just stated; expresses disbelief or rejection.

Examples

That's not exactly what I had in mind, sir.

Synset Examples

says he'll help me? Like hell he will!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: not at all - not in any way; absolutely not
  • Synonym: no way - absolutely not; no possibility
  • Antonym: certainly - with certainty; yes (opposite meaning)
