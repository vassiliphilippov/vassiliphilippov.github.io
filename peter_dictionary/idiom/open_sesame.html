open sesame

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊ.pən ˈsɛs.ə.mi / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.pən ˈsɛs.ə.mi / (BrE)
A method or means that will achieve a desired result easily, especially one that provides instant access, success, or entry.

Examples

The expression was his trade-mark, his open sesame to good luck, and his prayer that pilot and plane would always return.

Etymology

From the magical phrase 'Open Sesame!' in the tale 'Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves' from The Arabian Nights, used to open a hidden door.
