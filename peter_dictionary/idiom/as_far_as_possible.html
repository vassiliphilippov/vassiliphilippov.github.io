as far as possible

1. ADV

/ əz fɑr əz ˈpɑsəbəl / (AmE), / əz fɑːr əz ˈpɒsəbl / (BrE)
to the greatest extent that is feasible or achievable; as much as can be done.

Examples

Discussions of the giants one by one will include, as far as possible, data on these aspects of growth:

Synset Examples

she helped him as much as possible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as much as possible - to the greatest degree achievable
  • Antonym: as little as possible - to the smallest degree achievable
