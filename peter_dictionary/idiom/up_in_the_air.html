up in the air

1. ADJ

/ ʌp ɪn ði ɛr / (AmE), / ʌp ɪn ðiː eə / (BrE)
(of a plan or situation) not yet settled; uncertain or undecided.

Examples

It's all up in the air again ".

Synset Examples

left everything up in the air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undecided - not yet determined or settled
  • Antonym: certain - sure, definite, not in doubt
