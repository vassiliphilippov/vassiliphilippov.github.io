give or take

1. ADV

/ ˈɡɪv ər teɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɪv ɔː teɪk / (BrE)
Used to indicate that a number or amount is approximate and could be slightly more or less than stated.

Examples

Probably around midnight, give or take an hour either way ".

Synset Examples

it is a mile away, give or take a few hundred yards

Related Terms

  • Synonym: about - approximately, roughly
  • Synonym: roughly - approximately, not precisely
