once and for all

1. ADV

/ wʌns ənd fɔr ɔl / (AmE), / wʌns ənd fɔːr ɔːl / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is done decisively, finally, and will not need to be dealt with again

Examples

Maybe they 'll stop it now, once for all.
Ought not an edifying Trial have made every effort to demonstrate this once and for all by showing how representative types of "mere "anti-Semites were drawn step by step into the program of skull bashings and gassings?

Synset Examples

we settled the problem conclusively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: for good - permanently; for always
