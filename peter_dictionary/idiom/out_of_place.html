out of place

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv pleɪs / (AmE), / aʊt əv pleɪs / (BrE)
not appropriate or suitable for a particular situation; not in the correct or usual position

Examples

Let his bones and his memory rest in the fifteenth century where they belong; he is out of place in our times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • Synonym: out of context - not appropriate to the surrounding text or situation
  • Antonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
