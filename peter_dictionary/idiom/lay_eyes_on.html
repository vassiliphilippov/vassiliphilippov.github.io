lay eyes on

1. VERB

/ leɪ aɪz ɑn / (AmE), / leɪ aɪz ɒn / (BrE)
to see or notice someone or something, especially for the first time or with attention

Examples

She is the most beautiful thing you ever laid eyes on, and her dancing has a feminine suavity, lightness, sparkle, and refinement which are simply incomparable.

Synset Examples

behold Christ!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: behold - to see or observe, often with attention
  • Degree Lower: glimpse - to see briefly or partially
