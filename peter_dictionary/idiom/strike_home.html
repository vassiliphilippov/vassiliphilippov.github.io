strike home

1. VERB

/ straɪk hoʊm / (AmE), / straɪk həʊm / (BrE)
(of a remark or message) to have a strong effect or be fully understood; to be especially relevant or apt.

Examples

Whether it will strike home for you or not will be for you to determine.

Synset Examples

I hope this message hits home!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit home - (of a remark or message) to have a strong effect or be clearly understood
