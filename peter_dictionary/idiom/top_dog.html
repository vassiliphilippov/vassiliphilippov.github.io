top dog

1. NOUN

/ tɑp dɔg / (AmE), / tɒp dɒg / (BrE)
The person who is most important or has the highest authority in a group, organization, or situation.

Examples

Since he has just shown who is top dog, he may not be ready to receive this highest honor in the gift of the Soviet people.
Top dog, sleeping and eating right there with the Staff.
Top dog.

Synset Examples

the head of the whole operation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underdog - a person who is expected to lose in a competition or struggle

Etymology

Originally from dog fighting, where the 'top dog' was the winner.
