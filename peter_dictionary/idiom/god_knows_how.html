God knows how

1. ADV

/ ɡɑd noʊz haʊ / (AmE), / ɡɒd nəʊz haʊ / (BrE)
Used to express that the speaker does not know or cannot explain how something happened.

Examples

God knows how, but I got it.

Synset Examples

God knows how he did it, but he did climbed that steep wall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: somehow - in some way; by some means
