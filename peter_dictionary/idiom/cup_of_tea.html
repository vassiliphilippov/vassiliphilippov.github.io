cup of tea

1. NOUN

/ kʌp əv ti / (AmE), / kʌp əv tiː / (BrE)
something that someone likes or is good at; a person’s preference or area of expertise.

Examples

The three Indian brass deities, though - Ganessa, Siva, and Krishna - are an altogether different cup of tea.

Synset Examples

chemistry is not my cup of tea
his bag now is learning to play golf
marriage was scarcely his dish

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thing - thing that suits someone's taste
  • Antonym: not one's cup of tea - something one dislikes or is not good at
