break someone's heart

1. VERB

/ breɪk sʌmwʌnz hɑrt / (AmE), / breɪk sʌmwʌnz hɑːt / (BrE)
To cause someone deep emotional pain, especially by ending a romantic relationship or betraying their trust.

Examples

Break your father'’s **heart** on his birthday.

Synset Examples

The young man broke the girl's heart when he told her was going to marry her best friend

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devastate - to cause great emotional pain or shock
  • Antonym: console - to comfort someone who is sad or disappointed
