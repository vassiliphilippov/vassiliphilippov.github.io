get the best (of someone)

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə bɛst (əv ˈsʌmwʌn) / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə bɛst (ɒv ˈsʌmwʌn) / (BrE)
(Of an emotion, situation, or condition) to gain control over or defeat someone, often despite their efforts to resist.

Examples

High blood pressure can get the best of any of us.)

Synset Examples

Heart disease can get the best of us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overcome - to defeat or prevail over someone or something
  • Synonym: succumb - to fail to resist pressure, temptation, or some negative force
