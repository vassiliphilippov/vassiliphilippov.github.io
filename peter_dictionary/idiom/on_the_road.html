on the road

1. NOUN

/ ɑn ðə roʊd / (AmE), / ɒn ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
traveling from place to place, especially as part of one's work, activities, or performances; away from one's usual location, particularly in reference to teams or performers.

Examples

Ed Delahanty and Chuck Klein of the Phillies, the Braves' Joe Adcock, Lou Gehrig of the Yankees, Pat Seerey of the White Sox and Rocky Colavito, then with Cleveland, made their history on the road.

Synset Examples

they took the show on the road
they lost all their games on the road

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on tour - travelling from place to place for performances or events
