see eye to eye

1. VERB

/ siː aɪ tə aɪ / (AmE), / siː aɪ tʊ aɪ / (BrE)
to be in full agreement with someone; to have the same opinion or viewpoint

Examples

"Seems like we 're never going to see eye to eye, Lieutenant.

Synset Examples

We never saw eye to eye on this question

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agree - to have the same opinion
  • Antonym: disagree - to have a different opinion
