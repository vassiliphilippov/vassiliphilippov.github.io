out to

1. ADJ

/ aʊt tu / (AmE), / aʊt tʊ / (BrE)
Intending or determined to achieve something; having a clear purpose or aim.

Examples

Not surprisingly, Dr. Ellis says, some recently enlightened wives are out to claim these capabilities.

Synset Examples

bent on going to the theater
dead set against intervening
out to win every event

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bent on - having a determined intention to do something
  • Synonym: set on - having a firm intention to do something
