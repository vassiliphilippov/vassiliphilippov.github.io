taken for granted

1. ADJ

/ ˈteɪkən fər ˈɡræn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈteɪkən fə ˈɡrɑːn.tɪd / (BrE)
Accepted as true or existing without questioning or requiring proof.

Examples

The terms are generally taken for granted as though they referred to direct and axiomatic elements in the common experience of all.

Synset Examples

an axiomatic truth
we hold these truths to be self-evident

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-evident - obvious; needing no proof or explanation
  • Synonym: axiomatic - evident without proof or argument
