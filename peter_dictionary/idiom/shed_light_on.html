shed light on

1. VERB

/ ʃɛd laɪt ɑn / (AmE), / ʃɛd laɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to make something clearer or easier to understand; to clarify or explain

Examples

Before this week is out... ... we are going to knock down their doors... ... and shed the **light** of the law **on** that nest of vipers.

Synset Examples

Could you clarify these remarks?
Clear up the question of who is at fault

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarify - to make clear or easier to understand
  • Antonym: obscure - to make unclear or difficult to understand
