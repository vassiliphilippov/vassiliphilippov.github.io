from the heart

1. ADV

/ frəm ðə hɑrt / (AmE), / frəm ðə hɑːt / (BrE)
In a way that is very sincere and genuine.

Examples

These little songs, however, were sweet nothings from the heart, tender memories of his childhood, little melodies that anyone could hum and that would make one want to weep.

Synset Examples

he spoke from the heart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sincerely - in a sincere way; without pretense
