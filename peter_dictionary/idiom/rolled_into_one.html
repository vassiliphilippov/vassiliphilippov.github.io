rolled into one

1. ADJ

/ roʊld ˌɪntu ˈwʌn / (AmE), / rəʊld ˌɪntuː ˈwʌn / (BrE)
combined in such a way that several qualities, functions, or things are joined together in a single person or object

Examples

He kills when he pleases, takes his women where he finds them and always acts as judge, jury and executioner rolled into one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all in one - everything combined into a single entity
