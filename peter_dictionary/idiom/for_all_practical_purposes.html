for all practical purposes

1. ADV

/ fɔr ɔl ˈpræktɪkəl ˈpɜrpəsɪz / (AmE), / fə ɔːl ˈpræktɪkəl ˈpɜːpəsɪz / (BrE)
Used to indicate that although something is not technically or officially the case, it is so in practice.

Examples

Today Northern Vietnam is overtly Communist; Laos is teetering between Communism and pro Communist neutralism; Cambodia is, for all practical purposes, neutralist.

Synset Examples

to all intents and purposes the case is closed
the rest are for all practical purposes useless

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to all intents and purposes - used to mean 'in every practical sense'
