from time to time

1. ADV

/ frəm taɪm tə taɪm / (AmE), / frəm taɪm tə taɪm / (BrE)
occasionally; at intervals; not regularly or continuously

Examples

Yeah, I'm known to locate certain things from time to time.

Synset Examples

he was arrogant and occasionally callous
open areas are only occasionally interrupted by clumps of trees
they visit New York on occasion
now and again she would take her favorite book from the shelf and read to us
as we drove along, the beautiful scenery now and then attracted his attention

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - happening from time to time; not regularly or continuously
  • Degree Higher: frequently - happening often; at short intervals
  • Degree Lower: rarely - not happening often; seldom
