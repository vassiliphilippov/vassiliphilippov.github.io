hot potato

1. NOUN

/ hɑt pəˈteɪɾoʊ / (AmE), / hɒt pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
a difficult or controversial issue or situation that people avoid dealing with

Examples

A spectator picked up the ball and handed it to a small boy, who dropped this suddenly hot potato in a very playable lie.

Synset Examples

he dropped the topic like a hot potato

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sticky wicket - a tricky or awkward situation
  • Synonym: elephant in the room - an obvious problem that is being ignored
