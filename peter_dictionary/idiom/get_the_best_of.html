get the best of

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ðə bɛst ʌv / (AmE), / ɡɛt ðə bɛst ɒv / (BrE)
to defeat, overcome, or gain an advantage over (someone or something), often unexpectedly or despite the other's efforts

Examples

I like to think the last thing that went through his head, other than that bullet, was to wonder how the hell Andy Dufresne ever got the best of him.

Synset Examples

Heart disease can get the best of us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defeat - to win a victory over someone; to overcome
  • Antonym: succumb - to fail to resist, to be overcome
