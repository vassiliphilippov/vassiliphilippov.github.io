take a breather

1. VERB

/ teɪk ə ˈbriːðər / (AmE), / teɪk ə ˈbriːðə / (BrE)
to take a short break from an activity in order to rest and recover; to pause and relax briefly

Examples

After you have taken a breather, reverse the position of your legs so that the front thigh of the previous exercise is now to the rear, and the rear thigh now to the front, and perform the same movement in the same manner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rest - to stop work or movement in order to relax or recover strength
  • Synonym: take a break - stop an activity briefly to relax
