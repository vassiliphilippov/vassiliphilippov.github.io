under fire

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌndɚ ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
subjected to attack or strong criticism; being criticized or attacked, especially by many people or from many directions

Examples

The jury also commented on the Fulton ordinary's court which has been under fire for its practices in the appointment of appraisers, guardians and administrators and the awarding of fees and compensation.
He was n't troubled with the ordinary, rank-and-file fear that overcomes and paralyzes and sends individual soldiers and whole companies under fire running in panic.

Synset Examples

an official under fire for mismanagement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: under attack - subjected to an attack, usually used literally or figuratively
  • Antonym: out of the firing line - no longer under direct attack or criticism
