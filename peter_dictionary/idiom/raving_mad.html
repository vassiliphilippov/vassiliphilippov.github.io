raving mad

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪvɪŋ mæd / (AmE), / ˈreɪvɪŋ mæd / (BrE)
behaving or speaking in a way that is completely irrational or insane

Examples

He is "a man raving mad on the way to the churchyard ".

Synset Examples

a raving lunatic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - in a state of serious mental illness; behaving in a lunatic way
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not mad or mentally ill
