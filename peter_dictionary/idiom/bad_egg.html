bad egg

1. NOUN

/ bæd ɛg / (AmE), / bad ɛg / (BrE)
A person who behaves dishonestly or is untrustworthy; a rogue.

Examples

It would be a shame, Mr. Chairman, if we allowed a few rotten apples to give a bad name to the whole barrel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotten apple - a disreputable or corrupt person in a group

Etymology

Refers metaphorically to an egg that is spoiled and thus undesirable, extended in 19th-century American English to describe people of bad character.
