of all time

1. ADV

/ əv ɔl taɪm / (AmE), / əv ɔːl taɪm / (BrE)
used to describe someone or something as the greatest, worst, or most significant in history or across any period

Examples

Artur Schnabel was one of the greatest Schubert-Beethoven-Mozart players of all time, and any commentary of his on this repertory is valuable.
These questions have remained one of the great sea mysteries of all time.

Synset Examples

did you ever smoke?
the best con man of all time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ever - at any time; always
