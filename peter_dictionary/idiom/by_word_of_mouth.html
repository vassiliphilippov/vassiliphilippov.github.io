by word of mouth

1. ADV

/ baɪ wɝːd əv maʊθ / (AmE), / baɪ wɜːd ɒv maʊθ / (BrE)
by being told orally rather than through writing or print; through spoken communication.

Examples

This can be done by stories in your house organs, posters, special publications, letters to workers' homes as well as by word of mouth through your chain of command.

Synset Examples

I heard it viva voce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orally - by word of mouth, spoken rather than written
  • Antonym: in writing - communicated through written means
