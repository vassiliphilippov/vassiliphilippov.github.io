naked as the day you were born

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪkɪd əz ðə deɪ ju wɚ bɔrn / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd əz ðə deɪ juː wə bɔːn / (BrE)
Completely naked; wearing nothing at all, just as one is at birth.

Examples

They march you in, naked as the day you were born, skin burning and half-blind from that delousing shit they throw on you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stark naked - completely unclothed; not wearing any clothes
  • Synonym: in the buff - naked; not wearing any clothes (informal)
