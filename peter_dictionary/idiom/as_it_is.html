as it is

1. ADV

/ æz ɪt ɪz / (AmE), / az ɪt ɪz / (BrE)
Used to indicate the actual situation, typically implying that it is sufficient, unexpected, or contrary to what might have been expected.

Examples

As it is, they consider that the North is now reaping the fruits of excess egalitarianism, that in spite of its high standard of living the "American way "has been proved inferior to the English and Scandinavian ways, although they disapprove of the socialistic features of the latter.
They are about to do so at any moment as it is.

Synset Examples

he might have been killed; as it is he was severely injured

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in fact - actually, in reality; used to emphasize the truth of a statement
  • Synonym: as things stand - in the present circumstances; considering the situation now

2. ADV

/ æz ɪt ɪz / (AmE), / æz ɪt ɪz / (BrE)
In the existing situation; given the current circumstances, often implying that things are already sufficient or problematic.

Examples

I feel awkward enough as it is.

Synset Examples

he might have been killed; as it is he was severely injured
