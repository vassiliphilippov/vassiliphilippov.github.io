in one's right mind

1. ADJ

/ ɪn wʌnz raɪt maɪnd / (AmE), / ɪn wʌnz raɪt maɪnd / (BrE)
thinking or behaving in a normal, sensible, or rational way; not crazy or foolish.

Examples

Nobody in his right mind punishes a quarter-century old dereliction.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: crazy - mentally unsound; insane
  • Antonym: out of one's mind - not thinking or acting rationally; insane
