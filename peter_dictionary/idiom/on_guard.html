on guard

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / ɒn ˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
Alert and watchful for possible danger or trouble.

Examples

He wisely decided that it would be foolish to create a disturbance during the coming roundup, particularly since the Mexicans were on their guard.

Synset Examples

on guard against con artists
must remain on your guard in such a situation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigilant - keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties
  • Synonym: alert - quick to notice and respond; watchful
  • Antonym: off guard - not prepared or not alert
