pipe dream

1. NOUN

/ paɪp driːm / (AmE), / paɪp driːm / (BrE)
An idea, plan, or hope that is impossible or very unlikely to happen; a fantastic but unrealistic hope or ambition.

Examples

These are just shitty pipe dreams.

Synset Examples

I have this pipe dream about being emperor of the universe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy - imaginative idea, especially one that is unlikely or impossible
  • Synonym: delusion - false belief or impression
  • Antonym: realistic expectation - a hope or plan that is plausible or achievable

Etymology

Refers to hallucinations experienced after smoking an opium pipe, first recorded in the late 19th century.
