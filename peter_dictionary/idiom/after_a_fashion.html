after a fashion

1. ADV

/ ˈæftɚ ə ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / ˈɑːftə ə ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
to some extent, but not very well; in a way that is not entirely satisfactory

Examples

But Mercer hung on, living, after a fashion, in a Greenwich Village fourth flight walk-up.

Synset Examples

he speaks French after a fashion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sort of - to some extent; in a way but not exactly
  • Synonym: in a way - to some extent; not completely or exactly
