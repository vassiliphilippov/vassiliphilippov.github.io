food for thought

1. NOUN

/ fud fər θɔt / (AmE), / fuːd fə θɔːt / (BrE)
something that stimulates careful consideration or reflection

Examples

A brief list of the great detective's little idiosyncrasies would provide Dr. Freud with ample food for thought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brain-teaser - a problem or puzzle that requires careful thought
