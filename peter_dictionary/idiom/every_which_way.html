every which way

1. ADV

/ ˈɛvɹi wɪtʃ weɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛvɹi wɪtʃ weɪ / (BrE)
In all directions or in a random and disorganized manner.

Examples

"This is moving day ", Winston reminded her, "and I bet you left things every which way upstairs, your clothes all over the floor and the bed not made.

Synset Examples

the houses were randomly scattered
bullets were fired into the crowd at random

Related Terms

  • Synonym: randomly - in a way that is not ordered or planned
  • Synonym: haphazardly - in a way that lacks organization or planning
  • Antonym: neatly - in a tidy and orderly manner
