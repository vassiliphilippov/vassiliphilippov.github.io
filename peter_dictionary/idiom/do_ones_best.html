do one's best

1. VERB

/ du wʌnz bɛst / (AmE), / duː wʌnz best / (BrE)
to try as hard as possible to achieve the best result one can

Examples

"I do my best ", said Mr. Skyros earnestly, "just for you, my friend.
But I do my best for you ".

Synset Examples

The cast gives full measure every night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go all out - to make one's greatest possible effort
  • Synonym: give one's all - to put forth all of one's effort

2. VERB

/ du wʌnz bɛst / (AmE), / duː wʌnz best / (BrE)
to make the greatest possible effort to achieve something

Examples

- I'll do my best.

Synset Examples

The cast gives full measure every night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: try - to make an attempt to do something
  • Antonym: give up - to stop trying; to surrender
