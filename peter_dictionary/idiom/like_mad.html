like mad

1. ADV

/ laɪk mæd / (AmE), / laɪk mæd / (BrE)
With great speed, effort, or intensity; very hard or fast.

Examples

I swam like mad, got out of the pool, grabbed my robe, and ran to the car.

Synset Examples

drove like crazy
worked like hell to get the job done
ran like sin for the storm cellar
work like thunder
fought like the devil

Related Terms

  • Synonym: like crazy - with great speed or effort; very intensely
  • Synonym: like hell - with great intensity or speed (informal)
  • Synonym: furiously - in an extremely energetic or intense manner
