in due course

1. ADV

/ ɪn du ˈkɔrs / (AmE), / ɪn djuː ˈkɔːs / (BrE)
At the appropriate or natural time, after a normal or expected period has passed.

Examples

Well, the odious little toad went along chivying animals and humans who could n't retaliate, and in due course, as was inevitable, overreached himself.

Synset Examples

we'll get to this question in due course

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eventually - in the end, especially after a long delay
  • Antonym: promptly - without delay, immediately
