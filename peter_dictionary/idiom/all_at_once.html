all at once

1. ADV

/ ɔl ət wʌns / (AmE), / ɔːl ət wʌns / (BrE)
Suddenly and without warning; very quickly or unexpectedly.

Examples

The young man spoke steadily enough, but all at once he looked grotesquely unshaven.

Synset Examples

all at once, he started shouting

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all of a sudden - very quickly and without warning
