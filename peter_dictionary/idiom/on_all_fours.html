on all fours

1. ADV

/ ɑn ɔl fɔrz / (AmE), / ɒn ɔːl fɔːz / (BrE)
With hands and knees on the ground, as when crawling.

Examples

Follow this by crossing from one corner of the room to the other on all fours, kicking as high as possible.

Synset Examples

he got down on all fours to play with his grandson
