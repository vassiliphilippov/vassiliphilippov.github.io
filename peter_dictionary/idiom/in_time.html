in time

1. ADV

/ ɪn taɪm / (AmE), / ɪn taɪm / (BrE)
before the expected or final moment; soon enough; without being late

Examples

"Maybe in time to make a cross and dig our graves ".

Synset Examples

we made it to the party in time

Related Terms

  • Antonym: on time - at the scheduled or expected time, not late
