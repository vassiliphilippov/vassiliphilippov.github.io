the least bit

1. ADV

/ ðə list bɪt / (AmE), / ðə liːst bɪt / (BrE)
To any extent at all; in the slightest degree.

Examples

It was the only thing about her that was the least bit hard to remember.
"Not the least bit ", Arlene snapped.

Synset Examples

Are you at all interested? No, not at all
was not in the least unfriendly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at all - to any extent; in any way
