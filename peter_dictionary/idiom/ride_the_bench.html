ride the bench

1. VERB

/ raɪd ðə bɛntʃ / (AmE), / raɪd ðə bɛntʃ / (BrE)
(of a sports player) to not participate in a game and remain as a substitute; to not be part of the action.

Examples

That means Stan Musial probably will ride the bench on the seventh anniversary of his record five home run day against the Giants.

Synset Examples

Miller was riding the bench in Saturday's game

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sit on the bench - remain as a substitute in a sports game
