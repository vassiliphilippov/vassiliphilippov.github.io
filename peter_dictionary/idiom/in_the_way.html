in the way

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn ðə weɪ / (BrE)
Obstructing progress or causing a hindrance; preventing someone or something from moving forward or making progress.

Examples

Why is it, every time I come for you... ...somebody always gets in the way?

Synset Examples

she might have succeeded in her ambition, had not circumstances been in her way

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the way of - as an obstacle or hindrance to
  • Antonym: out of the way - not obstructing, not causing a hindrance
