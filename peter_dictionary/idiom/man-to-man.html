man-to-man

1. ADV

/ ˌmæn tə ˈmæn / (AmE), / ˌmæn tə ˈmæn / (BrE)
in a direct, honest, or personal way, typically in a conversation between two people

Examples

He had preached a short sermon, trying to talk man-to-man to the audience, to tell them who he was, what he had done in Macon and Birmingham, and what he proposed to do here.

Synset Examples

we must talk man-to-man
