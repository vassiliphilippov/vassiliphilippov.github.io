by heart

1. ADV

/ baɪ hɑrt / (AmE), / baɪ hɑːt / (BrE)
If you know or say something by heart, you have learned it so well that you can remember and repeat it without reading it.

Examples

They hope, of course, to reclaim the non-Catholic population to the Catholic faith, and at every Sunday Benediction they recite by heart the "Prayer for England ":

Synset Examples

she knew the poem by heart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: from memory - (idiom) without using written notes; from what one remembers
