in arrears

1. ADV

/ ɪn əˈrɪrz / (AmE), / ɪn əˈrɪəz / (BrE)
Owing money that should have been paid earlier; behind in paying debts or bills.

Examples

I don't care if this festival is in debt, I want a parade.

Synset Examples

he fell behind with his mortgage payments
a month behind in the rent
a company that has been run behindhand for years
in arrears with their utility bills

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in debt - owing money to someone; not having paid what is due
  • Antonym: up to date - having paid all owed amounts at the present time

Etymology

From Old French *arere* (behind), from Latin *ad retro* (to the rear).
