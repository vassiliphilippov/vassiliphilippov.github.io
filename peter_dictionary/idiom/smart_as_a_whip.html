smart as a whip

1. ADJ

/ smɑrt əz ə wɪp / (AmE), / smɑːt əz ə wɪp / (BrE)
Extremely intelligent; very quick-witted or sharp.

Examples

The wife is likely to be young, sophisticated, smart as a whip - often a girl who has sacrificed a promising career for marriage.

Synset Examples

some men dislike brainy women
a brilliant mind
a brilliant solution to the problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brilliant - extremely intelligent; exceptionally clever
  • Synonym: clever - quick to understand or do things; intelligent
  • Antonym: dull - lacking intelligence or wit
