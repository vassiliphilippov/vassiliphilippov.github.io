walk on air

1. VERB

/ wɑk ɑn ɛr / (AmE), / wɔːk ɒn eə / (BrE)
to feel extremely happy or elated; to be in a state of great joy.

Examples

The music drove them off, or away, and he was free to walk on air in a very few moments, humming and jiving within, beating the rhythm within.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: float on air - feel extremely happy or elated
  • Synonym: over the moon - extremely pleased or happy
  • Antonym: down in the dumps - feeling sad or depressed
