in vogue

1. ADJ

/ ɪn voʊɡ / (AmE), / ɪn vəʊɡ / (BrE)
Popular or fashionable at the present time.

Examples

It will readily be seen that in this suggested network (not materially different from some of the networks in vogue today) greater emphasis on monitoring is implied than is usually put into practice.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of fashion - no longer fashionable or popular

Etymology

From French 'vogue' (fashion, style), itself from Old French 'voguer' (to sail).
