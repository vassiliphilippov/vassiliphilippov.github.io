properly speaking

1. ADV

/ ˈprɑːpɚli ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəli ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Strictly or exactly speaking; in the most accurate sense of the term.

Examples

Flatness may now monopolize everything, but it is a flatness become so ambiguous and expanded as to turn into illusion itself - at least an optical if not, properly speaking, a pictorial illusion.

Synset Examples

properly speaking, they are not husband and wife

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strictly speaking - in the most precise or literal sense
