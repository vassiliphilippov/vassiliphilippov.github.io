in the midst

1. ADV

/ ɪn ðə mɪdst / (AmE), / ɪn ðə mɪdst / (BrE)
in the middle of or surrounded by (something or someone)

Examples

In the midst of this crowded region was the Allen African Methodist Episcopal Church.
Could he walk out in the midst of his piece?
As usual, Mrs. Crombie was standing in the midst of a confusion of cooking utensils.

Synset Examples

in the midst of the forest
could he walk out in the midst of his piece?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amid - surrounded by; in the middle of
  • Synonym: among - surrounded by; in the company of
  • Synonym: center - the middle point or part of something
