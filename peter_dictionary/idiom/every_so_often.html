every so often

1. ADV

/ ˈɛvɹi soʊ ˈɑfən / (AmE), / ˈɛvɹi səʊ ˈɒfən / (BrE)
occasionally; at intervals, but not frequently or regularly.

Examples

Every so often, Andy would show up with fresh bruises.

Synset Examples

every so often she visits her father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - at infrequent or irregular intervals
  • Antonym: frequently - happening often or regularly
