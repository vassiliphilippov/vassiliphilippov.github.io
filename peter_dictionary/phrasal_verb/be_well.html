be well

1. VERB

/ bi wɛl / (AmE), / bi wɛl / (BrE)
to be in a healthy or good physical condition; to feel healthy and not ill

Examples

She hoped they were well.

Synset Examples

She has not been well lately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feel well - to feel healthy or not ill
  • Antonym: be ill - to be sick or unwell
