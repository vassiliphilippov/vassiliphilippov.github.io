push through

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ θru / (AmE), / pʊʃ θruː / (BrE)
To break out or emerge forcefully, especially after overcoming resistance or obstruction.

Examples

Pat pushed through first.

Synset Examples

The tooth erupted and had to be extracted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erupt - to burst forth or break out suddenly
