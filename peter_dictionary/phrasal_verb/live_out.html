live out

1. VERB

/ lɪv aʊt / (AmE), / lɪv aʊt / (BrE)
to continue to live until the end of a particular period or one's life

Examples

He had come because he could not live out his life feeling that he had been a coward.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: die - to stop living; to cease to exist

2. VERB

/ lɪv aʊt / (AmE), / lɪv aʊt / (BrE)
to spend the remainder or the last period of one's life in a particular way or place; to live until the end of one's life

Examples

To my knowledge, he lived out the rest of his days drinking his food through a straw.
