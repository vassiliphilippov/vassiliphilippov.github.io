tire out

1. VERB

/ taɪɚ aʊt / (AmE), / taɪə aʊt / (BrE)
To make someone or oneself very tired; to exhaust through activity or effort.

Examples

When they got home at midnight she was tired out.

Synset Examples

We wore ourselves out on this hike

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhaust - to make someone extremely tired; to drain of energy
  • Antonym: refresh - to make someone less tired and more energetic
