close down

1. VERB

/ kloʊz daʊn / (AmE), / kləʊz daʊn / (BrE)
(of a business or organization) to cease operations, or to cause something to stop functioning permanently.

Examples

If this keeps up, you'll put me **out of business**.

Synset Examples

The owners decided to move and to close the factory
My business closes every night at 8 P.M.
close up the shop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shut down - to stop operating, especially permanently
