hinge on

1. VERB

/ hɪndʒ ɑn / (AmE), / hɪndʒ ɒn / (BrE)
to depend entirely on or be determined by something

Examples

Unfortunately, the whole picture hinges on this romance, at the expense of everything else.

Synset Examples

The outcomes rides on the results of the election
Your grade will depends on your homework

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depend on - to rely on or be controlled by something
  • Synonym: rest on - to be based on or determined by

Etymology

From 'hinge' (the movable joint), metaphorically referring to the way an outcome turns or pivots on something.
