get about

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to move around or travel from place to place, especially despite difficulties or without a specific means of transport.

Examples

she has had many major operations, and now gets about in a limited way on braces and crutches.

Synset Examples

How does she get around without a car?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get around - move from place to place; travel or circulate
