write up

1. VERB

/ ˈraɪt ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to report or bring someone or something to public attention in writing, especially in a formal or official manner, often with praise or criticism

Examples

"I should n't like to have to write you up for insubordination as well as dereliction of duty ".

Synset Examples

The New York Times wrote him up last week
Did your boss write you up?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: report - to give a spoken or written account of something
