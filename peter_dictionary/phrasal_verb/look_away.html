look away

1. VERB

/ lʊk əˈweɪ / (AmE), / lʊk əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to turn your eyes in another direction so as not to see something

Examples

His sandy hair was already beginning to thin and recede at the sides, and Abel looked quickly away.
I looked away.

Synset Examples

She looked away when the nurse pricked her arm with the needle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avert - to turn away (one's eyes or thoughts)
  • Antonym: stare - to look fixedly

2. VERB

/ lʊk əˈweɪ / (AmE), / lʊk əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to turn your eyes to another direction so that you are not looking at something or someone

Examples

Don't look anybody in the eye, but don't look away either.

Synset Examples

She looked away when the nurse pricked her arm with the needle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avert one's eyes - to turn one's eyes away from something
