stop dead

1. VERB

/ stɑp dɛd / (AmE), / stɒp dɛd / (BrE)
To come to a complete and sudden stop, especially due to surprise, shock, or fear.

Examples

Our man stopped dead and glowered at Felix.

Synset Examples

When he saw the police car he froze

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freeze - to stop moving or become motionless from shock or fear
