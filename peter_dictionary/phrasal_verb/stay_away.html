stay away

1. VERB

/ steɪ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / steɪ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to avoid going near or being involved with someone or something; to keep one's distance from

Examples

A lot of people may stay away.

Synset Examples

Keep your hands off my wife!
Keep your distance from this man--he is dangerous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avoid - to keep away from or prevent from happening
  • Antonym: approach - to come near or nearer to something
