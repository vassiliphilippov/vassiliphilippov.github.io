frown upon

1. VERB

/ fraʊn əˈpɑn / (AmE), / fraʊn əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to disapprove of something; to regard something with disapproval

Examples

It is like medical schools in India where, in that fairy land of religious inhibition, the dissection of dead bodies is frowned upon.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: approve of - to regard favorably or with approval
