set down

1. VERB

/ sɛt daʊn / (AmE), / sɛt daʊn / (BrE)
to write something down or record it in writing

Examples

Steinberg seems to have gone directly back to the score, discounting tradition, and has built his performance on the intention to reproduce as faithfully as possible exactly what Brahms set down on paper.
In particular he sought the gentle, sweet-faced nuns, with head coverings and veils coming to the middle of their foreheads, remembering their expressions until he reached home and set them down on paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: write down - to record in writing

2. VERB

/ set daʊn / (AmE), / set daʊn / (BrE)
to place or position something or someone in a particular location

Examples

Why should a white hotel be set down in the center of Black Bottom?

Synset Examples

The hotel was set down at the bottom of the valley

Related Terms

  • Synonym: place - to put something in a particular spot
  • Synonym: lay down - to put something down gently, especially horizontally

3. VERB

/ sɛt ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / sɛt ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
(of an aircraft or vehicle) to bring to the ground or to a halt; to land.

Examples

The first few days Bob Fogg set his plane down on Towne field back of the State House when the wind was right, and used Wilson flat above Barre when it was n't.

Synset Examples

The bird landed on the highest branch
The plane landed in Istanbul

Related Terms

  • Synonym: land - (of an aircraft or bird) to come down to the ground

4. VERB

/ sɛt daʊn / (AmE), / sɛt daʊn / (BrE)
to put something or someone in a particular position, especially by placing it carefully on a surface

Examples

He set his bundle down.

Synset Examples

set down your bags here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: put down - to place something on a surface

5. VERB

/ sɛt daʊn / (AmE), / sɛt daʊn / (BrE)
to put or place something in a particular position or location, especially carefully or deliberately

Examples

Set 'em **down**.

Synset Examples

The hotel was set down at the bottom of the valley

Related Terms

  • Synonym: put down - to place something on a surface
