do drugs

1. VERB

/ duː drʌɡz / (AmE), / duː drʌɡz / (BrE)
to take illegal or recreational drugs

Examples

Cause of death has been attributed to a violent allergic reaction... ... although authorities have not ruled out the possibility of drug use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take drugs - to consume drugs, especially recreational or illegal drugs
  • Synonym: use drugs - to consume recreational or illegal substances
