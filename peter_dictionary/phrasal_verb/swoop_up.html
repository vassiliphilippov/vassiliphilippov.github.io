swoop up

1. VERB

/ swup ʌp / (AmE), / swuːp ʌp / (BrE)
to quickly seize or pick up something with a sweeping or sudden motion

Examples

Maude swooped up the cup and hiked up her top hoop as if about to take off with a racing start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pick up - to lift or take something up from a surface
  • Synonym: grab - to take or seize suddenly or quickly
