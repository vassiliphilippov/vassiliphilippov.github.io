shut off

1. VERB

/ ʃʌt ɔf / (AmE), / ʃʌt ɒf / (BrE)
to stop the flow or operation of something, especially by turning a knob or switch

Examples

Then I shut off the device again.

Synset Examples

shut off the gas when you leave for a vacation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn off - to stop the flow of electricity, gas, etc. to something; to stop a device working
  • Antonym: shut on - to start the flow or operation of something; to activate a device
