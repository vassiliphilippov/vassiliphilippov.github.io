get along with

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt əˈlɔŋ wɪð / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈlɒŋ wɪð / (BrE)
to have a friendly or harmonious relationship with someone

Examples

Congress is full of politicians, and if you want to get along with them, you have to be politic.

Synset Examples

My boss and I get along very well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get on with - to have a friendly or harmonious relationship with someone
