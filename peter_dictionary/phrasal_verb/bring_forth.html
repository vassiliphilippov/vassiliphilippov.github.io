bring forth

1. VERB

/ brɪŋ fɔrθ / (AmE), / brɪŋ fɔːθ / (BrE)
to produce or give birth to (offspring); to cause something to originate, emerge, or come into existence

Examples

"To be creative is to have the ability to cause to exist - to produce where nothing was before - to bring forth an original production of human intelligence or power ".

Synset Examples

Abraham begot Isaac
Men often father children but don't recognize them

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beget - to father or produce (offspring); to cause to exist
  • Synonym: father - to be the male parent of (a child)
  • Synonym: give birth to - to deliver a baby; to produce (offspring)
