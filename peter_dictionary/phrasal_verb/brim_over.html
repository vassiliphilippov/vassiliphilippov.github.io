brim over

1. VERB

/ brɪm ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / brɪm ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to flow over the edge of a container; to overflow.

Examples

The fountain had brimmed over, the cannon were wet, the soldiers' monument glistened.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overflow - to flow over the limits of something
