take up arms

1. VERB

/ teɪk ʌp ɑrmz / (AmE), / teɪk ʌp ɑːmz / (BrE)
to begin fighting, especially with weapons, often as part of a conflict or war

Examples

But people differ in their religious beliefs on scores of doctrines, without taking up arms against those who disagree with them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go to war - to engage in armed conflict
