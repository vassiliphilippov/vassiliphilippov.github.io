get worse

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wɝs / (AmE), / ɡɛt wɜːs / (BrE)
(of a condition, situation, or health) to become more severe, unpleasant, or difficult than before.

Examples

After his pains got worse, Tom decided to see a real doctor, from whom he learned he was suffering from cancer of the lung.

Synset Examples

he relapsed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: improve - to become better
  • Synonym: deteriorate - to become progressively worse
