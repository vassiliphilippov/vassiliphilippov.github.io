ask out

1. VERB

/ æsk aʊt / (AmE), / ɑːsk aʊt / (BrE)
to invite someone to go on a date with you

Examples

All this was unknown to me, and yet I had dared to ask her out for the most important night of the year!

Synset Examples

Has he asked you out yet?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invite - to ask someone to participate in something
