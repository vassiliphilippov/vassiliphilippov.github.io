turn up

1. VERB

/ tɝn ʌp / (AmE), / tɜːn ʌp / (BrE)
to discover or find something by searching or examination, especially unexpectedly

Examples

A refuse bin at the Dumont turned up a florist's box - a very long box for very long stemmed flowers.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: find - to discover or obtain something through searching
  • Antonym: lose - to be unable to find something; to misplace
