ride herd

1. VERB

/ raɪd hɜrd / (AmE), / raɪd hɜːd / (BrE)
to drive or watch over animals, such as cattle or horses, while riding a horse alongside them

Examples

Two of the new hands, a Mexican named Jose Amado and a kid known only as Laredo, were picked for the first trick of riding night herd.

Synset Examples

Joe was riding herd during the day
