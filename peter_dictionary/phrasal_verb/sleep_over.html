sleep over

1. VERB

/ sliːp ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / sliːp ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to stay overnight at someone else's home or at a place other than one's own residence

Examples

You decided to spend the night at Michael Corleone'’s house in Tahoe...

Synset Examples

The boy's friends were allowed to sleep over after the birthday party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stay over - to remain overnight, usually at someone else's house
