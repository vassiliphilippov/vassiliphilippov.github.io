think twice

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk twaɪs / (AmE), / θɪŋk twaɪs / (BrE)
to consider something carefully before making a decision or taking action, especially to avoid an unwise choice

Examples

I 'm telling you, if these corpses ever knew the trouble they put us to, they 'd think twice before letting themselves get knocked off ".

Synset Examples

Think twice before you have a child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hesitate - pause before doing something, especially due to uncertainty
  • Antonym: act impulsively - act without forethought or consideration
