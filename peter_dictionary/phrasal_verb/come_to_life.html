come to life

1. VERB

/ kʌm tə laɪf / (AmE), / kʌm tə laɪf / (BrE)
to begin to exist, to become active, interesting, or lively; to start showing signs of life or activity

Examples

All sorts of plans come to life.
In vain his mind groped to reassemble the bones of the relationships he had sought so desperately, but they would not come to life.

Synset Examples

All these flowers come to life when the rains come

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spring to life - to suddenly become lively or active
