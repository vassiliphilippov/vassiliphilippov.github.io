sit in

1. VERB

/ sɪt ɪn / (AmE), / sɪt ɪn / (BrE)
to attend a meeting, class, or event as an invited observer or visitor, rather than as a participant.

Examples

Tom isn'’t gonna sit in with us, Johnny.

Synset Examples

Can I sit in on your Intermediate Hittite class?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attend - be present at an event or meeting
  • Antonym: participate - take part in something, as a full member
