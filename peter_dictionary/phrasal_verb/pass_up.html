pass up

1. VERB

/ pæs ʌp / (AmE), / pɑːs ʌp / (BrE)
to refuse or decline to accept something, especially an opportunity or offer

Examples

My advice, if you live long enough to continue your vocation, is that the next time you 're attracted by the exotic, pass it up - it's nothing but a headache.

Synset Examples

He refused my offer of hospitality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refuse - to not accept or agree to something
  • Antonym: accept - to agree to take or receive something
