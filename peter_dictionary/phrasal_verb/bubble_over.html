bubble over

1. VERB

/ ˈbʌbəl ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌbl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a person) to be so full of a particular feeling that it is expressed outwards in a lively or enthusiastic way.

Examples

Pat meanwhile was bubbling over with sentiment.

Synset Examples

The children bubbled over with joy
My boss was bubbling over with anger

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overflow - to be so full that the contents go over the sides or limits
