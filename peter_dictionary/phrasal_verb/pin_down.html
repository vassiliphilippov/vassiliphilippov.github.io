pin down

1. VERB

/ pɪn daʊn / (AmE), / pɪn daʊn / (BrE)
to define, describe, or identify something clearly and precisely

Examples

High-level abstractions are always difficult to pin down with precision.

Synset Examples

I cannot narrow down the rules for this game

Related Terms

  • Synonym: define - state or describe exactly the nature, scope, or meaning of something
  • Synonym: specify - identify clearly and definitely
  • Antonym: vague - not clearly expressed or defined
