wipe up

1. VERB

/ waɪp ʌp / (AmE), / waɪp ʌp / (BrE)
To clean a surface by wiping it, especially to remove a liquid or spilled substance.

Examples

And then you wiped up some grease ".

Synset Examples

Mop the hallway now
He mopped her forehead with a towel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mop up - to remove a liquid by wiping or absorbing it
