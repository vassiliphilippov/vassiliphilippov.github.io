drum out

1. VERB

/ drʌm aʊt / (AmE), / drʌm aʊt / (BrE)
to dismiss someone from a position or organization, especially in a formal or humiliating way

Examples

It concerned an officer who had been disgraced and drummed out.

Synset Examples

The chairman was ousted after he misappropriated funds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oust - to remove someone from a position or office

Etymology

From the tradition of using drums to ceremonially expel someone from a military unit.
