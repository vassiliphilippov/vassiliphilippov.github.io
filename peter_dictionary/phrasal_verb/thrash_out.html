thrash out

1. VERB

/ ˈθræʃ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈθræʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to discuss a problem or issue thoroughly and energetically in order to reach an agreement or solution

Examples

Besides, agreements "in principle ", with the petty details to be thrashed out later, were commonplace in diplomatic history.

Synset Examples

The leaders of the various Middle Eastern countries are trying to hammer out a peace agreement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hammer out - discuss and negotiate to reach an agreement
