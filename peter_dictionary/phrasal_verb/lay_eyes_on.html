lay eyes on

1. VERB

/ leɪ aɪz ɑn / (AmE), / leɪ aɪz ɒn / (BrE)
to see or notice someone or something, especially for the first time

Examples

The next morning, right about the time Raquel was spilling her little secret, a man nobody ever laid eyes on before strode into the Maine National Bank.

Synset Examples

behold Christ!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: behold - see or observe (something or someone)
  • Synonym: set eyes on - to see or notice (someone or something) for the first time
