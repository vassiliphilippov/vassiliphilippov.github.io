sponge off

1. VERB

/ spʌndʒ ɑf / (AmE), / spʌndʒ ɒf / (BrE)
to clean or wipe (a surface) by rubbing with a damp sponge

Examples

The large jar was brushed with Creek-Turn green toner and sponged off.
Outside was finished with Creek-Turn brown toner brushed on and sponged off to give antique finish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wipe down - to clean a surface with a sponge or cloth
