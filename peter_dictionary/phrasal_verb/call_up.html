call up

1. VERB

/ kɔl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
To bring something to attention or consideration, especially in a formal or official setting.

Examples

"Unfortunately ", says Chief Postal Inspector David H. Stephens, who has prosecuted many device quacks, "the ghouls who trade on the hopes of the desperately ill often cannot be successfully prosecuted because the patients who are the chief witnesses die before the case is called up in court ".
His very honest act called up the recent talk I had with another minister, a modest Methodist, who said: "I feel so deeply blessed by God when I can give a message of love and comfort to other men, and I would have it no other way: and it is unworthy to think of self.

Synset Examples

The case was called up in court

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bring up - to mention or introduce a topic for discussion

2. VERB

/ kɔl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
to bring a memory, thought, or image into one's mind; to recall or remember something

Examples

Nor could he call up memory-pictures of close friends or relatives.

Synset Examples

I can't remember saying any such thing
I can't think what her last name was
can you remember her phone number?
Do you remember that he once loved you?
call up memories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recall - to remember or recollect; to bring a memory to mind
  • Antonym: forget - to not remember

3. VERB

/ kɑl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
to make a telephone call to someone; to try to communicate with someone by telephone

Examples

I did have the decency to call up Thelma and tell her I 'd met old friends and would be home late.

Synset Examples

I tried to call you all night
Take two aspirin and call me in the morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone - to contact by telephone
  • Synonym: ring up - to call someone by telephone (chiefly British)

4. VERB

/ kɔl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
to contact or attempt to contact someone by telephone

Examples

He said to give him a call when the party started.

Synset Examples

I tried to call you all night
Take two aspirin and call me in the morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone - to contact someone by telephone
  • Synonym: ring up - to call someone by telephone (British English)
