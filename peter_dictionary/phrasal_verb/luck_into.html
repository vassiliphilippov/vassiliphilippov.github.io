luck into

1. VERB

/ lʌk ˈɪntu / (AmE), / lʌk ˈɪntʃuː / (BrE)
to obtain or achieve something by chance or without effort

Examples

He got in the oil business out at Odessa and lucked into some money ".

Synset Examples

She entered upon the estate of her rich relatives

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stumble upon - to find or discover something by accident
