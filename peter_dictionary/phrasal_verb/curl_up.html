curl up

1. VERB

/ kɝl ʌp / (AmE), / kɜːl ʌp / (BrE)
to arrange your body into a curved or rounded shape, often by drawing your knees up to your chest; to lie or sit with your arms and legs close to your body

Examples

They curl up and die at the sight of something strange and alien - like a spaceship ".

Synset Examples

She curled farther down under the covers
She fell and drew in

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coil - to wind into a spiral or ring shape
