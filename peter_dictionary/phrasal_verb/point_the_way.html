point the way

1. VERB

/ pɔɪnt ðə weɪ / (AmE), / pɔɪnt ðə weɪ / (BrE)
to indicate the correct direction, path, or course of action, either literally or figuratively

Examples

This may point the way toward international stabilization agreements in other products.
Called the Marmara Gazinosu, it is on the third floor, with signs pointing the way there, and has a terrace overlooking the Sea of Marmara.

Synset Examples

The sign pointed the way to London

Related Terms

  • Synonym: show the way - to guide or direct someone to a place or towards a solution
