mound over

1. VERB

/ maʊnd ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / maʊnd ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to cover or heap up with earth or other material in the form of a mound

Examples

They form effective fallout shelters when mounded over with earth, as shown in figure 10.

Synset Examples

The huts can be mounded over to form shelters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cover - to put something over or on top of
