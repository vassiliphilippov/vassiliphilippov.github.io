talk shop

1. VERB

/ tɔk ʃɑp / (AmE), / tɔːk ʃɒp / (BrE)
to discuss work-related matters, especially in social situations where such conversation is seen as inappropriate or boring to others

Examples

Teachers - men who wore brown suits and had gray hair and pleasant smiles - came to their table to talk shop and to be introduced to Scotty and Rachel.

Synset Examples

As soon as they met, the linguists started to talk shop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talk business - to discuss business matters; a synonym for 'talk shop'
