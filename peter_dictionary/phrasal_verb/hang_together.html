hang together

1. VERB

/ hæŋ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / hæŋ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
(of ideas, arguments, events, etc.) to be logically consistent or connected with each other.

Examples

He thought: Only in my heart can I make the world hang together.

Synset Examples

In my heart I can make the world hang together

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cohere - to form a unified whole, be logically connected
  • Synonym: make sense - to be comprehensible or logical
