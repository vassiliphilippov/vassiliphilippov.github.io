taken up

1. ADJ

/ ˈteɪkən ʌp / (AmE), / ˈteɪkən ʌp / (BrE)
Deeply absorbed in or preoccupied with something, often to the exclusion of other things.

Examples

She was taken up in worry for the reckless old man.

Synset Examples

became more and more haunted by the stupid riddle
was absolutely obsessed with the girl
got no help from his wife who was preoccupied with the children
he was taken up in worry for the old woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preoccupied - completely absorbed in one's own thoughts or concerns
  • Synonym: engrossed - entirely absorbed or occupied by something
  • Synonym: obsessed - having persistent, intrusive thoughts about something
