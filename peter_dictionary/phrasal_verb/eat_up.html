eat up

1. VERB

/ it ʌp / (AmE), / iːt ʌp / (BrE)
to finish eating all the food that is served or available, often leaving nothing behind

Examples

Read the next two verses: "When the wicked, even mine enemies and my foes, came upon me to eat up my flesh, they stumbled and fell.
The enemies at his gate, threatening to eat up his flesh, were nothing compared with the enemy of sin within his own soul.

Synset Examples

She polished off the remaining potatoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish - to bring something to an end or completion
  • Synonym: polish off - to quickly finish something, especially food
