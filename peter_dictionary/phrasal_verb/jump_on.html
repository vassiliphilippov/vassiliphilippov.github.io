jump on

1. VERB

/ dʒʌmp ɑn / (AmE), / dʒʌmp ɒn / (BrE)
to get up onto the back or surface of something by jumping

Examples

He went for the bed, jumped on it, and struck where he could, repeatedly.

Synset Examples

mount a horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mount - to get on the back of (e.g., horse, bicycle)
