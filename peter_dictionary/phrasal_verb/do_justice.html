do justice

1. VERB

/ du ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / də ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
to represent or portray someone or something fairly, accurately, or in a way that reveals their true value or quality

Examples

This brief resume hardly does the book justice, but I heartily recommend it to all those who are engages with the major problems of our time.

Synset Examples

This photograph does not do her justice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: do justice to - to treat or represent something as it deserves
