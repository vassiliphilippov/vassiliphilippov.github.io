follow suit

1. VERB

/ ˈfɑloʊ ˈsuːt / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ ˈsuːt / (BrE)
to do the same thing as someone else has just done; in card games, to play a card of the same suit as the previous card played.

Examples

Player immediately proceeded to follow suit.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imitate - to copy or do the same thing as someone else
  • Antonym: lead - in card games, to play the first card in a round (opposite action in context)
