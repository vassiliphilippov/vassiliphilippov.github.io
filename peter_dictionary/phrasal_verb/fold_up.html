fold up

1. VERB

/ foʊld ʌp / (AmE), / fəʊld ʌp / (BrE)
To bend or lay (something) so that one part covers another part, typically to make it smaller or more compact.

Examples

Thayer folded it up and offered a drink.

Synset Examples

fold up the newspaper
turn up your collar

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unfold - to open something that has been folded so that it becomes flat
