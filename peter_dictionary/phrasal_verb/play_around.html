play around

1. VERB

/ pleɪ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / pleɪ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
(of a married person) to have sexual relationships with someone other than one's spouse; to commit adultery.

Examples

Try to find out how happy he is with his wife, whether he plays around with women.

Synset Examples

he plays around a lot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheat - to have a sexual relationship with someone other than your partner
  • Antonym: be faithful - to remain loyal to one's spouse; not to have extramarital affairs
