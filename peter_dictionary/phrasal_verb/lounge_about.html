lounge about

1. VERB

/ laʊndʒ əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / laʊndʒ əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to spend time lazily or idly, without doing much activity

Examples

They got tin cups of coffee from the big pot on the coosie's fire, rolled and lighted brown paper cigarettes, lounged about.

Synset Examples

Her son is just bumming around all day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idle - to spend time doing nothing; not working or active
  • Synonym: laze - to relax and do very little
  • Antonym: work - to engage in physical or mental activity
