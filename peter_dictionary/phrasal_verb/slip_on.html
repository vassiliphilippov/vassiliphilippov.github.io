slip on

1. VERB

/ slɪp ɑn / (AmE), / slɪp ɒn / (BrE)
to put on (an item of clothing or footwear) quickly or with little effort

Examples

There I got my Colt Special and shoulder harness, slipped my coat on, and went back into the front room.

Synset Examples

slip into something more comfortable after work
slip on one's shoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: put on - to place clothing or something on one's body
