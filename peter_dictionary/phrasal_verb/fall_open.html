fall open

1. VERB

/ fɔl ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
(of a mouth, door, etc.) to open suddenly and without intention, often from surprise or shock.

Examples

His mouth fell open.

Synset Examples

His mouth dropped open
Her jaw dropped

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drop open - to open suddenly or without control
