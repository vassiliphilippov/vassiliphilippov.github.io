/ meɪk sɛns / (AmE), / meɪk sɛns / (BrE)

  • Synonym: be logical - to conform to logic or sound reasoning

2. VERB

/ meɪk sɛns / (AmE), / meɪk sens / (BrE)
To be reasonable, logical, or easy to understand.

Examples

All I want is to be back where things make sense, where I won't have to be afraid all the time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: add up - to be logical or make sense
  • Antonym: make no sense - to be illogical or not understandable
