bust up

1. VERB

/ bʌst ʌp / (AmE), / bʌst ʌp / (BrE)
to smash or break something forcefully

Examples

Hadley busted his head **up** pretty good.

Synset Examples

The kid busted up the car

Related Terms

  • Synonym: break up - to separate into pieces; to break or dismantle
  • Degree Higher: destroy - to damage something so badly that it no longer exists or cannot be repaired
