boost up

1. VERB

/ bust ʌp / (AmE), / buːst ʌp / (BrE)
to lift or push someone or something upwards, especially with the hands to help them reach a higher position

Examples

Arlene was boosting them up when the policeman came by.

Synset Examples

She pushed up her children when the policeman came by

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lift up - to raise something or someone to a higher position
  • Antonym: pull down - to move something to a lower position
