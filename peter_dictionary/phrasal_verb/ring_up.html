ring up

1. VERB

/ rɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / rɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
to record the sale of goods and the amount on a cash register or electronic point of sale

Examples

Katya Roslev, who would be Katharine Ross so very soon now, rang up her first sale of the day and counted back the change.

Synset Examples

Sally rang up Eve's purchase of tomatoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: check out - to pay for items at a store and have them recorded for purchase
