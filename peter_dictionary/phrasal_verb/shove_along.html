shove along

1. VERB

/ ʃʌv əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / ʃʌv əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
(informal) To leave a place, especially in a brisk or abrupt manner.

Examples

"I 'll shove along home ".

Synset Examples

shove off!

Blow now!

Related Terms
