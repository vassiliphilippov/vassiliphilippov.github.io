dry out

1. VERB

/ draɪ aʊt / (AmE), / draɪ aʊt / (BrE)
(of a substance or object) to become dry or drier, especially after being wet; to lose moisture.

Examples

It's dryin' myself out that does it ".
"He's got this idea about drying out ".

Synset Examples

The laundry dries in the sun
