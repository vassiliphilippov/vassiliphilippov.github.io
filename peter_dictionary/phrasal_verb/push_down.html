push down

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ daʊn / (AmE), / pʊʃ daʊn / (BrE)
to press or apply force to something so that it moves downward

Examples

The cars must have had their gas pedals pushed down to the floor boards.

Synset Examples

The policeman downed the heavily armed suspect
The mugger knocked down the old lady after she refused to hand over her wallet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depress - to press something down, especially a button or pedal
