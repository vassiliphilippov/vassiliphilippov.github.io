strike back

1. VERB

/ straɪk bæk / (AmE), / straɪk bak / (BrE)
to respond to an attack or criticism by attacking or criticizing in return; to retaliate.

Examples

They hit us, so we hit them **back**.

Synset Examples

The Empire strikes back
The Giants struck back and won the opener
The Israeli army retaliated for the Hamas bombing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retaliate - to make an attack or assault in return for a similar attack
  • Antonym: turn the other cheek - to refrain from retaliating after being attacked or insulted
