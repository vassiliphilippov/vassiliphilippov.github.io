keep going

1. VERB

/ kip ˈɡoʊ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / kiːp ˈɡəʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
to continue doing something without stopping or being interrupted

Examples

So you do what you can to keep going.

Synset Examples

The disease will run on unchecked
The party kept going until 4 A.M.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carry on - to continue; to persist in an activity
  • Antonym: stop - to cease doing something; to come to an end
