choose up

1. VERB

/ tʃuz ʌp / (AmE), / tʃuːz ʌp / (BrE)
to form opposing sides or teams, as for a game or contest

Examples

But the Legislature should be granted the opportunity to complete its work before choosing up sides for the race.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pick teams - to select members for teams, especially for a game
