foul out

1. VERB

/ faʊl aʊt / (AmE), / faʊl aʊt / (BrE)
(in baseball) to be put out by hitting a foul ball that is caught by a fielder before it touches the ground.

Examples

Momentarily the Reds were back in the ball game, trailing only 6 - 3, but the drive fizzled when John Edwards fouled out with men on second and third and two out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pop out - (baseball) to be out by hitting a short, high fly ball caught by a fielder
