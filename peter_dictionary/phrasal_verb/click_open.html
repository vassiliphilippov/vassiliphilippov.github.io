click open

1. VERB

/ klɪk ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / klɪk ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
to open something so that it makes a clicking sound, or by causing a click, especially using a device or a key

Examples

It is the business of cosmologists, the scientists who study the nature and structure of the universe, to try to solve the great cosmic mysteries by using keys that have clicked open other doors.

Synset Examples

These keys have clicked open many doors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: open - to move (something) so that it is no longer closed or covered
