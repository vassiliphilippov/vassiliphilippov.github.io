patch up

1. VERB

/ pætʃ ʌp / (AmE), / pætʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to repair or mend something by putting a patch on it

Examples

I figger it's probl' y a sixty-five mile walk, and I c' n maybe get this spring patched up in a couple of hours ".

Synset Examples

patch a hole

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repair - to restore something that is damaged, faulty, or worn to a good condition
  • Synonym: fix - to mend or repair something

2. VERB

/ pætʃ ʌp / (AmE), / pætʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to restore friendly relations or settle a disagreement after a conflict or argument

Examples

No more advice on how to patch things **up**.

Synset Examples

After some discussion we finally made up

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make up - to become friends again after a quarrel
  • Antonym: fall out - to have a disagreement or argument resulting in separation
