get over

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to recover from an illness, injury, or difficult experience and return to a normal state of health or well-being

Examples

A Michigan soldier stationed in Georgia wrote in 1864: "I expect to be tough as a knott as soon as I get over the Georgia Shitts ".

Synset Examples

He got well fast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recover - regain health or strength after illness or difficulty

2. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to cross or travel across something, especially an obstacle, surface, or area

Examples

Thereupon Hwang Pah said: "If I knew thou art an Arhat, I would have doubled you up before thou got over there "!
"But Fogg ", they countered, "we can n't get over there.
Once we get over the mountains others will come along.
"The road's washed badly ", said Dill, "but there's a trail you can get over with a horse.

Synset Examples

The caravan covered almost 100 miles each day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cross - to go across from one side to the other
  • Synonym: traverse - to travel across or through

3. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
To finish or complete a necessary but unpleasant task, often as quickly as possible.

Examples

Let's get this **over with**.

Synset Examples

Let's get this job over with
It's a question of getting over an unpleasant task

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish - to bring something to an end

4. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to recover from or deal successfully with a difficulty, illness, or emotional shock; to overcome

Examples

I can't get over the way your face looks.

Synset Examples

He overcame his shyness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overcome - to succeed in dealing with or controlling (a problem or feeling)
  • Synonym: move on - to stop focusing on something that is upsetting and start focusing on other things

5. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to recover from an illness or begin to feel better after being unwell

Examples

That wasn't easy to get over!

Synset Examples

He got well fast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recover - to return to a normal state of health after an illness or injury
