push around

1. VERB

/ pʊʃ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / pʊʃ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to treat someone in a bossy, domineering, or bullying manner; to give orders to someone in an unpleasant way

Examples

Another speaker, William H. Draper, Jr., former Under Secretary of the Army and now with the Palo Alto venture capital firm of Draper, Gaither + Anderson, urged the U. S. to "throw down the gauntlet of battle to communism and tell Moscow bluntly we won n't be pushed arouny any more ".

Synset Examples

Her big brother always bullied her when she was young

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bully - to intimidate or mistreat someone weaker
  • Synonym: order about - to tell someone what to do in a domineering way
