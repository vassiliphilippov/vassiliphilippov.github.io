be born

1. VERB

/ bi bɔrn / (AmE), / bi bɔːn / (BrE)
to come into existence through birth; to be given life when emerging from the body of a parent or from an egg

Examples

Shea was born in Manhattan.
But no art at all was born of the art effort in the early movies.
