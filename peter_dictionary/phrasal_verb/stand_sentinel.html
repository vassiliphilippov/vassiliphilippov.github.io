stand sentinel

1. VERB

/ stænd ˈsɛntənəl / (AmE), / stænd ˈsɛntɪnəl / (BrE)
to keep watch or guard over something in order to protect it

Examples

"We ourselves must stand sentinel ".

Synset Examples

We must stand sentinel to protect ourselves
The jewels over which they kept guard were stolen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guard - to protect or watch over something
  • Synonym: watch over - to keep a protective vigil
  • Antonym: stand down - to leave or relax guard duty

Etymology

Sentinel from French sentinelle, meaning 'watchman, guard'.
