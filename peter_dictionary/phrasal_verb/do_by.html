do by

1. VERB

/ ˈduː baɪ / (AmE), / ˈduː baɪ / (BrE)
to treat or behave toward someone in a particular way

Examples

Amy had to be safe, had to come back to them - if only to reap that share of life's experiences that were her due, if only to give her parents another chance to do better by her.

Synset Examples

Do right by her
Treat him with caution, please
Handle the press reporters gently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treat - to behave towards or deal with in a particular way
  • Synonym: handle - to manage, deal with, or behave toward someone
