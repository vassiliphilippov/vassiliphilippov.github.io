check into

1. VERB

/ tʃɛk ˈɪntu / (AmE), / tʃɛk ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to investigate or examine something carefully in order to find out the facts or assess its condition

Examples

If the house is not wired adequately for electricity or if plumbing or a central heating system must be installed, check into the cost of making these improvements.

Synset Examples

check the brakes
Check out the engine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: investigate - to carry out a systematic or formal inquiry to discover and examine facts
  • Synonym: look into - to try to discover the facts about something; investigate
  • Antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to something
