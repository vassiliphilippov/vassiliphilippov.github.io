drink up

1. VERB

/ drɪŋk ʌp / (AmE), / drɪŋk ʌp / (BrE)
to finish drinking all the liquid in a glass or cup

Examples

Sometimes he did n't seem to because he hid them so well he could n't find them himself or because he looked and did n't find anything and figured he had n't hid one after all or had drunk it up.

Synset Examples

drink up--there's more wine coming

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish - bring to an end; complete

2. VERB

/ drɪŋk ʌp / (AmE), / drɪŋk ʌp / (BrE)
to drink all of what is in your glass, cup, etc.; to finish a drink completely

Examples

Drink up while it's cold, ladies.

Synset Examples

drink up--there's more wine coming

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: drink - to take liquid into the mouth and swallow it
