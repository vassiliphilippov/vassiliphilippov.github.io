waste one's time

1. VERB

/ weɪst wʌnz taɪm / (AmE), / weɪst wʌnz taɪm / (BrE)
to spend time doing nothing productive; to be lazy or idle

Examples

I don't waste time on losers, Tommy.

Synset Examples

Her son is just bumming around all day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idle - spend time doing nothing; avoid work
  • Synonym: bum around - to spend time lazily or aimlessly; be idle
  • Antonym: work - to do tasks, especially for a job or duty
