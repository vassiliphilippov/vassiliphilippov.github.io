press on

1. VERB

/ prɛs ɑn / (AmE), / prɛs ɒn / (BrE)
to continue moving forward or to persist in an activity, especially despite difficulties.

Examples

New Jersey folk need not be told of the builder's march to the sea, for in a single generation he has parceled and populated miles of our shoreline and presses on to develop the few open spaces that remain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persevere - to continue trying to achieve something despite difficulties
  • Antonym: give up - to stop trying or abandon an effort
