climb on

1. VERB

/ klaɪm ɑn / (AmE), / klaɪm ɒn / (BrE)
to get onto the back or top of something by climbing

Examples

Those who are too weak, should climb on the chair and, starting at the top of the chin, let themselves slowly down.

Synset Examples

mount a horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mount - to get up on something, especially a horse or bicycle
