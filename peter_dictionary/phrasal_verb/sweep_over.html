sweep over

1. VERB

/ swip ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / swiːp ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a strong emotion or feeling) to suddenly affect someone powerfully or completely.

Examples

Sometimes a burst of aggressiveness will sweep over a man - or his wife - because he or she feels age creeping up.
A terrible, numbing sense of futility swept over him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overwhelm - to have a strong emotional effect on someone
