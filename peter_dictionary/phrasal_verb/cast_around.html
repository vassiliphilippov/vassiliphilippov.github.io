cast around

1. VERB

/ kæst əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / kɑːst əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to search anxiously or hurriedly for something, especially a solution, idea, or explanation

Examples

Again, I at first misconstrued this disconcertingly intense communication, and I quickly cast through my mind to account for her being able to speak, with such utter conviction, of an opinion held by my father, now several years deceased.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seek - to try to find or obtain something
  • Synonym: cast about - to search anxiously in various directions
