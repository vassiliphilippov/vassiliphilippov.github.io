fight off

1. VERB

/ faɪt ɑf / (AmE), / faɪt ɒf / (BrE)
to succeed in repelling or driving away someone or something, especially an attacker or threat

Examples

Sometimes he was able to fight 'em **off**.

Synset Examples

repel the attacker
fight off the onslaught
rebuff the attack

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repel - to drive back or keep something away
  • Synonym: rebuff - to reject or drive back forcefully
