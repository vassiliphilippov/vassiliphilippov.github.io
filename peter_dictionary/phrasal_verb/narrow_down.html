narrow down

1. VERB

/ ˈnæroʊ daʊn / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ daʊn / (BrE)
To make something more limited or specific by removing possibilities.

Examples

He narrowed the shape down to two: either a Zero or a U. S. Navy type aircraft.

Synset Examples

I cannot narrow down the rules for this game

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specify - to state something clearly and exactly
  • Antonym: broaden - to make something wider or more general
