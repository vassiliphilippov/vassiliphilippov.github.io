whoop it up

1. VERB

/ wup ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / wuːp ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
To celebrate very noisily and enthusiastically, often with drinking and revelry.

Examples

A posse is formed and pursues the robbers, who, having made their escape, are whooping it up with some wild, wild women in a honky-tonk hide-out.

Synset Examples

The members of the wedding party made merry all night
Let's whoop it up--the boss is gone!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make merry - to celebrate and enjoy oneself noisily
  • Synonym: revel - to take great pleasure in celebrating or partying
  • Antonym: take it easy - to relax and refrain from excitement
