pull through

1. VERB

/ pʊl θru / (AmE), / pʊl θruː / (BrE)
to recover or survive after a serious illness, difficulty, or adversity

Examples

Looks like he'll pull through.

Synset Examples

He survived the cancer against all odds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: survive - to continue to live or exist, especially in spite of danger or hardship
  • Antonym: succumb - to fail to resist (pressure, temptation, or some other negative force); to die from
