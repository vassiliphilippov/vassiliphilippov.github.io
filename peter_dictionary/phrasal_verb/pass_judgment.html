pass judgment

1. VERB

/ pæs ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (AmE), / pɑːs ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (BrE)
To express or form an opinion, especially a critical or authoritative one, about someone or something.

Examples

And passing judgment on how I run my family.

Synset Examples

I cannot judge some works of modern art
How do you evaluate this grant proposal?
We shouldn't pass judgment on other people

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judge - form an opinion or conclusion about
  • Synonym: evaluate - assess or determine the value or quality of
