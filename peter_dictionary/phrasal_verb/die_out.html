die out

1. VERB

/ daɪ aʊt / (AmE), / daɪ aʊt / (BrE)
(of a species, group, custom, etc.) to become extinct or gradually cease to exist.

Examples

As a result, the proportion of males (which leave the nest) increases, and eventually the old colony will die out completely.
The red glow from the cove had died out of the sky.

Synset Examples

Dinosaurs died out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extinct - no longer in existence; died out
  • Antonym: survive - to continue to exist
