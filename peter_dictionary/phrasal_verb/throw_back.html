throw back

1. VERB

/ θroʊ bæk / (AmE), / θrəʊ bæk / (BrE)
To move or toss something quickly and lightly backward, often referring to a quick gesture such as tossing the head or hand.

Examples

His steps were short and stiff, and, with his head thrown back, his progress was a supercilious strut.

Synset Examples

She tossed back her head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toss back - to throw something quickly backward
