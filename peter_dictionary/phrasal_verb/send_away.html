send away

1. VERB

/ sɛnd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / sɛnd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to make someone leave a place or to stop associating with them, often by telling them to go or dismissing them

Examples

My wife died in childbirth after I was sent away.

Synset Examples

They dropped her after she had a child out of wedlock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dismiss - to order or allow someone to leave; to discharge from employment or duties
