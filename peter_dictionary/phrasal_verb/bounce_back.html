bounce back

1. VERB

/ baʊns bæk / (AmE), / baʊns bæk / (BrE)
to recover quickly after being ill, injured, or experiencing difficulties

Examples

If he can bounce back with one of those 25 home runs years, the club will have to be better off offensively.
Let him bounce back, and he could really set up the staff.

Synset Examples

He got well fast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recover - to return to a normal state of health, mind, or strength
  • Antonym: relapse - to fall back into a previous condition, especially illness
