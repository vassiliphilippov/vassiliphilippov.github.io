count on

1. VERB

/ kaʊnt ɑn / (AmE), / kaʊnt ɒn / (BrE)
to expect or rely on something happening or on someone doing something; to be confident that something will happen or someone will help

Examples

Who but Thurber can be counted on to glean such nectareous essences?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depend on - to rely on someone or something for help or support
  • Synonym: rely on - to trust or depend on someone or something

2. VERB

/ kaʊnt ɑn / (AmE), / kaʊnt ɒn / (BrE)
To rely on someone or something; to trust that someone or something will help or do what is expected.

Examples

We're counting on you.

Synset Examples

you can count on me to help you any time
Look to your friends for support
You can bet on that!
Depend on your family in times of crisis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depend on - to rely on or trust someone or something
  • Synonym: trust - to have confidence in; to rely on
