hit it up

1. VERB

/ hɪt ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / hɪt ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
(informal) To become drunk or drink alcohol excessively.

Examples

The way Eileen and I were hitting it up, we needed ten or fifteen dollars an evening.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: get drunk - become intoxicated with alcohol
