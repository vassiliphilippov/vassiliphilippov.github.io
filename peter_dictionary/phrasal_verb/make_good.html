make good

1. VERB

/ meɪk ɡʊd / (AmE), / meɪk ɡʊd / (BrE)
to do what you have promised; to fulfill a pledge or commitment

Examples

Sheriff Felix Tabb said the ordinary apparently made good his promise.

Synset Examples

make good on promises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fulfill - to carry out a promise or obligation
  • Antonym: break a promise - to fail to act as promised
