carry over

1. VERB

/ ˈkæri ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈkæri ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to move something from one place, state, or situation to another, especially so that it continues to exist or be relevant

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transfer - to move something from one place to another
  • Antonym: bring forward - to move something to an earlier place, time, or state
