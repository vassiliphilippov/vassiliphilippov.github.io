chin up

1. VERB

/ tʃɪn ʌp / (AmE), / tʃɪn ʌp / (BrE)
to raise oneself by the arms on a horizontal bar until the chin is level with the bar, as in a gymnastic exercise

Examples

When they can take ten seconds to accomplish the descent, they will have the strength to chin up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pull up - an exercise where one pulls up the body on a bar until the chin is above the bar
