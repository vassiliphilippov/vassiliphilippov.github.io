catch fire

1. VERB

/ kætʃ ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / kætʃ ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
to begin to burn or burst into flames suddenly and often unexpectedly

Examples

The mudwagon had caught fire also.

Synset Examples

Marsh gases ignited suddenly
The oily rags combusted spontaneously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignite - to catch fire or cause something to catch fire
  • Antonym: extinguish - to cause a fire or light to stop burning
