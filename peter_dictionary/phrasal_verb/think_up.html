think up

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk ʌp / (AmE), / θɪŋk ʌp / (BrE)
To invent or devise something in your mind, especially an idea, plan, or excuse.

Examples

It could be some kind of trick Budd had thought up.
Any lingering suspicion that this was a trick Al Budd had thought up was dispelled.

Synset Examples

He thought up a plan to get rich quickly
no-one had ever thought of such a clever piece of software

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devise - to invent or plan something, especially by careful thought
  • Synonym: invent - create or design something that has not existed before
