size up

1. VERB

/ saɪz ʌp / (AmE), / saɪz ʌp / (BrE)
to carefully judge or assess a person or situation, especially by looking at them closely

Examples

Andrei's skilled eye sized them up.
But those who were in favor of the meeting felt that a frank exchange between the two men and an opportunity to size one another up would prove salutary.

Synset Examples

he scrutinized his likeness in the mirror

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evaluate - to judge or determine the significance, worth, or quality of
  • Synonym: scrutinize - to examine or inspect closely and thoroughly
  • Antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to; disregard
