press out

1. VERB

/ prɛs aʊt / (AmE), / prɛs aʊt / (BrE)
To extinguish something, especially a cigarette or cigar, by pressing and crushing it.

Examples

Pressing his cigarette out in the earth, Warren walked to the slit and scanned the jagged hills.

Synset Examples

stub out your cigar

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stub out - to extinguish (a cigar or cigarette) by pressing it against a surface
