rough out

1. VERB

/ rʌf aʊt / (AmE), / rʌf aʊt / (BrE)
to shape, plan, or prepare something in a preliminary or unfinished form before completing the final version

Examples

Scribe V-shaped lines on the bar and rough out with either a hack saw or a cutting disk in a hand power tool.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketch out - to make a rough or unfinished drawing or plan of something
  • Antonym: finish - to bring something to completion or a finished state
