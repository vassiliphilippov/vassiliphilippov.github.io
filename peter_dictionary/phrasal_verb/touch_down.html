touch down

1. VERB

/ tʌtʃ daʊn / (AmE), / tʌtʃ daʊn / (BrE)
(of an aircraft) to land on the ground after flying.

Examples

Everybody else was allowed to file off the plane after it touched down at El Paso at 4: 18 a. m..

Synset Examples

the plane touched down at noon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: land - (of an aircraft) to come down through the air and rest on the ground
