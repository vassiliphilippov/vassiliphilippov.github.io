run for

1. VERB

/ rʌn fɔr / (AmE), / rʌn fə / (BrE)
(of an event, process, or media) to continue or last for a specified period of time.

Examples

You were loyal to my father for years.

Synset Examples

The film runs 5 hours

Related Terms

  • Synonym: last - to continue for a specified period of time
  • Synonym: continue - to persist without interruption
