fuck up

1. VERB

/ fʌk ʌp / (AmE), / fʌk ʌp / (BrE)
to make a mess of; to destroy, ruin, or handle something very badly

Examples

Lousy Reactionary bastards been tryin to fuck up the Program for months.
To fuck up the Program.

Synset Examples

I botched the dinner and we had to eat out
the pianist screwed up the difficult passage in the second movement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botch - to do something badly or carelessly; to ruin through incompetence
  • Synonym: screw up - to make a serious mistake or handle something badly (informal)
