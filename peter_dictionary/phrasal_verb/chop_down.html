chop down

1. VERB

/ tʃɑp daʊn / (AmE), / tʃɒp daʊn / (BrE)
to cut through and cause to fall, especially a tree or similar upright object, typically using an axe or other sharp tool.

Examples

He did n't even bother to wipe himself off and he chopped part of Pa's door down before he stopped.

Synset Examples

George chopped down the cherry tree

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fell - to cut down a tree
  • Synonym: cut down - to make something fall by cutting it at the base
