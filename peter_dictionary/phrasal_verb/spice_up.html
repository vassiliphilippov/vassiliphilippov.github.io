spice up

1. VERB

/ spaɪs ʌp / (AmE), / spaɪs ʌp / (BrE)
To add herbs or spices to food; by extension, to make something more lively, interesting, or exciting.

Examples

And you 'll notice that in both places, there are acres of charming young ladies who with little effort spice up any chow line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liven up - make more lively or interesting
  • Synonym: jazz up - make more lively or interesting, especially visually or stylistically
  • Antonym: bland - lacking strong features or characteristics; opposite of spiced up
