fade out

1. VERB

/ feɪd aʊt / (AmE), / feɪd aʊt / (BrE)
to gradually become weaker or disappear, especially so that something is no longer seen or heard

Examples

They could hear the pony's feet on the dry leaves for a while, then the sound faded out.

Synset Examples

The sound faded out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fade - to lose brightness, loudness, or strength gradually
  • Synonym: fade away - to disappear slowly or gradually
