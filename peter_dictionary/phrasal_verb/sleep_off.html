sleep off

1. VERB

/ slip ɑf / (AmE), / sliːp ɒf / (BrE)
to recover from the effects of something, such as illness, intoxication, or tiredness, by sleeping

Examples

Ma never went near the old man when he was sleeping it off.

Synset Examples

sleep off a hangover

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleep - to rest with eyes closed, typically at night, in a state of reduced consciousness
