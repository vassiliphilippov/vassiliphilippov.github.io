pull together

1. VERB

/ pʊl təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / pʊl təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to assemble or bring together people, things, or ideas into a unified whole

Examples

They want to be fat cats, Pam thought, and lighted a cigarette and leaned back on a chaise and considered pulling her thoughts together.

Synset Examples

gather some stones
pull your thoughts together

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gather - to bring or come together into a group or mass
