bow down

1. VERB

/ baʊ daʊn / (AmE), / baʊ daʊn / (BrE)
to bend your body forward and down, especially as a sign of respect, submission, or worship

Examples

"The Bible forbids us to bow down to or to make graven images.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prostrate - lie flat on the ground in submission or worship
  • Antonym: stand tall - to stand with confidence or pride, not in submission
