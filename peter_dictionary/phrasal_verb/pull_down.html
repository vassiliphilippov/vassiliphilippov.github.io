pull down

1. VERB

/ pʊl daʊn / (AmE), / pʊl daʊn / (BrE)
to demolish or destroy (a building or structure), making it level with the ground

Examples

This is why I say we just can't go ahead and disarm the Germans and pull down our own defenses.
At the moment, many of these are being pulled down.

Synset Examples

The building was levelled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demolish - to completely destroy (a building or structure)
  • Synonym: raze - to completely destroy a building or site to the ground
