change over

1. VERB

/ tʃeɪndʒ ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tʃeɪndʒ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to shift from one system, activity, or way of working to another; to transfer or switch from one thing to another

Examples

Rather, they are impressed with the British Government's success in forcing - and helping - the British textile industry to shrink and to change over to other products.

Synset Examples

First Joe led; then we switched

Related Terms

  • Synonym: switch - to change from one thing to another
