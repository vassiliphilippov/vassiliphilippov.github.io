spin around

1. VERB

/ spɪn əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / spɪn əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to turn quickly and repeatedly around one's own axis or to turn one's body in a rapid, circular motion

Examples

Phil spun around and made an accurate throw into Mike Deegan's hands on third base.
This so infuriated Deegan that he spun around and said: "I 'll get that little bastard.
and Phil banged his locker door shut and spun around.

Synset Examples

The dervishes whirl around and around without getting dizzy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whirl - to move or cause to move rapidly around and around
  • Synonym: twirl - spin quickly and lightly around; rotate rapidly
