lie around

1. VERB

/ laɪ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / laɪ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to spend time doing nothing in particular; to lounge or be idle without purpose

Examples

Would he have to clean up after her every day, clean the kitchen, the bathroom, and get down on his knees and scrub the kitchen floor, then hang up her dresses, pick up her stockings, make the bed while she lay around?

Synset Examples

She did all the work while he lay around

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hang around - to spend time doing nothing in particular
  • Synonym: laze - to spend time in a relaxed, lazy way
