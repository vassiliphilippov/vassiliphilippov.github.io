look up to

1. VERB

/ lʊk ʌp tu / (AmE), / lʊk ʌp tʊ / (BrE)
to admire and respect someone

Examples

Sort of looked up to me, you know.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: admire - to respect or think well of someone or something
  • Antonym: look down on - to think that you are better than someone
