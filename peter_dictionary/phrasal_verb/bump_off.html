bump off

1. VERB

/ bʌmp ɔf / (AmE), / bʌmp ɒf / (BrE)
to kill someone deliberately, especially in a planned or secretive way

Examples

Dirty Reactionary bastards comin down here in the night and bumpin off ol Slater and those other poor bastards.
Why did n't the damn Reactionaries bump him off?

Synset Examples

The mafia boss ordered his enemies murdered

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murder - the intentional and unlawful killing of a person
  • Synonym: assassinate - to kill an important or famous person for political or religious reasons

2. VERB

/ bʌmp ɔf / (AmE), / bʌmp ɒf / (BrE)
to kill someone, especially as part of a criminal act or conspiracy

Examples

When the boss says "push a button" on a guy, I **push a button**.

Synset Examples

The mafia boss ordered his enemies murdered

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murder - the act or crime of intentionally killing someone
  • Synonym: assassinate - to kill someone important, often for political reasons
  • Antonym: spare - to refrain from killing someone
