fall for

1. VERB

/ fɔl fər / (AmE), / fɔːl fə / (BrE)
To become strongly attracted to or infatuated with someone, often romantically.

Examples

The Greek evidently fell for her, "Monsieur X "recounted, and to clinch what he thought was an affair in the making he gave her 100000 francs (about $ 300) and led her to the roulette tables.

Synset Examples

She fell for the man from Brazil

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fall in love - to begin to feel love for someone
