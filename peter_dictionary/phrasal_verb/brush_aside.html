brush aside

1. VERB

/ brʌʃ əˈsaɪd / (AmE), / brʌʃ əˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to refuse to consider or pay attention to something, treating it as unimportant

Examples

"You mean this punk can brush me aside and I 've got to hold still for it "?

Synset Examples

She dismissed his advances

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dismiss - to deliberately decide not to think about or consider something
  • Synonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something or someone
  • Antonym: consider - to think about something carefully before making a decision
