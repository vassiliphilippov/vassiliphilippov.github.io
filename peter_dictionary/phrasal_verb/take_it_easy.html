take it easy

1. VERB

/ teɪk ɪt ˈiːzi / (AmE), / teɪk ɪt ˈiːzi / (BrE)
to relax and avoid stress or effort; to rest or act in a leisurely manner

Examples

Take it easy ".
"You take it easy, boy ", Clayton whispered.
You take it easy, your time will come ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relax - to become less tense or anxious
  • Synonym: take a load off - to sit down and rest
