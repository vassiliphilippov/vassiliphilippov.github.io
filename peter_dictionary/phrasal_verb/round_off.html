round off

1. VERB

/ raʊnd ɔf / (AmE), / raʊnd ɒf / (BrE)
to make something round or smoother, especially by removing or smoothing sharp edges

Examples

Then the chines are rounded off and the bottom is rough sanded in preparation.

Synset Examples

round the edges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smooth - to make a surface even or free from roughness
