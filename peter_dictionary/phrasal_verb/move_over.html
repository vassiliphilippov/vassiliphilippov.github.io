move over

1. VERB

/ muːv ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / muːv ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to change your position in order to make space for someone or something

Examples

He moved over to the desk and stood looking at the papers on it but not touching anything.
Curt moved over beside the door and waited.

Synset Examples

The park gave way to a supermarket
`Move over,' he told the crowd

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make room - to provide space for someone or something by moving
  • Synonym: shift - to move slightly from one position to another
