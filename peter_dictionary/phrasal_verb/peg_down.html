peg down

1. VERB

/ pɛg daʊn / (AmE), / pɛg daʊn / (BrE)
To fasten or secure something to the ground using a peg or pin.

Examples

Try pegging down each separate branch to the earth, using a bobby pin to hold it there.

Synset Examples

peg a tent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stake down - to secure something with stakes driven into the ground
