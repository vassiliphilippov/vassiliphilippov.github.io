hold back

1. VERB

/ hoʊld ˈbæk / (AmE), / həʊld ˈbæk / (BrE)
to prevent (something) from being known or revealed; to keep something secret or concealed

Examples

I wouldn'’t want it to get around that you held back the money because you had second thoughts about the rebels.

Synset Examples

She conceals her anger well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conceal - to hide or keep something secret
  • Antonym: reveal - to make known or show something that was secret or hidden
