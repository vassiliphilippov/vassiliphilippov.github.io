knock over

1. VERB

/ nɑk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / nɒk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to cause something to fall from an upright or normal position, typically by hitting or pushing it

Examples

Another Reb writing under similar circumstances before Atlanta reported: "The Yankees keep Shooting so I am afraid they will knock over my ink, so I will close ".
He let down the tailgate and was knocked over by the sluice of water.

Synset Examples

The cat knocked over the flower vase
the clumsy customer turned over the vase
he tumped over his beer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overturn - to cause something to turn over or be upset
  • Antonym: upright - in a vertical or straight position
