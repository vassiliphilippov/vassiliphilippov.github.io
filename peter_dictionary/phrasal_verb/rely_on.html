rely on

1. VERB

/ rɪˈlaɪ ɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈlaɪ ɒn / (BrE)
to trust or depend on someone or something with confidence

Examples

Put your trust in the Lord.

Synset Examples

she is someone you can really rely on when times get rough
you can rely on his discretion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depend on - to rely on or be controlled by something or someone

Etymology

From 'rely' (from Old French relier, from Latin religare 'to bind fast'), combined with the preposition 'on'.
