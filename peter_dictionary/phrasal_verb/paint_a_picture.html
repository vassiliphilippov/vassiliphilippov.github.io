paint a picture

1. VERB

/ peɪnt ə ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / peɪnt ə ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
to describe or represent something in a way that creates a vivid impression or suggests a particular situation or feeling

Examples

The evident contradiction between the rosy picture of Russia's progress painted by the Communist party's program and the enormous dangers for all humanity posed by Premier Khrushchev's Berlin policy has already led to speculation abroad that the program may be severely altered.

Synset Examples

this remark evoked sadness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depict - to describe or show something using words, a picture, etc.
