live it up

1. VERB

/ lɪv ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / lɪv ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to enjoy yourself in an energetic or extravagant way, often spending money or celebrating

Examples

Pleasure, fame and fortune, drowning your troubles with a drink, and "living it up "with the gang are like candy bars when you 're hungry: they may ease your hunger temporarily, but they 'll never take the place of a satisfying, mouth-watering steak.

Synset Examples

it's your birthday, so let's live it up!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrate - to acknowledge a significant or happy day or event with a social gathering or enjoyable activity
