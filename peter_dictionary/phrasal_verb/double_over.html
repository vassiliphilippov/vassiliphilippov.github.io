double over

1. VERB

/ ˈdʌb.əl ˈoʊ.vɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl ˈəʊ.və / (BrE)
To bend forward or curl up, typically because of pain or laughter.

Examples

Then, as he doubled, gasping, vomiting the breakfast he had so lately eaten, Lord straightened him with an uppercut.

Synset Examples

He doubled and vomited violently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crumple - to suddenly collapse or bend forward
  • Antonym: straighten up - to return to an upright position
