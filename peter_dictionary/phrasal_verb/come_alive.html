come alive

1. VERB

/ kʌm əˈlaɪv / (AmE), / kʌm əˈlaɪv / (BrE)
to stop sleeping; to become alert, energetic, or animated after being inactive or tired.

Examples

Under the influence of marijuana the beatnik comes alive within and experiences a wonderfully enhanced sense of self as if he had discovered the open sesame to the universe of being.

Synset Examples

She woke up to the sound of the alarm clock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wake up - to stop sleeping
  • Antonym: doze off - to fall asleep, especially unintentionally
