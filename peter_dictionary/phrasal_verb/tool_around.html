tool around

1. VERB

/ tuːl əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / tuːl əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to drive or ride around casually, with no particular destination or purpose, for enjoyment

Examples

Now, you no doubt regard the preceding as pap; you 're tooling around full of gage in your hot rods, gorging yourselves on pizza and playing pinball in the taverns and generally behaving like Ubermenschen.

Synset Examples

We tooled down the street

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyride - drive around for pleasure, especially recklessly
