try for

1. VERB

/ ˈtraɪ fɔr / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ fə / (BrE)
to attempt to achieve or gain something, especially by making a special effort

Examples

Eighty thousand won top honors and a chance to try for the team itself.

Synset Examples

She tried for the Olympics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go for - to attempt to achieve or obtain something

2. VERB

/ traɪ fɔr / (AmE), / traɪ fə / (BrE)
to attempt to achieve or obtain (a particular goal, prize, position, etc.)

Examples

I was thinking of maybe trying for my high school equivalency.

Synset Examples

She tried for the Olympics

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attempt - to make an effort to do something
  • Synonym: go for - to try to get or achieve something
