ring out

1. VERB

/ rɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / rɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
to sound loudly and clearly, especially for a sound such as a bell, gunshot, or shout

Examples

The young Texas pianist can make great chords ring out as well as anyone, so last night the massive sonorities of this challenging concerto were no hazard to him.

Synset Examples

a shot rang out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: echo - (of a sound) to be repeated or reverberate after the original sound
