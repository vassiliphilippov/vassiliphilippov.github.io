flirt with

1. VERB

/ flɝt wɪð / (AmE), / flɜːt wɪð / (BrE)
To consider or toy with an idea, activity, or possibility, often without serious intent.

Examples

Instead of giving themselves spontaneously to the orgiastic release that jazz can give them, they undergo psychoanalysis or flirt with mysticism or turn to prostitutes for satisfaction.

Synset Examples

He entertained the notion of moving to South America

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertain - to consider or allow oneself to think about (an idea, etc.)
