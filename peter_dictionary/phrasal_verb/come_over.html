come over

1. VERB

/ kʌm ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / kʌm ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a feeling, impression, or meaning) to be communicated or perceived in a particular way by others.

Examples

Bitterness comes over him and the taste of time is like unripe persimmons in his mouth.

Synset Examples

He came across very clearly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: come across - to be perceived or give an impression in a certain way
