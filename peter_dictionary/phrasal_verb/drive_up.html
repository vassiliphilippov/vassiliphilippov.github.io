drive up

1. VERB

/ draɪv ʌp / (AmE), / draɪv ʌp / (BrE)
to arrive at or approach a place in a vehicle

Examples

Almost immediately Howard and his daughter Debora drove up in the Cadillac.

Synset Examples

The truck entered the driveway and drove up towards the house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pull up - stop a vehicle at a place
