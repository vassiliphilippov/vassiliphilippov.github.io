brush on

1. VERB

/ brʌʃ ɑn / (AmE), / brʌʃ ɒn / (BrE)
to apply (a substance) to a surface using a brush

Examples

Outside was finished with Creek-Turn brown toner brushed on and sponged off to give antique finish.

Synset Examples

Brush butter on the roast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paint on - to apply (paint or a similar liquid) to a surface using a brush
