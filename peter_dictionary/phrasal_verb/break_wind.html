break wind

1. VERB

/ breɪk wɪnd / (AmE), / breɪk wɪnd / (BrE)
to expel gas from the intestines through the anus; to fart

Examples

You'd cut **a** loud **fart**, he'd jump three feet in the air.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fart - to expel intestinal gas through the anus loudly or rudely
