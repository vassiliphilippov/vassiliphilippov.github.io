tell apart

1. VERB

/ tɛl əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / tɛl əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to recognize or distinguish the difference between two or more people or things

Examples

The one sure way to tell victim and villain apart is to examine the hind legs which in the case of the idler, Psithyrus, lack the pollen baskets - naturally!

Synset Examples

The fleeing convicts were picked out of the darkness by the watchful prison guards
I can't make out the faces in this photograph

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distinguish - to recognize or point out a difference
  • Synonym: differentiate - to show or find the difference between things
