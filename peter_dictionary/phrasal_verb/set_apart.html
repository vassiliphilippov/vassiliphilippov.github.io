set apart

/ sɛt əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / sɛt əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to select or designate someone or something for a particular purpose or to distinguish as different from others

Examples

What sets Negro-appeal programing apart from other radio shows?

Synset Examples
