write out

1. VERB

/ raɪt aʊt / (AmE), / raɪt aʊt / (BrE)
to put into writing; to write something in complete or full form, often rather than in shorthand or from memory

Examples

Hillman had written it all out, had n't he?
The director helped tailor-make a check list of the district manager's errors by asking various observers to write out sentences commenting on the mistakes they felt he made.

Synset Examples

write out a contract

Related Terms

  • Synonym: write - to mark letters, words, or symbols on a surface
