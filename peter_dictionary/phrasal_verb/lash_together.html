lash together

1. VERB

/ læʃ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / læʃ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to tie or bind objects together securely using a rope or cord

Examples

Captain Musmanno roared at his men to lash three of the casks of olive oil together for a raft.

Synset Examples

Lash together these barrels!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bind - to tie or fasten something tightly

Etymology

From 'lash' (to tie with a rope), possibly of Scandinavian origin, and 'together'.
