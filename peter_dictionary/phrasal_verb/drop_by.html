drop by

1. VERB

/ drɑp baɪ / (AmE), / drɒp baɪ / (BrE)
to visit someone informally and without arranging a specific time

Examples

"I 'll drop by again in a few days ".

Synset Examples

We frequently drop by the neighbors' house for a cup of coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stop by - to pay a short, casual visit
  • Degree Higher: call on - to visit someone, usually formally or for a purpose
