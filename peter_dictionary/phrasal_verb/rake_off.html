rake off

1. VERB

/ ˈreɪk ɑf / (AmE), / ˈreɪk ɒf / (BrE)
To take a share of money from an illegal or dishonest transaction, often secretly or without rightful claim.

Examples

You think I'm skimming off the top?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skim off - to take money or a portion from the top, often fraudulently
