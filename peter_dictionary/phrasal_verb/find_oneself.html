find oneself

1. VERB

/ faɪnd wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / faɪnd wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to come to understand and accept one's own identity, abilities, or place in life

Examples

He did not, however, find himself at once.

Synset Examples

My son went to Berkeley to find himself

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discover oneself - to learn about one's own character or abilities
