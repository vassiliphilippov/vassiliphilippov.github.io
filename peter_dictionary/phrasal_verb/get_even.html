get even

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈivən / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈiːvən / (BrE)
to take action in order to achieve equality, especially by retaliating for a wrong or loss; to make things square or balanced with someone who has wronged you

Examples

There was no way of getting even.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retaliate - to do something harmful or unpleasant to someone because they have done something harmful or unpleasant to you
