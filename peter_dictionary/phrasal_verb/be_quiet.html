be quiet

1. VERB

/ bi ˈkwaɪət / (AmE), / biː ˈkwaɪət / (BrE)
to stop talking or making noise; to become or remain silent

Examples

Spencer was quiet for a moment longer, then he said, "There is nothing I want to say, Captain ".

Synset Examples

The children shut up when their father approached

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be silent - to refrain from speaking; remain without sound
  • Synonym: shut up - to stop talking immediately (informal)
  • Antonym: speak - to express thoughts aloud; to talk
