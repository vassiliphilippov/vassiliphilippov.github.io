rough up

1. VERB

/ rʌf ʌp / (AmE), / rʌf ʌp / (BrE)
To attack or treat someone violently, especially by hitting or manhandling them.

Examples

If that goombah tries any rough stuff, tell him I'm no bandleader.

Synset Examples

The police strong-armed the suspect

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beat up - to attack someone physically, usually injuring them
  • Synonym: assault - to attack someone physically
