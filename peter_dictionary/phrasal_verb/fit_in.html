fit in

1. VERB

/ fɪt ɪn / (AmE), / fɪt ɪn / (BrE)
to be or appear suitable, compatible, or harmonious with something or someone; to go well together.

Examples

The analyst is apt to be too honest to fit in.
When Deegan smiled his eyes never fit in with his lips.

Synset Examples

The colors don't harmonize
Their ideas concorded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harmonize - to be in agreement or harmony with
  • Antonym: clash - to be incompatible or not go together
