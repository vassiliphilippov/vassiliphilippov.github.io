step forward

1. VERB

/ stɛp ˈfɔrwɚd / (AmE), / stɛp ˈfɔːwəd / (BrE)
to make oneself known or available, especially by volunteering or taking action in a situation

Examples

Juet had made plentiful enemies, several men stepped forward.

Synset Examples

Young people should step to the fore and help their peers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: come forward - to present oneself or volunteer for something
