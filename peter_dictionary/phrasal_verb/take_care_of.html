take care (of)

1. VERB

/ teɪk kɛr (ʌv) / (AmE), / teɪk keə (ɒv) / (BrE)
to be responsible for or deal with something or someone; to manage or handle a task or situation.

Examples

"Send Fredo off to do this, send Fredo off to do that!" "Let Fredo take care of some Mickey Mouse nightclub somewhere."

Synset Examples

She takes care of all the necessary arrangements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handle - to deal with or manage a situation or person
  • Synonym: look after - to take care of someone or something
