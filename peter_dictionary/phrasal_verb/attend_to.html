attend to

1. VERB

/ əˈtɛnd tu / (AmE), / əˈtɛnd tuː / (BrE)
to deal with, pay attention to, or take care of something or someone; to give one's attention or effort to a task or duty

Examples

"Mr. Palmer will attend to any applications relating to bridges and if desired will view the proposed site, and lay out and superintend the work, or recommend a suitable person to execute it.

Synset Examples

Attend to your duties, please

Related Terms

  • Synonym: take care of - to look after or deal with something or someone
  • Antonym: neglect - to fail to care for or pay attention to
