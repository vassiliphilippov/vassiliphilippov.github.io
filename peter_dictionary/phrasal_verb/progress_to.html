progress to

1. VERB

/ prəˈɡrɛs tu / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛs tʊ / (BrE)
to move forward or develop to reach a particular stage, state, or goal

Examples

From the fusty panaceas of spinach, eggs and prunes, the U. S. has progressed to curds, concentrates and capsules.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advance to - move forward to a particular stage or place
  • Antonym: regress to - return to a previous or less advanced state
