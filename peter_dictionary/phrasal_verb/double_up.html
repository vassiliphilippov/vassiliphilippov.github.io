double up

1. VERB

/ ˌdʌbəl ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəl ˈʌp / (BrE)
to bend over or curl up, especially because of laughter or pain

Examples

Thereupon Hwang Pah said: "If I knew thou art an Arhat, I would have doubled you up before thou got over there "!

Synset Examples

He doubled and vomited violently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collapse - to fall down or give way suddenly, usually from weakness or pain
