settle on

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛt̬l ɑn / (AmE), / ˈsɛtl ɒn / (BrE)
To become fixed or focused on something; to direct one's attention or gaze towards a particular object or point.

Examples

He settled on the sofa with his coffee, warming his hands on the cup, although the room was heavy with heat.

Synset Examples

Her eyes fixated on a point on the horizon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fixate on - to focus or become fixed on something
