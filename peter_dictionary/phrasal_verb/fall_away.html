fall away

1. VERB

/ fɔl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / fɔːl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to gradually diminish in size, strength, or intensity; to become less or disappear

Examples

Every slight sound that rose against that pressure fell away again, crushed beneath it.
The years suddenly fell away at this point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: subside - to become less intense, violent, or severe
  • Synonym: wane - to decrease gradually in strength or intensity
