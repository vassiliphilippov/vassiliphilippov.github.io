brush up

1. VERB

/ brʌʃ ʌp / (AmE), / brʌʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to improve or refresh one's knowledge or skill in something, especially after not practicing it for a while

Examples

Maggie was looking much happier already, clearing a space on the table and chattering about how she could put up a typewriter right there, and be brushing up on her typing so Eugenia would n't be ashamed of it.

Synset Examples

I reviewed the material before the test

Related Terms

  • Synonym: review - to look at or examine something again
  • Synonym: revise - to review or go over material to improve understanding
