draw together

1. VERB

/ drɔː təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / drɔː təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to bring people or things closer together in feeling, purpose, or action; to unite by a shared emotion or cause.

Examples

The members of the "family "are drawn together by a common love for Christ and a sincere devotion to His Kingdom.
It had drawn them together, and since his release from prison Dill had worked tirelessly to effect this night's escape.

Synset Examples

The death of their child had drawn them together

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unite - to come or bring together for a common purpose
  • Synonym: bond - to form a close connection or relationship
