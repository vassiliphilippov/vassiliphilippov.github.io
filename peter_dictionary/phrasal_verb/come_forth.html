come forth

1. VERB

/ kʌm fɔrθ / (AmE), / kʌm fɔːθ / (BrE)
to appear or emerge from a place or source; to come out into view

Examples

And he took heart; the final word came forth "place ".

Synset Examples

Water issued from the hole in the wall
The words seemed to come out by themselves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emerge - to come out into view or become apparent
  • Synonym: issue - to flow or come out from a source
  • Antonym: disappear - to cease to be visible; to vanish
