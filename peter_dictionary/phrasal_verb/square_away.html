square away

1. VERB

/ skwɛr əˈweɪ / (AmE), / skwɛə əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to arrange or organize things properly; to put in order or make ready for use or action

Examples

Maybe it's taking longer to get things squared away than the bankers expected.

Synset Examples

Tidy up your room!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tidy up - to make neat and orderly; to clean up
  • Synonym: straighten out - to bring order or clarity to; to sort out

Etymology

Originally a nautical term referring to adjusting ship sails to catch the wind properly, thus preparing or getting things in order.
