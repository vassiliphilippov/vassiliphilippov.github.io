talk down

1. VERB

/ tɑk daʊn / (AmE), / tɔːk daʊn / (BrE)
To speak to someone in a way that suggests they are less intelligent or important; to belittle or patronize through conversation.

"I 'm sure the hotel does n't know you 're wandering around the corridors, knocking on strangers' doors and talking down Eskimos ".

Related Terms
