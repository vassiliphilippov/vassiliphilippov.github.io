wake up

1. VERB

/ weɪk ʌp / (AmE), / weɪk ʌp / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awaken - to stop sleeping; to become awake or make someone awake
  • Antonym: fall asleep - to begin sleeping

2. VERB

/ weɪk ʌp / (AmE), / weɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to stop sleeping and become awake; to cause someone to stop sleeping

Examples

One always wakes up, even from one's own dreams.

Waking up was horrible.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fall asleep - to begin to sleep
