beat up

1. VERB

/ bit ʌp / (AmE), / biːt ʌp / (BrE)
to attack someone violently and injure them by hitting or kicking them repeatedly

Examples

What they did do... is beat him **within an inch of his life**.

Synset Examples

Thugs beat him up when he walked down the street late at night
The teacher used to beat the students

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assault - to attack someone physically
  • Antonym: protect - to keep someone or something safe from harm
