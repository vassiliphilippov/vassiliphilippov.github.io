touch up

1. VERB

/ tʌtʃ ʌp / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to improve or alter something slightly, especially to make it look better or neater

Examples

The result was like that of a beautiful painting with some of the highlights touched up almost to the point of garishness.

Synset Examples

This photograph has been retouched!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retouch - to improve or alter a photograph or painting, especially by making small corrections
