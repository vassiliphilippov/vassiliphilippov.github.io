work through

1. VERB

/ wɝk ˈθɹu / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈθruː / (BrE)
to deal with or complete something by taking all the necessary steps thoroughly, often by examining or considering each part in detail

Examples

Let us work through an example, and the simpler and commoner the better.

Synset Examples

We worked through an example

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go through - to examine or deal with all parts of something
