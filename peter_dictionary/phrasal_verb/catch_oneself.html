catch oneself

1. VERB

/ kætʃ wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / kæʧ wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to stop oneself just before doing or saying something, usually because one realizes it would be inappropriate or unwise

Examples

"God does n't have any appliance or cosmetics ", he said heatedly before he caught himself.

Synset Examples

She managed to catch herself before telling her boss what was on her mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stop oneself - to prevent oneself from continuing an action
