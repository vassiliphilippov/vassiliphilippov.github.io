pass out

1. VERB

/ pæs aʊt / (AmE), / pɑːs aʊt / (BrE)
to lose consciousness suddenly, typically because of a lack of oxygen to the brain, weakness, or emotional distress

Examples

I guess nobody wants to pass out and miss anything ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faint - to lose consciousness temporarily
  • Antonym: come round - to regain consciousness

2. VERB

/ pæs aʊt / (AmE), / pɑːs aʊt / (BrE)
to become unconscious suddenly, especially due to weakness, distress, or lack of blood supply to the brain; to faint

Examples

I passed out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faint - to lose consciousness temporarily, especially from weakness or distress
