toy with

1. VERB

/ tɔɪ wɪð / (AmE), / tɔɪ wɪð / (BrE)
to consider or think about something as a possibility, often in a casual or not very serious way

Examples

Toying with her field in the early stages, Garden Fresh was asked for top speed only in the stretch by Jockey Philip Grimm and won by a length and a half in 1.24 3 - 5 for the 7 furlongs.

Synset Examples

He entertained the notion of moving to South America

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertain - to think about or consider a possibility or idea
