wise up

1. VERB

/ waɪz ʌp / (AmE), / waɪz ʌp / (BrE)
to become aware of the truth about a situation, especially after being unknowing or naïve

Examples

Thoroughly Wised Up.

Synset Examples

They wised up to it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch on - to begin to understand; become aware
  • Synonym: get wise - to become aware or informed
  • Antonym: be fooled - to be deceived or misled
