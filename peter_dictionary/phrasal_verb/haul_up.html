haul up

1. VERB

/ hɔl ʌp / (AmE), / hɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
(Of a vehicle) to stop or come to a halt after moving, especially after driving.

Examples

Hauling up, Russ listened carefully.

Synset Examples

The Rolls pulled up on pour front lawn
The chauffeur hauled up in front of us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pull up - (Of a vehicle) to stop or come to a halt after driving
