slam on

1. VERB

/ slæm ɑn / (AmE), / slæm ɒn / (BrE)
to press or apply suddenly and forcefully, especially referring to brakes or similar controls

Examples

It was a district of small factories and loft buildings and occasional tenements, and he could see the police radio car as he rounded the corner and slammed on the brakes.

Synset Examples

slap some paint onto the wall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: step on - to press down sharply, especially a pedal
