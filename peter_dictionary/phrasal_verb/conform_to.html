conform to

1. VERB

/ kənˈfɔrm tu / (AmE), / kənˈfɔːm tuː / (BrE)
to act or behave in accordance with (a rule, standard, law, or custom)

Examples

Man's life, originally shaped for immortality and for communion with God, must now be conformed to the shape of death.
