Related Terms

2. VERB

/ nɑk daʊn / (AmE), / nɒk daʊn / (BrE)
To hit or strike someone or something with enough force to make them fall to the ground.

Examples

Four men were knocked down, but did not attempt to rise.

Synset Examples

He decked his opponent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: floor - to knock someone to the ground, especially in boxing
  • Degree Higher: knock out - to hit someone so hard they become unconscious
