put down

1. VERB

/ pʊt daʊn / (AmE), / pʊt daʊn / (BrE)
To place something or someone onto a surface so that it is resting in a horizontal position.

Examples

"Put the rifle down, Joseph ", the girl said.
He put her down on the couch, and going into the kitchen, saw that the boy had dropped into a chair beside the table.

Synset Examples

lay the books on the table
lay the patient carefully onto the bed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lay down - to place something in a horizontal position
