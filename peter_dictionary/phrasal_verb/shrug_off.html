shrug off

1. VERB

/ ʃrʌɡ ɔf / (AmE), / ʃrʌɡ ɒf / (BrE)
To dismiss or treat something as unimportant or not serious.

Examples

Madden took up this point with Garth, who shrugged it off.

Synset Examples

Jane shrugged off the news that her stock had fallen 3 points

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disregard - pay no attention to; ignore
  • Synonym: brush aside - to treat something as not important
