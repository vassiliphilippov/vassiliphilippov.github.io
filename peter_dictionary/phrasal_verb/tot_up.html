tot up

1. VERB

/ tɑt ʌp / (AmE), / tɒt ʌp / (BrE)
to add together a set of numbers or amounts to find the total; to sum up.

Examples

All these evaluations are then totted up and tabulated, by adding up the Hits and Significants, with the weight placed on those in the sitter's own reading.

Synset Examples

Add all the people in this town to those of the neighboring town

Related Terms

  • Synonym: add up - to find the total by adding numbers
  • Synonym: sum - to find the sum of something

Etymology

From 'tot', meaning to add up, origin uncertain but attested from late 19th century; 'up' adds emphasis on completion of the sum.
