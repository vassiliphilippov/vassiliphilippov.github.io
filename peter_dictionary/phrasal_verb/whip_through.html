whip through

1. VERB

/ wɪp θru / (AmE), / wɪp θruː / (BrE)
to complete or go through something very quickly; to deal with rapidly and efficiently

Examples

The Senate quickly whipped through its meager fare of House bills approved by committees, passing the three on the calendar.

Synset Examples

We whipped through the last papers that we had to read before the weekend

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rush through - to do or deal with something quickly
  • Synonym: dash through - to move or go through something very quickly
