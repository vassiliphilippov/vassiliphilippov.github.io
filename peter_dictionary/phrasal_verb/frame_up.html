frame up

1. VERB

/ freɪm ʌp / (AmE), / freɪm ʌp / (BrE)
to construct by assembling or joining parts together

Examples

Inside, bunks are framed up and installed as indicated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: build - to construct by putting parts or materials together
  • Synonym: assemble - to fit together the parts of something
