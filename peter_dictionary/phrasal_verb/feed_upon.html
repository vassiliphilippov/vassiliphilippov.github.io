feed upon

1. VERB

/ fid əˈpɑn / (AmE), / fiːd əˈpɒn / (BrE)
To draw sustenance, inspiration, or support from something, often in an abstract or figurative sense.

Examples

The Old Man silently fed upon these streets.

Synset Examples

He fed on the great ideas of her mentor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: feed on - draw nourishment or inspiration from; can be literal or figurative
