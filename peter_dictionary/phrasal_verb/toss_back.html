toss back

1. VERB

/ tɑs bæk / (AmE), / tɒs bæk / (BrE)
To throw something backwards with a sudden, light movement, especially one's head or hair.

Examples

Almost immediately she was ashamed of herself for feeling vain, at such a time, in such a place, and she tossed back her long yellow hair, smiling shyly as she entered the room.

Synset Examples

She tossed back her head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: throw back - to move something backward suddenly
