clear one's throat

1. VERB

/ klɪr wʌnz θroʊt / (AmE), / klɪə wʌnz θrəʊt / (BrE)
To cough or make a guttural sound in order to remove mucus or food from the throat, often done before speaking.

Examples

[CLEARS THROAT] I'’m very pleased you'’re all able to come from such distances to be with me today.

Synset Examples

he cleared his throat before he started to speak

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cough - to force air out of the throat suddenly, often to clear it or as a reflex
