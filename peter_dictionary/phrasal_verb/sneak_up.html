sneak up

1. VERB

/ snik ʌp / (AmE), / sniːk ʌp / (BrE)
to approach someone or something quietly and without being noticed, often with the intention of surprising or catching them off guard

Examples

You 're the kind of bastard who sneaks up on a man from behind and hits him with a club.

Synset Examples

Age creeps up on you

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creep up - to move slowly and quietly towards someone or something, especially in order not to be noticed
