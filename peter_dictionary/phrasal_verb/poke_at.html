poke at

1. VERB

/ poʊk æt / (AmE), / pəʊk æt / (BrE)
to prod or push something gently, often repeatedly or tentatively, with a finger, stick, or similar object

Examples

Mr. Crombie poked at it gingerly with his foot.

Synset Examples

She nudged my elbow when she saw her friend enter the restaurant

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nudge - push gently, especially with elbow or hand
