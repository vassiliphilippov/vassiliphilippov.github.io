bind off

1. VERB

/ baɪnd ɑf / (AmE), / baɪnd ɒf / (BrE)
to finish the last row of stitches in knitting or crochet in such a way that they will not unravel

Examples

Mark row on which first stitches have been bound off for armhole by drawing a contrasting colored thread through it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cast off - finish the last row of stitches in knitting
