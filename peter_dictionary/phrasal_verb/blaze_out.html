blaze out

1. VERB

/ bleɪz aʊt / (AmE), / bleɪz aʊt / (BrE)
to move rapidly forward or outward, as if blazing with light or energy

Examples

Hal knew that when that side of Earth rotated into the night zone, Sigmen City would blaze out into space.

Synset Examples

The spaceship blazed out into space

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rush - move quickly with urgent haste
  • Synonym: speed - move quickly
