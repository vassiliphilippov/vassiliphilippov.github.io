pep up

1. VERB

/ pɛp ʌp / (AmE), / pɛp ʌp / (BrE)
to make someone or something more lively, enthusiastic, or energetic; to cheer up or invigorate

Examples

"Several times I found the players pepping me up, where it usually is the coach who is supposed to deliver the fight talk.

Synset Examples

The crowd cheered the demonstrating strikers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheer up - to make someone feel happier or more hopeful
  • Synonym: invigorate - to give strength or energy to

Etymology

From 'pep,' a shortening of 'pepper' (energy), plus 'up' indicating increase.
