trip up

1. VERB

/ trɪp ʌp / (AmE), / trɪp ʌp / (BrE)
to cause someone to make a mistake or to detect an error in what they say or do, often by catching them off guard

Examples

Another case involves a newspaper reporter who tripped up a politician.

Synset Examples

The reporter tripped up the senator

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch out - to detect someone in a mistake or deception
  • Synonym: expose - to reveal the truth about someone's error or wrongdoing
