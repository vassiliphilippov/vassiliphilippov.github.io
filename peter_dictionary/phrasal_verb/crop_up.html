crop up

1. VERB

/ krɑp ʌp / (AmE), / krɒp ʌp / (BrE)
to appear or happen unexpectedly or suddenly, especially a problem, question, or issue

Examples

One question which inevitably crops up is whether such stations have a future in a nation where the Negro is moving into a fully integrated status.
He tried to turn right to the society page in each one, but interesting stories kept cropping up to distract him.

Synset Examples

The farm popped into view as we turned the corner
He suddenly popped up out of nowhere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arise - to become apparent or to come into existence
  • Synonym: emerge - to come into view or become known
  • Synonym: pop up - to appear suddenly or unexpectedly
