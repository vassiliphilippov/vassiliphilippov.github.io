get wise

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt waɪz / (AmE), / ɡɛt waɪz / (BrE)
to come to understand something, especially after some initial difficulty or lack of awareness; to realize the truth about a situation

Examples

I mean, I learned my lesson.

Synset Examples

She didn't know what her classmates were plotting but finally caught on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch on - to begin to understand or realize
  • Synonym: wise up - to become aware of the true nature of something
  • Synonym: learn one's lesson - to understand from experience, often after a mistake
