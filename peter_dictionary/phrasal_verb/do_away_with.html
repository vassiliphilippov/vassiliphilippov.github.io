do away with

1. VERB

/ du əˈweɪ wɪð / (AmE), / duː əˈweɪ wɪð / (BrE)
to put an end to or get rid of something

Examples

Things like that would increase rather than be done away with.

Synset Examples

Let's eliminate the course on Akkadian hieroglyphics
Socialism extinguished these archaic customs
eliminate my debts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eliminate - to completely remove or get rid of something
  • Synonym: abolish - formally put an end to (a system, practice, or institution)
