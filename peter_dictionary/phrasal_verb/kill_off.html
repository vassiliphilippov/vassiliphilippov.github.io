kill off

1. VERB

/ kɪl ɔf / (AmE), / kɪl ɒf / (BrE)
To eliminate or destroy completely, especially by killing every individual in a group or population.

Examples

Worse still, in a number of cases it has been claimed that the Psithyrus female kills off the Bombus queen.

Synset Examples

Hitler wanted to exterminate the Jews, Gypsies, Communists, and homosexuals of Europe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exterminate - destroy completely, especially a group or population
  • Synonym: wipe out - destroy or remove completely
