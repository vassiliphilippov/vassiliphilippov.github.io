march on

1. VERB

/ mɑrtʃ ɑn / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ ɒn / (BrE)
to continue to move forward, especially with the passage of time or in spite of obstacles

Examples

On the whole, however, one must wonder at just what it is that forces a beloved artist to besmirch her own reputation as time marches inexorably on.
Time marches on!

Synset Examples

Time marches on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advance - to move forward in position or progress
  • Synonym: proceed - to continue as planned or move forward
