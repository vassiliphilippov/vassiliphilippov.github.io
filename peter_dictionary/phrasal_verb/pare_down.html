pare down

1. VERB

/ pɛr daʊn / (AmE), / peə daʊn / (BrE)
to reduce something gradually, especially by removing unnecessary parts or amounts

Examples

The automobile expenses are about the only vacationing cost they can n't either eliminate or pare down drastically by camping along the way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reduce - to make something smaller or less in amount or degree
  • Synonym: cut back - to reduce the amount or quantity of something, often used for spending

Etymology

From 'pare' (to trim by cutting) + 'down'.
