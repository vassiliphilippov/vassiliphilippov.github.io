happen upon

1. VERB

/ ˈhæpən əˈpɒn / (AmE), / ˈhæpən əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to find or encounter something or someone by chance; to come across unexpectedly

Examples

After all, he had n't happened upon us in that second floor hall without warning.

Synset Examples

Related Terms
