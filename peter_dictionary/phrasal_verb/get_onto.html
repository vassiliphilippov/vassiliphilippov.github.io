get onto

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt ˈɑn.tu / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈɒn.tə / (BrE)
To begin to understand something, especially after some initial confusion or difficulty.

Examples

See, Communese is easy - once you get onto it.

Synset Examples

She didn't know what her classmates were plotting but finally caught on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catch on - To begin to understand; to grasp the meaning
  • Synonym: figure out - To understand or solve something
  • Synonym: take in - To understand or absorb information
