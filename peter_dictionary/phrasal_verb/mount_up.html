mount up

1. VERB

/ maʊnt ʌp / (AmE), / maʊnt ʌp / (BrE)
to get onto the back of a horse or similar animal in order to ride it

Examples

He 'd mounted up immediately and raced with a revolver ready toward the spot from which he 'd estimated the shot had come.
They mounted up and rode slowly behind the others at a safe distance.

Synset Examples

mount a horse

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dismount - to get off the back of a horse or similar animal
