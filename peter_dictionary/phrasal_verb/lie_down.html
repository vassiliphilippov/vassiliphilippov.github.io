lie down

1. VERB

/ laɪ daʊn / (AmE), / laɪ daʊn / (BrE)
to assume a resting or reclining position on a surface, usually by stretching out one's body horizontally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stand up - to rise from a lying or sitting position to an upright position
  • Degree Lower: sit down - to move from a standing to a seated position
