draw the line

1. VERB

/ drɔ ðə laɪn / (AmE), / drɔː ðə laɪn / (BrE)
to set a limit on what one is willing to accept or do, especially by deciding what is morally or socially acceptable

Examples

Otherwise, we go on endlessly trying to draw the line, color and other, as to which kind of man we wish to see dominate.

Synset Examples

I draw the line when it comes to lending money to friends!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: set a limit - to establish a boundary or restriction
