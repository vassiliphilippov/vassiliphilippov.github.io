pull over

1. VERB

/ pʊl ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / pʊl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to steer a vehicle to the side of the road and stop, typically in response to instructions or a situation requiring it

Examples

Pull over.

Synset Examples

The car pulled over when the ambulance approached at high speed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stop - cease moving or come to a halt
