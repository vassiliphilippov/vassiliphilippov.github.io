heat up

1. VERB

/ hit ʌp / (AmE), / hiːt ʌp / (BrE)
(of an object, place, or substance) to become hot or hotter; to gain heat.

Examples

These observations indicate that the lunar maria heat up more rapidly and also cool off more rapidly than do the mountainous regions.

Synset Examples

The room heated up quickly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cool off - to become cooler; to lose heat
