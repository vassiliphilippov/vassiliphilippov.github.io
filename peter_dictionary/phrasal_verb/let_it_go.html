let it go

1. VERB

/ lɛt ɪt ɡoʊ / (AmE), / lɛt ɪt ɡəʊ / (BrE)
to choose not to respond or act, especially to avoid escalating a situation or dwelling on something

Examples

"I asked her why she could n't do it tomorrow, but it seems the muse is working good tonight and she's afraid to let it go ".

Synset Examples

He thought of a reply but let it go

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to something
  • Antonym: react - to act in response to something; to respond
