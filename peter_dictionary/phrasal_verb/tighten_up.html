tighten up

1. VERB

/ ˈtaɪtn ʌp / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtn ʌp / (BrE)
to make rules, laws, controls, etc. stricter or more severe

Examples

He tightened up in a twinkling.

Synset Examples

Tighten the rules
stiffen the regulations

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loosen up - to make less strict or less tight; relax controls
