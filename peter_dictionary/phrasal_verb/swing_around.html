swing around

1. VERB

/ swɪŋ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / swɪŋ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to turn quickly and face the opposite direction, either literally or figuratively

Examples

There came a ghost of noise at the office door and Hague swung to see Kodyke in the entrance from the outer room.

Synset Examples

He turned around to face his opponent
My conscience told me to turn around before I made a mistake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: turn around - to move so as to face the opposite direction
