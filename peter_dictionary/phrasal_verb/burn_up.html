burn up

1. VERB

/ bɝn ʌp / (AmE), / bɜːn ʌp / (BrE)
To burn with sudden or intense brightness; to flare up.

Examples

"We 're lost and burning up already ", she bit out tensely.

Synset Examples

Every star seemed to flare with new intensity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flare up - suddenly burn or become brighter; to erupt in intensity
