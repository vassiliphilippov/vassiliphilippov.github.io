lend oneself

1. VERB

/ lɛnd ˈwʌnˌsɛlf / (AmE), / lɛnd ˈwʌnˌsɛlf / (BrE)
(of a thing) to be suitable or adaptable for a particular purpose or use.

Examples

However, they lend themselves to abuse and inadequate control measures.

Synset Examples

This theory lends itself well to our new data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be amenable to - to be open or responsive to something; suitable for
  • Synonym: be appropriate for - to be suitable or fitting for a purpose
