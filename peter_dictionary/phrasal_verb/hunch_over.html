hunch over

1. VERB

/ hʌntʃ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / hʌntʃ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
To bend forward with rounded shoulders, especially when sitting or standing, so that the back forms a curve.

Examples

At first they were only feathers and dark indistinguishable faces and bodies, hunched over their horses' heads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slouch - to sit or stand with a drooping posture
  • Antonym: stand up straight - to stand with the back and shoulders erect
