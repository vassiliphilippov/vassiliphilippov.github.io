wash up

1. VERB

/ wɑʃ ˈʌp / (AmE), / wɒʃ ˈʌp / (BrE)
to wash your hands and face, especially before a meal or after becoming dirty

Examples

When the meal was ready, he told Jones to wash up, and going into the front room, woke the girl.

Synset Examples

She freshened up in the bathroom
