throw up

1. VERB

/ θroʊ ʌp / (AmE), / θrəʊ ʌp / (BrE)
to bring food or liquid up from your stomach and out through your mouth; to vomit

Examples

It had come as no great surprise to Matson that the hot water in the showers did n't work, that Loren Severe had thrown up all over the stairs, or that some thieving bastard of a cop had walked off with his cigarettes.

Synset Examples

After drinking too much, the students vomited
He purged continuously
The patient regurgitated the food we gave him last night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vomit - to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
  • Synonym: regurgitate - to bring swallowed food back up to the mouth
