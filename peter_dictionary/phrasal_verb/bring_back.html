Related Terms

2. VERB

/ brɪŋ bæk / (AmE), / brɪŋ bæk / (BrE)
to cause someone to regain consciousness, awareness, or attention; to restore to a previous state of mind.

Examples

No sooner would I turn my head away from the counter before he would address me, at times quite sharply, in order to bring back my attention.

Synset Examples

These pictures bring back sad memories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revive - to bring someone back to consciousness
