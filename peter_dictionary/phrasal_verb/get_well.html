get well

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt wɛl / (AmE), / ɡɛt wɛl / (BrE)
to return to good health after being ill or injured

Examples

"I hope you get well, Grandpa, and wish I see you soon.

Synset Examples

He got well fast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recover - to become healthy again after an illness or difficulty
  • Antonym: relapse - to fall back into illness after partial recovery
