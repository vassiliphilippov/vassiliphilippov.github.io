band together

1. VERB

/ bænd təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / bænd təˈɡeðə / (BrE)
to form a group or unite with others, especially for a common purpose or cause

Examples

It recognizes the fact that what helps one county helps its neighbors and that by banding together in an area-wide effort better results can be accomplished than through the go-it-alone approach.

Synset Examples

The groups banded together

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unite - to come or bring together for a common purpose

Etymology

From 'band' (noun, a group) plus 'together', reflecting the idea of gathering as a group.
