turn to

/ tɝn tə / (AmE), / tɜːn tʊ / (BrE)

My host went over and stared out the window at his peacocks; then he turned to me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: address - to speak to; to direct speech toward

/ tɝn tu / (AmE), / tɜːn tʊ / (BrE)
to direct one's interest, attention, or efforts toward something or someone, often as an alternative or resource

Examples

But make no mistake about it, the first reason people turn to camping is one of economy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: avoid - to keep away from or not turn to something or someone
