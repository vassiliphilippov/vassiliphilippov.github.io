go to bed

1. VERB

/ ɡoʊ tə bɛd / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə bɛd / (BrE)
to lie down in order to sleep, especially at night; to prepare oneself to sleep by lying in bed

Ben and Stinky went home an hour ago and people started going to bed ".
