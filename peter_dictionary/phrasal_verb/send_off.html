send off

1. VERB

/ sɛnd ɔf / (AmE), / sɛnd ɒf / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to go or be taken to a particular place or for a particular purpose; to dispatch.

Examples

"Send Fredo **off** to do this, send Fredo off to do that!" "Let Fredo take care of some Mickey Mouse nightclub somewhere."
"Send Fredo off to do this, send Fredo **off** to do that!" "Let Fredo take care of some Mickey Mouse nightclub somewhere."

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dispatch - to send someone or something to a particular place for a particular purpose
