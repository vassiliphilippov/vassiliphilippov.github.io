cook up

1. VERB

/ kʊk ʌp / (AmE), / kʊk ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare or cook something by mixing ingredients, often quickly or with improvisation

Examples

First thing I knew he was in the kitchenette cooking up the breakfast and I was handing Eileen her coffee-cup and she was lying there handsome as a queen among her courtiers.

Synset Examples

concoct a strange mixture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concoct - to prepare by combining ingredients
