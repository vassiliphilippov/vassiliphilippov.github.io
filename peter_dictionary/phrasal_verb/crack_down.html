crack down

1. VERB

/ kræk daʊn / (AmE), / kræk daʊn / (BrE)
to take strong action to suppress or prevent undesirable or illegal activities

Examples

Since McCluskey's killing, the police have cracked down on our operations.

Synset Examples

The police clamped down on illegal drugs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clamp down - to take strict or repressive measures against
