guide on

1. VERB

/ ɡaɪd ɑn / (AmE), / ɡaɪd ɒn / (BrE)
to use something as a guide or point of reference for navigation or direction

Examples

What would his guns guide on then - the North Star?
And they also had the lights of the city, the port wall lanterns, and a shore crane's spotlight to guide on.

Synset Examples

They had the lights to guide on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guide - to lead or direct; to show the way
