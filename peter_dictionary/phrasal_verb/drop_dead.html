drop dead

1. VERB

/ drɑp dɛd / (AmE), / drɒp dɛd / (BrE)
(informal) To die suddenly and unexpectedly.

Examples

Why did n't he drop dead?
A man named Lars Simon, playwright director, had expressed a wish that Anthony Payne drop dead.

Synset Examples

She died from cancer
The children perished in the fire
The patient went peacefully
The old guy kicked the bucket at the age of 102

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - to cease living; to stop being alive
