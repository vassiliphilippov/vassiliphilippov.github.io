spot promote

1. VERB

/ spɑt prəˈmoʊt / (AmE), / spɒt prəˈməʊt / (BrE)
To promote someone immediately, often without following usual procedures or delays.

Examples

Fosterite bishops, after secret conclave, announced the Church's second Major Miracle: Supreme Bishop Digby had been translated bodily to Heaven and spot-promoted to Archangel, ranking with-but-after Archangel Foster.

Synset Examples

Supreme Bishop Digby had been spot-promoted to Archangel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promote - to raise someone to a higher rank or position
