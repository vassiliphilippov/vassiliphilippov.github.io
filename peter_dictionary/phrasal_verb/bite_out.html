bite out

1. VERB

/ baɪt aʊt / (AmE), / baɪt aʊt / (BrE)
to say something sharply or tensely, often in anger or impatience

Examples

He bit out the words.
"We 're lost and burning up already ", she bit out tensely.

Synset Examples

She bit out a curse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snap - to say something quickly and irritably
