account for

1. VERB

/ əˈkaʊnt fɔr / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt fɔː / (BrE)
to be the explanation or cause of something; to provide a reason for why something happens or exists

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explain - make something clear by describing it in detail or revealing relevant facts
  • Synonym: justify - show or prove to be right or reasonable

Etymology

From Middle English 'acounte' (to reckon) and 'for' (indicating purpose or reason).
