measure up

1. VERB

/ ˈmɛʒər ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒə ʌp / (BrE)
to be good enough or meet a required standard or expectation

Examples

Traffic frequently has failed to measure up to engineers' rosy estimates.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fall short - to not reach the required standard or expectation
  • Synonym: qualify - to meet the necessary requirements or standards
