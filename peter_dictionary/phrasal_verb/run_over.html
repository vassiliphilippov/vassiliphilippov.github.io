run over

1. VERB

/ rʌn ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / rʌn ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to overflow or spill over the edge of a container.

Examples

So when the Big House filled up and ran over, the sisters-in-law found beds for everyone in their own homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overflow - to flow over the brim or limits; spill over

2. VERB

/ ˈrʌn ˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌn ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
to injure or kill someone or something by driving a vehicle over them

Examples

The hospitals contain patients trampled by elephants or run over by sports cars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knock down - to hit someone with a vehicle and cause them to fall
