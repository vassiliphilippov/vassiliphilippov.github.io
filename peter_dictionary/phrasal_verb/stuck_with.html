stuck with

1. VERB

/ stʌk wɪð / (AmE), / stʌk wɪð / (BrE)
Unable to get rid of someone or something unwanted; burdened with.

Examples

He had gotten stuck with a job too big for his imagination; he had to cling to routine, tested procedures.

Synset Examples

stuck with the tab

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lumbered with - to be burdened or encumbered with something unwanted
