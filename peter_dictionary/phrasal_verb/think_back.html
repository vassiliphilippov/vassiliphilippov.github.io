think back

1. VERB

/ θɪŋk bæk / (AmE), / θɪŋk bæk / (BrE)
to recall or remember something that happened in the past

Examples

Though his inner thoughts cringed at it, he forced himself to think back, recreating the scene in which Mae claimed to have caught him molesting the child.
Joe Purvis was thinking back many years.

Synset Examples

he remembered how he used to pick flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remember - to bring a past event or fact into one's mind
  • Antonym: forget - to fail to remember
