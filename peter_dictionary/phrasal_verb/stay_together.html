stay together

1. VERB

/ steɪ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / steɪ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
To remain loyal to and supportive of one another, especially during difficult times.

Examples

"All we have left in the world is one another, and we must stay together the way Mother wanted ", Kowalski said in telling his children of their mother's death yesterday afternoon.

Synset Examples

The two families stuck together throughout the war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stick together - remain united and support each other, especially in difficulty
  • Antonym: fall apart - break up or lose unity, especially of a group or relationship
