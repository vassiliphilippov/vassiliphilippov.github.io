give forth

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv fɔrθ / (AmE), / ɡɪv fɔːθ / (BrE)
to produce or emit something such as a sound, smell, light, or substance.

Examples

The Percussive Twenties (SP 44006) stirs pleasant memories with well-known songs of that day, and Johnny Keating's Kombo gives forth with tingling jazz in Percussive Moods (SP 44005).

Synset Examples

The chimney exhales a thick smoke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emit - to send out or give off (a sound, smell, etc.)
  • Synonym: exhale - to breathe out; to emit air or vapor
