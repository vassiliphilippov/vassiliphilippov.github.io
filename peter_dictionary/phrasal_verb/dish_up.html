dish up

1. VERB

/ dɪʃ ʌp / (AmE), / dɪʃ ʌp / (BrE)
To serve food onto plates or to present something for people to experience or enjoy.

Examples

He also dishes up 3000 snacks.

Synset Examples

We serve meals for the homeless
She dished out the soup at 8 P.M.
The entertainers served up a lively show

Related Terms

  • Synonym: serve up - to provide food or present something
  • Synonym: dish out - to distribute food or other things, often informally

Etymology

From "dish" (as a noun meaning a plate or bowl for food) and "up" as a particle emphasizing completion of the action.
