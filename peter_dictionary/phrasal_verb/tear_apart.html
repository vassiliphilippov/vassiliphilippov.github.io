tear apart

1. VERB

/ tɛr əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / tɪə əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to criticize someone or something very harshly or severely

Examples

After drastically overhauling NATO, Western leadership should turn to reducing the suspicions that tear apart the East and West.

Synset Examples

The critics panned the performance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pan - to review or criticize something very negatively
  • Antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for something
