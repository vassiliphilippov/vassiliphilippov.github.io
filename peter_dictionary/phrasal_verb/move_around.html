move around

1. VERB

/ muːv əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / muːv əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to go past or to the other side of something by moving so as to avoid it

Examples

She showed her surprise by tightening the reins and moving the gelding around so that she could get a better look at his face.

Synset Examples

turn the corner
move around the obstacle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bypass - to go around an obstacle rather than through or over it
  • Synonym: avoid - to stay away from or keep clear of something
  • Synonym: pass - to move past or go ahead of
