stay in place

1. VERB

/ steɪ ɪn pleɪs / (AmE), / steɪ ɪn pleɪs / (BrE)
to remain stationary; to not move from a particular position

Examples

He had a round, frank Irish face, creased in a jovial grin that stayed bleakly in place even when he was pumping bullets into someone's body.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: move - to change position or go from one place to another
