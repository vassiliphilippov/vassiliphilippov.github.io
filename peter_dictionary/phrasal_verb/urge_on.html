urge on

1. VERB

/ ɝdʒ ɑn / (AmE), / ɜːdʒ ɒn / (BrE)
to encourage someone to continue or do something, especially by shouting or cheering

Examples

Come on, kill me!

Synset Examples

The crowd cheered the demonstrating strikers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheer on - to support or encourage someone, especially by shouting
  • Synonym: egg on - to encourage someone to do something, often something unwise
  • Antonym: dissuade - to persuade someone not to do something
