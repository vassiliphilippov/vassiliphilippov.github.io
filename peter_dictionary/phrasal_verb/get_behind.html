get behind

1. VERB

/ ɡɛt bəˈhaɪnd / (AmE), / ɡɛt bɪˈhaɪnd / (BrE)
to fail to keep up with others or fall behind in progress or schedule

Examples

In going away Debonnie got behind several lengths, stalling at the start - she is a little fussy.

Synset Examples

But in so many other areas we still are dragging

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fall behind - to fail to keep up with others
