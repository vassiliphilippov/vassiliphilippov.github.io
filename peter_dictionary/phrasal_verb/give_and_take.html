give and take

1. VERB

/ ɡɪv ənd teɪk / (AmE), / ɡɪv ənd teɪk / (BrE)
to be willing both to compromise and to accept compromise from others; to make mutual concessions

Examples

You 're not in the big leagues, but if you can n't give and take down here what the hell do you think it 'll be like up there "?

Synset Examples

In life you have to give and take

Related Terms
