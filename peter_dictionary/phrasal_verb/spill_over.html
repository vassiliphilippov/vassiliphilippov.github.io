spill over

1. VERB

/ spɪl oʊvɚ / (AmE), / spɪl əʊvə / (BrE)
To overflow or express a feeling so strongly that it cannot be contained.

Examples

He did not spill over with hatred for the enemy.

Synset Examples

The children bubbled over with joy
My boss was bubbling over with anger

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bubble over - to express a feeling with such intensity that it cannot be contained
