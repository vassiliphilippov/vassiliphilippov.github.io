hang in

1. VERB

/ hæŋ ɪn / (AmE), / hæŋ ɪn / (BrE)
to persist in a situation; to continue with determination despite difficulties

Examples

It hung in all her day clothes, sweet and strong; sometimes when he was pressing, Winston raised her dresses to his face.

Synset Examples

he persisted to call me every night
The child persisted and kept asking questions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persevere - to continue in a course of action despite difficulty or delay in achieving success
  • Antonym: give up - to stop trying; to cease making an effort
