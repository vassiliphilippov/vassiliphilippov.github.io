plow ahead

1. VERB

/ plaʊ əˈhɛd / (AmE), / plaʊ əˈhɛd / (BrE)
to continue steadily with a task or plan, especially despite difficulty or opposition

Examples

Yet Andy plowed ahead, mouthing the inconsequential words as if they possessed real meaning, and gradually his listeners warmed to him.

Synset Examples

He went ahead with the project

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press on - to continue moving forward or making progress
  • Antonym: hesitate - to pause before proceeding, often due to uncertainty or reluctance
