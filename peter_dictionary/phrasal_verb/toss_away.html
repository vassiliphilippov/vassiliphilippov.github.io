toss away

1. VERB

/ tɑs əˈweɪ / (AmE), / tɒs əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to throw or cast something away, often carelessly or without much thought

Examples

Mike Deegan tossed his glove away and began to swing at the catcher.
The pitcher tossed his glove away and came towards Mike Deegan.

Synset Examples

Put away your worries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discard - to get rid of something as no longer useful or desirable
  • Synonym: throw away - to dispose of something by throwing it out
