mix in

1. VERB

/ mɪks ɪn / (AmE), / mɪks ɪn / (BrE)
to add one substance to another and combine them thoroughly

Examples

The spectrometer was adjusted to minimize the amount of dispersion mode mixed in with the absorption signal.
I used bright red, mixing the pigment in thoroughly before adding the hardener.

Synset Examples

At this stage of making the cake, blend in the nuts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blend in - to combine and mix so as to be indistinguishable
  • Synonym: stir in - to mix an ingredient into a substance by stirring
