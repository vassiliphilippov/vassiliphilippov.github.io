plug away

1. VERB

/ plʌɡ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / plʌɡ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
To continue working hard and steadily, especially at something difficult or tedious.

Examples

Just let the rejection slips fall where they may, and keep on plugging, and finally you will make the grade.

Synset Examples

Students must plug away at this problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persevere - to continue in a course of action despite difficulty
  • Antonym: give up - to stop trying or quit some effort
