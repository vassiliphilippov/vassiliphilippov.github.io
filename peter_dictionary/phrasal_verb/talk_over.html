talk over

1. VERB

/ tɔk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to discuss something carefully and thoroughly with someone, often before making a decision

Examples

Two weeks, a month, we talk it over again, and maybe if nothing happens meanwhile to say the cops know this and that, then we make a little deal, is n't it "?

Synset Examples

We discussed our household budget

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discuss - to talk about something with someone, often in detail

2. VERB

/ tɔk ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to discuss something thoroughly with someone, especially to reach a decision or to understand it better

Examples

Well, erm... ..why don't we have a seat, and talk it over?

Synset Examples

We discussed our household budget

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discuss - to talk about something with another person or group
  • Synonym: hash out - to discuss something in detail to resolve or decide it
  • Synonym: talk through - to discuss something thoroughly so that it is fully understood
