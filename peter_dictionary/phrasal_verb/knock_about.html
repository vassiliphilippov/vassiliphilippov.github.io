knock about

1. VERB

/ nɑk əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / nɒk əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to strike or push something or someone around forcefully or repeatedly; to be subjected to rough handling or movement

Examples

A man knocked the roulette ball about idly in its track, and another dozed at one of the card tables.

Synset Examples

Winds buffeted the tent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buffet - to strike repeatedly and violently; batter
