cross off

1. VERB

/ krɑs ɑf / (AmE), / krɒs ɒf / (BrE)
to remove (a person or thing) from a list by drawing a line through their name or entry

Examples

His name had been crossed off a list.

Synset Examples

Cross the name of the dead person off the list

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strike off - remove from a list by drawing a line through
