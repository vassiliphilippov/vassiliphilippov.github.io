fuck off

1. VERB

/ fʌk ɑf / (AmE), / fʌk ɒf / (BrE)
(vulgar) Used to tell someone very rudely to go away or leave immediately.

Examples

I said fuck off!

Synset Examples

Scram!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: go away - to leave a place or person
  • Synonym: clear off - to go away quickly (informal, British)
  • Synonym: scram - leave immediately, usually in imperative
