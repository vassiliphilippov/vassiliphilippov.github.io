fall over

1. VERB

/ fɔl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to lose balance and collapse to the ground, often forward or sideways

Examples

A rifle cracked; the square head fell over.

Synset Examples

The old woman went over without a sound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fall down - to move from a higher to a lower position, typically hitting the ground
