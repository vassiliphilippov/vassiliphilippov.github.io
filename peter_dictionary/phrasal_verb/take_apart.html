take apart

1. VERB

/ teɪk əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / teɪk əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to separate something into its individual pieces or parts; to disassemble.

Examples

There was no weapon Early could not take apart and reassemble blind-folded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disassemble - to take something apart into separate pieces
  • Antonym: assemble - to put something together from parts or pieces
