discretionary

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈskrɛʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɛʃənəri / (BrE)
able to be decided or used according to one's own judgment; not fixed by rules.

Examples

Has your company thought through its strategy in this whole "discretionary buying "area?

Synset Examples

The commission has discretionary power to award extra funds

Etymology

From 'discretion' + '-ary', based on Latin 'discretio' (separation, discernment).
