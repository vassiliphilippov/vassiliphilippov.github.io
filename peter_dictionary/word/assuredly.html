assuredly

1. ADV

/ əˈʃʊrədli / (AmE), / əˈʃʊərɪdli / (BrE)
Without a doubt; certainly; used to emphasize that something is definitely true.

Examples

The various team sports assuredly have their place in every school, and they are important to proper physical development.
Most assuredly ideas are invaluable.
Assuredly in our political campaigns there is freedom to think, to examine any and all issues, and to speak without restraint.

Synset Examples

the grammar schools were assuredly not intended for the gentry alone

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certainly - without any doubt; surely
  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without doubt; certainly
  • Antonym: doubtfully - not confidently; with doubt

Etymology

From assure + -ly, formed on the pattern of adverbs from adjectives ending in -ed.
