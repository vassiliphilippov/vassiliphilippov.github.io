sticking

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
projecting or extending out above or beyond a surface or boundary

Examples

Without further discussion he appeared the next morning with a pile of boards sticking over the end of his light truck and proceeded with the paneling, which he then stained and waxed according to his taste.

Synset Examples

the jutting limb of a tree
massive projected buttresses
his protruding ribs
a pile of boards sticking over the end of his truck

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protruding - projecting or extending beyond a surface
  • Synonym: jutting - extending out above a surface
  • Antonym: receding - moving or sloping backward, opposite of sticking out
