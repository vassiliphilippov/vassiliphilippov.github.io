tourist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊrɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst / (BrE)
A person who travels to places for pleasure and sightseeing, rather than for business or work.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveler - a person who is traveling or who often travels
  • Antonym: local - a person who lives in a particular place (not a visitor or tourist)

Etymology

From French *touriste*, from *tour* (a turn, journey).
