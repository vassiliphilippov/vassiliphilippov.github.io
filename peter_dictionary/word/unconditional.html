unconditional

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kənˈdɪʃ.ən.əl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈdɪʃ.ən.əl / (BrE)
Not subject to any conditions or limitations; absolute.

Examples

"You get your unconditional release as of today ".

Synset Examples

unconditional surrender

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conditional - subject to conditions or requirements

Etymology

From un- (not) + conditional, based on condition.
