clothes

1. NOUN

/ kloʊðz / (AmE), / kləʊðz / (BrE)
items worn to cover the body; garments collectively.

Examples

Etymology

Plural of 'cloth,' from Old English 'clāthas' (plural of 'clāth,' meaning cloth or garment).
