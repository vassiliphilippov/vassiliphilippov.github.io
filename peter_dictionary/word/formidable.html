formidable

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrmɪdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmɪdəbəl / (BrE)
inspiring respect or fear by being impressively large, powerful, intense, or capable

Examples

"We "were Bill Garrett of the National Geographic Illustrations Staff, whose three cameras and eight lenses made him look as formidable as any fighting man we met; Boun My, our interpreter; and myself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intimidating - causing fear or nervousness; daunting
  • Synonym: daunting - seeming difficult to deal with; intimidating
  • Antonym: weak - lacking physical strength or power

Etymology

From Latin formidabilis (causing fear), from formidare (to fear).
