unveiling

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈveɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈveɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a ceremony or occasion at which something, such as a work of art or a monument, is shown or introduced to the public for the first time

Examples

Plans continue for the city's 200th birthday... ... as Mayor Borg announced today, the unveiling...

Synset Examples

he attended the unveiling of the statue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presentation - the act of presenting something to the public

Etymology

From 'unveil' (to remove a veil), itself from 'un-' (not) + 'veil' (Middle English, from Old French 'veile', from Latin 'velum' meaning 'covering').
