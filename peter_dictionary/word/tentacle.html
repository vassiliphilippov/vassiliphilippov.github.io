tentacle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛn.tə.kəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.tə.kəl / (BrE)
A means or influence that spreads out and exerts power or control, often in a subtle or far-reaching way.

Examples

The domestic tentacles even extend to the choice of a major field.

Synset Examples

caught in the tentacles of organized crime

Etymology

From Latin tentāculum (feeler, holdfast), from tentāre (to feel, try).
