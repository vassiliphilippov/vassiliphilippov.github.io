megakaryocytic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛɡəˌkæriəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡəˌkæriəˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of megakaryocytes, the large bone marrow cells responsible for producing platelets.

Examples

The cells of the erythroid, myeloid, and megakaryocytic series were normal except for their numbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: megakaryocyte - a large bone marrow cell that produces platelets

Etymology

From mega- (large) + karyon (Greek for 'kernel, nucleus') + -cytic (relating to cells).
