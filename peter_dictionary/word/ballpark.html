ballpark

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌpɑrk / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌpɑːk / (BrE)
A stadium or outdoor facility where baseball games or other ball games are played.

Examples

Everyone in the ball park seemed to be standing and shouting.

Synset Examples

take me out to the ballpark

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stadium - A large structure for sporting events with tiers of seats for spectators

Etymology

From 'ball' + 'park', referring to a park or stadium for playing ball games, especially baseball (early 20th century, US).
