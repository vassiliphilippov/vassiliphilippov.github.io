crippled

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪpəld / (AmE), / ˈkrɪpəld / (BrE)
Unable to use the feet or legs properly due to injury or disability (now considered offensive when referring to people).

Examples

The two kids were together a lot, they were having some kind of teen-age affair - God knows how far that had gone - and the kid's crippled.

Synset Examples

a crippled soldier
a game leg

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disabled - Having a physical or mental condition that limits movements, senses, or activities

Etymology

From Middle English 'crypel', from Old English 'crypel', related to 'cripple'.
