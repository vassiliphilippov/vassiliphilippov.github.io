nonproductive

1. ADJ

/ nɑn.prəˈdʌk.tɪv / (AmE), / nɒn.prəˈdʌk.tɪv / (BrE)
Not producing useful results, goods, or profit; not directly involved in creating something of value.

Examples

Would early retirement of non-productive, disabled employees reduce the number of make-work jobs?

Synset Examples

nonproductive labor

Related Terms

  • Antonym: productive - producing or capable of producing goods, results, or profit

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' (not) to 'productive'.
