gunstock

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌn.stɑk / (AmE), / ˈɡʌn.stɒk / (BrE)
the handle or wooden part at the rear of a rifle or shotgun, held against the shoulder when firing

Examples

I put a special tape on the trigger and the butt.

Synset Examples

the rifle had been fitted with a special stock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stock - the main supporting part of a rifle or similar firearm
  • Synonym: butt - the thick end of the stock of a gun

Etymology

Formed from 'gun' + 'stock', where 'stock' means a supporting part or handle.
