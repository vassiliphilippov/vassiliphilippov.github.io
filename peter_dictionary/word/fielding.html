fielding

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfiːldɪŋ / (BrE)
The action or skill of catching or stopping the ball and throwing it to teammates when playing as a fielder in sports such as baseball or cricket.

Examples

The rangy, Albany (Cal.) native, a surprise slugging sensation for the Flock last year as well as a defensive whiz, set "improved fielding "as his 1961 goal.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: batting - the action or skill of hitting the ball with a bat in baseball or cricket

Etymology

From the verb 'field' (to catch or stop the ball) + -ing.
