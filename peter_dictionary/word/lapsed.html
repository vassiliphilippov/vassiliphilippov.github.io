lapsed

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæpst / (AmE), / ˈlæpst / (BrE)
No longer active, involved, or practicing, especially in a particular activity, religion, or profession.

Examples

It should be admitted, too, that there is a good percentage of lapsed or nonchurchgoing Catholics (one paper writes 50 per cent).

Synset Examples

a lapsed Catholic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inactive - not engaged in activity; idle
  • Antonym: practicing - actively involved or engaged, especially in a religion or activity

Etymology

Past participle of 'lapse', from Latin 'lapsus' (a slipping or falling).
