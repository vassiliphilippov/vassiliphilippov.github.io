primly

1. ADV

/ ˈprɪmli / (AmE), / ˈprɪmli / (BrE)
In a demure, excessively proper, or precise manner.

Examples

Asked why, he replied primly:

Synset Examples

the new teacher alienates the children by behaving prissily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prissily - in an excessively proper or fussy manner
  • Synonym: demurely - in a modest and reserved manner

Etymology

From 'prim' (neat, precise) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
