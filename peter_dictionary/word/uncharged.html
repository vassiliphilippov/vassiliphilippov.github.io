uncharged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
(of a particle, object, or system) having no electric charge.

Examples

This is a theoretically complicated dipole interaction which causes any extremely small uncharged particle to agglomerate with other small uncharged particles, or to stick to an uncharged surface.

Synset Examples

an uncharged particle
an uncharged battery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neutral - having no net electric charge
  • Antonym: charged - having an electric charge

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'charged' (given an electric charge).
