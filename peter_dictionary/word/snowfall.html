snowfall

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnoʊˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊfɔːl / (BrE)
An occasion or period when snow falls from the sky; the amount of snow that falls during such a time.

Examples

This will be a problem in areas of heavy snowfall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snowstorm - a storm with heavy snow and wind

Etymology

From 'snow' + 'fall', formed in English.
