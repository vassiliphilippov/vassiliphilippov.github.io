tourism

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʊr.ɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˈtʊə.rɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The business and practice of providing services and facilities for people traveling for pleasure.

Examples

[TRANSLATOR TRANSLATING] PRESIDENT: Mr. Michael Corleone of Nevada, [TRANSLATOR TRANSLATING] representing our associates in tourism and leisure activities.

Synset Examples

Tourism is a major business in Bermuda

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travel - the act of traveling, especially for pleasure
  • Synonym: hospitality - the friendly and generous reception and entertainment of guests or strangers

Etymology

Formed from 'tour' + '-ism', first attested in the early 19th century.
