axial

1. ADJ

/ ˈæk.si.əl / (AmE), / ˈæk.si.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or situated around an axis, especially the axis of rotation.

Examples

This tube could be adjusted in its axial direction by an electric drive to establish the required electrode spacing.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: radial - relating to or arranged like rays or radii from a central point

Etymology

From Latin axis (axis) + -al (suffix forming adjectives).
