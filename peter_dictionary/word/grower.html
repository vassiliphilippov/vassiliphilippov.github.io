grower

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A person or company that cultivates plants, fruits, or crops for sale or use, often as a business.

Examples

Go to a reputable grower, preferably a pansy specialist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • Synonym: planter - someone who plants crops, especially on a large scale
