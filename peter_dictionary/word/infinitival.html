infinitival

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˌfɪnɪˈtaɪvəl / (AmE), / ɪnˌfɪnɪˈtʌɪv(ə)l / (BrE)
Relating to or formed with the infinitive form of a verb.

Examples

He wants us to send Michael to hear the proposition.

Synset Examples

infinitival clause

Related Terms

  • Synonym: infinitive - the basic form of a verb, typically preceded by 'to' in English, without inflection for tense or subject

Etymology

From 'infinitive' + '-al', indicating relating to the infinitive form.
