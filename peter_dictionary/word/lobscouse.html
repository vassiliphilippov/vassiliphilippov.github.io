lobscouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑbˌskaʊs / (AmE), / ˈlɒbˌskaʊs / (BrE)
A stew of meat, vegetables, and hardtack, traditionally eaten by sailors.

Examples

He dumped the pan of crumbled hardtack into the boiling pot of lobscouse.

Etymology

Possibly from Low German 'Labskaus', a seaman's dish.
