hyperbolic

1. ADJ

/ haɪ.pɚˈbɑ.lɪk / (AmE), / haɪ.pəˈbɒ.lɪk / (BrE)
(of language or style) expressed in exaggerated terms or enlarged beyond truth or reasonableness; exaggerated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exaggerated - described as larger, better, or worse than in reality
  • Antonym: understated - presented or expressed in a subtle and restrained way

Etymology

From Greek hyperbolikos (excessive), from hyperbolē (exaggeration, throwing over or beyond).
