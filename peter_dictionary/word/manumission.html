manumission

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn.jəˈmɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌman.jʊˈmɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the formal act of freeing a person from slavery; emancipation

Examples

I have held your papers of manumission since I married Mr. Lattimer ".

Synset Examples

he believed in the manumission of the slaves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emancipation - the process of being set free from legal, social, or political restrictions; liberation (especially from slavery)

Etymology

From Latin manumissio, from manus (hand) + missio (a sending).
