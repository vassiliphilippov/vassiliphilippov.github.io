alienated

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪliəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈeɪliəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
Feeling isolated, estranged, or disconnected from a group or society.

Examples

He is, like Phillip Marlowe, too alienated to be reliable.

Synset Examples

anomic loners musing over their fate
we live in an age of rootless alienated people

Related Terms

  • Antonym: connected - feeling involved with or part of a group or society
  • Synonym: estranged - feeling alienated or separated from others

Etymology

From Latin alienatus, past participle of alienare (to estrange).
