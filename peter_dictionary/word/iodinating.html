iodinating

1. ADJ

/ ˈaɪ.əˌdeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.əˌdeɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a substance or process) having the property of combining, or causing to combine, with iodine.

Examples

It is not clear whether this system bears any resemblance to the in vivo iodinating mechanism, and a system generating peroxide has not been identified in thyroid tissue.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: oxidizing - combining or causing to combine with oxygen

Etymology

From 'iodine' + '-ating', patterning after chemical terminology like 'oxidating'.
