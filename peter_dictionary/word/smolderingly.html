smolderingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsmoʊldərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsməʊldərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner showing barely suppressed anger or strong emotion.

Examples

"What happens when there's no more water "? she asked smolderingly.

Synset Examples

`I can't wait,' she answered smolderingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angrily - in a manner showing anger
  • Antonym: calmly - in a manner showing calmness

Etymology

From 'smoldering' + '-ly', where 'smolder' comes from Middle English 'smolderen' (to smolder).
