leisurely

1. ADV

/ ˈliʒərli / (AmE), / ˈleʒəli / (BrE)
In an unhurried and relaxed manner.

Examples

Pat let him go, following more leisurely.

Synset Examples

read the manual at your leisure
he traveled leisurely

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hurriedly - in a quick or rushed manner
  • Synonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly

Etymology

From 'leisure' + '-ly', patterned after the adjective 'leisurely'.
