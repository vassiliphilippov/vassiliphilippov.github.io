interpretable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtɜrprətəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprɪtəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be understood or explained; capable of being interpreted.

Examples

This something leads to a conception of an over-all Social Plan with a meaning interpretable in terms of ultimate ends; for example, a plan that fulfills the will of God, which advances the Kingdom of God, which involves social life as part of the Grand Design.

Synset Examples

explainable phenomena

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explainable - able to be explained or accounted for
  • Antonym: incomprehensible - impossible to understand

Etymology

From 'interpret' + '-able', influenced by Latin 'interpretabilis' (that may be interpreted).
