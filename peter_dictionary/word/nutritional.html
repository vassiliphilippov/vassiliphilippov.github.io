nutritional

1. ADJ

/ nuˈtrɪʃənəl / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth; of or concerning nutrition

Examples

Good taste and versatility, plus safety from spray poisons would be enough to recommend the frequent use of such a fruit, even if its nutritional values were limited.

Synset Examples

nutritional information

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutritive - providing nourishment; related to nutrition

Etymology

From 'nutrition' + '-al'. 'Nutrition' from Latin 'nutrīre' (to nourish).
