pistoleer

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɪstəˈlɪr / (AmE), / ˌpɪstəˈlɪə / (BrE)
A person armed with a pistol, especially a soldier or fighter so equipped.

Examples

Until 1924 O' Banion pistoleers and knuckle-duster bullyboys had kept his North Side domain solidly Democratic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunman - a person who uses or carries a gun

Etymology

From French 'pistolet' (little pistol) plus '-eer' (denoting a person associated with or concerned with something).
