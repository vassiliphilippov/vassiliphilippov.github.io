rollickingly

1. ADV

/ ˈrɑːlɪkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈrɒlɪkɪŋli / (BrE)
In a carefree, lively, and exuberant manner.

Examples

She was rollickingly happy.

Synset Examples

she was rollickingly happy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carefreely - in a carefree, unconcerned way
  • Synonym: joyfully - in a very happy or joyful way

Etymology

From "rollicking" + "-ly"; "rollicking" possibly from "frolic" by alteration.
