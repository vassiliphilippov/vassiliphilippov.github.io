dumb

1. ADJ

/ dʌm / (AmE), / dʌm / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes offensive) Lacking intelligence; slow to understand or learn; stupid.

Examples

Every man who dabbles in the market to make a little easy money on the side and suffers losses could at the time hardly face his wife who was wondering how her husband could be so dumb.
I was the first to get my squad on the ball, and anybody thinkin it was easy is pretty damn dumb.
All those dumb 8 - Balls croaked.
You can do anything with these dumb fucks if you know how.
See, he's already snapping it up, the dumb jerk * * h Coughlin grinned, feeling supremely on top of things * * h He watched the snow once again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or sense; foolish
  • Synonym: dim - not intelligent; slow to understand
  • Antonym: intelligent - having high mental capacity

Etymology

Old English dumb (unable to speak), later broadened to mean 'stupid.'
