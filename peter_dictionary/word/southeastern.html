southeastern

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaʊθˈiːstərn / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈiːstən / (BrE)
Located in or facing toward the southeast.

Examples

The focus of novelty in this world now lay in the south-eastern districts of the Greek mainland, and by 800 virtually the entire Aegean, always excepting its northern shores, had accepted the Geometric style of pottery.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northeastern - located in or facing toward the northeast
  • Antonym: southwestern - located in or facing toward the southwest
  • Antonym: northwestern - located in or facing toward the northwest
