worshipper

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɜːrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
A person who participates in religious worship or has religious faith.

Examples

A knot of old worshippers, chiefly women, listened weekly to a sermon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: believer - a person who has religious faith

Etymology

From Middle English 'worschiper', from 'worship' + '-er'.
