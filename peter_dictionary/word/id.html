ID

1. NOUN

/ aɪ ˈdi / (AmE), / aɪ ˈdiː / (BrE)
an official card or document that proves who you are, such as a driver's license or passport

Examples

He had all the proper ID, driver's license, birth certificate, social security card.

Synset Examples

you had to show your ID in order to get in

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identification - official proof of a person's identity

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'identification'.
