Heaven

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvən / (AmE), / ˈhɛvən / (BrE)
In various religions, the place regarded as the dwelling of God, angels, and the blessed after death.

Examples

Michael, do you believe in God, the Father, Creator of Heaven and Earth?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paradise - place of perfect happiness associated with the afterlife in many religions
  • Antonym: hell - place of suffering or punishment after death in some religions

Etymology

From Old English heofon, originally meaning 'sky' or 'firmament', later 'abode of God'.
