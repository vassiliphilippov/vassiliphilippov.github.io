wide-ranging

1. ADJ

/ waɪd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / waɪd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
Including or affecting a large variety of things; covering a broad area or scope.

Examples

The first is a wide-ranging sample of successful tonal analyses.

Synset Examples

the pianist's wide-ranging repertoire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensive - including all or nearly all elements or aspects
  • Antonym: limited - restricted in extent, amount, or scope
