modeled

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːdəld / (AmE), / ˈmɒdəld / (BrE)
(of a face or body) having features or form that appear finely shaped or sculpted, like a work of sculpture.

Examples

She smiled, and the teeth gleamed in her beautifully modeled olive face.

Synset Examples

her finely modeled features
rendered with...vivid sculptural effect
the sculpturesque beauty of the athletes' bodies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculpted - carved or shaped as if by sculpture

Etymology

From 'model' + '-ed', meaning shaped like a model or sculpture.
