oftentimes

1. ADV

/ ˈɔf.tənˌtaɪmz / (AmE), / ˈɒf.tənˌtaɪmz / (BrE)
On many occasions; frequently.

Examples

It is really as though the Russians have seen in this character the oftentimes underlying vitality and courage of supposed buffoons.

Synset Examples

we often met over a cup of coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: often - frequently; on many occasions
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; infrequently

Etymology

From Middle English 'oftetimes', alteration of 'ofttimes'.
