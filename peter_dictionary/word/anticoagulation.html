anticoagulation

1. NOUN

/ æn.tiˌkoʊ.əˌɡjuˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / æn.tiˌkəʊ.əˌɡjuːˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the prevention of blood clotting, typically by administering anticoagulant drugs

Examples

Therapy included digitalization and anticoagulation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anticoagulant - a drug that prevents blood coagulation

Etymology

From anti- (against) + coagulation (clotting).
