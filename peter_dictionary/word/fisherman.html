fisherman

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃəmən / (BrE)
A person whose job is to catch fish, especially as a commercial activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angler - one who fishes with a rod and line

Etymology

From Middle English 'fyssherman', from 'fish' + 'man'.
