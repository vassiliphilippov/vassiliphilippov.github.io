Southwest

1. PROPN

/ ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈwɛst / (BrE)
the southwestern region of the United States, generally including New Mexico, Arizona, Texas, Nevada, California, and sometimes Utah and Colorado

Examples

The chief literary antecedents of the Snopes clan appeared in the realistic, humorous writing which originated in the South and the Southwest in the three decades before the Civil War.

Etymology

From Old English 'sūð' (south) + 'west' (west), indicating the direction and by extension, the region.
