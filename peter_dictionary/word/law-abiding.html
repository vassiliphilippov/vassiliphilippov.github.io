law-abiding

1. ADJ

/ lɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / lɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
Obeying and respecting the laws and rules of society.

Examples

Because I can state from my own knowledge and experience that Italian-Americans are among the most loyal, most law-abiding, patriotic, hard-working American citizens in this land.

Synset Examples

law-abiding citizens
observant of the speed limit

Related Terms

  • Antonym: criminal - person who breaks the law

Etymology

From 'law' + present participle of 'abide,' meaning to conform or remain faithful to.
