sewing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of joining or attaching things, especially fabrics, with stitches using a needle and thread.

Examples

writes: What makes my hands numb when sewing?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stitching - the process of sewing or joining by stitches
  • Degree Higher: embroidery - the art or process of decorating fabric with needle and thread

Etymology

From Middle English *sewinge*, from sew (to join by stitches) + -ing.
