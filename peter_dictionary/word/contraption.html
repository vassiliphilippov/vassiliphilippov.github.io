contraption

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtræpʃən / (AmE), / kənˈtræpʃən / (BrE)
a machine or device, especially one that looks strange or complicated and is often considered makeshift or improvised

Examples

He oughta be able to build a new house with all them contraptions ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gadget - a small mechanical or electronic device or tool
  • Synonym: device - an object or piece of equipment made for a particular purpose

Etymology

Origin unknown; possibly an alteration of 'contrivance' or a blend with 'trap'.
