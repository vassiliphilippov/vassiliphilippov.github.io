forgotten

1. ADJ

/ fərˈɡɑtən / (AmE), / fəˈɡɒtn̩ / (BrE)
Not remembered or noticed; neglected or overlooked.

Examples

It was burning away, forgotten.

Synset Examples

her aching muscles forgotten she danced all night
he was scolded for his forgotten chores

Related Terms

  • Antonym: remembered - recalled or kept in mind
  • Synonym: neglected - not given proper attention

Etymology

Past participle of 'forget', from Old English *forgietan*.
