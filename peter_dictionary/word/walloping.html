walloping

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɑːləpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɒləpɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal) Very large; extremely big or impressive in size or amount.

Examples

Willie's wonderful walloping Sunday - four home runs - served merely to emphasize how happy he is to be playing for Alvin Dark.

Synset Examples

a thumping loss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: huge - extremely large in size or amount
  • Synonym: gigantic - very large; like a giant

Etymology

From the verb 'wallop' (to strike with a resounding blow), figuratively extended to mean 'strikingly large'.
