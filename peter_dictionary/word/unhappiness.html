unhappiness

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈhæpinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhæpinəs / (BrE)
The state of not being happy; the experience of sadness or dissatisfaction.

Examples

His face was clouded with unhappiness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sadness - the feeling of being sad
  • Antonym: happiness - the state of being happy

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'happiness'.
