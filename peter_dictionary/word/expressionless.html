expressionless

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈsprɛʃənləs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃənlɪs / (BrE)
(of a person or their face) showing no emotion or feeling; deliberately impassive.

Examples

The scar looked pure white in the half darkness; his eyes were black and deep-set, and expressionless.
Once again, he shook his head, kept his face expressionless and his voice very calm, and had a strongly supported alibi ready.

Synset Examples

deadpan humor
his face remained expressionless as the verdict was read

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impassive - showing no emotion
  • Synonym: deadpan - deliberately impassive or emotionless
  • Antonym: animated - full of expression or lively

Etymology

From 'expression' + '-less', meaning 'without expression'.
