scholastically

1. ADV

/ skəˈlæstɪkli / (AmE), / skəˈlæstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to schools, education, or academic activities.

Examples

But he was scholastically ineligible in 1959 and merely present last season.

Synset Examples

scholastically apt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: academically - in a manner relating to education or scholarship

Etymology

From scholastic (from Medieval Latin scholasticus, from Greek scholastikos) + -ally.
