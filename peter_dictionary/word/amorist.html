amorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmərɪst / (AmE), / ˈamərɪst / (BrE)
A person who is devoted to love or lovemaking, especially one who writes about love.

Examples

The jaded amorist conjured up pictures of the blasphemous rites with relish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic - someone who is idealistic or sentimental about love

Etymology

From Latin 'amor' (love) + '-ist' (one who practices).
