muddleheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌd.əlˌhɛd.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈmʌd.əlˌhɛd.ɪd / (BrE)
Having a confused or muddled way of thinking; lacking clear thought or judgment.

Examples

One more muddleheaded play like that one and they 'd be leading him away.

Synset Examples

blathering like the addlepated nincompoop that you are
a confused puddingheaded, muddleheaded fellow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addlebrained - stupid and confused
  • Antonym: clearheaded - thinking in a clear and sensible way

Etymology

From 'muddle' (to mix up in confusion) + 'headed'.
