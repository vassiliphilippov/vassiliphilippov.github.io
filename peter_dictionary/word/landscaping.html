landscaping

1. NOUN

/ ˈlændˌskeɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlæn(d)skeɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of arranging and modifying the features of an area of land for aesthetic or practical purposes, especially as a profession.

Examples

Some shrubs may be of good landscaping value, other areas of brush may need to be cleared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landscape gardening - the art or process of arranging gardens and grounds

Etymology

From 'landscape' + '-ing', where 'landscape' comes from Dutch 'landschap' (region, scenery).
