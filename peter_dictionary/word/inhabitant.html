inhabitant

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈhæbɪtənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈhæbɪtənt / (BrE)
a person or animal that lives in a particular place

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resident - person who lives somewhere permanently or for a long time
  • Synonym: occupant - person who lives in or occupies a place
  • Antonym: visitor - person who is temporarily in a place

Etymology

From Latin *inhabitare* (to dwell in).
