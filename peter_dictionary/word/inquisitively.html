inquisitively

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈkwɪzətɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwɪzɪtɪvli / (BrE)
In a curious or questioning manner; showing an eager interest in finding out things or asking questions.

Examples

[STATES HIS NAME IN ITALIAN] [REPEATS INQUISITIVELY] [LAUGHS]

Synset Examples

the baby looked around curiously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curiously - in a curious or interested manner

Etymology

From inquisitive + -ly; 'inquisitive' from Late Latin inquisitivus (fond of searching).
