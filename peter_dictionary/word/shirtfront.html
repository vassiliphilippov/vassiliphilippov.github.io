shirtfront

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɝːtˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
The front part of a shirt, especially the portion that is visible when worn with a jacket.

Examples

He backed Jess into a corner, grabbed a handful of the man's shirtfront, and drew back his right fist.

Synset Examples

he had spilled catsup on his shirtfront

Etymology

Formed from 'shirt' + 'front'.
