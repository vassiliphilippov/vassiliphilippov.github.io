weasel

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːzəl / (AmE), / ˈwiːzəl / (BrE)
A small carnivorous mammal with a slender, elongated body, short legs, and a long neck, belonging to the genus Mustela.

Examples

GOES THE WEASEL] [PEOPLE LAUGHING]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stoat - a similar small carnivorous mammal, also called ermine

Etymology

Old English *weosule*, from Proto-Germanic *wisulon*.
