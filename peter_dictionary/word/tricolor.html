tricolor

1. ADJ

/ ˈtraɪˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈtraɪˌkʌlə / (BrE)
Having or involving three colors; composed of three distinct colors.

Examples

Three separate amplifiers then drive a 21 - inch tricolor tube.

Synset Examples

trichromatic vision
a trichromatic printing process
trichromatic staining is the staining of tissue samples differentially in three colors
tricolor plumage
a tricolor process in photography

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bicolor - having or involving two colors
  • Degree Lower: monochrome - having or involving one color

Etymology

From Latin 'tri-' (three) + 'color' (color).
