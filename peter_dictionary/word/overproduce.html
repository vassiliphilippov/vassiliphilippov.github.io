overproduce

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvərprəˈdus / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəprəˈdjuːs / (BrE)
to produce more of something than is needed or wanted

Examples

In fact, over the years, the American farmer's capacity to over-produce has cost the taxpayers a large dollar.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underproduce - to produce too little or insufficiently

Etymology

From the prefix over- (excessively) + produce.
