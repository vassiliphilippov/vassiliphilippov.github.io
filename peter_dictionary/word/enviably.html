enviably

1. ADV

/ ˈɛnviəbli / (AmE), / ˈɛnviəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes envy; in such a way as to be desirable or admirable to others.

Examples

Over the rapidly diminishing outline of a jump seat piled high with luggage Herry's black brushcut was just discernible, near, or enviably near that spot where - hidden - more delicately textured, most beautifully tinted hair must still be streaming back in cool, oh cool wind sweetly perfumed with sagebrush and yucca flowers and engine fumes.

Synset Examples

she was enviably fluent in French

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enviable - deserving to be envied; desirable or admirable

Etymology

From 'enviable' + '-ly', with 'enviable' from Old French *enviable*, from Latin *invidiabilis* ('enviable').
