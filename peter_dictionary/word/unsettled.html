unsettled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɛt.əld / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛt.əld / (BrE)
not yet resolved, decided, or certain; still open to question or doubt

Examples

While most of his beliefs were still unsettled, he knew that he did not believe in killing.

Synset Examples

an unsettled issue
an unsettled state of mind

Related Terms

  • Antonym: settled - resolved, decided, or stable
  • Synonym: undecided - not yet having made a decision

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'settled', past participle of 'settle'.
