grudgingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows unwillingness or reluctance.

Examples

She took it grudgingly, her dark eyes baleful as they met his.

Synset Examples

he grudgingly agreed to have a drink in a hotel close by

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reluctantly - in a hesitant or unwilling way

Etymology

From 'grudging' + '-ly', ultimately from Middle English 'grucchen' (to murmur, grumble).
