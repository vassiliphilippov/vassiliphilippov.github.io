sterilize

1. VERB

/ ˈstɛr.əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɛr.ɪ.laɪz / (BrE)
to make something free from bacteria or other living microorganisms, especially by using heat or chemicals

Examples

The brushes can be cleaned and sterilized by boiling and are detachable so that every member of the family can have his own.
B' dikkat was sterilizing his knives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disinfect - to clean something using chemicals to kill bacteria

Etymology

From French stériliser, from Latin sterilis (unfruitful, barren).
