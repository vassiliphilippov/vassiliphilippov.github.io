odious

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊdiəs / (AmE), / ˈəʊdiəs / (BrE)
Extremely unpleasant; repulsive or deserving of hatred.

Examples

Well, the odious little toad went along chivying animals and humans who could n't retaliate, and in due course, as was inevitable, overreached himself.

Synset Examples

abominable treatment of prisoners
detestable vices
execrable crimes
consequences odious to those you govern

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detestable - deserving intense dislike
  • Synonym: abominable - causing moral revulsion
  • Antonym: delightful - giving great pleasure or satisfaction

Etymology

From Latin 'odiosus' (hateful), from 'odium' (hatred).
