unwittingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈwɪt̬ɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
Without being aware or intending to; unintentionally or unknowingly.

Examples

You have unwittingly set in motion forces so malign, so vindictive, that it would be downright inhumane of me not to warn you about them.
Frequently, wittingly or unwittingly, price consciousness has been fostered by manufacturers, distributors, and dealers.

Synset Examples

he unwittingly deleted the references

Related Terms

  • Antonym: knowingly - with full knowledge or intention
  • Antonym: intentionally - with intention or purpose

Etymology

From 'unwitting' + '-ly'; 'unwitting' from Old English 'witan' (to know).
