cooccurring

1. ADJ

/ koʊ.əˈkɝr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / kəʊ.əˈkɜː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
Happening or existing at the same time as something else.

Examples

The problem is to disentangle the linguistic features of pitch from the co-occurring nonlinguistic features.

Synset Examples

a series of coincident events

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simultaneous - happening or done at the same time
  • Synonym: coincident - happening at the same place or time

Etymology

From co- (together) and occur (to happen), patterned after 'co-occur'.
