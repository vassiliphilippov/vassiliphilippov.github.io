determinedly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈtɝːmɪndli / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪndli / (BrE)
In a manner showing firmness or resolve; with determination.

Examples

Many potters clung to the past the more determinedly as they were confronted with radically new ideas; the poet of the Iliad deliberately archaized.

Synset Examples

he clung to the past determinedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resolutely - in a determined way
  • Antonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant way, without determination

Etymology

From 'determined' + '-ly'.
