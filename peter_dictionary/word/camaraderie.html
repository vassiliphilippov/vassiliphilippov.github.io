camaraderie

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæməˈrɑdəri / (AmE), / ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri / (BrE)
a feeling of friendly trust, goodwill, and mutual support among people who spend a lot of time together

Examples

There was a great sense of camaraderie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendship - close and supportive relationship between friends
  • Antonym: hostility - unfriendly or antagonistic relationship

Etymology

From French 'camaraderie', based on 'camarade' (comrade).
