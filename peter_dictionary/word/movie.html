movie

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
A film; a story or event recorded by a camera as a set of moving images shown in a theater, on television, or on the internet, typically for entertainment.

"Our interior line and our linebackers played exceptionally well ", said Stram Monday after he and his staff reviewed movies of the game.

Etymology
