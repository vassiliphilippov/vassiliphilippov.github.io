causally

1. ADV

/ ˈkɔzəli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːzəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves a cause or causes.

Examples

These desires presuppose a sense of causally efficacious powers in which one is involved, some working for one's good, others threatening ill.

Synset Examples

causally efficacious powers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: causal - relating to or acting as a cause

Etymology

From 'causal' + '-ly', with 'causal' from Latin 'causalis', relating to a cause.
