maleness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being male; the set of characteristics regarded as typical of men or boys

Examples

"A marriage can survive almost any kind of stress except an open and direct challenge to the husband's maleness ", declares Dr. Calderone.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: femaleness - the quality or state of being female

Etymology

From 'male' + the suffix '-ness' indicating a quality or state.
