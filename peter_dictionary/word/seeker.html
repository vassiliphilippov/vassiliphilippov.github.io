seeker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːkər / (AmE), / ˈsiːkə / (BrE)
A person who searches for or tries to find or obtain something, especially knowledge, truth, or spiritual fulfillment.

Examples

"They are also honest seekers after truth.

Synset Examples

they are seekers after truth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: searcher - a person who searches for something
  • Synonym: finder - a person who finds something

Etymology

From Middle English seker, from seek + -er.
