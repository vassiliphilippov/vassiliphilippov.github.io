foppish

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑːpɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɒpɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a man or his dress) excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance in a way that is considered vain or affected.

Examples

The Ivy League enjoys no easy dominion here, and the boys are as likely to dress in rather foppish Continental fashion, or even in nondescript working class manner, as they are in the restrained, button-down Ivy way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandy - a man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance

Etymology

From 'fop', an English term for a man excessively concerned with his appearance, with the suffix '-ish'.
