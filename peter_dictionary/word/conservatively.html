conservatively

1. ADV

/ kənˈsɝː.və.tɪv.li / (AmE), / kənˈsɜː.və.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that is cautious, moderate, or avoiding excess; in keeping with traditional values or established conventions.

Examples

White shirted and conservatively cravated drivers stared conspicuously toward the eastern horizon and past my supplicating and accusing gaze.

Synset Examples

we estimated the number of demonstrators conservatively at 200,000.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cautiously - in a cautious manner
  • Antonym: radically - in a radical or extreme manner (opposite)

Etymology

From 'conservative' + '-ly'. 'Conservative' from Latin 'conservare' (to preserve).
