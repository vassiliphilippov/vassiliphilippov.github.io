battle-ax

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬l ˌæks / (AmE), / ˈbætl ˌæks / (BrE)
A large, broad-bladed axe designed and used as a weapon in battle.

Examples

An African knife and battle-ax are at this moment being sharpened by McKinley and Green.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axe - a tool or weapon with a heavy bladed head for chopping

Etymology

From 'battle' (combat) + 'ax', referring to its use in warfare.
