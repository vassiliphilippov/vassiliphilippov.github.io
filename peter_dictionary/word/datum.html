datum

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtəm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtəm / (BrE)
A single piece of factual information, especially as obtained by measurement or research.

Etymology

From Latin 'datum' (something given), neuter of 'datus', past participle of 'dare' (to give).
