piper

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpə / (BrE)
A person who plays the bagpipes.

Examples

No drummers here, no pipers, and the red coats were covered with a fine film of dust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bagpiper - someone who plays the bagpipes

Etymology

From Middle English 'pipere', from Old English 'pipere', from 'pipe' + '-er' (one who plays a pipe).
