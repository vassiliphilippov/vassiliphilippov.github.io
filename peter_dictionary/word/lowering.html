lowering

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of making something less or causing something to decrease.

Examples

[LOWERS TV VOLUME] Enjoy it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reduction - the process of making something smaller or less in amount

Etymology

From the verb 'lower' + -ing, itself from Old French 'lurer' (to bring down).
