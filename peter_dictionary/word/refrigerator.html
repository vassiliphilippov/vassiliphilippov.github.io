refrigerator

1. NOUN

/ rəˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtə / (BrE)
An electrical appliance used for keeping food and drinks cold by maintaining a low temperature inside a compartment.

Examples

Toys he has can be made to act as substitutes for family temptations such as refrigerator and gas stove.
Finding the key under his shoe, he started the engine, and while it warmed up he turned to her standing there in the dripping fog, and said, "Defrost the refrigerator ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fridge - short form for refrigerator; same meaning

Etymology

From Latin 'refrigerare' (to make cool), via the verb 'refrigerate' + '-or'.
