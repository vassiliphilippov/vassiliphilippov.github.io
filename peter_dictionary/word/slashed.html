slashed

1. ADJ

/ slæʃt / (AmE), / slæʃt / (BrE)
Patterned or marked with bold, sweeping strokes or streaks of color.

Examples

Ben's eyes strained with the bitter hurt, his homely face slashed with gray and crimson.

Synset Examples

brown iris...slashed with yellow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streaked - marked with long, narrow lines of a different color
  • Synonym: striped - having long, narrow bands of color

2. ADJ

/ slæʃt / (AmE), / slæʃt / (BrE)
(of a garment) having long, narrow ornamental cuts that show the fabric or lining beneath.

Examples

He furled the slashed sides of the canvas tarpaulins, leaving the ribs and wagon open.

Synset Examples

a slashed doublet
slashed cuffs showing the scarlet lining

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut - having an opening made by cutting; an alternative, general term
