coolly

1. ADV

/ ˈkuːlli / (AmE), / ˈkuːlli / (BrE)
In a calm and unemotional or unconcerned manner.

Examples

Looking the setup over, Stevens started coolly for the rear of the place.

Synset Examples

without more ado Barker borrowed a knife from his brigade Major and honed it on a carborundum stone as coolly as a butcher

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm and peaceful manner
  • Antonym: nervously - in an anxious or uneasy manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'cool' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
