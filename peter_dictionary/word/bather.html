bather

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðə / (BrE)
a person who is swimming or bathing in water, especially at a beach or public pool.

Examples

The seemingly quiet ocean had crashed a wall of water from 10 to 100 feet high upon beaches crowded with bathers, drowning thousands of them and flattening villages along the shore.

Synset Examples

he is not a good swimmer

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimmer - a person who swims

Etymology

From 'bathe' + '-er', indicating a person who bathes or swims.
