beaming

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or their expression) showing obvious pleasure and happiness; smiling brightly.

Examples

The beaming rok was carrying a thirty caliber machine gun; another man lugged the tripod and a box of ammunition.

Synset Examples

a beaming smile
a glad May morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiant - shining or glowing brightly, especially with joy
  • Antonym: glum - looking or feeling dejected; morose

Etymology

Present participle of 'beam', originally meaning 'to emit rays of light', later used figuratively of facial expressions.
