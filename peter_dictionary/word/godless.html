godless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.ləs / (BrE)
Having or showing no reverence for God; lacking religious belief or piety.

Examples

Geneva, instead of becoming the City of God, as John had dreamed, had in the two years since he had been there, continued to be a godless place where all manner of vice flourished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impious - not showing respect or reverence, especially for a god
  • Antonym: pious - having or showing religious devotion

Etymology

Formed from 'god' + '-less' (without).
