thorn

1. NOUN

/ θɔrn / (AmE), / θɔːn / (BrE)
A person or thing that repeatedly irritates, annoys, or troubles someone, especially in the expression 'a thorn in one's side.'

Examples

Biggest thorn in my side of the whole fucken squad.

Synset Examples

he's a thorn in my flesh

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritant - something that causes annoyance or discomfort
  • Synonym: nuisance - a person or thing causing inconvenience or annoyance

Etymology

Old English thorn, from Proto-Germanic *thurnus.
