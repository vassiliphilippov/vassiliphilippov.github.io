walkover

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔkˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkˌəʊvə / (BrE)
A gymnastic movement in which a person moves from a standing position into a backbend and then over into a handstand, continuing to complete the rotation to return to a standing position.

Examples

Later this can be combined with the handstand to provide a walkover.
