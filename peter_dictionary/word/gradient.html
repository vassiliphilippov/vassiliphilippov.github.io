gradient

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪdiənt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdiənt / (BrE)
a gradual change or rate of change in the value of a quantity, such as temperature, pressure, or concentration, within a specified distance, typically used in scientific and mathematical contexts
