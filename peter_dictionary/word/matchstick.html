matchstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætʃˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈmatʃ.stɪk / (BrE)
A small, thin stick of wood used to make matches for lighting fires.

Examples

Others build matchstick houses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: match - a small stick for producing a flame

Etymology

From 'match' (for lighting fire) + 'stick'.
