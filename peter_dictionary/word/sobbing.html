sobbing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒbɪŋ / (BrE)
A convulsive gasp or series of breaths made while weeping.

Examples

[SOBBING]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weeping - the act of shedding tears
  • Antonym: laughing - the act or sound of laughter

Etymology

From the verb 'sob', originally of imitative origin indicating the sound of sobbing, with the gerund suffix -ing.
