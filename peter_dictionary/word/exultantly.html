exultantly

1. ADV

/ ɪɡˈzʌltəntli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzʌltəntli / (BrE)
In a manner showing great joy, triumph, or jubilation.

Examples

After that, violence was exultantly easy.

Synset Examples

it was exultingly easy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumphantly - in a manner showing victory or triumph

Etymology

From Latin 'exsultare' (to leap up), via the verb 'exult'.
