insolently

1. ADV

/ ˈɪn.sə.lənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɪn.səl.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a rude and disrespectful manner; showing a lack of respect or courtesy.

Examples

One of the missing handspikes came out of its hiding place after Midshipman Tillotson had been insolently disobeyed by Seaman Wilson.

Synset Examples

he had replied insolently to his superiors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rudely - in an impolite or disrespectful manner
  • Antonym: respectfully - in a manner showing respect

Etymology

From Latin 'insolentia' (arrogance, unusualness), via 'insolent' + '-ly'.
