tutorial

1. NOUN

/ tuːˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / tjuːˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
a class or session where a tutor gives intensive instruction to one student or a small group

Examples

Some Catholic priests lecture there; Catholic seminarians attend tutorials and row on the Cherwell with non-Catholic students.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seminar - a meeting for discussion or training, typically with a larger group than a tutorial
  • Degree Higher: lecture - an educational talk to a large group of students

Etymology

From Latin 'tutorialis' (of or relating to a tutor), from 'tutor' (a guardian or instructor).
