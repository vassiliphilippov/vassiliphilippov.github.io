epitome

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈpɪtəmi / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪtəmi / (BrE)
A person or thing that is a perfect example of a particular quality or type.

Examples

Wiry and burr headed, with steel blue eyes and a chest splattered with medals, Chandler is the epitome of the old-time trooper.

Synset Examples

he is the prototype of good breeding
he provided America with an image of the good father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: embodiment - a tangible or visible form of an idea, quality, or feeling
  • Synonym: paragon - a person or thing regarded as a perfect example of a particular quality

Etymology

From Greek *epitomē* (abridgment, summary).
