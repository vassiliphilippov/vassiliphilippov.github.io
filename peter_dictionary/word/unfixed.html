unfixed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɪkst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪkst / (BrE)
not firmly placed, set, or fastened; not fixed in position

Examples

In my studio I work at a tilt-top table, but leave the paper unfixed so that I can move it freely to control the washes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fixed - firmly fastened or set in place
