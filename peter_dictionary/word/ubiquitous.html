ubiquitous

1. ADJ

/ juˈbɪkwɪtəs / (AmE), / juːˈbɪkwɪtəs / (BrE)
present, appearing, or found everywhere; widespread.

Examples

Language was no problem anyway; Mr. Phillips had only to signal from his doorway to summon aid from the ubiquitous bilingual children who played on the sidewalks of Mission Street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omnipresent - present everywhere at the same time

Etymology

From Latin 'ubique' meaning 'everywhere', from 'ubi' (where).
