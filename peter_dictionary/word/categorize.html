categorize

1. VERB

/ ˈkætəɡəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈkatɪɡəraɪz / (BrE)
to place people or things into particular groups according to their type

Examples

Getz is a difficult musician to categorize.

Synset Examples

Children learn early on to categorize

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classify - to arrange people or things into groups according to type or quality

Etymology

Ultimately from Greek 'katēgoria' (category, accusation) via medieval Latin and French.
