darkening

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑrkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːkənɪŋ / (BrE)
becoming dark or darker; growing less light

Examples

If Mahzeer was alone with the prime minister he could be arranging his execution while Hoag stood out here shivering in the darkening street.

Synset Examples

the darkening sky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dark - having little or no light
  • Antonym: lightening - becoming lighter or brighter

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'darken' (from Old English deorc, dark) plus the participial suffix '-ing'.
