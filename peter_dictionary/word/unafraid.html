unafraid

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈfreɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈfreɪd / (BrE)
not feeling fear about something; not frightened or worried, especially in situations that might cause fear.

Examples

When the skies grew dark and thunder rolled across the valley, he was unafraid.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: afraid - feeling fear or anxiety; frightened
  • Synonym: fearless - showing lack of fear; very brave

Etymology

un- (not) + afraid, from Old French esfrayé (frightened; see 'afraid').
