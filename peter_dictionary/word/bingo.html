bingo

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɪŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A game of chance in which players mark off numbers on cards as the numbers are drawn at random, the winner being the first to mark all their numbers.

Examples

Play the bingo game.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lotto - a similar game of chance involving cards and numbered balls

Etymology

Probably from American English, perhaps an alteration of 'beano', an earlier name for the game.
