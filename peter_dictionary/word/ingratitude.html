ingratitude

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈɡrætɪˌtuːd / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡrætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
Lack of gratitude; failure or refusal to show appreciation or thanks.

Examples

Injury and ingratitude would occur.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gratitude - the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation

Etymology

From Latin 'ingratitudo', from 'in-' (not) + 'gratitudo' (gratitude).
