linguistically

1. ADV

/ lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkli / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to language or the science of linguistics.

Examples

Not that such a list cannot be constructed; but the nearer it comes to attaining universality, the less significant will it be linguistically.

Synset Examples

linguistically interesting data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linguistic - relating to language or linguistics

Etymology

Formed from "linguistic" + "-ally", ultimately from Latin "lingua" (tongue, language).
