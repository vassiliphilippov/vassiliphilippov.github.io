painter

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪntər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntə / (BrE)
A person who creates artworks using paints, especially as a profession or as an artist.

Examples

If the change, at first sight, seems minor, we may recall that it took the Italian painters about two hundred years to make an analogous change, and the Italian painters, by universal consent, were the most brilliant group of geniuses any art has seen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artist - person who creates art, especially visual art such as paintings or sculptures
  • Antonym: sculptor - person who creates sculptures; related art profession

Etymology

From Middle English 'paintour', from Old French 'peintour', from Latin 'pictor' (painter).
