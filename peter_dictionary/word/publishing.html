publishing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or profession of preparing and issuing books, magazines, newspapers, or other printed material for sale or public distribution

Examples

However, he continued experimenting and lecturing, publishing the results of his experiments in German and Danish periodicals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publication - the act of making content public, especially printed material

Etymology

From publish + -ing; publish from Latin 'publicare' (to make public).
