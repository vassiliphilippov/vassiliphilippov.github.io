earthly

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜrθli / (AmE), / ˈɜːθli / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of this world as opposed to heaven or the spiritual realm; worldly rather than spiritual.

Examples

At the base of that wall, you'll find a rock that has no earthly business in a Maine hayfield.

Synset Examples

earthly beings
believed that our earthly life is all that matters
earthly love
our earthly home

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heavenly - relating to heaven or the spiritual realm as opposed to the physical world
  • Synonym: worldly - concerned with material values or ordinary life rather than a spiritual existence

Etymology

From Old English eorþlīc (of earth), from eorþe (earth) + -līc (-ly).
