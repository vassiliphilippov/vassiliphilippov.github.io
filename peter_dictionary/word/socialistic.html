socialistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊʃəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or supporting the principles of socialism.

Examples

As it is, they consider that the North is now reaping the fruits of excess egalitarianism, that in spite of its high standard of living the "American way "has been proved inferior to the English and Scandinavian ways, although they disapprove of the socialistic features of the latter.
The riotous onrush of industrialism after the War for Southern Independence and the general secular drift to the Religion of Humanity, however, prepared the way for a reception of the French Revolution's socialistic offspring of one sort of another.
The corporation in America is in reality our form of socialism, vying in a sense with the other socialistic form that has emerged within governmental bureaucracy.

Synset Examples

socialistic government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialist - a person who supports or believes in the principles of socialism
  • Antonym: capitalistic - relating to or supporting capitalism, the economic system based on private ownership

Etymology

Formed from 'socialist' + '-ic', denoting relating to socialist ideology.
