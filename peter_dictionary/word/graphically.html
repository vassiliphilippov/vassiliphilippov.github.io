graphically

1. ADV

/ ˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɡrafɪkli / (BrE)
in a diagrammatic manner; by means of diagrams or visual figures, especially to illustrate information

Examples

Figure 1 portrays the mean achievement scores of each sub-group graphically.

Synset Examples

the landscape unit drawn diagrammatically illustrates the gentle rolling relief, with a peat-filled basin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diagrammatically - in a manner using diagrams

Etymology

From graphic + -ally, deriving from Greek graphikos (of drawing or writing) via Latin graphicus.
