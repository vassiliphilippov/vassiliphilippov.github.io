unending

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
Having no end; continuing forever or for an unlimited time.

Examples

Today the private detective will also investigate insurance claims or handle divorce cases, but his primary function remains what it has always been, to assist those who have money in their unending struggle with those who have not.

Synset Examples

the ageless themes of love and revenge
eternal truths
life everlasting
hell's perpetual fires
the unending bliss of heaven

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eternal - lasting forever; without end
  • Synonym: interminable - endless; seemingly without end
  • Synonym: perpetual - never ending or changing
  • Synonym: endless - having or seeming to have no end

Etymology

From un- (not) + ending.
