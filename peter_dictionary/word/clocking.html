clocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
the time recorded for traversing a measured course, especially in races or timed events

Examples

Kerr, who set the world record earlier this month in New York with a clocking of 1.09.3, wiped out Mills's early pace and beat the young Big 10 quarter-mile king by 5 yards.

Synset Examples

it was a world record clocking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: time - the measured duration taken to complete something
