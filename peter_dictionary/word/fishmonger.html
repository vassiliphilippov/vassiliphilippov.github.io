fishmonger

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
A person or business that sells fish, especially for food.

Examples

The teeth will rain from his mouth like pebbles, his wife will make him cocu with fishmongers, and a trolley car will grow in his stomach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fish dealer - a person who sells fish (synonym)

Etymology

From Middle English 'fisshmonger', from 'fish' + 'monger' (dealer, trader).
