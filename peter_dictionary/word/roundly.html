roundly

1. ADV

/ ˈraʊndli / (AmE), / ˈraʊndli / (BrE)
In a round manner; so as to exhibit roundness in shape or form.

Examples

Carrying it to the living room, she imagined the picture she made: tall and roundly slim, a bit sophisticated in her yellow sheath, with a graceful swingy walk that she had learned as a twirler with the school band.

Synset Examples

she was roundly slim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circularly - in a circular manner or shape

Etymology

From the adjective 'round' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
