fuzz

1. NOUN

/ fʌz / (AmE), / fʌz / (BrE)
A mass of fine, soft, hairlike threads, especially the short downy growth on the skin of some fruits or plants.

Examples

Griffith was trying to clear his head of the champagne fuzz that encased it.

Synset Examples

peach fuzz

Related Terms

  • Synonym: down - soft, fine feathers or hairlike growth, especially on young birds or plants
