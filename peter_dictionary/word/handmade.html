handmade

1. ADJ

/ ˈhændˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈhændˌmeɪd / (BrE)
Made by hand rather than by machine; crafted using manual skills.

Examples

I have used a variety of heavy-weight hand-made papers, but prefer an English make, rough surface, in 400 - pound weight.
Then he hunkered down on the heels of his handmade boots, peered into the orderly chaos of axle, shock absorber, and spring.

Synset Examples

delicate handmade baby dresses

Related Terms

  • Antonym: machine-made - made by machine rather than by hand

Etymology

Formed from 'hand' + 'made', indicating the item was made using hands.
