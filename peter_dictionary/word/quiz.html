quiz

1. NOUN

/ kwɪz / (AmE), / kwɪz / (BrE)
a short test or examination, often with a few questions, given to assess knowledge or learning

Examples

One of the roughest was the TV quiz shows, which gave him inferiority complexes.
Hallmark Cards, Inc., Kansas City, Mo., has a do-it-yourself quiz game called "Benefit Bafflers ", which it distributes to employees.
There's gonna be a quiz later!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test - a formal assessment or examination of knowledge or ability
  • Synonym: exam - an examination, especially a formal one in education

Etymology

Probably from informal English slang of the late 18th century, origin uncertain.
