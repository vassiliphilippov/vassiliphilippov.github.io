staggeringly

1. ADV

/ ˈstæɡɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstæɡərɪŋli / (BrE)
Extremely; to a remarkable or overwhelming degree.

Examples

Staggeringly condensed versions of famous novels and famous plays were presented.
A man with so big and so staggeringly developed a torso and such long and powerful arms is expected to stand taller than five feet five.

Synset Examples

he was enormously popular

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enormously - to a very great extent; extremely
  • Synonym: incredibly - to a great or extreme degree; extremely
  • Degree Lower: slightly - to a small degree; not a lot

Etymology

From the verb 'stagger' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
