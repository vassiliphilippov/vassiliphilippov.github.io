whiff

1. NOUN

/ wɪf / (AmE), / wɪf / (BrE)
A brief, light gust of air or breath.

Examples

Not even a whiff.
Have a little... ...whiff of my posy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puff - A short, small burst of air or smoke

Etymology

Origin unknown; possibly imitative of the sound or action of a puff of air.
