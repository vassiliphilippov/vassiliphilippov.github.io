unscientific

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.saɪənˈtɪf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.saɪənˈtɪf.ɪk / (BrE)
Not based on or consistent with scientific methods or principles.

Examples

Occasionally there are examples of prevision which cannot be pushed aside without confessing an unscientific attitude.
My unscientific friend does not believe that human stature is measurable in terms of speed, momentum, weightlessness, or distance from earth, but is a matter of the development of the human mind.

Synset Examples

an unscientific lack of objectivity

Related Terms

  • Antonym: scientific - Based on or using scientific methods or principles

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'scientific'.
