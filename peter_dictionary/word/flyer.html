flyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈflaɪər / (AmE), / ˈflaɪə / (BrE)
A small printed notice or advertisement, usually on a single sheet, intended for wide distribution.

Examples

Proposed property sales of general interest to small business concerns are publicized through SBA regional news releases, and by "flyers "directed to the small business concerns.

Synset Examples

he mailed the circular to all subscribers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leaflet - a printed sheet of paper, typically folded, containing information or advertising
  • Synonym: brochure - a small booklet or pamphlet containing promotional material
