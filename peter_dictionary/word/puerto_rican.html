Puerto Rican

1. NOUN

/ ˌpwɛrtoʊ ˈriːkən / (AmE), / ˌpjʊətəʊ ˈriːkən / (BrE)
a native or resident of Puerto Rico

Examples

Negroes, Puerto Ricans, and rural newcomers are slowly making their way into the cities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Puerto Rico - an island in the Caribbean, a territory of the United States

Etymology

From Spanish 'Puerto Rico' (rich port), referring to the island, plus English '-an' denoting belonging or origin.
