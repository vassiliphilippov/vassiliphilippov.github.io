presidency

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛzɪdənsi / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdənsi / (BrE)
the period during which someone holds the office of president

Examples

And I select this sentence as its pertinent summation: "In essence the drama of his (Eisenhower's) Presidency can be described as the ordeal of a nation turned conservative and struggling - thus far with but limited and precarious success - to give effective voice and force to that conservatism ".

Synset Examples

things were quiet during the Eisenhower administration

Related Terms

  • Synonym: president - the elected head of a republic, e.g. a country or organization

Etymology

From president + -cy, meaning the state or office of being president.
