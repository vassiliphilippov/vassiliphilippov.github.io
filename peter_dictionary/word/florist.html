florist

1. NOUN

/ ˈflɔːrɪst / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪst / (BrE)
A person who sells and arranges flowers and ornamental plants, often working in a shop.

Examples

He was an expert florist, tenderly dextrous in the arrangement of bouquets and wreaths.

Synset Examples

the florist made up an attractive bouquet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower shop - a shop where flowers and related items are sold

Etymology

From French *floriste*, from Latin *flos* (flower).

2. NOUN

/ ˈflɔːrɪst / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪst / (BrE)
A shop that sells flowers and ornamental plants.

Examples

A refuse bin at the Dumont turned up a florist's box - a very long box for very long stemmed flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flower shop - A shop that sells flowers and floral arrangements

Etymology

From French *fleuriste*, from *fleur* (flower).
