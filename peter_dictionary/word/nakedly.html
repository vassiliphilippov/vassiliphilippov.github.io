nakedly

1. ADV

/ ˈneɪkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪdli / (BrE)
In an exposed or vulnerable manner; without protection, concealment, or defense.

Examples

Muller, nakedly exposed at the bright window like a deer pinned in a car's headlights, threw down the rifle and turned to jump from the table; his face wore a look of outrage.

Synset Examples

they were attacked as they huddled nakedly on the bare hill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: openly - in a way that is not secret or concealed
  • Antonym: covertly - in a concealed or secret manner

Etymology

Formed from 'naked' + '-ly', following the pattern for English adverbs.
