registration

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
The act or process of enrolling or entering information, especially officially, into a record or list.

Examples

The jury said it did find that many of Georgia's registration and election laws "are outmoded or inadequate and often ambiguous ".

Related Terms

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'registratio', from 'registrare' (to record).
