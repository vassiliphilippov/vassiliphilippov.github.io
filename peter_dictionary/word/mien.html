mien

1. NOUN

/ miːn / (AmE), / miːn / (BrE)
A person's manner, appearance, or demeanour, especially as it reveals their character or mood.

Examples

And so, after a flight southeast to Savannakhet, we found ourselves bouncing along in a Jeep right behind the Land-Rover of Prince Boun Oum of Champassak, a tall man of Churchillian mien in a bush jacket and a ten-gallon hat from Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearing - the way a person conducts or carries themselves
  • Synonym: demeanour - outward behaviour or bearing

Etymology

From French *mine* (appearance, expression), probably of Celtic origin.
