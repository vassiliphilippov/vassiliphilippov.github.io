humanely

1. ADV

/ hjuˈmeɪnli / (AmE), / hjuːˈmeɪnli / (BrE)
In a way that shows compassion or kindness towards people or animals.

Examples

In the pre Civil War years, the South argued that the slave was not less humanely treated than the factory worker of the North.

Synset Examples

let's treat the prisoners of war humanely

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cruelly - in a cruel manner; showing a lack of compassion
  • Synonym: kindly - in a kind or gentle manner

Etymology

From 'humane' + '-ly'; 'humane' from Latin 'humanus' (human).
