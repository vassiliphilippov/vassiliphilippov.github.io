severity

1. NOUN

/ səˈvɛrəti / (AmE), / sɪˈvɛrəti / (BrE)
The degree or intensity of something undesirable, such as pain, illness, or bad weather.

Examples

As an aid in reducing incidence and severity of bloat, provide 75 milligrams of oxytetracycline hydrochloride per animal daily.
Aids in reducing the incidence and severity of bloat in beef or dairy cattle on legume pasture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mildness - the quality or state of being moderate or not severe

Etymology

From Latin 'severitas' (strictness, seriousness).
