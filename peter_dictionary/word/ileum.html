ileum

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪliəm / (AmE), / ˈɪliəm / (BrE)
the third and final part of the small intestine, located between the jejunum and the cecum

Examples

The mucosa of the jejunum and ileum showed similar changes, and in some areas the submucosa was edematous and contained considerable numbers of neutrophils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jejunum - the middle section of the small intestine
  • Synonym: cecum - the pouch marking the beginning of the large intestine

Etymology

From Latin *ileum*, a variant of *ileus* (twisted gut).
