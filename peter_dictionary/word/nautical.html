nautical

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈnɔːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to ships, navigation, or sailors; maritime.

Examples

In a brighter nautical vein is Ile de France blue.

Synset Examples

nautical charts
maritime law
marine insurance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maritime - connected with the sea, especially in relation to seafaring commercial or military activity
  • Antonym: terrestrial - relating to the earth or land

Etymology

From Latin 'nauticus', from Greek 'nautikos', from 'nautēs' (sailor).
