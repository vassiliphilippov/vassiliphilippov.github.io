Northwest

1. PROPN

/ ˌnɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
a region in the northwestern part of the United States, especially including states such as Washington, Oregon, and Idaho

Examples

To find out, we traveled throughout that part of Laos still nominally controlled, in the daytime at least, by the Royal Lao Army: from Attopeu, the City of Buffalo Dung in the southeast, to Muong Sing, the City of Lions in the northwest, close to Communist China (map, page 250).

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Pacific Northwest - region including the northwestern U.S. states bordering the Pacific Ocean

Etymology

From 'north' and 'west', indicating the direction and region.
