surplus

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɝː.pləs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.pləs / (BrE)
more than what is needed, wanted, or used; extra or excessive

Etymology

From Old French 'surplus', from Latin 'super' (over) + 'plus' (more).

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shortage - a situation in which there is not enough of something
