deathly

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛθli / (AmE), / ˈdɛθli / (BrE)
Resembling death in appearance; extremely pale, lifeless, or lacking in color.

Examples

The boy shuddered at the deathly pale countenance with its wrinkles and gray hair.

Synset Examples

a deathly pallor

Etymology

From Middle English 'dethly', from 'death' + '-ly'.
