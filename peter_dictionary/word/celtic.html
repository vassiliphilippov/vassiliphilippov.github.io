Celtic

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɛltɪk, ˈsɛltɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɛltɪk, ˈsɛltɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the Celts or their languages, culture, or descendants.

Examples

And the great majority of these people are of Anglo-Saxon or Celtic descent.

Etymology

From Latin Celticus, from Celtae (the Celts), probably from Greek Keltoi.
