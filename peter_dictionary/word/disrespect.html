disrespect

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪs.rɪˈspɛkt / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.rɪˈspɛkt / (BrE)
A lack of respect or courtesy; an expression or act that shows a lack of regard for someone or something.

Examples

I meant no disrespect to you or your daughter.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: respect - a feeling of deep admiration or regard

Etymology

Formed from the prefix dis- (opposite of) + respect, from Latin respectus (regard, consideration).
