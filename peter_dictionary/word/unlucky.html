unlucky

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlʌki / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌki / (BrE)
Having or bringing misfortune; experiencing bad luck.

Examples

The poor, unlucky -
- You're unlucky.

Synset Examples

Friday the 13th is an unlucky date

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lucky - having or bringing good fortune

Etymology

From un- (not) + lucky.
