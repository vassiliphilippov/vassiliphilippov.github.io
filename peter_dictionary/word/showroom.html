showroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌruːm / (BrE)
A large room or area where goods for sale, especially cars or furniture, are displayed to customers.

Examples

Above it, from a second story showroom, wooden angels surveyed the neighborhood.

Synset Examples

in Britain a showroom is called a salesroom

Etymology

From 'show' + 'room', first recorded in late 18th century.
