pachinko

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtʃiŋkoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈtʃɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
A Japanese gambling game resembling pinball, played on a vertical board with small steel balls.

Examples

He was allowed to spend his nights at an inn near the hospital and he was given some extra money to go to the pachinko parlor - an excellent place to make contact with the enemy.

Etymology

From Japanese パチンコ (pachinko), likely imitative of the clicking sound ('pachi').
