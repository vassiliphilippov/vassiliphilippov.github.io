cracked

1. ADJ

/ krækt / (AmE), / krækt / (BrE)
(of skin) roughened and split, usually as a result of cold, dryness, or exposure.

Examples

"Hoa-whup "! he commanded from his raw throat, and felt the pain of movement in his cracked, black burned lips.

Synset Examples

chapped lips

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chapped - (of skin) roughened and sore, especially from wind or cold
