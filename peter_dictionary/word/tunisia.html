Tunisia

1. PROPN

/ tuˈniʒə / (AmE), / tjuˈniːziə / (BrE)
A country in North Africa on the Mediterranean coast, known officially as the Republic of Tunisia.

Examples

Mongi Slim of Tunisia and Frederick Boland of Ireland were early favorites in the running, but France did n't like the former and the Soviet Union would have none of the latter.

Synset Examples

southern Tunisia is mostly desert

Etymology

From French 'Tunisie', from Arabic 'Tūnis'.
