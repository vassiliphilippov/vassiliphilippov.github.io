bluish

1. ADJ

/ ˈbluːɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbluːɪʃ / (BrE)
Slightly blue in color; having a tint or shade of blue.

Examples

This was chiefly because of the bluish white autofluorescence from the cells.

Synset Examples

October's bright blue weather
a blue flame
blue haze of tobacco smoke

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: blue - having the color of a clear sky; fully blue
  • Degree Lower: grayish - having a slight gray color, tending towards gray

Etymology

Formed from 'blue' + '-ish' (suffix meaning 'somewhat' or 'having the quality of').
