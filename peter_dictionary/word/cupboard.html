cupboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌbərd / (AmE), / ˈkʌbəd / (BrE)
A cabinet or small recess, usually with a door, used for storing items such as dishes, food, or household supplies, especially in a kitchen.

Examples

In the kitchen Mama was wiping the cupboards.
In their search for what turned out to be the right breakfast china but the wrong table silver, they opened every cupboard door in the kitchen and pantry.

Etymology

From Middle English 'cobord', originally meaning a table for cups.
