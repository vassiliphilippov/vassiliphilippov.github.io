halftime

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæfˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌtaɪm / (BrE)
the interval or intermission between the first and second halves of a sports game

Examples

We 'd be losing at halftime to a good team and Hartweger would say,' Do n't worry, Coach - we 'll get 'em all right "'.

Etymology

From 'half' + 'time', referring to the midpoint in a timed event.
