cardiomegaly

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːrdioʊˈmɛɡəli / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊˈmɛɡəli / (BrE)
A medical condition involving abnormal enlargement of the heart.

Examples

Additional findings at this time included cardiomegaly, peripheral arteriosclerosis obliterans, and cholelithiasis.

Synset Examples

mild cardiomegaly is common in athletes

Etymology

From Greek 'kardia' (heart) + 'megale' (enlargement).
