trucking

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrʌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or business of transporting goods by truck.

Examples

The railroad siding is still important - it is usually, though not always, true that long-haul shipment by rail is cheaper than trucking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haulage - the commercial transport of goods, especially by road or rail

Etymology

From the noun 'truck' (vehicle for carrying goods) + '-ing'.
