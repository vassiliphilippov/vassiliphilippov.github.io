missus

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪz / (BrE)
(informal) a man's wife; used as a term of address or reference.

Examples

You just have some of this fine pie my missus made especially for you, and you think about that.

Etymology

Alteration of 'mistress', influenced by the pronunciation of 'Mrs.'
