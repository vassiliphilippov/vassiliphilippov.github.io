centering

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛntərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of concentrating attention or energy on a particular object, task, or subject.

Examples

Often dominant stress simply indicates a centering of attention or emotion.

Synset Examples

the focus of activity shifted to molecular biology
he had no direction in his life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: focus - the concentration of attention or energy on something

Etymology

From the verb 'center' + -ing.
