misogynist

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈsɑdʒənɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈsɒdʒənɪst / (BrE)
A person who dislikes, despises, or is prejudiced against women.

Examples

Holmes is addicted to the use of cocaine and other refreshing stimulants; he is prone to semi catatonic trances induced by the playing of the vioiln; he is a recluse, an incredible egotist, a confirmed misogynist.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: misandrist - a person who dislikes or is prejudiced against men

Etymology

From Greek 'misos' (hatred) + 'gynē' (woman).
