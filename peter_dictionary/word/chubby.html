chubby

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃʌbi / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌbi / (BrE)
having a round and plump, but pleasant or attractive, fullness of body or face.

Examples

I'll take... the chubby fat-ass there.

Synset Examples

a chubby child
pleasingly plump

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plump - having a full, rounded shape
  • Degree Higher: fat - having a large amount of excess flesh
  • Antonym: thin - having little fat on the body

Etymology

Probably from English dialect chub (to swell), influenced by chub (a type of fish noted for its plumpness).
