rakishly

1. ADV

/ ˈreɪkɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈreɪkɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner that is confidently unconventional, stylish, or slightly disreputable.

Examples

She laughed a little and tossed the dregs rakishly around in her glass.

Synset Examples

she wore her hat rakishly at an angle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rakish - confidently unconventional or dashing in appearance or manner

Etymology

From 'rakish' (carefree, dashing), itself from 'rake' (dissolute man).
