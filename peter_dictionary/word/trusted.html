trusted

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrʌstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌstɪd / (BrE)
Regarded as reliable or deserving of confidence.

Examples

He would send on by trusted messenger the dispatches with their electrifying news.

Synset Examples

a sure (or trusted) friend

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reliable - consistently good in quality or performance; able to be trusted
  • Synonym: dependable - trustworthy and reliable
  • Antonym: untrustworthy - not able to be relied on as honest or truthful

Etymology

From the verb 'trust' + past participle suffix '-ed'.
