overprotection

1. NOUN

/ ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəprəˈtɛkʃən / (BrE)
Excessive or unnecessary protection, often to the detriment of the person being protected.

Examples

For girls, the overprotection is far more pervasive.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: protection - the act of protecting or the state of being protected

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessive) + 'protection'.
