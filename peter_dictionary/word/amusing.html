amusing

1. ADJ

/ əˈmjuzɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzɪŋ / (BrE)
causing laughter or providing enjoyment; entertaining in a lighthearted or pleasant way

Examples

[PEOPLE LAUGHING] CHAIRMAN: Mr. Cicci, you may find this very amusing, but the members of this committee do not.

Synset Examples

an amusing speaker
a diverting story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertaining - providing enjoyment or pleasure
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; causing boredom
  • Degree Higher: funny - causing laughter; humorous
  • Synonym: diverting - entertaining; amusing

Etymology

From amuse + -ing; 'amuse' from Old French 'amuser', meaning 'to divert, entertain'.
