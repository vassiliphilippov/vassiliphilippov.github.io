goodbye

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
A word or remark used to express farewell when parting from someone.

Examples

Anthony, will you kiss your mother goodbye!

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell - a wish of happiness or safety at parting
  • Antonym: hello - a greeting or expression used to say 'hi'

Etymology

From 'God be with ye', altered through folk etymology to the modern spelling.
