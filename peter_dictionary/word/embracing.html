embracing

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈbreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of holding someone closely in one's arms as a sign of affection or greeting.

Examples

There was much embracing, much exclaiming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hugging - the act of holding someone tightly in one's arms

Etymology

From the verb 'embrace,' from Old French 'embrasser' (to clasp in the arms), from Latin 'in-' (in) + 'bracchium' (arm).
