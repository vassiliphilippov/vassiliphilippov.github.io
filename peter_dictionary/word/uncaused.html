uncaused

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkɔzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːzd / (BrE)
Not brought about by any cause; having no identifiable or apparent cause.

Examples

On the other hand, we cannot regard artistic invention as pure, uncaused, and unrelated to the times in which it occurs.

Synset Examples

a causeless miracle
fortuitous encounters--strange accidents of fortune
we cannot regard artistic invention as...uncaused and unrelated to the times

Related Terms

  • Antonym: caused - brought about by a cause

Etymology

From the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' + 'caused', past participle of 'cause'.
