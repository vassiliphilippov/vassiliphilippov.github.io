confines

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnˌfaɪnz / (AmE), / ˈkɒnˌfaɪnz / (BrE)
the borders or limits that enclose an area or restrict the extent of something; boundaries

Examples

Sanitation in the confines of the family shelter will require much thought and planning.

Synset Examples

he stayed within the confines of the city

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boundary - a real or imagined line that marks the edge or limit of something
  • Synonym: limit - the greatest amount, number, or level allowed or possible
  • Synonym: scope - the range of things that something deals with

Etymology

From Latin 'confinium' (boundary, limit).
