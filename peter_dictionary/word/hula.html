hula

1. NOUN

/ ˈhuːlə / (AmE), / ˈhuːlə / (BrE)
A traditional Hawaiian dance with rhythmic movement and gestures, often performed to chants or music.

Examples

All Andy needed was a suit and a tie and a little jiggly hula girl on his desk, and he would have been: "Mr. Dufresne, if you please"!

Etymology

From Hawaiian 'hula' meaning 'dance'.
