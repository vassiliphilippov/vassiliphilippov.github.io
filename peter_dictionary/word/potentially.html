potentially

1. ADV

/ pəˈtɛnʃəli / (AmE), / pəˈtɛnʃəli / (BrE)
with the possibility of becoming actual or happening; possibly, but not yet actual

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possibly - used to indicate that something might happen or be the case

Etymology

From potential (from Late Latin potentialis, from potentia meaning 'power') + -ly.
