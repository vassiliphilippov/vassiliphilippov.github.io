novelist

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑvəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈnɒvəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who writes novels.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: author - a person who writes books or other works

Etymology

Formed from 'novel' + '-ist', where 'novel' (from Italian 'novella', a story) and '-ist' denotes a person who practices or is concerned with something.
