Rembrandt

1. PROPN

/ ˈrɛm.brænt / (AmE), / ˈrɛm.brænt / (BrE)
(1606–1669) A Dutch painter and etcher, one of the greatest artists in European art history, renowned for his portraits, self-portraits, and use of light and shadow.

Examples

Shit, you're a Rembrandt!

Etymology

Rembrandt is the given name of Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn.
