ardent

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑrdənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːdənt / (BrE)
having or showing very strong feelings of passion, enthusiasm, or devotion.

Examples

And there is no section of the nation more ardent than the South in the cold war against Communism.

Synset Examples

ardent love
an ardent lover
a fervent desire to change society
a fervent admirer
fiery oratory
an impassioned appeal
a torrid love affair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fervent - having or displaying passionate intensity
  • Synonym: impassioned - filled with intense emotion
  • Synonym: passionate - showing or caused by strong feelings or a strong belief
  • Antonym: cold - lacking in passion or enthusiasm

Etymology

From Latin ardent-, ardens, present participle of ardēre (to burn).
