optically

1. ADV

/ ˈɑptɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves optics or the use of light.

Examples

Planes defined as parallel to the surface also cut through it into real space, and a depth is suggested optically which is greater than that established pictorially.

Synset Examples

optically distorted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visually - in a way that relates to seeing or sight

Etymology

From optic + -ally, in reference to optics (the study of light).
