newsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌman / (BrE)
A journalist who investigates, reports, or edits news stories, typically for newspapers, radio, or television.

Examples

"I 've had it ", he told a newsman.
He took a midnight train out of Cleveland Saturday, without an official word to anybody, and has stayed away from newsmen on his train trip across the nation to Reno, Nev., where his wife, former Olympic Diving Champion Zoe Ann Olsen, awaited.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reporter - a person who reports news or conducts interviews for newspapers or broadcasts
  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
  • Antonym: newswoman - a female journalist or reporter

Etymology

From 'news' + 'man'; modelled after similar compounds like 'businessman'.
