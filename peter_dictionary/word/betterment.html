betterment

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛtɚmənt / (AmE), / ˈbɛtəmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of making something better; improvement or progress.

Examples

When we consider the electronic industry potential for human betterment, the prospect is staggering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improvement - the process of making something better
  • Antonym: deterioration - the process of becoming worse

Etymology

Formed from 'better' (to improve) + '-ment' (noun-forming suffix).
