salesgirl

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlzˌɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
A girl or young woman who sells goods in a store; a female salesperson.

Examples

Aside from the fact that business was slow this time of year and his one salesgirl was not the most enterprising, Mr. Phillips had no worries at all, and he said as much to Gun Matson, who sat across from him in civilian clothes, on a Jiffy-Couch-a-Bed, mauve velour, $ 79.89 nothing down special!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: salesman - a man who sells goods or services
  • Synonym: salesperson - a person who sells goods or services

Etymology

Formed from 'sale' + 'girl', directly referring to a female engaged in sales.
