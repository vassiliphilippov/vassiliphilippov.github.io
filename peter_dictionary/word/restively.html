restively

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛstɪvli / (AmE), / ˈrɛstɪvli / (BrE)
In a restless, impatient, or uneasy manner.

Examples

Moreland went over to stare at his peacocks again, and then came back and sat down, restively.

Synset Examples

he sat down again, restively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restlessly - in a restless manner

Etymology

From 'restive', ultimately from Latin 'restare' (to remain, resist).
