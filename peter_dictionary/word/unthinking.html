unthinking

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or action) not showing careful thought or consideration; done without reflecting on possible consequences.

Examples

When he came to the movies - more or less by accident - they were still cheap entertainment capable of enthralling the unthinking for an idle few minutes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtless - lacking consideration for others or for consequences
  • Synonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • Antonym: reflective - characterized by deep thought or consideration

Etymology

Formed by prefixing "un-" (not) to "thinking".
