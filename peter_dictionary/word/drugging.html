drugging

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of administering a sedative agent or drug to induce calm or sleep.

Examples

His face was bloated with drugging, redder than normal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sedation - the act of calming or inducing sleep using a drug
