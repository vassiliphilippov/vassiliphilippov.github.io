unimproved

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪmˈpruːvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈpruːvd / (BrE)
not made better, more valuable, or more suitable for use; not improved or developed.

Examples

Their seeds produce vigorous blooming plants half again the size of the unimproved strains.

Synset Examples

taxes on unimproved land are low
unimproved dirt roads

Related Terms

  • Antonym: improved - made better in quality, value, or suitability; developed
