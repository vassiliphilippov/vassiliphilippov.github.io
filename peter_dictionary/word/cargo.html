cargo

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
goods or merchandise transported in bulk by ship, aircraft, or vehicle

Related Terms

Etymology

From Spanish 'cargar' (to load), from Late Latin 'carricare', from Latin 'carrus' (cart, wagon).
