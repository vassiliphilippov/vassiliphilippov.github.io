tuna

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːnə / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnə / (BrE)
A large and fast-swimming marine fish of the genus Thunnus, valued as a food and game fish, especially in warm waters.

Examples

Would you like a tuna sandwich?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mackerel - a type of fast-swimming marine fish related to tuna

Etymology

From Spanish 'atún,' from Arabic 'at-tūn,' from Latin 'thunnus,' from Greek 'thynnos.'
