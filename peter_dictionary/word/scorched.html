scorched

1. ADJ

/ skɔrtʃt / (AmE), / skɔːtʃt / (BrE)
dried out or burned on the surface, especially by intense heat or sunlight

Examples

And the valley stretched endlessly out ahead, scorched and baked and writhing in its heat, until it vanished into the throbbing wall of fiery orange brown haze.

Synset Examples

a vast desert all adust
land lying baked in the heat
parched soil
the earth was scorched and bare
sunbaked salt flats

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parched - extremely dry, especially through heat
  • Synonym: sunbaked - dried or hardened by exposure to the sun
  • Synonym: baked - made dry or hard by heat
