nervousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɜrvəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəsnəs / (BrE)
A state of being nervous, anxious, or worried, especially in anticipation of something.

Examples

Nervousness at the start must have caused the blemishes of her first scene, or she may warm up slowly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
  • Antonym: calmness - a state of being calm and free from anxiety

Etymology

From 'nervous' + '-ness'; 'nervous' from Latin 'nervosus' (sinewy, vigorous).
