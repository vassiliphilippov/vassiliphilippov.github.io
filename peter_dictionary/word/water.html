2. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
A clear, colorless, odorless, and tasteless liquid compound (H₂O) that occurs at room temperature, essential for life and widely used as a solvent.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: steam - gaseous form of water

Etymology
