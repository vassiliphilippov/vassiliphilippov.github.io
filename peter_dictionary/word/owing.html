owing

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of money or a sum) yet to be paid; still due or unsettled.

Examples

Any of the antithyroid drugs, of either type, if given in large enough doses for a long period of time will cause goitre, owing to inhibition of thyroid hormone synthesis, with production of hypothyroidism.

Synset Examples

outstanding bills
the amount still owed
undischarged debts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outstanding - remaining to be paid, resolved, or dealt with
  • Synonym: unpaid - not yet paid
  • Antonym: settled - (of a debt or account) paid in full or resolved

Etymology

Present participle of 'owe,' from Old English 'agan' (to possess, owe).
