enrollment

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈroʊlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈrəʊlmənt / (BrE)
The act or process of enrolling or being enrolled, especially in an organization, school, or course.

Examples

No comparable measures are available of enrollments and expenditures for private vocational education training.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: registration - the act of officially recording or enrolling

Etymology

From enroll + -ment; 'enroll' from Old French enroller.
