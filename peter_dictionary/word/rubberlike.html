rubberlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌbərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈrʌbəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having a texture, flexibility, or toughness similar to that of rubber; elastic or resilient like rubber.

Examples

It is appropriate to call attention to certain thermodynamic properties of an ideal gas that are analogous to rubber-like deformation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbery - having a flexible, elastic texture like rubber

Etymology

Formed from 'rubber' + 'like', meaning 'like rubber'.
