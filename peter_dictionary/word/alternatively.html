alternatively

1. ADV

/ ɔlˈtɝnətɪvli / (AmE), / ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪvli / (BrE)
Used to introduce another possible option or choice instead of the one just mentioned.

Examples

Whether this abnormal TSH differs chemically from pituitary TSH, or is, alternatively, normal TSH with its period of effectiveness modified by some other blood constituent, cannot be decided without chemical study of the activity in the blood of these patients and a comparison of the substance responsible for the blood activity with pituitary TSH.

Synset Examples

Felix became a herpetologist instead
alternatively we could buy a used car

Related Terms

  • Synonym: instead - in place of; as a substitute or alternative

Etymology

From 'alternative' (from Latin 'alternare', meaning to do by turns) + '-ly'.
