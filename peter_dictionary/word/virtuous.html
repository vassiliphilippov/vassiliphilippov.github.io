virtuous

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɝː.tʃu.əs / (AmE), / ˈvɜː.tju.əs / (BrE)
Having or showing high moral standards; morally excellent.

Examples

The girls around here are beautiful...but virtuous.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wicked - morally very bad; evil
  • Synonym: righteous - morally right or justifiable; virtuous

Etymology

From Latin 'virtuosus' (full of virtue, good), from 'virtus' (virtue).
