replication

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making an exact copy of something; reproduction or duplication

Examples

Although the present study was not a direct replication of their investigations, the results do not confirm their conclusion.

Synset Examples

Gutenberg's reproduction of holy texts was far more efficient

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duplication - the act of making a duplicate; copying exactly
  • Synonym: reproduction - the process of copying or reproducing something

Etymology

From Latin replicatio (a reply, repetition), from replicare (to fold back, repeat).
