mistress

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪstrəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪstrɪs / (BrE)
A woman who has a continuing sexual relationship with a man to whom she is not married, especially one who is married to someone else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lover - a person having a sexual or romantic relationship with someone
  • Synonym: paramour - a lover, especially one in an illicit relationship
  • Antonym: wife - a married woman considered in relation to her spouse

Etymology

From Old French *maistresse*, feminine of *maistre* (master), from Latin *magistra* (female teacher).
