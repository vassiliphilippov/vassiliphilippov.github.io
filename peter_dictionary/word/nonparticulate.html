nonparticulate

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnˌpɑrˈtɪkjələt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌpɑːˈtɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
Not composed of separate or distinct particles.

Examples

Stains, which include the wide variety of nonparticulate materials which give color even when present in very low concentration on the soiled object.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: particulate - consisting of separate or distinct particles

Etymology

Formed with the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not' and 'particulate' meaning 'composed of particles'.
