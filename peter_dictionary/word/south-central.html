south-central

1. ADJ

/ saʊθ ˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / saʊθ ˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
Located in the southern part of the central area of a particular geographical region.

Examples

He was born in the small Danish town of Rudkoebing on the island of Langeland in the south central part of Denmark on August 14, 1777.

Synset Examples

south-central London

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: central - in or near the center
  • Synonym: southwestern - located in the southwestern part of a region
  • Synonym: southeastern - located in the southeastern part of a region
