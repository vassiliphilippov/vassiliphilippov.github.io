impudently

1. ADV

/ ˈɪmpjədəntli / (AmE), / ˈɪmpjʊdəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of respect or shameless boldness.

Examples

She looked at him impudently over the corner of the paper.

Synset Examples

a lean, swarthy fellow was peering through the window, grinning impudently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impertinently - in an impertinent or disrespectful manner

Etymology

From Latin 'impudentem' (shameless), via Middle English and Old French.
