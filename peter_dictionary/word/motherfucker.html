motherfucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌðərˌfʌkər / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəˌfʌkə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar term for a person regarded with intense dislike or contempt, often used as an insult.

Examples

Every last motherfucker in here.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asshole - an offensive term for a contemptible person
  • Synonym: bastard - an offensive term for a disliked or contemptible person
  • Degree Lower: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person (less offensive)
