tier

1. NOUN

/ tɪr / (AmE), / tɪə / (BrE)
one of a series of rows or levels placed one above the other; a layer in a structure

Examples

Clear. - Man missing on Tier 2. - Cell 245. - Dufresne.

Synset Examples

tier upon tier of huge casks
a three-tier wedding cake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layer - a sheet, quantity, or thickness of material covering a surface or body

Etymology

From Old French *tire* (rank, sequence), of uncertain origin.
