Hungary

/ ˈhʌŋɡəri / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡəri / (BrE)
A country in central Europe, officially known as the Republic of Hungary.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Hungaria, from the ethnonym Hungarian (Hungarus).
