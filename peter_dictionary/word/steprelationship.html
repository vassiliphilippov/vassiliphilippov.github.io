steprelationship

1. NOUN

/ stɛpɹɪˈleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / stɛpɹɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a family relationship established by marriage to someone who already has children, such as that between a stepparent and stepchild

Examples

For several generations much fiction has appeared dealing with the steprelationship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stepfamily - a family that is formed on the remarriage of a parent

Etymology

Formed from 'step-' (as in stepchild) and 'relationship'.
