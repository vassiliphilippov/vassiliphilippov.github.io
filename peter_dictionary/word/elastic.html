2. ADJ

/ ɪˈlæstɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈlastɪk / (BrE)
able to adapt or adjust readily to different conditions or situations; flexible

Examples

Etymology

From Greek 'elastikos' (ductile, flexible), via Latin 'elastica'.
