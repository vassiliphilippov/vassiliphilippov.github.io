bronchiolar

1. ADJ

/ brɑŋkiˈoʊlər / (AmE), / brɒŋkɪˈəʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the bronchioles, the small air passages in the lungs

Examples

One bronchial arteriolar pulmonary arteriolar anastomosis was noted at the terminal bronchiolar level (fig. 26).

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronchiolar - relating to the bronchiole (synonym spelling variant)
  • Degree Higher: bronchial - relating to the bronchi, the larger air passages of the lungs

Etymology

From 'bronchiole', itself from 'bronchus' with diminutive suffix '-iole', plus -ar (forming adjectives).
