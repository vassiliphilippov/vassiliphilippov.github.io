audacity

1. NOUN

/ ɔːˈdæsəti / (AmE), / ɔːˈdæsəti / (BrE)
Willingness to take bold risks; fearless daring or boldness.

Examples

Since the latter obviously require an audacity you do not possess, you may perhaps suppose that I am taunting you as socially inferior.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boldness - Willingness to take risks with confidence
  • Antonym: timidity - Lack of courage or confidence

Etymology

From Latin audācia (boldness), from audāx (bold).
