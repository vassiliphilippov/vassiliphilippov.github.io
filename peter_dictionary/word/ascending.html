ascending

1. ADJ

/ əˈsɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
moving or going upward; rising from a lower to a higher position or level.

Examples

Facilitatory processes take place between neocortex and hypothalamus via ascending and descending pathways.
Certainly its composer was an ascending star on a new world horizon.

Synset Examples

the ascending plane
the ascending staircase
the ascending stems of chickweed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: descending - moving or going downward

Etymology

From Latin ascendere (to climb up) via the verb ascend.
