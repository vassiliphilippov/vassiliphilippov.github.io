salty

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɒlti / (AmE), / ˈsɔːlti / (BrE)
(of language, wit, or humor) engagingly stimulating, provocative, or slightly coarse, often in a way that is lively or piquant.

Examples

This masterpiece has gaiety, too, but it is the gaiety of dancing people: earthy, salty and humorous.

Synset Examples

a piquant wit
salty language

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piquant - pleasantly stimulating or exciting to the mind; having a lively character
