instructions

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz / (BrE)
A booklet or manual accompanying a product, especially a technical device, that explains how to set up or operate it.

Examples

More detailed instructions may be obtained from your local building supply houses and craftsmen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manual - a booklet with instructions explaining installation or operation

Etymology

From Latin 'instructio' (arrangement, teaching), from 'instruere' (to build, teach).
