enthusiasm

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈθuziˌæzəm / (AmE), / ɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm / (BrE)
A strong feeling of excitement or lively interest in something.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passion - strong or barely controllable emotion; intense enthusiasm
  • Antonym: apathy - lack of interest or enthusiasm

Etymology

From Greek 'enthousiasmos' (divine inspiration, enthusiasm).

2. NOUN

/ ɪnˈθuziˌæzəm / (AmE), / ɪnˈθjuːziˌæzəm / (BrE)
A strong feeling of excitement or eagerness about something; intense and eager enjoyment or approval.

Examples

Related Terms

Etymology
