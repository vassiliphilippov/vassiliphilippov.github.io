victoriously

1. ADV

/ vɪkˈtɔriəsli / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows victory or triumph over others.

Examples

They all prayed now that the North would realize that peace must come, for Virginia had defended her land victoriously.

Synset Examples

Virginia had defended her land victoriously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: triumphantly - in a triumphant manner; exhibiting victory

Etymology

From 'victorious' + '-ly'. 'Victorious' from Latin 'victoriosus', from 'victor' (conqueror).
