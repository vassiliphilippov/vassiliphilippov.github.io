Attica

1. PROPN

/ ˈætɪkə / (AmE), / ˈætɪkə / (BrE)
the region surrounding Athens in ancient Greece, known for its historical and cultural significance

Examples

Attica, though important, was not the only teacher of this age.

Etymology

From Ancient Greek Ἀττική (Attikē), the region around Athens.
