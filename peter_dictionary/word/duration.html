duration

1. NOUN

/ dʊˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / djʊəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
The property or quality of lasting or continuing over a period of time.

Examples

With the exception of treaty making, foreign relations were historically concerned for the most part with conditions of short or at least measurable duration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: length - measurement of how long something is in space or time

Etymology

From Latin 'duratio' (a lasting), from 'durare' (to last).
