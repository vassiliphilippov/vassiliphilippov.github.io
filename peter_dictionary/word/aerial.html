aerial

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛriəl / (AmE), / ˈeəriəl / (BrE)
(in American football) a forward pass to a receiver downfield from the passer.

Examples

In the past the club depended largely on Van Brocklin's aerials.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forward pass - a pass of the ball in American football thrown from behind the line of scrimmage to a receiver farther downfield
