filming

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or process of making a film or movie, including recording images and scenes with a camera

Examples

Neither of them, I understood, had been present at the filming session earlier.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting - the act of filming or taking pictures, especially in cinema or photography

Etymology

From 'film' + '-ing', denoting the action or process.
