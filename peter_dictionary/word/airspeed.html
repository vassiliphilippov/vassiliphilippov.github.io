airspeed

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌspiːd / (AmE), / ˈeəspiːd / (BrE)
the speed of an aircraft relative to the air through which it is moving

Examples

Greg's airspeed indicator was over 350 when he leveled off just above the trees.
His air speed dropped until he thought he would spin out.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: groundspeed - the speed of an aircraft relative to the ground

Etymology

Compound of "air" and "speed."
