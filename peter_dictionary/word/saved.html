saved

1. ADJ

/ seɪvd / (AmE), / seɪvd / (BrE)
(in Christian belief) delivered from sin and its consequences; having gained salvation.

Examples

Two thieves are crucified with Christ, one saved and the other damned.

Synset Examples

a saved soul

Related Terms

  • Antonym: damned - condemned to eternal punishment (religious context)
  • Synonym: redeemed - saved or delivered from sin
