foodstuff

1. NOUN

/ ˈfuːdˌstʌf / (AmE), / ˈfuːdˌstʌf / (BrE)
A substance suitable for consumption as food; usually used in the plural to refer to foods or groceries sold for consumption.

Examples

In a total blockade, action could also be taken against ships bringing in chemicals, oils, textiles, and even foodstuffs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provision - supplies of food, especially for a journey or for military use

Etymology

From 'food' + 'stuff', with 'stuff' meaning material or matter.
