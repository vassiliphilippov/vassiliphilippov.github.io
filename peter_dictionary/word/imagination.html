imagination

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌmadʒɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
The ability of the mind to form images or ideas of things not present to the senses or not considered real.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creativity - the use of imagination or original ideas to create something
  • Synonym: fantasy - the faculty or activity of imagining things, especially things that are impossible or improbable

Etymology

From Latin imaginatio (representation, image, idea), from imaginari (to picture to oneself).
