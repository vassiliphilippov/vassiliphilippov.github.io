decisively

1. ADV

/ dɪˈsaɪsɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪsɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows firmness and determination; with decision and resolve.

Examples

And contrary to what has been said recently, we did not wait for "outside pressures "and "world opinion "to bring down that Communist government; we moved decisively to effect an anti Communist coup d' etat.

Synset Examples

`I will come along,' she said decisively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firmly - in a strong, resolute manner
  • Antonym: hesitantly - in a tentative or unsure manner

Etymology

From 'decisive' + '-ly'. 'Decisive' from French décisif, from Latin decidere (to decide).
