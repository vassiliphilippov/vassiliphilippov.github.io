unachievable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnəˈtʃivəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnəˈtʃiːvəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to achieve or accomplish.

Examples

I would propose, first, an abandonment of attempts at a universal lexical list, as intrinsically unachievable, and operationally inadequate in proportion as it is achieved.

Synset Examples

an unattainable goal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattainable - impossible to reach or achieve
  • Antonym: achievable - able to be achieved or accomplished

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'achievable', from 'achieve' (from Old French 'achever').
