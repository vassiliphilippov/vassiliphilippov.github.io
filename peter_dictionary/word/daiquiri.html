daiquiri

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪkəri / (AmE), / ˈdækəri / (BrE)
A cocktail made with rum and lime or lemon juice, often sweetened and sometimes blended with fruit.

Examples

"Just befoh he left foh his academeh we wuh hevin dack-rihs on the vuhranduh, Major Roebuck an Ah, an Huhmun says' May Ah hev one too'?
WAITER: Banana Daiquiri, si senor.

Etymology

Named after Daiquiri, a village in Cuba where the drink originated.
