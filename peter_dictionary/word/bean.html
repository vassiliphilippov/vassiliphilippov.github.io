bean

1. NOUN

/ bin / (AmE), / biːn / (BrE)
an edible seed, typically in a pod, of various plants in the legume family, often eaten as food

Examples

And indeed the young garden beans were brackish in her mouth.
She swung her eyes up to the blue of the window, her jaws gently mashing the bitter beans.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lentil - a small, lens-shaped edible seed from a plant in the legume family
  • Synonym: pea - a small, round green legume, also used as food

Etymology

From Old English 'bēan', related to Dutch 'boon' and German 'Bohne'.
