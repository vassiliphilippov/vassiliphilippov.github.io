stager

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person who supervises and manages the staging and physical production aspects of a theatrical show or event

Examples

Martin Tahse has established quite a reputation for himself as a successful stager of touring productions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stage manager - person responsible for the technical and logistical aspects of a theater production

Etymology

From 'stage' + '-er', meaning one who stages (produces) a show.
