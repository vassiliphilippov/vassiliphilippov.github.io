weirdly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪrdli / (AmE), / ˈwɪədli / (BrE)
In a strange or unusual manner.

Examples

At the slope's base coils of concertina stretched out of eye range like a wild tangle of children's hoops, stopped simultaneously, weirdly poised as if awaiting the magic of the child's touch to start them all rolling again.

Synset Examples

she was dressed weirdly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strangely - in a strange manner
  • Antonym: normally - in a normal or usual manner

Etymology

Formed from 'weird' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
