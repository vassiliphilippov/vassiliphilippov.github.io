exultation

1. NOUN

/ ɛɡˌzʌlˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɛɡˌzʌlˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
A feeling or expression of great joy, triumph, or jubilation.

Examples

In France he had puzzled the meaning of the great stone monuments men had thrown up to the sky, and always as he wandered, he felt a stranger to their exultation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jubilation - a feeling of great happiness and triumph
  • Synonym: elation - great happiness or exhilaration
  • Antonym: despair - the complete loss or absence of hope

Etymology

From Latin exsultatio, from exsultare (to leap up, rejoice).
