sensuous

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛnʃuəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnʃuəs / (BrE)
Taking pleasure in or appreciative of beauty or sensory pleasure, especially in a way that emphasizes the senses rather than intellect.

Examples

The term idea refers to our more reflective or thoughtful consciousness as opposed to the immediacies of sensuous or emotional experience.

Synset Examples

the sensuous joy from all things fair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensual - relating to or gratifying the physical senses, especially in a sexual way

Etymology

From Latin sensus (sense) + -ous (full of).
