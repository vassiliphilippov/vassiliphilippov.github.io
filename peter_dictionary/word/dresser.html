dresser

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsə / (BrE)
A piece of furniture with drawers, typically used for storing clothes.

Examples

I'm going to put it on my dresser ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chest of drawers - a piece of furniture with drawers stacked vertically, used for storing clothes

Etymology

From Old French 'dresseur' (one who arranges), from 'dresser' (to arrange).
