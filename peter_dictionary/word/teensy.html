teensy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtiːnsi / (AmE), / ˈtiːnsi / (BrE)
Very small; tiny (used informally).

Examples

Just a big pile of self-confidence in an almost teensy package.

Synset Examples

a wee tot

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiny - very small in size
  • Synonym: wee - very small (often used in Scottish English)
  • Antonym: huge - very large in size
