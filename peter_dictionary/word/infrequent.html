infrequent

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈfriːkwənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfriːkwənt / (BrE)
not happening often; occurring rarely or at long intervals

Examples

Hino talked very little about himself except for the infrequent times when he used a personal illustration in connection with another subject.

Synset Examples

infrequent outbursts of temper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rare - not occurring often
  • Synonym: occasional - happening from time to time
  • Antonym: frequent - happening often
  • Antonym: regular - happening at uniform intervals

Etymology

From Latin prefix *in-* (not) + *frequens* (crowded, frequent).
