brushed

1. ADJ

/ brʌʃt / (AmE), / brʌʃt / (BrE)
(Of hair or clothing) groomed or made neat by using a brush.

Examples

The velvet smoking jackets, pearl gray, wine, and blue, which Miss Ada had bought him hung brushed and unworn in the closet.

Synset Examples

with shining hair neatly brushed
the freshly brushed clothes hung in the closet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: combed - (Of hair) made neat by using a comb
