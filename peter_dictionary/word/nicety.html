nicety

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪsəti / (AmE), / ˈnaɪsəti / (BrE)
The quality of being in accordance with refined standards of correctness, precision, or propriety.

Examples

Some distribution costs are kept up by competitive pressure, some by the fact that the customers have come to expect certain niceties and flourishes.

Synset Examples

it was performed with justness and beauty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precision - exactness or accuracy

Etymology

From Old French *niceté* (foolishness, delicacy), from Latin *nescitus* (ignorant).
