Sicily

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪsəli / (AmE), / ˈsɪsɪli / (BrE)
a large island and region in southern Italy, situated in the Mediterranean Sea, known for its rich history and distinctive culture

Examples

A Tibetan swami from Palermo, Sicily, announced in Beverly Hills a newly discovered, ancient yoga discipline for ripple breathing which increased both pranha and cosmic attraction between sexes.

Etymology

From Latin 'Sicilia', from Ancient Greek 'Σικελία' (Sikelía).
