authenticate

1. VERB

/ əˈθɛntɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to prove or show that something is genuine, true, or valid, especially by means of evidence or verification

Examples

And no messages can be transmitted on these circuits until senders and receivers authenticate in advance, by special codes, that the messages actually come from their purported sources.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verify - to make sure or demonstrate that something is true, accurate, or justified
  • Synonym: validate - to check or prove the validity or accuracy of something

Etymology

From Medieval Latin authenticare, from late Latin authenticus (genuine, authentic)
