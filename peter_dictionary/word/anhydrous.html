anhydrous

1. ADJ

/ ænˈhaɪdrəs / (AmE), / ænˈhɪdrəs / (BrE)
(of a substance) containing no water, especially water of crystallization.

Examples

Spectra were also obtained from a third sample of f which had been diluted to three times its original volume with powdered, anhydrous** alundum (**f).

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hydrous - containing water, especially as chemically bound water of crystallization

Etymology

From Greek an- (without) + hydōr (water).
