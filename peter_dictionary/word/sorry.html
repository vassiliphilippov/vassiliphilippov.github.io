sorry

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɑːri / (AmE), / ˈsɒri / (BrE)
feeling regret or sadness about something that one has done or failed to do; feeling or expressing apology or sympathy.

Examples

I'’m sorry, Mrs. Corleone, we'’re not to let you through.

Synset Examples

felt regretful over his vanished youth
regretful over mistakes she had made
he felt bad about breaking the vase

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regretful - feeling or showing regret
  • Synonym: remorseful - full of remorse; deeply regretful
  • Antonym: unrepentant - not feeling regret or remorse for one's actions

Etymology

From Old English 'sārig', meaning 'distressed, grieved'.
