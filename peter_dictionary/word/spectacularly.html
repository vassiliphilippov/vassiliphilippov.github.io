spectacularly

1. ADV

/ spəkˈtækjəlɚli / (AmE), / spɛkˈtækjʊləli / (BrE)
In a way that is very impressive, dramatic, or striking; in a spectacular manner.

Examples

Often these exercises work well for some bodybuilders but less spectacularly for others.
Such items recall the California journalist who reported an accident involving a movie star: "The area in which Miss N - was injured is spectacularly scenic ".

Synset Examples

the area was spectacularly scenic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dramatically - in a striking or impressive manner
  • Antonym: modestly - in a manner that is not showy or spectacular

Etymology

From 'spectacular' + '-ly'. 'Spectacular' from Latin 'spectaculum' (a show).
