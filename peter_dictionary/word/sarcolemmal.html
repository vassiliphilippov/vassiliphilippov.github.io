sarcolemmal

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɑːr.kəˈlɛm.əl / (AmE), / ˌsɑː.kəˈlɛm.əl / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the sarcolemma, the membrane surrounding a muscle fiber

Examples

In February, 1959, during the second admission to The New York Hospital, a biopsy specimen of the left gastrocnemius showed striking increase in the sarcolemmal sheath nuclei and shrunken muscle fibers in several sections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sarcolemma - the cell membrane surrounding a muscle fiber

Etymology

From sarcolemma (the cell membrane of a muscle fiber) + -al (forming adjectives).
