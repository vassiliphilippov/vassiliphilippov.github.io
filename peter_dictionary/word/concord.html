Concord

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑnkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋkɔːd / (BrE)
The capital city of the state of New Hampshire, located in the south-central part of the state on the Merrimack River.

Examples

The rider from Concord was as good as his word.

Etymology

From Latin 'concordia' (agreement, harmony), chosen to reflect unity.
