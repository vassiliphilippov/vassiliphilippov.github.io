pizza

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
A round, flat Italian dish made of thin bread dough, typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients, then baked.

Examples

Now, you no doubt regard the preceding as pap; you 're tooling around full of gage in your hot rods, gorging yourselves on pizza and playing pinball in the taverns and generally behaving like Ubermenschen.

Etymology

From Italian *pizza* (flatbread), of uncertain origin.
