vesicular

1. ADJ

/ vəˈsɪkjələr / (AmE), / vɛˈsɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or composed of vesicles, especially small fluid-filled sacs in the body or cells.

Examples

Occasionally there were small basophilic fibers that were devoid of myofibrillae and contained many vesicular nuclei with prominent nucleoli (Fig. 5).

Synset Examples

normal vesicular breathing

Etymology

From Latin 'vesicula' (small bladder, diminutive of 'vesica').
