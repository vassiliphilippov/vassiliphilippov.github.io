1. NOUN

/ riˈæk.ʃən / (AmE), / riˈæk.ʃən / (BrE)
An emotional or mental response to a specific event, situation, or experience.

Examples

Synset Examples

his reaction to the news was to start planning what to do

Related Terms

  • Synonym: response - a verbal or written answer; a reaction to something
  • Degree Higher: emotion - a strong feeling deriving from one's circumstances, mood, or relationships
  • Degree Lower: impulse - a sudden strong and unreflective urge or desire to act

Etymology

From Latin 'reactio', from 're-' (again) + 'actio' (action).
