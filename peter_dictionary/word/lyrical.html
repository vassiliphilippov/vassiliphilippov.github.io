lyrical

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪrɪkəl / (BrE)
Expressing deep personal emotion or suggestive of singing; suitable for or reminiscent of song.

Examples

Badura-Skoda-Vienna Konzerthaus (Westminster) and Demus-Schubert Quartet (Deutsche Grammophon) are both warm toned, pleasantly lyrical, but rather slack and tensionless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poetic - having qualities of poetry or expressing beautiful or imaginative thoughts

Etymology

From Greek *lyrikos* (pertaining to the lyre or song), via Latin *lyricus*.
