suspiciously

1. ADV

/ səˈspɪʃəsli / (AmE), / səˈspɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows doubt or distrust; with suspicion.

Examples

She glinted suspiciously at the dish before her: "Blowfish.
Griffith looked at him suspiciously through red-rimmed eyes.

Synset Examples

she regarded the food suspiciously

Related Terms

  • Antonym: trustfully - in a trusting manner, without suspicion

Etymology

From "suspicious" + "-ly".
