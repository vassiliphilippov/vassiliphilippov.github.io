elemental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛləˈmɛn(t)l / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪˈmɛntl / (BrE)
relating to or being an element, especially a chemical element in its pure, uncombined form

Examples

Because of the simplicity of the molecules, isotopic exchange reactions between elemental halogens and the corresponding carbon tetrahalides would appear to offer particularly fruitful possibilities for obtaining unambiguous basic kinetic data.

Synset Examples

elemental sulphur

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elementary - relating to the basic or fundamental aspects of something

Etymology

From Latin elementum (element), with '-al' forming the adjective.
