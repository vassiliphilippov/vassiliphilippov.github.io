kitten

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtən / (AmE), / ˈkɪtn̩ / (BrE)
A young cat, especially one that is not yet fully grown.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Middle English *kitoun*, from Old French *chitoun*, diminutive of *chat* (cat).
