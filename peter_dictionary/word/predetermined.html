predetermined

1. ADJ

/ ˌpriː.dɪˈtɝː.mɪnd / (AmE), / ˌpriː.dɪˈtɜː.mɪnd / (BrE)
Decided or arranged in advance; established or fixed beforehand.

Examples

Text reading is terminated when a pre-determined number of forms have been stored in the text form list.

Synset Examples

a preset plan of action
at a predetermined time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preset - set in advance; established beforehand

Etymology

From pre- + determine, meaning to determine beforehand; 'determine' from Latin determinare (to limit, fix).
