biomedical

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˈmɛd.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊˈmɛd.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Relating to the application of the natural sciences, especially biology and physiology, to clinical medicine.

Examples

In this article we will concentrate on the advances in the application of electronics in bio-medical research laboratories because this is where tomorrow's commonplace equipment originates.

Synset Examples

biomedical research laboratory

Etymology

From bio- (life) + medical, referring to the science of medicine based on biology.
