unwrinkled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈrɪŋkəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪŋk(ə)ld / (BrE)
(of a surface, fabric, or skin) having no wrinkles or creases; smooth.

Examples

It was a Cadillac, black grayed with the dust of the road, its windows closed tight so you knew that the people who climbed out of it would be cool and unwrinkled.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wrinkled - having wrinkles or creases

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' (not) to 'wrinkled'.
