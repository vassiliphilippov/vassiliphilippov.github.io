primacy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪməsi / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪməsi / (BrE)
The state of being first in importance or rank; preeminence.

Examples

This explains some group ends and provides a justification of their primacy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preeminence - the fact of surpassing all others; superiority
  • Antonym: inferiority - the quality of being lower in status or quality

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *primatia* (first place, leadership), from Latin *prīmus* (first).
