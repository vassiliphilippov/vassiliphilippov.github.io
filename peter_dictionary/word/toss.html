/ tɑs / (AmE), / tɒs / (BrE)

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chuck - to throw something carelessly or with little precision

Etymology

Probably of Scandinavian origin; compare Old Norse tǫs (heap, bundle) or dialectal Norwegian tossa (to swing, dash)
