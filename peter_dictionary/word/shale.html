shale

1. NOUN

/ ʃeɪl / (AmE), / ʃeɪl / (BrE)
A fine-grained sedimentary rock formed from layers of clay, often splitting into thin, flat pieces.

Examples

Shale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slate - a fine-grained metamorphic rock that can be split into thin layers

Etymology

From German 'Schiefer', meaning 'slate' or 'shale'.
