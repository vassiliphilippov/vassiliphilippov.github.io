chilly

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli / (BrE)
(of a person, manner, or atmosphere) not showing or expressing warmth or emotion; cold or unfriendly.

Examples

The already faded pastel charms of the naive music itself vanish entirely in Fistoulari's melodramatic contrasts between ultra vehement brute power and chilly, if suave, sentimentality.

Synset Examples

a female form in marble--a chilly but ideal medium for depicting abstract virtues

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold - emotionally unfeeling or distant
  • Antonym: warm - showing enthusiasm, kindness, or affection; emotionally responsive
