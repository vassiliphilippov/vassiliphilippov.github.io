lad

1. NOUN

/ læd / (AmE), / læd / (BrE)
a boy or young man; a male child, often used as a familiar or affectionate term of address

Examples

This happy, always smiling lad with the sunny disposition is our new Junior Mr. Canada - Henri de Courcy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boy - a male child or young man
  • Antonym: girl - a female child or young woman

Etymology

From Middle English ladde, of uncertain origin, possibly from Old Norse laddi (young man, servant).
