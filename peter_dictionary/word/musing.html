musing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmjuzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪŋ / (BrE)
A period of deep and reflective thought; contemplation or meditation.

Examples

At the same time another child - this one of Shelley's brain - was given to the world: Alastor, a poem of pervading beauty in which the reader may gaze into the still depths of a fine mind's musings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contemplation - the act of looking thoughtfully at something for a long time; deep reflective thought
  • Synonym: meditation - the act of thinking deeply or focusing one's mind for a period of time
  • Degree Lower: daydream - a series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present

Etymology

From the verb 'muse', from Old French *muser* (to ponder, to dream).
