reporting

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the presentation of news and information by journalists in newspapers, on radio, or on television

Examples

The second step is to recognize the substantial agreement - frequently blurred by emotionalism and inaccurate newspaper reporting - already existing between Catholics and non-Catholics concerning the over-all objectives of family planning.

Synset Examples

they accused the paper of biased coverage of race relations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coverage - news as presented by media outlets
  • Synonym: reportage - the reporting of news, especially by journalists

Etymology

From report + -ing, report from Latin reportare (to bring back).
