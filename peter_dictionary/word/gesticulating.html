gesticulating

1. ADJ

/ dʒɛˈstɪkjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Making gestures, especially while speaking.

Examples

At once my ears were drowned by a flow of what I took to be Spanish, but - the driver's white teeth flashing at me, the road wildly veering beyond his glistening hair, beyond his gesticulating bottle - it could have been the purest Oxford English I was half hearing; I would n't have known the difference.

Synset Examples

her gesticulating hands and arms made words almost unnecessary

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gesticulate - to make gestures, especially while speaking

Etymology

From Latin gesticulatus (to gesture), from gesticulare.
