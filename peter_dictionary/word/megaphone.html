megaphone

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛɡəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡəfəʊn / (BrE)
A cone-shaped device held to the mouth to amplify and direct the human voice, often used to address large crowds.

Examples

[MAN ON MEGAPHONE CHANTING SLOGANS IN SPANISH] [CROWD CHEERING] CROWD: [CHANTING] Fidel!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullhorn - an electrically powered portable device for amplifying the voice

Etymology

From Greek 'mega-' (great) + 'phone' (voice, sound).
