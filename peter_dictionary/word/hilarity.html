hilarity

1. NOUN

/ hɪˈlærəti / (AmE), / hɪˈlarəti / (BrE)
Great laughter or cheerful merriment.

Examples

his scrawny, tottering sound adds a delightful hilarity to the performance.)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mirth - amusement or laughter; joyfulness
  • Antonym: gloom - a state of sadness or depression

Etymology

From Latin *hilaritas*, from Greek *hilaros* (cheerful, merry).
