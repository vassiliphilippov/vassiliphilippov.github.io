cheque

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
A written order to a bank to pay a stated sum from the drawer's account to the person named in the order.

Examples

Within an hour, he signed a release for a certified cheque of 1,000 dollars.

Synset Examples

he paid all his bills by check

Related Terms

  • Synonym: check - written order directing a bank to pay money (American spelling)

Etymology

From French *chèque*, from English *check*; influenced by the 'check' mark as an indication of verification or control.
