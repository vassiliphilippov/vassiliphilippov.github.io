pâté

1. NOUN

/ pɑːˈteɪ / (AmE), / ˈpæteɪ / (BrE)
a mixture of finely minced or ground meat, liver, or fowl, often seasoned and served as a spread or loaf

Examples

That'’s a Ritz cracker and chopped liver!" [LAUGHING] Bring out the peppers and sardines!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrine - a dish similar to pâté, often baked in a pottery container
  • Synonym: liverwurst - a seasoned sausage made of liver and pork

Etymology

From French 'pâté', meaning a paste or pie.
