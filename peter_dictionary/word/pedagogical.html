pedagogical

1. ADJ

/ ˌpɛdəˈɡɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛdəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to teaching or education; concerning the principles, methods, or practice of teaching.

Examples

But Schnabel was a great teacher in addition to being a great performer, and the fact that four of the ten versions I listened to are by Schnabel pupils (Clifford Curzon, Frank Glazer, Adrian Aeschbacher, and Victor Babin) also sheds light on the master's pedagogical skills.

Synset Examples

pedagogical significance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - relating to education or the provision of education

Etymology

From French 'pédagogique', from Greek 'paidagōgikos' (pertaining to a teacher).
