unerringly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈɜr.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ʌnˈɜː.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that never makes mistakes; with unfailing accuracy or certainty.

Examples

Starting with the room completely blacked out, as it was when we came in, he unerringly fixed things so that the whole place was bathed in the maximum of light without at any point admitting even so much as a crack of glare.

Synset Examples

he unerringly fixed things for us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accurately - in a way that is correct and without mistakes

Etymology

Formed from 'unerring' (not making errors) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
