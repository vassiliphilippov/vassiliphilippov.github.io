stylemark

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪlˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a distinctive feature or characteristic, especially one that identifies a particular person, group, or thing

Examples

What I have observed time and time again is a process of integration, integration that begins as abstract design and gradually takes on recognizable form; color patterns that are made to weave throughout the whole composition; and that over all, amazing control of large washes which is the Mason stylemark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hallmark - a mark or feature indicating distinctive quality
  • Synonym: trademark - a distinctive feature associated with a person or thing

Etymology

Formed from 'style' + 'mark', indicating a mark of style or identity.
