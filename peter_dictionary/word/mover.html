mover

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvər / (AmE), / ˈmuːvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to move people's belongings from one place to another, especially as an employee of a moving company

Examples

"Look out for those movers "!
Neither of them trusted the movers.
"At least you could leave it for the movers ", Miss Ada said.

Synset Examples

the movers were very careful with the grand piano

Related Terms

  • Synonym: removalist - a person who moves household or office goods; used especially in Australia and the UK

Etymology

From move + -er, meaning 'one who moves'.
