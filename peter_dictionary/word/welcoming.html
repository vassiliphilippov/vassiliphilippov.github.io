welcoming

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɛlkəmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəmɪŋ / (BrE)
Behaving in a friendly and hospitable way; giving a warm reception.

Examples

My parents' welcoming arms would seem woeful, inadequate, unwanted.

Synset Examples

a welcoming smile

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unwelcoming - not friendly or making others feel unwelcome
  • Synonym: friendly - kind and pleasant

Etymology

From 'welcome' (from Old English 'wilcuma') + '-ing'.
