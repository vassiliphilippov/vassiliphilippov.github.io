apparition

1. NOUN

/ ˌæpəˈrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌapəˈrɪʃən / (BrE)
An appearance of a ghost or ghostlike figure; a supernatural or unexplained manifestation, especially one that is unexpected or startling.

Examples

We were not, however, prepared for anything like the apparition that confronted us as Felix came up the stairs.
He's a phantom, an apparition.

Synset Examples

we were unprepared for the apparition that confronted us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person, appearing to the living
  • Synonym: phantom - something seen, heard, or sensed, but having no physical reality

Etymology

From Latin 'apparitio', from 'apparere' (to appear).
