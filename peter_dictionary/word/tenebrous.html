tenebrous

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛn.ə.brəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.ɪ.brəs / (BrE)
Dark, shadowy, or obscure; gloomy.

Examples

Life was a short play of tenebrous shadows.

Synset Examples

a tenebrous cave

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dark - having little or no light
  • Synonym: gloomy - partially or totally dark; dismal

Etymology

From Latin tenebrosus, from tenebrae meaning 'darkness'.
