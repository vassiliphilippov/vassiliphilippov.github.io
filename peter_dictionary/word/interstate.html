interstate

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪntərˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌsteɪt / (BrE)
existing or occurring between, or involving, different states, especially within the United States

Examples

The latter provisions proscribe any contract, combination, or conspiracy in restraint of interstate or foreign trade, and monopolization of, or attempts, combinations, or conspiracies to monopolize, such trade.

Synset Examples

Interstate Highway Commission
interstate highways
Interstate Commerce Commission
interstate commerce

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intrastate - existing or occurring within a single state

Etymology

From inter- (between) + state, referring to US states.
