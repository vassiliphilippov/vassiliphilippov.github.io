lasagne

1. NOUN

/ ləˈzɑnjə / (AmE), / ləˈzan.jə / (BrE)
An Italian baked dish consisting of layers of wide pasta, sauce, cheese, and meat or vegetables.

Examples

You like your lasagne?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lasagna - Alternate spelling of lasagne, especially in American English

Etymology

From Italian 'lasagna', plural 'lasagne', meaning 'pasta in wide strips', from Latin 'lasanum' (cooking pot).
