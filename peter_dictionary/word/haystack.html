haystack

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪˌstæk / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌstak / (BrE)
A large, organized pile or mound of hay, typically found on a farm and used for storage.

Examples

If someone were to drop a match in here, this place would go up like a haystack ".

Etymology

From 'hay' (grass cut and dried for fodder) + 'stack' (a pile or heap).
