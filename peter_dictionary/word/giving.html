giving

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of giving; bestowing or donating something to someone.

Examples

Even if that's all the promise he ever gave or ever will give, the giving of it once was enough and you believed it then and you will always believe it, even when it is finally the only thing in the world you have left to believe, and the whole world is telling you that one was a lie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift - a thing given willingly to someone without payment; a present
  • Antonym: receiving - the act of getting or accepting something that is given

Etymology

From the verb 'give' + -ing suffix.
