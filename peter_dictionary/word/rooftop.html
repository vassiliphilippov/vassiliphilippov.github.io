rooftop

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːf.tɑp / (AmE), / ˈruːf.tɒp / (BrE)
The top surface or outer covering of a building's roof, especially when flat or accessible.

Examples

Ludie took his slingshot and climbed to the rooftop to shoot at crows.
Snow buried the streets and covered the slanting rooftops, as John trudged toward St. Peter's.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roof - the structure forming the upper covering of a building

Etymology

Formed from 'roof' + 'top'.
