turban

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝː.bən / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.bən / (BrE)
A traditional headdress made by winding a long scarf or piece of cloth around the head, commonly worn in South Asian, Middle Eastern, and some African cultures, especially among Muslim men.

Examples

The head was wrapped in a turban and on top of the turban rode a great hamper across which a piece of poncho had been flung.

Etymology

From Persian 'dulband' via Turkish 'tülbent'.
