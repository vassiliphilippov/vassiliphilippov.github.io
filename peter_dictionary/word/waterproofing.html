waterproofing

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚˌprufɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or act of making an object or material resistant to the penetration of water

Examples

An alternate roof, perhaps more within do-it-yourself reach, could be constructed of heavy wooden roof beams, overlaid with boards and waterproofing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sealing - the act of closing something to prevent escape or entry of substances
  • Synonym: coating - a layer of a substance spread over a surface for protection

Etymology

From 'waterproof' + '-ing', with 'waterproof' from 'water' + 'proof' (resistant).
