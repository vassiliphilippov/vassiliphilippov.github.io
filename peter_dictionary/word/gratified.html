gratified

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræt.ɪ.faɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡræt.ɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
feeling pleased or satisfied because one has received what was desired or expected.

Examples

Gratified fishermen hauled them in by the thousands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satisfied - pleased because you have achieved something or received what you wanted
  • Antonym: disappointed - unhappy because something you hoped for did not happen

Etymology

Past participle of 'gratify', from Latin 'gratificari' (to do a favor).
