long-lasting

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɔŋˌlæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌlɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
Continuing to exist or have an effect for a long period of time.

Examples

I just didn't expect the storm would last as long as it has.

Synset Examples

hopes for a durable peace
a long-lasting friendship

Related Terms

  • Synonym: durable - able to withstand wear, pressure, or damage; hard-wearing
  • Antonym: fleeting - lasting for a very short time
