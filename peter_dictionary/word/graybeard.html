graybeard

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌbɪəd / (BrE)
an old man, especially one with a gray beard; often used to refer to a man respected for his age and experience

Examples

For almost two months, the defendant and the world heard from individuals escaped from the grave about fathers and mothers, graybeards, adolescents, babies, starved, beaten to death, strangled, machine-gunned, gassed, burned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: old man - an elderly man

Etymology

From 'gray' + 'beard', referring to the common appearance of gray facial hair in elderly men.
