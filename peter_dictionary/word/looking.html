looking

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
The action of directing one's eyes toward something in order to see or observe it.

Examples

Instead of the observer's eye the image orthicon in the TV camera does the "looking ".

Synset Examples

he went out to have a look
his look was fixed on her eyes
he gave it a good looking at
his camera does his looking for him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: look - an act of directing one's gaze at something
