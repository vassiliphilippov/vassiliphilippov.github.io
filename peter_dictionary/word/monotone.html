monotone

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːnəˌtoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɒnətəʊn / (BrE)
(of a sequence or function) always increasing or always decreasing, never both; not changing direction in value.

Examples

Related Terms

Etymology
