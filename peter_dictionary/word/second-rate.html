second-rate

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛkənd ˈreɪt / (AmE), / ˌsekənd ˈreɪt / (BrE)
not of the best quality; mediocre or inferior in standard or value

Examples

The first was that America had become - or was in danger of becoming - a second-rate military power.

Synset Examples

they improved the quality from mediocre to above average

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good
  • Antonym: first-rate - of the best quality; excellent
  • Degree Lower: third-rate - of very poor quality

Etymology

From the ranking or grading of goods or people, with 'second-rate' meaning not in the first or top rank.
