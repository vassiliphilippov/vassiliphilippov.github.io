luckily

1. ADV

/ ˈlʌk.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈlʌk.ɪ.li / (BrE)
By good fortune; as a result of luck rather than planning.

Examples

Miraculously, the bottle was still in my hand, foam still geysering over my (luckily) waterproof watch.

Synset Examples

fortunately the weather was good

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fortunately - by good fortune; as luck would have it
  • Antonym: unluckily - by bad fortune; unfortunately

Etymology

Formed from 'luck' + '-ily', following standard adverbial formation.
