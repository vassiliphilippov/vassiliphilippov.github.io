unilateral

1. ADJ

/ ˌjuː.nəˈlæt̬.ɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪˈlæt.ər.əl / (BrE)
Involving or affecting only one side, party, or group; performed or decided by one party without the agreement or participation of others.

Examples

Should the action be unilateral or multilateral?

Synset Examples

unilateral paralysis
a unilateral decision

Related Terms

  • Antonym: multilateral - involving more than two sides or parties

Etymology

From Latin 'unus' (one) + 'lateralis' (of a side).
