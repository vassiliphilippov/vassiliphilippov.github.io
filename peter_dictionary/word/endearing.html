endearing

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
Inspiring affection or fondness, especially by being lovable in a charming or naive way.

Examples

This is the most endearing of Lao ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorable - inspiring great affection or delight
  • Synonym: lovable - easy to love; inspiring love or affection
  • Antonym: off-putting - unpleasant or repellent

Etymology

From the verb 'endear', which derives from Middle English 'enderen', from 'dear' meaning beloved or precious.
