lousiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊzi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlaʊzi.nɪs / (BrE)
Infestation with lice, especially the human louse (Pediculus humanus), resulting in severe itching.

Examples

Lousiness is a prerequisite for epidemic typhus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pediculosis - infestation with lice

Etymology

From 'lousy' + '-ness', where 'lousy' derives from 'louse' (Old English lūs).
