head-on

1. ADV

/ hɛd ˈɑn / (AmE), / hɛd ˈɒn / (BrE)
In a direct or unyielding manner; facing or dealing with a problem or opponent directly, without avoidance.

Examples

He 'll even bull head-on into the rules when he is sure he's right.

Synset Examples

we must meet the problem head-on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: directly - without delay or deviation
  • Antonym: indirectly - not in a direct way; by an indirect route or means
