gummy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʌmi / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmi / (BrE)
having a sticky consistency like glue or an adhesive; sticky

Examples

Light sticks of fingers, the tips gummy with dark earth, patted at Watson's throat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sticky - having the property of adhering or sticking to a surface
  • Synonym: gluey - resembling or having the properties of glue

Etymology

From gum (sticky resin) + -y; gum from Old French gomme, from Latin gummi.
