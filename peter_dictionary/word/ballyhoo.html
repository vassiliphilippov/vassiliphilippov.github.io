ballyhoo

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæliˌhu / (AmE), / ˈbæliˌhuː / (BrE)
extravagant or sensational publicity or promotion, especially one that draws excessive attention

Examples

No amount of ballyhoo will cover up the sordid facts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hype - extravagant or intensive publicity or promotion

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from circus or carnival slang in late 19th-century America.
