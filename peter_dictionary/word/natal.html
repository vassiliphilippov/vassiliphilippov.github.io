natal

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪ.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.təl / (BrE)
Relating to or associated with birth.

Examples

And how very often a water plane is featured in his landscapes, and how appropriate that he should appear in American Artist again, in his natal month of March!

Synset Examples

natal injuries
natal day
natal influences

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postnatal - occurring after birth
  • Antonym: prenatal - occurring before birth

Etymology

From Latin natalis (of or relating to birth), from natus (born).
