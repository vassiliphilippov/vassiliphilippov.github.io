Reno

1. PROPN

/ ˈriːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈriːnəʊ / (BrE)
A city in western Nevada, United States, famous for its gambling casinos and permissive divorce laws.

Examples

Okay, kid, you have to go to Reno with your pop.

Etymology

Named after Major General Jesse L. Reno, a Union officer in the American Civil War.
