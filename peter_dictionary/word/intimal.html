intimal

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪn.tɪ.məl / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.məl / (BrE)
relating to the intima, the innermost layer of a blood vessel or organ

Examples

The intimal surface of the aorta was covered with confluent, yellow-brown, hard, friable plaques along its entire course, and there was a marked narrowing of the orifices of the large major visceral arteries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intima - the innermost layer of a blood vessel or organ

Etymology

From 'intima', the innermost layer of a structure, itself from Latin 'intimus' (inmost).
