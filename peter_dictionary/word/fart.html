fart

1. NOUN

/ fɑrt / (AmE), / fɑːt / (BrE)
an instance of releasing gas from the bowels through the anus, often accompanied by a sound or odor

Examples

A man up and vanished like a fart in the wind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flatulence - the accumulation of gas in the alimentary canal

Etymology

From Middle English 'farten', from Old English 'feortan', from Proto-Germanic '*fertaną'.
