striving

1. NOUN

/ ˈstraɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstraɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making a great effort to achieve or obtain something

Examples

His unsuccessful strivings to give up drink are represented as religious strivings; he keeps a bottle in a wardrobe at home, and "before this wardrobe Praisegod Piepsam had before now gone literally on his knees, and in his wrestlings had bitten his tongue - and still in the end capitulated ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: effort - a vigorous or determined attempt
  • Synonym: struggle - a determined effort under difficulty

Etymology

From the verb 'strive' + suffix '-ing'. 'Strive' from Old French 'estriver', of Germanic origin.
