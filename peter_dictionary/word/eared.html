eared

1. ADJ

/ ɪrd / (AmE), / ɪəd / (BrE)
(of a book, page, or similar object) worn or shabby from overuse, especially having corners turned down.

Examples

There was little enough time to contemplate them, however; in an instant the doctor was stalking across the room with an antique ledger in his hands, thoroughly eared and big as a table top.

Synset Examples

a somewhat dog-eared duke...a bit run down
an old book with dog-eared pages

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog-eared - shabby or worn, especially with turned-down page corners
