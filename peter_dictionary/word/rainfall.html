rainfall

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪnˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈreɪnfɔːl / (BrE)
the amount of rain that falls in a particular area during a particular period of time

Examples

Lack of rainfall and extreme temperatures may call for the development of shade and irrigation of a site to make it useable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precipitation - the amount of rain, snow, etc. that falls in a place

Etymology

From 'rain' + 'fall', referring to rain that falls from the sky.
