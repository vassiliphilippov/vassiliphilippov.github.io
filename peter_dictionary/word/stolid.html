stolid

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɑːlɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɒlɪd / (BrE)
(of a person) calm and showing little emotion or animation; not easily excited or upset.

Examples

For three days, their stolid oxen had plodded up a blazing valley as flat and featureless as a dead sea.

Synset Examples

her impassive remoteness
he remained impassive, showing neither interest in nor concern for our plight
a silent stolid creature who took it all as a matter of course
her face showed nothing but stolid indifference

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impassive - showing no emotion or feeling
  • Synonym: stoic - enduring pain or hardship without showing emotion
  • Antonym: emotional - showing strong emotions

Etymology

From Latin *stolidus* (dull, stupid).
