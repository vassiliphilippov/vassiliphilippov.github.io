twelve

1. NUM

/ twɛlv / (AmE), / twɛlv / (BrE)
the number 12; a cardinal number representing the quantity of twelve items or units.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dozen - a group or set of twelve
  • Degree Lower: eleven - the number 11
  • Degree Higher: thirteen - the number 13

Etymology

From Old English *twelf*, from Proto-Germanic *twalif* (two left, i.e., two after ten).
