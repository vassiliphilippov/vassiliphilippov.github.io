round

1. NOUN

/ raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
A complete session of playing 18 holes of golf.

Examples

The world's best golfer, shooting below par, came to the last hole of the opening round of the Los Angeles open with every intention of delivering a final crusher.

2. ADJ

/ raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
(of a number or figure) expressed to the nearest whole number, ten, hundred, etc., rather than in exact terms.

Examples
