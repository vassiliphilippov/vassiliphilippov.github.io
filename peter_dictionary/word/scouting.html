scouting

1. NOUN

/ ˈskaʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskaʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of exploring or observing, especially in order to gain information about an area or an enemy.

Examples

Went out a Skouting yesterday.

Synset Examples

scouting in enemy territory is very dangerous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconnaissance - the act of exploring to gain military information

Etymology

From the verb 'scout', ultimately from Old French 'escouter' (to listen), from Latin 'auscultare'.
