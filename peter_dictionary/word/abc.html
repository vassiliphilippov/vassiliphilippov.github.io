ABC

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪˌbiːˈsiːz / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌbiːˈsiːz / (BrE)
the basic or elementary principles of a subject; the essentials (usually used in plural form: ABCs)

Examples

He started walking him through his ABCs.

Synset Examples

he mastered only the rudiments of geometry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rudiment - a basic principle or element
  • Synonym: fundamentals - essential or primary rules, elements, or principles

Etymology

From the first three letters of the Latin (and English) alphabet, used metaphorically to mean the basics.
