multiplication

1. NOUN

/ ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of increasing in number, especially by reproduction or the production of offspring.

Examples

Depending on the organism, there may be multiplication in some food or beverage products, i.e., in milk for example.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reproduction - the biological process by which new individual organisms are produced

Etymology

From Latin multiplicatio (a multiplying), from multiplicare (to multiply).
