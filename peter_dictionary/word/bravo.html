bravo

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɑːvoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːvəʊ / (BrE)
An exclamation or shout of approval or praise, especially at the end of a performance.

Examples

Now that's good work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encore - an additional performance in response to audience's applause
  • Antonym: boo - an exclamation indicating disapproval from an audience

Etymology

From Italian 'bravo' (brave, excellent), used to express approval.
