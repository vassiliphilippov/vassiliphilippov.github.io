verbalization

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɜrbələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɜːbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act or process of expressing thoughts, feelings, or ideas in words.

Examples

[BABY CRYING IN A DISTANCE] [BABY CRYING] [SPEAKING ITALIAN]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expression - the process of putting thoughts or feelings into words
  • Synonym: articulation - the act of expressing something clearly in speech

Etymology

From 'verbalize' + '-ation', ultimately from Latin 'verbum' (word).
