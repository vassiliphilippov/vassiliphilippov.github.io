goddess

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑːdəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdɪs / (BrE)
A female deity; a woman who is worshipped as a god.

Examples

They hail from Travancore, a state in the subcontinent where Kali, the goddess of death, is worshiped.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: god - a male deity

Etymology

From Old English *goddess*, from god + -ess (feminine suffix).
