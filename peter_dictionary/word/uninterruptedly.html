uninterruptedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtɚˌrʌptɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtʌrɪptɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner without interruptions; continuously and without pause.

Examples

Below the fort, high bluffs extended uninterruptedly for six miles along the Mississippi River.

Synset Examples

this pleasant state of affairs had continued peacefully and uninterruptedly for many years

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continuously - without pause or interruption
  • Synonym: constantly - happening all the time; continuously
  • Antonym: intermittently - with interruptions; not continuously
  • Antonym: sporadically - at irregular intervals; occasionally

Etymology

Formed from 'uninterrupted' + '-ly'. 'Uninterrupted' from Latin prefix 'un-' (not) + 'interruptus' (broken).
