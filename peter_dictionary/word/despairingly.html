despairingly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈspɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈspɛərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses despair; with utter loss of hope.

Examples

In the kitchen Glendora was despairingly picking chickens.

Synset Examples

`Why can't you understand?,' she asked despairingly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hopefully - in a manner expressing hope

Etymology

From 'despair' + '-ingly'. 'Despair' from Old French 'desperer', from Latin 'desperare' (to lose hope).
