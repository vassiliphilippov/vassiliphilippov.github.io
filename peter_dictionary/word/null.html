null

1. ADJ

/ nʌl / (AmE), / nʌl / (BrE)
having no legal or binding force; invalid or of no effect

Examples

The transducer is a null type instrument and employs a stretched diaphragm, 0.001 in. thick and 1 in. in diameter.

Synset Examples

null and void

Related Terms

  • Synonym: void - having no legal force or effect; invalid

Etymology

From Latin nullus (not any, none), from ne- (not) + ullus (any).
