chomp

1. NOUN

/ tʃɑmp / (AmE), / tʃɒmp / (BrE)
A noisy or vigorous bite or chewing action made with the teeth.

Examples

She stood there, watching Holden come in, and she put the piece of toast in her mouth and bit off one corner with a huge chomp of her white teeth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bite - an act of using the teeth to cut into or grip something
