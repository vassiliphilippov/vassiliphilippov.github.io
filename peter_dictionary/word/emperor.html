emperor

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmpɚɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛmpərə / (BrE)
The male ruler of an empire.

Examples

The little guys, they got knocked off and all their estates went to the Emperors.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: empress - female ruler of an empire, or the wife of an emperor
  • Synonym: king - male monarch of a kingdom

Etymology

From Latin 'imperator', meaning commander or ruler.
