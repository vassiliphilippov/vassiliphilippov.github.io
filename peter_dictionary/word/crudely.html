crudely

1. ADV

/ ˈkruːdli / (AmE), / ˈkruːdli / (BrE)
In a crude or unrefined manner; lacking in subtlety, refinement, or tact.

Examples

No, she would not pretend modesty, but neither must she be crudely bold.

Synset Examples

he was crudely bold

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roughly - in a rough or unpolished way
  • Antonym: refinedly - in a refined or polished manner

Etymology

From 'crude' + '-ly', with 'crude' from Latin 'crudus' (raw, rough).
