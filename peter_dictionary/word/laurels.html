laurels

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔrəlz / (AmE), / ˈlɔːrəlz / (BrE)
honors or awards given in recognition of achievement, distinction, or success

Examples

THE MODEST AND HAPPY Spahn waved off his new laurels as one of those good days.

Synset Examples

an award for bravery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: award - a prize or mark of recognition for achievement
  • Synonym: honor - public recognition of merit or achievement
  • Antonym: disgrace - loss of reputation or respect due to a dishonorable action

Etymology

From the practice of crowning victors with laurel wreaths in ancient Greece and Rome.
