militarily

1. ADV

/ mɪˈlɪtərəli / (AmE), / mɪˈlɪtərɪli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to the military or armed forces.

Examples

Moreover, it should not be so fastidious in its growth requirements as to make production on a militarily significant scale improbable.

Synset Examples

on a militarily significant scale

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - relating to the armed forces

Etymology

From 'military' + '-ly', from Latin 'militarius' (of soldiers).
