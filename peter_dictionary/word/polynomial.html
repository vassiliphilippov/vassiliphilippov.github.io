polynomial

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑː.ləˈnoʊ.mi.əl / (AmE), / ˌpɒl.ɪˈnəʊ.mi.əl / (BrE)
a mathematical expression consisting of the sum of several terms, each of which includes a variable raised to a non-negative integer power and multiplied by a coefficient
