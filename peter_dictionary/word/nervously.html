nervously

1. ADV

/ ˈnɜːrvəsli / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows or suggests nervousness or anxiety.

Examples

Then, quickly, and a little nervously, the doctor ordered a couple of ballplayers to carry the catcher into the dressing room.
[GIBBERING NERVOUSLY IN ITALIAN] I wish I could stay longer!

Synset Examples

we watched the stock market nervously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiously - In a manner showing anxiety or worry; similar emotional state
  • Antonym: calmly - In a quiet and relaxed manner, without nervousness

Etymology

Formed from 'nervous' + -ly (adverbial suffix).
