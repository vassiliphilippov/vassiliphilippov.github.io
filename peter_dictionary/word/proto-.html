proto-

1. X

/ ˈproʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
used as a prefix to indicate the first, earliest, or original form of something, especially an ancestral or primitive version.

Examples

There are more stems per item in Athabascan, which expresses the fact that the Athabascan languages have undergone somewhat more change in diverging from proto Athabascan than the Yokuts languages from proto Yokuts.

Synset Examples

`proto' is a combining form in a word like `protolanguage' that refers to the hypothetical ancestor of another language or group of languages

Etymology

From Greek prōto-, from prōtos (first).
