Churchillian

1. ADJ

/ tʃɜːrˈtʃɪliən / (AmE), / tʃɜːˈtʃɪliən / (BrE)
Characteristic of, relating to, or suggestive of Winston Churchill, especially his distinctive manner, speech, or leadership style.

Examples

And so, after a flight southeast to Savannakhet, we found ourselves bouncing along in a Jeep right behind the Land-Rover of Prince Boun Oum of Champassak, a tall man of Churchillian mien in a bush jacket and a ten-gallon hat from Texas.

Etymology

From the name Winston Churchill (1874–1965), British statesman and Prime Minister, with suffix -ian forming adjectives.
