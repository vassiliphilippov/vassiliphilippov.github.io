parted

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪd / (BrE)
divided into two or more distinct sections or parts, especially by a visible line or incision; often used to describe hair, lips, or botanical features.

Examples

Her lips, moist and parted, spoke his name.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divided - separated into parts or pieces

Etymology

Past participle of 'part,' from Latin 'partīre' meaning 'to divide.'
