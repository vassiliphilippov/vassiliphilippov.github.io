aimlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈeɪm.lɪs.li / (AmE), / ˈeɪm.lɪs.li / (BrE)
Without purpose or direction; in a way that lacks a goal or plan.

Examples

He had seen a dry, old, yellowing hand reach out, with that painful solicitude, to touch, to rearrange, to shift aimlessly, some object worth a pfennig.

Synset Examples

he wandered around aimlessly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: purposefully - in a way that shows purpose or intention
  • Synonym: randomly - in a random manner; without method or conscious decision

Etymology

Formed from 'aimless' + '-ly', where 'aimless' derives from 'aim' (intention, goal) + '-less' (without).
