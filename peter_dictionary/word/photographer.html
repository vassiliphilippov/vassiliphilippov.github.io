photographer

1. NOUN

/ fəˈtɑɡrəfər / (AmE), / fəˈtɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
A person who takes photographs, especially as a profession.

Examples

[PIANO MUSIC PLAYING] [CHOIR SINGING] PHOTOGRAPHER: The plaque.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snapper - (informal) a photographer, especially a press photographer
  • Synonym: photojournalist - a person who takes photographs to report news stories

Etymology

From 'photograph' + '-er', with 'photograph' from Greek 'phōtós' (light) + 'gráphein' (to write).
