pamphlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæmflət / (AmE), / ˈpæmflɪt / (BrE)
A small, thin booklet with a paper cover, often containing information or arguments about a particular subject.

Examples

"How many pamphlets do we have in stock "?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: booklet - a small book consisting of a few sheets, typically with a paper cover
  • Synonym: leaflet - a printed sheet of paper, usually folded, containing information or advertising

Etymology

From Middle French pamphilet, perhaps from Pamphilus, the title of a popular Latin love poem in the Middle Ages.
