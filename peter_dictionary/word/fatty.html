fatty

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæti / (AmE), / ˈfæti / (BrE)
containing or composed of fat

Examples

The marrow of the vertebral bodies was pale and showed areas of fatty replacement.

Synset Examples

fatty food
fat tissue

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lean - having little or no fat

Etymology

From 'fat' + '-y', forming adjectives meaning 'full of' or 'characterized by'.

2. NOUN

/ ˈfæti / (AmE), / ˈfæti / (BrE)
(offensive) A person who is overweight, used as an insult.

Examples

Hey, Fat Ass. Fat Ass. Talk to me, boy.
Hey, Fat Ass. Fat Ass. Talk to me, boy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatso - (offensive) An overweight person, used as an insult
  • Degree Lower: chubby - (mild, informal) Slightly overweight person
