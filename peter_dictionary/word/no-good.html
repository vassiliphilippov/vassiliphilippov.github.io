no-good

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊ ˌɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ ˌɡʊd / (BrE)
Worthless; having no merit or value.

Examples

For a no-good tramp!

Synset Examples

a sorry horse
a sorry excuse
a lazy no-count, good-for-nothing goldbrick
the car was a no-good piece of junk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or use
  • Antonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose; opposite of worthless
