cellulose

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛl.jəˌloʊs / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.jʊˌləʊs / (BrE)
a complex carbohydrate forming the main constituent of the cell wall in plants and of vegetable fibers, used in making paper, textiles, and as a source of chemical products
