sore

1. ADJ

/ sɔɹ / (AmE), / sɔː / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) painful or aching; feeling physical pain or tenderness.

Examples

"Bill White (sore ankles) should be ready.
And he would sleep, sleep, and never think of roads and horses' sore haunches, of colonial wars.

Synset Examples

the tender spot on his jaw

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tender - (of a part of the body) sensitive to pain; easily hurt
  • Synonym: painful - causing pain or discomfort
  • Antonym: comfortable - free from pain or discomfort

Etymology

Old English sār (painful, grievous, sore); related to German 'sehr' (very) and Dutch 'zeer' (sore, painful).
