activism

1. NOUN

/ ˈæktɪˌvɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈæktɪvɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the policy or action of using vigorous campaigning to bring about political or social change

Examples

"I do not care if your beliefs take you along a path of religion or a path of labor or a path of activism.
The keynotes of this style are activism and emphasis on achievements in gaining self-esteem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for political or social change
  • Antonym: passivism - the policy or practice of passive acceptance of events

Etymology

From German Aktivismus, from aktiv (active) + -ism.
