dark-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑrk hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈdɑːk heəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is dark in color.

Examples

Squeezing a look between Johnson's fat jowls and the car frame a handsome and still darkhaired lady inquired "Y' all drahve "?

Synset Examples

a dark-haired beauty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brunette - a person, especially a woman, with dark brown or black hair
  • Antonym: blond - having light-colored (typically yellow or pale gold) hair
  • Antonym: fair-haired - having light-colored hair
