wonderfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʌndɚfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəfəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being wonderful; admirable excellence or delightfulness.

Examples

But strength and poignancy develop thenceforth, and the windmill and deathbed episodes gather the threads of realization of the wonderfulness of the old boy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellence - the quality of being outstanding or extremely good
  • Synonym: wonder - a feeling of amazement and admiration

Etymology

From 'wonderful' + '-ness', forming a noun meaning the state or quality of being wonderful.
