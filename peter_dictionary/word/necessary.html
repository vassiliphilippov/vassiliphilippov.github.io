necessary

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛsəˌsɛri / (AmE), / ˈnesəsəri / (BrE)
Absolutely needed; essential for a particular purpose or required for something to be possible or to happen.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indispensable - absolutely necessary; cannot be done without
  • Synonym: essential - absolutely necessary; extremely important
  • Antonym: optional - left to choice; not required

Etymology

From Latin necessarius (unavoidable, indispensable), from necesse (necessary).

2. ADJ

/ ˈnɛsəˌsɛri / (AmE), / ˈnɛsəsəri / (BrE)

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inevitable - Certain to happen; unavoidable

Etymology

From Latin necessarius (unavoidable, inevitable), from necesse (necessary).
