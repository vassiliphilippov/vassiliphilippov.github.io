thereunder

1. ADV

/ ðɛrˈʌndər / (AmE), / ðeəˈʌndə / (BrE)
Under or in accordance with that (used especially in legal or formal contexts to refer to a preceding statement, rule, or document).

Examples

This subsection shall not be so construed as to deprive the owner of any background patent relating thereto of such rights as he may have thereunder.

Synset Examples

the headings and the items listed thereunder

Etymology

From 'there' + 'under', following formation pattern of legal English adverbs.
