classicist

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæs.ɪ.sɪst / (AmE), / ˈklæs.ɪ.sɪst / (BrE)
a person who practices or supports classicism, especially in the arts

Examples

To the Traditionalists, it was a brilliant satire on modernism; to the Neo Classicists, it was a challenge to the pre-war world.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: romanticist - someone who advocates or practices Romanticism

Etymology

From 'classicism' + '-ist', denoting an adherent or specialist.
