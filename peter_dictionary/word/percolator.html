percolator

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.kəˌleɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.kə.leɪ.tə / (BrE)
a coffeepot in which boiling water rises through a central tube and filters down through a basket of ground coffee, brewing it

Examples

I turned around with the percolator in my hand.

Etymology

From Latin 'percolare' meaning 'to filter through', via percolate + -or.
