incorrect

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.kəˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kəˈrɛkt / (BrE)
Not in accordance with fact, truth, or accuracy; wrong or mistaken.

Examples

As a matter of fact, the incorrect classification, the result of many weeks of labor by Dr. Hilprecht, was about to be published by him the following day.

Synset Examples

an incorrect calculation
the report in the paper is wrong
your information is wrong
the clock showed the wrong time
found themselves on the wrong road
based on the wrong assumptions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: correct - free from error; in accordance with fact or truth
  • Synonym: wrong - not correct; mistaken; erroneous

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + correctus (corrected).
