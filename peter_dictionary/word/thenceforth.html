thenceforth

1. ADV

/ ˈðɛnsˌfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈðɛnsˌfɔːθ / (BrE)
From that time onward; starting at that point in time.

Examples

But strength and poignancy develop thenceforth, and the windmill and deathbed episodes gather the threads of realization of the wonderfulness of the old boy.

Synset Examples

thereafter he never called again

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thereafter - from that time on

Etymology

From Middle English 'thensforth', from 'thence' (from that place or time) + 'forth'.
