snuffbox

1. NOUN

/ ˈsnʌfˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈsnʌfˌbɒks / (BrE)
A small ornamental box used for carrying snuff, typically designed to fit in a pocket.

Examples

From the curio cabinet on its south wall and the bureaus beneath, you abstracted seventeen ivory, metal, wood, and stone sculptures of Oriental and African origin, two snuffboxes, and a jade handled magnifying glass.

Etymology

From 'snuff' (a powdered tobacco product) + 'box'; attested from the 17th century.
