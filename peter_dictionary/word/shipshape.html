shipshape

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɪpˌʃeɪp / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌʃeɪp / (BrE)
tidy and in good order; neat and well-organized

Examples

It all did look very efficient and shipshape.

Synset Examples

even the barn was shipshape
a trim little sailboat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tidy - neat and arranged in order
  • Synonym: orderly - arranged or organized in a systematic way
  • Antonym: disorderly - not neat or organized

Etymology

From the phrase 'ship shape', originally referring to the order and neatness required on a ship.
