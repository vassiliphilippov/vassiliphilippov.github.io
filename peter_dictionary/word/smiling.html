smiling

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
Having or showing a smile, especially expressing happiness or optimism.

Examples

She greeted her husband's colleagues with smiling politeness, offering nothing.
In the middle of the party Chandler looked up to see four smiling faces bearing down upon him, each beaming above the biggest, greenest shamrock he had ever seen.

Synset Examples

Come to my arms, my beamish boy!
a room of smiling faces
a round red twinkly Santa Claus

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beaming - smiling radiantly with pleasure or joy
  • Antonym: frowning - showing a facial expression of disapproval or displeasure

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'smile', from Middle English *smilen*.
