locale

1. NOUN

/ loʊˈkæl / (AmE), / ləʊˈkɑːl / (BrE)
a place or setting where an event or activity takes place, especially considered in relation to its particular features or characteristics

Examples

His pictures generalize, though they are inspired by a particular locale; they universalize in terms of weather, skies, earth, and people.
Central city areas have become progressively poorer locales for the major denominations since the exodus of middle-class people from most central cities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: location - a particular place or position
  • Synonym: venue - the place where an event or meeting happens

Etymology

From French 'local', from Latin 'localis' (pertaining to a place).
