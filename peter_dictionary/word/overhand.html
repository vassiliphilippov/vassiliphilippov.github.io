overhand

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvɚˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəhænd / (BrE)
(of a movement or action) made with the hand or arm passing above and then down past the shoulder, as in certain throwing or hitting techniques.

Examples

The new Riverside pitcher turned out to have an overhand fast ball that took a hop.

Synset Examples

an overhand pitch
an overhand stroke

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underhand - performed with a motion under or below the shoulder, often upwards
