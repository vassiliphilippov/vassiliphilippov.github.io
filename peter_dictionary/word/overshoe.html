overshoe

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌʃuː / (BrE)
A waterproof covering worn over a shoe to protect it from rain, snow, or cold.

Examples

"You know what she likes, Winston ", he had said wearily, one evening in November when Winston was pulling off his overshoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: galosh - a waterproof overshoe, typically made of rubber

Etymology

From over- (above, covering) + shoe.
