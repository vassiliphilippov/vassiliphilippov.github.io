blissfully

1. ADV

/ ˈblɪsfəli / (AmE), / ˈblɪsfəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or causes perfect happiness; with great joy or contentment.

Examples

It might be that certain people were born with a compulsion to complicate their lives, while others could live blissfully motionless almost indefinitely, like lizards in the sun, too indolent to blink their eyes.

Synset Examples

he was blissfully unaware of the danger

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happily - in a happy way; with happiness

Etymology

From 'blissful' + '-ly'; 'blissful' from 'bliss' (Old English blīths) meaning 'joy, happiness'.
