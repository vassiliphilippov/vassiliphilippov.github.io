undrinkable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdrɪŋkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɪŋkəbəl / (BrE)
(of a liquid) not suitable for drinking; unsafe or unpleasant to drink.

Examples

But then the so-called coffee was bad enough at best, cold it was all but undrinkable - especially that cup!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: drinkable - suitable for drinking
  • Synonym: potable - safe to drink, especially of water

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) and 'drinkable' (fit to drink).
