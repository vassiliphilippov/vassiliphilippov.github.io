Indonesia

1. PROPN

/ ˌɪn.dəˈni.ʒə / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dəˈniː.zi.ə / (BrE)
a country in Southeast Asia consisting of a large archipelago of over 13,000 islands; achieved independence from the Netherlands in 1945

Examples

Indonesia, in the early days of the Republic, leaned toward the West.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Indonesiæ,' from Greek 'Indos' (India) + 'nesos' (island), meaning 'Indian islands'.
