ejaculation

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌdʒækjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌdʒækjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
an abrupt, emphatic exclamation expressing emotion, especially in speech or writing

Examples

My God, I understand!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exclamation - a sudden cry or remark expressing surprise, emotion, or pain

Etymology

From Latin *eiaculatio* (a throwing forth, an uttering) from *eiaculari* (to throw out).
