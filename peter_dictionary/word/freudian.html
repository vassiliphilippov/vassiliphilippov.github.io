Freudian

1. ADJ

/ frɔɪˈdiːən / (AmE), / frɔɪˈdiːən / (BrE)
Relating to the theories or ideas of Sigmund Freud, especially psychoanalysis and concepts concerning the unconscious mind.

Examples

As you have doubtless guessed already, the plot is plastered with Freudian, Jungian, and Meinckian theory.

Synset Examples

Freudian theories

Etymology

From the name Sigmund Freud (1856–1939), Austrian psychoanalyst, with the suffix -ian denoting relation.
