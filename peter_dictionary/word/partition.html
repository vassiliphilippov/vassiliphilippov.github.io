partition

1. NOUN

/ pɑrˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a structure, usually a wall or panel, that divides or separates spaces within a room or building

Examples

On the glass partition between me and the driver were three signs:
He moved ahead carefully, his left hand in front of him, and came to a wooden partition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divider - a thing placed between two areas to separate them
  • Synonym: wall - a continuous vertical brick or stone structure dividing or enclosing an area
  • Synonym: screen - a movable or fixed device that divides a room or space

Etymology

From Latin partitio (a division), from partire (to divide).
