artistically

1. ADV

/ ɑrˈtɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows artistic skill or sensibility; in an artistic manner.

Examples

Moreover, the most artistically successful of the nonfiction films have invariably borrowed the narrative form from the fiction feature.

Synset Examples

it was artistically decorated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creatively - in a creative manner
  • Antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned manner

Etymology

From 'artistic' + '-ally' (adverbial suffix).
