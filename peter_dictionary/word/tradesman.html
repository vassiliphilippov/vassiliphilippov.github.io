tradesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪdzmən / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdzmən / (BrE)
A person who owns or manages a shop; a shopkeeper or retailer.

Examples

"Very few wives ", says Dr. Calderone, "who balance the checkbook, fix the car, choose where the family will live and deal with the tradesmen, are suddenly going to become submissive where sex is concerned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shopkeeper - a person who owns or manages a shop

Etymology

From trade + -sman, meaning a man engaged in trade.
