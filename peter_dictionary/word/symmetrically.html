symmetrically

1. ADV

/ sɪˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
In a symmetrical manner; in such a way that both sides or parts are identical or balanced with respect to each other.

Examples

Therefore, the only unknown structural feature would appear to be whether the hydrogen atoms are located symmetrically (1) or asymmetrically (3).

Synset Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From 'symmetrical' + '-ly'; 'symmetrical' from Greek 'symmetria' (commensurateness).
