1. VERB

/ əˈkɑmplɪʃ / (AmE), / əˈkɒmplɪʃ / (BrE)
to achieve or complete successfully; to carry out or put into effect

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achieve - to successfully reach a desired objective or result
  • Synonym: fulfill - to bring to completion or reality
  • Antonym: fail - to be unsuccessful in achieving a goal

Etymology

From Old French *acomplir* (to fulfill), from Latin *ad-* (to) + *complēre* (to fill up, complete).
