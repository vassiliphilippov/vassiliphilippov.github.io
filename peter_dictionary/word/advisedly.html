advisedly

1. ADV

/ ædˈvaɪzɪdli / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzɪdli / (BrE)
Deliberately and with careful consideration; intentionally.

Examples

I use the phrase advisedly because there was something positively indecent about our relationship.

Synset Examples

he used that word intentionally
I did this by choice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliberately - in a way that is intentional or planned
  • Antonym: accidentally - in a way that is not planned or intended

Etymology

From Middle English, from Old French 'aviser' (to consider), based on Latin 'advisare' (to consider).
