pomaded

1. ADJ

/ poʊˈmeɪdɪd / (AmE), / pɒˈmeɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of hair) groomed or styled with pomade.

Examples

The lieutenant's sparse brown hair was heavily pomaded, and as Killpath raked the comb through it, it stuck together in thatches so that it looked like umbrella ribs clinging to his pink skull.

Synset Examples

pomaded hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pomade - a scented ointment used for grooming hair

Etymology

From 'pomade', which comes from French 'pommade', from Italian 'pomata', from 'pomo' (apple).
