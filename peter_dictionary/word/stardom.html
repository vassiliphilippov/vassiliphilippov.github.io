stardom

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrdəm / (AmE), / ˈstɑːdəm / (BrE)
the status or position of being a famous or celebrated performer, especially in entertainment or sports

Examples

Soaring to stardom along with him, Margo Felicity Brighetti, a luscious and curvaceously beguiling Italian starlet, turns in a creditable performance as an airplane mechanic.

Synset Examples

stardom meant nothing to her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrity - the state of being famous, well-known person
  • Antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or not famous

Etymology

From 'star' (famous performer) + '-dom' (state or condition).
