newspaperman

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpɚˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpəmæn / (BrE)
A male journalist who works for a newspaper or broadcast media.

Examples

The American newspaperman worried about getting to the cable office.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
  • Synonym: reporter - a person who reports, especially one employed to report news for a newspaper
  • Antonym: newspaperwoman - a female journalist who works for a newspaper

Etymology

Formed from 'newspaper' + 'man'.
