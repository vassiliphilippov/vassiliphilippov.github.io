1. VERB

/ dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: differentiate - to recognize or express the difference between things
  • Antonym: confuse - to fail to distinguish between; to mix up

Etymology
