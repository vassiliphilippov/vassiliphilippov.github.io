overwhelmingly

1. ADV

/ ˌoʊvɚˈwɛlmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɛlmɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely strong, intense, or difficult to resist.

Examples

She should offer substitutes for the temptations which seem overwhelmingly desirable to the child.
In 1952, it will be remembered, the G. O. P. without positive program campaigned on the popular disillusionment with liberal leadership and won overwhelmingly.

Synset Examples

the candy looked overwhelmingly desirable to the dieting man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overpoweringly - in a way that is very intense or difficult to resist

Etymology

From 'overwhelm' + '-ly', with 'overwhelm' from Middle English 'overwhelmen', from 'over-' + 'whelmen' (to turn upside down).
