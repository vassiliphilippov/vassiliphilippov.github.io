limitless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪmɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtlɪs / (BrE)
Without any limit in extent, size, or quantity; infinite or boundless.

Examples

She studied him hopefully, yearningly; against the limitless background of sky and wasteland it was easy to confirm her analysis.

Synset Examples

limitless vastness of our solar system

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boundless - without boundaries or limits
  • Synonym: infinite - without end or limit; immeasurably great or extensive
  • Antonym: limited - having boundaries or restrictions

Etymology

Formed from 'limit' + '-less' (without).
