garlic

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrlɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk / (BrE)
A strong-smelling plant of the onion family, used as a flavoring in cooking; its bulb consists of several cloves.

Examples

You start with a little oil, then fry some garlic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: onion - edible bulbous plant related to garlic
  • Synonym: leek - another member of the onion family

Etymology

From Old English *gārlēac*, from *gār* (spear) + *lēc* (leek), referring to the shape of its leaves.
