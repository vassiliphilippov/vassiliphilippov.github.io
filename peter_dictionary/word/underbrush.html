underbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndɚˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
low bushes, small trees, and other plants growing beneath the taller trees in a forest or wood

Examples

In the Delaware River, three long islands were overgrown with greening trees and underbrush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undergrowth - dense growth of plants under larger trees

Etymology

From 'under' (beneath) + 'brush' (dense vegetation).
