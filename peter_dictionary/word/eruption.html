eruption

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
A sudden and violent release of steam, ash, lava, or other material from a volcano.

Examples

The figure in the corner belched loudly, a deep, liquid eruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explosion - a violent and sudden burst, often with noise
  • Synonym: outburst - a sudden release, especially of force or emotion

Etymology

From Latin eruptio (a breaking out), from erumpere (to burst forth).
