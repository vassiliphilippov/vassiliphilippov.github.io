addlebrained

1. ADJ

/ ˈæd.əlˌbreɪnd / (AmE), / ˈæd.əlˌbreɪnd / (BrE)
Stupid and confused; lacking clear thought or intelligence.

Examples

His addle-brained knight-errant, self appointed to the ridiculous position in an age when armor had already been relegated to museums and the chivalrous code of knight-errantry had become a joke, is, as Cervantes no doubt intended, a gaunt but gracious symbol of good, moving soberly and sincerely in a world of cynics, hypocrites and rogues.

Synset Examples

blathering like the addlepated nincompoop that you are
a confused puddingheaded, muddleheaded fellow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: addlepated - foolish or mentally confused
  • Synonym: muddleheaded - mentally confused; unable to think clearly
  • Antonym: clearheaded - mentally alert; able to think clearly

Etymology

From 'addle' (empty, confused) + 'brained' (having a brain); 'addle' from Middle English 'adel', meaning liquid gone putrid.
