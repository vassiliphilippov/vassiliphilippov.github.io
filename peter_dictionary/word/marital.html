marital

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːrɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈmarɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to marriage or the relationship between a married couple.

Examples

But there is little doubt that in many marriages the wife is boss of the marital bed.
This woman repeatedly complained she was "too tired "for marital relations.

Synset Examples

marital status
marital fidelity
married bliss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matrimonial - relating to marriage or married people
  • Synonym: conjugal - relating to marriage or the relationship between married people

Etymology

From Latin maritalis (of marriage), from maritus (married man, husband).
