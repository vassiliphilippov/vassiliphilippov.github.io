fine-grained

1. ADJ

/ faɪn ɡreɪnd / (AmE), / faɪn ɡreɪnd / (BrE)
consisting of or characterized by very small particles or grains

Examples

The thing is that these bees love a fine-grained soil that is moist; yet the water in the ground should not be stagnant either.

Synset Examples

powdered cellulose
powdery snow
pulverized sugar is prepared from granulated sugar by grinding

Related Terms

  • Antonym: coarse-grained - consisting of relatively large particles or grains
