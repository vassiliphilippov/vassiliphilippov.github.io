vaquero

1. NOUN

/ væˈkɛroʊ / (AmE), / vakˈɛərəʊ / (BrE)
A cowboy, especially one from Spanish-speaking regions of the southwestern United States or Mexico.

Examples

He was in earnest conversation with her father and the old vaquero, Luis Hernandez.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowboy - A person who tends cattle, especially in the western US
  • Synonym: buckaroo - A cowboy, especially in California, influenced by Spanish

Etymology

From Spanish 'vaquero' (cowherd, cowboy), from 'vaca' (cow).
