kindness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaɪndnəs / (AmE), / ˈkaɪndnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate toward others.

Examples

Your kindness to that widow made me ashamed of myself.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cruelty - deliberate infliction of pain or suffering; antonym of kindness
  • Synonym: compassion - sympathetic concern for the sufferings of others
  • Synonym: generosity - willingness to give or share

Etymology

From Old English 'cyndnes' (nature, kindness), related to 'kind' (friendly, gentle).
