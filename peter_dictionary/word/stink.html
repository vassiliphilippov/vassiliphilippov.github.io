stink

1. VERB

/ stɪŋk / (AmE), / stɪŋk / (BrE)
(informal) To be extremely bad, unpleasant, or of poor quality; to perform very badly.

Examples

"I think you stink, Tom Lord!
' You stunk "'.

Synset Examples

This term paper stinks!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suck - to be very bad or unpleasant (informal)
  • Antonym: excel - to be exceptionally good or proficient
