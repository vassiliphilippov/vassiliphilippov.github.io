querulously

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɛr.ə.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈkwɛr.ʊ.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a complaining or peevish manner; expressing dissatisfaction or irritation.

Examples

One boy said querulously about Orthodox Jews: "It's the twentieth century, and they do n't have to wear beards ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peevishly - in a peevish or irritable manner
  • Synonym: complainingly - in a manner expressing complaint

Etymology

From Latin 'querulus' (complaining), from 'queri' (to complain) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
