2. VERB

/ kɑrv / (AmE), / kɑːv / (BrE)
to engrave, shape, or cut (a material) by chipping away at its surface, often to create a decorative or artistic object

Examples

"I am carving a Pieta from white Carrara marble.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculpt - to create or represent (something) by carving, modeling, or casting
  • Synonym: engrave - to cut or carve (a text or design) on the surface of a hard object

Etymology

Old English *ceorfan* (to cut, carve), akin to Old High German *kerban* (to notch).
