boastfully

1. ADV

/ ˈboʊstfəli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊstfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows excessive pride or self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities.

Examples

An Alabama soldier whose feminine associations were of the more admirable type wrote boastfully of his achievements among the Virginia belles: "They thout I was a saint.

Synset Examples

he talked big all evening

Related Terms

  • Antonym: modestly - in a modest or humble manner
