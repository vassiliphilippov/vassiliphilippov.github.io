naprapath

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæ.prəˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈnæ.prə.pæθ / (BrE)
A practitioner who treats disorders of connective tissue, often using manual therapy techniques, according to the principles of naprapathy.

Examples

He may call himself a naprapath, a physiotherapist, an electrotherapist, a naturopath, a sanipractor, a medical cultist, a masseur, a "doctor "- or what have you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturopath - a practitioner of naturopathy
  • Synonym: physiotherapist - a person qualified to treat disease or injury by physical methods such as massage or exercise

Etymology

From Czech 'naprapatie', coined from Czech 'napravit' (to correct) + Greek 'pathos' (suffering).
