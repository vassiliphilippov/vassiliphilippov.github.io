double-breasted

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʌbəlˈbrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəlˈbrɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of a jacket, coat, or similar garment) having a front that overlaps and is usually fastened with two parallel columns of buttons.

Examples

I wore a new double-breasted brown worsted suit with a faint herringbone design and wide lapels like a devil's ears.

Synset Examples

double-breasted jacket
double-breasted suit

Related Terms

  • Antonym: single-breasted - of clothing, having a narrow overlap and one row of buttons

Etymology

From 'double' (having two parts) and 'breasted' (referring to the chest area of a garment).
