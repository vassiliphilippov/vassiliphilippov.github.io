workboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A horizontal board that provides a firm, supported surface for manual work.

Examples

He 'd started a fire and put coffee on, and now was busy at the work board of his chuck wagon.

Etymology

Formed from 'work' + 'board', indicating a board used for working on.
