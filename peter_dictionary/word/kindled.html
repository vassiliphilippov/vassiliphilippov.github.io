kindled

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪndld / (AmE), / ˈkɪndld / (BrE)
Set on fire or made to burn; aroused or inspired.

Examples

But, kindled by his kiss, his caressing hand, her desire was aflame.

Synset Examples

the ignited paper
a kindled fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignited - set on fire; started burning
  • Antonym: extinguished - put out; caused to stop burning

Etymology

Past participle of 'kindle', from Old Norse 'kynda' (to light a fire).
