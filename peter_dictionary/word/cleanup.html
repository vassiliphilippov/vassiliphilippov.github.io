cleanup

1. NOUN

/ ˈklinˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈkliːnˌʌp / (BrE)
the process of making a place or object clean, especially by removing dirt or unwanted substances

Examples

Clean it **up**.

Synset Examples

he gave his shoes a good cleaning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaning - the action of making something clean

Etymology

Formed from the verb phrase 'clean up', converted into a noun in American English (late 19th century).
