crying

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkraɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of shedding tears, often accompanied by sobs or other vocal expressions of distress or emotion.

2. ADJ

/ ˈkraɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkraɪɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a situation or event) conspicuously bad, serious, or outrageous; demanding attention or action due to its severity or injustice.

Examples

Crying shame.

Synset Examples

a crying shame
an egregious lie
flagrant violation of human rights
a glaring error
gross ineptitude
gross injustice
rank treachery

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egregious - outstandingly bad; shocking
  • Synonym: flagrant - conspicuously offensive or bad
  • Antonym: minor - of lesser importance or seriousness
