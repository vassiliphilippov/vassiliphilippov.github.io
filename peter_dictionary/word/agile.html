agile

1. ADJ

/ ˈædʒaɪl / (AmE), / ˈædʒaɪl / (BrE)
able to move quickly and easily; nimble and light in movement or action.

Examples

This was a broth of a boy, our Felix, and nothing was more obvious than the joy he took in demonstrating how agile he was and how full of juice and spirit.

Synset Examples

sleek and agile as a gymnast
as nimble as a deer
nimble fingers
quick of foot
the old dog was so spry it was halfway up the stairs before we could stop it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nimble - quick and light in movement or action
  • Antonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or handling things

Etymology

From Latin agilis, from agere meaning 'to do, drive'.
