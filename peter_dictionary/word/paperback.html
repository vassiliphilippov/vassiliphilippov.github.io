paperback

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpərˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəbæk / (BrE)
A book with a flexible paper cover, rather than a hard binding.

Examples

I did book jackets and covers for paperback reprints: naked girls huddling in corners of dingy furnished rooms while at the doorway, daring the cops to take him, is the guy in shirt sleeves clutching a revolver.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hardback - book with a stiff, hard cover
  • Synonym: paperback book - book with a paper cover (full form)

Etymology

From 'paper' + 'back', referring to the book's paper cover.
