ambiance

1. NOUN

/ ˈæm.bi.ɑns / (AmE), / ˈæm.bi.ɑːns / (BrE)
The character, mood, or atmosphere of a place or situation.

Examples

On the one hand, there is a sense of not having moved beyond the ambiance of their high school.

Synset Examples

there was an atmosphere of excitement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: atmosphere - the mood or feeling in a place or situation

Etymology

From French 'ambiance', from Latin 'ambire' (to go around).
