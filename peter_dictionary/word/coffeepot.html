coffeepot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔfiˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈkɒfiˌpɒt / (BrE)
a tall container with a handle and spout, used for brewing and serving coffee

Examples

She lay under the covers making jabbing motions with her forefinger telling me where to look for the coffeepot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teapot - a container used for making and serving tea

Etymology

From 'coffee' + 'pot', indicating its use for making coffee.
