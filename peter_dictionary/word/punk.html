punk

1. NOUN

/ pʌŋk / (AmE), / pʌŋk / (BrE)
A young person, especially a man, who is aggressive, violent, or involved in criminal activity.

Examples

"You mean this punk can brush me aside and I 've got to hold still for it "?
Punk!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal
  • Synonym: delinquent - a young person who breaks the law or behaves badly
  • Synonym: gangster - a member of a criminal gang
