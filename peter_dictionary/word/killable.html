killable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɪləbəl / (BrE)
Able to be killed, especially suitable to be killed for food.

Examples

The morning hawk, hungry for any eatable, killable, digestible item, kept his eyes on the ring of anchored ships that lay off the shores in the bay, sheltered by the Jersey inlets.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unkillable - not able to be killed

Etymology

From 'kill' + suffix '-able', meaning able to be killed.
