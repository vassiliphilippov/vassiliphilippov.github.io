cobbler

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑb.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒb.lə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to make or repair shoes.

Examples

"Take your cobbler's shop somewhere else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoemaker - person who makes shoes

Etymology

From Middle English *cobeler*, of uncertain origin; possibly related to *cobble* (to mend roughly).
