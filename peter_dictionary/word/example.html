1. NOUN

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: specimen - an individual item or part considered typical of a group or whole
  • Synonym: instance - an occurrence of something regarded as typical; a particular case
  • Synonym: model - a thing used as an example to follow or imitate

Etymology

From Latin exemplum (sample, specimen, illustration).
