collegiate

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlidʒiət / (AmE), / kəˈliːdʒiət / (BrE)
Relating to a college or its students; typical of or characteristic of a college environment or college students.

Examples

The Brooklyn College student shows some striking departures from prevailing collegiate models.

Synset Examples

collegiate living
collegiate attitudes
collegiate clothes

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'collegiatus', meaning 'belonging to a college'.
