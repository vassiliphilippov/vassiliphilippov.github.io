unqualified

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkwɑːlɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɒlɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not limited or restricted in any way; absolute or complete

Examples

There was never a doubt any more how his structures would be received; it was always the same unqualified success now.

Synset Examples

an unqualified denial

Related Terms

  • Antonym: qualified - limited or restricted; not complete or absolute

Etymology

From un- (not) + qualified (from qualify), meaning 'not made limited or conditional.'
