unreleased

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈlist / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈliːst / (BrE)
not yet made available to the public; not released for distribution or publication.

Examples

Rudyard Kipling's scorn for the "jargon "of psychical research was altered somewhat when he wondered "how, or why, had I been shown an unreleased roll of my life film "?

Synset Examples

someone leaked the unreleased announcement
a film that remained unreleased for years

Related Terms

  • Antonym: released - made available for distribution or publication

Etymology

Formed by the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'released'.
