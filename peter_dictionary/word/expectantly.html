expectantly

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈspɛktəntli / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛktəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows you are excitedly or eagerly waiting for something to happen.

Examples

"You 're boiling milk, ai n't you "? soothing it with his hand, knowing the whiskered jowls and the swollen smoothness of teats that wrinkled expectantly to his touch.
He waited at the car side for a moment, looking down at her expectantly.

Synset Examples

she looked at him expectantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eagerly - in a keen or enthusiastic way

Etymology

From 'expectant' + '-ly'. 'Expectant' from Latin 'expectans', present participle of 'expectare' (to await).
