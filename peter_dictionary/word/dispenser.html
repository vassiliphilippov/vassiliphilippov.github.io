dispenser

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspɛnsər / (AmE), / dɪˈspensə / (BrE)
a container or device designed to release or give out a substance in measured amounts

Examples

Make sure milk for the coffee is placed in dispensers rather than in containers, if you are supplying the coffee.

Etymology

From Middle English dispensen (to distribute), from Old French dispenser, from Latin dispensare (to dispense).
