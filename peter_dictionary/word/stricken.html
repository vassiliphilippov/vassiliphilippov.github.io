stricken

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɪkən / (AmE), / ˈstrɪkən / (BrE)
Seriously affected by unpleasant feelings, trouble, or disease.

Examples

The soldier's voice was muffled again, stricken with chagrin.
Once out in the street, the traffic was a gadfly maze in which he wandered stricken.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afflicted - suffering from pain or distress
  • Antonym: unaffected - not influenced or affected by something

Etymology

Past participle of 'strike', from Old English 'strīcan' (to stroke, strike).
