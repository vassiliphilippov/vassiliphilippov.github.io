meritorious

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛrɪˈtɔriəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛrɪˈtɔːriəs / (BrE)
deserving reward or praise, especially because of good work or actions

Examples

Graham will be recognized for his meritorious service to baseball and will get the William J. Slocum Memorial Award.

Synset Examples

a lifetime of meritorious service
meritorious conduct

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commendable - deserving praise or approval
  • Synonym: praiseworthy - worthy of praise
  • Antonym: blameworthy - deserving blame or criticism

Etymology

From Latin 'meritorius', from 'meritum' (desert, merit).
