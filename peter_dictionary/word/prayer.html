prayer

/ ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈpreə / (BrE)

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supplication - the action of asking or begging for something earnestly or humbly, especially in prayer
  • Degree Lower: blessing - a prayer asking for divine favor and protection

Etymology

From Old French *preiere*, from Medieval Latin *precaria*, from Latin *prex* (prayer, request).

3. NOUN

/ ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈpreə / (BrE)
an earnest or urgent request, often addressed to someone in authority or power

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entreaty - an earnest or humble request
  • Synonym: appeal - an earnest or urgent request for assistance

Etymology

From Old French *preiere*, from Latin *precaria* (obtained by entreaty), from *precari* (to ask, beg).
