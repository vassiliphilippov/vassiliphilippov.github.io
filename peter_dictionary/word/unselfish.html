unselfish

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɛl.fɪʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛl.fɪʃ / (BrE)
Willing to put the needs or wishes of others before your own; not selfish.

Examples

The partners each bring to it unselfish love, and each takes away an equal share of pleasure and joy.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: selfish - concerned chiefly with one's own personal profit or pleasure

Etymology

From un- (not) + selfish (from self + -ish).
