wifely

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪfli / (AmE), / ˈwaɪfli / (BrE)
Relating to, befitting, or typical of a wife.

Examples

On the other hand, wifely supremacy demeans the husband, saps his self-respect, and robs him of his masculinity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wifish - having the qualities or attributes of a wife
  • Antonym: husbandly - relating to, befitting, or typical of a husband

Etymology

From 'wife' + '-ly', formed in analogy to 'manly', 'kingly', etc.
