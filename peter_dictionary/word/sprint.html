sprint

1. VERB

/ sprɪnt / (AmE), / sprɪnt / (BrE)
to run or move at full speed, typically for a short distance

Examples

The music sang nicely, sprinted evenly when necessary, was properly accented and balanced.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dash - to run or move quickly or suddenly

Etymology

Probably from Middle Dutch *sprinte* (jump, leap).
