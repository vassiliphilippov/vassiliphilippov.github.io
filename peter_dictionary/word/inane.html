inane

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈneɪn / (AmE), / ɪˈneɪn / (BrE)
lacking sense, significance, or intelligence; silly or pointless.

Examples

His fear of making some inane or inappropriate remark.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asinine - extremely foolish or stupid
  • Synonym: silly - lacking in common sense or seriousness
  • Antonym: sensible - having or showing good sense or judgment

Etymology

From Latin inanis (empty, void, foolish).
