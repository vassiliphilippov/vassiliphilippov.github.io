misnomer

1. NOUN

/ mɪsˈnoʊmər / (AmE), / mɪsˈnəʊmə / (BrE)
a name or term that is incorrect or not suitably applied to something

Examples

It wit is a misnomer because it covers slips as well as wit.

Etymology

From Old French 'mesnommer' (to misname), from 'mes-' (wrongly) + 'nommer' (to name), from Latin 'nominare'.
