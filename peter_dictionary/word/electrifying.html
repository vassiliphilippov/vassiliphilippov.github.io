electrifying

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɛktrəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a sudden feeling of excitement or intense emotion

Examples

He would send on by trusted messenger the dispatches with their electrifying news.

Synset Examples

she gave an electrifying performance
a thrilling performer to watch

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thrilling - causing excitement and pleasure
  • Antonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement

Etymology

From 'electrify' + '-ing'; 'electrify' from Latin 'electricus' (produced from amber by friction) and Greek 'ēlektron' (amber).
