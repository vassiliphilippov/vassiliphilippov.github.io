featureless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfitʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃələs / (BrE)
Having no distinctive or distinguishing features or characteristics.

Examples

For three days, their stolid oxen had plodded up a blazing valley as flat and featureless as a dead sea.

Synset Examples

the featureless landscape of the steppe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate; lacking special features
  • Synonym: nondescript - lacking distinctive or interesting features or characteristics
  • Antonym: distinctive - having a quality or characteristic that makes someone or something different or easily noticed

Etymology

From 'feature' + '-less', indicating lack or absence of features.
