commiserate

1. VERB

/ kəˈmɪzəˌreɪt / (AmE), / kəˈmɪzəreɪt / (BrE)
to feel or express sympathy or compassion for the misfortune of others

Examples

During these first days of the trial I did n't have as much time to commiserate with Viola as I should have liked.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sympathize - feel or express sympathy with someone
  • Synonym: empathize - understand and share the feelings of another

Etymology

From Latin 'commiserari' (to pity), from 'com-' (with) + 'miserari' (to lament).
