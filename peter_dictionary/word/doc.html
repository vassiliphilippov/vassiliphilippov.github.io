doc

1. NOUN

/ dɑk / (AmE), / dɒk / (BrE)
An informal term for a doctor, especially a medical practitioner.

Examples

The doc had already gone home for the night.

Synset Examples

I felt so bad I went to see my doctor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doctor - a licensed medical practitioner

Etymology

Shortened form of 'doctor'.
