aflame

1. ADJ

/ əˈfleɪm / (AmE), / əˈfleɪm / (BrE)
Feeling or showing intense emotion or excitement, especially desire; glowing with strong feeling.

Examples

But, kindled by his kiss, his caressing hand, her desire was aflame.

Synset Examples

his face all ablaze with excitement
he was aflame with desire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ablaze - filled with bright light or emotion

Etymology

From the prefixed 'a-' (on, in, at) + 'flame' (from Old French 'flamme').
