hideaway

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdəweɪ / (BrE)
a place used for hiding or as a refuge, often secret or remote

Examples

The reporters had not yet discovered that this was his hideaway.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hideout - a place for hiding, especially by criminals

Etymology

Formed from "hide" (to conceal) and "away" (at a distance), first recorded mid-20th century.
