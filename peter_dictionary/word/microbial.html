microbial

1. ADJ

/ maɪˈkroʊbiəl / (AmE), / maɪˈkrəʊbiəl / (BrE)
relating to, involving, or caused by microbes (microscopic living organisms such as bacteria, fungi, or viruses)

Examples

It appeared that most of the mixed liquor suspended solids were active microbial solids with the heavier, less active solids settling out.

Synset Examples

microbial warfare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bacterial - relating to or caused by bacteria
  • Synonym: viral - relating to or caused by a virus

Etymology

From microbe + -ial, with 'microbe' from French 'microbe', itself from Greek 'mikros' (small) + 'bios' (life).
