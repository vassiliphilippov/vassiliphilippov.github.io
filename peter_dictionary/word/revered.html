revered

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈvɪrd / (AmE), / rɪˈvɪəd / (BrE)
Considered with deep respect, admiration, and honor.

Examples

If some future Khrushchev decided to rake up the misdeeds of his revered predecessor, would not the factory workers pass the same resolutions applauding his dispossession?

Synset Examples

revered holy men

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respected - regarded with respect or esteem
  • Antonym: disdained - regarded with contempt or scorn

Etymology

From Latin 'revereri' (to stand in awe of).
