untrue

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtru / (AmE), / ʌnˈtruː / (BrE)
not in accordance with the facts; not true.

Examples

And this seems to me untrue.

Synset Examples

unfortunately the statement was simply untrue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: false - not true; incorrect or untruthful
  • Antonym: true - in accordance with fact or reality

Etymology

From un- (not) + true.
