quickie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɪki / (AmE), / ˈkwɪki / (BrE)
a task, action, or repair done quickly and with little attention to detail; a hurried job

Examples

Pelham said Sunday night there was research being done on whether the "quickie "vote on the increase can be repealed outright or whether notice would have to first be given that reconsideration of the action would be sought.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rush job - a task completed in haste with minimal care

Etymology

From 'quick' (fast) with diminutive '-ie' suffix, first attested mid-20th century.
