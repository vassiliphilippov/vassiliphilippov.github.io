shortened

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɔrtənd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtənd / (BrE)
made shorter; cut or reduced in length or duration.

Examples

At the age of ten, when he was working as a newsboy in the Loop, he was knocked down by a streetcar which resulted in his permanently shortened leg.

Synset Examples

a sawed-off shotgun
a sawed-off broomstick
the shortened rope was easier to use

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lengthened - made longer; increased in length
  • Degree Lower: short - having little length, not long
