naked

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪkəd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
(of a quality or emotion) expressed openly and without disguise or embellishment; bare or pure.

Examples

The simple, naked idea of one man chasing another is of its nature better fitted for the film than it is for any other form of fiction.

Synset Examples

naked ambition
raw fury
you may kill someone someday with your raw power

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bare - unconcealed; without covering or addition
  • Synonym: raw - not disguised or processed; in a natural or uncovered state
  • Antonym: concealed - kept hidden; not visible or known
  • Antonym: embellished - decorated or enhanced, not in a plain form

Etymology

From Old English *nacod*, related to German *nackt* and Dutch *naakt*.
