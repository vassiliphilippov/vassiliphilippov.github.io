botulinal

1. ADJ

/ boʊˈtjuːlɪnəl / (AmE), / bəˈtjuːlɪnəl / (BrE)
Relating to or produced by the botulinus bacterium or its toxin.

Examples

Botulinal toxin, for example, is several thousand-fold more toxic by this route than when given per os.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: botulinic - relating to or produced by botulinus
  • Synonym: botulinum - the bacterium Clostridium botulinum or its toxin

Etymology

From 'botulinus', the former genus name of the bacterium (from Latin 'botulus' meaning 'sausage', as food poisoning was first linked to sausages) + '-al'.
