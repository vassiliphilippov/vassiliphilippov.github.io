squared

1. ADJ

/ skwɛrd / (AmE), / skwɛəd / (BrE)
shaped or formed so as to have square corners or sides; made square in outline or form

Examples

Styles run the gamut from slender and tapered with elongated toes to a newer squared toe shape.
In the tintable group are high and little heels, squared and oval throats, and shantung like textures.
In a lacy open weave shoes have a luster finish, braided collar and bow highlight on the squared throat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square - having the shape of a square; two-dimensional geometric shape with four equal sides and right angles

Etymology

From square + -ed, indicating the state or result of being made square.
