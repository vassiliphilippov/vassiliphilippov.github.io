blackening

1. NOUN

/ ˈblækənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblækənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making or becoming black or darker in color

Examples

Using Edgerton's method, the fast closing action is obtained from the blackening of a window by exploding a series of parallel lead wires.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: darkening - the process of becoming dark or darker
