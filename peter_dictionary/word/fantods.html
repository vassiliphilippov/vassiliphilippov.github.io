fantods

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌtɑdz / (AmE), / ˈfæntɒdz / (BrE)
A state of uneasiness, nervousness, or irritability; the fidgets or a feeling of anxiety.

Examples

Hell's own amount of chaulmoogra oil did nothing to alleviate their torment; they expired amid indescribable fantods, imploring the Blessed One to forgive their desecration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jitters - a feeling of nervousness or anxiety
  • Synonym: fidgets - restless or nervous movements; a feeling of unease

Etymology

Origin uncertain; popularized in 19th-century American English, notably by Mark Twain.
