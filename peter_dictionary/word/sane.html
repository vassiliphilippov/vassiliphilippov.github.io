sane

1. ADJ

/ seɪn / (AmE), / seɪn / (BrE)
having a healthy mind; showing sound judgment and free from mental disorder

Examples

"Is it true that you believe the other animals are saner than the human species "?
Grudges simply do not keep that well in a sane mind.

Synset Examples

appears to be completely sane

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insane - not sane; suffering from mental illness or madness

Etymology

From Latin sanus (healthy, sound).
