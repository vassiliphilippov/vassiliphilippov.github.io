patched

1. ADJ

/ pætʃt / (AmE), / pætʃt / (BrE)
Mended by covering a hole or worn area with a patch, often in a clumsy or makeshift way.

Examples

The sharp wind slapped at him and his feet felt like ice as the snow penetrated the holes of his shoes, his only ones, now patched with folded parchment.

Synset Examples

patched jeans

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repaired - restored to good condition after damage
