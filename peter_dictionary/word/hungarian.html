Hungarian

1. ADJ

/ hʌnˈɡɛriən / (AmE), / hʌŋˈɡɛəriən / (BrE)
Relating to Hungary, its people, language, or culture.

Examples

There was the Hungarian Revolution which we praised and mourned, but did nothing about.

Synset Examples

Hungarian folk music

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hungary - a country in central Europe

Etymology

From Hungary + -an, modeled after the country name.
