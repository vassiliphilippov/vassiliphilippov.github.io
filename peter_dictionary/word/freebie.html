freebie

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriː.bi / (AmE), / ˈfriː.bi / (BrE)
An item or service given free of charge, especially as part of a promotional offer or marketing campaign.

Examples

Me? I'm giving away free money.

Synset Examples

the road map was a freebie

Related Terms

  • Synonym: giveaway - an item given free, often for promotional purposes

Etymology

Colloquial blend of 'free' and the diminutive suffix '-bie', originating in American English, mid-20th century.
