undone

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdʌn / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʌn / (BrE)
Not done, completed, or finished; left incomplete or not carried out.

Examples

They could be done or left undone and nobody really gave a damn.

Synset Examples

the work could be done or undone and nobody cared

Related Terms

  • Antonym: done - completed or finished

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'done' (past participle of 'do').
