somebody

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌmˌbɑːdi / (AmE), / ˈsʌmˌbɒdi / (BrE)
A person whose identity is not known or stated.

Examples

As it turned out... that somebody was me.

Synset Examples

there was too much for one person to do

Related Terms

  • Synonym: someone - a person who is not known or named
  • Antonym: nobody - no person; not anyone

Etymology

From 'some' + 'body', first recorded in the late Middle English period.
