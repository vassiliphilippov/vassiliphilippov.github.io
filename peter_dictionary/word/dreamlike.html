dreamlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdriːmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdriːmlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities that are characteristic of a dream; unreal, surreal, or ethereal.

Examples

His casual, dreamlike working methods, often as not in absentia, were an abrupt change from Harburg's, so that Arlen had to adjust again to another approach to collaboration.

Synset Examples

night invested the lake with a dreamlike quality
as irrational and surreal as a dream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surreal - having the qualities of surrealism; bizarre or dreamlike
