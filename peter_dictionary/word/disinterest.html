disinterest

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈɪntrəst / (AmE), / dɪsˈɪntrɪst / (BrE)
the state of not being involved or concerned; lack of interest or concern

Examples

Scotty watched with disinterest.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interest - a feeling of wanting to know or take part in something

Etymology

From prefix 'dis-' (expressing negation) + 'interest'.
