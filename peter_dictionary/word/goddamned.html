goddamned

1. ADJ

/ ɡɑdˈdæmd / (AmE), / ɡɒdˈdæmd / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) Used for emphasis, showing strong anger or annoyance; damned, accursed.

Examples

I hate Poland and all the goddamned mothers' sons of them.
"I ai n't taking no goddamned chances for no Jew business ".
I think you made a dam good chouise to turn off as nise a feler as Alf dyer and let that orney thefin, drunkard, damed card playing Sun of a bich com to Sea you, the god damed theaf and lop yeard pigen tode helion, he is too orney for hel.

Synset Examples

he's a blasted idiot
it's a blamed shame
a blame cold winter
not a blessed dime
I'll be damned (or blessed or darned or goddamned) if I'll do any such thing
he's a damn (or goddam or goddamned) fool
a deuced idiot
an infernal nuisance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damned - used as an intensifier, expressing strong disapproval or annoyance
  • Synonym: blasted - used as a mild expletive intensifier
  • Degree Lower: blessed - used as a euphemistic, mild intensifier

Etymology

From 'God' + 'damned', expressing a curse or condemnation.
