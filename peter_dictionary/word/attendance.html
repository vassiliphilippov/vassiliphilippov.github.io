attendance

1. NOUN

/ əˈtɛndəns / (AmE), / əˈtɛndəns / (BrE)
the act of being present at a place, event, or meeting

Examples

During Oersted's attendance at the university, it was poorly equipped with physical apparatus for experimenting in the sciences.
Do you protect your holiday privileges with an attendance requirement both before and after the holiday?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: absence - the state of being not present at a place or event

Etymology

From Old French 'attendance', from Latin 'attendere' (to attend).
