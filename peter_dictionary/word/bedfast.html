bedfast

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛdˌfæst / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌfɑːst / (BrE)
Confined to bed, typically because of illness or infirmity.

Examples

The electric gadget is most helpful when there are many crowned teeth and in individuals who are elderly, bedfast with a chronic disease, or are handicapped by disorders such as cerebral palsy or muscular dystrophy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedridden - unable to get out of bed because of illness or infirmity

Etymology

From 'bed' + 'fast', where 'fast' means 'fixed' or 'unable to move'.
