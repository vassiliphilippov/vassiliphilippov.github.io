hive

1. NOUN

/ haɪv / (AmE), / haɪv / (BrE)
a man-made structure used by beekeepers for housing a colony of bees

Examples

A bee's place is in the hive ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beehive - a structure in which bees are kept, especially a man-made one

Etymology

From Old English *hȳf* (beehive).
