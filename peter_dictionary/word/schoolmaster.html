schoolmaster

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
A male teacher or the head of a school, especially in a school for children.

Examples

Let him chafe with impatience to see Charles, rip open the note with trembling hands and read the formal report in Hillman's beautiful, schoolmaster's hand.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headmaster - male principal or head teacher of a school
  • Antonym: schoolmistress - female teacher or head of a school

Etymology

From school + master, reflecting historical use for a male head of a school.
