timecard

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmkɑːd / (BrE)
a card used with a time clock to record an employee's starting and ending working hours each day

Examples

The country club will have his old timecards, records, W-2s, - with his name on them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: timesheet - a sheet used to record hours worked by an employee

Etymology

From 'time' + 'card'; refers to a physical card recording times of arrival and departure.
