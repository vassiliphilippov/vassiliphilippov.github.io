yokel

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊkəl / (AmE), / ˈjəʊkəl / (BrE)
an uneducated or unsophisticated person from the countryside, often regarded as lacking in culture or intelligence

Examples

Why did these yokels still wear boots, anyway, when most had scarcely sat a horse in years?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bumpkin - an unsophisticated, awkward person from the countryside
  • Synonym: rube - an uneducated or unsophisticated country person
  • Antonym: city slicker - a person from the city who is regarded as sophisticated or cunning

Etymology

Perhaps from the English dialect word 'yokel' (simpleton), of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
