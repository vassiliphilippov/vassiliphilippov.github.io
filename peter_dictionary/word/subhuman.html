subhuman

1. ADJ

/ sʌbˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / sʌbˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) regarded as less than human or not worthy of being considered fully human.

Examples

Thus the image of man has suffered complete fragmentation in personal and spiritual qualities, and complete objectification in sub-human and quasi mechanistic powers.

Synset Examples

treated natives as subhuman
a subhuman spectacle
the subhuman primates

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inhuman - lacking human qualities of compassion or mercy

Etymology

From Latin 'sub-' (under) and 'humanus' (human).
