brighten

1. VERB

/ ˈbraɪtən / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtən / (BrE)
to make or become lighter, more luminous, or more cheerful

Examples

Some are so filled with gratitude, for the gift of life and the love of God, that their joy spills out on the paper and brightens the lives of thousands whom they have never known, and will never see.

Synset Examples

The paint will brighten the room

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lighten - make lighter or less dark
  • Antonym: darken - make or become darker

Etymology

From 'bright' + '-en' (verb-forming suffix).
