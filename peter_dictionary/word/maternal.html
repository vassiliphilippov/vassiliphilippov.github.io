maternal

1. ADJ

/ məˈtɜrnəl / (AmE), / məˈtɜːnəl / (BrE)
Relating to, characteristic of, or typical of a mother.

Examples

It was a fine broody hen, white, with a maternal eye and a striking abundance of feathers in the under region of the abdomen.

Synset Examples

warm maternal affection for her guest

Related Terms

  • Antonym: paternal - characteristic of a father

Etymology

From Latin 'maternus' (of a mother), from 'mater' (mother).
