double-glaze

1. VERB

/ ˌdʌbəlˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəlˈɡleɪz / (BrE)
To fit (a window or building) with two layers of glass in order to reduce heat loss and noise.

Examples

Insulate, weatherstrip, double-glaze to the maximum.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insulate - to protect something by interposing material that prevents the loss of heat or the intrusion of sound
