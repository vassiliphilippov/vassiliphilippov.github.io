overlooked

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚˈlʊkt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlʊkt / (BrE)
Not noticed or considered; not taken into account.

Examples

The Outdoor Education Project took cognizance of the fact, so often overlooked, that athletic activities stressed in most school programs have little or no relationship to the physical and mental needs and interests of later life.

Synset Examples

his retirement was not allowed to go unmarked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neglected - not given proper attention
  • Synonym: ignored - refused to take notice of
