counsellor

1. NOUN

/ ˈkaʊn.sə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl.ə / (BrE)
A person trained to give advice and guidance, especially on personal, social, or psychological problems.

Examples

Iike a counsellor, an advisor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advisor - a person who provides advice or guidance
  • Degree Higher: therapist - a person trained to treat people with mental or emotional problems

Etymology

From Old French *counseillour*, from Latin *consiliārius* (advisor).
