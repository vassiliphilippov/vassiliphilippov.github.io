grandpa

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌpɑ / (AmE), / ˈɡranˌpɑː / (BrE)
An informal term for one's grandfather; the father of one's father or mother.

Examples

"I hope you get well, Grandpa, and wish I see you soon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandfather - the father of one's father or mother

Etymology

Shortened and familiar form of 'grandfather', composed of 'grand' + 'pa' (an informal term for 'father').
