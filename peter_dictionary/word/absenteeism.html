absenteeism

1. NOUN

/ ˌæb.sənˈti.ɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˌæb.sənˈtiː.ɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The practice of regularly staying away from work or school without good reason.

Examples

Do you measure its relation to reduced absenteeism, turnover, accidents, and grievances, and to improved quality and output?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attendance - the act of being present at work or an event

Etymology

From 'absentee' + '-ism'. 'Absentee' is from Latin 'absens' (absent, being away).
