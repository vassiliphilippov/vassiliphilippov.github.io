impending

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈpɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an event) about to happen or likely to occur soon; imminent.

Examples

Among my school and neighborhood friends, during the next months, I bragged and swaggered and pompously described my impending date.

Synset Examples

retribution is at hand
some people believe the day of judgment is close at hand
in imminent danger
his impending retirement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imminent - about to happen very soon

Etymology

From Latin 'impendere' meaning 'to overhang, be imminent'.
