motionless

1. ADJ

/ ˈmoʊʃ(ə)n.ləs / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃ(ə)n.ləs / (BrE)
Not moving; completely still.

Examples

They lay on his lap, palms up, stiffly motionless, the tapered fingers a little thick at the joints.
It might be that certain people were born with a compulsion to complicate their lives, while others could live blissfully motionless almost indefinitely, like lizards in the sun, too indolent to blink their eyes.

Synset Examples

the inertia of an object at rest

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immobile - not moving; not able to move
  • Synonym: inactive - not engaging in physical activity or movement
  • Synonym: still - not moving, calm or quiet
  • Antonym: moving - changing position; in motion

Etymology

From 'motion' + '-less', meaning 'without motion'.
