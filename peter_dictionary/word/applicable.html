applicable

1. ADJ

/ ˈæplɪkəbəl, əˈplɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈaplɪkəbl, əˈplɪkəbl / (BrE)
Relevant or appropriate to a particular matter or situation; capable of being applied.

Examples

Thus, theory and doctrine applicable among the great nations and the smaller European states did not really comfortably fit less developed and less powerful societies elsewhere.

Synset Examples

gave applicable examples to support her argument

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irrelevant - not connected with or relevant to something
  • Synonym: relevant - closely connected or appropriate to the matter at hand

Etymology

From Late Latin applicabilis, from Latin applicare (to apply).
