healthful

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛlθ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ.fəl / (BrE)
Having a positive effect on health; promoting good health of body or mind.

Examples

A salad with greens and tomato is a popular and wonderfully healthful addition to a meal, but add an avocado and you have something really special.
A farm provides a wholesome and healthful environment for children.

Synset Examples

a healthful climate
a healthful environment
healthful nutrition
healthful sleep
Dickens's relatively healthful exuberance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healthy - in good health or conducive to good health
  • Antonym: unhealthful - harmful to health; not promoting health
  • Antonym: unhealthy - not in good health or not promoting good health

Etymology

From Middle English, from health + -ful (full of).
