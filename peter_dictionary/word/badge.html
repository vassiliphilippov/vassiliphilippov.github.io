badge

1. NOUN

/ bædʒ / (AmE), / bædʒ / (BrE)
A small emblem, often made of metal, cloth, or plastic, that is worn to show a person's rank, membership, or affiliation.

Examples

They have a sense of marginality at being denied that special badge of status, the out-of-town school.
But dully glinting on the dark form were the buttons and badge of a policeman.
"Do n't press your luck, badge toter ".

Synset Examples

they checked everyone's badge before letting them in

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insignia - an official badge or distinguishing mark of military rank, office, or membership
  • Synonym: emblem - an object or symbol representing a group or idea
  • Synonym: symbol - a mark or object standing for or representing something else

Etymology

From Old French *bage* (badge, sign), of unknown origin.
