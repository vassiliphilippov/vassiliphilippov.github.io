manifestly

1. ADV

/ ˈmænɪˌfɛstli / (AmE), / ˈmanɪˌfɛstli / (BrE)
In a way that is clear and obvious; unmistakably.

Examples

For such an industry, it is only "safe "to raise its price if such an increase is manifestly "justified "by rising costs (due to rising wages, etc.).

Synset Examples

the answer is obviously wrong
she was in bed and evidently in great pain
he was manifestly too important to leave off the guest list
it is all patently nonsense
she has apparently been living here for some time
I thought he owned the property, but apparently not
You are plainly wrong
he is plain stubborn

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily seen or understood
  • Synonym: evidently - plainly or obviously; in a way that is clear to see or understand
  • Synonym: apparently - as far as one knows or can see; seemingly

Etymology

From manifest + -ly, with 'manifest' from Latin manifestus (obvious, evident).
