doped

1. ADJ

/ doʊpt / (AmE), / dəʊpt / (BrE)
(of a material) treated or impregnated with a foreign substance, especially to alter its electrical or physical properties.

Examples

Carbon resistors and impurity doped germanium resistors have been investigated for use as precision secondary thermometers in the liquid helium temperature region.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impregnated - soaked or saturated with a substance

Etymology

From the verb "dope" meaning to treat or add a substance to something, originally from Dutch "doop" (sauce).
