ghostlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡoʊstˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a ghost; having an appearance or quality suggestive of a ghost.

Examples

For the unseen, ghostlike rifleman aimed a little higher the third time.

Synset Examples

a ghostly face at the window
a phantasmal presence in the room
spectral emanations
spiritual tappings at a seance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ghostly - having the characteristics of a ghost
  • Synonym: spectral - like or relating to a ghost or specter
  • Synonym: phantasmal - having the quality of a phantom; ghostlike
