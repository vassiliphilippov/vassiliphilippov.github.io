contributory

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtrɪbjəˌtɔri / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri / (BrE)
Helping to cause or bring about something; playing a part in achieving a result.

Examples

Choose carefully between contributory or non contributory pension plans.
Choose carefully between contributory or non contributory pension plans.
A non contributory plan usually won n't pay off for the worker until he retires.

Synset Examples

working conditions are not conducive to productivity
the seaport was a contributing factor in the growth of the city
a contributory factor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: causal - acting as a cause
  • Synonym: influential - having an influence on something
  • Antonym: irrelevant - not connected or relevant to something

Etymology

From Latin 'contributorius', from 'contribuere' (to contribute), meaning 'to bring together, add'.
