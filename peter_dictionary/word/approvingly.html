approvingly

1. ADV

/ əˈpruvɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈpruːvɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows approval or agreement.

Examples

Nodding approvingly and swelling with importance, the old lady whispered confidentially.

Synset Examples

she nodded approvingly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disapprovingly - in a way that shows disapproval
