Nebraska

1. PROPN

/ nəˈbræskə / (AmE), / nɪˈbrɑːskə / (BrE)
a state in the central United States, located on the Great Plains

Examples

Latin America was once an area as "safe "for the West as Nebraska was for Nixon.

Etymology

Named after the Nebraska River, from an Otoe word meaning 'flat water'.
