water ski

1. VERB

/ ˈwɔtər ski / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ski / (BrE)
to move over water on water skis, especially as a sport or leisure activity

Examples

Henri has always had shapely legs from swimming and water skiing and really does n't have to work them very much.
