broadly

1. ADV

/ ˈbrɔdli / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdli / (BrE)
In a general way, without considering specific details or exceptions.

Examples

He demonstrated by playing an imaginary piano, doing a staccato passage with a broadly exaggerated attack.

Synset Examples

he interprets the law broadly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: narrowly - in a limited or restricted way, considering only a few details
