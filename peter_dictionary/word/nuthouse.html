nuthouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A slang, pejorative term for a mental hospital or psychiatric institution.

Examples

Is this a nut-house?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • Synonym: mental hospital - a hospital for the treatment of mental illness

Etymology

From 'nut' (slang for a crazy person) + 'house'.
