unconvincing

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.kənˈvɪn.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌn.kənˈvɪn.sɪŋ / (BrE)
Not capable of making someone believe that something is true or valid; failing to persuade.

Examples

But this statement is completely unconvincing.
There are few data on the boa constrictor, those for the anaconda are unconvincing, and there is nothing at all on the amethystine python.

Synset Examples

unconvincing argument
as unconvincing as a forced smile

Related Terms

  • Antonym: convincing - able to make someone believe that something is true; persuasive

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'convincing'.
