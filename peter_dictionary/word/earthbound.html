earthbound

1. ADJ

/ ˈɜrθˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
Confined to the earth; not able to leave the earth or not engaged in flight or space travel.

Examples

In the wide range of experiences common to our earth-bound race none is more difficult to manage, more troublesome, and more enduring in its effects than the control of love and hate.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: celestial - relating to the sky or outer space; not confined to earth

Etymology

From 'earth' + 'bound', meaning restricted; in use since the 19th century.
