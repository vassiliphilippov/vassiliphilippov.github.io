irreverence

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈrɛvərəns / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛvərəns / (BrE)
A lack of respect or seriousness for people or things that are generally regarded as worthy of respect.

Examples

There was an air of revolt about the children - even irreverence for their own principles.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disrespect - lack of respect or courtesy
  • Antonym: reverence - deep respect for someone or something

Etymology

From Latin 'irreverentia' (lack of respect), from 'in-' (not) + 'reverentia' (reverence).
