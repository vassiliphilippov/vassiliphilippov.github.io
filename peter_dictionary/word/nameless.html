nameless

1. ADJ

/ ˈneɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈneɪmləs / (BrE)
not having a known or stated name; having an unknown or unnamed source or identity.

Examples

He thought of the men, the nameless thousands, huddling in them.

Synset Examples

a poem by an unknown author
corporations responsible to nameless owners
an unnamed donor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unnamed - not named or identified by name
  • Synonym: anonymous - of unknown authorship or origin

Etymology

From English 'name' + suffix '-less' (without).
