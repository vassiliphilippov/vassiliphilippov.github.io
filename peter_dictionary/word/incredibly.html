incredibly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈkrɛdəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛdəbli / (BrE)
To a degree that is difficult or impossible to believe; extremely or remarkably.

Examples

Linguists have not always been more enlightened than "practical people "and sometimes have insisted on incredibly trivial points while neglecting things of much greater significance.
Killpath pulled one thin leg up, clamping his arms around the shinbone to press his knee into an incredibly scrawny gut.

Synset Examples

behind you the coastal hills plunge to the incredibly blue sea backed by the Turkish mountains

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unbelievably - to an unbelievable or extraordinary degree
  • Synonym: extremely - to a very high degree
  • Degree Lower: slightly - to a small degree

Etymology

Formed from 'incredible' + '-ly'; 'incredible' from Latin incredibilis (not to be believed).
