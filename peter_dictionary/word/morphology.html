morphology

1. NOUN

/ mɔrˈfɑlədʒi / (AmE), / mɔːˈfɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of biology concerned with the form and structure of organisms and their specific structural features

Examples

A reason for such wide variation in the pulmonary morphology is entirely lacking at present.

Etymology

From Greek morphē (form) + -logia (study).
