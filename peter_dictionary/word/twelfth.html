twelfth

1. ADJ

/ twɛlfθ / (AmE), / twɛlfθ / (BrE)
coming after the eleventh in a sequence; 12th in order or position

Examples

It happened on the twelfth hole, a 155 - yarder.
In a far distant part of the United States, I was talking to an instructor about a boy who in the twelfth grade was doing special work.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: eleventh - 11th in order
  • Degree Higher: thirteenth - 13th in order

Etymology

From Old English 'twelfta', from 'twelf' (twelve) + '-ta' (adjective suffix).
