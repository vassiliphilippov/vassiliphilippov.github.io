springboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɪŋˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a flexible board that bends and rebounds, used for jumping in gymnastics, diving, or acrobatics

Examples

The parallel bars, horse, buck, springboard, horizontal bar, rings, and mats formerly in the school gyms were replaced by baseball, volleyball, basketball and football.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diving board - a board for diving into water, typically at a swimming pool

Etymology

From 'spring' (to leap) + 'board' (plank), referring to a board that springs back.
