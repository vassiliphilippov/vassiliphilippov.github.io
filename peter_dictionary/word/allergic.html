allergic

1. ADJ

/ əˈlɝːdʒɪk / (AmE), / əˈlɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
Having or caused by an allergy; reacting abnormally to a substance.

Examples

Cause of death has been attributed to a violent allergic reaction... ... although authorities have not ruled out the possibility of drug use.

Synset Examples

an allergic reaction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypersensitive - abnormally sensitive or easily affected

Etymology

From Greek 'allos' (other) + '-ergic' (producing), via the term 'allergy'.
