pityingly

1. ADV

/ ˈpɪtiɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpɪtiɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows pity or compassion towards someone.

Examples

"See that guy "? the operator asked pityingly.

Synset Examples

the nurse looked at him pityingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compassionately - in a manner showing compassion or sympathy

Etymology

From 'pitying' + '-ly', where 'pitying' is derived from 'pity' (from Old French pitié, from Latin pietas 'piety').
