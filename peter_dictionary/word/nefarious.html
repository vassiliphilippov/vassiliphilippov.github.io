nefarious

1. ADJ

/ nɪˈfɛriəs / (AmE), / nɪˈfeəriəs / (BrE)
Wicked or criminal; extremely evil or immoral.

Examples

Is it true that in the year 1950, you devised the murder of the heads of the so-called "Five Families" in New York to assume and consolidate your nefarious power?

Synset Examples

nefarious schemes
a villainous plot
a villainous band of thieves

Related Terms

  • Synonym: villainous - deliberately evil or wicked
  • Synonym: evil - profoundly immoral and wicked
  • Antonym: good - morally right; virtuous

Etymology

From Latin nefas, nefarious-, meaning 'crime, wrong, impious.'
