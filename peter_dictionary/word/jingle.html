jingle

1. VERB

/ ˈdʒɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
(of small metal objects) to make a light, ringing sound when shaken or moved together.

Examples

He, too, cocked his cap at a jaunty angle, jingled marbles in his pocket, and swaggered down Main Street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clink - make a short, sharp, ringing sound, typically of glass or metal

Etymology

Imitative in origin, echoing the sound itself.
