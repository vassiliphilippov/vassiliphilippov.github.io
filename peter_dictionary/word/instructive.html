instructive

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈstrʌktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌktɪv / (BrE)
Providing useful information or guidance; serving to instruct or inform.

Examples

Inspiring - yes; instructive - maybe; duplicable - no!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - providing education or instruction
  • Synonym: informative - providing useful or interesting information
  • Antonym: uninformative - not providing useful or interesting information

Etymology

From Latin instructivus (fitted for, useful for instructing), from instruere (to build, instruct).
