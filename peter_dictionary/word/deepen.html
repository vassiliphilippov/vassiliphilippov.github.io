deepen

1. VERB

/ ˈdiːpən / (AmE), / ˈdiːpən / (BrE)
(of feelings, situations, or qualities) to become more intense or stronger.

Examples

Even so, Madden's dislike of the suave, correct lawyer deepened.

Synset Examples

The debate intensified
His dislike for raw fish only deepened in Japan

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensify - to become or make more intense

Etymology

From deep + -en (Old English *dēop* + suffix *-en* forming verbs).
