graciously

1. ADV

/ ˈɡreɪʃəsli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a kind, courteous, or graceful manner.

Examples

Boxell did not have the chance to grow up graciously.

Synset Examples

he did not have a chance to grow up graciously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindly - in a kind or benevolent way
  • Antonym: rudely - in an impolite or offensive manner

Etymology

From 'gracious' + '-ly', where 'gracious' comes from Old French 'gracieux', from Latin 'gratia' (favor, esteem).
