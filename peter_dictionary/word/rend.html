rend

1. VERB

/ rɛnd / (AmE), / rɛnd / (BrE)
to tear or split something violently or with force

Examples

He and Mary would no longer have to tear and rend at each other; their mutual torture would be over.

Synset Examples

The curtain ripped from top to bottom
pull the cooked chicken into strips

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tear - to pull apart or split with force
  • Synonym: rip - to tear quickly and forcefully

Etymology

From Old English *rendan*, origin uncertain.
