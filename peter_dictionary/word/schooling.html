schooling

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the education or training received at a school; the process of teaching or being taught in a school setting

Examples

The bill is designed to provide special schooling for more deaf students in the scholastic age at a reduced cost to the state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: education - the process of receiving or giving systematic instruction

Etymology

From 'school' + '-ing', denoting the action or process of school-based education.
