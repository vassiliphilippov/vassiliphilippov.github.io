golfer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑlfər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒlfə / (BrE)
a person who plays the game of golf, either professionally or for recreation

Examples

The world's best golfer, shooting below par, came to the last hole of the opening round of the Los Angeles open with every intention of delivering a final crusher.
If there had been, he would have found a loophole, because Arnold is one golfer who knows the code as thoroughly as the man who wrote the book.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golf - a game played on a large open-air course, in which a ball is struck with a club into a series of small holes

Etymology

From golf + -er, denoting someone who plays golf.
