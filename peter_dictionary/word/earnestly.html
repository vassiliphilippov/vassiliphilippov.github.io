earnestly

1. ADV

/ ˈɜrnɪstli / (AmE), / ˈɜːnɪstli / (BrE)
In a serious and sincere way; with intense and genuine intention.

Examples

"I do my best ", said Mr. Skyros earnestly, "just for you, my friend.
He disliked Garth on sight, conservative clothes and haircut, smile a shade too earnestly boyish for a man who must be well into his thirties, handclasp too consciously quick and firm.

Synset Examples

talking earnestly with his son
she started studying snakes in earnest
a play dealing seriously with the question of divorce

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seriously - in a serious way; with seriousness

Etymology

From 'earnest' + '-ly', with 'earnest' from Old English 'eornost' (seriousness).
