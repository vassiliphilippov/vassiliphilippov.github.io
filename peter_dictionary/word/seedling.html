seedling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsidlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːdlɪŋ / (BrE)
A young plant or tree that has grown from a seed, especially before it becomes established.

Examples

We put a light mulch over the seedlings; now we must use a heavy one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sapling - a young tree, especially one with a slender trunk

Etymology

From 'seed' + diminutive suffix '-ling', meaning a small or young seed-derived organism.
