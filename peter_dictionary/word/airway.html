airway

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛəˌweɪ / (BrE)
A passage or duct through which air flows, especially in the respiratory system or for ventilation.

Related Terms

Etymology

From 'air' + 'way', denoting a way through which air passes.
