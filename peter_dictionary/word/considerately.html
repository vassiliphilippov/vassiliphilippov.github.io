considerately

1. ADV

/ kənˈsɪd.ər.ət.li / (AmE), / kənˈsɪd.ər.ət.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful thought for the needs or feelings of others; in a considerate manner.

Examples

Cameras stared at soloists' faces in extreme closeups, then considerately pulled back for full views of ensemble work.
Then, having wrapped the lot in an afghan my dog customarily slept on, you lammed out the front door, considerately leaving it open for neighbors to discover.

Synset Examples

they considerately withdrew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtfully - in a thoughtful or considerate manner
  • Antonym: inconsiderately - in a thoughtless or insensitive manner

Etymology

From 'considerate' + '-ly', where 'considerate' comes from 'consider' and '-ate', ultimately from Latin 'considerare'.
