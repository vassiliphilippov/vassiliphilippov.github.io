shipyard

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌjɑːd / (BrE)
A place where ships are built or repaired.

Examples

They visited the shipyards at Brest and Pierre had to sign the register, vouching for the integrity of the visiting foreigner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dockyard - a place with docks where ships are repaired, often synonymous with shipyard

Etymology

From 'ship' + 'yard' (an area or enclosure).
