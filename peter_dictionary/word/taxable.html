taxable

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæksəbl / (AmE), / ˈtæksəbl / (BrE)
(of income, goods, or funds) liable to be taxed by law.

Examples

The annual dividends paid to du Pont stockholders in shares of General Motors stock would be taxable as ordinary income to the extent of du Pont's earnings and profits.

Synset Examples

taxable income
nonexempt property

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nontaxable - not subject to taxation

Etymology

From 'tax' + '-able', with '-able' from Latin '-abilis' indicating ability or capacity.
