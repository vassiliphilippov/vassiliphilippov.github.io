Havana

1. PROPN

/ həˈvænə / (AmE), / həˈvɑːnə / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Cuba, situated in the western part of the island

Examples

The family is making an investment in Havana.
Anybody I know in Havana?
Gentlemen, to a night in Havana!

Etymology

From Spanish 'Habana', the Spanish name for the city.
