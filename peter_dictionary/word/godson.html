godson

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑdˌsʌn / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌsʌn / (BrE)
A male child for whom a person acts as sponsor at baptism, often forming a special spiritual relationship.

Examples

Johnny is my father's godson.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: goddaughter - female godchild; sponsored at baptism
  • Synonym: godchild - a child sponsored at baptism; gender-neutral term

Etymology

From 'god' + 'son' — referencing the spiritual relationship in baptism.
