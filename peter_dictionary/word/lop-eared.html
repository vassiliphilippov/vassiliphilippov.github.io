lop-eared

1. ADJ

/ lɑp ˈɪrd / (AmE), / lɒp ˈɪəd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having ears that bend or droop downward rather than standing upright.

Examples

I think you made a dam good chouise to turn off as nise a feler as Alf dyer and let that orney thefin, drunkard, damed card playing Sun of a bich com to Sea you, the god damed theaf and lop yeard pigen tode helion, he is too orney for hel.

Synset Examples

a lop-eared hound

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prick-eared - having ears that stand upright

Etymology

Formed from 'lop' (to hang loosely or droop) and 'eared' (having ears).
