recognize

1. VERB

/ ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɛkəɡnʌɪz / (BrE)
to formally acknowledge or express gratitude for someone's contribution, achievement, or service

Examples

Graham will be recognized for his meritorious service to baseball and will get the William J. Slocum Memorial Award.
The 53 - year old Shea, a prominent corporation lawyer with a sports background, is generally recognized as the man most responsible for the imminent return of a National League club to New York.

Synset Examples

We must acknowledge the kindness she showed towards us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acknowledge - to express recognition or gratitude for something

Etymology

From Old French 'reconoistre', from Latin 'recognoscere' (to recall to mind, review).
