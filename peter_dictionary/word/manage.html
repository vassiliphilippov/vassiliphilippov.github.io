/ ˈmænɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒ / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: succeed - to achieve the desired aim or result
  • Antonym: fail - to not achieve the desired aim or result

Etymology

From Old French *managier* (to handle, train), from Vulgar Latin *manidiare*, from Latin *manus* (hand).
