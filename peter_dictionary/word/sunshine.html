sunshine

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌnˌʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsʌnʃaɪn / (BrE)
the light and warmth that come from the sun when the sky is clear

Examples

The girl crawled out into the renewing warmth of the sunshine, hugging her shoulders and still trembling.

Synset Examples

the shingles were weathered by the sun and wind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunlight - light that comes from the sun

Etymology

From Middle English 'sonne' (sun) + 'shene'/'shine' (brightness, shining).
