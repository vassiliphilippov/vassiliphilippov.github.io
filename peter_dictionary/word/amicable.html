amicable

1. ADJ

/ ˈæmɪkəbəl / (AmE), / ˈamɪkəbl / (BrE)
(of relations or agreements) characterized by friendliness and a lack of conflict.

Examples

Attorneys for the mayor said that an amicable property settlement has been agreed upon.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic

Etymology

From Late Latin amicabilis, from Latin amicus (friend).
