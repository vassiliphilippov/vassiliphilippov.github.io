mama

1. NOUN

/ məˈmɑ / (AmE), / məˈmɑː / (BrE)
(informal) One's mother; used especially by children or in affectionate address.

Examples

He was safe in his Mama's arms.
In the kitchen Mama was wiping the cupboards.
"We have nine hundred and eleven members in our charge ", Mama announced, "and three hundred and eighty Sunday-school scholars ".
If there was a fire or a flood he would save Mama first and Violet next.
"Oh yes ", Mama laughed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mom - informal term for mother, chiefly North American
  • Synonym: mum - informal term for mother, chiefly British
  • Synonym: mother - female parent

Etymology

Imitative of infant speech; similar forms found in many languages.
