velocity

1. NOUN

/ vəˈlɑːsəti / (AmE), / vɪˈlɒsəti / (BrE)
The speed at which something moves in a particular direction; distance travelled per unit time, especially as a physical quantity in science and engineering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speed - rate at which someone or something moves

Etymology

From Latin velocitas, from velox (swift, rapid).
