epicure

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛpɪˌkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪˌkjʊə / (BrE)
a person who takes particular pleasure in fine food and drink

Examples

"I want the fish served whole, with head and tail ", the epicure explained, "and serve it with lemon in mouth ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gourmet - a person who has a refined taste in food and drink
  • Antonym: glutton - a person who eats to excess

Etymology

From Greek Epikouros (Epicurus), the philosopher who advocated pleasure as the highest good.
