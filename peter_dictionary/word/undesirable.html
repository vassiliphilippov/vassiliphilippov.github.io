undesirable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈzaɪɹəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪəɹəbəl / (BrE)
not wanted or welcome; regarded as objectionable or harmful

Examples

In doing so science has unquestionably cleared up widespread misconceptions, removed extraneous and illusory sources of fear, and dispelled many undesirable popular superstitions.
Hard physical labor and undesirable hours are a part of farm life.

Synset Examples

undesirable impurities in steel
legislation excluding undesirable aliens
removed the unwanted vegetation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: desirable - wanted; pleasing or attractive
  • Synonym: unwanted - not wanted or desired

Etymology

From un- (not) + desirable (from Old French désirable, from désirer 'to desire')
