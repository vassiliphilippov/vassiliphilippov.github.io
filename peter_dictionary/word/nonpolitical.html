nonpolitical

1. ADJ

/ nɑn.pəˈlɪt̬.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / nɒn.pəˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Not connected with or related to politics or political matters.

Examples

There were rumors that the Ministry of the Interior favored an arbitrary, "non-political "process, which were indirectly affirmed when the King personally intervened in the planned meetings.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: political - relating to government or public affairs

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'non-' to 'political'.
