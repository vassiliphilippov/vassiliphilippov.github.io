bricklayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪkˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkˌleɪə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to build walls, buildings, and other structures using bricks.

Examples

"There is a man named Grabski who is the foreman in charge of the bricklayers at Majdanek.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mason - a skilled builder and worker in stone or brick

Etymology

From 'brick' + 'layer', referring to one who lays bricks.
