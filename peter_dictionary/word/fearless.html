fearless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈfɪə.ləs / (BrE)
Not afraid of anything; showing courage in dangerous or difficult situations.

Examples

He thought of Simms Purdew rising up, fearless in glory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brave - showing courage and readiness to face danger or pain
  • Synonym: courageous - having or showing courage
  • Antonym: cowardly - lacking courage; easily frightened
  • Antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened

Etymology

From Old English 'fǣr' (fear) + '-less' (without).
