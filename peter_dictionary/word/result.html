Related Terms

  • Synonym: consequence - a result or effect of an action or condition
  • Synonym: outcome - the final result or effect of an action, event, or process
  • Antonym: cause - a person or thing that gives rise to an action, phenomenon, or condition

Etymology

From Latin resultare (to rebound, result), from re- (back) + saltare (to jump).
