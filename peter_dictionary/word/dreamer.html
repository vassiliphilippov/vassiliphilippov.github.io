dreamer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdriːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈdriːmə / (BrE)
a person who is experiencing a dream while asleep

Examples

For instance, the dreamer sees himself seated behind neighbor Smith and, with photographic realism, sees Smith driving the car; whereas, it is a matter of fact that Smith cannot drive a car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeper - a person who is asleep

Etymology

From Old English drēam (joy, music) and later 'dream', plus -er suffix indicating 'one who does'.
