husbandry

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌzbəndri / (AmE), / ˈhʌzbəndri / (BrE)
The practice of cultivating the land or keeping and breeding animals for food; farming.

Examples

Husbandry was bounded by snake-rail fences, and there were grazing cattle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agriculture - science or practice of farming
  • Synonym: animal husbandry - the care, breeding, and management of livestock

Etymology

From Old French *husbanderie* (household management), from 'husband' meaning master of a house.
