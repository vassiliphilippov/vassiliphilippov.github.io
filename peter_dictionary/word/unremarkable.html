unremarkable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈmɑrkəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈmɑːkəbəl / (BrE)
Not special or unusual in any way; ordinary or typical.

Examples

Done by a professional decorator, Madden thought, and somehow as impersonal, as unremarkable as its occupant.

Synset Examples

a placid everyday scene
it was a routine day
there's nothing quite like a real...train conductor to add color to a quotidian commute

Related Terms

  • Antonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
  • Synonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal
  • Synonym: commonplace - not unusual; ordinary

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + remarkable (worthy of notice).
