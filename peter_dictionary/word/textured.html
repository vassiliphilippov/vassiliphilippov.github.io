textured

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛkstʃɚd / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstʃəd / (BrE)
Having a surface that is rough or has an uneven, distinctive feel or pattern.

Examples

Over the rapidly diminishing outline of a jump seat piled high with luggage Herry's black brushcut was just discernible, near, or enviably near that spot where - hidden - more delicately textured, most beautifully tinted hair must still be streaming back in cool, oh cool wind sweetly perfumed with sagebrush and yucca flowers and engine fumes.

Synset Examples

a textured wall of stucco
a rough-textured tweed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • Synonym: rough - having an uneven or irregular surface

Etymology

Formed from 'texture' + '-ed', indicating possessing a texture.
