comic

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑː.mɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.mɪk / (BrE)
Intended to make people laugh; humorous or amusing.

Examples

He was n't a dwarf but he was a bit of a comic figure.
Apart from misplaced modifiers and headlinese, journalism contributes a wide variety of comic ambiguities in both editorial and advertising matter.
The comic indefinite comprises an extensive class of comedy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amusing - entertaining and causing laughter

Etymology

From Latin 'comicus', from Greek 'kōmikos' (pertaining to comedy).
