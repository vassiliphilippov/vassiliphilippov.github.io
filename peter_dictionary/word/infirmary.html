infirmary

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈfɜrməri / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːməri / (BrE)
A place within an institution such as a school, prison, or military base where people who are ill or injured are given medical care.

Examples

That way they have an infirmary for $ 400.
I'm thinking Andy could use a nice welcome back when he gets out of the infirmary.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hospital - a large facility for treating the sick and injured

Etymology

From Latin 'infirmaria', from 'infirmus' meaning weak or sick.
