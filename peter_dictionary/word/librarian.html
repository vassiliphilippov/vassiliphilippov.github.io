librarian

1. NOUN

/ laɪˈbrɛriən / (AmE), / laɪˈbrɛəriən / (BrE)
a person trained in library science who manages or assists in the operation of a library

Examples

The major studios maintain a cadre of film librarians and research specialists who look to this matter.
I come here in '05, and they made me librarian in 1912.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: library assistant - person who helps librarians in library duties

Etymology

From Latin 'librarius' (relating to books), from 'liber' (book).
