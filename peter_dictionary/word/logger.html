logger

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔɡər / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to cut down trees for timber.

Examples

This is no longer a way of life for the bearded logger and the wandering cowboy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lumberjack - a person who fells trees, especially in North America

Etymology

From 'log' (tree trunk) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
