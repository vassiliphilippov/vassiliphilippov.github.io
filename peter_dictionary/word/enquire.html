enquire

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈkwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪə / (BrE)
to ask for information; to inquire

Examples

"Vous voulez vos petits dejeuners tout de suite alors "? their hostess enquired.

Synset Examples

I asked about their special today
He had to ask directions several times

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inquire - to ask for information; to investigate
  • Synonym: ask - to put a question to someone

Etymology

From Old French *enquerre*, from Latin *inquaerere* (to seek, to inquire), from *in-* (into) + *quaerere* (to seek).
