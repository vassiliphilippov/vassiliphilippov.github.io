/ ˈɑfər / (AmE), / ˈɒfə / (BrE)
to present something for someone to accept or reject

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: propose - to put forward an idea or plan for consideration
  • Antonym: withdraw - to remove an offer or proposal

Etymology

From Latin 'offere', from 'ob-' (toward) + 'ferre' (to bring).
