sighting

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
An occasion when someone sees something, especially something unusual or rare.

Examples

Here's the sightings so far.

Synset Examples

several sightings of enemy troops were reported

Etymology

From the verb 'sight' + -ing, meaning the act of seeing.
