rooftree

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːfˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈruːfˌtriː / (BrE)
The main beam running along the top of a roof, where two sloping sides meet; the ridgepole.

Examples

Whosoever violates our rooftree, the legend states, can expect maximal sorrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ridgepole - a beam along the ridge of a roof

Etymology

From 'roof' + 'tree' (in the sense of timber or beam).
