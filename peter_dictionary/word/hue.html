hue

1. NOUN

/ hju / (AmE), / hjuÀê / (BrE)
a color or shade, especially in terms of its dominant wavelength; the attribute of a color that distinguishes it from other colors (such as red, green, or blue)

Examples

While white is the coolest summer shade, there are lots of pastel hues along with tintable fabrics that will blend with any wardrobe color.
They come in crisp basket weaves in natural honey hues, along with lacy open weaves with a lustre finish in natural, white, black and a whole range of colors.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shade - variation of a color produced by adding black
  • Synonym: tint - a slight or pale coloration; a lighter variety of a color produced by adding white

Etymology

From Old English *hiw* (form, appearance, color).
