balletomane

1. NOUN

/ bəˈlɛtəˌmeɪn / (AmE), / bəˈlɛt.ə.meɪn / (BrE)
A person who is an enthusiastic admirer or devotee of ballet.

Examples

A couple of days later a balletomane told me he had telephoned Allied Arts for ticket information and was told "the newspapers had made a mistake ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aficionado - an enthusiastic admirer or fan, especially of an activity or subject

Etymology

From French 'balletomane', from 'ballet' (ballet) + '-mane' (from Greek 'mania', meaning 'madness, enthusiasm').
