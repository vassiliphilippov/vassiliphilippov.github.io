alias

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪ.li.əs / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.li.əs / (BrE)
a false or assumed name used temporarily to conceal one's identity

Examples

Did he at times use an alias that was known in certain circles as Godfather?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pseudonym - a fictitious name, especially one used by an author

Etymology

From Latin 'alias', meaning 'at another time', 'otherwise'.
