hemispherical

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛmɪˈsfɪrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪˈsfɪərɪkəl / (BrE)
having the shape or characteristics of a hemisphere; relating to or being a hemisphere.

Examples

The top of the sample was nearly flat and the bottom hemispherical.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: spherical - having the shape of a sphere

Etymology

From hemisphere + -ical, influenced by Latin and Greek roots: Greek hēmisphairion (‘hemisphere’).
