rubbish

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrʌbɪʃ / (BrE)
Waste material or things that are no longer wanted or useful and are to be thrown away; garbage or trash.

Examples

"Officials Meet on Rubbish.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: garbage - (North American English) waste material; trash
  • Synonym: trash - waste matter or refuse, especially as in American English

Etymology

Probably from Anglo-Norman *rubbouse* (refuse, waste), possibly related to 'rub' or 'rubble'.
