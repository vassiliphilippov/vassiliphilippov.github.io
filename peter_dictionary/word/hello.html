hello

1. NOUN

/ həˈloʊ / (AmE), / həˈləʊ / (BrE)
An expression of greeting used when meeting someone, or the act of greeting itself.

Examples

When the therapist came to feel on sufficiently sure ground with him to ask him, "What is that, Bill - hello or farewell "?
Come on, say hello to Carlo.

Synset Examples

every morning they exchanged polite hellos

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeting - words or actions used to greet someone

Etymology

From alteration of earlier 'hallo', 'hollo', likely influenced by Old High German 'halâ', used to call attention.
