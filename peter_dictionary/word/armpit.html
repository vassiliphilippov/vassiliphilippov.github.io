armpit

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːrmˌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmpɪt / (BrE)
The hollow area under the arm at the shoulder, where the arm connects to the body.

Examples

The walk and his fears had served to overheat him and his sweaty armpits cooled at the touch of the night air.
He carried three guns - one in the right trouser pocket, one under his left armpit, one in the left outside coat pocket - and was equally lethal with both hands.

Synset Examples

they were up to their armpits in water

Related Terms

  • Synonym: axilla - the technical or medical term for the armpit

Etymology

From Middle English arme (arm) + pit (cavity, depression).
