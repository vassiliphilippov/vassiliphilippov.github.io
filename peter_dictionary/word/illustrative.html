illustrative

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlʌstrətɪv, ɪˈlʌstreɪtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈlʌstrətɪv, ɪˈlʌstreɪtɪv / (BrE)
serving to explain or clarify something by giving examples

Examples

These examples are illustrative of the use of the words "trial "and "experiment "as they are used in this chapter, but they are quite flexible words and it is well not to restrict them too narrowly.
On this basis, certain extreme kinds of networks will be discussed for illustrative purposes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explanatory - serving to explain something
  • Synonym: descriptive - serving to describe something

Etymology

From Latin illustrativus, from illustrare (to make clear, illuminate).
