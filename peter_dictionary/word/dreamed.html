dreamed

1. ADJ

/ ˈdriːmd / (AmE), / ˈdriːmd / (BrE)
Conceived of, imagined, or hoped for; existing only in dreams or in the imagination.

Examples

How lightly her "eventshah-leh "passed into the crannies where I was storing dialect material for some vaguely dreamed opus, and how the word would echo.
You were around the old-timers, who dreamed of how the families should be organized, basing them on the old Roman legions, and call '’em "regimes," the "capos" and "soldiers."

Synset Examples

his dreamed symphony that would take the world of music by storm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: imaginary - existing only in the imagination
  • Antonym: realized - actually achieved or made real
