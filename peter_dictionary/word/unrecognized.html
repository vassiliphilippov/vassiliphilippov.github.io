unrecognized

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪzd / (BrE)
Not recognized or identified; not officially accepted or acknowledged.

Examples

So, for all practical purposes, it was an independent nation and by now had its own organized government (unrecognized outside its own borders).

Synset Examples

he was unrecognized in his disguise

Related Terms

  • Antonym: recognized - officially accepted or identified

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'recognized', past participle of 'recognize'.
