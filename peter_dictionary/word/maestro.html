maestro

1. NOUN

/ maɪˈstroʊ / (AmE), / maɪˈstrəʊ / (BrE)
A distinguished artist, especially a conductor or musician, who is recognized for exceptional skill and mastery.

Examples

- Maestro!

Synset Examples

a master of the violin
one of the old masters

Etymology

From Italian 'maestro' meaning 'master', from Latin 'magister'.
