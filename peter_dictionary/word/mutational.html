mutational

1. ADJ

/ mjuːˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / mjuːˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by mutation, especially in genetic material or biological traits

Examples

It is possible that certain mutational forms may be produced such as antibiotic resistant strains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mutation - a change in genetic material

Etymology

Formed from 'mutation' with the suffix '-al' (relating to). 'Mutation' from Latin 'mutatio' (change).
