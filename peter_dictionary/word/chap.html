chap

1. NOUN

/ tʃæp / (AmE), / tʃap / (BrE)
An informal term for a boy or man.

Examples

For the hero of this work by John Osborne and Anthony Creighton is a chap embittered by more than the lack of beer during a jam session.

Synset Examples

that chap is your host
there's a fellow at the door
he's a likable cuss
he's a good bloke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fellow - a man or boy; a person in general
  • Synonym: bloke - a man (chiefly British, informal)

Etymology

Probably from a shortened form of 'chapman' (an old term for a customer or man).
