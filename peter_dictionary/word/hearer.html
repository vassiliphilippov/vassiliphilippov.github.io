hearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪrər / (AmE), / ˈhɪərə / (BrE)
a person who listens attentively to someone or something being said

Examples

An enthusiastic audience confirmed the "live "character of the hour, and provided the interaction between musician and hearer which almost always seems to improve the quality of performance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: listener - someone who listens

Etymology

From Old English *hēran* (to hear) + -er (agent noun suffix).
