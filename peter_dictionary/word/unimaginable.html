unimaginable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪˈmædʒɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈmadʒɪnəbl / (BrE)
impossible to think of or believe; extremely unlikely or difficult to conceive

Examples

The letters took their source from a stream of my imagination in which I was transformed into a young man not unlike my bunkmate Eliot Sands - he of the porch steps anecdotes - who smoked cigarettes, performed the tango, wore fifty dollar suits, and sneaked off into the dark with girls to do unimaginable things with them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impossible - not able to occur, exist, or be done
  • Antonym: conceivable - capable of being imagined or grasped mentally

Etymology

From un- (not) + imaginable, based on imagine (from Latin imaginari, to form a picture to oneself).
