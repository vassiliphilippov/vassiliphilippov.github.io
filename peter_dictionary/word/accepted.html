accepted

1. ADJ

/ əkˈsɛptɪd / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptɪd / (BrE)
Regarded as generally approved, recognized, or believed to be valid or correct by most people.

Examples

Benington recalled that he once told Hartweger that he doubted Gordon would ever play much for him because he seemed to be lacking in all of the accepted basketball skills.
When values of temperature derived with this instrument were compared with the accepted values associated with liquid helium 4 vapor pressures, differences of about 10 and 7 millidegrees respectively were found.

Synset Examples

several accepted techniques for treating the condition
his recognized superiority in this kind of work

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rejected - not accepted; refused or dismissed

Etymology

Past participle of 'accept', from Latin 'acceptare' (to receive, accept).
