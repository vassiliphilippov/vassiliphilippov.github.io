swanky

1. ADJ

/ ˈswæŋki / (AmE), / ˈswaŋki / (BrE)
Imposingly fashionable, stylish, and elegant, often in a way that is meant to impress others.

Examples

The hotels here are bigger and swankier than any of the rug joints we put in Vegas.

Synset Examples

a swank apartment

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegant - pleasingly graceful and stylish in appearance or manner
  • Synonym: posh - elegant or stylishly luxurious
  • Antonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate; simple

Etymology

Probably from English slang 'swank' (swagger, show off), late 19th century.
