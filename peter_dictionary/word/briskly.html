briskly

1. ADV

/ ˈbrɪskli / (AmE), / ˈbrɪskli / (BrE)
In a quick, energetic, and active manner.

Examples

Stilts, a more direct cat, leaped from the bed and trotted briskly toward the kitchen.

Synset Examples

she walked briskly in the cold air
`after lunch,' she said briskly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
