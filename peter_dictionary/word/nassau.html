Nassau

1. PROPN

/ ˈnæsɔː / (AmE), / ˈnæsɔː / (BrE)
the capital city of the Bahamas, located on New Providence Island

Examples

His money was tied up in a Nassau hotel, an Ohio pottery works, and a detergent for window-washing, and luck had been running against him.

Etymology

From the House of Nassau, a European royal dynasty, itself named after the town of Nassau in Germany.
