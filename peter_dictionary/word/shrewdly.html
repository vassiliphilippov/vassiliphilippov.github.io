shrewdly

1. ADV

/ ˈʃruːdli / (AmE), / ˈʃruːdli / (BrE)
In a clever, astute, or practical manner, especially in matters of judgment or decision-making.

Examples

Counsel for the defense, however, shrewdly allowing himself to be swept by the current of dreadful recollections, rarely raised an objection.

Synset Examples

he invested his fortune astutely
he was acutely insightful

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astutely - in a clever and perceptive way
  • Antonym: naively - in a simple or innocent way, lacking experience
