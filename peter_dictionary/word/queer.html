queer

1. ADJ

/ kwɪr / (AmE), / kwɪə / (BrE)
Strange or unusual; deviating from what is expected or normal.

Examples

Oh, he was being queer and careful, pawing about in the drawer and holding the bottle like a snake at the length of his arm.
Time is a queer thing and memory a queerer; the tricks that time plays with memory and memory with time are queerest of all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: odd - strange; different from what is usual or expected
  • Synonym: peculiar - strange or unusual, especially in a way that is unsettling
  • Antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; usual, typical, or expected

Etymology

Probably from German *quer* (oblique, perverse, odd) and earlier Low German.

2. NOUN

/ kwɪr / (AmE), / kwɪə / (BrE)
An offensive slang term for a homosexual man, especially one who is openly gay.

Examples

Bull queers take by force.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: faggot - an offensive slang term for a homosexual man
  • Synonym: gay - a homosexual person, particularly a man
