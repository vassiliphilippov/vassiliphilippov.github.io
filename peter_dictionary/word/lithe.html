lithe

1. ADJ

/ laɪð / (AmE), / laɪð / (BrE)
(of a person or their body) thin, supple, and graceful; moving and bending easily and elegantly.

Examples

One lithe leg straddled the railing and swung loosely before the creaking, torn pales.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supple - bending and moving easily and gracefully
  • Antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed; stiff

Etymology

From Old English līthe (gentle, soft, flexible), related to Old High German lindi (gentle).
