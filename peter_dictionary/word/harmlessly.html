harmlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈhɑrmlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmləsli / (BrE)
In a way that causes no harm or damage.

Examples

High, so it would only bounce harmlessly but loudly off the car's steel roof.

Synset Examples

this is a harmlessly childish game

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dangerously - in a manner likely to cause harm

Etymology

From 'harmless' + '-ly'. 'Harmless' ultimately from Old English 'hearm' (harm) + 'lēas' (less).
