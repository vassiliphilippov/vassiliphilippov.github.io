mow

1. VERB

/ moʊ / (AmE), / məʊ / (BrE)
to cut grass, crops, or similar vegetation using a blade or machine

Examples

Yet a grassy approach can turn a pool into a floating lawn every time the grass is mowed.

Synset Examples

mow the grass

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cut - to divide or trim with a sharp instrument

Etymology

From Old English *mawan* (to mow, cut down grass or grain), related to German *mähen* and Dutch *maaien*.
