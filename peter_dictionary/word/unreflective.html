unreflective

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪv / (BrE)
Not thinking deeply about things; lacking careful or considered thought.

Examples

The cyclist, by contrast, blond and blue-eyed, is simply unreflective, unproblematic Life, "blithe and carefree ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thoughtful - showing consideration or careful thought
  • Antonym: reflective - thinking deeply or carefully

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'reflective' (thoughtful).
