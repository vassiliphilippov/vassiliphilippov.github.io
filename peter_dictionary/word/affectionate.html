affectionate

1. ADJ

/ əˈfɛkʃənət / (AmE), / əˈfɛkʃənət / (BrE)
showing feelings of love or fondness; demonstrating affection or warmth towards others

Examples

The name fell with lazy affectionate remembrance from her lips.
Angelo gave him an affectionate smile.

Synset Examples

affectionate children
a fond embrace
fond of his nephew
a tender glance
a warm embrace

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fond - having affection or liking for someone or something
  • Synonym: tender - showing gentleness, kindness, or affection
  • Synonym: warm - showing enthusiasm, affection, or kindness
  • Antonym: cold - lacking affection or warmth
  • Antonym: aloof - not friendly or forthcoming; distant

Etymology

From Latin 'affectionem' (influence, emotion).
