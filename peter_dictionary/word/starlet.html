starlet

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrlət / (AmE), / ˈstɑːlət / (BrE)
A young actress, especially one who is being promoted as likely to become a movie star.

Examples

Soaring to stardom along with him, Margo Felicity Brighetti, a luscious and curvaceously beguiling Italian starlet, turns in a creditable performance as an airplane mechanic.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: star - a famous and celebrated performer

Etymology

Formed from 'star' + diminutive suffix '-let', indicating a small or young star.
