ruffled

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌfəld / (AmE), / ˈrʌfəld / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) having decorative ruffles or frills.

Examples

A tall lady, with a ruffled collar very low on her bosom, turned insolent green eyes upon him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frilled - having frills or decorative edging
