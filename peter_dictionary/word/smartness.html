smartness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmɑrtnəs / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being intelligent, quick-witted, or clever.

Examples

- A smart fellow, ain't he? -
I suppose I always knew you were too smart to let any of them ever beat you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleverness - the quality of being clever or ingenious
  • Antonym: dullness - the quality of lacking intelligence or quickness

Etymology

From 'smart' + noun suffix '-ness', meaning quality or state.
