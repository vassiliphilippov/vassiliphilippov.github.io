roadside

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊd.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd.saɪd / (BrE)
the land along the edge of a road

Examples

Cool air moving slowly through the open or smashed out side windows hinted of blooming roadside vegetation, and occasionally a faint fragrance of perfume swirled from the back seat.

Synset Examples

flowers along the wayside

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wayside - the edge of a way or path; land beside a road

Etymology

Compound of 'road' + 'side'.
