spoon

1. NOUN

/ spun / (AmE), / spuːn / (BrE)
A piece of cutlery with a shallow, typically oval or round bowl at the end of a handle, used for eating, stirring, or serving food.

Examples

She would hover over him and, looking like her brother, anxiously watch the progress of Scotty's fork or spoon.
That tall drink of water with the silver spoon up his ass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fork - an eating or serving utensil with prongs
  • Synonym: knife - an eating or cutting utensil with a blade and handle

Etymology

From Old English 'spōn' (chip, splinter, spoon), related to Old Norse 'spánn'.
