splintery

1. ADJ

/ ˈsplɪntəri / (AmE), / ˈsplɪntəri / (BrE)
prone to breaking into sharp, slender pieces or containing many splinters.

Examples

I started looking on the splintery truck bed for a piece of board, a dirt clod - anything I could throw and with better aim than I had thrown the beer bottle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splintered - broken into thin sharp pieces

Etymology

From 'splinter' + '-y', meaning having the quality of splinters.
