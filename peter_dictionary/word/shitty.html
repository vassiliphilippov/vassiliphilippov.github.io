shitty

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɪti / (AmE), / ˈʃɪti / (BrE)
Extremely bad, unpleasant, or of poor quality (vulgar and informal).

Examples

These are just shitty pipe dreams.

Synset Examples

a lousy play
it's a stinking world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awful - very bad, of poor quality
  • Synonym: terrible - extremely bad or serious
  • Antonym: excellent - extremely good, outstanding
