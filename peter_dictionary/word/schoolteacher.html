schoolteacher

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌtitʃər / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌtiːtʃə / (BrE)
A teacher who works in a school, especially at a level below college or university.

Examples

School teachers, all too unprepared for the job they must do, will need demonstrators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teacher - a person who teaches, especially in a school
  • Antonym: professor - a teacher at a college or university

Etymology

Formed from 'school' + 'teacher'.
