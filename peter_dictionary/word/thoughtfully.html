thoughtfully

1. ADV

/ ˈθɔt.fəli / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows consideration for the needs or feelings of others; kindly or considerately.

Examples

The rabbi said thoughtfully, "I would not want my people to get in trouble with the Church ".
And can n't say I blame you ", said Angie thoughtfully.
He looked thoughtfully at his wife's trunk, holding her meager treasures.

Synset Examples

he had thoughtfully brought with him some food to share

Related Terms

  • Synonym: considerately - in a way that shows care for others' feelings or needs
  • Antonym: thoughtless - without consideration for others

2. ADV

/ ˈθɔt.fəli / (AmE), / ˈθɔːtfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows careful consideration or deep thought.

Examples

However, first I thoughtfully study my sketch for improvement of color and design along the lines I have described.
"You know, Summers ", he said thoughtfully.
"Of course, I agree with the Captain ", Gansevoort said thoughtfully, "but the conspiracy is ferocious and desperate.

Synset Examples

he stared thoughtfully out the window

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reflectively - in a reflective manner; thoughtfully
  • Antonym: absentmindedly - in a distracted or inattentive manner
