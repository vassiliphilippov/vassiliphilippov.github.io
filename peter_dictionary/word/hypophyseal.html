hypophyseal

1. ADJ

/ haɪˌpɑfəˈsiəl / (AmE), / hɪˌpɒfɪˈsiːəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the hypophysis (pituitary gland)

Examples

Physiological experiments and clinical observations have shown that these procedures influence the hypothalamically controlled hypophyseal secretions and increase sympathetic discharges.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pituitary - relating to the pituitary gland

Etymology

From hypophysis (pituitary gland) + -eal, a common adjectival suffix.
