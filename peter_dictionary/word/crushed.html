crushed

1. ADJ

/ krʌʃt / (AmE), / krʌʃt / (BrE)
(of fabric or material) treated so as to have a permanently wrinkled or creased appearance, often used in textiles such as 'crushed velvet'.

Examples

Softness is found in crushed textures.
At right is a casual style in a crushed unlined white leather.

Synset Examples

crushed velvet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wrinkled - having small folds or lines, especially on a surface like fabric or skin
  • Antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface; free from roughness or wrinkles
