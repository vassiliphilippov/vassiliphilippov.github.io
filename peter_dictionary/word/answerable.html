answerable

1. ADJ

/ ˈæn.sɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
responsible to a higher authority for one's actions, especially morally or legally

Examples

Any man who opens fire on Jack Napier will answer to me.

Synset Examples

parents are answerable for their child's acts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: responsible - having an obligation to do something, or having control over or care for someone

Etymology

From 'answer' + '-able', ultimately from Old English 'andswarian' (to answer).
