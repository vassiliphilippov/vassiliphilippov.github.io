sprue

1. NOUN

/ spru / (AmE), / spru / (BrE)
A chronic disorder of the digestive system, occurring in tropical and non-tropical forms, in which nutrients are not properly absorbed, leading to symptoms such as foul-smelling diarrhea and emaciation.

Examples

Etymology

From Portuguese *espru*, likely from Dutch *spruw*, originally meaning 'thrush' (a disease), later extended to digestive disorders.
