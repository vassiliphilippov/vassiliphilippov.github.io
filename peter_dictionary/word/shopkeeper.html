shopkeeper

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɑpˌkipər / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌkiːpə / (BrE)
A person who owns or manages a shop, especially one selling goods to the public.

Examples

The other patrons were taxi drivers and art students and small shopkeepers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: storekeeper - a person in charge of a store, especially in American usage

Etymology

From 'shop' + 'keeper', indicating one who keeps (manages or owns) a shop.
