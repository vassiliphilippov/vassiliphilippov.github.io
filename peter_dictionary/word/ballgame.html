ballgame

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played on a field or court with a ball, especially baseball, football, or similar sports.

Examples

There'’s more people than at a ballgame in here.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball - a bat-and-ball game played between two teams

Etymology

Compound of 'ball' and 'game'.
