geographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to geography or to the features, location, and characteristics of an area or place.

Examples

It is the urge to obtain a list as free of geographical and cultural conditioning as possible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geographic - relating to the science of geography (alternative spelling)

Etymology

From Latin geographicus, from Ancient Greek geōgraphikos (relating to geography).
