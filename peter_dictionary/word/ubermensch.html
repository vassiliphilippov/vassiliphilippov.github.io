Ubermensch

1. NOUN

/ ˈuːbɚˌmɛnʃ / (AmE), / ˈuːbəmɛnʃ / (BrE)
(in Nietzschean philosophy) an ideal superior man who transcends conventional morality to create and impose his own values.

Examples

Now, you no doubt regard the preceding as pap; you 're tooling around full of gage in your hot rods, gorging yourselves on pizza and playing pinball in the taverns and generally behaving like Ubermenschen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superman - an ideal superior person in Nietzschean philosophy; often used as an English synonym for 'Ubermensch'

Etymology

German, literally ‘superman’, from über (over) + Mensch (man, human). Popularized by Nietzsche in the 19th century.
