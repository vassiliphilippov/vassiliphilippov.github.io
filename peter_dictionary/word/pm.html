PM

1. NOUN

/ pi ˈɛm / (AmE), / piː ˈɛm / (BrE)
an examination of a dead body to determine the cause of death; an autopsy

Examples

The PM might show he drowned instead, but that's what the once-over-lightly gives us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autopsy - a medical examination of a dead body to determine cause of death
  • Synonym: post-mortem - an examination of a body after death to determine cause

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'post mortem', meaning 'after death'.
