unenthusiastic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˌθuziˈæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪk / (BrE)
Lacking enthusiasm or excitement; showing little or no interest or eagerness.

Examples

Local school districts are hard pressed financially and unenthusiastic about vocational training.

Synset Examples

an unenthusiastic performance by the orchestra
unenthusiastic applause

Related Terms

  • Antonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • Synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • Synonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'enthusiastic'.
