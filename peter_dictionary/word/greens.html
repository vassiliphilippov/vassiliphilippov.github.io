greens

1. NOUN

/ ɡrinz / (AmE), / ɡriːnz / (BrE)
leafy vegetables such as spinach, lettuce, or kale, or their leaves and stems, eaten as food

Examples

A salad with greens and tomato is a popular and wonderfully healthful addition to a meal, but add an avocado and you have something really special.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetables - plants or parts of plants eaten as food
