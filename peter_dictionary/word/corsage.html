corsage

1. NOUN

/ kɔrˈsɑːʒ / (AmE), / kɔːˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
a small bouquet of flowers worn on clothing, typically by women at formal events, such as proms or weddings

Examples

Should I bring along a corsage or send one to her?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bouquet - an arrangement of flowers

Etymology

From French 'corsage' (bodice), referring to its placement on the bodice.
