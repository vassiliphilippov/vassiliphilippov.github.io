dark-brown

1. ADJ

/ dɑrk braʊn / (AmE), / dɑːk braʊn / (BrE)
Of a shade of brown that is deep or dark, similar to the color of rich wood or earth.

Examples

The daybed Eugene had slept in, made up now with its dark-brown velours cover and pillows.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - a color similar to that of wood or earth
  • Degree Higher: black - the darkest color, resulting from the absence of light
