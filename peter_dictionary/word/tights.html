tights

1. NOUN

/ taɪts / (AmE), / taɪts / (BrE)
A piece of skin-tight knitted clothing covering the body from the waist to the feet, worn especially by dancers, acrobats, and also as stockings by women and girls.

Examples

He's at home, washing his tights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pantyhose - sheer, close-fitting legwear for women covering from waist to toes
