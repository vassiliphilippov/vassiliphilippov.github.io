Picasso

1. PROPN

/ pɪˈkɑːsoʊ / (AmE), / pɪˈkæsəʊ / (BrE)
Pablo Picasso (1881–1973), a prolific and influential Spanish artist known for co-founding the Cubist movement and for his revolutionary contributions to modern art.

Etymology

From the artist's full name, Pablo Picasso, of Spanish origin.
