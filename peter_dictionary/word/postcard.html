postcard

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstkɑːd / (BrE)
a card, typically rectangular, used for sending a message by mail without an envelope

Examples

Not long after the Warden deprived us of his company, I got a postcard in the mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greeting card - a card sent on a special occasion, such as a birthday or holiday

Etymology

From 'post', relating to mail, and 'card', from Latin 'charta' (paper, card).
