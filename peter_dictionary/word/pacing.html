pacing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
The speed or rate at which a musical composition is performed.

Examples

Without losing the distinctive undertow of Brahmsian rhythm, the pacing is firm and the over-all performance has a tightly knit quality that makes for maximum cumulative effect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tempo - the speed at which a musical composition is to be played
