client

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈklaɪənt / (BrE)
A person who uses the services or seeks the advice of a lawyer or other professional adviser.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin 'cliens' (dependent, follower, client), related to 'clinare' (to lean).

2. NOUN

/ ˈklaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈklaɪənt / (BrE)
A person or organization that receives professional services or purchases goods from a business or professional.

Examples

Based on our experience with clients, we see 14 major problems which fall into three broad groups - the market place itself, marketing methods, and marketing management.

Related Terms

Etymology
