departmental

1. ADJ

/ dɪˌpɑːrtˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / dɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or connected with a department within an organization, institution, or business.

Examples

I tried to do so by calling to their attention some of the problems that a senior departmental policy officer faces.

Synset Examples

departmental policy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: department - a division of a large organization such as a government, university, or company

Etymology

From 'department' + '-al' (forming adjectives)
