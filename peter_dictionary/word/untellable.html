untellable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtɛləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛləbəl / (BrE)
too great, intense, or unusual to be described or expressed in words; indescribable

Examples

It would be a sign for the untellable, and he, Adam, would understand.

Synset Examples

indefinable yearnings
indescribable beauty
ineffable ecstasy
inexpressible anguish
unspeakable happiness
unutterable contempt
a thing of untellable splendor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribable - impossible to describe
  • Synonym: ineffable - too great or extreme to be expressed in words
  • Synonym: inexpressible - unable to be expressed

Etymology

From un- (not) + tell + -able, meaning 'not able to be told'.
