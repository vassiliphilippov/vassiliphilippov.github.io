sojourn

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.dʒɝn / (AmE), / ˈsɒdʒ.ən / (BrE)
a temporary stay in a place away from one's permanent home

Examples

The first result of Heidenstam's long sojourn abroad was a volume of poems, Pilgrimage and Wander Years (Vallfart och vandringsar), published in 1888.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visit - an act of going to see someone or somewhere for a short time

Etymology

From Old French 'sojorner', based on Latin 'subdiurnare' (to spend the day).
