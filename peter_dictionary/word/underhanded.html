underhanded

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɚˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈhændɪd / (BrE)
acting or done in a secret or dishonest way, especially to achieve an unfair advantage.

Examples

He suspected something underhanded and furtive about him.

Synset Examples

achieved success in business only by underhand methods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deceitful - dishonest, intending to mislead or trick
  • Antonym: honest - truthful and free from deceit
