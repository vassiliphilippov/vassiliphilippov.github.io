tantalizingly

1. ADV

/ ˈtænəˌlaɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtantəˌlaɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that arouses desire or interest, often by being tempting but unattainable.

Examples

One of the finest soft shoe tunes ever invented, "Once in Love with Amy "is also, of course, one of the most tantalizingly persistent of light love lyrics to come out of American musical comedy in our era.

Synset Examples

she smiled at him tantalizingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temptingly - in a way that tempts or entices
  • Synonym: frustratingly - in a way that causes frustration, often by being just out of reach

Etymology

Formed from the adjective 'tantalizing' (from the myth of Tantalus) + the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
