crystallite

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a minute, rudimentary crystalline particle, especially one found in glassy igneous rocks or partially crystalline solids

Examples

We found no obvious effects due to preferred orientation of the crystallites in this sample nor would we expect to on the basis of the shape found from electron microscopic examination.

Etymology

From Greek 'kristallos' (crystal) + '-ite' (denoting minerals and related substances).
