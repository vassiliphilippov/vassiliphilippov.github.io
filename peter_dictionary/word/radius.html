radius

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdiəs / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəs / (BrE)
A circular area defined by a specific distance from a central point; the region within a circle whose size is determined by the radius.

Examples

This is true because of savings in utility lines and the fact that your buildings have a useful radius equal in all directions.

Synset Examples

they located it within a radius of 2 miles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circumference - the distance around the edge of a circle

Etymology

From Latin 'radius' meaning 'ray' or 'spoke of a wheel'.
