graphical

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or presented by means of a graph or graphs, especially in scientific or technical contexts.

Examples

The ratio of the measured antenna temperature change during a drift scan across the moon to the average brightness temperature of the moon over the antenna beam (assuming that the brightness temperature of the sky is negligible) was found, by graphical integration of the antenna directivity diagram, to be 0.85.

Synset Examples

a graphic presentation of the data

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tabular - arranged in or relating to a table or tables (of information)
  • Synonym: graphic - relating to visual art or vivid representation, not specific to graphs

Etymology

From 'graphic' + -al, referring to representation by means of graphs.
