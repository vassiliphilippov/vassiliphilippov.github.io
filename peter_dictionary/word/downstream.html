downstream

1. ADV

/ ˌdaʊnˈstriːm / (AmE), / ˌdaʊnˈstriːm / (BrE)
In the direction away from the source of a stream or river, or in the same direction as the current.

Examples

The ball floated downstream.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: upstream - in the direction toward the source of a stream or river
