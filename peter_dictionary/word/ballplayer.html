ballplayer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔlˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌpleɪə / (BrE)
An athlete who plays baseball.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball player - an athlete who plays baseball

Etymology

Formed from 'ball' and 'player,' originally referring to players of ball games, now chiefly baseball in American English.
