paramedic

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɛrəˈmɛdɪk / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈmɛdɪk / (BrE)
A person trained to provide emergency medical care and assist medical professionals, especially in pre-hospital settings.

Examples

I need paramedics up here now!

Etymology

From 'para-' (meaning 'alongside') and 'medic', formed in the mid 20th century.
