latch

1. VERB

/ lætʃ / (AmE), / lætʃ / (BrE)
to fasten or secure (a door, window, etc.) by means of a latch

Examples

It latches when you close it, so stay as long as you like ".
"Do n't forget to turn it off and close the door good so it 'll latch ".

Synset Examples

latch the door

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unlatch - to open or release by removing a latch
  • Synonym: lock - to fasten or secure by means of a lock
