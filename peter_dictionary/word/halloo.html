halloo

1. NOUN

/ həˈlu / (AmE), / həˈluː / (BrE)
a loud shout or cry used to attract attention, especially traditionally in hunting or to greet someone from a distance

Examples

Hey, it's the fat guy!

Synset Examples

he gave a great halloo but no one heard him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hello - used as a greeting or to attract attention
  • Synonym: hail - to call out to attract attention

Etymology

From Middle English 'halow', variant of 'hallow', related to Old French 'haler' (to call).

2. VERB

/ həˈlu / (AmE), / həˈluː / (BrE)
to shout 'halloo!' as a greeting or to attract attention

Examples

Hey, Red!
Hey, Frankie!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hello - common greeting or call for attention
  • Synonym: hey - call to attract attention or greet someone

Etymology

Imitative in origin, echoing the sound of a shout; related to Old English and medieval calls for attention.
