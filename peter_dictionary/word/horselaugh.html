horselaugh

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrsˌlæf / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌlɑːf / (BrE)
A loud, harsh laugh that resembles the neighing of a horse, often used to express mockery or derision.

Examples

The sound mockery of sheep is like the salubrious horse laugh ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guffaw - a loud and boisterous laugh
