isotopic

1. ADJ

/ ˌaɪsəˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving isotopes; having a relationship based on isotopes.

Examples

Because of the simplicity of the molecules, isotopic exchange reactions between elemental halogens and the corresponding carbon tetrahalides would appear to offer particularly fruitful possibilities for obtaining unambiguous basic kinetic data.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: isotope - a variant of a chemical element with the same number of protons but different number of neutrons

Etymology

From isotope + -ic, derived from Greek isos (equal) and topos (place).
