congratulations

1. NOUN

/ kənˌɡrætʃəˈleɪʃənz / (AmE), / kənˌɡratjʊˈleɪʃənz / (BrE)
An expression of praise and approval, especially on someone's achievement or special occasion.

Examples

Congratulations.

Synset Examples

he always appreciated praise for his work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: praise - the expression of approval or admiration
  • Synonym: commendation - formal praise or recommendation

Etymology

From Latin 'congratulationem' (felicitation), from 'congratulare' (to wish joy).
