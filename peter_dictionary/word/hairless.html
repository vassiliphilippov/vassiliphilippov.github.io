hairless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈheə.ləs / (BrE)
having no hair or fur on the body or a part of it

Examples

We classify mice as "straight haired "or "wavy haired ", but a hairless mouse appears.

Synset Examples

a Mexican Hairless is about the size of a fox terrier and hairless except for a tufts on the head and tail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bald - having no hair on the scalp or a body part
