teetotaler

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiˌtoʊtələr / (AmE), / ˈtiːtəʊtlə / (BrE)
a person who never drinks alcoholic beverages

Examples

Johnson's fat hand, another bottle were protruding from the truck cab, and that self proclaimed Baptist teetotaler, had a bottle at his own lips.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abstainer - a person who refrains from something, especially alcohol

Etymology

From 'teetotal', an emphatic form of 'total', popularized in the 19th century temperance movement.
