equipped

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈkwɪpt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpt / (BrE)
supplied with the necessary items, tools, or abilities for a particular purpose

Examples

In a series of fairy tales and fantasies, Melies demonstrated that the film is superbly equipped to tell a straightforward story, with beginning, middle and end, complications, resolutions, climaxes, and conclusions.

Synset Examples

a well equipped playground
a ship equipped with every mechanical aid to navigation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unequipped - not provided with necessary items or abilities

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'equip', from French 'équiper', meaning 'to fit out'.
