insured

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃɔːd / (BrE)
protected by insurance against loss, damage, or liability

Examples

Check to see if some of your benefits - such as on-the-job disability pay - can be put on a direct payment rather than an insured basis at a savings to you.

Synset Examples

an insured risk
all members of the film cast and crew are insured

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uninsured - not protected by insurance
  • Synonym: covered - protected against loss or risk, especially by insurance

Etymology

From 'insure' + -ed, meaning a person or thing that is insured.
