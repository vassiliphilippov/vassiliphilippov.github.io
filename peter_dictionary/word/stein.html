stein

1. NOUN

/ staɪn / (AmE), / staɪn / (BrE)
a large mug, typically made of earthenware, used for serving beer, often with a hinged lid

Examples

One day in a bar, so the legend goes, someone put a beer stein with too much force on the monocle and broke it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beer mug - a mug used for serving beer

Etymology

From German 'Stein' meaning 'stone' (referring to stoneware mugs).
