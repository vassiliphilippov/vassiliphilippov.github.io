bunter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌnɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌntə / (BrE)
A baseball player who specializes in bunting the ball.

Examples

With the speed and power of the body beautiful he saw before him, Ol' Case wanted No. 7 to be not only the best homerun hitter, but also the best bunter, base-runner and outfielder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bunt - to lightly hit a pitched baseball without swinging the bat

Etymology

From 'bunt' (to lightly hit a baseball without swinging), with agent suffix '-er'.
