self-effacing

1. ADJ

/ sɛlf ɪˈfeɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛlf ɪˈfeɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
not claiming attention for oneself; modest and unassuming

Examples

He was smooth and civil spoken but it seemed to me there was something tough under his self-effacing manner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: modest - not seeking attention or praise for oneself
  • Synonym: humble - having a low estimate of one's own importance
  • Antonym: self-promoting - acting to attract attention or praise to oneself

Etymology

From self- + efface (from French effacer ‘to erase’) meaning 'to erase oneself'.
