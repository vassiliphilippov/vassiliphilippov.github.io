intradepartmental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.trə.dɪˌpɑrt.mɛn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trə.dɪˌpɑːt.mɛn.tl̩ / (BrE)
Existing or occurring within a single department, especially within an organization or institution.

Examples

Moreover, the hearing officer's report is but intradepartmental, is directed to the Attorney General and, of course, is not the recommendation of the Department.

Synset Examples

intradepartmental memos

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interdepartmental - between departments

Etymology

From Latin 'intra-' (within) and English 'departmental'.
