watercolorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtəˌkʌlərɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkʌlərɪst / (BrE)
An artist who paints with watercolors.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: painter - a person who paints pictures
  • Antonym: oil painter - a painter who uses oil paints

Etymology

From 'watercolor' + '-ist', denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something.
