self-locking

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlfˈlɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌselfˈlɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a device or mechanism) locking automatically when closed, without requiring manual action.

Examples

Our pin-curl clips and self-locking nuts achieved dominance in just a few years time, despite substantial, well established competition.

Etymology

Formed from 'self-' (on one's own) + 'locking' (from 'lock').
