pompousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːmpəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɒmpəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being pompous; self-importance or excessive dignity or display.

Examples

With her sharp tongue she 'd have cut his pompousness to ribbons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ostentation - pretentious and showy display
  • Antonym: humility - modest view of one's own importance
