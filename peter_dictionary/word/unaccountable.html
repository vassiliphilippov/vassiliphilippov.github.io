unaccountable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈkaʊntəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈkaʊntəbl / (BrE)
Impossible to explain or understand; inexplicable.

Examples

Even then, as you go into the house oppressed by the knowledge that something is cooking and that your house has passed under this unaccountable, official control, could you go on forgetting that you still had that ridiculous hat on your head and you were still carrying that childish horn in your hand?

Synset Examples

perceptible only as unaccountable influences that hinder progress
an unexplainable fear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexplicable - unable to be explained or accounted for
  • Antonym: accountable - able to be explained or held responsible for

Etymology

From un- (not) + accountable (from account + -able).
