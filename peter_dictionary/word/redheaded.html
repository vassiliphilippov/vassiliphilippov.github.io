redheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having red hair, especially referring to a person.

Examples

Eddie Lee, Riverside's redheaded playing manager, ran after the ball but it rolled past him.
I think I'’ll try one of those... redheaded Yolandas.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: auburn-haired - having hair of a reddish-brown color
  • Synonym: ginger - having red or reddish hair (informal, especially British)

Etymology

Formed from 'red' + 'headed'.
