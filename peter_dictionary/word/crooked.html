crooked

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrʊkɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrʊkɪd / (BrE)
having bends, curves, or angles; not straight or aligned

Examples

A flash illumined the trees as a crooked bolt twigged in several directions.

Synset Examples

crooked country roads
crooked teeth

Related Terms

  • Antonym: straight - having no bends or angles; direct

Etymology

From Middle English 'crokked', from Old Norse 'krǫkóttr' meaning 'bent, hooked'.
