unattached

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈtætʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈtætʃt / (BrE)
Not fastened, joined, or connected to anything else.

Examples

Instead of inflecting a verb or using an unattached particle to indicate the past or future, Siddo used an entirely different word.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attached - fastened, joined, or connected to something

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'attached' (from Old French 'atachier', meaning 'to attach').
