mottled

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑːtəld / (AmE), / ˈmɒtəld / (BrE)
marked with spots or patches of different color or shade

Examples

The small intestine and colon contained approximately 300 cc. of foul-smelling, sanguineous material, and the mucosa throughout was hyperemic and mottled green brown.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dappled - marked with spots or rounded patches of color

Etymology

From Middle English 'motil', related to 'mot' (a speck or spot).
