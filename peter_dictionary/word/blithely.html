blithely

1. ADV

/ ˈblaɪð.li / (AmE), / ˈblaɪð.li / (BrE)
In a cheerful, carefree, or unconcerned manner.

Examples

"I try ", Felix said blithely.

Synset Examples

they shouted happily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happily - in a happy or joyous manner
  • Synonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful or lighthearted manner
  • Antonym: gloomily - in a sad or depressed manner

Etymology

From Old English *blīþe* (joyous, kind), with the adverbial suffix *-ly*.
