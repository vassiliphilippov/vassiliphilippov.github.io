diving

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
An athletic sport or competition in which individuals leap or fall into water, often performing acrobatic movements before entering.

Examples

He took a midnight train out of Cleveland Saturday, without an official word to anybody, and has stayed away from newsmen on his train trip across the nation to Reno, Nev., where his wife, former Olympic Diving Champion Zoe Ann Olsen, awaited.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimming - the sport or activity of moving through water by using the limbs
