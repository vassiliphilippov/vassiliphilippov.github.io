1. NUM

/ fɪfˈtin / (AmE), / fɪfˈtiːn / (BrE)
being one more than fourteen; the number 15.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fourteen - the number 14; one less than fifteen
  • Degree Higher: sixteen - the number 16; one more than fifteen

Etymology

From Old English fīftīene (fīf 'five' + tīene 'ten'), literally 'five and ten'.
