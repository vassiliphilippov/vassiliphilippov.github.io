retribution

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛtrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
punishment that is considered to be morally right and fully deserved

Examples

Very peculiar retribution indeed seems to overtake such jokers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punishment - the infliction or imposition of a penalty as retribution for an offense

Etymology

From Latin retributio (repayment, recompense), from retribuere (to pay back).
