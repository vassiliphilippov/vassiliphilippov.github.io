anteater

1. NOUN

/ ˈænˌtiːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈænˌtiːtə / (BrE)
A mammal found in southern Africa and Asia, covered with horny scales and having a long snout, that feeds on ants and termites; also known as a pangolin.

Examples

Did an anteater want ants?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pangolin - scaly anteater of Africa and Asia
  • Synonym: true anteater - South American mammal of family Myrmecophagidae
