repeat

1. VERB

/ rɪˈpit / (AmE), / rɪˈpiːt / (BrE)
to happen or occur again; to do, experience, or produce something again

Examples

Whether the compromises - on both sides - that made possible the interim appointment can then be repeated remains to be seen.

Synset Examples

This is a recurring story

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recur - to happen again, especially at regular intervals

Etymology

From Latin repetere, from re- (again) + petere (to seek, go to).
