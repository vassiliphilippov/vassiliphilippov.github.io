vacationing

1. NOUN

/ veɪˈkeɪʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of spending time away from home, especially for rest or recreation; the act of taking a vacation

Examples

The automobile expenses are about the only vacationing cost they can n't either eliminate or pare down drastically by camping along the way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: travelling - the act of going from one place to another, especially over a distance
  • Antonym: working - engaging in paid employment or activity as opposed to being on vacation
