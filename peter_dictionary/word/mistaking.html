mistaking

1. NOUN

/ mɪˈsteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / mɪˈsteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of incorrectly interpreting something; a misinterpretation.

Examples

There was no mistaking that scream.

Synset Examples

his misinterpretation of the question caused his error
there was no mistaking her meaning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misinterpretation - The act of interpreting something incorrectly
