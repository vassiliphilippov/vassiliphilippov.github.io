knee-deep

1. ADJ

/ ˈniː ˌdip / (AmE), / ˈniː ˌdiːp / (BrE)
reaching up to the knees; immersed or involved up to the knees, literally or figuratively.

Examples

The Death's-Head Corps took in Ukrainian and Baltic Auxiliaries, and the Einsatzkommandos waded knee-deep in blood and turned into drunken, dope ridden maniacs.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: ankle-deep - reaching up to the ankles
  • Degree Higher: waist-deep - reaching up to the waist
