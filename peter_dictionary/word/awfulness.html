awfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːfʊlnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being extremely unpleasant or dreadful.

Examples

Would not the emotional catharsis eventually brought on by this awfulness have a calming, if not exhausting, effect likely to improve his client's chances?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dreadfulness - the quality of being extremely bad or unpleasant
  • Antonym: wonderfulness - the quality of being extremely good or pleasing

Etymology

From awful + -ness, forming a noun meaning 'the state or quality of being awful.'
