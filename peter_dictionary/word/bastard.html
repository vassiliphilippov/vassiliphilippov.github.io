bastard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæstɚd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstəd / (BrE)
An insulting or offensive term for a person who is considered unpleasant, obnoxious, or contemptible.

Examples

In fact it has caused us to give serious thought to moving our residence south, because it is not easy for the most objective Southerner to sit calmly by when his host is telling a roomful of people that the only way to deal with Southerners who oppose integration is to send in troops and shoot the bastards down.
He had a mean, unbroken sheer bastard in his outfit, and someone invented the name Trig for him.
The colored boy had it, and Trig lunged at him with a knife and said,' Give that to me, you black bastard.
If the crummy bastard could write!
Turn the bastards over to me - to me and my boys - no nigger ever got what would be comin to them - reactionary bastards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: asshole - a rude or contemptible person (offensive term)
  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person (informal/insult)
  • Synonym: bastard - person born of unmarried parents (original, now less common meaning)

Etymology

From Old French bastard, probably from medieval Latin bastardus.
