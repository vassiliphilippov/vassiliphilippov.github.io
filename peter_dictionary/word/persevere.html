persevere

1. VERB

/ ˌpɝː.səˈvɪr / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.sɪˈvɪə / (BrE)
to continue trying to do or achieve something despite difficulties or discouragement

Examples

But Voltaire perseveres.

Synset Examples

he persisted to call me every night
The child persisted and kept asking questions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persist - to continue firmly in an action in spite of difficulty
  • Antonym: give up - to stop trying; to surrender or quit

Etymology

From Latin perseverare (to persist, continue steadfastly).
