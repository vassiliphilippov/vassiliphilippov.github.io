weekday

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːkˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈwiːk.deɪ / (BrE)
any day of the week except Sunday (and sometimes except Saturday), typically referring to the days when most people work or attend school.

Examples

Paintings and drawings by Marie Moore of St. Thomas, Virgin Islands, are shown thru Nov. 5 at the Meadows gallery, 3211 Ellis Ave., week days, 3 p.m. to 8 p.m., Sundays 3 p.m. to 6 p.m., closed Mondays.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weekend - Saturday and Sunday, or the period from Friday evening to Sunday night

Etymology

Formed from 'week' + 'day'.
