dramatics

1. NOUN

/ drəˈmætɪks / (AmE), / drəˈmætɪks / (BrE)
the activities of acting and producing plays, especially as an extracurricular activity in schools or colleges

Examples

I 'd have been more impressed if I had n't remembered that she 'd played Hedda Gabler in her highschool dramatics course.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drama - plays and the performance of plays; the art or study of acting in, writing, or producing plays

Etymology

From 'dramatic' + -ics, following the pattern of academic disciplines or activities.
