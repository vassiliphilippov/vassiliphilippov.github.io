cockeyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑkˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒkˌaɪd / (BrE)
Turned or twisted to one side; not straight or level.

Examples

"How come your bows is always cockeyed "?

Synset Examples

a...youth with a gorgeous red necktie all awry
his wig was, as the British say, skew-whiff

Related Terms

  • Synonym: askew - not in a straight or level position
  • Synonym: awry - away from the usual or expected course; out of position
