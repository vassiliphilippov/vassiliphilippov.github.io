nonlinguistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑn.lɪŋˈɡwɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.lɪŋˈɡwɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Not consisting of or related to language.

Examples

Tone languages use for linguistic contrasts speech parameters which also function heavily in nonlinguistic use.
The problem is to disentangle the linguistic features of pitch from the co-occurring nonlinguistic features.

Synset Examples

depended on his nonlinguistic skills

Related Terms

  • Antonym: linguistic - relating to language or linguistics

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'linguistic' (relating to language).
