shapeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃeɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪplɪs / (BrE)
having no definite or clear form or shape

Examples

The steeple leaned backward, while the church advanced like a headless creature in a long, shapeless coat.
From under the shapeless huddle of blanket the feet moved in the mud.
This, no doubt, is part of what Gilbert Seldes implies when he says of the arts, "They give form and meaning to life which might otherwise seem shapeless and without sense ".

Synset Examples

amorphous clouds of insects
an aggregate of formless particles
a shapeless mass of protoplasm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formless - having no regular shape or structure; without form
  • Synonym: amorphous - lacking a clear structure or shape
  • Antonym: shaped - having a specific or regular form

Etymology

From 'shape' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without shape'.
