/ ˈvæl.ju / (AmE), / ˈvæl.juː / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worth - the level at which someone or something deserves to be valued or rated
  • Synonym: utility - the state of being useful, profitable, or beneficial
  • Antonym: uselessness - the quality of having no practical use or benefit

Etymology

From Old French 'value' (worth), from valoir (to be worth), from Latin 'valere' (to be strong, be worth).
