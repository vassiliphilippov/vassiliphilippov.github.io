jackass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒækˌæs / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌas / (BrE)
A person (especially a man) who behaves in a very stupid or foolish way; an incompetent fool.

Examples

The Man from Mars had interrupted him with his work half finished - and that stupid jackass Short was certain to louse it up.
You too were a stupid jackass until you poisoned me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Antonym: genius - a person of exceptional intelligence or ability

Etymology

Originally a term for a male donkey (jack + ass), later applied figuratively to foolish people.
