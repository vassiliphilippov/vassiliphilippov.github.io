purposely

1. ADV

/ ˈpɝː.pəs.li / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəs.li / (BrE)
In a deliberate or intentional manner; on purpose.

Examples

You would be surprised how many fans purposely stayed away from Bears Stadium last year because of the television policy.
Only in one instance have we moved purposively and effectively to dislodge existing Communist power:

Synset Examples

he used that word intentionally
I did this by choice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intentionally - in a deliberate or intended way
  • Antonym: accidentally - in a way that is not planned or intended

Etymology

From the noun 'purpose' + suffix '-ly'.
