glassless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlæs.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːs.ləs / (BrE)
(of a window, door, or similar object) not fitted with glass.

Examples

But it was only Johnson reaching around the wire chicken fencing, which half covered the truck cab's glassless rear window.

Synset Examples

windows were unglazed to admit as much light and air as possible

Related Terms

  • Antonym: glazed - fitted with glass, especially in a window or door

Etymology

Formed from 'glass' + '-less' (meaning 'without').
