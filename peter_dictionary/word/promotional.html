promotional

1. ADJ

/ prəˈmoʊʃənəl / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃənəl / (BrE)
intended to advertise or publicize something, especially a product or service.

Examples

The American gun and ammunition producers sponsor a successful promotional program through their industry trade association.
Since SAAMI's conception in 1926, and more specifically since the adoption of the Shooting Development Program in 1954, millions of dollars and promotional man-hours have gone into the development of more places to shoot for more youths and adults.

Synset Examples

promotional fares

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising - the activity of producing advertisements for commercial products or services
  • Synonym: publicity - notice or attention given to someone or something by the media

Etymology

Formed from 'promotion' + '-al', based on 'promote' from Latin 'promovere' (to move forward).
