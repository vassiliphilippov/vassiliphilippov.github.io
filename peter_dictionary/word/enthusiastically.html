enthusiastically

1. ADV

/ ɪnˌθuziˈæstɪkli / (AmE), / ɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great excitement, eagerness, or interest.

Examples

The Church Unity Octave, January 18 - 25, was enthusiastically devoted to prayer and discussion by the various churches.
Enthusiastically, Americans have swept subliterary and bogus materials like Paul Bunyan tales, Abe Lincoln anecdotes and labor union songs up as true products of our American oral tradition.

Synset Examples

they discussed the question enthusiastically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eagerly - in a keen or enthusiastic manner

Etymology

From 'enthusiastic' + '-ally', ultimately from Greek 'enthousiazein' (to be inspired by a god).
