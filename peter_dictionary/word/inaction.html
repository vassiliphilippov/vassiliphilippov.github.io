inaction

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈækʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈækʃən / (BrE)
lack of action or activity; the state of doing nothing or not taking action

Examples

There will of course be times for delay and inaction.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: action - the process of doing something to achieve a result
  • Synonym: idleness - the state of being idle; doing nothing

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + action, from actio (doing).
