South Carolina

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ ˌkæɹəˈlaɪnə / (AmE), / saʊθ ˌkærəˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
A state in the southeastern United States, one of the original 13 colonies.

Examples

Second and eight for S.C... MICHAEL: Mr. Roth?

Etymology

Named after King Charles I of England; from Latin 'Carolus' meaning 'Charles'.
