swimsuit

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmˌsuːt / (AmE), / ˈswɪm.suːt / (BrE)
A piece of clothing designed to be worn for swimming or bathing, typically made from a stretchable, quick-drying fabric and fitting closely to the body.

Examples

I do n't mean to pry, but do they hide the swimsuit with the bubbles?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bathing suit - another term for a swimsuit
  • Synonym: trunks - shorts-style swimwear for men
  • Degree Lower: bikini - two-piece swimsuit for women
  • Degree Higher: wetsuit - protective suit for cold water swimming

Etymology

Formed from 'swim' + 'suit', indicating a suit used for swimming.
