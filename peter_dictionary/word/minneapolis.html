Minneapolis

1. PROPN

/ ˌmɪniˈæpəlɪs / (AmE), / ˌmɪniˈæpəlɪs / (BrE)
The largest city in Minnesota, situated in the southeastern part of the state on the Mississippi River; known for its historic flour mills and as one of the Twin Cities alongside St. Paul.
