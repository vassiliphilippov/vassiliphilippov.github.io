O.K.

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)

Etymology

Popularized in 19th-century America, possibly from a jocular misspelling of 'all correct' as 'oll korrect.'
