tabulation

1. NOUN

/ ˌtæbjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtabjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
Information presented in a table or in tabular form; the process or result of arranging data in rows and columns.

Examples

A tabulation of these features permits the lungs to be grouped into three distinctive subgross types.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table - an arrangement of data in rows and columns

Etymology

From Late Latin tabulatio (a table or listing), from Latin tabula (table).
