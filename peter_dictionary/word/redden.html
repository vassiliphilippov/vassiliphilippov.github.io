redden

1. VERB

/ ˈrɛdən / (AmE), / ˈrɛdən / (BrE)
(of the face or skin) to become red, especially from embarrassment, shame, or anger.

Examples

McBride reddened.

Synset Examples

The girl blushed when a young man whistled as she walked by

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blush - show embarrassment or shame by becoming red in the face

Etymology

From red + -en, a suffix forming verbs meaning 'to become' or 'to make'.
