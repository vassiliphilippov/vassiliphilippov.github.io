clinically

1. ADV

/ ˈklɪnɪkli / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the observation and treatment of patients; in a clinical manner.

Examples

In both conditions the emotional and perceptual sensitivity is diminished, but no depression occurs such as is seen clinically or may be produced in normal persons by drugs.

Synset Examples

she is clinically qualified

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medically - in a medical manner; related to medicine

Etymology

From clinical + -ly.
