planned

1. ADJ

/ plænd / (AmE), / plænd / (BrE)
Designed or arranged in advance according to a plan; prearranged rather than spontaneous.

They then point out that with our present lack of knowledge of all the factors concerned in the rise and fall of epidemics, it is unlikely that a planned episode could be initiated.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unplanned - not planned; occurring without preparation or forethought
  • Synonym: premeditated - planned in advance, especially of a crime or act
