handlebar

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændəlˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈhændlˌbɑː / (BrE)
the curved or straight bar at the front of a bicycle, motorcycle, or similar vehicle, used for steering

Examples

With leather cups fitted in his handlebars, he steered his bicycle.

Etymology

From 'handle' + 'bar', indicating a bar used for handling or steering.
