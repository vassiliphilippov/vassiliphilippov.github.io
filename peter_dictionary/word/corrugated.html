corrugated

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔrəˌɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəˌɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a surface shaped into alternating parallel ridges and grooves, typically to strengthen the material

Examples

Pre shaped corrugated metal sections or pre-cast concrete can be used for shelters either above or below ground.

Synset Examples

the surface of the ocean was rippled and corrugated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ribbed - having a pattern or structure of raised lines or ridges

Etymology

From Latin corrugatus, past participle of corrugare (‘to wrinkle’)
