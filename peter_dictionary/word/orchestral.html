orchestral

1. ADJ

/ ɔːrˈkɛstrəl / (AmE), / ɔːˈkɛstrəl / (BrE)
Relating to or composed for an orchestra.

Examples

The concert opened with another big romantic score, Schumann's Overture to Manfred, which suffered fate, this time with orchestral thrusts to the Byronic point to keep it afloat.

Synset Examples

orchestral score

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchestra - a group of musicians playing together on various instruments

Etymology

From orchestra + -al, formed in English to relate to orchestras.
