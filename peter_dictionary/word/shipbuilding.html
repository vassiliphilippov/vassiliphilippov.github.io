shipbuilding

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪpˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or industry of designing and constructing ships.

Examples

Expenditures for shipbuilding are estimated at about the same level as in 1960.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shipyard - a place where ships are built or repaired

Etymology

From 'ship' + 'building', first recorded in English in the 15th century.
