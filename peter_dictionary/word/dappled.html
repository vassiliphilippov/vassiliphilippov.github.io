dappled

1. ADJ

/ ˈdæpəld / (AmE), / ˈdæpəld / (BrE)
marked with spots or patches of a different color or shade.

Examples

The air was cooler here, and the lacy pattern of the trees threw a dappled shadow on the grass, an effect which he found pleasant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - having spots
  • Synonym: mottled - marked with streaks or spots of different colors or shades

Etymology

From the Middle English 'dapple' (spot, mottled mark), of uncertain origin.
