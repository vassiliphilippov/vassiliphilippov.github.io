undisguised

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪsˈɡaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪsˈɡaɪzd / (BrE)
(of feelings, intentions, etc.) not concealed; openly obvious or apparent.

Examples

He looked at Gilborn with undisguised curiosity.

Synset Examples

undisguised curiosity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear, self-evident
  • Synonym: apparent - clearly visible or understood; obvious
  • Antonym: hidden - kept out of sight; concealed
  • Antonym: disguised - having its true nature concealed

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + disguised (from Old French desguiser, to change appearance).
