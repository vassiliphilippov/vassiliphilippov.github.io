undamaged

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈdæmɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdamɪdʒd / (BrE)
Not harmed or spoiled; in a sound or intact condition.

Examples

First of all, what is their evidence that the tactual apparatus was fundamentally undamaged?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: damaged - harmed or spoiled

Etymology

From un- (not) + damaged.
