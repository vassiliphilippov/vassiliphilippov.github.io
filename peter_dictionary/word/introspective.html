introspective

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪv / (BrE)
Characterized by or given to examining one's own thoughts, feelings, and sensations.

Examples

Pensive, introspective, he ached.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reflective - characterized by deep thought or contemplation
  • Antonym: outgoing - sociable and interested in external activities, the opposite of introspective

Etymology

From Latin introspectus, past participle of introspicere ('to look inside').
