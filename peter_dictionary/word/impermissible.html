impermissible

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪmpɚˈmɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈmɪsəbl̩ / (BrE)
Not allowed or permitted; not acceptable according to rules or law.

Examples

It's out of the question.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: permissible - allowed or permitted
  • Synonym: forbidden - not allowed; expressly prohibited
  • Synonym: inadmissible - not allowed, especially in a legal context

Etymology

Formed from 'im-' (not) + 'permissible' (able to be permitted).
