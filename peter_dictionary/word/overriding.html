overriding

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
More important than anything else; having the greatest importance or influence.

Examples

This may be of overriding importance in considering military objectives.

Synset Examples

the predominant mood among policy-makers is optimism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: predominant - having superior power and influence
  • Antonym: secondary - of lesser importance or influence

Etymology

From 'override' (verb) + -ing; 'override' from Old English 'oferrīdan' (to ride over).
