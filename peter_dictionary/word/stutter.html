stutter

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈstʌtə / (BrE)
A speech disorder characterized by frequent involuntary pauses and repetitions of sounds, syllables, or words.

Examples

[STUTTERS]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stammer - speech disorder involving involuntary pauses and repetitions

Etymology

From Middle English *stutten*, possibly imitative in origin.
