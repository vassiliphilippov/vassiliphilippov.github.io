custodial

1. ADJ

/ kʌˈstoʊdiəl / (AmE), / kʌˈstəʊdiəl / (BrE)
Relating to the providing of protective supervision, care, or guardianship, especially for children, vulnerable people, or property.

Examples

Are there possibilities of having cafeteria help work part-time on custodial or other jobs?

Synset Examples

daycare that is educational and not just custodial
a guardian angel
tutelary gods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guardianship - the position or state of being a guardian; care and protection of someone

Etymology

From 'custody' + '-al', ultimately from Latin 'custodia' (guardianship, keeping).
