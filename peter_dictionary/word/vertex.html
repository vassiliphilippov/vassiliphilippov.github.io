vertex

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɜrtɛks / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɛks / (BrE)
The point where two or more lines meet, especially the corner of a geometric shape such as a triangle or polygon, or the point opposite the base in such a figure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: base - the bottom side of a geometric figure, opposite the vertex

Etymology
