omelet

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːmlət / (AmE), / ˈɒmlət / (BrE)
A dish made of beaten eggs cooked until just set, often folded around fillings such as ham, cheese, or vegetables.

Examples

But... ...you can't make an omelet without breaking eggs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frittata - Italian egg dish similar to an omelet but not folded
  • Degree Lower: scrambled eggs - eggs beaten and cooked while stirring to a soft consistency

Etymology

From French *omelette*, altered from earlier *alemette*, of uncertain origin.
