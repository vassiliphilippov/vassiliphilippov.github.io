lengthwise

1. ADV

/ ˈlɛŋkθˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlɛŋθwʌɪz / (BrE)
In the direction of the length; along the longest dimension of something.

Examples

The resulting magnetic effect proved stronger than when the discharge was made lengthwise.

Synset Examples

He cut the paper lengthwise

Related Terms

  • Antonym: crosswise - in the direction across the width; at right angles to the length

Etymology

Formed from 'length' + 'wise' (meaning 'in the manner or direction of').
