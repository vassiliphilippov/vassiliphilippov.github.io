Weltanschauung

1. NOUN

/ vɛltˈɑnʃaʊʊŋ / (AmE), / vɛltˈanʃaʊʊŋ / (BrE)
A comprehensive worldview or philosophy of life; an overall conception of the world and humanity's place in it.

Examples

He is a Buddhist, which means that to him peace and the sanctity of human life are not only religious dogma, but a profound and unshakable Weltanschauung.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldview - a particular philosophy or view of life; the world as interpreted by an individual or group

Etymology

From German 'Weltanschauung', literally 'world view' ('Welt' = world, 'Anschauung' = view).
