animatedly

1. ADV

/ ˈæn.ɪ.meɪ.tɪd.li / (AmE), / ˈæn.ɪ.meɪ.tɪd.li / (BrE)
In a lively, expressive, or energetic manner.

Examples

[PEOPLE CHATTERING ANIMATEDLY] Ten-to-one. [CHUCKLES]

Synset Examples

they talked animatedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lively - active, energetic, or animated in manner

Etymology

From 'animated' + '-ly'. 'Animated' from Latin 'animatus' (filled with breath or spirit).
