residual

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈzɪdʒuəl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪdjuːəl / (BrE)
Remaining after the main part has been taken, used, or dealt with; relating to the remainder.

Examples

In the eyepiece a Wratten 2 B filter was used to filter off residual ultra-violet light.

Synset Examples

residual quantity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remaining - still left after others have been removed or used
  • Synonym: residue - the part that remains after the main part has been removed

Etymology

From Latin residuus (remaining), from residēre (to remain).
