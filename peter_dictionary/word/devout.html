devout

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈvaʊt / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊt / (BrE)
Having or showing deep religious feeling or commitment.

Examples

Devout, orthodox and plain like a family she might meet in Brooklyn or Malta or Ireland.

Synset Examples

a god-fearing and law-abiding people

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pious - having or showing religious devotion, especially in a hypocritical way
  • Antonym: irreligious - lacking religious beliefs or practices
  • Synonym: religious - having or showing belief in and reverence for a deity

Etymology

From Old French *devot*, from Latin *devotus* (meaning 'devoted, pious'), past participle of *devovēre* ('to vow, dedicate').
