grandchild

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræn(d)ˌtʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˈɡræn(d)tʃaɪld / (BrE)
a child of one's son or daughter

Examples

she was too indulgent a lover of dogs and grandchildren.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grandparent - the parent of one's father or mother
  • Degree Higher: great-grandchild - a child of one's grandchild
  • Degree Lower: child - a son or daughter

Etymology

From 'grand-' (denoting one generation removed) + 'child'.
