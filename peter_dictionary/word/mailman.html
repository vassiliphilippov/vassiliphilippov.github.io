mailman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlˌman / (BrE)
A man whose job is to deliver mail to people's homes and businesses.

Examples

It was mostly for the benefit of the mailman, because hardly anybody else ever visited us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mail carrier - person who delivers the mail
  • Synonym: postman - person, usually male, who delivers the mail (British usage)

Etymology

From 'mail' (letters and parcels sent by post) + 'man'.
