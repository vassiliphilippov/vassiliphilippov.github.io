poker-faced

1. ADJ

/ ˈpoʊkərˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkəˌfeɪst / (BrE)
Having a deliberately expressionless or emotionless face; showing no visible reaction.

Examples

He had the same bullet head of curly reddish hair but he did n't have Jim's poker-faced humor or his brains or his charm.

Synset Examples

deadpan humor
his face remained expressionless as the verdict was read

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadpan - Deliberately impassive or expressionless in manner
  • Synonym: expressionless - Showing no expression or emotion
  • Antonym: emotive - Showing or able to show emotion

Etymology

From 'poker', the card game, where a blank face is used to avoid revealing emotions.
