blackened

1. ADJ

/ ˈblækənd / (AmE), / ˈblækənd / (BrE)
(of a surface or object) darkened or sooted by exposure to smoke or fire.

Examples

What had been the ambassador's suite was now jagged walls of blackened brick.

Synset Examples

blackened rafters

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charred - burned and blackened on the surface
