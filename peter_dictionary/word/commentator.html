commentator

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑm.ənˌteɪ.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ənˌteɪ.tə / (BrE)
A person who reports on events or explains a subject, providing analysis and opinions, especially as a specialist in news, sports, or current affairs.

Examples

The materials for compromise are at hand: The Nation, Walter Lippmann and other sober commentators (see Alan Clark on p. 367) have spelled them out again and again.
Even so astute a commentator as Harold Clurman of The Nation has said that "Waiting for Godot "is "the concentrate of the contemporary European mood of despair ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analyst - a person who analyzes or gives opinions on a subject
  • Synonym: reporter - a person who reports news or events, often without detailed commentary

Etymology

From Latin commentātor (one who annotates or explains).
