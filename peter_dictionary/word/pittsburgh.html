Pittsburgh

1. PROPN

/ ˈpɪtsbɝːɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtsbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A large city in southwestern Pennsylvania at the confluence of the Allegheny and Monongahela rivers, known historically as an industrial center and home to several universities.
