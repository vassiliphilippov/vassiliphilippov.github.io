yearningly

1. ADV

/ ˈjɝnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈjɜːnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner showing strong desire or longing.

Examples

She studied him hopefully, yearningly; against the limitless background of sky and wasteland it was easy to confirm her analysis.

Synset Examples

he spent the rest of the act gazing longingly over my right shoulder at the illuminated word `Exit'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: longingly - in a manner showing longing or desire

Etymology

From "yearning" + "-ly"; "yearning" from Old English *giernan* (to desire).
