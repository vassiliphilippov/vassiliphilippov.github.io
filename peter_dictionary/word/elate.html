elate

1. VERB

/ ɪˈleɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈleɪt / (BrE)
to fill (someone) with high spirits or joy; to make very happy or proud

Examples

Hino was elated at the prospect.

Synset Examples

Music can uplift your spirits

Related Terms

  • Antonym: depress - to make (someone) feel sad or dispirited

Etymology

From Latin 'elatus', past participle of 'efferre' (to lift up)
