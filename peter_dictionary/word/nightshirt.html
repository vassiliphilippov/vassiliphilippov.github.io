nightshirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
A long loose shirt worn in bed, traditionally by men, as nightwear.

Examples

They curled around the cuff of his nightshirt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightgown - a loose dress worn in bed, especially by women
  • Synonym: pajamas - a loose-fitting jacket and trousers for sleeping in

Etymology

From 'night' + 'shirt', indicating a shirt worn at night.
