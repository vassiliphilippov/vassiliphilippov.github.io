packaging

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæk.ɪdʒ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpæk.ɪdʒ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or process of packing goods for transport, storage, or sale

Examples

The change may require different products, pricing, packaging, warehousing, salesmanship, advertising and executive attention - practically every link in the marketing network may have to be adjusted.

Synset Examples

his business is packaging for transport

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packing - the process of putting things into containers or packages

Etymology

From 'package' + '-ing'; 'package' likely from Middle French 'paquet'.
