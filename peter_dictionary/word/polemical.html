polemical

1. ADJ

/ pəˈlɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / pəˈlɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving strongly critical or contentious debate or argument

Examples

The first step toward the goal is the establishment of a new atmosphere of mutual good will and friendly communication on other than the polemical level.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: controversial - giving rise or likely to give rise to controversy or public disagreement
  • Antonym: conciliatory - intended or likely to pacify or placate

Etymology

From Greek *polemikos* (warlike, hostile) via French *polémique*.
