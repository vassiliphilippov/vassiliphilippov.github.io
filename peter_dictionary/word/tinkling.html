tinkling

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
making or sounding like light, high, ringing sounds, especially like that of a small bell.

Examples

[GUN FIRING] [GUNFIRE STOPS] [SHATTERED GLASS TINKLING] [MICHAEL PANTING] Kay, are you all right?

Synset Examples

sounding brass and a tinkling cymbal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clinking - making a light, sharp ringing sound, like glass or metal objects striking
  • Synonym: jingling - making a light metallic ringing sound, like small bells

Etymology

Present participle of 'tinkle', from Middle English *tinklen* (to make a light clinking sound).
