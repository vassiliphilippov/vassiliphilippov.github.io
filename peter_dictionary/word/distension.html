distension

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
The state or process of being swollen or expanded by pressure from within.

Examples

On July 4, 1959, the patient developed marked abdominal pain and distension, went into shock, and died.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swelling - an abnormal enlargement of a body part or area

Etymology

From Latin distensio, from distendere (to stretch apart).
