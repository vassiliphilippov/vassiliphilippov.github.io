judgement

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (BrE)
the ability to assess situations or circumstances and make sensible and sound decisions

Examples

Do you have faith in my judgement?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discernment - the ability to judge well
  • Synonym: wisdom - the quality of having experience, knowledge, and good judgment
  • Antonym: foolishness - lack of good sense or judgment

Etymology

From Old French *jugement*, from Latin *iūdicium* (judgment, decision).
