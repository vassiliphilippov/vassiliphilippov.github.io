advantageous

1. ADJ

/ ˌæd.vænˈteɪ.dʒəs / (AmE), / ˌæd.vənˈteɪ.dʒəs / (BrE)
helpful or useful in making someone or something more likely to succeed; giving an advantage.

Examples

For the occasion on which everyone already knows everyone else and the host wishes them to meet one or a few honored newcomers, then the "open house "system is advantageous because the honored guests are fixed connective points and the drifting guests make and break connections at the door.

Synset Examples

a contract advantageous to our country
socially advantageous to entertain often

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beneficial - producing good or helpful results; having a helpful effect
  • Antonym: disadvantageous - causing difficulty or harm; not giving an advantage

Etymology

From Old French avantageux, from avantage (‘advantage’).
