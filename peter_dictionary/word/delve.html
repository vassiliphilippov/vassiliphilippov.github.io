delve

1. VERB

/ dɛlv / (AmE), / dɛlv / (BrE)
To dig or turn over soil, especially with a spade or other tool.

Examples

Congratulations to N. C. for successfully delving into the heart of the problems that face the Peace Corps.

Synset Examples

Dig we must
turn over the soil for aeration

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dig - To break up and move earth with a tool or hands

Etymology

From Old English *delfan* (to dig).
