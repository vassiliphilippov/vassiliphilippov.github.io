addressed

1. ADJ

/ əˈdrɛst / (AmE), / əˈdrɛst / (BrE)
(of a letter or package) marked with the name and address of the intended recipient.

Examples

Juanita drooped about the place, wearing a haunted, brooding look, which Kate attributed to the baby's death, until the day a letter came for her addressed to "Miss Juanita Fitzroy ", bearing a Grafton postmark.

Synset Examples

I throw away all mail addressed to `resident'

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unaddressed - not marked with a recipient's address (for mail)
