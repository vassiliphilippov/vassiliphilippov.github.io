incompatible

1. ADJ

/ ɪn.kəmˈpæt.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ɪn.kəmˈpæt.ɪ.bəl / (BrE)
(of two or more things) so different in nature as to be incapable of coexisting or being combined without conflict.

Examples

A further example of the incompatible difference in personalities was when two policemen held up a Torrio beer convoy on a West Side street and demanded $ 300 to let it through.
Who will deny that in a vast portion of the South the Federal action is incompatible with the Jeffersonian concept of "the consent of the governed "?

Synset Examples

incompatible personalities
incompatible colors

Related Terms

  • Antonym: compatible - able to exist or work together without conflict

Etymology

From Medieval Latin incompatibilis, from Latin in- (not) + compatibilis (compatible).
