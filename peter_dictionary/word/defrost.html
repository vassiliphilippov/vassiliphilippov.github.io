defrost

1. VERB

/ dɪˈfrɔst / (AmE), / dɪˈfrɒst / (BrE)
to remove frost or ice from something, or to become free of frost or ice, especially by warming

Examples

Finding the key under his shoe, he started the engine, and while it warmed up he turned to her standing there in the dripping fog, and said, "Defrost the refrigerator ".

Synset Examples

Defrost the car window

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thaw - to become unfrozen or to make something become unfrozen

Etymology

from de- (remove) + frost
