Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ability - possession of the means or skill to do something
  • Synonym: potential - latent qualities or abilities that may be developed
  • Antonym: incapacity - lack of ability or power

Etymology

From Latin capacitas, from capax (able to hold, capable).
