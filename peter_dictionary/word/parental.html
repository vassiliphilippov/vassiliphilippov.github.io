parental

1. ADJ

/ pəˈrɛntəl / (AmE), / pəˈrɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to parents or the parental generation, especially as the source of offspring in genetics and biology.

Examples

In child care, the opposite extreme prevails; procedures change rapidly and parental confidence probably exceeds anything warranted by established psychological theory.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: filial - relating to or due from a son or daughter; pertaining to offspring

Etymology

From Latin 'parentalis' (of a parent), from 'parens' (parent).
