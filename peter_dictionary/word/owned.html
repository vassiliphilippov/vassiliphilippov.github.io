owned

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊnd / (AmE), / ˈəʊnd / (BrE)
Having an owner; used especially in combination to indicate possession or control by a person, group, or entity.

Examples

Legislation has already been proposed to authorize the sale of these Government owned systems in Alaska, and its early enactment is desirable.

Synset Examples

state-owned railways

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unowned - not having an owner
