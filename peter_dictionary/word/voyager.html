voyager

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɔɪ.ɪ.dʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪ.ɪ.dʒə / (BrE)
a person who travels to distant places, especially by sea

Examples

In 1610, Hudson was probably in his early forties, a good navigator, a stubborn voyager, but otherwise fatally unsuited to his chosen profession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: traveler - a person who travels
  • Synonym: explorer - a person who explores unknown regions

Etymology

From Old French voyageur (traveler), from voyager (to travel).
