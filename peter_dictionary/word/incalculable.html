incalculable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkælkjʊləbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈkælkjʊləbəl / (BrE)
Impossible to calculate or estimate; too great or numerous to be measured with accuracy.

Examples

The movie was The Great Train Robbery and its effects on the young industry and art were all but incalculable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immeasurable - too large or extreme to be measured
  • Antonym: calculable - able to be calculated or estimated

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + calculabilis (calculable), from calculus (small stone used for counting).
