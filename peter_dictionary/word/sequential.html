sequential

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈkwɛnʃəl / (AmE), / sɪˈkwɛnʃəl / (BrE)
Occurring in a regular order without interruption or gaps.

Examples

This is the same system as was used in the field sequential color TV system which preceded the present simultaneous system.

Synset Examples

serial concerts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consecutive - following one after another without interruption

Etymology

From Latin 'sequentia' (sequence) via Medieval Latin 'sequentiālis'.
