virginity

1. NOUN

/ vərˈdʒɪn.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪn.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The state of never having had sexual intercourse.

Examples

For a moment she thought of answering with the truth but she knew there were men who shied away from virginity, who demanded some degree of education in body as well as mind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chastity - the state of refraining from sexual activity considered immoral or outside marriage; sexual purity

Etymology

From Old French *virginité*, from Latin *virginitas* (maidenhood).
