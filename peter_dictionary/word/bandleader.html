bandleader

1. NOUN

/ ˈbændˌliːdɚ / (AmE), / ˈbændˌliːdə / (BrE)
the leader of a musical band, especially a dance band

Examples

If that goombah tries any rough stuff, tell him I'm no bandleader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conductor - the person who directs the performance of an orchestra or choir
  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument
