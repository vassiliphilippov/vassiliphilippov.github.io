ant

1. NOUN

/ ænt / (AmE), / ænt / (BrE)
A small social insect that lives in highly organized colonies, typically having a wingless worker caste and, during breeding seasons, winged males and a fertile queen.

Examples

Ants carry away the seeds so better be sure that there are no ant hills nearby.
