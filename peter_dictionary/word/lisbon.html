Lisbon

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪzbən / (AmE), / ˈlɪzbən / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Portugal, located on the Tagus River; a major port and cultural center in western Portugal.

Examples

One of the most damaging tsunami on record followed the famous Lisbon earthquake of November 1, 1755; its waves persisted for a week and were felt as far away as the English coast.

Etymology

From Portuguese Lisboa, itself from Latin Olisipo, of pre-Roman origin.
