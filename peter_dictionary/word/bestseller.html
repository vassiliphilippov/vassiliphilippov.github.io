bestseller

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛstˈsɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˌbɛstˈsɛlə / (BrE)
A book or other publication that sells in very large numbers, often over a short period of time.

Examples

A month ago he bought the movie rights to this book, a best-seller.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockbuster - an extremely popular or successful book, film, or other product

Etymology

From 'best' + 'seller', indicating something that sells best.
