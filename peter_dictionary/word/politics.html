politics

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑː.lɪ.tɪks / (AmE), / ˈpɒl.ɪ.tɪks / (BrE)
The activities or affairs concerned with gaining and using power in a country or organization, often involving competition or intrigue.

Examples

Synset Examples

office politics is often counterproductive

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government - the group of people with the authority to govern a country or state
  • Synonym: diplomacy - the practice of conducting negotiations between countries
  • Degree Lower: business - commercial or mercantile activity engaged in as a means of livelihood

Etymology

From Greek politikē (art of government), from politēs (citizen) and polis (city).
