stemmed

1. ADJ

/ stɛmd / (AmE), / stɛmd / (BrE)
having a stem or stems, or having a stem of a specified kind (often used in combination)

Examples

A refuse bin at the Dumont turned up a florist's box - a very long box for very long stemmed flowers.

Synset Examples

stemmed goblets
long-stemmed roses
