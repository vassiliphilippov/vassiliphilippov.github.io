inflated

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈfleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of ideas, statements, numbers, etc.) exaggerated or enlarged beyond what is reasonable or true.

Examples

In most discussions of this phenomenon, the figures are substantially inflated.

Synset Examples

a hyperbolic style

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exaggerated - made to seem greater than it actually is
  • Synonym: overstated - expressed in stronger terms than the truth
  • Antonym: understated - presented as smaller or less important than it actually is
