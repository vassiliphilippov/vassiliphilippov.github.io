uppercut

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌpɚˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌpəkʌt / (BrE)
A punch delivered with an upward motion, typically aimed at an opponent’s chin, especially in boxing.

Examples

Then, as he doubled, gasping, vomiting the breakfast he had so lately eaten, Lord straightened him with an uppercut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jab - a quick, straight punch

Etymology

Formed from 'upper' + 'cut', referring to the upward direction of the blow.
