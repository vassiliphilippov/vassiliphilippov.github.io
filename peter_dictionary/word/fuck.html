fuck

1. NOUN

/ fʌk / (AmE), / fʌk / (BrE)
(vulgar slang) Sexual intercourse.

Examples

What the fuck have you done?

Etymology

Probably from Dutch *fokken* (to breed, to copulate) or German *ficken* (to have sexual intercourse).

2. VERB

/ fʌk / (AmE), / fʌk / (BrE)
To have sexual intercourse with someone. (Vulgar slang)

Examples

You'll think you've been fucked by a train.

Synset Examples

This student sleeps with everyone in her dorm
Adam knew Eve
Were you ever intimate with this man?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleep with - to have sexual intercourse with
  • Synonym: make love to - to have sexual intercourse with (more polite)
  • Antonym: abstain - to deliberately refrain from sexual intercourse

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from Low German *fokken* (to strike, to copulate).
