pubescent

1. ADJ

/ pjuˈbɛsənt / (AmE), / pjuːˈbesənt / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having reached the stage of puberty but not yet fully mature.

Examples

Synset Examples

the budding breasts of a pubescent girl and the downy chin of pubescent boy

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: prepubescent - not yet having reached puberty
  • Degree Higher: postpubescent - having passed through puberty

Etymology

From Latin 'pubescentem', present participle of 'pubescere' (to reach puberty).
