believably

1. ADV

/ bəˈlivəbli / (AmE), / bɪˈliːvəbli / (BrE)
In a believable manner; in such a way as to be credible or convincing.

Examples

Stowey Rummel was internationally famous, a crafter of a genuine Americana in foreign eyes, an original designer whose inventive childishness with steel and concrete was made even more believably sincere by his personality.

Synset Examples

he acted believably sincere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credibly - in a credible manner
  • Antonym: unbelievably - in an unbelievable manner

Etymology

Formed from 'believable' + '-ly'.
