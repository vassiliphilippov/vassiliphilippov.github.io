torrent

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɔr.ənt / (AmE), / ˈtɒr.ənt / (BrE)
A sudden, heavy outpouring of rain.

Examples

And with what resource did Prokofieff back up his Credo of words - with torrents of powerful music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downpour - a heavy fall of rain
  • Degree Lower: drizzle - light rain falling steadily

Etymology

From Latin *torrentem* (rushing, roaring), present participle of *torrere* (to burn, parch).
