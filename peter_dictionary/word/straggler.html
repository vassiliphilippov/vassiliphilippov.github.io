straggler

1. NOUN

/ ˈstræɡlər / (AmE), / ˈstræɡlə / (BrE)
a person or animal that falls behind or becomes separated from a group, especially while moving

Examples

He had formed his own task force of three stragglers and led them up the hill in a Fighting Seventh charge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laggard - a person who makes slow progress and falls behind others
  • Antonym: leader - a person who leads or is ahead of others in a group

Etymology

From 'straggle' (to wander from the direct course), likely of Scandinavian origin.
