unselfconscious

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɑnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌsɛlfˈkɒnʃəs / (BrE)
Not embarrassed, nervous, or worried about what other people think; acting naturally and without self-awareness.

Examples

Jessica Packard lifted her head and followed the retreating figure, her eyes resting nearly closed on the unself-conscious rise and fall of the rounded hips.

Synset Examples

she grew up with him in unselfconscious friendship

Related Terms

  • Antonym: self-conscious - aware of and embarrassed by oneself; awkward in social situations

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'self-conscious'.
