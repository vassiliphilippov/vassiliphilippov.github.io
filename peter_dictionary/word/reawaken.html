reawaken

1. VERB

/ ˌriːəˈweɪkən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈweɪkən / (BrE)
To awaken or become conscious again, either literally or figuratively; to make (someone or something) feel or experience something again.

Examples

The slight flutter that had disturbed the motion of her heart when she entered the forest was gone now, and even the dim groves of trees through which she occasionally passed did not reawaken her fear.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: awaken - to wake up, or make someone wake up

Etymology

From re- (again) + awaken.
