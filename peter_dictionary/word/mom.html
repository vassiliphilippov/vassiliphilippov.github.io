mom

1. NOUN

/ mɑm / (AmE), / mɒm / (BrE)
(North American) an informal term for mother.

Examples

I'll square it with Mom, that you're not seeing her before you leave and...

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mother - a woman in relation to her child or children
  • Synonym: mum - informal British term for mother

Etymology

Reduction of 'mamma' or 'mummy', from childish speech; 'mom' is chiefly North American.
