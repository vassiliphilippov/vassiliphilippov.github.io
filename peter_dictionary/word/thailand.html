Thailand

1. PROPN

/ ˈtaɪ.lænd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
A country in Southeast Asia, located on the Indochinese Peninsula, known for its tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, and rich cultural heritage.

Examples

They may even enroll a colored student or two for show, though he usually turns out to be from Thailand, or any place other than the American South.

Synset Examples

Thailand is the official name of the former Siam

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Siam - the former official name of Thailand

Etymology

Formerly known as Siam; from Thai (the name of the majority ethnic group) + land.
