declared

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈklɛrd / (AmE), / dɪˈkleəd / (BrE)
made known formally or stated openly and publicly; openly avowed.

Examples

It would be the most severe reprisal, short of declared war, that the United States could invoke against Castro.

Synset Examples

their declared and their covert objectives
a declared liberal

Related Terms

  • Antonym: covert - not openly acknowledged or displayed; hidden or secret

Etymology

From the verb 'declare', which comes from Latin 'declarare' (to make clear).
