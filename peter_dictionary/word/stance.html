stance

1. NOUN

/ stæns / (AmE), / stɑːns / (BrE)
the way in which someone stands; a person's physical posture, especially in preparation for an action or sport

Examples

Then he played nine imaginary holes of golf, choosing his handicap, his irons, his stance, his opponents, and his weather in detail, but the green of the links seemed faded in the light of his business worries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: posture - the position in which someone holds their body when sitting or standing

Etymology

From Italian *stanza* (standing, position), from Latin *stantia*, from *stare* (to stand).
