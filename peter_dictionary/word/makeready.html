makeready

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪkˌrɛdi / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkˌrɛdi / (BrE)
The final preparation and adjustments made before a process, especially in printing or manufacturing, begins.

Examples

As a result, life had become a kind of continuous make-ready.

Etymology

From 'make' + 'ready', meaning to prepare for use.
