dominate

1. VERB

/ ˈdɑːməˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to have control or power over someone or something; to be the strongest or most important influence

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: control - to exercise authoritative or dominating influence
  • Synonym: subdue - to bring under control, especially by force
  • Antonym: submit - to yield to the authority or control of another

Etymology

From Latin dominari (to rule, control), from dominus (master).
