depict

1. VERB

/ dɪˈpɪkt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪkt / (BrE)
to show or represent something in a picture, painting, or other form of visual art; to describe vividly in words.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illustrate - to explain or decorate by providing pictures or examples
  • Synonym: portray - to depict or represent in a work of art or literature

Etymology

From Latin depictus, past participle of depingere (to paint, portray).

3. VERB

to represent or show someone by drawing, painting, or photographing them; to make a portrait of someone

Examples
