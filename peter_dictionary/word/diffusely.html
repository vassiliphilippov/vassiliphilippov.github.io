diffusely

1. ADV

/ dɪˈfjuzli / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːsli / (BrE)
In a way that is spread out over a wide area or not concentrated; in a diffuse manner.

Examples

The coronary arteries were sclerotic and diffusely narrowed throughout their courses, and the right coronary artery was virtually occluded by a yellow atheromatous plaque 1.5 cm. distal to its origin.

Synset Examples

the arteries were diffusely narrowed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: concentratedly - in a concentrated or focused manner

Etymology

From 'diffuse' + '-ly'; ultimately from Latin 'diffundere' (to pour out).
