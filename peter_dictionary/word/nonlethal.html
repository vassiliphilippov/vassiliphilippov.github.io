nonlethal

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈliθəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈliːθəl / (BrE)
Not capable of causing death; not deadly or fatal.

Examples

Well, if it was a toothbrush, I'd just quote a price, but then a toothbrush is a non-lethal object.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lethal - capable of causing death; deadly

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not' and 'lethal', from Latin 'letalis' meaning 'deadly'.
