trestle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈtrɛsəl / (BrE)
A horizontal beam or framework supported by two or more legs, used as a support for a table, bridge, or other structure.

Examples

Sangallo studied the drawings and improvised a trestle couch.

Etymology

From Old French 'trestel', from Latin 'transtellum' (little crossbeam).
