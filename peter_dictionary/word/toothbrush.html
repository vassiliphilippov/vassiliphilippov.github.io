toothbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːθˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ.brʌʃ / (BrE)
A small brush with a long handle used for cleaning the teeth.

Examples

It is not as convenient as the old type toothbrush and the paste tends to shimmy of the bristles.
Well, if it was a toothbrush, I'd just quote a price, but then a toothbrush is a non-lethal object.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothpaste - a paste used in cleaning teeth, typically used with a toothbrush

Etymology

Formed from 'tooth' + 'brush', referring to its specific function.
