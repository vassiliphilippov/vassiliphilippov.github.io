slob

1. NOUN

/ slɑb / (AmE), / slɒb / (BrE)
A person who is messy, lazy, or lacking in manners; a coarse or obnoxious person.

Examples

Bravado has been alternately described as "a symbol of the new grandeur of France and myself " (De Gaulle) and "a decadent, disgusting slob "!
Regardless of how much of a slob you knew yourself to be, you could be certain they would never find out - and even if they did it would make no difference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sloven - a person who is habitually untidy or careless in dress, habits, etc.
  • Antonym: neat freak - a person who is excessively tidy or clean

Etymology

Possibly from Irish slab (mud) or slob (muddy land).
