scrupulously

1. ADV

/ ˈskruːpjələsli / (AmE), / ˈskruːpjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great care and attention to doing what is right or proper.

Examples

There are boxed proclamations in the newspaper of watchings, pinnings, ringings, engagements, and marriages in a scrupulously graded hierarchy of felicity.

Synset Examples

he came religiously every morning at 8 o'clock

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conscientiously - in a thorough and careful way

Etymology

From scrupulous + -ly; 'scrupulous' from Latin 'scrupulosus', meaning 'anxious' or 'careful'.
