cloudburst

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaʊdˌbɝst / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdˌbɜːst / (BrE)
A sudden, very heavy rainfall, often over a short period.

Examples

The cloudburst cut off abruptly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: downpour - a heavy rainfall

Etymology

Formed from 'cloud' + 'burst', referring to the sudden release of water as if a cloud has burst.
