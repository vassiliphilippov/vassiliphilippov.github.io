automate

1. VERB

/ ˈɔtəˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəˌmeɪt / (BrE)
to make a process, system, or operation operate automatically, usually by using machines or computers

Examples

In the last few years the telephone company has managed to automate many areas of their service.

Synset Examples

automatize the production
automate the movement of the robot

Related Terms

  • Antonym: manual - done by hand or without automation
  • Synonym: automatize - make automatic or operate automatically (alternate verb)

Etymology

From automatic + -ate (to form verbs meaning to make or become).
