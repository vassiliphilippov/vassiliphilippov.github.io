ready-made

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛdi ˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi ˌmeɪd / (BrE)
(of goods) made in advance for sale and immediate use, rather than being made to order.

Examples

Most species seem to prefer a ready-made hollow such as a deserted mouse nest, a bird house, or the hole made by a woodpecker; some show a definite liking for making their nest in moss.

Etymology

Formed from 'ready' + 'made', meaning 'made and ready for use'.
