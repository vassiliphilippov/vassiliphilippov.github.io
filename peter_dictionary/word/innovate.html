innovate

1. VERB

/ ˈɪnəˌveɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnəʊveɪt / (BrE)
to introduce new methods, ideas, or products; to make changes by using new ways of doing something

Examples

Moreover, the shortage of treatment resources and the chronically persistent shortage of mental health manpower force us to innovate additional refinements of preventive intervention techniques to make services more widely available - and on a more effective basis to more people.

Synset Examples

A new word processor was introduced

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invent - create something that has not existed before
  • Synonym: modernize - make something more modern by introducing new methods or ideas

Etymology

From Latin 'innovare' meaning 'to renew', from 'in-' (into) + 'novus' (new).
