melodic

1. ADJ

/ məˈlɑːdɪk / (AmE), / mɪˈlɒdɪk / (BrE)
relating to or having a pleasant or tuneful melody; characterized by melody.

Examples

His creative development of melodic designs of Slavic dance tunes and love songs is captivating: witty, clever, adroit, and subtle.

Synset Examples

the melodious song of a meadowlark

Related Terms

  • Synonym: melodious - having a pleasant-sounding melody

Etymology

From Medieval Latin melodicus, from Greek melōidikos (musical, relating to song).
