prurient

1. ADJ

/ ˈprʊriənt / (AmE), / ˈprʊəriənt / (BrE)
Having or showing an excessive or inappropriate interest in sexual matters.

Examples

Still, it would be surprising if what one reads did not contribute to one's ideas of right and wrong; certainly the awakened alarm over the comic books and the continuous concern over prurient literature indicate some peripheral aspects of this influence.

Synset Examples

eluding the lubricious embraces of her employer
her sensuous grace roused his lustful nature
prurient literature
prurient thoughts
a salacious rooster of a little man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salacious - having or conveying undue or inappropriate interest in sexual matters
  • Synonym: lustful - having strong sexual desire
  • Antonym: modest - not showing or feeling excessive or inappropriate sexual interest

Etymology

From Latin 'pruriens', present participle of 'prurire' (to itch, to long for).
