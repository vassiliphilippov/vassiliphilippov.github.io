corduroys

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrdəˌrɔɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdjʊrɔɪz / (BrE)
Trousers made from corduroy fabric, typically durable and ribbed.

Examples

At home, he would n't even wash his hands for supper, and he wandered around the yard in a pair of sweaty old corduroys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corduroy - a thick cotton fabric with raised ribs
