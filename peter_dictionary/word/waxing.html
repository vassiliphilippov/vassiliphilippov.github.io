waxing

1. NOUN

/ ˈwæksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwæksɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying wax to a surface, such as for polishing, protecting, or cosmetic purposes

Examples

There is no dyeing or waxing or gassing needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polishing - making a surface smooth and shiny, often using wax or another substance
  • Synonym: buffing - rubbing a surface to give it a shiny finish, sometimes after waxing

Etymology

From English 'wax' + '-ing' denoting the action or process.
