traditionally

1. ADV

/ trəˈdɪʃənəli / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that follows tradition; according to established customs or practices.

Examples

Traditionally, rates of pay for retired military personnel have been proportionate to current rates of pay for active personnel.

Synset Examples

traditionally, we eat fried foods on Hanukah

Related Terms

  • Synonym: customarily - in a way that is usual for a particular person, place, or society

Etymology

From 'traditional' + '-ly'. 'Traditional' from Latin 'traditionem' (a handing down, delivery, surrender).
