beverage

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɛv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
A drink, especially one other than water; any liquid suitable for drinking.

Examples

Depending on the organism, there may be multiplication in some food or beverage products, i.e., in milk for example.
Many food and beverage companies are already on a highly planned basis.

Synset Examples

may I take your beverage order?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drink - any liquid that can be swallowed as refreshment

Etymology

From Old French 'bevrage', from 'beivre' (to drink), from Latin 'bibere' (to drink).
