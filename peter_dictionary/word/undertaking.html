undertaking

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌndərˈteɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈteɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
A task, project, or piece of work that someone has decided to do or attempt.

Examples

The doctor paid the bill and they repaired to the hotel, room number nine, to initiate Alex further into these undertakings.

Synset Examples

he prepared for great undertakings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enterprise - a project or undertaking, especially one that is difficult or requires effort
  • Synonym: project - a planned piece of work with a particular aim

Etymology

From undertake + -ing; 'undertake' from Middle English 'undertaken', from under + take.
