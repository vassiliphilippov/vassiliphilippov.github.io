theorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiərɪst / (AmE), / ˈθɪərɪst / (BrE)
A person who develops or studies theories, especially within science or the arts.

Examples

Location theorists have given these matters much consideration.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theoretician - a person concerned with the theoretical aspects of a subject

Etymology

From theory + -ist, influenced by French théoriste.
