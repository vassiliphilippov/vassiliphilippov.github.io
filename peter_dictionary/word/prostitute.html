prostitute

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːstəˌtut / (AmE), / ˈprɒstɪˌtjuːt / (BrE)
a person, usually a woman, who engages in sexual activity with others for payment

Examples

Dice rolled, prostitutes plied their trade, thieves stole, murderers stabbed, and the ungodly blasphemed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sex worker - a person who works in the sex industry, especially one who engages in sexual activity for payment

Etymology

From Latin 'prostituta', past participle of 'prostituere' (to expose publicly, offer for sale).
