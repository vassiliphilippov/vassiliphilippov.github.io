pulverized

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌlvɚˌaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpʌlvəraɪzd / (BrE)
Reduced to fine particles by crushing, grinding, or pounding.

Examples

I put a little scoop of pulverized phosphate rock or steamed bone meal into each hole with the plant.

Synset Examples

powdered cellulose
powdery snow
pulverized sugar is prepared from granulated sugar by grinding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powdered - reduced to powder; consisting of or resembling powder
  • Antonym: granulated - formed into grains or granules; not fine like powder

Etymology

From Latin 'pulverizare', from 'pulvis' meaning 'dust'.
