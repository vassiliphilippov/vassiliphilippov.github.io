unpleasantly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈplɛzəntli / (AmE), / ʌnˈplɛzəntli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes discomfort or displeasure; in an unpleasant way.

Examples

No, he had been surprised, unpleasantly surprised, but surprised.

Synset Examples

he had been unpleasantly surprised

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner

Etymology

From 'unpleasant' + '-ly', where 'unpleasant' is from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'pleasant'.
