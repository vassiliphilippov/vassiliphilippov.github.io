magic

1. ADJ

/ ˈmædʒɪk / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk / (BrE)
possessing or using supernatural powers; relating to, characteristic of, or capable of being produced by supernatural forces.

Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From Old French magique, from Latin magicus, from Ancient Greek magikos (‘magical’), from magos (‘magus, wise man’).
