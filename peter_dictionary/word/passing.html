passing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
(in American football) a play or action that involves throwing the ball to a teammate.

Etymology

/ ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
