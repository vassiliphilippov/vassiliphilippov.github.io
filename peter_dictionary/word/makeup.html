makeup

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (BrE)
Cosmetic products applied to the face to enhance or alter appearance.

Examples

Then, with a shrug of pretended indifference, she took a compact from her purse and went through the motions of fixing her make-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cosmetics - substances applied to the body for beautifying or altering the appearance

Etymology

From the phrasal verb 'make up', with the noun sense attested since the late 19th century in theatrical use.
