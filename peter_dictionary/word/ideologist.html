ideologist

1. NOUN

/ ˌaɪdiˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪdiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A person who advocates or supports a particular ideology.

Examples

It is n't what the ideologist believes in, but what he hates, that puts the world in jeopardy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ideologue - a proponent, theorist, or advocate of an ideology

Etymology

From French 'idéologiste' or from 'ideology' + '-ist'.
