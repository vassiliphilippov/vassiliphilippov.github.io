fella

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛlə / (AmE), / ˈfɛlə / (BrE)
(informal) A man or boy; used as a familiar form of address or reference.

Examples

Not like us fellas.
An' that could mean trouble with a fella that's workin' for crooks.
"Three shots in that fella' fore he hit the ground!
A politician's word so that... young fellas like yourself can wear a suit and a tie and have a job.

Synset Examples

that chap is your host
there's a fellow at the door
he's a likable cuss
he's a good bloke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fellow - a man or boy
  • Synonym: guy - informal term for a man
  • Synonym: chap - British informal term for a man

Etymology

Informal contraction of 'fellow.'
