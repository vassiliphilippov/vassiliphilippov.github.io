swab

1. VERB

/ swɑb / (AmE), / swɒb / (BrE)
to clean or wash a surface with a swab or mop

Examples

The pipes burst and they all laughed and stood in ice water to their ankles while they swabbed the bathrooms.

Synset Examples

swab the ship's decks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mop - to clean or wipe with a mop

Etymology

From Middle Dutch *swabbe* (mop, swab).
