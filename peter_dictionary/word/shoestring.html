shoestring

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃuːˌstrɪŋ / (BrE)
A thin lace or cord used for fastening shoes by threading through eyelets and tying.

Examples

Barbara bought some cotton aprons, and Harold bought shoestrings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoelace - a lace used for fastening shoes

Etymology

Formed from "shoe" + "string", meaning a string used for a shoe.
