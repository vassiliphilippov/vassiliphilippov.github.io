waterproof

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔtərˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌpruːf / (BrE)
Not allowing water to pass through; impervious to water.

Examples

Miraculously, the bottle was still in my hand, foam still geysering over my (luckily) waterproof watch.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: water-resistant - Able to resist water to some degree but not entirely impervious.

Etymology

Formed from 'water' + 'proof', meaning resistant to water.
