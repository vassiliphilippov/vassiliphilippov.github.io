ambition

1. NOUN

/ æmˈbɪʃən / (AmE), / æmˈbɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a strong desire and determination to achieve success or a particular goal

Examples

For sheer ambition, take the Decca series titled modestly "Wisdom ".
Again, Lawrence thought a little sadly, these were the fees of poverty and ambition.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drive - strong motivation or energy to achieve something
  • Synonym: aspiration - hope or ambition of achieving something
  • Antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern

Etymology

From Latin ambitio (a going around, striving for favor), from ambire (to go around).
