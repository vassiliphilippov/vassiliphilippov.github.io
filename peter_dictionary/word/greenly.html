greenly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡrinli / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnli / (BrE)
In a green manner; with a green color.

Examples

Abel sat and regarded the farm country which, spreading out from both sides of the road, rolled greenly up to where the silent white houses and long barns and silos nested into the tilled fields.

Synset Examples

the countryside rolled greenly down into the valley

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verdantly - in a lush, green, and fresh manner

Etymology

Formed from 'green' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
