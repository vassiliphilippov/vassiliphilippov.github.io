respectful

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈspɛktfəl / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktfʊl / (BrE)
showing or expressing respect; feeling or demonstrating deference or regard towards someone or something.

Examples

Further evidence that Roman Catholicism enjoys a more favorable position today than in 1861 is the respectful attention given to it in the mass media of England.
Only Cromwell, the giant boatswain, was mild-mannered and respectful.

Synset Examples

respectful behavior
a respectful glance

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disrespectful - showing a lack of respect
  • Synonym: deferential - showing polite submission or respect

Etymology

From respect + -ful, formed in English.
