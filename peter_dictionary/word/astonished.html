astonished

1. ADJ

/ əˈstɑnɪʃt / (AmE), / əˈstɒnɪʃt / (BrE)
very surprised or amazed by something unexpected

Examples

At the door Alex managed to persuade the increasingly astonished fille de chambre to return in ten minutes.
The Indian was again raising his bottle, but to my astonished relief - probably only a fraction of Johnson's - the bottle this time went to the Indian's lips.

Synset Examples

an amazed audience gave the magician a standing ovation
I stood enthralled, astonished by the vastness and majesty of the cathedral
astounded viewers wept at the pictures from the Oklahoma City bombing
stood in stunned silence
stunned scientists found not one but at least three viruses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amazed - feeling great surprise or wonder
  • Synonym: astounded - shocked or greatly surprised
  • Synonym: stunned - so surprised or shocked that you cannot react

Etymology

From astonish + -ed; 'astonish' from Old French 'estoner', from Latin 'extonare' (to thunder out).
