upcoming

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌpˌkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an event) taking place or expected to happen in the near future.

Examples

Her impact in the ZING commercials had led to her being considered for an excellent part in an upcoming TV series, Underwater Western Eye, a documentary type show to be sponsored by Oatnut Grits.

Synset Examples

the approaching election
this coming Thursday
the forthcoming holidays
the upcoming spring fashions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forthcoming - about to happen soon; imminent
  • Synonym: imminent - about to occur very soon
