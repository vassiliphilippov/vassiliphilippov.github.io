airtight

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛrˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈeə.taɪt / (BrE)
So well constructed or executed as to leave no possibility of error, flaw, or weakness; flawless or unassailable.

Examples

The FBI has him airtight.

Synset Examples

an airtight defense
an airtight argument

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unassailable - impossible to dispute or attack
  • Antonym: flawed - having imperfections or weaknesses

Etymology

From 'air' + 'tight', originally referring to containers that do not allow air in or out; figuratively extended to mean having no weaknesses.
