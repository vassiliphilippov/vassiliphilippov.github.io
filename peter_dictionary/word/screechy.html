screechy

1. ADJ

/ ˈskriːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈskriːtʃi / (BrE)
making or having a high-pitched, harsh, and piercing sound.

Examples

"Oh he's just an international spy ", Eileen would shout with her screechy laugh.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrill - having a high-pitched and piercing quality (of sound)
  • Synonym: raspy - having a harsh or grating sound
