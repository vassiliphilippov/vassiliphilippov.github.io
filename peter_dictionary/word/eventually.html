eventually

1. ADV

/ ɪˈvɛntʃuəli / (AmE), / ɪˈvɛntʃuəli / (BrE)
After a period of time, especially after a delay or series of events; in the end.

Examples

Eventually the class will be able to kick up high enough so that the teacher can catch the leading leg.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finally - after a long time or delay, at last

Etymology

From Latin 'eventualis' (relating to an event), via French 'éventuel'.
