loudspeaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaʊdˌspiːkər / (AmE), / ˈlaʊdˌspiːkə / (BrE)
A device that converts electrical signals into sounds loud enough to be heard at a distance.

Examples

It's those damn loudspeakers ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speaker - a device that converts electrical signals into sound

Etymology

Formed from 'loud' + 'speaker', referring to a device that 'speaks' loudly.
