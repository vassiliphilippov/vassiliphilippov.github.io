Alabamian

1. NOUN

/ ˌæl.əˈbeɪ.mi.ən / (AmE), / ˌal.əˈbeɪ.mi.ən / (BrE)
a native or resident of Alabama, a state in the southeastern United States

Examples

An Alabamian wrote: "Col. Henry is [ an ignoramus ] fit for nothing higher than the cultivation of corn ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Alabaman - a native or resident of Alabama

Etymology

From Alabama, the name of the U.S. state, with the suffix -ian denoting belonging or relation.
