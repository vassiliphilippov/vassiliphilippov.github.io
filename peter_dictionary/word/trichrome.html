trichrome

1. ADJ

/ ˈtraɪˌkroʊm / (AmE), / ˈtrɪkrəʊm / (BrE)
Having or involving three colors, especially as used in technical or scientific processes.

Examples

Trichrome stains failed to show fibrosis in the involved muscles.

Synset Examples

trichromatic vision
a trichromatic printing process
trichromatic staining is the staining of tissue samples differentially in three colors
tricolor plumage
a tricolor process in photography

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trichromatic - having or involving three colors

Etymology

From Greek 'tri-' (three) + 'chroma' (color).
