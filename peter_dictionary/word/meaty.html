meaty

1. ADJ

/ ˈmiːti / (AmE), / ˈmiːti / (BrE)
having the qualities of meat, or containing a substantial amount of meat; resembling meat in texture or substance.

Examples

It is roughly shaped like a large pear, and when properly ripened, its dark green skin covers a meaty, melon like pulp that has about the consistency of a ripe Bartlett pear, but oily.

Synset Examples

enough of vegetarianism; let's have a meaty meal

Related Terms

  • Antonym: meatless - containing no meat
  • Synonym: fleshy - having a soft, thick, and pulpy texture, often like flesh
