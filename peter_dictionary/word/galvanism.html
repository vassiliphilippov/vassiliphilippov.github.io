galvanism

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæl.vəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɡal.və.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
electricity produced by chemical action, especially as discovered by Luigi Galvani and observed in chemical reactions within batteries
