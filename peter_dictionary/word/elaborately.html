elaborately

1. ADV

/ ɪˈlæbərətli / (AmE), / ɪˈlæb(ə)rətli / (BrE)
In a detailed and complicated or ornate manner; with great care or complexity.

Examples

He would sit, slim-waisted and spare, on the edge of Scotty's bed, his legs crossed so elaborately that the crossed foot could tap the floor.

Synset Examples

it was elaborately spelled out

Related Terms

  • Antonym: simply - in a plain or uncomplicated manner

Etymology

From 'elaborate' + '-ly'. 'Elaborate' from Latin *elaboratus* (worked out).
