clamorous

1. ADJ

/ ˈklæmərəs / (AmE), / ˈklæmərəs / (BrE)
making a loud and confused noise; vehement or insistent in outcry

Examples

It has moved on various levels, it has been clamorous and confused.

Synset Examples

blatant radios
a clamorous uproar
strident demands
a vociferous mob

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blatant - conspicuously and offensively loud; brazenly obvious
  • Synonym: vociferous - loud and forceful in expressing opinions or feelings
  • Antonym: quiet - making little or no noise

Etymology

From Latin 'clamor' (a shout), via Old French 'clamoreux'.
