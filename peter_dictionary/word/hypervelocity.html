hypervelocity

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaɪpərvəˈlɑsəti / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəvəˈlɒsɪti / (BrE)
an extremely high velocity, typically far exceeding those usually encountered, especially in the context of physics, engineering, or space impacts
