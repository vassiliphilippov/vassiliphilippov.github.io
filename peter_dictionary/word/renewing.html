renewing

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈnuɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈnjuːɪŋ / (BrE)
Imparting new life or vigor; giving a sense of refreshment or rejuvenation.

Examples

The girl crawled out into the renewing warmth of the sunshine, hugging her shoulders and still trembling.

Synset Examples

the renewing warmth of the sunshine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invigorating - giving strength or energy
  • Synonym: refreshing - making someone feel less tired or hot; pleasantly new or different

Etymology

From the verb 'renew', which comes from Old French *renouveler*, from Latin *renovare* (to make new again).
