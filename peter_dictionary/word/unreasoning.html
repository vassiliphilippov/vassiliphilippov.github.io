unreasoning

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈriːzənɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːzənɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a feeling, action, etc.) not based on reason or evidence; not guided by rational thought.

Examples

Bitter, unreasoning jealousy?

Synset Examples

blind hatred
blind faith
unreasoning panic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable

Etymology

From un- (not) + reasoning (from reason, ultimately from Latin ratio).
