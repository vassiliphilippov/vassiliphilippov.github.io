comprehension

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnʃən / (BrE)
The ability to understand and grasp the meaning or significance of something.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin comprehensio, meaning 'a seizing, comprehension,' from comprehendere 'to seize, comprise'.
