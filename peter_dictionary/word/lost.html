/ lɔst / (AmE), / lɒst / (BrE)
(of a person, group, or thing) doomed, destroyed, or irretrievably gone, especially in a spiritual or physical sense.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: saved - rescued from harm, especially in a spiritual sense
