assess

1. VERB

/ əˈsɛs / (AmE), / əˈsɛs / (BrE)
to evaluate or estimate the nature, quality, ability, extent, or significance of something

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evaluate - to judge or calculate the value or importance of something
  • Synonym: appraise - to assess the value or quality of
  • Synonym: estimate - to roughly calculate or judge the value, number, quantity, or extent of

Etymology

From Latin 'assessus', past participle of 'assidēre' (to sit by, assist in judging).
