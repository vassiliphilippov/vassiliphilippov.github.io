individuality

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪndɪvɪdʒuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪvɪdjʊˈælɪti / (BrE)
The quality that makes a person or thing different from others; distinctiveness or uniqueness of character.

Examples

But the most impressive testimony to Schnabel's distinction as a teacher is reflected by the individuality which marks each student's approach as distinctly his own.

Synset Examples

so absorbed by the movement that she lost all sense of individuality

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identity - the fact of being who or what a person or thing is
  • Antonym: conformity - compliance with standards, rules, or laws; behavior in accordance with socially accepted conventions

Etymology

From Medieval Latin individualitas, from Latin individuus (not divisible).
