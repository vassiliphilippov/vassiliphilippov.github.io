rajah

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑdʒə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːdʒə / (BrE)
A king or prince in India, especially during former times.

Examples

Oddly enough, this is an amulet against housebreakers, presented to the mem and me by a local rajah in 1949.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: maharajah - a great king or prince in India

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'rājan' (king), via Hindi and Portuguese.
