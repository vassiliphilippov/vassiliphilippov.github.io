wholeheartedly

1. ADV

/ ˌhoʊlˈhɑrtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌhəʊlˈhɑːtɪdli / (BrE)
In a completely sincere, committed, or enthusiastic way; with all one's heart.

Examples

I love you with all my heart.

Synset Examples

he adopted wholeheartedly some of the policies that he had previously criticized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sincerely - in a sincere or genuine way
  • Synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows eager enjoyment or interest

Etymology

From 'wholehearted' + '-ly', where 'wholehearted' (entirely sincere or enthusiastic) combines 'whole' and 'heart'.
