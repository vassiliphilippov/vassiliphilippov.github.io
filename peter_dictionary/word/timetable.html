timetable

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪmˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌteɪbl / (BrE)
A schedule listing events and the times at which they will take place.

Examples

In general, however, the timetable of travel lags considerably behind that of the student at Harvard or Smith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schedule - a plan of things to be done and the times when they will be done

Etymology

From time + table; originally referring to a tabular list of times.
