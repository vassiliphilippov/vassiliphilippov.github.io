cooler

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːlər / (AmE), / ˈkuːlə / (BrE)
A portable insulated container for keeping food and drinks cold, typically used for picnics or travel.

Examples

Moving around camp we saw all kinds of camp stoves, lanterns, coolers, bedding, games, fishing tackle, windbreaks and sunshades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refrigerator - an appliance used to keep food and drinks cold
