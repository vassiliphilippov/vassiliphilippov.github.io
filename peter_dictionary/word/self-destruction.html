self-destruction

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf dɪˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / sɛlf dɪˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
The act of killing oneself; suicide.

Examples

Expressed differently: if the price for becoming a faithful follower of Jesus Christ is some form of self-destruction, whether of the body or of the mind - sacrificium corporis, sacrificium intellectus - then there is no alternative but that the price remain unpaid.

Synset Examples

it is a crime to commit suicide

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suicide - the act of intentionally killing oneself

Etymology

From self- + destruction, based on Latin destruere (to destroy).
