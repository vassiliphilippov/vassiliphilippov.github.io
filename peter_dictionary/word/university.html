/ ˌjuːnəˈvɝːsəti / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti / (BrE)

Etymology

From Latin universitas (community, corporation; the whole), especially in the phrase universitas magistrorum et scholarium (community of teachers and scholars).
