overcurious

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈkjʊriəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkjʊəriəs / (BrE)
Excessively curious; showing more curiosity than is appropriate or usual.

Examples

While she was thus engaged, McFeeley questioned her about her whereabouts the previous day, any recollections she had of people hanging around, of overcurious delivery boys or repairmen, of strange cars cruising the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: curious - eager to know or learn something

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'over-' (excessive) + 'curious'.
