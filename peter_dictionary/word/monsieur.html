monsieur

1. NOUN

/ mənˈsjɜr / (AmE), / mɒnˈsjɜː / (BrE)
a French courtesy title for a man, equivalent to 'Mr.' in English

Examples

"Bonjour, messieurs, vous etes matinals ", she greeted them pleasantly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mister - a courtesy title for a man in English

Etymology

From French 'monsieur', meaning 'my lord' or 'sir'.
