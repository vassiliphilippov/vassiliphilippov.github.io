serenely

1. ADV

/ səˈrinli / (AmE), / sɪˈriːnli / (BrE)
In a calm, peaceful, and untroubled manner.

Examples

Two hours of watching his serenely happy face, listening to his soft humming as he bent over his penciled figures, and Blanche had to leave.

Synset Examples

I had the feeling that he was waiting, too--serenely patient

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calmly - in a calm manner
  • Synonym: peacefully - in a peaceful way
  • Antonym: restlessly - in a restless manner

Etymology

From 'serene,' itself from Latin 'serenus' (clear, calm) + adverbial suffix '-ly.'
