counterproposal

1. NOUN

/ ˌkaʊntərprəˈpoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəprəˈpəʊzəl / (BrE)
A proposal put forward as an alternative to a previous proposal.

Examples

Du Pont's counterproposal was filed on May 14, 1958.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proposal - a plan or suggestion put forward for consideration

Etymology

Formed from 'counter-' (against, in opposition) and 'proposal'.
