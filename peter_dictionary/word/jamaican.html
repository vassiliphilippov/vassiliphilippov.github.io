Jamaican

1. NOUN

/ dʒəˈmeɪkən / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪkən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Jamaica

Examples

George Kerr, the swift striding Jamaican, set a meet record in the 600 - yard run in the Knights of Columbus track meet tonight, beating Purdue's Dave Mills in a hot duel in 1.10.1.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jamaican - of or relating to Jamaica or its people or culture (adjective)

Etymology

From Jamaica (name of the island nation) + -an (denoting nationality/inhabitant).
