conception

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / kənˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
An abstract or general idea formed by considering instances; a concept or notion.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concept - an abstract idea or a mental symbol; a notion
  • Synonym: notion - a general or vague idea; an impression or belief
  • Synonym: idea - a thought, a mental impression, or a formulated thought or opinion

Etymology

From Latin 'conceptionem' (act of conceiving), from 'concipere' (to take in, conceive).
