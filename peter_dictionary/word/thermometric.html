thermometric

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɝː.məˈmɛt.rɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜː.məˈmɛt.rɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the measurement of temperature or thermometry.

Examples

An acoustical interferometer has been constructed and used, with helium gas as the thermometric fluid, to measure temperatures near 4.2 and 2.1 * * f.

Synset Examples

helium gas was the thermometric fluid

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermometry - the science or technique of measuring temperature

Etymology

From Greek thermos (hot) and -metric (relating to measurement).
