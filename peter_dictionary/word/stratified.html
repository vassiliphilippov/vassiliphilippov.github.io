stratified

1. ADJ

/ ˈstræt̬.əˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈstræt.ɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
Arranged in layers, grades, or ranks one above another, especially in a systematic or hierarchical manner.

Examples

The subjects for this study were randomly selected from stratified areas of the distribution, one-third as underachievers, one-third medium, and one-third over-achievers.

Synset Examples

stratified areas of the distribution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: graded - arranged by degree or rank

Etymology

From Latin stratum (a layer) + -fy + -ed.
