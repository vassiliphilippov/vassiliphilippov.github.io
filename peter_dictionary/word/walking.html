walking

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a distance or location) close enough to be reached on foot; within a reasonable distance to walk.

Examples

We shared the expenses of the studio, and we all lived within walking distance of it, in cheap lodgings of one kind or another.

Synset Examples

walking distance
the factory with the big parking lot...is more convenient than the walk-to factory

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'walk'.
