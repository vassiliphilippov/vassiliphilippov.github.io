cornfield

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌfild / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌfiːld / (BrE)
A field where corn is grown.

Examples

He thought of Simms Purdew, who once had risen at the edge of a cornfield, a maniacal scream on his lips, and swung a clubbed musket like a flail to beat down the swirl of Rebel bayonets about him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maize field - a field where maize is grown; sometimes used interchangeably with 'cornfield'

Etymology

Formed from 'corn' + 'field'.
