applicability

1. NOUN

/ əˌplɪkəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌplɪkəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
The quality of being relevant or suitable to a particular matter or purpose.

Examples

One wonders about its applicability to people.
Fortunately, a general formula of wide applicability solves all problems of this kind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relevance - The quality of being closely connected or appropriate

Etymology

From 'applicable' + '-ity', following the pattern of Latin-derived nouns indicating quality or state.
