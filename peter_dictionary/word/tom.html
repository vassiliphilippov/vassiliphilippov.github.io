Tom

1. NOUN

/ tɑm / (AmE), / tɒm / (BrE)
(offensive) An insulting term for a Black man regarded as overly subservient or deferential to White people.

Examples

Well, now he's dead, Tom, and nothing can bring him back.

Etymology

From the character Uncle Tom in the novel 'Uncle Tom's Cabin' by Harriet Beecher Stowe.
