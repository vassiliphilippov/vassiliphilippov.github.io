cordial

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔr.dʒəl / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.dɪ.əl / (BrE)
Warm and friendly; showing sincere affection and kindness.

Examples

Maybe that was why they were cordial and loyal towards the unpredictability of Arlene.

Synset Examples

an affable smile
an amiable gathering
cordial relations
a cordial greeting
a genial host

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affable - friendly, pleasant, and easy to talk to
  • Synonym: genial - friendly and cheerful
  • Antonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic

Etymology

From Medieval Latin cordialis, from Latin cor (heart).
