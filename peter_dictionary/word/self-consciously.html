self-consciously

1. ADV

/ sɛlfˈkɑn.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / sɛlfˈkɒn.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is uncomfortably aware of oneself and how one is perceived by others.

Examples

His father would come upstairs and stand self-consciously at the foot of the bed and look at his son.

Synset Examples

the little girl self-consciously recited the poem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-aware - having knowledge or consciousness of one's own character and feelings
  • Antonym: unselfconsciously - in a manner that is natural and not self-conscious
