outclassed

1. ADJ

/ aʊtˈklæst / (AmE), / aʊtˈklɑːst / (BrE)
Surpassed decisively by someone or something else, so as to seem inferior in ability or quality.

Examples

The powerful New York Yankees won their 19th world series in a 5 - game romp over outclassed Cincinnati, crushing the Reds in a humiliating 13 - 5 barrage Monday in the loosely played finale.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surpassed - beaten or exceeded in achievement or quality
  • Degree Lower: inferior - lower in quality or status

Etymology

From prefix 'out-' (beyond) + 'class' (category, rank), meaning to surpass in class or quality.
