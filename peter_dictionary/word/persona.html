persona

1. NOUN

/ pərˈsoʊnə / (AmE), / pəˈsəʊnə / (BrE)
the character or role that an actor portrays in a play, film, or other performance

Examples

Jubal would have bet his life that if Anne were to witness the Second Coming, she would memorize date, time, personae, events, and barometric pressure without batting her calm blue eyes.

Synset Examples

she played the part of Desdemona

Related Terms

  • Synonym: character - a role or personality portrayed by an actor
  • Synonym: role - the part played by an actor in a performance

Etymology

From Latin 'persona' (mask, character, role).
