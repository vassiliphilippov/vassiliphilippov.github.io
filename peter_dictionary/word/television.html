television

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən / (BrE)
The system or process of transmitting visual images and sound that are reproduced on screens, primarily for entertainment, information, or education.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: TV - abbreviation for television
  • Antonym: radio - system for transmitting sound only; contrasted with television

Etymology

From Ancient Greek 'tele' (far) + Latin 'visio' (sight).
