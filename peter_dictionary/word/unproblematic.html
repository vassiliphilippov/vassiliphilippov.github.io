unproblematic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌprɑːbləˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌprɒbləˈmætɪk / (BrE)
Not presenting difficulties or complications; easy or straightforward.

Examples

The cyclist, by contrast, blond and blue-eyed, is simply unreflective, unproblematic Life, "blithe and carefree ".

Synset Examples

an elementary problem in statistics
elementary, my dear Watson
a simple game
found an uncomplicated solution to the problem

Related Terms

  • Antonym: problematic - causing difficulty or problems
  • Synonym: simple - easily understood or done; not complicated
  • Synonym: elementary - easy and not involved or complicated

Etymology

From un- (not) + problematic, meaning 'not causing problems.'
