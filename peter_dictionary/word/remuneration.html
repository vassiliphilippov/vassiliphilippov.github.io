remuneration

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌmjunəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌmjuːnəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
Money or other compensation paid for work or services.

Examples

He receives remuneration for his work.

Synset Examples

wages were paid by check
he wasted his pay on drink
they saved a quarter of all their earnings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wage - regular payment, typically weekly, for work
  • Synonym: salary - fixed regular payment, typically monthly, for employment
  • Synonym: stipend - a fixed regular sum paid as a salary or allowance
  • Synonym: compensation - something, typically money, awarded for work or loss

Etymology

From Latin remuneratio (a repayment, recompense), from remunerari (to reward).
