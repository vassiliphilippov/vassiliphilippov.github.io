shooter

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
A large marble used to shoot at other marbles in the game of marbles.

Examples

Ludie had a cigar box full of marbles and shooters, and a Roman candle from last Fourth of July.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taw - large marble used as a shooter in marbles
