printable

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈprɪn.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of language, comments, etc.) Suitable for publication, because it does not contain material that is offensive, profane, or legally objectionable.

Examples

But these comments are hardly printable.

Synset Examples

printable language

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unprintable - not suitable for publication because of being offensive or objectionable

Etymology

From 'print' + '-able', forming an adjective meaning capable of being printed.
