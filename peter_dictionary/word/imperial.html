imperial

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈpɪriəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl / (BrE)
Relating to an empire or to an emperor; associated with an empire.

Examples

One is an imperial London stockbroker called Jerebohm.

Synset Examples

imperial colony
the imperial gallon was standardized legally throughout the British Empire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: royal - relating to a king or queen
  • Antonym: republican - relating to a republic (as opposed to an empire)

Etymology

From Latin 'imperialis', from 'imperium' meaning 'command, empire'.
