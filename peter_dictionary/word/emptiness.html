emptiness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛmptinəs / (AmE), / ˈɛmptinəs / (BrE)
The state of being empty or containing nothing.

Examples

Into the emptiness of the street, and his spirit, moved a form.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: void - a completely empty space
  • Antonym: fullness - the state of being full

Etymology

From 'empty' + '-ness', denoting a state or quality.
