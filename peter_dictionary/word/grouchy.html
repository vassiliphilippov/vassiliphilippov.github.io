grouchy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡraʊtʃi / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊtʃi / (BrE)
often in a bad mood; easily annoyed or irritable

Examples

Too grouchy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crabby - easily annoyed, irritable
  • Antonym: good-tempered - friendly and not easily annoyed

Etymology

Perhaps from 'grouch' + -y; 'grouch' of uncertain origin, possibly imitative or from American English slang.
