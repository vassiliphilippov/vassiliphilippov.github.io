Related Terms

  • Antonym: present - being in a particular place

Etymology

From Latin 'absens', present participle of 'abesse' meaning 'to be away'.

2. VERB

/ ˈæb.sənt / (AmE), / ˈæb.sənt / (BrE)
(formal) To remove oneself from a place or situation; to go away or leave, especially deliberately.

Related Terms
