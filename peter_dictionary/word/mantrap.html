mantrap

1. NOUN

/ ˈmæn.træp / (AmE), / ˈmæn.trap / (BrE)
A woman who is very attractive or seductive, often considered capable of luring men into difficult or compromising situations.

Examples

"Well, I guess I know enough to turn you inside out, you sockdologizing old mantrap "! said Trenchard, otherwise Hawk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: femme fatale - an attractive and seductive woman, especially one likely to cause distress or disaster to a man

Etymology

From 'man' + 'trap,' figuratively a trap for men.
