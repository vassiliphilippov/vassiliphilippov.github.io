jammed

1. ADJ

/ dʒæmd / (AmE), / dʒæmd / (BrE)
So full that nothing more can be added; filled to capacity.

Examples

He peered in the boxes themselves; all were empty except one, and that one was jammed with letters and magazines.

Synset Examples

a suitcase jammed with dirty clothes
stands jam-packed with fans
a packed theater

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packed - filled tightly so that space remains only for a little or nothing; crowded
  • Synonym: crammed - completely filled with people or things
  • Synonym: jam-packed - very tightly packed or crowded
