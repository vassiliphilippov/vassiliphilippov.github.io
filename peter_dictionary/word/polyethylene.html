polyethylene

1. NOUN

/ ˌpɑliˈɛθəˌlin / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈɛθəliːn / (BrE)
A lightweight thermoplastic polymer used especially in packaging, insulation, and various consumer products.

Examples

Five of the tests were conducted with a polyethylene cover to simulate an ice cover.
The polyethylene cover reduced the oxygen transfer rate by 10 per cent, indicating that the maximum oxygen transfer is at the rotor rather than through the surface.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polymer - a substance made of large molecules composed of many repeating units
  • Degree Higher: polypropylene - a similar thermoplastic polymer used in packaging and textiles

Etymology

From 'poly-' meaning 'many' and 'ethylene', the monomer used to produce the polymer.
