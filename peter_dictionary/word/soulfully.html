soulfully

1. ADV

/ ˈsoʊlfəli / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlfəli / (BrE)
in a manner that expresses deep feeling or emotion; with soulful expression

Examples

The moonlit night was made for romance, and he had been looking at her soulfully for some time.

Synset Examples

he looked at her soulfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotionally - in an emotional manner

Etymology

From "soulful" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
