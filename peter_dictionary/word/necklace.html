necklace

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛk.ləs / (AmE), / ˈnɛk.lɪs / (BrE)
A piece of jewellery consisting of a chain, string, or cord, typically bearing beads, stones, or gems, worn around the neck as an ornament.

Examples

Hal, the linguist, saw the glittering discs and necklaces in terms of the languages spoken therein.

Etymology

From Old French 'nègle', related to 'neck' + '-lace', meaning string or cord.
