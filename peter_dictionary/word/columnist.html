columnist

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːləmnɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɒləmnɪst / (BrE)
A journalist who regularly writes columns, often expressing opinions or commentary, for a newspaper or magazine.

Examples

Shea, the chairman of Mayor Wagner's Baseball Committee, will be joined on the dais by Warren Spahn, the southpaw pitching ace of the Milwaukee Braves; Frank Graham, the Journal-American sports columnist; Bill Mazeroski, the World Series hero of the Pittsburgh Pirates, and Casey Stengel, the former manager of the Yankees.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites or prepares news to be broadcast

Etymology

From 'column' + '-ist'; 'column' in the sense of a regular feature in a publication.
