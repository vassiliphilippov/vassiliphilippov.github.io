sentimentalist

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛn.təˈmɛn.təl.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɪˈmɛn.təl.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who allows emotions and feelings to influence their opinions or actions excessively

Examples

On the other we have the flag wavers and the national sentimentalists who have been willing to use any patriotic, "frontier western "or colonial material willy-nilly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic - a person who is guided more by emotion or imagination than by reason
  • Antonym: realist - a person who accepts a situation as it is and deals with it in a practical way

Etymology

From sentiment + -alist, after sentimental + -ist.
