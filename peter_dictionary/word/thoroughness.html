thoroughness

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɜroʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈθʌrənəs / (BrE)
The quality of being complete and meticulous in performing all aspects of a task.

Examples

He assumed his academic career with the same intensity and thoroughness that had marked every step in his rise from boyhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meticulousness - quality of showing great attention to detail
  • Antonym: carelessness - lack of attention or thoroughness

Etymology

From 'thorough' + '-ness', with 'thorough' from Old English 'thurh' (through).
