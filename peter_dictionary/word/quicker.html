quicker

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkə / (BrE)
at a greater speed; more quickly

Examples

She brought up her free hand to hit him, but this time he was quicker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed
  • Degree Higher: quickest - at the fastest speed; most quickly
  • Antonym: slower - at a lower speed; less quickly
