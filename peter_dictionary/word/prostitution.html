prostitution

1. NOUN

/ ˌproʊ.stɪˈtuː.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɒs.tɪˈtjuː.ʃən / (BrE)
The practice or occupation of engaging in sexual activity with someone for payment.

Examples

The Bordel and other places of prostitution were emptied.
Unlike the Sicilians, he additionally conducted holdups, robberies, and safe cracking expeditions, and refused to touch prostitution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sex work - the provision of sexual services for money
  • Antonym: celibacy - the state of abstaining from sexual activity

Etymology

From Latin 'prostitutio', from 'prostituere' meaning 'to expose publicly'.
