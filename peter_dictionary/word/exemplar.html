exemplar

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɛmplɑr / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛmplɑː / (BrE)
A person or thing that serves as a typical or excellent example; a model to be imitated.

Examples

And the USSR existed as the revolutionary experiment in radical socialism, the ultimate exemplar.

Synset Examples

an exemplar of success
a model of clarity
he is the very model of a modern major general

Related Terms

  • Synonym: model - a person or thing regarded as an excellent example to imitate
  • Synonym: paragon - a person or thing regarded as a perfect example of a particular quality

Etymology

From Latin exemplum (example).
