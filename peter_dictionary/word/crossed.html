crossed

1. ADJ

/ krɔst / (AmE), / krɒst / (BrE)
(of limbs or objects) placed so as to lie across one another; arranged crosswise.

Examples

He would sit, slim-waisted and spare, on the edge of Scotty's bed, his legs crossed so elaborately that the crossed foot could tap the floor.

Synset Examples

spoken with a straight face but crossed fingers
crossed forks
seated with arms across

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uncrossed - not placed across each other; straightened
