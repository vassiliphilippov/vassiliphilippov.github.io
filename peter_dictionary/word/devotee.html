devotee

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛvəˈti / (AmE), / ˌdɛvəˈtiː / (BrE)
A person who is very enthusiastic about and strongly devoted to someone or something; an ardent follower or admirer.

Examples

The common codes, for religious action as such and in their ethical aspects for everyday moral behavior, bind the devotees together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a person who has a strong interest in or admiration for a particular person or thing
  • Synonym: admirer - someone who admires or esteems another
  • Synonym: follower - a person who supports and admires a particular person or set of ideas
  • Antonym: critic - someone who expresses an opposing opinion or disapproval

Etymology

From French dévoté (devoted), from Latin devotus (devoted).
