sari

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑːri / (AmE), / ˈsɑːri / (BrE)
A garment consisting of a long piece of light cloth, wrapped around the body and typically worn by women in South Asia, especially in India.

Examples

The door opened and three men and a woman in a sari swept past him and down the stairs.

Etymology

From Hindi sāṛī, from Sanskrit śāṭī (strip of cloth).
