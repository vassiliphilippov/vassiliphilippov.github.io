egg

1. NOUN

/ ɛɡ / (AmE), / ɛɡ / (BrE)
An oval or round object produced by a female bird, reptile, fish, or invertebrate, usually containing a developing embryo and surrounded by a protective shell or membrane.

Examples

3. NOUN

Examples
