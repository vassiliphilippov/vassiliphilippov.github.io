vintage

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.tɪdʒ / (BrE)
The wine produced in a particular year from a vineyard, or the yield of wine from a season.

Examples

It was an excellent vintage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harvest - the process or period of gathering in crops

Etymology

From Old French *vendage* (grape harvest), from Latin *vindemia*.
