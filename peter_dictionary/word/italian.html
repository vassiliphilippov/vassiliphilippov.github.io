2. NOUN

/ ɪˈtæliən / (AmE), / ɪˈtalɪən / (BrE)
the Romance language spoken mainly in Italy and parts of Switzerland, used as the official language of Italy

Examples

Previous presentations have been on French, Spanish, Russian, Italian, German and Japanese.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: French - the Romance language of France
  • Synonym: Spanish - the Romance language of Spain
  • Synonym: German - the West Germanic language of Germany and Austria

Etymology

From Latin 'italianus', from 'Italia' (Italy).
