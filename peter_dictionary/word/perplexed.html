perplexed

1. ADJ

/ pərˈplɛkst / (AmE), / pəˈplɛkst / (BrE)
feeling confused and unable to understand something; bewildered.

Examples

Hearing his voice ring raucously up from the road, Kate would await him anxiously and watch perplexed as he walked into the house, cold sober.
And so, still wondering and a little perplexed, he grinned at the girl and spoke lightly to make sure that she would know he was kidding.

Synset Examples

perplexed language
perplexed state of the world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
  • Synonym: bewildered - extremely confused or puzzled

Etymology

From Latin 'perplexus' (entangled, intricate), past participle of 'perplectere' (to entangle).
