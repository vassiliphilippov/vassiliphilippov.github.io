legume

1. NOUN

/ ləˈɡum / (AmE), / lɪˈɡjuːm / (BrE)
A plant of the pea family (Leguminosae or Fabaceae), such as a bean, pea, or lentil, typically grown for its edible seeds.

Examples

Aids in reducing the incidence and severity of bloat in beef or dairy cattle on legume pasture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pulse - an edible seed from a leguminous plant, such as beans, lentils, or peas

Etymology

From Latin legumen (pulse, bean, or pod-bearing plant).
