acoustically

1. ADV

/ əˈkuːstɪkli / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to sound or the science of acoustics.

Examples

Yet you feel the orchestra is near at hand, and the individual instruments have the same firm presence associated with listening from a good seat in an acoustically perfect hall.

Synset Examples

acoustically ill-equipped studios

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acoustic - relating to sound or the sense of hearing

Etymology

From 'acoustic' + '-ally'; 'acoustic' from Greek 'akoustikos' (pertaining to hearing).
