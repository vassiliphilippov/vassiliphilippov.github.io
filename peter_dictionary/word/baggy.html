baggy

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæɡi / (AmE), / ˈbaɡi / (BrE)
(of clothing) loose and hanging away from the body; not fitting closely.

Examples

His tweed suit was a baggy gray and green.

Synset Examples

baggy trousers
a loose-fitting blouse is comfortable in hot weather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loose - not tight or firmly fitted
  • Antonym: tight - fitting closely to the body

Etymology

From 'bag', patterned after the way a bag sags loosely.
