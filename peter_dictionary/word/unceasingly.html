unceasingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈsisɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːsɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that does not stop; continually or without interruption.

Examples

I looked unceasingly With my cold mind and with my burning heart ".

Synset Examples

dance inspires him ceaselessly to strive higher and higher toward the shining pinnacle of perfection that is the goal of every artiste

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ceaselessly - without stopping; continually
  • Synonym: endlessly - in a way that seems to have no end
  • Synonym: persistently - continuing firmly or obstinately
  • Antonym: intermittently - at irregular intervals; not continuously

Etymology

Formed from 'unceasing' (not ceasing) + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
