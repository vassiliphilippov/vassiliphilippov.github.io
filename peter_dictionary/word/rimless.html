rimless

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈrɪmləs / (BrE)
Lacking a rim or frame, especially referring to eyeglasses without a surrounding border around the lenses.

Examples

The door was answered by a slender man in his sixties - straight backed, somewhat clerical in manner, wearing rimless glasses.

Synset Examples

rimless glasses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frameless - without a frame; lacking a structural border
