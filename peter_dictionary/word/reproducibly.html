reproducibly

1. ADV

/ ˌriːprəˈduːsəbli / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˈdjuːsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that can be exactly copied or repeated, especially in scientific or experimental contexts.

Examples

The round cells were reproducibly positioned in the light beam which entered the thermostated mineral oil bath through a window.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reproducible - able to be reproduced or duplicated

Etymology

From 'reproducible' + '-ly'. 'Reproducible' from reproduce + -ible.
