cometary

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːməˌteri / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪtəri / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a comet.

Examples

According to Whipple (1955), cometary debris is sufficient to replenish the material spiraling into the sun, maintaining a fairly steady state.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comet - a celestial body of ice, dust, and gas that orbits the sun

Etymology

From 'comet' + '-ary', formed on the analogy of other adjectives relating to celestial bodies.
