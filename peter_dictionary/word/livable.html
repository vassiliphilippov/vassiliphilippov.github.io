livable

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪvəbəl / (BrE)
(of a place) suitable or good enough to live in.

Examples

And there was a house; livable perhaps, but badly in need of repairs.

Synset Examples

livable conditions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unlivable - not suitable to live in
  • Synonym: habitable - suitable to live in, particularly for habitation

Etymology

From 'live' + '-able', indicating the ability to be lived in.
