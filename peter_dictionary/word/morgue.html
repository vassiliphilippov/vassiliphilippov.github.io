morgue

1. NOUN

/ mɔrɡ / (AmE), / mɔːɡ / (BrE)
a building or room where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation

Examples

The usual congratulatory crowd was conspicuously absent; the place had the air of a morgue.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortuary - a room or building for the keeping of dead bodies; synonym for morgue

Etymology

From French 'morgue', originally meaning 'place for viewing', later 'mortuary'.
