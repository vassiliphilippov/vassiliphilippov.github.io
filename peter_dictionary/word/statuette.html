statuette

1. NOUN

/ ˌstætuˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌstatjuˈɛt / (BrE)
A small statue, especially one carved or molded in a decorative or artistic style.

Examples

First of all, the six figures of the Buddha you heisted - four Siamese heads, a black obsidian statuette in the earth touching position, and a large brass figure of the Dying Buddha on a teakwood base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: figurine - a small ornamental statue, usually of a human figure

Etymology

From French 'statuette', diminutive of 'statue'.
