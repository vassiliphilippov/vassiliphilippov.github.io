pad

1. NOUN

/ pæd / (AmE), / pad / (BrE)
a number of sheets of paper fastened together along one edge, used for writing or drawing

Examples

With his free hand he pulled a pad and pencil toward him and began to make notes as he listened, saying, "Uh-huh "and "I see "at intervals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: notepad - small book of blank paper for writing notes
  • Synonym: tablet - a pad of paper for writing or drawing

2. VERB

/ pæd / (AmE), / pæd / (BrE)
to add false or exaggerated details, especially to increase the apparent amount on records such as invoices or expense accounts

Examples

The taxpayer testified that in order to retain the account he had to pad his invoices and pay the excess to the manager.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inflate - to exaggerate or increase something (like numbers or costs) beyond the truth
