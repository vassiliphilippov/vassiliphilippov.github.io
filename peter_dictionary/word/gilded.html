gilded

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɪldɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɪldɪd / (BrE)
Having a deep, slightly brownish gold color; golden in appearance.

Examples

She leaned unconcerned against the broken porch fence, brushing and drying her wet, gilded hair in the sun.

Synset Examples

long aureate (or golden) hair
a gold carpet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: golden - having the color or shine of gold

Etymology

From Middle English 'gilden', from Old English 'gyldan' (to cover with gold), from 'gold' (gold).
