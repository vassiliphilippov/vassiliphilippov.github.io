fishy

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪʃi / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃi / (BrE)
smelling, tasting, or resembling fish; of or relating to fish.

Examples

Fish, fish, fish, fish, fishy.

Synset Examples

the soup had a fishy smell

Etymology

From 'fish' + '-y', forming adjectives meaning 'of, like, or pertaining to'.
