hothead

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who acts rashly or impulsively, especially without considering the consequences

Examples

I hope you're not a hothead like Sonny.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daredevil - reckless or daring person
  • Synonym: firebrand - a person who is passionate about a cause, typically inciting change or taking action impulsively
  • Antonym: coolhead - a calm, level-headed person

Etymology

Formed from 'hot' + 'head', referring metaphorically to someone with a hot temper or uncontrolled impulses.
