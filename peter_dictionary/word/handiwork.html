handiwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæn.diˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈhæn.dɪˌwɜːk / (BrE)
something made or done skillfully by hand, especially an individual piece of craftsmanship or manual labor.

Examples

We 'd seen his handiwork out in the back yard, and the little his tenants had told us of him did make him sound a little special.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craft - an activity involving skill in making things by hand
  • Synonym: handicraft - a particular skill or object made by hand

Etymology

From Middle English handiwerk, from Old English handgeweorc (hand-work, manual labor).
