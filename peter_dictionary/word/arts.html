arts

1. NOUN

/ ɑrts / (AmE), / ɑːts / (BrE)
Academic subjects such as literature, philosophy, history, and other disciplines intended to provide general knowledge and intellectual skills, rather than technical or professional training.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin 'ars' (art, skill, craft); in this sense relating to the classical division of university studies.
