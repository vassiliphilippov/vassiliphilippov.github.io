physicochemical

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪzɪkoʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪkəʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to both physical and chemical properties, processes, or phenomena, especially as considered together in physical chemistry.

Examples

Physicochemical investigations of anionic surfactants, including the soaps, have shown that there is little polymerization or agglomeration of the chain anions below a certain region of concentration called the critical micelle concentration.

Etymology

Formed from 'physical' and 'chemical', denoting the intersection of both sciences.
