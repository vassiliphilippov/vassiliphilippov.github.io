fiercely

1. ADV

/ ˈfɪrsli / (AmE), / ˈfɪəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows intense force, aggression, or strong emotion.

Examples

[WHISPERING FIERCELY]

Synset Examples

silence broken by dogs barking ferociously
they fought fiercely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ferociously - in a savagely fierce or violent manner

Etymology

From 'fierce' + '-ly'; 'fierce' from Old French *fers*, from Latin *ferus* (wild).
