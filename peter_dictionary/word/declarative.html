declarative

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈklær.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈklær.ətɪv / (BrE)
Relating to or having the nature of a declaration; making a statement or declaration, especially in grammar (as in a declarative sentence).

Examples

In Mack's the leader at camp, but Jack is here the is of the second main declarative represents is the leader and therefore has stress.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: interrogative - asking a question; opposite of declarative in grammar

Etymology

From Latin declarativus (making clear), from declarare (to declare).
