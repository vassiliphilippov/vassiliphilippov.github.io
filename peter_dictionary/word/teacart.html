teacart

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈtiːkɑːt / (BrE)
a small cart with wheels, used for serving tea or light refreshments

Examples

While she was settling the teacart, he went back across the hall to their bedroom, opened one of the suitcases, and took out powdered coffee and sugar.
She appeared with the teacart and he opened the windows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trolley - a wheeled cart for carrying items
  • Synonym: serving cart - a cart used to serve food or drinks

Etymology

Formed from 'tea' and 'cart'.
