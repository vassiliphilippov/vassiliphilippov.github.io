unattractive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (BrE)
Not appealing or interesting; lacking the ability to arouse interest or attention.

Examples

The large land areas required for oxidation ponds made this type of treatment financially unattractive to the developer.

Synset Examples

being unemployed is a most unattractive prospect

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attractive - pleasing or interesting; having qualities to arouse interest

Etymology

From un- (not) + attractive, from Latin attrahere (to draw toward).

2. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (BrE)
Not pleasing to look at; lacking beauty or charm.

Examples

She had a dried-out quality - a gray, lean woman, not unattractive.

Synset Examples

as unattractive as most mining regions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attractive - pleasing or appealing to the senses
  • Degree Lower: plain - not beautiful or attractive in appearance

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'attractive', from Latin 'attrahere' (to draw towards).
