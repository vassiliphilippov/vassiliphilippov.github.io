anti-Semitic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæntɪ səˈmɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌænti səˈmɪtɪk / (BrE)
characterized by or relating to hostility toward or discrimination against Jews

Etymology

From anti-, meaning 'against', and Semite, referring to a person of Semitic descent, especially Jews; popularized in late 19th century.
