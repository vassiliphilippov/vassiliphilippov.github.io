exhausted

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzɔstɪd / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd / (BrE)
Extremely tired; drained of energy or strength.

Examples

It sometimes ended in death-like trances with many lying exhausted and panting on chair and floor.
The women said they had seen him wave an exhausted farewell; but he might have been shooing away the fleas that hopped from his yellow dog onto him.
Beaten, bruised and exhausted, he pursues the elusive killer through the demi-monde of high society and low morals, always alone, always despised.

Synset Examples

the day's shopping left her exhausted
he went to bed dog-tired
was fagged and sweaty
the trembling of his played out limbs
felt completely washed-out
only worn-out horses and cattle
you look worn out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatigued - physically or mentally tired
  • Antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic again
  • Degree Lower: tired - in need of rest or sleep
  • Synonym: spent - completely used up or drained

Etymology

From Latin exhaustus, past participle of exhaurire (to drain out).
