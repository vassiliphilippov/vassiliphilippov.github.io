Dipylon

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈpaɪlən / (AmE), / dɪˈpɪlɒn / (BrE)
Of or relating to the Dipylon, a gateway on the west side of ancient Athens, or to the artistic styles and artifacts associated with it, such as Dipylon vases.

Examples

The antecedents of Dipylon vases and of the Iliad lie in the Aegean past.

Etymology

From Greek 'Dipylon', meaning 'double gate' (di- 'two' + pylon 'gate').
