raucously

1. ADV

/ ˈrɔːkəsli / (AmE), / ˈrɔːkəsli / (BrE)
In a loud, harsh, and strident manner.

Examples

Hearing his voice ring raucously up from the road, Kate would await him anxiously and watch perplexed as he walked into the house, cold sober.

Synset Examples

his voice rang raucously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noisily - in a loud or noisy manner
  • Antonym: softly - in a quiet or gentle manner

Etymology

From 'raucous' + -ly; 'raucous' from Latin 'raucus' (hoarse, harsh).
