concertina

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑnsərˈtinə / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsəˈtiːnə / (BrE)
A coil of barbed wire used as an obstacle, especially in military or security settings.

Examples

At the slope's base coils of concertina stretched out of eye range like a wild tangle of children's hoops, stopped simultaneously, weirdly poised as if awaiting the magic of the child's touch to start them all rolling again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barbed wire - strong wire with barbs used for fencing

Etymology

From Italian concertina (diminutive of concerto), likely referring to its resemblance to the musical instrument 'concertina.'
