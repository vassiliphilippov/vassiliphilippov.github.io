divinity

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɪnəti / (AmE), / dɪˈvɪnəti / (BrE)
A god or goddess; a supernatural being worshipped as controlling some part of the world or some aspect of life.

Examples

If, however, it would help to intensify your anguish, I can delimit the powers of a few of the divinities you 've affronted and describe the punishment they meted out in one analogous instance.
"No divinity will hold still for that.
Now a distinguished old man called on nine divinities to come and join us.
With the divinities present and our souls in place, we were wished health, happiness, and power.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deity - a god or goddess; a divine being
  • Synonym: god - a male deity; a supernatural being worshipped as divine
  • Synonym: goddess - a female deity; a woman who is worshipped as a god
  • Antonym: mortal - a human being; contrasted with a divine being

Etymology

From Latin divinitas (divine nature, deity), from divinus (divine).
