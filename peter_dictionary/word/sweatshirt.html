sweatshirt

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtˌʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈswɛtʃɜːt / (BrE)
A loose, warm cotton knit pullover with long sleeves, typically worn during athletic activity or for casual comfort.

Examples

After the game, Phil was taking off his sweatshirt in the dressing room when Mike Deegan came in.
Phil sighed and pulled the wet sweatshirt over his head.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoodie - a sweatshirt with a hood
  • Synonym: sweater - a knitted garment typically with long sleeves worn over the upper body

Etymology

Formed from 'sweat' + 'shirt'. Originally denoting athletic wear for absorbing sweat.
