priming

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of preparing or making something ready, especially for use or action

Examples

I wanted to wipe my flint, but I did n't dare to, the state my hands were in, just as I did n't dare to do anything about the priming.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preparation - the action or process of making ready or being made ready for use or consideration

Etymology

From the verb 'prime', from Middle French 'primer', from Latin 'primus' (first).
