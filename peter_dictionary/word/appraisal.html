appraisal

1. NOUN

/ əˈpreɪzəl / (AmE), / əˈpreɪzəl / (BrE)
the act of assessing or evaluating the worth, value, or quality of someone or something

Examples

Only within the framework of a mature relationship characterized by honest appraisals of performance can we provide telling assistance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assessment - the act of making a judgment about something
  • Synonym: evaluation - the making of a judgment about the amount, number, or value of something

Etymology

From late 16th-century French *appraisal* (now *appréciation*), from *appraise* + -al.
