sowbelly

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊˌbɛli / (AmE), / ˈsəʊˌbɛli / (BrE)
Salt pork taken from the belly of a pig, often used in traditional American cooking.

Examples

It is sowbelly with tits on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salt pork - pork preserved with salt, often from the belly or sides of the pig
  • Synonym: bacon - cured meat from the sides or back of a pig

Etymology

From 'sow' (an adult female pig) + 'belly', referring to pork from a pig's belly.
