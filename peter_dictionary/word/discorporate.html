discorporate

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈkɔr.pɚ.ət / (AmE), / dɪsˈkɔː.pər.ət / (BrE)
(of a being or entity) lacking a physical or material body; incorporeal.

Examples

Mike stopped to cherish all his brother selves, the many threes-fulfilled on Mars, corporate and discorporate, the precious few on Earth - the unknown powers of three on Earth that would be his to merge with and cherish now that at last long waiting he grokked and cherished himself.

Synset Examples

bodiless ghosts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorporeal - lacking a physical body; not composed of matter
  • Synonym: bodiless - having no physical body

Etymology

From Latin dis- (apart) + corpus (body).
