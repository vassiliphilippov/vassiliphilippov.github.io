Negroid

1. ADJ

/ ˈniːˌɡrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈniːˌɡrɔɪd / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of people traditionally classified as belonging to the so-called Negro race; now considered outdated and offensive.

Examples

The Negro composer Hall Johnson studied the Americanegro Suite and said of it, "Of all the many songs written by white composers and employing what claims to be a Negroid idiom in both words and music, these six songs by Harold Arlen and Ted Koehler easily stand far out above the rest.

Synset Examples

negroid features

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Caucasoid - outdated term for people of European descent, perceived as a racial category
  • Synonym: Mongoloid - outdated term for people of East Asian descent, perceived as a racial category

Etymology

From French *négroïde*, from *nègre* ('Negro') + -oïde ('-oid', resembling).
