custom

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌstəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəm / (BrE)
A traditional and widely accepted way of behaving or doing something that is specific to a particular society, place, or time.

Examples

It has been the custom for most universities to stretch the blanket of accreditation for their liberal arts school to cover the shivering body of their fine arts department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tradition - a custom or belief passed down through generations
  • Synonym: habit - a regular practice or custom, especially one that is hard to give up

Etymology

From Latin 'consuetudinem' (custom, habit), via Old French 'costume'.
