pug-nosed

1. ADJ

/ pʌɡ ˈnoʊzd / (AmE), / pʌɡ ˈnəʊzd / (BrE)
Having a nose that is short and turned up or flattened, resembling that of a pug dog.

Examples

Cause of all this commotion: squat, pug-nosed, balding, hopelessly ugly Jean-Pierre Bravado, a Bogartian figure, who plays a sadistic, amoral, philosophic Tasti-Freeze salesman in old New-Waver Fredrico de Mille Rossilini's endlessly provocative film, A Sour Sponge.

Synset Examples

a pug-nosed boy with freckles
a snub-nosed automatic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snub-nosed - having a short, turned-up nose

Etymology

From 'pug' (a breed of dog with a flattened nose) + 'nosed'.
