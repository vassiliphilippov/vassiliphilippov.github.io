swami

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈswɑːmi / (BrE)
A Hindu religious teacher or spiritual guide, often used as a title of respect.

Examples

A Tibetan swami from Palermo, Sicily, announced in Beverly Hills a newly discovered, ancient yoga discipline for ripple breathing which increased both pranha and cosmic attraction between sexes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guru - A spiritual teacher, especially in Hinduism

Etymology

From Sanskrit svāmī (master, lord, owner), also used as a title of respect in Hindu culture.
