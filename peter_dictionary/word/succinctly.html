succinctly

1. ADV

/ səkˈsɪŋktli / (AmE), / səkˈsɪŋktli / (BrE)
In a brief and clear manner; expressed with few words and without unnecessary detail.

Examples

Smaller systems can of course be stated much more succinctly.

Synset Examples

Please state your case as succinctly as possible
he wrote compactly but clearly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concisely - in a way that is brief but comprehensive; without unnecessary details
  • Synonym: tersely - in a way that uses few words, sometimes seeming unfriendly or abrupt
  • Antonym: verbosely - in a way that uses more words than needed; wordily

Etymology

From Latin 'succinctus', past participle of 'succingere' (to gird from below).
