postmark

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊstˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌmɑːk / (BrE)
an official mark stamped on a letter or package by a post office to show the date and place of mailing

Examples

Juanita drooped about the place, wearing a haunted, brooding look, which Kate attributed to the baby's death, until the day a letter came for her addressed to "Miss Juanita Fitzroy ", bearing a Grafton postmark.
It was blank but the postmark said...

Etymology

From 'post' (relating to mail) + 'mark'.
