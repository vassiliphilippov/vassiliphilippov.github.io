atheist

1. NOUN

/ ˈeɪθiɪst / (AmE), / ˈeɪθɪɪst / (BrE)
A person who does not believe in the existence of God or gods.

Examples

Or a Protestant, or one of those amusing dogmatic atheists, or a strict orthodox Communist.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: agnostic - a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God
  • Antonym: theist - a person who believes in the existence of God or gods

Etymology

From Greek 'atheos' (without god), where 'a-' means 'without' and 'theos' means 'god'.
