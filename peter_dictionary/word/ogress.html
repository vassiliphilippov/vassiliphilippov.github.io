ogress

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊɡrəs / (AmE), / ˈəʊɡrɛs / (BrE)
(in folklore) a female ogre, typically depicted as a large, hideous, man-eating giantess.

Examples

The stepmother, almost without exception, has been presented as a cruel ogress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ogre - (in folklore) a man-eating giant; a cruel or terrifying person

Etymology

From French 'ogresse', feminine of 'ogre'.
