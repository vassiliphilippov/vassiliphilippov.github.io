farmer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrmər / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
A person who owns or manages a farm, especially as a main occupation.

Examples

If, in Larkin's eyes, they are nothing but Piccadilly farmers, he has as much to learn about them as they have to learn about the ways of truly rural living.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agriculturist - a person skilled in agriculture; farm expert
  • Synonym: rancher - a person who owns or manages a ranch

Etymology

From Middle English 'fermer', from Anglo-French 'fermer', from Old French 'fermier', related to 'farm'.
