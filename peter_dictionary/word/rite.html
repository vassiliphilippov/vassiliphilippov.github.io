rite

1. NOUN

/ raɪt / (AmE), / raɪt / (BrE)
a formal or ceremonial act or procedure prescribed or customary in religious or other solemn practices

Examples

The religious symbolism, and especially the closely related rites and worship forms, constitute a powerful bond for the members of the particular faith.

Synset Examples

the rite of baptism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ritual - a series of actions or type of behavior regularly and invariably followed, especially as part of a ceremony

Etymology

From Latin ritus (religious observance, ceremony).
