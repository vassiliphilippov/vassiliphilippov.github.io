/ ˈɑːr.bəˌtrɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈɑː.bɪ.trəri / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: random - made, done, or happening without method or conscious decision

  • Antonym: reasoned - based on or presented with careful reasoning or logic

Etymology
