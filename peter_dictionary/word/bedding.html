bedding

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
cloth coverings such as sheets, blankets, or quilts placed on a bed for warmth or comfort

Examples

Moving around camp we saw all kinds of camp stoves, lanterns, coolers, bedding, games, fishing tackle, windbreaks and sunshades.
They bring their families and tents and camp kitchens and bedding.
Warm clothing and bedding, of course, are essential.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedclothes - coverings, such as sheets and blankets, used on a bed

Etymology

From Old English 'bedding', meaning 'a bed or lodging', from 'bed' + '-ing'.
