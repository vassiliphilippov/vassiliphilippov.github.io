Henry Ford

1. PROPN

/ ˈhɛnri fɔrd / (AmE), / ˈhɛnri fɔːd / (BrE)
(1863–1947) an American industrialist and founder of the Ford Motor Company, best known for developing assembly line production techniques and making automobiles affordable for many.
