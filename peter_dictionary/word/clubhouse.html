clubhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌbˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈklʌbˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building used by members of a club, especially a sports, social, or recreational club, for meetings and other activities.

Examples

"Spahnie does n't know how to merely go through the motions ", remarked Enos Slaughter, another all-out guy, who played rightfield that day and popped one over the clubhouse.
"It was a nice round figure, that 12 ", he said as he headed for the clubhouse, not too much perturbed.

Synset Examples

the clubhouse needed a new roof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: club - an association or organization of people with a common interest

Etymology

Club + house; 'club' meaning association or society, combined with 'house' for a building.
