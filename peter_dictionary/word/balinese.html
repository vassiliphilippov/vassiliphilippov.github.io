Balinese

1. NOUN

/ ˌbæləˈniz / (AmE), / ˌbæləˈniːz / (BrE)
the Austronesian language spoken by the people of Bali, Indonesia

Examples

Inscribed around its base is a charm in Balinese, a dialect I take it you do n't comprehend.

Etymology

From Bali (the island in Indonesia) + -nese (denoting language or people).
