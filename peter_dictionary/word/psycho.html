psycho

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkəʊ / (BrE)
an offensive term for a person suffering from a severe mental disorder, especially psychosis; used informally to describe someone deemed mentally unstable or dangerously unpredictable

Examples

I answer to Grissom, not to psychos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychotic - a person suffering from psychosis (medical/technical term)

Etymology

Shortened from 'psychotic', influenced by 'psychopath'.
