agilely

1. ADV

/ ˈædʒəli / (AmE), / ˈadʒaɪlli / (BrE)
In a nimble, quick, and light manner; with ease and dexterity.

Examples

Katie scrambled to her feet, Stevie agilely retaining his seat.

Synset Examples

nimbly scaling an iron gate
leaped agilely from roof to roof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nimbly - in a nimble and quick manner
