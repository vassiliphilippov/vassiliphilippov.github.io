overtly

1. ADV

/ oʊˈvɜrtli / (AmE), / əʊˈvɜːtli / (BrE)
In a manner that is open and not secret or hidden; openly.

Examples

Today Northern Vietnam is overtly Communist; Laos is teetering between Communism and pro Communist neutralism; Cambodia is, for all practical purposes, neutralist.

Synset Examples

he did it overtly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: openly - in an open manner; not hidden or secret
  • Antonym: covertly - in a concealed or secret manner

Etymology

From 'overt' (open, evident) + -ly.
