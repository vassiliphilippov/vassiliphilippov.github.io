tenfold

1. ADV

/ ˈtɛnˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.fəʊld / (BrE)
By ten times as much; increased or multiplied by a factor of ten.

Examples

Since the concept of high fidelity became important some dozen years ago, the claims of technical improvements have multiplied tenfold.
He'’s ten times tougher than me, my brother.

Synset Examples

the population increased tenfold

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ten times - by a factor of ten
  • Degree Lower: twofold - by two times as much
  • Degree Higher: hundredfold - by one hundred times as much

Etymology

Formed from 'ten' + 'fold' (meaning multiplied).
