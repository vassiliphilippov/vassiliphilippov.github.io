frowningly

1. ADV

/ ˈfraʊnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfraʊnɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner showing or marked by a frown; with a frown.

Examples

He does n't want her to look frowningly at him, or speak to him angrily.

Synset Examples

he looked at her frowningly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: angrily - in an angry manner
  • Antonym: smilingly - with a smile; in a smiling manner

Etymology

From 'frowning' (present participle of 'frown') + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
