presentness

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛzəntnəs / (AmE), / ˈprɛzntnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being present; the condition of existing or occurring in the present time.

Examples

For this does not account for the integral, elemental power of that which grows with abounding vigor as the play unfolds, nor does it explain the strange numinous sense of presentness which comes over those who watch the play like a spell.

Synset Examples

a study of the pastness of the present and...of the presentness of the past

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pastness - the quality or state of being in the past
  • Synonym: presence - the fact or state of being present

Etymology

Formed from 'present' + noun suffix '-ness'.
