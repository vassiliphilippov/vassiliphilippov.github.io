overindulge

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒ / (BrE)
To eat or drink excessively or immoderately; to give oneself too much of something, especially food or drink.

Examples

Altogether he had, since the seizure, the appearance of a boy who overindulged in food and took no exercise.

Synset Examples

She stuffed herself at the dinner
The kids binged on ice cream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gorge - to eat a lot in a greedy way
  • Synonym: binge - to indulge in an activity, especially eating or drinking, excessively

Etymology

From over- (excessively) + indulge (from Latin indulgere, to be kind or yielding).
