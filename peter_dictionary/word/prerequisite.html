prerequisite

1. NOUN

/ priˈrɛkwəzɪt / (AmE), / priˈrɛkwɪzɪt / (BrE)
a thing that is required as a prior condition for something else to happen or exist

Examples

Lousiness is a prerequisite for epidemic typhus.

Synset Examples

Latin was a prerequisite for admission

Related Terms

  • Synonym: requirement - something that is needed or demanded

Etymology

From Latin 'prærequīsitus', past participle of 'prærequīrere' meaning 'to require beforehand'.
