spineless

1. ADJ

/ ˈspaɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnləs / (BrE)
Lacking courage, determination, or strength of character; weak-willed or timid.

Examples

Curt wanted to get Jess alone, without interference from anyone, even as spineless a person as the store owner.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: courageous - having or showing courage
  • Synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened

Etymology

From 'spine' (backbone) + '-less', referring metaphorically to lacking a backbone (i.e., lacking courage or resolve).
