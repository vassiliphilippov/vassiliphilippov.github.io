generously

1. ADV

/ ˈdʒɛn.ə.rəs.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.ər.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a willingness to give more of something, especially money or time, than is strictly necessary or expected.

Examples

The legation was generously set back from the building line; if the angle of fire were too great the jutting buildings on either side would interfere.
"In addition, the Library District has generously responded with a charitable donation of used books and sundries.

Synset Examples

he gave liberally to several charities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liberally - in a generous or plentiful manner

Etymology

From generous + -ly.
