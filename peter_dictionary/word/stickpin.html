stickpin

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪkˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈstɪkˌpɪn / (BrE)
A decorative pin designed to be worn in a necktie, often for ornamental purposes.

Examples

In fact, O' Connor was honoured during the ceremony with the presentation of a $ 2500 diamond stickpin.

Etymology

Formed from 'stick' + 'pin', indicating a pin designed to be stuck through fabric.
