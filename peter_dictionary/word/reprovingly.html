reprovingly

1. ADV

/ rɪˈpruvɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈpruːvɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner showing disapproval or criticism; reproachfully.

Examples

"A public of one ", Mary echoed reprovingly.

Synset Examples

she spoke to him reprovingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reproachfully - in a reproachful or disapproving manner

Etymology

From "reproving" (present participle of "reprove") + "-ly".
