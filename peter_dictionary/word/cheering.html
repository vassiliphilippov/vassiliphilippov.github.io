cheering

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving encouragement or support by shouting or applauding, especially as done by a group of spectators

Related Terms

  • Synonym: applause - the sound of people clapping to show approval or praise
  • Antonym: booing - the act of showing disapproval by loudly shouting 'boo'

Etymology

From 'cheer' (Middle English) meaning shout of encouragement, from Old French 'chier', from Latin 'cara' (face, expression).
