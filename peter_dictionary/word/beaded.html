beaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪd / (BrE)
covered with small droplets or beads of liquid, such as sweat or condensation

Examples

His face took on a sudden pallor, became beaded with sweat, and he seemed to have trouble with his breathing.

Synset Examples

a face beaded with sweat

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: drenched - completely soaked with liquid
  • Degree Lower: moist - slightly wet or damp

Etymology

From the noun 'bead,' referring to a small round object, + -ed, forming an adjective meaning 'covered with beads (of liquid).'
