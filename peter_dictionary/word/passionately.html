passionately

1. ADV

/ ˈpæʃənətli / (AmE), / ˈpæʃənətli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or involves strong feelings or intense emotion.

Examples

She kissed him passionately upon his reappearance.
He who had tried so hard, who had yearned so passionately to be a great officer.

Synset Examples

she kissed him passionately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fervently - in a very enthusiastic or passionate way
  • Antonym: calmly - in a way that shows little or no emotion

Etymology

From 'passionate' + '-ly'; 'passionate' from Late Latin passionatus (suffering, full of passion).
