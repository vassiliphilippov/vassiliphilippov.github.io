godlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑːdˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or appropriate to a god in qualities such as power, beauty, or majesty.

Examples

Until a few weeks ago, however, Arnold Palmer was some god-like creature who had nothing in common with the duffers.

Synset Examples

the divine strength of Achilles
a man of godlike sagacity
man must play God for he has acquired certain godlike powers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divine - of, from, or like a god; godly
  • Antonym: mortal - subject to death; not divine

Etymology

Formed from 'god' + 'like', in the sense of similar to a god.
