protected

1. ADJ

/ prəˈtɛktɪd / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪd / (BrE)
kept safe from danger, harm, or injury; defended or guarded against possible threats or loss.

Examples

Here we are, protected.

Synset Examples

the most protected spot I could find

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unprotected - not protected; exposed to danger or harm
