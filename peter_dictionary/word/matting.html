matting

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmætɪŋ / (BrE)
a floor or surface covering made of coarse material such as straw, hemp, or similar fibers, used to protect or insulate surfaces

Examples

The side slopes were coated with fiberglas matting coated with asphalt to prevent erosion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mat - a piece of coarse material placed on a floor or other surface

Etymology

From 'mat' + -ing, indicating material or action related to mats.
