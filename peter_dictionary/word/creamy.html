creamy

1. ADJ

/ ˈkriːmi / (AmE), / ˈkriːmi / (BrE)
Having a pale yellowish-white color resembling cream.

Examples

We collected "lucky stones "- all the creamy translucent pebbles, worn smooth and round, that we could find in the driveway.

Synset Examples

creamy translucent pebbles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ivory - a pale, off-white color resembling the material ivory
  • Synonym: off-white - a color that is white with a slight tint of another color
  • Degree Lower: white - the color of fresh milk or snow
  • Degree Higher: yellowish - tinged slightly with yellow

Etymology

From 'cream' + '-y', meaning 'resembling cream' in appearance or color.
