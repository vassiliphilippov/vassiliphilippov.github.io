aloneness

1. NOUN

/ əˈloʊn.nəs / (AmE), / əˈləʊn.nəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being alone; solitude or isolation, often with an emphasis on the emotional or psychological aspects of being by oneself.

Examples

This place was hatred to him, just as hatred was his only companion in his aloneness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solitude - the state of being alone, often by choice
  • Synonym: loneliness - sadness or unhappiness arising from being alone

Etymology

From 'alone' + '-ness', forming a noun indicating state or condition.
