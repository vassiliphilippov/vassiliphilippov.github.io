Acapulco

1. PROPN

/ ˌækəˈpʊlkoʊ / (AmE), / ˌakəˈpʊlkəʊ / (BrE)
A port and popular resort city on the Pacific coast of southern Mexico, famous for its beaches and water sports.

Examples

That night the two of them left for a week's honeymoon in Acapulco.

Etymology

From Spanish Acapulco, from Nahuatl *Acatl-polco* meaning 'place of reeds'.
