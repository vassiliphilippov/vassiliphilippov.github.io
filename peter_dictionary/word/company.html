company

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A business organization that produces or sells goods or services for profit.

Examples

Synset Examples

he only invests in large well-established companies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corporation - a large business or organization authorized to act as a single entity
  • Synonym: firm - a business, especially one involving a partnership of owners

Etymology

From Old French *compaignie* (society, body of soldiers), from *compaignon* (companion). Later applied in English to businesses in the 16th century.
