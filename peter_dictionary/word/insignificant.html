insignificant

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnsɪgˈnɪfɪkənt / (AmE), / ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt / (BrE)
Too small or unimportant to be worth consideration or notice.

Examples

"I saw Pemberton and he is the most insignificant puke I ever saw.
Here in the God-forsaken place, the westerly end of nowhere, Tom Lord looked almost insignificant, almost contemptible.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: significant - important; worthy of attention

Etymology

From Latin insignificans, insignificantis, meaning 'not significant'.
