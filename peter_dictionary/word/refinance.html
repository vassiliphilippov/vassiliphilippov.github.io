refinance

1. VERB

/ ˌriːˈfaɪnæns, riːˈfaɪnæns / (AmE), / ˌriːˈfaɪnæns, riːˈfaɪnɑːns / (BrE)
to obtain new financing or loans to replace or restructure an existing debt, often with different terms

Examples

You can n't tell a customer how much it's going to cost him to refinance his payments before he even signs for a loan on the money down!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finance - to provide funding for; to supply with capital

Etymology

From re- (again) + finance (from French financer, from finance meaning payment).
