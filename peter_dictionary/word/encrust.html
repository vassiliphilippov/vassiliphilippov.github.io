encrust

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈkrʌst / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrʌst / (BrE)
to cover or coat something with a hard, outer layer or crust

Examples

Above the tongue, an aged yellow eye, sallow and time-cast, encrusted within a sphere of marbleized pink skin, stared unfalteringly at him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coat - to cover something with a layer of a substance
  • Synonym: crust - the hard outer surface or layer of something

Etymology

From Latin 'crusta' (a crust, shell) via French 'encruster'.
