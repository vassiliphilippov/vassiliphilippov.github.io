worriedly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɝːidli / (AmE), / ˈwʊərɪdli / (BrE)
In a worried manner; showing or expressing anxiety or concern.

Examples

Jess asked worriedly.

Synset Examples

`I wonder what to do,' she said worriedly
he paused worriedly before calling the bank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anxiously - in a manner resulting from anxiety or unease
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm, untroubled manner

Etymology

From 'worried' + '-ly', where 'worried' ultimately derives from Old English 'wyrgan' (to strangle, to harass).
