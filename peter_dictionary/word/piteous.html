piteous

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪtiəs / (AmE), / ˈpɪtiəs / (BrE)
deserving or causing feelings of pity; arousing compassion or sympathy

Examples

Shadow, the more talkative, began at once to talk, her voice piteous.

Synset Examples

a hapless victim
miserable victims of war
the shabby room struck her as extraordinarily pathetic
piteous appeals for help
pitiable homeless children
a pitiful fate
Oh, you poor thing
his poor distorted limbs
a wretched life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitiable - deserving pity; arousing compassion
  • Synonym: pitiful - causing feelings of pity or compassion
  • Synonym: pathetic - arousing pity, especially through vulnerability or sadness

Etymology

From Old French *piteus*, from Latin *pietās* (piety, compassion).
