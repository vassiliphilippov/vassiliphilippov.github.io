mileage

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the total distance measured in miles, especially as recorded by a vehicle or travelled over a period

Examples

Three seconds flat is the usual time, and the space is crossed by moderate mileage, while the overwhelming immensity of such journeys must be conceived as a static pulsation through an enormous number of coexistent frequencies which perpetuate all events.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distance - the amount of space between two points

Etymology

Formed from 'mile' + suffix '-age', modeled after similar terms such as 'poundage'.
