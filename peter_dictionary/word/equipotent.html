equipotent

1. ADJ

/ ˌiːkwɪˈpoʊtənt / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈpəʊtənt / (BrE)
Having equal power, strength, or effect; equally effective or potent.

Examples

The international unit is equipotent with the USP unit adopted in 1952, which was defined as the amount of activity present in 20 mg of the USP reference substance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: potent - having great power, influence, or effect

Etymology

From Latin 'aequipotens', from 'aequus' (equal) + 'potens' (powerful).
