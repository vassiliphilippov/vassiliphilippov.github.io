surf

1. NOUN

/ sɝf / (AmE), / sɜːf / (BrE)
Waves of the sea as they break upon a shore or reef.

Examples

An area on the coast may have relaxing views of the surf rolling in on a beach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breaker - a wave that breaks into foam on the shore

Etymology

From obsolete 'suff', of uncertain origin, perhaps related to 'surge'.
