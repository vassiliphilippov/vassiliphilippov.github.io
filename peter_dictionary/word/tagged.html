tagged

1. ADJ

/ tæɡd / (AmE), / tæɡd / (BrE)
Marked or labeled with a tag or label to indicate identity or other information.

Examples

Of several methods employed for tagging chlorine with radiochlorine, the exchange of inactive chlorine with tagged aluminum chloride at room temperature was found to be the most satisfactory.

Synset Examples

properly labeled luggage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: labeled - bearing or marked with a label

Etymology

From the verb 'tag' (to attach a label), with past participle ending -ed.
