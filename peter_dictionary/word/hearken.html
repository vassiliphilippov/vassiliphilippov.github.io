hearken

1. VERB

/ ˈhɑrkən / (AmE), / ˈhɑːkən / (BrE)
to listen attentively; to give careful attention, especially as a command or request

Examples

Listen, Hagen, I did not... TOM: I know.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: listen - to give attention with the ear

Etymology

From Old English heorcnian (to listen).
