touchy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʌtʃi / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃi / (BrE)
easily offended or upset; quick to take offense.

Examples

You're taking this very **personal**.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensitive - easily affected by criticism or insult
  • Antonym: thick-skinned - not easily offended or upset

Etymology

From 'touch' + -y, originally meaning 'sensitive to touch' and later 'delicate in temperament'.
