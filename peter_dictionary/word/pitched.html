pitched

1. ADJ

/ pɪtʃt / (AmE), / pɪtʃt / (BrE)
(of a sound) having a specified pitch or key, often used in combination (e.g., high-pitched, low-pitched).

Examples

Like the bell at Mass, the doorbell was pitched too high.
"Deegan ", the manager said, his voice pitched low, quivering.

Synset Examples

high-pitched

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: high-pitched - having a high pitch
  • Degree Lower: low-pitched - having a low pitch
