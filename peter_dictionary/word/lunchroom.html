lunchroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌntʃˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈlʌntʃˌruːm / (BrE)
A room or restaurant, typically within a school, office, or other facility, where people can buy and eat lunch.

Examples

However, the litter remained, augmented by several dozen lunchroom suppers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cafeteria - a self-service restaurant in a school, hospital, etc.

Etymology

From 'lunch' + 'room', meaning a room designated for eating lunch.
