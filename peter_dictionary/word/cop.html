cop

1. NOUN

/ kɑp / (AmE), / kɒp / (BrE)
an informal or slang term for a police officer

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: policeman - a male police officer
  • Synonym: police officer - a member of a police force

Etymology

Perhaps from Latin 'capere' (to seize) via obsolete English 'cop' (to catch, arrest) or from earlier slang for 'to capture', but exact origin uncertain.
