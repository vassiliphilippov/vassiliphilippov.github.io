Britannic

1. ADJ

/ brɪˈtænɪk / (AmE), / brɪˈtænɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to Britain or the British people.

Examples

This is the only case in modern history of a people of Britannic origin submitting without continued struggle to what they view as foreign domination.

Synset Examples

Her Britannic Majesty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: British - of or relating to Great Britain or its people

Etymology

From Latin 'Britannicus', from 'Britannia' (Britain).
