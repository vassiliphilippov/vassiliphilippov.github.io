digitalization

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the administration of digitalis, especially to treat certain heart disorders

Examples

Therapy included digitalization and anticoagulation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: digitalis - a drug prepared from foxglove plants used to treat heart conditions

Etymology

From 'digitalis' (a genus of plants and the name of a heart medication) + '-ization'.
