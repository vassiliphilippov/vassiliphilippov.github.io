milquetoast

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlkˌtoʊst / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌtəʊst / (BrE)
A person who is timid, submissive, or lacking in assertiveness or courage.

Examples

There is no evidence that these Milquetoasts became suddenly emboldened when they crossed the threshhold of the master bedroom.
In his stead is a milquetoast version known as "the corporation ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sissy - a man or boy regarded as effeminate or cowardly
  • Synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • Antonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those they perceive as vulnerable
  • Antonym: brave - having or showing courage

Etymology

From the character Caspar Milquetoast in the comic strip 'The Timid Soul' by H.T. Webster (1924).
