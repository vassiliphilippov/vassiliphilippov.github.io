molding

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of shaping a material or object by means of a mold or casting

Examples

This seems somewhat shortsighted since if the absolute educational qualifications for membership which the organizations profess are ever enforced, the educator will have the molding of the entire profession in his hands.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casting - the act of shaping material by pouring it into a mold

Etymology

From 'mold' (to shape), itself from Old French 'moule' and Latin 'modulus' (measure, model).
