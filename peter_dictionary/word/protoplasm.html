protoplasm

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ.təˌplæz.əm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.təˌplæz.əm / (BrE)
the jelly-like, living substance inside a cell, including the cytoplasm and nucleus, in which all life processes occur

Etymology
