euphoric

1. ADJ

/ juˈfɔrɪk / (AmE), / juˈfɒrɪk / (BrE)
feeling or expressing intense excitement, happiness, or well-being

Examples

In the past year there had been too many examples of his euphoric self-confidence and self aggrandisement for their liking.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness
  • Synonym: elated - marked by high spirits or joy
  • Antonym: depressed - feeling severe despondency and dejection
  • Degree Lower: happy - feeling or showing pleasure or contentment
  • Degree Higher: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joyful excitement; a higher degree than 'euphoric' in some contexts

Etymology

From Greek 'euphoros', meaning 'healthy, bearing well'.
