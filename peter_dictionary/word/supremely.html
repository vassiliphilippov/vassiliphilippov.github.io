supremely

1. ADV

/ suˈprim.li / (AmE), / suˈpriːm.li / (BrE)
To the highest possible degree; extremely.

Examples

See, he's already snapping it up, the dumb jerk * * h Coughlin grinned, feeling supremely on top of things * * h He watched the snow once again.

Synset Examples

he was supremely confident

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • Synonym: utterly - completely or absolutely

Etymology

From "supreme" + "-ly"; ultimately from Latin "supremus" (highest, most elevated).
