purveyor

1. NOUN

/ pɚˈveɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / pɜːˈveɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person or company that supplies or sells food, goods, or services, especially in a regular or official capacity

Examples

The phonograph today, for all its high fidelity and stereophonic sound, is precisely what the early art purveyors in the movies wished to make of the camera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supplier - a person or company that provides goods or services

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman *purveour*, from Old French *porveor*, from *porveoir* (to provide).
