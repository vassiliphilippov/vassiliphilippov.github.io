rotation

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / rəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
The action of turning around a central point or axis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revolution - the movement of an object around another object or a central point
  • Synonym: spin - to turn around quickly on an axis or centre
  • Antonym: translation - the act of moving something from one place to another without rotation

Etymology

From Latin 'rotatio' (a turning), from 'rotare' (to turn, rotate).
