Related Terms

2. NOUN

/ næp / (AmE), / næp / (BrE)
a short period of sleep, especially during the day

Examples

I'’m going in to take a nap.

Synset Examples

he felt better after a little sleep
there wasn't time for a nap

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: sleep - the natural periodic state of rest for the mind and body

Etymology

Probably from Middle English *nappen*, of Germanic origin; related to Old High German *hnapfen* (to doze).
