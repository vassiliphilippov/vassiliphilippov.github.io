canary

1. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛri / (AmE), / kəˈnɛːri / (BrE)
(dated, slang) A female singer, especially one who performs in nightclubs or jazz bands.

Examples

This is where the canary is, Johnny.

Etymology

Named after the canary bird, known for its singing ability.

2. NOUN

/ kəˈnɛri / (AmE), / kəˈneəri / (BrE)
(slang) An informer; a person who gives information to the police, especially in a criminal context.

Examples

Where's the canary?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snitch - (slang) A person who informs on others to the police
  • Synonym: fink - (slang) A person who betrays or informs on others

Etymology

Slang use possibly relating to the idea of a 'singing bird' revealing secrets, from the songbird 'canary'.
