givenness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvənnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvənnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being assumed, accepted, or taken for granted as a basis for reasoning or discussion.

Examples

One characteristic of the spirit in community is its givenness.

Etymology

From 'given' + '-ness', denoting the state or quality of being given or accepted.
