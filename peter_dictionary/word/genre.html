genre

1. NOUN

/ ˈʒɑnrə / (AmE), / ˈʒɒ̃rə / (BrE)
A category of artistic composition, as in music or literature, characterized by similarities in form, style, or subject matter.

Examples

But his facility in this genre was not great.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: type - a kind or category of thing
  • Synonym: form - particular kind or variety of literary or artistic work

Etymology

From French 'genre' (kind, style), from Latin 'genus, generis' (race, kind, sort).
