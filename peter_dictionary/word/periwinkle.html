periwinkle

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛrɪˌwɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɪˌwɪŋkəl / (BrE)
a trailing evergreen plant with glossy leaves and blue or purple star-shaped flowers, chiefly of the genus Vinca, some species of which are poisonous

Examples

Already the jonquils were blooming in a flock by the front gate, and the periwinkles were coming on, blue by the porch steps.

Etymology

From Old English *perwince*, from Late Latin *pervinca* (the plant).
