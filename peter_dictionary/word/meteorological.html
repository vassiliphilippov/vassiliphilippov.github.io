meteorological

1. ADJ

/ ˌmiːtiərəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the science of the atmosphere and weather or its phenomena.

Examples

In considering BW defense, it must be recognized that a number of critical meteorological parameters must be met for an aerosol to exhibit optimum effect.

Synset Examples

meteorological factors
meteorological chart
meteoric (or meteorological) phenomena

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meteorology - the scientific study of the atmosphere and weather

Etymology

From French 'météorologique', from Greek 'meteōrologikos' (concerning things in the air).
