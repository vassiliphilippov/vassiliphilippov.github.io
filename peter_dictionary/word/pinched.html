pinched

1. ADJ

/ pɪntʃt / (AmE), / pɪntʃt / (BrE)
(of a voice or sound) having a constricted, nasal quality, as if the nose were squeezed together.

Examples

The violinist, in particular, is very indulgent with swoops and slides, and his tone is pinched and edgy.

Synset Examples

a whining nasal voice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nasal - (of sound) pronounced with the nose, often with a twangy quality
  • Antonym: clear - (of a voice or sound) easy to hear and not nasal or muffled
