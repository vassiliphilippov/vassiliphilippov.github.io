reviewer

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈvjuːər / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuːə / (BrE)
A person who examines and judges written works, performances, products, etc., often for publication.

Examples

American newspaper reviewers like to call his plays nihilistic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: critic - a person who judges the merits of literary or artistic works

Etymology

From 'review' (to examine or assess) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
