chopper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑpər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpə / (BrE)
(Baseball) A batted ground ball that bounces high in the air.

Examples

The Twins tied the score in the sixth inning when Reno Bertoia beat out a high chopper to third base and scored on Lenny Green's double to left.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grounder - a batted baseball hit along the ground
