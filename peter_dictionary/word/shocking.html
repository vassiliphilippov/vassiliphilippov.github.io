shocking

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a description, report, or image) glaringly vivid, graphic, or sensational, often in a way that is intended to shock or horrify.

Examples

They moved toward the skiffs with shocking eagerness, elbowing and shoving.

Synset Examples

lurid details of the accident

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lurid - sensationally vivid or shocking, especially in a way that is intended to provoke interest or horror
