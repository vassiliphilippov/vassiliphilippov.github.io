Jew-baiter

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒuˌbeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːˌbeɪtə / (BrE)
A person who expresses hatred towards Jews and seeks to harass or persecute them.

Examples

One who could be linked to anti-Semitism only by overcoming his objections is scarcely a good specimen of the Jew-baiter throughout the ages.
The Final Solution proved that the Jew-baiter of any variety exposes himself as being implicated in the criminality and madness of others.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anti-Semite - person who is hostile or prejudiced against Jews

Etymology

Formed from 'Jew' (a member of the Jewish people) and 'baiter' (one who provokes or harasses).
