re-introduction

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːˌɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
the action or process of bringing back something that was previously removed, rejected, or discontinued

Examples

We should like to re-emphasise the importance of stating results solely in terms of international units of TSH activity and of avoiding the re-introduction of biological units.

Synset Examples

they avoided a re-introduction of the old terminology

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: introduction - the act of bringing something into use for the first time

Etymology

Formed from re- (again) + introduction.
