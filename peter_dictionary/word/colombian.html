Colombian

1. ADJ

/ kəˈloʊmbiən / (AmE), / kəˈlɒmbiən / (BrE)
Relating to Colombia or its people.

Examples

A Colombian female's brood of sixteen boa constrictors born in the Staten Island Zoo averaged 20 inches.

Synset Examples

Colombian coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Colombia - South American country

Etymology

Formed from 'Colombia', the country name, with suffix '-an'.
