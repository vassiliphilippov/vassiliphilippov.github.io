close-packed

1. ADJ

/ kloʊs ˈpækt / (AmE), / kləʊs ˈpækt / (BrE)
packed very tightly; having particles or components arranged so there is very little empty space between them, especially in a regular pattern

Examples

The O atoms in each sheet are close packed and each Cr atom is surrounded by a distorted octahedron of O atoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dense - having components closely compacted together
  • Antonym: loose - not tightly packed; with plenty of space between parts
