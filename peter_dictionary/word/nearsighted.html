nearsighted

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɪrˌsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɪəˌsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
Unable to see distant objects clearly; having better vision for near objects than for those far away.

Examples

We used to kid him by saying he only painted that way because he was so nearsighted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: myopic - unable to see distant objects clearly; short-sighted
  • Antonym: farsighted - able to see distant objects more clearly than near ones

Etymology

Formed from 'near' + 'sighted', first recorded in the 19th century.
