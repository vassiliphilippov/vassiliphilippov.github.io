unpremeditated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.priˈmɛdəˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.priːˈmɛd.ɪ.teɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Not planned or thought about in advance; spontaneous or unintentional.

Examples

The stained-glass windows may have developed unpremeditated patinas, the paneling may be no more durable than the planks in a political platform.

Synset Examples

asked an unpremeditated question

Related Terms

  • Antonym: premeditated - planned or thought about in advance

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'premeditated', from Latin 'praemeditari' (to think beforehand).
