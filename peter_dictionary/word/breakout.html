breakout

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪˌkaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkaʊt / (BrE)
An escape from a place of detention, especially a jail or prison.

Examples

It's about a prison break.

Synset Examples

the breakout was carefully planned

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prison break - an escape from prison
  • Synonym: jailbreak - an escape from jail or prison
