speckled

1. ADJ

/ ˈspɛkəld / (AmE), / ˈspɛkəld / (BrE)
marked with a pattern of small spots or dots, especially of a different color than the background

Examples

His eyes had the same dreadful rigid stare as Dr. Grimesby Roylott's when he was found before his open safe wearing the speckled band.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spotted - covered with spots
  • Synonym: dotted - covered with small round marks or dots

Etymology

From Middle English spekkel, spek, related to 'speck' (a small spot or mark).
