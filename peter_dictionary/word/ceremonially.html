ceremonially

1. ADV

/ səˌrəˈmoʊniəli / (AmE), / ˌsɛrəˈməʊniəli / (BrE)
In a ceremonious or ritualistic manner; with formal ceremony or in accordance with ritual.

Examples

"Tell Mr. Gorboduc what you 're doing these days ", my mother advised the children, ceremonially.

Synset Examples

my mother advised her children ceremoniously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formally - in a formal manner; with established procedure or etiquette
  • Antonym: informally - in a casual, unofficial manner

Etymology

From 'ceremonial' + '-ly'; ultimately from Latin 'caerimonia' (religious rite).
