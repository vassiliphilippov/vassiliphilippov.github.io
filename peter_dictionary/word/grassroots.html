grassroots

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡræsˌruts / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsˌruːts / (BrE)
Involving or originating from the ordinary people or the most basic level of an organization, society, or activity.

Examples

Help is needed from dealers, at the grass-roots level.

Synset Examples

the grassroots factor in making the decision

Related Terms

  • Synonym: popular - of or carried on by the people as a whole
  • Antonym: top-down - directed or imposed from the highest level

Etymology

From 'grass' + 'roots', originally likening the common people to the roots of grass, the most basic level of a system.
