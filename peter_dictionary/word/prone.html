prone

1. ADJ

/ proʊn / (AmE), / prəʊn / (BrE)
lying with the front of the body facing downward

Examples

Have the class lie prone.
He went prone on his stomach, the better to pursue his examination.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: supine - lying face upward

Etymology

From Latin 'pronus' (bent forward, inclined).
