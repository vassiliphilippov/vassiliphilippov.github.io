cremate

1. VERB

/ ˈkriːˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkriː.meɪt / (BrE)
to dispose of (a dead body) by burning it to ashes, especially as part of a funeral rite

Examples

And, therefore, being in disgrace, they would not be cremated and their ashes flung to the winds in public ceremony.

Synset Examples

Cremate a corpse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incinerate - to burn something completely to ashes

Etymology

From Latin cremāre (to burn, consume by fire).
