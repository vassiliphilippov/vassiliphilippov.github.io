well-dressed

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈdrɛst / (AmE), / wɛl ˈdrɛst / (BrE)
Wearing attractive and neat clothing that is appropriate and stylish.

Examples

Really put together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart - neatly and stylishly dressed (especially British English)
  • Antonym: scruffy - untidy or shabby in appearance or dress
