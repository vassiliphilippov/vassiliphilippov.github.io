crowbait

1. NOUN

/ ˈkroʊˌbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊˌbeɪt / (BrE)
An old or emaciated horse considered no longer useful and likely to die soon.

Examples

Even Black's old crowbait began to snort, and from the house Black yelled, "Jess!

Etymology

From 'crow' (the bird) and 'bait', referring to a horse so weak it may become food for crows.
