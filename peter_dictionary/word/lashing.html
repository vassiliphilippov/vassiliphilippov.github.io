lashing

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a beating or whipping with a rope, strap, or whip, especially as a form of punishment

Examples

Ice baths, electric shocks, lashings, wild dogs, testicle crushers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whipping - the act of beating with a whip as a punishment

Etymology

From 'lash' (strike with a whip or rope) + -ing (forming nouns); 'lash' possibly from Middle English lasche, meaning to strike.
