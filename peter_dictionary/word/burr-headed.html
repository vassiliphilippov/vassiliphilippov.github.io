burr-headed

1. ADJ

/ bɜr ˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / bɜː ˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having very short, straight hair, cut close to the scalp, giving a bristly appearance.

Examples

Wiry and burr headed, with steel blue eyes and a chest splattered with medals, Chandler is the epitome of the old-time trooper.

Etymology

From 'burr' (a rough, prickly seed head) + 'headed'.
