slovenliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈslʌvənlinəs / (AmE), / ˈslʌv(ə)nlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being untidy, careless, or lacking in neatness or order

Examples

"Is it because of my slovenliness that hair grows on my face?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tidiness - the quality of being neat and orderly
  • Antonym: neatness - the state of being clean and tidy

Etymology

From sloven + -liness, where 'sloven' is from Middle French *sloven* (a slovenly person).
