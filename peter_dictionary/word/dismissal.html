dismissal

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈmɪsəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈmɪsəl / (BrE)
(Law) A judgment by a court to end a case without a trial or further consideration.

Examples

However, this Court put to one side without consideration the Government's appeal from the dismissal of its Sherman Act allegations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: judgment - formal decision given by a court

Etymology

From dismiss + -al, formed in English after the verb 'dismiss'.
