forte

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔːrteɪ, ˈfɔrt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːteɪ, ˈfɔːt / (BrE)
A thing at which someone excels; a strong point or area of expertise.

Examples

Though versatile and capable of turning out a ballad lyric with the best of them, Mercer's forte is a highly polished quasi folk wit.

Synset Examples

cooking is his forte

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strength - a quality or attribute that is a strong point
  • Antonym: weakness - a quality or attribute that is a weak point

Etymology

From French 'fort' (strong), from Latin 'fortis'.
