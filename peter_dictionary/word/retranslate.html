retranslate

1. VERB

/ ˌriːtrænzˈleɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːtrænzˈleɪt / (BrE)
to translate something again, especially to improve accuracy or reflect changes in understanding

Examples

Originally recorded by hand, these words have been copied and recopied, translated and retranslated through the ages.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: translate - to express the meaning of something in another language

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 're-' (again) and 'translate'.
