quicken

1. VERB

/ ˈkwɪkən / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkən / (BrE)
to make or become faster; to move or proceed at a greater speed

Examples

Quickening his steps, John entered the vast church and climbed the tower steps to the bells.

Synset Examples

The car accelerated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accelerate - to speed up; to increase speed or rate
  • Antonym: slow - to decrease in speed; to move less quickly

Etymology

From Old English 'cwician' (to come to life), from 'cwic' (alive, living).
