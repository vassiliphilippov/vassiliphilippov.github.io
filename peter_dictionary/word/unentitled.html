unentitled

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtaɪtld / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtaɪtld / (BrE)
Not having a right or claim to something; not entitled.

Examples

At the base of that wall, you'll find a rock that has **no** earthly **business** in a Maine hayfield.

Synset Examples

a distinction to which he was unentitled

Related Terms

  • Antonym: entitled - having a right or claim to something

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'entitled', indicating negation.
