wintertime

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪntɚtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈwɪntətaɪm / (BrE)
The season of winter; the coldest part of the year, typically from the winter solstice to the vernal equinox in the northern hemisphere.

Examples

Pitcher Steve Barber joined the club one week ago after completing his hitch under the Army's accelerated wintertime military course, also at Fort Knox, Ky..

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winter - the coldest season of the year

Etymology

Formed from 'winter' + 'time'.
