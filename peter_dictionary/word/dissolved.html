dissolved

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈzɑlvd / (AmE), / dɪˈzɒlvd / (BrE)
(of a solid substance) made into or absorbed by a liquid so as to form a solution.

The dissolved oxygen in the aeration unit was consistently high until January 29, 1961.
