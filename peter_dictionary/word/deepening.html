deepening

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdiːpənɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

It is the gradual unfolding and deepening of this contradiction which creates the inner dialectic of the evolution of the mystery story.

Related Terms
