thick-skulled

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɪk ˌskʌld / (AmE), / ˈθɪk ˌskʌld / (BrE)
(informal) Stupid or slow to understand; lacking intelligence or quickness of mind.

Examples

Alexander knew Spencer too well to think him naive or thick-skulled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blockheaded - unintelligent; stupid (informal)
  • Synonym: dull-witted - slow to understand or think; unintelligent
  • Antonym: quick-witted - mentally sharp; able to think quickly

Etymology

Figurative use of 'thick' (dense) + 'skulled' (having a skull), suggesting mental dullness.
