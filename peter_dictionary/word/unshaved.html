unshaved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃeɪvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪvd / (BrE)
Not having shaved facial hair; with beard or stubble present.

Examples

The wraith-like events of the last few days flooded David's mind and he rubbed his unshaved chin and felt again the ache in his kidneys caused by his saddle odyssey from Boston.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shaven - with facial hair shaved off
  • Synonym: stubbly - having short, rough growth of beard

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'shaved', past participle of 'shave'.
