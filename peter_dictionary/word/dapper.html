dapper

1. ADJ

/ ˈdæpər / (AmE), / ˈdæpə / (BrE)
neat and stylish in appearance and dress, often in an elegant or lively way

Examples

But though I boasted and gave off a dapper front, I was beneath it all frightened.
Even the mess sergeant, Bill Brown, a dapper, cocky transfer from an airborne division, went out on the range.

Synset Examples

a dapper young man
a jaunty red hat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart - well-dressed and fashionable
  • Antonym: sloppy - messy or untidy, especially in appearance or dress

Etymology

From Middle Dutch *dapyr* (strong, quick, nimble).
