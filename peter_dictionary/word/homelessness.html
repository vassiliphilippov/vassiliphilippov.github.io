homelessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈhoʊm.lɪs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm.lɪs.nəs / (BrE)
The condition of having no home, especially living on the streets or in shelters.

Examples

Maybe it was the thought of them on the streets or his child growing up not knowing her daddy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vagrancy - the state of living as a vagrant without a settled home

Etymology

From 'homeless' + '-ness'; 'homeless' from Middle English, from home + -less.
