ringing

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
a clear, resonant sound produced by a bell, telephone, or similar object when struck or activated

Examples

The telephone was in the study but the ringing came from the hall.

Synset Examples

the distinctive ring of the church bell
the ringing of the telephone
the tintinnabulation that so voluminously swells from the ringing and the dinging of the bells

Related Terms

  • Synonym: peal - a loud ringing of a bell or bells
  • Degree Lower: ding - a short ringing sound, especially of a bell
  • Synonym: tintinnabulation - the ringing or sounding of bells
