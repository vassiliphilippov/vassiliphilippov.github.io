memoir

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛmwɑr / (AmE), / ˈmɛmwɑː / (BrE)
an account written by someone describing their personal experiences, often focusing on particular events or periods in their life

Examples

It is not that the facts of the persecution were unavailable; most of the information elicited in Jerusalem had been brought to the surface by the numerous War Crimes tribunals and investigating commissions, and by reports, memoirs, and survivors' accounts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: autobiography - the story of a person's life written by that person

Etymology

From French 'mémoire' meaning 'memory' or 'memoir', ultimately from Latin 'memoria' (memory).
