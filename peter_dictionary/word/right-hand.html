right-hand

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪtˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈraɪtˌhænd / (BrE)
Located on or directed toward the right side.

Examples

Johnson unwired the right hand door, whose window was, like the left one, merely loosely taped fragments of glass, and Johnson wadded himself into a narrow seat made still more narrow by three cases of beer.
When we make our move there, you'll be my right-hand man.

Synset Examples

a right-hand turn

Related Terms

  • Antonym: left-hand - located on or directed toward the left side
