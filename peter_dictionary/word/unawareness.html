unawareness

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌnəˈwɛrnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈweənɪs / (BrE)
the state of not being aware of something, especially as a result of not knowing or not paying attention

Examples

But the highroad, according to the description of its traffic, belongs to life as it is lived in unawareness of death, while the way to the churchyard belongs to some other sort of life:
The calmness and detachment of his tone suggested unawareness of how implicit was his own guilt in the words he had used to defend Cromwell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or information
  • Antonym: awareness - knowledge or perception of a situation or fact

Etymology

Formed from the prefix un- + awareness.
