classifier

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪfaɪə / (BrE)
a person who arranges or organizes things into categories or classes

Examples

But for students of musical forms and would-be classifiers, the work presents its problems.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: taxonomy - the practice or principle of classifying

Etymology

From classify + -er, meaning 'one who classifies'.
