buying

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of purchasing something; the process of obtaining goods or services in exchange for money.

Examples

Has your company thought through its strategy in this whole "discretionary buying "area?
Is your purchasing agent offering too much free buying service for employees?
When Johnson ejaculated "Howsabout my buying us all a nice cold Co-cola, Ma' am "?

Synset Examples

buying and selling fill their days
shrewd purchasing requires considerable knowledge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purchasing - the act of acquiring something by paying for it
  • Antonym: selling - the act of giving something in exchange for money

Etymology

From the verb 'buy' + -ing, used as a noun from the 15th century.
