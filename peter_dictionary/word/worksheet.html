worksheet

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːk.ʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.ʃiːt / (BrE)
a sheet of paper with columns used by accountants to organize and calculate figures, especially when preparing financial statements

Examples

The figures on the worksheet paper in front of her were jumping and waving around so badly it was all she could do to make them out clearly enough to copy them with the typewriter.

Etymology

From 'work' + 'sheet', indicating a sheet used for working out calculations.
