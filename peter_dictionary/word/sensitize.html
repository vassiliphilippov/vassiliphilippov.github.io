sensitize

1. VERB

/ ˈsɛn.sɪ.taɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.sɪ.taɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something sensitive or aware, especially to a particular stimulus or issue

Examples

Three of the anti - Rh sera used were taken from recently sensitized individuals.
In addition one serum was obtained from a donor (R. E..) who had been sensitized 6 years previously.

Synset Examples

He was not sensitized to her emotional needs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: desensitize - to make less sensitive or less aware

Etymology

From French sensibiliser, from Latin sensibilis (sensitive).
