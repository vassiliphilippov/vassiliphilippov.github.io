rubbery

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌbəri / (AmE), / ˈrʌbəri / (BrE)
Having an elastic texture or quality like rubber; flexible or tough in a way that resembles rubber.

Examples

The way his red rubber lips were stretched across his pearly little teeth I though he was only having a little joke, but, no, he wanted me to bend down from the roar of wind so he could roar something into my ear.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elastic - able to resume its normal shape after stretching or compressing

Etymology

From 'rubber' + '-y', denoting resemblance.
