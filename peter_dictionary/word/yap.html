yap

1. VERB

/ jæp / (AmE), / jæp / (BrE)
(of a dog) to bark in a sharp, high-pitched tone, often repeatedly and noisily.

Examples

I learned, for example, that he made a practice of yapping at dogs he encountered and, in winter, of sprinkling salt on the icy pavement to scarify their feet.

Synset Examples

the puppies yelped

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yelp - to give a short, sharp cry, especially of pain or alarm

Etymology

Imitative, representing the sound of a high, sharp bark.
