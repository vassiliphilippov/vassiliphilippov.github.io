railroad

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌroʊd / (AmE), / ˈreɪlrəʊd / (BrE)
A company or organization that operates a system for transporting passengers or freight by train.

Related Terms

Etymology

From rail + road, describing the road or track laid with rails on which trains run.
