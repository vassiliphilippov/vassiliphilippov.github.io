extern

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛkˌstɝn / (AmE), / ˈɛkstɜːn / (BrE)
A medical student or doctor who works at a hospital without residing there.

Examples

Four additional deputies be employed at the Fulton County Jail and "a doctor, medical intern or extern be employed for night and weekend duty at the jail ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intern - a medical student or doctor working and residing at a hospital as part of their training

Etymology

From Latin 'externus' meaning 'outside, external'.
