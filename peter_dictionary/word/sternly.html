sternly

1. ADV

/ ˈstɝnli / (AmE), / ˈstɜːnli / (BrE)
In a strict or severe manner, showing firmness or disapproval.

Examples

"Never mind ", she said sternly.

Synset Examples

`No,' she said sternly
peered severely over her glasses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: severely - in a strict or harsh manner
  • Antonym: gently - in a kind or soft manner

Etymology

From "stern" (severe, strict) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
