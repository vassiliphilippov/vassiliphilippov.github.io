jocular

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɑːkjələr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒkjʊlə / (BrE)
Fond of or characterized by joking; humorous or playful.

Examples

Not without good reason has the anatomical been called jocular journalese.
Apart from journalese and vaudeville gags, the anatomical is also found in jocular literature.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: serious - not joking or playful; thoughtful or solemn
  • Synonym: jesting - talking or acting in a joking manner

Etymology

From Latin 'jocularis', from 'jocus' meaning 'joke'.
