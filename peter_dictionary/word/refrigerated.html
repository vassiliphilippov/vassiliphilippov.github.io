refrigerated

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of food, rooms, vehicles, etc.) made or kept cold by means of refrigeration.

Examples

Today, refrigerated carriers have made the shipping of avocados possible to any place in the world.

Synset Examples

keep the milk refrigerated
a refrigerated truck

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: frozen - preserved or kept at a freezing temperature

Etymology

From the verb "refrigerate," which is from Latin "refrigerare" (to make cool).
