unborn

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈbɔrn / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɔːn / (BrE)
not yet born or brought into existence

Examples

Men seem almost universally to want a sense of function, that is, a feeling that their existence makes a difference to someone, living or unborn, close and immediate or generalized.

Synset Examples

unborn generations

Related Terms

  • Antonym: born - brought into existence by birth

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'born'.
