vascular

1. ADJ

/ ˈvæskjələr / (AmE), / ˈvaskjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to, consisting of, or having vessels that conduct and circulate fluids, such as blood or sap.

Examples

It is characterized by the presence of incompletely developed secondary lobules; well defined, but haphazardly arranged, interlobular septa and a thick, remarkably vascular pleura (fig. 9).

Synset Examples

vascular constriction
a vascular bundle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vascularized - having blood vessels or supplied with blood vessels
  • Antonym: nonvascular - lacking vessels that conduct and circulate fluids

Etymology

From Latin 'vasculum' (small vessel), diminutive of 'vas' (vessel).
