pistol-whip

1. VERB

/ ˈpɪstl̩ wɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɪstl wɪp / (BrE)
To strike someone with a pistol, especially using the handle or butt.

Examples

From the enlisted men he pistol-whipped to the subordinate officer whose wife he tried to rape, a lot of men had plenty of reason heartily to dislike Marcus Reno.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bludgeon - to strike with a heavy object
