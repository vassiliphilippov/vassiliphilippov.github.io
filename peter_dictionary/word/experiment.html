experiment

1. VERB

/ ɪkˈspɛrəmənt / (AmE), / ɪkˈsperɪmənt / (BrE)
to carry out a scientific test or investigation, especially in order to discover something new or to test a hypothesis

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test - to carry out a procedure to establish the quality, performance, or reliability of something
  • Synonym: investigate - to carry out research or study into a subject to discover facts or information

Etymology

From Latin 'experimentum' (a trial, test), from 'experiri' (to try, test).
