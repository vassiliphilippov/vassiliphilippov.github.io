geographic

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiː.əˈɡræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əˈɡræf.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to geography or the physical features, location, and distribution of the earth's surface

Examples

Have you considered the implications of continuing geographic shifts in terms of sales force allocation, strength of distributor organizations, and even plant location?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geographical - alternative form; relating to geography

Etymology

From French géographique, from Greek geōgraphikos (pertaining to geography), from geōgraphia (geography).
