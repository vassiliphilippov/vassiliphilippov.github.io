credible

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɛd.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɛd.ɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Able to be believed; convincing or trustworthy.

Examples

Having volunteered that he was a man of about sixty, he bounded up the stairs and with each leap rendered the number less credible.

Synset Examples

completely credible testimony
credible information

Related Terms

  • Antonym: incredible - impossible or very difficult to believe

Etymology

From Latin credibilis (worthy of belief), from credere (to believe).
