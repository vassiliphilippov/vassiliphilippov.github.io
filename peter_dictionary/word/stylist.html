stylist

1. NOUN

/ ˈstaɪlɪst / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɪst / (BrE)
An artist or writer who is highly skilled in and known for a distinctive style.

Examples

When Stravinsky shaped his purpose to the shifting scenes of many cultures, many salons, many dialectics, many personalities, he tried to refashion himself into a stylist of many styles, determined by many disparate cultures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: artist - a person skilled in a creative activity such as painting, writing, or music

Etymology

From style + -ist, indicating a person concerned with or practicing a particular style.
