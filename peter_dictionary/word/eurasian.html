Eurasian

1. ADJ

/ jʊˈreɪʒən / (AmE), / jʊəˈreɪʒən / (BrE)
Relating to Europe and Asia, or originating from both Europe and Asia.

Examples

The Dharma Dictionary, a list of highly unusual terms used in connection with Eurasian proto-senility cults.

Synset Examples

His mother was Eurasian, and his father Chinese
the Eurasian landmass is the largest in the world

Etymology

Formed from 'Europe' and 'Asia', referring to the combined landmass or shared heritage.
