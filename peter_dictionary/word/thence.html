thence

1. ADV

/ ðɛns / (AmE), / ðɛns / (BrE)
from that place; from there.

Examples

We walked down the Rue de L' Arcade, thence along beside the Madeleine and across to a sidewalk cafe opposite that church.

Synset Examples

proceeded thence directly to college
flew to Helsinki and thence to Moscow
roads that lead therefrom

Related Terms

  • Synonym: therefrom - from that place or thing; from there
  • Degree Lower: hence - from here; from this place
  • Antonym: thither - to that place

Etymology

From Old English 'ðanon' or Middle English 'thennes', meaning 'from there'.
