netlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having an open structure or appearance that resembles a net or web

Examples

With completion of filling, net-like anastomoses were noted to be present between these separately derived branches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lacy - delicate and openwork like lace
