virgin

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɝːdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪn / (BrE)
(of a material or area) not previously used, worked, or developed; in its original, natural state.

Examples

From God's Will and Wisdom, and from virgin earth.
From this earth, then, while it was still virgin God took dust and fashioned the man, the beginning of humanity ".

Synset Examples

virgin wool

Etymology

From Latin 'virginem' (maiden, virgin).

2. NOUN

/ ˈvɝːdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪn / (BrE)
A person, typically a woman, who has never had sexual intercourse.

Examples

"Eve was disobedient; for she did not obey when as yet she was a virgin.
