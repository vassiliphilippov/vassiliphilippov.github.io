frightened

1. ADJ

/ ˈfraɪtənd / (AmE), / ˈfraɪtənd / (BrE)
Afraid or anxious; feeling fear.

Examples

Very, very frightened that they botched it.

Synset Examples

the frightened child cowered in the corner
too shocked and scared to move

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afraid - feeling fear or anxiety; frightened
  • Synonym: scared - fearful or frightened
  • Antonym: brave - showing courage; not afraid

Etymology

From the verb 'frighten', from Middle English 'frighten', from 'fright' (sudden fear).
