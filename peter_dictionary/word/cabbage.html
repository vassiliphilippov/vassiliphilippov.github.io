cabbage

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæb.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkab.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
A round leafy vegetable with a dense head of green or purple leaves, belonging to the species Brassica oleracea, commonly cultivated as food.

Examples

It consists in saying,' That would be sending the goat to look after the cabbage'.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lettuce - leafy green vegetable often used in salads

Etymology

From Old French *caboche* (head), diminutive of *cape* (head), of uncertain origin.
