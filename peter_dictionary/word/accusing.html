accusing

1. ADJ

/ əˈkjuːzɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈkjuːzɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing or indicating blame or accusation

Examples

And a gray-haired man whose glance - direct, lifelike, and mildly accusing - was contradicted by the gilt and black frame.
White shirted and conservatively cravated drivers stared conspicuously toward the eastern horizon and past my supplicating and accusing gaze.

Synset Examples

an accusitive forefinger
black accusatory looks
accusive shoes and telltale trousers
his accusing glare

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accusatory - expressing accusation or blame

Etymology

From the verb 'accuse' + -ing, meaning expressing accusation.
