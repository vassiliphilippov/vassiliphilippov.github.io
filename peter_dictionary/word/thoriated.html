thoriated

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɔriˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈθɔːrɪeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Containing or alloyed with thorium oxide, especially referring to metals such as tungsten used in electrical applications.

Examples

The cathode consisted of a 1/4 "diameter thoriated tungsten rod attached to a water cooled copper tube.

Etymology

From thorium + -ated (denoting the addition of thorium oxide).
