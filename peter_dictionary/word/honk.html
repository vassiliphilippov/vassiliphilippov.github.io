honk

1. VERB

/ hɑŋk / (AmE), / hɒŋk / (BrE)
to use the horn of a car to make a loud, sharp sound

Examples

[CAR HONKING] [ALL SHOUTING IN ITALIAN] [HORN BLARING] MICHAEL: New York City.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beep - make a short, high-pitched sound with a horn or electronic device

2. NOUN

/ hɑŋk / (AmE), / hɒŋk / (BrE)
A loud, harsh sound, especially the characteristic cry of a goose or a similar noise.

Examples

[HORN HONKING] [EXCLAIMS] [SPEAKING ITALIAN]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoot - a loud, harsh sound, as made by an owl or a car horn
