coo

1. VERB

/ kuː / (AmE), / kuː / (BrE)
(of a pigeon or dove) to make a soft, murmuring sound; also, to speak in a soft, gentle voice often as a sign of affection.

Examples

[COOING] [ALL SINGING IN ITALIAN] [IN ITALIAN]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murmur - to make a low, continuous sound

Etymology

Imitative of the sound made by doves and pigeons. First recorded in English in the 17th century.
