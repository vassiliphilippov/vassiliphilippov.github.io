unimposing

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪmˈpoʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈpəʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
Not striking or impressive in appearance or manner; lacking in impressiveness.

Examples

The doctor opened the smallest of his cases, an unimposing straw bag, and exposed the contents for Alex's inspection.

Synset Examples

on the whole the results of this system are unimposing

Related Terms

  • Antonym: imposing - having an impressive or striking appearance

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'imposing' (impressive, striking).
