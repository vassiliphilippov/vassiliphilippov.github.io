hustler

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌs.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌs.lə / (BrE)
a person who is shrewd or unscrupulous and adept at overcoming difficulties, often by clever or dishonest means

Examples

I thought you were some cheap hustler Johnny was running in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swindler - a person who cheats others to make money
  • Synonym: fraudster - person who commits fraud
  • Antonym: honest person - someone who is not deceitful or unscrupulous

Etymology

From English 'hustle' (to push, to hurry), possibly from Dutch 'husselen' (to shake, to toss).
