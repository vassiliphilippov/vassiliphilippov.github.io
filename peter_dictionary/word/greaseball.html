greaseball

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːsbɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡriːsbɔːl / (BrE)
OFFENSIVE. An extremely derogatory slang term used as an ethnic slur, especially for people of Italian descent.

Examples

No matter how many Dago Guinea greaseballs come out of the woodwork!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wop - offensive ethnic slur for Italians
