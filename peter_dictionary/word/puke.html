puke

1. NOUN

/ pjuːk / (AmE), / pjuːk / (BrE)
An unpleasant or contemptible person (informal, often used as an insult).

Examples

"I saw Pemberton and he is the most insignificant puke I ever saw.

Synset Examples

only a rotter would do that

throw the bum out
you cowardly little pukes!
the British call a contemptible person a `git'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rat - a person regarded as despicable or treacherous
  • Synonym: bum - a person who is lazy or worthless
  • Synonym: git - British slang for a contemptible person
