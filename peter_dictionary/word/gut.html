gut

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌt / (AmE), / ɡʌt / (BrE)
the part of the digestive tract between the stomach and the anus; the intestine

Examples

Killpath pulled one thin leg up, clamping his arms around the shinbone to press his knee into an incredibly scrawny gut.
And then there was a numbing blow to the heart, and another gut flattening blow to the stomach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intestine - the lower part of the alimentary canal from the end of the stomach to the anus

Etymology

From Old English 'gut', related to Middle Low German 'gût' (bowel).
