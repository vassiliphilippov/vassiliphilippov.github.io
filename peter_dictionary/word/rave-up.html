rave-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪv ʌp / (AmE), / ˈreɪv ʌp / (BrE)
a lively and noisy party or gathering, especially one with loud music

Examples

There'll be a hot time in the old town tonight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests
  • Synonym: soirée - an evening party or gathering
  • Synonym: bash - a large, energetic party

Etymology

British slang, probably from 'rave' (to talk or act wildly) with 'up' as an intensifier; popularized in the 1960s.
