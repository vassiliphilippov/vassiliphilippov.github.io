recapitulate

1. VERB

/ ˌriːkəˈpɪtʃəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːkəˈpɪtjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to repeat or state again the main points of something; to summarize briefly

Examples

As you 've doubtless forgotten the circumstances in the press of more recent depredations, permit me to recapitulate them briefly.

Synset Examples

Let's recapitulate the main ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summarize - to give a brief statement of the main points

Etymology

From Late Latin recapitulare (to go over the main points again), from Latin re- (again) + capitulum (chapter, heading).
