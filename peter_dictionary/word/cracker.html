cracker

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækər / (AmE), / ˈkrækə / (BrE)
A thin, crisp biscuit made from flour and water, sometimes with leavening or shortening, typically unsweetened or only slightly sweetened.

Examples

GEARY: Break a cracker...

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biscuit - a small, typically crisp baked product made from flour, especially in British English
