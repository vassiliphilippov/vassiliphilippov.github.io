airless

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈeə.ləs / (BrE)
(of a place) lacking fresh or adequate air; stuffy or poorly ventilated.

Examples

Men blew dust on objects in a room on the seventeenth floor of the Hotel Dumont and blew it off again, and did the same in a tiny, almost airless room in a tenement in the West Forties.

Synset Examples

a dusty airless attic
the dreadfully close atmosphere
hot and stuffy and the air was blue with smoke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuffy - (of a room or building) lacking fresh air or ventilation
  • Antonym: ventilated - (of a place) supplied with fresh air, aerated
