positively

1. ADV

/ ˈpɑːzətɪvli / (AmE), / ˈpɒzɪtɪvli / (BrE)
Used to emphasize that something is the case to a high or extreme degree.

Examples

Perhaps it was his misfortune, or good fortune, whichever way one looked at it, to belong to the former group, and he was struggling unconsciously to build up pressure in a world which demanded none, which was positively antagonistic to it.

Synset Examples

it was positively monumental

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absolutely - completely; totally or definitely
  • Synonym: utterly - completely or absolutely

Etymology

From 'positive' + '-ly'. 'Positive' from Latin 'positivus' (having been placed, settled).
