Maltese

1. ADJ

/ mɔlˈtiz / (AmE), / mɔːlˈtiːz / (BrE)
Relating to the island or republic of Malta, its people, or their language.

Examples

A bit light after the war in Corto Maltese, no?

Synset Examples

Maltese customs officers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Maltese - the language of Malta

Etymology

From Malta + -ese (suffix denoting origin or relation), after Italian 'maltese'.
