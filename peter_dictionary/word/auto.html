auto

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ / (BrE)
A motor vehicle with four wheels, usually propelled by an internal combustion engine; a car.

Examples

Autos whizzed past.
Suddenly a treble auto horn tootley-toot-tootled, and, thumbing hopefully, I saw emergent in windshield flash: red lips, streaming silk of blonde hair and - ah, trembling confusion of hope, apprehension, despair - the leering face of old Herry.

Synset Examples

he needs a car to get to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car - a road vehicle with an engine, four wheels, and seats for a small number of people
  • Synonym: automobile - a formal term for a self-propelled passenger vehicle

Etymology

Shortened from 'automobile', which is from French 'automobile', from Greek 'auto-' (self) + Latin 'mobilis' (movable).
