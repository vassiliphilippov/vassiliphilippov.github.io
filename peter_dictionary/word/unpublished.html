unpublished

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpʌblɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌblɪʃt / (BrE)
(of a book, article, letter, etc.) not made available to the public by printing or other means of publication.

Examples

I came to England last summer to do research on the unpublished letters of Cardinal Newman.

Synset Examples

unpublished letters and diaries

Related Terms

  • Antonym: published - (of work) made available to the public in print or other media

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'published', from Latin 'publicare' (to make public).
