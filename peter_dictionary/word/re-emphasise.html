re-emphasise

1. VERB

/ ˌriːˈɛmfəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈemfəsaɪz / (BrE)
To emphasize something again or afresh, especially to highlight its continuing importance.

Examples

We should like to re-emphasise the importance of stating results solely in terms of international units of TSH activity and of avoiding the re-introduction of biological units.

Synset Examples

The director re-emphasized the need for greater productivity

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: emphasise - to give special importance to something

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 're-' (again) to 'emphasise', from Greek 'en-' (in) + 'phainein' (to show).
