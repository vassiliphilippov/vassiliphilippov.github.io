inst

1. ADJ

/ ɪnst / (AmE), / ɪnst / (BrE)
(dated) Abbreviation for 'instant'; used in correspondence to refer to the current month.

Examples

In time, you'’ll feel differently.

Synset Examples

your letter of the 10th inst

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'instante mense' (in the present month).
