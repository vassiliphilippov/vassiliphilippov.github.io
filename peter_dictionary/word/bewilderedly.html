bewilderedly

1. ADV

/ bɪˈwɪldɚdli / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldədli / (BrE)
In a bewildered or confused manner; showing or expressing confusion or puzzlement.

Examples

Watson spoke bewilderedly to the dark night flecked with pine-knot torches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusedly - in a confused or disorderly manner
  • Antonym: calmly - in a calm or composed manner

Etymology

From 'bewildered' + '-ly', with 'bewildered' from be- + 'wilder' (to lead astray).
