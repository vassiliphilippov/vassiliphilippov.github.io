washbasin

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃˌbeɪsən / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌbeɪsɪn / (BrE)
A fixed sink in a bathroom, connected to water supply and drain, used for washing hands and face.

Examples

"The place had no sink or washbasin, only a bathtub ", his mother discovered when she visited him.

Synset Examples

he ran some water in the basin and splashed it on his face

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sink - a fixed basin with a water supply and drain for washing, especially in a kitchen

Etymology

Formed from 'wash' + 'basin', meaning a bowl for washing.
