auld langsyne

1. NOUN

/ ɔld læŋˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ɔːld læŋˈzaɪn / (BrE)
times long past; days gone by, especially remembered with nostalgic affection.

Examples

Those **were** **the** great old **days**, you know.

Etymology

From Scots, literally 'old long since'; popularized by the poem and song by Robert Burns.
