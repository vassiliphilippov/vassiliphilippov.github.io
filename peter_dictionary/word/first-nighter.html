first-nighter

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɝst ˌnaɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌnaɪtə / (BrE)
A person who regularly attends the opening nights of theatrical productions.

Examples

At the end of the performance, Dave and Max came out into the brilliantly lit foyer among a surge of gowned and tuxedoed first nighters.

Etymology

From 'first night' (the opening night of a play) + '-er' (one who does).
