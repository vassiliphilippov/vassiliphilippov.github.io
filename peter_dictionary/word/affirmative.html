affirmative

1. NOUN

/ əˈfɝːmətɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɜːmətɪv / (BrE)
A word or statement expressing agreement or saying 'yes'; a positive reply or answer.

Examples

Yes, I have.

Synset Examples

he answered in the affirmative

Related Terms

  • Antonym: negative - a word or statement expressing denial, disagreement, or refusal; an answer of 'no'
  • Synonym: yes - the word used to express agreement or affirmation

Etymology

From Latin 'affirmativus', from 'affirmare' (to affirm).
