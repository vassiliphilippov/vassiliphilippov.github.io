sizzle

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪzəl / (BrE)
to make a hissing or crackling sound like that of food frying in hot fat

Examples

He thought of Simms Purdew snoring on his bunk while Pullen James crouched by the hearth, skirmishing an undershirt for lice, and a wet log sizzled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hiss - to make a sharp sibilant sound, like that of steam or a snake
  • Synonym: crackle - to make a series of short, sharp, popping sounds

Etymology

Imitative of the sound produced by frying; first attested in the 17th century.
