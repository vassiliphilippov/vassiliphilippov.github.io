reuse

1. VERB

/ riˈjuz / (AmE), / riːˈjuːz / (BrE)
to use something again after it has been used, often after it has been processed or treated

Examples

Further, the gland is able to re-use a larger fraction of the thyroid hormone de-iodinated peripherally.

Synset Examples

We must recycle the cardboard boxes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recycle - to process used materials so they can be used again
  • Antonym: discard - to throw something away after use

Etymology

From re- (again) + use, modeled on the structure of 'reuse' in French and Latin roots.
