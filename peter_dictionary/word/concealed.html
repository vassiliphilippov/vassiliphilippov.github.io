concealed

1. ADJ

/ kənˈsild / (AmE), / kənˈsiːld / (BrE)
kept out of sight; not visible or not meant to be seen

Examples

Just before leaving the arroyo where he was partially concealed, he did hear shots down at the house.
While several yards from it, still concealed by the shrubbery, she 'd seen two men on her left at the pool's edge.

Synset Examples

concealed (or hidden) damage
in stormy weather the stars are out of sight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hidden - kept out of sight; concealed
  • Antonym: exposed - in plain view; not hidden or covered

Etymology

From Latin 'con-' (together) + 'celare' (to hide).
