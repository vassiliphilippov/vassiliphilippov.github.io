viscometer

1. NOUN

/ vɪsˈkɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / vɪsˈkɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the viscosity of a liquid

Examples

A band viscometer is shown in Figure 2.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rheometer - an instrument for measuring the flow of liquids, especially their viscosity and elasticity

Etymology

From 'viscosity' + '-meter', the latter from Greek 'metron' meaning 'measure'.
