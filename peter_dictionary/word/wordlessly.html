wordlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɝːdləsli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdləsli / (BrE)
In a manner without speaking; without using words.

Examples

He put his chin on his kneecap, stretching his neck like that of a turkey on a chopping block, and stared wordlessly at his sergeant.

Synset Examples

he sat mutely next to her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silently - without sound; making no noise

Etymology

From 'wordless' + '-ly', where 'wordless' means 'without words'.
