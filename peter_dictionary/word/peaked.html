peaked

1. ADJ

/ ˈpikd / (AmE), / ˈpiːkt / (BrE)
having a pointed or sharply raised peak or top

Examples

It could never happen as long as God was alert and the Drew steeple stood guard with its peaked lance.

Synset Examples

the peaked ceiling
the island's peaked hills

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointed - having a sharp or tapered end
  • Antonym: flat - having a level or even surface without raised peaks
