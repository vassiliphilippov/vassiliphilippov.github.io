Labrador

1. PROPN

/ ˈlæbrəˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈlæbrədɔː / (BrE)
the mainland region of the Canadian province of Newfoundland and Labrador, located in the eastern part of the Labrador Peninsula in northeastern Canada

Examples

He named this opening, between Baffin Island and Labrador, the "Furious Overfall ".

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Terra do Lavrador' (land of the laborer), named after João Fernandes Lavrador, a Portuguese explorer.
