nimbly

1. ADV

/ ˈnɪmbli / (AmE), / ˈnɪmbli / (BrE)
In a quick, light, and agile manner.

Examples

Russ sprang through to bat it nimbly aside.

Synset Examples

nimbly scaling an iron gate
leaped agilely from roof to roof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agilely - in an agile manner; quickly and lightly

Etymology

Formed from "nimble" + "-ly"; "nimble" from Old English *nemel* (quick to grasp).
