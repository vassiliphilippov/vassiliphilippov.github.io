electrically

1. ADV

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkli / (BrE)
by means of electricity; using or involving electricity

Examples

A small pressure unbalance displaces the diaphragm and changes the capacitance between the diaphragm and an electrically insulated plate spaced 0.001 in. apart (for * * f).

Synset Examples

electrically controlled

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mechanically - by means of a machine or mechanism, not electricity
  • Degree Higher: electronically - by means of electronics

Etymology

Formed from "electric" + "-ally".
