unhesitatingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈhɛzɪteɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛzɪteɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no hesitation; without pausing or doubting.

Examples

For instance - what about all those people Harold Rhodes went toward unhesitatingly, as if this were the one moment they would ever have together, their one chance of knowing each other?
She described herself and her circumstances unhesitatingly.

Synset Examples

she said yes unhesitatingly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant or unsure manner

Etymology

Formed from 'unhesitating' + '-ly', with 'un-' (not) + 'hesitate' (from Latin 'haesitare').
