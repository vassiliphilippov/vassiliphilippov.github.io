veracity

1. NOUN

/ vəˈræsəti / (AmE), / vəˈræsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being honest and not telling lies; truthfulness

Examples

His claim was therefore "so highly exaggerated ", the Department concluded, that it "cast doubt upon his veracity and, consequently, upon his sincerity and good faith ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truthfulness - the quality of being truthful
  • Antonym: dishonesty - lack of honesty or integrity

Etymology

From Latin 'veracitas', from 'verax' (truthful), from 'verus' (true).
