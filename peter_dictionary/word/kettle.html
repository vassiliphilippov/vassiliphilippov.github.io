kettle

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɛtəl / (AmE), / ˈkɛtl̩ / (BrE)
a container, usually made of metal and often with a lid, used for boiling water or stewing food, typically on a stove

Examples

It was in the kitchen, as she was watching the kettle, waiting for the water to boil, that he had grabbed for her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teapot - a pot for making and serving tea
  • Degree Lower: pan - a shallow, often metal container for cooking

Etymology

From Old English 'cetel', from Latin 'catillus' (a small bowl or dish), diminutive of 'catinus' (deep vessel).
