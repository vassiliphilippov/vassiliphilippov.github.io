high-backed

1. ADJ

/ haɪ bækt / (AmE), / haɪ bækt / (BrE)
(of a chair or other seat) having a back that extends considerably higher than usual, offering support for the upper back and sometimes the head.

Examples

Monsieur Favre sat down in his high-backed stall, lips compressed, eyes glinting.

Synset Examples

a high-backed sofa
