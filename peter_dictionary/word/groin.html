groin

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɔɪn / (AmE), / ɡrɔɪn / (BrE)
the area of the body where the inner thigh meets the trunk, including the crease and adjacent region, often encompassing the external genitals

Examples

Its groin was bloody.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inguinal region - anatomical term for the groin area
