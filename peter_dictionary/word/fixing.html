fixing

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪksɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of repairing or restoring something to proper condition or working order.

Examples

There were three major administrative tasks: the fixing of electoral districts, the registration of voters and the registration of candidates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repair - the act of fixing something that is broken or damaged
  • Degree Lower: maintenance - the work of keeping something in proper condition
  • Degree Higher: restoration - the act of returning something to a former condition

Etymology

From the verb 'fix' + -ing, meaning the act of making something stable or repairing.
