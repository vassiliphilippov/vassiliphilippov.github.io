farm

1. NOUN

/ fɑrm / (AmE), / fɑːm / (BrE)
An area of land and its buildings used for growing crops and raising animals, typically as a business.

Examples

Etymology

From Old French 'ferme' (a fixed payment), from Medieval Latin 'firma' (contract, farm), from Latin 'firmare' (to fix, establish).
