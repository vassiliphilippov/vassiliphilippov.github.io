intercontinental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˌkɑːn.t̬əˈnen.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.təl / (BrE)
Existing, extending, or occurring between two or more continents.

Examples

He has gained only one thing - he has exploded a 50 - megaton bomb and he probably has rockets with sufficient thrust to lob it over the shorter intercontinental ranges.
The first capability is represented by a combination of manned bombers, carrier based aircraft, and intercontinental and intermediate range missiles.

Synset Examples

intercontinental exploration
intercontinental flights

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transcontinental - extending across a continent

Etymology

Formed from Latin prefix *inter-* (between) and 'continental'.
