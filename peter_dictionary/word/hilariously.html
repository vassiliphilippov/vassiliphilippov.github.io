hilariously

1. ADV

/ hɪˈlɛriəsli / (AmE), / hɪˈlɑːrɪəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely funny or causes a lot of laughter.

Examples

There are some sharp and whipping lines and some hilariously funny situations - the best of the latter being a mass impromptu plunge into a nightclub tank where a "mermaid "is performing.

Synset Examples

hilariously funny

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hilarious - extremely funny; causing great laughter

Etymology

Formed from 'hilarious' + adverbial suffix -ly; 'hilarious' from Latin 'hilaris' (cheerful, merry).
