reefer

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːfər / (AmE), / ˈriːfə / (BrE)
A marijuana cigarette; a joint.

Examples

Cigarettes, a bag of reefer - if that's your thing - a bottle of brandy to celebrate your kid's high-school graduation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joint - a marijuana cigarette

Etymology

Probably from 'reef' in the sense of rolling up, as in rolling a sail.
