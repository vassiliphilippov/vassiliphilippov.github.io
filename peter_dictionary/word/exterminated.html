exterminated

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈstɜrməˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having been destroyed completely; eradicated so nothing remains.

Examples

They're clean and exterminated. -

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destroyed - having been ruined or demolished
  • Antonym: intact - not damaged or impaired in any way
  • Degree Lower: damaged - partially harmed or impaired; not whole

Etymology

From Latin exterminare (to drive out, expel).
