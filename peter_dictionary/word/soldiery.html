soldiery

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊldʒəri / (AmE), / ˈsəʊldjəri / (BrE)
Soldiers collectively; the body of people who serve as soldiers.

Examples

The Prime Minister paid his respects to the Buddhist monks, strode rapidly among the houses, joked with the local soldiery, and made a speech.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: military - the armed forces; the military establishment
  • Synonym: troops - soldiers, especially in large numbers

Etymology

From soldier + -y, modeled after French *soldaterie* or Italian *soldatesca*.
