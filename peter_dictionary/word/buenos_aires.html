Buenos Aires

1. PROPN

/ ˌbweɪnoʊs ˈaɪriːz / (AmE), / ˌbwɛnɒs ˈaɪəriːz / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Argentina, located on the southeastern coast of the country; the country's main port and commercial, industrial, and cultural centre.

Examples

He landed in Buenos Aires yesterday.

Etymology

From Spanish, meaning 'fair winds'.
