swaybacked

1. ADJ

/ ˈsweɪˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈsweɪˌbækt / (BrE)
Having an abnormally sagging or hollowed spine, especially in horses.

Examples

There were two horses in the barn, a sway-backed dun and Jess Crouch's buckskin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hollow-backed - having a deeply curved or hollow back

Etymology

From 'swayback', meaning a spine that sags downward, with '-ed' indicating 'having'.
