unfitting

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Not suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose, situation, or person.

Examples

Most of us would say that in this delight at the killing of others or the causing of suffering there is something very unfitting.
If we were asked why we thought so, we should say that these things involve great evil and are wrong, and that to take delight in what is evil or wrong is plainly unfitting.
In such a case, any attitude would be as fitting or unfitting as any other, which means that the notion of fitness has lost all point.

Synset Examples

completely inappropriate behavior

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inappropriate - not suitable or proper in a particular situation
  • Synonym: unsuitable - not appropriate or right for a particular purpose or occasion
  • Antonym: fitting - appropriate or suitable for a particular purpose or occasion

Etymology

Formed from the prefix "un-" (not) and the adjective "fitting" (suitable).
