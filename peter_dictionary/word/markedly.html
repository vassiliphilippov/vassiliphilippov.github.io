markedly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɑrkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is clearly noticeable or distinct; strikingly.

Examples

For the boy, this epiphysis was markedly delayed at Onset but near the mean at Completion.
Sporting firearms change, markedly for the better.

Synset Examples

sales of luxury cars dropped markedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distinctly - in a way that is clear or unmistakable; synonym
  • Synonym: noticeably - in a way that is easily noticed; synonym

Etymology

Formed from the adjective 'marked' (with a clear sign or indication) plus the adverbial suffix '-ly.'
