fussily

1. ADV

/ ˈfʌsɪli / (AmE), / ˈfʌsɪli / (BrE)
In a fussy or overly particular manner, showing excessive attention to detail or concern about small matters.

Examples

He said fussily, "Just keep the cap on those strong emotions ".

Synset Examples

he spoke to her fussily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fussy - showing excessive concern about details or cleanliness

Etymology

From 'fussy' (English) + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
