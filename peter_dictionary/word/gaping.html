gaping

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡeɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
Having the mouth wide open, especially in wonder, awe, or surprise.

Examples

And the cast shell of a locust, straw colored and transparent, weighing nothing, fragile but entire, with eyes like bubbles and a gaping slit down its back.

Synset Examples

the gaping audience
we stood there agape with wonder
with mouth agape

Related Terms

  • Synonym: agape - with the mouth wide open in wonder or surprise
