portable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be easily carried or moved, especially because of being a lighter or smaller version than usual

Examples

The portable record player with a pile of classical records beside it.
Men were slaughtered every day, tumbled into eternity like so many torn parcels flung down a portable chute.
A portable electric heater is advisable for shelters in cold climates.

Synset Examples

a portable television set

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mobile - able to be moved or transported easily
  • Antonym: fixed - firmly attached and not intended to be moved

Etymology

From Latin portare (to carry), via Old French and Middle English.
