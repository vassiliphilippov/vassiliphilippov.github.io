sultry

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌl.tɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.tri / (BrE)
(especially of a person's expression, appearance, or manner) sexually attractive or exciting; arousing strong sexual feelings.

Examples

The ballet work is on the nose, especially in the opening number by "The New Ashmolean Marching Society and Students' Conservatory Band ", along with a fiery and sultry Brazilian fantasia later.

Synset Examples

sensual excesses
a sultry look
a sultry dance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensual - arousing or gratifying sexual desire

Etymology

Origin uncertain; probably from sulter, an alteration of swelter ('to suffer from oppressive heat').
