minimized

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪnɪˌmaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪˌmaɪzd / (BrE)
Reduced to the smallest possible size, amount, or degree.

Examples

Are you careful to restrict the number of people on leave at one time so that your total employment obligation is minimized?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reduced - made smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
  • Antonym: maximized - increased to the greatest possible amount or degree

Etymology

From the verb 'minimize', which derives from Latin 'minimus' (smallest).
