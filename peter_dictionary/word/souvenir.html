souvenir

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːvəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌsuːvəˈnɪə / (BrE)
An object kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event, often having sentimental value.

Examples

"And also, sir, two articles which were considered souvenirs now must be regarded in another light entirely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: keepsake - an object kept in memory of a person or event
  • Synonym: memento - an object kept as a reminder

Etymology

From French 'souvenir' (remembrance, memory), from Latin 'subvenire' (to come to mind).
