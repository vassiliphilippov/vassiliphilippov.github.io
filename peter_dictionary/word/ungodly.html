ungodly

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡɑd.li / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɒd.li / (BrE)
Morally wrong or wicked, especially in a way considered sinful or irreligious.

Examples

"That is the answer the ungodly will always make when the Church points its fingers at their sins.
Dice rolled, prostitutes plied their trade, thieves stole, murderers stabbed, and the ungodly blasphemed.

Synset Examples

iniquitous deeds
he said it was sinful to wear lipstick
ungodly acts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sinful - morally wrong or wicked, especially from a religious point of view
  • Antonym: righteous - morally right or justifiable

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'godly' (devout, pious).
