moneymaker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌniˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is successful in accumulating wealth or generating money, often through business or enterprise

Examples

And so well is such ignorance preserved by the amateur and the money-maker that even at the college level most of the hundred odd folklore courses given in the United States survive on sentiment and nationalism alone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earner - a person who earns money, especially for a job
  • Synonym: breadwinner - the person who earns money to support a family

Etymology

From 'money' + 'maker', meaning one who makes money.
