bald

1. ADJ

/ bɔld / (AmE), / bɔːld / (BrE)
having little or no hair on the scalp

Examples

By the time he was prosperous enough - his goals were high - he was bald and afraid of women.

Synset Examples

a bald pate
a bald-headed gentleman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairless - having no hair (on any part of the body)

Etymology

From Old English *bald* (white patch, bald spot), related to baldan (to be white, be bald).

2. ADJ

/ bɔld / (AmE), / bɔːld / (BrE)
(of a statement, lie, etc.) stated openly and plainly, with no attempt to disguise or conceal the truth or facts.

Examples

Nothing bald, open; but enough.

Synset Examples

a barefaced lie

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barefaced - open; shameless; undisguised (especially of lies or statements)
  • Synonym: blatant - (of bad behavior) done openly and unashamedly
  • Antonym: covert - not openly acknowledged or displayed

Etymology

From Old English *bald* (white patch, bald), later extended to mean 'without adornment or disguise'.
