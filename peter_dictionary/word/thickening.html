thickening

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɪkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθɪkənɪŋ / (BrE)
increasing in density, intensity, or obscurity; becoming more concentrated or intense, especially in reference to darkness, atmosphere, or emotion

Examples

He was standing there, he thought, in Virginia, in the thickening dusk, in a costly greatcoat that had belonged to another Jew.

Synset Examples

the deepening gloom
felt a deepening love
the thickening dusk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intensifying - becoming more intense
  • Antonym: lessening - becoming less in intensity; decreasing
