bulletin

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlətɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʊlətɪn / (BrE)
a brief official statement or report, especially one issued for immediate publication or broadcast

Examples

Periodic bulletins of the accomplishment in each province made the registration process into a kind of competition among provincial officials.
This happy bulletin convulsed Mr. Gorboduc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: announcement - public or formal notice giving information
  • Synonym: communiqué - official announcement or statement released to the media

Etymology

From French 'bulletin' (report, note), diminutive of 'bulle' (bull, official document, seal).
