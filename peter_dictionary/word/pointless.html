pointless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɔɪntləs / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntlɪs / (BrE)
Having no purpose or usefulness; serving no practical function.

Examples

It's got no use on the inside.

Synset Examples

otiose lines in a play
advice is wasted words
a pointless remark
a life essentially purposeless
senseless violence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: useless - not fulfilling any useful purpose
  • Synonym: meaningless - having no meaning or significance
  • Antonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose
  • Antonym: purposeful - having a clear purpose or aim

Etymology

From 'point' + '-less', meaning 'without a point or purpose'.
