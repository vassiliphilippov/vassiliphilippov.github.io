scram

1. VERB

/ skræm / (AmE), / skræm / (BrE)
to leave quickly or go away immediately, usually used as a command

Examples

Scram!

Synset Examples

Scram!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leave - to go away from a place
  • Antonym: linger - to stay in a place longer than necessary

Etymology

Probably from American slang, possibly an alteration of 'scramble'.
