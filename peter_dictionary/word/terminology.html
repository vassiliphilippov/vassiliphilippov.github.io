terminology

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɝː.məˈnɑː.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌtɜː.mɪˈnɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
the set of specialized words and expressions used in a particular subject, science, or profession

Examples

If the volume is the molal volume, then * * f is obtained on a molal basis which is the customary terminology of the chemists.
This terminology will also be applied to the corresponding points in the f-plane.

Synset Examples

legal terminology
biological nomenclature
the language of sociology

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nomenclature - the system of naming in a particular field

Etymology

From Medieval Latin terminologia, from Latin terminus (end, boundary) + -logia (study).
