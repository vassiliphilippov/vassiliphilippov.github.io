luster

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌstər / (AmE), / ˈlʌstə / (BrE)
a gentle sheen or soft glow, especially on a surface; a quality of brightness or brilliance.

Examples

In a lacy open weave shoes have a luster finish, braided collar and bow highlight on the squared throat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gloss - shine or luster on a smooth surface
  • Antonym: matte - having a dull or non-shiny surface

Etymology

From Latin *lustrum* (purification); via Old French and Middle English.
