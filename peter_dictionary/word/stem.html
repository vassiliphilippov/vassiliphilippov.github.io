Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arise - to originate or result from a particular source
  • Synonym: originate - to begin or start from a source
  • Synonym: result - to happen as a consequence of something

Etymology

Figurative use from the idea of growth or origin, derived from the noun 'stem.'

3. NOUN

/ stɛm / (AmE), / stɛm / (BrE)
(Linguistics) The main part of a word to which affixes are added, remaining after all inflectional affixes have been removed.

Examples

The many linguistic techniques for reducing the amount of dictionary information that have been proposed all organize the dictionary's contents around prefixes, stems, suffixes, etc..
