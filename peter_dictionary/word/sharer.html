sharer

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɛrɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃeərə / (BrE)
a person who has, gives, or receives a share of something; someone who shares in something with others

Examples

Or else the North really believes that all Southerners except a few quaint old characters have come around to realizing the errors of their past, and are now at heart sharers of the American Dream, like everybody else.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partaker - a person who takes part in something
  • Synonym: recipient - a person who receives something
  • Synonym: contributor - a person who gives or provides something as part of a group

Etymology

From the verb 'share' + -er (agent noun suffix).
