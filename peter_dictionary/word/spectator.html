spectator

1. NOUN

/ spɛkˈteɪtɚ / (AmE), / spɛkˈteɪtə / (BrE)
a person who watches an event, show, or activity, usually as part of an audience but without taking part

Related Terms

  • Synonym: audience - a group of people who watch or listen to a performance or event
  • Antonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity or event

Etymology

From Latin 'spectator', from 'spectare' (to watch).
