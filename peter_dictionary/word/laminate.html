laminate

1. VERB

/ ˈlæm.ə.neɪt / (AmE), / ˈlam.ɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
to create a product by bonding together thin layers of material with adhesive or other means

Examples

The stem is laminated from four pieces.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bond - join materials together using an adhesive or other means

Etymology

From Latin 'lamina' (thin plate, layer), via French 'laminer'.
