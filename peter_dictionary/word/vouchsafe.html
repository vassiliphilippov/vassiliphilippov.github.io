vouchsafe

1. VERB

/ vaʊtʃˈseɪf / (AmE), / vaʊtʃˈseɪf / (BrE)
to give or grant something to someone, often in a gracious or condescending manner

Examples

They hope that if history vouchsafes the West another Budapest, we will receive the opportunity gladly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grant - to give or allow something officially
  • Antonym: deny - to refuse to give or grant

Etymology

From Middle English 'vouchsaf', from Old French 'vosaf', from 'vo(u)chier' (to vouch) + 'sauf' (safe).
