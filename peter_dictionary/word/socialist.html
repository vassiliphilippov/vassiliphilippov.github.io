socialist

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who supports or advocates socialism, especially as a political philosophy or movement.

Examples

Whatever the merits of its intent, Utopian communism is far too naive, far too crude, to suit any modern socialist or communist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - a person who supports or advocates communism
  • Antonym: capitalist - a person who supports or advocates capitalism

Etymology

From French *socialiste*, from *social* (social), following the pattern of similar political terms.
