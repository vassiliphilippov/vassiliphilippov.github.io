coating

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
A thin layer or covering applied to the surface of something.

Examples

But our two Jeep mates - Keo Viphakone from Luang Prabang and John Cool from Beaver, Pennsylvania - were beaming under their coatings of dust.

Synset Examples

a second coat of paint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layer - a single thickness of a material covering a surface or between surfaces
  • Synonym: sheath - a protective covering

Etymology

Formed from 'coat' + '-ing', using the noun sense of 'coat' (covering layer).
