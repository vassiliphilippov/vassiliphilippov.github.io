punctuality

1. NOUN

/ ˌpʌŋktʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌpʌŋktjuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or habit of being on time or adhering to an agreed or appointed time

Examples

The parent was asked to describe the child's typical behavior in certain standard situations in which there was an opportunity to observe tendencies toward perfectionism in demands upon self and others, irrational conformity to rules, orderliness, punctuality, and need for certainty.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tardiness - the quality of being late or delayed

Etymology

Formed from 'punctual' (from Latin punctualis, from punctum 'a point') + '-ity'.
