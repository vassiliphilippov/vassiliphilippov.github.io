undeclared

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌndɪˈklɛrd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈklɛːd / (BrE)
Not officially announced or openly acknowledged.

Examples

Then suddenly we found ourselves in the middle of another fight, an irrational, an indecent, an undeclared and immoral war with our strongest (and some had thought noblest) ally.

Synset Examples

fighting an undeclared war

Related Terms

  • Antonym: declared - officially announced or openly stated

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'declared' (from Latin 'declarare,' to make clear).
