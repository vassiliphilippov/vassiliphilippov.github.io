lotus

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.təs / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.təs / (BrE)
A large aquatic plant native to eastern Asia, known for its broad leaves and showy pink or white flowers, often cultivated in water gardens.

Examples

Repeating it like a student telling himself that the jewel is in the lotus he sank into nirvana.

Etymology

From Latin 'lotus', from Greek 'lōtos', originally used of several plants.
