Guinea

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪni / (AmE), / ˈɡɪni / (BrE)
An offensive, derogatory term for a person of Italian descent.

Examples

You Guinea brat.
You Guineas make me laugh.

Etymology

Originally from the country name 'Guinea'; later used in the U.S. as an ethnic slur for Italians, possibly from association with dark-skinned people or Italian coins minted in Guinea gold.
