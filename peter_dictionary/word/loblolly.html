loblolly

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑːbˌlɑːli / (AmE), / ˈlɒbˌlɒli / (BrE)
A thick, soupy porridge or gruel.

Examples

The rain dripped on the freezing loblolly of the street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gruel - a thin liquid food of oatmeal or other meal boiled in milk or water
  • Synonym: porridge - a dish consisting of oatmeal or another meal or cereal boiled in water or milk

Etymology

Probably from English dialect 'lob' (to boil) + 'lolly' (a broth or stew).
