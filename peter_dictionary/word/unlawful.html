unlawful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɔfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɔːfəl / (BrE)
Not permitted by law; illegal or not conforming to accepted standards or rules.

Examples

Any agreement to the contrary shall be unlawful and void.

Synset Examples

an unconventional marriage
improper banking practices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegal - forbidden by law or statute
  • Antonym: lawful - permitted by law

Etymology

From Old English 'un-' (not) + 'lawful'.
