indefinable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.dəˈfaɪ.nə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈfaɪ.nə.bəl / (BrE)
Impossible to describe or explain exactly; not capable of being clearly defined.

Examples

For an instant the old aunt felt something indefinable flash through her smile.

Synset Examples

an indefinable feeling of terror
an abstract concept that seems indefinable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribable - impossible to describe
  • Antonym: definable - able to be defined or explained

Etymology

From Latin prefix in- (not) + English definable (able to be defined).
