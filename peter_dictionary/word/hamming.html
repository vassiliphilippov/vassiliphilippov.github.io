hamming

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhæmɪŋ / (BrE)
exaggerated or inauthentic acting, especially that which is overly theatrical or lacking subtlety

Examples

He dropped his earlier and delightful hamming, which is about the only way to handle the old war horse called "Charley's Aunt ", and let himself go with as an appealing an "Amy "as anybody could ask.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overacting - acting with excessive emotion or theatricality

Etymology

From 'ham', slang for an actor who overacts, of uncertain origin.
