1. NOUN

/ ˈɛvədəns / (AmE), / ˈɛvɪdəns / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proof - information that shows something is definitely true
  • Synonym: testimony - a formal written or spoken statement as evidence
  • Antonym: disproof - evidence that shows something is not true

Etymology

From Latin evidens (obvious, apparent), from ex- (out) + videre (to see).
