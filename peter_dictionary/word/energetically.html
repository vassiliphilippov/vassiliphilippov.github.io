energetically

1. ADV

/ ˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or involves a lot of energy and enthusiasm.

Examples

On Election Day - November 4 - he energetically marshalled his force of bludgeon men, bribers, and experts in forging repeat votes.
He, McBride, would be cited as in the wrong, and he, Lord, would go scot-free, an officer who had only done his duty, though perhaps too energetically.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigorously - in a vigorous manner; with force and energy
  • Antonym: lazily - in a lazy or languid manner; without energy

Etymology

Formed from 'energetic' + '-ally', patterned after 'energy'.
