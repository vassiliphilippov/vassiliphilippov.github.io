genius

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒiː.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.ni.əs / (BrE)
a person with exceptional skill or ability in a particular field

Examples

If the change, at first sight, seems minor, we may recall that it took the Italian painters about two hundred years to make an analogous change, and the Italian painters, by universal consent, were the most brilliant group of geniuses any art has seen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prodigy - a person, especially a young one, endowed with exceptional qualities or abilities

Etymology

From Latin *genius* (inborn nature, talent).
