ablaze

1. ADJ

/ əˈbleɪz / (AmE), / əˈbleɪz / (BrE)
Showing or filled with intense excitement or emotion, especially as reflected in one's expression.

Examples

Straightening up, his eyes ablaze, he held out the battered Stetson.

Synset Examples

his face all ablaze with excitement
he was aflame with desire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aflame - filled with or showing intense emotion, especially excitement or desire

Etymology

Formed from the prefix a- (on, in) + blaze (fire, flame), first attested in the 18th century.
