bewilder

1. VERB

/ bɪˈwɪldər / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldə / (BrE)
to confuse someone completely; to make someone unable to understand or think clearly

Examples

It bewilders and befuddles him.

Synset Examples

This beats me!
Got me--I don't know the answer!
a vexing problem
This question really stuck me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confuse - to make someone unable to think clearly or understand
  • Synonym: perplex - to make someone puzzled or unable to understand
  • Antonym: enlighten - to make someone understand clearly; opposite of confuse

Etymology

From be- (prefix) + obsolete 'wilder' (to lead astray).
