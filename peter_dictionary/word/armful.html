armful

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑrmˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈɑːm.fʊl / (BrE)
As much as can be carried in one or both arms at one time.

Examples

He saw the figure of a man in a poncho coming up the company street, with an armful of wood.

Etymology

From 'arm' + 'ful', modelled after similar noun formations indicating quantity.
