traveled

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ˈtrævəld / (BrE)
(of a road, route, or area) having been much used or passed over by travelers; frequently used or visited.

Examples

The bond issue will go to the state courts for a friendly test suit to test the validity of the act, and then the sales will begin and contracts let for repair work on some of Georgia's most heavily traveled highways.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untraveled - not having been traveled over or visited

2. ADJ

/ ˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ˈtrævəld / (BrE)
having visited or become familiar with many parts of the world; experienced in travel

Examples

But the firm has recognized the tight dollar and the tourist's desire to visit the "smaller, less traveled and relatively inexpensive countries ", and is now prepared to teach modern Greek and Portuguese through recordings.

Synset Examples

a traveled, educated man
well-traveled people

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-traveled - having traveled to many places; widely traveled
