philosophically

1. ADV

/ fəˈlɑːsəfɪkli / (AmE), / fəˈlɒsəfɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to philosophy or shows a calm, thoughtful attitude towards difficult situations.

Examples

Finally, we may also mention the several members of the self-consciously "neoliberal "movement that developed at the University of Chicago and is heavily indebted philosophically to the creative work of Alfred North Whitehead.

Synset Examples

she took it philosophically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thoughtfully - in a thoughtful or reflective manner
  • Synonym: stoically - in a calm, unemotional manner in difficult situations

Etymology

From 'philosophical' + '-ly', ultimately from Greek 'philosophia' (love of wisdom).
