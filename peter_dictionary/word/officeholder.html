officeholder

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.fɪsˌhoʊl.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈɒf.ɪsˌhəʊl.də / (BrE)
A person who holds a public office or an official position.

Examples

Our streets are overrun, our public officials are helpless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incumbent - the person currently holding an office or position

Etymology

Formed from 'office' + 'holder', meaning someone who holds an office.
