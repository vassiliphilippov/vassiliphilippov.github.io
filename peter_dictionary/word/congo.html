Congo

1. PROPN

/ ˈkɑːŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A country in central Africa, officially known as the Democratic Republic of the Congo, which gained independence from Belgium in 1960.

Examples

But at least the pessimists who believed that the world organization had plunged to its death in that plane crash in the Congo have been proved wrong.

Etymology

Named after the Congo River, itself derived from the Kongo people native to the region.
