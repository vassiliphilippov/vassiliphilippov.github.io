rubber

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
a tough, elastic substance made from the latex sap of certain tropical plants or produced synthetically, used in making tyres, boots, and many products

Related Terms

  • Synonym: latex - milky fluid from some plants, used to make rubber
  • Synonym: synthetic rubber - man-made elastic material similar to natural rubber

Etymology

Possibly from the verb 'rub', originally referring to rubber as an eraser for pencil marks.
