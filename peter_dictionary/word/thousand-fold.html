thousand-fold

1. ADV

/ ˈθaʊzənd foʊld / (AmE), / ˈθaʊzənd fəʊld / (BrE)
By a factor of one thousand; multiplied by a thousand times.

Examples

Botulinal toxin, for example, is several thousand-fold more toxic by this route than when given per os.

Synset Examples

this poison is a thousand-fold more toxic

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hundredfold - by a factor of one hundred
  • Degree Higher: millionfold - by a factor of one million

Etymology

Formed from 'thousand' plus '-fold', indicating multiplication.
