Colt

1. NOUN

/ koʊlt / (AmE), / kəʊlt / (BrE)
A type of revolver, especially one manufactured by Colt's Manufacturing Company.

Examples

Cursing himself for having ridden out the last few days without a rifle in his saddle boot, Russ drew his Colt and examined it briefly.

Etymology

From the name of the Colt's Manufacturing Company, founded by Samuel Colt.
