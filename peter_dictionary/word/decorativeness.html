decorativeness

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈkɔrətɪvnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈkɒrətɪvnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being decorative or serving to make something more attractive.

Examples

In analyzing the watercolors of Roy Mason, the first thing that comes to mind is their essential decorativeness, yet this word has such a varied connotation that it needs some elaboration here.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornamentation - the act of decorating or adorning with ornaments

Etymology

From 'decorative' + '-ness', formed on English roots.
