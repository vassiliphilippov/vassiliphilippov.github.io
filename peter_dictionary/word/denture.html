denture

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈdɛntʃə / (BrE)
A removable dental appliance used to replace missing teeth and surrounding tissues.

Examples

I do n't remember ever seeing teeth that were quite so white and at the same time quite so emphatically not dentures.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: false teeth - artificial teeth used as a replacement

Etymology

From French *denture* (set of teeth), from Latin *dens* (tooth).
