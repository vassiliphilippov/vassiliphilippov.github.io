tense

1. NOUN

/ tɛns / (AmE), / tɛns / (BrE)
(Grammar) A category of verbs that expresses the time at which an action or state occurs, such as past, present, or future.

Examples

The same use of an entirely different word applied for all the other tenses.
The system of indicating gender varied according to tense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aspect - A grammatical category that expresses how the action of a verb unfolds over time

Etymology

From Latin 'tempus' (time, tense in grammar).
