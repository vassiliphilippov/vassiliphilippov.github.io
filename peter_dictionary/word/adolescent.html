adolescent

1. ADJ

/ ˌædəˈlɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌadəˈlɛs(ə)nt / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of adolescence or young people in their teenage years.

Examples

They caused my love for Jessica to become warmer and at the same time more hopeless, as if my adolescent self knew that only torment would ever bring me the courage to ask to see her again.

Synset Examples

adolescent problems

Related Terms

  • Synonym: juvenile - relating to young people; youthful
  • Antonym: mature - fully developed emotionally or physically; adult

Etymology

From Latin adolescens, adolescentis, present participle of adolescere (to grow up).

2. NOUN

/ ˌædəˈlɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌadəˈlɛs(ə)nt / (BrE)
a young person who is developing from a child into an adult, typically between puberty and legal adulthood

Examples

For almost two months, the defendant and the world heard from individuals escaped from the grave about fathers and mothers, graybeards, adolescents, babies, starved, beaten to death, strangled, machine-gunned, gassed, burned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teenager - a person aged between 13 and 19 years old
  • Antonym: adult - a fully grown person
  • Antonym: child - a young human being before puberty

Etymology

From Latin 'adolescentem', accusative of 'adolescens' (growing up, young).
