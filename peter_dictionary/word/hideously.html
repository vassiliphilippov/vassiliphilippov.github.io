hideously

1. ADV

/ ˈhɪdiəsli / (AmE), / ˈhɪdiəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is extremely ugly, offensive, or shocking.

Examples

Griffith said, "Hoag told me to tell you "- he waited until they were close; it was hideously embarrassing - "not to let the prime minister be alone with Mahzeer ".

Synset Examples

her face was hideously disfigured after the accident

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horribly - in a horrible manner
  • Antonym: beautifully - in a beautiful manner

Etymology

From 'hideous' + '-ly'; 'hideous' from Old French 'hideus' (frightening, dreadful).
