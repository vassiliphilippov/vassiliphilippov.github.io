resonant

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛzənənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛzənənt / (BrE)
(of a sound, voice, or system) characterized by or exhibiting resonance; having a deep, clear, and continuing sound or able to reinforce or prolong sound.
