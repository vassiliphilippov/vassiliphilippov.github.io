Illinois

1. PROPN

/ ɪlɪˈnɔɪ / (AmE), / ɪlɪˈnɔɪ / (BrE)
A state in the midwestern region of the United States, located north of Missouri and west of Indiana, with Springfield as its capital and Chicago as its largest city.

Etymology
