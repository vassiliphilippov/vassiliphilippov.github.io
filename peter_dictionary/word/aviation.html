aviation

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪ.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.viˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the military aircraft operated by a country or armed force as an organized group

Examples

Primary target would be shipments of tanks, guns, aviation gasoline and ammunition coming from Russia and Czechoslovakia.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air force - a branch of a nation's armed services that conducts military operations in the air

Etymology

From French *aviation*, from Latin *avis* (bird) + *-ation*.
