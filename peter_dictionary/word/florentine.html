Florentine

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɔr.ənˌtin / (AmE), / ˈflɒr.ənˌtiːn / (BrE)
of or relating to the city of Florence, especially its people, culture, or art

Examples

He observed the Florentine vase in the hall, the Renoir painting in the library, as well as the long shelves of well-bound volumes; the pattern of the Oriental rug, the delicate cut-glass chandelier.

Synset Examples

Florentine art

Etymology

From Latin 'Florentinus', from 'Florentia' (Florence)
