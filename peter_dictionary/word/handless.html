handless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhændləs / (AmE), / ˈhændləs / (BrE)
Lacking a hand or hands.

Examples

They crowded the small room and peered over one another's shoulders to watch the handless man write his name in the book.

Synset Examples

a handless war veteran

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: one-handed - having only one hand
  • Degree Higher: armless - having no arms
