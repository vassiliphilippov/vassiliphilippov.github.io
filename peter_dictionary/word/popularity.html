popularity

1. NOUN

/ pɑːpjəˈlærəti / (AmE), / pɒpjʊˈlærɪti / (BrE)
The state or quality of being liked, admired, or supported by many people.

Etymology

From Latin popularitas (favour of the people), from popularis (belonging to the people).
