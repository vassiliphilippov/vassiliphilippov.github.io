redecorate

1. VERB

/ ˌriːˈdɛkəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈdɛkəreɪt / (BrE)
To change or update the decoration of a room or house, especially by painting, papering, or furnishing it differently.

Examples

How much will it cost to do any necessary modernizing and redecorating?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decorate - to add ornaments or color to improve the appearance of something

Etymology

Formed from re- (again) + decorate.
