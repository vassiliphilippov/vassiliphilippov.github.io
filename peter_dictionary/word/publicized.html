publicized

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪˌsaɪzd / (BrE)
Made known to the public, especially by being brought to the attention of many people.

Examples

A well publicized entrant which has achieved success only recently is the built liquid detergent, with which the major problem today is incorporation of builder and active into a small volume using a sufficiently high builder / active ratio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: publicize - to make something known to the public; to advertise or announce
  • Synonym: promoted - advertised or publicized so as to encourage sales or popularity
  • Antonym: secret - kept hidden or not known to others

Etymology

From the verb 'publicize' + -ed, meaning made public.
