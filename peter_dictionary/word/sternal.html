sternal

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɝːnəl / (AmE), / ˈstɜːnəl / (BrE)
relating to the sternum (breastbone) or located near it

Examples

Splenomegaly was first noted in 1956, and a sternal marrow biopsy at that time showed "scattered foci of fibrosis "suggestive of myelofibrosis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sternum - the breastbone; a long flat bone in the center of the chest

Etymology

From New Latin *sternalis*, from *sternum* (breastbone) + -al.
