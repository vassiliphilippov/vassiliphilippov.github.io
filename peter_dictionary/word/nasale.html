nasale

1. VERB

/ ˈneɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈneɪzəl / (BrE)
to speak in a nasal voice

Examples

"Let's not push our patience beyond the danger line, Sergeant ", Killpath nasaled.

Synset Examples

`Come here,' he nasaled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twang - to speak with a sharp nasal tone

Etymology

From nasal (relating to the nose) with the verb-forming suffix -e, modelled after French "nasaler".
