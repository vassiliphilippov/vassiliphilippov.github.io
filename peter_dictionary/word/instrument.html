instrument

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (BrE)
a tool or device, especially one that requires skill for proper use, such as for making music, conducting scientific experiments, or carrying out precise work

Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin instrumentum (tool, implement), from instruere (to build, prepare).
