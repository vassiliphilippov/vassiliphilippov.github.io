payroll

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪrəʊl / (BrE)
a list of a company's employees and the amount of money they are to be paid; the total amount paid to employees in wages and salaries.

Examples

And so deadheads on the payroll can be eased out at the earliest possible age.
Is he on the police payroll?

Synset Examples

the company had a long payroll

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wage bill - total amount paid to employees as wages

Etymology

From 'pay' + 'roll', originally referring to a list or register of those to be paid.
