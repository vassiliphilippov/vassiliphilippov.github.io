irresponsible

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪrɪˈspɑnsəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsəbl̩ / (BrE)
Not showing a proper sense of responsibility; showing a lack of care for the possible results of one's actions.

Examples

It is one of the most entertaining and irresponsible novels of the season.
We should do what we can to discourage this conclusion, both by offering assistance for their domestic needs and by reacting firmly to irresponsible actions on the world scene.

Synset Examples

behaved like an irresponsible idiot
hasty and irresponsible action

Related Terms

  • Antonym: responsible - having an obligation to do something or having control over or care for someone as part of one's job or role

Etymology

From Latin 'ir-' (not) + 'responsus', past participle of 'respondere' (to respond).
