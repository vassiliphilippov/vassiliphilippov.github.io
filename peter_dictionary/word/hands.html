2. NOUN

/ hændz / (AmE), / hændz / (BrE)
workers, especially manual laborers considered collectively as a workforce.

Examples

"It's the second time War Ax hands made a play for that money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workforce - all the workers employed in a company or country
  • Synonym: crew - a group of people working together, especially on ships or planes

Etymology

Plural of 'hand', in this sense referring to a person who works with their hands.
