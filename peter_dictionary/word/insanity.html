insanity

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈsænəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈsænəti / (BrE)
A severe mental illness or disorder causing a person to lose touch with reality.

Examples

"If we cannot tell evil, horror, and insanity from nonsense, what is the future of humor and comedy "?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: madness - state of severe mental illness; loss of reason
  • Antonym: sanity - sound mental health; rationality

Etymology

From Latin insania (unhealthiness of mind), from in- (not) + sanus (healthy).
