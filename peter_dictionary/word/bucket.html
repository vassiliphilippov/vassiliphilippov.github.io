bucket

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌkət / (AmE), / ˈbʌkɪt / (BrE)
a round, open container with a handle, used for carrying liquids or other substances

Examples

His hands snatched at an imaginary bucket, swooping down hard to grab it and coming away with equal snap like a ball that's been bounced hard.
And then came the water - not rain, but solid sheets that sluiced down like water slopping from a bucket.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pail - an open container for carrying liquids, similar to a bucket

Etymology

From Old English 'buc' (pitcher, bulging vessel).
