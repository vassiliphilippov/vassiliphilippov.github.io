/ siːm / (AmE), / siːm / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appear - to give the impression of being; to seem
  • Synonym: look - to seem or have the appearance of being

Etymology

From Middle English semen, from Old Norse sœma ('to conform to, befit').
