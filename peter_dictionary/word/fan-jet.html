fan-jet

1. NOUN

/ ˈfænˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈfanˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
An aircraft powered by a jet engine in which air is forced rearwards by a large fan in addition to exhaust gases.

Examples

That plane goes to Miami.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet - an aircraft powered by a jet engine

Etymology

From 'fan' + 'jet', referring to the type of jet engine that uses a fan to increase thrust.
