excellently

1. ADV

/ ˈɛksələntli / (AmE), / ˈɛksələntli / (BrE)
In an excellent or extremely good manner; very well.

Examples

The complexities of communication have been considerably abetted in this case by appropriately stilted English language that has been excellently dubbed in place of the Russian dialogue.

Synset Examples

he did splendidly in the exam
we got along famously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splendidly - in a splendid or impressive manner; very well
  • Antonym: poorly - in a poor or unsatisfactory manner

Etymology

From 'excellent' + adverbial suffix '-ly', from Latin 'excellens' (surpassing).
