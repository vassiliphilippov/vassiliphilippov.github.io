strenuous

1. ADJ

/ ˈstrɛnjuəs / (AmE), / ˈstrɛnjuːəs / (BrE)
requiring or marked by great effort, energy, or exertion

Examples

In their first two years in high school, Jewish boys in this town make strenuous exertions to win positions on the school teams.

Synset Examples

strenuous exercise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arduous - requiring a great deal of effort and endurance
  • Synonym: vigorous - done with force and energy
  • Antonym: effortless - requiring little or no effort

Etymology

From Latin *strenuus* (active, vigorous).
