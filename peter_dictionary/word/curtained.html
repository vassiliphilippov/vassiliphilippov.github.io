curtained

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɝːtənd / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtənd / (BrE)
furnished, covered, or concealed with curtains or draperies.

Examples

Far away, standing before a curtained window in the study room, was his father, hands tucked under his coattails, and staring into the dark church.

Synset Examples

a curtained alcove

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uncovered - not covered with curtains or anything else; exposed

Etymology

From 'curtain' + '-ed', denoting provided with or covered by curtains.
