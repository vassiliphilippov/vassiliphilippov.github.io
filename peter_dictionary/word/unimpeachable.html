unimpeachable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪmˈpitʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈpiːtʃəbəl / (BrE)
Not able to be doubted, questioned, or criticized; completely trustworthy or reliable.

Examples

In any given period piece the costumes, bric-a-brac, vehicles, and decor, bear the stamp of unimpeachable authenticity.

Synset Examples

an unimpeachable source

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impeccable - flawless; without fault or error
  • Antonym: questionable - open to doubt or criticism

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'impeachable', from 'impeach' meaning to accuse or bring into question.
