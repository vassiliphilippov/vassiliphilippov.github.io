crossroad

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɔsˌroʊd / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌrəʊd / (BrE)
A place where two or more roads cross each other; a junction.

Examples

Ahead there was a stirring of sudden movement at a crossroads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intersection - the place where two or more things, especially roads, meet or cross

Etymology

From cross + road, indicating the intersection of two roads.
