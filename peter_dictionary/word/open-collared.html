open-collared

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊ.pən ˌkɑː.lɚd / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən ˌkɒ.ləd / (BrE)
(of a shirt) having the collar unbuttoned at the neck; not fastened at the collar.

Examples

He was wearing sneakers and shorts and an open-collared shirt, and in his hand he carried a little black bag.

Synset Examples

dressed casually in shorts and an open-collared shirt

Related Terms

  • Antonym: buttoned-up - (of a shirt) having the collar fastened
