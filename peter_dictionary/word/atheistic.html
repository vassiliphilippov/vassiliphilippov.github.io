atheistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌæθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌæθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Rejecting or characterized by a rejection of belief in the existence of gods.

Examples

This is demonstrated especially when there is awareness of radically different value orientation elsewhere; for example Americans rally to Christian values vis-a-vis those of atheistic communism.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: theistic - relating to belief in the existence of a god or gods

Etymology

From 'atheist' + '-ic'. 'Atheist' from French 'athéiste', from Greek 'atheos' (without god).
