Israel

1. PROPN

/ ˈɪz.ri.əl / (AmE), / ˈɪz.ri.əl / (BrE)
A country in southwestern Asia, located at the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea; a Jewish republic established in 1948, formerly part of Palestine.

Examples

I went to Israel because I wish to live there as a Jew, in the twilight of my life.

Etymology

From Hebrew Yisra'el, meaning 'God contends' or 'He who struggles with God'.
