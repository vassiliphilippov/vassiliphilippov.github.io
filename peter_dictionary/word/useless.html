useless

1. ADJ

/ ˈjusləs / (AmE), / ˈjuːslɪs / (BrE)
Not having any practical use or unable to perform its function effectively.

Examples

Technical knowledge is a wonderful thing, but it's useless unless it eventually feeds the cash register.
The ship's compass was useless because of the nearness of the magnetic North Pole.
Feeling useless seems generally to be an unpleasant sensation.
That's one of the reasons art is becoming a useless occupation.
And now... ... comes the part... ... where I relieve you, the little people... ... of the burden... of your failed... ... and useless lives.

Synset Examples

a kitchen full of useless gadgets
she is useless in an emergency

Related Terms

  • Antonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose or in several ways
  • Synonym: worthless - having no value or use
  • Antonym: worthwhile - worth the time, money, or effort spent; of value or importance

Etymology

Formed from 'use' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without use'.
