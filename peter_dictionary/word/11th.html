11th

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɛvənθ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ / (BrE)
Coming after the tenth in a sequence; being number eleven in order or position.

Examples

On May 11, she reached Iceland.
Upon administrative approval of the reclassification, he was ordered to report for induction on June 11, 1956, but failed to do so.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 10th - ordinal number; tenth
  • Degree Higher: 12th - ordinal number; twelfth

Etymology

From 'eleven' + '-th' (forming ordinal numbers in English). 'Eleven' from Old English 'endleofan'.
