gala

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑlə / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlə / (BrE)
A special public celebration or party, often with entertainment or performances, held to mark a particular event or occasion.

Examples

The 200th anniversary birthday gala has been indefinitely postponed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: festivity - celebration or festive occasion
  • Synonym: celebration - a joyful public gathering or observance

Etymology

From Italian 'gala' (festive dress, show), perhaps from Spanish 'gala' (finery, show), of uncertain origin.
