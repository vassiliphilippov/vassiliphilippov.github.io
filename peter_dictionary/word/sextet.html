sextet

1. NOUN

/ sɛkˈstɛt / (AmE), / sɛkˈstɛt / (BrE)
A musical composition written for six performers.

Examples

She topped the sextet brilliantly.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: quartet - a musical composition for four performers
  • Degree Lower: quintet - a musical composition for five performers
  • Degree Higher: septet - a musical composition for seven performers

Etymology

From Italian 'sestetto', from 'sesto' (sixth), from Latin 'sextus' (sixth).
