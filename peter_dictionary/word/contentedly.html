contentedly

1. ADV

/ kənˈtɛntɪdli / (AmE), / kənˈtɛntɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows peaceful happiness or satisfaction.

Examples

"Now he's bought it ", said my wife contentedly.

Synset Examples

`I bought it,' she said contentedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happily - in a happy way
  • Antonym: discontentedly - in a dissatisfied or unhappy way

Etymology

From 'contented' (satisfied, happy), formed with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
