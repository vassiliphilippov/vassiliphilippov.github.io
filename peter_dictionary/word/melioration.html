melioration

1. NOUN

/ ˌmiːliəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmiːliəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something better; improvement, especially in the condition of something bad

Examples

After only eighteen years of non-interference, there were already indications of melioration, though "in a slight degree ", to be sure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improvement - the process of making something better

Etymology

From Latin melioratio, from meliorare (to make better).
