routinely

1. ADV

/ ruˈtinli / (AmE), / ruːˈtiːnli / (BrE)
In a regular or habitual way; as part of a routine or established practice.

Examples

Livestock must be tended every day, routinely.

Synset Examples

he routinely parked in a no-parking zone

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regularly - at regular intervals; consistently or habitually

Etymology

Formed from 'routine' (from French 'routine', meaning 'habit or practice') with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
