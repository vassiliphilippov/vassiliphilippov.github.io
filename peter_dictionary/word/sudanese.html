Sudanese

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuːdəˈniːz / (AmE), / ˌsuːdəˈniːz / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Sudan.

Examples

Whether the pair of Sudanese ivory carvings you lifted really possess the juju to turn your livers to lead, as a dealer in Khartoum assured me, I am not competent to say.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sudanese - of or relating to Sudan or its people

Etymology

Formed from Sudan + -ese, after similar demonyms (e.g. Chinese).
