fascist

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / ˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of fascism or fascist movements.

Examples

The world exploded when Fascism challenged all concepts of peace and liberty, and the outraged, freedom loving peoples of the Capitalist and Socialist worlds combined forces to stamp Fascist tyranny into cringing submission.

Synset Examples

fascist propaganda

Related Terms

  • Antonym: anti-fascist - opposed to fascism or fascists

Etymology

From Italian *fascista*, from *fascio* meaning 'bundle, group'.
