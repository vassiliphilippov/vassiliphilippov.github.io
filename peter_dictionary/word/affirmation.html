affirmation

1. NOUN

/ ˌæfərˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæfəˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
A statement that asserts the existence or truth of something.

Examples

The biblical symbol for this affirmation is expressed in the words: "So God created man in his own image; in the similitude of God he created him ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avowal - an open declaration or acknowledgment
  • Antonym: denial - statement that something is not true or does not exist

Etymology

From Latin affirmatio, from affirmare (to assert, affirm).
