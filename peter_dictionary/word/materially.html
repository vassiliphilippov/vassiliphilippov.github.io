materially

1. ADV

/ məˈtɪr.i.ə.li / (AmE), / məˈtɪə.ri.ə.li / (BrE)
In a way that relates to material or physical aspects, rather than abstract or emotional ones.

Examples

It will readily be seen that in this suggested network (not materially different from some of the networks in vogue today) greater emphasis on monitoring is implied than is usually put into practice.

Synset Examples

psychologically similar but materially different

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physically - in a way that involves the body or physical matter

Etymology

From the adjective 'material' + adverbial suffix '-ly'. 'Material' from Latin 'materialis', from 'materia' (matter, substance).
