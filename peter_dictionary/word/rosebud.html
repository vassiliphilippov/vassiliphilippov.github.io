rosebud

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊz.bʌd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊz.bʌd / (BrE)
the flower bud of a rose before it opens

Examples

If he had had a son, he would tell him, "Gather ye rosebuds while ye may.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bud - undeveloped or embryonic shoot, often the beginning stage of a flower
