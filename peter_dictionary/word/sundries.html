sundries

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn.dɹiz / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.dɹiz / (BrE)
various small, miscellaneous items or objects that are too numerous or minor to be named individually

Examples

"In addition, the Library District has generously responded with a charitable donation of used books and sundries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miscellaneous - consisting of a variety of different things, mixed

Etymology

From the adjective 'sundry', meaning 'various', from Old English 'syndrig' (separate, special).
