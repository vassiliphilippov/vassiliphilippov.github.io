prepackaged

1. ADJ

/ ˌpriˈpækɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈpækɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of goods or food) prepared, wrapped, and ready for sale before being placed in a store.

Examples

What are the possibilities for operating your cafeteria for a single shift only and relying upon vending machines or prepackaged sandwiches for the second - and third shift operations?

Synset Examples

prepackaged foods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: packaged - prepared and put into a package for sale

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'pre-' (before) and 'packaged' (from 'package').
