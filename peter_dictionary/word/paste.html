1. NOUN

/ peɪst / (AmE), / peɪst / (BrE)
a soft, moist, and malleable mixture or substance, often used in cooking, crafts, or industry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dough - thick, malleable mixture of flour and liquid used for baking
  • Synonym: putty - soft, malleable substance used in construction and repairs

Etymology

From Middle English 'pasta', from Old French, from Latin 'pasta', meaning dough or paste.
