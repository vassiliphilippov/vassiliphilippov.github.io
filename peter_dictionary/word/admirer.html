admirer

1. NOUN

/ ædˈmaɪɚ / (AmE), / ədˈmaɪərə / (BrE)
A person who admires, supports, or shows approval of someone or something, often expressing praise or affection.

Examples

But time is running out, and many of Stravinsky's admirers begin to fear that he will never find terra firma.

Synset Examples

all their supporters came out for the game
they are friends of the library

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supporter - A person who backs a person, cause, or team
  • Synonym: fan - A person who enthusiastically supports a person, group, or team
  • Antonym: critic - A person who expresses disapproval or finds fault

Etymology

From admire + -er; 'admire' from Latin admirari (to wonder at).
