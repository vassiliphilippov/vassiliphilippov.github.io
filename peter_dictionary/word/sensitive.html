1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛn.sə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.sɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
Quick to detect or respond to the feelings, attitudes, or circumstances of others; showing understanding and awareness of other people's needs or feelings.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin sensitivus (capable of sensation), from Latin sentire (to feel).
