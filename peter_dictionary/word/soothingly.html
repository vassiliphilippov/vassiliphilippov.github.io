soothingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsuːðɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsuːðɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that calms or relieves distress or discomfort.

Examples

[PANTING] No. [SOOTHINGLY]

Synset Examples

the mother talked soothingly to her child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reassuringly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried

Etymology

From the adjective 'soothing' + -ly.
