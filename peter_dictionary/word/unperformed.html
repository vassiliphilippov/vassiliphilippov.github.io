unperformed

1. ADJ

/ ʌn.pɚˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ʌn.pəˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
Not performed; not carried out, executed, or enacted.

Examples

Back and forth Duclos had to go, between M. de Cury and Jean Jacques and between the Duke d'Aumont and Jean Jacques again, as his little operetta, The Village Soothsayer, though still unperformed, took on ever more importance.

Synset Examples

the author of numerous unperformed plays

Related Terms

  • Antonym: performed - carried out, executed, or enacted
