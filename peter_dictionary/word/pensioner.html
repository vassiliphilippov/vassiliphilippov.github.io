pensioner

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnʃənər / (AmE), / ˈpɛnʃənə / (BrE)
a person who receives a pension, especially after retirement from work

Examples

William, he was called, in honor of the man who was at once Shelley's pensioner and his most bitter detractor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retiree - a person who has retired from work

Etymology

From pension + -er; 'pension' from Latin 'pensiō' (payment).
