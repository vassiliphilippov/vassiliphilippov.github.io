self

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf / (AmE), / sɛlf / (BrE)
a person's essential being that distinguishes them as a unique individual, especially in contrast to others or to their external environment

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identity - the fact of being who or what a person is

Etymology

From Old English 'self', related to Old High German 'selb', Latin 'sem-'.
