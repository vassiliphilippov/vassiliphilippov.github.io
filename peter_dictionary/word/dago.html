dago

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory term used for a person of Italian descent.

Examples

These Dagos are crazy when it comes to their wives.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from Spanish 'Diego' used as a generic name for a Spaniard, later applied derogatorily to Italians.
