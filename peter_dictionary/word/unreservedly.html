unreservedly

1. ADV

/ ʌn.rɪˈzɝv.ɪd.li / (AmE), / ʌn.rɪˈzɜːv.ɪd.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows complete openness, sincerity, or lack of holding back; without any reservation or hesitation.

Examples

His life-long friend, Serge Koussevitzky, gave unreservedly of his praise and brilliant performances in Boston, New York, and Washington, D. C., to which he added broadcastings and recordings for the whole nation.

Synset Examples

I can unreservedly recommend this restaurant!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wholeheartedly - with complete sincerity and commitment
  • Antonym: partially - in a way that is not complete or entire

Etymology

Formed from 'unreserved' + '-ly', where 'reserved' is from Latin 'reservare' (to keep back).
