blacken

1. VERB

/ ˈblæk.ən / (AmE), / ˈblæk.ən / (BrE)
to make or become black or darker in color

Examples

The mucosa of the stomach was atrophic and irregularly blackened over a 14 cm. area.
He patted the eye Joel had had blackened in a fight over being Rebel at the crossroads some days back.

Synset Examples

The smoke blackened the ceiling
The ceiling blackened

Related Terms

  • Synonym: darken - to make or become dark or darker
  • Antonym: whiten - to make or become white or whiter
