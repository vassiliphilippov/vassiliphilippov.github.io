heavenly

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛvənli / (AmE), / ˈhɛvənli / (BrE)
Relating to or belonging to heaven; of or inhabiting the divine or spiritual realm.

Examples

The glorious news had been held up pending Heavenly confirmation of the elevation of a new Supreme Bishop, Huey Short - a candidate accepted by the Boone faction after lots had been cast repeatedly.

Synset Examples

celestial beings
heavenly hosts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celestial - relating to heaven or the sky; heavenly

Etymology

From Old English heofonlic (of heaven), from heofon (heaven) + -lic (-ly).
