2. VERB

/ ˈkɑn.stəˌtut / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.stɪ.tjuːt / (BrE)
to formally appoint or establish someone in a role or with responsibility for a task

Examples

My mother constituted herself the voice of all of us.

Synset Examples

nominate a committee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appoint - to assign a duty, responsibility, or position
  • Antonym: dismiss - to remove someone from a position or duty

Etymology

From Latin 'constitut-', past participle of 'constituere' (to set up, establish).
