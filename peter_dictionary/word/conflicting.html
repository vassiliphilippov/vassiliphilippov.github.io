conflicting

1. ADJ

/ kənˈflɪktɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈflɪktɪŋ / (BrE)
(of ideas, beliefs, or results) in opposition or disagreement; incompatible or mutually exclusive.

Examples

He will not be surprised to find that general principles produce conflicting results in the factual situation with which he is confronted.

Synset Examples

the figures are at odds with our findings
contradictory attributes of unjust justice and loving vindictiveness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contradictory - mutually opposed or inconsistent
  • Antonym: compatible - able to exist or work together without conflict

Etymology

Present participle of 'conflict', from Latin 'conflictus' (a striking together, contest).
