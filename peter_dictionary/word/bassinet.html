bassinet

1. NOUN

/ bæsɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / bæsɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
A small bed, often with a hood, shaped like a basket and used for a baby's sleeping place.

Examples

It could have been anyone's infant, for it had not survived the bassinet.

Etymology

From French 'basinet', diminutive of 'bassin' (basin, cradle).
