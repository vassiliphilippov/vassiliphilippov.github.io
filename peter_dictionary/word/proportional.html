proportional

1. ADJ

/ prəˈpɔɹʃənl̩ / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃənl / (BrE)
having a constant or proper relationship in size, amount, degree, or intensity to another thing, often expressed as a ratio; usually followed by 'to'.
