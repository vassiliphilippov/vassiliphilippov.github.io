securely

1. ADV

/ sɪˈkjʊrli / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊəli / (BrE)
In a safe, protected, or stable manner; free from danger or risk.

Examples

His Mary would be slender of limb and delicate of proportion, yet she must hold this full-grown man as securely and convincingly as she would a child.

Synset Examples

she held the child securely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safely - in a safe manner, free from harm or risk
  • Antonym: dangerously - in a way that is not safe; likely to cause harm

Etymology

From 'secure' + suffix '-ly', with 'secure' from Latin 'securus' (free from care).
