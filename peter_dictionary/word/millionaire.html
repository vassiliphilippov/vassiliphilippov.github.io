millionaire

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪljəˈnɛr / (AmE), / ˌmɪljəˈneə / (BrE)
A person whose wealth is valued at more than a million dollars, pounds, or other units of currency.

Examples

By the time Norton retires, I'll have made him a millionaire.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: billionaire - person whose wealth exceeds a billion units of currency
  • Degree Higher: multimillionaire - person whose wealth exceeds several million units of currency
  • Antonym: pauper - very poor person with little or no money

Etymology

From French *millionnaire*, from 'million' + '-aire' (suffix indicating a person with a specified thing).
