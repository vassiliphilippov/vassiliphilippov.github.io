October

1. PROPN

/ oʊkˈtoʊbər / (AmE), / ɒkˈtəʊbə / (BrE)
The tenth month of the year in the Gregorian calendar, following September and preceding November.

Etymology

From Latin 'october', meaning 'eighth month' (originally the eighth in the Roman calendar).
