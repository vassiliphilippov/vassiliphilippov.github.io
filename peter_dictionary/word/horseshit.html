horseshit

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔrʃˌʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːʃˌʃɪt / (BrE)
Vulgar slang for nonsense, lies, or something regarded as worthless or misleading; rubbish or nonsense, especially when expressed contemptuously.

Examples

What the Christ is this happy horse shit?

Synset Examples

I put up with a lot of bullshit from that jerk
what he said was mostly bull

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - vulgar slang term for nonsense or lies
  • Synonym: rubbish - nonsense or worthless talk/ideas (informal, especially British English)
  • Synonym: nonsense - speech or writing that has no meaning or makes no sense

Etymology

From 'horse' + 'shit', patterned after similar intensifying compounds in English slang.
