endothelial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛn.doʊˈθi.li.əl / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dəʊˈθiː.li.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or situated in the endothelium, the thin layer of cells lining the blood vessels and some other body cavities.

Examples

The endothelial cells lining the sinusoids were prominent, and many contained large quantities of hemosiderin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endothelium - thin layer of cells lining blood vessels and cavities

Etymology

From endothelium + -al, patterned after Latin-derived adjectives.
