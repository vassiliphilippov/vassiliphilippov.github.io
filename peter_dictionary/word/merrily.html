merrily

1. ADV

/ ˈmɛrəli / (AmE), / ˈmɛrɪli / (BrE)
In a cheerful and lively way; with joy or happiness.

Examples

"Me ", he said merrily.

Synset Examples

they shouted happily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: happily - in a happy or joyful way
  • Synonym: cheerfully - in a cheerful manner
  • Antonym: gloomily - in a sad or depressed manner

Etymology

From Middle English 'meryly', from 'mery' (merry) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
