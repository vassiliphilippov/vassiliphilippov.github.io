treasured

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrɛʒərd / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒəd / (BrE)
Regarded with deep affection and great value; highly prized.

Examples

Of all their worldly belongings, next to the oxen and his gun, the seed grain had been the most treasured.

Synset Examples

a cherished friend
children are precious
a treasured heirloom
so good to feel wanted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cherished - regarded with deep affection; dearly loved
  • Synonym: prized - highly valued or esteemed
  • Antonym: neglected - not given proper care or attention

Etymology

From the noun 'treasure' (Old French *tresor*, from Latin *thesaurus*, meaning 'storehouse, treasure').
