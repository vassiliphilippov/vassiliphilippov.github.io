pfennig

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛnɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɛnɪɡ / (BrE)
A former monetary unit of Germany, equal to one hundredth of a Deutsche Mark.

Examples

He had seen a dry, old, yellowing hand reach out, with that painful solicitude, to touch, to rearrange, to shift aimlessly, some object worth a pfennig.

Etymology

From German 'Pfennig', related to Old High German 'pfenning'.
