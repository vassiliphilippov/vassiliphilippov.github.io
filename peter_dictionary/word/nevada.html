Nevada

1. PROPN

/ nəˈvædə / (AmE), / nɪˈvɑːdə / (BrE)
A state in the southwestern United States, known for its desert landscape, casinos, and the city of Las Vegas.

Examples

Michael, I'’m not going back to Nevada.

Etymology

From Spanish 'nevada', meaning 'snow-covered', referring to the Sierra Nevada mountains.
