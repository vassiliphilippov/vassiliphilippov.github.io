believable

1. ADJ

/ bəˈliːvəbəl / (AmE), / bɪˈliːvəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be accepted as true or possible; credible.

Examples

Dolores Hart, is charming in a leading role, and quite believable.

Synset Examples

completely credible testimony
credible information

Related Terms

  • Synonym: credible - able to be believed; convincing
  • Antonym: unbelievable - not able to be believed; incredible

Etymology

From believe + -able, modeled after 'credible'.
