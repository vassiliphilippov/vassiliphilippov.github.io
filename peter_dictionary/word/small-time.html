small-time

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmɔl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːl taɪm / (BrE)
Of minor importance or influence; not important or powerful; operating on a limited or insignificant scale.

Examples

The portwatchers were retired longshoremen and small time seafarers off towboats and barges who acted as watchmen on the wharves.

Synset Examples

a nickel-and-dime operation run out of a single rented room
a small-time actor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petty - of little importance; minor
  • Antonym: major-league - of major importance; significant
