branched

1. ADJ

/ bræn(t)ʃt / (AmE), / brɑːn(t)ʃt / (BrE)
Divided into branches or sections; having parts that extend from a main body like the branches of a tree.

It lacks pollen baskets and possesses only a large number of long, branched hairs on its legs, on which the pollen grains will collect.
