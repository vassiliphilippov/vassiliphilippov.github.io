worthless

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɜrθləs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːθləs / (BrE)
Having no value, importance, or usefulness.

Examples

For a time the President received hundreds of them every day, most of them worthless.
This guy must be the most worthless guy in America.

Synset Examples

a worthless idler

Related Terms

  • Antonym: valuable - having great worth or usefulness
  • Synonym: useless - not serving any useful purpose
  • Degree Higher: priceless - so precious its value cannot be determined

Etymology

From 'worth' + suffix '-less', meaning 'without worth'.
