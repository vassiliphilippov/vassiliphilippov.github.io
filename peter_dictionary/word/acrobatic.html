acrobatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌækrəˈbætɪk / (AmE), / ˌækrəˈbætɪk / (BrE)
involving, displaying, or requiring great agility or energetic movement, as in the manner of an acrobat

Examples

Investors breathed more freely when it was learned that this acrobatic dancer had turned magician and was only doing a best seller book to make some dough.

Synset Examples

an acrobatic dance
an athletic child
athletic playing
gymnastic exercises

Related Terms

  • Synonym: athletic - physically strong, fit, and active
  • Synonym: agile - able to move quickly and easily

Etymology

From French *acrobatique*, from Greek *akrobatos* (walking on tiptoe).
