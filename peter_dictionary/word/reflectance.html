reflectance

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈflɛktəns / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛktəns / (BrE)
The proportion of radiant energy that is reflected by a surface, often expressed as a fraction or percentage.

Examples

In this way, numerical values for the relative efficacy of various detergent formulations can be obtained by measuring the reflectance (whiteness) of the cloth swatches before and after washing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: albedo - fraction of solar energy reflected from a surface (especially in astronomy)

Etymology

From 'reflect' + '-ance' (denoting action or process).
