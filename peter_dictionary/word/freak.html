freak

1. NOUN

/ friːk / (AmE), / friːk / (BrE)
A person or animal that is markedly unusual, abnormal, or deformed, often used offensively to refer to those with physical differences.

Examples

He reasons that as anacondas 30 feet long are often found, some might be 38, and occasional "monstrous freaks "over 50.
Thus, his estimate lies between Oliver's suggestion of at least 37 feet and the 50 - foot "monstrous freaks "intimated by Heuvelmans.
The man was just this side of being a freak.
Twenty-four hours had changed him from a performer to a freak.
"Winged Freak... "...Terrorizes..."

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monster - a person or animal of abnormal form or structure

Etymology

Of uncertain origin; possibly related to dialectal English 'freke' (odd-shaped), or early modern English sense of a whim or caprice.
