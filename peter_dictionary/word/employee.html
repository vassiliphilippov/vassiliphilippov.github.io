employee

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈplɔɪi / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪiː / (BrE)
A person who is hired to work for another person or organization in return for payment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: employer - a person or organization that hires employees
  • Synonym: worker - a person who works, especially one employed for manual labor
  • Synonym: staff - the employees of an organization

Etymology

From French 'employé' (employed person), from 'employer' (to employ).
