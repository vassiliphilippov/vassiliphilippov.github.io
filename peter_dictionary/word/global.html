global

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡloʊbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbəl / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting the whole world; worldwide in scope or nature.

Examples

Thus he may be referring to some concrete thing, or incident, in his immediate environment by some symbolic sounding, hyperbolic reference to transcendental events on the global scene.

Synset Examples

global war
global monetary policy
neither national nor continental but planetary
a world crisis
of worldwide significance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldwide - extending throughout the world; global
  • Antonym: local - relating to a particular area, as opposed to the entire world

Etymology

From Latin 'globus' (a round mass, sphere).
