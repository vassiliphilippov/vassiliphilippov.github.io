Korean

1. ADJ

/ kəˈriː.ən / (AmE), / kəˈriː.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to Korea, its people, or its language

Examples

I recall the startling, vivid realism of a dream in which I lived through the horror of the bombing of a little Korean town.

Synset Examples

Korean handicrafts

Etymology

From Korea, the name of the country, with the suffix -an denoting association.

2. NOUN

/ kəˈriːən / (AmE), / kəˈriːən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of Korea, especially one who speaks the Korean language.

Examples

The Koreans fell back, but regrouped at the top of the hill and pinned down the cavalrymen with a screen of fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Korean - the language of Korea

Etymology

From Korea (country name) + -an, denoting association.
