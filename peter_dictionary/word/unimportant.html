unimportant

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪmˈpɔrtnt / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈpɔːtnt / (BrE)
Not significant or worthy of attention; not important.

Examples

Hence, if what is in question is whether in a given theology myth is or is not completely rejected, it is unimportant whether only a little bit of myth or a considerable quantity is accepted; for, in either event, the first possibility is excluded.
The Old Man was unimportant.

Synset Examples

a relatively unimportant feature of the system
the question seems unimportant

Related Terms

  • Antonym: important - of great significance or value
  • Synonym: insignificant - too small or unimportant to be worth consideration

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'important'.
