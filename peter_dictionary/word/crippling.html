crippling

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɪplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪplɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing severe damage, disability, or incapacity.

Examples

Not altogether a successful play, "Epitaph for George Dillon "overcomes through sheer vitality and power what in a lesser work might be crippling.

Synset Examples

a crippling injury

Related Terms

  • Synonym: damaging - causing harm or injury
  • Synonym: debilitating - making someone very weak and infirm
  • Antonym: minor - of less importance or severity, opposite of serious or crippling
