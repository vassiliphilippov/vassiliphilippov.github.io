weekly

1. NOUN

/ ˈwiːkli / (AmE), / ˈwiːkli / (BrE)
a magazine or newspaper that is published once every week

Examples

The shooting development program of the Sporting Arms and Ammunition Manufacturers' Institute has successfully published these facts in all major outdoor magazines, many national weeklies and the trade papers.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: daily - newspaper published every day
  • Degree Lower: monthly - magazine or periodical published once a month

Etymology

From week + -ly, originally as an adverb; the noun sense derives from the publication's schedule.
