blinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkə / (BrE)
A light that flashes on and off, used as a signal or to send messages.

Examples

During the night, a phone company technician had deadened the bells and installed red blinkers on the phones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indicator - a device in a vehicle that flashes to show direction (car turn signal)

Etymology

From the verb 'blink' (to open and close the eyes quickly, flicker), plus the agentive suffix '-er'.
