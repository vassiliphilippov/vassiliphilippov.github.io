international

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.təɹˈnæ.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈnæ.ʃə.nəl / (BrE)
existing, occurring, or carried on between two or more nations; involving or relating to different countries.

Examples

Historians have come to recognize two cardinal facts concerning nationalism and international influence.
The fact is due mainly to international wars, both hot and cold.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: domestic - relating to a single country or to internal affairs

Etymology

From inter- (between) + national (relating to a nation), first attested in the early 19th century.

Related Terms

Etymology
