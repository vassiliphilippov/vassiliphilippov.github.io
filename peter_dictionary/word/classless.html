classless

1. ADJ

/ ˈklæs.ləs / (AmE), / ˈklɑːs.ləs / (BrE)
not divided into social classes; favoring or characteristic of social equality.

Examples

The long road that had taken liberals in this country into the social religion of democracy, into a worship of man, led logically to the Marxist dream of a classless society under a Socialist State.

Synset Examples

a classless society

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egalitarian - believing in or based on the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities
  • Antonym: hierarchical - arranged in order of rank; relating to a system where people are ranked according to status or authority

Etymology

From 'class' + '-less' (suffix meaning 'without').
