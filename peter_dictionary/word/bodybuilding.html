bodybuilding

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdiˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of developing muscles and increasing physical strength through intensive exercise, often as a sport.

Examples

Instead of admonishing him to let the weights alone they personally took him to that master Montreal bodybuilding authority, Professor Roland Claude.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weightlifting - the sport or activity of lifting heavy weights
  • Synonym: powerlifting - a strength sport consisting of three attempts at maximal weight on three lifts

Etymology

Formed from 'body' + 'building', indicating the process of building the body.
