Norway

1. PROPN

/ ˈnɔr.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.weɪ / (BrE)
A country in northern Europe on the western side of the Scandinavian Peninsula, known officially as the Kingdom of Norway.

Examples

But he plunges into yet another, this time with Norway, and is killed in an assault on the fortress of Fredrikshall, being only thirty-six years of age when he died.

Etymology

From Old Norse 'Norðrvegr' meaning 'north way' or 'way north'.
