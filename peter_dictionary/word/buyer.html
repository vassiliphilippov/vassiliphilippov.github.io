buyer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.ə / (BrE)
A person who purchases something; especially someone who buys goods or property.

Examples

If the record buyer's tastes are somewhat eclectic or even the slightest bit esoteric, he will find them satisfied on educational records.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: seller - a person who sells something

Etymology

From Middle English 'biere', from 'byen' (to buy), related to Old English 'bycgan'.
