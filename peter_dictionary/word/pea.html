pea

1. NOUN

/ piː / (AmE), / piː / (BrE)
The small, round, green seed of the pea plant, eaten as a vegetable.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bean - an edible seed, often kidney-shaped, from various plants in the family Fabaceae

Etymology

From Middle English 'pease' (mistaken as a plural), from Latin 'pisum', from Greek 'pison'.
