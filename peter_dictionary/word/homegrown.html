homegrown

1. ADJ

/ ˌhoʊmˈɡroʊn / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmˈɡrəʊn / (BrE)
Produced or developed in one's own country, region, or home, rather than imported or acquired from elsewhere.

Examples

His face was ruddy and heavy and unlined, and when he laughed he showed his teeth, which were big and white and strong and unquestionably home-grown.

Synset Examples

stands selling homegrown fruits and vegetables

Related Terms

  • Synonym: domestic - produced or made within a particular country
  • Antonym: imported - brought into a country from abroad for sale

Etymology

Formed from 'home' + 'grown', meaning grown at home or locally.
