accused

1. NOUN

/ əˈkjud / (AmE), / əˈkjuːzd / (BrE)
A person who has been officially charged with a crime in a court of law.

Examples

We have the accused at the scene of the crime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defendant - a person sued or accused in a court of law

Etymology

From Old French *acuser*, from Latin *accusare* (to call to account, accuse).
