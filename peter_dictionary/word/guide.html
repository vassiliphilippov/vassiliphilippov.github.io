guide

1. NOUN

/ ɡaɪd / (AmE), / ɡaɪd / (BrE)
A book, manual, or document that provides information or instructions on a particular subject.

Examples

Teaching guides are included with each record.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manual - a handbook or book of instructions
  • Synonym: handbook - a concise reference book providing specific information

Etymology

From Old French guider (to guide), from Frankish *witan (to show the way).
