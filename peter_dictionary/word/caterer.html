caterer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪtərər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪtərə / (BrE)
A person or company that provides food and drinks, typically for social events or gatherings.

Examples

Then there was the caterer's ad which read: "Are you getting married or having an affair?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catering - the business or activity of providing food and drink for events

Etymology

From 'cater', ultimately from Old French *acater* (to buy, provision).
