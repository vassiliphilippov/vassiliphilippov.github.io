antipasto

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.tiˈpɑːs.toʊ / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈpæst.əʊ / (BrE)
A course of appetizers, typically of Italian cuisine, served before the main meal.

Examples

Sonny, come on, don'’t touch the antipasto until Pop sees it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hors d'oeuvre - small savory appetizers served before a meal
  • Synonym: starter - a small dish served before the main part of a meal

Etymology

From Italian 'antipasto', literally 'before the meal', from 'anti-' (before) + 'pasto' (meal).
