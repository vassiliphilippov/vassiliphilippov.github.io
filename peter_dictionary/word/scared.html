scared

1. ADJ

/ skɛrd / (AmE), / skɛəd / (BrE)
frightened or afraid, often as a result of a specific threat or cause

Examples

Lieutenant, every punk in this town is scared.

Synset Examples

the frightened child cowered in the corner
too shocked and scared to move

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afraid - feeling fear or anxiety; frightened
  • Degree Higher: terrified - extremely frightened
  • Antonym: unafraid - not feeling fear; brave
