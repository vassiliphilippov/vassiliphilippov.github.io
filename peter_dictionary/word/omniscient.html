omniscient

1. ADJ

/ ɑmˈnɪsiənt / (AmE), / ɒmˈnɪsiənt / (BrE)
knowing everything; having unlimited knowledge or awareness

Examples

Gansevoort said, "It requires an omniscient eye to select those if any on whom we can now rely.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: all-knowing - having complete or unlimited knowledge, awareness, or understanding

Etymology

From Latin 'omniscient-', stem of 'omniscientia' (all-knowing), from 'omni-' (all) + 'scientia' (knowledge).
