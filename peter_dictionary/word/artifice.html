artifice

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːɹ.tə.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈɑː.tɪ.fɪs / (BrE)
A clever or cunning trick used to deceive or achieve an end.

Examples

But it is characteristic of him, we are told, "his little artifice ", to be able to introduce "into a fairly vulgar and humorous piece of hackwork a sudden phrase of genuine creative art ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ruse - a trick intended to deceive
  • Synonym: stratagem - a plan or scheme, especially one used to outwit an opponent

Etymology

From Latin 'artificium', meaning craftsmanship or craftiness.
