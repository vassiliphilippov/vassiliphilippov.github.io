avowed

1. ADJ

/ əˈvaʊd / (AmE), / əˈvaʊd / (BrE)
openly acknowledged or declared

Examples

Today Sukarno's government is heavily besieged by avowed Communists, and for all of its "neutralist "pretensions, it is a firm ally of Soviet policy.

Synset Examples

an avowed enemy
her professed love of everything about that country
McKinley was assassinated by a professed anarchist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professed - openly declared or stated
  • Antonym: unacknowledged - not openly declared or recognized

Etymology

Past participle of 'avow', from Old French 'avouer', from Latin 'advocare' (to call upon).
