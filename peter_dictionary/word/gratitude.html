gratitude

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræt̬.ɪ.tud / (AmE), / ˈɡræt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
the feeling of being thankful and showing appreciation for something received

Examples

He wanted no part of the emotions of the exchange, no memory of the joy and gratitude that other men felt.

Synset Examples

he was overwhelmed with gratitude for their help

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thankfulness - the quality or feeling of being thankful; synonym for gratitude
  • Antonym: ingratitude - lack of gratitude; opposite of gratitude

Etymology

From Latin 'gratitudo' (thankfulness), from 'gratus' (pleasing, thankful).
