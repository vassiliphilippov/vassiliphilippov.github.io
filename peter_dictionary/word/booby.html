booby

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːbi / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi / (BrE)
A stupid or foolish person; someone easily fooled or lacking in common sense.

Examples

And this handsome booby, staring and sweating, was he her bridegroom?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment, synonym in this context
  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence, synonym (offensive in modern usage)

Etymology

Probably from Spanish 'bobo' (stupid, fool), itself from Latin 'balbus' (stammering, stuttering).
