gratifyingly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡræt̬.ɪ.faɪ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈɡræt.ɪ.faɪ.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that gives pleasure or satisfaction; in a gratifying manner.

Examples

All of the releases, however, are recorded at a gratifyingly high level, with resultant masking of any surface noise.

Synset Examples

the performance was at a gratifyingly high level

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pleasingly - in a pleasing manner
  • Antonym: unsatisfactorily - in a manner that does not give satisfaction

Etymology

From 'gratifying' + '-ly', where 'gratifying' comes from 'gratify' (from Old French 'gratifier', from Latin 'gratificari', to do a favor to).
