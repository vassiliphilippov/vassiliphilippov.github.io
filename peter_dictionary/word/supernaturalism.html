supernaturalism

1. NOUN

/ ˌsuː.pɚˈnætʃ.rə.lɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnætʃ.rə.lɪ.zəm / (BrE)
A belief in the existence of forces or phenomena beyond the natural world and ordinary human understanding, often involving the supernatural.
