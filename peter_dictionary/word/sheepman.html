sheepman

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃipˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpmən / (BrE)
a man who raises or tends sheep, especially as a profession

Examples

Knowing specifically what the many feed additives can do and how and when to feed them can make a highly competitive business more profitable for beef, dairy, and sheep men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shepherd - a person who tends and rears sheep

Etymology

Formed from 'sheep' + 'man'.
