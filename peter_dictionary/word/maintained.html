maintained

1. ADJ

/ meɪnˈteɪnd / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪnd / (BrE)
Kept in good condition through regular care or attention.

Examples

If these services are to be maintained, the New York Central must have the revenues to make them possible ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: neglected - not cared for or maintained
  • Synonym: preserved - kept in its original condition; protected from harm

Etymology

From the verb 'maintain', from Old French 'maintenir', from Latin 'manu tenere' (to hold in the hand).
