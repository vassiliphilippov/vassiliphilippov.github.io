marijuana

1. NOUN

/ ˌmærɪˈwɑːnə / (AmE), / ˌmærɪˈwɑːnə / (BrE)
a plant with strong-smelling leaves that are dried and smoked or consumed for their euphoriant and hallucinogenic effects

Examples

And one of your stars has just moved from marijuana to heroin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cannabis - the botanical genus that includes marijuana; also refers to the plant or its drug preparation

Etymology

From Mexican Spanish 'mariguana', of uncertain origin.
