regulatory

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛɡ.jəˌleɪ.tɔːr.i / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡ.jʊ.lə.tər.i / (BrE)
Having the function or effect of controlling or governing something according to rules or principles.

Examples

This indicates that this drug is being marketed under one trade name only or state regulatory organizations have approved its use on the feed tag.

Synset Examples

a regulatory gene

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regulative - having the function of regulating

Etymology

Formed from 'regulate' + '-ory', following Latin pattern.
