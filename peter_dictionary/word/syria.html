Syria

1. PROPN

/ ˈsɪriə / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriə / (BrE)
A country in Western Asia, at the eastern end of the Mediterranean Sea, known for its ancient history and centers of civilization.

Examples

In the Middle East, Iraq, Syria and Egypt were, a short while ago, in the Western camp.

Etymology

From Latin Syria, from Ancient Greek Συρία (Suría).
