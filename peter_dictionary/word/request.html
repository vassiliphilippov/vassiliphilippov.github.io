Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ask - to request or demand an answer, information, or action from someone
  • Antonym: demand - to ask for something forcefully, not politely or quietly

Etymology

From Old French *requeste*, from Latin *requisita*, feminine past participle of *requirere* (to seek, ask for).

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: demand - an insistent and authoritative request

  • Degree Lower: wish - a desire or hope for something to happen

Etymology

From Old French 'requeste', from Vulgar Latin *requaesta, from Latin 'requirere' (to seek, ask for).
