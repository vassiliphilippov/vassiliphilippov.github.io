christening

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.nɪŋ / (BrE)
a Christian ceremony in which a baby is given a name and officially welcomed into the Church; baptism, especially when naming is involved

Examples

He 'd come East for the christening, by God he would.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baptism - the Christian religious rite of sprinkling water on someone, symbolizing purification and admission to the Church

Etymology

From Middle English, from 'christen' (to baptize) + '-ing'.
