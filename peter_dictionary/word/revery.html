revery

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛvəri / (AmE), / ˈriːvəri / (BrE)
A state of being pleasantly lost in one's thoughts; a daydream or deep, abstracted contemplation.

Examples

The dawn progressed and it seemed that the doctor would never be done with his ministrations when quite abruptly something broke his revery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reverie - a state of being pleasantly lost in one's thoughts
  • Synonym: daydream - a series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention
  • Synonym: trance - a half-conscious state, often associated with deep thought

Etymology

Variant of 'reverie', from French 'rêverie' (daydream, reverie).
