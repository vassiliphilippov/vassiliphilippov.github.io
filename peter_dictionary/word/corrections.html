corrections

1. NOUN

/ kəˈrɛk.ʃənz / (AmE), / kəˈrɛk.ʃənz / (BrE)
The system or branch of the criminal justice system concerned with the management, rehabilitation, and supervision of people convicted of crimes, including imprisonment, probation, and parole.

Examples

But rather a genuine, progressive advance in corrections and rehabilitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: penology - the study of the punishment of crime and prison management
  • Synonym: prisons - institutions for the confinement of people convicted of crimes
