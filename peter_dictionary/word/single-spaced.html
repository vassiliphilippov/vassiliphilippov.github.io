single-spaced

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪŋɡəl speɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl speɪst / (BrE)
(of text or print) having no blank line or space between lines; formatted so that each line follows directly after the previous one.

Examples

He took up a white sheet of paper, dark with single-spaced data.

Synset Examples

business letters are usually single-spaced

Related Terms

  • Antonym: double-spaced - (of text or print) with a blank line between each line of text; opposite of single-spaced
