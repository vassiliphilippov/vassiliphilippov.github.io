cashier

1. NOUN

/ kæˈʃɪr / (AmE), / kæˈʃɪə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to receive and process payments for goods or services, especially in a shop, bank, or restaurant

Examples

Here's your cashier's check, sir.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teller - a person who receives and pays out money in a bank

Etymology

From French caissier (cash keeper), from caisse (box, money box), from Latin capsa.
