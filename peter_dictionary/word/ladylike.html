ladylike

1. ADJ

/ ˈleɪdiˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈleɪdilʌɪk / (BrE)
Having manners or qualities considered appropriate to a woman of good breeding; refined or genteel.

Examples

One could hear a very faint, ladylike sigh of relief.

Synset Examples

ladylike manners

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mannish - having characteristics regarded as typical of a man, usually when applied to a woman

Etymology

Formed from 'lady' + 'like', meaning 'like a lady'.
