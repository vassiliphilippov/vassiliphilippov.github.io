bonus

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.nəs / (BrE)
An extra payment, often given to employees to increase productivity or reward good performance, in addition to regular wages or salary.

Examples

Sort of like a bonus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incentive - something that motivates or encourages a person to do something

Etymology

From Latin bonus (good).
