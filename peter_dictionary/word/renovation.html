renovation

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
The act or process of repairing and improving something, especially a building, so that it is in good condition again.

Examples

The only chance of renovation is to open our eyes and see the mess.

Synset Examples

they are pursuing a general program of renovation to the entire property
a major overhal of the healthcare system was proposed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restoration - the act of returning something to a former condition

Etymology

From Latin renovatio (a renewing), from renovare (to renew).
