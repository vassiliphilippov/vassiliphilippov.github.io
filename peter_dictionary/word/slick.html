slick

1. NOUN

/ slɪk / (AmE), / slɪk / (BrE)
a smooth, slippery substance or the quality of being slippery and smooth

Examples

Sweat popped out over him and he felt the slick between his palm and the stick grip.

Synset Examples

he could feel the slickness of the tiller

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slipperiness - the state or quality of being slippery
  • Antonym: stickiness - the quality of being sticky (not slippery)
