deep-eyed

1. ADJ

/ diːp aɪd / (AmE), / diːp aɪd / (BrE)
Having eyes that are set deeply into the sockets, often giving a gaunt or hollow appearance.

Examples

He treats this deep-eyed, bearded, bony crackpot with tangible affection and respect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep-set - (of eyes) positioned deeply in the sockets
