uncontrollable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kənˈtroʊ.lə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈtrəʊ.lə.bəl / (BrE)
(of something) too strong or powerful to be managed, stopped, or alleviated.

Examples

Does our society have a runaway, uncontrollable growth of technology which may end our civilization, or a normal, healthy growth?

Synset Examples

uncontrollable pain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irresistible - impossible to resist or stop
  • Antonym: manageable - able to be controlled or dealt with easily

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'controllable' (able to be controlled).
