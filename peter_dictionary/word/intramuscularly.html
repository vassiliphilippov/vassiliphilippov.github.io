intramuscularly

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.trəˈmʌs.kjə.lɚ.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈmʌs.kjʊ.lə.li / (BrE)
In a manner involving injection into a muscle.

Examples

On Feb. 12, 1959, purified corticotropin (ACTH Gel), 20 units daily intramuscularly, was started but had to be discontinued 3 weeks later because of excessive fluid retention.

Synset Examples

administer the drug intramuscularly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: subcutaneously - by injection under the skin
  • Antonym: intravenously - by injection into a vein

Etymology

From intramuscular + -ly; ultimately from Latin intra ('within') and musculus ('muscle').
