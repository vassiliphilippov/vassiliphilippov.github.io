reecho

1. VERB

/ ˌriˈɛkoʊ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈekəʊ / (BrE)
(of a sound) to echo repeatedly or again; to resound multiple times.

Examples

And re-echo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: echo - (of a sound) to reflect off a surface and be heard again

Etymology

From re- (again) + echo, modeled on echo.
