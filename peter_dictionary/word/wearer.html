wearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛrər / (AmE), / ˈwɛərə / (BrE)
a person who wears something, especially clothing or an accessory

Examples

He caught her eye, came back around the car with the boot wearer's teetering, half mincing walk.

Synset Examples

the wearer of the crown

Related Terms

  • Synonym: user - a person who uses something
  • Synonym: bearer - a person who carries something

Etymology

From the verb 'wear' + agent suffix '-er'.
