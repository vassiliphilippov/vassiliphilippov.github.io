/ ˈaɪsəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəleɪtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

/ ˈaɪsəleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəleɪtɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Etymology
