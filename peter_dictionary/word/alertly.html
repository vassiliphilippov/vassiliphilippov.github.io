alertly

1. ADV

/ əˈlɝːtli / (AmE), / əˈlɜːtli / (BrE)
In a mentally perceptive and responsive manner; with alertness or vigilance.

Examples

Less assured than the tall, wide shouldered man in the lead, Cobb followed alertly, a hand on his gun butt.

Synset Examples

she got through the day alertly, despite being on drugs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigilantly - in a way that shows careful watching for possible danger or difficulties

Etymology

From 'alert' + '-ly', following the adjective to adverb formation.
