operations

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənz / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈreɪʃənz / (BrE)
The activities involved in the execution of trades and the management of customer records, especially in a brokerage or financial institution.

Examples

The Secretary may dispose of water and byproducts resulting from his operations under this Act.
