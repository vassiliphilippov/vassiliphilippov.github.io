Turk

1. NOUN

/ tɜrk / (AmE), / tɜːk / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Turkey.

Examples

The Turk wants to talk!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Turkish - relating to Turkey, its people, or their language

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Turcus, from Byzantine Greek Tourkos, ultimately from Old Turkic Türk.
