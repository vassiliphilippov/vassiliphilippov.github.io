dormitory

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔːr.məˌtɔːr.i / (AmE), / ˈdɔː.mɪ.təri / (BrE)
A building at a college or university where students live and sleep; a residence hall.

Examples

He was the dormitory gapt when I went to college and thought I was getting away from him.
The brothers continued to help each other during their studies, sharing a joint purse, lodging together in the dormitory and dining together at the home of their aunt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: residence hall - a college or university building for student accommodation
  • Synonym: boarding house - a house providing food and lodging, often for students

Etymology

From Latin 'dormitorium' (sleeping place), from 'dormire' (to sleep).
