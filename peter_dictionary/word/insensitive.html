insensitive

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
not responsive to physical stimuli or external influences

Examples

Since conventional methods are insensitive at the low frequencies of these molecular transitions, the paramagnetic resonance method is being used instead.

Synset Examples

insensitive to radiation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sensitive - quick to respond to physical stimuli

Etymology

From in- (not) + sensitive; 'sensitive' from Medieval Latin 'sensitivus'.
