downwind

1. ADV

/ ˈdaʊnˌwɪnd / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnwɪnd / (BrE)
In the direction that the wind is blowing.

Examples

The smaller the particle the further it will travel downwind before settling out.
It is quite likely that an even greater area was covered, particularly downwind.

Synset Examples

they flew downwind

Related Terms

  • Antonym: upwind - in the direction from which the wind is blowing
