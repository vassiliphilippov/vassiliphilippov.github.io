respectfully

1. ADV

/ rɪˈspɛktfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktfʊli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows respect or deference.

Examples

But you don't ask with respect.

Synset Examples

might I respectfully suggest to the Town Council that they should adopt a policy of masterly inactivity?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disrespectfully - in a disrespectful manner
  • Synonym: politely - in a polite manner

Etymology

Formed from 'respectful' + '-ly'; 'respectful' from respect + '-ful'.
