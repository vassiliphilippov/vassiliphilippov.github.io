inadvisable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnədˈvaɪzəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪnədˈvaɪzəbl̩ / (BrE)
not recommended; unwise or imprudent to do

Examples

The coalition was to prove inadvisable.

Synset Examples

running on the ice is inadvisable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: advisable - wise or recommended to do
  • Degree Higher: prudent - acting with care and thought for the future; wise
  • Synonym: unwise - not wise or sensible

Etymology

Formed from 'in-' (not) + 'advisable', ultimately from Latin 'advisare' (to consider).
