gardener

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdnər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdənə / (BrE)
A person whose job or hobby is taking care of a garden by growing and looking after plants, flowers, or vegetables.

Examples

I sought out the gardener and asked him what he did to produce such beauties in that weather.
It is n't easy to describe it, but every gardener knows it when his fingers touch such soil.
I think that my grandmother was not an impassioned gardener:

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horticulturist - a specialist in the science or art of cultivating gardens

Etymology

From Middle English 'gardinere', from Old French, from 'gardin' (garden).
