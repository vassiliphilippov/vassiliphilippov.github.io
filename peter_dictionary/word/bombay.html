Bombay

1. PROPN

/ bɑmˈbeɪ / (AmE), / bɒmˈbeɪ / (BrE)
The former name of Mumbai, the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra, located on the western coast of India.

Examples

In it was a stone Tibetan Buddha I had picked up in Bombay, and occasionally, to make merit, my wife and I garlanded it with flowers or laid a few pennies in its lap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mumbai - current official name of Bombay, major city in western India

Etymology

From Portuguese 'Bombaim', which derived from the local Marathi words 'Bomb Aai' (Good Mother).
