motorcar

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊ.t̬ɚˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.təˌkɑː / (BrE)
A road vehicle with four wheels, powered by an internal combustion engine or electric motor, used for transporting people.

Examples

The limits are suggested by an imaginary experiment: contrast the perceptual skill of English professors with that of their colleagues in discriminating among motor cars, political candidates, or female beauty.

Synset Examples

he needs a car to get to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car - a motor vehicle with four wheels, typically used for passenger transport
  • Synonym: automobile - a self-propelled passenger vehicle that usually has four wheels

Etymology

From 'motor' (engine) + 'car', late 19th century.
