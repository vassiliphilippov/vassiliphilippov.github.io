hush

1. VERB

/ hʌʃ / (AmE), / hʌʃ / (BrE)
to make someone become quiet or stop talking

Examples

"Hush ", said Uncle Randolph, smiling, "or I 'll give you another black eye ".

Synset Examples

Please silence the children in the church!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silence - to cause someone to stop speaking or making noise
  • Synonym: shush - to tell someone to be quiet, especially softly or in a whisper

Etymology

Imitative origin; formed from the natural sound associated with urging quietness.
