zombie

1. NOUN

/ ˈzɑːm.bi / (AmE), / ˈzɒm.bi / (BrE)
a dead body brought back to life by supernatural means, often depicted in folklore and popular culture as mindless and aggressive

Examples

Then the pixies and the zombies took over while the banshees wailed in the distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ghoul - an evil spirit or phantom, especially one supposed to rob graves and feed on dead bodies
  • Synonym: revenant - a person who has returned, especially from the dead

Etymology

From Haitian Creole and West African languages, possibly Kikongo 'nzambi' (god, spirit of a dead person).
