sierra

1. NOUN

/ siˈɛr.ə / (AmE), / siˈeə.rə / (BrE)
A range of mountains, especially one with jagged peaks and an irregular outline.

Examples

We also have, as a special added attraction, the Sierra Boys Choir who have chosen a certain special song and a special arrangement to honor their host, Mr. Michael Corleone.

Etymology

From Spanish 'sierra' meaning 'mountain range' or 'saw', from Latin 'serra' (saw).
