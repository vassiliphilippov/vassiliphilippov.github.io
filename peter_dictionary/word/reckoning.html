reckoning

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛkənɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of calculating or estimating something, especially involving numbers or quantities.

Examples

The Texans have two more road games - at Buffalo and Houston - before they play for the home folks again, and it looks as if coach Hank Stram's men will meet the Bills just as they are developing into the kind of team they were expected to be in pre-season reckonings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calculation - The process of using mathematics to determine an amount or number
  • Synonym: estimation - A rough calculation or judgment of value, number, quantity, or extent

Etymology

From Old English 'gerecenian' (to recount, relate), via Middle English 'rekenen' (to count, recount)
