saw

1. NOUN

/ sɔ / (AmE), / sɔː / (BrE)
A short, commonly known saying that expresses a traditional belief or piece of advice; a proverb.

Examples

I didn'’t believe him, but seeing is believing, huh!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proverb - a short, well-known saying stating a general truth or piece of advice
  • Synonym: maxim - a general truth, rule of conduct, or proverbial saying
  • Synonym: adage - an old and well-known saying that expresses a general truth

Etymology

From Old English 'sagu' (saying), related to 'secgan' (to say).
