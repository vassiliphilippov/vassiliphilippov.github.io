geometrical

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to geometry or determined according to the principles of geometry

Examples

Hence it is difficult to conceive of a packing of the atoms in this material in which the oxygen atoms are far from geometrical equivalence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geometric - relating to geometry (alternative form)

Etymology

From Late Latin geometricus, from Greek geometrikos (relating to geometry) + English -al.
