bullethead

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlətˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪthɛd / (BrE)
A head shaped like a bullet, typically rounded and tapering, sometimes used to describe a person with such a head.

Examples

He had the same bullet head of curly reddish hair but he did n't have Jim's poker-faced humor or his brains or his charm.

Etymology

From 'bullet' (projectile) + 'head', referring to the resemblance in shape.
