cognate

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑg.neɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒg.neɪt / (BrE)
Related in nature, character, or function; allied or similar in type or origin.

Examples

The notion of "inspiration "is somehow cognate to this feeling.

Synset Examples

connate qualities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kindred - of a similar nature or character; allied

Etymology

From Latin 'cognatus' (related by birth), from 'co-' (together) + 'gnatus', past participle of 'gignere' (to beget).
