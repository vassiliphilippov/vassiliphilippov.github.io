snare (snare drum)

1. NOUN

/ snɛr / (AmE), / snɛə / (BrE)
A small drum with two heads, having strands of curled wire (the snare) stretched across the lower head, producing a sharp staccato sound; commonly used in orchestras and drum kits.

Examples

Not one of them born... whose asshole wouldn't pucker up tighter than a snare drum when you asked them for funds.
