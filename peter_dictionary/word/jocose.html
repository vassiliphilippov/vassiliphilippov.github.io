jocose

1. ADJ

/ dʒoʊˈkoʊs / (AmE), / dʒəˈkəʊs / (BrE)
playful or humorous; characterized by joking and good humor

Examples

The collective by which I address you in the title above is neither patronizing nor jocose but an exact industrial term in use among professional thieves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jocular - fond of joking; humorous or playful
  • Antonym: solemn - serious and formal in manner

Etymology

From Latin 'jocosus', from 'jocus' meaning 'jest' or 'joke'.
