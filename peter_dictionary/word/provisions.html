provisions

1. NOUN

/ prəˈvɪʒənz / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒənz / (BrE)
supplies of food, especially for a journey or expedition

Examples

She carried, besides her captain, a crew of twenty-one and provisions for a voyage of exploration of the Arctic waters of North America.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supplies - general stores or resources kept for future use

Etymology

From Latin *provisionem* (a providing) via Old French *provision*.
