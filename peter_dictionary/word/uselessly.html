uselessly

1. ADV

/ ˈjuːslɪsli / (AmE), / ˈjuːsləsli / (BrE)
In a way that is without effect, value, or purpose; to no useful end.

Examples

We rode over roads so rough that our Jeep came to rest atop the soil between ruts, all four wheels spinning uselessly.

Synset Examples

the furniture was sitting around uselessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ineffectively - in a way that does not produce the desired result
  • Antonym: usefully - in a useful manner

Etymology

From 'useless' + '-ly', with 'useless' from use + -less.
