unbelievable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.bɪˈliː.və.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.bɪˈliː.və.bəl / (BrE)
So extraordinary or unlikely as to be difficult or impossible to believe.

Examples

It's still unbelievable that it was murder.

Synset Examples

at incredible speed
the book's plot is simply incredible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incredible - impossible or very difficult to believe; extraordinary
  • Antonym: plausible - seeming reasonable or probable; believable

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'believable'.
