societal

1. ADJ

/ səˈsaɪ.ɪ.t̬əl / (AmE), / səˈsaɪ.ɪ.təl / (BrE)
Relating to society or its organization and members, especially as a whole.

Examples

Religion is vitally necessary in both societal maintenance and regulation.

Synset Examples

social institutions
societal evolution
societal forces
social legislation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social - relating to society or its organization; often used more generally or in reference to interpersonal relations

Etymology

From society + -al, patterned after similar adjectival forms.
