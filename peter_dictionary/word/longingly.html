longingly

1. ADV

/ ˈlɔŋɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a strong desire or yearning for something.

Examples

[EXCLAIMS LONGINGLY]

Synset Examples

he spent the rest of the act gazing longingly over my right shoulder at the illuminated word `Exit'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yearningly - in a yearning or longing manner

Etymology

From 'longing' + '-ly', formed after the verb 'to long' (yearn) of Middle English origin.
