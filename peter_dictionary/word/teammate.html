teammate

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːmˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtiːmmeɪt / (BrE)
A person who is a member of the same team as someone else.

Examples

Unfortunately, Brooks' teammates were not in such festive mood as the Orioles expired before the seven hit pitching of three Kansas City rookie hurlers.
"It's a helluva thing ", Mike said, looking at Phil, "when a guy's own team-mate won n't come out and help him in a fight ".

Synset Examples

it was his first start against his former teammates

Related Terms

  • Antonym: opponent - a person who competes against you or your team

Etymology

Formed from 'team' + 'mate'. Pattern based on compounds like 'classmate'.
