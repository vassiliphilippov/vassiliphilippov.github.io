1. ADJ

/ ˈbrɪljənt / (AmE), / ˈbrɪljənt / (BrE)
exceptionally clever, talented, or impressive; of surpassing excellence

Examples

A tongue-in-cheek stylization of 18 th Century ideas; a trial balloon to test the aesthetic climate of the times; a brilliant piece de resistance?

Synset Examples

a brilliant performance
a superb actor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superb - of very high quality; excellent
  • Synonym: outstanding - extremely good; standing out from the rest
  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good

Etymology

From French 'brillant', present participle of 'briller' (to shine), from Italian 'brillare'.

Etymology
