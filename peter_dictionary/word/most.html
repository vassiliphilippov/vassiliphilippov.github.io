1. ADV

/ moʊst / (AmE), / məʊst / (BrE)
Used to form the superlative of adjectives and adverbs, indicating the greatest degree or amount.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: least - forming the superlative of little (lowest in amount or degree)
  • Degree Lower: more - used to form the comparative (greater in degree or amount)

Etymology

From Old English mǣst (greatest), related to Old High German meist.
