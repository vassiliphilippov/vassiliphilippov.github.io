Nonconformist

1. NOUN

/ nɑn.kənˈfɔrm.ɪst / (AmE), / nɒn.kənˈfɔː.mɪst / (BrE)
(in England) A member of a Protestant church or group that does not belong to the Church of England.

Examples

Most of these former churches are now used as warehouses, but "neither Anglicans nor Nonconformists object to selling churches to Roman Catholics ", and have done so.

Etymology

From non- + conformist, referring to those refusing to conform to the Church of England's practices.
