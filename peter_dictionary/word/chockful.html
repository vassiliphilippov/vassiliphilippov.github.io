chockful

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɑkˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒkˌfʊl / (BrE)
Filled to the limit; packed full.

Examples

Classified ads are also chockfull of misrelated constructions.

Synset Examples

chowder chockablock with pieces of fish

Related Terms

  • Synonym: full - Containing as much as possible; filled to capacity
  • Antonym: empty - Containing nothing; not filled

Etymology

Alteration of 'chock-full', from 'choke full', meaning stuffed to choking.
