mixed-up

1. ADJ

/ mɪkst ˈʌp / (AmE), / mɪkst ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of a person) confused or bewildered, often because of conflicting situations, emotions, or information.

Examples

Maybe we shouldn't get Mike mixed up in this too directly.

Synset Examples

obviously bemused by his questions
bewildered and confused
a cloudy and confounded philosopher
just a mixed-up kid
she felt lost on the first day of school

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or to understand what is happening or what somebody is saying
  • Synonym: bewildered - very confused and unable to understand something
  • Antonym: clear-headed - able to think clearly; not confused
