uninvolved

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
Not taking part in or connected with an activity, event, or situation; having no emotional attachment or influence.

Examples

Experience is not seen, as it is in classical rationalism, as presenting us initially with clear and distinct objects simply located in space and registering their character, movements, and changes on the tabula rasa of an uninvolved intellect.

Synset Examples

being uninvolved he remained objective

Related Terms

  • Antonym: involved - taking part in or connected with something

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'involved'.
