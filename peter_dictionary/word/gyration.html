gyration

1. NOUN

/ dʒaɪˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒʌɪˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a single complete turn or movement around an axis or central point, especially in a circular or spiral motion

Examples

What I am here to do is to report on the gyrations of the struggle - a struggle that amounts to self redefinition - to see if we can predict its future course.

Synset Examples

the plane made three rotations before it crashed
the revolution of the earth about the sun takes one year

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotation - the act of turning around a central point or axis
  • Synonym: revolution - a single complete turn around a central point or axis

Etymology

From Latin 'gyratio' (a turning around), from 'gyrare' (to turn in a circle), from 'gyrus' (circle, ring).
