luscious

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌʃəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌʃəs / (BrE)
(of a person) sexually attractive in a way that is strongly appealing or sensual.

Examples

Soaring to stardom along with him, Margo Felicity Brighetti, a luscious and curvaceously beguiling Italian starlet, turns in a creditable performance as an airplane mechanic.
That luscious tan... ... those ruby lips... ... and hair color so natural, only your undertaker knows for sure.

Synset Examples

juicy barmaids
a red-hot mama
a voluptuous woman
a toothsome blonde in a tight dress

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seductive - sexually attractive and tempting
  • Synonym: voluptuous - (of a woman) attractive in a sensual way
  • Antonym: unattractive - not sexually or physically attractive
