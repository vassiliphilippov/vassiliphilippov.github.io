roomie

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːmi / (AmE), / ˈruːmi / (BrE)
(informal) a person with whom you share a room or accommodation, especially a roommate.

Examples

Kind of roomie you pray you don't get.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roommate - a person with whom you share a room or apartment

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'roommate'.
