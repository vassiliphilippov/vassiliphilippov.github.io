sanguineous

1. ADJ

/ sæŋˈɡwɪniəs / (AmE), / sæŋˈɡwɪniəs / (BrE)
relating to or involving blood; accompanied by bloodshed

Examples

The small intestine and colon contained approximately 300 cc. of foul-smelling, sanguineous material, and the mucosa throughout was hyperemic and mottled green brown.

Synset Examples

this bitter and sanguinary war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bloody - covered in or involving blood
  • Synonym: gory - involving or showing bloodshed

Etymology

From Latin sanguineus (of blood), from sanguis (blood).
