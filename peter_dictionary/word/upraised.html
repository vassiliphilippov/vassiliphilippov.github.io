upraised

1. ADJ

/ ʌpˈreɪzd / (AmE), / ʌpˈreɪzd / (BrE)
held or lifted up in the air; raised upward

Examples

Pat saw Gyp Carmer staggering forward, a half filled bottle upraised as if to strike.

Synset Examples

stood with arms upraised
her upraised flag

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raised - lifted to a higher position
  • Antonym: lowered - moved to a lower position

Etymology

From prefix 'up-' (meaning upward) + 'raise' (to lift), past participle form.
