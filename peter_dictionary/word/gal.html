gal

1. NOUN

/ ɡæl / (AmE), / ɡæl / (BrE)
abbreviation for gallon, a United States liquid unit equal to 4 quarts or about 3.785 liters

Examples

The pilot plant was a circular lagoon 81 ft in diam at the surface and 65 ft in diam at the bottom, 4 ft below the surface, with a volume of 121000 gal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallon - a United States liquid unit equal to 4 quarts or 3.785 liters

Etymology

Abbreviation of gallon, from Old Northern French *galon* (container by the gallon).
