graded

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdɪd / (BrE)
arranged or classified in a sequence of grades, levels, or ranks

Examples

There are boxed proclamations in the newspaper of watchings, pinnings, ringings, engagements, and marriages in a scrupulously graded hierarchy of felicity.

Synset Examples

stratified areas of the distribution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ranked - arranged according to rank or level
  • Synonym: hierarchical - arranged in order of rank or authority

Etymology

From the verb 'grade' (to arrange in grades or levels) + -ed.
