hodgepodge

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑdʒˌpɑdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɒdʒˌpɒdʒ / (BrE)
A mixture of different things, often in a confused or disorganized way.

Examples

I am sure that nothing within me is capable of composing that life-like sequence, so complete in detail, from the hodge-podge of news pictures I have seen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jumble - a confused mixture or condition
  • Synonym: miscellany - a group or collection of different items; a mixture

Etymology

Alteration of hotchpotch, from Anglo-Norman *hochepot* (stew, mixture).
