wired

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəd / (BrE)
Equipped with electrical wiring or connected to a network, especially for electric or telephone services.

Examples

If the house is not wired adequately for electricity or if plumbing or a central heating system must be installed, check into the cost of making these improvements.

Synset Examples

a well-wired house

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wireless - not connected by wires; using radio waves

Etymology

From wire + -ed, denoting being provided with wires.
