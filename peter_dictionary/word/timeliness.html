timeliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪm.li.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm.li.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or fact of occurring at a suitable or appropriate time.

Examples

I think we need to concern ourselves also with the timeliness of action.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untimeliness - the quality of occurring at an unsuitable or inappropriate time
  • Synonym: punctuality - the quality of being on time or prompt

Etymology

From 'timely' + '-ness'; 'timely' from Middle English, ultimately from Old English 'tima' (time).
