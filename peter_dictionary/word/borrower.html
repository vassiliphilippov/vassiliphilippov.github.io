borrower

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːroʊər / (AmE), / ˈbɒrəʊə / (BrE)
a person or organization that receives something, especially money, with the promise to return it or its equivalent

Examples

The maximum loan which SBA may make to any one borrower is $ 350000.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lender - a person or organization that lends something, especially money

Etymology

From borrow + -er, meaning one who borrows.
