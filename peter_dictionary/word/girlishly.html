girlishly

1. ADV

/ ˈɡɜrlɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːlɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of a girl, especially in being shy, playful, or innocent.

Examples

To be passive, to be girlishly shy was palpably absurd.
Mr. Skyros did not at all like the look on Angelo's regular featured, almost girlishly good-looking face - or indeed anything about Angelo.

Synset Examples

she was girlishly shy

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boyishly - in a manner characteristic of a boy

Etymology

From 'girlish' + '-ly', where 'girlish' derives from 'girl'.
