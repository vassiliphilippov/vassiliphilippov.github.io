relatedness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈleɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being connected or related; the degree to which things are or feel connected or associated

Examples

The images themselves, like their counterparts in experience, are not neutral qualities to be surveyed dispassionately; they are fields of force exerting a unique influence on the sensibilities and a unique relatedness to one another.

Synset Examples

the relatedness of all living things

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connection - the state of being connected
  • Antonym: unrelatedness - the state of not being related or connected

Etymology

From relate + -ed + -ness, denoting the state of being related.
