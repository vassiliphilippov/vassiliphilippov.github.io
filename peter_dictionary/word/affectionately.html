affectionately

1. ADV

/ əˈfɛkʃənətli / (AmE), / əˈfɛkʃənətli / (BrE)
In a way that shows affection or fondness.

Examples

This unit, affectionately dubbed "The Monster ", can be wheeled to any convenient location and provides a wealth of information about the patient's breathing.
And it's no good looking at me like that ", as she wound affectionately around his ankles.

Synset Examples

she loved him dearly
he treats her affectionately

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fondly - in a loving or affectionate way

Etymology

From 'affectionate' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'affectio' (influence, feeling).
