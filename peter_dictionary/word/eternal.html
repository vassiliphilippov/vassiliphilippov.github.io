eternal

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈtɝːnəl / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜː.nəl / (BrE)
Lasting or existing forever; without end or beginning.

Etymology

From Latin aeternalis, from aeternus (lasting for an age, eternal).
