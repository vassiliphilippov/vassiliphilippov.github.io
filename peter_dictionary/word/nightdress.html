nightdress

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪtˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtdrɛs / (BrE)
a loose dress worn in bed by women or girls; a nightgown

Examples

There was a lady there, in a nightdress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nightgown - a loose garment for sleeping, also called a nightdress
  • Degree Lower: pajamas - loose-fitting clothes for sleeping, usually a shirt and trousers

Etymology

From 'night' + 'dress', formed in English to refer to clothing for sleeping.
