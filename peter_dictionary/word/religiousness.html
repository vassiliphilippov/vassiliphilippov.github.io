religiousness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɪdʒəsnɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being religious; devoutness or piety

Examples

His religiousness is intimately, or dialectically, connected with his sinfulness; the two may in fact be identical.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devoutness - the quality of being devout
  • Synonym: piety - the quality of being religiously reverent
