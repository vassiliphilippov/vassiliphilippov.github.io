narrowed

1. ADJ

/ ˈnæroʊd / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊd / (BrE)
made narrower or reduced in width, especially by squeezing or constricting; often used to describe eyes when the eyelids are partially closed.

Examples

Occasionally if I pushed him too far he 'd give me a look out of narrowed eyes and the hard cruel bony skull would show through that smooth face of his.

Synset Examples

his narrowed eyes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wide - greater in width; not narrowed
