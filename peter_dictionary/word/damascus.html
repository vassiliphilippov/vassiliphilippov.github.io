Damascus

1. PROPN

/ dəˈmæskəs / (AmE), / dəˈmæskəs / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Syria, widely recognized as one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities.

Examples

The young poet painter reproduces the French life of the streets; he tells stories of the Thousand and One Nights, and conjures up before us the bazaars of Damascus.

Etymology

From Latin Damascus, from Ancient Greek Damaskos, from Hebrew Dammeseq.
