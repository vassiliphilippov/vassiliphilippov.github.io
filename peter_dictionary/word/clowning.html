clowning

1. NOUN

/ ˈklaʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklaʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of behaving in a silly, humorous, or playful way, especially like a clown

Examples

"Ca n't you stop that stupid clowning for even a minute "?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buffoonery - foolish or clownish behavior
  • Synonym: tomfoolery - silly or foolish behavior

Etymology

From 'clown' + '-ing', forming the noun from the verb.
