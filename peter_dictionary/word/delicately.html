delicately

1. ADV

/ ˈdɛlɪkətli / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkətli / (BrE)
In a gentle, subtle, or refined manner; with sensitivity or carefulness.

Examples

Over the rapidly diminishing outline of a jump seat piled high with luggage Herry's black brushcut was just discernible, near, or enviably near that spot where - hidden - more delicately textured, most beautifully tinted hair must still be streaming back in cool, oh cool wind sweetly perfumed with sagebrush and yucca flowers and engine fumes.

Synset Examples

finely shaped features
her fine drawn body

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gently - in a gentle manner, softly
  • Antonym: coarsely - in a rough or crude manner, lacking refinement

Etymology

From 'delicate' + '-ly'. 'Delicate' from Latin 'delicatus' (giving pleasure, delightful).
