Pisces

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪsiz / (AmE), / ˈpaɪsiːz / (BrE)
The twelfth sign of the zodiac, covering approximately February 19 to March 20, symbolized by two fish and associated with people born during this period.

Examples

the water sign of Pisces (February 18 - March 20).
I thought I was a Pisces.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Aquarius - the eleventh sign of the zodiac; preceding Pisces
  • Degree Higher: Aries - the first sign of the zodiac; following Pisces

Etymology

From Latin *Pisces* (fishes), plural of *piscis* (fish).
