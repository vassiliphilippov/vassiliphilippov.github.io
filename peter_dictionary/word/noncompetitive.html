noncompetitive

1. ADJ

/ nɑnkəmˈpɛtətɪv / (AmE), / nɒnkəmˈpɛtətɪv / (BrE)
Not involving or characterized by competition; not competitive.

Examples

It is interesting to note how many of the plants on Massachusetts' Route 128 draw most of their income either from the government in non-competitive cost-plus arrangements, or from the exploitation of patents which grant at least a partial monopoly.

Synset Examples

noncompetitive positions
noncompetitive interest in games

Related Terms

  • Antonym: competitive - involving or characterized by competition

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'competitive'.
