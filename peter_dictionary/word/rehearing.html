rehearing

1. NOUN

/ riˈhɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈhɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of hearing something again, especially for further evaluation or enjoyment.

Examples

Other innovations with better claims to musical interest survived rehearing to acquire in time the status of classics.

Etymology

From re- (again) + hearing.
