haltingly

1. ADV

/ ˈhɔltɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhɔːltɪŋli / (BrE)
In a hesitant or faltering manner, as if lacking confidence or certainty.

Examples

"I do n't know, Mr. Brenner ", he said haltingly, beginning to get an inkling of Brenner's plans.

Synset Examples

he spoke haltingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant manner
  • Antonym: confidently - in a confident manner

Etymology

From “halting” (from halt, meaning to stop or hesitate) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
