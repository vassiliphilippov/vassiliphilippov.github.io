arsenal

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.sə.nəl / (AmE), / ˈɑː.sən.əl / (BrE)
A building or place where weapons, ammunition, and military equipment are stored and military personnel are trained in their use.

Examples

Living room, dining room, arsenal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armory - a place where arms and military equipment are stored

Etymology

From Italian 'arsenale', from Arabic 'dār al-ṣināʿa' (house of manufacture), via Old French and Medieval Latin.
