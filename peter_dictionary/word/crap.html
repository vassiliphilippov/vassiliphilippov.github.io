crap

1. NOUN

/ kræp / (AmE), / kræp / (BrE)
(Vulgar slang) Excrement; feces.

Examples

Ah, that's crap, kid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shit - vulgar slang for feces or excrement

Etymology

Probably from Middle English *crappe* (chaff), derivative of Old French *crappe* (siftings, waste).

2. NOUN

/ kræp / (AmE), / kræp / (BrE)
Used as a mild expletive to refer to statements, ideas, or behavior considered to be worthless, nonsensical, or annoying; similar in meaning to 'nonsense' or 'rubbish'.

Examples

Crap. Crap. Crap. Crap. Crap.
Crap. Crap. Crap. Crap. Crap.

Synset Examples

I put up with a lot of bullshit from that jerk
what he said was mostly bull

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bullshit - rude slang for lies or nonsense
  • Synonym: nonsense - words or ideas with no meaning or value
  • Synonym: rubbish - statements or ideas considered worthless or nonsensical

Etymology

Possibly from Dutch 'krappen' (to pluck off, cut off, separate), or related to obsolete English 'crap' (chaff, residue).
