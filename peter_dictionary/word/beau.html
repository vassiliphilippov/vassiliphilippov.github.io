beau

1. NOUN

/ boʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ / (BrE)
A boyfriend or male admirer, especially of a girl or young woman.

Examples

She lost her beau to you ".

Synset Examples

if I'd known he was her boyfriend I wouldn't have asked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boyfriend - a regular male companion with whom a person has a romantic or sexual relationship

Etymology

From French 'beau' (handsome, admirer), from Latin 'bellus' (pretty, handsome).
