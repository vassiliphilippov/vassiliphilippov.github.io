anomalous

1. ADJ

/ əˈnɑː.mə.ləs / (AmE), / əˈnɒm.ə.ləs / (BrE)
Deviating from what is usual, normal, or expected.

Examples

This anomalous status of the educator cannot fail to lower his standing in the eyes of the students.

Synset Examples

advanced forms of life may be anomalous in the universe

Related Terms

  • Antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; usual, typical, or expected

Etymology

From Late Latin anomalus, from Greek anōmalos (uneven, irregular; from an- not + homalos even).
