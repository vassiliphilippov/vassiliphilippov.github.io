aircraft

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈeəkrɑːft / (BrE)
A vehicle that is able to fly, such as an airplane, helicopter, or other machine that travels through the air.

Etymology

From 'air' + 'craft', meaning 'air vehicle'.
