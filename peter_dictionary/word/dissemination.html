dissemination

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of spreading information, knowledge, or ideas widely so that they reach many people

Examples

Two data centers have been established for the collection, indexing, critical evaluation, and dissemination of bibliographies and critical values in the fields of transition probabilities and collision cross sections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distribution - the act of sharing something out among recipients
  • Synonym: circulation - the process of making something known or available to many people

Etymology

From Latin 'disseminatio', from 'disseminare' (to scatter, spread).
