Satan

1. PROPN

/ ˈseɪtən / (AmE), / ˈseɪtən / (BrE)
In Judeo-Christian and Islamic tradition, the chief spirit of evil, regarded as the adversary of God and the tempter of humankind, and often depicted as the ruler of Hell.

Examples

Michael Francis Rizzi, do you renounce Satan?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devil - the supreme spirit of evil; synonym for Satan

Etymology

From Hebrew śāṭān (adversary), via Greek Satanas and Latin Sātān.
