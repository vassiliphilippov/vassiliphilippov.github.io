alright

1. ADV

/ ɔlˈraɪt / (AmE), / ɔːlˈraɪt / (BrE)
Used to express agreement or acceptance, often at the beginning of a sentence.

Examples

Johnny Ola... Sure... I remember Tom from the old days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: okay - used to express agreement or acceptance
  • Synonym: sure - used to indicate agreement or affirmation
  • Antonym: no - used to express refusal or disagreement
