identifiable

1. ADJ

/ aɪˌdɛntɪˈfaɪəbəl / (AmE), / aɪˌdɛntɪˈfaɪəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be recognized or distinguished as a particular person or thing; capable of being identified.

Examples

The clearly identifiable enemy continued on as if no one else were around.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unidentifiable - not able to be identified

Etymology

From identify + -able, patterned after French identifiable.
