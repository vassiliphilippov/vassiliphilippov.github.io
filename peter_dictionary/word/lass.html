lass

1. NOUN

/ læs / (AmE), / læs / (BrE)
a girl or young woman, especially one who is unmarried

Examples

Here he sketched, sitting in their flowing gowns of linen and silk, young girls not yet twenty, some about to be married, some married a year or two.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lad - a boy or young man

Etymology

From Middle English lasce, related to Old Norse lasa (unmarried woman).
