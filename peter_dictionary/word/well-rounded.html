well-rounded

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈraʊndɪd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈraʊndɪd / (BrE)
having a variety of skills, abilities, or experiences; fully developed in many aspects

Examples

She was such a well-rounded teenager, having been a twirler, Future Farmers sweetheart, and secretary of Future Homemakers.

Synset Examples

an all-around athlete
a well-rounded curriculum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: versatile - able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
