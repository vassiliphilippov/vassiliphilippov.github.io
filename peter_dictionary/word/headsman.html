headsman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdzmən / (AmE), / ˈhɛdzmən / (BrE)
an executioner, especially one who carries out beheadings

Examples

The unfortunate queen mounted the scaffold; the headsman swung his axe; the head dropped off; end of film.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executioner - a person who carries out a death sentence

Etymology

Formed from 'head' + '-sman' (man who does a particular job).
