unstilted

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈstɪltɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɪltɪd / (BrE)
(of speech or writing) flowing naturally, not stiff or formal; not stilted.

Examples

I was delighted with Paula Prentiss' comedy performance, which was as fresh and unstilted as one's highest hopes might ask.

Synset Examples

unstilted conversation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stilted - (of speech or writing) stiff, self-conscious, or unnatural

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' to 'stilted'.
