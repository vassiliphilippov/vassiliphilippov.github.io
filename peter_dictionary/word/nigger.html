nigger

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and insulting term for a Black person; used as a racial slur and considered extremely derogatory.

Examples

A nigger boy opened the door.
The nigger boy fled down the stairs, screaming, "Murder ".
Somewhere in the fog, the nigger boy was still yelling murder.
The nigger boy was close behind him.
Then the nigger boy turned back and he was alone.

Synset Examples

only a Black can call another Black a nigga

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Black - a person having dark skin, especially of African descent
  • Antonym: African American - a Black American, especially one of African descent

Etymology

Probably from Spanish or Portuguese *negro* (black), ultimately from Latin *niger*.
