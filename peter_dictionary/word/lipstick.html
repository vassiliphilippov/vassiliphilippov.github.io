lipstick

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪp.stɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪp.stɪk / (BrE)
A colored cosmetic applied to the lips to enhance or change their color.

Examples

Rachel wore a smart hat and, because she had been warned recently about smoking, puffed at her cigarettes through a long ivory holder stained with lipstick.
When she had wiped some of the lipstick from her mouth, she stared solemnly at her image in the mirror.
Avoid the following combinations: Deodorants with baby powder, hairspray, and lipstick.

Etymology

From 'lip' + 'stick', referring to the stick-shaped applicator for lips.
