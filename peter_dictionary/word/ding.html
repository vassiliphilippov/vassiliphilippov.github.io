ding

1. VERB

/ dɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a bell or similar device) to make a clear, resonant ringing sound.

Examples

[MUTTERS] [CASH REGISTER BELL DINGS] [LAUGHING]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clang - make a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • Synonym: ding-dong - ring with a repeated clang
  • Degree Lower: jingle - make a light ringing sound

Etymology

Imitative; from the sound of a bell or similar resonant noise.
