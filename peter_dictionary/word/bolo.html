bolo

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊləʊ / (BrE)
A type of necktie consisting of a cord fastened with an ornamental clasp, typically worn in Western fashion.

Examples

The night we first met, at one of Mrs. Monmouth's giant parties, he was wearing a brown cashmere jacket with silver buttons and a soft pink Viyella shirt; instead of a necktie he wore a leather bolo drawn through a golden ring in which was set a lump of pale pure jade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bolo tie - a necktie consisting of a piece of cord fastened with an ornamental clasp

Etymology

Shortened from 'bolo tie', possibly influenced by Spanish 'bola' (ball), referring to the decorative clasp.
