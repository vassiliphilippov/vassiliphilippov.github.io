disdainful

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈdeɪnfəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈdeɪnf(ə)l / (BrE)
showing contempt or lack of respect; expressing strong dislike or disapproval.

Examples

His glance at Gyp Carmer was disdainful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contemptuous - showing or expressing contempt or disdain
  • Antonym: respectful - showing respect or esteem

Etymology

From Middle French *dédaigneux*, from dédaigner (to disdain), from Latin *dignari* (to consider worthy) with the prefix *dis-* (the opposite).
