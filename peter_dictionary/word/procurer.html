procurer

1. NOUN

/ prəˈkjʊrər / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊərə / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who obtains customers for prostitutes; a pimp

Examples

I 'd thought him a pimp or procurer but he did n't seem to be.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pimp - a person who controls prostitutes and arranges clients for them
  • Synonym: madam - a woman who manages a brothel or prostitutes

Etymology

From French *procurer* (to procure), from Latin *procurare* (to take care of, manage).
