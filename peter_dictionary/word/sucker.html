sucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈsʌkə / (BrE)
a person who is easily deceived or tricked; someone who is gullible and easy to take advantage of

Examples

People who take us for suckers are like the Westerner who had on exhibit his superior marksmanship in the form of a number of bull's-eye achievements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chump - a foolish or easily deceived person
  • Synonym: dupe - a person who is easily deceived or tricked
  • Synonym: victim - a person who is tricked or deceived
