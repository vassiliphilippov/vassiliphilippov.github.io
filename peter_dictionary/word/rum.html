rum

1. NOUN

/ rʌm / (AmE), / rʌm / (BrE)
A strong alcoholic drink distilled from fermented sugarcane molasses or juice, typically associated with the Caribbean.

Examples

Rum?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whisky - a spirit distilled from malted grain
  • Synonym: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic beverage

Etymology

Possibly from English slang for 'good' or 'fine,' later applied to the beverage, or from Romani 'rum' (strong, potent).
