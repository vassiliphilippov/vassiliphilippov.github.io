personalize

1. VERB

/ ˈpɝːsənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something personal or adapted for a particular person

Examples

Paperweight may be personalized on back while clay is leather hard.

Synset Examples

personalized service

Related Terms

  • Synonym: customize - modify something to suit a particular individual or task
  • Antonym: generalize - make something less specific or less personal

Etymology

From personal + -ize, first attested in the mid-19th century.
