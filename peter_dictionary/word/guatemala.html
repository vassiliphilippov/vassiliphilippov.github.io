Guatemala

1. PROPN

/ ɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlə / (AmE), / ɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlə / (BrE)
A country in Central America, bordered by Mexico, Belize, Honduras, and El Salvador, known for its Mayan history and diverse geography.

Examples

in Guatemala.

Etymology

From Spanish 'Guatemala', likely derived from a Nahuatl word meaning 'land of many trees'.
