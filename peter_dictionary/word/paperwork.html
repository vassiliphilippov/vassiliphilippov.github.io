paperwork

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpərˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌwɜːk / (BrE)
Administrative work involving the preparation, handling, or processing of documents such as forms, reports, or correspondence.

Examples

He could lead a patrol and he knew his paper work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clerical work - administrative work in an office

Etymology

From 'paper' + 'work', indicating tasks involving paperwork or documentation.
