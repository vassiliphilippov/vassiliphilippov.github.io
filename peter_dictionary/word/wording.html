wording

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the choice or arrangement of words in a spoken or written statement; the way in which something is expressed in words

Examples

Few will quarrel with the aim of the schools or with the wording of their curriculum.

Synset Examples

use concise military verbiage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phrasing - the way in which something is put into words; expression

Etymology

From 'word' + '-ing', meaning the act or manner of expressing by words.
