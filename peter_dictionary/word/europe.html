Europe

1. PROPN

/ ˈjʊɹəp / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəp / (BrE)
The continent located to the west of Asia and north of Africa, considered the second smallest continent.

Etymology

From Latin *Europa*, from Ancient Greek Εὐρώπη (Eurṓpē), a mythological figure; later used for the region.
