malposed

1. ADJ

/ ˌmælˈpoʊzd / (AmE), / ˌmælˈpəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a body part or anatomical structure) situated in an incorrect or abnormal position.

Examples

In addition, it is small enough to get into crevices, jacket and crown margins, malposed anteriors, and the back teeth.

Synset Examples

crooked malposed teeth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misplaced - not in the correct position

Etymology

From prefix 'mal-' (from Latin 'malus', bad) + 'posed' (from Latin 'ponere', to place).
