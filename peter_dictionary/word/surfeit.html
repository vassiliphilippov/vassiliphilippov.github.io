surfeit

1. VERB

/ ˈsɝː.fɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.fɪt / (BrE)
to supply or fill with too much of something; to satiate or overfill

Examples

False ideas surfeit another sector of our life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: satiate - to satisfy fully or to excess
  • Antonym: starve - to suffer or die from lack of food

Etymology

From Old French *surfaire* (to overdo), from Medieval Latin *superfacere* (to do in excess).
