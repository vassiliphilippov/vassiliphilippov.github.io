V

1. NOUN

/ viː / (AmE), / viː / (BrE)
The symbol for volt, the SI unit of electric potential or electromotive force, equal to the potential difference across a resistance of one ohm when one ampere of current flows through it.

Examples

At 100 Amp the 360 cycle ripple was less than 0.5 V (peak to peak) with a resistive load.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: volt - SI unit of electric potential, symbol V

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'volt', named after Italian physicist Alessandro Volta.
