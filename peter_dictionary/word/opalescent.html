opalescent

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊpəˈlɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊpəˈlɛsənt / (BrE)
Showing a milky, lustrous play of colors like that of an opal.

Examples

Miss Pulova has a voice that Maria Callas once described as "like chipping teeth with a screw driver ", and her round, opalescent face becomes fascinatingly reflective of the emotions demanded by the role of Rosalie.

Synset Examples

an iridescent oil slick
nacreous (or pearlescent) clouds looking like mother-of-pearl
a milky opalescent (or opaline) luster

Related Terms

  • Synonym: iridescent - showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
  • Synonym: pearly - having a lustrous, pearl-like appearance

Etymology

From 'opal' (the gemstone) + '-escent' (becoming).
