correspondingly

1. ADV

/ kəˈspɑndɪŋli / (AmE), / kəˈspɒndɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that matches or is directly related to something else.

Examples

In the presence of a low iodine intake, thyroglobulin labelled in vivo with * * f is found to contain more mono-iodotyrosine than normal, the amounts of di-iodotyrosine and iodothyronines being correspondingly reduced.

Synset Examples

the temperature decreases correspondingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proportionally - in proportion to something else; in a proportional manner

Etymology

From 'corresponding' + '-ly', with 'correspond' from Latin 'correspondere' (to answer together).
