domineering

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɑː.məˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdɒ.mɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
asserting one's will over others in a way that is overbearing or controlling

Examples

For all her domineering ways, I can n't conceive of her having had a deadly enemy ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overbearing - unpleasantly or arrogantly domineering
  • Antonym: submissive - ready to conform to the authority or will of others

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'domineer' + '-ing'; 'domineer' from French 'dominer' (to rule) and ultimately Latin 'dominari' (to rule, lord over).
