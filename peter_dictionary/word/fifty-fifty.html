fifty-fifty

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪftiˈfɪfti / (AmE), / ˌfɪftiˈfɪfti / (BrE)
equally divided between two parties; each person, group, etc. receives or contributes the same amount.

Examples

Under the law as it existed until 1943, the Federal Government made grants to the States on the basis of population, matching State expenditures on a 50 - 50 basis.

Synset Examples

even amounts of butter and sugar
on even terms
it was a fifty-fifty (or even) split
had a fifty-fifty (or even) chance
an even fight

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equal - being the same in quantity, size, degree, or value
  • Antonym: unequal - not equal in quantity, size, or value

Etymology

Formed by repeating "fifty" to indicate 50 percent to each of two sides; originated in US English in the early 20th century.
