enrichment

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈrɪtʃmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈrɪtʃmənt / (BrE)
the process of making something more meaningful, fuller, or rewarding

Examples

It is possible that the idea of enrichment of emotion is a fifth idea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improvement - the act of making something better
  • Synonym: enrich - to make fuller or more meaningful (verb form)

Etymology

From French *enrichissement*, from *enrichir* (to enrich).
