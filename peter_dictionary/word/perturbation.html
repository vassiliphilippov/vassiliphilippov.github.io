perturbation

1. NOUN

/ pɜrˌtɜrˈbeɪʃən / (AmE), / pəːˌtɜːˈbeɪʃən / (BrE)
a state of anxiety or worry; mental disturbance or agitation

Examples

It was then that they heard the tread of one descending and, in some perturbation glancing up, saw the patronne coming towards them as they gained the landing.

Synset Examples

there was too much anger and disturbance
she didn't realize the upset she caused me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: disturbance - interruption of mental calm; agitation
  • Antonym: calm - a peaceful and untroubled mental state

Etymology

From Latin perturbatio (confusion, disturbance), from perturbare (to disturb).
