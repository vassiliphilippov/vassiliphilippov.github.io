sintered

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪntɚd / (AmE), / ˈsɪntəd / (BrE)
Formed into a solid mass by heating and compressing powder, without liquefying it.

Examples

Sintered porous metals should be usable in principle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fused - joined or blended by melting together

Etymology

From 'sinter', from German 'Sinter' meaning slag or cinder.
