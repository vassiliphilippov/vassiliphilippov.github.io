chilling

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
causing a feeling of fear, shock, or horror; deeply disturbing or frightening.

Examples

For a blood chilling ring of terror to the very sound of his name was the tool he needed for the job he 'd promised to do.
Heiser, breathless and wild-eyed, brought the chilling news that the handspikes, heavers and holystones had been mysteriously removed from their customary places.

Synset Examples

a scary movie
the most terrible and shuddery...tales of murder and revenge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: terrifying - causing great fear or terror
  • Synonym: disturbing - causing anxiety or worry; unsettling
  • Antonym: comforting - making one feel better or less afraid
