mating

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The process or act of pairing a male and female animal for reproduction.

Examples

Garden Fresh, the result of a mating of Better Self and Rosy Fingered, seems to improve with each start and appeared to win the St. Patrick's Day Purse with some speed in reserve.
The first worker bees do not mate or lay eggs; males and mating females do not emerge until later in the season.

Synset Examples

the casual couplings of adolescents
the mating of some species occurs only in the spring

Related Terms

  • Synonym: copulation - the act of sexual intercourse between male and female animals
  • Synonym: breeding - the producing of offspring by animals

Etymology

From the verb 'mate', ultimately from Middle Low German *maten* (match, pair).
