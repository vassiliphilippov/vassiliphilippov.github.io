blending

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of mixing or combining different substances, qualities, or elements thoroughly.

Examples

A mist was walking on the water, white as cotton, but with a blending and merging grace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixing - the act of combining or mixing things together
  • Antonym: separation - the process of keeping things apart

Etymology

From blend (verb) + -ing.
