sportswoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrtsˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtsˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who participates in sports, especially as an athlete or competitor.

Examples

Just as modern transportation has outmoded the early Studebaker covered wagon, the demand of today's sportsmen and women has necessitated changes in their equipment.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sportsman - a man who participates in sports
  • Synonym: athlete - a person who is proficient in sports and physical exercise

Etymology

Formed from 'sport' + 'woman', modeled after 'sportsman'.
