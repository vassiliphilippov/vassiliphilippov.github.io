shapely

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃeɪpli / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpli / (BrE)
having a well-proportioned and attractive shape, especially in reference to the human body

Examples

Henri has always had shapely legs from swimming and water skiing and really does n't have to work them very much.

Synset Examples

a slim waist and shapely legs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-proportioned - having pleasing, balanced proportions
  • Antonym: lanky - ungracefully tall and thin

Etymology

Formed from 'shape' + '-ly', meaning 'having the qualities of shape'.
