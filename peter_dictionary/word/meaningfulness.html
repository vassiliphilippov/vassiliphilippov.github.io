meaningfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈmiːnɪŋfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈmiːnɪŋfəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality of having value, purpose, or significance.

Examples

The artistic interest, then, lies in what the encounter may be made to represent, in the power of some central significance to draw the details into relevance and meaningfulness.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: meaninglessness - the quality of lacking meaning or significance
  • Synonym: significance - the quality of being important or of giving meaning

Etymology

From 'meaningful' + '-ness'.
