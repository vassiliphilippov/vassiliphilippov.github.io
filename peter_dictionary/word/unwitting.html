unwitting

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Not done deliberately or intentionally; accidental or unintentional.

Examples

I am usually filled with an uneasiness that through some unwitting slip all hell may break loose.

Synset Examples

an unintended slight
an unintentional pun
the offense was unintentional
an unwitting mistake may be overlooked

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intentional - done deliberately or on purpose
  • Antonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • Synonym: unintentional - not done on purpose or intentionally

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'witting' (knowing), from Old English 'witan' (to know).
