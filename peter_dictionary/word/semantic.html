semantic

1. ADJ

/ səˈmæn.tɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈmæn.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to meaning or the study of meaning, especially in language or logic.

Examples

Finally, information is retrieved from the dictionary as required by stages of the translation process - the grammatic description for sentence-structure determination, equivalent choice information for semantic analysis, and target-language equivalents for output construction.

Synset Examples

semantic analysis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semantical - alternative, less common form meaning relating to semantics

Etymology

From French sémantique, from Ancient Greek sēmantikos (significant, having meaning), from sēmainō (to signify, indicate).
