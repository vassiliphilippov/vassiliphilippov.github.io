unpatriotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnˌpeɪtriˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.pæ.triˈɒt.ɪk / (BrE)
not showing love or loyalty towards your own country; lacking patriotism.

Examples

No doubt such a thing would be considered unpatriotic.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: patriotic - showing love for your country

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'un-' (not) to 'patriotic'.
