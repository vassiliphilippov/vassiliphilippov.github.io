brightness

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being bright; the degree to which an object or area appears light or luminous, especially as measured along a scale from black to white.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Old English 'beorht' (bright) plus '-ness', forming a noun indicating the state or quality of being bright.
