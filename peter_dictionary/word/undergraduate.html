undergraduate

1. NOUN

/ ˌʌndərˈɡrædʒuət / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈɡrædʒuət / (BrE)
a student at a university or college who has not yet taken a first degree

Examples

After all, the field is large, difficult to define and seldom taught properly to American undergraduates.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: postgraduate - a student who has already received a first degree and is continuing studies

Etymology

From under- (lower in rank) + graduate (one who has a degree).
