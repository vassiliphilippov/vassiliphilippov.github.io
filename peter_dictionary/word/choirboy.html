choirboy

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwaɪərˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪəˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
A boy who sings in a choir, especially in a church.

Examples

He had been a choir boy at the Holy Name Cathedral and also served as an acolyte to Father O' Brien.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chorister - a choir member, especially a singer in a choir
  • Antonym: choirgirl - a girl who sings in a choir
