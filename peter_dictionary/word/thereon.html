thereon

1. ADV

/ ðɛrˈɑn / (AmE), / ðeəˈrɒn / (BrE)
On that; on the thing just mentioned, especially in legal or formal contexts.

Examples

At his trial, petitioner sought to secure through subpoena duces tecum the longhand notes of the Department's hearing officer, Evensen, as well as his report thereon.

Synset Examples

text and commentary thereon

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thereupon - immediately or as a result of that

Etymology

From Middle English 'theron', from Old English 'þǣr' (there) + 'on'.
