stirring

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɝː.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɜː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of mixing a liquid or substance by moving an implement such as a spoon around in it

Examples

Ahead there was a stirring of sudden movement at a crossroads.

Synset Examples

constant stirring prevents it from burning on the bottom of the pan

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mixing - combining substances so they become one mass
  • Synonym: beating - agitating a mixture briskly to blend or add air

Etymology

From the verb 'stir', Middle English 'stirren', of uncertain origin.
