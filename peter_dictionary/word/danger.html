danger

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ˈdeɪndʒə / (BrE)
The possibility of harm, injury, or loss; exposure to a risk or peril.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: risk - possibility of something bad happening
  • Antonym: safety - condition of being protected from danger or risk
  • Synonym: peril - serious and immediate danger

Etymology

From Old French *dangier* (power, authority, peril) and Medieval Latin *dominiarium* (power, jurisdiction).

Related Terms

  • Antonym: safety - the condition of being protected from danger, risk, or injury

Etymology
