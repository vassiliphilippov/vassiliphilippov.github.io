indefinity

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪndəˈfɪnɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈfɪnɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being vague, unclear, or not precisely defined.

Examples

No less ambiguous was the indefinity of a certain clergyman's sermon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefiniteness - the quality of being indefinite or not clearly defined
  • Antonym: definiteness - the quality of being clear and definite

Etymology

From Latin 'indefinitas' via 'indefinite' + '-ity'.
