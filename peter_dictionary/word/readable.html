readable

1. ADJ

/ ˈriːdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈriːdəbl / (BrE)
(of text, handwriting, or code) easy to read or decipher.

Examples

Ever since Bambi, and, more recently, Born Free, there have been a lot of books about animals, but few compare with Max Fink's wry, understated, charming, and immensely readable My Friend, the Quizzical Salamander.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: illegible - impossible or hard to read or decipher

Etymology

From English 'read' + '-able', meaning capable of being read.
