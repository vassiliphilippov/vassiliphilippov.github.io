housing

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
buildings or structures collectively in which people live; accommodation or dwellings provided for people

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accommodation - a place to live, work, or stay
  • Synonym: shelter - a place giving protection or refuge

Etymology

From the verb 'house' + -ing, originally meaning 'lodging, accommodation'.
