morphologic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɔrfəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɔːfəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with the form and structure of organisms or their parts; morphological.

Examples

However, the dynamics of airflow, from morphologic considerations alone, may conceivably be different in the monkey than in the horse.

Synset Examples

morphological differences

Related Terms

  • Synonym: morphological - relating to morphology, especially in biology or linguistics

Etymology

From Greek morphē (form) + -logic, from morphology.
