gubernatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡuːbə.nəˈtɔːri.əl / (AmE), / ˌɡjuː.bə.nəˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
Relating to a governor or the office of governor.

Examples

When the gubernatorial campaign starts, Caldwell is expected to become a campaign coordinator for Byrd.

Synset Examples

gubernatorial election

Etymology

From Latin gubernator (governor) + -ial; ultimately from Latin gubernare (to govern).
