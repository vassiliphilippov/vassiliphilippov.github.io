neurosis

1. NOUN

/ nuˈroʊsɪs / (AmE), / njʊəˈrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A mild mental or emotional disorder not caused by any known physical disease and characterized by symptoms such as anxiety, depression, or obsessive behavior.
