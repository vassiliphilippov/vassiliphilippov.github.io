tensionless

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛnʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈtɛnʃənləs / (BrE)
Free from tension; not exhibiting or characterized by tension.

Examples

Badura-Skoda-Vienna Konzerthaus (Westminster) and Demus-Schubert Quartet (Deutsche Grammophon) are both warm toned, pleasantly lyrical, but rather slack and tensionless.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: taut - stretched tight; under tension

Etymology

Formed by adding the suffix '-less' (meaning 'without') to 'tension.'
