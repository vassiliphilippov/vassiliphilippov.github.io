buttoned

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌtnd / (AmE), / ˈbʌtənd / (BrE)
(of clothing or other items) fastened or closed with buttons.

Examples

The bodice beneath was buttoned and, withdrawing his lips from hers, he set her upright on his knee and started to undo it, unhurriedly as if she were a child.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unbuttoned - not fastened with buttons; open
