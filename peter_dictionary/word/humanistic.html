humanistic

1. ADJ

/ hjuːməˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / hjuːməˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to a philosophy that emphasizes human dignity and the capacity for fulfillment through reason and scientific method, often rejecting religious beliefs.

Examples

Under the religious impulse, whether theistic or humanistic, men have joy in living; life leads somewhere.

Synset Examples

the humanist belief in continuous emergent evolution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanist - a follower or advocate of humanism

Etymology

From humanist + -ic, relating to the humanist movement and philosophy.
