evolution

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛvəˈluʃən, ˌiv- / (AmE), / ˌiːvəˈluːʃən, ˌɛv- / (BrE)
a gradual process of development or change through successive stages, especially toward a more advanced or mature form

Related Terms

  • Synonym: development - the process of growing or causing something to grow or become larger or more advanced
  • Synonym: progression - the process of developing or moving gradually towards a more advanced state
  • Antonym: regression - a return to a former or less developed state

Etymology

From Latin 'evolutio' (an unrolling), from 'evolvere' (to unroll).
