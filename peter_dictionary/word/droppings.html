droppings

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɑpɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpɪŋz / (BrE)
the solid waste matter excreted by animals

Examples

Also, aids in the control of horn flies by preventing them from hatching in the droppings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manure - animal dung used for fertilizing land
  • Synonym: feces - waste matter discharged from the bowels

Etymology

From the verb 'drop' + -ing, referring to what has been dropped by animals.
