congressman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːŋ.ɡrəs.mæn / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋ.ɡrəs.mæn / (BrE)
A member of the United States House of Representatives.

Examples

But he added that none of Georgia's congressmen specifically asked him to offer the resolution.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: congresswoman - a female member of the United States House of Representatives
  • Antonym: Senator - a member of the United States Senate

Etymology

From 'congress' + 'man', referring to a male member of the U.S. Congress; usage dates from the late 18th century.
