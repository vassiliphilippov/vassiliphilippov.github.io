vocalist

1. NOUN

/ ˈvoʊkəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈvəʊkəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who sings, especially as a professional or as a member of a musical group.

Examples

After leaving Whiteman, Mercer joined the Benny Goodman band as a vocalist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: singer - a person who sings

Etymology

From Latin 'vocalis' (of the voice), from 'vox' (voice), with '-ist' suffix to denote a person.
