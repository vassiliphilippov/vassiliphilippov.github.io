grandson

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌsʌn / (AmE), / ˈɡræn.sʌn / (BrE)
A son of one's son or daughter.

Examples

Your grandson Frank."

Related Terms

  • Antonym: granddaughter - female grandchild

Etymology

Formed from 'grand-' (in the sense of a generation removed) + 'son'. Patterned after related terms like 'grandfather' and 'granddaughter'.
