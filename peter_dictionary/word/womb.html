womb

1. NOUN

/ wuːm / (AmE), / wuːm / (BrE)
the organ in female mammals where offspring develop before birth; the uterus

Examples

The raped valley was a pregnant womb awaiting abortion.
Blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uterus - medical term for the womb

Etymology

Old English wamb, womb (belly), of Germanic origin.
