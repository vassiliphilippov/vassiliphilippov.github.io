arched

1. ADJ

/ ɑrtʃt / (AmE), / ɑːtʃt / (BrE)
Having a curved shape like that of an arch.

Examples

Across the front of a yard and down the side, they were iron, either spiked along the top or arched in half circles.

Synset Examples

an arched ceiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arched - having the shape of an arch
  • Antonym: straight - having no curve or bend; not arched

Etymology

From the noun 'arch' + past participle suffix '-ed'.
