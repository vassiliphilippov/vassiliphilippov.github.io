honeysuckle

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (BrE)
A climbing shrub or vine of the genus Lonicera, typically with fragrant flowers and sometimes sweet nectar.

Examples

Side fences were hidden beneath lilacs and hundred leaf roses; front fences were covered with Virginia creeper or trumpet vines or honeysuckle.

Etymology

From Middle English 'hony-soukel', literally meaning 'honey suckle', referring to the plant's sweet nectar.
