powder-puff

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaʊdər ˌpʌf / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə ˌpʌf / (BrE)
(of a sporting event or competition) involving only female participants, often for charity or entertainment rather than serious athletic competition.

Examples

The Orioles once again performed at the plate in powderpuff fashion, gathering only seven blows off the offerings of three Kansas City pitchers.

Synset Examples

powder-puff baseball
a powder-puff football game

Etymology

From 'powder puff', a soft pad for applying face powder, associated with femininity.
