whatsoever

1. ADJ

/ ˌwʌtsoʊˈɛvər / (AmE), / ˌwɒtsəʊˈɛvə / (BrE)
(used after a noun to emphasize a negative statement) at all; of any kind or degree.

Examples

That I have never been arrested or indicted for any crime whatsoever.

Synset Examples

give me any peaches you don't want

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whatever - anything or everything, no matter what

Etymology

From Middle English, from 'what so ever', literally 'whatever'.
