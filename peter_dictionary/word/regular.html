regular

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
happening or done at uniform intervals; following a fixed order, schedule, or procedure

Examples

The One Leg Lunge is a split and all lifters practice this in their regular workouts.

Synset Examples

regular duties

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irregular - not following a fixed order, routine, or schedule

Etymology

From Latin 'regularis' (according to rule), from 'regula' (a rule).
