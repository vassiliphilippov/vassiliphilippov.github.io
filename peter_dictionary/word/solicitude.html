solicitude

1. NOUN

/ səˈlɪsəˌtud / (AmE), / səˈlɪsɪtjuːd / (BrE)
the care or concern that someone shows for another person's well-being or comfort

Examples

Give them food, some shade, mulch, water and more food, and they 'll repay your solicitude with beauty.
He had seen a dry, old, yellowing hand reach out, with that painful solicitude, to touch, to rearrange, to shift aimlessly, some object worth a pfennig.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concern - a feeling of worry or care about a person or thing
  • Synonym: care - serious attention or consideration applied to doing something correctly or to avoid damage
  • Antonym: indifference - lack of interest, concern, or sympathy

Etymology

From Latin sollicitudo (anxiety, care, uneasiness); from sollicitus (full of care).
