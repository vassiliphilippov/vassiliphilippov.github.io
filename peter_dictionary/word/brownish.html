brownish

1. ADJ

/ ˈbraʊnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat brown in color; having a tinge or shade of brown.

Examples

Krakow Boulevard was filled with black Nazi uniforms and the dirty brownish ones of their Auxiliaries.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - a color resembling that of earth or wood
