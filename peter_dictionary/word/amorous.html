amorous

1. ADJ

/ ˈæm.ɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈæm.ər.əs / (BrE)
showing, feeling, or relating to sexual desire or romantic love

Examples

Occasionally gay young blades would write vividly to boon companions at home about their amorous exploits in Richmond, Petersburg, Washington, or Nashville.

Synset Examples

feeling amorous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: romantic - characterized by or suggestive of an idealized view of reality, especially in love
  • Synonym: passionate - showing or caused by strong feelings or a strong belief, especially of love or desire
  • Antonym: platonic - (of love or friendship) intimate and affectionate but not sexual

Etymology

From Old French *amoureux*, from Latin *amōrus* (loving), from *amor* (love).
