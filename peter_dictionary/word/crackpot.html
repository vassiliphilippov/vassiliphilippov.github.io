crackpot

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrækˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈkrækˌpɒt / (BrE)
A person who is considered eccentric, strange, or crazy, especially in a whimsical or impractical way.

Examples

He treats this deep-eyed, bearded, bony crackpot with tangible affection and respect.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eccentric - person with unconventional or slightly strange views or behavior
  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is mentally ill (dated/offensive); an extremely foolish person
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not crazy or foolish

Etymology

Originally American slang, combining 'crack' (slang for crazy) and 'pot' (head); dates from the early 20th century.
