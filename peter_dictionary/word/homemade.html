homemade

1. ADJ

/ hoʊmˈmeɪd / (AmE), / həʊmˈmeɪd / (BrE)
Made or produced in the home or by oneself rather than by a commercial manufacturer.

Examples

She went downstairs and received another curious shock, for when Glendora flapped into the dining room in her homemade moccasins, Sarah asked her when she had brought coffee to her room and Glendora said she had n't.

Synset Examples

homemade bread

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handmade - made by hand rather than by machine

Etymology

Formed from 'home' + 'made', meaning made at home; attested from the 17th century.
