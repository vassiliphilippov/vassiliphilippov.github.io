fixer

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪksər / (AmE), / ˈfɪksə / (BrE)
a person who arranges matters by using underhand or illegal methods, especially to obtain favors from authorities, usually for a fee

Examples

When Mayor Dever heard of the banquet he summoned Hughes for an explanation of why he had been dishonouring the police department by consorting with these felons and fixers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broker - person who arranges transactions between parties
