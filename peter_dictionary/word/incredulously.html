incredulously

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈkrɛdʒələsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrɛdjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows unwillingness or inability to believe something; with disbelief or skepticism.

Examples

[INCREDULOUSLY]

Synset Examples

the woman looked up at her incredulously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skeptically - in a skeptical manner

Etymology

From 'incredulous' (from Latin incredulus, ‘unbelieving’) + '-ly'.
