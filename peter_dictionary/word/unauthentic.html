unauthentic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.ɔːˈθɛn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɔːˈθɛn.tɪk / (BrE)
not genuine; intended to deceive or lacking authenticity

Examples

He talked about unauthentic storylines too.

Synset Examples

a spurious work of art

Related Terms

  • Antonym: authentic - genuine; real; not false or copied
  • Synonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
  • Synonym: spurious - not being what it purports to be; false or fake

Etymology

From prefix 'un-' (not) + 'authentic' (from Greek 'authentikos' meaning genuine, original).
