gourmet

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊrˈmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊə.meɪ / (BrE)
A person with a discerning palate who enjoys and appreciates fine food and drink.

Examples

At the home of a gourmet the new maid was instructed in the fine points of serving.
But since the gourmet insisted that it is done that way at the most fashionable dinners, the girl reluctantly agreed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: epicure - person who takes particular pleasure in fine food and drink
  • Synonym: gastronome - person who is knowledgeable about good food and drink

Etymology

From French 'gourmet' (wine taster, connoisseur), originally meaning a wine dealer's servant.
