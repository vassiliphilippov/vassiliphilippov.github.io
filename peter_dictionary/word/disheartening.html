disheartening

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈhɑːrtənɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈhɑːtənɪŋ / (BrE)
Making someone lose hope or confidence; causing discouragement.

Examples

The Redbirds' disheartening 11 - 7 collapse against the Phillies Sunday.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: encouraging - giving someone support or confidence; supportive
  • Synonym: demoralizing - causing someone to lose confidence or hope

Etymology

From dishearten + -ing; 'dishearten' from 'dis-' (expressing reversal) + 'hearten' (to give courage).
