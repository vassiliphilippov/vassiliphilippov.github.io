sedate

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈdeɪt / (AmE), / sɪˈdeɪt / (BrE)
calm, dignified, and unhurried; characterized by quiet and proper behavior or atmosphere

Examples

When Joe identified himself, he nodded, unsmiling, and ushered him into a sedate living room.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calm - quiet and peaceful, not excited or agitated
  • Antonym: lively - full of life and energy; active and energetic

Etymology

From Latin sedatus, past participle of sedare (to calm, to settle).
