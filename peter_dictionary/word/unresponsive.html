unresponsive

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.rɪˈspɑn.sɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈspɒn.sɪv / (BrE)
Not reacting or replying to a stimulus, influence, or request; not responsive.

Examples

Most attempts to develop any sympathy for Brown and his actions found an unresponsive audience in Rhode Island.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively to a stimulus or suggestion

Etymology

From un- (not) + responsive (from Latin respondēre, to answer).
