actual

1. ADJ

/ ˈæktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈæk.tʃu.əl / (BrE)
existing in fact; real, rather than imagined, assumed, or potential.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: potential - having or showing the capacity to develop into something in the future but not yet actual
  • Synonym: real - existing or occurring as fact, not imaginary or supposed

Etymology

From Late Latin actualis (active, practical), from Latin actus (deed)
