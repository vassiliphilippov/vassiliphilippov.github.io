crazed

1. ADJ

/ kreɪzd / (AmE), / kreɪzd / (BrE)
Behaving as if insane or driven mad, especially due to strong emotion, shock, or distress.

Examples

Half crazed by the weight dragging, the dust, and the heat, the horse leaped our wall, dashing out the rider's brains against it, and leaving him lying there among us - while the horse crashed away through the brush.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - mentally ill; deranged
  • Synonym: mad - mentally ill; insane
  • Antonym: sane - of sound mind; not insane

Etymology

From 'craze' (to derange or break), + -ed, meaning affected by craziness.
