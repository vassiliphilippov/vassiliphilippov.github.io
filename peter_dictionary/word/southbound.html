southbound

1. ADJ

/ ˈsaʊθ.baʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsaʊθ.baʊnd / (BrE)
(of vehicles, people, or things) moving or heading toward the south.

Examples

The New York Central today handles 60 percent of all southbound commuter traffic coming into New York City.

Synset Examples

a southbound train

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northbound - moving toward the north
  • Antonym: eastbound - moving toward the east
  • Antonym: westbound - moving toward the west

Etymology

Formed from 'south' + 'bound', meaning 'destined for or moving in the direction of the south'.
