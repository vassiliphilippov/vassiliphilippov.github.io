evocation

1. NOUN

/ ˌiː.voʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌiː.vəʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
The act of bringing something to mind or recreating it, especially in an imaginative or vivid way.

Examples

The costumes are stunning evocations of the voluminous gowns and picture hats of the Gibson Girl days.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recreation - the process of creating something again or anew
  • Synonym: imagination - the faculty of forming new ideas or images

Etymology

From Latin evocatio, from evocare (to call forth).
