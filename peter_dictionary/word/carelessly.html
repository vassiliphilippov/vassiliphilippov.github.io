carelessly

1. ADV

/ ˈkɛrlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈkeələsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of care or attention.

Examples

Brenner shrugged carelessly.

Synset Examples

carelessly raised the children's hopes without thinking of their possible disappointment

Related Terms

  • Antonym: carefully - in a way that shows care or attention

Etymology

From 'careless' + '-ly'. 'Careless' from Middle English 'carless', from 'care' + '-less'.
