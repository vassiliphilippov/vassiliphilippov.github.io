butterfat

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌtərˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌfæt / (BrE)
The natural fat component of milk from which butter is produced.

Examples

Fed to dairy cattle to increase milk production and butterfat percentage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: milk fat - The fat content of milk; another term for butterfat

Etymology

Formed from 'butter' and 'fat', referring to the fat in milk used to make butter.
