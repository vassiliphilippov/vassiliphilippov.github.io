Winnipeg

1. PROPN

/ ˈwɪnɪˌpɛg / (AmE), / ˈwɪnɪˌpɛg / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Manitoba, Canada, located in the southern part of the province and notable for its severe winters

Examples

By October the little colony about Fort Douglas (present-day Winnipeg) numbered 100.

Etymology

From Cree *win-nipi* (muddy waters), referring to the nearby Lake Winnipeg.
