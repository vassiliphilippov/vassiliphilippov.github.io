Savior

1. PROPN

/ ˈseɪvjər / (AmE), / ˈseɪvjə / (BrE)
Jesus Christ, regarded by Christians as the redeemer and central figure of Christianity.

Examples

To be the Savior and the Lord, Jesus Christ has to be a historical individual with a biography all his own; he dare not be a cosmic aeon that swoops to earth for a while but never identifies itself with man's history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jesus Christ - central figure of Christianity, regarded as the Savior

Etymology

From Latin salvator (a savior, deliverer), from salvare (to save).
