cohesiveness

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈhisɪvnəs / (AmE), / kəʊˈhiːsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of forming a united whole or sticking together

Examples

Apart from spirit there could be no community, for it is spirit which draws men into community and gives to any community its unity, cohesiveness, and permanence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coherence - the quality of being logical and consistent or forming a unified whole
  • Synonym: unity - the state of being united or joined as a whole

Etymology

From Latin cohaerere (to stick together), via "cohesive" + "-ness".
