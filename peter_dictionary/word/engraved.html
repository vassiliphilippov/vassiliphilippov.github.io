engraved

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈɡreɪvd / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡreɪvd / (BrE)
(of a design, text, or image) cut or impressed onto a hard surface.

Examples

Like many others, he had to work hard, long hours in a struggling family business which, though it was allied to art of a kind - the design and production of engraved seals - bore no relation to the painting of pictures.

Synset Examples

an incised design
engraved invitations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incised - cut into a surface; engraved

Etymology

Past participle of 'engrave', from Middle French 'engraver', from en- (in) + graver (to grave, carve).
