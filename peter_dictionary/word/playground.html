playground

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌɡraʊnd / (BrE)
A place or area where many people, especially adults, go for recreation or leisure activities, such as a holiday resort or popular vacation destination.

Examples

It is significant that the Catskills, which used to be the summer playground for older teen-agers, a kind of summer suburb of New York, no longer attracts them in great numbers - except for those who work there as waiters, bus boys, or counselors in the day camps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resort - place where people go for vacations and recreation
