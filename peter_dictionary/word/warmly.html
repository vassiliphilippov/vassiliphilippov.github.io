warmly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɔrmli / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmli / (BrE)
In a hearty or enthusiastic manner; with genuine friendliness or affection.

Examples

Warmly.

Synset Examples

`Yes,' the children chorused heartily
We welcomed her warmly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartily - with sincere enthusiasm or affection
  • Antonym: coldly - in an unfriendly or unemotional manner
