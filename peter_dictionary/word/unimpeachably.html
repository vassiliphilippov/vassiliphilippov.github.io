unimpeachably

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.ɪmˈpitʃ.ə.bli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪmˈpiː.tʃə.bli / (BrE)
In a manner that is impossible to criticize or question; beyond doubt or reproach.

Examples

Brooklyn College is unequivocally Jewish in tone, and efforts to detribalize the college by bringing in unimpeachably midwestern types on the faculty have been unavailing.

Synset Examples

Fred Winter is unquestionably the jockey to follow
they hired unimpeachably first-rate faculty members

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unquestionably - without doubt; certainly

Etymology

From 'unimpeachable' + suffix '-ly'; 'unimpeachable' from 'un-' (not) + 'impeach' (to accuse or charge) + '-able'.
