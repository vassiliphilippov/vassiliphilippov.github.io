1. NOUN

/ ˈnoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈnəʊʃən / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: impression - a vague idea or belief about something

  • Degree Higher: conviction - a firmly held belief or opinion

Etymology
