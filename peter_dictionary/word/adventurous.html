adventurous

1. ADJ

/ ədˈvɛn.tʃɚ.əs / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃərəs / (BrE)
willing to try new, exciting, or dangerous experiences or activities; eager for adventure.

Examples

Yet a moment did come that night when the adventurous letter writer and fantasist seemed to stride off my flashy pages, out of my mind, and plant himself in reality.

Synset Examples

adventurous pioneers
the risks and gains of an adventuresome economy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daring - willing to take risks; bold
  • Antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'adventurosus', from 'adventura' (a thing about to happen).
