smilingly

1. ADV

/ ˈsmaɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that involves or is accompanied by a smile; with a smile.

Examples

Mrs. Roebuck smilingly declined and began suddenly to go on about her son, who was "onleh a little younguh than you bawhs ".

Synset Examples

the girl reminded her smilingly of their childhood spent together

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grimly - in a stern or forbidding manner

Etymology

Formed from 'smiling' + '-ly', denoting manner.
