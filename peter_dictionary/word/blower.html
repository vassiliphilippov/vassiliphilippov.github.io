blower

1. NOUN

/ ˈbloʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbləʊ.ə / (BrE)
a machine or device that produces a strong current of air, especially for ventilation, heating, or cleaning purposes

Examples

A blower may be installed to increase comfort.
A blower is essential for the double wall shelter and for the underground shelters.
Blowers should be operated periodically on a regular schedule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fan - a device with rotating blades that creates a current of air
