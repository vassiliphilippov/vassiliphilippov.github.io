solved

1. ADJ

/ sɑlvd / (AmE), / sɒlvd / (BrE)
(of a problem or mystery) explained, answered, or worked out; no longer remaining uncertain or mysterious.

Examples

The rustling problem was by no means solved.

Synset Examples

mysteries solved and unsolved; problems resolved and unresolved

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsolved - not explained, answered, or worked out
  • Synonym: resolved - settled or solved (of problems or disputes)
