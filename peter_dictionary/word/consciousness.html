consciousness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑn.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the state of being aware of and able to think and perceive one's surroundings, thoughts, and self

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: awareness - the state of having knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • Antonym: unconsciousness - the state of not being aware, especially after loss of consciousness

Etymology

From Latin 'conscientia' (knowledge within oneself), via French 'conscience' and English 'conscious'.
