rousing

1. ADJ

/ ˈraʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
exciting or stimulating enthusiasm or strong emotion; stirring

Examples

So the audience last night was all ears and eyes just after Act 2, got a rousing opening chorus, "Where's Charley? "

Synset Examples

a rousing sermon
stirring events such as wars and rescues

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stirring - evoking strong emotion; exciting

Etymology

From the verb 'rouse' + -ing, originally meaning 'arousing' or 'stimulating.'
