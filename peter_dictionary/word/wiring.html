wiring

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
The system of wires installed in a building or device for carrying electricity.

Examples

He had a head full of bad wiring, I guess.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circuit - a closed path through which electricity flows

Etymology

From 'wire' + '-ing', denoting the action or result of fitting with wires.
