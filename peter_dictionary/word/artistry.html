artistry

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.tɪ.stri / (AmE), / ˈɑː.tɪ.stri / (BrE)
skill or craft of a high standard, especially as a result of artistic ability or creativity.

Examples

Since Russian was being spoken instead of Spanish, there is no violation of artistry or logic here.

Synset Examples

the art of conversation
it's quite an art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftsmanship - skill in a particular craft
  • Antonym: ineptitude - lack of skill or ability

Etymology

From French *artiste* (artist), ultimately from Latin *ars* (art).
