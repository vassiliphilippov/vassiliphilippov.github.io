aloof

1. ADJ

/ əˈluf / (AmE), / əˈluːf / (BrE)
Not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant in manner.

Examples

Still the guards did not move, but stood inert, aloof from the slow scrambling man.

Synset Examples

stood apart with aloof dignity
a distant smile
he was upstage with strangers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distant - not friendly; cool or reserved in manner
  • Synonym: unapproachable - not easy to talk to or deal with
  • Antonym: friendly - kind and pleasant; easy to approach
  • Antonym: sociable - willing to talk and engage with others; friendly

Etymology

Probably from Dutch *loef* (windward side, weather side), originally nautical.
