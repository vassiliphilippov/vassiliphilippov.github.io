broiler

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɔɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈbrɔɪlə / (BrE)
an oven or compartment of a cooker used for broiling food

Examples

He was tired, he had his business worries, and the sight of his wife arranging pork chops in the broiler only seemed like an extension of a boring day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grill - a device on a cooker or standalone appliance for cooking by radiant heat from above or below

Etymology

From 'broil' (to cook by direct heat) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
