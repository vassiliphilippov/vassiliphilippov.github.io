falling

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
Becoming lower in amount, level, or degree.

Examples

Because of the falling characteristic of the rectifier, no ballast resistor was required for stability of operation.

Synset Examples

falling temperature

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rising - increasing in amount or degree
  • Synonym: decreasing - becoming smaller in amount or degree

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'fall', from Old English 'feallan'.
