uncertified

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɝːtɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːtɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not having the official documents, approval, or endorsement required for a particular purpose.

Examples

Whether the check is certified or uncertified, the tax is not paid until the check is paid.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: certified - having official documentation or endorsement
