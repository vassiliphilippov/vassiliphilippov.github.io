preferable

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɛfərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈprɛfərəbl / (BrE)
More desirable or suitable than another; better or more acceptable.

Examples

Was it preferable to meet her at home or in the city?

Synset Examples

coffee is preferable to tea
Danny's preferred name is `Dan'

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: desirable - worth having or seeking; pleasing, excellent, or fine
  • Degree Higher: ideal - most suitable or perfect; exactly right for a particular purpose
  • Antonym: undesirable - not wanted or not attractive, opposite of desirable

Etymology

From Late Latin 'preferibilis', from Latin 'praeferre' (to prefer).
