concentration

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑn.sənˈtreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.sənˈtreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the action or power of focusing all one's attention or mental effort on a particular activity or object

Examples

The program had been arranged to permit the establishment of a mood of intense concentration on the music.
Amy did not laugh - this was work, concentration, achievement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attention - the act of concentrating or focusing mentally

Etymology

From Latin 'concentratio' (a gathering together), from 'com-' (together) + 'centrum' (center).
