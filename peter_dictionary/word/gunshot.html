gunshot

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnʃɒt / (BrE)
The firing of a gun or the sound produced when a gun is discharged.

Examples

Occasionally they heard gun-shot signals and a number of horsemen were sighted on the hills, disappearing at the posse's approach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunfire - the firing of guns, especially repetitively or in battle

Etymology

From 'gun' + 'shot', meaning the shooting or sound of a firearm.
