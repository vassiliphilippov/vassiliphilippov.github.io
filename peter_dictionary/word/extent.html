/ ɪkˈstɛnt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnt / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scope - the range of something's coverage or activity
  • Synonym: range - the extent or scope of something's reach or coverage
  • Antonym: limit - the point or edge beyond which something does not extend

Etymology

From Latin extensum, neuter of extensus, past participle of extendere (to stretch out).
