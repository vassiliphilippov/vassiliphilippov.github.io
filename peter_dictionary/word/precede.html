precede

1. VERB

/ prɪˈsid / (AmE), / prɪˈsiːd / (BrE)
to come before something in time; to happen or exist earlier than something else

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: follow - to come after in time or order
  • Synonym: predate - to exist or occur at a date earlier than something

Etymology

From Latin prae- (before) + cēdere (to go).
