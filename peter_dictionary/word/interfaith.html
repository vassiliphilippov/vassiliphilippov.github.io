interfaith

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈfeɪθ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈfeɪθ / (BrE)
involving or relating to people or groups belonging to different religious faiths

Examples

When I pressed for a purely religious definition, I encountered the familiar blend of liberal piety, interfaith good will, and a small residue of ethnic loyalty.

Synset Examples

an interfaith marriage
interfaith good will

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecumenical - promoting unity among the world's Christian churches or religions

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'inter-' (between, among) and 'faith' (religion or belief).
