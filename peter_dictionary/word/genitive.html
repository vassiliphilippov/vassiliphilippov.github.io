genitive

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛnətɪv / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪtɪv / (BrE)
(of a grammatical case) indicating possession or close association, or relating to such a case.

Examples

Our police have rounded up all of the Joker's men.

Synset Examples

possessive pronouns
the genitive endings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: possessive - (of a grammatical form) indicating possession

Etymology

From Latin *genitivus* (of birth, of origin), from *genitus*, past participle of *gignere* (to beget).
