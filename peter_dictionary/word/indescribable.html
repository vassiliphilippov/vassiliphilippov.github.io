indescribable

1. ADJ

/ ɪn.dɪˈskraɪ.bə.bəl / (AmE), / ɪn.dɪˈskraɪ.bə.bəl / (BrE)
Too unusual, extreme, or intense to be adequately described in words.

Examples

Hell's own amount of chaulmoogra oil did nothing to alleviate their torment; they expired amid indescribable fantods, imploring the Blessed One to forgive their desecration.

Synset Examples

indefinable yearnings
indescribable beauty
ineffable ecstasy
inexpressible anguish
unspeakable happiness
unutterable contempt
a thing of untellable splendor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indefinable - impossible to define or explain precisely
  • Synonym: ineffable - too great or extreme to be expressed in words
  • Synonym: inexpressible - impossible to communicate or describe in words
  • Synonym: unspeakable - too bad or horrific to be expressed in words

Etymology

Formed by adding the prefix 'in-' (not) to 'describable' (able to be described).
