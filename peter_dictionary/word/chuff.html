chuff

1. VERB

/ tʃʌf / (AmE), / tʃʌf / (BrE)
(of a person, animal, or engine) to blow or breathe out forcefully and loudly, often making a short, explosive sound.

Examples

We were coming to an intersection, turning right, chuffing to a stop.

Synset Examples

he huffed and puffed as he made his way up the mountain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puff - to blow in short quick bursts
  • Synonym: huff - to breathe heavily or blow out air noisily
