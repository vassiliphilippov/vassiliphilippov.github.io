rotary

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəˌri / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by rotation; turning around a central point or axis.

Examples

He went down to Mills and Bradley's Hardware Store and bought a full set of carpenter's tools, including a rotary power saw and several other pieces of power machinery that Mr. Mills said were essential for babbiting and doweling, whatever they were.

Synset Examples

rotary dial

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotating - moving or able to move in a circular motion around an axis
  • Antonym: stationary - not moving or not intended to be moved

Etymology

From Latin 'rotarius' (of or belonging to a wheel), from 'rota' (wheel).
