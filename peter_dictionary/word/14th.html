14th

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌtiːnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the thirteenth in order; being number fourteen in a series.

Examples

It was the first time an English Primate has done this since the 14th century.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 13th - Coming next after the twelfth in position; thirteenth
  • Degree Higher: 15th - Coming next after the fourteenth in position; fifteenth

Etymology

Formed from 'fourteen' (from Old English 'feowertēne') and the ordinal suffix '-th'.
