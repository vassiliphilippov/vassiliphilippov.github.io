ferocity

1. NOUN

/ fəˈrɑsəti / (AmE), / fəˈrɒsəti / (BrE)
The state or quality of being ferocious; intense, fierce, or violent aggression.

Examples

With incredible ferocity, he brought his fists together and struck.

Synset Examples

the storm's violence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fierceness - the quality of being fierce or intense
  • Antonym: gentleness - the quality of being gentle or mild

Etymology

From Latin ferocitas, from ferox (fierce, wild).
