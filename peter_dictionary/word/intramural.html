intramural

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntrəˈmjʊrəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈmjʊərəɫ / (BrE)
Occurring or undertaken within a particular institution, especially a school or college.

Examples

The year after that, they rescheduled the start of the intramural season to coincide with tax season.

Synset Examples

most of the students participated actively in the college's intramural sports program

Related Terms

  • Antonym: extramural - happening or situated outside the walls or boundaries of an institution

Etymology

From Latin intra (within) + murus (wall), originally meaning 'within the walls'.
