interdepartmental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərdɪˌpɑrtməntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntədɪˌpɑːtməntəl / (BrE)
Relating to or occurring between two or more departments within an organization.

Examples

We can no longer rely on interdepartmental machinery "somewhere upstairs "to resolve differences between this and other departments.

Synset Examples

interdepartmental competition

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intradepartmental - Within a single department

Etymology

From inter- (between) + departmental (relating to a department).
