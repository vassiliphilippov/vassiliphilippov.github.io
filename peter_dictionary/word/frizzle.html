frizzle

1. VERB

/ ˈfrɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzəl / (BrE)
to fry (food) until it curls up and becomes crisp

Examples

His long nose wiggled at the smells of frizzling bacon and heating java, but the fire was low, and he wanted to waste no time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fry - to cook (food) in hot fat or oil, typically in a shallow pan
