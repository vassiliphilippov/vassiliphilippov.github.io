crushing

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a blow, defeat, etc.) causing overwhelming physical or emotional damage; devastating.

Examples

The great, crushing ice masses coming into view made him sound like the voice of pure reason.
It was a spleen crushing day.

Synset Examples

a crushing blow
a crushing rejection
bone-crushing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devastating - causing severe shock, distress, or damage
  • Antonym: mild - not severe or strong; gentle or moderate
