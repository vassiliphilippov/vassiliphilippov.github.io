billiard

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪljɚd / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəd / (BrE)
Relating to or used in the game of billiards.

Examples

[BILLIARD BALLS CLATTERING]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: billiards - the cue sport known as billiards

Etymology

From French *billiard* (the game), likely derived from *bille* (ball).
