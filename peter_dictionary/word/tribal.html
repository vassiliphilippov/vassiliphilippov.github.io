tribal

1. ADJ

/ ˈtraɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a tribe or tribes.

Examples

Flanked by marble urns and alabaster lamps, they seemed to be posing for a tribal portrait.
Just figures out of a tribal folklore.

Synset Examples

tribal customs

Etymology

From tribe + -al; 'tribe' from Latin tribus (a division of the Roman people).
