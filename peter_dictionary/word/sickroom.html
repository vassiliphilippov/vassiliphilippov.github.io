sickroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪkˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈsɪk.ruːm / (BrE)
A room in a house or facility set aside for the care of a sick person.

Examples

He ran for the sick room, found his pistol was broken, and threw it away.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ward - a large room in a hospital for several patients

Etymology

From 'sick' + 'room'; attested since the 18th century.
