scarecrowish

1. ADJ

/ ˈskɛrˌkroʊɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskeəˌkrəʊɪʃ / (BrE)
Resembling a scarecrow, especially in being thin and ragged in appearance.

Examples

Deputy Marshal Luke Harper still stood guard on the veranda, a forlorn, scarecrowish figure in the murky dark.

Synset Examples

a forlorn scarecrowish figure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarecrow - an object made to resemble a person and scare birds from crops

Etymology

From 'scarecrow' + '-ish', forming an adjective meaning 'like a scarecrow'.
