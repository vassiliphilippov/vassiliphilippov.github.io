symmetrical

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl / (BrE)
having equal or corresponding parts mirroring each other in size, shape, and position around a central line or point

Examples

It was muscular but it was n't symmetrical.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: asymmetrical - not having equal or corresponding parts; lacking symmetry
  • Synonym: symmetry - the quality of being made up of exactly similar parts facing each other or around an axis

Etymology

From Greek 'symmetria', meaning 'with measure' or 'proportion'.
