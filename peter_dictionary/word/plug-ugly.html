plug-ugly

1. NOUN

/ ˈplʌɡˌʌɡli / (AmE), / ˈplʌɡˌʌɡli / (BrE)
A violent or brutal person, especially a bully or hired thug.

Examples

Now Pat may have been a lecher and a plug-ugly, but he was a good churchgoing Catholic and he loved his little sister.
Landesco thought him "just a superior sort of plugugly "but he was, in fact, with his aggression and hostility, and nerveless indifference to risking or administering pain, a casebook psychopath.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thug - a violent criminal or bully
  • Synonym: bully - a person who harms or intimidates those who are weaker

Etymology

Originally U.S. slang from 19th-century American English; 'plug' may refer to striking, and 'ugly' to appearance or behavior.
