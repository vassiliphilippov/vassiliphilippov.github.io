place-kicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪs ˌkɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪs ˌkɪkə / (BrE)
(in American football) a player whose role is to kick the ball from a stationary (placed) position, especially for field goals and extra points.

Examples

But ask coach Darrell Royal what position he plays and you 'll get the quick response, "place kicker ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: punter - a player who punts the ball in football
  • Synonym: kicker - a player who kicks the ball, especially for field goals or extra points

Etymology

From 'place kick' (a kick from a stationary ball) + 'kicker'.
