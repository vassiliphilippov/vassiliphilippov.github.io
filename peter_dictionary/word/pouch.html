pouch

1. NOUN

/ paʊtʃ / (AmE), / paʊtʃ / (BrE)
A small or medium-sized bag or container used for holding or carrying things.

Examples

Watson had presented his pouch and been led to the bombproof.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bag - a flexible container used for carrying things
  • Synonym: sack - a large bag made of strong material

Etymology

From Middle English pouche, from Old French poche (bag, pocket).
