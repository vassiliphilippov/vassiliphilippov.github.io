1. ADJ

/ wɝs / (AmE), / wɜːs / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: bad - of poor quality or low standard
  • Degree Higher: worst - most bad; of the lowest quality or most undesirable
  • Antonym: better - of higher quality or more desirable

Etymology

Comparative of 'bad', ultimately from Old English 'wiersa', 'wyrsa', comparative of 'yfel' (bad, evil).
