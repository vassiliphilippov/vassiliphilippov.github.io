bleary

1. ADJ

/ ˈblɪri / (AmE), / ˈblɪəri / (BrE)
(of eyes or vision) looking dull, unfocused, or filmy, typically due to tiredness or fatigue.

Examples

My eyes were so bleary I could barely see him but there he was, a little smooth olivefaced guy in a new spring overcoat and a taffy colored fedora.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clear - (of eyes or vision) sharp, not affected by tiredness or irritation
