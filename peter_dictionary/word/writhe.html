writhe

1. VERB

/ raɪð / (AmE), / raɪð / (BrE)
to twist or squirm with movements that suggest discomfort, pain, or struggle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contort - to twist or bend out of normal shape

Etymology

Old English *wrīthan* (to twist), from Proto-Germanic *wrīthaną* (to turn, twist).
