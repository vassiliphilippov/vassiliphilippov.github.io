ruthlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːθləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈruːθləsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of having no pity or compassion for others; a lack of mercy or empathy.

Examples

Maude had the cool ruthlessness to do whatever she made up her mind to do.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mercilessness - lack of mercy
  • Antonym: compassion - sympathetic pity and concern for others
  • Antonym: kindness - the quality of being kind

Etymology

From 'ruthless', itself from Middle English 'ruth' (pity, compassion) plus '-less'.
