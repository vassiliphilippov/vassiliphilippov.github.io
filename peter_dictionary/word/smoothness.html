smoothness

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmuð.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsmuːð.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being smooth, especially referring to a surface that is free from roughness or irregularities and is pleasant to the touch.

Examples

Even here there is room for some variation, for metal surfaces vary in smoothness, absorptive capacity, and chemical reactivity.

Etymology

Formed from 'smooth' + '-ness', with 'smooth' from Old English 'smōth'.
