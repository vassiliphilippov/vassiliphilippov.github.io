nasty

1. ADJ

/ ˈnæsti / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsti / (BrE)
Unpleasant, offensive, or malicious in nature, behavior, or appearance.

Examples

She gave the nastiest laugh I ever heard.
"What a nasty fall was there "!

Synset Examples

in a nasty mood
a nasty accident
a nasty shock
a nasty smell
a nasty trick to pull
Will he say nasty things at my funeral?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unpleasant - not enjoyable or agreeable
  • Synonym: disgusting - arousing revulsion or strong dislike
  • Antonym: pleasant - giving a sense of happy satisfaction or enjoyment

Etymology

Probably from Middle English *nasti*, of uncertain origin.
