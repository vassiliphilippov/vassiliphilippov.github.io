activated

1. ADJ

/ ˈæktəˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈaktɪˌveɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of sewage) treated with aeration and bacteria to aid decomposition, especially as in 'activated sludge'.

Examples

It seems that the aerated lagoon was a very heavily loaded oxidation pond or a lightly loaded activated sludge system.
It was found that the aerated lagoon was an activated sludge system rather than an oxidation pond.
The bacteria formed typical activated sludge floc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activated sludge - sewage sludge treated with aeration and bacteria to aid decomposition

Etymology

From the verb 'activate', from Latin 'activus' (active), here referring to process enhancement by biological activity.
