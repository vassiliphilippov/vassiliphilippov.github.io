den

1. NOUN

/ dɛn / (AmE), / dɛn / (BrE)
A room in a house that is comfortable, quiet, and used for study, relaxation, or informal activities.

Examples

He'’s in your den.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: study - a room used for reading, writing, or academic work
  • Synonym: living room - a room in a house for general and informal everyday use

Etymology

Originally from Old English 'denn' (animal lair, habitation).
