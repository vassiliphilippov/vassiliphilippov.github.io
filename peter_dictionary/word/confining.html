confining

1. ADJ

/ kənˈfaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈfaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
Restricting someone's freedom or the scope of their actions.

Examples

Farming is confining.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restrictive - serving to limit or control
  • Antonym: liberating - freeing from restriction

Etymology

From the verb 'confine', from Middle French confiner, from Latin confinare (‘to border, limit’).
