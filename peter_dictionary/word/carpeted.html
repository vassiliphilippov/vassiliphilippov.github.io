carpeted

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːrpɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a floor or surface) covered with carpet or a layer resembling carpet.

Examples

They were carpeted, but made for pumps and congress gaiters, not the great clodhoppers he wore.
She came to the ballroom and stood on the two carpeted steps that led down to it.

Synset Examples

the carpeted hallway
a flower-carpeted hillside

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uncarpeted - not covered with carpet
  • Synonym: carpet - a floor covering made from thick woven fabric

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'carpet' + '-ed', indicating covered with carpet.
