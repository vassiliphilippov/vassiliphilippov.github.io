topnotch

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɑpˌnɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɒpˌnɒtʃ / (BrE)
Of the highest quality; excellent.

Examples

Does your management climate and your management compensation plan attract and keep top-notch marketing people?

Synset Examples

an ace reporter
a crack shot
a first-rate golfer
a super party
played top-notch tennis
an athlete in tiptop condition
she is absolutely tops

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first-rate - of the highest quality; excellent
  • Synonym: superb - excellent; of very high quality
  • Antonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good

Etymology

Probably from 'top' + 'notch', meaning the highest notch or point.
