ugly

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌɡli / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli / (BrE)
Unpleasant or offensive to look at; unattractive to the senses.

Examples

Cause of all this commotion: squat, pug-nosed, balding, hopelessly ugly Jean-Pierre Bravado, a Bogartian figure, who plays a sadistic, amoral, philosophic Tasti-Freeze salesman in old New-Waver Fredrico de Mille Rossilini's endlessly provocative film, A Sour Sponge.
He 'd been in an angry mood: Conchita had thought his face almost ugly with the anger in him.
He'’s not ugly...

Synset Examples

an ugly face
ugly furniture

Related Terms

  • Antonym: attractive - pleasing to the senses or mind
  • Antonym: beautiful - very pleasing to the senses or mind

Etymology

From Middle English *ugly*, from Old Norse *uggligr* (dreadful), from *ugga* (to fear).
