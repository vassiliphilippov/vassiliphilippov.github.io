editorially

1. ADV

/ ˌɛdɪˈtɔriəli / (AmE), / ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəli / (BrE)
In the manner of an editorial; relating to or expressing the opinion of an editor or editorial staff.

Examples

The Manchester Journal commented editorially on the surprising amount of local telegraphic business.

Synset Examples

the paper commented editorially on the scandal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editorial - a newspaper or magazine article expressing the opinion of the editor or publisher

Etymology

From 'editorial' + '-ly'. 'Editorial' from 'editor' and '-ial', denoting relation to an editor.
