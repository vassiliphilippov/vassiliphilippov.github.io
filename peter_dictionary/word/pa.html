PA

1. NOUN

/ piː eɪ / (AmE), / piː eɪ / (BrE)
An electronic system for amplifying and distributing sound, especially speech or music, to a public audience in venues such as schools, stadiums, or public events.

Examples

[SHOW MUSIC PLAYING] [MAN ANNOUNCING IN SPANISH OVER PA] [SINGING IN SPANISH]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public address system - an electronic sound amplification system for public announcements

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'public address (system)'.
