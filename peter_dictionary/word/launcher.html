launcher

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɔntʃər / (AmE), / ˈlɔːntʃə / (BrE)
a device designed to launch rockets or missiles

Examples

The girls, very fetching in their uniforms, are shown firing rockets from a launcher mounted on a dump truck; they are also trained with carbines, automatic weapons, pistols, rifles and other such ladies' accessories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: missile launcher - device specifically for launching missiles

Etymology

From 'launch' + '-er', denoting something that launches.
