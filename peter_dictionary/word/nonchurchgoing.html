nonchurchgoing

1. ADJ

/ nɑnˈtʃɜrtʃˌɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈtʃɜːtʃˌɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) not attending church or not participating in church services.

Examples

It should be admitted, too, that there is a good percentage of lapsed or nonchurchgoing Catholics (one paper writes 50 per cent).

Synset Examples

a lapsed Catholic

Related Terms

  • Antonym: churchgoing - regularly attending church services

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (not) and 'churchgoing' (attending church services).
