Naples

1. PROPN

/ ˈneɪpəlz / (AmE), / ˈneɪpəlz / (BrE)
A large port city and tourist center in southwestern Italy, the capital of the Campania region.

Examples

Finally, a letter from Naples!

Etymology

From Italian 'Napoli', from Latin 'Neapolis' (new city), from Greek 'Nea Polis'.
