focally

1. ADV

/ ˈfoʊkəli / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkəli / (BrE)
In a focal or localized manner; with reference to a specific area.

Examples

The submucosa was focally infiltrated with neutrophils.

Synset Examples

the submucosa was focally infiltrated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: locally - in a particular, limited area

Etymology

From focal (from Latin focus, meaning 'hearth, fireplace') + -ly.
