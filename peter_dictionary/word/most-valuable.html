most-valuable

1. ADJ

/ ˌmoʊst ˈvæljuəbəl / (AmE), / ˌməʊst ˈvæljuəbl / (BrE)
(in sports) designating a player judged to be the most important or outstanding in a particular game, season, or event.

Examples

The trio who shared the most-valuable honors were introduced by Bob Broeg, sports editor of the Post-Dispatch.

Synset Examples

the most-valuable player award

Related Terms

  • Synonym: most valuable player - player recognized as the best or most important on a team or in a season
