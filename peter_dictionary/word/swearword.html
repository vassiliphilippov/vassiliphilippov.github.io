swearword

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛrˌwɝd / (AmE), / ˈswɛəˌwɜːd / (BrE)
A word or phrase that is considered socially offensive, rude, or obscene, typically used to express strong emotion such as anger or surprise.

Examples

That's a bad word to use.

Synset Examples

expletives were deleted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curse - an offensive word or phrase used to express anger or annoyance
  • Synonym: expletive - an oath or swear word; an exclamation
