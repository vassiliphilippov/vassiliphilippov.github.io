twirler

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɜrlər / (AmE), / ˈtwɜːlə / (BrE)
a person, especially in a marching band, who spins and manipulates a baton as a performance

Examples

Carrying it to the living room, she imagined the picture she made: tall and roundly slim, a bit sophisticated in her yellow sheath, with a graceful swingy walk that she had learned as a twirler with the school band.
She was such a well-rounded teenager, having been a twirler, Future Farmers sweetheart, and secretary of Future Homemakers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baton twirler - person who performs baton twirling
  • Synonym: majorette - female leader of a marching band who twirls a baton

Etymology

From 'twirl' + '-er', indicating one who performs the action.
