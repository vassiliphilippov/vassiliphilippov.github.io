carabao

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːrəˈbaʊ / (AmE), / ˌkærəˈbaʊ / (BrE)
A domesticated water buffalo native to the Philippines, used especially as a draft animal.

Examples

It's for carabao not airplanes ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water buffalo - Large domesticated bovine native to Asia, includes various subspecies

Etymology

From Spanish 'carabao', from Tagalog 'kalabáw'.
