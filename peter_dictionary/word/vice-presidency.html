vice-presidency

1. NOUN

/ ˌvaɪs ˈprɛzəˌdɛnsi / (AmE), / ˌvaɪs ˈprɛzɪdənsi / (BrE)
The office, position, or term of a vice president.

Examples

MICHAEL: I thought you were going to tell me that you were moving your family to Vegas and that you'’d been offered the, uh, Vice Presidency of the Houstan hotels there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presidency - the office and function of a president

Etymology

Formed by combining "vice-" (from Latin "vice", meaning "in place of") and "presidency".
