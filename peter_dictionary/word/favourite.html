favourite

1. ADJ

/ ˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
Preferred above all others; treated with special preference or partiality.

Examples

It turns out Andy's favorite hobby was toting his wall out into the exercise yard.

Synset Examples

the favored child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite - preferred above all others (American spelling)
  • Antonym: least favorite - least preferred or disfavored

Etymology

From Middle English 'favourite', from Old French 'favorit', from Latin 'favorem' (favor).
