publication

1. NOUN

/ ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a single copy of a book, magazine, newspaper, or other printed work offered for sale or distribution

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periodical - a magazine or newspaper published at regular intervals
  • Synonym: issue - a single edition or printing of a periodical

Etymology

From Latin publicatio (making public), from publicare (to make public).
