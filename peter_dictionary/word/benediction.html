benediction

1. NOUN

/ ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
a prayer asking for divine blessing or protection, especially at the end of a religious service

Examples

They hope, of course, to reclaim the non-Catholic population to the Catholic faith, and at every Sunday Benediction they recite by heart the "Prayer for England ":

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blessing - a prayer asking for God's favor or protection

Etymology

From Latin 'benedictio' (blessing), from 'benedicere' (to speak well of, bless).
