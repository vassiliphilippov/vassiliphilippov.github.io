big-chested

1. ADJ

/ bɪɡ ˈtʃɛstɪd / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈtʃɛstɪd / (BrE)
Having a large or well-developed chest.

Examples

He was big chested, big-shouldered and heavy-armed.

Synset Examples

he was big-chested, big-shouldered and heavy-armed

Related Terms
