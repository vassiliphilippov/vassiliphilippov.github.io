sunny

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌni / (AmE), / ˈsʌni / (BrE)
(of a person, manner, or disposition) cheerful, bright, and pleasant; promoting a feeling of happiness.

Examples

This happy, always smiling lad with the sunny disposition is our new Junior Mr. Canada - Henri de Courcy.
He had what was described by a psychologist as a "sunny brutality ".

Synset Examples

a cheery hello
a gay sunny room
a sunny smile

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • Antonym: gloomy - having a sullen or depressed mood
