shirtsleeves

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɝtˌslivz / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌsliːvz / (BrE)
Wearing only a shirt, without a jacket or outerwear; often used in the phrase 'in one's shirtsleeves.'

Examples

I did book jackets and covers for paperback reprints: naked girls huddling in corners of dingy furnished rooms while at the doorway, daring the cops to take him, is the guy in shirt sleeves clutching a revolver.

Synset Examples

in your shirtsleeves
in hot weather they dined in their shirtsleeves
