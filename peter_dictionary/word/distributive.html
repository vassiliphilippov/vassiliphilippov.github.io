distributive

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈstrɪbjətɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪv / (BrE)
relating to the act of distributing, allotting, or dispersing something.

Examples

The "distributive education "program operates in a similar way, with arrangements between the school and employers in merchandising fields.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: collective - relating to a group treated as a whole rather than separately

Etymology

From Latin distributivus, from distribuere (to divide, distribute).
