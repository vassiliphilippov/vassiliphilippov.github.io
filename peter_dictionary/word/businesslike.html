businesslike

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɪznəsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having a methodical, efficient, and practical manner, especially suitable for work or business.

Examples

Wilson stressed the fact that clear as this was, they must have a better church, a more business-like conduct of the church organization, and an effort to get this religious center out of its rut of wild worship into a modern church organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: efficient - performing tasks in a well-organized and productive way
  • Synonym: methodical - done in an orderly or systematic way

Etymology

Formed from 'business' + 'like', meaning having the qualities of business.
