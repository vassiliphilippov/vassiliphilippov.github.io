courteous

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɝːtiəs / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtiəs / (BrE)
Polite, respectful, and considerate in manner.

Examples

Her little brown face wrinkled up, her brown eyes gleamed, and with her little gestures she said all the courteous things.

Synset Examples

a nice gesture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polite - showing good manners and respect for others
  • Antonym: rude - offensively impolite or ill-mannered

Etymology

From Old French 'courtois', from court (court) + -eous.
