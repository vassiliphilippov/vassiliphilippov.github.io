tollhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊlˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small building or booth at a tollgate where tolls are collected from travelers.

Examples

Sometimes they went south and rolled past the tollhouse - "Afternoon, Reverend "!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tollbooth - a booth or small building where tolls are collected
  • Synonym: tollgate - a gate or barrier where a toll is collected

Etymology

From 'toll', meaning a fee charged for passage, and 'house', meaning a building.
