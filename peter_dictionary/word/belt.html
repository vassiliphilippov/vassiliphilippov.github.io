/ bɛlt / (AmE), / bɛlt / (BrE)

2. NOUN

/ bɛlt / (AmE), / bɛlt / (BrE)
A strip of material worn around the waist, often to support clothing or for decoration, typically fastened with a buckle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sash - a long strip worn around the waist or over the shoulder, often decorative

Etymology

From Old English 'bælt' and Old High German 'balz', both meaning a band or girdle.
