Icelandic

1. NOUN

/ aɪˈklændɪk / (AmE), / aɪˈklændɪk / (BrE)
the Scandinavian language spoken in Iceland and the country's official language

Examples

Siberia spoke Icelandic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Iceland - an island nation in the North Atlantic Ocean

Etymology

From Iceland + -ic, modeled after similar demonymic language names.
