supine

1. ADJ

/ suˈpaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsuːpaɪn / (BrE)
(of a person) lying on their back, face upward.

Examples

Have the class lie supine with knees apart and bent.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: prone - lying flat with the front of the body facing downward

Etymology

From Latin supinus (bent backward, lying on the back).
