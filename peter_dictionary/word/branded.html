branded

1. ADJ

/ ˈbrændɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrændɪd / (BrE)
(of goods or merchandise) marked with a distinctive trademark, label, or brand name indicating exclusive rights or ownership.

Examples

Average manufacturer frequently has helped build private brand business, delivering largely the same qualities and styles in private brand merchandise as in branded.

Synset Examples

branded merchandise is that bearing a standard brand name

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unbranded - goods not marked with a brand or trademark

Etymology

Formed from 'brand' (Old Norse 'brandr', meaning 'fire, sword') + '-ed', denoting something that has been marked or labeled.
