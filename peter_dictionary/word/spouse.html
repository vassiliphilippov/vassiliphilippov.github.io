spouse

1. NOUN

/ spaʊs / (AmE), / spaʊs / (BrE)
A husband or wife; a person's partner in marriage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partner - a person with whom one shares an intimate relationship, especially marriage or civil union
  • Degree Lower: husband - a married man considered in relation to his spouse
  • Degree Lower: wife - a married woman considered in relation to her spouse

Etymology

From Old French 'espous', from Latin 'sponsus', meaning 'betrothed, promised'.
