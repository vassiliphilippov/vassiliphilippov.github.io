straitlaced

1. ADJ

/ ˈstreɪtˌleɪst / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtˌleɪst / (BrE)
Having very strict moral or social standards; excessively proper or morally rigid.

Examples

This is a serious minded interpretation, but it is never strait-laced.

Synset Examples

my straitlaced Aunt Anna doesn't approve of my miniskirts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: priggish - self-righteously moralistic and superior
  • Antonym: unconventional - not bound by established rules or traditions
  • Antonym: permissive - allowing or characterized by great or excessive freedom of behavior

Etymology

From 'strait' (narrow or tight) + 'laced', referring to tightly laced clothing symbolizing strictness.
