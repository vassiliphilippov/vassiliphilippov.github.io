bestial

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛstʃəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛstɪəl / (BrE)
Resembling a beast; lacking human sensibility or compassion; cruel or brutal.

Examples

And she begins to regard him as savage, bestial and unworthy.

Synset Examples

beastly desires
a bestial nature
brute force
a dull and brutish man
bestial treatment of prisoners

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brutal - savagely violent; cruel; lacking compassion
  • Antonym: humane - showing compassion or benevolence

Etymology

From Latin 'bestialis' (pertaining to a beast), from 'bestia' (beast).
