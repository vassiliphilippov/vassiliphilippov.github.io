classically

1. ADV

/ ˈklæsɪkli / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or imitates the style of ancient Greek and Roman culture.

Examples

The dumbbell variation develops a most classically sculptured outline to the pecs.

Synset Examples

this exercise develops a classically shaped body

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classical - relating to ancient Greek or Roman tradition
