checkout

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛkˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk.aʊt / (BrE)
an act or process of inspecting or verifying, especially as part of a formal procedure

Examples

But his hands and those of Donovan moved automatically adjusting and arranging in the check-out procedure.

Synset Examples

they made a check of their equipment
the pilot ran through the check-out procedure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspection - the act of examining something carefully
  • Synonym: review - a formal evaluation or assessment

Etymology

From 'check' + 'out', originating in the sense of examining or reviewing before leaving or finishing.
