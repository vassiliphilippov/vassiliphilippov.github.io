worldwide

1. ADJ

/ wɝːldˌwaɪd / (AmE), / wɜːldˌwaɪd / (BrE)
Extending or happening throughout the entire world; global.

Examples

In this world-wide conscription of men, minds, and machines, Prokofieff was recalled to his native land.

Synset Examples

worldwide distribution
a worldwide epidemic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: global - relating to the whole world; worldwide

Etymology

Formed from 'world' + 'wide', indicating something as wide or extensive as the world.
