bottomless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑt̬əm.ləs / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.əm.ləs / (BrE)
Extremely deep; appearing to have no bottom or limit.

Examples

In contrast to this Stoic patristic view, Utopia implies that the nature of man is such that to rely on individual conscience to supply the deficiencies of municipal law is to embark on the bottomless sea of human sinfulness in a sieve.

Synset Examples

a bottomless pit
a bottomless lake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deep - extending far down from the top or surface
  • Antonym: shallow - of little depth
