rundown

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈrʌn.daʊn / (BrE)
a summary or account giving the main points or details of something

Examples

The detailed rundown on the Kansas City scoring in the sixth went like this:

Related Terms

  • Synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
  • Synonym: recap - a short version giving the main points
  • Antonym: full account - a detailed or comprehensive report

Etymology

From phrasal verb 'run down,' meaning to review or summarize.
