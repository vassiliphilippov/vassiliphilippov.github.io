illegitimate

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪləˈdʒɪtəmət / (AmE), / ˌɪlɪˈdʒɪtɪmət / (BrE)
Forbidden by law; illegal or not authorized by official rules or regulations.

Examples

This nettled the children into the revelation of exact truth, a sacrifice of their secret superiority over grown people, but a victory in the wide fields of perpetration and illegitimate accomplishment.

Synset Examples

an illegitimate seizure of power
illicit trade
an outlaw strike
unlawful measures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illegal - forbidden by law; not allowed by official rules
  • Antonym: lawful - permitted by law; legal

Etymology

From Latin illegitimus (not lawful), from in- (not) + legitimus (lawful, legitimate).
