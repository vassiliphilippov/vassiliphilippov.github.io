slicker

1. NOUN

/ ˈslɪkər / (AmE), / ˈslɪkə / (BrE)
a long, loose-fitting waterproof coat, usually made from oil-treated fabric, worn to protect against rain

Examples

Those who had slickers donned them.
Brannon had no slicker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raincoat - a waterproof or water-resistant coat worn to protect the body from rain
