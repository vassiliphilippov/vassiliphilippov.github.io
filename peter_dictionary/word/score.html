/ skɔr / (AmE), / skɔː / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: result - the outcome or consequence of an action or event

Etymology

From Old Norse 'skor' (notch, tally, twenty), related to making marks to count; later extended to mean a recorded total or result.
