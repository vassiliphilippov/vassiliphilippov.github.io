dentist

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛn.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.tɪst / (BrE)
A person who is qualified and licensed to treat diseases and care for the teeth and gums.

Examples

The electric toothbrush moves in a vertical direction, the way dentists recommend.
Several dentists and patients with special dental problems have experimented with the device.
The dentist last night, for instance.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: orthodontist - a dentist who specializes in correcting irregularities of the teeth

Etymology

From French *dentiste*, from *dent* (tooth), from Latin *dens, dentis*.
