cream

1. NOUN

/ krim / (AmE), / kriÀêm / (BrE)
The best people or things within a particular group; the elite.

Examples

In his wake came the District Traffic Supervisor and the cream of the telegraphic profession, ten of Boston's best, chosen for their long experience and thorough knowledge of golf.
Such ambiguous exercises compound confusion by making it worse compounded, and they are sometimes expanded until the cream of the jest sours.

Synset Examples

the cream of England's young men were killed in the Great War

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elite - the richest, most powerful, or best group in a society or category
  • Synonym: flower - the best part or example, as in 'the flower of youth'

Etymology

Metaphorical use from the idea that cream rises to the top of milk and is the richest part.
