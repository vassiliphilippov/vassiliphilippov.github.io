laced

1. ADJ

/ leɪst / (AmE), / leɪst / (BrE)
fastened or closed with a lace, especially referring to shoes, garments, or similar items

Examples

He knew at a glance that the biggest sandals belonged to Niobe, the neatest ones to Concetta, and the laced ones to Romeo, Concetta's idiot brother.

Synset Examples

snugly laced shoes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unlaced - not fastened with a lace; having the lace untied or loosened
