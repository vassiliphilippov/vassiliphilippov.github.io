decorative

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛkɚˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈdɛk(ə)rətɪv / (BrE)
Serving an aesthetic rather than a useful purpose; intended to look attractive rather than be functional.

Examples

Unlike many decorative patterns that present a static flat convention, this artist's pictures are full of atmosphere and climate.

Synset Examples

cosmetic fenders on cars
the buildings were utilitarian rather than decorative

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ornamental - serving as a decoration; ornamental, not functional
  • Antonym: utilitarian - practical rather than decorative; focused on use

Etymology

From decorate + -ive, ultimately from Latin 'decorare' (to adorn).
