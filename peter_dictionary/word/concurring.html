concurring

1. ADJ

/ kənˈkɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈkɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of people or statements) in agreement; having the same opinion.

Examples

We are agreed.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: disagreeing - not in agreement; having different opinions
  • Synonym: concordant - being in agreement; consistent

Etymology

From Latin 'concurrere' meaning 'to run together, agree'.
