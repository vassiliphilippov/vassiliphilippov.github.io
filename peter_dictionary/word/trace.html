1. VERB

/ treɪs / (AmE), / treɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: track - to follow the trail or movements of something or someone
  • Synonym: retrace - to go back over a route or path again

Etymology

From Old French *tracer* (to trace, draw), from Vulgar Latin *tractiare* (to drag, pull), from Latin *tractus* (a dragging).
