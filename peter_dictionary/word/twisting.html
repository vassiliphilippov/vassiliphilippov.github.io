twisting

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of distorting or misrepresenting something so it appears to mean something unintended.

Examples

The tines broke off under Jess's twisting, and he swung the handle in an attempt to knock Curt's brains out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distortion - the act of giving a misleading account or impression
  • Synonym: falsification - deliberate alteration of facts or evidence

Etymology

From the verb 'twist' + -ing, originally meaning to turn or wind, figuratively to distort.
