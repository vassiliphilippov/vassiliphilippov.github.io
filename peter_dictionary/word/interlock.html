interlock

1. VERB

/ ˌɪn.tɚˈlɑk / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈlɒk / (BrE)
to join or coordinate parts in such a way that they fit or work together effectively

Examples

We have developed an ingenious method of interlocking these so that you can make the major part of your house in your own workshop, panel by panel, according to plan.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mesh - to fit or work together (of parts or ideas)

Etymology

From inter- (between) + lock (to fasten or secure).
