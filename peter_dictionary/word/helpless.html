helpless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛlpləs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpləs / (BrE)
Unable to take action or protect oneself; lacking power or ability to help oneself or influence events.

Examples

Actually they were helpless to do anything about the nationwide policy.
Now, driving the horse and sulky borrowed from Mynheer Schuyler, he felt as if every bone was topped by burning oil and that every muscle was ready to dissolve into jelly and leave his big body helpless and unable to move.
But, admit or not, Bonner was helpless.
He'’s so sweet, and helpless without you.

Synset Examples

lying ill and helpless
helpless with laughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerless - without ability or influence
  • Antonym: strong - having great power or ability

Etymology

From Middle English 'helpeles', from 'help' + '-less'.

2. ADJ

/ ˈhɛlpləs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpləs / (BrE)
Unable to take action or protect oneself; lacking the power or means to help oneself.

Examples

Science has simply left us helpless and powerless in this important sector of our lives.
We are left helpless to cope with it because we do not dare speak of it as anything real for fear that to do so would imply a commitment to that which has already been discredited and proved false.
Churches can be strengthened through this process of co-optation so long as the environs of the church provide a sufficient pool of people who can fit the pattern of economic integration; once the pool of recruits diminishes, the congregation is helpless - friendly contacts no longer keep it going.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerless - lacking power or ability to act; unable to influence events
  • Antonym: capable - having the ability, fitness, or quality necessary to do or achieve a specified thing

Etymology

From Old English 'help' + '-less' (without).

3. ADJ

/ ˈhɛlpləs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlplɪs / (BrE)
Unable to take care of oneself or act without assistance; lacking the ability or power to manage independently.

Examples

She found she could cope with all kinds of problems for which she was once considered too helpless.

Synset Examples

as helpless as a baby

Related Terms

  • Antonym: self-reliant - able to do things and make decisions without help
  • Synonym: dependent - relying on others for help or support

Etymology

From Middle English helpeles, formed from 'help' + '-less'.
