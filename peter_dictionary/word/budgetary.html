budgetary

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʌdʒɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪtəri / (BrE)
relating to or concerning a budget or financial planning.

Examples

SBA can make complete facilities inventories of all small business concerns in labor surplus areas within budgetary and staff limitations.

Synset Examples

budgetary considerations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fiscal - relating to government revenue, especially taxes

Etymology

From 'budget' + -ary, with 'budget' from Old French 'bougette', meaning 'small bag'.
