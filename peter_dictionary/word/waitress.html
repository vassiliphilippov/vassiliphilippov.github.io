waitress

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪ.trɪs / (AmE), / ˈweɪ.trɪs / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to serve food and drinks to customers at their tables in a restaurant, café, etc.

Examples

They are relentlessly rebuffed on all sides by a waitress, the police, and an intruding government tutor.
He was banging cocktail waitresses!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waiter - a person who serves food and drinks, especially a man

Etymology

Formed from 'waiter' + '-ess', a suffix indicating female gender.
