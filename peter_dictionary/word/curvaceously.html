curvaceously

1. ADV

/ kɚˈveɪʃəsli / (AmE), / kɜːˈveɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is attractively curved, especially referring to the shape or form of a woman.

Examples

Soaring to stardom along with him, Margo Felicity Brighetti, a luscious and curvaceously beguiling Italian starlet, turns in a creditable performance as an airplane mechanic.

Synset Examples

his date was curvaceously beguiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curvaceous - having attractively curved shape, especially of a woman

Etymology

From 'curvaceous' + '-ly', with 'curvaceous' formed from 'curve' and the suffix '-aceous'.
