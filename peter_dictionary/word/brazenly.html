brazenly

1. ADV

/ ˈbreɪzənli / (AmE), / ˈbreɪzənli / (BrE)
In a bold and shameless manner; without embarrassment or shame.

Examples

"We all know that Jake Camaret and the woman are brazenly living together.

Synset Examples

he spoke brazenly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boldly - in a confident and courageous way
  • Synonym: shamelessly - without shame or embarrassment
  • Antonym: discreetly - in a careful and unobtrusive way
  • Antonym: modestly - in a modest or reserved way

Etymology

From 'brazen' (bold, shameless) + -ly (adverbial suffix). 'Brazen' from Old English 'bræsen' (of brass).
