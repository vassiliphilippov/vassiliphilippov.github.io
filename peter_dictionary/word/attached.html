attached

1. ADJ

/ əˈtætʃt / (AmE), / əˈtætʃt / (BrE)
closely connected or officially joined to a larger organization, group, or institution.

Examples

There will be no mitigation of these offences until all art schools, whether independent or attached to universities have separate accreditation - as do medical schools - by an art accreditation group such as the "National Association of Schools of Art ".

Synset Examples

affiliated clubs
all art schools whether independent or attached to universities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affiliated - closely associated with or officially attached to an organization
  • Antonym: independent - not connected or affiliated with a larger organization
