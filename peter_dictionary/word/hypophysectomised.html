hypophysectomised

1. ADJ

/ haɪˌpɑfəˈsɛktəˌmaɪzd / (AmE), / haɪˌpɒfɪˈsɛktəˌmaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an animal or person) having had the pituitary gland removed by surgery.

Examples

This connection was clarified by Smith and Smith (1922), who showed that saline extracts of fresh bovine pituitary glands could re-activate the atrophied thyroids of hypophysectomised tadpoles.

Synset Examples

hypophysectomized tadpoles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hypophysectomized - having the pituitary gland removed by surgery; American spelling

Etymology

From 'hypophysectomy' (removal of the pituitary gland) + '-ised', the British spelling of '-ized'.
