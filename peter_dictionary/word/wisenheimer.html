wisenheimer

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪzənˌhaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪzənˌhaɪmə / (BrE)
A person who makes smart-aleck, insolent, or sarcastic remarks; a wise guy.

Examples

In that case, listen to what befell another wisenheimer who tangled with our joss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wise guy - A person who is clever in an annoying or insolent way

Etymology

From German 'weisenheimer', combining 'weisen' (to show) with a playful or mock Germanic ending, possibly modeled after names like 'Oppenheimer'.
