croak

1. VERB

/ kroʊk / (AmE), / krəʊk / (BrE)
(informal) To die.

Examples

All those dumb 8 - Balls croaked.

Synset Examples

She died from cancer
The children perished in the fire
The patient went peacefully
The old guy kicked the bucket at the age of 102

Related Terms

  • Synonym: die - to stop living, to cease to exist (person or animal)
  • Synonym: perish - to die, especially in a sudden or violent way
