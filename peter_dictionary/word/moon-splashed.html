moon-splashed

1. ADJ

/ ˈmun splæʃt / (AmE), / ˈmuːn splæʃt / (BrE)
illuminated or marked with patches of moonlight; covered patchily with moonlight

Examples

Below the moon splashed world rolled away to insurmountable white peaks; above him the deep blue sky glittered with stars.

Synset Examples

the moon-splashed world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonlit - illuminated by moonlight
