questioner

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nə / (BrE)
A person who asks questions, especially in a formal or systematic manner.

Examples

This sentence would have most of the characteristics of a question, but it has some of the characteristics of a statement because the questioner has conveyed the fact that he has no faith in his own timepiece or the one attached to his car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inquirer - person who seeks information or asks questions

Etymology

From 'question' + '-er', where 'question' comes from Latin 'quaestionem' (inquiry).
