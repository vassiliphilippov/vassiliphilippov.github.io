idiotically

1. ADV

/ ˌɪdiˈɑtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪdiˈɒtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is very foolish or stupid.

Examples

Beaming idiotically, he pooched out his lips and attempted to kiss her.

Synset Examples

what arouses the indignation of the honest satirist is not the fact that people in positions of power or influence behave idiotically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foolishly - in a foolish manner
  • Antonym: sensibly - in a sensible manner; reasonably

Etymology

From 'idiotic' + '-ally'. 'Idiotic' from French 'idiotique', ultimately from Greek 'idiōtikos'.
