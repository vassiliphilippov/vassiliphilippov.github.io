hereabouts

1. ADV

/ ˌhɪrəˈbaʊts / (AmE), / ˌhɪərəˈbaʊts / (BrE)
in this area; in these parts; in or near this place

Examples

It has all been done in superb style, and the result is a show which deserves the support of every person hereabouts who enjoys good musical theater.
You know all the girls around here?

Synset Examples

the people are friendly hereabouts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: here - in, at, or to this place or position
  • Synonym: around here - in the area near this place; nearby

Etymology

Formed from 'here' + 'abouts,' on the model of 'thereabouts.'
