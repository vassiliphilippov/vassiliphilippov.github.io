Jungian

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒʌŋiən / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋiən / (BrE)
Relating to the ideas and theories of Carl Jung, a Swiss psychiatrist and psychoanalyst.

Examples

As you have doubtless guessed already, the plot is plastered with Freudian, Jungian, and Meinckian theory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Freudian - relating to the theories of Sigmund Freud

Etymology

From the name of Carl Jung (1875–1961), Swiss psychiatrist, with the suffix -ian.
