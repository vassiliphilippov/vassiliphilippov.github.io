delimit

1. VERB

/ dɪˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
to set or mark the limits or boundaries of something; to define or determine the extent of.

Examples

If, however, it would help to intensify your anguish, I can delimit the powers of a few of the divinities you 've affronted and describe the punishment they meted out in one analogous instance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: define - to state or describe exactly the nature, scope, or meaning of something
  • Synonym: determine - to establish exactly, typically as a result of research or calculation

Etymology

From Latin 'delimitare' (to mark out the boundaries), from 'de-' (completely) + 'limes' (boundary, limit).
