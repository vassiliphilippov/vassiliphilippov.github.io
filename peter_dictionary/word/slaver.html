slaver

1. VERB

/ ˈslævər / (AmE), / ˈslɑːvə / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) to let saliva run from the mouth; to drool.

Examples

He gloated, and his lips slavered.

Synset Examples

The baby drooled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drool - let saliva run from the mouth
  • Synonym: dribble - allow saliva to flow down from the mouth

Etymology

Probably from Old Norse slafra (to slaver, to slobber).
