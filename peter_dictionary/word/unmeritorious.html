unmeritorious

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnmɛrɪˈtɔriəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnmɛrɪˈtɔːriəs / (BrE)
Lacking merit or value; undeserving of praise or consideration.

Examples

"These actions should serve to protect in fact and in effect the court's wards from undue costs and its appointed and elected servants from unmeritorious criticisms ", the jury said.

Synset Examples

protect...from unmeritorious criticism

Related Terms

  • Antonym: meritorious - deserving reward or praise

Etymology

From Latin prefix 'un-' (not) + 'meritorious' (deserving reward).
