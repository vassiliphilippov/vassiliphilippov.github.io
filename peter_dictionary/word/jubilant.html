jubilant

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒuːbɪlənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːbɪlənt / (BrE)
Feeling or expressing great joy and triumph.

Examples

And the school newspaper sells space to jubilant fraternities, sororities, and houses (in the House Plan Association) that have good news to impart.

Synset Examples

rejoicing crowds filled the streets on VJ Day
a triumphal success
a triumphant shout

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joyful - feeling, expressing, or causing great pleasure and happiness
  • Synonym: elated - ecstatically happy
  • Antonym: sad - feeling sorrow or unhappiness

Etymology

From Latin jubilant-, present participle of jubilare (‘to shout for joy’).
