thumbnail

1. NOUN

/ ˈθʌmˌneɪl / (AmE), / ˈθʌmneɪl / (BrE)
the nail on the thumb

Examples

The colonel crouched tensely on one of the folding chairs, methodically tearing at his thumbnail.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: fingernail - the nail on a finger
  • Synonym: toenail - the nail on a toe

Etymology

Compound of 'thumb' and 'nail'.
