coarsened

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɔrsənd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːsənd / (BrE)
made rougher, less refined, or cruder, often as a result of lack of skill or deterioration.

Examples

It got the kind of scrambled, coarsened performance that can happen to best of orchestras when the man with the baton lacks technique and style.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: refined - improved or made more sophisticated

Etymology

From 'coarse' + '-en' (verb-forming suffix) + '-ed' (past participle ending).
