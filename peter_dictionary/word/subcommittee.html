subcommittee

1. NOUN

/ ˌsʌbkəˈmɪti / (AmE), / ˌsʌbkəˈmɪti / (BrE)
A smaller group formed from within a committee to deal with a specific matter or carry out particular functions.

Examples

Under committee rules, it went automatically to a subcommittee for one week.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function by a larger group or body

Etymology

Formed from 'sub-' (meaning under or lesser) and 'committee', indicating a subset of a committee.
