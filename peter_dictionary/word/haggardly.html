haggardly

1. ADV

/ ˈhæɡərdli / (AmE), / ˈhæɡədli / (BrE)
In a haggard manner; looking tired, worn, or exhausted.

Examples

Beaten with fear and sound and wet and chill, they crawled to the hurricane deck and looked out haggardly at a world of water that reached clear to the surrounding hills.

Synset Examples

she looked haggardly out of her tent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haggard - looking exhausted and unwell; worn out

Etymology

From 'haggard' + '-ly' (adverb suffix).
