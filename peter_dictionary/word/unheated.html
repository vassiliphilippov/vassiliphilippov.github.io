unheated

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhiːtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhiːtɪd / (BrE)
Not having been heated or warmed.

Examples

(An unheated greenhouse would have been better, if I had had one.)

Synset Examples

an unheated room
unwarmed rolls

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heated - made hot or warm

Etymology

Formed by the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not', combined with 'heated'.
