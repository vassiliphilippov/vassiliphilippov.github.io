cockpit

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑkˌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.pɪt / (BrE)
the compartment in an aircraft where the pilot sits and controls the plane

Examples

Greg climbed into the cockpit feeling as if he had never been in one before.
He shivered in the warm cockpit.

Etymology

Originally a term for the pit where cockfights were held, later applied to boat and aircraft compartments.
