democratic

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɛməˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɛməˈkrætɪk / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: egalitarian - believing in or based on the principle that all people are equal

Etymology

From French démocratique, from Greek dēmokratikos, from dēmos (people) + -kratos (rule).
