defection

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of abandoning or deserting a person, cause, or organization to which one owes allegiance or responsibility

Examples

How right he was; how clearly he saw the cultural defection of experimentation as an escape for those who dare not or prefer not to face the discipline of modern traditionalism.

Synset Examples

his abandonment of his wife and children left them penniless

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desertion - the act of abandoning someone or something

Etymology

From Latin defectio, from deficere (to desert, fail).
