electric

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk / (BrE)
(of a device or machine) using, operated by, or producing electricity.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin electricus (‘to produce electricity’), from Greek ēlektron (‘amber’), as rubbing amber was found to produce static electricity.
