speedily

1. ADV

/ ˈspiːdəli / (AmE), / ˈspiːdəli / (BrE)
In a rapid or quick manner; with speed.

Examples

Resolved, that we find in this fearful tragedy at Harper's Ferry a reason for more earnest effort to remove the evil of slavery from the whole land as speedily as possible.

Synset Examples

he works quickly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Synonym: rapidly - quickly; at a great rate
  • Antonym: slowly - in a slow manner; not quickly

Etymology

From Middle English *spedily*, from *spede* (speed) + *-ly*.
