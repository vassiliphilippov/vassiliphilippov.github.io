listlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈlɪstləsli / (AmE), / ˈlɪstləsli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking energy, enthusiasm, or interest.

Examples

Still nursing anger I listlessly thumbed a car that was slowly approaching, its pre-war chrome nearly blinding me.

Synset Examples

they shook hands rather listlessly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: energetically - in a manner showing energy or enthusiasm
  • Synonym: listless - lacking energy or enthusiasm (adjective form)

Etymology

From 'listless' + '-ly'. 'Listless' from Middle English 'list' (desire) + '-less'.
