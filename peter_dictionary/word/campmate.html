campmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmpˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæmpˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who shares the same camp with you

Examples

It was Pullen James, the campmate of Simms Purdew.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roommate - a person who shares a room with you
  • Synonym: classmate - a person who is in the same class as you

Etymology

Compound of 'camp' and 'mate.'
