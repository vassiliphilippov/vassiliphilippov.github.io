surrounded

1. ADJ

/ səˈraʊndəd / (AmE), / səˈraʊndɪd / (BrE)
Confined or enclosed on all sides; having something or someone all around.

Examples

Surrounded by crime and violence everywhere, the "hardboiled "private eye can retain his purity only through a life of self-imposed isolation.

Synset Examples

a camp surrounded by enemies
the encircled pioneers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: encircled - enclosed or shut in on all sides
