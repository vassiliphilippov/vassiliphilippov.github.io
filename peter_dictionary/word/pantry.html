pantry

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæntri / (AmE), / ˈpæntri / (BrE)
a small room or cupboard in a house where food, dishes, and utensils are stored

Examples

In their search for what turned out to be the right breakfast china but the wrong table silver, they opened every cupboard door in the kitchen and pantry.

Etymology

From Old French *paneterie*, from *panetier* (bread keeper), from Latin *panis* (bread).
