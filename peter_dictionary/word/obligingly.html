obligingly

1. ADV

/ əˈblaɪdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈblɪdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
In a helpful or willing manner; ready to do a favor or accommodate others.

Examples

Obligingly Gibby lowered his voice.

Synset Examples

obligingly, he lowered his voice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helpfully - in a helpful manner
  • Antonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling or hesitant manner

Etymology

From obliging + -ly, where obliging comes from the verb 'oblige,' from Old French obliger, from Latin obligare ('to bind').
