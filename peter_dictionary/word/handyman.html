handyman

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændiˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhændimæn / (BrE)
A man who is skilled at doing small repairs and various odd jobs, especially around the house.

Examples

No, he did n't know of any handyman carpenter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repairman - a person who repairs machines or equipment
  • Synonym: craftsman - a person skilled in a particular craft

Etymology

From 'handy' (skillful with the hands) + 'man'.
