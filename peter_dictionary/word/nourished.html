nourished

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɝɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈnʌrɪʃt / (BrE)
Having received enough food or nutrients to maintain health and growth.

Examples

The master of the house, his nourished face unrevealing, consented to postpone his departure a few minutes to talk to the detective.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undernourished - not having enough food or nutrients
  • Antonym: malnourished - having poor nutrition due to lack of proper food

Etymology

From the verb 'nourish', itself from Old French 'nourir', from Latin 'nutrire' (to feed, nourish).
