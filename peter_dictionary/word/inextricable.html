inextricable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈɛk.strɪ.kə.bəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈɛk.strɪ.kə.bəl / (BrE)
Impossible to disentangle, separate, or escape from.

Examples

They bring an inextricable component of value within themselves, with attractions and repulsions native to their own quality.

Synset Examples

an inextricable knot
inextricable unity

Related Terms

  • Antonym: extricable - able to be freed or disentangled

Etymology

From Latin inextricabilis, from in- 'not' + extricare 'to disentangle'.
