extremity

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈstrɛm.ə.ti / (AmE), / ɪkˈstrɛm.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a bodily limb or part that projects from the main structure, such as an arm, leg, hand, or foot, especially referring to the outermost parts

Examples

The muscles of the extremities, chest wall, neck, and abdominal wall were soft, pale, and atrophic.

Synset Examples

it is important to keep the extremities warm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limb - an arm, leg, wing, or similar projecting body part

Etymology

From Latin extremitas (the end, border, tip), from extremus (outermost, last).
