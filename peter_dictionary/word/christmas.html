Christmas

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪs.məs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs / (BrE)
The annual Christian festival celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ, observed on December 25.

Examples

"Tell me what to get her for Christmas ".
On Christmas night, they had had a disagreement about it.
Christmas shopping.

Etymology

From Old English 'Crīstesmæsse' (Christ's mass).
