futureless

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuːtʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃələs / (BrE)
having no prospect or hope of a future; lacking potential for continued existence or success.

Examples

You got no future.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hopeless - having no hope; despairing
  • Antonym: promising - showing signs of future success

Etymology

Formed from 'future' + '-less', suffix meaning 'without'.
