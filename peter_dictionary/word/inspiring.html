inspiring

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈspaɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
Making you feel encouraged, uplifted, or filled with positive emotion.

Examples

Inspiring - yes; instructive - maybe; duplicable - no!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: uninspiring - failing to excite or motivate; dull or unexciting

Etymology

From the verb 'inspire', from Latin 'inspirare' (to breathe into, inspire).
