insure

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈʃʊr / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃɔː / (BrE)
to buy insurance for something, so that you receive compensation if it is damaged, lost, or stolen

Examples

Ordinary Carey Williams, armed with a pistol, stood by at the polls to insure order.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ensure - to make certain that something happens

Etymology

From Old French 'enseurer', from 'en-' (make) + 'seur' (sure), from Latin 'securus' (safe).
