oaken

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊkən / (AmE), / ˈəʊkən / (BrE)
Made of or consisting of wood from the oak tree.

Examples

They tracked mud on the oaken floor, on the carpet.

Synset Examples

a solid oak table
the old oaken bucket

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oak - hardwood tree and its wood

Etymology

From Middle English oken, from oak + -en (a suffix forming adjectives meaning 'made of').
