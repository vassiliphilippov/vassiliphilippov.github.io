1. VERB

/ ʃɪp / (AmE), / ʃɪp / (BrE)
to send or transport goods, cargo, or people from one place to another, typically by a commercial carrier such as a ship, truck, train, or air freight

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliver - to take goods, letters, or packages to a person or place
  • Antonym: receive - to take delivery of goods or items; opposite process to shipping

Etymology

From Old English 'scipian', derived from 'scip' (ship), originally meaning to put or take on a ship.
