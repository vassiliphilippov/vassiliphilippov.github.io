clipboard

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪpˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈklɪpˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A small, flat board with a clip at the top for holding papers in place while writing.

Examples

I enter the names on this clipboard here.

Etymology

Formed from 'clip' + 'board', referring to a board equipped with a clip.
