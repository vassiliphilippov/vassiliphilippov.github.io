sociological

1. ADJ

/ ˌsoʊsiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with sociology or the study of society and social behavior

Examples

Just as present technology had to await the explanations of physics, so one might expect that social invention will follow growing sociological understanding.
First, the student trains on his hapless parents the heavy artillery of his newly acquired psychological and sociological insights.

Synset Examples

sociological studies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sociology - the study of society and social behavior

Etymology

Formed from 'sociology' + '-ical'; 'sociology' from French *sociologie*, from Latin *socius* (companion) + Greek *-logia* (study of).
