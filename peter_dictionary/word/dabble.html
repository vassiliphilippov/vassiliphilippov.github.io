dabble

1. VERB

/ ˈdæbəl / (AmE), / ˈdæbəl / (BrE)
to dip a hand or foot briefly into water or another liquid

Examples

I do n't mean a few aesthetes who play about with sensations, like a young prince in a miniature dabbling his hand in a pool.

Etymology

Probably from Dutch *dabbelen* (to paddle in water).
