sprinkling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
A small number of things or people scattered or distributed here and there.

Examples

When the first sprinkling of green appears I remove the board.

Synset Examples

the first scatterings of green
a sprinkling of grey at his temples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scattering - a small number of things spread over an area

Etymology

From Middle English sprenklen (to sprinkle) + -ing.
