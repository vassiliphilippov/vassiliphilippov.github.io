bloke

1. NOUN

/ bloʊk / (AmE), / bləʊk / (BrE)
A man; a fellow (used mainly in British English, usually informally).

Examples

"The world is full of blokes who put their hearts into making the tragic scene.

Synset Examples

that chap is your host
there's a fellow at the door
he's a likable cuss
he's a good bloke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chap - a man or boy (informal, chiefly British)
  • Synonym: fellow - an informal term for a man or boy

Etymology

Origin uncertain; first attested in British slang in the early 19th century.
