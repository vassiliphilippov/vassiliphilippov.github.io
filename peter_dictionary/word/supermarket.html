supermarket

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuːpərˌmɑːrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
A large self-service store selling a wide variety of food and household goods.

Examples

In a course for supermarket operators, a district manager who had been recently appointed to his position after being outstandingly successful as a store manager, found that in supervising other managers he was having a difficult time.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grocery store - a store selling food and other household goods

Etymology

Formed from 'super-' (above, large) + 'market', first used in mid 20th century.
