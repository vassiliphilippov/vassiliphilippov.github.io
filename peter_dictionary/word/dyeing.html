dyeing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdaɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of using dye to change the color of something, usually a fabric or material, permanently.

Examples

There is no dyeing or waxing or gassing needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dye - substance used to add color to materials

Etymology

From dye + -ing, formed from the verb 'dye' meaning to color with dye.
