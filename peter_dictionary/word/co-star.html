co-star

1. VERB

/ ˈkoʊ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ stɑː / (BrE)
to act as a co-star with another performer in a film, play, or other production; to feature as a co-star

Examples

He's starring in that new film.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: star - to play the leading role in a production

Etymology

Formed from 'co-' (together) and 'star' (leading performer).
