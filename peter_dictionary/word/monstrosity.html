monstrosity

1. NOUN

/ mɑnˈstrɑsəti / (AmE), / mɒnˈstrɒsəti / (BrE)
a person or animal that is extremely unusual or deformed, often in a way that is considered abnormal or grotesque

Examples

However, when there's a job to be done, I 'm a monstrosity of grim determination, I like to think.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freak - a person or animal that is markedly unusual or deformed

Etymology

From French ‘monstruosité’, from Late Latin ‘monstrositas’, from Latin ‘monstrum’ (monster).
