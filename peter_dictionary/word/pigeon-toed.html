pigeon-toed

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪdʒɪn toʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪn təʊd / (BrE)
(of a person) having feet that turn inward when walking or standing.

Examples

I think you made a dam good chouise to turn off as nise a feler as Alf dyer and let that orney thefin, drunkard, damed card playing Sun of a bich com to Sea you, the god damed theaf and lop yeard pigen tode helion, he is too orney for hel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: duck-footed - having feet that turn outward

Etymology

Refers to the supposed resemblance of the inward-turning feet to those of a pigeon.
