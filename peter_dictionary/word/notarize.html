notarize

1. VERB

/ ˈnoʊtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtəraɪz / (BrE)
to have a document officially certified or authenticated by a notary public

Examples

One, by Sen. Louis Crump of San Saba, would aid more than 17000 retailers who pay a group of miscellaneous excise taxes by eliminating the requirement that each return be notarized.

Synset Examples

We had to have the signature notarized

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certify - to formally attest or confirm in writing

Etymology

From notary + -ize, meaning to make or certify as by a notary.
