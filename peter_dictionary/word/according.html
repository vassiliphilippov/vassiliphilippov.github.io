according

1. ADJ

/ əˈkɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈkɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
In agreement with or conforming to something (used especially in the phrase 'according to').

Examples

Do as he says.

Synset Examples

according to instructions

Etymology

Present participle of 'accord', from Old French 'acorder', from Latin 'accordare' (to be of one heart).
