woolen

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊlən / (AmE), / ˈwʊlən / (BrE)
A fabric made from the hair of sheep; cloth woven from wool.

Examples

In spite of the fact that the state legislature voted us neutral, John Hunt Morgan is openly flying the Confederate flag over his woolen factory "!

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: worsted - a smooth, fine wool fabric made from long-staple wool

Etymology

From wool + -en, indicating made of wool.
