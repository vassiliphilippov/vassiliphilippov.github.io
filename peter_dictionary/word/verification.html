verification

1. NOUN

/ ˌvɛr.ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɛr.ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
The process of establishing the truth, accuracy, or validity of something through additional evidence or proof.

Examples

With names and addresses listed, verification came next.

Synset Examples

fossils provided further confirmation of the evolutionary theory

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confirmation - the action of confirming something as true or valid
  • Synonym: validation - the action of checking or proving the validity or accuracy of something
  • Antonym: falsification - the action of proving something to be false

Etymology

From Medieval Latin verificatio, from Latin verificare (to make true).
