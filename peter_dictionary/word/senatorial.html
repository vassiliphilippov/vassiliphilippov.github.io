senatorial

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛnəˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛnəˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to a senator or to a senate.

Examples

Just as now anyone may hurl insults at a citizen of Mars, or even of Tikopia, and no senatorial investigation will result.

Synset Examples

senatorial election

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senate - the legislative body or council, especially the upper house of a legislature
  • Synonym: senator - a member of a senate

Etymology

From Latin senātōrius, from senātor (senator).
