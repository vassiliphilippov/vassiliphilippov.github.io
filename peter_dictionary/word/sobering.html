sobering

1. ADJ

/ ˈsoʊbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbərɪŋ / (BrE)
Making you feel more serious or less cheerful, especially by making you think about something unpleasant or worrying.

Examples

During the morning hours, it became clear that the arrest of Spencer was having no sobering effect upon the men of the Somers.

Synset Examples

the news had a sobering effect

Related Terms

  • Synonym: alarming - causing worry or fear; making you more alert or serious

Etymology

From the verb 'sober' (to make serious or less drunk) + -ing.
