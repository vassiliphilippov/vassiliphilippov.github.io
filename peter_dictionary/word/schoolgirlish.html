schoolgirlish

1. ADJ

/ ˈskuːlˌɡɜːrlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌɡɜːlɪʃ / (BrE)
Characteristic of or befitting a schoolgirl; having qualities such as innocence, naivety, or youthful charm often associated with schoolgirls.

Examples

It made her look sweet and schoolgirlish, I was excited to be with her, but I did not know how to express it.

Synset Examples

girlish charm
a dress too schoolgirlish for office wear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: girlish - having qualities appropriate to a girl or young woman

Etymology

Formed from 'schoolgirl' + '-ish', meaning 'like or relating to a schoolgirl'.
