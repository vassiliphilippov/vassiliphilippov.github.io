housework

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊs.wɝk / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs.wɜːk / (BrE)
The regular work of cleaning and maintaining a home, such as cleaning, cooking, and laundry.

Examples

She was the only kind of Negro Laura Andrus would want around: independent, unservile, probably charging double what ordinary maids did for housework - and doubly efficient.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housekeeping - the duties and management of running a household

Etymology

Compound of 'house' and 'work'.
