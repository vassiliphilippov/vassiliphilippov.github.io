well-kept

1. ADJ

/ ˌwɛlˈkɛpt / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈkɛpt / (BrE)
(especially of places or things) kept neat, clean, and in good order; well maintained.

Examples

Look for a farm in a neighborhood of well-kept homes.

Synset Examples

even the barn was shipshape
a trim little sailboat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tidy - neat and arranged in good order
  • Synonym: shipshape - in good order; neat and tidy
  • Antonym: messy - untidy or disordered
