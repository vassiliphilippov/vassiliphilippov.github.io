abridged

1. ADJ

/ əˈbrɪdʒd / (AmE), / əˈbrɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of a text) shortened by condensing or rewriting while retaining essential content.

Examples

Reader's Digest condensed books.

Synset Examples

an abridged version

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unabridged - not shortened; complete (of a text)

Etymology

From abridge + -ed; abridge from Old French abregier, from Latin abbreviare (to shorten).
