haberdashery

1. NOUN

/ ˌhæbərˈdæʃəri / (AmE), / ˌhabəˈdaʃəri / (BrE)
a shop that sells men's clothing and accessories, especially in the U.K.

Examples

Some retailing, of course, always spreads with the population - grocery stores, drugstores, local haberdasheries and dress shops, candy stores and the like.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menswear shop - a store selling men's clothing
  • Antonym: millinery - women's hat shop

Etymology

From Middle English haberdassher, ultimately from Anglo-French haberdasher (of uncertain origin).
