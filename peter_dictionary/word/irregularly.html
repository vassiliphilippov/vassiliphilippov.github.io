irregularly

1. ADV

/ ɪˈrɛɡjələrli / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛɡjʊləli / (BrE)
In a way that does not follow a regular pattern, order, or arrangement.

Examples

The mucosa of the stomach was atrophic and irregularly blackened over a 14 cm. area.
A study of the distribution of WTV antigen within the pseudophloem cells indicates that it is irregularly distributed in the cytoplasm.

Synset Examples

the patient is breathing irregularly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: regularly - in a regular manner

Etymology

From 'irregular' + '-ly', with 'irregular' from Latin 'irregularis' (not according to rule).
