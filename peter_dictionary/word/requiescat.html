requiescat

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkwiˈɛskæt / (AmE), / ˌrɛkwɪˈɛskat / (BrE)
A prayer for the repose of the soul of a dead person, especially in the phrase 'requiescat in pace' (rest in peace).

Examples

Let's face it, with all due respect, the Don - rest in peace - was slipping.

Etymology

From Latin requiēscat ('may he/she rest'), third-person singular present subjunctive of requiēscere ('to rest').
