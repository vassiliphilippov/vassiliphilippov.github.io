Midwest

1. PROPN

/ ˈmɪdˌwɛst / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌwɛst / (BrE)
the north central region of the United States, often known as the heartland or breadbasket of America

Examples

In the midwest, oxidation ponds are used extensively for the treatment of domestic sewage from suburban areas.

Etymology

Formed from 'mid' and 'west,' referring to its historical position west of the Appalachian Mountains and in the middle of the country.
