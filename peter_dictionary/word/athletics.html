athletics

1. NOUN

/ əˈθlɛtɪks / (AmE), / æθˈlɛtɪks / (BrE)
Physical sports and games that require skill, strength, and competition, typically organized as events or activities.

Examples

He merely became victimized by a form of athletics that respects no one and aggravates all.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sport - an activity involving physical exertion and skill; a physical game

Etymology

From Greek *athlētikos* (pertaining to contests), from *athlētēs* (contestant).
