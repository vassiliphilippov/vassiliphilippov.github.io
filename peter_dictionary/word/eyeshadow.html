eyeshadow

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪˌʃædoʊ / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌʃædəʊ / (BrE)
A colored cosmetic applied to the eyelids and surrounding area to accentuate or darken the eyes.

Examples

The girls are prone to dress far more flamboyantly than their counterparts out of town, and eye shadow, mascara, and elaborate bouffant hairdos - despite the admonitions of cautious guidance personnel - are not unknown even in early morning classes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mascara - cosmetic for darkening and lengthening eyelashes

Etymology

From 'eye' + 'shadow', referring to the effect of adding color or shading around the eyes.
