democracy

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈmɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒkrəsi / (BrE)
A system of government in which power is vested in the people, who rule either directly or through freely elected representatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: republic - a political system in which the head of state is not a monarch and may be elected
  • Antonym: autocracy - a system of government by one person with absolute power
  • Antonym: dictatorship - a system of government in which one person or a small group holds absolute power

Etymology

From Greek dēmokratia, from dēmos (people) + -kratia (rule, power).
