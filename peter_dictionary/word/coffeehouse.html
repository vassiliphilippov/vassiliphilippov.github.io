coffeehouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːfiˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɒfiˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small restaurant where coffee and other drinks, as well as light snacks, are served, often as a place to relax or meet with others.

Examples

The altercation in the coffee house had done little to dampen his spirits, but he was still a little wary around Rector for they had not yet discussed the incident.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: café - a small restaurant serving coffee and light meals

Etymology

From 'coffee' + 'house', pattern from 17th-century European trade and social venues.
