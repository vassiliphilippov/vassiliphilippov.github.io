Khmer

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɛr / (AmE), / kəˈmɛə / (BrE)
The official language of Cambodia, belonging to the Mon-Khmer branch of the Austroasiatic language family.

Examples

Eight or ten years ago, a couple of French hoods stole a priceless Khmer head from the Musee Guimet, in Paris, and a week later crawled into the Salpetriere with unmistakable symptoms of leprosy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Cambodian - another term for Khmer language or the people of Cambodia

Etymology

From Khmer 'Khmae', the people's own name for themselves and their language.
