boardinghouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdɪŋˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪŋˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A private house where people can rent rooms and are provided with meals.

Examples

They stayed at hotels and boardinghouses, or at private homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guesthouse - a small, private hotel or lodging house
  • Degree Higher: hotel - an establishment offering accommodation, meals, and services for travelers

Etymology

From 'board' (as in 'to provide food') + 'house', describing a place where board (meals) and lodging are provided.
