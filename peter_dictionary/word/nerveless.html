nerveless

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɜrləs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvləs / (BrE)
showing or having calm self-control and composure, especially in difficult or stressful situations; unemotional or undisturbed under pressure.

Examples

Landesco thought him "just a superior sort of plugugly "but he was, in fact, with his aggression and hostility, and nerveless indifference to risking or administering pain, a casebook psychopath.

Synset Examples

play it cool
keep cool
stayed coolheaded in the crisis
the most nerveless winner in the history of the tournament

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coolheaded - calm and able to think clearly in difficult situations
  • Synonym: unflappable - not easily upset or perturbed; very calm
  • Antonym: nervous - easily agitated or alarmed; lacking calmness

Etymology

From "nerve" + "-less", originally meaning lacking physical nerve, later extended to emotional composure.
