ultra

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌltrə / (AmE), / ˈʌltrə / (BrE)
Extremely; far beyond what is usual or accepted, especially regarding opinions, actions, or attitudes.

Examples

The already faded pastel charms of the naive music itself vanish entirely in Fistoulari's melodramatic contrasts between ultra vehement brute power and chilly, if suave, sentimentality.

Synset Examples

extremist political views
radical opinions on education
an ultra conservative

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extremist - advocating or supporting extreme measures or views
  • Synonym: radical - favoring or effecting fundamental or revolutionary changes

Etymology

From Latin ultra (beyond, on the far side of).
