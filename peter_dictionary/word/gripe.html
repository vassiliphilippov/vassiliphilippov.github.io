gripe

1. NOUN

/ ɡraɪp / (AmE), / ɡraɪp / (BrE)
an informal complaint or grievance, especially a trivial one

Examples

The Boss wants performance, not gripes.

Synset Examples

I have a gripe about the service here

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complaint - an expression of dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • Antonym: praise - an expression of approval or admiration

Etymology

From Old English 'grīpan' (to seize, grasp); sense of 'complaint' developed in the 19th century.
