panting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpæn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpæn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of breathing quickly and heavily, usually after physical exertion or excitement

Examples

[GUN FIRING] [GUNFIRE STOPS] [SHATTERED GLASS TINKLING] [MICHAEL PANTING] Kay, are you all right?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gasping - sudden, short intake of breath (often from shock or difficulty breathing)

Etymology

From the verb 'pant', likely of imitative origin (imitating the sound of heavy breathing).
