numbering

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌmbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbərɪŋ / (BrE)
a system or arrangement in which numbers are assigned to items on a list or in a sequence.

Examples

The numbering system used in Tables 1 and 2 and Figures 1 and 2 was continued for the 21 growth centers.
The difference between the sequence of Onset of ossification for the sexes governs the numbering sequence in Figures 3 and 4.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enumeration - the action of mentioning things one by one

Etymology

From 'number' + '-ing', denoting the action or result of numbering.
