threatening

1. ADJ

/ ˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (BrE)
Indicating or suggesting the possibility of harm, danger, or tragic developments.

Examples

We feel the quality of these powers initially as in some degree wholesome or threatening.
They crowded him in that threatening way once more, forced him to give in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sinister - giving the impression that something harmful or evil is happening or will happen
  • Antonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm
