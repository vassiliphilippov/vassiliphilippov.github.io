refined

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
Having or showing well-developed manners, taste, or culture; elegant and genteel in conduct or appearance.

Examples

She is well-educated and refined, all wildcat and fur, and Union from the muzzle to the crupper ".

Synset Examples

she was delicate and refined and unused to hardship
refined people with refined taste

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultivated - having refined taste and manners
  • Synonym: genteel - polite and refined in manner
  • Antonym: crude - lacking refinement or taste

Etymology

From refine + -ed; refine from Old French 'refiner', from Latin 'refināre' (to purify).

2. ADJ

/ rɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
(of a substance) freed from impurities by processing or purification.

Examples

Will mere debate on that proposition, even though it be free and untrammeled, remove the dross and leave a residue of refined gold?

Synset Examples

refined sugar
refined oil
to gild refined gold

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrefined - not having had impurities removed; not processed or purified

Etymology

From refine + -ed, ultimately from Old French 'refiner', from Latin 'refinare' (to purify again).
