classmate

1. NOUN

/ ˈklæsˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsˌmeɪt / (BrE)
A person who is in the same class as you at school or college.

Examples

He may or may not be "better off "than his classmate who went on to a college and professional school.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schoolmate - a person you attend school with
  • Synonym: roommate - a person you share a room with, often at school or college

Etymology

Formed from 'class' + 'mate', first recorded in English in the mid-19th century.
