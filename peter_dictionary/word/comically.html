comically

1. ADV

/ ˈkɑːmɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɒmɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is comical or humorous; amusingly.

Examples

Though his character is broader and more comically rounded than the don, he gives it a firmness and toughness - a sort of peasant dignity - too.

Synset Examples

the tone was comically desperate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humorously - in a humorous manner
  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious manner

Etymology

Formed from 'comical' + '-ly'.
