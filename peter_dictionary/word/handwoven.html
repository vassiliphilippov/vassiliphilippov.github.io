handwoven

1. ADJ

/ ˈhændˌwoʊvən / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwəʊvən / (BrE)
(of fabric or material) made by hand on a loom rather than by machine.

Examples

His chelas were required to assume the matsyendra posture dressed in hand-woven diapers while he read aloud from Rig-Veda and an assistant guru examined their purses in another room - nothing was stolen; the purpose was less immediate.

Synset Examples

handwoven tablecloth

Related Terms

  • Antonym: machine-made - fabric or material made by machine, not by hand
