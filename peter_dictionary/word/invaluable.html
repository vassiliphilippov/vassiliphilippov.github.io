invaluable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvæljuəbl / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaljuəbl / (BrE)
Extremely useful or precious; too valuable to be measured or calculated.

Examples

Most assuredly ideas are invaluable.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: valuable - having considerable worth or importance
  • Synonym: priceless - so precious that its value cannot be determined

Etymology

From Latin 'invaluabilis', from 'in-' (not) + 'valuabilis' (valuable).
