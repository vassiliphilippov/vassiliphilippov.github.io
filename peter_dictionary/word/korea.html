Korea

1. PROPN

/ kəˈriːə / (AmE), / kəˈrɪə / (BrE)
A peninsula in East Asia, situated between the Yellow Sea and the Sea of Japan, comprising the territories of North Korea and South Korea.

Etymology

From Korean 한국 (Han-guk), meaning 'country of the Han people'.
