bullnecked

1. ADJ

/ ˈbʊlˌnɛkt / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌnɛkt / (BrE)
Having a thick, short, and powerful neck, resembling that of a bull.

Examples

Mantle, the bull-necked blond switch-hitter, had one sensational triple-crown season, 1959, when he batted .365 and also led the American League in home runs, 52, and RBIs, 130.

Etymology

From 'bull' (the animal) + 'necked' (having a neck of a specified kind).
