preferred

1. ADJ

/ prɪˈfɜrd / (AmE), / prɪˈfɜːd / (BrE)
Regarded as more desirable or suitable than others; favored over alternatives.

Examples

We found no obvious effects due to preferred orientation of the crystallites in this sample nor would we expect to on the basis of the shape found from electron microscopic examination.

Synset Examples

coffee is preferable to tea
Danny's preferred name is `Dan'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preferable - more desirable or suitable
  • Antonym: undesirable - not wanted or not desirable

Etymology

Past participle of 'prefer', from Latin 'praeferre' meaning 'to carry before, prefer'.
