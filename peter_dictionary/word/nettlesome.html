nettlesome

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɛt.əl.səm / (AmE), / ˈnɛt.əl.səm / (BrE)
Causing irritation or annoyance; troublesome.

Examples

The time has come for citizens of all faiths to unite in an effort to remove this divisive and nettlesome issue from the political and social life of our nation.

Synset Examples

tapping an annoying rhythm on his glass with his fork

a pesky mosquito
swarms of pestering gnats
a plaguey newfangled safety catch

a vexatious child
it is vexing to have to admit you are wrong

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoying - causing irritation or annoyance
  • Synonym: bothersome - causing irritation or nuisance
  • Synonym: irritating - causing annoyance or discomfort

Etymology

From 'nettle' (the stinging plant) + '-some' (forming adjectives). The term evokes the stinging, irritating effect of nettles.
