greenroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːnˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌruːm / (BrE)
a backstage room in a theater where performers can relax or receive visitors before or after a performance

Examples

Laura Keene had been in the green room.

Etymology

Refers to a waiting room in theaters, possibly named for its green furnishings or decoration in some early theaters.
