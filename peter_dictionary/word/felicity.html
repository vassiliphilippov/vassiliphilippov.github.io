felicity

1. NOUN

/ fəˈlɪsəti / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasing and appropriate in manner or style, especially in the choice and expression of words

Examples

There are boxed proclamations in the newspaper of watchings, pinnings, ringings, engagements, and marriages in a scrupulously graded hierarchy of felicity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elegance - graceful and stylish quality
  • Synonym: aptitude - natural ability or suitability

Etymology

From Latin felicitas (happiness, good fortune), from felix (happy, fortunate).
