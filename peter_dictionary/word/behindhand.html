behindhand

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈhaɪndˌhænd / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪndˌhænd / (BrE)
late in doing something or behind schedule; not having completed something by the expected time

Examples

Damn it, Dufresne, you're putting me behind!

Synset Examples

was behindhand with the rent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: late - not arriving, happening, or done at the expected or usual time
  • Antonym: on time - punctual; occurring at the scheduled or expected time

Etymology

Formed from 'behind' + 'hand', first used in the 17th century to mean delayed or tardy.
