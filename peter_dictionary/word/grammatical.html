grammatical

1. ADJ

/ ɡrəˈmætɪkəl / (AmE), / ɡrəˈmatɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to grammar or the rules that govern how words are combined in a language

Examples

Another obstacle was the grammatical construction of Siddo.
There are many grammatical misconstructions other than dangling modifiers and anatomicals which permit two different interpretations.
The grammatical description of each occurrence in the text must be retrieved from the dictionary to permit such an analysis.
Five of the papers deal with grammatical problems.

Synset Examples

the grammatic structure of a sentence
grammatical rules
grammatical gender

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ungrammatical - not conforming to the rules of grammar

Etymology

From Latin *grammaticalis*, from Greek *grammatikos* (relating to letters or grammar).
