racing

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of competing in races, typically involving running, horses, cars, or boats, to determine the fastest participant.

Examples

Maude swooped up the cup and hiked up her top hoop as if about to take off with a racing start.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - a competition of speed

Etymology

From the verb 'race' + -ing, forming a noun meaning the action or sport of racing.
