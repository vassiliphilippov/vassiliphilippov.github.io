swarthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈswɔɹði / (AmE), / ˈswɔːði / (BrE)
having a naturally dark complexion or skin color

Examples

Sweat bubbled out on Jess's swarthy face.

Synset Examples

a dark-skinned beauty
gold earrings gleamed against her dusky cheeks
a smile on his swarthy face
`swart' is archaic

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fair - having a light complexion or skin color

Etymology

From Middle English *swarthe*, probably from Old English *sweart* (black, dark). Related to 'swart.'
