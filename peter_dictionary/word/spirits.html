spirits

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪr.ɪts / (AmE), / ˈspɪr.ɪts / (BrE)
Strong alcoholic drinks that are distilled rather than only fermented, such as whiskey, gin, or vodka.

Examples

"I 'll get the spirits ", Ma said.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: liquor - strong alcoholic drink; distilled spirits
  • Antonym: wine - alcoholic drink made from fermented grapes
  • Antonym: beer - alcoholic drink made from fermented grains, not distilled

Etymology

From Latin 'spiritus' (breath, spirit), originally referring to a distilled essence.
