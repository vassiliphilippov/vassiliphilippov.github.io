unnoticed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnoʊtɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊtɪst / (BrE)
Not observed or remarked; not detected or given attention.

Examples

A guest was locking his room; they passed behind him and got to Alex's room unnoticed.

Synset Examples

hoped his departure had passed unnoticed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignored - deliberately paid no attention
  • Synonym: overlooked - not noticed or considered
  • Antonym: noticed - seen, observed, or remarked

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' (not) + 'noticed' (past participle of 'notice').
