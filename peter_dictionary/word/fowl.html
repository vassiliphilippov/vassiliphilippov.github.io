fowl

1. NOUN

/ faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
A domesticated bird such as a chicken, turkey, or duck; especially one kept for its eggs or meat.

Examples

All search was ended; he had found his fowl.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poultry - domesticated birds raised for meat or eggs

Etymology

Old English *fugol* (bird), related to Dutch *vogel* and German *Vogel*.
