descend

1. VERB

/ dɪˈsɛnd / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛnd / (BrE)
to move or come down from a higher to a lower place or level

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ascend - to move or go up from a lower to a higher place or level

Etymology

From Latin descendere, from de- (down) + scandere (to climb).
