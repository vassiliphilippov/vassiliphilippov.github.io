logistic

1. ADJ

/ loʊˈdʒɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ləˈdʒɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
relating to the organization and coordination of complex operations, especially involving the movement of goods, equipment, or people

Examples

An agent would likely be selected which would possess sufficient viability and virulence stability to meet realistic minimal logistic requirements.

Synset Examples

logistic requirements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: logistical - alternative form meaning relating to logistics

Etymology

From French 'logistique', from 'logistique' (logistics), itself from Greek 'logistikē' (pragmatic reasoning).
