Mrs

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪsɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪsɪz / (BrE)
a title used before the family name or full name of a married woman

Examples

MRS. ROTH: Excuse me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ms - title for a woman regardless of marital status
  • Synonym: Miss - title for an unmarried woman
  • Antonym: Mr - title for a man

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'mistress,' from Middle English 'maistresse.'
