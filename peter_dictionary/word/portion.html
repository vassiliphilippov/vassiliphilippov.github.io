portion

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɔrʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɔːʃən / (BrE)
an amount of something that is allotted or divided from a whole; a share or part assigned to someone or something.

Examples

Nevertheless, "we feel that in the future Fulton County should receive some portion of these available funds ", the jurors said.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: share - a part of something that has been divided between several people or things
  • Synonym: division - the act or process of dividing something into parts or shares
  • Antonym: whole - all of something; the entire amount, opposite of a portion
  • Degree Lower: fraction - a small part or segment of something

Etymology

From Old French *portion*, from Latin *portio* (a share, part).
