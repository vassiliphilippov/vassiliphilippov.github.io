transversally

1. ADV

/ trænzˈvɜrsəli / (AmE), / trænsˈvɜːsəli / (BrE)
In a manner that goes across something from side to side; in a transverse manner.

Examples

Beccaria had almost stumbled on a lead to the relationship between electricity and magnetism when a discharge from a Leyden jar was sent transversally through a piece of watch spring steel making its ends magnetic.

Synset Examples

they were cut transversely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transversely - in a transverse manner; across from side to side

Etymology

From 'transversal' (from Latin 'transversalis', from 'transversus' meaning 'turned across') + '-ly'.
