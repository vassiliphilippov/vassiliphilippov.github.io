nirvana

1. NOUN

/ nɪrˈvɑːnə / (AmE), / nɪəˈvɑːnə / (BrE)
(in Hinduism and Buddhism) a transcendent state in which there is neither suffering, desire, nor sense of self, and the individual is released from the effects of karma and the cycle of death and rebirth.

Examples

Repeating it like a student telling himself that the jewel is in the lotus he sank into nirvana.

Etymology

From Sanskrit *nirvāṇa* (blown out, extinction, liberation).
