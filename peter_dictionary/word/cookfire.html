cookfire

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌfaɪə / (BrE)
A fire made or used for cooking food, typically outdoors.

Examples

Hank had gathered wood for a cookfire, and his wife was busy at it now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campfire - an outdoor fire for warmth or light, often used for cooking
