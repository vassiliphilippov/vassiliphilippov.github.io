splenomegaly

1. NOUN

/ ˌspliːnoʊˈmɛɡəli / (AmE), / ˌspliːnəʊˈmɛɡəli / (BrE)
Abnormal enlargement of the spleen, often as a symptom of various underlying disorders.

Examples

Splenomegaly was first noted in 1956, and a sternal marrow biopsy at that time showed "scattered foci of fibrosis "suggestive of myelofibrosis.

Etymology

From Greek splēn (spleen) + -megaly (enlargement).
