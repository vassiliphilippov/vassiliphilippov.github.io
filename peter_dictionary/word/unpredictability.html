unpredictability

1. NOUN

/ ʌn.prɪˌdɪk.təˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ʌn.prɪˌdɪk.təˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality of being impossible to predict or foresee; the tendency to change suddenly or behave in unexpected ways.

Examples

Maybe that was why they were cordial and loyal towards the unpredictability of Arlene.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: predictability - the quality of being predictable

Etymology

From un- (not) + predict + -ability (denoting capacity) + -ity (forming nouns).
