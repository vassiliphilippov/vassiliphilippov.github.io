Ile-de-France

1. PROPN

/ il də ˈfrɑns / (AmE), / il də ˈfrɑ̃s / (BrE)
a region in north central France encompassing Paris and its surrounding areas

Examples

In a brighter nautical vein is Ile de France blue.

Etymology

French, meaning 'Island of France', from Old French 'île' (island) and 'France'.
