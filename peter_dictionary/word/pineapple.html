pineapple

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪnˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnæpəl / (BrE)
A large, sweet tropical fruit with rough orange skin, spiky green leaves on top, and juicy yellow flesh, widely cultivated in warm climates.

Examples

Cuba Libre... Pina Colada...

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ananas - another name for pineapple used in some languages

Etymology

Originally from Spanish 'piña' (pine cone), due to the fruit's resemblance, and 'apple' added by English speakers.
