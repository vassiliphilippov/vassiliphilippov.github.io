troika

1. NOUN

/ ˈtroʊ.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˈtrɔɪ.kə / (BrE)
A traditional Russian carriage drawn by three horses harnessed side by side.

Examples

Mr. Khrushchev's demand for a troika is dormant, not dead; the West may or not remain satisfied with the kind of neutralism that U Thant represents.

Etymology

From Russian тройка (trojka), meaning 'group of three', from три (tri), 'three'.
