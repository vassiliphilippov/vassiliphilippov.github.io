embroiled

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈbrɔɪld / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrɔɪld / (BrE)
deeply involved in a difficult, complicated, or confusing situation, often one that is problematic or controversial

Examples

I'm talking about a cop who's mixed up in drugs, a dishonest cop.

Synset Examples

embroiled in the conflict
felt unwilling entangled in their affairs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entangled - involved in a complicated or compromising situation

Etymology

From French 'embrouiller' (to tangle, confuse), from 'em-' (in) + 'brouiller' (to jumble, confuse)
