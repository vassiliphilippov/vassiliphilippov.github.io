volcanic

1. ADJ

/ vɑlˈkænɪk / (AmE), / vɒlˈkænɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, produced by, or consisting of volcanoes or volcanic activity.

Examples

A piece of black, volcanic glass.

Synset Examples

volcanic steam
volcanic islands such as Iceland
a volcanic cone is a conical mountain or hill built up of material from volcanic eruptions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: igneous - (of rock) formed by solidified lava or magma

Etymology

From Latin 'volcanicus', from 'Vulcanus' (the Roman god of fire).
