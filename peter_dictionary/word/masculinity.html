masculinity

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæskjəˈlɪnəti / (AmE), / ˌmæskjʊˈlɪnəti / (BrE)
The qualities or attributes regarded as characteristic of men or boys.

Examples

On the other hand, wifely supremacy demeans the husband, saps his self-respect, and robs him of his masculinity.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: femininity - qualities or attributes regarded as characteristic of women or girls

Etymology

From Latin masculinus (male), via French masculinité.
