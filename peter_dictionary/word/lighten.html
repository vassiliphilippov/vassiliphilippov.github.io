lighten

1. VERB

/ ˈlaɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtən / (BrE)
(of a person's face or expression) to become more cheerful or animated.

Examples

His London contract was rescinded, and now, he explains cheerfully, as a bright smile lightens his intense, mobile face, "I conduct only one hundred and twenty concerts "!

Synset Examples

the conversation lightened me up a bit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brighten - to make or become more cheerful or lively
  • Antonym: darken - (of a mood or expression) to become less cheerful, more serious or sad

Etymology

From Old English lȳhtan (to illuminate, make light or less heavy).
