fusiform

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuːzəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈfjuːzɪfɔːm / (BrE)
Tapering at both ends; spindle-shaped.

Examples

The intima of the larger coronary arteries was thickened by fibrous tissue containing fusiform clefts and mononuclear cells.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spindle-shaped - shaped like a spindle; tapering at each end

Etymology

From Latin 'fusus' (spindle) + 'forma' (shape, form).
