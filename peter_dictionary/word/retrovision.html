retrovision

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛtroʊˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
The supposed ability to perceive or see events from the distant past, often considered a paranormal phenomenon.

Examples

Dreams present many mysteries of telepathy, clairvoyance, prevision and retrovision.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clairvoyance - the supposed ability to perceive events beyond normal sensory contact
  • Antonym: prevision - the ability to see future events (as opposed to past)

Etymology

Formed from Latin 'retro' (back, behind) and English 'vision'.
