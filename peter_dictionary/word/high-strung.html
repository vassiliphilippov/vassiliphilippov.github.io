high-strung

1. ADJ

/ haɪˈstrʌŋ / (AmE), / haɪˈstrʌŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) very tense, nervous, or easily agitated; highly sensitive or excitable.

Examples

Hard to believe, high-strung as he was.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edgy - tense, nervous, or irritable
  • Antonym: relaxed - calm and free from tension or anxiety

Etymology

From 'high' + 'strung', figuratively referring to a musical instrument with taut strings, implying heightened sensitivity.
