roughneck

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌfnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrʌfnɛk / (BrE)
A rough or violent person, often who behaves in a tough or aggressive manner.

Examples

He was also at this time, although not so interwoven in high politics and the rackets as Torrio and Capone, the most powerful and most dangerous mob leader in the Chicago underworld, the roughneck king.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate those whom they perceive as vulnerable
