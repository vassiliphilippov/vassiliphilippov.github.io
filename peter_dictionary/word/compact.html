compact

1. NOUN

/ kəmˈpækt / (AmE), / kəmˈpækt / (BrE)
a small, flat case containing face powder and a mirror, designed to be carried in a woman's purse or handbag

Examples

Then, with a shrug of pretended indifference, she took a compact from her purse and went through the motions of fixing her make-up.
