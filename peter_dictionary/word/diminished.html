diminished

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈmɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / dɪˈmɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
Made smaller, weaker, or less; reduced in size, degree, importance, or strength.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reduced - made smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
  • Antonym: enhanced - increased or improved in quality, value, or extent

Etymology

From diminish + -ed, ultimately from Old French 'diminuer', from Latin 'deminuere' (to lessen).
