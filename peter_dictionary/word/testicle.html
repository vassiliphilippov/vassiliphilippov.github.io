testicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪkəl / (BrE)
Either of the two oval organs that produce sperm in males and secrete male hormones; located in the scrotum.

Examples

They set bouncing betties to jump and explode at testicle level while we more mercifully had them go off at the head.
Ice baths, electric shocks, lashings, wild dogs, testicle crushers.

Synset Examples

she kicked him in the balls and got away

Related Terms

  • Synonym: testis - medical term for testicle

Etymology

From Latin 'testiculus', diminutive of 'testis' (witness, testicle).
