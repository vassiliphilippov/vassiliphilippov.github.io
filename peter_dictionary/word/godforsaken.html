godforsaken

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑd.fərˌseɪ.kən / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.fəˌseɪ.kən / (BrE)
(of a place) remote, desolate, or bleak to the point of seeming abandoned or neglected.

Examples

Here in the God-forsaken place, the westerly end of nowhere, Tom Lord looked almost insignificant, almost contemptible.

Synset Examples

a desert island
a godforsaken wilderness crossroads
a wild stretch of land
waste places

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desolate - empty and showing no signs of life
  • Synonym: bleak - cold, empty, and not welcoming or attractive

Etymology

From 'God' + 'forsaken', meaning abandoned by God.
