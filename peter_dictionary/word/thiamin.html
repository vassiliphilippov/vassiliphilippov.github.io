thiamin

1. NOUN

/ ˈθaɪ.ə.mɪn / (AmE), / ˈθaɪ.ə.mɪn / (BrE)
a B vitamin (vitamin B1) essential for maintaining a healthy nervous system and preventing beriberi.

Examples

The B vitamins are well represented, especially thiamin and riboflavin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vitamin B1 - another name for thiamin, a B vitamin
  • Degree Higher: riboflavin - vitamin B2, another B vitamin

Etymology

Variant of thiamine, from 'thio-' (sulphur) and 'amine' (amino group).
