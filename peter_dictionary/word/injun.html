Injun

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndʒən / (AmE), / ˈɪndʒən / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) An offensive term for a Native American.

Examples

We'll dance around it like wild Injuns.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Indian - person belonging to the indigenous peoples of the Americas

Etymology

Corrupted pronunciation of 'Indian', referring to Native Americans.
