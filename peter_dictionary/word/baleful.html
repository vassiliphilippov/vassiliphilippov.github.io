baleful

1. ADJ

/ ˈbeɪlfəl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪlfʊl / (BrE)
threatening harm; menacing or sinister

Examples

She took it grudgingly, her dark eyes baleful as they met his.

Synset Examples

the Florida eagles have a fierce baleful look

Related Terms

  • Synonym: menacing - threatening or suggesting danger
  • Antonym: benign - gentle and kind; not threatening

Etymology

From Old English bealuful, from bealu (evil) + -ful.
