Palermo

1. PROPN

/ pəˈlɛrmoʊ / (AmE), / paˈlɛəmoʊ / (BrE)
The capital city of Sicily, located in northwestern Sicily and known as an important port for over 3,000 years.

Examples

A Tibetan swami from Palermo, Sicily, announced in Beverly Hills a newly discovered, ancient yoga discipline for ripple breathing which increased both pranha and cosmic attraction between sexes.

Etymology

From Italian 'Palermo', from Latin 'Panormus', from Greek 'Panormos' (meaning all-harbor).
