concluding

1. ADJ

/ kənˈkluːdɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈkluːdɪŋ / (BrE)
coming at the end; serving to finish or complete something

Examples

This must be stressed because it is absolutely essential to the argument of this concluding chapter.
The fourth and concluding point will be to estimate the long-run significance of the elections and how they figure in the current pattern of internal politics.

Synset Examples

his concluding words came as a surprise
the final chapter
the last days of the dinosaurs
terminal leave

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final - coming at the end; last
  • Antonym: opening - beginning; first in a series or event
