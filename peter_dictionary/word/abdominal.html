abdominal

1. ADJ

/ æbˈdɑmɪnəl / (AmE), / æbˈdɒmɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the abdomen (the part of the body between the chest and the pelvis)

Examples

They often fail, however, to develop real abdominal, back, chest, shoulder and arm strength.
On July 4, 1959, the patient developed marked abdominal pain and distension, went into shock, and died.
The muscles of the extremities, chest wall, neck, and abdominal wall were soft, pale, and atrophic.

Synset Examples

abdominal muscles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abdominal muscle - any muscle located in the abdomen

Etymology

From Latin 'abdominalis', from 'abdomen' (belly).
