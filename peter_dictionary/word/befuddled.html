befuddled

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈfʌdəld / (AmE), / bɪˈfʌdəld / (BrE)
Unable to think clearly, especially because of drunkenness.

Examples

In his teens O' Banion was enrolled in the vicious Market Street gang and he became a singing waiter in McGovern's Cafe, a notoriously low and rowdy dive in North Clark Street, where befuddled customers were methodically looted of their money by the singing waiters before being thrown out.

Synset Examples

the wino's poor befuddled mind
a mind befogged with drink

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuddled - confused or intoxicated, especially by drink
  • Antonym: clearheaded - thinking clearly, not affected by alcohol

Etymology

From the prefix 'be-' and 'fuddled', meaning confused, possibly from 'fuddle' (to confuse with drink).
