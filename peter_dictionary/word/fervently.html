fervently

1. ADV

/ ˈfɜrvəntli / (AmE), / ˈfɜːv(ə)ntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows intense passion, enthusiasm, or sincerity.

Examples

She realized I 'd have to notify the police, but fervently hoped I could avoid mentioning her name.

Synset Examples

both those for and against are fervently convinced they speak for the great majority of the people
a fierily opinionated book

Related Terms

  • Synonym: passionately - in a way that shows or involves strong feelings or emotion

Etymology

From Latin 'fervens' (boiling, glowing), via Old French and Middle English.
