baker

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to make bread, cakes, or other baked goods, usually for sale.

Examples

- Who are you? - I am Enzo, the baker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pastry chef - person who specializes in making pastries and desserts

Etymology

From Middle English *bakere*, from Old English *bæcere*, from *bacan* (to bake).
