pianistic

1. ADJ

/ piːəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / piəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the piano or the technique of piano playing

Examples

Certain pianistic traits are common to all five Schnabelian renditions, most notably the "Schnabel trill " (which differs from the conventional trill in that the two notes are struck simultaneously).

Synset Examples

pianistic abilities

Etymology

From piano + -istic, meaning pertaining to the piano.
