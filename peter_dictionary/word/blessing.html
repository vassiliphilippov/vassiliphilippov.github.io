blessing

1. NOUN

/ ˈblɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
approval or support given to a plan, action, or situation; formal or explicit sanction.

Examples

All this has not, however, been an unmixed blessing.
Give me your blessing.

Synset Examples

he gave the project his blessing
his decision merited the approval of any sensible person

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approval - the action of approving something
  • Synonym: sanction - official permission or approval
  • Antonym: disapproval - the feeling of not approving

Etymology

From Old English 'blēdsian', related to 'blōd' (blood), originally meaning to make sacred with blood.
