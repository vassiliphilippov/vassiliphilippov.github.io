regulator

1. NOUN

/ ˈɹɛɡjəˌleɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to control or maintain a steady flow, pressure, temperature, or similar parameter in a system

Examples

The argon flow from commercial bottles was regulated by a pressure regulator and measured with a gas flow rator.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: controller - a device that manages or directs a process or system
  • Synonym: valve - a device that controls the flow of fluid

Etymology

From Latin 'regulator', from 'regulare' (to regulate).
