warm-toned

1. ADJ

/ wɔrm toʊnd / (AmE), / wɔːm təʊnd / (BrE)
(of music, sound, color, or style) having a quality that is pleasantly mellow, rich, or full; lacking harshness.

Examples

Badura-Skoda-Vienna Konzerthaus (Westminster) and Demus-Schubert Quartet (Deutsche Grammophon) are both warm toned, pleasantly lyrical, but rather slack and tensionless.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warm - (of sound or color) having pleasant richness or mellowness
