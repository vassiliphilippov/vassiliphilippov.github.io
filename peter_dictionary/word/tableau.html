tableau

1. NOUN

/ təˈbloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtæbləʊ / (BrE)
A dramatic scene or group of people arranged in a striking pose, often as if in a painting; a vivid or picturesque representation.

Examples

"I would like to enact a little tableau this afternoon ", Rector said, He explained about the visit and the effect he wished to create, the picture of a very busy mission.

Etymology

From French 'tableau' (picture, scene), diminutive of 'table' (table, picture).
