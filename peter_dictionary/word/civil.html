2. ADJ

/ ˈsɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl / (BrE)
polite in a way that is satisfactory or minimally courteous, especially out of social obligation rather than genuine warmth

Examples

He was smooth and civil spoken but it seemed to me there was something tough under his self-effacing manner.

Synset Examples

even if he didn't like them he should have been civil

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polite - showing good manners and respect for others
  • Synonym: courteous - polite, respectful, or considerate in manner
  • Antonym: rude - offensively impolite or ill-mannered

Etymology

From Latin civilis (relating to a citizen, polite)
