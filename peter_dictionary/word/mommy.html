mommy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑmi / (AmE), / ˈmɒmi / (BrE)
An informal term for a mother, especially as used by or to young children.

Examples

He wants Mommy to think him a good boy.
If he can't play with Mommy's magazines, he should have some old numbers of his own.
Mommy, Daddy'’s fighting again!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mom - informal term for mother
  • Synonym: mum - British informal term for mother
  • Synonym: mama - informal term for mother
  • Synonym: mother - female parent

Etymology

Diminutive of 'mom', itself a variant of 'mum' or 'mother'.
