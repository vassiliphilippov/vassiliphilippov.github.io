regards

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈɡɑrdz / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːdz / (BrE)
Polite greetings or expressions of best wishes, typically sent to someone through another person.

Examples

To Mark: "Please give my regards to Myra ".

Synset Examples

give him my kind regards
my best wishes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: best wishes - an expression conveying good luck or kind regard
  • Synonym: greetings - words or gestures of welcome or goodwill

Etymology

From Old French 'regarder' (to look at), from re- (again) + garder (to watch).
