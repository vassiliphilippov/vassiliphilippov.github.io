seniority

1. NOUN

/ sɪnˈjɔriˌti / (AmE), / sɪnˈjɔːrɪti / (BrE)
the state of having a higher rank, status, or privilege within an organization or group, especially due to longer service or experience

Examples

Will your union accept seniority concessions in assigning work for older or disabled employees?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: juniority - the state of being junior or of lower rank due to less service or experience
  • Synonym: senior - a person of higher rank or someone with more years of service

Etymology

From senior + -ity, after Latin senior (older) and -itas (state, quality)
