repayment

1. NOUN

/ riˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / riˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of paying back money that was previously borrowed, received, or owed

Examples

A proposed loan must be for sound purposes or sufficiently secured so as to assure a reasonable chance of repayment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: refund - money returned to a payer
  • Degree Lower: payment - the act of paying money

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 're-' (again) and 'payment', from Latin 'pagare' (to pay).
