scrapheap

1. NOUN

/ ˈskræpˌhip / (AmE), / ˈskræpˌhiːp / (BrE)
A pile or collection of discarded material, waste, or things considered no longer useful.

Examples

Long ago they consigned the notions of Kyne and Dixon to the scrap heap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: junkyard - a place where scrap is collected before being disposed of or recycled
  • Synonym: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
