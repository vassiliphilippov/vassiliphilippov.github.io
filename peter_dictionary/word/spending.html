spending

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of paying out or disbursing money for goods or services.

Examples

Are you spending too much money on team uniforms that benefit only a few employees?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expenditure - the action of spending funds
  • Antonym: saving - the act of keeping or holding back money instead of spending it

Etymology

From the verb 'spend' + -ing, originally from Old English *spendan*, from Latin *expendere* (to pay out).
