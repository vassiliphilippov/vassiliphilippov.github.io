harem

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛrəm / (AmE), / ˈheə.rəm / (BrE)
the part of a Muslim household reserved for wives, concubines, and female relatives

Examples

Not everyone is bewitched by Byron's caliphs and harem beauties ".

Etymology

From Turkish 'harem', from Arabic *ḥarīm* (forbidden place, sanctuary).
