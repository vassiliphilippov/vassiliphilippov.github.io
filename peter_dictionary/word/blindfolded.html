blindfolded

1. ADJ

/ ˈblaɪndˌfoʊldɪd / (AmE), / ˈblaɪndˌfəʊldɪd / (BrE)
Wearing a blindfold over your eyes so that you cannot see.

Examples

There was no weapon Early could not take apart and reassemble blind-folded.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sighted - able to see, not blindfolded
