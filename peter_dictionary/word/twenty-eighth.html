twenty-eighth

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛntiˈeɪθ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛntiˈeɪθ / (BrE)
Coming after the twenty-seventh in a sequence; 28th.

Examples

These hard surface cleaners are discussed in Chapter 28.
A Yank writing from Vicksburg, May 28, 1863, stated "Not less than 50 balls have passed over me since I commenced writing.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-seventh - coming next before the twenty-eighth in position; 27th
  • Degree Higher: twenty-ninth - coming next after the twenty-eighth in position; 29th
