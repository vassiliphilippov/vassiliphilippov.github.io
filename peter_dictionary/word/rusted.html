rusted

1. ADJ

/ ˈrʌstɪd / (AmE), / ˈrʌstɪd / (BrE)
Covered with or affected by rust, typically as a result of oxidation.

Examples

It swayed with the wicker swings and screeched with the rusted hinges of screen doors.

Synset Examples

rusted hinges

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rusty - (of metal) covered with rust
