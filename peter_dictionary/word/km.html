km

1. NOUN

/ kiː ˈɛm / (AmE), / keɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
Abbreviation for kilometer, a metric unit of length equal to 1,000 meters or approximately 0.621 miles.

Examples

If such is the case, the particles within a distance of about * * f km of the Earth will have, relative to the Earth, a kinetic energy less than their potential energy and they will be captured into orbits about the Earth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kilometer - a metric unit of length equal to 1,000 meters

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'kilometer', from French 'kilomètre', from 'kilo-' (thousand) + 'mètre' (meter).
