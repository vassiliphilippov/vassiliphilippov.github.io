accommodation

1. NOUN

/ əˌkɑməˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
An agreement or compromise that settles a dispute or difference.

Examples

When did I ever refuse an accommodation?

Synset Examples

they reached an accommodation with Japan

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compromise - an agreement reached by making mutual concessions
  • Antonym: disagreement - a lack of consensus or settlement

Etymology

From Latin accommodatio (an adjustment, adaptation), from accommodare (make fit, adapt).
