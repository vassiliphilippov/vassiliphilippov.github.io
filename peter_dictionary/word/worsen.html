worsen

1. VERB

/ ˈwɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈwɜːsən / (BrE)
to become worse; to deteriorate in condition or quality

Examples

He declared the government is thinking of asking for foreign troops if the situation worsens.

Synset Examples

Conditions in the slum worsened

Related Terms

  • Antonym: improve - to become better
  • Synonym: deteriorate - to become progressively worse

Etymology

Formed from 'worse' (Old English 'wiersa') plus the verb-forming suffix '-en'.
