fourfold

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈfɔːfəʊld / (BrE)
Four times as great or as many; increased or multiplied by four.

Examples

In this instance, there was a dosage of 562 during the first two hours and a total dosage of 1980 for the four hour period, a four-fold increase.

Synset Examples

a fourfold increase in the dosage

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: single - onefold; only one of something
  • Degree Lower: double - twice as great or many
  • Degree Lower: triple - three times as great or many

Etymology

From Old English *feowerfeald* (fourfold), from *feower* (four) + *-feald* (-fold, multiple).
