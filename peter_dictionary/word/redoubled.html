redoubled

1. ADJ

/ riˈdaʊbəld / (AmE), / rɪˈdaʊbəld / (BrE)
Having been increased greatly in intensity, size, or amount.

Examples

Most marketing people agree it is going to take redoubled efforts to satisfy future requirements.

Synset Examples

we faced redoubled attacks from the enemy
despite our redoubled efforts

Etymology

From 'redouble', itself from French 'redoubler', from Latin 're-' (again) + 'duplare' (to double).
