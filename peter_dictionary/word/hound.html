hound

1. NOUN

/ haʊnd / (AmE), / haʊnd / (BrE)
an unpleasant or despicable person; a contemptible man

Examples

I never felt such a lowdown hound in my life.

Synset Examples

you dirty dog

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scoundrel - a dishonest or unscrupulous person
  • Synonym: rascal - a mischievous or dishonest person

Etymology

From Old English 'hund' (dog), used figuratively for despicable people.

2. NOUN

/ haʊnd / (AmE), / haʊnd / (BrE)
a dog of a breed used for hunting, typically with large drooping ears and keen sense of smell

Examples

He sank back on his thin haunches like a weary hound.
So, I'm a rock hound.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dog - a domesticated carnivorous mammal (Canis familiaris)

Etymology

From Old English 'hund' (dog), related to German 'Hund'.
