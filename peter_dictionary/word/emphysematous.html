emphysematous

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛm.fəˈsiː.mə.təs / (AmE), / ˌɛm.fɪˈsiː.mə.təs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling emphysema, or affected by emphysema

Examples

On the surfaces of both lungs there were emphysematous blebs measuring up to 3 cm. in diameter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emphysema - a condition in which air is abnormally present within body tissues; especially chronic lung disease

Etymology

From emphysema + -ous, where 'emphysema' is from Greek 'emphusēma' (inflation).
