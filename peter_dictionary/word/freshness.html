freshness

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being original, new, and refreshingly different

Examples

There was the freshness of color, the freedom of perception, the lack of self-consciousness, but with a twist that made the forms leap from the page and smack you in the eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: originality - the ability to think or express something new; the power of creative thought

Etymology

From 'fresh' + '-ness', with 'fresh' from Old English *fersc* (new, invigorating).
