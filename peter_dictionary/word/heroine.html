heroine

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛroʊɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɛrəʊɪn / (BrE)
the main female character in a book, play, film, or other work of fiction, typically noted for bravery or outstanding qualities

Claire felt suddenly small and cheap, heroine of a trivial episode in the voluminous history of Don Juan.
