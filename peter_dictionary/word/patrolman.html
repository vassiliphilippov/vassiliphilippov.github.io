patrolman

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtroʊlmən / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊlmən / (BrE)
A police officer whose job is to patrol a specific area.

Examples

"What if I said nobody was here but a couple of patrolmen "?

Related Terms

Etymology

From 'patrol' + 'man'; 'patrol' from French 'patrouiller' (to tramp around).
