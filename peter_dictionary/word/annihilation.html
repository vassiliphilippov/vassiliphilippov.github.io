annihilation

1. NOUN

/ əˌnaɪ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / əˌnaɪ.ɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
The complete destruction or obliteration of something.

Examples

The measures for annihilation proposed and accepted at the Conference affected industry, transportation, civilian agencies of government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: destruction - the act of destroying; the state of being destroyed

Etymology

From Latin 'annihilatio' (reduction to nothing).
