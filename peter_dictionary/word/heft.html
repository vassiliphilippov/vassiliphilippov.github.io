heft

1. VERB

/ hɛft / (AmE), / hɛft / (BrE)
to lift or raise something, especially something heavy

Examples

Ryan hefted his bulk up and supported it on one elbow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heave - to lift or haul something with effort
  • Synonym: lift - to raise to a higher position

Etymology

Probably from Middle English heft, from Old English hæft (weight, handle).
