overtone

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊvərˌtoʊn / (AmE), / ˈəʊvətəʊn / (BrE)
a subtle or implicit meaning or quality, often suggesting something beyond the explicit content

Examples

Here artistic realism involves the audience in an impassioned participation in events whose overtones and implications are transcendent.

Synset Examples

overtones of despair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undertone - a subdued or underlying quality or implication
