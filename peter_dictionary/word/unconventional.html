unconventional

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.kənˈvɛn.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈvɛn.ʃə.nəl / (BrE)
Not following accepted customs, standards, or rules; out of the ordinary or unusual.

Examples

One finds, for example, that a terse and stereotyped verbal expression, seeming at first to be a mere hollow convention, reveals itself over the months of therapy as the vehicle for expressing the most varied and intense feelings, and the most unconventional of meanings.

Synset Examples

her unconventional dress and hair style

Related Terms

  • Antonym: conventional - following accepted customs or standards

Etymology

From un- (not) + conventional (from Latin 'conventio', agreement).
