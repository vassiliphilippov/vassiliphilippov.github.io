stretching

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɛtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of lengthening, widening, or extending something, or the state of being made longer or wider.

Examples

Measurements of skins are of little value; every snake hide is noticeably longer than its carcass and intentional stretching presents no difficulty to the unscrupulous explorer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expansion - the process of becoming larger or more extensive

Etymology

From stretch (verb) + -ing.
