homosexual

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊməˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
A person who is sexually attracted to people of the same sex.

Examples

It now means, in my country, homosexual ", I said.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heterosexual - a person attracted to people of the opposite sex
  • Synonym: bisexual - a person attracted to both men and women

Etymology

From Greek 'homo-' (same) + Latin 'sexualis' (sexual).

2. ADJ

/ hoʊməˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / həʊməˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
sexually attracted to people of one's own sex

Examples

They are n't "frigid "and they are n't homosexual; they 're just restrained in all of life.
I don't suppose it would help any if I explained to them I'm not homosexual.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: heterosexual - sexually attracted to the opposite sex
  • Synonym: gay - sexually attracted to members of the same sex (informal, especially for men)
  • Synonym: lesbian - a woman who is sexually attracted to women

Etymology

From Greek 'homos' (same) + Latin 'sexualis' (relating to sex).
