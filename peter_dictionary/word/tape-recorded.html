tape-recorded

1. ADJ

/ ˈteɪp.rɪˌkɔrdɪd / (AmE), / ˈteɪp.rɪˌkɔːdɪd / (BrE)

Examples

I want to get this on tape.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recorded - captured and stored as audio or video

Etymology

From 'tape' (magnetic recording tape) + 'recorded'.
