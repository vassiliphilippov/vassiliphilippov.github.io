detected

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈtɛktɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛktɪd / (BrE)
discovered or perceived, especially through investigation or observation.

Examples

One may conclude that most of the detected micrometeoritic material is concentrated in orbital streams which intersect the Earth's orbit.
The particles traveled a maximum detected distance of some 450 miles.

Synset Examples

the detected micrometeoritic material

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undetected - not discovered or noticed

Etymology

From the verb 'detect' plus the suffix -ed, indicating a past participle.
