clothesbrush

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊðzˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðzˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
A brush with stiff bristles used for cleaning clothes by removing dust, lint, or hair from fabric.

Examples

Winston took the clothesbrush out of the closet and went to work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lint brush - a small brush for removing lint from clothing

Etymology

From 'clothes' + 'brush', referring to its function.
