Bavaria

1. PROPN

/ bəˈvɛriə / (AmE), / bəˈveəriə / (BrE)
a state in southern Germany, known for its beer culture and as the site of major automobile manufacturing

Examples

Back in Bavaria he had seen that gesture, and at that sight his heart had always died within him.
He thought: I am a Jew from Bavaria.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Bavaria', from 'Baiuvarii', the name of an early Germanic people in the region.
