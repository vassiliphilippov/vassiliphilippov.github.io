formalized

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrməˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmələˌaɪzd / (BrE)
marked by strict or rigorous adherence to established forms, conventions, or rules, especially in religion or art

Examples

Plays more highly formalized than "Waiting for Godot ", "Endgame ", and "Krapp's Last Tape "would be hard to find.

Synset Examples

highly formalized plays like `Waiting for Godot'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conventional - following traditional forms or conventions
  • Antonym: unconventional - not following established forms or conventions

Etymology

From 'formal', from Latin forma (form) + -ize + -ed.
