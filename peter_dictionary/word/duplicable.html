duplicable

1. ADJ

/ ˈduːplɪkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdjuːplɪkəbl̩ / (BrE)
capable of being copied or duplicated; able to be reproduced exactly.

Examples

Inspiring - yes; instructive - maybe; duplicable - no!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reproducible - able to be reproduced or copied

Etymology

From Latin 'duplicare' (to double, make a copy) + English '-able'.
