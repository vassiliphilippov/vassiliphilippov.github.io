unblinkingly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈblɪŋ.kɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɪŋ.kɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows no emotion or reaction; without blinking or flinching.

Examples

He looked at the bed unblinkingly.

Synset Examples

the convicted killer listened unblinkingly to the reading of his sentence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steadily - in a steady or unwavering manner

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'blinking' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
