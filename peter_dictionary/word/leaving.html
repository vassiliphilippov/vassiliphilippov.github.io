leaving

1. NOUN

/ ˈlivɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of departing from a place or person.

Examples

He loathed the idea of drying up swamps or the chills of malaria or of leaving his natural birthright.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: departure - the act of leaving, especially to start a journey
  • Antonym: arrival - the act of coming to a place

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'leave' + -ing, functioning as a noun.
