testily

1. ADV

/ ˈtɛs.tɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈtɛs.tɪ.li / (BrE)
In an irritable or petulant manner.

Examples

"Yehhh ", said Coughlin, testily, eyeing him up and down.

Synset Examples

he said testily; `Go away!'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritably - in an irritable manner

Etymology

From 'testy' (irritable, touchy) + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
