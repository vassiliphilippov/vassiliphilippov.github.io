demonstrable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈmɑnstrəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒnstrəbl / (BrE)
Able to be shown or proved to be true; capable of being demonstrated.

Examples

"Oh, that is demonstrable ", I told him.

Synset Examples

demonstrable truths

Related Terms

  • Synonym: provable - able to be proved true
  • Antonym: indemonstrable - not able to be shown or proved

Etymology

From Latin demonstrabilis (that can be demonstrated), from demonstrare (to show).
