coefficient

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊəˈfɪʃənt / (AmE), / ˌkəʊɪˈfɪʃənt / (BrE)
a numerical value that measures a particular property or characteristic, especially in mathematics and science as a constant multiplier of a variable.
