nodular

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːd.jələr / (AmE), / ˈnɒd.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by nodules; having small, rounded lumps or swellings.

Examples

Reduces losses from stomach, hookworm, and nodular worms by interfering with reproduction of the female worm by reducing the number of eggs laid and essentially rendering all laid eggs sterile.

Synset Examples

Nodular vaginitis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noduled - having small lumps or nodules

Etymology

From Latin 'nodulus', diminutive of 'nodus' (knot).
