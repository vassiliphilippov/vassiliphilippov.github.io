board

1. NOUN

/ bɔrd / (AmE), / bɔːd / (BrE)
A group of people who are officially responsible for controlling an organization or supervising a particular activity; a committee with decision-making or supervisory authority.

Examples

Money for its construction will be sought later on but in the meantime the State Hospital board can accept gifts and donations of a site.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: committee - A group of people appointed for a specific function or task, typically by a larger group.

Etymology

From Old English bord (plank, table); later developed meaning of people seated at a table (i.e., a committee).
