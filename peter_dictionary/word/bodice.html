bodice

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪs / (BrE)
The part of a woman's dress or blouse that covers the upper body above the waist.

Examples

The bodice beneath was buttoned and, withdrawing his lips from hers, he set her upright on his knee and started to undo it, unhurriedly as if she were a child.

Etymology

From Old French *bois* (body, corset), influenced by 'body'.
