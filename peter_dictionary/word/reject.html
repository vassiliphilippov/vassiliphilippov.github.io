1. VERB

/ rɪˈdʒɛkt / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛkt / (BrE)
to refuse to accept, consider, or approve of something or someone

Examples

Synset Examples

He refused my offer of hospitality

Related Terms

  • Antonym: accept - to consent to receive or approve; to take willingly
  • Synonym: refuse - to indicate unwillingness to do, accept, or comply; to decline

Etymology

From Latin 'rejectus', past participle of 'reicere' (to throw back, refuse).
