sandy

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæn.di / (AmE), / ˈsæn.di / (BrE)
(Of hair) having a pale yellowish to yellowish-brown color, like sand.

Examples

His sandy hair was already beginning to thin and recede at the sides, and Abel looked quickly away.

Synset Examples

flaxen locks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flaxen - pale yellow hair color
  • Synonym: blond - fair or pale yellow hair color

Etymology

From sand + -y, referring to the color of sand.
