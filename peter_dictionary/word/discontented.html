discontented

1. ADJ

/ ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌdɪskənˈtɛntɪd / (BrE)
feeling or showing dissatisfaction with one's circumstances; not content

Examples

He set out on his 700 - mile return journey with five families of discontented and disappointed Swiss who turned their eyes toward the United States.

Synset Examples

saw many discontent faces in the room
was discontented with his position

Related Terms

  • Antonym: contented - feeling or expressing happiness or satisfaction

Etymology

From Latin dis- (expressing reversal) + contentus (content).
