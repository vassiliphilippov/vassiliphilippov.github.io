patroller

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtroʊlər / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊlə / (BrE)
a person who patrols an area, often as part of a security or military force

Examples

But of course the paterollers won n't be of any help, not with everything so upset and that Yankee cavalry outfit they say is running around, God knows where ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: guard - a person who protects something or someone
  • Synonym: patrolman - a police officer or soldier who patrols an area

Etymology

From 'patrol' + '-er', denoting one who performs the action of patrolling.
