arboreal

1. ADJ

/ ɑrˈbɔriəl / (AmE), / ɑːˈbɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of trees; formed by or associated with trees.

Examples

In short, and to borrow an arboreal phrase, slash timber.

Synset Examples

an arborous roof

Etymology

From Latin 'arboreus', from 'arbor' meaning 'tree'.
