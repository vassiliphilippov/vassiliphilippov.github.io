britches

1. NOUN

/ ˈbrɪtʃɪz / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtʃɪz / (BrE)
Informal term for trousers; an old-fashioned or dialectal word for breeches or pants.

Examples

What say there, Fuzzy Britches?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breeches - trousers, especially those fastened below the knee

Etymology

Variant of 'breeches', from Old English 'brēc' (plural of 'brōc', leg covering).
