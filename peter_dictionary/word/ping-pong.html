Ping-Pong

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪŋ ˌpɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋ ˌpɒŋ / (BrE)
A game resembling tennis, played on a table with small paddles and a light hollow ball; also called table tennis.

Examples

Anyone for musical Ping-pong?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table tennis - A game similar to tennis, played on a table with paddles and a small ball

Etymology

Originally a trademark, imitative of the clicking sound made by the ball in play.
