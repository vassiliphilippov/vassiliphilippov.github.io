analyzer

1. NOUN

/ ˈænəˌlaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈænəˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
an instrument or device that performs chemical, physical, or other types of analyses.

Examples

Spring used a Brush surface analyzer in a metal cleaning study and showed considerable differences in soil removal, depending upon surface roughness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: analyser - chiefly British spelling of analyzer

Etymology

From 'analyze' + '-er', meaning something that analyzes.
