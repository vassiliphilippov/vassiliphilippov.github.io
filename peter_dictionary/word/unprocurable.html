unprocurable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌn.prəˈkjʊr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈkjʊə.rə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to obtain or acquire

Examples

It was not very reasonable to believe that Lucien had procured unprocurable opium and come back to Honotassa with a formed plan to murder her.

Synset Examples

a rare work, today almost inaccessible
timber is virtually unobtainable in the islands
untouchable resources buried deep within the earth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unattainable - impossible to achieve or reach
  • Antonym: obtainable - able to be obtained or acquired

Etymology

From un- (not) + procurable, from Latin procurare (to take care of, obtain).
