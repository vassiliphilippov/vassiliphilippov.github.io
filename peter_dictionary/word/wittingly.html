wittingly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is done with full knowledge and intention; knowingly and deliberately.

Examples

Frequently, wittingly or unwittingly, price consciousness has been fostered by manufacturers, distributors, and dealers.

Synset Examples

he wittingly deleted the references

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knowingly - in a way that shows awareness or intent; intentionally
  • Antonym: unwittingly - without knowing or intending; unintentionally

Etymology

From Middle English 'witingly', from 'witten' (to know).
