mm

1. NOUN

/ ɛm ɛm / (AmE), / ɛm ɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for millimeter, a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter

The film consists of a series of still, transparent photographs, or "frames ", 35 - mm. - wide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: millimeter - a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
  • Degree Higher: centimeter - a metric unit of length equal to one hundredth of a meter
  • Degree Higher: meter - the basic unit of length in the metric system
  • Degree Lower: micrometer - a metric unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'millimeter', from French 'millimètre', from Latin 'mille' (thousand) and Greek 'metron' (measure).
