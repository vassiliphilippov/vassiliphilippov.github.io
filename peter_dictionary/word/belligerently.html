belligerently

1. ADV

/ bəˈlɪdʒərəntli / (AmE), / bəˈlɪdʒərəntli / (BrE)
In a hostile and aggressive manner; showing a readiness to fight or argue.

Examples

When the fate of the individual is visited on the group, then (the warm sweet butter dripped from her raised trembling fork and she pushed her head forward belligerently), ah, then the true bitterness of existence could be tasted.

Synset Examples

he pushed her against the wall belligerently

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aggressively - in a forceful or hostile manner
  • Antonym: peacefully - in a calm, non-hostile manner

Etymology

Formed from 'belligerent' + '-ly'; 'belligerent' from Latin 'belliger' (waging war).
