tweeze

1. VERB

/ twiːz / (AmE), / twiːz / (BrE)
to pluck or remove (hair or splinters) with tweezers

Examples

Calloused fingers, caressed only by the smoothness of polished rosaries, had swayed excitedly beneath puckered chins where tiny black hairs sprouted, never to be tweezed away.

Synset Examples

tweeze facial hair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pluck - to remove hair or feathers by pulling
  • Synonym: remove - to take something away from a place
