talismanic

1. ADJ

/ ˌtælɪzˈmænɪk / (AmE), / ˌtælɪzˈmænɪk / (BrE)
Having or believed to have magical or protective power, like that of a talisman.

Examples

The cliche loses its talismanic virtue in the light of a little history.

Synset Examples

a talismanic amulet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: magical - having or using magic powers; enchanted

Etymology

Formed from 'talisman' (from Arabic *ṭilasm*, from Greek *telesma* 'completion, religious rite').
