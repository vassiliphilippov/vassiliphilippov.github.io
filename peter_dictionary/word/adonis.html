Adonis

1. NOUN

/ əˈdoʊnɪs / (AmE), / əˈdəʊnɪs / (BrE)
A very handsome young man, often admired for his physical attractiveness.

Examples

And while her Adonis did not suffer from inexperience, satiety might well be an equal handicap.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hunk - an attractive, well-built young man
  • Synonym: beauty - a person of great physical attractiveness

Etymology

From Greek mythological figure 'Adonis', noted for his beauty.
