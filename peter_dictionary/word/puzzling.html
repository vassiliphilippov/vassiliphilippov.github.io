puzzling

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌz.əl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌz.əl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
difficult to understand or explain; mysterious or confusing.

Examples

This is puzzling to an outsider conscious of the classic tradition of liberalism, because it is clear that these Democrats who are left-of-center are at opposite poles from the liberal Jefferson, who held that the best government was the least government.
In my experience, a great many of the patient's more puzzling verbal communications are so for the reason that concrete meanings have not become differentiated from figurative meanings in his subjective experience.

Synset Examples

I didn't grasp the meaning of that enigmatic comment until much later
prophetic texts so enigmatic that their meaning has been disputed for centuries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enigmatic - difficult to interpret or understand; mysterious
  • Antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or interpret

2. ADJ

/ ˈpʌz.əl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌz.əl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
difficult to understand or explain; causing confusion or perplexity.

Examples

There we see a basically simple phonemic system enmeshed in a very complex and puzzling morphophonemic system.
This hypothesis will account for a large part of the difficulties of tonal analysis, as well as the fact that vowel systems are often more puzzling than consonantal systems.

Synset Examples

sent confusing signals to Iraq
perplexing to someone who knew nothing about it
a puzzling statement

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusing - causing bewilderment or lack of clarity
  • Synonym: perplexing - difficult to understand or deal with, causing confusion
  • Antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or interpret

Etymology

From the verb 'puzzle' + '-ing', formed from Middle English 'pousle, posle' (to puzzle or bewilder).
