tail

1. NOUN

/ teɪl / (AmE), / teɪl / (BrE)
the final or last part of something, especially a period of time, event, or process

Examples

What is interesting is that his positive qualifications for the post were revealed only as a kind of tail to his candidacy.
The voice was that of Johnson, tail gunner off another crew.

Synset Examples

the fag end of this crisis-ridden century

Related Terms

  • Synonym: end - the last part of something

Etymology

From Old English *tægl* (tail), related to Old Norse *tagl* (horse's tail).
