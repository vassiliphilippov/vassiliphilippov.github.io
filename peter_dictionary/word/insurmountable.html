insurmountable

1. ADJ

/ ɪn.sɚˈmaʊn.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ɪn.səˈmaʊn.tə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible to overcome or deal with successfully

Examples

Below the moon splashed world rolled away to insurmountable white peaks; above him the deep blue sky glittered with stars.

Synset Examples

insurmountable disadvantages

Related Terms

  • Antonym: surmountable - able to be overcome or dealt with
  • Synonym: insuperable - impossible to overcome (synonym)

Etymology

From in- (not) + surmount + -able, meaning 'not able to be surmounted'.
