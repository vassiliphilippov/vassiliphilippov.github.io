regionally

1. ADV

/ ˈriːdʒənəli / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or affects a particular region or regions.

Examples

However, the regionally differentiated results, which appear below in tables, are interesting evidence of the problems of developing self-government under even the most favorable circumstances.

Synset Examples

regionally governed

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: locally - in a local manner; relating to a specific place
  • Degree Higher: nationally - in a national manner; affecting the whole country

Etymology

From 'regional' + '-ly'. 'Regional' from Late Latin 'regionalis'.
