daffodil

1. NOUN

/ ˈdæfəˌdɪl / (AmE), / ˈdæfədɪl / (BrE)
A bulbous spring-flowering plant of the genus Narcissus, typically bearing bright yellow or white flowers with a trumpet-shaped central crown.

Examples

I'm not pleading for the heart that leaps up when it beholds a rainbow in the sky, or for the heart that with rapture fills and dances with the daffodils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narcissus - any plant of the genus Narcissus, including daffodils and jonquils

Etymology

From Old French 'asphodile', from Latin 'asphodelus', from Greek 'asphodelos', later influenced in spelling by the Dutch 'de affodil'.
