malformed

1. ADJ

/ mælˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / mælˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
Badly or abnormally shaped or formed, often in a way that is unattractive or causes impairment.

Examples

The persistent horror of having a malformed child has, I believe, been reduced, not because we have gained any control over this misfortune, but precisely because we have learned that we have so little control over it.

Synset Examples

deformed thalidomide babies
his poor distorted limbs
an ill-shapen vase
a limp caused by a malformed foot
misshapen old fingers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deformed - badly formed or misshapen
  • Synonym: misshapen - having an abnormal or ugly shape

Etymology

From Latin mal- (badly) + formed (from Latin formare, to form).
