artisan

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːr.t̬ə.zən / (AmE), / ˈɑː.tɪ.zæn / (BrE)
A worker skilled in a particular craft, especially one that involves making things by hand.

Examples

He was an artisan, a man who studied his trade and developed his craftsmanship the way a goldsmith or a wood carver did.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: craftsman - a person skilled in a particular craft

Etymology

From Italian 'artigiano', from Late Latin 'artitianus', from 'ars' (art).
