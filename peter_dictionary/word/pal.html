pal

1. NOUN

/ pæl / (AmE), / pæl / (BrE)
An informal term for a close friend.

Examples

Your pals... ...they're not bad people.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buddy - Close friend or companion (informal)
  • Synonym: friend - Person one knows and has a mutual affection with
  • Antonym: enemy - Person who is actively opposed or hostile

Etymology

Probably from Romany 'phral' (brother), influenced by English 'pal' (to pair).
