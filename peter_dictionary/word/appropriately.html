appropriately

1. ADV

/ əˈproʊpriətli / (AmE), / əˈprəʊpriətli / (BrE)
In a manner that is suitable or proper for the circumstances.

Examples

The complexities of communication have been considerably abetted in this case by appropriately stilted English language that has been excellently dubbed in place of the Russian dialogue.
In the first trial an inert substance was disseminated from a boat travelling some ten miles off shore under appropriately selected meteorological conditions.

Synset Examples

he was appropriately dressed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inappropriately - in an unsuitable or improper way
  • Synonym: properly - in a correct or suitable way

Etymology

From 'appropriate' + '-ly'; 'appropriate' from Latin 'appropriatus', past participle of 'appropriare' (to make one's own).
