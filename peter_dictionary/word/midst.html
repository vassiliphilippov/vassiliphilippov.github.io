midst

1. NOUN

/ mɪdst / (AmE), / mɪdst / (BrE)
the middle part of a group, place, or situation; the situation of being surrounded by other things or people

Examples

Soon they will begin to hunt down the traitors they are assured are in our midst.

Synset Examples

in the midst of the crowd

Related Terms

  • Synonym: center - the middle point or area of something

Etymology

From Middle English 'middes', from Old English 'midt' (middle).
