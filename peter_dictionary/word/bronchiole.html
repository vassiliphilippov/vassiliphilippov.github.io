bronchiole

1. NOUN

/ brɑnkiˌoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋkiəʊl / (BrE)
any of the smallest branches of the bronchial tubes in the lungs, ending in alveoli where gas exchange takes place

Examples
