scum

1. NOUN

/ skʌm / (AmE), / skʌm / (BrE)
an insulting term for people regarded as worthless or despicable

Examples

If you'd come in friendship, the scum that ruined your daughter would be suffering this very day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trash - worthless people (insult)
  • Synonym: riffraff - people regarded as disreputable or undesirable
  • Antonym: elite - people of wealth or high social status

Etymology

From Middle English *scum*, probably from Middle Dutch *schum* or Middle Low German *schūm* (foam, froth).
