catching

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkatʃɪŋ / (BrE)
(Baseball) the act of playing the position of catcher on a baseball team.

Examples

Ex Oriole Clint Courtney, now catching for the A's is all for the American League's 1961 expansion to the West Coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catcher - the player who plays behind home plate in baseball or softball
