farmland

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑrˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈfɑːmlænd / (BrE)
Land used for farming, especially for growing crops or raising livestock.

Examples

The air took on a special strength now that they 'd left the fecund warmth of the farmland behind.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arable - land suitable for growing crops
  • Synonym: pasture - land covered with grass for grazing animals
