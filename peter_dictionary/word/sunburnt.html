sunburnt

1. ADJ

/ ˈsʌnˌbɝnt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbɜːnt / (BrE)
(of skin) having been reddened or burned by overexposure to the sun's rays.

Examples

The tall sunburnt rustler hunter stared in amazement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sunburned - skin burned due to overexposure to the sun

Etymology

Formed from 'sun' + 'burn' + past participle ending; parallels the formation of 'sunburned'.
