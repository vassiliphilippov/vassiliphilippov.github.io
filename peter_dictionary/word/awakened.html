awakened

1. ADJ

/ əˈweɪkənd / (AmE), / əˈweɪkənd / (BrE)
(of a feeling, interest, or state) aroused or activated; having been awakened to activity or awareness.

Examples

Still, it would be surprising if what one reads did not contribute to one's ideas of right and wrong; certainly the awakened alarm over the comic books and the continuous concern over prurient literature indicate some peripheral aspects of this influence.

Synset Examples

an awakened interest in ballet

Related Terms

  • Synonym: activated - made active or operative; stirred into action
  • Antonym: dormant - not active; in a state of rest or inactivity
