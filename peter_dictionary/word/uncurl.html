uncurl

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈkɜrl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɜːl / (BrE)
to straighten from a curled or coiled position; to move or cause to move out of a curled shape

Examples

Before midnight he speeded his heart, resumed normal breathing, ran down his check list, uncurled and sat up.

Synset Examples

He uncurled himself

Related Terms

  • Antonym: curl - to form into a curved or spiral shape

Etymology

From un- (not) + curl (to form into a spiral or ringlet).
