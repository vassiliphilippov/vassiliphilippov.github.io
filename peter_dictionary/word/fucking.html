fucking

1. ADV

/ ˈfʌkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌkɪŋ / (BrE)
Used for emphasis to express strong feelings or to intensify what is being said; very (considered vulgar).

Examples

You open this fucking bar and I'’ll blow your head in!

Synset Examples

what took you so fucking long?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: very - used to emphasize an adjective or adverb
  • Antonym: slightly - to a small degree; opposite of very
