cu ft

1. NOUN

/ si juː fɪt / (AmE), / siː juː fʊt / (BrE)
abbreviation for cubic foot, a unit of volume equal to a cube measuring one foot on each side (approximately 0.0283 cubic meters)

Examples

The organic loading on the unit averaged 32 lb of BOD / day or about 2 lb BOD / day 1000 cu ft aeration capacity.
