reasoning

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːzənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈriːzənɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of thinking about something in a logical and sensible way in order to form a conclusion or judgment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: logic - the science of reasoning, proof, or inference
  • Antonym: irrationality - lack of reason or logic

Etymology

From Middle English reasonen (to reason) + -ing.
