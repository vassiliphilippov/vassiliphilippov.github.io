supplemental

1. ADJ

/ ˌsʌpləˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌplɪˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
Serving as an addition or support to something basic or primary; extra and intended to enhance or support.

Examples

Consider installing supplemental serving lines in production areas.

Synset Examples

the main library and its auxiliary branches

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auxiliary - providing additional help or support
  • Antonym: main - principal; primary

Etymology

From Latin supplementum (that which fills up or completes), from supplere (to fill up).
