urgently

1. ADV

/ ˈɜrdʒəntli / (AmE), / ˈɜːdʒəntli / (BrE)
in a way that requires immediate action or attention

Examples

Production assistance often takes the form of locating tools or materials which are urgently needed.

Synset Examples

health care reform is needed urgently
the soil desperately needed potash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desperately - in a way that shows despair or a great need for something quickly

Etymology

From 'urgent' + '-ly'; 'urgent' from Latin 'urgent-', meaning 'pressing'.
