divorced

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈvɔrst / (AmE), / dɪˈvɔːst / (BrE)
Having had one's marriage legally dissolved; no longer married as a result of divorce.

Examples

What hope lay in the nephews, she asked the intensifying light out there, with one married to a barren woman and the other divorced, having sired two girl children, with none to bear on the Packard name?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: married - having a spouse; joined in marriage
  • Antonym: single - not married or in a romantic relationship
  • Antonym: widowed - having lost one's spouse by death and not remarried

Etymology

From divorce (noun) + -ed (forming past participles/adjectives); 'divorce' from Latin 'divortium' (separation).
