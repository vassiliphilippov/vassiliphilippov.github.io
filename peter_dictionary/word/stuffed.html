stuffed

1. ADJ

/ stʌft / (AmE), / stʌft / (BrE)
Filled with a substance, such as food, material, or padding.

Examples

The things left unsaid they both felt deeply, and with a sigh they fell back on the well stuffed pillows.

Synset Examples

a stuffed turkey

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filled - full of a substance, not empty
  • Antonym: empty - containing nothing inside

Etymology

Past participle of 'stuff', from Old French 'estoffer' (to equip, fill out).
