determinable

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈtɜːrmənəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being determined, defined, or fixed; able to be established or made certain

Examples

Here there may be an analogy with cancer: we can detect cancers by their rapidly accelerating growth, determinable only when related to the more normal rate of healthy growth.

Synset Examples

determinable velocities
matters determinable by law

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indeterminable - not able to be determined or decided

Etymology

From Latin determinabilis (able to be limited), from determinare (to bound, limit).
