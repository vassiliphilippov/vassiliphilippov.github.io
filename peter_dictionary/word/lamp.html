lamp

1. NOUN

/ læmp / (AmE), / læmp / (BrE)
A device that produces light, typically by means of electricity, oil, or gas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lightbulb - a glass bulb that provides light when an electric current passes through it
  • Synonym: lantern - a portable lamp with a protective enclosure
  • Antonym: candle - a stick of wax or tallow with a wick, burned to give light

Etymology

From Latin 'lampas', from Greek 'lampas' (torch, lamp), from 'lampein' (to shine).

2. NOUN

/ læmp / (AmE), / læmp / (BrE)
