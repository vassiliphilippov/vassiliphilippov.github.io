appalling

1. ADJ

/ əˈpɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
causing shock or dismay; extremely bad or horrifying

Examples

He could only watch with a sort of gentle dismay while his body did these quick, appalling, and efficient things.

Synset Examples

appalling conditions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horrifying - causing horror or alarm
  • Synonym: shocking - causing a strong sense of surprise or indignation
  • Antonym: pleasing - giving pleasure or satisfaction

Etymology

From English 'appall' + -ing; from Old French 'apalir', from Latin 'ad-' + 'pallere' (to grow pale).
