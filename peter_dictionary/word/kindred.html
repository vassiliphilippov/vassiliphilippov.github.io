kindred

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɪndrəd / (AmE), / ˈkɪndrɪd / (BrE)
similar in character, nature, or quality; alike or related

Examples

In common parlance they would be described as misses - misinterpreters, misunderstanders, misdirectors and kindred misdeeds.

Synset Examples

a feeling akin to terror
kindred souls
the amateur is closely related to the collector

Related Terms

  • Synonym: similar - having a resemblance in appearance or nature
  • Antonym: unrelated - not connected or similar in character

Etymology

From Old English cynrēden (kinship), from cyn (kin, family) + -rǣden (condition).
