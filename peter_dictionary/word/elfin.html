elfin

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛlfɪn / (AmE), / ˈɛlfɪn / (BrE)
small and delicate, especially in an attractively dainty or unusual way

Examples

The nude was small and black-haired and elfin, and was called "Eloise ".

Synset Examples

she was an elfin creature--graceful and delicate
obsessed by things elfin and small

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petite - small and attractively dainty (of a woman)
  • Synonym: dainty - delicately small and pretty

Etymology

From 'elf' + '-in', influenced by folklore about elves' diminutive size and grace.
