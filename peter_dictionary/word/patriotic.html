patriotic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpeɪtriˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpætriˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
Having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nationalistic - having strong identification with one's own nation; synonym for patriotic but often more political
  • Antonym: unpatriotic - not having or showing devotion to one's country

Etymology

From French patriotique, from Greek patriōtēs (fellow countryman), from patria (fatherland).
