lentil

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛn.təl / (AmE), / ˈlɛn.tɪl / (BrE)
A small, round, flat seed of the lentil plant, used as food.

Examples

Flies swarmed around the bowl of lentils in which he mopped thick black bread.

Etymology

From Old French 'lentille', from Latin 'lenticula' (diminutive of 'lens', lentil).
