cur

1. NOUN

/ kɝː / (AmE), / kɜː / (BrE)
A dog of mixed breed, especially one considered to be of inferior quality; sometimes used disparagingly for a worthless or aggressive dog.

Examples

Piepsam calls the cyclist "cur "and "puppy "among other things, and at the crisis of his fit a little fox-terrier stands before him and howls into his face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mongrel - a dog of mixed breed

Etymology

From Middle English 'curre', possibly imitative of a dog's growl.
