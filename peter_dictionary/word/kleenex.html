Kleenex

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliːnɛks / (AmE), / ˈkliːnɛks / (BrE)
A brand name for a disposable tissue made of soft, absorbent paper, often used for blowing or wiping the nose; also used generically for facial tissues.

Examples

"I guess so ", she said taking a Kleenex from her purse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tissue - a piece of soft absorbent paper for wiping or cleaning
  • Synonym: handkerchief - a square piece of cloth used for wiping the nose

Etymology

Originally a trademark of Kimberly-Clark; blend of 'clean' and 'ex'.
