theology

1. NOUN

/ θiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / θiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The study of the nature of God and religious beliefs, especially as an academic or professional discipline.

Examples

This does not mean that mythological language as such can no longer be used in theology and preaching.

Synset Examples

he studied theology at Oxford

Related Terms

  • Synonym: divinity - study of religion and religious questions

Etymology

From Greek *theologia* (discourse on God), from *theos* (god) + *-logia* (study of).
