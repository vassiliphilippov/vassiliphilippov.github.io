mesenteric

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛsənˈtɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛsɛnˈtɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the mesentery (the membrane that attaches the intestines to the abdominal wall).

Examples

A careful search failed to show occlusion of any of the mesenteric vessels.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mesentery - a fold of membrane that attaches the intestine to the abdominal wall

Etymology

From Greek 'mesenterion' ('meso-' meaning 'middle' + 'enteron' meaning 'intestine').
