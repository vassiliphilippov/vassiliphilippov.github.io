scoured

1. ADJ

/ ˈskaʊɚd / (AmE), / ˈskaʊəd / (BrE)
(of a surface) worn away or eroded by the action of water, ice, or wind.

Examples

The valley lay clear, and open to the eye, right up to the sharp limbed line of gaunt, scoured hills that formed the horizon twenty miles ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eroded - worn away by erosion
  • Synonym: weathered - altered or worn by exposure to the elements
