deterioration

1. NOUN

/ dəˌtɪriəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌtɪəriəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
The process or state of becoming worse in quality, value, or strength; decline or degeneration.

Examples

The general setting of the Moroccan election may also encourage the deterioration of local party organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decline - gradual decrease in quality, strength, or power
  • Antonym: improvement - process of becoming better; opposite of deterioration

Etymology

From Latin deterioratio, from deterior (worse).
