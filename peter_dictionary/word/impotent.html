impotent

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪmpətən̩t / (AmE), / ˈɪmpətənt / (BrE)
Lacking power or ability to act; helpless or ineffective.

Examples

Piepsam tries to stop him by force, receives a push in the chest from "Life ", and is left standing in impotent and growing rage, while a crowd begins to gather.

Synset Examples

Technology without morality is barbarous; morality without technology is impotent
felt impotent rage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerless - Lacking power or ability
  • Antonym: capable - Having power or ability

Etymology

From Latin 'impotens', meaning 'powerless' (in- 'not' + potis 'able').
