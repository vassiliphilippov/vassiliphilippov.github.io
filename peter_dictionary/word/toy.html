toy

1. NOUN

/ tɔɪ / (AmE), / tɔɪ / (BrE)
an object, especially for a child, designed to be played with for amusement

Examples

Toys he has can be made to act as substitutes for family temptations such as refrigerator and gas stove.
If he snatches a toy, she says, "Caroline wants her own truck just as you do ".
Where my children come and play with their toys.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaything - an object designed for playing with

Etymology

From Middle English 'toye' (amusement, plaything), of uncertain origin.
