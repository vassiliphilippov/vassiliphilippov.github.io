voiced

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɔɪst / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪst / (BrE)
(of a speech sound) produced with vibration of the vocal cords.

Examples

He can even be a mild voiced little town guy with big town ideas and level gray eyes and a heart even Houdini could n't figure out, how it is unlocked.

Synset Examples

a frequently voiced opinion
voiced consonants such as `b' and `g' and `z'

Related Terms

  • Antonym: voiceless - (of a speech sound) produced without vibration of the vocal cords

Etymology

From the verb 'voice', meaning 'to express', itself from Old French 'vois', from Latin 'vox, vocis' (voice).
