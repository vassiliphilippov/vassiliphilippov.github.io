fine

1. ADV

/ faɪn / (AmE), / faɪn / (BrE)
Used, especially at the beginning of a sentence, to express acceptance, agreement, or permission.

Examples

Moritz said Monday his leg feels fine and, as a result, he hopes to start practicing field goals this week.
"Fine ", Rector said.
"Fine ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: okay - expression used to show agreement or consent
  • Synonym: all right - expression used to indicate acceptance or agreement
