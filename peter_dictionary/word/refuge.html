refuge

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛfjudʒ / (AmE), / ˈrɛfjuːdʒ / (BrE)
A place or situation providing safety or shelter from danger or trouble.

Examples

I have not taken refuge behind the Fifth Amendment, although it'’s my right to do so.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanctuary - a place of refuge or safety
  • Synonym: asylum - protection given by a place of safety
  • Antonym: danger - exposure to harm or risk

Etymology

From Old French 'refuge', from Latin 'refugium' (a place to flee back to), from 'refugere' (to run away).
