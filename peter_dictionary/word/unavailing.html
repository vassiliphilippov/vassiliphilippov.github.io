unavailing

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈveɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈveɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
not achieving the intended result; producing no effect; futile.

Examples

Brooklyn College is unequivocally Jewish in tone, and efforts to detribalize the college by bringing in unimpeachably midwestern types on the faculty have been unavailing.

Synset Examples

a futile effort
the therapy was ineffectual
an otiose undertaking
an unavailing attempt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: futile - incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
  • Antonym: effective - producing the intended or expected result

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'avail' (to be of use or benefit).
