aboveboard

1. ADJ

/ əˈbʌvˌbɔrd / (AmE), / əˈbʌvˌbɔːd / (BrE)
Honest and open; without concealment or trickery.

Examples

All we want to know is if he'’s on the level, or if he'’s gonna bring his boys.

Synset Examples

their business was open and aboveboard
straightforward in all his business affairs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: straightforward - honest and direct; not deceitful
  • Antonym: deceptive - intended to mislead or hide the truth

Etymology

Originally from the gambling world, meaning 'on or above the board (table)', indicating fair play.
