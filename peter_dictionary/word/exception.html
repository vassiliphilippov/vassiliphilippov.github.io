1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
a person or thing that is not included in a general statement or does not follow a rule; an instance of exclusion or omission from something stated

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rule - a statement that is generally true or followed
  • Antonym: inclusion - the act or state of including or being included

Etymology

From Latin 'exceptio' (an excluding), from 'excipere' (to take out)
