destined

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛstɪnd / (AmE), / ˈdɛstɪnd / (BrE)
certain to happen in the future, especially because it is believed to be determined by fate or a higher power

Examples

I was in the bath one day... ...when I realized why I was destined for greatness.

Synset Examples

bound to happen
an old house destined to be demolished
he is destined to be famous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fated - believed to be controlled or decided by destiny
  • Degree Lower: doomed - certain to suffer an unhappy fate

Etymology

Past participle of 'destine,' from Latin destinare (to make firm, establish).
