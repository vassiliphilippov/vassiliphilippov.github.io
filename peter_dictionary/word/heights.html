heights

1. NOUN

/ haɪts / (AmE), / haɪts / (BrE)
An area of high ground or elevated land; a high place.

Examples

A fringe of housing and gardens bearded the top of the heights, and behind it were sandy roads leading past farms and hayfields.

Synset Examples

they stood on high and observed the countryside
he doesn't like heights

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hill - a naturally raised area of land, not as high or craggy as a mountain
