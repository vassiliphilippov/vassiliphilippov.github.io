speechlessness

1. NOUN

/ ˈspiːtʃləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃləsnɪs / (BrE)
The state or condition of being unable to speak, often because of shock, emotion, or surprise.

Examples

He sensed something precious in the perilous moment, something akin to the knowledge gained on his bicycle trip through the French countryside, a knowledge imprisoned in speechlessness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: speechless - unable to speak; struck dumb, typically by emotion

Etymology

Formed from "speechless" + "-ness"; "speechless" from "speech" + "-less".
