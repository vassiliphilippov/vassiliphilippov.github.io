willpower

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪlˌpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɪlˌpaʊə / (BrE)
The ability to control your own actions, emotions, or urges by strength of will.

Examples

In the unfertile land in Palestine humans broke their backs pushing will power to the brink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-control - the ability to control oneself, especially one's emotions and desires

Etymology

From 'will' (desire, determination) + 'power' (capacity, ability).
