giveaway

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a gift or allocation of public land or resources, especially one made for the private benefit of a select group, often implying undue generosity or favoritism

Examples

Have you permitted it to become a giveaway program rather than one that has the goal of improved employee morale and, consequently, increased productivity?

Etymology

From the verb 'give away' meaning to hand out for free, adapted into noun form.
