villa

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪlə / (AmE), / ˈvɪlə / (BrE)
A large and luxurious country house, often with extensive grounds.

Examples

I want you to move to a villa near Siracusa...right now.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mansion - a large, impressive house
  • Degree Lower: cottage - a small, simple country house

Etymology

From Italian 'villa', from Latin 'villa' (country house, farm).
