worldly

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːldli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli / (BrE)
concerned with material or temporal affairs, rather than spiritual matters

Examples

At its most ecstatic moments, husband and wife are elevated far above worldly cares.

Synset Examples

worldly goods and advancement
temporal possessions of the church

Related Terms

  • Antonym: spiritual - relating to matters of the spirit or soul rather than material things

Etymology

From Middle English 'wordly', from Old English 'woruldlic' (earthly, secular), from 'woruld' (world) + '-lic' (-ly).
