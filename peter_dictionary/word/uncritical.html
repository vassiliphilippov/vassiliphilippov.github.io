uncritical

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkrɪtɪkl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
not using or showing critical judgment; accepting things without questioning or considering their value or accuracy

Examples

Fundamentally, both these types, the amateur and the collector, are uncritical and many of them do n't distinguish well between real folklore and bogus material.

Synset Examples

news sources reflected uncritical estimates of the number of juvenile addicts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: critical - having or involving an ability to judge with discernment

Etymology

Formed with prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'critical'.
