extramarital

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛkstrəˈmɛrɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈmærɪtəl / (BrE)
Occurring outside of marriage; relating to a romantic or sexual relationship between a married person and someone who is not their spouse.

Examples

One such wife, Dr. Linden says, became disgusted with her weak husband and flurried through a series of extramarital affairs in the hope of finding a stronger man.

Synset Examples

an adulterous relationship
extramarital affairs
the extracurricular activities of a philandering husband

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adulterous - involving or characterized by adultery

Etymology

From Latin extra- (outside) + maritalis (of marriage).
