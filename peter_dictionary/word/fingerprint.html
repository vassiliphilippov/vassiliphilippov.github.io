fingerprint

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌprɪnt / (BrE)
A mark or impression made by the pattern of ridges on the skin of a finger, used especially for identification purposes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thumbprint - impression made by the thumb's ridges

Etymology

Formed from 'finger' + 'print', first recorded in the late 19th century.
