dominating

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑməˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪˌneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Being the most powerful, important, or influential; having commanding influence over others.

Examples

Society here and abroad has been built around the dominating male - even the Bible appears to endorse the concept.

Synset Examples

the economically ascendant class
D-day is considered the dominating event of the war in Europe

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dominant - having power and influence over others
