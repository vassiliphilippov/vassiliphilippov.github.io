unrelated

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnrɪˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
having no logical or causal connection; not related or connected

Examples

On the other hand, we cannot regard artistic invention as pure, uncaused, and unrelated to the times in which it occurs.
If it were wholly random and unrelated, it would be 2.0, assuming the five classes were equal in n, which approximately they are.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: related - connected by logical or causal relationship

Etymology

from un- (not) + related, from Latin relatus (brought back, referred)
