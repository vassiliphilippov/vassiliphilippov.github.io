high-powered

1. ADJ

/ haɪ ˈpaʊɚd / (AmE), / haɪ ˈpaʊəd / (BrE)
Very energetic, forceful, or influential; having great drive or power.

Examples

They 'd peddled the soap virtually alone, and without much success, until about a year ago, when - with the addition of "SX-21 "to their secret formula and the inauguration of a high-powered advertising campaign - sales had soared practically into orbit.

Synset Examples

a high-octane sales manager
a high-octane marketing plan
high-powered executives
a high-voltage theatrical entrepreneur

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dynamic - characterized by constant change, activity, or progress
  • Antonym: low-powered - having little energy, force, or influence
