San Francisco

1. PROPN

/ ˌsæn frənˈsɪskoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsan frənˈsɪskəʊ / (BrE)
A major city and port in western California, known for its picturesque setting, cultural diversity, and landmarks such as the Golden Gate Bridge.

Examples

Bobby Waters of Sylvania, Ga., relief quarterback for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League, will undergo a knee operation tomorrow at Franklin Hospital here.
