ecliptic

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈklɪptɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈklɪptɪk / (BrE)
the great circle on the celestial sphere representing the apparent annual path of the Sun, corresponding to the plane of Earth's orbit around the Sun

Examples

Synset Examples

Etymology

From Latin ecliptica (line of eclipses), from Greek ekleiptikos (of an eclipse).
