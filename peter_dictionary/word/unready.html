unready

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛdi / (BrE)
not prepared or not in a state of readiness; slow to act or respond.

Examples

They were both so young, after all, so unready for any final parting.

Synset Examples

she cursed her unready tongue

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ready - prepared or in a state of readiness

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) + 'ready'.
