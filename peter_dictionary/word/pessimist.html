pessimist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛsəmɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɛsɪmɪst / (BrE)
A person who tends to see the worst aspect of things or believes that the worst will happen.

Examples

But at least the pessimists who believed that the world organization had plunged to its death in that plane crash in the Congo have been proved wrong.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future

Etymology

From French 'pessimiste', from Latin 'pessimus' (worst).
