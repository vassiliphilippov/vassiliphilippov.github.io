fiftieth

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪf.ti.əθ / (AmE), / ˈfɪf.ti.ɪθ / (BrE)
coming after forty-ninth in a sequence; 50th.

Examples

An attempted middle course might lead to devices like a 5000 - word alphabetized dictionary from which every fiftieth word was selected.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: forty-ninth - ordinal number for 49th
  • Degree Higher: fifty-first - ordinal number for 51st

Etymology

From 'fifty' with the ordinal suffix '-eth'.
