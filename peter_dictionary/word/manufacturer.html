manufacturer

1. NOUN

/ ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərər / (AmE), / ˌmanjʊˈfæktʃ(ə)rə / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Antonym: consumer - person who uses goods and services

Etymology

From manufacture + -er, ultimately from Latin manus (hand) + facere (to make).
