enumerate

1. VERB

/ ɪˈnuːmɚˌeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈnjuːməˌreɪt / (BrE)
to mention (a number of things) one by one; to list separately

Examples

It will be shown that the objectives of the cooperative people in an organization determine the type of network required, because the type of network functions according to the characteristics of the messages enumerated in Table 1.

Synset Examples

She enumerated the many obstacles she had encountered
The doctor recited the list of possible side effects of the drug

Related Terms

  • Synonym: list - to make a list of items; to enumerate
  • Synonym: itemize - to list or mention items separately

Etymology

From Latin enumerare, from e- (out) + numerare (to count)
