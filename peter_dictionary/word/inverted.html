inverted

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvɝːtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɜːtɪd / (BrE)
Placed or positioned so that the top and bottom are reversed; turned upside down.

Examples

His maneuvering for the shot had placed him near the overcast, almost inverted and heading up into the clouds.

Synset Examples

a quotation mark is sometimes called an inverted comma
an upside-down cake

Related Terms

  • Antonym: upright - in a normal or vertical position

Etymology

From Latin 'invertĕre', meaning 'to turn upside down'.
