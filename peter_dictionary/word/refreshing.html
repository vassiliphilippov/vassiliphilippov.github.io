refreshing

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Making you feel more lively, energetic, or awake; invigorating.

Examples

"Where the Boys Are "also has a juvenile bounce that makes for a refreshing venture in comedy.
Holmes is addicted to the use of cocaine and other refreshing stimulants; he is prone to semi catatonic trances induced by the playing of the vioiln; he is a recluse, an incredible egotist, a confirmed misogynist.

Synset Examples

the bracing mountain air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invigorating - making someone feel more energetic or lively
  • Synonym: bracing - invigorating; giving strength or energy

Etymology

Present participle of 'refresh', from Old French 'refreschir', from Latin 're-' (again) + 'frescare' (to make cool, fresh).
