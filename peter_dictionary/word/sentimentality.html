sentimentality

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛn.tə.mənˈtæ.lə.ti / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɪ.mɛnˈtæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
Excessive or insincere expression of emotion, especially when it is superficial or mawkish.

Examples

The already faded pastel charms of the naive music itself vanish entirely in Fistoulari's melodramatic contrasts between ultra vehement brute power and chilly, if suave, sentimentality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mawkishness - excessive or insincere emotion; sickly sentimentality
  • Degree Lower: sentiment - a view or feeling; emotional thought

Etymology

From sentiment + -ality, modelled after sentiment (from Latin sentimentum, 'feeling').
