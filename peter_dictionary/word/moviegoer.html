moviegoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːviˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈmuːviˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
A person who regularly goes to watch movies at cinemas or theaters.

Examples

The effect, needless to say, is almost terrifying, and though at times a bit obscure, the film is certainly a much needed catharsis for the "repressed "movie-goer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinemagoer - person who goes to the cinema

Etymology

From 'movie' (motion picture) + 'goer' (one who goes).
