publicist

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to generate publicity for a person, product, or organization; someone who publicizes.

Examples

When East Germans fled to the West by the thousands, paeans of joy rose from the throats of Western publicists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promoter - a person who promotes or publicizes events, products, or people
  • Synonym: press agent - a person employed to generate and manage publicity for a person, organization, or work

Etymology

From French publiciste, from public (public) + -ist (one who practices).
