crumpled

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrʌmpəld / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmpəld / (BrE)
(of metal or similar material) bent or crushed out of shape; wrinkled or dented, especially as a result of pressure or impact.

Examples

Alison was still sound asleep; he made fresh coffee and searched through all the desk drawers for more cigarettes before thinking of her handbag, and found a crumpled stray cigarette at its bottom, which tasted peculiarly of face powder.

Synset Examples

bent nails
a car with a crumpled front end
dented fenders

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dented - having a surface that is sunken or indented, often due to a blow.
  • Synonym: bent - shaped or forced into a curve or angle (especially of a metal object).

Etymology

From the verb 'crumple', which derives from Middle English 'crumplen', related to 'crump', meaning to curl up or bend out of shape.
