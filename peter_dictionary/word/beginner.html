beginner

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈɡɪnər / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɪnə / (BrE)
A person who is new to and inexperienced in a particular activity or field.

Examples

The last essential to the beginner's gymnastic program is the somersault, or forward roll.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: novice - person new to or inexperienced in a field or situation
  • Antonym: expert - person with extensive skill or knowledge in a field

Etymology

From Middle English beginner, from 'begin' + '-er'.
