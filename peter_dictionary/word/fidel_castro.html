Fidel Castro

1. PROPN

/ fiˈdɛl ˈkæstroʊ / (AmE), / fiˈdɛl ˈkæstrəʊ / (BrE)
(1926–2016) Cuban revolutionary and statesman, leader of Cuba from 1959 to 2008, who established the country's first socialist state.
