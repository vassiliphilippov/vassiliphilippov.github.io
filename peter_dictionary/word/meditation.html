meditation

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of engaging in continuous and deep contemplation or reflection, especially on a profound or abstract subject.

Examples

Their meditations were approaching a violent cusp that would shape Martian art for many millennia.

Synset Examples

the habit of meditation is the basis for all real knowledge

Related Terms

  • Synonym: contemplation - deep reflective thought
  • Synonym: reflection - serious thought or consideration

Etymology

From Latin 'meditatio', from 'meditari' (to contemplate).
