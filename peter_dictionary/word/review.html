/ rɪˈvju / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconsider - to think about (something) again, especially with the possibility of change
  • Synonym: reexamine - to examine again or anew

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman 'revisier', from Latin 'revidēre' (see again).
