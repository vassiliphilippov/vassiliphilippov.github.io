asshole

1. NOUN

/ ˈæsˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɑːs.həʊl / (BrE)
A very offensive and vulgar term for a person who is regarded as obnoxious, foolish, or contemptible.

Examples

Fucking incredible how lucky some assholes get.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - slang for an unpleasant or contemptible person
  • Synonym: bastard - an offensive term for a despicable or unpleasant person
  • Synonym: idiot - a person regarded as stupid or foolish

2. NOUN

/ ˈæsˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɑːs.həʊl / (BrE)
A vulgar slang term for the anus.

Examples

Not one of them born... whose asshole wouldn't pucker up tighter than a snare drum when you asked them for funds.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anus - the opening at the end of the digestive tract through which solid waste leaves the body
