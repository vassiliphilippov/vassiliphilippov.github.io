trapped

1. ADJ

/ træpt / (AmE), / træpt / (BrE)
Unable to escape from a difficult or dangerous situation; forced to face an opponent or threat without the possibility of retreat.

Examples

He removed his hat to let the trapped sweat cut rivulets through the dust film upon his gaunt face.

Synset Examples

a stag at bay
she had me cornered between the porch and her car
like a trapped animal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cornered - trapped with no means of escape, especially when confronted
  • Synonym: at bay - forced to confront attackers, unable to retreat
  • Antonym: free - not confined or trapped
