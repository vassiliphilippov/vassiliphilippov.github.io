booked

1. ADJ

/ bʊkt / (AmE), / bʊkt / (BrE)
(of a place, person, or service) reserved in advance; already arranged to be used or occupied.

Examples

Whole families are moving and removal firms are booked for months ahead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reserved - kept for use by a particular person or group; not available to others
