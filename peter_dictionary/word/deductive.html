deductive

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈdʌktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈdʌktɪv / (BrE)
relating to or using logical deduction, especially reasoning from general principles to reach specific conclusions.

Examples

On the face of it, it is because he employs deductive techniques alien to official police routine.

Synset Examples

deductive reasoning

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inductive - relating to reasoning from specific cases to general conclusions

Etymology

From Latin deducere (to lead down or away).
