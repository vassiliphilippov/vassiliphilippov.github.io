earphone

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪrˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɪəfəʊn / (BrE)
A small device worn over or inside the ear to convert electrical signals into sound, typically used for listening to audio privately.

Examples

His earphones were constantly full of the sounds of enemy contacts made by other flights.

Synset Examples

it was not the typing but the earphones that she disliked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: headphone - pair of earphones worn over both ears
  • Synonym: earbud - small earphone inserted into the ear canal

Etymology

From 'ear' + 'phone', patterned after 'telephone'.
