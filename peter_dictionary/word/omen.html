omen

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.mən / (BrE)
An event or object regarded as a sign that something good or bad is about to happen.

Examples

Was it an omen?

Synset Examples

he looked for an omen before going into battle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: portent - a sign or warning that something momentous is about to happen
  • Synonym: harbinger - a person or thing that announces or signals the approach of another
  • Synonym: sign - an event or object indicative of something to come

Etymology

From Latin omen (foreboding, sign).
