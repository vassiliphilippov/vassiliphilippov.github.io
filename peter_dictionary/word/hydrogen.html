hydrogen

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdrədʒən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrədʒən / (BrE)
A colorless, odorless, highly flammable gas; the lightest and most abundant chemical element, symbol H, used in the production of water and many compounds.
