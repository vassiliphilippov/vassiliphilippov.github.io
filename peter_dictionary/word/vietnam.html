Vietnam

1. PROPN

/ viˈɛt nɑm / (AmE), / viːˈet næm / (BrE)
A country in Southeast Asia on the South China Sea, officially known as the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.

Examples

The Pathet Lao, stiffened by Communist Veterans from neighboring North Viet Nam, were supplied by Soviet aircraft.

Etymology

From Vietnamese 'Việt Nam', meaning 'Viet of the South'.
