coloratura

1. NOUN

/ ˌkʌl.ər.əˈtʊr.ə / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.ər.əˈtjʊə.rə / (BrE)
A lyric soprano singer who specializes in music with elaborate ornamentation and vocal technique.

Examples

Miss Sutherland appeared almost as another person in this scene: A much more girlish Lucia, a sensational coloratura who ran across stage while singing, and an actress immersed in her role.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soprano - a high female singing voice; or a singer with such a voice

Etymology

From Italian 'coloratura' (ornamentation), from 'colorare' (to color).
