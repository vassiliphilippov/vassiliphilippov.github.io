loveliness

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌvlinəs / (AmE), / ˈlʌvlinəs / (BrE)
The quality of being beautiful and attractive.

Examples

Pausing in the doorway he said: "The form of the human female, unlike her mind and her spirit, is the most challenging loveliness in all nature ".
There will be thousands who will thrill to the loveliness of Alastor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - a quality that gives pleasure to the senses or exalts the mind or spirit

Etymology

From 'lovely' + '-ness', forming a noun from the adjective 'lovely'.
