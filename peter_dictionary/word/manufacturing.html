manufacturing

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmæn.jʊˈfæk.tʃər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making products from raw materials, especially in factories

Examples

We have already witnessed great changes through mergers and acquisitions in the food industry - at both the manufacturing and retail ends.
The company grew out of efforts by two completely inexperienced men in their late twenties, neither having a formal education applicable to, or experience in, manufacturing or selling our type of articles.

Synset Examples

the synthesis and fabrication of single crystals
an improvement in the manufacture of explosives
manufacturing is vital to Great Britain

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fabrication - the making or construction of something, especially in industry
  • Synonym: production - the process of making or growing something for sale or use

Etymology

From manufacture + -ing; 'manufacture' from Latin *manu factus* (made by hand).
