retie

1. VERB

/ ˌriːˈtaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈtaɪ / (BrE)
to tie something again or in a new way

Examples

David finished shaving, washed his face clean of lather, and combed and retied his hair.

Synset Examples

retie the string and make it strong enough now

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tie - to fasten with a cord, rope, or string

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 're-' (again) and 'tie.'
