whinny

1. VERB

/ ˈwɪni / (AmE), / ˈwɪni / (BrE)
(of a horse) to make a gentle, high-pitched neighing sound.

Examples

Once more, Katie reared, and whinnied in fear.
Violet whinnied from the stable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neigh - to make the loud, characteristic sound of a horse

Etymology

Perhaps imitative in origin, reflecting the sound made by a horse.
