slime

1. VERB

/ slaɪm / (AmE), / slaɪm / (BrE)
to cover or stain with slime or a slimy substance

Examples

The plank floor was slimed beneath Watson's boots.

Synset Examples

The snake slimed his victim

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slime - a moist, soft, and slippery substance, typically regarded as repulsive
