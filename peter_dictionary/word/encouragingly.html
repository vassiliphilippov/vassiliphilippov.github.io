encouragingly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈkɝːɪdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that gives someone confidence or hope.

Examples

Signora Ferraro, bobbing her head encouragingly, asked Sam about Canada, having a special interest.

Synset Examples

`Go on,' he said encouragingly to his student

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supportively - in a way that shows support
  • Antonym: discouragingly - in a way that discourages or disheartens

Etymology

Formed from 'encouraging' + '-ly'. 'Encouraging' from 'encourage', which is from Old French 'encoragier'.
