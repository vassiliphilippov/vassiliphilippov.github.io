consumption

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌmpʃən / (AmE), / kənˈsʌmpʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act or process of taking food or drink into the body by eating or drinking

Examples

Then they were given 1 2 to 1 - 1 2 avocados per day as a substitute for part of their dietary fat consumption.
Aids in minimizing the occurrence of feed-lot bloat due to high consumption of concentrates.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ingestion - the process of taking food, drink, or another substance into the body by swallowing or absorbing it

Etymology

From Latin consumptio (a consuming, wasting), from consumere (to consume).
