prearrangement

1. NOUN

/ ˌpri.əˈreɪndʒ.mənt / (AmE), / ˌpriː.əˈreɪndʒ.mənt / (BrE)
An arrangement or agreement made in advance; something planned beforehand.

Examples

And I have to make arrangements to bring him back here safely.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: arrangement - something that has been arranged or planned

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'pre-' (before) + 'arrangement'.
