oiled

1. ADJ

/ ɔɪld / (AmE), / ɔɪld / (BrE)
Treated or coated with oil, usually to protect, lubricate, or enhance the appearance of a surface.

Examples

But even before he started across the oiled road to his Plymouth, parked in the lot under the cypress trees across from the station, he knew that he was n't going home.

Synset Examples

oiled country roads
an oiled walnut table

Related Terms

  • Synonym: greased - coated or treated with grease
