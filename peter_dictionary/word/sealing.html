sealing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of applying a substance or material to make something water-resistant or waterproof.

Examples

They had closed over, absolutely, with the sealing of old Izaak's grave.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waterproofing - the process of making something resistant to water

Etymology

From the verb 'seal', meaning to close or secure tightly, extended to treatment processes.
