milestone

1. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪlˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪlˌstəʊn / (BrE)
A stone post by the side of a road, marking distances along the route.

Examples

It was a real stimulant to a lot of guys I know who have moved past the 2 - score year milestone.
On last May 1, the Cardinals stood at 7 - 6, ending a two season fall-off on that milestone.

Etymology

From mile + stone; originally referred to stones placed at intervals of one mile along roads.

2. NOUN

/ ˈmaɪlˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪl.stəʊn / (BrE)
An important event or stage in the progress of development in something, such as a person's life or a project.

Examples

Seen in perspective, many of these releases have a genuine claim to be milestones.

Etymology

Originally referred to a stone set up beside a road to mark the distance in miles; from mile + stone.
