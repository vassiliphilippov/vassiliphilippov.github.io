bestubbled

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈstʌbəld / (AmE), / bɪˈstʌbəld / (BrE)
Having a short, rough growth of beard; covered with stubble.

Examples

Once covertly looking at Simms Purdew, the only man in the world whom he hated, he had seen the heavy, slack, bestubbled jaw open and close to emit the cruel, obscene banter, and had seen the pale blue eyes go watery with whisky and merriment, and suddenly he was not seeing the face of that vile creature.

Synset Examples

his stubbled chin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stubbled - having short, rough bristly hair, especially on the chin

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'be-' (intensive or causative) + 'stubbled' (from 'stubble', meaning short, rough growth).
