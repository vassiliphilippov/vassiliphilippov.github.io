circumference

1. NOUN

/ sərˈkʌm.fər.əns / (AmE), / səˈkʌm.fər.əns / (BrE)
The distance around the edge of a circle or any round object.

Examples

The small and large intestines were filled with gas, and the jejunum was dilated to about 2 times its normal circumference.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perimeter - the continuous line forming the boundary of a closed geometrical figure

Etymology

From Latin 'circumferentia', from 'circumferre' meaning 'to carry around'.
