shaping

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃeɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or activity of giving form, contour, or definition to something

Examples

By this time Henri's entire chest back - lat shoulder area is pumped-up to almost bursting point, and Claude takes time to do a bit more pectoral front deltoid shaping work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formation - the action of forming or process of being formed
  • Synonym: molding - the process of shaping something into a particular form

Etymology

From the verb 'shape' + -ing, forming a verbal noun.
