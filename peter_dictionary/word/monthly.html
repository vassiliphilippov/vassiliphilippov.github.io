monthly

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌnθli / (AmE), / ˈmʌnθli / (BrE)
Happening, done, or paid every month.

Examples

Plus a monthly payment of five percent of the gross of all four hotels, Mr., uh, [EMPHASIZES ITALIAN PRONUNCIATION] Corleone.

Synset Examples

monthly payments
the monthly newsletter

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: weekly - happening every week
  • Degree Higher: yearly - happening every year

Etymology

From month + -ly, forming an adjective meaning recurring every month.
