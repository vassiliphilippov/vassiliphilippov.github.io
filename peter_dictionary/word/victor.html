victor

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪktər / (AmE), / ˈvɪktə / (BrE)
A person who wins a contest or competition; a winner.

Examples

We have a winner!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: winner - a person who wins a contest or competition
  • Antonym: loser - a person who does not win; is defeated in a contest

Etymology

From Latin 'victor' (conqueror, winner), from 'vincere' (to conquer).
