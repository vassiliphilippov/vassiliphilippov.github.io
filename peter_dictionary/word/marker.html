marker

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrkər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkə / (BrE)
an object placed so as to indicate a position, boundary, or route

Examples

Forced to realize that this was the end of a very short line I scanned a road marker and discovered what the end of a slightly longer line would be for the old Mexican: Moriarty, New Mexico.

Synset Examples

the buoys were markers for the channel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign - object used to indicate or convey information
  • Synonym: indicator - object that shows or points out something

Etymology

From mark + -er, meaning something that marks.
