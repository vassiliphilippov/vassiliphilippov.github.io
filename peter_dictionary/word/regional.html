regional

1. ADJ

/ ˈriːdʒənəl / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a particular region; limited to a specific geographic area.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: local - relating to a particular area; nearby
  • Degree Higher: national - pertaining to an entire nation

Etymology

From region + -al, ultimately from Latin regio (region) + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).
