demonstrator

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛmənstreɪtə / (BrE)
a person, especially a teacher or assistant, who demonstrates principles or methods, especially as part of teaching

Examples

School teachers, all too unprepared for the job they must do, will need demonstrators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protester - person who takes part in a public demonstration as a form of protest

Etymology

From demonstrate + -or, modelled after Latin agent nouns.
