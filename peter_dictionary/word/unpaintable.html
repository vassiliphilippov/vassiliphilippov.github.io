unpaintable

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈpeɪntəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪntəbl / (BrE)
Not able to be painted, especially not suitable for artistic representation on canvas.

Examples

"I paint the nothing ", he said once to Franz Kline and myself, "the nothing that is behind the something, the inexpressible, unpaintable' tick' in the unconscious, the' spirit' of the moment resting forever, suspended like a huge balloon, in non time ".

Synset Examples

the inexpressible, unpaintable `tick' in the unconscious

Related Terms

  • Antonym: paintable - able to be painted

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'paintable'.
