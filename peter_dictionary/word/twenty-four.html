2. NUM

/ ˈtwɛnti fɔr / (AmE), / ˈtwenti fɔː / (BrE)
the number 24; the number that is four more than twenty

Examples

The injured German veteran was a former miner, twenty-four years old, who had been wounded by shrapnel in the back of the head.
Twenty-four classrooms in twelve unstructured schools furnished 156 cases, 87 boys and 69 girls.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-three - the number 23; one less than twenty-four
  • Degree Higher: twenty-five - the number 25; one more than twenty-four

Etymology

From 'twenty' (Old English 'twēntig') plus 'four' (Old English 'feower').
