bilingual

1. ADJ

/ baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (AmE), / baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl / (BrE)
Able to use or speak two languages fluently.

Examples

Language was no problem anyway; Mr. Phillips had only to signal from his doorway to summon aid from the ubiquitous bilingual children who played on the sidewalks of Mission Street.

Synset Examples

bilingual education

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monolingual - able to use only one language

Etymology

From Latin 'bi-' (two) + 'lingua' (tongue, language).
