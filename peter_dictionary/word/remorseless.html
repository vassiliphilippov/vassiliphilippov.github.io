remorseless

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈmɔrs.ləs / (AmE), / rɪˈmɔːs.ləs / (BrE)
Showing no pity or compassion; without mercy.

Examples

Sort of remorseless, is n't it?
You strike me as a particularly icy and remorseless man, Mr. Dufresne.

Synset Examples

an act of ruthless ferocity
a monster of remorseless cruelty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitiless - lacking pity or compassion
  • Synonym: merciless - showing no mercy
  • Synonym: ruthless - having no compassion or pity
  • Antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • Antonym: merciful - showing mercy or compassion

Etymology

Formed from 'remorse' + '-less', meaning 'without remorse'.
