belle

1. NOUN

/ bɛl / (AmE), / bɛl / (BrE)
A beautiful and charming young woman, especially one who is admired at a particular event or in a social group.

Examples

An Alabama soldier whose feminine associations were of the more admirable type wrote boastfully of his achievements among the Virginia belles: "They thout I was a saint.

Synset Examples

she was the belle of the ball

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty - a beautiful or attractive person, especially a woman

Etymology

From French 'belle' (beautiful woman), feminine of 'beau' (handsome).
