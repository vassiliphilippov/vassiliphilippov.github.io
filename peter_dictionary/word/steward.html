steward

1. NOUN

/ ˈstuərd / (AmE), / ˈstjuːəd / (BrE)
A person who manages property, finances, or affairs for another person or organization.

Examples

Prior to the featured race, the stewards announced that apprentice James P. Verrone is suspended ten days for crowding horses and crossing the field sharply in two races on Wednesday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: administrator - person responsible for managing or supervising the affairs of a business, institution, etc.
  • Synonym: manager - person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff

Etymology

From Old English 'stiweard', from stig (hall, household) + weard (guardian).
