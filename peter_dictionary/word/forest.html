1. NOUN

Related Terms

  • Synonym: woodland - land covered with trees; woods
  • Degree Higher: jungle - an area of land densely covered with tropical vegetation
  • Degree Lower: copse - a small group of trees; a thicket

Etymology

2. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɒr.ɪst / (BrE)
A large area of land covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth.

Examples

Related Terms

Etymology

From Old French *forest*, from Medieval Latin *forestis* (open wood), possibly of Germanic origin.
