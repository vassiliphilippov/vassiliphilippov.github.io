misread

1. VERB

/ mɪsˈriːd / (AmE), / mɪsˈriːd / (BrE)
To read or interpret something incorrectly.

Examples

"I 'm not worried about you mis-reading the dials, Nogol, just about a lug like you reading them at all.

Synset Examples

He misread the data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misinterpret - to interpret incorrectly
  • Antonym: read - to look at and comprehend the meaning of written or printed matter

Etymology

From prefix 'mis-' (wrongly) + 'read'.
