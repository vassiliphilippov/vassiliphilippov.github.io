picturing

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃəɹɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of creating a visual representation, especially by painting or photography.

Examples

A word taken in its dictionary meaning, a photographic image of a recognizable object, the mere picturing of a "scene "tends to lose experiential vividness and to connote such conventional abstractions as to invite neutral reception without the incitement of value feelings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: depiction - the action of depicting or representing something visually

Etymology

From the verb 'picture' (to represent in a picture) plus the suffix '-ing'.
