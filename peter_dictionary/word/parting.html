parting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of leaving or saying goodbye to someone, especially in a polite or emotional way.

Examples

There is a death in all partings, she knew, and promptly put it out of her mind.
They were both so young, after all, so unready for any final parting.

Synset Examples

he disliked long farewells
he took his leave
parting is such sweet sorrow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell - an act of saying goodbye
  • Synonym: departure - the act of leaving
  • Antonym: reunion - an instance of coming together again after separation

Etymology

From Middle English 'parten' (to divide, separate), from Old French 'partir', from Latin 'partīre' (to divide).
