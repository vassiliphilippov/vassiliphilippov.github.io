indelible

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɛləbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛlɪbəl / (BrE)
(of marks, memories, or impressions) unable to be removed, washed away, or erased.

Examples

For the last two years, this frontier of the arts has produced a number of so-called "non dramas "which have left indelible, bittersweet impressions on the psyche of this veteran theatregoer.

Synset Examples

an indelible stain
indelible memories

Related Terms

  • Antonym: erasable - able to be erased or removed

Etymology

From Latin 'indelibilis': in- (not) + delibilis (that can be destroyed or deleted).
