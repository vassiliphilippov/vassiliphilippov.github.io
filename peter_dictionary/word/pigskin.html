pigskin

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪɡˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡ.skɪn / (BrE)
leather made from the skin of a pig, used for gloves, balls, and other goods

Examples

Then he stood back to look at Mr. Jack, who was pulling on his pigskin gloves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leather - material made from the skin of animals, especially cattle, that has been treated to preserve it

Etymology

Formed from 'pig' and 'skin', referring to leather made from pig hide.
