roster

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɑːstər / (AmE), / ˈrɒstə / (BrE)
A list of people's names, especially in relation to their duties, membership, or participation in a group, team, or organization.

Examples

Moritz is listed on the Longhorn roster as a right halfback, the position at which he lettered on the 1956 team.
There the matter stands with the prospect that soon Manchester may be removed from the roster of towns contributing raw sewage to its main streams.

Synset Examples

his name was struck off the rolls

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roll - an official list or register of names, especially of members of a group

Etymology

From Dutch 'rooster' (list, table), literally 'gridiron', from 'roosten' (to roast).
