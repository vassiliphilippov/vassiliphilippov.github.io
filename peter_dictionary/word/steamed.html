steamed

1. ADJ

/ stiːmd / (AmE), / stiːmd / (BrE)
(of food) cooked by means of steam rather than by boiling or baking.

Examples

I put a little scoop of pulverized phosphate rock or steamed bone meal into each hole with the plant.

Synset Examples

steamed clams

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boiled - (of food) cooked by being immersed in boiling water
  • Antonym: baked - (of food) cooked by dry heat, usually in an oven
