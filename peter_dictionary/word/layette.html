layette

1. NOUN

/ leɪˈɛt / (AmE), / leɪˈɛt / (BrE)
a complete set of clothes and accessories for a newborn baby

Examples

When we separated that evening Pat pushed a hundred dollar bill into Eileen's hand to help towards a layette.

Etymology

From French 'layette' (a chest of drawers, trousseau for a baby).
