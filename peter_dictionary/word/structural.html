2. ADJ

/ ˈstrʌktʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈstrʌktʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the structure or construction of something rather than its decoration or function.

Examples

Discovering that draperies could be designed to serve structural purposes, he began a study of the anatomy of folds.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: decorative - intended to make something look attractive rather than have a function

Etymology

From Latin 'structura' (a fitting together, building).
