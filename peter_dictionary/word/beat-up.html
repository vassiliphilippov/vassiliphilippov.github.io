beat-up

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːt ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbiːt ˌʌp / (BrE)
In a poor condition because of hard use or having been damaged by blows.

Examples

The Champs Elysees is literally littered this summer with the prostrate bodies of France's beat-up beatnik jeunes filles.

Synset Examples

a battered old car
the beaten-up old Ford

Related Terms

  • Synonym: battered - damaged by repeated blows or rough treatment
