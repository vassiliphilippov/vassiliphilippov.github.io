dressy

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrɛsi / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsi / (BrE)
(of clothes or appearance) suitable for formal or special occasions; smart or elegant.

Examples

Stacked heels are also popular on dressy or tailored shoes.
At the left is a pair of dressy straw pumps in a light, but crisp texture.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: casual - (of clothes or appearance) suitable for everyday wear, not formal
  • Synonym: formal - suitable for official or important occasions; conventional
