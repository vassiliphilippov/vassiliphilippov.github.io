quarrelsome

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɔr.əlsəm / (AmE), / ˈkwɒr.əlsəm / (BrE)
Tending to argue or start disagreements frequently.

Examples

The subsequent two-weeks wait made the crew quarrelsome.

Synset Examples

arguing children
quarrelsome when drinking

Related Terms

  • Synonym: argumentative - prone to argue or dispute
  • Antonym: peaceful - not inclined to quarrel or fight

Etymology

Formed from 'quarrel' + suffix '-some', meaning characterized by.
