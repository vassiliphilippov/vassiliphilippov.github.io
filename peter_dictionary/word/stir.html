stir

1. VERB

/ stɝː / (AmE), / stɜː / (BrE)
To cause strong feelings or emotions in someone; to provoke or arouse an emotional response.

Examples

Well, Jerebohm and his wife Pinkie have reached the stage of affluence that stirs a longing for the more atrociously expensive rustic simplicities.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excite - to arouse strong feelings, especially enthusiasm or eagerness
  • Antonym: calm - to soothe or make peaceful, the opposite of stirring up feelings
