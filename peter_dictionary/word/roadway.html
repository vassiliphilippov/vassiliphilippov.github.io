roadway

1. NOUN

/ ˈroʊdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌweɪ / (BrE)
the part of a road intended for vehicles to travel on

Examples

Less ambitious freeway plans may be more successful - especially when the roadways and interchanges are raised, allowing for cross access at many points and providing parking areas below the ramp.
On unoccupied roadway the bottle shattered into a small amber flash.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carriageway - the part of a road intended for vehicles (chiefly British)
  • Antonym: shoulder - the part of a road outside the main roadway, used for stopping or emergencies
