/ ˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪmpəˈθetɪk / (BrE)
relating to or controlled by the sympathetic nervous system, which is part of the autonomic nervous system responsible for the body's rapid involuntary response to stressful situations.

Etymology
