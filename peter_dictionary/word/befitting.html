befitting

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Appropriate or suitable for a particular person, occasion, or situation.

Examples

Perhaps we can find something more... befitting a man of your education.

Synset Examples

behavior befitting a father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appropriate - suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • Antonym: unsuitable - not appropriate or fitting

Etymology

From the prefix 'be-' and the verb 'fit', meaning to be suitable.
