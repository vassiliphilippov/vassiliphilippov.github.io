lopsidedly

1. ADV

/ lɑpˈsaɪdɪdli / (AmE), / lɒpˈsaɪdɪdli / (BrE)
In an uneven, asymmetrical, or crooked manner.

Examples

There was the squat loose jowled man, smiling lopsidedly up at him.

Synset Examples

he smiled lopsidedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crookedly - in a crooked or twisted manner
  • Antonym: evenly - in a level or symmetrical manner

Etymology

From 'lopsided' + '-ly', with 'lopsided' likely from 'lop' (to hang loosely) and 'side'.
