topographical

1. ADJ

/ ˌtɑːpəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌtɒpəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the arrangement or physical features of an area, especially in maps or surveys.

Examples

The new editions of topographic maps being made by the federal government are excellent for orienting yourself to the natural features of the site.

Synset Examples

a topographical engineer
a topographical survey
topographic maps

Related Terms

  • Synonym: topographic - alternative form meaning relating to topography

Etymology

From Greek topos (place) + -graphia (writing, description); see topography.
