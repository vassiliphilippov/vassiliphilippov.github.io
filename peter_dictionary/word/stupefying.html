stupefying

1. ADJ

/ ˈstuːpəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely impressive, surprising, or astonishing to the point of causing shock or amazement.

Examples

From what I was able to gauge in a swift, greedy glance, the figure inside the coral colored boucle dress was stupefying.

Synset Examples

such an enormous response was astonishing
an astounding achievement
the amount of money required was staggering
suffered a staggering defeat
the figure inside the boucle dress was stupefying

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - very surprising; difficult to believe
  • Synonym: astounding - surprisingly impressive or notable
  • Synonym: staggering - deeply shocking; astonishing
  • Antonym: underwhelming - not impressive, disappointing

Etymology

From the verb 'stupefy', ultimately from Latin 'stupefacere' (to stun, make senseless).
