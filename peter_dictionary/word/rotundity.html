rotundity

1. NOUN

/ roʊˈtʌndəti / (AmE), / rəʊˈtʌndɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being round or spherical in shape

Examples

He expected Concetta's thin hand to reach down to grasp the boy, and her shrill, impetuous voice to sound against the rotundity of his disfigured flesh that was never sure of hearing anything.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sphericity - the quality of being like a sphere

Etymology

From Latin 'rotunditas' ('roundness'), from 'rotundus' ('round').
