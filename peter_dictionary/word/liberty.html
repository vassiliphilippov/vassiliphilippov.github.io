liberty

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪbərti / (AmE), / ˈlɪbəti / (BrE)
The state of being free within society from oppressive restrictions imposed by authority on one's way of life, behavior, or political views; political independence.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: freedom - the power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants without hindrance or restraint
  • Antonym: oppression - prolonged cruel or unjust treatment or control

Etymology

From Latin 'libertas' (freedom), from 'liber' (free).
