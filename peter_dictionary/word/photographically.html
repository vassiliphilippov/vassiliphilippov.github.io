photographically

1. ADV

/ ˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
By means of photography; using or relating to photographic methods.

Examples

If the photographically realistic continuity of dreams, however bizarre their combinations, denies that it is purely a composition of the brain, it must be compounded from views of diverse realities, although some of them may never be encountered in what we are pleased to call the real life.

Synset Examples

photographically recorded scenes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: photographic - relating to photography

Etymology

Formed from 'photographic' + '-ally', the adverbial suffix.
