thinly

1. ADV

/ ˈθɪnli / (AmE), / ˈθɪnli / (BrE)
With little force or sincerity; in an insincere or unenthusiastic way.

Examples

He smiled thinly, savoring his joke.

Synset Examples

smiled thinly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insincerely - without genuine feeling or sincerity
  • Antonym: heartily - with sincere enthusiasm or feeling
