finicky

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɪnɪki / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪki / (BrE)
Very fussy or excessively particular, especially about details or small requirements.

Examples

Some of Curzon's playing strikes me as finicky, however.

Synset Examples

a finicky eater
fussy about clothes
very particular about how her food was prepared

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fussy - excessively concerned with details or cleanliness
  • Synonym: fastidious - very attentive to detail or accuracy
  • Antonym: easygoing - relaxed and unconcerned

Etymology

Probably from 'finical', itself a derivative of 'fine'.
