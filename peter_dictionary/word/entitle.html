entitle

1. VERB

/ ɪnˈtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪtəl / (BrE)
to give someone the right to have or do something

Synset Examples

The Freedom of Information Act entitles you to request your FBI file

Related Terms

  • Synonym: authorize - to give official permission for something
  • Antonym: disqualify - to take away someone's legal right or eligibility

Etymology

From Old French 'entituler', from Latin 'titulus' (title).
