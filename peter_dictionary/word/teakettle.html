teakettle

1. NOUN

/ ˈtiːˌkɛtəl / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌkɛtəl / (BrE)
A kettle with a spout, used for boiling water, especially for making tea.

Examples

They found the teakettle and put water on to boil and then searched through the icebox.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kettle - a container for boiling water

Etymology

From 'tea' + 'kettle', referring to a kettle used for boiling water for tea.
