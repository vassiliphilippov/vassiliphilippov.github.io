overactive

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈæktɪv / (BrE)
More active than is normal or desirable.

Examples

It is low when the thyroid is overactive and high when the gland is sluggish.

Synset Examples

a hyperactive child

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hyperactive - excessively or abnormally active

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessive) + 'active'.
