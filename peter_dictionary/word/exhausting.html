exhausting

1. ADJ

/ ɪɡˈzɔstɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋ / (BrE)
Making you feel extremely tired; causing exhaustion.

Examples

Would not the emotional catharsis eventually brought on by this awfulness have a calming, if not exhausting, effect likely to improve his client's chances?

Synset Examples

an exhausting job in the hot sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tiring - causing tiredness or fatigue
  • Antonym: invigorating - making one feel stronger and more energetic

Etymology

From the verb 'exhaust,' from Latin 'exhaurire' (to draw out, drain).
