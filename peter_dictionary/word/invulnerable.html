invulnerable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvʌln(ə)rəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈvʌln(ə)rəbl / (BrE)
Impossible to harm or damage; immune to attack or injury.

Examples

So when Sollozzo is being guarded, he's invulnerable.

Synset Examples

gunners raked the beach from invulnerable positions on the cliffs

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vulnerable - able to be easily harmed or attacked

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + vulnerabilis (able to be wounded), from vulnus (wound).
