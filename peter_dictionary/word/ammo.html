ammo

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈaməʊ / (BrE)
Informal abbreviation for ammunition; bullets, shells, or other projectiles fired from a gun.

Examples

Warren and White each carried, in addition to their own weapons and ammo, a box of ammo for the ROK's machine gun.
Warren and White each carried, in addition to their own weapons and ammo, a box of ammo for the ROK's machine gun.
"Give me your machine gun ammo ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ammunition - full form; projectiles such as bullets and shells fired from guns

Etymology

Shortened form of 'ammunition' (19th century).
