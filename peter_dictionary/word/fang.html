fang

1. NOUN

/ fæŋ / (AmE), / fæŋ / (BrE)
a long, pointed tooth of a carnivorous animal, used for seizing and tearing prey

Examples

You should have put more gore under the fangs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: canine - a pointed tooth between the incisors and premolars

Etymology

From Old Norse *fang* (“grasp, catch”).
