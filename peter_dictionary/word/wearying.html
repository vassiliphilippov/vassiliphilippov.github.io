wearying

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪəriɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing tiredness or exhaustion; making someone feel physically or mentally fatigued.

Examples

It was all too wearying.

Synset Examples

an exhausting march
the visit was especially wearing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausting - causing complete exhaustion
  • Antonym: energizing - giving energy or vitality

Etymology

From 'weary' + '-ing', where 'weary' is from Old English 'wērig' (tired).
