two-year

1. ADJ

/ tuː jɪr / (AmE), / tʃuː jɪə / (BrE)
(of a plant) having a life cycle that lasts for two seasons; biennial.

Examples

Tommy Williams came to Shawshank in 1965 on a two-year stretch for B&E.

Synset Examples

a biennial life cycle
parsnips and carrots are biennial plants often grown as annuals

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biennial - (of a plant) living or lasting for two years
