nighted

1. ADJ

/ ˈnaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtɪd / (BrE)
(literary) Overtaken by night or enveloped in darkness.

Examples

Another body length or all the rest of his nighted life?

Synset Examples

benighted (or nighted) travelers hurrying toward home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: benighted - in a state of darkness or ignorance; overtaken by night

Etymology

From 'night' + '-ed', used in literary English.
