accenting

1. NOUN

/ ˈæk.sɛn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæk.səntɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving special emphasis or prominence to something

Examples

Then I plan my attack: the parts I will finish first, the range of values, the accenting of minor details - all in all, mechanics of producing the finished job with a maximum of crispness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emphasizing - the act of stressing importance or significance
  • Antonym: de-emphasizing - the act of reducing importance or prominence

Etymology

From 'accent' + '-ing', where 'accent' is from Latin 'accentus' (tone, intonation).
