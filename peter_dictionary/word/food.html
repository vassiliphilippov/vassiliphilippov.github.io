food

1. NOUN

/ fuːd / (AmE), / fuːd / (BrE)
Any substance that people or animals eat or drink, or that plants absorb, in order to maintain life and growth.

Examples

Seeing so many illustrations and reading so many testimonials to the value of Quick-Wate and Super-Protein, those two wonder-working Weider food supplements, he decided to try them and see what they could do for him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nourishment - food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition
  • Antonym: starvation - state of extreme hunger from lack of food

Etymology

From Old English 'fōda', related to 'fēdan' (to feed); of Germanic origin.
