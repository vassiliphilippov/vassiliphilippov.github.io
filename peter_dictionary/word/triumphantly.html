triumphantly

1. ADV

/ traɪˈʌmfəntli / (AmE), / traɪˈʌmfəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great joy, pride, or satisfaction as a result of a victory or achievement.

Examples

Stevie was shouting triumphantly.
As they merged, grokking together, Mike said softly and triumphantly: "Thou art God ".
He nodded triumphantly.
Then they had always been romping around him on these walks, yelping with delight, dashing off into the bushes on fruitless hunting expeditions, returning to jump up on him triumphantly with muddy paws.

Synset Examples

she shouted triumphantly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: victoriously - in a way that shows having won a victory

Etymology

From 'triumphant' + '-ly'; 'triumphant' from Latin 'triumphus' (a victory procession).
