bludgeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈblʌdʒən / (AmE), / ˈblʌdʒən / (BrE)
a thick stick with a heavy end, used as a weapon

Examples

On Election Day - November 4 - he energetically marshalled his force of bludgeon men, bribers, and experts in forging repeat votes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: club - a heavy stick used as a weapon

Etymology

Of uncertain origin, perhaps related to 'bludge,' an old dialect word meaning 'to hit'.
