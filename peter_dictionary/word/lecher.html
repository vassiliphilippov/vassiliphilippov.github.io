lecher

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛtʃər / (AmE), / ˈlɛtʃə / (BrE)
A man who shows excessive or offensive sexual desire.

Examples

Now Pat may have been a lecher and a plug-ugly, but he was a good churchgoing Catholic and he loved his little sister.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lech - a man who behaves in a lecherous manner
  • Synonym: satyr - a man who behaves lustfully, after the mythological creature
  • Antonym: prude - a person excessively proper or modest about sex

Etymology

From Old French *lecheor* (licker, lecher), from *lecher* (to lick), ultimately from Latin *lĭcĕre* (to lick).
