ill-equipped

1. ADJ

/ ɪl ɪˈkwɪpt / (AmE), / ɪl ɪˈkwɪpt / (BrE)
lacking sufficient or appropriate equipment for a particular purpose.

Examples

The school was small, dark and ill-equipped.

Synset Examples

the school was ill-equipped

Related Terms

  • Antonym: well-equipped - having sufficient or the necessary equipment
