virile

1. ADJ

/ ˈvɪrəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪərʌɪl / (BrE)
having qualities traditionally associated with adult men, such as strength, vigor, and masculinity.

Examples

They saw completely masculine and obviously virile men performing with incredible grace.

Synset Examples

a deep male voice
manly sports

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masculine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with men
  • Antonym: feminine - having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with women

Etymology

From Latin virilis (of a man, manly), from vir (man).

2. ADJ

/ ˈvɪrəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪərʌɪl / (BrE)
Having or showing strength, energy, or vigour; typically associated with qualities regarded as manly.

Examples

One need not waver in his belief in virile law enforcement to insist that there are other things in American life which are also of great importance, and to which even law enforcement must accommodate itself.

Synset Examples

a virile and ever stronger free society
a new and virile leadership

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vigorous - strong, healthy, and full of energy
  • Antonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly

Etymology

From Latin 'virilis' (of a man, masculine), from 'vir' (man).
