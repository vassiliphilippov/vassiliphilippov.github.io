hygiene

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdʒin / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdʒiːn / (BrE)
Conditions or practices aimed at maintaining health and preventing disease, especially through cleanliness.

Examples

Foods, alcohol, or beauty and hygiene products?

Synset Examples

personal hygiene

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sanitation - measures designed to protect public health, especially the provision of clean drinking water and adequate sewage disposal

Etymology

From French *hygiène*, from Greek *hygieinē* (art of health).
