bachelor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætʃələr / (AmE), / ˈbætʃələ / (BrE)
a man who has never been married

Examples

Michelangelo hurried to Sangallo's solitary bachelor room with his sketches, asked the architect to design a stand which would simulate the seated Madonna.
A nice bachelor apartment in a place called the Lancaster Arms ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spinster - an unmarried woman, typically an older one
  • Antonym: husband - a married man

Etymology

From Old French *bacheler* (knight in training), later signifying an unmarried man.
