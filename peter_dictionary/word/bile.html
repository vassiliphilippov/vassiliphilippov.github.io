bile

1. NOUN

/ baɪl / (AmE), / baɪl / (BrE)
A yellowish-green fluid produced by the liver and stored in the gallbladder that helps digest fats in the small intestine.

Examples

The gallbladder contained about 40 cc. of green brown bile and 3 smooth, dark-green calculi measuring up to 1 cm. in diameter.

Etymology

From Latin *bilis* (bile, gall).
