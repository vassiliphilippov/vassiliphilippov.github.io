henhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛnˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈhenhaʊs / (BrE)
A building on a farm where chickens or other poultry are kept.

Examples

Well, Miss Vale... ...another rooster in the hen-house?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chicken coop - an enclosure or building for keeping chickens

Etymology

Formed from 'hen' (female chicken) + 'house'.
