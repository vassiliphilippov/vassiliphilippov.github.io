outnumber

1. VERB

/ aʊtˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / aʊtˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
to be greater in number than someone or something else

Examples

The consequence of this is that the girls at Brooklyn College outnumber the boys and do somewhat better academically.
On these bonds, price rises since February 21 easily outnumber price declines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outweigh - be heavier or more important than something else

Etymology

From the prefix 'out-' meaning 'surpassing' and 'number'.
