useable

1. ADJ

/ ˈjuːzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈjuːzəbl / (BrE)
able to be used; fit or ready for use or service

Examples

Lack of rainfall and extreme temperatures may call for the development of shade and irrigation of a site to make it useable.

Synset Examples

the toaster was still functional even after being dropped
the lawnmower is a bit rusty but still usable
an operational aircraft
the dishwasher is now in working order

Related Terms

  • Synonym: usable - able to be used; alternative, more common spelling
  • Antonym: unusable - not able to be used

Etymology

From 'use' + '-able', patterned after Latin-derived formations.
