harmless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrm.ləs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːm.ləs / (BrE)
Not able or likely to cause harm or injury.

Examples

"Everybody knows I'm just a harmless, deaf old man who takes to drink.
They wouldn't be friendly long if I was involved in drugs instead of gambling, which they regard as a harmless vice, but drugs is a dirty business.

Synset Examples

harmless bacteria
rendered the bomb harmless

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dangerous - able or likely to cause harm or injury

Etymology

From 'harm' + '-less', with '-less' meaning 'without'.
