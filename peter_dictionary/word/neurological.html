neurological

1. ADJ

/ nuˌɑːrəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / njuˌɒrəlˈɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the science of neurology or the nervous system.

Examples

There are many possibilities, including poor circulation, a variety of neurological conditions, and functional disorders.

Synset Examples

neurological evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neurologic - relating to neurology or the nervous system (alternative form)

Etymology

From neurology + -ical, ultimately from Greek neuron (nerve) and -logia (study).
