TSH

1. NOUN

/ ti ɛs eɪtʃ / (AmE), / tiː ɛs eɪtʃ / (BrE)
Anterior pituitary hormone that stimulates the function and activity of the thyroid gland; abbreviation of thyroid-stimulating hormone.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thyroid-stimulating hormone - full form of TSH; hormone from pituitary stimulating thyroid

Etymology
