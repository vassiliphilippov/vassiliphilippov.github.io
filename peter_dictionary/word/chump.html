chump

1. NOUN

/ tʃʌmp / (AmE), / tʃʌmp / (BrE)
An easily fooled or gullible person; someone who is easy to take advantage of.

Examples

And you know you will always wonder all of your life whether it was because you wanted him so bad that you did n't get him, and you can feel nearly sorry enough to cry when you think of that other guy, the chump who begged you to marry him, the one with the plastered hair and the car he could n't afford and the too shiny shoes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dupe - a person who is easily deceived or tricked
  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: sucker - a gullible or naive person who is easily deceived

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from British slang meaning 'head' or 'block' in the mid-19th century.
