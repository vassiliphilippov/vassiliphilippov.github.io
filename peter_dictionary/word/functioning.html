functioning

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
working or able to operate as intended; performing its normal or intended role.

Examples

He had assumed that all these buildings had been divided into apartments, but this one, from a glance at the hall furnishings, was obviously still a functioning town house, and its owners were in residence; that made it doubtful as the hiding place of a man whose plans had to be made in advance.
I am the world's first fully-functioning homicidal artist.

Synset Examples

a functioning flashlight

Related Terms

  • Antonym: broken - not functioning; not working as intended

Etymology

From 'function' + '-ing', used as an adjective.
