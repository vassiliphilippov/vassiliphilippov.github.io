elucidate

1. VERB

/ ɪˈluːsɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt / (BrE)
to make something clear; to explain or clarify, especially by providing further details

Examples

The fundamental differences between physiological and pathological states of parasympathetic (and also of sympathetic) dominance remain to be elucidated.

Synset Examples

clarify the mystery surrounding her death

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarify - to make clear or easier to understand

Etymology

From Latin 'elucidare', from 'e-' (out) + 'lucidus' (clear, bright).
