weather

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛðər / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
The state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time, including conditions such as temperature, wind, clouds, and precipitation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: climate - the general or average weather conditions of a region over a long period

Etymology

From Old English 'weder' (air, sky, breeze, storm), related to German 'Wetter'.
