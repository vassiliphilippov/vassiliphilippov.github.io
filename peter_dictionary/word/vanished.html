vanished

1. ADJ

/ ˈvænɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈvanɪʃt / (BrE)
Having passed out of existence; no longer present or existing.

Examples

When we arrive at the events concerned in the vanished majority, they, of course, cannot impress us as anything familiar.

Synset Examples

vanished civilizations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extinct - no longer in existence (esp. species, traditions, etc.)
  • Antonym: existent - still existing or present

Etymology

Past participle of 'vanish', from Old French 'esvanir', from Latin 'evanescere' (to disappear).
