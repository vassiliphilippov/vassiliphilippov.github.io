Scotchman

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɑtʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈskɒtʃˌmæn / (BrE)
an archaic term for a native or inhabitant of Scotland

Examples

It would have been unwise policy, for instance, to apply the pound of flesh characterization to the thrifty Scotchman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Scot - a native or inhabitant of Scotland
  • Synonym: Scotsman - a male native or inhabitant of Scotland

Etymology

From Scotch, an earlier English word for Scottish, plus man.
