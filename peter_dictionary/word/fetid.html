fetid

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪd / (BrE)
Having a strong, unpleasant, and offensive smell.

Examples

The man wheezed weakly, his fetid breath beating softly against Watson's neck.

Synset Examples

a foul odor
the kitchen smelled really funky

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malodorous - having an unpleasant smell
  • Antonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell

Etymology

From Latin 'fetidus' meaning 'stinking'.
