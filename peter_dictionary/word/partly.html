partly

1. ADV

/ ˈpɑrtli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtli / (BrE)
To some extent; not completely or entirely.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partially - to some degree; not wholly
  • Antonym: entirely - completely; wholly
  • Antonym: completely - totally; with nothing missing

Etymology

From 'part' + '-ly', modeled after adverbial formation.
