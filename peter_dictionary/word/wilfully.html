wilfully

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪlfəli / (AmE), / ˈwɪlfʊli / (BrE)
In a willful manner; deliberately and intentionally, especially in defiance of rules or expectations.

Examples

She would n't be wilfully planning his destruction.

Synset Examples

she had willfully deceived me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: willfully - in a willful manner; deliberately and intentionally

Etymology

Alternative British spelling of 'willfully,' from 'willful' + '-ly.'
