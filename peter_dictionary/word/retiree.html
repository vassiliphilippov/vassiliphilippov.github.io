retiree

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌtaɪˈri / (AmE), / rɪˌtaɪˈriː / (BrE)
A person who has retired from work, usually because of age.

Examples

For some retired persons, part-time farming is a good way to supplement retirement income.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pensioner - a person who receives a pension, often after retirement

Etymology

Formed from 'retire' + '-ee', denoting the person affected by the action.
