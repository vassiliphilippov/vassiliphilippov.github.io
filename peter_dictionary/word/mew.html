mew

1. VERB

/ mju / (AmE), / mjuÀê / (BrE)
(of a cat) to make a soft crying sound; to meow.

Examples

The men mewed and scratched, begging to be taken away.

Synset Examples

the cat meowed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meow - to make the characteristic crying sound of a cat

Etymology

Imitative of a cat's cry; from Old French miauler, ultimately of echoic origin.
