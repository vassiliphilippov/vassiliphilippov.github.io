windbag

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌbaɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who talks too much, especially in a dull or tedious way

Examples

He's just a big windbag ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blowhard - a person who boasts or brags a lot
  • Synonym: bore - a person who talks too much about uninteresting things
  • Antonym: listener - a person who listens rather than talks

Etymology

Formed from 'wind' (suggesting empty talk) and 'bag' (as in container); first recorded in 19th century English.
