delegation

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɛləˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
A group of people officially chosen or appointed to represent others, especially at a conference or meeting.

Examples

Barber, who is in his 13th year as a legislator, said there "are some members of our congressional delegation in Washington who would like to see it (the resolution) passed ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deputation - A group of people appointed to undertake a mission or represent others, especially officially.

Etymology

From Latin delegatio(n-), from delegare (to send as a representative).
