colossal

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlɑsəl / (AmE), / kəˈlɒsəl / (BrE)
Extremely large in size, amount, or degree; so great as to elicit awe.

Etymology

From French 'colossal', from Italian 'colossale', from 'colosso' (colossus), ultimately from Ancient Greek 'kolossos'.
