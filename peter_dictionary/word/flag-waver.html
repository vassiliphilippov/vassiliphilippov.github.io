flag-waver

1. NOUN

/ flæɡ ˈweɪvər / (AmE), / flæɡ ˈweɪvə / (BrE)
A person who shows fervent or aggressive support for their country, often to an extreme or unquestioning degree; an extreme nationalist.

Examples

On the other we have the flag wavers and the national sentimentalists who have been willing to use any patriotic, "frontier western "or colonial material willy-nilly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chauvinist - a person displaying aggressive or exaggerated patriotism
  • Degree Lower: patriot - a person who strongly supports their country

Etymology

From 'flag' + 'waver', referring to waving a national flag as a display of patriotism.
