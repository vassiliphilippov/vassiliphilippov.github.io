unasked

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈæst / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːskt / (BrE)
Not requested or invited; not asked for.

Examples

If and when Hino decided to tell him about his experiences, he would do so unasked.
Indeed, there was a woman who, unasked, had already given him love.

Synset Examples

unasked advice
unsolicited junk mail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsolicited - not asked for or sought; given or supplied without request
