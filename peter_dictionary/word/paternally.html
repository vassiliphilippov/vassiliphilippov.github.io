paternally

1. ADV

/ pəˈtɜrnəli / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of or befitting a father; in a fatherly way.

Examples

He patted Angelo's thin shoulder paternally.

Synset Examples

he behaves very paternally toward his young bride

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatherly - showing the care, affection, or authority of a father
  • Antonym: maternally - in a motherly manner

Etymology

From 'paternal' (from Latin 'paternus', from 'pater' meaning 'father') with the adverbial suffix '-ly'.
