Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: frequently - at frequent intervals; regularly
  • Antonym: rarely - not often; seldom
  • Degree Higher: always - at all times; on every occasion
  • Degree Lower: sometimes - on some occasions but not always or often

Etymology

From Middle English *oftin*, from Old English *oft* (often) + -en (adverbial suffix).
