stepchild

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛpˌtʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌtʃaɪld / (BrE)
A child of one's spouse from a previous marriage or relationship.

Examples

Debate is not likely to resolve the tensions and make the lot of the stepchild a happier one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stepson - a male stepchild; a son of one's spouse from a previous marriage
  • Synonym: stepdaughter - a female stepchild; a daughter of one's spouse from a previous marriage

Etymology

From 'step-' (from Old English 'steop-', used in family terms to denote kinship by remarriage) + 'child'.
