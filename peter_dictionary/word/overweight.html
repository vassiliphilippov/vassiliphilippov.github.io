overweight

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈweɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈweɪt / (BrE)
Weighing more than is considered healthy or normal, especially for one's height or age.

Examples

If, however, the child is weak, overweight, or afraid, more help will be needed.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: obese - very overweight, with excessive body fat
  • Antonym: underweight - weighing less than is considered healthy or normal

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (exceeding) + 'weight'.
