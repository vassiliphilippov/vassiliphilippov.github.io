pathless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæθ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːθ.ləs / (BrE)
(of a place) having no paths or tracks; lacking any marked or established route.

Examples

He had picked out this pathless trail, instead of the common one, in a moment of romantic fancy, to give them privacy on their honeymoon.

Synset Examples

trackless wilderness
roadless areas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trackless - having no tracks or paths; unmarked by routes

Etymology

Formed from 'path' + '-less', meaning 'without a path.'
