cardroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrdˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌruːm / (BrE)
A room designated for playing or gambling at card games, especially within a club, casino, or similar establishment.

Examples

Leaving the card room, they moved back through the Palace the way they had come.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casino - an establishment for gambling, often including card rooms

Etymology

From 'card' (playing cards) + 'room'.
