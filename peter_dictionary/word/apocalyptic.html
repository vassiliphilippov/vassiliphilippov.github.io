apocalyptic

1. ADJ

/ əˌpɑkəˈlɪktɪk / (AmE), / əˌpɒkəˈlɪktɪk / (BrE)
Describing or suggesting the end of the world or great destruction; prophetic of devastation or ultimate doom.

Examples

It ran, this apocalyptic beast, on two thin legs, and its wings - were they feathered arms?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catastrophic - involving or causing sudden great damage or suffering
  • Synonym: disastrous - causing great damage or loss
  • Antonym: hopeful - giving hope; optimistic

Etymology

From Greek 'apokalyptikos' (revelatory), from 'apokalyptein' (to uncover).
