perchlorate

1. NOUN

/ pərˈklɔːˌreɪt / (AmE), / pəˈklɔːreɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester of perchloric acid, containing the anion ClO₄⁻

Examples

One type has a small univalent anion of the thiocyanate perchlorate fluoroboride type.

Etymology

From 'perchloric' (from Greek 'hyper' meaning over + chloric) + '-ate' (denoting a salt or ester).
