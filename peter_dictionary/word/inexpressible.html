inexpressible

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪkˈsprɛs.ɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Impossible to express or describe in words; defying expression.

Examples

"I paint the nothing ", he said once to Franz Kline and myself, "the nothing that is behind the something, the inexpressible, unpaintable' tick' in the unconscious, the' spirit' of the moment resting forever, suspended like a huge balloon, in non time ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indescribable - too unusual or extreme to be described; synonym of inexpressible

Etymology

From Latin 'in-' (not) + 'expressibilis' (able to be expressed).
