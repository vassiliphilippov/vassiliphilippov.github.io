talks

1. NOUN

/ tɑks / (AmE), / tɔːks / (BrE)
Formal discussions between two or more parties intended to reach an agreement.

Examples

For years the United States had been trying to get these countries to exclude Castro's representative from secret military talks.

Synset Examples

the buyout negotiation lasted several days
they disagreed but kept an open dialogue
talks between Israelis and Palestinians

Related Terms

  • Synonym: negotiation - the process of discussing something with someone to reach an agreement
  • Synonym: dialogue - a conversation or discussion to resolve a problem
