batting

1. NOUN

/ ˈbætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ / (BrE)
The action or skill of hitting the ball in baseball, especially in attempts to get on base.

Examples

Jensen got only six hits in 46 at-bats for a .130 batting average in the first 12 games.
Willie's lifetime batting average of .318 is 11 points beyond Mickey's.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hitting - the act of striking the ball in baseball
