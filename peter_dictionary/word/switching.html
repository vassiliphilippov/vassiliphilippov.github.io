switching

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of changing from one thing or position to another; the process of making a switch.

Examples

This assembly also supplies a 20 - cps switching gate for the electronics circuitry.)

Synset Examples

his switch on abortion cost him the election

Related Terms

  • Synonym: change - the act of becoming different or making something different

Etymology

From the verb 'switch' + -ing, forming a noun from the action.
