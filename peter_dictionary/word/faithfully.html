faithfully

1. ADV

/ ˈfeɪθfəlli / (AmE), / ˈfeɪθfəli / (BrE)
In a loyal and accurate manner; in a way that shows loyalty or accuracy to the original.

Examples

Steinberg seems to have gone directly back to the score, discounting tradition, and has built his performance on the intention to reproduce as faithfully as possible exactly what Brahms set down on paper.
It always came on, faithfully, just like a radio or juke box, whenever he started to worry too much about something, when the bad things tried to push their way into him.

Synset Examples

it always came on, faithfully, like the radio

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loyally - in a loyal manner
  • Synonym: accurately - in an accurate way; exactly
  • Antonym: unfaithfully - in a manner that is not faithful; disloyally

Etymology

Formed from 'faithful' + '-ly'; 'faithful' from Middle English, from 'faith' + '-ful'.
