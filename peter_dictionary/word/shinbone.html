shinbone

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪnˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃɪnbəʊn / (BrE)
the larger and inner of the two bones in the lower human leg, running from the knee to the ankle; also called the tibia

Examples

Killpath pulled one thin leg up, clamping his arms around the shinbone to press his knee into an incredibly scrawny gut.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tibia - the inner and typically larger of the two bones between the knee and the ankle

Etymology

Formed from 'shin' + 'bone', referring to the bone at the front of the lower leg.
