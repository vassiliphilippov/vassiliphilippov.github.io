startup

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrtˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtˌʌp / (BrE)
the act or process of starting or setting something into operation, especially a machine, system, or project

Examples

This reduces the number of expensive plant shutdowns and startups.

Synset Examples

repeated shutdowns and startups are expensive

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shutdown - the act of stopping operation, especially of a machine or system

Etymology

From 'start' + 'up', originally in technical contexts for machinery operation.
