unfolding

1. NOUN

/ ʌnˈfoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the gradual development or revealing of something; a process of becoming clearer or more complex over time

Examples

It is the gradual unfolding and deepening of this contradiction which creates the inner dialectic of the evolution of the mystery story.

Synset Examples

the flowering of antebellum culture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: development - the process of growing, changing, or becoming more advanced
  • Synonym: growth - the process of increasing in size or importance

Etymology

From the verb 'unfold' (to open or reveal) + noun-forming suffix '-ing'.
