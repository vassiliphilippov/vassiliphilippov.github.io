crotchety

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɑːtʃəti / (AmE), / ˈkrɒtʃəti / (BrE)
Irritable and easily annoyed, especially in an old person; having a difficult or contrary disposition.

Examples

"She's probably getting old - crotchety, I mean - and we figured uh-uh, better not.

Synset Examples

a cantankerous and venomous-tongued old lady

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cantankerous - difficult or irritating to deal with
  • Synonym: grumpy - bad-tempered and irritable
  • Antonym: amiable - having a friendly and pleasant manner

Etymology

From 'crotchet' (a perverse or whimsical notion), with the suffix -y.
