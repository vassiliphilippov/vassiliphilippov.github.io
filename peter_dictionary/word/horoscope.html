horoscope

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɔr.əˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈhɒr.ə.skəʊp / (BrE)
A prediction of a person's future based on the positions of the planets and stars at a specific time, especially at birth.

Examples

However one looks at it, therefore, I 'd say that your horoscope for this autumn is the reverse of rosy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astrology - the study of the movements and positions of celestial bodies interpreted as affecting human affairs

Etymology

From Greek *horoskopos* (observer of the hour).
