stunned

1. ADJ

/ stʌnd / (AmE), / stʌnd / (BrE)
Extremely surprised or shocked; so surprised or shocked that you are temporarily unable to react.

Examples

Somehow, Blanche managed to cover the stunned surprise and offer her congratulations.

Synset Examples

an amazed audience gave the magician a standing ovation
I stood enthralled, astonished by the vastness and majesty of the cathedral
astounded viewers wept at the pictures from the Oklahoma City bombing
stood in stunned silence
stunned scientists found not one but at least three viruses

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonished - very surprised or amazed
  • Synonym: amazed - filled with great surprise or wonder
  • Synonym: shocked - feeling surprised and upset by something unexpected

Etymology

Past participle of 'stun,' which comes from Old French 'estoner', from Vulgar Latin *extonare (to thunder out).
