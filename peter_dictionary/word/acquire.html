acquire

1. VERB

/ əˈkwaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈkwaɪə / (BrE)
to come into possession or ownership of something; to obtain or get something, whether concrete or abstract

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obtain - to get or acquire something
  • Synonym: get - to come to have or hold something; receive
  • Antonym: lose - to be deprived of or cease to have something

Etymology

From Latin 'acquirere', from 'ad-' (to) + 'quaerere' (to seek).
