trainman

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnmæn / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnmæn / (BrE)
a railroad employee, especially one involved in the operation or supervision of trains

Examples

"Po' Chavis "! the trainman called.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railroader - a person employed by a railroad
  • Synonym: brakeman - a railroad worker responsible for assisting with the braking of a train
  • Synonym: conductor - the person in charge of a train

Etymology

Formed from 'train' + 'man', following similar occupational compounds.
