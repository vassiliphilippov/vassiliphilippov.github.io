doorknob

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɔr.nɑb / (AmE), / ˈdɔː.nɒb / (BrE)
A round handle for opening or closing a door, typically operated by turning.

Examples

The doctor shot down to the lavatory and turned the doorknob, but to no effect: the lavatory was occupied.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: door handle - a handle used for opening or closing a door (British term and also broader)

Etymology

Formed from 'door' + 'knob', with 'knob' from Middle English knobbe (a small lump or rounded handle).
