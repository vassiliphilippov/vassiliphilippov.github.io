assigning

1. NOUN

/ əˈsaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈsaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of allocating or distributing something to particular people or places

Examples

The first is compiling a list of text forms, assigning an information cell to each, and replacing text occurrences with the information cell assigned to the form of each occurrence.

Synset Examples

the first task is the assignment of an address to each datum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assignment - the act of assigning; an allocated task or position

Etymology

From the verb 'assign', from Old French 'assigner', from Latin 'assignare' (to mark out, allot).
