suggestion

1. NOUN

/ səgˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / səgˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
A very slight or barely noticeable amount of something.

Examples

Just the barest suggestion of a heel is found on teenage pumps.

Synset Examples

he speaks French with a trace of an accent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trace - a very small amount
  • Synonym: hint - a slight indication or amount

Etymology

From Latin suggestio (placing under, prompting).
