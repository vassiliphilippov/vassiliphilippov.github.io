noted

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtɪd / (BrE)
well known and respected for a particular quality or achievement

Examples

Among the spectators was the noted exotic dancer, Patti Waggin who is Mrs. Don Rudolph when off the stage.
In 1948, Afranio do Amaral, the noted Brazilian herpetologist, wrote a technical paper on the giant snakes.
In fact, the noted psychologist and sex researcher, Dr. Albert Ellis, has declared flatly that women are "sexually superior "to men.

Synset Examples

a famous actor
a celebrated musician
a famed scientist
an illustrious judge
a notable historian
a renowned painter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celebrated - widely praised and acclaimed
  • Synonym: famous - known about by many people
  • Synonym: renowned - known or talked about by many people; famous
  • Antonym: obscure - not well known; unclear or unknown

Etymology

Past participle of 'note', from Latin 'notare' (to mark).
