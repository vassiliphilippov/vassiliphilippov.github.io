lineup

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪ.nʌp / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.nʌp / (BrE)
(Baseball) the list of batters arranged in the order they will bat during a game.

Examples

After a lengthy workout yesterday, an open date, Hemus said that Bob Nieman definitely would stay in the lineup.

Synset Examples

the managers presented their cards to the umpire at home plate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: batting order - the order in which batters go to bat in baseball

Etymology

From 'line' + 'up', indicating arrangement or sequence; 'lineup' has been used in sports contexts since the early 20th century.
