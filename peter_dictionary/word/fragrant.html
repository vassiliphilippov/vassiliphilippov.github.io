fragrant

1. ADJ

/ ˈfreɪɡrənt / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrənt / (BrE)
Having a pleasant or sweet smell.

Examples

After the storm, the sky cleared blue and cool, and fragrant air swept the hills.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aromatic - having a distinctive and pleasant smell
  • Synonym: redolent - strongly reminiscent or suggestive of (especially a smell)
  • Antonym: odorless - having no smell
  • Antonym: stinking - having a strong, unpleasant smell

Etymology

From Latin fragrans, fragrant- (smelling sweet).
