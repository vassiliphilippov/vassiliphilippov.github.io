airframe

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌfreɪm / (AmE), / ˈeəˌfreɪm / (BrE)
the structural framework and outer covering of an aircraft, excluding the engines

Examples

However, I am recommending that development work on the B-70 air-frame and engines be continued.

Etymology

From 'air' + 'frame', referring to the structure of an aircraft.
