protocol

1. NOUN

/ ˈproʊ.t̬əˌkɑl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.tə.kɒl / (BrE)
The official procedure or system of rules governing affairs of state, professional situations, or correct conduct.

Examples

Hey, Reeves, what'’s the protocol?

Synset Examples

safety protocols
academic protocol

Related Terms

  • Synonym: etiquette - the customary code of polite behavior in society or among members of a particular profession
  • Synonym: procedure - an established or official way of doing something
  • Antonym: impropriety - failure to observe standards of honesty or modesty; improper behavior

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'protocollum' (first sheet of a volume, official document), from Greek 'prōtokollon'.
