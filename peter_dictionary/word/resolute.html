resolute

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛzəˌlut / (AmE), / ˈrɛzəˌluːt / (BrE)
firm and determined in purpose or belief; not wavering or hesitant

Examples

Resolved that the anti slavery sentiment is becoming ripe for resolute action.

Synset Examples

stood resolute against the enemy
faced with a resolute opposition
a resolute and unshakeable faith

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determined - showing firmness or fixed purpose
  • Antonym: wavering - lacking firmness or resolution
  • Synonym: steadfast - firm and unchanging in purpose or loyalty
  • Synonym: tenacious - holding fast; persistent

Etymology

From Latin 'resolutus', past participle of 'resolvere' (to loosen, resolve).
