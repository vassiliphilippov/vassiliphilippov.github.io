padded

1. ADJ

/ ˈpædɪd / (AmE), / ˈpædɪd / (BrE)
containing or filled with a soft material for comfort, protection, or shape.

Examples

For added comfort some of the Italian designed sandals have foam padded cushioning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cushioned - made soft or comfortable by cushions or padding

Etymology

From the verb 'pad' (to fill or cover with soft material), with the suffix '-ed' forming the adjective.
