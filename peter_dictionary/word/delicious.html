delicious

1. ADJ

/ dəˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪʃ.əs / (BrE)
Very pleasing or delightful, especially in a way that gives great enjoyment.

Examples

Every detail in his interpretation has been beautifully thought out, and of these I would especially cite the delicious landler touch the pianist brings to the fifth variation (an obvious indication that he is playing with Viennese musicians), and the gossamer shading throughout.

Synset Examples

a delightful surprise
the comedy was delightful
a delicious joke

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delightful - very pleasing, charming, or enjoyable

Etymology

From Latin *delectare* (to delight), through Old French *delicieux*.
