anti-Catholicism

1. NOUN

/ ˌæn.ti kæˈθɑl.ɪˌsɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti kæˈθɒl.ɪˌsɪz.əm / (BrE)
Hostility toward or opposition to the Roman Catholic Church and its followers.

Examples

Nowhere have I seen public expression of anti-Catholicism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anti-Semitism - hostility toward or prejudice against Jews
  • Synonym: anti-Protestantism - hostility toward or opposition to Protestant churches

Etymology

From anti- (against) + Catholicism (beliefs and practices of the Roman Catholic Church).
