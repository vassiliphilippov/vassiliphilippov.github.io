pare

1. VERB

/ per / (AmE), / pe…ô / (BrE)
to reduce something gradually or in small amounts, often to make it more efficient or manageable

Examples

Attention to details can cut in half the size unit you need and pare operating expense proportionately.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reduce - to make something smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
  • Synonym: trim - to remove excess from something; to make something neater or smaller by cutting

Etymology

From Latin *parare* (to prepare, trim).
