abruptness

1. NOUN

/ əˈbrʌptnəs / (AmE), / əˈbrʌptnəs / (BrE)
a manner of behaving that is sudden, curt, or lacking in politeness; brusqueness

Examples

He was criticized for his curtness and abruptness - and he answered: "I am not working to become popular ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curtness - brief and potentially rude manner of speaking or acting
  • Antonym: politeness - showing good manners and respect

Etymology

From 'abrupt' + '-ness'. 'Abrupt' from Latin 'abruptus' (broken off, steep).
