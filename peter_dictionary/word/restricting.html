restricting

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈstrɪktɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈstrɪktɪŋ / (BrE)
limiting the scope, range, or freedom of something or someone

Examples

Important as was Mr. O' Donnell's essay, his thesis is so restricting as to deny Faulkner the stature which he obviously has.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confining - preventing someone from leaving a place or being free; limiting
  • Synonym: limiting - controlling or reducing the extent, amount, or range of something
  • Antonym: freeing - making free; removing limits or restrictions

Etymology

From the verb 'restrict', from Latin 'restringere' meaning to bind back or limit.
