storyteller

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɔːriˌtɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɔːriˌtɛlə / (BrE)
A person who tells stories, especially as an occupation or for entertainment.

Examples

Without exception Hino's brothers turned to either one or both of their father's occupations, but Hino showed a talent for neither and instead spent most of his time on the beach where he repaired nets and proved immensely popular as a storyteller.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narrator - a person who narrates or tells a story, especially in a book or film
  • Antonym: listener - a person who listens to a story or speaker

Etymology

From 'story' + 'teller'; attested since the 18th century.
