sought

1. ADJ

/ sɔt / (AmE), / sɔːt / (BrE)
Looked for or desired by many people.

Examples

Hudson now proposed to sail all the way through and test the seas beyond for the long sought waterway.

Synset Examples

the long sought relatives

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desired - wanted or wished for
  • Synonym: sought-after - much in demand; eagerly pursued

Etymology

Past participle of Old English 'sēcan' (to seek).
