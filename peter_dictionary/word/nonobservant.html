nonobservant

1. ADJ

/ ˌnɑnəbˈzɝvənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvənt / (BrE)
Not following or failing to follow religious customs or practices.

Examples

This is, no doubt, inaccurate, but it does convey how isolated she feels among the vast army of the nonobservant.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: observant - following or strictly observing religious rules or customs

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' meaning 'not' and 'observant', meaning 'adhering to customs'.
