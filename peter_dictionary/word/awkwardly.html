awkwardly

1. ADV

/ ˈɔkwərdli / (AmE), / ˈɔːkwədli / (BrE)
In an awkward manner; in a way that lacks grace, ease, or skill.

Examples

Watson bent awkwardly and lifted the man to his feet.

Synset Examples

he bent awkwardly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gracefully - in a graceful manner; with elegance or ease

Etymology

From 'awkward' + '-ly', with 'awkward' coming from Middle English and -ly as an adverbial suffix.
