disjointed

1. ADJ

/ dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / dɪsˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
(of speech, writing, or ideas) lacking a clear or orderly connection, making them difficult to follow or understand.
