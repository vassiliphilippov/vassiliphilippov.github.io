Tibet

1. PROPN

/ təˈbɛt / (AmE), / tɪˈbɛt / (BrE)
An autonomous region in southwestern China, located on the Tibetan Plateau in the Himalayas, known for its distinct culture, language, and history.

Examples

All around the Mediterranean Sea, Asia Minor, northern India, and Tibet, Hebrew was the native tongue.
There was the revolution in Tibet which we pretended did not exist.

Etymology

From French 'Tibet', from Tibetan 'Bod' (the native name for the country).
