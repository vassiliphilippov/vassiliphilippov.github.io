quickstep

1. VERB

/ ˈkwɪkˌstɛp / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkstɛp / (BrE)
to perform the quickstep, a lively ballroom dance characterized by quick stepping movements

Examples

So is that of the limber company of lasses who whirl and glide and quickstep under Jack Cole's expert choreographic direction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waltz - to dance the waltz, a smooth ballroom dance
  • Synonym: foxtrot - to perform the foxtrot dance, a ballroom dance characterized by smooth progress

Etymology

From 'quick' + 'step', referring to the lively steps of the dance.
