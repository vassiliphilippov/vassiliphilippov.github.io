insuperable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈsuːpərəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsjuːpərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to overcome or surmount; unconquerable.

Examples

The insuperable reward systems that most religions embody have great motivating effects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome
  • Synonym: insurmountable - too great to be overcome

Etymology

From Latin 'insuperabilis', from 'in-' (not) + 'superare' (to overcome).
