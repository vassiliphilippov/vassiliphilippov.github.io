papal

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəl / (BrE)
relating to the pope or the papacy.

Examples

And if he did stand on the margins of modernity, it was not in dying a martyr for such unity as Papal supremacy might be able to force on Western Christendom.

Synset Examples

papal dispensation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pontifical - relating to the pope; papal; also relating to bishops

Etymology

From Latin papalis, from papa (pope).
