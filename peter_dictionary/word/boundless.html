boundless

1. ADJ

/ ˈbaʊndləs / (AmE), / ˈbaʊndləs / (BrE)
Without limits; seeming to have no end or boundaries in extent, amount, or degree.

Examples

The boundless blue air was stretched over the zenith like the skin of a drum.

Synset Examples

unbounded enthusiasm
children with boundless energy
a limitless supply of money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limitless - having no limits or boundaries
  • Synonym: unbounded - having no bounds or limits; unrestricted
  • Antonym: finite - having limits or bounds

Etymology

From ‘bound’ (limit, boundary) + suffix ‘-less’ (without).
