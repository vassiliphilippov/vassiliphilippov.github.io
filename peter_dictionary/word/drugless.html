drugless

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrʌɡləs / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡləs / (BrE)
Not involving or using drugs, especially of medical treatment or therapy.

Examples

Doris Hull, suffering from tuberculosis, was taken by her husband to see Otis G. Carroll, a sanipractor - a licensed drugless healer - in Spokane.

Synset Examples

drugless therapy

Related Terms

  • Antonym: drug-based - involving or using drugs, especially in medical treatment

Etymology

Formed from 'drug' + '-less' (without).
