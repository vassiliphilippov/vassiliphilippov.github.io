intermeshed

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈmɛʃt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɛʃt / (BrE)
fitted or caught together as if in a mesh; intricately connected or entangled

Examples

Tone and intonation often become seriously intermeshed.

Synset Examples

enmeshed in financial difficulties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entangled - twisted together in a confusing mass
  • Synonym: enmeshed - caught as if in a net or mesh

Etymology

From prefix 'inter-' (between, mutual) + 'mesh' (entangle or fit together).
