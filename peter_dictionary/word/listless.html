listless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪstləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪstləs / (BrE)
Lacking energy, enthusiasm, or interest; indifferent or languid.

Examples

There was some idle talk, a listless discussion of this or that small happening during the day's drive.

Synset Examples

he was listless and bored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: languid - displaying or having a disinclination for physical exertion or effort; slow and relaxed
  • Antonym: energetic - showing or involving great activity or vitality

Etymology

From Middle English 'list' (desire, inclination) + '-less'; 'list' from Old English 'lystan' (to wish, desire).
