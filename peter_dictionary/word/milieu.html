milieu

1. NOUN

/ miːlˈjuː / (AmE), / miːlˈjɜː / (BrE)
the physical or social environment in which something occurs or develops

Examples

What you were looking for (unless you make a hobby of collecting old tennis rackets and fly screens) eludes me, but to judge from phonograph records scattered about a fumed oak Victrola, you danced two tangos and a paso doble, which must have been fairly enervating in that milieu.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: environment - the surroundings or conditions in which something lives or operates

Etymology

From French *milieu* (middle, environment), from Old French *mi* (middle) + *lieu* (place).
