indigestion

1. NOUN

/ ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
Discomfort or pain in the stomach associated with difficulty in digesting food, often accompanied by heartburn or nausea.

Examples

Indigestion plagued him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dyspepsia - medical term for indigestion

Etymology

From Latin 'indigestio' (lack of digestion), from 'in-' (not) + 'digestio' (digestion).
