dinnertime

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnərˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈdɪnətaɪm / (BrE)
the usual or designated time for eating dinner, typically in the evening

Examples

"I know it is n't dinnertime ".
The hell it is n't dinnertime, two waving tails told Pam North.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunchtime - the usual time for lunch

Etymology

From 'dinner' + 'time', modeled after similar compounds (19th century).
