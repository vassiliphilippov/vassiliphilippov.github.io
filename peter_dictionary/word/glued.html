glued

1. ADJ

/ ɡluːd / (AmE), / ɡluːd / (BrE)
firmly attached or fixed in place as if by glue; unable to move or detach

Examples

Whole platoons were taking up new positions on the steps, arriving and departing, while I stayed glued, like a signpost, to one spot.
His lips felt glued together.

Synset Examples

he stayed glued to one spot
pieces of pasted paper

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuck - unable to move or be moved; fixed in one place
