grill

1. NOUN

/ ɡrɪl / (AmE), / ɡrɪl / (BrE)
A restaurant where food, especially meat or fish, is cooked over an open flame or on a grill.

Examples

A taxi took him back to the bar and grill where he had left his car, and a few minutes later he found a parking place across the street from his apartment.
He called the bar and grill where he had picked Burton up that afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grillroom - a restaurant or room in a restaurant where grilled food is served

Etymology

From French *gril*, from Old French, from Latin *craticulum* (small grating).

2. VERB

/ ɡrɪl / (AmE), / ɡrɪl / (BrE)
