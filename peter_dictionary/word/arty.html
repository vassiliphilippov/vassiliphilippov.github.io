arty

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈɑːti / (BrE)
pretending to be interested in or knowledgeable about art, often in a showy or affected way

Examples

The story is of a famous strip cartoonist, an arty individual, whose specialty is the American boy and who adopts a 10 - year old to provide him with fresh idea material.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: artistic - having a genuine natural ability or appreciation for art
  • Synonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or culture than is actually possessed

Etymology

Formed from 'art' with the suffix '-y', denoting a pretended or superficial connection with art.
