fairyland

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛriˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈfeəriˌlænd / (BrE)
An imaginary or mythical place of great beauty and enchantment, or a world existing only in the imagination.

Examples

It is like medical schools in India where, in that fairy land of religious inhibition, the dissection of dead bodies is frowned upon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantasy world - an imaginary realm or universe existing only in the mind

Etymology

From 'fairy' + 'land', modeled after the idea of a land inhabited by fairies.
