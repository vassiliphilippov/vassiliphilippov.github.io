shirtsleeve

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɝtˌsliːv / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌsliːv / (BrE)
the sleeve of a shirt

Examples

A second twitched his shirtsleeve, and he felt a brief burn on his upper arm.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeve - the part of a garment that covers the arm

Etymology

Compound of 'shirt' and 'sleeve'.
