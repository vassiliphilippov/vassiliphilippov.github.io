favour

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvə / (BrE)
A kind or helpful act done for someone, often as a demonstration of goodwill.

Examples

That's why he'd like to ask a favour of you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favor - an act of kindness or help (American spelling)

Etymology

From Old French 'faveur', from Latin 'favorem' (support, goodwill).
