lucidity

1. NOUN

/ luˈsɪdəti / (AmE), / luˈsɪdəti / (BrE)
The quality of being easily understood; clarity of thought or expression.

Examples

The Glazer-Fine Arts edition (Concert-Disc) is a model of lucidity and organization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clarity - clearness or lucidity as to perception or understanding

Etymology

From French 'lucidité', from Latin 'lucidus' (clear, bright).
