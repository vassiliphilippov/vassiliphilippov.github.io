levelheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɛvəlˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˌlɛvəlˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
having or showing an ability to think clearly and make good decisions, especially in difficult situations; sensible and calm

Examples

If deja vu is an illusion, then peculiarly, it is a most prevalent mental disturbance affecting even the most level-headed people.

Synset Examples

healthy scepticism
a healthy fear of rattlesnakes
the healthy attitude of French laws
healthy relations between labor and management
an intelligent solution
a sound approach to the problem
sound advice
no sound explanation for his decision

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensible - showing good sense or judgment
  • Synonym: prudent - showing care and thought for the future; wise in practical affairs
  • Antonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable

Etymology

Formed from 'level' + 'headed', referring to having a steady (level) mind or temperament.
