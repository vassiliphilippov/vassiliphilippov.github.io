solo

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ / (BrE)
an activity or performance that is done by one person alone, without help from others

Examples

One or two practice runs should be sufficient for solo.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: duet - a performance by two people

Etymology

From Italian solo, meaning 'alone' or 'only'.
