angelic

1. ADJ

/ ænˈdʒɛlɪk / (AmE), / ænˈdʒɛlɪk / (BrE)
exceptionally kind, pure, or beautiful; resembling or befitting an angel

Examples

Foster listened with angelic patience until Digby ran down, then said, "Listen, junior, you 're an angel now - so forget it.
The sooner you act like an angel the quicker you 'll feel angelic.

Synset Examples

angelic beneficence
a beatific smile
a saintly concern for his fellow men
my sainted mother

Related Terms

  • Synonym: saintly - very holy or virtuous; like a saint
  • Antonym: devilish - like a devil; wicked or mischievous

Etymology

From Latin angelicus, from Greek angelikos (relating to a messenger, angel).
