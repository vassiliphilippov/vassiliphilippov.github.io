lit

1. ADJ

/ lɪt / (AmE), / lɪt / (BrE)
illuminated with artificial light; made bright by lighting

Examples

At the end of the performance, Dave and Max came out into the brilliantly lit foyer among a surge of gowned and tuxedoed first nighters.

Synset Examples

illuminated advertising
looked up at the lighted windows
a brightly lit room
a well-lighted stairwell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illuminated - made bright by light; lit up
  • Antonym: dark - having little or no light

Etymology

Past participle of 'light' (Old English 'līhtan').
