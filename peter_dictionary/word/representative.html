representative

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛprəˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a member of the United States House of Representatives; a person elected to the lower legislative chamber of the U.S. Congress

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: senator - a member of the United States Senate

Etymology

From Latin repraesentare (to show, exhibit), via Old French.
