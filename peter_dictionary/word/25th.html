25th

1. ADJ

/ twɛnti ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / twɛnti ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
Coming after twenty-four others in a sequence; ordinal form of twenty-five.

Examples

"I know when my reflexes are gone and I'm not going to be any 25th man on the ball club ".

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 24th - coming next after the twenty-third in position
  • Degree Higher: 26th - coming next after the twenty-fifth in position
