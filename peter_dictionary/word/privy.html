privy

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪvi / (AmE), / ˈprɪvi / (BrE)
an outdoor toilet, typically a small building with a seat over a hole, used as a place to defecate

Examples

Behind were privies, for there was no sewage system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outhouse - a small building used as a toilet outside a house

Etymology

From Old French *privé* (private place), from Latin *privatus* (private).

2. NOUN

/ ˈprɪvi / (AmE), / ˈprɪvi / (BrE)
(historical or regional) A small outdoor building used as a toilet, especially an outhouse or latrine.

Examples

She 'd found one and she had n't said a word while Big Hans and I had hunted and hunted as we always did all winter, every winter since the spring that Hans had come and I had looked in the privy and found the first one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outhouse - a small outdoor building containing a toilet

Etymology

From Old French 'privé' (private), from Latin 'privatus' (private).
