oversized

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊvərˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəsaɪzd / (BrE)
Larger than the usual or expected size for its type.

Examples

Oversized monsters are never brought home either alive or preserved, and field measurements are obviously open to doubt because of the universal tendency to exaggerate dimensions.
It was secured by an oversized padlock.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undersized - smaller than the usual or expected size for its type

Etymology

From 'over-' meaning 'excessive' + 'sized,' formed in English.
