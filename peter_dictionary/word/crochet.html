crochet

1. VERB

/ kroʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / krəʊˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
to make fabric or items by looping yarn with a hooked needle

Examples

Some women can sit and sew, crochet, tat or knit by the hour, and look calm and relaxed and turn out beautiful work.

Synset Examples

crochet a bedspread

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knit - to make fabric by interlocking loops of yarn with needles

Etymology

From French 'crochet' (small hook), from Old French 'crochet', diminutive of 'croche' (hook).
