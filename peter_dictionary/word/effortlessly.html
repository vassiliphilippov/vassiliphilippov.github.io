effortlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈɛfərtləsli / (AmE), / ˈɛfətləsli / (BrE)
In a way that requires no apparent effort; easily and gracefully.

Examples

Rector asked him to move it for the time being; he wanted the mission compound to be effortlessly spotless.

Synset Examples

she danced gracefully and effortlessly
swallows gliding effortlessly through the air

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easily - without difficulty; with little effort
  • Antonym: laboriously - with great effort and difficulty

Etymology

From 'effortless' + '-ly', where 'effortless' comes from 'effort' (from French 'effort') + '-less' (without).
