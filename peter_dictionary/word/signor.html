signor

1. NOUN

/ siˈnjɔr / (AmE), / siˈnjɔː / (BrE)
a courtesy title or form of address for an Italian man, equivalent to 'Mr.'

Examples

His name is Signor Roberto.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: senor - Spanish courtesy title for men, equivalent to 'Mr.'
  • Synonym: monsieur - French courtesy title for men, equivalent to 'Mr.'

Etymology

From Italian 'signore', meaning 'lord' or 'mister'.
