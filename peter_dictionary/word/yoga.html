yoga

1. NOUN

/ ˈjoʊɡə / (AmE), / ˈjəʊɡə / (BrE)
A Hindu spiritual and physical discipline aimed at attaining spiritual insight and tranquility, typically through specific bodily postures, breath control, and meditation.

Examples

A Tibetan swami from Palermo, Sicily, announced in Beverly Hills a newly discovered, ancient yoga discipline for ripple breathing which increased both pranha and cosmic attraction between sexes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meditation - the practice of focusing the mind for relaxation or spiritual purposes

Etymology

From Sanskrit 'yoga' (union, yoking), from 'yuj' (to join, to yoke).
