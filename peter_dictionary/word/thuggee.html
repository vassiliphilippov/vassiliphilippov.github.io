thuggee

1. NOUN

/ ˈθʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈθʌɡi / (BrE)
A historical practice of murder and robbery carried out by organized gangs known as thugs, especially in India during the 18th and 19th centuries.

Examples

Have you ever heard of thuggee?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thug - a member of a group of professional robbers and murderers in India

Etymology

From Hindi ṭhagī (deceit, swindling), from ṭhag (thief, swindler).
