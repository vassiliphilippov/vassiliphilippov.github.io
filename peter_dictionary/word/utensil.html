utensil

1. NOUN

/ juˈtɛnsl̩ / (AmE), / juːˈtɛnsɪl / (BrE)
A tool or implement, especially one used in the household for practical purposes such as cooking, eating, or cleaning.

Examples

Food, medical supplies, utensils, and equipment, if not already stored in the shelter, must be quickly gathered up and carried into it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tool - an implement used to carry out a particular function
  • Synonym: appliance - a device or piece of equipment for household use

Etymology

From Latin *utensilis* (fit for use), from *uti* (to use).
