unrealistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnriːəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnriːəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
Not showing or having a sensible and practical idea of what can be achieved or expected; not realistic.

Examples

The storyline, in sort, is wildly unrealistic.
To believe otherwise would be unrealistic.

Synset Examples

unrealistic expectations
prices at unrealistic high levels

Related Terms

  • Antonym: realistic - having or showing a sensible and practical idea of what can be achieved

Etymology

un- (prefix meaning 'not') + realistic.
