overheated

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvɚˈhiːtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhiːtɪd / (BrE)
Heated beyond a safe or desirable point; excessively hot.

Examples

He came home overheated, ran straight to the ice-chest, and gulped shivery cold water.

Synset Examples

the child became overheated
overheated metal

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hot - having a high degree of heat or a high temperature

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'over-' (excessively) and 'heated' (past participle of 'heat').
