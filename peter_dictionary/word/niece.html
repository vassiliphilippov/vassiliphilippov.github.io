niece

1. NOUN

/ nis / (AmE), / niÀês / (BrE)
the daughter of one's brother or sister, or of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law

Examples

He knows me as your niece, which, of course, I am.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: nephew - son of one's sibling

Etymology

From Old French *niece*, from Latin *neptia* (niece, granddaughter).
