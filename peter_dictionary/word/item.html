1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪtəm / (AmE), / ˈaɪtəm / (BrE)
a single fact, detail, or piece of information considered separately from a group or whole

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detail - a small part of something; a single feature or item
  • Synonym: point - a single aspect or item in a discussion

Etymology

From Latin item (likewise, also; entry in a list), used in Medieval Latin as a heading for new entries.
