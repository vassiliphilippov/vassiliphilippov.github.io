pertain

1. VERB

/ pərˈteɪn / (AmE), / pəˈteɪn / (BrE)
to be a part, attribute, or relevant aspect of something; to relate to or concern

Examples

As a nation, we feel an obligation to assist other countries in their development; but this obligation pertains only to countries which are honestly seeking to become responsible members of a stable and forward-moving world community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relate - have reference to; concern; to be connected with
  • Synonym: refer - to have relation or connection; to pertain

Etymology

From Latin 'pertinere' (to relate to), from 'per-' (through) + 'tenere' (to hold).

2. VERB

/ pərˈteɪn / (AmE), / pəˈteɪn / (BrE)
to be relevant or applicable to; to relate to something

Examples

His view is that every religion pertains to a community, and, conversely, every community is in one aspect a religious unit.
