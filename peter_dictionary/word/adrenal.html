adrenal

1. ADJ

/ əˈdriːnəl / (AmE), / əˈdriːnəl / (BrE)
Relating to the adrenal glands or their secretions.

Examples

Muscle weakness is now recognized as an uncommon though serious complication of steroid therapy, with most of the synthetic adrenal corticosteroids in clinical use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suprarenal - relating to or situated above the kidneys; another term for adrenal

Etymology

From Latin 'ad' (near) + 'renes' (kidneys), referring to the glands' position.
