cleanly

1. ADV

/ ˈklinli / (AmE), / ˈkliːnli / (BrE)
In a skillful, precise, or flawless manner; without error or unnecessary movement.

Examples

In the same way he pantomimed grasping a mantel and bouncing cleanly off that, pressing his hands against the floor and bouncing cleanly off that.
In the same way he pantomimed grasping a mantel and bouncing cleanly off that, pressing his hands against the floor and bouncing cleanly off that.

Synset Examples

he bounced it cleanly off the wall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flawlessly - without fault or error; perfectly
  • Synonym: skillfully - with skill or expertise

Etymology

From Middle English clene (clean) + -ly (adverbial suffix).
