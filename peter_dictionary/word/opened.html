opened

1. ADJ

/ ˈoʊpənd / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənd / (BrE)
(of mouth or eyes) in a state in which they are not closed; allowing entry, view, or passage.

Examples

Scotty looked at the children, his mouth slightly opened, his eyes dull.

Synset Examples

keep your eyes open
his mouth slightly opened

Related Terms

  • Antonym: closed - (of mouth or eyes) not open; shut
  • Antonym: shut - (of mouth or eyes) firmly closed
