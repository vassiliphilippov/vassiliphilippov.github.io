nutritive

1. ADJ

/ nuˈtrɪtɪv / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or providing nourishment; nourishing.

Examples

Therefore, an explanation of this alveolar bronchial artery supply might be the nutritive requirement of an increased amount of supportive tissue, not primarily diffusing in nature, in the region of the alveolus.

Synset Examples

good nourishing stew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nourishing - providing substances necessary for growth and health

Etymology

From Latin nutritivus, from nutrire (to nourish).
