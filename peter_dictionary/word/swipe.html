swipe

1. VERB

/ swaɪp / (AmE), / swaɪp / (BrE)
to strike or hit with a sweeping movement, especially with the hand or an object

Examples

He noticed a speck of dirt on the sill and swiped at it with his finger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hit - to make forceful contact with something
  • Antonym: miss - to fail to hit the target
