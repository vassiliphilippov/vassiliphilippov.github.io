steakhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪkˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪkˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A restaurant that specializes in serving steaks and other meat dishes.

Examples

Pat took Eileen and me out to dinner at a swell steak house and told us with tears in his eyes how happy he was we had come together again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chophouse - restaurant specializing in meat dishes, especially chops and steaks

Etymology

From 'steak' + 'house'; modeled after similar formations like 'roadhouse'.
