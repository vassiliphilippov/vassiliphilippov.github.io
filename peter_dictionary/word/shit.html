1. NOUN

/ ʃɪt / (AmE), / ʃɪt / (BrE)
A vulgar term for feces; excrement.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crap - slang term for feces
  • Synonym: excrement - waste matter discharged from the bowels

5. NOUN

/ ʃɪt / (AmE), / ʃɪt / (BrE)
A vulgar and insulting term used to address or refer to a person considered stupid, irritating, or contemptible.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person
