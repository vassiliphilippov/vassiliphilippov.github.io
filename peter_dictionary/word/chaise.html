chaise

1. NOUN

/ ʃeɪz / (AmE), / ʃeɪz / (BrE)
A long, reclining chair, typically upholstered, designed for relaxing while lying down.

Examples

They want to be fat cats, Pam thought, and lighted a cigarette and leaned back on a chaise and considered pulling her thoughts together.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chaise longue - a long reclining upholstered chair
  • Synonym: lounge chair - a comfortable chair for relaxing

Etymology

From French 'chaise longue' meaning 'long chair'.
