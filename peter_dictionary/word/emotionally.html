emotionally

1. ADV

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənəli / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or involves emotions.

Examples

If the inner functions of religion are performed, the individual is a composed, ordered, motivated, and emotionally secure associate; he is not greatly frustrated, and he is not anomic; he is better fitted to perform his social life among his fellows.
Emotionally, they penetrated him not at all.
"Results of psychological profile... "...violent mood swings, highly intelligent, emotionally unstable.

Synset Examples

emotionally secure

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emotionally - in an emotional manner, showing emotion
  • Antonym: intellectually - with regard to intellect or thinking

Etymology

Formed from 'emotional' + '-ly', where 'emotional' is from French 'émotionnel', based on 'émotion'.
