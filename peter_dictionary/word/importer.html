importer

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈpɔrtər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɔːtə / (BrE)
A person or company whose business is to bring goods or services into a country from abroad for sale.

Examples

They'’re olive oil importers, Don Ciccio.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: exporter - person or company that sells goods to other countries

Etymology

From Latin 'importare' (to bring in), via French 'importer'.
