void

1. ADJ

/ vɔɪd / (AmE), / vɔɪd / (BrE)
Having no legal effect; not legally binding or enforceable.

Examples

Any agreement to the contrary shall be unlawful and void.

Synset Examples

null and void

Related Terms

  • Antonym: valid - legally binding and effective

Etymology

From Latin 'vocĭdus', from 'vacare' (to be empty, vacant).
