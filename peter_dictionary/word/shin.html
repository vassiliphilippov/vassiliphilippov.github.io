shin

1. NOUN

/ ʃɪn / (AmE), / ʃɪn / (BrE)
The front part of the human leg below the knee and above the ankle.

Examples

Quickly but carefully lowering my duffel bag over the low side rack, I stepped on the running board; it flopped down, sprang back up and gouged my shin.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: calf - the fleshy back portion of the lower human leg

Etymology

From Old English 'scinu', related to Old High German 'scina' (shinbone).
