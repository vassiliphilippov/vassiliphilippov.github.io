fiddle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪdəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪd.əl / (BrE)
a violin, especially as used in folk or traditional music

Examples

Fiddles screeched; a piano tinkled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: violin - bowed string instrument, standard in orchestras

Etymology

From Old English *fiðele*, related to Old Norse *fiðla* and Medieval Latin *vitula* (a stringed instrument).
