T.B.

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈbi / (AmE), / tiː ˈbiː / (BrE)
An abbreviation for tuberculosis, an infectious disease caused by tubercle bacilli, commonly affecting the lungs.

Examples

Moreover, her husband and child contracted T.B. from her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tuberculosis - an infectious disease caused by Mycobacterium tuberculosis

Etymology

Abbreviation of tuberculosis.
