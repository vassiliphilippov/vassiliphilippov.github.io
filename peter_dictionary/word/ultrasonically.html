ultrasonically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌltrəˈsɑːnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈsɒnɪkli / (BrE)
By means of ultrasound or using ultrasonic waves.

Examples

The method used to scan the eye ultrasonically is illustrated in Fig. 6.

Synset Examples

the kidney stones were removed ultrasonically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ultrasound - sound waves with frequencies above the audible range

Etymology

From 'ultrasonic' + '-ally', formed from Latin 'ultra' (beyond) and Greek 'sonic' (sound).
