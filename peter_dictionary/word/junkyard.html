junkyard

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒʌŋkˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋkˌjɑːd / (BrE)
a place where scrap or discarded materials, especially metal, are collected and stored for resale or disposal

Examples

A tavern, filling station, junk yard, rendering plant, or some other business may go up near enough to hurt your home or to hurt its value.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scrapyard - place for storing and selling scrap metal and other recyclables

Etymology

From 'junk' (discarded material) + 'yard' (enclosed area).
