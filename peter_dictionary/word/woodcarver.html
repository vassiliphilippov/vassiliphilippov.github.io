woodcarver

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdˌkɑrvər / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌkɑːvə / (BrE)
A person who makes decorative or artistic objects and panels by carving wood.

Examples

He was an artisan, a man who studied his trade and developed his craftsmanship the way a goldsmith or a wood carver did.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sculptor - an artist who makes sculptures, especially in stone or metal

Etymology

From 'wood' + 'carver', literally one who carves wood.
