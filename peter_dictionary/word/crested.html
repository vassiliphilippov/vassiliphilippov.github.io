crested

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd / (BrE)
Bearing a heraldic device or emblem, especially a crest, often as a mark of distinction.

Examples

He had eaten almost nothing on the crested, three sectioned plate and had drunk about half the milk in its paper container.

Etymology

From 'crest' (Latin 'crista', a tuft or plume) + '-ed'.
