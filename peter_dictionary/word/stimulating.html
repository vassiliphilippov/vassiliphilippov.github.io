stimulating

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪmjʊˌleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Encouraging interest or enthusiasm; making one feel more active, alert, or excited.

Examples

Contact with him is stimulating.

Synset Examples

a stimulating discussion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exciting - causing strong feelings of enthusiasm and eagerness
  • Antonym: boring - not interesting; dull

Etymology

Present participle of 'stimulate', from Latin 'stimulatus', past participle of 'stimulare' (to goad, urge).
