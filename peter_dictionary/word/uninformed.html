uninformed

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪnˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
not having or showing awareness or knowledge; lacking information or understanding about something

Examples

I'’m totally in the dark.

Synset Examples

the uninformed public

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about a particular thing
  • Antonym: informed - having or showing knowledge of a subject or situation

Etymology

From un- (not) + informed (from Latin informare, to form or shape).
