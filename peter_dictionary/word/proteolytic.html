proteolytic

1. ADJ

/ ˌproʊtiəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtiəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or causing the breaking down of proteins into smaller peptides or amino acids by chemical means, especially by enzymes.

Examples

Besides proteolytic enzymes the thyroid possesses de-iodinating enzymes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protease - an enzyme that breaks down proteins and peptides

Etymology

From protein + Greek lysis 'loosening, dissolving' + -ic.
