preexistent

1. ADJ

/ priɪɡˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / priːɪɡˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
existing previously or before something else; having existence before a specified event or time

Examples

Existence is created and willed by God and is not the consequence of a pre-existent rebellion or of a cosmic descent from eternity into history.

Synset Examples

variations on pre-existent musical themes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preexisting - existing previously or before something else

Etymology

From Latin praexistens, present participle of praeesse 'to exist before.'
