finishing

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
A final surface coating or texture applied to a material or object, enhancing its appearance or durability.

Examples

Makes necessary purchases, places contracts, supervises construction, installation, finishing and placement of furniture, fixtures and other correlated furnishings, and follows through to completion of project ".

Synset Examples

the boat had a metallic finish
he applied a coat of a clear finish
when the finish is too thin it is difficult to apply evenly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finish - a final coat or surface that gives a desired appearance
