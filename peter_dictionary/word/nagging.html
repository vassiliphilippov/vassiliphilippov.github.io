nagging

1. ADJ

/ ˈnæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnaɡɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or voice) continually complaining or faultfinding, especially in an irritating or persistent way.

Examples

Only, unfortunately, he could not remove from his voice a nagging insinuation of the direct command.

Synset Examples

a shrewish wife
nagging parents

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shrewish - (of a woman) bad-tempered or aggressively assertive
