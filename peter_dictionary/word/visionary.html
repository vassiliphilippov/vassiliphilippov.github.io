visionary

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒənəri / (BrE)
a person who has original ideas about what the future will or could be like, often with little regard for what is currently possible

Examples

Okay, you're a visionary, and also the only one who believes me.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dreamer - a person who dreams or has impractical ideas
  • Antonym: realist - a person who accepts and deals with things as they really are

Etymology

From Latin visio (sight, vision) + -ary (relating to).
