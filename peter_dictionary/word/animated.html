animated

1. ADJ

/ ˈænɪˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈanɪmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
full of life, energy, or excitement; lively or spirited.

Examples

The road seemed animated with a few more wagons than usual; a carriage raising up the choking June dust, and beyond, in a meadow, a local militia company drilling with muskets, Kentuck' rifles, every kind of horse pistol, old sword, or cutlass.
On the second floor he saw the animated faces of the party guests; the scene looked like a Christmas card.

Synset Examples

an animated and expressive face
animated conversation
became very animated when he heard the good news

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lively - full of life and energy
  • Synonym: spirited - full of energy, enthusiasm, or determination
  • Antonym: dull - lacking interest or energy

Etymology

From Latin animatus (filled with life or spirit), from anima (soul).
