participation

1. NOUN

/ pɑːrˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən / (AmE), / pɑːˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of taking part in an activity or event with others, especially as part of a group

Participation for the educator in this case, however, would have to be raised to full and complete membership.

Synset Examples

the teacher tried to increase his students' engagement in class activities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: involvement - the state of being included in an activity or situation
  • Synonym: engagement - the act of being involved or participating in something
  • Antonym: withdrawal - the act of removing oneself from participation or group activity

Etymology

From Latin participatio (a sharing, partaking), from participare (to share in).
