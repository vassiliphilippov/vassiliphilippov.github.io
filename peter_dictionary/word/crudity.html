crudity

1. NOUN

/ ˈkruːdəti / (AmE), / ˈkruːdəti / (BrE)
the quality of being rude, vulgar, or lacking refinement or tact

Examples

Selena was the wrong woman for these crudities.

Synset Examples

the whole town was famous for its crudeness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crudeness - the quality of being crude; lack of refinement or sophistication
  • Antonym: refinement - elegance or cultured manners; politeness

Etymology

From Latin cruditas (rawness), from crudus (raw, unrefined).

2. NOUN

/ ˈkruːd.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈkruː.dɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being in a raw, unrefined, or primitive state; lack of sophistication or finish

Examples

It was n't the roughness and crudity and discomfort of the trip that had frightened her.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crudeness - the quality of being crude; lack of refinement

Etymology

From Latin cruditas (rawness), from crudus (raw, rough).
