decor

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪ.kɔr / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.kɔː / (BrE)
The style and arrangement of furniture, decorations, and color schemes in the interior of a building, especially as a reflection of a particular style or period.

Examples

In any given period piece the costumes, bric-a-brac, vehicles, and decor, bear the stamp of unimpeachable authenticity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interior decoration - the design and arrangement of a room's furnishings and style
  • Synonym: furnishings - the furniture, fittings, and other decorative accessories of a room
  • Synonym: design - a plan or specification for the arrangement or style of something, especially interiors

Etymology

From French 'décor', short for 'décoration', meaning 'decoration'.
