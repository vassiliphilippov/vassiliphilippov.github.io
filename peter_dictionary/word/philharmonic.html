philharmonic

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɪl.hɑːrˈmɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌfɪl.hɑːˈmɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a symphony orchestra or the love of music, especially orchestral music.

Examples

"The artist is like a fragment of a mosaic - no, he is more than that, a virtuoso performer in some vast philharmonic.

Synset Examples

philharmonic players

Related Terms

  • Synonym: orchestral - relating to an orchestra or orchestral music

Etymology

From Italian 'filarmonico', from Greek 'philos' (loving) + 'harmonia' (harmony, concord).
