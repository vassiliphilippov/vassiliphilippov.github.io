Mr

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪstər / (AmE), / ˈmɪstə / (BrE)
A title used before a man's surname or full name as a polite or formal way of addressing him.

Examples

QUESTADT: Mr. Pentangeli, you, uh...

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Mrs - title for a married woman
  • Antonym: Ms - title for a woman without indicating marital status
  • Antonym: Miss - title for an unmarried woman

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'mister', from Middle English 'maistre', from Old French 'maistre', from Latin 'magister' (master).
