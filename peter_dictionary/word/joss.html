joss

1. NOUN

/ dʒɑs / (AmE), / dʒɒs / (BrE)
(in Chinese culture) a god or spirit worshipped as an idol, particularly in the form of a statue.

Examples

In that case, listen to what befell another wisenheimer who tangled with our joss.

Etymology

From Chinese (Cantonese) dialect word *jo s* (god, lord).
