unhealthy

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɛlθi / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθi / (BrE)
Not in good health; physically or mentally unwell.

Examples

Yet many psychologists and marriage counselors agree that domination of the sex relationship by one partner or the other can be unhealthy and even dangerous.

Synset Examples

unhealthy ulcers

Related Terms

  • Antonym: healthy - in good health; well
  • Synonym: ill - physically or mentally unwell
  • Synonym: sick - unwell; ill

Etymology

From un- (not) + healthy.
