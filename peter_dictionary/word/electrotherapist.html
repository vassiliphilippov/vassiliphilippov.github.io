electrotherapist

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌlɛktroʊˈθɛrəpɪst / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈθɛrəpɪst / (BrE)
A medical professional who specializes in the treatment of disease or injury using electric currents or other forms of electricity.

Examples

He may call himself a naprapath, a physiotherapist, an electrotherapist, a naturopath, a sanipractor, a medical cultist, a masseur, a "doctor "- or what have you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiotherapist - a person qualified to treat disease, injury, or deformity using physical methods such as massage, heat, and exercise

Etymology

Formed from 'electro-' (relating to electricity) + 'therapist' (a practitioner of therapy).
