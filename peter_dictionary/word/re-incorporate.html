re-incorporate

1. VERB

/ riˈɪnkɔrpəˌreɪt / (AmE), / riːˈɪŋkɔːpəreɪt / (BrE)
to incorporate again or anew

Examples

It is assumed that the iodine released from the iodotyrosines remains in the iodide pool of the thyroid, where it is oxidised and re-incorporated into thyroglobulin.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorporate - to combine or include something as part of a whole

Etymology

Formed from prefix re- (again) + incorporate.
