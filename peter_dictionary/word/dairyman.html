dairyman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdeəriˌmæn / (BrE)
a man who owns or manages a dairy, or works in the production and distribution of milk and milk products

Examples

Knowing specifically what the many feed additives can do and how and when to feed them can make a highly competitive business more profitable for beef, dairy, and sheep men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dairywoman - a woman who owns or manages a dairy

Etymology

From 'dairy' + 'man', denoting a man involved with dairying.
