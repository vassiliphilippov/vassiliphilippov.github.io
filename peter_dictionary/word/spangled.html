spangled

1. ADJ

/ ˈspæŋɡəld / (AmE), / ˈspæŋɡəld / (BrE)
decorated or covered with small, bright objects such as beads, jewels, or sequins

Examples

[BAND PLAYING MUSIC] [BAND PLAYING THE STAR-SPANGLED BANNER] [SPEAKING ITALIAN]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bejeweled - decorated with jewels
  • Synonym: sequined - decorated with sequins

Etymology

From the noun 'spangle' (a small, sparkling object), itself from Middle English, probably from Old French espangle.
