decade

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛˌkeɪd, dəˈkeɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛk.eɪd, dɪˈkeɪd / (BrE)
A period of ten years.

Etymology

From French 'décade', from Latin 'decas', from Greek 'dekas' (group of ten).
