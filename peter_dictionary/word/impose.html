1. VERB

/ ɪmˈpoʊz / (AmE), / ɪmˈpəʊz / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enforce - to make people obey a law or rule by authority
  • Synonym: compel - to force someone to do something

Etymology

From Latin imponere, meaning 'to place upon', from in- 'in, upon' + ponere 'to place'.
