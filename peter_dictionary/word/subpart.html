subpart

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌbˌpɑrt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌpɑːt / (BrE)
a part that forms a section or subdivision of a larger part

Examples

Analysis means the evaluation of subparts, the comparative ratings of parts, the comprehension of the meaning of isolated elements.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: part - a piece or segment of something larger

Etymology

From Latin 'sub-' (under, below) and English 'part'.
