metabolic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɛtəˈbɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the chemical processes that occur within a living organism in order to maintain life

Examples

Drug elevates the metabolic rate of the cow.

Synset Examples

metabolic rate

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catabolic - relating to the breakdown of complex molecules in metabolism
  • Synonym: anabolic - relating to the synthesis of complex molecules in metabolism

Etymology

From French métabolique, from Greek metabolē (change), from metaballein (to change).
