entrepreneur

1. NOUN

/ ˌɑntrəprəˈnɝ / (AmE), / ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜː / (BrE)
A person who organizes and operates a business or businesses, taking on greater than normal financial risks in order to do so.

Etymology
