astonish

1. VERB

/ əˈstɑnɪʃ / (AmE), / əˈstɒnɪʃ / (BrE)
to surprise greatly; to fill with sudden and overpowering wonder or amazement

Examples

That any sort of duty was owed by his nation to other nations would have astonished a nineteenth-century statesman.

Synset Examples

Your ability to speak six languages amazes me!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amaze - to surprise or fill with wonder
  • Synonym: astound - to shock or greatly surprise
  • Antonym: bore - to cause to feel tired and uninterested

Etymology

From Old French *estoner* (to stun, astonish), from Latin *extonare* (to thunder out).
