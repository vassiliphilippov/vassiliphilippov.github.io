warden

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔrdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːdn̩ / (BrE)
the person in charge of a prison

Examples

Not long after the Warden deprived us of his company, I got a postcard in the mail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prison governor - person in charge of a prison (British term)

Etymology

From Middle English *wardein*, from Anglo-Norman *warden*, from Old Northern French *wardein*, from Frankish *wardōn* (to guard).
