glittering

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡlɪt̬ɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ / (BrE)
Shining with a bright, shimmering, or sparkling light, often in brief flashes.

Examples

Once there had been big glittering rooms where Ulanys bowed and kissed the ladies' hands as they flirted from behind their fans.
Hal, the linguist, saw the glittering discs and necklaces in terms of the languages spoken therein.
"Mor-ee-air-teeeee ", he shrieked, his white teeth grossly counterpointing those of the glittering blonde.

Synset Examples

`glistering' is an archaic term

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sparkling - shining with many small points of light
  • Antonym: dull - not bright or sparkling; lacking shine or vividness

Etymology

Present participle of 'glitter', from Middle English gliteren, of Germanic origin.
