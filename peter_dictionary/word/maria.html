maria

1. NOUN

/ məˈriːə / (AmE), / məˈriːə / (BrE)
Extensive dark, flat regions on the surface of the Moon, formed by ancient volcanic eruptions.

Examples

These observations indicate that the lunar maria heat up more rapidly and also cool off more rapidly than do the mountainous regions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mare - a dark, flat region on the Moon's surface; singular form of 'maria'

Etymology

Plural of Latin 'mare' meaning 'sea', originally thought to be seas by early astronomers.
