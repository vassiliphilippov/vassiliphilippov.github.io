ripening

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪpənɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of becoming fully developed or mature, especially in fruit or crops.

Examples

If the temperature is controlled properly, the avocado will delay its ripening until needed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maturation - the process of becoming mature

Etymology

From the verb 'ripen', which derives from 'ripe', from Old English 'rīpe'.
