choosy

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃuːzi / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːzi / (BrE)
Very selective; hard to please and inclined to pick only the best or most suitable options.

Examples

As befits a queen, a bumblebee female is rather choosy and may spend considerable time searching for a suitable nesting place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: picky - very particular; inclined to be difficult to please
  • Synonym: fussy - hard to please, especially about small details
