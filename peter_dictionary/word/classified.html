classified

1. ADJ

/ ˈklæsɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
Arranged into categories or groups according to shared characteristics.

Examples

Thus, a child's Skeletal Age "dots "may be classified as "advanced "when they appear above the middle curve, "moderate "when they appear immediately above or below the middle curve, and "delayed "when they appear below the lower curve.
Classified ads are also chockfull of misrelated constructions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: categorized - put into categories or groups
  • Synonym: organized - arranged systematically

Etymology

From 'classify' (from French 'classer', from 'classe', from Latin 'classis', a class), plus the -ed participle suffix.
