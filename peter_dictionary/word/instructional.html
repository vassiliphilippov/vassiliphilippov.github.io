instructional

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl / (BrE)
intended or used for teaching; relating to instruction.

Examples

RCA Victor has an ambitious and useful project in a stereo series called "Adventures in Music ", which is an instructional record library for elementary schools.

Synset Examples

instructional aids

Related Terms

  • Synonym: educational - relating to education or serving as education

Etymology

From 'instruction' + '-al', patterned after similar Latin-based adjectives.
