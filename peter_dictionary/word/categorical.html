categorical

1. ADJ

/ ˌkætəˈɡɔrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈɡɒrɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or included in a category or group.

Examples

You'’ve opened yourself to five counts of perjury.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classificatory - relating to the act of classifying or categorizing

Etymology

From Late Latin *categoricus*, from Greek *katēgorikos* (accusing, predicative).
