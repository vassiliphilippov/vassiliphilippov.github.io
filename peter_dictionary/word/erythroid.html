erythroid

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈrɪθrɔɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈrɪθrɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to red blood cells (erythrocytes) or their precursors

Examples

The cells of the erythroid, myeloid, and megakaryocytic series were normal except for their numbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: erythrocyte - a red blood cell

Etymology

From Greek 'erythros' (red) + '-oid' (form, resembling).
