1. VERB

/ rɪˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
to bring a fact, event, or piece of information back into your mind; to recall or be able to recollect something from memory

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: forget - to fail to remember

Etymology

From Old French *remembrer*, from Latin *rememorari* (to recall to mind).
