loose-jowled

1. ADJ

/ lus ˈdʒaʊld / (AmE), / luːs ˈdʒaʊld / (BrE)
Having sagging or hanging folds of flesh beneath the chin or lower jaw.

Examples

There was the squat loose jowled man, smiling lopsidedly up at him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double-chinned - having a second fold of flesh under the chin
