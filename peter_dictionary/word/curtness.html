curtness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɜːtnəs / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being rudely brief or abrupt in manner or speech

Examples

He was criticized for his curtness and abruptness - and he answered: "I am not working to become popular ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abruptness - the quality of being sudden or curt in manner or speech
  • Antonym: politeness - the quality of being courteous or considerate in manner or speech

Etymology

From 'curt' + '-ness'; 'curt' from Latin 'curtus' (short, cut short).
