despised

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈspaɪzd / (AmE), / dɪˈspaɪzd / (BrE)
Regarded with contempt or deep dislike.

Examples

Beaten, bruised and exhausted, he pursues the elusive killer through the demi-monde of high society and low morals, always alone, always despised.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hated - regarded with intense dislike or aversion
  • Antonym: admired - regarded with respect or warm approval

Etymology

From Old French 'despis-', stem of 'despreer', from Latin 'despicere' meaning 'to look down on'.
