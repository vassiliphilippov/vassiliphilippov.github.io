resentful

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈzɛntfəl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɛntf(ə)l / (BrE)
Feeling or showing bitterness or indignation at having been treated unfairly.

Examples

She was resentful of his easy success as compared with Shelley's failure.

Synset Examples

resentful at the way he was treated
a sullen resentful attitude

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bitter - feeling or showing anger, hurt, or resentment
  • Antonym: forgiving - willing or able to forgive

Etymology

From resent + -ful, ultimately from Latin *sentire* (to feel).
