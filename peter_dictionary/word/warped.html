warped

1. ADJ

/ wɔrpt / (AmE), / wɔːpt / (BrE)
(of timbers, boards, or other materials) bent or twisted out of shape, typically as a result of moisture.

Examples

The warped, broken trees in the valley assumed wraith-like shapes.

Synset Examples

the floors were warped and cracked

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bent - shaped into a curve or angle, not straight
  • Antonym: straight - without a curve or bend; not twisted or warped
