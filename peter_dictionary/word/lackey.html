lackey

1. NOUN

/ ˈlæki / (AmE), / ˈlæki / (BrE)
A male servant, especially one who performs menial tasks or acts in a servile manner.

Examples

Hired, hard lackeys of the Warmongering Capitalists.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: servant - a person employed to perform domestic duties or personal services
  • Synonym: footman - a male servant, especially one employed to assist in a household or to accompany carriages

Etymology

From French *laquais* (footman, servant).
