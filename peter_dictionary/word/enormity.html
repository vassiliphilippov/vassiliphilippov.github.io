enormity

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈnɔrməti / (AmE), / ɪˈnɔːmɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being shocking, outrageous, or morally monstrous

Examples

The enormity of what Conrad had told him made it impossible for Gilborn to accept, with any degree of realism, the actuality of it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outrageousness - the quality of being shocking or extreme

Etymology

From Latin *enormitas* (irregularity, extravagance), from *enormis* (out of the norm).
