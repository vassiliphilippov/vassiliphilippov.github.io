beefed-up

1. ADJ

/ ˌbifdˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌbiːfdˈʌp / (BrE)
increased in strength, size, or effectiveness; made greater or stronger

Examples

This absence of peaky highs and beefed-up bass not only produces greater fidelity, but it eliminates listener fatigue.

Synset Examples

beefed-up sales efforts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strengthened - made stronger or more powerful
  • Synonym: reinforced - given extra strength or support
  • Antonym: weakened - made less strong or powerful

Etymology

From the slang verb "beef up," meaning to increase or strengthen, originally referring to adding beef (muscle or power).
