aristocratic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛr.ə.stəˈkræt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌær.ɪ.stəˈkræt.ɪk / (BrE)
Belonging to or characteristic of the aristocracy or nobility; having qualities associated with the upper class, such as refinement or exclusiveness.

Examples

In time she presents her aristocratic husband with a coal-black child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patrician - of noble or high rank; aristocratic
  • Synonym: noble - belonging to a hereditary class with high social or political status; aristocratic
  • Antonym: plebeian - of or belonging to the common people

Etymology

From French *aristocratique*, from Greek *aristokratikos*.
