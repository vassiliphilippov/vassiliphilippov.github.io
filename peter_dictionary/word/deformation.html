deformation

1. NOUN

/ ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːfɔːˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process or result of changing shape, especially so as to be damaged, misshapen, or impaired; the act of distorting or the state of being distorted.
