gossamer

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡɑːsəmər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsəmə / (BrE)
Extremely light, delicate, or tenuous; characterized by unusual lightness and delicacy.

Examples

Every detail in his interpretation has been beautifully thought out, and of these I would especially cite the delicious landler touch the pianist brings to the fifth variation (an obvious indication that he is playing with Viennese musicians), and the gossamer shading throughout.

Synset Examples

this smallest and most ethereal of birds
gossamer shading through his playing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ethereal - extremely delicate and light; seeming too perfect for this world
  • Synonym: airy - light in appearance or texture; delicate

Etymology

From Middle English 'gossomer' (referring to filmy cobwebs), probably from 'gos' (goose) + 'somer' (summer), originally referring to cobwebs seen in late autumn.
