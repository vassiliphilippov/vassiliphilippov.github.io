shaven

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪvən / (BrE)
(of a person or part of the body) having had the hair or beard cut off very close to the skin.

Examples

He knew the faces of all, hairy or shaven, old or young, fat or thin, suffering or hardened, sad or gay, good or bad.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unshaven - not having had the beard or hair recently cut; with stubble or hair

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'shave.'
