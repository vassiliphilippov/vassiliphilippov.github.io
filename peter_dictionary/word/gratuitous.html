gratuitous

1. ADJ

/ ɡrəˈtuːɪtəs / (AmE), / ɡrəˈtjuːɪtəs / (BrE)
done without a good reason, cause, or justification; unnecessary

Examples

The gruesome humor of the Nazis was not forgotten - the gas chamber with a sign on it with the name of a Jewish foundation and bearing a copper Star of David - nor the gratuitous sadism of SS officers.

Synset Examples

a gratuitous insult

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unwarranted - not justified or authorized
  • Antonym: justified - done for a good reason or with valid justification

Etymology

From Latin 'gratuitus' (given freely, spontaneous), from 'gratus' (pleasing, thankful).
