centennial

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈtɛniəl / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɛnɪəl / (BrE)
the 100th anniversary of an event, or a celebration marking it

Examples

Of these there are surely few that would be more rewarding discoveries than Verner von Heidenstam, the Swedish poet and novelist who received the award in 1916 and whose centennial was celebrated two years ago.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: bicentennial - the 200th anniversary or its celebration
  • Degree Higher: sesquicentennial - the 150th anniversary or its celebration

Etymology

From Latin *centum* (hundred) + *annus* (year) via French *centenaire*.
