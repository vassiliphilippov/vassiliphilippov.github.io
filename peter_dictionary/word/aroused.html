aroused

1. ADJ

/ əˈraʊzd / (AmE), / əˈraʊzd / (BrE)
in a state of heightened emotion or excitement; emotionally stimulated

Examples

Apparently academic challenge in the structured setting creates an optimum of stress so that the child with high anxiety is able to achieve because he is aroused to an energetic state without becoming confused or panicked.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: calm - not emotionally aroused; steady and relaxed

Etymology

Past participle of 'arouse', from Middle English 'arousen'.
