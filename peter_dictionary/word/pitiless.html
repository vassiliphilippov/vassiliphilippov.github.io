pitiless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɪt̬iɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪtɪlɪs / (BrE)
showing no mercy or compassion; cruel and unfeeling.

Examples

The foregoing, aided by several clues I 'll withhold to keep you on your toes, will pursue you with a tenacity worthy of Inspector Javert, but before they close in, gird yourselves, I repeat, for a vengeance infinitely more pitiless.

Synset Examples

an act of ruthless ferocity
a monster of remorseless cruelty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merciless - showing no mercy; very cruel
  • Synonym: ruthless - having no compassion or pity
  • Antonym: pitying - feeling or showing sympathy and compassion
  • Antonym: compassionate - feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others

Etymology

From 'pity' + '-less', meaning 'without pity'.
