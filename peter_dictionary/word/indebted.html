indebted

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɛtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛtɪd / (BrE)
owing gratitude or recognition to someone for help or favours received

Examples

I owe that monkey **my life**.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grateful - feeling or showing thanks

Etymology

From in- (in) + Middle English 'dette' (debt), after 'indebted' in Middle English.
