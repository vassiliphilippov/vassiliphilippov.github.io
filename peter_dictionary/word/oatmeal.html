oatmeal

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊtˌmiːl / (AmE), / ˈəʊtmiːl / (BrE)
A hot porridge made from rolled or ground oats, typically eaten for breakfast.

Examples

"Is it anything like cooked over oatmeal "? asked one of the children.

Etymology

From 'oat' + 'meal', where 'meal' refers to ground grain.
