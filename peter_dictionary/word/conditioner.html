conditioner

1. NOUN

/ kənˈdɪʃənɚ / (AmE), / kənˈdɪʃənə / (BrE)
an exercise or activity that improves or maintains physical fitness

Examples

For some white-collar workers it is a welcome change from the regular job, and a physical conditioner.

Synset Examples

farm work can be a good conditioner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise - activity requiring physical effort for health or fitness
