dandily

1. ADV

/ ˈdændɪli / (AmE), / ˈdændɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is stylish, fashionable, or showy, resembling a dandy.

Examples

As far as I was concerned, she had already and had dandily shown what she could do.

Synset Examples

she had shown her talents dandily

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dandy - a man who is very concerned about his clothes and appearance

Etymology

From "dandy" (a man who places particular importance upon physical appearance), with the adverbial suffix -ly.
