polluted

1. ADJ

/ pəˈluːtɪd / (AmE), / pəˈluːtɪd / (BrE)
Made harmful or unclean by the presence of harmful substances or contaminants.

Examples

Frontage on a body of clear, clean water will be vastly different from the same amount of frontage on polluted water.

Synset Examples

had to boil the contaminated water
polluted lakes and streams

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clean - free from dirt, marks, or impurities
  • Synonym: contaminated - made impure by exposure to or addition of a poisonous or polluting substance

Etymology

From Latin *pollutus*, past participle of *polluere* (to soil, defile).
