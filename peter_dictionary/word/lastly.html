lastly

1. ADV

/ ˈlæstli / (AmE), / ˈlɑːstli / (BrE)
used to introduce the final point or item in a list or sequence

Examples

Lastly, governmental and private planners will at this stage begin to see large capital requirements looming ahead.

Synset Examples

last, I'll discuss family values

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finally - used to introduce the final point or action

Etymology

From 'last' + '-ly', forming an adverb from the adjective 'last'.
