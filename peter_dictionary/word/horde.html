horde

1. NOUN

/ hɔrd / (AmE), / hɔːd / (BrE)
A large group of people or things, especially one that is seen as unruly or overwhelming.

Examples

There are hordes of nubile young women there who, prodded by their impatient mothers, are determined to marry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crowd - a large number of people gathered together
  • Synonym: throng - a densely packed crowd of people or animals

Etymology

From Turkish 'ordu' (camp, army) via Russian 'orda'.
