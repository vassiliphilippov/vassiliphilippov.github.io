issuance

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪʃuəns / (AmE), / ˈɪʃuəns / (BrE)
the act of officially supplying or distributing something, especially in quantity or for official purposes

Examples

Vandiver opened his race for governor in 1958 with a battle in the Legislature against the issuance of $ 50 million worth of additional rural roads bonds proposed by then Gov. Marvin Griffin.

Synset Examples

a new issue of stamps
the last issue of penicillin was over a month ago

Related Terms

  • Synonym: issue - the act of giving or supplying something officially

Etymology

From issue + -ance, modeled on similar nouns forming from verbs with the suffix -ance.
