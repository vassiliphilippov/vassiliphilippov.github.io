diploma

1. NOUN

/ dəˈploʊmə / (AmE), / dɪˈpləʊmə / (BrE)
An official document certifying that someone has successfully completed a course of study at a school, college, or university.

Examples

I also built that library and used it to help a dozen guys get a high school diploma.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certificate - an official document attesting to a fact, in this case completion of study
  • Degree Higher: degree - an academic qualification awarded by a university or college

Etymology

From Greek 'diplōma', meaning 'folded paper', via Latin 'diploma'.
