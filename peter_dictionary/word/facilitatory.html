facilitatory

1. ADJ

/ fəˈsɪlɪtəˌtɔri / (AmE), / fəˈsɪlɪtətəri / (BrE)
helping to promote or enhance a physiological or neural process, especially in the nervous system.

Examples

Facilitatory processes take place between neocortex and hypothalamus via ascending and descending pathways.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inhibitory - tending to suppress or restrain neural activity

Etymology

From 'facilitate' + '-ory', meaning serving to.
