externally

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈstɜrnəli / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or on the outside; outwardly.

Examples

I'm only laughing on the outside.

Synset Examples

outwardly, the figure is smooth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outwardly - in an outward or external manner

Etymology

From Latin 'externus' (outside, outward) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
