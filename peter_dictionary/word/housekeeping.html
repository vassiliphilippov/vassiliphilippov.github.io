housekeeping

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊsˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
The management of household tasks such as cleaning and general upkeep of a house.

Examples

The housekeeping problems of living in a shelter will begin as soon as the shelter is occupied.
After the family has settled in the shelter, the housekeeping rules should be spelled out by the adult in charge.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: housework - work such as cleaning and washing done in a house

Etymology

From 'house' + 'keeping', referring to the act of keeping (maintaining) a house.
