hangout

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæŋˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈhæŋˌaʊt / (BrE)
a place that someone regularly spends time at, especially to relax or socialize

Examples

He even makes the city look like one of Thoreau's hangouts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haunt - a place frequently visited by someone

Etymology

From the phrasal verb 'hang out', meaning to spend time idly or socially.
