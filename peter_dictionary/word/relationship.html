Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: connection - a relationship in which a person or thing is linked or associated with something else
  • Synonym: bond - a strong connection or feeling of friendship between people
  • Synonym: affair - a romantic or sexual relationship, often secret or illicit
  • Antonym: alienation - the state of being isolated from a group or activity, often the opposite of a close relationship

Etymology

Formed from 'relation' + suffix '-ship', with 'relation' from Latin 'relatio' (a bringing back, report).
