illuminating

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈluːməˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
providing new knowledge or insight; making something easier to understand

Examples

But a somewhat more detailed analysis of this process may be illuminating.
Perhaps the most illuminating example of the reduction of fear through understanding is derived from our increased knowledge of the nature of disease.
But since 1927, researchers digging into ancient court records and legal files have been able to find illuminating pieces of information.

Synset Examples

an enlightening glimpse of government in action
an illuminating lecture

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enlightening - giving greater knowledge and understanding
  • Antonym: confusing - making something difficult to understand

Etymology

From Latin 'illuminare' (to light up, make clear).
