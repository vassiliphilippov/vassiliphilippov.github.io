overachieve

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvərəˈtʃiv / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəˈtʃiːv / (BrE)
to perform better or achieve greater success than is expected or considered typical

Examples

You could do much **better**.

Synset Examples

His daughter always overachieves

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underachieve - perform less well than expected or potential

Etymology

From over- (above, beyond) + achieve.
