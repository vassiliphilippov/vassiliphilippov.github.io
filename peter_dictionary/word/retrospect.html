retrospect

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛtrəˌspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌspɛkt / (BrE)
the act of thinking about or reviewing past events or situations

Examples

Statistically this has represented a loss to the nation, although one must admit that in an individual case the decision in retrospect may have been a wise one.

Synset Examples

in retrospect

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recollection - the action or faculty of remembering something
  • Antonym: anticipation - the action of anticipating something; expectation or prediction

Etymology

From Latin 'retrospectus', past participle of 'retrospecere' (to look back).
