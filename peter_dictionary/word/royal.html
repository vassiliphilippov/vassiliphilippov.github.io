royal

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɔɪəl / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl / (BrE)
Relating to, belonging to, or issued by a king, queen, or other monarch.

Etymology

From Old French *reial*, from Latin *regalis* (regal, kingly).
