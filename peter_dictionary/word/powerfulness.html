powerfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊ.ɚ.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of having great power or strength.

Examples

The "reality "to which they respond is rationally empty and their art is an imitation of the inescapable powerfulness of this unknown and empty world.

Synset Examples

the deterrent power of nuclear weapons
the power of his love saved her
his powerfulness was concealed by a gentle facade

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power - the ability to control people or things
  • Antonym: weakness - lack of strength or power

Etymology

From 'powerful' + '-ness', meaning the state or quality of being powerful.
