assortment

1. NOUN

/ əˈsɔrtmənt / (AmE), / əˈsɔːtmənt / (BrE)
A collection of different kinds of things or people.

Examples

How could anyone know what to do with an assortment like that?

Synset Examples

a great assortment of cars was on display
he had a variety of disorders
a veritable smorgasbord of religions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: variety - a number of different types or things; diversity
  • Synonym: selection - a number of things chosen from a larger group
  • Synonym: mixture - a combination of different things

Etymology

From French *assortiment*, from assortir (to sort, match).
