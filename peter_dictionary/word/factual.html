factual

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæk.tʃu.əl / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tʃu.əl / (BrE)
based on or relating to facts; existing in reality rather than imagined or theoretical.

Examples

He will not be surprised to find that general principles produce conflicting results in the factual situation with which he is confronted.

Synset Examples

rocks and trees...the actual world
actual heroism
the actual things that produced the emotion you experienced

Related Terms

  • Synonym: actual - existing in fact; real and not imagined or supposed
  • Antonym: fictional - invented for a story; not real

Etymology

From fact + -ual, patterned after actual.
