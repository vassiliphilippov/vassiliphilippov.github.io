stalked

1. ADJ

/ stɔkt / (AmE), / stɔːkt / (BrE)
(of a plant or organism) having or growing on a stalk or peduncle.

Examples

Only once were stalked ciliates found in the mixed liquor.

Synset Examples

a pedunculate flower
a pedunculate barnacle is attached to the substrate by a fleshy foot or stalk

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sessile - attached directly by the base, without a stalk
