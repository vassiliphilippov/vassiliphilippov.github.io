fillip

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪl.ɪp / (AmE), / ˈfɪl.ɪp / (BrE)
a stimulus or incentive that encourages activity or enthusiasm

Examples

Hugh Downs is heard interviewing Wright, for an added prestige fillip.

Synset Examples

his approval was an added fillip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boost - an act that encourages or improves; a stimulus
  • Synonym: impetus - something that makes a process or activity happen or happen more quickly

Etymology

From Middle English philippen (to strike with a finger), from Old English filippen.
