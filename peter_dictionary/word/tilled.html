tilled

1. ADJ

/ tɪld / (AmE), / tɪld / (BrE)
(of land) turned, stirred, or prepared for planting by plowing, harrowing, or hoeing.

Examples

Abel sat and regarded the farm country which, spreading out from both sides of the road, rolled greenly up to where the silent white houses and long barns and silos nested into the tilled fields.

Synset Examples

tilled land ready for seed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untilled - (of land) not turned or prepared for cultivation
