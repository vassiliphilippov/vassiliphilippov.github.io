thoroughfare

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝː.oʊˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈθʌr.əˌfeə / (BrE)
A main road or public street that people or vehicles use to travel from one place to another.

Examples

Along the main thoroughfares hardly a house had not been peppered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: street - a public road in a city or town
  • Synonym: avenue - a wide road, typically in a city

Etymology

From Middle English 'thorowfare', from 'thorow' (through) + 'fare' (journey, road).
