Cuba

1. PROPN

/ ˈkjuːbə / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbə / (BrE)
An island country in the Caribbean, officially the Republic of Cuba, known for its communist government since 1959.

Examples

He was marching up and down the locker room of the Grassy Brae Golf Club shouting, "Bomb Cuba!

Etymology

From Spanish 'Cuba', the name of the island; likely of Taíno origin.
