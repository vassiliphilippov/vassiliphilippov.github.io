unshielded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃildɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃiːldɪd / (BrE)
Not protected by a shield; exposed, especially referring to machinery or equipment.

Examples

The unshielded flux is given in the last column; these figures constitute the best estimate for the flux in interplanetary space near the Earth.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shielded - protected by a shield

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'shielded' (provided with a shield).
