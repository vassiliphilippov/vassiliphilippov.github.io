environmental

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˌvaɪɹənˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ɪnˌvaɪɹənˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to the external conditions or surroundings that affect someone or something, especially in relation to nature or ecology

Examples

This temperature was taken as environmental temperature to which the anode holder was exposed as far as radiation is concerned.

Synset Examples

environmental factors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ecological - relating to ecology or the environment

Etymology

From environment + -al, formed in English.
