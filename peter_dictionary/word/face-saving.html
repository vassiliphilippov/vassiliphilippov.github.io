face-saving

1. ADJ

/ ˈfeɪsˌseɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪsˌseɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
intended to preserve one's dignity, prestige, or avoid embarrassment

Examples

Not without a face-saving respite of at least a few minutes.

Synset Examples

a face-saving compromise

Related Terms

  • Antonym: humiliating - causing a loss of dignity or self-respect
  • Synonym: compromising - accepting standards that are lower than desirable, sometimes to avoid conflict

Etymology

From the phrase "save face," meaning to preserve one's reputation or dignity.
