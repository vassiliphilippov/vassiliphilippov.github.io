penalty

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnəlti / (AmE), / ˈpɛnəlti / (BrE)
A punishment imposed for breaking a law, rule, or contract.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: reward - something given in recognition of service, effort, or achievement; opposite of penalty
