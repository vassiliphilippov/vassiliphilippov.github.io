washbowl

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑʃˌboʊl / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃbəʊl / (BrE)
A bowl-shaped bathroom sink that is permanently installed with a water supply and drain, used for washing hands and face.

Examples

The lieutenant eyed Gun's reflection in the mirror over the washbowl and then glanced back at his own face, moving the comb methodically around his head.

Synset Examples

he ran some water in the basin and splashed it on his face

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washbasin - a basin fixed in a bathroom, used for washing hands and face
  • Synonym: sink - a fixed basin with a water supply and drain, used for washing

Etymology

From 'wash' + 'bowl'; meaning a bowl for washing, attested since the 18th century.
