mythic

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪθɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or having the qualities of myth; resembling or characteristic of myths.

Examples

And the common man was developing mythic power, or charisma, on his own.

Synset Examples

a novel of almost mythic consequence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mythical - imaginary or characteristic of myths

Etymology

From Greek 'mythikos', from 'mythos' (myth).
