twenty-fourth

1. ADJ

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
coming after the twenty-third in a sequence; 24th

Examples

On June 24 more than 400 families started the three month trip across the plains to the Mississippi.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenty-third - coming after the twenty-second; 23rd ordinal
  • Degree Higher: twenty-fifth - coming after the twenty-fourth; 25th ordinal
