boyfriend

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔɪˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
A man with whom someone is having a romantic or sexual relationship, especially one who is not married to them.

Examples

Two months ago he took her for a drive with another boyfriend.

Synset Examples

if I'd known he was her boyfriend I wouldn't have asked

Related Terms

  • Antonym: girlfriend - a woman with whom someone is having a romantic relationship

Etymology

Formed from 'boy' + 'friend'. Originally used in the late 19th century to mean a male friend, later acquiring its current romantic sense.
