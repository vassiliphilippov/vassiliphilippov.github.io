biter

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtə / (BrE)
A person or animal that bites.

Examples

"I fear explanations explanatory of things explained ", he said, leaving the biter bit - and bitter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biter - informal term for someone who plagiarizes or copies others (slang)
  • Antonym: non-biter - someone who does not bite
  • Synonym: biter - a biting insect or animal

Etymology

From Middle English 'biteren', from Old English 'bītan' (to bite) + '-er'.
