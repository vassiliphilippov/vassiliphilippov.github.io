Aristotelian

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛrɪstəˈtiliən / (AmE), / ˌærɪstəˈtiːliən / (BrE)
Relating to Aristotle or his philosophy and teachings.

Examples

The Aristotelian notion of catharsis, the purging of emotion, is a persistent and viable one.

Synset Examples

Aristotelean logic

Etymology

From Aristotle, the name of the ancient Greek philosopher, + -ian (forming adjectives/nouns).
