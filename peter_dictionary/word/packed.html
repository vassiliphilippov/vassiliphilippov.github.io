packed

1. ADJ

/ pækt / (AmE), / pækt / (BrE)
Completely full; filled to capacity, so that no more can be added.

Examples

At times, clumps of 10 to 15 closely packed nuclei were also observed.

Synset Examples

a suitcase jammed with dirty clothes
stands jam-packed with fans
a packed theater

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jammed - filled tightly; crowded to capacity
  • Antonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled
