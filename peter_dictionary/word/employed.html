employed

1. ADJ

/ ɪmˈplɔɪd / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪd / (BrE)
Having a paid job; having one's services engaged for wages or salary.

Examples

The official statistics show that 60 per cent are employed workers or independent professional people.

Synset Examples

most of our graduates are employed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unemployed - not having a paid job

Etymology

From the verb 'employ' + -ed suffix, denoting past participle and adjectival use.
