forties

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔr.tiz / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tiz / (BrE)
The period of a person's life between the ages of 40 and 49.

Examples

In 1610, Hudson was probably in his early forties, a good navigator, a stubborn voyager, but otherwise fatally unsuited to his chosen profession.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirties - the period of life from 30 to 39
  • Degree Higher: fifties - the period of life from 50 to 59
