unanswered

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈæn.sɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːn.səd / (BrE)
not replied to, responded to, or returned in kind; not having a response or answer.

Examples

In fact, insofar as science generates any fear, it stems not so much from scientific prowess and gadgets but from the fact that new unanswered questions arise, which, until they are understood, create uncertainty.

Synset Examples

unrequited (unanswered) love

Related Terms

  • Antonym: answered - replied to or responded to

Etymology

From un- (not) + answered (past participle of 'answer').
