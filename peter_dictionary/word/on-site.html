on-site

1. ADJ

/ ɑnˈsaɪt / (AmE), / ɒnˈsaɪt / (BrE)
Located at or happening at the place where something is being done or where an activity is taking place.

Examples

cause on-site inspections to be made of promising projects, domestic and foreign, and, in the case of projects located in the United States, cooperate and participate in their development in instances in which the purposes of this Act will be served thereby;

Synset Examples

on-site inspection

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off-site - occurring or situated away from the site of activity
