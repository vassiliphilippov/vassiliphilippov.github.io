catchphrase

1. NOUN

/ ˈkætʃˌfreɪz / (AmE), / ˈkatʃˌfreɪz / (BrE)
A well-known phrase or expression, especially one that is associated with a particular person, group, or context and is often repeated.

Examples

"Don't leave home without it."

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slogan - a short, memorable phrase used in advertising or by groups
  • Synonym: catchword - a word or phrase that catches attention, often as a slogan

Etymology

From 'catch' + 'phrase', indicating a phrase that catches or attracts attention.
