talkative

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɔk.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈtɔː.kə.tɪv / (BrE)
Tending to talk a lot, especially about unimportant matters.

Examples

Shadow, the more talkative, began at once to talk, her voice piteous.

Synset Examples

kept from her housework by gabby neighbors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chatty - inclined to talk informally or in a friendly way

Etymology

From Middle English talken (to talk) + -ative (forming adjectives).
