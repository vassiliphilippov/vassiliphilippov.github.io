statesmanship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪts.mənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsteɪts.mən.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the skill and wisdom in managing public affairs, particularly as a government leader

Examples

National leaders will have to display the highest skills of statesmanship to guide their people through times of uncertainty and confusion which destroy men's sense of identity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diplomacy - skill in managing international relations

Etymology

Formed from 'statesman' + suffix '-ship', denoting quality or skill.
