combo

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑm.boʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.bəʊ / (BrE)
A small group of musicians, especially one playing jazz or popular music together.

Examples

A combo was staying there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: band - a group of musicians who play together
  • Degree Higher: orchestra - a large group of musicians with string, brass, woodwind, and percussion sections

Etymology

Shortened form of 'combination', first attested in American English in the 20th century in reference to musical groups.
