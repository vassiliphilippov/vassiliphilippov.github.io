integrated

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪntəˌɡreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪd / (BrE)
combined to form or function as a whole; unified.

Examples

It is, moreover, a perfectly integrated ensemble effort.
The goal will be to provide the reader with an integrated rationale to aid him in applying roleplaying techniques in this unique training area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unified - brought together as a single unit or entity
  • Antonym: fragmented - broken into pieces; not united or coherent

Etymology

From Latin 'integratus', past participle of 'integrare' (to make whole).
