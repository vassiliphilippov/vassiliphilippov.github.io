grassfire

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡræsˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːsˌfaɪə / (BrE)
An uncontrolled fire that burns in an area covered with grass.

Examples

Rumor of a big battle spread like a grassfire up the valley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wildfire - an uncontrolled fire in a wilderness area

Etymology

From 'grass' + 'fire', indicating a fire occurring in grass-covered areas.
