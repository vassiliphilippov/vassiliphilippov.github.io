marvelously

1. ADV

/ ˈmɑrvələsli / (AmE), / ˈmɑːvələsli / (BrE)
Extremely well; in a way that causes great admiration or wonder.

Examples

They slept - Mynheer with a marvelously high-pitched snoring, the damn seahorse ivory teeth watching him from a bedside table.

Synset Examples

her voice is superbly disciplined
the colors changed wondrously slowly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wonderfully - in a wonderful or extremely pleasing way

Etymology

From the adjective 'marvelous', from Old French *merveilleux* (marvelous), from *merveille* (marvel), ultimately from Latin *mirabilia* (wonderful things).
