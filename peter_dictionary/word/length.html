length

1. NOUN

/ lɛŋkθ / (AmE), / lɛŋθ / (BrE)
The measurement or extent of something from end to end; the greatest of the dimensions of an object or space.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: width - extent of something from side to side
  • Antonym: height - measurement from base to top
  • Synonym: distance - amount of space between two points

Etymology

From Old English 'lengðu', from 'lang' (long).
