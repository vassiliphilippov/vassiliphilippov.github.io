clammy

1. ADJ

/ ˈklæmi / (AmE), / ˈklæmi / (BrE)
Unpleasantly damp, cold, and sticky to the touch.

Examples

The clammy air revived him.

Synset Examples

a clammy handshake
clammy weather
a dank cellar
dank rain forests

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dank - unpleasantly damp and cold
  • Antonym: dry - free from moisture or dampness

Etymology

Origin uncertain; perhaps from clam (as in sticky) plus -y.
