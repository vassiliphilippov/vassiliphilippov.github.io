shyly

1. ADV

/ ˈʃaɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪ.li / (BrE)
In a manner showing shyness, timidity, or bashfulness.

Examples

Almost immediately she was ashamed of herself for feeling vain, at such a time, in such a place, and she tossed back her long yellow hair, smiling shyly as she entered the room.

Synset Examples

he smiled shyly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bashfully - in a bashful or shy manner
  • Antonym: boldly - in a confident and courageous manner
