parenthetically

1. ADV

/ ˌpɛrənˈθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌparənˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is expressed as a parenthesis or as an aside; in a manner that adds incidental or extra information.

Examples

I mean...

Synset Examples

he added parenthetically that he would not attend the wedding ceremony

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incidentally - introducing a new topic or adding information as an aside

Etymology

From parenthetical + -ly; ultimately from Greek 'parenthēsis' meaning interpolation.
