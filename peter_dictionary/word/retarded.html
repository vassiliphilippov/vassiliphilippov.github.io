retarded

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈtɑrdɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈtɑːtɪd / (BrE)
(offensive, dated) Having a slower than normal mental, emotional, or physical development; formerly used to refer to people with intellectual disabilities.

Examples

(Call it mentally young; call it retarded.)

Synset Examples

providing a secure and sometimes happy life for the retarded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: developmentally delayed - having slower than usual mental or physical development (preferred, non-offensive)
  • Antonym: normal - having typical mental or physical development

Etymology

From Latin retardare (to delay, slow up) via French retarder.
