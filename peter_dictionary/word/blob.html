blob

1. NOUN

/ blɑb / (AmE), / blɒb / (BrE)
a small drop or lump of something, typically with an indistinct or shapeless form

Examples

"Arianist "! a rowdy with a big blob of a nose roared.
The pale blob of the woman disappeared.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spot - a small round mark or stain
  • Synonym: splotch - an irregularly shaped spot or blotch

Etymology

Origin unknown, possibly imitative of the shape or sound.
