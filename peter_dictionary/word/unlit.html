unlit

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlɪt / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪt / (BrE)
not set afire or burning; not illuminated.

Examples

Lights out!

Synset Examples

the table was bare, the candles unlighted
held an unlit cigarette

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lit - set afire or burning; illuminated

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'lit', the past participle of 'light'.
