crummy

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrʌmi / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmi / (BrE)
Of very poor quality; shabby or inferior.

Examples

If the crummy bastard could write!
Jesus, imagine, the crummy bastards, they 'll get em, they 'll get what's comin to em * * h He whirled about suddenly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoddy - badly made or of poor quality
  • Antonym: excellent - of very high quality

Etymology

Probably from 'crumb', originally meaning 'covered in crumbs, dirty'.
