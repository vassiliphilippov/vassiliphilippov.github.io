pixie

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk.si / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.si / (BrE)
(in folklore) a small, mischievous supernatural being believed to inhabit forests and other natural places, similar to an elf or fairy.

Examples

Then the pixies and the zombies took over while the banshees wailed in the distance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elf - a small, often mischievous, supernatural creature in folklore
  • Synonym: fairy - a small magical being with wings in folklore

Etymology

Probably from Cornish *piskie*, of uncertain origin.
