enquiry

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈkwaɪri / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪəri / (BrE)
A question or request for information, especially a formal or official one.

Examples

In response to your repeated enquiries, the State has allocated the enclosed funds for your library project."

Synset Examples

there was a question about my training
we made inquiries of all those who were present

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inquiry - question or formal request for information (American spelling)
  • Synonym: question - something asked to get information

Etymology

From Old French *enquerre* (to ask), from Latin *inquirere* (to seek, investigate).
