vehement

1. ADJ

/ ˈviːəmənt / (AmE), / ˈviːəmənt / (BrE)
Showing strong and passionate feeling; marked by extreme intensity of emotions or convictions.

Examples

The already faded pastel charms of the naive music itself vanish entirely in Fistoulari's melodramatic contrasts between ultra vehement brute power and chilly, if suave, sentimentality.

Synset Examples

fierce loyalty
in a tearing rage
vehement dislike
violent passions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fierce - showing intense aggressiveness or intensity
  • Synonym: passionate - showing or caused by strong feelings or a strong belief
  • Antonym: mild - gentle or not strong in effect, feeling, or intensity

Etymology

From Latin 'vehemens', meaning 'impetuous, violent'.
