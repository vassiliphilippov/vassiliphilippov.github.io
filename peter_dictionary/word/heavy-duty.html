heavy-duty

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɛviˈduti / (AmE), / ˌhɛviˈdjuːti / (BrE)
(of equipment, materials, etc.) designed to withstand hard use or perform difficult tasks; suitable for heavy work.

Examples

The best known field of application for builders is in heavy-duty, spray-dried detergent formulations for household use.

Synset Examples

a heavy-duty detergent
heavy-duty gloves

Related Terms

  • Antonym: light-duty - designed for less demanding or lighter work
