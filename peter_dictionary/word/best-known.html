best-known

1. ADJ

/ ˌbɛstˈnoʊn / (AmE), / ˌbɛstˈnəʊn / (BrE)
most widely known or famous; most familiar to the public within a particular group or field

Examples

The best known field of application for builders is in heavy-duty, spray-dried detergent formulations for household use.

Synset Examples

Stevenson's best-known work is probably `Treasure Island'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-known - widely recognized or familiar
  • Antonym: unknown - not known or familiar
