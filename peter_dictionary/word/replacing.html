replacing

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpleɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈpleɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of substituting one person or thing for another; the process of providing an equivalent in place of something else

Examples

The first is compiling a list of text forms, assigning an information cell to each, and replacing text occurrences with the information cell assigned to the form of each occurrence.

Synset Examples

replacing the star will not be easy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: replacement - the act of replacing; a person or thing that replaces another

Etymology

From the verb 'replace' (from re- 'again' + place), with the '-ing' suffix forming a noun indicating the act or process.
