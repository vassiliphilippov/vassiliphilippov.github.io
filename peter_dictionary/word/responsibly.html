responsibly

1. ADV

/ rɪˈspɑːnsəbli / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that shows good judgment, reliability, or a sense of duty; in a responsible manner.

Examples

Mythological concepts may by all means still be used, but they can be used responsibly only as "symbols "or "ciphers ", that is, only if they are also constantly interpreted in non mythological (or existential) terms.

Synset Examples

we must act responsibly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: irresponsibly - in a way that lacks responsibility

Etymology

From 'responsible' + '-ly'; 'responsible' from Late Latin 'responsabilis' (answerable).
