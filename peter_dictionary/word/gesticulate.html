gesticulate

1. VERB

/ dʒəˈstɪkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to express or indicate something by making movements or gestures, especially while speaking

Examples

Watson gesticulated wildly.

Synset Examples

He gestured his desire to leave

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gesture - to make a movement of the body or hands to express an idea or feeling

Etymology

From Latin 'gesticulatus', past participle of 'gesticulare', meaning 'to gesture'.
