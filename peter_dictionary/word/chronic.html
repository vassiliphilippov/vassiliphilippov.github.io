chronic

1. ADJ

/ ˈkrɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈkrɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
(of an illness or problem) persisting for a long time or constantly recurring, often difficult to eradicate or cure.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: acute - (of diseases) characterized by rapid onset and/or short duration

Etymology

From Greek *khronikos* (of time), from *khronos* (time).
