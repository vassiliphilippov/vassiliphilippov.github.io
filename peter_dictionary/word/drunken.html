drunken

1. ADJ

/ ˈdrʌŋkən / (AmE), / ˈdrʌŋkən / (BrE)
affected by or showing the effects of alcoholic drink; given to excessive drinking.

Examples

The Death's-Head Corps took in Ukrainian and Baltic Auxiliaries, and the Einsatzkommandos waded knee-deep in blood and turned into drunken, dope ridden maniacs.
"I saw a man once beat his wife When on a drunken spree.
They get all the glory and most of the pay and do n't earn ten cents apiece on the average, the drunken rascals ".

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol
  • Synonym: inebriated - under the influence of alcohol
  • Synonym: intoxicated - affected by alcohol

Etymology

From Old English *druncen*, past participle of *drincan* (to drink).
