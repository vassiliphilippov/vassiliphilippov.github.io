near-blind

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɪr ˈblaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈnɪə ˈblaɪnd / (BrE)
Having greatly reduced vision; almost blind.

Examples

He started down the steps to meet the near-blind preacher, who had been one of the early Gospelers in Paris.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: blind - unable to see; sightless
