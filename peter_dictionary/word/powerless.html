powerless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpaʊər.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə.ləs / (BrE)
Not having power or ability to act; unable to control or influence a situation.

Examples

Science has simply left us helpless and powerless in this important sector of our lives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: helpless - unable to help oneself; lacking strength or power
  • Antonym: powerful - having great power or influence

Etymology

From 'power' + the suffix '-less,' meaning 'without'.
