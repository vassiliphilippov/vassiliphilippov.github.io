stunning

1. ADJ

/ ˈstʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely impressive or attractive; commanding attention due to beauty, excellence, or impact.

Examples

The costumes are stunning evocations of the voluminous gowns and picture hats of the Gibson Girl days.

Synset Examples

an arresting drawing of people turning into animals
a sensational concert--one never to be forgotten
a stunning performance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: striking - impressively noticeable or attractive
  • Antonym: unimpressive - not evoking admiration or attention

Etymology

From 'stun' (to amaze, bewilder) + -ing (adjective-forming suffix).
