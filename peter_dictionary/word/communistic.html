communistic

1. ADJ

/ kəˌmjuːˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of communism or communists.

Examples

Perhaps one way to sharpen our sense of the modernity of Utopian communism is to contrast it with the principal earlier types of communistic theory.

Synset Examples

Communist Party
communist governments
communistic propaganda

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - relating to communism or a supporter of communism

Etymology

From 'communist', ultimately from French 'communiste', from 'commun' (common).
