puncture

1. VERB

/ ˈpʌŋktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋktʃə / (BrE)
to pierce or make a small hole in something with a sharp object

Examples

Do avoid puncturing or cutting into meats to test them.

Synset Examples

puncture a tire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pierce - to penetrate or make a way through with a sharp instrument

Etymology

From Latin punctura (a pricking), from pungere (to prick).
