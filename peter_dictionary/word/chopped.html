chopped

1. ADJ

/ tʃɑpt / (AmE), / tʃɒpt / (BrE)
(of food) cut into small pieces, especially for cooking or serving.

Examples

A kid comes up to me in a white jacket, gives me a Ritz cracker with chopped liver and says, "canapes."
That'’s a Ritz cracker and chopped liver!" [LAUGHING] Bring out the peppers and sardines!

Synset Examples

sliced tomatoes
sliced ham
chopped clams
chopped meat
shredded cabbage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: minced - (of food) cut up into very small pieces, usually by machine
  • Synonym: shredded - (of food) cut or torn into thin strips
  • Antonym: whole - not cut up or prepared; entire
