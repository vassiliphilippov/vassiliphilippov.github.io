dental

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛntəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl / (BrE)
Relating to the teeth.

Examples

The brush moves up and down and is small enough to clean every dental surface, including the back of the teeth.
Several dentists and patients with special dental problems have experimented with the device.

Synset Examples

dental floss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tooth - each of the hard, white structures in the mouth

Etymology

From Latin 'dentālis', from 'dens' (tooth).
