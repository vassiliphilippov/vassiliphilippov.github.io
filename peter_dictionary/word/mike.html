mike

1. NOUN

/ maɪk / (AmE), / maɪk / (BrE)
An informal term for a microphone, a device that converts sound waves into electrical energy for recording or amplification.

Examples

"Mike darling "- They kissed, and went on kissing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: microphone - Device that converts sound into electrical signals

Etymology

Shortened from 'microphone'.
