ruefully

1. ADV

/ ˈruː.fəli / (AmE), / ˈruː.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows sorrow or regret.

Examples

"It is possible that I over corrected ", he said ruefully.

Synset Examples

`I made a big mistake,' he said ruefully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regretfully - in a regretful manner; with regret

Etymology

From 'rueful' + '-ly'; 'rueful' from 'rue' (to regret), Old English 'hrēowan'.
