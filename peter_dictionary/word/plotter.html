plotter

1. NOUN

/ ˈplɑːtər / (AmE), / ˈplɒtə / (BrE)
a person who secretly plans to do something illegal or harmful, especially as part of a group

Examples

When a plot against the Emperor failed, the plotters were always given a chance to let their families keep their fortunes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspirator - person involved in a conspiracy
  • Synonym: accomplice - person who helps another commit a crime

Etymology

From 'plot' (to scheme or plan secretly) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
