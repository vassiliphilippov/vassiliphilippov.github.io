contributing

1. ADJ

/ kənˈtrɪbjutɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a factor or cause) helping to produce or bring about an effect or result; playing a part in something.

Examples

A contributing factor is the perspective, the uniform aesthetic distance which is maintained.

Synset Examples

working conditions are not conducive to productivity
the seaport was a contributing factor in the growth of the city
a contributory factor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conducive - making a certain situation or outcome likely or possible
  • Synonym: contributory - helping to cause or bring about

Etymology

From the verb 'contribute', from Latin 'contributus', past participle of 'contribuere' (to bring together, add).
