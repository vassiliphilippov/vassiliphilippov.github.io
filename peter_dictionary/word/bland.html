bland

1. ADJ

/ blænd / (AmE), / blænd / (BrE)
lacking strong taste or flavor; mild or insipid in taste

Examples

The flavor is neither sweet, like a pear, nor tart like an orange; it is subtle and rather bland, nut like.

Synset Examples

a bland diet
insipid hospital food
flavorless supermarket tomatoes
vapid beer
vapid tea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insipid - lacking flavor; dull or uninteresting
  • Synonym: flavorless - without taste or flavor
  • Synonym: tasteless - having little or no flavor

Etymology

From Latin 'blandus' meaning 'mild, smooth, flattering'.
