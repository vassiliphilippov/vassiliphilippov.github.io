unquestioningly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you do something without doubt or hesitation, without questioning

Examples

He is not one to remain more comfortably and unquestioningly within a body of social, cultural, or literary traditions than he was within the traditions - or possibly the regulations - governing his tenure in the post office at Oxford, Mississippi, thirty-five years ago.

Synset Examples

he followed his leader unquestioningly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: doubtfully - in a way that shows doubt or uncertainty
