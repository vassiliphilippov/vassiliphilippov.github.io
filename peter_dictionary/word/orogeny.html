orogeny

1. NOUN

/ əˈrɑːdʒəˌni / (AmE), / ɒˈrɒdʒɪni / (BrE)
the process of mountain formation, especially by the upward displacement of the Earth's crust

Examples

An Ice Age here, a million years of mountain-building there.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mountain-building - the geological process of forming mountains

Etymology

From Greek 'oros' (mountain) + '-geny' (origin, formation).
