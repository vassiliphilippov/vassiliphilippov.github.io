calorimetric

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæləˈrɪmɪtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæl.ər.ɪˈmɛt.rɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of heat, especially by means of a calorimeter.

Examples

The element is inserted in the discharge circuit in place of the exploding wire, and the calorimetric heating of the element is measured with high accuracy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: calorimetry - the science of measuring heat

Etymology

From French calorimétrique, from 'calorimetry' (Latin 'calor' meaning heat + Greek 'metron' meaning measure).
