unthematic

1. ADJ

/ ʌnθəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ʌnθɪˈmatɪk / (BrE)
Not related to or characterized by a theme, especially not concerning a recurring melodic subject in music.

Examples

In method as well as in theme this little anecdote with its details selected as much for expressiveness and allegory as for "realism ", anticipates a kind of musical composition, as well as a kind of fictional composition, in which, as Leverku^hn says, "there shall be nothing unthematic ".

Synset Examples

there is nothing unthematic in this composition

Related Terms

  • Antonym: thematic - relating to a theme or melodic subject

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' meaning 'not' and 'thematic' relating to 'theme'.
