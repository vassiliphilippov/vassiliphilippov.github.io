offering

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑfərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒfərɪŋ / (BrE)
something that is offered, such as a proposal, bid, or product made available for consideration or purchase

Examples

The Orioles once again performed at the plate in powderpuff fashion, gathering only seven blows off the offerings of three Kansas City pitchers.

Synset Examples

noteworthy new offerings for investors included several index funds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proposal - a suggestion, especially a formal or written one, put forward for consideration
  • Synonym: bid - an offer of a price, especially at an auction

Etymology

From Old English offrung (an offering, sacrifice), from offrian (to offer).
