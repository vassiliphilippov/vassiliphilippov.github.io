Related Terms

  • Synonym: black - completely without light or color; the darkest color
  • Antonym: bright - full of light; shining strongly
  • Degree Lower: dim - having only a little light; not bright
  • Degree Higher: pitch-dark - completely dark; having no light at all

Etymology

From Old English *deorc* (dark, obscure).
