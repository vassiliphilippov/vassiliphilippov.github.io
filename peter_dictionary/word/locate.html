1. VERB

/ loʊˈkeɪt / (AmE), / ləʊˈkeɪt / (BrE)

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: find - to discover or come upon by searching or effort
  • Antonym: lose - to be unable to find or keep something

Etymology

From Latin locare (to place, to set), from locus (place).
