unconstitutional

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnkənˌstɪˈtuʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkɒnˌstɪˈtjuːʃənəl / (BrE)
not allowed by or not in accordance with a constitution, especially the constitution of a particular country; illegal under a constitution

Examples

Cox argued that the bill is "probably unconstitutional "since, he said, it would impair contracts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: constitutional - in accordance with a constitution
  • Synonym: illegal - forbidden by law

Etymology

From un- (not) + constitutional; constitutional from Latin constitutio (establishment, ordinance).
