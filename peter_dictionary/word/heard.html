heard

1. ADJ

/ hɝd / (AmE), / hɜːd / (BrE)
Perceived or detected by the sense of hearing.

Examples

How come I never heard of you before?

Synset Examples

a conversation heard through the wall

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unheard - not heard; not perceived by the ear

Etymology

Past participle of 'hear', from Old English 'hieran'.
