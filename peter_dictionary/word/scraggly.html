scraggly

1. ADJ

/ ˈskræɡli / (AmE), / ˈskræɡli / (BrE)
(of hair, vegetation, or similar) thin, uneven, and untidy or lacking neatness.

Examples

The old man gave the room a stare in leaving; under the scraggly brows the pale old eyes burned with a bitter memory.

Synset Examples

the old man's scraggly beard
a scraggly little path to the door

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unkempt - untidy or messy in appearance
  • Antonym: tidy - neat, orderly in appearance
