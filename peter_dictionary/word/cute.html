cute

1. ADJ

/ kjuːt / (AmE), / kjuːt / (BrE)
(of actions, behavior, or things) deliberately or obviously designed to be charming, sometimes to the point of annoyance or affectation.

Examples

The daughter replied, "Oh, I had dinner with - well, you do n't know him but he's awfully nice - and we went to a couple of places - I do n't suppose you 've heard of them - and we finished up at a cute little night club - I forget the name of it.

Synset Examples

an insufferably precious performance
a child with intolerably cute mannerisms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precious - overly affected or contrivedly charming
  • Synonym: affected - artificial or pretentious in manner

2. ADJ

/ kjuːt / (AmE), / kjuːt / (BrE)
attractive in a pretty or endearing way, often because of smallness or charm

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plain - not beautiful or attractive

Etymology

Originally US slang, possibly from 'acute' (sharp, clever).
