indistinguishable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to tell apart or recognize as different; exactly alike.

Examples

From the beginning of commercial recording, new discs purported to be indistinguishable from The Real Thing have regularly been put in circulation.

Synset Examples

rows of identical houses
cars identical except for their license plates
they wore indistinguishable hats

Related Terms

  • Synonym: identical - exactly the same; alike in every way
  • Antonym: different - not the same as another; dissimilar

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + distinguere (to separate, distinguish).
