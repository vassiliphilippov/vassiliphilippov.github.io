moonlit

1. ADJ

/ ˈmuːnˌlɪt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnlɪt / (BrE)
Illuminated by the light of the moon.

Examples

The moonlit night was made for romance, and he had been looking at her soulfully for some time.

Synset Examples

the moonlit landscape

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sunlit - illuminated by sunlight

Etymology

Formed from 'moon' + 'lit' (past participle of 'light').
