unrealistically

1. ADV

/ ˌʌn.ri.əˈlɪs.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪəˈlɪs.tɪk.li / (BrE)
In a way that is not realistic; not based on what is actually possible or practical.

Examples

Her friends and professional associates would sympathize with her, not because she had lost a beloved husband, but because she had been married to a man who thought unrealistically.

Synset Examples

his expectations were unrealistically high

Related Terms

  • Antonym: realistically - in a realistic manner
