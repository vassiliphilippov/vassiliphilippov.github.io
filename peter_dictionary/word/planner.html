planner

1. NOUN

/ ˈplænər / (AmE), / ˈplænə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make plans for something, especially as a profession

Examples

For site planning work, it is best to have a qualified and experienced park planner to carry through the study.
Most park planners look to water frontage for basic park areas.
Lastly, governmental and private planners will at this stage begin to see large capital requirements looming ahead.
City planners do not always use this boundary as effectively as they might.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schemer - a person who forms secret or devious plans
  • Synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or coordinates activities or events
  • Antonym: improviser - a person who acts with little or no preparation

Etymology

From 'plan' + '-er', denoting a person who plans.
