tart

1. ADJ

/ tɑrt / (AmE), / tɑːt / (BrE)
Having a sharp or acidic taste; sour like a lemon.

Examples

The flavor is neither sweet, like a pear, nor tart like an orange; it is subtle and rather bland, nut like.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sour - having an acidic, sharp taste
  • Antonym: sweet - having a sugary taste, not sour or tart
