salivary

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæləˌvɛri / (AmE), / ˈsælɪvəri / (BrE)
relating to saliva or the glands that produce saliva

Examples

The pain became thus the symbol for food and elicited salivary secretion (conditioned reflex).

Synset Examples

salivary gland

Etymology

From Latin saliva (saliva) + -ary (adjective suffix).
