intensifying

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈtɛnsɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Becoming greater in strength or intensity; increasing in force or effect.

Examples

What hope lay in the nephews, she asked the intensifying light out there, with one married to a barren woman and the other divorced, having sired two girl children, with none to bear on the Packard name?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: strengthening - becoming stronger; increasing in strength
  • Antonym: diminishing - decreasing in intensity or amount

Etymology

From 'intensify' + '-ing'. 'Intensify' from Latin 'intensus' (stretched, strained) + '-fy' (make).
