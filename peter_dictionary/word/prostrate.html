prostrate

1. ADJ

/ ˈprɑːstreɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɒstreɪt / (BrE)
lying stretched out on the ground with the face downward; lying at full length along the ground.

Examples

The Champs Elysees is literally littered this summer with the prostrate bodies of France's beat-up beatnik jeunes filles.

Synset Examples

found himself lying flat on the floor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prone - lying flat, especially face downward
  • Antonym: supine - lying flat on one's back, face upward

Etymology

From Latin 'prostratus', past participle of 'prosternere' (to throw down).
