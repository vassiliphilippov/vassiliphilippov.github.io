deathlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛθˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɛθˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Resembling death in appearance, especially in being pale, still, or lacking animation.

Examples

It sometimes ended in death-like trances with many lying exhausted and panting on chair and floor.

Synset Examples

a deathly pallor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deathly - having qualities or appearance of death
  • Antonym: lifelike - having the appearance of being alive

Etymology

Formed from 'death' + 'like,' meaning resembling death.
