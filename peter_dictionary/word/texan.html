Texan

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛksən / (AmE), / ˈtɛksən / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of the U.S. state of Texas.

Examples

State representatives decided Thursday against taking a poll on what kind of taxes Texans would prefer to pay.
A Texan wrote to a male companion at home: "What has become of Halda and Laura?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Texas - a state in the southern United States

Etymology

From Texas (the U.S. state) + -an (a suffix forming adjectives and nouns).
