creche

1. NOUN

/ kreɪʃ / (AmE), / kreɪʃ / (BrE)
A hospital or institution where foundlings (infant children of unknown parents) are taken in and cared for, especially in historical contexts.

Examples

He was my gapt in the creche.

Etymology

From French 'crèche' meaning 'crib' or 'manger'; ultimately from Old French 'cresche', 'crèche'.
