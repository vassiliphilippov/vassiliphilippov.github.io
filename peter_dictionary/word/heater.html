heater

1. NOUN

/ ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
A device that produces heat, especially for warming the air in a room or heating water.

Examples

Even if the electric power fails after an attack, any time that the heater has been used will make the shelter that much more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: radiator - a device for heating a room, usually using hot water or steam
  • Synonym: furnace - an enclosed structure for heating, often a synonym for large heater
  • Antonym: air conditioner - a device that cools air, opposite function of a heater

Etymology

From 'heat' + '-er', meaning 'one that heats'.
