tenderly

1. ADV

/ ˈtɛndɚli / (AmE), / ˈtɛndəli / (BrE)
In a gentle, caring, or affectionate manner.

Examples

He was an expert florist, tenderly dextrous in the arrangement of bouquets and wreaths.

Synset Examples

tenderly she placed her arms round him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gently - in a gentle, kind, or soft manner
  • Antonym: harshly - in a rough, cruel, or severe manner

Etymology

From "tender" + "-ly". "Tender" from Old French *tendre*, from Latin *tener* (delicate, soft).
