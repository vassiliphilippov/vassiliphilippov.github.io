Related Terms

  • Antonym: old - Having lived or existed for many years; opposite of young
  • Synonym: youthful - Typical of or having the characteristics of youth
  • Degree Lower: infant - Very young child or baby
  • Degree Higher: adolescent - In the stage of development between child and adult (teenager)

Etymology

From Old English geong, related to German jung and Dutch jong.
