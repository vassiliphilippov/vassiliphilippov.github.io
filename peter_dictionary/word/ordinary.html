/ ˈɔrdəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪnəri / (BrE)

/ ˈɔːrdəˌneri / (AmE), / ˈɔːdnəri / (BrE)
Normal or usual; not exceptional in any way, especially in quality, ability, size, or degree.

Etymology
