2. ADV

/ ðɛn / (AmE), / ðɛn / (BrE)
After that; subsequently or soon afterward, often used to connect events in sequence.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: afterwards - at a later time; after an event

Etymology

From Old English 'ðænne', related to 'than', meaning 'at that time'.

Related Terms
