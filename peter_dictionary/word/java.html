java

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑ.və / (AmE), / ˈdʒɑː.və / (BrE)
(informal) A cup of coffee; the beverage made from roasted and ground coffee beans.

Examples

His long nose wiggled at the smells of frizzling bacon and heating java, but the fire was low, and he wanted to waste no time.

Synset Examples

he ordered a cup of coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coffee - the beverage made from roasted coffee beans

Etymology

From the Indonesian island of Java, a leading producer of coffee in the 19th century. The term came to refer to coffee generally.
