Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress
  • Synonym: problem - a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful and needing to be dealt with
  • Antonym: ease - absence of difficulty or effort
  • Synonym: challenge - a task or situation that tests someone's abilities

Etymology
