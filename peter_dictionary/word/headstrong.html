headstrong

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛdˌstrɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌstrɒŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) determined to do as they want, even when advised not to; stubborn and wilful.

Examples

I knew Moe, I knew he was headstrong.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stubborn - refusing to change one's mind or attitude; difficult to move or deal with
  • Synonym: obstinate - stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or chosen course of action
  • Antonym: compliant - willing to follow rules or instructions; opposite of headstrong

Etymology

Formed from 'head' + 'strong' (with the sense of strong-minded or obstinately wilful); used since late Middle English.
