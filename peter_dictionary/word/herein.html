herein

1. ADV

/ hɪrˈɪn / (AmE), / hɪəˈrɪn / (BrE)
in this document, text, or place

Examples

It is these other differences between North and South - other, that is, than those which concern discrimination or social welfare - which I chiefly discuss herein.

Synset Examples

I shall discuss the question herein

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereinafter - from this point in the document onward

Etymology

From Middle English 'herein', from 'here' + 'in'.
