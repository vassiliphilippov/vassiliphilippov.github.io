suited

/ ˈsuːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsuːtɪd / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsuited - not having the qualities necessary for a particular purpose

Etymology

From 'suit' (to be appropriate, from Old French suivre, to follow or pursue).
