saute

1. VERB

/ sɔːˈteɪ / (AmE), / sɔːˈteɪ / (BrE)
To fry food quickly in a small amount of hot fat over high heat.

Examples

While this is baking, saute mushrooms, fresh or canned, in butter.

Synset Examples

saute the onions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fry - to cook food in hot fat or oil

Etymology

From French 'sauté', past participle of 'sauter' (to jump), referring to the tossing motion of food in the pan.
