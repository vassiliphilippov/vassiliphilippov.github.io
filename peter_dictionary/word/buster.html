buster

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈbʌstə / (BrE)
An informal term of address for a man or boy, often used to express annoyance or assert authority.

Examples

I don't run the scams, Red, I just process the profits.

Synset Examples

Say, fellow, what are you doing?
Hey buster, what's up?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fellow - informal term for a man or boy
  • Synonym: buddy - informal friendly term of address for a man
  • Synonym: pal - informal friendly term of address for a man
