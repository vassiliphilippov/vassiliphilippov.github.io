birdhouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɝːdˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small structure built to provide shelter and nesting space for birds.

Examples

Most species seem to prefer a ready-made hollow such as a deserted mouse nest, a bird house, or the hole made by a woodpecker; some show a definite liking for making their nest in moss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest box - an artificial enclosure provided for animals to nest in
