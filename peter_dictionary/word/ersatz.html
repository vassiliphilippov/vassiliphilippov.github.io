ersatz

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛrˌsɑts, ɛrˈzɑts / (AmE), / ˈɛːzɑːts, ˈɜːzæts / (BrE)
(of a product or substitute) made or used as a replacement, typically of inferior quality to the original.

Examples

Does the writer know the difference between an "ersatz "ballad or tall tale and a true product of the folk?

Synset Examples

ersatz coffee
substitute coffee

Related Terms

  • Synonym: substitute - a thing or person acting or serving in place of another
  • Antonym: genuine - truly what something is said to be; authentic
  • Antonym: authentic - of undisputed origin; genuine

Etymology

From German 'Ersatz' (replacement, substitute).
