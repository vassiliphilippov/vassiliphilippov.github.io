severance

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛv.ɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˈsɛv.ər.əns / (BrE)
the act of ending a connection or relationship; the process of being separated or cut off.

Examples

Severance pay for 19 years.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: separation - the process of moving or being moved apart

Etymology

From Old French *sevrance* 'separation', from *sevrer* 'to sever', from Latin *separare* 'to separate'.
