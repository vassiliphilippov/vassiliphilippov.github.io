grandmother

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrænˌmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡrændˌmʌðə / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandma - informal term for grandmother
  • Degree Higher: grandparent - the parent of one’s father or mother
  • Antonym: grandfather - the father of one's parent

Etymology

From Middle English 'grandmoder', combining 'grand-' (meaning 'of a generation above') and 'mother'.
