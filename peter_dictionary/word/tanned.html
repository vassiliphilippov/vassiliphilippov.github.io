tanned

1. ADJ

/ tænd / (AmE), / tænd / (BrE)
(of skin) having a brownish color from exposure to the sun.

Examples

Her tanned foot, whose arch swept high and white, pointed artfully toward tapering toes - toes like fingers, whose tips glowed white.

Synset Examples

a young bronzed Apollo

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bronzed - (of skin) having a brown color from exposure to the sun
