sliding

1. ADJ

/ ˈslaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
moving smoothly and continuously along a surface; capable of or designed for movement in this way.

Examples

He reached out to pull the door shut and fasten it with a sliding bolt.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gliding - moving smoothly and continuously without interruption

Etymology

Present participle of the verb 'slide,' from Old English 'slīdan.'
