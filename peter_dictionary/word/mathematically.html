mathematically

1. ADV

/ ˌmæθəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmæθɪˈmatɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is based on mathematics.

Examples

These droplets appear to be ellipsoids, and it is mathematically convenient to assume that they are.
Mathematically, we shall not distinguish the experiment of three dice tossed once from that of one die tossed three times.

Synset Examples

mathematically impossible

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arithmetically - in a way relating to arithmetic

Etymology

From mathematical + -ly, where 'mathematical' is from Latin mathematicus, from Greek mathematikos.
