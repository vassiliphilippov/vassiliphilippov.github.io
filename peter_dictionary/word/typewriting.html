typewriting

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪpˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of writing with a typewriter

Examples

Perhaps a course in typewriting might be regarded as the exception which proves the rule.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: typing - the action of writing using a keyboard or typewriter

Etymology

From 'typewriter' + '-ing', denoting the action or process of using a typewriter.
