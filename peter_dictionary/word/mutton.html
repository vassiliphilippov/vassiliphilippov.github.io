mutton

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌtən / (AmE), / ˈmʌtn̩ / (BrE)
meat from a mature sheep, used as food

Examples

"Slice o' mutton, bhoy "? she had queried in her soft guttural.
"Slice o' mutton "?

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lamb - meat from a young sheep

Etymology

From Old French 'moton' (sheep), later meaning the flesh, from Vulgar Latin *multo.
