rudely

1. ADV

/ ˈrudli / (AmE), / ˈruːdli / (BrE)
In an impolite or disrespectful manner.

Examples

"I have told you before, and I tell you again ", Monsieur Favre said rudely.

Synset Examples

he treated her impolitely

Related Terms

  • Antonym: politely - in a polite or courteous manner
  • Synonym: impolitely - in an impolite manner

Etymology

From 'rude' + '-ly'; 'rude' from Latin 'rudis' (rough, unrefined).
