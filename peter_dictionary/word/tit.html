tit

1. NOUN

/ tɪt / (AmE), / tɪt / (BrE)
a woman's breast; one of the two soft, fleshy, milk-secreting organs on a woman's chest (informal and often considered vulgar)

Examples

A chicken with six tits and a tail lak a corkscrew.
It is sowbelly with tits on.
He puts his hand in your shirt and squeezes your tit till it's purple.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breast - either of the two soft, protruding organs on the chest of a woman
  • Synonym: teat - the nipple of a mammary gland, especially of non-human mammals

Etymology

Probably of Germanic origin; compare Old Norse titr and Middle Low German titte (teat, nipple).
