savvy

1. VERB

/ ˈsævi / (AmE), / ˈsavi / (BrE)
(informal) To understand or grasp the meaning of something.

Examples

He stopped, embarrassed, and Morgan said, "I understand that, but I do n't savvy why you 'd go off and leave your jobs in the first place ".

Synset Examples

Do you comprehend the meaning of this letter?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: understand - to know or grasp the meaning of something
  • Synonym: comprehend - to grasp mentally; to understand

Etymology

Probably from West Indies English, based on Spanish 'sabe' (he/she knows), from Latin 'sapere' (to be wise).
