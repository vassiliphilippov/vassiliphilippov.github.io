lifeless

1. ADJ

/ ˈlaɪfləs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfləs / (BrE)
Deprived of life; dead or no longer living.

Examples

Several slugs fired into the bed jerked aside the blanket to reveal an apparently lifeless hand.

Synset Examples

a lifeless body

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dead - no longer alive
  • Antonym: alive - living; not dead

Etymology

Formed from 'life' + '-less' (meaning 'without life').
