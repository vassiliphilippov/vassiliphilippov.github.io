writhing

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪðɪŋ / (BrE)
moving in a twisting or contorted fashion, like a snake or worm

Examples

And the valley stretched endlessly out ahead, scorched and baked and writhing in its heat, until it vanished into the throbbing wall of fiery orange brown haze.

Synset Examples

wiggly worms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: twisting - turning or bending in a spiral or contorted manner
  • Synonym: wiggling - moving with small rapid movements from side to side or up and down

Etymology

Present participle of 'writhe', from Old English 'wrīthan' (to twist, bend, wind).
