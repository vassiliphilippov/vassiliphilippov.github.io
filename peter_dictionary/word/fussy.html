fussy

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌsi / (AmE), / ˈfʌsi / (BrE)
Easily annoyed or irritable; quick to become upset or difficult to please.

Examples

Under normal circumstances Casey was a little fussy when people told him what to do with pictures he had taken.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or made angry
  • Antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or irritation
