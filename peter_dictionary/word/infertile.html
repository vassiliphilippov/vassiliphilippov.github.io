infertile

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈfɜrtəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːtaɪl / (BrE)
Unable to produce offspring or seeds; incapable of reproduction.

Examples

The Roman Catholic Church sanctions only abstention or the rhythm method, also known as the use of the infertile or safe period.

Synset Examples

an infertile couple

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fertile - able to produce offspring; capable of reproduction

Etymology

From Latin 'infertilis', from 'in-' (not) + 'fertilis' (fertile).
