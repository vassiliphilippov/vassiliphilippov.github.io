kneecap

1. NOUN

/ ˈniːˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈniːˌkæp / (BrE)
the round, flat bone at the front of the knee joint; also called the patella.

Examples

He put his chin on his kneecap, stretching his neck like that of a turkey on a chopping block, and stared wordlessly at his sergeant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: patella - the medical term for the kneecap bone
