/ dɪˈlɪvɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvə / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surrender - to give up or hand over (a person, right, or possession) to another
  • Synonym: hand over - to give or transfer (someone or something) to another's possession
  • Antonym: retain - to keep possession of, not to surrender or hand over

Etymology

From Latin 'deliberare' via Old French 'delivrer', meaning 'to set free, hand over'.
