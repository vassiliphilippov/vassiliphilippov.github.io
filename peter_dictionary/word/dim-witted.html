dim-witted

1. ADJ

/ dɪm ˈwɪtɪd / (AmE), / dɪm ˈwɪtɪd / (BrE)
slow to understand or lacking intelligence; not mentally quick or clever.

Examples

And dumb-witted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dull - lacking intelligence or mental sharpness
  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
  • Antonym: bright - intelligent and quick to understand

Etymology

Formed from "dim" (not bright) + "witted" (having wit or intelligence).
