deliberation

1. NOUN

/ dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
careful consideration or discussion of all sides of a question before making a decision, especially in a formal or official setting

Examples

The Interstate Commerce Commission will commence its deliberations on the proposed C. + O. - B. + O. merger on June 18.

Synset Examples

the deliberations of the jury

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discussion - the act of talking about something to reach a decision or exchange ideas

Etymology

From Latin deliberatio (careful consideration), from deliberare (to weigh or consider well).
