scrambled

1. ADJ

/ ˈskræmbəld / (AmE), / ˈskræmbəld / (BrE)
Thrown together in a disorderly or confused manner; jumbled.

Examples

It got the kind of scrambled, coarsened performance that can happen to best of orchestras when the man with the baton lacks technique and style.

Synset Examples

a scrambled plan of action

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jumbled - mixed up in a confused or untidy way
  • Antonym: ordered - arranged or organized methodically
