no-nonsense

1. ADJ

/ ˌnoʊˈnɑːnsəns / (AmE), / ˌnəʊˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
practical and straightforward; not tolerating silliness, distractions, or unnecessary details

Examples

In the stiff neutral lines of the telephone poles he saw the no-nonsense pen strokes of Aunt Jessica's letter.

Synset Examples

the no-nonsense tones of a stern parent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pragmatic - dealing with things sensibly and realistically; practical
  • Synonym: businesslike - efficient and practical in approach
  • Antonym: fussy - paying excessive attention to detail or trivial aspects
