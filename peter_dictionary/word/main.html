main

1. ADJ

/ meɪn / (AmE), / meɪn / (BrE)
most important; chief or principal in rank, importance, or value.

Examples

The half-intensity diameter of the antenna beam was about 9', and the angle subtended by the moon included the entire main beam and part of the first side lobes.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: secondary - less important; not primary
  • Synonym: chief - most important; main
  • Synonym: principal - most important; leading

Etymology

From Old English mægen (power, strength), later influenced by the sense of 'chief' or 'principal'.
