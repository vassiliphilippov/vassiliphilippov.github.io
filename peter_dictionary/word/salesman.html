salesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈseɪlzmən / (AmE), / ˈseɪlzmən / (BrE)
A man whose job is to sell goods or services to customers.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: salesperson - person who sells goods or services (gender-neutral)
  • Antonym: saleswoman - woman whose job is to sell goods or services

Etymology

Formed from 'sale' + 'man', following the common English pattern for occupational titles.
