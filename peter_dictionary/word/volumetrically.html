volumetrically

1. ADV

/ ˌvoʊ.ljəˈmɛ.trɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌvɒl.jʊˈmɛ.trɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the measurement or consideration of volume.

Examples

The serum was measured volumetrically and subsequently dialyzed in the cold for at least 24 hr against three to four changes, approximately 750 ml each, of "starting buffer ".

Synset Examples

analyzed volumetrically

Related Terms

  • Antonym: gravimetrically - in a manner relating to the measurement of weight

Etymology

From 'volumetric' + '-ally', with 'volumetric' from 'volume' originating from Latin 'volumen' (a roll, scroll).
