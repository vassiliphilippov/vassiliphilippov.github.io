two-way

1. ADJ

/ ˈtuˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtuː.weɪ / (BrE)
Involving or supported by both sides; agreed to by or benefiting two parties.

Examples

We must ask ourselves which of the two alternatives will help the commuter - the two-way B. + O. - C. + O. merger, or the three way New York Central B. + O. - C. + O. merger.

Synset Examples

a two-way treaty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bipartisan - involving the agreement or cooperation of two political parties
