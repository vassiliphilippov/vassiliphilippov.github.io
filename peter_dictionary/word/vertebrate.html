vertebrate

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɜrtɪˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪbrət / (BrE)
an animal having a backbone or spinal column, including mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and fishes

Examples

Etymology
