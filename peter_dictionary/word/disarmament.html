disarmament

1. NOUN

/ dɪsˈɑːrməmənt / (AmE), / dɪsˈɑːməmənt / (BrE)
The process of reducing or eliminating military weapons or armed forces.

Examples

He's telling them now to write letters to their Congressmen opposing the disarmament of Germany'.

Synset Examples

the disarmament of the aggressor nations must be complete

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rearmament - the process of arming again or building up military weapons

Etymology

From 'disarm' + '-ment', modeled after French désarmement.
