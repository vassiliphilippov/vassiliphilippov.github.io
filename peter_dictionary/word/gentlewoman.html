gentlewoman

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɛntəlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntəlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman of high social status, refinement, or good manners; a lady.

Examples

GEARY: Thank you very much, ladies and gentlemen.

Synset Examples

a chauffeur opened the door of the limousine for the grand lady

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lady - a woman of social standing or refinement

Etymology

Formed by analogy to gentleman; from 'gentle' (noble) + 'woman'.
