assigned

1. ADJ

/ əˈsaɪnd / (AmE), / əˈsaɪnd / (BrE)
Appointed or designated to a particular post, task, or duty.

Examples

Early in November the clouds lifted enough to carry out the assigned missions.

Synset Examples

assigned personnel
assigned duties

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unassigned - not appointed to a post or duty

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'assign', from Old French 'assigner', from Latin 'assignare' (to mark out, allocate).
