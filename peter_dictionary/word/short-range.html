short-range

1. ADJ

/ ʃɔrtˈreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ʃɔːtˈreɪndʒ / (BrE)
concerned with or involving a short period in the near future; not long-term

Examples

Corporations react violently to short-range stimuli, e. g., quarterly and annual dividend reports.

Synset Examples

a short-range policy

Related Terms

  • Antonym: long-range - concerned with a long period in the future
