expressive

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈsprɛsɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsɪv / (BrE)
effectively conveying thought or feeling; showing emotion or meaning clearly in appearance or manner

Examples

These words are more expressive than labels like "A "and "not - A ".
Some of their figures of speech were colorful and expressive.
Some of the letters were pungently expressive.

Synset Examples

a very expressive face

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inexpressive - not conveying feeling or meaning
  • Synonym: emotive - arousing or able to arouse intense feeling

Etymology

From French 'expressif', from Latin 'expressus' (clearly presented, distinct).
