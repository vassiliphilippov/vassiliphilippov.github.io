riding

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of riding on horseback while controlling the horse's movements.

Examples

That was the first and last time I gave him a riding lesson.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equestrianism - the sport or skill of horse riding
  • Antonym: driving - the sport or activity of controlling a horse-drawn vehicle

Etymology

From the verb 'ride' + -ing (gerund/noun suffix).
