gratuitously

1. ADV

/ ɡrəˈtuːɪtəsli / (AmE), / ɡrəˈtjuːɪtəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is uncalled for, lacking good reason, or unnecessary.

Examples

The presumption in the literature would appear to be that the basic wage rate would be unchanged in this case, on the grounds that it is "clearly "not in the interest of the industry to raise wages gratuitously.

Synset Examples

he insulted us gratuitously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: needlessly - without necessity; unnecessarily

Etymology

From Latin 'gratuitus' (free, spontaneous) + English adverbial suffix '-ly'.
