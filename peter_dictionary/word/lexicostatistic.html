lexicostatistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌlɛksɪkoʊstəˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ˌlɛksɪkəʊstəˈstætɪk / (BrE)
relating to statistical methods used to study the relationships between languages, especially through comparing vocabularies

Examples

It seems time to consider a revision of operational procedures for lexicostatistic studies on a more humble, solid, and limited basis.

Synset Examples

lexicostatistic techniques

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lexicostatistics - the application of statistical techniques to the study of lexical similarities between languages

Etymology

From 'lexico-' (relating to words or vocabulary) and 'statistic'.
