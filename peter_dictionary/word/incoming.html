incoming

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪnˌkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
arriving at or coming to a place, position, or office; newly arriving.

Examples

In spite of the dreams of the host for oneness in the group, the * * f incoming messages for each guest overload his receiving system beyond comprehension if N exceeds about six.

Related Terms

Etymology

Formed from 'in-' (into) and 'coming' (present participle of 'come').
