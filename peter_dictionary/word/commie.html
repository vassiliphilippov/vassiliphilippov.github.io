commie

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈkɒmi / (BrE)
an informal term for a communist, especially used disparagingly

Examples

Mays' day came a day earlier for Willie than for the kids and Commies this year.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communist - a person who supports or believes in the principles of communism

Etymology

Shortened and altered form of 'communist'.
