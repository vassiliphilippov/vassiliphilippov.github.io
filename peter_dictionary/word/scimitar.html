scimitar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪmɪtɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɪmɪtə / (BrE)
A sword with a curved blade, sharp on the outer edge, traditionally originating from the Middle East or South Asia.

Examples

"I am tired of sultans and scimitars ".
He at once cancels the celebrations and, buckling on his scimitar, stumbles blindly from the house, where he is hit and killed by a passing oxcart.

Etymology

From Italian scimitarra or French cimeterre, of unknown ultimate origin.
