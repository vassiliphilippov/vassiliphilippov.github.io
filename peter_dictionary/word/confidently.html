confidently

1. ADV

/ ˈkɑːn.fə.dənt.li / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fɪ.dənt.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows confidence; in a confident manner.

Examples

In their vases were embodied the basic aesthetic and logical characteristics of Greek civilization, at first hesitantly in Protogeometric work, and then more confidently in the initial stages of the Geometric style.

Synset Examples

we have to do what is right confidently

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hesitantly - in a hesitant or unsure manner

Etymology

From 'confident' + '-ly', patterned after the Latin suffix '-us'.
