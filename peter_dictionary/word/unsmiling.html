unsmiling

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
Not smiling; having a serious or solemn expression.

Examples

Scotty did not mind the doctor's unsmiling teasing as he used to.
When Joe identified himself, he nodded, unsmiling, and ushered him into a sedate living room.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smiling - having or showing a smile

Etymology

Formed by combining the prefix 'un-' (not) with 'smiling', the present participle of 'smile'.
