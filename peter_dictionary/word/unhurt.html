unhurt

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈhɜrt / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɜːt / (BrE)
Not injured; not harmed or damaged, especially after an accident or attack.

Examples

A second bullet ricocheted off Max's belt buckle, leaving him unhurt but in some distress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unharmed - not hurt or damaged
  • Antonym: hurt - injured or wounded

Etymology

From prefix un- (not) + hurt (injured).
