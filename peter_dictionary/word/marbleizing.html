marbleizing

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɑrbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːbəˌlaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
The technique or effect of producing a texture, appearance, or finish similar to that of marble.

Examples

In certain other pictures, however, Braque began to paint areas in exact simulation of wood graining or marbleizing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marbling - imitation of marble texture or pattern

Etymology

From 'marble' + '-ize' + '-ing', meaning to make or become like marble.
