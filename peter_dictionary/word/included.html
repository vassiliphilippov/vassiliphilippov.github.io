included

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkluːdɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːdɪd / (BrE)
Enclosed or provided in the same envelope, package, or correspondence.

Examples

In response to your repeated enquiries, the State has allocated the enclosed funds for your library project."

Synset Examples

the included check

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enclosed - shut in or provided in the same envelope or package

Etymology

Past participle of 'include', from Latin includere (to shut in, enclose).
