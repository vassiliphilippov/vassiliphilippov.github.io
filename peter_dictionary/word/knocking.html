knocking

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
The sound made by striking a surface, especially a door, or the recurring noise produced by a mechanical fault in an engine or bearing.

Examples

A knocking at Alex's door roused him at six o'clock the following morning.
[KNOCKING ON DOOR]

Synset Examples

the knocking grew louder

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapping - the act or sound of knocking sharply or rapidly
  • Synonym: tapping - the sound produced by a light, quick blow, often on a door or surface

Etymology

From the verb 'knock', Middle English 'knoken', of imitative origin.
