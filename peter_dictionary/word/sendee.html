sendee

1. NOUN

/ sɛnˈdi / (AmE), / sɛnˈdi / (BrE)
the person who is intended to receive a message, letter, or communication

Examples

Vito, leave this to us.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recipient - person who receives something
  • Antonym: sender - person who sends something

Etymology

Formed by analogy with 'addressee', from 'send' + '-ee' (person who is the object of an action).
