sultan

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌl.tən / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.tən / (BrE)
A Muslim sovereign, especially the ruler of a former Ottoman Empire or other Islamic country.

Examples

"I am tired of sultans and scimitars ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: king - male sovereign ruler of a nation or territory
  • Synonym: emperor - sovereign ruler of an empire
  • Synonym: caliph - Islamic religious and political leader

Etymology

From Arabic 'sulṭān' (authority, power).
