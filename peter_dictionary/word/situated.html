situated

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪtʃuˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪtjueɪtɪd / (BrE)
in a particular place or position

Examples

Distally the bronchus is situated between a pulmonary artery on one side and a pulmonary vein on the other, as in type 1, (fig. 24).
At the inn, which was situated close to a broad weir, Byron was greeted by the landlord with obsequious deference and addressed as "milord ".

Synset Examples

valuable centrally located urban land
strategically placed artillery
a house set on a hilltop
nicely situated on a quiet riverbank

Related Terms

  • Synonym: located - being in a particular place or position
  • Synonym: placed - put or set in a particular position

Etymology

From Latin situs (site, place), via situate + -ed.
