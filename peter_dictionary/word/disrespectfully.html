disrespectfully

1. ADV

/ ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfəli / (AmE), / ˌdɪsrɪˈspɛktfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of respect; without respect.

Examples

What have I ever done to make you treat me so disrespectfully?

Synset Examples

he treats his parents rather disrespectfully

Related Terms

  • Antonym: respectfully - in a manner that shows respect

Etymology

From 'disrespectful' + '-ly'.
