coordinator

1. NOUN

/ koʊˈɔrdəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to organize people and activities to ensure they work well together and efficiently towards a common goal

Examples

When the gubernatorial campaign starts, Caldwell is expected to become a campaign coordinator for Byrd.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: organizer - a person who arranges and manages activities or events

Etymology

From Latin 'coordinare' (to arrange), via 'coordinate' + '-or'.
