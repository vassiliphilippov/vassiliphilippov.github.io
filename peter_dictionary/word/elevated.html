elevated

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛləˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
Situated or placed higher than the surrounding area or level; raised above the ground.

Examples

A 6 - year study of the transport properties of air at elevated temperatures has been completed.

Synset Examples

an elevated platform

Related Terms

  • Synonym: raised - lifted or moved to a higher position
  • Antonym: lowered - moved to a lower position or level

Etymology

From Latin elevatus, past participle of elevare, meaning ‘to raise’ or ‘lift up.’
