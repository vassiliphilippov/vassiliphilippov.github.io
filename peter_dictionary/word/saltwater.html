saltwater

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɔltˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
Water that contains salt, especially sea water.

Examples

There was the smell of the coast, like a primeval memory, composed of equal parts salt water, clams, seaweed and northern air.

Synset Examples

the water in the ocean is all saltwater

Related Terms

  • Antonym: freshwater - water that is not salty, especially as found in lakes and rivers
