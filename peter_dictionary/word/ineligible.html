ineligible

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈnɛlədʒəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛlɪdʒəbl / (BrE)
not having the right or qualifications to do or receive something; not eligible.

Examples

But he was scholastically ineligible in 1959 and merely present last season.

Synset Examples

ineligible to vote
ineligible for retirement benefits

Related Terms

  • Antonym: eligible - having the right or qualifications to do or receive something

Etymology

From Latin in- (not) + eligibilis (that may be chosen).
