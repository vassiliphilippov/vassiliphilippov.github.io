ornately

1. ADV

/ ɔrˈneɪtli / (AmE), / ɔːˈneɪtli / (BrE)
In a highly decorated and elaborate manner.

Examples

In the room next to theirs was a huge cradle, of mahogany, ornately carved and decorated with gold leaf.

Synset Examples

the cradle was ornately carved

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned manner

Etymology

From 'ornate' + '-ly', from Latin 'ornatus' (adorned).
