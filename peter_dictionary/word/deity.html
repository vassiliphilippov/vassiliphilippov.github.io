deity

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːət̬i, ˈdeɪəti / (AmE), / ˈdiːɪti, ˈdeɪɪti / (BrE)
a god or goddess; a supernatural being considered divine and worshipped by people

Examples

The three Indian brass deities, though - Ganessa, Siva, and Krishna - are an altogether different cup of tea.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: god - a male deity worshipped as divine
  • Synonym: goddess - a female deity worshipped as divine

Etymology

From Latin 'deus' (god) via Old French 'deité'.
