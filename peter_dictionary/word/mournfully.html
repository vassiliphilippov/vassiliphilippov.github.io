mournfully

1. ADV

/ ˈmɔrn.fəli / (AmE), / ˈmɔːn.fəli / (BrE)
In a way that expresses sorrow, grief, or sadness.

Examples

Lips pursed mournfully, he stared down at its crazily sagging left side.

Synset Examples

the young man stared into his glass mournfully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sadly - in a sad manner
