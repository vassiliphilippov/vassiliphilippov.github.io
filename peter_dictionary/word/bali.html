Bali

1. PROPN

/ ˈbɑːli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːli / (BrE)
an island in Indonesia, east of Java, known for its volcanic scenery, traditional dances, rituals, and handicrafts

Examples

But it is the wooden sculpture from Bali, the one representing two men with their heads bent backward and their bodies interlaced by a fish, that I particularly call to your attention.

Etymology

From Indonesian 'Bali', the native name of the island.
