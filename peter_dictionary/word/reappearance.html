reappearance

1. NOUN

/ ˌriːəˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
The act or event of appearing again after having been gone or absent.

Examples

She kissed him passionately upon his reappearance.

Synset Examples

the reappearance of Halley's comet

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: appearance - the act of coming into view or becoming visible
  • Antonym: disappearance - the act of ceasing to be visible or present

Etymology

From re- (again) + appearance (from Latin 'apparere', to appear).
