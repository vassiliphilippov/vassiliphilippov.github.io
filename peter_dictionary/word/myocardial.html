myocardial

1. ADJ

/ ˌmaɪoʊˈkɑrdiəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪəˈkɑːdɪəl / (BrE)
relating to the myocardium (the muscular tissue of the heart)

Examples

In addition, other sections contained focal areas of recent myocardial necrosis that were infiltrated with neutrophils.
Many of the myocardial fibers were hypertrophied and had large, irregular, basophilic nuclei.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cardiac - relating to the heart

Etymology

From Greek myo- (muscle) + kardia (heart) + -al (relating to).
