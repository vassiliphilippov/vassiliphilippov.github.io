jabbing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒæbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒabɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, quick movement with a finger, hand, or object, resembling a poke or thrust

Examples

She lay under the covers making jabbing motions with her forefinger telling me where to look for the coffeepot.

Synset Examples

he warned me with a jab with his finger
he made a thrusting motion with his fist

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poke - a quick thrust with a finger or object
  • Synonym: thrust - a sudden strong push or drive

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'jab' (late 19th century), origin uncertain, possibly imitative.
