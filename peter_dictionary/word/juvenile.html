juvenile

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒuːvəˌnaɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːvənaɪl / (BrE)
Relating to or suitable for young people or children.

Examples

"Where the Boys Are "also has a juvenile bounce that makes for a refreshing venture in comedy.

Synset Examples

juvenile diabetes
juvenile fashions

Related Terms

  • Antonym: adult - relating to fully grown people; mature

Etymology

From Latin juvenilis (youthful), from juvenis (young).
