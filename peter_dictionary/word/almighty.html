almighty

1. ADJ

/ ɑlˈmaɪti / (AmE), / ɔːlˈmaɪti / (BrE)
(of a being, especially God) having unlimited power; all-powerful.

Examples

Would you be willing to swear before a judge and jury, having placed your hand on the Good Book and taken an oath before Almighty God himself?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: omnipotent - having unlimited power; able to do anything

Etymology

Old English 'ælmīhtig', from 'eall' (all) + 'mihtig' (mighty).
