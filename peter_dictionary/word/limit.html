limit

1. VERB

/ ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
to restrict the size, amount, or range of something; to keep within a defined boundary

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restrict - to put a limit on; keep within bounds
  • Synonym: confine - to keep within limits; not allow to go beyond a certain boundary
  • Antonym: allow - to permit; to let something happen (opposite)

Etymology

From Latin *limitare* (to bound), from *limes* (boundary, limit).
