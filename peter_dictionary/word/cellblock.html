cellblock

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛlˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈsɛlˌblɒk / (BrE)
A section of a prison consisting of a number of prison cells grouped together.

Examples

I want every man on this cell block questioned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prison - a building where people are kept as punishment for a crime

Etymology

From 'cell' (prison room) + 'block' (group of rooms or section of a building).
