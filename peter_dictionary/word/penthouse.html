penthouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛnthaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpɛnthaʊs / (BrE)
an apartment or suite located on the top floor or floors of a building, typically luxurious and offering fine views

Examples

The party at Floyd's penthouse gave the "chorines "a chance for a nostalgic frolic through all those hackneyed routines which have become a classic choreographic statement of the era's nonsense.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apartment - a set of rooms used as a dwelling, usually in a building containing several such sets

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman *pentiz* (a small structure attached to a building), influenced by the word 'house.'
