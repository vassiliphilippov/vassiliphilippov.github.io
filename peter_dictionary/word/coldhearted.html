coldhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˈkoʊldˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊldˌhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Lacking sympathy, compassion, or warmth of feeling; unfeeling or indifferent to others' suffering.

Examples

You lousy cold-hearted bastard!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: heartless - lacking compassion or pity
  • Antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others

Etymology

Formed from 'cold' + 'hearted', meaning 'having a cold heart'.
