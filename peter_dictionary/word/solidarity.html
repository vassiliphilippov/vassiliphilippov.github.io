solidarity

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɑːlɪˈdɛrəti / (AmE), / ˌsɒlɪˈdarɪti / (BrE)
unity or agreement of feeling or action among individuals with a common interest; mutual support within a group

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unity - the state of being united or joined as a whole
  • Antonym: division - the action or state of being separated or divided

Etymology

From French solidarité, from solidaire (interdependent), from Latin solidum (whole sum).
