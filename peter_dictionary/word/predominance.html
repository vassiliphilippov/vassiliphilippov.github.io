predominance

1. NOUN

/ prɪˈdɑmənəns / (AmE), / prɪˈdɒmɪnəns / (BrE)
The state or condition of being greater in number, influence, power, or importance than others.

Examples

The Istiqlal was still firmly united in 1957, but the P.D.I. (Parti Democratique de l'Independance), the most important minor party at the time, objected to the Istiqlal's predominance in the civil service and influence in Radio Maroc.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dominance - the state of having power and influence over others
  • Antonym: subordination - the state of being lower in rank or position

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *praedominantia*, from Latin *prae-* (before) + *dominari* (to rule).
