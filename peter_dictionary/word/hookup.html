hookup

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʊkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈhʊkʌp / (BrE)
A device or system that provides a connection to a power source or utility service, such as electricity, water, or gas.

Examples

Without monitoring, a military hookup becomes a noisy party.

Synset Examples

some campsites have electrical hookups for trailers

Etymology

From the phrasal verb 'hook up,' meaning to attach or connect (late 19th century).
