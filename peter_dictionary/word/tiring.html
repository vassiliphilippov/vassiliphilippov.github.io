tiring

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪɹɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəɹɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing one to feel tired; making you lose energy or strength.

Examples

He did not resent their supervision or Virginia's sometimes tiring sympathy.

Synset Examples

an exhausting march
the visit was especially wearing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exhausting - making you extremely tired
  • Antonym: refreshing - making you feel less tired or giving new energy

Etymology

Present participle of 'tire', from Old French *tirer* ('to pull, draw'), later influenced by the sense of becoming weary.
