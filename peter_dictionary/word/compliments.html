compliments

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːmpləmənts / (AmE), / ˈkɒmplɪmənts / (BrE)
(usually plural) Polite expressions of praise, good wishes, or respect offered to someone.

Examples

My compliments.

Synset Examples

give him my kind regards
my best wishes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regards - polite expressions of greeting or goodwill
  • Synonym: congratulations - expressions of joy or praise for someone's achievement

Etymology

From Latin 'complere' (to fill up, complete), via French 'compliment'.
