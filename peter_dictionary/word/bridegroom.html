bridegroom

1. NOUN

/ ˈbraɪdˌɡrum / (AmE), / ˈbraɪdˌɡruːm / (BrE)
a man who has recently married, or is about to be married

Examples

And this handsome booby, staring and sweating, was he her bridegroom?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bride - woman who is being or has just been married

Etymology

Old English *brȳdguma* (‘bride’ + ‘man’), later influenced by folk etymology to match ‘bride’ and ‘groom’.
