adventurer

1. NOUN

/ ədˈvɛn.tʃɚ.ɚ / (AmE), / ədˈvɛn.tʃər.ə / (BrE)
a person who seeks and enjoys exciting, often risky activities and new experiences

Examples

Sam Spade joins forces with a band of adventurers in search of a priceless jeweled statue of a falcon; but when the bird is found at last, it turns out to be a fake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: explorer - a person who investigates unknown regions
  • Synonym: daredevil - a person who takes reckless risks
  • Synonym: risk-taker - a person who is willing to take risks

Etymology

From French 'aventureur', from 'aventure' (adventure).
