resiny

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛzɪni / (AmE), / ˈrɛzɪni / (BrE)
having the characteristics or qualities of resin, such as being sticky, fragrant, or semi-transparent; resembling or containing resin, pitch, or tar

Examples

When McFeeley was halfway to the door, the proprietor emerged - a mountainous, dark man, his head thick with resiny black hair, his eyes like two of the black olives he imported in boatloads.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: resinous - having the properties of or containing resin
  • Synonym: pitchy - resembling or containing pitch or tar

Etymology

From 'resin' + '-y', denoting 'having the qualities of'.
