scalloped

1. ADJ

/ ˈskæləpt / (AmE), / ˈskæl.əpt / (BrE)
Having an edge or border with a series of rounded projections or indentations, resembling the edge of a scallop shell.

Examples

Flats have a scalloped throat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crenate - having a scalloped or notched edge, especially in botany

Etymology

From 'scallop', referring to the shellfish with a characteristically wavy shell edge.
