lummox

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʌməks / (AmE), / ˈlʌməks / (BrE)
An awkward or clumsy person, often regarded as stupid or inept.

Examples

Peter, you lummox, you 've forgot to order the musicians ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oaf - a clumsy or unintelligent person
  • Synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive man or boy

Etymology

Origin uncertain; probably a dialect or slang formation in English in the late 19th century.
