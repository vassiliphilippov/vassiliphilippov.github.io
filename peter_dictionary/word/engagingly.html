engagingly

1. ADV

/ ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that attracts and holds attention or interest; charmingly or appealingly.

Examples

Petite, lovely Yvette Chadroe plays the nymphomaniac engagingly.

Synset Examples

she played the role engagingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: charmingly - in a charming manner
  • Synonym: winsomely - in a winsome or engaging manner

Etymology

From 'engaging' + '-ly'. 'Engaging' from engage plus -ing, originally from Old French engager.
