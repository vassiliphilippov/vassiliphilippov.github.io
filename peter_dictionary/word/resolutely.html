resolutely

1. ADV

/ ˈrɛzəˌlutli / (AmE), / ˈrɛzəluːtli / (BrE)
In a determined and unwavering manner; with firm purpose.

Examples

Then she went back to the wicker chair and resolutely adjusted her eyes to the glare on the water.

Synset Examples

she resolutely refused to look at him or speak to him
he entered the building resolutely

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determinedly - in a purposeful and persistent manner

Etymology

From the adjective 'resolute', from Latin 'resolutus' (loosened, resolved).
