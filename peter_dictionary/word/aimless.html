aimless

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈeɪmləs / (BrE)
without purpose or direction; not having a particular aim or goal

Examples

The men were at first puzzled, then angered by the aimless tacking.
And so he walked, aimless again.
The aimless milling about of what had been a well trained, well organized crew struck Alexander with horror.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: purposeful - having a definite purpose or intention

Etymology

From 'aim' + '-less', meaning 'without aim'.
