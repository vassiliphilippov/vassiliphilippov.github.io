unashamedly

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnəˈʃeɪmɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈʃeɪmɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no feeling of shame or embarrassment.

Examples

Then, getting back into bed, he thought unashamedly of Mrs. Flannagan, planning where they would next meet and what they would do.

Synset Examples

he unashamedly abandoned the project when he realized he would not gain from it

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shamelessly - in a shameless manner; without shame or embarrassment

Etymology

From 'unashamed' + '-ly'. 'Unashamed' from 'un-' (not) + 'ashamed'.
