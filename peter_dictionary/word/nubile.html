nubile

1. ADJ

/ ˈnuː.baɪl / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.baɪl / (BrE)
(of a young woman) sexually mature and suitable or eligible for marriage, often used to emphasize physical attractiveness.

Examples

There are hordes of nubile young women there who, prodded by their impatient mothers, are determined to marry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marriageable - suitable or old enough to be married

Etymology

From Latin nubilis (marriageable, suitable for marriage), from nubere (to marry).
