self-reliant

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛlf rɪˈlaɪənt / (AmE), / ˌself rɪˈlaɪənt / (BrE)
Able to depend on one's own abilities, judgment, or resources rather than those of others; independent.

Examples

For this reason, he appears as an independent and self-reliant figure, whose rugged individualism need not be pressed into the mold of a 9 to 5 routine.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: independent - not subject to the authority of others; self-governing or self-sufficient

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'reliant', from rely (from Old French 'relier' 'to bind together').
