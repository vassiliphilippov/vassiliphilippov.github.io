Guatemalan

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡwɑtəˈmælən / (AmE), / ˌɡwætəˈmælən / (BrE)
relating to Guatemala or its people

Examples

We served our national interests, and by so doing we saved the Guatemalan people the ultimate in human misery.

Synset Examples

Guatemalan coffee

Etymology

From Guatemala + -an, the suffix indicating origin or relation.
