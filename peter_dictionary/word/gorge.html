gorge

1. VERB

/ ɡɔrdʒ / (AmE), / ɡɔːdʒ / (BrE)
To eat a large amount of food greedily; to overeat or stuff oneself.

Examples

Now, you no doubt regard the preceding as pap; you 're tooling around full of gage in your hot rods, gorging yourselves on pizza and playing pinball in the taverns and generally behaving like Ubermenschen.

Synset Examples

She stuffed herself at the dinner
The kids binged on ice cream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stuff oneself - to eat until very full, often uncomfortably so
  • Synonym: binge - to eat or drink excessively in a short period
  • Antonym: nibble - to eat in small bites, especially slowly or delicately

Etymology

From Old French *gorger* (to gorge, to fill the throat), from *gorge* (throat).
