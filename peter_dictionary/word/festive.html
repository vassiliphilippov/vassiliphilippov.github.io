festive

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɛstɪv / (AmE), / ˈfɛstɪv / (BrE)
cheerful and lively; suitable for or characteristic of a celebration or festival

Examples

Unfortunately, Brooks' teammates were not in such festive mood as the Orioles expired before the seven hit pitching of three Kansas City rookie hurlers.
All about me there was a hectic interplay of meetings taking place, like abrupt, jerky scenes in old silent movies, joyous greetings and beginnings, huggings and kissings, enthusiastic forays into the festive night.

Synset Examples

a festive (or festal) occasion
gay and exciting night life
a merry evening

Related Terms

  • Synonym: merry - cheerful and lively
  • Synonym: joyful - feeling, expressing, or causing great pleasure and happiness
  • Antonym: depressing - making you feel sad and without enthusiasm

Etymology

From Latin festivus (of a feast or festival), from festum (feast).
