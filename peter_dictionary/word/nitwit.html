nitwit

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪtˌwɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɪtˌwɪt / (BrE)
A person who is stupid or incompetent; a fool.

Examples

Don't you listen to these nitwits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dimwit - a stupid or silly person
  • Synonym: halfwit - a foolish or unintelligent person
  • Antonym: genius - a person with extraordinary intellectual ability

Etymology

First recorded in early 20th century; probably imitative or constructed as a nonsense word.
