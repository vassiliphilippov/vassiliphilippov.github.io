visage

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪzɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪdʒ / (BrE)
A person's face, with reference to its form or expression.

Examples

The voice is light in timbre, with a rough edge that corresponds to his visage.

Synset Examples

a pleasant countenance
a stern visage

Related Terms

  • Synonym: countenance - a person's face or facial expression

Etymology

From Old French visage (face), from Latin visus (a look, appearance), from videre (to see).
