holy

1. ADJ

/ ˈhoʊli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli / (BrE)
Connected to a god or religion; regarded as sacred or having a divine quality.

Examples

Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sacred - Regarded with reverence and often associated with the divine
  • Antonym: profane - Not relating to that which is sacred or religious; secular

Etymology

Old English hālig, related to hāl (whole, uninjured).
