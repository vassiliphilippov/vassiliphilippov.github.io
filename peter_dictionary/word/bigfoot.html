Bigfoot

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪɡˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡ.fʊt / (BrE)
an apelike creature said to inhabit remote forests in the United States and Canada, noted for being large, hairy, and elusive

Examples

Had any sightings of Big Foot lately? - They buried your story on Batman. - They bury garbage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Sasquatch - another name for Bigfoot, especially in Canadian usage

Etymology

From 'big' + 'foot', referring to the large footprints attributed to the creature.
