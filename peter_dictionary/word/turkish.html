Turkish

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɝː.kɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.kɪʃ / (BrE)
relating to Turkey, its people, or its language

Examples

Eugene was in his pajamas and dressing gown, and on his large feet he wore yellow Turkish slippers that turned up at the toes.

Synset Examples

Turkish towels

Etymology

From Turkey + -ish, denoting origin or relation.
