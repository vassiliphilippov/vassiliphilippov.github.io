boulevard

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːləˌvɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbuːləˌvɑːd / (BrE)
A wide city street, often lined with trees.

Examples

Down the boulevard he could see a portion of the ghetto wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: avenue - a broad road in a city, often with trees

Etymology

From French 'boulevard', originally meaning rampart, later applied to a broad city street.
