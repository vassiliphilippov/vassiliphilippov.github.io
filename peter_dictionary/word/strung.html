strung

1. ADJ

/ strʌŋ / (AmE), / strʌŋ / (BrE)
Threaded or arranged on a string or cord.

Examples

Here "- She thrust a bundle of keys strung on a thick red cord into Sarah's hand.

Synset Examples

keys strung on a red cord

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threaded - passed through or onto a thread or string

Etymology

Past participle of 'string', from Old English 'streng'.
