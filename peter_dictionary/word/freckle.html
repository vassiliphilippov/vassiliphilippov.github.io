freckle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfrɛk.əl / (AmE), / ˈfrɛk.əl / (BrE)
a small, light brown spot on the skin, often becoming more pronounced with sun exposure, especially in people with fair complexions

Examples

He was seeing, somehow, the face of a young boy, the boy Simms Purdew must once have been, a boy with sorrel hair, and blue eyes dancing with gaiety, and the boy mouth grinning trustfully among the freckles.

Etymology

From Middle English 'freken', from Old Norse 'freknur'.
