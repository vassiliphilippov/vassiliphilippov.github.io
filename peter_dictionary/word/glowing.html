glowing

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
expressing strong approval or enthusiasm; highly complimentary

Examples

How titillating it was to go among people who did not know him as the composer, but who talked in the most glowing terms of the promise of the piece after having heard the first rehearsals.

Synset Examples

glowing praise

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval
  • Antonym: scathing - harshly critical or severely negative, especially in remark or review

Etymology

Figurative use from the literal sense 'shining with warmth or light', from Old English *glōwan* (to glow).
