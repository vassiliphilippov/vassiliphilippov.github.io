admirably

1. ADV

/ ˈæd.mɪr.əb.li / (AmE), / ˈæd.mɪr.əb.li / (BrE)
In a way that deserves respect or approval; in an admirable manner.

Examples

"What is it "? asked the children, whose reflexes and replies were invariably so admirably normal and predictable.
Admirably written, it is a perfect introduction to Swedish history for readers of other countries.

Synset Examples

the children's responses were admirably normal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellently - in an excellent manner
  • Antonym: poorly - in a poor or inadequate manner

Etymology

From admirable + -ly, modelled after the pattern of adjective plus adverbial suffix.
