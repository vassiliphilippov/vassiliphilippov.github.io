etiquette

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛt̬ɪˌkɛt / (AmE), / ˈɛtɪkɛt / (BrE)
The formal rules or conventions governing polite or acceptable social behavior in a particular society or group.

Examples

I made inquiries, I read a book of etiquette.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: protocol - the official procedure or system of rules in formal situations
  • Synonym: politeness - showing good manners and respect for others

Etymology

From French 'étiquette' (prescribed behavior), originally 'label, ticket, note of instructions'.
