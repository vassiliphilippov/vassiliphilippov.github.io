chocolate

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑklət / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət / (BrE)
A hot drink made from cocoa powder, milk, and sugar.

Examples

And listening to such a conversation one morning while taking a cup of chocolate in a cafe, Rousseau found himself bathed in perspiration, trembling lest his authorship become known, and at the same time dreaming of the startling effect he would make if he should proclaim himself suddenly as the composer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cocoa - A drink made from cocoa powder and hot water or milk, usually plainer than chocolate

Etymology

From Nahuatl *chocolātl* (a drink made with cocoa); adopted into Spanish as *chocolate* and then into English.
