krauthead

1. NOUN

/ ˈkraʊtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊtˌhɛd / (BrE)
An offensive and derogatory term for a person of German descent.

Examples

I 've got a quarter of a million Germans in my state, and those krautheads tune in on Father Werther every night, and if he tells them to go out and piss in the public square, that's what they do.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kraut - offensive slang term for a German person

Etymology

From 'kraut' (slang for German, from German 'Kraut', meaning 'herb' or 'cabbage') + 'head'.
