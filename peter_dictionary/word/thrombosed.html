thrombosed

1. ADJ

/ θrəmˈboʊzd / (AmE), / θrɒmˈbəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a blood vessel or tissue) affected with or blocked by a blood clot.

Examples

The terminal hospital admission on June 27, 1959, was necessitated by continued weakness and debility complicated by urinary retention and painful thrombosed hemorrhoids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clotted - having formed or containing clots, often of blood

Etymology

From Greek 'thrombos' (clot).
