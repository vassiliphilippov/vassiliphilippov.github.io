murderer

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝːdərər / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdərə / (BrE)
A person who unlawfully and intentionally kills another human being.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killer - person who kills another, not necessarily unlawfully or intentionally
  • Synonym: assassin - person who murders for political or religious reason, often by surprise
  • Antonym: victim - person harmed or killed by another; opposite party in a crime

Etymology

From Middle English 'mordrer', from Old French 'murdreor', from Medieval Latin 'murderator', from Old English 'mordor' (murder).
