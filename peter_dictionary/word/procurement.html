procurement

1. NOUN

/ prəˈkjʊrmənt / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊəmənt / (BrE)
the process of acquiring goods, services, or materials, especially for business or government purposes

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acquisition - the act of buying or obtaining something

Etymology

From Middle French 'procurement' (management, administration), from Medieval Latin 'procuramentum', from Latin 'procurare' (to take care of).
