underside

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌndərˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the lower or bottom side or surface of something

Examples

And all the time, she had the heat of hatred in her, like charcoal that is burning on its under side, but not visibly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottom - the lowest part or surface of something
  • Antonym: top - the upper part or surface of something
