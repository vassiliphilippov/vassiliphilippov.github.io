fourteenth

1. ADJ

/ fɔrˈtinθ / (AmE), / fɔːˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming after the thirteenth in order; 14th

Examples

He was born in the small Danish town of Rudkoebing on the island of Langeland in the south central part of Denmark on August 14, 1777.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thirteenth - coming after the twelfth in order; 13th
  • Degree Higher: fifteenth - coming after the fourteenth in order; 15th

Etymology

Formed from 'fourteen' (Old English 'féowertīene') plus the ordinal suffix '-th'.
