lengthen

1. VERB

/ ˈlɛŋθən / (AmE), / ˈlɛŋθən / (BrE)
to make or become longer in length

Examples

This would, naturally, lengthen their courses far beyond the largely esthetic demands of interior designer's training.

Synset Examples

Lengthen this skirt, please

Related Terms

  • Antonym: shorten - to make or become shorter in length
  • Synonym: extend - to make something longer or greater in distance or time

Etymology

From the noun 'length' + -en (verb forming suffix).
