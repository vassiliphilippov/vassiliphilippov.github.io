alveolar

1. ADJ

/ ælˈviːələr / (AmE), / ælˈviːələ / (BrE)
Relating to the tiny air sacs (alveoli) of the lungs.

Related Terms

Etymology

From Latin 'alveolus' (small cavity, air sac) + '-ar' (adjectival suffix).
