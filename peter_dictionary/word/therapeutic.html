therapeutic

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɛrəˈpjutɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the treatment of disease or to therapy; having a beneficial effect on health.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curative - having the ability to cure diseases or heal
  • Antonym: palliative - relieving pain without dealing with the cause of the condition

Etymology

From Greek therapeutikos (fit for service, treating), from therapeuein (to attend, treat).
