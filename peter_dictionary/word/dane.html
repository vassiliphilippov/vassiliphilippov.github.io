Dane

1. NOUN

/ deɪn / (AmE), / deɪn / (BrE)
A person from Denmark; a native or inhabitant of Denmark.

Examples

This comes out in "When the Bells Ring ", which describes the rallying of the peasants in southern Sweden to repel an invasion by the Danes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Danish - Relating to Denmark, its people, or language

Etymology

From Old English 'Dene', from Old Norse 'Danir', meaning 'Danes'.
