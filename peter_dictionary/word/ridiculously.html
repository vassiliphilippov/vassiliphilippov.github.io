ridiculously

1. ADV

/ rɪˈdɪkjələsli / (AmE), / rɪˈdɪkjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that is so extreme, unreasonable, or out of proportion as to arouse or deserve laughter.

Examples

The pilots' heads looked ridiculously small.

Synset Examples

her income was laughably small, but she managed to live well

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absurdly - in an absurd or ridiculous manner
  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious manner; with seriousness

Etymology

From ridiculous + -ly; 'ridiculous' from Latin 'ridiculosus' (laughable, absurd).
