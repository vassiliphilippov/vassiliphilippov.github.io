worktable

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝkˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
A table designed and used for a particular kind of work or task, often equipped with drawers or special features for convenience.

Examples

He did n't seem to think that attaching a pegboard to a stone wall was much of a problem and he tossed off the building of the worktable equally lightly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbench - a strong table at which manual work is done; especially by a carpenter, machinist, etc.
