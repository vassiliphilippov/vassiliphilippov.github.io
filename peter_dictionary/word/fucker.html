fucker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌk.ər / (AmE), / ˈfʌk.ə / (BrE)
(Vulgar slang) An offensive term for a stupid or despised man.

Examples

Big, twitchy fucker.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bastard - an offensive term for an unpleasant or despicable man
  • Synonym: jerk - an annoying or contemptible person (informal, American)

Etymology

From the verb 'fuck' plus the agentive suffix '-er'.
