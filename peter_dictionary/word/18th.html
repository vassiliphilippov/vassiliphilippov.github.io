18th

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪtiːnθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪtiːnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the seventeenth in position; ordinal form of eighteen.

Examples

A tongue-in-cheek stylization of 18 th Century ideas; a trial balloon to test the aesthetic climate of the times; a brilliant piece de resistance?
At twelve minutes after five on the morning of Wednesday, April 18, 1906, San Francisco was shaken by a severe earthquake.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eighteenth - coming next after the seventeenth in position
  • Degree Lower: 17th - coming before the 18th in sequence
  • Degree Higher: 19th - coming after the 18th in sequence

Etymology

Formed from 'eighteen' (from Old English eahtatiene) + '-th', ordinal suffix (from Old English -teoða).
