low-ceilinged

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊ ˌsiːlɪŋd / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌsiːlɪŋd / (BrE)
(of a room or building) having a ceiling that is lower than usual.

Examples

The bombproof was a low-ceilinged structure of heavy timbers covered with earth.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-ceilinged - having a ceiling higher than normal
