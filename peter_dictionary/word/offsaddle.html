offsaddle

1. VERB

/ ˈɔfˌsædəl / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌsædəl / (BrE)
to remove the saddle from a horse or other mount

Examples

The rest of the crew offsaddled their mounts and turned them into the remuda.

Synset Examples

They unsaddled their mounts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsaddle - remove the saddle from a horse

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'off-' + 'saddle,' modeled after 'unsaddle'. Common in South African English.
