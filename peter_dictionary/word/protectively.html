protectively

1. ADV

/ prəˈtɛktɪvli / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner intended to protect someone or something from harm or danger.

Examples

Now she kept herself protectively ready to laugh again and sure enough he pointed at her with his index finger and said "Toot "! once more and roared off into the fog, his foot evidently surprising him with the suddenness with which it pressed the accelerator, just as his hand did when he worked.

Synset Examples

he bent protectively over the woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: defensively - in a manner intended to defend

Etymology

From 'protective' + '-ly', ultimately from Latin 'protectus', past participle of 'protegere' (to cover in front).
