fervor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɜrvɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːvə / (BrE)
Intense and passionate feeling; great warmth or enthusiasm.

Examples

George Meredith has said that fervor is the core of style.

Synset Examples

he spoke with great ardor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ardor - intense passion or enthusiasm
  • Synonym: enthusiasm - strong excitement or eagerness
  • Antonym: apathy - lack of interest or feeling

Etymology

From Latin 'fervor', meaning 'heat, boiling'
