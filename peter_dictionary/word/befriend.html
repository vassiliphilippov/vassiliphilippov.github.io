befriend

1. VERB

/ bɪˈfrɛnd / (AmE), / bɪˈfrɛnd / (BrE)
to act as a friend to someone, especially by offering help or support; to become friends with someone

Examples

We can join with old friends, we can make new **friends** and we help celebrate a young man'’s first Communion.

Synset Examples

John and Eric soon became friends
Have you made friends yet in your new environment?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: make friends - to form a friendship with someone

Etymology

From be- (prefix meaning 'make' or 'cause to be') + friend (Middle English, from Old English 'frēond').
