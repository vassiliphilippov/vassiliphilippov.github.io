Lorelei

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɔrəˌlaɪ / (AmE), / ˈlɒrəlaɪ / (BrE)
A siren in German legend reputed to sit on the rock of the Lorelei on the Rhine River, luring boatmen to their doom with her singing.

Examples

He had dared to defy nature, to turn his back to the Lorelei, and he was punished.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: siren - a mythological creature who lures sailors to destruction with singing

Etymology

From German Lorelei, name of a rock on the Rhine and of the legendary siren associated with it.
