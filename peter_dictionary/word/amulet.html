amulet

1. NOUN

/ ˈæmjələt / (AmE), / ˈamjʊlɪt / (BrE)
An ornament or small piece of jewelry, typically worn around the neck, believed to provide magical protection against evil, danger, or disease.

Examples

Oddly enough, this is an amulet against housebreakers, presented to the mem and me by a local rajah in 1949.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talisman - an object thought to have magical powers and bring good luck

Etymology

From Latin 'amulētum' (means of defense, charm).
