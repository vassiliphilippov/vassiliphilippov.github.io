unloaded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈloʊdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈləʊdɪd / (BrE)
(of a weapon) not loaded with ammunition; not containing bullets or cartridges.

Examples

Maybe he only intended to scare the blackmailer, whoever he was, in which case an unloaded gun would be good enough.

Synset Examples

many people are killed by guns thought to be unloaded

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loaded - (of a weapon) containing ammunition

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (meaning 'not') and 'loaded' (past participle of 'load').
