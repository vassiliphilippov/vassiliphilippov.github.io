unsuitably

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈsuː.t̬ə.bli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsuː.tə.bli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not appropriate or fitting for the circumstances.

Examples

Can you imagine yourself forgetting under the circumstances that you are approaching this startling and unexpected situation so unsuitably hatted and armed with a paper horn?

Synset Examples

he was inappropriately dressed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inappropriately - in a manner that is not suitable or proper
  • Antonym: appropriately - in a suitable or proper manner

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'suitable' + '-ly' (adverbial suffix).
