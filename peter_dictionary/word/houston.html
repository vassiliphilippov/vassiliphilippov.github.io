Houston

1. PROPN

/ ˈhjuːstən / (AmE), / ˈhjuːstən / (BrE)
A large city in southeastern Texas, near the Gulf of Mexico, known as the largest city in Texas and the site of NASA's Johnson Space Center.

Etymology

Named after Sam Houston, an American soldier and politician who was instrumental in Texas' independence from Mexico.
