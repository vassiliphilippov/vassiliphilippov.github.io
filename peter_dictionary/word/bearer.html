bearer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈbeərə / (BrE)
a person who delivers or presents something, such as a message or document

Examples

Another less ambiguous case read as follows: "The bearer of this letter has served me for two years to his complete satisfaction.

Synset Examples

a bearer of good tidings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messenger - a person who carries messages or announcements

Etymology

From Old English *beran* (to bear, carry) + -er (agent suffix).
