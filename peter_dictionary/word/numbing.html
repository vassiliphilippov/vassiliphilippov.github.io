numbing

1. ADJ

/ ˈnʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing a loss of sensation or responsiveness; making someone or something unable to feel, think, or react normally.

Examples

And then there was a numbing blow to the heart, and another gut flattening blow to the stomach.
A terrible, numbing sense of futility swept over him.

Synset Examples

the numbing effect of grief

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paralyzing - causing an inability to move or feel; rendering powerless
  • Synonym: deadening - making something less lively, intense, or sensitive
