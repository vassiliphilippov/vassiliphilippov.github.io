inexcusable

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪnɪkˈskjuːzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪkˈskjuːzəbəl / (BrE)
Too bad or wrong to be excused or justified; impossible to excuse.

Examples

But to continue to divorce advanced students from reality is inexcusable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unforgivable - impossible to forgive
  • Antonym: excusable - able to be excused or justified

Etymology

From Latin inexcūsābilis, from in- (not) + excūsābilis (that may be excused).
