piazza

1. NOUN

/ piˈɑzə / (AmE), / piˈatsə / (BrE)
A public square or open space in a city or town, especially in Italy.

Examples

And when they stood by the fountain in the piazza looking at Santa Maria he had to keep a straight face, not letting on he had been there with Alberto.

Synset Examples

they met at Elm Plaza
Grosvenor Place

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaza - a public square or open area in a city
  • Synonym: square - an open, typically four-sided area in a town or city

Etymology

From Italian 'piazza' meaning 'public square', from Latin 'platea' (broad street).
