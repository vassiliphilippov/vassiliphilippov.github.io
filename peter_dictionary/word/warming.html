warming

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
Imparting or giving off heat; making something warm.

Examples

Furthermore - and this, to me, strikes an especially warming note - it shall avail the vandals naught to throw away or dispose of their loot.

Synset Examples

a warming fire

Related Terms

  • Antonym: cooling - making cooler; reducing heat

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'warm' + '-ing'.
