dope

1. NOUN

/ doʊp / (AmE), / dəʊp / (BrE)
A slang term for marijuana.

Examples

The Death's-Head Corps took in Ukrainian and Baltic Auxiliaries, and the Einsatzkommandos waded knee-deep in blood and turned into drunken, dope ridden maniacs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pot - slang term for marijuana
  • Synonym: weed - informal term for marijuana

2. VERB

/ doʊp / (AmE), / dəʊp / (BrE)
to give a narcotic drug to someone, especially illegally or in order to influence performance

Examples

And junk, dope!

Synset Examples

The athletes were dope by the coach before the race

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drug - to administer a drug or narcotic to someone

Etymology

Probably from Dutch *doop* meaning 'sauce' or 'dip,' later applied to drugs or preparations.
