one-off

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ɔf / (AmE), / wʌn ɒf / (BrE)
an event or occurrence that happens only once and is not repeated

Examples

The IRS allows a one-time-only gift to your spouse for up to $60,000.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: one-time - happening, done, or appearing only once

Etymology

Originally British, from 'one' and 'off' (as in 'one time off the normal run').
