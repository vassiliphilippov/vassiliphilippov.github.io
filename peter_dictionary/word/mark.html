Mark

1. PROPN

/ mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
the second and shortest of the four Gospels in the New Testament, recounting the life and teachings of Jesus

Examples

Mark 13:35.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Matthew - the first Gospel in the New Testament
  • Synonym: Luke - the third Gospel in the New Testament
  • Synonym: John - the fourth Gospel in the New Testament

Etymology

From Latin *Marcus*, the traditional name of the Gospel's attributed author.
