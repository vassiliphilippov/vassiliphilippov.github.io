superman

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuː.pɚˌmæn, ˈsjuː.pɚˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌmæn, ˈsjuː.pəˌmæn / (BrE)
A person of extraordinary physical or mental powers and abilities; someone who seems able to achieve feats far beyond the normal.

Examples

That'’s why they call him Superman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demigod - a being with partial divine status; a person with great power

Etymology

From German *Übermensch* ('overman'), coined by philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche.
