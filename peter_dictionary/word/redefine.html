redefine

1. VERB

/ ˌriː.dɪˈfaɪn / (AmE), / ˌriː.dɪˈfaɪn / (BrE)
to give a new or different definition, meaning, or interpretation to something

Examples

Meeting in New Delhi under the auspices of the International Commission of Jurists, a body of lawyers from the free world, the Congress redefined and expanded the traditional Rule of Law to include affirmative governmental duties.

Synset Examples

She redefined his duties

Related Terms

  • Synonym: define - to state or explain the meaning of a word or phrase

Etymology

From re- (again) + define (from Latin definire, to set limits to)
