hitless

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈhɪtləs / (BrE)
(of a batter or player) not achieving a hit, especially during a game or series in baseball or similar sports.

Examples

Of the two cherished achievements the elderly Spahn's hitless pitching probably reached the most hearts.

Synset Examples

he went hitless for three innings

Related Terms

  • Antonym: hitting - successfully striking the ball to score a hit, especially in baseball
