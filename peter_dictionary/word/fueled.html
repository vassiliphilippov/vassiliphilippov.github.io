fueled

1. ADJ

/ ˈfjuəld / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəld / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, device, or engine) heated, driven, or powered by burning fuel.

Examples

The Minuteman solid fueled ICBM is planned to be operational about mid 1963.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fuelled - alternative spelling, chiefly British English
