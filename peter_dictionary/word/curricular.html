curricular

1. ADJ

/ kəˈrɪkjələr / (AmE), / kəˈrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the subjects comprising a course of study in a school or college

Examples

The suggested course of the A.I.D. was based on the usual course offered and on the opinion of many educators as to curricular necessities.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: extracurricular - not part of the standard curriculum, outside the regular course of study

Etymology

From 'curriculum' (Latin: a running, course) + -ar (forming adjectives).
