humane

1. ADJ

/ hjuˈmeɪn / (AmE), / hjuˈmeɪn / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with the humanities or humanistic studies.

Examples

Avoiding runaway technology can be done only by assuring a humane society; and for this human beings must be firmly in control of the economics on which our society rests.

Synset Examples

humanistic studies
a humane education

Related Terms

  • Synonym: humanistic - relating to or supporting the humanities; humanist

Etymology

From Latin 'humanus' (human), through Middle French 'humain' and English 'human'; sense extended in Renaissance education to relate to classical studies.
