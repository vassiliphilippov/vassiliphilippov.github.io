shrieking

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃriːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃriːkɪŋ / (BrE)
A sharp, high-pitched, piercing cry or sound.

Examples

This time there was no sound of brakes but the shrieking of women.

Synset Examples

her screaming attracted the neighbors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scream - a loud, sudden, and usually high-pitched cry

Etymology

From 'shriek' + '-ing', where 'shriek' comes from Middle English 'shrikken' (to shriek).
