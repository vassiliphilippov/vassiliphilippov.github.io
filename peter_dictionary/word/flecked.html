flecked

1. ADJ

/ flɛkt / (AmE), / flɛkt / (BrE)
Marked or covered with small spots or patches of a different color.

Examples

Watson spoke bewilderedly to the dark night flecked with pine-knot torches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dotted - marked with a pattern of dots
  • Synonym: spotted - having spots of a different color

Etymology

From Middle English *flecken* (to spot) from Old Norse *fleckja* (to spot, stain).
