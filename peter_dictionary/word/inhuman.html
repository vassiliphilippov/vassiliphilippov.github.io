inhuman

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ɪnˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
lacking compassion or human feeling; cruel or unfeeling.

Examples

Individual human strength is needed to pit against an inhuman condition.

Synset Examples

in cold blood
cold-blooded killing
insensate destruction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cruel - willingly causing pain or suffering; unkind
  • Synonym: merciless - showing no mercy or pity
  • Antonym: humane - having or showing compassion or benevolence

Etymology

From Latin 'inhumanus', from 'in-' (not) + 'humanus' (human).
