afghan

1. NOUN

/ ˈæfˌɡæn / (AmE), / ˈæf.ɡæn / (BrE)
A blanket, usually knitted or crocheted in colorful strips or squares, sometimes used as a shawl.

Examples

Then, having wrapped the lot in an afghan my dog customarily slept on, you lammed out the front door, considerately leaving it open for neighbors to discover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blanket - Large piece of soft fabric for warmth, often on a bed

Etymology

Named after Afghanistan, where similar textiles were originally made.
