pictorially

1. ADV

/ pɪkˈtɔːriəli / (AmE), / pɪkˈtɔːriəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or resembling pictures; by means of pictures or visual representation.

Examples

Planes defined as parallel to the surface also cut through it into real space, and a depth is suggested optically which is greater than that established pictorially.

Synset Examples

depth is established pictorially

Related Terms

  • Synonym: visually - in a manner relating to sight or seeing

Etymology

From 'pictorial' + -ly; 'pictorial' from Latin 'pictorius' (of a painter), from 'pingere' (to paint).
