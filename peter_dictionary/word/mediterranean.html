Mediterranean

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən / (BrE)
the sea that lies between southern Europe, northern Africa, and western Asia, known as the largest inland sea in the world

Examples

The Navy, on April 25, announced it is bringing back the carrier Shangri-La from the Mediterranean, increasing to four the number of attack carriers in the vicinity of Cuba.

Etymology

From Latin 'Mediterraneus' meaning 'inland' (medius 'middle' + terra 'land'), meaning 'in the middle of the land'.
