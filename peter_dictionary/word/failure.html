failure

1. NOUN

/ ˈfeɪljɚ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪljə / (BrE)
An act of not achieving a desired aim or result; an instance of failing to do something.

Examples

"Failure to do this will continue to place a disproportionate burden "on Fulton taxpayers.
Rep. Charles E. Hughes of Sherman, sponsor of the bill, said a failure to enact it would "amount to making a gift out of the taxpayers' pockets to banks, insurance and pipeline companies ".

Synset Examples

his failure to pass the test

Related Terms

  • Antonym: success - the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
  • Degree Higher: fiasco - a complete, humiliating failure
  • Synonym: mistake - an incorrect act or decision

Etymology

From Old French *faillure*, from *faillir* (fail).

Examples

Related Terms
