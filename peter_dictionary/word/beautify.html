beautify

1. VERB

/ ˈbjuːtəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbjuːtɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make someone or something more beautiful

Examples

they "transform and beautify our inner nature ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: adorn - to decorate or add beauty to
  • Synonym: embellish - to make more attractive by adding decorative details
  • Antonym: uglify - to make ugly or less attractive

Etymology

From beauty + -ify, modeled after similar Latin-derived verbs.
