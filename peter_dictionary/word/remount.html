remount

1. VERB

/ riˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to mount again, especially to get back onto a horse, bicycle, or similar thing after having dismounted

Examples

Leading his pony, he hurried that way, not remounting till he was well below the level of the surrounding range.

Synset Examples

he remounted his horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mount - to climb onto (a horse, bicycle, etc.) for riding
  • Antonym: dismount - to get off a horse, bicycle, or other vehicle

Etymology

From re- (again) + mount (from Old French monter, from Latin montare, 'to mount').
