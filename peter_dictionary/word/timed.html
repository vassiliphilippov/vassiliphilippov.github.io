timed

1. ADJ

/ taɪmd / (AmE), / taɪmd / (BrE)
Occurring or arranged at regular intervals; scheduled to happen at particular points in time.

Examples

Although the standard deviation values on which spread of the lines are based are relatively larger for those centers which begin to ossify early (Table 1), there are considerable differences in this value between centers having the closely timed Onsets.

Synset Examples

closely timed intervals

Related Terms

  • Synonym: periodic - happening at regular intervals
  • Antonym: irregular - not occurring at regular intervals
