fullback

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈfʊlˌbæk / (BrE)
(in American football) a player positioned in the offensive backfield, generally responsible for powerful running plays and blocking.

Examples

He broke that boy (Air Force fullback Nick Arshinkoff) in two and knocked him loose from the football ".
Except for sophomore center Mike Kelsey and fullback Mike Rice, Meek expects the squad to be physically sound for Rice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: halfback - a running back who plays the halfback position on the offensive team
