low-pitched

1. ADJ

/ loʊ pɪtʃt / (AmE), / ləʊ pɪtʃt / (BrE)
(of a sound or voice) having a low frequency; deep in tone.

Examples

She stood up, pulled the coat from her shoulders and started to slide it off, then let out a high-pitched scream and I let out a low-pitched, wobbling sound like a muffler blowing out.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-pitched - (of a sound or voice) having a high frequency; shrill in tone
