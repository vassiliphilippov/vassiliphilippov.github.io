all-round

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔlˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈraʊnd / (BrE)
Having many different skills or abilities; versatile; able to do many things well.

Examples

Kieffer, the only junior in the group, was commended for his ability to hit in the clutch, as well as his all-round excellent play.

Synset Examples

an all-around athlete
a well-rounded curriculum

Related Terms

  • Synonym: well-rounded - having a variety of skills or qualities; developed in all aspects
  • Antonym: specialized - focused on a single area or skill
