groggy

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrɑɡi / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒɡi / (BrE)
Dazed, weak, or unsteady, often due to illness, exhaustion, or the aftereffects of alcohol.

Examples

"I'm a little groggy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dazed - unable to think or react properly; confused
  • Antonym: alert - quick to notice and react; attentive

Etymology

Originally referring to the effects of drinking grog (rum and water); from 'grog', named after Admiral Vernon, nicknamed 'Old Grog'.
