lethality

1. NOUN

/ lɪˈθæləti / (AmE), / lɪˈθæləti / (BrE)
the ability or quality of causing death; deadliness.

Examples

An enemy might choose an acutely debilitating microorganism, a chronic disease producer or one causing a high rate of lethality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadliness - the state of being deadly

Etymology

From Latin *letalis* (deadly) plus the noun-forming suffix *-ity*.
