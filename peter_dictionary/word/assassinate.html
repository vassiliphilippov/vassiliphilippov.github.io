assassinate

1. VERB

/ əˈsæsɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / əˈsæsɪneɪt / (BrE)
to murder (especially a prominent person) for political or ideological reasons

Examples

Before I reach my hotel, I'’ll be assassinated.

Synset Examples

Anwar Sadat was assassinated because many people did not like his peace politics with Israel

Related Terms

  • Synonym: murder - to unlawfully kill someone with intent
  • Degree Lower: kill - to cause the death of a living being

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *assassinatus*, past participle of *assassinare* (to assassinate), from *assassin* (murderer).
