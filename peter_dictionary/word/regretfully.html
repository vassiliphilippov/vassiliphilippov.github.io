regretfully

1. ADV

/ rɪˈɡrɛtfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈɡrɛtfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that expresses regret, especially in a polite or formal refusal or apology.

Examples

Regretfully Sam explained that he had no experience with boxcars.

Synset Examples

I must regretfully decline your kind invitation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regrettably - unfortunately; in a regrettable way

Etymology

From 'regret' + '-fully', meaning 'full of regret'.
