hellion

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛliən, ˈhɛljən / (AmE), / ˈhɛliən, ˈhɛljən / (BrE)
A rowdy, mischievous, or troublemaking person, especially a child or young man.

Examples

I think you made a dam good chouise to turn off as nise a feler as Alf dyer and let that orney thefin, drunkard, damed card playing Sun of a bich com to Sea you, the god damed theaf and lop yeard pigen tode helion, he is too orney for hel.

Synset Examples

he chased the young hellions out of his yard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person, especially a child
  • Synonym: scamp - a person, especially a child, who is mischievous in a likable or amusing way
  • Antonym: angel - a very well-behaved person, especially a child; opposite of mischievous

Etymology

Probably alteration of 'hell' with an added diminutive suffix.
