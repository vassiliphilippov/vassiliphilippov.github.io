categorized

1. ADJ

/ ˈkæt̬əɡəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkatɪɡəˌrʌɪzd / (BrE)
arranged or sorted into categories or groups according to common characteristics

Examples

Mark easily looked years older than himself, settled, his world comfortably categorized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sorted - arranged in order or groups
  • Synonym: classified - arranged into classes or groups
  • Antonym: unclassified - not assigned to a category or group

Etymology

From the verb 'categorize', from Greek 'kategoria' (accusation, predicate, category).
