filtration

1. NOUN

/ fɪlˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / fɪlˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of separating solid particles from a liquid or gas by passing it through a filter.

Examples

That's where the filtering process starts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: filtering - the act or process of filtering

Etymology

From Medieval Latin filtratio, from Latin filtrum (felt used as a filter).
