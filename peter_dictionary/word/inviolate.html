inviolate

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈvaɪ.ə.lət / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪ.ə.lət / (BrE)
(of a woman) having the hymen unbroken; virginal.

Examples

He had left her inviolate, thinking familiarity would gentle her in time.

Synset Examples

she was intact, virginal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: virginal - not having had sexual intercourse; pure; maidenly
  • Synonym: intact - wholly uninjured or undamaged; in original condition

Etymology

From Latin inviolatus (not violated, unhurt), from in- (not) + violare (to violate).
