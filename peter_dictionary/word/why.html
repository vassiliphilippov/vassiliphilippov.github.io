why

1. NOUN

/ waɪ / (AmE), / waɪ / (BrE)
(noun) The reason or cause for something, especially used in phrases like 'the whys and wherefores.'

Examples

Why are they saps?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reason - a cause or explanation for something
  • Synonym: cause - something that produces an effect or result; explanation
  • Synonym: motive - something that causes a person to act in a certain way

Etymology

From Old English hwȳ, instrumental case of hwæt (what), used as an interrogative adverb and later nominalized.
