tat

1. VERB

/ tæt / (AmE), / tæt / (BrE)
to make lace by hand using a small shuttle to knot and loop thread together

Examples

Some women can sit and sew, crochet, tat or knit by the hour, and look calm and relaxed and turn out beautiful work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tatting - the craft or lace made by tatting

Etymology

Of uncertain origin, possibly from French 'tatting' (dentelle).
