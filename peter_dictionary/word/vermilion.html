vermilion

1. ADJ

/ vərˈmɪliən / (AmE), / vəˈmɪliən / (BrE)
of a bright red to reddish-orange color

Examples

I have more than once sat cross-legged in the grass through a long summer morning and watched without touching while a poppy bud higher than my head slowly but visibly pushed off its cap, unfolded, and shook out like a banner in the sun its flaming vermilion petals.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scarlet - brilliant red color with a slightly orange tinge
  • Degree Lower: crimson - deep red color
  • Degree Lower: orange - color between red and yellow

Etymology

From Latin 'vermiculus' (little worm), referring to a dye made from the insect Kermes vermilio.
