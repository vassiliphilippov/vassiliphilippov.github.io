petting

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
Kissing and touching someone in a sexual or affectionate way, especially as foreplay but without sexual intercourse.

Examples

Finally he asked, "Do you object to petting "?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caressing - gently touching or stroking in an affectionate way

Etymology

From the verb 'pet' (to stroke or caress), with the -ing suffix denoting the action.
