bedside

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɛd.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.saɪd / (BrE)
the area or space beside a bed, often referring to the place where someone is attended, especially when ill

Examples

They slept - Mynheer with a marvelously high-pitched snoring, the damn seahorse ivory teeth watching him from a bedside table.

Synset Examples

the doctor stood at her bedside

Etymology

Formed from 'bed' + 'side', meaning the side or area next to a bed.
