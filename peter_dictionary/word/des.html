DES

1. NOUN

/ diˌɛθəlstɪlˈbɛstrɒl / (AmE), / diˌiːˌɛs / (BrE)
a synthetic estrogen formerly used in medicine and as a growth promoter in livestock and poultry

Examples

These are to be fed at a rate to provide 10 milligrams DES per head daily.

Etymology

Acronym from diethylstilbestrol, named from its chemical structure.
