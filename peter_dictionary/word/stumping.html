stumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of campaigning for something, especially by making political speeches in different locations.

Examples

But our stumping tour of the south was n't all misery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: campaigning - the act of working in an organized and active way toward a political goal
  • Synonym: speechmaking - the process of delivering formal speeches, especially to persuade or inform

Etymology

From the phrase 'stump speech,' originally referring to speeches made by standing on a tree stump.
