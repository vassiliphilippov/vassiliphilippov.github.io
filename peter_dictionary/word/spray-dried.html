spray-dried

1. ADJ

/ spreɪ draɪd / (AmE), / spreɪ draɪd / (BrE)
Dried by turning a liquid into a fine spray and exposing it to hot air, so that it becomes a powder.

Examples

The best known field of application for builders is in heavy-duty, spray-dried detergent formulations for household use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dehydrated - preserved by removal of water
