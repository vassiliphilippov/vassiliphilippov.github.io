worn-out

1. ADJ

/ wɔrn aʊt / (AmE), / wɔːn aʊt / (BrE)
(of an object) used so much that it is no longer in good condition or useful.

Examples

Just a used-up con with arthritis in both hands.

Synset Examples

battered trumpets and raddled radios
worn-out shoes with flapping soles

Related Terms

  • Synonym: used-up - (of something) exhausted or consumed, no longer available or useful
  • Synonym: battered - damaged by repeated use or blows
  • Antonym: new - not previously used or worn
