answering

1. ADJ

/ ˈæn.sɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(attributive) Serving as or constituting a reply or response to something.

Examples

This time Lewis had his own rifle in his hands, and he threw some answering fire back at the mysterious far-off shot, then spent most of the day searching out the area.

Synset Examples

an answering glance
an answering smile

Related Terms

  • Synonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively; responding readily
