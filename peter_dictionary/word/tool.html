1. NOUN

/ tuːl / (AmE), / tuːl / (BrE)
A means or method by which something is achieved or carried out, especially in a metaphorical or abstract sense.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: instrument - a means or agency for achieving something, often abstract
  • Synonym: means - method, way, or instrument by which something can be accomplished
  • Synonym: mechanism - a system or structure by which something is accomplished

Etymology

From Old English tǒl (implement, instrument), related to Old Norse tól (tools, utensils).
