inadvertently

1. ADV

/ ˌɪn.ədˈvɝː.tənt.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ədˈvɜː.tənt.li / (BrE)
Without intending to; unintentionally or by accident.

Examples

He flung off Lord's hand and attempted to push past him, inadvertently shoving him into a storefront.

Synset Examples

he unwittingly deleted the references

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accidentally - without intention or by chance
  • Antonym: deliberately - consciously and intentionally; on purpose

Etymology

From the adjective 'inadvertent' + -ly; 'inadvertent' comes from Latin 'in-' (not) + 'advertere' (to turn the mind to, to notice).
