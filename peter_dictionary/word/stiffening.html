stiffening

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪfənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪfənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or act of becoming stiff or more rigid

Examples

Indeed, the apparent stiffening of the industry's attitude in the recent steel strike has a direct explanation in terms of the model here presented.

Synset Examples

stiffening his shoulders, he prepared to advance
