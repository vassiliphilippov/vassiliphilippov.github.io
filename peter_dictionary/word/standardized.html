standardized

1. ADJ

/ ˈstændərdˌaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈstændədaɪzd / (BrE)
Brought into conformity with a standard; made uniform according to a set system or norm.

Examples

These are ways of jointly participating in significantly symbolized, standardized, and ordered religiously sanctified behavior.
The sine qua non of the elections was naturally an impartial and standardized procedure.

Synset Examples

standardized education

Related Terms

  • Synonym: uniform - Remaining the same in all cases; not varying
  • Antonym: customized - Made or changed according to a customer's or user's particular needs

Etymology

From 'standard' + '-ize' + '-ed', indicating something made to conform to a standard.
