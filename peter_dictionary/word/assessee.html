assessee

1. NOUN

/ əˌsɛˈsi / (AmE), / əˌsɛˈsi / (BrE)
A person or entity whose property, income, or similar is officially assessed, especially for taxation purposes.

Examples

The truth is, Norton wanted to size Andy up.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: assessor - person who evaluates or estimates the nature, ability, or quality of someone or something

Etymology

Formed from 'assess' + '-ee' (denoting a person who is the recipient or beneficiary of an action).
