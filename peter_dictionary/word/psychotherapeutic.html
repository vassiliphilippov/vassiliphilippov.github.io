psychotherapeutic

1. ADJ

/ ˌsaɪkoʊˌθɛrəˈpjutɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˌθerəˈpjuːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving psychotherapy; concerned with the treatment of mental or emotional disorders by psychological means

Examples

I am not aware of great attention by any of these authors or by the psychotherapeutic profession to the role of literary study in the development of conscience - most of their attention is to a pre-literate period of life, or, for the theologians of course, to the influence of religion.

Synset Examples

psychotherapeutic sessions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychotherapy - treatment of mental or emotional disorders using psychological methods

Etymology

From psycho- (mind, mental) + therapeutic (related to treatment); ultimately from Greek 'psyche' (soul, mind) and 'therapeuein' (to treat).
