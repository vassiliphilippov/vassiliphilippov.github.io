interspecies

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪntərˈspiːʃiːz / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈspiːʃiːz / (BrE)
Occurring, existing, or taking place between different species.

Examples

It is evident that many marked and striking differences exist between lungs when an inter-species comparison is made.
In addition, a detailed interspecies survey of the incidence of generalized pulmonary emphysema in mammals would be interesting and pertinent.

Synset Examples

an interspecific hybrid

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intraspecies - occurring within a single species

Etymology

From Latin 'inter-' (between) and 'species' (a kind, form, or species).
