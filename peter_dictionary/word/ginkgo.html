ginkgo

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪŋkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪŋkəʊ / (BrE)
A deciduous Chinese tree (Ginkgo biloba) with fan-shaped leaves and yellowish, fleshy seeds, widely cultivated as an ornamental and street tree.

Examples

Sameness for the Old Man was framed in by a wall of ginkgo trees which divided these quarters from the city.

Etymology

From Japanese *ginkyō*, itself from Chinese *yíngguǒ* (silver apricot).
