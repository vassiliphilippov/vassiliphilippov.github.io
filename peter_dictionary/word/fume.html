fume

1. NOUN

/ fjuːm / (AmE), / fjuːm / (BrE)
A gas or vapor, often with a strong or unpleasant smell, consisting of fine particles suspended in the air.

Examples

The fumes in that place...

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smoke - the visible vapor and gases given off by a burning substance
  • Degree Lower: mist - a cloud of tiny water droplets suspended in the atmosphere

Etymology

From Latin 'fumus' (smoke).
