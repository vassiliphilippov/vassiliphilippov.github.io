directory

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛktəri, daɪˈrɛktəri / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktəri / (BrE)
An alphabetical list of names and addresses, especially of people or organizations, often used for reference.

Examples

Too bad your number is in the directory ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: register - official or formal list of names, often for a specific purpose
  • Synonym: catalogue - systematic list, often of items or people
  • Synonym: address book - book or list containing people's names, addresses, and phone numbers

Etymology

From Latin 'dirigere' (to direct, guide) via Medieval Latin 'directorium' (guidebook).
