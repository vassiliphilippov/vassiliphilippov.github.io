storied

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɔr.id / (AmE), / ˈstɔː.rid / (BrE)
having a famous or illustrious history; celebrated or renowned in story or legend

Examples

The hero, who is himself, is represented as a pilgrim in the storied lands of the East, a sort of Faustus type, who, to quote from Professor Bo ^ o ^ k again, "even in the pleasure gardens of Sardanapalus can not cease from his painful search after the meaning of life.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: famed - well known for something; famous; renowned
  • Synonym: celebrated - greatly admired; renowned; famous

Etymology

From 'story' + -ed, meaning bearing stories or legends; sense of 'celebrated in stories' dates from the 16th century.
