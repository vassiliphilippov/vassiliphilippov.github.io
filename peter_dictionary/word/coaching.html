coaching

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the job or activity of training and directing athletes or performers, especially as a professional coach

Examples

He began his coaching career at Pittsburgh Central Catholic High School in 1949.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: teaching - the profession or activity of instructing others

Etymology

From 'coach' + '-ing', originally relating to instruction or tutoring.
