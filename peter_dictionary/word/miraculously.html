miraculously

1. ADV

/ mɪˈrækjələsli / (AmE), / mɪˈrækjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that seems extraordinary, unexplainable, or as if by a miracle.

Examples

Miraculously, she found exactly the right statement.
Miraculously, the bottle was still in my hand, foam still geysering over my (luckily) waterproof watch.

Synset Examples

my hand grasped the gun that was, miraculously, lying on the ground beside my finger tips

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wonderfully - in a wonderful or remarkable way
  • Synonym: amazingly - in an amazing or astonishing manner

Etymology

From 'miraculous' + '-ly'; 'miraculous' from Latin 'miraculosus', from 'mirari' (to wonder).
