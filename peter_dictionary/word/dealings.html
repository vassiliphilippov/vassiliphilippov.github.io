dealings

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːlɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈdiːlɪŋz / (BrE)
interactions, communication, or social or business transactions with others, especially as part of a regular relationship or exchange

Examples

But aside from everything else, it would scarcely be pleasant to have dealings with one who was nominally an underling and actually held - you could say - the whip hand.
What happened after you argued with your wife?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interaction - communication or direct involvement with someone or something
  • Synonym: transaction - an instance of buying or selling something; a business deal

Etymology

From 'deal', meaning to do business or interact, plus the plural noun suffix '-ings'.
