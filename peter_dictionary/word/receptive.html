receptive

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈsɛptɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛptɪv / (BrE)
Willing to consider new ideas or listen to others; open to arguments, suggestions, or change.

Examples

Jane sat receptive and interested.

Synset Examples

receptive to reason and the logic of facts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: closed-minded - unwilling to consider new ideas
  • Synonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas

Etymology

From Latin receptivus (fit for receiving), from recipere (to receive).
