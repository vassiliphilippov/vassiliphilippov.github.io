avenue

1. NOUN

/ ˈævəˌnuː / (AmE), / ˈævənjuː / (BrE)
A way of making progress toward something; a possible method of achieving an aim or dealing with a problem.

Examples

By combining the talents of a medical man, Dr. Aterman, a biophysicist, Mr. Berkely, and an electronics expert, Dr. Zworykin, this novel technique has been developed which promises to open broad avenues to understanding life processes.

Synset Examples

they explored every avenue they could think of
it promises to open new avenues to understanding

Related Terms

  • Synonym: approach - a way of dealing with something; method
  • Synonym: method - a particular procedure for accomplishing something
  • Synonym: opportunity - a chance for advancement or progress

Etymology

From French 'avenue' (approach, avenue), from Old French 'avenir' (to come to), from Latin 'advenire' (to come to).
