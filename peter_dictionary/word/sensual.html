sensual

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛn.ʃu.əl / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tjʊ.əl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving gratification of the senses or physical appetites, especially as distinguished from the mind or spirit.

Examples

He drove sensual patterns off, carefully shaving his long upper lip.

Synset Examples

animal instincts
carnal knowledge
fleshly desire
a sensual delight in eating
music is the only sensual pleasure without vice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sensory - relating to sensation or physical senses
  • Antonym: spiritual - relating to the spirit or mind rather than the body

Etymology

From Latin 'sensualis' (endowed with feeling, sensitive), from 'sensus' (sense).
