puzzlement

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌz.əl.mənt / (AmE), / ˈpʌz.əl.mənt / (BrE)
a feeling of confusion or inability to understand something

Examples

To his puzzlement, there suddenly was no haze.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bewilderment - a feeling of being completely confused or puzzled
  • Synonym: perplexity - the state of being very confused because something is difficult to understand

Etymology

From 'puzzle' + '-ment'; 'puzzle' likely from dialectal 'pose' (to perplex).
