nearness

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɪrnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɪənəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being close in space; proximity.

Examples

The ship's compass was useless because of the nearness of the magnetic North Pole.

Synset Examples

the sudden closeness of the dock sent him into action

Related Terms

  • Synonym: proximity - the state of being near in space or relationship
  • Antonym: remoteness - the state of being far away; distance

Etymology

From near + -ness, formed in Middle English.
