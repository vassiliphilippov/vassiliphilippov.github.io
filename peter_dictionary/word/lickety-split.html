lickety-split

1. ADV

/ ˌlɪkəti ˈsplɪt / (AmE), / ˌlɪkəti ˈsplɪt / (BrE)
Very quickly; without delay.

Examples

Lickety-split. - I want to get home.

Synset Examples

she tackled the job lickety-split

Related Terms

  • Synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
  • Antonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly

Etymology

Originated as American slang in the late 19th century, probably imitative in sound.
