wonderfully

1. ADV

/ ˈwʌndɚfəli / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəfəli / (BrE)
Extremely well or to a remarkable degree; in a wonderful manner.

Examples

For Larkin is already wonderfully contented with his lot.
A salad with greens and tomato is a popular and wonderfully healthful addition to a meal, but add an avocado and you have something really special.
He has a wonderfully retentive memory.

Synset Examples

her voice is superbly disciplined
the colors changed wondrously slowly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marvelously - extremely well; in a marvellous manner

Etymology

Formed from 'wonderful' + '-ly', with 'wonderful' from 'wonder' + '-ful'.
