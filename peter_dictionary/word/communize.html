communize

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑːmjəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɒmjʊˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to make Communist or bring into alignment with Communist principles or policies

Examples

Against this invincible determination to communize the whole world stands a group of nations unable to agree on fundamentals and each refusing to make any sacrifice of sovereignty for the common good of all.

Synset Examples

communize the government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: socialize - to bring under government or collective ownership or control
  • Antonym: privatize - to transfer from public or government control to private ownership

Etymology

Formed from 'communist' + '-ize', patterned after similar English verbs.
