in-person

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
(of an event, activity, or appearance) taking place with the physical presence of the people involved, rather than remotely or virtually.

Examples

Some of the in-person performers were Jack Teagarden, Gene Krupa, Bud Freeman, Pee Wee Russell, Johnny St. Cyr, Joe Sullivan, Red Allen, Lil Armstrong, Blossom Seeley.

Synset Examples

he carried out the negotiations in person
a personal appearance is an appearance by a person in the flesh

Related Terms

  • Antonym: virtual - not physically present; carried out by means of computers or online
