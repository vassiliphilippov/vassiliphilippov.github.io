wonderworking

1. ADJ

/ ˈwʌndərˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʌndəˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) performing or able to perform wonders or miracles.

Examples

Seeing so many illustrations and reading so many testimonials to the value of Quick-Wate and Super-Protein, those two wonder-working Weider food supplements, he decided to try them and see what they could do for him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: miracle-working - having the power to perform miracles

Etymology

Formed from 'wonder' (from Old English *wundor*) + 'working'.
