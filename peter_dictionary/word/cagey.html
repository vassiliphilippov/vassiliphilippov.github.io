cagey

1. ADJ

/ ˈkeɪdʒi / (AmE), / ˈkeɪdʒi / (BrE)
Cautious and shrewd in dealing with others; careful not to give away information.

Examples

He was a cagey sonofabitch.

Synset Examples

a cagey lawyer
too clever to be sound

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wary - watchful and cautious about possible dangers or problems
  • Synonym: shrewd - having or showing sharp powers of judgment; astute
  • Antonym: naive - lacking experience, wisdom, or judgment
