neuronal

1. ADJ

/ nʊˈroʊnəl / (AmE), / njʊəˈrəʊnəl / (BrE)
Relating to neurons, the basic nerve cells of the nervous system.

Examples

He showed convincingly that anxiety is a learned (conditioned) reaction and is the basis of experimental and clinical neuroses and assumed, therefore, that the neuronal changes which underlie the neuroses are functional and reversible.

Synset Examples

neural network

Related Terms

  • Synonym: neural - relating to nerves or the nervous system

Etymology

From neuron + -al, formed on the pattern of anatomical adjectives.
