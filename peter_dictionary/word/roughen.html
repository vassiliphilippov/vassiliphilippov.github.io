roughen

1. VERB

/ ˈrʌfən / (AmE), / ˈrʌfən / (BrE)
to make or become rough or rougher in texture or surface

Examples

Back of clay scored or roughened for proper gripping surface.

Synset Examples

roughen the surfaces so they will stick to each other

Related Terms

  • Antonym: smooth - make or become smooth; opposite action to roughen

Etymology

Formed from 'rough' with the verb-forming suffix '-en'; 'rough' from Old English 'ruh'.
