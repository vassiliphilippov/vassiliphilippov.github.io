oven

1. NOUN

/ ˈʌvən / (AmE), / ˈʌvən / (BrE)
A chamber or appliance used for baking, roasting, or heating food, typically found in kitchens.

Examples

Young Mrs. Arthur had opened the oven and there was a drifting odor of hot biscuits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stove - kitchen appliance for cooking food, often includes an oven

Etymology

From Old English *ofen*, from Proto-Germanic *ubanaz*.
