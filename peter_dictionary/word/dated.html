dated

1. ADJ

/ ˈdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Appearing old-fashioned or belonging to a recent but now past era; no longer current or fashionable.

Examples

Aeschbacher's work is very much akin to Schnabel's, but the sound on his Decca disc is dated, and you will have a hard time locating a copy of it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outdated - no longer useful or fashionable; obsolete
  • Antonym: modern - characteristic of the present or recent times; up-to-date
