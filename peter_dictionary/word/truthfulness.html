truthfulness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtruːθfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈtruːθf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being truthful; honesty; the tendency to tell the truth

Examples

He did n't doubt her truthfulness, although he had heard the words a hundred times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: honesty - the quality of being honest; truthfulness
  • Antonym: deceitfulness - the quality of being deceitful; not telling the truth

Etymology

From 'truthful' + '-ness', formed in English.
