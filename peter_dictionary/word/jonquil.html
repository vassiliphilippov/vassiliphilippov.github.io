jonquil

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒɑŋkwɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒŋkwɪl / (BrE)
A plant of the narcissus family with slender leaves and fragrant yellow or white clustered flowers, native to southern Europe and widely cultivated as an ornamental.

Examples

Already the jonquils were blooming in a flock by the front gate, and the periwinkles were coming on, blue by the porch steps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: narcissus - a plant of the genus Narcissus, which includes jonquils and daffodils

Etymology

From French 'jonquille', from Spanish 'junquillo', diminutive of 'junco' (rush), from Latin 'juncus' (rush).
