/ kleɪm / (AmE), / kleɪm / (BrE)

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: demand - to ask for forcefully or assertively as something due
  • Antonym: relinquish - to voluntarily give up claim to something

Etymology

From Old French *clamer*, from Latin *clamare* (to call, cry out).
