negotiator

1. NOUN

/ nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtər / (AmE), / nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪtə / (BrE)
a person who discusses with others to reach an agreement, especially in business or diplomacy

Examples

The negotiator plays cards until Mike is back safe. -

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mediator - a person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement

Etymology

From Latin negotiator (a businessman, agent), from negotiari (to carry on business).
