racist

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.sɪst / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.sɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that one race is inherently superior to others and who discriminates or expresses prejudice against people of other races

Examples

The two main charges levelled against the Bourbons by liberals is that they are racists and social reactionaries.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bigot - a person who is intolerant toward those holding different opinions
  • Antonym: antiracist - a person who opposes racism
  • Synonym: racism - the belief that races have distinct characteristics, usually involving the idea that some are superior to others

Etymology

From race + -ist, meaning someone who practices racism or believes in racial superiority.
