knotted

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɒtɪd / (BrE)
tied or fastened with a knot

Examples

His hand went to her shoulder and pushed aside the knotted scarf that surmounted the striped poplin gown; then, to better purpose, he took hold of the knot and with dextrous fingers, untied it.

Synset Examples

his carefully knotted necktie

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untied - not tied or fastened with a knot

Etymology

From the verb 'knot' + -ed, meaning tied in a knot.
