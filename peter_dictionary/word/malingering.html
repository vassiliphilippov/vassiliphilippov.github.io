malingering

1. NOUN

/ məˈlɪŋɚɪŋ / (AmE), / məˈlɪŋərɪŋ / (BrE)
pretending to be ill or incapacitated to avoid work or duty

Examples

A lock on the toilet would encourage malingering and primping.

Synset Examples

they developed a test to detect malingering

Related Terms

  • Synonym: malinger - to pretend to be ill in order to avoid duty or work

Etymology

Present participle of 'malinger', from French 'malingre' (sickly).
