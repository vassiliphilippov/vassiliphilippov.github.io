overdone

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈdʌn / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdʌn / (BrE)
Represented as greater than is true or reasonable; exaggerated.

Examples

No man ever had a better opinion of himself and indeed, with one so favored, flattery could hardly seem overdone.
"The hero of his next poem is Napoleon Bonaparte ", said Claire, with slightly overdone carelessness.

Synset Examples

an exaggerated opinion of oneself

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exaggerated - represented as greater than is true or reasonable
  • Antonym: understated - represented as less than is true or reasonable

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessively) + 'done' (past participle of 'do').
