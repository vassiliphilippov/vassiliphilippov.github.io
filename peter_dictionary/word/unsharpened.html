unsharpened

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈʃɑr.pənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɑː.pənd / (BrE)
(of an object, especially a tool or blade) not made sharp; having no sharpened edge or point.

Examples

They look as if they had been sculptured with an unsharpened chisel.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: sharp - having a fine edge or point; not blunt
