sciatica

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈætɪkə / (AmE), / saɪˈatɪkə / (BrE)
pain or discomfort along the sciatic nerve, typically radiating from the lower back down the leg

Examples

writes: Does a brace help in sciatica?
A back brace might help, depending upon the cause of sciatica.

Etymology

From late Latin *sciatica* (pain affecting the hip), from Greek *ischiadikos* (of the hips)
