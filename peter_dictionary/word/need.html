need

1. NOUN

/ nid / (AmE), / niːd / (BrE)
A state in which something necessary is required or desired; a condition requiring relief or fulfillment.

Examples

But it is to say that this need is far more important for such infatuation than most of the pundits seem to have suspected.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: want - a lack of something desirable or essential
  • Synonym: necessity - the fact of being required or indispensable
  • Antonym: surplus - an amount that is more than needed

Etymology

Old English nēad, nēd (distress, necessity), of Germanic origin.
