threatened

1. ADJ

/ ˈθrɛtənd / (AmE), / ˈθrɛtənd / (BrE)
(of a species) likely to become endangered in the near future; at risk of extinction if current trends continue.

Examples

In short, as frequently happens in analyses, the individual feels threatened and defends himself.

Synset Examples

the spotted owl is a threatened species, not yet an endangered one

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: endangered - (of a species) at serious risk of extinction
  • Degree Lower: vulnerable - (of a species) at risk but less so than threatened
