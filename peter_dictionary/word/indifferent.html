indifferent

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɪf.ɚ.ənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪf.ərənt / (BrE)
Not important; having no particular relevance, influence, or consequence.

Examples

Thus, if what is at issue is whether "All S is P ", it is indifferent whether "Some S is not P "or "No S is P ", since in either case the judgment in question is false.

Synset Examples

whether you choose to do it or not is a matter that is quite immaterial (or indifferent)
what others think is altogether indifferent to him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: immaterial - not important; irrelevant
  • Synonym: insignificant - unimportant; lacking significance
  • Antonym: relevant - closely connected or appropriate to what is being done
  • Antonym: important - of great significance or value

Etymology

From Latin 'indifferens', from 'in-' (not) + 'differre' (to differ).

2. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɪfrənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪfrənt / (BrE)
Having no particular interest or concern; showing a lack of care or enthusiasm.

Examples

Rachel was polite, Scotty indifferent.
On the east side of the island of Manhattan the indifferent hawk knew the East River that connected New York Bay with Long Island Sound.
The Union soldiers grounded arms and settled into healthy, indifferent postures to watch the feeble boarding of the skiffs.
He saw Sweden as a country of smug and narrow provincialism, indifferent to the heroic spirit of its former glory.

Synset Examples

an apathetic audience
the universe is neither hostile nor friendly; it is simply indifferent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or concern
  • Antonym: interested - showing curiosity or concern, paying attention

Etymology

From Latin 'indifferens', from 'in-' (not) + 'differre' (to differ).

3. ADJ

/ ɪnˈdɪfərənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪfərənt / (BrE)
having or showing no interest, concern, or feeling; unconcerned or apathetic

Examples

Katya made up the amount in indifferent silence.
I don't care if this festival is in debt, I want a parade.

Synset Examples

indifferent to the sufferings of others
indifferent to her plea

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or emotion
  • Antonym: concerned - worried or interested; showing care or attention

Etymology

From Latin 'indifferens', from 'in-' (not) + 'differre' (to differ).
