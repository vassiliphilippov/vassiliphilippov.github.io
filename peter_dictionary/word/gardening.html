gardening

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrdn̩ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdnɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of cultivating and caring for a garden, especially as a hobby or occupation

Examples

She had dug a hole for each bulb, each tree wore a tag with her writing on it; where would she go for her gardening now?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horticulture - the art or practice of garden cultivation and management

Etymology

From 'garden' + '-ing', with 'garden' from Old North French 'gardin' (enclosure, garden), ultimately of Germanic origin.
