marbleized

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɑr.bə.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.bə.laɪzd / (BrE)
having veins, streaks, or coloring that resembles marble; patterned so as to look like marble.

Examples

Above the tongue, an aged yellow eye, sallow and time-cast, encrusted within a sphere of marbleized pink skin, stared unfalteringly at him.

Synset Examples

marbleized pink skin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marbled - having a pattern or appearance similar to marble

Etymology

From the verb 'marbleize', based on 'marble' with the suffix '-ize' meaning to make like.
