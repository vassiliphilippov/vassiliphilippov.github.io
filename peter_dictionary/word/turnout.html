turnout

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɝnˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːn.aʊt / (BrE)
a section of a road that is widened to allow vehicles to pull over, pass, or park temporarily

Examples

They weren't home, so I... parked in the turnout, and waited.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lay-by - a designated place at the side of a road for vehicles to stop temporarily (chiefly British)
  • Synonym: pullout - a place beside a road where cars can stop or park
