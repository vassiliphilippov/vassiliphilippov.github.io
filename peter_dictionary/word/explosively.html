explosively

1. ADV

/ ɪkˈsploʊsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊsɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that happens very suddenly and rapidly, often with great force or intensity.

Examples

For that legend was growing explosively, Rumor was insisting he received a price of $ 600 a man.

Synset Examples

the population in Central America is growing explosively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: suddenly - in a sudden manner, without warning
  • Synonym: rapidly - very quickly; at a fast rate

Etymology

From 'explosive' + '-ly', forming the adverb from the adjective 'explosive.'
