instrumental

1. ADJ

/ ˌɪn.strəˈmɛn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.strəˈmen.tl̩ / (BrE)
Relating to or performed on musical instruments; composed for or involving instruments rather than voices.

Examples

In some of the numbers the instrumental parts have even been recorded at different times and then later combined on the master tape to produce special effects.
Your ear takes you into the ensemble, and you may well become aware of instrumental details which previously were apparent only in the score.

Synset Examples

instrumental compositions
an instrumental ensemble

Related Terms

  • Antonym: vocal - relating to the voice or singing (as opposed to instruments)

Etymology

From Medieval Latin instrumentalis (relating to an instrument or tool).
