hurler

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɝːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɜːlə / (BrE)
(Baseball) a player who pitches the ball to the batter; a pitcher.

Examples

Then Dick Hyde, submarine ball hurler, entered the contest and only five batters needed to face him before there existed a 3 - to 3 deadlock.
Unfortunately, Brooks' teammates were not in such festive mood as the Orioles expired before the seven hit pitching of three Kansas City rookie hurlers.

Synset Examples

our pitcher has a sore arm

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitcher - the player who throws the ball to the batter in baseball

Etymology

From 'hurl' (to throw with force) + -er (agent noun suffix).
