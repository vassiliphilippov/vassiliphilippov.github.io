pharmaceutical

1. ADJ

/ ˌfɑrməˈsuːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to the preparation, use, or sale of medicinal drugs or to pharmacy as a profession.

Examples

Eager as he was to pursue this promising line, he was so loaded down with the management of the pharmacy and lectures in the medical and pharmaceutical faculties at the university that he could devote only Sunday afternoons to "galvanizing ".

Synset Examples

the pharmaceutical industry

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pharmacy - the science or practice of preparing and dispensing medicinal drugs

Etymology

From Greek pharmakeutikos (pertaining to drugs), from pharmakeuein (to administer drugs), from pharmakon (drug).
