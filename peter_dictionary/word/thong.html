thong

1. NOUN

/ θɔŋ / (AmE), / θɒŋ / (BrE)
A strip of leather forming the flexible part of a whip.

Examples

He saw her hand start to work down the leather thong toward the handle of the quirt, and he grabbed her wrist.

Etymology

Old English *þwong* (narrow strip of leather), related to Old Norse *þvengr* and German *Zwang*.
