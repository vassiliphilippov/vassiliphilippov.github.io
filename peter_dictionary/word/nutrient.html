nutrient

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːtriənt / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtriənt / (BrE)
A substance that provides nourishment essential for growth and the maintenance of life.

Examples

Avocados, however, are very rich in nutrients.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nutriment - substance that provides nourishment; food

Etymology

From Latin 'nutrientem', present participle of 'nutrire' (to nourish).

2. ADJ

/ ˈnuːtriənt / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtriənt / (BrE)
Providing nourishment or sustenance; feeding or nourishing.

Examples

However, this artery is known to be a nutrient vessel with a distribution primarily to the proximal airways and supportive tissues of the lung.

Synset Examples

good nourishing stew

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nourishing - providing nourishment or sustenance

Etymology

From Latin nutritius, from nutrire (to nourish).
