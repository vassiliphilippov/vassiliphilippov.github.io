cosily

1. ADV

/ ˈkoʊ.zɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.zɪ.li / (BrE)
In a cozy, comfortable, or pleasant manner.

Examples

The best could not escape from the worst and the worst nestled cosily beside the better.

Synset Examples

nestled cozily by the fire

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cozily - in a cozy manner (American spelling)

Etymology

From 'cosy' + '-ly', with 'cosy' from Scandinavian origin (possibly Norwegian 'koselig', pleasant, agreeable).
