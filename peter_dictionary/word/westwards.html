westwards

1. ADV

/ ˈwɛstwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstwədz / (BrE)
In the direction of the west.

Examples

The great "sea to the westwards "was a dead end.

Synset Examples

they traveled westward toward the setting sun

Related Terms

  • Antonym: eastwards - toward the east
  • Synonym: westward - toward the west (alternative form)

Etymology

From 'westward' with the adverbial '-s' suffix, similar to 'forwards' and 'backwards'.
