handset

1. NOUN

/ ˈhændˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈhændˌsɛt / (BrE)
the part of a telephone that contains both the receiver and transmitter and is held in the hand during a call

Examples

[HANDSET CLATTERING] [SIGHS]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receiver - part of a telephone that you hold to your ear and mouth to listen and speak

Etymology

From 'hand' + 'set', referring to a set intended to be held by hand.
