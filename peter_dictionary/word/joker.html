joker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʒoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊkə / (BrE)
A person who enjoys telling or playing jokes; someone who jokes or teases others for amusement.

Examples

Very peculiar retribution indeed seems to overtake such jokers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prankster - a person who plays mischievous tricks or practical jokes
  • Antonym: serious person - a person who does not make jokes or take things lightly

Etymology

From joke + -er, forming agent noun; attested from the 18th century.
