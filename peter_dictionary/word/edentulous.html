edentulous

1. ADJ

/ iˈdɛntʃʊləs / (AmE), / iˈdɛntjʊləs / (BrE)
Having lost all natural teeth; toothless.

Examples

The body was that of a well developed, somewhat debilitated white man weighing 108 lb. There were bilateral pterygia and arcus senilis, and the mouth was edentulous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: toothless - without teeth; lacking teeth

Etymology

From Latin 'edentulus', from 'e-' (without) + 'dens' (tooth).
