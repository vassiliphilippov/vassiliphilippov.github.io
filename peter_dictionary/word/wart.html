wart

1. NOUN

/ wɔrt / (AmE), / wɔːt / (BrE)
a small, rounded bump or protuberance, especially one found on plants or animals

Examples

By scrutinizing the flowers, one can also notice that the scale bears one or two tiny warts.

Etymology

Old English *wearte*, related to Dutch *wrat* and German *Warze* (wart).
