Stetson

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɛtsən / (AmE), / ˈstɛtsən / (BrE)
a hat made of felt with a creased crown, especially a broad-brimmed cowboy hat

Examples

Straightening up, his eyes ablaze, he held out the battered Stetson.

Etymology

Named after John B. Stetson, the American hat manufacturer (1830–1906).
