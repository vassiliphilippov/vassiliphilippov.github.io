godmother

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑdˌmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌmʌðə / (BrE)
A woman who sponsors a child at baptism, taking a special interest in their spiritual upbringing.

Examples

Even though my wife is godmother to your only child.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: godfather - A man who sponsors a child at baptism

Etymology

From 'god' + 'mother', modeled on 'godfather'; attested in Middle English.
