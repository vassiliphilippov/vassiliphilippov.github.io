stylistic

1. ADJ

/ staɪˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / staɪˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerning style, especially in the use of language, art, or design.

Examples

But the historian of literature need not confine his attention to biography or to stylistic questions of form, "texture ", or technique.

Synset Examples

stylistic devices

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stylistical - alternative or less common form of 'stylistic'

Etymology

From style + -istic, patterned after French stylistique.
