cushioning

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃ(ə)nɪŋ / (BrE)
A soft or resilient material used to provide padding, shape, protection, or added comfort.

Examples

For added comfort some of the Italian designed sandals have foam padded cushioning.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: padding - soft material used to protect or add comfort

Etymology

From 'cushion' + '-ing', denoting the act or result of cushioning.
