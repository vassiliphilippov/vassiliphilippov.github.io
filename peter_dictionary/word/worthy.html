worthy

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːði / (AmE), / ˈwɜːði / (BrE)
Deserving to be chosen or regarded as suitable, especially as a partner or candidate.

Examples

Telefunken has accorded him beautiful sound, and this bargain-priced disc (it sells for $ 2.98) is worthy of consideration.

Synset Examples

the parents found the girl suitable for their son

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unworthy - not deserving; not suitable to be chosen
  • Synonym: suitable - right or appropriate for a particular person, purpose or situation

Etymology

From Old English weorþig (valuable, important, deserving), from weorþ (worth, value).

  • Antonym: unworthy - not deserving respect, attention, or admiration
