Marine

1. NOUN

/ məˈrin / (AmE), / məˈriːn / (BrE)
A member of the United States Marine Corps.

Examples

There 'd been a pretty bad incident back at the Marine base.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soldier - a person who serves in an army
  • Synonym: sailor - a person who works on a ship, especially in the navy

Etymology

From Latin 'marinus' (of the sea), via French 'marin'; adopted for military branches operating on land and sea.
