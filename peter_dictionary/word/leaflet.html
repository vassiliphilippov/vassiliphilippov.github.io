leaflet

1. NOUN

/ ˈliːflət / (AmE), / ˈliːflət / (BrE)
a thin, flat, triangular segment of a heart valve, especially in the context of anatomy or medicine

Examples

The valves were normal except for thin yellow plaques on the inferior surface of the mitral leaflets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cusp - pointed or rounded end of a structure, especially a valve in the heart

Etymology

From 'leaf' + diminutive suffix '-let'.
