churchgoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɝtʃˌɡoʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who goes to church regularly, especially one who attends Christian worship services habitually

Examples

She found this a marvel because, as she said, only six per cent of English people are churchgoers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worshipper - a person who worships, especially in a religious building

Etymology

Formed from 'church' + 'goer', meaning one who goes to church.
