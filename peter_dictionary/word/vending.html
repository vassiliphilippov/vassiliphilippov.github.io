vending

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
The business or activity of selling goods, especially from machines or stalls.

Examples

Have you considered using vending equipment to replace or reduce the number of cafeteria employees?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: retailing - the activity of selling goods to the public, usually in shops

Etymology

From Latin 'vendere' (to sell) via French 'vendre'.
