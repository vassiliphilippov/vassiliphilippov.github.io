Turkey

1. PROPN

/ ˈtɝː.ki / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.ki / (BrE)
A country located at the crossroads of southeastern Europe and western Asia, officially known as the Republic of Turkey.

Examples

In "The King's Ride ", Charles breaks out of a long period of petulance and inertia, regains his old self, escapes from Turkey, and finally reaches his own land after an absence of eighteen years.
He has fields in Turkey, where they grow poppy.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin Turcia, from Turkish Türk (name of the people).
