advisable

1. ADJ

/ ədˈvaɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
Worth recommending or suggesting; prudent or wise.

Examples

A portable electric heater is advisable for shelters in cold climates.

Synset Examples

such action is neither necessary nor advisable
extreme caution is advisable
it is advisable to telephone first

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inadvisable - not recommended; unwise

Etymology

From Middle French 'advisable', from 'aviser' (to advise).
