inflammatory

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈflæməˌtɔri / (AmE), / ɪnˈflamətəri / (BrE)
relating to or caused by inflammation in the body, especially as part of a disease or injury process.

Examples

Inflammatory cells were strikingly absent.

Synset Examples

an inflammatory process
an inflammatory response

Related Terms

  • Antonym: anti-inflammatory - (of a drug or substance) reducing inflammation

Etymology

From Medieval Latin inflammatorius, from Latin inflammare (to set on fire, to inflame).
