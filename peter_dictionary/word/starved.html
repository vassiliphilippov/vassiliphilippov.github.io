starved

1. ADJ

/ stɑrvd / (AmE), / stɑːvd / (BrE)
Suffering or having suffered from lack of food; extremely hungry.

Examples

Observing their distressing condition, Colonel Snelling allowed these half starved immigrants to settle on the military reservation.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hungry - feeling the need for food

Etymology

Past participle of 'starve', from Old English *steorfan* (to die).
