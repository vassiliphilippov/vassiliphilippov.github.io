unwed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈwɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛd / (BrE)
not married; never having been married.

Examples

Her mother had done it before her, and even her old grandmother, who had collected money for smallpox and unwed mothers.

Synset Examples

unwed mother

Related Terms

  • Antonym: married - having a husband or wife
  • Synonym: single - not married

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (not) and 'wed' (marry).
