hibachi

1. VERB

/ hɪˈbɑːtʃi / (AmE), / hɪˈbɑːtʃi / (BrE)
to cook food over a hibachi grill, a small Japanese-style portable barbecue

Examples

In the winter, hibachi in the kitchen or grill over the logs of the fireplace.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grill - to cook food over direct heat, usually on a grill

Etymology

From Japanese 'hibachi', meaning 'fire bowl'.
