freewheeler

1. NOUN

/ ˈfriˌwilər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌwiːlə / (BrE)
a person who acts freely, without caring about rules or responsibilities; someone who is independent and sometimes considered irresponsible

Examples

Even the non church members - the freewheelers, marginal religionists and so on - have the values of Christian civilization internalized in them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: maverick - an independent-minded person who refuses to conform
  • Synonym: rebel - a person who resists authority or control

Etymology

From 'freewheel' (to move freely without constraint) + '-er' (agent noun suffix).
