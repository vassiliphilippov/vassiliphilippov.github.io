cps

1. NOUN

/ si pi ɛs / (AmE), / siː piː ɛs / (BrE)
Abbreviation for cycles per second; a formerly common unit of frequency equivalent to one hertz.

Examples

Figure 3 shows the data on a silicone fluid, labeled 12500 cps which gave a high positive normal pressure.
A modulation frequency of 40 cps with an amplitude as small as possible, commensurate with reasonably good signal-to-noise quality, was used.
This assembly also supplies a 20 - cps switching gate for the electronics circuitry.)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hertz - the current SI unit of frequency, equal to one cycle per second

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'cycles per second'. Now largely replaced by 'hertz', named after Heinrich Hertz.
