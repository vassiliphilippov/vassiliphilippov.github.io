afoot

1. ADV

/ əˈfʊt / (AmE), / əˈfʊt / (BrE)
On foot; walking rather than riding or driving.

Examples

The citrus tones popular in clothing are also to be found afoot.

Synset Examples

they went to the village afoot
quail are hunted either afoot or on horseback

Etymology

From Middle English, on + foot.
