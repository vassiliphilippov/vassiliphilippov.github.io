terrorist

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛrərɪst / (AmE), / ˈtɛrərɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses unlawful violence and intimidation, especially against civilians, in the pursuit of political aims

Examples

Throughout the land their hit-and-run terrorists spread fear of ambush and death.
He was a thief... and a terrorist.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: freedom fighter - a person who takes part in a violent struggle to achieve a political goal, especially to overthrow a government

Etymology

From French *terroriste*, from Latin *terror* (fear, alarm); originally used during the French Revolution.
