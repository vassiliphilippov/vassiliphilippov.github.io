shorten

1. VERB

/ ˈʃɔrtən / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtən / (BrE)
To make something shorter in length, duration, or extent; to reduce in size or amount.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lengthen - to make longer in length or duration
  • Synonym: curtail - to reduce in extent or quantity; to cut short

Etymology

Formed from 'short' + '-en' (verb-forming suffix); Middle English.
