panoramic

1. ADJ

/ ˌpænəˈræmɪk / (AmE), / ˌpænəˈræmɪk / (BrE)
Offering a wide, comprehensive, or unobstructed view of an area in all directions.

Examples

Let's take a panoramic look back over the course we have come.

Synset Examples

a bird's-eye survey
a panoramic view

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comprehensive - including all or nearly all elements or aspects of something
  • Synonym: sweeping - broad or extensive in range or effect

Etymology

From French panoramique, from Greek pan- (all) + horama (view).
