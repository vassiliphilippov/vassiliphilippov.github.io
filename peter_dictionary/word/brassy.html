brassy

1. ADJ

/ ˈbræsi / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsi / (BrE)
having a loud, harsh, and resonant sound similar to that produced by a brass instrument

Examples

The penalty for this is noticeable in the big, bold, brilliant, but brassy piano sounds in Melody and Percussion for Two Pianos (SP 44007).

Related Terms

  • Synonym: metallic - having the sound or appearance of metal
  • Antonym: mellow - having a smooth, soft, or gentle sound, especially in music

Etymology

From 'brass', the metal used in brass musical instruments, with the suffix '-y' meaning 'having the characteristics of'.
