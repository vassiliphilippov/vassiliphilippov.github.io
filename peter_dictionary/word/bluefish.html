bluefish

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a predatory, bluish marine fish (Pomatomus saltatrix), found in warm waters and valued both as food and game fish

Examples

We were camping a few weeks ago on Cape Hatteras Campground in that land of pirates, seagulls and bluefish on North Carolina's famed Outer Banks.
