cholelithiasis

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊ.lə.lɪˈθaɪ.ə.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.lɪ.lɪˈθaɪ.ə.sɪs / (BrE)
The presence of gallstones in the gallbladder.

Examples

Additional findings at this time included cardiomegaly, peripheral arteriosclerosis obliterans, and cholelithiasis.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gallstone - a small, hard mass formed in the gallbladder

Etymology

From Greek 'chole' (bile) + 'lithos' (stone) + '-iasis' (medical condition).
