impossibility

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˌpɑːsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɒsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The state or fact of being unable to occur, exist, or be done.

Examples

There'’s no way we can get to him!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: possibility - the state of being possible

Etymology

From Latin 'impossibilitas', from 'im-' (not) + 'possibilis' (possible).
