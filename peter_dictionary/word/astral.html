astral

1. ADJ

/ ˈæstrəl / (AmE), / ˈæstrəl / (BrE)
Relating to or coming from the stars or outer space.

Examples

The titles he could read easily through the criss-crossed wires: works on theology, astral physics, history, biology, political science.

Synset Examples

an astral body
stellar light

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stellar - relating to stars

Etymology

From Latin 'astralis', from 'astrum' (star).
