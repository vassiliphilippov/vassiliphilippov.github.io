fabulous

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæbjələs / (AmE), / ˈfæb.jʊ.ləs / (BrE)
Extremely good, pleasing, or impressive; wonderful.

Examples

Alone with the fabulous Byron!

Synset Examples

a fabulous vacation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fantastic - extraordinarily good or attractive
  • Synonym: marvelous - causing great wonder; extraordinarily good
  • Antonym: terrible - very bad or unpleasant

Etymology

From Latin 'fabulosus' (celebrated in fable, legendary), from 'fabula' (story, fable).
