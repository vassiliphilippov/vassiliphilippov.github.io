cumulative

1. ADJ

/ ˈkjuːmjəˌleɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈkjuːmjʊlətɪv / (BrE)
increasing or growing by successive additions; resulting from gradual accumulation

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incremental - increasing gradually by small amounts
  • Synonym: successive - following in order or sequence

Etymology

From Latin cumulativus, from cumulatus (heaped up), past participle of cumulare (to heap up).
