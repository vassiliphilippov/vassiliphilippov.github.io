timepiece

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪm.piːs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm.piːs / (BrE)
A device, such as a clock or watch, used for measuring and displaying the time.

Examples

This sentence would have most of the characteristics of a question, but it has some of the characteristics of a statement because the questioner has conveyed the fact that he has no faith in his own timepiece or the one attached to his car.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clock - a device for measuring and indicating time, typically mounted on a wall or desk
  • Synonym: watch - a small timepiece worn typically on a strap on one's wrist

Etymology

Formed from 'time' + 'piece' in English, denoting a piece or device for showing time.
