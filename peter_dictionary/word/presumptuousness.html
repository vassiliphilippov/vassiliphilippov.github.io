presumptuousness

1. NOUN

/ prɪˈzʌmptʃuəsnəs / (AmE), / prɪˈzʌmptjʊəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being overly bold or forward, especially in a way that is considered disrespectful or inappropriate.

Examples

Listen, I came over to... ...clear... - I don't know who you think you are!

Synset Examples

he despised them for their presumptuousness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: presumption - behavior that is too confident and shows lack of respect
  • Antonym: modesty - the quality of not being too proud or confident

Etymology

From 'presumptuous' + '-ness'; 'presumptuous' from Latin 'praesumptuosus' (full of presumption).
