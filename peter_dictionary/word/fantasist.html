fantasist

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæn.təs.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈfan.tə.sɪst / (BrE)
a person who creates or indulges in fantasies, often imagines things that are not true or real

Examples

Yet a moment did come that night when the adventurous letter writer and fantasist seemed to stride off my flashy pages, out of my mind, and plant himself in reality.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dreamer - someone who dreams or has fanciful ideas
  • Antonym: realist - a person who accepts a situation as it is

Etymology

From 'fantasy' + suffix '-ist', meaning one who practices or is concerned with something.
