satirically

1. ADV

/ səˈtɪrɪkli / (AmE), / səˈtɪrɪkli / (BrE)
In a satirical manner; using satire to criticize or mock.

Examples

And what news, Sarah thought as satirically as Maude might have said it.

Synset Examples

she spoke satirically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ironically - in an ironic manner
  • Antonym: seriously - in a serious manner

Etymology

From satirical + -ly; 'satirical' from French satirique, from Latin satiricus.
