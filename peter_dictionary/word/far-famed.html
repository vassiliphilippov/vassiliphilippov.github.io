far-famed

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɑr ˌfeɪmd / (AmE), / ˈfɑː ˌfeɪmd / (BrE)
Famous and widely known; celebrated.

Examples

Now, under the impact of his wife's disclosures, he was brought suddenly to the realization that there was a limit to tolerance, however brilliant, however far-famed the offender might be.

Synset Examples

a famous actor
a celebrated musician
a famed scientist
an illustrious judge
a notable historian
a renowned painter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: famous - known by many people; renowned
  • Synonym: celebrated - widely praised and respected
  • Synonym: illustrious - well known and admired for past achievements
  • Antonym: unknown - not known or recognized

Etymology

Formed from 'far' + 'famed', meaning famed far and wide.
