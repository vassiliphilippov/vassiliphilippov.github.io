selected

1. ADJ

/ səˈlɛktɪd / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛktɪd / (BrE)
chosen from a group or collection, typically because of being considered the best, most suitable, or most desirable

Examples

Selected bibliographies and tables of available data are now in preparation.
The following table shows, for selected years, the authorizations, appropriations, allotment base, Federal grants to States and State matching funds for this part of the grant program.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chosen - having been picked out or selected
  • Antonym: rejected - not chosen or selected
