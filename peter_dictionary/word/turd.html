turd

1. NOUN

/ tɝd / (AmE), / tɜːd / (BrE)
A piece of solid waste passed from the body; feces. (Vulgar)

Examples

By the year Kennedy was shot, Andy had transformed a storage room smelling of rat turds and turpentine into the best prison library in New England, complete with a fine selection of Hank Williams.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crap - vulgar term for feces
  • Synonym: feces - solid bodily waste discharged from the bowels

Etymology

Probably from Middle English *tord*, from Old English *tord* (piece of excrement).
