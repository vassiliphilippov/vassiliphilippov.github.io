elementary

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛləˈmɛn.tə.ri / (AmE), / ˌɛl.ɪˈmen.tər.i / (BrE)
Simple and not difficult; straightforward or uncomplicated.

Examples

We are trying to study a linear operator T on the finite dimensional space V, by decomposing T into a direct sum of operators which are in some sense elementary.
"If there was collusion between an outside murderer and a member of the household it would be an elementary precaution to check on the door later.

Synset Examples

an elementary problem in statistics
elementary, my dear Watson
a simple game
found an uncomplicated solution to the problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: simple - easy to understand or do; not complicated
  • Antonym: complicated - consisting of many interconnecting parts; complex

Etymology

From Latin 'elementarius', from 'elementum' (element).
