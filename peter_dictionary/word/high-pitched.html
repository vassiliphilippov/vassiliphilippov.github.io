high-pitched

1. ADJ

/ haɪ pɪtʃt / (AmE), / haɪ pɪtʃt / (BrE)
(of a roof or surface) having a steep or sharp angle; sloping at a high angle.

Examples

Square corner - and gate posts were an open-work pattern of cast-iron foliage; they were topped by steeples complete in every detail: high pitched roof, pinnacle, and narrow gable.

Synset Examples

a high-pitched roof

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steep - having a sharp incline or slope
