renunciation

1. NOUN

/ rɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of rejecting, disowning, or disclaiming something, especially formally or publicly.

Examples

What a patchwork of proclamations and renunciations!

Synset Examples

Congressional repudiation of the treaty that the President had negotiated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repudiation - the action of rejecting or disowning something

Etymology

From Latin renuntiatio (announcing back, denial), from renuntiare (to deny, reject).
