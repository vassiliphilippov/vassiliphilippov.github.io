Luger

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːɡər / (AmE), / ˈluːɡə / (BrE)
A German semiautomatic pistol, especially the P08 model, known for its distinctive design and historical use.

Examples

From his shoulder bag peeked the seven inch barrel of a Luger.

Etymology

Named after Georg Luger (1849–1923), the Austrian inventor who designed the pistol.
