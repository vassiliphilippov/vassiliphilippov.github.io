reverie

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛvəri / (AmE), / ˈrɛvəri / (BrE)
A state of being pleasantly lost in one's thoughts; a daydream.

Examples

The slow tapping of a cane on the stone steps coming up to the tower interrupted his reverie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daydream - A series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present

Etymology

From French 'rêverie' (daydream, fantasy), from 'rêver' (to dream).
