wide-cut

1. ADJ

/ ˈwaɪdˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdˌkʌt / (BrE)
(of clothing) made with a wide shape or ample fabric; cut generously to be loose or full.

Examples

He felt cheerful again, refreshed; presentable in his wide-cut brown suit, the well-made riding boots.

Synset Examples

the current taste for wide trousers
a full skirt

Related Terms

  • Antonym: narrow-cut - (of clothing) cut to be narrow or close-fitting
