rewarding

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈwɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈwɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
Giving personal satisfaction or providing a sense of achievement.

Examples

Of these there are surely few that would be more rewarding discoveries than Verner von Heidenstam, the Swedish poet and novelist who received the award in 1916 and whose centennial was celebrated two years ago.
I do n't know that it was always as rewarding as I had expected it to be.

Synset Examples

a rewarding career as a paramedic

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fulfilling - making someone satisfied or happy because of fully developing their abilities or character; satisfying
  • Synonym: satisfying - giving pleasure or satisfaction
  • Antonym: disappointing - not as good as expected; failing to satisfy

Etymology

From 'reward' + '-ing', meaning giving or providing a reward (originally a tangible benefit, now often figurative).
