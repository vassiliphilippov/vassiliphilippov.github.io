abused

1. ADJ

/ əˈbjudzd / (AmE), / əˈbjuːzd / (BrE)
used improperly or excessively, especially referring to substances, authority, or language.

Examples

My definition of this much abused adjective is that a reconstructed rebel is one who is glad that the North won the War.

Synset Examples

an abused substance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: misused - used in the wrong way; synonym for 'abused'
  • Antonym: neglected - not given proper care or attention; sometimes the antonym of 'abused' in caregiving contexts

Etymology

From the verb 'abuse,' which comes from Latin 'abusus', past participle of 'abuti', meaning 'to use up' or 'misuse'.
