gray-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡreɪ hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪ heəd / (BrE)
Having hair that is turning or has turned gray or white, typically as a sign of aging.

Examples

And a gray-haired man whose glance - direct, lifelike, and mildly accusing - was contradicted by the gilt and black frame.
Looking back I saw a gray-haired man getting out of his halted car and trying to read our license number.

Synset Examples

whose beard with age is hoar
nodded his hoary head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white-haired - having white hair, typically due to age
  • Degree Higher: elderly - (of a person) old or advanced in age
