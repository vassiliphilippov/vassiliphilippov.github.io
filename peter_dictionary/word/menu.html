menu

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛnju / (AmE), / ˈmen.juː / (BrE)
a list of the dishes available to order in a restaurant or café

Examples

Would it be feasible to limit the menu in order to reduce feeding costs?
They need wine in the front room, and a Mrs. Daley needs a copy of the menu.

Synset Examples

the menu was in French

Etymology

From French *menu* (detailed list), from Latin *minutus* (small, minute).
