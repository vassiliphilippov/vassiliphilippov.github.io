limbo

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɪmbəʊ / (BrE)
a state or condition in which someone or something is ignored, forgotten, or left unresolved

Examples

Because they are "minority "exercises and have but a limited appeal they soon find themselves in the limbo of the forgotten.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oblivion - the state of being forgotten or unknown

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *limbus* (edge, boundary, border).
