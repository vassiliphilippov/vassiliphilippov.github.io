mum

1. ADJ

/ mʌm / (AmE), / mʌm / (BrE)
not speaking; silent or unwilling to speak

Examples

I went and, mum and nervous, all but made a fool of myself.

Synset Examples

the witness remained silent

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silent - not making or accompanied by any sound
  • Antonym: talkative - inclined to talk a great deal

Etymology

Possibly from Middle English *mum*, imitative of a closed mouth; seen in the old expression 'mum's the word.'
