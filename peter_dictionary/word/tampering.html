tampering

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæmpərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæmpərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of secretly or improperly interfering with or altering something.

Examples

It places certain values at least beyond questioning and tampering.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tamper - to interfere with something in a harmful or unauthorized way
