Pennsylvania

1. PROPN

/ ˌpɛnsəlˈveɪniə / (AmE), / ˌpɛnsɪlˈveɪniə / (BrE)
A state in the northeastern United States, one of the original thirteen colonies, located in the Mid-Atlantic region.
