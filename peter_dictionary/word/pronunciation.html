pronunciation

1. NOUN

/ prəˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / prəˌnʌnsɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
The way in which a word or language is spoken, especially according to standard or accepted forms.

Examples

Plus a monthly payment of five percent of the gross of all four hotels, Mr., uh, [EMPHASIZES ITALIAN PRONUNCIATION] Corleone.

Synset Examples

the pronunciation of Chinese is difficult for foreigners
that is the correct pronunciation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: articulation - the clear and precise pronunciation of words
  • Synonym: enunciation - the act of pronouncing words clearly

Etymology

From Medieval Latin *pronuntiatio*, from Latin *pronuntiare* (to proclaim).
