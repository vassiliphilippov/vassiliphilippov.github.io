unarmed

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɑrmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːmd / (BrE)
Not carrying or equipped with weapons.

Examples

Pistol whipping an unarmed man might come easy to someone like Jess, but Curt could n't bring himself to do it.

Synset Examples

went alone and unarmed
unarmed peasants were shot down
unarmed vehicles

Related Terms

  • Antonym: armed - carrying or equipped with weapons

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'armed', from Latin arma (arms, weapons).
