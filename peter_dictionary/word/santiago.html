Santiago

1. PROPN

/ sænˈtjɑːɡoʊ / (AmE), / sænˈtjɑːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A major port city in southeastern Cuba, known as an industrial and cultural center.

Examples

Due to serious setbacks to our troops in Guantanamo and Santiago... ...my position in Cuba is untenable.

Etymology

Spanish, from the name of Saint James (Santiago), the patron saint of Spain.
