vehicle

1. NOUN

/ ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈviːɪkəl / (BrE)
A machine, usually with wheels and an engine, used for transporting people or goods, such as a car, truck, bus, or cart.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: car - a road vehicle powered by an engine, typically with four wheels
  • Synonym: conveyance - a means of transporting people or goods
  • Antonym: pedestrian - a person walking, as opposed to using a vehicle

Etymology

From Latin 'vehiculum' (means of transport), from 'vehere' (to carry).
