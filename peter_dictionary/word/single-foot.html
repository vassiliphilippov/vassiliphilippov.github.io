single-foot

1. VERB

/ ˈsɪŋɡəlˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəlˌfʊt / (BrE)
(Of a horse) to move at a fast, smooth gait known as a single-foot or rack, in which each foot moves independently.

Examples

This Sparky can rack and single-foot and he's the fastest thing in Washoe County.

Synset Examples

the horses single-footed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rack - a gait of a horse in which each foot strikes the ground separately at a fast pace
