collapsible

1. ADJ

/ kəˈlæpsəbl̩ / (AmE), / kəˈlæpsəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of an object) able to be folded or collapsed into a smaller size, especially for easy storage or transport.

Examples

At the Dumont, a guest had come in a collapsible wheel chair.

Synset Examples

a collapsible boat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: folding - able to be folded (especially for furniture or objects)

Etymology

From 'collapse' + '-ible', where 'collapse' is from Latin 'collapsus' (fallen together).
