baffling

1. ADJ

/ ˈbæf.əl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæf.əl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely difficult to understand or explain; puzzling or confusing.

Examples

This baffling lack of distinct details recalls the secretary whose employer was leaving the office and told her what to answer if anyone called in his absence.

Synset Examples

a baffling problem
I faced the knotty problem of what to have for breakfast
a problematic situation at home

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusing - causing confusion; difficult to understand
  • Antonym: straightforward - simple and easy to understand; not complicated

Etymology

From 'baffle' (to confuse), originally of unknown origin, with the present participle suffix '-ing'.
