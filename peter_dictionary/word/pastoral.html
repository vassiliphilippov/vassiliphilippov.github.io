pastoral

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæstərəl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstərəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a pastor or the duties of a pastor, especially in the Christian church.

Examples

The proclamation of the churches is almost totally confined to pastoral contacts by the clergy (17.3 per cent of new members) and friendly contacts by members (over two thirds if organizational activities are included).

Synset Examples

pastoral work
a pastoral letter

Etymology

From Latin 'pastoralis' (of a shepherd/pastor), from 'pastor' (shepherd, pastor).
