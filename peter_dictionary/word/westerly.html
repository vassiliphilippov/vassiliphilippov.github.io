westerly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɛstɚli / (AmE), / ˈwɛstəli / (BrE)
In the direction of the west.

Examples

Although they journeyed westerly as far as Germantown, beyond the Erie roundhouse and the machine shop, and along the Delaware and Hudson Canal, and northward to Brooklyn, below Point Peter, he could see the church spire wherever he looked back.

Synset Examples

we began to steer away westerly

Related Terms

  • Antonym: easterly - toward the east (adverb)
  • Synonym: westward - in the direction of the west (adverb)

Etymology

From 'west' + '-erly', denoting direction.
