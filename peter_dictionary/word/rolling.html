rolling

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a speech sound, especially an 'r') pronounced with a trill or rapid vibration of the tongue.

Examples

Her speech was barren of southernisms; she was one of Eliot Sparling's neutralized minorities, adopting the rolling R's and constricted vowels of Los Angeles.

Synset Examples

she used rolling r's as in Spanish

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trilled - pronounced with a rapid vibration of the tongue
