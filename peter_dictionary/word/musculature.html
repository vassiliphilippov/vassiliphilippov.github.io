musculature

1. NOUN

/ ˈmʌs.kjəˌleɪ.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.lə.tʃə / (BrE)
the system or arrangement of muscles in a body or part of a body

Examples

By Nov. 8, 1958, weakness, specifically involving the pelvic and thigh musculature, was pronounced, and a common complaint was "difficulty in stepping up on to curbs ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: muscular system - the bodily system composed of muscles

Etymology

From French 'musculature', from Latin 'musculus' (little mouse, muscle).
