monaural

1. ADJ

/ məˈnɔrəl / (AmE), / məˈnɔːrəl / (BrE)
(of sound or hearing) involving or using only one ear or one channel of audio.

Examples

This is a characteristic of good stereo recording and one of its tremendous advantages over monaural sound.

Synset Examples

monaural deafness

Related Terms

  • Antonym: binaural - relating to or involving both ears

Etymology

From Latin 'mon-' (one) + 'auris' (ear).
