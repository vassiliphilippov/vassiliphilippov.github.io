peoples

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiːpəlz / (AmE), / ˈpiːpəlz / (BrE)
Distinct groups of people belonging to a specific nation, community, or ethnic group.

Examples

Our people with the New York detectives said he was half dead, scared stiff and talking out loud that you'’d turned on him.

Synset Examples

the indigenous peoples of Australia

Related Terms

  • Synonym: people - the members of a community, nation, or group considered collectively
