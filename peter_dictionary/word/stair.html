stair

1. NOUN

/ stɛr / (AmE), / stɛə / (BrE)
A single step in a staircase or stairway, providing a place to rest the foot while going up or down.

Examples

"If you find it necessary, Sam, go ahead ", she said, turning on the stair.

Synset Examples

he paused on the bottom step

Related Terms

  • Synonym: step - a flat surface for placing the foot, especially in a stairway

Etymology

From Old French 'estroi' (narrow passage), from Latin 'scala' (ladder, staircase).
