urinary

1. ADJ

/ ˈjʊrəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to urine, or the organs, processes, or conditions involved in the production, secretion, or passage of urine

Examples

The terminal hospital admission on June 27, 1959, was necessitated by continued weakness and debility complicated by urinary retention and painful thrombosed hemorrhoids.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: renal - relating to the kidneys

Etymology

From Latin urina (urine) + -ary (pertaining to).
