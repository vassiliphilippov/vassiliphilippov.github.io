Land Rover

1. PROPN

/ lænd ˈroʊ.vɚ / (AmE), / lænd ˈrəʊ.və / (BrE)
A make of four-wheel-drive vehicle designed for traveling over rough ground, originally manufactured by the British company Rover.

Examples

And so, after a flight southeast to Savannakhet, we found ourselves bouncing along in a Jeep right behind the Land-Rover of Prince Boun Oum of Champassak, a tall man of Churchillian mien in a bush jacket and a ten-gallon hat from Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jeep - a small, sturdy motor vehicle used for traveling over rough ground

Etymology

From the British car manufacturer Land Rover, originally a division of Rover Company.
