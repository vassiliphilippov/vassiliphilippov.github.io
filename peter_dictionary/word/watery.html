watery

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɑːtəri / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəri / (BrE)
Containing or saturated with water; having a high water content.

Examples

He poured the water off the sourdough and off the flour, salvaging the chunky, watery messes for biscuits of a sort.

Synset Examples

watery soil

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: moist - slightly wet or damp
  • Degree Higher: soggy - excessively wet and soft

Etymology

From Middle English wateri, from water + -y.
