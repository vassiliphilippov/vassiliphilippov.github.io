awaited

1. ADJ

/ əˈweɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈweɪtɪd / (BrE)
expected or looked forward to, often with anticipation or hope.

Examples

The occasion for this marathon: Angel's long awaited reissue in its "Great Recordings of the Century "series of the Schnabel-Pro Arte version.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anticipated - regarded as probable; expected or predicted

Etymology

Past participle of 'await', from Old French 'awaiter', from a- (to) + 'waiter' (to watch).
