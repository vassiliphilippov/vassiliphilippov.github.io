posy

1. NOUN

/ ˈpoʊzi / (AmE), / ˈpəʊzi / (BrE)
a small bunch of flowers, typically given as a gift

Examples

Have a little... ...whiff of my posy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bouquet - an arrangement of flowers
  • Synonym: nosegay - a small bunch of flowers, often carried as an accessory

Etymology

Probably from Middle English *poesie* (a short motto or inscription), meaning originally a verse or line accompanying a bunch of flowers.
