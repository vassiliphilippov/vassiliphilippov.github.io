rapist

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪ.pɪst / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.pɪst / (BrE)
a person who forces another to have sexual intercourse without their consent

Examples

Since the hero, a sterling and upright fellow, is a rich Brown senior, while two Yalies are cast as virtual rapists, I suppose I should disqualify myself from sitting in judgment on "Where the Boys are ", but I shall do nothing of the sort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perpetrator - a person who carries out a harmful, illegal, or immoral act
  • Antonym: victim - a person who is harmed as a result of a crime, accident, or other event or action

Etymology

From 'rape' (to force sex upon) + '-ist' (person who practices or is concerned with something).
