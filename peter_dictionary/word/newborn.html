2. ADJ

/ ˈnuˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːˌbɔːn / (BrE)
(of something) having just or recently arisen or come into existence; newly created.

Examples

In Blanche's defense, it must be said she was unaware of the newborn hope.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: new - recent, not existing before
  • Synonym: emergent - just coming into existence

Etymology

From Middle English newe borne, from new + born.
