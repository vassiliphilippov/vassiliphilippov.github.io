merging

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɜrdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or act of joining two or more things together to form one

Examples

A mist was walking on the water, white as cotton, but with a blending and merging grace.

Synset Examples

the merging of the two groups occurred quickly
there was no meeting of minds

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blend - to mix together so as to become indistinguishable
  • Synonym: fusion - the process or result of joining two or more things together to form a single entity
  • Antonym: separation - the action or state of moving or being moved apart

Etymology

From the verb 'merge', from Latin 'mergere' (to dip, plunge).
