bathing

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of immersing the body in water or exposing it to sunshine, usually for cleansing, recreation, or health purposes

Examples

This follows naturally since frontage on an ocean, stream, or lake provides scenic values and opportunities for the very popular recreation activities of bathing, fishing, boating, and other water sports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimming - the sport or activity of moving through water by using the limbs
  • Synonym: sunbathing - exposing the body to sunlight for pleasure or health

2. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of washing yourself or another person, especially by immersion in water or by using water and soap.

Examples

Finally, he was cooking, washing dishes, bathing the children, and even ironing - and still his wife refused to have relations as often as he desired them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: washing - the act of cleaning something with water and, typically, soap or detergent

Etymology

From Middle English 'bathen', derived from 'bath'.
