response

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
the way in which someone reacts to something that has happened or has been said, especially in terms of attitude or reception

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reaction - an action performed or a feeling experienced in response to a situation or event
  • Synonym: reception - the way in which something, such as an idea, proposal, or person, is received

Etymology

From Latin responsum (an answer), from respondēre (to answer).
