rump

1. NOUN

/ rʌmp / (AmE), / rʌmp / (BrE)
the hind part of an animal's body, especially the area around the base of the tail

Examples

Reaching across the side of the stall, he slapped the buckskin on the rump.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hindquarters - the back part of an animal, including the hips and hind legs

Etymology

From Middle English 'rump(e)', probably from Old Norse 'rumpr' (backside, rump).
