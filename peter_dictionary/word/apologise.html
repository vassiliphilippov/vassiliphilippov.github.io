apologise

1. VERB

/ əˈpɑlədʒaɪz / (AmE), / əˈpɒlədʒaɪz / (BrE)
to express regret for a fault, mistake, or offense; to say sorry

Examples

I don't apologise.

Synset Examples

I apologized for being late
He apologized for the many typoes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apologize - express regret for a fault (American spelling)

Etymology

From Greek apologizesthai (to give an account), from apologia (a speech in defense).
