yesteryear

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛstərˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈjɛstəjɪə / (BrE)
The past, especially a time considered to be better or more romantic than the present; former times.

Examples

The medical device pirate of today, of course, is a far more sophisticated operator than his predecessor of yesteryear - the gallus snapping hawker of snake oil and other patent medicines.

Synset Examples

forget the past

Related Terms

  • Synonym: past - the time before the present
  • Synonym: bygone - belonging to an earlier time

Etymology

Formed in Middle English from 'yester-' (as in 'yesterday') + 'year'.
