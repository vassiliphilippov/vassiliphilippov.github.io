dealing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the way in which someone behaves or conducts themselves towards others, especially in business or social matters

Examples

Indeed, as already noted, the Court proceeded on the assumption that the executives involved in the dealings between du Pont and General Motors acted "honorably and fairly "and exercised their business judgment only to serve what they deemed the best interests of their own companies.

Synset Examples

honest dealing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conduct - the manner in which a person behaves, especially on a particular occasion or in a particular context
  • Synonym: behavior - the way in which one acts or conducts oneself

Etymology

From Middle English 'delen' (to divide, share), from Old English 'dælan'.
