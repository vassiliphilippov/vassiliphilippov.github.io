eighteenth

1. ADJ

/ ˈeɪˌtinθ / (AmE), / ˈeɪt.iːnθ / (BrE)
Coming next after the seventeenth; in position number 18 in a sequence.

Examples

As the play opens the audience is introduced to the community of Salem in Puritan America at the end of the eighteenth century.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: nineteenth - coming next after the eighteenth in position

Etymology

From 'eight' + '-teenth', formed on the pattern of teen numerals.
