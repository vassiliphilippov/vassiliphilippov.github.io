pertinence

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝː.tən.əns / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.tɪ.nəns / (BrE)
the quality of being relevant or applicable to the matter at hand

Examples

Religion seeks to satisfy human needs of great pertinence.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relevance - the quality of being closely connected or appropriate
  • Antonym: irrelevance - the lack of connection or applicability

Etymology

From Latin 'pertinentia' (relevance, connection).
