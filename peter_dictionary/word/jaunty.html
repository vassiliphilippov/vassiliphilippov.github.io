jaunty

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɑnti / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːnti / (BrE)
Having a lively, cheerful, and self-confident manner, often reflected in one’s appearance or style.

Examples

He, too, cocked his cap at a jaunty angle, jingled marbles in his pocket, and swaggered down Main Street.

Synset Examples

a dapper young man
a jaunty red hat

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dapper - neat and trim in dress or appearance
  • Synonym: smart - well-dressed and stylish
  • Antonym: slovenly - untidy and messy in appearance or habits

Etymology

From French *gentil* (nice, elegant), from Latin *gentilis* (of the same clan, noble).
