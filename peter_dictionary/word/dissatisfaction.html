dissatisfaction

1. NOUN

/ ˌdɪsˌsætɪsˈfækʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.sæt.ɪsˈfæk.ʃən / (BrE)
A feeling of unhappiness or disappointment because something is not as good as expected or desired.

Examples

This dissatisfaction led to Howsam's request that the video not be terminated before the end of the game.

Synset Examples

he was never slow to express his dissatisfaction with the service he received

Related Terms

  • Antonym: satisfaction - the pleasant feeling when desires or expectations are fulfilled

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'dis-' (expressing negation) and 'satisfaction'.
