anti-intellectual

1. ADJ

/ ˌæn.tiˌɪn.təˈlɛk.tʃu.əl / (AmE), / ˌantiˌɪn.təˈlɛk.tjʊ.əl / (BrE)
hostile or indifferent to intellectual, artistic, or cultural values and pursuits; opposed to or distrustful of intellectuals

Examples

However, the teacher can only go so far if the attitude of the community and the family is anti-intellectual.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intellectual - relating to the intellect or intellectuals

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'anti-' (against) and 'intellectual' (from Latin 'intellectus', understanding).
