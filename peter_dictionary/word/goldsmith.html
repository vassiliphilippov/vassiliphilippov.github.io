goldsmith

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡoʊldˌsmɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌsmɪθ / (BrE)
a person who makes jewelry and other objects out of gold

Examples

He was an artisan, a man who studied his trade and developed his craftsmanship the way a goldsmith or a wood carver did.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: silversmith - a person who makes objects out of silver

Etymology

From Old English goldsmið, from gold (gold) + smith (craftsman).
