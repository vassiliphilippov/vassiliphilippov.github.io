wherefore

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛrˌfɔr / (AmE), / ˈweəˌfɔː / (BrE)
the reason or explanation for something; the cause, especially in the phrase 'the whys and wherefores' meaning all the reasons for something

Examples

What's he in here **for**, anyway?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: why - the reason or explanation for something

Etymology

From Middle English, from 'where' + 'fore' (meaning 'for what reason').
