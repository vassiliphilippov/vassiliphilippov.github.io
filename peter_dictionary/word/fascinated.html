fascinated

1. ADJ

/ ˈfæsəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
very interested or attracted by something, so that you give it all your attention

Examples

Certain this menace was only imaginary, he yet stared in fascinated horror, his hand sticky against the stock of his weapon.
I'm intrigued by Alex's "giant bat" stories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intrigued - very interested or curious
  • Antonym: bored - uninterested, feeling weary due to lack of interest

Etymology

Past participle of 'fascinate,' from Latin 'fascinatus' (bewitched).
