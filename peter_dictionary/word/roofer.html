roofer

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːfər / (AmE), / ˈruːfə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to lay, repair, or maintain roofs.

Examples

Ludie chewed roofer's tar.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: builder - person who constructs buildings
  • Synonym: carpenter - person who builds and repairs wooden structures

Etymology

From 'roof' + '-er', denoting one who works with roofs.
