regenerating

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of cells, tissues, or organisms) undergoing renewal, restoration, or growth after loss or damage.

Examples

These were thought to represent regenerating fibers.

Synset Examples

regenerating fibers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: regenerative - having the ability to regenerate or promote regeneration

Etymology

From Latin 'regenerare' (to produce again, be renewed).
