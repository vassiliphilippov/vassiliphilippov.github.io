lookup

1. NOUN

/ ˈlʊkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈlʊkˌʌp / (BrE)
an act or process of retrieving information from a list, database, or system, typically to find out whether a particular item is present or to obtain related information

Related Terms

Etymology
