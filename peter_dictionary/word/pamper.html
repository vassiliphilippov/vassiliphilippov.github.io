pamper

1. VERB

/ ˈpæmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈpæmpə / (BrE)
to treat someone with excessive care or indulgence, often making them comfortable or giving them special privileges

Examples

But I would not pamper myself in that silly way.

Synset Examples

grandparents often pamper the children
Let's not mollycoddle our students!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spoil - to harm the character of someone, especially a child, by giving them everything they want
  • Synonym: indulge - to allow oneself or someone else to have something enjoyable
