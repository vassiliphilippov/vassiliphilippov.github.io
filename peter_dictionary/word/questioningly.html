questioningly

1. ADV

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃən.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows curiosity or a desire to know more, typically by asking questions or expressing doubt.

Examples

He bellowed orders and watched the alert response of some of his men and watched, too, the way a dozen or more turned their heads questioningly toward the shackled figure as though for further instruction.

Synset Examples

they turned their heads questioningly, as if awaiting further instructions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curiously - in a way that shows a desire to know or learn
