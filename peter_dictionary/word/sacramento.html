Sacramento

1. PROPN

/ ˌsækrəˈmɛntoʊ / (AmE), / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəʊ / (BrE)
the capital city of California, located in north central California on the Sacramento River

Examples

In the Sacramento valley in California, for instance, it has been observed that there was not one day's difference between the emergence of the andrenas and the opening of the willow catkins.

Etymology

From Spanish 'sacramento' meaning 'sacrament'; the city was named after the Sacramento River, which was so named by Spanish explorer Gabriel Moraga.
