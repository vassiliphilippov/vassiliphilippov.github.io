dry-eyed

1. ADJ

/ ˈdraɪˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdraɪˌaɪd / (BrE)
Not showing tears, especially in situations where others might cry.

Examples

She would weep in private, he was sure, for she loved him in her frigid way, though in public she would be dry eyed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tearless - not shedding or marked by tears
