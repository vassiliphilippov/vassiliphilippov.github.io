overprotective

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəprəˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
excessively protective of someone, especially by limiting their independence or exposure to risk

Examples

However, a growing intellectual sophistication and the new certitudes imparted by courses in psychology and anthropology make the students increasingly critical of their somewhat provincial and overprotective parents.

Synset Examples

overprotective parents

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: protective - tending to protect someone or something

Etymology

Formed from 'over-' (excessive) + 'protective'.
