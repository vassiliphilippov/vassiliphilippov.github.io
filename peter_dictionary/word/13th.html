13th

1. ADJ

/ ˈθɜrˈtiːnθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
coming after the twelfth in order; being number thirteen in a sequence

Examples

Barber, who is in his 13th year as a legislator, said there "are some members of our congressional delegation in Washington who would like to see it (the resolution) passed ".
On November 13, 1952, he was classified in Class 1, - A.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 12th - coming after the eleventh in order
  • Degree Higher: 14th - coming after the thirteenth in order

Etymology

From Old English þreotiene (thirteen) with ordinal suffix '-th'.
