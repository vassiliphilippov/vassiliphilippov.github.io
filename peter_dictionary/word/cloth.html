cloth

1. NOUN

/ klɔθ / (AmE), / klɒθ / (BrE)
A material made by weaving, knitting, felting, or crocheting natural or synthetic fibers, used typically for making clothes or furnishings.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fabric - material made by weaving, knitting, or bonding fibers
  • Synonym: textile - any type of woven or knitted fabric

Etymology

From Old English *clāth* (cloth, garment), related to Old High German *kleid* (garment) and Dutch *kleed*.
