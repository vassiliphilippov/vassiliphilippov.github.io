lab

1. NOUN

/ læb / (AmE), / læb / (BrE)
a room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching

Examples

The lab to check.
The lab: Sorry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: laboratory - a room or building for scientific experiments and research

Etymology

Shortened form of 'laboratory', from Medieval Latin 'laboratorium' (a place for labor or work).
