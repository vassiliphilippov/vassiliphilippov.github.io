processor

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑsɛsər / (AmE), / ˈprɒsɛsə / (BrE)
a company or person that processes raw agricultural products and prepares them for sale or further use

Examples

The drug's chemical name is listed, since most states require feed processors to use this name instead of the trade name on the feed tag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manufacturer - a company that makes goods from raw materials

Etymology

From process + -or, denoting an agent that processes.
