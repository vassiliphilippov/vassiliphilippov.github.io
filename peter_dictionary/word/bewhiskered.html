bewhiskered

1. ADJ

/ bɪˈwɪskɚd / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪskəd / (BrE)
Having hair, whiskers, or a beard on the cheeks and chin.

Examples

It had the features of a man bewhiskered by clumps of loose feathers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bearded - having a beard or facial hair on the chin and cheeks
  • Antonym: clean-shaven - having no hair on the face, especially the chin and cheeks

Etymology

Formed from 'be-' (intensifying prefix) + 'whisker' (hair on the face).
