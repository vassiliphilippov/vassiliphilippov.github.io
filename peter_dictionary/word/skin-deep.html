skin-deep

1. ADJ

/ skɪn dip / (AmE), / skɪn diːp / (BrE)
(of feelings, qualities, or appearances) not very deep or sincere; superficial.

Examples

My smile is just skin deep.

Synset Examples

her beauty is only skin-deep

Related Terms

  • Synonym: superficial - existing or occurring at or on the surface; not deep or thorough

Etymology

Formed from 'skin' + 'deep', originally used to signify the literal depth of an injury or feature, later used metaphorically for superficiality.
