centenary

1. ADJ

/ sɛnˈtɛˌnɛri / (AmE), / sɛnˈtiːnəri / (BrE)
Relating to a period of 100 years or to a centenary celebration or anniversary.

Examples

The London Universe devoted its centenary issue last December 8 to mapping out various aspects of Catholic progress during the last one hundred years.

Synset Examples

centennial celebration

Related Terms

  • Synonym: centennial - relating to a hundredth anniversary

Etymology

From Latin 'centenarius' (containing a hundred), from 'centum' (hundred).
