isothermal

1. ADJ

/ ˌaɪsəˈθɜrməl / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəʊˈθɜːməl / (BrE)
(of a process or change) occurring at a constant temperature.

Examples

The energy for any isothermal work done by the perfect gas must come as thermal energy from its surroundings.

Etymology

From Greek iso- (equal) + thermos (heat).
