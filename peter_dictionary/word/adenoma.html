adenoma

1. NOUN

/ ˌæd.əˈnoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˌad.ɪˈnəʊ.mə / (BrE)
A benign tumor formed from glandular tissue.

Examples

In addition, there were 2 small papillary adenomas in the right kidney.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: carcinoma - A malignant tumor of epithelial origin
  • Synonym: tumor - An abnormal growth of tissue

Etymology

From Greek adēn (gland) + -oma (tumor, swelling).
