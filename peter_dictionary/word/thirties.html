thirties

1. NOUN

/ ˈθɝːtiz / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiz / (BrE)
The period in a person's life between the ages of 30 and 40.

Examples

He disliked Garth on sight, conservative clothes and haircut, smile a shade too earnestly boyish for a man who must be well into his thirties, handclasp too consciously quick and firm.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twenties - the period of life between ages 20 and 30
  • Degree Higher: forties - the period of life between ages 40 and 50
