telling

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
revealing information, especially in a way that is unintended or indirect; showing the true nature of something

Examples

Only within the framework of a mature relationship characterized by honest appraisals of performance can we provide telling assistance.

Synset Examples

a telling smile
a telltale panel of lights
a telltale patch of oil on the water marked where the boat went down

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revealing - making something known that was hidden or secret
  • Synonym: telltale - revealing or indicating something, especially in an unintended way
