rangy

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪndʒi / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒi / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) tall and slim, often with long, slender limbs.

Examples

The rangy, Albany (Cal.) native, a surprise slugging sensation for the Flock last year as well as a defensive whiz, set "improved fielding "as his 1961 goal.

Synset Examples

a gangling teenager
a lanky kid transformed almost overnight into a handsome young man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lanky - tall, thin, and ungraceful
  • Synonym: gangly - awkwardly tall and thin

Etymology

From 'range' + '-y', probably from the notion of 'moving over a wide area' and applied to body shape.
