homeward

1. ADV

/ ˈhoʊmwɚd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmwəd / (BrE)
Towards home; in the direction of one's home.

Examples

Others added that Juet had wanted to turn the ship homeward.

Synset Examples

fought his way homeward through the deep snow

Related Terms

  • Antonym: outward - away from home; in the direction away from home or the starting point

Etymology

From Old English 'hāmweard', from 'hām' (home) + '-weard' (toward).
