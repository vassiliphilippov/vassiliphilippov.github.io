self-conscious

1. ADJ

/ sɛlfˈkɑnʃəs / (AmE), / sɛlfˈkɒnʃəs / (BrE)
nervous or uncomfortable because you are worried about what people think about you or are acutely aware of yourself

Examples

All his emotions had been inward, self-conscious.

Synset Examples

self-conscious awareness
self-conscious about their roles as guardians of the social values

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confident - not shy or self-conscious; sure of oneself

Etymology

Formed from 'self' + 'conscious' (from Latin 'conscius', knowing with others or oneself).
