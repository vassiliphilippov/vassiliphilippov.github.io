happening

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhapənɪŋ / (BrE)
An event or occurrence that takes place; something that happens.

Examples

There was some idle talk, a listless discussion of this or that small happening during the day's drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: event - something that happens, especially something important
  • Synonym: incident - an individual occurrence or event

Etymology

From the verb 'happen' + '-ing'.
