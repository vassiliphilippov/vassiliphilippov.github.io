dazzling

1. ADJ

/ ˈdæzlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdæzlɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely impressive, beautiful, or skillful; so bright or splendid as to amaze or overwhelm.

Examples

So we had some dazzling and memorable Milstein, but not great Brahms.
This discovery of Melies was vastly more important than his sometimes dazzling, magician's tricks produced on film.

Synset Examples

the skater's dazzling virtuosic leaps
these great best canvases still look as astonishing and as invitingly new as they did...when...his fulgurant popularity was in full growth
adventures related...in a style both vivid and fulgurous

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brilliant - very bright or impressive
  • Synonym: blinding - extremely bright and difficult to look at
  • Antonym: average - not outstanding or impressive
  • Synonym: glittering - shining with flashing light; impressive
  • Synonym: stunning - extremely impressive or attractive

Etymology

From the verb 'dazzle' + -ing.
