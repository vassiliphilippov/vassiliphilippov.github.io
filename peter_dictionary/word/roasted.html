roasted

1. ADJ

/ ˈroʊ.stɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.stɪd / (BrE)
(of meat or other food) cooked by dry heat in an oven or over an open fire.

Examples

Still another, annoyed by the brevity of a recently received missive, wrote: "Yore letter was short and sweet, jist like a roasted maget ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grilled - cooked over direct heat, often on a grill
  • Synonym: baked - cooked by dry heat without direct exposure to flame, usually in an oven
  • Antonym: steamed - cooked with the use of steam
  • Antonym: boiled - cooked in boiling water
