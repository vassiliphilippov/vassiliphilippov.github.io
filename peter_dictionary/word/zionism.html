Zionism

1. NOUN

/ ˈzaɪ.ə.nɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈzaɪ.ə.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
A movement for the re-establishment, development, and protection of a Jewish nation in what is now Israel.

Examples

This was how Alexander Brandel expressed pure Zionism.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: anti-Zionism - opposition to the principles and goals of Zionism

Etymology

From Zion, a biblical term for Jerusalem, + -ism.
