hub

1. NOUN

/ hʌb / (AmE), / hʌb / (BrE)
the central part of a wheel, fan, or propeller through which the shaft or axle passes

Examples

A wheel squeaked on a hub, was still, and squeaked again.
The water level was higher than their hubs.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from dialectal hubbe (protuberance).
