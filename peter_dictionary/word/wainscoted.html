wainscoted

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪnskətɪd / (AmE), / ˈweɪnskətɪd / (BrE)
(of a wall or surface) fitted or decorated with wooden panels or wainscoting.

Examples

He pulled with all his strength at the heavy, brass bound door, and shuffled along the wainscoted wall.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: paneled - covered or decorated with panels

Etymology

From 'wainscot', referring to wooden paneling, + English -ed (past participle/comparative suffix).
