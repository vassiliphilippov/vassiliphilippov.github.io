misrelated

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɪsrɪˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
incorrectly connected or related; wrongly establishing a relationship between things, especially grammatical elements or ideas.

Examples

Classified ads are also chockfull of misrelated constructions.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unrelated - having no connection or relationship
  • Antonym: related - connected or having a relationship

Etymology

From mis- (wrongly) + related, from Latin relatus (brought back, related).
