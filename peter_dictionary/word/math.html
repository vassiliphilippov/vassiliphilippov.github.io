math

1. NOUN

/ mæθ / (AmE), / mæθ / (BrE)
The study of numbers, shapes, and patterns; mathematics.

Examples

"A person with a master's degree in physics, chemistry, math or English, yet who has not taken Education courses, is not permitted to teach in the public schools ", said Grover.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mathematics - The study of numbers, quantities, and shapes
  • Synonym: maths - British English term for mathematics

Etymology

Short form of 'mathematics'; chiefly American English.
