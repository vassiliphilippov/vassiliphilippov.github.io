singer

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋə / (BrE)
a person who sings, especially professionally or as a performer

Examples

He may respect too much the Italian tradition of letting singers hold on to their notes, but to restrain them in a singers' opera may be quite difficult.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vocalist - a person who sings, especially in jazz or popular music
  • Synonym: musician - a person who plays a musical instrument or is musically talented

Etymology

From Middle English 'singere', from Old English 'singer', from 'singan' (to sing).
