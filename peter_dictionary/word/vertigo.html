vertigo

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɜrtɪˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
A sensation of spinning or whirling, often causing a feeling that you or your surroundings are moving or falling.

Examples

He fought the panic of vertigo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dizziness - a sensation of lightheadedness or unsteadiness

Etymology

From Latin 'vertigo' (a turning, dizziness), from 'vertere' (to turn).
