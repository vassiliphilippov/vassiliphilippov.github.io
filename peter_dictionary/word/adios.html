adios

1. NOUN

/ ɑːˈdi.oʊs / (AmE), / əˈdiːɒs / (BrE)
(informal) Used to express farewell or goodbye.

Examples

Adios ", I said, exhausting my Spanish vocabulary on my host and exchanging one of a scarcely tapped store of smiles with my host's daughters.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - an expression of farewell
  • Synonym: adieu - a farewell remark, especially in French or formal contexts
  • Antonym: hello - an expression used to greet someone

Etymology

From Spanish 'adiós' meaning 'to God' or 'goodbye'.
