Hampshire

1. PROPN

/ ˈhæmpʃər / (AmE), / ˈhæmpʃə / (BrE)
A county in southern England on the English Channel, known for cities such as Winchester and Southampton.

Examples

Go back to New Hampshire, and I'll call you at your parents' house.

Etymology

Named after the city of Southampton; from Old English 'Hamtunscir' (the shire of Hamtun, i.e. Southampton).
