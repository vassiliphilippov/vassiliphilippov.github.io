excelsior

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˈsɛl.si.ɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛl.si.ə / (BrE)
Thin, curly wood shavings used for packing, stuffing, or bedding.

Examples

It is brought to packing houses, cleaned and graded as to size and quality, and packed in protective excelsior.

Etymology

From Latin 'excelsior' meaning 'higher' or 'loftier', used as a trade name for this product.
