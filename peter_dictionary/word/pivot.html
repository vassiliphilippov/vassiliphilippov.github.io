pivot

1. VERB

/ ˈpɪvət / (AmE), / ˈpɪvət / (BrE)
to turn or rotate on or as if on a fixed point

Examples

His hands were swinging at his sides, and he passed through the dingy market place with his back straight and, pivoting on his heel, he entered an old stone building.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rotate - to turn around a central point or axis
  • Synonym: twist - to turn something in a circular motion

Etymology

From French 'pivot' (central point, pin), of uncertain origin.
