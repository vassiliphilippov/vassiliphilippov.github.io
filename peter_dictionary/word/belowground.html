belowground

1. ADJ

/ bəˈloʊˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / bɪˈləʊˌɡraʊnd / (BrE)
Situated or occurring under the surface of the ground.

Examples

The basement or belowground shelters also will serve for tornado or hurricane protection.
A belowground basement can cut the fallout radiation to one tenth of the outside level.

Synset Examples

belowground storage areas
underground caverns

Related Terms

  • Synonym: underground - situated, occurring, or operating below the surface of the earth

Etymology

Formed from 'below' and 'ground'.
