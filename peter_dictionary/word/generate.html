generate

1. VERB

/ ˈdʒɛn.əˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.əˌreɪt / (BrE)
(formal) To produce offspring; to beget or father children.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beget - to father or produce offspring
  • Synonym: father - to become the male parent of a child

Etymology

From Latin generare (to beget, produce), from genus (race, kind).
