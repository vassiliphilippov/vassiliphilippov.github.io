bargain-priced

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɑrɡən ˌpraɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡɪn ˌpraɪst / (BrE)
Sold at a price lower than the usual or standard price; inexpensive or discounted.

Examples

Telefunken has accorded him beautiful sound, and this bargain-priced disc (it sells for $ 2.98) is worthy of consideration.

Synset Examples

buying bargain-priced clothes for the children
cut-rate goods

Related Terms

  • Synonym: discounted - sold at a reduced price
  • Synonym: cut-rate - offered at a reduced price
  • Antonym: full-priced - sold at the regular price

Etymology

Formed from 'bargain' (from Old French 'bargaigner', to haggle) and 'priced'.
