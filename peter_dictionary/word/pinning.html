pinning

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
(historical, US college) A mutual promise between a couple not to date anyone else, traditionally marked by the giving of a fraternity pin.

Examples

There are boxed proclamations in the newspaper of watchings, pinnings, ringings, engagements, and marriages in a scrupulously graded hierarchy of felicity.
