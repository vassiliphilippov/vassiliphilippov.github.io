boyish

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɔɪ.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.ɪʃ / (BrE)
Having qualities or characteristics typical of a young boy, such as innocence, playfulness, or charm.

Examples

He disliked Garth on sight, conservative clothes and haircut, smile a shade too earnestly boyish for a man who must be well into his thirties, handclasp too consciously quick and firm.

Synset Examples

a boyish grin
schoolboyish pranks

Related Terms

  • Antonym: girlish - having qualities typical of a young girl

Etymology

From "boy" + "-ish", the latter being a suffix forming adjectives meaning 'like' or 'characteristic of'.
