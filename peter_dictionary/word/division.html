division

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / dɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a separate part of a large organization, such as a government department or company, that has its own particular responsibilities or work

Examples

The owners would like each club in the fourteen team league to play a home and home series with teams in its division, plus two games against teams in the other division.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: section - a subdivision within an organization
