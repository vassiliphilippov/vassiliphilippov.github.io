tiled

1. ADJ

/ taɪld / (AmE), / taɪld / (BrE)
Covered or furnished with tiles, especially relating to surfaces such as floors or walls.

Examples

Virginia treated him with attention and tried to tempt his appetite with special food: biscuits, cookies, candies - the result of devoted hours in the tiled kitchen.

Synset Examples

baths with tiled walls
a tiled kitchen

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untiled - not covered with tiles

Etymology

From the noun 'tile' + -ed suffix forming the adjective.
