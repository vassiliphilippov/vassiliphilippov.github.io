Related Terms

  • Synonym: editorial - an article expressing the opinion of the editor or publisher

  • Antonym: news story - an article presenting factual news rather than opinion

Etymology

From Latin columna (pillar, post); applied to regular articles in journalism from the arrangement of text in vertical blocks.
