gunplay

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌnˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌpleɪ / (BrE)
violent conflict involving the use of firearms, typically with the intent to kill or frighten

Examples

There was to be no gunplay.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunfight - a fight with firearms between two or more people

Etymology

From 'gun' + 'play', referring metaphorically to the use of guns in action.
