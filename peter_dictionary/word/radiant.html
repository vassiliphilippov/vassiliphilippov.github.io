radiant

1. ADJ

/ ˈreɪdiənt / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiənt / (BrE)
emitting or seeming to emit light; shining or glowing brightly

Examples

Yet no detail was too small to receive attention from this master, and as a result the playing here has humor, delicacy, and radiant humanity.

Synset Examples

the beaming sun
the effulgent daffodils
a radiant sunrise
a refulgent sunset

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luminous - emitting or reflecting light, bright
  • Antonym: dim - not shining brightly or clearly

Etymology

From Latin radiāns, present participle of 'radiare' (to emit rays), from 'radius' (ray).
