incomparable

1. ADJ

/ ɪnˈkɑmpərəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒmpərəbəl / (BrE)
too unusual, excellent, or unique to be compared with anything else; impossible to compare because of being so different or excellent.

Examples

That this is love beyond compare?

Synset Examples

an incomparable week of rest and pleasure
the computer proceeds with its incomparable logic and efficiency
this report is incomparable with the earlier ones because of different breakdowns of the data

Related Terms

  • Synonym: matchless - without equal; unable to be matched
  • Synonym: unparalleled - having no equal; exceptional

Etymology

From Late Latin incomparabilis, from Latin in- (not) + comparabilis (comparable).
