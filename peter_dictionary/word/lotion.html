lotion

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈləʊʃən / (BrE)
A thick, smooth liquid preparation applied to the skin for cosmetic or medicinal purposes, often to moisturize, soften, or protect.

Examples

The place smelt of some kind of hair lotion these pimp like characters use.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cream - a thick, smooth cosmetic preparation for the skin
  • Synonym: ointment - a smooth, oily substance for application to the skin

Etymology

From Latin 'lotio' (a washing), from 'lavare' (to wash).
