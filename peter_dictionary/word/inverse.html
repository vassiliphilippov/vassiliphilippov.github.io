inverse

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪnˌvɝs / (AmE), / ˈɪn.vɜːs / (BrE)
reversed in position, direction, order, or effect; opposite to what is usual or expected

Examples

We shall consider these in the inverse order of their presentation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reverse - opposite in direction or order
  • Antonym: direct - in the usual or straightforward order or effect

Etymology

From Latin 'inversus' (turned upside down, inverted), past participle of 'invertere'.
