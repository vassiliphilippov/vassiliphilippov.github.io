bewilderingly

1. ADV

/ bɪˈwɪldərɪŋli / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that causes confusion or puzzlement; in a confusing or perplexing manner.

Examples

These trumps were more touching than they were anything else, and seemed to imply that the nights were long, her children ungrateful, and her marriage bewilderingly threadbare.

Synset Examples

her situation was bewilderingly unclear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: confusingly - in a manner that causes confusion

Etymology

From "bewildering" + "-ly", with "bewilder" from be- (thoroughly) + "wilder" (lead astray).
