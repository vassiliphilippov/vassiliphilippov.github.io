piety

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaɪ.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.ə.ti / (BrE)
The quality of being religious or reverent; devotion to religious duties and practices.

Examples

When I pressed for a purely religious definition, I encountered the familiar blend of liberal piety, interfaith good will, and a small residue of ethnic loyalty.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: devotion - deep religious feeling or commitment
  • Antonym: impiety - lack of piety or reverence, especially for a god

Etymology

From Latin pietas (devoutness, dutifulness).
