semiskilled

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛmiˈskɪld / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈskɪld / (BrE)
Having or requiring only a limited amount of training or skill.

Examples

The major training need of a new industrial plant is a short period of pre employment training for a large number of semi-skilled machine operators.

Synset Examples

semiskilled dockworkers
a semiskilled job

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: unskilled - having no particular skills or training
  • Degree Higher: skilled - having special skill or training

Etymology

Formed from "semi-" (partly) and "skilled"; first attested in the early 20th century.
