starlight

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌlaɪt / (BrE)
The light emitted by stars, especially as seen from Earth at night.

Examples

In the starlight he could see the trees stripped of their leaves.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moonlight - the light reflected from the moon

Etymology

Formed from 'star' + 'light' in Middle English.
