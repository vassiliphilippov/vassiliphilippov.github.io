Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: term - a fixed period of time during which classes are held at a school or university
  • Synonym: semester - half of an academic year, typically lasting about six months
  • Synonym: quarter - one of four divisions of the academic year

Etymology

From Latin 'sessio' (the act of sitting), from 'sedere' (to sit), originally referring to a sitting or assembly.
