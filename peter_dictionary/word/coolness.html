coolness

1. NOUN

/ ˈkuːlnəs / (AmE), / ˈkuːlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being moderately cold or slightly chilly.

Examples

The Old Man's son threw himself down, belly first, upon a concrete step, taking in the coolness of it, and dreaming of the day he would be rich.

Synset Examples

the chilliness of early morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chilliness - the quality of being noticeably cold
  • Antonym: warmth - the quality or state of being warm

Etymology

From 'cool' + '-ness', indicating the state or quality of being cool.
