daybed

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌbɛd / (BrE)
A long, armless couch that can be used as a seat during the day and as a bed at night.

Examples

The daybed Eugene had slept in, made up now with its dark-brown velours cover and pillows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sofa - A long, upholstered seat with a back and arms, for two or more people
  • Synonym: couch - A long upholstered piece of furniture for seating
  • Synonym: futon - A padded mattress, often used as a bed or couch

Etymology

Formed from 'day' + 'bed', indicating a bed that may be used during the day as a couch.
