re-create

1. VERB

/ ˌriˈkreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkreɪt / (BrE)
to create something again or anew; to produce a copy, version, or simulation of something that existed before

Examples

Depicted, Cubist flatness is now almost completely assimilated to the literal, undepicted kind, but at the same time it reacts upon and largely transforms the undepicted kind - and it does so, moreover, without depriving the latter of its literalness; rather, it underpins and reinforces that literalness, re-creates it.

Synset Examples

Re-create the boom of the West on a small scale

Related Terms

  • Synonym: create - to bring something into existence

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 're-' (again) and 'create' from Latin 'creare' (to make, bring forth).
