isle

1. NOUN

/ aɪl / (AmE), / aɪl / (BrE)
a small island, especially one used in a poetic or literary context

Examples

The music arrived, taking him, its rhythm stroked him, snaked all through him, the lyrics lifted him, took him from one magic isle to another, stopping briefly at each.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: island - a piece of land surrounded by water

Etymology

From Old French *ile*, from Latin *insula* (island).
