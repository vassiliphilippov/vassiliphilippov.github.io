transmitting

1. NOUN

/ trænˈsmɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of sending or conveying a message, signal, or information from one place or person to another

Examples

Not discussed here are some military problems of modern times such as undersea warfare, where the surveillance, sending, transmitting, and receiving are all so inadequate that networks and decision making are not the bottlenecks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: transmission - the action or process of transmitting something

Etymology

From the verb 'transmit', from Latin 'transmittere' (to send across).
