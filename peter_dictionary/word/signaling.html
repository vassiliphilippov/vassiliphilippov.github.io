signaling

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪɡ.nəl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nəl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of sending a message by means of a gesture, action, or system of nonverbal cues

Examples

The need for monitoring became greater when radio was adopted for military signaling.

Synset Examples

signals from the boat suddenly stopped

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sign - a gesture or action used to convey information
  • Synonym: communication - the act of transferring information

Etymology

Formed from 'signal' + '-ing', from Latin 'signum' (mark, sign).
