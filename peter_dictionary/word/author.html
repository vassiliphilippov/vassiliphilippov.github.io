author

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔθər / (AmE), / ˈɔːθə / (BrE)
a person who writes books, stories, articles, or other texts, especially as a profession

Examples

The dialogue is sharp, witty and candid - typical "do n't eat the daisies "material - which has stamped the author throughout her books and plays, and it was obvious that the Theatre-by-the-Sea audience liked it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: writer - a person who writes as a profession or hobby
  • Synonym: novelist - an author of novels

Etymology

From Latin 'auctor' (originator, creator), from 'augere' (to increase).
