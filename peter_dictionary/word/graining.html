graining

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
A texture or pattern resembling the grain of wood, or the technique of producing such a texture in painting or finishing surfaces.

Examples

In certain other pictures, however, Braque began to paint areas in exact simulation of wood graining or marbleizing.

Etymology

From grain + -ing, referring to the grain (texture) of wood or other materials.
