check

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
an examination or inspection of something to determine its condition or quality; an appraisal

Examples

Fishing interest calls for a check of the species found, quantity and size, the season they are available, and the stocking program of the fish commission.
Check the elevation of the ground, degree and direction of slopes, drainage, rock outcrops, topsoil types and quality, as well as subsoil.
As soon as the shelter is completed a radio reception check must be made.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspection - a careful examination; inspection to assess or check quality or condition
  • Synonym: test - a procedure intended to establish the quality, performance, or reliability of something

Etymology

From Old French *eschequier* (to check at chess), later generalized to various meanings involving examination or restraint.
