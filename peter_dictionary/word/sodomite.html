sodomite

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɑdəˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒdəˌmaɪt / (BrE)
A person who engages in anal intercourse, especially a man with another man; historically used as a term of condemnation or insult.

Examples

I'll pull you out of that one-bunk Hilton and cast you down with the Sodomites.

Etymology

From Old French sodomite, from Latin Sodomita, referring to the biblical city of Sodom.
