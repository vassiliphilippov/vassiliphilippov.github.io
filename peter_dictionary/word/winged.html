winged

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɪŋd / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋd / (BrE)
having wings, or having the appearance, characteristics, or attributes of wings

Examples

Winged battle flies through the night and finds me ready.

Synset Examples

the winged feet of Mercury

Related Terms

  • Antonym: wingless - without wings

Etymology

From Old English 'gewenged', past participle of 'wengan' (to provide with wings); related to 'wing'.
