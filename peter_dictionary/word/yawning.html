yawning

1. ADJ

/ ˈjɔnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an opening or space) very wide and open, especially in a way that threatens to engulf or swallow.

Examples

Somewhere, beyond, where shadows lurked, must be the yawning pit of which Papa preached and the dreadful Lake of Fire.

Synset Examples

the yawning mine shaft
a yawning abyss

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gaping - very wide open
