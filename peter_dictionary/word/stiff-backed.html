stiff-backed

1. ADJ

/ stɪfˈbækt / (AmE), / stɪfˈbækt / (BrE)
Having a stiff or rigid back, often implying an upright or formal posture.

Examples

He sat stiff-backed in a chair that did not swivel, though it was obvious to Gun that Killpath felt his position as acting captain plainly merited a swivel chair.

Synset Examples

the guards stood stiff-backed and unsmiling

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rigid - not flexible; stiff
  • Antonym: slouching - bending the back in a relaxed manner
