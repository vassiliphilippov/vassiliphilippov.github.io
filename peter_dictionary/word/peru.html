Peru

1. PROPN

/ pəˈru / (AmE), / pəˈruː / (BrE)
A country in western South America, formerly the heart of the Inca Empire, which gained independence from Spain in 1821.

Examples

"The sweaty bastard's probably halfway to Peru with our money by now ".

Etymology

From Spanish Perú, likely from Quechua or another local Indigenous language.
