hacker

1. NOUN

/ ˈhækər / (AmE), / ˈhækə / (BrE)
(Golf, informal) A golfer who plays poorly, often with little skill or control.

Examples

The idol of the hackers, of course, is Ray Ainsley, who achieved a 19 in the United States Open.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: duffer - an unskilled or incompetent golfer
