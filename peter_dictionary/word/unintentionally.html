unintentionally

1. ADV

/ ˌʌnɪnˈtɛnʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɛnʃənəli / (BrE)
Without intending to; in an unintentional manner.

Examples

We were forbidden to swing on the gates, lest they sag on their hinges in a poor-white-trash way, but we could stand on them, when they were latched, rest our chins on the top, and stare and stare, committing to memory, quite unintentionally, all the details that lay before our eyes.

Synset Examples

she hit him unintentionally

Related Terms

  • Antonym: intentionally - with intention; deliberately

Etymology

From 'unintentional' + '-ly'; 'unintentional' from 'un-' (not) + 'intentional.'
