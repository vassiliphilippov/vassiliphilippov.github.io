primping

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɪmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of carefully or excessively grooming or arranging one's appearance

Examples

A lock on the toilet would encourage malingering and primping.

Synset Examples

the primping alone took more than an hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preening - the act of making oneself neat or smart in appearance
  • Synonym: grooming - cleaning and maintaining one's appearance

Etymology

From the verb 'primp', likely of imitative origin, first recorded in the 19th century.
