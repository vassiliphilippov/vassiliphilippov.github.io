stopover

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑpˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈstɒpˌəʊvə / (BrE)
A short stay at a place between parts of a journey, especially when travelling long distances.

Examples

Later on he had an idea to build a city out of a desert stop-over for G.I.s on the way to the West Coast.

Synset Examples

they made a stopover to visit their friends

Related Terms

  • Synonym: layover - a short stay at a location between parts of a journey, often used for scheduled breaks on airline routes

Etymology

From 'stop' + 'over', meaning to pause and stay temporarily during a journey.
