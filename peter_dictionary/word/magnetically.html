magnetically

1. ADV

/ mæɡˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
By means of or relating to magnetism; using, produced by, or affected by a magnetic field.

Examples

Since f is ferromagnetic, we felt that any results obtained from the magnetically** contaminated **f would be suspect.

Synset Examples

magnetically contaminated material

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrically - by means of electricity

Etymology

From 'magnetic' + '-ally', after Middle English 'magnetik', from Latin 'magnet-', from Greek 'magnetis (lithos)' meaning 'Magnesian stone'.
