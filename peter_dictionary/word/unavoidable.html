unavoidable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnəˈvɔɪdəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈvɔɪdəbl / (BrE)
Impossible to prevent or avoid; certain to happen regardless of any attempt to stop it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inevitable - certain to happen; unavoidable
  • Antonym: escapable - able to be avoided or escaped
  • Synonym: inescapable - impossible to escape or avoid

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'avoidable' (from avoid + -able).
