scope

1. NOUN

/ skoʊp / (AmE), / skəʊp / (BrE)
the range or extent of the area or subject that something deals with or where something has influence or control

Related Terms

  • Synonym: range - the area covered by something; the extent to which something reaches
  • Synonym: ambit - the scope, extent, or bounds of something
  • Synonym: compass - the range or scope of something
  • Antonym: limit - a point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend

Etymology

From Italian 'scopo' (aim, purpose), from Greek 'skopos' (watcher, target).
