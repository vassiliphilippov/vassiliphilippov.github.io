pardonable

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɑrdnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːdnəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be forgiven or excused; not too serious to be pardoned.

Examples

With a pardonable irony Shelley wrote to the father who had publicly disowned his daughter:

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unpardonable - not able to be forgiven or excused
  • Synonym: forgivable - able to be forgiven

Etymology

From Old French *pardonable*, from Medieval Latin *pardonabilis* (able to be forgiven).
