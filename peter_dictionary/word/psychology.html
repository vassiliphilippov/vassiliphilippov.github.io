psychology

1. NOUN

/ saɪˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / sʌɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the mind and behavior

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: psychiatry - the medical study and treatment of mental illness
  • Synonym: psychotherapy - the treatment of mental disorders by psychological means
  • Antonym: sociology - the study of the development, structure, and functioning of human society

Etymology

From Greek psukhē (soul, mind) + -logia (study, discourse).
