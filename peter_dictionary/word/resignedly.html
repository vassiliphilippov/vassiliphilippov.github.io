resignedly

1. ADV

/ rɪˈzaɪnɪdli / (AmE), / rɪˈzaɪnɪdli / (BrE)
In a resigned manner; accepting something unpleasant or inevitable without protest.

Examples

Resignedly, Macklin turned to the back door.

Synset Examples

resignedly, I telegraphed back that it was all right with me if he insisted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stoically - in a way that endures hardship without showing feelings or complaining

Etymology

From 'resigned' + '-ly', where 'resigned' derives from Latin 'resignare' (to give up, relinquish).
