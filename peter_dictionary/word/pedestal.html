pedestal

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɛdəstl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɛdɪstl̩ / (BrE)
A base or support on which a statue, column, or other object is mounted.

Examples

Here and there on work table or pedestal stood a shape with a sheet or a tarpaulin draped over it.

Synset Examples

the base of the lamp

Related Terms

  • Synonym: base - the bottom support of anything; that on which something stands

Etymology

From Italian 'piedistallo', from 'piede' (foot) and 'stallo' (stall, stand).
