immigrant

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪməɡrənt / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪɡrənt / (BrE)
a person who comes to live permanently in a country other than their own

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: emigrant - a person who leaves their own country to settle elsewhere

Etymology

From Latin 'immigrans', present participle of 'immigrare' (to go into).
