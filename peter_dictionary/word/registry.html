registry

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛdʒɪstri / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstri / (BrE)
An official list or record of names, events, or transactions, often maintained by an authority or organization.

Examples

Cows receiving drug may not be officially tested under breed registry testing programs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: register - an official record or book for keeping a list of names or items

Etymology

From Latin 'registrum', from 'regesta' (things recorded), from 'regere' (to rule, to guide).
