convertible

1. NOUN

/ kənˈvɝːtəbl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːtɪbl̩ / (BrE)
a car with a roof that can be folded down or removed

Examples

He was happy enough to take the convertible and race up the hill to the Blevins', thinking that they might give him a drink.
"I 've seen you walking your dogs on Sunday and driving by in the convertible ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: coupe - a car with a fixed roof and two doors

Etymology

From 'convert' + '-ible'; car sense dates to early 20th century.
