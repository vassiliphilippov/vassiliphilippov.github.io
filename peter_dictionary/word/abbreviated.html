abbreviated

1. ADJ

/ əˈbri.viˌeɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / əˈbriː.vi.eɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
(of clothing or style) very short; cut to a shorter length than usual.

Examples

More than a beautiful visualization of the illustrious adventures and escapades of the tragi-comic knight-errant and his squire, Sancho Panza, in seventeenth century Spain, this inevitably abbreviated rendering of the classic satire on chivalry is an affectingly warm and human exposition of character.

Synset Examples

an abbreviated swimsuit
a brief bikini

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brief - short in duration or length

Etymology

From Latin abbreviatus, past participle of abbreviare (to shorten).
