cherished

1. ADJ

/ ˈtʃɛr.ɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛ.rɪʃt / (BrE)
Greatly loved, valued, or held dear.

Examples

Of the two cherished achievements the elderly Spahn's hitless pitching probably reached the most hearts.
"How wonderful to see your cherished self.

Synset Examples

a cherished friend
children are precious
a treasured heirloom
so good to feel wanted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasured - highly valued or loved
  • Synonym: precious - of great value or affection
  • Antonym: neglected - not given proper care or attention

Etymology

Past participle of 'cherish', from Old French 'cherir' (to hold dear), from Latin 'carus' (dear).
