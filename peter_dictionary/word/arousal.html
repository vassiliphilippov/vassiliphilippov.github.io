arousal

1. NOUN

/ əˈraʊzl̩ / (AmE), / əˈraʊz(ə)l / (BrE)
the act of stimulating or awakening a feeling, emotion, or response in someone

Examples

The idea here is one of discharge but this must stand in opposition to a second view, Plato's notion of the arousal of emotion.

Synset Examples

the purpose of art is the arousal of emotions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stimulation - the action of arousing interest, enthusiasm, or excitement

Etymology

From 'arouse' + '-al', modeled after nouns such as 'arrival', first used in late 19th century.
