coal-black

1. ADJ

/ koʊlˈblæk / (AmE), / kəʊlˈblæk / (BrE)
Extremely black in color; as black as coal or jet.

Examples

In time she presents her aristocratic husband with a coal-black child.
He was handsome, with his coal-black hair and eyes, his fine chiseled features.
The metal strip they had taken off from was coal black against the green jungle around it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: jet-black - as black as the gemstone jet
  • Degree Lower: black - of the color black

Etymology

Formed from 'coal' + 'black', referring to the deep black color of coal.
