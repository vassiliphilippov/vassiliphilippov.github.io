nondriver

1. NOUN

/ ˌnɑnˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
A person who does not drive a vehicle.

Examples

The 2 possible outcomes on each trial are "driver "or "nondriver ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: driver - a person who drives a vehicle

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'non-' (meaning 'not') and 'driver'.
