greenness

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡriːnnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being lush and covered with thriving green vegetation.

Examples

What one actually remembers is its greenness.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: verdancy - the lush greenness of flourishing vegetation

Etymology

From Middle English 'grennesse', from Old English 'grēn' (green) + '-ness' (state, quality).
