puppyish

1. ADJ

/ ˈpʌpiɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpʌpiɪʃ / (BrE)
Having qualities or characteristics typical of a puppy, especially playfulness, enthusiasm, or innocence.

Examples

He felt a puppyish need for company as strong as his earlier necessity for quiet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: puppy - a young dog

Etymology

From 'puppy' + '-ish' (forming adjectives meaning 'like a puppy').
