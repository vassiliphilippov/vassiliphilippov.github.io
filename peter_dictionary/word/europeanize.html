Europeanize

1. VERB

/ jʊˈroʊpiəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiənaɪz / (BrE)
to make something adopt European customs, character, or ideas

Examples

In both respects, international law was Europeanized.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: westernize - to make conform to Western (especially Western European or North American) customs or ideas

Etymology

Formed from 'European' + '-ize'.
