St. Petersburg

1. PROPN

/ seɪnt ˈpiːt̬ərzˌbɝːɡ / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈpiːtəzˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A city in western Florida, situated on Tampa Bay; known as a popular winter tourist resort.

Examples

Ralph Houk, successor to Casey Stengel at the Yankee helm, plans to bring the entire New York squad here from St. Petersburg, including Joe Dimaggio and large crowds are anticipated for both weekend games.

Etymology

Named after Saint Petersburg in Russia, which in turn was named for Saint Peter.
