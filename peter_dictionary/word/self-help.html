self-help

1. NOUN

/ ˌsɛlfˈhɛlp / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈhɛlp / (BrE)
the practice of improving or helping yourself without the aid of others, especially by learning new skills or solving personal problems independently

Examples

"What you see are self-help projects ", John said.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-improvement - the process of making yourself better through your own efforts

Etymology

Formed from 'self-', meaning 'by oneself', and 'help'.
