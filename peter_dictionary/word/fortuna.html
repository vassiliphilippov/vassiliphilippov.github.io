Fortuna

1. PROPN

/ fɔrˈtuːnə / (AmE), / fɔːˈtjuːnə / (BrE)
In Roman mythology, the goddess of fortune and good luck; the counterpart of the Greek Tyche.

Examples

Buona fortuna.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Tyche - Greek goddess of fortune and luck

Etymology

From Latin Fortuna, meaning 'chance, luck'; derived from fors (chance, luck).
