spokesman

1. NOUN

/ ˈspoʊksˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈspəʊksmən / (BrE)
A man who speaks on behalf of a group, organization, or another person to the public or media.

Examples

At the outset, the Government's spokesman explained that counsel for the Government and for du Pont had already held preliminary discussions with a view to arriving at a relief plan that both sides could recommend to the court.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spokesperson - a person who speaks on behalf of a group or organization (gender-neutral)
  • Antonym: spokeswoman - a woman who speaks on behalf of a group or organization

Etymology

Formed from 'spokes' (as in one who speaks) + 'man'; modelled after 'man' as suffix for male roles.
