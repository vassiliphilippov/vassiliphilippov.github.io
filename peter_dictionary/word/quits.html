quits

1. ADJ

/ kwɪts / (AmE), / kwɪts / (BrE)
Even or square; having no debt or obligation remaining between two parties, especially after repayment or an exchange.

Examples

We're quits.

Synset Examples

we're now quits
finally quits with the loan

Related Terms

  • Synonym: square - even; having no outstanding debt or favor

Etymology

Probably from the plural of 'quit' in the sense of 'freed, discharged'.
