ministration

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɪn.ɪˈstreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪn.ɪˈstreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of providing care, assistance, or service, especially during a time of need or difficulty

Examples

The dawn progressed and it seemed that the doctor would never be done with his ministrations when quite abruptly something broke his revery.

Synset Examples

the contributions provided some relief for the victims

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aid - help, typically practical assistance in a time of need
  • Synonym: relief - assistance or help provided in difficult situations

Etymology

From Latin ministerium (service, office) via Old French ministration.
