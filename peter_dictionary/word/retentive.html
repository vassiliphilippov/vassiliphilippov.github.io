retentive

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈtɛntɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈtɛntɪv / (BrE)
(of a person's mind or memory) able to retain or remember information easily.

Examples

He has a wonderfully retentive memory.

Synset Examples

a retentive mind
tenacious memory

Related Terms

  • Antonym: forgetful - unable to remember things; tending to forget

Etymology

From Latin retentivus (having the power to retain), from retinere (to retain).
