unmistakably

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈmɪstəkəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɪstɪkəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is clear and impossible to mistake for anything else; without the possibility of error or doubt.

Examples

The first slanting rays of the new day cannot yet dispel all the dark shadows which lie across the Aegean world; but our evidence grows considerably in variety and shows more unmistakably some of the lines of change.
True, a Mason watercolor is unmistakably a synthesis of nature rather than a detailed inventory.

Synset Examples

this watercolor is unmistakably a synthesis of nature

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • Synonym: evidently - clearly; obviously
  • Antonym: ambiguously - in a way that is open to more than one interpretation

Etymology

From 'unmistakable' + '-ly', where 'unmistakable' means 'not able to be mistaken'.
