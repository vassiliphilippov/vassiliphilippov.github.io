mustached

1. ADJ

/ mʌˈstæʃt / (AmE), / məˈstɑːʃt / (BrE)
Having a moustache.

Examples

Andrei glanced up over the top of the paper and looked into the mustached, homely face of Sergeant Styka.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: clean-shaven - without facial hair, especially a moustache or beard
  • Degree Higher: bearded - having a beard

Etymology

Formed from 'mustache' + '-ed', describing someone who has a mustache. 'Mustache' from French 'moustache', from Italian 'mustaccio'.
