engineer

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to design, build, or maintain engines, machines, structures, or public works by applying scientific and mathematical principles

Related Terms

Etymology

From Old French *engigneor* (one who constructs military engines), from Latin *ingeniator* (contriver, deviser).
