rooting

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːtɪŋ, ˈrʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈruːtɪŋ, ˈrʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of putting forth roots, especially in plants, and beginning to grow

Examples

For my seedbed I use good garden soil with a little sand added to encourage rooting.
That encourages rooting, and the better developed the roots, the larger and more plentiful the flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: germination - the process by which a plant grows from a seed

Etymology

From the verb 'root' + -ing.
