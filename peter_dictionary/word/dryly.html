dryly

1. ADV

/ ˈdraɪli / (AmE), / ˈdraɪli / (BrE)
In a dry, laconic, or understated manner, especially when expressing something with subtle humor or irony.

Examples

"That much Latin we remember ", Gibby said dryly.
