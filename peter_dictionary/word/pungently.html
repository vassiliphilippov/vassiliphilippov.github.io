pungently

1. ADV

/ ˈpʌndʒəntli / (AmE), / ˈpʌndʒəntli / (BrE)
In a manner that is sharply expressive, biting, or having a strong, penetrating quality, especially in taste, smell, or style.

Examples

Some of the letters were pungently expressive.

Synset Examples

he wrote pungently about his contemporaries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sharply - in a very critical or incisive manner
  • Antonym: mildly - in a gentle or not sharp manner, the opposite of pungently

Etymology

From 'pungent' + '-ly'; 'pungent' from Latin pungens (pricking, sharp).
