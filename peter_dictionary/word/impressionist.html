impressionist

1. NOUN

/ ɪmˈprɛʃənɪst / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛʃənɪst / (BrE)
an artist, especially a painter, who follows the style of Impressionism, a movement characterized by capturing the effects of light and color

Examples

Still painting, still a kind of modern impressionist, beautiful canvases of mountains and farms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Impressionism - an artistic movement focusing on light, color, and immediate visual impression

Etymology

From French impressionniste, from impression (influence from Impressionism in art).

2. ADJ

/ ɪmˈprɛʃənɪst / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛʃənɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Impressionism, especially in art, music, or literature.

Examples

"Yes, it sounds great ", I said, "but suppose you do n't think of yourself as an impressionist painter "?

Synset Examples

impressionist music

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Impressionism - a style or movement in painting, music, or literature

Etymology

From French impressionniste, from impression (influence, effect).
