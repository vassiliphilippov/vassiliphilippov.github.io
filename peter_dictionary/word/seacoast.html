seacoast

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːˌkoʊst / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌkəʊst / (BrE)
The land along or bordering a sea or ocean.

Examples

Other natural features which can be of high interest are the forests, canyons, mountains, deserts, seacoast, beaches, sand dunes, waterfalls, springs, etcetera with which the area is blessed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seashore - land along the edge of a sea
  • Synonym: coast - the land next to the sea
  • Antonym: inland - away from the coast; interior land

Etymology

From 'sea' + 'coast', referring to land beside the sea.
