dully

1. ADV

/ ˈdʌli / (AmE), / ˈdʌli / (BrE)
In a dull, spiritless, or unenthusiastic manner; without liveliness or brightness.

Examples

Andy nodded dully.

Synset Examples

she nodded her head dully

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lively - full of life and energy

2. ADV

/ ˈdʌli / (AmE), / ˈdʌli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks brightness, luster, or shine.

Examples

But dully glinting on the dark form were the buttons and badge of a policeman.

Synset Examples

the light shone dully through the haze
unpolished buttons glinted dully

Related Terms

  • Antonym: brightly - in a way that is full of light, shine, or brilliance
