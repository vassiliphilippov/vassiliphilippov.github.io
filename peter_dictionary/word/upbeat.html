upbeat

1. ADJ

/ ˈʌpˌbiːt / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌbiːt / (BrE)
Cheerful, optimistic, or positive, especially in attitude or mood.

Examples

Eliminating the patter and the upbeat numbers left little but blues and other songs of equal melancholy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • Antonym: downbeat - pessimistic or negative in attitude

Etymology

Originally a musical term referring to the unaccented beat before the downbeat; figurative sense developed in the 20th century.
