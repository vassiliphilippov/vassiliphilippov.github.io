min

1. NOUN

/ mɪn / (AmE), / mɪn / (BrE)
Abbreviation for minute, a unit of time equal to 60 seconds or 1/60th of an hour.

the tubes were then centrifuged at 1000 rpm for 1 min and examined macroscopically for agglutination.

Etymology
