pianism

1. NOUN

/ ˈpiː.əˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpɪə.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the art or technique of playing the piano, especially as demonstrated by a particular pianist

Examples

And although Schnabel's pianism bristles with excitement, it is meticulously faithful to Schubert's dynamic markings and phrase indications.

Synset Examples

a program of pianism

Related Terms

  • Synonym: piano playing - the act of playing the piano
  • Degree Higher: virtuosity - great technical skill in music or performance

Etymology

From Italian 'pianismo', from 'piano' (piano) + '-ism'.
