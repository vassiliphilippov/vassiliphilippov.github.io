overpriced

1. ADJ

/ ˌoʊvərˈpraɪst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpraɪst / (BrE)
Costing more than is reasonable or more than the item's value justifies.

Examples

"Sex is overpriced ", he said.

Synset Examples

overpriced items at resort shops

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: expensive - costing a lot of money; high-priced
  • Antonym: cheap - costing little money; low-priced

Etymology

From 'over-' (excessively) + 'priced' (given a stated cost).
