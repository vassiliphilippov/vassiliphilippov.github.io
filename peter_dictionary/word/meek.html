meek

1. ADJ

/ mik / (AmE), / miːk / (BrE)
Quiet, gentle, and easily imposed on; submissive or humble in spirit or manner.

Examples

The meekest, most submissive wife of today is a tiger by her mother's or grandmother's standards.

Synset Examples

meek and self-effacing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: submissive - ready to conform to the authority or will of others
  • Antonym: assertive - having or showing a confident and forceful personality

Etymology

From Old Norse *mjūkr* (soft) via Middle English.
