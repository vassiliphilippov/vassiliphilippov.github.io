combinable

1. ADJ

/ kəmˈbaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / kəmˈbaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be combined; capable of being joined or mixed together.

Examples

The commands are specified by the military regulations; are few in number, briefly worded, all different in sound; and are combinable into sequences which permit any marching maneuver that could be desired on a parade ground.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: incompatible - not able to be combined or to exist together in harmony

Etymology

Formed from 'combine' + adjectival suffix '-able'.
