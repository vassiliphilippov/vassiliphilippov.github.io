humidity

1. NOUN

/ hjuˈmɪdəti / (AmE), / hjuːˈmɪdəti / (BrE)
The amount of water vapor present in the air.

Examples

There are optimal humidity requirements for various agents when airborne.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: dryness - lack of moisture in the air

Etymology

From Latin humiditas (moisture).
