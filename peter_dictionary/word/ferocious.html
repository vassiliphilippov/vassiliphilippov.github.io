ferocious

1. ADJ

/ fəˈroʊʃəs / (AmE), / fəˈrəʊʃəs / (BrE)
Displaying fierce and violent energy; savagely intense.

Examples

"Of course, I agree with the Captain ", Gansevoort said thoughtfully, "but the conspiracy is ferocious and desperate.

Synset Examples

a ferocious beating
fierce fighting
a furious battle

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fierce - having or displaying a violent or intense aggressiveness
  • Antonym: gentle - having or showing a mild, kind, or tender temperament or character

Etymology

From Latin 'ferox', 'ferocis' (fierce, wild).
