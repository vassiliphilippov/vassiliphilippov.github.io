shithead

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃɪtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃɪthɛd / (BrE)
An offensive slang term for a stupid or contemptible person.

Examples

Hey, Skeet, come on, give me that, you shithead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a very stupid person
  • Synonym: dunce - a slow-witted or stupid person
