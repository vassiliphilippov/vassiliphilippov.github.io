twinkling

1. NOUN

/ ˈtwɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
A very short period of time; an instant.

Examples

He tightened up in a twinkling.

Synset Examples

if I had the chance I'd do it in a flash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: moment - a very brief period of time
  • Synonym: second - a very short unit of time
  • Synonym: flash - an instant; a very short time

Etymology

From the verb 'twinkle', referring to the rapid movement or shimmer of light.
