grammarian

1. NOUN

/ ɡrəˈmɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ɡrəˈmɛə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in grammar and syntax

Related Terms

  • Synonym: linguist - a person skilled in languages or the scientific study of language

Etymology
