naturopath

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃəroʊˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈnatʃərəˌpaθ / (BrE)
A person trained in and practicing naturopathy, a system of alternative medicine based on natural remedies and the body's ability to heal itself.

Examples

He may call himself a naprapath, a physiotherapist, an electrotherapist, a naturopath, a sanipractor, a medical cultist, a masseur, a "doctor "- or what have you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturopathy - system of alternative medicine using natural remedies

Etymology

From nature + -path, influenced by naturopathy.
