caliph

1. NOUN

/ ˈkeɪlɪf, ˈkælɪf / (AmE), / ˈkælɪf, ˈkeɪlɪf / (BrE)
the chief Muslim civil and religious ruler, regarded as the successor to the Prophet Muhammad and leader of the Islamic community

Examples

Not everyone is bewitched by Byron's caliphs and harem beauties ".

Synset Examples

many radical Muslims believe a Khalifah will unite all Islamic lands and people and subjugate the rest of the world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: khalif - alternative spelling of caliph

Etymology

From Arabic khalīfa (successor, steward, caliph).
