2. ADJ

/ ˈvæl.jud / (AmE), / ˈvæl.juːd / (BrE)
Regarded with great respect or appreciation; highly esteemed or prized.

Examples

Most valued friend.

Synset Examples

a valued friend
precious memories

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prized - highly valued or esteemed; treasured
  • Synonym: treasured - held in very high regard; cherished
  • Synonym: precious - of great value; not to be wasted or treated carelessly
  • Antonym: unvalued - not esteemed or appreciated; not given importance

Etymology

From the verb 'value,' from Old French 'valuer,' from Latin 'valere' (to be strong, be worth).
