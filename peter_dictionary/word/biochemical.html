biochemical

1. ADJ

/ ˌbaɪ.oʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈkɛm.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to biochemistry or to the chemical processes occurring in living organisms.

Examples

Mutants may also be developed with changes in biochemical properties that are of importance in identification.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chemical - relating to chemistry or chemicals

Etymology

From 'bio-' (life) + 'chemical', reflecting its relation to chemistry in biological contexts.
