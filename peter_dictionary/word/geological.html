geological

1. ADJ

/ dʒiəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / dʒiːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science of geology or the structure, history, and processes of the Earth's physical formation

Examples

Those which tell a story of the earth's formation in each area can add geological interest to the recreation sites.
In 1819 under royal command he undertook a very successful geological expedition to Bornholm, one of the Danish islands, being one of three scientists in the expedition.

Synset Examples

geological formations
geologic forces

Related Terms

  • Synonym: geologic - relating to geology; variant spelling

Etymology

From French géologique, from géologie (geology) + -ique (-ical).
