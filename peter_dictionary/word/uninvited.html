uninvited

1. ADJ

/ ʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnɪnˈvaɪtɪd / (BrE)
Not having been asked or requested to attend or participate; unwelcome or unwanted.

Examples

Had Faith Constable's explanation of her confidence, so uninvited, been a little thin?

Synset Examples

uninvited guests
uninvited thoughts

Related Terms

  • Antonym: invited - having been asked or welcomed to attend or participate

Etymology

Formed by prefixing 'un-' to 'invited'.
