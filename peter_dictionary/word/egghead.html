egghead

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛɡˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is highly academic or intellectual, often in a way that is regarded as excessive or socially awkward

Examples

A Southerner married to a New Englander, I have lived for many years in a Connecticut commuting town with a high percentage of artists, writers, publicity men, and business executives of egghead tastes.

Synset Examples

in spite of her love of reading she denied being an egghead

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intellectual - a person possessing a highly developed intellect
  • Synonym: nerd - a person who is very interested in academic or technical pursuits; socially awkward
  • Synonym: bookworm - a person who enjoys reading and studying a lot

Etymology

Originally American slang from the mid-20th century, possibly referring to the supposed shape of an intellectual's bald head resembling an egg.
