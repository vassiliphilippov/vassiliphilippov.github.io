gentlemanly

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛntəlmənli / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntlmənli / (BrE)
polite, honorable, and respectful in a manner befitting a gentleman

Examples

Spahn not only is a superior pitcher but a gentlemanly fine fellow, a ball player's ball player, as they say in the trade.

Synset Examples

gentlemanly behavior

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ungentlemanly - not befitting a gentleman; rude or dishonorable

Etymology

From 'gentleman' plus '-ly', meaning 'in the manner of a gentleman'.
