unpromising

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈprɑːmɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɒmɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
Not likely to yield favorable results or enjoyment; not giving hope of success.

Examples

It is from this unpromising background that the fictional private detective was recruited.

Synset Examples

faced an unpromising task
music for unpromising combinations of instruments

Related Terms

  • Antonym: promising - likely to be successful or favorable

Etymology

Formed from 'un-' (not) + 'promising'.
