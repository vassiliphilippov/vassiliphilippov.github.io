dispensary

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈspɛnsɛri / (AmE), / dɪˈspensəri / (BrE)
a place, often attached to a clinic or hospital, where medicines and medical supplies are prepared and distributed to patients

Examples

An hour of bouncing, a brief stop in a village to inspect a new school or dispensary.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin dispensarium (store-room), from Latin dispensare (to distribute).
