equivalence

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɪv.ə.ləns / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪv.ələns / (BrE)
The state of being equal or interchangeable in value, function, meaning, or effect.

Examples

Hence it is difficult to conceive of a packing of the atoms in this material in which the oxygen atoms are far from geometrical equivalence.

Etymology
