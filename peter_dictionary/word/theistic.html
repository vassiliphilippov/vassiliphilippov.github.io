theistic

1. ADJ

/ θiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / θɪˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or believing in theism, the doctrine that there is a god or gods.

Examples

Under the religious impulse, whether theistic or humanistic, men have joy in living; life leads somewhere.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: atheistic - relating to disbelief or lack of belief in the existence of God or gods

Etymology

From theism + -ic; theism from Greek theos (god).
