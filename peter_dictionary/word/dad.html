dad

1. NOUN

/ dæd / (AmE), / dæd / (BrE)
An informal term for one's father.

Examples

"Dad.
Dad, wait ".
"Just this one favor, Dad.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: father - male parent
  • Antonym: mom - informal term for mother

Etymology

Probably from childish or baby talk, similar to dada.
