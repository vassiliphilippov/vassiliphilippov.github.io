irritable

1. ADJ

/ ˈɪr.ɪ.t̬ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈɪr.ɪ.tə.bəl / (BrE)
easily annoyed or angered by small things; quick to become irritated.

Examples

It is an irritable rule that does baseball more harm than good, especially at the minor league level.

Synset Examples

an incorrigibly fractious young man
not the least nettlesome of his countrymen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cranky - easily annoyed or difficult to please
  • Antonym: placid - not easily upset or excited

Etymology

From Latin 'irritabilis' (liable to be provoked).
