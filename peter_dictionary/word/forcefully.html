forcefully

1. ADV

/ ˈfɔrs.fə.li / (AmE), / ˈfɔːs.fʊl.i / (BrE)
In a way that uses or shows great force, strength, or intensity.

Examples

[SIGHS FORCEFULLY]

Synset Examples

we are seeing this film too late to feel its original impact forcefully

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerfully - in a strong or forceful manner
  • Antonym: gently - in a mild or soft manner

Etymology

From 'forceful' + adverbial suffix '-ly'.
