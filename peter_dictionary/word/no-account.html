no-account

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊ əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
Of no value or importance; worthless or insignificant.

Examples

One Federal soldier wrote: "The docters is no a conte.

Synset Examples

a sorry horse
a sorry excuse
a lazy no-count, good-for-nothing goldbrick
the car was a no-good piece of junk

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worthless - Having no value or use
  • Antonym: valuable - Having great value or worth

Etymology

Formed from 'no' + 'account' as in 'of no account' (of no importance), dating from 18th-century English usage.
