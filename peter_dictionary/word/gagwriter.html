gagwriter

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡæɡˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɡæɡˌraɪtə / (BrE)
A person who writes jokes or comic material for entertainers, especially for comedians or comedy shows.

Examples

In recent years gagwriters have discovered this brand of blunder and thus the misplaced modifier has acquired a new habitat in the gagline.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: joke writer - a person who writes jokes
  • Degree Higher: comedian - an entertainer who performs comedy

Etymology

From 'gag' (slang for a joke) + 'writer'.
