majestically

1. ADV

/ məˈdʒɛstɪkli / (AmE), / məˈdʒɛstɪkli / (BrE)
In a majestic manner; with dignity, grandeur, or impressive beauty.

Examples

The slim pirogues in harmony with the majestically meandering Mekong River.

Synset Examples

flamingoes walk majestically through the marshes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grandly - in a grand or impressive manner
  • Antonym: humbly - in a humble or modest way

Etymology

From majestic + -ally, ultimately from Latin 'majestaticus' (majestic).
