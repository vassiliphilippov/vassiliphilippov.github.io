esteemed

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈstimd / (AmE), / ɪˈstiːmd / (BrE)
Regarded with respect and admiration; having a high reputation.

Examples

CHAIRMAN: I'’m sure we all agree with our esteemed colleague.

Synset Examples

our esteemed leader
a prestigious author

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respected - admired or held in high esteem
  • Synonym: prestigious - having a high reputation; honored
  • Antonym: disreputable - not respected or trusted by people

Etymology

Past participle of 'esteem', from Latin 'aestimare' (to value, appraise).
