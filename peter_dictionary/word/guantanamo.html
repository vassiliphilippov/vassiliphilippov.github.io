Guantanamo

1. PROPN

/ ɡwɑnˈtɑːnəˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ɡwanˈtænəməʊ / (BrE)
A city in southeastern Cuba, known for hosting a United States naval base.

Examples

Due to serious setbacks to our troops in Guantanamo and Santiago... ...my position in Cuba is untenable.

Etymology

From Spanish Guantánamo, of Taíno origin.
