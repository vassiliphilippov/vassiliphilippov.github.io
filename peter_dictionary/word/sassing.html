sassing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsæsɪŋ / (BrE)
An impudent or cheeky reply; insolence or disrespectful talk.

Examples

"Listen to me, Jorge, I 've had enough to your sassing.

Synset Examples

don't give me any of your sass

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sass - impudent or disrespectful talk

Etymology

From the verb 'sass,' itself a shortening of 'sauciness' or 'saucy.'
