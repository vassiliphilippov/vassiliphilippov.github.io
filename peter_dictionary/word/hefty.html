hefty

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɛfti / (AmE), / ˈhɛfti / (BrE)
(of a person) large and strong; having considerable weight and power.

Examples

The first ball the hefty pitcher threw came in for Mike's head.

Synset Examples

a hefty athlete
a muscular boxer
powerful arms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brawny - physically strong; muscular
  • Synonym: muscular - having well-developed muscles; strong
  • Synonym: powerful - having great strength or force

Etymology

Perhaps from heft (weight, heaviness) + -y.
