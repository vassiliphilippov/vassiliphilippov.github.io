capitalistic

1. ADJ

/ ˌkæpɪtəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæpɪtəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or supporting capitalism; favoring or adhering to the principles of capitalism.

Examples

But let us not complain of the evils of capitalism by referring to a form that is not truly capitalistic.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: socialistic - relating to socialism; favoring principles of socialism

Etymology

From 'capitalism' + '-istic', patterned after other economic adjectives.
