unkind

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈkaɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkaɪnd / (BrE)
Not kind; lacking sympathy or consideration for others.

Examples

Therefore it's a genuine pleasure to tell you about an entirely happy bodybuilder who has never had to train in secret; has never heard one unkind word from his parents; and never has been taunted by his schoolmates!

Synset Examples

a thoughtless and unkind remark
the unkindest cut of all

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kind - having or showing a friendly, generous, and considerate nature
  • Degree Higher: cruel - willingly causing pain or suffering to others

Etymology

From un- (not) + kind, meaning 'not kind'.
