escaped

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈskeɪpt / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪpt / (BrE)
Having gotten away from a place of confinement or control; no longer held or captured.

Examples

In 1966, Andy Dufresne escaped from Shawshank Prison.

Synset Examples

a convict still at large
searching for two escaped prisoners

Related Terms

  • Antonym: captured - having been caught or detained
