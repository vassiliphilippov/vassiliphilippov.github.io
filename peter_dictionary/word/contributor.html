contributor

1. NOUN

/ kənˈtrɪbjətɚ / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjʊtə / (BrE)
a person who gives or promises to give a sum of money, especially to a fund, project, or cause

Examples

The major contributor was a shopping center with houses being added to the system as the subdivision developed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: donor - a person who gives something, especially money to a cause
  • Synonym: benefactor - a person who gives money or help to a person or cause

Etymology

From Latin 'contributus', past participle of 'contribuere' (to bring together, contribute).

2. NOUN

/ kənˈtrɪbjətər / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjʊtə / (BrE)
a person, especially a writer, whose work is published in a newspaper, magazine, or as part of a book

Examples

The contributors to this testament were all well-known:

Related Terms

  • Synonym: author - person who writes books or other works
  • Antonym: editor - person who prepares written material for publication

Etymology

From Latin 'contribuere' (to bring together, contribute) via English 'contribute'.
