unlovely

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈlʌvli / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌvli / (BrE)
not attractive or lacking beauty or charm

Examples

He saw no life, but still stood there for a time peering at the unlovely hills, his gaze continually returning to Papa-san.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lovely - attractive or beautiful
  • Synonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance

Etymology

From un- (not) + lovely.
