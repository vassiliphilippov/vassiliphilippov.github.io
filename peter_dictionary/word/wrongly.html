wrongly

1. ADV

/ ˈrɔŋli / (AmE), / ˈrɒŋli / (BrE)
In a way that is not correct or accurate.

Examples

Yet, if he used all of the little means at his disposal, he would be instructing his students wrongly.

Synset Examples

he decided to reveal the details only after other sources had reported them incorrectly
she guessed wrong

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorrectly - in a way that is not correct
  • Antonym: rightly - in a correct or accurate manner

Etymology

Formed from 'wrong' + adverbial '-ly' suffix; 'wrong' from Old English *wrang* (a wrong, a wrong-doing).
