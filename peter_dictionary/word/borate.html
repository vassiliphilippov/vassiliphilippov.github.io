borate

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔːˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɔːˌreɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester of boric acid

Examples

Hydroxides, orthophosphates, borates, carbonates, and silicates are important inorganic ingredients of hard surface cleaners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boric acid - a weak acid of boron used as an antiseptic and flame retardant

Etymology

From boric acid, with '-ate' indicating a salt or ester in chemical nomenclature.
