girlfriend

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɜrlˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːlˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
A female friend, especially a woman's close female companion.

Examples

"I'm terribly upset ", she told a girl-friend.

Synset Examples

Mary and her girlfriend organized the party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friend - a person whom one knows and has a bond of mutual affection
  • Antonym: boyfriend - a male friend; also can refer to a romantic partner

2. NOUN

/ ˈɡɝlˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːl.frɛnd / (BrE)
A girl or woman with whom a person is romantically or sexually involved.

Examples

In order to exonerate himself, he is compelled to find the real criminal, who happens to be his girl friend.
I'll get a message to that girlfriend when the time is right.

Synset Examples

his girlfriend kicked him out

Related Terms

  • Antonym: boyfriend - male romantic partner
  • Synonym: partner - person with whom one has a romantic or sexual relationship

Etymology

Formed from 'girl' + 'friend', in use since the late 19th century.
