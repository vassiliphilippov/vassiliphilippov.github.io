granulocytic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɡræn.jə.ləˈsɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡran.jʊ.ləˈsɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving granulocytes (a type of white blood cell containing granules)

Examples

Some of the sinusoids contained large numbers of nucleated red cells, and cells of the granulocytic series were found in small numbers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: granulocyte - a type of white blood cell with granules in its cytoplasm

Etymology

From 'granulocyte' (from Latin 'granulum' meaning 'small grain' + 'cyte,' from Greek 'kytos' meaning 'cell') + '-ic'.
