marimba

1. NOUN

/ məˈrɪmbə / (AmE), / məˈrɪmbə / (BrE)
a percussion instrument consisting of a set of wooden bars tuned to a chromatic scale and played with mallets, with resonators beneath to amplify the sound.

Examples

Jesus marimba!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: xylophone - a percussion instrument with wooden bars of graduated length played with mallets

Etymology

From Bantu (Kimbundu) *marimba*, a type of xylophone.
