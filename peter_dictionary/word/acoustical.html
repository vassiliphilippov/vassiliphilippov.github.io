acoustical

1. ADJ

/ əˈkuːstɪkəl / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science of acoustics or to sound

Examples

An acoustical interferometer has been constructed and used, with helium gas as the thermometric fluid, to measure temperatures near 4.2 and 2.1 * * f.

Synset Examples

acoustic properties of a hall

Related Terms

  • Synonym: acoustic - relating to sound or the sense of hearing

Etymology

From acoustic + -al, denoting 'relating to.'
