misfire

1. VERB

/ ˌmɪsˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈfaɪə / (BrE)
(of a gun or other weapon or engine) to fail to fire or detonate as intended.

Examples

It had misfired.

Synset Examples

The guns misfired

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ignite - to catch fire or cause to catch fire

Etymology

From prefix 'mis-' (wrongly) + 'fire' (to discharge a weapon).
