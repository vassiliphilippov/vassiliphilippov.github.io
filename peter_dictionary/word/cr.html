Cr

1. NOUN

/ si ɑr / (AmE), / si ɑː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for chromium, a hard, brittle metallic element resistant to corrosion and tarnishing

Examples

These data lead to a structure in which sheets of Cr atoms lie between two sheets of O atoms.
The O atoms in each sheet are close packed and each Cr atom is surrounded by a distorted octahedron of O atoms.
Chromium analysis gave 58.8 % Cr as compared with 61.2 % theory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chromium - a hard, brittle, corrosion-resistant metallic element

Etymology

From the first two letters of 'chromium'; symbol assigned by international convention.
