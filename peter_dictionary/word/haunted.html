haunted

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑntɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or their expression) appearing troubled or obsessed, especially as if preoccupied by unhappiness, anxiety, or worry.

Examples

Juanita drooped about the place, wearing a haunted, brooding look, which Kate attributed to the baby's death, until the day a letter came for her addressed to "Miss Juanita Fitzroy ", bearing a Grafton postmark.

Synset Examples

became more and more haunted by the stupid riddle
was absolutely obsessed with the girl
got no help from his wife who was preoccupied with the children
he was taken up in worry for the old woman

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsessed - preoccupied with or constantly thinking about something
  • Synonym: preoccupied - absorbed or concerned with something to the exclusion of other things

Etymology

From the verb 'haunt', from Old French 'hanter' (to frequent, visit, inhabit).
