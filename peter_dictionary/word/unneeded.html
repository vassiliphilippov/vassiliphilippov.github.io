unneeded

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈnidɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈniːdɪd / (BrE)
Not required or necessary; surplus to requirements.

Examples

Do you insist that unneeded salary employees take their vacations during plant shutdowns?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unnecessary - not needed; not required
  • Antonym: needed - required; necessary
