communicating

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunɪˌkeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of conveying information or exchanging messages.

Examples

It is not easy for the therapist to discern when, in the patient's communicating, an introject has appeared and is holding sway.

Synset Examples

they could not act without official communication from Moscow

Related Terms

  • Synonym: communication - the imparting or exchanging of information

Etymology

From Latin 'communicare' (to share, to make common), via 'communicate.'
