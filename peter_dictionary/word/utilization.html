utilization

1. NOUN

/ ˌjuːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of using something for a practical purpose

Examples

This may be helpful in improving the competitive position of established firms through diversification and expansion or through more economical utilization of plant capacity.
Finally, a balance must be effected among project finance, utilization of agricultural surpluses, and general balance of payments support.
Improves utilization of low moisture corn (less than 14 %).

Synset Examples

he warned against the use of narcotic drugs
skilled in the utilization of computers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: use - the act of using something
  • Synonym: consumption - the act of consuming or using up resources
  • Antonym: waste - the act of using something carelessly or without achieving a useful purpose

Etymology

From utilize + -ation, with utilize from French 'utiliser', from Latin 'utilis' (useful).
