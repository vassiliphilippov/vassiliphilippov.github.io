approachable

1. ADJ

/ əˈproʊ.tʃə.bəl / (AmE), / əˈprəʊ.tʃə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a text, subject, or idea) easy to approach, understand, or engage with, especially for non-experts.

Examples

These conditions are unobtainable - are not even approachable in the qualified sense I have indicated - without the prior defeat of world Communism.

Synset Examples

readily accessible to the nonprofessional reader
the tales seem more approachable than his more difficult novels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accessible - (of a text or topic) easy to understand or reach

Etymology

From 'approach' + '-able', patterned after French 'approchable'.
