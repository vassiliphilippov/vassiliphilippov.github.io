mononuclear

1. ADJ

/ ˌmoʊnoʊˈnuːkliɚ / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
Having only one nucleus, especially referring to a cell with a single nucleus.

Examples

The intima of the larger coronary arteries was thickened by fibrous tissue containing fusiform clefts and mononuclear cells.

Etymology

From mono- (one) and nuclear (relating to a nucleus).
