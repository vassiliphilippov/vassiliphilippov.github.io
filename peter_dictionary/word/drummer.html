drummer

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrʌmər / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmə / (BrE)
A person who plays a drum, especially as part of a band or musical group.

Examples

No drummers here, no pipers, and the red coats were covered with a fine film of dust.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percussionist - person who plays percussion instruments

Etymology

From 'drum' + -er (agent noun suffix).
