Jeffersonian

1. ADJ

/ ˌdʒɛfərˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛfəˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Thomas Jefferson, his political theories, or his principles.

Examples

Who will deny that in a vast portion of the South the Federal action is incompatible with the Jeffersonian concept of "the consent of the governed "?

Synset Examples

Jeffersonian democracy

Etymology

From the name of Thomas Jefferson (1743–1826), third President of the United States, with the adjectival suffix -ian.
