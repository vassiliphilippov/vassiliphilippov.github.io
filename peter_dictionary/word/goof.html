goof

1. VERB

/ ɡuf / (AmE), / ɡuːf / (BrE)
to make a silly or careless mistake; to blunder

Examples

He was the guy what always goofed at Question Time.

Synset Examples

I blundered during the job interview

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blunder - to make a careless or serious mistake
  • Synonym: slip up - to make a minor mistake

Etymology

Probably of imitative origin, 20th-century American slang.
