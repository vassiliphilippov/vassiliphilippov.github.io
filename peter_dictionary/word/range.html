1. NOUN

/ reɪndʒ / (AmE), / reɪndʒ / (BrE)
The extent or scope within which something acts, operates, or has power or control.

Examples

This big, flexible voice with uncommon range has been superbly disciplined.
We simply find ourselves in the position of having no means for inquiring into the structure and meaning of this range of our experience.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scope - extent or range of view, outlook, application, operation, effectiveness, etc.
  • Synonym: reach - distance or extent to which something can be reached or affected
  • Antonym: limit - the point or edge beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass

Etymology

From Old French 'range', from 'rengier' (to arrange), from Latin 'rēnga' (line or row).
