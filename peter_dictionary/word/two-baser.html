two-baser

1. NOUN

/ ˈtuːˌbeɪsɚ / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌbeɪsə / (BrE)
(Baseball) a base hit that allows the batter to reach second base safely; a double.

Examples

A double by Green, Allison's run scoring 2 baser, an infield single by Lemon and Gardner's solid single to center put the Twins back in front in the last of the fifth.

Synset Examples

he hit a double to deep centerfield

Related Terms

  • Synonym: double - base hit in baseball allowing the batter to reach second base
