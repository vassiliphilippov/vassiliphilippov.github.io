possessed

1. ADJ

/ pəˈzɛst / (AmE), / pəˈzɛst / (BrE)
(of a person) strongly influenced or controlled by a powerful force such as an emotion, idea, or spirit, as if they have lost control of themselves.

Examples

"What possessed you to tell me a clotheshorse would be a good idea "?

Synset Examples

by love possessed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsessed - preoccupied or dominated by a particular thought or feeling

Etymology

From Latin possessus, past participle of possidēre (to possess).
