untraditional

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌntrəˈdɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌntrəˈdɪʃənl̩ / (BrE)
not following or conforming to established customs, beliefs, or practices

Examples

Faulkner is a most untraditional traditionalist.

Synset Examples

nontraditional designs
nontraditional practices

Related Terms

  • Antonym: traditional - following established customs and practices

Etymology

Prefix un- (not) + traditional.
