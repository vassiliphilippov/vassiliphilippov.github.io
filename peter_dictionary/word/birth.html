birth

1. NOUN

/ bɝθ / (AmE), / bɜːθ / (BrE)
The time when a person, animal, or thing comes into existence; the act or event of being born.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: death - end of life or existence
  • Synonym: beginning - the time or place of starting something

Etymology

Old English *byrð*, from *beran* (to bear).
