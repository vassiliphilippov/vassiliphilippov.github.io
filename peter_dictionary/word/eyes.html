eyes

1. NOUN

/ aɪz / (AmE), / aɪz / (BrE)
(in someone's eyes) According to someone's opinion or judgment; as regarded or perceived by someone.

Examples

This anomalous status of the educator cannot fail to lower his standing in the eyes of the students.
He had n't shown up too well in their eyes, letting himself be browbeaten by a woman.
At the same time, because the personal code of the detective coincides with the legal dictates of his society, because he likes to catch criminals, he is in middle class eyes a virtuous man.

Synset Examples

in the eyes of the law
I was wrong in her eyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: view - an opinion or attitude
  • Synonym: regard - attention or consideration; opinion
  • Synonym: estimation - opinion or judgment about someone or something
