wasted

1. ADJ

/ ˈweɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈweɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of resources, effort, etc.) not used to good effect; squandered or used without achieving something useful.

Examples

All of this would be wasted, of course, if the performance lacked authority and musical distinction.

Synset Examples

squandered money cannot be replaced
a wasted effort

Related Terms

  • Synonym: squandered - wasted or used carelessly without benefit

2. ADJ

/ ˈweɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈweɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of effort, words, etc.) Serving no useful purpose; used or expended with no result.

Examples

He was a fine and showy rider, but his skill was wasted on us.

Synset Examples

otiose lines in a play
advice is wasted words
a pointless remark
a life essentially purposeless
senseless violence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pointless - having no meaning or purpose
  • Synonym: futile - incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
  • Antonym: useful - able to be used for a practical purpose
