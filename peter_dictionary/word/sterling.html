sterling

1. ADJ

/ ˈstɜrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
of the highest quality; excellent or admirable, especially in character or performance.

Examples

Since the hero, a sterling and upright fellow, is a rich Brown senior, while two Yalies are cast as virtual rapists, I suppose I should disqualify myself from sitting in judgment on "Where the Boys are ", but I shall do nothing of the sort.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excellent - extremely good; outstanding
  • Synonym: superb - of very high quality; excellent

Etymology

From Middle English *sterlyn*, originally meaning a silver penny, later extended to signify excellence.
