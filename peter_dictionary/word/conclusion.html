1. NOUN

/ kənˈkluʒən / (AmE), / kənˈkluːʒən / (BrE)
an opinion or decision that is formed after careful thought or consideration of evidence

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decision - a choice or judgment made after considering options
  • Synonym: judgment - an opinion or decision formed after thought
  • Degree Lower: assumption - something that is accepted as true without proof; often contrasted as less conclusive

Etymology

From Latin 'conclusio', from 'concludere' (to conclude, finish).
