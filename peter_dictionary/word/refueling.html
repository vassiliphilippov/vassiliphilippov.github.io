refueling

1. NOUN

/ riˈfjuəlɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈfjuːəlɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of supplying fuel to a vehicle, aircraft, machine, or similar object.

Examples

I am recommending additional acquisitions of the improved version of the B-52 (the B-52 H with the new turbofan engine) and procurement of the B-58 supersonic medium bomber, together with the supporting refueling tankers in each case.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fueling - the process of supplying fuel

Etymology

From re- (again) + fueling (from 'fuel', ultimately from Old French 'feu' meaning 'fire').
