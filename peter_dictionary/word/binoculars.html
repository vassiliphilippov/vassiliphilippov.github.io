binoculars

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnɑkjələrz / (AmE), / bɪˈnɒkjʊləz / (BrE)
an optical instrument with two lenses for viewing distant objects with both eyes at once

Examples

They bring their fishing rods and binoculars and bathing suits.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: monocular - an optical instrument designed for use with one eye

Etymology

From Latin 'bini' (double, two at a time) + ocular (of the eye).
