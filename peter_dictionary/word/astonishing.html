astonishing

1. ADJ

/ əˈstɑː.nɪ.ʃɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈstɒn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ / (BrE)
very surprising; causing great wonder or amazement.

Examples

(2) The patient attained an astonishing efficiency in a new trade.
They can even be rather grand, like Edward Land's monument to the astonishing success of Polaroid.
The guards came to life with astonishing menace.

Synset Examples

she does an amazing amount of work
the dog was capable of astonishing tricks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amazing - causing great surprise or wonder
  • Synonym: startling - very surprising, causing alarm or shock
  • Antonym: ordinary - not unusual or surprising

Etymology

From English 'astonish' + '-ing'; 'astonish' from Old French 'estoner', from Latin 'extonare' (to thunder out).
