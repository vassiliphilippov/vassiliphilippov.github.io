cuss

1. NOUN

/ kʌs / (AmE), / kʌs / (BrE)
a profane or obscene word or expression, especially one used when angry or surprised

Examples

Institutionalized, my ass.

Synset Examples

expletives were deleted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: curse - a profane or obscene word or oath
  • Synonym: swear word - an obscene or profane word; an expletive

Etymology

Variant of 'curse', originally from Old English *curs* (curse).
