brat

1. NOUN

/ bræt / (AmE), / bræt / (BrE)
A child, typically one who is badly behaved or troublesome.

Examples

You Guinea brat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: urchin - mischievous child, especially one who is poor or ragged
  • Antonym: angel - well-behaved child (opposite)

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from Middle English 'brat' (cloak, rag), later applied to children in ragged clothing.
