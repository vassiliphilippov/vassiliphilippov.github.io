nauseated

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɔziˌeɪtɪd, ˈnɑziˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːziˌeɪtɪd, ˈnɔːsiˌeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Feeling sickness in the stomach accompanied by an urge to vomit.

Examples

Whatever we felt about the redcoats, we respected them in terms of their trade, which was killing; and I know that I, myself, was nauseated with apprehension and fear and that my hands were soaking wet where they held my gun.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queasy - feeling sick and likely to vomit
  • Synonym: nauseous - causing or feeling nausea
  • Antonym: well - not feeling sick; in good health

Etymology

From Latin *nauseare* (to feel sick, to be seasick), from *nausea* (sea-sickness).
