needlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈniːdləsli / (AmE), / ˈniːdləsli / (BrE)
In a way that is unnecessary or without need.

Examples

To greet them with repulsion would turn what before was neutral into something bad; it would needlessly bring badness into the world; and even on subjectivist assumptions that does not seem very bright.

Synset Examples

it would needlessly bring badness into the world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unnecessarily - in an unnecessary manner
  • Antonym: necessarily - as needed or required

Etymology

From 'needless' + '-ly', meaning 'without need'.
