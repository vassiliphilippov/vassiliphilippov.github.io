all-time

1. ADJ

/ ˈɔlˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈɔːlˌtaɪm / (BrE)
Unsurpassed or unmatched in degree, amount, or extent up to the present time; never previously equaled.

Examples

But there surely can be no doubt about the slender southpaw belonging with the all time great lefthanders in the game's history.

Synset Examples

prices at an all-time high
morale at an all-time low
among the all-time great lefthanders

Related Terms

  • Synonym: record-breaking - surpassing any previous record
  • Synonym: unprecedented - never done or known before
