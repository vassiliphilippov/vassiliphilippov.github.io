debacle

1. NOUN

/ dəˈbɑkəl / (AmE), / deɪˈbɑːkəl / (BrE)
a sudden and complete failure; a disaster or collapse, especially one that is dramatic or humiliating

Examples

To minimize the chances of repeating the Balafrej debacle the Ibrahim government was formed a titre personnel and a special office was created in the Ministry of the Interior to plan and to conduct the elections.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: catastrophe - an event causing great and often sudden damage or suffering
  • Synonym: disaster - a sudden event causing great damage or loss

Etymology

From French débâcle, originally referring to the breaking up of ice, from débâcler 'to unbar, free'
