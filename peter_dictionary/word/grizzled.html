grizzled

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡrɪzəld / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪzəld / (BrE)
(of hair or fur) having dark hairs mixed with grey or white; streaked or sprinkled with grey, especially due to age.

Examples

Scotty regarded the ear and the grizzled hair around it with a moment of interest.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grey - of a color between black and white; often associated with hair color change due to age

Etymology

From Middle English *grisel*, from Old French *gris* (grey).
