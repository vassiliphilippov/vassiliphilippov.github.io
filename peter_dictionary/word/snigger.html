snigger

1. VERB

/ ˈsnɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈsnɪɡə / (BrE)
to laugh quietly or in a suppressed manner, often in a disrespectful or mocking way

Examples

Grosse ducked and sniggered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snicker - to laugh quietly and partly to oneself

Etymology

Probably of imitative origin, related to 'snicker'.
