coastal

1. ADJ

/ ˈkoʊ.stəl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.stəl / (BrE)
Situated on, near, or bordering a coast.

Examples

Clams, crabs, and other marine life may add interest at coastal areas.

Synset Examples

coastal marshes
coastal waters
the Atlantic coastal plain

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inland - situated away from the coast

Etymology

From 'coast' + '-al', where 'coast' comes from Old French 'coste', from Latin 'costa' meaning 'rib, side'.

of or relating to the coast; situated on or near a coast.

Examples

Also, we should not even to-day discount the fact that a region such as the coastal lowlands centering on Charleston had closer ties with England and the West Indies than with the North even after independence.

Synset Examples

coastal erosion

Related Terms

From 'coast' + '-al', formed in English.
