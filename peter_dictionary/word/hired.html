hired

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd / (BrE)
(of a person or their services) engaged or employed for payment.

Examples

Hired, hard lackeys of the Warmongering Capitalists.
Privately, he created and magnified an image of himself as a hired assassin.

Synset Examples

hired hands
a hired gun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: employed - having a paid job
  • Degree Lower: freelance - working for different companies rather than being permanently employed
