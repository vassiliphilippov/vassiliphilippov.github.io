inflow

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnˌfloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fləʊ / (BrE)
the act or process of flowing in, especially of a liquid, gas, or resource entering a place

Examples

Inflow of air from the surrounding atmosphere was prevented by the two disks shown in figure 2.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: outflow - the act or process of flowing out

Etymology

Formed from the prefix in- (into) + flow.
