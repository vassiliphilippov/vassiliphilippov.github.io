shoes

1. NOUN

/ ʃuz / (AmE), / ʃuːz / (BrE)
a person's situation, position, or circumstances, especially as considered from their perspective; used in the phrases 'in someone's shoes' or 'step into someone's shoes.'

Examples

He was again tingling with pleasure, seeing himself clearly in Slater's shoes.

Synset Examples

If you were in my place what would you do?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: position - the situation that someone is in
  • Synonym: circumstance - a person's condition or situation
