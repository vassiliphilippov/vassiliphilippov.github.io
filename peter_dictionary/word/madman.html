madman

1. NOUN

/ ˈmædˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmad.man / (BrE)
A man who is mentally ill, or who behaves in a wildly foolish, dangerous, or uncontrolled way.

Examples

The Rebs were, to a Massachusetts corporal, "fighting madmen or not men at all but whiskey + gunpowder put into a human frame ".
From being a hated tyrant and madman he was now the symbol of all that was noblest and best in the history of Sweden.
Find out what this madman is poisoning us with.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunatic - a person who is insane
  • Synonym: maniac - a person exhibiting extremely wild or violent behavior
  • Antonym: sane person - a person who is not mentally ill

Etymology

From 'mad' (insane) + 'man', first recorded in English in the late 16th century.
