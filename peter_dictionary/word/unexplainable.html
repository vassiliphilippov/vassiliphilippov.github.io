unexplainable

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌnɪkˈspleɪnəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈspleɪnəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to explain or account for; not able to be given a reason or cause

Examples

There is the unexplainable, and there art raises questions that it does not attempt to answer ".

Synset Examples

perceptible only as unaccountable influences that hinder progress
an unexplainable fear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inexplicable - impossible to explain or interpret
  • Antonym: accountable - able to be explained or justified; responsible

Etymology

From un- (not) + explain + -able (capable of), meaning 'not able to be explained'.
