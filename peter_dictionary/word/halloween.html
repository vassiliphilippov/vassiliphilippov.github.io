Halloween

1. NOUN

/ ˌhæloʊˈiːn, ˌhæləˈwiːn / (AmE), / ˌhaləʊˈiːn, ˌhælɪˈwiːn / (BrE)
The evening of 31 October, celebrated with activities such as trick-or-treating, costume parties, and pranks, especially by children.

Examples

Having received permission to give a camp reunion Halloween party, she asked that I come and be her date.

Etymology

Contraction of 'All Hallows’ Even' (the evening before All Hallows’ Day, now All Saints’ Day).
