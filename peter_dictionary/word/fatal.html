1. ADJ

/ ˈfeɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtəɫ / (BrE)
Causing death or leading to failure; deadly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deadly - causing or able to cause death
  • Synonym: lethal - sufficient to cause death
  • Antonym: nonfatal - not causing death

Etymology

From Latin fatalis (destined, decreed by fate), from fatum (fate).
