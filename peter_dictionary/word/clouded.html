clouded

1. ADJ

/ ˈklaʊdɪd / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person's expression or features) showing signs of trouble, unhappiness, or distress.

Examples

His face was clouded with unhappiness.

Synset Examples

his face was clouded with unhappiness

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troubled - showing signs of distress or worry
  • Antonym: clear - (of features or expression) free from distress or worry
