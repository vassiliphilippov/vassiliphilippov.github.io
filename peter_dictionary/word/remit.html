remit

1. VERB

/ rɪˈmɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪt / (BrE)
to send (money) as payment for goods or services, especially by mail or electronically

Examples

If your computation on Form 1040 or Form 1040 A shows you owe additional tax, it should be remitted with your return unless you owe less than $ 1, in which case it is forgiven.

Synset Examples

remit $25

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pay - to give money owed for goods or services

Etymology

From Latin remittere (to send back, release) via Old French remettre.
