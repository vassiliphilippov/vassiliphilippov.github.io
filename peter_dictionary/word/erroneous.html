erroneous

1. ADJ

/ əˈroʊniəs / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊniəs / (BrE)
containing or characterized by error; incorrect

Examples

However, the surface temperature gradient can produce erroneous vapor-pressure measurements for the bulk liquid helium unless precautions are taken to isolate the tube (which passes through the surface to the vapor pressure bulb) from the liquid helium surface.

Synset Examples

erroneous conclusions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incorrect - not correct; containing mistakes
  • Antonym: accurate - free from error; correct in all details

Etymology

From Latin 'erroneus' (wandering, straying), from 'errare' (to err).
