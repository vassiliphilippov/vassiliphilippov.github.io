1. ADJ

/ ɪˈsɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃəl / (BrE)
Absolutely necessary; extremely important or indispensable.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necessary - required to be done, achieved, or present; needed
  • Synonym: indispensable - absolutely necessary; cannot be done without
  • Antonym: superfluous - unnecessary, especially through being more than enough

Etymology

From Late Latin essentialis, from Latin essentia (being, essence).
