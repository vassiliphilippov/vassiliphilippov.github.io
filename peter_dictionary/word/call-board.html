call-board

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔl ˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːl ˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A bulletin board located backstage in a theater, used for posting notices to cast and crew.

Examples

He instructed Shirl Winter to compose a note of thanks to be posted on the call board.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: noticeboard - board for posting public notices
  • Synonym: bulletin board - board for displaying notices and information
