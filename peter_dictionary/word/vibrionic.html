vibrionic

1. ADJ

/ vaɪˈbraɪ.ɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / vɪˈbrɪɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a disease) caused by bacteria of the genus Vibrio.

Examples

To reduce vibrionic abortion in breeding sheep, feed 80 milligrams per head daily.

Synset Examples

vibrionic dysentery

Etymology

From New Latin Vibrio (a genus of bacteria) + -ic.
