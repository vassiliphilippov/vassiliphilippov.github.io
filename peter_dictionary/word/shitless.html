shitless

1. ADJ

/ ˈʃɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈʃɪtləs / (BrE)
Extremely frightened or terrified, usually used in the expression 'scared shitless'.

Examples

- I heard he had you shitting in your pants.

Synset Examples

he was scared shitless

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scared stiff - extremely frightened or unable to move from fear
