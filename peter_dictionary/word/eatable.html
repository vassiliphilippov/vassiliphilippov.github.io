eatable

1. ADJ

/ ˈiːtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈiːtəbl̩ / (BrE)
suitable or safe for eating

Examples

The morning hawk, hungry for any eatable, killable, digestible item, kept his eyes on the ring of anchored ships that lay off the shores in the bay, sheltered by the Jersey inlets.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: edible - suitable or safe to be eaten

Etymology

From eat + -able, following the pattern of other adjectives meaning 'able to be [verb]ed'.
