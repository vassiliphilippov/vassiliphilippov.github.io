Toronto

1. PROPN

/ təˈrɑːn(toʊ) / (AmE), / təˈrɒn.təʊ / (BrE)
The capital city of Ontario and the largest city in Canada, located in southern Ontario on the northwestern shore of Lake Ontario.

Examples

"Grandma was n't expected to like it ", Dr. Marion Hilliard, the late Toronto gynecologist, once summed up the attitude of the' 90 s.

Etymology

From the Mohawk word *tkaronto* meaning 'place where trees stand in the water', originally referring to a location near Lake Simcoe.
