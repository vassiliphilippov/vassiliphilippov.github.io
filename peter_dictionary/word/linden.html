linden

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪndən / (AmE), / ˈlɪndən / (BrE)
The soft, light-colored wood of a linden tree, used in making furniture, carving, boxes, and other woodwork.

Examples

I even ferreted out the materials from which shields were made - linden wood covered with leather - so I 'd get the light reflections accurate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: basswood - soft wood from North American linden trees

Etymology

From Old English *lind* (linden tree), related to German *Linde*.
