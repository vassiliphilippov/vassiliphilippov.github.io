whirr

1. NOUN

/ wɜr / (AmE), / wɜː / (BrE)
a continuous, low, rapidly vibrating sound made by something moving quickly or spinning

Examples

[MACHINE WHIRRING SOFTLY] SENATOR: [IN ENGLISH] Mr. Cicci, from the year 1942 to the present time, you were an employee of the Genco Olive Oil Company?

Synset Examples

whir of a bird's wings
the whir of the propellers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: whir - a sound similar to a whirr; alternative spelling and pronunciation
