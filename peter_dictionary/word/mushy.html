mushy

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃi / (BrE)
soft and pulpy in texture, like mush.

Examples

Especially that big, white, mushy butt of yours.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soft - having a yielding or supple surface
  • Antonym: firm - solid or unyielding in texture

Etymology

From mush (soft or pulpy food) + -y (adjective suffix).
