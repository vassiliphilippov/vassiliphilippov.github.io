daintily

1. ADV

/ ˈdeɪntɪli / (AmE), / ˈdeɪntɪli / (BrE)
In a refined, delicate, or graceful manner.

Examples

After he 'd got out fresh liver for Bast, he paused to look at her crouched daintily over her dish.

Synset Examples

she nibbled daintily at her cake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gracefully - in an elegant or graceful manner
  • Antonym: clumsily - in an awkward or ungainly manner

Etymology

From 'dainty' + '-ly', with 'dainty' from Old French 'deintie' (delicacy, pleasure).
