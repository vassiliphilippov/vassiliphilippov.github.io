wickedly

1. ADV

/ ˈwɪkɪdli / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is wicked, evil, or morally wrong.

Examples

Again Katie reared, and now, wickedly, he compelled her to bring her hooves down again and again upon the sprawled figure of the stranger.

Synset Examples

act wickedly
grin evilly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evilly - in an evil manner
  • Antonym: virtuously - in a morally good or righteous manner

Etymology

From 'wicked' + '-ly'. 'Wicked' from Old English 'wicca' (wizard, sorcerer), later meaning evil.
