1. ADV

/ stɪl / (AmE), / stɪl / (BrE)

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yet - up until now; so far (adverb)
  • Antonym: anymore - at the present time, no longer (adverb, in negative contexts)

Etymology

From Old English 'stille' (motionless, quiet); related to Dutch 'stil' and German 'still'.
