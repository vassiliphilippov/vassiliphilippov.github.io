yen

1. NOUN

/ jɛn / (AmE), / jɛn / (BrE)
A strong desire or craving for something.

Examples

It was neither a spirit of self-sacrifice nor a yen to encourage the downtrodden that motivated Arnold.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hankering - a strong desire for something
  • Synonym: longing - a strong feeling of wanting something
  • Synonym: craving - an intense desire for something, especially food
  • Antonym: aversion - a strong feeling of dislike or opposition

Etymology

From Chinese (Cantonese) 'yin', meaning 'craving' or 'opium craving'.
