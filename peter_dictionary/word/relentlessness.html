relentlessness

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈlɛntləsnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈlɛntlɪsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being persistent and unyielding; an unwillingness to let up or show mercy.

Examples

It is his relentlessness and unwavering adherence to this revolutionary artistic philosophy that has enabled him to paint such pictures as "The Invasion of Cuba ".
The two men resemble each other closely in their cunning, their egotism, their relentlessness.

Synset Examples

the relentlessness or their pursuit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: persistence - the quality of continuing firmly in a course of action despite difficulty
  • Antonym: mercy - compassion or forgiveness shown towards someone

Etymology

From 'relentless' + '-ness', where 'relentless' is from 'relent' + '-less'.
