unsung

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsʌŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsʌŋ / (BrE)
not celebrated or praised; not given the recognition or acclaim deserved

Examples

THE MUSIC OF BINI SALFININISTAS, CAPITAL LP 63711 - R, one of the rare recordings of this titanic, yet unsung, composer.

Synset Examples

an obscure family
unsung heroes of the war

Related Terms

  • Antonym: celebrated - famous and praised

Etymology

Past participle of 'unsing'; figurative use based on 'sing the praises of'.
