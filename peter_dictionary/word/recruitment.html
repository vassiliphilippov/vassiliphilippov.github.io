recruitment

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkrutmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈkruːtmənt / (BrE)
the process of finding and enlisting people to join an organization, such as the army, a company, or a cause

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hiring - the act of employing someone for a job
