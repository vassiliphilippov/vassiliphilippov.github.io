evaluative

1. ADJ

/ ɪˈvæljuˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈvæljʊətɪv / (BrE)
involving or based on careful assessment and judgment

Examples

When an evaluative situation is set up, and no concern is with the details that lead to an over-all estimate, we say that roleplaying is used for evaluation.

Synset Examples

looked him over with an appraising eye
the literary judge uses many evaluative terms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appraising - making or involving a judgment about value or quality

Etymology

From Latin 'evaluare' (to assess the value of), via 'evaluate' + '-ive'.
