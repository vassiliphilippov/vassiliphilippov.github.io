revealing

1. ADJ

/ rɪˈvivlɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈviːlɪŋ / (BrE)
showing something that was previously hidden or unknown, often unintentionally

Examples

But fathers often addressed communications to their small children; and these, full of homely advice, are among the most human and revealing of Civil War letters.

Synset Examples

a telling smile
a telltale panel of lights
a telltale patch of oil on the water marked where the boat went down

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telling - showing or indicating something without words, especially unintentionally
  • Synonym: telltale - revealing or indicating something hidden, especially unintentionally
  • Antonym: concealing - keeping something hidden; not revealing

Etymology

From English 'reveal' + '-ing' (present participle form).
