dark-skinned

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɑrk ˌskɪnd / (AmE), / ˈdɑːk ˌskɪnd / (BrE)
Having skin that contains a high amount of melanin pigment; of a relatively dark complexion.

Examples

He was tall and dark-skinned, a half-breed, Wilson thought.

Synset Examples

National Association for the Advancement of Colored People
dark-skinned peoples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: light-skinned - having skin of a light complexion
