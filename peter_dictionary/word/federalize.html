federalize

1. VERB

/ ˈfɛdəɹəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɛdəɹəlaɪz / (BrE)
to unite (states, regions, or groups) into a federation or place under a federal system of government

Examples

There was no directive for it - the Security Council's resolution had not mentioned political matters, and in any case the United Nations by the terms of its charter may not interfere in the political affairs of any nation, whether to unify it, federalize it or Balkanize it.

Synset Examples

The country was federated after the civil war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unify - to make or become united, uniform, or whole
  • Antonym: balkanize - to divide (a region or body) into smaller mutually hostile states or groups

Etymology

From 'federal' + '-ize', based on Latin 'foedus' (league, compact).
