savory

1. ADJ

/ ˈseɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri / (BrE)
morally respectable, acceptable, or wholesome; not disreputable or scandalous.

Examples

The more I probed into this young man's activities and character, the less savory I found him.

Synset Examples

a past that was scarcely savory

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unsavory - morally offensive or objectionable
