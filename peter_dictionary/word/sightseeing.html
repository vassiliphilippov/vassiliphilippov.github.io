sightseeing

1. NOUN

/ ˈsaɪtˌsiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪtsiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of visiting places of interest, especially while on holiday

Examples

Rather than sit around waiting for the suitcases to be delivered, they had gone sight-seeing.
He was not going to lose the mission by default, and whatever reason Kayabashi had for bringing his little sight-seeing group to the mission, he was going to be in for a surprise.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tourism - the business or activity of traveling for pleasure
  • Synonym: traveling - the act of making a journey or trip

Etymology

Formed from 'sight' + 'seeing', first recorded in the late 19th century.
