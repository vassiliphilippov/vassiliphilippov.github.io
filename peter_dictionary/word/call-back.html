call-back

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlˌbæk / (BrE)
an instance of returning a telephone call or request; a return call, especially in response to an earlier message or contact

Examples

There's a couple of call-backs I can work on ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: return call - a phone call made in response to a previous call

Etymology

Formed from the verb 'call back' (to return a call), with noun-forming usage dating from the mid-20th century.
