one-thousandth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
Each of one of a thousand equal parts into which a whole is divided; the ordinal number matching 1,000 in a sequence.

Examples

Not once, in the time that he had known her, had he ever considered the possibility, not once, not for one one-thousandth of a second, of her infidelity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thousandth - ordinal number; position 1,000 or one of 1,000 equal parts

Etymology

Formed from 'one' and 'thousandth', with 'thousandth' from Old English 'þūsenda' (ordinal of thousand).
