popularly

1. ADV

/ ˈpɑː.pjɚ.lɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɒp.jʊ.ləli / (BrE)
by or among the general public; in a manner that is widely accepted or believed by many people.

Examples

Now again in 1961, in England, there is perhaps nothing in the religious sphere so popularly discussed as Christian unity.
As I have repeatedly stated, this provision is much more restrictive than the general law, popularly known as the Buy American Act.

Synset Examples

this topic was popularly discussed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: commonly - in a way that is usual, frequent, or widespread
  • Synonym: generally - by most people, usually

Etymology

From 'popular' + '-ly', with 'popular' from Latin 'popularis' (belonging to the people).
