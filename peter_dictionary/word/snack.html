snack

1. NOUN

/ snæk / (AmE), / snæk / (BrE)
a small amount of food eaten between meals, typically a light or informal meal

Examples

"I ought to be asleep, too, but I felt like a snack.
Brought you a little snack, Eckhardt. -

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: meal - an occasion when food is eaten

Etymology

Probably from Middle Dutch or Middle Low German *snacken* (to chatter, snap), possibly relating to the idea of 'snapping' at food.
