wop

1. NOUN

/ wɑp / (AmE), / wɒp / (BrE)
An offensive and derogatory term for a person of Italian descent.

Examples

I didn'’t mean to say Wop. [SCREAMS]

Etymology

Origin uncertain; possibly from Neapolitan 'guappo' (a dandy or swaggerer) or as an acronym for 'without papers' (now considered folk etymology).
