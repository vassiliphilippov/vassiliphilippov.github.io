Storeria

1. NOUN

/ stoʊˈrɪəriə / (AmE), / stəˈrɪəriə / (BrE)
A genus of small, nonvenomous North American snakes in the family Colubridae, including the red-bellied snake.

Examples

These three were much alike: lined snake (Tropidoclonion), one year and nine months; red-bellied snake (Storeria), two years; cottonmouth (Ancistrodon), two years.

Etymology

Named after American naturalist David H. Storer (1804–1891).
