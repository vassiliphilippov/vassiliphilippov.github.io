performer

1. NOUN

/ pərˈfɔrmər / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːmə / (BrE)
A person who entertains an audience by acting, singing, playing music, dancing, or similar acts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entertainer - person whose job is to entertain others
  • Synonym: artist - person skilled in performing arts

Etymology

From perform + -er, dating to late Middle English.
