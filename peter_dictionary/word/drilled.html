drilled

1. ADJ

/ drɪld / (AmE), / drɪld / (BrE)
Trained or disciplined through repeated practice, especially as part of a group or organization.

Examples

Jackson runs first and his Cavalry are well drilled to follow their leader.

Synset Examples

well-drilled in military procedures

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trained - taught or prepared through instruction or practice
  • Antonym: untrained - not trained or lacking practice
