cortically

1. ADV

/ ˈkɔrtɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or involving the cortex, especially the cerebral cortex of the brain.

Examples

Even more complex and obviously cortically induced forms of emotional arousal could be elicited in monkey A on seeing monkey B (but not a rabbit) in emotional stress.

Synset Examples

cortically induced arousal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cortical - relating to the cortex, especially the cerebral cortex

Etymology

From 'cortical' (from Latin 'corticis', meaning 'bark' or 'rind') with adverbial suffix '-ly'.
