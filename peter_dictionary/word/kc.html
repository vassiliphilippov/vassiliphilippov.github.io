kc

1. NOUN

/ keɪ siː / (AmE), / keɪ siː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for kilohertz, meaning one thousand cycles (or periods) per second.

Examples

As shown in Fig. 4, an external antenna is placed over or around the patient and excited 3000 times a second with short 400 - kc. bursts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kHz - kilohertz; standard SI abbreviation for 1,000 Hertz

Etymology

Abbreviation of kilohertz, from 'kilo-' (thousand) and 'cycles (per second)'.
