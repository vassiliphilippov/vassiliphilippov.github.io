lair

1. NOUN

/ lɛr / (AmE), / leə / (BrE)
A place where a wild animal lives and rests; a den or resting place of a wild animal.

Examples

The men were huddled in those lairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: den - the home of certain animals, especially predators

Etymology

Old English 'leger' (resting place, bed), from Proto-Germanic 'legraz'.
