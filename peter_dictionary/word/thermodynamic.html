thermodynamic

1. ADJ

/ ˌθɜrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with the principles of thermodynamics.

Examples

This is interesting for it combines both the thermodynamic concept of a minimum Gibbs function for equilibrium and minimum mechanical potential energy for equilibrium.
It is appropriate to call attention to certain thermodynamic properties of an ideal gas that are analogous to rubber-like deformation.

Synset Examples

the thermodynamic limit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermal - relating to heat
  • Synonym: thermodynamical - variant adjective form relating to thermodynamics

Etymology

From thermodynamics (thermo- relating to heat + dynamics), patterned after scientific terminology.
