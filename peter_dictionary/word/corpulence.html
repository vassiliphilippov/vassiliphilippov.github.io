corpulence

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔːrpjələns / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpjʊləns / (BrE)
The state of being excessively fat; obesity.

Examples

A Yank, disturbed by his increasing corpulence, wrote: "I am growing so fat I am a burden 2 myself ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obesity - the condition of being very overweight
  • Antonym: emaciation - the state of being abnormally thin or weak

Etymology

From Latin 'corpulentia' (stoutness), from 'corpus' (body).
