Californian

1. NOUN

/ ˌkæ.lɪˈfɔr.ni.ən / (AmE), / ˌkæl.ɪˈfɔː.ni.ən / (BrE)
a native or resident of California, a state in the western United States

Examples

When Peter B. Kyne (Pride of Palomar, 43) informed us in 1921 that we had an instinctive dislike for the Japanese, did the heated debates of the Californians settle the truth or falsity of the proposition?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Californian - of or relating to California (adjective)

Etymology

From California + -an (forming demonyms).
