hearts

1. NOUN

/ hɑrts / (AmE), / hɑːts / (BrE)
A card game for usually four players in which the object is to avoid winning tricks containing any hearts or the queen of spades.

Examples

Come on out, let'’s play some Hearts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spades - A card game where players try to win tricks and spades are always trumps

Etymology

Named after the heart suit in playing cards, which is central to the gameplay.
