deadly

1. ADJ

/ ˈdɛdli / (AmE), / ˈdɛdli / (BrE)

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatal - causing death; leading to failure or disaster
  • Synonym: lethal - sufficient to cause death
  • Antonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm or injury

Etymology

From Old English 'dēadlīc', meaning 'mortal' or 'fatal'.
