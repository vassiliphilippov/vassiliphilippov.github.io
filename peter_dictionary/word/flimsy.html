flimsy

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɪmzi / (AmE), / ˈflɪmzi / (BrE)
weak and easily broken or damaged; lacking solidity or strength

Examples

Early equipment was very flimsy; the smallest gusts of wind toppled poles, making communications impossible.

Synset Examples

a flimsy table
flimsy construction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
  • Antonym: sturdy - strongly and solidly built
