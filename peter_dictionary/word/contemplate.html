contemplate

1. VERB

/ ˈkɑːn.təmˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.təm.pleɪt / (BrE)
To think deeply and at length about something, often for spiritual, philosophical, or careful consideration.

Examples

Contemplating these posthumous punishments, Stalin should not lose all hope.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meditate - to focus one's mind for a period of time, especially for reflection or spiritual reasons
  • Synonym: ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
  • Synonym: consider - to think carefully about something, especially in making decisions

Etymology

From Latin contemplari (to observe, contemplate), from templum (temple, sacred space).
