fungal

1. ADJ

/ ˈfʌŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəl / (BrE)
of or relating to fungi

Examples

Bacterial and fungal enzymes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fungus - a member of the group of spore-producing organisms including molds, yeast, mushrooms, and toadstools

Etymology

From Latin fungus (mushroom, fungus) + -al (adjectival suffix).
