unteach

1. VERB

/ ʌnˈtitʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtiːtʃ / (BrE)
to cause someone to unlearn something, especially by teaching them the contrary of what they had learned before

Examples

"And God help anyone who tries to unteach you "!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: teach - to impart knowledge or skill
  • Synonym: unlearn - to discard knowledge or habits previously learned

Etymology

Formed from the prefix 'un-' (reversing the action) + 'teach'.
