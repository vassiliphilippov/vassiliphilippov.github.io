quarterdeck

1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɔrtɚˌdɛk / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtədɛk / (BrE)
the part of a ship's upper deck located near the stern, traditionally reserved for officers and official ceremonies

Examples

Put me down for a quarter deck.

Etymology

From quarter (nautical term for a part of a ship) + deck.
