westerner

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛstərnər / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənə / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from the western part of a region or country, especially the western United States

Examples

People who take us for suckers are like the Westerner who had on exhibit his superior marksmanship in the form of a number of bull's-eye achievements.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: easterner - a person from the eastern part of a country or region, especially the U.S.

Etymology

From 'west' + '-er' (person from), formed by analogy with 'easterner.'
