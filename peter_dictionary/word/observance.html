observance

1. NOUN

/ əbˈzɜrvəns / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːvəns / (BrE)
the act of observing carefully or paying close attention; attentive watching or noticing

Examples

The misplaced modifier is another species more honored in the observance of obscurity than in the breach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: observation - the action or process of closely observing or monitoring

Etymology

From Old French observance, from Latin observantia (act of observing, respect).
