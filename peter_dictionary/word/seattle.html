Seattle

1. PROPN

/ siˈætəl / (AmE), / siˈætəl / (BrE)
A major city and port in west central Washington State, USA, located on Puget Sound; the largest city in Washington and a significant center for aerospace, technology, and education.

Etymology

Named after Chief Si'ahl (anglicized as Seattle), a 19th-century Duwamish and Suquamish chief.
