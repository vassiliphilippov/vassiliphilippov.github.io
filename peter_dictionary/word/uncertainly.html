uncertainly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈsɝtənli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːtənli / (BrE)
In an unsteady or hesitant manner; without confidence or assurance.

Examples

He finished his champagne and climbed uncertainly to the next landing.

Synset Examples

he walked unsteadily toward the exit
The wounded soldier was swinging unsteadily on his legs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unsteadily - in an unsteady manner
  • Antonym: confidently - in a confident or assured manner

Etymology

From 'uncertain' + '-ly'; 'uncertain' from Latin 'certus' (fixed) with prefix 'un-'.
