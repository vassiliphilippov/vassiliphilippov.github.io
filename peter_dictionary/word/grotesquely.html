grotesquely

1. ADV

/ groʊˈtɛskli / (AmE), / grəʊˈtɛskli / (BrE)
In a way that is comically or repulsively ugly or distorted; in a grotesque manner.

Examples

The young man spoke steadily enough, but all at once he looked grotesquely unshaven.

Synset Examples

behind the house lay two nude figures grotesquely bald, with deliberate knife-slashes marking their bodies

Related Terms

  • Synonym: grotesque - comically or repulsively ugly or distorted

Etymology

From 'grotesque' + '-ly'; 'grotesque' from French 'grotesque', from Italian 'grottesca', from 'grotta' (cave).
