unmoved

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈmuːvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmuːvd / (BrE)
not affected or influenced emotionally; showing no visible emotion or reaction

Examples

But explanations leave him confused and unmoved.
He was not unmoved.

Synset Examples

always appeared completely unmoved and imperturbable

Related Terms

  • Antonym: moved - affected emotionally
  • Synonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned
  • Synonym: impassive - not feeling or showing emotion

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'moved' (affected emotionally), based on 'move' (to affect with emotion).
