admix

1. VERB

/ ædˈmɪks / (AmE), / ædˈmɪks / (BrE)
to mix or blend with something else, especially as a minor or additional ingredient

Examples

Occasional tubules contained hyaline casts admixed with neutrophils.

Synset Examples

Hyaline casts were admixed with neutrophils

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mix - to combine or blend substances together

Etymology

From Latin 'admiscere', from 'ad-' (to) + 'miscere' (to mix).
