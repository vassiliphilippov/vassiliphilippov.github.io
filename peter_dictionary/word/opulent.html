opulent

1. ADJ

/ ˈɑː.pjə.lənt / (AmE), / ˈɒp.jʊ.lənt / (BrE)
Richly decorated or luxurious; having or displaying wealth and abundance.

Examples

"Uncle Sam "was, indeed, a rich uncle to Prokofieff, in those opulent, post-war victory years of peace and prosperity, bold speculations and extravaganzas, enjoyment and pleasure: "The Golden Twenties ".

Synset Examples

a princely sum
gilded dining rooms

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxurious - very comfortable, elegant, or enjoyable; especially in a way that involves great expense
  • Synonym: sumptuous - splendid and expensive-looking, lavish
  • Antonym: austere - severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance; lacking luxury

Etymology

From Latin 'opulentus' (wealthy, rich).
