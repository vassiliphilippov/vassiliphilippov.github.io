untrammeled

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈtræməl(d) / (AmE), / ʌnˈtræməld / (BrE)
Not restricted or limited; free and uninhibited.

Examples

Will mere debate on that proposition, even though it be free and untrammeled, remove the dross and leave a residue of refined gold?

Synset Examples

the gift of a fresh eye and an untrammeled curiosity
the untrammeled rush that the snows had shown in the first spring sun

Related Terms

  • Synonym: free - not under control or in restraint
  • Antonym: restricted - confined or limited in scope

Etymology

From 'un-' (not) + 'trammeled', from 'trammel' (a restriction or hindrance), ultimately from Old French 'tramail'.
