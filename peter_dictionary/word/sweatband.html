sweatband

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɛtˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈswɛtˌbænd / (BrE)
A strip of fabric or leather sewn inside the crown of a hat to absorb sweat.

Examples

Inside the crown, stuffed behind the stained sweatband, could be seen thin, crumpled wads of currency.

Etymology

From 'sweat' + 'band', indicating its function to absorb sweat.
