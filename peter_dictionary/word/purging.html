purging

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝːdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of cleansing or removing unwanted substances or elements, especially as a process of purification

Examples

The Aristotelian notion of catharsis, the purging of emotion, is a persistent and viable one.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: purge - to cleanse or remove impurities

Etymology

From 'purge', derived from Latin 'purgare' (to cleanse, purify).
