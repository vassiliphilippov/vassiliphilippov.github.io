outmoded

1. ADJ

/ aʊtˈmoʊdɪd / (AmE), / aʊtˈməʊdɪd / (BrE)
No longer fashionable or useful; out of date.

Examples

The jury said it did find that many of Georgia's registration and election laws "are outmoded or inadequate and often ambiguous ".

Synset Examples

a suit of rather antique appearance
demode (or outmoded) attire
outmoded ideas

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
  • Synonym: antiquated - old-fashioned; outdated
  • Antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
