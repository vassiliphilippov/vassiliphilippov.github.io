  • Degree Higher: day - unit of time equal to 24 hours

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: minute - unit of time equal to one sixtieth of an hour
  • Degree Lower: second - unit of time equal to one sixtieth of a minute
  • Degree Higher: day - period of twenty-four hours

Etymology

From Anglo-Norman *houre*, from Old French *hore*, from Latin *hora* (‘hour, time’), from Ancient Greek *hōra* (‘season, time, hour’).
