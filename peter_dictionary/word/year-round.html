year-round

1. ADJ

/ jɪrˈraʊnd / (AmE), / jɪəˈraʊnd / (BrE)
happening, operating, or available throughout the entire year; not limited to a particular season

Examples

If you have a year-round, full-time job you can n't expect to grow much more than your family uses - unless other members of the family do a good deal of the work or you hire help.

Synset Examples

a year-round resort
a year-round job
