tactile

1. ADJ

/ ˈtæktaɪl / (AmE), / ˈtæktaɪl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the sense of touch; perceptible by touch.

Examples

They can have as much tactile appeal as flesh and bone ".
Next he turned to wax because there was a similarity of wax to marble in tactile quality and translucence.

Synset Examples

haptic data
a tactile reflex

Related Terms

  • Synonym: haptic - relating to the sense of touch

Etymology

From Latin tactilis (tangible), from tangere (to touch).
