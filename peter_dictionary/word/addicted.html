addicted

1. ADJ

/ əˈdɪktɪd / (AmE), / əˈdɪktɪd / (BrE)
Physically or mentally dependent on a habit-forming substance or activity and unable to stop using or doing it.

Examples

Holmes is addicted to the use of cocaine and other refreshing stimulants; he is prone to semi catatonic trances induced by the playing of the vioiln; he is a recluse, an incredible egotist, a confirmed misogynist.

Synset Examples

she is addicted to chocolate
addicted to cocaine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dependent - relying on or requiring something for support
  • Synonym: hooked - informal: very enthusiastic or addicted to a thing or activity
  • Antonym: clean - free from addiction

Etymology

From Latin 'addictus' (bound to, devoted), past participle of 'addicere' (to give over, award).
