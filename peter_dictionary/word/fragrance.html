fragrance

1. NOUN

/ ˈfreɪɡrəns / (AmE), / ˈfreɪɡrəns / (BrE)
A pleasant and distinctive smell, especially one that is sweet or agreeable.

Examples

Cool air moving slowly through the open or smashed out side windows hinted of blooming roadside vegetation, and occasionally a faint fragrance of perfume swirled from the back seat.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aroma - a strong, pleasant smell, often from food or drink
  • Synonym: perfume - a pleasant smell, especially from a substance used to give a pleasant scent

Etymology

From Latin 'fragrantia', meaning 'sweet smell', from 'fragrare' (to smell sweet).
