clearheaded

1. ADJ

/ ˈklɪrˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɪəˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
able to think clearly and sensibly, not mentally confused

Examples

He had been weary; now he felt light and gay and clear-headed, ready for the many actions he saw spreading out before him.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: confused - unable to think clearly or to understand what is happening
  • Synonym: lucid - expressed clearly; easy to understand; showing ability to think clearly

Etymology

Formed from 'clear' + 'headed', with 'headed' meaning 'having a (specified kind of) head or mind'.
