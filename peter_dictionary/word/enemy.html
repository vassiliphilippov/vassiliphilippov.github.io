Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foe - an enemy or opponent, especially in battle
  • Synonym: adversary - one's opponent in a conflict, contest, or dispute
  • Antonym: ally - a state or person cooperating with another for a military or other purpose

Etymology

From Old French *enemi*, from Latin *inimicus* (hostile, unfriendly) — from *in-* (not) + *amicus* (friend).
