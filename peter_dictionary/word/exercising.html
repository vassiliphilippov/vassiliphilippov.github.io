exercising

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛksɚˌsaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛksəˌsaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of performing physical movements or routines in order to maintain or improve fitness or health

Examples

Of course he did some exercising - he's crazy about water skiing and swimming and this vigorous exercise in conjunction with the added food supplements packed pounds of solid muscle on his skinny frame.

Synset Examples

the doctor recommended regular exercise
he did some exercising
the physical exertion required by his work kept him fit

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exercise - physical activity to improve fitness or health

Etymology

Present participle of 'exercise' from Latin 'exercitium' (training).
