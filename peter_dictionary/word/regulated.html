regulated

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
controlled or governed according to rules, principles, or law

Examples

Coffee breaks can be a real headache if not regulated.

Synset Examples

well regulated industries
houses with regulated temperature

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unregulated - not controlled by rules or laws
  • Synonym: controlled - managed or directed by rules or authority

Etymology

From the verb 'regulate', from Latin 'regulatus', past participle of 'regulare' (to regulate), from 'regula' (rule).
