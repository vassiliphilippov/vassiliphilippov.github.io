typify

1. VERB

/ ˈtɪpɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɪpɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to be a typical example or representative of something; to embody the essential characteristics of

Examples

Greasy soils, which are typified by hydrocarbons and fats (esters of glycerol with long chain organic acids).
The other group of antithyroid agents or drugs is typified by thiouracil.

Synset Examples

The fugue typifies Bach's style of composition

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exemplify - to be a typical example of
  • Synonym: characterize - to be characteristic of; to distinguish

Etymology

From Greek 'typos' (impression, figure, type) via French 'typifier'.
