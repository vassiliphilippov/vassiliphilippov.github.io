heavens

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvənz / (AmE), / ˈhɛv.ənz / (BrE)
The sky, especially as the place where the sun, moon, and stars appear to be; the apparent vault of the sky.

Examples

He stood very still, his arms at his sides, staring up at the heavens, then down at the blinking lights below.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sky - the region of the atmosphere and outer space seen from the earth

Etymology

Plural of 'heaven', from Old English 'heofon' (sky, firmament, abode of God).
