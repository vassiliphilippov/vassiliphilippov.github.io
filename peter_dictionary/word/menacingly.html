menacingly

1. ADV

/ ˈmɛnəsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmɛnəsɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows the intention to harm or threaten; in a threatening manner.

Examples

[SPEAKS MENACINGLY] [SPEAKING ITALIAN] I understand.

Synset Examples

the voice at the other end of the line dropped menacingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: threateningly - in a threatening manner
  • Antonym: harmlessly - in a way that causes no harm

Etymology

From 'menacing' + '-ly', where 'menacing' comes from the verb 'menace', from Old French 'manace', from Latin 'minacia' (threats).
