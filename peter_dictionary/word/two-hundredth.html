two-hundredth

1. ADJ

/ tuː ˈhʌndrədθ / (AmE), / tuː ˈhʌndrɪdθ / (BrE)
The ordinal number equal to two hundred in a sequence; 200th.

Examples

We'll celebrate this 200th anniversary proudly and publicly.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: hundredth - the ordinal representing one hundred (100th)
  • Degree Higher: three-hundredth - the ordinal representing three hundred (300th)
