lumpish

1. ADJ

/ ˈlʌm.pɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlʌm.pɪʃ / (BrE)
Slow-witted, mentally sluggish, or dull; lacking liveliness or intelligence.

Examples

In More's Utopia communism is not a means of separating out a warrior elite from the lumpish mass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dull - lacking interest or intelligence; mentally slow
  • Synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or capacity for reasoning
  • Antonym: brilliant - exceptionally clever or talented

Etymology

From 'lump' + '-ish', originally meaning 'like a lump' and later extended to dullness of mind or body.
