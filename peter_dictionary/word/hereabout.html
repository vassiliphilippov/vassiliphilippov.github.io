hereabout

1. ADV

/ ˌhɪrəˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˌhɪərəˈbaʊt / (BrE)
In this general area; near this place.

Examples

A walk and a talk that just wasn't normal around here.

Synset Examples

the people are friendly hereabouts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hereabouts - in this general area, around here

Etymology

From Middle English 'here aboute' (in this vicinity), combining 'here' and 'about'.
