bash

1. NOUN

/ bæʃ / (AmE), / bæʃ / (BrE)
A forceful or vigorous blow, typically delivered with the hand or an object.

Examples

Ought not an edifying Trial have made every effort to demonstrate this once and for all by showing how representative types of "mere "anti-Semites were drawn step by step into the program of skull bashings and gassings?

Synset Examples

the sudden knock floored him
he took a bash right in his face
he got a bang on the head

Related Terms

  • Synonym: knock - a sharp blow or hit
  • Synonym: bang - a sudden loud blow or impact
  • Degree Lower: tap - a light touch or blow
  • Degree Higher: thump - a heavy, dull-sounding blow
