staggering

1. ADJ

/ ˈstæɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstæɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
So surprising or impressive that it shocks or overwhelms you.

Examples

When we consider the electronic industry potential for human betterment, the prospect is staggering.

Synset Examples

such an enormous response was astonishing
an astounding achievement
the amount of money required was staggering
suffered a staggering defeat
the figure inside the boucle dress was stupefying

Related Terms

  • Synonym: astonishing - very surprising; difficult to believe
  • Synonym: astounding - surprisingly impressive or notable
  • Synonym: stupefying - so astonishing that it leaves you unable to think properly

Etymology

From the verb 'stagger', meaning to move unsteadily, from Middle English 'stakeren'.
