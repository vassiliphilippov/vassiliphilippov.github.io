quarterly

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɔrtərli / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəli / (BrE)
happening, produced, or done every quarter of a year; relating to a quarter of a year.

Examples

Corporations react violently to short-range stimuli, e. g., quarterly and annual dividend reports.

Synset Examples

quarterly report

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: monthly - occurring every month
  • Degree Higher: annual - occurring every year
  • Degree Lower: biannual - occurring twice a year

Etymology

From 'quarter' + '-ly', formed in analogy to periods of three months; 'quarter' from Latin 'quartarius' (a fourth part).
