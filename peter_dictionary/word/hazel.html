hazel

1. ADJ

/ ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
of a light brown or yellowish-brown colour, especially used to describe eyes

Examples

The hazel eyes met Alexander's.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: brown - of a dark or medium brown colour
  • Degree Higher: amber - of a yellowish-orange or golden brown colour, especially for eyes

Etymology

From Old English *hæsel* (hazel tree), originally referring to the color of hazel nuts.
