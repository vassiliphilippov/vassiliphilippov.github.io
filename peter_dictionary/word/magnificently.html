magnificently

1. ADV

/ mæɡˈnɪfɪsəntli / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɪfɪsəntli / (BrE)
In an impressive, splendid, or excellent manner.

Examples

There are other good representations of peasants and people of the court by actors who are finely costumed and magnificently photographed in this last of the Russian films to reach this country in the program of joint cultural exchange.
In the fullness of her vocal splendor, however, she could sing the famous scene magnificently.

Synset Examples

he did splendidly in the exam
we got along famously

Related Terms

  • Synonym: splendidly - in a splendid or excellent manner
  • Antonym: poorly - in a poor or inferior manner

Etymology

From "magnificent" + "-ly"; "magnificent" from Latin "magnificus" (grand, noble).
