penciled

1. ADJ

/ ˈpɛnsəld / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsəld / (BrE)
Drawn or written using a pencil.

Examples

Two hours of watching his serenely happy face, listening to his soft humming as he bent over his penciled figures, and Blanche had to leave.

Synset Examples

a penciled sketch
the penciled message

Related Terms

  • Antonym: inked - drawn or written with ink
