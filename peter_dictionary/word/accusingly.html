accusingly

1. ADV

/ əˈkjuːzɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈkjuːzɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows someone believes another person is at fault or to blame.

Examples

Intelligence jabbed at him accusingly.

Synset Examples

he looked at her accusingly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: accuse - to claim someone has done something wrong
