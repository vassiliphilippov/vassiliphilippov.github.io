blasphemous

1. ADJ

/ ˈblæsfəməs / (AmE), / ˈblɑːsfəməs / (BrE)
Showing great disrespect or lack of reverence toward what is considered sacred.

Examples

The jaded amorist conjured up pictures of the blasphemous rites with relish.

Synset Examples

blasphemous rites of a witches' Sabbath
profane utterances against the Church
it is sacrilegious to enter with shoes on

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sacrilegious - grossly irreverent toward the sacred
  • Antonym: reverent - showing deep respect or veneration

Etymology

From Late Latin blasphemosus, from Greek blasphemos (slanderous, profane).
