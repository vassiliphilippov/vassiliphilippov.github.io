dried-out

1. ADJ

/ draɪd aʊt / (AmE), / draɪd aʊt / (BrE)
completely lacking moisture; thoroughly dried

Examples

She had a dried-out quality - a gray, lean woman, not unattractive.

Synset Examples

old boxes of desiccated Cuban cigars
dried-out boards beginning to split

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desiccated - thoroughly dried out; lacking moisture
  • Antonym: moist - slightly or moderately wet; not completely dry
  • Synonym: dry - free from moisture; not wet
