Related Terms

  • Synonym: typical - having the usual features or qualities of a particular type of person or thing
  • Synonym: customary - usual, typical, or expected
  • Antonym: exceptional - unusually good or outstanding, or different from what is usual

Etymology

From Old French 'estandart' (military standard), from Frankish or Germanic origin, originally referring to a flag or emblem, later extended to the idea of an accepted model or norm.
