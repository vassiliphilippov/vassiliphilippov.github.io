mystical

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to mysticism or having a spiritual or supernatural significance beyond ordinary understanding.

Examples

But very mystical too.

Synset Examples

mystical religion

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mystic - having a spiritual meaning or relating to mysticism
  • Synonym: spiritual - relating to the spirit or soul rather than physical things
  • Antonym: worldly - concerned with the material rather than the spiritual

Etymology

From Greek mystikos (having a secret meaning) via Latin and Old French.
