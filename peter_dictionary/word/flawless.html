flawless

1. ADJ

/ ˈflɔləs / (AmE), / ˈflɔːləs / (BrE)
Without any imperfections or faults; perfect.

Examples

His portrayal of an edgy head-in-the-clouds artist is virtually flawless.
Plus flawless skin, smooth brow and cheeks, lips that looked as if you could get a shock from them.

Synset Examples

a flawless gemstone

Related Terms

  • Synonym: perfect - Having all the required or desirable elements; as good as it is possible to be
  • Antonym: imperfect - Not perfect; having faults or errors

Etymology

From 'flaw' + '-less', meaning 'without a flaw'.
