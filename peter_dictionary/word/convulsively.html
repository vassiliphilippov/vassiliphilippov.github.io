convulsively

1. ADV

/ kənˈvʌlsɪvli / (AmE), / kənˈvʌlsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way characterized by sudden, violent, irregular movement or spasms.

Examples

The thin legs twitched convulsively once, then Kate felt the little body stiffening in her arms and heard one strangled sound.

Synset Examples

her leg twitched convulsively

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spasmodically - in a manner characterized by spasms or sudden bursts

Etymology

From 'convulsive' + '-ly', where 'convulsive' is from Latin 'convulsus', past participle of 'convellere' (to tear loose).
