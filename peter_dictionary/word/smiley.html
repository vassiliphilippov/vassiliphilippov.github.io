smiley

1. NOUN

/ ˈsmaɪli / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪli / (BrE)
A representation of a smiling face, especially as an emoticon used in electronic communications.

Examples

Remember... ... put on a happy face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: emoticon - A pictorial representation of a facial expression using keyboard characters

Etymology

From 'smile' + '-y', first used for the simple graphic representation ':' and ')' in early computer culture.
