meticulous

1. ADJ

/ məˈtɪkjələs / (AmE), / məˈtɪkjʊləs / (BrE)
Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.

Examples

I imagine it appealed to his meticulous nature.

Synset Examples

a meticulous craftsman
almost worryingly meticulous in his business formalities

Related Terms

  • Antonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • Synonym: scrupulous - very concerned to avoid doing wrong; extremely attentive to details or morality

Etymology

From Latin 'meticulosus' (fearful, timid), from 'metus' (fear).
