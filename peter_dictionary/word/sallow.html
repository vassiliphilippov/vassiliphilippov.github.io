sallow

1. ADJ

/ ˈsæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaləʊ / (BrE)
(of a person's face or complexion) having an unhealthy yellow or pale brown color.

Examples

Above the tongue, an aged yellow eye, sallow and time-cast, encrusted within a sphere of marbleized pink skin, stared unfalteringly at him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pallid - pale, typically because of poor health
  • Synonym: livid - discolored, as from a bruise; pale

Etymology

Old English *saluw* (dusky, yellowish), related to Old High German *salawa* (greyish, dirty yellow).
