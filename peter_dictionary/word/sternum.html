sternum

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɝː.nəm / (AmE), / ˈstɜː.nəm / (BrE)
the flat bone at the front center of the chest that connects with the clavicles and the first seven pairs of ribs

Examples

The bone of the vertebral bodies, ribs, and sternum was soft and was easily compressed.

Etymology

From Latin *sternum* (chest bone), from Greek *sternon* (chest).
