axially

1. ADV

/ ˈæksiəli / (AmE), / ˈæksiəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or along an axis.

Examples

The transpiring gas ejected from the anode formed a jet directed axially towards the cathode below.

Synset Examples

the jet was directed axially toward the cathode

Etymology

From axial + -ly, axial from axis (Latin axis).
