Coca-Cola

1. NOUN

/ ˌkoʊ.kə ˈkoʊ.lə / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.kə ˈkəʊ.lə / (BrE)
A trademarked carbonated soft drink with a cola flavor, produced by The Coca-Cola Company.

Examples

When Johnson ejaculated "Howsabout my buying us all a nice cold Co-cola, Ma' am "?

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Pepsi - brand of carbonated cola soft drink produced by PepsiCo

Etymology

From Coca (the coca plant, an original ingredient) and Cola (the kola nut, another key ingredient).
