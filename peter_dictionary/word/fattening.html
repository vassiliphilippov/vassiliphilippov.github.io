fattening

1. ADJ

/ ˈfætənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfætənɪŋ / (BrE)
(of animals) being fed or kept in order to increase body weight, typically before slaughter.

Examples

Improves growth rate and feed efficiency of fattening beef animals.

Synset Examples

a fattening hog
fattening pens

Related Terms

  • Antonym: lean - not containing much fat; not fattened (of animals)
