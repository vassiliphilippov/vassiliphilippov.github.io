comer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmə / (BrE)
A person who is likely to succeed or become important in the future.

Examples

The engineer had more than seven years of experience in the firm, was well trained, was considered a hard worker, was respected by his fellow engineers for his technical competence and was regarded as a "comer ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rising star - a person who is rapidly gaining prominence or success
  • Synonym: up-and-comer - a person who is gaining recognition and is expected to succeed
