scarred

1. ADJ

/ skɑrd / (AmE), / skɑːd / (BrE)
Marked or damaged by scars, injuries, or rough use.

Examples

He stared at the clerk who sat at a scarred and ancient fumed-oak desk stuffing envelopes.

Synset Examples

the scarred piano bench
walls marred by graffiti

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marred - damaged or spoiled to a certain extent; made less perfect

Etymology

From 'scar' + '-ed' (past participle/adj. suffix).
