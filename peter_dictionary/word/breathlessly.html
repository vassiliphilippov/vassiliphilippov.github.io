breathlessly

1. ADV

/ ˈbrɛθləsli / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows excitement, effort, or anticipation and a lack of breath.

Examples

Presently his water brother said breathlessly.

Synset Examples

she spoke breathlessly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excitedly - in an excited manner

Etymology

From 'breathless' + '-ly', where 'breathless' derives from 'breath' + '-less'.
