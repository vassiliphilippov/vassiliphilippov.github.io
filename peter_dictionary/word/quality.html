1. NOUN

/ ˈkwɑləti / (AmE), / ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
A distinctive attribute, characteristic, or property that someone or something possesses.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trait - a distinguishing characteristic or quality, especially of one's personal nature
  • Synonym: characteristic - a feature or quality belonging typically to a person, place, or thing and serving to identify it

Etymology

From Latin qualitas, from qualis meaning 'of what kind'.
