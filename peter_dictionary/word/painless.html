painless

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnləs / (BrE)
Requiring little effort, trouble, or difficulty; not causing hardship or inconvenience.

Examples

Even the loss of hotel business to the outskirt's motel has been relatively painless; the hotel-motel demarcation is becoming harder to find every year.

Synset Examples

a painless solution to the problem

Related Terms

  • Synonym: easy - requiring little effort or difficulty
  • Synonym: effortless - requiring no effort; achieved with ease
  • Antonym: difficult - needing much effort or skill to accomplish

Etymology

From pain + -less, meaning 'without pain'; sense extended to lack of difficulty or hardship.
