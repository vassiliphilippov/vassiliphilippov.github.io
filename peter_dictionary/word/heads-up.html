heads-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛdzˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈhɛdzˌʌp / (BrE)
an advance warning or piece of information about something that may happen

Examples

Heads up.

Synset Examples

he sent a heads-up to the District Attorney

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warning - a statement or event that warns of something
  • Synonym: alert - a warning that something may happen

Etymology

Originally from the phrase 'heads up!' meaning to be alert or pay attention.
