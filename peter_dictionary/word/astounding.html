astounding

1. ADJ

/ əˈstaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈstaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
causing great surprise or amazement; so extraordinary as to be almost unbelievable

Examples

Now when Henri was just 12 he was only 4' 10 "tall and weighed an astounding 72 pounds, and his greatest desire was to pack on some weight.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amazing - causing great surprise or wonder
  • Synonym: remarkable - worthy of attention; striking
  • Antonym: ordinary - with no special or distinctive features; normal

Etymology

Present participle of 'astound', from Middle English 'astonden', meaning to astonish, from Old French 'estoner'.
