bunny

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʌni / (AmE), / ˈbʌni / (BrE)
(informal) A young rabbit, often used affectionately or as a term for a rabbit in general.

Examples

Honey Bunny, you'’re going to break your eardrums.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rabbit - a small, furry mammal with long ears

Etymology

Diminutive form of 'bun', an old dialect word for a rabbit.
