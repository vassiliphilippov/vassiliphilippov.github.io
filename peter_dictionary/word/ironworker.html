ironworker

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪərnˌwɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈaɪənˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or install articles or structures made of iron, especially in construction.

Examples

There I was a retired wobbly and structural iron worker who 'd never gouged a cent off a fellow worker in my thirty years in the movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blacksmith - a person who makes and repairs things in iron by hand
  • Synonym: steelworker - a person who works in the production or installation of steel

Etymology

Formed from 'iron' and 'worker', denoting someone who works with iron.
