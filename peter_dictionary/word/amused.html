amused

1. ADJ

/ əˈmjuzd / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzd / (BrE)
finding something entertaining or enjoyable; showing pleasure at something funny or interesting

Examples

"I 'll get my references in order ", Needham said, and though he spoke with a smile, Casey somehow got the idea that he was not particularly amused.

Synset Examples

We are not amused

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bored - feeling weary and uninterested
  • Synonym: entertained - experiencing amusement or enjoyment

Etymology

From the verb 'amuse', which comes from Old French 'amuser' (to amuse, to cause to muse).
