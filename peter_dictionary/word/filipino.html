Filipino

1. NOUN

/ ˌfɪlɪˈpinoʊ / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A native or inhabitant of the Philippines.

Examples

When we got there, Trig and the Negro were quarreling over possession of a gold crucifix around the neck of a wounded Filipino.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Philippine - of or relating to the Philippines or its people

Etymology

From Spanish 'Filipino', from 'Filipinas' (the Philippines), named after King Philip II of Spain.
