inspection

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈspɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɛkʃən / (BrE)
a formal or official examination of something, especially to determine its condition or compliance with regulations

Examples

The doctor opened the smallest of his cases, an unimposing straw bag, and exposed the contents for Alex's inspection.
Folks around this joint love surprise inspections.

Synset Examples

the platoon stood ready for review
we had to wait for the inspection before we could use the elevator

Related Terms

  • Synonym: examination - a detailed inspection or investigation
  • Synonym: review - a formal assessment or inspection

Etymology

From Latin 'inspectio' (a looking into), from 'inspicere' (to look into).
