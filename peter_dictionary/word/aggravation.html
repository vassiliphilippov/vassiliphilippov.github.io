aggravation

1. NOUN

/ ˌæɡrəˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæɡrəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
A feeling of intense annoyance or exasperation.

Examples

Can't stand the aggravation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annoyance - a feeling of being annoyed or irritated
  • Synonym: irritation - a feeling of being annoyed, especially continuously or repeatedly
  • Antonym: relief - a feeling of reassurance and relaxation following release from anxiety or distress

Etymology

From Latin aggravatio (a making heavier), from aggravare (to make heavier, irritate).
