knowledgeable

1. ADJ

/ ˈnɑːlɪdʒəbəl / (AmE), / ˈnɒlɪdʒəbəl / (BrE)
having or showing knowledge, especially through extensive education or experience

Examples

This may both divert the attention of the uninitiate and cause confusion for the more knowledgeable.

Synset Examples

knowing instructors
a knowledgeable critic
a knowledgeable audience

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness
  • Synonym: informed - having or showing knowledge of a particular subject

Etymology

From 'knowledge' + '-able', formed in English.
