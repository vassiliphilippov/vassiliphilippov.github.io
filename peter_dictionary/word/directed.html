directed

1. ADJ

/ dəˈrɛktɪd, daɪˈrɛktɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktɪd, daɪˈrɛktɪd / (BrE)
Having a specified or particular direction; oriented toward a certain point or goal.

Examples

The transpiring gas ejected from the anode formed a jet directed axially towards the cathode below.

Synset Examples

a positively directed vector
goal-directed

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undirected - not having a particular direction; lacking specific orientation

Etymology

Past participle of 'direct', from Latin 'dirigere' (to set straight).
