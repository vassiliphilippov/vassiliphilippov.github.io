liberated

1. ADJ

/ ˈlɪbəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪbəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a gas or other substance) released from chemical combination.

Examples

After titration of the liberated * * f with * * f, aliquots of the aqueous and of the organic phase were counted in a solution type Geiger tube.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: released - allowed to escape or set free
  • Antonym: bound - chemically combined or held within a compound

Etymology

From Latin 'liberare' meaning 'to set free'.
