beefy

1. ADJ

/ ˈbiːfi / (AmE), / ˈbiːfi / (BrE)
(of a person) muscular and heavily built.

Examples

He was a big thick beefy violent man.

Synset Examples

a beefy wrestler
had a tall burly frame
clothing sizes for husky boys
a strapping boy of eighteen
`buirdly' is a Scottish term

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burly - large and strong; heavily built
  • Synonym: husky - strong and heavily built
  • Synonym: strapping - tall, strong, and healthy
  • Antonym: slight - (of a person) small and thin

Etymology

From "beef" (meat of a cow) + "-y" (adjective-forming suffix).
