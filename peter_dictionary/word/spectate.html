spectate

1. VERB

/ ˈspɛkteɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɛkteɪt / (BrE)
To watch an event, especially a sports contest, as a spectator.

Examples

We'll go to the city, see a show and have dinner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch - to look at or observe attentively

Etymology

Back-formation from 'spectator', which is from Latin 'spectator' (observer).
