grace

1. NOUN

/ ɡreɪs / (AmE), / ɡreɪs / (BrE)
(in Christian theology) The free and unearned favor of God, especially as manifested in the salvation of sinners and the granting of blessings.

3. NOUN

/ ɡreɪs / (AmE), / ɡreɪs / (BrE)
Polite and considerate behaviour; a sense of propriety and courtesy towards others.

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: rudeness - lack of manners or consideration; impolite behaviour

Etymology
