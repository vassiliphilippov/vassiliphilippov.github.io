well-wishing

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛl ˌwɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛl ˌwɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or expression of wishing someone well or expressing good will

Examples

It takes place in the household, a rite of well wishing for myriad occasions - for the traveler, a wedding, a newborn child, the sick, the New Year, for any good purpose.
MAN: Happy New Year!

Synset Examples

much hand-shaking and well-wishing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blessing - words or gestures expressing a wish for someone's well-being; invocation of good fortune
  • Synonym: congratulation - expression of pleasure at another person's success or good fortune

Etymology

From well (good, satisfactorily) + wishing (present participle of wish).
