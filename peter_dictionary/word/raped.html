raped

1. ADJ

/ reɪpt / (AmE), / reɪpt / (BrE)
(of a place or thing) subjected to severe destruction, exploitation, or violation, especially by force or violence.

Examples

The raped valley was a pregnant womb awaiting abortion.

Synset Examples

the raped countryside

Related Terms

  • Synonym: despoiled - having been robbed or stripped of value
  • Synonym: plundered - having been looted or pillaged
