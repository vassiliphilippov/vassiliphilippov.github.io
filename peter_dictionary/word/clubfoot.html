clubfoot

1. NOUN

/ ˈklʌb.fʊt / (AmE), / ˈklʌb.fʊt / (BrE)
A congenital deformity of the foot, in which the foot is twisted out of its normal position, often with a curled shape or twisted ankle, heel, and toes.

Examples

With his club foot he might well be grateful.

Etymology

From 'club' (describing the twisted, club-like appearance) + 'foot'.
