schoolmarm

1. NOUN

/ ˈskuːlˌmɑrm / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌmɑːm / (BrE)
A woman schoolteacher, especially one regarded as strict or old-fashioned.

Examples

A comedy in Three Acts ", in which, under "Personages ", Henrietta appeared as "A Schoolmarm ", and Bertha, who was only a trifle less brilliant in high school than Henrietta had been, appeared as "Dummkopf ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: schoolteacher - person who teaches in a school

Etymology

From 'school' + 'marm' (a colloquial term for 'madam').
