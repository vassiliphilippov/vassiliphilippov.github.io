chosen

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃoʊzən / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊzən / (BrE)
A person who is selected as the favorite or given special preference over others.

Examples

He was Papa's chosen; therefore, nothing but good could happen to him, even in God's wrathful storms.
We also have, as a special added attraction, the Sierra Boys Choir who have chosen a certain special song and a special arrangement to honor their host, Mr. Michael Corleone.

Synset Examples

she was Mama's chosen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: favorite - person especially liked or preferred
  • Synonym: pick - person chosen or selected from a group

Etymology

Past participle of the verb 'choose', from Old English *ceosan*.
