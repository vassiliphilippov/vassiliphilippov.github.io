enzymatic

1. ADJ

/ ˌɛnzaɪˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛnzaɪˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by an enzyme.

Examples

There is so far no evidence to indicate conclusively that this coupling is under enzymatic control.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enzyme - a substance produced by a living organism that acts as a catalyst in biochemical reactions

Etymology

From enzyme + -atic, patterned after enzymatisch in German.
