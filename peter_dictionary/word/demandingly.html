demandingly

1. ADV

/ dɪˈmændɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːndɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lot of demands or requires much effort or attention.

Examples

Sometimes this leads to his becoming demandingly dominant in marriage.

Synset Examples
