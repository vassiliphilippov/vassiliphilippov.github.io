outing

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
a short trip or excursion taken for pleasure, often by a group

Examples

It appears that an Orthodox girl in the community disrupted plans for an outing sponsored by one of the Jewish service groups because she would not travel on Saturday and, in addition, required kosher food.

Synset Examples

many summer excursions to the shore
it was merely a pleasure trip
after cautious sashays into the field

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excursion - a short journey or trip, especially one engaged in as a leisure activity
  • Synonym: trip - a journey or outing, typically for leisure

Etymology

From 'out' + '-ing', meaning the act of going out (late 19th century).
