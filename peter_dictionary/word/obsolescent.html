obsolescent

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑb.səˈlɛs.ənt / (AmE), / ˌɒb.səˈlɛs.ənt / (BrE)
Becoming outdated or no longer used; in the process of becoming obsolete.

Examples

Some weapons systems have become obsolescent while still in production, and some while still under development.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date

Etymology

From Latin obsolescent-, stem of obsolescens, present participle of obsolescere (to fall into disuse).
