unusual

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈjuːʒuəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈjuːʒʊəl / (BrE)
not common, rare, or different from what is usual or expected

Examples

He has given only the one pass in his 27 innings, an unusual characteristic for a southpaw.

Synset Examples

two-career families are no longer unusual

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rare - not occurring often; uncommon
  • Antonym: common - occurring often; usual
  • Antonym: usual - normal; commonly occurring

Etymology

From un- (not) + usual, based on Latin usualis (ordinary, customary).

not common, ordinary, or typical; out of the ordinary and unexpected, sometimes slightly odd or strange

Examples

Synset Examples
