observational

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑbzərˈveɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃənl / (BrE)
Based on or involving observation or experiment, especially as a scientific method.

Examples

The observational data determining the concentration distribution have a range of error which is magnified in the extension into the micrometeorite region.

Synset Examples

experimental results that supported the hypothesis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: experimental - based on untested ideas or using experiments

Etymology

From observation + -al; ultimately from Latin observatio (observation).
