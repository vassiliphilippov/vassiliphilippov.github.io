stealthily

1. ADV

/ ˈstɛlθɪli / (AmE), / ˈstɛlθɪli / (BrE)
In a stealthy manner; so as to avoid being seen or heard.

Examples

Thus, stealthily they advanced upstream; then they turned to the right, climbed the embankment, and walked into the valley again.

Synset Examples

stealthily they advanced upstream

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretly - in a secret manner
  • Antonym: openly - in an open manner, not concealed

Etymology

From 'stealthy' + '-ly', with 'stealthy' from Middle English 'stelthe' (theft, act of stealing).
