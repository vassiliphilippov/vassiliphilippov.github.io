telecast

1. VERB

/ ˈtɛlɪˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌkɑːst / (BrE)
to broadcast (a program or event) by television

Examples

[MAN SPEAKING INDISTINCTLY ON TV] MAN: [ON TV] ...pick up of two by Holden.

Synset Examples

The Royal wedding was televised

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broadcast - to transmit (a program or some information) by radio or television

Etymology

Blend of 'television' and 'broadcast', first used in the mid-20th century.
