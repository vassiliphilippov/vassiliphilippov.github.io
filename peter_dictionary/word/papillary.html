papillary

1. ADJ

/ ˈpæpɪˌlɛri / (AmE), / ˈpapɪləri / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a papilla, often referring to small, nipple-like projections in biological or medical contexts.

Examples

In addition, there were 2 small papillary adenomas in the right kidney.

Etymology

From Latin papilla (nipple, small projection) + -ary (relating to).
