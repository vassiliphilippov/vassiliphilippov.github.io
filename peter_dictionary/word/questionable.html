questionable

1. ADJ

/ ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nə.bəl / (BrE)
Open to doubt or suspicion; likely to be doubted or questioned as to truth or validity.

Examples

Thereafter the audience waxed applause happy, but discriminating operagoers reserved judgment as her singing showed signs of strain, her musicianship some questionable procedure and her acting uncomfortable stylization.
That is questionable, to say the least.

Synset Examples

questionable motives
a questionable reputation
a fire of questionable origin

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dubious - hesitating or doubting; not to be relied upon
  • Antonym: indisputable - impossible to doubt or question

Etymology

From Middle English, from Old French 'questionnable', from 'questionner' (to question).
