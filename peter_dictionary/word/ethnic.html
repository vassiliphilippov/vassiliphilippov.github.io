ethnic

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛθ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɪk / (BrE)
Relating to a group of people who share a common cultural tradition, language, or heritage.

Examples

My interviews with teen-agers confirmed this portrait of the weakening of religious and ethnic bonds.
When I pressed for a purely religious definition, I encountered the familiar blend of liberal piety, interfaith good will, and a small residue of ethnic loyalty.
For most Brooklyn College students, college is at once a perpetuation of their ethnic attachments and a breaking away from the cage of neighborhood and family.

Synset Examples

influenced by ethnic and cultural ties
ethnic food

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cultural - relating to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society
  • Synonym: racial - relating to race or large groups of people classified by physical characteristics

Etymology

From Greek *ethnikos* (national, foreign, gentile), from *ethnos* (nation, people).
