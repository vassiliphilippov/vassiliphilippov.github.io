manoeuvre

1. NOUN

/ məˈnuːvər / (AmE), / məˈnjuːvə / (BrE)
an action or movement carefully planned and executed to achieve a particular purpose, especially to gain an advantage

Examples

It's the smart move.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tactic - a plan or action for achieving a particular goal
  • Synonym: strategy - a plan of action designed to achieve a long-term aim

Etymology

From French 'manœuvre', from Latin 'manu operari' (to work by hand).
