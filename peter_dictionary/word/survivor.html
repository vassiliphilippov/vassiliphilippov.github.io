survivor

1. NOUN

/ sərˈvaɪvər / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvə / (BrE)
A person who remains alive after others have died, especially after an event such as a war, accident, or illness.

Examples

He did this by the charming practice of buying up used electric blankets for $ 5 to $ 10 from survivors of patients who had died, reconditioning them, and selling them at $ 185 each.

Synset Examples

he left his farm to his survivors

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bereaved - a person who has lost a loved one by death

Etymology

From survive + -or; ultimately from Latin survivere (to live beyond).

2. NOUN

/ sərˈvaɪvər / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvə / (BrE)
An animal that continues to live or exist, especially after coming through hardship or adversity.

Examples

The survivors emerge on some nice, sunny day in March or April, when the temperature is close to 50 ` F and there is not too much wind.

Synset Examples

only the fittest animals were survivors of the cold winters

Related Terms

  • Antonym: victim - an animal that does not survive adversity or is harmed or killed

Etymology

From Latin 'supervivere' (to live beyond).

3. NOUN

/ sərˈvaɪvɚ / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvə / (BrE)
a person who continues to live after an event in which others have died or suffered serious harm

Examples

It is not that the facts of the persecution were unavailable; most of the information elicited in Jerusalem had been brought to the surface by the numerous War Crimes tribunals and investigating commissions, and by reports, memoirs, and survivors' accounts.
For hundreds of years, the evidence available consisted of (1) the captain's fragmentary journal, (2) a highly prejudiced account by one of the survivors, (3) a note found in a dead man's desk on board, and (4) several second-hand reports.
By fall, 443 survivors of this arduous journey were clustered about Fort Snelling, but most of them were sent on to Galena and St. Louis, with a few going as far as Vevay, Indiana, a notable Swiss center in the United States.
They're great survivors.

Synset Examples

the survivors of the fire were taken to a hospital

Related Terms

  • Antonym: victim - a person harmed or killed by an event or action

Etymology

From survive + -or, modeled on Latin patterns for agent nouns.
