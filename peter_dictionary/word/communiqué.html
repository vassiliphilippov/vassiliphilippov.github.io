communiqué

1. NOUN

/ kəˌmuː.nɪˈkeɪ / (AmE), / kɒˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ / (BrE)
an official announcement or statement, especially one made to the media or public by a government or other organization

Examples

Then epistolatory me was a foreign correspondent dispatching exciting cables and communiques, full of dash and wit and glamor, quoting from the books I read, imitating the grand styles of the authors recommended by a teacher in whose special, after school class I was enrolled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dispatch - an official report or message, especially sent quickly

Etymology

From French 'communiqué' (communicated), past participle of 'communiquer' (to communicate).
