participant

1. NOUN

/ pərˈtɪs.ə.pənt / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪs.ɪ.pənt / (BrE)
a person who takes part in an activity or event

Examples

For we have said, in effect, that of the two alternatives to his position variously represented by the other participants in the demythologizing discussion, only one is really an alternative.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attendee - a person who is present at an event or activity
  • Antonym: observer - a person who watches an event or activity without directly taking part

Etymology

From Latin participans, present participle of participare (to share in).
