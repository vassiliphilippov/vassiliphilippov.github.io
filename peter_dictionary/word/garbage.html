garbage

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ / (BrE)
Waste food and other refuse, especially as discarded from a kitchen.

Examples

And little Zeme North, a Dora with real spirit and verve, was fascinating whether she was singing of her love for Floyd, the cop who becomes sewer commissioner and then is promoted into garbage, or just dancing to display her exuberant feelings.
A covered container such as a kitchen garbage pail might do as a toilet.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rubbish - waste material; unwanted things or refuse
  • Synonym: trash - waste material or things that are no longer wanted or needed
  • Degree Higher: compost - decayed organic material used as a fertilizer

Etymology

Possibly from Anglo-Norman *garbage* (offal, the scraps from a fowl, kitchen waste).
