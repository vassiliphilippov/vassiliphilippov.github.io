airflow

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛrˌfloʊ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌfləʊ / (BrE)
the movement or flow of air, especially as it moves through, around, or past something

Examples

However, the dynamics of airflow, from morphologic considerations alone, may conceivably be different in the monkey than in the horse.

Synset Examples

she adjusted the fan so that the airflow was directed right at her

Related Terms

  • Synonym: draught - a current of air, especially one moving through an enclosed space
