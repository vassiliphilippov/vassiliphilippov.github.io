cornmeal

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnˌmiːl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌmiːl / (BrE)
Coarsely ground meal made from dried corn, used in cooking and baking.

Examples

She doled out what Glendora vaguely guessed were the right amounts of dried peas, eggs, cornmeal, a little salt.

Etymology

From 'corn' + 'meal', referring to meal (flour) made from corn (maize).
