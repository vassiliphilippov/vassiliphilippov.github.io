hardworking

1. ADJ

/ ˈhɑrdˌwɝːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌhɑːdˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Putting a lot of effort and care into work; industrious and diligent.

Examples

Some members of the bee family have become idlers, social parasites that live at the expense of their hardworking relatives.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: industrious - willing to work hard; diligent
  • Antonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy

Etymology

Formed from 'hard' + 'working', meaning working with great effort.
