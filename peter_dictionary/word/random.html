random

1. ADJ

/ ˈrændəm / (AmE), / ˈrændəm / (BrE)
Done, chosen, or occurring without a specific plan, order, or purpose; determined by chance rather than design.

Etymology

From Middle English 'random', from Old French 'randon' (force, impetus, haste), from 'randoner' (to run fast, gallop).
