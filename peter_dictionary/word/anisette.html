anisette

1. NOUN

/ ˌænɪˈsɛt / (AmE), / ˌænɪˈsɛt / (BrE)
A colorless, sweet liqueur flavored with aniseed, often served as an aperitif.

Examples

Anisette.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ouzo - an anise-flavored Greek liqueur
  • Synonym: sambuca - an Italian anise-flavored liqueur

Etymology

From French anisette, diminutive of anis (anise).
