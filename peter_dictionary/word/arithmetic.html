arithmetic

1. NOUN

/ əˈrɪθmətɪk / (AmE), / əˈrɪθmətɪk / (BrE)
the branch of mathematics concerned with the study and calculation of numbers, including operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: mathematics - the abstract science of number, quantity, and space
  • Degree Higher: algebra - branch of mathematics dealing with symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols
  • Degree Higher: geometry - branch of mathematics concerned with the properties of space and figures

Etymology

From Old French arismetique, from Latin arithmetica, from Greek arithmētikē (the art of counting), from arithmos (number).
