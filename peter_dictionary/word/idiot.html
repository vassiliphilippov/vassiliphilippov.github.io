idiot

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪdiət / (AmE), / ˈɪdiət / (BrE)
A person considered to be very stupid or lacking in intelligence.

Examples

He knew at a glance that the biggest sandals belonged to Niobe, the neatest ones to Concetta, and the laced ones to Romeo, Concetta's idiot brother.
I'’ll look like an idiot.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fool - a person lacking good sense or judgment
  • Antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual ability

Etymology

From Greek idiōtēs (private person, layman, ignorant person).
