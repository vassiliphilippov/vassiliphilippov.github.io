statesmanlike

1. ADJ

/ ˈsteɪts.mən.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈsteɪts.mən.laɪk / (BrE)
Having the qualities or abilities characteristic of a statesman; displaying wisdom, diplomacy, and leadership.

Examples

But to imitate an opponent when he has made the mistake of his life would be a new high in statesmanlike folly.

Synset Examples

a man of statesmanlike judgment
a statesmanlike solution of the present perplexities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diplomatic - having or showing skill in dealing with people or sensitive matters

Etymology

From 'statesman' + '-like', indicating resemblance.
