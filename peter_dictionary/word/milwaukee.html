Milwaukee

1. PROPN

/ mɪlˈwɑki / (AmE), / mɪlˈwɔːki / (BrE)
a city in southeastern Wisconsin, United States, located on the western shore of Lake Michigan, known for its brewing industry

Examples

The Giants who had been anemic with the bat in their windy Candlestick Park suddenly found the formula in Milwaukee's park.

Etymology

Named after the Milwaukee River; likely from Algonquian languages, possibly Potawatomi 'minwaki' (good land).
