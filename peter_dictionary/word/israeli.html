Israeli

1. NOUN

/ ɪzˈreɪli / (AmE), / ɪzˈreɪli / (BrE)
A native or citizen of Israel.

Examples

In southern Europe, between the Israeli Republics and the Icelandic speaking peoples of northern Europe, was a thin but long stretch of territory called March.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Palestinian - a native or inhabitant of Palestine

Etymology

From Israel, the name of the country, plus the suffix -i denoting nationality.
