glutton

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡlʌtən / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌtən / (BrE)
A person who habitually eats and drinks to excess; someone with an insatiable appetite for food or drink.

Examples

Pansies are gluttons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gourmand - a person who enjoys eating and often eats too much
  • Antonym: moderate eater - a person who eats in moderation

Etymology

From Latin 'glutto' (glutton), from 'gluttire' (to gulp down, swallow).
