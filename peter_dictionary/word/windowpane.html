windowpane

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndoʊˌpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊˌpeɪn / (BrE)
a single pane of glass set into a window frame

Examples

The walls bulged, the floor trembled, the windowpanes rattled.
He looked out through windowpanes turned a faint violet by sun and weather, looked out at King's Bridge toward Westchester.

Synset Examples

the ball shattered the window

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pane - a sheet of glass in a window or door

Etymology

From 'window' + 'pane', the latter from Middle English 'pan' (section, piece of glass).
