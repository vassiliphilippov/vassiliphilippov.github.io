malfunction

1. NOUN

/ mælˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / mælˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
A failure to function properly or normally.

Examples

What is your malfunction, you fat barrel of monkey spunk?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: breakdown - a sudden failure of a machine or system
  • Antonym: function - an action or activity proper to a person or thing

Etymology

From Latin 'male' (badly) + 'function' (to perform, execute).
