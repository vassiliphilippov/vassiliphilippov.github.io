artfully

1. ADV

/ ˈɑrt.fəli / (AmE), / ˈɑːt.fəli / (BrE)
In a skillful or clever manner, often showing creativity or cunning.

Examples

Her tanned foot, whose arch swept high and white, pointed artfully toward tapering toes - toes like fingers, whose tips glowed white.

Synset Examples

her foot pointed artfully toward tapering toes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skillfully - in a skillful manner
  • Antonym: clumsily - in a clumsy or awkward manner

Etymology

From 'artful' + '-ly'; 'artful' from 'art' (skill) + '-ful'.
