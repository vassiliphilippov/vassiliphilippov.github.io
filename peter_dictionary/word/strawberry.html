strawberry

1. NOUN

/ ˈstrɔˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːb(ə)ri / (BrE)
A sweet, soft, red fruit with tiny yellow seeds on its surface, often eaten raw or used in desserts.

Examples

Like strawberries in December, pansies are far more exciting in February than in May.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: berry - any small, pulpy, and often edible fruit

Etymology

From Old English 'streawberige', combining 'streaw' (straw) and 'berige' (berry).
