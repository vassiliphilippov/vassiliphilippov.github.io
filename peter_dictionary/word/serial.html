serial

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl / (BrE)
Occurring or arranged in a regular sequence, especially immediately one after another, without gaps.

Examples

Serial serum potassium levels remained normal; the serum glutamic oxaloacetic transaminase was 10 units per ml. per min..

Synset Examples

serial concerts

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consecutive - following continuously; in unbroken or logical sequence

Etymology

From Latin 'serialis', from 'series' (row, succession).
