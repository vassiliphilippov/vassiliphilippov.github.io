bestowal

1. NOUN

/ bɪˈstoʊ.əl / (AmE), / bɪˈstəʊ.əl / (BrE)
The act of giving, conferring, or presenting something, especially an honor or a gift.

Examples

They speak of the work of Christ as the bestowal of incorruptibility, which can mean (though it does not have to mean) deliverance from time and history.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gift - something given voluntarily to another
  • Synonym: presentation - the formal act of giving or presenting something

Etymology

From 'bestow' + -al, formed in English.
