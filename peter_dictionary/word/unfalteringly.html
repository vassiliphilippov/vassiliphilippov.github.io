unfalteringly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈfɔltərɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːltərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that does not weaken, hesitate, or lose determination; steadily and resolutely.

Examples

Above the tongue, an aged yellow eye, sallow and time-cast, encrusted within a sphere of marbleized pink skin, stared unfalteringly at him.

Synset Examples

he clung to the past determinedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determinedly - in a determined manner
  • Antonym: hesitatingly - in a way that shows hesitation

Etymology

From 'unfaltering' + '-ly', where 'un-' means not, and 'falter' from Middle English 'falteren', to waver or be unsteady.
