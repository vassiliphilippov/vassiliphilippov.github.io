theatregoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈθiət̬ɚˌɡoʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈθiətəˌɡəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A person who regularly attends the theater to watch plays or performances.

Examples

For the last two years, this frontier of the arts has produced a number of so-called "non dramas "which have left indelible, bittersweet impressions on the psyche of this veteran theatregoer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: playgoer - person who attends plays at the theater

Etymology

Formed from 'theatre' + 'goer' (one who goes to); modeled after 'moviegoer'.
