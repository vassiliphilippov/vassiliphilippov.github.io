hardhearted

1. ADJ

/ ˌhɑrdˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːdˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
Lacking compassion, kindness, or pity; unfeeling or unsympathetic.

Examples

To show you that I'm not a hard-hearted man.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: kindhearted - having a kind and sympathetic nature
  • Synonym: callous - showing or having an insensitive and cruel disregard for others

Etymology

From 'hard' + 'hearted', meaning having a hard heart (figuratively unfeeling or unsympathetic).
