gospeler

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑːspələr / (AmE), / ˈɡɒspələ / (BrE)
A preacher or proponent of the Christian gospel.

Examples

He started down the steps to meet the near-blind preacher, who had been one of the early Gospelers in Paris.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: evangelist - a person who seeks to convert others to the Christian faith, especially by public preaching

Etymology

From 'gospel' + '-er', formed in English.
