entertainer

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛn.t̬ɚˈteɪ.nɚ / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈteɪ.nə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to amuse or interest an audience, such as a singer, comedian, or actor.

Examples

He has also enjoyed a successful career as an entertainer (his records have sold in the millions) and is a sharp businessman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: performer - person who performs for an audience, especially on stage

Etymology

From entertain + -er, meaning one who entertains.
