streaming

1. ADJ

/ ˈstriːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstriːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of the eyes, face, etc.) producing tears or another bodily fluid in large, continuous amounts, as if flowing in a stream.

Examples

One hebephrenic woman often became submerged in what felt to me like a somehow phony experience of pseudo emotion, during which, despite her wracking sobs and streaming cheeks, I felt only a cold annoyance with her.

Synset Examples

his streaming face
her streaming eyes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weeping - shedding tears; crying
  • Degree Lower: trickling - flowing or seeping slowly in drops or stream
