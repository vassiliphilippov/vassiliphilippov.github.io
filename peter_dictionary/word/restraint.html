restraint

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈstreɪnt / (AmE), / rɪˈstreɪnt / (BrE)
the act of limiting, controlling, or holding back someone or something; a way of preventing free action or movement

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: constraint - a limitation or restriction that makes something difficult or prevents it from happening
  • Antonym: freedom - the state of being free; absence of restraint

Etymology

From Old French 'restreinte' (constraint), from Latin 'restringere' (to bind back).
