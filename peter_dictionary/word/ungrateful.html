ungrateful

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈɡreɪtfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪtfəl / (BrE)
Not showing or feeling thanks for something that someone has done for you.

Examples

These trumps were more touching than they were anything else, and seemed to imply that the nights were long, her children ungrateful, and her marriage bewilderingly threadbare.

Synset Examples

ungrateful heirs
How sharper than a serpent's tooth it is / To have a thankless child!

Related Terms

  • Antonym: grateful - showing or feeling thanks
  • Synonym: thankful - pleased and relieved; feeling gratitude

Etymology

From un- (not) + grateful.
