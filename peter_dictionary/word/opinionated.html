opinionated

1. ADJ

/ əˈpɪnjəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjəˌneɪtɪd / (BrE)
Having or showing very strong opinions that are not easily changed, often stubbornly so.

Examples

One thing Papa had not taught Henrietta was how to handle a young man as high-spirited and opinionated as herself.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas; not opinionated
  • Synonym: dogmatic - inclined to lay down principles as undeniably true

Etymology

From 'opinion' + '-ated', formed as an English derivation in the 17th century.
