attach

1. VERB

/ əˈtætʃ / (AmE), / əˈtatʃ / (BrE)
to fasten, join, or connect one thing to another; to make something part of something else

Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: detach - to disconnect or remove from something to which it is attached
  • Synonym: fasten - to close or join securely

Etymology

From Old French *atachier* (to fix, fasten), from a Frankish root meaning 'to attach'.
