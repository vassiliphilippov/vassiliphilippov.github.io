operagoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːpərəˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈɒpərəˌɡəʊə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends performances at the opera

Examples

Thereafter the audience waxed applause happy, but discriminating operagoers reserved judgment as her singing showed signs of strain, her musicianship some questionable procedure and her acting uncomfortable stylization.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: theatergoer - person who attends the theater

Etymology

Formed from 'opera' + 'goer', meaning one who goes to the opera.
