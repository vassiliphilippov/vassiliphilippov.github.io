defined

1. ADJ

/ dɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
clearly marked, characterized, or delimited; having distinct boundaries or features.

Examples

It is characterized by the presence of incompletely developed secondary lobules; well defined, but haphazardly arranged, interlobular septa and a thick, remarkably vascular pleura (fig. 9).
In their place came local variations within the common style - tentative, as it were, in Protogeometric products but truly distinct and sharply defined as the Geometric spirit developed.

Synset Examples

lost in a maze of words both defined and undefined
each child has clearly defined duties

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undefined - not clearly characterized or delimited

Etymology

Past participle of 'define', from Latin 'definire' (to limit, set bounds).
