pap

1. NOUN

/ pæp / (AmE), / pæp / (BrE)
Trivial, worthless, or oversimplified ideas or content, especially in writing, speech, or entertainment.

Examples

Now, you no doubt regard the preceding as pap; you 're tooling around full of gage in your hot rods, gorging yourselves on pizza and playing pinball in the taverns and generally behaving like Ubermenschen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nonsense - ideas or speech that have no meaning or value
  • Synonym: drivel - silly or senseless talk or ideas
  • Antonym: substance - serious or meaningful ideas

Etymology

Originally meaning 'soft food' or 'mush', later extended metaphorically to trivial or bland ideas.
