mitral

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪ.trəl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.trəl / (BrE)
Relating to or located near the mitral valve of the heart.

Examples

The valves were normal except for thin yellow plaques on the inferior surface of the mitral leaflets.

Synset Examples

mitral insufficiency

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mitral valve - the valve in the heart between the left atrium and left ventricle

Etymology

From Latin *mitra* (headband or turban), referencing the valve's shape resembling a bishop's miter.
