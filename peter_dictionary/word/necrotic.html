necrotic

1. ADJ

/ nəˈkrɑtɪk / (AmE), / nɪˈkrɒtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, characterized by, or affected by necrosis (the death of cells or tissues).

Examples

In the gastrocnemius and biceps there were many swollen and homogeneous necrotic fibers such as that shown in Figure 2.

Synset Examples

necrotic tissue

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necrosis - the death of most or all of the cells in an organ or tissue

Etymology

From Greek nekros (dead body) + -tic (relating to).
