courthouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building where judicial courts are held and legal matters are conducted.

Examples

They won't be scared when you get Grissom in the courthouse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court - the place where legal proceedings happen

Etymology

From court + house, meaning a building for conducting court business.
