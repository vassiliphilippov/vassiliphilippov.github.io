congratulatory

1. ADJ

/ kənˈɡrætʃələˌtɔri / (AmE), / kənˈɡrætjʊlətəri / (BrE)
expressing congratulations or pleasure at someone else's success or good fortune

Examples

The usual congratulatory crowd was conspicuously absent; the place had the air of a morgue.

Synset Examples

a congratulatory telegram
the usual congratulatory crowd was conspicuously absent
a gratulatory address

Related Terms

  • Synonym: complimentary - expressing praise, admiration, or approval

Etymology

From Latin 'congratulatus', past participle of 'congratulāri' (to congratulate).
