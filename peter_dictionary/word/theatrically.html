theatrically

1. ADV

/ θiˈætrɪkli / (AmE), / θɪˈætrɪkli / (BrE)
In an exaggerated or overly dramatic manner, as if performing in a theater.

Examples

The figure was so theatrically dressed, that it was as though a character from some other play had blundered into this one.
"Chicken ", Mose said, and theatrically licked his lips.

Synset Examples

`I cannot show my face at her house,' he declared theatrically

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dramatically - in a dramatic manner; with drama or feeling

Etymology

From 'theatrical' + '-ly', ultimately from Greek 'theatron' (theatre).
