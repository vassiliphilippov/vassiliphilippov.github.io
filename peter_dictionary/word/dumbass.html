dumbass

1. NOUN

/ ˈdʌmˌæs / (AmE), / ˈdʌmˌas / (BrE)
A person who is very stupid or acts without thinking; an insult used to criticize someone's intelligence.

Examples

Dumb-ass?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idiot - a person of low intelligence or common sense
  • Antonym: genius - a person of exceptional intellectual ability

Etymology

From 'dumb' (stupid, lacking intelligence) and 'ass' (fool, idiot) in American slang.
