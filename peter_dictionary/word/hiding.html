hiding

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or practice of keeping something secret; concealment

Examples

Pa had a knack for hiding.
He took pride in his hiding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concealment - the act of hiding or keeping something out of sight or secret

Etymology

From verb 'hide' + suffix '-ing', denoting the action or process.
