unsaid

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛd / (BrE)
not spoken or expressed in words; not stated aloud or explicitly

Examples

The things left unsaid they both felt deeply, and with a sigh they fell back on the well stuffed pillows.
Some things are best left unsaid.

Synset Examples

the unexpressed terms of the agreement
things left unsaid
some kind of unspoken agreement
his action is clear but his reason remains unstated

Related Terms

  • Synonym: unspoken - not expressed in words; tacit
  • Antonym: expressed - clearly stated in speech or writing
