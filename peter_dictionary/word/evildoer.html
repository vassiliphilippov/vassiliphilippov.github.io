evildoer

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːvəlˌduːɚ / (AmE), / ˈiːv(ɪ)lˌduːə / (BrE)
A person who commits wicked or morally wrong acts; a person who does evil.

Examples

Let evildoers contemplate their ways, and let every man beware "!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: villain - a wicked or evil person; a scoundrel
  • Antonym: saint - a person of exceptional holiness; opposite of evildoer

Etymology

Formed from 'evil' + 'doer', meaning one who does evil.
