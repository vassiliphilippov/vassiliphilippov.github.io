medication

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A substance or preparation used to treat, prevent, or alleviate the symptoms of disease.

Examples

This medication was continued until February, 1958.
Discontinue medication 48 hours before slaughter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medicine - a compound or preparation used for medical treatment
  • Synonym: drug - a chemical substance used to treat or prevent illness

Etymology

From Latin 'medicatio' meaning 'a healing' or 'a remedy'.
