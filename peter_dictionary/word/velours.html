velours

1. NOUN

/ vəˈlʊrz / (AmE), / vɛˈlʊərz / (BrE)
A heavy, soft fabric with a texture and appearance similar to velvet, often used for upholstery and clothing.

Examples

The daybed Eugene had slept in, made up now with its dark-brown velours cover and pillows.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: velvet - a soft fabric with a dense pile of cut fibers

Etymology

From French 'velours', meaning 'velvet', from Old French 'velu' (hairy).
