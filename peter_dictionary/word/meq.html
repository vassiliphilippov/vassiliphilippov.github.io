mEq

1. NOUN

/ ɛm iː ˈkwɪvələnt / (AmE), / ɛm iː ˈkwɪvələnt / (BrE)
abbreviation for milliequivalent, a unit equal to one-thousandth of an equivalent, used to express the concentration of electrolytes and other substances in solution
