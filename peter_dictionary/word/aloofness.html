aloofness

1. NOUN

/ əˈluːfnəs / (AmE), / əˈluːfnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being distant, detached, or uninvolved, especially in feeling or interest

Examples

One can meet with aloofness almost anywhere: the Thank-Heaven-We 're - not-Involved viewpoint, It Does n't Affect Us!

Synset Examples

emotional distance

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detachment - the state of being objective or aloof
  • Synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
  • Antonym: engagement - state of being involved or committed
  • Antonym: involvement - the act of participating or being engaged

Etymology

From 'aloof' + '-ness'; 'aloof' from Dutch 'lof' (weatherward), originally a nautical term.
