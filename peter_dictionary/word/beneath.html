beneath

1. ADV

/ bəˈniθ / (AmE), / bɪˈniːθ / (BrE)
In or to a lower position; below.

Examples

From the curio cabinet on its south wall and the bureaus beneath, you abstracted seventeen ivory, metal, wood, and stone sculptures of Oriental and African origin, two snuffboxes, and a jade handled magnifying glass.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: below - at a lower level or position
  • Antonym: above - at a higher level or position

Etymology

From Old English 'beneothan', meaning 'underneath' or 'below'.
