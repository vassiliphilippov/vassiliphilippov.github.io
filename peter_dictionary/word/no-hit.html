no-hit

1. ADJ

/ ˈnoʊ hɪt / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ hɪt / (BrE)
(of a baseball game or pitching performance) in which a pitcher allows the opposing team no hits.

Examples

And so it was over the weekend what with 40 - year old Warren Spahn pitching his no-hit masterpiece against the Giants and the Giants' Willie Mays retaliating with a record tying 4 - homer spree Sunday.

Synset Examples

a no-hit pitcher
a no-hit game

Related Terms

  • Synonym: no-hitter - a game in which a pitcher allows no hits
