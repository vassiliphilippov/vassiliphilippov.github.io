secondarily

1. ADV

/ ˌsɛkənˈdɛrəli / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənˈdeərəli / (BrE)
As a secondary consideration; in a way that is of less importance or not primary.

Examples

Man may also be injured secondarily by damage to his food crops or domestic animals.
The congregation is first and foremost an economic peer group; it is secondarily a believing and worshiping fellowship.

Synset Examples

secondarily affected

Related Terms

  • Antonym: primarily - as of first importance or in the first place
  • Synonym: mainly - for the most part; chiefly

Etymology

From 'secondary' + '-ly'. 'Secondary' from Latin 'secundarius', from 'secundus' (following, second).
