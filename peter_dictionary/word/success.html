success

1. NOUN

/ səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
The state of attaining prosperity, fame, or recognition for achievement.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: achievement - something that has been accomplished, especially by superior ability or special effort
  • Antonym: failure - lack of success or not achieving the desired end

Etymology

From Latin 'successus' (an advance, succession, happy outcome), from 'succedere' (to come after, succeed).
