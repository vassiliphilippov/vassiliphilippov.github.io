hyaline

1. ADJ

/ ˈhaɪ.əˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.əˌliːn / (BrE)
transparent or translucent like glass; having a glassy appearance

Examples

Occasional tubules contained hyaline casts admixed with neutrophils.

Synset Examples

the morning is as clear as diamond or as hyaline

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glassy - having a glass-like appearance or texture
  • Antonym: opaque - not transparent or translucent; not allowing light to pass through

Etymology

From Greek 'hualinos', from 'hualos' meaning glass.
