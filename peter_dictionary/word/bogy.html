bogy

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊɡi / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡi / (BrE)
an unidentified aircraft, especially suspected to be hostile

Examples

Three bogies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bogey - an unidentified (possibly hostile) aircraft; variant spelling

Etymology

Originally of unknown origin; possibly from Scottish 'bogle' (goblin, specter), applied to unknown or threatening targets in 20th-century military slang.
