pooh-pooh

1. VERB

/ puːˈpuː / (AmE), / puːˈpuː / (BrE)
to dismiss or express contempt for (an idea or suggestion) as unworthy of consideration

Examples

But to Welch's chagrin, the police captain pooh-poohed Welch's credulity in Barco's confession.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dismiss - to reject or disregard something as unimportant
  • Synonym: scorn - to treat with contempt or derision

Etymology

Imitative, echoic of the sound made to express disdain; first recorded in the early 19th century.
