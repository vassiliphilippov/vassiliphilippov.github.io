midsection

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdˌsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the middle part of the human torso, especially the front area between the chest and the waist

Examples

Curt managed to duck beneath the man's flailing fist, and drove home a solid left to Jess's mid section.

Synset Examples

young American women believe that a bare midriff is fashionable

Related Terms

  • Synonym: abdomen - the part of the body between the chest and the pelvis

Etymology

From 'mid-' (meaning 'middle') + 'section' (from Latin 'sectio', cutting).
