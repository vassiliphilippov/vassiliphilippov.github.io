ceremonial

1. ADJ

/ ˌsɛrəˈmoʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛrəˈməʊniəl / (BrE)
Relating to or used in a ceremony, especially one marked by formal, traditional, or elaborate procedures.

Examples

[CEREMONIAL MUSIC PLAYING] CONDUCTOR: [ON MICROPHONE] Ladies and gentlemen... CICCI: Frank, wake up.

Synset Examples

a ceremonial occasion
ceremonial garb

Related Terms

  • Synonym: formal - done in accordance with rules of convention or etiquette
  • Antonym: informal - having a relaxed, friendly, or unofficial style, manner, or nature

Etymology

From Late Latin ceremonialis (pertaining to ceremony), from ceremonia (ceremony).
