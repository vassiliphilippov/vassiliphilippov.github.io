43rd

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrti ˈθɜrd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːti ˈθɜːd / (BrE)
the ordinal form of the number forty-three; coming after the forty-second in a sequence

Examples

Try 309 West 43rd Street. - Know a good spot on the West Side? - I'll think about it.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: 42nd - the ordinal number for forty-two; preceding 43rd
  • Degree Higher: 44th - the ordinal number for forty-four; following 43rd
