Anglophilia

1. NOUN

/ ˌæŋɡləˈfɪliə / (AmE), / ˌæŋɡləˈfɪliə / (BrE)
Admiration or love for England or British customs, culture, or people.

Examples

In certain respects defeat increased the persistent Anglophilia of the Old South.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Anglophobia - dislike or fear of England or English customs

Etymology

From Latin 'Anglus' (English) + Greek 'philia' (love, friendship).
