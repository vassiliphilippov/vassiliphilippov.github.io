murdered

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɝːdɚd / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdəd / (BrE)
Having been unlawfully killed; killed as a result of murder.

Examples

Perhaps if Felix had first come upon us when this boy was not cavorting so gaily up and down the hall outside the murdered woman's apartment, we might have had less trouble convincing Felix of our seriousness.

Synset Examples

the murdered woman
lay a wreath on murdered Lincoln's bier

Related Terms

  • Synonym: killed - having been killed; dead as a result of being killed
  • Antonym: alive - not dead; living

Etymology

From the verb 'murder', from Middle English *mordre*, from Old English *mordor* (murder, mortal sin).
