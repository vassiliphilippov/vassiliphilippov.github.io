riddance

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪdən(t)s / (AmE), / ˈrɪd(ə)ns / (BrE)
The act of removing or getting rid of something or someone.

Examples

Get rid of them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elimination - the act of eliminating or removing something

Etymology

From Middle English riddance, from 'rid' + '-ance'.
