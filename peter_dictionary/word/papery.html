papery

1. ADJ

/ ˈpeɪpəri / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəri / (BrE)
Thin and resembling paper in texture or appearance.

Examples

Each now makes a tough, papery cocoon and pupates.

Synset Examples

papery leaves
wasps that make nests of papery material

Etymology

From 'paper' + '-y', denoting having the qualities of paper.
