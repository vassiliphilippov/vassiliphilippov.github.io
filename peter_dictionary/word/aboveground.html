aboveground

1. ADJ

/ əˈbʌvˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / əˈbʌvˌɡraʊnd / (BrE)
Situated on or above the surface of the ground.

Examples

An outdoor, aboveground fallout shelter also may be built with concrete blocks.

Synset Examples

aboveground nuclear testing
surface instruments for detecting oil deposits
surface transportation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underground - located beneath the surface of the ground

Etymology

Formed by compounding 'above' and 'ground'.
