academy

1. NOUN

/ əˈkædəmi / (AmE), / əˈkadəmi / (BrE)
a society or institution dedicated to the advancement of art, science, or literature

Examples

He worked in oil for years before beginning his work in watercolor, and his first public recognition and early honors, including his election to the Academy, were for his essays in the heavier medium.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: institute - an organization for a specific purpose, especially education or research
  • Synonym: society - an organization of people with a common interest, often for scholarly or artistic advancement

Etymology

From French *académie* and Latin *academia*, from Ancient Greek *Akadēmía* (the garden where Plato taught).
