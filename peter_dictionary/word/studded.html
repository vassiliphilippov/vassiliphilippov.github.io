studded

1. ADJ

/ ˈstʌdɪd / (AmE), / ˈstʌdɪd / (BrE)
Decorated or covered with studs or similar small, raised ornaments; often used in combination to indicate numerous small objects attached or embedded.

Examples

He was heralded as "Sportsman of the Year "by Sports Illustrated, and last night was acclaimed in Rochester as the "Professional Athlete of the Year ", a distinction that earned for him the $ 10000 diamond - studded Hickok Belt.

Synset Examples

star-studded heavens
diamond-studded belt

Related Terms

  • Antonym: plain - lacking ornamentation or decoration

Etymology

From 'stud' (a small ornament or nail), with the English adjective-forming suffix '-ed'.
