unsigned

1. ADJ

/ ʌnˈsaɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaɪnd / (BrE)
not having a signature; lacking a written or electronic signature to indicate authorization or approval.

Examples

But Mercer's explanation was simple: "I made out the check and carried it around a few days unsigned - in case I lost it ".

Synset Examples

the message was typewritten and unsigned

Related Terms

  • Antonym: signed - having a signature

Etymology

Formed from prefix 'un-' meaning 'not', and 'signed', past participle of 'sign'.
