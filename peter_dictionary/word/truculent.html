truculent

1. ADJ

/ ˈtrʌkjʊlənt / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkjʊlənt / (BrE)
aggressively defiant or eager to fight; showing fierce or hostile opposition

Examples

Here, this happy, roving son of good fortune proved that he could accept the disciplines of a new social economic order fighting for its very existence and ideals in a truculent world.

Synset Examples

a truculent speech against the new government

Related Terms

  • Synonym: belligerent - hostile and aggressive
  • Antonym: peaceful - not inclined to fight; calm and non-aggressive

Etymology

From Latin 'truculentus' (fierce, savage).
