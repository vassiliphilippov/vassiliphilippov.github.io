transversely

1. ADV

/ trænzˈvɜrsli / (AmE), / trænzˈvɜːsli / (BrE)
In a direction or position across or at right angles to the main axis; in a transverse manner.

Examples

The unstained strips were then marked, using the stained ones as a guide, and cut transversely so as to separate the various protein bands.

Synset Examples

they were cut transversely

Related Terms

  • Antonym: longitudinally - in a direction parallel to the long axis

Etymology

From Latin transversus (turned or lying across) + -ly.
