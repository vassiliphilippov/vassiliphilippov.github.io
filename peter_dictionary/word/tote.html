tote

1. VERB

/ toʊt / (AmE), / təʊt / (BrE)
to carry or haul something, especially something heavy or awkward, with effort

Examples

It turns out Andy's favorite hobby was toting his wall out into the exercise yard.

Synset Examples

You'll have to lug this suitcase

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lug - to carry or drag something heavy with effort
  • Synonym: haul - to pull or drag with effort, often something heavy

Etymology

Possibly from dialectal English, meaning to pull or drag, or from West African origin (e.g., Kikongo 'tota', to pick up or carry).
