bonemeal

1. NOUN

/ ˈboʊnˌmiːl / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnˌmiːl / (BrE)
a fertilizer made from ground animal bones, used to enrich soil with phosphorus and calcium

Examples

I put a little scoop of pulverized phosphate rock or steamed bone meal into each hole with the plant.

Etymology

From bone + meal, where 'meal' means ground or powdered substance.
