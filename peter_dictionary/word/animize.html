animize

1. VERB

/ ˈænəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈænᵻˌmaɪz / (BrE)
to give lifelike or animate qualities to something

Examples

As the dancer is depersonalized, his accouterments are animized, and the combined elements give birth to a new being.

Synset Examples

animated cartoons

Related Terms

  • Synonym: animate - to give life or movement to; to make lively or active

Etymology

From Latin anima (soul, life) + -ize (verb-forming suffix).
