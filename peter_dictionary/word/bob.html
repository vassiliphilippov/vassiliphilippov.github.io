bob

1. VERB

/ bɑb / (AmE), / bɒb / (BrE)
to move quickly up and down repeatedly, especially in a light or buoyant way

Examples

I bobbed my head each time it appeared.
Signora Ferraro, bobbing her head encouragingly, asked Sam about Canada, having a special interest.

Synset Examples

her rucksack bobbed gently on her back

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bounce - to move quickly up, back, or away from a surface after hitting it
