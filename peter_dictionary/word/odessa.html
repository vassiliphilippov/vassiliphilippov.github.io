Odessa

1. PROPN

/ oʊˈdɛsə / (AmE), / əʊˈdɛsə / (BrE)
A port city in southern Ukraine, located on the northwestern shore of the Black Sea.

Examples

He got in the oil business out at Odessa and lucked into some money ".

Etymology

From Russian Одесса (Odéssa), probably named after the ancient Greek colony of Odessos.
