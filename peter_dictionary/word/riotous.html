riotous

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪətəs / (AmE), / ˈraɪətəs / (BrE)
(of plants or growth) produced or growing in extreme abundance; luxuriant or profuse.

Examples

The more I talked with him, the more convinced I became that that was the secret of their riotous blooming.
The riotous onrush of industrialism after the War for Southern Independence and the general secular drift to the Religion of Humanity, however, prepared the way for a reception of the French Revolution's socialistic offspring of one sort of another.

Synset Examples

their riotous blooming

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luxuriant - (of vegetation) rich and profuse in growth; lush
  • Synonym: abundant - existing or available in large quantities; plentiful
  • Antonym: sparse - thinly dispersed or scattered

Etymology

From Old French *rioteux* (disorderly), from *riote* (dispute, quarrel). The sense of abundance is figurative, likening exuberant growth to unrestrained behavior.
