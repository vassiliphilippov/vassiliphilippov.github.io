overfeed

1. VERB

/ ˌoʊvərˈfid / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈfiːd / (BrE)
to give too much food to (a person or animal); to feed excessively

Examples

I doubt if it is possible to overfeed them.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: underfeed - to give insufficient food to

Etymology

From over- (excessively) + feed.
