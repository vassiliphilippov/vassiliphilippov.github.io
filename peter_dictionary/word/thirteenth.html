thirteenth

1. ADJ

/ θərˈtinθ / (AmE), / θəːˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
Coming after the twelfth in a sequence; 13th.

Examples

His thirteenth chapter includes many exciting accounts of huge serpents with prodigious strength, but these seem to be given to complete his picture, not to be believed.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: twelfth - coming after the eleventh; 12th
  • Degree Higher: fourteenth - coming after the thirteenth; 14th

Etymology

Formed from 'thirteen' + '-th', the ordinal suffix.
