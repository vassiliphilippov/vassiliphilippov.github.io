unhurriedly

1. ADV

/ ʌnˈhɜridli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʌrɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not hurried; without haste.

Examples

The bodice beneath was buttoned and, withdrawing his lips from hers, he set her upright on his knee and started to undo it, unhurriedly as if she were a child.
Macklin was the third man to come out, and he came unhurriedly.

Synset Examples

she proceeded unhurriedly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slowly - at a slow speed or pace
  • Antonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly

Etymology

Formed from 'unhurried' + '-ly'.
