sold-out

1. ADJ

/ soʊld ˈaʊt / (AmE), / səʊld ˈaʊt / (BrE)
(of a person) having accepted a bribe or betraying one's principles, typically for personal gain.

Examples

It was an interesting fraternisation of ex convicts, union racketeers, ward heelers, sold-out officials, and gunmen.

Synset Examples

a sold-out politician

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corrupt - dishonest or immoral, especially through bribery
  • Antonym: upright - honest and morally correct (opposite)
