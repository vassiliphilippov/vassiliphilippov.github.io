pander

1. NOUN

/ ˈpændər / (AmE), / ˈpændə / (BrE)
a person who procures clients for prostitutes; a pimp

Examples

Greene was in actuality a young ruffian from Kent, who had broken with his parents in order to keep the company he preferred - pimps, panders and whores.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pimp - a person who procures clients for prostitutes

Etymology

From Middle English *Pandare*, name of a character in Chaucer's 'Troilus and Criseyde', who helped arrange a romantic liaison.
