expectable

1. ADJ

/ ɪkˈspɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛktəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being anticipated or likely to happen; to be expected

Examples

It is evident that Swadesh has not only had much experience with basic vocabulary in many languages but has acquired great tact and feeling for the expectable behavior of lexical items.

Synset Examples

differences of opinion are quite expectable given the present information

Related Terms

  • Synonym: expected - regarded as likely to happen; anticipated
  • Antonym: unexpected - not likely to happen; not anticipated

Etymology

From 'expect' + '-able', meaning able to be expected.
