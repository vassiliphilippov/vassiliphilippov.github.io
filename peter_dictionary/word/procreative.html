procreative

1. ADJ

/ ˈproʊkrieɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈprəʊkrieɪtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or capable of producing new life or offspring; reproductive.

Examples

Sheer plumpness, he knew, is not a vital part of the body and has no procreative functions.

Synset Examples

the reproductive potential of a species is its relative capacity to reproduce itself under optimal conditions
the reproductive or generative organs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reproductive - relating to or capable of reproduction

Etymology

From Latin procreare (to beget, produce), from pro- (forth) + creare (to create).
