noncommissioned

1. ADJ

/ nɑn.kəˈmɪʃ.ənd / (AmE), / nɒn.kəˈmɪʃ.ənd / (BrE)
(of military officers) having a position gained through promotion from enlisted ranks, rather than by holding a formal commission.

Examples

Its entire complement of non-commissioned officers on the platoon level had departed as cadre for another unit, and its vehicles were still those used in the drive across Luzon in World War 2,.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: commissioned - having a military rank by commission

Etymology

From prefix 'non-' (not) + 'commissioned', from 'commission', meaning official appointment.
