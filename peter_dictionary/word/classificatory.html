classificatory

1. ADJ

/ klæsɪˈfɪkəˌtɔri / (AmE), / klæsɪfɪˈkeɪtəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving the action of classifying or sorting into categories

Examples

But consideration of the frequency of stems per constant meaning seems to be established as having significance in comparative situations with diachronic and classificatory relevance; and Gleason presumably is on the way with a further contribution in this area.

Synset Examples

classificatory criteria

Related Terms

  • Synonym: classificational - relating to the act or process of classification; often used interchangeably

Etymology

From classify + -atory, modeled after Latin '-atorius' (relating to action)
