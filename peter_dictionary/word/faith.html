1. NOUN

/ feɪθ / (AmE), / feɪθ / (BrE)
Strong belief or trust in a god, religious doctrine, or a system of religious belief, especially without logical proof.

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: religion - The belief in and worship of a superhuman controlling power, especially a personal God or gods.
  • Antonym: doubt - Uncertainty or lack of conviction, especially regarding belief or religious conviction.

Etymology

From Old French *feid*, from Latin *fides* (trust, faith, confidence).
