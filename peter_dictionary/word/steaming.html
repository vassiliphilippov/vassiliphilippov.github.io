steaming

1. ADJ

/ ˈstiːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstiːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of something) filled with steam or emitting vapor or mist; very hot and giving off steam.

Examples

Hans had laid steaming towels over the kid's chest and stomach.

Synset Examples

a steaming kettle
steamy towels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steamy - full of steam; humid with mist or vapor
  • Degree Lower: smoking - emitting smoke or visible vapor
