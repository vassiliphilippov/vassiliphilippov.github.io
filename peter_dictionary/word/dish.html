dish

1. NOUN

/ dɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪʃ / (BrE)
a particular type or item of prepared food, often as part of a meal

Examples

She glinted suspiciously at the dish before her: "Blowfish.
After he 'd got out fresh liver for Bast, he paused to look at her crouched daintily over her dish.

Synset Examples

she prepared a special dish for dinner

Related Terms

  • Synonym: course - a stage of a meal served at one time

Etymology

From Old English *disc* (plate, dish), from Latin *discus* (disk, plate).

Related Terms
