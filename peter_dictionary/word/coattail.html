coattail

1. NOUN

/ ˈkoʊtˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtˌteɪl / (BrE)
the loose back part of a coat or jacket, typically one that hangs below the waist

Examples

Far away, standing before a curtained window in the study room, was his father, hands tucked under his coattails, and staring into the dark church.

Etymology

Formed from 'coat' + 'tail', referring to the tail-like extension at the back of a coat.
