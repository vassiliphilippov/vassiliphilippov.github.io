rivulet

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪvjələt / (AmE), / ˈrɪvjʊlɪt / (BrE)
A very small stream or brook.

Examples

He removed his hat to let the trapped sweat cut rivulets through the dust film upon his gaunt face.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stream - a small, narrow river
  • Synonym: brook - a small, natural stream of fresh water
  • Degree Higher: river - a large natural stream of water

Etymology

From Latin 'rivulus', diminutive of 'rivus' (stream).
