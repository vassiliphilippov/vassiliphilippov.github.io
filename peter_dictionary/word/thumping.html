thumping

1. NOUN

/ ˈθʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
a heavy, dull sound made by the impact of a heavy object

Examples

Amid the crackle of small arms and automatic weapons, I heard the thumping of mortars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thud - a dull sound, as of a heavy object striking or falling
  • Degree Higher: bang - a sudden loud noise, especially from impact
  • Degree Lower: tap - a light gentle sound, as from a small knock

Etymology

From the verb 'thump' (to strike heavily), with '-ing' used nominally.
