a.m.

1. ADV

/ ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
Used to refer to the time between midnight and noon; before noon.

Examples

The committee will stand in recess until 10:00 a.m. Monday morning, at which time the committee will produce a witness who'’ll corroborate the charges which are made against you today.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: p.m. - the period of time from noon to midnight

Etymology

Abbreviation of Latin 'ante meridiem' meaning 'before midday'.
