obscene

1. ADJ

/ əbˈsin / (AmE), / əbˈsiːn / (BrE)
offensive or disgusting by accepted standards of morality and decency, especially in a way intended to incite lust or indecency

Examples

Once covertly looking at Simms Purdew, the only man in the world whom he hated, he had seen the heavy, slack, bestubbled jaw open and close to emit the cruel, obscene banter, and had seen the pale blue eyes go watery with whisky and merriment, and suddenly he was not seeing the face of that vile creature.
And so they went, he choosing of all places an inn near Medmenham Abbey, scene a generation ago of the obscene orgies of the Hellfire Club.

Synset Examples

the dance often becomes flagrantly obscene

Related Terms

  • Synonym: indecent - not conforming with generally accepted standards of behavior, especially regarding sexual matters
  • Synonym: lewd - crude and offensive in a sexual way
  • Antonym: decent - conforming with generally accepted standards of respectable or moral behavior

Etymology

From Latin obscenus (ill-omened, abominable, disgusting).
