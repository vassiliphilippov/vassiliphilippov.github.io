fascinatingly

1. ADV

/ ˈfæsəˌneɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfæsɪneɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that captures intense interest or attention; in a manner that is fascinating.

Examples

Miss Pulova has a voice that Maria Callas once described as "like chipping teeth with a screw driver ", and her round, opalescent face becomes fascinatingly reflective of the emotions demanded by the role of Rosalie.

Synset Examples

her face became fascinatingly distorted

Related Terms

  • Synonym: interestingly - in a way that arouses curiosity or interest
  • Antonym: boringly - in a dull or uninteresting manner

Etymology

From 'fascinating' + '-ly', where 'fascinating' comes from Latin 'fascināre' (to bewitch).
