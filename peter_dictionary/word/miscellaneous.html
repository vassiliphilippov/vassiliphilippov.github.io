miscellaneous

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪsəˈleɪniəs / (AmE), / ˌmɪsələˈneɪɪəs / (BrE)
consisting of a mixture of various kinds; made up of diverse or varied elements that do not seem to belong together

Examples

Miscellaneous soils, which primarily include sticky substances and colorless liquids which evaporate to leave a residue.
Greases, stains, and miscellaneous soils are usually sorbed onto the soiled surface.

Synset Examples

an arrangement of assorted spring flowers
assorted sizes
miscellaneous accessories
a mixed program of baroque and contemporary music
a motley crew
sundry sciences commonly known as social

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assorted - of various or different sorts; mixed
  • Synonym: sundry - various kinds, several
  • Synonym: mixed - composed of different or diverse elements or ingredients
  • Antonym: homogeneous - of the same kind; alike or uniform

Etymology

From Latin 'miscellāneus', from 'miscēre' (to mix).
