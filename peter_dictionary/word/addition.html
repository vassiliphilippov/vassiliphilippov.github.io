addition

1. NOUN

/ əˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / əˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a thing that is added to something else, especially in order to improve it

Examples

Shortly after this study ended septic conditions resulted which required the addition of sodium nitrate.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: improvement - something that makes a situation or thing better
  • Antonym: subtraction - the process or act of taking something away

Etymology

From Latin additio (an adding to), from addere (to add).
