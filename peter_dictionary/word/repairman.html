repairman

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈpɛrˌmæn / (AmE), / rɪˈpeəˌmæn / (BrE)
A man whose job is to repair machinery, equipment, or other things.

Examples

While she was thus engaged, McFeeley questioned her about her whereabouts the previous day, any recollections she had of people hanging around, of overcurious delivery boys or repairmen, of strange cars cruising the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: repairwoman - a woman whose job is to repair things
  • Synonym: technician - a person skilled in the technical aspects of a subject, often involving repair

Etymology

Formed from 'repair' + 'man', indicating a man who repairs things.
