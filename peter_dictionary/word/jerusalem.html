Jerusalem

1. PROPN

/ dʒəˈruːsələm / (AmE), / dʒəˈruːsələm / (BrE)
The capital and largest city of Israel, considered a holy city by Jews, Christians, and Muslims, with a long and complex history.

Etymology

From Hebrew 'Yerushalayim', meaning 'foundation of peace'.
