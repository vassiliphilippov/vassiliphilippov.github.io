founding

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of establishing an organization, institution, or system for the first time

Examples

Another source of intellectual stimulus was opened to her at that time by the founding of Johns Hopkins University within walking distance of home.
If of the founders of glottochronology Swadesh has escaped our steady plodding, and Lees has repudiated his own share in the founding, that is no reason why we should swerve.

Synset Examples

she looked forward to her initiation as an adult
the foundation of a new scientific society

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foundation - the establishment of an institution or organization

Etymology

From the verb 'found' (to establish), which comes from Old French 'fonder', from Latin 'fundare' (to lay the bottom, establish).
