comforts

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌmfərts / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfəts / (BrE)
things that provide physical or emotional ease and well-being, especially items, conditions, or amenities that make life more pleasant

Examples

A fire had just been lighted, he saw, and things had been set out for drinks, and, like any stray, his response to these comforts was instantaneous.
It would be a mistake to sell those thousands of beginning campers on the idea they 're buying the comforts of home.
Home is the place to find the comforts of home.
Today's campers want comforts, and they have them.

Synset Examples

all the comforts of home

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: luxuries - things that are not essential but are desirable and bring comfort
  • Degree Lower: necessities - essential things needed for basic comfort or survival
