reborn

1. ADJ

/ riˈbɔrn / (AmE), / riˈbɔːn / (BrE)
Having been spiritually renewed or converted; having experienced a revival of faith or purpose.

Examples

Through the swathings of terror, she jabbed deceit's sharp point - Amy would be reborn, a new child, with new parents, living under new circumstances.

Synset Examples

a born-again Christian

Related Terms

  • Synonym: born-again - characterized by or relating to a spiritual rebirth, especially relating to Christian conversion
