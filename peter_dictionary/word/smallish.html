smallish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsmɔːlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlɪʃ / (BrE)
fairly small in size; somewhat small

Examples

Byron, since the separation from his wife had been living in a smallish house in Piccadilly Terrace.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: small - of a size that is less than normal or usual
  • Degree Higher: medium - of moderate size

Etymology

Formed from 'small' plus the suffix '-ish' meaning 'somewhat' or 'rather'.
