tested

1. ADJ

/ ˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
Proved by trial or experience to be reliable or effective.

Examples

He had gotten stuck with a job too big for his imagination; he had to cling to routine, tested procedures.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untried - not yet tested or proven
  • Antonym: unproven - not shown to be reliable or effective

2. ADJ

/ ˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
Tried and proven to be useful or correct; having been subjected to a test and shown to work or be valid.

Examples

But is the result new barnsful of tested knowledge on the basis of which we can with confidence solve our domestic and international problems?
In the field of the social sciences a considerable fund of tested knowledge has been accumulated that can be used to good advantage.

Synset Examples

a tested method

Related Terms

  • Antonym: untried - not tested or attempted before
  • Synonym: proven - demonstrated by evidence or proof to be true or valid
