titanic

1. ADJ

/ taɪˈtænɪk / (AmE), / tɪˈtænɪk / (BrE)
Having great strength, force, or power; enormous or colossal.

Examples

THE MUSIC OF BINI SALFININISTAS, CAPITAL LP 63711 - R, one of the rare recordings of this titanic, yet unsung, composer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: powerful - having great power or strength
  • Antonym: weak - lacking physical strength or power

Etymology

From Greek Titan, a race of giant gods in Greek mythology.
