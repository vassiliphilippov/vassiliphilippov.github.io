droopy

1. ADJ

/ ˈdruːpi / (AmE), / ˈdruːpi / (BrE)
hanging down limply, as from exhaustion or weakness

Examples

Oh, that droopy thing over there, that'’s my brother Mike.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drooping - hanging or bending downwards
  • Antonym: upright - standing straight up

Etymology

From 'droop' (to hang or bend down) + '-y' (adjective-forming suffix).
