nigra

1. NOUN

/ ˈnaɪɡrə / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡrə / (BrE)
(offensive, archaic) An extremely offensive ethnic slur, historically used as a derogatory term for a Black person.

Examples

We do n't' low nigras to walk on the same sidewalk with white men where I come from'.

Synset Examples

only a Black can call another Black a nigga

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nigger - an extremely offensive slur against Black people

Etymology

Variant pronunciation and spelling of 'Negro', ultimately from Spanish and Portuguese 'negro' (black), from Latin 'niger'.
