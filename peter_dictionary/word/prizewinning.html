prizewinning

1. ADJ

/ ˈpraɪzˌwɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪzwɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
Having won a prize or competition; receiving recognition as the best in a contest.

Examples

Undoubtedly you have read the case histories of some of his prize winning pupils (every pupil has a physique title of some kind or other).

Synset Examples

a champion show dog
a prizewinning wine

Related Terms

  • Synonym: award-winning - having received an award
  • Synonym: champion - person or thing that has won first place in a contest
  • Antonym: losing - having not won; the opposite of winning

Etymology

From 'prize' + present participle 'winning'.
