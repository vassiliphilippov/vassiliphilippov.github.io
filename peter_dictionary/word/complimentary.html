complimentary

1. ADJ

/ ˌkɑːmpləˈmɛn.tə.ri / (AmE), / ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛn.təri / (BrE)
expressing praise, approval, or admiration; conveying a compliment

Examples

All four types of message listed in Table 1 are permitted, although decorum and cocktail tradition require holding the commands to a minimum, while exclamations having complimentary intonations are more than customarily encouraged.

Synset Examples

a complimentary remark

Related Terms

  • Antonym: insulting - expressing disrespect or rudeness

Etymology

From compliment + -ary, formed on the pattern of words like 'elementary.'
