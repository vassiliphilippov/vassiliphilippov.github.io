Hebraic

1. ADJ

/ hɪˈbreɪɪk / (AmE), / hɪˈbreɪɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Hebrew language, its people, or culture

Examples

He went into the Jewish quarter, wanting to draw Hebraic faces so that he could reach a visual understanding of how Christ might have looked.

Synset Examples

Hebrew vowels

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Hebrew - relating to the Hebrew language or people

Etymology

From Late Latin Hebraicus, from Greek Hebraikos, from Hebraios (Hebrew).
