equipping

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkwɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of supplying or furnishing with weapons, equipment, or gear, especially in preparation for war or conflict.

Examples

Funds are also included in this budget to continue the equipping of the B-52 wings with the Hound Dog air-to-surface missile.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arming - the act of equipping with weapons
