squeaky

1. ADJ

/ ˈskwiːki / (AmE), / ˈskwiːki / (BrE)
Making or having a high-pitched or creaking sound, like that of a mouse or rusty hinge.

Examples

But neither was the statement empirical, for goodness was not a quality like red or squeaky that could be seen or heard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: creaky - making a creaking sound
  • Degree Higher: noisy - making a lot of noise
  • Antonym: silent - not making any sound
