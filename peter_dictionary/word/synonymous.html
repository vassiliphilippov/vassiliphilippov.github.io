synonymous

1. ADJ

/ sɪˈnɑːnɪməs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒnɪməs / (BrE)
(of words or things) having the same or nearly the same meaning as another word or thing; closely associated with or suggestive of something.

Examples

His name became synonymous with cold-blooded cruelty.
Across this nation... ...the words "Gotham City" are synonymous with crime.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equivalent - having the same meaning or value
  • Antonym: antonymous - having the opposite meaning

Etymology

From Late Latin 'synonymus', from Greek 'synōnymos' (having the same name): 'syn-' (together) + 'onyma' (name).
