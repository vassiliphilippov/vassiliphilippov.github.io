alphabetize

1. VERB

/ ˈæl.fə.bə.taɪz / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bə.taɪz / (BrE)
to arrange items or words in alphabetical order

Examples

An attempted middle course might lead to devices like a 5000 - word alphabetized dictionary from which every fiftieth word was selected.

Synset Examples

Alphabetize the list

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sort - to arrange according to a particular order or system

Etymology

From alphabet + -ize, first recorded in English in the 17th century.
