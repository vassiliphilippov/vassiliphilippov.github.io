selfish

1. ADJ

/ ˈsɛlfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfɪʃ / (BrE)
primarily concerned with one's own personal advantage or welfare rather than that of others.

Examples

When a husband is sexually selfish and heedless of his wife's desires, she is cheated of the fulfillment and pleasure nature intended for her.
He must begin, paradoxically, by becoming more selfish.
But I have selfish reasons.

Synset Examples

Selfish men were...trying to make capital for themselves out of the sacred cause of civil rights

Related Terms

  • Antonym: unselfish - not putting oneself before others; generous or selfless
  • Synonym: egotistic - driven by self-centeredness or excessive self-interest

Etymology

From self + -ish, meaning 'of or belonging to a self.'
