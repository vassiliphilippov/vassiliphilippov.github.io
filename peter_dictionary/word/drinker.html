drinker

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈdrɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who drinks, especially liquids such as water, milk, or alcohol

Examples

Otherwise, you may be saddled with a good size milk bill by milk drinkers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains completely from alcoholic drinks

Etymology

From drink + -er (agent noun suffix).
