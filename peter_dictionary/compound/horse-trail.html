horse-trail

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs treɪl / (AmE), / hɔːs treɪl / (BrE)
A trail or path specially designated for horses to travel over.

Examples

They had traveled only a short distance when they spotted five Mexicans riding along a horse-trail across the stream just ahead of them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bridle path - a path or trail designated for horses
