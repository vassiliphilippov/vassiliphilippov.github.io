West Berliner

1. NOUN

/ wɛst bɚˈlɪnɚ / (AmE), / wɛst bəˈlɪnə / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from West Berlin, especially during the period it was separated from East Berlin

Examples

They are less vocal now, when it is the West Berliners who are migrating.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: East Berliner - an inhabitant of East Berlin

Etymology

From 'West Berlin' (the western sectors of Berlin during the Cold War) + 'er' denoting an inhabitant.
