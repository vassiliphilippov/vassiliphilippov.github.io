parking space

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑrkɪŋ speɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ speɪs / (BrE)
an area marked or designated for parking a motor vehicle

Examples

A taxi took him back to the bar and grill where he had left his car, and a few minutes later he found a parking place across the street from his apartment.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking spot - an alternative term for a space where a vehicle can be parked
