business executive

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznɪs ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv / (BrE)
a person with senior managerial responsibilities in a business corporation or company

Examples

A Southerner married to a New Englander, I have lived for many years in a Connecticut commuting town with a high percentage of artists, writers, publicity men, and business executives of egghead tastes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: executive - person with managerial authority, especially in business
