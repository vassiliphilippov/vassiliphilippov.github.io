dancing school

1. NOUN

/ ˈdænsɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
A school where people are taught to dance.

Examples

And dancing school, so helpful in artistic and psychological development, also contributes to this essential early training - and can contribute even more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dance academy - an institution offering dance instruction
  • Antonym: music school - a school where students learn music (for contrast)
