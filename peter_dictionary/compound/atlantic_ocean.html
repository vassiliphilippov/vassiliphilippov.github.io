Atlantic Ocean

1. PROPN

/ əˈtlæn.tɪk ˈoʊ.ʃən / (AmE), / ətˈlæn.tɪk ˈəʊ.ʃən / (BrE)
the ocean separating North and South America from Europe and Africa; the second largest ocean in the world

Examples

Tsunami are rare, however, in the Atlantic Ocean; they are far more common in the Pacific.

Etymology

From Latin 'Atlanticus', meaning 'of Atlas', referring to the mythical Atlas Mountains; 'ocean' from Ancient Greek 'ōkeanos'.
