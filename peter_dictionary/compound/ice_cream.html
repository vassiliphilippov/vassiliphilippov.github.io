ice cream

1. NOUN

/ aɪs ˈkrim / (AmE), / aɪs ˈkriːm / (BrE)
A sweet frozen food made from cream or milk, sugar, and flavorings, often eaten as a dessert.

Examples

"But brother I can n't take a job right now ", she said with her eyes on her ice cream, "I'm going to have a baby, Francis Xavier's baby, my own husband's baby ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gelato - Italian-style ice cream with less fat and less air than traditional ice cream
  • Degree Lower: sorbet - frozen dessert made from sweetened water and fruit juice or puree, but not dairy
