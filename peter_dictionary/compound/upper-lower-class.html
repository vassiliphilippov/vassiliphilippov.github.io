upper-lower-class

1. ADJ

/ ˌʌpɚ ˈloʊɚ klæs / (AmE), / ˌʌpə ˈləʊə klɑːs / (BrE)
Belonging to the higher segment of the lower social or economic class.

Examples

Equal proportions of children in each city were drawn from upper-lower and lower-middle class neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: lower-lower-class - the lowest segment of the lower class
  • Degree Higher: lower-middle-class - the lowest segment of the middle class
