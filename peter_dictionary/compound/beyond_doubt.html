beyond doubt

1. ADV

/ biˈjɑnd daʊt / (AmE), / biˈɒnd daʊt / (BrE)
Without any doubt; certainly; in a manner that cannot be questioned.

Examples

No doubt about it.

Synset Examples

it was immediately and indubitably apparent that I had interrupted a scene of lovers
his guilt was established beyond a shadow of a doubt

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undoubtedly - without doubt; certainly
  • Antonym: questionably - in a way that is open to doubt
