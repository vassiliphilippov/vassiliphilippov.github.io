coffee break

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑfi ˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌbreɪk / (BrE)
A short rest period during the workday, often involving drinking coffee or having a light snack.

Examples

Coffee breaks can be a real headache if not regulated.

Synset Examples

a ten-minute coffee break
the British have tea breaks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tea break - a short rest during the workday for tea or a snack (British English)
