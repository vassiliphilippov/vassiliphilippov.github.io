Scotland Yard

1. PROPN

/ ˈskɑːt.lənd jɑrd / (AmE), / ˈskɒt.lənd jɑːd / (BrE)
the headquarters and detective department of the London Metropolitan Police, often used to refer to the force's criminal investigation service

Examples

The curious relationship between Holmes and Scotland Yard provides an important clue to the deeper significance of his eccentric behavior.
Although he is perfectly willing to cooperate with Scotland Yard, Holmes has nothing but contempt for the intelligence and mentality of the police.

Etymology

Named after Great Scotland Yard, a street in Westminster, London, where the original headquarters were located.
