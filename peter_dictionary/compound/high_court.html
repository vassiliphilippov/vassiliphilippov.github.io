high court

1. NOUN

/ haɪ kɔrt / (AmE), / haɪ kɔːt / (BrE)
the highest court of law in a country or state, whose decisions are final unless overturned by a higher authority

Examples

REPORTER: Can you give us your reaction to the High Court of Israel'’s ruling?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: supreme court - the highest court in a country or state, especially the United States Supreme Court
