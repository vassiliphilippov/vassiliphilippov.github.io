second hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd hænd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd hænd / (BrE)
A means or person from which something, especially information, is obtained indirectly rather than directly from the original source.

Examples

He could learn at second hand from books, but could not thus capture the real Jewish spirit.

Synset Examples

he could learn at second hand from books

Related Terms

  • Antonym: first hand - directly from the original source
