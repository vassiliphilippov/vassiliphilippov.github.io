ten-gallon hat

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛn ˈɡælən ˌhæt / (AmE), / ˌtɛn ˈɡælən ˌhæt / (BrE)
A broad-brimmed, high-crowned hat traditionally associated with American cowboys, typically made of felt.

Examples

And so, after a flight southeast to Savannakhet, we found ourselves bouncing along in a Jeep right behind the Land-Rover of Prince Boun Oum of Champassak, a tall man of Churchillian mien in a bush jacket and a ten-gallon hat from Texas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cowboy hat - another term for a ten-gallon hat, wide-brimmed hat worn by cowboys

Etymology

The term perhaps derives from the Spanish 'galón' meaning 'braid', referring to decorative hatbands; not from a literal capacity of ten gallons.
