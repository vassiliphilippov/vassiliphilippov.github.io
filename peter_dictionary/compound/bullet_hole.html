bullet hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈbʊlət hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪt həʊl / (BrE)
A hole made by a bullet, typically indicating where a bullet has passed through a surface.

Examples

And no wonder, for Vientiane, the old City of Sandalwood, had become the City of Bullet Holes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hole - an opening in or through something
