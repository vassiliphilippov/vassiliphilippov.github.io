solar system

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlər ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
the sun and all the planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other celestial objects that are gravitationally bound to it

Etymology
