weight gaining

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪt ˌɡeɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪt ˌɡeɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
The process or activity of increasing body weight, especially by building muscle mass, as in bodybuilding.

Examples

About that time he began reading Mr. America and Muscle Builder and he learned of the famous Weider way to fast weight gaining.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: weight loss - the process of losing body weight
