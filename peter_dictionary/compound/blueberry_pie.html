blueberry pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈbluːˌbɛri paɪ / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri paɪ / (BrE)
A dessert pie filled with blueberries and sugar, typically baked in a pastry crust.

Examples

"The blueberry pie is good, Scotty.
