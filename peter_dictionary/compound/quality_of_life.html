quality of life

1. NOUN

/ ˌkwɑːləti əv laɪf / (AmE), / ˌkwɒləti əv laɪf / (BrE)
The general well-being and satisfaction a person experiences in their life, especially with regard to mental, emotional, and social aspects rather than just material wealth.

Examples

I take the central meaning here to be the contrast between the drab empty quality of life without literature and a life enriched by it.

Synset Examples

the new art museum is expected to improve the quality of life

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: standard of living - level of material comfort in terms of goods and services available
