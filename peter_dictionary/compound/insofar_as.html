insofar as

1. ADV

/ ɪnsoʊˈfɑɹ æz / (AmE), / ɪnsəʊˈfɑː æz / (BrE)
To the extent or degree that; to such an extent that a particular statement applies.

Examples

They, uh, they went home and sat in a hot bath, opened up their veins and bled to death.

Synset Examples

insofar as it can be ascertained, the horse lung is comparable to that of man
so far as it is reasonably practical he should practice restraint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: as far as - to the degree or extent that
  • Synonym: so far as - to the degree or extent that

Etymology

From 'in so far as', using 'so far' meaning 'to such an extent'.
