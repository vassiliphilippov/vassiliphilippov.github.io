fast-growing

1. ADJ

/ fæst ˈɡroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈɡrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
Growing or increasing rapidly in size, number, or importance.

Examples

How can you cash in on this fast-growing type of outlet and still maintain relationships with older existing outlets which are still important?

Synset Examples

an aggressive tumor

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rapid - happening or moving very quickly
  • Antonym: slow-growing - growing or increasing slowly
