hard-pressed

1. ADJ

/ hɑrdˈprɛst / (AmE), / hɑːdˈprɛst / (BrE)
facing great difficulty or pressure, especially with money or resources.

Examples

Local school districts are hard pressed financially and unenthusiastic about vocational training.

Synset Examples

distressed companies need loans and technical advice
financially hard-pressed Mexican hotels are lowering their prices
we were hard put to meet the mortgage payment
found themselves in a bad way financially

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distressed - facing hardship or difficulty, especially financially
  • Synonym: struggling - having difficulty in a difficult situation, especially with finances
