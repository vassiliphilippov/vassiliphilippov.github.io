legal right

1. NOUN

/ ˈliɡəl raɪt / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl raɪt / (BrE)
A right that is recognized and protected by law.

Examples

Now Mr. Corleone, you have been advised as to your legal rights.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: civil right - a right guaranteed to a person by law, especially by the U.S. Constitution
  • Degree Lower: human right - a basic right that is believed to belong to every person
  • Degree Lower: privilege - a special right, advantage, or immunity granted to a particular person or group
