shoe repairing

1. NOUN

/ ʃu rɪˈpɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃuː rɪˈpeərɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or occupation of mending or restoring shoes to a usable condition.

Examples

They were learning trades as diverse as shoe repairing, bricklaying, carpentry, cabinet making, auto mechanics, and airplane mechanics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shoemaking - the craft or occupation of making or repairing shoes
