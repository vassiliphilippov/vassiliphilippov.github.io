ill-conceived

1. ADJ

/ ɪl kənˈsivd / (AmE), / ɪl kənˈsiːvd / (BrE)
(of a plan, idea, etc.) not carefully planned or thought out; likely to fail due to poor judgment or preparation.

Examples

Among the chief victims of such an ill conceived imposition would be religion itself.

Synset Examples

an ill-conceived plan to take over the company

Related Terms

  • Antonym: well-conceived - carefully planned or thought out
  • Synonym: misguided - based on mistaken or misjudged ideas
