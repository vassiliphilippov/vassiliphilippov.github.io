contact sport

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnˌtækt spɔrt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport in which physical body contact between opposing players is an integral and necessary part of the game.

Examples

But with the exception of professional athletes, few contact sports and physical education activities in our schools have any carryover in the adult life of the average American man or woman.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: non-contact sport - a sport in which physical contact between opponents is not allowed or is minimal
