end point

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛnd ˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɛnd ˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
A point or place where something ends, is completed, or terminates, especially the limit or boundary of an interval, process, or object.

Examples

The first possibility results in a closed interval of tangent points in the f-plane, the end points of which fall into category (b) or (c).
