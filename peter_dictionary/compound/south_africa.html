South Africa

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (AmE), / saʊθ ˈæf.rɪ.kə / (BrE)
A country at the southernmost part of Africa, officially known as the Republic of South Africa.

Examples

Justice is a worthy objective; but if justice for Bantus entails driving the government of the Union of South Africa away from the West, then the Bantus must be prepared to carry their identification cards yet a while longer.

Etymology

Named for its geographic location at the south of the African continent.
