cold sober

1. ADJ

/ koʊld ˈsoʊbər / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
completely sober; not affected by alcohol or any other intoxicant in any way.

Examples

Hearing his voice ring raucously up from the road, Kate would await him anxiously and watch perplexed as he walked into the house, cold sober.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stone cold sober - completely and unmistakably sober
