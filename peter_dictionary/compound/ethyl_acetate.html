ethyl acetate

1. NOUN

/ ˈiːθɪl ˈæsəˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːθɪl ˈæsɪteɪt / (BrE)
a colorless, flammable liquid with a sweet, fruity smell, used as a solvent and in perfumes and flavorings
