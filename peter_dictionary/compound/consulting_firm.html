consulting firm

1. NOUN

/ kənˈsʌl.tɪŋ fɝm / (AmE), / kənˈsʌl.tɪŋ fɜːm / (BrE)
a company of experts that provides professional advice to organizations for a fee

Examples

The report, culminating a year long study of the ADC program in Cook county by a New York City welfare consulting firm, listed 10 long range recommendations designed to reduce the soaring ADC case load.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consultancy - a professional practice that provides expert advice
  • Synonym: firm - a business organization, especially one involving a partnership of lawyers or accountants
