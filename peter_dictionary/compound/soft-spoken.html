soft-spoken

1. ADJ

/ sɔft ˈspoʊkən / (AmE), / sɒft ˈspəʊkən / (BrE)
(of a person) having a gentle, quiet, or mild manner of speaking; not loud or harsh in speech.

Examples

Sleepy-eyed, soft-spoken Johnson.

Synset Examples

she was always soft-spoken

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loud-voiced - having a loud or strong speaking manner
