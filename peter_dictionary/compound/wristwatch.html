wristwatch

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɪstˌwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɪstˌwɒtʃ / (BrE)
A small clock worn on a strap around the wrist.

Examples

He looked at the luminous dial of his wrist watch.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watch - a small timepiece worn typically on a strap on the wrist or carried in a pocket

Etymology

From 'wrist' + 'watch', combining the body part and the timepiece.
