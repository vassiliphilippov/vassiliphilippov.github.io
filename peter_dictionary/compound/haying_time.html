haying time

1. NOUN

/ ˈheɪɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈheɪɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
The season when grass is cut, dried, and stored as hay for fodder.

Examples

Haying time was close at hand, and they needed some strong branches to repair a hay rack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: harvest time - the season for harvesting crops
