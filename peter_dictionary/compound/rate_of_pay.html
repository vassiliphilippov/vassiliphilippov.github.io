rate of pay

1. NOUN

/ reɪt əv peɪ / (AmE), / reɪt əv peɪ / (BrE)
the amount of money paid for work per unit of time, such as per hour or per week

Examples

Traditionally, rates of pay for retired military personnel have been proportionate to current rates of pay for active personnel.
Traditionally, rates of pay for retired military personnel have been proportionate to current rates of pay for active personnel.

Synset Examples

women's pay rate is lower than men's

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wage - regular payment for work, typically paid hourly, daily, or weekly
  • Synonym: salary - fixed regular payment, typically paid monthly or biweekly
