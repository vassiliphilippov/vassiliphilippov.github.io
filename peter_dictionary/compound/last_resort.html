last resort

1. NOUN

/ læst rɪˈzɔrt / (AmE), / lɑːst rɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
a final course of action or option used when all else has failed or been tried

Examples

But I promised Joyce I would mention her name, if at all, only as a last resort.

Synset Examples

`pis aller' is French for `worst going'

Related Terms

  • Synonym: final recourse - ultimate source of help when all other means fail
  • Antonym: first choice - preferred or initial option before others are considered

Etymology

From French 'pis aller' (worst going), calqued into English.
