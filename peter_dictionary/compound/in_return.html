in return

1. ADV

/ ɪn rɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / ɪn rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
as a response or payment to someone for something they have done; in exchange or reciprocation.

Examples

Not for a million, not for ten million.
Not for a million, not for ten million.

Synset Examples

gave up our seats on the plane and in return received several hundred dollars and seats on the next plane out
we get many benefits in return for our taxes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in exchange - as substitution or trade for something else
  • Synonym: in compensation - as something given to balance or offset something else
