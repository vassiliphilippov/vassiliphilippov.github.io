sports jacket

1. NOUN

/ spɔrts ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / spɔːts ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A lightweight, tailored jacket, typically single-breasted, often worn as informal or semi-formal outerwear and originally associated with sporting or country attire.

Examples

Dark gray sports jacket, lighter gray slacks, pink flannel shirt, black silk necktie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blazer - a lightweight, often brightly colored or striped jacket, frequently with a club badge
