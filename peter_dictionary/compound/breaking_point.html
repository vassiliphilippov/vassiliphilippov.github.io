breaking point

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
The moment or level of stress at which a person loses control or a situation becomes critical or unsustainable.

Examples

Every man has his breaking point.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snap - to suddenly lose control under stress
  • Synonym: collapse - to break down completely from stress or strain
