garbage collector

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ kəˌlɛktər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ kəˌlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect and dispose of household or commercial refuse

Examples

His energy was prodigious; sometimes he would be up before dawn, clad as a garbage collector and hurling pails into areaways to exasperate us, and thereafter would hurry to the Bronx Zoo to grimace at the lions and press cigar butts against their paws.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustman - a person employed to collect refuse (chiefly British)
  • Synonym: refuse collector - person who collects and disposes of refuse
