tv room

1. NOUN

/ tiː ˈviː ruːm / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or building designated for watching television

Examples

Charlie ate some supper in the kitchen and went into the TV room to hear the news.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: living room - room in a house for general everyday use, often with a television
