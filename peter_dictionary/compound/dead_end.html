dead end

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ɛnd / (AmE), / dɛd ɛnd / (BrE)
A road, passage, or path that has an entrance but no exit; it ends abruptly and does not lead anywhere else.

Examples

The great "sea to the westwards "was a dead end.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cul-de-sac - a street or passage closed at one end
