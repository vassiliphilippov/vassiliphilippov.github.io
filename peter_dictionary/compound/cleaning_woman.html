cleaning woman

1. NOUN

/ ˈkliː.nɪŋ ˌwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈkliː.nɪŋ ˌwʊ.mən / (BrE)
A woman whose job is to clean houses or offices for other people.

Examples

Well, where I get hazy is where the cleaning woman shows up the following morning and finds your wife in bed with her lover, riddled with .38 caliber bullets.

Synset Examples

the char will clean the carpet
I have a woman who comes in four hours a day while I write

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cleaner - a person whose job is to clean; may refer to any gender
  • Synonym: cleaning lady - another term for a female cleaner
  • Synonym: charwoman - a woman employed to do cleaning, especially in offices or other people’s houses
  • Antonym: cleaning man - a man employed to do cleaning work
