domestic animal

1. NOUN

/ dəˈmɛstɪk ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk ˈanɪməl / (BrE)
An animal that has been tamed and kept by humans for work, food, or as a pet.

Examples

Man may also be injured secondarily by damage to his food crops or domestic animals.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: pet - an animal kept for companionship
  • Antonym: wild animal - an animal living independently of humans
