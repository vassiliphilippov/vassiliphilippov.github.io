living arrangement

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪvɪŋ əˈreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ əˈreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
the way in which people organize and manage their living situation, especially regarding who lives together and how

Examples

Television has yet to work out a living arrangement with jazz, which comes to the medium more as an uneasy guest than as a relaxed member of the family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: household - a group of people, often a family, who live together in a single home
