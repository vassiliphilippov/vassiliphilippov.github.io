do-it-yourself

1. ADJ

/ ˌdu ɪt jərˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˌduː ɪt jəˈsɛlf / (BrE)
(of activities or projects) done by oneself rather than employing a professional; relating to or involving making, building, or repairing things on one's own.

Examples

Hallmark Cards, Inc., Kansas City, Mo., has a do-it-yourself quiz game called "Benefit Bafflers ", which it distributes to employees.
An alternate roof, perhaps more within do-it-yourself reach, could be constructed of heavy wooden roof beams, overlaid with boards and waterproofing.

Synset Examples

their house was a do-it-yourself project
he opened a do-it-yourself store

Related Terms

  • Synonym: DIY - abbreviation for do-it-yourself

Etymology

Formed from the words 'do', 'it', and 'yourself', gaining popularity in the mid-20th century, especially in home improvement contexts.
