last-minute

1. ADJ

/ ˈlæst ˌmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɑːst ˌmɪnɪt / (BrE)
Happening or done at the latest possible time before a deadline or before something happens.

Examples

Andy invented a job to keep him busy, sending him ahead to El Dorado to supervise last minute arrangements.

Synset Examples

last-minute arrangements

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eleventh-hour - happening at the last possible moment
