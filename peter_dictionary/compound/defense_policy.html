defense policy

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈfɛns ˈpɑləsi / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
A government's official strategy or program for protecting the country against external threats or military attacks.

Examples

We cannot regard foreign policy as something left over after defense policy or trade policy or fiscal policy has been extracted.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foreign policy - government's plan for dealing with other countries
  • Synonym: military policy - government's policy regarding armed forces
