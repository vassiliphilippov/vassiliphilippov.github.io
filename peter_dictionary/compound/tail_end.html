tail end

1. NOUN

/ teɪl ɛnd / (AmE), / teɪl ɛnd / (BrE)
The final part or concluding section of something.

Examples

...but towards the end, he was paroled to help with the American war effort, so for six months he's worked in my pastry shop.

Synset Examples

the fag end of this crisis-ridden century
the tail of the storm

Related Terms

  • Antonym: beginning - the first or starting part of something
  • Synonym: end - the final point or part of something

Etymology

From 'tail' (the rear part of an animal or object) and 'end.'
