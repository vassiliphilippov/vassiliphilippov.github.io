good time

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
an occasion or experience that is very enjoyable or exciting

Examples

Niggers are having a good time with our policy banks in Harlem.

Synset Examples

we had a good time at the party
celebrating after the game was a blast

Related Terms

  • Synonym: blast - an enjoyable and exciting experience
