White House

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪt ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
the executive branch of the United States government, headed by the President; the office and administration of the U.S. President

Examples

On April 25, the White House reported that a total embargo of remaining U. S. trade with Cuba was being considered.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: administration - the government in power, especially in the U.S.; the executive branch

Etymology

From the official residence of the U.S. President at 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue, named for its white-painted exterior.
