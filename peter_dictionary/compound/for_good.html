for good

1. ADV

/ fər ɡʊd / (AmE), / fə ɡʊd / (BrE)
Permanently; for all time; forever.

Examples

Changing his clothes, he put on his dark-blue flannel suit, and laid away the gray jacket with the feeling that he might be putting it aside for good.

Synset Examples

he is permanently disabled

Related Terms

  • Synonym: permanently - in a way that lasts or continues without change
  • Antonym: temporarily - for a limited or short time only
