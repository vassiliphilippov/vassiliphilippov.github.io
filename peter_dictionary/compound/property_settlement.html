property settlement

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɑːpɚti ˈsɛtəlmənt / (AmE), / ˈprɒpəti ˈsɛtəlmənt / (BrE)
an agreement or legal process for dividing property between spouses after their marriage ends, especially during divorce or separation

Examples

Attorneys for the mayor said that an amicable property settlement has been agreed upon.
