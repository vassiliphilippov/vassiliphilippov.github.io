course of action

1. NOUN

/ kɔrs əv ˈækʃən / (AmE), / kɔːs əv ˈækʃən / (BrE)
A plan or series of steps taken to achieve a particular result; a way of dealing with a situation.

Examples

Dare I suggest that your present course of action... ... might simply strengthen her resolve?

Synset Examples

if you persist in that course you will surely fail
once a nation is embarked on a course of action it becomes extremely difficult for any retraction to take place

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plan - a detailed proposal for doing or achieving something
  • Synonym: strategy - a plan of action designed to achieve a long-term goal
