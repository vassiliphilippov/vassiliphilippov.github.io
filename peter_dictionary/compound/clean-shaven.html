clean-shaven

1. ADJ

/ klin ˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / kliːn ˈʃeɪvən / (BrE)
(of a man) possessing a face that has been recently and closely shaved; without facial hair.

Examples

They were stocky men, well fed and clean-shaven, with neat uniforms and sturdy boots.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bearded - having a beard; with facial hair
