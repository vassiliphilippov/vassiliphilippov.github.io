military police

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri pəˈlis / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri pəˈliːs / (BrE)
A branch of the armed forces responsible for maintaining discipline, enforcing laws, and guarding prisoners within the military.

Examples

A rebel was being arrested by the military police.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: MP - abbreviation for military police
