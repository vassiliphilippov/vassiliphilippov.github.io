flat bench

1. NOUN

/ flæt bɛntʃ / (AmE), / flæt bɛntʃ / (BrE)
a bench with a flat surface, used for lying on while performing weightlifting exercises such as the bench press.

Examples

Super-Set No. 2 is made up of similar exercises, but this time done with dumbbells, and using both "moon "and flat benches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bench press - an exercise performed lying on a bench while pressing a weight upward from the chest
