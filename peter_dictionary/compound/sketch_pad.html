sketch pad

1. NOUN

/ ˈskɛtʃ ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈskɛtʃ ˌpad / (BrE)
a pad of blank sheets of paper bound together, used for making sketches or drawings

Examples

A man with a sketch pad in hand sat with a large pink woman in a small office at the end of a long, dim corridor and made pencil lines on paper and said, "Is this more like it, Mrs. MacReady?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sketchbook - a book of blank pages for sketches and drawings
