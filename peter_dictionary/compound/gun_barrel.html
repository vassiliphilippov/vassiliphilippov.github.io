gun barrel

1. NOUN

/ ɡʌn ˈbærəl / (AmE), / ɡʌn ˈbærəl / (BrE)
the metal tube of a firearm through which a bullet or projectile travels when the gun is fired

Examples

He drew back his arm to slash the gunbarrel across Jess's face, but did n't finish the motion.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: barrel - the tube of a gun through which the bullet travels
