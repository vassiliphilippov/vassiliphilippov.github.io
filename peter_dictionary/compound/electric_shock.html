electric shock

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ʃɑk / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ʃɒk / (BrE)
the use of electricity to inflict punishment or torture by passing an electric current through the body

Examples

Ice baths, electric shocks, lashings, wild dogs, testicle crushers.

Synset Examples

they used cattle prods to administer electric shocks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shock - a sudden painful or damaging event affecting the body or mind
