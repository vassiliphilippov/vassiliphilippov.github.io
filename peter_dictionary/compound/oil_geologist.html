oil geologist

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl dʒiˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɔɪl dʒɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a geologist who specializes in the exploration and study of petroleum and natural gas resources

Examples

This was an enormously long building whose walls were made of rocks, some of them brought home from every continent during his six years as an oil geologist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: petroleum geologist - a geologist who specializes in petroleum geology
