brain tumor

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈtuːmɚ / (AmE), / breɪn ˈtjuːmə / (BrE)
An abnormal mass of cells growing within the brain, which can affect brain function and health.

Examples

writes: Does numbness in the left hand at night, which awakens the person, indicate brain tumor?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tumor - an abnormal mass of tissue
  • Degree Higher: cancer - malignant tumor of potentially unlimited growth
