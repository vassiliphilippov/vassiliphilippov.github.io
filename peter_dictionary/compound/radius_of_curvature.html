radius of curvature

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdiəs əv kərˈveɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəs əv kɜːˈveɪtjə / (BrE)
The radius of the circle that best approximates a curve at a particular point, equal to the reciprocal of the curvature at that point.
