popularity contest

1. NOUN

/ pɑːpjəˈlærɪti ˌkɑːntɛst / (AmE), / pɒpjʊˈlarɪti ˌkɒntɛst / (BrE)
A situation, competition, or event where the winner is decided by who gains the most popular approval rather than by objective criteria.

Examples

You could win a popularity contest at that school without any trouble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty contest - competition judged on attractiveness rather than objective skills
