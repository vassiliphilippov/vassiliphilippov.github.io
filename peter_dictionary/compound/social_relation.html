social relation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
an interaction or association between two or more individuals, especially within a society or group

Examples

And social relations arising out of business ties impose courtesy, if not sympathy, toward resident and visiting Northerners.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: relationship - a connection or association between people or things
