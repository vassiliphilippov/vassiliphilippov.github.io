morale builder

1. NOUN

/ məˈræl ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / məˈrɑːl ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or thing that helps to raise or strengthen the confidence, enthusiasm, or spirit of a group or individual

Examples

That cold frame was my morale builder; its mass of bright bloom set in a border of snow made my spirits rise every time I looked at it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspirer - someone or something that inspires
  • Antonym: demoralizer - someone or something that lowers morale
