very loudly

1. ADV

/ ˈvɛri ˈlaʊdli / (AmE), / ˈvɛri ˈlaʊdli / (BrE)
At a very high volume or intensity of sound; extremely loudly.

Examples

The hall was empty and Alex beckoned; they climbed the stairs which creaked, very loudly to their sensitive ears, and reached the next floor.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: loudly - at a high volume or with much noise
