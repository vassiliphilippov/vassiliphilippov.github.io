insurance claim

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊrəns kleɪm / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃɔːrəns kleɪm / (BrE)
A formal request made to an insurance company for payment based on the terms of an insurance policy.

Examples

Today the private detective will also investigate insurance claims or handle divorce cases, but his primary function remains what it has always been, to assist those who have money in their unending struggle with those who have not.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: claim - a demand or request for something considered due
