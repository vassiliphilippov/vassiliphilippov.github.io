liquid nitrogen

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪkwɪd ˈnaɪtrədʒən / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈnaɪtrɪdʒən / (BrE)
Nitrogen that has been cooled and pressurized into a clear, colorless liquid, used especially for freezing and refrigeration in scientific and industrial applications.
