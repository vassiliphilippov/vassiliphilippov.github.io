slender-waisted

1. ADJ

/ ˈslɛndɚ ˌweɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə ˌweɪstɪd / (BrE)
Having a small or narrow waist.

Examples

Shy, slender-waisted girls at the loom in harmony with the frangipani by the wayside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slim-waisted - having a slim waist
  • Antonym: thick-waisted - having a thick or wide waist
