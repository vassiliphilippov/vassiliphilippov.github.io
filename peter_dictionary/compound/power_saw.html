power saw

1. NOUN

/ ˈpaʊər sɔ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə sɔː / (BrE)
A saw powered by electricity or another motor, used for cutting wood and other materials.

Examples

He went down to Mills and Bradley's Hardware Store and bought a full set of carpenter's tools, including a rotary power saw and several other pieces of power machinery that Mr. Mills said were essential for babbiting and doweling, whatever they were.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: circular saw - A power saw with a circular blade
  • Antonym: handsaw - A saw powered by hand, not by motor
