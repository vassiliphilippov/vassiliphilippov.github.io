gas stove

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs stoʊv / (AmE), / ɡas stəʊv / (BrE)
A stove powered by burning natural gas or propane, used for cooking and heating food.

Examples

Toys he has can be made to act as substitutes for family temptations such as refrigerator and gas stove.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: electric stove - a stove powered by electricity, rather than gas
