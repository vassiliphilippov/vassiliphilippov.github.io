at the least

1. ADV

/ ət ðə list / (AmE), / ət ðə liːst / (BrE)
No less than; at a minimum.

Examples

It's inevitable in this case, for the couple will be separated for, at the least, forty years.
On that first day, Blanche literally thought she was going to die, or, at the very least, go out of her mind.

Synset Examples

at least two hours studying the manual
a tumor at least as big as an orange

Related Terms

  • Synonym: at least - no less than; at a minimum
