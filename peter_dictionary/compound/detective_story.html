detective story

1. NOUN

/ dəˈtɛktɪv ˌstɔri / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛktɪv ˌstɔːri / (BrE)
A story or novel about a person, usually a detective, who investigates crimes and gathers evidence to solve them.

Examples

The second feature, "The Price of Silence ", is a British detective story that will talk your head off.
In the American "hardboiled "detective story of the' 20 s and' 30 s, the spirit of the mad genius from Baker Street lives on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mystery - genre of fiction involving solving a crime or uncovering secrets
