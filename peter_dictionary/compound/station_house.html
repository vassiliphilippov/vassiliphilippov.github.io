station house

1. NOUN

/ ˈsteɪʃən haʊs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪʃən haʊs / (BrE)
a building serving as the headquarters for police in a particular district; a police station

Examples

He did not want to bring the Andruses to the station house too early - Rheinholdt had summoned a press conference, and he did n't want them subjected to the reporters again.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police station - the headquarters or main office of the police in a district
