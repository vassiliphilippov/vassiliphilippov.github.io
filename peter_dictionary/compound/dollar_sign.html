dollar sign

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɑlər saɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒlə saɪn / (BrE)
A symbol representing commercialism or greed, especially when used metaphorically.

Examples

His love of folklore has romanticism in it, and he does n't care much about the dollar sign or the footnote.

Synset Examples

he worships the almighty dollar
