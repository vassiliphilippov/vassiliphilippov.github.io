taxi driver

1. NOUN

/ ˈtæksi ˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈtæksi ˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to drive a taxi, carrying passengers for a fare.

Examples

The other patrons were taxi drivers and art students and small shopkeepers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cab driver - person who drives a taxi cab for a living
