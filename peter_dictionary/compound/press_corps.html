press corps

1. NOUN

/ prɛs kɔːrps / (AmE), / prɛs kɔːps / (BrE)
a group of journalists from different news organizations who regularly cover the same subjects or events, often within a particular field or location

Examples

The bulk of the press corps was covering Rheinholdt's conference.

Synset Examples

the White House press corps

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press - journalists and media collectively
  • Synonym: reporter - person who reports news for a media outlet
