family planning

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæm.ə.li ˈplæn.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæm.ɪl.i ˈplæn.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of controlling the number and timing of children in a family, typically through the use of contraception or other birth control methods
