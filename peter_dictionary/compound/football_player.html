football player

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊtˌbɔl ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
An athlete who plays football, especially American football.

Examples

In her sophomore year she had started going steady with Bobby Joe, who was a football player, Future Homemakers sweetheart, and president of Future Farmers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: footballer - a person who plays football, usually used for soccer in British English
