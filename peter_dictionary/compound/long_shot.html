long shot

1. NOUN

/ lɔŋ ʃɑt / (AmE), / lɒŋ ʃɒt / (BrE)
An attempt or guess that has only a slight chance of succeeding but promises significant rewards if successful.

Examples

But it's such a long shot "-

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outside chance - a small or unlikely possibility
  • Antonym: safe bet - something with a high chance of success
