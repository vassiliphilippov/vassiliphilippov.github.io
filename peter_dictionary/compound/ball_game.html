ball game

1. NOUN

/ bɔl ɡeɪm / (AmE), / bɔːl ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a game played on a field or court in which a ball is used, especially baseball or similar sports.

Examples

Momentarily the Reds were back in the ball game, trailing only 6 - 3, but the drive fizzled when John Edwards fouled out with men on second and third and two out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball - a bat-and-ball game played between two teams
  • Synonym: football - a team sport played with a ball, especially known as soccer in the US
