breathing space

1. NOUN

/ ˈbriðɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (BrE)
A short period of rest or relief from difficulty or pressure.

Examples

About that same time John Crosby's TV series on the popular arts proved again that giving jazz ample breathing space is one of the most sensible things a producer can do.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: respite - a short period of rest or relief from something difficult
  • Synonym: interval - a period of time between events or activities
