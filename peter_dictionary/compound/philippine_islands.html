Philippine Islands

1. PROPN

/ ˌfɪlɪˈpin ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːn ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
an archipelago of over 7,000 islands in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, forming the country of the Philippines

Examples

Australia, the Philippine Islands, Japan, and northern China were inhabited by those members of the Haijac Union that spoke American.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Philippines - Southeast Asian country consisting of the Philippine Islands

Etymology

From 'Philippine', after King Philip II of Spain, and 'islands'.
