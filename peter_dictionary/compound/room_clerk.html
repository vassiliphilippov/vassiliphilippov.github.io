room clerk

1. NOUN

/ ˈruːm klɝk / (AmE), / ˈruːm klɑːk / (BrE)
a hotel employee whose job is to assign rooms to guests and manage room reservations

Examples

Shrugs met that, from room clerks, from bellhops.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: receptionist - a person employed in an office or hotel to receive guests and deal with their requests
