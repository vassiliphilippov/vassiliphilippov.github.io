television camera

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən ˈkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈtelɪˌvɪʒən ˈkæmərə / (BrE)
A camera designed for capturing moving images to be broadcast on television.

Examples

Instead of the observer's eye the image orthicon in the TV camera does the "looking ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: video camera - camera that records moving images electronically
  • Antonym: still camera - camera used to take photographs, as opposed to motion pictures
