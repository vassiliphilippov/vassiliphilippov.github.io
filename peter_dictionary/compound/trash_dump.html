trash dump

1. NOUN

/ træʃ dʌmp / (AmE), / træʃ dʌmp / (BrE)
a place where waste materials and refuse are deposited and left, often in large quantities

Examples

The yards, front and back, were narrow; some were trash dumps, some had flower gardens.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: landfill - a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
  • Synonym: garbage dump - a place where waste materials are dumped
