loud-voiced

1. ADJ

/ laʊd vɔɪst / (AmE), / laʊd vɔɪst / (BrE)
having a noticeably loud or strong-sounding voice

Examples

He was a loud-voiced man, once vigorous but for many years now declining in strength and ability.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft-spoken - having a quiet or gentle voice
