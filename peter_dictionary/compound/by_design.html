by design

1. ADV

/ baɪ dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / baɪ dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
Intentionally; deliberately; as the result of a plan or intention rather than by chance.

Examples

The cowboy films, the cops and robbers films, and the slapstick comedy films culminating in an insane chase are not only catering to what critics may assume to be a vulgar taste for violence; these films and these sequences are also seeking out - instinctively or by design - the peculiarly cinematic elements of narrative.

Synset Examples

he used that word intentionally
I did this by choice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deliberately - In a careful and intentional way; on purpose
  • Antonym: accidentally - Without intention; by chance
