every bit

1. ADV

/ ˈɛv.ɹi bɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ɹi bɪt / (BrE)
used to emphasize that someone or something has as much of a quality as someone or something else (often followed by 'as')

Examples

I saw "Fiorello! "performed in New York by the original cast and I think this company is every bit as good, and perhaps better.

Synset Examples

they were equally beautiful
birds were singing and the child sang as sweetly
sang as sweetly as a nightingale
he is every bit as mean as she is

Related Terms

  • Synonym: equally - to the same degree or extent
