Social Security number

1. NOUN

/ ˌsoʊʃəl səˈkjʊrəti ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃəl sɪˈkjʊərɪti ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
A unique number assigned to an individual by the United States government for Social Security and identification purposes.

Examples

Mr. Stevens has a birth certificate, a driver's license, social security number.
