business agent

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an official who manages business affairs on behalf of an organization or an individual, especially for a labor union or similar body

Examples

Sposato needed a front, some labor stiff with a clean record to act as business agent of the Redhook local.
