wooden leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈwʊdən lɛg / (AmE), / ˈwʊdn lɛg / (BrE)
an artificial leg made of wood, used as a prosthesis to replace a missing leg

Examples

The "wooden-leg "gag of vaudeville, another standby of this sort, had endless variations.
""There's a man outside with a wooden leg named Smith ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prosthesis - an artificial replacement for a body part
