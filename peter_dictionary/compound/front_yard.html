front yard

1. NOUN

/ frʌnt jɑrd / (AmE), / frʌnt jɑːd / (BrE)
An area of land in front of a house, typically between the house and the street, often used as a lawn or garden.

Examples

The heavy branches in his front yard would hide and then reveal them.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: back yard - yard at the rear of a house
