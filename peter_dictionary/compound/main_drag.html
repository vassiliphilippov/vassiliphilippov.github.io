main drag

1. NOUN

/ meɪn dræɡ / (AmE), / meɪn dræɡ / (BrE)
The main street of a town or city, especially one with the most shops and activities; the principal thoroughfare.

Examples

Separating, they took different sides of the main drag and systematically combed the bars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: main street - the primary street in a town, often where most businesses are located

Etymology

Informal American English; 'drag' meaning street or road, from 19th-century slang.
