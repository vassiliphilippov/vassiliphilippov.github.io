public debate

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
A formal discussion or argument on a specific proposal, held in public and usually followed by a vote.

Examples

"If William agrees, we should insist on a public debate ", he said at length.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
