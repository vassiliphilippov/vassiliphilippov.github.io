sweat gland

1. NOUN

/ swɛt ɡlænd / (AmE), / swɛt ɡlænd / (BrE)
A gland in the skin that produces and secretes sweat to help regulate body temperature.

Examples

No, other than cleaning out the pores and making the sweat glands work harder.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sebaceous gland - gland in the skin that secretes oily matter (sebum)
