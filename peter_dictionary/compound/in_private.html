in private

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpraɪvət / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpraɪvət / (BrE)
without other people present; not publicly; out of the view or hearing of others.

Examples

She would weep in private, he was sure, for she loved him in her frigid way, though in public she would be dry eyed.

Synset Examples

it was discussed privately between the two men
privately, she thought differently
some member of his own party hoped privately for his defeat
he was questioned in private

Related Terms

  • Synonym: privately - in a private manner; not publicly
  • Antonym: in public - with other people present; openly
