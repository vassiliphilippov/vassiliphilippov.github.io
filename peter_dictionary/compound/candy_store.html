candy store

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændi stɔr / (AmE), / ˈkændi stɔː / (BrE)
A shop that sells candy and other sweet confections.

Examples

Some retailing, of course, always spreads with the population - grocery stores, drugstores, local haberdasheries and dress shops, candy stores and the like.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sweet shop - British term for a shop selling sweets and confections
