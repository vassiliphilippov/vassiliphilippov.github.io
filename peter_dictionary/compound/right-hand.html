right-hand

1. ADJ

/ ˈraɪtˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈraɪtˌhænd / (BrE)
Designed or intended for use with the right hand.

Examples

Where there is a left-hand entry in the ledger, there is a right-hand one, he remembered from his school days.

Synset Examples

a right-hand glove

Related Terms

  • Antonym: left-hand - designed or intended for the left hand
