temperature change

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛmpərətʃɚ tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərətʃə tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a process in which the degree of hotness or coldness of a substance or environment alters

Examples

The ratio of the measured antenna temperature change during a drift scan across the moon to the average brightness temperature of the moon over the antenna beam (assuming that the brightness temperature of the sky is negligible) was found, by graphical integration of the antenna directivity diagram, to be 0.85.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: temperature - the degree of hotness or coldness measured on a definite scale
  • Synonym: thermal variation - another term for change in temperature
