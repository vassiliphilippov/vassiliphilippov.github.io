low-level

1. ADJ

/ ˈloʊ ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
Not intense; existing in a small degree or amount.

Examples

Continuous low-level lighting may be provided in the shelter by means of a 4 - cell hot-shot battery to which is wired a 150 - milliampere flashlight type bulb.

Synset Examples

low-level radiation

Related Terms

  • Antonym: high-level - very intense or in great quantity
  • Degree Higher: moderate - not extreme; of medium degree or amount
