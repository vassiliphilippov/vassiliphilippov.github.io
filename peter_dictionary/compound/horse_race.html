horse race

1. NOUN

/ hɔrs reɪs / (AmE), / hɔːs reɪs / (BrE)
A contest of speed between horses, typically held as a sporting event and often involving betting.

Examples

Dallas may get to hear a debate on horse race parimutuels soon between Reps. V. E. Red Berry and Joe Ratcliff.
"We 're getting more' pro' letters than' con' on horse race betting ", said Ratcliff.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: race - a competition to see who is fastest
