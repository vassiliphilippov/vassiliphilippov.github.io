in public

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
In a way that can be seen or heard by other people; openly.

Examples

For these crimes he was sentenced to be hanged in public on Friday, December 2, 1859.
She would weep in private, he was sure, for she loved him in her frigid way, though in public she would be dry eyed.

Synset Examples

she admitted publicly to being a communist

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in private - not seen or heard by others; secretly

2. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
In a way that can be seen or heard by other people; openly and not in private.

Examples

Is that why you slapped my brother around in public?

Synset Examples

she admitted publicly to being a communist

Related Terms

  • Antonym: in private - so that other people cannot see or hear; privately
