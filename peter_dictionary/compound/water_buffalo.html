water buffalo

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌbʌfəloʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌbʌfələʊ / (BrE)
A large domesticated Asian buffalo, often used as a draft animal in agriculture.

Examples

They look after their fields and children and water buffaloes in ten or eleven thousand villages, with an average of 200 souls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buffalo - large wild or domesticated bovine animal
