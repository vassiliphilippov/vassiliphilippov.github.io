in unison

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈjunɪsən / (AmE), / ɪn ˈjuːnɪsən / (BrE)
acting or speaking together at the same time; simultaneously, in agreement or harmony

Examples

And the sheep said - all in unison, I have no doubt -' Ba-a-a'!

Synset Examples

they shouted `Yes!' in unison
they responded in chorus to the teacher's questions

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in chorus - speaking or singing at the same time
  • Synonym: in harmony - in agreement or accord
