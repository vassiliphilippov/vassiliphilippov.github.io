funny house

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni haʊs / (AmE), / ˈfʌni haʊs / (BrE)
An informal and pejorative term for a mental institution or insane asylum.

Examples

"Oh, I forgot to say that if one is taken to the funny house in the funny wagon, he is removed to a mental institution in an ambulance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane asylum - a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • Synonym: mental hospital - a hospital for the treatment of mental illness
