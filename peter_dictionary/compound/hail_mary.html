Hail Mary

1. NOUN

/ heɪl ˈmɛri / (AmE), / heɪl ˈmeəri / (BrE)
A traditional prayer addressed to the Virgin Mary, used especially in the Roman Catholic Church.

Examples

FREDO: Hail Mary, full of grace.

Etymology

From Medieval Latin 'Ave Maria' (Hail Mary), the opening words of the prayer in Latin.
