insurance premium

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈʃʊrəns priˈmiəm / (AmE), / ɪnˈʃʊərəns ˈpriːmiəm / (BrE)
A regular payment made to an insurance company to keep an insurance policy active.

Examples

Have you publicized the cents per hour value of the company's share of insurance premiums?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premium - sum paid for an insurance policy
