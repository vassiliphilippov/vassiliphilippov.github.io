of necessity

1. ADV

/ ʌv nəˈsɛsɪti / (AmE), / ɒv nɪˈsɛsɪti / (BrE)
Used to mean 'necessarily' or 'inevitably'; as something that must happen or be the case.

Examples

An idea, of the sort that we have in mind, although of necessity readily available to imagination, is more general in connotation than most poetic or literary images, especially those appearing in lyric poems that seek to capture a moment of personal experience.

Synset Examples

it is necessarily so
we must needs by objective

Related Terms

  • Synonym: necessarily - in a way that cannot be otherwise
  • Synonym: inevitably - in such a manner as cannot be avoided
