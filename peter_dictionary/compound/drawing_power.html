drawing power

1. NOUN

/ ˈdrɔɪŋ ˌpaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈdrɔː.ɪŋ ˌpaʊ.ə / (BrE)
the ability or capacity to attract people, customers, or supporters

Examples

It is the spirit which is the source of a community's drawing power by means of which others are drawn into it from the world outside so that the community grows and prospers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: appeal - the quality of being attractive or interesting
  • Synonym: charisma - compelling attractiveness or charm
