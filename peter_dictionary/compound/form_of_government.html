form of government

1. NOUN

/ fɔrm əv ˈɡʌvərnmənt / (AmE), / fɔːm əv ˈɡʌvənmənt / (BrE)
a particular system or structure by which a community, nation, or organization is governed

Examples

We are reminded, however, that freedom of thought and discussion, the unfettered exchange of ideas, is basic under our form of government.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: government - the group of people with the authority to govern a country or state
  • Synonym: political system - a system of politics and government
