sum of money

1. NOUN

/ sʌm əv ˈmʌni / (AmE), / sʌm əv ˈmʌni / (BrE)
an amount of money; a quantity of money regarded as a single total

Examples

He does n't really need the immense sum of money (probably converted from American gold on the London Exchange) he makes them pay.

Synset Examples

he borrowed a large sum
the amount he had in cash was insufficient

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amount of money - a quantity of money, often interchangeable with 'sum of money'
  • Synonym: sum - the total amount resulting from the addition of two or more numbers; here extended to money
