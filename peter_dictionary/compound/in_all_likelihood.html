in all likelihood

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɔl ˈlaɪklihʊd / (AmE), / ɪn ɔːl ˈlaɪklihʊd / (BrE)
Almost certainly; with a high probability.

Examples

And in all likelihood, by now, there was more than one person in the house who knew the terms of her marriage contract.

Synset Examples

He is probably out of the country
in all likelihood we are headed for war

Related Terms

  • Synonym: probably - very likely; almost certainly
  • Degree Lower: possibly - perhaps; it might happen or be true
  • Degree Higher: certainly - without any doubt; definitely
