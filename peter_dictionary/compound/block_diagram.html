block diagram

1. NOUN

/ blɑk ˈdaɪəˌɡræm / (AmE), / blɒk ˈdaɪəɡram / (BrE)
A schematic diagram using blocks to represent components and lines to show their interconnections, especially in electronic or technical systems.
