board of regents

1. NOUN

/ bɔrd əv rɪˈdʒɛnts / (AmE), / bɔːd əv rɪˈdʒɛnts / (BrE)
A governing body at a college or university, responsible for overseeing policy, administration, and the welfare of the institution.

Examples

The board of regents of Paris Junior College has named Dr. Clarence Charles Clark of Hays, Kan. as the school's new president.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: board of trustees - a group that oversees an institution, often used interchangeably with board of regents, especially outside the US
