car door

1. NOUN

/ kɑɹ dɔɹ / (AmE), / kɑː dɔː / (BrE)
A door fitted to an automobile, allowing entry and exit for passengers or the driver.

Examples

He drove into the paved space before the garage and got out, slamming the car door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: door - a hinged, sliding, or revolving barrier at the entrance to a building, room, or vehicle
