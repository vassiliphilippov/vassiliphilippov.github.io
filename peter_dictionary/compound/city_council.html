city council

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪti ˌkaʊnsəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˌkaʊnsəl / (BrE)
the elected governing body of a city, responsible for making laws, policies, and decisions for the municipality

Examples

His political career goes back to his election to city council in 1923.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: town council - the governing body of a town

Etymology

From 'city' + 'council', the latter from Latin 'concilium' (assembly, meeting).
