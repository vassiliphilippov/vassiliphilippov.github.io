drum roll

1. NOUN

/ drʌm roʊl / (AmE), / drʌm rəʊl / (BrE)
a rapid, continuous beating of a drum, usually a snare drum, often used to create suspense before an announcement or event

Examples

Folks, I want you to join with me in giving a real Nevada "Thank you" [DRUMROLL] to Mr. and Mrs. Michael Corleone! [INAUDIBLE]
