on the way

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə weɪ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə weɪ / (BrE)
While traveling to a particular place; during the course of a journey to a destination.

Examples

Later on he had an idea to build a city out of a desert stop-over for G.I.s on the way to the West Coast.

Synset Examples

help is on the way
we saw him on the way to California

Related Terms

  • Synonym: en route - on the way; along the route to a place
  • Synonym: on the road - traveling, especially by vehicle or as part of a journey
