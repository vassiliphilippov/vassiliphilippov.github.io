Indian summer

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪndiən ˈsʌmɚ / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈsʌmə / (BrE)
A period of warm, dry weather occurring in late autumn, often after the first frost.

Examples

"Warm enough to swim - real Indian summer.

Etymology

Origin uncertain; attested in American English since the late 18th century.
