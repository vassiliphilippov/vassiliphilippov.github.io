fund-raising drive

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ draɪv / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ draɪv / (BrE)
an organized campaign or effort to collect money for a particular cause or purpose

Examples

A fund-raising drive to buy tractors and machinery was launched.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fundraiser - an event or campaign to collect money for a cause
