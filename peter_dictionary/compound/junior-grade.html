junior-grade

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒuːniɚ ɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːniə ɡreɪd / (BrE)
Of inferior or lower rank or status; holding a position below others of the same kind.

Examples

They want to own a junior-grade castle, or a manor house, or some modest little place where Shakespeare might once have staged a pageant for Great Elizabeth and all her bearded courtiers.

Synset Examples

the junior faculty
a lowly corporal
petty officialdom
a subordinate functionary

Related Terms

  • Antonym: senior-grade - of higher rank or status
