Christmas gift

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪsməs ɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs ɡɪft / (BrE)
A present given to someone at Christmas.

Examples

I would like to take this opportunity to thank United Telephone and Telegraph for their lovely Christmas gift.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Christmas present - another term for a present given at Christmas
  • Degree Lower: gift - something given willingly to someone without payment
  • Degree Lower: present - something given to someone on a special occasion
