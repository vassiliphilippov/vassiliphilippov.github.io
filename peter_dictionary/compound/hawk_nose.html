hawk nose

1. NOUN

/ hɑk noʊz / (AmE), / hɔːk nəʊz / (BrE)
A nose that is curved downward like the beak of a hawk.

Examples

Finally, Orville intoned through his hawk nose, "We can n't have people running in any time they please, Sergeant ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aquiline nose - a nose that is curved or hooked like an eagle's beak
