personal business

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɝsənl ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
private matters that are of personal concern and not intended to be shared with others

Examples

Don't ask me about my business.

Synset Examples

get his affairs in order

Related Terms

  • Synonym: private affairs - matters kept confidential to oneself
  • Degree Higher: business - a person's regular occupation, profession, or private concerns
