flashlight battery

1. NOUN

/ ˈflæʃˌlaɪt ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˈflæʃˌlaɪt ˈbætəri / (BrE)
a small dry battery used for powering a flashlight, typically containing dry cells

Examples

The safe is a repository for three dead flashlight batteries, a hundred and fifty unused left-hand fingerprint cards, a stack of unsold Policemen's Ball tickets from last year, and thirty-seven cents in coins and stamps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dry cell - an electric cell in which the electrolyte is absorbed in a solid to form a paste
