evaporative cooler

1. NOUN

/ ɪˌvæpəˌreɪtɪv ˈkulər / (AmE), / ɪˌvapəˌreɪtɪv ˈkuːlə / (BrE)
a device that reduces air temperature by passing it over or through water so that it cools by evaporation

Examples

The evaporative cooler had been moved to Granny's room, and her door was kept shut; so that the rest of the house stayed open, though there was a question as to whether it was hotter or cooler that way.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: air conditioner - a machine that cools and dehumidifies the air in a building or vehicle

Etymology

From 'evaporative', relating to evaporation, and 'cooler', meaning a device that cools.
