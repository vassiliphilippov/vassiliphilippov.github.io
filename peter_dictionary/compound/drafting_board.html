drafting board

1. NOUN

/ ˈdræftɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A flat, smooth board used as a surface for drawing plans or technical illustrations, often by architects or designers.

Examples

His files, desk, drafting board and a high stool formed the only clean island in the chaos.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drawing board - a board for drawing on, especially for technical designs
