man and wife

1. NOUN

/ mæn ænd waɪf / (AmE), / mæn ænd waɪf / (BrE)
A man and a woman who are married to each other; husband and wife considered together as a couple.

Examples

The petition said that the couple has not lived together as man and wife for more than a year.

Synset Examples

his second marriage was happier than the first
a married couple without love

Related Terms

  • Synonym: married couple - a pair of people joined by marriage
  • Synonym: bride and groom - man and woman at their wedding ceremony
