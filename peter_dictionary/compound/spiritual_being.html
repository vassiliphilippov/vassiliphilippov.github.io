spiritual being

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl ˈbiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtjʊəl ˈbiːɪŋ / (BrE)
A supernatural or incorporeal entity believed to have powers to influence human affairs, such as angels, demons, or fairies.

Examples

Demons, fairies, angels, and a host of other spiritual beings were as much a part of the experiential world of western man as were rocks and trees and stars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: spirit - a non-physical being or force believed to animate living things or exist independently
