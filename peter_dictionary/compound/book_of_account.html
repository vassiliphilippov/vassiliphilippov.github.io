book of account

1. NOUN

/ bʊk əv əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / bʊk əv əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a written record in which a business's financial transactions are systematically entered; a ledger

Examples

And behind every shady deal, behind every dollar earned, there was Andy, keeping the books.

Synset Examples

they got a subpoena to examine our books

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ledger - a book or digital record in which financial transactions are recorded
