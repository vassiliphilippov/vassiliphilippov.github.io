covered wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌvɚd ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈkʌvəd ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
a large wagon with broad wheels and an arched canvas cover, used by pioneers in the United States to travel across the prairies in the 19th century

Examples

Just as modern transportation has outmoded the early Studebaker covered wagon, the demand of today's sportsmen and women has necessitated changes in their equipment.
