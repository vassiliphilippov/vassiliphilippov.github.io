tax-free

1. ADJ

/ tæks ˈfri / (AmE), / tæks ˈfriː / (BrE)
Not subject to taxation; exempt from tax.

Examples

Stocks, securities, tax-free municipals.

Synset Examples

tax-exempt bonds
an untaxed expense account

Related Terms

  • Antonym: taxable - subject to tax
  • Synonym: tax-exempt - exempt from tax, not required to pay tax
