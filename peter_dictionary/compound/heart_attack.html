heart attack

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrt əˌtæk / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt əˌtæk / (BrE)
A sudden and severe medical condition in which the blood supply to the heart is blocked, causing damage to heart muscle.

Examples

No, no, that was no heart attack.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: myocardial infarction - medical term for heart attack
