rock outcrop

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈaʊtkrɑp / (AmE), / rɒk ˈaʊtkrɒp / (BrE)
A part of a rock formation that is visible above the ground surface.

Examples

Check the elevation of the ground, degree and direction of slopes, drainage, rock outcrops, topsoil types and quality, as well as subsoil.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: outcrop - an exposed part of bedrock or rock formation at the surface
