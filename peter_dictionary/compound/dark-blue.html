dark-blue

1. ADJ

/ dɑrk bluː / (AmE), / dɑːk bluː / (BrE)
Of a dark shade of blue; deeper or more intense than standard blue.

Examples

Changing his clothes, he put on his dark-blue flannel suit, and laid away the gray jacket with the feeling that he might be putting it aside for good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: navy blue - a very dark shade of blue
  • Degree Lower: blue - the color between green and violet on the spectrum
