Icelandic-speaking

1. ADJ

/ aɪˌslændɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / aɪˌslændɪk ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
Able to communicate in the Icelandic language.

Examples

In southern Europe, between the Israeli Republics and the Icelandic speaking peoples of northern Europe, was a thin but long stretch of territory called March.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: Icelandic - the North Germanic language spoken in Iceland
  • Degree Higher: multilingual - able to speak several languages
