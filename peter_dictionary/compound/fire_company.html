fire company

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪɚ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
an organized group of people equipped and trained to fight fires, usually forming a unit within a fire department

Examples

"Eagle's Nest ought to have a fire company.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fire brigade - an organized body of firefighters, often referring to the overall service
