operating procedure

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑpəˌreɪtɪŋ prəˈsidʒər / (AmE), / ˈɒpəˌreɪtɪŋ prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
a set way of doing something, especially as part of the routine operations of an organization or system

Examples

The jury said it found the court "has incorporated into its operating procedures the recommendations "of two previous grand juries, the Atlanta Bar Association and an interim citizens committee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: standard operating procedure - official established method for carrying out operations
