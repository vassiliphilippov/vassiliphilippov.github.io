sweat bag

1. NOUN

/ swɛt bæɡ / (AmE), / swɛt baɡ / (BrE)
a porous canvas bag for holding water, which cools the contents by evaporation

Examples

They could no longer afford the luxury of the canvas sweat bag that cooled it by evaporation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: water bag - a container for carrying water, often made of leather or canvas
