three-way

1. ADJ

/ ˈθriˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθriːweɪ / (BrE)
Involving or shared by three parties, elements, or directions.

Examples

We must ask ourselves which of the two alternatives will help the commuter - the two-way B. + O. - C. + O. merger, or the three way New York Central B. + O. - C. + O. merger.

Synset Examples

a tripartite treaty
a tripartite division
a three-way playoff

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: two-way - involving two parties or elements
  • Degree Higher: four-way - involving four parties or elements
