fellow worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɛloʊ ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈfɛləʊ ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
A person who works with another; a colleague or coworker.

Examples

There I was a retired wobbly and structural iron worker who 'd never gouged a cent off a fellow worker in my thirty years in the movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: colleague - a person with whom one works, especially in a profession or business
  • Synonym: coworker - a person with whom one works, typically someone in a similar position or department
