waste of money

1. NOUN

/ weɪst əv ˈmʌni / (AmE), / weɪst əv ˈmʌni / (BrE)
An unnecessary or excessive expenditure that provides little or no value in return.

Examples

Waste of money, if you ask me.

Synset Examples

the senator said that the project was a waste of money

Related Terms

  • Synonym: extravagance - excessive spending of money
  • Antonym: bargain - something bought for less than the usual price; good value
