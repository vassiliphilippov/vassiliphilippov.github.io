given name

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvən neɪm / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvən neɪm / (BrE)
A person's name that is given at birth or baptism, placed before the family name or surname.

Examples

Mary, come here.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: first name - the name that precedes the surname
  • Antonym: surname - the family name, usually placed after the given name
