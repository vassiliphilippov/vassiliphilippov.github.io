life science

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / laɪf ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
a branch of science that deals with the structure and behavior of living organisms, such as biology, botany, or zoology

Examples

In the life sciences, there has been an enormous increase in our understanding of disease, in the mechanisms of heredity, and in bio - and physiological chemistry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: biology - the scientific study of life and living organisms
