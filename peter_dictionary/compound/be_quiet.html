be quiet

1. VERB

/ bi ˈkwaɪət / (AmE), / bi ˈkwaɪət / (BrE)
to stop talking, making noise, or causing disturbance; to become or remain silent

Examples

We had to be quiet when we played near here.

Synset Examples

The children shut up when their father approached

Related Terms

  • Synonym: be silent - to refrain from speaking or making noise; to be silent
  • Antonym: be noisy - to make a lot of noise, the opposite of being quiet
