sketch map

1. NOUN

/ skɛtʃ mæp / (AmE), / skɛtʃ mæp / (BrE)
A simple map drawn from observation, showing only the main features of an area, not based on exact measurements.

Examples

It is usually helpful to make a sketch map in the field, showing the size and location of the features of interest and to take photographs at the site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: map - a visual representation of an area
