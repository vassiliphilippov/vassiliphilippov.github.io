labor movement

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.bɚ ˌmuːv.mənt / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə ˌmuːv.mənt / (BrE)
an organized effort by workers, often through unions, to improve their working conditions, rights, and status

Examples

Pat had contacts all over the labor movement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade unionism - the system or principles of organizing workers into unions
  • Synonym: unionism - support for labor unions and their activities
