club soda

1. NOUN

/ klʌb ˈsoʊdə / (AmE), / klʌb ˈsəʊdə / (BrE)
A type of carbonated water that is artificially infused with carbon dioxide and often contains added minerals.

Examples

And, uh, a club soda.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soda water - carbonated water, sometimes with minerals added
  • Synonym: sparkling water - natural or artificial carbonated water, usually without added minerals
