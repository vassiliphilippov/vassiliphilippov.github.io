back tooth

1. NOUN

/ bæk tuθ / (AmE), / bæk tuːθ / (BrE)
A tooth located towards the rear of the mouth, such as a premolar or molar, used primarily for grinding food.

Examples

In addition, it is small enough to get into crevices, jacket and crown margins, malposed anteriors, and the back teeth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: molar - a large tooth at the back of the mouth used for grinding food
