bad guy

1. NOUN

/ bæd ɡaɪ / (AmE), / bad ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a villainous or hostile manner, especially an antagonist in a story, film, or conflict

Examples

The material of the Porter film is simplicity itself; much of it has continued to be used over the years and the heart of it - good guys and bad guys in the old West - pretty well dominated television toward the end of the 1950's.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: antagonist - a person who actively opposes or is hostile to someone or something; an adversary
  • Antonym: good guy - a person who behaves in a heroically good or friendly manner, especially the protagonist
