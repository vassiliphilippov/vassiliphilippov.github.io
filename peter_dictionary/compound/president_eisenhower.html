President Eisenhower

1. PROPN

/ ˈprɛzɪdənt ˈaɪzənˌhaʊər / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdənt ˈaɪz(ə)nˌhaʊə / (BrE)
(1890–1961) The 34th President of the United States, Dwight D. Eisenhower, who was also a five-star general in World War II and supervised the invasion of Normandy and the defeat of Nazi Germany.
