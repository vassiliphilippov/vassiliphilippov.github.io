straw hat

1. NOUN

/ strɔ hæt / (AmE), / strɔː hæt / (BrE)
a stiff hat made from woven straw, typically with a flat crown and brim, worn for sun protection or as a summer accessory

Examples

There was a man's jacket on the chair and a straw hat on the table.
Miss Ada had been out back, in a straw hat, planting flowers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: boater - a stiff, flat-crowned straw hat, also known as a 'boater'
