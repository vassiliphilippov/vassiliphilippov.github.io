loading dock

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊdɪŋ dɑk / (AmE), / ˈləʊdɪŋ dɒk / (BrE)
A platform at the back or side of a building where trucks or trains are loaded or unloaded.

Examples

Andy come down to the loading dock today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dock - a structure for loading or unloading ships or vehicles
