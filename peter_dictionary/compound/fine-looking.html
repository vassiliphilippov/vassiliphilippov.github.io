fine-looking

1. ADJ

/ ˈfaɪn ˌlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪn ˌlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
Attractive or pleasing in appearance, especially due to good proportion or conformity to ideals of beauty.

Examples

Winston watched him hurry down the drive to his car; a handsome, fine-looking man it made him proud to see.

Synset Examples

a fine-looking woman
a good-looking man
better-looking than her sister
very pretty but not so extraordinarily handsome
our southern women are well-favored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good-looking - attractive in appearance
  • Synonym: handsome - having a pleasing and impressive appearance
  • Antonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance
