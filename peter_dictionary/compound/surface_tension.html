surface tension

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːfɪs ˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs ˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
the force that causes the molecules on the surface of a liquid to be pushed together and form a layer, due to intermolecular attraction
