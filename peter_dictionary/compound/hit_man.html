hit man

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɪt mæn / (AmE), / ˈhɪt mæn / (BrE)
A person who is hired to kill someone, usually with a firearm, for payment; a contract killer.

Examples

I don'’t want to hear about it!...are your button men!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assassin - a person who murders a prominent figure, often for political or ideological reasons
  • Synonym: gunman - a person who uses a gun to commit violent acts
  • Antonym: bodyguard - a person hired to protect someone from harm
