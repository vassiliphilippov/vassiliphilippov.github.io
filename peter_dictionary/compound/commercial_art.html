commercial art

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɜrʃəl ɑrt / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃl ɑːt / (BrE)
art created primarily for commercial purposes, such as advertising, packaging, or promotional materials

Examples

But it did teach Roy the basic techniques of commercial art, and later, for twelve years, he and his sister Nina conducted an advertising art studio in Philadelphia.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: fine art - art created primarily for aesthetic or intellectual purposes
