participation loan

1. NOUN

/ pɑrˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən loʊn / (AmE), / pɑːˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃən ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan in which a group of banks or lenders share the lending and risk, typically for amounts too large for any one institution to provide alone

Examples

SBA business loans are of two types: "participation "and "direct ".
Participation loans are those made jointly by the SBA and banks or other private lending institutions.
