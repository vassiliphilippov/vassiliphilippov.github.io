governor's race

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡʌvərnərz reɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌv(ə)nəz reɪs / (BrE)
an election campaign for the office of governor in a state or territory

Examples

Georgia Republicans are getting strong encouragement to enter a candidate in the 1962 governor's race, a top official said Wednesday.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: election - a formal process of selecting a person for public office
