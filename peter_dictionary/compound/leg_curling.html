leg curling

1. NOUN

/ lɛɡ ˈkɜrlɪŋ / (AmE), / lɛɡ ˈkɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
An exercise in which the flexor muscles of the leg are strengthened by repeatedly bending the knee against resistance, typically using a machine.

Examples

But for purely definition purposes - used in conjunction with your regular Squatting, Leg Curling, Leg Extensor programs - a heavy weight is not needed.
