tightly fitting

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪtli ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtli ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Fitting closely or snugly to the shape of something, without being loose.

Examples

A 10 - gallon garbage can, with a tightly fitting cover, could be used to keep the wastes until it is safe to leave the shelter.

Synset Examples

a tightly-fitting cover
tight-fitting clothes

Related Terms

  • Antonym: loose-fitting - not fitting closely; somewhat loose
