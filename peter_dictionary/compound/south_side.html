south side

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ saɪd / (AmE), / saʊθ saɪd / (BrE)
the part or area of something that is toward the south

Examples

He does n't think that potting them from a deck chair on the south side of the house with a quart glass of beer for sustenance is entirely sporting.
There remained a family named Kahler, owners of a two story Tudor style house on the south side of the Andrus home.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: north side - the part of something that is toward the north
  • Antonym: east side - the part of something that is toward the east
  • Antonym: west side - the part of something that is toward the west
