canyonside

1. NOUN

/ ˈkænjənˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkænjənˌsaɪd / (BrE)
The steeply sloping side of a canyon.

Examples

Behind its ornate facade the notorious dive clung like a bird's nest to the rocky ribs of the canyonside.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cliff - a steep rock face, especially at the edge of the sea
  • Synonym: canyon - a deep gorge, typically with a river flowing through it

Etymology

Formed from 'canyon' + 'side'.
