black flag

1. NOUN

/ blæk flæɡ / (AmE), / blæk flaɡ / (BrE)
a flag, typically bearing a white skull and crossbones on a black background, used by pirates to signify their identity or intentions

Examples

Do you not think these men might choose the black flag here and now "?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Jolly Roger - traditional name for a pirate flag with a skull and crossbones
