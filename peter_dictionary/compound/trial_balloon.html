trial balloon

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪəl bəˈluːn / (AmE), / ˈtraɪəl bəˈluːn / (BrE)
an action or statement made to test public opinion or reaction to an idea before committing to it fully

Examples

A tongue-in-cheek stylization of 18 th Century ideas; a trial balloon to test the aesthetic climate of the times; a brilliant piece de resistance?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: test case - an instance used to try or evaluate something

Etymology

From 'trial' (test) + 'balloon', likening the act to releasing a small balloon to see how it fares.
