good person

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
A person who is kind, ethical, and behaves well toward others.

Examples

Still another boy asserted: "To be a good Jew is to do no wrong; it's to be a good person ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: bad person - a person who behaves immorally or unkindly
