bath towel

1. NOUN

/ bæθ ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / bɑːθ ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
a large towel used for drying the body after bathing or showering

Examples

He reached out and felt the bath towel hanging on the towel rack over the tub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towel - piece of absorbent cloth for drying things
