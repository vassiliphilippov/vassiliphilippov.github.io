magnetic needle

1. NOUN

/ mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˈniːdl / (BrE)
a slender, magnetized bar free to pivot in a compass, used to indicate the direction of the Earth's magnetic pole

Examples

At this lecture Oersted happened to place the conducting wire over and parallel to a magnetic needle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: compass needle - the needle inside a compass; usually a magnetic needle
