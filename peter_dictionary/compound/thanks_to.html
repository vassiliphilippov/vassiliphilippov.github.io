thanks to

1. ADP

/ ˈθæŋks tu / (AmE), / ˈθæŋks tʊ / (BrE)
Owing to; because of; as a result of.

Examples

And he had a feeling - thanks to the girl - that things would get worse before they got better.
Neither are beds, thanks to air mattresses and sleeping bags.
Do you deny this confession, and do you realize what will happen as a result of your denial?

Synset Examples

thanks to hard work it was a great success
