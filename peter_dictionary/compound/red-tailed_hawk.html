red-tailed hawk

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛd teɪld hɑk / (AmE), / ˈrɛd teɪld hɔːk / (BrE)
a large North American hawk with dark brown plumage and a characteristic reddish-brown tail

Examples

A red-tailed hawk flew in behind them and stayed there, watching for any snakes or rabbits that they might stir up from the side of the road.
