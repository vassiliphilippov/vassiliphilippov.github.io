human death

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən dɛθ / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən dɛθ / (BrE)
the death of a human being, especially as a result of an accident or disaster

Examples

Each man can identify himself with the history and the death of Jesus Christ because Jesus Christ has identified himself with human history and human death, coming as the head of a new humanity.

Synset Examples

a decrease in the number of automobile fatalities

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fatality - a death resulting from an accident or disaster
