educational activity

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃənəl ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən(ə)l ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
an activity or set of activities that aim to impart knowledge or skills through instruction or teaching

Examples

The transmutation of mission to co-optation is further indicated by the insignificance of educational activities, worship, preaching, and publicity in reaching new members.
