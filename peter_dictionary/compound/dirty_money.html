dirty money

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɝːti ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti ˈmʌni / (BrE)
money obtained illegally or dishonestly, especially through crime or corruption

Examples

There's a river of dirty money running through this place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: loot - goods or money stolen or taken illegally
  • Synonym: ill-gotten gains - money or items obtained dishonestly or illegally
