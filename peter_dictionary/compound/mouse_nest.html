mouse nest

1. NOUN

/ maʊs nɛst / (AmE), / maʊs nɛst / (BrE)
A nest built and used by mice as a place to bear and raise their young.

Examples

Most species seem to prefer a ready-made hollow such as a deserted mouse nest, a bird house, or the hole made by a woodpecker; some show a definite liking for making their nest in moss.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest - a structure built by an animal to live in and rear its young
