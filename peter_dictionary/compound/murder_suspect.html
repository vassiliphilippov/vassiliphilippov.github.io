murder suspect

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɝːdɚ səˌspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə səˌspɛkt / (BrE)
a person who is believed to be responsible for committing a murder, but has not yet been proven guilty

Examples

Finally, in The Maltese Falcon among others, the clash between detective and police is carried to its logical conclusion: Sam Spade becomes the chief murder suspect.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: suspect - a person thought to be guilty of a crime
  • Degree Higher: murderer - a person who has committed murder
