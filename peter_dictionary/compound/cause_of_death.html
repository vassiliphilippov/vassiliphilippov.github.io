cause of death

1. NOUN

/ kɔz əv dɛθ / (AmE), / kɔːz ɒv dɛθ / (BrE)
the reason for which a person or animal dies; the event, condition, or agent that brings about death

Examples

Cause of death has been attributed to a violent allergic reaction... ... although authorities have not ruled out the possibility of drug use.

Synset Examples

heart disease is the biggest killer in the United States

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manner of death - how the cause of death came about (e.g., accidental, natural)
