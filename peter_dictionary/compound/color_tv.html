color TV

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər ˌtiː ˈviː / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ˌtiː ˈviː / (BrE)
a television set that displays images in color rather than black and white

Examples

This is the same system as was used in the field sequential color TV system which preceded the present simultaneous system.
