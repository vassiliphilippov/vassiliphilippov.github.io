golf widow

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf ˈwɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ˈwɪdəʊ / (BrE)
A woman whose husband spends so much time playing golf that she is often left alone.

Examples

Twenty years ago, she would have been known as a golf widow, and the sum of her manner was perhaps one of bereavement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: football widow - woman left alone because husband plays or watches football

Etymology

Formed by analogy with "widow", used metaphorically; pattern seen in similar expressions (e.g., "football widow").
