railroad terminal

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪlˌrɑd ˈtɚmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌrɔːd ˈtəːmɪnəl / (BrE)
A terminal station where trains load or unload passengers or goods.

Examples

In final separation from them, in the railroad terminal across the river from New York, I would nearly cry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railway station - a station where trains stop to load or unload passengers or goods
