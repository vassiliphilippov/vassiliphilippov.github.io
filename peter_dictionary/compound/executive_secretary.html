executive secretary

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv səˈkrɛtɛri / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv sɪˈkrɛtəri / (BrE)
A secretary with administrative duties and responsibilities, often assisting high-level executives and managing office operations.

Examples

Arnold sent for Joe Dey, the executive secretary of the golf association.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personal assistant - an employee assisting a manager or executive with administrative tasks
  • Degree Lower: secretary - an employee who handles administrative work, such as correspondence and scheduling
