at home

1. ADV

/ æt hoʊm / (AmE), / at həʊm / (BrE)
In sports, playing at one's own team's field or stadium.

Examples

THE DENVER-AREA TV audience was privileged to see Mays' four home runs, thanks to a new arrangement made by Bob Howsam that the games are not to be blacked out when his Bears are playing at home.

Synset Examples

they played at home last night

Related Terms

  • Antonym: away - playing on the opponent's field (sports)

2. ADV

/ æt hoʊm / (AmE), / at həʊm / (BrE)
At or towards the place where one resides; in one's own house or apartment.

Examples

He's at home, washing his tights.

Synset Examples

he worked at home

Related Terms

  • Antonym: at work - in one's place of employment
  • Synonym: home - one's place of residence
