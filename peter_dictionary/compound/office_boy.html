office boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.fɪs bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒ.fɪs bɔɪ / (BrE)
a young male employee who performs simple or routine tasks in a business office, such as running errands or delivering messages

Examples

Reduced to beggary, he at last got a job as office boy to a television producer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: office girl - a young female employee who does simple office tasks
