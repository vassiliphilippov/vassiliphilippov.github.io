in name

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈneɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ˈneɪm / (BrE)
By title or reputation but not in reality; existing only in name.

Examples

Operating as a one man police force in fact if not in name, he is at once more independent and more dedicated than the police themselves.

Synset Examples

he's a doctor in name only
