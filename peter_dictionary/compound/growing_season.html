growing season

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡroʊɪŋ ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊɪŋ ˈsiːzən / (BrE)
The part of the year when conditions are suitable for crops or plants to grow, typically due to favorable temperature, rainfall, and daylight.

Examples

Even if there are no livestock, the farmer cannot leave the farm for long periods, particularly during the growing season.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: harvest - the process or period of gathering crops
