week by week

1. ADV

/ wik baɪ wik / (AmE), / wiːk baɪ wiːk / (BrE)
At regular intervals of one week; every week in succession.

Examples

Day by day, week by week, month by month, the betrayal gnawed at Andrei's heart.

Synset Examples

week by week, the betrayal gnawed at his heart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: weekly - happening every week
