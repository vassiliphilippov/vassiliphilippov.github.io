hand brake

1. NOUN

/ hænd breɪk / (AmE), / hænd breɪk / (BrE)
A brake operated by hand, especially a lever in a vehicle that is used to keep it stationary when parked.

Examples

Even as the conviction of truth roared through him, shattering his last hope of safety, he was reaching to release the hand brake, to head up the road for home, doing her bidding.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: parking brake - a brake used to keep a stationary vehicle from moving
