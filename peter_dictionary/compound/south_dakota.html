South Dakota

1. PROPN

/ saʊθ dəˈkoʊtə / (AmE), / saʊθ dəˈkəʊtə / (BrE)
A state located in the north-central region of the United States.

Examples

The most beautiful bed of pansies I 've seen was in a South Dakota yard on a sizzling day.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: North Dakota - a neighboring state to the north of South Dakota

Etymology

Named after the Dakota Sioux Native American tribe; 'South' distinguishes it from North Dakota.
