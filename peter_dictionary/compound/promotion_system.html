promotion system

1. NOUN

/ prəˈmoʊʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a set of rules or procedures for advancing individuals or participants to higher-status positions within an organization or context

Examples

Dominated by the vicious circle of the university promotion system, the analyst looks down on and gets along poorly with the other three groups, although he cannot deny his debt to the collector.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: promotion - advancement to a higher position or rank
  • Antonym: demotion system - system for moving individuals to lower-status positions
