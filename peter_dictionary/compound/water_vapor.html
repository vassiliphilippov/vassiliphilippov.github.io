water vapor

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌveɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌveɪpə / (BrE)
Water in its gaseous form, especially as diffused in the atmosphere below boiling point.

Examples

Mass spectrometric analysis of gases evolved upon heating to 410 ` C indicated nitrogen oxides and water vapor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: steam - water in the form of gas, especially when produced by boiling
