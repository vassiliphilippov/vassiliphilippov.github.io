ice age

1. NOUN

/ aɪs eɪdʒ / (AmE), / aɪs eɪdʒ / (BrE)
A period in Earth's history during which large areas of the surface were covered by glaciers.

Examples

An Ice Age here, a million years of mountain-building there.

Synset Examples

the most recent ice age was during the Pleistocene

Related Terms

  • Synonym: glaciation - the process or state of being covered by glaciers
