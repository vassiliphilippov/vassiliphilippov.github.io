business firm

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs fɜrm / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs fɜːm / (BrE)
A company or commercial organization engaged in business activities.

Examples

SBA makes loans to individual small business firms, providing them with financing when it is not otherwise available through private lending sources on reasonable terms.

Synset Examples

he worked for a brokerage house

Related Terms

  • Synonym: company - A business organization that makes, buys, or sells goods or provides services
  • Synonym: firm - A business organization, especially one that employs people
