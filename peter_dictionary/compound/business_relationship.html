business relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs rɪˌleɪʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs rɪˌleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a formal connection between organizations or individuals established for commercial purposes, such as for providing regular services or conducting transactions

Examples

If you are unable to sign the request, because of illness or other good cause, another person who stands in close personal or business relationship to you may sign the request on your behalf, stating the reason why you are unable to sign.

Synset Examples

he asked to see the executive who handled his account

Related Terms

  • Synonym: partnership - an association of two or more people engaged in business for profit
  • Antonym: personal relationship - a connection or association between people outside a business context
