sweet clover

1. NOUN

/ ˈswit ˌkloʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈswiːt ˌkləʊvə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Melilotus, typically erect and grown as an annual or biennial, valued for use as hay and for improving soil.
