tube-nosed fruit bat

1. NOUN

/ tuːb noʊzd frut bæt / (AmE), / tjuːb nəʊzd fruːt bæt / (BrE)
Any of various fruit bats of the genus Nyctimene, distinguished by nostrils that form outward-diverging tubes.
