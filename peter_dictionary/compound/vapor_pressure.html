vapor pressure

1. NOUN

/ ˈveɪpɚ ˌprɛʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈveɪpə ˌprɛʃə / (BrE)
The pressure exerted by a vapor in equilibrium with its liquid or solid form, especially the pressure of a vapor in contact with its liquid at a given temperature.

Examples
