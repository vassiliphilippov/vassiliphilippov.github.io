advertising agency

1. NOUN

/ ˈæd.vɚˌtaɪ.zɪŋ ˌeɪ.dʒən.si / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.taɪ.zɪŋ ˌeɪ.dʒən.si / (BrE)
A business that creates and manages advertisements for other companies, helping them promote their products or services.

Examples

Present at the scene - in addition to the dead man, who was indeed Louis Thor - had been Thor's partner Bill Blake, and Antony Rose, an advertising agency executive who handled the ZING account.
Among us, we three handled quite a few small commissions, from spot drawings for advertising agencies uptown to magazine work and quick lettering jobs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ad agency - shortened form of advertising agency
  • Degree Lower: public relations firm - company managing public image and communication for clients
