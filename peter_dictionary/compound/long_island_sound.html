Long Island Sound

1. PROPN

/ lɔŋ ˈaɪ.lənd saʊnd / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈaɪ.lənd saʊnd / (BrE)
A tidal estuary between Long Island, New York, and the southern coast of Connecticut.

Examples

On the east side of the island of Manhattan the indifferent hawk knew the East River that connected New York Bay with Long Island Sound.
