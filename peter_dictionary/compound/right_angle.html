right angle

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪt ˌæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌæŋɡl / (BrE)
An angle of exactly 90 degrees, as formed by two perpendicular lines.

Examples

The entrance must have at least one right-angle turn.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: acute angle - an angle less than 90 degrees
  • Degree Higher: obtuse angle - an angle greater than 90 degrees but less than 180 degrees
