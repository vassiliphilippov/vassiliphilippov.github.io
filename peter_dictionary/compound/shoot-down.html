shoot-down

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃutˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtˌdaʊn / (BrE)
An act of killing someone by shooting them deliberately, often in cold blood.

Examples

They would n't o' stood no chance with you in a plain, straight-out shoot-down ".
Which would you be most scairt of - a dry-gulchin' or a shoot-down "?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting - the action or practice of shooting a gun
