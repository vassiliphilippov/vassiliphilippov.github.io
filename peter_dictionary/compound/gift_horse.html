gift horse

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪft hɔrs / (AmE), / ɡɪft hɔːs / (BrE)
A gift, often of questionable value or quality, that should be accepted without criticism.

Examples

"A gift horse to be viewed with suspicion ".

Synset Examples

it wasn't much, but don't look a gift horse in the mouth

Related Terms

  • Synonym: don't look a gift horse in the mouth - idiom: do not criticize or question the value of a gift

Etymology

From the proverb 'don't look a gift horse in the mouth', referring to the practice of inspecting a horse's age by looking at its teeth.
