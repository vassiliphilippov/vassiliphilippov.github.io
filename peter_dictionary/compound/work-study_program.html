work-study program

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝk ˌstʌdi ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk ˌstʌdi ˈprəʊɡram / (BrE)
an educational arrangement in which students alternate periods of paid employment with periods of formal academic study

Examples

Adult education courses, work-study programs of various sorts - these are all evidence of a continuing interest of the schools in furthering educational opportunities for out-of-school youth.
