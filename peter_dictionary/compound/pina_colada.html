pina colada

1. NOUN

/ ˌpiːnə koʊˈlɑːdə / (AmE), / ˌpiːnə kəˈlɑːdə / (BrE)
A sweet cocktail made from rum, coconut cream, and pineapple juice, typically served blended or shaken with ice.

Examples

Cuba Libre... Pina Colada...

Etymology

From Spanish 'piña colada', literally 'strained pineapple'.
