natural order

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃrəl ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
the physical universe regarded as an orderly system governed by natural laws rather than by human or supernatural influence

Examples

The embarrassment of these theories over the naturalness of death is an illustration of the thesis that death cannot be only a punishment, for some termination seems necessary in a life that is lived within the natural order of time and change.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nature - the phenomena of the physical world collectively
  • Synonym: cosmos - the universe seen as a well-ordered whole
