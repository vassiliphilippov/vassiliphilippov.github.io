dark bread

1. NOUN

/ dɑrk brɛd / (AmE), / dɑːk brɛd / (BrE)
Bread made from whole wheat flour or other dark grains, giving it a brown or dark color.

Examples

Several sections of a loaf of dark bread; butter; jam; a tiny cake of ice.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: white bread - bread made from refined white flour
