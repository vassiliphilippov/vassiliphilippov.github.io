hard drug

1. NOUN

/ hɑrd drʌɡ / (AmE), / hɑːd drʌɡ / (BrE)
A powerful narcotic that is likely to cause addiction, such as heroin or cocaine.

Examples

He's known as a top narcotics man.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: soft drug - A drug considered less potent or less likely to cause addiction, such as cannabis
