water project

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɑtɚ ˌprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
an engineering scheme or development designed to improve, manage, or make use of water resources, such as dams, irrigation systems, or water supply facilities.

Examples

The bill would increase from $ 5000000 to $ 15000000 the maximum loan the state could make to a local water project.
