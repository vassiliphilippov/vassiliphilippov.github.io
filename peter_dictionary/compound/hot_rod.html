hot rod

1. NOUN

/ hɑt rɑd / (AmE), / hɒt rɒd / (BrE)
A car that has been modified to increase its speed and acceleration, often for racing or for style.

Examples

Now, you no doubt regard the preceding as pap; you 're tooling around full of gage in your hot rods, gorging yourselves on pizza and playing pinball in the taverns and generally behaving like Ubermenschen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: custom car - a car that has been altered for performance or appearance

Etymology

From 'hot' (meaning fast or exciting) + 'rod' (slang for car, originally referring to an automobile's connecting rod).
