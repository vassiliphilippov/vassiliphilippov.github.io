sexual relationship

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃʊəl rɪˈleɪʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
A relationship between two people that involves sexual intimacy.
