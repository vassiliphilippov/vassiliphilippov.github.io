track meet

1. NOUN

/ træk mit / (AmE), / træk miːt / (BrE)
An organized athletic competition in which individuals or teams compete in various track and field events.

Examples

George Kerr, the swift striding Jamaican, set a meet record in the 600 - yard run in the Knights of Columbus track meet tonight, beating Purdue's Dave Mills in a hot duel in 1.10.1.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meet - an organized event where athletes compete against each other
