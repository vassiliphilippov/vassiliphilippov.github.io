ex-husband

1. NOUN

/ ɛks ˈhʌzbənd / (AmE), / ɛks ˈhʌzbənd / (BrE)
A man who was formerly married to a particular woman.

Examples

Exboyfriend meet exhusband ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ex-wife - a woman who was formerly married to a particular man
  • Degree Lower: husband - a married man, especially in relation to his spouse

Etymology

Formed from 'ex-' (Latin ex-, meaning 'out of; former') and 'husband'.
