kitchen table

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃᵊn ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
A table kept and used in a kitchen, typically for dining, food preparation, or other household activities.

Examples

Hoag pushed open the door: at the far end of the long dark room Muller was faintly silhouetted against the window, the rifle still raised; he stood with his feet apart on a kitchen table he had dragged to the sill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dining table - a table at which meals are eaten
