turning point

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɜrnɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
A time or event at which a significant and often decisive change occurs.

Examples

When was the turning point?

Synset Examples

the agreement was a watershed in the history of both nations

Related Terms

  • Synonym: watershed - a critical turning point marking a change in course or development
  • Synonym: milestone - an important event marking a stage in progress
