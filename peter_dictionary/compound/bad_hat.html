bad hat

1. NOUN

/ bæd hæt / (AmE), / bæd hæt / (BrE)
a person who deliberately causes trouble or behaves in a dishonest or disruptive way

Examples

Our suspicions eventually centered, by the process of elimination, on a grocer's boy, a thoroughly bad hat, who delivered cartons to the people overhead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: troublemaker - a person who causes trouble
