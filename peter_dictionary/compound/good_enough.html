good enough

1. ADJ

/ ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf / (BrE)
adequately satisfactory for a particular purpose or situation; sufficient to meet requirements, though not necessarily the best.

Examples

Maybe he only intended to scare the blackmailer, whoever he was, in which case an unloaded gun would be good enough.

Synset Examples

if it's good enough for you it's good enough for me

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sufficient - adequate to meet the needs or purpose
  • Synonym: adequate - acceptable or enough in quantity or quality
  • Degree Lower: acceptable - satisfactory but not outstanding
  • Degree Higher: excellent - extremely good; exceptional in quality
