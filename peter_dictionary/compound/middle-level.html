middle-level

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɪd.əl ˌlɛv.əl / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl ˌlɛv.əl / (BrE)
Of intermediate rank or position within an organization or group; not at the top or bottom level.

Examples

Technical assistance in training middle - and upper-level management personnel is still needed in many cases.

Synset Examples

middle-level management

Related Terms
