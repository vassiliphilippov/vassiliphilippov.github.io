educational institution

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃənəl ˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
An organization such as a school, college, or university that is dedicated to education.

Examples

SBA cosponsors these courses with educational institutions and community groups.
enter into contracts with educational institutions, scientific organizations, and industrial and engineering firms;

Related Terms

  • Synonym: school - a place where children are educated
  • Synonym: college - an educational institution or establishment, in particular one providing higher education
  • Synonym: university - an institution of higher education and research
