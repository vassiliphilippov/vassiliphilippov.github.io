beauty parlor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbjuːti ˌpɑːrlər / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti ˌpɑːl.ə / (BrE)
A shop or business where hairdressers and beauticians provide cosmetic treatments, such as hairstyling, facials, and manicures.

Examples

Three mysterious deaths at a beauty parlor... ... were discovered today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beauty salon - another term for a place where cosmetic treatments are provided
