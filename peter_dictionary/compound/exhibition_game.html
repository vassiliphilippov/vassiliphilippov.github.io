exhibition game

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛksəˈbɪʃən ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃən ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a sports game played for practice or entertainment, whose result does not count towards official records or standings

Examples

I remember his beardown performance in a meaningless exhibition game at Bears Stadium Oct. 14, 1951, before a new record crowd for the period of 18792.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: friendly - a soccer or sports match not counting towards a competition
