ocean floor

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊʃən flɔr / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃən flɔː / (BrE)
the bottom surface of the sea or ocean

Examples

Various methods of pulsing, scanning, and displaying these sound waves are used to detect submarines, map ocean floors, and even communicate under water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: seabed - the ground at the bottom of the sea
  • Synonym: sea floor - the floor or bottom surface of a sea
