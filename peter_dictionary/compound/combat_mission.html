combat mission

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmbæt ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɒmbæt ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a military operation involving active fighting, typically undertaken to capture or defend a target or area

Examples

He wondered where the superstition had originated that it was bad luck for a crew chief to watch his plane take off on a combat mission.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mission - a specific task or duty assigned, especially a military or diplomatic one
