good afternoon

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˌæftərˈnun / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˌɑːftəˈnuːn / (BrE)
Used as a polite greeting or farewell to someone in the afternoon.

Examples

[CHUCKLING] Good afternoon, gentlemen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: good morning - polite greeting or farewell used in the morning
  • Synonym: good evening - polite greeting or farewell used in the evening
