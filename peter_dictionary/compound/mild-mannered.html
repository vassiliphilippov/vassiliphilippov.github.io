mild-mannered

1. ADJ

/ maɪld ˈmænərd / (AmE), / maɪld ˈmænəd / (BrE)
Having a gentle, quiet, or unassuming manner.

Examples

Only Cromwell, the giant boatswain, was mild-mannered and respectful.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gentle - having or showing a mild, kind, or tender temperament
  • Antonym: aggressive - behaving in an angry or forceful way
