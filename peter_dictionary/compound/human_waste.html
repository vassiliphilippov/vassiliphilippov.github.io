human waste

1. NOUN

/ ˈhjuːmən weɪst / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmən weɪst / (BrE)
Solid or liquid excretions from the human body, such as urine and feces.

Examples

Provision for emergency toilet facilities and disposal of human wastes will be an unfamiliar problem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sewage - waste water and excrement conveyed in sewers
  • Degree Lower: animal waste - bodily waste matter from animals
