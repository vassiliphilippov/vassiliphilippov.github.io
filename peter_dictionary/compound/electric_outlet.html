electric outlet

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈaʊtˌlɛt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈaʊtlɛt / (BrE)
A socket on a wall or other surface that provides access to electrical power for appliances or devices.

Examples

There should be a regular electrical outlet in the shelter as power may continue in many areas.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: power socket - socket on a wall for connecting electric appliances
  • Antonym: plug - the male connector that fits into an electric outlet
