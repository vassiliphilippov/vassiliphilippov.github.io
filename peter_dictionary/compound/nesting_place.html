nesting place

1. NOUN

/ ˈnɛstɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈnɛstɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a place that is suitable for an animal, especially a bird, to build its nest and lay eggs

Examples

As befits a queen, a bumblebee female is rather choosy and may spend considerable time searching for a suitable nesting place.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nest - a structure built by birds or other animals for laying eggs or raising young
