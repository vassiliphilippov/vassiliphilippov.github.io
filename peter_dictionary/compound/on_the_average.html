on the average

1. ADV

/ ɑn ðə ˈævərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɒn ði ˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
Used to indicate what is typical or usual when considering all instances together; on average.

Examples

They get all the glory and most of the pay and do n't earn ten cents apiece on the average, the drunken rascals ".

Synset Examples

on average he watches three movies a week

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on average - used to indicate a typical or mean value
