in secret

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈsiː.krət / (AmE), / ɪn ˈsiː.krɪt / (BrE)
Without letting other people know; not openly; covertly.

Examples

Therefore it's a genuine pleasure to tell you about an entirely happy bodybuilder who has never had to train in secret; has never heard one unkind word from his parents; and never has been taunted by his schoolmates!

Synset Examples

met secretly to discuss the invasion plans
the children secretly went to the movies when they were supposed to be at the library
they arranged to meet in secret

Related Terms

  • Synonym: secretly - Without others knowing; covertly
  • Antonym: openly - In an open or public way; not secretly
