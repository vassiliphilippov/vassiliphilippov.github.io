as a group

1. ADV

/ æz ə ɡruːp / (AmE), / æz ə ɡruːp / (BrE)
All together in a single unit; collectively rather than individually.

Examples

The improvement was most noticeable in the greater consistency among reaction cells prepared as a group on the same manifold.

Synset Examples

the students turned out en masse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: collectively - as a group; together
  • Antonym: individually - one by one; separately
