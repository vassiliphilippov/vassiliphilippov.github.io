home movie

1. NOUN

/ hoʊm ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / həʊm ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
a film made by an amateur, typically at home, especially to record family events or celebrations

Examples

If, as a home movie maker, you shoot the inevitable footage of your child taking its first steps, you have merely recorded an historical event.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: amateur film - a film made by someone who is not a professional
