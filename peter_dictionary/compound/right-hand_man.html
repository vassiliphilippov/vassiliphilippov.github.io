right-hand man

1. NOUN

/ ˌraɪtˈhænd ˌmæn / (AmE), / ˌraɪtˈhænd ˌmæn / (BrE)
A person who is the most trusted assistant or helper to someone, especially in their work.

Examples

When we make our move there, you'll be my right-hand man.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assistant - a person who helps someone in their work

Etymology

Refers to the tradition of the right-hand position signifying trust or importance, dating from the literal use in English since the 16th century.
