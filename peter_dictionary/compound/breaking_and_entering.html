breaking and entering

1. NOUN

/ ˈbreɪkɪŋ ənd ˈɛnɚɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkɪŋ ənd ˈɛntərɪŋ / (BrE)
The crime of illegally entering a building or premises, especially by force, with the intention of committing another offence.

Examples

That's breaking and entering to you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: burglary - the illegal entry of a building with intent to commit a crime, especially theft
