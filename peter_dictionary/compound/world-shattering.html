world-shattering

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːld ˌʃætərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːld ˌʃætərɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely important or significant, having a strong impact or effect as if affecting the entire world.

Examples

The conversation that ensued may have been engrossing but it could hardly be called world shattering.

Synset Examples

earthshaking proposals
the contest was no world-shaking affair
the conversation...could hardly be called world-shattering

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earthshaking - extremely significant or influential
  • Synonym: momentous - of great importance or significance
  • Synonym: world-shaking - having a major, global impact
