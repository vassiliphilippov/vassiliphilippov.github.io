chopping block

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɑpɪŋ ˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpɪŋ ˌblɒk / (BrE)
A heavy wooden block used as a surface for chopping food or splitting wood.

Examples

He put his chin on his kneecap, stretching his neck like that of a turkey on a chopping block, and stared wordlessly at his sergeant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cutting board - A board used for cutting food, especially in kitchens
