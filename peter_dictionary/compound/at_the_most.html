at the most

1. ADV

/ ət ðə moʊst / (AmE), / ət ðə məʊst / (BrE)
No more than; the maximum possible or allowable amount or degree.

Examples

But since the gourmet insisted that it is done that way at the most fashionable dinners, the girl reluctantly agreed.

Synset Examples

spend at most $20 on the lunch

Related Terms

  • Antonym: at the least - no less than; the minimum possible or allowable amount
