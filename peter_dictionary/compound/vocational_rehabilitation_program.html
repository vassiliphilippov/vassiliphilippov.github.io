vocational rehabilitation program

1. NOUN

/ voʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˌriˌhəˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˌriˌhəˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a structured program designed to help individuals with disabilities or other barriers acquire skills and training for suitable employment

Examples

The 1954 Amendments completely changed the financing of the vocational rehabilitation program, providing for a three part grant structure - for (1) basic support; (2) extension and improvement; and (3) research, demonstrations, training and traineeships for vocational rehabilitation - and in addition for short-term training and instruction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: job training - training to develop skills for employment
  • Synonym: rehabilitation - the process of restoring someone to health or normal life
