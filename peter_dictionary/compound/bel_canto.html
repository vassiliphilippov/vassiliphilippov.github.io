bel canto

1. NOUN

/ bɛl ˈkɑːn(toʊ) / (AmE), / bɛl ˈkæntəʊ / (BrE)
A style of operatic singing that emphasizes beautiful tone, smooth phrasing, and agility rather than dramatic expression.

Examples

His bel canto style gave the performance a special distinction.

Etymology

Italian, meaning "beautiful singing" (from bel 'beautiful' + canto 'singing').
