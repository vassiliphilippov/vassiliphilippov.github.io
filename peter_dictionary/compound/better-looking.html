better-looking

1. ADJ

/ ˈbɛtər ˈlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛtə ˈlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
More attractive in appearance, especially in comparison to another person or thing.

Examples

They were better trained, better looking, better built, better disciplined - and something else - they were better dancers.

Synset Examples

a fine-looking woman
a good-looking man
better-looking than her sister
very pretty but not so extraordinarily handsome
our southern women are well-favored

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: good-looking - attractive in appearance
  • Degree Higher: best-looking - most attractive (superlative)
