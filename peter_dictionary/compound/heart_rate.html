heart rate

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɑrt ˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːt ˌreɪt / (BrE)
The number of times the heart beats per minute, used as an indicator of health or fitness.

Examples

From this doctors can read heart rate, change in diameter, pressure, and effective heart power.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pulse - the rhythmic throbbing of arteries as blood is propelled through them, often corresponding to heart rate
