steering wheel

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɪrɪŋ wil / (AmE), / ˈstɪərɪŋ wiːl / (BrE)
A wheel in a vehicle that the driver turns to control the direction in which the vehicle moves.

Examples

Lord whistled tunelessly as he fought the steering wheel.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wheel - circular object that revolves on an axle and is fixed below a vehicle to enable it to move over the ground
