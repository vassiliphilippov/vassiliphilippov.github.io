telephone operator

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn ˈɑpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ˈɒpəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to assist callers in making telephone connections, especially by manually connecting calls on a switchboard
