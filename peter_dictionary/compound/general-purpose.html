general-purpose

1. ADJ

/ ˈdʒɛnərəl ˌpɝːpəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl ˌpɜːpəs / (BrE)
Suitable for a wide range of uses or functions; not restricted to a particular purpose.

Examples

Hard surface cleaning in household application is represented by two classes of alkaline products: (1) the formulations made expressly for machine dishwashers, and (2) the general-purpose cleaners used for walls and woodwork.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: specialized - restricted to a particular use or purpose
  • Synonym: multi-purpose - having many different uses or functions
