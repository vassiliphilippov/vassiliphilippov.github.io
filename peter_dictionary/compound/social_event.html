social event

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊ.ʃəl ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
An occasion where people gather for social interaction, such as a party or reception.

Examples

This monitoring is necessary because, on a parade ground, everyone can hear too much, and without monitoring a confused social event would develop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering for celebration or fun
  • Synonym: gathering - an assembly of people, typically for social reasons
