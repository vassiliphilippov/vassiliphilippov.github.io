to that extent

1. ADV

/ tə ðæt ɪkˈstɛnt / (AmE), / tə ðæt ɪkˈstɛnt / (BrE)
To the degree or level indicated; as far as that is concerned.

Examples

At any rate, the substance of Eichmann's testimony was that all his actions flowed from his membership in the party and the SS, and though the Prosecutor did his utmost to prove actual personal hatred of Jews, his success on this score was doubtful and the anti-Semitic lesson weakened to that extent.

Synset Examples

insofar as it can be ascertained, the horse lung is comparable to that of man
so far as it is reasonably practical he should practice restraint

Related Terms

  • Synonym: to that degree - to the same amount or level; similar fixed phrase
