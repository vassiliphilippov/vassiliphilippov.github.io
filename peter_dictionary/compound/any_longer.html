any longer

1. ADV

/ ˌɛni ˈlɔŋɡər / (AmE), / ˌeni ˈlɒŋɡə / (BrE)
Used, especially with negatives, to indicate that something does not happen or is not true now or anymore.

Examples

No cow thief could count on a jury of his sympathetic peers to free him any longer.

Synset Examples

Alice doesn't live here anymore
the children promised not to quarrel any more

Related Terms

  • Synonym: anymore - no longer; at the present or from now on (usually with negatives)
