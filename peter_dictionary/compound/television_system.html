television system

1. NOUN

/ ˌtɛləˌvɪʒən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A telecommunication system for transmitting images of objects, either stationary or moving, between distant locations.

Examples

A completely new insight into living cells and their structure will be possible by use of a new technique which replaces visible light with ultraviolet radiation and combines a microscope with a color - TV system to view the results.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - the system or process of transmitting visual images and sound; the medium itself

2. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌvɪʒən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌvɪʒən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system for transmitting visual images and sound by electrical signals over a distance, enabling objects, scenes, or events to be viewed remotely.

Examples

[MAN SPEAKING INDISTINCTLY ON TV] MAN: [ON TV] ...pick up of two by Holden.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - the system or technology of transmitting moving images and sound electronically
