white flag

1. NOUN

/ waɪt flæɡ / (AmE), / waɪt flæɡ / (BrE)
a white cloth or banner displayed to indicate surrender or a request for a truce

Examples

In the corner was the soldier with the white flag.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: truce - an agreement between enemies or opponents to stop fighting for a certain time
  • Synonym: surrender - the act of giving up, especially in a conflict or competition
