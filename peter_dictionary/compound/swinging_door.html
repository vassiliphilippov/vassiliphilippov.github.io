swinging door

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪŋɪŋ dɔːr / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɪŋ dɔː / (BrE)
A door that is mounted on double hinges so it can open in either direction.

Examples

Winston hurried through the swinging door.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revolving door - a door that rotates around a central pivot
