electric toothbrush

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈtuθˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈtuːθˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
A toothbrush powered by electricity, with a motor in the handle that makes the brush head move or vibrate.

Examples

An electric toothbrush (Broxodent) may soon take its place next to the electric razor in the American bathroom.
The electric toothbrush moves in a vertical direction, the way dentists recommend.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: manual toothbrush - a toothbrush operated by hand without electricity
