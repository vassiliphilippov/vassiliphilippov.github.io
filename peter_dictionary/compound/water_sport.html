water sport

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtɚ spɔrt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport played in or on water, such as swimming, sailing, or water skiing.

Examples

This follows naturally since frontage on an ocean, stream, or lake provides scenic values and opportunities for the very popular recreation activities of bathing, fishing, boating, and other water sports.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aquatic sport - a sport performed in water or on water
