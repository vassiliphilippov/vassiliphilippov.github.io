top executive

1. NOUN

/ tɑp ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪv / (AmE), / tɒp ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɪv / (BrE)
the highest-ranking executive in an organization, typically responsible for overall management and decision-making

Examples

Mr. Speaker, I rise today to pay tribute to a great newspaper, the New York Times, on the occasion of a major change in its top executive command.

Synset Examples

an oil baron

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chief executive officer - person with the highest-ranking executive position in a company
  • Degree Lower: manager - person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff
