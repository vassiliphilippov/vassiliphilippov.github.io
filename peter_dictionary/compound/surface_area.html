surface area

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɝːfɪs ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs ˈeərɪə / (BrE)
the total area that the surface of an object occupies or encloses, measured in square units

Examples

One gram of cotton has been found to have a specific surface area of * * f.
The oxidation pond has a surface area of 4.77 acres and a depth of 4 ft.

Synset Examples

the area of a rectangle
it was about 500 square feet in area

Related Terms

  • Synonym: area - the amount of space enclosed within a set of boundaries
