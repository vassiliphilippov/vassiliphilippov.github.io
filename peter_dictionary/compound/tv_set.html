tv set

1. NOUN

/ tiː ˈviː sɛt / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː sɛt / (BrE)
an electronic device that receives television signals and displays them on a screen

Examples

And I got the TV like this.

Synset Examples

the British call a tv set a telly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television - system for transmitting visual images and sound
  • Synonym: telly - informal British term for television set

Etymology

'TV' from 'television' (from Greek 'tele' far + Latin 'visio' seeing); 'set' here means a piece of equipment.
