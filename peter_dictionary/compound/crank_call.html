crank call

1. NOUN

/ kræŋk kɔl / (AmE), / kræŋk kɔːl / (BrE)
a telephone call made with the intention of annoying, harassing, or disturbing the recipient, often as a prank or for malicious reasons

Examples

"Few crank calls ", McFeeley said.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prank call - a telephone call made as a prank, usually for amusement rather than hostility
