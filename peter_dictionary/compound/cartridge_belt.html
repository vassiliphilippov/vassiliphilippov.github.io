cartridge belt

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑrtɹɪdʒ bɛlt / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtɹɪdʒ bɛlt / (BrE)
a broad belt fitted with loops or pockets for carrying cartridges or ammunition

Examples

Hitching his cartridge belt around, Pat glanced upward briefly at the Palace and started that way with Cobb at his side.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bandolier - a shoulder belt with loops for cartridges
