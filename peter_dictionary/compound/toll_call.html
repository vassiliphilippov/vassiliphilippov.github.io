toll call

1. NOUN

/ toʊl kɔl / (AmE), / təʊl kɔːl / (BrE)
A long-distance telephone call for which the caller is charged more than the standard local rate.

Examples

This eliminated toll calls between the two towns.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: local call - a telephone call within a local area at a basic rate

Etymology

From 'toll' (a charge or fee) + 'call'.
