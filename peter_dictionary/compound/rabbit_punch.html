rabbit punch

1. NOUN

/ ˈræbɪt pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt pʌntʃ / (BrE)
A short, chopping blow delivered to the back of the neck, regarded as dangerous and often illegal in boxing and combat sports.

Examples

A rabbit punch redoubled him.

Etymology

From its resemblance to the way a hunter might dispatch a rabbit with a blow to the back of the neck.
