mutual affection

1. NOUN

/ ˈmju.tʃu.əl əˈfɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.tʃu.əl əˈfɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
A feeling of fondness or love shared equally between two or more people.

Examples

There is general agreement also that sex union between husbands and wives as an expression of mutual affection without relation to procreation is right ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: affection - a gentle feeling of fondness or liking
  • Degree Higher: love - an intense feeling of deep affection
  • Antonym: mutual dislike - shared feeling of dislike
