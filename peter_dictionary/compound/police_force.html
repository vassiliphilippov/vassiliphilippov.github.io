police force

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis fɔrs / (AmE), / pəˈliːs fɔːs / (BrE)
An organized body of police officers responsible for maintaining law and order in a particular area.

Examples

Operating as a one man police force in fact if not in name, he is at once more independent and more dedicated than the police themselves.

Synset Examples

the law came looking for him

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police department - a specific division or agency of police officers within a city or town
  • Synonym: constabulary - the police force of a particular area, especially in Britain
