full of life

1. ADJ

/ fʊl əv laɪf / (AmE), / fʊl əv laɪf / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) lively, energetic, and enthusiastic; showing a lot of spirit or vitality.

Examples

"I would like them to appear very busy today, not busy exactly, but joyous, exuberant, full of life.

Synset Examples

a dynamic full of life woman
a vital and charismatic leader
this whole lively world

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lively - full of energy and enthusiasm
  • Synonym: vivacious - attractively lively and animated (especially of a woman)
