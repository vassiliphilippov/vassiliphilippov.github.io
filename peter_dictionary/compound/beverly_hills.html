Beverly Hills

1. PROPN

/ ˌbɛvərli ˈhɪlz / (AmE), / ˌbɛvəli ˈhɪlz / (BrE)
A city in southwestern California, known for being home to many Hollywood actors and celebrities.

Examples

Johnny Ola bumped into me in Beverly Hills.

Etymology

Named after Beverly Farms in Massachusetts and the surrounding hills.
