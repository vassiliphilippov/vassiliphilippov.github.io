border patrolman

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔrdər pəˈtroʊlmən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə pəˈtrəʊlmən / (BrE)
a law enforcement officer who patrols and monitors the borders of a country to prevent illegal entry or activities

Examples

He has served as a border patrolman and was in the Signal Corps of the U. S. Army.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: border guard - a person who guards a national border
