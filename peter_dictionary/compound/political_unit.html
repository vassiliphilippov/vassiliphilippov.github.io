political unit

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
an area or entity with its own political authority or government, such as a country, state, or district

Examples

The epic poems, the consolidation of the Greek pantheon, the rise of firm political units, the self-awareness which could permit painted and sculptured representations of men - all these had to await the progress of following decades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: state - an organized political community under one government
  • Synonym: country - a nation with its own government, occupying a particular territory
