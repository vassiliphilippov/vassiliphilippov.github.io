weak point

1. NOUN

/ wiːk pɔɪnt / (AmE), / wiːk pɔɪnt / (BrE)
an aspect or feature that is deficient, vulnerable, or less effective compared to others

Examples

Analysis in roleplaying is usually done for the purpose of understanding strong and weak points of an individual or as a process to eliminate weak parts and strengthen good parts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: strength - an attribute that is strong or advantageous
