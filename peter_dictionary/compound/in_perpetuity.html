in perpetuity

1. ADV

/ ɪn pərˈpetʃuˌəti / (AmE), / ɪn pəˈpɛtjʊɪti / (BrE)
For all time; forever; for an indefinite or unlimited duration, often used in legal, financial, or formal contexts.

Examples

They send you here for life.

Synset Examples

desire happiness in perpetuity
an annuity paid in perpetuity

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forever - for all time; perpetually

Etymology

From Latin *in perpetuitate* ('in perpetuity'), from *perpetuus* ('continuous, unbroken').
