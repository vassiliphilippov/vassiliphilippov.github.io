golf pro

1. NOUN

/ ɡɑlf proʊ / (AmE), / ɡɒlf prəʊ / (BrE)
A professional golfer, especially someone who earns a living by playing or teaching golf.

Examples

She's fucking this prick, see, this golf pro.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: professional golfer - someone who plays golf for a living
  • Antonym: amateur golfer - someone who plays golf as a hobby, not for a living
