head of hair

1. NOUN

/ ˌhɛd əv ˈhɛr / (AmE), / ˌhɛd əv ˈheə / (BrE)
The full amount or growth of hair covering a person's scalp.

Examples

Her eyebrows were definite and heavy and formed two lines moving upward toward a high forehead and a great head of brown hair that fell to her shoulders.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mane - long, thick growth of hair on the neck of a horse or around the head of a lion; also used figuratively for humans
