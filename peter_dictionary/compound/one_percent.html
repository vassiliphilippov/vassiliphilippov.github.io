one percent

1. NOUN

/ wʌn pərˈsɛnt / (AmE), / wʌn pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
One part in every hundred; 1%.

Examples

Probably less than one percent of our previsions escape final obliteration before we wake.
But the radiation may be so intense at the start that one percent may be extremely dangerous.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: percent - one part in every hundred

Etymology

Formed from 'one' and 'percent' (from Latin 'per centum' meaning 'by the hundred').
