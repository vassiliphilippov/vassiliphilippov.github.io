close up

1. ADV

/ kloʊs ʌp / (AmE), / kləʊs ʌp / (BrE)
From a very short distance; very near to something or someone.

Examples

You definitely hear some of the instruments close up and others farther back, with the difference in placement apparently more distinct than would result from the nearer instruments merely being louder than the ones farther back.

Synset Examples

without my reading glasses I can hardly see things close up
even firing at close range he missed

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nearby - at or to a short distance from a place; not far away
  • Antonym: far away - at or to a great distance
