opera bouffe

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑː.pɚ buːf / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ərə ˈbuːf / (BrE)
A form of comic opera with a happy ending, typically featuring spoken dialogue as well as singing.

Examples

This may be opera bouffe now, but it will become more serious should the cold war mount in frenzy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comic opera - light opera with spoken dialogue and a happy ending

Etymology

From French 'opéra bouffe' (comic opera), from Italian 'opera buffa'.
