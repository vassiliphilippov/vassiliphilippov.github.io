steam bath

1. NOUN

/ stiːm bæθ / (AmE), / stiːm bɑːθ / (BrE)
A room filled with steam where people bathe for relaxation or health purposes.

Examples

writes: Do steam baths have any health value?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sauna - a small room used as a hot-air or steam bath for cleaning and refreshing the body
  • Synonym: vapour bath - British term for a bath using steam or hot vapor for cleansing and relaxation
