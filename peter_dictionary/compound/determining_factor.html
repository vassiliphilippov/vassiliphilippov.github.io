determining factor

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪŋ ˈfæktər / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnɪŋ ˈfæk.tər / (BrE)
a circumstance or element that decisively influences the outcome of something

Examples

The size of the area alone can be a determining factor.

Synset Examples

education is an important determinant of one's outlook on life

Related Terms

  • Synonym: determinant - something that strongly influences what happens or how things develop
