mug file

1. NOUN

/ mʌɡ faɪl / (AmE), / mʌɡ faɪl / (BrE)
a collection of mug shots (photographs of known criminals) maintained by the police for identification purposes

Examples

He wanted to take the mother to headquarters at once and start her on the mug file.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rogues' gallery - collection of photographs of criminals kept by the police

Etymology

From 'mug' (slang for face or photograph of a face) and 'file' (a collection of documents or records).
