2. NUM

/ tɛn ˈθaʊ.zənd / (AmE), / ten ˈθaʊ.zənd / (BrE)
The number 10,000; the cardinal number equal to ten times one thousand.

Examples

He offered him 10,000 dollars to let Johnny go, but the bandleader said no.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: thousand - the number 1,000; one thousand
  • Degree Higher: hundred thousand - the number 100,000; one hundred thousand
