back brace

1. NOUN

/ bæk breɪs / (AmE), / bæk breɪs / (BrE)
a device worn to support the back, typically used for medical reasons or injury recovery

Examples

A back brace might help, depending upon the cause of sciatica.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brace - an orthopedic device used to support or align body parts
