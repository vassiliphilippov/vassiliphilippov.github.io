political arena

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪdɪkəl əˈrinə / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl əˈriːnə / (BrE)
the sphere or environment in which political activity, debate, or competition takes place

Examples

It is becoming harder and harder to tell law courts and political arenas from the modern theatre ".
Debate in the political arena can be productive of good.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: political sphere - realm or domain where political activity takes place

Etymology

Formed from 'political' and 'arena', the latter from Latin 'harena' (place of combat or contest).
