singing voice

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪŋɪŋ vɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɪŋ vɔɪs / (BrE)
the musical quality or capability of a person's voice when singing

Examples

On the other hand, he had a tremendous singing voice.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: voice - the sound produced in a person's larynx and uttered through the mouth
