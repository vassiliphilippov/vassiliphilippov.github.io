cigar box

1. NOUN

/ sɪˈɡɑr bɑks / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː bɒks / (BrE)
A box designed for storing cigars, typically made of wood or cardboard.

Examples

Ludie had a cigar box full of marbles and shooters, and a Roman candle from last Fourth of July.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cigarette case - a case for holding cigarettes
