ventilating system

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛn.tɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.tɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
A system in a building that mechanically provides fresh air and removes stale air.

Examples

The air conditioning and ventilating systems of large buildings are subject to attack.

Synset Examples

she was continually adjusting the ventilation

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ventilation - the process of supplying fresh air and removing stale air
