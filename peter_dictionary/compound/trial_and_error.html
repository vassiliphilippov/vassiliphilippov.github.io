trial and error

1. NOUN

/ ˈtraɪəl ənd ˈɛrər / (AmE), / ˈtraɪəl ənd ˈɛrə / (BrE)
a method of solving problems by trying different possibilities until a successful one is found

Examples

There was no process of trial and error.
Trial and error are better placed in the preliminary sketch than in hoping for miracles in the final painting.
