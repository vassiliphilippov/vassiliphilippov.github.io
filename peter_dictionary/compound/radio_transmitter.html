radio transmitter

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪdioʊ trænzˈmɪtər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ trænzˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a device in a radio system that sends out radio signals, usually to be received by a radio receiver

Examples

Several years ago headlines were made by a small radio transmitter capsule which could be swallowed by the patient and which would then radio internal pressure data to external receivers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: radio receiver - device that receives and decodes radio signals
