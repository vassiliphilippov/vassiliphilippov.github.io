hand blower

1. NOUN

/ hænd ˈbloʊɚ / (AmE), / hænd ˈbləʊə / (BrE)
A small electric device held in the hand, used to blow warm air onto the hair for drying and styling.

Examples

I also use a small electric hand blower to dry large washes in the studio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hair dryer - an electrical device for drying hair by blowing warm air
