umbrella tent

1. NOUN

/ ʌmˈbɹɛlə tɛnt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɹɛlə tɛnt / (BrE)
A small tent with a single supporting pole and radiating metal ribs, resembling the structure of an umbrella.

Examples

There were umbrella tents, wall tents, cottage tents, station wagon tents, pup tents, Pop tents, Baker tents, tents with exterior frames, camper trailers, travel trailers, and even a few surplus parachutes serving as sunshades over entire family camps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pup tent - a small, simple tent for two people

Etymology

Formed by analogy to 'umbrella' due to the similar structure of supporting ribs.
