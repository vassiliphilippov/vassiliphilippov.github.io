hand tool

1. NOUN

/ hænd tuːl / (AmE), / hænd tuːl / (BrE)
A tool designed to be used by hand, rather than powered by electricity or engine.

Examples

If you are going to produce for home use only, you will need only hand tools.
That was where the pegboard would go on which he would hang his hand tools.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: power tool - a tool powered by an external source, such as electricity or a battery
