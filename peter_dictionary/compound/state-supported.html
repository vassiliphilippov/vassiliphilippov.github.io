state-supported

1. ADJ

/ steɪt səˈpɔrtɪd / (AmE), / steɪt səˈpɔːtɪd / (BrE)
Receiving financial support and operated by the government of a state, especially referring to institutions such as universities.

Examples

Accounts have been published of Northern liberals in the South up against segregationist prejudice, especially in state-supported universities where pressure may be strong to uphold the majority view.

Synset Examples

a state university

Related Terms

  • Synonym: state-funded - Receiving money from the state government
