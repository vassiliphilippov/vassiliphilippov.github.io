dwindling away

1. NOUN

/ ˈdwɪndəlɪŋ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈdwɪndlɪŋ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
A gradual decrease or reduction, especially to the point of disappearance.

Examples

There's no greater catastrophe in the universe, she reflected dourly, impaling tender green beans on the silver fork, than the dwindling away of a family.

Synset Examples

there is no greater sadness that the dwindling away of a family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decline - a gradual decrease or deterioration
  • Synonym: waning - decreasing or diminishing in power, size, or intensity
