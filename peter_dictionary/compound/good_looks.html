good looks

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd lʊks / (AmE), / ɡʊd lʊks / (BrE)
Attractive physical appearance, especially having regular, well-defined facial features.

Examples

His easy good looks made him a favorite with the ladies.
But she 'd known plenty of handsomer guys, and, conceding his good looks, what was there left?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handsomeness - the quality of having a pleasing appearance (especially of a man)
  • Synonym: beauty - a combination of qualities that pleases the aesthetic senses, especially the sight
