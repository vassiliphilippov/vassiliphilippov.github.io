won-lost record

1. NOUN

/ wʌn lɔst ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / wɒn lɒst ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
A record showing the number of games or matches won and lost by a team or individual, typically in sports.

Examples

Broglio's 4 - 0 won-lost record and 1.24 earned-run mark against Pittsburgh a year ago; 2.
