high up

1. ADV

/ haɪ ʌp / (AmE), / haɪ ʌp / (BrE)
At a great height or altitude above the ground or a reference point.

Examples

"I reached into that funny little pocket that is high up on my dress.

Synset Examples

he climbed high on the ladder

Related Terms

  • Antonym: low down - at a low position or near the ground
