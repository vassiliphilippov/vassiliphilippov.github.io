roulette ball

1. NOUN

/ ruːˈlɛt bɔl / (AmE), / ruːˈlɛt bɔːl / (BrE)
A small ball used in the game of roulette, which is spun around the edge of a roulette wheel and lands in a numbered slot to determine the winning outcome.

Examples

A man knocked the roulette ball about idly in its track, and another dozed at one of the card tables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: roulette wheel - wheel used in the game of roulette

Etymology

From French 'roulette' meaning 'little wheel', referring to the game, combined with 'ball'.
