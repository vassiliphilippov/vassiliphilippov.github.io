railway station

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛl.weɪ ˌsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˌsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A place where trains stop for passengers to board or alight, or for loading and unloading goods.

Examples

In the railway station at Berlin, a uniformed attendant was chanting,' Foreigners this way!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: train station - a place where trains stop for passengers or goods
