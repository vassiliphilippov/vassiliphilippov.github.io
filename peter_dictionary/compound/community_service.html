community service

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmjunɪti ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
Unpaid work performed for the benefit of the public, often as a legal penalty imposed on lawbreakers as part or all of their sentence.

Examples

Ned, we're providing a valuable community service here.
