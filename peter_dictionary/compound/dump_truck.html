dump truck

1. NOUN

/ dʌmp trʌk / (AmE), / dʌmp trʌk / (BrE)
a large truck with a platform that can be tilted to unload its contents by gravity, typically used for transporting and dumping loose materials such as sand, gravel, or demolition waste

Examples

The girls, very fetching in their uniforms, are shown firing rockets from a launcher mounted on a dump truck; they are also trained with carbines, automatic weapons, pistols, rifles and other such ladies' accessories.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tipper truck - British term for dump truck
