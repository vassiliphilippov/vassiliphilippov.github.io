roof of the mouth

1. NOUN

/ ruːf əv ðə maʊθ / (AmE), / ruːf əv ðə maʊθ / (BrE)
the upper surface inside the mouth that separates the oral cavity from the nasal cavity; the palate.

Examples

With his mouth opened wide, he squirted the warm white milk against the roof of his mouth and his tongue savored the light, earthy taste of it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palate - the roof of the mouth
