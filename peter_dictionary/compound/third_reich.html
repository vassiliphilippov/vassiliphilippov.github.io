Third Reich

1. PROPN

/ θɜrd raɪk / (AmE), / θɜːd raɪk / (BrE)
the Nazi regime in Germany under Adolf Hitler from 1933 to 1945

Examples

No doubt many of the citizens of the Third Reich had conceived their anti-Semitism as an "innocent "dislike of Jews, as do others like them today.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Nazi Germany - the period of German history under Nazi rule (1933–1945)

Etymology

From German 'Drittes Reich', meaning 'Third Empire'.
