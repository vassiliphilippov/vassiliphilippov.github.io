Little Rock

1. PROPN

/ ˈlɪt̬əl rɑk / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəl rɒk / (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Arkansas, located on the Arkansas River in central Arkansas, USA

Examples

Since the Supreme Court's decision of that year this is more doubtful; and if a poll had been taken immediately following the dispatch of troops to Little Rock I believe the majority would have been for the Old South.

Etymology

Named after a small rock formation on the Arkansas River, called 'La Petite Roche' by early French explorers.
