chain store

1. NOUN

/ tʃeɪn stɔr / (AmE), / tʃeɪn stɔː / (BrE)
A retail store that is one of a group of stores under the same ownership or management, offering similar products or services across different locations.

Examples

Moreover, the larger and more aggressive mass distribution outlets and chain stores have insisted on high quality - and the customer seems to have caught on.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: franchise - a business that operates under authorization from a parent company, similar distribution model
