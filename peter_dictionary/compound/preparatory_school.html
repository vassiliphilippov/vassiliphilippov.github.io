preparatory school

1. NOUN

/ prɪˈpɛrəˌtɔri skul / (AmE), / prɪˈparəˌtɔːri skuːl / (BrE)
A private school preparing students for higher education, especially college or university.

Examples

There one finds concentrated in a comparatively small area the chief universities, colleges, and preparatory schools of the United States.
He was referring not only to the general college situation but more especially to the preparatory schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: prep school - informal term for preparatory school

Etymology

From 'preparatory' (from Latin preparatorius: to prepare) + 'school'.
