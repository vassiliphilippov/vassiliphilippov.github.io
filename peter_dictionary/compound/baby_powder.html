baby powder

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪ.bi ˌpaʊ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌpaʊ.də / (BrE)
A fine, scented powder applied to a baby's skin to prevent chafing and absorb moisture, especially in the diaper area.

Examples

Avoid the following combinations: Deodorants with baby powder, hairspray, and lipstick.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: talcum powder - a cosmetic or toilet preparation consisting of pulverized talc or a similar substance, often used as baby powder
