vice chairman

1. NOUN

/ vaɪs ˈtʃɛrˌmæn / (AmE), / vaɪs ˈtʃeəˌmæn / (BrE)
an official who ranks immediately below the chairman and may act as chairman in their absence

Examples

[TRANSLATOR TRANSLATING] PRESIDENT: Mr. Petty, Regional Vice President of the Pan American Mining Corporation.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: chairman - the person leading a meeting or organization
  • Synonym: deputy chairman - an alternative term for vice chairman
