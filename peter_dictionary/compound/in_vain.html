in vain

1. ADV

/ ɪn veɪn / (AmE), / ɪn veɪn / (BrE)
without success or without producing the desired result; to no avail

Examples

I'll not have the Lord's name taken in vain in my prison.

Synset Examples

he looked for her in vain
the city fathers tried vainly to find a solution

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vainly - in a way that produces no result; to no avail
