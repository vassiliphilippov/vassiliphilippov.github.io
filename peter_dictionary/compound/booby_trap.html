booby trap

1. NOUN

/ ˈbuːbi træp / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi træp / (BrE)
A hidden explosive device designed to detonate when triggered by the unsuspecting presence or actions of a person or vehicle.

Examples

Between the ponderous hulk and himself, in the valley over which Papa-san reigned, men had hidden high explosives, booby traps, and mines.
Booby traps to fill the head with chunks of metal.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: land mine - an explosive device placed on or just under the ground

Etymology

Booby from Spanish 'bobo' (fool, simpleton); 'trap' from Old English 'treppe' (snare, trap).
