stretch pants

1. NOUN

/ stɹɛtʃ pænts / (AmE), / stɹɛtʃ pænts / (BrE)
Trousers made from a fabric containing elastic fibers, designed to stretch and fit closely to the body.

Examples

We 'd know him by his stretch pants and the flowers he 'd wear twined in his hair.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leggings - tight-fitting stretch trousers, often worn by women
  • Degree Lower: jeans - hardwearing casual trousers made of denim
