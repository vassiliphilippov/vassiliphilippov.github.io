air defense

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr dɪˌfɛns / (AmE), / ˈeə dɪˌfɛns / (BrE)
the protection of a country or area against enemy aircraft or missiles, using military systems designed to detect, intercept, and destroy them

Examples

Meanwhile, other air defense forces are being made effective, as described later in this message.
