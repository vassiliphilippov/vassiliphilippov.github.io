guilt pang

1. NOUN

/ ɡɪlt pæŋ / (AmE), / ɡɪlt pæŋ / (BrE)
A sudden, sharp feeling of guilt or remorse.

Examples

In this manner, he seeks to expunge from his own soul the guilt pangs caused by his personal assaults against the English at Dunkirk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: remorse - a deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
  • Synonym: regret - a feeling of sadness or disappointment over something one has done or failed to do
