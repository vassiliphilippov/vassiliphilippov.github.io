lower class

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊ.ər klæs / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ə klɑːs / (BrE)
The social group consisting of people with the least wealth, status, or power in a society; the lowest stratum of a social hierarchy.

Examples

There were no less than six or seven saloons in Ganado, not counting the lower class dives, all vying for the trade of celebrating miners and teamsters.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: working class - the social group consisting of people employed in lower-paid, especially manual, jobs
  • Degree Higher: middle class - the social group between the upper and working classes
  • Degree Higher: upper class - the social group with the highest status, wealth, and power
