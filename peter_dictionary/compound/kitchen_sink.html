kitchen sink

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk / (BrE)
A large basin in a kitchen used for washing dishes, food, or hands and usually equipped with a faucet.
