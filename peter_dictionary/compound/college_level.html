college level

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪdʒ ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪdʒ ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
the standard or quality of education associated with collegiate studies, typically considered beyond high school and before graduate school

Examples

And so well is such ignorance preserved by the amateur and the money-maker that even at the college level most of the hundred odd folklore courses given in the United States survive on sentiment and nationalism alone.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: college - a higher education institution or its student body
