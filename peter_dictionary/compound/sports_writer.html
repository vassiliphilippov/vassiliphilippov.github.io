sports writer

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌraɪtə / (BrE)
A journalist who specializes in writing about sports and sporting events.

Examples

Sports Writer Ensign Ritchie of the Ogden Standard Examiner went to his compartment to talk with him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sports journalist - journalist who covers sports, often in various media
  • Degree Lower: reporter - person who reports news stories; a journalist
