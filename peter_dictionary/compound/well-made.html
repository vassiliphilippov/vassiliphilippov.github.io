well-made

1. ADJ

/ wɛl ˈmeɪd / (AmE), / wɛl ˈmeɪd / (BrE)
(of an object) skillfully or strongly constructed; made with care and quality.

Examples

He felt cheerful again, refreshed; presentable in his wide-cut brown suit, the well-made riding boots.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: poorly made - constructed with low quality or care
  • Synonym: well-built - strongly or solidly constructed
