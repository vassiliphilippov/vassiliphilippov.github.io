police headquarters

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ˈhɛdˌkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈhɛdˌkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the main office or station of the police in a particular district, from which police officers are dispatched and to which arrested persons are brought

Examples

McFeeley told the parents he would escort them to police headquarters in a half hour.
In place of the police headquarters was a new square filled with rubble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police station - a building where the police work and where people under arrest are taken
