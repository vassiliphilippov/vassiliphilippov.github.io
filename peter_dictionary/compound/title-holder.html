title-holder

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪtəl ˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtl ˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
A person who currently holds a championship title, especially as a result of winning first place in a competition.

Examples

Deane Beman, the National Amateur champion, and all the metropolitan district champions, including Bob Gardner, the amateur title-holder, also will receive awards.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: champion - person who has won first place in a competition

Etymology

Formed from 'title' and 'holder', meaning one who holds a particular title.
