wound tumor virus

1. NOUN

/ ˈwuːnd ˈtuːmɚ ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈwuːnd ˈtjuːmə ˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a plant virus transmitted by leafhoppers, known to cause tumors in certain plants

Examples

Wound tumor virus is a leafhopper transmitted virus not easily transmissible by mechanical inoculation (Black, 1944; Brakke et al., 1954).
