delivery boy

1. NOUN

/ dɪˈlɪvəri bɔɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəri bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or young man employed to deliver goods to customers

Examples

While she was thus engaged, McFeeley questioned her about her whereabouts the previous day, any recollections she had of people hanging around, of overcurious delivery boys or repairmen, of strange cars cruising the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: delivery man - a person employed to make deliveries, regardless of age or gender
  • Synonym: delivery girl - a girl or young woman employed to deliver goods
