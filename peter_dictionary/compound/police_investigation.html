police investigation

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ˌɪn.vɛs.tɪˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˌɪn.vɛs.tɪˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)

Examples
