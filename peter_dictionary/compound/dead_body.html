dead body

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / dɛd ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
The body of a person or animal that has died; a corpse.

Examples

It is like medical schools in India where, in that fairy land of religious inhibition, the dissection of dead bodies is frowned upon.

Synset Examples

they found the body in the lake

Related Terms

  • Synonym: corpse - the dead body of a human being
  • Synonym: cadaver - a dead human body, especially one used for dissection
