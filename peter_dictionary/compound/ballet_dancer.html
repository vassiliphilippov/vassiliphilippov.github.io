ballet dancer

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæleɪ ˈdænsər / (AmE), / ˈbæleɪ ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
A person who is trained in the art of ballet and performs as a member of a ballet company.

Examples

He was moving like a ballet dancer, playing for laughs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dancer - a person who dances, especially professionally

Etymology

From French 'ballet' (dance) and English 'dancer'.
