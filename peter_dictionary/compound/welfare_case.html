welfare case

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛlˌfɛr keɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌfeə keɪs / (BrE)
A person who is receiving assistance from government welfare programs, especially as a client handled by a welfare worker.

Examples

But he's a welfare case now - a human wreck - thanks to this modern witch doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: welfare recipient - a person who receives welfare benefits
