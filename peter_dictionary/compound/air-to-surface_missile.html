air-to-surface missile

1. NOUN

/ ɛr tə ˈsɝːfɪs ˈmɪsaɪl / (AmE), / ɛə tə ˈsɜːfɪs ˈmɪsaɪl / (BrE)
A missile designed to be launched from an aircraft and strike a target on the ground.

Examples

Funds are also included in this budget to continue the equipping of the B-52 wings with the Hound Dog air-to-surface missile.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: surface-to-air missile - a missile launched from the ground to destroy aircraft or other missiles
