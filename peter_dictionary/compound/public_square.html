public square

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk skwɛr / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk skweə / (BrE)
an open, typically paved area in a town or city where streets meet, often used for gatherings and events

Examples

I 've got a quarter of a million Germans in my state, and those krautheads tune in on Father Werther every night, and if he tells them to go out and piss in the public square, that's what they do.
"While Henry Morgan was escorting Miss Vera Green from the church social last Saturday night, a savage dog attacked them and bit Mr. Morgan on the public square ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: plaza - an open public space in a city or town, often used for community gatherings
  • Synonym: market square - a town square where a public market is or was held
  • Degree Higher: park - a public green area in a town, usually larger and with more vegetation than a square
