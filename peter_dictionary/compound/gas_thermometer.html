gas thermometer

1. NOUN

/ ɡæs θərˈmɑmɪtɚ / (AmE), / ɡæs θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
A type of thermometer that measures temperature by detecting changes in the pressure of a gas maintained at a constant volume.

Examples

Such an interferometer provides a means of absolute temperature measurement, and may be used as an alternative to the gas thermometer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: thermometer - instrument for measuring temperature
