higher education

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaɪ.ər ˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.ə ˌɛdjuːˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
education provided by colleges or universities, typically beyond the secondary school level

Examples

High motivation towards higher education must start early enough so that by the time the boy or girl reaches grade 9 he or she has at least developed those basic skills which are essential for academic work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tertiary education - education at university or college level; another term for higher education
  • Degree Lower: secondary education - education provided in middle and high schools, preceding higher education
