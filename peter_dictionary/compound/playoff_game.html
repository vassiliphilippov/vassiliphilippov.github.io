playoff game

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪˌɑf ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpleɪɒf ɡeɪm / (BrE)
An individual game that is part of a series of games played to determine the overall winner in a sports tournament or competition.

Examples

I never did miss one in a playoff game - I kicked about 20 in the five playoff games my last two years ".
