ad campaign

1. NOUN

/ æd kæmˈpeɪn / (AmE), / æd kæmˈpeɪn / (BrE)
An organized series of advertisements designed to promote a product, service, or idea.

Examples

"They were supposed to meet Thor at nine p. m. for a conference concerning the ad campaign for their soap, a new angle based on this SX-21 stuff ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advertising campaign - an organized program of advertisements

Etymology

Shortened form of 'advertising campaign'; 'ad' from 'advertisement', and 'campaign' from French 'campagne'.
