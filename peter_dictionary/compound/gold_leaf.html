gold leaf

1. NOUN

/ ɡoʊld lif / (AmE), / ɡəʊld liːf / (BrE)
very thin sheets of gold used for decoration, gilding, or art

Examples

In the room next to theirs was a huge cradle, of mahogany, ornately carved and decorated with gold leaf.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gold foil - thin sheet of gold, typically thicker than gold leaf
