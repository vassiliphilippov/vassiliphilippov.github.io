crew member

1. NOUN

/ kruː ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / kruː ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
A person who is part of the team operating an aircraft, ship, or other vehicle, especially a member of a flight crew.

Examples

On the third voyage, a near mutiny rising from a quarrel between Dutch and English crew members on the Half Moon had almost forced him to head the ship back to Amsterdam in mid Atlantic.
Hudson, presiding, heard Juet's defense, then called for testimony from crew members.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flight attendant - an airline employee who serves passengers and ensures their safety
  • Synonym: pilot - a person who operates the controls of an aircraft
