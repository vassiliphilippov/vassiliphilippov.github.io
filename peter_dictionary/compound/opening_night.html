opening night

1. NOUN

/ ˈoʊpənɪŋ naɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənɪŋ naɪt / (BrE)
The first public performance of a theatrical production, such as a play or musical.

Examples

The first night's the toughest.

Synset Examples

the opening received good critical reviews

Related Terms

  • Synonym: premiere - the first public performance of a play, film, etc.
