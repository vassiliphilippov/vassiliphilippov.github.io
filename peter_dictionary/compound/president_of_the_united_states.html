President of the United States

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɛzɪdənt əv ði ˌjuːˈnaɪtɪd steɪts / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdənt ɒv ðə ˌjuːˈnaɪtɪd steɪts / (BrE)
the head of state and head of government of the United States, responsible for leading the executive branch of the federal government

Examples

The President of the United States proclaimed the first Sunday in November as "National Grandmothers' Day "and urged America to say it with flowers.

Synset Examples

the President likes to jog every morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: president - the elected head of a republican state
  • Antonym: prime minister - the head of government in many countries, often contrasted with president

2. PROPN

/ ˈprɛzɪdənt əv ðə ˌjunəˈtɛd steɪts / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdənt əv ðə ˌjuːnɪtɪd steɪts / (BrE)
the elected head of state and chief executive officer of the United States of America

Examples

Just one small step looking for a man that wants to be President of the United States and having the cash to make it possible.

Synset Examples

the President likes to jog every morning

Related Terms

  • Synonym: president - the elected head of a republican state
  • Antonym: prime minister - the principal government minister in some countries
