lemon meringue pie

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛmən məˈrɪŋ paɪ / (AmE), / ˈlɛmən məˈrɪŋ paɪ / (BrE)
A dessert pie with a filling of lemon-flavored custard topped with a layer of fluffy meringue.

Examples

The innocence that they tried to conceal at the beginning is clearly destroyed forever when one of them, asking for a piece of lemon-meringue pie, gets a plate of English muffins instead.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon tart - dessert with lemon filling, usually without meringue topping
  • Degree Lower: custard pie - pie filled with sweetened custard
