license plate

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪsəns pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsəns pleɪt / (BrE)
A metal or plastic plate attached to a vehicle displaying its official registration number.

Examples

The roof of the license-plate factory needs resurfacing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: number plate - British English term for license plate
