fashion industry

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæʃ.ən ˌɪn.də.stri / (AmE), / ˈfæʃ.ən ˌɪn.də.stri / (BrE)
the business sector involved in the design, production, and sale of fashionable clothing and accessories

Examples

The fashion world was stunned today... ...by the sudden deaths of models Candy Walker and Amanda Keeler.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apparel industry - industry sector focused on clothing and garments
