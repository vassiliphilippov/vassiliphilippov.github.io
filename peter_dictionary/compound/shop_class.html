shop class

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑp klæs / (AmE), / ʃɒp klɑːs / (BrE)
A class in school that teaches practical skills related to trades such as woodworking, metalworking, or electronics.

Examples

It got me out of the wood shop a month out of the year, and that was fine by me.

Synset Examples

I built a birdhouse in shop

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wood shop - a school class teaching woodworking
  • Synonym: industrial arts - school subjects involving manual skills and technical crafts
