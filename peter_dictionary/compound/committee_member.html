committee member

1. NOUN

/ kəˈmɪɾi ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / kəˈmɪti ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who belongs to a committee, taking part in its meetings and decisions

Examples

But questions with which committee members taunted bankers appearing as witnesses left little doubt that they will recommend passage of it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
  • Synonym: member - a person belonging to a group or organization
