Department of Health Education and Welfare

1. PROPN

/ dɪˈpɑːrt.mənt əv hɛlθ ˌedʒ.ʊˈkeɪ.ʃən ənd ˈwɛl.fɛr / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːt.mənt əv hɛlθ ˌedjʊˈkeɪʃən ənd ˈwɛlfɛə / (BrE)
The former executive department of the United States government responsible for health, education, and welfare matters, established in 1953 and divided in 1979.

Examples

The fullest cooperation by and with Atomic Energy Commission, the Department of Health Education and Welfare, the Department of State, and other concerned agencies shall also be carried out in the interest of achieving the objectives of this Act.
