don't-know

1. NOUN

/ doʊnt ˈnoʊ / (AmE), / dəʊnt ˈnəʊ / (BrE)
A person who answers 'I don't know' in response to a question, especially in a public opinion poll.

Examples

I don't know.

Synset Examples

70% in favor, 13% opposed and 17% don't-knows

Related Terms

  • Synonym: undecided - someone who has not made up their mind, especially in a poll or vote
