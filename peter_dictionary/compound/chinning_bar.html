chinning bar

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɪnɪŋ bɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪnɪŋ bɑː / (BrE)
a strong, horizontal bar fixed above ground level, used for performing chin-ups or pull-up exercises by grasping with the hands and lifting oneself up

Examples

For arm and shoulder strength a chinning bar is recommended.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pull-up bar - bar used for pull-up or chin-up exercises

Etymology

From 'chin' (the front part of the lower jaw) + 'bar', relating to the exercise movement of lifting the chin above the bar.
