Second Coming

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
(In Christian belief) the anticipated return of Jesus Christ to earth, especially to judge the living and the dead at the Last Judgment.

Examples

Jubal would have bet his life that if Anne were to witness the Second Coming, she would memorize date, time, personae, events, and barometric pressure without batting her calm blue eyes.

Etymology

Refers to the 'second' coming or advent of Christ, as opposed to His first appearance.
