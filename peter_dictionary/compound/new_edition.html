new edition

1. NOUN

/ nu ɪˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / njuː ɪˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
A version of a book, publication, or other work that has been updated or revised and released again.

Examples

The new editions of topographic maps being made by the federal government are excellent for orienting yourself to the natural features of the site.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revised edition - a version of a publication that has been revised
