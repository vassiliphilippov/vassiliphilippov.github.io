public security

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk sɪˈkjʊərɪti / (BrE)
The condition of being protected from crime, disorder, or threats in public places or within a community.

Examples

We cannot guarantee public safety.

Synset Examples

he was arrested for disturbing the peace

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public safety - the welfare and protection of the general public, often used interchangeably with public security
