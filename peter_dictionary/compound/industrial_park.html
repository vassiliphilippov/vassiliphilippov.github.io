industrial park

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈdʌstriəl pɑrk / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl pɑːk / (BrE)
an area of land, often located outside a city, that is specially planned and equipped for businesses and factories to operate

Examples

In ever greater numbers, factories are locating in the suburbs or in "industrial parks "removed from the city's political jurisdiction.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: business park - area for offices and light industry, similar to industrial park
