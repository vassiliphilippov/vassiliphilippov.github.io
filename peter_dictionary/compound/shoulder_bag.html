shoulder bag

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldɚ bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə bæɡ / (BrE)
A large handbag with a strap designed to be carried over the shoulder.

Examples

From his shoulder bag peeked the seven inch barrel of a Luger.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handbag - a small bag for carrying personal items, often held in the hand or over the arm
