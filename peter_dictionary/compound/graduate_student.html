graduate student

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡrædʒuət ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / ˈɡradjʊət ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
A student who is pursuing advanced academic studies beyond a bachelor's degree, such as a master's or doctoral degree.

Examples

Then Kerr, a graduate student from Illinois, moved past him on a straightaway and held off Mills's challenge on the final turn.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: undergraduate - a student at a college or university who has not yet earned a bachelor's or equivalent degree
