labor market

1. NOUN

/ ˈleɪ.bɚ ˈmɑːr.kɪt / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə ˈmɑː.kɪt / (BrE)
the area or system in which workers seek employment and employers look for workers, usually governed by the supply and demand for labor

Examples

While the factories were always the center of the labor market, they were often on the city's periphery.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: job market - the market or environment where jobs are offered and sought
