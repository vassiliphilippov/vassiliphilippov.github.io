swimming hole

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmɪŋ hoʊl / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ həʊl / (BrE)
A natural pool, usually in a creek or river, deep enough to be used for swimming.

Examples

Where Cuddleback Brook purled into the Neversink was a magnificent swimming hole.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pool - a small area of still water, often used for swimming
