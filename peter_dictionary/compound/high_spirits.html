high spirits

1. NOUN

/ haɪ ˈspɪrɪts / (AmE), / haɪ ˈspɪərɪts / (BrE)
a feeling of lively joy or cheerful enthusiasm; an exuberant or buoyant mood

Examples

Some who have written on Utopia have treated it as "a learned diversion of a learned world ", "a phantasy with which More amused himself ", "a holiday work, a spontaneous overflow of intellectual high spirits, a revel of debate, paradox, comedy and invention ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: elation - a feeling of great happiness and excitement
  • Degree Lower: happiness - the state of being happy
  • Degree Higher: euphoria - an intense feeling of excitement and happiness
