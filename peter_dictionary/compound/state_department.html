State Department

1. PROPN

/ steɪt dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / steɪt dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
the federal executive department of the United States government responsible for international relations and foreign policy.

Examples

State Department officials refusing to show their passes at the boundary, and driving two blocks into East Berlin under military escort, will not avail.
