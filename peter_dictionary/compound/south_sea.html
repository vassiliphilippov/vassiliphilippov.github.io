South Sea

1. NOUN

/ saʊθ si / (AmE), / saʊθ siː / (BrE)
any sea located south of the equator, especially referring to the South Pacific Ocean

Examples

For he seemed to sense at once that before him was no South Sea, but the solid bulk of the North American continent.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: South Pacific - the southern part of the Pacific Ocean
