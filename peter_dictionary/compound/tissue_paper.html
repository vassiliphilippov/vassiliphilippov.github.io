tissue paper

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪʃu ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪʃu ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a soft, thin, and often translucent paper, used especially for wrapping delicate items or for decorative purposes

Examples

Winston followed her around the room, collecting the small frail objects (Christmas, birthday, and anniversary) and wrapping them in tissue paper.
Winston folded the tissue paper carefully.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tissue - thin, soft, absorbent paper used as a disposable cloth
