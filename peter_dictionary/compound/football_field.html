football field

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʊt.bɔl fiːld / (AmE), / ˈfʊt.bɔːl fiːld / (BrE)
A rectangular area marked out for playing football, with specified dimensions and markings.

Examples

That's the length of five football fields.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soccer field - the playing field on which soccer is played
