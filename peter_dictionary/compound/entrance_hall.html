entrance hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛntrəns hɔl / (AmE), / ˈɛntrəns hɔːl / (BrE)
a large room or area near the entrance of a building, used for receiving guests or as a passage to other rooms

Examples

They advanced in a line across the entrance hall to the stairway and up, with gingerly steps, towards the first landing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foyer - an entrance hall or other open area in a building used by the public
  • Synonym: lobby - a large open area inside the entrance of a public building
  • Synonym: vestibule - an antechamber or small foyer leading into a larger space
