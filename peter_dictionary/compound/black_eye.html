black eye

1. NOUN

/ blæk aɪ / (AmE), / blæk aɪ / (BrE)
A dark, swollen area of skin around the eye, caused by a blow or injury.

Examples

"Hush ", said Uncle Randolph, smiling, "or I 'll give you another black eye ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shiner - slang for 'black eye', bruise around the eye
