telephone number

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn ˌnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ˌnʌmbə / (BrE)
A series of digits assigned to a particular telephone line, used for making phone calls to that line.

Examples

Anyone fortunate enough to have one of those early phones advertised the fact along with the telephone number in the Manchester Journal.

Synset Examples

he has an unlisted number

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone number - A series of numbers used to call a telephone
