work of art

1. NOUN

/ wɝːk əv ɑːrt / (AmE), / wɜːk əv ɑːt / (BrE)
a creation in one of the fine arts, such as a painting or sculpture, that is considered to have artistic value or merit

Examples

For it is such a distinguished place, with such fine works of art and such a big library, that there can be little doubt but that the owner has become depraved by all this culture.
"But, Rabbi Melzi, you do n't object to others creating works of art "?
Understanding a work of art involves recognition of the ideas that it reflects or embodies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masterpiece - a work considered outstanding or the best of an artist's output

2. NOUN

/ wɝk əv ɑrt / (AmE), / wɜːk əv ɑːt / (BrE)
an object made with artistic skill, especially a painting, sculpture, or other item considered to have artistic merit

Examples

So now, like me... ...she is a living work of art.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: masterpiece - an outstanding work of art or craftsmanship
