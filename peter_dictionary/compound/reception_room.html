reception room

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈsɛpʃən ruːm / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛpʃən ruːm / (BrE)
A room in a house, hotel, or other building used for receiving and entertaining visitors.

Examples

At a ceremony in the reception room of Mayor Richardson Dilworth, the Eagles were honored for winning the championship.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drawing room - a room in a house where guests are received and entertained
  • Degree Lower: living room - a room in a house for general and informal everyday use
