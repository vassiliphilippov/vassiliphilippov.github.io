real world

1. NOUN

/ ˈriːəl wɝːld / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl wɜːld / (BrE)
The state of things as they actually exist outside of academic, theoretical, or speculative contexts; practical or everyday life.

Examples

I send that money out into the real world.

Synset Examples

a good consultant must have a lot of experience in the real world

Related Terms

  • Antonym: academia - the academic or scholarly world, especially as contrasted with practical experience
