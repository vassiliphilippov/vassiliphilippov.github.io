exponential function

1. NOUN

/ ɪkˌspoʊnɛnʃəl ˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˌspəʊnɛnʃəl ˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
A mathematical function in which the variable appears in the exponent, typically expressed as f(x) = a^x.
