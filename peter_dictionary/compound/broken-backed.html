broken-backed

1. ADJ

/ ˈbroʊkən ˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən ˌbækt / (BrE)
having a broken or damaged spine, typically referring to books or animals

Examples

Everywhere else his ideas lay or hung in visible form: his models, drawings, ten foot canvases in monochromes from his painting days, and underfoot a windfall of broken-backed books that looked as though their insides had been ransacked by a maniac.

Synset Examples

a broken-backed book
a broken-backed old horse

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broken - damaged or no longer in one piece
