mass murder

1. NOUN

/ mæs ˈmɝːdɚ / (AmE), / mæs ˈmɜːdə / (BrE)
the deliberate and excessive killing of a large number of people, often at the same time or over a short period

Examples

And though it was logical that a man who could plot mass murder would not hesitate to speak an untruth, still it was difficult to understand why Spencer spoke only for Cromwell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: genocide - the deliberate killing of a large group of people, especially those of a particular nation or ethnic group
