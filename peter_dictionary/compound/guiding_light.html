guiding light

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdɪŋ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪŋ laɪt / (BrE)
A person who serves as an inspiration or leader to others, often through their actions, ideas, or achievements.

Examples

In this country, the two guiding lights are, without doubt, Felix Fing and Anna Pulova.

Synset Examples

he was host to a large gathering of luminaries

Related Terms

  • Synonym: luminary - a person who inspires or influences others; a leading light
