national park

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæʃənəl pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈnæʃnəl pɑːk / (BrE)
An area of land protected and managed by a national government for public enjoyment, wildlife preservation, and conservation.

Examples

The National Parks, always popular camping places, are facing the same pressure.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nature reserve - an area designated for the protection of wildlife and natural resources
  • Synonym: state park - a park or area protected and managed by a state government
