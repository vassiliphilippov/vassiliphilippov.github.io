as we say

1. ADV

/ æz wi seɪ / (AmE), / æz wi seɪ / (BrE)
Used to introduce or acknowledge a commonly used phrase or expression, often suggesting a figurative or conventional usage.

Examples

The pathetic evidence is no longer before us; and we are now so fatigued in body and mind that feeling is, as we say, quite dead.

Synset Examples
