sweet-smelling

1. ADJ

/ ˈswit ˌsmɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswiːt ˌsmelɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a pleasant, natural fragrance.

Examples

Drenched and shaking, he stood near the sweet-smelling stall and dared to pat her muzzle.

Synset Examples

odoriferous spices
the odorous air of the orchard
the perfumed air of June
scented flowers

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fragrant - having a pleasant or sweet smell
  • Synonym: aromatic - having a noticeable and pleasant smell
  • Antonym: malodorous - having an unpleasant smell
