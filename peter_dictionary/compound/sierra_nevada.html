Sierra Nevada

1. PROPN

/ siˌɛrə nəˈvɑdə / (AmE), / siˌɛərə nɪˈvɑːdə / (BrE)
a major mountain range in eastern California, USA, known for its high peaks including Mount Whitney

Examples

Michael, you'’re sitting high up in the Sierra mountains.

Etymology

From Spanish 'sierra' (mountain range) + 'nevada' (snowy), meaning 'snowy mountains'.
