pork chop

1. NOUN

/ pɔrk tʃɑp / (AmE), / pɔːk tʃɒp / (BrE)
A slice or cut of pork, typically taken from the rib or loin and often cooked by frying, grilling, or broiling.

Examples

He was tired, he had his business worries, and the sight of his wife arranging pork chops in the broiler only seemed like an extension of a boring day.
Oh, I want me a pork chop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lamb chop - slice or cut of lamb, often cooked similarly
