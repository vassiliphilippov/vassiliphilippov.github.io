lending institution

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛndɪŋ ˌɪnstəˈtuʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɛndɪŋ ˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
A financial organization that provides loans to individuals or businesses.

Examples

Participation loans are those made jointly by the SBA and banks or other private lending institutions.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bank - a financial institution licensed to receive deposits and make loans
  • Synonym: credit union - a cooperative financial institution providing loans and banking services
