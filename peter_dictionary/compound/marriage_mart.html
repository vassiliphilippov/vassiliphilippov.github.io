marriage mart

1. NOUN

/ ˈmærɪdʒ mɑrt / (AmE), / ˈmærɪdʒ mɑːt / (BrE)
a business or place where prospective marriage partners are matched or where marriages are arranged, especially for commercial reasons

Examples

These announcements are, in effect, advertisements for themselves as thriving marriage marts.

Etymology

Formed from 'marriage' and 'mart' (from Middle English, meaning market).
