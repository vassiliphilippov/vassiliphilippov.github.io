inside job

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪn.saɪd ʤɑb / (AmE), / ˈɪn.saɪd dʒɒb / (BrE)
a crime or wrongdoing committed by or with the help of someone from within the organization targeted

Examples

And it makes a very poor red herring for an inside job.

Synset Examples

the police decided that the crime was an inside job

Related Terms

  • Synonym: betrayal - the act of betraying someone or something
