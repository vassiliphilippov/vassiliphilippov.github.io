worldly belongings

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːldli bəˈlɔŋɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli bɪˈlɒŋɪŋz / (BrE)
all the possessions and property that someone owns in this world, especially material things

Examples

Of all their worldly belongings, next to the oxen and his gun, the seed grain had been the most treasured.

Synset Examples

he left all his worldly possessions to his daughter

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worldly possessions - all the property owned by someone
