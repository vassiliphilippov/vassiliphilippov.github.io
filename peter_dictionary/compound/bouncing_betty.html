bouncing betty

1. NOUN

/ ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˈbɛti / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsɪŋ ˈbɛti / (BrE)
an antipersonnel land mine that jumps into the air before exploding, designed to wound or kill personnel above ground

Examples

They set bouncing betties to jump and explode at testicle level while we more mercifully had them go off at the head.

Etymology

American English; 'Betty' is a nickname, with 'bouncing' describing the mine's jumping mechanism.
