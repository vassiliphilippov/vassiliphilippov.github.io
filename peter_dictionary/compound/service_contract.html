service contract

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɜrvɪs ˌkɑnˌtrækt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌkɒntrækt / (BrE)
a formal agreement in which one party agrees to provide maintenance or other services for a specified period, usually in exchange for payment

Examples

When Johnny was starting out, he was signed to a personal service contract with a big bandleader.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: warranty - written guarantee for repair or replacement of goods
