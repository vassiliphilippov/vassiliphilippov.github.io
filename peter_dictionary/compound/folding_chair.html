folding chair

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊldɪŋ t͡ʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldɪŋ tʃeə / (BrE)
A chair with a frame that allows it to be folded flat for easy storage and transport.

Examples

The colonel crouched tensely on one of the folding chairs, methodically tearing at his thumbnail.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chair - a seat for one person, typically with a back and four legs
