apartment house

1. NOUN

/ əˈpɑːrtmənt haʊs / (AmE), / əˈpɑːtmənt haʊs / (BrE)
A building containing several separate apartments for people to live in.

Examples

He lived in an apartment house not over three or four years old, a reclaimed island of landscaped brick and glass on the fringe of the business district.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: apartment building - a building divided into apartments

Etymology

From 'apartment' meaning a set of rooms for living in, and 'house' meaning a building for people to live in.
