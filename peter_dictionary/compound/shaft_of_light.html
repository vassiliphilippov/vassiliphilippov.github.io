shaft of light

1. NOUN

/ ʃæft əv laɪt / (AmE), / ʃɑːft əv laɪt / (BrE)
a narrow column or beam of light, especially when seen shining through an opening or in an otherwise dark space

Examples

At the end of a shaft of light, the pews appeared to be broad stairs in a long dungeon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: beam of light - a ray or shaft of light
