potential energy

1. NOUN

/ pəˈtɛnʃəl ˈɛnərdʒi / (AmE), / pəˈtɛnʃəl ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
the energy that a body possesses because of its position or condition, especially in relation to other objects or forces
