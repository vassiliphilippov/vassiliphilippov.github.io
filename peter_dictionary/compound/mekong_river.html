Mekong River

1. PROPN

/ ˈmeɪˌkɔŋ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈmiːˌkɒŋ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in Southeast Asia, flowing from the Tibetan Plateau through several countries to the South China Sea in southern Vietnam

Examples

The slim pirogues in harmony with the majestically meandering Mekong River.

Etymology

From Lao and Thai 'Mènam Khong', meaning 'Mother of Waters'.
