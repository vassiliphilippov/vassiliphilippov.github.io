book jacket

1. NOUN

/ bʊk ˈdʒækət / (AmE), / bʊk ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A removable paper cover wrapped around a book, often printed with promotional information and illustrations.

Examples

I did book jackets and covers for paperback reprints: naked girls huddling in corners of dingy furnished rooms while at the doorway, daring the cops to take him, is the guy in shirt sleeves clutching a revolver.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dust jacket - Removable outer cover for a book, typically made of paper
