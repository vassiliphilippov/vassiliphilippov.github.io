good health

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd hɛlθ / (AmE), / ɡʊd hɛlθ / (BrE)
A state of physical and mental well-being, marked by vigor and the absence of disease or illness.

Examples

Their main asset is an abundance of unsaturated fatty acids, so necessary for maintaining the good health of the circulatory system.
McFeeley noted the immaculate lawn and gardens: each blade of grass cropped, bright and firm; each shrub glazed with good health.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: ill health - state of being unhealthy or suffering from illness
  • Synonym: health - the general condition of the body or mind with reference to soundness and vigor
