loosely knit

1. ADJ

/ ˈluːsli nɪt / (AmE), / ˈluːsli nɪt / (BrE)
Describing a group or organization whose members are only weakly connected by social or formal ties.

Examples

All of them were there in this loosely knit association of diversified ideologies, and each berated the other and beat his breast for his own approaches.

Synset Examples

a loosely knit group

Related Terms

  • Antonym: tightly knit - Describing a group whose members are closely united or connected
