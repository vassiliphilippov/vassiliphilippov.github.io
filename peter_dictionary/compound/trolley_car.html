trolley car

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑli kɑr / (AmE), / ˈtrɒli kɑː / (BrE)
A passenger vehicle that runs on rails in city streets and is powered by electricity, typically collected from an overhead wire.

Examples

The teeth will rain from his mouth like pebbles, his wife will make him cocu with fishmongers, and a trolley car will grow in his stomach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streetcar - a passenger vehicle running on rails in streets
  • Synonym: tram - a rail vehicle that runs on tracks in city streets
