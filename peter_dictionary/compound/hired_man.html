hired man

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪərd mæn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd mæn / (BrE)
A man employed, often on a farm or ranch, to do manual labor or various jobs.

Examples

"Fred was mighty crude about the way he took in cattle "his own hired man, Andy Ross, mentioned later.
The hired man ran over to help his boss.

Synset Examples

the hired hand fixed the railing
a ranch hand

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hired hand - person employed to do manual labor on a farm or ranch
  • Synonym: farmhand - laborer on a farm
