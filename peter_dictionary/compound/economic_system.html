economic system

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈkɑnəmɪk ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / iːˈkɒnəmɪk ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system by which goods and services are produced, distributed, and consumed within a society or country.

Examples

If we cannot stop warfare in our own economic system, how can we expect to abolish it internationally?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: economy - the wealth and resources of a country or region, especially in terms of production and consumption
