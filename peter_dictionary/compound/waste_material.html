waste material

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪst məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈweɪst məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
material that is left over and discarded because it is considered worthless or unwanted

Examples

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste - material that is no longer needed and discarded
  • Synonym: refuse - something discarded as worthless or useless; trash or rubbish
