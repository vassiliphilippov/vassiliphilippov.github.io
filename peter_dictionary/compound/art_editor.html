art editor

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈɛdɪtə / (BrE)
an editor responsible for overseeing illustrations, graphics, and the visual layout of printed materials such as magazines, books, or newspapers

Examples

McKenzie, the art editor, took one look at my finished sketch and said, "Nothing doing, Rufus.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editor - person who prepares content for publication
