old times

1. NOUN

/ oʊld taɪmz / (AmE), / əʊld taɪmz / (BrE)
Times in the past, especially remembered with nostalgia or affection.

Examples

Those were the great old days, you know.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auld lang syne - the good old times; times long past, remembered fondly
  • Synonym: bygone days - days in the past, now gone
