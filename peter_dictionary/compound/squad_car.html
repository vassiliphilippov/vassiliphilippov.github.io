squad car

1. NOUN

/ skwɑd kɑr / (AmE), / skwɒd kɑː / (BrE)
a police car used for patrolling streets, typically equipped with communication devices

Examples

The meat wagon, therefore, was not out in front of the house any more, but the cluster of squad cars was still there and there was a cop on the door downstairs to screen any comings and goings.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police car - vehicle used by police officers for official duties
