life cycle

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
the series of changes in the life of an organism, including reproduction, from one stage to the recurrence of that same stage

Examples

Its life history is much simpler than that of the truly colonial bumblebees and can serve as an example of the life cycle of many other species.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life span - the length of time for which an organism lives
