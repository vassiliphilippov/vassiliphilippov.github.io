packinghouse

1. NOUN

/ ˈpækɪŋˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈpækɪŋˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building where foodstuffs are cleaned, processed, and packed for distribution.

Examples

It is brought to packing houses, cleaned and graded as to size and quality, and packed in protective excelsior.

Synset Examples

they came from an apple packinghouse

Etymology

From 'packing' (the act of putting goods into containers) + 'house' (building for a specific purpose).
