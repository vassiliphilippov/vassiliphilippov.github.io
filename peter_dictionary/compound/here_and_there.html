here and there

1. ADV

/ hɪr ənd ðɛr / (AmE), / hɪə ənd ðeə / (BrE)
in various places; in one place and then another.

Examples

A lovely beast like that running around... ...could put steam in a man's strides.

Synset Examples

he worked here and there but never for long in one town
we drove here and there in the darkness

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: everywhere - in or to all places
  • Degree Lower: somewhere - in or to an unspecified place
