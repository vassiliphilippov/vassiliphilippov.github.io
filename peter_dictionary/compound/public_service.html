public service

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
A service provided for the benefit of the community or its institutions, typically organized by the government or an official body.

Examples

Our inmates, properly supervised, will be put to work outside these walls, performing all manner of public service.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: community service - unpaid work intended to be of social use, often as a legal penalty
