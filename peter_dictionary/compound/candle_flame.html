candle flame

1. NOUN

/ ˈkændl fleɪm / (AmE), / ˈkændl fleɪm / (BrE)
the small, bright flame produced by a burning candle

Examples

Lord Byron poured himself another glass of wine and held it up to the candle flame admiring the rich color.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: flame - the visible, gaseous part of a fire
  • Synonym: candlelight - the light emitted by a burning candle
