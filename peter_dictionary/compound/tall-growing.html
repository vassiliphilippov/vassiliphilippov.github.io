tall-growing

1. ADJ

/ tɔl ˈɡroʊɪŋ / (AmE), / tɔːl ˈɡrəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(Of plants) growing with tall, thin or spindly stems, often with excessive height in relation to their overall robustness.

Examples

The day will come, in midsummer, when you find your plants becoming "leggy ", running to tall-growing foliage at the expense of blossoms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: leggy - (Of plants) having long, thin stems; spindly
