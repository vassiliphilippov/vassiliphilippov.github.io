standard of measurement

1. NOUN

/ ˈstændɚd əv ˈmɛʒɚmənt / (AmE), / ˈstændəd əv ˈmɛʒəmənt / (BrE)
An established reference or basis used to compare, measure, or judge quantities or qualities.

Examples

The dimensions of these waves dwarf all our usual standards of measurement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yardstick - something used as a standard of comparison; a measuring device or guideline
  • Synonym: benchmark - a standard or point of reference against which things may be compared
