objet d'art

1. NOUN

/ əbˌʒeɪ ˈdɑr / (AmE), / ɒbˌʒeɪ ˈdɑː / (BrE)
An object of artistic value, especially a small decorative work of art.

Examples

Above all, disabuse yourselves of any thought that I propose to vent moral indignation at your rifling my residence, to whimper over the loss of a few objets d'art, or to shame you into rectitude.

Synset Examples

this store sells only objets d'art
it is not known who created this piece

Etymology

From French, literally 'object of art'.
