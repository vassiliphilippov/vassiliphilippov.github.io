in evidence

1. ADJ

/ ɪn ˈɛvədən(t)s / (AmE), / ɪn ˈɛvɪdəns / (BrE)
Clearly visible or noticeable; easy to see.

Examples

This is especially in evidence among the present generation of the suburban middle class.
The trends have been in evidence for many years - population shifts to the Southwest and Far West, and from city to suburbs.
Its growth continues steadily on a par with past growth; and no limitation is in evidence.
Some cases in evidence of the use of roleplaying for analysis may help explain the procedure.

Synset Examples

they were much in evidence during the fighting
she made certain that her engagement ring was in evidence

Related Terms

  • Synonym: conspicuous - easily seen or noticed; attracting attention
  • Synonym: obvious - easily perceived or understood; clear
  • Antonym: hidden - not visible or easily noticed
