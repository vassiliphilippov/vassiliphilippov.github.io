Word of God

1. NOUN

/ wɝd əv ɡɑd / (AmE), / wɜːd əv ɡɒd / (BrE)
a manifestation of God's mind and will, especially as expressed through scriptures or divine revelation

Examples

That parallelism affects his picture of man's disobedience too; for as it was Christ, the Word of God, who came to rescue man, so it was disobedience to the word of God in the beginning that brought death into the world, and all our woe.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: revelation - the divine or supernatural disclosure to humans of something relating to human existence or the world
