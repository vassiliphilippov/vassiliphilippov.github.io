on-the-scene

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ðə sin / (AmE), / ɒn ðə siːn / (BrE)
Present at the actual location where an event is happening, especially used of reporters, investigators, or observers.

Examples

The husky 6 - 3, 205 - pound lefthander, was in command all the way before an on the scene audience of only 949 and countless of television viewers in the Denver area.

Synset Examples

an on-the-spot reporter
on-the-spot inspections
an on-the scene newscast

Related Terms
