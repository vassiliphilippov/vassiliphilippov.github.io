public transit

1. NOUN

/ ˌpʌblɪk ˈtrænzɪt / (AmE), / ˌpʌblɪk ˈtrænzɪt / (BrE)
A system of buses, trains, or other vehicles that is available for use by the general public, usually running on fixed routes and schedules.

Examples

What hurts most is the damage the automobile has done to central-city retailing, especially in those cities where public transit is feeble.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: public transport - British English term for public transit
  • Synonym: mass transit - system of transportation that carries large numbers of people
