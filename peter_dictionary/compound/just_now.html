just now

1. ADV

/ dʒʌst naʊ / (AmE), / dʒʌst naʊ / (BrE)
a very short time ago; only a moment ago

Examples

"Why, Winston ", she 'd cry, "I just now saw you eating it out of the jar "!

Synset Examples

he has just arrived
the sun just now came out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: recently - not long ago, a short time ago
  • Antonym: right now - at this very moment
