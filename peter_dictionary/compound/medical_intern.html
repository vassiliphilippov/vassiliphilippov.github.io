medical intern

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈɪnˌtɝn / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈɪnˌtɜːn / (BrE)
A graduate or advanced student in medicine who is undergoing supervised practical training in a hospital.

Examples

Four additional deputies be employed at the Fulton County Jail and "a doctor, medical intern or extern be employed for night and weekend duty at the jail ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: houseman - A junior doctor undergoing training in a hospital (British term)
