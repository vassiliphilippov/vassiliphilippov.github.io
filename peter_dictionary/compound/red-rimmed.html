red-rimmed

1. ADJ

/ ˈrɛd rɪmd / (AmE), / ˈrɛd rɪmd / (BrE)
Having a red border or edge, especially used to describe eyes that are outlined with redness due to irritation or emotion.

Examples

Griffith looked at him suspiciously through red-rimmed eyes.

Synset Examples

your red-rimmed eyes reveal that you have been crying
