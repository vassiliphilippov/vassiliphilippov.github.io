good day

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈdeɪ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈdeɪ / (BrE)
Used as a polite greeting or farewell, particularly in formal or old-fashioned contexts.

Examples

- Good day, sir. - Good day.
- Good day, sir. - Good day.

Synset Examples

they said their good-byes

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goodbye - expression used when parting
  • Antonym: hello - expression used as a greeting
