political system

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
The set of formal legal institutions, organizations, and processes through which a society organizes, distributes, and exercises political power.
