witch doctor

1. NOUN

/ wɪtʃ ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / wɪtʃ ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
A person, especially in some traditional societies, who is believed to have healing powers and uses magic or supernatural methods to cure illnesses.

Examples

But it is our health - more precious than all the money in the world - that these modern witch doctors with their fake therapeutic gadgets are gambling away.
But he's a welfare case now - a human wreck - thanks to this modern witch doctor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: healer - a person who heals or attempts to heal others
  • Synonym: shaman - a person regarded as having access to, and influence in, the world of good and evil spirits
