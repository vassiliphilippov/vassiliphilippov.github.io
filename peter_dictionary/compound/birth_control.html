birth control

1. NOUN

/ bɝːθ kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / bɜːθ kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
The methods or practice of preventing pregnancy, especially by using contraception.

The laws of many states permit birth control only for medical reasons.

Related Terms
