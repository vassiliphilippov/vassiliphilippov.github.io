chorus line

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrəs laɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɔːrəs laɪn / (BrE)
A group of dancers or singers who perform together in a theatrical production, especially in musical theatre or revues.

Examples

I want 'em all lined up here, just like a pretty little chorus line.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chorus - A group of performers who sing or dance together in a show
