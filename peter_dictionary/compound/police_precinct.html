police precinct

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlis ˈpriː.sɪŋkt / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈpriː.sɪŋkt / (BrE)
A district in a city or town established for police administration and patrolled by a particular police force.

Examples

Louis' restaurant in the Bronx. - Is it reliable? - My man in McCluskey's precinct.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: precinct - a district or area within certain boundaries, especially for administrative purposes
