contract killing

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːnˌtrækt ˈkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒntrækt ˈkɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
An act of murder in which someone is killed by a hired person, usually for payment, pursuant to an agreement.

Examples

They don't have men killed.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: assassination - the act of murdering a prominent person for political or religious reasons
  • Synonym: homicide - the killing of one person by another
