cooking utensil

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʊkɪŋ juːˈtɛnsl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkʊkɪŋ juːˈtɛnsəl / (BrE)
an implement used in the kitchen for preparing or cooking food, typically made from materials that withstand heat

Examples

As usual, Mrs. Crombie was standing in the midst of a confusion of cooking utensils.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kitchen utensil - implement used in the kitchen
  • Synonym: cookware - pots, pans, and other vessels for cooking
