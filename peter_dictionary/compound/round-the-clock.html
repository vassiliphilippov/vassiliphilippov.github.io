round-the-clock

1. ADJ

/ ˌraʊnd ðə ˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˌraʊnd ðə ˈklɒk / (BrE)
Happening or continuing all day and all night without stopping; continuous, day and night.

Examples

A police captain is on call 24 hours a day.

Synset Examples

around-the-clock nursing care

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continuous - not stopping; happening without interruption
  • Antonym: intermittent - happening at irregular intervals; not continuous
