air mattress

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr ˌmætrəs / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌmætrəs / (BrE)
A mattress that can be deflated for storage and inflated for use, typically used as a portable or temporary bed.

Examples

Neither are beds, thanks to air mattresses and sleeping bags.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: camp bed - a portable bed for temporary use, especially outdoors
  • Synonym: sleeping bag - a padded or insulated bag for sleeping outdoors
