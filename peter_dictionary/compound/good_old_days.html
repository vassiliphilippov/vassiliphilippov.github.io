good old days

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd oʊld deɪz / (AmE), / ɡʊd əʊld deɪz / (BrE)
A time in the past remembered fondly or nostalgically, often seen as better than the present.

Examples

Oh, seeing you reminds me of New York, the old days!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: yesteryear - a period in the past, especially nostalgically recalled
