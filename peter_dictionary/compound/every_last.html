every last

1. ADJ

/ ˈɛvri læst / (AmE), / ˈɛvri lɑːst / (BrE)
Used to emphasize the totality or completeness of a group, meaning absolutely every one without exception.

Examples

And for the briefest of moments... every last man at Shawshank felt free.

Synset Examples

every last one of you

Related Terms

  • Synonym: every - all the individual members of a group
