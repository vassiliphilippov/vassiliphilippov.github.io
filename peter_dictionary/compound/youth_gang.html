youth gang

1. NOUN

/ juθ gæŋ / (AmE), / juːθ gæŋ / (BrE)
A group of young people, typically teenagers, who associate closely and often engage in criminal or antisocial activities together.

Examples

The Nacionale will go to the Lakeville Road Boys, the Capri to the Corleone family, the Sevilla Biltmore also, but Eddie Levine of Newport will bring in the Pennino brothers, Dino and Eddie, for a piece and also to handle the actual casino operations.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gang - an organized group of criminals or troublemakers
