ice pack

1. NOUN

/ aɪs pæk / (AmE), / aɪs pæk / (BrE)
a bag or pouch filled with ice, used to cool the body or reduce swelling and pain from injuries

Examples

Joel came and sat mutely with her, sharing her pain and anguish, averting his eyes from the ice packs on her bosom.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cold pack - an alternate term for a similar device used to apply cold to the body
