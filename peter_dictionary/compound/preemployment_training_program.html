preemployment training program

1. NOUN

/ priːɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / priːɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
A program of instruction designed to equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed before starting paid employment.

Examples

The opportunity exists for states to reserve some of their vocational education funds to apply on an ad hoc flexible basis to subsidize any local preemployment training programs that my be quickly set up in a community to aid a new industrial plant.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: training program - a course or system of teaching to develop skills
