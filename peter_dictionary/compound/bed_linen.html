bed linen

1. NOUN

/ bɛd ˈlɪnɪn / (AmE), / bɛd ˈlɪnɪn / (BrE)
Sheets, pillowcases, and other fabric articles used to make up a bed.

Examples

Scrapings from the bed linen identical with the lint and thread found in the nasal and oral cavities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bedding - the materials used to make up a bed, including bed linen and blankets
