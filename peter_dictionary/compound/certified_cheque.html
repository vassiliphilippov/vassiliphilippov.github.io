certified cheque

1. NOUN

/ sərˈtɪfaɪd tʃɛk / (AmE), / səˈtɪfʌɪd tʃɛk / (BrE)
A cheque for which the bank guarantees payment by certifying that sufficient funds are available in the issuer's account.

Examples

Within an hour, he signed a release for a certified cheque of 1,000 dollars.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cashier's check - a check drawn by a bank on its own funds and signed by a bank cashier
