ball field

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɔl ˌfild / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl ˌfiːld / (BrE)
A field or stadium where the game of baseball is played.

Examples

Nearly level areas are required for parking areas, beaches, camp areas, ball fields, etcetera.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: baseball diamond - a baseball field with the characteristic diamond-shaped layout
  • Antonym: football field - field where football is played
