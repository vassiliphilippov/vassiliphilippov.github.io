done with

1. ADJ

/ dʌn wɪð / (AmE), / dʌn wɪð / (BrE)
Having finished, completed, or no longer concerned with (something or someone).

Examples

The dawn progressed and it seemed that the doctor would never be done with his ministrations when quite abruptly something broke his revery.

Synset Examples

he was through with school and he was through with family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: finished with - no longer involved or concerned with
  • Synonym: through with - no longer having any further concern with
