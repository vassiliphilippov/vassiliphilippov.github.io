life tenant

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈtɛnənt / (AmE), / laɪf ˈtɛnənt / (BrE)
A person whose right to occupy or use property continues during their lifetime or the lifetime of another specified person.

Examples

If you are a life tenant, you deduct allowable depreciation and depletion.

Etymology

Formed from 'life' (duration of living) and 'tenant' (one who holds property).
