never again

1. ADV

/ ˈnɛvɚ əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
At no time in the future; not on any future occasion.

Examples

It would summon him once; if he missed it, never again.

Synset Examples

Quoth the raven, nevermore!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: never - at no time; not ever

2. ADV

/ ˈnɛvɚ əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə əˈɡɛn / (BrE)
At no time in the future; not ever again from this point onward.

Examples

Never tell anybody outside the family what you're thinking **again**.

Synset Examples

Quoth the raven, nevermore!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: never - at no time; not ever
