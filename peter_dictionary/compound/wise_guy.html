wise guy

1. NOUN

/ ˈwaɪz ˌɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz ˌɡaɪ / (BrE)
an informal term for someone who makes sarcastic, insolent, or conceited remarks; a smart aleck

Examples

"Listen, wise guy, if you think I 'm gonna do all the fighting for this ball club you 're crazy ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: smart aleck - a person who behaves as if they know everything, often irritating others
