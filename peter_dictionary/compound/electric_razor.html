electric razor

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈreɪzər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈreɪzə / (BrE)
A razor powered by an electric motor, used for shaving hair from the face or body.

Examples

An electric toothbrush (Broxodent) may soon take its place next to the electric razor in the American bathroom.
The same can be said of shaving yet the electric razor has proved useful to many men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: safety razor - a razor with a protective device to prevent deep cuts
  • Synonym: straight razor - a razor with a long, bare blade that folds into its handle
