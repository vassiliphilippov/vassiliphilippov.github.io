best friend

1. NOUN

/ bɛst frɛnd / (AmE), / bɛst frɛnd / (BrE)
A person to whom one feels the closest friendship; one's closest or most trusted friend.

Examples

"Black is Gilborn's best friend ".

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: friend - a person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
  • Degree Lower: acquaintance - a person one knows only slightly, not a close friend
