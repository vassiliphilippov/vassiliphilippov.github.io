public press

1. NOUN

/ ˈpʌblɪk prɛs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk prɛs / (BrE)
newspapers and magazines collectively regarded as the print media that gather and publish news

Examples

Such a series of articles was certainly never printed in the public press of mid Victorian England.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press - print or broadcast news organizations collectively
  • Synonym: media - means of mass communication, especially news outlets
