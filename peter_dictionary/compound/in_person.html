in person

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
Present physically at a place, rather than being represented by someone else or communicating remotely.

Examples

Dr. Verdi had told him to deliver his package in person.
He had sold oil stock to Bob Hope and Bing Crosby in person; he had helped fight an oil-well fire that raged six days and nights.

Synset Examples

I went there personally
he appeared in person

Related Terms

  • Synonym: personally - in a way that involves being physically present; without intermediaries
