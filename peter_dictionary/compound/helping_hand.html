helping hand

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlpɪŋ hænd / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpɪŋ hænd / (BrE)
assistance or support given to someone, especially in practical tasks

Examples

Instead we have stood idly by, watched our commuter railroad service decline, and have failed to offer a helping hand.

Synset Examples

give me a hand with the chores

Related Terms

  • Synonym: aid - help or support
  • Synonym: assistance - the act of helping
  • Antonym: hindrance - something that makes it more difficult to do something
