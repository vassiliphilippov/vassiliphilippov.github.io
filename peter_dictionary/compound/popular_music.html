popular music

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑːpjələr ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
A genre of music that has widespread appeal, typically commercial in nature and enjoyed by a large audience, often for a limited period.

Examples

There are 12 of these to choose from, all of them of popular music except for the star release, Pass in Review (SP 44001).
No reference is made to the possibility of recording other than popular music in this manner, and it would not seem to lend itself well to serious music.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pop music - a genre of popular music with a strong rhythm and simple melodies
