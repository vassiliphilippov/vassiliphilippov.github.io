life sentence

1. NOUN

/ laɪf ˈsɛntəns / (AmE), / laɪf ˈsɛntəns / (BrE)
A punishment by which a person is sent to prison for the rest of their life.

Examples

Your files say you've served 40 years of a life sentence.

Synset Examples

he got life for killing the guard

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life imprisonment - imprisonment for the rest of a person's life
