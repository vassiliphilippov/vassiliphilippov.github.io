country club

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌntri klʌb / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri klʌb / (BrE)
a private club located in the suburbs, offering recreational and social activities such as golf, tennis, and dining to its members

Examples

These were the years when people flocked to Manchester not only to play golf, which had come into vogue, but also to witness the Ekwanok Country Club tournaments.
