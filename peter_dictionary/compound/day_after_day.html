day after day

1. ADV

/ ˈdeɪ ˌæftɚ ˈdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌɑːftə ˈdeɪ / (BrE)
happening repeatedly every day over a period of time; continually on successive days.

Examples

North and south, east and west, back and forth he sailed in the land-locked bay, plowing furiously forward until land appeared, then turning to repeat the process, day after day, week after week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day in, day out - continually, regularly over a period of time
