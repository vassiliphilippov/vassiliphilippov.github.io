basketball team

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæskətˌbɔl tim / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl tiːm / (BrE)
A group of players forming a team to compete in the sport of basketball.

Examples

During his college career, Dr. Clark was captain of his basketball team and was a football letterman.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: team - a group of people forming one side in a competitive game or sport
