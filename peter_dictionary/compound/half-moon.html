half-moon

1. NOUN

/ ˈhæf muːn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf muːn / (BrE)
the crescent-shaped whitish area at the base of a human fingernail, also known as a lunula

Examples

The son and heir of a prominent family marries a girl who has tell-tale shadows on the half-moons of her finger nails.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lunula - the crescent-shaped whitish area at the base of a human fingernail

Etymology

From 'half' and 'moon', describing the crescent shape resembling a partial moon.
