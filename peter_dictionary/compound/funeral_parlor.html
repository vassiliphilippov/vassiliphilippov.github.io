funeral parlor

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjuː.nɚl ˌpɑːɹ.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.nərəl ˌpɑː.lə / (BrE)
A business or establishment where the dead are prepared for burial or cremation, and where friends and relatives can pay their last respects before the funeral.

Examples

Mickie sat over his second whisky-on-the-rocks in a little bar next to the funeral parlor on Pennsylvania Avenue.
A funeral parlor chain was indicted for price-cutting.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mortuary - a place where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation
  • Synonym: funeral home - an establishment for arranging and conducting funerals
