black-haired

1. ADJ

/ ˈblæk hɛrd / (AmE), / ˈblæk heəd / (BrE)
Having black or very dark-colored hair.

Examples

The nude was small and black-haired and elfin, and was called "Eloise ".

Synset Examples

a dark-haired beauty

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dark-haired - having hair of a dark color
  • Antonym: blonde - having fair or light-colored hair
  • Degree Lower: brown-haired - having brown hair
