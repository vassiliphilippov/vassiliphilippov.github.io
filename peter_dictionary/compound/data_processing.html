data processing

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪtə ˌprɑsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪtə ˌprəʊsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
The handling and manipulation of data by a computer to retrieve, transform, or classify information.

Examples

We suggest the application of certain data-processing techniques as a solution to the problem.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: data entry - the process of inputting data into a computer or database
  • Degree Higher: data analysis - the process of examining data to draw conclusions

Etymology

Formed from 'data', from Latin 'datum' (something given), and 'processing', from Latin 'processus' (going forward).
