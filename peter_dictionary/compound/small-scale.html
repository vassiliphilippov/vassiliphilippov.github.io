small-scale

1. ADJ

/ smɔl skeɪl / (AmE), / smɔːl skeɪl / (BrE)
Produced, done, or occurring in a small size, scope, or level.

Examples

Cost of power and machinery is often a serious problem to the small-scale farmer.

Synset Examples

small-scale maps
a small-scale model

Related Terms

  • Antonym: large-scale - Produced, done, or occurring in a large size, scope, or level
