stage direction

1. NOUN

/ steɪdʒ daɪˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / steɪdʒ dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
an instruction in the script of a play indicating movement, position, or tone of an actor, or the sound effects and lighting

Examples

The remainder of the cast fulfilled its assignments no more than satisfactorily just as the old production and limited stage direction proved only serviceable.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: instruction - something that someone tells you to do; a directive
