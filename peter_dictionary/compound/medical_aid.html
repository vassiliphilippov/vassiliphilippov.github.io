medical aid

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl eɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl eɪd / (BrE)
help or treatment given by medical professionals to people who are ill or injured

Examples

The men inside informed him that they had some wounded men among them but he would not be allowed to see them even though he offered medical aid.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: medical care - professional treatment for illness or injury
