New Hampshire

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈhæmpʃɚ / (AmE), / njuː ˈhæmpʃə / (BrE)
A state in the northeastern United States, located in New England; one of the original thirteen colonies.

Examples

The state universities of Maine, New Hampshire, And Vermont are older and more "respectable "; they had less immigration to contend with.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Vermont - state in New England bordering New Hampshire
  • Synonym: Maine - state in New England near New Hampshire

Etymology

Named after the English county of Hampshire.

2. PROPN

/ nuː ˈhæmpʃɚ / (AmE), / njuː ˈhæmpʃə / (BrE)
A state in the northeastern United States, one of the six New England states and one of the original thirteen colonies.

Examples

Go back to New Hampshire, and I'll call you at your parents' house.

Etymology

Named after the county of Hampshire in England.
