body servant

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑdi ˌsɜrvənt / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌsɜːvənt / (BrE)
A personal attendant, typically a valet or maid, who performs household or personal tasks for an employer.

Examples

Early in the war it was not uncommon for planters' sons to retain in camp Negro "body servants "to perform the menial chores such as cooking, foraging, cleaning the quarters, shining shoes, and laundering clothes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: valet - a man's personal male attendant
  • Synonym: maid - female domestic servant
