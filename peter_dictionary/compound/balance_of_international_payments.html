balance of international payments

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæl.əns əv ˌɪn.təˈnæʃ.ə.nəl ˈpeɪ.mənts / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns əv ˌɪn.təˈnæʃ.ən.əl ˈpeɪ.mənts / (BrE)
The record of all economic transactions between a country and the rest of the world over a specific period, usually one year.

Examples

It develops and analyzes the national income, balance of international payments, and many other business indicators.

Synset Examples

a favorable balance of payments exists when more payments are coming in than going out

Related Terms

  • Synonym: balance of payments - record of all economic transactions between a country and the world
