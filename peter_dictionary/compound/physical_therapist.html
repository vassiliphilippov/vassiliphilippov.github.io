physical therapist

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈθɛrəpɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈθɛrəpɪst / (BrE)
A healthcare professional who treats injury or physical dysfunction by using exercises and other physical methods to promote mobility and reduce pain.

Examples

He may call himself a naprapath, a physiotherapist, an electrotherapist, a naturopath, a sanipractor, a medical cultist, a masseur, a "doctor "- or what have you.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: physiotherapist - healthcare professional specializing in physical therapy
