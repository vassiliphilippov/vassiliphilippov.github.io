tire tool

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaɪɚ ˌtuːl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪə ˌtuːl / (BrE)
A hand tool, typically a lever, used for removing or fitting the casing of a pneumatic tire onto a wheel.
