steel blue

1. NOUN

/ stil blu / (AmE), / stiːl bluː / (BrE)
a greyish-blue color resembling the color of steel

Examples

Wiry and burr headed, with steel blue eyes and a chest splattered with medals, Chandler is the epitome of the old-time trooper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slate blue - a medium-dark blue-grey color
  • Degree Lower: sky blue - a light bluish color like that of a clear sky
  • Degree Higher: navy blue - a dark shade of blue

Etymology

From 'steel', referring to the metal, and 'blue', describing the color.
