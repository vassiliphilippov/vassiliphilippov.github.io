sea bottom

1. NOUN

/ si ˈbɑtəm / (AmE), / siː ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
the lowest surface of the sea or ocean; the ground beneath the water

Examples

A tsunami may be started by a sea bottom slide, an earthquake or a volcanic eruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ocean floor - the bottom surface of the ocean
