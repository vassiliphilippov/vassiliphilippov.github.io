line coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈlaɪn ˌkoʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn ˌkəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an assistant football coach responsible for training and managing the linemen on a team

Examples

Nick Skorich, the line coach for the football champion Philadelphia Eagles, was elevated today to head coach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: offensive line coach - coach responsible specifically for the offensive linemen
  • Synonym: defensive line coach - coach responsible specifically for the defensive linemen
