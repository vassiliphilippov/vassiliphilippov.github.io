mite box

1. NOUN

/ maɪt bɑks / (AmE), / maɪt bɒks / (BrE)
A small box placed in a church for collecting money offerings, especially for charity.

Examples

No one told on Ludie, not even when he slipped live grasshoppers into the mite-box.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poor box - box in a church for collecting money for the poor

Etymology

From 'mite' meaning a small coin or offering, referencing the biblical story of the widow's mite.
