outdoor man

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtˌdɔɹ mæn / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌdɔː mæn / (BrE)
A man who enjoys and participates in outdoor activities, such as hiking, camping, or hunting.

Examples

He was dressed in a manner Esquire might suggest for the outdoor man's country weekend.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sportsman - a man who engages in sports or outdoor activities
