plastic surgeon

1. NOUN

/ ˈplæstɪk ˈsɜrdʒən / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk ˈsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
A medical doctor who specializes in performing surgery to improve a person's appearance, particularly of the face and body.

Examples

But, as my plastic surgeon always said, "If you've got to go... "...go with a smile."

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cosmetic surgeon - a surgeon who performs operations to improve appearance

Etymology

From 'plastic' (reshaping) + 'surgeon'; 'plastic' here derives from Greek 'plastikos' (fit for molding).
