fur-piece

1. NOUN

/ fɝː piːs / (AmE), / fɜː piːs / (BrE)
A neckpiece or ornamental accessory made from animal fur, worn around the shoulders for warmth or decoration.

Examples

With a tiny fur-piece wrapped around her shoulders, she wiggled her satin covered buttocks down the street before him and did n't stop.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stole - a woman's long, narrow scarf or shawl, often made of fur or similar material
