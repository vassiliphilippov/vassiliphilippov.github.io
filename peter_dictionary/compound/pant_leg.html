pant leg

1. NOUN

/ ˈpænt lɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈpænt lɛɡ / (BrE)
The section of a pair of pants or trousers that covers one leg.

Examples

There was no cover here, only grass sighing against pant-legs.
Handing the money over, Russ wiped his hands on his pants-legs as if ridding himself of something unclean.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trouser leg - the leg of a pair of trousers (British equivalent)
