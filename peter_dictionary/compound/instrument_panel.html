instrument panel

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪnstrəmənt ˈpænəl / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrɪmənt ˈpænl̩ / (BrE)
A flat surface in a vehicle or machine that displays dials, switches, and meters for controlling and monitoring its operation.

Examples

Greg's eyes flicked up from his instrument panel.

Synset Examples

he checked the instrument panel
suddenly the board lit up like a Christmas tree

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dashboard - control panel in a vehicle, often synonym for instrument panel
