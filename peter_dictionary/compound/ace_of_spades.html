ace of spades

1. NOUN

/ ˌeɪs əv ˈspeɪdz / (AmE), / ˌeɪs əv ˈspeɪdz / (BrE)
The playing card with a single spade symbol and the highest or lowest value in the suit of spades; sometimes regarded as a symbol of death or bad luck.

Examples

An ace of spades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ace of hearts - the ace in the hearts suit
  • Synonym: ace - a playing card with a single symbol, typically signifying the highest value in its suit
