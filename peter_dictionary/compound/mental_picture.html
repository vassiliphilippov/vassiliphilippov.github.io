mental picture

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.t̬əl ˈpɪk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmen.təl ˈpɪk.tʃə / (BrE)
An image or impression created in the mind, often as a result of imagination, memory, or visualization.

Examples

His mind flicked through the mental pictures he had from the hours of Aircraft Identification.

Synset Examples

he described his mental picture of his assailant
he had no clear picture of himself or his world
the events left a permanent impression in his mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mental image - a mental representation of something not present to the senses
  • Synonym: impression - an effect or image retained in the mind
