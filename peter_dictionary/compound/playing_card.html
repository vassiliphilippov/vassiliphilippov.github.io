playing card

1. NOUN

/ ˈpleɪɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpleɪɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
a small, flat, rectangular piece of stiff paper or thin plastic, with distinctive markings, used as one of a set in card games

Examples

Playing cards with naked ladies on them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: card - a piece of stiff paper or thin cardboard; used for playing games
