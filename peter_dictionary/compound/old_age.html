old age

1. NOUN

/ oʊld eɪdʒ / (AmE), / əʊld eɪdʒ / (BrE)
The period in a person's life when they are old, typically considered to begin around the later years following middle age.

Examples

He is confronted with the recurrent crises, such as great natural catastrophes and the great transitions of life - marriage, incurable disease, widowhood, old age, the certainty of death.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Antonym: youth - the earlier period in life, especially before adulthood
