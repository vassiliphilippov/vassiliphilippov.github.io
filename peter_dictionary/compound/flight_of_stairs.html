flight of stairs

1. NOUN

/ flaɪt əv stɛrz / (AmE), / flaɪt əv stɛəz / (BrE)
A set of steps between two floors or landings in a building.

Examples

He was prancing along the hall, heading for the next flight of stairs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staircase - a set of stairs and its surrounding structure
  • Synonym: stairway - a way upward consisting of a set of steps
