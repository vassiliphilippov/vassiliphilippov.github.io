second cousin

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛk.ənd ˈkʌz.ən / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.ənd ˈkʌz.ən / (BrE)
A child of one's parent's first cousin; a relative who shares a set of great-grandparents with you but not grandparents.

Examples

"Oh he's a second cousin or something.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: first cousin - child of an uncle or aunt; shares grandparents with you
  • Degree Higher: third cousin - child of a parent's second cousin; shares great-great-grandparents

2. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌzən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈkʌzən / (BrE)
A child of your parent's first cousin; a cousin with whom you share great-grandparents but not grandparents.

Examples

Second cousin to Harvey the Rabbit.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: first cousin - parent's sibling's child
  • Degree Higher: third cousin - child of your parent's second cousin
