Yellow Pages

1. NOUN

/ ˈjɛloʊ ˌpeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌpeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
a telephone directory, typically printed on yellow paper, listing businesses and services by category, often including advertisements

Examples

But outside, all you need is the Yellow Pages.

Etymology

Refers to directories printed on yellow paper; 'yellow' from the color, 'pages' from the format.
