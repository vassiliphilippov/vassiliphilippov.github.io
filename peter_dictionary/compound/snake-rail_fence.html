snake-rail fence

1. NOUN

/ ˈsneɪk reɪl fɛns / (AmE), / ˈsneɪk reɪl fɛns / (BrE)
A type of fence made from rails laid in a zigzag pattern, with the rails interlocking at the angles.

Examples

Husbandry was bounded by snake-rail fences, and there were grazing cattle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: worm fence - a fence made of zigzagged rails; another name for snake-rail fence

Etymology

So called due to its serpentine, zigzag pattern resembling a snake's movement.
