zinc cadmium sulfide

1. NOUN

/ zɪŋk kædˈmiəm ˈsʌlfaɪd / (AmE), / zɪŋk kadˈmiːəm ˈsʌlfaɪd / (BrE)
A chemical compound that is cadmium sulfide containing zinc, used mainly in scientific applications.

Examples

Zinc cadmium sulfide in particles of 2 microns in size were disseminated.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cadmium sulfide - A yellow compound of cadmium and sulfur used as a pigment

Etymology

From the names of the elements zinc, cadmium, and sulfide, reflecting its chemical composition.
