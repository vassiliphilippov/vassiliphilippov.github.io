field glasses

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld ˌɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
a pair of small binoculars used for viewing distant objects, especially outdoors

Examples

The mere fact that the tall figure with the rifle and field glasses had been seen riding that way was enough to frighten three rustling homesteaders out of the Upper Laramie country in a single week.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: binoculars - an optical instrument with two lenses for seeing distant objects using both eyes
