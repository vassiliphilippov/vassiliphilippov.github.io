matter-of-course

1. ADJ

/ ˌmætər əv ˈkɔrs / (AmE), / ˌmætə əv ˈkɔːs / (BrE)
Happening or done as a natural or expected result; not surprising or unusual.

Examples

Of course, the dog stays.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inevitable - certain to happen; unavoidable
  • Synonym: expected - regarded as likely to happen; anticipated
  • Antonym: surprising - causing surprise; unexpected

Etymology

Formed from the phrase 'as a matter of course', meaning 'as a usual or expected result'.
