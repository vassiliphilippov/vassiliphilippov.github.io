health problem

1. NOUN

/ hɛlθ ˈprɑbləm / (AmE), / hɛlθ ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
a condition or illness that affects a person's health and may prevent normal functioning or cause discomfort or pain

Examples

Do you rigidly distinguish between job - and non job connected health problems and avoid treating the latter?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: illness - state of being physically or mentally unwell
  • Synonym: disease - a disorder of structure or function in a human, animal, or plant
