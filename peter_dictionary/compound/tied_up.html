tied up

1. ADJ

/ taɪd ʌp / (AmE), / taɪd ʌp / (BrE)
Busy or occupied and therefore unable to attend to other matters at the moment.

Examples

The only trouble was that he himself was tied up on the school job.

Synset Examples

she's tied up at the moment and can't see you
the phone was tied up for almost an hour

Related Terms

  • Synonym: busy - actively engaged in work or activity
  • Antonym: available - free to do something; not occupied
