bag of tricks

1. NOUN

/ bæɡ əv trɪks / (AmE), / bæɡ əv trɪks / (BrE)
a set or supply of clever methods, techniques, or resources used to achieve a goal or solve problems

Examples

This used to be part of every child's bag of tricks, but few children can do it today; some are actually incapable of rolling forward and are completely confused when not sitting or standing upright.

Synset Examples

every good mechanic has a large bag of tricks

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arsenal - a collection of resources or tools for a particular purpose
  • Synonym: toolkit - a set of tools or resources for solving problems
