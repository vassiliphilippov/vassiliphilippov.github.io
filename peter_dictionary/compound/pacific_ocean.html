Pacific Ocean

1. PROPN

/ pəˈsɪfɪk ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
the largest and deepest ocean in the world, lying between Asia and Australia on the west and the Americas on the east

Examples

The Pacific Ocean?

Etymology

Named from Latin 'pacificus' (peaceful), given by explorer Ferdinand Magellan.
