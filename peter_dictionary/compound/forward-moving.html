forward-moving

1. ADJ

/ ˈfɔrwərd ˌmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwəd ˌmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
progressing or making advancement; moving ahead or developing

Examples

As a nation, we feel an obligation to assist other countries in their development; but this obligation pertains only to countries which are honestly seeking to become responsible members of a stable and forward-moving world community.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: advancing - moving forward or making progress
  • Synonym: progressive - favoring or implementing social reform or new, forward-thinking ideas
  • Antonym: retreating - withdrawing or moving back
