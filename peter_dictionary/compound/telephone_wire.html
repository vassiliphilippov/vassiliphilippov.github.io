telephone wire

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn waɪr / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn waɪə / (BrE)
A wire designed to carry telegraph or telephone signals.

Examples

Telephone wires from Louis Dufresne's house in East Manchester to the Dufresne lumber job near Bourn Pond were up about 1895.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telegraph wire - wire used to carry telegraph signals
