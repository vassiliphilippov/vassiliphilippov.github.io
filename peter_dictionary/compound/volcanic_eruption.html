volcanic eruption

1. NOUN

/ voʊlˈkænɪk ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / vɒlˈkænɪk ɪˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
a sudden, violent release of steam, ash, lava, and other materials from a volcano

Examples

A tsunami may be started by a sea bottom slide, an earthquake or a volcanic eruption.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: eruption - a sudden outbreak or explosion, especially from within the earth
