Puerto Rico

1. PROPN

/ ˌpwɛərtoʊ ˈriːkoʊ / (AmE), / ˌpwɛːtəʊ ˈriːkəʊ / (BrE)
A self-governing territory of the United States comprising an island in the Caribbean Sea; its official name is the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
