corner post

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɔrnɚ ˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnə ˌpəʊst / (BrE)
A vertical, usually square, post located at the corner of a building or structure, providing support to the structure at that point.

Examples

Square corner - and gate posts were an open-work pattern of cast-iron foliage; they were topped by steeples complete in every detail: high pitched roof, pinnacle, and narrow gable.
