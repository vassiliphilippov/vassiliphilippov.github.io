rock python

1. NOUN

/ rɑk ˈpaɪθɑn / (AmE), / rɒk ˈpaɪθən / (BrE)
a very large species of python found in tropical and southern Africa, known for its considerable size and strength

Examples

The African rock python, a close second, is followed in turn by the reticulate python.
