tea party

1. NOUN

/ ti ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / tiː ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
A social gathering where tea and light refreshments are served.

Examples

On the Christophers' lawn, little girls in white pinafores were playing grownups at a tea party.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests
