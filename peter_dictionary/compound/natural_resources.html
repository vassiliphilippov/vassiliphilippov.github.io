natural resources

1. NOUN

/ ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl rɪˈsɔrsɪz / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl rɪˈzɔːsɪz / (BrE)
Materials or substances such as minerals, forests, water, and fertile land that occur in nature and can be used for economic gain or human benefit.
