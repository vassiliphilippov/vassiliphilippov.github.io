medical care

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl kɛr / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl keə / (BrE)
Professional treatment given to people who are ill or injured.

Examples

By preying on the sick, by playing callously on the hopes of the desperate, by causing the sufferer to delay proper medical care, these medical ghouls create pain and misery by their very activity.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: health care - the organized provision of medical services to individuals or a community
