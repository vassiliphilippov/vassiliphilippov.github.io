black and white

1. ADJ

/ ˌblæk ən ˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌblæk ən ˈwaɪt / (BrE)
(of images, television, film, etc.) consisting of shades of black, white, and grey, without any color.

Examples

Specific staining by DEAE-cellulose treated * * f and * * f, although clearly distinguishable under the microscope from either nonspecific staining or autofluorescence of cells, was not satisfactorily photographed to show such differences in spite of many attempts with black and white and color photography.

Synset Examples

black-and-white film

Related Terms

  • Synonym: monochrome - an image or display in varying tones of only one color, especially black and white
  • Antonym: color - having or producing color
