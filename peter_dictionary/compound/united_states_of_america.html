United States of America

1. PROPN

/ juˈnaɪtɪd ˈsteɪts əv əˈmɛrɪkə / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈsteɪts əv əˈmerɪkə / (BrE)
A country in North America consisting of 50 states, including Alaska and Hawaii; commonly referred to as the United States or America; established in 1776 after declaring independence from Great Britain.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: USA - abbreviation for United States of America
  • Synonym: America - informal or short form for United States of America
  • Synonym: United States - common colloquial name for the United States of America

Etymology

Named for the union of states formed after the Declaration of Independence in 1776; 'America' from the Latinized name of explorer Amerigo Vespucci.
