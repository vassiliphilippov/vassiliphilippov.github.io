magazine article

1. NOUN

/ məˈɡæzɪn ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / məˈɡæziːn ˈɑːtɪkəl / (BrE)
An article that is published in a magazine, typically covering a particular topic or issue.

Examples

Marriage manuals and women's magazine articles began to stress the importance of the female climax.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newspaper article - article published in a newspaper
