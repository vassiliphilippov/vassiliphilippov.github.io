banana peel

1. NOUN

/ bəˈnænə piːl / (AmE), / bəˈnænə piːl / (BrE)
the outer skin of a banana, especially after it has been removed and discarded

Examples

These two slipped on a banana peel.

Synset Examples

he slipped on a banana skin and almost fell

Related Terms

  • Synonym: banana skin - the skin of a banana (British English)
