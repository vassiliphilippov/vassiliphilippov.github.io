mentally ill

1. ADJ

/ ˈmɛn.təl.i ɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təli ɪl / (BrE)
Suffering from a mental disorder; having a serious mental illness.

Examples

The writer took a class of college students to the state hospital for the mentally ill in St. Joseph, Missouri.

Synset Examples

of unsound mind

Related Terms

  • Synonym: insane - suffering from severe mental illness; not of sound mind
  • Antonym: sane - having a healthy mind; not mentally ill
