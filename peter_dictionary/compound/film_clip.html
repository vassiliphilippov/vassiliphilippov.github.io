film clip

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪlm klɪp / (AmE), / ˈfɪlm klɪp / (BrE)
A short segment or extract from a motion picture, typically used in television broadcasts or presentations.

Examples

Another source of NBC pride was its rare film clip of Bix Beiderbecke, but this view of the great trumpeter flew by so fast that a prolonged wink would have blotted out the entire glimpse.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clip - a short extract from a longer piece of film or video
