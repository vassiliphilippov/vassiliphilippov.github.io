good example

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ɪɡˈzæmpəl / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɪɡˈzɑːmpəl / (BrE)
A person or thing regarded as worthy of imitation; an exemplar or model.

Examples

A good example of the results obtainable with ultrasonic radiation is contained in papers presented by Dr. G. Baum who has explored the human eye.

Synset Examples

an exemplar of success
a model of clarity
he is the very model of a modern major general

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exemplar - a person or thing serving as a typical example or model
  • Synonym: model - something or someone to be imitated
