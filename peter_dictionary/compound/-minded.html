-minded

1. ADJ

/ ˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
(in combination) having the specified attitude, outlook, or interest; oriented mentally toward something specified.

Examples

Her woolly minded parent agreed.

Synset Examples

civic-minded
career-minded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: -hearted - (in combination) having the specified kind of heart or temperament

Etymology

From 'mind' + '-ed'. Used as a combining form to indicate orientation of thought or attitude.
