work table

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːk ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk ˌteɪbəl / (BrE)
a table designed or used for a specific task or type of work

Examples

Here and there on work table or pedestal stood a shape with a sheet or a tarpaulin draped over it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: workbench - a sturdy table at which manual work is done
