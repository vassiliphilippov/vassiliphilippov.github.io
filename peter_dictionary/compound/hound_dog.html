hound dog

1. NOUN

/ haʊnd dɔg / (AmE), / haʊnd dɒg / (BrE)
A dog of a breed used for hunting, usually with large, drooping ears, keen scenting abilities, and a loud voice.

Examples

Frozen, they stared at it whirling down the valley, gouging and spitting out boulders and chunks of earth like a starving hound dog cracking marrowbones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hound - dog bred for hunting by scent or sight
