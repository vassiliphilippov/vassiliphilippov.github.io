training ship

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship used to train students to become sailors, often by providing practical experience at sea

Examples

This was a training ship and the training would continue, but there was an element of frightful absurdity here which Alexander recognized.
