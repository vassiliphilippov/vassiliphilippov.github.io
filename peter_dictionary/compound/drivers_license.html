driver's license

1. NOUN

/ ˈdraɪvɚz ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈdraɪvəz ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
An official document permitting a person to drive a motor vehicle on public roads.

Examples

He had all the proper ID, driver's license, birth certificate, social security card.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: driving licence - British English term for a driver's license
