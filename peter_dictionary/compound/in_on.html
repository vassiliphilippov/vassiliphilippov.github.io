in on

1. ADV

/ ɪn ɑn / (AmE), / ɪn ɒn / (BrE)
Involved in or having knowledge of something, especially a secret or plan.

Examples

And Roth were in on a big deal together.

Synset Examples

was in on the scheme

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of the loop - not informed or not participating in a secret or plan
