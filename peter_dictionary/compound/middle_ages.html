Middle Ages

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪdəl ˌeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪdəl ˌeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
The period of European history between classical antiquity and the Renaissance, roughly from the 5th to the late 15th century.
