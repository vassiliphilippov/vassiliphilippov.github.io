sexual arousal

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈraʊzəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃʊəl əˈraʊzəl / (BrE)
a physiological and psychological state of excitement and desire in anticipation of sexual activity

Examples

According to Dr. Ellis, the average 20 - year old American woman is capable of far greater sexual arousal than her partner.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: libido - sexual desire or drive
  • Synonym: desire - a strong feeling of wanting to have something or wishing for something to happen
