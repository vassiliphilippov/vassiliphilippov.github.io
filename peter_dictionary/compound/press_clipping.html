press clipping

1. NOUN

/ prɛs ˈklɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / prɛs ˈklɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
An article or extract cut out from a newspaper or magazine, usually for reference or record.

Examples

This meant sorting out press clippings and the like.

Synset Examples

he searched through piles of letters and clippings

Related Terms

  • Synonym: newspaper clipping - an extract cut from a newspaper
