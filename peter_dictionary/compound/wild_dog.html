wild dog

1. NOUN

/ waɪld dɔɡ / (AmE), / waɪld dɒɡ / (BrE)
Any of various undomesticated mammals of the family Canidae that resemble domestic dogs, but are distinct from jackals or wolves.

Examples

Ice baths, electric shocks, lashings, wild dogs, testicle crushers.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: domestic dog - a dog that has been tamed and kept by humans as a pet or for work
  • Antonym: wolf - large wild canine mammal that is distinct from dogs and jackals
  • Antonym: jackal - medium-sized wild canine native to Africa and Eurasia, distinct from wild dogs
