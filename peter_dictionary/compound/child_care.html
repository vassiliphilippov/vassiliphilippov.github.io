child care

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃaɪld ˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪld ˌkeə / (BrE)
The care and supervision of children, especially by someone other than the child's parents or guardians, as a paid service.

Examples

In child care, the opposite extreme prevails; procedures change rapidly and parental confidence probably exceeds anything warranted by established psychological theory.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: daycare - care of children during the day, often at a center
