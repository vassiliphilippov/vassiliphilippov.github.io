sewage system

1. NOUN

/ ˈsuˌɪdʒ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsjuːɪdʒ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A network of pipes and other structures for the collection and disposal of wastewater and sewage from buildings and streets.

The sewage system from Kansas City was not expected to serve the Red Bridge area for several years.
