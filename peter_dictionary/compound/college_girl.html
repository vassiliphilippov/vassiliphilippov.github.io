college girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑlɪdʒ ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪdʒ ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a female student at a college or university, especially a coeducational institution

Examples

She is even prone to regard the college girl as immature.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: college student - a person enrolled as a student at a college or university
  • Antonym: college boy - a male student at a college or university
