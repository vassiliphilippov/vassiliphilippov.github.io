legal age

1. NOUN

/ ˈliɡəl eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl eɪdʒ / (BrE)
The age at which a person is legally recognized as an adult and considered competent to manage their own affairs.

Examples

Mike was of legal age and presumed able to defend himself in the clinches.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: age of majority - the age when a person is considered an adult by law
