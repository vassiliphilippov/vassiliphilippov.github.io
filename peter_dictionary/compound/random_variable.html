random variable

1. NOUN

/ ˈrændəm ˈveriəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈrændəm ˈvɛːrɪəbl̩ / (BrE)
A quantity whose values are determined by the outcomes of a random phenomenon, typically used in probability and statistics.
