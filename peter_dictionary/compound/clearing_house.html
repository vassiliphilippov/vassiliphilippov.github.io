clearing house

1. NOUN

/ ˈklɪrɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈklɪərɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
an institution or organization that acts as an intermediary for the exchange of payments, securities, or information, especially between banks or financial institutions

Examples

SBA serves also as a clearing house for information on products and processes particularly adaptable for exploitation by small firms.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: exchange - an organization where trading of financial instruments takes place
