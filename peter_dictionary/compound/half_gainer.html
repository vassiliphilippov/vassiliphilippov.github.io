half gainer

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈɡeɪnər / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈɡeɪnə / (BrE)
A dive in which the diver moves their feet forward and upward to complete a half backward somersault, entering the water facing the diving board.

Examples

Just like shooting at a duck while performing a half-gainer from a diving board.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gainer - a dive in which the diver faces forward while rotating backward
