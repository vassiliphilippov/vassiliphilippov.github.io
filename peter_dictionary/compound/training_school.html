training school

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
A school that provides practical vocational and technical training, preparing students for specific trades or careers.

Examples

Some vocational training schools provide such training, but the current need exceeds the facilities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vocational school - school specifically focused on teaching skills for particular trades
