technical school

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛknɪkəl skuːl / (AmE), / ˈtɛknɪkəl skuːl / (BrE)
a school that offers instruction in technical, industrial, or applied sciences, preparing students for specific trades or professions

Examples

There are a great number and variety of private commercial schools, trade schools and technical schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trade school - school teaching skills for a specific trade or occupation
