matching funds

1. NOUN

/ ˈmætʃɪŋ fʌndz / (AmE), / ˈmatʃɪŋ fʌndz / (BrE)
Funds provided in an amount equal to those contributed by another source, especially as a condition for receiving a grant or aid.

Examples

The following table shows, for selected years, the authorizations, appropriations, allotment base, Federal grants to States and State matching funds for this part of the grant program.
