year-end

1. NOUN

/ jɪr ˈɛnd / (AmE), / jɪə ˈɛnd / (BrE)
The end of a calendar or fiscal year.

Examples

- This budget will provide in the fiscal year 1961 for the continued support of our forces at approximately the present level - a year-end strength of 2489000 men and women in the active forces.

Synset Examples

he had to unload the merchandise before the year-end

Related Terms

  • Synonym: year - a period of 12 months, especially from January 1 to December 31
