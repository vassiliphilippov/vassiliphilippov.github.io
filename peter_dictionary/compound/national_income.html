national income

1. NOUN

/ ˈnæʃənəl ˈɪnˌkʌm / (AmE), / ˈnaʃənəl ˈɪnˌkʌm / (BrE)
the total value of all income earned within a nation, including wages, profits, interest, rents, and pension payments, usually measured annually

Examples

It develops and analyzes the national income, balance of international payments, and many other business indicators.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gross domestic product - the total value of all goods and services produced within a country in a year
