outboard motor

1. NOUN

/ ˈaʊtbɔrd ˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈaʊtbɔːd ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
a detachable internal-combustion engine mounted on the stern of a small boat, used for propulsion

Examples

Crossing the 4000 - foot width of the Mekong at Champassak, on a raft with an outboard motor, we took off our dusty shirts and enjoyed a veritable ocean breeze.
