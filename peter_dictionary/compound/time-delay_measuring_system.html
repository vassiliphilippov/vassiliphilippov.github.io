time-delay measuring system

1. NOUN

/ taɪm dɪˈleɪ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / taɪm dɪˈleɪ ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system or instrument used to measure the time interval between two events, often in scientific or technical applications

Examples

A sonar or radar type of pulse generator and time-delay measuring system is required for body tissue evaluation.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chronoscope - an instrument for measuring very short time intervals
