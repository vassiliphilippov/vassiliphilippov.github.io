figure of speech

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡjɚ əv spiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə əv spiːtʃ / (BrE)
An expression in which words are used imaginatively or non-literally, often to achieve a particular effect or emphasis (such as a metaphor, simile, or hyperbole).

Examples

Some of their figures of speech were colorful and expressive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trope - a figurative or metaphorical use of a word or expression
