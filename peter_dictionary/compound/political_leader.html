political leader

1. NOUN

/ pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈliːdər / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈliːdə / (BrE)
A person who holds a prominent position in government or political organizations and influences policy and decision-making.

Examples

Another great danger is that the emerging middle class will feel itself increasingly alienated from the political leaders who still justify their dominance by reference to the struggle for independence or the early phase of nationalism.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: politician - a person professionally involved in politics, especially as a holder of or a candidate for an elected office
