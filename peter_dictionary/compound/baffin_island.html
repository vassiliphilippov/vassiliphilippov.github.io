Baffin Island

1. PROPN

/ ˈbæfɪn ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈbæfɪn ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
the largest island of Arctic Canada and the fifth largest island in the world, located between Greenland and Hudson Bay

Examples

He named this opening, between Baffin Island and Labrador, the "Furious Overfall ".

Etymology

Named after English explorer William Baffin (c. 1584–1622).
