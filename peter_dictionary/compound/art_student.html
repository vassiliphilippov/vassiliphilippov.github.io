art student

1. NOUN

/ ɑrt ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
a student who studies art, especially as a main subject at school, college, or university

Examples

The other patrons were taxi drivers and art students and small shopkeepers.

Related Terms
