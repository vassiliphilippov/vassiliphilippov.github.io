Mon-Khmer

1. NOUN

/ mɑn kəˈmɛr / (AmE), / mɒn kəˈmɛː / (BrE)
A branch of the Austro-Asiatic language family that includes languages spoken mainly in Southeast Asia, such as Khmer and Mon.
