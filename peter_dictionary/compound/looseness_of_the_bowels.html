looseness of the bowels

1. NOUN

/ ˈluːsənəs əv ðə ˈbaʊəlz / (AmE), / ˈluːsənəs əv ðə ˈbaʊəlz / (BrE)
A condition marked by frequent and watery bowel movements; another term for diarrhea.

Examples

Men of more delicate sensibilities referred to this condition as "looseness of the bowels "; but a much more common designation was "the sh-ts ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: diarrhea - frequent and watery bowel movements
