solar radiation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊlər ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
Electromagnetic radiation emitted by the Sun, including visible light, ultraviolet, and infrared rays, that reaches the Earth or other bodies in space.
