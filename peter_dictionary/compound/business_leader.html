business leader

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪznəs ˌliːdər / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ˌliːdə / (BrE)
A person with significant influence, power, or authority in the business world, often leading a company or organization.

Examples

Programs usually are expanded only when outside funds are available or local business leaders demand it.

Synset Examples

an oil baron

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tycoon - a wealthy and powerful businessperson
  • Synonym: baron - an extremely wealthy or influential businessman
