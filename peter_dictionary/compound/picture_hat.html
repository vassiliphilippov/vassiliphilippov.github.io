picture hat

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃər hæt / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə hæt / (BrE)
A woman's stylish hat with a wide brim, often decorated, popular in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.

Examples

The costumes are stunning evocations of the voluminous gowns and picture hats of the Gibson Girl days.

Etymology

Named for its frequent appearance in portraits and fashion illustrations of the era.
