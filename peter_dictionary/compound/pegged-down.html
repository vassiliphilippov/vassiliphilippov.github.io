pegged-down

1. ADJ

/ pɛɡd daʊn / (AmE), / pɛɡd daʊn / (BrE)
secured or fastened to the ground with pegs, especially to encourage rooting or stability

Examples

Pick the flowers, keep the soil dampened, and each of the pegged-down branches will take root and become a little plant and go on blooming for the rest of the season.

Synset Examples

the pegged-down branches of the plant will take root

Related Terms

  • Synonym: staked - secured with stakes, especially for support
