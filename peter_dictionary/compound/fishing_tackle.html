fishing tackle

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌtækəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌtækəl / (BrE)
the equipment used for catching fish, such as rods, lines, hooks, and other related gear

Examples

Moving around camp we saw all kinds of camp stoves, lanterns, coolers, bedding, games, fishing tackle, windbreaks and sunshades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fishing gear - equipment used for fishing
