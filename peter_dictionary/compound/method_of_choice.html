method of choice

1. NOUN

/ ˌmɛθ.əd əv ˈtʃɔɪs / (AmE), / ˌmɛθ.əd əv ˈtʃɔɪs / (BrE)
the preferred or optimal method for achieving a particular result, especially in technical or professional contexts

Examples

Since a Skeletal Age rating can be made at any age during growth, from Elbow, Shoulder, Knee, or Foot as well as Hand, it seems to be the method of choice when one wishes to study most aspects of skeletal developmental progress during childhood.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: preferred method - the method that is most favored for a given task
