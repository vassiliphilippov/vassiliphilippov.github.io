horizontal bar

1. NOUN

/ hɔɹɪˈzɑntəl bɑɹ / (AmE), / hɒrɪˈzɒntəl bɑː / (BrE)
A gymnastic apparatus consisting of a bar supported horizontally by uprights at both ends, used especially for athletic exercises and competitions.

Examples

The parallel bars, horse, buck, springboard, horizontal bar, rings, and mats formerly in the school gyms were replaced by baseball, volleyball, basketball and football.
