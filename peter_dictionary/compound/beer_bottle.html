beer bottle

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɪr ˌbɑtəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪə ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a glass bottle designed for holding beer, typically sealed and sold commercially

Examples

Suddenly the Spanish became an English in which only one word emerged with clarity and precision, "son of a bitch ", sometimes hyphenated by vicious jabs of a beer bottle into Johnson's quivering ribs.
I started looking on the splintery truck bed for a piece of board, a dirt clod - anything I could throw and with better aim than I had thrown the beer bottle.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bottle - a container with a narrow neck, used for storing liquids
