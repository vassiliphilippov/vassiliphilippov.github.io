lemon tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɛm.ən tri / (AmE), / ˈlɛm.ən triː / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree, native to Asia, cultivated worldwide for its yellow, acidic fruit (the lemon).

Examples

The china lemon tree.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lemon - the yellow, acidic fruit produced by the lemon tree
