in toto

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈtoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈtəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
Completely; in its entirety; as a whole, without omission or exception.

Examples

We are forced, in our behavior towards others, to adopt empirically successful patterns in toto because we have such a minimal understanding of their essential elements.

Synset Examples

they bought the business in toto
in recommendations were adopted in toto

Related Terms

  • Synonym: entirely - completely; in every respect
  • Antonym: partially - in part; not completely

Etymology

From Latin *in toto* (in the whole, entirely).
