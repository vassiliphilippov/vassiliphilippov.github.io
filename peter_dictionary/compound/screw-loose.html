screw-loose

1. ADJ

/ skruː luːs / (AmE), / skruː luːs / (BrE)
(of a person) behaving abnormally or considered to be crazy or mentally unstable.

Examples

"The world that was once foot-loose and fancy-free ", he said, "has now become screw-loose and frenzy free.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: crazy - mentally deranged; insane
  • Antonym: normal - conforming to standard; usual

Etymology

From the idea that a loose screw in a mechanism makes it work improperly; figuratively applied to mental stability since the early 20th century.
