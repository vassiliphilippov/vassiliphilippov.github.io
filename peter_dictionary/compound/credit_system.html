credit system

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɛdɪt ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a method or arrangement that allows people or organizations to buy goods or services and pay for them at a later time

Examples

The Farm Credit Administration, an independent agency located within the Department of Agriculture, supervises and coordinates a cooperative credit system for agriculture.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: installment plan - a system of paying for goods in parts at intervals
