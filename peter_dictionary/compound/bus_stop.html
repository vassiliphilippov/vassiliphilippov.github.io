bus stop

1. NOUN

/ bʌs stɑp / (AmE), / bʌs stɒp / (BrE)
A designated place on a bus route where buses stop to pick up or drop off passengers.

Examples

Will your local bus company erect and / or maintain the bus stops at your plant?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus station - large facility where buses start or finish journeys
