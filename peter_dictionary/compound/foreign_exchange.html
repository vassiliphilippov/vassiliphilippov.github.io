foreign exchange

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɔrən ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
the system or process by which the currency of one country is exchanged for that of another, facilitating international trade and finance
