ill health

1. NOUN

/ ɪl hɛlθ / (AmE), / ɪl hɛlθ / (BrE)
a state of being unwell or not in good health, often involving inability to function normally or experiencing pain

Examples

Sadie, like Beth March, suffered ill health - got rheumatic fever and had to be careful of her heart - but that never dampened her spirits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: poor health - a state of reduced health or fitness
  • Antonym: good health - a condition of being well and free from illness
