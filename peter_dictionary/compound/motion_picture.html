motion picture

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊʃən ˌpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən ˌpɪktʃə / (BrE)
A series of moving images, usually with sound, that tell a story or provide entertainment; a film or movie.
