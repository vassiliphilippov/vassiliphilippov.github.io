criminal possession

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪnəl pəˈzɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl pəˈzɛʃən / (BrE)
The legal possession of property or substances for which criminal penalties are prescribed because the property is unlawful to possess or possessed under prohibited circumstances.

Examples

They already had him on possession, bookmaking, murder one and a lot more.
