little sister

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɪt̬əl ˈsɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ˈsɪs.tə / (BrE)
a sister who is younger than oneself

Examples

Now Pat may have been a lecher and a plug-ugly, but he was a good churchgoing Catholic and he loved his little sister.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: big sister - an older sister
  • Synonym: younger sister - a sister who is younger than oneself (synonym)
