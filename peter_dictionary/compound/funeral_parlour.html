funeral parlour

1. NOUN

/ ˈfjunərəl ˌpɑrlɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjuːnərəl ˌpɑːlə / (BrE)
A place where the dead are prepared for burial or cremation and where people may pay their respects to the deceased.

Examples

He will be at your funeral parlour in one hour.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: funeral home - A place where funeral services and burials are arranged; American equivalent.

Etymology

From 'funeral' (Late Latin 'funeralis', from 'funus': burial, funeral) + 'parlour' (from Old French 'parloir', from 'parler': to speak).
