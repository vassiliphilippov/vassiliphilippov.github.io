recording studio

1. NOUN

/ rɪˈkɔrdɪŋ ˈstudiˌoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdɪŋ ˈstjuːdiəʊ / (BrE)
A room or building equipped for making sound recordings, typically music, using professional equipment.

Examples

Publishers want books that will sell, recording studios want discs that will not seem strange to ears used to hillbilly and jazz music, grade and high schools want quaint, but moral, material.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: studio - a room where recording or artistic work takes place
