total darkness

1. NOUN

/ ˈtoʊt̬l ˈdɑːrk.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl ˈdɑːk.nəs / (BrE)
A complete absence of light; a state where no light is present at all.

Examples

He could see in almost total darkness.

Synset Examples

they fumbled around in total darkness
in the black of night

Related Terms

  • Synonym: pitch darkness - complete darkness; no light at all
  • Antonym: brightness - the quality of being full of light; the opposite of darkness
