southern hemisphere

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌðɚn ˈhɛməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən ˈhɛmɪsˌfɪə / (BrE)
the half of the Earth that lies south of the equator

Examples

"On the side toward the horizon - the southern hemisphere - it is spring; plants are being taught to grow ".

Related Terms

  • Antonym: northern hemisphere - the half of the Earth that lies north of the equator
