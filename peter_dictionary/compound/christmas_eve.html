Christmas Eve

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪsməs iv / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs iːv / (BrE)
The day before Christmas, celebrated on December 24th.

Examples

And it was interesting to observe that B.B.C..'s television film on Christmas Eve was The Bells of St. Mary's.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: Christmas Day - the day of Christmas, celebrated on December 25th

Etymology

From 'Christmas', the Christian holiday, and 'eve', meaning the day or night before an event.
