Church of Rome

1. PROPN

/ tʃɝtʃ əv roʊm / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ əv rəʊm / (BrE)
Another name for the Roman Catholic Church, the Christian church based in the Vatican and led by the pope.

Examples

Peter Caroli had come to Geneva, saying that he had been a bishop of the Church of Rome and had been persecuted in Paris for his Reformed faith.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Roman Catholic Church - the worldwide Christian church based in the Vatican, led by the pope
