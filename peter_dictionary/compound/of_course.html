of course

1. ADV

/ ʌv kɔrs / (AmE), / ɒv kɔːs / (BrE)
Used to say that something is obvious or expected, or to affirm a response or agreement.

Examples

And of course, the most important item.

Synset Examples

naturally, the lawyer sent us a huge bill

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturally - as might be expected; in a natural manner
  • Synonym: certainly - without doubt; definitely
  • Antonym: not at all - used to express denial or contradiction
