foster home

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɑstər hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə həʊm / (BrE)
A household in which a child who is unable to live with their parents is cared for, usually as a temporary arrangement organized by a social service agency.

Examples

The grand jury took a swipe at the State Welfare Department's handling of federal funds granted for child welfare services in foster homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: foster care - the system or arrangement of caring for children in foster homes
