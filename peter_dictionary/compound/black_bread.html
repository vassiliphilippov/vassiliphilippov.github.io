black bread

1. NOUN

/ blæk brɛd / (AmE), / blæk brɛd / (BrE)
A type of bread made using coarse rye flour, typically darker in color and denser in texture than bread made from wheat.

Examples

Flies swarmed around the bowl of lentils in which he mopped thick black bread.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rye bread - bread made from rye flour
