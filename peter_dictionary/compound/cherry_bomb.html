cherry bomb

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛri bɑm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri bɒm / (BrE)
A small, red, ball-shaped firecracker known for its high explosive power.

Examples

He would lay in wait in the garage, and when the terrier came scratching around, he 'd let fly with a cherry bomb.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firecracker - A small explosive device for making noise, used in celebrations
