head covering

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛd ˌkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛd ˌkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
A garment or piece of material worn to cover the head, sometimes also the face, for reasons such as religion, fashion, protection, or modesty.

Examples

In particular he sought the gentle, sweet-faced nuns, with head coverings and veils coming to the middle of their foreheads, remembering their expressions until he reached home and set them down on paper.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hat - a shaped covering for the head, often with a brim
  • Synonym: veil - a piece of fine material worn to cover the head or face
  • Synonym: helmet - a protective head covering, usually made of hard material
