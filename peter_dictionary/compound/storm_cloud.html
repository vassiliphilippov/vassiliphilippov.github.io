storm cloud

1. NOUN

/ stɔrm klaʊd / (AmE), / stɔːm klaʊd / (BrE)
A large, dark cloud that indicates that a storm or rain is likely to occur.

Examples

Above him slid the evasive shadow of a storm cloud.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cloud - a visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere
