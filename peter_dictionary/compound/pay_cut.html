pay cut

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪ ˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ ˌkʌt / (BrE)
a reduction in a person's salary or wages

Examples

There were other shifts and pay cuts according to the way individuals had conducted themselves.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: pay rise - an increase in a person's salary or wages
