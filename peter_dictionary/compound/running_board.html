running board

1. NOUN

/ ˈrʌnɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A narrow flat board or step attached beneath the doors of some older cars or trucks, used as a step to enter or exit the vehicle.

Examples

Quickly but carefully lowering my duffel bag over the low side rack, I stepped on the running board; it flopped down, sprang back up and gouged my shin.
