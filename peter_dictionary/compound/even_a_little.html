even a little

1. ADV

/ ˈivən ə ˈlɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈiːvən ə ˈlɪtəl / (BrE)
to any extent at all; even the smallest amount.

Examples

If I hear so much as a mouse fart in here the rest of the night, I swear by God and sonny Jesus, you will all visit the infirmary.

Synset Examples

are you in the least interested?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in the least - to any extent at all
  • Synonym: so much as - even to the slightest degree
