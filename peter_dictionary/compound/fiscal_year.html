fiscal year

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪskəl jɪr / (AmE), / ˈfɪskəl jɪə / (BrE)
A 12-month period used by a government, business, or organization for accounting and financial reporting purposes, which does not necessarily coincide with the calendar year.
