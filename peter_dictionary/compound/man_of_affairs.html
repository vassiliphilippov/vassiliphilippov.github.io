man of affairs

1. NOUN

/ ˌmæn əv əˈfɛrz / (AmE), / ˌmæn əv əˈfeəz / (BrE)
A man who is skilled in managing commercial or industrial business, especially as an owner or executive.

Examples

Not in Eichmann's anti-Semitism but in the anti-Semitism of the sober German man of affairs lay the potential warning of the Trial.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: businessman - a person engaged in commercial or industrial business
