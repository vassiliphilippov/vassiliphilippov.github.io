knuckle duster

1. NOUN

/ ˈnʌkəl ˌdʌstər / (AmE), / ˈnʌkl ˌdʌstə / (BrE)
A small metal weapon, often made of brass, designed to be worn over the knuckles to enhance the impact of a punch.

Examples

Until 1924 O' Banion pistoleers and knuckle-duster bullyboys had kept his North Side domain solidly Democratic.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brass knuckles - metal weapon worn on the knuckles to increase punching power

Etymology

From 'knuckle' (the joint of a finger) and 'duster' (slang for something that delivers a blow).
