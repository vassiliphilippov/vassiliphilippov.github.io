strong breeze

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ briːz / (AmE), / strɒŋ briːz / (BrE)
A wind with a speed of 25–31 knots (29–36 miles per hour or 46–57 kilometers per hour); classified as force 6 on the Beaufort scale.

Examples

Looked like a stiff breeze would blow him over.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: moderate breeze - wind of 17–21 knots; Beaufort scale 4
  • Degree Higher: near gale - wind of 28–33 knots; Beaufort scale 7
