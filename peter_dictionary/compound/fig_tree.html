fig tree

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪɡ ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡ ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Ficus, especially one that produces edible figs.

Examples

Love and hatred and fear were one here, shaded only by fig trees and grape vines.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fig - the edible fruit of the fig tree
