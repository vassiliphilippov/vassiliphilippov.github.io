heavenly body

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛvənli ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈhɛvənli ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
A natural object in space, such as a star, planet, or moon, that is visible in the sky.

Examples

It has recently become practical to use the radio emission of the moon and planets as a new source of information about these bodies and their atmospheres.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: celestial body - natural object in space, like a star or planet

Etymology

From 'heavenly' (relating to the heavens or sky) + 'body' (object); 'heavenly' from Old English 'heofonlic'.
