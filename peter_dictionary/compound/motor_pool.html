motor pool

1. NOUN

/ ˈmoʊtər puːl / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə puːl / (BrE)
A fleet of vehicles, especially military, controlled by a single authority and available for use as needed.

Examples

This is not unlike the order received by the sergeant of an army motor pool: "Four trucks to Fort Mason gym, 7: 30 tonight, for hauling girls to dance.
