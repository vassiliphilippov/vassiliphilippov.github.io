brain damage

1. NOUN

/ breɪn ˈdæmɪdʒ / (AmE), / breɪn ˈdamɪdʒ / (BrE)
Injury to the brain that impairs its normal functions, often permanently, caused by trauma, disease, or other factors.

Examples

But you should know that sudden, serious brain injury causes the victim to bite down hard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: head injury - trauma to the head
