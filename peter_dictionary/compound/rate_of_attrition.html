rate of attrition

1. NOUN

/ reɪt əv əˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / reɪt əv əˈtrɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
The speed or proportion at which people or things leave or disappear from a group, reducing its size over time.

Examples

Interviewing, checking references, training the salesmen, having them go with more experienced salesmen was expensive - and the rate of attrition due to resignations or unsatisfactory performance was too high.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: attrition - gradual reduction in number or strength
