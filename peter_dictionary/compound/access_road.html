access road

1. NOUN

/ ˈæksɛs roʊd / (AmE), / ˈækses rəʊd / (BrE)
A short road that leads to and from a main road or expressway, providing entry or exit for vehicles.

Examples

Do you know the trend in your cost of maintaining access roads and parking lots?

Synset Examples

in Britain they call an access road a slip road

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slip road - a British English term for a road allowing vehicles to enter or leave a motorway or dual carriageway
