tightly knit

1. ADJ

/ ˈtaɪtli nɪt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtli nɪt / (BrE)
(of a group, structure, or organization) closely and firmly integrated or united; having members who are closely connected with each other.

Examples

Without losing the distinctive undertow of Brahmsian rhythm, the pacing is firm and the over-all performance has a tightly knit quality that makes for maximum cumulative effect.

Synset Examples

a tight-knit organization

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tight-knit - closely integrated, united group (variant spelling)
