pay rate

1. NOUN

/ peɪ reɪt / (AmE), / peɪ reɪt / (BrE)
The amount of money paid to a worker per unit of time, such as per hour, day, or week.

Examples

I endorse pending legislation that will restore the traditional relationship between retired and active duty pay rates.

Synset Examples

women's pay rate is lower than men's

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wage - amount paid to a worker for work performed
  • Synonym: salary - agreed regular payment for employment, usually monthly or annually
