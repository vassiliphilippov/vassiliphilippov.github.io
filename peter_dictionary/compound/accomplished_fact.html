accomplished fact

1. NOUN

/ əˈkɑmplɪʃt fækt / (AmE), / əˈkɒmplɪʃt fækt / (BrE)
Something that has already been done or completed and cannot be changed or reversed.

Examples

What's done is done.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fait accompli - a thing that has already happened and is thus regarded as irreversible
