funny wagon

1. NOUN

/ ˈfʌni ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈfʌni ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
An informal term for an ambulance used to transport patients to a mental hospital.

Examples

"Oh, I forgot to say that if one is taken to the funny house in the funny wagon, he is removed to a mental institution in an ambulance.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: ambulance - vehicle equipped for taking sick or injured people to and from hospital
