lead sheet

1. NOUN

/ lid ʃit / (AmE), / liːd ʃiːt / (BrE)
A sheet of music showing the melody, lyrics, and chords of a song, written in a simple format that provides the essential information for performance.

Examples

He had yet to meet Harold Arlen, for although they had "collaborated "on "Satan's Li' l Lamb ", Mercer and Harburg had worked from a lead sheet the composer had furnished them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheet music - written music in printed or handwritten form
