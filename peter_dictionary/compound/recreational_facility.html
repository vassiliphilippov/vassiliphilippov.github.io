recreational facility

1. NOUN

/ ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl fəˈsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl fəˈsɪlɪti / (BrE)
A public or private place designed for people to engage in recreational activities, such as sports, exercise, or leisure.

Examples

Do employees contribute their share of money to recreational facilities?
