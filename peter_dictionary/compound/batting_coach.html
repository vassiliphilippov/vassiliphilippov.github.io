batting coach

1. NOUN

/ ˈbæt̬ɪŋ koʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ kəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A coach in baseball whose primary responsibility is to train and advise players on batting technique and performance.

Examples

The famed Yankee Clipper, now retired, has been assisting as a batting coach.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: coach - person who trains or teaches sports participants
