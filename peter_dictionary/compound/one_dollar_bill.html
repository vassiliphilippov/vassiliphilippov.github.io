one dollar bill

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈdɑlər bɪl / (AmE), / wʌn ˈdɒlə bɪl / (BrE)
A paper banknote with a value of one dollar, especially in the United States.

Examples

The one-dollar bill.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dollar bill - a piece of paper money worth one dollar or multiple dollars
