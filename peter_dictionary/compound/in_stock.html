in stock

1. ADJ

/ ɪn stɑk / (AmE), / ɪn stɒk / (BrE)
(of goods) available for use or sale; present in inventory.

Examples

"How many pamphlets do we have in stock "?

Synset Examples

they have plenty of stonewashed jeans in stock

Related Terms

  • Antonym: out of stock - not available for use or sale
