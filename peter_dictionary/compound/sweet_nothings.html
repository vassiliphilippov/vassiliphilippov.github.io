sweet nothings

1. NOUN

/ swit ˈnʌθɪŋz / (AmE), / swiːt ˈnʌθɪŋz / (BrE)
Words or phrases expressing affection or endearment, typically whispered to someone, that are pleasant but unimportant.

Examples

These little songs, however, were sweet nothings from the heart, tender memories of his childhood, little melodies that anyone could hum and that would make one want to weep.

Synset Examples

he whispered sweet nothings into her ear

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endearment - an expression of affection
