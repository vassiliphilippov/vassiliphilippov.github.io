right of entry

1. NOUN

/ raɪt əv ˈɛntri / (AmE), / raɪt əv ˈɛntri / (BrE)
The legal right to take possession of real property in a peaceful manner.

Examples

right-of-entry only and right-of-entry plus enforcement authority;
right-of-entry only and right-of-entry plus enforcement authority;
