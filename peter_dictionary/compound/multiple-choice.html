multiple-choice

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌltɪpəl tʃɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪpl tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
(of a question or test) offering several possible answers from which the correct one must be chosen; consisting of such questions.

Examples

An interview schedule of open-ended questions and a multiple-choice questionnaire were prepared, and one parent of each of the sample children was seen in the home.

Synset Examples

multiple-choice questions
a multiple-choice test
