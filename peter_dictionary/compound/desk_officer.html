desk officer

1. NOUN

/ dɛsk ˈɑfɪsər / (AmE), / dɛsk ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a military or police officer whose duties involve administrative or office work rather than active field assignment

Examples

Though he knew no more about military science and tactics than any other desk officer, he managed to get transferred to the combat forces.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: field officer - an officer engaged in active field duty
