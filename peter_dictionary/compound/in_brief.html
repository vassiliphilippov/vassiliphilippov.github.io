in brief

1. ADV

/ ɪn briːf / (AmE), / ɪn briːf / (BrE)
Used to indicate that what follows is a short summary or a concise statement of something.

Examples

In brief, Berman played himself and not Bolger.

Synset Examples

the history is summed up concisely in this book
she replied briefly
briefly, we have a problem
to put it shortly

Related Terms

  • Synonym: briefly - using few words; concisely
  • Synonym: in short - to summarize concisely
