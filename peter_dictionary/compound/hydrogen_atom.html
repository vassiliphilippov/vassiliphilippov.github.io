hydrogen atom

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaɪdrədʒən ˈætəm / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrɪdʒən ˈætəm / (BrE)
An atom of the chemical element hydrogen, consisting of a single proton and usually one electron.
