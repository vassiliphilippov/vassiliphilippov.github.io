three-hitter

1. NOUN

/ θriˈhɪtər / (AmE), / θriˈhɪtə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a game in which the pitcher allows the opposing team only three hits.

Examples

Nischwitz was working on a 3 hitter when the Indians bunched three of their eight hits for two runs in the sixth.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: no-hitter - a baseball game in which the pitcher allows no hits
  • Degree Lower: one-hitter - a game in which only one hit is allowed by the pitcher
  • Degree Lower: two-hitter - a game in which only two hits are allowed by the pitcher
  • Degree Higher: four-hitter - a game in which only four hits are allowed by the pitcher
  • Degree Higher: five-hitter - a game in which only five hits are allowed by the pitcher
