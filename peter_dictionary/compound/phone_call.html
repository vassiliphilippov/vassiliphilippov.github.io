phone call

1. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn ˌkɔl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn ˌkɔːl / (BrE)
an instance of communication by telephone

Examples

It was marked by controversy, anonymous midnight phone calls and veiled threats of violence.

Synset Examples

she reported several anonymous calls
he placed a phone call to London
he heard the phone ringing but didn't want to take the call

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call - an instance of making contact by phone

2. NOUN

/ ˈfoʊn ˌkɑl / (AmE), / ˈfəʊn ˌkɔːl / (BrE)
an act of speaking to someone on the telephone

Examples

Hey, Mikey! - Yeah. - Phone call! - Who is it?

Synset Examples

she reported several anonymous calls
he placed a phone call to London
he heard the phone ringing but didn't want to take the call

Related Terms

  • Synonym: call - an attempt to communicate via telephone
