sexual relation

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkʃuəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃʊəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
An act of sexual intercourse between two people.

Examples

In one extreme case, cited by a Pittsburgh psychologist, an office worker's wife refused to have sexual relations with her husband unless he bought her the luxuries she demanded.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sexual intercourse - the physical act of sex between people involving genital contact
