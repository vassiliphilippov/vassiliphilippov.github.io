every year

1. ADV

/ ˈɛv.ɹi jɪɹ / (AmE), / ˈɛv.ɹi jɪə / (BrE)
each year; once during every period of twelve months

Examples

Even the loss of hotel business to the outskirt's motel has been relatively painless; the hotel-motel demarcation is becoming harder to find every year.

Synset Examples

they travel to China annually

Related Terms

  • Synonym: annually - once a year, every year
