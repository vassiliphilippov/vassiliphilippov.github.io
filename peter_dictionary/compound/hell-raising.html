hell-raising

1. NOUN

/ ˈhɛlˌreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛlˌreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
rowdy or disruptive behavior intended to cause trouble or excitement, usually for fun

Examples

The seventh man was Red Hogan, a wiry little puncher with a wild streak and a liking for hell raising.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: mischief - playful misbehavior or troublemaking
  • Synonym: rowdiness - boisterous or disorderly behavior
