raw deal

1. NOUN

/ rɔ dɪl / (AmE), / rɔː dɪəl / (BrE)
unfair or harsh treatment; an instance of being treated badly or unjustly

Examples

Of course he could n't say much, really, because of Debora, but Linda Kay could imagine what kind of woman his wife had been and what a raw deal he had got.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: injustice - unfair treatment; lack of fairness
  • Antonym: fair shake - fair or just treatment
