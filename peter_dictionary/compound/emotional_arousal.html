emotional arousal

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈmoʊʃənl əˈraʊzl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl əˈraʊzl̩ / (BrE)
A state of heightened emotion, often resulting in intense emotional reactions or behaviors.

Examples

Even more complex and obviously cortically induced forms of emotional arousal could be elicited in monkey A on seeing monkey B (but not a rabbit) in emotional stress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: excitement - A feeling of eager enthusiasm and interest
  • Antonym: calm - The state of being emotionally undisturbed or tranquil
