wedding cake

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ keɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ keɪk / (BrE)
A rich, decorative cake with two or more tiers, typically covered with frosting and served at a wedding reception.

Examples

Wait till you see the wedding cake I made for your daughter!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cake - a sweet baked food made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients
