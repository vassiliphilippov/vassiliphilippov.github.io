swimming trunks

1. NOUN

/ ˈswɪmɪŋ trʌŋks / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ trʌŋks / (BrE)
A close-fitting or loose-fitting garment worn by men for swimming, usually covering the lower part of the body and sometimes extending to the knees or mid-thigh.

Examples

He went down the hall to Eugene's bathroom, to turn on the hot-water heater, and on the side of the tub he saw a pair of blue wool swimming trunks.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimsuit - a garment worn for swimming, typically by women or as a general term
  • Synonym: bathing suit - an alternative term for swimsuit, used for both men and women
  • Degree Lower: bikini - a two-piece swimsuit for women
  • Synonym: swimming shorts - an alternative style of men's swimwear, often looser fitting
