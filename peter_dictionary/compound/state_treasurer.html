state treasurer

1. NOUN

/ steɪt ˈtrɛʒərər / (AmE), / steɪt ˈtrɛʒərə / (BrE)
the official responsible for managing the financial affairs of a state government, including overseeing funds, investments, and revenue collection

Examples

The bill, which Daniel said he drafted personally, would force banks, insurance firms, pipeline companies and other corporations to report such property to the state treasurer.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: treasurer - an official in charge of funds
