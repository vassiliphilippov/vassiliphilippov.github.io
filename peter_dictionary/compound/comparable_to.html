comparable to

1. ADJ

/ ˈkɑːm.pɚ.ə.bəl tuː / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pər.ə.bəl tʊ / (BrE)
having qualities, amount, or nature that can be compared to something else; as good as or similar to.
