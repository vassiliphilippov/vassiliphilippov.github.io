trained worker

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnd ˈwɝːkɚ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnd ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
A worker who has acquired special skills or training for a particular job or field.

Examples

Planning of vocational education programs and courses is oriented to local employer needs for trained workers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: skilled worker - a worker with skills acquired through training or experience
  • Antonym: unskilled worker - a worker who does not possess special training or skills
