time of life

1. NOUN

/ taɪm əv laɪf / (AmE), / taɪm əv laɪf / (BrE)
a specific period or stage in a person's life, usually relating to their age or phase of development

Examples

And knowing its humble place in the scale of things, why did he, at this time of life, seem almost ready to sell his soul for plumpness?
More potent a charm to bring back that time of life than this record of a few pictures and a few remembered facts would be a catalogue of the minutiae which are of the very stuff of the mind, intrinsic, because they were known in the beginning not by the eye alone but by the hand that held them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: life stage - a distinct phase or period in a person's life
