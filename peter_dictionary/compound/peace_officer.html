peace officer

1. NOUN

/ pis ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / piːs ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
A law enforcement official with legal authority to maintain public order and enforce laws.

Examples

He wore no gun - a strange ommission for a peace officer in this country.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: police officer - a member of a police force
  • Synonym: lawman - an officer of the law

Etymology

From 'peace' meaning public order, and 'officer' meaning a person holding a position of authority.
