waiting list

1. NOUN

/ ˈweɪtɪŋ lɪst / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪŋ lɪst / (BrE)
a list of people who are waiting for something, such as an available space, position, or service.

Examples

In the city, he said, the waiting list for those who want to join the union is so long that unless a boy has an inside track he can n't get in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: queue - a line or sequence of people or vehicles awaiting their turn
  • Synonym: priority list - a list in which certain individuals have precedence over others
