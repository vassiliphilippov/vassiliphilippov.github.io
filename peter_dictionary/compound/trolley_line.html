trolley line

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrɑli laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtrɒli laɪn / (BrE)
A route or system of tracks or wires used by streetcars or trolley buses for public transportation.

Examples

Until internal combustion became cheap, he had to be near a railroad siding and a trolley line or an existing large community of lower-class homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: streetcar line - a transit line used by streetcars
  • Synonym: bus route - a route along which buses travel
