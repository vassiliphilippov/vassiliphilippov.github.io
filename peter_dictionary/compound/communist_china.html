Communist China

1. PROPN

/ kəˈmjunɪst ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪst ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
A term used to refer to the People's Republic of China, especially during and after its establishment as a communist state under the Chinese Communist Party in 1949.

Examples

To find out, we traveled throughout that part of Laos still nominally controlled, in the daytime at least, by the Royal Lao Army: from Attopeu, the City of Buffalo Dung in the southeast, to Muong Sing, the City of Lions in the northwest, close to Communist China (map, page 250).

Related Terms

  • Synonym: China - a country in East Asia, officially the People's Republic of China
  • Synonym: People's Republic of China - official name of Communist China since 1949
