picture book

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪktʃɚ bʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə bʊk / (BrE)
A book, especially for children, in which the story is told chiefly through pictures rather than text.

Examples

If Daddy's books are out of bounds his own picture books are not.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: comic book - a magazine or book that contains stories told mainly in pictures, often in the form of a sequence of drawings
