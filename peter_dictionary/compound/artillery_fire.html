artillery fire

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪləri ˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪləri ˌfaɪə / (BrE)
the shooting or bombardment carried out by large-caliber guns, such as cannons or howitzers, usually in warfare

Examples

In the end we ran into Communist artillery fire.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gunfire - the firing of guns, especially repeatedly or continuously
