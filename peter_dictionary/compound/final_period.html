final period

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪ.nəl ˈpɪr.i.əd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.nəl ˈpɪə.ri.əd / (BrE)
the last segment or division of play in a game or sporting event.

Examples

The Broncs did hit two quick strikes in the final period against the Texans, but Dallas had enough of a lead to hold them off.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: overtime - extra period added to a game if the score is tied after regulation
