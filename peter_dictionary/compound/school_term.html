school term

1. NOUN

/ skuːl tɝːm / (AmE), / skuːl tɜːm / (BrE)
A period of the academic year during which a school holds regular classes.

Examples

Dr. Clark will succeed Dr. J. R. McLemore, who will retire at the close of the present school term.

Synset Examples

they had to shorten the school term

Related Terms

  • Synonym: semester - half-year division of the academic year, especially in US
  • Synonym: term - a fixed or limited period of time, especially in a schedule
