concert hall

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑnsɚt hɔl / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsət hɔːl / (BrE)
A large building or room designed for the performance of concerts, especially classical music.

Examples

It was like being in the concert hall in the afternoon and hearing the piano virtuoso rehearsing.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: auditorium - a large room or hall for an audience, often used for performances or public meetings
  • Degree Lower: theater - a building or room for dramatic performances
