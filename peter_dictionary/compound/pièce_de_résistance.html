pièce de résistance

1. NOUN

/ piˌɛs də reɪˈzɪstɑ̃s / (AmE), / pjɛs də reɪˈzɪstɑ̃s / (BrE)
The most outstanding or important item, feature, or achievement in a particular collection, event, or situation.

Examples

A tongue-in-cheek stylization of 18 th Century ideas; a trial balloon to test the aesthetic climate of the times; a brilliant piece de resistance?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: highlight - the most outstanding part or moment
  • Synonym: showpiece - an outstanding or excellent example; exhibition item

Etymology

From French, literally 'piece of resistance' (main dish of a meal, later transferred to mean main or outstanding item).
