lower rank

1. NOUN

/ ˈloʊɚ ræŋk / (AmE), / ˈləʊə ræŋk / (BrE)
a position or status that is below others in a hierarchy, especially in an organization or the military

Examples

Commands go only from an officer to the man of nearest lower rank.
And (d) all action of a physical kind pertinent to the mission is relegated to the line of men on the lower rank.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: higher rank - a position or status above others in a hierarchy
  • Synonym: inferiority - the state of being lower in status or quality
