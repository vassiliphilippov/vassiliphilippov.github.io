cashier's check

1. NOUN

/ kəˈʃɪɹz tʃɛk / (AmE), / kəˈʃɪəz tʃɛk / (BrE)
A check issued by a bank and drawn on its own funds, signed by a cashier or other authorized officer, and considered as secure as cash.

Examples

Here's your cashier's check, sir.

Synset Examples

cashier's checks are as good as cash

Related Terms

  • Synonym: certified check - a check guaranteed by a bank with funds set aside from the buyer's account
  • Antonym: personal check - a check drawn by a person on their own account
