step-by-step

1. ADJ

/ stɛp baɪ stɛp / (AmE), / stɛp baɪ stɛp / (BrE)
Happening or done gradually, with each stage following in sequence; proceeding one stage at a time.

Examples

This involves step-by-step progress, and such will have to be the day-by-day work of lexicostatistics as a growing body of scientific inquiry.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gradual - taking place slowly over time rather than suddenly
