activation energy

1. NOUN

/ ˌæk.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən ˈɛn.ɚ.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌæk.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən ˈɛn.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
the minimum amount of energy that a system, especially a chemical reactant, must acquire before a reaction or process can begin

Related Terms

Etymology
