reporting weight

1. NOUN

/ ɹɪˈpɔɹtɪŋ weɪt / (AmE), / ɹɪˈpɔːtɪŋ weɪt / (BrE)
an athlete's body weight as measured at the start of a season or upon first reporting to practice

Examples

The 6 - foot 3 inch Hansen checked in close to 200 pounds, 15 pounds lighter than his reporting weight last spring.
