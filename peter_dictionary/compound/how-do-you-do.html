how-do-you-do

1. NOUN

/ ˌhaʊ də ju ˈdu / (AmE), / ˌhaʊ djuː ˈduː / (BrE)
A formal greeting or expression used when meeting someone for the first time.

Examples

How do you do, sir?

Synset Examples

every morning they exchanged polite hellos

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hello - an informal expression of greeting
  • Synonym: good morning - a polite greeting used in the morning
