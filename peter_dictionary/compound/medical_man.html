medical man

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛdɪkəl mæn / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl man / (BrE)
A man who is qualified and licensed to practice medicine; a physician or doctor.

Examples

By combining the talents of a medical man, Dr. Aterman, a biophysicist, Mr. Berkely, and an electronics expert, Dr. Zworykin, this novel technique has been developed which promises to open broad avenues to understanding life processes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: doctor - a person qualified to practice medicine
  • Antonym: medical woman - a woman who is qualified to practice medicine
