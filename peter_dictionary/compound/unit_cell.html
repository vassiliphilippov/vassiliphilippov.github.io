unit cell

1. NOUN

/ ˈjunɪt sɛl / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪt sɛl / (BrE)
The smallest repeating structural unit in a crystal lattice, consisting of a group of atoms or molecules arranged in a specific pattern in three dimensions.

Examples
