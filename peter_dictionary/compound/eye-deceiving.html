eye-deceiving

1. ADJ

/ aɪ dɪˈsiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / aɪ dɪˈsiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
creating a visual illusion that makes something appear real or different from reality, especially in art

Examples

In later collages of both masters, a variety of extraneous materials are used, sometimes in the same work, and almost always in conjunction with every other eye-deceiving and eye-undeceiving device they can think of.

Synset Examples

the visual deception of trompe-l'oeil art

Related Terms

  • Synonym: trompe-l'oeil - art technique creating the illusion of three-dimensionality
