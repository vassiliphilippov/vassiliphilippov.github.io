half sister

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈsɪstər / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈsɪstə / (BrE)
A sister with whom you share only one biological parent.

Examples

He tried defiance and openly flaunted his devotion to his half sister, but he soon saw, as did she, that this course if persisted in would involve them in a common ruin.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: stepsister - daughter of one's stepparent by a previous relationship, not biologically related

Etymology

Formed from 'half' (indicating partial relation) + 'sister'.
