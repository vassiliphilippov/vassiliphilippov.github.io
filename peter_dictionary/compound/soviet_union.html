Soviet Union

1. PROPN

/ ˌsoʊvi.ət ˈjuːni.ən / (AmE), / ˌsəʊvɪ.ət ˈjuːni.ən / (BrE)
a former communist state in eastern Europe and northern Asia, established in 1922 and dissolved in 1991, comprising Russia and 14 other Soviet republics
