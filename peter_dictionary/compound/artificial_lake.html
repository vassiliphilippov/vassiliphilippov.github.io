artificial lake

1. NOUN

/ ɑrˈtɪfɪʃəl leɪk / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪfɪʃəl leɪk / (BrE)
A lake that has been created by humans, typically to store water for community, agricultural, or industrial use.

Examples

There was an artificial lake just out of sight in the first stand of trees, fed by a half dozen springs that popped out of the ground above the hillside orchard.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reservoir - an artificial or natural lake used to store water for community use
