tape recorder

1. NOUN

/ teɪp rɪˈkɔrdər / (AmE), / teɪp rɪˈkɔːdə / (BrE)
a device for recording and playing back sound using magnetic tape

Examples

And she felt amply rewarded for her suffering when the evidence of Lee's quack shenanigans, gathered by the tape recorder under her friend's clothing, proved adequate in court for convicting Franklin D. Lee.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dictaphone - a machine used to record speech for transcription
  • Degree Lower: cassette recorder - a recorder using cassette tapes

Etymology

From 'tape', referring to magnetic tape, and 'recorder', from record + -er.
