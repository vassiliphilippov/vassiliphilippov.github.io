all-weather

1. ADJ

/ ˌɔlˈwɛðər / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈwɛðə / (BrE)
suitable for use or operation in any weather conditions.

Examples

All-weather roads must be provided next to the feeding floor so access will be possible all year.

Synset Examples

a good all-weather road
