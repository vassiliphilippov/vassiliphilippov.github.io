street clothes

1. NOUN

/ striːt kloʊðz / (AmE), / striːt kləʊðz / (BrE)
Ordinary clothing worn in public, as distinct from uniforms, costumes, sportswear, or work clothes.

Examples

Outside in the dressing room, Frankie Ricco sat on the bench dressed in his street clothes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: casual clothes - clothing designed for informal wear
