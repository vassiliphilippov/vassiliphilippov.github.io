race problem

1. NOUN

/ reɪs ˈprɑːbləm / (AmE), / reɪs ˈprɒbləm / (BrE)
A social and political issue arising from conflict or tension between different racial groups living in the same or neighboring areas.

Examples

The race problem has tended to obscure other, less emotional, issues which may fundamentally be even more divisive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: racial tension - strained relations between racial groups
  • Synonym: race relations - relations between people of different racial backgrounds
