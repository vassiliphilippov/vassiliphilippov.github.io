Flag Day

1. NOUN

/ flæɡ deɪ / (AmE), / flæɡ deɪ / (BrE)
A day in the United States, observed on June 14, commemorating the adoption of the national flag in 1777.

Examples

On June 14, 1900 the Manchester Journal reported that an electrical engineer was installing an electric light plant for Edward S. Isham at "Ormsby Hill ".
