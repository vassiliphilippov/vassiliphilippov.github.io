broody hen

1. NOUN

/ ˈbruːdi hɛn / (AmE), / ˈbruːdi hɛn / (BrE)
A domestic hen that is ready to sit on eggs and incubate them.

Examples

It was a fine broody hen, white, with a maternal eye and a striking abundance of feathers in the under region of the abdomen.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: broody - of a bird, inclined to incubate eggs
