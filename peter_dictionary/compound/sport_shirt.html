sport shirt

1. NOUN

/ spɔrt ʃɜrt / (AmE), / spɔːt ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A casual shirt with short sleeves, designed for comfort and everyday wear, often worn without a tie.

Examples

He wore a brown knit sports shirt with no tie.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: polo shirt - a casual shirt with a collar and a few buttons near the neck
