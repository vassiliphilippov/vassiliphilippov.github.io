soon enough

1. ADV

/ suːn ɪˈnʌf / (AmE), / suːn ɪˈnʌf / (BrE)
at a sufficiently early time; early enough to avoid being late or missing something

Examples

"I guess I 'll find out soon enough.
She jerked the coat back on and squeezed it around her again, but not soon enough.

Synset Examples

we made it to the party in time

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in time - early enough; not too late
  • Antonym: too late - not soon enough; after the appropriate or desired time
