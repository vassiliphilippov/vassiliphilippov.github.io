agricultural agent

1. NOUN

/ əˌɡrɪkjəˈltʃɚl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / əˌɡrɪkjʊˈltʃərəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
An official, usually employed by a government agency, who provides advice and assistance to farmers and rural communities on agriculture and home economics.

Examples

If you can n't tell, get help from your county agricultural agent or other local specialist.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: county agent - official advising farmers and rural residents, especially in the US
