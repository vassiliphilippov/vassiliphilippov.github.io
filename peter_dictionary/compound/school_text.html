school text

1. NOUN

/ skuːl tɛkst / (AmE), / skuːl tɛkst / (BrE)
A book used as the standard study material in schools or colleges, focusing on a particular subject.

Examples

His duty was to write a school text and to teach the entire personnel of the Gabriel how to speak Ozagen.

Synset Examples

his economics textbook is in its tenth edition
the professor wrote the text that he assigned students to buy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: textbook - A book used as a standard source of information on a particular subject for study
