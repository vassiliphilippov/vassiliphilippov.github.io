side yard

1. NOUN

/ saɪd jɑrd / (AmE), / saɪd jɑːd / (BrE)
an area of land on either side of a house, typically within the boundaries of the property

Examples

He helped Kate and Juanita enlarge the flower garden in the side yard, where they sometimes sat in the still evenings watching the last fat bees working against the summer's purple dusk.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: front yard - an area of land in front of a house
  • Synonym: backyard - an area of land at the back of a house
