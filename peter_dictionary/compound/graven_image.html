graven image

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡreɪvən ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪvən ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
An idol or statue, especially one carved from stone or wood, that is worshipped as a god.

Examples

"The Bible forbids us to bow down to or to make graven images.

Synset Examples

thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image
money was his god

Related Terms

  • Synonym: idol - an object or image worshipped as a god

Etymology

From 'graven', archaic past participle of 'grave' (to carve), and 'image'.
