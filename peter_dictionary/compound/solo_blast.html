solo blast

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊloʊ blæst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ blɑːst / (BrE)
(Baseball) A home run hit when there are no runners on base.

Examples

A two run homer by Norm Siebern and a solo blast by Bill Tuttle tied the game, and single runs in the eighth and ninth gave the Athletics their fifth victory in eight starts.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: solo homer - a home run with no runners on base; synonym in baseball slang
  • Degree Higher: grand slam - a home run hit when all three bases are occupied, scoring four runs
