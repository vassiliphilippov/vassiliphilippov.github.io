air wave

1. NOUN

/ ˈɛr weɪv / (AmE), / ˈeə weɪv / (BrE)
A sound wave that is transmitted through the air, especially as distinct from waves transmitted through other media.

Examples

The information is furnished by each of the guests, is sent by oral broadcasting over the air waves, and is received by the ears.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sound wave - a wave of compression and rarefaction, by which sound is propagated in an elastic medium
