elementary school

1. NOUN

/ ˌɛlɪˈmɛntəri skuːl / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪˈmɛntri skuːl / (BrE)
A school for children between the ages of about five and eleven, typically covering the first six to eight grades of formal education.

Examples

RCA Victor has an ambitious and useful project in a stereo series called "Adventures in Music ", which is an instructional record library for elementary schools.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: primary school - school for children between about 5 and 11 years old (British usage)
