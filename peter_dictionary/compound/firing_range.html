firing range

1. NOUN

/ ˈfaɪrɪŋ ˌreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərɪŋ ˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
A designated area where people practice shooting at targets with firearms or other weapons.

Examples

An argument with Orville Torrence Killpath was as frustrating and as futile as a cap pistol on a firing range.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: shooting range - area for practicing shooting at targets
