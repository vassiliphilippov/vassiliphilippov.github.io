world-shaking

1. ADJ

/ ˈwɝːld ˌʃeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːld ˌʃeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
Having an impact or importance great enough to affect the entire world; extremely significant or momentous.

Examples

They comprise a great variety of scene and interest: grim episodes of war, idyllic interludes, superb canvases of world-shaking events, and delightfully humorous sketches of odd characters.

Synset Examples

earthshaking proposals
the contest was no world-shaking affair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: earthshaking - having a very great effect; extremely important
  • Synonym: world-shattering - extremely significant or shocking; having a major impact
