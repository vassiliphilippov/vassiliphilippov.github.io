silver gray

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ɡreɪ / (BrE)
A light shade of grey, resembling the color of silver.

Examples

It was a delayed moon, but now the sky had cleared of scudding black and the stars sugared the silver-gray sky.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: gray - the color between black and white
  • Synonym: silver - a shiny grayish-white color, like that of the metal
