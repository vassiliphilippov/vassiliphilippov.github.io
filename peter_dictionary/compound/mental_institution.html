mental institution

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.tl ɪn.stəˌtu.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl ɪn.stɪˌtjuː.ʃən / (BrE)
A hospital or facility for the care and treatment of people with mental illnesses or disorders.
