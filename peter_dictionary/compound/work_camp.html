work camp

1. NOUN

/ wɝk kæmp / (AmE), / wɜːk kamp / (BrE)
a camp where prisoners or detainees are employed in government or other labor projects, typically under supervision

Examples

A bevy of interlacing lagers, work camps, concentration camps erupted in the area.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: concentration camp - a camp where large numbers of people are detained under armed guard, often in harsh conditions
