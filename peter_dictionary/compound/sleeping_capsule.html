sleeping capsule

1. NOUN

/ ˈsliːpɪŋ ˌkæpsəl / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ ˌkæpsjuːl / (BrE)
A capsule-shaped pill containing a drug that helps induce sleep.

Examples

He finally fell asleep around six in the morning with the aid of a sleeping capsule, a crutch he rarely used, and did n't awaken until early afternoon.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sleeping pill - a pill containing a drug that helps induce sleep
  • Synonym: sedative - a substance promoting calm or inducing sleep
