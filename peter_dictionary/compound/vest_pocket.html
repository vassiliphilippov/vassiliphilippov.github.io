vest pocket

1. NOUN

/ vɛst ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / vɛst ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a small pocket in the front of a man's vest or waistcoat, typically used for carrying a watch or small items

Examples

He would order her to bring coffee, and would take from his vest pocket a thin black pipe which he would stuff - he would not remove his gloves - and light and smoke.
