child welfare service

1. NOUN

/ tʃaɪld ˈwɛlfɛr ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / tʃaɪld ˈwelfeə ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
a public or private program or agency responsible for social work that promotes the welfare and protection of children, often including services such as foster care and support for families

Examples

The grand jury took a swipe at the State Welfare Department's handling of federal funds granted for child welfare services in foster homes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: child welfare agency - an organization responsible for children's welfare
