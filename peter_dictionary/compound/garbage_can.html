garbage can

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ kæn / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ kæn / (BrE)
A large container for holding rubbish until it is collected for disposal, typically made of metal or plastic and often used outdoors or in public places.

Examples

A 10 - gallon garbage can, with a tightly fitting cover, could be used to keep the wastes until it is safe to leave the shelter.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dustbin - container for rubbish disposal (British term)
  • Synonym: trash can - another term for a container for rubbish (chiefly US)
