prison term

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪzən tɜrm / (AmE), / ˈprɪzən tɜːm / (BrE)
a period of time for which someone is sentenced to be imprisoned

Examples

Prison time is slow time.

Synset Examples

he served a prison term of 15 months
his sentence was 5 to 10 years
he is doing time in the county jail

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sentence - the punishment assigned to a defendant found guilty by a court, especially in terms of imprisonment
  • Antonym: parole - the conditional release of a prisoner before the end of their prison term
