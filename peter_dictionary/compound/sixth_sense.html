sixth sense

1. NOUN

/ sɪksθ sɛns / (AmE), / sɪksθ sɛns / (BrE)
an intuitive ability to understand or know something without the use of the five senses

Examples

My sixth sense tells me your brother Fredo brought a bag full of money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intuition - the ability to understand something instinctively, without the need for conscious reasoning
