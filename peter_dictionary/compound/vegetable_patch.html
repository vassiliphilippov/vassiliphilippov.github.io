vegetable patch

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɛdʒtəbl pætʃ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl patʃ / (BrE)
A small area of ground where vegetables are grown, typically for household use.

Examples

When he needs money to buy something like, say, the Rolls-Royce he keeps near his vegetable patch, he takes a flyer in the sale of surplus army supplies.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kitchen garden - a garden where vegetables and herbs are grown for household use
