self-acceptance

1. NOUN

/ sɛlf əkˈsɛptəns / (AmE), / sɛlf əkˈsɛptəns / (BrE)
The recognition and acceptance of one's own abilities and characteristics as they are, including both strengths and weaknesses.

Examples

For he has the pride that comes of self acceptance and the humility, perhaps of the same genesis, not to impose himself upon another.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: self-esteem - confidence in one's own worth or abilities; self-respect
