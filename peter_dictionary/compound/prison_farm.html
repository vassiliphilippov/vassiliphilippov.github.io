prison farm

1. NOUN

/ ˈprɪzən fɑrm / (AmE), / ˈprɪzən fɑːm / (BrE)
a correctional facility where prisoners are employed in agricultural or farming work, often as part of their sentence

Examples

The jury praised the administration and operation of the Atlanta Police Department, the Fulton Tax Commissioner's Office, the Bellwood and Alpharetta prison farms, Grady Hospital and the Fulton Health Department.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work camp - a camp where prisoners are employed in physical labor, not limited to agriculture
