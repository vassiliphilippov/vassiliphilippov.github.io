absentee rate

1. NOUN

/ ˌæb.sənˈti reɪt / (AmE), / ˌæb.sənˈtiː reɪt / (BrE)
The percentage or proportion of employees who are absent from work during a specific period.

Examples

This cuts the absentee rate.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absenteeism - habitual absence from work or duty
