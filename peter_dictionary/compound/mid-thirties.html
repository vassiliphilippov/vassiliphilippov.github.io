mid-thirties

1. NOUN

/ mɪd ˈθɝːtiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈθɜːtiz / (BrE)
The period in a person's life between about 34 and 36 years of age.

Examples

In their mid thirties.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: early thirties - the period between ages 30 and 33
  • Degree Higher: late thirties - the period between ages 37 and 39
