make-up

1. NOUN

/ ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (BrE)
Cosmetic products such as powder, lipstick, and eye shadow applied to the face to enhance or alter appearance.

Examples

I have taken off my make-up.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cosmetics - substances applied to the body for beautification or cleansing
