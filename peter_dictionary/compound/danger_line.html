danger line

1. NOUN

/ ˈdeɪndʒɚ laɪn / (AmE), / ˈdeɪndʒə laɪn / (BrE)
an imaginary or marked boundary beyond which it is considered dangerous to go

Examples

"Let's not push our patience beyond the danger line, Sergeant ", Killpath nasaled.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: limit - a point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass
  • Antonym: safety line - a demarcation indicating the limit of safe area
