phone booth

1. NOUN

/ foʊn buθ / (AmE), / fəʊn buːð / (BrE)
A small structure with a public telephone inside, designed to allow people to make calls in private.

Examples

"I could scratch her eyes out ", Eileen cried and stamped her foot when I came back from the phone booth.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone booth - a booth for using a telephone
