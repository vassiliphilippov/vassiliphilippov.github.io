Caribbean Island

1. NOUN

/ kəˌrɪbi.ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / kəˌrɪb.i.ən ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
any island located in the Caribbean Sea, typically part of a group known as the West Indies

Examples

Hal saw in his mind the rest of Earth: Iceland, Greenland, the Caribbean Islands, and the eastern half of South America.

Etymology

Caribbean: from the name of the Carib people, the region's indigenous inhabitants.
