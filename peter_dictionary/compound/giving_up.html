giving up

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡɪvɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
the act of admitting defeat or surrender; ceasing to continue an effort

Examples

He emphasized to the Presiding Elder the plan of giving up the old church and moving across the river.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surrender - the act of giving up to an enemy or opponent
  • Synonym: resignation - acceptance of something undesirable but inevitable
