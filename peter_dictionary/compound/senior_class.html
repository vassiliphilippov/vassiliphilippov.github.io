senior class

1. NOUN

/ ˈsiːniər klæs / (AmE), / ˈsiːniə klɑːs / (BrE)
the group of students in their final year at a high school or college

Examples

"Almost everybody in the senior class is married ", students say dogmatically.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: junior class - students in the year prior to the final year at school or college
