before long

1. ADV

/ bɪˈfɔr lɔŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔː lɒŋ / (BrE)
In a short time from now; soon.

Examples

"He should be getting back in the groove before long.

Synset Examples

the doctor will soon be here
the book will appear shortly
she will arrive presently
we should have news before long

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soon - in a short time; quickly or promptly

2. ADV

/ bɪˈfɔr lɔŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈfɔː lɒŋ / (BrE)
In the near future; after a short time.

Examples

Before long, Andy started him on his course requirements.

Synset Examples

the doctor will soon be here
the book will appear shortly
she will arrive presently
we should have news before long

Related Terms

  • Synonym: soon - in a short time; before long
  • Synonym: presently - after a short time; soon
  • Synonym: shortly - in a short time; soon
