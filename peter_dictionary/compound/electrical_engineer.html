electrical engineer

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ɪˈlektrɪkəl ˌendʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who designs, develops, and maintains electrical systems and equipment

Examples

On June 14, 1900 the Manchester Journal reported that an electrical engineer was installing an electric light plant for Edward S. Isham at "Ormsby Hill ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: engineer - a person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or structures
