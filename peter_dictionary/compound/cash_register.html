cash register

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæʃ ˌrɛdʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˈkæʃ ˌrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
a machine used in shops and businesses for recording sales, calculating the total amount of money taken, and storing cash

Examples

Technical knowledge is a wonderful thing, but it's useless unless it eventually feeds the cash register.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: till - a drawer or box in a shop for holding money, often used as a synonym in British English

Etymology

cash + register, referring to a device that registers (records) cash transactions.

2. NOUN

/ ˈkæʃ ˌrɛdʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˈkæʃ ˌrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
A machine used in shops and stores for calculating and recording sales, and storing cash; usually equipped with an adding machine and a cash drawer.

Examples

[MUTTERS] [CASH REGISTER BELL DINGS] [LAUGHING]

Related Terms

  • Synonym: till - drawer, box, or device for keeping money in a shop
