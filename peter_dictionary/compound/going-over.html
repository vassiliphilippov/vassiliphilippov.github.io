going-over

1. NOUN

/ ˌɡoʊɪŋ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊɪŋ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
A careful and thorough examination or inspection.

Examples

He gave Mr. Jack a real going over; he brushed his shoulders and his back and his collar with long, firm strokes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: inspection - a close and critical examination
  • Synonym: scrutiny - close and detailed examination
