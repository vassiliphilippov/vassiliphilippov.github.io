bright blue

1. ADJ

/ braɪt blu / (AmE), / braɪt bluː / (BrE)
A vivid, deep blue color, somewhat purplish and similar to a clear October sky.

Examples

O' Banion was a complex and frightening man, whose bright blue eyes stared with a kind of frozen candour into others'.

Synset Examples

October's bright blue weather

Related Terms

  • Synonym: azure - a bright blue color similar to a clear sky
