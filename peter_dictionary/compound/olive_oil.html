olive oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈɑːlɪv ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ɔɪl / (BrE)
A pale yellow or greenish oil obtained from the fruit of the olive tree, used in cooking, salad dressings, and cosmetics.

Examples

They'’re olive oil importers, Don Ciccio.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: vegetable oil - oil derived from plants, used in cooking

Etymology

From olive (the fruit) + oil; 'olive' from Latin *oliva*, 'oil' from Latin *oleum*.
