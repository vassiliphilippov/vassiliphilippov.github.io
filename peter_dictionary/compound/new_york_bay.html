New York Bay

1. PROPN

/ nu ˈjɔrk beɪ / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɔːk beɪ / (BrE)
a bay at the mouth of the Hudson River, forming part of the harbor of New York City and opening into the North Atlantic Ocean

Examples

On the east side of the island of Manhattan the indifferent hawk knew the East River that connected New York Bay with Long Island Sound.
