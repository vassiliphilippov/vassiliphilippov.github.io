target area

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrɡət ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˌeəriə / (BrE)
The specific location or region that is intended to be hit or affected during an operation, attack, or activity.

Examples

An important operational procedure in BW for an enemy would be to create an aerosol or cloud of agent over the target area.
A few obvious target areas of great importance might be mentioned.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: target - an object or place that is aimed at
