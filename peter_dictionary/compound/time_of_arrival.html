time of arrival

1. NOUN

/ taɪm əv əˈraɪvəl / (AmE), / taɪm əv əˈraɪvəl / (BrE)
the scheduled or expected time at which someone or something arrives at a place, especially a public vehicle or person

Examples

By means of charts showing wave travel times and depths in the ocean at various locations, it is possible to estimate the rate of approach and probable time of arrival at Hawaii of a tsunami getting under way at any spot in the Pacific.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: arrival time - the time at which someone or something arrives
  • Antonym: time of departure - the scheduled or expected time of leaving a place
