prize money

1. NOUN

/ ˈpraɪz ˌmʌni / (AmE), / ˈpraɪz ˌmʌni / (BrE)
Money awarded as a prize in a competition, contest, or event.

Examples

He won the Masters, the United States Open and a record $ 80738 in prize money.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: award - something given as a reward for achievement
  • Synonym: winnings - money won, especially in gambling or competition
