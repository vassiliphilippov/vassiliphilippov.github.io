in passing

1. ADV

/ ɪn ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
While doing or saying something else; incidentally.

Examples

But youth asked nothing of its parents - not a touch of the hand or a kiss given in passing.

Synset Examples

he made this remark in passing

Related Terms

  • Synonym: incidentally - in a way that is not the main point; by the way
