workforce

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkˌfɔːrs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌfɔːs / (BrE)
All the people who are employed in a particular company, industry, or country.

Examples

They are segregated businesses, combining again on one site the factory and the office, drawing their work force from segregated communities.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: labor force - people available to work, especially those employed or seeking work

Etymology

Formed from 'work' + 'force'.
