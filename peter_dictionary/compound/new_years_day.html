New Year's Day

1. PROPN

/ nuː ˈjɪrz deɪ / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɪəz deɪ / (BrE)
The first day of the year, celebrated as a holiday in many countries.

Examples

He left his home the day after New Year's wearing a mackinaw and sheepskin mittens and without a hat.
This, you will remember, was still New Year's Day.

2. NOUN

/ nuː ˈjɪrz deɪ / (AmE), / njuː ˈjɪəz deɪ / (BrE)
The first day of the year, celebrated as a holiday in many countries.

Examples

Happy New Year!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: New Year - the period or celebrations marking the start of the new year

Etymology

From 'new year' + 'day', marking the start of the calendar year.
