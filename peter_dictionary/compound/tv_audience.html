TV audience

1. NOUN

/ tiː ˈviː ˈɔːdiəns / (AmE), / tiː ˈviː ˈɔːdiəns / (BrE)
the people who watch a particular television program or television in general

Examples

THE DENVER-AREA TV audience was privileged to see Mays' four home runs, thanks to a new arrangement made by Bob Howsam that the games are not to be blacked out when his Bears are playing at home.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: television viewers - people watching television
  • Antonym: radio audience - people listening to radio broadcasts
