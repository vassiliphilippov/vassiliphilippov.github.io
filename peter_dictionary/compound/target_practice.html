target practice

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɑrɡɪt ˌpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˌpræktɪs / (BrE)
the activity of shooting at targets in order to improve one's aim

Examples

During a round of target practice the sun comes from behind a cloud and dazzles the marksman, lowering his chance of a bull's-eye.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: marksmanship - skill in shooting at targets
