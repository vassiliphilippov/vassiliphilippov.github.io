belt buckle

1. NOUN

/ bɛlt ˈbʌkəl / (AmE), / bɛlt ˈbʌkəl / (BrE)
a fastening device attached to a belt, used to secure it around the waist

Examples

A second bullet ricocheted off Max's belt buckle, leaving him unhurt but in some distress.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: buckle - a clasp used for fastening two loose ends
