slow-moving

1. ADJ

/ sloʊ ˈmuːvɪŋ / (AmE), / sləʊ ˈmuːvɪŋ / (BrE)
Moving at a slow speed; not rapid or fast.

Examples

Cousin Joshua and some others felt that we should march toward Lexington and take up new positions ahead of the slow-moving British column, but another group maintained that we should stick to this spot and this section of road.

Synset Examples

slow-moving cars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slow - Moving or operating at a low speed
  • Antonym: fast-moving - Moving at a fast speed
