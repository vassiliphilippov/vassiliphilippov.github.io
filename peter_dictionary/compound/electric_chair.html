electric chair

1. NOUN

/ əˈlɛktrɪk tʃɛr / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk tʃɛː / (BrE)
A specially designed chair used as an instrument of execution by electrocution.

Examples

Mr. Christiansen knows that a soldier will get the Distinguished Service Medal for conduct that would land him in prison for life or the electric chair as a civilian.

Synset Examples

the murderer was sentenced to die in the chair

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gas chamber - an airtight room used for executing prisoners by poison gas
  • Synonym: lethal injection - a method of executing prisoners by injecting drugs
