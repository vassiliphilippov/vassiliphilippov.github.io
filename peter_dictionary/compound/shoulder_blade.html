shoulder blade

1. NOUN

/ ˈʃoʊldər bleɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə bleɪd / (BrE)
either of the two flat, triangular bones located at the back of the shoulder in humans and some animals

Examples

You 'll know when you 've made the greatest stretch because your shoulder blades will touch!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: scapula - the technical/medical term for the shoulder blade
