ice chest

1. NOUN

/ aɪs tʃɛst / (AmE), / aɪs tʃɛst / (BrE)
A container, often insulated and portable, for keeping food and drinks cold, typically by placing ice inside it.

Examples

He came home overheated, ran straight to the ice-chest, and gulped shivery cold water.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cooler - a container for keeping things cool, often used for drinks or food

Etymology

Formed from 'ice' and 'chest', referring to a box or case for storing ice or chilled items.
