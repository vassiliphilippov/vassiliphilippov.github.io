criminal record

1. NOUN

/ ˈkrɪmɪnəl ˈrekərd / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnl ˈrekɔːd / (BrE)
An official record listing a person's previous criminal convictions.

Examples

What about his prison record? - One term in Italy, one here.

Synset Examples

he ruled that the criminal record of the defendant could not be disclosed to the court
the prostitute had a record a mile long

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rap sheet - a slang term for a criminal record
