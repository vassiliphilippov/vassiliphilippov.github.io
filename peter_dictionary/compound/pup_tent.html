pup tent

1. NOUN

/ pʌp tɛnt / (AmE), / pʌp tɛnt / (BrE)
A small, wedge-shaped tent, usually low to the ground and without a floor or windows, often used for camping.

Examples

There were umbrella tents, wall tents, cottage tents, station wagon tents, pup tents, Pop tents, Baker tents, tents with exterior frames, camper trailers, travel trailers, and even a few surplus parachutes serving as sunshades over entire family camps.

Etymology

Named by analogy to a 'puppy' for its small size, first attested in American English in the late 19th century.
