dead person

1. NOUN

/ dɛd ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / dɛd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
A person who is no longer alive; a deceased individual.

Examples

It was the kind of frame that is only put around the photograph of a dead person.

Synset Examples

I wonder what the dead person would have done

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deceased - a person who has died
  • Synonym: corpse - a dead body, especially of a human
  • Antonym: living person - a person who is alive
