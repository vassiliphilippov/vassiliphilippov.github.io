strong point

1. NOUN

/ strɔŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / strɒŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a particular skill or area in which someone excels; a notable strength or asset

Examples

Analysis in roleplaying is usually done for the purpose of understanding strong and weak points of an individual or as a process to eliminate weak parts and strengthen good parts.

Synset Examples

cooking is his forte

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forte - a thing at which someone excels
  • Antonym: weak point - a particular skill or area in which someone is deficient
