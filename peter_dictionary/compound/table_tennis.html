table tennis

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪbəl ˈtɛnɪs / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl ˈtɛnɪs / (BrE)
A game similar to tennis, played on a table with small paddles and a lightweight ball.

Examples

Also, perhaps, table-tennis and other indoor sports to keep them fit and contented.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Ping-Pong - trademarked term for table tennis
  • Degree Higher: tennis - outdoor sport played with rackets and a ball on a court
