rare earth

1. NOUN

/ ˈrɛr ˌɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈreə ˌɜːθ / (BrE)
any of a group of chemically similar metallic elements with atomic numbers 57 through 71, known as the lanthanide series.

Examples

Research continues on the very complex spectra of the rare earth elements.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: lanthanide - element belonging to the lanthanide series

Etymology

Refers to their original discovery in rare minerals and their early perception as scarce.
