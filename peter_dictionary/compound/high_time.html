high time

1. NOUN

/ haɪ taɪm / (AmE), / haɪ taɪm / (BrE)
the latest possible or an overdue moment for something to happen

Examples

Anyhow, it was high time the boy was salted.

Synset Examples

it is high time you went to work

Related Terms

  • Synonym: about time - finally the appropriate or expected time
