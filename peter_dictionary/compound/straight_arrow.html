straight arrow

1. NOUN

/ ˈstreɪt ˌɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˈstreɪt ˌærəʊ / (BrE)
A person who is morally upright and follows the rules; someone considered honest and principled.

Examples

You know, the funny thing is, on the outside, I was an honest man, straight as an arrow.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: goody-goody - a person who behaves in a very correct way to gain approval
  • Antonym: crook - a dishonest person or a criminal
