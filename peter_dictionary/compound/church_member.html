church member

1. NOUN

/ tʃɝtʃ ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends or is officially associated with a church

Examples

Even the non church members - the freewheelers, marginal religionists and so on - have the values of Christian civilization internalized in them.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: churchgoer - a person who goes to church regularly
  • Synonym: parishioner - a member of a particular parish, typically of the Christian faith
