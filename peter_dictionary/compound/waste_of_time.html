waste of time

1. NOUN

/ weɪst əv taɪm / (AmE), / weɪst əv taɪm / (BrE)
An activity or period that results in no useful outcome and consumes time pointlessly.

Examples

In such diseases as cancer, tuberculosis, and heart disease, early diagnosis and treatment are so vital that the waste of time by the patient with Doctor Fraud's cure-all gadget can prove fatal.

Synset Examples

the waste of time could prove fatal

Related Terms

  • Synonym: waste - to use something in a careless or unnecessary way
  • Synonym: time-waster - a person or thing that wastes time
