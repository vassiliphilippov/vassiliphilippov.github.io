more than

1. ADJ

/ mɔr ðæn / (AmE), / mɔː ðan / (BrE)
Used to indicate a quantity, amount, or degree that is greater than a specified number or quantity.

Examples

You weigh more than 108. - Really? - Let's go.

Synset Examples

more land
more support
more rain fell
more than a gallon

Related Terms

  • Antonym: less than - a quantity or degree smaller than a specified amount
