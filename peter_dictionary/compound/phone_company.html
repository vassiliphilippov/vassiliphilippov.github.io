phone company

1. NOUN

/ foʊn ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / fəʊn ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
A company that provides telephone services to the public.

Examples

During the night, a phone company technician had deadened the bells and installed red blinkers on the phones.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: telephone company - company providing telephone services
  • Degree Higher: utility company - company supplying essential public services
