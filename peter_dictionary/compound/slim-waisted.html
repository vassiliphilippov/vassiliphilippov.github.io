slim-waisted

1. ADJ

/ slɪm ˈweɪstɪd / (AmE), / slɪm ˈweɪstɪd / (BrE)
Having a small or slender waist.

Examples

He would sit, slim-waisted and spare, on the edge of Scotty's bed, his legs crossed so elaborately that the crossed foot could tap the floor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: slender-waisted - having a slender waist
