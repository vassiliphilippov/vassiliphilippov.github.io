training program

1. NOUN

/ ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˌproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnɪŋ ˌprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
an organized course or set of activities designed to teach specific skills or knowledge, often in a workplace or educational setting
