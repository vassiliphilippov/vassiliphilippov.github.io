social status

1. NOUN

/ ˈsoʊʃl̩ ˈsteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈsteɪtəs / (BrE)
A person's standing or rank within the hierarchy of a society, often determined by factors such as wealth, occupation, education, or family background.

Examples

Members of higher and lower social status often cluster around this nucleus, so that Protestant figures on social class give the impression of spread over all social classes; but this is deceptive, for the core of membership is concentrated in a single social and economic stratum.

Synset Examples

the British are more aware of social status than Americans are

Related Terms

  • Synonym: social class - A division of a society based on social and economic status
  • Synonym: status - A person's social or professional position
