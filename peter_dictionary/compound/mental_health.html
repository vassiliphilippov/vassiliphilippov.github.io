mental health

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.t̬əl hɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl hɛlθ / (BrE)
A person's condition with regard to their psychological and emotional well-being.

Examples

Mrs. Ten Eyke did mental health.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: mental illness - a condition that affects a person's thinking, feeling, mood, or behavior
