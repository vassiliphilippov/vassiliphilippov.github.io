sweet roll

1. NOUN

/ swit roʊl / (AmE), / swiːt rəʊl / (BrE)
A small, yeast-raised roll often containing sweet ingredients such as sugar, raisins, nuts, spices, or a glaze.

Examples

Two men he did not recognize were sipping coffee and munching sweet rolls.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bun - a small bread roll, sometimes sweet
  • Synonym: pastry - a sweet baked good made of dough
