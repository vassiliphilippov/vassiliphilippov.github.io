sonny boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌni ˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌni ˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
An affectionate or familiar term used to address a male child or young man.

Formed from 'sonny', a diminutive of 'son', combined with 'boy'.
