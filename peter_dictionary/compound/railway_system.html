railway system

1. NOUN

/ ˈreɪl.weɪ ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a commercial organization and its infrastructure responsible for operating a network of trains that transport passengers or freight

Examples

For the past 40 years Congress has advocated a carefully planned, balanced and competitive railway system.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: railroad - a system of tracks and trains for transporting passengers or goods by land
