conference room

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑːn.fɚ.əns ruːm / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.fər.əns ruːm / (BrE)
a room specially designated or equipped for holding conferences, meetings, or group discussions

Examples

Roleplaying was offered as a solution - and the procedure worked as follows: all candidates were invited to a hotel conference room, where the president explained the difficulty he had, and how unnecessary it seemed to him to hire people who just did not work out.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: meeting room - a room used for meetings, especially smaller or less formal ones
