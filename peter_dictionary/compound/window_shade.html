window shade

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪndoʊ ʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ʃeɪd / (BrE)
A piece of material hung at the top of a window and pulled down to cover it, used to block light or provide privacy.

Examples

He meandered down Pike Street, past the First National Bank with its green window shades.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: window blind - a covering for a window that can be adjusted to control light
