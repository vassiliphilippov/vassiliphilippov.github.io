petit dejeuner

1. NOUN

/ pəˌtiː deɪˈʒɜːnər / (AmE), / pəˌtiː deɪˈʒɜːnə / (BrE)
A light breakfast, typically consisting of bread or a roll with coffee or tea, especially in French style or as served in continental Europe.

Examples

"Le petit dejeuner ", Harold said, in an accent that did credit to Miss Sloan, his high-school French teacher.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: continental breakfast - a light breakfast with bread, rolls, and hot drinks

Etymology

From French 'petit déjeuner' meaning 'little breakfast'.
