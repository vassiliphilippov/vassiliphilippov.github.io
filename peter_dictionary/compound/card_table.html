card table

1. NOUN

/ kɑrd ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / kɑːd ˈteɪbəl / (BrE)
A small, often portable, table designed specifically for playing cards or other games.

Examples

A man knocked the roulette ball about idly in its track, and another dozed at one of the card tables.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: table - a piece of furniture with a flat top supported by legs
