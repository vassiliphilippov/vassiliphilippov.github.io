whisky on the rocks

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɪski ɑn ðə rɑks / (AmE), / ˈwɪski ɒn ðə rɒks / (BrE)
whisky served poured over ice cubes.

Examples

Mickie sat over his second whisky-on-the-rocks in a little bar next to the funeral parlor on Pennsylvania Avenue.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: whisky neat - whisky served without ice or any mixer

Etymology

From the practice of serving whisky over ice cubes, commonly referred to as "rocks" in bar slang.
