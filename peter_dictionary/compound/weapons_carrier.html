weapons carrier

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛpənz ˈkæriər / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənz ˈkærɪə / (BrE)
a light military truck designed to transport weapons such as mortars or machine guns and their crews

Examples

A weapons carrier took Greg, Todman, Belton, Banjo Ferguson, and Walters and the others the two miles from the bivouac area to the strip.
