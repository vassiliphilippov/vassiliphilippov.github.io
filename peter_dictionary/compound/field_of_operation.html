field of operation

1. NOUN

/ ˈfiːld əv ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfiːld əv ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the particular area of business or activity in which an organization or individual is engaged

Examples

These services include procurement and technical assistance and notice of surplus sales and invitations to bid on Government contracts for products and services within the registrants' field of operations.

Synset Examples

they are outstanding in their field

Related Terms

  • Synonym: field - an area of activity or interest
