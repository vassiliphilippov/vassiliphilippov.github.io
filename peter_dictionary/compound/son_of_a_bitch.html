son of a bitch

1. NOUN

/ sʌn əv ə bɪtʃ / (AmE), / sʌn əv ə bɪtʃ / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar term used to insult or express annoyance at someone regarded as obnoxious, stupid, or detestable.
