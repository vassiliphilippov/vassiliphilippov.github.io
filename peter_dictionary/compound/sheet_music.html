sheet music

1. NOUN

/ ʃit ˈmjuː.zɪk / (AmE), / ʃiːt ˈmjuː.zɪk / (BrE)
Printed or written music notation that shows how a musical piece should be performed.

Examples

For example, there was sheet music with the word "jazz "in the title, to illustrate how a word of uncertain origin took hold.

Synset Examples

she turned the pages of the music as he played

Related Terms

  • Synonym: score - the written or printed form of a musical composition, especially for an orchestra
