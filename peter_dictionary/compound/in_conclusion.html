in conclusion

1. ADV

/ ɪn kənˈkluːʒən / (AmE), / ɪn kənˈkluːʒən / (BrE)
Used to introduce the final point or summary in speech or writing.

Examples

In conclusion, these findings suggest the need for a comparative physiology, pathology, and histology of mammalian lungs.
