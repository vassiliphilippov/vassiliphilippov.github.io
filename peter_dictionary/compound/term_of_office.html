term of office

1. NOUN

/ tɝm əv ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / tɜːm əv ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
The period during which a person holds a particular official position or job, especially in government.

Examples

The mayor's present term of office expires Jan. 1.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenure - the period during which someone holds a position or office
