at random

1. ADV

/ æt ˈrændəm / (AmE), / ət ˈrændəm / (BrE)
Without any specific pattern, order, or purpose; by chance.

Examples

Each trial consists of choosing a student manager at random.

Synset Examples

the houses were randomly scattered
bullets were fired into the crowd at random

Related Terms

  • Synonym: randomly - in a random manner; without pattern or order
