sports editor

1. NOUN

/ ˈspɔrts ˌɛdɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌedɪtə / (BrE)
the editor of a newspaper or magazine who is responsible for sports news and related content

Examples

The trio who shared the most-valuable honors were introduced by Bob Broeg, sports editor of the Post-Dispatch.
Bob Burnes, sports editor of the Globe-Democrat, presented Bob Nordmann with his award.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: editor - person who prepares content for publication
