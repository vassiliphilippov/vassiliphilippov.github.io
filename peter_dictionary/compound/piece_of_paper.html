piece of paper

1. NOUN

/ pis əv ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / piːs əv ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
A single, discrete sheet of paper used for writing or printing.

Examples

Some time in 1912, Picasso cut out and folded a piece of paper in the shape of a guitar; to this he glued and fitted other pieces of paper and four taut strings, thus creating a sequence of flat surfaces in real and sculptural space to which there clung only the vestige of a picture plane.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sheet of paper - a single piece of paper, especially for writing or printing
  • Degree Lower: scrap of paper - a small or leftover piece of paper, often not used for official writing
