suspended animation

1. NOUN

/ səˈspɛndɪd ˌænɪˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / səˈspɛndɪd ˌænɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
A temporary state in which someone's vital functions stop or are slowed to the point of resembling death, often with loss of consciousness; may result from asphyxia or be induced in scientific and science fiction contexts.

Examples

Here they would lie in suspended animation for many years.
And the crew would be automatically brought out of their suspended animation.
