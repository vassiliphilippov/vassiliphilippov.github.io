idle talk

1. NOUN

/ ˈaɪdəl tɑk / (AmE), / ˈaɪdəl tɔːk / (BrE)
talk that is trivial, foolish, or irrelevant

Examples

There was some idle talk, a listless discussion of this or that small happening during the day's drive.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chatter - talk that is quick, trivial, or meaningless
  • Synonym: gossip - casual or unconstrained conversation about other people
  • Synonym: prattle - to talk at length in a foolish or inconsequential way
