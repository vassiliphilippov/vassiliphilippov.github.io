hundred dollar bill

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌndrəd ˈdɑlər bɪl / (AmE), / ˈhʌndrəd ˈdɒlə bɪl / (BrE)
A United States banknote with a value of one hundred dollars.

Examples

In one hand she clutched a hundred dollar bill and in the other a straw suitcase.
Perhaps he had known then where that hundred dollar bill had come from and where it was taking his wife.

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: fifty dollar bill - a United States bill worth 50 dollars
  • Degree Lower: twenty dollar bill - a United States bill worth 20 dollars
