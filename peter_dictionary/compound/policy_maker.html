policy maker

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɑləsi ˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɒləsi ˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is responsible for making important plans or decisions, especially in government, business, or other organizations

Examples

And I suggest further that the main cause of the trouble we are in has been the failure of American policy-makers, ever since we assumed free world leadership in 1945, to deal with this problem realistically and seriously.

Synset Examples

policy makers often make the right decision for the wrong reason

Related Terms

  • Synonym: decision maker - a person who makes important decisions
