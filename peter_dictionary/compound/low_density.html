low density

1. NOUN

/ loʊ ˈdɛnsəti / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈdɛnsɪti / (BrE)
a state or quality of having relatively little mass or matter per unit volume; a low concentration

Examples

To what extent such low density applies to micrometeorites is unknown.

Synset Examples

the tenuity of the upper atmosphere

Related Terms

  • Synonym: rarity - the state of being rare or uncommon; low frequency or low density
  • Antonym: high density - the state of having a great mass per unit volume; high concentration
