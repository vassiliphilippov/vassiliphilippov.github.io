family name

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli neɪm / (AmE), / ˈfæməli neɪm / (BrE)
The part of a person's name that shows which family they belong to, as opposed to their given name; a surname.

Examples

In the hopes of clearing my family name and in the sincere desire to give my children their fair share of the American way of life, without a blemish on their name and background, I have appeared before this committee and given it all the cooperation in my power.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: surname - the hereditary name shared by members of a family
  • Antonym: given name - the personal name given to an individual at birth or baptism, as opposed to a family name
  • Synonym: last name - the final name in a person's full name, usually the family name
