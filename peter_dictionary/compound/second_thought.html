second thought

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛkənd θɔt / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd θɔːt / (BrE)
An act of reconsidering a decision or opinion previously formed.

Examples

He hit the theme about dying to defend your country, and you were ready to do it right then, without a second thought.

Synset Examples

he had second thoughts about his purchase

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reconsideration - the act of considering something again
