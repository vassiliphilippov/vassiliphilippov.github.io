floor covering

1. NOUN

/ flɔr ˈkʌvɚɪŋ / (AmE), / flɔː ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
A material or layer used to cover a floor for purposes of comfort, decoration, or protection, such as carpet, tile, or vinyl.

Examples

Makes drawings and plans of rooms showing placement of furniture, floor coverings, wall decorations, and determines color schemes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: carpet - A heavy fabric covering for floors, often woven or tufted
  • Synonym: rug - A piece of thick heavy fabric for covering part of a floor
  • Synonym: flooring - Permanent covering of a floor, often of wood, tile, or another hard surface
