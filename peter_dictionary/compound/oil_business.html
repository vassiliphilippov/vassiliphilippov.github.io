oil business

1. NOUN

/ ɔɪl ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈbɪznəs / (BrE)
The industry involved in the production, processing, and distribution of oil and oil products.

Examples

He got in the oil business out at Odessa and lucked into some money ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: oil industry - the sector involved in oil production and distribution
  • Synonym: petroleum industry - the sector concerned with exploration, extraction, refining, transporting, and marketing petroleum products
