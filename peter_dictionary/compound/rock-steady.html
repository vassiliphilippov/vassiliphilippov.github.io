rock-steady

1. ADJ

/ rɑk ˈstɛdi / (AmE), / rɒk ˈstɛdi / (BrE)
Consistently reliable and stable in behavior or performance.

Examples

Even rock-steady Anne - Hell, Anne was the worst!

Synset Examples

dependable in one's habits
a steady-going family man

Related Terms

  • Synonym: dependable - able to be relied on; trustworthy
  • Antonym: unreliable - not able to be trusted or depended on
