blue-ribbon

1. ADJ

/ blu ˈrɪb.ən / (AmE), / bluː ˈrɪb.ən / (BrE)
(of a group, event, or panel) selected for having special qualifications, importance, or distinction.

Examples

State and federal legislation against racial discrimination in employment was called for yesterday in a report of a "blue ribbon "citizens committee on the aid to dependent children program.

Synset Examples

the blue-ribbon event of the season

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distinguished - recognized for excellence or importance
  • Synonym: prestigious - having high status or honor

Etymology

Refers to the blue ribbon traditionally awarded for first place or high honor, especially at competitions.
