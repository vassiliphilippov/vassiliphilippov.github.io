free of charge

1. ADV

/ fri əv tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / fri əv tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
Without requiring any payment; at no cost.

Examples

Nearly free of charge.

Synset Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: gratis - without charge; free
  • Synonym: complimentary - given free of charge
  • Antonym: paid - requiring payment
