on-street

1. ADJ

/ ɑn ˈstriːt / (AmE), / ɒn ˈstriːt / (BrE)
Located or occurring at the sides of, or on, public streets, especially for vehicles or activities (such as parking).

Examples

My brother Sonny found Tom Hagen in the street.

Synset Examples

on-street parking is prohibited at rush hour

Related Terms

  • Antonym: off-street - located away from public streets, especially of parking
