every so often

1. ADV

/ ˈɛvri soʊ ˈɔfən / (AmE), / ˈɛvri səʊ ˈɒfən / (BrE)
Occasionally; at intervals; now and then.

Examples

Teeth again flashing back at me, the driver released a deluge of Spanish in which "amigo "appeared every so often like an island in the stormy waves of surrounding sound.

Synset Examples

every so often she visits her father

Related Terms

  • Synonym: occasionally - at irregular or infrequent intervals; now and then
  • Degree Higher: frequently - at frequent intervals or often
  • Degree Lower: rarely - not often; seldom
