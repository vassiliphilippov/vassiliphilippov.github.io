good morning

1. NOUN

/ ɡʊd ˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
A conventional expression used to greet someone in the morning.

Examples

Good morning, Portland Daily Bugle.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: good night - expression used as a farewell at night
  • Degree Higher: good afternoon - expression used to greet someone in the afternoon
  • Degree Higher: good evening - expression used to greet someone in the evening
