small arm

1. NOUN

/ smɔl ɑrm / (AmE), / smɔːl ɑːm / (BrE)
A portable firearm designed to be carried and operated by a single person, such as a rifle, pistol, or revolver, typically distinguished from heavier weaponry like artillery or mounted guns.

Examples

And millions of rounds of entirely new and modern small-arms ammunition, designed for today's hunting and target shooting.
Amid the crackle of small arms and automatic weapons, I heard the thumping of mortars.

Synset Examples

he wore his firearm in a shoulder holster

Related Terms

  • Synonym: firearm - a gun, especially a portable one such as a pistol or rifle

Etymology

From 'small' (not large) + 'arm' (weapon); 'arm' from Latin 'arma' meaning weapons.
