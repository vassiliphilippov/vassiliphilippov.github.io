enlisted man

1. NOUN

/ ɪnˈlɪstɪd mæn / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɪstɪd man / (BrE)
A male member of the armed forces who holds a rank below commissioned officer.

Examples

From the enlisted men he pistol-whipped to the subordinate officer whose wife he tried to rape, a lot of men had plenty of reason heartily to dislike Marcus Reno.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enlisted person - a non-commissioned member of the armed forces, regardless of gender
  • Antonym: officer - a member of the armed forces with a commission, holding a position of authority

2. NOUN

/ ɛnˈlɪstɪd mæn / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɪstɪd mæn / (BrE)
A male member of the armed forces who holds a rank below commissioned officers and is not a warrant officer.

Examples

Later on he had an idea to build a city out of a desert stop-over for G.I.s on the way to the West Coast.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: enlisted woman - female member of the armed forces below commissioned officers
  • Antonym: officer - member of the armed forces with a commissioned rank
