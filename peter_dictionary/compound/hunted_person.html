hunted person

1. NOUN

/ ˈhʌntɪd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is being actively pursued or chased, especially with the intention of capture or harm

Examples

I'm the hunted one!

Related Terms

  • Synonym: fugitive - a person who is fleeing from custody or danger
  • Antonym: pursuer - a person who is chasing someone
