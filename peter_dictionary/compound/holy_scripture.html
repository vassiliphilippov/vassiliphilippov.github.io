Holy Scripture

1. NOUN

/ ˌhoʊli ˈskrɪptʃɚ / (AmE), / ˌhəʊli ˈskrɪptʃə / (BrE)
The sacred writings of Christianity; the Bible.

Examples

Unless God expected a man to believe the Holy Scriptures, why had He given them to him?

Synset Examples

he went to carry the Word to the heathen

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Bible - The Christian scriptures consisting of the Old and New Testaments

Etymology

From Latin 'scriptura sacra' meaning 'sacred writing'.
