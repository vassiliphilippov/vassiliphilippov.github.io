management personnel

1. NOUN

/ ˈmænɪdʒmənt ˌpɝːsəˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒmənt ˌpɜːsəˈnel / (BrE)
Employees who are responsible for planning, directing, and overseeing the operations and staff of an organization.

Examples

Technical assistance in training middle - and upper-level management personnel is still needed in many cases.
Does your company have a program for selecting and developing sales and marketing management personnel for the longer term?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: manager - a person responsible for controlling or administering an organization or group of staff
