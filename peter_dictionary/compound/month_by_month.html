month by month

1. ADV

/ mʌnθ baɪ mʌnθ / (AmE), / mʌnθ baɪ mʌnθ / (BrE)
gradually over each successive month; as each month passes

Examples

Day by day, week by week, month by month, the betrayal gnawed at Andrei's heart.

Synset Examples

month by month, the betrayal gnawed at his heart

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day by day - as each day passes; gradually day after day
