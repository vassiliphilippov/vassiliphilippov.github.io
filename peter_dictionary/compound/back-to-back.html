back-to-back

1. ADJ

/ ˌbæk tə ˈbæk / (AmE), / ˌbæk tə ˈbæk / (BrE)
Following one after the other, with no interruption in between.

Examples

By the power vested in me by the state of Maine, I hereby order you to serve two life sentences, back to back.

Synset Examples

back-to-back home runs

Related Terms

  • Synonym: consecutive - following one after another without interruption
