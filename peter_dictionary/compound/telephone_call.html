telephone call

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn kɔl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn kɔːl / (BrE)
an act of making or receiving a connection by telephone to speak with someone

Examples

During the election campaign, both candidates, Davis and Bush, reportedly received anonymous telephone calls.
The manager of a movie theater received a telephone call from a woman who was equally indefinite.

Synset Examples

she reported several anonymous calls
he placed a phone call to London
he heard the phone ringing but didn't want to take the call

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phone call - an informal term for a telephone call
