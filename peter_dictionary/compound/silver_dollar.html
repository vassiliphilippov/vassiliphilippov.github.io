silver dollar

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɪlvɚ ˈdɑlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
a coin worth one dollar, formerly minted in silver, especially in the United States

Examples

It had a festering hole as big as a silver dollar.

Etymology

silver from Old English 'seolfor' + dollar from German 'Thaler', a large silver coin.
