working girl

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɝːkɪŋ ˌɡɝːl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ ˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
A young woman who has a job, especially an office worker.

Examples

The Jewish working girl almost invariably works in an office - in contradistinction to gentile factory workers - and, buttressed by a respectable income, she is likely to dress better and live more expansively than the college student.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: career woman - A woman whose main priority in life is achieving success in her career or profession.
