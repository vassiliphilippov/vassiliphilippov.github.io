transit line

1. NOUN

/ ˈtrænzɪt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈtrænzɪt laɪn / (BrE)
A route along which public transportation vehicles regularly operate.

Examples

Department stores congregated in the "one hundred per cent location ", where all the transit lines converged.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bus route - a route along which a bus travels
  • Synonym: rail line - a railway used for passenger or freight trains
