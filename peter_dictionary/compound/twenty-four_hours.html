twenty-four hours

1. NOUN

/ ˌtwɛnti fɔr ˈaʊɚz / (AmE), / ˌtwɛnti fɔː ˈaʊəz / (BrE)
A period of time equal to one full day; the time it takes for the Earth to complete one rotation on its axis.

Examples

"He is aware he will not live for twenty-four hours if he betrays you ".
Twenty-four hours had changed him from a performer to a freak.

Synset Examples

two days later they left
they put on two performances every day
there are 30,000 passengers per day

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day - a period of twenty-four hours
