stuck-up

1. ADJ

/ stʌk ˈʌp / (AmE), / stʌk ˈʌp / (BrE)
Having or showing an attitude that you think you are better or more important than other people; conceited or arrogant.

Examples

A Mississippi soldier wrote: "Our General Reub Davis is a vain, stuck-up, illiterate ass ".

Synset Examples

a snotty little scion of a degenerate family
they're snobs--stuck-up and uppity and persnickety

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snobbish - having or showing the attitude of people who think they are better than others
  • Antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
