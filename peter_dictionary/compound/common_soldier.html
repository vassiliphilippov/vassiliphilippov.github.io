common soldier

1. NOUN

/ ˈkɑmən ˈsoʊldʒər / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
an enlisted member of the army or marines of the lowest rank; a private

Examples

The letters of the common soldiers are rich in humor.

Synset Examples

our prisoner was just a private and knew nothing of value

Related Terms

  • Synonym: private - enlisted person of the lowest military rank
