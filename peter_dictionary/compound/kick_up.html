kick up

1. NOUN

/ kɪk ʌp / (AmE), / kɪk ʌp / (BrE)
A gymnastic movement in which a person lifts their legs backward and upward while placing their hands on the ground, typically the initial action of performing a handstand.

Examples

At first each child should do a kick up by himself so that the teacher can determine those ready to work alone, and those who need help.
