tilt-top table

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɪlt tɑp ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪlt tɒp ˈteɪbl / (BrE)
A small table with a hinged top that can be tilted to a vertical position for easy storage.

Examples

In my studio I work at a tilt-top table, but leave the paper unfixed so that I can move it freely to control the washes.
