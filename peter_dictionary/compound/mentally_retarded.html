mentally retarded

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛn.t̬əl.i rɪˈtɑːr.dɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təli rɪˈtɑː.dɪd / (BrE)
(offensive, archaic) People collectively who have significantly below-average intellectual functioning; now considered offensive and replaced by terms such as 'people with intellectual disabilities'.

Examples

Without dissent, senators passed a bill by Sen. A. R. Schwartz of Galveston authorizing establishment in the future of a school for the mentally retarded in the Gulf Coast district.

Synset Examples

he started a school for the retarded

Related Terms

  • Synonym: intellectually disabled - people collectively with intellectual disabilities (current, non-offensive term)
