combination in restraint of trade

1. NOUN

/ ˌkɑːmbəˈneɪʃən ɪn rɪˈstreɪnt əv treɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən ɪn rɪˈstreɪnt əv treɪd / (BrE)
A monopoly, contract, partnership, or conspiracy intended to restrict commerce or limit economic competition, especially when such actions are illegal under United States antitrust law.

Examples

The latter provisions proscribe any contract, combination, or conspiracy in restraint of interstate or foreign trade, and monopolization of, or attempts, combinations, or conspiracies to monopolize, such trade.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: restraint of trade - an action or agreement restricting free competition in business
  • Synonym: monopoly - exclusive control by one group of the means of producing or selling a commodity or service
