wrapped up

1. ADJ

/ ræpt ʌp / (AmE), / ræpt ʌp / (BrE)
Fully occupied or deeply engrossed in something; giving all one's attention or devotion to a person, activity, or thing.

Examples

The key to effective marketing is wrapped up in defining your company's marketing problems realistically.

Synset Examples

bound up in her teaching
is wrapped up in his family

Related Terms

  • Synonym: absorbed - deeply interested or involved; preoccupied
  • Synonym: engrossed - completely occupied or involved in
