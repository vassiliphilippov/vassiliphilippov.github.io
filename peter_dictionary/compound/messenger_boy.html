messenger boy

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɛsəndʒɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪndʒə bɔɪ / (BrE)
A boy employed to carry messages or run errands for an employer or business.

Examples

During the summers, while he was still in school, Mercer worked for his father's firm as a messenger boy.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messenger - a person who carries messages or runs errands

2. NOUN

/ ˈmɛsəndʒɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈmesəndʒə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy employed to deliver messages or run errands, often as part of his job

Examples

Your father did business with Hyman Roth, your father respected Hyman Roth, but your father never trusted Hyman Roth or his Sicilian messenger boy, Johnny Ola.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: messenger - person who carries messages
