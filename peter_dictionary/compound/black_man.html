Black man

1. NOUN

/ blæk mæn / (AmE), / blak man / (BrE)
A man who is Black, especially of African descent.

Examples

The Deacon Board, headed by a black man named Carlson, had practically taken over as the pastor grew old, and had its way with the support of the Amen corner.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: Black woman - a female person who is Black
  • Antonym: white man - a male person who is white
