newspaper columnist

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuːzˌpeɪpər ˈkɑːləmnɪst / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzpeɪpə ˈkɒləmnɪst / (BrE)
A journalist who regularly writes a column for a newspaper, typically offering opinions or commentary.

Examples

a former Democratic candidate for President, a New Deal poet, the magazine's chief editorial writer, two newspaper columnists, head of a national broadcasting company, a popular Protestant evangelist, etc..

Related Terms

  • Synonym: columnist - a person who writes regular articles for a publication
