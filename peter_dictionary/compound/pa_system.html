PA system

1. NOUN

/ piː ˈeɪ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / piː ˈeɪ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
An electronic sound amplification and distribution system used to make announcements or play music in public areas.

Examples

Andy told him, "Bake, I wish you 'd talk to Skolman, see if some kind of p. a. system can be rigged up outside.
Did n't they tell you what I wanted the p. a. system for "?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: megaphone - a handheld device for amplifying the voice

Etymology

Abbreviation of 'public address system'.
