Sunday school

1. NOUN

/ ˈsʌn.deɪ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.deɪ skuːl / (BrE)
A class or school, usually held on Sundays, for religious instruction, typically for children and young people.

Examples

"We have nine hundred and eleven members in our charge ", Mama announced, "and three hundred and eighty Sunday-school scholars ".
