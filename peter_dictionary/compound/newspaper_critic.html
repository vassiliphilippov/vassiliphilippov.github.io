newspaper critic

1. NOUN

/ ˈnuzˌpeɪpər ˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
A critic who regularly writes reviews or columns for newspapers, often in areas such as the arts, literature, or current events.

Examples

One might argue that the ultimate purpose of literary scholarship is to correct this spontaneous provincialism that is likely to obscure the horizons of the general public, of the newspaper critic, and of the creative artist himself.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: reviewer - a person who writes reviews for publications or broadcasts
