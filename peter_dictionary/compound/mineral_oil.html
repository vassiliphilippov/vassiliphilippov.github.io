mineral oil

1. NOUN

/ ˈmɪnərəl ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl ɔɪl / (BrE)
A clear, colorless oil derived from the distillation of petroleum, used in medicine as a laxative and in industry as a lubricant or moisturizer.

Examples

The round cells were reproducibly positioned in the light beam which entered the thermostated mineral oil bath through a window.
