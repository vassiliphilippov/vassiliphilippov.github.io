electric motor

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
A machine that converts electrical energy into mechanical motion.

Examples

Well, it was a little car with an electric motor that he can ride in.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: motor - a machine that supplies motive power for a vehicle or other device
