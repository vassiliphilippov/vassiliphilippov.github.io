fingerprint expert

1. NOUN

/ ˈfɪŋɡɚˌprɪnt ˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌprɪnt ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
a person who is specially trained in the identification and analysis of fingerprints, especially for purposes such as forensic investigation and criminal identification.

Examples

He adjusted the hat, lifted the horn to his lips as though it were a flute, and fell in alongside our fingerprint expert to cavort with him.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: forensic scientist - an expert in applying scientific methods to solve crimes
  • Synonym: detective - someone whose job is to discover information about crimes
