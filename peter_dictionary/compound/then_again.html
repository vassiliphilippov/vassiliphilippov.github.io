then again

1. ADV

/ ðɛn əˈɡɛn / (AmE), / ðen əˈɡen / (BrE)
used to introduce a contrasting point of view or a different possibility

Examples

"I may be back ", he explained, "and then again, I may not ".
"Then again he might not ".
And then again perhaps the reason why he could n't find time to do any of the things he had planned to do after retirement: reading, roaming, gardening, lying on his back and watching the clouds go by, was because he did n't want to do them.

Synset Examples

on the other hand, she is too ambitious for her own good
then again, she might not go

Related Terms

  • Synonym: on the other hand - introducing an alternative or contrasting point
