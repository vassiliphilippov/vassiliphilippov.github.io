bromophenol blue

1. NOUN

/ ˌbroʊmoʊfəˌnoʊl ˈblu / (AmE), / ˌbrəʊməʊfiːˌnəʊl ˈbluː / (BrE)
A synthetic dye used as an acid-base indicator, especially in laboratory procedures.

Examples

Five milliamperes / cell were applied for 18 hr, after which the strips were stained with bromophenol blue and densitometry was carried out using a Spinco Analytrol.

Etymology

From 'bromo-' (referring to the presence of bromine) and 'phenol blue' (a phenolic compound used as a dye).
