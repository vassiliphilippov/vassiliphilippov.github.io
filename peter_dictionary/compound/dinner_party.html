dinner party

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɪnər ˌpɑrti / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
A social gathering where guests are invited to have dinner together, often in a private home.

Examples

The lawyer did n't know him very well although he saw him occasionally at some dinner party - Thayer, like himself, Madden reflected, was the extra man so prized by hostesses - and found him easy enough to talk to.

Synset Examples

guests should never be late to a dinner party

Related Terms

  • Synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests
