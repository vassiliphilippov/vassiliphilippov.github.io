creep feed

1. NOUN

/ krip fid / (AmE), / kriːp fiːd / (BrE)
Feed specially formulated and provided to young animals placed in a creep, an enclosure that restricts access to adult animals.

Examples

It may be incorporated into cattle creep feeds in levels from 1.0 to 1.5 milligrams of diethylstilbestrol per pound of feed.
