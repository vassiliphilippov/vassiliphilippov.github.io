publicity man

1. NOUN

/ pəˈblɪsəti mæn / (AmE), / pəˈblɪsəti man / (BrE)
A man employed to arrange and manage publicity for an organization, company, or public figure.

Examples

A Southerner married to a New Englander, I have lived for many years in a Connecticut commuting town with a high percentage of artists, writers, publicity men, and business executives of egghead tastes.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: press agent - a person employed to arrange publicity
  • Synonym: publicist - a person responsible for generating publicity
