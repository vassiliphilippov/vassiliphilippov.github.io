part of speech

1. NOUN

/ pɑrt əv spiːtʃ / (AmE), / pɑːt əv spiːtʃ / (BrE)
any of the classes into which words in a language are divided according to their grammatical function, such as noun, verb, adjective, etc.

Examples

All the other parts of speech: nouns, pronouns, adjectives, adverbs, and conjunctions operated under the same system as the verbs.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: word class - a category of words with similar grammatical properties

Etymology

From Latin 'pars orationis' (part of speech).
