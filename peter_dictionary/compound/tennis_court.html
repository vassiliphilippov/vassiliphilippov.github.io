tennis court

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛnɪs kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs kɔːt / (BrE)
A rectangular area marked with lines and equipped with a net, on which tennis is played.

Examples

They are definitely in the same room with you, but your head starts to swing as though you were sitting on the very edge of a tennis court watching a spirited volley.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: court - an area marked out for ball games such as tennis or basketball
