mother-naked

1. ADJ

/ ˈmʌðər ˌneɪkəd / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə ˌneɪkɪd / (BrE)
Completely naked, as one was at birth.

Examples

I knew better but I was thinking of the Pedersen kid mother-naked in all that dough.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: bare - Not clothed or covered; naked
  • Synonym: stark naked - Completely without clothes

Etymology

Formed from 'mother' + 'naked', alluding to the state of being born.
