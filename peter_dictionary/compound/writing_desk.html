writing desk

1. NOUN

/ ˈraɪtɪŋ dɛsk / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ dɛsk / (BrE)
a desk, often with a flat or sloping surface, designed for writing and typically fitted with drawers or compartments for storing papers and writing materials

Examples

Save for brief periods in garrison or winter quarters, soldiers rarely enjoyed the luxury of a writing desk or table.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: desk - a piece of furniture with a flat or sloping surface for writing, reading, or using a computer
