bobby pin

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːbi pɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɒbi pɪn / (BrE)
A small, flat hairpin made of wire, with prongs that press tightly together, used to hold hair in place.

Examples

Try pegging down each separate branch to the earth, using a bobby pin to hold it there.

Synset Examples

in Britain they call a bobby pin a grip

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hairpin - a device for fastening hair
  • Synonym: hair grip - British term for a bobby pin

Etymology

Likely from 'bobbed' (short) hairstyle and 'pin'.
