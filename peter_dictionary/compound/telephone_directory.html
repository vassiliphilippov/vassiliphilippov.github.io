telephone directory

1. NOUN

/ ˈtɛləˌfoʊn dəˈrɛktəri / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn dɪˈrɛktəri / (BrE)
a book or online listing that provides an alphabetical list of telephone subscribers and their telephone numbers

Examples

As soon as he could think logically again he reached for the telephone directory and found Jerry Burton's home number.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: phonebook - a book listing names, addresses, and phone numbers of subscribers
