man in the street

1. NOUN

/ mæn ɪn ðə strit / (AmE), / mæn ɪn ðə striːt / (BrE)
an ordinary or typical person; the average member of the public

Examples

Yet nationalism has lost few of its charms for the historian, writer or man in the street.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: everyman - an ordinary or typical person
  • Synonym: average joe - an ordinary or typical man
  • Synonym: woman in the street - an ordinary or typical woman; counterpart to 'man in the street'
