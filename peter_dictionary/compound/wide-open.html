wide-open

1. ADJ

/ waɪd ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / waɪd ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
Completely open; with the widest possible opening.

Examples

When painting, Mason's physical eyes are half closed, while his mind's eye is wide open, and this circumstance accounts in part for the impression he wishes to convey.

Synset Examples

left the doors wide-open

Related Terms

  • Degree Lower: open - not closed or blocked; allowing access
