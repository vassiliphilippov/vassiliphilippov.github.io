exotic dancer

1. NOUN

/ ɪɡˈzɑtɪk ˈdænsər / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪk ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
A performer who entertains an audience by dancing erotically and removing some or all of their clothing, typically to music.

Examples

Among the spectators was the noted exotic dancer, Patti Waggin who is Mrs. Don Rudolph when off the stage.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: stripper - a performer who undresses as entertainment
