body armor

1. NOUN

/ ˈbɑːdi ˌɑːrmər / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌɑːmə / (BrE)
Protective clothing or covering designed to shield the wearer's body from injury, typically in military, law enforcement, or security contexts.

Examples

Some kind of body armor.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: armor - protective covering for the body, especially in combat
