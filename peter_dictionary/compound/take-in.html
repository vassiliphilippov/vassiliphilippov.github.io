take-in

1. NOUN

/ ˈteɪk ɪn / (AmE), / ˈteɪk ɪn / (BrE)
an act of deceiving or cheating someone; a trick or swindle

Examples

What amazes me most is you were taken in by it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: deception - the act of deceiving someone
  • Synonym: swindle - an instance of cheating or trickery for personal gain
