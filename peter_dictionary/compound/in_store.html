in store

1. ADJ

/ ɪn stɔɹ / (AmE), / ɪn stɔː / (BrE)
(of something) awaiting or ready to happen or be experienced in the future.

Examples

If competition beats you to it, this exciting new product era can have real headaches in store.

Synset Examples

gave us a hint of the excitement that was in store for us

Related Terms

  • Synonym: in reserve - kept back for future use
  • Synonym: in stock - available for immediate use or sale
