house of prostitution

1. NOUN

/ ˈhaʊs əv ˌprɑːstəˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs əv ˌprɒstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
A building where prostitutes offer sexual services to clients.

Examples

"It's a house of prostitution for white men with white girls as inmates.
This, of course, was the sort of thing that used to take place in Southern cities - putting white houses of prostitution colored girls in colored neighborhoods and carrying them on openly.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: brothel - a building where prostitutes are available to clients
  • Synonym: whorehouse - a slang term for a house of prostitution
