university student

1. NOUN

/ juˈnɪvərsəti ˈstuːdənt / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəsəti ˈstjuːdənt / (BrE)
A person who is enrolled in and attends a university to study for a degree or qualification.

Examples

From the town surveyor, Hans learned drawing and mathematics and, from a university student, some academic subjects.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: college student - student enrolled in a college or university
