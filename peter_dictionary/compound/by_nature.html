by nature

1. ADV

/ baɪ ˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / baɪ ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
used to describe someone's inherent qualities or character; inherently, innately.

Examples

Corporations are apt by nature to be impersonal, inhumane, shortsighted and almost exclusively profit motivated, a picture they could scarcely afford to present to the public.

Synset Examples

he was naturally lazy

Related Terms

  • Synonym: naturally - by inherent quality or character
