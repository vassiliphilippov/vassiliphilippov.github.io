wild man

1. NOUN

/ waɪld mæn / (AmE), / waɪld man / (BrE)
A man who behaves in a very uncontrolled, uncivilized, or energetic way, often appearing rough or untamed.

Examples

Then they let out a bellow of anguish and rage and, cursing, screaming and hollering "Garryowen "! they charged into the enemy like wild men.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: savage - a person regarded as primitive, brutal, or uncivilized
  • Synonym: barbarian - an uncivilized or primitive person
  • Antonym: gentleman - a man who is polite and well-mannered
