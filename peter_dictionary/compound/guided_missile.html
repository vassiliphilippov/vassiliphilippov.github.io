guided missile

1. NOUN

/ ˈɡaɪdɪd ˌmɪsaɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪdɪd ˌmɪsaɪl / (BrE)
A rocket-propelled weapon whose route can be directed during flight using radio signals or internal guidance systems.

Examples

The same delivery vehicles - whether they be airplanes, submarines or guided missiles - should be usable.
