starting point

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑrtɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the place, time, or situation at which something begins or from which something can develop

Examples

Yet he never allowed these models to become fixed in his mind; they remained rough starting points.
The Congress of Vienna is a convenient starting point because it both epitomized and symbolized what was to follow.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: end point - the place, time, or situation at which something ends
  • Synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins or is created
