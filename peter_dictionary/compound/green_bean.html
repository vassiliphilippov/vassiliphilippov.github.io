green bean

1. NOUN

/ ɡrin bin / (AmE), / ɡriːn biːn / (BrE)
A long, slender, unripe bean pod eaten as a vegetable.

Examples

There's no greater catastrophe in the universe, she reflected dourly, impaling tender green beans on the silver fork, than the dwindling away of a family.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: snap bean - another term for green bean, especially in American English
  • Synonym: string bean - another common term for green beans, especially those with a fibrous string
