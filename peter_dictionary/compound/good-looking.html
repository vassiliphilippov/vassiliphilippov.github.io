good-looking

1. ADJ

/ ˈɡʊd ˌlʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊd ˌlʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
Physically attractive; having a pleasing appearance, especially by conventional standards.

Examples

Mr. Skyros did not at all like the look on Angelo's regular featured, almost girlishly good-looking face - or indeed anything about Angelo.
He'’s good-looking, isn'’t he?

Synset Examples

a fine-looking woman

our southern women are well-favored

Related Terms

  • Synonym: handsome - attractive in a strong or striking way, especially of men
  • Synonym: beautiful - pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically
  • Antonym: ugly - unpleasant or repulsive in appearance
