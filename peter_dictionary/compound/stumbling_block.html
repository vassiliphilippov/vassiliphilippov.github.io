stumbling block

1. NOUN

/ ˈstʌmblɪŋ ˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈstʌmblɪŋ ˌblɒk / (BrE)
An obstacle or hindrance that prevents progress or makes something more difficult to achieve.

Examples

The statement is often made that when Bultmann argues in this way, he "overestimates the intellectual stumbling-block which myth is supposed to put in the way of accepting the Christian faith ".

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obstacle - something that blocks one's way or makes progress difficult
  • Synonym: impediment - something that hinders or obstructs
