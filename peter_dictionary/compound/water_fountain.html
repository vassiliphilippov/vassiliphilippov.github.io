water fountain

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɔtər ˌfaʊntn̩ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌfaʊntən / (BrE)
A device in a public place that provides a jet of water for people to drink from, usually by pressing a button or lever.

Examples

Phil Rossoff, coming in from left field, stopped at the water fountain for a drink.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: drinking fountain - a public fountain that provides drinking water
