obsessive-compulsive

1. ADJ

/ əbˌsɛsɪv kəmˈpʌlsɪv / (AmE), / əbˌsɛsɪv kəmˈpʌlsɪv / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by recurring, uncontrollable thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions).

Examples

In theory, compulsive behavior is a way of diminishing anxiety, and one might expect a negative association except for the possibility that for many children the obsessive-compulsive defenses are not sufficient to quell the amount of anxiety they suffer.

Synset Examples

obsessive-compulsive neurosis

Related Terms

  • Synonym: obsessive - Having persistent, unwanted thoughts or impulses
  • Synonym: compulsive - Resulting from or relating to an irresistible urge
