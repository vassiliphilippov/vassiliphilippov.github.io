weak part

1. NOUN

/ wik pɑrt / (AmE), / wiːk pɑːt / (BrE)
a part or area that is especially vulnerable or lacking in strength compared to others

Examples

Analysis in roleplaying is usually done for the purpose of understanding strong and weak points of an individual or as a process to eliminate weak parts and strengthen good parts.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: strong part - a part that is especially robust or resilient
