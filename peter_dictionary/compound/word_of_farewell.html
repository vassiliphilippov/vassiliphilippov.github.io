word of farewell

1. NOUN

/ wɝd əv ˌfɛrˈwɛl / (AmE), / wɜːd əv ˌfeəˈwɛl / (BrE)
a spoken or written remark expressing goodwill when parting from someone

Examples

She had begun to turn back toward the house, but his look caught her and she stood still, waiting there for what his expression indicated would be a serious word of farewell.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: farewell - an expression of good wishes when parting
  • Synonym: goodbye - a word or phrase used to express farewell
