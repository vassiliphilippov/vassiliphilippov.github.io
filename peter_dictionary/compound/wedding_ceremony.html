wedding ceremony

1. NOUN

/ ˈwɛdɪŋ ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ ˈsɛrɪməʊni / (BrE)
A formal event in which a marriage is officially celebrated and the couple are married.

Examples

It had been five days too late before he learned that she 'd gone through the wedding ceremony in a semitrance of laudanum, administered by her mother.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: wedding - a marriage ceremony and any celebrations that follow
