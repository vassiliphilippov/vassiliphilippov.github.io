family business

1. NOUN

/ ˈfæməli ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
A company or commercial enterprise that is owned and managed by members of the same family.

Examples

Like many others, he had to work hard, long hours in a struggling family business which, though it was allied to art of a kind - the design and production of engraved seals - bore no relation to the painting of pictures.
After more years of concentrated effort, Roy and his brother Max finally established a thriving family business at the old stand.
During all this time Roy continued to paint, first only on weekends, and then, as the family business permitted, for longer periods.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family firm - business organization owned and operated by a family

2. NOUN

/ ˈfæm.ə.li ˈbɪz.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfæm.ɪ.li ˈbɪz.nɪs / (BrE)
A business that is owned and operated by members of the same family.

Examples

Today I settle all family business, so don't tell me you're innocent, Carlo.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: family firm - a firm owned and controlled by family members
  • Degree Higher: corporation - a large business company
