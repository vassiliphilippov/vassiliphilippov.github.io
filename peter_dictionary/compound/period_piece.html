period piece

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪriəd pis / (AmE), / ˈpɪəriəd piːs / (BrE)
A work of art, literature, theatre, or film whose distinguishing feature is its depiction of the characteristics and style of a specific historical period.

Examples

In any given period piece the costumes, bric-a-brac, vehicles, and decor, bear the stamp of unimpeachable authenticity.
