leading man

1. NOUN

/ ˈlidɪŋ mæn / (AmE), / ˈliːdɪŋ mæn / (BrE)
An actor who plays the principal male role in a play, film, or television production.

Examples

The twist lay in using Bing Crosby's voice on the sound track while leading man Eddie Bracken mouthed the words.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: leading lady - actress who plays the principal female role in a play or film
