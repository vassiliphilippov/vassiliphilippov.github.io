kick in the butt

1. NOUN

/ kɪk ɪn ðə bʌt / (AmE), / kɪk ɪn ðə bʌt / (BrE)
a physical punishment in which someone is kicked in the buttocks

Examples

He carried the wood, carried the water, did the cooking, cleaning and mending, and occasionally got a kick in the butt for his pains.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: kick in the pants - punishment inflicted by kicking the victim in the behind
