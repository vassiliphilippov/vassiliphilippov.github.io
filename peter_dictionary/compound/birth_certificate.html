birth certificate

1. NOUN

/ bɝθ sərˈtɪfɪkət / (AmE), / bɜːθ səˈtɪfɪkət / (BrE)
an official document recording a person's birth, including details such as date and place of birth and parentage

Examples

He had all the proper ID, driver's license, birth certificate, social security card.
