one-tenth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn tɛnθ / (AmE), / wʌn tɛnθ / (BrE)
A tenth part; one part in ten equal parts.

Examples

Aside from this, the average portion contains some protein, an appreciable amount of vitamins and C - about one-tenth of the minimum daily requirement, and about a third of the official vitamin E requirement.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tenth - a tenth part; one out of ten equal parts
  • Degree Higher: one-fifth - a fifth part; one out of five equal parts
  • Degree Lower: one-twentieth - a twentieth part; one out of twenty equal parts
