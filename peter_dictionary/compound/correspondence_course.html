correspondence course

1. NOUN

/ kəˌrɑːspəndəns kɔrs / (AmE), / kəˌrɒspəndəns kɔːs / (BrE)
A course of study that is completed by exchanging lessons and assignments by mail or online, rather than by attending classes in person.

Examples

Further, he had taken full advantage of the Army's correspondence courses.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: distance learning - education delivered remotely, often online
  • Degree Higher: online course - course delivered primarily via the internet
