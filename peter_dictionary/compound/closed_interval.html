closed interval

1. NOUN

/ ˈkloʊzd ˈɪn.t̬ɚ.vəl / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzd ˈɪn.tə.vəl / (BrE)
An interval on the real number line that contains both its endpoints, usually written in the form [a, b], where a and b are included in the interval.
