on-the-spot

1. ADJ

/ ˌɑn ðə ˈspɑt / (AmE), / ˌɒn ðə ˈspɒt / (BrE)
Happening or done at the exact place where something is occurring.

Examples

The ME's boys had finished their on-the-spot examination and the body had been removed for autopsy.

Synset Examples

an on-the-spot reporter
on-the-spot inspections
an on-the scene newscast
