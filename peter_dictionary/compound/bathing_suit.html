bathing suit

1. NOUN

/ ˈbeɪðɪŋ suːt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ sjuːt / (BrE)
A tight-fitting garment worn for swimming or bathing.

Examples

And the thing about hurt feelings, the wet bathing suit pointed out, is that the person who has them is not quite the innocent party he believes himself to be.
They bring their fishing rods and binoculars and bathing suits.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: swimsuit - a garment worn for swimming, similar in meaning
  • Synonym: swimming costume - British English term for bathing suit
  • Degree Lower: trunks - men's swimming shorts
  • Degree Higher: bikini - two-piece women's swimsuit
