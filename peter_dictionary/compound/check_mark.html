check mark

1. NOUN

/ tʃɛk mɑrk / (AmE), / tʃek mɑːk / (BrE)
a mark (✓) placed beside an item to indicate it has been noted, checked, or completed

Examples

After every session, the check marks were totaled up and graphed, and in this way the supervisor's progress was charted.

Synset Examples

as he called the role he put a check mark by each student's name

Related Terms

  • Synonym: tick - synonym for 'check mark', more common in British English
