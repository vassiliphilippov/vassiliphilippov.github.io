out-of-bounds

1. ADJ

/ aʊt əv baʊndz / (AmE), / aʊt əv baʊndz / (BrE)
Not allowed to be entered, used, or accessed; forbidden or off-limits to certain people or groups.

Examples

If Daddy's books are out of bounds his own picture books are not.
Their curiosity went happily out of bounds.

Synset Examples

that area is off-limits

Related Terms

  • Synonym: off-limits - forbidden to enter or use; not allowed
