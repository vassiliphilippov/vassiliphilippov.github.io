twenty-two rifle

1. NOUN

/ ˌtwɛnti ˈtu ˈraɪfəl / (AmE), / ˌtwenti ˈtjuː ˈraɪfəl / (BrE)
A rifle designed to use .22-caliber ammunition, commonly used for target shooting and small game hunting.

Examples

Like, for example, a man carrying a twenty-two rifle, probably with a telescopic sight attached?

Related Terms

  • Synonym: .22 - .22-caliber ammunition
  • Synonym: rifle - a firearm with a rifled barrel

Etymology

Refers to the .22-inch (5.6 mm) caliber of the bullet; 'rifle' from French 'rifler' (to scratch, groove).
