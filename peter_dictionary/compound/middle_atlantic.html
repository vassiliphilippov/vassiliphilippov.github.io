middle Atlantic

1. ADJ

/ ˌmɪd(ə)l ætˈlæn.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɪd(ə)l ətˈlæn.tɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to a region of the eastern United States that generally includes Delaware, Maryland, Virginia, and usually New York, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey.

Examples

This time he turned westward, to the middle Atlantic coast of North America.

Synset Examples

mid-Atlantic states

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Mid-Atlantic - relating to the Mid-Atlantic region (alternative form, often capitalized)
