detonating fuse

1. NOUN

/ ˈdɛtəˌneɪtɪŋ fjuz / (AmE), / ˈdɛtənətɪŋ fjuːz / (BrE)
A fuse containing a small charge of explosive material, used to set off a main explosive charge.

Examples

He devised a detonating fuse in which a short wire was caused to glow by an electric current.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: detonator - a device used to set off an explosive charge
