direct loan

1. NOUN

/ dəˈrɛkt loʊn, daɪˈrɛkt loʊn / (AmE), / dəˈrɛkt ləʊn, daɪˈrɛkt ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan made by a lender directly to a borrower, without involving an intermediary or third party

Examples

SBA business loans are of two types: "participation "and "direct ".
Direct loans are those made by SBA alone.

Related Terms

  • Antonym: indirect loan - a loan made via a third party or intermediary
