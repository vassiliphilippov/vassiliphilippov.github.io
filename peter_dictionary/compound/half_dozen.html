half dozen

1. NOUN

/ hæf ˈdʌzən / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈdʌzən / (BrE)
A group or set of six items or units.

Examples

There are a half dozen reasons helping to account for the migration to the campgrounds.
Most of them could be eliminated; Muller's would have to be one of the half dozen almost directly opposite.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: dozen - a group of twelve items or units
  • Synonym: half - one of two equal parts of a whole
