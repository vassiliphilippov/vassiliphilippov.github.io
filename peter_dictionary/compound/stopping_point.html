stopping point

1. NOUN

/ ˈstɑpɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈstɒpɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the time or stage at which an activity, process, or event ends or is brought to a halt

Examples

The treatment seems unnecessarily loose jointed and complex, largely because the method is lax and the analysis seems never to be pushed to a satisfactory or even a consistent stopping-point.

Synset Examples

the stopping point of each round was signaled by a bell
the market was up at the finish
they were playing better at the close of the season

Related Terms

  • Synonym: endpoint - the final point in a process
  • Synonym: finish - the end or conclusion of an event or process
