one-fourth

1. NOUN

/ wʌn ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
One of four equal parts of a whole; 1/4.

Examples

In distal regions its diameter would be one-fourth to one-fifth that of the pulmonary artery.
