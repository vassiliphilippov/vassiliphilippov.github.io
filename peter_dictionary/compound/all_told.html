all told

1. ADV

/ ɔl toʊld / (AmE), / ɔːl təʊld / (BrE)
When everything is taken into account; in total.

Examples

All told, "Where's Charley? "ought not to be missed.
All told, they offered a highly confused picture.

Synset Examples

altogether he earns close to a million dollars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altogether - completely; with everything included

2. ADV

/ ɔl toʊld / (AmE), / ɔːl təʊld / (BrE)
With everything included; in total.

Examples

All told, he blew town with better than $370,000 of Warden Norton's money.

Synset Examples

altogether he earns close to a million dollars

Related Terms

  • Synonym: altogether - when everything is considered; in total
