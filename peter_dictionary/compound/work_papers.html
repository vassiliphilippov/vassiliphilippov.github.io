work papers

1. NOUN

/ wɝk ˈpeɪpɚz / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈpeɪpəz / (BrE)
Official documents providing information required for employment, especially for workers subject to legal restrictions or foreign nationals.

Examples

He can make you work papers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: work permit - an official document granting permission to work, especially in a foreign country
