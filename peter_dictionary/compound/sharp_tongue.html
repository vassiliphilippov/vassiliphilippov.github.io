sharp tongue

1. NOUN

/ ʃɑrp tʌŋ / (AmE), / ʃɑːp tʌŋ / (BrE)
a tendency to speak in a bitter, critical, or cutting manner

Examples

With her sharp tongue she 'd have cut his pompousness to ribbons.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caustic - marked by incisive sarcasm or biting wit
  • Synonym: acerbic - sharp and forthright in speech or manner
