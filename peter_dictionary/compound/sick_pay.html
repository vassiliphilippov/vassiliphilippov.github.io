sick pay

1. NOUN

/ sɪk peɪ / (AmE), / sɪk peɪ / (BrE)
money paid to an employee while they are absent from work due to illness

Examples

Sick pay, if included in your Gross Income, is deducted in arriving at Adjusted Gross Income.
If your sick pay is not included in your Gross Income, you may not deduct it.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: sick leave - time permitted away from work due to illness
