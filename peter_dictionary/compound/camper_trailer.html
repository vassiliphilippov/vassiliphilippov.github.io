camper trailer

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæmpər ˈtreɪlər / (AmE), / ˈkæmpə ˈtreɪlə / (BrE)
A trailer designed and equipped with living accommodations for use during camping trips, especially holidays.

Examples

There were umbrella tents, wall tents, cottage tents, station wagon tents, pup tents, Pop tents, Baker tents, tents with exterior frames, camper trailers, travel trailers, and even a few surplus parachutes serving as sunshades over entire family camps.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: caravan - a vehicle equipped for living in, often towed by a car (British English synonym)
  • Synonym: travel trailer - a similar towable trailer with living space, North American synonym
