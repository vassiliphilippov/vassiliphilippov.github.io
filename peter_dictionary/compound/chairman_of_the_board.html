chairman of the board

1. NOUN

/ ˈtʃɛrˌmæn əv ðə bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛəˌmæn əv ðə bɔːd / (BrE)
the person who leads the board of directors of a corporation, overseeing its activities and representing its interests

Examples

I am pleased to note that Mr. Sulzberger will continue to serve as chairman of the board of the New York Times.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: chairperson - person who leads a board, meeting, or committee
