electrical work

1. NOUN

/ ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl wɜrk / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl wɜːk / (BrE)
the installation, maintenance, or repair of electrical wiring, equipment, or systems; the tasks or craft performed by an electrician

Examples

These opportunities are to be found in certain cities in such crafts as auto mechanics, carpentry, drafting, electrical work, tool-and-die work, and sheet-metal work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: electrician - a person who installs or repairs electrical systems
