calendar day

1. NOUN

/ ˈkæl.ən.dɚ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈkæl.ən.də deɪ / (BrE)
A period of time lasting from midnight to midnight, as used in calendars and official reckoning.

Examples

Hey... 100 buttonmen on the street 24 hours a day.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: day - a period of 24 hours; a unit of time
