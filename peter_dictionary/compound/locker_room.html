locker room

1. NOUN

/ ˈlɑkɚ ˌrum / (AmE), / ˈlɒkə ˌruːm / (BrE)
A room in a sports facility, gym, or workplace, equipped with lockers and used for changing clothes and storing personal belongings temporarily.

Examples

He was marching up and down the locker room of the Grassy Brae Golf Club shouting, "Bomb Cuba!
He was brigadier of the club's locker-room light infantry, and at one time or another declared war on Russia, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, and China.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: changing room - room for changing clothes, often without lockers
