towel rack

1. NOUN

/ ˈtaʊəl ɹæk / (AmE), / ˈtaʊ.əl ræk / (BrE)
a bar or frame, usually fixed to a wall, used for hanging towels so they can dry

Examples

He reached out and felt the bath towel hanging on the towel rack over the tub.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: towel rail - a rail for hanging towels (chiefly British)
