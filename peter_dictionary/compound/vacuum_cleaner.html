vacuum cleaner

1. NOUN

/ ˈvæk.jum ˌklin.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈvæk.juːm ˌkliː.nər / (BrE)
An electrical appliance for cleaning floors, carpets, and other surfaces by suctioning up dust and dirt.

Examples

And men also used vacuum cleaners in both rooms, sucking dust up once more.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hoover - British term for a vacuum cleaner
