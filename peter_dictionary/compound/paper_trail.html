paper trail

1. NOUN

/ ˈpeɪpər treɪl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə treɪl / (BrE)
A series of written or recorded documents that provide evidence of someone's activities, decisions, or transactions.

Examples

I know you're good, Andy, but all that paper leaves a **trail**.

Synset Examples

this paper trail consisted mainly of electronically stored information
