sense of responsibility

1. NOUN

/ ˈsɛns əv rɪˌspɑnsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈsɛns əv rɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
an awareness of one's obligations or duty to behave responsibly

Examples

Caring for a calf, a pig, or some chickens develops in children a sense of responsibility for work.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: responsibility - the state or fact of having a duty to deal with something
