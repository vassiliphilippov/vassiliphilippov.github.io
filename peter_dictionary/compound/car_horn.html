car horn

1. NOUN

/ kɑɹ hɔrn / (AmE), / kɑː hɔːn / (BrE)
a device in a car that makes a loud warning sound, usually by pressing a button or lever, to alert other drivers or pedestrians

Examples

[REBEL SHOUTING IN SPANISH] Viva Fidel! [CAR HORN BLARING] [EXCLAIMS] I hope my age is correct.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: horn - a device that makes a loud warning sound, especially in vehicles
