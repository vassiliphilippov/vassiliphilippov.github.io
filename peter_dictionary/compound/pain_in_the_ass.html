pain in the ass

1. NOUN

/ peɪn ɪn ði æs / (AmE), / peɪn ɪn ði ɑːs / (BrE)
A person or thing that is very annoying or troublesome.

Examples

I left it noisy, so it scares away any pain-in-the-ass innocent bystanders.

Synset Examples

washing dishes was a nuisance before we got a dish washer
a bit of a bother
he's not a friend, he's an infliction

Related Terms

  • Synonym: nuisance - a person or thing causing inconvenience or annoyance
  • Synonym: pain in the neck - a person or thing that is very annoying or troublesome (politer form)
