English people

1. NOUN

/ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈpipəl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈpiːpl̩ / (BrE)
the people who come from England or are of English descent

Examples

She found this a marvel because, as she said, only six per cent of English people are churchgoers.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: Englishman - a man from England
  • Synonym: Englishwoman - a woman from England
  • Degree Higher: British people - people of the United Kingdom
