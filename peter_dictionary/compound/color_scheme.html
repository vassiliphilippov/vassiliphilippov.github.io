color scheme

1. NOUN

/ ˈkʌlər skiːm / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə skiːm / (BrE)
A planned combination of colors used together for a particular purpose, especially in interior design, fashion, or art.

Examples

Makes drawings and plans of rooms showing placement of furniture, floor coverings, wall decorations, and determines color schemes.

Synset Examples

the color scheme for this room was determined by an interior decorator

Related Terms

  • Synonym: palette - range of colors used by an artist or designer
  • Antonym: monochrome - containing or using only one color
