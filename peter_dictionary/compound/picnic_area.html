picnic area

1. NOUN

/ ˈpɪk.nɪk ˌɛr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪk ˌeə.ri.ə / (BrE)
An outdoor space or section of land designated and equipped for people to have picnics, often found in parks or recreational sites.

Examples

A large picnic area or camping development is most efficient in shape as a square or rectangle several hundred feet in width in preference to a long narrow area less than one hundred feet wide.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: park - public area of land for recreation
