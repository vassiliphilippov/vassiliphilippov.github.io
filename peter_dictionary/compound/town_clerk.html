town clerk

1. NOUN

/ taʊn klɝk / (AmE), / taʊn klɑːk / (BrE)
an official responsible for keeping a town’s records and documents, and often other administrative duties in local government

Examples

Since that time the telegraph office has shifted in location from the railroad station at the Depot and shops at the Center back to the town clerk's office and drugstore at the Village.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: clerk - an official responsible for administrative tasks and record-keeping
