flag of truce

1. NOUN

/ flæɡ əv truːs / (AmE), / flæɡ əv truːs / (BrE)
A white flag displayed to signal a request for a ceasefire or negotiation, or to indicate surrender.

Examples

The man had come floundering aboard the flat-bottomed barge at the last instant, brandishing the flag of truce.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: white flag - a white flag used to indicate surrender or a desire to negotiate
