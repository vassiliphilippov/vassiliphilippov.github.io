movie theater

1. NOUN

/ ˈmuːvi ˌθiːətər / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌθɪətə / (BrE)
A building or room where films are shown to the public.

Examples

The manager of a movie theater received a telephone call from a woman who was equally indefinite.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: cinema - place where films are shown to the public
  • Synonym: theater - building for dramatic performances or films

Etymology

From 'movie' (short for 'moving picture') + 'theater' (from Greek 'theatron', a place for viewing).
