in haste

1. ADV

/ ɪn heɪst / (AmE), / ɪn heɪst / (BrE)
In a hurried or hasty manner; quickly and without careful thought.

Examples

It was the doctor, dressed and ready for the expedition to the market, and Alex was obliged to prepare himself in haste.

Synset Examples

the way they buried him so hurriedly was disgraceful
hastily, he scanned the headlines
sold in haste and at a sacrifice

Related Terms

  • Synonym: hurriedly - in a hurried or hasty manner
  • Synonym: hastily - with excessive speed or urgency

2. ADV

/ ɪn heɪst / (AmE), / ɪn heɪst / (BrE)
