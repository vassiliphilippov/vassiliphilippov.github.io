auto mechanics

1. NOUN

/ ˈɔːtoʊ məˈkænɪks / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊ mɪˈkænɪks / (BrE)
the craft or trade of building and repairing automobiles

Examples

These opportunities are to be found in certain cities in such crafts as auto mechanics, carpentry, drafting, electrical work, tool-and-die work, and sheet-metal work.
They were learning trades as diverse as shoe repairing, bricklaying, carpentry, cabinet making, auto mechanics, and airplane mechanics.

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automotive repair - maintenance and repair of motor vehicles

Etymology

From 'auto' (short for automobile) + 'mechanics' (branch of applied physics or the trade involving mechanical devices).

the craft or trade of building, maintaining, and repairing automobiles

Examples

Related Terms

  • Synonym: automotive repair - the repair of motor vehicles

Etymology
