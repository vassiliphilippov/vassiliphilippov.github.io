visual space

1. NOUN

/ ˈvɪʒuəl speɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒjʊəl speɪs / (BrE)
the spatial extent or arrangement as perceived through sight; the space that appears to the eyes in vision

Examples

(2) Spatiality becomes part of the tactual sensation only by way of visual representations; that is, there is, in the true sense, only a visual space ".

Related Terms
