ankle-deep

1. ADJ

/ ˈæŋkəl ˌdip / (AmE), / ˈæŋkəl ˌdiːp / (BrE)
(of a liquid or other substance) reaching up to the ankles in depth.

Examples

The street was unpaved and rose steeply toward the center; it was mud in wet weather and dust, ankle-deep, in dry, and could be crossed only at the corner where there were stepping stones.

Related Terms

  • Degree Higher: knee-deep - reaching up to the knees in depth
  • Degree Higher: waist-deep - reaching up to the waist in depth
