1. ☢️ radiation illness ("radiation syndrome after accident")

Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən ˈsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən ˈsɪn.drəʊm / (BrE)
an illness caused by exposure to high doses of ionizing radiation, characterized by symptoms such as nausea, vomiting, hair loss, and in severe cases, organ failure or death
illness from exposure to high ionizing radiation

Examples

Exposure to the fallout resulted in several cases of acute radiation syndrome among the population.
Many workers developed radiation syndrome after the nuclear plant accident.
Symptoms of radiation syndrome include vomiting, diarrhea, and hair loss.
