NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊs əv ˈwɔr / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs əv ˈwɔː / (BrE)
in Islamic political theory, a region or country where Muslims are in the minority and are subject to persecution or where Islamic law is not established
regions where Muslims are persecuted or Islamic law is absent
