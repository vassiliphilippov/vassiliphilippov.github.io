NOUN

a large cloud, shaped like a mushroom, formed by the aftermath of a powerful explosion, especially a nuclear bomb, consisting of dust, smoke, and debris rising high into the sky

Witnesses described the mushroom-shaped cloud rising rapidly after the bomb detonated.
