1. ⚖️ weight measurer ("spring scale measures weight")

Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ skeɪl / (AmE), / sprɪŋ skeɪl / (BrE)
a device for measuring weight or force by the tension of a helical spring, the amount of extension indicating the measurement
device that measures weight using a spring

Examples

In physics class, we used a spring scale to measure the force needed to lift the object.
The fisherman checked the weight of his catch with a handheld spring scale.
You can quickly test the pressure by attaching a spring scale to the container's lid.
