1. ❝ quotation mark ("open inverted comma")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɝːtɪd ˈkɑːmə / (AmE), / ɪnˌvɜːtɪd ˈkɒmə / (BrE)
a punctuation mark (‘ or ’, “ or ”) used especially to indicate that words are being quoted or attributed to someone else
punctuation mark used for quotations

Examples

In British English, single inverted commas are often used instead of double quotation marks.
Please put the phrase in inverted commas to show it’s a direct quote.
The teacher reminded students to use inverted commas around words spoken by characters.
