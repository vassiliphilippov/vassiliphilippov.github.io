1. ⏰ relaxed punctuality ("African time for meetings")

Pronunciation: / ˈæf.rɪ.kən taɪm / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən taɪm / (BrE)
A humorous or informal term referring to a relaxed or flexible approach to punctuality, especially in some African cultures.
relaxed attitude to punctuality

Examples

On African time, the meeting began well after the scheduled hour.
She assured us not to worry because everyone arrives late when it's African time.
When planning events in some regions, allowing for African time can help avoid frustration.
