1. ✂️ short haircut ("a crew cut hairstyle")

Pronunciation: / ˈkru ˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈkruː ˌkʌt / (BrE)
A very short men's haircut in which the hair is cropped close to the scalp, typically the same length all over.
