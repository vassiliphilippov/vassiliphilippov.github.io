1. ✈️ to be too late ("to miss a flight because of traffic")

VERB
Pronunciation: / mɪs ə flaɪt / (AmE), / mɪs ə flaɪt / (BrE)
to fail to board an airplane at the scheduled departure time, often because of arriving late or unexpected delays.
fail to catch a scheduled airplane

Examples

I was stuck in traffic and ended up missing my flight to New York.
If you miss your flight, the airline may offer you a seat on the next available plane.
She set two alarms to make sure she wouldn't miss her flight early in the morning.
