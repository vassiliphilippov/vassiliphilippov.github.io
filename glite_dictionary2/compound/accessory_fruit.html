Examples

Strawberries are often discussed in botany classes as a classic accessory fruit.
The apple is an example of an accessory fruit because its flesh develops from the receptacle, not just the ovary.
Unlike a true berry, an accessory fruit includes tissues derived from flower parts other than the ovary.
