The legal right of an accused person to have a prompt and open trial before an impartial jury, as guaranteed by the Sixth Amendment to the US Constitution.
legal right to a prompt and public jury trial

Every citizen has the right to speedy and public trial by jury if they are charged with a crime.

The Sixth Amendment guarantees the right to speedy and public trial by jury for all criminal defendants.
