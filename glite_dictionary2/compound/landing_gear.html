NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪŋ ˌɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ ˌɡɪə / (BrE)
the wheels and supporting structures of an aircraft that support its weight while on the ground and during takeoff or landing
aircraft undercarriage for support on ground

Examples

A problem with the landing gear forced the crew to abort takeoff.
The aircraft's landing gear retracted smoothly after liftoff.
The pilot checked the landing gear before making his final approach to the runway.
