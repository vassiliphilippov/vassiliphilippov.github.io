logic symbol asserting existence

Examples

A statement containing an existential quantifier claims the existence of at least one object meeting certain conditions.
In mathematics, the existential quantifier is used to assert that at least one solution exists to an equation.
The symbol ∃ is commonly used as the existential quantifier in formal logic.
