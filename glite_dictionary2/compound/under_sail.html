1. ⛵ using sails ("under sail across the sea")

Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ ˈseɪl / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ˈseɪl / (BrE)
(of a boat or ship) having its sails set and using wind power to move; in the act of sailing.
with sails set and sailing

Examples

After hours of preparation, the boat was finally under sail.
By noon, the crew had the yacht under sail and making good speed.
The old schooner looked majestic under sail as it crossed the bay.
