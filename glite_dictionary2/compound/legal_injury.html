1. ⚖️ harm to rights ("legal injury to rights")

Pronunciation: / ˈliɡəl ˈɪndʒəri / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈɪndʒəri / (BrE)
Harm or damage suffered as a result of the violation of a legal right, recognized as grounds for a legal claim.
harm from violation of legal right

Examples

The court found that the plaintiff suffered a legal injury when his rights were violated.
Without evidence of a legal injury, a lawsuit cannot proceed in this case.
You must demonstrate a legal injury to be entitled to damages under the law.
