1. ⚖️ judge for change ("controversial activist judge")

Pronunciation: / ˈæk.tɪ.vɪst dʒʌdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæk.tɪ.vɪst dʒʌdʒ / (BrE)
a judge whose rulings are influenced by personal beliefs or who actively seeks to promote social change through judicial decisions

Examples
