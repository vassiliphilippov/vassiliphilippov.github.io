NOUN
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ʃɔːt dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
A method of division in which the steps are performed mentally or with minimal writing, rather than recording all intermediate calculations.
division method performed without written steps

Examples
