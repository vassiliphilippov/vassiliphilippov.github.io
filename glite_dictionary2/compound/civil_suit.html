1. ⚖️ legal case ("file a civil suit")

Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl suːt / (AmE), / ˈsɪv.əl suːt / (BrE)
A lawsuit brought to resolve a dispute involving alleged violations of civil law, rather than criminal law.
lawsuit under civil, not criminal, law

Examples

After the accident, the family initiated a civil suit to seek compensation for damages.
She filed a civil suit against her landlord for breach of contract.
The company faced a civil suit after allegedly violating environmental regulations.
