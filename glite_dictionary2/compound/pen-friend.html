1. ✉️ letter friend ("pen-friend from abroad")

Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn ˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈpɛn ˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a person with whom one becomes friendly by exchanging letters, typically someone one has not met in person
a friend made via letter correspondence

Examples

After months of exchanging letters, she finally met her pen-friend during the summer holidays.
I've had a pen-friend in France for over ten years, though we've never met in person.
When I was a child, my school encouraged students to get a pen-friend from another country.
