1. ☠️ toxic plant ("nightshade plant berries")

Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtʃeɪd ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtʃeɪd ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant belonging to the genus Solanum, many of which are toxic, including cultivated and wild species such as deadly nightshade and black nightshade.
toxic plant of the genus Solanum

Examples

Ingesting a nightshade plant can be dangerous for both humans and animals.
Many gardeners avoid planting the nightshade plant because of its toxic berries.
The nightshade plant is part of a large family that includes potatoes and tomatoes.
