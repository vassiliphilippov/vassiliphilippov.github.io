1. ❤️ heart valve ("atrioventricular valve in heart")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.trioʊ.vɛnˈtrɪk.jə.lɚ vælv / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.tri.əʊ.vɛnˈtrɪk.jʊ.lə vælv / (BrE)
either of the two valves in the heart that allow blood to flow from an atrium to a ventricle but prevent its return
valve between atrium and ventricle in the heart

Examples

Damage to the atrioventricular valve can disrupt normal blood flow in the heart.
Surgery was required to repair his faulty atrioventricular valve.
The doctor explained how the atrioventricular valve prevents blood from leaking back into the atrium.
