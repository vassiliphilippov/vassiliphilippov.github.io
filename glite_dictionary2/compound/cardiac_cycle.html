1. ❤️ heartbeat process ("the cardiac cycle of the heart")

Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr.di.æk ˈsaɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.di.æk ˈsaɪ.kl̩ / (BrE)
the sequence of events in the heart from the start of one heartbeat to the beginning of the next, including contraction and relaxation of the atria and ventricles
one complete heartbeat, including contraction and relaxation

Examples

Doctors often study the cardiac cycle to assess heart health.
The cardiac cycle consists of phases of contraction and relaxation within the heart.
Understanding the cardiac cycle is essential in interpreting ECG readings.
