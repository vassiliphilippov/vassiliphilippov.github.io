1. ✈️ health travel ("medical tourism for surgery")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈtʊrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl ˈtʊərɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice of traveling to another country to obtain medical treatment or care, usually for lower costs or specialized services not available at home
traveling abroad for medical care

Examples

Medical tourism has become increasingly popular as people seek affordable treatments abroad.
She decided on medical tourism after learning about the high cost of surgery at home.
The clinic specializes in assisting patients who come to the country for medical tourism.
