1. ⚓ ship officer ("first mate on a ship")

Pronunciation: / ˌfɝst ˈmeɪt / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈmeɪt / (BrE)
the officer ranking immediately below the captain on a commercial ship, responsible for the ship's crew and deck operations
officer below ship's captain

Examples

After the captain fell ill, the first mate assumed temporary command of the vessel.
The first mate ensured that all safety procedures were followed during the storm.
The first mate oversaw the crew's work during the early morning shift.
