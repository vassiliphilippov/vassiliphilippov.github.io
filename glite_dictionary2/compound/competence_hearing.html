1. ⚖️ court mental check ("competence hearing for defendant")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmpətəns ˈhɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpɪtəns ˈhɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
A legal hearing held to determine whether a defendant is mentally capable of understanding the charges and assisting in their defense.
hearing to assess a defendant's mental capacity for trial

Examples

After the results of the competence hearing, the court ruled that the accused was fit to proceed.
His attorney requested a competence hearing due to concerns about his mental health.
The judge ordered a competence hearing to decide if the defendant could stand trial.
