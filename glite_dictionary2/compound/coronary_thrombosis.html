NOUN

the blockage of a coronary artery by a blood clot, often leading to a heart attack
blockage of coronary artery by clot

Examples

A coronary thrombosis can quickly cut off blood supply to the heart muscle, causing damage.
After suffering a coronary thrombosis, she was placed on medication to prevent further clots.
