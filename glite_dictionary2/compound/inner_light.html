1. ✨ divine guidance ("follow the Inner Light")

Pronunciation: / ˈɪnər laɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnə laɪt / (BrE)
(in Quaker belief) a divine presence within each person believed to guide and enlighten the soul.
Quaker belief in guiding divine presence

Examples

Early Quaker writings frequently emphasize the importance of following one's Inner Light.
Her actions were guided by the belief in the Inner Light within every person.
Many Quakers refer to the Inner Light as God's presence speaking directly to their conscience.
