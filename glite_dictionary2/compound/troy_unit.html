1. ⚖️ weight measure ("troy unit of gold")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / trɔɪ ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / trɔɪ ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
any of the units of measurement used in the troy system of weights, historically used for precious metals such as gold and silver
unit in the troy system of weights

Examples

Collectors sometimes confuse the troy unit with other systems of measurement.
Silver bars are weighed in troy units for international trade.
The price of gold is quoted per troy unit rather than in standard ounces.
