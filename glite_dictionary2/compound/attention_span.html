1. ⏳ focus time ("short attention span")

Pronunciation: / əˈtɛn.ʃən spǣn / (AmE), / əˈtɛn.ʃən spæn / (BrE)
the length of time a person can concentrate on a particular activity or subject without becoming distracted
length of time one can concentrate

Examples

Children often have a shorter attention span than adults, making long lectures challenging for them.
My attention span tends to decrease when I am tired or hungry.
Social media seems to have reduced the average person's attention span over the past decade.
