1. ☎️ reverse-charge call ("make a collect call")

Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛkt kɔl / (AmE), / kəˈlɛkt kɔːl / (BrE)
A telephone call for which the person receiving the call pays the charge instead of the caller.
telephone call paid by receiver

Examples

He made a collect call to his parents after missing the last train.
Receiving a collect call can be expensive, especially for long-distance calls.
The operator asked if I would accept the charges for a collect call.
