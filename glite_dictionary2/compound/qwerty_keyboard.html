1. ⌨️ standard keyboard ("QWERTY keyboard layout")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɝti ˌkiːbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkwɜːti ˈkiːbɔːd / (BrE)
The standard keyboard layout for typewriters and computers, named after the first six letters on the top row of alphabet keys: Q, W, E, R, T, and Y.
standard typewriter/computer keyboard layout

Examples

I found it difficult to adapt to the QWERTY keyboard after using a different layout abroad.
Most computers in the United States use a QWERTY keyboard for typing.
She prefers the QWERTY keyboard layout over other formats like AZERTY or Dvorak.
