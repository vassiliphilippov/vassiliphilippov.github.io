NOUN

A European evergreen plant (Helleborus niger) with white or purplish rose-like flowers that bloom in winter.
European winter-blooming plant with rose-like flowers

Examples

Botanists often refer to the winter rose as a symbol of resilience in harsh weather.
She planted a winter rose by the front door to admire its flowers during December.
The winter rose blooms when most other plants are dormant, bringing color to the garden in cold months.
