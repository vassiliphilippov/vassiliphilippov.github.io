A bearing that supports and allows the rotation of a shaft, typically by means of a layer of lubricant separating the moving parts.
bearing for supporting a rotating shaft

Examples

Proper lubrication is crucial for the long-term operation of journal bearings.
Replacing a worn journal bearing can prevent serious damage to machinery.
The engine's crankshaft relies on journal bearings to reduce friction during rotation.
