tertium quid

1⚖️third optiona tertium quid solution

1. ⚖️ third option ("a tertium quid solution")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜrʃiəm ˈkwɪd / (AmE), / ˌtɜːʃɪəm ˈkwɪd / (BrE)
A third thing that is similar to two opposites but distinct from both; something intermediate or different in character or nature.
something distinct from two opposites

Examples

In philosophical debates, some argued for a tertium quid instead of a simple binary.
The political party sees itself as a tertium quid between the left and the right.
The proposed solution was a tertium quid, neither tradition nor innovation.
