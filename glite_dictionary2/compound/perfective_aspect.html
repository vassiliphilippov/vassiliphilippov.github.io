NOUN
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɛktɪv ˈæspɛkt / (AmE), / pəˈfɛktɪv ˈæspɛkt / (BrE)
a grammatical aspect of verbs that expresses a completed action or whole event, rather than a process or ongoing action
aspect expressing completed action

Examples

In Russian, the perfective aspect of a verb indicates that the action has been completed.
Some languages use prefixes to indicate the perfective aspect in verbs.
The perfective aspect contrasts with the imperfective aspect, which describes ongoing or habitual actions.
