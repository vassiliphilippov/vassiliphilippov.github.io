1⚾hard straight hit baseballhit a line drive

1. ⚾ hard straight hit ("hit a line drive")

Pronunciation: / laɪn draɪv / (AmE), / laɪn draɪv / (BrE)
(in baseball) a ball that is hit hard and flies straight and low after being struck by the batter.
baseball hit traveling straight and low

Examples

He hit a line drive into the left field gap for a double.
She drilled a line drive past the pitcher's mound, scoring two runs.
The shortstop caught the line drive for the final out of the inning.
