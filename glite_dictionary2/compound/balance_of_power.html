1. ⚖️ equal strength ("maintain the balance of power")

Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.əns əv ˈpaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns əv ˈpaʊ.ə / (BrE); / ˈbæləns əv ˈpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈbæləns əv ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
a situation where power is distributed among groups, nations, or branches so that no single one can dominate the others
distribution of power among entities / equilibrium between rival powers or nations

During the Cold War, the arms race was partly driven by the desire to maintain a balance of power.
The treaty established a balance of power that prevented any one country from becoming too dominant.
