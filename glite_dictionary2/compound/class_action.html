1. ⚖️ group lawsuit ("class action lawsuit")

Pronunciation: / klæs ˈækʃən / (AmE), / klɑːs ˈækʃən / (BrE)
a lawsuit in which one or more people sue on behalf of a larger group of people who have similar claims
lawsuit by a group representative

Examples

A large class action suit can result in significant compensation for the plaintiffs.
The judge certified the class action after determining the group's claims were similar enough.
The victims filed a class action against the company for false advertising.
