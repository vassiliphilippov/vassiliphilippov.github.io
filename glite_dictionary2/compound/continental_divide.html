1. ⛰️ watershed line ("the continental divide trail")

Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.tɪˈnen.t̬əl dɪˈvaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.tl dɪˈvaɪd / (BrE)
the line of highest ground separating the river systems of a continent, especially the watershed in North America that runs from Alaska to Mexico, which divides rivers flowing to different oceans
main watershed line of a continent

Examples

Hikers often follow trails along the continental divide to experience stunning panoramic views.
Many rivers have their source near the continental divide.
The Rocky Mountains form a significant part of the continental divide in North America.

Related Terms

  • synonym – watershed: an area or ridge of land that separates waters flowing to different rivers, basins, or seas
