1. ❤️ heart pouch ("auricular appendix of heart")

Pronunciation: / ɔːˈrɪkjələr əpˈɛndɪks / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə əˈpɛndɪks / (BrE)
a small pouch projecting from the upper front part of each atrium of the heart
heart atrium pouch

Examples

Blood clots can occasionally form in the left auricular appendix in patients with atrial fibrillation.
Surgical removal of the auricular appendix may be necessary in cases of severe infection.
The auricular appendix is sometimes referred to as the atrial appendage in medical literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym – atrium: an upper chamber of the heart that receives blood
  • synonym – auricle: the external ear or the atrium of the heart
