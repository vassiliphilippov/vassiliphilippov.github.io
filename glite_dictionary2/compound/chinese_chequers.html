Chinese chequers

1⭐board gameplay Chinese chequers with friends

1. ⭐ board game ("play Chinese chequers with friends")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈtʃɛkərz / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈtʃɛkəz / (BrE)
A board game played on a star-shaped board in which players race to move their marbles across the board to the opposite point of the six-pointed star.
board game with marbles and six-pointed star board

Examples

Each player selects a different color for their marbles in Chinese chequers.
The rules of Chinese chequers are simple enough for children to understand.
We spent the rainy afternoon playing Chinese chequers by the fireplace.
