1. ⚖️ court arrest order ("issue a bench warrant")

Pronunciation: / bɛntʃ ˈwɔːrənt / (AmE), / bentʃ ˈwɒrənt / (BrE)
an official order issued by a judge, authorizing the arrest of a person and requiring that person be brought before the court
a court-issued arrest warrant

Examples

A bench warrant allows the police to arrest someone for not complying with a court order.
Because he missed his trial date, a bench warrant was put out for his arrest.
The judge issued a bench warrant for the defendant who failed to appear in court.
