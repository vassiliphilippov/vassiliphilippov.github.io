1. ⚖️ legal father case ("file a paternity suit")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɜrnəti suːt / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəti sjuːt / (BrE)
A legal action to determine the biological father of a child born outside marriage and to secure the child's financial support.
legal case to establish child's father

Examples

After a lengthy paternity suit, the man was identified as the father.
She won the paternity suit and received child support payments.
The court heard the paternity suit filed by the child's mother.
