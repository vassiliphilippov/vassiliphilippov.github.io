1. ⚡ electric train rail ("touch the third rail")

Pronunciation: / θɜrd reɪl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːd ˈreɪl / (BrE)
an additional rail alongside the running rails of a railway track, through which electric current is supplied to electric trains or locomotives
rail supplying electricity to trains

Examples

Many safety precautions are taken to prevent people from touching the third rail.
The subway uses a third rail to provide electricity to the trains.
The technician inspected the third rail after a power outage on the track.
