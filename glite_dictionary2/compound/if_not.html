1. ⬆️ maybe even more ("talented, if not a genius")

Pronunciation: / ɪf nɑt / (AmE), / ɪf nɒt / (BrE)
used to suggest that something may be even more the case than what was just stated, indicating a possible greater degree or extent.
possibly to a greater extent

Examples

She is talented, if not a true genius in her field.
The hike was challenging, if not nearly impossible for beginners.
The project will take weeks, if not months, to complete.
