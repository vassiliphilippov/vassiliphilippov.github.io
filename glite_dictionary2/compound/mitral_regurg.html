1. ❤️ heart valve leak ("mitral regurg causes murmur")

Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪtrəl rɪˈgɝdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪtrəl rɪˈgɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a medical term for mitral regurgitation, a condition where the heart's mitral valve does not close tightly, allowing blood to flow backward into the left atrium
backflow of blood due to mitral valve leakage

Examples

Her cardiologist diagnosed her with mitral regurg after hearing a heart murmur during the checkup.
Routine echocardiograms are important for patients with mitral regurg to monitor changes in heart function.
The surgical team discussed whether to repair or replace the valve in cases of severe mitral regurg.
