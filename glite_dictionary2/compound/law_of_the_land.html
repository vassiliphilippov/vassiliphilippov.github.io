1. ⚖️ national law ("the law of the land applies")

Pronunciation: / lɔ əv ðə lænd / (AmE), / lɔː əv ðə lænd / (BrE)
The fundamental law or legal code of a country, especially as established by its constitution or governing authority.
fundamental law of a country

Examples

Every citizen is subject to the law of the land, regardless of status or wealth.
He argued that the new policy violated the law of the land as established by parliament.
The Supreme Court must interpret the law of the land when deciding on constitutional matters.

Related Terms

  • synonym – constitution: a system of fundamental principles or established precedents governing a country
  • synonym – statute: a written law passed by a legislative body
