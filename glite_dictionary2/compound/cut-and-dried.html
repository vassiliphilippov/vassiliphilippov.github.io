1. ✅ clear and fixed ("cut-and-dried answer")

Pronunciation: / ˌkʌt ənd ˈdraɪd / (AmE), / ˌkʌt ən ˈdraɪd / (BrE)
(of an idea, situation, or procedure) simple and clear, lacking in originality or subject to no change; according to established rules or expectations.
simple, clear, and unchangeable

Examples

Many people appreciate cut-and-dried rules because they leave little room for confusion.
The plan wasn't as cut-and-dried as we first thought; unexpected issues kept coming up.
Their solution to the problem was anything but cut-and-dried, requiring lots of negotiation.
