1. ☠️ poisonous gas ("carbon monoxide poisoning")

Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrbən məˈnɑksaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɑːbən məˈnɒksaɪd / (BrE); / ˌkɑrbən məˈnɑksaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɑːbən ˌmɒˈnɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a colorless, odorless, and highly poisonous gas produced by the incomplete burning of carbon materials
poisonous gas from burning carbon / poisonous, colorless gas (CO)
