NOUN
Pronunciation: / wik ti / (AmE), / wiːk tiː / (BrE)

tea with mild flavor; something lacking impact

Examples

He handed me a cup of weak tea, barely colored at all.
Her apology sounded like weak tea and failed to convince anyone.
The movie was just weak tea compared to the book.
