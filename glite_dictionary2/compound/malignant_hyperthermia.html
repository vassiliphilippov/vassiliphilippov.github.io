NOUN

A rare inherited disorder in which exposure to certain anesthetics causes dangerously high body temperature and severe muscle rigidity.
rare inherited reaction to some anesthetics

Examples

Because her brother had experienced malignant hyperthermia, extra precautions were taken for her operation.
