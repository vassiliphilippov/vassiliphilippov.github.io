1. ✈️ direct flight ("book a nonstop flight")

Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈstɑp flaɪt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈstɒp flaɪt / (BrE)
a flight that travels directly between its origin and destination without any intermediate stops
direct flight with no stops

Examples

A nonstop flight is usually more expensive than one with layovers.
She prefers to take a nonstop flight whenever possible to avoid the hassle of connecting airports.
We booked a nonstop flight from New York to Los Angeles to save time.
