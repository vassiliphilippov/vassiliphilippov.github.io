1. ✈️ airport walkway ("walk through the jet bridge")

Pronunciation: / dʒɛt brɪdʒ / (AmE), / dʒɛt brɪdʒ / (BrE)
An enclosed, extendable bridge at an airport terminal that connects directly to an aircraft, allowing passengers to board or disembark while being sheltered from the weather.
enclosed boarding bridge at airports

Examples

During the storm, the jet bridge sheltered everyone from the rain as they boarded.
The passengers waited while the jet bridge was repositioned to the aircraft door.
You must remain seated until the jet bridge is securely attached to the plane.
