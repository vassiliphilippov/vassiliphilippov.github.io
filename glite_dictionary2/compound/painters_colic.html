1. ⚠️ lead poisoning ("painter's colic from lead paint")

Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntərz ˈkɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntəz ˈkɒlɪk / (BrE)
A form of chronic lead poisoning, especially among painters, characterized by severe constipation and abdominal pain.
lead poisoning marked by constipation

Examples
