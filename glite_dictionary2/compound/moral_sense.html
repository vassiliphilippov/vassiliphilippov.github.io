1. ⚖️ ethical awareness ("strong moral sense")

Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrəl sɛns / (AmE), / ˈmɒrəl sɛns / (BrE)
the ability to distinguish right from wrong, guiding a person's thoughts and actions according to ethical principles
awareness of right and wrong; ethical instinct

Examples

Children often develop a moral sense by observing the behavior of adults around them.
Her strong moral sense led her to always act with integrity, even when it was difficult.
His moral sense made it impossible for him to ignore the injustice he witnessed.
