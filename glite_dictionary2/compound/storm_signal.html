1. ⚠️ storm warning ("raise a storm signal")

Pronunciation: / stɔrm ˈsɪɡ.nəl / (AmE), / stɔːm ˈsɪɡ.nəl / (BrE)
a warning signal, often visual or audible, indicating that a severe storm is approaching
a warning of an approaching storm

Examples

The coast guard raised a storm signal to alert fishermen to return to shore.
The school closed early after a storm signal was issued by local authorities.
When the storm signal was displayed, the harbor emptied quickly as boats sought shelter.

Related Terms

  • antonym – all clear: a signal indicating an end to danger or that a threat has passed
  • synonym – warning signal: a signal giving notice of impending danger
