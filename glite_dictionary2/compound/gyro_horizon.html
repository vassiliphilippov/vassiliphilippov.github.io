1. ✈️ flight instrument ("gyro horizon indicator")

Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪroʊ həˈraɪzən / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪrəʊ həˈraɪzən / (BrE)
An aircraft instrument, using a gyroscope, that provides a simulated horizon to assist pilots in determining their orientation relative to the earth.
aircraft instrument simulating the horizon

Examples

Before takeoff, the pilot checked the gyro horizon to ensure it was functioning correctly.
In poor visibility, the gyro horizon allows aviators to maintain proper flight attitude.
The instructor explained how to interpret readings from the gyro horizon during instrument training.
