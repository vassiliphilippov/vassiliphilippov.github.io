1. ♾️ one-sided surface ("Mobius strip shape")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.bi.əs strɪp / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.bi.əs strɪp / (BrE)
A continuous surface with only one side and one boundary, formed by joining the ends of a rectangular strip after twisting one end 180 degrees.
surface with one side and one edge

Examples

Artists sometimes use the Mobius strip as a symbol of infinity or unity.
If you cut a Mobius strip along its center line, you get an even more curious surface.
The Mobius strip fascinated the students with its surprising one-sided property.
