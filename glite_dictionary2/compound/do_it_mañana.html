VERB
Pronunciation: / du ɪt mɑˈnjɑnə / (AmE), / duː ɪt mæˈnɪɑːnə / (BrE)
(informal) To postpone an action until tomorrow; to delay doing something until a later time, often with a relaxed attitude.
postpone something until tomorrow
