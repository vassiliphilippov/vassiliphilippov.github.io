1. ❤️ heart surgery ("undergo a coronary artery bypass graft")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəˌnɛri ˈɑːrtəri ˈbaɪˌpæs ɡræft / (AmE), / ˈkɒrənəri ˈɑːtəri ˈbaɪpɑːs ɡrɑːft / (BrE)
surgical procedure in which a blood vessel is grafted to bypass a blocked section of a coronary artery in order to improve blood supply to the heart
surgical procedure to bypass blocked coronary arteries

Examples

Doctors recommended a coronary artery bypass graft after the blockage was discovered during testing.
Recovery from a coronary artery bypass graft generally takes several weeks.
The patient underwent a coronary artery bypass graft to restore proper blood flow to his heart.

Related Terms

  • antonym – angioplasty: surgical repair or unblocking of a blood vessel, especially a coronary artery
  • synonym – bypass surgery: surgery to create a new pathway for blood flow around blocked arteries
