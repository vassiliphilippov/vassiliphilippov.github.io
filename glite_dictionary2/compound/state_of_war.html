1. ⚔️ official conflict ("declare a state of war")

Pronunciation: / steɪt əv wɔɚ / (AmE), / steɪt əv wɔː / (BrE)
A legal condition between nations in which the rules of warfare apply, created and ended by official declarations.
formal legal condition of war

Examples

A formal state of war was declared, triggering international wartime protocols.
He argued that certain actions were justified only during a state of war.
The two countries maintained a state of war despite the lack of open hostilities.

  • antonym – peace: a period or condition in which there is no war or fighting
