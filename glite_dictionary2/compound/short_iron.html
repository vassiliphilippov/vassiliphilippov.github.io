A type of golf club with a short shaft and a pitched face, designed for hitting short, high shots.
golf club for short, high shots

Examples

Golfers often rely on their short irons for accuracy in the final strokes of a hole.
He selected his short iron to approach the green from 120 yards away.
With his short iron, he hit a high shot that landed softly on the green.
