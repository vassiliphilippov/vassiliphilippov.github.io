1. ⚰️ place for funerals ("visit a funeral home")

Pronunciation: / ˈfjunɚəl hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈfjʊənərəl həʊm / (BrE)
A place where bodies are prepared for burial or cremation and where people can gather to pay their last respects to the deceased.
establishment for funerals and memorial services

Examples

Many people sent flowers to the funeral home to express their condolences.
The funeral home offered support and guidance throughout the difficult process.
The family gathered at the funeral home to say their final goodbyes.
