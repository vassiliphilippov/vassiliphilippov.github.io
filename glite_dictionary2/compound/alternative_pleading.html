1. ⚖️ legal claim options ("alternative pleading in court")

Pronunciation: / ɔlˈtɝːnətɪv ˈplidɪŋ / (AmE), / ɒlˈtɜːnətɪv ˈpliːdɪŋ / (BrE)
A legal pleading in which different and sometimes mutually exclusive sets of facts or claims are presented, making it unclear which one the party intends to pursue.
legal pleading with multiple or inconsistent claims

Examples

Because of uncertainties in the facts, the attorney relied on alternative pleading to protect the client’s interests.
The defense filed an alternative pleading to cover both possible scenarios presented by the evidence.
The judge allowed the alternative pleading, recognizing the complexity of the case.
