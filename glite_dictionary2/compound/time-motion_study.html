1. ⏱️ work process analysis ("time-motion study of factory work")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈmoʊʃən ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / taɪm ˈməʊʃən ˈstʌdi / (BrE)

analysis to improve work efficiency

Examples
