1. ✏️ grammar mistake ("fix a dangling participle")

a participle or participial phrase that is not clearly or logically related to the noun it modifies, often causing ambiguity or confusion in a sentence
unattached or misrelated participle in a sentence

Examples

A dangling participle can make your writing confusing or unintentionally humorous.

Writers should carefully revise their sentences to eliminate any dangling participle.
