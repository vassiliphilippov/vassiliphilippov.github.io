1. ⚖️ average with weights ("weighted mean of scores")

Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtɪd ˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪd ˌmiːn / (BrE)
An average calculated by giving different values or items varying degrees of importance, based on assigned weights.
an average with values weighted by importance

Examples

The weighted mean reflects the higher influence of certain measurements in the data set.
To account for different sample sizes, the researcher used a weighted mean in the final analysis.
When calculating overall grades, teachers often use a weighted mean rather than a simple average.
