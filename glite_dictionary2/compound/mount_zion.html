1. ⛰️ Jerusalem hill ("Mount Zion in Jerusalem")

Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˈzaɪ.ən / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈzaɪ.ən / (BrE)
A hill in Jerusalem, originally a fortress captured by King David, later associated with the Temple and often used as a synonym for Jerusalem.
