1. ⚖️ chief legal officer ("Attorney General leads investigations")

Pronunciation: / əˌtɝːni dʒəˈnɝːəl / (AmE), / əˌtɜːni dʒəˈnɜːrəl / (BrE); / əˈtɝni dʒəˈnɪrəl / (AmE), / əˈtɜːni dʒəˈnɛrəl / (BrE)
the chief legal officer who represents a government (country or state) in legal matters and is usually the head of the Department of Justice
chief legal officer of a government / principal legal officer of a government

Examples

After a long investigation, the Attorney General filed charges against several officials.
Each state has its own Attorney General responsible for upholding the law within its jurisdiction.
The Attorney General announced new measures to combat cybercrime today.
As Attorney General, she led the investigation into government corruption.
Each state in the U.S. has its own Attorney General responsible for legal affairs.
The Attorney General announced a new policy on cybercrime enforcement.
