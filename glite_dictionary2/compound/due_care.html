1. ⚖️ proper attention ("show due care in work")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / du ˈkɛr / (AmE), / djuː ˈkeə / (BrE)
the level of caution or attention that a reasonable person would show in a particular situation, especially as a standard in legal cases
legal standard of reasonable care

Examples

Drivers are required by law to take due care when approaching a pedestrian crossing.
If a company fails to show due care in maintaining its equipment, it may be held liable for any accidents.
The doctor was found guilty of not exercising due care during the operation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – negligence: failure to exercise due care, resulting in damage or injury
