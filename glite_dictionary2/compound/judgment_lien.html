1. ⚖️ legal claim ("judgment lien on property")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌdʒmənt liːn / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒmənt liːn / (BrE)
A legal claim on a debtor's property granted to a creditor by court judgment, which may be enforced to satisfy the debt.
court-ordered legal claim on property

Examples

A judgment lien allows the creditor to claim proceeds from the sale of the debtor's property.
He was unable to sell his land because it was encumbered by a judgment lien.
The creditor obtained a judgment lien on the debtor's house after winning the lawsuit.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lien: a legal right to keep possession of property until a debt is paid
