1. ☢️ nuclear weapon ("detonate a plutonium bomb")

Pronunciation: / pluːˈtoʊniəm bɑm / (AmE), / pluːˈtəʊniəm bɒm / (BrE)
a type of nuclear weapon that uses plutonium-239 as its core to release enormous energy through nuclear fission
nuclear weapon using plutonium core

Examples

A plutonium bomb relies on the rapid assembly of a supercritical mass of plutonium-239.
The United States developed the first plutonium bomb during World War II.
The dangers of a plutonium bomb include radioactive fallout and immense explosive power.
