1. ☕ coffee with milk ("a cup of cafe au lait")

Pronunciation: / kæˈfeɪ oʊ ˈleɪ / (AmE), / kaˈfeɪ əʊ ˈleɪ / (BrE)
A drink made with equal parts of hot coffee and hot milk, often served in a cup or bowl.
coffee mixed with equal parts hot milk

Examples

She ordered a cafe au lait to go with her croissant.
The French-style cafe au lait is made with coffee and hot milk in equal parts.
