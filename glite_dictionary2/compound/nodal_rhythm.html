1. ❤️ heart beat pattern ("nodal rhythm on ECG")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊ.dl ˈrɪð.əm / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.dl ˈrɪð.əm / (BrE)
a cardiac rhythm in which the heartbeat is controlled by the atrioventricular node rather than the sinoatrial node
heart rhythm initiated by AV node

Examples

Doctors noted the presence of a nodal rhythm instead of the usual sinus rhythm.
In some cases, a nodal rhythm indicates that the sinoatrial node is not functioning properly.
The electrocardiogram showed a nodal rhythm following the patient's surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sinus rhythm: normal cardiac rhythm originating at the sinoatrial node
