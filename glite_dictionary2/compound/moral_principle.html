1. ⚖️ ethical rule ("follow moral principle in life")

Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrəl ˈprɪnsəpəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒrəl ˈprɪnsɪpl / (BrE)

belief guiding right and wrong behavior / standard of right and wrong conduct

Examples
