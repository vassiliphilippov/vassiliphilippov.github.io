1. ❤️ heart valve problem ("mitral valve prolapse diagnosis")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪtrəl vælv proʊˈlæps / (AmE), / ˈmaɪtrəl vælv prəʊˈlæps / (BrE)
A condition in which the mitral valve of the heart does not close properly, allowing blood to leak backward from the left ventricle into the left atrium.
improper closing of the mitral heart valve

Examples

People with mitral valve prolapse may not experience any symptoms at all.
Routine check-ups can help detect conditions like mitral valve prolapse early.
The doctor diagnosed her with mitral valve prolapse after detecting a heart murmur.
