1. ✈️ no plane change ("direct flight to Paris")

Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkt flaɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkt flaɪt / (BrE)
A flight between two places that may have one or more stops but does not require passengers to change aircraft.
flight with stops but no aircraft change

Examples

Although the direct flight stops in Chicago, travelers stay on the same plane throughout the journey.
The airline offers several direct flights from New York to Los Angeles each day.
We chose a direct flight to save time, even though it made a stop along the way.
