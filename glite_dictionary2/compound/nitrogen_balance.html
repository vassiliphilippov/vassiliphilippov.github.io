1. ⚖️ nutrient status ("nitrogen balance in the body")

Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪ.tɹə.dʒən ˈbæ.ləns / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.trɪ.dʒən ˈbæ.ləns / (BrE)
the difference between the amount of nitrogen taken in by an organism or soil and the amount lost or excreted, used as an indicator of nutritional or soil health status
difference between nitrogen intake and loss

Examples

A negative nitrogen balance can indicate malnutrition or illness in patients.
Farmers monitor nitrogen balance to optimize soil fertility and crop yields.
Proper nutrition is essential to maintain a nitrogen balance in the human body.
