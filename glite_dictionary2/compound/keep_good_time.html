Pronunciation: / kip ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / kiːp ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
(of a clock or watch) to show the correct time consistently; to be accurate in measuring time.
be accurate in timekeeping

Examples

She checked to make sure her new clock would keep good time before buying it.
The school bell keeps good time, ringing exactly at the top of each hour.
