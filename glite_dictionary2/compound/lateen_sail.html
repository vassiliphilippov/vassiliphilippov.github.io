1. ⛵ triangular sail ("lateen sail on a boat")

Pronunciation: / ləˈtin seɪl / (AmE), / ləˈtiːn seɪl / (BrE)
A triangular sail set on a long yard mounted at an angle on the mast, used especially on boats in the Mediterranean region.
triangular Mediterranean sail

Examples

The lateen sail allowed sailors to tack efficiently against the wind in the Mediterranean.
The fisherman rigged his boat with a lateen sail before heading out to sea.
Traditional dhows are easily recognized by their distinctive lateen sail.
