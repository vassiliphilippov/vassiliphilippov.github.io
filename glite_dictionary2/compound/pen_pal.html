NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn ˌpæl / (AmE), / ˈpɛn ˌpæl / (BrE)
a person with whom one becomes friendly by exchanging letters, especially someone in a different country or location
friend by regular correspondence

Examples

As a child, I had a pen pal in Japan and we exchanged letters every month.
My pen pal and I finally met in person after years of correspondence.
Writing to a pen pal can help you learn about different cultures and improve your language skills.
