1. ☠️ toxic plant ("eat a poison-berry")

Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzənˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪz(ə)nˌbɛri / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with white flowers, hairy poisonous foliage, and black berries that can be poisonous or edible.
a Eurasian plant with variable toxicity in berries

Examples

Children are warned not to eat the poison-berry due to its unpredictable effects.
Some birds can safely consume poison-berries that are toxic to humans.
The poison-berry plant thrived along the fence, its dark fruit standing out amid the leaves.
