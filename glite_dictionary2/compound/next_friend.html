1. ⚖️ legal representative ("next friend in court")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / nɛkst frɛnd / (AmE), / nɛkst frɛnd / (BrE)
(law) A person who represents an infant or someone unable to appear on their own behalf in a court of law.
legal representative for infant or incapable person

Examples

If an adult is found mentally incapacitated, a next friend can initiate legal proceedings on their behalf.
The claim was filed by a next friend since the plaintiff was underage.
The court appointed a next friend to represent the child's interests during the trial.
