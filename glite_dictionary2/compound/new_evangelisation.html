1. ⛪ renewed church mission ("new evangelisation in Europe")

Pronunciation: / nu iˌvæn.dʒə.ləˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / njuː iˌvan.dʒə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the renewed initiative within the Catholic Church, especially since the late 20th century, to reawaken and strengthen Christian faith in regions where it has declined, particularly in Europe
Catholic Church movement to revive faith

Examples

Many church leaders have organised conferences to discuss strategies for the new evangelisation.
Pope John Paul II was a key proponent of the new evangelisation in the late twentieth century.
The new evangelisation aims to rekindle faith among Catholics in traditionally Christian countries.
