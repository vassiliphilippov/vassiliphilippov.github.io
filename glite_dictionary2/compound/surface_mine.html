1. ⛏️ to dig for minerals ("to surface mine coal")

Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfɪs maɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs maɪn / (BrE)
to extract ore or minerals by removing earth and rock from the surface, rather than by underground mining
extract by removing earth at surface

Examples

After decades of surface mining, the once-forested hills were left barren and scarred.
Several companies surface mine coal throughout the region, dramatically altering the landscape.
The new regulations aim to minimize environmental damage caused when operations surface mine for copper.

Related Terms

  • synonym – strip-mine: to mine by removing large strips of soil and rock
  • antonym – underground mine: to mine minerals by extracting them from below the earth's surface
