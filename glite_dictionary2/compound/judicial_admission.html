NOUN
Pronunciation: / dʒuˈdɪʃəl ədˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒuˈdɪʃəl ədˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
(law) a formal statement, concession, or agreement made by a party or their attorney in a court proceeding, which is accepted as evidence and is binding in the case; typically must be written or entered into the court record.
formal statement or concession in court
