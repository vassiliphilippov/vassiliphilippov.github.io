1. ❤️ heart valve narrowing ("pulmonary stenosis diagnosis")

Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlməˌnɛri stəˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌlmənəri stɪˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a condition in which there is an abnormal narrowing of the opening from the right ventricle of the heart into the pulmonary artery
abnormal narrowing of pulmonary artery opening

Examples

Mild cases of pulmonary stenosis often require only regular monitoring.
Surgery may be necessary to correct severe pulmonary stenosis.
The child was diagnosed with pulmonary stenosis after a routine heart examination.
