1. ♻️ energy spreads ("the second law of thermodynamics explains entropy")

the principle in physics stating that energy spontaneously spreads from regions of higher concentration to regions of lower concentration, resulting in an increase in entropy in any spontaneous process
law stating entropy increases in spontaneous processes
