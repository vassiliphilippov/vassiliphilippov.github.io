1. ❤️ heart chamber ("heart ventricle pumps blood")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / hɑrt ˈvɛntrɪkəl / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈvɛntrɪkəl / (BrE)
Either of the two lower chambers of the heart that receive blood from an atrium and pump it to the arteries.
lower chamber of the heart

Examples

An ultrasound was used to examine the function of the heart ventricle after the patient’s heart attack.
Damage to the right heart ventricle can affect blood flow to the lungs.
The surgeon explained that the left heart ventricle is responsible for pumping oxygenated blood to the body.
