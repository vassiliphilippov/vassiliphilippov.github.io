NOUN

something that attracts attention or interest because it is new, flashy, or appealing, but lacks real importance or substance; a distraction.
attention-grabbing but superficial distraction

Examples

In meetings, he is easily distracted by the latest bright shiny object instead of finishing tasks.
The new software was just a bright shiny object—it looked impressive but didn't solve their real problems.
They warned her not to chase every bright shiny object that appeared promising.
