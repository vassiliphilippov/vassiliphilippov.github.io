1. ☠️ poisonous herb ("poisonous belladonna plant")

Pronunciation: / ˌbɛləˈdɑnə plænt / (AmE), / ˌbɛləˈdɒnə plɑːnt / (BrE)
A poisonous perennial Eurasian herb (Atropa belladonna) with reddish bell-shaped flowers and shiny black berries, whose roots and leaves yield the alkaloid atropine.
poisonous plant (Atropa belladonna) with black berries

Examples

Atropine, a valuable medicinal compound, is extracted from the belladonna plant.
Botanists often study the belladonna plant for its unique chemical properties.
The berries of the belladonna plant are highly toxic and should never be eaten.
