1. ⚖️ punishment payment ("exemplary damages for misconduct")

Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɛmplɛri ˈdæmɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛmpləri ˈdamɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
compensation awarded by a court, beyond actual damages, as a form of punishment for malicious or willful misconduct
additional legal damages as punitive measure

Examples

Exemplary damages are rarely granted unless there is clear evidence of malice.
Because of the defendant's reckless behavior, exemplary damages were included in the judgment.
The court awarded exemplary damages to deter others from committing similar fraud.
