1. ✈️ jet engine ("modern turbofan engine")

Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.boʊ.fæn ˈɛn.dʒən / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.bə.fæn ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (BrE)
A type of jet engine in which a turbine-driven fan forces additional air into the engine to provide extra thrust and improve efficiency.
