1. ⚖️ water use law ("riparian right to water use")

Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛəriən raɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈpeəriən raɪt / (BrE)
the legal right of a landowner whose property borders a river or stream to make reasonable use of the water
legal right of access to water for landowners

Examples

Disputes over riparian right often arise between neighboring property owners along a river.
The farmer asserted his riparian right to draw water from the stream for irrigation.
The new regulations aim to clarify how riparian right applies during periods of drought.
