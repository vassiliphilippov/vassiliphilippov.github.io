1. ✈️ speed limit (aircraft) ("break the heat barrier")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / hit ˈbæriər / (AmE), / hiːt ˈbæriə / (BrE)
a limit to the speed of an aircraft imposed by aerodynamic heating, beyond which structural damage or failure may occur
limit to speed due to aerodynamic heating

Examples

As speeds increased, pilots encountered the dangerous effects of the heat barrier on their aircraft's structure.
Engineers had to develop new materials to help aircraft safely cross the heat barrier during high-speed flight.
The prototype jet broke the heat barrier, opening new possibilities for supersonic travel.
