1. ☎️ long-distance phone ("business WATS line")

Pronunciation: / wæts laɪn / (AmE), / wæts laɪn / (BrE)
A telephone line providing long-distance service at fixed rates for specified geographic zones, especially used for business purposes.
long-distance telephone service line

Examples
