A fraction in which the numerator is equal to or greater than the denominator.
fraction with numerator ≥ denominator

Examples

A fraction like improper fraction 7/4 can also be written as a mixed number, such as 1¾.
To convert a improper fraction to a mixed number, divide the numerator by the denominator.
When you divide 9 by 2, you get the improper fraction 9/2.
