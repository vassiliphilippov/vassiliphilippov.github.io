1. ⚙️ straight-tooth gear ("spur gear with straight teeth")

Pronunciation: / spɝ ɡɪr / (AmE), / spɜː ɡɪə / (BrE)
a gear with straight teeth that are parallel to the axis of rotation and mesh with another gear in the same plane
gear with straight, parallel teeth

Examples

He replaced the stripped spur gear to restore the clock's movement.
Spur gears are commonly used when the requirement is to transfer motion between parallel shafts.
The machine malfunctioned because a spur gear was damaged.
