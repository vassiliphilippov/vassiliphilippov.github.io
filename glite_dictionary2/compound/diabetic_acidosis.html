NOUN
Pronunciation: / daɪəˈbɛtɪk æsɪˌdoʊsɪs / (AmE), / daɪəˈbiːtɪk æsɪˈdəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A serious medical condition involving excess acid and accumulation of ketone bodies in the body, usually occurring in people with diabetes mellitus.
acidic condition related to diabetes

Examples

Early detection and treatment are crucial in cases of diabetic acidosis to prevent serious complications.
The patient was hospitalized due to symptoms of diabetic acidosis, including nausea and rapid breathing.
Untreated type 1 diabetes can quickly lead to diabetic acidosis, which is a life-threatening emergency.
