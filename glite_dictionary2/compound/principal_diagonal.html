the diagonal of a square matrix that runs from the upper left corner to the lower right corner
main diagonal of a square matrix

Examples

Adding the elements on the principal diagonal gives you the trace of the matrix.
Every identity matrix has ones on its principal diagonal and zeros elsewhere.
The principal diagonal contains all the entries where the row and column indices are equal.
