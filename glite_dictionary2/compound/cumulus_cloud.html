1. ☁️ fluffy cloud ("white cumulus cloud sky")

Pronunciation: / ˈkjumjələs klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈkjuːmjʊləs klaʊd / (BrE)
a large, white, fluffy cloud with a rounded top and flat base, often seen in fair weather
rounded, fluffy fair-weather cloud

Examples

A cumulus cloud usually indicates stable weather and often forms on warm days.
On a sunny afternoon, several cumulus clouds drifted slowly across the blue sky.
The children looked up at a cumulus cloud that resembled a giant rabbit in the sky.
