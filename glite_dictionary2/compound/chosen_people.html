1. ✡️ God's special group ("the chosen people of God")

Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊzən ˈpipəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊzn ˈpiːpl / (BrE)
a group of people who believe that they are selected by God for a special purpose or favor, especially in a religious context
people believing themselves chosen by God

Examples

Many have referred to the Israelites as the chosen people in the context of the Old Testament.
Some religious groups consider themselves the chosen people of their faith.
