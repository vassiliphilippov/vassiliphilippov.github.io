NOUN

A law designed to encourage free competition in the market by prohibiting monopolies and other unfair business practices.
law against monopolies to promote competition

Examples

Many countries have developed their own versions of antitrust law to prevent unfair business practices.
The government filed a lawsuit under the antitrust law to break up the large corporation.
The merger was blocked because it violated existing antitrust law.
