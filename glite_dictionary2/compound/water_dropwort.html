1. ☠️ poisonous plant ("water dropwort plant")

Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ ˈdrɑpˌwɝt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈdrɒpˌwɜːt / (BrE)
a European poisonous herb with tuberous roots, yellow juice that stains the skin, yellow flowers, and celery-like foliage; all parts are extremely poisonous
poisonous European herb with tuberous roots

Examples

Farmers are warned to keep livestock away from water dropwort because of its extreme toxicity.
The leaves of the water dropwort resemble those of celery, making it easy to mistake.
The yellow juice from water dropwort can stain the skin and is highly poisonous.
