1. ☠️ poisoned state ("dangerous toxic condition")

Pronunciation: / ˈtɑksɪk kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk kənˈdɪʃən / (BrE)

medical state caused by toxins or poisons

Examples
