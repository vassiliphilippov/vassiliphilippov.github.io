1. ✋ ask to stop ("just say when finished")

Pronunciation: / dʒʌst seɪ / (AmE), / dʒʌst seɪ / (BrE)
Used to prompt someone to state when something being offered, such as food or drink, is enough or sufficient.
prompt to indicate when enough is enough

Examples

I'll keep pouring water, so just say when you've had enough.
I'm adding sugar—just say when it's sweet enough for you.
If you want more salad, just say and I'll serve you some more.
