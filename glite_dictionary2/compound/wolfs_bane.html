1. ☠️ poisonous plant ("wolf's bane plant")

Pronunciation: / wʊlfz beɪn / (AmE), / wʊlfs beɪn / (BrE)
a poisonous Eurasian perennial plant with broad rounded leaves, yellow flowers, and a fibrous rootstock, belonging to the genus Doronicum
a poisonous yellow-flowered plant

Examples

Gardeners should handle wolf's bane with care due to its poisonous nature.
In folklore, wolf's bane was reputed to ward off evil spirits and wolves.
