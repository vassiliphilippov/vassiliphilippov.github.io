1. ❄️ powdery snow ("dry snow falls lightly")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / draɪ snoʊ / (AmE), / draɪ snəʊ / (BrE)
Snow with very little or no liquid water content, making it less dense and not sticky.
snow with low water content; not sticky

Examples

Because dry snow doesn't stick together, it's hard to make snowballs or snowmen.
Freshly fallen dry snow can be easily blown away by the wind.
The ski resort is famous for its excellent dry snow conditions during winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – powder snow: fresh, dry, loose snow; often used as a synonym for dry snow
  • antonym – wet snow: snow that contains significant liquid water, making it denser and stickier
