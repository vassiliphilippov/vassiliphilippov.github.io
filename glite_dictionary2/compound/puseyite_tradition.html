1. ⛪ Anglican revival ("Puseyite tradition in Anglicanism")

Pronunciation: / ˈpjuːziˌaɪt trəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈpjuːziˌaɪt trəˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
A movement within Anglicanism advocating a return to traditional Catholic beliefs and practices, associated with Edward Bouverie Pusey and the Oxford Movement in the 19th century.
Anglican movement for Catholic revival

Examples

Her theological stance reflects core values of the Puseyite tradition within Anglicanism.
Many churches influenced by the Puseyite tradition incorporate elaborate rituals into their worship services.
The Puseyite tradition emphasized the importance of sacraments and apostolic succession in the Church.
