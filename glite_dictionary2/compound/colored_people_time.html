NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚd ˈpipəl taɪm / (AmE), / ˈkʌləd ˈpiːpl taɪm / (BrE)
(offensive) A humorous or stereotypical term referring to the perception that Black people are habitually late or have a relaxed attitude toward punctuality, sometimes used within the Black community, but considered offensive.
(offensive) stereotype about Black people and tardiness

Examples

References to Colored People Time are now widely criticized as perpetuating harmful stereotypes.
She apologized for arriving late, saying she was running on Colored People Time.
The meeting started late, and everyone joked it must be Colored People Time.
