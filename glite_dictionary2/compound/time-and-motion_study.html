1. ⏱️ work efficiency test ("conduct a time-and-motion study")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / taɪm ən ˈmoʊʃən ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / taɪm ən ˈməʊʃən ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
A study that examines the way a specific job is performed in order to identify the most efficient method, minimizing time and effort required.
analysis for work efficiency

Examples

A time-and-motion study revealed that workers could save two hours a day by rearranging their workstations.
After the time-and-motion study, several steps in the process were eliminated to streamline operations.
The company conducted a time-and-motion study to improve productivity on the factory floor.
