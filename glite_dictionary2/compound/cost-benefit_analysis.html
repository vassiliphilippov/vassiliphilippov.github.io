1. ⚖️ weigh pros cons ("do a cost-benefit analysis")

Pronunciation: / ˈkɑst ˈbɛnɪfɪt əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɒst ˈbɛnɪfɪt əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
A systematic process of comparing the costs and benefits of a decision or project to determine whether the benefits outweigh the costs.
systematic comparison of costs and benefits

Examples

Performing a thorough cost-benefit analysis helped the company avoid unnecessary expenses.
The government conducted a cost-benefit analysis before approving the new infrastructure project.
We must include a cost-benefit analysis in our proposal to justify the investment.
