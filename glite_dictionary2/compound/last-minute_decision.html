1. ⏰ late choice ("make a last-minute decision")

Pronunciation: / læst ˈmɪnɪt dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈmɪnɪt dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a choice or judgment made at the latest possible time, often just before something is to happen or be finalized
choice made at the very last moment

Examples

His last-minute decision cost the team the victory they had hoped for.
She made a last-minute decision to join the trip, surprising all her friends.
The wedding was saved by a last-minute decision to move it indoors because of the rain.

Related Terms

  • antonym – premeditated decision: a decision made after careful thought or planning
  • synonym – snap decision: a decision made quickly, often without much thought
