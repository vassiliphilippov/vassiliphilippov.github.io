1. ↔️ keeping distance ("social distancing rules")

Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ʃəl ˈdɪs.tənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ˈdɪs.tənsɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of maintaining a safe physical distance from others and avoiding large gatherings to prevent the spread of infectious diseases.
keeping physical distance to prevent disease spread

Examples

Continuous social distancing measures helped reduce the rate of infection in the city.
Many schools implemented social distancing to protect students and staff during the pandemic.
Signs reminded customers to observe social distancing while waiting in line at the grocery store.

  • synonym – physical distancing: keeping physical space between individuals to reduce disease transmission
