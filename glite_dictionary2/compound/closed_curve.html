1. ⭕ loop shape ("closed curve like a circle")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / kloʊzd kɝv / (AmE), / kləʊzd kɜːv / (BrE)
a continuous curve in which the starting point and ending point are the same, having no endpoints
continuous line forming a loop; no endpoints

Examples

A closed curve such as a circle or an ellipse has no endpoints.
In geometry, a polygon is formed by connecting line segments into a closed curve.
The artist drew a closed curve to outline the shape of a balloon.
