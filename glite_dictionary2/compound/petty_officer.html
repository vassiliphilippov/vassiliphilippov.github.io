1. ⚓ Navy sergeant ("Navy petty officer")

Pronunciation: / ˈpɛt̬i ˌɑːfɪsər / (AmE), / ˈpɛti ˌɒfɪsə / (BrE)
A noncommissioned officer in the Navy or Coast Guard, equivalent in rank to a sergeant in the Army.
Navy/Coast Guard noncommissioned officer

Examples

After serving for eight years, she was promoted to petty officer in the Navy.
As a petty officer, he supervised training sessions for new recruits.
