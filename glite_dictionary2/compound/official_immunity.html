1. ⚖️ legal protection ("official immunity from lawsuits")

Pronunciation: / əˈfɪʃ.əl ɪˈmjuː.nə.t̬i / (AmE), / əˈfɪʃ.əl ɪˈmjuː.nɪ.ti / (BrE)
Legal protection that prevents a public official from being held personally liable for actions taken when exercising official authority.
legal protection for public officials

Examples

Debates continue over whether official immunity should protect officials from lawsuits by citizens.
The mayor was granted official immunity for actions taken during the state of emergency.
Without official immunity, public servants might hesitate to make critical decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – qualified immunity: type of legal immunity for government officials that shields them from liability unless they violated clearly established law
  • synonym – sovereign immunity: legal doctrine preventing lawsuits against the government without its consent
