a symbol or term in logic and mathematics that indicates the existence of at least one thing for which a proposition is true; usually represented as '∃'
logical symbol asserting existence

Examples

In formal proofs, the existential operator is usually written as the symbol '∃'.
The existential operator is used in logic to show that some value satisfies a specific condition.
The statement 'There exists an x such that x > 0' uses the existential operator.
