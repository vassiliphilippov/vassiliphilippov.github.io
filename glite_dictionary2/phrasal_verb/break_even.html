1. ⚖️ no profit ("break even on costs")

VERB
Pronunciation: / breɪk ˈivən / (AmE), / breɪk ˈiːvən / (BrE)
to have expenses equal to income, so that there is no profit and no loss
have no net profit or loss / make neither profit nor loss
