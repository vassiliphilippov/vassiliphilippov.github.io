1. ✋ to go away ("to sod off angrily")

Pronunciation: / sɑd ɔf / (AmE), / sɒd ɒf / (BrE)
to go away or leave someone alone, often used as a rude or forceful command
tell someone to go away rudely

Examples

He was being annoying, so I told him to sod off.
His boss shouted at him to sod off and stop complaining.
The kids wouldn't stop bothering us until we finally told them to sod off.
