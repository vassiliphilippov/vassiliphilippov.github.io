1. ⚠️ to be punished ("to get in trouble at school")

Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ɪn ˈtrʌbəl / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɪn ˈtrʌbəl / (BrE)
to become subject to criticism, punishment, or other negative consequences for one's actions
be punished or face consequences

Examples

He got in trouble at work for being late too often.
If you skip school, you might get in trouble with your parents.
She always tries to follow the rules so she doesn’t get in trouble.
