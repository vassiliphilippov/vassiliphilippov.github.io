1. ✨ to make different ("set apart special talents")

Pronunciation: / sɛt əˈpɑrt / (AmE), / sɛt əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to make someone or something different or special by separating, distinguishing, or reserving them for a particular purpose
select for a special purpose / separate or distinguish from others

They set apart these funds especially for emergency repairs.
