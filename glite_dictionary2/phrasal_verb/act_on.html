1. ✅ to follow advice ("to act on advice")

VERB
Pronunciation: / ˈækt ɑn / (AmE), / ˈakt ɒn / (BrE); / ækt ɑn / (AmE), / ækt ɒn / (BrE)

take action according to advice or information / take action in response to something

Examples

She decided to act on her doctor's advice and started exercising regularly.
The government needs to act on the recommendations in the report.
