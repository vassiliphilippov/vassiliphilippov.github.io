1. ⏳ to be patient ("to bear with me patiently")

Pronunciation: / bɛr wɪð / (AmE), / beə wɪð / (BrE)
to be patient or tolerant with someone or something, especially while waiting for something to happen or be resolved.
be patient or tolerant with

Examples

I know this is taking a while, but please bear with me as I explain the details.
She asked the audience to bear with her while she fixed the microphone.
Thank you for your understanding and for bearing with us during these technical difficulties.
