1. ✏️ to remove ("cross out a word")

VERB

to draw a line through a word, name, or item to show it should be removed, ignored, or is no longer needed
draw a line through to remove / remove from a list by drawing a line through

Examples

Remember to cross out any outdated information before submitting the report.
