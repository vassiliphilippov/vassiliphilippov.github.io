VERB
Pronunciation: / dræɡ daʊn / (AmE), / dræɡ daʊn / (BrE)

pull or force downward

Examples

The extra equipment started to drag down the balloon, lowering it to the ground.
The weight of the wet clothes began to drag down the washing line.
