1. ⬆️ to lift higher ("to push up a window")

Pronunciation: / pʊʃ ʌp / (AmE), / pʊʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to move or lift something to a higher position by pushing from below or behind
force something upwards by pushing / move or lift by pushing from beneath / raise by pushing from below
