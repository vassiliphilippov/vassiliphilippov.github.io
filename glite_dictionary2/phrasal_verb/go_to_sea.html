1. ⛵ to become sailor ("to go to sea as a sailor")

Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tu siː / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə siː / (BrE)
to begin or engage in a life or career sailing on the ocean, often as a sailor or aboard a ship
embark on a maritime career or journey

Examples

After graduation, he decided to go to sea and explore the world as a sailor.
Many young men in coastal towns would traditionally go to sea to seek adventure and fortune.
She had always wanted to go to sea, inspired by stories of distant lands and ocean voyages.

Related Terms

  • antonym – return to port: come back to land after spending time at sea
  • synonym – sail: travel on water in a ship or boat, especially as a sailor
