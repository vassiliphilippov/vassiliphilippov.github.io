1. ⏳ to finally do ("to get around to cleaning")

Pronunciation: / ɡɛt əˈraʊnd tu / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈraʊnd tuː / (BrE)
to finally do something after intending to do it for some time, often despite difficulties or a lack of time
do something after delay or obstacles

Examples

I meant to clean the garage last weekend, but I never got around to it.
It took him months to get around to calling his old friends.
She finally got around to answering all her emails after her vacation.
