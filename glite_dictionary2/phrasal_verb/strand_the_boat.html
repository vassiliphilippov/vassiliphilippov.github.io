1. ⛵ to trap ("to strand the boat on shore")

Pronunciation: / strænd ðə boʊt / (AmE), / strænd ðə bəʊt / (BrE)
to leave a boat in a position where it cannot move, especially on shore or in shallow water
leave a boat stuck and immobile

Examples

Fishermen often warn against navigating at low tide to avoid stranding the boat.
Heavy winds stranded the boat far from the harbor.
The captain accidentally stranded the boat on a hidden reef during the night.
