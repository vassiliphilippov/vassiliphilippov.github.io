1. ⏳ run steadily ("tick over quietly")

Pronunciation: / tɪk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tɪk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to operate or run in a steady, continuous, or idle way, often without doing much or being very active
(of engines) run slowly without working hard / operate steadily or smoothly
