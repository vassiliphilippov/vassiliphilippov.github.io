1. ⏳ to do nothing ("to wait around for news")

Pronunciation: / weɪt əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / weɪt əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to spend time doing nothing in particular while expecting something to happen or someone to arrive
spend time idly waiting

Examples

If you wait around long enough, someone will eventually give you the information you need.
She didn’t want to wait around all afternoon for a phone call that might never come.
We had to wait around at the airport for several hours because of the delay.
