Pronunciation: / kʌm tə ˈnʌθɪŋ / (AmE), / kʌm tʊ ˈnʌθɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Her hopes for promotion came to nothing when the company restructured.
If all our efforts come to nothing, we will have to try a different approach.
