1. ⚾ to bunt and run ("to drag a bunt past the pitcher")

Pronunciation: / dræɡ ə bʌnt / (AmE), / dræɡ ə bʌnt / (BrE)
(in baseball) to bunt the ball so that it rolls slowly, typically by allowing the ball to meet the bat as the batter moves toward first base, aiming to reach base safely.
bunt a baseball short while running

Examples

He tried to drag a bunt down the first-base line to surprise the infielders.
She dragged a bunt and managed to beat the throw to first.
The speedy player often drags a bunt when he needs to get on base quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bunt: to gently tap a pitched ball with the bat, without swinging, in baseball
