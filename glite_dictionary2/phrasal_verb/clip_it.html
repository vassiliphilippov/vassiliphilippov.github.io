1. ✂️ to cut down ("to clip it shorter")

Pronunciation: / klɪp ɪt / (AmE), / klɪp ɪt / (BrE)
to cut out, remove, or shorten a section of something, often a video or document; sometimes used informally to mean 'be quiet' or 'stop talking'
