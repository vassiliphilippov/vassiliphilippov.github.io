1. ⬇️ spread gradually ("filter down through the company")

Pronunciation: / ˈfɪltɚ daʊn / (AmE), / ˈfɪltə daʊn / (BrE)
(of information, decisions, etc.) to become known or available to more people at lower levels in an organization or society; (of selections) to reduce a group of choices to a smaller set.
gradually spread, or narrow down a group

Examples

Once decisions are made at the top, they usually filter down through the entire company.
The revised procedures will filter down to employees by the end of the week.
We need to filter down these candidates to find the right fit for the role.
