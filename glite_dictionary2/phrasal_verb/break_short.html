1. ⏹️ to interrupt ("break short a meeting")

VERB
Pronunciation: / breɪk ʃɔrt / (AmE), / breɪk ʃɔːt / (BrE)
to interrupt or bring something to an end before its natural or planned conclusion
end or interrupt prematurely

Examples

She had to break short her speech due to an emergency.
The thunderstorm forced us to break short the outdoor party.
When the fire alarm sounded, the meeting was broken short immediately.
