1. ⏳ to stop gradually ("to phase out old products")

Pronunciation: / feɪz aʊt / (AmE), / feɪz aʊt / (BrE)
To gradually stop using or producing something; to bring something to an end in stages.
end or stop something gradually

Examples

Plastic bags are being phased out in many stores to reduce waste.
The company will phase out the old software over the next year.
They plan to phase out coal-fired power plants by 2030.
