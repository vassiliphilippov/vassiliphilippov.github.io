1. ⭐ to be essential ("play a vital role in success")

Pronunciation: / pleɪ ə ˈvaɪtəl roʊl / (AmE), / pleɪ ə ˈvaɪtəl rəʊl / (BrE)
to have a significant or essential influence on something or to be extremely important in a situation or process
to be extremely important or influential

Examples

Teachers often play a vital role in shaping a child's future.
Volunteers have played a vital role during the disaster recovery process.
