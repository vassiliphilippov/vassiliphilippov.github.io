1. ✂️ to do less work ("to cut corners on quality")

VERB
Pronunciation: / kʌt ˈkɔrnərz / (AmE), / kʌt ˈkɔːnəz / (BrE); / kʌt ˈkɔrnɚz / (AmE), / kʌt ˈkɔːnəz / (BrE)
