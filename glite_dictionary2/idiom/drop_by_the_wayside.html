1. ❌ to give up ("to drop by the wayside after problems")

Pronunciation: / drɑp baɪ ðə ˈweɪ.saɪd / (AmE), / drɒp baɪ ðə ˈweɪ.saɪd / (BrE)
to stop participating or making progress, especially because of difficulty or defeat; to give up or fail to continue
cease participation or effort; give up

Examples

Many students drop by the wayside before finishing the demanding program.
We must keep going and not drop by the wayside when things get tough.
