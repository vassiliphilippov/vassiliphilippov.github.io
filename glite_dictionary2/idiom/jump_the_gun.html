1. ⏩ act too soon ("jump the gun on plans")

Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp ðə ɡʌn / (AmE), / dʒʌmp ðə ɡʌn / (BrE)
to act before the proper or expected time, especially by doing something too soon
act prematurely or too soon

Examples

If we send the invitations now, we might jump the gun since the date isn't confirmed.
They jumped the gun by releasing the product before it passed all safety tests.
Try not to jump the gun during the negotiations, or we could ruin the deal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – act prematurely: to do something before the appropriate or proper time
  • antonym – hold back: to wait or delay taking action
