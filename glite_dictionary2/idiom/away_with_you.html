1. ✋ go away ("away with you now")

Pronunciation: / əˈweɪ wɪð ju / (AmE), / əˈweɪ wɪð juː / (BrE)
used to command someone to leave or go away, often as a forceful or dismissive rejection.
expression to tell someone to leave

Examples

'Away with you and never return!' the queen commanded sternly.
As soon as he saw the intruder, the old man shouted, 'away with you!'
She scoffed and said, 'away with you—I have no time for nonsense.'
