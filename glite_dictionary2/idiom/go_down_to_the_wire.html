1⏳finish at endgo down to the wire in a race

1. ⏳ finish at end ("go down to the wire in a race")

Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ daʊn tə ðə ˈwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ daʊn tə ðə ˈwaɪə / (BrE)
(of a competition or situation) to continue until the very last possible moment, especially when the outcome is uncertain until the end.
remain undecided until the last moment

Examples

The championship basketball game went down to the wire before our team secured the victory.
The election went down to the wire, with the winner declared by just a few votes.
Their negotiations are going down to the wire as the deadline approaches.

Related Terms

  • synonym – come down to the wire: reach a point where the outcome is decided at the last moment
