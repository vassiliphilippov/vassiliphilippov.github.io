1. ⏳ order of arrival ("first come, first served tickets")

Pronunciation: / ˌfɜrst ˈkʌm ˌfɜrst ˈsɝvd / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈkʌm ˌfɜːst ˈsɜːvd / (BrE)
used to indicate that people will be dealt with or given something in the order in which they arrive or request it, with no exceptions made for later arrivals
service or priority given by order of arrival

Examples

Admission to the lecture is first come, first served, so lines formed an hour before it started.
Camping spots at the park are first come, first served, so arrive early to get a good one.
The student tickets for the play are distributed on a first come, first served basis.
