1. ✅ certainly ("by all means go ahead")

Pronunciation: / baɪ ɔl minz / (AmE), / baɪ ɔːl miːnz / (BrE)

certainly; giving permission

Examples

He said I could use his office by all means whenever he's away.
If you have any questions, by all means ask them now.
