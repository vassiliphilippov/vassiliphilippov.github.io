1. ⏳ do not expect ("don't hold your breath for an answer")

Pronunciation: / doʊnt hoʊld jɚ brɛθ / (AmE), / dəʊnt həʊld jɔː brɛθ / (BrE)
used to tell someone not to expect something to happen soon, or at all
used to say something is unlikely to happen

Examples

If you're waiting for a reply to that email, don't hold your breath.
She said she might return the money soon, but don't hold your breath.
They promised to finish the project this month, but I'd don't hold your breath if I were you.
