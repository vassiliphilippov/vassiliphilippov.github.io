1. ⬆️ still increasing ("200 and counting")

ADV
Pronunciation: / ænd ˈkaʊntɪŋ / (AmE), / ənd ˈkaʊntɪŋ / (BrE)
used after a stated number to show that the amount is still increasing and has not stopped yet.
still increasing; not final number

Examples

Her collection of rare books now stands at 200 and counting.
The app has been downloaded over a million times and counting.
The charity has raised $5,000 and counting for the cause.
