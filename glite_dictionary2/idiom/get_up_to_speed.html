get up to speed

1⚡to catch upto get up to speed on news

1. ⚡ to catch up ("to get up to speed on news")

Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ʌp tu spiːd / (AmE), / ɡɛt ʌp tə spiːd / (BrE)
to become fully informed or familiar with the latest information or developments in a particular area.
become fully informed or up-to-date

Examples

Before we start, let's get up to speed with the latest updates.
It didn't take her long to get up to speed after joining the project.
The manager will help new hires get up to speed on company policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – catch up: to reach the same standard or level as others
  • antonym – fall behind: to fail to keep pace with others or with progress
