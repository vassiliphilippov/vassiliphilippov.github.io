1. ⚖️ to punish legally ("to bring to justice a criminal")

Pronunciation: / brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / brɪŋ tʊ ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE); / brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (AmE), / brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌstɪs / (BrE)
to make sure someone who committed a crime is found and punished by the law
hold accountable or punish for wrongdoing / hold legally accountable; prosecute
