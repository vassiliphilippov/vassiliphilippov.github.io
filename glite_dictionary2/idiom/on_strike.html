1. ✊ refusing work ("workers on strike")

Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈstraɪk / (AmE), / ɒn ˈstraɪk / (BrE)
refusing to work as a form of protest, usually by a group of employees
refusing to work in protest

Examples

If the nurses go on strike, hospital services will be disrupted.
Many public sector employees went on strike after talks broke down.
The workers at the factory are on strike demanding safer conditions.
