1. ⏳ to waste time ("to dally too long before leaving")

Pronunciation: / ˈdæli tu lɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈdæli tʃuː lɒŋ / (BrE)
to waste time by delaying or lingering longer than is advisable or necessary.
to waste time by delaying too much

Examples

If you dally too long, you'll miss the bus.
She realized she couldn't dally too long over the decision, or the opportunity would disappear.
We must not dally too long if we want to finish by noon.
