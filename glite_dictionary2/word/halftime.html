1. ⏸️ game intermission ("halftime break")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈhæf.taɪm / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf.taɪm / (BrE)
An intermission between the two halves of a sports game, especially football or basketball, during which players rest and coaches discuss strategy.
break between halves of a game

Examples

The team regrouped during halftime to discuss a new strategy for the second half.
There was an impressive performance at halftime during the championship match.
We grabbed snacks at halftime while the players took a short break.
