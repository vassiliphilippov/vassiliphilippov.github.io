1. ⚖️ court attendance ("court appearing")

Pronunciation: / əˈpɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
The formal attendance of a party at court or at a hearing, especially as required by law.
formal court attendance

Examples

Failure of appearing as required may result in a default judgment.
His appearing before the judge was necessary for the trial to proceed.
The defendant's appearing in court was scheduled for Monday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – appearance: the act of coming into court as a party to a case
