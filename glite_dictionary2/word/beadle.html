1. ⛪ church official ("church beadle")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdəl / (AmE), / ˈbiːdəl / (BrE)

minor parish official with ceremonial duties

Examples

The beadle led the procession through the church at the start of the service.
The old beadle had served the cathedral for over thirty years, always in his traditional uniform.
