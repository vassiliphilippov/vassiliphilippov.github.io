1. ❤️ to revive ("to resuscitate a patient")

Pronunciation: / rɪˈsʌsɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈsʌsɪteɪt / (BrE)
to bring someone back to consciousness or life after they have fainted or appeared to be dead
revive from unconsciousness or apparent death / to revive from unconsciousness

Examples

Doctors did everything possible to resuscitate her when she collapsed at work.
Quick action by the lifeguard helped resuscitate the drowning swimmer.
The paramedics managed to resuscitate the victim after he stopped breathing.
Doctors tried desperately to resuscitate her after she collapsed on the street.
The lifeguard managed to resuscitate the child who had nearly drowned.
The paramedics resuscitated him using CPR and a defibrillator.
