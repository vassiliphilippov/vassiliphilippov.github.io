NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈkatˌbəʊt / (BrE)
A small sailboat with a single mast placed far forward and usually carrying one sail.
type of sailboat with a single forward mast

Examples

A catboat is easy to recognize by its single mast near the bow.
She learned to handle a catboat before trying larger sailboats.
The old fisherman sailed his catboat along the quiet bay.
