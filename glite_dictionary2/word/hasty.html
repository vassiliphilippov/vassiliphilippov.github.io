1. ⚡ too quick ("hasty decision")

Pronunciation: / ˈheɪsti / (AmE), / ˈheɪsti / (BrE)
done or made quickly, often without enough thought or care
done too quickly; lacking consideration / excessively quick; done too fast

Examples

Do not be hasty in judging someone's actions before knowing the full story.
She made a hasty decision without thinking it through.
The meeting ended with a hasty conclusion due to time constraints.
After a hasty meal, they rushed to catch the last train home.
She offered a hasty apology, but it did not seem sincere.
The manager regretted his hasty decision to fire the employee without a full investigation.
