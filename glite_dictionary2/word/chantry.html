1. ⛪ church chapel ("chantry chapel")

Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæntri / (AmE), / ˈtʃæntri / (BrE); / ˈtʃæn.tɹi / (AmE), / ˈtʃæn.tri / (BrE)
a chapel or fund in a church, especially in medieval times, set up for saying prayers or Masses for the soul of the person who paid for it
endowed chapel for Masses for the dead / endowment for Masses (medieval context)
