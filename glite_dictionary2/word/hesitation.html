1. ⏸️ pause from doubt ("moment of hesitation")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛzɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhɛzɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)

pause caused by indecision / pausing due to uncertainty or reluctance / the act of pausing due to uncertainty
