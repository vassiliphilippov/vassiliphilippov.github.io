1. ☁️ cloud formation ("clouding in the sky")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which water vapor condenses into visible clouds in the sky
formation of clouds

Examples

Humidity and temperature both influence the rate of clouding in the atmosphere.
Meteorologists study clouding to predict weather changes more accurately.
The sudden clouding of the sky signaled the arrival of a cold front.
