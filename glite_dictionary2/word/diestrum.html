NOUN
Pronunciation: / daɪˈɛstrəm / (AmE), / daɪˈɛstrəm / (BrE)
A period of reproductive inactivity or quiescence between estrous cycles in animals with several cycles per breeding season.
pause between estrous cycles (animals)

Examples

Veterinarians monitor the diestrum phase to help manage breeding cycles in livestock.
