adjournment

1⏸️pause in sessionadjournment of the meeting

1. ⏸️ pause in session ("adjournment of the meeting")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒɝːnmənt / (AmE), / əˈdʒɜːnmənt / (BrE)
the act of suspending or formally ending a meeting, court session, or other proceedings, usually to be resumed later
formal suspension of a meeting / suspension or postponement to a later time

Examples

The judge announced an adjournment of the hearing until next Friday.
There was an unexpected adjournment due to the technical difficulties in the courtroom.
We requested an adjournment to gather additional evidence for the case.
Due to lack of time, an adjournment was necessary until next week.
The chairman called for the adjournment of the meeting after all agenda items were discussed.
The unexpected power outage led to the immediate adjournment of the court session.

Related Terms

  • antonym – continuation: the act of resuming something after being suspended
  • synonym – postponement: the action of delaying something
  • synonym – recess: a temporary break during a meeting or session
