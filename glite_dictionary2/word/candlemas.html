1. ⛪ Christian festival ("Candlemas church service")

Pronunciation: / ˈkændlˌmæs / (AmE), / ˈkændlˌməs / (BrE)
A Christian feast day celebrated on February 2, commemorating the presentation of Jesus at the temple; traditionally regarded as a quarter day in Scotland.
Christian feast on February 2

Examples

Every year, the church holds a special service on Candlemas to mark the presentation of Christ.
In Scotland, Candlemas historically signified one of the quarter days for settling rents.
Many cultures have traditions involving the blessing of candles on Candlemas.
