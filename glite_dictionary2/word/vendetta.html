1. ⚔️ bitter feud ("family vendetta")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / vɛnˈdɛtə / (AmE), / vɛnˈdɛtə / (BrE)
a prolonged and bitter feud or campaign, especially one involving acts of revenge between families or groups, often resulting in violence or murder
prolonged, violent feud involving revenge

Examples

After the brutal attack, rumors spread that a vendetta had begun between the rival gangs.
He feared that refusing to help would spark a vendetta that could last for generations.
The family was caught in a decades-long vendetta with their neighbors.
