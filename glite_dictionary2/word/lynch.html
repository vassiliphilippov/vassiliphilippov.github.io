1. ⚰️ to kill illegally ("to lynch an accused man")

VERB
Pronunciation: / lɪntʃ / (AmE), / lɪntʃ / (BrE)
(of a mob) to kill (someone), especially by hanging, without a legal trial, usually as a punishment for an alleged offense.
kill without legal trial (by mob)

Examples

A violent crowd tried to lynch the accused man before the police arrived.
In the past, mobs would often lynch people based on mere suspicion.
The authorities warned citizens that anyone who attempted to lynch prisoners would face prosecution.

Related Terms

  • antonym – execute: to carry out a death sentence legally
  • synonym – murder: the unlawful killing of a person, especially with intent
