1. ✝️ to forgive sins ("to shrive a sinner")

Pronunciation: / ʃraɪv / (AmE), / ʃraɪv / (BrE)
(of a priest) to hear the confession of someone and grant them absolution for their sins.
grant absolution after confession

Examples

He confessed his sins and asked to be shriven by the village priest.
In the old days, people would queue at the church to be shriven before Lent.
The priest agreed to shrive the repentant man before the festival began.
