1. ⚖️ weighing tool ("old steelyard scale")

Pronunciation: / ˈstiːliˌɑrd / (AmE), / ˈstiːlˌjɑːd / (BrE)
A portable balance consisting of a pivoted bar with arms of unequal length, used for weighing objects by suspending them at different points.
portable balance with unequal arms

Examples

By adjusting the weight along the arm of the steelyard, he could measure different loads accurately.
She admired the engineering of the old steelyard displayed in the museum.
The ancient merchant used a steelyard to weigh sacks of grain before selling them.
