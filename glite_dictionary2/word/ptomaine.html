1. ☠️ toxic substance ("ptomaine poisoning case")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈtəʊmeɪn / (BrE); / toʊˈmeɪn / (AmE), / təˈmeɪn / (BrE)
a toxic substance produced when animal or plant matter decays, especially one once thought to cause food poisoning (now an old scientific term)
obsolete term for food poisoning toxin / toxic compound formed during decomposition
