1. ⚡ electric unit ("one abampere current")

Pronunciation: / æbˈæmpɪr / (AmE), / abˈæmpɪə / (BrE)
A unit of electric current equal to 10 amperes, used in the centimetre-gram-second (CGS) system of units.
unit of electric current (10 amperes)

Examples

A current of two abamperes is equivalent to twenty amperes in the SI system.
In the CGS system, an abampere is used to measure larger currents than an ampere.
The abampere is now rarely used, having been largely replaced by the ampere.
