a measure of fuel consumption for vehicles, representing the number of liters of fuel used to travel one hundred kilometers
vehicle fuel consumption measure (liters per 100 kilometers)

Examples

Many European countries report vehicle fuel consumption using the L/100 km standard rather than miles per gallon.
The new hybrid car boasts a fuel efficiency of just 4 L/100 km on the highway.
With a consumption rate of 7 L/100 km, this model is considered quite economical for its class.
