NOUN

A person, country, or organization that has signed an official document and is therefore legally bound by its terms.
person or entity that signs a document

Examples

As a signatory to the contract, she is legally obligated to fulfill its conditions.
Each signatory must adhere to the rules set forth in the agreement.
The treaty was ratified by every signatory nation in the alliance.
