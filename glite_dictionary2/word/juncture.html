NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋktʃə / (BrE); / ˈdʒʌŋktʃər / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋktʃə / (BrE)
a particular point in time, especially when things come together or when an important decision must be made
a critical or important point in time / critical point in time or situation / the joining point or manner of connection
