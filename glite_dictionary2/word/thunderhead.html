1. ⛈️ storm cloud ("towering thunderhead cloud")

Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈθʌndəhɛd / (BrE)
A large, rounded, and projecting mass of a cumulus cloud, often with gleaming edges, that typically appears before a thunderstorm.
rounded mass of cloud before a thunderstorm

Examples

A towering thunderhead loomed on the horizon as the storm approached.
Farmers watched the thunderhead forming, knowing that rain was likely to follow.
The setting sun cast a golden glow along the rim of the massive thunderhead.
