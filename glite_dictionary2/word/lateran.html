PROPN
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪtərən / (AmE), / ˈleɪtərən / (BrE)
The site in Rome that includes the Basilica of St. John Lateran and the Lateran Palace, known as the official ecclesiastical seat of the Pope.
site in Rome with church and palace

Examples

Many important councils of the Catholic Church were held at the Lateran over the centuries.
The Lateran is often referred to as the cathedral of Rome and the mother church of all churches.
The Pope delivered his speech at the Lateran before an audience of thousands.
