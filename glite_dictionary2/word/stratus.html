1. ☁️ flat cloud ("stratus cloud layer")

Pronunciation: / ˈstrætəs / (AmE), / ˈstrɑːtəs / (BrE)
A type of low, gray cloud that forms a uniform, layer-like sheet covering the sky, often bringing overcast weather.
a low, uniform gray cloud

Examples

A thick layer of stratus covered the city, blocking out the sun for most of the day.
Pilots often find it difficult to land when stratus obscures the runway.
The forecast predicted that stratus clouds would linger through the morning.
