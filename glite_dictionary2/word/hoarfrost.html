1. ❄️ ice crystals ("hoarfrost on grass")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔr.frɔst / (AmE), / ˈhɔː.frɒst / (BrE)
A deposit of white ice crystals formed by direct condensation of water vapor on exposed surfaces, especially outdoors, when the air is cold and moist.
white ice crystals formed on surfaces

Examples

After a cold night, a layer of hoarfrost coated the trees and fences.
Children marvelled at the delicate hoarfrost patterns on the window pane.
The fields sparkled with hoarfrost under the morning sun.
