1. ⚠️ to endanger ("to jeopardise safety")

VERB
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛpərˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛpədaɪz / (BrE)
to put someone or something in danger of harm, loss, or failure; to endanger
put at risk; endanger

Examples

Careless driving can jeopardise your safety and the safety of others.
The new policy may jeopardise our chances of winning future contracts.
