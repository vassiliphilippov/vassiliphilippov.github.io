(c. 245–207 BC) A Carthaginian general who commanded an army in Spain and later joined his brother Hannibal in Italy; killed by the Romans at the Battle of Metaurus River.
Carthaginian general; brother of Hannibal

Hasdrubal Barca led Carthaginian forces in Spain before marching to join Hannibal in Italy.
