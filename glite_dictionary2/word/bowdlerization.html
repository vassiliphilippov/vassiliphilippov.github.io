1. ✂️ removing offensive parts ("bowdlerization of books")

Pronunciation: / ˌbaʊdlɚəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbaʊdlərəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE); / ˌboʊdlərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbəʊdləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of removing or changing parts of a text that are considered offensive or inappropriate, often making the text less authentic or effective
