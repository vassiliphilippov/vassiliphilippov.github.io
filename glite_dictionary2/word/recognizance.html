1. ⚖️ court promise ("on recognizance release")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑːɡnɪzəns / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒɡnɪzəns / (BrE)
(Law) An obligation entered into before a court to perform a specific act, with the forfeiture of a sum of money if the act is not performed.
legal obligation to perform an act, or forfeit money

Examples

Failure to comply with the terms of the recognizance can result in forfeiture of the deposited sum.
She signed a recognizance promising to appear in court on the agreed date.
The defendant was released on recognizance pending his trial next month.
