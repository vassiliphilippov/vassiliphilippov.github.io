NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˌvælɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvælɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE); / ˌvælɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvalɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)

process of confirming validity or accuracy / the act of confirming accuracy or truth

Examples

The software underwent a thorough validation process before its release to ensure reliability.
