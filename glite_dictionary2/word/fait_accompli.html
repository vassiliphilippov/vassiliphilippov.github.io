1. ✅ done deal ("a fait accompli situation")

Pronunciation: / feɪt æˈkɑmˌpli / (AmE), / feɪt akɒmˈpli / (BrE)
A thing that has already been done or decided and cannot be changed; an accomplished fact.
an accomplished, irreversible fact

Examples

By the time the opposition organized, the law had already become a fait accompli.
He accepted the job offer as a fait accompli, knowing there was little point in negotiation.
The merger was presented to employees as a fait accompli, leaving no room for discussion.
