1. ☮️ arms talks ("SALT negotiations begin")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / sɔlt / (AmE), / sɔːlt / (BrE)
(in full: Strategic Arms Limitation Talks) a series of negotiations between the United States and the Soviet Union, beginning in 1969, aimed at limiting each country’s stockpile of nuclear weapons.
US–Soviet nuclear arms limitation talks, 1969 onwards

Examples

Diplomats met in Helsinki to begin the SALT negotiations, aiming to limit each nation's nuclear arsenal.
Many historians cite SALT as a pivotal step toward de-escalating nuclear tensions.
The signing of the SALT treaties marked a milestone in arms control during the Cold War.
