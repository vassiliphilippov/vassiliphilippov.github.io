1. ❓ beyond recognition ("unrecognizably changed")

ADV
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈrɛk.əɡ.naɪ.zəb.li / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈrɛk.əɡ.nʌɪ.zəb.li / (BrE)
In a way that is impossible to recognize; to such a degree that something cannot be identified as it was before.

Examples

After the fire, the building was unrecognizably damaged and had to be demolished.
He returned from his trip unrecognizably changed, both in appearance and personality.
The manuscript was unrecognizably altered by careless editing.
