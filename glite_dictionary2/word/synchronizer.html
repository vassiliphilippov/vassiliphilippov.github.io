1. ⏱️ timing device ("synchronizer for engines")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋkrəˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋkrənaɪzə / (BrE)
an instrument used to indicate or ensure that two or more periodic motions, such as propellers or engines, are operating in synchrony, especially in aircraft with multiple engines
instrument for synchronizing periodic motions

Examples

Modern aircraft are equipped with an automatic synchronizer for propeller control.
The pilot checked the synchronizer to ensure both engines were running in harmony.
Using the synchronizer, she adjusted the propellers to eliminate any vibration.
