Pronunciation: / klɛntʃt / (AmE), / klɛntʃt / (BrE)
(of parts of the body, especially the hands or teeth) closed or squeezed together tightly, typically as an expression of strong emotion such as anger or determination.
tightly closed or squeezed (esp. hands, teeth)

Examples

His jaw remained clenched as he struggled to keep his composure.
She raised her clenched fist in protest during the rally.
With clenched teeth, she forced herself to endure the pain.
