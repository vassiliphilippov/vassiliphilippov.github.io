1. ☠️ poisonous plant ("Atropa belladonna poisonous berries")

Pronunciation: / əˈtroʊpə ˌbɛləˈdoʊnə / (AmE), / əˈtrəʊpə ˌbɛləˈdɒnə / (BrE)
A perennial Eurasian herb, also known as deadly nightshade, with reddish bell-shaped flowers and shiny black berries; its roots and leaves are a source of the drug atropine.
poisonous Eurasian herb, deadly nightshade

Examples

Gardeners are warned never to handle Atropa belladonna without gloves due to its toxicity.
Some medicinal drugs derive their active ingredient from Atropa belladonna.
The berries of Atropa belladonna are highly poisonous to humans and many animals.
