1. ⚓ ship officer ("the bosun gives orders")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊsən / (AmE), / ˈbəʊsən / (BrE)
a petty officer on a ship, especially a merchant ship, responsible for supervising the work of the deck crew
petty officer in charge of deck crew

Examples

During the storm, the bosun made sure all equipment was safely secured.
She was promoted to bosun after years of hard work on the vessel.
The bosun gave orders to the deckhands as the ship prepared to leave port.

  • synonym – boatswain: an officer in charge of the equipment and crew on a ship's deck
