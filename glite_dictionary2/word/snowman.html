1. ⛄ snow figure ("build a snowman")

Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌmæn / (BrE)
A figure resembling a human, made by shaping packed snow, often as a child's winter pastime.
figure made of packed snow

Examples

After the storm, the park was filled with snowmen of all shapes and sizes.
She gave the snowman a carrot nose and coal eyes.
The children spent the afternoon building a snowman in the front yard.
