NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊθˌaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊθ.aɪt / (BrE)
A red, yellow, or brown mineral consisting of iron(III) oxide-hydroxide, commonly found as a component of rust.
iron oxide-hydroxide mineral

The mineral gothite is often found in the oxidized zones of iron ore deposits.
