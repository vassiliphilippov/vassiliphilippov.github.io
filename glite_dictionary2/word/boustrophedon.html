1. ↔️ alternating lines ("boustrophedon writing style")

Pronunciation: / buːˌstrɑːfəˈdɑːn / (AmE), / buːˌstrɒfɪˈdiːɒn / (BrE); / buːˌstrɑːfrəˈdɑːn / (AmE), / buːˌstrɒfrɪˈdɒn / (BrE)
