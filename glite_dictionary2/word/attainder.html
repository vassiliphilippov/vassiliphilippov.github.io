1. ⚖️ loss of rights ("attainder of rights")

Pronunciation: / əˈteɪndər / (AmE), / əˈteɪndə / (BrE)
the loss of civil rights following a sentence of death or outlawry, especially by act of legislation rather than by judicial process; now chiefly historical
loss of civil rights by legal sentence

Examples

Historically, attainder was used as a tool against political opponents.
