1. ✋ palm turning up ("supination of the hand")

Pronunciation: / suːpɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsuːpɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the action of rotating the hand or forearm so that the palm faces upward or forward
rotation turning palm upward

Examples

Excessive supination during walking can lead to ankle problems.
Physical therapists may evaluate supination when diagnosing wrist or forearm injuries.
The exercise involved alternating supination and pronation of the forearm.

  • antonym – pronation: rotation of the hand or forearm so the palm faces downward or backward
