1. ✅ to allow officially ("to authorise a payment")

Pronunciation: / ˈɔːθəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈɔːθəraɪz / (BrE)
to give official permission for something to happen or to give someone official power to do something
give official permission or power

Examples

Guests must be authorised to enter this area of the building.
She is authorised to sign documents on behalf of the company.
The bank did not authorise the transaction due to suspicious activity.
Her supervisor has authorised her to make purchases up to $5,000.
Only a doctor can authorise this medication for patients.
The manager must authorise all expense claims before payment is made.

Related Terms

  • synonym – authorize: to give official permission or power (US spelling)
  • synonym – empower: give someone the authority or power to do something
  • antonym – forbid: to refuse to allow something
