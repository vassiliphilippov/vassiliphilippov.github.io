1. ✅ validity ("legitimacy of an argument")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / lɪˈdʒɪtɪməsi / (AmE), / lɪˈdʒɪtɪməsi / (BrE)
The quality of being accepted as valid, reasonable, or having undisputed credibility.
undisputed credibility or validity

Examples

His argument lacked legitimacy in the eyes of his peers.
Questions about the legitimacy of the election results led to widespread protests.
The new government's legitimacy was quickly recognized by other countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym – authenticity: the quality of being authentic or genuine
  • antonym – illegitimacy: the state or quality of not being legitimate or lawful
