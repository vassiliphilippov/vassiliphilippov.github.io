(1478–1534) Italian pope who served from 1523 to 1534, noted for his conflict with Henry VIII over the English king's divorce and the resulting split with the Catholic Church.

Examples

Pope Clement VII refused to annul Henry VIII’s marriage to Catherine of Aragon, leading to England's break with Rome.
During his papacy, Pope Clement VII faced the sack of Rome by imperial troops in 1527.
Historians often discuss how Pope Clement VII's decisions influenced the course of European history.
