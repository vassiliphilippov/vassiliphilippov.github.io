1. ⏰ clock control ("clock escapement mechanism")

Pronunciation: / ɪˈskeɪpmənt / (AmE), / ɪˈskeɪpmənt / (BrE)
A mechanism in a clock or watch that controls and regulates the release of power from the spring or weight, allowing the gear train to advance at regular intervals.
mechanism regulating clock movement

Examples

An intricate escapement allows each gear of the antique timepiece to move at a precise rate.
The watchmaker adjusted the escapement to ensure the clock kept accurate time.
Without a properly functioning escapement, mechanical clocks cannot maintain a steady rhythm.
