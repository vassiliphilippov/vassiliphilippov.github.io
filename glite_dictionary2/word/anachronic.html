1. ⏳ out of date ("anachronic style")

Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈkrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌanəˈkrɒnɪk / (BrE)
Belonging to a time other than the present; chronologically out of place.
chronologically misplaced

Examples

Her writing style, though charming, is somewhat anachronic for contemporary audiences.
It would be anachronic to refer to DVDs as the latest technology.
The use of a rotary telephone in a modern office setting feels completely anachronic.
