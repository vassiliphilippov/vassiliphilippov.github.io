1. ⚔️ Hindu war god ("Kartikeya god of war")

Pronunciation: / kɑːrˈtɪkɪjə / (AmE), / kɑːˈtɪkɪjeɪ / (BrE)
In Hinduism, the god of war and bravery, son of Shiva and Parvati, and commander-in-chief of the gods' army.
Hindu god of war and bravery

Examples

During the festival of Skanda Shashti, devotees celebrate the valor of Kartikeya with grand processions and rituals.
Legend says that Kartikeya was born to defeat the demon Tarakasura and restore peace among the gods.
Many temples in South India are dedicated to Kartikeya, revered as a powerful warrior deity.
