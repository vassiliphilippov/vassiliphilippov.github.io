1. ✊ demonstrator ("protester holding sign")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / proʊˈtɛstər / (AmE), / prəˈtɛstə / (BrE)
a person who publicly shows strong objection or disapproval, usually as part of a group
person taking part in a public demonstration / person who objects publicly to policies

Examples

A lone protester stood in front of the factory holding a sign.
The police arrested several protesters during the march.
Thousands of protesters gathered outside the parliament building demanding political reform.
As a passionate protester, she regularly attends rallies for environmental justice.
Hundreds of protesters gathered outside the government building demanding change.
The police tried to disperse the protesters after the march became unruly.
