1. ⚖️ make legal ("to decriminalize drug use")

VERB
Pronunciation: / diˈkrɪmənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / diˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz / (BrE)
To stop treating an action as illegal or criminal by changing the law, especially to remove criminal penalties from certain activities.
make something no longer illegal

Examples

Many countries have decided to decriminalize the possession of small amounts of marijuana.
Some advocates argue it is time to decriminalize certain traffic offenses to reduce overcrowding in prisons.
The government plans to decriminalize public drunkenness, treating it as a health issue instead.

Related Terms

  • synonym – legalize: to make something lawful by official means
  • antonym – prohibit: to formally forbid something by law or rule
