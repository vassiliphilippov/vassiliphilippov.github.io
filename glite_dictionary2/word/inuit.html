1. ❄️ Arctic people ("Inuit people")

Pronunciation: / ˈɪnuˌɪt, ˈɪnjuˌɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnjuːɪt / (BrE)
A member of the indigenous people inhabiting the Arctic regions of northern Canada, Greenland, Alaska, and eastern Siberia, traditionally known for their unique culture and adaptation to cold environments.
indigenous Arctic people

Examples

Artifacts made by ancient Inuit peoples are displayed in the museum.
Many Inuit communities continue to maintain their traditional way of life despite modern influences.
The Inuit are renowned for their exceptional skills in hunting and surviving in extreme climates.
