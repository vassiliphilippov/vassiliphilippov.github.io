1. ⚡ electric brain treatment ("electroshock therapy session")

Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktroʊˌʃɑk / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrəʊʃɒk / (BrE)
the administration of a strong electric current to the brain to induce convulsions, especially as a treatment for certain psychiatric conditions
strong electric current used in psychiatric treatment

Examples

Researchers are studying the long-term effects of electroshock treatments.
The patient received electroshock as a last resort for his severe depression.
There has been much debate over the use of electroshock in modern psychiatry.
