1. ⏱️ tiny time unit ("one nanosecond interval")

Pronunciation: / ˈnænəˌsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈnanəʊˌsɛkənd / (BrE)
A unit of time equal to one billionth (10⁻⁹) of a second.
one billionth of a second

Examples

Light travels about 30 centimeters in a single nanosecond.
Response times are often measured in nanoseconds in high-speed electronics.
The computer processor completes each instruction in just a few nanoseconds.
