1. ⚖️ to outweigh ("to outbalance the risks")

VERB
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈbæləns / (AmE), / aʊtˈbæləns / (BrE)
to be more important, significant, or influential than something else; to outweigh or surpass in weight or effect.
to outweigh or surpass in importance

Examples

Concerns about security often outbalance the desire for more convenience.
His contributions to the project outbalanced any initial doubts about his experience.
The potential risks outbalance the expected benefits in this case.
