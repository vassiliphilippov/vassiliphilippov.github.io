hippie

1✌️peace-loving persona hippie lifestyle

1. ✌️ peace-loving person ("a hippie lifestyle")

Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpi / (AmE), / ˈhɪpi / (BrE)
a person, especially in the 1960s and 1970s, who rejected conventional values and customs, often promoting peace, love, and simple living
member of 1960s counterculture movement / person rejecting conventional values (esp. 1960s-70s)

Examples

My uncle was a hippie in the 1970s, traveling across the country in a painted van.
She wore tie-dye shirts and flowers in her hair, embracing the hippie lifestyle.
The hippie movement emphasized nonviolence and communal living.
He wore a tie-dye shirt and long hair, looking like one of the hippies from the 1960s.
Many hippies gathered at the 1969 Woodstock Festival to celebrate peace and music.
The influence of hippies can still be seen in today's alternative communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beatnik: member of a 1950s countercultural movement preceding the hippies
  • synonym – bohemian: a person leading an unconventional lifestyle, often associated with artistic or intellectual pursuits
  • antonym – conformist: a person who readily adapts to established conventions
  • antonym – square: a conventional, old-fashioned person, especially opposed to counterculture
