1. ⚡ half-god ("mythical demigod")

Pronunciation: / ˈdɛmiˌɡɑd / (AmE), / ˈdɛmiɡɒd / (BrE)
a being who is part human and part god, especially in mythology; also, a person with powers or abilities far greater than ordinary people (sometimes used figuratively for someone with exceptional talent or influence)
part-human, part-god being / person with extraordinary powers or abilities

Hercules, known as a demigod, possessed strength beyond that of any mortal man.

Some cultures believed their kings to be demigods descended from the heavens.
