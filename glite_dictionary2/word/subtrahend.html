1. ➖ number subtracted ("subtrahend in subtraction")

Pronunciation: / ˈsʌb.trəˌhɛnd / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.trəhɛnd / (BrE)
A number that is to be subtracted from another number (the minuend) in a subtraction operation.
number to be subtracted in subtraction

Examples

In the equation 7 - 3 = 4, the number 3 is the subtrahend.
Students were asked to identify the subtrahend in each subtraction problem.
The result of subtracting the subtrahend from the minuend is called the difference.
