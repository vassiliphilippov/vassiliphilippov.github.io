1. ❓ not fixed ("indefinite period")

Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛfɪnət / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛfɪnət / (BrE)
not clearly defined, fixed, or limited; vague or without exact limits
not clearly defined or decided / not clearly defined or stated
