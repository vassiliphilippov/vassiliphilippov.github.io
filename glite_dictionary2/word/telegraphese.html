1. ✉️ short, clipped style ("telegraphese writing style")

Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləɡrəˈfiz / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪɡrəˈfiːz / (BrE)
a style of writing or language marked by extreme brevity and omission of unnecessary words, resembling the style used in telegrams
terse style like telegrams

Examples

Early news bulletins sometimes adopted telegraphese for brevity and clarity.
His report was written in telegraphese, omitting articles and unnecessary words.
Instructions sent by radio were often composed in telegraphese to save time and space.
