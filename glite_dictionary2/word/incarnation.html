1. ✝️ God as Jesus ("the Incarnation of Jesus")

Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkɑrˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnkɑːˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
(in Christianity) The belief that God became flesh by being born as Jesus Christ; the union of divine and human nature in Jesus.
Christian doctrine of God becoming human in Jesus

Examples

Artists have long tried to depict the mystery of the Incarnation.
Christmas celebrates the Incarnation—the belief that God became human in Jesus.
The doctrine of the Incarnation is central to Christian theology.
