1. ⚔️ French heroine ("Saint Joan of Arc")

Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈdʒoʊn / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒəʊn / (BrE)
(c. 1412–1431) A French heroine and military leader, known for leading French resistance against the English and for having Charles VII crowned, before being executed for heresy. Also known as Joan of Arc.
