1. ⚖️ case referral ("remitment of a case")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɪtmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪtmənt / (BrE)
(law) the act of remitting, especially the referral of a law case to another court for further action or review.
referral of a legal case to another court

Examples

After the remitment, the new judge reviewed all the evidence from the beginning.
Legal procedures require proper documentation for every remitment between courts.
The court ordered the remitment of the case to a lower court for further proceedings.
