1. ⏱️ tempo device ("metronome ticking steadily")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrəˌnoʊm / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrənəʊm / (BrE)
a device that produces regular ticks or clicks at adjustable intervals to indicate the tempo for musicians
device marking musical tempo

Examples

Modern metronomes can produce both visual and audio cues for musicians.
Please set the metronome to 120 beats per minute for this exercise.
The pianist used a metronome to practice keeping a steady rhythm.
