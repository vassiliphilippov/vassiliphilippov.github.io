1. ⛏️ iron ore ("limonite ore sample")

Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪ.məˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪm.əˌnaɪt / (BrE)
A widely occurring brownish iron oxide ore, consisting mainly of a mixture of hydrated ferric oxides such as goethite, hematite, and lepidocrocite.
brownish iron ore mineral

Examples

Early iron tools were often made from limonite extracted from bogs.
Geologists collected samples of limonite from the abandoned mine for analysis.
The brownish color of the soil was due to the presence of limonite.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hematite: an iron ore mineral; a component of limonite
