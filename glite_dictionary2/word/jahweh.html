A name for God in the Old Testament, transliterated from the Hebrew YHVH.
a Hebrew name for God (YHVH)

Examples

Many biblical scholars use Jahweh to refer to the God worshipped in ancient Israel.
Some English translations of the Bible render YHVH as Jahweh rather than Jehovah.
The pronunciation of the divine name Jahweh remains a subject of debate among theologians.
