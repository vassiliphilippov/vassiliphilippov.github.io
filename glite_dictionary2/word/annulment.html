1. ❌ official cancellation ("annulment of marriage")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / əˈnʌlmənt / (AmE), / əˈnʌlmənt / (BrE)
the official act of cancelling something, especially a marriage or contract, so that it is treated as if it never existed
legal cancellation or invalidation / official cancellation or invalidation / the act or state of being annulled
