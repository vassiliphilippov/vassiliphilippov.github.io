1. ❌ wrong word use ("catachresis in language")

Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈkriːsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈkriːsɪs / (BrE)
The incorrect or deliberately paradoxical use of a word or phrase, especially in error or as a mixed metaphor.
misuse of a word in language or literature

Examples

He committed a catachresis when he said ‘literally’ instead of ‘figuratively’.
Her speech was full of catachresis, mixing metaphors in unexpected ways.
The poet’s use of catachresis gave the phrase an unusual and striking quality.
