1. ⏸️ inactive ("abeyant status")

Pronunciation: / əˈbeɪənt / (AmE), / əˈbeɪənt / (BrE)
(of a condition, state, or process) currently inactive but capable of becoming active; temporarily suspended.
temporarily inactive; suspended

Examples

Old traditions often lie abeyant for years before being revived.
The project remains abeyant until we receive further funding.
The volcano is currently abeyant, though experts continue to monitor it closely.
