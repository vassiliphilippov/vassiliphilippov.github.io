1. ⛷️ snow bike ("ride a skibob downhill")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈskiˌbɑb / (AmE), / ˈskiːˌbɒb / (BrE)
a type of vehicle resembling a bicycle but equipped with skis instead of wheels, ridden down snowy slopes; the rider usually wears short skis for balance
a bicycle-like vehicle with skis for snow

Examples

Riding a skibob down the alpine slopes was an exhilarating experience for the beginners.
She bought a new skibob for her winter vacation in the Swiss Alps.
The instructor demonstrated how to balance properly while moving on a skibob.
