1. ⚖️ official investigation ("inquest into death")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌkwɛst / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkwɛst / (BrE)
an official investigation, especially into the cause of a person's death, usually carried out by a coroner
official inquiry into a death

Examples

An inquest revealed the man had died of natural causes.
The coroner opened an inquest into the circumstances of the sudden death.
The family's lawyer attended the inquest to represent their interests.
