1. ✈️ wing shape ("airfoil shape design")

Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈeə.fɔɪl / (BrE)
A structure with a curved surface, such as a wing or blade, designed to generate lift or control movement in the air when moving relative to it.
a surface that generates lift in air (e.g. wing, blade)

Examples

Engineers spent months optimizing the airfoil to improve the aircraft's efficiency.
Helicopter rotor blades are a type of airfoil designed for lift and control.
The shape of the airfoil allows the airplane to rise smoothly into the sky.
