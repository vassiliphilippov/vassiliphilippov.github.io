1. ✍️ using few words ("conciseness in writing")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / kənˈsaɪsnəs / (AmE), / kənˈsaɪsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of expressing much in few words; terseness and economy in speech or writing
the quality of being concise

Examples

Good academic writing values conciseness over unnecessary elaboration.
Her conciseness in interviews is appreciated by journalists.
The report's conciseness made it easy to understand the key points.

  • synonym – brevity: shortness or briefness in speech or writing
