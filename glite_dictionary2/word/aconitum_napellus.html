1. ☠️ poisonous flower ("Aconitum napellus plant")

Pronunciation: / əˌkoʊnɪtəm nəˈpɛləs / (AmE), / əˌkəʊnɪtəm nəˈpɛləs / (BrE)
A poisonous plant of northern Europe with hooded blue-purple flowers; its dried leaves and roots yield aconite, a potent toxin.
a poisonous northern European plant

Examples

Gardeners admire the striking flowers of Aconitum napellus, but handle the plant with care.
Some traditional remedies once used Aconitum napellus, despite its dangerous properties.
The roots of Aconitum napellus are highly toxic if ingested.
