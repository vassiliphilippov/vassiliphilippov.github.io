(of a substance or element) consisting of single atoms rather than molecules or groups of atoms.

Examples

A monoatomic molecule consists of only one atom, as opposed to diatomic or polyatomic molecules.
Helium is a monoatomic gas under standard conditions.
Some noble gases in their natural state exist as monoatomic elements.
