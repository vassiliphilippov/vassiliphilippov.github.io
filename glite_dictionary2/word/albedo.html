1. ☀️ reflectivity ("albedo of snow")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ælˈbeɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ælˈbiːdəʊ / (BrE)
the proportion of the incident light or radiation that is reflected by a surface, typically that of a planet or moon
proportion of reflected light

Examples

Fresh snow has a high albedo, reflecting most of the sunlight that hits it.
The Earth's albedo affects how much of the sun's energy is absorbed by the planet.
The scientist measured the albedo of the asteroid to help determine its surface composition.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reflectance: the proportion of light reflected by a surface
