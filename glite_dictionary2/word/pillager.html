1. ⚔️ looter ("pillager attacks village")

Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlədʒər / (AmE), / ˈpɪlɪdʒə / (BrE)

History books describe the ruthless pillager who burned the village to the ground.
The ancient city fell to the pillager, its treasures scattered and lost.
