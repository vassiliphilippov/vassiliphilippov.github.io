1. ⛪ church priest ("parish vicar")

Pronunciation: / ˈvɪkər / (AmE), / ˈvɪkə / (BrE)
a Christian priest or clergyman in charge of a church or acting as a representative of a higher authority
Episcopal Church clergyman in charge of a chapel / priest acting for higher-ranking clergyman / priest in charge of a parish (Church of England)

  • synonym – deputy: a person who acts as a substitute for someone else
