1. ✈️ forced removal ("deportation order")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / diˌpɔrˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːpɔːˈteɪʃən / (BrE); / diˌpɔrˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌpɔːˈteɪʃən / (BrE)

expulsion from a country / expulsion of someone from a country
