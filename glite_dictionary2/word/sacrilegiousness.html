1. ⛪ disrespect for sacred ("open sacrilegiousness in church")

Pronunciation: / ˌsækrɪˈlɪdʒəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌsækrɪˈlɪdʒəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of showing disrespect or irreverence for something regarded as sacred.
quality of being sacrilegious

Examples

She was criticized for her sacrilegiousness during the sacred ceremony.
The sacrilegiousness of the act outraged the entire religious community.
There was a feeling of sacrilegiousness in painting graffiti on the church walls.
