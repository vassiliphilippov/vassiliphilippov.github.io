1. ⏰ hourly pay ("timework payment system")

Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌwɜːk / (BrE)
work for which payment is made according to the amount of time spent, rather than by the piece or completed task.
work paid by the unit of time

Examples

Factory workers are often paid for their timework rather than by how much they produce.
He preferred timework because it gave him a predictable income regardless of output.
The company offers both timework and piecework contracts, depending on the job role.
