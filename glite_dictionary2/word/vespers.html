1. ⛪ evening prayer ("evening vespers service")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛspərz / (AmE), / ˈvɛspəz / (BrE)
the evening prayer service in many Christian traditions, originally the sixth of the seven canonical hours of the divine office, often held publicly on Sundays
evening church prayer service

Examples

The choir sang beautifully during vespers at the old cathedral.
The peaceful atmosphere of vespers helped him reflect and pray.
We attended vespers on Sunday evening with the congregation.
