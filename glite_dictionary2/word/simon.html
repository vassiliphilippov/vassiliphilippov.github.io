PROPN

One of the twelve Apostles of Jesus Christ in the first century.
one of the twelve Apostles of Jesus

Examples

Simon was called to be one of the original twelve Apostles by Jesus himself.
Early Christian texts often refer to Simon as Simon Peter.
Stained glass windows in many churches depict Simon alongside the other Apostles.
