1. ⬆️ pubic mound ("the mons pubis")

Pronunciation: / mɑnz / (AmE), / mɒnz / (BrE)
a mound of fatty tissue covering the pubic area, especially in women; often refers to the mons pubis.
mound of tissue above female pubic bone

Examples

The mons provides protection for the underlying pubic bone.
