1. ⚾ turn to hit ("a at-bat in baseball")

Pronunciation: / ˈæt ˌbæt / (AmE), / ˈæt ˌbat / (BrE)
(in baseball) a player's turn to try to hit the ball and reach base during an inning.
baseball turn to hit

Examples

After three consecutive at-bats without a hit, she finally singled to right field.
He was injured during his last at-bat in the ninth inning.
The rookie hit a home run in his first at-bat of the season.
