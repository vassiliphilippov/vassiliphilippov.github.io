1. … missing words ("ellipsis in a sentence")

Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪpsɪs / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪpsɪs / (BrE)
the omission of one or more words from a sentence, especially when the meaning can still be understood
omission of words in a sentence

Examples

His answer contained an ellipsis that left the true meaning open to interpretation.
In writing, an ellipsis often indicates that part of a quotation has been left out.
The author used ellipsis to create suspense by omitting certain details.
