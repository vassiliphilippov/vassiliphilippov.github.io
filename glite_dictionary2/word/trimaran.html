1. ⛵ three-hull boat ("sail a trimaran fast")

Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪm.əˌræn / (AmE), / ˈtrɪm.əræn / (BrE)
A fast sailboat with three parallel hulls joined by a frame, designed for stability and speed.
sailboat with three hulls

Examples

He sailed his trimaran across the Atlantic in record time.
Racing enthusiasts often prefer a trimaran for its speed and agility.
The trimaran design provides extra stability compared to a monohull.
