NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪl ˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈkwɪl ˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
(historical, sometimes humorous) A person whose occupation is writing, especially in a routine or clerical way; a writer or scribe. Rarely, a tool used for writing, such as a pen.
person who writes; sometimes, a writing tool

Examples

In Victorian novels, a quill-driver often appears as a minor clerical character.
She prided herself on not being a mere quill-driver, but a true author.
The company hired another quill-driver to handle the mountain of paperwork.
