1. ❤️ heart vein ("vena pericardiaca damage")

Pronunciation: / ˈveɪnə ˌpɛrɪˌkɑrdiˈækə / (AmE), / ˈviːnə ˌpɛrɪˌkɑːdiˈækə / (BrE)
Any of several small veins that drain blood from the pericardium (the membrane surrounding the heart) into the adjacent veins.
small veins from the heart's pericardium

Examples

Damage to the vena pericardiaca can lead to complications during cardiac procedures.
During surgery, the vena pericardiaca must be carefully preserved to prevent excessive bleeding.
The doctor explained that the vena pericardiaca drains blood from the membrane surrounding the heart.
