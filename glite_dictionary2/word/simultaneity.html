simultaneity

1⏱️same timesimultaneity of events

1. ⏱️ same time ("simultaneity of events")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪməlˌteɪˈniəti / (AmE), / ˌsɪm(ə)lˌteɪnɪˈæɪti / (BrE)
The state or quality of two or more events or actions occurring at the same time.
occurrence at the same time

Examples

In Einstein's theory of relativity, simultaneity is not absolute but depends on the observer's frame of reference.
The simultaneity of the two concerts made it impossible to attend both.
The project requires the simultaneity of several processes to be completed successfully.

Related Terms

  • antonym – asynchrony: lack of occurrence at the same time
  • synonym – synchrony: occurrence of events at the same time
