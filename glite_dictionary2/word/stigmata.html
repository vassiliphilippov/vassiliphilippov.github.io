1. ✝️ Christ-like wounds ("miraculous stigmata wounds")

Pronunciation: / stɪgˈmɑtə / (AmE), / stɪgˈmɑːtə / (BrE)
(in Christian tradition) marks or wounds resembling those received by Christ during crucifixion, sometimes believed to appear miraculously on the bodies of certain people.
marks resembling Christ's crucifixion wounds

Examples

Believers came from far and wide to witness the stigmata on the monk's body.
Some saints were said to have stigmata appear spontaneously on their hands and feet.
The occurrence of stigmata is often regarded as a divine sign in Christian faith.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stigmatization: the act or process of marking with a stigma
