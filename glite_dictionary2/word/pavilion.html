NOUN
Pronunciation: / pəˈvɪliən / (AmE), / pəˈvɪliən / (BrE)
A large and often richly decorated tent, especially used for outdoor events or by important people.
large, sumptuous tent

Examples

A large pavilion was erected in the field for the guests to gather.
The royal couple dined under a magnificent pavilion draped in silks.
Traders set up their wares inside a colorful pavilion during the festival.
