Having equal balance or lack of dominance between sides, especially in being neither right-handed nor left-handed.
lacking lateral dominance (neither right- nor left-handed)

Examples

Being equipoised gives athletes an advantage in sports requiring coordination on both sides.
The violinist's equipoised technique allowed him to bow with either hand effortlessly.
