1. ✋ hand preference ("right handedness")

Pronunciation: / ˈhændɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈhændɪdnəs / (BrE)
the tendency or preference to use one hand more skillfully or frequently than the other, especially in tasks requiring fine motor skills
preference for use of one hand over the other

Examples

Her strong right handedness became obvious when she started playing tennis.
Many artists believe that their handedness influences their approach to drawing and painting.
Some studies have investigated the genetic basis of handedness in humans.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ambidexterity: ability to use both hands equally well
