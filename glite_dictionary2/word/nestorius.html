Pronunciation: / nɛˈstɔriəs / (AmE), / nɛˈstɔːrɪəs / (BrE)
(c. 386–451) Syrian Christian bishop and Patriarch of Constantinople whose teachings led to the theological doctrine known as Nestorianism.
Syrian Christian patriarch, namesake of Nestorianism

Examples

Many councils were convened to debate the doctrines put forth by Nestorius.
The followers of Nestorius were later referred to as Nestorians.
The teachings of Nestorius sparked significant controversy within early Christianity.
