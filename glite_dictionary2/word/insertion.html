1. ➕ adding in ("insertion of a comment")

Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɜrʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːʃn / (BrE); / ɪnˈsɝː.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜː.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of putting something into something else, such as adding a comment to text or placing an object inside another
act of adding something written or spoken / the act of putting one thing into another

Examples

Her speech contained a few well-timed insertions of humor to keep the audience engaged.
The editor made several insertions in the manuscript to clarify the meaning.
The margins of the ancient text were filled with later insertions by different hands.
During surgery, the insertion of a catheter must be done carefully to avoid infection.
Her essay was improved by the insertion of a relevant quotation.
The insertion of a key into the lock triggered the alarm system.
