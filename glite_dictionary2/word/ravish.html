1. ⚠️ to rape ("to ravish a woman")

Pronunciation: / ˈræv.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrav.ɪʃ / (BrE)
(archaic or literary) To force someone to have sex against their will; to rape.
force sex upon; rape (archaic)

Examples

In the ancient legend, the princess was ravished by an invading warlord.
She wrote in her diary about her terror at the thought she might be ravished during the siege.
The story claimed the villagers feared their daughters would be ravished by outlaws lurking in the woods.
