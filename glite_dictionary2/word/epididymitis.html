1. ⚕️ testicle inflammation ("acute epididymitis pain")

Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˌdɪdəˈmaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˌdɪdəˈmaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Painful inflammation of the epididymis, a tube at the back of the testicle that stores and carries sperm.
inflammation of the epididymis

Examples

The doctor diagnosed him with epididymitis after he complained of severe testicular pain.
Treatment for epididymitis usually includes antibiotics and rest.
Untreated epididymitis can sometimes lead to complications such as infertility.
