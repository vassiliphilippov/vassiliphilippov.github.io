1. ❤️ heart recording ("cardiography test results")

Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrdiˈɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiˈɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
The process of recording the electrical or mechanical activity of the heart using a cardiograph, usually to diagnose heart conditions.
recording heart activity medically

Examples

Cardiography is commonly used to assess the heart's rhythm and electrical activity in clinical settings.
Recent advances in cardiography have made it easier to detect subtle heart abnormalities early.
The doctor recommended a cardiography test after the patient complained of chest pain.
