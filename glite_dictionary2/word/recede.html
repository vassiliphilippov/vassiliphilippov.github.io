1. ↩️ to move back ("to recede into distance")

Pronunciation: / rɪˈsid / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːd / (BrE)
to move back or away, or to become less strong, noticeable, or present (physically or in memory or feeling)
become fainter or more distant / move back or withdraw / move back; retreat
