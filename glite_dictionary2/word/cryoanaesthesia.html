1. ❄️ numbness from cold ("cryoanaesthesia with cold spray")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪoʊˌænəsˈθiʒə / (AmE), / ˌkraɪəʊˌanɪsˈθiːzɪə / (BrE)
Loss of sensation or insensibility caused by the application of cold.
loss of sensation due to cold

Examples

Modern clinics sometimes employ cryoanaesthesia instead of traditional local anaesthetics.
Prolonged exposure to extreme cold can result in cryoanaesthesia of the affected limbs.
The surgeon used cryoanaesthesia to numb the area before the minor procedure.
