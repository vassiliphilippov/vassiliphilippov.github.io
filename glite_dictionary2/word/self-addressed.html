1. ✉️ sender's address ("self-addressed envelope")

ADJ
Pronunciation: / sɛlf əˈdrɛst / (AmE), / sɛlf əˈdrɛst / (BrE)
(of an envelope or package) having the name and address of the sender written on it, usually so it can be returned or replied to.
addressed to oneself

Examples

Contest entries must be accompanied by a self-addressed envelope to receive a response.
He sent out invitations with self-addressed reply cards for RSVPs.
Please include a stamped self-addressed envelope with your application.
