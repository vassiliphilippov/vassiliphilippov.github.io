NOUN
Pronunciation: / reɪz ˈʤɛstaɪ / (AmE), / reɪz ˈʤɛstaɪ / (BrE); / reɪs ˈdʒɛsti, reɪz ˈdʒɛstaɪ / (AmE), / reɪs ˈdʒɛstiː, rɛz ˈdʒɛstaɪ / (BrE)
(in law) facts, circumstances, or statements so closely connected to an event that they are considered part of the event and can be used as evidence in court
legal doctrine; facts or remarks part of an event / legal facts surrounding an act
