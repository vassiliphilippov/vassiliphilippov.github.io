VERB
Pronunciation: / tʃuz / (AmE), / tʃuːz / (BrE)
to select or decide on someone, something, or an action from a number of possibilities
decide to do something / to select from alternatives / to select one from several options

Examples

After much thought, she chose the blue dress for the party.
Please choose your seats before the show starts.
You may choose any dessert from the menu.

You are free to choose how you spend your free time.
After much thought, we chose to stay at home for the holidays.
She couldn't choose between the blue dress and the red one.
You must choose a password with at least eight characters.
