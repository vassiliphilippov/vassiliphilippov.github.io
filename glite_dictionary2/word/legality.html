1. ⚖️ lawfulness ("legality of actions")

Pronunciation: / lɪˈgæləti / (AmE), / lɪˈɡælɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being in accordance with the law or a legal statute
lawfulness; conformity to law

Examples

Debates about the legality of certain substances continue around the world.
The legality of the contract was called into question by several experts.
We need to check the legality of their business practices before proceeding.

Related Terms

  • antonym – illegality: the state of being illegal or unlawful
  • synonym – lawfulness: the quality of conforming to the law
