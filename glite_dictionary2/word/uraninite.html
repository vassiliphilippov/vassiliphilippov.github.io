1. ☢️ uranium ore ("radioactive uraninite mineral")

Pronunciation: / jʊˈrænɪˌnaɪt / (AmE), / jʊəˈranɪnʌɪt / (BrE)
A naturally occurring mineral consisting mainly of uranium oxide, and often containing small amounts of radium, thorium, polonium, lead, and helium; the chief ore of uranium, also called pitchblende when found in massive form.
uranium-rich mineral; main uranium ore

Examples

Because uraninite contains radioactive elements, handling it requires special safety precautions.
Scientists studied a sample of uraninite to determine its uranium content.
The richest deposits of uraninite have been discovered in Canada and Africa.
