NOUN

an unproven claim or accusation / formal accusation, especially in law

Examples

The court will consider the allegation when reviewing the evidence.
The politician faced several allegations of corruption during the election campaign.
She made a serious allegation against her former employer in court.
The company denied the allegation that it had been involved in fraud.
Until there is evidence to support the allegation, we should not jump to conclusions.
