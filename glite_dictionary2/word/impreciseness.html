1. ❓ inaccuracy ("unhelpful impreciseness")

Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpriːsɪnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈpriːsɪnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of lacking precision; inaccuracy or vagueness.
lack of precision or accuracy

Examples

Her instructions suffered from impreciseness, leaving the team unsure how to proceed.
Legal documents should avoid impreciseness to prevent misunderstandings.
The impreciseness of the measurements led to errors in the experiment.
