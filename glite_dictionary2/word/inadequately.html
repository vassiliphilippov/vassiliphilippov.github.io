1. ⚠️ not enough ("inadequately explained problem")

Pronunciation: / ɪnˈædɪkwətli / (AmE), / ɪnˈædɪkwətli / (BrE)
In a way that is not sufficient or satisfactory; to an insufficient degree.
not sufficiently or satisfactorily

Examples

His report inadequately addressed the main issues raised by the committee.
Many students felt the instructions were inadequately explained before the test began.
The building was inadequately heated during the winter months, causing discomfort for the residents.
