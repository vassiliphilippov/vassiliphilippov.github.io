Hindu god of the sky

Examples

In the Rigveda, Dyaus is often mentioned as the father of other gods.
Scholars compare Dyaus with the Greek god Zeus due to similarities in their attributes.
The myths surrounding Dyaus reflect ancient people's reverence for the sky.
