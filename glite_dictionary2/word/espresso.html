1. ☕ strong coffee ("a cup of espresso")

Pronunciation: / ɛsˈproʊsoʊ / (AmE), / ɛsˈpresəʊ / (BrE)
A strong black coffee made by forcing hot water under pressure through finely ground coffee beans.
strong black coffee made by pressure brewing

Examples

At the café, he watched as the barista prepared a perfect shot of espresso.
Many Italian desserts are enhanced by a splash of espresso.
She ordered a double espresso to help her stay awake during the meeting.
