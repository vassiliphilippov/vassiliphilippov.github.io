1. ⚔️ internal fighting ("internecine conflict")

Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈnɛsin / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈniːsaɪn / (BrE); / ˌɪn.tɚˈniː.saɪn / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈniː.siːn / (BrE)
(of conflict) occurring within a group and causing harm or destruction to all sides involved
conflict within a group or organization / destructive to both sides, especially in war

Examples

Many historians have written about the internecine wars that devastated the region for decades.
The internecine conflict caused massive casualties on both sides of the dispute.

The company's downfall was hastened by an internecine struggle between top executives.
The merger talks broke down amid internecine competition among the directors.
