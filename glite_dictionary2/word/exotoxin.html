1. ☠️ poison from bacteria ("bacterial exotoxin")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.soʊˈtɑk.sɪn / (AmE), / ˌɛk.səʊˈtɒk.sɪn / (BrE)

toxin secreted by microorganisms

Examples

Some bacteria produce dangerous exotoxins that can cause severe illness in humans.
