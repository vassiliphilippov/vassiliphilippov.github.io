1. ↔️ opposite side ("contralateral side injury")

Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈlæt̬ərəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈlætərəl / (BrE)
Occurring on or affecting the opposite side of the body.
on the opposite side (of the body)

Examples

Damage to the left side of the brain can cause contralateral paralysis on the right side of the body.
Doctors observed that the pain was not contralateral but remained on the affected side.
The contralateral limb showed no signs of swelling or injury.
