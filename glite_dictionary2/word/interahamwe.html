1. ⚔️ Rwandan militia ("the Interahamwe militia")

Pronunciation: / ɪn.tɛr.əˈhæm.weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn.tɛr.əˈham.weɪ / (BrE)
A paramilitary group in Rwanda known for perpetrating the 1994 genocide against the Tutsi and for seeking to restore Hutu dominance in the country.
Rwandan Hutu paramilitary group

Examples

International courts have prosecuted several former Interahamwe leaders for crimes against humanity.
Many members of the Interahamwe fled Rwanda after the end of the conflict.
The Interahamwe played a leading role in the Rwandan genocide of 1994.
