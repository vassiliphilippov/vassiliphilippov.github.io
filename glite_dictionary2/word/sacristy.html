1. ⛪ church storage room ("church sacristy")

NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈsækrɪsti / (AmE), / ˈsakrɪsti / (BrE)
a room in a church where sacred vessels, vestments, and other liturgical items are kept, and sometimes where clergy meet or prepare for services
room in a church for sacred items and clergy preparation

Examples

After the mass, she returned the choir robes to the sacristy.
The priest retrieved the chalice from the sacristy before the morning service.
Volunteers gathered in the sacristy to prepare for the Easter ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym – vestry: a room in a church where vestments and sometimes parish records are kept
