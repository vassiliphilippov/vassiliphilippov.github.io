1. ⛷️ to turn quickly ("to wedel down slopes")

Pronunciation: / ˈveɪdəl / (AmE), / ˈwɛdəl / (BrE)
To ski downhill by making a series of quick, short turns with alternating directions.
ski with rapid, alternating turns

Examples

Many expert skiers prefer to wedel rather than use wide, sweeping turns on narrow runs.
She loves to wedel down the steep slopes, weaving effortlessly between the trees.
The instructor showed us how to wedel to control our speed on icy patches.
