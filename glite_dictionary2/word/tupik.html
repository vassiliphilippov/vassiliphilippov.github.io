1. ⛺ Inuit tent ("Inuit tupik shelter")

Pronunciation: / ˈtuː.pɪk / (AmE), / ˈtuː.pɪk / (BrE)
A traditional tent made of animal skins, used as a summer dwelling by Inuit people.
traditional Inuit summer tent

Examples
