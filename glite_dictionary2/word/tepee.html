NOUN
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌpi / (AmE), / ˈtiːpiː / (BrE)
A tent of conical shape traditionally used by some Native American peoples, especially Plains tribes.
conical Native American tent

Examples

A traditional tepee was made from animal hides stretched over wooden poles.
Each family constructed a tepee as their home during the summer months.
The tribe assembled their tepee in a wide circle on the open prairie.
