1. ✨ superficial flashiness ("the meretriciousness of a show")

Pronunciation: / ˌmɛr.əˈtrɪʃ.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛr.ɪˈtrɪʃ.əs.nəs / (BrE); / ˌmɛrəˈtrɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəsnɪs / (BrE)

showy but insincere or superficial attractiveness / superficial attractiveness; deceptive appearance
