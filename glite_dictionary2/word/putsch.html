1. ⚔️ violent takeover ("military putsch")

Pronunciation: / pʊtʃ / (AmE), / pʊtʃ / (BrE)
A sudden attempt to overthrow a government, carried out illegally or by force.
sudden illegal overthrow of government

Examples

After the failed putsch, many of the leaders were arrested and put on trial.
Rumors of a putsch spread quickly through the capital, causing widespread panic.
The military staged a putsch that toppled the government overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coup: a sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government
  • degree lower – rebellion: an act of violent or open resistance to an established government
  • degree higher – revolution: a forcible overthrow of a government or social order, in favor of a new system
