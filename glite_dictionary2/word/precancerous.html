1. ⚠️ before cancer ("precancerous cells")

Pronunciation: / priˈkænsəɹəs / (AmE), / priˈkænsərəs / (BrE)
(of a growth or cell) not malignant, but likely to develop into cancer if untreated.
likely to develop into cancer

Examples

She was relieved to hear that the cells were only precancerous, not malignant.
The doctor found a precancerous lesion during my routine screening.
