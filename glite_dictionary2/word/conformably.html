1. ✅ in agreement ("conformably to rules")

Pronunciation: / kənˈfɔrməbli / (AmE), / kənˈfɔːməbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is in agreement with or consistent to certain standards, rules, or principles.
in a manner consistent with rules or standards

Examples

The parties acted conformably with the terms of the agreement.
The policy was revised conformably to the latest regulations.
