depolarize

1. ⚡ to remove charge difference ("to depolarize a cell membrane")

Pronunciation: / diˈpɔləˌraɪz / (AmE), / diˈpɔːləraɪz / (BrE)
To eliminate or reduce the polarization of something, especially in the context of electrical charge, such as a cell membrane or an electrical device.
eliminate polarization (especially electrically)

Examples

Exposure to certain toxins can depolarize muscle fibers, affecting their function.
The technician used a special device to depolarize the capacitor.
When a nerve cell is stimulated, it will depolarize and generate an electrical impulse.

  • antonym – polarize: to cause to become polar or more polar, especially electrically
