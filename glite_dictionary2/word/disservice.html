Pronunciation: / dɪsˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / dɪsˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
an action that harms someone or something, especially one that was meant to help
harmful or damaging action

Examples

He meant to encourage her, but his words were actually a disservice.
