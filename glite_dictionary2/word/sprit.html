1. ⛵ sail support pole ("sprit of a sailboat")

Pronunciation: / sprɪt / (AmE), / sprɪt / (BrE)
a light pole or spar that runs diagonally across a fore-and-aft sail to extend and support it
diagonal spar on a sailboat sail

Examples

He replaced the broken sprit before setting out to sea again.
The small boat's sprit kept the sail stretched tight on the windy lake.
Traditional dinghies often use a sprit rather than a boom to support their sails.
