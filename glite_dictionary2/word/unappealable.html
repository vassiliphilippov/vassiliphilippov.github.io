1. ⚖️ cannot be challenged ("unappealable court decision")

Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈpiːləbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈpiːləbəl / (BrE)
(of a decision or ruling) not able to be challenged or changed by an appeal to a higher court or authority.
not subject to appeal

Examples

An order designated as unappealable cannot be challenged in any court.
Because the contract stated the arbitration was final and unappealable, neither party could contest the outcome.
The decision of the disciplinary committee was unappealable under the association's rules.

Related Terms

  • antonym – appealable: subject to appeal; able to be appealed
