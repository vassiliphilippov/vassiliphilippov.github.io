1. ↩️ rotating light left ("levorotary sugar solution")

Pronunciation: / ˌliːvəˈroʊtəri / (AmE), / ˌliːvəˈrəʊtəri / (BrE)
(of a substance) rotating the plane of polarized light to the left, that is, counterclockwise as viewed towards the light source.
rotating light to the left (chemistry/physics)

Examples

Some sugars exhibit levorotary behavior due to their molecular structure.
