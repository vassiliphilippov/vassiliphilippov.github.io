grey kingbird

noun
a large gray kingbird found in the Americas
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈkɪŋˌbɝd / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈkɪŋˌbɜːd / (BrE)
a large kingbird (Tyrannus dominicensis) native to the southeastern United States and the Caribbean, known for its gray plumage and aggressive behavior
