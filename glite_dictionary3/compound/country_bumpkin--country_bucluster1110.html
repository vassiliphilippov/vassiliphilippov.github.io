country bumpkin

noun
unsophisticated rural person
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri ˈbʌmpkɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ˈbʌmpkɪn / (BrE)
A person from a rural area who is considered to be unsophisticated or lacking in social polish.

Examples

a country bumpkin in the city
She worried that her accent would make her sound like a country bumpkin at the job interview.
The locals dismissed him as a country bumpkin, but he was actually quite clever.
They teased their friend for being a country bumpkin after he admitted he'd never seen the ocean.

Related Terms

  • antonym – city slicker: a sophisticated urban person, especially contrasted with rural simplicity
  • synonym – yokel: an unrefined, unsophisticated person from the countryside
