prima donna

noun
lead female opera singer
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmə ˈdɑːnə / (AmE), / ˌpriːmə ˈdɒnə / (BrE)
A leading female singer in an opera company, especially one who is distinguished or celebrated for her talent.

Examples

famous prima donna
Many young singers aspire to become the next prima donna at the Metropolitan Opera.
She has the voice of a true prima donna, commanding attention with every note.
The renowned prima donna received a standing ovation after her powerful performance.
