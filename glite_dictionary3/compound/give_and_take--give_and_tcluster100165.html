give and take

noun
mutual compromise or exchange
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪv ənd ˈteɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɪv ən ˈteɪk / (BrE)
the practice of mutual compromise or exchange of favors in a relationship or negotiation

Examples

give and take in negotiations
During the meeting, there was a healthy amount of give and take between the two groups.
His approach to teamwork is based on give and take rather than strict rules.
Successful marriages often involve a lot of give and take from both partners.
