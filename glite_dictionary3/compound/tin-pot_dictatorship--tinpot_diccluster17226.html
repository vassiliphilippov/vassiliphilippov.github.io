tin-pot dictatorship

noun
petty or insignificant dictatorship
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪn ˌpɑt ˌdɪkˈteɪtɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtɪn ˌpɒt ˌdɪkˈteɪtəʃɪp / (BrE)
A small, petty, or insignificant authoritarian regime, often regarded with contempt or ridicule.

Examples

corrupt tin-pot dictatorship
Journalists criticized the leader's rule as nothing more than a tin-pot dictatorship.
