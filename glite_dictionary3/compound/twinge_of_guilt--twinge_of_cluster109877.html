twinge of guilt

noun
a sudden feeling of guilt
Pronunciation: / twɪndʒ əv ɡɪlt / (AmE), / twɪndʒ əv ɡɪlt / (BrE)

Examples

feel a twinge of guilt
After lying to his parents, a twinge of guilt kept him awake at night.

She felt a twinge of guilt as she walked past the homeless man.
