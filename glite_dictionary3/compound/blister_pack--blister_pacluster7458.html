blister pack

noun
packaging with plastic cover and cardboard backing
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪstɚ pæk / (AmE), / ˈblɪstə pæk / (BrE)
A type of packaging in which a product is sealed between a clear plastic cover and a cardboard backing.

Examples

blister pack of tablets
Each tablet comes in a blister pack to ensure its freshness.
He struggled to open the blister pack without scissors.
Many medications are sold in a blister pack for convenience and hygiene.
