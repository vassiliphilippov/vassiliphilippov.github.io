avian dinosaur

noun
dinosaur related to birds
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪviən ˈdaɪnəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈeɪvɪən ˈdaɪnəsɔː / (BrE)
any member of a group of dinosaurs that includes modern birds and their extinct bird-like ancestors

Examples

fossil avian dinosaur
Paleontologists now agree that modern birds are, in fact, a type of avian dinosaur.
The avian dinosaur Archaeopteryx is often cited as a transitional species between reptiles and birds.
The discovery of feathers on a fossilized avian dinosaur shed new light on bird evolution.
