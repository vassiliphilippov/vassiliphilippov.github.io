free agent

noun
person acting independently or unpredictably
Pronunciation: / friː ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / friː ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who acts independently, without being controlled by others, sometimes in an unpredictable or irresponsible manner

Examples

independent free agent
Ever since he left the company, John has been a free agent, following his own path.
In group work, it's hard to coordinate when one member is a free agent who won't stick to the plan.
She enjoyed being a free agent, making decisions without worrying about others' expectations.
