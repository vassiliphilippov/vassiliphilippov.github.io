acting out

noun
impulsive or disruptive outburst
Pronunciation: / ˈæktɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈæktɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
impulsive or disruptive behavior that expresses emotions through actions instead of words, often seen in children or adults
