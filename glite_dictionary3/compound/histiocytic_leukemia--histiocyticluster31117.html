histiocytic leukemia

noun
type of leukemia involving monocytes/monoblasts
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstioʊˈsɪtɪk lɪˈkiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhɪstɪəʊˈsɪtɪk lɪˈkiːmiə / (BrE)
a form of leukemia marked by the abnormal increase of monocytes and monoblasts in the blood

Examples

histiocytic leukemia diagnosis
Pediatric cases of histiocytic leukemia are rare but require immediate medical attention.
The patient was diagnosed with histiocytic leukemia following a series of blood tests and biopsies.
Treatments for histiocytic leukemia often include chemotherapy and stem cell transplants.
