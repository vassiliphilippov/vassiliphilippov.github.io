digestive tube

noun
the tube from mouth to anus in the digestive system
Pronunciation: / dɪˈdʒɛstɪv tuːb / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛstɪv tjuːb / (BrE)
the tubular passage extending from the mouth to the anus through which food passes, where digestion and elimination of waste occur

Examples

the digestive tube of animals
Damage to any part of the digestive tube can lead to serious health issues.
In some animals, the digestive tube is highly specialized for different diets.
The digestive tube is responsible for both digesting food and eliminating waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alimentary canal: the whole passage along which food passes through the body, from mouth to anus
  • synonym – digestive tract: the series of organs in the digestive system forming a continuous passage from mouth to anus
