anchovy pear

noun
West Indian tree and its edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈæntʃoʊvi pɛr / (AmE), / ˈantʃəvi peə / (BrE)
a West Indian tree (Grias cauliflora) with edible fruit resembling a mango, or the fruit itself
