CS gas

noun
potent tear gas used in riot control
Pronunciation: / si ɛs ɡæs / (AmE), / siː ɛs ɡæs / (BrE)
a powerful tear gas that is more potent than CN gas, causing irritation to eyes, skin, and respiratory system, sometimes resulting in skin burns or serious lung injury; often used in riot control

Examples

CS gas for crowd control
Exposure to CS gas can cause severe coughing and eye irritation.
Police used CS gas to disperse the protesters blocking the main street.
The military trained personnel on how to handle CS gas safely during exercises.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – CN gas: an older and less potent form of tear gas (chloroacetophenone)
  • synonym – tear gas: gas used to cause irritation and disperse crowds
