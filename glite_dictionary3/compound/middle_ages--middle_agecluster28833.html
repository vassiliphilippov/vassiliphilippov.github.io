Middle Ages

noun
period in European history (5th–15th centuries)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdəl ˈeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪdəl ˈeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
the period of European history between the end of classical antiquity and the beginning of the Renaissance, roughly from the 5th to the late 15th century
