worker bee

noun
sterile female bee that collects food and maintains the hive
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkɚ biː / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkə biː / (BrE)
A sterile female bee in a colony, specialized in collecting food and maintaining the hive.

Examples

worker bee collects nectar
Every worker bee helps maintain the health and cleanliness of the hive.
The worker bee spends its entire life gathering nectar for the hive.
Unlike the queen, the worker bee is sterile and cannot lay eggs.

Related Terms

  • antonym – drone: a male bee whose main role is mating with the queen
  • antonym – queen bee: the fertile female bee in a colony
