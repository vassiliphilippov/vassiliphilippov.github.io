pure absence

noun
simple form of absence seizure
Pronunciation: / pjʊr ˈæbsəns / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈæbsəns / (BrE)
A type of absence seizure that occurs without other complications, typically characterized by 3-per-second brainwave spikes.

Children with pure absence often experience brief, sudden lapses in awareness without other symptoms.
