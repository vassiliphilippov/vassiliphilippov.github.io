train depot

noun
place where trains load/unload passengers or goods
Pronunciation: / treɪn ˈdiːpoʊ / (AmE), / treɪn ˈdiːpəʊ / (BrE)
a building or area where trains take on or discharge passengers or freight

Examples

Freight cars were unloaded at the train depot near the industrial park.
Passengers waited inside the train depot for the next departure to the city.
The old train depot was converted into a museum after it closed.
