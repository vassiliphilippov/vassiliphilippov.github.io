long suit

noun
card suit with most cards in a hand
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ suːt / (AmE), / lɒŋ suːt / (BrE)
In a hand of cards, the suit that contains the greatest number of cards.

Examples

strongest long suit in cards
His strategy relied on developing the long suit to win extra tricks.
In bridge, having a long suit in your hand can be a significant advantage.
She led with her long suit, hoping to establish it early in the game.
