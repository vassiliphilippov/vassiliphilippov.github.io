fetal age

noun
age of embryo or fetus from fertilization
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtəl eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfiːtəl eɪdʒ / (BrE)
the age of an embryo or fetus calculated from the time of fertilization

Examples

fetal age calculation
Accurate determination of fetal age is important for monitoring pregnancy milestones.
Fetal development can vary depending on the fetal age at the time of the scan.
The doctor estimated the fetal age using ultrasound measurements.
