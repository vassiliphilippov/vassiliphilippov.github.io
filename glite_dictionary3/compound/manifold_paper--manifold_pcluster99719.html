manifold paper

noun
lightweight paper for making multiple copies
Pronunciation: / ˈmænəˌfoʊld ˈpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈmænɪfəʊld ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
A thin, lightweight paper used with carbon paper for making multiple copies of documents.

Examples

manifold paper for copies
For legal forms, manifold paper is often used with carbon copies.
