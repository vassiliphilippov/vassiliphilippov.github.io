coco plum

noun
tropical American fruit used for preserves
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkoʊ ˌplʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkəʊ ˌplʌm / (BrE)
A small, plum-shaped fruit with whitish to almost black skin, native to tropical America and often used for preserves.
