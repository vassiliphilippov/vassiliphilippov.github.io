the Nazarene

proper noun
title for Jesus Christ, teacher from Nazareth
Pronunciation: / ðə ˌnæzəˈrin / (AmE), / ðə ˌnæzəˈriːn / (BrE)
a title for Jesus Christ, referring to his association with Nazareth; a teacher and prophet whose life forms the basis of Christianity

Examples

the Nazarene taught crowds
Many early Christians referred to Jesus as the Nazarene in their writings.
Paintings from the Renaissance often depict the Nazarene with a gentle expression.
Pilate asked the crowd what he should do with the Nazarene.
