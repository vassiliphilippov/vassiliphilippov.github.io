treasure ship

noun
16th-century ship carrying treasure
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛʒər ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈtrɛʒə ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship, especially from the 16th century, that was loaded with valuable cargo such as gold, silver, and precious goods
