passing comment

noun
an incidental or casual remark
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ ˈkɑːmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ ˈkɒmɛnt / (BrE)
a remark made casually and without much thought or attention; an incidental remark

Examples

a passing comment in conversation

His passing comment went unnoticed by most of the audience.
She made a passing comment about the weather as she entered the room.
