digital camera

noun
camera that stores images digitally
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl ˈkæm(ə)ɹə / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈkæm(ə)rə / (BrE)
A camera that records images in digital form, allowing them to be stored and viewed electronically.

use a digital camera outdoors
Most smartphones now function as a digital camera as well as a phone.
