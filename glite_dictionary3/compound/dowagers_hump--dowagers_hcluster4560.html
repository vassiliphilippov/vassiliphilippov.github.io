dowager's hump

noun
spinal curvature caused by osteoporosis
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊədʒɚz hʌmp / (AmE), / ˈdaʊədʒəz hʌmp / (BrE)
an abnormal outward curvature of the upper spine, resulting in a hunched appearance, especially in elderly women, often due to osteoporosis
