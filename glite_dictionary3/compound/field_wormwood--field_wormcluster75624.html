field wormwood

noun
a species of European wormwood plant
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˈwɜrmˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˈwɜːmˌwʊd / (BrE)
A European species of wormwood (Artemisia campestris) similar in properties to common wormwood, often found in dry fields and waste grounds.

Field wormwood is often found in dry, sandy soils across much of Europe.
