sea hare

noun
marine mollusc with ear-like tentacles
Pronunciation: / siː hɛr / (AmE), / siː heə / (BrE)
a large, soft-bodied marine mollusc with reduced internal shell and two pairs of ear-like tentacles, belonging to the family Aplysiidae.

Examples

sea hare gliding underwater
Divers often spot the brightly colored sea hare gliding over the seabed.
