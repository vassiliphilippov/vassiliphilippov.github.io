facile explanation

noun
an overly simple explanation
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsl ɛkˌspleɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfæs.əl ɪkˌspleɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an explanation that is too simplistic or superficial to be accurate or convincing

Examples

Critics dismissed his theory as just another facile explanation lacking real evidence.
