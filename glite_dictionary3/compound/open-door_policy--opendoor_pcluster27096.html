open-door policy

noun
policy allowing open communication and accessibility
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpən ˈdɔr ˌpɑləsi / (AmE), / ˌəʊpən ˈdɔː ˌpɒləsi / (BrE)
a policy of encouraging openness, accessibility, and communication, allowing people to approach those in authority freely

Examples

open-door policy for staff
Her open-door policy made it easy for team members to approach her with new ideas.
Many universities have adopted an open-door policy to encourage feedback from students and staff.
Our company maintains an open-door policy to ensure all employees feel comfortable sharing their concerns.

  • synonym – open-door: allowing unrestricted access or movement
