stirrup pump

noun
hand-operated fire-fighting pump
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrəp pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈstɪrəp pʌmp / (BrE)
a portable hand-operated pump, typically mounted on a footplate, used to spray water in fighting fires
