pyramid scheme

noun
fraudulent investment recruitment scheme
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪrəmɪd skiːm / (AmE), / ˈpɪrəmɪd skiːm / (BrE)
a fraudulent investment scheme in which participants recruit others to make payments, with profits mainly derived from recruiting new participants rather than selling products or services

Examples

illegal pyramid scheme
Before joining any investment opportunity, make sure it's not a disguised pyramid scheme.
He was arrested for running a pyramid scheme that promised huge returns to new members.
Many investors lost their life savings in the pyramid scheme before authorities shut it down.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Ponzi scheme: an investment fraud that pays earlier investors with funds from recent investors
