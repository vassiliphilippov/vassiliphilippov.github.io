to roller skate

verb
move using shoes with wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə skeɪt / (BrE)
to move or travel on shoes with wheels attached to the soles, typically for recreation or sport

Examples

to roller skate in the park
As a child, she loved to roller skate in the park every weekend.
He learned to roller skate before he could even ride a bicycle.
Waiters sometimes roller skate around the restaurant to serve food quickly.
