multicultist community

noun
group supporting cultural diversity
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈkʌltɪst kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈkʌltɪst kəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
A group or collective that advocates for, supports, or promotes cultural diversity within society.
