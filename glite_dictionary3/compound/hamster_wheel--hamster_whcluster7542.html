hamster wheel

noun
monotonous routine without progress
Pronunciation: / ˈhæm.stɚ ˌwil / (AmE), / ˈhæm.stə ˌwiːl / (BrE)
a situation or activity that involves continuous, repetitive work or effort without achieving progress or change

Examples

stuck on a hamster wheel
After years at the same job, she realized she was stuck on a hamster wheel with no real advancement.
He decided to leave the hamster wheel of corporate life and travel the world instead.
Their daily routine felt like a hamster wheel, repeating the same activities over and over.
