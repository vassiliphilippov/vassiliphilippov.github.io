tandem bicycle

noun
bicycle for two riders
Pronunciation: / ˈtændəm ˈbaɪsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtændəm ˈbaɪsɪkl / (BrE)
a bicycle designed for two riders, with two sets of pedals and two seats arranged one behind the other
