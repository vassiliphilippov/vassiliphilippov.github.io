cattley guava

noun
a tropical shrub with red or yellow fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈkætli ˈɡwɑvə / (AmE), / ˈkætli ˈɡwɑːvə / (BrE)
a small tropical shrubby tree (Psidium cattleyanum) that bears deep red or sometimes yellow oval fruit, also called strawberry guava
