clinical depression

noun
severe, diagnosable depression requiring treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪnɪkəl dɪˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪkəl dɪˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
a medically diagnosed mental disorder characterized by persistent and severe feelings of sadness and loss of interest, often requiring professional treatment

Examples

clinical depression diagnosis

Effective treatment for clinical depression often includes a combination of medication and therapy.
