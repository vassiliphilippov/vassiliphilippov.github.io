voice-over

noun
unseen narration in media
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪs ˌoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪs ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
a commentary or narration in a film, television program, or commercial, spoken by someone who is not seen on screen

Examples

voice-over narration
She works as a voice-over artist, recording ads and documentaries.
The film's opening voice-over sets the scene for the story.
