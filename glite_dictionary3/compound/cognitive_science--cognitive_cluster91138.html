cognitive science

noun
the study of the mind and its processes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑɡnəˌtɪv ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡnətɪv ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
the interdisciplinary scientific study of the mind and its processes, combining aspects of psychology, neuroscience, linguistics, computer science, and philosophy
