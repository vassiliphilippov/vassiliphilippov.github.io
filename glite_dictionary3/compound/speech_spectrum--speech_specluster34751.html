speech spectrum

noun
range of frequencies in the human voice
Pronunciation: / spiːtʃ ˈspɛktrəm / (AmE), / spiːtʃ ˈspɛktrəm / (BrE)
the range of frequencies present in the average human voice, as represented in a sound spectrum

Examples

speech spectrum analysis
Acoustic engineers often analyze the speech spectrum to improve the quality of voice communication systems.
Differences in the speech spectrum can help identify regional accents and speaker characteristics.
Hearing aids are designed to amplify the frequencies most important in the speech spectrum.
