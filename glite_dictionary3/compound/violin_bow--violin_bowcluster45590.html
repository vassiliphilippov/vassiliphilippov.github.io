violin bow

noun
bow used to play a violin
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈoʊlɪn boʊ / (AmE), / vaɪˈɒlɪn bəʊ / (BrE)
A thin, typically curved stick strung with horsehair, used to play the violin by drawing it across the strings.
