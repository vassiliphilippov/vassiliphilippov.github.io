sea pork

noun
the flesh or meat of certain marine fish
Pronunciation: / siː pɔrk / (AmE), / siː pɔːk / (BrE)
the flesh of certain marine animals, such as porgy or porcupinefish, used as food; sometimes refers to either the fish itself or its meat
