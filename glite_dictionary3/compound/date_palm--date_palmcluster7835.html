date palm

noun
tall palm tree producing edible dates
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪt ˌpɑm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪt ˌpɑːm / (BrE)
A tall tropical palm tree native to the Middle East and North Africa, known for producing sweet edible dates.
