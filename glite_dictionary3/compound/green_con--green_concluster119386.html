green con

noun
scam involving false environmental claims
Pronunciation: / ɡrin kɑn / (AmE), / ɡriːn kɒn / (BrE)
A scam or trick involving deceptive or exaggerated claims about environmental friendliness, often to mislead people or gain financial benefit.

Examples

fall for a green con
Dozens of consumers fell victim to a green con that promised to plant a tree for every purchase.
She became suspicious when the investment opportunity sounded like another green con.
The company was accused of running a green con by exaggerating the eco-friendliness of its products.

Related Terms

  • synonym – greenwashing: the act of misleading consumers about the environmental practices of a company or the environmental benefits of a product or service
