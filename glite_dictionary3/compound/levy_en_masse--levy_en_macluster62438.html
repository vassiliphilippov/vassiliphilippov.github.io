levy en masse

noun
mass conscription into military service
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛvi ɑ̃ ˈmæs / (AmE), / ˌlɛvi ɒ̃ ˈmas / (BrE)
the act of conscripting or drafting large numbers of civilians into military service, especially during times of national emergency

Examples
