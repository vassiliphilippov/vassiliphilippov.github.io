Welsh Black

noun
breed of Welsh black cattle
Pronunciation: / wɛlʃ blæk / (AmE), / wɛlʃ blak / (BrE)
a breed of hardy, dual-purpose cattle, black in color, developed in Wales for both beef and milk production
