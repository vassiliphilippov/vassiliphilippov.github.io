major fast day

noun
Jewish principal day of fasting
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒər fæst deɪ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə fɑːst deɪ / (BrE)
Either of two principal days of fasting observed in the Jewish calendar, namely Yom Kippur or Tisha B'Av.

Yom Kippur, a major fast day, requires total abstention from food and water.
