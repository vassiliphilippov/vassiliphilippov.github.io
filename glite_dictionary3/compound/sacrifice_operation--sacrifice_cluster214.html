sacrifice operation

noun
deliberate self-killing attack on others
Pronunciation: / ˈsækrəˌfaɪs ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsæk.rɪ.faɪs ˌɒp.əˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an act in which a person deliberately kills or injures others while causing their own death, usually by means of an explosive device
