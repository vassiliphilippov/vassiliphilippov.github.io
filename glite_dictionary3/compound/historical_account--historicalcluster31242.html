historical account

noun
narrative or record of past events
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔr.ɪ.kəl əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒr.ɪ.kəl əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a written or spoken narrative or record describing events or conditions from the past

Examples

detailed historical account
Her book offers a vivid historical account of life during the Great Depression.
Many historical accounts differ in their explanation of how the empire collapsed.
The researcher consulted a historical account of the city's founding to gather reliable details.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chronicle: a factual written record of important or historical events in the order of their occurrence
  • antonym – myth: a traditional story, especially one concerning the early history of a people or explaining a natural or social phenomenon, which is not based on fact
