jack-o-lantern fungus

noun
poisonous orange luminescent mushroom
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒæk oʊ ˈlæntɚn ˌfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˌdʒæk əʊ ˈlæntən ˌfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a large poisonous mushroom with orange caps and clustered narrow stalks, whose gills can glow in the dark

Examples

glowing jack-o-lantern fungus
At night, the gills of a jack-o-lantern fungus can emit an eerie greenish glow.
The jack-o-lantern fungus is often mistaken for edible chanterelles by inexperienced foragers.
