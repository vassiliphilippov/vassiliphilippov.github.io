Thematic Apperception Test

noun
projective psychological test using pictures for storytelling
Pronunciation: / θəˈmæt̬ɪk əˌpɚˈsɛpʃən tɛst / (AmE), / θɪˈmatɪk əˌpɜːˈsɛpʃən tɛst / (BrE)
a projective psychological test in which a person is shown black-and-white pictures and asked to create a story about each image, revealing underlying thoughts and feelings

Examples

Thematic Apperception Test story pictures
During the Thematic Apperception Test, participants are encouraged to interpret ambiguous images.
The psychologist administered the Thematic Apperception Test to assess the patient's personality traits.
The results of the Thematic Apperception Test provided insights into his unconscious motivations.
