drafting table

noun
worktable with adjustable top for drawing
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftɪŋ ˌteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftɪŋ ˌteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
a large table with an adjustable top, used by architects, engineers, and artists for drawing or drafting designs

Examples

drafting table for blueprints
He spent hours at his drafting table perfecting the technical drawings for the new building.
She inherited her grandfather's old drafting table, which had been used for decades in his studio.
The architect spread her blueprints across the drafting table, carefully adjusting the angle for better visibility.
