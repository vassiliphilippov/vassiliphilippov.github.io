wool oil

noun
oil applied to wool before spinning
Pronunciation: / wʊl ɔɪl / (AmE), / wʊl ɔɪəl / (BrE)
any oil applied to wool before spinning to facilitate processing and improve fiber quality

Examples

wool oil for spinning
Before spinning, the raw fleece was treated with wool oil to prevent static.
Specialty wool oil blends are often used to enhance the texture of the final yarn.
The machinery requires wool oil to keep the fibers soft and manageable during spinning.
