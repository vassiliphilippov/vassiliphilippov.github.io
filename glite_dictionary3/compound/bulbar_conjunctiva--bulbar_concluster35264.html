bulbar conjunctiva

noun
part of the eye's conjunctiva covering the front of sclera/cornea
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌl.bɑr kənˌdʒʌŋkˈtaɪ.və / (AmE), / ˈbʌl.bɑː kənˌdʒʌŋkˈtɪ.və / (BrE)
the part of the conjunctiva that covers the front of the sclera and the surface of the cornea in the eye
