Disciples of Christ

proper noun
Protestant Christian denomination
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪpəlz əv kraɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪpəlz əv kraɪst / (BrE)
A Protestant Christian denomination that regards the Bible as the sole source of faith and baptizes by immersion.
