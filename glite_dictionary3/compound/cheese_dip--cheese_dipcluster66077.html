cheese dip

noun
savory dip made from cheese
Pronunciation: / tʃiz dɪp / (AmE), / tʃiːz dɪp / (BrE)
A dish made by melting cheese and often other ingredients into a savory dip, usually served with chips, bread, or vegetables.
