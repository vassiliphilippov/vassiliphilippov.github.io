full beam headlamp

noun
car headlamp with undirected, bright light
Pronunciation: / fʊl bim ˈhɛd.læmp / (AmE), / fʊl biːm ˈhɛd.læmp / (BrE)
A car headlamp providing a bright, focused light with no control to avoid dazzling other drivers, used only when no other vehicles are present on the road.

Examples

You should only use the full beam headlamp when there's no one coming toward you on the road.
