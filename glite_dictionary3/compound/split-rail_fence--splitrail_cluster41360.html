split-rail fence

noun
fence of stacked split logs
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɪt reɪl fɛns / (AmE), / ˈsplɪt reɪl fɛns / (BrE)
a fence made by stacking split logs horizontally, typically in a zigzag pattern

Examples

split-rail fence around a field
After the storm, several sections of the split-rail fence were knocked down and needed repairs.
The old farmhouse was surrounded by a split-rail fence that gave it a rustic charm.
They built a split-rail fence along the property to mark the boundary.
