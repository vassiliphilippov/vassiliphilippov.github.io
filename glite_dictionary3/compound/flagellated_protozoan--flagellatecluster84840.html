flagellated protozoan

noun
protozoan with one or more flagella
Pronunciation: / fləˈdʒɛl.eɪ.tɪd proʊˈtoʊ.zoʊ.ən / (AmE), / fləˈdʒɛl.ɪ.eɪ.tɪd prəʊˈtəʊ.zəʊ.ən / (BrE)
a protozoan organism that moves using one or more whip-like flagella; some are free-living, while others can cause disease in humans and animals

Examples

Scientists observed the rapid movement of the flagellated protozoan under the microscope.
