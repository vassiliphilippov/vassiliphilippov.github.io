rock hyrax

noun
small mammal of rocky areas (Africa, Middle East)
Pronunciation: / rɑk ˈhaɪˌræks / (AmE), / rɒk ˈhaɪ.raks / (BrE)
A small, herbivorous mammal native to Africa and the Middle East, living in rocky terrain; also called a rock rabbit or dassie.
