goatskin rug

noun
rug made from goat skin
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊtskɪn rʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊtskɪn rʌɡ / (BrE)
a rug made from the hide or skin of a goat, often used as a decorative floor covering
