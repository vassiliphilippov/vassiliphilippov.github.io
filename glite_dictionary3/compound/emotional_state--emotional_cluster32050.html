emotional state

noun
a person's condition of emotions at a time
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənl steɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl steɪt / (BrE)
a person's condition with respect to their feelings and emotions at a particular time, especially regarding pleasure or dejection
