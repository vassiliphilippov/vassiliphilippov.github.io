dole bludger

noun
person living off government benefits without working
Pronunciation: / doʊl ˈblʌdʒɚ / (AmE), / dəʊl ˈblʌdʒə / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly Australian) A person who habitually lives off government unemployment benefits without seeking work.

Examples

lazy dole bludger
He was unfairly labeled a dole bludger by his neighbors after losing his job.
Some newspapers criticize policy changes by suggesting they will encourage more dole bludgers.
The term dole bludger is often used in political debates about welfare reform.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shirker: a person who avoids work or responsibility
  • synonym – welfare queen: (offensive, US) woman who manipulates government welfare systems for financial gain
