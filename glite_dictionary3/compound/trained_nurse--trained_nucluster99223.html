trained nurse

noun
nurse with formal training
Pronunciation: / treɪnd nɜrs / (AmE), / treɪnd nɜːs / (BrE)
a nurse who has completed formal education and clinical training at a recognized nursing school

Examples

trained nurse at hospital
After her studies, Maria became a trained nurse and began working in the city hospital.
