mother figure

noun
woman who acts as a mother
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðər ˌfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə ˌfɪɡə / (BrE)
a woman who provides guidance, support, or care similar to that of a mother, often without being the biological mother

Examples

a mother figure in life
After her mother died, her aunt became a mother figure to her.
She looked up to her coach as a mother figure and often sought her advice.
The teacher was a mother figure for many of her students, guiding them through difficult times.

Related Terms

  • antonym – father figure: a man who provides care or guidance like a father
