stone curlew

noun
a type of nocturnal shorebird
Pronunciation: / stoʊn ˈkɜr.luː / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈkɜː.ljuː / (BrE)
a large-headed, large-eyed nocturnal or crepuscular shorebird of the Old World and tropical America, known for its thickened knee joint
