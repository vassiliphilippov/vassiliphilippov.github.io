morning dew

noun
moisture on surfaces in the morning
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ du / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ djuː / (BrE)
Tiny drops of moisture that form on outdoor surfaces during the early morning, usually as a result of condensation.

Examples

morning dew on grass
After a cool night, the windowpanes were covered in morning dew.
He photographed the delicate morning dew on each flower petal.
The grass was still wet with morning dew as she walked barefoot across the lawn.

  • synonym – dew: moisture in small drops on surfaces due to condensation
