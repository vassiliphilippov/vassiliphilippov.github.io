rose apple

noun
fragrant tropical fruit used in jellies
Pronunciation: / roʊz ˈæp.əl / (AmE), / rəʊz ˈæp.əl / (BrE)
A fragrant, oval, yellowish tropical fruit, often used in jellies and confections, from various trees of the genus Syzygium.

fresh rose apple fruit
