involutional depression

noun
depression related to menopause or aging
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌvɑːluːˈʃənəl dɪˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌvɒljuːˈʃənəl dɪˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
a major depressive episode occurring during or after the climacteric, particularly associated with menopause or aging.
