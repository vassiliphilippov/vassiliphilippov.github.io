itch mite

noun
whitish skin-infesting mite
Pronunciation: / ɪtʃ maɪt / (AmE), / ɪtʃ maɪt / (BrE)
a small whitish mite that infests the skin of humans and other animals, causing irritation and sometimes scabies

Examples

itch mite infestation
Itch mite infestations can lead to severe itching and skin irritation.
Farm animals are sometimes treated for itch mite infestations to protect their health.
The doctor explained that the rash was caused by an itch mite infection.
