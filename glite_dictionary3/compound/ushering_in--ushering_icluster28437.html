ushering in

noun
the start or introduction of something new
Pronunciation: / ˈʌʃərɪŋ ɪn / (AmE), / ˈʌʃərɪŋ ɪn / (BrE)

Examples

The collapse of the regime marked the ushering in of a new political order.
