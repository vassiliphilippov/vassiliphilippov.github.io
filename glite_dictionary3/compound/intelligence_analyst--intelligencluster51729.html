intelligence analyst

noun
government or military information analyst
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈænəˌlaɪst / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈænəlɪst / (BrE)
a person employed to collect and evaluate information about enemies or potential threats, typically for a government or military organization

Examples

intelligence analyst for the military
As an intelligence analyst, she provides daily briefings to senior officials on emerging threats.
The intelligence analyst carefully examined satellite images to assess the military buildup.
The government hired several new intelligence analysts to improve national security assessments.
