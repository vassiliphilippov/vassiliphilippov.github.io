night bird

noun
bird active at night (e.g. owl)
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt bɝd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt bɜːd / (BrE)
A bird that is active or commonly seen at night, such as an owl or nightjar.

Examples

Naturalists study the habits of the night bird to better understand nocturnal wildlife.
The barn owl is a well-known night bird found on every continent except Antarctica.
