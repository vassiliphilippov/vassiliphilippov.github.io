family Crotalidae

noun
family of pit vipers (venomous snakes)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli kroʊˈtæləˌdi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli krəʊˈtælɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of venomous snakes, commonly known as pit vipers, characterized by heat-sensing pits between the eyes and nostrils.
