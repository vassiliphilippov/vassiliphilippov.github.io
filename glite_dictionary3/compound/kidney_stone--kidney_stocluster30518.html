kidney stone

noun
hard mass formed in the kidney
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdni stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɪdni stəʊn / (BrE)
a hard mass or deposit that forms in the kidney from minerals or crystals, often causing pain and urinary problems
