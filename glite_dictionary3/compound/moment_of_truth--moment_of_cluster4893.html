moment of truth

noun
pre-purchase online research stage
Pronunciation: / mo8 m59nt 59v trub8 / (AmE), / m598am59nt 59v trud0b8 / (BrE)
(in marketing and digital analytics) the pre-purchase stage in the customer journey when consumers actively search for information (reviews, comparisons, recommendations) about a product or brand before deciding to buy.

Examples

shopper's moment of truth
Digital teams monitor social posts to spot the moment of truth when shoppers research a product.
Marketers optimize product pages to capture the moment of truth before customers choose a brand.
In training sessions they explain how to help customers at the moment of truth during research.

Related Terms

  • synonym – customer journey: the stages a consumer passes through from awareness to purchase and beyond
  • synonym – zero moment of truth: a marketing term (ZMOT) for the online pre-purchase research consumers do before buying
