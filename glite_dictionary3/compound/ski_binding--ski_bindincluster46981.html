ski binding

noun
device attaching ski boot to ski
Pronunciation: / ski ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ski ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
a mechanical device fixed to a ski that secures the boot and releases it in the event of a fall, enhancing safety

Examples

ski binding adjustment
Make sure your ski bindings are adjusted correctly before heading down the mountain.
She replaced the ski bindings on her new skis for better safety.
The ski binding released automatically when he fell, preventing injury.
