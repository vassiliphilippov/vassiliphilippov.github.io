poisonous nightshade

noun
a highly toxic perennial plant with violet flowers and red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzənəs ˈnaɪtʃeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzənəs ˈnaɪtʃeɪdʒ / (BrE)
