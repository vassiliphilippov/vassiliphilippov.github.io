articulated lorry

noun
large truck with a cab and trailer
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrˈtɪkjəˌleɪtɪd ˈlɔːri / (AmE), / ˌɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd ˈlɒri / (BrE)
A large vehicle with a cab and a trailer connected by a joint, used especially for transporting goods over long distances.
