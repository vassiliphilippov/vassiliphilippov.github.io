bottled gas

noun
fuel gas in pressurized bottles
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdəld ˈɡæs / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəld ˈɡas / (BrE)
Hydrocarbon gases, usually propane or butane, stored under pressure in portable containers for use as fuel.

Examples

use bottled gas for cooking
After the earthquake, residents relied on bottled gas for heating and lighting.
The campsite provides bottled gas refills for campervans and caravans.
We use bottled gas for cooking in our holiday cabin, as there is no main supply.
