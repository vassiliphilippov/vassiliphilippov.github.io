polysemous word

noun
word with multiple meanings
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪsɪməs wɝd / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪsɪməs wɜːd / (BrE)
a word that has more than one meaning or sense

Examples

a polysemous word in English
In English, many common terms are polysemous words, making the language rich but sometimes confusing.
Linguists often study how context determines which meaning of a polysemous word is intended.
The word 'bank' is a classic example of a polysemous word because it can mean the side of a river or a financial institution.
