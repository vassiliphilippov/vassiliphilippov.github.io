electron lens

noun
device focusing electron beams
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛk.trɑn lɛnz / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛk.trɒn lɛnz / (BrE)
An electronic device that uses electric or magnetic fields to focus a beam of electrons, typically used in electron microscopes and particle accelerators.
