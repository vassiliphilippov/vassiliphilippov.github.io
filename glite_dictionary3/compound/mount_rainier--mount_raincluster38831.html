Mount Rainier

proper noun
a high mountain peak in Washington, USA
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ɹeɪˈnɪɹ / (AmE), / maʊnt reɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
a mountain peak in central Washington, USA, and the highest point in the Cascade Range, reaching 14,410 feet (4,392 meters) in elevation
