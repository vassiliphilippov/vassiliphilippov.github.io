Tribes of Israel

noun
twelve kin groups of ancient Israel
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪbz əv ˈɪzriəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪbz əv ˈɪzriəl / (BrE)
the twelve ancient kin groups of Israel, each traditionally descended from one of the twelve sons of Jacob, considered the founders of the ancient Israelite people

Examples

the Tribes of Israel in Canaan
Archaeologists have studied how the Tribes of Israel settled in different regions of Canaan.
