racial tension

noun
hostility or conflict between races
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪʃəl ˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəl ˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
A state of hostility, conflict, or strained relations between people or groups of different races.

Examples
