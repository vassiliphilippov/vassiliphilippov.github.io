club sandwich

noun
triple-decker sandwich with poultry and bacon
Pronunciation: / klʌb ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / klʌb ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A sandwich consisting of three slices of usually toasted bread layered with cooked poultry, bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise.

Examples

classic club sandwich layers
He prepared a homemade club sandwich with toasted bread and layers of ham and cheese.
She ordered a club sandwich and a glass of iced tea for lunch.
The club sandwich came stacked high with turkey, bacon, and fresh lettuce.
