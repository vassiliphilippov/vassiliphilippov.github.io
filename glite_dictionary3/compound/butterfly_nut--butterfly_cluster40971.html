butterfly nut

noun
nut with winglike projections; turned by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ nʌt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ nʌt / (BrE)
A nut with two flat, winglike projections, designed to be turned easily by hand without tools.

Examples

tighten a butterfly nut
He secured the metal frame with a butterfly nut to make assembly easier.
The lamp's shade can be quickly removed thanks to the butterfly nut fastening it.
You don't need a wrench; just tighten the butterfly nut by hand.
