gluteus minimus

noun
smallest gluteal muscle
Pronunciation: / ɡluˈtiəs ˈmɪnɪməs / (AmE), / ɡluːˈtiːəs ˈmɪnɪməs / (BrE)
the smallest and innermost of the three gluteal muscles, located in the buttocks
