cottage pie

noun
dish of minced meat topped with mashed potatoes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːt̬ɪdʒ paɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒtɪdʒ paɪ / (BrE)
A traditional dish consisting of minced meat, typically beef, baked in a dish and topped with a layer of mashed potatoes.

Examples

homemade cottage pie
Her grandmother's recipe for cottage pie uses plenty of onions and carrots in the filling.
We had cottage pie for dinner last night, and everyone loved the comforting taste.
You can prepare a cottage pie in advance and simply reheat it before serving.
