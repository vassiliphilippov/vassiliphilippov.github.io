lightly armoured

adjective
having minimal protective armor
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtli ɑrˌmɔrd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtli ˈɑːməd / (BrE)
equipped with armor that is sufficient to protect against small arms fire but not against heavier weapons

Examples

lightly armoured vehicle
Because the patrol cars were lightly armoured, they avoided routes with heavy enemy fire.
Some peacekeeping units operate in lightly armoured vehicles to reduce their intimidating appearance.
The scout vehicle was lightly armoured to enhance mobility in the field.

Related Terms

  • antonym – heavily armoured: equipped with very strong and heavy armor for maximum protection
