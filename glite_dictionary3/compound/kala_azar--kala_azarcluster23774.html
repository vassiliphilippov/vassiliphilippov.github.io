kala azar

noun
tropical disease caused by Leishmania parasites
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːlə əˈzɑr / (AmE), / ˌkɑːlə əˈzɑː / (BrE)
A severe tropical disease caused by protozoa of the genus Leishmania, transmitted by sandflies, characterized by fever, weight loss, and skin sores.
