pachydermatous response

noun
insensitive or thick-skinned reaction
Pronunciation: / ˌpæk.ɪˈdɜːr.mə.təs rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˌpæk.ɪˈdɜː.mə.təs rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
an emotionally insensitive or thick-skinned reaction, showing little or no concern for criticism or feelings

Examples

pachydermatous response to criticism
A pachydermatous response is not always helpful when dealing with sensitive topics.
