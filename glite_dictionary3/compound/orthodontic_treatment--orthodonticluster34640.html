orthodontic treatment

noun
dental treatment to straighten teeth
Pronunciation: / ˌɔr.θoʊˈdɑn.tɪk ˈtriːt.mənt / (AmE), / ˌɔː.θəˈdɒn.tɪk ˈtriːt.mənt / (BrE)
dental treatment that corrects irregularities of the teeth or their alignment, typically using braces or other mechanical devices

Examples
