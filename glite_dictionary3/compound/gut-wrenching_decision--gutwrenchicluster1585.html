gut-wrenching decision

noun
very emotionally difficult choice
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌt ˌrɛntʃɪŋ dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt ˌrɛn.tʃɪŋ dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
