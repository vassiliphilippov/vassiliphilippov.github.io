paternity test

noun
test to determine biological fatherhood
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɜrnəti tɛst / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəti tɛst / (BrE)
a medical test, often using blood or DNA, performed to determine whether a particular man is the biological father of a child

Examples

take a paternity test
After the paternity test, he discovered he was not the child's biological father.
She was anxious while waiting for the paternity test results.
The judge ordered a paternity test to resolve the dispute over child support.
