goose-tansy

noun
a perennial herbaceous plant with silvery leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːs ˌtænzi / (AmE), / ˈɡuːs ˌtænzi / (BrE)
A low-growing perennial plant with leaves that are silvery underneath, found in northern parts of the United States, Europe, and Asia.
