ideal solid

noun
a three-dimensional Platonic solid
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiːəl ˈsɑːlɪd / (AmE), / aɪˈdɪəl ˈsɒlɪd / (BrE)
any of the five three-dimensional shapes whose faces are congruent regular polygons and whose angles are all congruent, known as the Platonic solids

Examples

ideal solid with equal faces
Mathematicians have studied each ideal solid since ancient times for its symmetrical properties.
