straight razor

noun
traditional folding shaving razor
Pronunciation: / streɪt ˈreɪzər / (AmE), / streɪt ˈreɪzə / (BrE)
a razor with a long, straight blade that folds into its handle, used for shaving

Examples

shave with a straight razor
After sharpening the straight razor, she carefully trimmed the customer’s beard.
He inherited his grandfather’s straight razor and learned to shave the old-fashioned way.
Some barbers still prefer using a straight razor for a close, smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cut-throat razor: another term for straight razor, especially in British English
  • antonym – safety razor: a razor with a protective device positioned between the edge of the blade and the skin
