meeting of minds

noun
full agreement or understanding
Pronunciation: / ˈmit̬ɪŋ əv ˈmaɪndz / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪŋ əv ˈmaɪndz / (BrE)
a situation in which people fully agree with each other, typically leading to cooperation or mutual understanding

Examples

a meeting of minds on ideas
A true partnership is built on a meeting of minds and shared goals.
Negotiations succeeded because there was a meeting of minds between the two companies.
The project failed due to a lack of a meeting of minds among the team leaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agreement: the state of sharing the same opinion; harmony
  • antonym – disagreement: lack of consensus or harmony
