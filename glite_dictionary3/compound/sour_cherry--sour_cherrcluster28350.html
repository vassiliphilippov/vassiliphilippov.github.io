sour cherry

noun
deep red colour
Pronunciation: / saʊə ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / saʊə ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A deep, vivid red colour resembling the skin or flesh of ripe sour cherries, used to describe paints, fabrics, cosmetics, and other coloured materials.

Examples

sour cherry color
She bought a pair of heels in sour cherry to wear with the navy suit.
The designer specified a sour cherry fabric for the autumn collection's standout coat.
For the photoshoot, the stylist chose a matte sour cherry lipstick to create a bold look.
