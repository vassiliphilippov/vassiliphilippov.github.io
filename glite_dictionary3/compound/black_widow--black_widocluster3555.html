black widow

noun
venomous spider with red hourglass marking
Pronunciation: / ˌblæk ˈwɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌblæk ˈwɪdəʊ / (BrE)
a venomous spider found in the Americas, the female of which is black with a red, hourglass-shaped marking on the underside of the abdomen

Examples

female black widow spider
A distinctive red hourglass shape helps identify the female black widow.
The bite of a black widow can be dangerous, especially to small children and the elderly.
We found a black widow in the garage and called pest control.
