smallmouth bass

noun
freshwater North American game fish
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔlˌmaʊθ bæs / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlˌmaʊθ bæs / (BrE)
a North American freshwater fish (Micropterus dolomieu) of the sunfish family, distinguished by its relatively small mouth and valued as a game fish.

Examples

catch a smallmouth bass
Smallmouth bass are popular among anglers because they put up a good fight.
He caught a large smallmouth bass while fishing in the lake.
The river is known for its abundant smallmouth bass population.
