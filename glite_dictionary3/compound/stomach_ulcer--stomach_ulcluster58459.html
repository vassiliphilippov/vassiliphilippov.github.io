stomach ulcer

noun
sore on the stomach lining
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmək ˈʌlsər / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək ˈʌlsə / (BrE)
A sore that develops on the lining of the stomach, often causing pain or discomfort.

Examples

stomach ulcer pain
After months of discomfort, he finally discovered it was a stomach ulcer causing his pain.
Doctors warned that stress and certain medications can contribute to the development of a stomach ulcer.
She needed to change her diet to help her stomach ulcer heal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gastric ulcer: ulcer specifically located in the stomach
  • synonym – peptic ulcer: ulcer occurring in the stomach or upper part of the small intestine
