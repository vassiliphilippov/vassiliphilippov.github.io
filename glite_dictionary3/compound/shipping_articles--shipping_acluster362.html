shipping articles

noun
contract between ship's crew and captain
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpɪŋ ˈɑrtɪkəlz / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpɪŋ ˈɑːtɪklz / (BrE)
a formal contract detailing the agreement between the crew and the captain of a ship, specifying the conditions of service, wages, and duties during a voyage

Examples

sign shipping articles
Before boarding, every sailor had to sign the shipping articles in the captain's cabin.
If a dispute arose, the shipping articles served as the guiding legal document.
The shipping articles clearly outlined each crew member's duties and share of any profits.
