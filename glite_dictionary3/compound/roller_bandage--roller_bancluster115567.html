roller bandage

noun
a strip of rolled fabric used as a bandage
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
a bandage made of a strip of sterile fabric rolled into a cylinder, used to cover wounds or support injured body parts

Examples

A roller bandage is ideal for wrapping sprained ankles because it provides uniform pressure.
