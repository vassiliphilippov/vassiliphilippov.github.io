auditory image

noun
mental representation of a sound
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdəˌtɔri ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪtəri ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a mental representation of a sound as if heard in the mind, similar to actual auditory perception

Examples
