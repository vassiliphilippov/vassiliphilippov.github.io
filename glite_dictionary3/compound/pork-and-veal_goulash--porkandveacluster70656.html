pork-and-veal goulash

noun
a stew of pork and veal with sauerkraut
Pronunciation: / pɔrk ənd vil ɡuˈlɑʃ / (AmE), / pɔːk ənd viːl ɡuːˈlæʃ / (BrE)
A stew made from pork and veal, prepared with sauerkraut and caraway seeds, and typically served with sour cream.

Examples

hearty pork-and-veal goulash
He prepared a hearty pork-and-veal goulash topped with a spoonful of sour cream.
The restaurant's specialty is pork-and-veal goulash served with rustic bread.
We enjoyed a traditional pork-and-veal goulash with plenty of caraway flavor.
