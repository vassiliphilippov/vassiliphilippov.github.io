cow brush

noun
grooming device for cows
Pronunciation: / kaʊ brʌʃ / (AmE), / kaʊ brʌʃ / (BrE)
a stationary or rotating device with brushes, used by cows to scratch and groom themselves

Examples

cow brush in barn
Cows often gather around the cow brush for a good scratch and grooming.
The dairy farm installed a cow brush in the barn to help keep the cows clean.
Using a cow brush can improve animal welfare and increase milk production.
