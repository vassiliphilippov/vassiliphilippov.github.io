King of the Jungle

noun
title for dominant big cats, esp. lions
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ əv ðə ˈdʒʌŋɡəl / (AmE), / kɪŋ əv ðə ˈdʒʌŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
an informal title given to lions or tigers, symbolizing their dominance and strength, especially in their natural habitat

Examples

King of the Jungle lion
After winning the championship, the boxer declared himself the King of the Jungle.
Many children admire the King of the Jungle for its courage and strength.
The lion is often called the King of the Jungle because of its power and majesty.
