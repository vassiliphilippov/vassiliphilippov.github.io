civet cat

noun
cat-like mammal producing musk
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvət kæt / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪt kæt / (BrE)
A small, cat-like mammal found in Asia and Africa, known for secreting a musky substance used in perfumes.

Examples

wild civet cat
Farmers sometimes complain that the civet cat raids their fruit crops at night.
Researchers studied the habits of the civet cat in its natural forest environment.
The civet cat is prized for the musky scent it produces, often used in high-end perfumes.
