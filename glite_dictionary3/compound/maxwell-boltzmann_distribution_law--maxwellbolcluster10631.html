Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution law

noun
law describing molecular energy distribution in gases
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksˌwɛl ˈbɔlt.smən dɪˌstrɪb.juˈʃən lɔ / (AmE), / ˈmæks.wɛl ˈbɒlts.mən dɪ.strɪˌbjuː.ʃən lɔː / (BrE)
a law in physics that describes how the energies of molecules in a gas are distributed when the gas is in thermal equilibrium
