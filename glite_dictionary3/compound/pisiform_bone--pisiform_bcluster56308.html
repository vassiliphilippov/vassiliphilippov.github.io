pisiform bone

noun
a small bone in the wrist joint
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪsɪˌfɔrm boʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɪsɪfɔːm bəʊn / (BrE)
a small, pea-shaped bone in the wrist that articulates only with the triquetral bone

Examples

pisiform bone in wrist
An X-ray revealed a fracture in the pisiform bone of his wrist.
Pain over the pisiform bone can result from repetitive wrist movements.
The pisiform bone is located on the ulnar side of the carpal region.

Related Terms

  • synonym – triquetral bone: a carpal bone in the wrist that articulates with the pisiform
