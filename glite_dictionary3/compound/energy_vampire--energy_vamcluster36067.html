energy vampire

noun
person who drains others' positivity or energy
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnɚdʒi ˈvæmpaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛnədʒi ˈvæmpaɪə / (BrE)
a person who habitually drains other people's emotional energy or positivity, often through negative or demanding behavior

Examples

office energy vampire
After spending an hour with the energy vampire at work, I felt completely exhausted.
Many people don't realize they are behaving like an energy vampire until someone points it out.
You should set boundaries with energy vampires to protect your own well-being.
