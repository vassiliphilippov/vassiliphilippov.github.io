Secretary of Education

noun
head of the Education Department (government)
Pronunciation: / sɛk.rəˌtɛɹ.i əv ˌɛdʒ.əˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.rə.təri əv ˌɛd.jʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
