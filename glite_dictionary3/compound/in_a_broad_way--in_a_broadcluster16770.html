in a broad way

adverb
generally; non-specifically
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə brɔd weɪ / (AmE), / ɪn ə brɔːd weɪ / (BrE)
In a general or non-specific manner; without focusing on details.

Examples

explain in a broad way
It's important to understand the topic in a broad way before diving into the technical aspects.
The speaker explained the new policy in a broad way without giving the finer details.
When describing her research, she often talks in a broad way before discussing specifics.

Related Terms

  • synonym – broadly: in a wide or general manner
  • antonym – specifically: in a detailed and precise manner
