el stupido

noun
humorous term for foolishness
Pronunciation: / ɛl stuˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / ɛl stjuːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
A humorous or self-deprecating term for someone acting foolish or making a silly mistake.

Examples

call yourself el stupido
After tripping over his own shoelaces, he laughed and called himself el stupido.
He forgot where he parked his car again and muttered, "Nice going, el stupido."
She texted the wrong person and joked, "Well done, el stupido strikes again!"
