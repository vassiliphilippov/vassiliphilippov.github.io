tonne-kilometre

noun
freight measurement unit (one tonne over one kilometre)
Pronunciation: / tʌn kɪˈlɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / tʌn kɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
A unit of measurement for freight transport, representing the transport of one tonne of goods over a distance of one kilometre.

Examples

tonne-kilometre of freight
A railway company reported an increase in tonne-kilometres due to longer haul distances.
In logistics, calculating tonne-kilometres helps track environmental impact per transport mode.
The total freight moved last year was measured in tonne-kilometres to compare efficiency.
