1st class

noun
priority mail category for letters and sealed packages
Pronunciation: / fɜrst klæs / (AmE), / fɜːst klɑːs / (BrE)
the category of mail that includes letters, postcards, and sealed packages, typically handled with priority service

Examples

1st class mail delivery
A letter sent by 1st class usually arrives within one or two business days.
Please send the documents by 1st class to ensure they arrive quickly.
She paid extra to mail the package 1st class rather than standard.
