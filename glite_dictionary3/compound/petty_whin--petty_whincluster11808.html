petty whin

noun
prickly yellow-flowered moor shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛti wɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɛti wɪn / (BrE)
A small, prickly shrub with yellow flowers, found on moors in New England and Europe.

Examples
