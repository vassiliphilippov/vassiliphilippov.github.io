wild oats

noun
plant with yellow, bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / waɪld oʊts / (AmE), / waɪld əʊts / (BrE)
any of various perennial plants of the genus Uvularia, having yellowish, drooping, bell-shaped flowers, found in North America.
