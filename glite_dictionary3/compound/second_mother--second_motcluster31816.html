second mother

noun
a woman acting as another mother
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd ˈmʌðər / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
a woman who fills a motherly role in someone's life, especially in addition to or instead of one's biological mother

Examples

second mother to a child
After her grandmother moved in, she quickly became a second mother to the children.
He often called his foster mom his second mother when introducing her to friends.
My aunt has always been a second mother to me, supporting me through every challenge.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mother figure: a woman who is regarded as a mother, or who acts as a mother to someone other than her child
