thrill of the hunt

noun
excitement from pursuing or searching
Pronunciation: / θɹɪl əv ðə hʌnt / (AmE), / θɹɪl əv ðə hʌnt / (BrE)
the excitement or pleasure derived from pursuing or searching for something, whether literally during hunting or metaphorically in other activities.

Examples

the thrill of the hunt in collecting
Many collectors are motivated by the thrill of the hunt rather than the items themselves.
She experiences the thrill of the hunt whenever she searches for rare antiques.
The thrill of the hunt is what keeps him engaged in competitive sports.

Related Terms

  • synonym – thrill: a sudden feeling of excitement and pleasure
