Scottish deerhound

noun
breed of large hunting dog from Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˈskɒtɪʃ ˈdɪrˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈskɒtɪʃ ˈdɪəhɑʊnd / (BrE)
a very large and tall breed of rough-coated dog developed in Scotland for hunting deer; known as the royal dog of Scotland

Examples

a Scottish deerhound dog
