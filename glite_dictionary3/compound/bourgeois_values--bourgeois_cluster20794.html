bourgeois values

noun
middle-class attitudes and beliefs
Pronunciation: / bʊrˈʒwɑː væl.juz / (AmE), / ˌbʊəʒˈwɑː ˈvæl.juːz / (BrE)
The beliefs and attitudes commonly associated with the middle class, particularly those emphasizing material success, social respectability, and conventional standards.

Examples

bourgeois values of respectability
Her parents held strongly to traditional bourgeois values, expecting financial stability and social conformity.
The artist rejected bourgeois values in favor of a more bohemian lifestyle.
The novel offers a biting critique of bourgeois values and the pursuit of material comfort.
