pine siskin

noun
small North American finch
Pronunciation: / paɪn ˈsɪskɪn / (AmE), / paɪn ˈsɪskɪn / (BrE)
a small finch native to North America, characterized by brown-streaked plumage and a faint touch of yellow on the wings, commonly found in coniferous forests
