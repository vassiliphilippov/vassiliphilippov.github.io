far post

noun
goalpost farthest from the player
Pronunciation: / fɑr poʊst / (AmE), / fɑː pəʊst / (BrE)
the goalpost that is farthest from the player on the field, especially when referring to aiming or positioning in sports such as football (soccer) or hockey

Examples

Players often try to cross the ball to a teammate waiting at the far post.
