juggling act

noun
balancing multiple activities or responsibilities
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡlɪŋ ækt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡlɪŋ ækt / (BrE)
a situation in which someone must manage several different responsibilities or activities at the same time, often with difficulty

Examples

a juggling act at work
After her promotion, her life turned into a complicated juggling act of meetings and family time.
Juggling full-time work and evening classes has become a daily juggling act for Mia.
Raising twins while running a business is a constant juggling act for him.
