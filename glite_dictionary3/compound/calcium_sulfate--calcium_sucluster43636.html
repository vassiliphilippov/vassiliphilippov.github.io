calcium sulfate

noun
a white inorganic salt, CaSO₄
Pronunciation: / ˈkælsiəm ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkalsɪəm ˈsʌlfeɪt / (BrE)
a white, odorless, inorganic salt (CaSO₄) used in construction, food, and medicine; occurs naturally as gypsum and anhydrite
