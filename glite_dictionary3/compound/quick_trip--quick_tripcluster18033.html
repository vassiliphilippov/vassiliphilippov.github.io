quick trip

noun
brief or fast journey
Pronunciation: / kwɪk trɪp / (AmE), / kwɪk trɪp / (BrE)
a journey to a place and back that is done in a short time

Examples

She made a quick trip to the grocery store before dinner.
We're planning a quick trip to Paris for the weekend.
