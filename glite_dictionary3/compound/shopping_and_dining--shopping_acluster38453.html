shopping and dining

noun
buying goods and eating out
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpɪŋ ənd ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ ənd ˈdaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the activities of buying goods and eating meals at restaurants or cafes, often as a combined leisure activity
