shade of red

noun
degree of facial redness due to embarrassment
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪd əv rɛd / (AmE), / ʃeɪd əv rɛd / (BrE)
a figurative expression referring to the degree of embarrassment or blushing shown, typically as the face's redness increases

Examples

face turns shade of red
He went through several shades of red as everyone sang 'Happy Birthday' to him.
Her face shifted to a lighter shade of red as she accepted the award.
When she realized her mistake, she turned a deep shade of red in front of the entire class.

  • synonym – blush: a reddening of the face from embarrassment or shame
