chicken roundworm

noun
intestinal parasitic worm of chickens
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən ˈraʊn(d)ˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˈraʊndˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a species of parasitic roundworm (Ascaridia galli) that infects the intestines of domestic chickens and other fowl

Examples

chicken roundworm infection
Improper cleaning of coops increases the risk of chicken roundworm spreads among hens.
The presence of chicken roundworm can significantly reduce egg production in a flock.
Veterinarians recommend regular deworming to protect poultry from chicken roundworm infections.
