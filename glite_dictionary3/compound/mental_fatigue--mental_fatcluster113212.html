mental fatigue

noun
exhaustion from prolonged mental effort
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.t̬l fəˈtiːɡ / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl fəˈtiːɡ / (BrE)
a state of exhaustion caused by prolonged mental effort or stress

Examples

severe mental fatigue

Chronic mental fatigue can affect your concentration and memory.
Regular breaks are important to prevent mental fatigue during long workdays.
