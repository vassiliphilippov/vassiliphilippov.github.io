Taiwanese language

noun
Chinese language variety spoken in Taiwan and Fujian
Pronunciation: / ˌtaɪwəˈniːz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌtaɪwəˈniːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Sinitic language spoken mainly in Taiwan and Fukien province, also known as Hokkien or Minnan.
