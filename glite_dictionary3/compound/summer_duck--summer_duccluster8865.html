summer duck

noun
a showy North American duck species
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɚ dʌk / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə dʌk / (BrE)
A showy North American duck (the wood duck, Aix sponsa) that nests in hollow trees, noted for its colorful plumage.

Examples
