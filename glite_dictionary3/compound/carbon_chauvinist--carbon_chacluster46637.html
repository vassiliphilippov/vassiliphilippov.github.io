carbon chauvinist

noun
person favoring fossil fuels over renewables
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.bən ˈʃoʊ.və.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.bən ˈʃəʊ.vɪ.nɪst / (BrE)
