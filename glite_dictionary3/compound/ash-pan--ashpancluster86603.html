ash-pan

noun
tray beneath grate for ashes
Pronunciation: / ˈæʃˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈæʃ.pæn / (BrE)
A metal tray or receptacle located beneath a grate or stove to collect and allow removal of ashes.

Examples

ash-pan under stove
After the fire was out, she emptied the ash-pan before setting a new one.
Always check the ash-pan weekly to prevent buildup and ensure safe operation.
The old stove's ash-pan was rusted and needed to be replaced.
