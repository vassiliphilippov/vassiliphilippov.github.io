the other end of the country

noun
the farthest place within a country
Pronunciation: / ði ˈʌðɚ ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / ði ˈʌðə ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
