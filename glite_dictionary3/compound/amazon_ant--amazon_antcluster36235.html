Amazon ant

noun
slave-making ant species
Pronunciation: / ˈæməzɑn ænt / (AmE), / ˈaməzən ant / (BrE)
a small reddish ant of the genus Polyergus, known for raiding nests of other ants and enslaving their workers

Examples

Amazon ant raid
Scientists observed a nest of Amazon ants preparing for a raid on a nearby Formica colony.
The Amazon ant is notorious for attacking other ant colonies and taking their pupae as slaves.
Unlike most ants, the Amazon ant relies entirely on captured workers for basic tasks.
