Spielmeyer-Vogt disease

noun
rare inherited brain disorder in children
Pronunciation: / ˌspiːlmaɪər ˈfɔːkt dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˌspiːlmaɪə ˈvɒkt dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
A rare inherited disorder of lipid metabolism that begins in early childhood and causes progressive blindness, dementia, and early death.
