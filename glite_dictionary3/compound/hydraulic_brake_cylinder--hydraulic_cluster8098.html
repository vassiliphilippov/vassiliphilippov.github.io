hydraulic brake cylinder

noun
cylinder part in hydraulic brake system
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɑlɪk breɪk ˈsɪlɪndər / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒlɪk breɪk ˈsɪlɪndə / (BrE)
a cylinder in a hydraulic braking system containing brake fluid that is compressed by a piston to apply braking force
