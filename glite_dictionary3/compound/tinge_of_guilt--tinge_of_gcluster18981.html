tinge of guilt

noun
a slight feeling of guilt
Pronunciation: / tɪndʒ əv ɡɪlt / (AmE), / tɪndʒ əv ɡɪlt / (BrE)
a slight amount or trace of guilt felt in a particular situation

Examples

feel a tinge of guilt
A tinge of guilt crept into her voice as she explained why she hadn't called.
She couldn't help but feel a tinge of guilt after canceling their plans last minute.
Whenever he passed the old bookstore, he felt a tinge of guilt for stealing a comic book as a child.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pang of guilt: a sudden sharp feeling of guilt
  • degree higher – remorse: deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
  • degree higher – shame: a painful feeling of humiliation or distress caused by guilt
