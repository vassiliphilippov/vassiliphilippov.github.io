paper currency

noun
money in printed note form
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpɚ ˈkɜːrənsi / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˈkʌrənsi / (BrE)
money in the form of printed notes, issued by a government or central bank, and used as a substitute for coins
