boat race

noun
competition involving rowing or driving boats
Pronunciation: / boʊt reɪs / (AmE), / bəʊt reɪs / (BrE)
a sporting competition in which people row or drive boats against each other to see who is fastest

Examples

university boat race
He trained for months to compete in the university's famous boat race.
Spectators lined the riverbanks to watch the exciting boat race.
The annual boat race attracted teams from all over the country.
