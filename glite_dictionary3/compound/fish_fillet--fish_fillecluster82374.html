fish fillet

noun
boneless slice of fish
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ fɪˈleɪ / (AmE), / fɪʃ ˈfɪlɪt / (BrE)
A slice of fish that has had the bones removed, often cut parallel to the spine.

Examples

grilled fish fillet
I bought a fresh fish fillet from the seafood market for dinner.
She prefers to eat fish fillet because it doesn’t have any bones.
The chef prepared a grilled fish fillet with a lemon butter sauce.
