false lupine

noun
western US bushy herb with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈluːpaɪn / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈluːpaɪn / (BrE)
A bushy herb of the western United States with yellow, pea-like flowers, resembling true lupine plants.
