amphibious vehicle

noun
vehicle operating on land and water
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbiəs ˈviɪkəl / (AmE), / amˈfɪbɪəs ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
A vehicle designed to operate both on land and in water, typically with a flat bottom and motorized propulsion.

Examples

amphibious vehicle crosses river
Engineers are developing a new amphibious vehicle for rescue operations in disaster-prone areas.
The army deployed an amphibious vehicle to cross the flooded river.
Tourists enjoyed a ride in the amphibious vehicle that toured both the city streets and the harbor.
