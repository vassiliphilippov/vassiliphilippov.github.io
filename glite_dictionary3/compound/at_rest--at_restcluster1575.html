at rest

adjective
in a state of stillness or sleep
Pronunciation: / æt rɛst /; / ət rɛst / (AmE), / æt rɛst /; / ət rɛst / (BrE)
in a state of quiet, stillness, calm, or not moving; inactive or at peace
