seed beetle

noun
beetle that infests legume seeds
Pronunciation: / sid ˈbit̬l̩ / (AmE), / siːd ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a small beetle, often of the family Bruchidae, that infests and feeds on the seeds of legumes

Examples

seed beetle infestation
Farmers implement strict controls to prevent seed beetle infestations in their fields.
Researchers studied how the seed beetle population affected crop yields.
The seed beetle can cause significant damage to stored beans and peas.
