sabine pine

noun
pine tree native to southwestern California
Pronunciation: / səˈbiːn paɪn / (AmE), / səˈbiːn paɪn / (BrE)
A medium-sized pine tree native to southwestern California, with five needles per cluster and long cylindrical cones.
