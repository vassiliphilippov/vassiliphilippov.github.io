list price

noun
stated catalogue price before discounts
Pronunciation: / lɪst praɪs / (AmE), / lɪst praɪs / (BrE)
the price of a product as stated in a catalogue or price list, before any discounts are applied

Examples

list price before discount
Most stores offer significant discounts off the list price during sales.
The list price of this laptop is $1200, but you can often find it for less.
The car's list price was much higher than what I actually paid.
