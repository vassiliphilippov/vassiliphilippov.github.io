flag smut fungus

noun
fungus causing cereal disease with black spores
Pronunciation: / flæɡ smʌt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / flæɡ smʌt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungal disease chiefly affecting cereals and grasses, in which sori form in the plant tissue and release black masses of spores

Examples

flag smut fungus infects wheat
Researchers developed resistant varieties to protect fields from flag smut fungus.
Signs of flag smut fungus include dark streaks on leaves and stems.
The flag smut fungus can devastate wheat crops if not controlled.
