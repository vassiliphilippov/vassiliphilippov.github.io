kilowatt hour

noun
unit of energy (1,000 watts for one hour)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪl.oʊˌwɑt ˌaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɪl.əwɒt ˌaʊə / (BrE)
a unit of energy equal to the work done by a power of 1,000 watts operating for one hour

Examples
