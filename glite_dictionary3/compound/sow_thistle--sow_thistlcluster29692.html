sow thistle

noun
a coarse yellow-flowered weed (Sonchus genus)
Pronunciation: / soʊ ˈθɪsl̩ / (AmE), / səʊ ˈθɪsl̩ / (BrE)
any of several coarse, prickly-leaved plants of the genus Sonchus, with yellow flowers and milky sap, often considered weeds in cultivated soil

Examples

sow thistle weed
Gardeners often remove sow thistle to keep their vegetable beds clear of weeds.
The field was overrun with sow thistle after the heavy rains.
Wild rabbits were nibbling on the leaves of the sow thistle near the hedgerow.
