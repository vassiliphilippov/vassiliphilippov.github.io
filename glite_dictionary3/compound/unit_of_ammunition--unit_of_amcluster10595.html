unit of ammunition

noun
single charge of ammunition
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnɪt əv ˌæməˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈjuːnɪt əv ˌaməˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
A single charge of ammunition, such as a cartridge or shell, prepared for firing one shot.
