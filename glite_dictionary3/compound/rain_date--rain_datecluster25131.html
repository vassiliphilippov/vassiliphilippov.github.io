rain date

noun
alternative date for an outdoor event
Pronunciation: / reɪn deɪt / (AmE), / reɪn deɪt / (BrE)
an alternative date scheduled for an outdoor event, to be used if the original date is cancelled due to rain

Examples

rain date for the event
If the parade is cancelled, the rain date will be next Saturday.
Please mark your calendar for both the event and its rain date, just in case of bad weather.
They rescheduled the outdoor wedding to the rain date after thunderstorms were predicted.
