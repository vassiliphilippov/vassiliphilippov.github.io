molasses cookie

noun
spicy, molasses-sweetened cookie
Pronunciation: / məˈlæsəz ˈkʊki / (AmE), / məˈlæsɪz ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A soft, spicy cookie sweetened in part with molasses, often flavored with ginger, cinnamon, and cloves.

fresh molasses cookie
