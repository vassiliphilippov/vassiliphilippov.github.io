Shetland pony

noun
small, hardy breed of pony
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛt.lənd ˈpoʊ.ni / (AmE), / ˈʃɛt.lənd ˈpəʊ.ni / (BrE)
a breed of very small, sturdy pony with a long shaggy mane and tail, originally from the Shetland Islands of Scotland

Examples

Shetland pony with thick coat
A Shetland pony can easily withstand harsh weather due to its thick coat.
