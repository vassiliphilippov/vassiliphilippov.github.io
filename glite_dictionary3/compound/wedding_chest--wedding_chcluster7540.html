wedding chest

noun
chest for storing trousseau before marriage
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛdɪŋ tʃɛst / (AmE), / ˈwɛdɪŋ tʃɛst / (BrE)
a chest used to store clothing and household goods collected in anticipation of marriage, often forming part of a bride's trousseau

Examples

antique wedding chest
At the museum, we admired a 17th-century Italian wedding chest adorned with gold leaf and painted scenes.
She filled her wedding chest with linens, dresses, and cherished keepsakes before the big day.
The antique wedding chest was intricately carved and had been in her family for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dowry: property or money brought by a bride to her husband on marriage
  • synonym – hope chest: a chest for storing a woman's clothing and household articles in preparation for marriage
