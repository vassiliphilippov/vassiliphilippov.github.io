mystery play

noun
medieval biblical drama
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstəri pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɪstəri pleɪ / (BrE)
a type of medieval drama based on biblical stories, especially episodes from the life of Christ, performed in churches or public places
