anterior cerebral vein

noun
a vein in the brain draining into the basal vein
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪriɚ səˈribəl veɪn / (AmE), / ænˈtɪəriə səˈriːbəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein running alongside the anterior cerebral artery and draining blood from the front part of the brain into the basal vein

Examples

anterior cerebral vein drains brain
Blood from the frontal lobes drains into the anterior cerebral vein.
During surgery, the anterior cerebral vein must be identified to prevent complications.
The MRI revealed a blockage in the anterior cerebral vein, affecting normal blood flow.
