illegal smuggling

noun
unlawful transportation of goods or people
Pronunciation: / ɪˈliɡəl ˈsmʌɡlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈliːɡəl ˈsmʌɡlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of transporting goods or people in violation of the law, especially across borders

Examples

illegal smuggling of drugs
Authorities set up roadblocks to stop illegal smuggling along the border.
He was convicted for his role in illegal smuggling of counterfeit goods.
The new law aims to reduce illegal smuggling of endangered animals.
