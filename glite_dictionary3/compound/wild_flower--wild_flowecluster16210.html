wild flower

noun
uncultivated flowering plant
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈflaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / waɪld ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A flowering plant that grows naturally in the wild, rather than being cultivated or planted by humans.

Examples

wild flower meadow
She picked a bouquet of wild flowers during her walk through the forest.
Some people prefer wild flowers to cultivated garden varieties because of their natural charm.
The meadow was covered in beautiful wild flowers of every color.
