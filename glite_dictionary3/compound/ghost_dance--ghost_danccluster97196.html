ghost dance

noun
Native American religious ritual dance
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊst dæns / (AmE), / ɡəʊst dɑːns / (BrE)
A religious dance originally practiced by some Native American tribes in the late 19th century, believed to bring about communication with the spirits of the dead and hope for renewal.
