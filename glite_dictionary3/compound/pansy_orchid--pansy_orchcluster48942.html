pansy orchid

noun
orchid of genus Miltonia with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpænzi ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈpænzi ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
Any of several species of orchids of the genus Miltonia, known for their solitary or loosely clustered, showy, broad-spreading flowers resembling pansies.

Examples

pansy orchid flower display
Many Miltonia species are popularly known as pansy orchids because of their resemblance to garden pansies.

The pansy orchid is prized by gardeners for its vibrant and colorful blooms.
