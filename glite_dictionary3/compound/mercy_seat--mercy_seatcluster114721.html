mercy seat

noun
the throne of God; place of divine presence
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːsi siːt / (AmE), / ˈmɜːsi siːt / (BrE)
the golden lid of the Ark of the Covenant in the Hebrew Bible, regarded as the place where God's presence appeared and atonement was made; also seen as the symbolic throne of God in Judaism and Christianity
