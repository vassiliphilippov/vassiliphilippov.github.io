phylum Cnidaria

noun
group of aquatic animals such as jellyfish and corals
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪləm naɪˈdɛriə / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪləm nɪˈdɪəriə / (BrE)
A large group of aquatic animals that includes hydras, jellyfishes, sea anemones, and corals, characterized by stinging cells called cnidocytes.
