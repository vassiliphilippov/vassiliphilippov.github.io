sheep-tick

noun
parasitic insect on sheep and cattle
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːp tɪk /; / ʃip tɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃiːp tɪk /; / ʃiːp tɪk / (BrE)
a small parasite (fly or tick) that feeds on the blood of sheep and can sometimes spread diseases
