litres per hundred kilometres

noun
fuel consumption measure (litres/100 km)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtərz pɚ ˈhʌndrəd kɪˈlɑmɪtɚz / (AmE), / ˈliːtəz pə ˈhʌndrəd kɪˈlɒmɪtəz / (BrE)
a measure of a vehicle's fuel consumption, equal to the number of litres of fuel used to travel one hundred kilometres

Examples

litres per hundred kilometres for cars
Many European countries report fuel consumption in litres per hundred kilometres rather than miles per gallon.
New models boast lower litres per hundred kilometres, making them more economical for drivers.
