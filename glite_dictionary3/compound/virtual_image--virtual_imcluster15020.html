virtual image

noun
computer-rendered digital image
Pronunciation: / \u02c8v\u025dt\u0283u\u0259l \u02c8\u026am\u026ad\u0292 / (AmE), / \u02c8v\u025c\u02d0tj\u028a\u0259l \u02c8\u026am\u026ad\u0292 / (BrE)
A digital image produced by computer graphics or rendering inside a virtual environment or simulation rather than captured by a physical camera.

Examples

computer virtual image
The dataset used thousands of virtual image renders to train the autonomous vehicle's vision system.
