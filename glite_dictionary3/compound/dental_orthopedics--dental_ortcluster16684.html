dental orthopedics

noun
branch of dentistry correcting dental irregularities
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəl ˌɔrθəˈpidɪks / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl ˌɔːθəʊˈpiːdɪks / (BrE)
the branch of dentistry concerned with the prevention or correction of irregularities of the teeth and jaws
