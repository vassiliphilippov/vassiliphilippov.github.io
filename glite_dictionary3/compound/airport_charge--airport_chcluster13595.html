airport charge

noun
fee for airport aircraft operations and facilities
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpɔrt tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈeəpɔːt tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
A fee paid for the take-off, landing, parking of aircraft, and use of passenger facilities at an airport.

Examples

airport charge for landing
Negotiations over the new airport charge structure took several months to conclude.
Rising airport charges have contributed to increased airfares for travelers.
