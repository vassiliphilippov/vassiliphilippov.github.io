low St Andrew's cross

noun
low shrubby plant with cross-shaped yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˌsɛnt ˈændruːz krɑs / (AmE), / ləʊ ˌsənt ˈændruːz krɒs / (BrE)
