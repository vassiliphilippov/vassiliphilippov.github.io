burlesque show

noun
theatrical entertainment with comic and provocative acts
Pronunciation: / bɜːrˈlɛsk ʃoʊ / (AmE), / bɜːˈlɛsk ʃəʊ / (BrE)
A theatrical performance featuring comic and often provocative acts, typically including striptease and parody.

classic burlesque show
She starred in a burlesque show that drew large crowds every weekend.
The city is famous for its lively burlesque shows and vibrant nightlife.
