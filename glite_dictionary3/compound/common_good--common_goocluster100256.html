common good

noun
the benefit of the community as a whole
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ɡʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ɡʊd / (BrE)
the benefit or interests of all members within a community or society collectively

Examples

The new policy was designed to serve the common good rather than individual interests.
