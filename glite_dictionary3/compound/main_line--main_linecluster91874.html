main line

noun
principal route of a transportation system
Pronunciation: / meɪn laɪn / (AmE), / meɪn laɪn / (BrE)
the principal route or railway of a transportation system, typically the most important or heavily used line.

Examples

train main line
Engineers worked overnight to repair damage to the main line after the storm.
Freight traffic is generally restricted to the main line due to its greater capacity.
The new trains will run exclusively on the main line between the two cities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – branch line: a secondary railway line diverging from a main line
  • synonym – route: a way or course taken in getting from a starting point to a destination
