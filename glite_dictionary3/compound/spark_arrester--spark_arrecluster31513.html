spark arrester

noun
device to prevent escape of sparks
Pronunciation: / spɑrk əˈrɛstɚ / (AmE), / spɑːk əˈrɛstə / (BrE)
A device, typically a wire mesh, fitted to an open fireplace or smokestack to prevent the escape of sparks and reduce the risk of fire.

Examples

spark arrester on chimney
A proper spark arrester can significantly reduce the risk of wildfires caused by machinery.
The old train was fitted with a spark arrester to prevent accidental fires along the tracks.
You should make sure the spark arrester on your chimney is cleaned regularly for safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fireguard: screen placed in front of a fireplace to prevent sparks from escaping
