Old World buffalo

noun
large African or Asian wild bovine (e.g., water or Cape buffalo)
Pronunciation: / oʊld wɜrld ˈbʌfəloʊ / (AmE), / əʊld wɜːld ˈbʌfələʊ / (BrE)
