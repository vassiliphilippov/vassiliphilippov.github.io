iron heel

noun
instrument of torture for foot and leg
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪɚn hiːl / (AmE), / ˈaɪən hiːl / (BrE)
An instrument of torture designed to heat or crush the foot and leg, often made of iron.

Examples

the iron heel torture device
Historical accounts describe the iron heel as a device used to inflict brutal punishment on captives.
