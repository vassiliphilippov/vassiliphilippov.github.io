print media

noun
newspapers and magazines in printed form
Pronunciation: / prɪnt ˈmidiə / (AmE), / prɪnt ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
Newspapers, magazines, and other publications that are printed on paper, as opposed to being published electronically.
