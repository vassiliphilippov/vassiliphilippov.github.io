great blue shark

noun
a large pelagic shark with blue coloring
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt blu ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ɡreɪt bluː ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a large, slender, pelagic shark with a blue back and white belly, found in oceans worldwide and considered dangerous to humans, especially after shipwrecks

Examples
