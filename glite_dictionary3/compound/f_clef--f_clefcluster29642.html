F clef

noun
musical clef marking fourth line as F below middle C
Pronunciation: / ɛf klɛf / (AmE), / ɛf klɛf / (BrE)
a clef in musical notation that designates the fourth line of the staff as the F below middle C, commonly used for bass voices and instruments
