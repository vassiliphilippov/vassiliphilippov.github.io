retractable dome

noun
dome that opens and closes
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtræktəbl doʊm / (AmE), / rɪˈtræktəbl dəʊm / (BrE)
a dome, especially on a building, that can be opened or closed mechanically

Examples

retractable dome covers stadium
During heavy rain, the retractable dome was closed to keep spectators dry.
The new arena’s retractable dome is operated by a state-of-the-art mechanical system.
The stadium installed a retractable dome to allow for both indoor and outdoor events.

Related Terms

  • synonym – roof: the structure forming the upper covering of a building or vehicle
