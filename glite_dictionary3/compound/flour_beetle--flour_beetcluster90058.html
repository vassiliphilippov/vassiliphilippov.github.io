flour beetle

noun
beetle that infests flour and grains
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚ ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a small beetle that infests flour and stored grains, often considered a pest in food storage areas

Examples

flour beetle infestation
A single flour beetle can contaminate an entire bag of flour if left unchecked.
Inspect your baking supplies regularly to prevent a flour beetle problem.
The cereal was ruined after flour beetles infested the pantry.
