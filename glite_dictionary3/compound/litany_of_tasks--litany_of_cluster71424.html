litany of tasks

noun
a long, tedious list of tasks
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtəni əv tæsks / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəni əv tɑːsks / (BrE)
a lengthy and often tedious list of tasks, especially one that is repeatedly mentioned or complained about

Examples

endless litany of tasks
After the holiday, my inbox contained a litany of tasks waiting to be addressed.
She sighed as she looked at the litany of tasks written on her whiteboard.
The project manager presented the team with a litany of tasks for the upcoming week.
