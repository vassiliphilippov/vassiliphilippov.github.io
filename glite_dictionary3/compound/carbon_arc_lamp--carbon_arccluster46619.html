carbon arc lamp

noun
lamp producing light via carbon arc
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrbən ˈɑrk læmp / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbən ˈɑːk læmp / (BrE)
a type of electric lamp in which a bright light is produced by an arc between carbon electrodes.
