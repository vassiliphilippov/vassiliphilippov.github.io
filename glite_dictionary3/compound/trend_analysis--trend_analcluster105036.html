trend analysis

noun
examination of data changes over time
Pronunciation: / trɛnd əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / trɛnd əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the process of examining data over a period of time to identify consistent patterns or trends

Examples

trend analysis of sales data
By conducting a trend analysis, researchers identified significant changes in consumer preferences.
Financial experts rely on trend analysis to forecast future market behavior.
The company's trend analysis revealed a steady increase in sales over the past five years.
