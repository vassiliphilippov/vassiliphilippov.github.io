hock-joint

noun
joint in the hind leg of hoofed mammals
Pronunciation: / hɑk dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / hɒk dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the tarsal joint on the hind leg of a hoofed mammal, corresponding to the human ankle
