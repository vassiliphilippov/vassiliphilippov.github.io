guaiac wood

noun
aromatic heartwood from the guaiac tree
Pronunciation: / ˈɡwaɪ.æk wʊd / (AmE), / ˈɡwaɪ.æk wʊd / (BrE)
The hard, dense heartwood of the guaiac tree, used especially for its aromatic oil in perfumes and medicinal products.

Examples

guaiac wood carving
Artisans carved intricate figurines out of guaiac wood because of its hardness and beauty.
The aromatic oil extracted from guaiac wood is prized in the perfume industry.
Traditional medicine sometimes uses guaiac wood for its supposed healing properties.
