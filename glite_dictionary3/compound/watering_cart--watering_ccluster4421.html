watering cart

noun
vehicle for sprinkling water on roads
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚɪŋ kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərɪŋ kɑːt / (BrE)
a vehicle equipped with a tank and sprinkler used for spraying water on roads or surfaces to control dust or clean them

Examples

watering cart sprays streets
Every morning, a watering cart makes its way down the street, spraying water to settle the dirt.
The city deployed a watering cart to keep the roads clear of dust during construction.
The park uses a watering cart to maintain clean and cool pathways during the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – water cart: a cart for carrying water, sometimes for supply or sprinkling
