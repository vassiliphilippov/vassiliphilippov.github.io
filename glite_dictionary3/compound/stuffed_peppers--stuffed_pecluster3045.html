stuffed peppers

noun
dish of peppers stuffed with filling
Pronunciation: / stʌft ˈpɛpɚz / (AmE), / stʌft ˈpɛpəz / (BrE)
A dish of peppers, usually green, that are hollowed out and filled with a mixture such as rice and meat, then baked or cooked.

Examples

stuffed peppers with rice
He served stuffed peppers as the main course at the family dinner.
My grandmother taught me how to make delicious stuffed peppers filled with herbs and rice.
The restaurant's vegetarian stuffed peppers quickly became a favorite among customers.
