holandric gene

noun
gene found on the Y chromosome
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈlændrɪk dʒin / (AmE), / həʊˈlændrɪk dʒiːn / (BrE)
a gene located on the Y chromosome and inherited only through the male line

Examples

A holandric gene is passed exclusively from father to son because it is found only on the Y chromosome.
