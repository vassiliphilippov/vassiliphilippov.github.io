fully booked

adjective
all spaces or seats reserved; no vacancies
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊli bʊkt / (AmE), / ˈfʊli bʊkt / (BrE)
With all available places, rooms, or tickets already reserved; having no vacancies remaining.

Examples

fully booked hotel
Our flight was fully booked, and several passengers were placed on the waiting list.
She tried to reserve a table at the new restaurant, but it was fully booked for the entire weekend.
The hotel was fully booked during the holiday season, so we had to stay elsewhere.
