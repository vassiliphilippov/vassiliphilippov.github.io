yeah, no

interjection
informal dismissive or uncertain response
Pronunciation: / jæ, noʊ / (AmE), / jæ, nəʊ / (BrE)
an informal spoken response used to express disagreement or polite refusal, often softening the directness of 'no'

Examples

yeah, no thanks
Yeah, no, I don't see how that plan would work out.
Yeah, no, I'm not really in the mood for pizza tonight.
He suggested going for a hike, but she just said, "yeah, no, thanks anyway."
