wild rosemary

noun
bog shrub native to Eurasia
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈroʊzmɛri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈrəʊzməri / (BrE)
a bog shrub native to northern and central Europe and eastern Siberia to Korea and Japan, known for its aromatic leaves but unrelated to true rosemary
