air well

noun
shaft for ventilation in a building
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌwɛl / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌwɛl / (BrE)
A vertical shaft or passage in a building designed to provide ventilation by allowing the movement of air.

Examples

air well in a building
Regulations require an air well in high-rise buildings to ensure proper ventilation.
The architect designed a large air well to bring natural light and fresh air into the interior spaces.
The old apartment building was constructed around a central air well to improve airflow in every unit.
