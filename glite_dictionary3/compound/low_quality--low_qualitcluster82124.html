low quality

noun
inferior quality or standard
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
An inferior standard of quality; something considered to be of poor quality or low grade.

Examples

low quality product
Customers complained that the service at the restaurant was of low quality.
Some of the imported goods were returned because of their low quality.
The movie received negative reviews due to its low quality of production.
