protective covering

noun
a covering that provides protection
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktɪv ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪv ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
A covering used to shield something from damage or injury.

Examples

use a protective covering for safety
Always use a protective covering when handling chemicals in the laboratory.
The table was shielded with a protective covering to avoid scratches during the move.
The workers wore a protective covering over their uniforms to prevent contamination.
