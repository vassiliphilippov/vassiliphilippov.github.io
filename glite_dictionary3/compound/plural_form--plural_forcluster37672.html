plural form

noun
form used for more than one
Pronunciation: / ˈplʊrəl fɔrm / (AmE), / ˈplʊərəl fɔːm / (BrE)
the form of a word used to indicate that it refers to more than one person or thing

Examples

plural form of a noun
Most English nouns add 's' or 'es' in their plural form.
She struggled to remember the plural form of some irregular nouns.
The plural form of 'child' is 'children'.
