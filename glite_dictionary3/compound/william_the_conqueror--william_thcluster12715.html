William the Conqueror

proper noun
Norman conqueror and first Norman King of England
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪl.jəm ðə kənˈkɝː.ər / (AmE), / ˈwɪl.jəm ðə kənˈkʌr.ər / (BrE)
(c. 1028–1087) The Duke of Normandy who became the first Norman King of England after leading the Norman invasion and defeating Harold II at the Battle of Hastings in 1066.

Examples

William the Conqueror invaded England
William the Conqueror changed the course of English history by invading in 1066.
William the Conqueror introduced Norman culture to the English court.
Many castles in England were constructed during the reign of William the Conqueror.
