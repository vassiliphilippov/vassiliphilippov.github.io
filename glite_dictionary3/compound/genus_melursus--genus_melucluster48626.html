genus Melursus

noun
taxonomic genus of sloth bears
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs məˈlɜːrsəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs mɛlˈɜːsəs / (BrE)
A genus of bears comprising the sloth bears, sometimes not distinguished as a separate genus from Ursus.

Examples

genus Melursus classification
Some taxonomists debate whether the genus Melursus should be merged with the genus Ursus.
The genus Melursus contains only the sloth bear, native to the Indian subcontinent.
Unlike other bear genera, the genus Melursus is characterized by long, shaggy hair and a diet high in insects.
