Milwaukee goiter

noun
humorous term for feeling overfull after eating
Pronunciation: / mɪlˈwɔki ˈɡɔɪtɚ / (AmE), / mɪlˈwɔːki ˈɡɔɪtə / (BrE)
a humorous expression for feeling overly full around the neck or throat, especially after eating a large meal

Examples

big Milwaukee goiter after dinner
After the Thanksgiving dinner, he joked that his Milwaukee goiter was acting up again.
