anamnestic response

noun
rapid antibody production after repeat antigen exposure
Pronunciation: / ˌænəmˈnɛstɪk rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˌanəmˈnɛstɪk rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a renewed and accelerated production of antibodies upon a second or later exposure to the same antigen

Examples
