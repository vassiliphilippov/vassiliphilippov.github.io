family Moraceae

noun
a family of trees and shrubs with milky sap
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli mɔːˈreɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli mɒˈreɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of trees or shrubs with milky sap, including mulberries and figs; sometimes includes the genus Cannabis in certain classifications.
