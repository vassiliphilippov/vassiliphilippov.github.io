gamma hydroxybutyrate

noun
sedative and recreational drug (GHB)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæmə haɪˌdrɒksiˌbjuːtəˈreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡæmə hʌɪˌdrɒksɪˌbjuːtəˈreɪt / (BrE)
A colorless, odorless substance used as a recreational drug and a sedative; commonly known as GHB, it can cause loss of consciousness and is sometimes misused to facilitate sexual assault.
