tea tray

noun
tray for carrying a tea set
Pronunciation: / ti treɪ / (AmE), / tiː treɪ / (BrE)
A flat, usually rectangular or oval tray designed for carrying a tea set, cups, or other items for serving tea.

Examples

tea tray with cups
After the guests left, he carried the empty tea tray back to the kitchen.
She placed the porcelain teapot and cups neatly on the tea tray before heading to the living room.
The antique silver tea tray was the centerpiece of the afternoon tea service.
