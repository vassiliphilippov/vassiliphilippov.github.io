chlorophyll a

noun
principal plant pigment type
Pronunciation: / klɔːrəˌfɪl ˈeɪ / (AmE), / klɔːrəˌfɪl ˈeɪ / (BrE)
the principal form of chlorophyll in higher plants, algae, and cyanobacteria, with a blue-black color in pure form and blue-green in solution

Chlorophyll a absorbs light most efficiently in the blue-violet and red parts of the spectrum.
