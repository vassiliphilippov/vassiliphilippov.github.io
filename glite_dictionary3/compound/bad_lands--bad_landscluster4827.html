Bad Lands

noun
eroded, barren region in the US Midwest
Pronunciation: / ˈbæd ˌlændz / (AmE), / ˈbæd ˌlændz / (BrE)
A region of heavily eroded, barren land in southwestern South Dakota and northwestern Nebraska, notable for its striking landscapes.
