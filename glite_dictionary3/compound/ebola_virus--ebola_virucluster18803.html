Ebola virus

noun
virus causing Ebola hemorrhagic fever
Pronunciation: / ɪˈboʊlə ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / iˈbəʊlə ˈvaɪrəs / (BrE)
A highly infectious filovirus that causes Ebola hemorrhagic fever in humans and animals.

Examples

Ebola virus outbreak
