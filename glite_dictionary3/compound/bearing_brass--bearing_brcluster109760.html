bearing brass

noun
brass lining for a bearing
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪŋ bræs / (AmE), / ˈbɛːrɪŋ brɑːs / (BrE)
a brass bushing or lining used in a bearing to reduce friction and wear between moving parts

Examples

bearing brass in machinery
He carefully fitted the bearing brass into the housing before assembling the machinery.
