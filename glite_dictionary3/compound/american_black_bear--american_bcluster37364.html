American black bear

noun
species of North American bear
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈblæk ˌbɛɹ / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹɪkən ˈblæk ˌbeə / (BrE)
A medium-sized North American bear (Ursus americanus), usually with black or dark brown fur, smaller and less aggressive than the brown bear.
