(in information technology) a coordinated effort to recover data, restore systems, and resume services after a cyberattack, system failure, outage, or other major data loss incident.

Examples

IT rescue operation
The IT team launched a rescue operation to recover corrupted databases after the outage.
After the ransomware attack, engineers coordinated a quick rescue operation to restore customer services.
Management approved extra budget for the major rescue operation needed to rebuild the cloud infrastructure.
