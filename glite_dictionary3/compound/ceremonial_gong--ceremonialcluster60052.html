ceremonial gong

noun
gong used in ceremonies or rituals
Pronunciation: / sɛˌrəˈmoʊniəl ɡɑŋ / (AmE), / sɛˌrɪˈməʊniəl ɡɒŋ / (BrE)
a large percussion instrument, typically a metal disc, used in formal or ritual ceremonies to mark important moments or transitions
