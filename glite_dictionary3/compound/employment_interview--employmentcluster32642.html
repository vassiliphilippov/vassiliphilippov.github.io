employment interview

noun
formal job application meeting
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈɪntɚˌvju / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔɪmənt ˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
a formal meeting in which a job applicant is evaluated by a potential employer to determine suitability for a position

Examples

attend an employment interview
After submitting his resume, Mark was invited to an employment interview at a local engineering firm.
Many candidates feel nervous before their first employment interview but gain confidence with practice.
She prepared thoroughly for the employment interview and answered all the questions confidently.
