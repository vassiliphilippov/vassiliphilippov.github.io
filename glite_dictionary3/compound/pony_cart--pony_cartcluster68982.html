pony cart

noun
light cart pulled by a pony
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊni kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊni kɑːt / (BrE)
a light two-wheeled or four-wheeled cart, drawn by a pony, typically with seats for two people
