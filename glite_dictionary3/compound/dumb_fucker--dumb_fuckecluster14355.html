dumb fucker

noun
offensive insult for a foolish person
Pronunciation: / dʌm ˈfʌk.ɚ / (AmE), / dʌm ˈfʌk.ə / (BrE)
A highly offensive and vulgar term for a very foolish or stupid person, often used as an insult or, less commonly, self-deprecatingly.

Examples

He called his coworker a dumb fucker after the expensive mistake.
Sometimes I feel like a real dumb fucker when I make careless errors.
The coach was furious and yelled at the player, calling him a dumb fucker for missing the goal.
