Western Isles

proper noun
group of islands off western Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərn aɪlz / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən aɪlz / (BrE)
a group of more than 500 islands off the western coast of Scotland, also known as the Outer Hebrides.

Examples

the Western Isles of Scotland
