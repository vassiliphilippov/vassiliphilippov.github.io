campaign hat

noun
broad-brimmed felt military hat
Pronunciation: / kæmˈpeɪn hæt / (AmE), / kæmˈpeɪn hæt / (BrE)
a broad-brimmed felt hat with a high crown, traditionally worn as part of the uniform by United States Army and Marine Corps personnel
