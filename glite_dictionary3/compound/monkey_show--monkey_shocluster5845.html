monkey show

noun
performance featuring trained monkeys
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a performance, often entertaining, in which monkeys are trained to perform tricks or acts for an audience

Examples

a monkey show performance
