silver vine

noun
ornamental Asian vine with silvery leaves and edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə vaɪn / (BrE)
an ornamental vine of eastern Asia with yellow edible fruit and leaves marked with silver-white patterns, known for its attractiveness and occasional use as a cat stimulant
