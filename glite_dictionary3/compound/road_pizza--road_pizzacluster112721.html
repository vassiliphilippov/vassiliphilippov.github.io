road pizza

noun
flattened object or wreck on the road
Pronunciation: / roʊd ˈpiːtsə / (AmE), / rəʊd ˈpiːtsə / (BrE)
an informal term for a tire, vehicle, or object that has been flattened or destroyed on the road, often after an accident
