worldly concern

noun
matter related to material life, not spiritual
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːldli kənˈsɝːn / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
a matter or interest related to the practical affairs and material aspects of daily life, as opposed to spiritual or religious matters

Examples

The monks renounce worldly concerns to focus on their faith.
