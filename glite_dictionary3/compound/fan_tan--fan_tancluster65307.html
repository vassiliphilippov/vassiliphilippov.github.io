fan tan

noun
Chinese gambling game involving counters and betting
Pronunciation: / fæn ˈtæn / (AmE), / fan ˈtan / (BrE)
A traditional Chinese gambling game in which players bet on how many counters, when divided by four, will remain after a random number are placed under a bowl.

Examples

play fan tan game
During the festival, a crowd gathered to watch a lively game of fan tan in the teahouse.
He learned to play fan tan from his grandfather, who had grown up in Guangdong.
Many gamblers were drawn to the fan tan tables in the back rooms of the old Chinatown.
