manner adverbial

noun
adverbial describing the manner of an action
Pronunciation: / ˈmænər ædˈvɝːbiəl / (AmE), / ˈmænə ædˈvəːbɪəl / (BrE)
an adverbial that indicates the way in which the action of a verb is performed

Examples

manner adverbial like quickly
Common manner adverbials include words like quickly, carefully, or silently.
In the sentence 'She spoke quietly,' 'quietly' functions as a manner adverbial.
When using a manner adverbial, it often comes immediately after the verb.
