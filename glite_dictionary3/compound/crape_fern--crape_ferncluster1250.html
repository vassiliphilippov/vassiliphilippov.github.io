crape fern

noun
New Zealand fern with woolly stalks
Pronunciation: / kreɪp fɝn / (AmE), / kreɪp fɜːn / (BrE)
A fern native to New Zealand with pinnate leaves and densely woolly stalks, sometimes classified under the genus Todea.
