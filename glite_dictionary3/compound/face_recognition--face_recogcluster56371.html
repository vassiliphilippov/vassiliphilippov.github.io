face recognition

noun
automated identification by facial features
Pronunciation: / feɪs ˌrɛk.əɡˈnɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / feɪs ˌrɛk.ɒɡˈnɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the automated process of identifying or verifying a person by analyzing and comparing their facial features with stored data

Examples

face recognition software
Concerns about privacy have arisen due to the widespread use of face recognition by law enforcement agencies.
