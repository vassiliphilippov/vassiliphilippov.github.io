war zone

noun
area with active conflict or chaos
Pronunciation: / wɔr zoʊn / (AmE), / wɔː zəʊn / (BrE)
an area where armed conflict is taking place or a situation marked by chaos and danger

Examples

looked like a war zone
After the hurricane, the city looked like a war zone with debris everywhere.
Journalists often risk their lives to report from a war zone.
The negotiations failed, and the region quickly descended into a war zone.
