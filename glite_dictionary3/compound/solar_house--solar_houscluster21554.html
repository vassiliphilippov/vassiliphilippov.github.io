solar house

noun
house using solar energy for heating
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər haʊs / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə haʊs / (BrE)
a house designed to use solar energy for heating, typically featuring large glass areas and heat-absorbing materials

Examples

solar house with large windows
Living in a solar house can significantly reduce your heating bills and environmental impact.
The architect designed a solar house that stays warm in winter using only sunlight.
They toured a model solar house featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and special heat-absorbing walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym – passive house: a house with minimal energy requirements for heating or cooling, focusing on insulation and airtightness
