ear-piercing scream

noun
very loud, high-pitched scream
Pronunciation: / ɪr ˈpɪrsɪŋ skrim / (AmE), / ɪə ˈpɪəsɪŋ skriːm / (BrE)
A scream that is extremely loud and high-pitched, often causing discomfort to the listener.

Examples

an ear-piercing scream echoed
An ear-piercing scream startled the birds in the trees, sending them flying into the sky.
Everyone in the room froze when a sudden ear-piercing scream echoed down the hallway.
The movie was so scary it made her let out an ear-piercing scream during the climax.
