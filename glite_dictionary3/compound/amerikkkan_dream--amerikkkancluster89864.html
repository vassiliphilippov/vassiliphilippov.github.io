Amerikkkan dream

noun
critical take on the American dream focused on inequality
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛr.ɪk.ən driːm / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪk.ən driːm / (BrE)
A critical reinterpretation of the American dream that highlights systemic racism, inequality, and the exclusion of marginalized groups in the United States.
