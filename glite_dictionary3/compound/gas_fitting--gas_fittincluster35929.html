gas fitting

noun
pipe or device for conveying gas in buildings
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡas ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a pipe, valve, meter, or similar device used to convey gas from the main supply to fixtures within a building

Building codes require all gas fittings to meet strict safety standards.
