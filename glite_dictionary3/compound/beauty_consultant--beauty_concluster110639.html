beauty consultant

noun
person who advises on personal appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːti kənˌsʌltənt / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti kənˌsʌltənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to give advice about personal appearance, especially concerning cosmetics and skincare

Examples

beauty consultant recommends products
A beauty consultant can help you choose the right makeup for your skin type.
She decided to become a beauty consultant after years of working in cosmetics.
The beauty consultant recommended a new skincare routine for me.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cosmetologist: a person licensed to provide cosmetic treatments to hair, skin, and nails
