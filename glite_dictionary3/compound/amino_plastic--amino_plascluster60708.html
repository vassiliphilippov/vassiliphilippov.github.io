amino plastic

noun
synthetic resin from amino compounds
Pronunciation: / əˌminoʊ ˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / əˌmiːnəʊ ˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
a synthetic resin made from amino compounds, used as an adhesive and as a coating for paper and textiles

Examples

amino plastic coating
Manufacturers often use amino plastic coatings to increase the water resistance of paper products.
Many kitchen countertops are made with amino plastic laminates for durability.
The adhesive properties of amino plastic make it suitable for woodworking.
