cereal grass

noun
grain-producing grass (e.g. wheat, rice)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəl ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any type of grass grown for its edible starchy grains, such as wheat, rice, rye, oats, maize, buckwheat, or millet
