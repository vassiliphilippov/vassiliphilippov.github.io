siren song

noun
alluring but dangerous appeal
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪɹən sɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪən sɒŋ / (BrE)
an appealing offer or attraction that is difficult to resist but may lead to trouble or danger

Examples

the siren song of money
Despite her better judgment, she followed the siren song of adventure into unknown territory.
He couldn't resist the siren song of the online gambling site, even though he knew the risks.
The promise of easy money proved to be a siren song for many naive investors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – temptation: the desire to do something, especially something wrong or unwise
  • synonym – trap: a situation from which it is difficult or impossible to escape, often one that is enticing
