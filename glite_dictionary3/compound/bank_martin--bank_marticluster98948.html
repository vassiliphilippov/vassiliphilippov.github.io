bank martin

noun
a swallow that nests in sand or clay banks
Pronunciation: / bæŋk ˈmɑrtɪn / (AmE), / bæŋk ˈmɑːtɪn / (BrE)
a small swallow found in the Northern Hemisphere that nests in holes dug into sand or clay banks
