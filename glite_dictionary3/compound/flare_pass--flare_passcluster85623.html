flare pass

noun
short forward pass to a back in football
Pronunciation: / flɛr pæs / (AmE), / fleə pɑːs / (BrE)
a short forward pass made to a back running toward the sidelines, particularly in American football

Examples

flare pass to a running back
On third down, they executed a quick flare pass to evade the defensive rush.
The quarterback threw a flare pass to the running back, who then sprinted down the sideline.
The team's strategy often includes a flare pass to exploit gaps in the defense.
