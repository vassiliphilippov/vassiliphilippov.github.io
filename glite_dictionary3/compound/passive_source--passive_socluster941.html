passive source

noun
informant who passes on information routinely
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪv sɔrs / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪv sɔːs / (BrE)
an informant who provides information routinely, without being tasked to gather specific intelligence

Examples

trusted passive source
As a passive source, she provided useful insights without ever being directly questioned.
The agency was able to confirm the rumors thanks to a passive source within the organization.
Unlike an active informant, a passive source shares information they come across in their normal activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – active source: an informant tasked to obtain specific information or intelligence
