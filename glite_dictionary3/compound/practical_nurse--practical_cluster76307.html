practical nurse

noun
licensed nurse providing basic care
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪkəl nɝs / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪkəl nɜːs / (BrE)
a nurse who is trained and licensed to provide routine care for the sick, usually under the supervision of a registered nurse or doctor

Examples

practical nurse cares for patients
After her operation, a practical nurse visited her daily to check her recovery.
As a practical nurse, he assists patients with their daily activities in the clinic.
The hospital is hiring several practical nurses for its long-term care unit.

Related Terms

  • synonym – licensed practical nurse: nurse licensed to provide basic medical care, especially in the US and Canada
  • degree higher – registered nurse: nurse with higher training and responsibilities than a practical nurse
