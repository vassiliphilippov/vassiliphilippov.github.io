bulbourethral gland

noun
male gland that contributes to seminal fluid
Pronunciation: / ˌbʌlboʊjʊˈriθrəl ˌɡlænd / (AmE), / ˌbʌlbjʊˈriːθrəl ˌɡlænd / (BrE)
either of a pair of small glands in males that discharge a component of seminal fluid into the urethra, homologous to Bartholin's gland in females
