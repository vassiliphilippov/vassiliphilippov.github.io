motor mower

noun
lawn mower with a motor
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtər ˌmoʊər / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ˌməʊə / (BrE)
A lawn mower powered by a gasoline or electric motor rather than by manual effort.

Unlike manual versions, a motor mower can cover large areas quickly and efficiently.
