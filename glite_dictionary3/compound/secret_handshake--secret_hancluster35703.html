secret handshake

noun
cryptographic authentication exchange
Pronunciation: / \u02c8si\u02d0kr\u0259t \u02c8h\u00e6nd\u0283e\u026ak / (AmE), / \u02c8si\u02d0kr\u026at \u02c8h\u00e6nd\u0283e\u026ak / (BrE)
(in computing, especially computer security) a protocol exchange between two or more parties that authenticates participants and negotiates cryptographic keys or parameters to establish a secure communication channel.

Examples

secret handshake in security
During setup, the client and server perform a secret handshake to negotiate encryption keys.
The penetration test captured several failed secret handshakes exposing a misconfigured TLS setting.
If the certificates don't match, the application aborts the secret handshake and closes the session.
