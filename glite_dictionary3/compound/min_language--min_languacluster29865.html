Min language

noun
group of Chinese dialects in Fujian region
Pronunciation: / mɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɪn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of several related forms of Chinese spoken primarily in Fujian province and nearby regions, including dialects such as Hokkien and Teochew.
