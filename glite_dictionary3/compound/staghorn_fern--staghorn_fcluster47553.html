staghorn fern

noun
tropical fern with antler-like fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈstæɡˌhɔrn fɝn / (AmE), / ˈstæɡˌhɔːn fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical fern of the genus Platycerium, known for its large, flat, lobed fronds that resemble stag antlers
