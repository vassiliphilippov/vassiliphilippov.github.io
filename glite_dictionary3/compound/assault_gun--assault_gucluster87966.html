assault gun

noun
armored vehicle with a large mounted gun
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔlt ɡʌn / (AmE), / əˈsɔːlt ɡʌn / (BrE)
an armored vehicle equipped with a large gun, mounted on a tank chassis without a turret, used to support infantry and as an antitank weapon

Examples

armored assault gun vehicle
During the battle, the assault gun targeted enemy tanks from a concealed position.
Many World War II movies feature scenes with assault guns supporting soldiers in urban combat.
The army deployed an assault gun to provide heavy support for the advancing infantry.
