mastoid process

noun
bony projection behind the ear
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstɔɪd ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈmæstɔɪd ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
a bony projection of the temporal bone located just behind the ear at the base of the skull

Examples

mastoid process behind ear
Swelling behind the ear may indicate infection of the mastoid process.
The surgeon made an incision near the mastoid process to access the middle ear.
You can feel the mastoid process as a bump just behind your earlobe.
