clothes tree

noun
freestanding pole with hooks for clothes
Pronunciation: / kloʊðz tri / (AmE), / kləʊðz triː / (BrE)
a freestanding upright pole with pegs or hooks for hanging clothes, hats, or umbrellas

clothes tree in hallway
