red-spotted purple

noun
North American butterfly with red spots under wings
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd ˈspɑtɪd ˈpɜrt͡ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛd ˈspɒtɪd ˈpɜːpl̩ / (BrE)
