chilli pepper

noun
a spicy, hot variety of pepper plant
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪli ˌpɛpər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli ˌpɛpə / (BrE)
A plant of the Capsicum genus that produces small, hot, and often red peppers used for seasoning food.

Examples

spicy chilli pepper
Red chilli pepper is often used in Indian and Thai cuisine.
She chopped a chilli pepper to add more heat to the stew.
The seeds of the chilli pepper can be very spicy.
