metalworking vise

noun
tool for holding metal objects tightly
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛt̬əlˌwɝːkɪŋ vaɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlˌwɜːkɪŋ vaɪs / (BrE)
a heavy tool with two parallel iron jaws and a wide opening below, used for holding metal objects firmly during metalworking processes
