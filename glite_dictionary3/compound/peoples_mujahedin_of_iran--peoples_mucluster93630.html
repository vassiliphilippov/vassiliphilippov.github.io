People's Mujahedin of Iran

proper noun
Iranian militant political organization
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːpəlz muːˈhædʒədɪn əv aɪˈræn / (AmE), / ˈpiːplz mjuːˈhædʒədɪn ɒv ɪˈrɑːn / (BrE)
