inorganic compound

noun
compound not containing carbon
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌɔːɹˈɡænɪk kəmˈpaʊnd / (AmE), / ɪnˌɔːɡˈænɪk kəmˈpaʊnd / (BrE)
a chemical compound that does not contain carbon, or is not considered organic; typically includes minerals, salts, and metals

Examples
