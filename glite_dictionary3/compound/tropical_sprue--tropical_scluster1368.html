tropical sprue

noun
chronic tropical intestinal disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːpɪkəl spru / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkəl spruː / (BrE)
A chronic disease, most common in tropical regions, characterized by impaired absorption of nutrients in the small intestine, leading to diarrhea, weight loss, and malnutrition.
