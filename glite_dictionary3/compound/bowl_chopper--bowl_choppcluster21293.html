bowl chopper

noun
meat processing machine for chopping/mixing
Pronunciation: / boʊl ˈtʃɑːpər / (AmE), / bəʊl ˈtʃɒpə / (BrE)
a machine used in food processing to chop, mix, and emulsify meat or other ingredients by rotating them in a bowl with high-speed blades

Examples

bowl chopper processes meat
A commercial bowl chopper can process large amounts of meat into a uniform texture.
The butcher used the bowl chopper to prepare the sausage mixture quickly and efficiently.
The recipe called for ground meat, which was easily achieved with a bowl chopper.
