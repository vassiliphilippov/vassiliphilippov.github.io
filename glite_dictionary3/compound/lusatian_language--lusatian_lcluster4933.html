Lusatian language

noun
West Slavic language of Lusatia, Germany
Pronunciation: / luːˈseɪʃən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / luːˈseɪʃən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a West Slavic language spoken by the Sorbian people in southeastern Germany, particularly in Lusatia
