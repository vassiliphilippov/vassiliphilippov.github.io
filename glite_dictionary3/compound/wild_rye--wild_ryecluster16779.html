wild rye

noun
a grass of the genus Elymus
Pronunciation: / waɪld raɪ / (AmE), / waɪld raɪ / (BrE)
any of several perennial grasses of the genus Elymus, typically found in North America and Eurasia, often used for forage or soil conservation.
