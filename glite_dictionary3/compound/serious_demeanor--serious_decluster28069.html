serious demeanor

noun
grave or earnest manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəs dɪˈmiːnər / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəs dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
A manner of behaving that is grave, earnest, and shows a lack of frivolity or humor.

Examples

a serious demeanor at work
Despite the celebration, his serious demeanor suggested he was thinking about more important matters.
Teachers often adopt a serious demeanor to establish authority and focus in the classroom.
The judge maintained a serious demeanor throughout the trial, setting the tone for the courtroom.
