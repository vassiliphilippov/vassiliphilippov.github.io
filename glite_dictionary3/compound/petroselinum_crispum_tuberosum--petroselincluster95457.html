Petroselinum crispum tuberosum

noun
root parsley; parsley with edible taproot
Pronunciation: / pɛtɹəˈsiːlɪnəm ˈkrɪspəm tuːˈbɛɹəsəm / (AmE), / pɛtɹəˈsiːlɪnəm ˈkrɪspəm tjuːˈbɛrəsəm / (BrE)
A variety of parsley with smooth leaves and an enlarged edible taproot, resembling a savory parsnip; also known as Hamburg parsley or root parsley.
