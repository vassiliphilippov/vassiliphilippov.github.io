lister plough

noun
type of double moldboard plough
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪstər plaʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɪstə plaʊ / (BrE)
a moldboard plough with a double moldboard designed to move soil to either side of a central furrow, used especially to prepare land for planting row crops

Examples

lister plough for row crops
A lister plough creates two ridges of soil with a deep furrow between them.
Modern lister ploughs are designed for efficient row crop preparation.
The farmer used a lister plough to prepare the field for planting corn.
