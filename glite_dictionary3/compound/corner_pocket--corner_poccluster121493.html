corner pocket

noun
a corner pocket on a billiard table
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnər ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnə ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
A pocket located at one of the four corners of a billiard or pool table, into which balls are aimed during play.

Examples

corner pocket shot
Missing the corner pocket cost him the game.
She aimed her shot carefully and sunk the eight ball in the corner pocket.
The ball rolled smoothly into the corner pocket, earning him the win.
