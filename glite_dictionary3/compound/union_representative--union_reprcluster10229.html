union representative

noun
person representing workers in a labor union
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniən ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˈjuːniən ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person who is elected or appointed to represent members of a labor union in dealings with management or other authorities

Examples

union representative negotiates pay
As a union representative, she negotiated better working conditions for her colleagues.
If you have any problems at work, you can speak to your union representative for advice.
The union representative attended the meeting to discuss workers' concerns with management.
