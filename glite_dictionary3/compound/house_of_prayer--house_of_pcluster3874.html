house of prayer

noun
place of worship for congregational prayer
Pronunciation: / haʊs əv ˈprɛr / (AmE), / haʊs əv ˈpreə / (BrE)
a building or place established for prayer and religious worship by a congregation

Examples

a house of prayer for worship
Each religion may have its own type of house of prayer for gatherings and ceremonies.
The old synagogue was considered a house of prayer for the entire community.
Visitors were welcome to meditate in the peaceful house of prayer at any time.
