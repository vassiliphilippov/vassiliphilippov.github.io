Indian lettuce

noun
edible plant in the genus Montia
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈlɛtɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈlɛtɪs / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Montia with edible, pleasant-tasting leaves, often eaten as a salad green or cooked vegetable
