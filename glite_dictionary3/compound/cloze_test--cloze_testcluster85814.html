cloze test

noun
passage with blanks to test reading skill
Pronunciation: / kloʊz tɛst / (AmE), / kləʊz tɛst / (BrE)
a test or task where words are removed from a passage and must be filled in, used to assess reading ability or to train/evaluate language models

Examples

complete a cloze test
Creating a cloze test is a popular technique in English language teaching.

Researchers compared models using a challenging cloze test containing idioms and rare vocabulary items.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gap-fill: a similar test requiring completion of missing words in a text
