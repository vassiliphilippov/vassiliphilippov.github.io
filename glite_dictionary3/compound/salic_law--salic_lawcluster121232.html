Salic law

noun
legal code of the Salian Franks
Pronunciation: / ˈsælɪk lɔ / (AmE), / ˈsalɪk lɔː / (BrE)
the code of laws of the Salian Franks, especially noted for prohibiting female succession to thrones or property in certain European countries
