unvarnished opinion

noun
honest, straightforward view or statement
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɑrnɪʃt əˈpɪnjən / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɑːnɪʃt əˈpɪnjən / (BrE)
an honest and direct statement of one's views, without any attempt to make them seem more pleasant or acceptable than they are

Examples

give an unvarnished opinion
Journalists are expected to provide the public with the unvarnished opinion on important issues.
She appreciated his unvarnished opinion about her performance, even though it was hard to hear.
Whenever you ask for an unvarnished opinion, prepare yourself for absolute honesty.

Related Terms

  • synonym – frank opinion: an open and candid expression of belief or view
  • antonym – sugarcoated opinion: an opinion expressed in a gentler or more pleasant way than the reality
