mushroom cloud

noun
cloud from large explosion, especially nuclear bomb
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃruːm klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃrəm klaʊd / (BrE)
a large cloud shaped like a mushroom, formed by the smoke and debris rising into the air after a large explosion, especially a nuclear bomb

Examples

a mushroom cloud after explosion
Photographs of the mushroom cloud became iconic symbols of the nuclear age.
Scientists studied the formation of a mushroom cloud after the test explosion.
The atomic bomb left behind a massive mushroom cloud that could be seen for miles.
