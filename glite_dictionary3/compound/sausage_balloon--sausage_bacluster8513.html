sausage balloon

noun
small nonrigid airship for observation or defense
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɪdʒ bəˈluːn / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a small, nonrigid airship, typically cylindrical in shape, used for observation or as a barrage balloon, especially in wartime.

Examples

a sausage balloon over the city
A single sausage balloon hovered over the city, monitoring for enemy aircraft.
During World War I, the sausage balloon was a common sight above the trenches.
The museum displays a restored sausage balloon used in early aerial reconnaissance.
