coal car

noun
open railway freight car for coal
Pronunciation: / koʊl kɑr / (AmE), / kəʊl kɑː / (BrE)
A railroad freight car with fixed sides and no roof, designed for transporting coal.

Examples

loaded coal car
After the storm, several coal cars were found derailed near the tracks.
The train was pulling a long line of coal cars through the valley.
Workers loaded the coal car before the shipment left the mine.
