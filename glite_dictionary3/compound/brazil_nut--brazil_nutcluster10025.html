Brazil nut

noun
South American tree producing edible nuts
Pronunciation: / brəˈzɪl nʌt / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl nʌt / (BrE)
A tall South American tree (Bertholletia excelsa) of the Amazon rainforest that produces edible seeds known as Brazil nuts.
