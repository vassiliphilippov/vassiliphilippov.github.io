keynote speaker

noun
main speaker at an event
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌnoʊt ˈspiːkər / (AmE), / ˈkiːnəʊt ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
the main speaker at a meeting, conference, or other formal event, who sets the underlying tone or theme

Examples

The organizers announced a Nobel laureate as this year's keynote speaker.
