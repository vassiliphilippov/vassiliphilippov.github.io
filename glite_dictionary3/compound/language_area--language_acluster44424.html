language area

noun
region of the brain for language functions
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌɛriə / (AmE), / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˌeəriə / (BrE)
a large region of the cerebral cortex, usually in the left hemisphere, containing centers involved in language processing and production
