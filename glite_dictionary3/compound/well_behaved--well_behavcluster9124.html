well behaved

adjective
behaving in a polite or proper way
Pronunciation: / wɛl bɪˈheɪvd / (AmE), / wɛl bɪˈheɪvd / (BrE)
(especially of children) behaving in a polite or proper way, as expected or desired by adults or authorities.
