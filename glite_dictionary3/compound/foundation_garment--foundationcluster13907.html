foundation garment

noun
undergarment for shaping the body
Pronunciation: / faʊnˈdeɪʃən ˈɡɑrmənt / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃən ˈɡɑːm(ə)nt / (BrE)
a woman's undergarment designed to shape or support the body's contours, such as a girdle or corset
