robe-de-chambre

noun
dressing gown or lounging robe
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊb də ˈʃɑːmbrə / (AmE), / ˌrəʊb də ˈʃɑːmbrə / (BrE)
A long, loose garment worn over nightwear before dressing or while lounging at home; a dressing gown.
