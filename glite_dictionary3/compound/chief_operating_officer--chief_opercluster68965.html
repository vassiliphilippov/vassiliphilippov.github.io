chief operating officer

noun
senior executive overseeing company operations
Pronunciation: / tʃif ˌɑpəˈreɪtɪŋ ˈɑfəsər / (AmE), / tʃiːf ˌɒpəˈreɪtɪŋ ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
the executive responsible for managing the day-to-day operations of a company, typically reporting directly to the chief executive officer or the board of directors.
