wrist bone

noun
one of the small bones in the wrist
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪst ˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɪst bəʊn / (BrE)
any of the eight small bones that make up the wrist in primates, also known as carpal bones

Examples

broken wrist bone
An X-ray revealed that her pain was due to a broken wrist bone.
He fractured a wrist bone during the fall while playing soccer.
The doctor explained that each wrist bone plays a role in joint movement.
