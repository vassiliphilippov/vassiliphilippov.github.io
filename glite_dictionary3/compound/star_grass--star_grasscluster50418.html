star grass

noun
a type of grass used for lawns and pastures
Pronunciation: / stɑr ɡræs / (AmE), / stɑː ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a creeping grass native to Europe and widely grown in warm regions, especially used for lawns and pastures in the southern United States and India

Examples

star grass lawn pasture
Farmers in the region prefer star grass for its durability and rapid growth.
In India, star grass is commonly used to create dense, resilient pastures.
The star grass on the golf course stays green through most of the year.
