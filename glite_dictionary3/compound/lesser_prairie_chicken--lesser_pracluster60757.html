lesser prairie chicken

noun
a smaller species of prairie grouse
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsɚ ˈprɛri ˈtʃɪkən / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈprɛəri ˈtʃɪkɪn / (BrE)
