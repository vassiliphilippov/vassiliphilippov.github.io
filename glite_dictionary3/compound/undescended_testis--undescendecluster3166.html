undescended testis

noun
testis failing to descend into the scrotum
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈsɛndɪd ˈtɛs.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈsɛndɪd ˈtɛs.tɪs / (BrE)
a testis that does not move into the scrotum before birth, remaining in the abdomen or groin

Examples

an undescended testis in a baby
If an undescended testis is not treated in childhood, fertility problems may arise later.
Surgical correction is often recommended for a child born with an undescended testis.
The pediatrician detected an undescended testis during the infant's routine examination.
