sweet chestnut

adjective
reddish‑brown colour
Pronunciation: / swit \u02c8t\u0283\u025bs.n\u028ct / (AmE), / swi\u02d0t \u02c8t\u0283\u025bs.n\u028ct / (BrE)
A medium to dark reddish‑brown colour resembling the nut or timber of the sweet chestnut tree, used to describe hair, fabrics, leather, and finishes.

Examples

sweet chestnut hair
She dyed her long hair a warm sweet chestnut that gleamed in the sunlight.
The boutique stocked a sweet chestnut leather jacket that matched many outfits.
For autumn decor, they chose curtains in a deep sweet chestnut to warm the room.
