club steak

noun
small steak from short loin of beef
Pronunciation: / klʌb steɪk / (AmE), / klʌb steɪk / (BrE)
A small, tender steak cut from the front portion of the short loin of beef, often similar to but smaller than a T-bone steak.

Examples

grilled club steak
Club steaks are usually more affordable than porterhouse or ribeye cuts.
He ordered a club steak at the steakhouse for his birthday dinner.
The chef recommended grilling the club steak over high heat for the best flavor.
