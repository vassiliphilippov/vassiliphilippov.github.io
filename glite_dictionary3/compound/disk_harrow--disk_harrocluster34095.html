disk harrow

noun
agricultural implement with angled disks
Pronunciation: / dɪsk ˈhæroʊ / (AmE), / dɪsk ˈharəʊ / (BrE)
an agricultural implement with a series of concave disks set on edge at an angle, used for breaking up and smoothing soil

Examples

disk harrow the field
After plowing, the disk harrow was run over the soil to break up large clods.
