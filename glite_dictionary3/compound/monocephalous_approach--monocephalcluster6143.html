monocephalous approach

noun
unified or single-minded method
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˈsɛfələs əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈsɛfələs əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A method or way of thinking characterized by a single guiding principle or authority, lacking diversity of perspectives.

Examples

a monocephalous approach to decision-making
Relying on a monocephalous approach can sometimes hinder creative solutions to complex problems.
The committee favored a monocephalous approach to ensure consistency in decision-making.
Their monocephalous approach to management prioritized efficiency over diversity of opinion.

Related Terms

  • antonym – polycephalous approach: an approach involving multiple centers of authority or viewpoints
  • synonym – unified approach: an approach based on a single, coordinated principle or system
