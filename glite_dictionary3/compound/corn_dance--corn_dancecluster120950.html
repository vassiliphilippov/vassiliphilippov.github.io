corn dance

noun
Native American rain dance ritual
Pronunciation: / kɔrn dæns / (AmE), / kɔːn dɑːns / (BrE)
a traditional rain dance performed by some Native American peoples, especially to ensure a good corn harvest

Examples

attend the corn dance festival
Every summer, the pueblo holds a corn dance to celebrate the planting season.
Many visitors come to witness the colorful costumes and rhythmic drumming of the corn dance.
The corn dance is an important part of their cultural heritage and spiritual practice.
