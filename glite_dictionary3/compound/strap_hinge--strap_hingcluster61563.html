strap hinge

noun
hinge with two long plates for doors or lids
Pronunciation: / stræp hɪndʒ / (AmE), / stræp hɪndʒ / (BrE)
a hinge consisting of two long, narrow plates (straps), one attached to a movable part such as a door or lid and the other fixed to the frame, allowing the part to pivot open or closed

Examples

iron strap hinge
He chose a decorative iron strap hinge to add a rustic touch to the gate.
The barn door swung open smoothly thanks to the new strap hinge installed last week.
The carpenter demonstrated how to mount a strap hinge on the heavy wooden lid.

Related Terms

  • synonym – butt hinge: a type of hinge with rectangular plates, typically used for doors
