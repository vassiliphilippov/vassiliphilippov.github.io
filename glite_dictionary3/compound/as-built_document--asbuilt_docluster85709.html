as-built document

noun
record of completed construction project
Pronunciation: / æz bɪlt ˈdɑːkjəmənt / (AmE), / æz bɪlt ˈdɒkjʊmənt / (BrE)
an official record detailing the final specifications and changes of a construction project as it was actually completed

Examples

as-built document for a building
