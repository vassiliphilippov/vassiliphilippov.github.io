gastrocnemius muscle

noun
large calf muscle in the lower leg
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæstroʊkˈniːmiəs ˈmʌsl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɡæstrəʊkˈniːmɪəs ˈmʌsl / (BrE)
the large muscle at the back of the lower leg forming the main part of the calf, responsible for flexing the foot downward

Examples

gastrocnemius muscle injury
A torn gastrocnemius muscle can significantly limit an athlete’s ability to run or jump.
