foul line

noun
line on a bowling alley; not to be crossed
Pronunciation: / faʊl laɪn / (AmE), / faʊl laɪn / (BrE)
a line across a bowling alley that a player must not cross when delivering the ball

Examples

foul line in bowling alley
If your foot crosses the foul line, your throw is not counted.
She carefully approached the foul line before releasing the ball.
The foul line is clearly marked to ensure fair play in bowling tournaments.
