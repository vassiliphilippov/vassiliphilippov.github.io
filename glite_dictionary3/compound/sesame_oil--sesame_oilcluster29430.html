sesame oil

noun
oil from sesame seeds used for cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛsəmi ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɛsəmi ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow to dark brown oil obtained from sesame seeds, used in cooking and as a flavoring

Examples
