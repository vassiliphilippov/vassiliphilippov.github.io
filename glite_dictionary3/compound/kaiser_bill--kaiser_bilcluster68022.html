Kaiser Bill

proper noun
nickname for German Emperor Wilhelm II (1859–1941)
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪzər bɪl / (AmE), / ˈkaɪzə bɪl / (BrE)
a nickname for Wilhelm II, the German Emperor from 1888 to 1918, especially used in English contexts referring to World War I

Examples

British newspapers often depicted Kaiser Bill as the main antagonist during World War I.
