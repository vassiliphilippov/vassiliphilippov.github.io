death cup

noun
a deadly poisonous white mushroom
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ ˌkʌp / (BrE)
an extremely poisonous white mushroom with a prominent cup-shaped base, distinguished by its white gills; also known as the destroying angel (Amanita species)

Examples

deadly death cup mushroom
Botanists warn that confusing a death cup with an edible mushroom can be fatal.
The death cup is notorious for being mistaken for benign woodland fungi.
The hiker unknowingly picked a death cup and suffered severe poisoning as a result.
