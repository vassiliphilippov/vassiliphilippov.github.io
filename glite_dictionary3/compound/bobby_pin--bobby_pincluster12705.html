bobby pin

noun
flat hairpin for holding hair in place
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːbi pɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɒbi pɪn / (BrE)
A small, flat wire hairpin with prongs pressed tightly together, used to hold hair in place.
