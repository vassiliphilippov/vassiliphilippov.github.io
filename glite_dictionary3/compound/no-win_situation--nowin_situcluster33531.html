no-win situation

noun
an unwinnable or hopeless situation
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊ ˈwɪn sɪt͡ʃuˌeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˈwɪn sɪtjuːˌeɪʃən / (BrE)
a situation in which any possible action will result in a negative or unsatisfactory outcome

Examples

a no-win situation for both sides
It's truly a no-win situation when both teams have to forfeit their spots due to rule violations.
She felt trapped in a no-win situation, knowing that either choice would have negative consequences.
We're facing a no-win situation with these negotiations—any agreement will upset someone.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lose-lose situation: a situation where all outcomes are bad
  • antonym – win-win situation: a situation where all outcomes are beneficial
