kissing bug

noun
large bloodsucking bug of the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪsɪŋ bʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋ bʌɡ / (BrE)
A large bloodsucking bug of Central and South America, known for biting humans near the mouth, potentially transmitting disease.

Examples

dangerous kissing bug

The bite of a kissing bug can cause severe allergic reactions in some people.
