tabasco plant

noun
pepper plant used for hot sauce
Pronunciation: / təˈbæskoʊ plænt / (AmE), / təˈbæskəʊ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant bearing very hot, medium-sized, oblong red peppers, cultivated mainly in the Gulf Coast states for making hot sauce.
