thermos bottle

noun
vacuum bottle for preserving temperature of drinks
Pronunciation: / ˈθɝːmoʊs ˌbɑːt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːmɒs ˌbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A bottle with a vacuum between its inner and outer walls, used to keep liquids hot or cold for extended periods.

Examples

hot tea in a thermos bottle
A stainless steel thermos bottle can keep tea warm all day.
His lunch always includes a thermos bottle filled with hot soup.
She poured coffee into her thermos bottle before heading out on her hike.
