entrance exam

noun
exam for entry to an academic program
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntrəns ɪɡˌzæm / (AmE), / ˈɛntrəns ɪɡˌzæm / (BrE)
an examination that determines a candidate's readiness or qualification for admission to a particular course of study, especially at a school or university

Examples

pass the entrance exam
Many students feel anxious before taking the entrance exam.
She studied all summer to pass the entrance exam for the medical school.
The university requires an entrance exam as part of its admissions process.
