permanent press

noun
wrinkle-resistant treated fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈpɚmənənt prɛs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːmənənt prɛs / (BrE)
A type of fabric that has been chemically treated to resist wrinkles and maintain its shape after washing.
