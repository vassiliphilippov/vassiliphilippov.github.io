part-time girlfriend

noun
casual or non-exclusive female romantic partner
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑrtˌtaɪm ˈɡɜrlˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˌpɑːtˌtaɪm ˈɡɜːlˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
a woman who is someone's romantic partner with only limited commitment or availability, often in a casual or non-exclusive relationship
