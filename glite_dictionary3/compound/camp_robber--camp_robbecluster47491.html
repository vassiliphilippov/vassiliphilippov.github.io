camp robber

noun
a bold North American jay (Canada jay)
Pronunciation: / kæmp ˈrɑbər / (AmE), / kæmp ˈrɒbə / (BrE)
a jay of northern North America, noted for its boldness in taking food from camps; the Canada jay or gray jay.

Examples

camp robber steals food
Early campers nicknamed the Canada jay the camp robber because of its habit of raiding campsites.
The bold camp robber swooped down to steal a sandwich from the picnic table.
We were warned to keep our food sealed to avoid attracting camp robbers.
