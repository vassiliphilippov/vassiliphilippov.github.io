British English orthography

noun
spelling system used in the UK and most Commonwealth countries
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪtɪʃ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔrˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtɪʃ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the conventional spelling system and rules of the English language as used in the United Kingdom and most Commonwealth countries, distinct from American English spelling
