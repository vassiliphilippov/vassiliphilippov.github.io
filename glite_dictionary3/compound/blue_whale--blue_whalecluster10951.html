blue whale

noun
dangerous online challenge
Pronunciation: / blu weəl / (AmE), / bluː weəl / (BrE)
an informal name for a dangerous online social-media challenge reported in the 2010s that encouraged participants to complete harmful tasks and was sometimes linked to self-harm.

Examples

blue whale challenge
Reports described the blue whale challenge spreading across social platforms among teens.
After schools warned parents, authorities investigated alleged blue whale posts encouraging self-harm.
The documentary examined how the blue whale phenomenon spread through private messaging apps.
