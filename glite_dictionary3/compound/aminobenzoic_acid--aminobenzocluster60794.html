aminobenzoic acid

noun
a type of benzoic acid derivative
Pronunciation: / əˌminoʊbɛnˈzoʊɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / əˌmiːnəʊbɛnˈzəʊɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
Any of several crystalline derivatives of benzoic acid in which one or more hydrogen atoms are replaced by an amino group, especially para-aminobenzoic acid.
