aerosol container

noun
pressurized spray dispenser
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr əˌsɑl kənˈteɪnər / (AmE), / ˈeə rəsɒl kənˈteɪnə / (BrE)
A container designed to hold substances under pressure and release them as a fine spray, usually using a propellant gas.

Examples

aerosol container for spray paint
Please dispose of the aerosol container properly, as it may still be pressurized.
She used an aerosol container to apply paint evenly to the surface.
The aerosol container failed to spray after being left in the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pump dispenser: a container that releases liquid by pumping without propellant gas
  • synonym – spray can: a small can that dispenses liquid as a spray
