knee brace

noun
support device worn on the knee
Pronunciation: / niː breɪs / (AmE), / niː breɪs / (BrE)
a support device worn around the knee to provide protection or stability, often used during recovery from injury or for joint weakness

Examples

wear a knee brace
After his surgery, he wore a knee brace to help stabilize the joint during recovery.
She bought a knee brace to prevent further injury while playing basketball.
Some athletes use a knee brace to support weak ligaments during intense training.

Related Terms
