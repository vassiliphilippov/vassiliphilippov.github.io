safety zone

noun
protected area for pedestrians in the roadway
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti zəʊn / (BrE)
an area set apart on a roadway, usually bordered or marked, where pedestrians are protected from vehicle traffic

Examples

safety zone for pedestrians
Crosswalks often have a safety zone in the center for people to wait safely.
Please wait for the bus within the safety zone instead of standing on the curb.
The city installed a new safety zone near the intersection to protect pedestrians from traffic.
