vena phrenica

noun
vein draining the diaphragm
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnə frəˈnɪkə / (AmE), / ˈviːnə frəˈnɪkə / (BrE)
Either of two veins that drain blood from the diaphragm.

Examples

vena phrenica drains diaphragm
Damage to the vena phrenica can lead to impaired blood drainage from the diaphragm.
Surgeons carefully avoided the vena phrenica during the abdominal procedure.
The vena phrenica carries deoxygenated blood away from the diaphragm.
