open house

noun
public event with open hospitality
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpən ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
an informal event where people are invited to visit, usually with hospitality for all who come

Examples

open house event
During the holidays, many families host an open house to welcome friends and relatives.
The school organized an open house for prospective students and their families.
We held an open house so our neighbors could come and get to know us.
