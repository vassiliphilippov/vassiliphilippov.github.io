Rob Roy

proper noun
Scottish folk hero and outlaw
Pronunciation: / rɑb ˈrɔɪ / (AmE), / rɒb ˈrɔɪ / (BrE)
(1671–1734) A Scottish clan leader and outlaw, Robert Roy MacGregor, who became a folk hero and was immortalized in Sir Walter Scott's 1817 novel.
