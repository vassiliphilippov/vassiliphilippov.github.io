White House

noun
U.S. president's residence; executive branch (metonym)
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪt ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
the executive branch and official residence of the President of the United States, often used metonymically to refer to the administration or its actions

Examples

the White House announced
Congress is waiting for the White House to respond to the proposed legislation.
Journalists gathered outside the White House for the daily briefing.
The White House announced new policies on climate change today.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Oval Office: the official office of the President of the United States in the White House
  • synonym – administration: the government in power, especially in the United States
