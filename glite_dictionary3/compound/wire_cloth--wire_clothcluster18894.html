wire cloth

noun
fabric made of woven metal wire
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚ klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə klɒθ / (BrE)
A fabric woven from metallic wires, used for sieves, screens, and industrial applications.

Examples

wire cloth for sieves
Wire cloth is often used in construction to reinforce plaster or concrete.
