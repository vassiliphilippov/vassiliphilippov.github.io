Cape Horn

proper noun
southernmost headland of South America
Pronunciation: / keɪp hɔrn / (AmE), / keɪp hɔːn / (BrE)
A rocky headland belonging to Chile, located at the southernmost tip of South America, south of Tierra del Fuego.
