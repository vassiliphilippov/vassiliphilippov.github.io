broken trust

noun
loss of confidence in someone
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkən trʌst / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən trʌst / (BrE)
the loss of confidence or faith in someone due to their actions or behavior

Examples

broken trust in a relationship
