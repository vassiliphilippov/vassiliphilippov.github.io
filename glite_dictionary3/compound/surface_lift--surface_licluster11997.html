surface lift

noun
ski lift pulling riders without leaving the ground
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfɪs lɪft / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs lɪft / (BrE)
a ski tow that pulls skiers up a slope without lifting them off the ground

Examples
