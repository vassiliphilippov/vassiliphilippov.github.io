aniseed oil

noun
essential oil from anise seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪˌsid ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈænɪsiːd ɔɪl / (BrE)
an essential oil obtained from the seeds of the anise plant, Pimpinella anisum, with a strong licorice-like aroma and flavor

Examples

aniseed oil flavoring cakes
A drop of aniseed oil can enhance the flavor of baked goods.
Many candies are flavored with aniseed oil to give them a licorice taste.
Pharmaceutical cough syrups sometimes contain aniseed oil as an ingredient.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anise: a plant whose seeds are used as a spice or for essential oil
  • synonym – star anise: a spice from an unrelated plant, also with a licorice flavor
