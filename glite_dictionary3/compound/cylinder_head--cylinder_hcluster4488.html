cylinder head

noun
detachable cover for engine or pump cylinder
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlɪndɚ hɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪndə hɛd / (BrE)
a detachable cover that closes the top of a cylinder in an internal combustion engine or pump, often housing parts such as valves and spark plugs
