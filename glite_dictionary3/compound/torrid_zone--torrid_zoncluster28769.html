Torrid Zone

noun
tropical region of the Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrɪd zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtɒrɪd zəʊn / (BrE)
the region of the Earth's surface between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn, known for its consistently hot climate
