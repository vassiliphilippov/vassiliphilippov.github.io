red shirt

noun
member of a socialist group
Pronunciation: / rɛd ʃɝt / (AmE), / rɛd ʃɜːt / (BrE)
a member of a socialist or leftist group, especially identified by wearing a red shirt as a symbol of socialism or communism

Some historical movements are remembered for their distinctive red shirts and leftist slogans.
