banded water snake

noun
a North American water-dwelling snake with dark bands
Pronunciation: / ˈbæn.dɪd ˈwɔ.tɚ sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd ˈwɔː.tə sneɪk / (BrE)
a nonvenomous snake (Nerodia fasciata) found in the western United States, known for its dark bands and preference for habitats near water
