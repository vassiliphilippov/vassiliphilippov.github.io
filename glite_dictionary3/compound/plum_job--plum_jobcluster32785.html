plum job

noun
desirable or prestigious job
Pronunciation: / plʌm dʒɑb / (AmE), / plʌm dʒɒb / (BrE)
a job or position that is considered highly desirable, often because of good pay, benefits, or prestige

Examples

a plum job offer
After years of hard work, she was offered a plum job at a global consulting company.
He finally got a plum job with a leading law firm in the city.
Many people apply for the plum job because of its generous benefits and flexible hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dead-end job: a job with no chance of advancement or improvement
  • synonym – dream job: a job that is perfectly suited to one's ambitions or desires
