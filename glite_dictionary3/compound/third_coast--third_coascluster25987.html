Third Coast

proper noun
nickname for the Great Lakes region
Pronunciation: / θɜrd koʊst / (AmE), / θɜːd kəʊst / (BrE)
a nickname for the Great Lakes region of the United States, especially the area around Lake Michigan, highlighting its cultural and geographical significance as a 'third coast' alongside the East and West Coasts.

Examples

the Third Coast region
Food festivals on the Third Coast attract visitors from all over the country.
Many artists and musicians are drawn to the Third Coast because of its vibrant creative scene.
She moved to the Third Coast to experience the mix of city life and lakefront beauty.
