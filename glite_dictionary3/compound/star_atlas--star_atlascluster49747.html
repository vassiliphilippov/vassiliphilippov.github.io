star atlas

noun
book of astronomical star maps
Pronunciation: / stɑr ˈætləs / (AmE), / stɑː ˈatləs / (BrE)
a book or collection of maps showing the positions of stars and constellations in the night sky, often organized by time or region

Examples

star atlas for stargazing
Before digital apps, astronomers relied on a star atlas to navigate the night sky.
He used a star atlas to identify the constellation he saw through his telescope.
The observatory gift shop sold a beautifully illustrated star atlas for amateur astronomers.
