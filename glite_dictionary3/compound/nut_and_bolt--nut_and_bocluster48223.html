nut and bolt

noun
type of fastener using a screw and nut
Pronunciation: / nʌt ənd boʊlt / (AmE), / nʌt ənd bəʊlt / (BrE)
a fastener consisting of a bolt and a matching nut, used together to join materials securely by means of a screw thread
