German police dog

noun
breed of large working dog
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜrmən pəˈlis dɒg / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən pəˈliːs dɒg / (BrE)
A breed of large, intelligent dog (the German Shepherd), known for its use in police work and as a guide for the blind.
