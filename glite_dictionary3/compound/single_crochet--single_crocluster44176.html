single crochet

noun
a basic crochet stitch
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl kroʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl krəʊˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
A basic crochet stitch in which a hook is inserted into a stitch, yarn is drawn through, and a single loop is completed.
