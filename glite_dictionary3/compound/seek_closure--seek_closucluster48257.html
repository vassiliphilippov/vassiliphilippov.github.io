to seek closure

verb
try to find emotional resolution
Pronunciation: / siːk ˈkloʊʒɚ / (AmE), / siːk ˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
to try to achieve a sense of emotional resolution, especially after a painful or troubling experience

Examples

Many victims attend court hearings to seek closure after a crime has been committed against them.
