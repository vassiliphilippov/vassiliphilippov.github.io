whistleblower

noun
person exposing organizational wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪsəlˌbloʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɪsəlˌbləʊə / (BrE)
a person who reports secret or illegal activity within an organization, usually to stop wrongdoing
