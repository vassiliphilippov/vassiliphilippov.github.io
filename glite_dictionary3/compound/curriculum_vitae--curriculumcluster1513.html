curriculum vitae

noun
summary of academic and work history
Pronunciation: / kəˌrɪkjələm ˈviˌtaɪ / (AmE), / kəˌrɪkjʊləm ˈviːtaɪ / (BrE)
A written summary of a person's education, qualifications, and previous experience, typically sent with a job application.

Examples

curriculum vitae for a job
Applicants are asked to submit a curriculum vitae and a cover letter with their application.
She keeps her curriculum vitae updated with her latest achievements.
The interview panel reviewed each candidate's curriculum vitae before shortlisting potential hires.

Related Terms

  • synonym – résumé: brief summary of a person's qualifications and experience
