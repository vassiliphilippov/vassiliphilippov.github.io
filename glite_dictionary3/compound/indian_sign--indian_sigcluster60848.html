Indian sign

noun
an omen or portent indicating action
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən saɪn / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən saɪn / (BrE)
an omen or sign believed to indicate what action should be taken or what will happen, often regarded as portentous or supernatural

Examples

an Indian sign of trouble
He took it as an Indian sign to move forward.
Many superstitious people believe in the power of the Indian sign.
She hesitated, thinking the odd coincidence might be an Indian sign to wait.
