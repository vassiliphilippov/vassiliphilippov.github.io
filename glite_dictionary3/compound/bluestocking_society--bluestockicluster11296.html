bluestocking society

noun
group of literary-minded women
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌstɑkɪŋ səˈsaɪəti / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌstɒkɪŋ səˈsaɪəti / (BrE)
a group or society of educated women who gather to discuss literature and intellectual topics, especially in the 18th and 19th centuries
