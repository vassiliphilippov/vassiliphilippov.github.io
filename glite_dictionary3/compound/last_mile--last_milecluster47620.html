last mile

noun
final walk to execution (prisoner)
Pronunciation: / læst maɪl / (AmE), / lɑːst maɪl / (BrE)
the final walk taken by a condemned prisoner from their cell to the place of execution.

Examples

walk the last mile
He dreaded the moment he would walk his own last mile.
Reading about the last mile always gives me chills; it symbolizes finality.
The prisoner took the last mile in silence, surrounded by guards.

Related Terms

  • synonym – green mile: the walk to the execution chamber for condemned prisoners (used especially in U.S. prisons)
