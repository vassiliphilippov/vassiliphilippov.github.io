people of colour

noun
non-white racial or ethnic groups
Pronunciation: / ˈpipəl əv ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpiːpl əv ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
persons belonging to racial groups with skin pigmentation different from that of white people, especially Black, Asian, and other non-white groups
