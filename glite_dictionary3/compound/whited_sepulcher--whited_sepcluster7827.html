whited sepulcher

noun
hypocrite; outwardly good, inwardly corrupt person
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtɪd ˈsɛpəlkɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtɪd ˈsɛp(ə)lkə / (BrE)
