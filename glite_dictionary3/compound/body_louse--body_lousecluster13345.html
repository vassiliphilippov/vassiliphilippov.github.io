body louse

noun
parasitic insect infesting the human body
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi laʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi laʊs / (BrE)
a parasitic insect that lives on clothing and feeds on the blood of humans, often causing itching and potentially spreading disease

Examples

a body louse infestation
She was diagnosed with a body louse problem after returning from her trip.
The spread of typhus has historically been linked to the presence of body louse infestations.
Unlike a head louse, a body louse lives in clothing, not in the hair.

  • synonym – head louse: parasitic louse infesting the scalp of humans
