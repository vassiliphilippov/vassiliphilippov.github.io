puffball mushroom

noun
round, spore-releasing fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌfˌbɔl ˈmʌʃˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈpʌfbɔːl ˈmʌʃrʊm / (BrE)
A type of round, ball-shaped fungus that releases spores in a cloud when mature and disturbed, commonly found in fields and forests.

Examples

giant puffball mushroom
It is best to identify a puffball mushroom carefully before considering it edible.
