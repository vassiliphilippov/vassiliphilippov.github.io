pressure level

noun
amount of force per unit area
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃər ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
the amount of force exerted per unit area on a surface, usually measured in pascals or dynes.

Examples

pressure level in the tank
He adjusted the valve to decrease the pressure level in the tank.
Loud sounds can be harmful if the pressure level exceeds safe limits.
The technician recorded the pressure level inside the container every hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pressure: the continuous physical force exerted on or against an object by something in contact with it
