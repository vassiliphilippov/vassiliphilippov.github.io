nitrogen oxide

noun
group of nitrogen-oxygen chemical compounds
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪ.tɹə.dʒən ˈɑk.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.tɹɪ.dʒən ˈɒk.saɪd / (BrE)
any of several chemical compounds consisting of nitrogen and oxygen, especially those found in car emissions and air pollution

Examples
