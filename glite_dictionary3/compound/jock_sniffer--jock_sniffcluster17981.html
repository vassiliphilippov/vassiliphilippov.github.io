jock sniffer

noun
person who fawns over athletes (slang)
Pronunciation: / dʒɑk ˈsnɪfər / (AmE), / dʒɒk ˈsnɪfə / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) A person who excessively admires, flatters, or seeks the attention of athletes, especially in an obsequious or sycophantic way.

Examples

annoying jock sniffer
He's such a jock sniffer that he memorizes every stat about the team and never misses a game.
Nobody wanted to be called a jock sniffer in high school because it meant you had no independence.
The coach grew tired of jock sniffers pestering him for autographs after practice.
