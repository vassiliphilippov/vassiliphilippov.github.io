to angle-park

verb
to park a car at an angle to the curb
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəl pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡl pɑːk / (BrE)
to park a vehicle at an angle to the curb rather than parallel to it

Examples

to angle-park a car
Drivers are required to angle-park their cars along this section of the street.
He angle-parked quickly before jumping out to catch the train.
