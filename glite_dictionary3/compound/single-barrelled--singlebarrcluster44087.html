single-barrelled

adjective
having one barrel (firearms)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋɡəl ˈbærəld / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡl ˈbærəld / (BrE)
(of a firearm) having only one barrel through which bullets or shot can be fired.

Examples

single-barrelled shotgun
He owns a classic single-barrelled shotgun that belonged to his grandfather.
Most traditional muskets are single-barrelled by design.
The hunter chose a single-barrelled rifle for its light weight and accuracy.
