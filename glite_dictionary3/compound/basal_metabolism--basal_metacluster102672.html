basal metabolism

noun
energy used for basic body functions at rest
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.səl məˈtæb.əˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.səl məˈtæb.ɪl.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
the minimal amount of energy required to maintain vital bodily functions in a resting state
