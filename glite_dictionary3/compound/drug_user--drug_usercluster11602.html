drug user

noun
person who uses drugs
Pronunciation: / drʌɡ ˈjuːzər / (AmE), / drʌɡ ˈjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who habitually or occasionally uses drugs, especially illegal or recreational drugs

Examples

a drug user in recovery
Many drug users struggle with social stigma and limited access to treatment services.
She became a drug user during her teenage years, which affected her education.
The organization provides support and healthcare for every drug user in the community.
