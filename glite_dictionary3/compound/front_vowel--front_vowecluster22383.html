front vowel

noun
vowel sound made at front of mouth
Pronunciation: / frʌnt ˈvaʊəl / (AmE), / frʌnt ˈvaʊəl / (BrE)
A vowel sound produced with the tongue positioned towards the front of the mouth, such as the vowel sounds in 'man' or 'gel'.
