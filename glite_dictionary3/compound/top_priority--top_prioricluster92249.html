top priority

noun
most important concern or task
Pronunciation: / tɑp praɪˈɔrəti / (AmE), / tɒp praɪˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the task, goal, or matter that is considered most important and given the highest level of attention

Examples

When resources are limited, safety must remain the top priority.
