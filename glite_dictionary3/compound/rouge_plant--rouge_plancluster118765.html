rouge plant

noun
tropical bushy plant with scarlet berries
Pronunciation: / ruːʒ plænt / (AmE), / ruːʒ plɑːnt / (BrE)
A bushy tropical houseplant with white to pale pink flowers, followed by clusters of bright scarlet berries; native to the Americas.
