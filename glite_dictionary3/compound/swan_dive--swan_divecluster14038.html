swan dive

noun
dive with arched back and outstretched arms
Pronunciation: / swɑn daɪv / (AmE), / swɒn daɪv / (BrE)
A dive in which the diver arches the back and stretches out the arms before entering the water, resembling the shape of a swan.

Examples

perfect swan dive
His swan dive won the highest marks from the judges at the competition.
She learned how to perform a graceful swan dive during her swimming lessons.
The diver executed a perfect swan dive from the ten-meter platform.
