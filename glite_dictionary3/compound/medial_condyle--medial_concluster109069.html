medial condyle

noun
inner lower projection of femur bone
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiəl ˈkɑnˌdaɪl / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiəl ˈkɒndaɪl / (BrE)
the rounded projection on the inner side at the lower end of the femur, forming part of the knee joint

Examples

medial condyle of femur
An x-ray showed a small chip out of the medial condyle after the accident.
Swelling near the medial condyle can indicate an injury to the inner knee.
The surgeon repaired the fractured medial condyle during the knee operation.
