to bullet vote

verb
to concentrate votes on one candidate
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlət voʊt / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪt vəʊt / (BrE)
to cast multiple votes for a single candidate rather than distributing them among several candidates, especially in cumulative voting systems

Examples
