USS Constitution

proper noun
historic U.S. Navy frigate (Old Ironsides)
Pronunciation: / ˌju ɛs ɛs kənˈstɪtʃuˌʃən / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɛs ɛs kənˈstɪtjuːʃən / (BrE)
a historic United States Navy frigate launched in 1797, renowned for its victories in the War of 1812 and preserved as a museum ship in Boston.
