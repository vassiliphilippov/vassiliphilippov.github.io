flatbed press

noun
printing press with flat type bed
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌbɛd ˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈflætbɛd ˌprɛs / (BrE)
a type of printing press in which the type or image is arranged on a flat surface and the paper is pressed onto it by a rolling cylinder
