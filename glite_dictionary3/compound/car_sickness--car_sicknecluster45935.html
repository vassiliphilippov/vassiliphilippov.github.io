car sickness

noun
nausea from riding in a car
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr ˌsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈkɑː ˌsɪknəs / (BrE)
A feeling of nausea and discomfort caused by motion while riding in a car.

Examples

car sickness during travel
Car sickness can make even short drives unpleasant for some people.
My little brother often experiences car sickness on long road trips.
She took medicine to prevent car sickness before the journey.
