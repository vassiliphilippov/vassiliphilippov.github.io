Virginian sumac

noun
deciduous shrub or small tree of eastern North America
Pronunciation: / vərˈdʒɪniən ˈsuːmæk / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪniən ˈsjuːmæk / (BrE)
A deciduous shrubby tree native to eastern North America, with compound leaves that turn brilliant red in autumn and dense clusters of greenish-yellow flowers followed by crimson, acidic berries.
