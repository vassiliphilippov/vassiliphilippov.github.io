wheel fiddle

noun
stringed instrument played with a wheel
Pronunciation: / wil ˈfɪdəl / (AmE), / wiːl ˈfɪdl̩ / (BrE)
a stringed musical instrument that produces sound by means of a rotating wheel rubbing against the strings, also known as a hurdy-gurdy
