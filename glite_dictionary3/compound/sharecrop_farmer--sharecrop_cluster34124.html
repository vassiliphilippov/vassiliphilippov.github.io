sharecrop farmer

noun
farmer who works land for a share of crops
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛrˌkrɑp ˈfɑrmər / (AmE), / ˈʃeəkrɒp ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
A farmer, especially a tenant, who cultivates land owned by someone else in return for a share of the crops produced.
