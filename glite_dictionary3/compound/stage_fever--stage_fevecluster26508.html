stage fever

noun
nervousness or excitement before performing
Pronunciation: / steɪdʒ ˈfiːvɚ / (AmE), / steɪdʒ ˈfiːvə / (BrE)
A feeling of nervousness or excitement experienced by performers before going on stage.

Examples

stage fever before performing
Despite her confidence, a touch of stage fever crept in as the audience filled the hall.
Many actors experience stage fever just before the curtain rises.
The band laughed off their stage fever and put on a great show.
