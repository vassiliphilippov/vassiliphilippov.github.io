conveyor belt

noun
moving band for transporting objects
Pronunciation: / kənˈveɪ.ər bɛlt / (AmE), / kənˈveɪ.ə bɛlt / (BrE)
A continuous moving band used for transporting objects from one place to another, especially in factories.

Examples

conveyor belt in a factory
She watched her luggage roll out onto the conveyor belt at the airport.
The factory relies on a conveyor belt to move boxes through each stage of production.
The workers stand beside the conveyor belt inspecting items for quality control.
