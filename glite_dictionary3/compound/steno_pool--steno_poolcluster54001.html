steno pool

noun
group of workplace stenographers
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛnoʊ puːl / (AmE), / ˈstɛnəʊ puːl / (BrE)
a group of stenographers in an organization who work together, especially to provide typing and transcription services for different departments

Examples

work in the steno pool
She was promoted to supervisor after two years in the steno pool.
The steno pool was responsible for preparing all legal documents on short notice.
With the rise of modern technology, the traditional steno pool has become less common.
