pyloric vein

noun
vein draining upper stomach, connects to portal vein
Pronunciation: / paɪˈlɔr.ɪk veɪn / (AmE), / paɪˈlɒr.ɪk veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the upper surfaces of the stomach and empties into the portal vein

Examples

pyloric vein blockage
An obstruction of the pyloric vein can lead to complications in gastric blood flow.
