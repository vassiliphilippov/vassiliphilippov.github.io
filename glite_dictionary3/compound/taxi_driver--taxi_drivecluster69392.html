taxi driver

noun
person who drives a taxi professionally
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksi ˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈtæksi ˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to drive a taxi, carrying passengers to their destinations for a fare.

Examples

taxi driver picks up passengers
After a long flight, we hired a taxi driver to take us home from the airport.
He worked as a taxi driver in London for over twenty years.
The taxi driver knew all the shortcuts and got us to the theater on time.
