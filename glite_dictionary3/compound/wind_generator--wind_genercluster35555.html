wind generator

noun
machine producing electricity from wind
Pronunciation: / wɪnd ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / wɪnd ˈdʒɛnəreɪtə / (BrE)
a machine that produces electrical power by converting the kinetic energy of wind into usable energy

Examples

A modern wind generator can power hundreds of homes using only wind energy.
