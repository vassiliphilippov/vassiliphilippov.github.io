word salad

noun
disorganized, incoherent speech or writing
Pronunciation: / wɝːd ˈsæl.əd / (AmE), / wɜːd ˈsæl.əd / (BrE)
incoherent or nonsensical speech or writing, especially as a symptom of mental illness such as schizophrenia
