nose ring

noun
ring worn in or through the nose
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊz ˌrɪŋ / (AmE), / nəʊz rɪŋ / (BrE)
A ring worn through the nose as a decoration, or placed in an animal's nose to help control it.

Examples

wear a nose ring
In many cultures, wearing a nose ring has significant symbolic meaning.
She decided to get a gold nose ring as a birthday present to herself.
The farmer attached a nose ring to the bull for easier handling.
