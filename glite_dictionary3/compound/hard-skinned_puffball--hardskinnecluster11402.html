hard-skinned puffball

noun
a tough-skinned underground puffball fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrd ˌskɪnd ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːd ˌskɪnd ˈpʌf.bɔːl / (BrE)
