community service

noun
voluntary or required work for the community
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti ˈsɝvɪs /; / kəˈmjunəti ˈsɝvəs / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəti ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
unpaid work done to help people in a community, either voluntarily or as a legal penalty
