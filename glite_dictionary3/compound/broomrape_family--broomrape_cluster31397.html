broomrape family

noun
family of parasitic, leafless herbs (Orobanchaceae)
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːmˌreɪp ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈbruːmreɪp ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family of brown or yellow leafless herbs, sometimes classified in the order Scrophulariales, including the genus Orobanche and related parasitic plants.
