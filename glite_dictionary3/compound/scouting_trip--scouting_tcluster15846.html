scouting trip

noun
expedition to gather information
Pronunciation: / ˈskaʊtɪŋ trɪp / (AmE), / ˈskaʊtɪŋ trɪp / (BrE)
An expedition or journey made to gather information about an area, especially before taking further action.

Examples

scouting trip before camping
Before choosing a campsite, we took a short scouting trip around the area.
The team went on a scouting trip to the mountains before the main expedition.
Their scouting trip revealed several possible routes through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reconnaissance: a military survey or exploratory mission to gain information
