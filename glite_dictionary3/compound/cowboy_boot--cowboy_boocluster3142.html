cowboy boot

noun
a high-heeled western-style boot
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊ.bɔɪ buːt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ.bɔɪ buːt / (BrE)
A high-heeled leather boot with a tall shaft and decorative stitching, traditionally worn by cowboys in the western United States.

Examples

leather cowboy boot
He bought a new pair of cowboy boots for the rodeo.
Her cowboy boots made her outfit stand out at the country music concert.
The shop sold leather cowboy boots in every color imaginable.
