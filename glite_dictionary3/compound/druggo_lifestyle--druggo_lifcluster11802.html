druggo lifestyle

noun
lifestyle involving regular drug use
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡoʊ ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡəʊ ˈlaɪfstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living characterized by regular drug use and the behaviors, attitudes, and patterns commonly associated with drug users, especially in an informal or subcultural context.

Examples
