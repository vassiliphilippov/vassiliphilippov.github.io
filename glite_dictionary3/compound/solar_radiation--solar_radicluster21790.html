solar radiation

noun
radiation from the Sun
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the electromagnetic radiation emitted by the Sun, including visible light, ultraviolet, and infrared radiation, which reaches the Earth
