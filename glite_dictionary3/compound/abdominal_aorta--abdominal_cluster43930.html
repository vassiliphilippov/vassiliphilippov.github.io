abdominal aorta

noun
the part of the aorta in the abdomen
Pronunciation: / æbˈdɑːmɪnəl eɪˈɔːrtə / (AmE), / æbˈdɒmɪnəl eɪˈɔːtə / (BrE)
the portion of the aorta that runs through the abdomen, supplying blood to the abdominal organs and lower body

Examples

ruptured abdominal aorta
A blockage in the abdominal aorta can lead to serious circulation problems in the legs.
An aneurysm of the abdominal aorta requires immediate medical attention.
The surgeon carefully examined the abdominal aorta before proceeding with the operation.
