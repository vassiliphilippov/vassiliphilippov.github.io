eagle freak

noun
enthusiastic fan of the Eagles or eagles
Pronunciation: / ˈiɡəl friːk / (AmE), / ˈiːɡəl friːk / (BrE)
A person who is extremely enthusiastic about the Eagles (especially the Philadelphia Eagles football team), or who is passionate about eagle-related activities.

As an eagle freak, she owns every team jersey ever released.

The club for eagle freaks organizes trips to every home and away match.
