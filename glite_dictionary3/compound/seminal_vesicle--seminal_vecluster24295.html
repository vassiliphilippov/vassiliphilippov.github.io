seminal vesicle

noun
male gland near bladder that secretes seminal fluid
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmɪnəl ˈvɛsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪnəl ˈvɛsɪkəl / (BrE)
either of a pair of glands in males, located near the bladder, that secrete fluid contributing to semen during ejaculation
