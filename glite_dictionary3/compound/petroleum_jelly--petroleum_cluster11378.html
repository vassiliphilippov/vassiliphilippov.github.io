petroleum jelly

noun
semi-solid ointment and lubricant
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊliəm ˈdʒɛli / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊliəm ˈdʒɛli / (BrE)
A soft, semisolid mixture derived from petroleum, used in ointments, skin care, and as a lubricant.

Examples

petroleum jelly on dry skin
Doctors often recommend petroleum jelly to protect small cuts and scrapes.
I applied petroleum jelly to my dry hands before bed.
The mechanic used petroleum jelly to lubricate the gasket.
