letter box

noun
post office compartment for collecting mail
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtər ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈletə ˌbɒks / (BrE)
a numbered compartment in a post office where letters and packages are placed for collection by the person it is assigned to

Examples

post office letter box
He checked his letter box at the post office every morning before work.
The package was waiting for her in her letter box last Friday.
You can rent a letter box if you need a secure place to receive mail.

Related Terms

  • synonym – post office box: a locked box in a post office assigned to a person or organization for receiving mail
