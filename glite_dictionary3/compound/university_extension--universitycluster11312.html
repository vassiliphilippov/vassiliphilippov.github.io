university extension

noun
public courses offered by colleges and universities
Pronunciation: / juˌnɪvərˈsɪti ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / juːˌnɪvəˈsɪti ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
An educational program offered by a college or university to members of the public who are not enrolled as regular students, often including evening or online courses.

Examples

university extension course
Many people take advantage of university extension programs to continue their education.
She enrolled in a university extension course to improve her business skills.
The university extension offers evening classes for working adults.
