silver screen

noun
cinema projection surface
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər skrin / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə skriːn / (BrE)
a white or silvered surface used for projecting and viewing motion pictures, especially in a cinema

Examples

projected on silver screen
