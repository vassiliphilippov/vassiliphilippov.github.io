pastry board

noun
board for kneading dough or pastry
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪstri bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpeɪstri bɔːd / (BrE)
a large flat board, usually made of wood, used as a surface for kneading dough or rolling out pastry
