beef Wellington

noun
beef dish in pastry
Pronunciation: / bif ˈwɛlɪŋtən / (AmE), / biːf ˈwɛlɪŋtən / (BrE)
A dish consisting of rare-roasted beef tenderloin coated with a layer of mushroom paste and wrapped in puff pastry, then baked.
