Chinese magnolia

noun
ornamental Asian shrub or small tree with rosy to purplish flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪˌniz mæɡˈnoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪˌniːz mæɡˈnəʊliə / (BrE)
