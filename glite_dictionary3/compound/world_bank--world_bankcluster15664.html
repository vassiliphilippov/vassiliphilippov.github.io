World Bank

proper noun
international financial institution belonging to the UN
Pronunciation: / wɜrld bæŋk / (AmE), / wɜːld bæŋk / (BrE)
an international financial institution established by the United Nations to provide loans and technical assistance to developing countries, with member governments guaranteeing the loans
