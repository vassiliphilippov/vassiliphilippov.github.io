fence rail

noun
horizontal piece of wood in a fence
Pronunciation: / fɛns reɪl / (AmE), / fɛns reɪl / (BrE)
a long, narrow piece of wood, often split from a log, used horizontally in the construction of a fence

Examples

fence rail made of wood
A split fence rail can be easily fitted into the posts to repair a section of fencing.
He replaced the broken fence rail to keep the cattle from escaping.
The old farm's wooden fence rails had weathered to a silvery gray.
