golden retriever

noun
breed of large, golden-coated dog
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən rɪˈtrivər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən rɪˈtriːvə / (BrE)
a breed of large dog with a long, silky golden coat, known for its friendly temperament and intelligence

Examples

friendly golden retriever
Her golden retriever loves to swim and play fetch in the lake.
The golden retriever wagged its tail as it greeted the children at the park.
They adopted a rescue golden retriever puppy last year.
