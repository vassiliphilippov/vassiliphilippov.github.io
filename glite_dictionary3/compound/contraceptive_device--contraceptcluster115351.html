contraceptive device

noun
device to prevent pregnancy
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈsɛptɪv dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈsɛptɪv dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A device used to prevent pregnancy by impeding conception.

Examples

use a contraceptive device
Many clinics provide free contraceptive devices to encourage responsible family planning.
She decided to use a hormonal contraceptive device for long-term birth control.
The doctor explained how to use each contraceptive device safely and effectively.
