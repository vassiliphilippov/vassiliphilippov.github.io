registered bond

noun
bond with recorded ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd bɑnd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd bɒnd / (BrE)
A bond for which the owner's name is recorded by the issuing organization, and which can be transferred to another owner only when properly endorsed by the registered owner.

Examples

own a registered bond
She invested in a registered bond to ensure her holdings were officially recorded.
Transferring a registered bond requires the endorsement of the listed owner.
Unlike a bearer bond, a registered bond provides greater security if lost or stolen.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bearer bond: a bond not registered to an owner, payable to whoever holds it
