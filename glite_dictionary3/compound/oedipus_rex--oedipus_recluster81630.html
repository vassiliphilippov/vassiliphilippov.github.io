Oedipus Rex

proper noun
tragic king in Greek mythology; play by Sophocles
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdɪpəs ˈrɛks / (AmE), / ˈiːdɪpəs ˈreks / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, a tragic king of Thebes who unknowingly killed his father and married his mother; also the subject of the famous tragedy by Sophocles.
