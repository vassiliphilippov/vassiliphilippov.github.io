gauze bandage

noun
thin cotton cloth bandage
Pronunciation: / ɡɔz ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡɔːz ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
A thin, loosely woven cotton cloth used for bandages and dressings, especially in medical settings.

Examples

gauze bandage for wounds
Ensure the gauze bandage is clean before applying it to the injury.
The nurse wrapped a gauze bandage around his arm to protect the wound.
We keep several gauze bandages in the first aid kit for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bandage: a strip of material used to bind a wound or injured part of the body
  • synonym – dressing: a piece of material applied to a wound to protect it
