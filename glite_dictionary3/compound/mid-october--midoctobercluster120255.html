mid-October

noun
the middle part of October
Pronunciation: / mɪd əkˈtoʊbɚ / (AmE), / mɪd ɒkˈtəʊbə / (BrE)
the period around the middle of October, usually taken as approximately the 10th to 20th of the month.

Examples

mid-October weather
Autumn leaves are at their most colorful in mid-October.
By mid-October, temperatures usually begin to drop noticeably in this region.
The company plans to release the new product in mid-October.
