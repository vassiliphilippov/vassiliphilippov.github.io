great deal

noun
an excellent offer or discount
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt diːl / (AmE), / ɡreɪt diːl / (BrE)
an especially advantageous offer or discount, typically resulting in significant savings or value

Examples

a great deal on shoes

This website is known for offering great deals on travel packages.
