antiprofessional attitude

noun
opposition to professionalism in attitude or behavior
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.taɪ.prəˈfɛʃ.ə.nəl ˈæt.əˌtud / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.prəˈfeʃ.ən.əl ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
an attitude or behavior that is opposed to or undermines professionalism, often characterized by disregard for standards, ethics, or conduct expected in a professional setting
