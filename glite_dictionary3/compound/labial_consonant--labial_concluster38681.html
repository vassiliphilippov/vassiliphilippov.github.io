labial consonant

noun
consonant articulated with the lips
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bi.əl ˈkɑn.sə.nənt / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bɪ.əl ˈkɒn.sə.nənt / (BrE)
a consonant sound in which the lips are used to produce the articulation, such as 'p', 'b', and 'm'
