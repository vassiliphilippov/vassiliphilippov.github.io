hiatus hernia

noun
protrusion of the stomach through the diaphragm
Pronunciation: / haɪˈeɪtəs ˈhɜrniə / (AmE), / hʌɪˈeɪtəs ˈhɜːnɪə / (BrE)
A condition in which part of the stomach protrudes upward through the diaphragm into the chest cavity.
