butt shaft

noun
blunt practice arrow in archery
Pronunciation: / bʌt ʃæft / (AmE), / bʌt ʃɑːft / (BrE)
A blunt arrow without a barb, used in archery for target practice.

Examples

butt shaft for archery practice
He replaced the sharp tip with a butt shaft for safe training sessions.
Museums often display a butt shaft alongside hunting arrows to show the difference.
