social organisation

noun
system of relationships in society
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the structure or system of relationships and roles among people within a society or group
