black-capped chickadee

noun
North American songbird with a black cap
Pronunciation: / blæk kæpt ˈtʃɪkədi / (AmE), / blak kæpt ˈtʃɪkədi / (BrE)
a small North American songbird with a black cap and bib, white cheeks, and grayish body, known for its distinctive call
