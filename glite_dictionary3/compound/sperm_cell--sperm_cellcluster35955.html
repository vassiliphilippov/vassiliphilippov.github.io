sperm cell

noun
male reproductive cell
Pronunciation: / spɝm sɛl / (AmE), / spɜːm sɛl / (BrE)
The male reproductive cell that fertilizes the female egg cell in sexual reproduction.

Examples

sperm cell fertilizes egg
Each sperm cell contains half the genetic material required to form a new organism.
Scientists observed the movement of a sperm cell under the microscope.
The sperm cell unites with the egg cell during fertilization.
