cardiac glucoside

noun
heart-stimulating plant compound
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdiæk ˈɡluːkoʊsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdiæk ˈɡluːkəsaɪd / (BrE)
a compound obtained from certain plants, used to stimulate the heart in cases of heart failure
