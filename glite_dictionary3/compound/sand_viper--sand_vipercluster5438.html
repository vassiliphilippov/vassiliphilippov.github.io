sand viper

noun
a venomous viper of North Africa and Southwest Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈsænd ˌvaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈsænd ˌvaɪpə / (BrE)
a highly venomous viper found in northern Africa and southwestern Asia, distinguished by a horny spine above each eye
