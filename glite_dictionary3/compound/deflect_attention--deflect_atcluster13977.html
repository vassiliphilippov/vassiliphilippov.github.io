to deflect attention

verb
redirect focus away from something
Pronunciation: / dɪˈflɛkt əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈflɛkt əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
to cause people's focus to move away from something, especially to avoid criticism or scrutiny

Examples

to deflect attention from problems
Politicians often try to deflect attention from scandals during interviews.
