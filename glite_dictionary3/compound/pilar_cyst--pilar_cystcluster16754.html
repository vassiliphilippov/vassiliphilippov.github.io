pilar cyst

noun
skin cyst filled with sebum, often on the scalp
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlər sɪst / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlə sɪst / (BrE)
a common cyst of the skin, usually occurring on the scalp, filled with sebum due to blockage of a sebaceous gland

Examples

pilar cyst on scalp
A pilar cyst is usually harmless but can become infected if irritated.
During surgery, several pilar cysts were found on the patient's scalp.
The doctor diagnosed the lump on her scalp as a pilar cyst and recommended removal.
