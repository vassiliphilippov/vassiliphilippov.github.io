dot dot dot

noun
three-dot icon for more options
Pronunciation: / d51t d51t d51t / (AmE), / d52t d52t d52t / (BrE)
a small graphical icon of three dots (often vertically or horizontally aligned) in a software interface that opens a menu of additional actions or settings when tapped or clicked.

Examples

tap the dot dot dot button
She tapped the dot dot dot in the corner to open the options menu.
On the messaging app, press the dot dot dot to forward or delete a conversation quickly.
Developers often hide settings behind a small dot dot dot so the interface looks cleaner for users.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ellipsis: the typographic series of three dots (...) indicating omission or continuation; the visual origin of the icon
  • synonym – overflow menu: a menu that contains additional actions or settings not shown in the main interface
