nuclear-powered submarine

noun
submarine powered by nuclear energy
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːkliɚ ˌpaʊɚd ˌsʌbməˈriːn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːklɪə ˌpaʊəd ˌsʌbməˈriːn / (BrE)
