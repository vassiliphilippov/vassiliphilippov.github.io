chili pepper

noun
hot, spicy fruit used as seasoning
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪli ˌpɛpər /; / ˈtʃɪli ˌpepər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli ˌpɛpə /; / ˈtʃɪli ˌpepə / (BrE)
a small, hot and spicy red or green pod (fruit) used to add heat and flavor to food; also, the plant that produces these pods
