stuffing nut

noun
nut used to tighten a stuffing box
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌf.ɪŋ nʌt / (AmE), / ˈstʌf.ɪŋ nʌt / (BrE)
a nut used to compress packing in a stuffing box to prevent leakage around a moving shaft
