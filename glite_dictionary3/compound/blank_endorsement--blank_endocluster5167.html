blank endorsement

noun
undesignated endorsement on negotiable instrument
Pronunciation: / blæŋk ɪnˈdɔrsmənt / (AmE), / blæŋk ɪnˈdɔːsmənt / (BrE)
an endorsement on a negotiable instrument that does not specify a payee, making it payable to whoever holds it

Examples

blank endorsement on a check
A blank endorsement allows anyone holding the instrument to cash or deposit it.
He was advised not to use a blank endorsement for large checks due to security concerns.
To transfer the check, simply sign your name as a blank endorsement on the back.
