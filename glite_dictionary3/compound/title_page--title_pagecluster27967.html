title page

noun
page in a book with title, author, publisher
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪt̬l̩ ˌpeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtl̩ ˌpeɪdʒ / (BrE)
the page at the beginning of a book that shows the title, author, and publisher

Examples

title page of a book
