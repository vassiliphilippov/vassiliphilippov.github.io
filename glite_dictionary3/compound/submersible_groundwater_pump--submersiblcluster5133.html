submersible groundwater pump

noun
pump for extracting underground water
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɜrsəbl ˈɡraʊndˌwɔtɚ pʌmp / (AmE), / səbˈmɜːsəbəl ˈɡraʊndˌwɔːtə pʌmp / (BrE)
A pump designed to be submerged in water and used to sample or extract water from underground sources.
