wild yam

noun
plant with medicinal rhizome
Pronunciation: / waɪld jæm / (AmE), / waɪld jam / (BrE)
A plant with an underground rhizome that was formerly dried and used as a remedy for rheumatism or liver disorders.

Examples

wild yam supplement
Researchers are studying the potential benefits of wild yam supplements.
Some herbalists recommend wild yam for easing symptoms of rheumatism.
