right of first publication

noun
exclusive right to first publish a work
Pronunciation: / raɪt əv fɜrst ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / raɪt əv fɜːst ˌpʌblɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
