storm lamp

noun
lamp with glass chimney for wind protection
Pronunciation: / stɔrm læmp / (AmE), / stɔːm læmp / (BrE)
An oil lamp or candlestick fitted with a glass chimney and a perforated metal lid to protect the flame from wind.

Examples

storm lamp with glass chimney
During the blackout, they relied on a storm lamp for light.
She lit a storm lamp to illuminate the campsite during the windy night.
The old storm lamp cast a warm glow in the rustic cabin.
