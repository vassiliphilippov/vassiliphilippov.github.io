ripple mark

noun
small sand ridge formed by water or wind
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpəl mɑrk / (AmE), / ˈrɪpəl mɑːk / (BrE)
a small ridge formed on sand or sediment by the movement of water currents or wind

Examples

ripple mark in sand
Geologists studied the ripple marks to learn about ancient water currents.
The ripple marks in the sand were created by the outgoing tide.
Walking along the beach, she admired the ripple marks left by the gentle breeze.
