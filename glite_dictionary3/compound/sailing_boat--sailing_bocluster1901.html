sailing boat

noun
small boat propelled by sails
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlɪŋ ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈseɪlɪŋ bəʊt / (BrE)
a small boat that is propelled by sails, usually having a single mast

Examples

small sailing boat
Many children learn basic navigation skills on a small sailing boat at summer camp.
The old sailing boat moved slowly across the lake under a gentle breeze.
We rented a sailing boat for the weekend and explored the quiet river.

Related Terms

  • antonym – motorboat: a boat propelled by an engine, not sails
  • synonym – sailboat: a boat propelled by sails; chiefly used in American English
