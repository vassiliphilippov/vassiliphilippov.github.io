fibroid tumor

noun
benign tumor of fibrous tissue, esp. in uterus
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪ.brɔɪd ˈtu.mɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.brɔɪd ˈtjuː.mə / (BrE)
