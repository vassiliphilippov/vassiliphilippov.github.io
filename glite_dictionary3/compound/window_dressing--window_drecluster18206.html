window dressing

noun
decoration of shop windows
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
The arrangement and decoration of items in the windows of a shop to attract customers.

Examples

window dressing for shops
