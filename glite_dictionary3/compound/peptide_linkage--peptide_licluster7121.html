peptide linkage

noun
chemical bond between amino acids in proteins
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛp.taɪd ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɛp.taɪd ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ / (BrE)
the chemical bond formed between the carboxyl group of one amino acid and the amino group of another, linking them in a protein or peptide chain
