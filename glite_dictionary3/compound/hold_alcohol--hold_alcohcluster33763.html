to hold alcohol

verb
to tolerate drinking alcohol well
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ˈælkəhɔl / (AmE), / həʊld ˈælkəhɒl / (BrE)
to be able to drink alcoholic beverages without becoming noticeably drunk or incapacitated

Examples
