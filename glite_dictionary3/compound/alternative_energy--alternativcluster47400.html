alternative energy

noun
energy from environmentally friendly sources
Pronunciation: / ɔlˈtɝnətɪv ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / ɒlˈtɜːnətɪv ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
energy obtained from sources that do not deplete natural resources or damage the environment, such as solar, wind, or hydroelectric power

Examples

alternative energy sources
Many governments are investing in alternative energy to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.
Switching to alternative energy can significantly lower greenhouse gas emissions.
The company committed to using alternative energy sources like wind and solar.
