Pehlevi language

noun
ancient Iranian language, 3rd–10th c.
Pronunciation: / pɛhˈleɪvi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / pɛhˈleɪvi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
An Iranian language used in Zoroastrian literature from the 3rd to the 10th centuries, also known as Middle Persian.
