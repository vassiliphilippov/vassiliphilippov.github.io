bird dog

noun
a hunting dog for locating or retrieving birds
Pronunciation: / bɝd dɔg / (AmE), / bɜːd dɒg / (BrE)
a dog trained to locate and retrieve birds, especially game birds, for hunters

Examples

trained bird dog
After many hours of training, the bird dog was able to locate quail with ease.
Several breeds, such as the Labrador retriever, can be trained as a bird dog.
The hunter relied on his bird dog to flush pheasants from the tall grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gun dog: a dog trained to assist hunters by finding and retrieving game
