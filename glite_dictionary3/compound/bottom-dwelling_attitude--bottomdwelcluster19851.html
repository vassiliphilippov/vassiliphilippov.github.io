bottom-dwelling attitude

noun
pessimistic or negative outlook
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtəm ˌdwɛlɪŋ æˈtɪtjud / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm ˌdwɛlɪŋ əˈtɪtjuːd / (BrE)
A consistently negative or pessimistic outlook; a tendency to focus on the worst aspects of situations.

Examples

negative bottom-dwelling attitude
Despite the team's achievements, he maintained a bottom-dwelling attitude throughout the season.
Don't let his bottom-dwelling attitude influence your excitement about the project.
Her bottom-dwelling attitude made it hard for her to enjoy good news.

Related Terms

  • antonym – optimistic outlook: a tendency to see the best in situations
  • synonym – pessimism: the belief that the worst will happen
