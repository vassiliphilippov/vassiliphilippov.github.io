process cheese

noun
cheese made by blending several cheeses
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.ses tʃiːz / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.ses tʃiːz / (BrE)
a type of cheese made by blending several varieties of natural cheese, often with emulsifying agents, to produce a uniform and meltable product

Examples
