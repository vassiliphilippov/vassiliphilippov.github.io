standard atmosphere

noun
unit of pressure (atm)
Pronunciation: / ˌstændɚd ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˌstændəd ˈætməsˌfɪə / (BrE)
a unit of pressure equal to the pressure required to support a column of mercury 760 millimetres high at sea level and 0°C (approximately 101,325 pascals)

Examples

one standard atmosphere pressure
At higher altitudes, the air pressure drops below a standard atmosphere.
Engineers often use the standard atmosphere when calibrating instruments.
The pressure at sea level is defined as one standard atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym – atmosphere: unit of pressure, sometimes abbreviated as 'atm'
  • synonym – pascal: SI unit of pressure, equal to one newton per square metre
