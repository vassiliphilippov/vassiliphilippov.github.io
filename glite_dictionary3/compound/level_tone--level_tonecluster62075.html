level tone

noun
calm, even voice without emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl toʊn / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl təʊn / (BrE)
a calm and even manner of speaking, without showing emotion or variation in pitch

Examples

speak in a level tone
Even when delivering bad news, she speaks in a level tone to avoid upsetting her audience.
He tried to keep a level tone during the negotiation, never betraying his true feelings.
She gave her instructions in a level tone, making it clear but unemotional.

Related Terms

  • antonym – emphatic: showing or giving emphasis; expressing something forcibly and clearly
  • synonym – monotone: a voice or sound that does not change in pitch
