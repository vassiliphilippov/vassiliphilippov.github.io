Ural Mountains

proper noun
mountain range dividing Europe and Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊrəl ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəl ˈmaʊntənz / (BrE)
a mountain range in western Russia that stretches from the Arctic Ocean to the Caspian Sea, forming a traditional boundary between Europe and Asia

Examples

the Ural Mountains separate continents
Many minerals are mined in the Ural Mountains, making the region rich in natural resources.
The Ural Mountains are often cited as the geographical divide between Europe and Asia.
The train journey across the Ural Mountains offers stunning views of forests and rugged peaks.
