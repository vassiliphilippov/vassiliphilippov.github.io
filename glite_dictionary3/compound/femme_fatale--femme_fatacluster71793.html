femme fatale

noun
seductively dangerous woman
Pronunciation: / fɛm fəˈtæl / (AmE), / fam faˈtal / (BrE)
a mysterious or seductive woman who is considered to be dangerously attractive, often leading men into compromising or dangerous situations

Examples

a femme fatale character
Her mysterious aura and calculated charm made her the perfect femme fatale in the detective novel.
In classic film noir, the femme fatale often lures the hero into danger.
Many legends feature a femme fatale whose beauty brings misfortune to those who fall for her.

Related Terms

  • synonym – seductress: a woman who seduces someone, especially one who does so deliberately
  • synonym – vamp: a woman who uses her sexual attractiveness to exploit men
