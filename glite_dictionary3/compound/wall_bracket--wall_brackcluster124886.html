wall bracket

noun
support fixed to wall for holding a shelf
Pronunciation: / wɔl ˈbrækət / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈbrækɪt / (BrE)
A support attached to a wall and projecting outward, used to hold a shelf or similar object.

Examples

She bought a decorative wall bracket to hold her collection of small plants.
The shelf collapsed when the old wall bracket gave way.
The television was mounted on a sturdy wall bracket above the fireplace.
