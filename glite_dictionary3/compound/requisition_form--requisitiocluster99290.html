requisition form

noun
official request document
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən fɔrm / (AmE), / ˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃən fɔːm / (BrE)
an official document used to formally request something, especially supplies or services, from an organization

Examples

fill out a requisition form
Before ordering equipment, you must complete a requisition form and submit it to the office.
Each department is required to fill out a new requisition form for additional supplies.
The laboratory will not process samples without the appropriate requisition form.
