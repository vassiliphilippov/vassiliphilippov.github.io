barndoor skate

noun
large Atlantic skate species
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrnˌdɔr skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːndɔː skeɪt / (BrE)
a large species of skate (Dipturus laevis) found in the western Atlantic, reaching up to 5 feet in length and noted for being relatively easy to catch.

Examples

barndoor skate caught by fishermen
Fishermen often report catching a barndoor skate because of its size and availability.
