shower bath

noun
washing by standing under a water spray
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊər bæθ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə bɑːθ / (BrE)
a method of washing by standing under water sprayed from a nozzle, typically in a shower

Examples

take a shower bath
After the long hike, a refreshing shower bath helped him cool off.
Every morning, she starts her day with a quick shower bath.
He prefers to take a shower bath rather than a traditional soak in the tub.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bath: the act of washing the body, especially by immersion in water
  • synonym – shower: a device that produces a spray of water for washing yourself
