summer cohosh

noun
a plant with white racemes, also bugbane
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɚ ˈkoʊhɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˈkəʊhɒʃ / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the eastern United States, known for its tall, upright clusters (racemes) of white flowers; also called bugbane.
