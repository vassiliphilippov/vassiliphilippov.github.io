ski wax

noun
wax applied to skis for better glide
Pronunciation: / ski wæks / (AmE), / ski waks / (BrE)
a substance, usually a type of wax, applied to the base of skis to enhance their performance on snow

Examples

apply ski wax to skis
He applied a fresh layer of ski wax before heading to the slopes.
The type of ski wax you use depends on the snow conditions.
Without ski wax, the skis would not glide smoothly over the snow.
