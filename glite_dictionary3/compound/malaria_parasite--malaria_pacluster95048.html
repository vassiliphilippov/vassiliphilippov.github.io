malaria parasite

noun
protozoan causing malaria
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪriə ˈpɛrəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / məˈlɛːriə ˈparəsʌɪt / (BrE)
a parasitic protozoan of the genus Plasmodium that infects humans and causes malaria
