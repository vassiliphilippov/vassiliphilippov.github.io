onomatoclast tendency

noun
dislike of sound-imitating words
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːnəˌmætəˈklæst tənˈdɛnsɪ / (AmE), / ˌɒnəməˌklɑːst tɛnˈdɛnsɪ / (BrE)
A dislike or aversion to sound-imitating words, such as onomatopoeia.
