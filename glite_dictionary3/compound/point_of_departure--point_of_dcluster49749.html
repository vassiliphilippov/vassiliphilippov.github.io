point of departure

noun
starting point for an activity or idea
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt əv dɪˈpɑrtʃər / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv dɪˈpɑːtʃə / (BrE)
the place, time, or idea at which something begins; the starting point for an activity, discussion, or process

Examples

point of departure for discussion
In our meeting, we used last year's report as a point of departure for discussions.
The author's childhood served as the point of departure for her memoir.
The theory takes traditional models as its point of departure before proposing new ideas.
