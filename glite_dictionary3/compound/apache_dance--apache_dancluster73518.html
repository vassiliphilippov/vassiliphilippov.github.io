apache dance

noun
a dramatic French vaudeville dance
Pronunciation: / əˈpætʃi dæns / (AmE), / əˈpæʃi dɑːns / (BrE)
A violent, fast-paced dance originating in early 20th-century French vaudeville, characterized by dramatic movements that mimic a struggle, often between a man and a woman.
