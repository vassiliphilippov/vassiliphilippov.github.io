lumbar plexus

noun
network of nerves in the lower back
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbɑr ˈplɛksəs /; / ˈlʌm.bɑr ˈplɛk.səs / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbɑː ˈplɛksəs /; / ˈlʌm.bɑː ˈplɛk.səs / (BrE)
a network of nerves in the lower back formed by the lumbar spinal nerves, supplying the lower limb, lower abdomen, and pelvis
