comminuted fracture

noun
fracture where bone is splintered or crushed
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.məˌnjuː.t̬ɪd ˈfræk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.mɪ.njuː.tɪd ˈfræk.tʃə / (BrE)
a fracture in which the bone is broken into several pieces or splintered

Examples

comminuted fracture after accident
A comminuted fracture often results from severe trauma, such as a fall or direct blow.
He required surgery to repair the comminuted fracture of his arm.
The X-ray revealed a comminuted fracture in her right femur after the car accident.

Related Terms

  • antonym – compound fracture: a fracture where the bone pierces the skin
  • antonym – simple fracture: a fracture in which the bone breaks but does not pierce the skin
