critical analysis

noun
careful evaluation of something's merits and faults
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪd.ɪ.kəl əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪt.ɪ.kəl əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (BrE)
the detailed examination and evaluation of something, especially a text or work of art, to judge its merits and faults

Examples

critical analysis of a text
Before publication, every scientific paper undergoes a critical analysis by peer reviewers.
Her critical analysis revealed several strengths and weaknesses in the argument.
The professor asked us to write a critical analysis of the novel's main themes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – critique: a detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, philosophical, or political theory
  • synonym – evaluation: the act of judging or assessing the value of something
