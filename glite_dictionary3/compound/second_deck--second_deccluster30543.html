second deck

noun
uppermost sheltered deck on a large ship
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd dɛk / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd dɛk / (BrE)
the uppermost sheltered deck that extends the whole length of a large ship or vessel.

Examples

on the second deck of a ship
During the storm, passengers were instructed to remain below the second deck for safety.
The second deck offered a clear view of the approaching coastline.
The officers gathered on the second deck to discuss the day's operations.
