private personality

noun
tendency to keep personal matters to oneself
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət pɚˌsænəˈlɪti / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət pɜːsəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
a tendency to keep personal thoughts, feelings, or matters to oneself and avoid sharing them with others
