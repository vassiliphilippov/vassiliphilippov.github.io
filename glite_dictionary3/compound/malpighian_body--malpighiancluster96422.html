malpighian body

noun
structure in the kidney's nephron
Pronunciation: / mælˈpɪɡiən ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / malˈpɪɡiən ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
The capsule in the kidney containing Bowman's capsule and a glomerulus at the expanded end of a nephron.

Examples

malpighian body in kidney
Damage to the malpighian body can impair proper kidney function.
Each nephron begins with a malpighian body that filters waste from the blood.
The malpighian body is crucial for the filtration of blood in the nephron.
