prenatal diagnosis

noun
testing for fetal abnormalities before birth
Pronunciation: / priˈneɪt̬əl daɪˈæɡnəsɪs / (AmE), / priːˈneɪtəl daɪˈaɡnəsɪs / (BrE)
the medical procedures used to determine if a fetus has a genetic abnormality or disorder before birth

Examples

prenatal diagnosis of genetic disorders
Advances in prenatal diagnosis have improved outcomes for many families facing inherited diseases.
Doctors discussed the results of the prenatal diagnosis with the expectant parents in detail.
The couple opted for prenatal diagnosis to check for possible genetic disorders.
