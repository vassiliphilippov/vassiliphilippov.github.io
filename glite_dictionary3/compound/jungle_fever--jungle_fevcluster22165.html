jungle fever

noun
strong urge for exotic adventure or travel
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋɡəl ˌfiːvər / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡl ˌfiːvə / (BrE)
an intense desire for adventure or excitement, especially relating to travel to exotic or natural places

Examples

catch jungle fever
Ever since she read about the Amazon, she's had jungle fever and dreams of exploring rainforests.

Travel magazines often fuel readers' jungle fever with stories of wild, untouched landscapes.
