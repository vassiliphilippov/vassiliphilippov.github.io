service industry

noun
sector providing services, not goods
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪs ˌɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs ˌɪndəstri / (BrE)
the sector of the economy that provides services rather than physical goods, such as hospitality, retail, healthcare, or education
