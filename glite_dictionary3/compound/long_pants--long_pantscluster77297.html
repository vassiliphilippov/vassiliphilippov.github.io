long pants

noun
trousers extending to the feet
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ pænts / (AmE), / lɒŋ pænts / (BrE)
Trousers that extend to the feet, covering the full length of the legs.

Examples

wear long pants outdoors
Children are required to wear long pants in the winter months at this school.
He changed into long pants for the formal dinner.
These long pants are too loose at the waist and need a belt.
