weight gainer

noun
high-calorie supplement to gain weight
Pronunciation: / \u02c8we\u026at \u02cc\u0261e\u026an\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8we\u026at \u02cc\u0261e\u026an\u0259 / (BrE)
a commercially sold high-calorie supplement or shake designed to help a person gain weight, muscle mass, or calories.

Examples

weight gainer shake
He started using a weight gainer shake to bulk up for the competition.
At the supplement store she compared calories listed on two different weight gainers before choosing.
His nutritionist warned that relying solely on a weight gainer can lead to unhealthy fat gain.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dietary supplement: a product taken to add nutrients or calories to the diet; often used to support weight or muscle gain.
  • synonym – mass gainer: a supplement similar to a weight gainer, marketed to help increase body mass.
