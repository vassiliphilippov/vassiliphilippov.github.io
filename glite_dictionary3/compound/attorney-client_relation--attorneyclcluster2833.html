attorney-client relation

noun
legal relationship between a lawyer and their client
Pronunciation: / əˈtɝːni ˈklaɪənt rəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / əˈtɜːni ˈklaɪənt rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the legal relationship in which a lawyer has a duty to act in the best interests of their client
