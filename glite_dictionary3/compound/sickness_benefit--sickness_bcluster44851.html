sickness benefit

noun
government payment for those unable to work due to illness
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪknəs ˌbɛnəfɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪknɪs ˌbɛnɪfɪt / (BrE)
money paid by the government to someone who is unable to work due to illness

Examples
