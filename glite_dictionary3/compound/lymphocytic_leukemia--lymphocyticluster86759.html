lymphocytic leukemia

noun
leukemia with excess lymphocytic cells
Pronunciation: / lɪmˌfoʊˈsɪtɪk luˈkiːmiə / (AmE), / lɪmˌfəˈsɪtɪk luːˈkiːmiə / (BrE)
A type of leukemia marked by an abnormal increase of lymphocytic cells in the blood and enlargement of lymphoid tissues.
