Sonora lac

noun
resinous substance from Mexican shrubs
Pronunciation: / səˈnɔrə læk / (AmE), / səˈnɔːrə læk / (BrE)
A resinous substance resembling lac, secreted by a scale insect living on twigs of certain Mexican shrubs and used locally as medicine.
