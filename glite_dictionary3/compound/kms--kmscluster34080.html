km/s

noun
unit of speed (kilometers per second)
Pronunciation: / kiː ɛm pər səkənd / (AmE), / keɪ ɛm pə sɪkənd / (BrE)
a unit of speed equal to one kilometer traveled per second

Examples

km/s speed measurement
Radio waves propagate at around 300,000 km/s in a vacuum.
Some asteroids travel through space at thousands of km/s relative to Earth.
The spacecraft reached a speed of 10 km/s during reentry.
