rainbow bridge

noun
pet afterlife; place deceased pets go
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌboʊ brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈreɪnbəʊ brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a metaphorical place where deceased pets go, often described as a comforting afterlife for pets, especially in literature and discussions about pet loss

Examples

the rainbow bridge for pets
After her beloved cat died, the idea of the rainbow bridge gave her comfort.
Many people find solace in the belief that their pets have crossed the rainbow bridge.
She wrote a heartfelt message about her old dog's journey over the rainbow bridge.
