small-leaved lime

noun
European linden tree species with small leaves
Pronunciation: / smɔl livd laɪm / (AmE), / smɔːl liːvd laɪm / (BrE)
A large European linden tree (Tilia cordata) with small, dark green leaves, often grown as an ornamental.
