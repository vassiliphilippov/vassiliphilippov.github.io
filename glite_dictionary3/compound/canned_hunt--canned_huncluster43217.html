canned hunt

noun
hunting of captive-bred animals for trophies
Pronunciation: / kænd hʌnt / (AmE), / kænd hʌnt / (BrE)
a hunt in which animals, often bred in captivity, are pursued and killed in enclosed areas, typically for trophies

Examples

canned hunt for trophies
Many conservation groups campaign against the practice of organizing a canned hunt.
Opponents argue that a canned hunt gives animals no fair chance to escape.
