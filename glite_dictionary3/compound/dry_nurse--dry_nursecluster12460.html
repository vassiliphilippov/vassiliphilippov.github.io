dry nurse

noun
a woman who cares for but does not breastfeed a baby
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪ ˌnɜrs / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ ˌnɜːs / (BrE)
a woman who cares for an infant without breastfeeding it

Examples

dry nurse cares for baby
A dry nurse provided daily care and comfort to the infant.
In Victorian times, a dry nurse would care for children from wealthy families.
The family hired a dry nurse when the mother could not breastfeed.
