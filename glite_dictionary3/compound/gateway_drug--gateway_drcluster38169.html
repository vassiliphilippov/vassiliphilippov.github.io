gateway drug

noun
a drug leading to use of more harmful drugs
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌweɪ drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌweɪ drʌɡ / (BrE)
a drug that is not very dangerous or addictive by itself, but may lead to the use of more harmful or addictive drugs
