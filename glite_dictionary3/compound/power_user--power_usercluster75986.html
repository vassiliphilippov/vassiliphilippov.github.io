power user

noun
advanced user of computers or technology
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚ ˌjuː.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˌjuː.zə / (BrE)
a person who uses advanced features of computers or other technology and requires highly capable equipment and software

Examples

power user customizes settings
As a power user, she customizes her computer’s settings to maximize efficiency.
Software companies often seek feedback from power users to improve advanced features.
Unlike casual users, a power user may use keyboard shortcuts and automation tools extensively.
