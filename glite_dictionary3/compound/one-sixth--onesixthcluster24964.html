one-sixth

noun
one part out of six equal parts
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈsɪksθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈsɪksθ / (BrE)
Each of six equal parts into which something is divided; 1/6.

Examples

one-sixth of the cake
One-sixth of the population voted in the local election.
She ate one-sixth of the chocolate cake at the party.
The pie was divided into six slices, and each person took one-sixth.
