dog collar

noun
band worn around a dog's neck
Pronunciation: / dɔg ˈkɑlɚ / (AmE), / dɒg ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
a band worn around a dog's neck, usually to attach a leash or identification tag

Examples

dog collar with leash
She attached the leash to the dog collar before going for a walk.
The dog collar was too tight, so we had to get a larger size.
We bought a new dog collar with a name tag for Max.
