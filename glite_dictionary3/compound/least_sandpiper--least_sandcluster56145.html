least sandpiper

noun
smallest North American sandpiper
Pronunciation: / list ˈsændˌpaɪpər / (AmE), / liːst ˈsændˌpaɪpə / (BrE)
a very small American sandpiper (Calidris minutilla), noted as the smallest shorebird in North America
