flotation device

noun
buoyant equipment for keeping afloat
Pronunciation: / floʊˈteɪʃən dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / fləʊˈteɪʃən dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A piece of equipment, such as a buoyant belt or jacket, designed to keep a person afloat and prevent drowning.

Examples

wear a flotation device in water
Every passenger is required to know how to use a flotation device in case of emergency.
The children wore a flotation device while learning to swim in the pool.
You must always secure your flotation device before assisting others on the boat.
