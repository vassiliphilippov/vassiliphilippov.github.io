bad ending

noun
unsatisfactory conclusion to a story or event
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / bad ˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
an unsatisfactory or disappointing conclusion to a story, event, or experience

Examples

The concert had a bad ending when the sound system failed during the last song.
