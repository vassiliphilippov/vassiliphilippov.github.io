black vomit

noun
vomit with dark or black color
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈvɑmɪt / (AmE), / blak ˈvɒmɪt / (BrE)
Vomit that is dark in color, typically due to the presence of blood, and is often associated with yellow fever and other serious diseases.

Examples

black vomit in yellow fever
Doctors identified the substance as black vomit, an indicator of possible liver failure.
In severe cases of yellow fever, patients may experience black vomit due to internal bleeding.
The appearance of black vomit is a critical warning sign that requires immediate medical attention.
