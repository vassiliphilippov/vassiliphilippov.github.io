media consultant

noun
professional advising on communication media use
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiə kənˈsʌltənt / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiə kənˈsʌltənt / (BrE)
a professional who advises organizations or individuals on the effective use of communication media, such as television, radio, internet, and print, for publicity or advertising

Examples

media consultant for campaigns
As a media consultant, she specializes in digital marketing strategies for small businesses.
