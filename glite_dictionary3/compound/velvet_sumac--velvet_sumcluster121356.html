velvet sumac

noun
a North American shrub or tree with velvety branches and red autumn leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlvət ˈsuːmæk / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt ˈsjuːmæk / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern North America, with velvety branches, compound leaves that turn bright red in autumn, and clusters of greenish-yellow flowers followed by crimson, acidic berries
