at home

adverb
feeling comfortable in a place
Pronunciation: / æt hoʊm / (AmE), / ət həʊm / (BrE)
In one's own residence or in a place or situation where one feels comfortable and relaxed.

Examples

feel at home
After moving to the new city, it took a while before she felt at home.
During interviews, try to appear relaxed and at home to make a good impression.
He always makes his guests feel at home when they visit.
