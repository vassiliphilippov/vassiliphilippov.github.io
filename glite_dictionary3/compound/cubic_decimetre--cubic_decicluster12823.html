cubic decimetre

noun
metric unit of volume (1,000 cubic cm/1 litre)
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈdɛs.ɪˌmiː.tər / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈdɛs.ɪˌmiː.tə / (BrE)
a metric unit of volume equal to one litre or 1,000 cubic centimetres, commonly used to measure capacity

Examples
