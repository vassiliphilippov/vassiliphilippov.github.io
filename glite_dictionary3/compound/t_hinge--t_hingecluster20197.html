T hinge

noun
hinge shaped like the letter T
Pronunciation: / ti hɪndʒ / (AmE), / ti hɪndʒ / (BrE)
a type of hinge shaped like the letter T, with one long strap and one short leaf, used to fasten doors or gates

Examples

T hinge on a gate
After years of use, the old T hinge began to squeak and needed oiling.
The barn door swings smoothly on its sturdy T hinge.
We chose a black T hinge for the rustic wooden gate.
