small talk

noun
polite casual conversation
Pronunciation: / smɔl tɑk / (AmE), / smɔːl tɔːk / (BrE)
polite or casual conversation about unimportant or non-controversial matters, often used to break the ice in social situations

Examples

small talk at a party
His small talk put everyone at ease during the formal dinner.
She finds small talk difficult at parties and prefers deeper conversations.
We made some small talk about the weather while waiting for the meeting to start.
