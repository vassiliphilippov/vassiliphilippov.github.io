regular convex solid

noun
a type of symmetrical geometric solid; Platonic solid
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr kənˈvɛks ˈsɑlɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə kənˈvɛks ˈsɒlɪd / (BrE)
any of the five three-dimensional shapes in which all faces are congruent regular polygons and all angles between faces are equal

Examples

a regular convex solid shape
A cube is an example of a regular convex solid because all its faces are equal squares.
Each regular convex solid exhibits perfect symmetry, making them fascinating to both mathematicians and artists.
Mathematicians have proved that there are only five regular convex solids in three-dimensional space.
