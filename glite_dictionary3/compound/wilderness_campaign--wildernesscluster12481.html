Wilderness Campaign

noun
series of 1864 Civil War battles between Grant and Lee
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪldərnəs kæmˌpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɪldənəs kamˌpeɪn / (BrE)
a series of battles fought in 1864 during the American Civil War between General Grant's and General Lee's armies, marked by heavy casualties and indecisive outcomes
