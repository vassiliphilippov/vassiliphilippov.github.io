cartoon strip

noun
sequence of comic drawings
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈtun strɪp / (AmE), / kɑːˈtuːn strɪp / (BrE)
a sequence of drawings, typically with captions, that together tell a story or joke, found especially in newspapers or comic books
