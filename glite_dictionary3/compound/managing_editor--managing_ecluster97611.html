managing editor

noun
editorial head of a publication
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒɪŋ ˈɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒɪŋ ˈɛdɪtə / (BrE)
the person responsible for overseeing all editorial operations of a newspaper, magazine, or other publication

Examples

managing editor of a newspaper
After years of reporting, she was promoted to managing editor of the newspaper.
As managing editor, he was responsible for ensuring the magazine met its publishing deadlines.
The managing editor coordinates the work of reporters, photographers, and copy editors.

  • synonym – editor-in-chief: the chief editor with final responsibility for a publication
