order Sphaeriales

noun
order of ascomycetous fungi
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər sfɪəˈriəˌliz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sfɪəˈrɪəliːz / (BrE)
a large order of ascomycetous fungi, typically having dark, hard perithecia with definite ostioles; in modern classifications, its species are often reassigned to several different orders.
