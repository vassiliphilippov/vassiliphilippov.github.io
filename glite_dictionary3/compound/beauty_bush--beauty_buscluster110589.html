beauty bush

noun
ornamental flowering shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːti bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti bʊʃ / (BrE)
A Chinese deciduous shrub (Kolkwitzia amabilis) with yellow-throated pinkish flowers and bristly fruit, often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
