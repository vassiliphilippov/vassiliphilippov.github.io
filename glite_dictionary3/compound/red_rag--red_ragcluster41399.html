red rag

noun
something provoking strong anger
Pronunciation: / rɛd ræɡ / (AmE), / rɛd ræɡ / (BrE)
an action or remark that provokes strong anger or response, especially in an individual already predisposed to react

Examples

red rag to a bull
For some people, criticism is a red rag that instantly triggers a defensive reaction.
His rude comment about her work acted as a red rag to the normally calm manager.
Mentioning politics in that meeting was like waving a red rag in front of a bull.

Related Terms

  • synonym – provocation: an action or speech that elicits anger or irritation
  • antonym – soothe: to calm or reduce anger
