dorsal scapular vein

noun
vein near the shoulder blade
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔr.səl ˈskæp.jə.lɚ veɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɔː.səl ˈskæp.jʊ.lə veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that accompanies the descending scapular artery and is a tributary of the subclavian vein or external jugular vein

Examples

the dorsal scapular vein drains blood
During surgery, care must be taken not to damage the dorsal scapular vein.
The dorsal scapular vein drains into the subclavian vein near the base of the neck.
The dorsal scapular vein runs parallel to the descending scapular artery in the shoulder region.

Related Terms

  • synonym – descending scapular artery: artery that accompanies the dorsal scapular vein
  • synonym – external jugular vein: vein that drains blood from the head, face, and neck
  • synonym – subclavian vein: major vein that receives blood from the upper limb and parts of the head and neck
