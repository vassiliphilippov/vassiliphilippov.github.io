security blanket

noun
object or habit giving emotional comfort
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrəti ˌblæŋkət / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪti ˌblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
Something that provides psychological comfort or reassurance, especially when used habitually to ease anxiety.

Examples

security blanket for comfort
For many adults, their morning coffee acts as a security blanket before facing the day.
His trusty old jacket serves as a kind of security blanket whenever he travels.
She always keeps her planner with her as a security blanket in stressful situations.
