column inch

noun
unit of measurement for printed space
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːləm ɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɒləm ɪntʃ / (BrE)
a unit of measurement of printed text or advertising space, equal to one column wide and one inch deep
