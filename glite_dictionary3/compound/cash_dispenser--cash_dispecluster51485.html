cash dispenser

noun
machine dispensing cash with a bank card
Pronunciation: / kæʃ dɪˈspɛnsər / (AmE), / kæʃ dɪˈspensə / (BrE)
a machine, usually outside a bank, that gives out money when a bank card and code are used

Examples

use a cash dispenser
Many people prefer using a cash dispenser rather than going inside the bank.
She stopped at the cash dispenser to withdraw some money before going shopping.
The cash dispenser was out of service, so I couldn't get any cash.
