tentorial sinus

noun
venous channel in the brain's dura mater
Pronunciation: / tɛnˈtɔriəl ˈsaɪnəs / (AmE), / tɛnˈtɔːrɪəl ˈsaɪnəs / (BrE)
an unpaired venous channel in the dura mater of the brain, located near the tentorium cerebelli

Examples

the tentorial sinus vein
Damage to the tentorial sinus can increase the risk of intracranial bleeding.
The neurosurgeon identified the tentorial sinus during the delicate brain procedure.
Venous blood drains through the tentorial sinus into larger sinuses of the dura mater.
