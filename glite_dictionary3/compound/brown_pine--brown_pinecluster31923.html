brown pine

noun
Australian tree with wood turning brown
Pronunciation: / braʊn paɪn / (AmE), / braʊn paɪn / (BrE)
a large Australian tree (Podocarpus elatus) with straight-grained yellow wood that turns brown when exposed to air
