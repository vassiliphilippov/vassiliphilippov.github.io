saccharine tone

noun
excessively sweet or sentimental manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsækərɪn toʊn / (AmE), / ˈsakərɪn təʊn / (BrE)
an excessively sweet, sentimental, or cloying manner of speaking or writing

Examples

saccharine tone in compliments
He adopted a saccharine tone when complimenting his boss, making everyone in the room uncomfortable.
Her speech was criticized for its saccharine tone that seemed more manipulative than heartfelt.
The ad's saccharine tone felt insincere and put off many potential customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym – acerbic: having a sharp or bitter tone or character
  • synonym – cloying: disgust or sicken (someone) with an excess of sweetness, richness, or sentiment
  • synonym – sentimental: having or showing excessive or superficial emotion
