all-terrain bike

noun
bicycle for rough/off-road use
Pronunciation: / ˌɔl təˈreɪn baɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːl təˈreɪn baɪk / (BrE)
a bicycle with a strong frame and wide tires, designed for use on rough ground or off-road surfaces, often in mountainous areas

Examples

ride an all-terrain bike off-road
He rode his all-terrain bike along the rocky trail through the forest.
The store offers a wide selection of all-terrain bikes for beginners and experienced riders alike.
Using her all-terrain bike, she could easily navigate muddy paths and steep hills.
