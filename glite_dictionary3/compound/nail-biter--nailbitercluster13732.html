nail-biter

noun
tense, suspenseful situation
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪl ˌbaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈneɪl ˌbaɪtə / (BrE)
An event or situation that causes intense suspense or anxiety, making people very nervous about the outcome.

Examples

a real nail-biter game
Every election night seems to be a nail-biter with results coming in at the last minute.

The championship game was a real nail-biter that kept fans on the edge of their seats.
