sneak peek

noun
early preview of something unreleased
Pronunciation: / snik pik / (AmE), / sniːk piːk / (BrE)
a preview or early look at something, especially a product or show that has not yet been released to the public

Examples

exclusive sneak peek
Fans got a sneak peek at the upcoming movie in a special trailer.
The company offered a sneak peek of its new smartphone during the conference.
The magazine published a sneak peek of the designer's new collection.

  • synonym – preview: an advance showing or viewing of something before its official release
