Battle of the Little Bighorn

proper noun
1876 battle in Montana where Custer's forces were defeated
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.əl əv ðə ˈlɪt̬.əl ˈbaɪˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ðə ˈlɪt.əl ˈbaɪˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a famous 1876 battle near the Little Bighorn River in Montana, where United States cavalry led by General Custer was defeated by a coalition of Native American tribes, including Sioux and Cheyenne

the Battle of the Little Bighorn in Montana
