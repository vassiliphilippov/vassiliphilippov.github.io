Republic of Tunisia

proper noun
country in northwestern Africa
Pronunciation: / rəˈpʌblɪk əv tuˈniʒə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv tjuːˈniːzɪə / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Africa, on the Mediterranean coast, which gained independence from France in 1956; its capital is Tunis.
