Fukien language

noun
Chinese dialects of Fujian province
Pronunciation: / fuˈkiːən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / fjʊˈkiːən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the forms of Chinese spoken in Fujian (Fukien) province, especially Min Chinese dialects such as Hokkien and Min Nan.
