foot soldier

noun
infantry soldier who fights on foot
Pronunciation: / fʊt ˈsoʊldʒɚ /; / fʊt ˈsoʊl.dʒɚ / (AmE), / fʊt ˈsəʊldʒə /; / fʊt ˈsəʊl.dʒə / (BrE)
a soldier who fights on foot, usually with small weapons, rather than on horseback or in vehicles

Examples
