buffet car

noun
train car serving food and drinks
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈbʊfeɪ kɑː / (BrE)
a railway passenger car where food and beverages are served to passengers during the journey

Examples

train buffet car
Passengers can buy snacks and drinks from the buffet car during the journey.
The buffet car was crowded at lunchtime, with people queuing for sandwiches and coffee.
We decided to meet in the buffet car to have lunch while the train was moving.
