hen of the woods

noun
a type of edible woodland fungus
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛn əv ðə ˈwʊdz / (AmE), / ˌhɛn əv ðə ˈwʊdz / (BrE)
a large, greyish-brown edible fungus (Grifola frondosa) that forms a mass of overlapping caps, resembling a hen, commonly found at the base of trees
