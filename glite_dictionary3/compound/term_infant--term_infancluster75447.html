term infant

noun
infant born between 37-42 weeks
Pronunciation: / tɝm ˈɪn.fənt / (AmE), / tɜːm ˈɪn.fənt / (BrE)

Examples

healthy term infant

The doctor explained that a term infant generally has a lower risk of complications than a preterm baby.
