pungent smell

noun
strong, sharp smell
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌn.dʒənt smɛl / (AmE), / ˈpʌn.dʒənt smɛl / (BrE)
a strong, sharp, and often unpleasant smell that is easily noticed

Examples

strong pungent smell
A pungent smell wafted through the corridor from the trash bins.
After the storm, a pungent smell of wet earth and decay hung in the air.
The pungent smell of onions made her eyes water as she chopped them.
