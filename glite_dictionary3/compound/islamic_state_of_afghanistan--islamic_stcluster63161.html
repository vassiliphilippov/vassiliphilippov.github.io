Islamic State of Afghanistan

proper noun
historical name of Afghanistan (1992–2001)
Pronunciation: / ɪzˈlɑmɪk steɪt əv æfˈɡænɪstæn / (AmE), / ɪzˈlɑːmɪk steɪt əv æfˈɡænɪstæn / (BrE)
The official name of Afghanistan from 1992 to 2001, a mountainous landlocked country in central Asia bordered by Iran, Russia, and Pakistan.
