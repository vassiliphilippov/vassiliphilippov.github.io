fair game

noun
person considered a legitimate target
Pronunciation: / fɛr ɡeɪm /; / fɛr ˈɡeɪm / (AmE), / feə ɡeɪm /; / feə ˈɡeɪm / (BrE)
a person or thing that is considered a legitimate target for criticism, attack, or pursuit
