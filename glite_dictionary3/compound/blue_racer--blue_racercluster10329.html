blue racer

noun
bluish-green snake found in US Midwest/South
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈreɪsər / (AmE), / bluː ˈreɪsə / (BrE)
A bluish-green, nonvenomous snake (Coluber constrictor foxii) native to regions from Ohio to Texas in the United States.
