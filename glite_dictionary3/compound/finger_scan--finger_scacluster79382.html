finger scan

noun
electronic fingerprint identification
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚ skæn / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə skæn / (BrE)
the process of identifying an individual by electronically scanning their fingerprints for authentication or security purposes

Examples

finger scan for entry
Access to the secure area requires a finger scan at the entrance.
She used her finger scan instead of a password to log in to her account.
The new smartphone features finger scan technology for unlocking the device.
