corpus callosum

noun
band of nerve fibers connecting brain hemispheres
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrpəs kəˈloʊsəm / (AmE), / ˌkɔːpəs kəˈləʊsəm / (BrE)
a broad band of nerve fibers joining the two hemispheres of the brain

Examples
