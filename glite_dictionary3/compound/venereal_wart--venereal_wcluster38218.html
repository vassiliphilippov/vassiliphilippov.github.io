venereal wart

noun
wart on or near the genitals or anus, often from HPV
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɪəri.əl wɔrt / (AmE), / vɪˈnɪəri.əl wɔːt / (BrE)
a small, benign growth appearing on or around the genitals or anus, typically caused by certain types of human papillomavirus (HPV)
