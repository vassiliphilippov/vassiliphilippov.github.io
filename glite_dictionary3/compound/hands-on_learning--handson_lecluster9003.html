hands-on learning

noun
learning by practical experience
Pronunciation: / ˌhændz ˈɑn ˈlɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌhændz ˈɒn ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
Learning gained through practical, direct experience rather than theoretical instruction.

Examples

hands-on learning with tools
During the workshop, participants will experience hands-on learning with real equipment.
Many students benefit from hands-on learning because it allows them to apply concepts immediately.
The school emphasizes hands-on learning in its science program to engage students more deeply.
