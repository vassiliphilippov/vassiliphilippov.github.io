skin diving

noun
underwater swimming using only a snorkel
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪn ˌdaɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɪn ˌdaɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of swimming underwater with only a snorkel and no scuba gear

Examples

skin diving in the ocean
Many tourists enjoy skin diving in the clear, warm waters of the Caribbean.
She bought a new mask and fins for her skin diving trip.
Unlike scuba diving, skin diving requires no heavy equipment.

  • synonym – snorkeling: swimming on or through water while equipped with a diving mask and a snorkel
