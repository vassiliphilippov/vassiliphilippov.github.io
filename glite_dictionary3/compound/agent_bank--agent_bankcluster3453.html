agent bank

noun
bank managing syndicated loan
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒənt ˌbæŋk /; / ˈeɪdʒənt bæŋk / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒənt ˌbaŋk /; / ˈeɪdʒənt baŋk / (BrE)
a bank that acts on behalf of other banks or a group of lenders, especially to manage loans or financial transactions
