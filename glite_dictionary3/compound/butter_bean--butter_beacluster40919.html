butter bean

noun
a type of edible flat bean
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚ ˌbiːn /; / ˈbʌt̬ɚ ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.ə ˌbiːn /; / ˈbʌtə ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a type of bean (and the plant that produces it), small, flat, and edible, often pale green or whitish, similar to a lima bean
