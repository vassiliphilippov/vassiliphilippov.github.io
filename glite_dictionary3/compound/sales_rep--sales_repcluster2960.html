sales rep

noun
virtual automated sales representative
Pronunciation: / seɪlz rɛp / (AmE), / seɪlz rɛp / (BrE)
A virtual or automated sales representative (software or AI) that interacts with customers, answers questions, and can complete purchases on digital platforms.

Examples

virtual sales rep chats
The website now uses an AI sales rep to answer customer queries and complete purchases automatically.
During the product launch, their sales rep chatbot persuaded hesitant visitors to add items to their carts.
Companies train virtual sales reps with customer data to give personalized recommendations and faster checkout.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chatbot: an automated computer program that simulates conversation, often used to interact with customers
  • synonym – sales representative: a person who sells products for a company; the human equivalent of this automated role
