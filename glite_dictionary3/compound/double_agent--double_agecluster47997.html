double agent

noun
spy who works for two opposing sides
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a spy who pretends to spy for one country or organization while actually spying for another, usually for two opposing sides
