orchid cactus

noun
epiphytic cactus with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrkɪd ˌkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːkɪd ˌkæktəs / (BrE)
A cactus of the genus Epiphyllum, having flattened, jointed, irregularly branching stems and large, showy tubular flowers.
