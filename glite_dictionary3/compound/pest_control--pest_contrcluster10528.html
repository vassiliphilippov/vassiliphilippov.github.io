pest control

noun
management of pests or insects
Pronunciation: / pɛst kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / pɛst kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
the process of managing or eliminating pests, especially insects and rodents, to protect property, health, or crops

Examples

pest control service
Regular pest control is essential for maintaining healthy crops on the farm.
The apartment building scheduled pest control services after several tenants reported seeing cockroaches.
We hired a pest control expert to get rid of the ants in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym – extermination: the act of killing or destroying pests completely
  • antonym – infestation: the presence of a large number of pests
