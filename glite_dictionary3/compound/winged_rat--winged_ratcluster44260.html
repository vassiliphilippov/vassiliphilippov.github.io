winged rat

noun
derogatory nickname for pigeons or bats
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋd ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋɡd ˌræt / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a bird or flying animal resembling a rat, especially used to refer to pigeons or bats.

Examples

city winged rat
He called the bats in the attic winged rats and wanted them gone immediately.
Many people refer to pigeons as winged rats because of their scavenging habits.
The park was overrun with winged rats searching for leftover food.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bat: flying nocturnal mammal, sometimes called 'winged rat'
  • synonym – pigeon: a common city-dwelling bird often called 'winged rat'
