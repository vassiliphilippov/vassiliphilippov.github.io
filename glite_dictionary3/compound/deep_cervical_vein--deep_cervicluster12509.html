deep cervical vein

noun
a vein in the deep part of the neck
Pronunciation: / diːp ˈsɝvɪkəl veɪn / (AmE), / diːp ˈsɜːvɪkəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein located in the neck that accompanies the deep cervical artery, responsible for draining blood from muscles and tissues near the cervical spine
