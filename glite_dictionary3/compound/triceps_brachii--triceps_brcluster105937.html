triceps brachii

noun
muscle on the back of the upper arm
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪsɛps ˈbreɪkiaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtraɪsɛps ˈbræki.aɪ / (BrE)
the large muscle on the back of the upper arm, responsible for extending the forearm at the elbow
