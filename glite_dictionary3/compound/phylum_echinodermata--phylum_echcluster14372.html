phylum Echinodermata

noun
group of marine invertebrates (starfish, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪləm ˌɛkɪnoʊdərˈmeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪləm ˌɛkɪnəʊdəˈmɑːtə / (BrE)
A major group of radially symmetrical marine invertebrates that includes starfish, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers.

Examples

phylum Echinodermata includes starfish

Starfish and sea cucumbers are part of the phylum Echinodermata.
The phylum Echinodermata is characterized by radial symmetry and a hard calcareous skeleton.
