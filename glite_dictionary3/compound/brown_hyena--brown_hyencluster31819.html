brown hyena

noun
southern African hyena species
Pronunciation: / ˌbraʊn haɪˈinə / (AmE), / ˌbraʊn haɪˈiːnə / (BrE)
A species of hyena native to southern Africa, characterized by its shaggy brown coat and scavenging habits.

Examples

wild brown hyena
Researchers tracked a brown hyena across the Kalahari Desert to study its behavior.
The brown hyena is rarely seen during the day, as it is mostly nocturnal.
Unlike the spotted hyena, the brown hyena has a distinctive shaggy brown coat.
