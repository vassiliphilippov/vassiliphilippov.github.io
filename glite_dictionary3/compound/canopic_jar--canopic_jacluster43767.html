canopic jar

noun
ancient Egyptian jar for mummified organs
Pronunciation: / kəˈnoʊpɪk dʒɑr / (AmE), / kəˈnɒpɪk dʒɑː / (BrE)
A lidded jar used in ancient Egypt to hold the internal organs of a mummified body during the embalming process.
