organic fertilizer

noun
fertilizer from natural sources
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˈɡænɪk ˈfɜːrtəˌlaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ɔːˈɡænɪk ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
A fertilizer made from animal or plant materials, rather than synthetic chemicals, used to improve soil fertility.

Examples

organic fertilizer for plants
Composted manure is a common type of organic fertilizer used in agriculture.
Many gardeners prefer to use organic fertilizer to enrich their soil and grow healthy vegetables.
The farm switched from chemical fertilizer to organic fertilizer for more sustainable crop production.
