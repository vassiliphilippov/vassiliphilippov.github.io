linden tree

noun
deciduous tree of the genus Tilia
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪndən tri / (AmE), / ˈlɪndən triː / (BrE)
A deciduous tree of the genus Tilia, with heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant yellowish flowers, valued for its timber and shade.

Examples

linden tree shade
Many city parks are lined with linden trees because of their broad, shady leaves.
The linden tree in our backyard fills the air with a sweet scent every summer.
The wood from the linden tree is prized by sculptors for its fine grain and softness.

Related Terms

  • synonym – basswood: North American species of Tilia, also called linden
  • synonym – lime tree: another common name for Tilia trees
