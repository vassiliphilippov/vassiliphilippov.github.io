order Sirenia

noun
order of aquatic mammals: manatees, dugongs
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər sɪˈriːniə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sɪˈriːniə / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of large, aquatic, herbivorous mammals that includes manatees, dugongs, and the extinct Steller's sea cow.
