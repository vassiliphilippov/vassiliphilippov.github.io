bulgur pilaf

noun
pilaf using bulgur wheat
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɡər pɪˈlɑf / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɡə pɪˈlɑːf / (BrE)
A dish made by cooking bulgur wheat with water or broth, sometimes with vegetables and spices, resembling traditional rice pilaf but using bulgur instead of rice.

Examples

serve bulgur pilaf with chicken
A bowl of bulgur pilaf is a hearty and healthy alternative to traditional rice dishes.
She learned to make bulgur pilaf from her grandmother's recipe, adding tomatoes and onions for extra taste.
The restaurant's bulgur pilaf was flavorful and paired perfectly with grilled chicken.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bulgur: a cereal food made from the cracked parboiled groats of wheat
  • synonym – pilaf: a dish of rice cooked in stock with spices and vegetables or meat
