taxi rank

noun
place where taxis wait for passengers
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksi ræŋk / (AmE), / ˈtæksi ræŋk / (BrE)
a place where taxis park and wait for passengers to hire them, especially in a designated area

Examples

taxi rank outside station
There was a long line of taxis at the taxi rank during rush hour.
We waited at the taxi rank outside the train station for a cab to arrive.
You can usually find a taxi rank near major hotels in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym – taxi stand: a place where taxis park while awaiting customers (chiefly American)
