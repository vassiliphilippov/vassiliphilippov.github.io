smoke bush

noun
ornamental shrub with smoke-like flower clusters
Pronunciation: / smoʊk bʊʃ / (AmE), / sməʊk bʊʃ / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub or small tree of the genus Cotinus, known for its clusters of airy, smoke-like pinkish-purple flowers

Examples

smoke bush pink flowers
Gardeners love the smoke bush for its unusual feathery blooms that resemble puffs of smoke.
The smoke bush in my garden turns a brilliant red in autumn.
We planted a smoke bush to add color and texture to the landscape.
