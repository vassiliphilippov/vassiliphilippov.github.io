fish fry

noun
event featuring fried fish as main dish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃ ˌfraɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ ˌfraɪ / (BrE)
an event or informal meal where fried fish is served as the main dish, often prepared and eaten outdoors or in a communal setting

Examples

community fish fry
Every summer, the community hosts a fish fry at the park by the lake.
He volunteered to help cook at the annual church fish fry last weekend.
We went to a local fish fry and enjoyed freshly cooked catfish with friends.
