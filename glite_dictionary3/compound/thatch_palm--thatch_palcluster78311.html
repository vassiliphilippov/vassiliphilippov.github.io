thatch palm

noun
palm tree of southern Florida and West Indies
Pronunciation: / θætʃ pɑlm / (AmE), / θætʃ pɑːm / (BrE)
A small palm tree of southern Florida and the West Indies, resembling the silvertop palmetto, with leaves used for thatching roofs.

Examples

thatch palm leaves for roofs
Artisans weave baskets and hats from the fronds of the thatch palm.
Several species of thatch palm thrive in the subtropical climate of southern Florida.
