natural history

noun
scientific study of plants and animals by observation
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚl ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
the scientific study of plants and animals through observation rather than experiment, especially as recorded in popular or non-academic outlets

Examples

natural history museum
David Attenborough's documentaries have made natural history popular with a wide audience.
She has a keen interest in natural history and often observes birds in her garden.
The museum features an extensive natural history collection, including fossils and taxidermy.
