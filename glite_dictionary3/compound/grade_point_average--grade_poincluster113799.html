grade point average

noun
a student's academic performance score
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪd pɔɪnt ˈævərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪd pɔɪnt ˈævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
a numerical measure of a student's academic performance, typically calculated by dividing the total number of grade points earned by the number of credits attempted
