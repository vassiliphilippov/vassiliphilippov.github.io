carbon tetrachloride

noun
toxic, colorless chemical compound
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrbən ˌtɛtrəˈklɔraɪd / (AmE), / ˌkɑːbən ˌtɛtrəˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
a colorless, toxic, nonflammable liquid formerly used as a solvent and in fire extinguishers, now largely discontinued due to health risks
