garden cart

noun
small wheeled cart for garden use
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdən kɑːt / (BrE)
a small cart with handles and one or more wheels, used in gardens to carry tools, plants, or other light loads

Examples

garden cart for tools
After a day of work, he left his tools in the garden cart by the shed.
She loaded the soil into the garden cart and wheeled it across the yard.
The garden cart was perfect for transporting heavy pots and bags of mulch.
