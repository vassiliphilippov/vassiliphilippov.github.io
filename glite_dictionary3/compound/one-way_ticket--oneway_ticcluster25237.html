one-way ticket

noun
ticket for travel in one direction only
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn weɪ ˈtɪkət / (AmE), / ˈwʌn weɪ ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket that allows travel to a destination without returning, valid for a single journey only

Examples

buy a one-way ticket
A one-way ticket can sometimes be more expensive than a round-trip ticket.
He bought a one-way ticket to Paris, intending never to return.
They decided to get one-way tickets for their spontaneous road trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym – round-trip ticket: ticket valid for both outgoing and return journey
