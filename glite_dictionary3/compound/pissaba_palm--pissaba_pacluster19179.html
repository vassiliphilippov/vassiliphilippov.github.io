pissaba palm

noun
Brazilian palm yielding useful fibers
Pronunciation: / pɪˈsɑːbə pɑːm / (AmE), / pɪˈsɑːbə pɑːm / (BrE)
A Brazilian palm tree whose strong leaf fibers are used to make ropes, mats, and brushes.
