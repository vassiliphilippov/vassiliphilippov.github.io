deep-set eye

noun
eye positioned further back in skull
Pronunciation: / ˌdipˈsɛt aɪ / (AmE), / ˌdiːpˈsɛt aɪ / (BrE)
An eye that is positioned further back in the skull than is typical, often creating the appearance of a prominent brow or shadowed sockets.
