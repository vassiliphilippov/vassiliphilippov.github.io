saddle sore

noun
sore caused by chafing from a saddle
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl sɔr / (AmE), / ˈsædəl sɔː / (BrE)
A sore or irritation, especially one caused by chafing from a saddle on a horseback rider.

Examples

painful saddle sore after riding
After a long ride, she developed a painful saddle sore on her thigh.
He treated his saddle sore with ointment and took a break from riding.
Wearing padded shorts can help prevent saddle sores when riding for extended periods.
