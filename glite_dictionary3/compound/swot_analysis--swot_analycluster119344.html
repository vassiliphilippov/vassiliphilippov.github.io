SWOT analysis

noun
business evaluation method (strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats)
Pronunciation: / swa:t əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / swɒt əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
a structured method for identifying and evaluating the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats related to a business or project

Examples

SWOT analysis for a company
A thorough SWOT analysis helped the team recognize both potential risks and areas for growth.
Many companies conduct a SWOT analysis before launching a new product.
Their annual strategy meeting always begins with a SWOT analysis of the organization's current position.
