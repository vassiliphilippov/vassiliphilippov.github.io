liver cancer

noun
malignant tumor of the liver
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɚ ˌkænsɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvə ˌkænsə / (BrE)
A malignant tumor that originates in or spreads to the liver, often causing symptoms such as loss of appetite, weakness, and jaundice.

liver cancer diagnosis
Early diagnosis of liver cancer can significantly improve the chances of survival.
