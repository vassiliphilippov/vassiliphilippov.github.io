Spanish lime

noun
tropical fruit tree with edible green fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈspænɪʃ laɪm / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ laɪm / (BrE)
A tropical American tree (Melicoccus bijugatus) that bears a small edible fruit with green leathery skin and sweet, juicy translucent pulp.

Examples

Spanish lime tree
She showed us how to peel the Spanish lime and eat the juicy pulp inside.
The Spanish lime is popular in many Caribbean countries as a summer snack.
