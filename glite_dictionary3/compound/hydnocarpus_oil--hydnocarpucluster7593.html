hydnocarpus oil

noun
oil from Hydnocarpus seeds, used medicinally
Pronunciation: / hɪdˈnɑːkɑrpəs ɔɪl / (AmE), / hɪdˈnɒkɑːpəs ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil obtained from the seeds of trees in the genus Hydnocarpus, especially Hydnocarpus wightiana, formerly used in the treatment of leprosy
