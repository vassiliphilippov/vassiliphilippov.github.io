second baseman

noun
baseball player at second base
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkənd ˈbeɪsmən / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈbeɪsmən / (BrE)
(Baseball) the player who fields the position near second base and is responsible for plays at that base.
