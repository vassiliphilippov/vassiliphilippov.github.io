fizzy drink

noun
carbonated soft drink
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzi drɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈfɪzi drɪŋk / (BrE)
A non-alcoholic beverage that contains carbon dioxide, making it bubbly or sparkling; a carbonated soft drink.
