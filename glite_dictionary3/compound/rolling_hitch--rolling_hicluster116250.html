rolling hitch

noun
type of fastening knot for ropes
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɪŋ hɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ hɪtʃ / (BrE)
a knot used to fasten a rope to a spar, post, or to another rope so that it resists movement in one direction

Examples
