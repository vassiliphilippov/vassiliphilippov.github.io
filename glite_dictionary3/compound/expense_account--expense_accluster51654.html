expense account

noun
business account for job-related expenses
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛns əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛns əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an account used by employees, especially salespeople or executives, to charge business-related travel, meals, and entertainment expenses, to be reimbursed by their employer

Examples

expense account for travel costs
She submitted the restaurant bill to her expense account for reimbursement.
The company closely monitors each executive's expense account to prevent misuse.
Travel costs are usually charged to an expense account when employees are on business trips.

Related Terms

  • synonym – travel allowance: money given to cover travel costs for work
