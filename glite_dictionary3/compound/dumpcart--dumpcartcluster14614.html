dumpcart

noun
tiltable cart for easy unloading
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmpˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈdʌmpˌkɑːt / (BrE)
A cart designed so that it can be tilted to discharge its contents quickly and easily, without manual handling.

Examples

dumpcart for soil
A sturdy dumpcart can save time and effort during heavy landscaping projects.
After filling the dumpcart with soil, she tilted it back to empty it into the garden bed.
The workers loaded gravel into the dumpcart and wheeled it to the edge of the site.
