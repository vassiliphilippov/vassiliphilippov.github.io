computer paper

noun
perforated continuous paper for computer printers
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpjuːtɚ ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
Continuous paper, usually with perforated edges and holes for feeding, used in computer printers such as dot-matrix printers.
