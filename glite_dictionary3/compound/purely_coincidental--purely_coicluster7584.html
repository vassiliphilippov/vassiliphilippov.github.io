purely coincidental

adjective
entirely accidental; by chance
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrli ˌkoʊɪnˈsɪdəntəl / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəli ˌkəʊɪnˈsɪdəntəl / (BrE)
Happening by chance, without any intention or planning; entirely accidental.

Examples

purely coincidental similarity
Any similarities to real people in this story are purely coincidental.
Our meeting at the cafe was purely coincidental and not prearranged.
The resemblance between their logos is purely coincidental and not a result of copying.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accidental: happening by accident, not planned or intended
  • antonym – intentional: done on purpose, not by chance
