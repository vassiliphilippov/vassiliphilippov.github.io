worsted yarn

noun
smooth, strong yarn from long wool fibers
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːstɪd jɑrn / (AmE), / ˈwʊstɪd jɑːn / (BrE)
a tightly twisted yarn made from long-staple wool fibers, producing a smooth, durable thread used in weaving and knitting

Examples

worsted yarn for weaving
After spinning long-staple wool, they created a strong worsted yarn for weaving.
She chose a soft blue worsted yarn to knit her new sweater.
The texture of worsted yarn makes it ideal for finely woven suits.
