mountain quail

noun
a quail species of western US mountains
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən kweɪl / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn kweɪl / (BrE)
a North American quail species (Oreortyx pictus) of mountainous regions, slightly larger than the California quail, known for its long head plume
