distributor cap

noun
protective cover on an ignition distributor
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪbjətər kæp / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjʊtə kap / (BrE)
the protective cover on the distributor of a vehicle's ignition system that holds the wires connecting to the spark plugs

Examples

distributor cap on engine
He checked the distributor cap for corrosion before reassembling the engine.
If the distributor cap cracks, it can cause starting problems.
The mechanic replaced the old distributor cap to fix the engine misfire.
