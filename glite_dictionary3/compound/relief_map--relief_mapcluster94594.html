relief map

noun
map showing land elevation
Pronunciation: / rəˈlif mæp / (AmE), / rɪˈliːf mæp / (BrE)
a map that shows the elevation and shape of the land, often using contour lines or shading to indicate differences in height

Examples

relief map of mountains
A relief map can help hikers understand changes in elevation before planning their route.
She admired the detailed relief map displayed at the visitor center.
The students used a relief map to study the mountains and valleys in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym – topographic map: a map showing the physical features of an area, including elevation
