arcus senilis

noun
corneal arc-shaped deposit in older adults
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrkəs səˈnɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈɑːkəs sɛˈnɪlɪs / (BrE)
A grayish or whitish arc-shaped deposit visible in the cornea, often seen in older adults and usually benign.

Examples

arcus senilis in the eye
Although arcus senilis is common in elderly individuals, it does not typically affect vision.
