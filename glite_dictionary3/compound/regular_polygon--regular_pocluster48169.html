regular polygon

noun
polygon with equal sides and angles
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr ˈpɑːliɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə ˈpɒlɪɡən / (BrE)
A polygon in which all sides are of equal length and all angles are equal.

Examples

a regular polygon shape
A square is a classic example of a regular polygon.
Every side of a regular polygon is the same length as every other side.
The pentagon-shaped building was designed as a regular polygon with five equal sides.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irregular polygon: a polygon with sides and angles not all equal
