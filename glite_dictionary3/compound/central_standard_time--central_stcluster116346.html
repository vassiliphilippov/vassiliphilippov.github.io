Central Standard Time

noun
standard time zone of central US
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəl ˈstændɚd taɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəl ˈstændəd taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time of the central region of the United States, six hours behind Greenwich Mean Time, based on the 90th meridian

Examples

Central Standard Time in Chicago
Chicago operates on Central Standard Time for most of the year.
Please schedule the meeting for 10:00 a.m. Central Standard Time.
When traveling from New York, remember that Central Standard Time is one hour behind Eastern Time.
