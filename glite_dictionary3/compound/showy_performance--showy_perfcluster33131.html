showy performance

noun
flamboyant or ostentatious display
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ.i pɚˈfɔr.məns / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i pəˈfɔː.məns / (BrE)
an act or presentation that is ostentatious, flamboyant, or intended to attract attention

Examples

give a showy performance
Her showy performance at the gala drew both admiration and criticism from the critics.
Some judges prefer subtlety over a showy performance in dance competitions.
The actor's showy performance stole the spotlight from the rest of the cast.

Related Terms
