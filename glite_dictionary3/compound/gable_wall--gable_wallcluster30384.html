gable wall

noun
triangular wall under a gable roof
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪbəl wɔl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪbəl wɔːl / (BrE)
The vertical triangular section of a wall enclosed by the sloping sides of a gable roof.

Examples

gable wall of a house
After the storm, the gable wall needed repairs due to structural damage.
The architect designed a window into the gable wall to let in more light.
The large gable wall of the house was decorated with a mural.
