one-eighth

noun
one of eight equal parts; 1/8
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈeɪtθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈeɪtθ / (BrE)
One part of something divided into eight equal parts; 1/8.

Examples

one-eighth of a cake
He divided the cake into eight slices, so each person got one-eighth.
Only one-eighth of the land was suitable for farming.
The recipe calls for one-eighth of a teaspoon of salt.
