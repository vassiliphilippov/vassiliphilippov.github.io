mackerel scad

noun
a small Atlantic fish used for food and bait
Pronunciation: / ˈmækərəl skæd / (AmE), / ˈmakərəl skad / (BrE)
a small, silvery marine fish (Decapterus macarellus) found in the Atlantic Ocean from Nova Scotia to Brazil, valued for food and as bait.
