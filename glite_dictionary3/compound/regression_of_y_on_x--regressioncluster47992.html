regression of y on x

noun
statistical equation relating y to x
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡrɛʃən əv waɪ ɑn ɛks / (AmE), / rɪˈɡreʃən əv waɪ ɒn ɛks / (BrE)
an equation in statistics that expresses the relationship between a dependent variable (y) and an independent variable (x), used to predict values of y based on x

Examples

regression of y on x predicts values
The regression of y on x showed a significant positive relationship between study hours and test scores.
To make predictions about future sales, the analyst calculated the regression of y on x using monthly data.
Understanding the regression of y on x is essential in many fields, such as economics and psychology.
