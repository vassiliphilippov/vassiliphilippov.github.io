smell test

noun
assessment of quality or acceptability, literal or figurative
Pronunciation: / smɛl tɛst / (AmE), / smɛl tɛst / (BrE)
a test or assessment to determine quality, acceptability, or authenticity, either literally by evaluating a scent or figuratively as an initial screening

Examples

give a smell test
Before investing, always give the proposal a smell test to check for red flags.
If something doesn’t pass the smell test, it’s probably best to avoid it.
The new detergent passed the smell test with consumers due to its fresh scent.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sniff test: informal test for authenticity or acceptability, often used interchangeably
