common iguana

noun
large tropical arboreal lizard of the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmən ɪˈɡwɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ɪˈɡwɑːnə / (BrE)
a large, herbivorous lizard native to Central and South America, with a spiny crest along its back, commonly found in trees and sometimes used as food
