false saber-toothed tiger

noun
extinct North American prehistoric cat
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈseɪbər tuːθ ˈtaɪɡər / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈseɪbə tuːθ ˈtaɪɡə / (BrE)
