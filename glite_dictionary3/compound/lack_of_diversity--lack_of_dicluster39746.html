lack of diversity

noun
insufficient mix of groups or perspectives
Pronunciation: / læk əv daɪˈvɜːr.sə.t̬i / (AmE), / læk əv daɪˈvɜː.sɪ.ti / (BrE)
an insufficient or inadequate representation of different groups, backgrounds, or perspectives within a particular setting, organization, or context

Examples

lack of diversity in the workplace
A lack of diversity among the candidates limited the perspectives raised during the discussion.
Many universities are working to address their lack of diversity among faculty and students.
The company is being criticized for its lack of diversity in leadership positions.
