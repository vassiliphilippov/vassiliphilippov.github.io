application form

noun
form used to apply for something
Pronunciation: / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən fɔrm / (AmE), / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən fɔːm / (BrE)
a document with questions or fields to be filled in when formally requesting something such as a job, course, or membership

Examples

fill out an application form
He downloaded the application form for the scholarship program from the university website.
Many companies now provide an online application form instead of a paper one.
Please complete the application form and return it to the admissions office.
