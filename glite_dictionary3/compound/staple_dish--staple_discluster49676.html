staple dish

noun
essential main food of a cuisine
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪpəl dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪpəl dɪʃ / (BrE)
A food that is a basic or essential part of the diet in a particular region or culture.

Examples

staple dish in Asia
Beans and corn are a staple dish combination in several South American cuisines.
Bread is considered a staple dish in many European cultures.
Rice is a staple dish in many Asian countries and appears at almost every meal.
