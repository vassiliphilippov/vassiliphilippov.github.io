key fob

noun
small item attached to a key ring
Pronunciation: / ki fɑb / (AmE), / ki fɒb / (BrE)
A small ornament or device attached to a key ring or key chain, often to make keys easier to handle or identify.

Examples

key fob with charm
He attached his new key fob to his car keys for easier access.
She lost her house keys along with the leather key fob her friend had given her.
The store offers a personalized key fob engraving service.
