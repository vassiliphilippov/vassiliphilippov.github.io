creative design

noun
innovative or imaginative design process
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪt̬ɪv dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪv dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
the process of developing original or imaginative ideas and applying them to the design of objects, products, or concepts
