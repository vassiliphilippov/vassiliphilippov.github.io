brush cut

noun
very short, upright haircut
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ kʌt / (AmE), / brʌʃ kʌt / (BrE)
A short haircut in which the hair is cut upright so that it stands up like the bristles of a brush.

Examples

military brush cut
After joining the army, he immediately got a brush cut to fit regulations.
Her brush cut made her stand out among her friends with longer hairstyles.
The barber gave me a brush cut just as I had asked.
