black-and-white

adjective
composed of only black and white colors
Pronunciation: / ˌblæk ən ˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌblæk ən ˈwaɪt /; / ˌblæk ənd ˈwaɪt / (BrE)
having only black and white (and sometimes gray), with no other colors; especially used for images, films, or patterns
