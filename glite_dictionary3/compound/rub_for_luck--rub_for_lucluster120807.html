to rub for luck

verb
perform a ritual to get good luck
Pronunciation: / rʌb fər lʌk / (AmE), / rʌb fə lʌk / (BrE)
to touch or rub an object in the hope of bringing good fortune or success, often as part of a ritual or superstition

Examples

to rub for luck a lucky object
Before every exam, she would always rub for luck by touching her favorite pen.
He reached out to rub for luck the rabbit's foot on his keychain before entering the interview room.
Superstitious fans often rub for luck the nose of a statue at the stadium before the big game.
