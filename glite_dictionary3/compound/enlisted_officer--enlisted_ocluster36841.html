enlisted officer

noun
officer appointed from enlisted ranks
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈlɪstɪd ˈɑːfɪsər / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɪstɪd ˈɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a military officer who has been appointed from among enlisted personnel rather than commissioned directly

Examples

enlisted officer in the army
After years of dedicated service, she became an enlisted officer in the army.

Unlike commissioned officers, an enlisted officer begins their leadership role from the enlisted ranks.
