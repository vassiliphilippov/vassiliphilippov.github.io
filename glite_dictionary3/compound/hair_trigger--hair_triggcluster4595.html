hair trigger

noun
gun trigger needing very little pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛr ˌtrɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈheə ˌtrɪɡə / (BrE)
a trigger on a gun that responds to very slight pressure, causing it to fire easily

Examples

hair trigger on a rifle
He adjusted the hair trigger on his rifle for a lighter pull.
Some competitive shooters prefer using a hair trigger for faster response.
The old revolver had a hair trigger, so extra care was needed when handling it.
