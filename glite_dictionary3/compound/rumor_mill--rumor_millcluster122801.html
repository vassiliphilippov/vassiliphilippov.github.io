rumor mill

noun
network or source of gossip
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmɚ mɪl / (AmE), / ˈruːmə mɪl / (BrE)
a group of people, institution, or social network that spreads gossip or unverified information, especially in an informal way

Examples

office rumor mill
After the announcement, the rumor mill started spinning with wild theories about who would take over.
Despite her efforts to keep her life private, the rumor mill never seems to stop.
Office politics often feed the rumor mill, making it hard to know what is true.

Related Terms

  • synonym – grapevine: an informal way that information, especially rumors, is passed from one person to another
