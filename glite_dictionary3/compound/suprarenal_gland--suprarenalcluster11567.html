suprarenal gland

noun
adrenal gland above the kidney
Pronunciation: / suːprəˈriːnəl ɡlænd / (AmE), / sjuːprəˈriːnəl ɡlænd / (BrE)
either of a pair of complex endocrine glands located above each kidney, also known as the adrenal gland
