roller ironer

noun
machine for ironing and drying linens
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər ˈaɪərnər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə ˈaɪənə / (BrE)
a machine with a large, heated roller that is used to iron and dry sheets, table linens, pillowcases, and similar items

Examples

roller ironer for sheets
At the laundry facility, a large roller ironer made the linens crisp and smooth.
Modern households rarely own a roller ironer due to its size and cost.
The hotel uses a roller ironer to quickly press bed sheets and tablecloths.
