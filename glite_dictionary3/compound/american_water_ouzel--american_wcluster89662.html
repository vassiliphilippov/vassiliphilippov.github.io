American water ouzel

noun
aquatic songbird of western North America
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈwɑ.tər ˈaʊ.zəl / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈwɔː.tə ˈaʊ.zəl / (BrE)
a small aquatic songbird of western North America, known for living near streams and diving into water for food; also called the American dipper.
