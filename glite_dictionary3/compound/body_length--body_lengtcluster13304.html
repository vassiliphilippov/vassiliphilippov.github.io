body length

noun
distance from head to feet/tail
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi lɛŋθ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi leŋθ / (BrE)
the measurement of the distance from the head to the feet or tail of a person or animal

Examples

a body length ahead
She finished the race a full body length ahead of her closest competitor.
The snake measured over two meters in body length.
The veterinarian estimated the lizard's age based on its body length.
