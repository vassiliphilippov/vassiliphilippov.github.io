lambent light

noun
softly glowing or flickering light
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmbənt laɪt / (AmE), / ˈlæmbənt laɪt / (BrE)
A softly glowing or gently flickering light, often with a delicate or wavering quality.

Examples

lambent light on water
He watched the lambent light reflect off the gentle ripples of the lake.
Moonbeams cast a lambent light on the quiet garden.
The lambent light from the fireplace gave the room a warm, inviting glow.
