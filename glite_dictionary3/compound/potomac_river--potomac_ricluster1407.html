Potomac River

proper noun
major river in eastern United States
Pronunciation: / pəˈtoʊmək ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / pəˈtəʊmɪk ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in the eastern United States, rising in West Virginia and flowing east to the Chesapeake Bay, forming part of the boundary between Maryland and Virginia
