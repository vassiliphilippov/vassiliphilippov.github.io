speech balloon

noun
cartoon text bubble
Pronunciation: / spiːtʃ bəˈluːn / (AmE), / spiːtʃ bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a graphic element in cartoons or comics, usually a rounded outline, that contains the words spoken by a character

Examples

speech balloon in comics
In many comics, a speech balloon indicates dialogue or thoughts.
Kids had fun creating their own speech balloons in the comic workshop.
The artist added a speech balloon to show what the character was saying.
