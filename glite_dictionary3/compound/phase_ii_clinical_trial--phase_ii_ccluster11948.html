phase II clinical trial

noun
clinical trial for treatment efficacy and side effects
Pronunciation: / feɪz tu ˈklɪnɪkəl ˈtraɪəl / (AmE), / feɪz tuː ˈklɪnɪkəl ˈtraɪəl / (BrE)
a clinical trial conducted with more participants than phase I, designed to evaluate the effectiveness of a treatment and monitor possible side effects
