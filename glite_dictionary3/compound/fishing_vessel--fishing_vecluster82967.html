fishing vessel

noun
ship or boat for catching fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌvɛsəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ ˌvɛsəl / (BrE)
A ship or boat designed or equipped for catching fish, often fitted with storage wells to keep the catch alive.

Examples

large fishing vessel
Many coastal communities rely on the income from their fishing vessels.
Regulations require every fishing vessel to have safety equipment and proper documentation.
The fishing vessel returned to port with its hold full of fresh tuna.
