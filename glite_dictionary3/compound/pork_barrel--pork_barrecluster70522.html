pork barrel

noun
government funds for local projects to win votes
Pronunciation: / pɔrk ˈbɛrəl / (AmE), / pɔːk ˈbærəl / (BrE)
the use of government funds for local projects intended primarily to please voters or legislators and win votes

Examples

pork barrel spending
Critics argue that pork barrel projects often lead to government waste.
Many voters are frustrated by pork barrel spending that benefits only a few communities.
The senator was accused of using pork barrel politics to secure funding for projects in his own state.

Related Terms

  • synonym – graft: political corruption, especially in the use of a politician's authority for personal gain
  • synonym – patronage: support, encouragement, or financial aid provided by a patron, especially in politics
