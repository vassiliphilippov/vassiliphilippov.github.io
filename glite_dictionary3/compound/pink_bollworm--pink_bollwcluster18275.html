pink bollworm

noun
larva pest damaging cotton bolls
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk ˈboʊlˌwɜrm / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈbəʊlˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a small moth larva that feeds on the seeds within cotton bolls, known for being a significant pest to cotton crops
