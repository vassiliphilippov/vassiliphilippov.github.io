tooth fairy

noun
mythical figure who collects children's lost teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈtuθ ˌfɛri /; / tuθ ˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˈtuːθ ˌfeəri /; / tuːθ ˈfeəri / (BrE)
a mythical being in children's stories who is said to visit when a child loses a baby tooth, leaving money or a small gift under the pillow in exchange
