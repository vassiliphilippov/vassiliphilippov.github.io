wind instrument

noun
musical instrument sounded by air
Pronunciation: / wɪnd ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (AmE), / wɪnd ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (BrE)
a musical instrument in which sound is produced by the movement of air through an enclosed column, typically by blowing or with bellows
