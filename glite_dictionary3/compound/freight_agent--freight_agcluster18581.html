freight agent

noun
employee who handles receipt and delivery of freight
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈfreɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an employee responsible for organizing and overseeing the receipt and delivery of goods for a freight carrier

Examples

freight agent handles shipments
A skilled freight agent must be organized and familiar with shipping regulations.
The freight agent ensured that all shipments were logged and tracked efficiently.
When the delivery arrived, the freight agent coordinated with the warehouse staff to receive the goods.
