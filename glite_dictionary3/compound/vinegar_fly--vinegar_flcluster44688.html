vinegar fly

noun
small fly feeding on fermenting foods
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪnɪɡɚ flaɪ / (AmE), / ˈvɪnɪɡə flaɪ / (BrE)
a small fly whose larvae feed on fermenting or decaying fruit and preserved foods, especially those not properly sealed

Examples

vinegar fly on fruit
Fruit vendors often have to deal with vinegar flies hovering near their produce.
She was annoyed to find several vinegar flies around the bowl of overripe peaches.
The kitchen attracted a swarm of vinegar flies after a jar of jam was left open.

  • synonym – fruit fly: a small fly whose larvae also feed on fermenting fruit
