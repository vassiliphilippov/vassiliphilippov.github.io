boiling point

noun
extreme anger or agitation
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪlɪŋ pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪlɪŋ pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a state of extreme anger or agitation; the moment when someone's emotions are about to erupt
