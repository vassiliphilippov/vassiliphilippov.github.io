fragile fern

noun
a delicate woodland fern species
Pronunciation: / ˈfrædʒəl fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈfrædʒaɪl fɜːn / (BrE)
a delicate fern of North America and Europe, with thin, deeply divided fronds and brittle stems; commonly found in moist woodlands.

Examples

a fragile fern in woodlands
Many gardeners appreciate the fragile fern for its delicate beauty and soft green foliage.

The fragile fern grew abundantly in the shady, damp corners of the forest.
