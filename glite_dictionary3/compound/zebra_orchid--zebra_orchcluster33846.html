zebra orchid

noun
orchid species with purple-marked yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈzibrə ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈziːbrə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
an orchid of southwestern Australia with reddish linear leaves and panicles of pale yellow flowers marked with purple, featuring a deep red or purple lip
