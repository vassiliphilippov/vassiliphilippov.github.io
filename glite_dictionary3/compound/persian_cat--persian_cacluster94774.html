Persian cat

noun
long-haired breed of domestic cat
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːʒən kæt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʒən kæt / (BrE)
A breed of domestic cat with long fur, a round face, and a short muzzle, known for its gentle temperament.

Examples

fluffy Persian cat
Her Persian cat loves to curl up by the window in the sunlight.
Many people are attracted to the calm nature of a Persian cat.
The Persian cat requires regular grooming to maintain its soft, long fur.
