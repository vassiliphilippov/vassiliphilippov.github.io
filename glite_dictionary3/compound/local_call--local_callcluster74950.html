local call

noun
telephone call within a local area
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəl kɔl / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəl kɔːl / (BrE)
a telephone call made within a defined local area, usually charged at a lower rate than long-distance calls

Examples

make a local call
I made a local call to the restaurant to reserve a table.
In the past, payphones would only allow you to make a local call without a calling card.
There is no extra charge for a local call from this phone.

Related Terms

  • antonym – long-distance call: a telephone call made to a location outside the local calling area
