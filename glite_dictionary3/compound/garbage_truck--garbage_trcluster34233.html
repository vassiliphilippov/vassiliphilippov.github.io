garbage truck

noun
vehicle for collecting waste
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
A large vehicle designed to collect and transport household waste to a disposal site.
