cat-foot detail

noun
ornamental paw-shaped furniture detail
Pronunciation: / kæt fʊt diːteɪl / (AmE), / kat fʊt diːteɪl / (BrE)
a decorative element in furniture design that resembles the paw of a cat, often carved at the foot of a table or chair leg
