penny pincher

noun
a very frugal or stingy person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛni ˌpɪntʃər /; / ˈpɛni ˌpɪntʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɛni ˌpɪntʃə / (BrE)
a person who is very unwilling to spend money; someone who is extremely careful or stingy with money
