ketoacidosis-resistant diabetes mellitus

noun
form of adult-onset diabetes without ketoacidosis
Pronunciation: / ˌkiːtoʊəˌsɪdoʊsɪs rɪˈzɪstənt daɪəˈbiːtiːz məˈlaɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌkiːtəʊəˌsɪdəʊsɪs rɪˈzɪstənt daɪəˈbiːtiːz məˈlɪtəs / (BrE)
A mild form of diabetes mellitus developing gradually in adults, often triggered by obesity, severe stress, menopause, or similar factors, and usually controlled by diet or hypoglycemic agents rather than insulin injections.
