done deal

noun
finalized agreement or arrangement
Pronunciation: / dʌn dil / (AmE), / dʌn diːl / (BrE)
an agreement or arrangement that has been completed and cannot be changed

Examples

a done deal already
After the negotiations ended, the merger was a done deal.
He can't back out now; it's already a done deal.
Once the funds arrive, it will be a done deal.
