blow dryer

noun
electric hair drying device
Pronunciation: / bloʊ ˌdraɪɚ / (AmE), / bləʊ ˌdraɪə / (BrE)
A hand-held electric device that blows warm air onto the hair, used to dry and style it.

Examples

blow dryer for hair
After taking a shower, he grabbed his blow dryer from the bathroom shelf.
She used a blow dryer to style her hair before the interview.
The hotel provides a blow dryer in every room for guest convenience.
