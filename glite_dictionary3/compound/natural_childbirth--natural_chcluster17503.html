natural childbirth

noun
birth without medical intervention
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃərəl ˈtʃaɪldˌbɝθ / (AmE), / ˈnætʃrəl ˈtʃaɪldˌbɜːθ / (BrE)
A method of giving birth in which the mother has minimal medical intervention, and no drugs are given to relieve pain or assist the birth process.

Examples

natural childbirth without drugs
Her doctor supported her decision to pursue natural childbirth for her second baby.
Many women choose natural childbirth to avoid the risks associated with anesthesia.
Some people believe that natural childbirth helps mothers recover more quickly after delivery.
