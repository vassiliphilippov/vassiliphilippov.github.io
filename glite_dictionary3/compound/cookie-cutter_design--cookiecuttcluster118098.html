cookie-cutter design

noun
stereotyped, uniform design
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊki ˌkʌtɚ dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʊki ˌkʌtə dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a design that is uniform, stereotyped, and lacking individuality or originality
