House of Hanover

proper noun
English royal dynasty, 1714–1901
Pronunciation: / haʊs əv ˈhænəvɚ / (AmE), / haʊs əv ˈhanəvə / (BrE)
the English royal dynasty that ruled from 1714 to 1901, beginning with George I and ending with Queen Victoria
