music stand

noun
stand for holding sheet music
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzɪk stænd / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk stænd / (BrE)
a portable stand designed to hold sheet music for a musician, usually at a height and angle suitable for reading while playing or singing

Examples

music stand for sheet music
He forgot his music stand at home and had to borrow one from the school.
She adjusted the height of the music stand before starting her piano practice.
The orchestra provided each player with a music stand for their scores.
