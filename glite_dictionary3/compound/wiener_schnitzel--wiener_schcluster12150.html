Wiener schnitzel

noun
breaded, deep-fried veal cutlet dish
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːnər ˈʃnɪtsəl / (AmE), / ˈviːnə ˈʃnɪtsəl / (BrE)
A dish of thin veal cutlets that are breaded and deep-fried, typically served in Austrian cuisine.

Examples

eat Wiener schnitzel with lemon
Many people consider Wiener schnitzel the national dish of Austria.
She learned how to prepare Wiener schnitzel from her grandmother's traditional recipe.
The restaurant serves authentic Wiener schnitzel with potato salad and lemon.
