able-bodied seawoman

noun
female trained merchant sailor
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪbəlˈbɑːdid ˈsiːˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˌeɪblˈbɒdid ˈsiːˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A female sailor in the merchant marine who is fully trained and qualified in special skills.

experienced able-bodied seawoman

As an able-bodied seawoman, she was skilled in emergency procedures and handling heavy equipment.
The ship's crew included two able-bodied seawomen responsible for navigation and deck duties.
