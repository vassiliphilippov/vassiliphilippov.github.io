incontinence slip

noun
garment for managing urinary incontinence
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑntənəns slɪp / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒntɪnəns slɪp / (BrE)
A disposable or reusable garment worn to manage urinary incontinence, designed to absorb urine and protect clothing and bedding.

Examples

wear an incontinence slip
After surgery, he found the incontinence slip made daily life much more manageable.
Many care homes provide incontinence slips to residents for added comfort and dignity.
The nurse recommended wearing an incontinence slip to help manage overnight leaks.
