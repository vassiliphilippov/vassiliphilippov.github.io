Taoist Trinity

noun
three principal gods of Taoism
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊ ɪst ˈtrɪn ɪ ti / (AmE), / ˈtaʊ ɪst ˈtrɪn ɪ ti / (BrE)
In Taoism, the three supreme deities known as the Three Pure Ones: Yuanshi Tianzun, Lingbao Tianzun, and Daode Tianzun.
