economic assistance

noun
financial or material help for support or development
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkɑnəmɪk əˈsɪstəns / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk əˈsɪstəns / (BrE)
help in the form of money or other resources given to support a person, group, or country in financial need or to promote economic development.

Examples

economic assistance for recovery
Following the disaster, neighboring countries offered economic assistance to help with recovery efforts.
Many developing nations rely on economic assistance from international organizations to improve infrastructure and services.
The government announced new economic assistance programs to help small businesses survive the recession.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aid: help, especially financial or material, provided to those in need
  • synonym – economic aid: assistance, usually from one country to another, for economic development
