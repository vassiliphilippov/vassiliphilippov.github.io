lobster plant

noun
tropical American plant with scarlet bracts
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑbstɚ plænt / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a tropical American plant with poisonous milky sap and showy tapering scarlet bracts surrounding small yellow flowers

Examples

lobster plant red bracts
Although beautiful, the lobster plant contains a poisonous milky sap and should be handled with care.
Gardeners appreciate the lobster plant for attracting hummingbirds with its bright flowers.
