intervertebral disc

noun
cushioning disc between spinal vertebrae
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tɚ.vɚˈtiː.brəl dɪsk / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tə.vɜːˈtiː.brəl dɪsk / (BrE)
A fibrocartilaginous structure that acts as a cushion between the vertebrae of the spinal column, except between the first two vertebrae.

Examples

intervertebral disc herniation
Degeneration of the intervertebral disc can lead to chronic back pain.
Each intervertebral disc helps absorb shock between the bones of the spine.
The surgeon repaired a herniated intervertebral disc during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spinal column: the series of vertebrae forming the backbone
  • synonym – vertebra: each of the bones forming the spinal column
