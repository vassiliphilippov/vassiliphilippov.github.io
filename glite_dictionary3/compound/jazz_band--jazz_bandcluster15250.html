jazz band

noun
group of jazz musicians
Pronunciation: / dʒæz bænd / (AmE), / dʒæz band / (BrE)
A small group of musicians who play jazz music together, usually featuring instruments such as saxophone, trumpet, piano, bass, and drums.

Examples

jazz band performance
He joined a jazz band after learning to play the saxophone.
The jazz band played lively tunes that got the whole crowd dancing.
The school’s jazz band performs at festivals around the country.
