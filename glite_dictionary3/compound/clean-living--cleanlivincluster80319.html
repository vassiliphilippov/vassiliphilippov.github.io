clean-living

adjective
living in a morally pure and healthy way
Pronunciation: / klin ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / kliːn ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
leading a life that is morally pure and often also abstaining from unhealthy habits such as drinking, smoking, or drug use

Examples

She has always been known for her clean-living and kind nature.
