per se

adverb
by itself; in itself
Pronunciation: / pɜr ˈseɪ / (AmE), / pɜː ˈseɪ / (BrE)
by or in itself; taken alone, with respect to its inherent nature

Examples

per se as such
He didn't object to the proposal per se, just to its timing.
The idea is not bad per se, but it could use improvement.
Violence per se is not encouraged in any form of media.
