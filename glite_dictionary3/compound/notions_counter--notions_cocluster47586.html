notions counter

noun
counter selling small sewing articles
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊʃənz ˌkaʊntər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊʃənz ˌkaʊntə / (BrE)
A counter or section in a store where small articles, especially sewing supplies such as buttons, thread, pins, etc., are sold.

Examples

notions counter sells buttons
Many customers visit the notions counter for last-minute sewing supplies.
