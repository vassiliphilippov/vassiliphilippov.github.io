crow blackbird

noun
North American blackbird with long tail and iridescent plumage
Pronunciation: / kroʊ ˈblækˌbɝd / (AmE), / krəʊ ˈblækˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A North American blackbird with a long tail and iridescent black feathers, also known as the common grackle.
