virginal membrane

noun
fold of tissue at entrance to vagina (hymen)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdʒɪnəl ˈmɛmˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪnəl ˈmɛm.breɪn / (BrE)
a fold of tissue that partly covers the entrance to the vagina, usually present at birth and sometimes referred to as the hymen
