red-shouldered hawk

noun
North American hawk species with reddish shoulders
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈʃʊldɚd hɑk / (AmE), / rɛd ˈʃəʊldəd hɔːk / (BrE)
A North American hawk (Buteo lineatus) with reddish-brown shoulders, often found in woodlands and near water.
