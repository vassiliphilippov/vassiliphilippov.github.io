pie crust

noun
pastry for lining or topping a pie
Pronunciation: / paɪ krʌst / (AmE), / paɪ krʌst / (BrE)
a layer of pastry used as the base and, sometimes, the top of a pie, enclosing the filling

Examples

pie crust recipe
She made a flaky pie crust from scratch for the apple pie.
The recipe calls for a pre-baked pie crust to save time.
The secret to a good pie crust is keeping the butter cold.
