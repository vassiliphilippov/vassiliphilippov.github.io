Chile bonito

noun
Pacific coast bonito fish species
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːleɪ bəˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːleɪ bəˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
A species of bonito fish found along the Pacific coast of the Americas, known for its dark, oily flesh that is suitable for canning.
