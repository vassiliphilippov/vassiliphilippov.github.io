gamboge tree

noun
tree yielding gamboge resin for pigment
Pronunciation: / ɡæmˈboʊdʒ triː / (AmE), / ɡamˈbəʊdʒ triː / (BrE)
A low, spreading tree of Indonesia that produces an orange to brown gum resin (gamboge) used as a pigment.
