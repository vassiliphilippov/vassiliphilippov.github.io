standard of living

noun
level of material comfort and wealth
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɚd əv ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstændəd əv ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
The level of material comfort, wealth, and access to goods and services that a person, community, or country enjoys.

Examples

high standard of living
A high standard of living usually includes access to quality healthcare, education, and housing.
Economic recessions can sometimes lead to a decline in the standard of living for many families.
Over the past decade, the standard of living in many countries has significantly improved.
