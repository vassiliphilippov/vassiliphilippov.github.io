Alaska rein orchid

noun
a North American orchid species
Pronunciation: / əˈlæskə reɪn ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / əˈlɑːskə reɪn ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A North American orchid (Platanthera unalascensis) similar to the coastal rein orchid, but with smaller flowers, found from Alaska to Baja California and east to the Dakotas and Colorado.
