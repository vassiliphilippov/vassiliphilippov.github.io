English foxhound

noun
English breed of foxhunting dog
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈfɑksˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈfɒksˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
a breed of dog originally developed in England for hunting foxes in packs, slightly larger than the American foxhound
