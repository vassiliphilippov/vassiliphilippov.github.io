water project

noun
large-scale water management undertaking
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌprɑdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
an organized plan or undertaking for developing, managing, or making an area of water more useful, such as through building dams, reservoirs, or irrigation systems

Examples

water project construction
Environmental groups are concerned about the impact of the proposed water project on local wildlife.
Local engineers worked for years to complete the massive water project along the river.
The government announced funding for a new water project to improve irrigation in rural areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dam: a barrier constructed to hold back water, often part of a water project
  • synonym – irrigation project: a project specifically aimed at providing water for agriculture
