Cycladic civilisation

noun
Bronze Age civilisation of the Cyclades islands
Pronunciation: / səɪˈklædɪk ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / sɪˈkladɪk ˌsɪvɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the Bronze Age civilisation that developed on the Cyclades islands in the southern Aegean Sea, flourishing from about 3000 to 1100 BC
