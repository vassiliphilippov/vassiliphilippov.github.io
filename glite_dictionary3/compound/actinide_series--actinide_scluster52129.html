actinide series

noun
set of 15 radioactive chemical elements
Pronunciation: / ˈæktɪˌnaɪd ˈsɪriz / (AmE), / ˈæktɪnʌɪd ˈsɪəriːz / (BrE)
(chemistry) a group of 15 radioactive elements in the periodic table from actinium to lawrencium, with increasing atomic numbers.
