fuel filter

noun
filter for removing impurities from fuel
Pronunciation: / ˈfjul ˌfɪltɚ / (AmE), / ˈfjʊəl ˌfɪltə / (BrE)
A device in a fuel line that removes dirt and rust particles from fuel before it reaches the engine.

Examples

fuel filter replacement
A clogged fuel filter can cause engine performance issues.
Always check your fuel filter if you notice a drop in fuel efficiency.
