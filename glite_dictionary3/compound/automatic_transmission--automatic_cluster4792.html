automatic transmission

noun
system that changes car gears automatically
Pronunciation: / ˌɔt̬əˈmæt̬ɪk trænsˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk trænzˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a system in a vehicle that automatically changes gear ratios as the vehicle moves, freeing the driver from manually shifting gears

Examples

automatic transmission car
His old sports car has a manual gearbox, not an automatic transmission.
Learning to drive with an automatic transmission can be less intimidating for beginners.
Many drivers prefer cars with an automatic transmission because they are easier to operate in heavy traffic.

Related Terms

  • antonym – manual transmission: a vehicle's transmission in which the driver manually selects gears
