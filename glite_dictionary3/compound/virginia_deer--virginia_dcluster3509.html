Virginia deer

noun
common North American deer species
Pronunciation: / vərˈdʒɪniə dɪr / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪnɪə dɪə / (BrE)
A common North American deer species (Odocoileus virginianus) with a tail that has a white underside.
