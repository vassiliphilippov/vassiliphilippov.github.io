basset horn

noun
tenor clarinet pitched in F
Pronunciation: / ˈbæsɪt hɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbæsɪt hɔːn / (BrE)
a tenor member of the clarinet family, pitched in F below the B-flat clarinet, often used in orchestral and chamber music
