residential district

noun
area mainly for private homes
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl ˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / ˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl ˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
an area of a city or town where most buildings are homes or private residences, rather than businesses or factories

Examples
