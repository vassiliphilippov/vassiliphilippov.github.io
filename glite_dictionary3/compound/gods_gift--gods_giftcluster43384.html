God's gift

noun
special gift or talent from God
Pronunciation: / ɡɑdz ɡɪft / (AmE), / ɡɒdz ɡɪft / (BrE)
a special talent, quality, or blessing regarded as given by God; often used to describe a person with exceptional ability or something considered especially fortunate

Examples

God's gift to music
After being reunited, he said their friendship was God's gift during difficult times.
Many consider his wisdom to be God's gift to the community.
She believes her musical talent is truly God's gift to her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blessing: a beneficial thing for which one is grateful; something that brings well-being
  • synonym – talent: a natural aptitude or skill
