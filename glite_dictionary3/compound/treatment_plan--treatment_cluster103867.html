treatment plan

noun
medical care outline for a patient
Pronunciation: / ˈtritmənt plæn / (AmE), / ˈtriːtmənt plan / (BrE)
a detailed outline created by a medical professional describing a patient's proposed interventions, therapies, and expected outcomes

Examples

treatment plan for cancer
Before starting therapy, the team developed a comprehensive treatment plan.
Her treatment plan includes chemotherapy and regular blood tests.
The doctor discussed the treatment plan with the patient and their family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – care plan: a broader outline of ongoing care and support for a patient
