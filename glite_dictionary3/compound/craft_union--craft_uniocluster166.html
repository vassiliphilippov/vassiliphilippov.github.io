craft union

noun
labor union restricted to a particular craft
Pronunciation: / kræft ˈjuːniən / (AmE), / krɑːft ˈjuːniən / (BrE)
a labor union whose membership consists of workers practicing the same trade or craft, rather than the same industry

Examples

craft union for electricians
Electricians often belong to a craft union that represents their specific trade.
Membership in the craft union is limited to skilled workers in the field.
The carpenters' craft union negotiated better working conditions for its members.

Related Terms

  • antonym – industrial union: a union whose members work in the same industry, regardless of specific craft or trade
