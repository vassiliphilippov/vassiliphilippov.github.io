water snake

noun
non-venomous snake living near water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtər ˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌsneɪk / (BrE)
any of various mostly non-venomous snakes that inhabit or are often found near water.

Examples

water snake swimming
A water snake is usually not aggressive unless threatened.
The lake's edge is a common habitat for the water snake.
We spotted a water snake gliding silently through the pond.

Related Terms

  • synonym – garter snake: a harmless American snake often found near water
  • antonym – moccasin: a venomous water snake, especially the cottonmouth
