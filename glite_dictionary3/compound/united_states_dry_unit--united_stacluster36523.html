United States dry unit

noun
US unit for measuring dry volume
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː.naɪ.tɪd ˈsteɪts ˈdraɪ ˌjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪ.tɪd ˈsteɪts ˈdraɪ ˌjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
