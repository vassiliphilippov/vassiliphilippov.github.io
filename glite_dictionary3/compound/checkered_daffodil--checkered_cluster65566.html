checkered daffodil

noun
ornamental plant with checkered bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkɚd ˈdæfədɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkəd ˈdafədɪl / (BrE)
A Eurasian ornamental plant (Fritillaria meleagris) with bell-shaped, pendent flowers typically veined and checkered with shades of purple or maroon on a pale background.
