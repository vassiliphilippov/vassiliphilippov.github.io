World War 2

proper noun
global war from 1939 to 1945
Pronunciation: / wɝrld wɑr tuː / (AmE), / wɜːld wɔː tuː / (BrE)
The global war fought from 1939 to 1945 between the Allies and the Axis powers, resulting in significant worldwide impact.
