in the midst

adverb
in the middle part or among
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə mɪdst / (AmE), / ɪn ðə mɪdst / (BrE)
in the middle or central part or point of something or among something

Examples

in the midst of trouble
She found herself in the midst of a heated discussion about politics.
The city was silent in the midst of the night.
We camped in the midst of the dense forest, far from any towns.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amid: surrounded by; in the middle of
  • synonym – among: in or through the middle of a group
