cloven foot

noun
mark or hoof of Satan in legend
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊ.vən fʊt /; / ˈkloʊvən fʊt / (AmE), / ˈkləʊ.vən fʊt /; / ˈkləʊvən fʊt / (BrE)
a hoof that is split into two parts at the tip (as found in animals like pigs and goats); sometimes seen in folklore as a sign of the Devil
