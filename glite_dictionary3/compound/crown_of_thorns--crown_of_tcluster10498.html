crown of thorns

noun
a spiny Madagascan spurge plant
Pronunciation: / kraʊn əv θɔrnz / (AmE), / kraʊn əv θɔːnz / (BrE)
a bushy spurge (Euphorbia milii) native to Madagascar, with long, spiny stems, few leaves, and flowers with bright scarlet bracts

Examples

crown of thorns plant
Gardeners often choose crown of thorns for its drought tolerance and striking appearance.

The crown of thorns is popular as a houseplant because of its colorful bracts and resilience.
