devoted fan

noun
loyal and enthusiastic supporter
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvoʊtɪd fæn / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊtɪd fan / (BrE)
a person who shows strong loyalty and enthusiasm for a person, team, or group

Examples

devoted fan of a singer
As a devoted fan of the author, she attended every book signing in her city.
He remained a devoted fan of the football club even when they lost every match.
The band’s concerts were filled with devoted fans who knew every lyric by heart.
