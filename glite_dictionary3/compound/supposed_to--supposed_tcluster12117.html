to supposed to

auxiliary verb
expected or intended to happen
Pronunciation: / səˈpoʊzd tu / (AmE), / səˈpəʊzd tuː / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is expected or intended to happen, or that someone is required or obliged to do something

Examples

supposed to follow rules
I wasn't supposed to open the gift until my birthday.
The train is supposed to arrive at 8:15, but it's running late.
You are supposed to turn off your phone during the movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym – meant to: used to express intention or something that should occur
