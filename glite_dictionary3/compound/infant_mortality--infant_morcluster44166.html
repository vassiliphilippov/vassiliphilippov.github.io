infant mortality

noun
death rate of infants under one year old
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnfənt mɔrˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˈɪnfənt mɔːˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the death rate among infants less than one year old in a given population

Examples

infant mortality rate
High levels of infant mortality often indicate problems with access to healthcare and nutrition.
Public health initiatives have helped reduce infant mortality in many developing countries.
The government is committed to lowering infant mortality through vaccination and maternal care programs.
