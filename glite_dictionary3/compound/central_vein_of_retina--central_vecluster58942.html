central vein of retina

noun
major vein draining blood from the retina
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəl veɪn əv rəˈtaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəl veɪn əv rɪˈtiːnə / (BrE)
the vein formed by the union of the retinal veins, accompanying the central arteries of the retina within the optic nerve
