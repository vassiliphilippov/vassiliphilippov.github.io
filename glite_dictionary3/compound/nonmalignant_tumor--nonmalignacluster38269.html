nonmalignant tumor

noun
tumor that is not cancerous
Pronunciation: / nɑn.məˈlɪɡ.nənt ˈtu.mɚ / (AmE), / nɒn.məˈlɪɡ.nənt ˈtjuː.mə / (BrE)
A tumor that is not cancerous and does not spread to other parts of the body.

Examples

benign nonmalignant tumor
Although a nonmalignant tumor is not cancerous, it may still require removal if it causes symptoms.
Recent scans confirmed the growth was a nonmalignant tumor, which brought great relief to the patient.
The doctor assured her that the nonmalignant tumor would not spread to other organs.

Related Terms
