sea star

noun
star-shaped marine animal
Pronunciation: / ˈsi ˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌstɑː / (BrE)
A marine echinoderm with a star-shaped body, typically having five arms extending from a central disk.

Examples
