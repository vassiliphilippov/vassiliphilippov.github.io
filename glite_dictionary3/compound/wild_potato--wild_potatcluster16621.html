wild potato

noun
perennial plant with potato-like tubers
Pronunciation: / waɪld pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / waɪld pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the southwestern United States and Mexico with small, pale brown to cream-colored tubers that resemble potatoes
