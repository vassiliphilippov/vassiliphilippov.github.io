pilot balloon

noun
meteorological balloon for observing air currents
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət bəˌluːn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət bəˌluːn / (BrE)
A small unmanned balloon released to determine air currents and wind direction, especially in meteorological observations.

Examples

pilot balloon ascent
Before launching the new instrument, they studied the data from the pilot balloon ascent.
Observations from the pilot balloon helped the forecasters update the weather models more accurately.
The meteorological team released a pilot balloon at dawn to track the direction and speed of upper air currents.

Related Terms

  • synonym – weather balloon: a balloon used for carrying meteorological instruments into the atmosphere
