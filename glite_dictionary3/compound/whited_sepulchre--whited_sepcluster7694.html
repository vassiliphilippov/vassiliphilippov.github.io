whited sepulchre

noun
hypocrite; outwardly virtuous but inwardly corrupt person
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtɪd ˈsɛpəlkɚ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtɪd ˈsɛpəlkə / (BrE)
