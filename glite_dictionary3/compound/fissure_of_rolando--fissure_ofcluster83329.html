fissure of Rolando

noun
brain groove between frontal and parietal lobes
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɚ əv roʊˈlændoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃə əv rəʊˈlændəʊ / (BrE)
a deep groove on the lateral surface of the brain, separating the frontal lobe from the parietal lobe; also called the central sulcus
