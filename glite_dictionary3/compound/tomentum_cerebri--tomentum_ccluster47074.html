tomentum cerebri

noun
network of brain blood vessels
Pronunciation: / toʊˈmɛntəm səˈriːbraɪ / (AmE), / təʊˈmɛntəm səˈriːbri / (BrE)
a network of tiny blood vessels located between the cerebral surface of the pia mater and the cerebral cortex
