branding iron

noun
metal tool for marking livestock
Pronunciation: / ˈbrændɪŋ ˌaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈbrændɪŋ ˌaɪən / (BrE)
a long-handled metal tool heated and used to burn a mark onto livestock to show ownership

Examples

hot branding iron
A rusty branding iron hung on the wall of the old barn.
Modern farms rarely use the traditional branding iron thanks to new identification methods.
The rancher heated the branding iron in the fire before marking the new calves.
