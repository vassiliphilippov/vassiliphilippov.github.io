boot sale

noun
outdoor sale from car trunks
Pronunciation: / buːt seɪl / (AmE), / buːt seɪl / (BrE)
An outdoor market where people sell goods from the boots (trunks) of their cars, typically held in a car park or field.

Examples

a boot sale bargain
Many people visit boot sales to look for bargains or unique items.
She decided to clear out her attic by selling things at a boot sale.
We found a beautiful antique vase at the local boot sale last weekend.
