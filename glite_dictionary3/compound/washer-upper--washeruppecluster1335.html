washer-upper

noun
person who washes dishes
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃər ˈʌpər / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃə ˈʌpə / (BrE)
a person whose job or chore is to wash dishes, especially in a kitchen

Examples

kitchen washer-upper
After the big family dinner, I ended up as the washer-upper while everyone else relaxed.
At the restaurant, he worked evenings as a washer-upper in the busy kitchen.
No one volunteered to be the washer-upper after our party, so the dishes piled up.
