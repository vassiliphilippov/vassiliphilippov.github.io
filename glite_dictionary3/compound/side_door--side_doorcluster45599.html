side door

noun
door on the side of a building
Pronunciation: / saɪd dɔr / (AmE), / saɪd dɔː / (BrE)
an external door located on one side of a building, typically used as an entrance or exit other than the main door

Examples

open the side door
Guests were asked to enter the building via the side door due to construction at the front entrance.
He quietly slipped out through the side door to avoid being noticed.
The delivery person left the package by the side door as instructed.
