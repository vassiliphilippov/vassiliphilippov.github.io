Hopey McChange

noun
playful nickname for an optimist or advocate of change
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊpi məkˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpi məkˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
A playful or sometimes ironic nickname for a person who expresses excessive optimism or advocates for significant change, especially in a naïve or idealistic manner.
