Bloody Mary

proper noun
Queen Mary I of England, 1516–1558
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdi ˈmɛri / (AmE), / ˈblʌdi ˈmeəri / (BrE)
Queen Mary I of England (1516–1558), known for her persecution of Protestants and restoration of Roman Catholicism during her reign from 1553 to 1558.
