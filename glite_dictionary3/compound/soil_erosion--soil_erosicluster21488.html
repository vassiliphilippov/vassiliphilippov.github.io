soil erosion

noun
loss of soil by water or wind
Pronunciation: / sɔɪl ɪˈroʊ.ʒən /; / ˈsɔɪl ɪˈroʊʒən / (AmE), / sɔɪl ɪˈrəʊ.ʒən /; / ˈsɔɪl ɪˈrəʊʒən / (BrE)
the gradual removal of the top layer of soil (topsoil) by natural forces such as water or wind
