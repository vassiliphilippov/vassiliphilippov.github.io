anaesthetic agent

noun
drug causing loss of sensation
Pronunciation: / ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌanɪsˈθɛtɪk ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a drug that induces temporary loss of sensation in the body, particularly to prevent pain during medical procedures

Examples
