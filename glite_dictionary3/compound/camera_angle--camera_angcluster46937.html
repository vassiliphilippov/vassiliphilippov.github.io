camera angle

noun
angle or position from which a camera captures a subject
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmərə ˌæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈkæmərə ˌæŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
the position from which a camera views its subject, affecting what is seen and how it is perceived in a photograph or film
