holding pen

noun
temporary enclosure for livestock
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊldɪŋ pɛn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldɪŋ pɛn / (BrE)
an enclosure where livestock are kept temporarily before being moved or processed

Examples

holding pen for cattle
After the auction, the animals remained in the holding pen until their new owners arrived.
Cattle were led one by one from the holding pen to the weighing station.
The sheep waited nervously in the holding pen before being examined by the vet.

Related Terms

  • synonym – corral: an enclosure for confining livestock, especially in North America
  • antonym – pasture: a field where animals graze
