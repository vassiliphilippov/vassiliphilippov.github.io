turtle's head

noun
humorous slang for needing to defecate urgently
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːt̬əlz hɛd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːtlz hɛd / (BrE)
(informal, slang) A humorous term for the urgent need to defecate, likening the appearance of feces starting to emerge to a turtle poking its head out of its shell.

Examples

have a turtle's head
She whispered to her friend that she had a turtle's head, causing them both to giggle.
Suddenly, he announced he had a turtle's head and sprinted to the bathroom.
When the meeting dragged on, Tom joked he was getting a turtle's head and needed a break.
