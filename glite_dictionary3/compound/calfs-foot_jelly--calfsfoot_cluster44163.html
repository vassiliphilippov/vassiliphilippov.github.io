calf's-foot jelly

noun
savory jelly made from calves' feet
Pronunciation: / ˈkæfs ˌfʊt ˈdʒɛli / (AmE), / ˈkɑːfs ˌfʊt ˈdʒɛli / (BrE)
A savory jelly prepared using gelatin extracted from boiled calves' feet, often served as a traditional dish or used in aspics.
