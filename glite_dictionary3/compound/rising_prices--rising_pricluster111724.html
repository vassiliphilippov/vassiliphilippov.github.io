rising prices

noun
increase in costs of goods and services
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzɪŋ ˈpraɪsɪz / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ ˈpraɪsɪz / (BrE)
a situation in which the costs of goods and services are increasing over a period of time

Examples

rising prices for food
Rising prices have made it more difficult for families to afford basic groceries.
Many workers are asking for wage increases to keep up with rising prices.
The government is taking steps to address the problem of rising prices in the housing market.
