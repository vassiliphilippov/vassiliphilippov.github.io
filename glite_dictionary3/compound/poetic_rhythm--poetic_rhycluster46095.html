poetic rhythm

noun
pattern of stresses in poetry
Pronunciation: / poʊˈɛtɪk ˈrɪðəm / (AmE), / pəʊˈɛtɪk ˈrɪðəm / (BrE)
the pattern of stressed and unstressed syllables in poetry, giving verse its musical quality and flow
