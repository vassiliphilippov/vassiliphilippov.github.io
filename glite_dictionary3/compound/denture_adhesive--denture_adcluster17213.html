denture adhesive

noun
substance for securing dentures
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛn.tʃɚ ædˈhi.sɪv / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.tʃə ədˈhiː.sɪv / (BrE)
A product applied to dentures to help keep them securely in place in the mouth.
