Chinese yam

noun
a vine with edible tubers, native to China
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈniz ˌjæm / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz ˌjam / (BrE)
a climbing vine native to China, with glossy heart-shaped leaves and edible tubers, often grown ornamentally or for food

Examples

Chinese yam plant leaves
Gardeners appreciate the Chinese yam for its attractive leaves as well as its edible roots.
