word of advice

noun
cautionary suggestion or warning
Pronunciation: / wɝd əv ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / wɜːd əv ədˈvaɪs / (BrE)
a cautionary suggestion or warning, often given about a potential problem or danger

Examples

a word of advice before action
Before you sign the contract, let me offer a word of advice: read the fine print carefully.
Her mother gave her a word of advice before she left for college.
If I may give you a word of advice, don't wait until the last minute to start your project.
