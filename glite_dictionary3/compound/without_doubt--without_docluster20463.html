without doubt

adverb
certainly; unquestionably
Pronunciation: / wɪðaʊt ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / wɪðaʊt ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
Certainly; unquestionably; used to emphasize that something is definitely true or admitted.

Examples

without doubt the best
Without doubt, she is one of the best players on the team.
He will, without doubt, be remembered as a great leader.
This is, without doubt, the most difficult decision I've ever made.

Related Terms

  • antonym – possibly: used to express possibility, not certainty
