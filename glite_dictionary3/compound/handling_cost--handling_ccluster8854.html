handling cost

noun
charge for preparing and shipping goods
Pronunciation: / ˈhændlɪŋ kɑst / (AmE), / ˈhændlɪŋ kɒst / (BrE)
the amount charged or spent for the process of preparing, packaging, and delivering goods, especially when fulfilling an order
