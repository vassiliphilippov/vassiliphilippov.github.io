insulin shock

noun
dangerous condition of low blood sugar from too much insulin
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.sə.lɪn ʃɑk / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sjʊ.lɪn ʃɒk / (BrE)
a condition caused by excessive insulin in the body leading to dangerously low blood sugar, often resulting in unconsciousness or coma

Examples

insulin shock after overdose
He collapsed at work from insulin shock after accidentally injecting too much insulin.
Managing diabetes includes knowing the signs of insulin shock and how to respond quickly.
The patient was rushed to the hospital after going into insulin shock during the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hypoglycemia: abnormally low level of sugar in the blood
  • synonym – insulin coma: coma resulting from an overdose of insulin
