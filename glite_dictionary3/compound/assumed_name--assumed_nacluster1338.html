assumed name

noun
name adopted instead of one's real name
Pronunciation: / əˈsumd neɪm / (AmE), / əˈsjuːmd neɪm / (BrE)
a name that someone adopts instead of their real name, often to conceal their identity; an alias

Examples

use an assumed name
Many criminals are known to travel under an assumed name to evade capture.
She wrote the novel under an assumed name to protect her privacy.
The witness testified in court using an assumed name for safety reasons.
