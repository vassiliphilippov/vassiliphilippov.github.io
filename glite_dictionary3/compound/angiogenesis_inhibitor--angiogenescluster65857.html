angiogenesis inhibitor

noun
drug preventing blood vessel growth
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒi.oʊˈdʒɛnəs ɪnˈhɪbɪtər / (AmE), / ˌændʒi.əʊˈdʒɛnɪs ɪnˈhɪbɪtə / (BrE)
