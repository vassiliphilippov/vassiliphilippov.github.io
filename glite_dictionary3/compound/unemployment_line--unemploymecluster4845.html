unemployment line

noun
queue of people seeking unemployment benefits or jobs
Pronunciation: / ʌn.ɪmˈplɔɪ.mənt laɪn / (AmE), / ʌn.ɪmˈplɔɪ.mənt laɪn / (BrE)
a line or queue of people waiting to receive unemployment benefits or to apply for jobs, typically at an employment office

Examples

wait in the unemployment line
After losing his job, he found himself waiting in the unemployment line for the first time in his life.
Many people stood silently in the unemployment line outside the government office early this morning.
The news report showed images of a long unemployment line stretching around the block.
