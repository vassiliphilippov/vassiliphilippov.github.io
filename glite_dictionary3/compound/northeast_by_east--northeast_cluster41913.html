northeast by east

noun
compass point east of northeast
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˈist baɪ ˈist / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːst baɪ ˈiːst / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point east of northeast, lying halfway between northeast and east-northeast.

Examples

northeast by east direction
He set the compass at northeast by east and began his trek across the moor.
The pilot reported flying in the direction of northeast by east while approaching the coastline.
The ship altered its course to northeast by east to avoid the storm.
