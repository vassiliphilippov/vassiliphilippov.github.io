rabbit punch

noun
illegal blow to back of the neck
Pronunciation: / ˈræb.ɪt pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈræb.ɪt pʌntʃ / (BrE)
A short, chopping blow delivered to the back of the neck, especially one considered illegal in boxing or fighting sports.
