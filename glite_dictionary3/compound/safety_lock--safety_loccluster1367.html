safety lock

noun
bolt preventing opening from outside
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti lɑk / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti lɒk / (BrE)
A bolt or mechanism that prevents a door or gate from being opened from the outside, enhancing security.

Examples

safety lock on a door
He forgot to release the safety lock, so the gate wouldn't open.
Please make sure the safety lock is engaged before you leave the house.
The apartment door has a sturdy safety lock for additional protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deadbolt: a strong lock with a sliding metal bolt, often for doors
