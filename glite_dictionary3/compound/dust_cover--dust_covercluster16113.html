dust cover

noun
protective cloth for furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌst ˌkʌvɚ /; / dʌst ˈkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʌst ˌkʌvə /; / dʌst ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
a removable protective cover, usually made of cloth or plastic, used to keep furniture or equipment clean and free from dust when not in use

Examples

dust cover for furniture
Before going on vacation, she draped a dust cover over the chairs to keep them clean.
He pulled off the dust cover to reveal an antique desk beneath.
The old sofa was hidden under a dust cover in the attic.
He lifted the dust cover and started cleaning the keyboard.
Please remember to put the dust cover back on the printer after using it.
The record player came with a clear plastic dust cover to protect the turntable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cover: a thing that lies on, over, or around something, especially in order to protect or conceal it
  • synonym – dust sheet: a cloth used to protect furniture or floors from dust, especially during periods of non-use or decoration (primarily British)
