north-south direction

noun
direction aligned with north and south
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ saʊθ daɪˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / nɔːθ saʊθ dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
a direction that runs parallel to lines of longitude, from north to south

Examples

north-south direction of a road
The mountain range extends mainly in a north-south direction.
The river flows in a north-south direction through the center of the country.
They built the highway to run in a north-south direction to connect the two states.

Related Terms

  • antonym – east-west direction: direction parallel to lines of latitude, east to west
