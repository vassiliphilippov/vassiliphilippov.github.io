mother-of-thousands

noun
a plant with red-and-white flowers that spreads by stolons
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌðər əv ˈθaʊzəndz / (AmE), / ˌmʌðə əv ˈθaʊzəndz / (BrE)
An eastern Asian saxifrage with clusters of small red-and-white flowers; it spreads by numerous creeping stolons.
