marketing research

noun
gathering and analyzing market information
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkətɪŋ rɪˈsɝtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtɪŋ rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the process of gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information about a market, including information about the target market, consumers, competitors, and the effectiveness of marketing strategies
