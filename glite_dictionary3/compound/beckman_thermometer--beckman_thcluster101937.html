Beckman thermometer

noun
precise mercury thermometer for small temperature changes
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛkmən θəˈmɑːrmətər / (AmE), / ˈbɛkmən θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
A mercury thermometer designed to measure very small differences or changes in temperature, often used in scientific experiments.
