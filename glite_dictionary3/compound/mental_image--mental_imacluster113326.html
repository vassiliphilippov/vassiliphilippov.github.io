mental image

noun
picture or scene in the mind
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛntl ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɛntl ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a picture or representation formed in the mind, especially one of something that is not actually present

Examples

form a mental image
Artists often use a mental image before beginning a new work.
Closing her eyes, she tried to recall the mental image of her childhood home.
The description in the novel helped me create a vivid mental image of the scene.
