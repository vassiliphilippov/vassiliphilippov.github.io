correspondence school

noun
school teaching by mail to nonresidents
Pronunciation: / kəˈspɑːndəns skuːl / (AmE), / kəˈspɒndəns skuːl / (BrE)
a school providing instruction to students by mail, allowing them to study from home without attending in person

Examples

enroll in a correspondence school
Enrolling in a correspondence school gave him the flexibility to study at his own pace.
Many people turned to correspondence schools for education in remote areas.
She earned her diploma from a correspondence school while working full-time.
