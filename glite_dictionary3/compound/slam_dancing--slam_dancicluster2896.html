slam dancing

noun
energetic dance with people slamming into each other
Pronunciation: / ˈslæm ˌdansɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslæm ˌdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of dancing in which participants forcefully bump or slam into each other, typically performed to punk rock music.

Examples

slam dancing at a concert
At the concert, many fans were slam dancing near the stage.
He injured his ankle while slam dancing at a punk show.
The energetic crowd started slam dancing as soon as the band began to play.

Related Terms

  • synonym – moshing: a similar style of dancing with aggressive movements, commonly at rock concerts
