pointing trowel

noun
small tool for finishing masonry joints
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ˌtraʊəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ˌtraʊəl / (BrE)
a small trowel with a pointed blade, used to fill and finish masonry joints with mortar or cement
