great millet

noun
a tropical cereal grass, edible grain
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈmɪlət / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈmɪlɪt / (BrE)
a tall cereal grass of tropical Africa cultivated for its edible grain, used in food for humans and animals
