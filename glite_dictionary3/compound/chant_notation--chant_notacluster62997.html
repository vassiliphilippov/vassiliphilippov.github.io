chant notation

noun
musical notation for Gregorian chant
Pronunciation: / tʃænt noʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / tʃɑːnt nəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a system of musical notation, traditionally using neumes, for transcribing Gregorian chant and other plainchant melodies
