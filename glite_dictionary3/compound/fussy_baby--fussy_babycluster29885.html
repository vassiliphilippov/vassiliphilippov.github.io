fussy baby

noun
a baby who is hard to calm
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌsi ˈbeɪbi / (AmE), / ˈfʌsi ˈbeɪbi / (BrE)
an infant who is difficult to calm or soothe, often crying or showing signs of discomfort

Examples

crying fussy baby
A warm bath sometimes helps calm a fussy baby before bedtime.
She carried her fussy baby around the house, gently rocking him until he fell asleep.
The new parents were exhausted after spending hours trying to comfort their fussy baby.
