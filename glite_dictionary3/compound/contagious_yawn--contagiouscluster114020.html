contagious yawn

noun
a yawn causing others to yawn
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪdʒəs jɔn / (AmE), / kənˈteɪdʒəs jɔːn / (BrE)
A yawn that prompts or induces others nearby to yawn as well.

Examples

a contagious yawn spreads
During the meeting, one contagious yawn set off a chain reaction among the attendees.
Seeing his friend’s contagious yawn, he couldn’t help but yawn as well.
The sound of her contagious yawn quickly spread around the office, making everyone sleepy.

  • synonym – yawn: an involuntary wide opening of the mouth with deep inhalation
