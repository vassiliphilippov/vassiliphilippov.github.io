Light Within

noun
Quaker belief in divine inner guidance
Pronunciation: / laɪt wɪˈðɪn / (AmE), / laɪt wɪˈðɪn / (BrE)
(in Quaker belief) a divine presence or inner light within each person, believed to enlighten and guide the soul.

Examples

the Light Within guides decisions
Many Quakers strive to listen to the Light Within as they make important decisions.
She felt guided by the Light Within during times of uncertainty.
The concept of the Light Within is central to Quaker spiritual practice.
