Montezuma cypress

noun
Mexican highland cypress tree species
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnteɪˈzuːmə ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˌmɒntɪˈzuːmə ˈsaɪprəs / (BrE)
a large cypress tree (Taxodium mucronatum) native to river valleys in the highlands of Mexico, known for its broad trunk and longevity
