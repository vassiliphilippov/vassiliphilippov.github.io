wild cinnamon

noun
a tropical aromatic shrub or tree
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈsɪnəmən / (AmE), / waɪld ˈsɪnəmən / (BrE)
a large evergreen shrub or small tree with aromatic white bark, leathery leaves, and small purple to red flowers, native to certain tropical regions

Examples

wild cinnamon tree in jungle
Botanists discovered a rare species of wild cinnamon deep in the jungle.
The aromatic bark of wild cinnamon is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
The forest was filled with the scent of wild cinnamon after the rain.
