at war

adjective
in a state of conflict or fighting
Pronunciation: / æt wɔr / (AmE), / at wɔː / (BrE)
engaged in active conflict or fighting, usually between countries, groups, or organizations.

Examples

at war with another country
Ever since the merger failed, the departments have been at war with each other.
The novel is set in a time when Europe was at war and uncertainty ruled.
The two neighboring countries have been at war for over a decade.
