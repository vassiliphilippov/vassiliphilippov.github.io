seed weevil

noun
beetle that infests legume seeds
Pronunciation: / sid ˈwiːvəl / (AmE), / siːd ˈwiːvəl / (BrE)
a small beetle that lays eggs in the seeds of legumes, where its larvae develop, often damaging the seeds

Examples

seed weevil infestation
Seed weevil infestations can significantly reduce crop yields for legume farmers.
After inspecting the seeds, they found evidence of seed weevil larvae feeding inside.
The farmer discovered several seed weevils inside the damaged bean pods.

Related Terms

  • synonym – weevil: a small beetle with a long snout, often a pest
