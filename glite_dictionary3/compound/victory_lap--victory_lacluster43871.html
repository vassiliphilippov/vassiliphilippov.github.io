victory lap

noun
celebratory lap after winning a race
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪktəri læp / (AmE), / ˈvɪktəri læp / (BrE)
an extra lap taken by the winner of a race or competition to celebrate their victory

Examples

victory lap after winning a race
After crossing the finish line first, she took a victory lap around the stadium, waving to the cheering crowd.
Fans lined up to watch the drivers as they made their victory lap at the end of the race.
The team celebrated their championship win with a jubilant victory lap around the track.
