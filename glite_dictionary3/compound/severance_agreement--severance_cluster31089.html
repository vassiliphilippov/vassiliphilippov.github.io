severance agreement

noun
contract about leaving a job
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛvɚəns əˌɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛvərəns əˌɡriːmənt / (BrE)
a legal document outlining the terms and conditions under which an employee leaves a company, often including compensation or benefits

Examples

sign a severance agreement
Before leaving, he negotiated a severance agreement with favorable terms.
Her severance agreement included several months of salary and health benefits.
The employee and the company signed a severance agreement after the layoff.

Related Terms

  • synonym – severance package: the compensation and benefits an employee receives when leaving a company
