civil law

noun
Roman-derived legal system; codified law
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪv.əl lɔ /; / ˈsɪvəl lɔ / (AmE), / ˈsɪv.əl lɔː /; / ˈsɪvəl lɔː / (BrE)
the system of law that deals with private matters between people (not criminal, military, or religious law), especially the legal tradition based on Roman law used in many European countries
