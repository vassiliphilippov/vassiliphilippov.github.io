technical analysis

noun
forecasting prices by analyzing past market trends
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛknɪkəl əˈnæləsɪs /; / ˈtɛknɪkəl əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˌtɛknɪkəl əˈnalɪsɪs /; / ˈtɛknɪkəl əˈnalɪsɪs / (BrE)
the study of past market prices and trading volumes to predict future trends in financial markets

Examples

technical analysis of stock prices
He used technical analysis to predict when the stock price would reach its next high point.
