reticulated python

noun
very large Asian snake; world's longest
Pronunciation: / rəˈtɪkjəˌleɪtɪd ˈpaɪθɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd ˈpʌɪθən / (BrE)
a very large, nonvenomous snake of southeast Asia and the East Indies, known for its net-like skin pattern; the longest snake species in the world
