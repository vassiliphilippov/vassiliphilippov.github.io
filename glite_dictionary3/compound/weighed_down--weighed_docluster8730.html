weighed down

adjective
burdened with weight or responsibility
Pronunciation: / weɪd daʊn / (AmE), / weɪd daʊn / (BrE)
carrying a great weight so as to be burdened or slowed, either physically or figuratively

Examples

weighed down with heavy bags
His backpack was weighed down with books and supplies for his classes.
She felt weighed down by the pressure to succeed at work.
The old branches were weighed down with snow after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – burdened: carrying a heavy load or responsibility
  • antonym – unburdened: not burdened; free from a load or responsibility
