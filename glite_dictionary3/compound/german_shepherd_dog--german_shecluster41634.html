German shepherd dog

noun
breed of large herding dog
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːmən ˈʃɛpɚd dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˌdʒɜːmən ˈʃɛpəd dɒɡ / (BrE)
A breed of large, intelligent dog originally developed in Germany, commonly used as a police dog or guide dog for the blind.
