adductor muscle

noun
muscle that draws a part toward the body's midline
Pronunciation: / əˈdʌktər ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / əˈdʌktə ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
A muscle that draws a body part toward the median line of the body or of a limb.

Examples
