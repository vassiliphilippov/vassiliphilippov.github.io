satyr play

noun
ancient Greek comic play with satyrs
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.tɚ pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.tə pleɪ / (BrE)
an ancient Greek play that combines elements of tragedy with burlesque comedy, featuring a chorus of satyrs
