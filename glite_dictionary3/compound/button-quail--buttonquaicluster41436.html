button-quail

noun
small terrestrial bird resembling a quail
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtən kweɪl / (AmE), / ˈbʌtən kweɪl / (BrE)
A small, quail-like terrestrial bird found in southern Eurasia and northern Africa, lacking a hind toe and inhabiting grassy plains.
