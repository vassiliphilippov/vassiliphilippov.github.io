reverse Polish notation

noun
parenthesis-free mathematical notation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɝːs ˈpoʊlɪʃ noʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs ˈpɒlɪʃ nəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a mathematical notation in which every operator follows all of its operands, eliminating the need for parentheses
