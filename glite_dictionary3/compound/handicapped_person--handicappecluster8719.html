handicapped person

noun
person with a limiting disability
Pronunciation: / ˈhændiˌkæpt ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / ˈhændiˌkæpt ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
A person with a physical, mental, or social condition that significantly limits their ability to function.

Examples

support a handicapped person
He volunteered at an organization that supports every handicapped person in the community.
The school has ramps and other facilities to help a handicapped person access the building safely.
Using the term 'handicapped person' is discouraged in favor of more respectful language such as 'person with a disability'.
