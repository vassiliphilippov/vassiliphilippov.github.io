microcytic anaemia

noun
type of anaemia with small red blood cells
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪ.krə(ʊ)ˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə / (BrE)
A type of anaemia in which the average size of red blood cells is smaller than normal.

Examples

microcytic anaemia diagnosis
Blood tests revealed that she was suffering from microcytic anaemia.
Doctors often check mean cell volume to diagnose microcytic anaemia.
Iron deficiency is the most common cause of microcytic anaemia worldwide.
