colic root

noun
plant of genus Aletris, used medicinally
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːlɪk ruːt / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪk ruːt / (BrE)
any of several perennial herbaceous plants of the genus Aletris, having grasslike leaves and bitter roots formerly used as a remedy for colic.
