finishing coat

noun
final layer applied to a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ koʊt / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ kəʊt / (BrE)
the last layer of paint, plaster, varnish, or similar material applied to a surface to complete the finish
