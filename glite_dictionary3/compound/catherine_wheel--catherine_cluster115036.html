Catherine wheel

noun
spinning circular firework
Pronunciation: / ˈkæθərɪn wiːl / (AmE), / ˈkæθərɪn wiːl / (BrE)
A type of circular firework that spins rapidly when ignited, sending out a display of colored sparks.

Examples

spinning Catherine wheel firework
