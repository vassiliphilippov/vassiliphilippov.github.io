sphere of influence

noun
area of power or influence
Pronunciation: / sfɪr əv ɪnˈfluəns / (AmE), / sfɪə ɒv ɪnˈfluəns / (BrE)
an area in which a person, organization, or country has power to affect events or decisions, often beyond its formal authority

Examples

within his sphere of influence
After years of work, his sphere of influence now extends to government policy.
Her innovative ideas quickly expanded her sphere of influence within the organization.
International companies often compete for a larger sphere of influence in emerging markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym – control: the power to influence or direct people's behavior or the course of events
  • synonym – domain: an area or field over which someone has control or influence
