puffed wheat

noun
light, expanded wheat grains (cereal)
Pronunciation: / pʌft wiːt / (AmE), / pʌft wiːt / (BrE)
Wheat grains that have been heated until they expand and become light and puffy, often eaten as a breakfast cereal.

Examples

bowl of puffed wheat
For breakfast, she poured herself a bowl of puffed wheat with cold milk.
Many cereals use puffed wheat as a healthy base ingredient.
The company introduced a new chocolate bar made with puffed wheat.
