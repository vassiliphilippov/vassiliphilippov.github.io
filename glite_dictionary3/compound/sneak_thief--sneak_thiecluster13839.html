sneak thief

noun
a nonviolent, stealthy thief
Pronunciation: / sniːk θiːf / (AmE), / sniːk θiːf / (BrE)
A thief who steals furtively and without the use of violence.

Examples

a sneak thief steals wallets
A sneak thief stole her purse while she was browsing in the store.
Police warned shoppers to be on guard against sneak thieves during the busy holiday season.
The museum increased its security after a sneak thief took a valuable necklace.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pickpocket: A person who steals from people's pockets or bags in public places
  • antonym – robber: A person who steals, sometimes using force or violence
