voting booth

noun
private enclosure for casting votes
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊtɪŋ buːθ / (AmE), / ˈvəʊtɪŋ buːθ / (BrE)
A small, private enclosure where a person casts their vote in an election.

Examples

inside a voting booth
Election officials ensured each voting booth was secure and accessible to all voters.
Lines formed outside the gymnasium as people waited for an open voting booth.
She stepped into the voting booth to mark her ballot in private.
