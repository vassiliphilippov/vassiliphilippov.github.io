luncheon meat

noun
cooked, sliced processed meat
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌn.tʃən mit / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.tʃən miːt / (BrE)
a type of cooked, processed meat, usually sold in a can or loaf, that is sliced and served cold

Examples
