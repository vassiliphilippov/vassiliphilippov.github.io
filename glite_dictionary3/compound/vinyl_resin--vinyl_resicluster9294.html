vinyl resin

noun
thermoplastic polymer derived from vinyl compounds
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.nəl ˈrɛz.ɪn / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.nɪl ˈrɛz.ɪn / (BrE)
