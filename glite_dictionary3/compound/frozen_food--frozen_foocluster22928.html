frozen food

noun
food preserved by freezing
Pronunciation: / ˈfroʊzən fud / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən fuːd / (BrE)
Food that has been preserved by freezing to prevent spoilage and extend its shelf life.

Examples

buy frozen food
Most supermarkets offer a wide selection of frozen food such as vegetables, pizzas, and desserts.
She bought a variety of frozen food to save time on cooking during the week.
To prepare for the storm, they stocked up on canned and frozen food.
