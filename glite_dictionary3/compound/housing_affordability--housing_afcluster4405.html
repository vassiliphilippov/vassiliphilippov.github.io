housing affordability

noun
cost of housing compared to income
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊzɪŋ əˌfɔrdəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ əˌfɔːdəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the extent to which housing is affordable to people, typically measured by the cost of housing relative to income

Examples

housing affordability crisis
Improving housing affordability has become a key goal of urban policy makers.
Many cities are facing a significant housing affordability crisis as prices continue to rise.
The government is introducing measures to boost housing affordability for first-time buyers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – affordable housing: housing that is reasonably priced relative to income
