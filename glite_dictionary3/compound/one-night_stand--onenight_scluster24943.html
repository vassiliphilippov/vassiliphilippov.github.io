one-night stand

noun
brief sexual encounter or relationship
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌnˌnaɪt ˈstænd / (AmE), / ˈwʌn naɪt ˈstænd /; / ˈwʌn.naɪt ˈstænd / (BrE)
a brief sexual encounter or relationship that lasts for only one night, with no expectation of further relationship or commitment
