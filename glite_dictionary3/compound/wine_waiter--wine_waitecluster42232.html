wine waiter

noun
waiter responsible for serving wine
Pronunciation: / waɪn ˈweɪtər / (AmE), / waɪn ˈweɪtə / (BrE)
a waiter in a hotel or restaurant whose responsibility is to manage and serve wine to guests
