solid-propellant motor

noun
type of rocket engine using solid fuel
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlɪd prəˈpɛl(ə)nt ˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪd prəˈpɛl(ə)nt ˈməʊtə / (BrE)
