funny bone

noun
point on elbow causing tingling when struck
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌni boʊn / (AmE), / ˈfʌni bəʊn / (BrE)
the point on the elbow where the ulnar nerve passes close to the surface, which causes a sharp tingling sensation when knocked

Examples

hit your funny bone
He accidentally hit his funny bone on the table and winced in pain.
She laughed as her friend complained about hitting his funny bone again.
When you bump your funny bone, the tingling feeling can be quite intense.
