tax return

noun
official tax filing document
Pronunciation: / tæks rɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / tæks rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
an official document that a person or business submits to the tax authorities, detailing income and other relevant information used to calculate tax owed or refund due

Examples

file a tax return
After reviewing her tax return, she realized she was eligible for a refund.
Businesses are required to submit an annual tax return to the government.
You must file your tax return by the end of April to avoid penalties.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tax form: a form used to report income and calculate taxes owed
