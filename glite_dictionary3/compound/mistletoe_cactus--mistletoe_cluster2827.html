mistletoe cactus

noun
a tropical epiphytic cactus (Rhipsalis)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsəlˌtoʊ ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪsəltəʊ ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
A tropical epiphytic cactus of the genus Rhipsalis, with slender stems and small white or pinkish fruit, often found growing on trees.
