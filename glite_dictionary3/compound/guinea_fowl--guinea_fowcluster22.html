guinea fowl

noun
African bird with spotted plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪni ˌfaʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡɪni ˌfaʊl / (BrE)
a domesticated African bird with dark plumage speckled with white, often raised for its meat and eggs

Examples

raise guinea fowl for eggs
A flock of guinea fowl wandered noisily across the dusty yard.
Dishes made with guinea fowl are popular in many African and European cuisines.
The farmer raised several guinea fowl alongside his chickens for their tasty eggs.
