directed study

noun
supervised independent academic course
Pronunciation: / daɪˈrɛktɪd ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktɪd ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
an academic course in which a student undertakes independent work under the supervision and guidance of a specialist in the subject

Examples

directed study project
His thesis project was part of a directed study supervised by the department head.
She completed a directed study in environmental science with her professor this semester.
You must obtain instructor approval before registering for a directed study course.
