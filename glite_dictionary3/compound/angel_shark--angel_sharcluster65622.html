angel shark

noun
a broad, flat-bodied shark species
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a type of shark with a broad, flat body and winglike pectoral fins, resembling a ray but swimming like other sharks
