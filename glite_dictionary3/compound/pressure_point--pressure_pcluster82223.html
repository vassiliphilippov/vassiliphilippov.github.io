pressure point

noun
point on body to control bleeding
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃər pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a place on the body where pressure can be applied to an artery to control bleeding at a point further from the heart

Examples

pressure point on artery
Paramedics are trained to identify each pressure point in case of emergencies.
She pressed firmly on the pressure point in his arm until the bleeding stopped.
When treating a severe cut, apply direct pressure over the pressure point to slow the bleeding.
