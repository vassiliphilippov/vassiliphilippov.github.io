half track

noun
vehicle track for only rear wheels
Pronunciation: / hæf træk / (AmE), / hɑːf træk / (BrE)
A continuous track fitted to the rear wheels of a vehicle, typically leaving the front wheels as regular tires.

Examples

military half track
Engineers restored a vintage half track for the military museum display.
The old army half track moved slowly across the muddy field.
This vehicle was converted to a half track to improve traction on rough terrain.
