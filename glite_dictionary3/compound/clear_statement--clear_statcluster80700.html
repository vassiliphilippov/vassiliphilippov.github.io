clear statement

noun
obvious or unmistakable expression or indication
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪr ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈklɪə ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an expression or indication that is unmistakable and easy to understand

Examples

make a clear statement
Her refusal to attend was seen as a clear statement of her disapproval.
The company's response was a clear statement that they would not tolerate discrimination.
The judge made a clear statement regarding the consequences of breaking the law.
