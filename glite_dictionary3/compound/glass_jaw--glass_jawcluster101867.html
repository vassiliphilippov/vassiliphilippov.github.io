glass jaw

noun
vulnerability to being easily defeated
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs dʒɔ / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs dʒɔː / (BrE)
a vulnerability to being easily knocked out or defeated, especially in combat sports or competition

Examples

a glass jaw in boxing
Despite his impressive physique, his glass jaw was well-known among his opponents.
She criticized the team's glass jaw when it came to handling pressure in big games.
The fighter's glass jaw ended his championship dreams after a single hard punch.

  • synonym – weakness: lack of strength or resilience
