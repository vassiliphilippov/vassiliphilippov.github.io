smart aleck

noun
person who makes annoying clever remarks
Pronunciation: / smɑrt ˈælək / (AmE), / smɑːt ˈælək / (BrE)
A person who behaves as if they know everything and enjoys making sarcastic or clever remarks that annoy others.

Examples

smart aleck makes jokes
He acted like a smart aleck during the meeting and annoyed everyone with his comments.
Nobody likes working with a smart aleck who always has a sarcastic comeback.
The teacher quickly tired of the smart aleck interrupting the lesson with witty remarks.

  • synonym – wise guy: a person who acts as if they know everything, often in an annoying way
