throwing stick

noun
a curved stick that returns when thrown
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊɪŋ stɪk / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊɪŋ stɪk / (BrE)
a curved piece of wood that, when thrown correctly, returns to the thrower; commonly associated with the boomerang
