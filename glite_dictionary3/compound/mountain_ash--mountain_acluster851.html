mountain ash

noun
a tree of the genus Sorbus
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən æʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn æʃ / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Sorbus (such as the rowan), with pinnate leaves and clusters of white flowers and red or orange berries
