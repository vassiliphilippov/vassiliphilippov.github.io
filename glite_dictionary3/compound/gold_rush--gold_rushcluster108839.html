gold rush

noun
sudden migration to a new gold discovery
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊld rʌʃ / (AmE), / ɡəʊld rʌʃ / (BrE)
a period or event when many people suddenly move to a place where gold has been discovered, hoping to get rich
