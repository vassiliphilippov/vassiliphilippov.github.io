polyester fiber

noun
synthetic fiber made from polyester
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈɛstər ˈfaɪbər / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈɛstə ˈfaɪbə / (BrE)
a quick-drying, resilient synthetic fiber made primarily from polyester, used especially in textiles

Examples

polyester fiber clothing
Many clothes today are made from polyester fiber because it is both durable and easy to care for.
The tent's outer layer is constructed with polyester fiber to make it water-resistant.
Unlike cotton, polyester fiber does not shrink or wrinkle easily after washing.
