swamp honeysuckle

noun
shrub with honeysuckle-like flowers, found in eastern U.S. swamps
Pronunciation: / swɑmp ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / swɒmp ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (BrE)
