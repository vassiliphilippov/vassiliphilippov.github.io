grey area

noun
unclear or ambiguous area
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈeəriə / (BrE)
a situation or topic that is not clearly defined, making it difficult to know what is right, acceptable, or how to decide

Examples
