fast food

noun
quickly prepared inexpensive food
Pronunciation: / fæst ˈfuːd / (AmE), / fɑːst ˈfuːd / (BrE)
Inexpensive food, such as hamburgers or fried chicken, prepared and served quickly, typically by restaurants specializing in quick service.

Examples

fast food restaurant
