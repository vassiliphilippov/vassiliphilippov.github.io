cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator

noun
gene mutated in cystic fibrosis
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪstɪk faɪˈbroʊsɪs trænzˈmɛmˌbreɪn kənˈdʌktəns rɪˈɡjuleɪtər / (AmE), / ˌsɪstɪk faɪˈbrəʊsɪs trænzˈmɛmˌbreɪn kənˈdʌktəns rɪˈɡjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
the gene that encodes a membrane protein responsible for regulating the movement of chloride and sodium ions across epithelial cells; mutations in this gene cause cystic fibrosis
