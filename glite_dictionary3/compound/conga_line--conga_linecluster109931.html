conga line

noun
line of dancers performing the conga
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡə laɪn /; / ˈkɑːŋɡə laɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡə laɪn / (BrE)
a group of people forming a line and dancing the conga, usually by placing their hands on the person in front and moving together to music
