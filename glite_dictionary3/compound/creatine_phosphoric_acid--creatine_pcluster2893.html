creatine phosphoric acid

noun
an energy-storing compound in muscle tissue
Pronunciation: / ˈkriː.əˌtin ˌfɔsˈfɔrɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˈkriː.əˌtiːn ˌfɒsˈfɒrɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
an organic compound found in the muscles of vertebrates that stores energy and releases it during muscular contraction
