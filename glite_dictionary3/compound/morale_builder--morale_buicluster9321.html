morale builder

noun
something that improves morale
Pronunciation: / məˈræl ˌbɪldər / (AmE), / məˈrɑːl ˌbɪldə / (BrE)
a person or thing that helps improve confidence, enthusiasm, or a positive attitude in a group or team

Examples

The company retreat turned out to be a real morale builder for the employees.
Sometimes, a simple thank-you can be a powerful morale builder.
The coach acted as a real morale builder during the tough season.
Weekly recognition of achievements serves as a morale builder in the workplace.
