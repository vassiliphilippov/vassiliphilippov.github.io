tabula rasa

noun
the mind as a blank slate; unmarked by experience
Pronunciation: / ˌtæbjələ ˈrɑsə / (AmE), / ˌtabjʊlə ˈrɑːsə /; / ˌtabjʊlə ˈrɑːzə / (BrE)
the idea of a mind or situation as a blank slate, unmarked by experience or existing content, ready to be shaped
