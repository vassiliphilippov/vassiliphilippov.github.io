arborescent plant

noun
plant with tree-like form
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrbəˈrɛsənt plænt / (AmE), / ˌɑːbəˈrɛsənt plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that has the shape or characteristics of a tree, typically with a single stem and a branching canopy.

Examples

tall arborescent plant
Botanists classified the unusual specimen as an arborescent plant due to its tall, branching structure.
Some ferns have evolved into arborescent plants with woody stems resembling small trees.
The cactus is an arborescent plant that can reach impressive heights in the desert.
