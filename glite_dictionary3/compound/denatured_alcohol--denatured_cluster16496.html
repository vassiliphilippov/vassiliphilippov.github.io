denatured alcohol

noun
ethanol rendered undrinkable by additives
Pronunciation: / dɪˈneɪtʃɚd ˈælkəhɔl / (AmE), / dɪˈneɪtʃəd ˈælkəhɒl / (BrE)
Ethanol that has been made unfit for drinking by the addition of chemicals, used for cleaning, fuel, and industrial purposes.
