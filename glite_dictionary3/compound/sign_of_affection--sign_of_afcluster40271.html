sign of affection

noun
gesture expressing love or fondness
Pronunciation: / saɪn əv əˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / saɪn əv əˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
a gesture or action that shows love, fondness, or warmth toward someone

Examples

a sign of affection like a hug

Giving someone a warm hug is often seen as a sign of affection.
He left flowers on her desk as a small sign of affection.
