copy editor

noun
person who edits text before publication
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpi ˌɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkɒpi ˌɛdɪtə / (BrE)
a person who edits and prepares written material for publication by correcting errors in grammar, style, and accuracy

Examples

copy editor checks grammar
Every manuscript is reviewed by a copy editor before being published online.
She works as a copy editor at a major newspaper, ensuring articles are clear and accurate.
The copy editor caught several mistakes before the book went to print.
