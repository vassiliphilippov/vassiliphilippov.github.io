aminoalkanoic acid

noun
an organic compound with amino and carboxylic acid groups
Pronunciation: / əˌminoʊˌælkəˈnoʊɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / əˌmiːnəʊˌalkəˈnəʊɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
an organic compound containing both an amino group (–NH₂) and a carboxylic acid group (–COOH), forming a principal type of amino acid
