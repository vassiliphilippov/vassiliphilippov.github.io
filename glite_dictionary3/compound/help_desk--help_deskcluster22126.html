help desk

noun
customer/technical support service
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlp dɛsk / (AmE), / ˈhɛlp dɛsk / (BrE)
a service or department that provides information and assistance, especially for computer users or customers needing technical support

Examples

call the help desk for support
If you encounter any issues, please contact the help desk for assistance.
She called the help desk when her software suddenly stopped working.
The company set up a help desk to resolve employees' computer problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – customer support: assistance provided to customers before, during, and after purchasing products
  • synonym – technical support: assistance given to users having technical problems with electronic devices
