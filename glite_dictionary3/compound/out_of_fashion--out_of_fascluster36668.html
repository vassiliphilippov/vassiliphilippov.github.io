out of fashion

adjective
no longer fashionable or trendy
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / aʊt əv ˈfæʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
no longer popular, stylish, or in trend at a particular time

Examples

out of fashion clothes
Bell-bottom jeans are out of fashion these days.
Certain words in the English language fall out of fashion over time.
He refuses to throw away his old jacket, even though it's out of fashion.
