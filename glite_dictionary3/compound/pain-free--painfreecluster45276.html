pain-free

adjective
without pain; not causing pain
Pronunciation: / peɪn friː / (AmE), / peɪn friː / (BrE)
Not causing or accompanied by physical pain.

Examples

pain-free recovery
A pain-free birth experience is possible with the right support and preparation.
After switching medications, her movements became nearly pain-free.
The dentist promised the procedure would be completely pain-free.
