chest tone

noun
lower vocal register in singing or speech
Pronunciation: / tʃɛst toʊn / (AmE), / tʃɛst təʊn / (BrE)
the lower range or register of the human voice, especially as used in speaking or singing, characterized by resonance in the chest area
