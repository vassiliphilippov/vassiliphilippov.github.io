kick starter

noun
motorcycle lever for starting engine with foot
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪk ˌstɑrtɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɪk ˌstɑːtə / (BrE)
a lever on a motorcycle or similar vehicle used to start the engine by pushing it down with the foot

Examples

kick starter lever
After fixing the engine, she tried the kick starter to see if it would work.
He had to use all his weight to push down the kick starter.
The old motorcycle still has a kick starter instead of an electric ignition.
