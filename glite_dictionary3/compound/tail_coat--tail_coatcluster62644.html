tail coat

noun
formal men's evening jacket with tails
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪl ˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈteɪl ˌkəʊt / (BrE)
A type of formal evening jacket for men, with a short front and long double tails at the back, worn as part of full evening dress.

Examples

tail coat evening event
He wore a crisp tail coat and white tie to the gala event.
In the 19th century, gentlemen would never attend a ball without a proper tail coat.
The orchestra conductor's traditional tail coat added an air of elegance to the performance.
