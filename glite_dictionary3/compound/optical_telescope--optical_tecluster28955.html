optical telescope

noun
instrument for observing with visible light
Pronunciation: / ˈɑptɪkəl ˈtɛlɪˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkəl ˈtelɪskəʊp / (BrE)
an astronomical instrument that gathers and focuses visible light to observe distant objects
