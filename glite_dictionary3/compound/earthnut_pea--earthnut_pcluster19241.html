earthnut pea

noun
tuber-producing European plant
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθ.nʌt pi / (AmE), / ˈɜːθ.nʌt piː / (BrE)
A European plant (Lathyrus tuberosus) having small edible tubers, used as food and, in Scotland, to flavor whiskey.
