conscience money

noun
voluntary payment to atone for dishonesty
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnʃəns ˌmʌni /; / ˈkɑnʃəns ˌmʌni / (AmE), / ˈkɒnʃəns ˌmʌni / (BrE)
money given voluntarily to make up for past dishonesty or wrongdoing, usually to relieve feelings of guilt
