Asian Russia

proper noun
the Asian part of Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪʒən ˈrʌʃə / (AmE), / ˈeɪʒ(ɪ)ən ˈrʌʃə / (BrE)
the part of Russia located in the continent of Asia, east of the Ural Mountains

Examples

Asian Russia covers Siberia
