nicotinic acid

noun
a B vitamin (vitamin B3); essential nutrient
Pronunciation: / nɪkəˈtɪnɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / nɪkəˈtɪnɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
a form of vitamin B3; a B vitamin essential for normal nerve and digestive system function

Examples
