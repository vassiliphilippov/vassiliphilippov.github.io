sweet cicely

noun
a European aromatic herb with fern-like leaves
Pronunciation: / swit ˈsɪsəli / (AmE), / swiːt ˈsɪsɪli / (BrE)
a European herb (Myrrhis odorata) with fern-like leaves and a sweet aniseed flavor, used as a garden plant, garnish, or flavoring
