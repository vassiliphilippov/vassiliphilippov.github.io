winter cherry

noun
ornamental shrub with cherry-like fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˌtʃɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˌtʃɛr.i / (BrE)
a small South American shrub grown as a houseplant, valued for its abundant ornamental but poisonous red or yellow cherry-sized fruit

Examples

winter cherry houseplant
Although the winter cherry is attractive, you should keep it away from pets and children.
Many people keep a winter cherry on their windowsill for decoration, unaware that its berries are toxic.
The bright red fruit of the winter cherry stands out against its dark green leaves.
