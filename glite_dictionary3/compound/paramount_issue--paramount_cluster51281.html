paramount issue

noun
most important issue requiring settlement
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌmaʊnt ˈɪʃuː / (AmE), / ˈpærəmaʊnt ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
an issue that is more important than all others and must be resolved before any other matters can be addressed

Examples

the paramount issue facing us
Before we move on, we need to address this paramount issue that affects the entire project.
Education reform has become the paramount issue in the upcoming election debates.
The board agreed that climate change is the paramount issue facing the company today.

Related Terms

  • synonym – priority issue: an issue that is treated as more important than others
  • antonym – secondary issue: an issue of less importance than more urgent matters
