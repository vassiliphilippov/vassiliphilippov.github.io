stinky squid

noun
a type of stinkhorn fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋki skwɪd / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋki skwɪd / (BrE)
A type of stinkhorn fungus of the genus Pseudocolus, whose fruiting body first appears as a small puffball and later develops tapering arms that arch and narrow to a common point.
