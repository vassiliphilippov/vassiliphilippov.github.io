giant seaperch

noun
large edible sporting fish species
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈsiːˌpɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈsiːˌpɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A large species of perch known for its sporting and eating qualities, found in marine, estuarine, and freshwater environments.
