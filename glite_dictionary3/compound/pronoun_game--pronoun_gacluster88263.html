pronoun game

noun
evasive use of pronouns to avoid clarity
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊˌnaʊn ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊnaʊn ɡeɪm / (BrE)
the practice of evading directness in conversation by using pronouns instead of specific names, often to avoid accountability or reveal information
