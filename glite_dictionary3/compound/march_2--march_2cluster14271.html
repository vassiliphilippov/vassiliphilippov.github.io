Texas Independence Day (March 2)
Pronunciation: / mɑrtʃ ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / mɑːtʃ ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
In Texas, the date commemorating the anniversary of the state's declaration of independence from Mexico in 1836.
