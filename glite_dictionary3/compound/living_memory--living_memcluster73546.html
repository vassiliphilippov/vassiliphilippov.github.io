living memory

noun
time period people can still recall
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈmɛməri / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˈmɛməri / (BrE)
the period of time within a person's or group's life that can be remembered by people still alive
