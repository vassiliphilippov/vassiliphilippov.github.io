Coffea robusta

noun
species of coffee plant
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔfi ə roʊˈbʌstə / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ə rəʊˈbʌstə / (BrE)
A species of coffee plant, native to West Africa, widely grown for its beans, which are resistant to coffee rust and used in commercial coffee production.

Examples

Many instant coffees are made from Coffea robusta beans due to their strong flavor.
