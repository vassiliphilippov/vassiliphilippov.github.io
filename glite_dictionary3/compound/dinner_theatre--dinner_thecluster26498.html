dinner theatre

noun
theater with dinner included
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪnɚ ˌθiətər / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə ˌθɪətə / (BrE)
A theater where a meal is served to the audience as part of the price of admission to a performance.

Examples

dinner theatre show
Many Broadway actors have performed in dinner theatres early in their careers.
The city’s new dinner theatre offers guests a three-course meal and a comedy show.
We spent our anniversary at a local dinner theatre, enjoying both the meal and the play.
