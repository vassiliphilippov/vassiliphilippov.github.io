to smatter paint

verb
apply paint carelessly or lightly
Pronunciation: / ˈsmætər peɪnt / (AmE), / ˈsmætə peɪnt / (BrE)
to apply paint in a light or careless way; to paint in a scattered or uneven manner

Examples
