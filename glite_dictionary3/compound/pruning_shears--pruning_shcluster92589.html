pruning shears

noun
garden tool for cutting plants
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnɪŋ ʃɪrz / (AmE), / ˈpruːnɪŋ ʃɪəz / (BrE)
A type of strong, hand-held scissors used for cutting small branches and stems of woody plants.
