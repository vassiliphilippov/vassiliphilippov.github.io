Gulf of Aden

proper noun
arm of the Indian Ocean, near Yemen and Somalia
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌlf əv ˈeɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌlf əv ˈeɪdn̩ / (BrE)
a gulf of the Indian Ocean situated between Yemen on the Arabian Peninsula and Somalia in eastern Africa, forming the entrance to the Red Sea

Examples

the Gulf of Aden shipping route
