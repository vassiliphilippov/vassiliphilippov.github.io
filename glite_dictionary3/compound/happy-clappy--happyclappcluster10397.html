happy-clappy

adjective
excessively cheerful or enthusiastic (often religious)
Pronunciation: / ˌhæpiˈklæpi / (AmE), / ˌhæpiˈklæpi / (BrE)
(usually derogatory) characterized by overt and sometimes excessive cheerfulness and enthusiasm, particularly in religious or social contexts.

Examples

happy-clappy church service
At the conference, his happy-clappy style didn't appeal to the more serious attendees.
The new manager introduced a happy-clappy approach to team building, which some employees found exhausting.
Their church services are known for their happy-clappy music and enthusiastic worship.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exuberant: full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness
  • antonym – solemn: serious, dignified, not cheerful or energetic
