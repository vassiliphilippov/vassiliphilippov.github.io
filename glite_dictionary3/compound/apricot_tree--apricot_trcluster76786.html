apricot tree

noun
tree producing apricots
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.rɪ.kɑt triː / (AmE), / ˈæp.rɪ.kɒt triː / (BrE)
an Asian tree with clusters of usually white blossoms and edible fruit resembling a peach, from which apricots are obtained
