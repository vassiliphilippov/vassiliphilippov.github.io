submandibular salivary gland

noun
salivary gland beneath the jaw
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌb.mænˈdɪb.jələr ˈsæ.ləˌvɛri ɡlænd / (AmE), / ˌsʌb.mænˈdɪb.jʊ.lə ˈsæl.ɪ.vəri ɡlænd / (BrE)
one of a pair of salivary glands situated beneath the lower jaw that produce saliva, particularly at night, and release it under the tongue
