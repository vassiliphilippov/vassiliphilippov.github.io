good night's sleep

noun
restful, restorative night of sleep
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd naɪts sliːp / (AmE), / ɡʊd naɪts sliːp / (BrE)
A period of sleep during the night that is sufficiently restful to restore energy and improve well-being.
