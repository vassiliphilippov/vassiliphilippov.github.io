thoroughbred race

noun
race between thoroughbred horses
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜːroʊˌbrɛd reɪs / (AmE), / ˈθʌrəbɹɛd reɪs / (BrE)
A horse race in which only thoroughbred horses are allowed to compete.

Examples

famous thoroughbred race
