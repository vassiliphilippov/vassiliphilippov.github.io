midday sun

noun
the sun at or near noon
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌdeɪ sʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌdeɪ sʌn / (BrE)
the sun when it is at or near its highest point in the sky, typically around noon, and often very bright or intense

Examples

hot midday sun
The crops withered in the intense midday sun.

Working in the midday sun can lead to heat exhaustion if you're not careful.
