Tropic of Cancer

noun
line of latitude 23.5° north of Equator
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːpɪk əv ˈkænsər / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪk əv ˈkɑːnsə / (BrE)
the parallel of latitude approximately 23.5° north of the Equator, marking the northernmost point at which the sun can appear directly overhead

Examples

the Tropic of Cancer line
Deserts near the Tropic of Cancer are known for their hot and dry climates.
The Tropic of Cancer passes through several countries including Mexico, Egypt, and India.
The sun is directly overhead at the Tropic of Cancer during the June solstice.
