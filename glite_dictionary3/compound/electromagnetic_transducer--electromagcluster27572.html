electromagnetic transducer

noun
device converting electromagnetic and electrical signals
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊmæɡˌnɛtɪk trænzˈdjuːsər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊmæɡˌnɛtɪk trænzˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
A device that converts electromagnetic waves or magnetic fields to and from electrical signals.

Examples

electromagnetic transducer in a speaker
Each speaker contains an electromagnetic transducer that converts electrical signals into sound.
