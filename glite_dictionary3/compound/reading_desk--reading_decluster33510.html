reading desk

noun
desk with slanted top for reading
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdɪŋ dɛsk / (AmE), / ˈriːdɪŋ dɛsk / (BrE)
a desk or stand with a slanted top used to hold a book or text at a convenient height for reading or lecturing

Examples

reading desk in a library
A sturdy reading desk stood in the corner of the library, inviting visitors to browse its books.
She adjusted the height of the reading desk to get a better view of her manuscript.
