wood sage

noun
a European plant with yellowish flowers
Pronunciation: / wʊd seɪdʒ / (AmE), / wʊd seɪdʒ / (BrE)
a European plant of the mint family with one-sided racemes of yellow flowers (Teucrium scorodonia), sometimes naturalized in North America
