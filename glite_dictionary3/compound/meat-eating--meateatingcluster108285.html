meat-eating

adjective
carnivorous; feeding on meat
Pronunciation: / ˈmitˌitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtˌiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of animals) feeding on the flesh of other animals; carnivorous.

Examples

meat-eating animal
Despite their fearsome appearance, not all large reptiles are meat-eating animals.
Several meat-eating dinosaurs once roamed this prehistoric landscape.
The meat-eating lion hunted silently as dusk fell over the savannah.
