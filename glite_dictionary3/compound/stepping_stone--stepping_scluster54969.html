stepping stone

noun
means of advancement; aid to progress
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpɪŋ stoʊn /; / ˈstɛpɪŋ ˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈstɛpɪŋ stəʊn /; / ˈstɛpɪŋ ˌstəʊn / (BrE)
