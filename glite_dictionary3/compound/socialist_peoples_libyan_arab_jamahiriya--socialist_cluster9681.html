Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya

proper noun
Libya's official name (1977–2011) under Gaddafi
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəˌlɪst ˈpipəlz lɪˈbiːən ˈærəb dʒəˌmɑːhɪˈriːə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlɪst ˈpiːpəlz lɪˈbɪən ˈærəb dʒəˌmɑːhɪˈriːə / (BrE)
the official name of Libya under the rule of Muammar Gaddafi from 1977 to 2011, a country in North Africa known for its desert landscape and oil exports
