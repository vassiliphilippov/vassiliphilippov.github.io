crepe rubber

noun
crude, natural rubber material
Pronunciation: / kreɪp ˈrʌbər / (AmE), / kreɪp ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
A crude, natural rubber material with a crinkled texture, used mainly for shoe soles and other products.

Examples

crepe rubber shoe soles
After harvesting latex, the workers process it into crepe rubber sheets at the factory.
Many shoemakers prefer using crepe rubber for its durability and flexibility.
The soles of these work boots are made from crepe rubber, providing excellent grip and comfort.
