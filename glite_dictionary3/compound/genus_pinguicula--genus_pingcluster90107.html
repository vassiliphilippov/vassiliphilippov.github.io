genus Pinguicula

noun
genus of carnivorous bog plants (butterworts)
Pronunciation: / ˈpiŋɡwɪˌkjuːlə / (AmE), / ˌpɪŋɡwɪˈkjuːlə / (BrE)
A genus of almost stemless carnivorous bog plants, commonly known as butterworts, found in Europe, America, and Antarctica.
