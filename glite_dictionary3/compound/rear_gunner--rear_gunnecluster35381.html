rear gunner

noun
aircraft crew member operating rear gun
Pronunciation: / rɪr ˈɡʌnɚ / (AmE), / rɪə ˈɡʌnə / (BrE)
A crew member responsible for operating a defensive gun at the back of an aircraft, especially in military planes.

Examples

rear gunner in a bomber
During the mission, the rear gunner spotted enemy fighters approaching from behind.
Her grandfather flew as a rear gunner in a bomber during the war.
The rear gunner had to remain alert at all times to defend the plane from attacks.
