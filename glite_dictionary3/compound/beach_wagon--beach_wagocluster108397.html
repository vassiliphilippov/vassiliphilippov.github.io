beach wagon

noun
car with extended rear cargo space
Pronunciation: / bitʃ ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈwæɡən / (BrE)
a car with a long body and a rear door, providing extra space behind the rear seats, typically known as a station wagon
