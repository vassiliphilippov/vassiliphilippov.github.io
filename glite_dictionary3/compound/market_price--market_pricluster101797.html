market price

noun
the current price in an open market
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrkɪt praɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt prʌɪs / (BrE)
the price at which a product or service is bought and sold in an open and competitive market

Examples

current market price
Before investing, check the current market price of the shares.
She sold the house below the market price to make a quick sale.
The market price of oil fluctuates daily depending on supply and demand.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cost price: the price paid to produce or buy something, not to sell it
  • synonym – selling price: the price at which something is actually sold
