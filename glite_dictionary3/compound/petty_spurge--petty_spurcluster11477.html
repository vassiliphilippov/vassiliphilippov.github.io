petty spurge

noun
a small weedy plant (Euphorbia peplus)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛti spɜrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɛti spɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a small annual plant of the spurge family (Euphorbia peplus) native to the Old World and introduced as a weed in parts of North America
