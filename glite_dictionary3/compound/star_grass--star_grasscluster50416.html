star grass

noun
plant of the genus Aletris with grasslike leaves
Pronunciation: / stɑr ɡræs / (AmE), / stɑː ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
Any of several perennial plants of the genus Aletris, with narrow, grasslike leaves and bitter roots once used in folk medicine.

Examples

star grass bitter roots
In the wildflower meadow, star grass blooms in early summer with its white, star-shaped flowers.
The slender, grasslike leaves of star grass can be easily mistaken for actual grass.
Traditional herbalists sometimes used the root of star grass for medicinal purposes.
