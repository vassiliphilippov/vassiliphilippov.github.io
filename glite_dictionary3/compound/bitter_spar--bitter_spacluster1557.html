bitter spar

noun
calcium magnesium carbonate mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪt̬ɚ ˌspɑr / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə ˌspɑː / (BrE)
A light-colored mineral composed of calcium magnesium carbonate, used as a source of magnesium, in ceramics, and as fertilizer.
