rash decision

noun
hasty or careless choice
Pronunciation: / ræʃ dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ræʃ dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a choice or judgment made quickly and without careful thought, often resulting in negative consequences

Examples

make a rash decision
He regretted his rash decision as soon as the consequences became clear.
Her rash decision to quit school left her with limited career options.
Making a rash decision at work can sometimes damage your professional reputation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – careful decision: a decision made with caution and consideration
  • synonym – impulsive decision: a decision made spontaneously or without thinking
