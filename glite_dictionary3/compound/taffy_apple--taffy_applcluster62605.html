taffy apple

noun
apple coated in caramelized sugar or candy
Pronunciation: / ˈtæfi ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈtæfi ˌapəl / (BrE)
An apple coated with a hard, sweet layer of caramelized sugar or candy, usually served on a stick as a treat.

Examples

taffy apple on a stick
Every autumn, my grandmother makes homemade taffy apples for the neighborhood kids.
She bit into the taffy apple and caramel stretched from her lips.
Vendors at the fair sold bright red taffy apples on wooden sticks.

  • synonym – toffee apple: an apple coated in toffee, chiefly British term
