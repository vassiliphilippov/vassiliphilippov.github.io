jumping bristletail

noun
small wingless leaping insect
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˈbrɪsəlˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˈbrɪs(ə)lˌteɪl / (BrE)
