Battle of Tarawa

proper noun
World War II battle in the Pacific (1943)
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.əl əv təˈrɑː.wə / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv təˈrɑː.wə / (BrE)
a battle in the Pacific during World War II (November 1943) in which United States Marines captured Tarawa Atoll from Japanese forces after intense fighting
