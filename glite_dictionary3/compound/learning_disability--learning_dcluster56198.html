learning disability

noun
difficulty in learning specific skills despite normal intelligence
Pronunciation: / ˈlɝːnɪŋ dɪsəˌbɪləti /; / ˈlɝːnɪŋ dɪˌsəbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnɪŋ dɪsəˌbɪləti /; / ˈlɜːnɪŋ dɪˌsəbɪlɪti / (BrE)
a condition that makes it difficult for a person with normal intelligence to learn specific academic skills such as reading, writing, or mathematics
