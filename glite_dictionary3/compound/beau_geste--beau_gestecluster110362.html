beau geste

noun
noble but often meaningless gesture
Pronunciation: / boʊ ˈʒɛst / (AmE), / bəʊ ˈʒɛst / (BrE)
an action that appears gracious, noble, or generous, but is often merely ceremonial or lacking in real significance

Examples

a beau geste of generosity
He offered to resign as a beau geste, fully expecting his offer would not be accepted.
Lighting a candle at the ceremony was more a beau geste than a meaningful act of remembrance.
The donation was a beau geste, but it did little to solve the real problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym – token gesture: a symbolic act with little practical effect
