full-court press

noun
intense, aggressive effort or tactic
Pronunciation: / ˌfʊlˈkɔrt ˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˌfʊlˈkɔːt ˌprɛs / (BrE)
an intense and aggressive effort to achieve a goal, originally a basketball tactic but often used figuratively
