Brown Swiss

noun
breed of Swiss dairy cattle
Pronunciation: / braʊn swɪs / (AmE), / braʊn swɪs / (BrE)
a large, hardy breed of brown dairy cattle originally from Switzerland, known for high milk production and adaptability
