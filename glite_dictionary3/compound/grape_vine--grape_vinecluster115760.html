grape vine

noun
woody vine producing grapes
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪp ˌvaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪp ˌvaɪn / (BrE)
A woody climbing plant of the genus Vitis, whose clusters of edible berries are known as grapes.

Examples

grape vine in vineyard
Farmers carefully tended each grape vine to ensure a good harvest for the winery.
She decided to plant a grape vine along the garden fence to provide shade and fresh grapes.
The old grape vine in the backyard produced an abundance of sweet fruit this summer.
