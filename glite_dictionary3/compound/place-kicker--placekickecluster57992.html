place-kicker

noun
football player who kicks for goal
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsˌkɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsˌkɪkə / (BrE)
(in American and Canadian football) a player who specializes in kicking the ball from a stationary position, usually during field goal or extra point attempts.
