bare-boned design

noun
minimalist, essential-only design
Pronunciation: / bɛr boʊnd dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / beə bəʊnd dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a design that is stripped down to its basic or essential features, with no unnecessary elements

Examples

bare-boned design for a product
Many startups prefer a bare-boned design for their first product to save costs and test ideas.
The bare-boned design of the website makes it easy to navigate.
The architect chose a bare-boned design to highlight the building’s functional aspects.
