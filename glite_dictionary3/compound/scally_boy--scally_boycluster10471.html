scally boy

noun
mischievous or troublesome boy
Pronunciation: / ˈskæli bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈskæli bɔɪ / (BrE)
A mischievous or troublesome boy, especially one who is cheeky or behaves badly.

Examples

a scally boy causing trouble
My mum warned me not to hang around with that scally boy from down the street.
The scally boy always gets into trouble at school.
The teacher sighed as another scally boy disrupted her lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scallywag: a person, especially a child, who behaves badly but in an amusingly mischievous rather than harmful way
