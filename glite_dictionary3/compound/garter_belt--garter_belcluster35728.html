garter belt

noun
elastic belt with straps for holding stockings
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrtɚ bɛlt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːtə bɛlt / (BrE)
A wide elastic belt worn around the waist or hips with attached straps used to hold up stockings.

Examples

garter belt with stockings
Many brides choose to wear a garter belt as part of their wedding attire.
