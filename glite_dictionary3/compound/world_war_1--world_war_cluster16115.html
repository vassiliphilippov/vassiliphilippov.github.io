World War 1

proper noun
the First World War, 1914–1918
Pronunciation: / wɜrld wɔr wʌn / (AmE), / wɜːld wɔː wʌn / (BrE)
the global war fought mainly in Europe from 1914 to 1918, involving the Allies and the Central Powers

Examples

World War 1 began in 1914
