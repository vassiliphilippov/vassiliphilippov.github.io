pit stop

noun
brief stop for rest or refreshment during a trip
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪt ˌstɑp / (AmE), / ˈpɪt ˌstɒp / (BrE)
a short stop made during a journey by car or other vehicle, typically to rest, get fuel, or refresh oneself

Examples

make a pit stop on trip
After three hours on the road, everyone was ready for a pit stop to grab snacks.
He took a pit stop to stretch his legs and use the restroom.
We made a quick pit stop at the gas station before continuing our journey.
