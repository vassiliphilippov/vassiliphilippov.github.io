sticking plaster

noun
adhesive bandage for wounds
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkɪŋ ˌplæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɪkɪŋ ˌplɑːstə / (BrE)
A small piece of adhesive material used to cover and protect minor wounds.
