French weed

noun
foul-smelling Eurasian weed plant
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ wiːd / (AmE), / frɛntʃ wiːd / (BrE)
A foul-smelling Eurasian plant with round flat seed pods, now widely naturalized in North America.

Examples

French weed produces flat, round pods that can easily spread across disturbed soil.
Gardeners often pull out French weed because of its unpleasant odor.
