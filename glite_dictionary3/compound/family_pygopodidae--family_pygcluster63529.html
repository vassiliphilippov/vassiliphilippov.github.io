family Pygopodidae

noun
Australian and Tasmanian legless lizard family
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli paɪˌɡɑpəˈdɪdi / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli paɪˌɡɒpəˈdɪdiː / (BrE)
