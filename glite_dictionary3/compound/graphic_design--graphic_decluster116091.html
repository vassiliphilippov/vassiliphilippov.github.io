graphic design

noun
art of visual communication using text and images
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪk dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪk dɪˈzaɪn /; / ˈɡrafɪk dɪˈzʌɪn / (BrE)
the art or skill of creating visual content by combining text and images to communicate ideas, especially in media like advertisements, magazines, or branding
