fluorescent fixture

noun
light fitting for fluorescent lamps
Pronunciation: / fluˈrɛsnt ˈfɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / flʊəˈrɛsnt ˈfɪkstʃə / (BrE)
A lighting unit designed to hold and power one or more fluorescent lamps.
