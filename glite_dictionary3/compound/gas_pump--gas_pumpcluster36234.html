gas pump

noun
device dispensing gasoline at stations
Pronunciation: / ɡæs pʌmp / (AmE), / ɡas pʌmp / (BrE)
a device at a service station used to pump gasoline from underground storage tanks into vehicles

Examples

gas pump at station
I stopped at the gas pump to fill up my car before heading out of town.
She paid at the gas pump with her credit card for convenience.
The gas pump was out of order, so I had to use the one on the other side.
