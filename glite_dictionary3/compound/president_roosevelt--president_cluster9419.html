President Roosevelt

proper noun
the 26th President of the United States (Theodore Roosevelt)
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzɪdənt ˈroʊzəˌvɛlt / (AmE), / ˌprɛzɪdənt ˈrəʊzəˌvɛlt / (BrE)
the 26th President of the United States (1901–1909), known for leading the country during the Spanish-American War and for overseeing the construction of the Panama Canal
