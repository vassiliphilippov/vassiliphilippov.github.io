male hypogonadism

noun
impaired testicular function in males
Pronunciation: / meɪl ˌhaɪpoʊˈɡoʊnəˌdɪzəm / (AmE), / meɪl ˌhʌɪpəʊˈɡəʊnədɪzəm / (BrE)
a medical condition in which a male fails to produce sufficient levels of sex hormones due to absent, underdeveloped, or malfunctioning testicles

Examples
