Commonwealth English orthography

noun
spelling system in UK and Commonwealth English
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑmənˌwɛlθ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔrˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌkɒmənˌwɛlθ ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
The system of English spelling as used in the United Kingdom, Ireland, and most Commonwealth countries, distinguished by variants such as 'analyse', 'centre', and 'useable'.

Commonwealth English orthography in books
Some writers prefer to use Commonwealth English orthography when publishing international editions of their books.
