polyurethane foam

noun
spongy plastic material for insulation or cushions
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈjʊrəˌθeɪn foʊm / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈjʊərəˌθeɪn fəʊm / (BrE)
A light, spongy material made from polyurethane plastics by adding water during manufacturing; commonly used for insulation, cushioning, and packaging.
