risk of infection

noun
likelihood of contracting an infection
Pronunciation: / ɹɪsk əv ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɹɪsk əv ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
the likelihood or probability of becoming infected by a disease or pathogen, especially after exposure

Examples

high risk of infection
After surgery, the risk of infection is highest in the first week.

Wearing gloves helps reduce the risk of infection while treating patients.
