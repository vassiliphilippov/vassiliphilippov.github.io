lexical database

noun
database of information about words
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛksɪkəl ˈdeɪtəˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɛksɪkəl ˈdeɪtəˌbeɪs / (BrE)
A database that contains structured information about words, such as their meanings, forms, and relationships.

Examples

a lexical database of words
Many natural language processing tools rely on a comprehensive lexical database for accurate word analysis.
The linguistics department created a lexical database to support their research on language change.
WordNet is a widely used lexical database for the English language.
