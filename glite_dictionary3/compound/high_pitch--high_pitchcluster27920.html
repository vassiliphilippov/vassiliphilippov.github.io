high pitch

noun
sound/tone with higher frequency
Pronunciation: / haɪ pɪtʃ / (AmE), / haɪ pɪtʃ / (BrE)
a sound or tone that is perceived as having a higher frequency compared to others

Examples

a high pitch sound
A mosquito's buzzing is often characterized by its high pitch.
As he got excited, his laughter rose to a high pitch.
Her voice reached a high pitch when she sang the final note.
