cotton rose

noun
a plant of the genus Filago
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtn ˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈkɒtn ˌrəʊz / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Filago, characterized by compact clusters of small, woolly flower heads.
