fistulous withers

noun
chronic inflammation of a horse's withers
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪstʃu·ləs ˈwɪðɚz / (AmE), / ˈfɪstjʊləs ˈwɪðəz / (BrE)
a chronic inflammatory disease of the withers in a horse, resulting in swelling and discharge

Examples

fistulous withers in horses
