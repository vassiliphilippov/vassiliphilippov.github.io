sand martin

noun
a small migratory swallow of sandy banks
Pronunciation: / sænd ˈmɑrtɪn / (AmE), / sænd ˈmɑːtɪn / (BrE)
a small migratory swallow found in the Northern Hemisphere, nesting in tunnels dug in sandy banks or cliffs

Examples

sand martin colony
A colony of sand martins nested in the riverbank near the village.
Every spring, thousands of sand martins return from Africa to breed in Europe.
We watched a sand martin swoop over the water in search of insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bank swallow: another common name for the sand martin, especially in North America
