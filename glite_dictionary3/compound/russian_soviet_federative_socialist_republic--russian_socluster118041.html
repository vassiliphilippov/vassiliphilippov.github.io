Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic

proper noun
the largest former republic of the USSR
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃən ˈsoʊvi.ət ˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪv səˈʃælɪst rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃ(ə)n ˈsəʊvɪət ˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪv səˈʃælɪst rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
