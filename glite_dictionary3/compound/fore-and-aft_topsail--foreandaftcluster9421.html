fore-and-aft topsail

noun
triangular sail with foot on gaff, luff on topmast
Pronunciation: / fɔr ənd æft ˈtɑpˌseɪl / (AmE), / fɔːr ənd ɑːft ˈtɒpseɪl / (BrE)
A triangular fore-and-aft sail set with its foot along the gaff and its luff attached to the topmast.

Examples
