to tickle someone's nose

verb
cause a tingling sensation in the nose
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkəl sʌmˌwʌnz noʊz / (AmE), / ˈtɪkəl sʌmˌwʌnz nəʊz / (BrE)
to cause a light tingling sensation in someone's nose, often prompting the urge to sneeze.

Examples

tickle someone's nose with dust
The chilly wind tickled her nose during their evening stroll.
The dust in the attic tickled his nose until he sneezed loudly.
The scent of the fresh flowers tickled my nose as I walked into the room.
