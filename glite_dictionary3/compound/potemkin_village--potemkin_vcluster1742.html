Potemkin village

noun
something deceptively impressive or insubstantial
Pronunciation: / poʊˈtɛm.kɪn ˈvɪl.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / pəˈtɛm.kɪn ˈvɪl.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
an impressive or elaborate facade that hides an undesirable fact or reality; something that looks good on the outside but is actually false or deceptive
