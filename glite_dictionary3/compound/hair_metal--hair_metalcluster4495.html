hair metal

noun
1980s glam-oriented rock music genre
Pronunciation: / hɛr ˈmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / heə ˈmetl̩ / (BrE)
A style of rock music popular in the 1980s, characterized by flashy clothes, heavy use of guitar effects, and elaborate hairstyles.
