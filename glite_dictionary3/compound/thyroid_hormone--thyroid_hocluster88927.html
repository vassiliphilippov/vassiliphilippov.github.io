thyroid hormone

noun
hormone from thyroid gland
Pronunciation: / ˈθaɪˌrɔɪd ˈhɔrˌmoʊn / (AmE), / ˈθaɪ.rɔɪd ˈhɔː.məʊn / (BrE)
any of several hormones produced by the thyroid gland, including thyroxine (T4) and triiodothyronine (T3), that regulate metabolism and other bodily functions
