calabur tree

noun
tropical tree with edible fruit and useful fiber
Pronunciation: / ˈkæləbɚ ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈkæləbə ˌtriː / (BrE)
A fast-growing tropical American evergreen tree with white flowers and fleshy edible fruit; its bark yields silky fiber used for cordage and its wood is valued for making staves.
