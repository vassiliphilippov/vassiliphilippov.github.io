finger spelling

noun
spelling words with hand signs
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚˌspɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌspɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of spelling words by using hand signs to represent each letter of an alphabet, commonly used in sign language communication

Examples

finger spelling in sign language
I became proficient at finger spelling after several weeks of practice.
Some deaf individuals use finger spelling when there is no sign for a specific word.
The teacher demonstrated finger spelling to help the students learn the sign language alphabet.
