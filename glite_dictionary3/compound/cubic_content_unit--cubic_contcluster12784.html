cubic content unit

noun
unit for measuring volume or capacity
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːbɪk ˈkɑnˌtɛnt ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˈkjuːbɪk ˈkɒn.tɛnt ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a unit of measurement for determining the volume or capacity of an object, typically expressed in cubic meters, cubic centimeters, etc.
