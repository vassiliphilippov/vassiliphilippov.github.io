battle of Crecy

proper noun
decisive 1346 battle in Hundred Years' War
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.əl əv ˈkres.i / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ˈkreɪ.si / (BrE)
the first decisive battle of the Hundred Years' War, fought in 1346, in which English forces under Edward III defeated the French army led by Philip of Valois.

Examples

the battle of Crecy in 1346
Historians debate the tactics employed by Edward III at the battle of Crecy.
