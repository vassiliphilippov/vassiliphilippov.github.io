horse race

noun
competition of speed between horses
Pronunciation: / hɔrs reɪs / (AmE), / hɔːs reɪs / (BrE)
a contest in which horses are ridden or driven to compete in speed, usually as a sporting event and often for betting

Examples

attend a horse race
Betting on the outcome of a horse race is a popular pastime in many countries.
He trained for months to prepare his mare for the local horse race.
The annual horse race attracted thousands of spectators and bettors.
