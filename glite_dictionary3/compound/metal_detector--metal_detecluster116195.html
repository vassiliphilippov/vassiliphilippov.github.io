metal detector

noun
device for detecting metal objects
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəl dɪˌtɛktər / (AmE), / ˈmɛtl dɪˌtɛktə / (BrE)
a device that emits a signal when it detects metal, commonly used to find hidden or buried metal objects or to check for metal contamination in products

Examples

metal detector on the beach
He swept the beach with his metal detector, hoping to uncover lost jewelry or coins.
Industrial metal detectors help ensure that food products are free from metal contamination.
Security guards use a metal detector at the entrance to screen visitors for weapons.

  • synonym – detector: device that detects the presence of something
