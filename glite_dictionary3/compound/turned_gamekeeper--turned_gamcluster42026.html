turned gamekeeper

noun
former poacher now wildlife protector
Pronunciation: / tɝnd ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɚ / (AmE), / tɜːnd ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpə / (BrE)
A person who was once a poacher but has become a protector of wildlife, often serving as a gamekeeper.

Examples

a turned gamekeeper protects animals
After years in the shadows, the notorious poacher became a turned gamekeeper and worked to protect endangered species.
His intimate knowledge of illegal hunting techniques made him a highly effective turned gamekeeper.
The tale of the turned gamekeeper is often cited as an example of redemption and change.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gamekeeper: A person employed to look after game and prevent poaching on an estate.
  • antonym – poacher: A person who illegally hunts or catches animals, especially on private land.
