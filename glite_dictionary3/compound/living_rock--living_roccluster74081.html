living rock

noun
South African succulent plant resembling stone
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ rɑk / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ rɒk / (BrE)
a highly succulent, stemless, clump-forming plant, native to South Africa, with grey-green leaves resembling granite; especially from the genus Lithops

Examples

living rock succulent plant
Because living rock plants blend with stones, they are less likely to be eaten by grazing animals.
Many collectors admire the unique appearance of living rock succulents in their gardens.
She bought several living rocks to create a miniature desert landscape on her windowsill.
