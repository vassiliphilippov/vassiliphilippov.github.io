water carrier

noun
person who transports water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˌkæriər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌkæriə / (BrE)
a person whose job or role is to transport water, especially in times or places without indoor plumbing

Examples

village water carrier
In the old city, the water carrier delivered fresh water to every doorstep each morning.
The role of water carrier was considered essential before the arrival of running water.
Without the water carrier, the remote village would not have survived the summer drought.
