hot water plant

noun
facility producing hot water for heating
Pronunciation: / \u02c8h\u0251t \u02c8w\u0254t\u025a \u02ccpl\u00e6nt / (AmE), / \u02c8h\u0252t \u02c8w\u0254\u02d0t\u0259 \u02ccpl\u0251\u02d0nt / (BrE)
A facility or plant that produces and supplies hot water for heating systems, such as central or district heating networks.

Examples

hot water plant supplies heating
The city council approved funding for a new hot water plant to supply several neighborhoods.
Engineers inspected the aging hot water plant before deciding whether to replace its boilers next year.
Tenants complained about low heat until the landlord repaired the malfunctioning hot water plant.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boiler plant: a facility containing boilers and related equipment to produce steam or hot water for heating
  • synonym – district heating plant: a central plant that produces heat (often hot water) distributed to multiple buildings through a network
