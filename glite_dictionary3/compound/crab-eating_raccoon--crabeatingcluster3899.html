crab-eating raccoon

noun
raccoon species native to Central and South America
Pronunciation: / ˈkræb ˌitɪŋ rəˈkun / (AmE), / ˈkrab ˌiːtɪŋ rəˈkuːn / (BrE)
a raccoon species native to Central and South America, known for frequenting areas near water and eating crabs and other aquatic animals

Examples

Unlike the North American raccoon, the crab-eating raccoon is often found near rivers and coastal areas.
