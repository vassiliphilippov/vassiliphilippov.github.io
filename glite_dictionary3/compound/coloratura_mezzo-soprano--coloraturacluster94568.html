coloratura mezzo-soprano

noun
mezzo-soprano with agility and wide range
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.əˈrɑː.tʃər.ə ˌmɛt.soʊ səˈprɑː.noʊ / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.ər.əˈtjʊə.rə ˌmɛt.səʊ səˈprɑː.nəʊ / (BrE)
A mezzo-soprano singer with the ability to perform elaborate ornamentation and rapid passages, typically with a range from about G3 to B5.
