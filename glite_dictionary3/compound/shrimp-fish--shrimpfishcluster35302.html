shrimp-fish

noun
slender tropical fish with transparent plates
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪmpˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪmpˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A slender tropical fish found in shallow East Indian waters, characterized by a body covered with transparent plates; commonly belonging to the family Centriscidae.

Examples

tropical shrimp-fish
