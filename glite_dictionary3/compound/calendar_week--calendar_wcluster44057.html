calendar week

noun
seven-day period in the calendar
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.ən.dɚ ˌwiːk / (AmE), / ˈkæl.ən.də ˌwiːk / (BrE)
A period of seven consecutive days as defined by the calendar, usually from Sunday to Saturday or from Monday to Sunday.
