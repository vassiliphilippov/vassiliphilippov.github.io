seminiferous tubule

noun
long, coiled sperm-producing tubes in the testis
Pronunciation: / ˌseməˈnɪfərəs ˈtuːbjuːl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪˈnɪfərəs ˈtjuːbjuːl / (BrE)
Any of the numerous long, coiled tubules in the testis where sperm develop and mature.

Examples

seminiferous tubule produces sperm
During puberty, the seminiferous tubules become active in producing sperm cells.
Microscopic examination revealed damage in several seminiferous tubules.
The seminiferous tubule is essential for the production of healthy sperm.
