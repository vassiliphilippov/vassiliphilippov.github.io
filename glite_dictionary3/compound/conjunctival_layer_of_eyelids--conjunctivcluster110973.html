conjunctival layer of eyelids

noun
part of eye lining inside the eyelids
Pronunciation: / kənˌdʒʌŋk.tɪˈvaɪ.əl ˈleɪ.ər əv ˈaɪˌlɪdz / (AmE), / kənˌdʒʌŋk.tɪˈvaɪ.əl ˈleɪ.ə əv ˈaɪˌlɪdz / (BrE)
the part of the conjunctiva that lines the inner surface of the eyelids and is continuous with the bulbar conjunctiva
