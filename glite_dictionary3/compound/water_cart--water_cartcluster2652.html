water cart

noun
cart with tank for carrying water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə kɑːt / (BrE)
A cart fitted with a tank for carrying and distributing water, especially fresh water for sale.

Examples

water cart delivers water
A water cart was stationed near the construction site to provide drinking water for the workers.
During the drought, residents relied on the water cart to fill their household containers.
The villagers gathered as the water cart arrived to deliver their daily supply of fresh water.
