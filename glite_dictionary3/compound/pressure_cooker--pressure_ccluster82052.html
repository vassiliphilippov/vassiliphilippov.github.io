pressure cooker

noun
sealed cooking device using steam pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃər ˌkʊkər / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə ˌkʊkə / (BrE)
A sealed cooking device that uses steam pressure to cook food at temperatures above the boiling point of water.

Examples

open the pressure cooker carefully
Be careful when opening the pressure cooker after use, as hot steam may escape.
Modern recipes often include instructions for using a pressure cooker.
She cooked beans quickly in the pressure cooker for dinner.
