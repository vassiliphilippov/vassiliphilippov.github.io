DNA fingerprint

noun
unique DNA pattern for identification
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːˌɛnˈeɪ ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˌdiːˌɛnˈeɪ ˈfɪŋɡəˌprɪnt / (BrE)
a method of identifying an individual by analyzing the unique patterns in their DNA, used especially in forensic science

DNA fingerprint analysis
Obtaining a DNA fingerprint from old samples requires very precise laboratory techniques.
