dog stinkhorn

noun
a stinkhorn fungus lacking a cap
Pronunciation: / dɔg ˈstɪŋˌhɔrn / (AmE), / dɒg ˈstɪŋˌhɔːn / (BrE)
A type of stinkhorn fungus with a stalk lacking a cap and a slimy gleba near the apex that attracts insects.
