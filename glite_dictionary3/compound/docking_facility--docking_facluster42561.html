docking facility

noun
harbor area for loading or repairing ships
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑkɪŋ fəˌsɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˈdɒkɪŋ fəˌsɪlɪti / (BrE)
a place in a harbor where ships are loaded, unloaded, or repaired, often equipped with gates to control water levels
