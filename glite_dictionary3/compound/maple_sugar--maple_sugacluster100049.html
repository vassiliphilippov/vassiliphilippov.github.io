maple sugar

noun
sugar made from maple tree sap
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪpəl ˈʃuɡər / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəl ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
Sugar obtained by boiling down the sap of the sugar maple tree, commonly used as a sweetener or in confections.
