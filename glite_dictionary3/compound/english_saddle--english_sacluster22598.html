English saddle

noun
a hornless saddle used in English riding
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈsæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈsæd.əl / (BrE)
a type of horse saddle with a steel cantle and pommel, lacking a horn, used in English-style riding

Examples
