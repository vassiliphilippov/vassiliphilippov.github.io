Meckel's diverticulum

noun
congenital pouch in the small intestine
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛkəlz dɪˌvɝːtɪˈkjuːləm / (AmE), / ˈmɛkəlz dɪˌvɜːtɪˈkjuːləm / (BrE)
a congenital pouch or bulge in the lower part of the small intestine (ileum), resulting from incomplete closure of the embryonic yolk sac
