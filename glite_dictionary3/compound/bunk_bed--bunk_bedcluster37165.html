bunk bed

noun
bed with one above another
Pronunciation: / bʌŋk bɛd / (AmE), / bʌŋk bɛd / (BrE)
A bed consisting of two or more single beds arranged one above the other, allowing more than one person to sleep in the same vertical space.

Examples

sleep in a bunk bed
Many hostels provide bunk beds for travelers to maximize accommodation.
The children love sleeping in their bunk bed because it feels like an adventure.
We bought a bunk bed to save space in the small guest room.
