sexual discrimination

noun
unfair treatment based on sex or gender
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃuəl dɪˌskrɪməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
unfair treatment of people, typically in employment, based on their sex, especially disadvantaging women

Examples

sexual discrimination at work
He filed a formal complaint alleging sexual discrimination during the hiring process.
Laws have been introduced to combat sexual discrimination in the workplace.
The company was sued for sexual discrimination after failing to promote several qualified women.
