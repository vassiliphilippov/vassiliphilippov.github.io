financial organisation

noun
institution that invests collected funds in financial assets
Pronunciation: / fəˈnænʃəl ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃəl ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
An institution, either public or private, that collects funds from the public or other institutions and invests them in financial assets.
