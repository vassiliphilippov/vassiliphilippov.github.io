drawn butter

noun
clarified butter used as a sauce
Pronunciation: / drɔn ˈbʌtər / (AmE), / drɔːn ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
Butter that has been clarified by heating and removing the milk solids, typically used as a sauce for seafood.

Examples

drawn butter for lobster
He melted drawn butter to drizzle over the steamed vegetables.
Lobster is often served with a side of drawn butter for dipping.
The recipe calls for drawn butter as the base of the sauce.
