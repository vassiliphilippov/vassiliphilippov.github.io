search and destroy mission

noun
Vietnam War military operation
Pronunciation: / sɜrtʃ ənd dɪˈstrɔɪ ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ ənd dɪˈstrɔɪ ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
