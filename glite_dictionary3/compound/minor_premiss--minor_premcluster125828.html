minor premiss

noun
logic: premise with minor term in syllogism
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnər prɪˈmɪs / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə prɪˈmɪs / (BrE)
The premise in a syllogism that contains the minor term, which is the subject of the conclusion.
