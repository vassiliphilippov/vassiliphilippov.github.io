malted milk

noun
powder of dried milk and malted cereals
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔltɪd mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪd mɪlk / (BrE)
A powder made from dried milk and malted cereals, often used to make a sweet drink or added to foods.
