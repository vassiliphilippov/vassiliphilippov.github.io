Saint Francis River

proper noun
tributary of the Mississippi River in Missouri and Arkansas
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈfrænsɪs ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈfrænsɪs ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
