golden ear

noun
exceptional ability in discerning music or sound quality
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ɪər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ɪə / (BrE)
an exceptional ability to perceive differences in sound quality or recognize musical tones with great accuracy
