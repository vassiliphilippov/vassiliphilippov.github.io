writing pad

noun
pad of paper for writing
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌpæd / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ˌpad / (BrE)
A pad of paper used for writing notes or messages.

Examples

writing pad for notes
He scribbled his phone number on a writing pad and handed it to her.
She always keeps a writing pad on her desk for jotting down ideas during meetings.
The hotel room included a pen and a writing pad next to the phone.

  • degree lower – sketch pad: a pad of paper used for drawing or sketching rather than writing
