urban legend

noun
widely believed but false story
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.bən ˈlɛdʒ.ənd / (AmE), / ˈɜː.bən ˈlɛdʒ.ənd / (BrE)
A widely circulated story of questionable truth, often involving humorous or horrifying events, that is popularly believed to be true.

Examples

famous urban legend
Despite being debunked, that urban legend still scares children every Halloween.
Many people believed the urban legend about deadly spiders hiding under toilet seats.
The story of the alligators living in the New York City sewers is a classic urban legend.
