income tax

noun
tax on annual earnings
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌkʌm tæks / (AmE), / ˈɪn.kʌm tæks / (BrE)
A tax that individuals or entities must pay on the money they earn within a year.

Examples

income tax return
Every working adult is required to file an income tax return annually in April.
He was frustrated by the amount of income tax deducted from his paycheck this year.
The government uses income tax revenues to fund education and healthcare services.

  • antonym – sales tax: tax levied on the sale of goods and services
