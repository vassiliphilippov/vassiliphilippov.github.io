shire horse

noun
large British draft horse breed
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪɚ hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪə hɔːs / (BrE)
A large, strong British breed of draft horse, known for its size and power, traditionally used for heavy work such as pulling carts and plows.

Examples

a shire horse pulling a cart
