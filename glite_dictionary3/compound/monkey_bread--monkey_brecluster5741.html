monkey bread

noun
pull-apart cinnamon-sweet pastry
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki brɛd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki brɛd / (BrE)
A sweet, pull-apart yeast-based pastry made of small dough pieces coated in butter, sugar, and often cinnamon, baked together and served warm.

Examples

sticky monkey bread pastry
For the holidays she always makes monkey bread, a sticky pull-apart pastry everyone loves.
At the café we shared a warm slice of monkey bread with coffee and fresh fruit.
He baked monkey bread using sweet dough rolled in cinnamon and brown sugar for guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pull-apart bread: a bread baked as small pieces so it can be torn apart by hand; often sweet and sticky
