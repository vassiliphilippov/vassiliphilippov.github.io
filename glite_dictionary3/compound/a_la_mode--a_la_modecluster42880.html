a la mode

adverb
served with ice cream
Pronunciation: / ˌɑ lə ˈmoʊd / (AmE), / ˌa lə ˈməʊd / (BrE)
(of pie or dessert) served with ice cream on top or on the side.

Examples

a la mode apple pie
She ordered the brownie a la mode for dessert.
The restaurant is famous for its cherry cobbler a la mode.
Would you like your apple pie a la mode or plain?
