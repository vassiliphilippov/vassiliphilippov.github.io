stink fly

noun
a smelly pale green lacewing fly
Pronunciation: / stɪŋk flaɪ / (AmE), / stɪŋk flaɪ / (BrE)
A pale green lacewing fly known for producing an unpleasant smell, whose larvae are carnivorous.
