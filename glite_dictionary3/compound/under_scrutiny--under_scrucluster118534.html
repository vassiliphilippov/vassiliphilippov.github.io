under scrutiny

adjective
closely examined or observed
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ ˈskruːtəni / (AmE), / ˈʌndə ˈskruːtəni / (BrE)
being carefully examined or observed, especially in order to judge or assess something

Examples

under scrutiny by officials

Every aspect of the new policy is under scrutiny by the media.
The company’s financial records are under scrutiny after reports of misconduct.
