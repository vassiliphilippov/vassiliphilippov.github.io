to donate a kidney

verb
give a kidney for transplant
Pronunciation: / doʊˈneɪt ə ˈkɪdni / (AmE), / dəʊˈneɪt ə ˈkɪdni / (BrE)
to give one of your kidneys to another person in need of a transplant, usually through a medical procedure
