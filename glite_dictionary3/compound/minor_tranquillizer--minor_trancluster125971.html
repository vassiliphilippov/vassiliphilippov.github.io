minor tranquillizer

noun
anti-anxiety drug of milder potency
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnɚ trænˈkwɪlaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə træŋˈkwɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
