papilionaceous blossom

noun
butterfly-shaped flower petal arrangement
Pronunciation: / pəˌpɪlioʊˈneɪʃəs ˈblɑsəm / (AmE), / pəˌpɪlɪəˈneɪʃəs ˈblɒsəm / (BrE)
a blossom having petals arranged in a way that resembles a butterfly, typically found in plants of the pea family
