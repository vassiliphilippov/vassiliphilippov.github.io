shaky performance

noun
an unsteady or unreliable display of skill
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪki pɚˈfɔrməns / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪki pəˈfɔːməns / (BrE)
an uncertain or unreliable display of skill or ability, often lacking confidence or stability

Examples

give a shaky performance
After a shaky performance in the first half, the team recovered and won the match.
She admitted that nerves led to her shaky performance during the audition.
The actor's shaky performance was criticized by several reviewers after the opening night.
