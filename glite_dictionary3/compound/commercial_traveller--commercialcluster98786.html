commercial traveller

noun
travelling salesperson
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɝːʃəl ˈtræv(ə)lɚ / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəl ˈtræv(ə)lə / (BrE)
a salesperson who travels to visit customers, especially to solicit sales for a company

Examples

commercial traveller visits clients
As a commercial traveller, she spent most weeks on the road visiting clients across the country.
In the past, being a commercial traveller involved long journeys by train or coach.
The commercial traveller arrived every few months with samples of the latest fabrics.
