free to chat

adjective
available to talk
Pronunciation: / friː tə tʃæt / (AmE), / friː tə tʃat / (BrE)
available and willing to have a conversation, typically in a casual or online context.

Examples

free to chat after work
I'm usually free to chat in the evenings after dinner.
Let me know when you're free to chat about the project.
She isn't free to chat right now but will call you later.
