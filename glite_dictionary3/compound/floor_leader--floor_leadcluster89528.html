floor leader

noun
chief party organizer in a legislature
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔr ˌlidɚ / (AmE), / ˈflɔː ˌliːdə / (BrE)
the member of a legislative body chosen to lead and organize their party's strategy, especially in managing debates and votes on the floor

Examples

floor leader in parliament
As floor leader, she played a crucial role in passing major legislation.
The floor leader addressed the assembly to outline the party's position.
The floor leader coordinated his party's response to the proposed bill.

Related Terms

  • synonym – majority leader: the floor leader of the majority party in a legislative body
  • synonym – minority leader: the floor leader of the minority party in a legislative body
  • synonym – whip: a legislator responsible for party discipline and ensuring members vote according to party line
