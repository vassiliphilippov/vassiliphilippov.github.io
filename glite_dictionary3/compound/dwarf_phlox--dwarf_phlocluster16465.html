dwarf phlox

noun
low perennial plant with pink or white flowers
Pronunciation: / dwɔrf flɑks / (AmE), / dwɔːf flɒks / (BrE)
A low-growing, tufted perennial plant of the phlox family, with needlelike evergreen leaves and pink or white flowers, native to the United States and often grown as a ground cover.
