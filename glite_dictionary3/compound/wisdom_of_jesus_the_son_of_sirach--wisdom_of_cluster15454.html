Wisdom of Jesus the Son of Sirach

proper noun
Apocryphal book of maxims and wisdom (Ecclesiasticus)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzdəm əv ˈdʒiːzəs ði ˈsʌn əv sɪˈræk / (AmE), / ˈwɪzdəm əv ˈdʒiːzəs ði ˈsʌn əv sɪˈræk / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book consisting mainly of maxims and moral teachings, similar in style to Proverbs, also known as Ecclesiasticus
