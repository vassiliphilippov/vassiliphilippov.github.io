public works

noun
government-funded infrastructure projects
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk wɝks / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk wɜːks / (BrE)
structures and facilities such as roads, schools, and bridges, built and maintained by the government for public use
