little man

noun
an insignificant or powerless person
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.əl mæn / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl mæn / (BrE)
an ordinary person who feels insignificant or powerless, especially when facing authority or large organizations

Examples

the little man in society
After being ignored by the manager, he realized he was just a little man in the company.
Moving to a vast and unfamiliar city can make anyone feel like a little man.
Politicians often promise to protect the interests of the little man.
