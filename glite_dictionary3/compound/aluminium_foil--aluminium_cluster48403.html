aluminium foil

noun
thin sheets of aluminium for wrapping food
Pronunciation: / əˌluːməˈnɪəm fɔɪl / (AmE), / ˌaljʊˈmɪnɪəm fɔɪl / (BrE)
Thin sheets of aluminium, used especially for wrapping food or covering containers for cooking or storage.

Examples

wrap food in aluminium foil
Cover the dish with aluminium foil to prevent it from drying out in the oven.
He wrapped the leftovers tightly in aluminium foil before putting them in the fridge.
Many people use aluminium foil to line baking trays for easier cleanup.
