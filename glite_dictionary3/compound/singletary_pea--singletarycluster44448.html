singletary pea

noun
Mediterranean-origin annual pasture/crop plant
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəlˌtɛri pi / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəltəri piː / (BrE)
A weak-stemmed winter annual plant, native to the Mediterranean region but long naturalized in the southern United States, cultivated as a cover and pasture crop.
