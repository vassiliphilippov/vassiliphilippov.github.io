calf bone

noun
outer lower leg bone (fibula)
Pronunciation: / kælf boʊn / (AmE), / kɑːf bəʊn / (BrE)
the outer and thinner of the two bones in the lower human leg, situated between the knee and ankle, also known as the fibula

Examples

broken calf bone
Doctors explained that the calf bone is more slender than the shin bone.
She broke her calf bone while hiking in the mountains.
The X-ray showed that the calf bone had fractured near the ankle.
