tufted titmouse

noun
small crested North American songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌftɪd ˈtɪtˌmaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtʌftɪd ˈtɪtˌmaʊs / (BrE)
a small songbird with a gray crest found in the eastern and midwestern United States, noted for its distinctive tufted head
