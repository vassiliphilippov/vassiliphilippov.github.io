al-Aksa Martyrs Brigades

proper noun
militant Palestinian faction active in the West Bank
Pronunciation: / æl ˈæksə ˈmɑrtɚz brɪˈgeɪdz / (AmE), / æl ˈæksə ˈmɑːtəz brɪˈgeɪdz / (BrE)
a militant Palestinian organization and armed faction, originally an offshoot of al-Fatah, active primarily in the West Bank and known for carrying out attacks against Israel, especially in the early 2000s.
