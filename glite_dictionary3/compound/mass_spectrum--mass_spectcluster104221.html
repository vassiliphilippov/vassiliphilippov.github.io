mass spectrum

noun
distribution of ions by mass/charge
Pronunciation: / mæs ˈspɛktrəm / (AmE), / mæs ˈspɛktrəm / (BrE)
a distribution of ions of different mass-to-charge ratios recorded by a mass spectrograph or mass spectrometer

Examples

mass spectrum analysis results
By analyzing the mass spectrum, scientists identified the molecular structure of the protein.
Each chemical substance produces a characteristic mass spectrum under identical experimental conditions.
The mass spectrum revealed the presence of several unknown compounds in the sample.
