old biddy

noun
unpleasant, nosy old woman
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈbɪdi / (AmE), / əʊld ˈbɪdi / (BrE)
an unpleasant, fussy, or nosy old woman, often regarded with mild annoyance or contempt

Examples

nosy old biddy
At the meeting, an old biddy kept interrupting with complaints about the new plan.
Everyone in the neighborhood avoids the old biddy because she gossips about them all.
The old biddy next door watches everyone's comings and goings from her window.

Related Terms

  • synonym – busybody: a person who meddles in the affairs of others
  • synonym – crone: an old woman who is often disagreeable or malicious
