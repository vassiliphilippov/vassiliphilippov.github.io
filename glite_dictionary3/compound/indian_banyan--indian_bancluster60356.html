Indian banyan

noun
large East Indian tree with aerial roots
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈbænjən / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈbænjən / (BrE)
A large East Indian tree (Ficus benghalensis) that develops aerial roots which grow down into the soil to form additional trunks.

Examples

huge Indian banyan tree
Hundreds of people sought shade beneath the Indian banyan during the summer festival.
The Indian banyan in the park has created a network of trunks and roots over decades.
The Indian banyan is famous for its sprawling canopy and aerial roots.
