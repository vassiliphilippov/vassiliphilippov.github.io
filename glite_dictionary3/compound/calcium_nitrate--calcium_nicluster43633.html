calcium nitrate

noun
a white, water-soluble chemical salt
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.si.əm ˈnaɪ.treɪt / (AmE), / ˈkalsɪəm ˈnʌɪtreɪt / (BrE)
a white, deliquescent salt, soluble in water, used as a fertilizer and in various industrial processes
