like jet fuel

adverb
extremely intense or powerful
Pronunciation: / laɪk dʒɛt fjul / (AmE), / laɪk dʒɛt fjʊəl / (BrE)
(informal) Extremely intense or powerful; having a strong effect or impact, as if driven by the force of jet fuel.

Examples

energy like jet fuel
After his morning coffee, his energy levels were like jet fuel for hours.
Her motivation to succeed was like jet fuel and inspired the whole team.
The new coach's speech was like jet fuel for the players before the big game.
