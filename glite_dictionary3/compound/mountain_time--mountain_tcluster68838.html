Mountain Time

noun
U.S. and Canadian time zone standard
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊn.tən taɪm / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time observed in the Mountain Time Zone of the United States and Canada, corresponding to the time at the 105th meridian west of Greenwich.

Examples

Mountain Time zone
He lives in Denver, which operates on Mountain Time throughout the year.
Please check that your clocks are set to Mountain Time before the broadcast begins.
The conference call is scheduled for 3:00 p.m. Mountain Time on Thursday.
