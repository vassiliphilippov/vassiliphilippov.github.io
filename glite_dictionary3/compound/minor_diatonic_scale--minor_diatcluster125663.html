minor diatonic scale

noun
a type of minor diatonic scale in music
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnɚ daɪ.əˈtɑnɪk skeɪl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə daɪ.əˈtɒnɪk skeɪl / (BrE)
A diatonic scale in which the intervals between the 2nd and 3rd, and the 5th and 6th notes are semitones, giving it a minor tonality.
