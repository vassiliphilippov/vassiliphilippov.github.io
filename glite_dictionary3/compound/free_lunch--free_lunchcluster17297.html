free lunch

noun
something obtained without effort or payment
Pronunciation: / friː lʌntʃ / (AmE), / friː lʌntʃ / (BrE)
something that can be obtained without effort, cost, or obligation; a benefit or advantage given freely with no apparent strings attached

Examples

a free lunch offer
He accepted the offer, but he wondered what the free lunch would really cost him later.
Many believe there's no such thing as a free lunch in the business world.
Politicians often promise a free lunch, but someone always ends up paying.
