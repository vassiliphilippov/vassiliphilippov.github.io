United States House of Representatives

proper noun
lower legislative chamber of US Congress
Pronunciation: / juˈnaɪtɪd steɪts haʊs əv rɛprɪˌzɛntətɪvz / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd steɪts haʊs əv rɛprɪˌzɛntətɪvz / (BrE)
the lower legislative chamber of the United States Congress, consisting of elected representatives from each state, responsible for creating and passing federal laws
