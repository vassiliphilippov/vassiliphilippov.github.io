pond-skater

noun
insect that walks on water
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑnd ˌskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɒnd ˌskeɪtə / (BrE)
a slender, long-legged insect that can move across the surface of water without sinking

Examples

pond-skater on water
Children watched as a pond-skater darted across the quiet surface of the lake.
Several pond-skaters glided smoothly on the pond after the rain stopped.
The delicate legs of the pond-skater allow it to stay afloat on the water.
