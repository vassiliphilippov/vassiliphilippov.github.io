Yorkshire terrier

noun
small dog breed with long silky coat
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔrkˌʃɪr ˈtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈjɔːkʃə ˈtɛriə / (BrE)
a very small breed of dog with a long, silky, bluish-grey and tan coat, originally from Yorkshire, England.
