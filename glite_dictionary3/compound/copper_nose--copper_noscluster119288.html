copper nose

noun
enlarged, reddish nose (alcohol-related)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpər ˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə nəʊz / (BrE)
An enlarged, reddish nose with visible blood vessels, often associated with excessive alcohol consumption.

Examples

a copper nose from drinking
His copper nose was a telltale sign of years of heavy drinking.
