mental imagery

noun
the ability to form images in the mind
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛntəl ˈɪmɪdʒəri / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəl ˈɪmɪdʒəri / (BrE)
the ability to form pictures or images in the mind of objects, scenes, or events that are not present
