zebra finch

noun
small Australian songbird with zebra-like stripes
Pronunciation: / ˈziː.brə fɪntʃ / (AmE), / ˈziː.brə fɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small Australian songbird (Taeniopygia guttata) with black-and-white markings resembling those of a zebra, often kept as a pet or aviary bird.
