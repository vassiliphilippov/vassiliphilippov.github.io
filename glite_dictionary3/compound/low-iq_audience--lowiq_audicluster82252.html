low-IQ audience

noun
audience perceived as lacking intelligence
Pronunciation: / loʊ aɪ ˈkju ˈɔdiəns / (AmE), / ləʊ aɪ ˈkjuː ˈɔːdiəns / (BrE)
An audience regarded as lacking intelligence or having below-average intellectual ability.

Examples

a low-IQ audience laughs easily
Journalists sometimes complain about being told to write articles that appeal to a low-IQ audience.
Some advertisers target products toward a low-IQ audience, assuming simpler messages will be more effective.
The director denied that his movie was made for a low-IQ audience.
