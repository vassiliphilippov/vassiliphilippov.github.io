for example

adverb
introducing an example
Pronunciation: / fər ɪɡˈzæmpəl / (AmE), / fə ˈɪɡ.zɑːm.pəl / (BrE)
used to introduce an example in order to illustrate a point or explain something more clearly.

Examples

Certain fruits, for example, bananas and oranges, are high in potassium.
