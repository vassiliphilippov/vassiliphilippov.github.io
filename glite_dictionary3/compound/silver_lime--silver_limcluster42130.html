silver lime

noun
a large ornamental tree with white-backed leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər laɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə laɪm / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree native to eastern Europe and Asia Minor, noted for its leaves with a white, downy underside; widely planted as an ornamental tree.
