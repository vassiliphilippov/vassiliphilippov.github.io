ground level

noun
floor at or nearest ground outside building
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
the floor of a building that is at or nearest to the level of the ground outside

Examples

ground level apartment
Our apartment is on the ground level, so it's easy to step outside into the garden.
Shops at ground level tend to have the highest rents in this city center.
The lobby is located at the ground level of the hotel, just beyond the main entrance.

Related Terms

  • antonym – basement: floor of a building that is partly or entirely below ground level
  • synonym – first floor: the floor of a building at street or ground level (especially in American English)
