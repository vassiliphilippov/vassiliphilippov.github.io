camping site

noun
place for camping with tents or caravans
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpɪŋ saɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæmpɪŋ saɪt / (BrE)
A place where people can set up tents or park caravans for camping, typically with basic facilities.
