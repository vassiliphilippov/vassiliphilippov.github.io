particulate matter

noun
tiny solid or liquid airborne particles (pollutant)
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈtɪkjələt ˈmætər / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪkjʊlət ˈmætə / (BrE)
very small solid or liquid particles suspended in air or another gas, especially as pollutants that can be harmful to health

Examples
