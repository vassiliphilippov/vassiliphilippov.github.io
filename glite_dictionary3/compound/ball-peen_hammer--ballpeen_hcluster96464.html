ball-peen hammer

noun
hammer with a flat and rounded end
Pronunciation: / bɔl pin ˈhæmɚ / (AmE), / bɔːl piːn ˈhamə / (BrE)
A hammer with one flat face and one rounded (peen) end, used chiefly for shaping metal.

Examples

use a ball-peen hammer for metal
A ball-peen hammer is essential for riveting and general metalwork.
He struck the chisel carefully with a ball-peen hammer to avoid damaging the edge.
The mechanic grabbed a ball-peen hammer to shape the sheet metal.
