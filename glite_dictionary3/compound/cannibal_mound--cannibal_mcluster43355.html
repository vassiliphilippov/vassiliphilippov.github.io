cannibal mound

noun
raw beef dish with egg and seasonings
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.ɪ.bəl maʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkæn.ɪ.bəl maʊnd / (BrE)
A dish consisting of ground beef mixed with raw egg and other ingredients such as onions, capers, and anchovies, eaten raw.
