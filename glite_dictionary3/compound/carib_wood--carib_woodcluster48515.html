carib wood

noun
Dominican tree with crimson flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪb wʊd / (AmE), / ˈkærɪb wʊd / (BrE)
A small tree native to Dominica, known for producing masses of large crimson flowers before its pinnate leaves appear.
