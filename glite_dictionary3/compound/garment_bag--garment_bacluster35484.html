garment bag

noun
a bag for carrying clothes wrinkle-free
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrmənt bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːmənt bæɡ / (BrE)
a flexible bag or suitcase designed to carry clothes without folding or wrinkling, often hung up when unfolded

Examples
