vegetable wax

noun
waxy substance from plants
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ wæks / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ waks / (BrE)
a waxy substance derived from plants, especially from the trunks of certain palms, used in polishes, cosmetics, and other products

Examples

vegetable wax in cosmetics
Carnauba is a type of vegetable wax commonly used in car polishes and cosmetics.
