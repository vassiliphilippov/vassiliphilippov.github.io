Victorian age

noun
British era of Queen Victoria's reign
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔːriən eɪdʒ / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriən eɪdʒ / (BrE)
the period in British history during the reign of Queen Victoria (1837–1901), marked by distinctive social, cultural, and moral attitudes

Examples

the Victorian age in Britain
Literature from the Victorian age often explores themes of social class and morality.
Many of the city's grand buildings were constructed during the Victorian age.
The strict morals associated with the Victorian age influenced British society for decades.
