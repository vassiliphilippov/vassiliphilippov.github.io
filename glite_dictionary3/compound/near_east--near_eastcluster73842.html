Near East

proper noun
historical region around eastern Mediterranean
Pronunciation: / nɪr ist / (AmE), / nɪə iːst / (BrE)
a historical term for the region around the eastern Mediterranean, typically including Southwest Asia and Egypt, notable as the birthplace of several ancient civilizations and major religions

ancient Near East history
Archaeologists uncovered new artifacts from the Near East dating back thousands of years.
