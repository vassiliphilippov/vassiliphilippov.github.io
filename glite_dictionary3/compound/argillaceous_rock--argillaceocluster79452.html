argillaceous rock

noun
sedimentary rock made of clay
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrdʒɪˈleɪʃəs rɑk / (AmE), / ˌɑːdʒɪˈleɪʃəs rɒk / (BrE)

Examples

argillaceous rock layer
