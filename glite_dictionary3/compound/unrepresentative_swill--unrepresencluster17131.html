unrepresentative swill

noun
misleading or irrelevant comments or reports
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv swɪl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˌprɛzənˈteɪtɪv swɪl / (BrE)
used to refer to remarks, information, or reports that are irrelevant, misleading, or fail to accurately reflect reality
