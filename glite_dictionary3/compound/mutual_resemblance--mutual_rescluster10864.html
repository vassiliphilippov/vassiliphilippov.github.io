mutual resemblance

noun
similarity between two or more things, reciprocally
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːtʃuəl rɪˈzɛmbləns / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtʃuəl rɪˈzɛmbləns / (BrE)
A situation in which two or more people or things closely resemble each other in a reciprocal or symmetrical manner.

Examples

mutual resemblance in appearance
Despite coming from different backgrounds, the friends developed a mutual resemblance in mannerisms over the years.
The mutual resemblance between the two works of art hinted at a shared inspiration.
There was a striking mutual resemblance between the twins, making it hard to tell them apart.
