environmental impact

noun
effect on the environment from an activity
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈɪmpækt / (AmE), / ɪnˌvʌɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈɪmpakt / (BrE)
the effect that a particular activity, process, or substance has on the natural environment

Examples

environmental impact report
Government agencies require a report on the environmental impact before approving large construction projects.
Reducing the environmental impact of transportation is a priority for many cities worldwide.
The company commissioned a study to measure the environmental impact of its new manufacturing plant.
