indigo plant

noun
plant source of indigo dye
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.dɪ.ɡoʊ plænt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.dɪ.ɡəʊ plɑːnt / (BrE)
A deciduous subshrub of southeastern Asia with pinnate leaves and clusters of red or purple flowers, used as a source of indigo dye.
