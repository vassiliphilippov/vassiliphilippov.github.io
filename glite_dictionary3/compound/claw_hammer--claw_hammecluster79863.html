claw hammer

noun
hammer with a curved, split end for pulling nails
Pronunciation: / klɔː ˈhæmɚ / (AmE), / klɔː ˈhamə / (BrE)
A hammer with one end of the head curved and split to form a claw for pulling nails, used especially in carpentry.

Examples

use a claw hammer for nails
He used a claw hammer to remove the stubborn nail from the wall.
Make sure to use a claw hammer when you need to pull out old nails.
The toolbox contained a claw hammer, some screwdrivers, and a measuring tape.
