small stores

noun
personal items sold aboard ship or base (naval)
Pronunciation: / smɔl stɔrz / (AmE), / smɔːl stɔːz / (BrE)
Personal items permitted by regulations, sold aboard ships or at naval bases and charged to a person's pay, especially in naval contexts.

Examples
