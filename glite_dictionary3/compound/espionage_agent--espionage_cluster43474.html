espionage agent

noun
a person employed as a spy
Pronunciation: / ˌɛspiəˌnɑːʒ ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌɛspiˈɒnɪdʒ ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who is employed to obtain secret information about another country or business competitor

Examples

undercover espionage agent
An espionage agent must be skilled at blending in and gathering intelligence without arousing suspicion.
He lived undercover for years as an espionage agent in Eastern Europe.
The government arrested an espionage agent who was leaking classified information to a rival nation.
