quarter-vine

noun
woody vine with cross-patterned stem
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrtər vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə vaɪn / (BrE)
A woody flowering vine native to the southern United States, whose stems show a cross-shaped pattern in transverse section.
