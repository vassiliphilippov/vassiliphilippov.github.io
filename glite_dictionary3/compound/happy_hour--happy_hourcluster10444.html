happy hour

noun
time with reduced drink prices in bars
Pronunciation: / ˈhæpi aʊər /; / ˈhæpi ˌaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈhæpi aʊə /; / ˈhæpi ˌaʊə / (BrE)
a period of time, usually in the late afternoon or early evening at a bar or restaurant, when drinks (and sometimes food) are sold at reduced prices

Examples

She always orders a cocktail during happy hour to take advantage of the lower prices.
The restaurant's happy hour specials attract a large crowd every Friday.

The restaurant offers half-price appetizers during happy hour.
We often go to the pub for happy hour on Fridays after work.
