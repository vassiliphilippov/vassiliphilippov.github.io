chicken mousse

noun
savory mousse made with chicken
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən mus / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn muːs / (BrE)
A savory dish made by blending cooked chicken into a smooth, airy mousse, often served as an appetizer or spread.

Examples

chicken mousse appetizer
For the party, she prepared a chicken mousse and garnished it with fresh herbs.
Guests enjoyed spreading the creamy chicken mousse on slices of toasted bread.
