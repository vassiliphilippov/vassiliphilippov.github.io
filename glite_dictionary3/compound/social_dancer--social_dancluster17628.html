social dancer

noun
person who attends social dance events
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈdænsər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a person who participates in social dance events or gatherings, such as balls or dance parties, often for enjoyment rather than competition

Examples

experienced social dancer

Unlike competitive dancers, a social dancer focuses on enjoyment and interaction.
