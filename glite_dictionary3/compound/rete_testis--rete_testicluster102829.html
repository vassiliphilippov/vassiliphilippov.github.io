rete testis

noun
network of sperm-carrying tubules in the testis
Pronunciation: / ˌriːti ˈtɛstɪs / (AmE), / ˌriːti ˈtɛstɪs / (BrE)
a network of tubules in the testis that carries sperm from the seminiferous tubules to the vasa efferentia

Examples

rete testis carries sperm
Blockage in the rete testis can cause reduced fertility due to impaired sperm transport.
During dissection, the rete testis was carefully examined to trace the pathway of sperm.
The seminiferous tubules converge into the rete testis before leading to the vasa efferentia.
