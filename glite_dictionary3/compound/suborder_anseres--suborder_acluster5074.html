suborder Anseres

noun
old zoological grouping for ducks, geese, swans
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈɔrdər ænˈsiːriːz / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈɔːdə ˈænsɪriːz / (BrE)
a former zoological classification that included all birds now classified in the family Anatidae, such as ducks, geese, and swans
