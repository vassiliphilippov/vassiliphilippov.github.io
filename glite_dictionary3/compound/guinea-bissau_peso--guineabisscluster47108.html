Guinea-Bissau peso

noun
former currency unit of Guinea-Bissau
Pronunciation: / ˌgɪni bɪˈsaʊ ˈpeɪsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌgɪni bɪˈsaʊ ˈpeɪsəʊ / (BrE)
