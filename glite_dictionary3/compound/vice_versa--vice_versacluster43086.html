vice versa

adverb
the other way around; in reverse order
Pronunciation: / vaɪs ˈvɜrsə / (AmE), / vaɪs ˈvɜːsə / (BrE)
used to indicate that the reverse of what has been said is also true

Examples

vice versa applies equally
My sister helps me with homework, and vice versa.
Parents influence children, and vice versa.
She takes care of him, and vice versa when he is sick.
