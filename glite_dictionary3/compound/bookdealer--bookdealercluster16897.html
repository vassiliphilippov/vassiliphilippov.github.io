bookdealer

noun
person or business selling books
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌdiːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌdiːlə / (BrE)
a person or business that buys and sells books, especially rare or second-hand ones

Examples

bookdealer buys books
Many bookdealers gather at the annual book fair to buy and sell rare titles.
She worked as a bookdealer specializing in antique and first-edition books.
The bookdealer recommended an old edition of Shakespeare for my collection.
