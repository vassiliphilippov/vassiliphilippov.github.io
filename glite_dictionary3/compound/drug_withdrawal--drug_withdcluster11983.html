drug withdrawal

noun
discontinuation of drug use
Pronunciation: / drʌɡ wɪðˈdrɔː.əl / (AmE), / drʌɡ wɪðˈdrɔː.əl / (BrE)
the process of discontinuing the use of a drug, often resulting in physical or psychological symptoms

Examples

symptoms of drug withdrawal
Gradual reduction of dosage can help ease the effects of drug withdrawal.
He began to experience severe symptoms of drug withdrawal after stopping his medication suddenly.
Hospitals often provide support for patients going through drug withdrawal.
