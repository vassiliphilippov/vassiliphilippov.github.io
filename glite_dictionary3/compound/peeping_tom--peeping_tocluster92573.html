Peeping Tom

noun
person who spies for sexual pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˈpipɪŋ tɑm / (AmE), / ˈpiːpɪŋ tɒm / (BrE)
a person who secretly watches others, especially for sexual gratification, often by spying on them without their knowledge

Examples

Peeping Tom watching people
No one suspected that their quiet neighbor might be a Peeping Tom.
She was frightened to discover a Peeping Tom watching her through the window.
The police caught the Peeping Tom hiding in the bushes behind the apartment building.
