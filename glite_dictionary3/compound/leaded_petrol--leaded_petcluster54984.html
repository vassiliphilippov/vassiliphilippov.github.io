leaded petrol

noun
petrol treated with a lead compound
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdɪd ˈpɛtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈlɛdɪd ˈpɛtrəl / (BrE)
petrol to which a lead compound has been added to reduce engine knocking

Examples

leaded petrol for old cars
Cars manufactured before the 1990s often required leaded petrol for their engines to run smoothly.
Studies linked the widespread use of leaded petrol to increased levels of lead pollution in cities.
The use of leaded petrol was phased out in many countries due to environmental concerns.
