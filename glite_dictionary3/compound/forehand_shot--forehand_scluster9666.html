forehand shot

noun
stroke with palm facing forward (sports)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌhænd ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌhænd ʃɒt / (BrE)
(in sports) a stroke made with the palm of the hand facing the direction of the movement, particularly in tennis, badminton, or squash.

Examples

powerful forehand shot
His forehand shot has improved after months of practice.
She delivered a powerful forehand shot to win the point.
The coach demonstrated the correct grip for a forehand shot.

Related Terms

  • antonym – backhand shot: a stroke made with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke in sports
