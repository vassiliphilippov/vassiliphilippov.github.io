telepathetic bond

noun
intuitive connection between people
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləpəˈθɛtɪk bɑnd / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪpəˈθɛtɪk bɒnd / (BrE)
A deep, intuitive connection or understanding between two people, as if they can sense each other's thoughts or feelings without words.

Examples

strong telepathetic bond
After only a few meetings, they developed a telepathetic bond that surprised their friends.
Despite living far apart, their telepathetic bond allowed them to understand each other's moods instantly.
The twins claimed to share a telepathetic bond that let them sense when the other was upset.
