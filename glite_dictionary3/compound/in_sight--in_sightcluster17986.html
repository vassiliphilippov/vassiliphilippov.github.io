in sight

adjective
visible or within visual range
Pronunciation: / ɪn saɪt / (AmE), / ɪn saɪt / (BrE)
visible; within a distance at which something can be seen.

Examples

mountains in sight
As soon as the mountains came in sight, everyone started taking photographs.
Keep the children in sight at all times when near the water.
There was not a single car in sight on the empty road.
