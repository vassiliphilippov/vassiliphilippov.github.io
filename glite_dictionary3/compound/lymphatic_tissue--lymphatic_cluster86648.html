lymphatic tissue

noun
tissue of the lymphatic system
Pronunciation: / lɪmˈfætɪk ˈtɪʃu / (AmE), / lɪmˈfætɪk ˈtɪʃuː / (BrE)
Tissue that forms part of the lymphatic system and contains lymphocytes and other cells involved in the immune response.

Examples

lymphatic tissue in lymph nodes
Diseases such as lymphoma can affect lymphatic tissue throughout the body.
Doctors examined a sample of lymphatic tissue taken from her lymph nodes.
The tonsils are composed of lymphatic tissue that helps protect the body from infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lymphoid tissue: tissue consisting mainly of lymphocytes, similar to lymphatic tissue
