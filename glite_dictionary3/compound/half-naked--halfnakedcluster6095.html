half-naked

adjective
partially clothed; not fully dressed
Pronunciation: / ˌhæfˈneɪkɪd, ˌhɑːfˈneɪkɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
partially or only partly clothed; wearing few clothes so that a significant part of the body is uncovered

Examples

half-naked man
He was caught on camera half-naked after jumping into the pool unexpectedly.
She wrapped a towel around herself, embarrassed to be standing half-naked at the door.
The children ran around the backyard half-naked during the summer heat.
