sacramental wine

noun
wine used in Christian Communion
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəl waɪn / (AmE), / ˌsakrəˈmɛntl waɪn / (BrE)
Wine consecrated and used during Christian Communion or Eucharist services.

Examples

bless sacramental wine
During the Easter service, the sacramental wine was blessed and distributed to all.
Many churches use red sacramental wine to symbolize the blood of Christ.
The priest poured the sacramental wine into the chalice before the congregation gathered for Communion.
