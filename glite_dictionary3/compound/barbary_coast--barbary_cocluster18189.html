Barbary Coast

noun
notorious district known for vice and nightlife
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbɛri ˈkoʊst / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbəri ˈkəʊst / (BrE)
An area of a city, especially San Francisco's waterfront in the late 19th century, known for its gambling, brothels, saloons, and lawlessness.

Examples

the Barbary Coast nightlife
After dark, the Barbary Coast was alive with music, laughter, and danger.
City leaders vowed to clean up the Barbary Coast and restore order to the waterfront.
The novel is set in the days when the Barbary Coast was infamous for its wild nightlife.
