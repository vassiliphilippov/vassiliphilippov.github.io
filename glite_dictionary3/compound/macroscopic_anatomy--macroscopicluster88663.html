macroscopic anatomy

noun
study of body structures visible to the naked eye
Pronunciation: / ˌmæk.rəˈskɑː.pɪk əˈnæt.ə.mi / (AmE), / ˌmæk.rəˈskɒ.pɪk əˈnæt.ə.mi / (BrE)
The study of the structure of the body and its parts that can be seen without a microscope; also known as gross anatomy.
