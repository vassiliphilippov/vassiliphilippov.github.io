gut reaction

noun
instinctive emotional response
Pronunciation: / ɡʌt riˈæk.ʃən / (AmE), / ɡʌt riˈæk.ʃən / (BrE)
an instinctive and immediate emotional response to a situation or event, without conscious reasoning

Examples

immediate gut reaction
Her gut reaction was to refuse the offer without a second thought.
My gut reaction was to distrust the new manager, though I couldn't explain why.
Sometimes you just have to trust your gut reaction when making a difficult choice.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intuition: the ability to understand something quickly without the need for conscious reasoning
