brotherly love

noun
kindly affection or goodwill toward others
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌðərli lʌv / (AmE), / ˈbrʌðəli lʌv / (BrE)
a kindly and caring attitude of goodwill or affection toward others, especially as if they were one's brothers

Examples

brotherly love for others
He treated strangers with such brotherly love that everyone felt welcome.
Many faiths encourage the practice of brotherly love toward all humanity.
The community was united by a sense of brotherly love and mutual support.
