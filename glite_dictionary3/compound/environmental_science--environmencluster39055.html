environmental science

noun
study of organisms and their environment
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
The branch of science that studies the interactions between living organisms and their natural environment, including the impact of human activity.

Examples

environmental science research project
Recent advances in environmental science have helped address global climate challenges.
She decided to major in environmental science because she was interested in protecting natural resources.
Working in environmental science often involves both laboratory research and field studies.
