Cape Kennedy

proper noun
cape in Florida, site of NASA center
Pronunciation: / keɪp ˈkɛnədi / (AmE), / keɪp ˈkɛnədi / (BrE)
a sandy cape on the eastern coast of Florida, formerly known as Cape Canaveral, notable as the site of a major NASA spaceflight center

Examples

Cape Kennedy space launches
Many significant Apollo missions were launched from Cape Kennedy during the 1960s.
The renaming of Cape Kennedy honored President John F. Kennedy’s support for the space program.
Tourists often visit Cape Kennedy to learn about the history of American spaceflight.
