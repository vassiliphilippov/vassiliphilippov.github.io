Monopoly money

noun
play or fake currency; valueless money
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːpəli ˈmʌni / (AmE), / məˈnɒpəli ˈmʌni / (BrE)
Money used in the board game Monopoly, or any play money or currency with no real value; figuratively, money that is treated as if it has no real-world consequences or value.

Examples

spend Monopoly money freely
After getting his bonus, he was so excited he started spending cash like it was Monopoly money.
Her little brother filled his piggy bank with Monopoly money from the game box.
The casino handed out chips that looked just like Monopoly money to new players.

Related Terms

  • synonym – play money: toy or imitation money used for playing games
  • antonym – real money: actual currency with real purchasing power
