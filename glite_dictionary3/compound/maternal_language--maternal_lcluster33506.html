maternal language

noun
native language; first language
Pronunciation: / məˈtɝːnəl ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məˈtɜːnəl ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language that a person learns from birth and speaks as their native language, typically passed down from parents
