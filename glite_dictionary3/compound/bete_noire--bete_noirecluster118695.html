bete noire

noun
a person or thing greatly disliked
Pronunciation: / beɪt ˈnwɑr / (AmE), / beɪt ˈnwɑː / (BrE)
A person or thing that is particularly disliked, avoided, or feared; an object of aversion.

Examples

bete noire of teachers
For years, grammar mistakes have been the bete noire of every English teacher.
Taxes are the bete noire of small business owners everywhere.
The new manager quickly became the bete noire of many employees due to his strict rules.
