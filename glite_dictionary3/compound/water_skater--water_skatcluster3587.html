water skater

noun
insect moving on water's surface
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˌskeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌskeɪtə / (BrE)
a long-legged insect that moves quickly across the surface of water, supported by surface tension

Examples

water skater gliding on pond
Children watched a water skater gliding effortlessly over the pond.
Several water skaters darted away as I tossed a pebble into the stream.
The water skater uses its long legs to distribute its weight and avoid sinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pond skater: another name for the water skater, a surface-dwelling insect
  • synonym – water strider: common synonym for the insect that moves on water surfaces
