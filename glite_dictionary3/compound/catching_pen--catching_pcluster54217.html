catching pen

noun
enclosure for holding animals
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃɪŋ pɛn / (AmE), / ˈkatʃɪŋ pɛn / (BrE)
A small or medium-sized enclosure used to temporarily hold animals, especially for sorting or handling.

Examples

move cattle into catching pen
After rounding up the cattle, the ranchers guided them into the catching pen.
The sheep were led into the catching pen before being sorted for shearing.
Workers used the catching pen to temporarily hold the goats during health checks.
