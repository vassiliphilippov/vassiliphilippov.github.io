Kuki-Chin language

noun
language group of western Myanmar and surrounding regions
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːki ˈtʃɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkuːki ˈtʃɪn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
Any of a group of Kamarupan languages spoken mainly in western Myanmar (Burma), Bangladesh, and easternmost India.
