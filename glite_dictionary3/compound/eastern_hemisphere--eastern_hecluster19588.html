eastern hemisphere

noun
half of earth east of the prime meridian
Pronunciation: / ˈistɚn ˈhɛməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈiːstən ˈhemɪsˌfɪə / (BrE)
the half of the earth that lies east of the prime meridian, including Europe, Asia, Africa, and Australia
