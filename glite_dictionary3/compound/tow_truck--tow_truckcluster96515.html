tow truck

noun
vehicle for towing other vehicles
Pronunciation: / toʊ trʌk / (AmE), / təʊ trʌk / (BrE)
a truck fitted with special equipment for lifting and pulling vehicles, especially ones that are damaged or illegally parked

Examples

tow truck removes cars
A tow truck came to remove the illegally parked vehicle from the street.
We had to call a tow truck after our car broke down on the highway.
When his engine failed, a tow truck arrived within half an hour.
