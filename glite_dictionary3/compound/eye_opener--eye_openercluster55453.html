eye opener

noun
something that is surprising and revealing
Pronunciation: / aɪ ˈoʊpənɚ / (AmE), / aɪ ˈəʊpənə / (BrE)
an event, experience, or fact that is surprising and reveals something previously unknown or unexpected

Examples

an eye opener experience
His frank confession proved to be an eye opener for the entire team.
Reading the report was an eye opener that changed her perspective on the issue.
The trip to the developing world was a real eye opener for me.

Related Terms

  • synonym – revelation: a surprising fact that is made known
  • degree higher – shock: a sudden upsetting event or fact
