bottle rat

noun
person who drinks excessively at social events
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑt̬l rǽt / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl ˌrat / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who habitually drinks excessively, especially in social settings, often seeking out free drinks.

Examples

bottle rat at parties
Don't invite any bottle rats to the dinner; they just come for the booze.
I knew he was a bottle rat when he hovered near the bar all night.
She called him a bottle rat after he finished everyone else's drinks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lush: person who drinks alcohol excessively
  • antonym – teetotaler: person who abstains completely from alcohol
