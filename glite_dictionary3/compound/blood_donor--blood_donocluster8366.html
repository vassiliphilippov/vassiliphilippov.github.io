blood donor

noun
person who donates blood
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈdoʊ.nɚ / (AmE), / blʌd ˈdəʊ.nə / (BrE)
a person who voluntarily gives blood, typically for use in medical transfusions or research

Examples

blood donor registration
After registering as a blood donor, she gave her first pint of blood at the local hospital.
The Red Cross is running a blood donor drive this weekend to increase national supplies.
Without generous blood donors, many surgeries and emergency treatments would not be possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym – donor: a person who gives something, especially blood or an organ, to help someone else
  • antonym – recipient: a person who receives something, such as a transfusion
