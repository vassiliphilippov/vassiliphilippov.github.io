cover song

noun
a non-original recording of a previously released song
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvər sɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə sɒŋ / (BrE)
a recording or performance of a song that was previously released or made popular by another artist

Examples

famous cover song
He became famous online by posting cover song videos on his channel.
Her latest album features several cover songs from classic rock legends.
The band played a cover song at the wedding, and everyone recognized the tune.
