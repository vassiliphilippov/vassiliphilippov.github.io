tree swift

noun
crested, tree-nesting bird of SE Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈtriː ˌswɪft / (AmE), / ˈtriː ˌswɪft / (BrE)
a bird of Southeast Asia and the East Indies, related to true swifts but distinguished by upright crests and tree-nesting habits

Examples

tree swift in forest
Several species of tree swift are found in the forests of Indonesia and Malaysia.
The tree swift is notable for its elegant flight and unique nesting habits.
Unlike common swifts, the tree swift often builds its nest high in the branches of trees.
