chin diaper

noun
humorous term for a bib or chin cover
Pronunciation: / tʃɪn ˈdaɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / tʃɪn ˈdaɪ.pə / (BrE)
A humorous or informal term for a bib, face mask, or cloth cover placed over or under the chin, typically used to catch spills during messy eating or activities, or as a playful term for a baby's bib.

Examples

wear a chin diaper
During lunch, he wore a chin diaper to protect his shirt from spills.
She handed her father a chin diaper before he attempted the spicy wing challenge.
The toddler's bright blue chin diaper matched his onesie perfectly.
