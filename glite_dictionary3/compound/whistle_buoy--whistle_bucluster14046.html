whistle buoy

noun
buoy that emits a whistling sound for navigation
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪsəl ˌbuːi / (AmE), / ˈwɪsl ˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a buoy equipped with a device that makes a whistling sound, used as a navigational aid at sea
