review article

noun
critical evaluation or summary essay
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː ˈɑːtɪkl / (BrE)
an essay or article that provides a critical evaluation or summary of another work, such as a book, play, or research paper

Examples

write a review article
After reading the review article, I decided not to see the movie.
She spent weeks working on her review article about 19th-century English novels.
The scientist published a comprehensive review article summarizing the latest advances in cancer research.

Related Terms

  • synonym – critique: a detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, philosophical, or political theory
