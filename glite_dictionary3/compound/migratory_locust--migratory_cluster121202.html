migratory locust

noun
a locust species swarming in large groups
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪɡrəˌtɔri ˈloʊkəst / (AmE), / ˈmɪɡrəˌtəri ˈləʊkəst / (BrE)
an Old World species of locust that moves in large swarms, consuming crops and vegetation over wide areas
