to grow up and settle down

verb
mature and establish a stable life
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊ ʌp ənd ˌsɛt̬l ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ ʌp ənd ˌsɛtl ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
to mature and establish a stable, routine lifestyle, especially in adulthood, often by getting a steady job or starting a family

Examples
