kola nut

noun
tree with caffeine-containing nuts; source of cola extract
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə nʌt / (BrE)
A tropical African tree bearing large brown nuts that contain caffeine and are used as a source of cola extract.

Examples

kola nut tree
In many West African cultures, the kola nut is offered as a symbol of hospitality.
The caffeine in the kola nut provides a mild stimulating effect.
The extract from the kola nut is commonly used to flavor soft drinks.
