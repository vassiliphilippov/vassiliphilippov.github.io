shaping machine

noun
machine tool for shaping metal or wood
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine tool used for cutting and shaping metal or wood by moving a single-point cutting tool linearly against the workpiece

Examples

operate a shaping machine
Our workshop recently acquired a new shaping machine for complex woodwork projects.
Proper maintenance of the shaping machine ensures smoother operation and longer tool life.
The engineer operated the shaping machine to create precise grooves in the metal plate.
