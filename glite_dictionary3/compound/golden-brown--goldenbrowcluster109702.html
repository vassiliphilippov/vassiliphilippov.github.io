golden-brown

adjective
brown tinged with gold
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˌbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color tinged with gold; having a warm, rich brown hue with a golden cast.

Examples

golden-brown hair
Her golden-brown hair shone in the sunlight as she walked through the park.
The autumn leaves turned a beautiful golden-brown before falling to the ground.
The pastries came out of the oven a perfect golden-brown, ready to be served.
