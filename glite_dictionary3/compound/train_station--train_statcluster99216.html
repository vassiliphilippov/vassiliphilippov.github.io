train station

noun
place where trains stop for passengers or goods
Pronunciation: / treɪn ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / treɪn ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where trains stop for passengers to get on or off or for goods to be loaded or unloaded.

Examples

at the train station entrance
He met her at the train station after her long journey.
The city built a new train station to accommodate more commuters.
We arrived at the train station just in time to catch the midnight express.

  • synonym – railway station: a station where trains stop on a railway line
