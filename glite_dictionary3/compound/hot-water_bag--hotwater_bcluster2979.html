hot-water bag

noun
rubber container for hot water, used for warmth
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑt ˈwɔtər bæɡ / (AmE), / ˌhɒt ˈwɔːtə bæɡ / (BrE)
A stoppered rubber container that is filled with hot water and used to warm a bed or parts of the body.

Examples

fill a hot-water bag
On cold winter nights, a hot-water bag kept his feet warm in bed.
She placed a hot-water bag on her stomach to relieve the cramps.
The nurse gave him a hot-water bag to soothe his back pain.
