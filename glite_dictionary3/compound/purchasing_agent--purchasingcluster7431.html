purchasing agent

noun
person authorised to buy for others
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrtʃəsɪŋ ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːtʃəsɪŋ ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person or company authorized to buy goods or services on behalf of another person or organization, especially for business purposes

Examples

company purchasing agent
Every large company employs at least one purchasing agent to manage procurement.
She became the lead purchasing agent due to her expertise in sourcing raw materials.
The purchasing agent negotiated better prices for all office supplies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – buyer: someone who purchases goods or services
  • antonym – seller: someone who sells goods or services
