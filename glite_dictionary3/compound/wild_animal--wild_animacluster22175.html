wild animal

noun
non-domesticated animal in the wild
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈænəməl / (AmE), / waɪld ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
An animal that lives in its natural environment and is not domesticated.

Examples

a wild animal in nature
During the safari, we saw several wild animals roaming freely through the grasslands.
It's important not to feed wild animals as it can disrupt their natural behavior.
The forest reserve is home to many rare wild animals that are protected by law.

Related Terms

  • antonym – domesticated animal: an animal that has been tamed and kept by humans
  • synonym – feral animal: an animal that has reverted to a wild state from domestication
