fictile imagination

noun
creative ability to shape narratives
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪk.taɪl ɪˌmæ.dʒəˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɪk.taɪl ɪˌmadʒɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
