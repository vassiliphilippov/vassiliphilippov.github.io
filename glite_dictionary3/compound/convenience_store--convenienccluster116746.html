convenience store

noun
small shop open long hours for basic goods
Pronunciation: / kənˈvinjəns stɔr / (AmE), / kənˈviːniəns stɔː / (BrE)
A small retail shop selling a limited range of everyday items, such as snacks, drinks, and basic groceries, and typically open for extended hours.

Examples

local convenience store
I stopped at the convenience store to buy some milk and snacks on my way home.
Many convenience stores are open 24 hours a day for the benefit of late-night shoppers.
You can usually find basic medicines at a convenience store if the pharmacy is closed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – corner shop: small shop selling basic goods, especially in Britain
  • antonym – supermarket: large self-service store selling groceries and household goods
