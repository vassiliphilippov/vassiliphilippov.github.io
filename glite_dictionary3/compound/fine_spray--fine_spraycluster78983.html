fine spray

noun
very small liquid droplets in the air
Pronunciation: / faɪn spreɪ / (AmE), / faɪn spreɪ / (BrE)
very small droplets of liquid dispersed in the air, often forming a light mist

Examples

fine spray of water
A fine spray of water cooled the greenhouse during the hottest hours.
Morning dew left a fine spray gleaming on the lawn.
The perfume bottle emitted a fine spray with each press.
