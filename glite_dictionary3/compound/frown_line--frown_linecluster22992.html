frown line

noun
wrinkle formed by frowning
Pronunciation: / fraʊn laɪn / (AmE), / fraʊn laɪn / (BrE)
a line or wrinkle on the face, especially between the eyebrows, that forms as a result of frequent frowning

Examples

frown line between eyebrows
She was concerned about the frown lines that had started to appear on her forehead.
Some people use creams to try to reduce their frown lines.
Years of stress had left deep frown lines between his eyebrows.
