rotary encoder

noun
device for measuring shaft rotation
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊˌtɛri ɪnˈkoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəri ɪnˈkəʊdə / (BrE)
an electro-mechanical device that converts the angular position or motion of a rotating shaft or axle to an analog or digital code
