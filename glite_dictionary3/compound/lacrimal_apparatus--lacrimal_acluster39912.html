lacrimal apparatus

noun
tear-producing and draining system in the eye
Pronunciation: / ˈlækrɪməl əˈpærətəs / (AmE), / ˈlækrɪməl əˈparətəs / (BrE)
the parts of the eye that produce, convey, and drain tears, including the lacrimal gland and associated ducts
