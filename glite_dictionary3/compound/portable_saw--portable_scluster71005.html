portable saw

noun
handheld circular saw
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːrtəbəl sɔː / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəbəl sɔː / (BrE)
A saw equipped with a circular blade that is designed to be easily carried and operated by hand.

Examples

use a portable saw outdoors
A portable saw is handy for jobs that require moving between different locations.
He used a portable saw to cut through the wooden planks at the construction site.
Make sure the portable saw is unplugged before changing the blade.
