square metre

noun
unit of area equal to one metre by one metre
Pronunciation: / skwɛr ˈmitɚ / (AmE), / skwɛː ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to the area of a square with sides each measuring one metre

Examples

covers one square metre
The garden is approximately one hundred square metres.
The room measures twelve square metres in size.
They bought a roll of carpet that covers fifty square metres.
