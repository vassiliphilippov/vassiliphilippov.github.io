central city

noun
main central area of a city
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəl ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəl ˈsɪti / (BrE)
the main or most important area in the center of a city, especially where business and cultural activities are concentrated.

Examples

busy central city streets
Efforts are being made to revitalize the central city and attract new businesses.
Many residents commute from the suburbs into the central city for work each day.
The central city is bustling with activity during the holidays.
