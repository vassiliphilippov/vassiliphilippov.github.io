banded gecko

noun
a gecko with dark bands and movable eyelids
Pronunciation: / ˈbændɪd ˈɡɛkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd ˈɡɛkəʊ / (BrE)
a small gecko native to the southwestern United States and the Florida Gulf Coast, notable for dark bands across its body and movable eyelids
