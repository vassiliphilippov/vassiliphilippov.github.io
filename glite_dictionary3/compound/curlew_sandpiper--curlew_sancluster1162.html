curlew sandpiper

noun
a small Old World sandpiper with a curved bill
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜr.lu ˈsændˌpaɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.ljuː ˈsændˌpaɪ.pə / (BrE)
a small migratory sandpiper (Calidris ferruginea) of the Old World with a curved bill resembling that of a curlew
