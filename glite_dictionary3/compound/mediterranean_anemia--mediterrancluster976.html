Mediterranean anemia

noun
inherited anemia (thalassemia)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdəˌtɛrˈeɪniən əˈniːmiə / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən əˈniːmiə / (BrE)
