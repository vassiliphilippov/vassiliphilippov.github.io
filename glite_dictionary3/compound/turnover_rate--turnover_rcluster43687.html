turnover rate

noun
rate of employee replacement
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrˌnoʊvər reɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnˌəʊvə reɪt / (BrE)
the rate at which employees leave a workforce and are replaced by new employees within a specific period

Examples

turnover rate among staff
A low turnover rate often indicates satisfied employees and good management.
Human resources implemented new policies to reduce the annual turnover rate.
The company's high turnover rate made it difficult to maintain a stable team.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attrition rate: the rate at which staff or employees leave a company, often measured over a specific period
