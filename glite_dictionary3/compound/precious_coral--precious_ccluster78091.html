precious coral

noun
red or pink coral used in jewelry
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃəs ˈkɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃəs ˈkɒrəl / (BrE)
A hard, red or pink stony skeleton of certain Mediterranean corals, valued for use in jewelry and ornamentation.

Examples

precious coral jewelry
Antique jewelers often use precious coral to craft unique rings and pendants.
Because of overharvesting, some varieties of precious coral are now protected.
The necklace was made from rare precious coral found in the Mediterranean Sea.
