golf hole

noun
one playing unit in golf (tee to green)
Pronunciation: / ɡɑlf hoʊl / (AmE), / ɡɒlf həʊl / (BrE)
A single playing unit on a golf course, from tee to green, completed from the first stroke to when the ball is holed.
