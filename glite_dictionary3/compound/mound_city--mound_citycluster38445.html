Mound City

proper noun
archaeological site of Native American mounds in Ohio
Pronunciation: / maʊnd ˈsɪti / (AmE), / maʊnd ˈsɪti / (BrE)
A significant archaeological site in Ohio, United States, associated with ancient Native American Hopewell cultures and known for its earthen mounds.

Examples

Mound City archaeological site
Archaeologists have conducted extensive research at Mound City to understand ancient Native American cultures.
Many visitors travel to Mound City to see the prehistoric earthworks.
The museum exhibit featured artifacts excavated from Mound City.
