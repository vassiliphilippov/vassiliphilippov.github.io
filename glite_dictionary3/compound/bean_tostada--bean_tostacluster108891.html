bean tostada

noun
Mexican dish with tortilla and beans
Pronunciation: / bin toʊˈstɑːdə / (AmE), / biːn tɒˈstɑːdə / (BrE)
A Mexican dish consisting of a flat, crispy tortilla topped with refried beans, and often other ingredients such as lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
