Kodiak bear

noun
large brown bear of Alaska and British Columbia
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdiˌæk bɛr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdiˌæk beə / (BrE)
a very large subspecies of the brown bear found on the coasts of Alaska, especially on Kodiak Island, and in British Columbia

Examples
