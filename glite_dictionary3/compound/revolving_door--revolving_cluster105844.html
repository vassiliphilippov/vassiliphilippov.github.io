revolving door

noun
rotating entrance door with panels
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑlvɪŋ dɔr / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒlvɪŋ dɔː / (BrE)
A door made of several panels arranged around a central axis which rotate to allow people to enter or leave a building while minimizing the exchange of air.

Examples

glass revolving door
Revolving doors are often used in office buildings to save energy by limiting airflow.
She got briefly stuck in the revolving door during the morning rush.
The hotel lobby featured a grand revolving door that spun smoothly as guests arrived.
