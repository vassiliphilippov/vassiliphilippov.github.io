agricultural labourer

noun
farm worker; manual farm laborer
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈleɪbərər / (AmE), / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈleɪbərə / (BrE)
a person who works on the land, especially doing manual farm work such as planting, cultivating, and harvesting crops

Examples

agricultural labourer picking crops
During the harvest season, every agricultural labourer was needed in the fields from dawn until dusk.
For decades, he worked as an agricultural labourer on several large farms in the region.
The government introduced new policies to improve the living conditions of agricultural labourers.
