mutual aid

noun
grassroots reciprocal community support
Pronunciation: / mjutu59l e6ad / (AmE), / mjutu.59l e6ad / (BrE)
(in community and activist contexts) voluntary, reciprocal help organized among neighbors or peers, especially during crises, independent of formal institutions.

Examples

local mutual aid group
During the lockdown, local mutual aid groups organized food deliveries for vulnerable neighbors every week.
Volunteers set up a mutual aid fund that helped pay bills for families who lost income.
Activists argue that mutual aid builds solidarity by allowing people to help one another without relying on institutions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – charity: one-directional aid or donations provided by organizations or individuals rather than reciprocal mutual help
  • synonym – community support: practical help provided within a local community, often informally organized
  • synonym – solidarity: unity and mutual support within a group, often used in activist contexts
