whip scorpion

noun
tailed arachnid resembling a scorpion
Pronunciation: / wɪp ˈskɔːrpiən / (AmE), / wɪp ˈskɔːpiən / (BrE)
A nonvenomous arachnid similar to a scorpion but with a long, thin, whip-like tail and no stinger.
