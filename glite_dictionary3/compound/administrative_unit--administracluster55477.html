administrative unit

noun
virtual container for delegated administration
Pronunciation: / 59dccm6an6ac8stre6at6av c8jud0n6at / (AmE), / 59dccm6an6asc8tre6at6av c8jud0n6at / (BrE)
(in cloud computing and identity management) a virtual or logical container used to group users, devices, or resources so that administration, permissions, and policies can be delegated or scoped to that subset.

Examples

cloud administrative unit
In Azure AD, an administrative unit lets you delegate management for a subset of users and devices.
The IT team created several administrative units to scope group policies and restrict admin roles.
During onboarding, she assigned new hires to an administrative unit responsible for regional compliance and support.

Related Terms

  • synonym – group: a collection of users or resources used to assign permissions or policies in IT systems.
  • synonym – organizational unit: a directory or administrative subdivision used to group objects for management (common in directory services).
