travel allowance

noun
money provided for travel expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəl əˈlaʊəns / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl əˈlaʊəns / (BrE)
an amount of money given to cover the costs of travel, especially for work or official purposes

Examples

travel allowance for work trips
Employees are entitled to a travel allowance when attending conferences in other cities.
Her travel allowance covered accommodation and meals during her business trip.
The company increased the travel allowance for field staff this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – per diem: an allowance or payment for daily expenses, especially while traveling for work
