Mongol dynasty

noun
imperial Chinese dynasty (1279–1368) established by Mongols
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑŋˌɡoʊl ˈdaɪ.nə.sti / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋ.ɡɒl ˈdaɪ.nə.sti / (BrE)
the imperial dynasty that ruled China from 1279 to 1368, established by the Mongols and known as the Yuan dynasty

Examples

the Mongol dynasty in China
Many important inventions were introduced during the rule of the Mongol dynasty in China.
The Mongol dynasty brought significant changes to China's administration and culture.
Under the Mongol dynasty, trade along the Silk Road greatly expanded.
