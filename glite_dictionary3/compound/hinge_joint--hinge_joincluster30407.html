hinge joint

noun
a joint allowing movement in one plane
Pronunciation: / hɪndʒ dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / hɪndʒ dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a joint in the body, such as the knee or elbow, that allows movement mainly in one direction, like a door hinge
