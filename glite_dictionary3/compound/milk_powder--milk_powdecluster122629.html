milk powder

noun
dehydrated milk product
Pronunciation: / mɪlk ˈpaʊdɚ / (AmE), / mɪlk ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
Powdered milk made by removing the water from liquid milk through evaporation, used as a substitute for fresh milk.

Examples

milk powder in coffee
After the disaster, aid workers distributed milk powder to families with young children.
Instant coffee mixes often include milk powder for convenience.
She always keeps a tin of milk powder in the kitchen for emergencies.
