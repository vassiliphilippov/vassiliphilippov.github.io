pommel horse

noun
gymnastic apparatus with handles for routines
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌməl hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈpɒməl hɔːs / (BrE)
a piece of gymnastic apparatus with a cylindrical, leather-covered body and two handles (pommels) fixed near the center, used for performing routines involving swings and balances

Examples

on the pommel horse routine
He won a gold medal on the pommel horse at the gymnastics championships.
Practicing the pommel horse requires a great deal of strength and coordination.
The gymnastics coach demonstrated a complicated move on the pommel horse.
