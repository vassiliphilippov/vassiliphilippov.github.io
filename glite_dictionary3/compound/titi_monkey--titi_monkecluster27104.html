titi monkey

noun
small South American monkey
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːti ˌmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈtiːti ˌmʌŋki / (BrE)
A small, long-furred South American monkey with a long nonprehensile tail, found in forests.

Examples

titi monkey in trees
Scientists observed a family of titi monkeys leaping between the branches.
The titi monkey is known for its incredibly soft and colorful fur.
Unlike some other primates, the titi monkey has a nonprehensile tail.
