bottle-nosed dolphin

noun
species of dolphin with rounded forehead
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːtəl noʊzd ˈdɑːlfɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɒtl nəʊzd ˈdɒlfɪn / (BrE)
A large, intelligent dolphin with a rounded forehead and a prominent beak, found mainly in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean.

Examples

bottle-nosed dolphin jumps
Children enjoyed watching the bottle-nosed dolphin leap out of the water at the aquarium.
The bottle-nosed dolphin is known for its intelligence and playful behavior.
We spotted a group of bottle-nosed dolphins swimming near the shore.
