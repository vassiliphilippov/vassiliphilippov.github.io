Ethiopian coffee ceremony

noun
traditional Ethiopian ritual of coffee preparation
Pronunciation: / ˌiːθiˈoʊpiən ˈkɔfi ˌsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˌiːθiˈəʊpiən ˈkɒfi ˌsɛrəˈməʊni / (BrE)
a traditional Ethiopian ritual involving the preparation, serving, and drinking of coffee, often performed as a social or ceremonial event

Examples

Ethiopian coffee ceremony with guests
At the cultural festival, a woman in traditional dress prepared coffee for guests as part of the Ethiopian coffee ceremony.
