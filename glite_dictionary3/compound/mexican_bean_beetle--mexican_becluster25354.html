Mexican bean beetle

noun
a beetle pest of bean plants
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛksɪkən bin ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən biːn ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a beetle originally from Mexico that feeds on the leaves of bean plants, often considered a pest in the United States
