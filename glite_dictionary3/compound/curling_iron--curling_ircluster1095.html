curling iron

noun
electrical device for curling hair
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːlɪŋ ˌaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈkɜːlɪŋ ˌaɪən / (BrE)
A cylindrical electrical device used for curling hair by heating a lock wound around it.

Examples

Be careful not to burn your fingers when using a curling iron.
She used a curling iron to add waves to her hair before the party.
