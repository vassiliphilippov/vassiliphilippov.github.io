Holy Spirit

noun
Christian term: third person of the Trinity
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
(in Christian belief) the third person of the Trinity, regarded as God actively working in the world.

Examples

the Holy Spirit descends
After Jesus' resurrection, the Holy Spirit descended upon the apostles at Pentecost.
Christians believe that the Holy Spirit guides and comforts believers.
Many prayers invoke the presence of the Holy Spirit for guidance and wisdom.
