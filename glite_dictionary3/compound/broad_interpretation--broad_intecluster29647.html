broad interpretation

noun
liberal approach to constitutional interpretation
Pronunciation: / brɔd ˌɪntərprəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / brɔːd ˌɪntəprɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an approach to interpreting the U.S. Constitution in which judges consider not only the text but also the spirit of the times and the needs of the nation
