night bell

noun
doorbell for nighttime use
Pronunciation: / naɪt bɛl / (AmE), / naɪt bɛl / (BrE)
A doorbell designed or intended to be used at night, especially to alert someone during nighttime hours.

Examples

ring the night bell
If you arrive late, just ring the night bell by the main entrance.
The hospital's night bell was installed for emergency cases after hours.
