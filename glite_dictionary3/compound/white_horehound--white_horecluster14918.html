white horehound

noun
European aromatic herb with white flowers
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈhɔr.haʊnd / (AmE), / waɪt ˈhɔː.haʊnd / (BrE)
A European aromatic herb (Marrubium vulgare) with hairy leaves and clusters of small white flowers, whose leaves produce a bitter extract used medicinally and as flavoring.
