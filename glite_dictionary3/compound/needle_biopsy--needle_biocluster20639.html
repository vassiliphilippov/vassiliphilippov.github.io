needle biopsy

noun
tissue sample taken with a hollow needle
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəl ˈbaɪɒpsi / (AmE), / ˈniːdl ˈbaɪəpˌsi / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which a sample of tissue is taken from the body using a hollow needle for examination

Examples

needle biopsy procedure
A needle biopsy can help diagnose cancer without the need for surgery.
She was nervous before her needle biopsy, but the procedure was quick and mostly painless.
The doctor performed a needle biopsy to obtain a tissue sample from the tumor.
