birch oil

noun
fragrant oil from birch bark, used for pain relief
Pronunciation: / bɜrtʃ ɔɪl / (AmE), / bɜːtʃ ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fragrant oil extracted from birch bark, containing methyl salicylate and used externally for minor muscle and joint pain

Examples

birch oil for sore muscles
Applying birch oil to the joints may help reduce minor aches and pains.
Some athletes use birch oil to soothe sore muscles after intense exercise.
The strong scent of birch oil is reminiscent of wintergreen candy.
