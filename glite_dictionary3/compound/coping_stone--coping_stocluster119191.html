coping stone

noun
top protective stone on a wall
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊpɪŋ stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkəʊpɪŋ stəʊn / (BrE)
a stone placed on top of a wall to protect it and sometimes to add decoration or a finished look
