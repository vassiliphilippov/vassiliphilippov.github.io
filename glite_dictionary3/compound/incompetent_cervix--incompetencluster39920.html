incompetent cervix

noun
uterine cervix that opens too early in pregnancy
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑːmpətənt ˈsɜːrvɪks / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənt ˈsɜːvɪks / (BrE)
a condition in which the uterine cervix weakens and opens prematurely during pregnancy, potentially causing miscarriage or preterm birth

Examples

incompetent cervix during pregnancy
A cervical cerclage procedure can help women with an incompetent cervix carry a pregnancy to term.
The doctor diagnosed her with an incompetent cervix after a series of premature miscarriages.
Women experiencing repeated pregnancy loss may be tested for an incompetent cervix.
