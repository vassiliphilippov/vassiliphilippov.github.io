Spanish peseta

noun
former currency unit of Spain
Pronunciation: / spænɪʃ pəˈseɪtə / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ pəˈsiːtə / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Spain, equal to 100 centimos, used before the adoption of the euro

Examples

Spanish peseta coin
Before the euro, people in Spain used the Spanish peseta for all their daily transactions.
