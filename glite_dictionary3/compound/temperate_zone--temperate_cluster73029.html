temperate zone

noun
region between tropics and polar circles; mild climate
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərət zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərət zəʊn / (BrE)
the region of the Earth's surface lying between the tropics and the polar circles, characterized by mild or moderate climate
