pawn ticket

noun
receipt from pawnbroker for pledged items
Pronunciation: / pɔn ˈtɪkɪt / (AmE), / pɔːn ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a receipt given by a pawnbroker for items left as security for a loan

Examples

pawn ticket for a watch
He lost the pawn ticket and was worried he couldn’t retrieve his watch.
She kept the pawn ticket in her purse until she could reclaim her jewelry.
The shop refused to return the guitar without the original pawn ticket.
