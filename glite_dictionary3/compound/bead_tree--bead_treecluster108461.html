bead tree

noun
tropical tree with ornamental seeds
Pronunciation: / bid triː / (AmE), / biːd triː / (BrE)
a small tropical tree of the West Indies and northeastern Venezuela, with large oblong pointed leaflets, clusters of purple flowers, and ornamental seeds that are black or scarlet with black spots

Examples

bead tree seeds
In the botanical gardens, several species of bead tree are on display for visitors.
The bead tree produces seeds that are often used in jewelry and crafts.
The leaves of the bead tree provide shade in many Caribbean villages.
