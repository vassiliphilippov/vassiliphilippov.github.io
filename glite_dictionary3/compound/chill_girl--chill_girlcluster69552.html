chill girl

noun
relaxed, easy-going woman
Pronunciation: / tʃɪl ɡɜrl / (AmE), / tʃɪl ɡɜːl / (BrE)
A woman or girl who is easy-going, relaxed, and does not get upset or anxious easily.

Examples

a chill girl at parties
As a chill girl, she never overreacts or creates unnecessary drama.
Everyone loves inviting the chill girl to gatherings because she keeps things laid-back.
Her friends admire her for being such a chill girl, always cool under pressure.
