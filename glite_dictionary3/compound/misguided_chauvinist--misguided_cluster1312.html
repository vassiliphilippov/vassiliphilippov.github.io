misguided chauvinist

noun
person with mistaken sexist beliefs
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈɡaɪdɪd ˈʃoʊvɪnɪst / (AmE), / mɪsˈɡaɪdɪd ˈʃəʊvɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who holds a prejudiced or exaggerated belief in the dominance or superiority of one gender, especially when such beliefs are based on errors or misunderstandings
