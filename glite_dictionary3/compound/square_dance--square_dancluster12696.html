to square dance

verb
perform a square dance
Pronunciation: / skwɛr dæns / (AmE), / skweə dɑːns / (BrE)
to perform or take part in a square dance, moving in set patterns typically as directed by a caller.

Examples

to square dance with friends
Every Saturday, dozens of couples square dance at the local community center.
Last year, we square danced in the parade, wearing matching costumes.
They decided to square dance at the festival after watching a demonstration.
