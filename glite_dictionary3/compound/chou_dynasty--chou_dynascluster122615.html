Chou dynasty

proper noun
Chinese imperial dynasty, 1122–221 BC
Pronunciation: / tʃoʊ ˈdaɪnəsti / (AmE), / tʃəʊ ˈdɪnəsti / (BrE)
the imperial dynasty of China from about 1122 to 221 BC, notable for the development of Confucianism and Taoism

Examples

the Chou dynasty period
Artifacts from the Chou dynasty provide valuable insight into ancient Chinese society.
During the Chou dynasty, the concept of the Mandate of Heaven became central to Chinese political thought.
The Chou dynasty saw the emergence of both Confucianism and Taoism as major philosophies.
