Christian religion

noun
religion based on Jesus Christ's teachings
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstʃən rɪˈlɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən rɪˈlɪdʒən / (BrE)
The religion based on the life and teachings of Jesus Christ, as described in the New Testament, emphasizing belief in Jesus as the savior.

Examples

the Christian religion teaches love
Debates about morality are often influenced by the values of the Christian religion.
Many people celebrate holidays that are rooted in the Christian religion.
The Christian religion teaches forgiveness and love for one's neighbor.
