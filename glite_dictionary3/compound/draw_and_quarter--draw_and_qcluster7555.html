to draw and quarter

verb
execute by pulling apart with horses
Pronunciation: / drɔː ənd ˈkwɔrtər /; / drɔː ənd ˈkwɔːrtɚ / (AmE), / drɔː ənd ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
