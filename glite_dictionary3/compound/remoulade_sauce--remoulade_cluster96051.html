remoulade sauce

noun
mayonnaise-based sauce with herbs and mustard
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛməˈlɑd sɔs / (AmE), / ˌrɛməˈlɑːd sɔːs / (BrE)
A cold, creamy sauce made with mayonnaise, herbs, mustard, and capers, often served with salads, seafood, or cold meats.

Examples

remoulade sauce with seafood
Many seafood dishes in Louisiana are paired with remoulade sauce for extra flavor.
She made a homemade remoulade sauce to accompany the cold roast beef.
The crab cakes were served with a tangy remoulade sauce on the side.
