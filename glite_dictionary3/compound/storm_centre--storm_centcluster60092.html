storm centre

noun
central low-pressure area of a storm
Pronunciation: / stɔrm ˈsɛntɚ / (AmE), / stɔːm ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
the central area of a storm where the barometric pressure is lowest

Examples

the storm centre of a hurricane
High winds were recorded near the storm centre during the hurricane.
Meteorologists tracked the storm centre as it moved across the Gulf Coast.
The barometric pressure dropped sharply at the storm centre of the approaching cyclone.
