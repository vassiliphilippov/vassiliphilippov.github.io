DNA polymerase

noun
enzyme for DNA synthesis/replication
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːɛnˈeɪ pəˈlɪm.əˌreɪs / (AmE), / ˌdiːɛnˈeɪ pɒˈlɪm.əˌreɪz / (BrE)
