cooking apple

noun
apple variety used for cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkɪŋ ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈkʊkɪŋ ˌæpəl / (BrE)
an apple variety grown or selected for use in cooking, such as in pies or sauces, rather than for eating raw

Examples

cooking apple pie

Unlike dessert apples, cooking apples tend to soften quickly when heated.
