wildlife documentary

noun
film or TV about animals and nature
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌlaɪf ˌdɑkjəˈmɛntəri / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldlaɪf ˌdɒkjʊˈmɛntri / (BrE)
A film or television program that features animals in their natural habitats and explores aspects of nature and the environment.
