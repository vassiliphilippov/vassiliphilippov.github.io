melamine resin

noun
a thermosetting plastic material
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛləˌmiːn ˈrɛzən / (AmE), / ˈmɛləmiːn ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
a hard, thermosetting plastic material made by combining melamine with an aldehyde, used in molded objects, adhesives, and surface coatings
