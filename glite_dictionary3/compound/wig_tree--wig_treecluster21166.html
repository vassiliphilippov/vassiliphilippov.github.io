wig tree

noun
Old World shrub with puffy yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / wɪɡ triː / (AmE), / wɪɡ triː / (BrE)
An Old World shrub (Cotinus coggygria) with large plumes of yellowish, feathery flowers that resemble puffs of smoke.
