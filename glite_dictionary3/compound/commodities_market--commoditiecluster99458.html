commodities market

noun
exchange for trading raw materials and goods
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɑːdədiːz ˈmɑːrkɪt / (AmE), / kəˈmɒdətiːz ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
an organized exchange where raw materials and primary products are bought and sold, often for future delivery

Examples

commodities market prices rise
Investors closely monitor the commodities market for changes in oil and gold prices.
Many agricultural products are traded on the commodities market through futures contracts.
The commodities market can be volatile due to fluctuations in supply and demand.
