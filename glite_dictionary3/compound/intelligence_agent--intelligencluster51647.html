intelligence agent

noun
person secretly gathering information for government
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person secretly employed by a government to gather secret information, especially related to national security

Examples

undercover intelligence agent
She wrote a novel based on the life of a former intelligence agent.
The intelligence agent operated undercover for several years before being discovered.
The embassy was suspected of harboring an intelligence agent posing as a cultural attaché.
