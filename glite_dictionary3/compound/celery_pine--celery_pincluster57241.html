celery pine

noun
Australasian evergreen coniferous tree
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛləri paɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri paɪn / (BrE)
An Australasian evergreen coniferous tree with foliage resembling celery, comprising phyllodes borne in the axils of scalelike leaves.
