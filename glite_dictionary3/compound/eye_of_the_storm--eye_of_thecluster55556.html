eye of the storm

noun
calm center in chaos (literal or figurative)
Pronunciation: / aɪ əv ðə stɔrm / (AmE), / aɪ əv ðə stɔːm / (BrE)
the calm area at the center of a storm, especially a hurricane or cyclone; also used metaphorically to describe a period or place of peace in the middle of chaos or conflict

Examples

eye of the storm moment
After hours of intense negotiations, the company was finally in the eye of the storm before the next round of debate began.
For a moment, the small town was in the eye of the storm as the hurricane passed overhead.
In the chaos of the busy hospital, the doctor’s office felt like the eye of the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – calm before the storm: a peaceful period before a period of turmoil
