light show

noun
display of moving colored lights
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt ˌʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌʃəʊ / (BrE)
a display of colored lights, often moving and changing in patterns, typically as part of an entertainment event

Examples

amazing light show
During the festival, a spectacular light show illuminated the entire park.
The concert featured an impressive light show that synchronized with the music.
We were mesmerized by the colorful light show at the science museum.
