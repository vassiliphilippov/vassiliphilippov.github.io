Peyer's patch

noun
lymphoid nodule in the intestine
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪərz pætʃ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪəz pætʃ / (BrE)
one of the small, oval lymphoid nodules found in the wall of the small intestine, especially near the junction of the ileum and colon, involved in immune responses
