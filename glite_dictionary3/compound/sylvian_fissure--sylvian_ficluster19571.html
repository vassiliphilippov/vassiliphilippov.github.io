Sylvian fissure

noun
deep groove dividing frontal and temporal lobes of the brain
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlviən ˈfɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlviən ˈfɪʃə / (BrE)
the deep groove separating the frontal and temporal lobes of the brain in both hemispheres; also known as the lateral sulcus
