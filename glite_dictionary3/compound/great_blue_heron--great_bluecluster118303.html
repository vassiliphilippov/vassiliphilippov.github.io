great blue heron

noun
large North American heron
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt blu ˈhɛr.ən / (AmE), / ɡreɪt bluː ˈhe.rən / (BrE)
a large North American heron with bluish-grey plumage, long legs, and a long neck, commonly found near shores and wetlands
