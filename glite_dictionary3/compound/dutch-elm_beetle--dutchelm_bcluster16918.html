Dutch-elm beetle

noun
beetle that spreads Dutch elm disease
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌtʃ ˌɛlm ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌtʃ ˌɛlm ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a beetle that transmits the fungus responsible for Dutch elm disease, particularly affecting elm trees
