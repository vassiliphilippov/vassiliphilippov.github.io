measuring worm

noun
caterpillar of Geometridae moths, moves in loops
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ wɜrm / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ wəːm / (BrE)
a small hairless caterpillar, especially of the family Geometridae, with legs only on its front and rear segments, noted for its characteristic looping movement

Examples
