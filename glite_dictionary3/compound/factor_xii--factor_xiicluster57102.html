factor XII

noun
blood coagulation protein (Hageman factor)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ ˈtwɛl.v / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tə ˈtwɛlv / (BrE)
a coagulation protein in blood plasma whose deficiency causes increased clotting time; also known as Hageman factor
