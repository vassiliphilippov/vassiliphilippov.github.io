drain system

noun
system for carrying off excess water
Pronunciation: / dreɪn ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / dreɪn ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system of drains or watercourses designed to carry off excess water from an area.

Examples

drain system removes water
A blocked drain system can cause water to accumulate on roads and sidewalks.
Farmers rely on an efficient drain system to keep their fields from becoming waterlogged.
The city upgraded its drain system to prevent flooding during heavy rains.
