Czech Republic

proper noun
country in central Europe
Pronunciation: / ˈʧɛk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / ˈʧɛk rɪˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
A landlocked country in central Europe, formed after the peaceful separation from Slovakia in 1993, known for its historical cities, castles, and cultural heritage.
