lead sandwich

noun
humorous term for a very heavy sandwich
Pronunciation: / lɛd ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / lɛd ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A humorous term for an especially heavy or filling sandwich; sometimes used metaphorically to describe a burdensome or difficult situation.
