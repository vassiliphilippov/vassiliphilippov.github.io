reviewing stand

noun
platform for viewing parades or ceremonies
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvjuːɪŋ stænd / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuːɪŋ stand / (BrE)
a temporary platform or structure from which officials or spectators can observe and review a parade, procession, or ceremony

Examples

reviewing stand for parade
Dignitaries gathered in the reviewing stand to watch the military display.
Spectators lined the street while officials applauded from the reviewing stand.
The mayor sat on the reviewing stand as the parade marched past.
