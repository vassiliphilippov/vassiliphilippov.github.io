Jacob's ladder

noun
nautical rope or chain ladder
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪkəbz ˈlædər / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪkəbz ˈladə / (BrE)
a hanging ladder made of ropes or chains with wooden or metal rungs, used especially on ships
