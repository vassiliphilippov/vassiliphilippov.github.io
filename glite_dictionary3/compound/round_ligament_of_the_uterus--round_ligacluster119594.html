round ligament of the uterus

noun
ligament connecting uterus to labia majora
Pronunciation: / raʊnd ˈlɪɡəmənt əv ði juˈtɛrəs / (AmE), / raʊnd ˈlɪɡəmənt əv ði juːˈtɛrəs / (BrE)
either of a pair of cord-like ligaments attached to the uterus, running in front of and below the opening of the Fallopian tube, passing through the inguinal canal to the labia majora

Examples

pain in the round ligament of the uterus
During pregnancy, pain can occur in the round ligament of the uterus due to stretching.
The round ligament of the uterus helps maintain the anteverted position of the uterus.
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the round ligament of the uterus during the procedure.
