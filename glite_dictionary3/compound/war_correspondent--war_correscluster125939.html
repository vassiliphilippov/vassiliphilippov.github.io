war correspondent

noun
journalist reporting from war zones
Pronunciation: / wɔr ˌkɔrəˈspɑndənt / (AmE), / wɔː ˌkɒrɪˈspɒndənt / (BrE)
A journalist who reports news and commentary directly from combat zones for publication or broadcast.

Examples

As a war correspondent, she risked her life to give a firsthand account of the conflict.
