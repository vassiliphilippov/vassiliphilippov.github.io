out of gear

adjective
with gears disengaged
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv ɡɪr / (AmE), / aʊt əv ɡɪə / (BrE)
(of a machine or vehicle) having its gears disengaged so that power is not transmitted to its parts.

Examples

out of gear in a car
After stopping on the hill, he left the truck out of gear for a moment.
Make sure the machine is out of gear before performing any maintenance.
The engine was still running, but the transmission was out of gear.

Related Terms

  • antonym – in gear: with gears engaged and transmitting power
