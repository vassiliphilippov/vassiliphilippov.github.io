secondary dentition

noun
set of permanent teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənˌdɛr.i dɛnˈtɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.əndəri dɛnˈtɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
the set of permanent teeth that replace the primary teeth in humans and some animals
