radio detection and ranging

noun
system using microwaves to locate distant objects
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊ dɪˈtɛkʃən ənd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊ dɪˈtɛkʃən ənd ˈreɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
A system or instrument that uses the echo of pulses of microwave radiation to detect and locate distant objects, such as aircraft or ships.
