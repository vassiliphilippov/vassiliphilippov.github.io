trading card

noun
collectible card traded as a hobby
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdɪŋ kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdɪŋ kɑːd / (BrE)
a small card, typically featuring a picture or information on one side, collected and traded as a hobby, especially by children

Examples

trading card collection
After school, children gathered to exchange their trading cards in the park.
He has a huge collection of rare trading cards from the 1980s.
She found her favorite baseball player's trading card in a new pack.

Related Terms

  • synonym – collector's card: a card collected as a hobby, often for its value or rarity
  • antonym – playing card: card used in games, not primarily for trading or collecting
