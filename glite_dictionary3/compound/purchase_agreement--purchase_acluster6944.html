purchase agreement

noun
legal contract for a purchase
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.tʃəs əˌɡriː.mənt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.tʃəs əˌɡriː.mənt / (BrE)
a legal contract stating the details and terms under which a purchase will take place, especially for property or goods

Examples

sign a purchase agreement
Before finalizing the deal, both parties signed a purchase agreement outlining the terms.
The purchase agreement clearly stated that all appliances were included in the sale.
You should always read the purchase agreement carefully before buying a house.
