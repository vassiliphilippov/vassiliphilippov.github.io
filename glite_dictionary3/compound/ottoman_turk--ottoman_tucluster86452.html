Ottoman Turk

noun
Turkish member of the Ottoman Empire
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːtəˌmæn tɜrk / (AmE), / ˈɒtəmən tɜːk / (BrE)
a member of the Turkish people, especially one belonging to the tribe or dynasty founded by Osman I, who established the Ottoman Empire.
