neem tree

noun
large medicinal tree of the Indian subcontinent
Pronunciation: / nim tri / (AmE), / niːm triː / (BrE)
A large, semi-evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent, known for its medicinal bark, aromatic oil-producing seeds, and gum-producing trunk.

Examples

Many farmers plant neem trees along their fields to repel insects naturally.
Oil extracted from the neem tree is used in traditional remedies for skin conditions.
