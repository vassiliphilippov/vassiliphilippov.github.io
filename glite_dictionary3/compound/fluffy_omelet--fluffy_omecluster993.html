fluffy omelet

noun
light, airy omelet beaten with separate whites
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌfi ˈɑːmlət / (AmE), / ˈflʌfi ˈɒmlət / (BrE)
An omelet made extra light and airy by beating the egg whites separately before folding them into the yolks.

Examples

a fluffy omelet with cheese
At the café, their signature dish is a fluffy omelet with cheese and spinach.
He ordered a fluffy omelet filled with fresh herbs for breakfast.
The secret to a perfect fluffy omelet is beating the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
