monkey trap

noun
device used to catch monkeys
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki træp / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki træp / (BrE)
a device designed to catch monkeys, often using bait to entice the animal into a mechanism from which it cannot escape

Examples

set a monkey trap
He nervously checked the monkey trap each morning, hoping to find his missing pet.
Some conservationists oppose the use of the traditional monkey trap because it can harm the animals.
The locals taught us how to construct a simple monkey trap in the forest.
