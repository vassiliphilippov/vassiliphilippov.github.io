irresistible impulse

noun
uncontrollable urge to act or speak
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɪzɪˈstɪbəl ˈɪmpʌls / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪzɪˈstɪbəl ˈɪmpʊls / (BrE)
a strong urge to do or say something that cannot be controlled, even when it may be unwise

Examples

an irresistible impulse to act
Despite his best efforts, he gave in to the irresistible impulse to check his phone again.
She felt an irresistible impulse to laugh during the serious meeting.
Sometimes an irresistible impulse can lead people to say things they later regret.

Related Terms

  • synonym – compulsion: an irresistible urge to behave in a certain way, especially against one's conscious wishes
