crow pheasant

noun
large South Asian cuckoo species
Pronunciation: / kroʊ ˈfɛzənt / (AmE), / krəʊ ˈfɛzənt / (BrE)
a large, ground-dwelling cuckoo (Centropus sinensis) of India and China, with glossy black plumage and a long tail
