security measures

noun
precautions against theft or espionage
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrəti ˈmɛʒərz / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərɪti ˈmɛʒəz / (BrE)
actions or devices put in place to prevent unauthorized access, theft, espionage, or sabotage.

Examples

install security measures
New security measures were installed to protect sensitive government data.
Strict security measures are enforced at the airport for all passengers.
The company upgraded its security measures after a break-in last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – countermeasures: an action taken to counteract a danger or threat
  • synonym – precautions: actions taken in advance to prevent harm or danger
