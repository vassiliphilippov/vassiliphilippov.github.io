Meibomian gland

noun
sebaceous gland in the eyelids
Pronunciation: / maɪˈboʊmiən ɡlænd / (AmE), / maɪˈbəʊmiən ɡlænd / (BrE)
a long sebaceous gland located in the eyelids, responsible for secreting oils that lubricate the surface of the eye
