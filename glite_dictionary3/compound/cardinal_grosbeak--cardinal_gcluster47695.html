cardinal grosbeak

noun
North American finch with red male plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdɪnəl ˈɡroʊsˌbik / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪnəl ˈɡrəʊsbɪk / (BrE)
A crested, thick-billed North American finch, especially the male, which has bright red plumage; also known as the northern cardinal.
