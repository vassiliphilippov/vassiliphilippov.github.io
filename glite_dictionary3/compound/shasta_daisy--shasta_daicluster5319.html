Shasta daisy

noun
hybrid garden daisy with large white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæstə ˌdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːstə ˌdeɪzi / (BrE)
A hybrid garden plant with large white flower heads, resembling the oxeye daisy, often grown ornamentally.
