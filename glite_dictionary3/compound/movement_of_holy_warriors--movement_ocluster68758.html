Movement of Holy Warriors

noun
Islamic militant group in Pakistan and Kashmir
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːvmənt əv ˈhoʊli ˈwɔːriərz / (AmE), / ˈmuːvmənt əv ˈhəʊli ˈwɔːriəz / (BrE)
an Islamic militant group founded in Pakistan, originally fighting the Soviet Union in Afghanistan in the 1980s, now operating mainly in Kashmir as a terrorist organization seeking Kashmir's accession to Pakistan
