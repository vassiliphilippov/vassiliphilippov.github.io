multiple correlation coefficient

noun
statistical measure of combined variable effects
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltəpəl kəˌrɛləˈʃən koʊˈɪfɪʃənt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪpl kəˌrɛləˈʃən kəʊˈɪfɪʃənt / (BrE)
a statistical measure estimating the combined effect of two or more independent variables on a dependent variable
