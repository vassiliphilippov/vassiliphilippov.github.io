charge per unit

noun
cost for each individual unit
Pronunciation: / tʃɑrdʒ pɚ ˈjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / tʃɑːdʒ pə ˈjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
the cost or fee charged for each individual item, measure, or unit of use or consumption

Examples

charge per unit of electricity
