Holy Land

proper noun
region sacred to Christians, Jews, and Muslims
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli lænd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli lænd / (BrE)
the region in the eastern Mediterranean, particularly present-day Israel and Palestine, regarded as sacred by Christians, Jews, and Muslims

Examples

pilgrimage to the Holy Land
Every year, thousands of pilgrims travel to the Holy Land to visit sacred sites.
She always dreamed of taking a journey to the Holy Land to explore its ancient cities.
The Holy Land is significant in Judaism, Christianity, and Islam for its historical and religious importance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Israel: modern country much of which lies within the traditional Holy Land
  • synonym – Palestine: historical/geographical region in the Middle East that largely overlaps with the Holy Land
