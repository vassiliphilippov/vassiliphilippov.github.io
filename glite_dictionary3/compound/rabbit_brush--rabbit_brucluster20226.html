rabbit brush

noun
shrubby plant of western North America
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪt ˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
a pleasantly aromatic North American shrub with slender, hairy branches and clusters of small yellow flowers, found on western plains; used as a source of yellow dye
