tallow oil

noun
oil pressed from tallow, used industrially
Pronunciation: / ˈtæloʊ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈtæləʊ ɔɪl / (BrE)
an animal oil obtained by pressing tallow, used as a lubricant or in industrial applications
