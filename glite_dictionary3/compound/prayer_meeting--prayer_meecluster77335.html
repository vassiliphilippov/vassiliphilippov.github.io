prayer meeting

noun
religious meeting for prayer and hymns
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr ˌmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpreə ˌmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a meeting, usually held in a church or private home, at which people gather to pray and sing hymns together

Examples

evening prayer meeting
She found comfort in joining the evening prayer meeting with her neighbors.
The prayer meeting began with everyone singing a well-known hymn.
We attended the weekly prayer meeting at the community church.
