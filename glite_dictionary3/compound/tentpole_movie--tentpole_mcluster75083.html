tentpole movie

noun
major, highly profitable film for a studio
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛntˌpoʊl ˈmuːvi / (AmE), / ˈtɛntpəʊl ˈmuːvi / (BrE)
A major film produced by a studio that is expected to generate substantial revenue and support the funding of other, less profitable projects.

Examples

blockbuster tentpole movie
Superhero franchises are often considered the quintessential tentpole movies of modern cinema.
The studio invested heavily in its new tentpole movie hoping for a record-breaking release.
This summer's tentpole movie is expected to boost the company's profits significantly.
