cross-disciplinary research

noun
research involving multiple academic disciplines
Pronunciation: / krɔs ˌdɪsəˈplɪnɛri rɪˈsɜrtʃ / (AmE), / krɒs ˌdɪsəˈplɪnəri rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
research that combines or integrates methods, knowledge, or approaches from two or more academic disciplines.

Examples

cross-disciplinary research project
Funding agencies often favor proposals that demonstrate cross-disciplinary research and collaboration.
Her latest project is an example of cross-disciplinary research in education and computer science.
Universities are increasingly promoting cross-disciplinary research to address complex global issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interdisciplinary research: research involving two or more disciplines, often integrating them
  • synonym – multidisciplinary research: research involving several disciplines, but not necessarily integrating them
