breakfast nook

noun
small eating area near a kitchen
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛkfəst nʊk / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst nʊk / (BrE)
a small space or alcove, often near the kitchen, set aside for eating light meals such as breakfast

Examples

cozy breakfast nook
The new house has a charming breakfast nook with built-in benches and cushions.
They enjoyed their morning coffee together in the breakfast nook overlooking the garden.
We usually eat quick weekday meals at the breakfast nook instead of the formal dining room.
