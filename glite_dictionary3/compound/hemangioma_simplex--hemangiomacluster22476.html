hemangioma simplex

noun
soft red birthmark
Pronunciation: / hɪˌmændʒiˈoʊmə ˈsɪmplɛks / (AmE), / hɛˌmandʒɪˈəʊmə ˈsɪmplɛks / (BrE)
A soft, red, benign birthmark caused by a cluster of blood vessels near the skin's surface.
