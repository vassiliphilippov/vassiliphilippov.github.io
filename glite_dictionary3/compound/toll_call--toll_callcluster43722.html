toll call

noun
a long-distance call with higher charges
Pronunciation: / toʊl kɔl / (AmE), / təʊl kɔːl / (BrE)
a telephone call, especially a long-distance one, for which the cost is higher than the standard local rate

Examples

make a toll call
In the past, making a toll call to another city could be quite expensive.
She accidentally made a toll call and saw it on her phone bill.
The company restricted employees from making toll calls during business hours.

Related Terms

  • antonym – local call: a telephone call charged at the local rate
  • synonym – long-distance call: a telephone call made outside the local area, usually at a higher rate
