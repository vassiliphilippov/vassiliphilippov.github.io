C major scale

noun
major scale with no sharps or flats
Pronunciation: / siː ˈmeɪ.dʒɚ skeɪl / (AmE), / siː ˈmeɪ.dʒə skeɪl / (BrE)
the major scale in music that consists of the notes C, D, E, F, G, A, and B, and has no sharps or flats
