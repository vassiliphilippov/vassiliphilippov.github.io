chicory plant

noun
plant grown for edible roots and leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkəri plænt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkəri plɑːnt / (BrE)
A perennial plant with bright blue flowers, cultivated for its edible roots and leaves used in salads.
