eider duck

noun
a sea duck valued for its soft down
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdɚ dʌk / (AmE), / ˈaɪdə dʌk / (BrE)
a large sea duck of the Northern Hemisphere, prized for the fine soft down of the female, used as a filling for quilts and pillows.

Examples

eider duck feathers
During migration, flocks of eider ducks can be seen along the northern coasts.
She learned that the finest duvets are filled with eider duck down.
The eider duck is known for supplying the luxurious down used in high-quality bedding.
