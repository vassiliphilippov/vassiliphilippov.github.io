bus lane

noun
traffic lane reserved for buses
Pronunciation: / bʌs leɪn / (AmE), / bʌs leɪn / (BrE)
a section of a road reserved for buses to use, usually to give them priority over other traffic

Examples

drive in the bus lane
All vehicles except buses are prohibited from using the bus lane during rush hour.
Drivers caught driving in the bus lane face a hefty fine.
The city installed a dedicated bus lane to help public transport move more efficiently.
