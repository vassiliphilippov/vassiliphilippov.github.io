broadcast journalist

noun
journalist working in radio or television
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌkæst ˈdʒɜrnəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːst ˈdʒɜːnəlɪst / (BrE)
