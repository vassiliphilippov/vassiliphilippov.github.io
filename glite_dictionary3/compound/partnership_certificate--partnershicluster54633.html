partnership certificate

noun
document listing interests in a partnership
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtnərʃɪp sərˈtɪfɪkət / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtnəʃɪp səˈtɪfɪkət / (BrE)
an official document that indicates the interests or ownership shares of all parties in a business partnership

Examples

partnership certificate shows ownership
Each member received a copy of the partnership certificate outlining their rights and responsibilities.
The law firm required a partnership certificate to confirm each partner's share in the business.
When applying for a loan, the bank asked for the company's partnership certificate.
