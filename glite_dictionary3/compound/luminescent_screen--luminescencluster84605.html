luminescent screen

noun
screen in CRT that lights up from electron beam
Pronunciation: / luːmɪˈnɛsənt skrin / (AmE), / luːmɪˈnɛs(ə)nt skriːn / (BrE)
the surface in a cathode-ray tube that emits light when struck by an electron beam

Examples

luminescent screen in a monitor
Technicians examined the luminescent screen for defects after prolonged use in the laboratory.
The luminescent screen displays images by converting electron beams into visible light.
