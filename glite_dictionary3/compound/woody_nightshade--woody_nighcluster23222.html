woody nightshade

noun
poisonous perennial vine with violet flowers and red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdi ˈnaɪtˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈwʊdi ˈnaɪtʃeɪd / (BrE)
A poisonous perennial climbing plant of the Old World, with violet flowers and oval coral-red berries, also widespread as a weed in North America; also called bittersweet or Solanum dulcamara.
