hard knocks

noun
difficult or painful experiences
Pronunciation: / hɑrd nɑks / (AmE), / hɑːd nɒks / (BrE)
Difficult or painful experiences, often involving adversity or misfortune, especially as a means of learning.

Examples

school of hard knocks
After years of hard knocks, she finally achieved her dream of owning a business.
He learned valuable lessons from the hard knocks he faced growing up in poverty.
Many successful people credit their success to the hard knocks they experienced early in life.
