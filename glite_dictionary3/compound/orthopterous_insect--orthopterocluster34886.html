orthopterous insect

noun
insect of the Orthoptera order
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθɑptərəs ˈɪnsɛkt / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒptərəs ˈɪnsɛkt / (BrE)
an insect of the order Orthoptera, typically having leathery forewings, membranous hind wings, and chewing mouthparts, such as a grasshopper or cricket
