west side

noun
side facing toward the west
Pronunciation: / wɛst saɪd / (AmE), / wɛst sʌɪd / (BrE)
the side of something that faces or is located toward the west

Examples

the west side of town
She grew up on the west side of the city, where the old parks are located.
The west side of the mountain receives less rainfall than the east.
We could see the sunset from the west side of the building.
