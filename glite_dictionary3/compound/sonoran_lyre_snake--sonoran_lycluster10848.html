Sonoran lyre snake

noun
species of desert snake native to southwestern North America
Pronunciation: / səˈnɔrən ˈlaɪər sneɪk / (AmE), / səˈnɔːrən ˈlaɪə sneɪk / (BrE)
A species of snake (Trimorphodon lambda) native to desert regions of southwestern North America, known for its lyre-shaped head markings.
