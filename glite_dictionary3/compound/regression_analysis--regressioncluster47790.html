regression analysis

noun
statistical method for predicting variable relationships
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡrɛʃən əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈɡrɛʃən əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
a statistical method for estimating the relationships among variables, often used to predict one variable based on the values of others

Examples

regression analysis of data
Economists rely on regression analysis to understand how different factors affect income levels.
Her research paper included a detailed regression analysis of the survey results.
The company used regression analysis to forecast future sales based on previous years' data.

Related Terms

  • synonym – correlation: the relationship between two variables, especially with respect to how one may predict the other
