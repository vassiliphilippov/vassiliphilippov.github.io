Arab-Berbers

noun
North African ethnic group of Berber and Arab descent
Pronunciation: / ˈærəb ˈbɜrbərz / (AmE), / ˈærəb ˈbɜːbəz / (BrE)
A North African ethnic group descended from both Berbers and Arabs.
