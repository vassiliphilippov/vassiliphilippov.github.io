London plane

noun
a large urban street tree
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌn.dən pleɪn / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.dən pleɪn / (BrE)
a very large, fast-growing tree (Platanus × acerifolia), widely planted as a street tree in cities for its tolerance of pollution and urban conditions

After the storm, several old London plane branches had to be cleared from the road.
