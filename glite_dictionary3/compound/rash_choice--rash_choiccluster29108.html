rash choice

noun
an impulsive or hasty decision
Pronunciation: / ræʃ tʃɔɪs / (AmE), / ræʃ tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
a decision made quickly without careful thought or consideration of the consequences

Examples

make a rash choice
His parents warned him against making another rash choice regarding his future.
Making a rash choice in a moment of anger can have lasting consequences.
She regretted her rash choice to quit her job without another position lined up.

Related Terms

  • synonym – impulsive decision: a decision made without careful thought
