lace curtain

noun
thin decorative lace curtain for privacy
Pronunciation: / leɪs ˈkɝːtn̩ / (AmE), / leɪs ˈkɜːtn̩ / (BrE)
A thin, often decorative curtain made of lace, designed to let in light while providing privacy by obscuring the view from outside.

Examples

lace curtain window
She chose a white lace curtain to add a touch of elegance to the kitchen.
Sunlight filtered softly through the delicate lace curtain, casting intricate patterns on the floor.
The old house still had a lace curtain hanging in the living room window.
