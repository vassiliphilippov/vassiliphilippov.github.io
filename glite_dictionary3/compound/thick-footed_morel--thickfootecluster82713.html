thick-footed morel

noun
edible morel mushroom with a thick stem
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪk ˌfʊtɪd məˈrɛl / (AmE), / ˈθɪk ˌfʊtɪd mɒˈrɛl / (BrE)
A type of edible morel mushroom distinguished by its thick stem and conical cap with deep, irregular pits.
