Education Secretary

noun
government minister for education
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri /; / ˌɛʤəˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃən ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
the government official in charge of national education policy and administration (such as a minister or head of the Education Department)
