oast house

noun
building for drying hops
Pronunciation: / oʊst haʊs / (AmE), / əʊst haʊs / (BrE)
a building, typically with a conical or pyramidal roof, used for drying hops as part of the brewing process

Examples

old oast house
Many oast houses have distinctive conical roofs and white cowls.
The old oast house in Kent has been converted into a family home.
