hooded sheldrake

noun
a small North American duck with a large crest
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊdəd ˈʃɛlˌdreɪk / (AmE), / ˈhʊdɪd ˈʃɛldrɛɪk / (BrE)
a small North American duck (Lophodytes cucullatus), the male distinguished by a large, fan-shaped crest on its head
