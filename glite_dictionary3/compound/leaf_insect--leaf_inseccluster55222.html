leaf insect

noun
tropical insect resembling a leaf
Pronunciation: / lif ˈɪn.sɛkt / (AmE), / liːf ˈɪn.sɪkt / (BrE)
A tropical insect of southern Asia and the East Indies, known for its flattened, leaf-like body that serves as camouflage.
