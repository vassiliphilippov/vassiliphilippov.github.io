pipe organ

noun
large keyboard musical instrument with pipes
Pronunciation: / paɪp ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / paɪp ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
a large musical instrument in which sound is produced by air passed through pipes, played using one or more keyboards

Examples

church pipe organ
He has played the pipe organ since he was a teenager, mastering its complex stops and pedals.
Restoration work on the historic pipe organ took several years to complete.
The cathedral’s magnificent pipe organ filled the hall with rich, resonant music.
