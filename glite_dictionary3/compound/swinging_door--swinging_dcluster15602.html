swinging door

noun
door that opens in both directions
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋɪŋ dɔɚ / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɪŋ dɔː / (BrE)
a door mounted on double hinges that allows it to open in both directions

Examples

kitchen swinging door

Waiters often prefer a swinging door for convenience when carrying trays.
