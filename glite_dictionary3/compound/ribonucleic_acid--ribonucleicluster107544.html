ribonucleic acid

noun
nucleic acid molecule in cells
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪboʊnjuːˌkleɪɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˌraɪbəʊnjuːˌkliːɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
