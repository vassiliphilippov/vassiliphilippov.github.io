flannel leaf

noun
plant with woolly leaves in genus Verbascum
Pronunciation: / ˈflænəl liːf / (AmE), / ˈflænəl liːf / (BrE)
Any of various plants of the genus Verbascum, characterized by large, usually woolly leaves and terminal spikes of yellow, white, or purple flowers.

Gardeners admire the soft, woolly texture of the flannel leaf for its ornamental appeal.

The flannel leaf flourished in the sunny border of her garden, its yellow flowers glowing in the afternoon light.
