Battle of the Alamo

noun
1836 siege of the Alamo mission in Texas
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l̩ əv ði əˈlæmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ðiː əˈlæməʊ / (BrE)
A significant 1836 siege during the Texas Revolution, in which Mexican forces captured the Alamo mission in San Antonio and killed the defenders.
