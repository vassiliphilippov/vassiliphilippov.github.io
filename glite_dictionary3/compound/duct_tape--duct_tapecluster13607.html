duct tape

noun
strong adhesive tape with many uses
Pronunciation: / dʌkt teɪp / (AmE), / dʌkt teɪp / (BrE)
a strong, wide, silver-gray adhesive tape originally designed to seal joints in metal ductwork but widely used for various repairs

Examples

duct tape a broken pipe
A wallet made from duct tape has become a popular DIY project.
He used duct tape to quickly fix the broken pipe under the sink.
We always keep a roll of duct tape in the car in case of emergencies.
