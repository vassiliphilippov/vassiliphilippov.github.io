critical mass

noun
minimum amount needed for success
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪdɪkəl mæs /; / ˌkrɪdɪkəl ˈmæs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəl mæs /; / ˌkrɪtɪkəl ˈmæs / (BrE)
the minimum size, amount, or level needed to start or sustain a process, venture, or effect
