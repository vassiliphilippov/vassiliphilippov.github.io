electrical disturbance

noun
unwanted signals interfering electrically
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktrɪkəl dɪsˈtɝbəns / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrɪkəl dɪsˈtɜːb(ə)ns / (BrE)
unwanted electrical signals or noise, often produced by external sources, that interfere with the normal operation of electrical or electronic devices

Examples
