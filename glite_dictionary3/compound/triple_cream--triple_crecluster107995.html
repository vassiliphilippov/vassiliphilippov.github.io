triple cream

noun
very rich moisturizer
Pronunciation: / tr6ap59l krim / (AmE), / tr6ap59l krid0m / (BrE)
A very rich moisturizing cream or cosmetic product marketed as a 'triple-cream' formulation, intended to provide intense hydration.

Examples

triple cream moisturizer
After the long flight, she applied a thick triple cream to soothe her dry, tired skin.
The brand launched several scented triple creams promising intense hydration for winter months.
Dermatologists sometimes advise avoiding heavy triple cream products on acne-prone skin to prevent breakouts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – emollient: a substance that softens and soothes the skin; often a main ingredient in rich creams
  • synonym – moisturizer: a cosmetic cream or lotion used to make the skin less dry
