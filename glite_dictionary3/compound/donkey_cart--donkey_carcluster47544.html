donkey cart

noun
simple two-seat cart pulled by a donkey
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑŋki kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈdɒŋki kɑːt / (BrE)
A simple cart pulled by a donkey, typically with an underslung axle and seating for two.

Examples

ride a donkey cart
Farmers often use a donkey cart to transport goods to the market.
The children enjoyed riding in the old donkey cart through the village.
Tourists sometimes take a donkey cart tour to explore rural areas.
