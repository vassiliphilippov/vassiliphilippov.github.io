temple tree

noun
tropical tree grown in temple gardens
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpəl triː / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpl̩ triː / (BrE)
A tropical tree of India, especially the frangipani, Plumeria alba, with an upright, conical growth, commonly grown in temple gardens.

Examples

temple tree flowers
A mature temple tree can provide both shade and a pleasant aroma near shrines.
Many temple gardens feature the fragrant temple tree for its beautiful white blossoms.
The temple tree is often associated with sacred and cultural rituals in India.
