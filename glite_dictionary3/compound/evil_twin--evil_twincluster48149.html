evil twin

noun
a person with similar appearance but bad traits
Pronunciation: / ˈiːvəl twɪn / (AmE), / ˈiːvəl twɪn / (BrE)
a person who closely resembles another but is considered to have opposite or bad traits, or a humorous reference to one's negative side or behavior
