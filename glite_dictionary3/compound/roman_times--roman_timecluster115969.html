Roman times

noun
historical period of ancient Rome's dominance
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.mən taɪmz / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mən taɪmz / (BrE)
the period in history when ancient Rome dominated Europe, typically from the founding of Rome (traditionally 753 BC) to the fall of the Western Roman Empire (AD 476)
