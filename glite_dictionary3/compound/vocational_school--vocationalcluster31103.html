vocational school

noun
school for trade or occupational training
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃənəl skuːl / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəl skuːl / (BrE)
A secondary or post-secondary school where students learn the skills needed for a particular trade or occupation.

Examples

vocational school for mechanics
After graduating from vocational school, she found a job as a mechanic.
He enrolled in a vocational school to become an electrician.
Many students choose a vocational school instead of a traditional high school.

  • synonym – trade school: a school offering instruction in skilled trades
