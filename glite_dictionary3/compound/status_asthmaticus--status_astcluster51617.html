status asthmaticus

noun
severe, unresponsive asthma attack
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtəs æsθˈmætɪkəs / (AmE), / ˈsta.təs asˈma.tɪ.kəs / (BrE)
A prolonged and severe asthma attack that does not respond to standard treatments and may be life-threatening.
