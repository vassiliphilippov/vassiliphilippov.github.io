posterior serratus muscle

noun
back muscle that lowers rib cage
Pronunciation: / pəˈstɪriɚ səˈreɪtəs ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / pɒˈstɪəri.əs səˈreɪtəs ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
a muscle of the back that pulls the rib cage backward and downward
