wild winterpea

noun
Mediterranean winter annual plant used as cover crop
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈwɪntər ˌpiː / (AmE), / waɪld ˈwɪntə ˌpiː / (BrE)
A weak-stemmed winter annual plant native to the Mediterranean, long established in the southern United States and cultivated as a cover and pasture crop.
