death mask

noun
cast or impression of a dead person's face
Pronunciation: / dɛθ mæsk / (AmE), / dɛθ mɑːsk / (BrE)
a cast or impression made of a deceased person's face, usually after death, used as a memento or for artistic purposes

Examples

death mask of a famous person
Artists often use a death mask as a reference for creating portraits of historical figures.
He was fascinated by the detail preserved in Napoleon's death mask.
The museum displayed the death mask of the famous composer in a glass case.
