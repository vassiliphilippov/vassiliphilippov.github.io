conservation of electricity

noun
principle that electric charge remains constant
Pronunciation: / kənˌsɜːrˈveɪʃən əv ɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsəti / (AmE), / kənˌsɜːvˈeɪʃən əv ɪˌlɛkˈtrɪsəti / (BrE)
the principle that the total electric charge in a closed system remains constant, regardless of changes within the system
