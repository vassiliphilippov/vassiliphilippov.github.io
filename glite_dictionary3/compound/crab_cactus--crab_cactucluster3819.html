crab cactus

noun
South American houseplant cactus with red flowers
Pronunciation: / kræb ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / krab ˈkaktəs / (BrE)
A South American cactus with jointed stems, often grown as a houseplant for its typically red flowers; sometimes classified as genus Schlumbergera.
