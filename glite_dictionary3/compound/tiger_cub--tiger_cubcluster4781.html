tiger cub

noun
hedge fund started by ex-Tiger Management staff
Pronunciation: / \u02c8ta\u026a\u0261\u025a k\u028cb / (AmE), / \u02c8ta\u026a\u0261\u0259 k\u028cb / (BrE)
(finance) a hedge fund founded by a former employee of the hedge fund Tiger Management; often one of the funds collectively known as the 'Tiger Cubs'.
