knuckle sandwich

noun
punch in the face (usually joking)
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəl ˈsænwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəl ˈsænwɪtʃ / (BrE)
a punch in the mouth, especially said as a threat or in jest

Examples

give a knuckle sandwich

She threatened a knuckle sandwich to anyone who spoiled the movie.
