personal appeal

noun
attractiveness or charm to influence others
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənl əˈpil / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl əˈpiːl / (BrE)
a quality of attractiveness or charm that enables a person to influence or attract others

Examples

strong personal appeal
Despite their differences, his personal appeal brought the team together.
Her personal appeal made her an instant favorite among the new group of friends.
The politician relied on his personal appeal rather than on policies to win support.
