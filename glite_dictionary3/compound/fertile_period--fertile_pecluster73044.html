fertile period

noun
time in menstrual cycle when conception is likely
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːt̬əl ˈpɪriəd / (AmE), / ˈfɜːtaɪl ˈpɪəriəd / (BrE)
the phase in the menstrual cycle during which fertilization is most likely to occur, typically around ovulation.

Examples

fertile period in cycle
Apps can help track your fertile period by analyzing your menstrual cycle.
Couples trying to conceive often monitor the fertile period to improve their chances.
During her fertile period, a woman's body is most receptive to pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – infertile period: period in the menstrual cycle when conception is unlikely
  • synonym – ovulation: release of an egg during the menstrual cycle
