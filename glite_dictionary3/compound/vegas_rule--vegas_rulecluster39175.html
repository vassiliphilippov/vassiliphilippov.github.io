Vegas rule

noun
principle of keeping events secret
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪɡəs ruːl / (AmE), / ˈveɪɡəs ruːl / (BrE)
an informal principle stating that things that happen in a particular place or situation should not be shared outside it, often phrased as 'what happens in Vegas, stays in Vegas'

Examples

Vegas rule at parties
She joked that the Vegas rule applies whenever her friends go on vacation together.
They all agreed to follow the Vegas rule and never mention the party again.
Under the Vegas rule, their embarrassing karaoke performances were just between them.
