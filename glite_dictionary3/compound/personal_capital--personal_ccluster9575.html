personal capital

noun
individual assets or resources for success
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
an individual's assets, resources, or qualities that contribute to their potential success, including skills, education, reputation, and personal networks

Examples

grow personal capital
Building personal capital through volunteering can open doors to new career opportunities.
Effective leaders rely on their personal capital to inspire and motivate their teams.
She invested in her personal capital by acquiring new certifications and expanding her professional network.
