sweet pepper

noun
mild, bell-shaped or oblong pepper
Pronunciation: / swit ˈpɛpɚ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
a large, mild, crisp, thick-walled pepper, usually bell-shaped or oblong, commonly eaten raw or cooked

Examples

chopped sweet pepper

Roasted sweet pepper makes a delicious addition to sandwiches and pasta dishes.
