mass murder

noun
the killing of many people at once
Pronunciation: / mæs ˈmɜrdɚ / (AmE), / mæs ˈmɜːdə / (BrE)
the deliberate and brutal killing of a large number of people at the same time or over a short period

Examples

mass murder case
Documentaries often explore the psychological motives behind mass murder cases.
The police described the event as a mass murder that shocked the entire community.
