cover boy

noun
male model featured on a magazine cover
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə bɔɪ / (BrE)
A young man or boy whose image appears on the front cover of a magazine, especially as a model representing attractiveness or fashion.
