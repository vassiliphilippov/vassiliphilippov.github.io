woman's hat

noun
hat designed for women
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənz hæt / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənz hæt / (BrE)
a hat designed and made specifically for women, often following fashion trends and styles distinct from men's hats

Examples

elegant woman's hat
Historically, the woman's hat was an important statement of social status and personal taste.
She wore an elegant woman's hat to the garden party, decorated with silk flowers.
The shop window displayed a beautiful collection of women's hats for spring.
