sick list

noun
list of people who are ill
Pronunciation: / sɪk lɪst / (AmE), / sɪk lɪst / (BrE)
a list of people who are absent from work, duty, or other responsibilities because of illness, especially within an organization

Examples

sick list of employees
As of this morning, only two names remain on the regiment's sick list.
Several employees were added to the sick list during the outbreak of influenza.
The captain checked the sick list to see which sailors were unable to report for duty.
After catching the flu, Mark was added to the sick list at his office.
Each morning, the supervisor updates the sick list to keep track of staff absences.
The team was missing two players who were on the sick list this week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – absentee list: list of people absent from a place or duty
  • synonym – roster: a list of people belonging to a group, such as members of a team or employees
