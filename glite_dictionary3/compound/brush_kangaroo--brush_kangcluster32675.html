brush kangaroo

noun
a small or medium brightly colored kangaroo
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ kæŋˈɡæɹoʊ / (AmE), / brʌʃ kæŋˈɡaruː / (BrE)
any of various small or medium-sized kangaroos, often brightly colored, found in brush and forested areas of Australia.

Examples

wild brush kangaroo

The brush kangaroo is well adapted to life in dense forests and undergrowth.
Unlike larger species, the brush kangaroo spends more time in trees and low bushes.
