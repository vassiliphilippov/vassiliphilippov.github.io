lumbar vein

noun
vein in lower back region
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌm.bɑr veɪn / (AmE), / ˈlʌm.bɑː veɪn / (BrE)
any of several veins that drain the posterior body wall and the lumbar vertebral venous plexuses in the lower back area

Examples

lumbar vein blockage
An obstruction of the lumbar vein can cause swelling and discomfort in the lower back.
During the surgery, the surgeon carefully avoided injuring the lumbar vein.
The lumbar vein helps return blood from the lower back to the main circulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lumbar artery: artery supplying blood to the lumbar region
  • synonym – vein: blood vessel returning blood to the heart
