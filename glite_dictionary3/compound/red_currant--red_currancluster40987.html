red currant

noun
a small tart red berry used in cooking
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈkɜr.ənt /; / rɛd ˈkɜrənt / (AmE), / rɛd ˈkʌr.ənt /; / rɛd ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
a small, sour, bright red berry or the shrub that produces it, commonly used in jams, jellies, and desserts
