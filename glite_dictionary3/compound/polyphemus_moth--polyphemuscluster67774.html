polyphemus moth

noun
large North American silkworm moth with eyespots
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪˈfiːməs mɑθ / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈfiːməs mɒθ / (BrE)
a very large yellowish-brown North American silkworm moth (Antheraea polyphemus) with large eyespots on its hind wings; its larvae feed on fruit and shade trees
