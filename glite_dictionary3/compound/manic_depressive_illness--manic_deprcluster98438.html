manic depressive illness

noun
mental disorder with mania and depression
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv ˈɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈmænɪk dɪˈprɛsɪv ˈɪlnɪs / (BrE)
A mental disorder characterized by alternating episodes of mania and depression; now more commonly known as bipolar disorder.

Examples

manic depressive illness diagnosis
He was diagnosed with manic depressive illness in his early twenties.
Her family supported her through several episodes of manic depressive illness.
The doctor explained that manic depressive illness requires careful management and medication.
