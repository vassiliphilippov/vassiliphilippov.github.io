myeloblastic leukemia

noun
cancer with excess myeloblasts in blood
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.ə.loʊˌblæstɪk luˈkiː.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.ə.ləˌblæstɪk luːˈkiː.mi.ə / (BrE)
