fluosilicic acid

noun
a poisonous, corrosive acid used in industry
Pronunciation: / ˌfluː.oʊ.sɪˈlɪs.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd / (AmE), / ˌfluː.əʊ.sɪˈlɪs.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd / (BrE)
