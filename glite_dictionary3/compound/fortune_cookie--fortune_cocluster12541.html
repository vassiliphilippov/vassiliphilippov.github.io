fortune cookie

noun
crisp biscuit with a message inside
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrtʃən ˌkʊki / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtʃuːn ˌkʊki / (BrE)
a thin, crisp, folded biscuit containing a piece of paper with a proverb, prediction, or saying inside, typically served as a dessert in Chinese restaurants

Examples

open a fortune cookie
Every meal at the restaurant ended with a fortune cookie and an amusing message.
She broke open her fortune cookie to read, 'You will have a pleasant surprise soon.'
The children laughed as they compared the messages from their fortune cookies.
