continental breakfast

noun
light breakfast of bread, pastries, and hot drinks
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.t̬ənˈen.t̬əl ˈbrɛk.fəst / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪˈnɛn.tl ˈbrɛk.fəst / (BrE)
A light breakfast that typically consists of bread, pastries, butter, jam, and hot drinks such as coffee or tea, usually served in hotels and restaurants.

Examples

continental breakfast with croissants
Most European hotels serve a simple continental breakfast instead of a full English one.
