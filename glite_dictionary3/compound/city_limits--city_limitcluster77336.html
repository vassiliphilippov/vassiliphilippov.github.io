city limits

noun
boundaries of a city or town
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti ˌlɪmɪts / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˌlɪmɪts / (BrE)
the boundaries that mark the outer edge of a city or town, separating it from surrounding areas

Examples

outside the city limits
He lives just outside the city limits, on a quiet country road.
The speed limit changes once you cross the city limits.
They announced plans to expand the city limits to include more neighborhoods.
