southern red cedar

noun
a juniper tree found in southeastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðɚn rɛd ˈsiːdɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən rɛd ˈsiːdə / (BrE)
A type of juniper tree (Juniperus silicicola) native to swampy coastal regions of the southeastern United States, similar in appearance to the eastern red cedar.
