genus Haliaeetus

noun
genus of sea eagles (birds of prey)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌhæliˈiːtəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌhæliˈiːtəs / (BrE)
