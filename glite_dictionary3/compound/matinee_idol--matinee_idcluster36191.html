matinee idol

noun
handsome, popular male film or theater star
Pronunciation: / ˌmætɪˈneɪ ˈaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌmatɪˈneɪ ˈaɪdəl / (BrE)
a popular male actor, especially in films or theater, who is admired for his good looks and charm, often attracting large numbers of devoted fans.

Examples

famous matinee idol
In the 1940s, he was a true matinee idol with fans lined up outside every theater.
Many classic Hollywood films starred a handsome matinee idol as the leading man.
The young actor became a matinee idol almost overnight after his breakout role.
