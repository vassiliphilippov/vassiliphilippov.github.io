cup of sugar

noun
unit of sugar measurement (one cup)
Pronunciation: / kʌp əv ˈʃʌɡɚ / (AmE), / kʌp əv ˈʃʌɡə / (BrE)
a standard unit of sugar measurement in cooking, equal to the volume of one cup

Examples

a cup of sugar for baking
