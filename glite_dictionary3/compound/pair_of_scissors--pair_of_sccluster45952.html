pair of scissors

noun
cutting instrument with two blades
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛr əv ˈsɪsərz / (AmE), / ˈpeə əv ˈsɪzəz / (BrE)
A hand-operated cutting instrument with two crossed blades pivoted together, used for cutting paper, cloth, and other thin materials.
