main course

noun
principal square sail on the mainmast
Pronunciation: / meɪn ˈkɔrs / (AmE), / meɪn ˈkɔːs / (BrE)
(Nautical, historical) The principal square sail set on the mainmast of a sailing ship.

Examples

ship's main course
Sailors worked together to lower the heavy main course before the approaching storm.
The ship unfurled its main course as the wind picked up.
With the main course set, the vessel gained impressive speed across the open sea.
