utopian vision

noun
idealized concept of a perfect future
Pronunciation: / juːˈtoʊpiən ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / juːˈtəʊpiən ˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
an idealized and perfect concept of the future, often viewed as unrealistic or unattainable

Examples

a utopian vision for society
Critics argue that her utopian vision is idealistic and disconnected from reality.
Science fiction often explores society’s longing for a utopian vision that may never exist.
The leader inspired hope by sharing his utopian vision of a peaceful and prosperous world.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dystopian vision: an imagined future with great suffering or injustice
  • synonym – utopia: an imagined place or state of things in which everything is perfect
