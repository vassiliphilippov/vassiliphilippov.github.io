musty air

noun
stale, damp-smelling air
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsti ɛr / (AmE), / ˈmʌsti eə / (BrE)
air that smells stale and damp, often from being in a closed or humid place for a long time.

Examples

stale musty air
The musty air in the old library made her wrinkle her nose.
Ventilating the basement helped get rid of the musty air.
When they opened the cabin after a long winter, the musty air hit them immediately.
