damaged ligament

noun
injured ligament in a joint
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmɪdʒd ˈlɪɡəmənt / (AmE), / ˈdamɪdʒd ˈlɪɡəmənt / (BrE)
a ligament that has been torn, stretched, or otherwise injured, especially in a joint such as the knee or ankle

Surgery was required to repair the damaged ligament in her knee.
