Six Day War

proper noun
brief 1967 war between Israel and Arab states
Pronunciation: / sɪks deɪ wɔr / (AmE), / sɪks deɪ wɔː / (BrE)
a war fought in June 1967 between Israel and neighboring Arab states, lasting six days, in which Israel gained significant territory and emerged as a major regional power

Examples

the Six Day War in 1967
After the Six Day War, various peace initiatives were discussed by regional leaders.
Many historians still debate the causes and consequences of the Six Day War.
The Six Day War dramatically altered the political landscape of the Middle East.
