shouting match

noun
noisy argument with raised voices
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊtɪŋ mætʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊtɪŋ matʃ / (BrE)
a noisy argument or dispute in which people raise their voices and try to outshout each other

Examples

shouting match between coworkers
The meeting quickly turned into a shouting match between the two managers.
They got into a shouting match over whose turn it was to do the dishes.
What began as a discussion escalated to a heated shouting match in front of everyone.
