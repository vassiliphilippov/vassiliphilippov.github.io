flower-cup fern

noun
a North American fern with bipinnate fronds and chestnut stipes
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊər ˌkʌp ˌfɜrn / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə ˌkʌp ˌfɜːn / (BrE)
