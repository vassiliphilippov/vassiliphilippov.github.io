bath plug

noun
stopper for sealing a bath or basin drain
Pronunciation: / bæθ plʌɡ / (AmE), / bɑːθ plʌɡ / (BrE)
A stopper used to seal the drain of a bath, washbasin, or similar water container, preventing water from escaping.

Examples

bath plug seals drain
After washing, please remember to replace the bath plug so no water leaks out.
He reached down to pull out the bath plug and let the water drain away.
The hotel bathroom was missing its bath plug, so I couldn't fill the tub.

  • synonym – stopper: an object used to block an opening
