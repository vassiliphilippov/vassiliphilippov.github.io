amino resin

noun
synthetic resin used as adhesive or coating
Pronunciation: / əˈminoʊ ˈrɛzən / (AmE), / əˈmiːnəʊ ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
A synthetic resin made from amino compounds, used as an adhesive and in coatings for paper and textiles.

Examples

Paper treated with amino resin becomes more resistant to moisture and wear.
The manufacturer chose an amino resin for its strong adhesive properties in the furniture assembly.
