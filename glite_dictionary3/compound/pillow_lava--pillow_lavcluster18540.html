pillow lava

noun
rounded lava masses from underwater eruptions
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪl.oʊ ˈlæ.və / (AmE), / ˈpɪl.əʊ ˈlæ.və / (BrE)
A type of lava that hardens in rounded, pillow-shaped masses, typically formed by underwater volcanic eruptions.

underwater pillow lava
