magic smoke

noun
humorous term for smoke from failed electronics
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪk smoʊk / (AmE), / ˈmædʒɪk sməʊk / (BrE)
a humorous term for the smoke released when an electronic component fails, suggesting that the device functioned because of the smoke inside it
