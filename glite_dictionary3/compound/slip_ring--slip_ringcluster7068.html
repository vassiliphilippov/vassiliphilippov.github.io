slip ring

noun
rotating metal ring for electrical connection
Pronunciation: / slɪp rɪŋ / (AmE), / slɪp rɪŋ / (BrE)
a metal ring mounted on a rotating part of a machine, allowing continuous electrical connection between moving and stationary parts via brushes

Examples

slip ring in a motor
A faulty slip ring can cause intermittent failures in rotating electrical equipment.
Many wind turbines use a slip ring to transfer power from the rotating blades to the stationary base.
The engineer replaced the slip ring to restore proper electrical contact in the alternator.
