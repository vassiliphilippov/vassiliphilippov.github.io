red-tailed hawk

noun
a North American hawk species with a reddish tail
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd teɪld hɑk / (AmE), / ˌrɛd teɪld hɔːk / (BrE)
a large North American hawk with a characteristic reddish-brown tail, dark brown above and pale below, commonly found in open countryside

Examples

soaring red-tailed hawk
A red-tailed hawk soared above the fields, searching for prey.
The distinctive cry of the red-tailed hawk echoed through the canyon.
They spotted a red-tailed hawk perched on a fencepost near the highway.
