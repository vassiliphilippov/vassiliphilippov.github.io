linear regression

noun
statistical method modeling variables with a linear equation
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪniɚ rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɪniə rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (BrE)
a statistical method for modeling the relationship between two or more variables by fitting a linear equation to observed data

Examples

linear regression analysis
In statistics, linear regression is often one of the first models taught to students.
The data scientist used linear regression to analyze the relationship between advertising spend and sales.
They applied linear regression to predict house prices based on various features.
