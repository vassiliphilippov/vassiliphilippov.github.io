baling press

noun
machine for compacting waste into bales
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪlɪŋ prɛs / (AmE), / ˈbeɪlɪŋ prɛs / (BrE)
a machine that compresses waste materials such as cardboard or plastic into compact bales for easier handling, transport, or recycling

Examples

baling press compacts cardboard
The recycling facility installed a new baling press to handle increased volumes of cardboard.
With its powerful hydraulics, the baling press can compress large amounts of material in minutes.
Workers fed the plastic waste into the baling press to prepare it for shipping.

  • synonym – baler: machine that makes bales from waste or crops
