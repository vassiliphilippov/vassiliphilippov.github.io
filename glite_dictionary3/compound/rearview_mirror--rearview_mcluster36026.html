rearview mirror

noun
mirror for seeing behind in a car
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪr.vju ˌmɪr.ɚ /; / r6ar.vju ccm6ar.5a / (AmE), / ˈrɪə.vju ˌmɪr.ər /; / r6a59.vju ccm6ar.59r / (BrE)
