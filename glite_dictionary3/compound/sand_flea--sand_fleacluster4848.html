sand flea

noun
a small jumping beach crustacean
Pronunciation: / sænd fliː / (AmE), / sænd fliː / (BrE)
a small crustacean, especially an amphipod, found on sandy beaches that moves by jumping, resembling a flea

Examples

sand flea jumps on beach
After walking barefoot on the beach, she found several sand fleas clinging to her feet.
Children like to watch sand fleas hop along the shoreline at low tide.
Many birds feed on sand fleas that live in the wet sand near the water.
