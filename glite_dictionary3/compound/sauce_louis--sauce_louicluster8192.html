sauce Louis

noun
creamy salad or seafood sauce
Pronunciation: / sɔs ˈluː.i / (AmE), / sɔːs ˈluː.i / (BrE)
A creamy sauce made from mayonnaise and heavy cream combined with chopped green pepper and green onion, seasoned with chili sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice.

Examples

sauce Louis with shrimp
She prefers shrimp served with sauce Louis rather than cocktail sauce.
The chef prepared a tangy sauce Louis to accompany the fresh seafood platter.
The crab salad was topped with a generous portion of sauce Louis.
