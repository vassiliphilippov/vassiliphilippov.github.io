Strauss the Younger

proper noun
Austrian composer known as the 'waltz king'
Pronunciation: / straʊs ði ˈjʌŋɚ / (AmE), / straʊs ðə ˈjʌŋə / (BrE)
(1825–1899) Austrian composer, the son of Strauss the Elder, celebrated for his many famous waltzes and known as the 'waltz king'.
