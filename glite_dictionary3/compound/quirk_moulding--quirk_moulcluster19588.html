quirk moulding

noun
moulding with a small groove
Pronunciation: / kwɝk ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / kwɜːk ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of moulding with a small groove separating the convex part from the adjoining surface.
