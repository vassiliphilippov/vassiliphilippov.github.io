powder keg

noun
container for gunpowder or blasting powder
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdər kɛg / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə kɛg / (BrE)
A strong container, typically made of metal, used to store gunpowder or blasting powder.

Examples

old powder keg
Accidentally dropping a lantern near the powder keg could have caused a huge explosion.
Each miner carried a small powder keg to the blasting site.
The old fort still had a powder keg stored in its cellar.
