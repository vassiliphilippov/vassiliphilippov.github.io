congo snake

noun
aquatic eel-shaped salamander of the southern US
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡoʊ sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ sneɪk / (BrE)
A species of aquatic, eel-shaped salamander with two pairs of very small feet, found in still muddy waters of the southern United States.
