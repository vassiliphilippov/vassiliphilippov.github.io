unlicked envelope

noun
envelope not sealed by licking
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪkt ˈɛn.vəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪkt ˈɛn.və.ləʊp / (BrE)
an envelope that has not been sealed by licking the adhesive flap

Examples

an unlicked envelope on the table
An unlicked envelope may indicate an unread or unsent letter.
