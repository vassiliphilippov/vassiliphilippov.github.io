pâte à choux

noun
light pastry dough for filled pastries
Pronunciation: / pɑt ɑ ʃu / (AmE), / pɑːt a ʃuː / (BrE)
a light pastry dough used to make hollow cases for pastries such as éclairs and cream puffs, which can be filled with sweet or savory fillings

Examples

pate a choux dough for eclairs
Churros are made from a dough similar to pâte à choux before being fried.
The baker piped pâte à choux onto the baking sheet to make cream puffs.
You can fill pâte à choux with whipped cream or custard for a delicious dessert.
