audio system

noun
electronic sound equipment set
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːdioʊ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəʊ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A set of electronic devices designed for recording, reproducing, or amplifying sound.

audio system for music
She installed a new audio system in her living room to enjoy high-quality music.
The audio system in this car delivers a rich and immersive sound experience.
