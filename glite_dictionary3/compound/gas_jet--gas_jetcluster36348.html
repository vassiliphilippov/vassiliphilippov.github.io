gas jet

noun
device emitting a flame from combustible gas
Pronunciation: / ɡæs dʒɛt / (AmE), / ɡæs dʒɛt / (BrE)
a device in which combustible gas is released through a nozzle to produce a steady flame, often used for heating or lighting
