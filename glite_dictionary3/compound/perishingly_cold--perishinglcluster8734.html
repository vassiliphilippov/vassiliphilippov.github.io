perishingly cold

adjective
extremely cold (of weather)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrɪʃɪŋli koʊld / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɪʃɪŋli kəʊld / (BrE)
(of weather or conditions) extremely cold; unbearably cold.
