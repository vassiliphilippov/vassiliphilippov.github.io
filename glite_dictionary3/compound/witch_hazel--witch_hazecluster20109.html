witch hazel

noun
astringent lotion from the witch hazel plant
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃ ˌheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃ ˌheɪzl̩ / (BrE)
a lotion or liquid extract prepared from the bark and leaves of the witch hazel plant, used especially for its astringent and soothing properties

Examples

apply witch hazel lotion
After shaving, he applied witch hazel to soothe his skin.
Many people use witch hazel as a natural remedy for minor cuts and irritation.
The pharmacy carries several brands of witch hazel for skin care.
