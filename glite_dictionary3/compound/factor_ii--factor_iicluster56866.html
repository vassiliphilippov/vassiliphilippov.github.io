factor II

noun
blood protein, precursor of thrombin
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ tu / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tə tuː / (BrE)
A protein in blood plasma that acts as the inactive precursor of thrombin, essential for blood clotting.
