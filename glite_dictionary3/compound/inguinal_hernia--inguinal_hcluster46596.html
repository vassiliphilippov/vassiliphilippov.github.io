inguinal hernia

noun
hernia where intestine enters inguinal canal
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡwənl ˈhɝːniə / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡwɪnəl ˈhɜːniə / (BrE)
a hernia in which a loop of intestine protrudes into the inguinal canal, most commonly occurring in males

Examples

painful inguinal hernia
