off-the-peg suit

noun
ready-made suit available immediately
Pronunciation: / ˌɔf ðə ˈpɛɡ suːt / (AmE), / ˌɒf ðə ˈpeɡ suːt / (BrE)
A suit that is ready-made and available for immediate purchase from a shop, not made to order.

Examples

buy an off-the-peg suit
He bought an off-the-peg suit for the interview because he needed something quickly.
Many department stores have a wide variety of off-the-peg suits in different sizes.
Unlike bespoke tailoring, an off-the-peg suit can be worn immediately after purchase.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bespoke suit: a suit made to a customer's individual specifications
  • synonym – off-the-rack suit: a ready-made suit available for immediate purchase (American English)
