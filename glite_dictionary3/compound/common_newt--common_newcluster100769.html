common newt

noun
a small European semiaquatic salamander
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən nut / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən njuːt / (BrE)
a small semiaquatic salamander found throughout much of Europe, known for its smooth skin and preference for ponds and wetlands
