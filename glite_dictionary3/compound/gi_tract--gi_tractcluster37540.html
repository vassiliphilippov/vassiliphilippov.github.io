GI tract

noun
digestive passage from mouth to anus
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː ˈtrækt / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː ˈtrækt / (BrE)
the entire passage in the body, extending from the mouth to the anus, where food is digested and waste is eliminated
