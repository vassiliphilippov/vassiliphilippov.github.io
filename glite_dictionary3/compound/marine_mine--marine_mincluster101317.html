marine mine

noun
underwater explosive device for ships
Pronunciation: / məˈrin maɪn / (AmE), / məˈriːn maɪn / (BrE)
An underwater explosive device placed to destroy ships that come into contact with it.

Examples

underwater marine mine
Clearing the area of marine mines was critical before any vessels could pass safely.
During the war, several ships were sunk by marine mines hidden beneath the surface.
The navy detected a marine mine floating near the entrance to the harbor.
