aspect ratio

noun
ratio of width to height in an image or screen
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɛkt ˌreɪʃioʊ / (AmE), / ˈæspɛkt ˌreɪʃiəʊ / (BrE)
the ratio of the width to the height of an image, screen, or display, such as on a television or computer monitor

Examples

aspect ratio of a screen
Older movies often used a 4:3 aspect ratio, resulting in a more square-shaped image.
The new television has an aspect ratio of 16:9, which is standard for high-definition displays.
When printing a photo, be sure to maintain its original aspect ratio to avoid distortion.
