deadbeat dad

noun
father who avoids child support
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdˌbit dæd / (AmE), / ˈdɛdˌbiːt dad / (BrE)
A father who deliberately fails to provide required financial support for his children.

Examples

deadbeat dad avoids payments
Many states have enacted stricter laws to track down deadbeat dads and enforce their responsibilities.
She struggled to raise three children after her ex-husband turned into a deadbeat dad.
The court issued a warrant for the deadbeat dad who refused to pay child support.
