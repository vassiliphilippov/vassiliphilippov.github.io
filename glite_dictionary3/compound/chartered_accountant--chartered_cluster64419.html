chartered accountant

noun
qualified British or Canadian accountant
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑr.tɚd əˈkaʊn.tənt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑː.təd əˈkaʊn.tənt / (BrE)
A British or Canadian accountant who is a member of a professional body recognized by royal charter, authorized to offer accounting services to the public.

Examples

chartered accountant in London
After years of study, he finally became a chartered accountant with a leading London firm.
Many companies prefer to hire a chartered accountant to oversee their financial reporting.
She is a chartered accountant specializing in international tax law.
