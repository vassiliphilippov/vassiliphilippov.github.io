voting system

noun
method for conducting elections
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈvəʊtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A set of rules or procedures used to conduct elections and determine the outcome based on voters' choices.

Examples

new voting system rules
After much debate, the committee decided to switch to a ranked-choice voting system.
Many countries use a proportional representation voting system to ensure fairer results.
The choice of voting system can significantly affect the outcome of an election.

  • synonym – electoral system: system for organizing and conducting elections
