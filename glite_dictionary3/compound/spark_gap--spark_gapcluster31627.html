spark gap

noun
device with electrodes for electrical spark
Pronunciation: / spɑrk gæp /; / spɑrk ɡæp / (AmE), / spɑːk ɡap / (BrE)
a device or space with two electrodes or conductors separated by a gap, where an electric spark can jump across, used to control or measure electrical discharges
