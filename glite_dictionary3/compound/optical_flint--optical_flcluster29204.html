optical flint

noun
high-dispersion optical glass
Pronunciation: / ˈɑptɪkəl flɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪkəl flɪnt / (BrE)
a type of optical glass with high dispersion and a high refractive index, used in lenses and prisms
