skillet bread

noun
bread cooked in a skillet
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪlɪt brɛd / (AmE), / ˈskɪlɪt brɛd / (BrE)
Bread cooked in a skillet, often over an open fire; especially refers to cornbread or soda bread sometimes with ham or other additions.

Examples

fresh skillet bread
