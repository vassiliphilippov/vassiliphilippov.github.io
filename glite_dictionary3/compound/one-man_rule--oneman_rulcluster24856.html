one-man rule

noun
system of government by a single dictator
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn ˌmæn ˈruːl / (AmE), / ˈwʌn ˌmæn ˈruːl / (BrE)
a system of government in which all power is held by a single individual who acts as an absolute dictator, without checks or opposition.

Examples

History has shown that one-man rule often leads to widespread corruption and repression.
