passing tone

noun
nonharmonic note for melodic transition
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ toʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ təʊn / (BrE)
A nonharmonic note inserted between two harmonic notes to create a smooth melodic transition.
