rap session

noun
informal gathering to perform rap
Pronunciation: / ræp ˈsɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ræp ˈseʃ.ən / (BrE)
an informal musical gathering where rappers perform, often freestyling over beats or backing tracks, sometimes collaboratively or competitively.

Examples

freestyle rap session
After school, the crew organized a rap session where they freestyled over old-school beats.
The festival's late-night rap session drew local MCs eager to try new verses.
They held several rap sessions in the studio to record freestyles for their mixtape.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jam session: an informal musical gathering where musicians improvise together
  • synonym – open mic: an event where performers are invited to perform short pieces, often including rappers and singers
