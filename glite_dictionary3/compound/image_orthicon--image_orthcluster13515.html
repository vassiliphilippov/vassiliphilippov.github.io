image orthicon

noun
obsolete television camera tube
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒ ˈɔrθɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒ ˈɔːθɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
an obsolete type of television camera tube in which electrons emitted from a photoemissive surface, in proportion to the intensity of incident light, are focused onto a target, causing secondary emission of electrons

Examples

an image orthicon camera tube
