sick headache

noun
severe recurring headache (migraine)
Pronunciation: / sɪk ˈhɛdˌeɪk / (AmE), / sɪk ˈhed.eɪk /; / sɪk ˈhɛdˌeɪk / (BrE)
a severe headache that is often accompanied by nausea (now commonly called a migraine)
