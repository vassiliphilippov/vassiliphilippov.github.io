laundry cart

noun
wheeled cart for transporting laundry
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔn.dri kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈlɔːn.dri kɑːt / (BrE)
a wheeled cart used for transporting laundry, especially in laundromats or large households

Examples

laundry cart full of clothes

She loaded the wet clothes into the laundry cart and wheeled it across the laundromat.
The hotel staff uses a large laundry cart to collect towels and sheets from each room.
