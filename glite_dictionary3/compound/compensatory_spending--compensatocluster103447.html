compensatory spending

noun
government borrowing to stimulate the economy
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛnsəˌtɔri ˈspɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛnsətəri ˈspɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
government expenditure financed by borrowing, intended to stimulate the economy, especially during periods of recession or low growth
