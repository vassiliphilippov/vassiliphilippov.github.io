personality inventory

noun
questionnaire assessing personality traits
Pronunciation: / pərˌsɑːnələti ˈɪnvənˌtɔri / (AmE), / pɜːˌsɒnələti ˈɪnvəntri / (BrE)
a questionnaire designed to evaluate and describe an individual's personality traits

Examples

take a personality inventory
Completing a personality inventory can help individuals learn more about their traits and preferences.
The personality inventory consists of several questions about attitudes, behaviors, and emotions.
The psychologist administered a personality inventory to assess the candidate's suitability for the job.
