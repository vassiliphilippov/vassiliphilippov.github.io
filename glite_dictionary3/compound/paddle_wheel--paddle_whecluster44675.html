paddle wheel

noun
wheel with paddles for boat propulsion
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəl ˌwiːl / (AmE), / ˈpadl ˌwiːl / (BrE)
a large wheel with boards (paddles) attached around its edge, turned by an engine to move a boat through water

Examples

paddle wheel steamboat
Engineers restored the paddle wheel to preserve the authenticity of the historic vessel.
The old steamboat featured a massive paddle wheel at its stern.
