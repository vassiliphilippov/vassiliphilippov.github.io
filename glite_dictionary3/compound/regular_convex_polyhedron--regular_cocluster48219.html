regular convex polyhedron

noun
a polyhedron with congruent regular polygonal faces and angles
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr kənˈvɛks pɑˈliːˌhiːdrən / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə kənˈvɛks pɒlɪˈhiːdrən / (BrE)
a polyhedron whose faces are congruent regular polygons and whose angles are all congruent, one of the five Platonic solids
