fine-tooth comb

noun
comb with closely spaced teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪn tuθ koʊm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪn tuːθ kəʊm / (BrE)
a comb with teeth that are set very close together, used for precise grooming or removal of lice and other debris from hair

Examples

use a fine-tooth comb on hair
She examined the document with a fine-tooth comb for any errors or inconsistencies.
The police went over the crime scene with a fine-tooth comb to find every clue.
You should use a fine-tooth comb to check your child’s hair for lice.
