cinema verite

noun
realistic, documentary-style filmmaking
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəmə vɛrəˈteɪ / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəmə vɛrəˈteɪ / (BrE)
A style of filmmaking that depicts real-life situations and ordinary people, often with minimal direction and naturalistic techniques.

Examples

cinema verite documentary style
Many critics praised the cinema verite style for its raw portrayal of everyday life.
She studied cinema verite techniques to enhance the realism of her latest documentary.
The director's use of cinema verite made the film feel incredibly authentic and unfiltered.
