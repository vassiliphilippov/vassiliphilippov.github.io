chair lift

noun
ski lift with chairs suspended from cable
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛr lɪft / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛː lɪft / (BrE)
A type of ski lift consisting of a series of seats suspended from an overhead cable, used to carry people up or down a mountainside.

Examples

ride a chair lift
Learning to get on and off the chair lift can be tricky for beginner skiers.
The chair lift stopped suddenly, leaving us dangling above the snowy slope.
We rode the chair lift to the top of the mountain for a spectacular view.
