tubular device

noun
tubular-shaped climbing belay device
Pronunciation: / ˈtuː.bjələr dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈtjuː.bjʊ.lə dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A tubular-shaped belay device used to control a rope for arresting a climber's fall or rappelling.

Examples

tubular device for climbing rope
Before starting the descent, he checked that his tubular device was correctly threaded with the rope.
Many climbers prefer a tubular device for its versatility and ease of use during rappelling.
The climber used a tubular device to safely belay her partner up the rock face.
