light-green

adjective
pale green color
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪtˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈɡriːn / (BrE)
of a pale shade of green, like the color of fresh grass; of the color between blue and yellow in the color spectrum, but lighter than standard green

Examples

The walls were painted a soft light-green to brighten up the room.
