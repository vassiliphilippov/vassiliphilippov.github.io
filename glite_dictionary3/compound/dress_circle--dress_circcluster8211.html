dress circle

noun
first balcony tier in a theater
Pronunciation: / drɛs ˈsɝkəl / (AmE), / drɛs ˈsɜːkl / (BrE)
the section of seats in a theater or opera house, usually the first tier above the orchestra, often curved in shape

Examples

Seats in the dress circle provide a great view of the stage.
The theater was almost full, especially in the dress circle.
We managed to get tickets in the dress circle for the opening night.
