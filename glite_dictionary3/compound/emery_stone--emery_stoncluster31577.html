emery stone

noun
grindstone made from emery and binder
Pronunciation: / ˈɛməɹi stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɛməri stəʊn / (BrE)
A hard stone made from emery dust and a binder, often molded into grindstones for sharpening or polishing.

Examples

sharpen a knife on emery stone
After years of use, the emery stone needed to be replaced due to wear.
Polishing jewelry is often done with a fine-grained emery stone.
The workshop used an emery stone to sharpen the blades.
