bodily process

noun
organic function occurring in the body
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdɪli ˈprɑːsɛs / (AmE), / ˈbɒdɪli ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
a natural function or activity that takes place within a living body, such as breathing, digestion, or circulation
