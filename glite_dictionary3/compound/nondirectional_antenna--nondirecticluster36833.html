nondirectional antenna

noun
antenna radiating equally in all directions
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈrɛk.ʃə.nəl ænˈtɛn.ə / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈrɛk.ʃə.nəl ænˈtɛn.ə / (BrE)
An antenna that transmits or receives radio signals equally well in all directions.

Examples

nondirectional antenna covers area
A nondirectional antenna is ideal for covering a wide geographic area without aiming.
He replaced his old directional device with a nondirectional antenna for better all-around reception.
The airport uses a nondirectional antenna to broadcast signals for navigational aids.
