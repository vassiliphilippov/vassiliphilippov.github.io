pole vault

noun
athletic event jumping high bar with a pole
Pronunciation: / poʊl vɔlt / (AmE), / pəʊl vɔːlt / (BrE)
an athletic event in which competitors jump over a high horizontal bar using a long flexible pole for assistance

Examples

the pole vault event
He specializes in the pole vault but also competes in other track and field events.
She set a new record in the pole vault at the championship.
Training for the pole vault requires both speed and upper body strength.
