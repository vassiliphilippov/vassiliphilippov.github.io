hepatitis C

noun
a type of viral hepatitis caused by hepatitis C virus
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛpəˈtaɪtɪs si / (AmE), / ˌhɛpəˈtaɪtɪs siː / (BrE)
A form of viral hepatitis caused by the hepatitis C virus, primarily transmitted through blood-to-blood contact, leading to liver inflammation.
