ice-clogged

adjective
blocked or obstructed by ice
Pronunciation: / aɪs klɑgd / (AmE), / aɪs klɒgd / (BrE)
(of a place or passage) blocked or obstructed by ice, especially to the extent that movement is prevented or hindered.

Examples

ice-clogged road
After days of heavy snow, the ice-clogged road was finally closed to all vehicles.
Ships were unable to leave the harbor because it was ice-clogged after the coldest night of the season.
The explorers struggled to navigate the ice-clogged river during their winter expedition.
