Sistine Chapel

proper noun
famous papal chapel in Vatican City
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstiːn ˈtʃæpəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪstiːn ˈtʃæpəl / (BrE)
The private chapel in Vatican City, built by order of Pope Sixtus IV in the late 15th century, famous for its Renaissance artwork, including Michelangelo's ceiling frescoes.
