lackluster attitude

noun
uninspired or dull demeanor
Pronunciation: / ˈlækˌlʌstər ˈæ.t̬ə.tuːd / (AmE), / ˈlækˌlʌstə ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a demeanor characterized by dullness and lack of enthusiasm or inspiration

Examples

lackluster attitude at work
A lackluster attitude can negatively impact workplace productivity and morale.
Her lackluster attitude during the meeting made her disinterest obvious to everyone.
The team's performance suffered due to the captain's lackluster attitude on the field.
