pouf paste

noun
light batter for pastry cases
Pronunciation: / puf peɪst / (AmE), / puːf peɪst / (BrE)
a light batter used to make hollow pastry cases for various fillings

Examples

pouf paste shells
The chef filled the crisp pouf paste shells with a creamy chicken mixture.
