brandy glass

noun
glass for serving brandy
Pronunciation: / ˈbræn.di ˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈbræn.di ˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a short-stemmed, wide-bowled glass with a narrow top, designed for serving brandy

Examples

a brandy glass of cognac
A good brandy glass enhances the flavour and bouquet of the spirit.
He swirled the amber liquid in his brandy glass by the fire.
The waiter brought two brandy glasses filled nearly to the rim.
