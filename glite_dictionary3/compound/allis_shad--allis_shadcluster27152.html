allis shad

noun
European migratory shad species
Pronunciation: / ˈælɪs ʃæd / (AmE), / ˈælɪs ʃad / (BrE)
A species of shad (Alosa alosa) found in European coastal and freshwater habitats, notable for its migratory spawning behavior.
