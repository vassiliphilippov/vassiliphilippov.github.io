intensive care

noun
specialized hospital care for critical patients
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnsɪv kɛr / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsɪv keə / (BrE)
specialized medical treatment and close monitoring given to patients with severe or life-threatening conditions, typically in a dedicated hospital unit
