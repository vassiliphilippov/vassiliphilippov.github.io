Plastic Paddy

noun
inauthentic adopter of Irish identity
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪk ˈpædi / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk ˈpædi / (BrE)
a person who adopts Irish customs or identity inauthentically, typically without Irish heritage
