clostridial myonecrosis

noun
deadly bacterial muscle infection (gas gangrene)
Pronunciation: / klɑːˈstrɪdiəl ˌmaɪoʊnɛˈkroʊsɪs / (AmE), / klɒˈstrɪdɪəl ˌmaɪəʊnɛˈkrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A severe, often fatal bacterial infection of muscle tissue, usually caused by Clostridium bacteria, resulting in rapid tissue death and gas production.
