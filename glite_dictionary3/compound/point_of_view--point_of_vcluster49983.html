point of view

noun
particular perspective or viewpoint
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt əv vjuː / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv vjuː / (BrE)
a particular attitude or way of considering a matter; a mental position or perspective from which something is observed or considered
