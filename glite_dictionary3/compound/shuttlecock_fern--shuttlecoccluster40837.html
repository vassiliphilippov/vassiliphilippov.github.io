shuttlecock fern

noun
tall northern fern with plume-like fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬əlˌkɑk fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtəlkɒk fɜːn / (BrE)
a tall fern of northern temperate regions with gracefully arched fronds and fertile fronds resembling ostrich plumes, especially Matteuccia struthiopteris
