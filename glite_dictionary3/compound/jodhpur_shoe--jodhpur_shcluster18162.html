jodhpur shoe

noun
short riding boot with side buckle
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑd.pɚ ˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒd.pə ˌʃuː / (BrE)
A short leather riding boot that fastens with a strap and buckle at the side, often worn with jodhpurs.
