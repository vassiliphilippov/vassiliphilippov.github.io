sliding bolt

noun
metal bar for locking doors manually
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪdɪŋ ˌboʊlt / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdɪŋ ˌbəʊlt / (BrE)
A horizontal metal bar that moves manually to lock or unlock a door or gate.

Examples

sliding bolt on a door
He reached up and pushed the sliding bolt across before going to bed.
She secured the shed by locking the sliding bolt into place every night.
The old wooden door still had a sturdy sliding bolt instead of a modern lock.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deadbolt: a type of lock with a turning bolt, often key-operated
  • synonym – latch: a fastening device for a door or gate
