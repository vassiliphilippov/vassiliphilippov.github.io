saddle of lamb

noun
a cut of lamb including backbone and loins
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl əv læm / (AmE), / ˈsædəl əv læm / (BrE)
a cut of lamb consisting of the backbone and both loins, often roasted as a single joint
