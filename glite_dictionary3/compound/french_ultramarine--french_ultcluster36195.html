French ultramarine

noun
artificial bright blue pigment
Pronunciation: / ˌfrɛntʃ ˌʌltrəˈmɛriːn / (AmE), / ˌfrɛntʃ ˌʌltrəˈmɑːriːn / (BrE)
a bright blue pigment similar to natural ultramarine, produced artificially from sodium aluminosilicate and used in paint and other applications.
