overdraft credit

noun
credit covering account overdrafts
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərdræft ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvədrɑːft ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)

Examples

overdraft credit for payments
