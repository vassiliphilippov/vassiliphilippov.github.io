true sago palm

noun
palm tree yielding sago starch
Pronunciation: / tru ˈseɪɡoʊ pɑm / (AmE), / truː ˈseɪɡəʊ pɑːm / (BrE)
a species of palm (Metroxylon sagu) native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific, whose trunk yields sago starch used as a food thickener and fabric stiffener
