sorb apple

noun
acidic fruit of the sorb tree
Pronunciation: / sɔrb ˈæpəl / (AmE), / sɔːb ˈæpəl / (BrE)
A small, acidic fruit with a gritty texture, produced by the sorb tree (Sorbus domestica).
