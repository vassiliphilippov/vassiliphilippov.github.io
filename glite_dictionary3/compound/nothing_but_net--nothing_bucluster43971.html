nothing but net

noun
perfect basketball shot (no rim or backboard)
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət ˈnɛt / (BrE)
(in basketball) a perfect shot where the ball goes through the net without touching the rim or backboard.
