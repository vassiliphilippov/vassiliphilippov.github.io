rosin bag

noun
bag of rosin for pitchers' grip
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑzɪn bæg / (AmE), / ˈrɒzɪn bæɡ / (BrE)
A small bag filled with powdered rosin, used by baseball pitchers to improve their grip on the ball.

Examples

rosin bag for pitchers
A rosin bag is kept on the mound so players can keep their hands dry.
The pitcher grabbed the rosin bag before throwing his next pitch.
Umpires sometimes inspect the rosin bag during a game for any irregularities.
