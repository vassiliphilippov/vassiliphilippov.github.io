hook shot

noun
basketball shot made with a sweeping overhead motion
Pronunciation: / hʊk ʃɑt / (AmE), / hʊk ʃɒt / (BrE)
a basketball shot in which the player arches the ball over their head with a sweeping motion of the arm, typically using the hand farthest from the basket

Examples

a hook shot in basketball
Kareem Abdul-Jabbar was famous for his unstoppable hook shot in NBA games.
She scored by executing a perfect hook shot from just inside the free-throw line.
The defender couldn't block his hook shot because of the high arc and quick release.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jump shot: a basketball shot made by jumping and releasing the ball at the peak of the jump
