field goal

noun
score by kicking ball through goalposts (football)
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌɡoʊl / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌɡəʊl / (BrE)
(in American football) a score made by kicking the ball between the opponents' goalposts.

Examples

kick a field goal
He missed a 45-yard field goal in the second quarter.
The team won the game with a last-minute field goal.
Their only points in the first half came from a field goal.
