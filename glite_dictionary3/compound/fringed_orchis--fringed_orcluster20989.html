fringed orchis

noun
a North American orchid with a fringed lip
Pronunciation: / frɪndʒd ˈɔr.kɪs / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ˈɔː.kɪs / (BrE)
Any of several summer-flowering North American orchids distinguished by having a fringed or deeply cut lip.
