Bat-Signal

noun
distress signal to summon Batman
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt ˌsɪɡ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈbæt ˌsɪɡ.nəl / (BrE)
a large spotlight with an image of a bat, used in the Batman comics and films as a distress signal to summon Batman
