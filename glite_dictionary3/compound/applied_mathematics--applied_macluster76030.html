applied mathematics

noun
mathematics for practical applications
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪd ˌmæθəˈmætɪks / (AmE), / əˈplaɪd ˌmæθəˈmætɪks / (BrE)
the branch of mathematics concerned with mathematical methods that are used in practical fields such as science, engineering, business, and industry
