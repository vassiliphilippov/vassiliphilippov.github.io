compass north

noun
direction indicated by a compass needle
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm.pəs nɔrθ / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pəs nɔːθ / (BrE)
the direction indicated by the north-pointing end of a magnetic compass, which aligns with the Earth's magnetic north

Examples

compass north points on map
In navigation, compass north can differ from true north due to magnetic declination.
