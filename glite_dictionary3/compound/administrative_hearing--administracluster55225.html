administrative hearing

noun
legal proceeding before an administrative body
Pronunciation: / ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪtɪv ˈhɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv ˈhɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
a hearing held outside the traditional court system, typically before an official or tribunal authorized to resolve disputes involving government agencies or regulations
