charter school

noun
publicly funded independent school
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːɹɾɚ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtə skuːl / (BrE)
A publicly funded independent school established by teachers, parents, or community groups under a charter with a local or national authority, often with more flexibility than traditional public schools.
