United Self-Defense Group of Colombia

proper noun
Colombian paramilitary organization (1997–)
Pronunciation: / juˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ɡruːp əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (AmE), / juˈnaɪtɪd ˈsɛlf dɪˈfɛns ɡruːp əv kəˈlʌmbiə / (BrE)
A paramilitary and former terrorist organization in Colombia formed in 1997 as an umbrella group for various regional paramilitary forces, financed largely by narcotics trafficking and aimed at protecting its members' economic interests.
