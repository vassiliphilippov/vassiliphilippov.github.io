ball valve

noun
valve with a movable ball for flow control
Pronunciation: / bɔl vælv / (AmE), / bɔːl vælv / (BrE)
A valve that controls the flow of liquid or gas by means of a movable ball that seals against a seat.

Examples

ball valve controls flow
A ball valve is often used in industrial settings due to its reliability and simple operation.
The plumber recommended installing a ball valve for easier water shutoff in case of emergencies.
To stop the leak, turn the handle on the ball valve to the off position.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gate valve: valve using a flat gate to control flow
  • synonym – globe valve: valve where flow is regulated by a disk against a seat
