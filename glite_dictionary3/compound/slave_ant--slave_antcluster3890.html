slave ant

noun
ant forced to serve another ant species
Pronunciation: / sleɪv ænt / (AmE), / sleɪv ænt / (BrE)
an ant that is captured as a larva by another species and raised to work for the colony as a 'slave'

Examples

slave ant colony
Researchers observed slave ant larvae being carried into the nest by the raiding species.
Some species of ants rely on slave ants to conduct most of their colony's labor.
The role of a slave ant is often limited to foraging and tending to the brood.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slave-making ant: an ant species that captures and enslaves other ants
