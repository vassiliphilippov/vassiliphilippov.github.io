side gig

noun
secondary job for extra income
Pronunciation: / saɪd ɡɪɡ / (AmE), / saɪd ɡɪɡ / (BrE)
a secondary job or source of income that someone takes in addition to their main employment, usually to earn extra money

Examples

side gig for extra money
After paying his bills, he used money from his side gig to fund his vacation.
Many people started a side gig during the pandemic to help make ends meet.
She runs a side gig as a freelance graphic designer in the evenings.

  • synonym – side hustle: an informal job or project for extra money apart from one's main employment
