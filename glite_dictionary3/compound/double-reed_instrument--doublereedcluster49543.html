double-reed instrument

noun
a woodwind instrument with two joined reeds
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəl ˈrid ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (AmE), / ˌdʌb.əl ˈriːd ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (BrE)
a woodwind instrument that produces sound by means of two joined reeds vibrating together, such as the oboe or bassoon
