hero worship

noun
intense admiration for someone
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪroʊ ˌwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈhɪərəʊ ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
intense or excessive admiration and reverence for someone, especially a famous or distinguished person, often to an uncritical extent.

Examples

hero worship of celebrities
He dismissed stories of hero worship and insisted on seeing his achievements in a realistic light.
Her hero worship of the actor was obvious from the posters covering her bedroom walls.
Many students develop a kind of hero worship for popular scientists and inventors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adulation: excessive admiration or praise
  • antonym – contempt: the feeling that someone or something is worthless or deserves scorn
  • synonym – idolization: the act of admiring or loving somebody or something too much
