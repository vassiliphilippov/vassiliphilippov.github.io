glass sponge

noun
marine sponge with glassy skeleton
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs spʌndʒ / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs spʌndʒ / (BrE)
a type of marine sponge having a skeleton made of siliceous, glass-like spicules, belonging to the class Hyalospongiae
