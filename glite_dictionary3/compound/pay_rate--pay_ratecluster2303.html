pay rate

noun
amount paid per time worked
Pronunciation: / peɪ reɪt / (AmE), / peɪ reɪt / (BrE)
the amount of money paid to a worker per unit of time, usually per hour or per week

Examples

hourly pay rate
Many workers negotiated a higher pay rate after the new contract was signed.
The company increased the pay rate for its employees this year.
Your pay rate will determine how much you earn per hour.
