land agent

noun
broker/manager of virtual land
Pronunciation: / ˈlænd ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈlænd ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
(in digital contexts) a professional who buys, sells, brokers, or manages virtual land and property in online platforms (for example metaverse environments), handling listings, transfers, and often tokenization or sales of digital plots.

Examples

land agent brokers plots
She works as a land agent for a major metaverse firm, brokering virtual plots to collectors.
The startup hired several experienced land agents to manage sales of its blockchain-based city parcels.
During the conference, the panel discussed how land agents set prices for virtual neighbourhoods and parcels.

Related Terms

  • synonym – estate agent: a person whose business is selling, renting, or managing property and land (physical or digital).
  • synonym – virtual real estate broker: a professional who buys, sells, or manages property in virtual worlds and online platforms.
