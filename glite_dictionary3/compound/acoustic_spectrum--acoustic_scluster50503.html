acoustic spectrum

noun
distribution of sound energy by frequency
Pronunciation: / əˈkuːstɪk ˈspɛktrəm / (AmE), / əˈkuːstɪk ˈspɛktrəm / (BrE)
the distribution of sound energy across different frequencies produced by a particular source

Examples

acoustic spectrum of a sound
Changes in the acoustic spectrum can indicate damage to a machine or structure.
Different musical instruments produce a unique acoustic spectrum, allowing us to distinguish their sounds.
The acoustic spectrum of the violin was analyzed using advanced computer software.
