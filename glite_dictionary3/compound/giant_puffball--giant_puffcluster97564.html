giant puffball

noun
very large edible white fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈpʌfˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a very large edible white fungus, typically spherical and sometimes reaching up to 2 feet in diameter, known for its edible qualities when young
