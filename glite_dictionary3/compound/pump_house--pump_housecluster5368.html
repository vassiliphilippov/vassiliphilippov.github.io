pump house

noun
building for operating pumps
Pronunciation: / pʌmp haʊs / (AmE), / pʌmp haʊs / (BrE)
A building where pumps are installed and operated, especially for purposes such as irrigation or water supply.

Examples

pump house for water supply
During the inspection, the engineer checked all valves and gauges inside the pump house.
The farm's pump house ensures a steady supply of water to the fields.
They built a new pump house near the river to improve the town's water system.
