character assassination

noun
deliberate attack on someone's reputation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrɪktər əˌsæsəˈneɪʃən /; / ˈkærɪktɚ əˌsæsəˈneɪʃ(ə)n / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktə əˌsæsɪˈneɪʃn /; / ˈkærɪktə əˌsæsɪˈneɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act of deliberately damaging someone's reputation, especially by making false or misleading statements about them
