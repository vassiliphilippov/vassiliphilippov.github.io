Scotch broth

noun
traditional Scottish barley and meat soup
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ brɔθ / (AmE), / skɒtʃ brɒθ / (BrE)
A thick Scottish soup made from beef or mutton, various vegetables, and pearl barley.

hot Scotch broth soup
