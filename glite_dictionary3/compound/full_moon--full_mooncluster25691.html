full moon

noun
phase of the Moon when fully illuminated
Pronunciation: / fʊl mun / (AmE), / fʊl muːn / (BrE)
the phase of the Moon when its entire face is illuminated as seen from Earth

Examples

bright full moon
Many cultures hold festivals during a full moon because of its beauty and significance.
The children were fascinated by the bright full moon shining in the night sky.
We planned our hike to coincide with the full moon for better visibility at night.
