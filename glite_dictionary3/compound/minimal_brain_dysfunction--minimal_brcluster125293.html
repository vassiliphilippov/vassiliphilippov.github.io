minimal brain dysfunction

noun
historical medical term for a behavioral disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnəməl breɪn dɪsˌfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməl breɪn dɪsˌfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
a medical condition, mainly affecting boys, characterized by behavioral and learning disorders, historically used to describe what is now more commonly known as attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)
