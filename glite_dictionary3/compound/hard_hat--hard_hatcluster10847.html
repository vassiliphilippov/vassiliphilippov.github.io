hard hat

noun
protective helmet worn by construction workers
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrd ˌhæt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːd ˌhæt / (BrE)
A lightweight, protective helmet made of plastic or metal, worn to protect the head, especially by construction workers.

Examples

All workers are required to wear a hard hat while on the construction site.
