pure rockism

noun
preference for traditional rock music
Pronunciation: / pjʊr ˈrɑːkɪzəm / (AmE), / pjʊə ˈrɒkɪzəm / (BrE)
An attitude that strongly values traditional rock music and regards it as superior to other genres.

Examples

pure rockism in music reviews
His review displayed a clear sense of pure rockism, dismissing any style that wasn't guitar-driven.
Some critics argue that pure rockism overlooks innovation happening outside the rock genre.
The debate between pure rockism and more inclusive musical tastes has shaped many music magazines.

Related Terms

  • antonym – popism: The attitude or critical position valuing popular music, especially in opposition to rockism.
  • synonym – rockism: The belief that traditional rock music is superior to other styles.
