northern snakehead

noun
an air-breathing, invasive Asian freshwater fish
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərn ˈsneɪkhɛd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˈsneɪkhɛd / (BrE)
a voracious freshwater fish native to East Asia, known for its ability to breathe air and survive out of water, considered invasive in North America
