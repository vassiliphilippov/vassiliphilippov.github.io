transparent gem

noun
a gemstone that is clear and allows light through
Pronunciation: / trænˈsperənt dʒɛm / (AmE), / trænˈspærənt dʒɛm / (BrE)
a gemstone that allows light to pass through it with minimal scattering so that objects can be clearly seen through it

Examples

sparkling transparent gem
Diamonds are often prized as the most valuable transparent gems in jewelry.
Light passed through the transparent gem, creating a dazzling array of colors on the table.
The museum display included a variety of transparent gems such as sapphires and emeralds.
