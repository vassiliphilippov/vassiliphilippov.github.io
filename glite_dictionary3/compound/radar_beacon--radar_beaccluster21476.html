radar beacon

noun
device transmitting coded radar signals for navigation
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪˌdɑr ˈbiːkən / (AmE), / ˈreɪˌdɑː ˈbiːkən / (BrE)
a device that receives radar signals and transmits coded signals in response to assist navigators in determining their position

Examples

radar beacon signal response
An aircraft automatically responded to signals from a radar beacon set up near the airstrip.
