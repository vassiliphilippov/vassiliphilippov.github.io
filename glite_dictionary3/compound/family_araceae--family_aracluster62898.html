family Araceae

noun
botanical family of arums (flowering plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌɛəˈreɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌæˈræsiː / (BrE)
A large family of flowering plants, also known as the arum family, that includes anthuriums, calla lilies, jack-in-the-pulpits, and philodendrons.
