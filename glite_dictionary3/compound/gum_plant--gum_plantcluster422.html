gum plant

noun
western American Grindelia plant; resinous and sometimes toxic
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌm ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌm ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
any of several western American plants of the genus Grindelia, with sticky, resinous leaves and stems, sometimes used medicinally and often poisonous to livestock

Examples
