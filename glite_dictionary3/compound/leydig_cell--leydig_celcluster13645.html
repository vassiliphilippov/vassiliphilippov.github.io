Leydig cell

noun
testicular cell producing testosterone
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪdɪɡ sɛl / (AmE), / ˈleɪdɪɡ sɛl / (BrE)
A cell found in the testes that produces and secretes the hormone testosterone.
