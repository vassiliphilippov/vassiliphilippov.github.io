hoof-mark

noun
impression made by an animal's hoof
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊf mɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhʊf mɑːk / (BrE)
A visible impression on a surface made by the hoof of an animal.

Examples

hoof-mark in mud
After the rain, the hoof-mark was clearly visible in the soft earth.
Fresh hoof-marks in the mud showed that the horses had passed by recently.
The hunter followed a trail of hoof-marks through the forest.
