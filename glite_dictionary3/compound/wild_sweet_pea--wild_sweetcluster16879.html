wild sweet pea

noun
North American perennial plant with yellowish-rose flowers
Pronunciation: / waɪld swit pi / (AmE), / waɪld swiːt piː / (BrE)
A perennial subshrub native to eastern North America, with downy leaves and yellowish-rose flowers, and known as a source of rotenone.
