rose gum

noun
tall Australian eucalyptus tree
Pronunciation: / roʊz ɡʌm / (AmE), / rəʊz ɡʌm / (BrE)
a very tall eucalyptus tree (Eucalyptus grandis) native to Queensland and New South Wales, valued for its timber and ornamental uses
