spiral notebook

noun
notebook with spiral coil binding
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪrəl ˈnoʊtbʊk / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəl ˈnəʊtbʊk / (BrE)
A notebook bound with a metal or plastic spiral coil, allowing the pages to be turned easily.
