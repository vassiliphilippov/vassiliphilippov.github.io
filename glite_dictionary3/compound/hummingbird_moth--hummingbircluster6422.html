hummingbird moth

noun
moth that resembles a hummingbird
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmɪŋˌbɝd mɑθ / (AmE), / ˈhʌmɪŋˌbɜːd mɒθ / (BrE)
A type of moth with long, narrow forewings, capable of powerful flight and often seen hovering over flowers while feeding, resembling a hummingbird.

Examples

a hummingbird moth hovering over flowers
Gardeners often mistake the hummingbird moth for a tiny bird because of its rapid wing movements.
The hummingbird moth hovered over the flowers, sipping nectar with its long proboscis.
We spotted a hummingbird moth at dusk, feeding among the blossoms in the backyard.
