Windsor eyeglasses

noun
eyeglasses with round wire or tortoiseshell frames
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnzər ˈaɪˌɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɪnzə ˈaɪˌɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
A style of eyeglasses with small, round frames made of thin wire or tortoiseshell, popularized in the early 20th century and associated with figures such as John Lennon.
