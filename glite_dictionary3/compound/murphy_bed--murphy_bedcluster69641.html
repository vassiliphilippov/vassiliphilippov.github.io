Murphy bed

noun
foldable bed stored in a cabinet or wall
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːfi bɛd / (AmE), / ˈmɜːfi bɛd / (BrE)
A bed that can be folded or swung into a cabinet or wall when not in use, saving space in a room.
