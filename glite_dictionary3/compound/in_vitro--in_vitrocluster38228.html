in vitro

adjective, adverb
performed outside a living organism
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈviːtroʊ / (AmE), / ɪn ˈviːtrəʊ / (BrE)
happening or done outside a living organism, in an artificial environment such as a test tube or laboratory

Examples
