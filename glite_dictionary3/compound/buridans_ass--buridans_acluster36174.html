Buridan's ass

noun
philosophical dilemma of indecision between two equal choices
Pronunciation: / bʊˈrɪdənz æs / (AmE), / bjʊˈrɪdənz æs / (BrE)
a situation in which a person is unable to choose between two equally attractive options, resulting in indecision or inaction

Examples

Buridan's ass dilemma
He found himself in a Buridan's ass scenario, unable to pick between two appealing job offers.
The committee reached a Buridan's ass moment, unable to agree on either budget proposal.
Whenever I face equally tempting options, I feel like Buridan's ass—stuck in endless hesitation.
