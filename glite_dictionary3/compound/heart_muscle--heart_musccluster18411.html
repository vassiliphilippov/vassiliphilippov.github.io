heart muscle

noun
muscle tissue of the heart
Pronunciation: / hɑrt ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
the muscle tissue that forms the walls of the heart and is responsible for its rhythmic contractions
