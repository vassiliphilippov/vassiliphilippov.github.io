food coma

noun
drowsiness after a large meal
Pronunciation: / fuːd ˈkoʊmə / (AmE), / fuːd ˈkəʊmə / (BrE)
A feeling of extreme drowsiness or sleepiness that occurs after eating a large meal.

Examples

after food coma nap
After Thanksgiving dinner, I always fall into a food coma and need a nap.
She warned us about the food coma that hits after a big Italian lunch.
The food coma set in, and everyone at the table grew quiet and sleepy.
