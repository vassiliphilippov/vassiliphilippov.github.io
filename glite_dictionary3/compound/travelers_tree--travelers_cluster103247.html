traveler's tree

noun
tropical plant with fan-shaped leaves, also called traveler's palm
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævələrz ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈtræv(ə)ləz ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tall tropical plant (Ravenala madagascariensis) with fan-shaped leaves, edible nuts, and leafstalks that provide drinkable sap, reputedly used by travelers as an emergency water source

Examples

tall traveler's tree

The traveler's tree is a striking feature of Madagascar’s landscape, easily recognized by its large, fan-shaped leaves.
