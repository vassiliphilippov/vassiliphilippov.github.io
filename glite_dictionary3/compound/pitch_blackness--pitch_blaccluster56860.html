pitch blackness

noun
total darkness; absence of light
Pronunciation: / pɪtʃ ˈblæknəs / (AmE), / pɪtʃ ˈblæknɪs / (BrE)
A state or condition of complete darkness; total absence of light.

Examples

total pitch blackness
During the power outage, the house was left in complete pitch blackness.
The cave was filled with pitch blackness, making it impossible to see anything.
They wandered through the forest, surrounded by pitch blackness after their flashlight died.
