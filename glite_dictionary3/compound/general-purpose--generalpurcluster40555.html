general-purpose

adjective
designed for many uses, not specialized
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛnərəl ˈpɝːpəs / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnərəl ˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
intended for use in a wide range of situations or for many different tasks, rather than being limited to a specific purpose.

Examples

general-purpose tool
A general-purpose tool is handy to have in any workshop.
They purchased a general-purpose computer to run multiple types of software.
This general-purpose cleaner can be used on a variety of surfaces in your home.

Related Terms

  • synonym – multipurpose: having several different uses or functions
  • antonym – specialized: designed for a specific purpose or use
