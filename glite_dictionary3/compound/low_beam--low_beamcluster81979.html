low beam

noun
short-range vehicle headlight setting
Pronunciation: / loʊ bim / (AmE), / ləʊ biːm / (BrE)
The setting of a vehicle's headlights that provides illumination over a short distance, used to avoid dazzling other drivers.

Examples

switch to low beam
Please switch to low beam when driving through the city at night.
She flicked her lights from low beam to high beam as she turned onto the empty country road.
The manual recommends using low beam headlights in foggy weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym – high beam: the setting of a vehicle's headlights that provides illumination over a greater distance
