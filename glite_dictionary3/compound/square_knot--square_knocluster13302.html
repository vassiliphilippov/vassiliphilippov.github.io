square knot

noun
a knot for joining two cords
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɛr nɑt / (AmE), / ˈskweə nɒt / (BrE)
A knot made by tying two half-hitches in opposite directions, used for joining two ends of cord or rope.

Examples

tie a square knot
He secured the bundle with a square knot to make sure it would not come undone.
Learning how to tie a square knot is essential in most basic first aid training.
Sailors often use the square knot when they need to join two ropes of equal thickness.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reef knot: another name for a square knot, especially in British English
