white fir

noun
a North American fir tree species
Pronunciation: / waɪt fɜr /; / waɪt fɝː / (AmE), / waɪt fɜː / (BrE)
a medium to tall fir tree (Abies concolor) native to central and western North America, with soft wood and a conical or narrow crown
