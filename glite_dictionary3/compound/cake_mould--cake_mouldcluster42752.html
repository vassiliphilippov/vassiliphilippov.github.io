cake mould

noun
container for shaping cake during baking
Pronunciation: / keɪk moʊld / (AmE), / keɪk məʊld / (BrE)
a container made of metal, silicone, or other materials, used to shape a cake as it bakes

Examples

Different shapes of cake moulds can make your desserts more decorative.
