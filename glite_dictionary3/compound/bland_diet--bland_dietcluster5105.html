bland diet

noun
mild, non-irritating diet
Pronunciation: / blænd ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / blænd ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet consisting of foods that are mild in flavor and easy to digest, typically to avoid irritating the digestive system

Examples

bland diet after surgery
After his surgery, the doctor recommended a bland diet to help with his recovery.
People with stomach ulcers often follow a bland diet to reduce symptoms.
She found it challenging to maintain a bland diet because she missed spicy foods.
