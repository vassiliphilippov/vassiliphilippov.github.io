Tom and Jerry

noun
hot alcoholic drink with beaten egg
Pronunciation: / tɑm ənd ˈdʒɛri / (AmE), / tɒm ənd ˈdʒɛəri / (BrE)
A hot drink made with rum, brandy, sugar, and a beaten egg, often topped with hot milk or water; traditionally served around Christmas in the United States.

Examples

a cup of Tom and Jerry
Every Christmas, my grandmother serves a delicious Tom and Jerry to all the adults.
On cold winter nights, nothing warms you up like a Tom and Jerry.
The bartender whipped up a classic Tom and Jerry using fresh eggs and dark rum.
