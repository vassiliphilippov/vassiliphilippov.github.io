wall panel

noun
wall-mounted electronic control panel
Pronunciation: / wɔl ˈpænəl / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈpænəl / (BrE)
A wall-mounted electronic control panel or user interface used to operate building or home systems such as lighting, heating, security, or automation.

Examples

smart wall panel
The hotel installed a touch-sensitive wall panel so guests could control lighting and temperature.
Engineers mounted a secure wall panel in the lobby to manage climate and access systems.
During the tour, the building manager showed us the new wall panel for security controls.

Related Terms

  • synonym – control panel: a panel containing controls and displays for operating a machine or system
