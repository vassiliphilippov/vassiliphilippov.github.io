goldfish bowl

noun
a situation with no privacy
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌfɪʃ boʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌfɪʃ bəʊl / (BrE)
a situation in which a person has no privacy and their actions are watched closely by others

Examples
