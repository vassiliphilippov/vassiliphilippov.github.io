lecture demonstration

noun
illustrative demonstration by a lecturer
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛktʃɚ ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɛktʃə ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a presentation in which a lecturer shows a practical example of the topic being discussed, to illustrate or explain key points to an audience

Examples

lecture demonstration in class
Attending a lecture demonstration can make complex scientific concepts much clearer for students.
During the physics lecture demonstration, the lecturer used real equipment to explain Newton's laws.
The chemistry professor prepared a lecture demonstration to show how different metals react with acids.
