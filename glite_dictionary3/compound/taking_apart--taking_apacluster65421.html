taking apart

noun
the act of cutting something into pieces
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkɪŋ əˈpɑɹt / (AmE), / ˈteɪkɪŋ əˈpɑːt / (BrE)
the act of dismembering or cutting something, especially a body, into pieces; the removal of limbs.
