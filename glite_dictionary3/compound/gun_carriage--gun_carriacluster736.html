gun carriage

noun
support structure for a mounted gun
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡʌn ˈkarɪdʒ / (BrE)
a strong frame or wheeled support on which a gun, especially a cannon or artillery piece, is mounted and from which it is fired

Examples

Engineers repaired the gun carriage before the cannon could be moved into position.
