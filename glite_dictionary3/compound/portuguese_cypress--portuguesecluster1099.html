Portuguese cypress

noun
a tall spreading evergreen tree
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔrtʃəˌgiːz ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˌpɔːtʃəˌgiːz ˈsʌɪprəs / (BrE)
a tall, spreading evergreen tree native to Mexico, known for its drooping branches and introduced into Portugal from Goa
