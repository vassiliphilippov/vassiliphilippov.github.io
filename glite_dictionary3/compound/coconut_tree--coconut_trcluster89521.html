coconut tree

noun
tropical palm tree bearing coconuts
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌkʌt ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌkʌt ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tall tropical palm tree that produces coconuts as its fruit, commonly found in warm coastal regions.

Examples

tall coconut tree
A coconut tree grew in their backyard, providing shade and fresh coconuts each season.
He climbed a coconut tree to pick some coconuts for the villagers.
The island was lined with tall coconut trees swaying gently in the breeze.
