cubic millimeter

noun
metric unit of volume (mm³)
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈmɪl.əˌmiː.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈmɪl.ɪˌmiː.tə / (BrE)
a unit of volume in the metric system, equal to the volume of a cube with sides of 1 millimeter

Examples

one cubic millimeter of water
A single drop of water contains thousands of cubic millimeters of liquid.
Scientists count the number of bacteria in a cubic millimeter of soil.
The tumor measured less than one cubic millimeter on the scan.
