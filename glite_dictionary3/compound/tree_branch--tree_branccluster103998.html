tree branch

noun
limb growing from a tree trunk
Pronunciation: / triː bræntʃ / (AmE), / triː brɑːntʃ / (BrE)
a limb that grows from the trunk or main bough of a tree, typically supporting leaves, flowers, or fruit

Examples
