Siamese cat

noun
slender blue-eyed cat breed with color points
Pronunciation: / saɪəˈmiz kæt / (AmE), / saɪəˈmiːz kæt / (BrE)
A slender, short-haired breed of cat with blue eyes, a pale body, and darker coloring on the ears, face, paws, and tail tip.
