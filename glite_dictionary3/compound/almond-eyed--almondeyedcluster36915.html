almond-eyed

adjective
having almond-shaped eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmənd aɪd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd aɪd / (BrE)
Having eyes that are shaped like almonds, typically slightly pointed at the outer corners.

Examples

almond-eyed beauty
A portrait depicted the queen as graceful and almond-eyed.
His almond-eyed gaze lingered across the crowded room, searching for a familiar face.
