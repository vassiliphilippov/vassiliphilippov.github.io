financial analyst

noun
expert in analyzing company finances
Pronunciation: / fəˈnænʃl ˈænəlɪst / (AmE), / faɪˈnanʃl ˈanəlɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to study the financial performance of companies and provide advice or reports about investments, profitability, and financial trends

Examples

financial analyst at a bank
Many aspiring professionals aim to become a financial analyst at a major investment firm.
She met with her financial analyst to review her portfolio and discuss new investment opportunities.
The financial analyst provided a thorough report on the company's quarterly earnings.

  • degree higher – chief financial officer: senior executive responsible for managing the financial actions of a company
  • synonym – investment analyst: expert who specializes in analyzing investments
