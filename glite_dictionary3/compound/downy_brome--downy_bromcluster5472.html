downy brome

noun
softly hairy Mediterranean grass species
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊni broʊm / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni brəʊm / (BrE)
an annual or winter annual grass with softly hairy leaves, native to the Mediterranean and introduced to many regions as a weed

Examples

downy brome grass weed
Downy brome spreads rapidly and can dominate disturbed grasslands.
Farmers often struggle to control downy brome because it competes with crops for nutrients.
In spring, fields covered with downy brome take on a characteristic pale green hue.
