flying bridge

noun
highest deck above pilot house on a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ brɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ brɪdʒ / (BrE)
a small, often open, deck above the pilot house on a ship, serving as the highest navigational bridge

Examples

on the flying bridge deck
During the storm, the crew secured the equipment stored on the flying bridge.
The captain gave orders from the flying bridge as the ship entered the harbor.
Visitors were allowed to climb up to the flying bridge for a better view of the surroundings.
