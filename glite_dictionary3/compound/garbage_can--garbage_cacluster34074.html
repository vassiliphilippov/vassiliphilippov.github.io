garbage can

noun
container for household waste
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrbɪdʒ kæn / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːbɪdʒ kan / (BrE)
A container, typically with a lid, used for holding household waste until it is collected.

Examples

empty the garbage can

The raccoons knocked over the garbage can last night, scattering trash everywhere.
We put the recycling in the blue garbage can outside.
