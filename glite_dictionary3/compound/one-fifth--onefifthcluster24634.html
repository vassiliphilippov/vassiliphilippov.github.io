one-fifth

noun
one part of five equal parts; 1/5
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈfɪfθ / (AmE), / wʌn ˈfɪfθ / (BrE)
One of five equal parts into which something is or can be divided; 1/5.

Examples

one-fifth of the group
One-fifth of the city’s population is under the age of eighteen.
He donated one-fifth of his salary to charity every month.
Only one-fifth of the applicants were accepted into the program this year.

  • degree lower – one-sixth: one of six equal parts; 1/6
