tree stump

noun
base of a tree left after cutting
Pronunciation: / tri stʌmp / (AmE), / tri stʌmp / (BrE)
The base portion of a tree left remaining after the upper part has been cut down or has fallen.

Examples

old tree stump
After the old oak was cut down, a large tree stump remained in the yard.
Children gathered around the tree stump to play during recess.
The forest path was blocked by a massive tree stump after the storm.
