sound reflection

noun
bouncing of sound waves; echo
Pronunciation: / saʊnd rɪˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / saʊnd rɪˈflɛkʃən / (BrE)
the return or repetition of a sound when sound waves bounce off a surface

Examples

Caves often produce a noticeable sound reflection, making even small noises seem louder.
