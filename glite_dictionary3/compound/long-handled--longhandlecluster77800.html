long-handled

adjective
having a long handle
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ˈhændəld / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈhændld / (BrE)
having a handle that is longer than usual or extended in length

Examples

long-handled pan
A long-handled pan makes it easier to cook over an open fire.
Gardeners often use long-handled tools to avoid bending over.
He swung the long-handled broom to reach the cobwebs near the ceiling.
