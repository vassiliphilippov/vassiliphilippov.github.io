clock radio

noun
radio with built-in clock timer
Pronunciation: / klɑk ˈreɪdioʊ / (AmE), / klɒk ˈreɪdiəʊ / (BrE)
a radio combined with a clock that can be set to switch the radio on or off automatically at a chosen time

Examples

clock radio on bedside
He bought a new clock radio that also has a USB charging port.
She set her clock radio to wake her up at 7 a.m. every morning.
The clock radio on the bedside table began to play music to gently wake him.
