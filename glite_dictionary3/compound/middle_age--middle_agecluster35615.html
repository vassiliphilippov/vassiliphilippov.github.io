middle age

noun
life stage between youth and old age
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdəl eɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl eɪdʒ / (BrE)
the period of life between young adulthood and old age, typically between about 40 and 60 years old
