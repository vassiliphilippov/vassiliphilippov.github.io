to prune the bush

verb
trim or cut back a bush
Pronunciation: / prun ðə bʊʃ / (AmE), / pruːn ðə bʊʃ / (BrE)
to trim or cut back the branches of a bush to improve its shape and growth

Examples

to prune the bush carefully
Every spring, I prune the bush in my front yard to keep it healthy.
Gardeners recommend that you prune the bush before new growth appears in the spring.
If you neglect to prune the bush, it can become tangled and unhealthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prune: to trim a tree, shrub, or bush by cutting away dead or overgrown branches
  • synonym – trim: to make something neat by cutting away unwanted parts
