states' rights

noun
powers reserved to U.S. states by the Constitution
Pronunciation: / steɪts raɪts /; / ˈsteɪts raɪts / (AmE), / steɪts raɪts /; / ˈsteɪts raɪts / (BrE)
the political powers and rights that individual U.S. states have, and the belief that these should not be controlled by the federal government
