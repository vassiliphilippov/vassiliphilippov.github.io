more or less

adverb
to some extent; approximately
Pronunciation: / mɔɹ ər lɛs /; / mɔːr ər lɛs / (AmE), / mɔː ə lɛs /; / mɔːr ɔː lɛs / (BrE)
approximately; not exactly but close enough

Examples

more or less correct
She understood the instructions, more or less, and managed to complete the task.

The repairs will cost $600, more or less, depending on the parts needed.
There were twenty people at the meeting, more or less.
