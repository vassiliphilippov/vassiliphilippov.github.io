sense of movement

noun
awareness of body or limb movement
Pronunciation: / sɛns əv ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / sɛns əv ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
the ability to perceive the movement of one's limbs or body, usually through internal sensory mechanisms

Examples

lost sense of movement
