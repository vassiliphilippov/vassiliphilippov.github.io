Coral Sea

proper noun
naval battle in World War II (May 1942)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəl si / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl siː / (BrE)
the first naval battle in history fought entirely by aircraft based on carriers, taking place in May 1942 during World War II between Japanese and Allied forces, resulting in a Japanese defeat
