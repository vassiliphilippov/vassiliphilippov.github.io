too much

adverb
excessively; more than necessary
Pronunciation: / tuː mʌtʃ / (AmE), / tuː mʌtʃ / (BrE)
to a greater degree or amount than is necessary, desirable, or acceptable; excessively

Examples

talk too much

You added too much sugar to the cake and made it overly sweet.
