closed syllable

noun
syllable ending with a consonant
Pronunciation: / kloʊzd ˈsɪləbəl / (AmE), / kləʊzd ˈsɪləbəl / (BrE)
A syllable that ends with a consonant, typically resulting in a short vowel sound.

Examples

short closed syllable
A closed syllable usually contains a single vowel followed by one or more consonants.
In the word 'cat,' the 'a' is in a closed syllable, which makes it short.
Many English words are divided into closed syllable and open syllable types.
