brood sow

noun
female breeding pig
Pronunciation: / bruːd saʊ / (AmE), / bruːd saʊ / (BrE)
A female pig that is kept and raised specifically for breeding purposes.

Examples

healthy brood sow
Choosing the right brood sow can significantly impact the quality of piglets produced.
Each brood sow was carefully monitored during pregnancy for health and nutrition.
The farmer invested in a new brood sow to improve his herd.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sow: an adult female pig, especially one that has given birth
