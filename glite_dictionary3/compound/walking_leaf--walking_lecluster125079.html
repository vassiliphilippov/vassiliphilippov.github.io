walking leaf

noun
tropical insect mimicking a leaf
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkɪŋ lif / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ liːf / (BrE)
A tropical insect that resembles a flattened leaf, found commonly in southern Asia and the East Indies.
