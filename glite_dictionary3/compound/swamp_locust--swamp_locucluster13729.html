swamp locust

noun
honey locust tree of southern U.S. swamps
Pronunciation: / swɑmp ˈloʊkəst / (AmE), / swɒmp ˈləʊkəst / (BrE)
A species of honey locust tree found in swamps and bottomlands of the southern United States, known for its short oval pods and heavy dark wood.
