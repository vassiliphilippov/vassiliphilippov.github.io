cubic decimeter

noun
metric unit of volume (1,000 cm³)
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈdiː.sɪˌmiː.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˈdiː.sɪˌmiː.tə / (BrE)
