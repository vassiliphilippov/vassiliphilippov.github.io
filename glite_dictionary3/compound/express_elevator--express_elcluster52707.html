express elevator

noun
elevator that skips floors for faster access
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛs ˈɛləˌveɪtɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs ˈɛlɪˌveɪtə / (BrE)
an elevator in a multi-story building that travels directly to certain higher floors without stopping at intermediate ones

Examples

take the express elevator to the top
Only residents with key cards can use the express elevator to reach the penthouse suites.
The new office tower features an express elevator that takes employees directly to the upper floors.
We waited a few minutes for the express elevator rather than stopping at every single floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym – local elevator: an elevator that stops at every floor
