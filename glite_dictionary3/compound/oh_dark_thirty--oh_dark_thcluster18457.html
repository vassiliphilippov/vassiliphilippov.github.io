oh dark thirty

noun
very early time in the morning
Pronunciation: / oʊ dɑrk ˈθɜːrti / (AmE), / əʊ dɑːk ˈθɜːti / (BrE)
A humorous term for a very early time in the morning, typically around 12:30 a.m. or before dawn, used especially in military contexts.
