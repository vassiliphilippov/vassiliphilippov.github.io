Takayasu's arteritis

noun
rare inflammatory disease of large arteries
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːkaɪˈjɑːsuːz ɑːrˈtɛrɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌtækəˈjɑːsuːz ɑːˈtɛrɪtɪs / (BrE)
a rare inflammatory disease of the large blood vessels, especially the aorta and its main branches, often resulting in absence of pulse in the arms and carotid arteries
