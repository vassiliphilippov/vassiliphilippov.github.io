Gulf States

proper noun
countries bordering the Persian Gulf
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf steɪts / (AmE), / ɡʌlf steɪts / (BrE)
the countries in southwestern Asia that border the Persian Gulf, typically including Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar, the United Arab Emirates, and Oman

Gulf States in the Middle East
