Mogul empire

noun
historic empire in India (1526–1857)
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊɡəl ˈɛmˌpaɪr / (AmE), / ˈməʊɡəl ˈɛmˌpaɪə / (BrE)
the empire established by the Mogul (Mughal) conquerors of India, ruling from 1526 to 1857

Examples
