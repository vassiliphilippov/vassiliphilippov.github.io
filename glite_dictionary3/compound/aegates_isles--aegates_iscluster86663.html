Aegates Isles

proper noun
islands west of Sicily, site of historic naval battle
Pronunciation: / iˈɡeɪtiːz aɪlz / (AmE), / iˈɡeɪtiːz aɪlz / (BrE)
a group of islands west of Sicily, now known as the Egadi Islands, famous as the site of a decisive Roman naval victory over Carthage in 241 BC that ended the First Punic War
