mucous colitis

noun
recurrent abdominal pain and bowel disturbance
Pronunciation: / ˈmjukəs koʊˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˈmjuːkəs kəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition marked by recurrent abdominal pain, diarrhea or constipation, often associated with emotional stress; now commonly referred to as irritable bowel syndrome.

Examples

mucous colitis with cramps
After years of abdominal pain, Mark was finally diagnosed with mucous colitis.
Doctors now often refer to mucous colitis as irritable bowel syndrome.
Symptoms of mucous colitis can worsen during times of stress.
