on the cusp of

preposition
at the point of transition or verge of change
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə kʌsp ʌv / (AmE), / ɒn ðə kʌsp ɒv / (BrE)

Examples

The company is on the cusp of launching a revolutionary new product.
