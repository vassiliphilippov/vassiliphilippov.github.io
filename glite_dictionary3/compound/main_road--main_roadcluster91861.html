main road

noun
major road for motor traffic
Pronunciation: / meɪn roʊd / (AmE), / meɪn rəʊd / (BrE)
A major road used for motor traffic, especially one connecting towns or serving as the principal route through an area.

Examples

busy main road
Heavy traffic is common on the main road during rush hour.
Our house is just off the main road that leads into the city.
They built a new supermarket beside the main road to attract more customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – highway: a main public road, especially in the US
  • antonym – side road: a minor or less important road joining a main road
