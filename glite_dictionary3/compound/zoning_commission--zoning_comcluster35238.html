zoning commission

noun
board supervising local land use zones
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊnɪŋ kəˌmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈzəʊnɪŋ kəˌmɪʃən / (BrE)
an official board responsible for supervising the designation of land for residential, commercial, or other uses within a city or municipality

Examples

zoning commission meeting
Any major construction project must first receive approval from the local zoning commission.
Residents voiced their concerns about the proposed changes at the zoning commission meeting.
The zoning commission approved plans for a new apartment complex in the downtown district.
