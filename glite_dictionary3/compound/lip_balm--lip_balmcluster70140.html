lip balm

noun
salve for preventing or healing chapped lips
Pronunciation: / lɪp bɑm / (AmE), / lɪp bɑːm / (BrE)
A soft substance applied to the lips to prevent or heal chapping or dryness.

Examples

apply lip balm
My lips were so dry that I had to put on some lip balm several times a day.
She always carries a lip balm in her purse, especially during the winter.
Using lip balm regularly can help prevent chapped or cracked lips.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chapstick: a trademarked brand often used generically for lip balm
  • synonym – lipstick: a cosmetic applied to the lips for color and protection
