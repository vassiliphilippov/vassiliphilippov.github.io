think tank

noun
organization for research and policy advice
Pronunciation: / θɪŋk tæŋk / (AmE), / θɪŋk taŋk / (BrE)
An organization or group of experts that carries out research and provides advice on particular economic, political, or social problems.

Examples

think tank report
Several think tanks released studies on climate change policy this year.
She works at a think tank specializing in international economics.
The government hired a think tank to analyze the proposed reforms.
