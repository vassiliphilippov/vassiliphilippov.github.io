turnip-rooted parsley

noun
parsley with an edible, parsnip-like root
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnɪp ˌrutɪd ˈpɑrsli / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪp ˌruːtɪd ˈpɑːsli / (BrE)
A plant with smooth leaves and an enlarged edible white taproot, resembling a parsnip but belonging to the parsley family.
