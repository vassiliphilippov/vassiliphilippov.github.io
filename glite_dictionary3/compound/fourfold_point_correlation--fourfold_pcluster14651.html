fourfold point correlation

noun
statistical index for binary variable relations
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌfoʊld pɔɪnt ˌkɔrəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɔːfəʊld pɔɪnt ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
an index measuring the relationship between two sets of scores, each represented as ordered binary dimensions, such as male-female.
