wild celery

noun
a type of aquatic plant
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈsɛləri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈsɛləri / (BrE)
a submerged aquatic plant with long, narrow leaves, found in Europe, Asia, and Australia; often referring to various species of the genus Vallisneria
