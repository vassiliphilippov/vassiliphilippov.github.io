globohomo view

noun
belief in global cultural homogeneity
Pronunciation: / ˌɡloʊboʊˈhoʊmoʊ vjuː / (AmE), / ˌɡləʊbəʊˈhəʊməʊ vjuː / (BrE)
A controversial term, often used disparagingly, to describe the belief in or promotion of global cultural homogeneity at the expense of local traditions.
