traditional clog

noun
Dutch wooden shoe
Pronunciation: / trəˈdɪʃ.ən.əl klɑːɡ / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃ.ən.əl klɒɡ / (BrE)
A type of wooden shoe traditionally worn in the Netherlands, usually made from a single piece of wood.

Examples

traditional clog from Netherlands
Farmers in rural Dutch villages still wear traditional clogs when working in muddy fields.
On her trip to Amsterdam, she bought a pair of traditional clogs as a souvenir.
The dancers performed in brightly painted traditional clogs during the folk festival.
