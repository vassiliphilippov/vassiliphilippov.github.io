fingerprint expert

noun
specialist in fingerprint identification
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚˌprɪnt ˈɛkspɚt / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌprɪnt ˈɛkspɜːt / (BrE)
a person trained to identify, analyze, and compare fingerprints for the purposes of identification, typically in forensic investigations

Examples

fingerprint expert analyzes evidence
A fingerprint expert can often identify a suspect with remarkable accuracy.
After years of training, she became the leading fingerprint expert in the department.
The prosecution called a fingerprint expert to testify about the evidence found at the crime scene.
