pas de deux

noun
ballet duet for two dancers
Pronunciation: / pɑ də ˈdu / (AmE), / pɑː də ˈdɜː / (BrE)
A dance duet for two performers, especially a ballerina and a male dancer, in ballet.

Examples

pas de deux in ballet
She trained for months to perfect every movement in the challenging pas de deux.
The principal dancers performed a breathtaking pas de deux in the second act.
Their pas de deux captivated the audience with its grace and precision.
