God Emperor

noun
deified supreme ruler
Pronunciation: / ɡɑd ˈɛmpərər / (AmE), / ɡɒd ˈɛmp(ə)rər / (BrE)
A supreme ruler who is worshipped as a deity, often possessing both political and religious authority.

Examples

the God Emperor rules
As a child, she read tales about the God Emperor and his legendary empire.
In ancient myth, the God Emperor was believed to rule both the heavens and the earth.
The God Emperor issued decrees that no one dared to question.
