parhelic ring

noun
horizontal halo at sun's altitude
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈhɛlɪk rɪŋ / (AmE), / pɑːˈhiːlɪk rɪŋ / (BrE)
A luminous, horizontal halo appearing parallel to the horizon at the same altitude as the sun, caused by sunlight interacting with ice crystals in the atmosphere.
