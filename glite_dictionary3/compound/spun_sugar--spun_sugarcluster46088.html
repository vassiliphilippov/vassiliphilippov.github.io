spun sugar

noun
sugar spun into fine threads as candy
Pronunciation: / spʌn ˈʃʌɡɚ / (AmE), / spʌn ˈʃʌɡə / (BrE)
A sweet made by spinning sugar that has been boiled to a high temperature, forming fine threads or filaments, often used for decoration or as a confection.

Examples

spun sugar decoration
Children watched in awe as the confectioner created a web of spun sugar at the fair.
Her wedding cake was crowned with a golden nest of spun sugar.
The chef decorated the dessert with delicate strands of spun sugar for an elegant touch.
