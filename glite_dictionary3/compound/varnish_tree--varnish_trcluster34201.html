varnish tree

noun
Asian tree yielding lacquer sap
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑrnɪʃ tri / (AmE), / ˈvɑːnɪʃ triː / (BrE)
a small Asian tree that produces a poisonous sap used to make lacquer
