bodily cavity

noun
a natural hollow or space in the body
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdəli ˈkævəti / (AmE), / ˈbɒdəli ˈkævəti / (BrE)
(anatomy) a hollow space or compartment within the body, such as the chest or abdominal cavity, containing organs.

Examples
