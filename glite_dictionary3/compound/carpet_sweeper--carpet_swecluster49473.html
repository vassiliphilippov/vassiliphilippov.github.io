carpet sweeper

noun
device for cleaning carpets
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌswipər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪt ˌswiːpə / (BrE)
a cleaning device with revolving brushes, used for removing dirt from carpets by pushing it over the surface

Examples

use a carpet sweeper on rugs
My grandmother still prefers her old carpet sweeper for tidying rugs instead of modern machines.
She used a carpet sweeper to clean the living room floor quickly before her guests arrived.
Unlike a vacuum, a carpet sweeper makes very little noise while picking up crumbs and dust.
