milk yield

noun
amount of milk produced by a cow or herd
Pronunciation: / mɪlk jiːld / (AmE), / mɪlk jiːld / (BrE)
the amount of milk produced by a cow or herd within a specific period.

Examples

milk yield per cow
Advancements in breeding have led to higher milk yields over the past decade.
Farmers track the milk yield of each animal to optimize production.
The milk yield of dairy cows can vary depending on their diet and health.
