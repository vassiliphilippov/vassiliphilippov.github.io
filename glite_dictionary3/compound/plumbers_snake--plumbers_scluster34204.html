plumber's snake

noun
tool for clearing pipe blockages
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmɚz sneɪk / (AmE), / ˈplʌməz sneɪk / (BrE)
a long, flexible steel coil used to clear blockages from pipes, especially in plumbing systems

Examples

plumber's snake clears pipes
A plumber's snake is essential for tackling stubborn blockages in household pipes.
After several failed attempts with a plunger, he resorted to a plumber's snake.
The plumber's snake successfully removed the clog from the kitchen sink drain.
