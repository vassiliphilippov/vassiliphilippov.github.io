stinging nettle

noun
a plant with stinging hairs and toothed leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋɪŋ ˈnɛtəl / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋɪŋ ˈnɛtəl / (BrE)
a perennial plant with coarsely toothed leaves covered in stinging hairs that cause skin irritation on contact, native to Eurasia and now common in North America

Examples
