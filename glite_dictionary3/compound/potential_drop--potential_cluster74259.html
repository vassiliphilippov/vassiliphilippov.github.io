potential drop

noun
difference in electrical potential
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɛnʃəl drɑp / (AmE), / pəˈtɛnʃəl drɒp / (BrE)

potential drop across wires

Engineers monitor the potential drop to ensure the circuit functions efficiently.
