photo opportunity

noun
occasion arranged for publicity photos
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.t̬oʊ ˌɑp.əˈtuː.nə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.təʊ ˌɒp.əˈtjuː.nə.ti / (BrE)
an occasion arranged or suitable for taking photographs, especially one intended to give favorable publicity to the people being photographed

Examples

Critics called the charity event nothing more than a photo opportunity for celebrities.
Reporters gathered for the royal couple's photo opportunity during their visit to the hospital.
