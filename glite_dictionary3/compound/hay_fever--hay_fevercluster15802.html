hay fever

noun
allergic reaction to pollen
Pronunciation: / heɪ ˈfiːvɚ / (AmE), / heɪ ˈfiːvə / (BrE)
An allergy causing inflammation of the nose and eyes, typically triggered by pollen and occurring seasonally.

Examples

suffer from hay fever
Every spring, my hay fever symptoms worsen because the pollen count is so high.
People with hay fever often experience sneezing, a runny nose, and itchy eyes.
She takes antihistamines to manage her hay fever during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allergic rhinitis: inflammation of the nasal passages caused by allergies
