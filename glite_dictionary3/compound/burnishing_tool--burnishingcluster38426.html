burnishing tool

noun
tool for polishing gold leaf in gilding
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːnɪʃɪŋ tuːl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪʃɪŋ tuːl / (BrE)
a hand tool with a polished agate or gemstone tip, used to rub and produce a highly polished surface on gold leaf in gilding work

Examples

burnishing tool for gold leaf
Careful handling of the burnishing tool is essential to avoid damaging delicate gilded surfaces.
She selected a burnishing tool with an agate tip to finish the restoration of the manuscript's golden border.
The artisan used a burnishing tool to give the gold leaf a mirror-like sheen on the frame.
