Abbe condenser

noun
microscope lens system for focusing light
Pronunciation: / ˈæbi kənˈdɛnsɚ / (AmE), / ˈæbi kənˈdɛnsə / (BrE)
A microscope condenser with two or three wide-aperture lenses, used to focus light onto the specimen.

Examples

Abbe condenser focus light
Adjusting the Abbe condenser can significantly improve image clarity when examining slides.
Many modern microscopes come equipped with an Abbe condenser as a standard feature.
