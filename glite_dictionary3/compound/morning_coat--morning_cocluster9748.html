morning coat

noun
formal men's jacket with tails
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ koʊt / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ kəʊt / (BrE)
a man's formal jacket with a cutaway front and two long tapering tails at the back, traditionally worn for daytime formal occasions

Examples

wear a morning coat
A traditional morning coat is often paired with a top hat and waistcoat.
He looked elegant in his morning coat and striped trousers at the wedding.
The men wore morning coats to the formal daytime reception.
