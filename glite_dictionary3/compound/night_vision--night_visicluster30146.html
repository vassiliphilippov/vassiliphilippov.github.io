night vision

noun
ability to see in low light
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt ˌvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt ˌvɪʒən / (BrE)
the ability to see in low light or darkness, such as by moonlight or under minimal illumination

Examples

animal night vision
A lack of vitamin A can affect your night vision, making it harder to see at dusk.
Some animals, like owls, have excellent night vision that allows them to hunt in the dark.
Wearing special goggles can enhance your night vision during nighttime hiking.
