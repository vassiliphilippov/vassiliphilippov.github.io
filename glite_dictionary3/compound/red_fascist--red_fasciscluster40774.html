red fascist

noun
person or policy with far-left authoritarian views
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈfæʃɪst / (AmE), / rɛd ˈfæʃɪst / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a person or policy espousing far-left authoritarian or oppressive views, often equating them with fascism.

She rejected the label of red fascist, insisting her actions were in defense of democracy.
Some accused the government of acting like a red fascist regime during the crackdown.
