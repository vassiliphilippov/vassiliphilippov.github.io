pear haw

noun
American hawthorn with pear-shaped berries
Pronunciation: / pɛr hɑ / (AmE), / pɛə hɔː / (BrE)
An erect, mostly thornless species of American hawthorn that produces pear-shaped berries.

Examples

pear haw tree berries
The pear haw is a native plant admired for its ornamental berries in autumn.
Unlike some other hawthorns, the pear haw has very few thorns and is easier to handle.
We planted a pear haw in the backyard to attract birds and pollinators.
