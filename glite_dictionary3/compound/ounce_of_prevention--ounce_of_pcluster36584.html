ounce of prevention

noun
small preventive action
Pronunciation: / aʊns əv prɪˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / aʊns əv prɪˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
a small action or measure taken to prevent problems or difficulties

Examples

an ounce of prevention for illness
A regular exercise routine can be an ounce of prevention for many health issues.
Applying sunscreen before going outdoors is an ounce of prevention against sunburn.
Installing smoke detectors is one simple ounce of prevention that can save lives.
