manual labor

noun
work involving physical effort
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.ju.əl ˈleɪ.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈmæn.ju.əl ˈleɪ.bə / (BrE)
work that is done by hand or with physical effort, not by machines or using mental skills
