gas line

noun
queue of vehicles for gasoline
Pronunciation: / ɡæs laɪn / (AmE), / ɡas laɪn / (BrE)
a line of vehicles waiting to buy gasoline, especially at a filling station during a fuel shortage

Examples

wait in gas line
During the fuel crisis, we had to wait in a gas line for over two hours.
Local news reported on the frustration felt by those stuck in long gas lines last weekend.
The gas line stretched around the block as drivers tried to fill their tanks before the shortage worsened.
