fly ash

noun
fine ash particles from burned fuel
Pronunciation: / flaɪ æʃ / (AmE), / flaɪ æʃ / (BrE)
fine particles of ash carried into the air during the combustion of fuel, especially in power stations

Examples

fly ash in concrete
Concrete made with fly ash is often stronger and more durable.
