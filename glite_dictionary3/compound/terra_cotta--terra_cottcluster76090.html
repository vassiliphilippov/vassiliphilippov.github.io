terra cotta

noun
brownish-red earthenware
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrə ˈkɑtə / (AmE), / ˌtɛrə ˈkɒtə / (BrE)
a hard, usually unglazed, brownish-red earthenware used for pottery, architectural decoration, and sculptures
