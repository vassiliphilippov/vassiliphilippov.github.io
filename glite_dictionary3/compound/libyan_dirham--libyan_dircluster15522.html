Libyan dirham

noun
monetary unit of Libya
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbiən dɪrˈhæm / (AmE), / ˈlɪbiən dɪˈrɑːm / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Libya, equal to one hundredth of a dinar.

Examples

one Libyan dirham coin
One Libyan dirham is worth one hundredth of a Libyan dinar.
Prices for small items are often given in Libyan dirhams rather than dinars.
The cashier handed me twenty Libyan dirhams in change after my purchase.
