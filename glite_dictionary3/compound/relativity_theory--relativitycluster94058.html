relativity theory

noun
a physics theory about relativity of space and time
Pronunciation: / rɛˈlæt̬.əˌvɪ.ti ˈθi.ə.ri / (AmE), / rɪˈlæt.ɪ.və.ti ˈθɪə.ri / (BrE)
a scientific theory in physics stating that space and time are relative concepts, not absolute, most notably developed by Albert Einstein
