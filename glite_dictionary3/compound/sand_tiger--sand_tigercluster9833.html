sand tiger

noun
shallow-water shark sometimes dangerous to swimmers
Pronunciation: / sænd ˈtaɪɡɚ / (AmE), / sænd ˈtaɪɡə / (BrE)
a large, shallow-water shark with sharp, jagged teeth, found in the Atlantic Ocean and sometimes considered dangerous to swimmers
