free pass

noun
complimentary admission ticket or permission
Pronunciation: / fri pæs / (AmE), / friː pɑːs / (BrE)
A ticket or permission allowing someone to enter a place or use a service without paying.

Examples

free pass to the zoo
After winning the contest, he received a free pass to the amusement park.
Her friend surprised her with a free pass to the theater's opening night.
The museum offers a free pass to all local schoolchildren.

  • synonym – complimentary ticket: A ticket given for free to provide admission
