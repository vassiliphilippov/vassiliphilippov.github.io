play group

noun
informal nursery group for young children
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪ ˌɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ ˌɡruːp / (BrE)
a small informal group for young children, usually meeting for part of the day to play and learn together before school age

Examples

a play group for toddlers
My daughter has made lots of friends at her play group.
She volunteers to help run the morning play group in her neighborhood.
The local community center hosts a weekly play group for toddlers and their parents.
