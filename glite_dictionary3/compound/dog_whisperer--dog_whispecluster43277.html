dog whisperer

noun
expert in dog training
Pronunciation: / dɔg ˈwɪspɚɚ / (AmE), / dɒg ˈwɪspərə / (BrE)
a person who is especially skilled at training and handling dogs, often using gentle, intuitive methods

Examples

dog whisperer trains pets

The neighborhood dog whisperer has helped dozens of owners with their unruly pets.
