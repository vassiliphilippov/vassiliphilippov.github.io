Uncle Sam

noun
personification of the U.S. government
Pronunciation: / ˌʌŋkəl ˈsæm / (AmE), / ˌʌŋkl ˈsæm / (BrE)
a personification of the United States government, often depicted as a tall, elderly man with a white beard, wearing a top hat and star-spangled suit

Examples

Uncle Sam wants taxes
During tax season, Americans often joke that Uncle Sam wants more of their money.
Many people associate Uncle Sam with calls for patriotism and support of the military.
