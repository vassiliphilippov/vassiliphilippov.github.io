hierarchical menu

noun
computer menu with secondary submenus
Pronunciation: / haɪˌrɑr.kɪ.kəl ˈmɛn.ju / (AmE), / haɪəˌrɑː.kɪ.kəl ˈmen.juː / (BrE)
a menu in a computer program that displays secondary menus when a user hovers over or selects certain items on the primary menu

Examples

open a hierarchical menu
Hover your mouse over the main option to reveal its hierarchical menu of advanced settings.
Reorganizing the hierarchical menu structure made the application much easier to use.
The software features a hierarchical menu that lets users navigate quickly to any function.

  • synonym – submenu: a menu that is contained within another menu
