Benedictine order

noun
Catholic monastic order founded in the 6th century
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnəˈdɪktɪn ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈdɪktɪn ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
A Roman Catholic monastic order founded in the 6th century, known for its liturgical worship and scholarly activities.
