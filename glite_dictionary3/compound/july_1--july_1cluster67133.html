July 1

proper noun
Canadian national holiday; Canada Day
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈlaɪ fɜrst / (AmE), / dʒʊˈlaɪ fɜːst / (BrE)
The legal holiday in Canada commemorating the anniversary of the formation of the Dominion of Canada in 1867, now known as Canada Day.

July 1 celebrations
Every year on July 1, Canadians celebrate their nation’s history and heritage.
