safe space

noun
environment free from judgment or discrimination
Pronunciation: / seɪf speɪs / (AmE), / seɪf speɪs / (BrE)
a place or environment where a person or group can feel confident that they will not be exposed to discrimination, criticism, or any emotional or physical harm

Examples

a safe space for discussion
Many support groups are designed as a safe space for open discussions.
She valued the therapy office as her personal safe space to share fears and struggles.
Universities often aim to provide a safe space for students to express their identities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hostile environment: a place where people feel threatened or unsafe
  • synonym – sanctuary: a place of refuge or safety
