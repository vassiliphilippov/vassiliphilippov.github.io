life-support system

noun
machine aiding vital bodily functions
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf səˌpɔrt ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf səˌpɔːt ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
equipment that keeps a person alive by providing the necessary conditions for life when natural functions or the environment are not enough (such as in hospitals, space, or underwater)
