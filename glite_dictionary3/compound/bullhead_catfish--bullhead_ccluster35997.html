bullhead catfish

noun
a type of North American freshwater catfish
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌhɛd ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌhɛd ˈkatˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a common name for several species of small, stout North American freshwater catfish of the genus Ameiurus, characterized by a broad, flat head and barbels around the mouth
