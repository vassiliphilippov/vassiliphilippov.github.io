fish hawk

noun
large hawk that feeds on fish; osprey
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ hɑk / (AmE), / fɪʃ hɔːk / (BrE)
a large hawk that feeds mainly on fish, especially the osprey, found worldwide and known for its bulky, long-used nests
