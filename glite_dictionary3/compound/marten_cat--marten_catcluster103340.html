marten cat

noun
arboreal carnivorous mammal, marten
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtən kæt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtən kat / (BrE)
A small, agile, slender-bodied carnivorous mammal of the weasel family, typically arboreal and somewhat larger than a weasel; a marten.

Examples

wild marten cat
Farmers sometimes mistake a marten cat for a domestic cat due to its size and agility.
The marten cat moves swiftly through the branches, hunting for prey.
The fur of a marten cat was once highly valued in the fur trade.
