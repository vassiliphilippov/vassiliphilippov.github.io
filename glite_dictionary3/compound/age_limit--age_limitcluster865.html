age limit

noun
maximum or minimum permitted age for an activity or role
Pronunciation: / eɪdʒ ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / eɪdʒ ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
A regulation or rule that sets the maximum or minimum age at which a person is allowed to do something or hold a particular position.

Examples

age limit for drivers
He could not enter the contest because he was above the age limit.
Many sports events have an age limit to ensure fair competition.
There is an upper age limit for joining the military in most countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym – age restriction: a restriction that is based on age; commonly used synonym
