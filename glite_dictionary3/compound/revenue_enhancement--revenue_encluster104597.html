revenue enhancement

noun
business strategies to increase revenue
Pronunciation: / \u02c8r\u025bv\u0259\u02ccnu \u026an\u02c8h\u00e6ns\u00b7m\u0259nt / (AmE), / \u02c8r\u025bv.ju\u02d0 \u026an\u02c8h\u0251\u02d0ns.m\u0259nt / (BrE)
a plan, set of strategies, or specific initiatives a business uses to increase its revenue, such as pricing changes, product bundling, or upselling.

Examples

revenue enhancement business plan
The board approved a revenue enhancement plan to boost quarterly earnings through new pricing tiers.
Their marketing team proposed several revenue enhancements including bundles, loyalty discounts, and targeted upsells for high-value customers.
During the meeting the CFO outlined revenue enhancement strategies like dynamic pricing and improved product packaging.
