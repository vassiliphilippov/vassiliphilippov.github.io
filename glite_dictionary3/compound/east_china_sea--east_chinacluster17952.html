East China Sea

proper noun
sea in the western Pacific Ocean near eastern Asia
Pronunciation: / ist ˈtʃaɪnə si / (AmE), / iːst ˈtʃaɪnə siː / (BrE)
a marginal sea in the western Pacific Ocean, located between China, Korea, Japan, and Taiwan

Examples

the East China Sea coast
Many oil and gas reserves are believed to be located beneath the East China Sea.
Tensions have risen over fishing rights in the East China Sea.
The East China Sea is bordered by China to the west and Japan to the east.
