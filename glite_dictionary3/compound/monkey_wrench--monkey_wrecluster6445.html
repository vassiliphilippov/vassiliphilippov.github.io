monkey wrench

noun
adjustable wrench with one movable jaw
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki rɛntʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki rɛntʃ / (BrE)
a type of adjustable tool with one fixed and one movable jaw, used for gripping and turning objects
