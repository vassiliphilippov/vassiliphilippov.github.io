goa bean vine

noun
Old World tropical vine; winged bean plant
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ.ə ˌbin ˌvaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ.ə ˌbiːn ˌvaɪn / (BrE)
a twining annual plant from the Old World tropics, bearing clusters of purplish flowers and distinctive pods with four jagged wings; also called winged bean, used for its edible parts
