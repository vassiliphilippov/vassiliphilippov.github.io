lawn cart

noun
small wheelbarrow for gardening loads
Pronunciation: / lɔn kɑrt / (AmE), / lɔːn kɑːt / (BrE)
a small cart with handles and one or more wheels, used for carrying gardening materials and small loads.

Examples

lawn cart for grass clippings
After mowing, she collected the grass clippings in the lawn cart for disposal.
He loaded the bags of mulch onto the lawn cart before spreading them around the trees.
We used the lawn cart to move soil from the shed to the garden beds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – wheelbarrow: a small cart with a single wheel, used for carrying loads
