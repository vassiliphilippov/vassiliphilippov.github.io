Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus

proper noun
self-declared state in Northern Cyprus
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrkɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈnɔrðərn ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈnɔːðən ˈsaɪprəs / (BrE)
