playing field

noun
conditions for competition, esp. fairness
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪɪŋ fiːld / (AmE), / ˈpleɪɪŋ fiːld / (BrE)
the conditions or circumstances in which competitors operate, especially regarding fairness or equality

Examples

level playing field
Reforms are necessary to ensure a level playing field in access to government contracts.
Smaller firms often struggle when the playing field is tilted in favor of large corporations.
The new trade regulations are designed to create a playing field where all companies can compete fairly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – level playing field: a situation in which everyone has the same opportunity
