lawn bowling

noun
outdoor game rolling balls at a jack
Pronunciation: / lɔn ˈboʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / lɔːn ˈbəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
A game played on a level lawn in which players roll biased wooden balls to rest near a smaller white ball called a jack.

Examples

play lawn bowling outdoors
Every Saturday morning, the club hosts lawn bowling tournaments for its members.
She learned the basics of lawn bowling from her grandfather, who was a champion player.
The rules of lawn bowling are simple but require great precision and strategy.
