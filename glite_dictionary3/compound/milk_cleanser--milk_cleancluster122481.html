milk cleanser

noun
creamy facial cleanser
Pronunciation: / mɪlk ˈklɛnzɚ / (AmE), / mɪlk ˈklɛnzə / (BrE)
A face cleanser with a creamy, milk-like texture, used to remove makeup and impurities from the skin.

Examples

gentle milk cleanser
After wearing heavy makeup all day, she used a milk cleanser to gently clean her face.
Many people with sensitive skin prefer using a milk cleanser instead of foam-based washes.
The skincare routine recommends starting with a milk cleanser to dissolve oils and impurities.
