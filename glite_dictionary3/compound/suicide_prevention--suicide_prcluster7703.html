suicide prevention

noun
measures to reduce suicide risk
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.aɪ.saɪd prɪˈvɛn.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈsuː.ɪ.saɪd prɪˈvɛn.ʃən / (BrE)
the practice or policy of taking measures intended to reduce the risk or incidence of suicide

Examples

suicide prevention program
Many schools have introduced suicide prevention programs to support students' mental health.
The government launched a national suicide prevention campaign to raise public awareness.
Training in suicide prevention is recommended for all healthcare professionals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – suicide intervention: actions taken during a suicidal crisis to prevent suicide
  • synonym – suicide prevention hotline: a telephone service that provides support to people at risk of suicide
