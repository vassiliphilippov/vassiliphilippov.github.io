nun buoy

noun
conical navigational buoy
Pronunciation: / nʌn ˈbɔɪ / (AmE), / nʌn ˈbɔɪ / (BrE)
A conical buoy used as a navigational marker, typically to mark channels or hazards at sea.

Examples

nun buoy marks channel
Sailors are trained to recognize a nun buoy by its distinctive conical shape.
