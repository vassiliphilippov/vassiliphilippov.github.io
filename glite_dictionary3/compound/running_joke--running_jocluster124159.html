running joke

noun
repeatedly mentioned joke or humorous topic
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ dʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a joke or humorous topic that is repeatedly referred to over a period of time, especially among a particular group

Examples

running joke at work
Her punctuality, or lack thereof, became a running joke in the office.
It was a running joke in our group that Tom would always forget his wallet.
The chef's overuse of garlic turned into a running joke among the restaurant staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gag: a joke, especially one in a performance
  • synonym – in-joke: a private joke understood only by a particular group
