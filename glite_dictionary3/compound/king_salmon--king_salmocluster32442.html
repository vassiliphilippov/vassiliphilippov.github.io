king salmon

noun
flesh of a large Pacific salmon (as food)
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ ˈsæmən / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈsæmən / (BrE)
The pink or white flesh of a large Pacific salmon, valued as food.

Examples

king salmon fillet
At the market, she bought a fillet of fresh king salmon for dinner.
Sushi chefs often prefer king salmon for its rich flavor and buttery texture.
The grilled king salmon was perfectly seasoned and flaky.
