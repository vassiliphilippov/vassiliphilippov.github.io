band-tail pigeon

noun
wild pigeon of western North America
Pronunciation: / ˈbænd teɪl ˈpɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈbænd teɪl ˈpɪdʒən / (BrE)
A wild pigeon native to western North America, larger than the typical city pigeon, often mistaken for the extinct passenger pigeon.
