crepe myrtle

noun
ornamental shrub with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / kreɪp ˈmɜːrtəl / (AmE), / kreɪp ˈmɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
An ornamental shrub or small tree of the genus Lagerstroemia, native to Asia and widely grown for its showy clusters of flowers and attractive bark, especially in warm climates.
