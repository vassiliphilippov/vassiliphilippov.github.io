on repeat

adverb
played continuously or repeatedly
Pronunciation: / ɑn rɪˈpiːt / (AmE), / ɒn rɪˈpiːt / (BrE)
(of a song, video, etc.) played continuously or repeatedly, especially using a playback device's repeat function.

Examples

song on repeat
I've had that song on repeat all week because I can't get enough of it.
She listened to his voicemail on repeat, missing his voice every time.
The movie played on repeat in the background while we worked.
