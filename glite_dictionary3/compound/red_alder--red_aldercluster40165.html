red alder

noun
tree with reddish wood used for furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd ˈɔldɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd ˈɔːldə / (BrE)
A large tree native to the Pacific coast of North America, known for its hard reddish wood, often used in furniture making.

Examples

tall red alder tree
