herbal therapy

noun
use of plants or plant extracts as medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrbəl ˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəl ˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the use of plants or plant extracts for medicinal purposes, especially those not part of a normal diet
