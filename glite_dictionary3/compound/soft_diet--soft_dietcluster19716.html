soft diet

noun
diet of easily-chewed foods
Pronunciation: / sɔft ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / sɒft ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet consisting of foods that are easy to chew and swallow, often recommended for people with digestive or chewing problems

Examples

soft diet after surgery
After his dental surgery, the doctor recommended a soft diet for a week.
Mashed potatoes are a common item included in a soft diet.
People with swallowing difficulties are often placed on a soft diet by their physicians.
