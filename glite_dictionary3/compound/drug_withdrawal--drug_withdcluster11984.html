drug withdrawal

noun
official removal of a drug
Pronunciation: / dr\u028c\u0261 w\u026a\u00f0\u02c8dr\u0254\u02d0.\u0259l / (AmE), / dr\u028c\u0261 w\u026a\u00f0\u02c8dr\u0254\u02d0.\u0259l / (BrE)
an official removal or recall of a pharmaceutical drug from the market by a regulator or manufacturer, typically because of safety, quality, or efficacy concerns.

Examples

official drug withdrawal
The agency announced an immediate drug withdrawal after new evidence linked the treatment to rare heart problems.
Patients awaiting the medication were notified of the drug withdrawal and advised to consult their doctors immediately.
The manufacturer initiated a voluntary drug withdrawal while it investigated reports of unexpected side effects overseas.

  • synonym – recall: an official action to remove a product from the market because of safety, defect, or regulatory concerns
