club fungus

noun
fungus with a club-shaped body
Pronunciation: / klʌb ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / klʌb ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
A fungus with a club-shaped fruiting body, especially a member of the phylum Basidiomycota.
