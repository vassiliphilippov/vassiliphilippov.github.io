prompter's box

noun
booth in front of stage for theater prompter
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːmptərz bɑːks / (AmE), / ˈprɒmptəz bɒks / (BrE)
A small booth projecting above the floor in front of a theater stage, where the prompter sits to cue actors during a performance.
