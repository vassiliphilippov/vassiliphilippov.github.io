Gulf War

noun
1980–88 war between Iran and Iraq
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf wɔr / (AmE), / ɡʌlf wɔː / (BrE)
the armed conflict between Iraq and Iran from 1980 to 1988, fought primarily over control of strategic waterways and territory

Examples

the Gulf War with Iran
Many books have analyzed the political impact of the Gulf War on the Middle East.
The Gulf War caused significant economic and human losses for both Iran and Iraq.
The cease-fire that ended the Gulf War was brokered by the United Nations in 1988.
