cultural movement

noun
group working to advance cultural goals
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a group effort by people aiming to promote specific cultural values, ideas, or practices, often leading to widespread change in society
