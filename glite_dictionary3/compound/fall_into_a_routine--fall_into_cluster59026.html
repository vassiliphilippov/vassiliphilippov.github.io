to fall into a routine

verb
begin a regular pattern of behavior
Pronunciation: / fɔl ˈɪntu ə ruˈtin / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈɪntuː ə ruːˈtiːn / (BrE)
to begin to follow a regular and often unvaried pattern of behavior, especially one that develops unconsciously over time
