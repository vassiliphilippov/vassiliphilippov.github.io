close-knit

adjective
united by strong bonds (group)
Pronunciation: / kloʊs nɪt / (AmE), / kləʊs nɪt / (BrE)
(of a group of people) united or held together by strong relationships or bonds, especially emotional or social ones.

Examples

close-knit family group
Despite living far apart, the friends remained a close-knit group throughout the years.
The town is known for its close-knit community, where everyone supports each other.
They grew up in a close-knit family that valued spending time together.
