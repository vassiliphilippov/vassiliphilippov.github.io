float-type rain gauge

noun
rain gauge using a float mechanism
Pronunciation: / floʊt taɪp reɪn ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / fləʊt taɪp reɪn ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
A rain gauge that measures rainfall by means of a float resting on the water surface to indicate the level of collected rainwater.

Examples

float-type rain gauge measures rainfall
A technician checked the float-type rain gauge after the storm to record the data.
Many weather stations rely on a float-type rain gauge for accurate precipitation monitoring.
The float-type rain gauge provides continuous measurements of rainfall throughout the day.
