post office

noun
children's game involving exchanging kisses
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊst ˌɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊst ˌɒfɪs / (BrE)
A children's game in which participants pretend to send and deliver letters, exchanging kisses for the letters.

Examples

play post office game
At the party, the children played post office and giggled every time someone had to deliver a letter.
She remembered her first kiss during a game of post office at summer camp.
The old-fashioned game called post office was once a popular pastime among schoolchildren.
