vice chairwoman

noun
female deputy of a chairperson
Pronunciation: / vaɪs ˈtʃɛrˌwʊmən / (AmE), / vaɪs ˈtʃeəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who ranks below the chairperson and may act as a substitute if the chairperson is unavailable

Examples

vice chairwoman of the board
After years of service, she was promoted to vice chairwoman of the committee.
The vice chairwoman led the meeting in the absence of the chairman.
The vice chairwoman presented the annual report to the board.
