critical review

noun
evaluative essay or article on a work
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪd.ɪ.kəl rɪˈvjuː / (AmE), / ˈkrɪt.ɪ.kəl rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
an essay or article that gives an evaluative analysis and judgment of a book, play, film, or similar work

Examples

a critical review of a film
After reading the critical review of the play, I decided not to buy tickets.
Her critical review was praised for its insightful analysis and fair judgment.
The newspaper published a critical review of the author's latest novel.
