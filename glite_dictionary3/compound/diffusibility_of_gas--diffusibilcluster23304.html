diffusibility of gas

noun
ability of a gas to disperse in a medium
Pronunciation: / dɪˌfjuːsəˈbɪlɪti əv ɡæs / (AmE), / dɪˌfjuːsəˈbɪlɪti əv ɡæs / (BrE)
the ability of a gas to spread or disperse evenly through another substance, such as a liquid or another gas
