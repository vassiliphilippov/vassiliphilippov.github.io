St. Vitus dance

noun
chorea linked to rheumatic fever, mainly in children
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈvaɪtəs dæns / (AmE), / sənt ˈvaɪtəs dɑːns / (BrE)
A disorder characterized by involuntary, rapid, irregular movements, occurring mainly in children and associated with rheumatic fever; also known as Sydenham's chorea.
