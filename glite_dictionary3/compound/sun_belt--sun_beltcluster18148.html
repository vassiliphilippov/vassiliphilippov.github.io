Sun Belt

proper noun
region of the US with a warm climate
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌn ˌbɛlt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn ˌbɛlt / (BrE)
the region of the United States encompassing southern and southwestern states characterized by a warm climate and often associated with population growth and conservative politics

Examples

the Sun Belt region
In recent decades, many people have migrated to the Sun Belt in search of better job opportunities and warmer weather.
Retirees are especially drawn to the Sun Belt because of its sunny climate and lower cost of living.
The political landscape of the Sun Belt often influences national elections in the United States.
