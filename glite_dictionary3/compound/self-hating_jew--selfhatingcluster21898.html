self-hating Jew

noun
Jew critical or ashamed of own identity
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈheɪtɪŋ dʒu / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈheɪtɪŋ dʒuː / (BrE)
a Jewish person who expresses criticism of, or feels shame about, their own Jewish identity or heritage
