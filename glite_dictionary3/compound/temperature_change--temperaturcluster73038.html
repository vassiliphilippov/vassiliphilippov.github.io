temperature change

noun
change in degree of hotness or coldness
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərətʃɚ tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərətʃə tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a process in which the degree of hotness or coldness of something increases or decreases

Examples

sudden temperature change
A gradual temperature change may go unnoticed without precise instruments.
Sudden temperature change can affect the growth of certain plants.
