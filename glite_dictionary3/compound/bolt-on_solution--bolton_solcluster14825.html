bolt-on solution

noun
additional component enhancing a system
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlt ɑn səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlt ɒn səˈluːʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an additional product or component that can be incorporated into an existing system to enhance its functionality without major changes

Examples

bolt-on solution for software
Many businesses choose a bolt-on solution instead of redeveloping their core systems from scratch.
The company implemented a bolt-on solution to extend the functionality of its existing software.
This bolt-on solution is compatible with both legacy and modern platforms.
