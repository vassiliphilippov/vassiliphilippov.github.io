death chamber

noun
room for execution by poison gas
Pronunciation: / dɛθ ˈtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / dɛθ ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
a sealed room used for executions, into which poison gas is released to kill people or animals
