out of play

adjective
not in play during a game (sports)
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv pleɪ / (AmE), / aʊt ɒv pleɪ /; / aʊt əv pleɪ / (BrE)
(in sports) not able to be played or to participate in the game, usually because the ball or player is outside the playing area or play has stopped
