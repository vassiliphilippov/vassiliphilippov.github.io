grey catbird

noun
North American songbird with catlike call
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈkætˌbɝd / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈkatˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A North American songbird (Dumetella carolinensis) with gray plumage, known for its call that resembles a cat's mewing.

Examples

a grey catbird singing
A grey catbird built its nest near the garden last spring.
