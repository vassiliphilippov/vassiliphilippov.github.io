reniform leaf

noun
kidney-shaped leaf
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛnɪfɔrm liːf / (AmE), / ˈriːnɪfɔːm liːf / (BrE)
A leaf that is shaped like a kidney; a kidney-shaped simple leaf.

Examples

reniform leaf shape
A reniform leaf is broader than it is long, resembling the shape of a kidney.
Botanists distinguish the reniform leaf from other shapes, such as lanceolate or ovate.
The common violet often has a reniform leaf, which helps in its identification.
