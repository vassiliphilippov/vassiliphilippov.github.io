touch football

noun
informal American football, touch instead of tackle
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ ˈfʊtbɔl / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ˈfʊtbɔːl / (BrE)
A form of American football in which players stop the ball carrier by touching them instead of tackling.

Examples

play touch football outside
Because it's safer, many schools organize touch football instead of tackle football during gym class.
Every weekend, the neighborhood kids gather at the park for a game of touch football.
He hurt his finger while playing touch football with his coworkers during the lunch break.
