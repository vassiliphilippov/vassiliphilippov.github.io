tear sac

noun
dilated end of lacrimal duct near the eye
Pronunciation: / tɪr sæk / (AmE), / tɪə sæk / (BrE)
either of the two small, dilated ends of the lacrimal ducts located near the inner corners of the eyes, which collect tears produced by the lacrimal glands

Examples

swollen tear sac
After her surgery, the swelling around her left tear sac gradually subsided.
Tears collect in the tear sac before draining into the nasal cavity.
The doctor explained that an infection in the tear sac could lead to watery eyes and swelling.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lacrimal sac: the sac that collects tears from the eye and drains into the nasal cavity
