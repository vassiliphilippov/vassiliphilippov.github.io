kangaroo mouse

noun
a small leaping rodent of desert areas
Pronunciation: / ˈkæŋɡəˌruː maʊs / (AmE), / ˈkaŋɡəˌruː maʊs / (BrE)
a small rodent native to North American deserts, with long hind legs for jumping, external cheek pouches, and a resemblance to a miniature kangaroo
