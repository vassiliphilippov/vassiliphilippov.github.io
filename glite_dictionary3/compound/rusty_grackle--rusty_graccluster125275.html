rusty grackle

noun
North American blackbird with rusty-edged fall plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti ˈɡrækəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti ˈɡrakəl / (BrE)
A North American blackbird (Quiscalus species) whose bluish-black plumage becomes fringed with rusty edges in the fall.

A flock of rusty grackles gathered in the field at sunset.
