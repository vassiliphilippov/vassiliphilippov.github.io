self-employed person

noun
person working independently without long-term employer
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf ɛmˈplɔɪd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪmˈplɔɪd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who works for themselves and is not employed by a company or other individual, often offering services to various clients without a long-term contract

Examples

a self-employed person works independently
As a self-employed person, she managed her own schedule and chose her clients.
Being a self-employed person means handling your own taxes and business expenses.
The government has introduced new benefits specifically for the self-employed person.
