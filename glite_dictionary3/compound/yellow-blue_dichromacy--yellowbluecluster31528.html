yellow-blue dichromacy

noun
form of color blindness affecting yellow and blue
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ bluː daɪˈkroʊməsi / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ bluː daɪˈkrɒməsi / (BrE)
