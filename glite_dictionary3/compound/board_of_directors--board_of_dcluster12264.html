board of directors

noun
governing group for a company or organization
Pronunciation: / bɔrd əv dəˈrɛktɚz /; / bɔrd əv dəˈrɛktərz / (AmE), / bɔːd əv dɪˈrɛktəz / (BrE)
a group of people elected or appointed to oversee and govern the activities and policies of a company or organization
