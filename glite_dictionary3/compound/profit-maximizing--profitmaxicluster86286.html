profit-maximizing

adjective
intended to maximize profit
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfɪt ˌmæksɪˌmaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪt ˌmæksɪˌmaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an action, strategy, or amount) intended to make the profit as great as possible.
