stable factor

noun
blood coagulation factor (Factor VII)
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪbəl ˈfæktər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbəl ˈfaktə / (BrE)
a coagulation factor produced in the kidney under the influence of vitamin K, also known as Factor VII, important in blood clotting
