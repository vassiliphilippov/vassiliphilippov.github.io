long hour

noun
an extended work period
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈaʊə / (BrE)
an extended period of time spent working or engaged in an activity, often longer than usual or expected

Examples

As a nurse, he regularly works long hours during the holiday season.
She spent a long hour waiting at the airport for her delayed flight.
