Book of Baruch

proper noun
apocryphal Old Testament book ascribed to Baruch
Pronunciation: / bʊk əv bəˈruːk / (AmE), / bʊk əv bəˈruːk / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book of the Old Testament, ascribed to Baruch, included in some versions of the Bible but not in the Hebrew canon

Examples

the Book of Baruch in the Bible
Scholars debate the authorship of the Book of Baruch and its place among apocryphal literature.
Some Christian denominations include the Book of Baruch in their Old Testament.
The Book of Baruch is often read during certain liturgical seasons in some churches.
