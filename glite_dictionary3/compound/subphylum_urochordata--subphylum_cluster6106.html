subphylum Urochordata

noun
subphylum of marine animals (tunicates)
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈfaɪləm ˌjʊroʊˈkɔrdətə / (AmE), / sʌbˈfʌɪləm ˌjʊərəʊˈkɔːdətə / (BrE)
A subphylum of marine animals within the phylum Chordata, commonly known as tunicates, characterized by a sac-like body and a notochord during their larval stage.
