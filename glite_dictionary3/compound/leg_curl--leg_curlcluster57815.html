leg curl

noun
exercise for hamstring muscles
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ kɜrl / (AmE), / lɛɡ kɜːl / (BrE)
an exercise in which you bend your knee to move your lower leg toward your buttocks against resistance, intended to strengthen the hamstring muscles
