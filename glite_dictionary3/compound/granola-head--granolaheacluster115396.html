granola-head

noun
health-conscious, environmentally aware person (slang)
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈnoʊlə hɛd / (AmE), / ɡrəˈnəʊlə hɛd / (BrE)
An informal, often mildly humorous or critical term for a person who is notably health-conscious and environmentally aware, especially in diet and lifestyle.

Examples

a granola-head friend
My brother turned into a real granola-head after he moved to Portland.
People called her a granola-head because of her love for hiking and organic food.
The new yoga instructor is a total granola-head, always talking about eco-friendly living.
