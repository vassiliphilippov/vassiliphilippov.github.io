pro-Beijing

adjective
supporting the Chinese Communist Party
Pronunciation: / proʊ bəˈdʒeɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / prəʊ bɪˈdʒeɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
politically supporting or aligned with the Chinese Communist Party, especially in the context of Hong Kong and China.
