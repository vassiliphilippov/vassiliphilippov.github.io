milk wagon

noun
wagon for delivering milk
Pronunciation: / mɪlk ˈwæɡən / (AmE), / mɪlk ˈwaɡən / (BrE)
a wagon, traditionally horse-drawn, used for delivering milk to homes or businesses.

Examples

horse-drawn milk wagon
Before refrigeration, the milk wagon delivered fresh milk directly to people's doors.
Children would often chase after the milk wagon for a chance to see the horses.
The milk wagon made its early morning rounds through the quiet streets.
