Judeo-Bolshevism

noun
antisemitic theory linking Jews and Bolshevism
Pronunciation: / dʒuːˌdeoʊ boʊlˈʃɛvɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːdɪəʊ bɒlˈʃɛvɪzəm / (BrE)
The belief or propaganda alleging a connection between Jewish people and Bolshevism, often used historically as an antisemitic conspiracy theory.

Examples

spread Judeo-Bolshevism ideas
Many historians have studied the political use of Judeo-Bolshevism in early 20th-century Europe.
References to Judeo-Bolshevism can still be found in some contemporary extremist rhetoric.
The propaganda campaign relied heavily on promoting the idea of Judeo-Bolshevism.
