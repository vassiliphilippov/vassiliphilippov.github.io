worm fish

noun
small elongated tropical fish, related to gobies
Pronunciation: / wɜrm fɪʃ / (AmE), / wɜːm fɪʃ / (BrE)
A small, elongated tropical shallow-water fish of the family Microdesmidae, related to gobies.

Examples

a worm fish in seagrass
Because of its resemblance to a worm, the worm fish can easily camouflage among seagrass.
Scientists discovered a new species of worm fish off the coast of Indonesia.
The worm fish is rarely seen in aquariums due to its secretive nature.
