birth canal

noun
passage for baby during childbirth
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝθ kəˌnæl / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ kəˌnæl / (BrE)
the passage in the female reproductive system, comprising the uterus and vagina, through which a baby passes during childbirth

Examples

narrow birth canal
Doctors monitored the width of the birth canal during labor.
In some cases, a narrow birth canal can make delivery more difficult.
The baby moved through the birth canal without complications.
