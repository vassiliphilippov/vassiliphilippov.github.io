potassium nitrate

noun
chemical compound (KNO₃); used in fertilizers and explosives
Pronunciation: / pəˈteɪsiəm ˈnaɪtreɪt / (AmE), / pəˈtæsɪəm ˈnaɪtreɪt / (BrE)
a white crystalline salt (KNO₃) used especially in fertilizers, explosives, and food preservation

Examples
