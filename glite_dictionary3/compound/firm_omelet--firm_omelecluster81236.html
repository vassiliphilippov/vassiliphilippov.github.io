firm omelet

noun
an omelet cooked until fully set
Pronunciation: / fɝːm ˈɑːmlət / (AmE), / fɜːm ˈɒmlət / (BrE)
An omelet made by beating eggs with milk or cream and cooking until fully set, resulting in a firm texture.

Examples

a firm omelet for breakfast
If you like your eggs less runny, order a firm omelet for breakfast.
She prefers a firm omelet with a golden crust on both sides.
The chef explained that a firm omelet is made by cooking the eggs until they are fully set.
