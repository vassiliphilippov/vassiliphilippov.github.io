football tee

noun
device for holding a football for kickoff
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌbɔl ti / (AmE), / ˈfʊtbɔːl tiː / (BrE)
A device that holds a football upright above the ground before it is kicked, especially at the kickoff.

Examples

football tee for kickoff
The kicker placed the ball on the football tee before running up for the kickoff.
The referees checked to make sure every player had a regulation football tee for practice.
Without a football tee, it would be difficult to keep the ball steady before kicking.
