deliberative assembly

noun
formal body for discussion and decision-making
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɪbərətɪv əˈsɛmbli / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪbərətɪv əˈsɛmbli / (BrE)
a formal gathering in which members meet to discuss and decide on matters through orderly, reasoned debate
