analytic thinking

noun
process of breaking down and analyzing parts
Pronunciation: / əˈnælɪtɪk ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈnælɪtɪk ˈθɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of breaking down a complex whole into its constituent parts to better understand their relationships
