male-patterned baldness

noun
hair loss on top/crown in men
Pronunciation: / ˌmeɪl ˈpætərnd ˈbɔldnəs / (AmE), / ˌmeɪl ˈpætn̩d ˈbɔːldnəs / (BrE)
A common type of hair loss in men, characterized by thinning or loss of hair on the crown and temples, often progressing to partial or complete baldness.
