bombproof shelter

noun
shelter resistant to explosions
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑmˌpruf ˈʃɛltɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒmpruːf ˈʃɛltə / (BrE)
a shelter built to protect people or things from bomb explosions by being strong enough to withstand the blast
