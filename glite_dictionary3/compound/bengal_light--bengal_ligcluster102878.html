Bengal light

noun
type of firework emitting steady colored light
Pronunciation: / bɛnˈɡɑl laɪt / (AmE), / bɛnˈɡæl laɪt / (BrE)
a type of firework that produces a steady, bright colored light, originally blue, used formerly as a signal and now mainly for display
