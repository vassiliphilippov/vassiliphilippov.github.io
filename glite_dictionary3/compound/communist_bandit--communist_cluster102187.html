communist bandit

noun
derogatory term for a communist guerrilla or rebel
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmjənɪst ˈbændɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmjʊnɪst ˈbændɪt / (BrE)
(derogatory, historical) A person, especially a guerrilla or rebel, associated with communist movements and accused of revolutionary theft or violence.
