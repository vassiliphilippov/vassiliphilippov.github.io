cold spell

noun
short period of cold weather
Pronunciation: / koʊld spɛl / (AmE), / kəʊld spel / (BrE)
a period of unusually cold weather lasting for a short time

Examples

a cold spell hits
During the cold spell, temperatures dropped well below freezing each night.
Farmers worried about their crops during the unexpected cold spell in late spring.
The city experienced a cold spell that lasted nearly two weeks.
