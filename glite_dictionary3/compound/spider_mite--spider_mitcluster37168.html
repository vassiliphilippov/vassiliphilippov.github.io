spider mite

noun
web-spinning plant pest mite
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdər ˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdə ˌmaɪt / (BrE)
A tiny, web-spinning mite that feeds on and damages garden plants and fruit trees.

Examples
