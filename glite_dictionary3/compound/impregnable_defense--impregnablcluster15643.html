impregnable defense

noun
defense impossible to breach or defeat
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛɡnəbəl dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛɡnəbl̩ dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)

Examples

The fortress was protected by an impregnable defense that withstood every assault.
The team's strategy relied on building an impregnable defense to frustrate their opponents.
