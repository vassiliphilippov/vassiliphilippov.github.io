temporal lobe

noun
region of the brain near the temples
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərəl loʊb / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl ləʊb / (BrE)
the part of the cerebral cortex located at the sides of the brain, responsible for processing auditory information and memory

Examples

the temporal lobe processes sound
Damage to the temporal lobe can affect a person's ability to recognize sounds and understand language.
Scientists are studying how the temporal lobe influences memory formation and emotional responses.
The patient's MRI scan revealed lesions in the left temporal lobe.
