Battle of the Teutoburg Forest

proper noun
decisive 9 AD battle, Germans defeated Romans
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬.əl əv ðə tuːˈtoʊ.bɜːrɡ ˈfɔːr.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈbæt.əl əv ðə tjuːˈtɒb.ɜːɡ ˈfɒr.ɪst / (BrE)
