man-of-war

noun
stinging marine animal with a gas float
Pronunciation: / ˌmæn əv ˈwɔr / (AmE), / ˌmæn əv ˈwɔː / (BrE)
a large floating marine animal (Portuguese man-of-war) with a bladder-like float and long stinging tentacles, found in warm seas

Examples

Portuguese man-of-war jellyfish
A group of man-of-war drifted past the boat, their tentacles trailing through the water.
Swimmers are warned to avoid the man-of-war washed up on the shore.
The sting of a man-of-war can be extremely painful and dangerous.
