Egyptian flu

noun
humorous term for travel-related illness
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən fluː / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃ(ə)n fluː / (BrE)
A humorous term for mild flu-like symptoms experienced by travelers, especially after visiting Egypt or similar destinations, often implying travel-related illness or malaise.

Examples

catch Egyptian flu after travel
After returning from her holiday, she claimed she had caught the Egyptian flu.
He blamed his tiredness on the Egyptian flu from his recent travels.
Many tourists laughed about getting the Egyptian flu after sampling the local cuisine.

Related Terms

  • synonym – traveler's diarrhea: common travel-related gastrointestinal illness
