tailless tenrec

noun
a Madagascan mammal that eats earthworms
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪl.ləs ˈtɛn.rɛk / (AmE), / ˈteɪl.ləs ˈtɛn.rɛk / (BrE)
a species of tenrec native to Madagascar, recognized for its lack of tail and prolific breeding, that feeds mainly on earthworms
