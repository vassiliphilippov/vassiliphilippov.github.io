reign of terror

noun
period of brutal oppression
Pronunciation: / reɪn əv ˈtɛrɚ / (AmE), / reɪn əv ˈtɛrərə / (BrE)
a period during which there is brutal or ruthless oppression, often with widespread violence, resembling the Reign of Terror in France

Examples

a reign of terror began
During the civil war, many lived in fear throughout the reign of terror.
The city emerged from a reign of terror once the criminal gang was dismantled.
The dictator's rule became a reign of terror, with opponents disappearing overnight.
