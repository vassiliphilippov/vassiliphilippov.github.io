lead pencil

noun
graphite-based writing pencil
Pronunciation: / lɛd ˈpɛnsl̩ / (AmE), / lɛd ˈpɛnsəl / (BrE)
A pencil in which the marking substance is graphite rather than actual lead.

Examples

draw with a lead pencil
He sketched the outline with a lead pencil before adding watercolor.
Please bring a lead pencil to the exam; pens are not allowed.
The artist preferred to use a lead pencil for precise shading in her drawings.

Related Terms

  • antonym – colored pencil: A pencil with a colored core for drawing rather than a graphite or lead core
  • synonym – pencil: An instrument for writing or drawing, usually with a graphite or colored core
