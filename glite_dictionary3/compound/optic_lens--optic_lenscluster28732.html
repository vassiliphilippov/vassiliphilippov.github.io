optic lens

noun
lens for viewing or vision
Pronunciation: / ˈɑptɪk lɛnz / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪk lɛnz / (BrE)
a lens designed for aiding vision or viewing, especially as used in optical instruments or the eye

Examples

an optic lens in glasses
He adjusted the optic lens for a clearer view.
She cleaned her glasses and inspected each optic lens for dust.
The microscope's optic lens magnified the tiny organism.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lens: a piece of glass or other transparent material shaped to converge or diverge transmitted light and form an image
