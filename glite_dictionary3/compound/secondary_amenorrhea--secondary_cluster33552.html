secondary amenorrhea

noun
loss of periods after previous menstruation
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛk.ənˈdɛr.i əˌmen.əˈriː.ə / (AmE), / ˌsɛk.ənˈdeə.ri əˌmiː.nəˈriː.ə / (BrE)
the cessation of menstruation in a woman who had previously menstruated, typically due to an underlying medical condition

Examples

secondary amenorrhea after normal periods
If you have not had a period for several months after normal cycles, you may be experiencing secondary amenorrhea.
Polycystic ovary syndrome is a common cause of secondary amenorrhea in young women.
The doctor explained that secondary amenorrhea can result from severe stress or excessive exercise.
