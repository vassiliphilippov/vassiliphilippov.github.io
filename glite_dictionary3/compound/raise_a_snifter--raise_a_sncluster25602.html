to raise a snifter

verb
make a celebratory toast with a snifter
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪz ə ˈsnɪftər / (AmE), / ˈreɪz ə ˈsnɪftə / (BrE)
To lift a (typically brandy) glass in a gesture of salute, celebration, or toast.

Examples

to raise a snifter in toast
As the speech ended, everyone in the room raised a snifter to honor the guest.
Before leaving, she raised a snifter and wished everyone a happy year ahead.
He likes to raise a snifter of brandy while reminiscing about old friends.
