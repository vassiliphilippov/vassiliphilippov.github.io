working day

noun
day on which work is done
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkɪŋ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkɪŋ deɪ / (BrE)
A day on which work is usually done, especially in contrast to weekends or public holidays.

Examples

a working day (not weekend)
During the working day, the offices are busy with employees and visitors.
Friday is considered a working day in many countries, while Saturday and Sunday are not.
My shift covers every working day from 9 to 5.

Related Terms

  • synonym – business day: a day, typically Monday to Friday, when normal business operations are conducted
  • antonym – holiday: a day of celebration or rest when work is usually suspended
  • synonym – weekday: any day of the week except Saturday and Sunday
