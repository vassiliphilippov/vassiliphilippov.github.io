egg timer

noun
timer for boiling eggs
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡ ˌtaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈɛɡ ˌtaɪmə / (BrE)
a device, often a small hourglass or timer, used to measure the time required to boil eggs, typically lasting around three minutes
