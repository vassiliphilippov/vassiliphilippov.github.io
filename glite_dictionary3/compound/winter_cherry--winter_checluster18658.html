winter cherry

noun
ornamental plant with papery orange-red calyx
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntər ˌtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˌtʃɛri / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the nightshade family, cultivated for its decorative orange-red, papery calyx that encloses the fruit.

Examples

winter cherry orange lanterns
I bought a bunch of winter cherry stems to decorate the living room.
She grows winter cherry in her garden for the striking orange-red color in autumn.
The winter cherry is often used in floral arrangements for its bright, lantern-like pods.

Related Terms

  • synonym – physalis: any plant of the genus Physalis, such as the ground cherry or cape gooseberry
