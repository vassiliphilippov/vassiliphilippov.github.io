private line

noun
telephone line for a single user
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət laɪn / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət laɪn / (BrE)
a telephone line that is dedicated to and used only by a single subscriber or user

Examples

a private line for calls
In the past, having a private line was considered a luxury in many neighborhoods.
She requested a private line so her business calls wouldn't be interrupted.
The company installed a private line for more secure communications.

Related Terms

  • antonym – party line: a telephone line shared by multiple subscribers
