clean and jerk

noun
weightlifting movement with barbell
Pronunciation: / klin ənd dʒɝk / (AmE), / kliːn ənd dʒɜːk / (BrE)
a weightlifting movement in which a barbell is lifted to shoulder height (the clean) and then thrust overhead in one motion (the jerk)

Examples

Olympic clean and jerk
Proper technique is crucial to avoid injury during the clean and jerk.
She set a new record in the clean and jerk at the national weightlifting championship.
The athlete struggled with the clean and jerk but excelled in the snatch.
