papaya tree

noun
tree bearing papaya fruit
Pronunciation: / pəˈpaɪ.ə triː / (AmE), / pəˈpaɪ.ə triː / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with large, deeply lobed leaves and large, yellow-orange fruit known as papaya.

Examples

A mature papaya tree can reach up to ten meters in height.
Farmers often plant papaya trees in tropical regions for their nutritious fruit.
