French West Indies

proper noun
Caribbean islands under French administration
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ wɛst ˈɪndiz / (AmE), / frɛntʃ wɛst ˈɪndiːz / (BrE)
the islands in the Caribbean's Lesser Antilles that are administered by France, including Guadeloupe, Martinique, Saint Martin, and Saint Barthélemy.
