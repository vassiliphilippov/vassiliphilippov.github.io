light and fluffy

adjective
having a soft, airy texture
Pronunciation: / laɪt ənd ˈflʌfi / (AmE), / laɪt ənd ˈflʌfi / (BrE)
(of food or material) having a soft, airy texture that is not dense or heavy.

Examples

light and fluffy pancakes
Her pancakes turned out light and fluffy, just the way she likes them.
The baker promised that his bread would be light and fluffy inside.
Whip the cream until it becomes light and fluffy in texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – airy: light or delicate in texture
  • antonym – dense: having a heavy, thick, and compact texture
