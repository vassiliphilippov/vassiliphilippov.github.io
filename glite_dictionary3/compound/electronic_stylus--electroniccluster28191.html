electronic stylus

noun
stylus for electronic displays
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈstaɪləs / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈstaɪləs / (BrE)
A device used to interact with electronic displays, especially touchscreens, by detecting position and sometimes pressure or illumination at the point of contact.

Examples

electronic stylus on tablet
Many modern smartphones come with an electronic stylus that enhances touchscreen navigation.
The artist drew directly onto the tablet using an electronic stylus for greater precision.
You can make handwritten notes on the computer if you have an electronic stylus.
