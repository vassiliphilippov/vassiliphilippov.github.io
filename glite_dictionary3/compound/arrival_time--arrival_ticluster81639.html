arrival time

noun
scheduled or expected time of arrival
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪvəl taɪm / (AmE), / əˈraɪvəl taɪm / (BrE)
the scheduled or expected time at which a vehicle or person arrives at a particular place

Examples

arrival time of the train
