power failure

noun
interruption of electricity supply
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ər ˈfeɪ.ljɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə ˈfeɪ.ljə / (BrE)
an interruption in the supply of electricity to a particular area, often due to equipment malfunction or external factors

Examples

sudden power failure
Due to a power failure, the elevators stopped working for several hours.
Heavy winds last night caused a power failure across the city.
Hospitals have backup generators in case of a power failure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blackout: a period when all lights in a region go out due to loss of electric power
  • synonym – power outage: a temporary loss of electrical power
