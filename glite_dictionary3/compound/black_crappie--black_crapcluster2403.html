black crappie

noun
a species of dark-colored freshwater fish
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈkræpi / (AmE), / blæk ˈkræpi / (BrE)
a species of North American freshwater fish (Pomoxis nigromaculatus) of the sunfish family, distinguished by its dark coloration and irregular blotches
