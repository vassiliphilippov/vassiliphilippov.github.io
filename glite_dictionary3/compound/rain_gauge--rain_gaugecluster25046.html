rain gauge

noun
instrument for measuring rainfall
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪn ˌɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈreɪn ˌɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the amount of rainfall over a period of time

Examples

rain gauge measures rainfall
After the storm, the rain gauge showed nearly two inches of rain had fallen overnight.
Farmers often rely on a rain gauge to monitor local rainfall and plan irrigation.
The weather station technician emptied the rain gauge every morning to record the previous day's precipitation.
