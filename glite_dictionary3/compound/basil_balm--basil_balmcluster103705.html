basil balm

noun
a fragrant European mint plant
Pronunciation: / ˈbæzəl bɑːm / (AmE), / ˈbæzəl bɑːm / (BrE)
A fragrant European mint plant (Melissa officinalis) with clusters of small violet-and-white flowers, naturalized especially in eastern North America.
