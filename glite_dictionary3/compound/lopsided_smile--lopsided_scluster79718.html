lopsided smile

noun
an uneven or crooked smile
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑpˌsaɪdɪd smaɪl / (AmE), / ˈlɒpˌsaɪdɪd smaɪl / (BrE)
A smile in which one side of the mouth is higher or more pronounced than the other, giving an uneven or crooked appearance.

Examples

give a lopsided smile
A lopsided smile played on his lips as he remembered the funny story.
He gave me a lopsided smile as if he was unsure how to react.
She tried to hide her embarrassment with a lopsided smile.
