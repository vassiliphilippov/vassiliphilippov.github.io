guy wire

noun
cable or rope for stabilizing a structure
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪ ˌwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ɡaɪ ˌwaɪə / (BrE)
A strong cable, wire, or rope used to support and stabilize a structure, such as a pole, mast, or tent, by anchoring it to the ground or another fixed point.

Examples

guy wire supports pole
A team inspected each guy wire for signs of wear before the storm.
Please make sure the guy wires are tight before setting up the tent.
The tall radio tower was secured by several guy wires anchored to the ground.
