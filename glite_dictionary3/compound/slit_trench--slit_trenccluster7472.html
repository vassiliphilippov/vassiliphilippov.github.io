slit trench

noun
narrow battlefield shelter trench
Pronunciation: / slɪt trɛntʃ / (AmE), / slɪt trɛntʃ / (BrE)
a narrow excavation in the ground, typically used as a shelter for soldiers during battle

soldiers in a slit trench
