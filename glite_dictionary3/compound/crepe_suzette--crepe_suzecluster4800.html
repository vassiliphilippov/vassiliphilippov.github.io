crepe Suzette

noun
French pancake dessert with orange liqueur sauce
Pronunciation: / kreɪp suːˈzɛt / (AmE), / krep sjuːˈzɛt / (BrE)
A French dessert consisting of thin pancakes served with a sauce made of orange and lemon juice, sugar, butter, and liqueur, often flambéed at the table.
