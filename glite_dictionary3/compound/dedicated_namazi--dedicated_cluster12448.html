dedicated namazi

noun
devout Muslim who prays regularly
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdəˌkeɪtɪd nəˈmɑːzi / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd nəˈmɑːzi / (BrE)
