downy ash

noun
a North American ash tree with velvety twigs
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊni æʃ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊni æʃ / (BrE)
a small American ash tree (Fraxinus pubescens) with velvety branchlets and soft, downy undersides to its leaves
