capacity unit

noun
digital resource quota
Pronunciation: / kəˈpæsəti ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / kəˈpæsəti ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
(in computing and cloud services) an allocated amount or quota of resources (such as storage, compute, or bandwidth) included in a plan or purchased for use.

Examples

a capacity unit of storage
Our subscription includes ten capacity units of storage available per month for backups.
When load spikes the service automatically consumed an extra capacity unit to maintain performance.
You can purchase additional capacity units of compute through the cloud provider's billing portal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allocation: an assigned portion of resources reserved for a particular purpose or user
  • synonym – quota: an allocated amount of a resource (for example storage or bandwidth) assigned to a user or plan
