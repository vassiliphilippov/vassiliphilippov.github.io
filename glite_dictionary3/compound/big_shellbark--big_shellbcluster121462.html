big shellbark

noun
large hickory tree with big nuts
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈʃɛlˌbɑrk / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈʃɛlˌbɑːk / (BrE)
A large hickory tree of the eastern United States, similar to the shagbark hickory but with much larger nuts.
