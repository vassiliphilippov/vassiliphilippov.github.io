to perpetuate racism

verb
to maintain or continue racist attitudes
Pronunciation: / pərˈpɛtʃuˌeɪt ˈreɪsɪzəm / (AmE), / pəˈpɛtʃʊeɪt ˈreɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
to cause racist attitudes or practices to continue or persist.

Examples

to perpetuate racism in society
Certain stereotypes in the media continue to perpetuate racism, even if unintentionally.
Leaders must take active steps to prevent messages that perpetuate racism from spreading.
Policies that ignore systemic inequalities often perpetuate racism in schools and workplaces.
