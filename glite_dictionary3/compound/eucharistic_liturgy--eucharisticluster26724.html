Eucharistic liturgy

noun
Christian rite commemorating the Last Supper
Pronunciation: / juːˌkærɪstɪk ˈlɪtərdʒi / (AmE), / juːˌkærɪstɪk ˈlɪtədʒi / (BrE)
The Christian ceremony commemorating the Last Supper, in which bread and wine are consecrated and consumed.
