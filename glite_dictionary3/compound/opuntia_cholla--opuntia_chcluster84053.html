Opuntia cholla

noun
a spiny cactus species
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpʌn.ti.ə ˈtʃoʊ.jə / (AmE), / əˈpʌn.tɪ.ə ˈtʃɒl.ə / (BrE)
A species of arborescent cactus with very spiny, cylindrical stem segments, native to the southwestern United States and Mexico.
