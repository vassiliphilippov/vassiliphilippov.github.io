hack saw

noun
hand saw for cutting metal
Pronunciation: / ˈhæk sɔ / (AmE), / ˈhæk sɔː / (BrE)
A hand-operated saw with a fine-toothed blade, used for cutting metal and other hard materials.

Examples

cut metal with a hack saw
A carpenter's toolbox often includes a hack saw for tasks that require precision.
He used a hack saw to cut through the metal pipe in the workshop.
You should wear gloves when operating a hack saw to protect your hands from sharp edges.
