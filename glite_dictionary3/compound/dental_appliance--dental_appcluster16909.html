dental appliance

noun
device to repair or replace teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəl əˈplaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl əˈplaɪəns / (BrE)
A device designed to repair teeth or replace missing teeth, such as dentures, braces, or bridges.

Examples

dental appliance for missing teeth
After losing two teeth in an accident, he had a dental appliance fitted.
Modern dental appliances are designed to be comfortable and nearly invisible.
The dentist explained that a dental appliance would be necessary to correct her bite.
