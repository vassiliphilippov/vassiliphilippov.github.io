detonating device

noun
explosive initiator device
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛtəˌneɪtɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / ˈdɛtəʊneɪtɪŋ dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
A mechanical or electrical apparatus, or a small amount of explosive material, used to initiate the explosion of a larger charge of explosives.

Examples

detonating device triggers explosion
A small detonating device is required to trigger the main charge in many explosives.
Experts examined the detonating device to determine what started the explosion.
The bomb squad safely removed the detonating device from the suspicious package.
