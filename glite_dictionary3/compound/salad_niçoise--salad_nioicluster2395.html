salad Niçoise

noun
French salad with tuna, eggs, and vegetables
Pronunciation: / səˌlɑd niˈswɑz / (AmE), / səˌlɑd niˈswɑːz / (BrE)
a French salad typically made with tomatoes, tuna, hard-boiled eggs, olives, anchovies, and capers, often served with a vinaigrette.

Examples

salad Nicoise with tuna
Her homemade salad Niçoise was full of fresh vegetables and tuna.
