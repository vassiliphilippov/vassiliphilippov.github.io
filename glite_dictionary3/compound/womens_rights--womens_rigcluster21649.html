women's rights

noun
legal and social equality for women
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmɪnz raɪts / (AmE), / ˈwɪmɪnz raɪts / (BrE)
the legal and social rights claimed for and granted to women, especially with respect to equality with men

Examples

women's rights movement
Several laws have been introduced to protect women's rights in the workplace.
She devoted her life to fighting for women's rights in her country.
The protest was organized to raise awareness about women's rights issues globally.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gender equality: the state of equal access to resources and opportunities regardless of gender
  • antonym – men's rights: the rights and interests claimed for men, especially as a counterpart to women's rights
