oxidizing agent

noun
substance that causes oxidation in chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksəˌdaɪzɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪˌdaɪzɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
