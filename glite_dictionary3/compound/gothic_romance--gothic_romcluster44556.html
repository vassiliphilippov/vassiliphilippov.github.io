Gothic romance

noun
literary genre featuring mystery and the supernatural
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθɪk roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪk rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
A literary genre characterized by mysterious, desolate, and often grotesque events, typically set in gloomy or medieval environments.
