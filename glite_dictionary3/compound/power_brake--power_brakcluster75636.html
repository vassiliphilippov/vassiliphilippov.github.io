power brake

noun
brake system with power assistance in vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊər breɪk / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə breɪk / (BrE)
a brake system in a vehicle that uses power assistance to increase the force applied by the driver, making braking easier and more effective

Examples

If the power brake system fails, stopping the vehicle may require much more effort.
