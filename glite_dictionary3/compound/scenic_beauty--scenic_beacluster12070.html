scenic beauty

noun
attractiveness of a landscape
Pronunciation: / ˈsinɪk ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈsiːnɪk ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
The attractive and picturesque quality of a landscape, especially as viewed by visitors.

Examples

scenic beauty of mountains
Developers are required to protect the scenic beauty of the coastal region.
The national park is famous for its scenic beauty and attracts thousands of visitors each year.
Tourists paused often to photograph the scenic beauty along the mountain trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym – picturesque: visually charming, as if resembling a picture
  • antonym – ugliness: the quality of being unpleasant to look at
