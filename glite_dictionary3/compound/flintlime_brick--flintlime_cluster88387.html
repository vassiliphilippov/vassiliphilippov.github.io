flintlime brick

noun
brick made with sand and slaked lime
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪntˌlaɪm brɪk / (AmE), / ˈflɪntˌlaɪm brɪk / (BrE)
A type of brick made with sand and slaked lime instead of clay, usually light gray or off-white in color.
