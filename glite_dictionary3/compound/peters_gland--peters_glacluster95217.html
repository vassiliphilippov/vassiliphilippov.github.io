Peter's gland

noun
lymph nodes in intestinal wall near ileum and colon
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːtərz ɡlænd / (AmE), / ˈpiːtəz ɡlænd / (BrE)
any of several lymph nodes located in the intestinal wall near the junction of the ileum and colon
