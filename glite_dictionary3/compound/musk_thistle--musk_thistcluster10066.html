musk thistle

noun
a spiny Eurasian plant with musky crimson flowers
Pronunciation: / mʌsk ˈθɪsəl / (AmE), / mʌsk ˈθɪsl̩ / (BrE)
A Eurasian perennial plant with spiny, cottony leaves and nodding crimson, musky-scented flower heads, often found in North America and valued for its nectar.
