bluefin tuna

noun
flesh of a large tuna used as food
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːfɪn ˈtuːnə / (AmE), / ˈbluːfɪn ˈtjuːnə / (BrE)
the flesh of a very large species of tuna, prized as food, especially in sushi and sashimi

Examples

bluefin tuna sushi
