Wilkes Land

proper noun
region of Antarctica south of Australia
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪlks lænd / (AmE), / ˈwɪlks land / (BrE)
a coastal region of Antarctica bordering the Indian Ocean, situated south of Australia; most of the area is claimed by Australia

Examples

Wilkes Land in Antarctica
Australia claims sovereignty over Wilkes Land in Antarctica.
