outer space

noun
region beyond Earth's atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtɚ speɪs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtə speɪs / (BrE)
the physical universe beyond Earth's atmosphere, especially the region where there is very little air and objects such as stars, planets, and satellites are found

outer space travel
Many scientists dream of traveling to outer space to explore other planets.
Movies often depict outer space as a place full of mystery and danger.
