the Great Starvation

proper noun
famine in Ireland (1846–1851) caused by potato blight
Pronunciation: / ðə ɡreɪt stɑrˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ðə ɡreɪt stɑːˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
