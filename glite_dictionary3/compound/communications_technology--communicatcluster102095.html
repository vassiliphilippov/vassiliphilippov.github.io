communications technology

noun
technology for transmitting information
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃənz tɛkˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz tɛkˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or use of systems and equipment for transmitting information, such as telephones, radio, television, and the internet.
