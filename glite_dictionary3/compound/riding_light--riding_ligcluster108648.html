riding light

noun
anchor light on a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪdɪŋ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ laɪt / (BrE)
A light displayed by a ship at anchor, usually placed in the rigging, to indicate its presence at night or in low visibility.
