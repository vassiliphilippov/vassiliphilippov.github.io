detention cell

noun
temporary holding cell for groups
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɛnʃən sɛl / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛnʃən sɛl / (BrE)
a large room or enclosure in a jail or facility where several people are temporarily held in custody, often while awaiting trial, sentencing, or processing

Examples
