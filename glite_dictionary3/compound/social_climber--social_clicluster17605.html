social climber

noun
person seeking higher social status
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈklaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃl ˈklaɪmə / (BrE)
a person who seeks to gain a higher social status, often by cultivating influential relationships or adopting behaviors they believe are associated with a higher class

Examples

social climber at parties
He was known as a social climber who would do anything to join the city's elite circles.
Her reputation as a social climber made others wary of her motives.
The novel depicts a ruthless social climber determined to gain favor with powerful people.
