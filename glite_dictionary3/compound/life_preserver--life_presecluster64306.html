life preserver

noun
buoyant device for water safety
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf prɪˌzɝːvɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf prɪˌzɜːvə / (BrE)
a buoyant device, such as a belt or jacket, worn to keep a person afloat in water and prevent drowning

Examples

wear a life preserver boating
Always make sure to wear a life preserver when boating on the lake.
Every passenger was given a life preserver before the ship left the dock.
The lifeguard tossed a life preserver to the struggling swimmer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – life jacket: a sleeveless buoyant jacket for keeping a person afloat
  • synonym – lifebuoy: a circular ring of buoyant material used for rescuing people in water
