white knight

noun
performative online defender
Pronunciation: / waɪt naɪt / (AmE), / waɪt naɪt / (BrE)
an internet user who publicly defends someone in a performative or self‑congratulatory way, often to show moral superiority or gain attention

Examples

white knight defends online
A white knight online rushed to defend the streamer after a controversial claim.
She accused him of being a white knight who only argued to look morally superior to strangers.
Instead of helping, the white knight kept posting long comments praising the victim for attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym – defender: someone who protects or supports another person
  • synonym – virtue-signaling: publicly expressing moral opinions mainly to gain approval or appear virtuous
