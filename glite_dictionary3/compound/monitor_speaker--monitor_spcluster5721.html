monitor speaker

noun
stage loudspeaker for performers
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnɪtɚ ˈspiːkɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɒnɪtə ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
A loudspeaker placed on stage and directed at performers to allow them to hear themselves during a live performance.

Examples

monitor speaker on stage
Each musician had their own monitor speaker to better hear their instruments.
The guitarist asked the sound engineer to turn up the monitor speaker during the performance.
Without a monitor speaker, the singer struggled to stay in tune on stage.
