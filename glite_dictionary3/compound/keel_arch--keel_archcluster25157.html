keel arch

noun
S-shaped pointed arch
Pronunciation: / kil ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / kiːl ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a pointed arch characterized by an S-shaped curve on both sides, resembling a ship’s keel; commonly found in Islamic architecture

Examples

Islamic keel arch
Several monuments of the Mughal era feature the distinctive keel arch in their design.
The architect explained that a keel arch differs from a traditional pointed arch due to its S-shaped curves.
The mosque’s entrance is adorned with a beautiful keel arch that draws the eye upward.
