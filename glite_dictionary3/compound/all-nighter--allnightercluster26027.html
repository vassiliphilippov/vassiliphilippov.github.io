all-nighter

noun
a night spent awake to work or study
Pronunciation: / ˌɔlˈnaɪtər / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈnaɪtə / (BrE)
A night during which a person stays awake, especially to study or work.

Examples

pull an all-nighter
After pulling an all-nighter to finish his project, Josh was exhausted.
Many students resort to an all-nighter before big exams.
She made coffee to power through the all-nighter at the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym – late-nighter: someone or something active late at night
  • synonym – overnighter: an event or period of staying somewhere overnight
