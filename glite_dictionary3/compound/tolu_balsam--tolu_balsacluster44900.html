tolu balsam

noun
aromatic resin from the tolu tree
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlu ˈbælsəm / (AmE), / ˈtɒluː ˈbælsəm / (BrE)
an aromatic yellowish-brown resin obtained from the South American tolu balsam tree, used especially in cough syrups, perfumes, and as a flavoring
