earless seal

noun
a seal without external ear flaps
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrləs siːl / (AmE), / ˈɪəlɪs siːl / (BrE)
a seal of the family Phocidae, lacking external ear flaps and having hind limbs modified for swimming

Researchers spotted several earless seals swimming near the ice floes.
The earless seal is well adapted to life in cold polar regions.
