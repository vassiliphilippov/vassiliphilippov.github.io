utility man

noun
baseball player able to play several positions
Pronunciation: / juˈtɪl.ə.ti mæn / (AmE), / juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti mæn / (BrE)
A male baseball player who is valued for the ability to play competently at several different positions.

Examples

utility man plays positions
As a utility man, he played outfield, infield, and even caught in a pinch.
Having a reliable utility man gives the manager more options during the season.
The team's utility man filled in at shortstop when the starter was injured.
