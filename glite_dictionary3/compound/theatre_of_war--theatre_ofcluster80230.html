theatre of war

noun
area where military operations occur in war
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːətər əv wɔr / (AmE), / ˈθɪətə əv wɔː / (BrE)
the entire area in which military operations are conducted during a war, including land, sea, and air zones directly involved in fighting
