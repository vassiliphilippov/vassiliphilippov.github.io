in low spirits

adverb
feeling sad or discouraged
Pronunciation: / ɪn loʊ ˈspɪrɪts / (AmE), / ɪn ləʊ ˈspɪərɪts / (BrE)
feeling sad, discouraged, or lacking in enthusiasm or energy

Examples

in low spirits after bad news
After hearing the disappointing news, he sat in low spirits for the rest of the evening.
She was in low spirits after losing her job but tried to remain hopeful.
The rainy weather left everyone in low spirits during the picnic.
