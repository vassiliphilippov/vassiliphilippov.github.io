cleft foot

noun
congenital deformity of the foot
Pronunciation: / klɛft fʊt / (AmE), / klɛft fʊt / (BrE)
a congenital deformity in which the space between the third and fourth toes extends unusually into the body of the foot

Examples

a cleft foot deformity
A cleft foot may be associated with other congenital anomalies.
Surgical treatment can improve function and appearance in patients with a cleft foot.
The newborn was diagnosed with a cleft foot after examination by the pediatrician.
