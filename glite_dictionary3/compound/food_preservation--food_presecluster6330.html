food preservation

noun
methods to keep food from spoiling
Pronunciation: / fuːd prɛzərˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / fuːd prɛzəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the methods or processes used to prevent food from spoiling and to keep it safe and edible for longer periods
