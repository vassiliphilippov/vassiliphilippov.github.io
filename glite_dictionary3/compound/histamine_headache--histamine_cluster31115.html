histamine headache

noun
headache caused by histamine release
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪstəˌmiːn ˈhɛdˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈhɪstəmiːn ˈhɛdeɪk / (BrE)
A recurring headache triggered by the release of histamine from body cells, often causing intense pain.

Examples

severe histamine headache
Doctors may prescribe medications to help manage the pain of a histamine headache.
