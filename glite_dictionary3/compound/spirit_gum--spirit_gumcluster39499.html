spirit gum

noun
adhesive for attaching false hair to skin
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt ˌɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt ˌɡʌm / (BrE)
an adhesive made from gum and ether, used especially for attaching false hair or prosthetics to the skin

Examples
