willow in the wind

noun
person who is graceful and flexible
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪloʊ ɪn ðə wɪnd / (AmE), / ˈwɪləʊ ɪn ðə wɪnd / (BrE)
a person who is graceful, supple, or flexible, especially in movement or character

Her yoga teacher, a true willow in the wind, demonstrated remarkable balance and poise.
On stage, she became a willow in the wind, captivating the audience with her fluid, graceful dance.
