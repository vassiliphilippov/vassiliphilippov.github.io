hole-in-the-wall

noun
small, inconspicuous place; modest shop or restaurant
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊl ɪn ðə wɔl /; / ˌhoʊl ɪn ðə ˈwɔl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊl ɪn ðə wɔːl /; / ˌhəʊl ɪn ðə ˈwɔːl / (BrE)
