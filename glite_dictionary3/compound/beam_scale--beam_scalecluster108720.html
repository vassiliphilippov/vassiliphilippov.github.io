beam scale

noun
type of balance scale with a pivoted bar
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːm skeɪl / (AmE), / ˈbiːm skeɪl / (BrE)
a portable balance device consisting of a pivoted bar with arms of unequal length, used for measuring weight
