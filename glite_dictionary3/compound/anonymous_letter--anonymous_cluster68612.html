anonymous letter

noun
letter without the sender's name
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑnɪməs ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / əˈnɒnɪməs ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a letter sent without indicating the sender's name or identity

Examples

receive an anonymous letter
An anonymous letter arrived at the office, warning us about a possible mistake in the report.
She was surprised to find an anonymous letter in her mailbox, offering kind words of encouragement.
The police took the anonymous letter seriously, as it contained information about a potential crime.
