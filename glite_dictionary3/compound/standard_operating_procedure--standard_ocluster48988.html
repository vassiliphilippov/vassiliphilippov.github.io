standard operating procedure

noun
prescribed routine procedure
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɚd ˈɑpəˌreɪtɪŋ prəˈsiːdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈstændəd ˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ prəˈsiːdʒə / (BrE)
an established, prescribed method to be followed routinely in carrying out a particular operation or in specific circumstances

Examples

standard operating procedure for safety checks
During emergencies, adhering to the standard operating procedure can save lives.
It is standard operating procedure for the staff to check all equipment before the start of a shift.
The company follows a strict standard operating procedure when handling customer complaints.
