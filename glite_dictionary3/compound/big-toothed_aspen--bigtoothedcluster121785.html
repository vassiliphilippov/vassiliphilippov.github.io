big-toothed aspen

noun
North American aspen tree with toothed leaves
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ tuːθt ˈæspən / (AmE), / bɪɡ tuːθt ˈaspən / (BrE)
A species of aspen tree with a narrow crown and leaves with large, coarse teeth, native to eastern North America.

In the fall, the leaves of the big-toothed aspen turn a brilliant yellow.
