President of the United States

proper noun
the chief executive of the US government
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzɪdənt əv ðə ˌjuː.naɪ.tɪd steɪts / (AmE), / ˈprɛzɪdənt əv ðə ˌjuːˈnaɪ.tɪd steɪts / (BrE)
the head of state and head of government of the United States, serving as the chief executive officer of the federal government
