fricative consonant

noun
consonant made by breath friction
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪk.ə.tɪv ˈkɑn.sə.nənt / (AmE), / ˈfrɪk.ə.tɪv ˈkɒn.sə.nənt / (BrE)
a consonant sound produced by forcing breath through a narrow gap in the vocal tract, causing friction
