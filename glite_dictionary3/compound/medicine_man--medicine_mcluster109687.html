medicine man

noun
Native American healer or shaman
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪsən mæn / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪsɪn mæn / (BrE)
A traditional Native American healer or shaman, believed to have spiritual and medicinal powers.

Examples

a medicine man heals people
During the ceremony, the medicine man led the group in traditional chants and rituals.
Many stories have been passed down about the wisdom of the medicine man in ancient tribes.
The tribe called upon their medicine man to heal the sick child.
