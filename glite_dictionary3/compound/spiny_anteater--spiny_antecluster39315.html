spiny anteater

noun
burrowing spiny mammal of Australia/New Guinea
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪni ænˌtidər /; / ˈspaɪni ænˌtiːtər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˈæntiːtə /; / ˈspaɪni ˈænˌtiːtə / (BrE)
a burrowing, egg-laying mammal with spines, a long snout, and claws for eating ants and termites; also called an echidna, native to Australia and New Guinea
