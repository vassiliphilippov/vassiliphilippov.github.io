musculoskeletal system

noun
system of muscles, bones, and joints
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌs.jə.loʊˈskɛl.ə.tl̩ ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˌmʌs.jʊ.ləʊˈskɛl.ɪ.tl̩ ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
the system in the body made up of muscles, bones, joints, tendons, and ligaments, which supports movement and gives the body its form

Examples

the musculoskeletal system supports movement
A healthy diet and regular exercise are important for maintaining the musculoskeletal system.
Doctors often assess the musculoskeletal system when diagnosing joint or muscle pain in patients.
Injuries to the musculoskeletal system can significantly impact a person’s ability to move and perform daily activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – skeletal system: the system of bones and cartilage that supports the body
