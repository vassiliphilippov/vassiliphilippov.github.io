diuretic drug

noun
drug that increases urine flow
Pronunciation: / daɪjʊˈrɛtɪk drʌɡ / (AmE), / daɪjʊəˈrɛtɪk drʌɡ / (BrE)
A drug or substance that increases the flow of urine, helping the body eliminate excess water.
