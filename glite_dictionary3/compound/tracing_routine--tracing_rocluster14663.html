tracing routine

noun
routine that records program execution
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪsɪŋ ruˈtin / (AmE), / ˈtreɪsɪŋ ruːˈtiːn / (BrE)
a routine in a computer program that records a chronological sequence of its execution, often for debugging or analysis purposes

Examples
