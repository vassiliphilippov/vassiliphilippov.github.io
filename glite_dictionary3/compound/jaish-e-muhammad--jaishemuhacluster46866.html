Jaish-e-Muhammad

proper noun
militant Islamist group active in Kashmir
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪʃ eɪ mʊˈhɑːməd / (AmE), / dʒaɪʃ eɪ mʊˈhɑːməd / (BrE)
