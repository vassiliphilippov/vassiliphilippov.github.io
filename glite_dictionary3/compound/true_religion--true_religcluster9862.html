true religion

noun
belief system considered ultimately correct
Pronunciation: / tru rɪˈlɪdʒən / (AmE), / truː rɪˈlɪdʒən / (BrE)
a faith regarded as the ultimate or correct one, often in contrast to other religions

Examples
