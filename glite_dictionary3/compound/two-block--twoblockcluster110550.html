two-block

noun
distance of two city blocks
Pronunciation: / tuː blɑk / (AmE), / tuː blɒk / (BrE)
a distance equivalent to two city blocks, used to describe how far one location is from another, especially in urban areas

Examples

two-block walk
My office is just a two-block walk from here.
The café is located two-block down from the station.
They parked the car a two-block distance from the concert venue.
