electronic reconnaissance

noun
detection and analysis of foreign electromagnetic signals
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˌrɛkəˈnɑsəns / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˌrɛkəˈnɒsəns / (BrE)
The detection, identification, evaluation, and location of foreign electromagnetic emissions (excluding radioactive sources), particularly for military or intelligence purposes.
