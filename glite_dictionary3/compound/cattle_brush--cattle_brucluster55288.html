cattle brush

noun
device for cows to scratch and groom themselves
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬l̩ brʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈkætl brʌʃ / (BrE)
a stationary or rotating device with one or more brushes, designed for cows to rub against for scratching and grooming

Examples

cattle brush for cows
Cows often line up to use the cattle brush after feeding time.
Regular use of a cattle brush can help improve animal welfare and reduce stress among the herd.
The dairy farm installed a cattle brush to keep the cows clean and comfortable.
