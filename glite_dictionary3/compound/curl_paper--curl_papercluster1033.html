curl paper

noun
soft paper for curling hair
Pronunciation: / kɝl ˈpeɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / kɜːl ˈpeɪ.pə / (BrE)
A piece of soft paper used to wrap a lock of hair for curling.

Examples

curl paper for hair
Each lock was carefully rolled in a curl paper to achieve tighter ringlets.
Old-fashioned hairdressers sometimes still use curl paper when setting curls.
She wrapped her damp hair with a curl paper for the night.
