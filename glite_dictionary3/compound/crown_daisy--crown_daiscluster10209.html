crown daisy

noun
Mediterranean plant with yellowish-white flowers
Pronunciation: / kraʊn ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / kraʊn ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a shrubby annual plant of the Mediterranean region, with yellowish-white flowers, often grown as an ornamental or used as a leafy vegetable
