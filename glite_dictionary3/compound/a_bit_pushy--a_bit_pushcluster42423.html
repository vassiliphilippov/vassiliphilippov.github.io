a bit pushy

adjective
somewhat aggressive or assertive
Pronunciation: / ə bɪt ˈpʊʃi / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈpʊʃi / (BrE)
Somewhat aggressive or assertive in manner or behavior; tending to press one's views or wishes a little too strongly.

Examples

a bit pushy with requests
During the meeting, some colleagues found him a bit pushy when sharing his ideas.
My parents say I was a bit pushy as a child, always insisting on getting my way.
She can be a bit pushy if she really wants something done quickly.
