silver tongue

noun
eloquence or persuasive speaking ability
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər tʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə tʌŋ / (BrE)
the ability to speak in a way that is eloquent and highly persuasive

Examples

a silver tongue speaker
He was known for his silver tongue and could charm even the toughest crowd.
Thanks to his silver tongue, the team secured funding for the new project.
With her silver tongue, she managed to convince the whole committee to back her proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – eloquence: fluent or persuasive speaking or writing
  • synonym – gift of the gab: the ability to speak easily and confidently, especially in a way that makes people want to listen
  • degree lower – glibness: fluent and voluble but insincere or shallow speech
