Late Latin

noun
the form of Latin used 3rd–8th centuries AD
Pronunciation: / leɪt ˈlæt̬.ən /; / leɪt ˈlæt.ɪn / (AmE), / leɪt ˈlæt.ɪn / (BrE)
the form of the Latin language used between the 3rd and 8th centuries AD, marking the transition from Classical Latin to Medieval Latin
