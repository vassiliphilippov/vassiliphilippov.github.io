battle royal

noun
multiplayer last-player-standing game mode
Pronunciation: / \u02ccb\u00e6t\u0259l \u02c8r\u0254\u026a\u0259l / (AmE), / \u02ccb\u00e6tl \u02c8r\u0254\u026a\u0259l / (BrE)
(in video games) a multiplayer game mode or genre in which many players or teams compete, often until only one player or team remains.
