nasal bone

noun
bone forming the bridge of the nose
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.zəl boʊn / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.zəl bəʊn / (BrE)
either of a pair of elongated, rectangular bones forming the bridge of the nose in humans and some animals
