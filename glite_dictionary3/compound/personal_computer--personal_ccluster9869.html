personal computer

noun
a computer for individual use
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl kəmˈpjuːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
A small digital computer intended for use by one person at a time, typically based on a microprocessor.

Examples

new personal computer
I bought a new personal computer for my home office last week.
Many students use a personal computer to complete their assignments and research online.
The company's IT department is responsible for maintaining every personal computer in the building.
