traveller's tree

noun
palm-like plant; source of water for travelers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævələrz ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈtrævələz ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tall, palm-like plant native to Madagascar, known for its edible nuts and for storing clear sap in its leafstalks, which can provide water in emergencies.
