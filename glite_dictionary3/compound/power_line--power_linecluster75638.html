power line

noun
cable distributing electricity
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.ɚ laɪn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.ə laɪn / (BrE)
A cable or set of cables used for transmitting and distributing electricity from a power source to consumers.

Examples
