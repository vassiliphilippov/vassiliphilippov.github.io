cat burglar

noun
burglar who climbs and enters stealthily
Pronunciation: / kæt ˈbɝɡlɚ / (AmE), / kæt ˈbɜːɡlə / (BrE)
a burglar who enters buildings by climbing, especially through upper windows or roofs, in a stealthy and agile way

Examples

a cat burglar climbs roofs
Police suspect a cat burglar is responsible for the recent string of neighborhood break-ins.
The cat burglar scaled the wall and slipped in through an open window without making a sound.
The movie featured a master cat burglar who always left a calling card at every crime scene.
