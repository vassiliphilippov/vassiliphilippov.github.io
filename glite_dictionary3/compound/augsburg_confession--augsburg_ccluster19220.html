Augsburg Confession

noun
foundational Lutheran doctrinal document (1530)
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɡzˌbɝɡ kənˈfɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡzbɜːɡ kənˈfɛʃən / (BrE)
an official statement of Lutheran principles, drawn up in 1530, defending the catholicity of Lutheran doctrine and justifying reforms; still recognized in Lutheran churches today
