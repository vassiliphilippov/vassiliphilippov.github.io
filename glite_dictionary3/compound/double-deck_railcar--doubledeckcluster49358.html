double-deck railcar

noun
railcar with two levels for passengers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl ˌdɛk ˈreɪlˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈdʌbl dɛk ˈreɪlkɑː / (BrE)
a railcar or passenger train carriage with two levels of seating, designed to carry more passengers than a single-level car
