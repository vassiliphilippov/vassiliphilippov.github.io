shopping mall

noun
large complex of shops and restaurants
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpɪŋ mɔl / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ mɔːl / (BrE)
A large indoor or outdoor complex containing many different shops, restaurants, and other businesses, usually with parking facilities.

Examples

crowded shopping mall
A new shopping mall opened in the city last month, attracting large crowds.
The shopping mall has a wide variety of stores, a food court, and a cinema.
They decided to meet at the shopping mall after lunch.
