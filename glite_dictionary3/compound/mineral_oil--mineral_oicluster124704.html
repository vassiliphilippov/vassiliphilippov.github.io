mineral oil

noun
oil distilled from petroleum, used in medicine and industry
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnərəl ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl ɔɪl / (BrE)
a colorless or pale, oily liquid distilled from petroleum, used especially as a laxative and in making cosmetics, lubricants, and ointments

Examples

mineral oil for skin
Mineral oil can also be used to lubricate small mechanical parts.
Some lotions and creams list mineral oil as a main ingredient.
The doctor recommended mineral oil to relieve her constipation.
