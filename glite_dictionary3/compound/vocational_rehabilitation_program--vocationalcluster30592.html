vocational rehabilitation program

noun
program for job training and employment support
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˌriː(h)əˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˌriː(h)əˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a program designed to help individuals with disabilities or impairments gain or return to employment, typically through job training and related support services
