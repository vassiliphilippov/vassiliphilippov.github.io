black-tailed deer

noun
a North American deer with a black-tipped tail
Pronunciation: / ˌblæk ˈteɪld ˌdɪr / (AmE), / ˌblæk ˈteɪld ˌdɪə / (BrE)
a deer found in western North America, characterized by a black-tipped tail and considered a subspecies of the mule deer
