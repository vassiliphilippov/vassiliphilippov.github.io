darning needle

noun
large-eyed sewing needle
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrnɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnɪŋ ˌniːdl̩ / (BrE)
A long needle with a large eye, used for sewing with heavy thread or for embroidery.

Examples

a darning needle for thick thread
A darning needle is essential when you're working with thick yarn or thread.
She threaded the darning needle carefully to repair the hole in her sweater.
The old sewing box contained a silver darning needle passed down from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • synonym – embroidery needle: needle designed for embroidery, with a large eye
