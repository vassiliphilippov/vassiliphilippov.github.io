United Nations Day

proper noun
international observance marking the UN's founding
Pronunciation: / juˈnaɪt̬ɪd ˈneɪʃənz deɪ / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd ˈneɪʃənz deɪ / (BrE)
An international observance held on October 24th each year to commemorate the founding of the United Nations in 1945.

United Nations Day celebration
Every year, schools around the world celebrate United Nations Day with cultural performances and discussions about global cooperation.
