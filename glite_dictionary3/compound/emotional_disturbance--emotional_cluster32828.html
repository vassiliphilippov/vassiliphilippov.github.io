emotional disturbance

noun
disorder with dominant emotional disturbance
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənl dɪsˈtɝːbəns / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl dɪsˈtɜːbəns / (BrE)
A mental disorder characterized mainly by significant disturbances in mood or emotion, not due to detectable organic brain abnormalities.

Examples

severe emotional disturbance
Her unpredictable mood swings were eventually traced to an emotional disturbance rather than a physical illness.
Support programs are available for students with emotional disturbances to help them manage their behavior.
The child was diagnosed with an emotional disturbance that affected his performance at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mood disorder: a psychological disorder characterized by the elevation or lowering of a person's mood
