storm lantern

noun
wind-protected portable oil lamp
Pronunciation: / stɔrm ˈlæntɚn / (AmE), / stɔːm ˈlæntən / (BrE)
A portable oil lamp with a glass chimney and a ventilated metal cover designed to protect the flame from wind and rain.

Examples

old storm lantern
He carried an old-fashioned storm lantern when walking through the dark countryside.
The storm lantern provided a steady light during the thunderstorm.
We hung a storm lantern on the porch to keep the area lit in the evening breeze.
