toxaemia of pregnancy

noun
pregnancy disorder with high blood pressure and swelling
Pronunciation: / tɑɡˈzimɪə əv prəˈɡnænsi / (AmE), / tɒkˈsiːmiə əv prɪɡˈnænsi / (BrE)
A medical condition during pregnancy marked by high blood pressure, swelling, and protein in the urine, potentially leading to serious complications if untreated.
