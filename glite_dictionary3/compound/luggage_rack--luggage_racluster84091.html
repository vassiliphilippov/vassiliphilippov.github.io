luggage rack

noun
frame or shelf for carrying luggage
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌɡɪdʒ ræk / (AmE), / ˈlʌɡɪdʒ ræk / (BrE)
A framework or shelf for carrying luggage, typically positioned above the seats in a train or on the roof of a car.

Examples

luggage rack above seats
Please make sure your suitcase fits securely on the luggage rack before the train departs.
She placed her bag on the luggage rack above her seat as soon as she boarded the train.
The car’s luggage rack made it easy to transport extra suitcases on their road trip.
