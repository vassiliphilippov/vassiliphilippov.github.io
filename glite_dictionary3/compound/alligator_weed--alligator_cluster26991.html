alligator weed

noun
aquatic plant that clogs waterways
Pronunciation: / ˈælɪˌɡeɪtər wiːd / (AmE), / ˈælɪˌɡeɪtə wiːd / (BrE)
a vigorous South American aquatic plant with grasslike leaves and small spikes of white flowers, known for forming dense floating masses that clog waterways

Examples

alligator weed invasion
Alligator weed is considered an invasive species in many regions outside its native South America.
Authorities have implemented control measures to prevent the spread of alligator weed in local waterways.
