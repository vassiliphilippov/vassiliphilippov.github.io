social death

noun
state of social exclusion or loss of status
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ʃəl dɛθ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl dɛθ / (BrE)
the condition of being ostracized, excluded, or losing social status and relationships within a community

Examples

social death after scandal
After the scandal, he experienced social death and was ignored at all social gatherings.
Losing her trusted friends resulted in a feeling of social death that was hard to overcome.
Many teenagers fear social death if they are excluded from the most popular group at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alienation: feeling of being isolated or estranged
  • synonym – ostracism: exclusion from a society or group
