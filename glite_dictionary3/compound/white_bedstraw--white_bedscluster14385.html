white bedstraw

noun
a Eurasian herb with small white flowers
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈbɛdˌstrɔ / (AmE), / waɪt ˈbɛdstrɔː / (BrE)
A Eurasian herb (Galium mollugo) with clusters of small white flowers, often found in meadows and naturalized in North America.
