dumpy level

noun
a type of surveyor's leveling instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmpi ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌmpi ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
a surveyor's instrument consisting of a short, fixed telescope mounted on a horizontally rotating table and equipped with a spirit level, used to measure horizontal levels

Examples

use a dumpy level for surveying
Before beginning construction, the engineers checked the ground flatness with a dumpy level.
The surveyor carefully set up the dumpy level to measure the elevation difference between the two points.
Training included hands-on use of the dumpy level for accurate site measurements.
