rocket larkspur

noun
an ornamental plant with tall spiky flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkɪt ˈlɑrkˌspɝ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt ˈlɑːkspɜː / (BrE)
a plant of southern Europe with tall, unbranched spikes of blue, purple, or pink flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental; sometimes placed in the genus Delphinium.
