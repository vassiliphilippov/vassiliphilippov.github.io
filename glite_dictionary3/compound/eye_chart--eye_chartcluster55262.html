eye chart

noun
chart used to test eyesight
Pronunciation: / aɪ tʃɑrt / (AmE), / aɪ tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a chart displaying letters or symbols of varying sizes, used to measure visual acuity during an eye examination

Examples

eye chart test
Every year at school, we had to take an eye chart test during our physical exams.
She struggled to make out the smallest letters on the eye chart.
The optometrist asked me to read the eye chart from across the room.
