genus Aerobacter

noun
genus of aerobic bacteria
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌɛəroʊˈbæktər / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌɛərəʊˈbæktə / (BrE)
A genus of aerobic bacteria widely distributed in nature, formerly used for several species now reclassified under other genera.
