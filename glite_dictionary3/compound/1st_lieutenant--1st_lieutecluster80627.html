1st lieutenant

noun
military rank above 2nd lieutenant, below captain
Pronunciation: / fɜrst luːˈtɛnənt / (AmE), / fɜːst lɛfˈtɛnənt / (BrE)
a commissioned officer in the Army, Air Force, or Marines who ranks above a second lieutenant and below a captain
