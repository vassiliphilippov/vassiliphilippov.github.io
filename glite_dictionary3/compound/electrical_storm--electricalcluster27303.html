electrical storm

noun
storm with thunder and lightning
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl stɔrm / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkəl stɔːm / (BrE)
A storm characterized by thunder and lightning, typically accompanied by heavy rain or hail.

Examples

severe electrical storm
An electrical storm caused the power to go out in several neighborhoods.
Pilots try to avoid flying through an electrical storm whenever possible.
