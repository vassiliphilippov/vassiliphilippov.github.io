epicyclic gear

noun
gear revolving around sun gear in a gear train
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈsaɪklɪk ɡɪr / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈsɪklɪk ɡɪə / (BrE)
a gear in an epicyclic gear train that revolves around a central sun gear, commonly used in mechanical transmissions to alter speed or torque
