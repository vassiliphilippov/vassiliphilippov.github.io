Book of Psalms

noun
a book of the Bible containing psalms
Pronunciation: / bʊk əv ˈsɑːmz / (AmE), / bʊk əv ˈsɑːmz / (BrE)
the collection of religious songs, prayers, and poems included as a book in the Old Testament of the Bible, traditionally used in Jewish and Christian worship
