box spring

noun
spring-supported bed base
Pronunciation: / bɑks sprɪŋ / (AmE), / bɒks sprɪŋ / (BrE)
A strong, cloth-covered frame containing coiled springs, used as a base to support a mattress.

Examples

box spring under mattress
A box spring can help extend the life of your mattress.
The movers carried the heavy box spring up the stairs.
We bought a new box spring to go with our mattress.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mattress: the padded part of a bed that is placed on top of the box spring or bed frame
