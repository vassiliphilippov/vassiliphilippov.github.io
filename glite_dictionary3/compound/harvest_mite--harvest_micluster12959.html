harvest mite

noun
mite larva causing skin irritation
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrvəst maɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪst maɪt / (BrE)
a tiny larval mite that feeds on the blood of vertebrates, including humans, often causing severe skin irritation or itching

Examples

harvest mite bite marks
After walking through the field, she noticed several harvest mite bites on her ankles.
Farmers are often affected by harvest mites during the late summer months.
The bite of a harvest mite can cause severe itching and red bumps on the skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chigger: another name for the harvest mite larva, especially in American English
