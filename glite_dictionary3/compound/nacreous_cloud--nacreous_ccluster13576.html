nacreous cloud

noun
high-altitude iridescent cloud
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkriəs klaʊd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkriəs klaʊd / (BrE)
A luminous, iridescent cloud occurring at high altitudes, usually visible when the sun is just below the horizon.

Examples

colorful nacreous cloud
A rare nacreous cloud painted the winter twilight with shimmering colors above the Arctic Circle.
Photographers gathered to capture the vibrant hues of the nacreous cloud at dawn.
The sudden appearance of a nacreous cloud delighted scientists observing the upper atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym – polar stratospheric cloud: a cloud forming in the polar stratosphere at very low temperatures, often a synonym for nacreous cloud
