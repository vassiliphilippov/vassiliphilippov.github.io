V sign

noun
gesture formed with raised V-shaped fingers
Pronunciation: / vi saɪn / (AmE), / viː saɪn / (BrE)
a gesture in which the index and middle fingers are raised and parted to form a V shape, typically signifying victory or peace

Examples

make a V sign
Making a V sign is popular in photographs, especially among young people.
She flashed a V sign to express peace during the protest.
The crowd cheered and made the V sign when the team won the match.
