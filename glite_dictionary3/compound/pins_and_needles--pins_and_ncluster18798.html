pins and needles

noun
state of nervous anxiety or anticipation
Pronunciation: / pɪnz ənd ˈniːdəlz / (AmE), / pɪnz ənd ˈniːdlz / (BrE)
a state of nervous anxiety, excitement, or anticipation, often accompanied by restlessness

Examples

on pins and needles waiting
I was on pins and needles while waiting for the phone call.
The audience sat on pins and needles as the winner was announced.
Waiting for the test results, she was on pins and needles all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anxious: experiencing worry, unease, or nervousness
  • antonym – calm: not showing nervousness or anxiety; relaxed
