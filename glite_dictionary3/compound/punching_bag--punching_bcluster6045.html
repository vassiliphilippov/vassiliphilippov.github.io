punching bag

noun
bag used for boxing practice
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃɪŋ bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃɪŋ bæɡ / (BrE)
A sturdy, often inflated or padded bag, typically suspended, that is punched repeatedly for training in boxing or other combat sports.

Examples

hit the punching bag
He trained every evening by hitting the punching bag in his garage.
She let out her frustration on the punching bag after a long day.
The gym installed a new punching bag for the boxing classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – heavy bag: a large, heavy punching bag used for strength training
  • synonym – speed bag: a small punching bag used to improve hand speed and timing
