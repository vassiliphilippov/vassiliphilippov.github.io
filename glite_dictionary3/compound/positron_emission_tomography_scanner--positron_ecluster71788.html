positron emission tomography scanner

noun
medical imaging device for metabolic processes (PET)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑzɪˌtrɑn ɪˈmɪʃən təˌmɑɡrəfi ˈskænər / (AmE), / ˈpɒzɪˌtrɒn ɪˈmɪʃən təˈmɒɡrəfi ˈskænə / (BrE)
a medical imaging device that creates detailed cross-sectional images of metabolic processes in the body using positron emission tomography (PET) technology
