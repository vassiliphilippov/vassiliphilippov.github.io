mandibular notch

noun
curved depression in the lower jawbone
Pronunciation: / mænˈdɪbjələr nɑtʃ / (AmE), / mænˈdɪbjʊlə nɒtʃ / (BrE)
a small curved depression in the upper border of the lower jawbone (mandible), located between the condylar and coronoid processes

Examples

mandibular notch fracture
A fracture near the mandibular notch can impair jaw movement.
During the surgery, the dentist carefully avoided damaging the mandibular notch.
The nerve passes through the mandibular notch before reaching the lower teeth.
