heart murmur

noun
abnormal heart sound
Pronunciation: / hɑrt ˈmɝː.mɚ / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈmɜː.mə / (BrE)
an abnormal sound heard during a heartbeat, sometimes indicating a problem with the heart valves

Examples

detect a heart murmur
An untreated heart murmur can sometimes lead to more serious heart conditions.
Many heart murmurs in children are harmless and resolve over time.
