time interval

noun
a period between two times or events
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈɪntərˌvɛl / (AmE), / taɪm ˈɪntəvəl / (BrE)
a definite, measurable period of time between two specific points or events

Examples

time interval between events
The doctor measured the time interval between the patient’s heartbeats.
There was a short time interval between the two explosions.
You should allow a sufficient time interval between meals and exercise.
