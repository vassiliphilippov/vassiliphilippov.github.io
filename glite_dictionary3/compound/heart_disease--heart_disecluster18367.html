heart disease

noun
disease affecting the heart
Pronunciation: / hɑrt dɪˈziz / (AmE), / hɑːt dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
Any condition that affects the normal functioning of the heart, including various disorders such as coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and heart failure.
