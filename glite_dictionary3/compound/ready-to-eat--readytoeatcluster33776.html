ready-to-eat

adjective
food that can be eaten as sold
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛdi tə ˈit / (AmE), / ˌrɛdi tə ˈiːt / (BrE)
(of food) prepared in advance and able to be eaten without further cooking or preparation.

Examples

ready-to-eat meal
Most ready-to-eat foods are clearly labeled on supermarket shelves.
She packed several ready-to-eat snacks for the road trip.
The store offers a wide selection of ready-to-eat meals for busy professionals.
