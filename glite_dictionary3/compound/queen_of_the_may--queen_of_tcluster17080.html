queen of the May

noun
girl selected as queen for May Day festival
Pronunciation: / kwiːn əv ðə meɪ / (AmE), / kwiːn ɒv ðə meɪ / (BrE)
the girl who is chosen to preside as queen during a May Day festival, often crowned and celebrated as part of traditional festivities
