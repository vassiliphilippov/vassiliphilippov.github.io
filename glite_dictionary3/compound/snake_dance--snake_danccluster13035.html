snake dance

noun
serpentine group dance or procession
Pronunciation: / sneɪk dæns / (AmE), / sneɪk dɑːns / (BrE)
A procession or dance in which people advance in a single-file, winding or serpentine path, often as part of a celebration or sporting event.

Examples

join a snake dance parade
At the end of the parade, everyone joined in a snake dance through the streets.
People held hands and moved in a snake dance around the bonfire.
The students performed a lively snake dance after their team won the championship.
