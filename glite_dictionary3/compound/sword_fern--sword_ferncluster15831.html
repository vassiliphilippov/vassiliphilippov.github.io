sword fern

noun
a tropical fern with sword-shaped fronds
Pronunciation: / sɔrd fɚn / (AmE), / sɔːd fɜːn / (BrE)
any of several tropical ferns, especially those with long, narrow, sword-shaped fronds, including the species from which the Boston fern developed

Examples

sword fern plant
Gardeners often choose the sword fern for its attractive, cascading fronds.
Many homes display a sword fern in hanging baskets due to its easy care and lush appearance.
The sword fern is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
