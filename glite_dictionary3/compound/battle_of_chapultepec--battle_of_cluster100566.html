Battle of Chapultepec

proper noun
decisive 1847 battle in the Mexican-American War
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l əv tʃəˌpʊl.təˈpɛk / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv tʃəˌpʊl.təˈpɛk / (BrE)
a decisive battle during the Mexican-American War (1847) in which American forces defeated Mexican troops at Chapultepec, near Mexico City
