postage franking

noun
postage marks showing payment of fees
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstɪdʒ ˈfræŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪdʒ ˈfræŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
The stamps or markings on a mail item indicating payment of postage and acceptance for delivery by a postal system.

Examples

postage franking on envelope
All outgoing letters must have the correct postage franking visible on the envelope.
She collected envelopes with rare postage franking from different countries.
The lack of postage franking caused the mail to be returned to the sender.
