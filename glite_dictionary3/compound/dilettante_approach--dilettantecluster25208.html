dilettante approach

noun
superficial or amateurish attitude
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪləˈtænt əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌdɪlɪˈtænti əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an attitude or method characterized by superficial or amateurish interest or lack of deep commitment

Examples

dilettante approach to work
Despite his enthusiasm, his dilettante approach prevented him from achieving real expertise.
Her dilettante approach to writing was evident in the lack of research in her articles.
The committee criticized the team's dilettante approach to solving the complex problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amateur approach: an amateur or inexperienced method
  • antonym – professional approach: a method showing expertise and commitment
