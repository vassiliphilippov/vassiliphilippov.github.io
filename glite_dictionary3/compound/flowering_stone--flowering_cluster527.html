flowering stone

noun
African succulent plant resembling a stone
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊərɪŋ stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈflaʊərɪŋ stəʊn / (BrE)
A succulent plant of the genus Lithops, native to Africa, characterized by thick leaves that resemble stones and produce solitary yellow or white flowers.
