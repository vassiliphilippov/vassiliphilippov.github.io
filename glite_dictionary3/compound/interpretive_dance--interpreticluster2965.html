interpretive dance

noun
modern dance expressing emotions or stories
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɜrprətɪv dæns / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprətɪv dɑːns / (BrE)
A style of modern dance in which movements are used to express emotions or narrate a story.

Examples

interpretive dance performance
She chose to express her feelings through an interpretive dance at the school talent show.
The artist uses interpretive dance to explore themes of love and loss in her work.
The performance featured an interpretive dance that captured the joy and sorrow of the story.
