Mayberry Machiavelli

noun
cunning or manipulative schemer
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪˌbɛri ˌmɑːkiəˈvɛli / (AmE), / ˈmeɪˌbɛri ˌmakɪəˈvɛli / (BrE)
