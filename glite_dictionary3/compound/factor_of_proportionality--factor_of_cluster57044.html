factor of proportionality

noun
constant in proportional relationship
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ əv prəˌpɔr.ʃəˈnæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tər əv prəˌpɔː.ʃəˈnæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
