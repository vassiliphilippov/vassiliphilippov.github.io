order Cypriniformes

noun
an order of freshwater fishes (carps, loaches, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚ saɪˌprɪnɪˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə saɪˌprɪnɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of freshwater fishes that includes carps, minnows, loaches, and suckers, sometimes considered a suborder of Ostariophysi.
