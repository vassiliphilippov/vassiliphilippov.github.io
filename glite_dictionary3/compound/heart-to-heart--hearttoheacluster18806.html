heart-to-heart

noun
private intimate conversation
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑrt tə ˈhɑrt / (AmE), / ˌhɑːt tə ˈhɑːt / (BrE)
a serious and intimate conversation held in private, typically to discuss personal feelings or problems

Examples

have a heart-to-heart
A late-night heart-to-heart brought them closer together.
After the argument, she felt they needed a heart-to-heart to clear the air.
They decided to have a heart-to-heart about their relationship problems.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – chat: an informal conversation
  • synonym – confession: the act of admitting or discussing something personal with someone
  • degree higher – confrontation: a direct and sometimes tense discussion about feelings or problems
