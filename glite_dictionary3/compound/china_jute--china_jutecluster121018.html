China jute

noun
Asian plant yielding strong fiber
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪnə dʒut / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnə dʒuːt / (BrE)
a tall annual herb or subshrub of tropical Asia, with velvety leaves and yellow flowers, grown for its strong fiber; also naturalized in southeastern Europe and the United States
