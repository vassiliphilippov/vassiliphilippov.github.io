waist-high

adverb
reaching up to the waist
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌhaɪ / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌhaɪ / (BrE)
Reaching or extending up to the level of the waist.

Examples

waist-high grass
After the storm, snow drifts were waist-high along the driveway.
Children waded in the waist-high water, laughing and splashing each other.
The grass in the abandoned field grew waist-high during the summer.
