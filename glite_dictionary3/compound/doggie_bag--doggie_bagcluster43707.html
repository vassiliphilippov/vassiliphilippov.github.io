doggie bag

noun
bag for taking home restaurant leftovers
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑɡi bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡi bæɡ / (BrE)
A bag provided by a restaurant to take home leftover food not eaten during the meal.

Examples

restaurant doggie bag
After the large dinner, there was enough steak left for a doggie bag.
He never leaves food on his plate, so he has no need for a doggie bag.
She asked the waiter for a doggie bag so she could enjoy the rest of her meal at home.
