portal system

noun
system of veins carrying blood to the liver
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔr.t̬əl ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.təl ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a system of veins that carries blood from the abdominal organs to the liver

Examples

the portal system of veins
Cirrhosis can disrupt the normal flow of blood through the portal system.
Diseases affecting the portal system can seriously impair liver function.
The portal system plays a crucial role in detoxifying blood from the digestive tract.
