folding door

noun
door that opens by folding back in sections
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊldɪŋ dɔr / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldɪŋ dɔː / (BrE)
A door designed for interior use that opens by folding back in several sections rather than swinging on hinges.

Examples

folding door between rooms
The folding door moves smoothly along its track and is easy to open.
The kitchen was separated from the dining room by a folding door.
They installed a folding door in the closet to save space.
