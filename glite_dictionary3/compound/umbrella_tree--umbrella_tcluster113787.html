umbrella tree

noun
Australian small tree with compound leaves
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbrɛl.ə tri / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛl.ə triː / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or small tree native to Australia and northern New Guinea, with palmately compound leaves, often grown as an ornamental plant

Examples

indoor umbrella tree
Many homes feature an umbrella tree indoors because it is easy to care for and attractive.
Native to Australia, the umbrella tree adapts well to a variety of climate conditions.
