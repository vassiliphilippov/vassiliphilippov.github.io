love bite

noun
mark on skin caused by kissing or sucking
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌv ˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌbaɪt / (BrE)
A temporary red or purple mark on the skin caused by kissing or sucking, especially by a lover.
