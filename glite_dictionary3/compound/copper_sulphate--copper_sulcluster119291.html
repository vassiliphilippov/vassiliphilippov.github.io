copper sulphate

noun
a blue chemical compound used as a fungicide
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpər ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə ˈsʌlfeɪt / (BrE)
a bright blue crystalline chemical compound, used chiefly as a fungicide and in electroplating, obtained by the action of sulfuric acid on copper oxide

Examples
