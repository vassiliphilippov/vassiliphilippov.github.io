silk oak

noun
Australian timber tree with fernlike leaves
Pronunciation: / sɪlk oʊk / (AmE), / sɪlk əʊk / (BrE)
Any of several Australian timber trees, especially Grevillea robusta, with fernlike foliage and mottled wood often used in cabinetry and veneering.
