running hand

noun
cursive handwriting style with connected letters
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ hænd / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ hand / (BrE)
a style of rapid handwriting in which letters are fully formed and connected cursively within words, without lifting the pen from the paper

Examples

running hand script style
She admired her grandmother's elegant running hand on the letters she sent.
Teachers once required students to master running hand as part of their penmanship lessons.
The original manuscript was written in a neat running hand that was difficult to reproduce.
