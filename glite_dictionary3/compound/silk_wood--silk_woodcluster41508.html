silk wood

noun
tropical American tree with silky fiber
Pronunciation: / sɪlk wʊd / (AmE), / sɪlk wʊd / (BrE)
A fast-growing tropical American evergreen tree with white flowers, fleshy edible fruit, silky fiber in its bark used for cordage, and wood valued for staves.

Examples

silk wood tree trunk
Craftsmen prefer silk wood for making staves due to its durability and smooth finish.

The fruit of the silk wood is both edible and enjoyed by locals in tropical regions.
