sand fly

noun
disease-carrying biting fly
Pronunciation: / sænd flaɪ / (AmE), / sænd flaɪ / (BrE)
a small, biting fly of warm climates, whose bloodsucking female can transmit diseases such as sandfly fever and leishmaniasis

Examples

sand fly bite
Sand flies are known to thrive in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
Researchers are developing new ways to protect people from sand flies in affected areas.
The bite of a sand fly can transmit dangerous diseases such as leishmaniasis.
