resistance furnace

noun
electric furnace heated by resistance
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstəns ˈfɜrnɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstəns ˈfɜːnɪs / (BrE)
An electric furnace in which heat is produced by passing an electric current through a resistor.

Examples

industrial resistance furnace
He replaced the old kiln with a modern resistance furnace to improve efficiency.
Industries often rely on a resistance furnace for controlled heat treatment processes.
The laboratory used a resistance furnace to heat samples to precise temperatures.
