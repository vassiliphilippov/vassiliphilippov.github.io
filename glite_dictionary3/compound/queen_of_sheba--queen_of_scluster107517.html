Queen of Sheba

proper noun
legendary queen known for wisdom and wealth
Pronunciation: / kwiːn əv ˈʃiːbə / (AmE), / kwiːn əv ˈʃiːbə / (BrE)
A legendary queen famed for her wisdom and wealth, mentioned in biblical, Qur'anic, and Ethiopian traditions, who is said to have visited King Solomon.

Examples

the Queen of Sheba visits Solomon
He called her the Queen of Sheba because of her elegance and intelligence.
Her wealth was compared to that of the Queen of Sheba.
The story of the Queen of Sheba has fascinated historians and writers for centuries.
