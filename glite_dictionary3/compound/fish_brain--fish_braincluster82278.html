fish brain

noun
forgetful or absent-minded person (slang)
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ breɪn / (AmE), / fɪʃ breɪn / (BrE)
(informal, slang) A forgetful or absent-minded person; used to describe someone who is not attentive or who easily forgets things.
