letter-perfect

adjective
accurate to the smallest detail
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtər ˌpɜrfɪkt / (AmE), / ˈletə ˌpɜːfɪkt / (BrE)
Completely accurate or correct in every detail, especially by following the exact words of something to be remembered or recited.

Examples

letter-perfect performance
His translation was letter-perfect, capturing the original meaning word for word.
She gave a letter-perfect recitation of the poem during the competition.
The actor was letter-perfect in every line of his script.
