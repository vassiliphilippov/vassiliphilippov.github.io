rust-resistant

adjective
not easily affected by rust
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌst rɪˌzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈrʌst rɪˌzɪstənt / (BrE)
(of a material or product) not easily damaged or affected by rust.

Examples

rust-resistant metal tools
I always buy rust-resistant screws for bathroom projects to prevent corrosion.
The new garden tools are rust-resistant, so they last much longer outdoors.
This pan is made from rust-resistant stainless steel, making it ideal for everyday use.
