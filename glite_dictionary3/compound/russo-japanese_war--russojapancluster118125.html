Russo-Japanese War

noun
war between Russia and Japan (1904–05)
Pronunciation: / ˌruːsoʊ dʒəˈpæniz wɔɹ / (AmE), / ˌrʊsəʊ dʒəˈpæniːz wɔː / (BrE)
the war fought between Russia and Japan from 1904 to 1905, resulting in a Japanese victory and giving Japan control over Korea and Manchuria
