tax system

noun
system for assessing and collecting taxes
Pronunciation: / tæk ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / tæks ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
The set of laws and procedures by which a government assesses and collects taxes.

Debates about the fairness of the tax system are common during election years.
