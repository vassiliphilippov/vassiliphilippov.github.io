nighty night

noun
playful way to say goodnight
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪti ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈnaɪti ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
A playful or affectionate way of saying goodnight, often used to children.

Examples

say nighty night to children
She tucked her daughter into bed and whispered, 'nighty night.'
The babysitter said 'nighty night' before turning off the bedroom light.
Waving from the doorway, he called out, 'nighty night, everyone!'
