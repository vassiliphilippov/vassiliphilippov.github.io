Oom Paul Kruger

proper noun
Boer statesman; President of Transvaal
Pronunciation: / um pɔl ˈkruːɡər / (AmE), / uːm pɔːl ˈkruːɡə / (BrE)
The popular name of Paul Kruger (1825–1904), a Boer statesman who was President of the South African Republic (Transvaal) and a prominent leader during the Boer War.

Oom Paul Kruger led Transvaal
Oom Paul Kruger was a central figure in South African history during the late nineteenth century.
