double-hung window

noun
window with two vertically sliding sashes
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbl ˈhʌŋ ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˌdʌbl ˈhʌŋ ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
