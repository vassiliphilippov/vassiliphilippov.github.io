fulminic acid

noun
unstable, explosive acid (HCNO)
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈmɪnɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / fʊlˈmɪnɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
An unstable acid (HCNO) that exists primarily in the form of explosive salts and esters, isomeric with cyanic acid.
