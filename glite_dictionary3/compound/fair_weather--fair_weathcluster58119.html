fair weather

noun
pleasant, moderate weather
Pronunciation: / fɛr ˈwɛðər / (AmE), / feə ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
Weather that is pleasant and not extreme, usually dry and suitable for outdoor activities.

Examples

enjoy fair weather
Sailors prefer to travel in fair weather to avoid rough seas.
The festival will take place outdoors, provided there is fair weather.
We decided to go for a picnic because the fair weather was perfect for it.

  • synonym – fine weather: weather that is clear and pleasant
