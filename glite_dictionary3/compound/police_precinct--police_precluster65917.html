police precinct

noun
local area served by a police division
Pronunciation: / pəˈlis ˈpriː.sɪŋkt / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈpriː.sɪŋkt / (BrE)
A district in a city or town that is served by a particular division of the police force.

Examples

police precinct boundaries
Detective Ramirez transferred from the downtown police precinct to one in the suburbs last year.
Each police precinct organizes its own community outreach programs to engage with local residents.
The city promised to increase patrols in every police precinct after the recent string of burglaries.
