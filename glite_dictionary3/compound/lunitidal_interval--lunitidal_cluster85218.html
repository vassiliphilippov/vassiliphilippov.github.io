lunitidal interval

noun
time between moon's crossing and next local high tide
Pronunciation: / ˌluːnɪˈtaɪdəl ˈɪntɚvəl / (AmE), / ˌluːnɪˈtaɪdəl ˈɪntəvəl / (BrE)
the time period between the moon's transit over a particular meridian and the following high tide at that location
