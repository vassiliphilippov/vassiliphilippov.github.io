workers' rights

noun
legal and social rights of workers
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkərz raɪts / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkəz raɪts / (BrE)
the legal and social rights entitled to people in employment, such as fair wages, safe working conditions, and the right to organize

Examples

workers' rights protection
Protesters gathered outside the factory to demand improvements in workers' rights.
The new law aims to strengthen workers' rights in the manufacturing sector.
Unions play a crucial role in defending workers' rights around the world.
