Athapaskan language

noun
a group of North American indigenous languages
Pronunciation: / ˌæθəˈpæskən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌæθəˈpæskən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of a group of related indigenous languages spoken in parts of North America, primarily in Alaska, western Canada, and the southwestern United States
