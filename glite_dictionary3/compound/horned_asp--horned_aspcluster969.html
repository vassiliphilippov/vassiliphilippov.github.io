horned asp

noun
venomous viper with eye spines
Pronunciation: / hɔrn(d) æsp / (AmE), / hɔːnd æsp / (BrE)
A highly venomous viper native to northern Africa and southwestern Asia, distinguished by a horny spine above each eye.

Examples

dangerous horned asp
A zoologist carefully studied the coloration patterns of the horned asp.
Explorers in the Sahara are often warned about the horned asp and its potent venom.
The horned asp is well known for the prominent horns above its eyes.
