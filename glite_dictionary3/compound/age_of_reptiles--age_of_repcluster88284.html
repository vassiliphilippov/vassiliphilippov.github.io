Age of Reptiles

noun
Mesozoic era when reptiles dominated the Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒ əv ˈrɛptaɪlz / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒ əv ˈrɛptaɪlz / (BrE)
the geological era from about 230 million to 63 million years ago, dominated by reptiles such as dinosaurs; also known as the Mesozoic Era
