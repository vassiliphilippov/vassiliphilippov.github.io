madeleine moment

noun
nostalgic memory triggered by a sensory experience
Pronunciation: / ˌmæd(ə)ˌleɪn ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˌmadlˈeɪn ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
A situation in which a sensory experience suddenly and vividly brings back a wave of nostalgia or a memory from the past.

Examples

a madeleine moment of nostalgia
Hearing that old song proved to be a madeleine moment for him, instantly recalling high school dances.
Opening the box of crayons created a madeleine moment, flooding him with memories of his first art class.
