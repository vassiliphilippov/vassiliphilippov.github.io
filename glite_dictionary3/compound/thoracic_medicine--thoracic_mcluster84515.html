thoracic medicine

noun
branch of medicine for chest diseases
Pronunciation: / θɔˈræsɪk ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / θəˈræsɪk ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases affecting the chest, including the lungs and respiratory system
