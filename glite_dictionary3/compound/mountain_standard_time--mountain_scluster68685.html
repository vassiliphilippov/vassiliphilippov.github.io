Mountain Standard Time

noun
time zone in US/Canada mountain states
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntn ˈstændɚd taɪm / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈstændəd taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time zone that is 7 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time, used in the mountain states of the US and Canada

Examples

Mountain Standard Time in Colorado
Colorado is in Mountain Standard Time, which is one hour ahead of Pacific Time.
Please set your clocks to Mountain Standard Time for tomorrow's meeting.
The live show will begin at 8 p.m. Mountain Standard Time.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – Central Standard Time: the time zone immediately east of Mountain Standard Time
  • degree lower – Pacific Standard Time: the time zone immediately west of Mountain Standard Time
