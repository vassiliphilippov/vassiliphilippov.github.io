cutaneous senses

noun
sense by which touch is perceived
Pronunciation: / kjuːˈteɪniəs ˈsɛnsɪz / (AmE), / kjuːˈteɪnɪəs ˈsɛnsɪz / (BrE)
the faculty by which outside objects or forces are perceived through contact with the skin, especially the hands; the sense of touch

Examples

cutaneous senses in the skin
