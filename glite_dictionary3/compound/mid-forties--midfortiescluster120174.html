mid-forties

noun
age between 40 and 50
Pronunciation: / mɪd ˈfɔrtiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈfɔːtiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between about 44 and 46 years old, or more generally, the ages between 40 and 50

Examples

in his mid-forties
In his mid-forties, he finally decided to return to school.
Many people consider making career changes in their mid-forties.
She began training for a marathon in her mid-forties to stay active.
