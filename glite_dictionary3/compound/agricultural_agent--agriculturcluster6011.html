agricultural agent

noun
government advisor on rural farming methods
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌaɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
an official employed by the government to advise rural residents on farming practices and home economics
