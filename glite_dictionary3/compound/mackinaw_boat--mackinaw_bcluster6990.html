Mackinaw boat

noun
flat-bottomed boat from Great Lakes
Pronunciation: / ˈmækɪˌnɔ boʊt / (AmE), / ˈmakɪnɔː bəʊt / (BrE)
A flat-bottomed, double-ended boat traditionally used on the upper Great Lakes, especially for fishing and transport.

historic Mackinaw boat
