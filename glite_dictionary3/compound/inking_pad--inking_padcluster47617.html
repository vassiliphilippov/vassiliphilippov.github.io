inking pad

noun
pad soaked with ink for stamping
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkɪŋ pæd / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkɪŋ pad / (BrE)
a small block of absorbent material soaked with ink, used to apply ink evenly to a rubber stamp
