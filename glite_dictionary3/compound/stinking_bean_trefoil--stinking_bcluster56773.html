stinking bean trefoil

noun
shrub with foul-smelling leaves and yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ biːn trɪˈfɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ biːn trɪˈfɔɪl / (BrE)
a shrub with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, producing curved seed pods; its leaves emit a foul smell when crushed
