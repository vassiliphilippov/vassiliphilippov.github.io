mince pie

noun
small pie with mincemeat filling
Pronunciation: / mɪns paɪ / (AmE), / mɪns paɪ / (BrE)
A small round pie filled with mincemeat, traditionally eaten at Christmas in Britain.

Examples

a mince pie at Christmas
Every December, supermarkets stock up on mince pies.
I baked a dozen mince pies for our Christmas party.
Would you like a mince pie with your tea?
