work party

noun
group organized for a specific job
Pronunciation: / wɝk ˈpɑɹti / (AmE), / wɜːk ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
a group of people organized to carry out a particular task or job together, especially manual or physical work

Examples

work party clears debris
A work party was assembled to clear the fallen trees after the storm.
On Saturday, volunteers formed a work party to repair the hiking trails in the park.
The company organized a work party to repaint the community center.
