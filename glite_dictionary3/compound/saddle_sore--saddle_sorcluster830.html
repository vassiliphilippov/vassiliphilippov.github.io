saddle sore

noun
sore on a horse from saddle
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl sɔr / (AmE), / ˈsædəl sɔː / (BrE)
A sore or lesion on a horse's back caused by an ill-fitting or badly adjusted saddle.

Examples

saddle sore on horse
Regular cleaning and proper fitting of equipment can help prevent saddle sores in horses.
The horse developed a saddle sore after a long day of riding with poorly adjusted tack.
The veterinarian treated the saddle sore with a medicated ointment to promote healing.
