quilting bee

noun
social gathering to make quilts
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪltɪŋ bi / (AmE), / ˈkwɪltɪŋ biː / (BrE)
a social gathering at which people, traditionally women, come together to make quilts, often in a communal and festive spirit
