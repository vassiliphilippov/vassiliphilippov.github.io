glow lamp

noun
small electric lamp where gas glows
Pronunciation: / ɡloʊ læmp / (AmE), / ɡləʊ lamp / (BrE)
A small electric light in which a gas, such as neon, glows when electrified, typically used as a signal or indicator.

Examples

glow lamp indicator light
He replaced the faulty glow lamp with a new one to restore the panel's visibility.
Many stroboscopes rely on a glow lamp to produce rapid flashes of light.
The old radio has a small glow lamp to indicate when it is powered on.

  • synonym – neon lamp: a type of glow lamp that uses neon gas
