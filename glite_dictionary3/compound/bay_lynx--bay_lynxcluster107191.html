bay lynx

noun
North American wild cat, bobcat
Pronunciation: / beɪ lɪŋks / (AmE), / beɪ lɪŋks / (BrE)
a small wild cat of North America, also known as the bobcat, distinguished by its short tail and spotted coat

The bay lynx is well adapted to woodland and semi-desert environments across the continent.
Unlike the Canadian lynx, the bay lynx does not depend solely on snowshoe hares for food.
