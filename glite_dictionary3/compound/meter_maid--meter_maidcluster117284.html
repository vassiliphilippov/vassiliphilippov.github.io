meter maid

noun
woman who issues parking tickets
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtər meɪd / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə meɪd / (BrE)
a female official, especially in the US, whose job is to issue parking tickets for illegally parked vehicles

Examples

meter maid gives tickets
As a meter maid, she spent her days walking city streets checking for expired meters.
People often complained when the meter maid appeared on their block.
The meter maid wrote a ticket for every car parked in the no-parking zone.
