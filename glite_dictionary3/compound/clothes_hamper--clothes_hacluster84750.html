clothes hamper

noun
container for holding dirty laundry
Pronunciation: / kloʊðz ˈhæmpər / (AmE), / kləʊðz ˈhampə / (BrE)
a basket or container used for holding dirty or wet clothes before they are washed or dried

Examples

clothes hamper full of laundry
He bought a new clothes hamper to help keep his bedroom tidy.
She tossed her dirty socks into the clothes hamper before taking a shower.
The clothes hamper in the bathroom was overflowing with laundry.
