visual image

noun
image formed by visual perception
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈɪmɪd͡ʒ /; / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuːəl ˈɪmɪdʒ /; / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
