pubococcygeus exercises

noun
pelvic muscle strengthening exercises
Pronunciation: / ˌpjuːboʊˌkɑksɪˈdʒiːəs ˈɛksɚˌsaɪzɪz / (AmE), / ˌpjʊbəʊˌkɒksɪˈdʒiːəs ˈɛksəsaɪzɪz / (BrE)
