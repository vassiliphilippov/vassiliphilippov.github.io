second moment

noun
expected value of the squared deviation from the origin (statistics)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
In statistics, the expected value of the square of the deviations of a random variable from the origin, used to measure spread or variation.
