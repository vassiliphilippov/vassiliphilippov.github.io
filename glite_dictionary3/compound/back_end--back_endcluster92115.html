back end

noun
rear side or end of something
Pronunciation: / bæk ˈɛnd / (AmE), / bæk ˈɛnd / (BrE)
the side or part of an object, space, or structure that is opposite to the front; the rear portion

Examples

the back end of a truck
Please place the heavy boxes at the back end of the truck.
The back end of the garden gets the least sunlight during winter.
They entered through the back end of the building to avoid the crowd.

Related Terms

  • antonym – front end: the side or end of something that faces forward or is most often seen first
