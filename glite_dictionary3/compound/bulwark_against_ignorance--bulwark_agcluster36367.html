bulwark against ignorance

noun
strong defense against ignorance
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊl.wɚk əˈɡɛnst ˈɪɡ.nɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.wək əˈɡɛnst ˈɪɡ.nə.rəns / (BrE)
something that strongly protects or prevents people from being uneducated or uninformed.

Examples

bulwark against ignorance in education
Education is widely regarded as a bulwark against ignorance in modern society.
Libraries have long served as a bulwark against ignorance by providing free access to information.
Public access to reliable news sources is a critical bulwark against ignorance in a democracy.
