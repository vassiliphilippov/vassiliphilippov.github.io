ground-floor

adjective
on the floor at ground level
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊnd flɔr / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊnd flɔː / (BrE)
Situated on or relating to the floor of a building that is at the same level as the ground outside.

Examples

ground-floor apartment
Our apartment has a ground-floor balcony that opens onto the garden.
