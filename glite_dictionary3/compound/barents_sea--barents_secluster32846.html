Barents Sea

proper noun
sea north of Norway and Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrənts si / (AmE), / ˈbærənts siː / (BrE)
the part of the Arctic Ocean located north of Norway and Russia, known for its cold climate and rich marine life

Examples

the Barents Sea region
