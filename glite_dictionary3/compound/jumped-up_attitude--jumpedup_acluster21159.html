jumped-up attitude

noun
arrogant or presumptuous attitude
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpt ʌp ˈæt̬.ɪ.tuːd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpt ʌp ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
An inflated or arrogant attitude or manner, often considered presumptuous or self-important.

Examples

jumped-up attitude at work
Don't let his jumped-up attitude fool you; he started here just last year.
Her jumped-up attitude made it hard for her coworkers to relate to her.
People grew tired of his jumped-up attitude after only a few meetings.
