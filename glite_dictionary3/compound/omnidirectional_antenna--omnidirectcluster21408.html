omnidirectional antenna

noun
antenna transmitting/receiving in all directions
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːmnɪdəˈrɛkʃənəl ænˈtɛnə / (AmE), / ˌɒmnɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəl ænˈtɛnə / (BrE)
an antenna designed to transmit or receive signals equally well in all directions

Examples

omnidirectional antenna for Wi-Fi
For Wi-Fi routers, an omnidirectional antenna helps distribute the signal evenly throughout a home.
The radio station upgraded to an omnidirectional antenna to reach listeners across the entire city.
Unlike a directional antenna, an omnidirectional antenna does not need to be aimed towards the source.

Related Terms

  • antonym – directional antenna: an antenna designed to focus signals in a particular direction
