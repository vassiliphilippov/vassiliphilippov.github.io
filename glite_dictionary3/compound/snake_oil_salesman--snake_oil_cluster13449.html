snake oil salesman

noun
fraudulent seller of ineffective products
Pronunciation: / sneɪk ɔɪl ˈseɪlzˌmæn / (AmE), / sneɪk ɔɪl ˈseɪlzmən / (BrE)
a person who fraudulently sells ineffective or bogus products, especially medical remedies

Examples

dishonest snake oil salesman
Many called the dietary supplement company CEO a snake oil salesman after his exaggerated claims were debunked.
Politicians accused their opponent of being a snake oil salesman, selling empty promises to voters.
The television ad promised miracle results, but it felt like the tactics of a snake oil salesman.

Related Terms

  • synonym – charlatan: a person falsely claiming to have special knowledge or skills
  • synonym – quack: an unqualified or fraudulent medical practitioner
