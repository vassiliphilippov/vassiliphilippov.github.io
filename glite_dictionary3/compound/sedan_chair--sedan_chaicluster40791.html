sedan chair

noun
enclosed chair carried by bearers
Pronunciation: / sɪˈdæn tʃɛr / (AmE), / sɪˈdæn tʃɛə / (BrE)
a type of enclosed chair for one person, carried on poles by bearers, used especially in the past for transport
