first-class mail

noun
priority class of mail for letters and sealed packages
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrst klæs meɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst klɑːs meɪl / (BrE)
a class of mail service for letters, postcards, and sealed packages given priority in handling and delivery

Examples

send first-class mail quickly
I sent the invitation by first-class mail to ensure it arrived quickly.
It's more expensive to use first-class mail, but it's faster and more secure.
Packages sent via first-class mail are usually delivered within a few days.
