second wind

noun
renewed energy after fatigue
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd wɪnd /; / ˈsɛk.ənd wɪnd / (AmE), / ˈsekənd wɪnd /; / ˈsek.ənd wɪnd / (BrE)
a feeling of renewed energy or strength that allows someone to continue an activity after feeling tired
