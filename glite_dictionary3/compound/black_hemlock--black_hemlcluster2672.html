black hemlock

noun
western US evergreen tree with hard wood
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈhɛmlɑk / (AmE), / blæk ˈhɛmlɒk / (BrE)
A large evergreen tree native to the western United States, known for its much harder wood than the Canadian hemlock.
