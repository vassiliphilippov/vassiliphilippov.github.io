full faith and credit

noun
government's unconditional guarantee to repay debt
Pronunciation: / fʊl feɪθ ənd ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / fʊl feɪθ ənd ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
the unconditional guarantee by a government, especially the United States, to repay its debt obligations, including interest and principal
