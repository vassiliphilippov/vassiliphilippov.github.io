calcium light

noun
historical intense stage light using lime
Pronunciation: / ˈkælsiəm laɪt / (AmE), / ˈkalsɪəm laɪt / (BrE)
a powerful light produced by directing a flame at a cylinder of lime, used especially in theaters before the introduction of electric lighting
