Indian reservation

noun
land reserved for Native American tribe
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˌrɛzərˈveɪʃn / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪən ˌrɛzəˈveɪʃn / (BrE)
An area of land in the United States reserved for use and occupation by a Native American tribe, under the supervision of the federal government.

Examples

visit an Indian reservation museum
Life on an Indian reservation can be very different from life in nearby cities.
Many tribes still live on Indian reservations across the United States today.
The government provided health services to those living on the Indian reservation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Native American: a member of the indigenous peoples of the United States
  • synonym – reservation: an area of land set aside for a particular purpose
