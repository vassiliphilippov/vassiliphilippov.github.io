sprinkler system

noun
automatic fire-extinguishing system
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋklɚ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋklə ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A network of pipes fitted with sprinklers that automatically discharge water to extinguish fires when a certain temperature is reached.

Examples

sprinkler system in buildings
All modern office buildings are required to have a sprinkler system installed for safety.
The faulty sprinkler system flooded the entire warehouse during the night.
When the kitchen fire started, the sprinkler system activated and quickly doused the flames.
