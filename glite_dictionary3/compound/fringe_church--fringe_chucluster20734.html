fringe church

noun
non-mainstream religious group
Pronunciation: / frɪndʒ tʃɝtʃ / (AmE), / frɪndʒ tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a religious group or organization that operates outside mainstream denominations, often characterized by unconventional practices and beliefs

Examples

join a fringe church
After years of dissatisfaction, he decided to join a fringe church with a small but dedicated following.
Local authorities monitored the fringe church due to concerns about its secretive ceremonies.
The documentary explored how a fringe church challenged traditional religious doctrines in the community.
