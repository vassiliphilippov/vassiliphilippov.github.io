split-brain technique

noun
surgical procedure severing the corpus callosum
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɪt ˌbreɪn tɛkˈniːk / (AmE), / ˈsplɪt ˌbreɪn tɛkˈniːk / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which the corpus callosum (and sometimes the optic chiasm) is severed to interrupt communication between the cerebral hemispheres
