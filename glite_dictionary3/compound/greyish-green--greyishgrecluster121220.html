greyish-green

adjective
green tinged with grey
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a green color tinged with grey; having both green and grey qualities.

Examples

greyish-green leaves
She painted her bedroom walls a calming greyish-green to create a relaxing atmosphere.
The leaves had a distinct greyish-green hue that blended with the overcast sky.
Their uniforms were a dull greyish-green, intended to provide camouflage in the forest.
