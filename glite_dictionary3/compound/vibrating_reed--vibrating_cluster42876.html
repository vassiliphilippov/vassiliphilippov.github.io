vibrating reed

noun
strip that vibrates to produce sound
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.breɪ.tɪŋ riːd / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.brə.tɪŋ riːd / (BrE)
a thin, stiff strip of material that vibrates to produce a sound or tone when air passes over it, commonly used in musical instruments such as clarinets or harmonicas.
