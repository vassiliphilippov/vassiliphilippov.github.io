Bastille Day

noun
French national holiday, July 14th
Pronunciation: / bæˈstil deɪ / (AmE), / bæˈstiːl deɪ / (BrE)
The national holiday in France celebrated on July 14th, marking the anniversary of the storming of the Bastille prison in 1789 and the beginning of the French Revolution.

Examples

Bastille Day celebrations
French communities around the world hold special events on Bastille Day.
Many tourists visit Paris to celebrate Bastille Day with fireworks and parades.
Schools in France are closed on Bastille Day, the country's most important national holiday.
