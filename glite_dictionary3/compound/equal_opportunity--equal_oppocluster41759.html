equal opportunity

noun
fairness in access to opportunities
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkwəl ɑːpərˈtunəti / (AmE), / ˈiːkwəl ɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
the principle that all people should have the same chances for employment, education, or advancement regardless of their race, gender, or background

Examples

promote equal opportunity for all
A truly fair society promotes equal opportunity in education and employment.
Many laws were passed to ensure equal opportunity regardless of race or gender.
The company is committed to equal opportunity for all its employees.
