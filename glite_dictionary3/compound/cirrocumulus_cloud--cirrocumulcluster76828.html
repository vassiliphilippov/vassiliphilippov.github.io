cirrocumulus cloud

noun
high-altitude cloud with rippled appearance
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪroʊˈkjuːmjələs klaʊd / (AmE), / ˌsɪrəʊˈkjuːmjʊləs klaʊd / (BrE)
a high-altitude cloud consisting of small, regularly arranged cloudlets that often appear as ripples or patches in the sky
