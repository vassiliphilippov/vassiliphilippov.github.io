ground pine

noun
type of club moss resembling a pine
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd paɪn / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd paɪn / (BrE)
Any of several club mosses with long creeping stems and upright branches, especially those resembling small pine trees.

Examples

ground pine club moss
Botanists collected samples of ground pine for their study of native club mosses.
Children sometimes mistake ground pine for baby Christmas trees due to its pine-like appearance.
