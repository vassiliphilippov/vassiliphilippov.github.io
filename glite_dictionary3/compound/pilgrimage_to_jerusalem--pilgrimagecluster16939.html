pilgrimage to Jerusalem

noun
religious journey to Jerusalem
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlɡrəmɪdʒ tə dʒəˈruːsələm / (AmE), / ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒ tə dʒəˈruːsələm / (BrE)
a journey to Jerusalem made for religious or spiritual reasons, often as an act of devotion by members of the Christian, Jewish, or Muslim faiths

Examples

make a pilgrimage to Jerusalem
Each year, thousands of Christians make a pilgrimage to Jerusalem during Easter Week.
Her lifelong dream was to undertake a pilgrimage to Jerusalem to visit the holy sites.
The documentary followed the experiences of a group on their pilgrimage to Jerusalem.
