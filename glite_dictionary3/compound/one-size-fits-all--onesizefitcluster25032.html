one-size-fits-all

adjective
suitable for all situations or people
Pronunciation: / ˌwʌn saɪz ˌfɪts ˈɔl / (AmE), / ˌwʌn saɪz ˌfɪts ˈɔːl / (BrE)
(of a solution, product, or policy) suitable for or intended to apply in every instance, circumstance, or for every person, without individual tailoring.

Examples

one-size-fits-all solution
It's important to recognize that mental health care cannot take a one-size-fits-all approach.
Many students felt that the one-size-fits-all curriculum did not address their individual learning needs.
The company rejected a one-size-fits-all marketing strategy in favor of targeted campaigns.

Related Terms

  • antonym – customized: made or modified to suit specific requirements or individuals
  • synonym – universal: existing or having effect everywhere, applicable in all cases
