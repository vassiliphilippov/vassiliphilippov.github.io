genus Mycoplasma

noun
bacterial genus in Mycoplasmataceae family
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmaɪkoʊˈplæzmə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmaɪkəʊˈplazmə / (BrE)
the only genus of the family Mycoplasmataceae, comprising bacteria lacking cell walls, some of which are pathogenic in humans and animals
