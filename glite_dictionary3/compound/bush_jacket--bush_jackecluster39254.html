bush jacket

noun
loose jacket with pockets and belt
Pronunciation: / bʊʃ ˈdʒækət / (AmE), / bʊʃ ˈdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A loose-fitting jacket resembling a shirt, typically with four patch pockets and a belt, originally designed for use in hot climates.
