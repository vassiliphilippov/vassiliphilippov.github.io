Ibizan hound

noun
breed of slender, agile hound from the Balearic Islands
Pronunciation: / ɪˈbizən haʊnd / (AmE), / ɪˈbiːzən haʊnd / (BrE)
a breed of slender, agile, medium-sized hound, originally from the Balearic Islands, known for its graceful build and keen hunting abilities
