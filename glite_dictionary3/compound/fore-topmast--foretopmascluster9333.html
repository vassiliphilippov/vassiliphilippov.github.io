fore-topmast

noun
mast above the foremast on a ship
Pronunciation: / fɔr ˈtɑpmæst / (AmE), / fɔː ˈtɒpmɑːst / (BrE)
the section of a ship's mast located immediately above the foremast, supporting the upper fore sails

Examples

The crew climbed the fore-topmast to rig new sails before the storm hit.
