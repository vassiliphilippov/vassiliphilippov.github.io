Blueshirt movement

noun
right-wing Irish political movement (early 20th cent.)
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌʃɜrt ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈbluːʃɜːt ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
