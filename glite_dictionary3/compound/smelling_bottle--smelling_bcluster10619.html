smelling bottle

noun
bottle for smelling salts
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɛlɪŋ ˌbɑːtəl / (AmE), / ˈsmelɪŋ ˌbɒtəl / (BrE)
a small bottle containing smelling salts, used especially to revive someone who has fainted or feels faint
