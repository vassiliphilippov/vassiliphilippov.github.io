scenic route

noun
a road chosen for its beautiful views
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.nɪk ruːt / (AmE), / ˈsiː.nɪk ruːt / (BrE)
a road or path chosen for its beautiful and picturesque views, typically longer than the most direct way

Examples

take the scenic route
She prefers the scenic route whenever she travels to enjoy the countryside.
The scenic route offered breathtaking views of the lake along the way.
We decided to take the scenic route through the mountains, even though it took longer.

Related Terms

  • antonym – shortcut: a quicker, more direct route between two places
