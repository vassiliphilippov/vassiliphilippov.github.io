zoological garden

noun
place where wild animals are exhibited
Pronunciation: / zuːəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl ˈɡɑːrdn̩ / (AmE), / zuːəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈɡɑːdn̩ / (BrE)
A place where a collection of wild animals is kept for public exhibition and often for conservation and breeding.

Examples

visit a zoological garden
Children enjoyed watching the lions and elephants at the zoological garden during their field trip.
Many conservation programs operate out of the zoological garden to help preserve threatened animals.
The city is planning to expand its zoological garden to provide better habitats for endangered species.
