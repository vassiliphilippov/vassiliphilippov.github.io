shepherd's purse

noun
a small white-flowered weed with heart-shaped seed pods
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛpərdz ˌpɜrs / (AmE), / ˈʃɛpədz ˌpɜːs / (BrE)
a small annual plant (Capsella bursa-pastoris) with white flowers and heart-shaped seed pods, native to Europe and widely naturalized as a weed
