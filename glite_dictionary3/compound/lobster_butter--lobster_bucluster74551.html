lobster butter

noun
butter flavored with lobster or its essence
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑb.stɚ ˈbʌt̬.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɒb.stə ˈbʌt.ə / (BrE)
Butter that is blended with chopped lobster meat or flavored with essence extracted from lobster shells, used as a condiment or in cooking.

Examples

lobster butter on toast
He spread some lobster butter on warm toast as a decadent snack.
The chef finished the dish with a generous spoonful of lobster butter.
You can make lobster butter by simmering lobster shells in melted butter.
