specialty store

noun
shop selling one type of product
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃəlti stɔr / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəlti stɔː / (BrE)
A retail shop that sells only one particular type or category of merchandise.

Examples

specialty store for shoes
Many specialty stores offer unique products that are hard to find elsewhere.
She prefers to shop at a specialty store for her hiking gear because of the expert advice.
The new specialty store downtown sells only imported teas and accessories.
