inferior pulmonary vein

noun
vein returning blood from lower lung lobes
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriɚ ˈpʌlməˌnɛri veɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪəriə ˈpʌlmənəri veɪn / (BrE)
either of the two pulmonary veins (left and right) that return blood from the lower lobes of the lungs to the left atrium of the heart

Examples

left inferior pulmonary vein
A blood clot in the inferior pulmonary vein can cause complications after lung surgery.
CT imaging revealed an obstruction in the right inferior pulmonary vein.
The surgeon carefully preserved both the inferior pulmonary vein and the bronchus during the procedure.
