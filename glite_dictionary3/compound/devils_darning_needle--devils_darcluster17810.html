devil's darning needle

noun
climbing plant with creamy white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəlz ˈdɑrnɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz ˈdɑːnɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (BrE)
