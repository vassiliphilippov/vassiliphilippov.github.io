off-speed pitch

noun
a slower baseball pitch to deceive the batter
Pronunciation: / ˈɔf spiːd pɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɒf spiːd pɪtʃ / (BrE)
a baseball pitch thrown at a slower speed than usual to deceive the batter, particularly when the batter is expecting a fastball

Examples

off-speed pitch fools batter
Batters sometimes struggle when they can't anticipate an off-speed pitch.
He mixed in an off-speed pitch after two consecutive fastballs.
The pitcher threw an off-speed pitch to catch the batter off guard.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fastball: a baseball pitch thrown at high speed
