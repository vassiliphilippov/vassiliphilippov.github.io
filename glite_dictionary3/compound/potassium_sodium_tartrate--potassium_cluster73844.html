potassium sodium tartrate

noun
a double salt used as a mild laxative
Pronunciation: / pəˈtæsiəm ˈsoʊdiəm ˈtɑːrˌtreɪt / (AmE), / pəˈtasiəm ˈsəʊdiəm ˈtɑːtreɪt / (BrE)
A double salt of potassium and sodium with tartaric acid, used especially as a mild laxative and in certain medical powders.
