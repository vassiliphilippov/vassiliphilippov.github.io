middle temporal vein

noun
vein near the eye joining the superficial temporal veins
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪd.əl ˈtɛmp(ə)r.əl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.əl ˈtɛmp(ə)r.əl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that arises near the eye and joins the superficial temporal veins to form the retromandibular vein

Examples

the middle temporal vein drains blood
Damage to the middle temporal vein can lead to excessive bleeding in the temporal area.
