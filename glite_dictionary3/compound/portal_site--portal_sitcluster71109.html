portal site

noun
major entry-point website on the internet
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔr.t̬l̩ saɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.tl̩ saɪt / (BrE)
a website designed to serve as a main entry point to a range of resources or services on the internet, often providing search, email, news, and links to other sites

Examples

portal site homepage
Many people use a portal site as their browser's start page for convenience.
The portal site offered news, shopping, and online community features.
Yahoo was one of the most popular portal sites in the early days of the web.
