jumping orchid

noun
an orchid species that ejects pollen on touch
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌɔrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ ˌɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A species of orchid with both male and female flowers on the same raceme, notable for ejecting pollen when a sensitive part is touched.
