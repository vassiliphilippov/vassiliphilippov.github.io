subconscious mind

noun
part of mind below conscious awareness
Pronunciation: / səbˈkɑn.ʃəs maɪnd / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒn.ʃəs maɪnd / (BrE)
the part of the mind that operates below the level of conscious awareness but influences thoughts, feelings, and behavior

Examples

the subconscious mind controls actions
Dreams often reveal concerns that lie in the subconscious mind.
Many of our habits are shaped by the subconscious mind without us realizing it.
Therapists sometimes help patients explore their subconscious mind to better understand their behaviors.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conscious mind: the part of the mind that is aware of thoughts and environment
  • synonym – unconscious mind: the part of the mind inaccessible to conscious thought, often broader than subconscious
