appeal for donations

noun
request for financial contributions
Pronunciation: / əˈpil fər doʊˈneɪʃənz / (AmE), / əˈpiːl fə dəʊˈneɪʃənz / (BrE)
an organized request made by a person or organization, usually to the public, asking for financial contributions

Examples

appeal for donations after disaster
After the disaster, the government issued an appeal for donations to help rebuild the community.
Every year, the hospital sends out an appeal for donations to purchase new medical equipment.
The charity made an appeal for donations to support families affected by the floods.
