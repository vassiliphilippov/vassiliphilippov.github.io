hamamelid dicot family

noun
family of woody dicotyledonous plants
Pronunciation: / ˌhæməˈmiːlɪd ˈdaɪˌkɑt ˈfæmli / (AmE), / ˌhæməˈmiːlɪd ˈdaɪkɒt ˈfæmli / (BrE)
