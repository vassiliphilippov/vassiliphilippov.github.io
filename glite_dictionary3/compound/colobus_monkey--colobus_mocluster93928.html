colobus monkey

noun
African tree-dwelling monkey with silky fur
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑləbəs ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈkɒləbəs ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
A type of arboreal monkey native to western and central Africa, known for its long silky fur and reduced or absent thumbs.

Examples

colobus monkey in trees
The distinctive black and white fur of the colobus monkey makes it easy to identify.
Unlike other monkeys, the colobus monkey has a very small or absent thumb.
