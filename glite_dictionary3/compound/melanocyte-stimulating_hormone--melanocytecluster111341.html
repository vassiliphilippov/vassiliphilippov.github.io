melanocyte-stimulating hormone

noun
hormone regulating skin pigmentation
Pronunciation: / məˌlænoʊˌsaɪt ˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɪŋ ˌhɔrmoʊn / (AmE), / mɪˌlanəˌsaɪt ˈstɪmjʊˌleɪtɪŋ ˌhɔːməʊn / (BrE)
