River Styx

proper noun
mythical river of the underworld in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvər ˈstɪks / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˈstɪks / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a river in the underworld, Hades, across which the ferryman Charon carried the souls of the dead.
