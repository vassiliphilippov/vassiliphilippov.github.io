medical diagnosis

noun
identification of a disease from symptoms
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl daɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl daɪəɡˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the identification of a disease or condition by examining a patient's symptoms and signs, often with the help of medical tests
