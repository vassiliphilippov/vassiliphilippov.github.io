four-card monte

noun
a gambling game with four cards
Pronunciation: / fɔr kɑrd ˈmɑnti / (AmE), / fɔː kɑːd ˈmɒnti / (BrE)
A gambling game similar to three-card monte, but played with four cards, typically used by swindlers to trick players.

Examples

four-card monte street game
Police arrested the scam artists for running a four-card monte game downtown.
The street hustler convinced several bystanders to try their luck at four-card monte.
Unlike the traditional version, four-card monte adds an extra card to make it more confusing.
