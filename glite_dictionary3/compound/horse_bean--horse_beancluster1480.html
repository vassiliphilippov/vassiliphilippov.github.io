horse bean

noun
a variety of bean used as animal fodder
Pronunciation: / hɔrs bin / (AmE), / hɔːs biːn / (BrE)
a type of broad bean (Vicia faba), often grown to be used as animal feed rather than for human consumption

Examples

horse bean animal feed
Horse bean is well-suited to poor soils and provides valuable nutrition for animals.
After harvesting the wheat, they alternated the field with a crop of horse bean.
The farmer planted horse bean to feed his livestock through the winter.
