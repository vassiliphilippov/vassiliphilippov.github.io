brass instrument

noun
musical wind instrument of brass
Pronunciation: / bræs ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / brɑːs ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
a musical wind instrument made of brass or other metal, played by buzzing the lips into a cup- or funnel-shaped mouthpiece

Examples

brass instrument in orchestra
Many brass instruments have a rich, resonant sound that fills the concert hall.
She learned to play the brass instrument in her school orchestra.
The trumpet is a popular type of brass instrument used in jazz music.
