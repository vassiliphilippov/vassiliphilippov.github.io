winning streak

noun
series of consecutive wins
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnɪŋ strik / (AmE), / ˈwɪnɪŋ striːk / (BrE)
A period during which someone or a team achieves several wins in succession.

Examples

on a winning streak run
After their third victory in a row, the team was on a winning streak.
Her winning streak ended abruptly when she lost in the finals.
The club is hoping to continue its winning streak through the next round.
