con artist

noun
person who cheats by deception
Pronunciation: / kɑn ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / kɒn ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who deceives others by gaining their trust in order to cheat them out of money or valuables

Examples

Movies often depict a con artist developing complex schemes to cheat their targets.
She realized too late that the charming stranger was actually a skilled con artist.
The police arrested the notorious con artist who had been tricking people for years.
