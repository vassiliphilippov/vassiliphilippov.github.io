shipping pneumonia

noun
deadly infection in cattle and sheep from shipping stress
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpɪŋ nəˈmoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpɪŋ njʊˈməʊniə / (BrE)
a fatal bacterial infection in cattle and sheep, marked by high fever and pneumonia, often contracted when animals are stressed or transported
