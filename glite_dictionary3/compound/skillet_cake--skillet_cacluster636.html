skillet cake

noun
cake baked over fruit in a skillet, then inverted
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪlət keɪk / (AmE), / ˈskɪlɪt keɪk / (BrE)
A cake made by baking batter over a layer of sweetened fruit in a skillet, then inverting it so the fruit is on top.

Examples

fruit skillet cake
After baking, the skillet cake is flipped so the fruit becomes the topping.
She served a warm skillet cake with caramelized peaches for dessert.
The recipe calls for making the skillet cake in a cast iron pan.
