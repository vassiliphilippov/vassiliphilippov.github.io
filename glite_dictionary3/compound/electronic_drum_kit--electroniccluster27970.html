electronic drum kit

noun
set of electronic drums and controls
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk drʌm kɪt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk drʌm kɪt / (BrE)
A set of electronic drums and related equipment, played by striking pads or sensors to produce a variety of drum and percussion sounds electronically.

Examples

electronic drum kit practice
After switching to an electronic drum kit, she was able to practice quietly at night.
He programmed custom beats on his electronic drum kit for the recording session.
Many bands use an electronic drum kit on stage for its versatile sound options.
