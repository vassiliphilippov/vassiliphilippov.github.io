barbituric acid

noun
organic acid used to make barbiturates
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːrbɪˈtjʊrɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˌbɑːbɪˈtjʊərɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a white crystalline organic acid, derived from pyrimidine, used in the manufacture of barbiturate drugs

Examples
