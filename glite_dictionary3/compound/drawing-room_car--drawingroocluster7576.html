drawing-room car

noun
luxury passenger rail car with individual chairs
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪŋ ruːm kɑr / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ruːm kɑː / (BrE)
A railroad passenger car equipped with individual chairs, for which an extra fare is charged, designed for comfortable day travel.

Examples

luxury drawing-room car
She always reserved a seat in the drawing-room car for her business trips.
The train featured a luxurious drawing-room car with plush seats and extra amenities.
Travelers paid a higher fare to enjoy the comfort of the drawing-room car.
