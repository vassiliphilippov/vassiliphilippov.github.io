figured bass

noun
musical notation indicating chords above bass
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚd ˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡjəd ˌbeɪs / (BrE)
A bass part in music that is written out in full and accompanied by numbers indicating the chords to be played above it.
