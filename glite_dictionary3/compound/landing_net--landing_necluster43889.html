landing net

noun
fishing net for lifting caught fish
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪŋ nɛt / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ nɛt / (BrE)
a bag-shaped net on a long handle used by anglers to lift a caught fish from the water

Examples

use a landing net for fish
Always use a landing net to safely catch and release fish without harming them.

The angler reached for his landing net as the trout neared the surface.
