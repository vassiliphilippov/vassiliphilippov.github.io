perceptual experience

noun
an experience based on sensory perception
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɛptʃuəl ɪkˈspɪriəns / (AmE), / pəˈsɛptjʊəl ɪkˈspɪəriəns / (BrE)
an experience resulting from the process of perceiving through the senses; a basic element in the formation of concepts and knowledge

Examples

direct perceptual experience
Children learn about their environment through direct perceptual experience.
Every perceptual experience we have contributes to the way we interpret the world.
Pain is a type of perceptual experience that alerts us to potential harm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – perception: the ability to see, hear, or become aware of something through the senses
