cola nut

noun
seed used in cola flavoring
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə nʌt / (BrE)
the bitter brown seed of the kola tree, containing caffeine and used as a source of cola extract for flavoring drinks

Examples

Many soft drinks originally contained extracts from the cola nut.
