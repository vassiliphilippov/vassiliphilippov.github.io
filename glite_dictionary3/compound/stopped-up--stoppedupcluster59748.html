stopped-up

adjective
blocked, congested (of a nose)
Pronunciation: / stɑpt ʌp / (AmE), / stɒpt ʌp / (BrE)
(of a nose) blocked and unable to allow air to pass through; congested.

Examples

stopped-up nose
Allergy season left him with a constantly stopped-up nose.
She was frustrated because her stopped-up sinuses just wouldn’t clear.
Whenever I have a cold, my stopped-up nose makes it hard to breathe at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – congested: (of a part of the body) blocked with fluid; especially referring to a nose
