asynchronous nasalization

noun
nasalization where oral vowel precedes nasal semivowel
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs ˌneɪ.zəl.ɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / əˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs ˌneɪ.zə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A type of nasalization in which an oral vowel is followed by a nasal semivowel, rather than both features occurring simultaneously.
