Ember Day

noun
Christian day for fasting and prayer
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmbər deɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛmbə deɪ / (BrE)
In the Christian church, one of a series of days set aside for fasting and prayer at the beginning of the seasons of the year.

Examples

Ember Day fasting season
He explained the significance of Ember Days to his children during Lent.
Many Christians observe Ember Days with fasting and extra prayers.
The church held a special service on the first Ember Day of spring.
