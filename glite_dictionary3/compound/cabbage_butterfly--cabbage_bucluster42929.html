cabbage butterfly

noun
white butterfly, larvae feed on cabbage
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbɪdʒ ˌbʌtərˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkabɪdʒ ˌbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
