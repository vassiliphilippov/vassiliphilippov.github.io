pop-up book

noun
children's book with 3D paper illustrations
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑp ˌʌp bʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɒp ˌʌp bʊk / (BrE)
a book, usually for children, whose pages contain folded paper illustrations that rise into three dimensions when the page is opened

Examples

colorful pop-up book
Many collectors seek out rare pop-up books from the early twentieth century.
She gave her nephew a pop-up book for his birthday, and he was fascinated with the moving dinosaurs.
The children were delighted when the pop-up book revealed a castle that sprang up from the page.
