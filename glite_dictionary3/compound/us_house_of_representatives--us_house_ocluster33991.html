U.S. House of Representatives

proper noun
lower chamber of the U.S. Congress
Pronunciation: / ju ɛs ˈhaʊs əv ˌrɛprɪˌzɛntəˈtɪvz / (AmE), / juː ɛs ˈhaʊs əv ˌrɛprɪˌzɛntətɪvz / (BrE)
the lower legislative chamber of the United States Congress, consisting of elected representatives from each state, responsible for making and passing federal laws
