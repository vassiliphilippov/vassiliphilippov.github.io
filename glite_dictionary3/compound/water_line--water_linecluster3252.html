water line

noun
mark showing ship's water level
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌlaɪn /; / ˈwɔt̬ɚ laɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌlaɪn /; / ˈwɔːtə laɪn / (BrE)
a line that shows the level reached by water on a ship, wall, or other object
