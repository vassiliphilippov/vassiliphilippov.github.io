electrostatic machine

noun
device generating high-voltage static electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk məˈʃin / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
an electrical device that generates a high voltage by accumulating a charge of static electricity
