swimming costume

noun
garment worn for swimming
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪmɪŋ ˌkɑstum / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋ ˌkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
A garment designed to be worn while swimming, usually fitting closely to the body.

Examples

swimming costume for the pool
His swimming costume was decorated with bright tropical patterns.
Remember to bring your swimming costume for the trip to the beach.
She put on her swimming costume before heading to the pool.
