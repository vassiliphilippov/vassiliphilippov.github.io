paramilitary organisation

noun
civilian group organized like military
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈmɪlɪtəri ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a group of civilians organized in military style, often operating in place of or in support of a regular army
