darning needle

noun
a dragonfly; slender-winged insect
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrnɪŋ ˌnidəl / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnɪŋ ˌniːdl̩ / (BrE)
A slender-bodied insect with iridescent wings, commonly known as a dragonfly, whose adults and nymphs feed on mosquitoes and other small insects.

Examples

a darning needle flying over water
A darning needle darted swiftly through the air, hunting for mosquitoes near the water's edge.
Children used to believe that a darning needle could sew their lips shut if they told lies.
We watched a darning needle hover over the pond, its wings catching the sunlight.
