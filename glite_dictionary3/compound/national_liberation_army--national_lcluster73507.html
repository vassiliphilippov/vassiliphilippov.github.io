National Liberation Army

proper noun
Colombian Marxist insurgent group
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəl lɪbəˈreɪʃən ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl lɪbəˈreɪʃən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
