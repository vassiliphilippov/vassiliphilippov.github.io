mosquito bite

noun
itchy swelling from a mosquito's sting
Pronunciation: / məˈskitoʊ baɪt / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ baɪt / (BrE)
A small, itchy swelling on the skin caused by a mosquito's sting when it feeds on blood.

Examples

itchy mosquito bite
I always seem to get at least one mosquito bite whenever I go camping.
She scratched the mosquito bite on her arm until it turned red.
The mosquito bite became swollen after she spent time near the lake.
