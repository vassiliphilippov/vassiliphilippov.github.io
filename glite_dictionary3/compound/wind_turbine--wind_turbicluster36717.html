wind turbine

noun
device generating electricity from wind
Pronunciation: / wɪnd ˈtɝː.baɪn / (AmE), / wɪnd ˈtɜː.baɪn / (BrE)
A device with blades that is turned by the wind to generate electricity.

Examples

wind turbine generates electricity
Many farms are now powered by electricity generated from a wind turbine.
Standing on the hill, the massive wind turbine spun quickly in the strong breeze.
The city installed a wind turbine to produce clean energy for the local school.

Related Terms

  • synonym – windmill: structure that uses wind to turn machinery, often for grinding grain or pumping water
