paper jogger

noun
machine for aligning stacks of paper
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpər ˈdʒɑɡər / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə ˈdʒɒɡə / (BrE)
A machine used to align and straighten stacks of paper by shaking or vibrating them.

Examples

paper jogger aligns sheets
A paper jogger can save time and reduce jams by properly aligning large paper stacks.
Before binding the booklets, she used a paper jogger to organize the pages neatly.
The printer operator placed the documents in the paper jogger to straighten the edges.
