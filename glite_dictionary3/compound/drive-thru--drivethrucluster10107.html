drive-thru

noun
restaurant where food is served to customers in their cars
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪv θru / (AmE), / ˈdraɪv θruː / (BrE)
a place or service (such as a restaurant, bank, or pharmacy) where you can get goods or be served without leaving your car
