sweet grass

noun
a fragrant, moisture-loving grass
Pronunciation: / swit ɡræs / (AmE), / swiːt ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any of several species of grass, especially of the genus Glyceria, that grow in moist areas and are noted for their sweet flavor or fragrance.

Examples

sweet grass for ceremonies
Native Americans traditionally use sweet grass for ceremonial purposes.
The air was filled with the scent of sweet grass after the rain.
The basket was woven from sweet grass found near the riverbank.
