lappet moth

noun
a type of hairy moth
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpɪt mɔθ / (AmE), / ˈlæpɪt mɒθ / (BrE)
a medium-sized hairy moth of the family Lasiocampidae, whose larvae are known as lappet caterpillars

Examples

lappet moth caterpillar
A lappet moth rested on the tree trunk, blending perfectly with the bark.
Biologists studied the silk produced by the lappet moth to understand its structure.
Gardeners sometimes find lappet moth caterpillars feeding on the leaves of local shrubs.
