the more ... the easier

x
comparative structure showing increased ease
Pronunciation: / ðə mɔr ðə ˈiziər / (AmE), / ðə mɔː ðə ˈiːziə / (BrE)
Used to indicate that as the amount or degree of one thing increases, the action or state described becomes less difficult.

Examples

the more ... the easier it gets
The more organized you are, the easier it is to meet deadlines.
The more practice you get, the easier it becomes to play this song.
The more you explain the topic, the easier it is for everyone to understand.
