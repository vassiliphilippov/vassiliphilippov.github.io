paranasal sinus

noun
air-filled cavity near the nose
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.əˈneɪ.zəl ˈsaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˌpær.əˈneɪ.zəl ˈsaɪ.nəs / (BrE)
any of several air-filled cavities in the bones around the nose, lined with mucous membrane and connected to the nasal cavity

Examples

paranasal sinus infection
CT scans can help detect infections in the paranasal sinus regions.
Mucous buildup in a paranasal sinus can result in facial pain and pressure.
The doctor explained that inflammation of the paranasal sinuses can cause sinus headaches.

  • synonym – sinus: a cavity within a bone or other tissue, especially those in the bones of the face or skull
