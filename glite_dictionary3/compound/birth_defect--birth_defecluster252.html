birth defect

noun
abnormality present at birth
Pronunciation: / bɝθ dɪˌfɛkt / (AmE), / bɜːθ dɪˌfɛkt / (BrE)
an abnormality or problem that is present in a baby at birth, affecting appearance, structure, or function
