floor wax

noun
wax-based floor polish
Pronunciation: / flɔr wæks / (AmE), / flɔː wæks / (BrE)
A substance containing wax, used for polishing and preserving the surface of floors.

Examples

apply floor wax to tiles
After cleaning the kitchen, she applied floor wax to give the tiles a bright shine.
The janitor uses floor wax on the hardwood every week to keep it protected.
You should let the floor wax dry completely before walking on the surface.
