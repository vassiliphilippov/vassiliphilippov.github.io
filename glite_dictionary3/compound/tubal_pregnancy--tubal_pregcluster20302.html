tubal pregnancy

noun
ectopic pregnancy in a Fallopian tube
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbəl ˈprɛɡnənsi / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbəl ˈprɛɡnənsi / (BrE)
A pregnancy in which the fertilized egg implants and develops in a Fallopian tube rather than the uterus.

Examples

tubal pregnancy diagnosis
A tubal pregnancy can be life-threatening if not treated promptly.
She was unaware of her tubal pregnancy until she experienced severe abdominal pain.
The doctor diagnosed her condition as a tubal pregnancy and scheduled emergency surgery.
