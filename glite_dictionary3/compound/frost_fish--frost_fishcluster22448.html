frost fish

noun
long, slender marine fish related to snake mackerel
Pronunciation: / frɔst fɪʃ / (AmE), / frɒst fɪʃ / (BrE)
a long-bodied marine fish with a slender, scaleless, whiplike body and sharp teeth, closely related to the snake mackerel
