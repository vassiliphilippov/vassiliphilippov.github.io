malted milk

noun
sweet drink with malted milk powder
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔltɪd mɪlk / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪd mɪlk / (BrE)
A sweet drink made from milk, ice cream, and malted milk powder, usually blended like a milkshake.
