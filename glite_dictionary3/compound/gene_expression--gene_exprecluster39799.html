gene expression

noun
biological process of using gene information
Pronunciation: / dʒiːn ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / dʒiːn ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
the process by which information from a gene is used to synthesize a functional product, typically a protein, through transcription and translation
