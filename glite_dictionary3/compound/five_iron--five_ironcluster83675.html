five iron

noun
golf club for mid-range shots
Pronunciation: / faɪv ˈaɪɚn / (AmE), / faɪv ˈaɪən / (BrE)
a golf club with an iron head that has a moderate loft, typically used for mid-range shots on the fairway

Examples

hit with a five iron
He used a five iron to reach the green from the fairway.
My five iron is my favorite club for mid-distance approach shots.
