sweet rocket

noun
fragrant flowering plant, Hesperis matronalis
Pronunciation: / swit ˈrɑkɪt / (AmE), / swiːt ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
A tall herbaceous plant (Hesperis matronalis), cultivated for its fragrant, usually purple or white flowers that are especially scented at night; widely naturalized in Europe, Asia, and North America.
