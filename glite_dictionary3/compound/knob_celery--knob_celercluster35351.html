knob celery

noun
celery grown for its edible root
Pronunciation: / nɑb ˈsɛl.ə.ri / (AmE), / nɒb ˈsɛl.əri / (BrE)
A type of celery cultivated for its thick, edible, aromatic root, commonly used in cooking; also known as celeriac.
