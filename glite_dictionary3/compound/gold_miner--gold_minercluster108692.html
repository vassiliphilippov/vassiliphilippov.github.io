gold miner

noun
person who mines gold
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊld ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / ɡəʊld ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to extract gold from the earth, especially by digging or panning in gold-bearing areas
