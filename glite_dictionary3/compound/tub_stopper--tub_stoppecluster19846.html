tub stopper

noun
plug for sealing a bathtub or sink
Pronunciation: / tʌb ˈstɑpər / (AmE), / tʌb ˈstɒpə / (BrE)
A small plug used to seal the drain in a bathtub or washbasin, preventing water from escaping.

Examples

tub stopper blocks drain
He replaced the old tub stopper with a new rubber one.
Make sure the tub stopper is securely in place before filling the tub.
She pulled the tub stopper out to let the bathwater drain away.
