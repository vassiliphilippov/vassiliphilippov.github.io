legacy of imperialism

noun
lasting impact of colonial rule
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡəsi əv ɪmˈpɪriəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡəsi əv ɪmˈpɪəriəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
the enduring political, economic, social, and cultural consequences resulting from a period of colonial rule and exploitation by imperial powers
