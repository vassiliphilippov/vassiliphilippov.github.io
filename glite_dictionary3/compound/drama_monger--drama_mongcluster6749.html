drama monger

noun
person who stirs up drama or trouble
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑːmə ˌmʌŋɚ / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːmə ˌmʌŋə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately stirs up drama, trouble, or conflict among others

Examples

Social media tends to reward the loudest drama mongers with the most attention.
