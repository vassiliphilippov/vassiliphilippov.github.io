soul mate

noun
person with deep or natural affinity
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊl ˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊl ˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person ideally suited to another as a close friend or romantic partner, based on a profound or natural affinity

Examples

true soul mate
He always felt that Anna was his soul mate from the moment they met.
It can take years to find your true soul mate.
Many novels tell stories of lovers searching for their soul mates.
