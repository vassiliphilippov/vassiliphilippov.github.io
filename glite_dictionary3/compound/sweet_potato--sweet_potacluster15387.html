sweet potato

noun
edible sweet orange-fleshed root vegetable
Pronunciation: / swit pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / swiːt pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
a large, sweet-tasting, starchy root vegetable with orange or white flesh, eaten as food and grown in warm climates; also, the tropical plant that produces this root
