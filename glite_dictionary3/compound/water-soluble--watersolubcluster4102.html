water-soluble

adjective
able to dissolve in water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˈsɑljəbəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈsɒljʊbəl / (BrE)
(of a substance) able to dissolve in water.

Examples

water-soluble vitamins
Always store water-soluble chemicals in a dry place to prevent accidental dissolution.
The packaging is made from water-soluble material that completely breaks down when wet.
Vitamin C is a water-soluble vitamin, meaning it dissolves easily in water.

Related Terms

  • antonym – insoluble: not able to be dissolved
  • synonym – soluble: able to be dissolved, especially in water
