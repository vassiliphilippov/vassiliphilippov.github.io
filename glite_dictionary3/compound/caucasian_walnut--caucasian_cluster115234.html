Caucasian walnut

noun
tree species with winged fruit, native to Caucasus
Pronunciation: / kɔːˈkeɪʒən ˈwɔlnʌt / (AmE), / kɔːˈkeɪziən ˈwɔːlnʌt / (BrE)
a medium-sized tree native to the Caucasus region, distinguished from other walnut trees by its much-branched form and winged fruit
