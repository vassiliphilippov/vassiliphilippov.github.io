obvious choice

noun
clearly best or logical option
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbviəs ʧɔɪs / (AmE), / ˈɒbviəs tʃɔɪs / (BrE)
a decision or option that is clearly the best or most logical among all possibilities

Examples

the obvious choice for dessert
Chocolate cake was the obvious choice for his birthday dessert.
For their family vacation, the beach was the obvious choice.
Given the circumstances, hiring Sarah was the obvious choice for the manager.

Related Terms

  • synonym – natural choice: an option that feels right or is most suitable
  • antonym – unlikely choice: an option that is not expected or logical
