Spanish Inquisition

proper noun
historic Catholic tribunal in Spain (1478–1834)
Pronunciation: / ˌspænɪʃ ɪnkwəˈzɪʃən /; / ˌspænɪʃ ɪnˌkwɪˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌspænɪʃ ɪŋkwɪˈzɪʃən /; / ˌspanɪʃ ɪnˌkwɪˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a tribunal and period of religious investigation and persecution in Spain, established in 1478 and active for several centuries to maintain Catholic orthodoxy
