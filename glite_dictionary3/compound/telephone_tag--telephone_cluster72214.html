telephone tag

noun
repeated missed phone connections
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌfoʊn tæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtelɪfəʊn tæɡ / (BrE)
a situation in which two or more people repeatedly try to call each other by telephone but fail to connect and end up leaving messages instead
