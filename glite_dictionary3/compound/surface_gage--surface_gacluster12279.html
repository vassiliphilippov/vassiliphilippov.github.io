surface gage

noun
instrument for testing flatness of surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfɪs ˌɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfɪs ˌɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
A measuring instrument consisting of a scriber on an adjustable stand, used to test the accuracy of flat surfaces.

surface gage for marking lines
A surface gage is essential for marking precise lines on metal surfaces.
