pearly-shelled mussel

noun
mussel with a pearly shell interior
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝli ʃɛld ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːli ʃɛld ˈmʌsəl / (BrE)
A freshwater or marine mussel that has a shell with a shiny, pearly interior, often used as a source of mother-of-pearl.
