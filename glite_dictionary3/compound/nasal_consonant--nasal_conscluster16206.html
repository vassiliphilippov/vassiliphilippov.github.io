nasal consonant

noun
consonant pronounced through the nose
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.zəl ˈkɑn.sə.nənt / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.zəl ˈkɒn.sə.nənt / (BrE)
A consonant sound produced with airflow through the nose while the mouth is closed, as in 'm', 'n', and 'ng' sounds.

Examples

nasal consonant like m
In English, the letter 'm' is pronounced as a nasal consonant.
Linguists often study the distribution of nasal consonants in different languages.
The sound at the end of 'song' is a nasal consonant.
