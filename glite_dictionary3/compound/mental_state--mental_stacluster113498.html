mental state

noun
condition of one's mind or emotions
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.tl̩ steɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl steɪt / (BrE)
a person's condition with regard to their thoughts, feelings, and psychological well-being, often relatively consistent during a given period

Examples

stable mental state
After hearing the good news, her mental state noticeably improved.
Meditation can help improve your mental state during stressful periods.
Therapists often assess a patient's mental state before beginning treatment.
