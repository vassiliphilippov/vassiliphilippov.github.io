Ash Wednesday

proper noun
first day of Lent in Christianity
Pronunciation: / æʃ ˈwɛnzdeɪ / (AmE), / æʃ ˈwɛnzdeɪ / (BrE)
The first day of Lent in the Christian calendar, observed seven weeks before Easter as a day of fasting and repentance.

Examples

Ash Wednesday church service
Ash Wednesday marks the beginning of the Lenten season in many Christian traditions.
On Ash Wednesday, many Christians attend church services to receive a cross of ashes on their foreheads.
She decided to give up chocolate for Lent starting on Ash Wednesday.
