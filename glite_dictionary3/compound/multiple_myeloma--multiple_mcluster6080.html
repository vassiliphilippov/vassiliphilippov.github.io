multiple myeloma

noun
cancer affecting multiple sites in bone marrow
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltɪpl ˌmaɪəˈloʊmə / (AmE), / ˈmʌltɪpl ˌmaɪəˈləʊmə / (BrE)
A type of cancer that forms in plasma cells and typically develops in several places within the bone marrow at the same time.
