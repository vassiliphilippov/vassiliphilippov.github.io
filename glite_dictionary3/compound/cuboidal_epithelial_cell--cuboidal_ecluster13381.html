cuboidal epithelial cell

noun
cube-shaped epithelial cell
Pronunciation: / kjuːˈbɔɪ.dl̩ ˌiː.pɪˈθiː.li.əl sɛl / (AmE), / kjuːˈbɔɪ.dəl ˌiː.pɪˈθiː.li.əl sɛl / (BrE)
an epithelial cell that is roughly cube-shaped, with approximately equal height, width, and depth, commonly found in certain glands and ducts
