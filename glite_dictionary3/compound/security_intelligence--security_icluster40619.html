security intelligence

noun
information on hostile threats or activities
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrəti ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti ɪnˈtelɪdʒəns / (BrE)
Information relating to the identity, capabilities, and intentions of hostile individuals or organizations, often collected to prevent espionage, sabotage, subversion, or terrorism.

security intelligence on terrorist groups
