magic bullet

noun
a simple solution to a complex problem
Pronunciation: / ˈmædʒɪk ˈbʊlɪt / (AmE), / ˈmadʒɪk ˈbʊlɪt / (BrE)
a simple and highly effective solution to a difficult or complex problem, especially one that is hoped for but unlikely to exist

Examples
