stone pine

noun
pine tree with edible seeds (pine nuts)
Pronunciation: / stoʊn paɪn / (AmE), / stəʊn paɪn / (BrE)
a medium-sized pine tree of southern Europe (Pinus pinea), with a broad, spreading crown, cultivated for its edible seeds (pine nuts)

Examples

majestic stone pine tree
A row of stone pines provides shade along the coastal road in Italy.
Many gardens in southern Europe feature a stone pine as a decorative tree.
The stone pine is prized throughout the Mediterranean for its delicious pine nuts.
