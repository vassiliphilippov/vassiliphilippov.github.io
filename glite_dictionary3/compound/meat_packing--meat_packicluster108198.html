meat packing

noun
wholesale processing and packaging of meat
Pronunciation: / mit ˈpækɪŋ / (AmE), / miːt ˈpakɪŋ / (BrE)
the wholesale slaughtering, processing, and packaging of meat for distribution and sale to retailers
