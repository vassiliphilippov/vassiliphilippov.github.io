diffusion pump

noun
vacuum pump producing high vacuum
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjuʒən pʌmp / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːʒən pʌmp / (BrE)
a vacuum pump that uses a jet of vapor to capture and remove gas molecules, producing a high vacuum

Examples

diffusion pump creates vacuum
A diffusion pump is essential for processes that require very low pressures, such as electron microscopy.
The laboratory installed a new diffusion pump to achieve ultra-high vacuum conditions for their experiments.
Unlike mechanical pumps, a diffusion pump operates without moving parts to generate a high vacuum.

Related Terms

  • synonym – vacuum pump: a pump that removes gas from a sealed volume to create a vacuum
