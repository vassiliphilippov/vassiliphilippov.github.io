to frack the land

verb
extract gas by hydraulic fracturing
Pronunciation: / fræk ðə lænd / (AmE), / fræk ðə lænd / (BrE)
to extract natural gas from beneath the ground using the process of hydraulic fracturing.

Examples

to frack the land for gas
Before agreeing to frack the land, the farmers insisted on strict environmental safeguards.
Environmental activists protested when they heard about plans to frack the land in their region.
The company plans to frack the land near the town to access natural gas reserves.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hydraulic fracturing: the process of injecting liquid at high pressure to fracture rocks and extract oil or gas
