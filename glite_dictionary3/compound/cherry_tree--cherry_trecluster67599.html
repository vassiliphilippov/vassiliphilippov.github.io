cherry tree

noun
tree producing cherries and hardwood
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛri tri / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛri triː / (BrE)
A tree or shrub that bears cherries, small fleshy round fruits with a single hard stone, and often yields valuable hardwood.

Examples

old cherry tree
Children picked ripe fruit from the old cherry tree at the edge of the field.
Many pieces of fine furniture are made from the wood of the cherry tree.
The cherry tree in our backyard is covered in beautiful pink blossoms every spring.
