Anglican Church

proper noun
national church of England and related churches
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡlɪkən tʃɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡlɪkən tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
The national church of England and the group of churches in other countries that share its beliefs, with its main see in Canterbury and the British monarch as its temporal head.

Examples

the Anglican Church in England
She attended an Anglican Church service while traveling in Canada.
The Anglican Church has its historic seat at Canterbury Cathedral.
The Anglican Church plays an important role in the spiritual life of England.
