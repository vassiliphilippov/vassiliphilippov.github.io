metabolic rate

noun
rate at which the body uses energy
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈbɑlɪk reɪt / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk reɪt / (BrE)
The rate at which a person's or animal's body uses energy, usually measured over a specific period of time.

Examples

high metabolic rate
Age is one of the factors that affect a person's metabolic rate.
An increase in exercise can boost your metabolic rate and help with weight loss.
Certain medical conditions may cause a low metabolic rate.
