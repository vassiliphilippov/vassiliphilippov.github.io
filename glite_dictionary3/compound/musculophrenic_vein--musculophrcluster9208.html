musculophrenic vein

noun
vein draining upper abdomen and lower chest wall
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌs.kjə.loʊˈfrɛn.ɪk veɪn / (AmE), / ˌmʌs.kjʊ.ləʊˈfrɛn.ɪk veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the upper abdominal wall and lower intercostal spaces into the internal thoracic vein

Examples
