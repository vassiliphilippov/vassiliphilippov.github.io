upside-down cake

noun
cake baked with fruit at the bottom and inverted before serving
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌsaɪd daʊn keɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌp.saɪdˈdaʊn keɪk / (BrE)
A cake made by baking a layer of sweetened fruit with batter on top, then turning the finished cake upside down so the fruit forms the topping.

Examples

pineapple upside-down cake
After baking, let the upside-down cake cool slightly before flipping it onto a plate.
She served a warm upside-down cake covered in caramelized pineapple and cherries for dessert.
The recipe called for peaches instead of the usual pineapples in the upside-down cake.
