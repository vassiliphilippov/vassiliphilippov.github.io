gentle breeze

noun
a light wind; Beaufort scale 3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ briːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnt.l̩ briːz / (BrE)
a wind blowing at a speed of 8–12 knots (about 15–22 km/h), rated as number 3 on the Beaufort scale

Examples

a gentle breeze cools
A gentle breeze stirred the leaves, bringing relief from the afternoon heat.
The boat moved smoothly under the steady push of a gentle breeze.
We sat outside enjoying the gentle breeze coming in from the lake.
