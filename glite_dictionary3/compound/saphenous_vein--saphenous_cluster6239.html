saphenous vein

noun
major superficial vein in the leg
Pronunciation: / səˈfinəs veɪn / (AmE), / səˈfiːnəs veɪn / (BrE)
either of two major superficial veins in the leg that drain blood from the foot

Examples

the saphenous vein in the leg
An ultrasound was used to assess the flow in her saphenous vein.
The surgeon located the saphenous vein before beginning the bypass procedure.
Varicose veins commonly develop in the saphenous vein due to faulty valves.
