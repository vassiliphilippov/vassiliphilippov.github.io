Scottish Highlander

noun
person from the Scottish Highlands
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːtɪʃ ˈhaɪləndər / (AmE), / ˈskɒtɪʃ ˈhaɪləndə / (BrE)
a person native to or living in the Highlands of Scotland, often associated with distinct cultural traditions
