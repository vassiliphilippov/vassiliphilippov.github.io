civil service

noun
government administration employees
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪv.əl ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪv.əl ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
the permanent professional branches of a government's administration, excluding military and judicial branches, whose employees are usually hired based on competitive examinations or merit.

Examples

work in the civil service
Jobs in the civil service are often secured through rigorous examinations.
She spent her entire career working in the civil service as a tax inspector.
The government is implementing reforms to make the civil service more efficient.
