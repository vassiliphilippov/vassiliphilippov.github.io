air sac

noun
small sac in lungs holding air
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌsæk /; / ˈɛr sæk / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌsæk /; / ˈeə sæk / (BrE)
a thin-walled, air-filled sac in the respiratory system of birds and some other animals that helps with breathing and gas exchange
