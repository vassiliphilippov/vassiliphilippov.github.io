paddle steamer

noun
steam vessel powered by paddle wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd.əl ˌstiː.mɚ / (AmE), / ˈpæd.əl ˌstiː.mə / (BrE)
a steam-powered vessel propelled by large paddle wheels on the sides or rear

Examples

historic paddle steamer cruise
The paddle steamer slowly made its way down the river, its wheels churning the water.
The museum houses a restored paddle steamer with original steam engines and wooden decks.
Tourists enjoyed a scenic ride on an old paddle steamer built in the nineteenth century.
