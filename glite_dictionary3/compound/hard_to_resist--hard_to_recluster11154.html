hard to resist

adjective
very tempting or difficult to refuse
Pronunciation: / hɑrd tu rɪˈzɪst / (AmE), / hɑːd tə rɪˈzɪst / (BrE)
very appealing or tempting, making it difficult to refrain from accepting or doing something

Examples

hard to resist a sweet offer
His charming smile was hard to resist at the party.
She found the offer hard to resist despite her initial doubts.
The dessert looked absolutely hard to resist, so I had a second helping.

Related Terms

  • antonym – easy to resist: easy to refuse or ignore
  • synonym – irresistible: impossible to resist
