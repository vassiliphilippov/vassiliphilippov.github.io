gudgeon pin

noun
pin joining piston to connecting rod
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌdʒən pɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡʌdʒən pɪn / (BrE)
A pin that connects the piston to the connecting rod in an internal combustion engine, allowing the piston to pivot during operation.

Examples

gudgeon pin in engine
A broken gudgeon pin can cause severe engine damage if not addressed promptly.
Modern engines use lightweight materials for the gudgeon pin to improve performance.
