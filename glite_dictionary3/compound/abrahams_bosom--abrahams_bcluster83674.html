Abraham's bosom

noun
place of rest for the righteous after death
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.brəˌhæmz ˈbʊz.əm /; / əˈbreɪhəmz ˈbʊzəm / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.brəˌhæmz ˈbʊz.əm /; / əˈbreɪəmz ˈbʊzəm / (BrE)
a place of comfort or paradise for the souls of the righteous after death, especially in biblical or Christian tradition
