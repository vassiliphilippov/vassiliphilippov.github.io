Rotary Club

noun
international service organization
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊˌtɛri klʌb / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəˌri klʌb / (BrE)
an international organization of local clubs, originally composed of business and professional people, devoted to community service and promoting goodwill and peace throughout the world

Examples

join the Rotary Club
Many professionals volunteer their time through the Rotary Club to improve global health initiatives.
She became a member of the local Rotary Club to support community projects.
The Rotary Club organized a fundraiser to help rebuild the town library.
