cost overrun

noun
excess cost above budget
Pronunciation: / kɑst ˈoʊvɚˌrʌn / (AmE), / kɒst ˈəʊvəˌrʌn / (BrE)
the amount by which actual costs exceed the budgeted or estimated costs for a project or activity

Examples

cost overrun in a project
A careful risk assessment can help avoid a major cost overrun in future projects.
Because of the cost overrun, the company had to secure additional financing to complete the work.
The construction project suffered a significant cost overrun due to delays and unexpected expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – budget overrun: an excess of expenditure above the planned budget
  • antonym – cost saving: a reduction in expected or actual costs
