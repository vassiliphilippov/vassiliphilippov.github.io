long in coming

adjective
delayed or overdue
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ɪn ˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋ ɪn ˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of something expected or desired) arriving after a considerable delay or taking a long time to happen.

Examples

long in coming answer
Her decision to move abroad was long in coming and surprised none of her friends.
Justice for the victims was long in coming, arriving years after the incident.
The official announcement was long in coming, but finally brought relief to the staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym – belated: happening or arriving later than expected or desired
  • synonym – overdue: not having arrived, happened, or been done by the expected time
