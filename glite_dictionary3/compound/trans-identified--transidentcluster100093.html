trans-identified

adjective
identifying as a different gender from birth
Pronunciation: / trænz aɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / tranz ɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd / (BrE)
Identifying as a gender that is different from the one assigned to a person at birth.

Examples

Many support groups offer resources for trans-identified individuals seeking medical and social support.

The study focused on the experiences of trans-identified youth in rural areas.
