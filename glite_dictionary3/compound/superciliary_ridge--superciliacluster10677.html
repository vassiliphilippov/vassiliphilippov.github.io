superciliary ridge

noun
ridge above the eye socket (anatomy)
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈsɪl.i.ˌɛr.i rɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈsɪl.i.ˌɛə.ri rɪdʒ / (BrE)
a ridge on the frontal bone located above the eye socket, forming the prominence of the brow
