variegated scouring rush

noun
species of horsetail plant
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛriˌeɪɡeɪtɪd ˈskaʊərɪŋ rʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈvɛəriˌeɪɡeɪtɪd ˈskaʊərɪŋ rʌʃ / (BrE)
