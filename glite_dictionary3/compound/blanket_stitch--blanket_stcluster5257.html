blanket stitch

noun
sewing stitch for blanket edges
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋkɪt stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈblaŋkɪt stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a strong, decorative sewing stitch used to reinforce the edges of blankets and other thick materials, similar to a buttonhole stitch

Examples

blanket stitch edges
For thicker fabrics, the blanket stitch provides extra strength and durability.
