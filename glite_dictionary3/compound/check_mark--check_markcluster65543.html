check mark

noun
mark showing completion or approval
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛk mɑrk /; / ˈtʃɛk ˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛk mɑːk / (BrE)
a written mark (✓) used to show that something is correct, approved, or completed
