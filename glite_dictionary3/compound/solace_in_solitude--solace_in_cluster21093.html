solace in solitude

noun
comfort found in being alone
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑlɪs ɪn səˈluːt̬ud / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪs ɪn səˈluːtjuːd / (BrE)
A state of comfort or peace experienced when one is alone.

Examples

Many writers claim they find their greatest inspiration and solace in solitude.
