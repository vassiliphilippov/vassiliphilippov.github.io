rebound relationship

noun
romantic relationship after a breakup
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌbaʊnd ˌrileɪˈʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈriːˌbaʊnd ˌriːleɪˈʃənʃɪp / (BrE)
a romantic relationship that someone enters soon after ending a previous one, often as a way to cope with emotional distress

Examples

a rebound relationship after breakup
After her long-term relationship ended, she found herself in a rebound relationship before she was truly ready.
He admitted that his latest romance was just a rebound relationship to fill the void.
Sometimes a rebound relationship can help someone move on, but it doesn't always last.
