cream sauce

noun
a white sauce made with cream
Pronunciation: / krim sɔs / (AmE), / kriːm sɔːs / (BrE)
a white sauce made primarily with cream, often used as a base or accompaniment in various dishes

Examples

cream sauce for pasta
Many pasta dishes use cream sauce as a delicious alternative to tomato-based sauces.
