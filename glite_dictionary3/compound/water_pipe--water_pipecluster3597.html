water pipe

noun
tobacco pipe with water cooling (hookah)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌpaɪp / (BrE)
an oriental smoking device consisting of a container of water through which smoke is drawn and cooled before being inhaled, typically using a long flexible tube; also known as a hookah or narghile.

Examples

smoke a water pipe
He spent the evening with friends, sharing stories and passing around a water pipe.
In many Middle Eastern cultures, smoking a water pipe is a social tradition.
The café offered traditional water pipes for guests to enjoy flavored tobacco.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hookah: an oriental smoking pipe with water for cooling smoke
  • synonym – narghile: variant of water pipe used for smoking
