box office

noun
ticket sales counter at a venue
Pronunciation: / bɑks ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / bɒks ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
The place in a theatre, cinema, or other venue where tickets are sold to the public.

Examples

at the box office window
I waited in line at the box office to buy tickets for the concert.
The movie was such a hit that the box office sold out within hours.
Tickets are available online or from the box office at the theater.
