liveborn infant

noun
infant alive after birth
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪvˌbɔrn ˈɪnfənt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪvbɔːn ˈɪnfənt / (BrE)
An infant who shows signs of life after birth, such as breathing, heartbeat, or movement.
