weather forecasting

noun
prediction of future weather conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðɚ fɔrkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ˈfɔːkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
The scientific process of predicting atmospheric conditions, such as temperature, precipitation, and wind, for a future time and location.

Examples

modern weather forecasting
Advancements in technology have greatly improved weather forecasting accuracy over the last few decades.
She studied meteorology because she was fascinated by weather forecasting.
The television station has a dedicated team for weather forecasting and updates.
