pectoralis major

noun
a large chest muscle
Pronunciation: / ˌpek.təˈreɪ.lɪs ˈmeɪ.dʒɚ / (AmE), / ˌpek.təˈreɪ.lɪs ˈmeɪ.dʒə / (BrE)
the thick, fan-shaped muscle located at the chest that helps move the arm by adducting and rotating it
