surgical vacuum pump

noun
device for extracting fluids or gases in surgery
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrdʒɪkəl ˈvækjuːm pʌmp / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪkəl ˈvækjuːm pʌmp / (BrE)
