Big Ben

proper noun
famous clock and bell in London
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ bɛn / (AmE), / bɪɡ bɛn / (BrE)
The great bell and commonly the clock in the clock tower of the Houses of Parliament in London, often used to refer to the tower itself.

London Big Ben clock
Maintenance work on Big Ben has temporarily silenced its famous hourly strikes.
