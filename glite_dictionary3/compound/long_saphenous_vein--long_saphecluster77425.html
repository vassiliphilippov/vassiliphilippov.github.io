long saphenous vein

noun
the longest vein in the human body
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ səˈfinəs veɪn / (AmE), / lɒŋ səˈfiːnəs veɪn / (BrE)
the longest vein in the human body, extending from the foot to the groin where it joins the femoral vein

Examples
