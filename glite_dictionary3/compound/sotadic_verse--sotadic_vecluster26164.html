sotadic verse

noun
verse that reads the same forwards and backwards
Pronunciation: / soʊˈtædɪk vɜrs / (AmE), / səʊˈtadɪk vɜːs / (BrE)
Palindromic verse in which the sequence of characters or words is the same when read forwards and backwards; named after Sotades, an ancient Greek poet.
