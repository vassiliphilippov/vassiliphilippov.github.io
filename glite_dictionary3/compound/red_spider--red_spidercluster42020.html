red spider

noun
small web-spinning mite, orchard pest
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈspaɪdɚ / (AmE), / rɛd ˈspaɪdə / (BrE)
A small web-spinning mite, often a serious pest in orchards and greenhouses, that feeds on plant leaves.

Examples

red spider on leaves
A close inspection revealed tiny red spiders spinning webs on the underside of the leaves.
Gardeners often spray to control red spider populations in greenhouses.
The apple orchard was infested with red spiders, damaging the leaves and fruit.
