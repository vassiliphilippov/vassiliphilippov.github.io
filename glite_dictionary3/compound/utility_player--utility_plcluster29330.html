utility player

noun
sports player able to play several positions
Pronunciation: / juˈtɪl.ə.ti ˌpleɪ.ər / (AmE), / juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti ˌpleɪ.ə / (BrE)
a player, especially in baseball, who is valued for the ability to play competently at several different positions

Examples

baseball utility player
As a utility player, she could fill in wherever the team needed her most.
His reputation as a utility player made him an invaluable member of the roster.
The team relied on their utility player to cover multiple positions throughout the season.
