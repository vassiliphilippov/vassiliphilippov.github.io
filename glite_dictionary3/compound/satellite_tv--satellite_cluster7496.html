satellite TV

noun
television system using satellites for broadcasting
Pronunciation: / ˈsæt̬.əl.aɪt ˌtiːˈviː / (AmE), / ˈsæt.ə.laɪt ˌtiːˈviː / (BrE)
A television broadcasting system in which the signal is transmitted to an orbiting satellite and then sent back to Earth for reception.

Examples

satellite TV channels
Many rural areas rely on satellite TV due to limited cable access.
Their satellite TV package includes hundreds of sports and movie channels.
We decided to get satellite TV so we could watch international channels.
