sensing element

noun
component that detects and responds to stimuli
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsɪŋ ˈɛləmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪŋ ˈɛlɪmənt / (BrE)
a device or component that detects a physical stimulus, such as heat, pressure, light, or motion, and responds with a measurable signal

Examples

sensing element in a sensor
A pressure gauge contains a sensing element that deforms in response to pressure changes.
Light detectors use a sensing element to convert incoming photons into electrical signals.
The thermocouple acts as the sensing element in many temperature measurement instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sensor: a device that detects or measures a physical property and records or responds to it
