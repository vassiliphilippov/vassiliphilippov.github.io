scalp lock

noun
long tuft of hair on a shaven head
Pronunciation: / skælp lɑk / (AmE), / skælp lɒk / (BrE)
A long tuft of hair left on the top of an otherwise shaved head, especially as worn by some Indigenous peoples of North America.

Examples

traditional scalp lock
After shaving his head, he left a scalp lock for cultural reasons.
Historical portraits often show men with a prominent scalp lock in traditional dress.
The young warrior wore a single scalp lock as a symbol of his bravery.
