contour feather

noun
feather covering a bird's body
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tʊr ˌfɛð.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tjʊə ˌfɛð.ər / (BrE)
one of the feathers that cover the body of an adult bird, giving it its shape and smooth outline

Examples

contour feather covers body
After molting, the sparrow's new contour feathers made its plumage look especially sleek.
The penguin's contour feathers form a waterproof layer that helps it swim efficiently.
While preening, the bird carefully realigned a few loose contour feathers on its wing.

Related Terms
