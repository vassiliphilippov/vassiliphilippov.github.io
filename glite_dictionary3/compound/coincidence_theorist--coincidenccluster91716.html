coincidence theorist

noun
someone who sees patterns in random events
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɪnsɪdəns ˈθɪəɹɪst / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns ˈθɪərɪst / (BrE)
a person who attributes significance or patterns to events that are likely random or coincidental
