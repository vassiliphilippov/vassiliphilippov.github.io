goose skin

noun
temporary raised bumps on skin
Pronunciation: / ˈɡus skɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡuːs skɪn / (BrE)
a temporary roughness of the skin with small raised bumps, caused by cold, fear, or emotional stress; also called goose bumps

Examples

goose skin from cold
She felt goose skin rise when she heard the eerie music in the dark.
Some people experience goose skin during strong emotional moments, like listening to moving music.
The sudden chill in the air gave me goose skin all over my arms.
