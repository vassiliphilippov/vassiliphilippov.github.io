angular furniture

noun
furniture with sharp, geometric shapes
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡjələr ˈfɝːnɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡjʊlə ˈfɜːnɪtʃə / (BrE)
Furniture characterized by sharp, geometric, and angular shapes, often associated with modern or contemporary styles.

Examples

angular furniture in modern rooms
Designers often use angular furniture to create a sense of sophistication in contemporary spaces.
