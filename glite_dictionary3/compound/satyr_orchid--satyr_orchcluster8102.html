satyr orchid

noun
a type of orchid with broad leaves and irregular flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪtɚ ˈɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / ˈseɪtə ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
A type of orchid with broad, ovate leaves and long-bracted, green, very irregular flowers, commonly found in southern Africa.
