close call

noun
a narrow escape or near miss
Pronunciation: / kloʊs kɔl / (AmE), / kləʊs kɔːl / (BrE)
a situation where something bad or dangerous almost happens but is narrowly avoided

That car almost hit us—it was a close call but everyone is okay.
