Bay of Ob

proper noun
inlet of the Kara Sea in western Siberia
Pronunciation: / beɪ əv ɑb / (AmE), / beɪ əv ɒb / (BrE)
a large inlet of the Kara Sea off the coast of western Siberia, Russia, receiving the waters of the Ob River.

Examples

the Bay of Ob in Siberia
Numerous rivers, including the Ob River, drain into the Bay of Ob.
Scientists conducted climate studies along the Bay of Ob coastline.
The Bay of Ob freezes over for several months each year due to the harsh Siberian winter.
