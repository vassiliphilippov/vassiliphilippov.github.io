occupancy rate

noun
percentage of occupied rental units
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkjəpənsi reɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒkjəpənsi reɪt / (BrE)
the percentage of available rooms or rental units that are occupied or rented at a particular time, especially in hotels or real estate

Examples

occupancy rate in hotels
A high occupancy rate is usually a sign of a successful apartment complex.
Due to the pandemic, the city's occupancy rate for hotels dropped dramatically.
The hotel boasted an impressive occupancy rate of 95% during the summer season.

Related Terms

  • antonym – vacancy rate: percentage of unoccupied rental units at a given time
