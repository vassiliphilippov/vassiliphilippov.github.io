bran muffin

noun
muffin made with wheat bran
Pronunciation: / bræn ˈmʌf.ɪn / (AmE), / bræn ˈmʌf.ɪn / (BrE)
a muffin made with bran, usually wheat bran, often considered a healthier baked good due to its high fiber content
