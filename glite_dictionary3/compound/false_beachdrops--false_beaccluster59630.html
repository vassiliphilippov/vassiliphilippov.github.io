false beachdrops

noun
a saprophytic woodland herb of North America
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈbitʃˌdrɑps / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈbiːtʃdrɒps / (BrE)
A fleshy, tawny or reddish saprophytic herb resembling the Indian pipe, found in woodland humus of eastern North America; sometimes placed in the separate genus Hypopitys.
