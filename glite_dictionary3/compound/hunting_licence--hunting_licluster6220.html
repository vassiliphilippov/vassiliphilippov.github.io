hunting licence

noun
permit to hunt animals
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ˌlaɪsəns / (BrE)
an official permit that allows someone to hunt and kill specific types of animals during a certain period
