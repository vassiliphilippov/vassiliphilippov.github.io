bilevel car

noun
rail car with two levels for passengers
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌlɛvəl kɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌlɛvəl kɑː / (BrE)
a railway passenger car with two levels, designed to increase capacity by providing seating on both upper and lower decks
