breast pocket

noun
chest pocket on a jacket or coat
Pronunciation: / brɛst ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / brɛst ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a pocket on the upper part of a jacket or coat, usually on the inside or outside of the chest area, especially in men's clothing

Examples

breast pocket handkerchief
A handkerchief peeped out from his breast pocket.
He slipped his wallet into the breast pocket of his suit jacket.
She found a ticket in the breast pocket of her old coat.
