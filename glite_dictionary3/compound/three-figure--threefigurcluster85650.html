three-figure

adjective
having three digits (100–999)
Pronunciation: / ˈθri ˌfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈθri ˌfɪɡjə / (BrE)
(of a number) consisting of three digits; between 100 and 999 inclusive.

Examples

three-figure salary
His salary jumped to a three-figure amount for the first time.
Only three-figure scores count for the competition.
The code must be a three-figure number to be valid.
