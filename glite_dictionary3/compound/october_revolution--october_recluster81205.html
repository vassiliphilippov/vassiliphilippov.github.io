October Revolution

noun
1917 Bolshevik takeover in Russia
Pronunciation: / ɑkˈtoʊbɚ ˌrɛvəˈluʃən / (AmE), / ɒkˈtəʊbə ˌrɛvəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
the overthrow of the Russian Provisional Government by the Bolsheviks led by Lenin in November 1917, marking the start of communist rule in Russia
