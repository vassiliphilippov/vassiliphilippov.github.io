family Bible

noun
large Bible for recording family events
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˈbaɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˈbaɪbəl / (BrE)
A large Bible kept in a family home, often with blank pages for recording marriages, births, and deaths of family members.

Examples

old family Bible
After the wedding, they carefully inscribed the date in their family Bible.
