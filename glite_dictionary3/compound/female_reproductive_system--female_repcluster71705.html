female reproductive system

noun
organs involved in female reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːmeɪl ɹɪˌproʊˈdʌktɪv ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl rɪˌprəʊˈdʌktɪv ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of organs and structures in female animals or humans responsible for producing eggs and supporting reproduction, including the ovaries, fallopian tubes, uterus, and vagina.
