esophageal smear

noun
test of material from the esophagus
Pronunciation: / ɪˌsɑfəˈdʒiəl smɪr / (AmE), / ɪˌsɒfəˈdʒiːəl smɪə / (BrE)
a medical test in which material is collected from the esophagus and spread on a slide for examination under a microscope
