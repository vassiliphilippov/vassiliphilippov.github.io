Kennelly-Heaviside layer

noun
region of the ionosphere reflecting radio waves
Pronunciation: / kəˌnɛli ˈhɛvisaɪd ˌleɪɚ / (AmE), / kəˌnɛli ˈhɛvɪsaɪd ˌleɪə / (BrE)
