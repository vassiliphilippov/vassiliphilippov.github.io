photographic print

noun
printed photograph from film or digital image
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪk prɪnt / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪk prɪnt / (BrE)
a picture on paper produced by exposing photographic film and developing it; a print made from a photographic negative or digital image.

Examples

old photographic print
He kept a photographic print of his grandparents in his wallet for years.
