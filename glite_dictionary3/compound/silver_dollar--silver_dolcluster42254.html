silver dollar

noun
plant with silver-white decorative seedpods
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ ˈdɑlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈdɒlə / (BrE)
A southeastern European plant (Lunaria annua) cultivated for its fragrant purplish flowers and distinctive round, flat, silver-white seedpods often used in indoor decoration.
