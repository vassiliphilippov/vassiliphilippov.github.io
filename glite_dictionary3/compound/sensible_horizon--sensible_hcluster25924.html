sensible horizon

noun
apparent line where sky meets Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsəbəl həˈraɪzn̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪbəl həˈrʌɪz(ə)n / (BrE)
the line at which the sky and Earth (or sea) appear to meet as observed from a particular point

Examples
