icebox cake

noun
chilled layered dessert, sometimes ice cream cake
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪs.bɑks keɪk / (AmE), / ˈaɪs.bɒks keɪk / (BrE)
A dessert made by layering ingredients such as cookies and whipped cream, then chilling until set; sometimes refers to ice cream molded to resemble a cake.
