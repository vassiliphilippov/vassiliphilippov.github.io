winter-blooming

adjective
flowering in winter (of plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntɚ ˌbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˌbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) flowering during the winter season.
