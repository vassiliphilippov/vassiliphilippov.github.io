half boot

noun
boot reaching halfway to the knee
Pronunciation: / hæf buːt / (AmE), / hɑːf buːt / (BrE)
a boot that reaches halfway up to the knee, typically covering the ankle and lower calf

Examples

leather half boot
His wardrobe included several kinds of footwear, but the brown half boots were his favorite.
She picked out a stylish pair of half boots for the autumn season.
The half boot is perfect for people who need more support than an ankle boot but less coverage than a knee boot.

Related Terms
