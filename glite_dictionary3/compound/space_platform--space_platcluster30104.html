space platform

noun
manned satellite for research
Pronunciation: / speɪs ˈplætˌfɔrm / (AmE), / speɪs ˈplætfɔːm / (BrE)
a manned artificial satellite maintained in a fixed orbit, used especially for scientific research and observation

Examples

Plans for an international space platform are being discussed by several national space agencies.
