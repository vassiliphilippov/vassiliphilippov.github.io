day return

noun
return ticket valid for same-day travel
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ rɪˌtɝn / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ rɪˌtɜːn / (BrE)
A return ticket that allows travel to a destination and back on the same day, usually at a reduced fare.

Examples

day return train ticket
A day return is often cheaper than two single tickets for the same route.
Do you offer a day return fare for students traveling to Oxford?
She bought a day return to London for her business meeting.
