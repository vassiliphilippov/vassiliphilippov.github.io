primary health care

noun
first-contact basic health care
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪˌmɛri hɛlθ kɛr / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪməri hɛlθ kɛː / (BrE)
basic health care provided as the first point of contact between a patient and the health care system, usually by general practitioners or community clinics.
