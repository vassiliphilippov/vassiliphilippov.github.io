night raven

noun
nocturnal or twilight-active heron
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt ˌreɪvən / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt ˌreɪvən / (BrE)
A type of heron that is active at night or during twilight, especially the night herons.

Examples

night raven calls at dusk
The night raven is rarely seen during the day, as it prefers to hunt after sunset.
Unlike most birds, the night raven thrives in the cool hours of dusk and night.
We spotted a night raven fishing silently along the moonlit riverbank.
