tank top

noun
sleeveless shirt with shoulder straps
Pronunciation: / tæŋk tɑp / (AmE), / tæŋk tɒp / (BrE)
A sleeveless, tight-fitting shirt with wide shoulder straps and a low neckline, worn as an outer or undergarment.

Examples

tank top with jeans
He layered a white tank top under his shirt for extra comfort.
She wore a bright blue tank top with jeans on the hot summer day.
The shop displays tank tops in every color for the new season.
