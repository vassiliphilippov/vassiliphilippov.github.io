time-challenged

adjective
struggling to manage time
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / taɪm ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
having difficulty managing time effectively or frequently lacking enough time to complete tasks

Examples

a time-challenged worker
As a time-challenged student, he often rushed to finish his assignments at the last minute.
He acknowledged being time-challenged but was determined to improve his scheduling skills.
The time-challenged manager tried using a planner to organize her meetings more efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disorganized: lacking order or the ability to plan effectively
  • antonym – efficient: working in an organized and productive way
