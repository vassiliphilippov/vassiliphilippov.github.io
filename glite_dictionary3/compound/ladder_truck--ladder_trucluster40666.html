ladder truck

noun
fire engine with extendable ladders
Pronunciation: / ˈlædər ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈlædə ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a fire engine equipped with extendable ladders used by firefighters to access high places or rescue people

Examples

firefighters use a ladder truck
A new ladder truck was added to the city's fleet to improve emergency response times.
The firefighters quickly arrived in their ladder truck to rescue people stranded on the upper floors.
When the building caught fire, the ladder truck was essential for reaching residents on the top floor.
