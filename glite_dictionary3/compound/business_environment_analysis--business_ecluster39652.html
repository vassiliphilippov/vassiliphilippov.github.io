business environment analysis

noun
analysis of external factors affecting a business
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ɪnˈvaɪrənmənt əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs ɪnˈvaɪərənmənt əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the systematic process of identifying and assessing external factors affecting a business, and developing strategies in response

Examples
