parenthetical expression

noun
word or phrase in parentheses
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛr.ənˈθɛt.ɪ.kəl ɪkˈsprɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌpær.ənˈθɛt.ɪ.kəl ɪkˈsprɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
A word, phrase, or clause inserted into a sentence within parentheses to provide explanation or commentary without affecting its grammatical structure.

Examples

parenthetical expression in a sentence
Her article contains several parenthetical expressions that clarify complex points.
The use of a parenthetical expression can help readers better understand the author's intent.
