shopping centre

noun
large complex of shops and restaurants
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpɪŋ ˌsentər / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ ˌsentə / (BrE)
A large building or group of connected buildings containing a variety of shops and often restaurants, with parking facilities, designed for shoppers to visit for retail and leisure purposes.

Examples

busy shopping centre
Many families spend their weekends at the shopping centre browsing shops and enjoying meals together.
The shopping centre was decorated with festive lights during the holiday season.
The city recently opened a new shopping centre with over fifty stores and several cafes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mall: a large enclosed shopping area with many stores and restaurants
  • degree lower – marketplace: an open area or building where goods are bought and sold
