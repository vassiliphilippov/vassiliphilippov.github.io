soft-shoe

noun
style of tap dance with soft-soled shoes
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑft ˌʃu / (AmE), / ˈsɒft ˌʃuː / (BrE)
A style of tap dancing performed in soft-soled shoes, typically with smooth, light, and graceful steps and little or no emphasis on loud foot taps.

Examples

soft-shoe dance routine
She learned the graceful movements of soft-shoe as part of her dance training.
The comedian delighted the audience with a classic soft-shoe routine.
Their performance combined singing, comedy, and a little soft-shoe.
