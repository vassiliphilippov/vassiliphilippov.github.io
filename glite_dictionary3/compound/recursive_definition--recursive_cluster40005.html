recursive definition

noun
mathematical definition using recursion
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɝːsɪv ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈkɜːsɪv ˌdɛfɪˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
