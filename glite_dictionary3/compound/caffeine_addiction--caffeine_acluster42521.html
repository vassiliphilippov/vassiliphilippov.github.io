caffeine addiction

noun
dependence on caffeine
Pronunciation: / kæˈfiːn əˈdɪkʃən / (AmE), / kæˈfiːn əˈdɪkʃən / (BrE)
A condition in which a person becomes physically or psychologically dependent on caffeine, often experiencing withdrawal symptoms when intake is reduced.

Examples

serious caffeine addiction
After years of drinking multiple cups of coffee a day, she realized she had a serious caffeine addiction.
He sought advice from his doctor about how to overcome his caffeine addiction.
Some people experience headaches or irritability when they try to break their caffeine addiction.
