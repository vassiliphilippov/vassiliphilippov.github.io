drop-off charge

noun
fee for returning a rental vehicle to a different location
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑp ˌɔf tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdrɒp ɒf tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a fee charged for returning a rented vehicle to a location different from the one where it was originally picked up

Examples

drop-off charge for rental car
Before booking, always check if a drop-off charge applies for returning the car to a different office.
Some rental companies waive the drop-off charge for one-way trips during promotions.
When I rented a car in Los Angeles and returned it in San Francisco, I had to pay a drop-off charge.
