little hand

noun
the hour hand of a clock or watch
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l hænd / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl hand / (BrE)
the shorter hand of a clock or watch that indicates the hours

Examples

little hand points hours
If the little hand is between eight and nine, it's almost nine o'clock.
