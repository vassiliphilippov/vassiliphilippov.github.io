common year

noun
a non-leap year
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˌjɪr / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˌjɪə / (BrE)
a year with 365 days, not a leap year

Examples

a common year in the calendar
Most years in the Gregorian calendar are classified as common years.
Since it's a common year, February has only 28 days.
The year 2023 is a common year because it does not include an extra day in February.
