least squares

noun
curve-fitting method minimizing squared errors
Pronunciation: / list skwɛrz / (AmE), / liːst skwɛəz / (BrE)
a method in mathematics and statistics for fitting a curve to a set of data points by minimizing the sum of the squares of the distances of the points from the curve

Examples

least squares method
Most statistical software includes tools for least squares curve fitting and error analysis.
She applied least squares to analyze the experimental data and determine the optimal coefficients.
The least squares method is commonly used to find the best-fitting line in linear regression.
