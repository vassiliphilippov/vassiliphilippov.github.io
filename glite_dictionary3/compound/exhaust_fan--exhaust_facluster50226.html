exhaust fan

noun
fan that expels air from an area
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔst fæn / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst fan / (BrE)
A fan that removes air from an enclosed space, typically to improve ventilation.

Examples

exhaust fan in kitchen
The kitchen exhaust fan helps to remove smoke and cooking odors quickly.
They installed a powerful exhaust fan in the bathroom to reduce moisture and prevent mold.
Without an exhaust fan, the workshop became hot and stuffy during the summer.
