ovarian vein

noun
vein draining blood from the ovaries
Pronunciation: / oʊˈvɛriən veɪn / (AmE), / əʊˈvɛəriən veɪn / (BrE)
either of the veins that drain blood from the ovaries; the right ovarian vein drains into the inferior vena cava, and the left into the left renal vein.

Examples

ovarian vein blockage
A blockage in the ovarian vein can lead to a rare condition called ovarian vein thrombosis.
During surgery, the doctor carefully avoided damaging the ovarian vein.
The ovarian vein plays a crucial role in returning blood from the ovary to the heart.
