wall rue

noun
species of small fern on walls or cliffs
Pronunciation: / wɔl ru / (AmE), / wɔːl ruː / (BrE)
A small, delicate fern (Asplenium ruta-muraria) that grows on walls and rocky slopes in Eurasia and North America.
