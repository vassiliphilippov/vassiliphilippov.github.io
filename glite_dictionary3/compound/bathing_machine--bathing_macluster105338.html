bathing machine

noun
historical beach changing hut on wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪðɪŋ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈbeɪðɪŋ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a small hut on wheels used at beaches in the 18th and 19th centuries to allow people to change clothes and enter the sea in privacy
