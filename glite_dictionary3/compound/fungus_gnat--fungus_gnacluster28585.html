fungus gnat

noun
small fly destructive to fungi and seedlings
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋɡəs næt / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋɡəs nat / (BrE)
a small, dark fly (insect) whose larvae feed on fungi or decaying matter and can damage plants
