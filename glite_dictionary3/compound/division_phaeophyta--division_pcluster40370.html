division Phaeophyta

noun
division of brown algae (biology)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɪʒən fiːˈɒfəˌfaɪtə / (AmE), / dɪˈvɪʒən fiːˈɒfɪˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a division of brown algae in some biological classifications, equivalent to class Phaeophyceae or sometimes included in the division Heterokontophyta

Examples

division Phaeophyta includes kelp
Many species of kelp belong to the division Phaeophyta and play important roles in marine ecosystems.
Some older textbooks refer to brown seaweeds as members of the division Phaeophyta.
The division Phaeophyta is known for its characteristic brown pigments that mask the green of chlorophyll.
