Middle Irish

noun
historical stage of Irish (1100–1500)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdəl ˈaɪrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪdəl ˈaɪərɪʃ / (BrE)
the form of the Irish Gaelic language spoken and written from about 1100 to 1500
