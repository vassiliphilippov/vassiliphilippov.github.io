Erb-Duchenne paralysis

noun
paralysis of the arm from brachial plexus injury
Pronunciation: / ɜrb duːˈʃɛn pəˈræləsɪs / (AmE), / ɜːb djuːˈʃɛn pəˈralɪsɪs / (BrE)
A form of paralysis of the arm caused by injury to the brachial plexus, often occurring during childbirth.
