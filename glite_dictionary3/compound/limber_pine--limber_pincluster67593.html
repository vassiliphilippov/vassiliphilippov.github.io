limber pine

noun
western North American pine tree
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmbər paɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪmbə paɪn / (BrE)
A North American pine tree (Pinus flexilis) with long needles, flexible branches, and dark-grey furrowed bark, found in mountainous regions in the west of the continent.
