elkhorn fern

noun
ornamental fern with antler-shaped fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlkˌhɔrn ˌfɜrn / (AmE), / ˈɛlkˌhɔːn ˌfɜːn / (BrE)
a large epiphytic fern of Australia, southeastern Asia, and Polynesia, with fronds shaped like elk antlers, often cultivated as an ornamental plant
