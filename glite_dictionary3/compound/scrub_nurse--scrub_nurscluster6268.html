scrub nurse

noun
nurse assisting in surgery
Pronunciation: / skrʌb nɝːs /; / skrʌb nɜrs / (AmE), / skrʌb nɜːs / (BrE)
a nurse who assists the surgeon during an operation by keeping things sterile and handling surgical instruments
