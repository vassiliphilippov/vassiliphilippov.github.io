negative Nancy

noun
habitually pessimistic person (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪv ˈnænsi / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv ˈnænsi / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is habitually pessimistic or always sees the negative side of things

Examples

negative Nancy complains often
Don't be such a negative Nancy—try to see the positive side for once!
Stop acting like a negative Nancy and give the new plan a chance.
Whenever something goes wrong, he turns into a real negative Nancy.
