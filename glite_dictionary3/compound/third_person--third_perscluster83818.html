third person

noun
grammatical category for 'he', 'she', 'it', etc.
Pronunciation: / θɝd ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / θɜːd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
the grammatical category used for pronouns and verb forms referring to someone or something other than the speaker or the person addressed

Examples

third person pronoun
In English, the pronoun 'he' is used for the third person.
Stories written in the third person use 'he', 'she', or 'they' instead of 'I' or 'you'.
The third person singular form of the verb 'to be' is 'is'.
