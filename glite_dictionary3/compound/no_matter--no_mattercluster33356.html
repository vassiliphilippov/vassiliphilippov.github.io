no matter

x
regardless of circumstance; whatever happens
Pronunciation: / noʊ ˈmæt̬ər / (AmE), / nəʊ ˈmætə / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something makes no difference or is true in all circumstances; regardless of what happens or what is true.

Examples

no matter how hard
He plans to finish the project no matter how difficult it gets.
She was determined to run the race, no matter the weather.
You should keep trying, no matter what obstacles you face.

Related Terms

  • synonym – regardless: without being affected by; despite the circumstances
  • synonym – whatever: anything that happens or is true; no matter what
