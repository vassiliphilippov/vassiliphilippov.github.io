spiny puffer

noun
a pufferfish with spines
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪni ˈpʌfər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˈpʌfə / (BrE)
A type of pufferfish characterized by having rigid or erectile spines on its body.
