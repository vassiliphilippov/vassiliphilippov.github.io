nasal sinus

noun
air-filled cavity near the nasal cavity
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪzəl ˈsaɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪzl̩ ˈsaɪnəs / (BrE)
any of the paired air-filled cavities in the bones of the face adjacent to the nasal cavity, lined with mucous membrane continuous with the nasal lining

Examples

blocked nasal sinus
Allergies can sometimes cause inflammation of a nasal sinus.
He was diagnosed with a severe infection in his nasal sinus.
The doctor explained that the pain was coming from her left nasal sinus.
