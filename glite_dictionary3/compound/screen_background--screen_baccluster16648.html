screen background

noun
area behind icons/windows on screen
Pronunciation: / skrin ˈbækˌɡraʊnd / (AmE), / skriːn ˈbækɡraʊnd / (BrE)
in computing, the part of a display screen in a graphical user interface against which icons, folders, and windows are shown

Examples

screen background image
She prefers a simple screen background to avoid distractions while working.
The screen background was cluttered with icons and open windows, making it hard to find anything.
You can personalize your computer by changing the screen background to your favorite photo.
