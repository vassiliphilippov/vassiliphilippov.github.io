Spanish pointer

noun
Spanish breed of gun dog; points game
Pronunciation: / ˈspænɪʃ ˈpɔɪntər / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ ˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
a breed of strong, slender, short-haired gun dog of Spanish origin, typically white with brown or black patches, known for its ability to scent and point out game

During the hunt, the Spanish pointer expertly located and pointed to the hidden quail.
