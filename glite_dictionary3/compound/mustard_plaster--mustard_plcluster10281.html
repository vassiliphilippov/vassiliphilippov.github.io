mustard plaster

noun
cloth dressing with mustard for pain relief
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstərd ˌplæstər / (AmE), / ˈmʌstəd ˌplɑːstə / (BrE)
a cloth or dressing prepared with powdered mustard, applied to the skin to produce warmth or irritation as a remedy for aches and pains

Examples

apply a mustard plaster
After injuring her shoulder, she tried applying a mustard plaster in hopes of relieving the pain.
Her grandmother swore by a mustard plaster to ease chest congestion during cold winters.
The doctor recommended a mustard plaster for his aching back, citing its traditional use.
