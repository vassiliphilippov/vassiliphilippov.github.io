ethmoid sinus

noun
air-filled cavity between nose and eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθ.mɔɪd ˈsaɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.mɔɪd ˈsʌɪ.nəs / (BrE)
One of a group of small air-filled cavities located between the nose and the eyes, behind the bridge of the nose.

Examples

infected ethmoid sinus
A CT scan revealed an infection in her left ethmoid sinus.
Because the ethmoid sinus is close to the eyes, severe infections may affect vision.
The doctor explained that inflammation of the ethmoid sinus can cause pressure between the eyes.
