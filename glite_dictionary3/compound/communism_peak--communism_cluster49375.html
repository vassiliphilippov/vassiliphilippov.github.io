Communism Peak

proper noun
former name of Tajikistan's highest mountain
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪzəm piːk / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪzəm piːk / (BrE)
the former name for Ismail Samani Peak, the highest mountain in the Pamir range in northeastern Tajikistan, near the Chinese border (24,590 feet/7,495 meters high)
