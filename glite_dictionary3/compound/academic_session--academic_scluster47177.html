academic session

noun
period when classes are held at an institution
Pronunciation: / əˈkædəmɪk ˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / əˈkædəmɪk ˈsɛʃən / (BrE)
the period during which classes are held at a school, college, or university, usually as part of the academic year

Examples

academic session starts in September
Due to unforeseen circumstances, the academic session was extended by two weeks.
Many students travel home during the holidays between each academic session.
The university's academic session begins in September and ends in May.
