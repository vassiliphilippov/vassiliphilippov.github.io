giant willowherb

noun
a tall flowering North American perennial plant
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈwɪloʊˌhɜrb / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈwɪləʊˌhɜːb / (BrE)
