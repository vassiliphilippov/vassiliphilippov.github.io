safety valve

noun
device or means to relieve pressure or stress
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti vælv / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti vɑːlv / (BrE)
A device that automatically releases pressure in a system when it becomes too high, or a means of relieving excess emotion or stress.

Examples

safety valve for stress
Taking regular exercise acts as a safety valve for many people struggling with stress.
The safety valve released steam to prevent the boiler from exploding.
The government introduced new policies as a safety valve for public dissatisfaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym – relief valve: a device for letting out excess pressure from a system
