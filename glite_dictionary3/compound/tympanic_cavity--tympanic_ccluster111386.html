tympanic cavity

noun
middle ear cavity between eardrum and inner ear
Pronunciation: / tɪmˈpænɪk ˈkævəti / (AmE), / tɪmˈpænɪk ˈkævəti / (BrE)
the main cavity in the middle ear, located between the eardrum and the inner ear, containing the auditory ossicles
