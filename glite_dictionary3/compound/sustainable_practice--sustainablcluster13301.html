sustainable practice

noun
environmentally friendly method or action
Pronunciation: / səˈsteɪnəbl ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / səˈsteɪnəbl ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
a method or approach that has minimal negative impact on the environment and can be maintained over time

Examples

sustainable practice in farming
Educating students about sustainable practices helps prepare them for a greener future.
Many companies are adopting sustainable practices to reduce their carbon footprint.
The transition to sustainable practices in agriculture is crucial for protecting biodiversity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – green practice: a practice that is especially focused on helping the environment
  • antonym – unsustainable practice: a practice that cannot be maintained long-term due to negative impacts
