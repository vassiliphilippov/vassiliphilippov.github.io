drop forge

noun
machine for shaping metal by impact
Pronunciation: / drɑp fɔrdʒ / (AmE), / drɒp fɔːdʒ / (BrE)
A heavy machine used to shape metal by dropping a hammer or die onto heated material, producing large forgings.

Examples

operate a drop forge
A skilled operator is required to manage the drop forge safely and efficiently.
During the demonstration, the engineer explained how a drop forge shapes hot metal.
The factory uses a drop forge to produce heavy steel components for machinery.
