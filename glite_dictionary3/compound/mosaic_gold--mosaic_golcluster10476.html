mosaic gold

noun
a yellow pigment used in gilding
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪ.ɪk ɡoʊld / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪ.ɪk ɡəʊld / (BrE)
a bright yellow pigment, often made from tin(IV) sulfide, sometimes suspended in lacquer and used in gilding and decorative painting
