celery-topped pine

noun
Tasmanian evergreen coniferous tree
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛləri tɑpt paɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri tɒpt paɪn / (BrE)
a medium-sized evergreen coniferous tree (Phyllocladus aspleniifolius) native to Tasmania, with leaves resembling celery and valued for its timber
