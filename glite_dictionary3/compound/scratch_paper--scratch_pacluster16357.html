scratch paper

noun
paper for rough notes or sketches
Pronunciation: / skrætʃ ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / skrætʃ ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
Paper used for writing notes, calculations, or sketches quickly and without concern for neatness; often for preliminary or temporary work.

Examples

scratch paper for notes
Can I borrow some scratch paper for my rough draft notes?
He grabbed a sheet of scratch paper to work out the math problem.
Students are allowed to use scratch paper during the exam for calculations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – notepad: a small pad of blank or lined paper for writing notes
  • synonym – scribbling block: British term for a pad used for scribbling notes or sketches
