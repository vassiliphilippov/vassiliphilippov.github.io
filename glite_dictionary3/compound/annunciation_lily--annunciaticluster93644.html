Annunciation lily

noun
lily with white funnel-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / əˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən ˈlɪli / (AmE), / əˌnʌnsɪˈeɪʃ(ə)n ˈlɪli / (BrE)
