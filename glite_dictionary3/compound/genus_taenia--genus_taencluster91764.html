genus Taenia

noun
genus of parasitic tapeworms
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs tiˈniːə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs tiˈniːə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Taeniidae, consisting of tapeworms that are parasitic in the intestines of vertebrates, including humans.
