rock dove

noun
a species of pigeon; ancestor of domestic pigeons
Pronunciation: / rɑk dʌv / (AmE), / rɒk dʌv / (BrE)
a pale grey pigeon native to Eurasia, with black-striped wings, from which most domestic pigeons are descended

Examples

rock dove on cliffs
A flock of rock doves circled above the rocky cliffs.
Many modern domestic pigeons are descended from the wild rock dove.
