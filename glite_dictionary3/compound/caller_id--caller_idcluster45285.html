caller ID

noun
telephone service displaying caller's number/name
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔlɚ aɪˈdi / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlə aɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a service or device that displays the phone number and sometimes the name of an incoming caller

Examples

caller ID shows number
He blocked his number so his caller ID would not be displayed.
I didn't recognize the number on the caller ID, so I let the call go to voicemail.
Most modern smartphones display the caller ID before you answer a call.
