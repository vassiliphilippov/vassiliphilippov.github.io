New Wave

noun
a French film movement (1960s) with innovative techniques
Pronunciation: / nuː weɪv / (AmE), / njuː weɪv / (BrE)
A movement in French cinema of the late 1950s and 1960s characterized by innovative narrative techniques and visual styles, rejecting traditional filmmaking conventions.
