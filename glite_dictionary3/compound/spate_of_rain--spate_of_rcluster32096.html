spate of rain

noun
sudden or heavy burst of rain
Pronunciation: / speɪt əv reɪn / (AmE), / speɪt əv reɪn / (BrE)
a sudden or heavy outbreak of rain occurring over a short period of time

Examples

heavy spate of rain
After a long dry spell, a spate of rain revived the wilted crops in the region.
Construction was delayed by an unexpected spate of rain that flooded the worksite.
The forecast warned of a spate of rain throughout the afternoon, so we packed umbrellas just in case.
