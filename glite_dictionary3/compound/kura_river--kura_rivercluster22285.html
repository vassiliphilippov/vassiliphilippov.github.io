Kura River

proper noun
river in western Asia, flows to Caspian Sea
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊrə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈkʊərə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in western Asia that rises in northeast Turkey and flows eastward through Georgia and Azerbaijan into the Caspian Sea.

Examples

Kura River flows east
Industrial pollution has affected fish populations in the Kura River basin.
Several major cities, including Tbilisi, are situated along the Kura River.
The Kura River plays a vital role in the irrigation systems of Azerbaijan.
