percussive maintenance

noun
fixing a device by hitting it
Pronunciation: / pərˈkʌsɪv meɪnˈteɪnəns / (AmE), / pəˈkʌsɪv meɪnˈteɪnəns / (BrE)
the act of attempting to fix a mechanical or electronic device by hitting or tapping it, often humorously or when other fixes have failed

Examples
