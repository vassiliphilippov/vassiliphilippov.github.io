battle of Trasimeno

noun
defeat of Romans by Hannibal, 217 BC
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəl əv trəˈsɪməˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv trəˈsɪmɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a battle in central Italy in 217 BC where Hannibal defeated the Romans under Flaminius during the Second Punic War

Examples

the battle of Trasimeno in Italy
Historians often study the tactics used by Hannibal at the battle of Trasimeno.
