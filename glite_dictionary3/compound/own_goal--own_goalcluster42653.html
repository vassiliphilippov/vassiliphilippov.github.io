own goal

noun
goal accidentally scored for the opposing team
Pronunciation: / oʊn ɡoʊl / (AmE), / əʊn ɡəʊl / (BrE)
(in soccer and some other sports) a goal scored inadvertently by a player against their own team.

Examples

score an own goal
It was unfortunate that his mistake resulted in an own goal during such an important game.
That own goal put the home team at a disadvantage early in the match.
The defender was devastated after scoring an own goal in the final minute.
