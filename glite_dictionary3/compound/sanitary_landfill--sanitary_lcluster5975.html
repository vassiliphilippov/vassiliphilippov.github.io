sanitary landfill

noun
waste disposal site with buried refuse
Pronunciation: / ˈsæn.ə.ˌtɛr.i ˈlænˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˈsæn.ɪ.tər.i ˈlæn.fɪl / (BrE)
A site where waste material is buried and covered with layers of earth to reduce pollution and smell.

Examples

sanitary landfill for waste
Household trash is collected and then transported to a sanitary landfill outside the city limits.
Proper construction of a sanitary landfill can minimize groundwater contamination.
The city built a sanitary landfill to manage its growing waste problem.
