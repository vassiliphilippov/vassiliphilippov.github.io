left atrium

noun
upper left heart chamber
Pronunciation: / lɛft ˈeɪtriəm /; / lɛft ˈeɪ.tri.əm / (AmE), / lɛft ˈeɪtrɪəm /; / lɛft ˈeɪ.tri.əm / (BrE)
the upper left chamber of the heart that receives oxygenated blood from the lungs and sends it to the left ventricle
