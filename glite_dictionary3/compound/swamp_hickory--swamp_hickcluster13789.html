swamp hickory

noun
a type of hickory tree with bitter nuts
Pronunciation: / swɑmp ˈhɪkəri / (AmE), / swɒmp ˈhɪkəri / (BrE)
a hickory tree native to the eastern United States, distinguished by leaves with 7 or 9 leaflets and thin-shelled, very bitter nuts
