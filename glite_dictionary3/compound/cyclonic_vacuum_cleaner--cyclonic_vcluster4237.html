cyclonic vacuum cleaner

noun
vacuum cleaner with cyclonic dust separation
Pronunciation: / saɪˈklɑnɪk ˈvækyum kliːnɚ / (AmE), / saɪˈklɒnɪk ˈvækjʊəm ˈkliːnə / (BrE)
A vacuum cleaner that uses cyclonic separation to remove dust and debris into a detachable cylindrical bin, rather than using a filtration bag.

Examples

cyclonic vacuum cleaner collects dust
She prefers using a cyclonic vacuum cleaner because it requires no replacement bags.
The new cyclonic vacuum cleaner collects dust in a transparent bin that is easy to empty.
Unlike traditional upright models, a cyclonic vacuum cleaner doesn't lose suction as the bin fills up.
