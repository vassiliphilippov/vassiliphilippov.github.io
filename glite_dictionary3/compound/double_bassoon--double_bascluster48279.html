double bassoon

noun
very large, low-pitched bassoon
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl bəˈsun / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl bəˈsuːn / (BrE)
a large woodwind instrument in the bassoon family, pitched an octave lower than the standard bassoon
