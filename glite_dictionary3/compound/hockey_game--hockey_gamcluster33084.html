hockey game

noun
game on ice with teams and puck
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑki ˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈhɒki ˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played on an ice rink by two teams of six players each, who use sticks to direct a puck into the opposing team's goal.

Examples

exciting hockey game
The kids are playing a hockey game on the frozen pond.
Tickets for the championship hockey game sold out in minutes.
