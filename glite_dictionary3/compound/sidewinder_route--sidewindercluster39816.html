sidewinder route

noun
an indirect path or method
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌwaɪndər ruːt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌwaɪndə ruːt / (BrE)
an indirect or circuitous path or method, often taken to avoid obstacles or detection

Examples

take a sidewinder route
He took the sidewinder route to avoid traffic on the main road.
Sometimes a sidewinder route is the best way around difficult problems.
They suspected he was using a sidewinder route instead of dealing with the issue directly.

Related Terms
