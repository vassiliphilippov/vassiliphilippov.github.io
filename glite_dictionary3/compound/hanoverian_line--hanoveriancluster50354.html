Hanoverian line

proper noun
English royal house (1714–1901)
Pronunciation: / ˌhænəˈvɪriən laɪn / (AmE), / ˌhanəˈvɪərɪən laɪn / (BrE)
the English royal house that ruled from 1714 to 1901, beginning with George I and ending with Queen Victoria
