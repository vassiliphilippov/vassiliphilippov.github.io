Neapolitan ice cream

noun
ice cream with layers of different flavors and colors
Pronunciation: / niˈɑːpəlɪtən aɪs ˈkriːm / (AmE), / niːˌæpəˈlɪtən aɪs ˈkriːm / (BrE)
A type of ice cream consisting of three or more distinct layers, each a different color and flavor, arranged side by side in a single block.

Examples

slice of Neapolitan ice cream
She served slices of Neapolitan ice cream for dessert at the birthday party.
The presentation of the old-fashioned Neapolitan ice cream reminded her of her childhood.
Whenever we buy Neapolitan ice cream, my brother always eats the chocolate layer first.
