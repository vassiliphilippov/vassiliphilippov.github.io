strelitzia family

noun
family of tropical woody plants
Pronunciation: / strəˈlɪtsiə ˈfæməli / (AmE), / strɛˈlɪtsiə ˈfamɪli / (BrE)
a family of tropical woody plants with erect stems, native to South America, South Africa, and Madagascar, sometimes classified with the bananas (Musaceae)

the strelitzia family of plants
Botanists debate whether the strelitzia family should be grouped with the banana family.
