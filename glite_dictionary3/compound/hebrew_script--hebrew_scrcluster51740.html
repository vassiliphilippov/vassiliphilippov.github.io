Hebrew script

noun
alphabet used for Hebrew, Yiddish, and Ladino
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːbruː skrɪpt / (AmE), / ˈhiːbruː skrɪpt / (BrE)
the alphabetic writing system used for the Hebrew language and, later, for languages such as Yiddish and Ladino.
