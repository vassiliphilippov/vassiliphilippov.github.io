wild raspberry

noun
raspberry plant or fruit growing wild
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈræzˌbɛri / (AmE), / waɪld ˈræzˌbəri / (BrE)
A raspberry plant that grows naturally without cultivation, especially the common European species; its fruit is typically red or orange.
