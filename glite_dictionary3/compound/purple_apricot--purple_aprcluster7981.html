purple apricot

noun
a hybrid apricot with purple fruit and twigs
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˈæp.rɪ.kɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈæp.rɪ.kɒt / (BrE)
a small hybrid apricot native to Asia and Asia Minor, notable for its purplish twigs, white flowers, and purple fruit considered inferior in quality
