steam boiler

noun
sealed vessel generating steam from water
Pronunciation: / stiːm ˈbɔɪlɚ / (AmE), / stiːm ˈbɔɪlə / (BrE)
a sealed container in which water is heated until it becomes steam, especially for providing power in engines or heating systems
