waste-paper basket

noun
open-topped bin for discarded paper
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪst ˌpeɪpɚ ˈbæskət / (AmE), / ˈweɪst ˌpeɪpə ˈbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A small container with an open top, used for holding discarded paper and other rubbish, especially in offices or homes.

Examples

waste-paper basket under desk
Please empty your waste-paper basket before leaving for the weekend.
She tossed the crumpled letter into the waste-paper basket under her desk.
The office was tidy except for the overflowing waste-paper basket in the corner.
