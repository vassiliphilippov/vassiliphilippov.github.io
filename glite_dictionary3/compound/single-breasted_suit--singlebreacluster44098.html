single-breasted suit

noun
suit with a single-breasted jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl ˈbrɛstɪd suːt / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˈbrɛstɪd suːt / (BrE)
A suit in which the jacket fastens with a single row of buttons and has no overlapping of fabric at the front.

Examples

classic single-breasted suit
During the summer wedding, most guests opted for a lightweight single-breasted suit.
He wore a classic single-breasted suit to the interview, which made him look sharp and professional.
The store offers both double-breasted and single-breasted suits to match different styles.

Related Terms

  • antonym – double-breasted suit: a suit with a jacket having wide, overlapping front flaps and two parallel columns of buttons
