state supreme court

noun
highest court in a U.S. state
Pronunciation: / steɪt səˈpriːm kɔrt / (AmE), / steɪt səˈpriːm kɔːt / (BrE)
the highest judicial court within a state of the United States, which has ultimate appellate jurisdiction over state law matters
