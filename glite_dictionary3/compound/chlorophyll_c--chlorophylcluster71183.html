chlorophyll c

noun
pigment found in brown algae and some protists
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔrəˌfɪl ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˈklɒr.əˌfɪl ˈsiː / (BrE)
a type of chlorophyll found in brown algae, diatoms, and flagellates, involved in photosynthesis
