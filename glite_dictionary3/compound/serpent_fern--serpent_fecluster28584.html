serpent fern

noun
a tropical fern with lobed bluish-green fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrpənt fɜrn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːpənt fɜːn / (BrE)
a tropical American fern with brown scaly rhizomes and large, deeply lobed bluish-green fronds, sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant; sometimes classified in the genus Polypodium
