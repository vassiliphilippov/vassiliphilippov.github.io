calf roping

noun
rodeo event: lassoing and tying a calf
Pronunciation: / kæf ˈroʊ.pɪŋ / (AmE), / kɑːf ˈrəʊ.pɪŋ / (BrE)
A rodeo event in which a contestant on horseback chases, lassos, and ties the legs of a calf as quickly as possible.

Examples

win calf roping contest
He won first place in calf roping at the county rodeo last weekend.
Many young cowboys dream of becoming champions in calf roping competitions.
The crowd cheered as the rider completed the calf roping in record time.
