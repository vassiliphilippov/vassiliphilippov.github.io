honest woman

noun
woman who has married her partner after cohabiting
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnəst ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪst ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who has married a man with whom she had previously lived, especially when marriage follows pregnancy; used chiefly in the phrase 'make an honest woman (of her)'.

Examples

make an honest woman
He finally married her after five years and made her an honest woman.
In those days, society expected a man to make an honest woman of his partner.
She joked that after the wedding, she was officially an honest woman.

Related Terms

  • synonym – make an honest woman (of her): to marry a woman with whom one has been living out of wedlock
  • synonym – wife: a married woman
