radiator grille

noun
front grating allowing airflow to a car's radiator
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdiˌeɪtɚ ɡrɪl / (AmE), / ˈreɪdɪeɪtə ɡrɪl / (BrE)
A framework of slats or mesh at the front of a vehicle that allows air to reach the engine's radiator for cooling.

Examples

radiator grille of a car
Debris caught in the radiator grille can block airflow and cause the engine to overheat.
