Battle of Philippi

proper noun
decisive 42 BC battle in Macedonia
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəl əv fɪˈlɪpaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbætəl əv fɪˈlɪpʌɪ / (BrE)
a decisive battle in 42 BC near Philippi in Macedonia, where the forces of Octavian and Mark Antony defeated the armies of Brutus and Cassius
