opera hood

noun
large cloak worn over evening clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːpɚ hʊd / (AmE), / ˈɒpərə hʊd / (BrE)
a large, loose cloak, often with a hood, traditionally worn over evening clothes to attend the opera or other formal occasions
