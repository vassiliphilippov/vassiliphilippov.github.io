glove anesthesia

noun
loss of hand sensation from psychological cause
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌv ænəsˈθiʒə / (AmE), / ɡlʌv ænɪsˈθiːzɪə / (BrE)
a condition in which numbness or loss of sensation affects the hand and wrist in a pattern resembling a glove, typically due to psychological rather than physical causes

Examples

glove anesthesia in the hand
Patients with conversion disorder may exhibit glove anesthesia during episodes of stress.
