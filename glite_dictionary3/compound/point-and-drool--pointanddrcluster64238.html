to point-and-drool

verb
show excessive admiration or desire
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt ənd drul / (AmE), / pɔɪnt ənd druːl / (BrE)
(informal) To express excessive admiration or desire, often in a conspicuous or exaggerated way.
