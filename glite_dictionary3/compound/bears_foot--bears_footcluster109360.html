bear's foot

noun
a hellebore with finger-like leaves and bad odor
Pronunciation: / bɛrz fʊt / (AmE), / beəz fʊt / (BrE)
A hellebore plant with digitate (finger-like) leaves, known for its unpleasant odor and irritant properties if ingested.
