liver spot

noun
brown patch on aging skin
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvər spɑt / (AmE), / ˈlɪvə spɒt / (BrE)
A small, brownish patch on the skin, commonly seen in older people, caused by exposure to the sun or aging.

Examples

liver spot on skin
After turning sixty, she noticed several liver spots appearing on her hands.
He was concerned about the liver spots on his face and consulted a dermatologist.
Many people develop liver spots as a natural part of the aging process.
