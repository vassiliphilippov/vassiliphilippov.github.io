trace detector

noun
device for detecting traces of explosives
Pronunciation: / treɪs dɪˈtɛktɚ /; / treɪs dɪˈtɛktə / (AmE), / treɪs dɪˈtɛktə / (BrE)
a device used to detect or measure very small amounts of substances, such as explosives or chemicals, especially for security or environmental purposes
