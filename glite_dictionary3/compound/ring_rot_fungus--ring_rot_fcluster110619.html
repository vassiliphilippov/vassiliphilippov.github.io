ring rot fungus

noun
fungus causing ring rot in produce
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ rɑt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / rɪŋ rɒt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus that causes soft, watery rot in fruits and vegetables, often forming rings of dry rot around the roots of sweet potatoes

Examples

ring rot fungus infects potatoes
Proper storage can help prevent the spread of ring rot fungus in sweet potatoes.
Symptoms of ring rot fungus include a watery decay and rings of dry rot near the roots.
The farmer lost much of his potato crop to ring rot fungus last season.

  • synonym – fungus: a member of a group of eukaryotic organisms such as molds, yeasts, and mushrooms
