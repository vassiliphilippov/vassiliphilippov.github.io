sales incentive

noun
reward for exceeding a sales goal
Pronunciation: / seɪlz ɪnˈsɛntɪv / (AmE), / seɪlz ɪnˈsɛntɪv / (BrE)
a reward or form of remuneration given to a salesperson for surpassing a set sales target

Examples

sales incentive for employees
Many employees worked extra hours to qualify for the quarterly sales incentive.
Thanks to the sales incentive, the team's performance improved significantly.
The company introduced a new sales incentive to motivate its representatives to boost revenue.
