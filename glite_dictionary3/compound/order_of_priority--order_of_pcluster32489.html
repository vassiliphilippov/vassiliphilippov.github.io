order of priority

noun
ranking by importance or urgency
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər əv praɪˈɔrɪɾi / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə əv praɪˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the arrangement of tasks or items according to their relative importance or urgency
