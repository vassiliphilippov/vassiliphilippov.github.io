team teaching

noun
method of teaching by a team of teachers
Pronunciation: / tiːm ˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tiːm ˈtiːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
A method of classroom instruction in which two or more teachers work together to teach a group of students.

Examples

team teaching in schools
Our science department uses team teaching to combine different areas of expertise for each lesson.
Students benefit from team teaching because they receive multiple perspectives on the subject matter.
The school recently implemented team teaching to encourage collaboration between educators.
