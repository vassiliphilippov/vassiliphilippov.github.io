first class

adverb
in the highest standard of travel or accommodation
Pronunciation: / ˌfɜrst ˈklæs / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈklɑːs / (BrE)
In a way that involves the highest standard of travel or accommodation, especially by train, plane, or ship.

Examples

to travel first class
If you travel first class, you'll enjoy extra comfort and better meals.
She always insists on going first class when she takes the train.
We decided to fly first class for our vacation this year.
