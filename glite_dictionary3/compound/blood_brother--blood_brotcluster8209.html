blood brother

noun
man with a sworn brotherly bond
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈbrʌðər / (AmE), / blʌd ˈbrʌðə / (BrE)
a man who is not a biological brother but has sworn, often through a ceremony involving the mixing of blood, to have a bond as strong as that between brothers

Examples

blood brother for life
He regarded his old friend as a blood brother for life.
The two warriors pledged loyalty to each other as blood brothers.
They became blood brothers after performing the ritual in the forest.
