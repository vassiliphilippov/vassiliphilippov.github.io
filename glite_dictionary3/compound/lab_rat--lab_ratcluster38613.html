lab rat

noun
person used for experimentation/testing
Pronunciation: / læb ˌræt / (AmE), / læb ˌræt / (BrE)
A person who is used as the subject of experiments or testing, especially in an environment where they feel manipulated or observed.

Examples

lab rat in experiments
After months of new training programs, employees joked that they were just lab rats for management.
As a volunteer in the clinical trial, she felt a bit like a lab rat.
I’m tired of being treated like a lab rat whenever they want to try a new policy.
