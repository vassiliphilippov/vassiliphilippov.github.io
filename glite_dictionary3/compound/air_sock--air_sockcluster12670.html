air sock

noun
device showing wind direction
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr sɑk / (AmE), / ˈeə sɒk / (BrE)
a truncated cloth cone, usually mounted on a mast, used to show the direction and strength of the wind, especially at airports

Examples

air sock at airport
Airport maintenance staff replaced the torn air sock with a new one.
Pilots relied on the air sock to assess wind direction before taking off.
The air sock at the end of the runway indicated a strong crosswind.
