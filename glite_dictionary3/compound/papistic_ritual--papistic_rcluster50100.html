papistic ritual

noun
ceremony resembling papal or Catholic practice
Pronunciation: / pəˈpɪstɪk ˈrɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / pəˈpɪstɪk ˈrɪtʃuəl / (BrE)
a ceremony or practice resembling those of the Roman Catholic Church, especially as associated with the papacy or regarded as characteristic of it

Examples

elaborate papistic ritual
Her criticism focused on the excessive use of papistic rituals during mass.
Many Protestant reformers objected to what they considered papistic rituals in the church.
The ceremony was condemned by some as containing too many papistic rituals not found in scripture.
