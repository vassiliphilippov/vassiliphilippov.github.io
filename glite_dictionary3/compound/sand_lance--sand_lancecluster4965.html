sand lance

noun
small silvery fish that burrow into sand
Pronunciation: / sænd ˌlæns / (AmE), / sænd ˌlɑːns / (BrE)
a small, slender, silvery fish found in coastal waters, known for swimming in schools and burrowing into sandy beaches
