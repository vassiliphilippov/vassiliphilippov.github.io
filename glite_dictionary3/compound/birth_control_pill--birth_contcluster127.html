birth control pill

noun
oral contraceptive pill
Pronunciation: / bɝθ kənˈtroʊl pɪl / (AmE), / bɜːθ kənˈtrəʊl pɪl / (BrE)
A pill containing hormones, such as estrogen and progestin, taken to prevent pregnancy by inhibiting ovulation.

Examples

take the birth control pill daily
I started taking the birth control pill last year on my doctor's recommendation.
She forgot to take her birth control pill yesterday and was worried about the consequences.
The birth control pill is one of the most effective forms of contraception when used correctly.
