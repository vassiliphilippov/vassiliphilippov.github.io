cinnamon bark

noun
aromatic bark used as a spice
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəmən bɑrk / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən bɑːk / (BrE)
the aromatic bark of certain cinnamon trees, used as a spice in cooking, baking, and sometimes medicine

Traditional Chinese medicine sometimes includes cinnamon bark as a warming ingredient in herbal remedies.
