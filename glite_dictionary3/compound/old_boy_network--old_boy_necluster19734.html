old boy network

noun
informal professional support network based on social ties
Pronunciation: / oʊld bɔɪ ˈnɛt.wɝk /; / oʊld bɔɪ ˈnɛtwɝk / (AmE), / əʊld bɔɪ ˈnɛt.wɜːk /; / əʊld bɔɪ ˈnɛtwɜːk / (BrE)
an informal system where men with similar backgrounds, especially from elite schools or social groups, use their connections to help each other professionally, often excluding outsiders
