Canal Zone

proper noun
historical Panama Canal territory
Pronunciation: / kəˈnæl zoʊn / (AmE), / kəˈnal zəʊn / (BrE)
the area of land across the Isthmus of Panama that included the Panama Canal and surrounding territory, formerly administered by the United States

Examples

the Canal Zone in Panama
Many workers migrated to the Canal Zone during the construction of the Panama Canal.
She collected stamps from the Canal Zone, which are now considered valuable by philatelists.
The United States once governed the Canal Zone before returning control to Panama.
