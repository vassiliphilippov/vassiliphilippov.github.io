consumer credit

noun
credit for personal or household use
Pronunciation: / kənˈsuːmɚ ˈkrɛdɪt / (AmE), / kənˈsjuːmə ˈkrɛdɪt / (BrE)
A line of credit or lending extended to individuals for personal, family, or household purposes, rather than for business or commercial use.

Examples

consumer credit application
Rising interest rates have made consumer credit more expensive for many families.
Statistics show that consumer credit card debt is increasing nationwide.
The bank approved his application for consumer credit to purchase new furniture.
