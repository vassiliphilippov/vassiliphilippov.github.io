heliothis moth

noun
medium-sized moth; larvae are corn earworms
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliˈoʊθɪs mɑθ / (AmE), / ˌhiːliˈɒθɪs mɒθ / (BrE)
A medium-sized moth of the genus Heliothis, whose larvae are known as corn earworms and are major agricultural pests.
