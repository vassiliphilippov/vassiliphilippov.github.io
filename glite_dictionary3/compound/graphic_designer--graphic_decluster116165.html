graphic designer

noun
person who creates visual designs
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪk dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪk dɪˈzaɪnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to create visual concepts and designs for printed or digital media, using computer software or by hand

Examples

graphic designer creates logos
Many graphic designers use Adobe Creative Suite to produce their work.
Sarah works as a graphic designer for an advertising agency in New York.
The company hired a graphic designer to refresh its logo and branding materials.
