million instructions per second

noun
unit of computer CPU processing speed
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪljən ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz pər ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈmɪljən ɪnˈstrʌkʃənz pə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
a unit for measuring the processing speed of a computer's CPU, equal to one million instructions executed per second
