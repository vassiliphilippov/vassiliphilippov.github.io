cradle cap

noun
infant scalp skin condition
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪdəl kæp / (AmE), / ˈkreɪdəl kap / (BrE)
a common skin condition in infants causing yellowish, greasy, scaly patches on the scalp

Examples

cradle cap on baby's scalp
Gently brushing your baby's hair can help remove the flakes caused by cradle cap.
Most infants experience cradle cap within the first few months of life.
The doctor explained that cradle cap is common and usually clears up on its own.
