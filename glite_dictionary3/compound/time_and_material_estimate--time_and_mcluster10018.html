time and material estimate

noun
cost estimate based on time and materials
Pronunciation: / taɪm ənd məˈtɪriəl ˈɛstɪmət / (AmE), / taɪm ənd məˈtɪəriəl ˈɛstɪmət / (BrE)
An estimate of costs for a project or job based on the actual time spent and the materials used, rather than a fixed total price.

Examples

time and material estimate for a project
Before starting the renovations, the client requested a time and material estimate to better understand possible costs.
Many software projects use a time and material estimate to allow for flexibility in the scope of work.
The contractor gave us a time and material estimate instead of a fixed-price quote.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fixed-price contract: a contract with a set total price for the entire job
