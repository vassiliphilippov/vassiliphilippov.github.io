heavy hydrogen

noun
isotope of hydrogen with one neutron (deuterium)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi ˈhaɪdrədʒən / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi ˈhaɪdrədʒən / (BrE)
an isotope of hydrogen, also called deuterium, which contains one neutron in its nucleus instead of none.
