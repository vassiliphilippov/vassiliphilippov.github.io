trunk line

noun
main railway or transport route
Pronunciation: / trʌŋk laɪn / (AmE), / trʌŋk laɪn / (BrE)
a principal route or main railway line connecting important destinations; the central or main line in a transport system

Examples

main trunk line railway
An accident on the trunk line disrupted rail services across the country.
Plans are underway to upgrade the old trunk line to accommodate faster trains.
The city's main trunk line carries thousands of passengers every day.
