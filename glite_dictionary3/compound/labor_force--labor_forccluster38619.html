labor force

noun
people available for work
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bɚ fɔrs / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə fɔːs / (BrE)
all the people in a country or area who are able and available to work, including both the employed and those seeking employment.

Examples

the labor force grows
Technological change is reshaping the global labor force.
The country's labor force is expected to grow by 2% next year.
Women now make up a significant portion of the labor force.

Related Terms

  • synonym – workforce: all the people who work for a particular company or organization
