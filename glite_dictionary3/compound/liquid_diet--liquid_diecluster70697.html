liquid diet

noun
diet consisting mainly of liquids
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈdaɪət / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈdaɪət / (BrE)
a diet consisting mainly of liquids, such as soups, juices, and broths, sometimes including custards or puddings, often prescribed after surgery or for medical reasons

Examples

liquid diet after surgery
After her surgery, the doctor recommended a liquid diet to aid her recovery.
Children with severe tonsillitis are sometimes placed on a liquid diet until they can swallow more easily.
He found it challenging to get enough calories while following a liquid diet.
