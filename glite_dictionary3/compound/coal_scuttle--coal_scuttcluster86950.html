coal scuttle

noun
container for storing and pouring coal
Pronunciation: / koʊl ˈskʌt̬l̩ / (AmE), / kəʊl ˈskʌt.l̩ / (BrE)
a metal container with a sloping lip, used for holding and pouring coal onto a fire

Examples

coal scuttle by fireplace
He carefully poured coal from the coal scuttle onto the glowing embers.
She filled the coal scuttle before lighting the fire in the hearth.
