universal donor

noun
person with blood type O negative, suitable for all recipients
Pronunciation: / juˈnɪvərsəl ˈdoʊnər / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəsəl ˈdəʊnə / (BrE)
a person with type O Rh-negative blood, whose blood can be transfused to people of all other blood types

Examples

universal donor blood type
Because he is a universal donor, his blood can be given to anyone in need of a transfusion.
Hospitals rely on universal donors during emergencies when there is no time for blood typing.
She discovered she was a universal donor after taking a routine blood test.

Related Terms

  • antonym – universal recipient: a person with blood type AB Rh-positive, who can receive blood from all types
