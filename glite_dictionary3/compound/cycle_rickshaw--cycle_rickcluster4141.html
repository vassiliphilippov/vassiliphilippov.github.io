cycle rickshaw

noun
pedal-powered passenger tricycle for hire
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkəl ˈrɪkˌʃɔ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkəl ˈrɪkʃɔː / (BrE)
a small, three-wheeled vehicle, usually pedalled by a driver, used to carry passengers for hire
