deposit account

noun
bank account for fixed-term or notice withdrawals
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑzɪt əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɒzɪt əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
a bank account in which money is deposited and kept for a fixed term, or from which money can only be withdrawn with notice or by foregoing interest

Examples

deposit account for savings
She opened a deposit account to save money for her holiday next year.
Some deposit accounts offer higher interest rates if the money is left untouched for several months.
Withdrawing funds from a deposit account often requires advance notice to the bank.

Related Terms

  • synonym – savings account: a bank account that earns interest and is used for saving money
