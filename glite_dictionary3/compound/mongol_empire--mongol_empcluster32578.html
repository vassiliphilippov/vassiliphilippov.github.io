Mongol empire

proper noun
historic Asian empire founded by Genghis Khan
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑŋˌɡoʊl ˈɛmˌpaɪər / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋˌɡəʊl ˈɛmpaɪə / (BrE)
the vast empire founded by Genghis Khan in the 13th century, which at its height extended across much of Asia and into Europe
