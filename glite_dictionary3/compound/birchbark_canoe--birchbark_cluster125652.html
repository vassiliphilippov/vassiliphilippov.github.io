birchbark canoe

noun
canoe made with birch bark
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrtʃˌbɑrk kəˈnu / (AmE), / ˈbɜːtʃˌbɑːk kəˈnuː / (BrE)
a lightweight canoe traditionally made by covering a wooden frame with the bark of a birch tree, used especially by Native peoples of North America
