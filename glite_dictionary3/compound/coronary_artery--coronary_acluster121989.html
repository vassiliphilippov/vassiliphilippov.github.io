coronary artery

noun
an artery supplying blood to the heart
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəˌnɛri ˈɑːrˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.əˌnəri ˈɑː.təri / (BrE)
an artery that branches from the aorta and supplies blood to the heart muscle
