personal space

noun
one's immediate surrounding area
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənl speɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl speɪs / (BrE)
the area immediately surrounding an individual, regarded as their own and into which others should not intrude without permission

Examples

invade personal space
Children often need to be taught about the importance of respecting other people's personal space.
He appreciates having his own personal space when working in a crowded office.
She felt uncomfortable when the stranger stood too close and invaded her personal space.
