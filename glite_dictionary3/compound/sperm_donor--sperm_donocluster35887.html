sperm donor

noun
a man who donates sperm for reproduction
Pronunciation: / spɝm ˈdoʊnɚ / (AmE), / spɜːm ˈdəʊnə / (BrE)
a man who donates sperm, typically to a clinic, so that it may be used to help someone else have a baby

Examples

sperm donor at a clinic
After years of trying, they decided to use a sperm donor to start a family.
Some people prefer to choose a sperm donor based on detailed medical history and background.
The clinic carefully screens every sperm donor before accepting a donation.
