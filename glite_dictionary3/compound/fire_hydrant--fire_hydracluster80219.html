fire hydrant

noun
street water pipe for firefighters
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪ.ɚ ˌhaɪ.drənt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.ə ˌhaɪ.drənt / (BrE)
A large upright pipe in the street with a nozzle for attaching a hose, providing water, especially for putting out fires.

Examples

park by a fire hydrant
It's illegal to park your car in front of a fire hydrant.
The city inspects each fire hydrant regularly to ensure proper function.
