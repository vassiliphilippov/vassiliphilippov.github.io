ab initio

adverb
from the beginning
Pronunciation: / æb ɪˈnɪʃioʊ / (AmE), / æb ɪˈnɪʃɪəʊ / (BrE)
from the beginning; starting from the origin or outset.

Examples

ab initio calculations
