elephant yam

noun
a tropical plant with edible tuber
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlɪfənt jæm / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪfənt jam / (BrE)
a large, putrid-smelling aroid plant native to southeastern Asia, especially the Philippines, cultivated for its edible tuber
