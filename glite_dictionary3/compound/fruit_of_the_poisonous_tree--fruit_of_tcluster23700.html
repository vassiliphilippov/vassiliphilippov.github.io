fruit of the poisonous tree

noun
legal rule excluding evidence derived from illegality
Pronunciation: / fruːt əv ðə ˈpɔɪzənəs triː / (AmE), / fruːt əv ðə ˈpɔɪzənəs triː / (BrE)
a legal rule that excludes evidence in court if it was obtained illegally or comes from illegal actions
