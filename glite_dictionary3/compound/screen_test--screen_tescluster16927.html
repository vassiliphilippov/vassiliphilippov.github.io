screen test

noun
filmed audition of an actor or actress
Pronunciation: / skrin tɛst / (AmE), / skriːn tɛst / (BrE)
a filmed audition to assess an actor's suitability for a role in film or television

Examples

screen test for actors
He nailed his screen test and was cast as the lead in the movie.
Many famous actors got their first big break after a successful screen test.
The director asked her to come in for a screen test before making a final decision.
