sharp-tasting

adjective
having a strong, bitter, or acidic flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrpˌteɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpˌteɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a strong, pungent, or somewhat bitter flavor that is noticeable and often slightly sour or acidic.

sharp-tasting cheese
He prefers sharp-tasting cheeses like blue cheese or aged cheddar.

The salad was dressed with a sharp-tasting vinaigrette that woke up my taste buds.
