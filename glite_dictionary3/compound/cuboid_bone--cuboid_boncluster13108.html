cuboid bone

noun
bone on the outer side of the tarsus
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɔɪd ˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɔɪd ˌbəʊn / (BrE)
a bone in the foot, located on the outer side of the tarsus, having a roughly cube-like shape

Examples

cuboid bone fracture
Fracture of the cuboid bone can significantly affect a person's ability to walk.
On the X-ray, the doctor noted a small crack in the cuboid bone.
The cuboid bone plays a crucial role in stabilizing the lateral side of the foot.
