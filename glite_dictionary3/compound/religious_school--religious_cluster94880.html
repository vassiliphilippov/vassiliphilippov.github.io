religious school

noun
school operated by a religious body
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs skuːl / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs skuːl / (BrE)
A school that is operated by a religious organization and often incorporates religious instruction into its curriculum.
