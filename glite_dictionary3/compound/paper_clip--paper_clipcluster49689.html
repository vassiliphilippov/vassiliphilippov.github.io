paper clip

noun
wire or plastic clip for holding papers
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpər klɪp /; / ˈpeɪpɚ klɪp / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə klɪp / (BrE)
a small piece of bent wire or plastic used to hold sheets of paper together
