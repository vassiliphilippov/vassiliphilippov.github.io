bull's eye

noun
center spot of a target
Pronunciation: / bʊlz aɪ / (AmE), / bʊlz aɪ / (BrE)
the small central spot of a target, typically the highest scoring area in archery, darts, and similar activities

Examples

hit the bull's eye in darts
Getting the answer exactly right is like hitting a bull's eye.
He hit the bull's eye with his first arrow.
The dart landed in the bull's eye, earning her the most points.
