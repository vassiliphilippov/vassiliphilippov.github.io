vegan cuisine

noun
animal-product-free style of food
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɡən kwɪˈzin / (AmE), / ˈviːɡən kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
food that is prepared and cooked without any animal products, including meat, dairy, eggs, or honey.
