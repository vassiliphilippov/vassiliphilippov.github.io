price increase

noun
an upward change in price
Pronunciation: / praɪs ɪnˌkriːs / (AmE), / praɪs ˈɪŋkriːs / (BrE)
an occasion when the cost of something becomes higher than before

Examples

a price increase on tickets
Many customers were upset by the recent price increase in train tickets.
The company announced a price increase for its products next month.
The restaurant justified the price increase by citing higher ingredient costs.

Related Terms

  • antonym – price cut: a reduction in the price of something
  • synonym – price hike: a sudden or significant increase in price
