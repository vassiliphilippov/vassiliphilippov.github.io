Erse language

noun
historic term for Gaelic languages of Ireland and Scotland
Pronunciation: / ɜːrs ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɜːs ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Goidelic Celtic language group spoken in Ireland (Irish Gaelic) and Scotland (Scottish Gaelic), especially referred to historically as 'Erse'
