line printer

noun
high-speed computer printer printing lines at once
Pronunciation: / laɪn ˈprɪn.tɚ / (AmE), / laɪn ˈprɪn.tə / (BrE)
A high-speed printer used as a computer output device, which prints an entire line of characters at once.

Examples

line printer prints reports
Because the line printer prints a whole line at a time, it was much faster than earlier character printers.
Engineers received continuous output from the line printer during overnight batch runs.
The old computer lab was equipped with a line printer that could quickly print entire documents.
