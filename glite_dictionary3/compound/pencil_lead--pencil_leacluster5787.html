pencil lead

noun
core material of a pencil
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnsl lɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsl lɛd / (BrE)
the mixture of graphite and clay that forms the marking core of a pencil, available in different degrees of hardness
