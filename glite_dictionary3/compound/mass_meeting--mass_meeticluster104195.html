mass meeting

noun
large organized public gathering
Pronunciation: / mæs ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / mæs ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a large public gathering, often organized to inspire enthusiasm or rally support for a cause or purpose

Examples

hold a mass meeting
A mass meeting was held to protest the new government policy.
Hundreds gathered for the mass meeting in support of the workers' strike.
The organizers invited everyone to attend the mass meeting at the town hall.
