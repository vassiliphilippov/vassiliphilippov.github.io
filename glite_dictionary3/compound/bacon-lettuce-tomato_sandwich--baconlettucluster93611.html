bacon-lettuce-tomato sandwich

noun
sandwich with bacon, lettuce, and tomato
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.kən ˈlɛ.tɪs təˈmeɪ.toʊ ˈsæn.wɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.kən ˈlɛ.tɪs təˈmɑː.təʊ ˈsæn.wɪdʒ / (BrE)
A sandwich made with slices of bacon, fresh lettuce, and tomato, usually served between slices of toasted bread and often with mayonnaise.
