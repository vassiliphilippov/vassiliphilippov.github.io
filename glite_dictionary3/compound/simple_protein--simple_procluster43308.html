simple protein

noun
protein yielding only amino acids on hydrolysis
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəl proʊˈtiːn / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl prəʊˈtiːn / (BrE)
a protein that yields only amino acids when hydrolyzed, containing no non-protein groups
