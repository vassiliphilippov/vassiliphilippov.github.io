sudden cramp

noun
an unexpected painful muscle spasm
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌd(ə)n kræmp / (AmE), / ˈsʌdn̩ kræmp / (BrE)
a sharp, unexpected muscle spasm that causes pain

Examples

sudden cramp in leg
He woke up in the middle of the night with a sudden cramp in his calf.
Many swimmers are advised to stretch beforehand to avoid a sudden cramp in the water.
She grabbed her leg in pain as a sudden cramp stopped her during the race.
