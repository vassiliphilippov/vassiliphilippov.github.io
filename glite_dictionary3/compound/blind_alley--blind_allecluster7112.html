blind alley

noun
a street closed at one end; dead end
Pronunciation: / blaɪnd ˈæli / (AmE), / blaɪnd ˈæli / (BrE)
a street or passage closed at one end, having only one way in or out; a dead end

Examples

quiet blind alley
Living on a blind alley means you don’t get much through traffic.
The children played at the end of the blind alley with no fear of traffic.
