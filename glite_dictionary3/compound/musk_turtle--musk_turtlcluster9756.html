musk turtle

noun
small North American freshwater turtle with a musky odor
Pronunciation: / mʌsk ˈtɝːtəl / (AmE), / mʌsk ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
a small freshwater turtle, native to North America, noted for producing a strong musky odor as a defense mechanism
