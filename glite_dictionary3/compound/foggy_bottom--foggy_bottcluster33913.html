Foggy Bottom

proper noun
nickname for the U.S. State Department
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑɡi ˈbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡi ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
A nickname for the United States Department of State, originating from the name of the area in Washington, D.C. where the department is located.
