gunky residue

noun
sticky or unpleasant leftover material
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌŋki rɛˈzɪdju / (AmE), / ˈɡʌŋki rɪˈzɪdjuː / (BrE)
a sticky or unpleasant substance left behind after something has been removed or has come to an end

Examples

gunky residue on glass
Old stickers often leave gunky residue on glass surfaces.

The blender was covered in a layer of gunky residue after making the smoothie.
