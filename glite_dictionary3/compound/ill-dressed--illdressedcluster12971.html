ill-dressed

adjective
wearing untidy or unfashionable clothes
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ɪlˈdrɛst / (BrE)
Wearing clothes that are untidy, inappropriate, or not fashionable.

Examples

ill-dressed man
Despite being ill-dressed, he spoke with confidence and charm.
She felt self-conscious at the party because she was ill-dressed compared to everyone else.
The ill-dressed man stood out among the elegantly attired guests.

  • antonym – well-dressed: wearing smart or fashionable clothes
