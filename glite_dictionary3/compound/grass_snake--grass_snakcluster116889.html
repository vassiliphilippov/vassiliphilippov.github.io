grass snake

noun
North American green insect-eating snake
Pronunciation: / ɡræs sneɪk / (AmE), / ɡrɑːs sneɪk / (BrE)
a small, harmless, non-venomous snake found in North or Central America, usually green or striped, living in grassy areas
