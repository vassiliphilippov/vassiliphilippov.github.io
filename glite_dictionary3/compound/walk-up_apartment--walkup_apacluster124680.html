walk-up apartment

noun
apartment in a building without an elevator
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkˌʌp əˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkˌʌp əˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
an apartment located in a building without an elevator, requiring residents to use stairs to reach their unit

Examples

old walk-up apartment
Many older buildings in the city are walk-up apartments without any elevator access.
She avoided renting a walk-up apartment because she didn't want to climb stairs every day.
We live in a walk-up apartment on the third floor, so moving in took some effort.
