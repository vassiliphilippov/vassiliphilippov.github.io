bow window

noun
curved window projecting from a wall
Pronunciation: / boʊ ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a window that projects outward from the exterior wall of a building, forming a curved shape

Examples

large bow window
The bow window in her kitchen offers a lovely view of the garden.
They added a bow window to the master bedroom to create extra seating space.
We replaced the old living room window with a beautiful bow window that lets in more light.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bay window: a window that projects outward from an outside wall but is typically angular, not curved
