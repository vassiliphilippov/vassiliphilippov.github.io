tinea pedis

noun
fungal infection of the feet
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪniə ˈpɛdɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɪniə ˈpiːdɪs / (BrE)
A fungal infection of the skin on the feet, especially between the toes; also known as athlete's foot.

Examples

tinea pedis infection
Many athletes are particularly susceptible to tinea pedis due to frequent use of communal showers.
Treatment for tinea pedis usually involves antifungal creams or sprays.
Wearing damp socks for extended periods increases the risk of developing tinea pedis.
