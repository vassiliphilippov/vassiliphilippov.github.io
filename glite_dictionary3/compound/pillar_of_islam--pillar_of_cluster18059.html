pillar of Islam

noun
one of five basic duties in Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlɚ əv ɪsˈlɑm / (AmE), / ˈpɪlə ɒv ɪsˈlɑːm / (BrE)
any of the five fundamental religious duties required of every Muslim: faith, prayer, almsgiving, fasting, and pilgrimage to Mecca.

Charity or zakat is recognized as one pillar of Islam that supports the community.
