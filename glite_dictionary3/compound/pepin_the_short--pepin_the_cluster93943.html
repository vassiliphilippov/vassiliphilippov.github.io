Pepin the Short

proper noun
king of the Franks, father of Charlemagne
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpɪn ðə ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ˈpɛpɪn ðə ʃɔːt / (BrE)
(c. 714–768) King of the Franks from 751 to 768; father of Charlemagne and founder of the Carolingian dynasty, known for defending papal interests.

Pepin the Short crowned king
