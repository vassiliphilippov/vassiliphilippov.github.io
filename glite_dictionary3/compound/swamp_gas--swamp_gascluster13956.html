swamp gas

noun
methane-rich gas from wetlands
Pronunciation: / swɑmp ˌɡæs / (AmE), / swɒmp ˌɡæs / (BrE)
a gas, chiefly methane, released from decaying organic matter in wetlands or marshy areas

Examples
