wire wool

noun
fine steel fibers used as an abrasive
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚ wʊl / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə wʊl / (BrE)
A mass of fine steel fibers used as an abrasive for cleaning, polishing, or smoothing surfaces.
