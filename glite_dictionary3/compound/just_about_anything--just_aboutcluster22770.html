just about anything

pronoun
almost everything; nearly any possibility
Pronunciation: / dʒʌst əˈbaʊt ˈɛniˌθɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒʌst əˈbaʊt ˈeniˌθɪŋ / (BrE)
almost everything; any item, situation, or possibility one can think of, with very few exceptions.

Examples

just about anything you want
Their collection includes just about anything you could imagine.
With so many skills, she can fix just about anything around the house.
You can wear just about anything to this casual party.
