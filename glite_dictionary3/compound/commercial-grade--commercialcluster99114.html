commercial-grade

adjective
of quality suitable for business use
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɝːʃəl ɡreɪd / (AmE), / kəˈmɜːʃəl ɡreɪd / (BrE)
Of a quality suitable for use in commerce, business, or industry, but not usually of the highest or specialized standard.

Examples

commercial-grade equipment
She bought a commercial-grade vacuum cleaner for her cleaning business.
The company installed commercial-grade kitchen appliances in their office cafeteria.
This paint is commercial-grade, making it ideal for large-scale projects but not for home use.

Related Terms

  • antonym – consumer-grade: of a quality suitable for individual consumer use
  • synonym – industrial-grade: of a quality suitable for industrial use
