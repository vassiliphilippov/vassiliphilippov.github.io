insulin shock

noun
coma or convulsions caused by excess insulin
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.sə.lɪn ʃɑk / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sjʊ.lɪn ʃɒk / (BrE)
a severe condition or treatment resulting from excessive insulin administration, causing convulsions and coma
