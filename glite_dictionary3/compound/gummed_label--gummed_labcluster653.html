gummed label

noun
an adhesive label with a gummed backing
Pronunciation: / ɡʌmd ˈleɪbəl / (AmE), / ɡʌmd ˈleɪbəl / (BrE)
an adhesive label that has a surface coated with gum or glue, activated by moisture or pressure for sticking to surfaces

Examples

gummed label on envelope
Each envelope had a gummed label with the recipient's address attached to it.
He purchased a box of gummed labels for organizing his files at work.
Please write your name on a gummed label and stick it to your folder.
