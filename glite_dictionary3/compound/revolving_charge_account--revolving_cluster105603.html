revolving charge account

noun
credit account with unpaid balance allowed
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑlvɪŋ tʃɑrdʒ əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒlvɪŋ tʃɑːdʒ əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
A credit account that allows the holder to carry a balance from month to month with no requirement to pay the full amount each billing cycle.

Examples

revolving charge account at a store
He opened a revolving charge account at the department store to make monthly purchases.
If you keep a balance on your revolving charge account, you'll accrue interest until it's paid off.
The interest rate on a revolving charge account can be much higher than on other types of loans.

Related Terms

  • synonym – credit card: a payment card allowing the holder to make purchases on credit
