big pharma

noun
the large pharmaceutical industry
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈfɑːrmə / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
(often disapproving) The large pharmaceutical companies or the pharmaceutical industry collectively, especially regarded as pursuing profit and influencing policy, sometimes at the expense of public health.
