mystery meat navigation

noun
unclear or confusing navigation instructions
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstəri miːt ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪstəri miːt ˌnavɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
navigation instructions or interface elements that are unclear, confusing, or do not visibly indicate their purpose, making it difficult for users to understand where to go or what actions to take

Examples

mystery meat navigation confuses users
Designers are advised to avoid mystery meat navigation in user interfaces for clarity.
If your app relies on mystery meat navigation, people might become frustrated and leave.
Many early websites suffered from mystery meat navigation, forcing users to guess what icons would do.
