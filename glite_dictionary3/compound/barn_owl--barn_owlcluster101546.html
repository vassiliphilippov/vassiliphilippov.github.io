barn owl

noun
a medium-sized, pale owl that often lives in barns
Pronunciation: / bɑrn aʊl / (AmE), / bɑːn aʊl / (BrE)
a medium-sized owl with mottled buff and white plumage, often found in barns and other buildings, known for its role in controlling rodent populations

Examples

barn owl hunts mice
A barn owl swooped silently across the field at dusk.
Farmers appreciate the barn owl for keeping down the mouse population in their barns.
We spotted a barn owl nesting in the old church tower.
