free form

noun
a morpheme that can occur alone
Pronunciation: / ˈfri ˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈfriː ˌfɔːm / (BrE)
(Linguistics) A morpheme that can stand alone as a word.

Examples

free form as a word
In linguistics, a free form is distinct from a bound morpheme.
Most English nouns and verbs are free forms, unlike prefixes or suffixes.
The word 'book' is a free form because it can stand on its own.
