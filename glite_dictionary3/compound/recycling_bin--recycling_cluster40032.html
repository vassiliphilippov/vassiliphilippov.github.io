recycling bin

noun
container for recyclables
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsaɪklɪŋ bɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈsaɪklɪŋ bɪn / (BrE)
A container used for collecting items that are to be recycled, such as paper, plastic, or glass.

Examples

recycling bin for bottles
I forgot to take out the recycling bin before the collection truck arrived.
Please put your empty bottles in the recycling bin instead of the trash.
The city has placed a recycling bin on every street corner to encourage waste sorting.
