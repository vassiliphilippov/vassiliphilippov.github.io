upper deck

noun
top or higher deck
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɚ dɛk / (AmE), / ˈʌpə dɛk / (BrE)
the higher or topmost deck on a ship, vehicle, or structure, especially when there are two or more decks

Examples

the upper deck of a ship
Passengers can enjoy fresh air on the upper deck of the ferry.
The stadium's upper deck was packed with enthusiastic fans.
We sat on the upper deck to get a better view of the parade.
