Madagascar jasmine

noun
floral perfume ingredient/extract
Pronunciation: / ˌmædəˈgæskər ˈdʒæsəmɪn / (AmE), / ˌmædəˈgæskə ˈdʒæsəmɪn / (BrE)
(in perfumery) the floral scent, extract, or ingredient obtained from the Madagascar jasmine plant, used as a perfume note or raw material in fragrances.

Examples

Madagascar jasmine perfume scent
The perfumer added a hint of Madagascar jasmine to the base to enhance the floral heart.
The new fragrance features Madagascar jasmine as a prominent note among citrus and sandalwood accords.
Ingredient lists often label the extract simply as Madagascar jasmine when sourced from stephanotis plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jasmine (scent): a common floral perfume note with a rich, sweet aroma
  • synonym – perfume note: a distinct scent component perceived within a fragrance
