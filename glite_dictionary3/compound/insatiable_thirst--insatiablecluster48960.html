insatiable thirst

noun
an unending, intense desire or craving
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈseɪʃəbəl ˈθɝst / (AmE), / ɪnˈseɪʃəbl ˈθɜːst / (BrE)
an intense and unending desire for something, often used metaphorically to mean a strong craving that cannot be satisfied

Examples

insatiable thirst for success
After her promotion, she developed an insatiable thirst for success and recognition.
Despite achieving so much, she felt an insatiable thirst for knowledge that could never be fully satisfied.
His insatiable thirst for adventure led him to travel to remote corners of the world.
