token payment

noun
a symbolic or minimal payment
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊkən ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊkən ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
a very small amount of money paid, usually as a symbolic gesture, in recognition of an obligation or as part of a formal arrangement

Examples

token payment of one dollar
As a token payment, she paid one dollar to secure the contract until she could pay the rest.
He offered a token payment to settle the overdue bill, acknowledging he couldn't pay the full amount.
The artist received only a token payment for her work on the project.
