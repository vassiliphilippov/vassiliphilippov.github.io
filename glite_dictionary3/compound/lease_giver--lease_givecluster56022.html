lease giver

noun
person who grants a lease
Pronunciation: / lis ˈɡɪvər / (AmE), / liːs ˈɡɪvə / (BrE)
a person or entity who grants a lease of property to another

Examples

lease giver signs contract
If the lease giver fails to make repairs, the tenant may seek legal remedies.
The lease giver is responsible for ensuring the property meets safety standards before renting it out.
The agreement clearly specifies the rights and obligations of both the lease giver and the lessee.
