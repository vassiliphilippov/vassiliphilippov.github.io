child welfare agency

noun
agency responsible for children's welfare
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪld ˈwɛlfɛr ˈeɪdʒənsi / (AmE), / tʃaɪld ˈwɛlfeə ˈeɪdʒənsi / (BrE)
an organization or government unit responsible for protecting children's well-being and providing support services for their development and vocational training

Examples

child welfare agency investigates abuse
After being contacted by the school, the child welfare agency provided counseling services to the family.
The child welfare agency intervened to ensure the children's safety after reports of neglect.
The director of the child welfare agency spoke about the importance of protecting vulnerable youth.
