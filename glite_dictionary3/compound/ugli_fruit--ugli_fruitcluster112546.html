ugli fruit

noun
hybrid citrus fruit (tangerine × grapefruit)
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡli frut / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli fruːt / (BrE)
a large, sweet citrus fruit with thick, wrinkled skin, created by crossing a tangerine (or mandarin) and a grapefruit
