fertilization membrane

noun
membrane formed after an egg is fertilized
Pronunciation: / ˌfɝːt̬əlaɪˈzeɪʃən mɛmˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˌfɜːtɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən mɛmˌbreɪn / (BrE)
a membrane that forms around a fertilized ovum, preventing entry of additional sperm
