English bulldog

noun
breed of muscular, short-haired dog
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈbʊlˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈbʊl.dɒɡ / (BrE)
a sturdy, muscular dog breed with a short coat, large head, and pronounced lower jaw, originally developed in England for bull baiting
