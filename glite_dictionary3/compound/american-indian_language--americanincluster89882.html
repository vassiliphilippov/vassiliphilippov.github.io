American-Indian language

noun
a language spoken by Native Americans
Pronunciation: / əˌmɛrɪkən ˈɪndiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˌmɛrɪkən ˈɪndiən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the languages spoken by the indigenous peoples of North, Central, and South America before European colonization.
