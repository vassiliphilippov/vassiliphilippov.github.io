gorgonian coral

noun
coral with branching skeleton
Pronunciation: / ɡɔrˈɡoʊniən ˈkɔrəl / (AmE), / ɡɔːˈɡəʊniən ˈkɒrəl / (BrE)
A type of coral with a horny or calcareous branching skeleton, commonly found in warm seas and known for its tree-like forms.
