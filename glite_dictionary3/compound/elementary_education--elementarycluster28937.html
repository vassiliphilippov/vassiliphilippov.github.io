elementary education

noun
basic education for young children
Pronunciation: / ˌɛləˈmɛn.tə.ri ˌɛdʒ.əˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛl.ɪˈmen.tər.i ˌed.jʊˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
