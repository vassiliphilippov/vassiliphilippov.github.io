feeding bottle

noun
bottle for feeding babies
Pronunciation: / ˈfidɪŋ ˌbɑtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfiːdɪŋ ˌbɒtl̩ / (BrE)
A bottle fitted with a rubber or silicone teat, used for feeding milk or formula to infants and very young children.
