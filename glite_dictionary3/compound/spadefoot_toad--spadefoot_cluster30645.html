spadefoot toad

noun
burrowing toad with spade-like hind feet
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪd.fʊt ˌtoʊd / (AmE), / ˈspeɪd.fʊt ˌtəʊd / (BrE)
A burrowing toad found in the Northern Hemisphere, characterized by a horny, spade-like projection on each hind foot used for digging.

Examples
