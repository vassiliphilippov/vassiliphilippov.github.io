amplitude modulation

noun
modulation by varying a carrier's amplitude
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.plɪˌtuːd ˌmɑː.dʒəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈæm.plɪˌtjuːd ˌmɒd.jʊˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a method of transmitting signals by varying the amplitude of a carrier wave, especially in radio broadcasting.

Examples
