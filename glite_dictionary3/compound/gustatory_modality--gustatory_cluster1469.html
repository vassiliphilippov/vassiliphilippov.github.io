gustatory modality

noun
the ability to taste; sense of taste
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌstəˌtɔri məˈdæləti / (AmE), / ˈɡʌstətəri məˈdælɪti / (BrE)
the faculty or ability to perceive and distinguish tastes such as sweet, sour, bitter, and salty in the mouth
