self-report personality inventory

noun
personality test based on self-reporting traits or choices
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf rɪˈpɔrt pɚˌsænəˈlɪti ɪnˌvɛntɔri / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf rɪˈpɔːt pəˌsænəˈlɪti ɪnˌvɛntəri / (BrE)
a psychological assessment in which individuals answer questions about themselves to reveal personality traits, often by selecting descriptors or indicating likely behaviors
