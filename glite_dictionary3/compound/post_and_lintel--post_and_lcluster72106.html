post and lintel

noun
structure with vertical posts and a horizontal lintel
Pronunciation: / poʊst ənd ˈlɪntəl / (AmE), / pəʊst ənd ˈlɪntl̩ / (BrE)
A structure consisting of vertical supports (posts) holding up a horizontal member (lintel), commonly used in architecture and construction.
