button pusher

noun
person doing repetitive, unskilled tasks
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬n ˌpʊʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtn ˌpʊʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job involves performing simple, repetitive tasks that require little skill or independent thought

Examples

factory button pusher
He resented being treated like a mere button pusher rather than a valued team member.
In many large companies, entry-level workers may start as button pushers before moving on to more complex roles.
The manager wanted creative thinkers, not just another button pusher in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym – automaton: a person who acts in a mechanical or unemotional way
  • synonym – drone: a person who does tedious or menial work
  • antonym – specialist: a person highly skilled in a specific field, the opposite of a button pusher
