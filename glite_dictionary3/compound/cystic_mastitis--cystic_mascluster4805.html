cystic mastitis

noun
breast condition involving cysts
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstɪk mæˈstaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɪstɪk mæsˈtɪtɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by the presence of one or more cysts in the breast, causing swelling, pain, or tenderness.

Examples

cystic mastitis with pain
The doctor diagnosed her with cystic mastitis after she reported persistent breast pain and swelling.
Treatments for cystic mastitis may include medication to reduce inflammation and manage symptoms.
Ultrasound imaging can help detect cystic mastitis and differentiate it from other breast conditions.
