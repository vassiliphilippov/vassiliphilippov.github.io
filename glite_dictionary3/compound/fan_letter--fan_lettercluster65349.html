fan letter

noun
a letter from a fan to a celebrity
Pronunciation: / fæn ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / fæn ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a letter written by an admirer to a celebrity, performer, or public figure expressing praise or support

Examples

send a fan letter
After her performance, the actress received dozens of fan letters from people who loved her work.
He spent the afternoon replying to fan letters sent by enthusiastic readers of his novels.
When I was a teenager, I wrote a fan letter to my favorite singer and was thrilled to get a response.
