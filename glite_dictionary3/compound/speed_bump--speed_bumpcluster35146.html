speed bump

noun
raised road strip to slow traffic
Pronunciation: / spiːd bʌmp / (AmE), / spiːd bʌmp / (BrE)
A raised band across a road, designed to slow down traffic.

Examples

hit a speed bump
Drivers must slow down when they approach a speed bump in residential areas.
She didn't notice the speed bump and spilled her coffee in the car.
The city installed several speed bumps to reduce accidents near the school.
