sewing stitch

noun
stitch made by sewing needle and thread
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊɪŋ stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ stɪtʃ / (BrE)
A stitch made by passing a threaded needle through fabric or leather when sewing.

Examples

sewing stitch on fabric
A strong sewing stitch ensures that the hem will last through many washes.
She used a neat sewing stitch to repair the tear in her shirt.
The instructor demonstrated a basic sewing stitch to the beginners in class.
