Japanese black pine

noun
a Japanese ornamental pine tree
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæniz blæk paɪn / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz blæk paɪn / (BrE)
a Japanese pine tree (Pinus thunbergii) with long needles in pairs, widely grown as an ornamental and valued for its resistance to salt and pollution
