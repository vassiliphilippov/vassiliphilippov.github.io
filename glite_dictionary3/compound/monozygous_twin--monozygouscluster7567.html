monozygous twin

noun
one of a pair of identical twins
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊ.nəˈzaɪ.ɡəs twɪn / (AmE), / ˌmɒ.nəˈzaɪ.ɡəs twɪn / (BrE)
either of two twins that develop from the same fertilized ovum and share identical genetic material; identical twin.

Examples
