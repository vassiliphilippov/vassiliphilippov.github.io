Japanese leek

noun
Asiatic onion used as green onion
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæniz lik / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz liːk / (BrE)
An Asiatic onion with slender bulbs, commonly used as an early green onion in cooking.
