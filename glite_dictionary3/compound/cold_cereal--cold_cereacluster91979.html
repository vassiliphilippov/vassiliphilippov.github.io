cold cereal

noun
uncooked breakfast cereal
Pronunciation: / koʊld ˈsɪriəl / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈsɪəriəl / (BrE)
A breakfast cereal that is eaten without being heated, typically served with milk and ready to eat out of the box.

Examples

bowl of cold cereal
For a quick breakfast, she poured a bowl of cold cereal and added milk.
Many children prefer cold cereal to oatmeal in the morning.
The hotel breakfast buffet included fruits, pastries, and several types of cold cereal.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hot cereal: a cereal that is cooked before serving, such as oatmeal
