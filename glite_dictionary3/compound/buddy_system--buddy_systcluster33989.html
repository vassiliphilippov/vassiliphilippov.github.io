buddy system

noun
cooperative pairing for safety
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌdi ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈbʌdi ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a method of pairing two or more people together so they can help or look out for each other, especially for safety or support

Examples

use the buddy system for safety
Before going hiking, we implemented the buddy system to ensure no one got lost.
Most schools encourage the buddy system so new students always have someone to rely on.
Using the buddy system at the swimming pool increases everyone's safety.
