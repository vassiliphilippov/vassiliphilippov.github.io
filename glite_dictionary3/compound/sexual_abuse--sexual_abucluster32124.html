sexual abuse

noun
crime of coerced or forced sexual activity
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈbjus / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃuəl əˈbjuːs / (BrE)
The crime of forcing or coercing another person into unwanted sexual activity, often through force, threat, or manipulation.

Examples

sexual abuse of a child

The organization works to prevent sexual abuse through education and awareness programs.
Victims of sexual abuse often require long-term counseling and support.
