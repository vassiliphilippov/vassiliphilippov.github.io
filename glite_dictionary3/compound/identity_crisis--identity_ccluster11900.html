identity crisis

noun
period of self-uncertainty or confusion
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɛntəti ˈkraɪsɪs / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪti ˈkraɪsɪs / (BrE)
A period of uncertainty and confusion in which a person questions their sense of self or their place in society.

Examples

go through an identity crisis
After moving to a new city, she experienced an identity crisis and struggled to fit in.
Many teenagers go through an identity crisis as they search for meaning and direction in life.
The novel explores the identity crisis of a man torn between two cultures.
