public safety

noun
protection of people from danger or crime
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈseɪfti / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈseɪfti / (BrE)
the condition of being protected from crime, danger, and threats to the well-being of the general public
