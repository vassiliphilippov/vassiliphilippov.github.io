mummy bag

noun
tapered sleeping bag style
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌmi bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈmʌmi bæɡ / (BrE)
A sleeping bag designed with a shape that tapers from the head to the foot, fitting closely to minimize heat loss.

Examples

mummy bag for camping
Because it fits snugly, a mummy bag is lighter and more efficient than a rectangular sleeping bag.
For winter camping, a mummy bag provides excellent insulation and warmth.
She zipped herself into a mummy bag before settling down for the night.
