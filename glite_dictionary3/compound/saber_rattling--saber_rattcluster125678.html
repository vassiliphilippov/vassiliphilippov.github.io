saber rattling

noun
threatening display of military power
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪbər ˌræt.əl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪbə ˌræt.əl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the display or threat of military force, typically to intimidate or influence others without actual conflict

Examples
