genus Colchicum

noun
genus of fall-blooming perennial herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs kɒlˈkɪ.kəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs kɒlˈkɪ.kəm / (BrE)
A genus of chiefly fall-blooming perennial herbs with underground corms, sometimes placed in the family Colchicaceae.

Examples

genus Colchicum species
Botanists debate whether the genus Colchicum should be placed in the family Colchicaceae or Liliaceae.
Many species in the genus Colchicum bloom in the autumn, brightening gardens late in the season.
The genus Colchicum is sometimes confused with true crocuses due to its similar flowers.
