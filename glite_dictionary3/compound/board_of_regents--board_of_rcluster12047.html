board of regents

noun
university governing body
Pronunciation: / bɔrd əv ˈriːdʒənts / (AmE), / bɔːd əv ˈriːdʒənts / (BrE)
a governing body of a university or college, responsible for overseeing its policies, administration, and welfare of students and staff

Examples

board of regents meeting
After a lengthy meeting, the board of regents decided to increase funding for student scholarships.
She was appointed to the board of regents due to her longstanding support of higher education.
The board of regents approved the university's new academic policies this year.
