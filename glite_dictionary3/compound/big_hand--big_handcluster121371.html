big hand

noun
enthusiastic applause or praise
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ hænd / (AmE), / bɪɡ hand / (BrE)
enthusiastic applause or praise, especially as a sign of approval for a performance

Examples

a big hand for performers

Let's give the band a big hand for their fantastic performance tonight!
The speaker received a big hand at the end of her inspiring lecture.
