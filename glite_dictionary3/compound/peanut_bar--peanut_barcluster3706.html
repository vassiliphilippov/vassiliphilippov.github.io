peanut bar

noun
a bar-shaped peanut confection
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnʌt bɑr / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt bɑː / (BrE)
A sweet confection made from peanuts held together with taffy or syrup, usually shaped into a bar.
