light cream

noun
pale off-white colour
Pronunciation: / laɪt krim / (AmE), / laɪt kriːm / (BrE)
A very pale off-white or light beige colour used to describe paint, fabrics, cosmetics, and interior finishes.

Examples

light cream paint color
The walls were painted in a soft light cream to brighten the room.
She chose a light cream dress for the interview because it looked professional and fresh.
The graphic designer tested the logo on a light cream background and adjusted the contrast.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cream (colour): a pale yellowish-white colour similar to light cream
  • synonym – off-white: a colour that is almost white but slightly toned with beige or yellow
