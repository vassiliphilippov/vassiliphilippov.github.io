pop fly

noun
short high fly ball in baseball
Pronunciation: / pɑp faɪ / (AmE), / pɒp faɪ / (BrE)
A high fly ball in baseball or softball that does not travel far, usually easy for a fielder to catch.

Examples

catch a pop fly
She called for the pop fly and made the catch before anyone else could react.
The batter hit a pop fly to left field, and the outfielder caught it easily.
With two outs, he lifted a pop fly that ended the inning.
