litter basket

noun
bin for public rubbish disposal
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬ɚ ˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A container, usually in or outside public buildings, provided for people to dispose of rubbish.

Examples

litter basket on street
Please put your empty cup in the litter basket near the entrance.
Someone left a half-eaten sandwich on top of the litter basket instead of throwing it inside.
The city installed new litter baskets along the busy street to reduce rubbish.
