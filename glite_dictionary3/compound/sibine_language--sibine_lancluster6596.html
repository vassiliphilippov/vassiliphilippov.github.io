Sibine language

noun
Chadic language of southern Chad
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːbiːneɪ ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsiːbɪni ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Chadic language spoken in the Tandjile Region of Chad, particularly in the Tandjile Est department and Lai subprefecture.

Examples

Sibine language speakers

The Sibine language belongs to the Chadic branch of the Afroasiatic language family.
