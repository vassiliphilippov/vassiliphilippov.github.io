gill fungus

noun
fungus with gills under its cap
Pronunciation: / ɡɪl ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ɡɪl ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
A basidiomycete fungus that has gills on the underside of its cap for spore dispersal.

Examples

gill fungus mushroom cap
Many common mushrooms belong to the gill fungus group.
The gill fungus is easily identified by the delicate blades radiating from the center of its cap.
The spores of a gill fungus are released from the gills beneath the cap.
