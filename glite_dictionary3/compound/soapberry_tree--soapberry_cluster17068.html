soapberry tree

noun
tree of genus Sapindus with saponin-rich fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊpˌbɛri triː / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpˌbɛri triː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Sapindus, whose fruit contains a high concentration of saponin and is sometimes used as a natural soap.

A mature soapberry tree can provide shade and attract wildlife to the garden.
