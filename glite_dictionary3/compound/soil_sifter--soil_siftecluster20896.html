soil sifter

noun
tool for separating soil from debris
Pronunciation: / sɔɪl ˈsɪftər / (AmE), / sɔɪl ˈsɪftə / (BrE)
a device used in gardening to separate fine soil from stones, debris, or other unwanted materials

Examples

soil sifter for gardening
A soil sifter makes preparing potting mix much easier and ensures that roots can grow freely.
After digging up the old plants, she ran the dirt through a soil sifter to get rid of clumps and debris.
I used a soil sifter to remove rocks from the garden bed before planting.

  • synonym – sieve: a device with a mesh or perforated bottom used for separating coarse from fine parts
