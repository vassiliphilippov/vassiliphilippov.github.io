highly strung

adjective
very tense or easily upset
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪli strʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪli strʌŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) very nervous and easily upset; extremely tense or sensitive.

Examples

a highly strung person
After weeks of stressful deadlines, she became highly strung and irritable.
As a highly strung individual, he found it hard to relax in noisy environments.
Their highly strung behavior made group projects challenging.
