methadone hydrochloride

noun
synthetic narcotic for opioid addiction treatment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛθəˌdoʊn haɪˌdrɑːkləˈraɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɛθədəʊn haɪˌdrɒklaɪd / (BrE)
a synthetic narcotic drug, less habit-forming than morphine, used in the treatment and maintenance therapy of opioid addiction, particularly heroin dependency
