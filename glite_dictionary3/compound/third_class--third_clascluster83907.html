third class

noun
cheapest ship or train accommodations
Pronunciation: / θɝd klæs / (AmE), / θɜːd klɑːs / (BrE)
the cheapest category of accommodations on a ship or train, typically offering basic services and limited comfort

Examples

third class ticket
Many immigrants traveled in third class when coming to America by ship.
She could only afford a third class ticket for the cross-country train journey.
The conditions in third class were crowded but affordable for families.
