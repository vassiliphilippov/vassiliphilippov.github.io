tribute album

noun
album honoring an artist or cause
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪbjut ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / ˈtrɪbjuːt ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
an album featuring various artists performing songs in honor of a particular musician, band, or cause

Examples

tribute album for a famous singer
Several artists contributed tracks to the tribute album celebrating Bob Dylan's influence.
The band released a tribute album to honor the legendary singer's contributions to music.
Their latest project is a tribute album benefiting a charity for children's education.
