Virginia bluebell

noun
North American plant with blue bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / vərˈdʒɪn.jə ˈbluːˌbɛl / (AmE), / vəˈdʒɪn.i.ə ˈbluːˌbɛl / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to eastern North America, with smooth stems, broad leaves, and clusters of blue bell-shaped flowers, pink when in bud.
