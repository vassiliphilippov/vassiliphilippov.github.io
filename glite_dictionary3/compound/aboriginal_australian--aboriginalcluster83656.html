Aboriginal Australian

noun
Indigenous peoples or languages of Australia
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˈrɪdʒənl ɔːˈstreɪliən / (AmE), / ˌabəˈrɪdʒɪn(ə)l ɔːˈstreɪliən / (BrE)
any of the Indigenous peoples of Australia, or the languages spoken by them, especially those traditionally spoken before European colonization

Examples

Aboriginal Australian culture
Efforts are underway to preserve Aboriginal Australian languages that are at risk of disappearing.
He is studying the linguistic structures of several Aboriginal Australian dialects.
Many traditional stories are passed down in various Aboriginal Australian languages across the continent.
