gum albanum

noun
a bitter aromatic gum resin, like asafetida
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm ælˈbeɪnəm / (AmE), / ɡʌm alˈbɑːnəm / (BrE)
a bitter, aromatic gum resin similar to asafetida, used in traditional medicine and sometimes as a flavoring.

The bitter aroma of gum albanum is said to repel certain insects.
