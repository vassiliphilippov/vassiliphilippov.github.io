real estate agent

noun
person who arranges property sales
Pronunciation: / ˈriːəl ɪˌsteɪt ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ɪsˌteɪt ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange the sale, purchase, or rental of properties such as houses, land, or buildings for others

Examples

real estate agent shows houses
After months of searching, our real estate agent finally found the perfect apartment.
The real estate agent showed us several properties in the city center.
We contacted a real estate agent to help us find a new house.
