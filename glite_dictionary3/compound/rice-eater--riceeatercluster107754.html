rice-eater

noun
person who regularly eats rice
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪs ˌitɚ / (AmE), / ˈraɪs ˌiːtə / (BrE)
A person who regularly eats rice; sometimes used humorously or, less commonly, as a pejorative term referring to someone of Asian descent.

Examples

typical rice-eater
As a rice-eater, he appreciates the many varieties found in Asian cuisine.
Some people jokingly call themselves rice-eaters because they eat rice with most meals.
The documentary explored the daily life of a typical rice-eater in rural Thailand.

Related Terms

  • synonym – meat-eater: a person who eats meat
  • antonym – vegetarian: a person who does not eat meat, often eats rice and vegetables
