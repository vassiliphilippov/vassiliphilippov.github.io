criminal offense

noun
an act punishable as a crime
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmɪnəl əˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl əˈfɛns / (BrE)
An act that is forbidden by law and punishable as a crime.

Examples

serious criminal offense
