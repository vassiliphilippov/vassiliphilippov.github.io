plane tree

noun
species of Platanus tree with peeling bark
Pronunciation: / pleɪn triː / (AmE), / pleɪn triː / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree of the genus Platanus, with thin, pale, peeling bark, lobed leaves, and spherical fruit clusters
