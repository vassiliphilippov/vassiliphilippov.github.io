sash cord

noun
cord used in sash windows
Pronunciation: / sæʃ kɔrd / (AmE), / sæʃ kɔːd / (BrE)
a strong cord that connects the sash weight to a sliding sash in a double-hung window, allowing the sash to be raised and lowered

Examples

sash cord in a window
He replaced the worn-out sash cord with a new, sturdy rope.
The old sash cord snapped, making it difficult to open the window.
When the sash cord broke, the sash fell abruptly down.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sash weight: a weight used to balance a window sash
  • synonym – window sash: the movable part of a window that holds the glass
