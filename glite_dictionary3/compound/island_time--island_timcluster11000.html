island time

noun
relaxed, unhurried pace of life
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.lənd taɪm / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.lənd taɪm / (BrE)
A relaxed and unhurried attitude toward time, typically associated with life on islands, where schedules are flexible and the pace of life is slow.

Examples

on island time vacation
After a week on the coast, we found ourselves slipping into island time and letting go of our schedules.
Locals told us not to worry about tardiness—everyone here lives by island time.
Visitors are often surprised at how shops open and close on island time, with little regard for the clock.
