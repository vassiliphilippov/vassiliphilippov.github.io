bubble brain

noun
state of confusion or distraction
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbəl breɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌbəl breɪn / (BrE)
A state of mental distraction or confusion, often making it difficult to think clearly.

Examples

total bubble brain
After five hours of meetings, I became a complete bubble brain and couldn't answer simple questions.
During the stressful week, he admitted feeling like a bubble brain and struggled to concentrate.
She called herself a bubble brain when she forgot her keys for the third time that week.
