near vision

noun
ability to see objects close up
Pronunciation: / nɪr ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / nɪə ˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
the ability to see objects clearly at close range, typically within 2 feet of the eyes

Examples

near vision test
As people age, their near vision often deteriorates, making close work difficult.
Good near vision is essential for reading and sewing.
The optometrist tested my near vision and prescribed reading glasses.
