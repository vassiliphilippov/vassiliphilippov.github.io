barn grass

noun
a coarse annual grass, weed, sometimes used for hay
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrn ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːn ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a coarse annual panic grass (Echinochloa crus-galli), found worldwide as a weed and sometimes used for hay or grazing

Examples

barn grass grows wild
Farmers sometimes harvest barn grass for hay when better options are scarce.
