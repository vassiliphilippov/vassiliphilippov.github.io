sweet lemon

noun
a lemon variety with mildly sweet fruit
Pronunciation: / swit ˈlɛm.ən / (AmE), / swiːt ˈlɛm.ən / (BrE)
A variety of lemon tree that produces fruit with a mildly sweet, rather than tart, flavor; also, the fruit of this tree.

fresh sweet lemon juice
