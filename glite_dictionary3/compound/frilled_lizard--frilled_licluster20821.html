frilled lizard

noun
Australian lizard with a neck frill
Pronunciation: / frɪld ˈlɪzɚd / (AmE), / frɪld ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a large Australian lizard with a distinctive ruff of skin around its neck, which it can flare out; it lives in trees and eats insects

Examples

a frilled lizard flares neck
A frilled lizard can run quickly on its hind legs when threatened.
Many tourists in Australia hope to spot a frilled lizard in the wild.
The frilled lizard expanded its colorful neck frill to scare away the predator.
