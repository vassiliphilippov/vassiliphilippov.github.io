protective reaction

noun
a response to keep someone safe
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛktɪv riˈækʃən / (AmE), / prəˈtɛktɪv riˈækʃən / (BrE)
an action or response intended to keep someone safe from harm or danger

Examples

a protective reaction to danger
His protective reaction was to shield his friend from the falling debris.
Parents often show a protective reaction when they sense their child is in danger.
When the fire alarm sounded, her immediate protective reaction was to guide everyone outside safely.
