yellow-green algae

noun
algae with yellow pigment, division Chrysophyta
Pronunciation: / ˌjɛloʊˈɡrin ˈældʒi / (AmE), / ˌjɛləʊˈɡriːn ˈaldʒiː / (BrE)
Any algae of the division Chrysophyta in which the chlorophyll is masked by yellow pigment.
