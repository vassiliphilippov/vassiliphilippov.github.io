standard language

noun
most accepted variety of a language
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɚd ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstændəd ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
The variety of a language that is accepted as the norm for official, educational, and formal use, often considered the most correct form.
