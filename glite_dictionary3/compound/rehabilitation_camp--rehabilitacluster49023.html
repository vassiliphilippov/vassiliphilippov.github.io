rehabilitation camp

noun
facility for enforced re-education or behavioral change
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən kæmp / (AmE), / ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən kamp / (BrE)
a facility where individuals are detained, often forcibly, for re-education or enforced behavioral change

Examples

rehabilitation camp for prisoners
Documentaries have exposed the harsh realities faced by inmates in rehabilitation camps.
