sweet little time

noun
leisurely or slow passage of time
Pronunciation: / swit ˈlɪt̬əl taɪm / (AmE), / swiːt ˈlɪtl taɪm / (BrE)
a leisurely or deliberately slow amount of time taken to do something, especially when causing annoyance or impatience
