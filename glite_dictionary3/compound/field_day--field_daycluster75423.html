field day

noun
a period of notable pleasure or success
Pronunciation: / ˈfild ˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfiːld deɪ / (BrE)
An occasion or period of exceptional enjoyment, success, or opportunity, often when someone is able to do something they enjoy a great deal.

Examples

have a field day with
Chefs had a field day trying new recipes at the food festival.
Our team had a field day scoring goals against the weaker opponent.
The press had a field day with the scandal, reporting every new detail.
