snow mist

noun
suspended ice crystals in air
Pronunciation: / snoʊ mɪst / (AmE), / snəʊ mɪst / (BrE)
A thin cloud of minute ice crystals suspended in the air, often reducing visibility.

Examples

thick snow mist
A dense snow mist blanketed the valley, making it difficult to see the road ahead.
The snow mist created a magical, glittering effect under the streetlights.
The skiers moved cautiously through the snow mist that formed during the early morning hours.

  • synonym – fog: thick cloud of water droplets near ground, reducing visibility
  • synonym – haze: slight obscuration of the lower atmosphere
