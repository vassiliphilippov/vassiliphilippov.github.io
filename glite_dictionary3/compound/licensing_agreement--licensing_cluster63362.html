licensing agreement

noun
legal contract granting usage rights
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsənsɪŋ əˌɡriːmənt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪs(ə)nsiŋ əˌɡriːmənt / (BrE)
a legal contract granting someone permission to use a patent, trademark, or other intellectual property owned by another party

Examples

sign a licensing agreement
The licensing agreement stipulates the fees and terms for using the software.
The company signed a licensing agreement to manufacture products using the patented technology.
Without a licensing agreement, selling branded merchandise would be considered copyright infringement.
