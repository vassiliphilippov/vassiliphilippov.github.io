river birch

noun
a birch tree native to swampy areas of eastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvɚ bɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a North American birch tree (Betula nigra) found in swamps and river bottoms, noted for its peeling reddish-brown bark
