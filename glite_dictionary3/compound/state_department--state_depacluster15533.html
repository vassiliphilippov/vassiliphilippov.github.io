State Department

noun
US state government department
Pronunciation: / steɪt dɪˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / steɪt dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
A government department within one of the 50 states of the United States that manages specific administrative functions.

Examples

the State Department of Texas

Officials from various State Departments attended the annual conference.
