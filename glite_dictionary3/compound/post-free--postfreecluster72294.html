post-free

adjective
with postage paid by sender
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈfri /; / poʊstˈfri / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈfriː /; / pəʊstˈfriː / (BrE)
sent with the postage already paid by the sender, so the recipient does not have to pay for delivery

Examples

post-free delivery
All items will be sent post-free within the country.
Books ordered from this catalog are delivered post-free to your address.
If you reply before the deadline, we will ship your order post-free.
If you order more than $50 worth of goods, your package will be delivered post-free.
The supplier offered to send the catalog post-free to all interested customers.
They advertised the books as being available post-free anywhere in the country.
