Botany Bay fig

noun
Australian tree resembling banyan
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəni beɪ fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəni beɪ fɪɡ / (BrE)
An Australian tree (Ficus macrophylla) resembling the banyan, often grown as an ornamental and introduced elsewhere as brushwood.
