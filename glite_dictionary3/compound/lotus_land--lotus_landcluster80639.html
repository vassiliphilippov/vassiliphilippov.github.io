lotus land

noun
place of dreamy pleasure and escapism
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊtəs lænd / (AmE), / ˈləʊtəs lænd / (BrE)
an imaginary place of idyllic contentment, pleasure, and self-indulgence, often implying escapism or detachment from reality

Examples

Vacationers often seek a lotus land to escape the demands of everyday life.
