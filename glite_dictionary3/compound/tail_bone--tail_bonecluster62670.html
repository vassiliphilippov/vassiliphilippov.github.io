tail bone

noun
the small bone at the end of the spine (coccyx)
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪl ˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈteɪl ˌbəʊn / (BrE)
the small, bony structure forming the end of the vertebral column in humans and some apes; the coccyx

Examples

injured tail bone
She slipped on the ice and injured her tail bone.
Sitting on hard surfaces for too long can make your tail bone sore.
The x-ray showed a fracture in his tail bone.
