viola da gamba

noun
historical bowed string instrument; bass viol
Pronunciation: / viˈoʊlə də ˈɡæmbə / (AmE), / viˈəʊlə də ˈɡæmbə / (BrE)
A bass viol, a bowed string instrument from the viol family, with a range similar to the cello, commonly used during the Renaissance and Baroque periods.
