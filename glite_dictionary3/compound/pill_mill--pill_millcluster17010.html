pill mill

noun
clinic that prescribes medication illegally or irresponsibly
Pronunciation: / pɪl mɪl / (AmE), / pɪl mɪl / (BrE)
A clinic or doctor's office that prescribes medications, especially controlled substances, irresponsibly or illegally, often for profit rather than legitimate medical need.

Examples

illegal pill mill clinic
Investigators found that the pill mill had written thousands of unnecessary prescriptions.
She lost her medical license after her clinic was declared a pill mill.
The government has been cracking down on pill mills that fuel the opioid epidemic.
