windfall profit

noun
unexpected, accidental profit
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌfɔl ˈprɑfɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌfɔːl ˈprɒfɪt / (BrE)
a profit that comes unexpectedly, especially one resulting from an event beyond the control of those who benefit

Examples

windfall profit from oil prices
Lawmakers are debating whether to impose a tax on windfall profit gained by energy companies.
The oil company made a significant windfall profit after prices unexpectedly soared.
The sudden change in regulations resulted in a windfall profit for several investors.
