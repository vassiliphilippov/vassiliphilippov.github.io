personal credit line

noun
maximum amount an individual can borrow from a lender
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝsənəl ˈkrɛdɪt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl ˈkrɛdɪt laɪn / (BrE)
an arrangement with a bank or lender that allows a person to borrow up to a specified maximum amount of money, which can be used as needed
