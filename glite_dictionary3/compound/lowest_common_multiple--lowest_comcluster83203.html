lowest common multiple

noun
smallest shared multiple (math)
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊɪst ˈkɑmən ˈmʌltəpəl / (AmE), / ˈləʊɪst ˈkɒmən ˈmʌltɪpl̩ / (BrE)
