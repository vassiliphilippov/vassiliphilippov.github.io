credit system

noun
societal behaviour-rating system
Pronunciation: / c8kr d6at ccs6ast59m / (AmE), / c8kr d6at ccs6ast59m / (BrE)
A governmental or societal framework that assigns individuals a non-monetary score or rating (based on behaviour, compliance, or trust) which can affect their access to services, benefits, or opportunities.

Examples

social credit system
The city's credit system evaluates residents' behaviour and restricts travel for those with low scores.
Activists organised a rally after the government announced a new credit system linking benefits to online activity.
Several tech firms publish reports about data used by regional credit systems that affect housing eligibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym – credit scoring: the process of assigning numerical scores to individuals based on data; here used for behavioural or trust evaluations rather than financial risk
  • synonym – social credit: a governmental or societal scoring system that evaluates individuals' behaviour or trustworthiness
