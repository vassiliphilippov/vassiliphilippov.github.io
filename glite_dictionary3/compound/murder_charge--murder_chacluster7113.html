murder charge

noun
an accusation of committing murder
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːdɚ tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
an official accusation that a person has committed murder, usually brought in a court of law

Examples
