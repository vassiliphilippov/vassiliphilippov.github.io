sesame seed

noun
small seeds from the sesame plant
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛsəmi siːd / (AmE), / ˈsɛsəmi siːd / (BrE)
the small, oval seeds of the sesame plant, used as a food ingredient and for their oil

Examples

sesame seed topping bread
She sprinkled sesame seeds over the salad before serving it at dinner.
The hamburger bun was topped with sesame seeds for extra flavor and texture.
To make tahini, you first roast sesame seeds and then grind them into a paste.
