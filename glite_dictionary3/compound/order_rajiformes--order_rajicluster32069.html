order Rajiformes

noun
taxonomic order of flattened fish (rays, skates, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ˌreɪ.dʒɪˈfɔr.miːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌreɪ.dʒɪˈfɔː.miːz / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of cartilaginous fish with flattened bodies, including rays, skates, guitarfishes, and sawfishes.
