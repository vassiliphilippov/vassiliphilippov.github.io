Flying Spaghetti Monster

proper noun
satirical deity in a parody religion
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ spəˈɡɛti ˈmɑnstər / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ spəˈɡɛti ˈmɒnstə / (BrE)
a satirical deity created to mock certain arguments for the existence of God, representing the central figure of the Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster, often used as a parody of religion.
