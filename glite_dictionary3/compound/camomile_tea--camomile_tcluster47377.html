camomile tea

noun
herbal infusion made from camomile flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməˌmiːl tiː, kəˈmoʊməl tiː / (AmE), / ˈkæməmʌɪl tiː, ˈkaməmiːl tiː / (BrE)
a drink made by infusing dried camomile flowers in hot water, often used for its calming properties
