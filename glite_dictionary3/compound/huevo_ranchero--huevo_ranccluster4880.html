huevo ranchero

noun
Mexican egg and tortilla breakfast dish
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvoʊ rɑnˈtʃɛɾoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɛvəʊ ranˈtʃɛəɹəʊ / (BrE)
A Mexican breakfast dish consisting of eggs served with tortillas and a tomato-chili sauce.

Examples

huevo ranchero with salsa
For breakfast, I ordered the huevo ranchero with a side of beans.
He learned to cook huevo ranchero while traveling in Mexico.
The café is famous for its spicy huevo ranchero served with fresh salsa.
