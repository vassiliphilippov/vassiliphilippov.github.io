custard pie

noun
pie thrown in slapstick comedy
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstɚd paɪ / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəd paɪ / (BrE)
A pie filled with custard, especially one used as a prop to be thrown in slapstick comedies.

Examples

throw a custard pie
Children laughed as the clown was hit with a custard pie at the party.
No slapstick movie is complete without someone throwing a custard pie.
The comedian got a custard pie in the face during the performance.
