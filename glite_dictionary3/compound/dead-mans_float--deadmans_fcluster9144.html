dead-man's float

noun
face-down floating swimming position
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdˌmænz floʊt / (AmE), / ˈdɛdˌmænz fləʊt / (BrE)
A floating position in swimming where the body is facedown in the water with arms extended forward, used typically to conserve energy or rest.
