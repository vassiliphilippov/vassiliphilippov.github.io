your all's

pronoun
possessive of 'you all' (informal, US)
Pronunciation: / jʊr ɔlz / (AmE), / jɔːr ɔːlz /; / jɔː ɔːlz / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly US) the possessive form of 'you all,' used to show that something belongs to the group of people being addressed
