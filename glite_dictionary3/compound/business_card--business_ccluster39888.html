business card

noun
digital contact card
Pronunciation: / c8b6az.n59s k51rd / (AmE), / c8b6az.n59s k51d0d / (BrE)
a digital version of a business card: an electronic file or link that contains a person's name, job title, company, and contact details, shared electronically (for example by QR code, NFC, link, or vCard).

Examples

scan business card
After the meeting she scanned my business card and saved it to her phone's contacts.
Many startups now share business cards as contactless links instead of handing out printed cards.
You can create a digital business card with a LinkedIn profile link and a company logo.

Related Terms

  • antonym – printed business card: the traditional physical card with contact information, typically printed on paper.
  • synonym – vCard: a standard file format for electronic business cards, often used to share contact details.
