credit analyst

noun
person who evaluates financial trustworthiness
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪt ˈænəˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˈanəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who examines the financial details of individuals or businesses to assess their ability to repay borrowed money

Examples
