left field

noun
unexpected or unconventional idea or action
Pronunciation: / lɛft fiːld / (AmE), / lɛft fiːld / (BrE)
If something is described as 'left field', it is unexpected, unconventional, or surprising, often in an unusual or creative way.

Examples

an idea from left field
Her idea for the project was really left field, but it ended up being a huge success.
Sometimes the best solutions are the most left field ones.
That comment came out of left field and surprised everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – off the wall: strange or very unconventional
  • synonym – out of the blue: unexpectedly
