stopped play

noun
pause in a game due to conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpt pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈstɒpt pleɪ / (BrE)
A pause or halt in a game or sporting activity, typically due to external conditions such as weather or unforeseen events.

Examples

stopped play for rain
Heavy fog stopped play for over an hour at the tournament.
Rain unexpectedly stopped play just before the final innings began.
The umpire declared that light conditions had stopped play for the evening.
