red nigger

noun
offensive racial slur for Indigenous people
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈnɪɡər / (AmE), / rɛd ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and derogatory racial term historically used as a slur against Indigenous peoples in North America. Use of this term is strongly condemned and considered highly inappropriate.

Examples
