prairie anemone

noun
a spring-flowering perennial plant
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛri əˈnɛməni / (AmE), / ˈprɛəri əˈnɛməni / (BrE)
a short, hairy perennial plant with blue-violet or lilac flowers that blooms in early spring, native to North America and Siberia
