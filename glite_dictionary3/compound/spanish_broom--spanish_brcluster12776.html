Spanish broom

noun
a shrub with golden yellow flowers, native to southwestern Europe
Pronunciation: / ˌspænɪʃ ˈbruːm /; / ˈspænɪʃ bruːm / (AmE), / ˌspænɪʃ ˈbruːm /; / ˈspænɪʃ bruːm / (BrE)
a tall, thornless Mediterranean shrub (Spartium junceum) with fragrant yellow flowers and flexible stems, often grown as an ornamental plant and used for making baskets
