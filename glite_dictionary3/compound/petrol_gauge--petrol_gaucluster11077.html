petrol gauge

noun
instrument showing amount of petrol left in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛt̬rəl ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrəl ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument in a vehicle that shows how much petrol (gasoline) is left in the fuel tank

Examples

petrol gauge shows level
She checked the petrol gauge before setting off on her journey.
The petrol gauge showed that the tank was almost empty.
When the petrol gauge drops to red, it's time to refuel.
