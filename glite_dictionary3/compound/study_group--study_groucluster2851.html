study group

noun
group meeting to study together
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdi ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈstʌdi ɡruːp / (BrE)
a group of people who regularly meet to study and discuss academic material together

Examples

study group meets weekly
Her study group meets every Thursday afternoon at the library.
Joining a study group improved his understanding of the course material.
We formed a study group to help prepare for the final exams.

Related Terms

  • synonym – workshop: a meeting at which people engage in intensive discussion and activity on a particular subject
