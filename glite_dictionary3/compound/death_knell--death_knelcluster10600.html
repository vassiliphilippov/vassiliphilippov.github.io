death knell

noun
bell rung to signal a death
Pronunciation: / dɛθ nɛl / (AmE), / dɛθ nɛl / (BrE)
A bell rung to announce a death, or an event or sign marking the end or destruction of something.

Examples

church death knell
He heard the death knell from the church tower as he passed by.
The closing of the local factory sounded the death knell for the town's economy.
The rise of digital media was the death knell for many print newspapers.
