cotton fiber

noun
soft fibers from cotton plants
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑtn ˈfaɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒtn ˈfaɪbə / (BrE)
the soft, silky fibers obtained from the cotton plant, used as the raw material for making cotton textiles

Examples
