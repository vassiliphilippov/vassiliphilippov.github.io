hemp nettle

noun
a bristly Eurasian weed with nettle-like leaves
Pronunciation: / hɛmp ˈnɛt̬.əl / (AmE), / hɛmp ˈnɛt.əl / (BrE)
a coarse, bristly plant of the genus Galeopsis, native to Eurasia, with white or reddish flowers and foliage resembling nettle; often found as a weed in North America

Examples

hemp nettle weed
Farmers often struggle to control hemp nettle because it spreads so easily.
Some species of hemp nettle have small, purplish flowers and hairy stems.
