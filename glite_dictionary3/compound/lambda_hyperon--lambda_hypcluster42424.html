lambda hyperon

noun
subatomic baryon particle with isotopic spin 1
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.də ˈhaɪ.pɚˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈlæm.də ˈhaɪ.pərˌɒn / (BrE)
an electrically neutral subatomic particle (baryon) with isotopic spin 1, found in high-energy physics and containing a strange quark.
