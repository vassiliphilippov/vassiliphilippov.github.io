vegetative cell

noun
non-reproductive cell in plants or animals
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒ.əˌteɪ.tɪv sɛl / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒ.ɪ.tə.tɪv sɛl / (BrE)
any cell of a plant or animal body that is not involved in the production of gametes; a non-reproductive cell

Examples

vegetative cell divides
During cell division, a vegetative cell replicates its own DNA but does not produce gametes.
Scientists studied the mutation in the vegetative cells of the plant to learn about its effects on growth.
Unlike a spore or a gamete, a vegetative cell functions primarily in growth and metabolism.

Related Terms

  • synonym – somatic cell: any cell of a living organism other than the reproductive cells
