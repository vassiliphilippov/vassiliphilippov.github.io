Cabernet Sauvignon

noun
type of red wine or grape variety
Pronunciation: / ˌkæbərneɪ soʊˈviːnjən / (AmE), / ˌkæbɜːneɪ səʊˈviːnjɒ̃ / (BrE)
A variety of red wine, originally from the Bordeaux region, made from a particular black grape; also, the grape used to produce this wine.
