tumble drier

noun
machine for drying clothes with heated air
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmbəl ˌdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbəl ˌdraɪə / (BrE)
A machine that dries clothes by spinning them in a drum while blowing heated air through them.

Examples

tumble drier dries clothes
Our tumble drier stopped working during the rainy season, which made life difficult.
She put the wet laundry into the tumble drier before heading out.
Using a tumble drier can save time compared to air drying your clothes.
