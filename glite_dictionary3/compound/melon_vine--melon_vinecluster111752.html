melon vine

noun
plant that produces melons
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛlən vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɛlən vaɪn / (BrE)
A climbing or trailing plant of the gourd family that produces melons, such as muskmelons, watermelons, or cantaloupes.

Examples

climbing melon vine
Farmers monitor the growth of each melon vine to ensure a good harvest.
She planted a melon vine next to the trellis in hopes of growing sweet watermelons.
The melon vine spread quickly over the garden fence, producing several large cantaloupes.
