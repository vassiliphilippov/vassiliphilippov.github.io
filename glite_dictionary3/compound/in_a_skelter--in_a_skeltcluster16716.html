in a skelter

adverb
in a disorderly manner
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə ˈskɛltər / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈskɛltə / (BrE)
in a state of chaos or disorder; in a confused or hurried manner

Examples

in a skelter of activity
Before the performance, the backstage area was in a skelter of last-minute preparations.
Papers flew in a skelter as the wind swept through the open window.
The children rushed about the playground in a skelter, laughter echoing in the air.
