strawberry haemangioma

noun
bright red vascular birthmark in infants
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔːˌbɛri hiːˌmænˈdʒaɪoʊmə / (AmE), / ˈstrɒbəri hɪˌmanˈdʒaɪəmə / (BrE)
a congenital bright red, raised birthmark caused by a cluster of blood vessels near the skin's surface, usually fading as a child grows
