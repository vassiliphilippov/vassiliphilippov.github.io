September 29

noun
a quarter day known as Michaelmas
Pronunciation: / sɛpˈtɛmbɚ ˈtwɛnti ˈnaɪn / (AmE), / sɛpˈtɛmbə ˈtwɛnti ˈnaɪn / (BrE)
In England, Wales, and Ireland, the day known as Michaelmas, marking a quarter day and honoring the archangel Michael.
