to keep one's sanity

verb
maintain one's mental stability
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz ˈsænəti / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz ˈsænɪti / (BrE)
to remain mentally healthy and avoid becoming mentally disturbed, especially under stress or difficult circumstances

Examples

keep one's sanity under stress
In the face of constant pressure at work, he managed to keep his sanity.
She struggled to keep her sanity while caring for three energetic children during lockdown.
Traveling alone through remote areas, I had to find ways to keep my sanity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – keep one's cool: to remain calm and unemotional
  • antonym – lose one's sanity: to become mentally unstable or disturbed
