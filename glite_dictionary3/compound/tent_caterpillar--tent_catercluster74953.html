tent caterpillar

noun
larval moth living in communal silken webs
Pronunciation: / tɛnt ˈkæt̬ɚˌpɪlɚ / (AmE), / tɛnt ˈkat.əˌpɪl.ə / (BrE)
the larva of certain moths that builds and lives in communal silken webs, typically found on orchard or shade trees

Examples

tent caterpillar nest in tree
A nest of tent caterpillars hung from the branches of the apple tree.
Gardeners use netting to protect their plants from tent caterpillars in the early season.
The tent caterpillar can quickly defoliate entire trees during the spring.
