lesser omentum

noun
membrane fold between stomach and liver
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsɚ oʊˈmɛntəm / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə əʊˈmɛntəm / (BrE)
a fold of the peritoneum attaching the stomach and the liver and supporting the hepatic vessels

Examples

lesser omentum supports liver
Damage to the lesser omentum can compromise blood supply to the liver.
The anatomy textbook includes a detailed diagram of the lesser omentum and its attachments.
The surgeon carefully lifted the lesser omentum to expose the hepatic vessels.
