bathroom fixture

noun
device permanently installed in a bathroom
Pronunciation: / ˈbæθˌruːm ˈfɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθruːm ˈfɪkstʃə / (BrE)
a device or fitting permanently installed in a bathroom, such as a sink, toilet, or bathtub

Examples

Cleaning the bathroom fixture regularly prevents the buildup of limescale.
He replaced all the old bathroom fixtures to give the space a contemporary look.
The new house has modern bathroom fixtures, including a rainfall shower and a stylish sink.
