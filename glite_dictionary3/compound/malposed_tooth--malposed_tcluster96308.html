malposed tooth

noun
tooth grown in abnormal position
Pronunciation: / ˌmælˌpoʊzd ˈtuθ / (AmE), / ˌmælˌpəʊzd ˈtuːθ / (BrE)
A tooth that has grown or erupted in an abnormal or faulty position within the mouth.
