internal medicine

noun
medical specialty treating internal organs (adults)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɝnəl ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːnəl ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
The branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and nonsurgical treatment of diseases affecting internal organs, especially in adults.
