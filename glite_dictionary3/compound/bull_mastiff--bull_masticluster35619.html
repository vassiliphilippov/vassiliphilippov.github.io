bull mastiff

noun
a large, powerful dog breed
Pronunciation: / bʊl ˈmæstɪf / (AmE), / bʊl ˈmɑːstɪf / (BrE)
a large, powerful breed of dog originally developed by crossing the bulldog and the mastiff, often used as a guard dog

Examples

a bull mastiff guard dog
Despite its size, a properly trained bull mastiff can be very gentle and affectionate.
The bull mastiff is known for its loyal and protective nature around children.
Their new security dog is a bull mastiff named Max.
