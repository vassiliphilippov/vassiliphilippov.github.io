in any case

adverb
used to add or emphasize a point
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈɛni keɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈeni keɪs / (BrE)
used to add a point, emphasize something, or show that something is true regardless of other things; anyway; regardless
