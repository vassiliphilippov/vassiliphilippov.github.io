battle flag

noun
flag carried into battle by troops
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɾl ˌflæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbatəl ˌflaɡ / (BrE)
A flag carried at the front of a military formation to serve as a rallying point or symbol during battle.
