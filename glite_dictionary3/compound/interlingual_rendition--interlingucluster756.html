interlingual rendition

noun
translation into another language
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlɪŋɡwəl rɛnˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlɪŋɡwəl rɛnˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a written version of a text that expresses the same meaning in a different language from the original
