dripping pan

noun
pan for catching drippings from roasting meat
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪpɪŋ pæn / (AmE), / ˈdrɪpɪŋ pan / (BrE)
A shallow pan placed underneath roasting meat to catch fat and juices that drip during cooking.

Examples

dripping pan under roast
After the turkey cooked, she used the juices from the dripping pan to make gravy.
Place a dripping pan under the rack to catch any fat from the roast lamb.
Remember to empty the dripping pan halfway through roasting if it becomes too full.

Related Terms

  • synonym – roasting pan: a pan used for roasting meat or vegetables, often larger and deeper than a dripping pan
