dairy farm

noun
farm for producing milk and dairy products
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛri fɑrm / (AmE), / ˈdeəri fɑːm / (BrE)
A farm where milk is produced from cows or other animals and processed into dairy products such as cheese and butter.

Examples

large dairy farm
Modern dairy farm practices often include automated milking systems.
She grew up on a dairy farm in rural Wisconsin.
The dairy farm supplies fresh milk to several local stores.
