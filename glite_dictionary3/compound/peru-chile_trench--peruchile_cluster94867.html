Peru-Chile Trench

noun
deep ocean trench off Peru and Chile
Pronunciation: / pəˌru tʃɪˈleɪ trɛntʃ / (AmE), / pəˌruː tʃɪˈleɪ trɛntʃ / (BrE)
A deep depression in the floor of the Pacific Ocean, located off the coasts of Peru and Chile.
