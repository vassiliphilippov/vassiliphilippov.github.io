body suit

noun
tight-fitting stretchy garment covering torso
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdi suːt / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi sjuːt / (BrE)
A tight-fitting garment made of stretchy material that covers the torso and sometimes the arms and legs; typically worn by dancers, acrobats, or as a fashion item.

Examples

wear a body suit
For the audition, she changed into a black body suit and tights.
Many fashion designers experimented with the body suit as a statement piece in their collections.
The gymnast wore a sparkling body suit during her performance at the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym – leotard: a close-fitting one-piece garment for the body, often without sleeves or legs, worn by dancers and gymnasts
