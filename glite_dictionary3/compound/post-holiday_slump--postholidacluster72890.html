post-holiday slump

noun
period of low mood after holidays
Pronunciation: / poʊst ˈhɑːləˌdeɪ slʌmp / (AmE), / pəʊst ˈhɒlədeɪ slʌmp / (BrE)
a period of low mood or lack of motivation following the end of holidays or festive seasons

Examples

feel a post-holiday slump
After the excitement of Christmas, a sense of post-holiday slump settled over the entire family.
Many employees experience a post-holiday slump when they return to work in January.
To combat the post-holiday slump, she planned a series of small celebrations throughout the winter.
