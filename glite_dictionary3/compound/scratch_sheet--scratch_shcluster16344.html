scratch sheet

noun
horse racing publication with race outcomes and data
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃ ʃit / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃ ʃiːt / (BrE)
a publication that provides information on horses and the results of horse races, used especially for betting purposes

Examples

scratch sheet for horse races
He checked the scratch sheet before placing his bet at the track.
Many gamblers rely on the scratch sheet for the latest race statistics.
The scratch sheet listed the odds and outcomes for every horse in the event.
