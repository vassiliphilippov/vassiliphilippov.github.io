eggs Benedict

noun
toasted muffin with ham, poached egg, and hollandaise
Pronunciation: / ɛɡz ˈbɛnədɪkt / (AmE), / ɛɡz ˈbɛnədɪkt / (BrE)
A dish consisting of a toasted English muffin topped with ham and a poached egg, covered with hollandaise sauce.

Examples

eggs Benedict with ham
For brunch, she ordered the eggs Benedict, which arrived with perfectly poached eggs and creamy hollandaise sauce.
He had never tasted eggs Benedict before but quickly became a fan of the classic breakfast dish.
Many restaurants serve their own variation of eggs Benedict, substituting the ham with smoked salmon or spinach.
