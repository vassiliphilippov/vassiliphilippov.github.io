Indian rosewood

noun
tree yielding dark hardwood, native to India
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈroʊzˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈrəʊzˌwʊd / (BrE)
A large tree native to the Indian subcontinent, valued for its hard, dark-colored wood used especially in furniture and musical instruments.

Indian rosewood furniture
Demand for Indian rosewood has increased due to its durability and beauty in fine woodworking.
