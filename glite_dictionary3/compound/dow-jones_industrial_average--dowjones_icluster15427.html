Dow-Jones Industrial Average

noun
major U.S. stock market index
Pronunciation: / daʊ ˈdʒoʊnz ˌɪn.dəˈstrɪl ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / daʊ ˈdʒəʊnz ˌɪn.dəˈstrɪəl ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
an index that measures the stock performance of 30 prominent companies listed on the New York Stock Exchange and NASDAQ, used as a key indicator of overall market health
