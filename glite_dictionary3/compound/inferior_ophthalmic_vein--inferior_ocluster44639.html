inferior ophthalmic vein

noun
vein draining part of the eye socket
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriər ɑfˈθælmɪk veɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪərɪə ɒfˈθælmɪk veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the floor and inner wall of the eye socket

Examples
