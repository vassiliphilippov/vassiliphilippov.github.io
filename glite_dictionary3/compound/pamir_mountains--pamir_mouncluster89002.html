Pamir Mountains

proper noun
mountain range in Central Asia
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈmɪr ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / pəˈmɪə ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in Central Asia, mainly in Tajikistan, extending into Kyrgyzstan, Afghanistan, Pakistan, and western China

Examples

the Pamir Mountains in Tajikistan
Expeditions to the Pamir Mountains attract climbers and researchers from all over the globe.
Glaciers in the Pamir Mountains provide vital water sources for the surrounding regions.
The Pamir Mountains are often referred to as the "Roof of the World" due to their high peaks.
