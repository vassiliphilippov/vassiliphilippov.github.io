polyvinyl acetate

noun
synthetic resin used in paints and adhesives
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˈvaɪnəl ˈæsɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈvaɪnɪl ˈæsɪteɪt / (BrE)
a synthetic resin produced by the polymerization of vinyl acetate, commonly used as an ingredient in paints, adhesives, and other products

Examples

polyvinyl acetate glue
