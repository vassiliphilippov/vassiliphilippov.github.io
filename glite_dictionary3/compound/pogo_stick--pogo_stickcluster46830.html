pogo stick

noun
spring-loaded jumping toy
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊɡoʊ ˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈpəʊɡəʊ ˌstɪk / (BrE)
A toy consisting of a pole with foot rests and a strong spring, used for jumping up and down.

Examples

jump on a pogo stick
After school, Lily practiced new tricks with her pogo stick in the driveway.
He bounced across the yard on his pogo stick, laughing all the way.
The children took turns to see who could jump higher on the pogo stick.
