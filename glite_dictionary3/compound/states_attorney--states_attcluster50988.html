state's attorney

noun
lawyer who prosecutes on behalf of a state
Pronunciation: / steɪts əˈtɝːni / (AmE), / steɪts əˈtɜːni / (BrE)
a lawyer who represents the government and prosecutes criminal cases on behalf of a state, especially in the United States.

Examples

state's attorney prosecutes crimes
After a lengthy investigation, the state's attorney decided to proceed with a grand jury.
She worked as a state's attorney before becoming a judge.
The state's attorney announced new charges against the defendant this afternoon.
