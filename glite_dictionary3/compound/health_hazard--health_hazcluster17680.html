health hazard

noun
something posing a health risk
Pronunciation: / hɛlθ ˈhæzərd / (AmE), / hɛlθ ˈhazəd / (BrE)
Something that poses a risk to people's health; a source of potential harm or danger to health.

Examples

health hazard in buildings
Exposure to asbestos is considered a serious health hazard in buildings.
Smoking in public places is now banned because it is a health hazard for non-smokers.
The report warned that contaminated water could be a health hazard to the local population.

Related Terms

  • synonym – danger: the possibility of harm or injury
  • antonym – safety: the condition of being protected from harm
