pack animal

noun
animal used to carry loads
Pronunciation: / pæk ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / pæk ˈanɪməl / (BrE)
an animal, such as a mule, donkey, or horse, trained and used to carry loads

Examples

pack animal carries supplies
In remote areas, a pack animal can be the most reliable means of moving equipment.
Mules are often chosen as pack animals because of their strength and endurance.
The caravan relied on each pack animal to transport food and supplies across the mountains.
