garden webworm

noun
larva of a moth that is a garden pest
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən ˈwɛbˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdən ˈwɛbˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a moth larva that spins webs on various plants and is considered a pest in gardens, specifically the larva of Loxostege similalis.

Examples

garden webworm infestation
A severe infestation of garden webworm was found on the lettuce crops this summer.
Farmers use natural predators to keep the garden webworm population in check.
The garden webworm can quickly defoliate vegetable plants if not controlled.
