carriage dog

noun
dog breed with spotted coat; Dalmatian
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪdʒ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪdʒ dɒɡ / (BrE)
A large breed of dog with a smooth white coat marked with black or brown spots, originally bred to run alongside carriages; also known as a Dalmatian.
