business traveler

noun
a person who travels for work
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˈtrævələr / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈtrævələ / (BrE)
a person who travels for work and whose travel expenses are paid by their employer

Examples

As a frequent business traveler, she values efficiency and comfort in her accommodations.
