snowed in

adjective
unable to leave due to heavy snow
Pronunciation: / snoʊd ɪn / (AmE), / snəʊd ɪn / (BrE)
Unable to leave a place because of heavy snow blocking the way.

Examples

snowed in at home
After the storm, we were snowed in for two days and couldn't get to work.
Many travelers found themselves snowed in when the blizzard hit unexpectedly.
The mountain cabin was snowed in, making it impossible for anyone to reach us.
