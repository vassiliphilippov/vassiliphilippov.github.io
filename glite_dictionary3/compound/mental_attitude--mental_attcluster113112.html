mental attitude

noun
way of thinking or feeling about something
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.tl̩ ˈæt.əˌtud / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a particular way of thinking or feeling about something, often reflected in beliefs, values, and readiness to act

Examples

positive mental attitude
A positive mental attitude can greatly influence your overall well-being.
His mental attitude towards challenges has helped him succeed in difficult situations.
Successful athletes often attribute their performance to their mental attitude as much as to physical skill.
