foster father

noun
man who cares for a foster child
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstər ˈfɑðər / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə ˈfɑːðə / (BrE)
A man who acts as a father to a child, but is not the child's biological or adoptive parent, and is responsible for their care and upbringing, usually on a temporary basis.

Examples

foster father cares for child
After being placed in care, he lived with a foster father who supported his interests in music.
The child’s foster father attended every school event and parent-teacher conference.
Though not related by blood, his foster father treated him as his own son.
