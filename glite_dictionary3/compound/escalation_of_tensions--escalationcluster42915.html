escalation of tensions

noun
increase in conflict or hostility
Pronunciation: / ˌɛskəˈleɪʃən əv ˈtɛnʃənz / (AmE), / ˌɛskəˈleɪʃən əv ˈtɛnʃənz / (BrE)
a situation in which conflict, hostility, or dispute becomes more intense or serious

Examples
