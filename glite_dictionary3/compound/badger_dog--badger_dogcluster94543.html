badger dog

noun
small German breed of dog; dachshund
Pronunciation: / ˈbædʒər dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈbadʒə dɒɡ / (BrE)
A small, long-bodied, short-legged breed of dog with a short, smooth coat and long, drooping ears, originally bred in Germany for following game into burrows; also known as the dachshund.

Examples

a badger dog puppy
Her family has owned a badger dog since she was a child.
I saw a badger dog competing in the agility trials at the local dog show.
The badger dog is known for its courage and tenacity when chasing prey underground.
