semiconductor device

noun
electronic component made from semiconducting material
Pronunciation: / sɛmɪkənˈdʌktɚ dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / sɛmɪkənˈdʌktə dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
An electronic component made from a material that has electrical conductivity between that of a conductor and an insulator, typically used in circuits.
