motion-picture show

noun
entertainment using moving images and sound
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊʃən ˈpɪktʃər ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊʃən ˈpɪktʃə ʃəʊ / (BrE)
a form of entertainment in which a story is enacted by using sound and a sequence of moving images to create the illusion of continuous motion

Examples

classic motion-picture show
Attending a motion-picture show was a favorite pastime for her grandparents.
The popularity of the motion-picture show has grown tremendously since the early 20th century.
They decided to watch a motion-picture show at the historic theater downtown.
