plain stitch

noun
basic knitting stitch
Pronunciation: / pleɪn stɪtʃ / (AmE), / pleɪn stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a basic knitting stitch made by inserting the needle through the front of the stitch from the lefthand side

Examples

plain stitch in knitting
She prefers to use plain stitch when practicing her knitting skills.
The pattern calls for alternating between plain stitch and purl stitch.
To start the scarf, knit every row in plain stitch for a smooth texture.
