eleventh-hour decision

noun
last-minute decision or action
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛvənθ aʊər dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ aʊə dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
a decision or choice made at the last possible moment, often just before a deadline or critical point

Examples

last-minute eleventh-hour decision
