Feast of the Dedication

noun
Jewish festival commemorating the Temple's rededication
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːst əv ðə ˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfiːst əv ðə ˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
(Judaism) an eight-day Jewish festival, also known as Hanukkah, commemorating the rededication of the Temple in Jerusalem in 165 BC.
