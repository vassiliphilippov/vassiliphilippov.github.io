feast or famine

noun
alternating plenty and scarcity
Pronunciation: / fist ɔr fæmɪn / (AmE), / fiːst ɔː ˈfæmɪn / (BrE)
a situation in which there are alternating periods of plenty and scarcity

Examples

feast or famine cycle
Freelancers often experience feast or famine when new projects suddenly stop coming in.
In agriculture, weather patterns can create a cycle of feast or famine for farmers.
The company's sales history showed a clear pattern of feast or famine over the past decade.
