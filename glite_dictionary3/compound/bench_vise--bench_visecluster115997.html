bench vise

noun
workbench clamping device with jaws
Pronunciation: / bɛntʃ vaɪs / (AmE), / bɛntʃ vaɪs / (BrE)
a clamping device attached to a workbench, consisting of two jaws for holding a workpiece securely in place during work.

Examples

bench vise holds wood
Every workshop should have a sturdy bench vise for detailed projects.
He secured the piece of wood in the bench vise before sanding it.
The mechanic tightened the bench vise to hold the metal part still.
