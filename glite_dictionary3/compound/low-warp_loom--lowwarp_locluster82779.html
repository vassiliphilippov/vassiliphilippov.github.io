low-warp loom

noun
horizontal handloom for tapestry weaving
Pronunciation: / loʊ wɑrp luːm / (AmE), / ləʊ wɑːp luːm / (BrE)
a hand-operated loom in which the warp threads are arranged horizontally, used for weaving tapestry.

Examples
