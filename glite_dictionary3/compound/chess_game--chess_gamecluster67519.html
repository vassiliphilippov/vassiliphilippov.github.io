chess game

noun
two-player strategy board game with checkmate objective
Pronunciation: / tʃɛs ɡeɪm / (AmE), / tʃɛs ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A board game for two players, each controlling 16 pieces, where the objective is to checkmate the opponent's king.

Examples

win a chess game
Her first chess game ended in a draw after fifty moves.
The championship chess game was broadcast live on television.
They spent hours playing an intense chess game in the library.
