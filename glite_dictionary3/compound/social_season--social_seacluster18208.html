social season

noun
period for major social events
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəl ˈsiːzn̩ / (BrE)
the time of year when major social events and gatherings are traditionally held, especially in fashionable or upper-class society

Examples

the social season in London
