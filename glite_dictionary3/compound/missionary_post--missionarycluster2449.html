missionary post

noun
station or organization for missionary work
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃəˌnɛri poʊst / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃənəri pəʊst / (BrE)
A station or organization established in a foreign country where missionaries conduct religious and often social work.

Examples

a missionary post abroad
After years of preparation, she was finally assigned to a missionary post in Kenya.
Many volunteers joined the missionary post to help with building projects and teaching.
The missionary post provided education and medical care for the local community.
