pea shooter

noun
toy tube for blowing pellets
Pronunciation: / ˈpi ˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈpiː ˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
A simple toy consisting of a narrow tube through which small pellets, such as dried peas, are blown at a target.

Examples

play with a pea shooter
As a child, he used a pea shooter to knock over empty cans in the backyard.
She made a homemade pea shooter out of a straw and some dried peas.
The teacher confiscated the pea shooter after students used it in class.
