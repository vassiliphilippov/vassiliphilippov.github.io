bulletin board

noun
board for posting notices
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlətɪn bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbʊlətɪn bɔːd / (BrE)
A board mounted on a wall for posting public notices, messages, or announcements.

Examples

bulletin board with notices
Please check the bulletin board for new announcements before leaving the room.
She pinned the missing dog poster to the bulletin board at the community center.
The teacher updates the classroom bulletin board every month with student artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym – noticeboard: a board for displaying notices or announcements (chiefly British term)
