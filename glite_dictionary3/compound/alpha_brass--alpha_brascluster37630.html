alpha brass

noun
a brass alloy for cold working
Pronunciation: / ˈælfə bræs / (AmE), / ˈælfə brɑːs / (BrE)
a type of brass alloy containing up to about 37% zinc, suitable mainly for cold working processes

Examples

alpha brass sheet metal
Engineers selected alpha brass for the project because it bends easily without cracking.
The pipes were formed by cold working sheets of alpha brass.
This manufacturer specializes in components made from alpha brass due to its ductility.
