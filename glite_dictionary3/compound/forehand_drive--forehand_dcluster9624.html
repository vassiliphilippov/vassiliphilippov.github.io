forehand drive

noun
hard straight forehand shot in racket sports
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌhænd draɪv / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌhænd draɪv / (BrE)
a hard, straight shot played on the forehand side, especially in sports such as tennis, badminton, or squash.

Examples

The coach praised her technique after watching her hit several perfect forehand drives.
