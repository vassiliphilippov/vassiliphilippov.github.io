ethnic music

noun
traditional music of a cultural group
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθ.nɪk ˈmju.zɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɪk ˈmjuː.zɪk / (BrE)
Traditional music characteristic of a particular cultural group, often passed down orally and reflecting the customs and life of a community.
