drill master

noun
military instructor for marching and discipline
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪl ˌmæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈdrɪl ˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a noncommissioned officer responsible for instructing recruits in military marching and discipline

Examples

drill master trains soldiers
After weeks of training, the drill master praised the soldiers' improved discipline.
Every morning, the drill master led physical exercises before marching drills began.
The drill master barked instructions as the recruits marched across the parade ground.
