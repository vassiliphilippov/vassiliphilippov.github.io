ball carrier

noun
player carrying the ball in football/rugby
Pronunciation: / bɔl ˈkæriər / (AmE), / bɔːl ˈkæriə / (BrE)
(in football or rugby) the player who is carrying the ball and attempting to advance it during an offensive play.

Examples

ball carrier runs forward
Each ball carrier must keep control of the ball under heavy pressure from defenders.
He tackled the ball carrier just a few yards short of the goal line.
The ball carrier broke through the defense and scored a touchdown.

Related Terms

  • synonym – runner: player who advances the ball, especially in American football
