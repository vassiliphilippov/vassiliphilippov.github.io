menstrual phase

noun
stage of the menstrual cycle with uterine lining shedding
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnstruəl feɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛnstrʊəl feɪz / (BrE)
the stage of the menstrual cycle when the lining of the uterus is shed, typically marked by menstrual bleeding

Examples

the menstrual phase begins
Doctors recommend tracking the menstrual phase to better understand reproductive health.
Some symptoms, like cramps and fatigue, are common during the menstrual phase of the cycle.
The menstrual phase usually lasts three to seven days, depending on the individual.
