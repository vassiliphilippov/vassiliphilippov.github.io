soft goods

noun
textiles and clothing merchandise
Pronunciation: / sɑft ɡʊdz / (AmE), / sɒft ɡʊdz / (BrE)
textiles, clothing, and similar merchandise, as distinguished from hard goods like machinery or furniture.

Examples

soft goods department
Retailers often divide their inventory into soft goods like towels and apparel, and hard goods such as electronics.
Sales of soft goods have increased this season, especially in the bedding department.
The store expanded its range of soft goods to include more fashionable clothing and linens.
