front projector

noun
device that projects onto a screen from the front
Pronunciation: / frʌnt pɹəˈdʒɛktɚ / (AmE), / frʌnt prəˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
A projector designed to display images or video onto a screen from the front, often using digital input.

Examples

front projector in classroom
Many schools now use a front projector connected to computers for interactive lessons.
The conference room was equipped with a front projector for presentations and video calls.
Using a front projector allows for larger images in home theater setups.
