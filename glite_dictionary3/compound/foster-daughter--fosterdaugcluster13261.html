foster-daughter

noun
girl raised as a daughter, not by birth
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstər ˌdɔt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə ˌdɔːtə / (BrE)
A girl or woman who is raised by someone as their daughter, though not biologically related to them.

Examples

adopt a foster-daughter
After years in the system, Maria finally became the beloved foster-daughter of the Johnson family.
Her foster-daughter surprised her with a handmade birthday card.
The couple attended the graduation ceremony to support their foster-daughter.
