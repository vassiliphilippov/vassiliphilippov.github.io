light colonel

noun
informal term for lieutenant colonel
Pronunciation: / laɪt kəˈloʊnəl / (AmE), / laɪt kəˈləʊnəl / (BrE)
A commissioned officer in the United States Army, Air Force, or Marines ranking above major and below colonel; an informal term for lieutenant colonel.

Examples

a light colonel in the Army
After serving twenty years, he finally achieved the rank of light colonel in the Air Force.
Many officers refer to a lieutenant colonel as a light colonel in informal conversation.
