Italian greyhound

noun
a small slender dog breed
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtæliən ˈɡreɪˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ɪˈtæl.i.ən ˈɡreɪ.haʊnd / (BrE)
a small, slender breed of dog originally developed from the greyhound, known for its elegance and affectionate nature
