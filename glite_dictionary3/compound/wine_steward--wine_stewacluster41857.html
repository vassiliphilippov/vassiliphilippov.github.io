wine steward

noun
waiter responsible for wine service
Pronunciation: / waɪn ˈstuː.ərd / (AmE), / waɪn ˈstjuː.əd / (BrE)
a waiter in a restaurant or hotel who is responsible for the service and selection of wine

Examples

wine steward recommends wine
A good wine steward knows how to pair wines with every course of the menu.
After consulting the wine steward, we decided to try a rare vintage from France.
The wine steward recommended an excellent Pinot Noir to accompany our meal.
