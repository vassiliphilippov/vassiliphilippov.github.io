civilian garb

noun
civilian clothing; non-uniform dress
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɪl.jən ɡɑrb / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪl.jən ɡɑːb / (BrE)
Ordinary clothing, as opposed to uniforms, work clothes, or official attire worn in certain professions.

Examples

civilian garb instead of uniform
After retiring from the army, he was glad to wear civilian garb again.
The detective blended into the crowd by dressing in civilian garb instead of her usual work attire.
Visitors must change out of their uniforms and into civilian garb before entering the facility.
