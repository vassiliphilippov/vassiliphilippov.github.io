drone pipe

noun
bagpipe pipe producing continuous tone
Pronunciation: / ˈdroʊn ˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈdrəʊn ˌpaɪp / (BrE)
a pipe of the bagpipe that produces a single continuous tone, usually serving as a constant background note

Examples
