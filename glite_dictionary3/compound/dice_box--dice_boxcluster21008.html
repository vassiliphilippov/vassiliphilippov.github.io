dice box

noun
container for shaking and throwing dice
Pronunciation: / daɪs bɑks / (AmE), / daɪs bɒks / (BrE)
a small container, open at one end, used for shaking and throwing dice by hand

Examples

dice box for board games
A well-crafted dice box makes board games feel more authentic and organized.
He placed the dice in the dice box before shaking them and rolling onto the table.
Players used a wooden dice box to prevent the dice from scattering across the floor.
