reed rhapis

noun
species of slender Chinese lady palm
Pronunciation: / rid ˈræpɪs / (AmE), / riːd ˈræpɪs / (BrE)
A species of Chinese lady palm (Rhapis humilis) known for having more slender stems and finer sheath fibers than Rhapis excelsa.
