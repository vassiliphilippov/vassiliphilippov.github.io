liquid refreshment

noun
a drink that refreshes
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd rɪˈfrɛʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd rɪˈfrɛʃmənt / (BrE)
a drink, especially one that is cool or refreshing, sometimes implying an alcoholic beverage

They served a variety of liquid refreshments at the summer party, including juices and cocktails.
