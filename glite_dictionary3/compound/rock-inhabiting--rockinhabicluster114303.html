rock-inhabiting

adjective
living or growing on rocks
Pronunciation: / rɑk ɪnˈhæbɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / rɒk ɪnˈhæbɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant, fungus, or organism) living or growing on rocks, especially used of ferns and lichens.

Examples

rock-inhabiting lichens
Many rock-inhabiting lichens thrive in harsh, exposed mountain environments.
Some species of ferns are specifically adapted to be rock-inhabiting rather than growing in soil.
