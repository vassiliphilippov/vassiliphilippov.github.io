effects of imperialism

noun
results of imperial rule or colonial domination
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛkts əv ɪmˈpɪriəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛkts əv ɪmˈpɪəriəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
the consequences or outcomes resulting from the policy or practice of one country extending its control over another, especially in a colonial context

Examples

effects of imperialism on society
Historians continue to debate the long-term effects of imperialism on economic development.
Many indigenous languages declined due to the effects of imperialism.
The film explores the cultural effects of imperialism in South Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym – colonialism: the policy or practice of acquiring full or partial control over another country and exploiting it economically
