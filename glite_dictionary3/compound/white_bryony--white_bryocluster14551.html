white bryony

noun
a vine with white flowers and black berries
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈbraɪəni / (AmE), / waɪt ˈbraɪəni / (BrE)
A climbing vine with white flowers, thick roots, and small black berries, native to Europe and extending to Iran.
