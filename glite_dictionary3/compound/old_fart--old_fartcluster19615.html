old fart

noun
slang for an out-of-touch old person
Pronunciation: / oʊld fɑrt / (AmE), / əʊld fɑːt / (BrE)
(slang) an older person, typically implying that they are out of touch or set in their ways, often used humorously or affectionately.

Examples

grumpy old fart
Don't let them call you an old fart just because you like things the old way.
My grandfather jokes that he has become an old fart who can't stay up past ten.
Sometimes I feel like an old fart when I don't understand new trends.
