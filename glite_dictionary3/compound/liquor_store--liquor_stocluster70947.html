liquor store

noun
shop selling alcoholic drinks for take-away
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkər stɔr / (AmE), / ˈlɪkə stɔː / (BrE)
A shop that sells alcoholic beverages, such as wine, beer, or spirits, for consumption off the premises.
