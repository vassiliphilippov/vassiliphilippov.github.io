first strike

noun
general pre-emptive attack
Pronunciation: / f5dst stra6ak / (AmE), / f5cd0st stra6ak / (BrE)
a pre-emptive attack intended to neutralize an imminent threat before it can act or retaliate; used generally across domains (e.g., military, cyber).

Examples
