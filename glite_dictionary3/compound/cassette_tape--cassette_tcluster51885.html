cassette tape

noun
magnetic tape audio storage in a case
Pronunciation: / kəˈsɛt teɪp / (AmE), / kəˈsɛt teɪp / (BrE)
a small, flat case containing magnetic tape that is used for recording and playing back sound or music

Examples

Before CDs became popular, most people listened to music on cassette tapes.
