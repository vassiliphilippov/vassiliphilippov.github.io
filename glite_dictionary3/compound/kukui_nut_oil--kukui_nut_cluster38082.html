kukui nut oil

noun
oil from kukui nuts, used in cooking and skincare
Pronunciation: / kuˈkuː.i nʌt ɔɪl / (AmE), / kuˈkuː.i nʌt ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oil extracted from the nuts of Aleurites moluccanus, used in cooking, skincare, and traditional medicine, especially in Hawaii
