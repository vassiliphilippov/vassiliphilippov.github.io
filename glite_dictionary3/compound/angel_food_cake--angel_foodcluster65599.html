angel food cake

noun
light sponge cake without egg yolks
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəl fuːd keɪk / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəl fuːd keɪk / (BrE)
A light, airy sponge cake made without egg yolks, typically baked in a tube pan and known for its fluffy texture.

Examples

slice of angel food cake
Many people enjoy angel food cake because it is low in fat and very light.
