place bet

noun
horse racing bet on first or second place
Pronunciation: / pleɪs bɛt / (AmE), / pleɪs bɛt / (BrE)
A bet in horse racing in which the bettor wins if the chosen horse finishes first or second.

Examples

place bet on a horse
He decided to make a place bet on the favorite in the third race.
Her place bet paid off when her horse finished second.
If you want a safer wager, you might consider placing a place bet instead of a win bet.
