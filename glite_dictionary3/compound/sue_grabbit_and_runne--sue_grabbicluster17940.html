Sue, Grabbit and Runne

noun
humorous term for opportunists or dubious firms
Pronunciation: / suː ˈɡræbɪt ənd ˈrʌn / (AmE), / suː ˈɡræbɪt ənd ˈrʌn / (BrE)
a humorous phrase referring to someone who is opportunistic or acts hastily for personal gain, often used to describe dubious or unscrupulous businesses, especially fictional law firms

Examples

Sue, Grabbit and Runne law firm
