family Cestidae

noun
family of flattened, elongated ctenophores
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli sɛˈstaɪdi / (AmE), / ˈfæməli sɛsˈtiːdiː / (BrE)
A family of ctenophores characterized by greatly flattened and elongated bodies, coextensive with the order Cestida.
