herpes simplex

noun
herpes virus causing skin and nerve infection
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːpiːz ˈsɪm.plɛks / (AmE), / ˈhɜː.piːz ˈsɪm.pleks / (BrE)
a virus from the herpesvirus family that causes skin and nerve infections such as cold sores or genital herpes
