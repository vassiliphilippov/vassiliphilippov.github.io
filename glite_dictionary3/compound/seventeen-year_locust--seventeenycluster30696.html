seventeen-year locust

noun
periodical North American cicada
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛv.ənˌtiːn ˈjɪr ˈloʊ.kəst / (AmE), / ˌsɛv.ənˌtiːn ˈjɪə ˈləʊ.kəst / (BrE)
