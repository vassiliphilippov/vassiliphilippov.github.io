to ring the gong

verb
to signal an event by striking a gong
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ ðə ɡɑŋ / (AmE), / rɪŋ ðə ɡɒŋ / (BrE)
to strike a gong to signal or announce the start or end of an event

Examples

ring the gong to start
At exactly noon, the master of ceremonies rang the gong to mark the beginning of the festivities.
Everyone grew quiet as soon as Michael was chosen to ring the gong before the awards ceremony.
She rang the gong three times to indicate the end of the meditation session.

Related Terms

  • synonym – strike the bell: to make a ringing sound by hitting a bell, usually as a signal
