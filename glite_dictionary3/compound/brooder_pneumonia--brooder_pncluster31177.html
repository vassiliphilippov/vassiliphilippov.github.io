brooder pneumonia

noun
acute respiratory disease in young poultry
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdər pəˈmoʊniə / (AmE), / ˈbruːdə pəˈməʊniə / (BrE)
a severe respiratory disease occurring as an acute, rapidly fatal pneumonia in young chickens and turkeys
