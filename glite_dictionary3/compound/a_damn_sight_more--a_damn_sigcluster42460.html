a damn sight more

adverb
much more; a lot more (emphatic intensifier)
Pronunciation: / ə dæm saɪt mɔr / (AmE), / ə dæm saɪt mɔː / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is much greater in degree or amount than something else

Examples

a damn sight more difficult
He's a damn sight more talented than anyone else on the team.
The new phone is a damn sight more expensive than last year's model.
This book is a damn sight more interesting than I expected it to be.
