slip noose

noun
loop that tightens when pulled
Pronunciation: / slɪp nuːs / (AmE), / slɪp nuːs / (BrE)
A loop in a rope or cord formed with a slipknot, which tightens as the rope is pulled.

Examples

make a slip noose
He demonstrated how to make a slip noose for emergency situations.
Once pressure is applied, the slip noose tightens quickly around the object.
The trap was set using a slip noose to catch the small animal.
