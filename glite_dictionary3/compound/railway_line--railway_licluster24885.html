railway line

noun
route of tracks for trains
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪlˌweɪ laɪn / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ laɪn / (BrE)
A route consisting of railroad tracks and the underlying structure on which trains run.

Examples

new railway line
Construction of the railway line was delayed due to difficult terrain and weather conditions.
The new railway line between the two cities opened last year and quickly became popular with commuters.
They plan to upgrade the old railway line to support high-speed trains.
