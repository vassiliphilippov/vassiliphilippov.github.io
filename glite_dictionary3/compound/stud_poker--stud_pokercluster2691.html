stud poker

noun
a variety of poker with face-up and face-down cards
Pronunciation: / stʌd ˈpoʊkər / (AmE), / stʌd ˈpəʊkə / (BrE)
A type of poker in which each player receives some cards dealt face down (hole cards) and the rest face up, with rounds of betting after each card is dealt.

Examples

play stud poker with friends
He has been playing stud poker with his friends every Friday night for years.
Many casinos offer both stud poker and other poker variants at their tables.
Unlike Texas Hold'em, stud poker reveals some cards face up during the game.
