offshore drilling rig

noun
platform for extracting oil at sea
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌʃɔr ˈdrɪlɪŋ rɪg / (AmE), / ˈɒfʃɔː ˈdrɪlɪŋ rɪg / (BrE)
A large platform located at sea, used for extracting oil or gas from beneath the seabed.
