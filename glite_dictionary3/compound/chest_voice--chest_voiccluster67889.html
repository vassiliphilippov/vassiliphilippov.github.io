chest voice

noun
lower vocal register or range
Pronunciation: / tʃɛst vɔɪs / (AmE), / tʃɛst vɔɪs / (BrE)
the lower ranges of the human voice, typically used in speaking or singing and associated with resonance in the chest

Examples

sing in chest voice
Many pop singers use their chest voice to achieve a powerful sound.

Switching from chest voice to head voice can be challenging for beginners.
