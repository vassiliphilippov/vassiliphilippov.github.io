stomach flu

noun
illness causing vomiting and diarrhea
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmək fluː / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək fluː / (BrE)
an illness causing inflammation of the stomach and intestines, typically resulting in vomiting and diarrhea; gastroenteritis

Examples

catch stomach flu
Doctors recommend drinking plenty of fluids if you have the stomach flu.
My daughter stayed home from school because she caught the stomach flu last night.
The whole family came down with the stomach flu after the picnic.
