Chinese angelica

noun
plant used in Chinese medicine and cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪniz ænˈdʒɛlɪkə / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ænˈdʒɛlɪkə / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to China, used in traditional medicine and cooking; less prickly than the American angelica tree.
