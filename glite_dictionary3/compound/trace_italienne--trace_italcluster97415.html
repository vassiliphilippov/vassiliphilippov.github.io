trace italienne

noun
fortification style with triangular bastions
Pronunciation: / ˌtreɪs iˌtælˈjɛn / (AmE), / ˌtreɪs iˌtælˈjɛn / (BrE)
A style of fortification featuring many projecting triangular bastions, designed to provide overlapping fields of fire, first developed in Italy in the mid-15th century.
