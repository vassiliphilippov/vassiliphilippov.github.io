royal flush

noun
highest poker hand (A, K, Q, J, 10 same suit)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.əl flʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl flʌʃ / (BrE)
A poker hand consisting of the ace, king, queen, jack, and ten all in the same suit, regarded as the highest possible hand.
