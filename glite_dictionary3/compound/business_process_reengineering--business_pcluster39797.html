business process reengineering

noun
redesign of business workflows for improvement
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˈprɑːsɛs ˌriːɪnˈdʒɪnɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˈprəʊsɛs ˌriːɪnˈdʒɪnɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the examination and redesign of business workflows and processes to achieve significant improvements in performance, efficiency, and quality
