sand spurry

noun
prostrate weedy herb with pink flowers
Pronunciation: / sænd ˈspɜːri / (AmE), / sand ˈspɜːri / (BrE)
A small, prostrate weedy herb with tiny pink flowers, widespread across Europe and Asia, especially on sandy soils and coastal cliffs, also naturalized in parts of North America.

Examples

sand spurry grows wild
Gardeners consider sand spurry a weed due to its rapid spread in sandy soil.
