Marconi rig

noun
sailboat rig with triangular sails
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈkoʊni rɪɡ / (AmE), / mɑːˈkəʊni rɪɡ / (BrE)
A sailboat rig featuring triangular sails, typically with the mainsail hoisted on a tall, single mast without a gaff.

Examples

Marconi rig sailboat setup
Many modern sailboats use a Marconi rig because of its efficiency and simplicity.
Switching from a gaff rig to a Marconi rig improved the vessel's speed and handling.
The yacht was equipped with a Marconi rig for optimal performance in light winds.
