Cape tulip

noun
South African plant with cup-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / keɪp ˈtuːlɪp / (AmE), / keɪp ˈtjuːlɪp / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to South Africa, known for its large reddish-purple-barred leaves and coral-red, cup-shaped flowers with yellow stamens.
