ground pepper

noun
finely crushed pepper spice
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd ˈpɛpɚ / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
Peppercorns that have been crushed into a fine powder or small pieces, used as a spice in cooking.

Examples

ground pepper in cooking
I prefer using ground pepper instead of whole peppercorns for salad dressings.
She sprinkled a pinch of ground pepper onto her scrambled eggs.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of freshly ground pepper.
