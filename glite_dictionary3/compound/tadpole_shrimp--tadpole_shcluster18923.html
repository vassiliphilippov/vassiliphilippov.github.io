tadpole shrimp

noun
freshwater crustacean resembling a tadpole
Pronunciation: / ˈtædˌpoʊl ˌʃrɪmp / (AmE), / ˈtad.pəʊl ˌʃrɪmp / (BrE)
a small freshwater crustacean with a broad, shield-like carapace, resembling a tadpole, belonging to the order Notostraca
