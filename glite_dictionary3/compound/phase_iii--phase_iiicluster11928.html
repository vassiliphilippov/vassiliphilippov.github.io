phase III

noun
final large-scale clinical trial stage
Pronunciation: / feɪz ˌθriː / (AmE), / feɪz ˌθriː / (BrE)
The third stage of clinical trials for a treatment or drug, involving large-scale testing to confirm its effectiveness and monitor side effects before FDA approval.

Examples

phase III clinical trial
After passing phase III, the new medication was submitted for FDA approval.
Several drugs failed in phase III due to unexpected side effects.
The cancer treatment showed promising results during its phase III trials.
