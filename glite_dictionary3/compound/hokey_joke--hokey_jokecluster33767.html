hokey joke

noun
corny or overly sentimental joke
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊki ˌdʒoʊk / (AmE), / ˈhəʊki ˌdʒəʊk / (BrE)
A joke that is considered corny, silly, or overly sentimental, often eliciting groans rather than genuine laughter.

Examples

tell a hokey joke
He always tells a hokey joke at family gatherings to make everyone roll their eyes.
She laughed at his hokey joke, even though she usually found them silly.
The comedian's act was full of hokey jokes that relied on simple puns and old clichés.
