prince consort

noun
husband of a reigning queen
Pronunciation: / ˌprɪns kənˈsɔrt / (AmE), / ˌprɪns ˈkɒnsɔːt / (BrE)
The husband of a reigning female sovereign, holding the title of prince rather than king.

Examples

the prince consort of a queen
As prince consort, he played a significant role in the royal court.
Prince Albert was the most famous prince consort in British history.
The prince consort is not in the line of succession but holds a prominent public position.
