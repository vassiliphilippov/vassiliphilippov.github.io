ascending colon

noun
section of large intestine running upward
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛndɪŋ ˈkoʊlən / (AmE), / əˈsɛndɪŋ ˈkəʊlən / (BrE)
the section of the large intestine that runs upward from the cecum to the transverse colon
