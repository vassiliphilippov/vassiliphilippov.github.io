emergency meeting

noun
a sudden meeting for urgent issues
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrdʒənsi ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
A meeting called at short notice to discuss urgent or critical issues that require immediate attention.

Examples

call an emergency meeting
City officials scheduled an emergency meeting to respond to the growing public health concerns.
Due to the storm warning, an emergency meeting was called to coordinate evacuation plans.
The board held an emergency meeting late last night after the unexpected resignation of the CEO.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crisis meeting: a meeting called to address a crisis
  • antonym – regular meeting: a scheduled meeting as part of a routine
