intelligence operation

noun
covert mission to gather information
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˌɑːpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a secret activity carried out by a government or organization to gather information, especially about an enemy or potential threat

Examples

covert intelligence operation
An effective intelligence operation requires skilled analysts and advanced surveillance technology.
During the war, many intelligence operations were conducted behind enemy lines.
The agency launched a complex intelligence operation to infiltrate the criminal network.
