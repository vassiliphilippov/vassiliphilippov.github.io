Prince of Darkness

noun
another name for Satan, the chief evil spirit
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪns əv ˈdɑːrknəs / (AmE), / ˈprɪns əv ˈdɑːknəs / (BrE)
(in Judeo-Christian and Islamic belief) the chief spirit of evil; another name for Satan, the adversary of God and master of Hell.
