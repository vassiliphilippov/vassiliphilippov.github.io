unconscious process

noun
mental process outside conscious awareness
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɑn.ʃəs ˈprɑː.sɛs / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɒn.ʃəs ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
a mental process that takes place without one's direct awareness or conscious control
