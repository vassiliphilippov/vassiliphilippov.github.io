change of life

noun
menopause; end of menstrual cycles
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv laɪf / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv laɪf / (BrE)
the time in a woman's life when menstruation ends and she is no longer able to have children; menopause

Examples

the change of life for women
Her doctor explained that the change of life is a natural biological process.
