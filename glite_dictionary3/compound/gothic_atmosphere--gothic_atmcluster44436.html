Gothic atmosphere

noun
dark, mysterious, and eerie mood or setting
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθɪk ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪk ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
a dark, mysterious, and eerie mood or setting, especially characteristic of Gothic literature, architecture, or art

Examples

a Gothic atmosphere in a castle
Heavy shadows and old stone architecture contributed to the Gothic atmosphere of the cathedral.
The abandoned castle had a Gothic atmosphere that sent chills down my spine.
The novelist skillfully created a Gothic atmosphere through vivid descriptions and haunting imagery.
