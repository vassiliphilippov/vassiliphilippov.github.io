royal stag

noun
stag with 12+ branched antlers
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪəl stæɡ / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪəl staɡ / (BrE)
A stag with antlers that have 12 or more branches, considered a distinguished specimen among deer.

Examples

majestic royal stag
During the autumn rut, the royal stag attracted attention from all the photographers in the park.
Legends often tell of a magnificent royal stag roaming the ancient forests.
The hunter was thrilled to spot a royal stag among the herd in the highlands.
