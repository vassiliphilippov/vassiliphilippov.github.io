réchauffé version

noun
recycled or reused version
Pronunciation: / reɪˈʃoʊfeɪ ˈvɜrʒən / (AmE), / reɪˈʃɒfeɪ ˈvɜːʒən / (BrE)
a re-used or recycled version of something, especially lacking originality or freshness

Examples

rechauffe version of a trend
Audiences are quick to spot a réchauffé version of last year's trend.
Many critics dismissed the novel as a réchauffé version of classic detective stories.
The director's new film felt like a réchauffé version of his earlier work.
