os ischii

noun
lower, back section of hip bone
Pronunciation: / ɑs ˈɪskiˌaɪ / (AmE), / ɒs ˈɪskiːˌʌɪ / (BrE)
the lower and back part of the hip bone; one of the three sections that make up the pelvic bone, located below the ilium.
