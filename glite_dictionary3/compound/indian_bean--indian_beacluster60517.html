Indian bean

noun
tree (Catalpa) with long seed pods
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Catalpa with large heart-shaped leaves, white flowers, and long slender seed pods
