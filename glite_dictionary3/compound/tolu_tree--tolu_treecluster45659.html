tolu tree

noun
tropical tree yielding tolu balsam and hard wood
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊluˌ triː / (AmE), / ˈtəʊluː ˌtriː / (BrE)
A medium-sized tropical American tree (Myroxylon balsamum) that yields tolu balsam and fragrant, hard wood used in high-quality furniture and cabinetwork.

Examples

tolu tree resin
The fragrant resin from the tolu tree has been used in perfumes and traditional medicine.
The wood from the tolu tree is prized for making fine furniture and cabinetry.
Tolu balsam is harvested from the trunk of the tolu tree in South America.
