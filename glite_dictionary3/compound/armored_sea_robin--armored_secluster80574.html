armored sea robin

noun
fish with bony scutes and chin barbels
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmərd si ˈrɑbɪn / (AmE), / ˈɑːməd siː ˈrɒbɪn / (BrE)
a type of sea robin fish with bony plates (scutes) on its body and barbels on its chin, typically found along the continental slope
