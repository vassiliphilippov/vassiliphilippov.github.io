sea horse

noun
small marine fish with horse-like head
Pronunciation: / ˈsi ˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌhɔːs / (BrE)
a small marine fish with a horse-like head, upright swimming posture, and a curled tail, belonging to the genus Hippocampus

Examples

tiny sea horse swimming
A sea horse camouflaged itself among the seaweed near the coral reef.
Children were fascinated by the tiny sea horses swimming upright in the aquarium.
Some species of sea horse are endangered due to overfishing and habitat loss.
