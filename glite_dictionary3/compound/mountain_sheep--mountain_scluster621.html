mountain sheep

noun
wild sheep inhabiting mountains
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən ʃiːp / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ʃiːp / (BrE)
any wild sheep that lives in mountainous regions, such as the bighorn sheep or mouflon.
