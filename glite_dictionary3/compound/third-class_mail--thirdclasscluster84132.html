third-class mail

noun
mail category for printed matter at reduced rates
Pronunciation: / ˌθɝd ˌklæs ˈmeɪl / (AmE), / ˌθɜːd ˌklɑːs ˈmeɪl / (BrE)
a category of mail consisting of printed matter that is eligible for reduced postal rates

Examples
