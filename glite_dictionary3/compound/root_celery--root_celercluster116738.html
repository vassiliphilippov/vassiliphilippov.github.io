root celery

noun
variety of celery with edible root
Pronunciation: / ruːt ˈsɛləri / (AmE), / ruːt ˈsɛləri / (BrE)
a variety of celery grown for its large, round, edible root rather than its stalks or leaves; also called celeriac.
