tourist trap

noun
overpriced place for tourists
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrɪst træp / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst træp / (BrE)
A place that attracts tourists and charges them too much money, often offering products or experiences of low value or authenticity.

Examples

busy tourist trap street
Locals warned us to avoid that tourist trap near the beach.
The city's main square has become something of a tourist trap over the years.
We realized too late that the souvenir shop was just a tourist trap.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rip-off: something that is unreasonably expensive or not worth its price
