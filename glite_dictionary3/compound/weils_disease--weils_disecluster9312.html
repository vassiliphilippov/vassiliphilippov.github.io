Weil's disease

noun
severe form of leptospirosis in humans
Pronunciation: / waɪlz dɪˈziz / (AmE), / waɪlz dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
a severe, sometimes fatal form of leptospirosis in humans, characterized by jaundice, fever, and kidney damage, caused by infection with Leptospira bacteria
