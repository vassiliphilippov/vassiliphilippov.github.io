complex preposition

noun
multi-word prepositional phrase acting as one unit
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.plɛks ˌprɛp.əˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.plɛks ˌprɛp.əˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a preposition consisting of two or more words that function together as a single prepositional unit, such as 'in spite of' or 'according to'
