tourist court

noun
roadside hotel with direct room access
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrɪst kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtʊərɪst kɔːt / (BrE)
a roadside hotel or group of cabins where guests can park their car near their room, especially common in the early and mid-20th century
