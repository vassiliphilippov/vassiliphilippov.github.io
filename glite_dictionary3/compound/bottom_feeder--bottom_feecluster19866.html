bottom feeder

noun
person profiting from others' misfortune
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəm ˌfidər / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm ˌfiːdə / (BrE)
a person who tries to gain something by taking advantage of others or bad situations, often in a dishonest or unethical way
