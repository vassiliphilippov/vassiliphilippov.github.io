gin rummy

noun
a card game; variant of rummy
Pronunciation: / dʒɪn ˈrʌmi / (AmE), / dʒɪn ˈrʌmi / (BrE)
A card game in which players try to form sets and runs, and can end the game if the cards left in their hand total less than ten points.

Do you want to join us for a game of gin rummy after dinner?

We played gin rummy every evening during our vacation.
