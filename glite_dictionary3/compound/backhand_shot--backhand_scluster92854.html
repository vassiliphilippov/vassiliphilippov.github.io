backhand shot

noun
stroke with back of hand facing direction
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌhænd ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈbakhænd ʃɒt / (BrE)
a stroke in tennis or other sports played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke

Examples

powerful backhand shot

Practicing your backhand shot can greatly improve your tennis game.
