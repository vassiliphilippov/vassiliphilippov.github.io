accident of birth

noun
circumstance one is born into, not chosen
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.sɪ.dənt əv bɝːθ / (AmE), / ˈæk.sɪ.dənt əv bɜːθ / (BrE)
a circumstance or condition, such as social status or nationality, into which a person is born, that affects their life or future opportunities and over which they have no control
