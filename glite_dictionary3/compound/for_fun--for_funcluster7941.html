for fun

adverb
not seriously; as a joke
Pronunciation: / fər fʌn / (AmE), / fə fʌn / (BrE)
Not intended seriously; meant as a joke or for enjoyment.

Examples

do it for fun
They dressed up as pirates for fun at the party.
They only told that story for fun, not to hurt anyone's feelings.
We tried skydiving just for fun and ended up loving it.
