throw of the dice

noun
risky or uncertain decision
Pronunciation: / θɹoʊ əv ðə daɪs / (AmE), / θrəʊ əv ðə daɪs / (BrE)
an action or decision involving risk or uncertainty, with outcomes largely dependent on chance

Examples

a throw of the dice decision
For many entrepreneurs, launching a startup is a real throw of the dice.
Her decision to invest all her savings in the project was a huge throw of the dice.
Taking the job offer in another country felt like a throw of the dice to him.

Related Terms

  • antonym – certainty: something that is known for sure or has no risk
  • synonym – gamble: an act of risking something with an uncertain outcome
