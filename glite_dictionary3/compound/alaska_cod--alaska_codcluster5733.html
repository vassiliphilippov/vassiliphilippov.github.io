Alaska cod

noun
fish species related to Atlantic cod
Pronunciation: / əˈlæskə kɑd / (AmE), / əˈlæskə kɒd / (BrE)
A fish species closely related to the Atlantic cod, found in the northern Pacific Ocean, commonly used as food.

Examples

Alaska cod fillet
Many seafood restaurants serve Alaska cod in their fish and chips dishes.
The population of Alaska cod has been carefully monitored by marine biologists.
Unlike Atlantic cod, Alaska cod is primarily found in the northern Pacific Ocean.
