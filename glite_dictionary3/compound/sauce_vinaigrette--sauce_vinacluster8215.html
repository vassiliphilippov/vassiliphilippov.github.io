sauce vinaigrette

noun
cold salad dressing with oil, vinegar, mustard, and garlic
Pronunciation: / soʊs vɪnəˈɡrɛt / (AmE), / sɔːs vɪnəˈɡrɛt / (BrE)
A cold sauce made from oil and vinegar, typically mixed with mustard and garlic, used as a salad dressing.

Examples

sauce vinaigrette salad dressing
A traditional sauce vinaigrette often includes Dijon mustard and minced garlic for extra flavor.
He whisked olive oil and red wine vinegar to make a simple sauce vinaigrette for the greens.
She dressed the salad with a light sauce vinaigrette to complement the fresh vegetables.
