unskilled person

noun
person lacking technical training
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskɪld ˈpɜrsən / (AmE), / ʌnˈskɪld ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who lacks specialized training, expertise, or technical skills for a particular task or job.

Examples

an unskilled person at work
It can be difficult for an unskilled person to find a well-paying job in today's economy.
Many factories hire unskilled person for tasks that do not require technical expertise.
The workshop was designed so that even an unskilled person could operate the equipment safely.
