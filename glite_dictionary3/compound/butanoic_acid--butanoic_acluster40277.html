butanoic acid

noun
a fatty acid found in butter
Pronunciation: / bjuːˈteɪnoʊɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / bjuːˈteɪnəʊɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
a fatty acid with an unpleasant odor, found in butter and some other fats

Examples

butanoic acid in butter
Cheddar cheese owes some of its distinctive flavor to butanoic acid.
