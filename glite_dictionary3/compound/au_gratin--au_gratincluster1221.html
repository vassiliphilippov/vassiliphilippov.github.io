au gratin

adjective
cooked with browned breadcrumbs and/or cheese
Pronunciation: / oʊ ˈɡrɑːtən / (AmE), / əʊ ˈɡrætæn / (BrE)
(of food) covered with browned breadcrumbs and sometimes cheese, then baked or broiled.

Examples

au gratin potatoes
He prepared a delicious cauliflower au gratin for dinner last night.
The potatoes were served au gratin with a crispy golden topping.
You can make almost any vegetable taste better by baking it au gratin.
