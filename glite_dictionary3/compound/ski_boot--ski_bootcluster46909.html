ski boot

noun
boot fastened to a ski
Pronunciation: / ski buːt / (AmE), / ski buːt / (BrE)
A stiff, supportive boot designed to be secured to a ski by a ski binding, used in skiing.

Examples

ski boot fitting
He adjusted his ski boots before heading to the slopes.
Make sure your ski boot fits well to avoid foot pain during skiing.
Modern ski boots provide excellent ankle support and warmth.
