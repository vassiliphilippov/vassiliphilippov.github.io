fibrocystic breast disease

noun
benign breast tissue condition with cysts
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪ.broʊˈsɪs.tɪk brɛst dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ˌfʌɪ.brə(ʊ)ˈsɪs.tɪk brɛst dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
