dumping ground

noun
place for discarding waste
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmpɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈdʌmpɪŋ ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
a place where unwanted items or waste are discarded, often in an uncontrolled or unauthorized manner

Examples

illegal dumping ground
Some people treat the countryside as a dumping ground for their unwanted belongings.
The empty lot soon became a dumping ground for old appliances and rubbish.
The local council is working to clear up the illegal dumping ground near the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dump: a site for depositing rubbish, typically one that is not officially sanctioned
  • synonym – landfill: a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
