field goal

noun
basket scored in basketball during play
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ɡoʊl / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ɡəʊl / (BrE)
a basket scored in basketball by a player throwing the ball through the opponent’s hoop during regular play, excluding free throws

Examples

field goal percentage
His accuracy with the field goal has improved this season.
She sank the winning field goal just before the buzzer sounded.
The team struggled to make a single field goal in the first quarter.
