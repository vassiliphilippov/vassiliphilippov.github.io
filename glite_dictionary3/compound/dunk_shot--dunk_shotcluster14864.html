dunk shot

noun
basketball shot thrust downward into the hoop
Pronunciation: / dʌŋk ʃɑt / (AmE), / dʌŋk ʃɒt / (BrE)
A basketball shot in which the ball is thrust downwards into the hoop with one or both hands.

Examples

slam dunk shot
He thrilled the crowd with a spectacular dunk shot in the fourth quarter.
Practicing your dunk shot can greatly improve your presence near the basket.
The player leapt high above the defenders and finished with a powerful dunk shot.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slam dunk: a powerful basketball shot in which the ball is slammed through the hoop from above the rim
