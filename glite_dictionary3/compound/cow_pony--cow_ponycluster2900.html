cow pony

noun
small horse used for herding cattle
Pronunciation: / kaʊ ˈpoʊni / (AmE), / kaʊ ˈpəʊni / (BrE)
a small, agile saddle horse trained for herding cattle, typically used by cowboys or ranchers

Examples

cow pony herds cattle
Experienced riders know that a well-trained cow pony is essential for managing a large herd.
She learned how to rope and ride on her family's old cow pony.
The rancher rode his trusted cow pony across the open plains to gather stray cattle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cutting horse: a horse specially trained to separate cattle from a herd
