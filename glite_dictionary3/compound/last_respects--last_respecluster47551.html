last respects

noun
expression of respect for the dead
Pronunciation: / læst rɪˈspɛkts / (AmE), / lɑːst rɪˈspɛkts / (BrE)
the act of showing honor or expressing respect to someone who has died, usually at a funeral or memorial service

Examples

Family and friends gathered quietly to show their last respects before the burial.
He traveled across the country to offer his last respects at his friend's funeral.
Many people came to the church to pay their last respects to the late mayor.
