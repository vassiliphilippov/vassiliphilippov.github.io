takeout food

noun
food to be eaten off the premises
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkˌaʊt fuːd / (AmE), / ˈteɪkaʊt fuːd / (BrE)
food prepared by a restaurant or store to be eaten elsewhere rather than on the premises

Examples

order takeout food
After the meeting, everyone grabbed some takeout food to eat back at the office.
The city offers a wide variety of takeout food options, from pizza to Thai cuisine.
We ordered takeout food because we didn't feel like cooking last night.
