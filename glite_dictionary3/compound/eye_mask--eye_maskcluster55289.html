eye mask

noun
mask covering area around the eyes
Pronunciation: / aɪ mæsk / (AmE), / aɪ mɑːsk / (BrE)
a mask that covers the area around the eyes, often with holes for the eyes, used for sleeping or as part of a costume

Examples

The actor removed his eye mask after the performance.
