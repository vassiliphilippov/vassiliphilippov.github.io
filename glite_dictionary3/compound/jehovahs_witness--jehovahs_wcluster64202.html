Jehovah's Witness

noun
member of a Christian religious group
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈhoʊvəz ˈwɪtnəs / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhəʊvəz ˈwɪtnəs / (BrE)
A member of a Christian religious movement known for its belief in the imminent end of the world and for its active evangelism.

Examples

a Jehovah's Witness visits homes
A Jehovah's Witness may decline a blood transfusion for religious reasons.
