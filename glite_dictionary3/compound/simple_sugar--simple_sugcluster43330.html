simple sugar

noun
basic form of sugar; monosaccharide
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəl ˈʃʊɡər / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl ˈʃʊɡə / (BrE)
a sugar that cannot be broken down into simpler sugars and is the most basic form of carbohydrate, such as glucose or fructose

Examples

simple sugar like glucose
Fructose is another type of simple sugar that is naturally present in honey.
Glucose is a common simple sugar found in many fruits and vegetables.
Some sports drinks contain simple sugars to provide quick energy during exercise.
