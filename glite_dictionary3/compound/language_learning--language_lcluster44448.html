language learning

noun
process of acquiring a language
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ ˈlɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ ˈlɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of acquiring the ability to understand, speak, read, or write a language, typically one that is not one's native tongue
