vacuum aspiration

noun
abortion procedure using suction
Pronunciation: / ˈvæk.jum ˌæspəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈvæk.juːm ˌæspɪˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a medical procedure for inducing abortion in early pregnancy, in which suction is applied to the dilated cervix to remove the embryo and placenta.

Examples

vacuum aspiration procedure
Complications from vacuum aspiration are rare when performed by trained medical professionals.
She underwent vacuum aspiration as a safe method for ending an unplanned pregnancy.
The doctor explained that vacuum aspiration is often used in early pregnancy terminations.
