adverbial of cause

noun
adverbial indicating reason for an action
Pronunciation: / ædˈvɝː.bi.əl əv kɔz / (AmE), / ədˈvɜː.bi.əl əv kɔːz / (BrE)
an adverbial phrase or clause that indicates the reason why an action is performed

Examples

adverbial of cause in a sentence
In the sentence 'He apologized because he was late,' 'because he was late' is an adverbial of cause.
