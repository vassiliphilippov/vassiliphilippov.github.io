interior door

noun
door within a building
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɪriər dɔr / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɪəriə dɔː / (BrE)
a door that separates rooms inside a building, as opposed to an exterior door

Examples

interior door between rooms
She closed the interior door to block out the noise from the hallway.
The contractor recommended replacing every interior door for a more modern look.
The paint color on this interior door matches the trim perfectly.
