arteria labialis superior

noun
artery supplying the upper lip
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪriə læˈbeɪliɪs suːˈpɪəriɚ / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪərɪə læˈbeɪlɪɪs suːˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
A branch of the facial artery that supplies blood to the upper lip.
