fairy cup

noun
a North American woodland plant; miterwort
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ˌkʌp / (BrE)
A small perennial plant of northeastern North America, usually with two opposite leaves on upright stems that produce racemes of white flowers; also known as miterwort.

Examples

blooming fairy cup plant
Many gardeners cherish the fairy cup for its tiny white blossoms and unique foliage.

We stopped to admire a patch of fairy cups growing beneath the trees.
