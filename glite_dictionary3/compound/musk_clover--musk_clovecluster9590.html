musk clover

noun
a musky-scented annual European herb
Pronunciation: / mʌsk ˈkloʊvɚ / (AmE), / mʌsk ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
a low-growing annual European herb of the genus Medicago, naturalized in America, with a musky scent, resembling alfilaria
