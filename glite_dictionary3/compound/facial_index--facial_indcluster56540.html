facial index

noun
ratio of face width to height (in percent)
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪʃəl ˈɪndɛks / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ˈɪndɛks / (BrE)
the ratio, expressed as a percentage, of the maximum width of the face to its maximum height

Examples

facial index measurement
A lower facial index indicates a longer, narrower face.
Anthropologists often use the facial index to classify skull shapes.
The doctor calculated the facial index from the patient's measurements.
