plate armour

noun
hardened steel protective plating
Pronunciation: / pleɪt ˈɑrmər / (AmE), / pleɪt ˈɑːmə / (BrE)
specially hardened steel plates used to protect fortifications or vehicles from enemy fire

Examples

plate armour for tanks
Advancements in plate armour made military vehicles much harder to penetrate during the war.
Engineers reinforced the bunker with additional plate armour for greater protection against explosions.
The tank's plate armour was designed to withstand heavy artillery fire.
