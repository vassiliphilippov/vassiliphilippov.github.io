chigoe flea

noun
tropical flea that burrows under skin
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪɡoʊ ˌfli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪɡəʊ ˌfliː / (BrE)
a small tropical flea (Tunga penetrans) whose fertile female burrows under the skin of humans and other mammals, causing irritation or infection
