bush baby

noun
small nocturnal African primate
Pronunciation: / bʊʃ ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / bʊʃ ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
An agile, long-tailed nocturnal primate found in Africa, known for its large eyes, ears, and dense woolly fur; also called a galago.

Examples

bush baby in Africa
Many tourists hope to spot a bush baby while on safari in East Africa.
Researchers observed a bush baby feeding on insects and fruit during the night.
The bush baby leaps effortlessly through the trees, using its strong hind legs.
