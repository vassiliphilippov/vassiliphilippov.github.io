street name

noun
brokerage firm's name holding securities
Pronunciation: / ˈstrit ˌneɪm / (AmE), / ˈstriːt ˌneɪm / (BrE)
the name of a brokerage firm in which securities are held on behalf of a customer, rather than in the customer's own name

Examples

held in street name
Holding securities in street name simplifies transactions and transfers.
If your stock is in street name, the brokerage manages all paperwork for you.
Most investors have their shares registered in street name for convenience.
