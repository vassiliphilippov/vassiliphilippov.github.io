heel counter

noun
supportive back part of a shoe or boot
Pronunciation: / hil ˈkaʊntɚ / (AmE), / hiːl ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
a piece of material, often leather or plastic, forming and reinforcing the back part of a shoe or boot to support the heel

High-quality boots often have a sturdy heel counter to provide extra stability for the foot.
