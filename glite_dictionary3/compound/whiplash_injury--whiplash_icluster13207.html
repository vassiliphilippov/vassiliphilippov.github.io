whiplash injury

noun
neck injury from sudden motion
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpˌlæʃ ˈɪndʒɚi / (AmE), / ˈwɪpˌlæʃ ˈɪndʒəri / (BrE)
an injury to the neck caused by sudden, forceful movement of the head, often from a car accident or similar impact
