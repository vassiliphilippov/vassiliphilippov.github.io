Grevy's zebra

noun
species of zebra with narrow stripes
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɛviz ˈziːbrə / (AmE), / ˈɡrɛviz ˈziːbrə / (BrE)
A large African zebra species (Equus grevyi) characterized by narrow, closely set stripes and a white belly, native to eastern Africa.
