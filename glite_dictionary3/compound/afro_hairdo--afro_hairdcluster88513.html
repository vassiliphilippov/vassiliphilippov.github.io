Afro hairdo

noun
rounded, thickly curled hairstyle
Pronunciation: / ˈæfroʊ ˈhɛrdu / (AmE), / ˈæfrəʊ ˈheəduː / (BrE)
A hairstyle in which the hair is allowed to grow naturally outward in a rounded, thickly curled shape.
