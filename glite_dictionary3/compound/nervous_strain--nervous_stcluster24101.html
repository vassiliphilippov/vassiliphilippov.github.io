nervous strain

noun
nervousness from mental stress
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːvəs streɪn / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvəs streɪn / (BrE)
A state of nervousness or tension caused by prolonged mental stress or pressure.

Examples
