hemlock tree

noun
evergreen coniferous tree (genus Tsuga)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛm.lɑk ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈhɛmlɒk ˌtriː / (BrE)
An evergreen coniferous tree of the genus Tsuga, native to North America and Asia, characterized by flat needles and small cones.
