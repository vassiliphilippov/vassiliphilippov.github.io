sockeye salmon

noun
a Pacific salmon with red flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːkaɪ ˈsæmən / (AmE), / ˈsɒkaɪ ˈsæmən / (BrE)
a species of salmon (Oncorhynchus nerka) native to northern Pacific rivers, known for its red flesh and valued as food; adults die after spawning
