literary festival

noun
event celebrating literature and authors
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬əˌreri ˈfɛstəvəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəri ˈfɛstəvəl / (BrE)
An event at which literature, authors, writers, and books are celebrated, often involving readings, discussions, and book signings.

Examples

annual literary festival
Many publishers unveil new books during the spring literary festival each year.
She met her favorite poet during a panel discussion at the literary festival.
The city hosted its annual literary festival, attracting writers and readers from across the country.
