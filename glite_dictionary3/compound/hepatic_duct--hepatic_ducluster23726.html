hepatic duct

noun
bile duct from the liver
Pronunciation: / hɪˈpætɪk dʌkt / (AmE), / hɪˈpætɪk dʌkt / (BrE)
a duct that carries bile from the liver to the gallbladder and small intestine

Examples

blocked hepatic duct
Blockage of the hepatic duct can cause serious liver problems.
The surgeon carefully examined the hepatic duct during the procedure.
The ultrasound revealed a stone lodged in the hepatic duct.
