Admiralty Metal

noun
tin-containing brass resistant to seawater
Pronunciation: / ˈædmɪrælti ˈmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈædmɪrəlti ˈmɛtəl / (BrE)
A type of alpha-beta brass alloy containing tin, designed to resist corrosion in seawater; used especially in marine engineering.
