trundle bed

noun
low bed stored under another bed
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌndəl bɛd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌndəl bɛd / (BrE)
A low bed on wheels or casters that can be stored under a higher bed and pulled out for use.

Examples

trundle bed under another bed
She bought a trundle bed so her friends could easily stay over.
The children's room has a trundle bed to save space during the day.
We pulled the trundle bed out from under the main bed for the guest to sleep on.
