junior school

noun
British school for children aged 7-11
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːniər skuːl / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːniə skuːl / (BrE)
(in Britain) a school for children aged between 7 and 11, typically following infant school and preceding secondary school.
