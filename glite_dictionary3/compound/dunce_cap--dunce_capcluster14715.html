dunce cap

noun
cone-shaped hat used as school punishment
Pronunciation: / dʌns kæp / (AmE), / dʌns kap / (BrE)
a cone-shaped paper hat, traditionally used as a punishment for slow or lazy pupils in schools

Examples

wear a dunce cap
In old classrooms, the slowest student was sometimes forced to wear the dunce cap as a punishment.
She read a story about a boy embarrassed by the dunce cap during his school days.
The teacher placed a dunce cap on the desk as a reminder not to misbehave.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dunce: a person who is slow to learn; a slow-witted or stupid person
