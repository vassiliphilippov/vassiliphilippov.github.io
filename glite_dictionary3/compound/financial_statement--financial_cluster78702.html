financial statement

noun
official record of financial accounts
Pronunciation: / fəˈnænʃəl ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / faɪˈnænʃl ˈsteɪtmənt /; / faɪˈnanʃəl ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an official document or record that summarizes the financial position and activities of a business or person, including details like income, expenses, assets, and liabilities
