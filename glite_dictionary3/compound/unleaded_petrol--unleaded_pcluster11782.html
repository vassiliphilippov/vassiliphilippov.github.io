unleaded petrol

noun
petrol without added lead compounds
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɛdɪd ˈpɛtroʊl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɛdɪd ˈpɛtrəl / (BrE)
petrol that does not contain added lead compounds, used to fuel vehicles in order to reduce air pollution.

Examples

fill up with unleaded petrol
Most modern cars are designed to run on unleaded petrol instead of leaded varieties.
She stopped at the station and filled her tank with unleaded petrol before heading out of town.
The transition to unleaded petrol has helped reduce air pollution in cities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – leaded petrol: petrol containing added lead compounds
  • synonym – unleaded gasoline: gasoline that does not contain added lead compounds
