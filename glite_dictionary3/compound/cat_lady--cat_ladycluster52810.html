cat lady

noun
woman who owns many cats, often eccentric
Pronunciation: / kæt ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / kæt ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
a woman who owns many cats, especially to an unusual degree, and is often regarded as eccentric or socially awkward
