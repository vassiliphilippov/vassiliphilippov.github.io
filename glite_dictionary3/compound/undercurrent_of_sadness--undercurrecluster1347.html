undercurrent of sadness

noun
subtle hidden feeling of sorrow
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌkɜːr(ə)nt əv ˈsædnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkʌrənt ɒv ˈsædnəs / (BrE)
a hidden or subtle feeling of sorrow that is present beneath the surface of someone's mood or a situation
