drawing room

noun
formal entertaining room in a house
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪŋ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ ruːm / (BrE)
a formal room in a house where visitors are received and entertained

Examples

elegant drawing room
After dinner, the ladies retired to the drawing room while the men remained at the table.
Guests were shown into the drawing room for tea and conversation.
The manor's drawing room was elegantly decorated with antique furniture.
