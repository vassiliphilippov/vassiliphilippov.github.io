anseriform bird

noun
web-footed swimming bird (Anseriformes)
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.sɚ.ɪ.fɔːrm bɝːd / (AmE), / ˈan.sər.ɪ.fɔːm bɜːd / (BrE)
