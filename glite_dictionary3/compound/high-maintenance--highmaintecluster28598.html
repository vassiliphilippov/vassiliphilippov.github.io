high-maintenance

adjective
requiring a lot of attention or care
Pronunciation: / haɪˈmeɪntənəns / (AmE), / haɪˈmeɪntənəns / (BrE)
Requiring a lot of attention, care, or effort to satisfy or maintain.

Examples

a high-maintenance person
Her new puppy is adorable but definitely high-maintenance and needs constant care.
I don't want a high-maintenance garden that requires daily watering and pruning.
Some people find luxury cars too high-maintenance for their busy lifestyles.
