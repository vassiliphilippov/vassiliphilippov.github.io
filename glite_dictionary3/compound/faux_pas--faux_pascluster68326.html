faux pas

noun
social blunder; tactless act
Pronunciation: / foʊ ˈpɑ / (AmE), / fəʊ ˈpɑː / (BrE)
an embarrassing or tactless act or remark in a social situation

Examples

commit a faux pas
He made a faux pas by mentioning her ex-husband at the dinner table.
She quickly apologized for her faux pas during the meeting.
Wearing jeans to the black-tie event was considered a major faux pas.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blunder: a careless or stupid mistake
  • antonym – civility: formal politeness in behavior or speech
  • synonym – gaffe: an unintentional act or remark causing embarrassment
