retirement plan

noun
plan to decommission systems
Pronunciation: / r\u026a\u02c8ta\u026a\u0259rm\u0259nt pl\u00e6n / (AmE), / r\u026a\u02c8ta\u026a\u0259m\u0259nt pl\u00e6n / (BrE)
a plan for taking hardware, software, or systems out of service and managing their decommissioning or disposal within an organization.

Examples

retirement plan for equipment
The IT department drafted a retirement plan for servers scheduled to be decommissioned next year.
Before upgrading applications, the team reviewed each project's retirement plan to avoid data-loss surprises.
A clear retirement plan helped budget the costs of replacing outdated network equipment this quarter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – decommission: to take a system, equipment, or service out of active operation
  • synonym – decommissioning plan: a plan to remove equipment, systems, or services from active use
