pouched mouse

noun
small marsupial resembling a mouse
Pronunciation: / paʊtʃt maʊs / (AmE), / paʊtʃt maʊs / (BrE)
Any of several small, sharp-nosed insectivorous marsupials that superficially resemble mice or rats.
