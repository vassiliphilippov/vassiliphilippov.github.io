family Staphylinidae

noun
family of beetles; rove beetles
Pronunciation: / ˌstæfɪˈlɪnɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˌstæfɪlɪˈnɪːdiː / (BrE)
A large family of small, slender, and active beetles commonly known as rove beetles, characterized by very short wing covers.
