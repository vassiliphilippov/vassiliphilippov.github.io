lesser spearwort

noun
semiaquatic Eurasian plant with spear-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛsər ˈspɪrˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈlɛsə ˈspɪəwɔːt / (BrE)
A semiaquatic Eurasian perennial plant of the buttercup family, with spear-shaped leaves, naturalized in New Zealand.

Examples

Botanists easily identified the lesser spearwort by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
