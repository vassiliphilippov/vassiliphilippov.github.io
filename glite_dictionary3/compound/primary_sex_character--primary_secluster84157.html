primary sex character

noun
sex organs directly involved in reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪˌmɛri sɛks ˈkærəktɚ / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪməri sɛks ˈkarɪktə / (BrE)
A genetically determined sex characteristic directly related to the reproductive organs and genitals, distinguishing the biological sexes.
