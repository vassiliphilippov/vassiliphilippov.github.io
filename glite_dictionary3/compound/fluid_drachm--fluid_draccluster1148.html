fluid drachm

noun
apothecary unit of liquid measure
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːɪd dræm / (AmE), / ˈfluːɪd dræm / (BrE)
a small unit of liquid measurement used in pharmacy or the apothecary system, equal to about 3.6 milliliters
