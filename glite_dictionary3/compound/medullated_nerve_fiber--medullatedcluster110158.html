medullated nerve fiber

noun
nerve fiber with myelin sheath
Pronunciation: / məˈdʌlˌeɪtɪd nɜːrv ˈfaɪbər / (AmE), / mɪˈdʌl.jeɪ.tɪd nɜːv ˈfaɪ.bə / (BrE)
