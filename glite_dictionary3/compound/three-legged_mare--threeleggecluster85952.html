three-legged mare

noun
a horse with three legs; used metaphorically
Pronunciation: / ˈθriˌlɛɡɪd mɛr / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌlɛɡɪd mɛə / (BrE)
A horse with three legs, often used metaphorically to refer to something that is handicapped, imperfect, or difficult.

Examples

ride a three-legged mare
Despite the odds, he chose to ride the three-legged mare to prove his determination.
