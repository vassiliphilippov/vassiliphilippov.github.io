American basswood

noun
a large North American shade tree
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən ˈbæs.wʊd / (AmE), / əˈmɛɹ.ɪ.kən ˈbæs.wʊd / (BrE)
A large North American tree with a broad, rounded crown and large, dark green leaves, valued for its shade and soft wood.
