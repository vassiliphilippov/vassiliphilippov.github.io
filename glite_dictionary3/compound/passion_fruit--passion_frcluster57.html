passion fruit

noun
tropical fruit with sweet-tart flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈpæʃən fruːt /; / ˈpæʃən frut / (AmE), / ˈpæʃən fruːt / (BrE)
a small, tropical fruit with a tough rind and sweet-tart, aromatic pulp, produced by passionflower vines and used in desserts and drinks
