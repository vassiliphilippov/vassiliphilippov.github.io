grammar cop

noun
person who strictly corrects grammar
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræmɚ kɑp / (AmE), / ˈɡræmə kɒp / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who habitually corrects others' grammar or language usage, often in a strict or pedantic manner

Examples

the grammar cop corrects mistakes
As the group’s resident grammar cop, Mark never misses an error.
Don't be such a grammar cop when people make small mistakes in emails.
Sometimes being a grammar cop makes conversations less enjoyable for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pedant: a person excessively concerned with minor details or rules, especially in learning
  • synonym – stickler: a person who insists on a certain quality or type of behavior, especially correctness
