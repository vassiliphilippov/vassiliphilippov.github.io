electric eye

noun
device for detecting light or radiation
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk aɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk aɪ / (BrE)
A device that detects and measures light or other radiation, often used for automatic control or security purposes.

Examples

electric eye security system
He installed an electric eye in the mailbox to let him know when mail is delivered.
The automatic door uses an electric eye to sense when someone is approaching.
The museum's alarms are triggered by an electric eye if anyone enters after hours.

  • synonym – photocell: device that generates an electric current when exposed to light
