speed limit

noun
maximum legal driving speed
Pronunciation: / spid ˈlɪmɪt / (AmE), / spiːd ˈlɪmɪt / (BrE)
the maximum speed at which a vehicle is legally permitted to travel on a particular road

Examples

speed limit on highways
He was fined for exceeding the speed limit in a residential area.
New road signs were installed to remind drivers of the speed limit.
The speed limit on this highway is 65 miles per hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym – maximum speed: the greatest speed allowed or possible
  • antonym – minimum speed: the lowest speed permitted or possible
