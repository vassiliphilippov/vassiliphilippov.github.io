lido deck

noun
open deck with a swimming pool on a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈliːdoʊ dɛk / (AmE), / ˈliːdəʊ dɛk / (BrE)
an open deck on a passenger ship or cruise liner, usually including a swimming pool, used for sunbathing and relaxation
