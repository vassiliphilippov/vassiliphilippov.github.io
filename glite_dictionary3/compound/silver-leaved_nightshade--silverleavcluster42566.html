silver-leaved nightshade

noun
a weedy plant with silvery leaves and colorful flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər liːvd ˈnaɪtˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə liːvd ˈnaɪtʃeɪd / (BrE)
a weedy plant with silvery leaves, violet, blue, or white flowers, and round berries, native to central United States and South America, often used to curdle milk
