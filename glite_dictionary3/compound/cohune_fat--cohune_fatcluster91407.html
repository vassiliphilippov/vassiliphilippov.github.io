cohune fat

noun
fat from cohune palm nuts
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhuːn fæt / (AmE), / kəˈhuːn fat / (BrE)
A semisolid fat obtained from the nuts of the cohune palm, used chiefly in cooking and soap making.

Examples

cohune fat in cooking
Soap makers favor cohune fat for its creamy texture and natural moisturizing properties.
The chef used cohune fat as a substitute for lard in the traditional recipe.
