paint roller

noun
tool for evenly spreading paint
Pronunciation: / peɪnt ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / peɪnt ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a cylindrical tool with an absorbent surface, used to apply paint evenly to large flat areas

Examples

use a paint roller on walls
A paint roller makes painting ceilings much easier than using a brush.
After dipping the paint roller into the tray, she rolled it back and forth to remove excess paint.
He used a paint roller to quickly cover the wall with a fresh coat of blue.

Related Terms

  • antonym – paintbrush: a brush for applying paint
  • synonym – roller: a cylindrical tool that rolls over a surface
