3rd October Organization

noun
militant group formed in 1975 targeting Turkey over the Armenian genocide
Pronunciation: / θɜrd ɑkˈtoʊbɚ ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / θɜːd ɒkˈtəʊbə ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a militant Marxist-Leninist group formed in 1975 aiming to force Turkey to acknowledge the Armenian genocide, pay reparations, and cede territory to Armenia

Examples

the 3rd October Organization attacked diplomats
