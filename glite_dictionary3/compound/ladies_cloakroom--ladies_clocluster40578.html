ladies' cloakroom

noun
women's changing or coat room
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiz ˈkloʊkˌrum / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz ˈkləʊkruːm / (BrE)
a room in a public place where women can change clothes, freshen up, or leave their coats and bags

Examples

the ladies' cloakroom at a theater
