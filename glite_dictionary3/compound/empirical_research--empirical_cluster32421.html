empirical research

noun
research based on observed or experimental evidence
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪrɪkəl rɪˈsɝtʃ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪərɪkəl rɪˈsɜːtʃ / (BrE)
research based on observation, experience, or experiment rather than theory or pure logic

Examples

empirical research findings
Her dissertation includes several case studies as examples of empirical research.
Scientists rely on empirical research to validate their hypotheses and theories.
