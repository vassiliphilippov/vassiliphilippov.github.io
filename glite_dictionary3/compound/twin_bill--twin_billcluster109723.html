twin bill

noun
two games played on the same day
Pronunciation: / twɪn bɪl / (AmE), / twɪn bɪl / (BrE)
A set of two games played by the same teams on the same day, especially in baseball.

Examples

twin bill baseball games
Both managers had to adjust their lineups for the twin bill.
Fans flocked to the stadium for the twin bill on Sunday.
The teams played a twin bill to make up for yesterday’s rainout.
