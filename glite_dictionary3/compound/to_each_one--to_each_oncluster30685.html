to each one

adverb
for every individual separately
Pronunciation: / tu iːtʃ wʌn / (AmE), / tə iːtʃ wʌn / (BrE)
to or for every individual person or item, considered separately

Examples

gifts to each one
Prizes were awarded to each one who completed the challenge.
The manager gave a small gift to each one at the end of the meeting.
The teacher handed out a worksheet to each one of the students.
