truck stop

noun
roadside facility for truck drivers
Pronunciation: / trʌk stɑp / (AmE), / trʌk stɒp / (BrE)
A roadside service station, sometimes with a restaurant and other amenities, that caters primarily to truck drivers.

Examples

truck stop with diner
Many truck stops offer showers and laundry facilities for long-haul drivers.
The diner at the truck stop was crowded with travelers and locals alike.
We pulled into a truck stop for fuel and a quick meal before continuing our journey.
