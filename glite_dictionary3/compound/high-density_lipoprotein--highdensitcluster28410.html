high-density lipoprotein

noun
cholesterol-transporting blood protein
Pronunciation: / haɪ ˈdɛnsəti ˌlaɪpəˈproʊtin / (AmE), / haɪ ˈdɛnsɪti ˌlɪpəˈprəʊtiːn / (BrE)
a substance in the blood that transports cholesterol, composed of a high proportion of protein and associated with lower risk of heart disease
