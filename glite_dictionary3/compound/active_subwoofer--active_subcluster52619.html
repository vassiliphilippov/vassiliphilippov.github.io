active subwoofer

noun
subwoofer with built-in amplifier
Pronunciation: / ˈæktɪv səˌwʊfər / (AmE), / ˈæktɪv səˌwʊfə / (BrE)
A subwoofer that includes a built-in amplifier, requiring only a line-level audio signal to operate.

Examples

active subwoofer with built-in amplifier
Most compact soundbars are paired with an active subwoofer to enhance low-frequency sound.
The new home theater system includes an active subwoofer for powerful bass performance.
You only need to connect the active subwoofer to your receiver; the amplifier is built in.

Related Terms
