king vulture

noun
large black-and-white vulture of Central and South America
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ ˈvʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / kɪŋ ˈvʌltʃə / (BrE)
a large black-and-white vulture (Sarcoramphus papa) of Central and South America, with colorful wattles and wartlike growths on its head and neck.

Examples

soaring king vulture
During our trip to Costa Rica, we spotted a rare king vulture soaring above the rainforest.
The king vulture is known for its strikingly colorful head and neck.
Unlike other scavengers, the king vulture often arrives first at a carcass to feed.
