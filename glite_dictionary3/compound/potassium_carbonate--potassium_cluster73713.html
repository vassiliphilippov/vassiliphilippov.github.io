potassium carbonate

noun
a basic white salt used in glass and cleaning agents
Pronunciation: / pəˈtæsiəm ˈkɑrbəˌneɪt / (AmE), / pəˈtæsiəm ˈkɑːbənət / (BrE)
a white, water-soluble salt (K₂CO₃) that is basic in solution, used in the manufacture of glass, soap, and cleaning agents
