gray mullet

noun
a widely distributed food fish
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈmʌlɪt / (BrE)
a species of freshwater or coastal fish with a spindle-shaped body, found worldwide and often used as food.
