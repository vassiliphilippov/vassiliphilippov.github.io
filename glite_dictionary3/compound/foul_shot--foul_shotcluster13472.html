foul shot

noun
basketball penalty shot from foul line
Pronunciation: / faʊl ʃɑt / (AmE), / faʊl ʃɒt / (BrE)
An unhindered shot taken from the foul line in basketball, awarded as a penalty for a foul committed by the opposing team.

Examples

make a foul shot
After being fouled, he was given two foul shots and scored both.
Missing a foul shot can cost a team dearly in a close basketball match.
She calmly made the foul shot to tie the game in the final seconds.
