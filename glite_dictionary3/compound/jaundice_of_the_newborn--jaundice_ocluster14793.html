jaundice of the newborn

noun
yellowish skin in newborn babies
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔndɪs əv ðə ˈnuːˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːndɪs əv ðə ˈnjuːˌbɔːn / (BrE)
