northern barramundi

noun
a large spotted Australian river fish
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɹðɚn ˌbæɹəˈmʊndi / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən ˌbærəˈmʊndi / (BrE)
a large freshwater fish native to northern Australian rivers, recognized by several reddish crescent-shaped spots on each scale
