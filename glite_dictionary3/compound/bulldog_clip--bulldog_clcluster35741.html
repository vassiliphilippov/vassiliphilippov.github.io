bulldog clip

noun
spring-loaded metal paper clip
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌdɔɡ klɪp / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.dɒɡ klɪp / (BrE)
a strong, spring-loaded metal clip with flat jaws, used for holding sheets of paper together
