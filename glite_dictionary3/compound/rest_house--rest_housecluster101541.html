rest house

noun
shelter for travelers, esp. in remote areas
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛst ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈrɛst ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building or shelter provided for travelers to rest, especially in remote areas where there are no hotels

Examples

mountain rest house
After walking all day, the hikers were relieved to find a rest house on the mountain trail.
In remote villages, a simple rest house is often the only place travelers can sleep for the night.
The charity built a rest house to provide shelter for pilgrims passing through the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym – guesthouse: a private house offering accommodation to paying guests
  • synonym – inn: an establishment for the lodging and entertaining of travelers
