fairy ring

noun
ring of mushrooms formed by fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfeəri rɪŋ / (BrE)
A naturally occurring ring or arc of mushrooms, typically forming at the edge of underground fungal growth.
