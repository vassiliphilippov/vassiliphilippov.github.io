definite article

noun
the word 'the' indicating specificity
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛfɪnət ˈɑːrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛfɪnət ˈɑːtɪkl / (BrE)
the word 'the' used before a noun to indicate that the identity of the noun is known to the reader or listener
