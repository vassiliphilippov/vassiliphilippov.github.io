camp bed

noun
folding portable bed
Pronunciation: / kæmp bɛd / (AmE), / kæmp bɛd / (BrE)
a small, portable bed with a folding frame, used especially for camping or temporary accommodation

Examples

After hiking all day, she was glad to rest on her camp bed inside the tent.

We set up a camp bed in the living room for our guest to sleep on.
