tragic mistake

noun
grave error with disastrous results
Pronunciation: / ˈtrædʒɪk mɪˈsteɪk / (AmE), / ˈtrædʒɪk mɪˈsteɪk / (BrE)
a very serious error or misjudgment that leads to grave or disastrous consequences

Examples

a tragic mistake causes disaster
Losing the confidential files was a tragic mistake that damaged the company's reputation.
She realized too late that trusting him had been a tragic mistake.
The pilot's tragic mistake resulted in an accident that claimed several lives.

Related Terms
