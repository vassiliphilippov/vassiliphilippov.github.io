shark oil

noun
oil from shark livers, source of vitamin A
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrk ɔɪl / (AmE), / ʃɑːk ɔɪl / (BrE)
a fatty yellow or brown oil obtained from the livers of sharks, used in dressing leather and as a source of vitamin A

Examples

shark oil supplement
Supplements containing shark oil are sometimes marketed for their vitamin A content.
The fisherman exported barrels of shark oil to supply the leather industry.
Traditional remedies in some regions use shark oil for medicinal purposes.
