sealed instrument

noun
formal contract with seal
Pronunciation: / siːld ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (AmE), / siːld ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
a formal contract signed by the parties and bearing a wax or embossed seal as evidence of authority or authenticity
