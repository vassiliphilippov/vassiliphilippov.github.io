quartic polynomial

noun
polynomial of the fourth degree
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrtɪk pəˈlɪnoʊmiəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtɪk pəˈlɪnəmiəl / (BrE)
A polynomial of degree four, having the highest exponent of the variable as four.
