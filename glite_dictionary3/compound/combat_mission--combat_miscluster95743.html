combat mission

noun
military operation involving fighting
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmˌbæt ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɒmbæt ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a military operation involving active fighting, usually to capture or defend a target or achieve a specific objective

Examples

dangerous combat mission

Not every military assignment is a combat mission; some are for humanitarian aid.
The squad was sent on a combat mission to secure the enemy stronghold.
