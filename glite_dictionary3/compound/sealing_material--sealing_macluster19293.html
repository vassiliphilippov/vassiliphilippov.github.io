sealing material

noun
substance used for sealing joints or cracks
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlɪŋ məˈtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈsiːlɪŋ məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a substance used to seal joints or fill cracks in a surface to prevent leakage or entry of air, water, etc.

Examples

sealing material for cracks
Always use a high-quality sealing material to ensure the bathroom tiles stay waterproof.
Before painting, check for any cracks that need to be filled with sealing material.
Over time, the sealing material around the windows may deteriorate and require replacement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sealant: a material used to block the passage of fluids through the surface or joints or openings
