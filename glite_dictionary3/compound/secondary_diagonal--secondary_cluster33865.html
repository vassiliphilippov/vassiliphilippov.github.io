secondary diagonal

noun
diagonal from bottom-left to top-right in a matrix
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənˌdɛr.i daɪˈæɡ.ən.əl / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.əndəri dʌɪˈaɪ.ə.ɡən.əl / (BrE)
in a square matrix, the diagonal running from the bottom-left corner to the top-right corner

Examples

secondary diagonal of a matrix
