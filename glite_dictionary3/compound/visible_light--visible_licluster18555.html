visible light

noun
light visible to the human eye
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzəbəl laɪt / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪbəl laɪt / (BrE)
Electromagnetic radiation that is perceptible to the human eye and enables sight.

Examples

visible light spectrum
Many insects can see beyond visible light into the ultraviolet spectrum.
Plants use visible light from the sun to carry out photosynthesis.
The rainbow contains all the colors of visible light.
