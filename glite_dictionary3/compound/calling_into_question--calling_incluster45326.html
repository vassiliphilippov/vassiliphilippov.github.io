calling into question

noun
expression of doubt or challenge
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔlɪŋ ˌɪntu kwɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːlɪŋ ˌɪntuː ˈkwɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
