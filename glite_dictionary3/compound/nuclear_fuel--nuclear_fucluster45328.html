nuclear fuel

noun
material used to produce energy in nuclear reactors
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.kli.ɚ ˈfjuːl / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˈfjʊəl / (BrE)
A material, such as uranium or plutonium, that can be used as a source of energy in a nuclear reactor.
