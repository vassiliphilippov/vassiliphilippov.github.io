idiopathic hemochromatosis

noun
genetic disorder of excessive iron accumulation
Pronunciation: / ˌɪdioʊˈpæθɪk ˌhiːmoʊˌkroʊməˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk ˌhiːməˌkrəʊməˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
an inherited disorder in which excessive amounts of iron accumulate in the body, potentially causing organ damage
