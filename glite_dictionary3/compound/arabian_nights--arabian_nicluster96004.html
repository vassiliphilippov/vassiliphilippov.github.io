Arabian Nights

proper noun
collection of Middle Eastern folktales
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ.bi.ən ˈnaɪts / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən ˈnaɪts / (BrE)
A famous collection of Middle Eastern and South Asian folktales, originally written in Arabic during the 10th century, also known as 'One Thousand and One Nights'.
