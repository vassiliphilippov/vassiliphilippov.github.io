climbing onion

noun
South African ornamental plant with bulbous stems
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈʌn.jən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ ˈʌn.jən / (BrE)
a much-branched, leafless, twining South African plant with bright green stems and large aboveground bulbs, often grown as an ornamental

Examples
