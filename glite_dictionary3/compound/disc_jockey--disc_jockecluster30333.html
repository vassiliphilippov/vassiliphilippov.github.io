disc jockey

noun
person who plays and announces recorded music
Pronunciation: / dɪsk ˈdʒɑki / (AmE), / dɪsk ˈdʒɒki / (BrE)
a person whose job is to select, announce, and play recorded music for an audience, especially on radio or at events

Examples
