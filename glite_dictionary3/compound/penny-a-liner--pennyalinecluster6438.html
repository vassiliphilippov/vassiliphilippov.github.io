penny-a-liner

noun
writer of cheap or low-quality work
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛni ə ˈlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˌpɛni ə ˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
a writer who produces cheap or low-quality written work, often for little pay

Examples

Though talented, he resorted to becoming a penny-a-liner to pay the bills.
