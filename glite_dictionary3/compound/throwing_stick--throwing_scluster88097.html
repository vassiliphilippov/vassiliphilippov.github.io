throwing stick

noun
tool for propelling darts or spears
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊɪŋ stɪk / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊɪŋ stɪk / (BrE)
a tool used to propel a dart or spear, especially in some Indigenous and ancient societies, increasing distance and speed

Examples

throwing stick launches a spear
Archaeologists discovered an ancient throwing stick among the artifacts at the dig site.
Some Aboriginal Australians traditionally employ a throwing stick for hunting small game.
The hunter skillfully used his throwing stick to launch the spear farther than by hand alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym – atlatl: a stick used to throw spears with greater force and distance
