hangar rash

noun
minor aircraft damage from storage or handling
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋər ræʃ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋə ræʃ / (BrE)
minor damage to an aircraft, such as scratches or dents, resulting from improper storage or careless handling in a hangar

Examples

hangar rash on airplane wing
After being moved too many times, the old Cessna developed hangar rash along its wings.
Pilots are trained to avoid hangar rash by using proper towing techniques.
The insurance claim covered repairs for the hangar rash found during inspection.
