to advantage

adverb
in a most favorable way
Pronunciation: / tə ədˈvæn.tɪdʒ / (AmE), / tə ədˈvɑːn.tɪdʒ / (BrE)
in a way that makes someone or something appear in the best or most favorable light

Examples
