Porkulus plan

noun
stimulus package seen as wasteful
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrkjʊləs plæn / (AmE), / ˈpɔːkjʊləs plan / (BrE)
A government stimulus package, especially one criticized as involving excessive or wasteful spending.

Examples

the Porkulus plan spending bill
Critics of the administration referred to the recovery bill as the Porkulus plan, citing its perceived waste.
Many taxpayers were frustrated by how much money was allocated in the Porkulus plan.
The opposition claimed that the Porkulus plan was full of unnecessary projects and failed to help the economy.
