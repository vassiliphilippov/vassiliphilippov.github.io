loudspeaker system

noun
equipment for reproducing amplified sound
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊdˌspiːkɚ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈlaʊdˌspiːkə ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
An electronic device consisting of one or more loudspeakers and associated components, designed to reproduce and amplify sound so that it can be heard at a distance.

Examples

loudspeaker system in a hall
After the upgrade, the school's loudspeaker system broadcasted announcements with much better clarity.
He installed a new loudspeaker system in his living room for a more immersive audio experience.
The concert hall's loudspeaker system ensures clear sound throughout the entire venue.
