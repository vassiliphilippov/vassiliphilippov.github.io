fog in someone's mind

noun
mental confusion; unclear thinking
Pronunciation: / fɑɡ ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz maɪnd / (AmE), / fɒɡ ɪn ˈsʌmwənz maɪnd / (BrE)
a state in which someone feels mentally confused or unable to think clearly

Examples

fog in my mind after waking
After only a few hours of sleep, I felt a fog in my mind and struggled to concentrate at work.
He tried to shake off the fog in his mind before the important interview.
The medication left a persistent fog in her mind that made reading difficult.

Related Terms

  • antonym – mental clarity: the state of being able to think clearly and not confused
  • synonym – mental fog: a state of being unable to think clearly
