order Embioptera

noun
an order of web-spinning insects
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ɛm.biˈɒp.tɚ.ə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ɛm.bɪˈɒp.tər.ə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of insects, known as web spinners, characterized by their ability to spin silken galleries using silk produced from glands in their front legs.

Examples

Scientists discovered a new species belonging to the order Embioptera in the rainforest.
The silk-spinning capabilities of insects in the order Embioptera help them build protective galleries.
Unlike ants or bees, members of the order Embioptera do not form large colonies.
