swallow-tailed coat

noun
man's formal jacket with tails
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːloʊ teɪld koʊt / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊ teɪld kəʊt / (BrE)
A man's formal jacket with a front cut away and two long tapering tails at the back, worn for full-dress occasions.
