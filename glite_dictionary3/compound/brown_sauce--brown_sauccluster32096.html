brown sauce

noun
soy sauce-based sauce in East Asian cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊn sɔs / (AmE), / ˈbraʊn sɔːs / (BrE)
A sauce made with soy sauce as a base, often used in Chinese and other East Asian cuisines.

Examples

brown sauce in Chinese food
Many Chinese takeout dishes are served with brown sauce that adds a rich flavor to vegetables and meats.
She tried to replicate the restaurant's brown sauce for her homemade stir-fry.
The chef recommended using oyster sauce if you don't have brown sauce available.
