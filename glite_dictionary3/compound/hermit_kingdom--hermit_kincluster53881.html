Hermit Kingdom

noun
an isolated country, especially North Korea
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrmɪt ˈkɪŋdəm / (AmE), / ˈhɜːmɪt ˈkɪŋdəm / (BrE)
a country, especially North Korea, that deliberately isolates itself from the rest of the world

Examples

the Hermit Kingdom of North Korea
Many journalists refer to North Korea as the Hermit Kingdom due to its extreme isolation.
Outsiders rarely get a glimpse into daily life within the Hermit Kingdom.
The Hermit Kingdom has been resistant to international engagement and reform.
