digital vein

noun
vein in fingers or toes
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl veɪn / (BrE)
any of the veins that carry blood away from the fingers or toes.

Examples

digital vein injury
A blockage in a digital vein can cause swelling and discomfort in the affected finger.
Bruising along a digital vein may indicate an underlying circulatory problem.
Surgeons carefully avoided damaging the digital veins during the procedure.
