cranberry heath

noun
a shrub with cranberry-like fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈkrænˌbɛri hiːθ / (AmE), / ˈkrænˌbəri hiːθ / (BrE)
A small prostrate or ascending shrub with scarlet flowers and succulent fruit resembling cranberries, sometimes classified in the genus Styphelia.
