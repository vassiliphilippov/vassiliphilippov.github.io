rear-view mirror

noun
mirror for seeing behind a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪr.vjuː ˌmɪr.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɪə.vjuː ˌmɪr.ə / (BrE)
a mirror attached to the inside of a vehicle, allowing the driver to see the area behind the vehicle

Examples
