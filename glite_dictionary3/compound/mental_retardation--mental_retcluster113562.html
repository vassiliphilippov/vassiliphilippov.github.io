mental retardation

noun
outdated term for intellectual disability
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.təl rɪˌtɑrˈdeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl rɪˌtɑːˈdeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
an outdated and now offensive term for a condition characterized by significantly below-average intellectual functioning and limitations in adaptive behavior; now usually replaced by 'intellectual disability'

Examples

mental retardation diagnosis
Historically, mental retardation appeared in diagnostic manuals but is no longer used.
The term mental retardation has largely been replaced by 'intellectual disability' in medical and educational fields.
