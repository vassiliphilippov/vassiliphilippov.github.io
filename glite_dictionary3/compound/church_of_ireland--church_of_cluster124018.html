Church of Ireland

proper noun
the Anglican church in Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɝːtʃ əv ˈaɪərlənd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃ əv ˈaɪələnd / (BrE)
The self-governing Anglican church in Ireland, formerly part of the Church of England but now autonomous.

Examples

attend the Church of Ireland service
His family has attended the Church of Ireland for generations.
The Church of Ireland has played a significant role in the country's history.
The Church of Ireland is part of the worldwide Anglican Communion.

Related Terms

  • antonym – Roman Catholic Church: the main Catholic church, often contrasted with Anglican churches in Ireland
