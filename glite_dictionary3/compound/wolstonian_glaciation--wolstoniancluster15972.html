Wolstonian glaciation

noun
British Pleistocene glaciation period
Pronunciation: / ˌwʌlstəniən ɡleɪsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌwɒlstəniən ɡleɪsɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the next-to-last glaciation of the Pleistocene epoch in Britain, which is marked by specific geological deposits
