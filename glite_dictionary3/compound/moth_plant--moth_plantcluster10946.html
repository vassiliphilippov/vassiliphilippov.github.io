moth plant

noun
a type of orchid of the genus Phalaenopsis
Pronunciation: / mɑθ plænt / (AmE), / mɒθ plɑːnt / (BrE)
Any of several orchids of the genus Phalaenopsis, with broad, often drooping, glossy dark green leaves, sometimes with purple or mottled markings.
