moth miller

noun
moth with powdery wings
Pronunciation: / mɔθ ˈmɪlər / (AmE), / mɒθ ˈmɪlə / (BrE)
Any of various moths that have powdery wings, especially those whose wings resemble the dust from milled flour.

Examples

a moth miller at night
During the summer, moth millers often gather near porch lights at night.
She brushed away a moth miller that had landed on her sleeve.
The old barn was filled with moth millers fluttering around the light.
