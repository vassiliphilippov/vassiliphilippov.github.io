wiper blade

noun
windshield-cleaning blade on vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪpɚ bleɪd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪpə bleɪd / (BrE)
a long, narrow rubber blade attached to a vehicle's wiper arm, used to clean the windshield by wiping away water, dirt, or debris
