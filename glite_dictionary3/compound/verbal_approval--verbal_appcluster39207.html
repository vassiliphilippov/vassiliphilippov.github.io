verbal approval

noun
spoken approval or consent
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrbəl əˈpruvəl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːbəl əˈpruːvəl / (BrE)
a statement of consent or agreement given in spoken words, rather than in writing

Examples

verbal approval from manager
He sought his client's verbal approval before making any changes to the plan.
The manager's verbal approval allowed the team to start the project immediately.
Without verbal approval from the supervisor, the process cannot move forward.
