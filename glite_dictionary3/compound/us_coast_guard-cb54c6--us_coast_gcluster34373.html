US Coast Guard

proper noun
United States maritime law enforcement agency
Pronunciation: / juː ɛs koʊst ɡɑrd / (AmE), / juː ɛs kəʊst ɡɑːd / (BrE)
The official agency of the United States government responsible for patrolling coasts, enforcing maritime law, and facilitating safe nautical commerce.
