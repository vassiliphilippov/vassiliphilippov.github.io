plate glass

noun
large flat sheet of glass
Pronunciation: / pleɪt ɡlæs / (AmE), / pleɪt ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
Glass made in large, flat sheets, commonly used for windows and doors.

Examples

plate glass window
Plate glass is preferred for shop windows because of its strength and clarity.
He replaced the broken plate glass in the storefront after the storm.
The building's lobby features floor-to-ceiling plate glass windows for a modern look.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sheet glass: glass made in thin, flat sheets, often as an alternative to plate glass
