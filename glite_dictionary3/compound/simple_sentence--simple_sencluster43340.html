simple sentence

noun
sentence with one clause
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəl ˈsɛntəns / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl ˈsɛntəns / (BrE)
A sentence that consists of a single clause, without any subordinate or coordinate clauses.

Examples

write a simple sentence
'The dog barked.' is an example of a simple sentence.
A simple sentence is easy for beginners to understand.
He wrote a simple sentence that clearly expressed his thoughts.
