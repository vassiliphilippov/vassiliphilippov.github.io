Paschal Lamb

noun
lamb symbolic of Christ in Christian tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈpæskəl læm / (AmE), / ˈpæskəl læm / (BrE)
a lamb that represents Jesus Christ's sacrifice, especially as a symbol in Christian tradition and Easter
