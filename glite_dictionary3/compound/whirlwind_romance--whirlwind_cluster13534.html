whirlwind romance

noun
brief, intense romantic relationship
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrlˌwɪnd roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / ˈwɜːlˌwɪnd rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
A romantic relationship that develops rapidly and is characterized by intense emotion, often lasting for a short period.

Examples

a whirlwind romance begins quickly
After a whirlwind romance, they decided to get married within a month.
The movie tells the story of a whirlwind romance between two strangers in Paris.
Their whirlwind romance surprised everyone and ended just as quickly as it began.
