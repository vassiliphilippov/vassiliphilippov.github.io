silver plate

noun
thin layer of silver on another material
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ pleɪt /; / ˈsɪlvɚ ˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə pleɪt /; / ˈsɪlvə ˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a thin layer of silver on the surface of another material, or objects (especially tableware) made with this coating
