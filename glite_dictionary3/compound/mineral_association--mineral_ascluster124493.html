mineral association

noun
minerals found together in rocks
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnərəl əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪnərəl əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a group of minerals that are commonly found together in a particular rock or geological environment.

Examples

mineral association in granite
Geologists studied the mineral association to better understand the formation of the granite.
Identifying the mineral association can help determine the conditions under which the rock formed.
