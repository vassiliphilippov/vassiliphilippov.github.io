mosaic culture

noun
highly diverse and multicultural society
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪɪk ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪɪk ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
a society or community characterized by a diverse mix of cultures, ethnicities, or backgrounds, where each group maintains its unique identity within the whole

Examples

mosaic culture in Canada
Canada is often described as having a mosaic culture instead of a melting pot.
Exhibitions in the museum highlight the influence of the country's mosaic culture on contemporary art.
The city is proud of its mosaic culture, which celebrates the traditions of many different communities.
