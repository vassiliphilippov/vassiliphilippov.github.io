faecal occult test

noun
test for blood in stool
Pronunciation: / ˈfiː.kəl əˈkʌlt tɛst / (AmE), / ˈfiː.kəl əˈkʌlt tɛst / (BrE)
A medical test that checks for hidden traces of blood in stool samples, used to screen for colorectal cancer and other digestive diseases.

Examples

faecal occult test for blood
A positive faecal occult test result may indicate bleeding in the digestive tract.
My doctor recommended a faecal occult test as part of my annual check-up.
You can perform a faecal occult test at home and send the samples to the laboratory for analysis.
