stocking cap

noun
close-fitting knitted woolen cap
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːkɪŋ kæp / (AmE), / ˈstɒkɪŋ kap / (BrE)
A close-fitting knitted woolen cap, often tapering to a point and sometimes finished with a tassel.

Examples

warm stocking cap
During winter, children often wear a stocking cap when playing outside.
His red stocking cap had a long tail with a little tassel at the end.
She pulled her stocking cap down over her ears to keep warm.
