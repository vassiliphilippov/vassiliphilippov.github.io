on faith

adverb
with trust and no proof
Pronunciation: / ɔn feɪθ / (AmE), / ɒn feɪθ / (BrE)
with trust and confidence, without requiring proof or evidence

Examples

accept on faith
Many people accept certain traditions on faith rather than questioning them.
She believed his story on faith, without any supporting evidence.
They invested in the project on faith because they trusted the founder.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blindly: without questioning or seeking proof
