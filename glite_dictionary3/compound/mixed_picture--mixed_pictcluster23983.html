mixed picture

noun
situation with both positive and negative aspects
Pronunciation: / mɪkst ˈpɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / mɪkst ˈpɪktʃə / (BrE)
a situation or assessment in which both positive and negative aspects are present, resulting in an unclear or ambiguous overall impression

Examples

a mixed picture of results
International test scores provide a mixed picture of student achievement across the region.
The company’s annual report gave a mixed picture of its overall financial health.
The review presented a mixed picture, praising the cast but criticizing the plot.
