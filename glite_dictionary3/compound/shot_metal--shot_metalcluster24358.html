shot metal

noun
alloy of lead and arsenic for making shot
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt ˈmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ʃɒt ˈmɛtl̩ / (BrE)
an alloy consisting of approximately 98% lead and 2% arsenic, used in the manufacture of small pellets for shotgun cartridges.

Examples

shot metal pellets
