Mojave rattlesnake

noun
a venomous rattlesnake species of the Southwest US and Mexico
Pronunciation: / moʊˈhɑːvi ˈræt̬lˌsneɪk / (AmE), / məˈhɑː.vi ˈrætl.sneɪk / (BrE)
a highly venomous rattlesnake (Crotalus scutulatus) found in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, especially in scrubby areas from the Mojave Desert to western Texas

Examples

a Mojave rattlesnake in desert

The Mojave rattlesnake is well known for its potent neurotoxic venom.
You should be cautious when hiking in areas where the Mojave rattlesnake is common.
