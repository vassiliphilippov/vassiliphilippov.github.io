pate de foie gras

noun
goose liver paste (delicacy)
Pronunciation: / pɑːt də fwaɪ ɡrɑː / (AmE), / pæt də fwa ɡrɑː / (BrE)
A rich, smooth paste made from the liver of a goose, often marinated with Cognac and truffles.

Examples

pate de foie gras on bread
She brought home a jar of pate de foie gras from her trip to France.
The chef paired the pate de foie gras with freshly baked bread and fig jam.
The restaurant serves an exquisite pate de foie gras as an appetizer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – foie gras: the fattened liver of a goose or duck, used as a delicacy
