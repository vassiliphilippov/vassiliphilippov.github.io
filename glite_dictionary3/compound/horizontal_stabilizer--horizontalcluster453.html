horizontal stabilizer

noun
fixed horizontal tail surface of an aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntəl ˈsteɪbəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntəl ˈsteɪbɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
the fixed horizontal surface on the tail of an aircraft, to which the elevator is attached, providing stability in pitch

Examples

horizontal stabilizer on airplane tail
Engineers inspected the horizontal stabilizer for signs of stress and fatigue.
The aircraft's horizontal stabilizer was damaged during the rough landing.
Without a functioning horizontal stabilizer, a plane would be difficult to control in pitch.
