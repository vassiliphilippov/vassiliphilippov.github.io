genus Hieracium

noun
genus of perennial herbs (hawkweeds)
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːnəs haɪˈræsiəm / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːnəs hɪəˈræsɪəm / (BrE)
A large genus of perennial hairy herbs, commonly known as hawkweeds, native to Europe, western Asia, northwestern Africa, and North America; sometimes considered to include Pilosella.
