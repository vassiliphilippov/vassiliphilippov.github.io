three-martini lunch

noun
indulgent lunch with several martinis
Pronunciation: / θri mɑrˈtini lʌntʃ / (AmE), / θriː mɑːˈtiːni lʌntʃ / (BrE)
a leisurely lunch, typically in a business context, during which several martinis are consumed, often implying indulgence and relaxation
