psoriatic arthritis

noun
form of arthritis linked to psoriasis
Pronunciation: / soʊˌraɪˈætɪk ɑrˈθraɪtɪs / (AmE), / sɔːˌraɪˈatɪk ɑːˈθraɪtɪs / (BrE)
A chronic form of arthritis associated with psoriasis, typically affecting the joints of the fingers and toes.

Examples

psoriatic arthritis joint pain
Many people with psoriatic arthritis experience joint pain along with skin symptoms.
The doctor diagnosed her with psoriatic arthritis after noticing swelling in her fingers and patches of psoriasis.
Treatment for psoriatic arthritis often involves medication to reduce inflammation and ease discomfort.
