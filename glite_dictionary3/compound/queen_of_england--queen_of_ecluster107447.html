Queen of England

noun
female sovereign monarch of England
Pronunciation: / kwin əv ˈɪŋɡlənd / (AmE), / kwiːn əv ˈɪŋlənd / (BrE)
the female sovereign ruler of England; a queen who reigns as the monarch of England.

Queen Elizabeth II held the title of Queen of England as part of her reign.
