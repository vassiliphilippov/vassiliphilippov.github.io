wood block

noun
wooden printing block
Pronunciation: / wʊd blɑk / (AmE), / wʊd blɒk / (BrE)
a block of wood with a design cut into its surface, used for printing images or patterns by pressing it onto a surface

Examples

wood block printing
Many traditional Japanese prints were made using wood blocks.
The artist carefully carved a design into the wood block for her new print.
The museum displayed an original wood block alongside the printed artwork.
