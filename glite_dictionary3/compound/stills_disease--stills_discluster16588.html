Still's disease

noun
juvenile rheumatoid arthritis affecting children
Pronunciation: / stɪlz dɪˈziz /; / st5alz d6ac8ziz / (AmE), / stɪlz dɪˈziːz /; / st6alz d6ac8zid0z / (BrE)
a rare inflammatory disease (a type of arthritis) that causes fever, joint pain, and other symptoms, and can affect both children and adults
