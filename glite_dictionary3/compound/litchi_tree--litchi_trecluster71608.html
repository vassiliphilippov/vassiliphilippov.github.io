litchi tree

noun
tropical Asian fruit tree
Pronunciation: / ˈliː.tʃi ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈlɪtʃ.i ˌtriː / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to China, grown for its sweet, edible fruit called litchi; cultivated mainly in the Philippines, India, and other warm climates.

Examples

litchi tree fruit harvest
Farmers in India have started cultivating the litchi tree for its high market value.
The litchi tree in their backyard produces fruit every summer.
The litchi tree is admired for both its delicious fruit and its beautiful, glossy leaves.
