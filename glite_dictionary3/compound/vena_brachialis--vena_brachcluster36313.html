vena brachialis

noun
vein in the upper arm accompanying the brachial artery
Pronunciation: / ˌviːnə brəˈkaɪəlɪs / (AmE), / ˌviːnə brəˈkeɪlɪs / (BrE)
either of two veins in each arm that accompany the brachial artery and drain into the axillary vein

Examples

vena brachialis in the arm
Blood from the forearm drains via the vena brachialis to the axillary vein.
During the anatomy exam, students were asked to identify the vena brachialis on the cadaver.
The vena brachialis is often used as a landmark during surgical procedures on the upper arm.
