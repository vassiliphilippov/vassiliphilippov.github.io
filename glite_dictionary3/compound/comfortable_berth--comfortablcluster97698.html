comfortable berth

noun
cozy sleeping place (especially on transport)
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmfərtəbəl bɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈkʌmfətəbl bɜːθ / (BrE)
a sleeping place that is cozy and provides physical comfort, especially on a train, ship, or similar means of transport

Examples

a comfortable berth on a train

She was grateful to have a comfortable berth after a long day of travel.
The first-class ticket included a comfortable berth in a private compartment.
