rental income

noun
income from rented property
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn.t̬əl ˈɪn.kəm / (AmE), / ˈrɛnt.əl ˈɪn.kəm / (BrE)
income that is earned from renting out property, such as land, buildings, or apartments.

Examples

rental income from apartments

The landlord relies on rental income to pay his mortgage each month.
You must report all rental income on your annual tax return.
