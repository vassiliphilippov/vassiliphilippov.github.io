toxic waste site

noun
place for toxic waste disposal
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑksɪk weɪst saɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɒksɪk weɪst saɪt / (BrE)
a place where toxic or hazardous waste materials are stored, disposed of, or have been abandoned, sometimes illegally

Examples

abandoned toxic waste site
Groundwater contamination was traced back to an abandoned toxic waste site.
Regulations now require stricter monitoring of every toxic waste site in the region.
The community demanded action after a toxic waste site was discovered near the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hazardous waste: waste material that poses substantial or potential threats to public health or the environment
  • synonym – landfill: a site for the disposal of waste materials by burial
