posttraumatic epilepsy

noun
seizures after a head injury
Pronunciation: / poʊsttrəˈmætɪk ˌɛpɪˈlɛpsi / (AmE), / pəʊsttrɔːˈmatɪk ˌɛpɪˈlepsi / (BrE)
A form of epilepsy characterized by recurring seizures that result from a previous head injury.

Examples

posttraumatic epilepsy after head injury
Doctors monitor patients with severe head injuries for signs of posttraumatic epilepsy.
Effective treatment of posttraumatic epilepsy often requires both medication and ongoing neurological evaluation.
Following the car accident, he was diagnosed with posttraumatic epilepsy after experiencing several seizures.
