inhalation general anaesthetic

noun
gas used for general anesthesia by inhalation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌheɪlˈeɪʃən ˌdʒɛnərəl ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ɪnˌheɪlˈeɪʃ(ə)n ˌdʒɛnərəl ˌanɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
