lingual artery

noun
artery supplying the underside of the tongue
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋɡwəl ˈɑr.t̬ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡwəl ˈɑː.tər.i / (BrE)
an artery that branches from the external carotid artery and supplies blood to the underside of the tongue

Examples

lingual artery supplies tongue
Damage to the lingual artery can result in significant bleeding under the tongue.
The lingual artery is a key structure supplied by the external carotid artery.
