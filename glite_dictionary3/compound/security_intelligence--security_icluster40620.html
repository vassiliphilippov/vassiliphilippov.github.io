(in cybersecurity) information collected, analysed, and organised about digital threats, attacker activity, software vulnerabilities, and indicators of compromise used to detect, prevent, or investigate cyber attacks.

Examples

security intelligence for cyber attacks
The company's security intelligence team monitors network logs and indicators to prevent data breaches.
During the incident response, our analyst used security intelligence to identify the attackers' command-and-control servers.
Small businesses often subscribe to threat feeds and security intelligence services to stay ahead of ransomware groups.
