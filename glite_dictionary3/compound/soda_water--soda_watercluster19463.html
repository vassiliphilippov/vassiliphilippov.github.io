soda water

noun
carbonated water beverage
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdə ˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə ˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
a clear, carbonated beverage made by dissolving carbon dioxide in water, often used as a mixer or drink

Examples

plain soda water

Many cocktails call for a splash of soda water to add fizz.
She ordered a glass of soda water with lemon at the bar.

  • synonym – sparkling water: carbonated water, usually without added minerals
