radio announcer

noun
person presenting/introducing radio programs
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdioʊ əˌnaʊnsər / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ əˌnaʊnsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to present or introduce radio programs and read announcements on the radio

radio announcer introduces music
She dreamed of becoming a radio announcer ever since she was a child.
The radio announcer broke the news to listeners early in the morning.
The radio announcer has a calm and reassuring voice.
