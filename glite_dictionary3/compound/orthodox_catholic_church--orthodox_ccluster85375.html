Orthodox Catholic Church

proper noun
Christian church of Byzantine tradition
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˌdɑks ˈkæθəlɪk tʃɝtʃ / (AmE), / ˌɔːθədɒks ˈkæθlɪk tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A Christian church derived from the Byzantine Church and following Byzantine rites; also known as the Eastern Orthodox Church.
