Gulf States

proper noun
U.S. states bordering the Gulf of Mexico
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf steɪts / (AmE), / ɡʌlf steɪts / (BrE)
the group of U.S. states that border the Gulf of Mexico, specifically Alabama, Florida, Louisiana, Mississippi, and Texas
