chef's salad

noun
salad with meats, cheese, and eggs
Pronunciation: / ʃɛfs ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ʃɛfs ˈsaləd / (BrE)
a salad typically consisting of mixed greens, meats such as ham or turkey, cheese, hard-boiled eggs, and assorted vegetables, often served as a main course
