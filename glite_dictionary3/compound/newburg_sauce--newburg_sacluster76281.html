Newburg sauce

noun
rich creamy seafood sauce with sherry
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːbɚɡ sɔs / (AmE), / ˈnjuːbəɡ sɔːs / (BrE)
A rich sauce made of lobster butter, cream, and egg yolks, seasoned with onions and sherry or Madeira, often served with seafood.

Examples

Newburg sauce with lobster
He impressed his guests with homemade Newburg sauce drizzled on steamed seafood.
Scallops served in a Newburg sauce make for a truly decadent appetizer.
The chef poured Newburg sauce over the freshly cooked lobster to enhance its flavor.
