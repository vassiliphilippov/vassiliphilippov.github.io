urban guerrilla

noun
guerrilla fighting in cities
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.bən ɡəˈrɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˈɜː.bən ɡəˈrɪl.ə / (BrE)
A guerrilla fighter who operates primarily in cities and towns rather than in rural areas.

Examples

urban guerrilla attacks city
Authorities struggled to prevent the activities of urban guerrillas during the height of the conflict.
The urban guerrilla tactics often include sabotage and surprise attacks on city infrastructure.
Unlike rural fighters, an urban guerrilla relies on blending into the city population.
