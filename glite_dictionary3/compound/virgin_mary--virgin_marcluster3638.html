Virgin Mary

noun
non-alcoholic tomato juice cocktail
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdʒɪn ˈmɛri / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪn ˈmeəri / (BrE)
A non-alcoholic cocktail made with tomato juice and various spices, similar to a Bloody Mary but with no alcohol.

Examples

Virgin Mary cocktail
At brunch, the bar offers both Bloody Marys and Virgin Marys for guests.
He prefers a Virgin Mary with extra spice and celery as garnish.
She ordered a Virgin Mary because she was driving and didn’t want any alcohol.
