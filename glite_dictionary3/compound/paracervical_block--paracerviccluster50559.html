paracervical block

noun
regional anesthesia for childbirth
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.əˈsɜr.vɪ.kəl blɑk / (AmE), / ˌpar.əˈsɜː.vɪ.kəl blɒk / (BrE)
a type of regional anesthesia produced by injecting a local anesthetic on each side of the cervix, commonly used during labor and childbirth
