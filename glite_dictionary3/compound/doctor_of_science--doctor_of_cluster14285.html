Doctor of Science

noun
advanced honorary academic degree in science
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑktɚ əv ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə əv ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
an advanced or honorary academic degree awarded in science, often designated as DSc or ScD

Examples

Doctor of Science degree awarded
After years in the field, he received an honorary Doctor of Science from his alma mater.
She was awarded the Doctor of Science degree for her groundbreaking research in biochemistry.
The university offers a Doctor of Science program for distinguished scientists with a substantial body of published work.
