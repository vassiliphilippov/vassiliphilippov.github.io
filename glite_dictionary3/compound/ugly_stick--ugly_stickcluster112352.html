ugly stick

noun
humorous term for unattractive appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡli stɪk / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli stɪk / (BrE)
a humorous term for something or someone that is very unattractive or unpleasant in appearance, often used in the phrase 'hit with the ugly stick'

Examples
