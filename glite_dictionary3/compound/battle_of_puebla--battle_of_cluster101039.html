Battle of Puebla

proper noun
1862 battle—Mexican victory over French forces
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l əv ˈpwɛblə / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˈpwɛblə / (BrE)
A battle fought on May 5, 1862, in which Mexican forces defeated the French army near the city of Puebla.

Examples

the Battle of Puebla victory
Despite being outnumbered, Mexican troops won the Battle of Puebla against the French.
