California live oak

noun
evergreen oak of coastal western North America
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˈfɔrniə laɪv oʊk / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈfɔːniə laɪv əʊk / (BrE)
A highly variable, usually shrubby evergreen oak (Quercus agrifolia) of the coastal zone of western North America, known for its small, thick, often spiny-toothed dark-green leaves.
