makeshift vase

noun
improvised container for flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪkˌʃɪft veɪs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkʃɪft vɑːz / (BrE)
a temporary or improvised container used to hold flowers when a proper vase is unavailable

Examples
