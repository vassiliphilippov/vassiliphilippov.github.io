the ould fella

noun
affectionate term for an old man (Irish English)
Pronunciation: / ði oʊld ˈfɛlə / (AmE), / ði əʊld ˈfɛlə / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly Irish English) an old man, especially used affectionately to refer to one's father or an elderly male.
