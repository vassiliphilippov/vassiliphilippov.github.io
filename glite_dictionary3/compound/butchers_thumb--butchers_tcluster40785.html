butcher's thumb

noun
method for testing meat quality by thumb
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃərz θʌm / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃəz θʌm / (BrE)
a method of skillfully measuring or assessing, especially used by butchers to test meat quality by pressing with the thumb
