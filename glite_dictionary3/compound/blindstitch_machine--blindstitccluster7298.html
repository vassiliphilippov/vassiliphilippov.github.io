blindstitch machine

noun
sewing machine for invisible stitches
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪnd.stɪtʃ məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈblaɪnd.stɪtʃ məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A sewing machine designed to join two pieces of fabric with stitches that are invisible or nearly invisible on the surface.

Examples

blindstitch machine for hems
