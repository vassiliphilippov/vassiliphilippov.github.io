sweat duct

noun
tube carrying sweat to skin's surface
Pronunciation: / swɛt dʌkt / (AmE), / swɛt dʌkt / (BrE)
a small tube in the skin that carries sweat from a sweat gland to the skin's surface

Examples

sweat duct blockage
A blocked sweat duct can lead to skin irritation and rashes.
Each sweat duct opens onto the surface of the skin as a tiny pore.
Heat and humidity may increase the risk of sweat duct obstruction in infants.
