delinquent account holder

noun
person with overdue debts
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɪŋkwənt əˈkaʊnt ˈhoʊldər / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪŋkwənt əˈkaʊnt ˈhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person or customer who has failed to pay debts on time, especially to a bank or financial institution

Examples
