uropygial gland

noun
oil-secreting gland at base of bird's tail
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊrəˈpaɪdʒiəl ɡlænd / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˈpɪdʒɪəl ɡlænd / (BrE)
a gland located at the base of a bird's tail that secretes oil used to maintain and waterproof feathers

Examples

uropygial gland secretes oil
