to double stitch

verb
sew with two rows of stitches
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl stɪtʃ / (BrE)
To sew with two rows of stitches, typically for extra strength or decoration.

Examples

to double stitch a seam
For added durability, she always double stitches the seams on children's clothing.
