tracheal vein

noun
vein draining blood from trachea
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪkiəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈtreɪkɪəl veɪn / (BrE)
any of several small veins that drain blood from the trachea.

Examples

tracheal vein injury
During surgery, the doctor carefully avoided damaging the tracheal vein.
Inflammation near the tracheal vein can cause localized swelling in the neck.
The anatomy lesson highlighted the role of each tracheal vein in returning blood to the larger vessels.
