tung-oil tree

noun
Chinese tree producing tung oil
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ ɔɪl ˌtri / (AmE), / tʌŋ ɔɪl ˌtriː / (BrE)
A deciduous Chinese tree (Vernicia fordii) whose seeds are used to produce tung oil.

Examples

tung-oil tree seeds
A mature tung-oil tree can yield large quantities of seeds each year.
Farmers harvest the seeds of the tung-oil tree to produce drying oil for wood finishing.
The tung-oil tree is widely cultivated in China for its valuable seeds.
