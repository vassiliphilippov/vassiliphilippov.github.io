mefloquine hydrochloride

noun
antimalarial drug for chloroquine-resistant cases
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛf.ləˌkwiːn haɪˌdrɒk.sɪˈlɔːr.aɪd / (AmE), / ˌmɛf.ləˌkwiːn hʌɪˌdrɒksɪˈlɔːraɪd / (BrE)
