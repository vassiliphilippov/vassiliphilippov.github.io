strainer vine

noun
tropical climbing plant with sponge-like fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪnər vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈstreɪnə vaɪn / (BrE)
a tropical climbing plant with large yellow flowers and edible young fruits, cultivated for its mature fruit, the fibrous interior of which is used as a sponge

Once dried, the inside of the strainer vine fruit can be used as a natural kitchen sponge.
