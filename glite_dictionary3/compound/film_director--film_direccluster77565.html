film director

noun
person who directs a film
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlm dəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / ˈfɪlm dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
A person responsible for overseeing and managing the creative aspects of making a film, including directing the actors and guiding the technical crew.

Examples

film director wins award
Many aspiring actors hope to work with a renowned film director one day.
She dreamed of becoming a film director after watching classic movies in her youth.
The film director accepted the award for best picture on behalf of his cast and crew.
