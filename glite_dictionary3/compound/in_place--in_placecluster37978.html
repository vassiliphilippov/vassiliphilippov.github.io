in place

adverb
in the original position or location
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈpleɪs / (AmE), / ɪn ˈpleɪs / (BrE)
in the original or proper position or location; not moved from where it belongs

Examples

in place after cleaning
After cleaning, make sure everything is in place before leaving the room.
Please keep the documents in place until the inspector arrives.
The tiles stayed in place during the entire storm.
