oral contract

noun
contract made verbally, not in writing
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrəl ˈkɑnˌtrækt / (AmE), / ˈɔːrəl ˈkɒntrækt / (BrE)
A legally binding agreement made verbally rather than in writing.

Examples

an oral contract is valid
Although there was no written agreement, the oral contract was still legally binding.
Disputes about the scope of an oral contract can be difficult to resolve in court.
