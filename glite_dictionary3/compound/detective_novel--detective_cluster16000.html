detective novel

noun
novel focused on solving a crime or mystery
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɛktɪv ˈnɑvəl / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛktɪv ˈnɒvəl / (BrE)
A novel whose plot revolves around solving a crime or mystery, often challenging the reader to solve the puzzle before the detective reveals the answer.

classic detective novel
Agatha Christie is renowned for her mastery of the detective novel genre.
She enjoys reading classic detective novels featuring famous sleuths like Sherlock Holmes.
The detective novel kept me guessing until the final page revealed the solution.
