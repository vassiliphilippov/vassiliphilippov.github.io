true heath

noun
any plant of the genus Erica
Pronunciation: / truː hiːθ / (AmE), / truː hiːθ / (BrE)
any plant of the genus Erica, a group of shrub species with small, often bell-shaped flowers, commonly found in Europe and Africa.
