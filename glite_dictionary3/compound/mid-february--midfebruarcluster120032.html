mid-February

noun
the middle part of February
Pronunciation: / mɪd ˈfɛb.juˌɛri / (AmE), / mɪd ˈfɛb.ruː.əri / (BrE)
The middle part of the month of February, usually considered to be around the 14th to the 16th.

Examples

mid-February weather
By mid-February, most students have settled into their new routines.
The conference is scheduled for mid-February each year.
We usually expect the coldest weather in mid-February.
