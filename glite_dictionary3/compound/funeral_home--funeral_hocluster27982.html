funeral home

noun
establishment for funerals and memorial services
Pronunciation: / ˈfjunɚəl hoʊm / (AmE), / ˈfjʊənərəl həʊm / (BrE)
A place where bodies are prepared for burial or cremation and where people can gather to pay their last respects to the deceased.
