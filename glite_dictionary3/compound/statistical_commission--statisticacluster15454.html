Statistical Commission

proper noun
UN body for international statistics
Pronunciation: / stəˈtɪstɪkəl kəˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəl kəˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the main body of the United Nations responsible for coordinating international statistical activities and standards among member states.
