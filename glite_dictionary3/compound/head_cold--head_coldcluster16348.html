head cold

noun
mild cold affecting the nose and head
Pronunciation: / hɛd koʊld / (AmE), / hɛd kəʊld / (BrE)
a mild viral infection affecting the nose and upper respiratory passages, causing congestion, sneezing, and headache

Examples

catch a head cold
His head cold caused him to sneeze and have a stuffy nose all day.
If you have a head cold, it's important to drink plenty of fluids and rest.
