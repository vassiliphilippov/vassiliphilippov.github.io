dysostosis multiplex

noun
hereditary disorder affecting bone development
Pronunciation: / daɪˌsɔːstoʊsɪs ˈmʌltɪplɛks / (AmE), / dɪˌsɒstəʊsɪs ˈmʌltɪplɛks / (BrE)
A rare hereditary disorder caused by a defect in mucopolysaccharide metabolism, resulting in severe skeletal abnormalities and often intellectual disability.
