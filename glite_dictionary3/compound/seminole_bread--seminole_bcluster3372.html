Seminole bread

noun
woody plant yielding arrowroot starch
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmɪˌnoʊl brɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪnəʊl brɛd / (BrE)
A small, tough, woody plant (Zamia integrifolia) native to Florida, the West Indies, and Cuba, whose roots and stems yield an edible arrowroot starch.
