personal representative

noun
person managing another's affairs, often legally
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsənl ˌrɛprɪˌzɛntəˈtɪv / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsənl ˌrɛprɪˌzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person who is legally appointed to manage the affairs or estate of another person, especially after their death

Examples

personal representative of an estate
As the personal representative, she was responsible for distributing the assets according to the will.
The court appointed a personal representative to settle his estate after he passed away.
The insurance company required documentation from the deceased's personal representative before releasing any funds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – administrator: a person legally appointed to manage and dispose of the estate of an intestate
  • synonym – executor: a person appointed to carry out the terms of a will
