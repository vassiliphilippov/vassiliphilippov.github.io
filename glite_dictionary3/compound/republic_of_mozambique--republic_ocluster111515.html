Republic of Mozambique

proper noun
country in southeastern Africa
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌmoʊzəmˈbiːk / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌməʊzæmˈbiːk / (BrE)
