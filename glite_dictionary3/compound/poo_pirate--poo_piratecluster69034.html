poo pirate

noun
humorous term for animal-waste collector or a dog that buries waste
Pronunciation: / puː ˈpaɪrət / (AmE), / puː ˈpaɪərət / (BrE)
a humorous or playful term for a person who collects animal waste, especially dog feces, or for a dog that buries its waste
