head gate

noun
flow-control gate at canal or lock entrance
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛd ˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɛd ˌɡeɪt / (BrE)
a gate or movable barrier at the entrance of a canal, lock, or channel, used to control the flow of water
