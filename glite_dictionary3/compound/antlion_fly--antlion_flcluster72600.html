antlion fly

noun
insect like a dragonfly; larvae are called doodlebugs
Pronunciation: / ˈæntˌlaɪ.ən flaɪ / (AmE), / ˈæntˌlaɪ.ən flaɪ / (BrE)
A winged insect resembling a dragonfly, whose larvae (called doodlebugs) dig conical pits to trap ants and other small insects.
