wire-haired pointing griffon

noun
a breed of medium-sized, wiry-coated gun dog
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərˌhɛrd ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ɡrɪˈfɑn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌheəd ˈpɔɪntɪŋ ɡrɪˈfɒn / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized gun dog with a long head, a downy undercoat, and a harsh wiry outer coat, developed mainly in France
