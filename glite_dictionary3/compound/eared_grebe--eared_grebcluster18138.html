eared grebe

noun
small grebe with yellow ear tufts
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrd ˌɡrib / (AmE), / ˈɪəd ˌɡriːb / (BrE)
a small grebe with yellow ear tufts and a black neck, found in parts of Eurasia, southern Africa, and western United States
