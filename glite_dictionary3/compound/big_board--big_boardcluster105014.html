Big Board

noun
large stock price display at an exchange
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ bɔrd / (AmE), / bɪɡ bɔːd / (BrE)
a large electronic or physical display board showing current stock prices, especially at a stock exchange

Examples

the Big Board shows prices
Many traders watched the Big Board anxiously as the market fluctuated throughout the day.
The Big Board at the New York Stock Exchange is an iconic symbol of Wall Street.
Tourists often take photos of the Big Board when they visit the stock exchange.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ticker tape: a device that prints stock prices as they are updated, formerly used in trading floors
