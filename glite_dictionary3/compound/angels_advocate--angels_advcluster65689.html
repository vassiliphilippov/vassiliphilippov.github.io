angel's advocate

noun
one who argues for virtuous or unpopular causes
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈædvəkət / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈædvəkət / (BrE)
a person who defends or promotes positive, virtuous, or often unpopular causes, especially in opposition to criticism or skepticism

Examples

angel's advocate for good causes
As an angel's advocate, he spoke up for those who couldn't defend themselves.
