cylinder block

noun
main casing for engine cylinders
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlɪndər blɑk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlɪndə blɒk / (BrE)
the solid metal casting in an internal combustion engine that contains the cylinders and cooling ducts
