anonymous review

noun
a review with undisclosed author
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑː.nə.məs rɪˈvjuː / (AmE), / əˈnɒn.ɪ.məs rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
a review in which the identity of the author is not revealed

Examples
