rooming house

noun
house renting rooms to tenants
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈruːmɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A house in which rooms are rented out to tenants, usually with shared common facilities such as bathrooms or kitchens.

Examples

rooming house with shared kitchen
After graduation, he stayed in a cheap rooming house until he found permanent housing.
She moved into a rooming house downtown after starting her new job.
The city is passing new regulations on rooming houses to improve tenant safety.
