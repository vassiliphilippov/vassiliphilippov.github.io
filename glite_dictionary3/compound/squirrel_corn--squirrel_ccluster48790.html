squirrel corn

noun
North American plant with tuberous roots
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːəl kɔrn / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəl kɔːn / (BrE)
A North American woodland plant (Dicentra canadensis) with creamy-white flowers and small, yellow tuber-like roots that resemble kernels of corn.
