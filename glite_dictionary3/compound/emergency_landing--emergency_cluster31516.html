emergency landing

noun
unscheduled aircraft landing in an emergency
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɝːdʒənsi ˈlændɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi ˈlændɪŋ / (BrE)
an unscheduled landing made by an aircraft due to unforeseen circumstances such as engine failure or adverse weather conditions

Examples

emergency landing at airport
During heavy fog, the crew prepared for an emergency landing at a nearby airport.
Thanks to her quick thinking, everyone survived the emergency landing safely.
The pilot made an emergency landing after the engine failed mid-flight.
