vernal equinox

noun
time in spring when day and night are equal
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrnəl ˈiːkwənɑks /; / ˈvɜrnəl ˈikwəˌnɑks / (AmE), / ˈvɜːnəl ˈiːkwɪnɒks / (BrE)
the time in spring when day and night are of equal length, marking the start of spring in the northern hemisphere, when the Sun crosses the celestial equator moving northward
