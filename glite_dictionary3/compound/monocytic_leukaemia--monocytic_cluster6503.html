monocytic leukaemia

noun
leukemia with excess monocytes in blood
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˌsaɪtɪk luˈkiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌmɒn.əˌsaɪ.tɪk ljʊəˈkiː.mi.ə / (BrE)
A type of leukemia characterized by an abnormal increase of monocytes and monoblasts in the blood.
