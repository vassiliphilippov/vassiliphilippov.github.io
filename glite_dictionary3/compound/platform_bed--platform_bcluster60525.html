platform bed

noun
bed frame without springs
Pronunciation: / ˈplæt.fɔrm bɛd / (AmE), / ˈplætfɔːm bɛd / (BrE)
A bed frame that consists of a sturdy, raised, flat surface that supports a mattress without the need for box springs.

Examples

platform bed frame
A platform bed eliminates the need for a box spring, making it a practical choice.
Many platform beds come with built-in storage drawers underneath.
