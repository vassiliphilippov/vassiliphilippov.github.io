hairshirted life

noun
austere or self-denying lifestyle
Pronunciation: / hɛrˌʃɜrtɪd laɪf / (AmE), / heəˌʃɜːtɪd laɪf / (BrE)
A life characterized by self-denial, strict austerity, or acceptance of hardship, often for moral or religious reasons.

Examples

live a hairshirted life
After his financial downfall, he chose a hairshirted life to rebuild his character.
She adopted a hairshirted life, giving up all unnecessary comforts.
The monk's hairshirted life inspired others to donate to the needy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – asceticism: severe self-discipline and avoidance of all forms of indulgence
  • antonym – luxurious life: a life of comfort and self-indulgence
