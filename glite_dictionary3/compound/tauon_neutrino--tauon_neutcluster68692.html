tauon neutrino

noun
neutrino linked to the tau lepton
Pronunciation: / ˌtaʊ.ɒn nuˈtrinoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtaʊ.ɒn njuːˈtriːnəʊ / (BrE)
A type of neutrino associated with the tau lepton, one of the three known neutrinos in particle physics.

Examples

Detection of a tauon neutrino is extremely challenging due to its weak interaction with matter.
