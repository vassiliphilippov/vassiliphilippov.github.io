to rain check

verb
postpone or decline now, reschedule later

(informal) to postpone or decline something now while indicating a desire or intention to accept or reschedule it at a later time.

Examples

to rain check dinner plans

She decided to rain check the meeting after learning most team members were ill.
He texted, 'Can I rain check dinner?' and suggested rescheduling for Saturday night.
