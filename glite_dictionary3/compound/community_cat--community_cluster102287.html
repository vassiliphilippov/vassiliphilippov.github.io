community cat

noun
cat cared for by neighborhood residents
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti kæt / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪti kat / (BrE)
A cat that lives outdoors and is cared for by several people in a neighborhood, often not belonging to a single household.

Examples

community cat in the neighborhood
Children in the area often stop to play with the friendly community cat near the park.
Neighbors pitched in to pay for the community cat to be spayed and vaccinated.
The community cat lounged comfortably on the front porch, waiting for its usual meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stray cat: a homeless or lost domestic cat living outdoors
