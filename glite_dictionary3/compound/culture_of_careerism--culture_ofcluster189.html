culture of careerism

noun
focus on career advancement above other values
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃər əv kəˈrɪəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃə əv kəˈrɪəˌrɪzəm / (BrE)
an environment or atmosphere in which advancing one's career is given overriding importance, often at the expense of other values

Examples

culture of careerism in companies
Many young professionals feel pressured by the culture of careerism in their industry.
She challenged the culture of careerism by advocating for a better work-life balance.
