to run downstairs

verb
move quickly to a lower floor
Pronunciation: / rʌn daʊnˈstɛɹz / (AmE), / rʌn daʊnˈsteəz / (BrE)
to move quickly on foot to a lower floor, especially by using the stairs

Examples

to run downstairs quickly
He runs downstairs every morning to grab the newspaper before breakfast.
Hearing his name called, Tom ran downstairs to join his friends.
When the fire alarm went off, she ran downstairs as fast as she could.
