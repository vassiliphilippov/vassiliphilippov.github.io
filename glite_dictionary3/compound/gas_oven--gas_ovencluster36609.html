gas oven

noun
oven powered by gas
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ˈʌvən / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈʌvən / (BrE)
A domestic oven that is powered by gas, used for cooking and baking.

Examples

bake bread in the gas oven
Every evening, delicious smells filled the kitchen as the bread baked in the gas oven.
The recipe suggests using a gas oven, but it can work in an electric one too.
We decided to replace our old stove with a new gas oven for better temperature control.
