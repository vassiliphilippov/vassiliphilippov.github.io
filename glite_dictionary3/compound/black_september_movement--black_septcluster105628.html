Black September Movement

noun
Palestinian militant group active in the 1970s
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈsɛptəmbɚ ˈmuːvmənt / (AmE), / blæk ˈsɛptəmbə ˈmuːvmənt / (BrE)
a Palestinian militant organization responsible for high-profile attacks in the early 1970s, including the murder of 11 Israeli athletes at the 1972 Munich Olympics.
