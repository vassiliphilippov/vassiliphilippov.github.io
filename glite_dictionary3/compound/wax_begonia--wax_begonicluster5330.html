wax begonia

noun
a hybrid ornamental begonia plant
Pronunciation: / wæks bɪˈgoʊniə / (AmE), / waks bɪˈgəʊniə / (BrE)
A hybrid begonia plant with waxy leaves and small clusters of white, pink, or red flowers, commonly grown as a bedding plant.
