powder room

noun
women's restroom (public place)
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.dɚ ˌruːm /; / ˈpaʊdɚ ruːm / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.də ˌruːm /; / ˈpaʊdə ruːm / (BrE)
a polite term for a bathroom, especially a women's restroom in a public place or a small guest bathroom in a home
