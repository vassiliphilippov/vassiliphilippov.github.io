duffel coat

noun
a hooded coat fastened with toggles
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌfəl koʊt / (AmE), / ˈdʌfəl kəʊt / (BrE)
a thick, warm coat, typically with a hood, made from duffel material and fastened with toggle buttons

Examples

warm duffel coat
In winter, he prefers a duffel coat because it keeps him warm and comfortable.
She wore her favorite duffel coat on the chilly walk to the market.
The duffel coat is easily recognized by its distinctive toggle fastenings.
