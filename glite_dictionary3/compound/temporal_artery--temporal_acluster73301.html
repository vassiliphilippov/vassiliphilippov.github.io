temporal artery

noun
artery supplying temporal region
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərəl ˈɑrtəri / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
any of several arteries located on either side of the head, supplying blood to the temporal region of the brain and scalp
