proscenium wall

noun
wall separating stage from auditorium in a theater
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsiːniəm wɔl / (AmE), / prəˈsiːniəm wɔːl / (BrE)
the wall that separates the stage from the auditorium in a modern theater, often containing the proscenium arch through which the audience views the stage
