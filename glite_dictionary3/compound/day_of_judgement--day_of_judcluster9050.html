Day of Judgement

noun
day when God judges all people (Christian belief)
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ əv ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (BrE)
In Christian belief, the future day when God will judge every person and decide their eternal fate, following the final battle between good and evil.

Examples

the Day of Judgement arrives
According to Christian doctrine, the Day of Judgement follows the events of Armageddon.
Many believers await the Day of Judgement as a time of final reckoning.
She feared for her soul and prayed to be forgiven before the Day of Judgement.
