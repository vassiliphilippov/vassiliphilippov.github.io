organ of speech

noun
anatomical structure used in speech
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɡən əv spiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡən əv spiːtʃ / (BrE)
any of the anatomical structures involved in the production of speech, such as the tongue, lips, vocal cords, or larynx.

Examples

organ of speech like tongue
Damage to any organ of speech can affect a person's ability to communicate clearly.
