back circle

noun
gymnastic feat bending back until toes touch head
Pronunciation: / bæk ˈsɜːrkəl / (AmE), / bæk ˈsɜːkl / (BrE)
a gymnastic feat in which an acrobat arches their back from a lying position and bends their knees until their toes touch their head
