for show

x
as a superficial display
Pronunciation: / fɔɹ ʃoʊ / (AmE), / fɔː ʃəʊ / (BrE)
intended to impress others but not serving a practical purpose; superficial display.

Examples

for show only
His generosity was all for show and didn't extend to genuine help.
She bought an expensive vase for show, never intending to use it.
The company installed new lights for show, but the factory remained inefficient.
