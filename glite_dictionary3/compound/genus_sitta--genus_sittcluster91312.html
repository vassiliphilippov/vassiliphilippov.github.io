genus Sitta

noun
type genus of the nuthatch family
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtə / (AmE), / ˈsɪtə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Sittidae, comprising the nuthatches, a group of small passerine birds known for climbing trees headfirst.
