chuck short ribs

noun
beef cut from between chuck and brisket
Pronunciation: / tʃʌk ʃɔrt rɪbz / (AmE), / tʃʌk ʃɔːt rɪbz / (BrE)
A cut of beef taken from the area between the chuck and the brisket, consisting of short sections of rib bone with surrounding meat.

Chuck short ribs are perfect for slow-cooked dishes like stews and barbecues.
