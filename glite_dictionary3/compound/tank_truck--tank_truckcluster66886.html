tank truck

noun
vehicle carrying liquids or gases on roads
Pronunciation: / tæŋk trʌk / (AmE), / tæŋk trʌk / (BrE)
a large motor vehicle with a tank for carrying liquids, gases, or bulk materials by road

Examples

a tank truck delivers fuel

Drinking water was distributed to the affected area using a tank truck after the storm.
The tank truck delivered thousands of gallons of fuel to the service station.
