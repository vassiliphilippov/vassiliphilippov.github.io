balsam pear

noun
tropical vine with yellow-orange fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔl.səm ˌpɛr / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.səm ˌpɪə / (BrE)
A tropical Old World vine, Momordica charantia, cultivated for its yellow-orange, bitter fruit, often used in Asian cuisine and traditional medicine.
