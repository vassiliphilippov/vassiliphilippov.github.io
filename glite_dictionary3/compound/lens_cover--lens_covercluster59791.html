lens cover

noun
protective cap for a lens
Pronunciation: / lɛnz ˈkʌvɚ / (AmE), / lɛnz ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
A removable cap or cover designed to protect a lens from dust, scratches, and dirt when not in use.

Examples

lens cover for camera
A lens cover helps prevent scratches and dust buildup on your camera lens.
He lost the lens cover for his binoculars during the hike.
Please remember to put the lens cover back on the camera after using it.
