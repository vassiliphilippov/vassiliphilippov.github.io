to mushed up

verb
squashed or crushed; made soft and pulpy
Pronunciation: / mʌʃt ʌp / (AmE), / mʌʃt ʌp / (BrE)
Squashed or crushed to a soft, pulpy state by pressing, mixing, or crushing.

Examples

mushed up bananas
After being stepped on, the cake was all mushed up and looked unappetizing.
She mushed up the peas before feeding them to the baby.
The bananas in my lunchbox got completely mushed up on the way to school.
