American parasol

noun
a pale agaric mushroom with a swollen-based stalk
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈpærəˌsɑl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈpærəsɒl / (BrE)
A type of agaric mushroom characterized by a pale cap and a stalk that is swollen near the base.
