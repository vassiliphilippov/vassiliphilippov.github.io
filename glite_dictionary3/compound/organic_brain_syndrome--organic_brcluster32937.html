organic brain syndrome

noun
mental disorder from brain damage or disease
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈɡænɪk breɪn ˈsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ɔːˈɡanɪk breɪn ˈsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
A mental disorder caused by physical injury or disease affecting the structure or function of the brain.

Examples
