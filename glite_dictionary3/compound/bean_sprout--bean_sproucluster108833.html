bean sprout

noun
edible young shoot from a bean
Pronunciation: / bin spraʊt / (AmE), / biːn spaʊt / (BrE)
a young shoot that has just sprouted from a bean seed, especially mung beans, lentils, or soybeans, often used as a vegetable in cooking

Examples

fresh bean sprout salad
I added fresh bean sprout to the salad for extra crunch.
Most Asian stir-fries include bean sprout for flavor and texture.
She garnished the soup with a handful of bean sprout before serving.
