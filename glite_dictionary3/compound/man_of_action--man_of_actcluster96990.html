man of action

noun
decisive, action-oriented man
Pronunciation: / mæn əv ˈækʃən / (AmE), / mæn əv ˈækʃən / (BrE)
a man who tends to act decisively and quickly rather than spending much time thinking or planning

Examples

decisive man of action
As a man of action, he preferred to solve problems directly rather than discussing them endlessly.
Many admired him as a man of action, always ready to face challenges head-on.
The company needed a man of action to take charge during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym – man of thought: a man who is contemplative and thoughtful rather than action-oriented
