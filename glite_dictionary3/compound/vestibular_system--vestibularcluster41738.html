vestibular system

noun
system of organs for balance and orientation
Pronunciation: / vɛˈstɪbjələr ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / vɛˈstɪbjʊlə ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of organs in the inner ear that is responsible for maintaining balance and sensing spatial orientation

Damage to the vestibular system can result in severe balance disorders.
