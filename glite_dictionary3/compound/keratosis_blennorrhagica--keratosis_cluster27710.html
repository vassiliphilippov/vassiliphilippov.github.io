keratosis blennorrhagica

noun
scaly rash linked to Reiter's syndrome
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛrəˈtoʊsɪs blɛnˌɔrəˈdʒaɪkə / (AmE), / ˌkɛrəˈtəʊsɪs blɛnˌɒrəˈdʒaɪkə / (BrE)
A skin disease marked by scaly rashes on the palms and soles, typically associated with Reiter's syndrome.

Patients with keratosis blennorrhagica often develop scaly lesions on the soles of their feet.
The presence of keratosis blennorrhagica is an important clue in the diagnosis of Reiter's syndrome.
