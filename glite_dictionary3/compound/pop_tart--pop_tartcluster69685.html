pop tart

noun
sweet, filled toaster pastry
Pronunciation: / pɑp tɑrt / (AmE), / pɒp tɑːt / (BrE)
A brand of thin, sweet pastry with a filling, often fruit-flavored, that is typically heated in a toaster.
