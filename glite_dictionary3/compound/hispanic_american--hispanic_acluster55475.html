Hispanic American

noun
American with Spanish-speaking heritage
Pronunciation: / hɪˈspænɪk əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / hɪˈspænɪk əˈmɛrɪkən / (BrE)
an American citizen or resident who has origins in a Spanish-speaking country, especially in Latin America or Spain

Examples

a Hispanic American family
First- and second-generation Hispanic Americans often speak both English and Spanish at home.
Many Hispanic Americans live in the southwestern United States.
The festival celebrated the culture and history of Hispanic Americans.
