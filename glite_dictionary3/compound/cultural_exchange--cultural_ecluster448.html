cultural exchange

noun
sharing of cultural ideas and traditions
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃɚəl ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃ(ə)rəl ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a process in which people from different cultures share ideas, traditions, and customs with each other

Examples

cultural exchange program
Participating in a cultural exchange program allowed her to experience life in another country.
The university organizes annual cultural exchange events to promote diversity on campus.
Through cultural exchange, students learn to appreciate and respect other traditions.
