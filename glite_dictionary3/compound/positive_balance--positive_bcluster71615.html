positive balance

noun
account with credits exceeding debits
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːzɪtɪv ˈbæləns / (AmE), / ˈpɒzɪtɪv ˈbæləns / (BrE)
an account balance in which credits exceed debits, resulting in the bank owing money to the customer

Examples

positive balance in account
Despite several withdrawals, his savings account maintained a positive balance throughout the year.
It's important to keep a positive balance to avoid overdraft fees at most banks.
The company finished the quarter with a positive balance in its checking account.
