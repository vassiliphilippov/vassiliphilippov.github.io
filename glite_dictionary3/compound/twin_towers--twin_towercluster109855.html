twin towers

noun
the two World Trade Center skyscrapers in New York City
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪn ˈtaʊɚz / (AmE), / ˈtwɪn ˈtaʊəz / (BrE)
the two identical skyscrapers in New York City known as the World Trade Center, which stood from 1973 until their destruction in the terrorist attacks of September 11, 2001.
