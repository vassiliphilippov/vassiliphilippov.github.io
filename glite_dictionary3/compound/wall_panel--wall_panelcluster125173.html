wall panel

noun
panel forming part of a wall
Pronunciation: / wɔl ˈpænəl / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈpænəl / (BrE)
A flat, typically rectangular piece of material that forms part of a wall, usually as part of the interior or exterior surface.

Examples

wall panel installation
Moisture-resistant wall panels are recommended for use in bathrooms and kitchens.
She chose oak wall panels for the living room to give it a classic look.
The carpenter installed a new wall panel to replace the damaged section.

  • synonym – paneling: material or boards used to cover walls or ceilings
