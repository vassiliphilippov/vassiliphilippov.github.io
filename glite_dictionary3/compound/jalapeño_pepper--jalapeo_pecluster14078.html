jalapeño pepper

noun
a medium-sized, spicy Mexican chili pepper
Pronunciation: / hɑːləˈpeɪnjoʊ ˈpɛpər / (AmE), / ˌhaləˈpeɪnjəʊ ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
A medium-sized, spicy chili pepper of Mexican origin, usually green or red when ripe, commonly used in cooking.
