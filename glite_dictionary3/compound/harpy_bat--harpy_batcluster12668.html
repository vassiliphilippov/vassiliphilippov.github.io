harpy bat

noun
a fruit bat of the genus Nyctimene
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑr.pi bæt / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.pi bæt / (BrE)
any of several fruit bats of the genus Nyctimene, known for their tubular, outward-facing nostrils.

Examples

a harpy bat in rainforest
Scientists discovered a new species of harpy bat in the rainforest last year.
The harpy bat is easily recognized by its unusual tube-shaped nostrils.
Unlike most bats, the harpy bat prefers eating fruit and nectar at night.
