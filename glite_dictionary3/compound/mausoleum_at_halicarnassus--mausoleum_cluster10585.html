Mausoleum at Halicarnassus

noun
ancient tomb, one of Seven Wonders of the World
Pronunciation: / məˌzoliːəm æt ˌhælɪˈkɑrnəsəs / (AmE), / məˌzɒliːəm ət ˌhælɪˈkɑːnəsəs / (BrE)
a monumental tomb built in 352 BC at Halicarnassus as a memorial to King Mausolus, regarded as one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World; destroyed in 1402
