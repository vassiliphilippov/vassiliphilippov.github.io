loose smut

noun
fungal disease of cereal grains
Pronunciation: / luːs smʌt / (AmE), / luːs smʌt / (BrE)
a disease of cereal grains (like wheat and barley) where the head of the plant turns into a powdery mass of fungal spores

Examples

loose smut infection in wheat
