os triquetrum

noun
a triangular wrist bone
Pronunciation: / oʊs traɪˈkwɛtrəm / (AmE), / ɒs trɪˈkwiːtrəm / (BrE)
a wrist bone that articulates with the pisiform, hamate, and lunate bones
