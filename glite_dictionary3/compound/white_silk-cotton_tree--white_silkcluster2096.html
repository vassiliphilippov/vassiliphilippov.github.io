white silk-cotton tree

noun
large tropical tree, source of kapok fiber
Pronunciation: / waɪt sɪlk ˈkɑtən tri / (AmE), / waɪt sɪlk ˈkɒtən triː / (BrE)
A massive tropical tree with deeply ridged trunk and large pods containing seeds covered with silky floss; source of kapok fiber.
