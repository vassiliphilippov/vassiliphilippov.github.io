elapsed time

noun
time that has passed during an event
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæpst taɪm / (AmE), / ɪˈlæpst taɪm / (BrE)
the amount of time that has passed while something has been happening or between two events

Examples

elapsed time between events
The elapsed time between calls made it difficult to track the problem.
The elapsed time for the race was just under two hours.
You should record the elapsed time from the start to the end of the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – duration: the length of time that something lasts
  • synonym – interval: a period of time between two events
