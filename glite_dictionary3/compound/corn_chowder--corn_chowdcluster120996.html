corn chowder

noun
thick soup with corn, often creamy
Pronunciation: / kɔrn ˈtʃaʊdɚ / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈtʃaʊdə / (BrE)
A thick soup made with corn, milk or cream, and often other ingredients such as potatoes and onions.
