sea bird

noun
bird living near the sea or ocean
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː bɝːd / (AmE), / ˈsiː bɜːd / (BrE)
a bird that lives mainly near the sea or over the open ocean, such as a gull, pelican, or albatross

Examples

a sea bird flying
Many sea birds nest on rocky cliffs along the coastline.
Oil spills pose a serious threat to sea birds and their habitats.
We watched as a flock of sea birds dived for fish near the harbor.

Related Terms

  • antonym – land bird: a bird living mainly inland, away from the sea
  • synonym – seagull: a common type of sea bird; a gull living near the sea
