silicic acid

noun
a jellylike weak acid from hydrated silica
Pronunciation: / sɪˌlɪsɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / sɪˌlɪsɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
a jellylike weak acid, formed by hydrating silica, and existing chiefly in solution or as insoluble silicate minerals

Examples
