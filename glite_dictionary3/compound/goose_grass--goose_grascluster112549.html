goose grass

noun
a type of coarse annual grass found worldwide
Pronunciation: / ˈɡus ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈɡuːs ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a coarse or weedy annual grass, often found in lawns and pastures, sometimes used for hay (such as Eleusine or similar grasses)
