British Imperial System

noun
system of weights and measures used in Britain
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɪtɪʃ ɪmˈpɪriəl ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌbrɪtɪʃ ɪmˈpɪəriəl ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the system of weights and measures formerly used in Britain and some other countries, based on units such as the foot, pound, and pint
