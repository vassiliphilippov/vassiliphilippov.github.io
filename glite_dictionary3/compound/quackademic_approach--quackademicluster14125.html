quackademic approach

noun
pseudo-academic or unscientific method
Pronunciation: / kwækəˈdɛmɪk əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / kwækəˈdɛmɪk əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A method or practice that pretends to be academic or scientific but lacks real scientific rigor or legitimacy.

Examples
