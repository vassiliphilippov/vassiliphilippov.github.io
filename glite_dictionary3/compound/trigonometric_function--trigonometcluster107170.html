trigonometric function

noun
function relating angles to triangle side ratios
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪɡənəˈmɛtrɪk ˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrɪɡənəˈmɛtrɪk ˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
a mathematical function of an angle, often defined as the ratio of the lengths of sides in a right triangle, such as sine, cosine, or tangent
