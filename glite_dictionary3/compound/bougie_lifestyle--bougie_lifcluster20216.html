bougie lifestyle

noun
extravagant, upper-class way of living
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːʒi ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈbuːʒi ˈlaɪfˌstaɪl / (BrE)
A way of living characterized by luxury, extravagance, or association with the upper class, often with a sense of conspicuous consumption or aspiration.
