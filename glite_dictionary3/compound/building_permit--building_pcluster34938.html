building permit

noun
official document for construction approval
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪl.dɪŋ pɚˌmɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.dɪŋ pəˌmɪt / (BrE)
an official document granting permission to construct, alter, or demolish a building on a specific site

Examples

a building permit application
The city council refused to issue a building permit for the proposed shopping center.
Without a building permit, construction work may be halted by local authorities.
You need a building permit before starting the renovation on your house.

Related Terms

  • synonym – planning permission: official approval to build, often broader in scope (esp. UK)
