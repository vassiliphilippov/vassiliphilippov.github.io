Bohai Sea

proper noun
inlet of the Yellow Sea, China
Pronunciation: / boʊˈhaɪ siː / (AmE), / bəʊˈhaɪ siː / (BrE)
an inlet of the Yellow Sea on the northeast coast of China, near Beijing and Tianjin

Examples
