trip wire

noun
wire that triggers device when tripped over
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪp ˌwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪp ˌwaɪə / (BrE)
A wire stretched near the ground, designed to activate a trap, alarm, camera, or weapon when someone trips over it.

Examples

hidden trip wire
A hidden trip wire set off the alarm as soon as he entered the room.
Soldiers are trained to look for trip wires while moving through enemy territory.
The camera was activated by a simple trip wire stretched across the path.
