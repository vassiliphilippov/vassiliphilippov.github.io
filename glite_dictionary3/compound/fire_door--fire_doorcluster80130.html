fire door

noun
fire-resistant door for blocking fire spread
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ dɔːr / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə dɔː / (BrE)
A door made of fire-resistant material, designed to prevent the spread of fire and smoke between sections of a building.

fire door in a building
