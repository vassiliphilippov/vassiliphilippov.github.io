Irish soda bread

noun
round loaf made with soda and buttermilk
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈsoʊdə brɛd / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ˈsəʊdə brɛd / (BrE)
A round loaf of bread made with baking soda and buttermilk, often containing caraway seeds and raisins.
