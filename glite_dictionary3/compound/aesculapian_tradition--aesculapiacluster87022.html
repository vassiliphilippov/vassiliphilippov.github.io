Aesculapian tradition

noun
tradition in medicine and healing
Pronunciation: / ˌɛskjəˈleɪpiən trəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːskjʊˈleɪpɪən trəˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
the system or practice of medicine and healing, especially as derived from or associated with classical traditions or the school of Asclepius
