minke whale

noun
a small baleen whale species
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪŋki weɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɪŋki weɪl / (BrE)
a small species of baleen whale found in coastal waters of the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, distinguished by its relatively small size among finback whales

Examples

a minke whale swimming
During the cruise, we spotted a minke whale surfacing near the bow of the boat.
Researchers study the migration patterns of the minke whale to better understand its behavior.
The minke whale is often identified by its sleek, dark body and small dorsal fin.
