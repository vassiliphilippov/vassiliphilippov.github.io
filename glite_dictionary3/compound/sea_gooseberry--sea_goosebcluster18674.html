sea gooseberry

noun
marine ctenophore with cilia
Pronunciation: / si ˈɡusˌbɛri / (AmE), / siː ˈɡuːsbəri / (BrE)
A small marine animal (ctenophore) with a rounded, transparent body and rows of cilia used for swimming.
