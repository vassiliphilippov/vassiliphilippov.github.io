Republic of Namibia

proper noun
country in southwestern Africa
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv nəˈmɪbiə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv nəˈmɪbiə / (BrE)
A country in southwestern Africa on the Atlantic coast, formerly known as South West Africa, which gained independence from South Africa in 1990.

Examples

the Republic of Namibia borders Angola
Mining is a major industry in the Republic of Namibia, especially the extraction of diamonds and uranium.
The Republic of Namibia became independent in 1990 after years of struggle against South African rule.
The president of the Republic of Namibia addressed the United Nations General Assembly.
