Enewetak Atoll

proper noun
atoll in the Marshall Islands, WWII and atomic test site
Pronunciation: / ɛˈneɪwiˌtɑk əˈtɑl / (AmE), / ɛˈniːwɪtæk əˈtɒl / (BrE)
an atoll in the Marshall Islands, known as the site of a major World War II battle and later as a location for U.S. atomic bomb testing
