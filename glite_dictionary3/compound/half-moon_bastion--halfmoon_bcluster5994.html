half-moon bastion

noun
curved bastion used in 16th-century fortifications
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌmun ˈbæstiən / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌmuːn ˈbæstɪən / (BrE)
a type of curved bastion used in 16th-century fortifications, shaped like a half-moon and designed to provide defensive fire but later abandoned due to strategic disadvantages
