Brillo pad

noun
abrasive metal cleaning pad
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪloʊ pæd / (AmE), / ˈbrɪləʊ pad / (BrE)
A small, abrasive metal pad used for scrubbing and cleaning tough stains, especially on pots, pans, and kitchen surfaces.

Examples

scrub with a Brillo pad
Please hand me the Brillo pad so I can clean the stubborn stains from the frying pan.
She always keeps a spare Brillo pad under the sink for tough cleaning jobs.
The old grill rack came out shiny after I scrubbed it with a Brillo pad.
