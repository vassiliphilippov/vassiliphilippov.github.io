marine glue

noun
waterproof adhesive for marine uses
Pronunciation: / məˈrin ɡlu / (AmE), / məˈriːn ɡluː / (BrE)
a waterproof adhesive or cement used especially for shipbuilding and repairing objects exposed to water

Examples

marine glue for boats
He coated the bottom of the boat with marine glue to prevent leaks.
Shipbuilders often prefer marine glue for sealing joints that will be submerged in seawater.
The repair required a strong, water-resistant adhesive, so we used marine glue.
