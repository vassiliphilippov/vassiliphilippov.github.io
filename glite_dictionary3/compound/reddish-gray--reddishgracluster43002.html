reddish-gray

adjective
grey with a hint of red
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdɪʃˌɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃˌɡreɪ / (BrE)
Of a grey color tinged with red; having a slight red hue in a grey shade.

Examples

reddish-gray scarf

The sky took on a reddish-gray hue at dawn, hinting at a warm day ahead.
The stones along the riverbank were mostly reddish-gray, worn smooth by years of water flow.
