trenchant remark

noun
sharp, insightful comment
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛntʃənt rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈtrɛntʃənt rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment or observation that is sharply insightful and often cutting or penetrating

Examples

make a trenchant remark
During the meeting, she offered a trenchant remark that revealed the core of the problem.
His trenchant remarks about corporate ethics challenged the industry to reconsider its practices.
The critic's trenchant remark about the film's flaws sparked lively debate among the audience.
