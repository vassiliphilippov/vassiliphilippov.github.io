back door

noun
unauthorized computer system access method
Pronunciation: / ˈbæk ˌdɔr /; / bæk dɔr / (AmE), / ˈbæk ˌdɔː /; / bæk dɔː / (BrE)
a secret or unofficial way of getting access to a place, system, or opportunity, often by avoiding normal rules or controls
