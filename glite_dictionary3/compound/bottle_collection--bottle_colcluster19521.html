bottle collection

noun
gathering bottles for reuse or recycling
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːdəl kəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəl kəˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the activity of gathering bottles, especially for the purpose of reuse or recycling

Examples

weekly bottle collection
As part of her job, she oversees the weekly bottle collection from local restaurants.
He started a bottle collection to raise funds for charity.
The city organized a bottle collection event to encourage recycling among residents.
