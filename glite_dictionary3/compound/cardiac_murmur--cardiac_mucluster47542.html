cardiac murmur

noun
abnormal heart sound; possible valve issue
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdiˌæk ˈmɝːmɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdiæk ˈmɜːmə / (BrE)
an abnormal sound heard during a heartbeat, sometimes indicating a problem with the heart valves or blood flow
