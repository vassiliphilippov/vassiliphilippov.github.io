fraud in law

noun
presumed fraud regardless of intent (legal)
Pronunciation: / frɔd ɪn lɔ / (AmE), / frɔːd ɪn lɔː / (BrE)
A type of fraud presumed from the circumstances, regardless of the fraudster's intent.
