learning curve

noun
graph of progression in learning over time
Pronunciation: / ˈlɝːnɪŋ kɝːv /; / ˈlɝː.nɪŋ kɝːv / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnɪŋ kɜːv /; / ˈlɜː.nɪŋ kɜːv / (BrE)
