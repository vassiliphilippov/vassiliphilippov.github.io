English setter

noun
breed of dog with silky white coat, England
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈsɛtər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈsɛtə / (BrE)
a breed of dog originating in England, with a long silky white coat, often marked with colored patches, and a feathered tail, bred primarily as a hunting and retrieving dog
