vestibulocochlear nerve

noun
cranial nerve for hearing and balance
Pronunciation: / vɛˌstɪbjəloʊˈkoʊkliɚ nɜrv / (AmE), / vɛˌstɪbjʊl.əʊˈkəʊkli.ə nɜːv / (BrE)
the eighth cranial nerve, responsible for transmitting sound and balance information from the inner ear to the brain
