phenyl salicylate

noun
a chemical used in lotions and enteric pills
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːnəl səˈlɪsɪleɪt / (AmE), / ˈfiːnɪl səˈlɪsɪleɪt / (BrE)
a white, crystalline powder used as a light-absorbing agent in sun creams, and as a preservative, antiseptic, or coating for enteric-release pills
