absorbent cotton

noun
cotton treated to increase absorbency
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɔrbənt ˈkɑtn̩ / (AmE), / əbˈzɔːbənt ˈkɒtn̩ / (BrE)
cotton that has been made absorbent by removing its natural wax, commonly used for medical or hygiene purposes
