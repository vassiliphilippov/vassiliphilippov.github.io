gun emplacement

noun
a prepared position for a gun
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn ɪmˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / ɡʌn ɪmˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
a prepared position or structure where a gun is mounted and operated, typically for defense or attack in military operations

Examples

military gun emplacement
A new gun emplacement was constructed on the hillside overlooking the valley.
The soldiers fortified the gun emplacement to withstand enemy fire.
They camouflaged the gun emplacement so it would not be spotted from the air.
