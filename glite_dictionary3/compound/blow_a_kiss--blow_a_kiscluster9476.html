to blow a kiss

verb
gesture of affection; kiss blown toward someone
Pronunciation: / bloʊ ə kɪs / (AmE), / bləʊ ə kɪs / (BrE)
to make a gesture of affection by kissing one's hand and then blowing the kiss towards someone

Examples

to blow a kiss to someone
After finishing her speech, she blew a kiss to the supportive crowd.
As the train pulled away, he blew a kiss to his waiting family.
She blows a kiss to her daughter every night before bedtime.
