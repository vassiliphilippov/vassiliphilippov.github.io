skin eruption

noun
outbreak of spots or rashes on skin
Pronunciation: / skɪn ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / skɪn ɪˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
a sudden appearance of raised or inflamed areas on the skin, often as a symptom of a disease or irritation

Examples

skin eruption after allergy
If you notice a skin eruption spreading quickly, consult a doctor immediately.
Some medications can cause an allergic reaction resulting in a skin eruption.
