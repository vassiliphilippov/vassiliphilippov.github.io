card catalogue

noun
library catalogue system using cards
Pronunciation: / kɑrd ˈkætəlɔg /; / kɑrd ˈkætəlˌɔg / (AmE), / kɑːd ˈkatəlɒg / (BrE)
a physical library catalogue where each item is listed on a separate card, usually stored in drawers or boxes
