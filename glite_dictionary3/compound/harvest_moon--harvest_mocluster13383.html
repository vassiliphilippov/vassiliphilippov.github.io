harvest moon

noun
the full moon closest to the September equinox
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrvɪst mun /; / ˈhɑrvəst mun / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪst muːn / (BrE)
the full moon that appears closest to the September or autumn equinox, often associated with the harvest season and appearing larger or more orange than usual
