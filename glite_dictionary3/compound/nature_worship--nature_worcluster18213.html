nature worship

noun
worship of natural forces or phenomena
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪtʃɚ ˌwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈneɪtʃə ˌwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
a religious system in which natural forces and phenomena are regarded as deities and are worshipped
