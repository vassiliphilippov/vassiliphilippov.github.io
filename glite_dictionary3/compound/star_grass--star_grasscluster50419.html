star grass

noun
plant with grasslike leaves and yellow star-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / stɑr ɡræs / (AmE), / stɑː ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
Any plant of the genus Hypoxis, having long grasslike leaves and yellow star-shaped flowers, found in Africa, Australia, southern Asia, and North America.

Examples

star grass yellow flowers
Gardeners admire star grass for its attractive yellow flowers and easy maintenance.
Some species of star grass are used in traditional medicine in southern Africa.
The meadows were filled with blooming star grass during the early summer.
