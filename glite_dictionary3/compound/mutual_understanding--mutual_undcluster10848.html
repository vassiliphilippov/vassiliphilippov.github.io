mutual understanding

noun
shared sympathy or agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːtʃuəl ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmjuːtʃu.əl ˌʌndəˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
a situation in which two or more people share similar feelings or opinions and are able to sympathize with each other
