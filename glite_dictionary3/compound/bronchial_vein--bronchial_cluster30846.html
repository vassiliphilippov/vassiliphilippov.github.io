bronchial vein

noun
vein draining the bronchi
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑŋkiəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrɒŋkɪəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the bronchi and empties into the azygos vein

Examples

bronchial vein drains blood
During surgery, care was taken not to damage the bronchial vein.
Obstruction of the bronchial vein can contribute to respiratory complications.
The bronchial vein drains only a small portion of the blood supplied to the lungs.
