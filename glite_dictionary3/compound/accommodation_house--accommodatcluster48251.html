accommodation house

noun
place offering temporary lodging
Pronunciation: / əˌkɑməˈdeɪʃən haʊs / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən haʊs / (BrE)
a building or establishment that provides lodging or temporary accommodation, especially for travelers or visitors

Examples

a accommodation house for travelers
Many backpackers chose the accommodation house because of its affordable rates.
The university owns an accommodation house for international students.
We booked a room at the accommodation house near the conference center.
