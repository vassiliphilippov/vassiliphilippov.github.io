hunting and gathering society

noun
group living by hunting and gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntɪŋ ən ˈɡæðərɪŋ səˈsaɪəti / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ənd ˈɡæðərɪŋ səˈsʌɪəti / (BrE)
a group of people whose subsistence is based on hunting animals and gathering wild plants, typically characterized by a nomadic lifestyle

Examples

ancient hunting and gathering society
Agricultural development replaced many traditional hunting and gathering societies over thousands of years.
Anthropologists have studied how a hunting and gathering society organizes its daily life.
