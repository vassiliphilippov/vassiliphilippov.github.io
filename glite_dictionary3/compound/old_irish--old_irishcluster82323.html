Old Irish

noun
early form of Irish Gaelic (c. 600–1100)
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈaɪrɪʃ / (AmE), / əʊld ˈaɪərɪʃ / (BrE)
the earliest form of the Irish Gaelic language, spoken and written in Ireland and parts of Scotland from around the 6th to the 12th centuries
