systems analyst

noun
expert who analyzes and designs information systems
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəmz ˈænəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəmz ˈanəlɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to analyze and design information systems to meet a business's requirements

Examples

systems analyst designs solutions
After meeting with the client, the systems analyst prepared a report with recommendations for upgrading the IT network.
As a systems analyst, she identifies inefficiencies in organizational workflows and suggests technology solutions.
The company hired a new systems analyst to oversee the transition to a cloud-based database system.
