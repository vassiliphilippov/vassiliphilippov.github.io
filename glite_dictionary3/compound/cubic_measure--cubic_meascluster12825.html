cubic measure

noun
unit for measuring volume
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˌmɛʒ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkjuː.bɪk ˌmɛʒ.ə / (BrE)
a unit for measuring volume or capacity, typically based on a cube with sides of a specified length

Examples

cubic measure in liters
A cubic meter is a common cubic measure used in scientific calculations.
Many countries use the litre as a standard cubic measure for liquids.
The shipping container's capacity is often listed in cubic measure to specify its volume.
