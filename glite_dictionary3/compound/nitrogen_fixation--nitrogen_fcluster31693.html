nitrogen fixation

noun
biological conversion of atmospheric nitrogen
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪ.tɹoʊ.dʒən fɪkˈseɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.trə.dʒən fɪkˈseɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process by which certain soil bacteria convert atmospheric nitrogen into forms that plants can absorb and use

Examples
