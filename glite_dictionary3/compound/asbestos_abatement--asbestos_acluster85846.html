asbestos abatement

noun
removal of asbestos from buildings
Pronunciation: / æsˈbɛstoʊs əˈbeɪtmənt / (AmE), / æsˈbɛstɒs əˈbeɪtmənt / (BrE)
the process of safely removing or reducing asbestos material from a building, especially to prevent health risks

Examples

asbestos abatement project
Local regulations require proper asbestos abatement procedures in older commercial buildings.
