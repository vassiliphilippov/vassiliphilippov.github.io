student teacher

noun
trainee teacher working under supervision
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːdənt ˈtiːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt ˈtiːtʃə / (BrE)
a college or university student who teaches in a school under the supervision of a certified teacher as part of their training to become a qualified teacher

Examples

student teacher in training
During her final year, she worked as a student teacher at the local elementary school.
Every student teacher is assigned a mentor to guide their development.
The program requires each student teacher to spend one semester in the classroom.
