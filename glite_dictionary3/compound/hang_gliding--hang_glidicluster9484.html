hang gliding

noun
flying sport using a hang glider
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋ ˌɡlaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhæŋ ˌɡlaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of flying in a light, non-motorized aircraft called a hang glider, typically launched from a hill or cliff
