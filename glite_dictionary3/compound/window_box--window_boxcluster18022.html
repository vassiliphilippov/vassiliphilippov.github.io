window box

noun
container for plants on a windowsill
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ ˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ ˌbɒks / (BrE)
A long narrow container for growing plants, typically placed on a windowsill or fixed to the outside of a window.
