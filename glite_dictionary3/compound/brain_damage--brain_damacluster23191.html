brain damage

noun
injury impairing brain function
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪn ˌdæmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪn ˌdamɪdʒ / (BrE)
injury to the brain that impairs its function, often causing lasting physical or mental effects
