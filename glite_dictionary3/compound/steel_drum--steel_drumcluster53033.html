steel drum

noun
Caribbean percussion instrument made from an oil drum
Pronunciation: / stil drʌm / (AmE), / stiːl drʌm / (BrE)
a percussion instrument from the Caribbean, made from a metal oil drum with flattened areas that produce different tones when struck
