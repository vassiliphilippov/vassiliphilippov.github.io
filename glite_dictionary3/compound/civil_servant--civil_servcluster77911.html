civil servant

noun
government employee in the civil service
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl ˈsɝːvənt / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl ˈsɜːvənt / (BrE)
a person who works for a government department or agency, typically as part of the permanent professional staff rather than as an elected official
