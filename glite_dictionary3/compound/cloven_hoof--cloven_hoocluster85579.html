cloven hoof

noun
the mark of Satan, split hoof
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊ.vən huːf / (AmE), / ˈkləʊ.vən huːf / (BrE)
A split hoof; in mythology and popular culture, a symbol or mark associated with the Devil or Satan.

Examples

the Devil's cloven hoof
In old folktales, the Devil often appears with a cloven hoof instead of a human foot.
Many believed that the witch's birthmark was a cloven hoof and proof of her pact with the Devil.
Superstitions in some cultures hold that a cloven hoof is the unmistakable sign of evil.
