masquerade party

noun
party with costumes and masks
Pronunciation: / ˌmæskəˈreɪd ˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˌmæskəˈreɪd ˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a party where guests wear costumes and masks, often to conceal their identities

Examples

masquerade party with masks
Everyone arrived at the masquerade party in elaborate masks and elegant costumes.
She spent hours preparing her outfit for the annual masquerade party.
The invitation specified that a mask was required to enter the masquerade party.
