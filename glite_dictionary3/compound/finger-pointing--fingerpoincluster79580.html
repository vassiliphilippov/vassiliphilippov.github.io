finger-pointing

noun
the act of blaming others
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˌpɔɪntɪŋ /; / ˈfɪŋɡər ˌpɔɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌpɔɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
