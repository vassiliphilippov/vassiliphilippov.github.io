lymphatic vessel

noun
tube carrying lymph in the body
Pronunciation: / lɪmˈfætɪk ˈvɛsəl / (AmE), / lɪmˈfætɪk ˈvɛsəl / (BrE)
a thin-walled tube in the lymphatic system that carries lymph and returns it to the blood circulation
