rear lamp

noun
red lamp at back of vehicle
Pronunciation: / rɪr læmp / (AmE), / rɪə læmp / (BrE)
a lamp, usually red, mounted at the back of a motor vehicle to indicate its presence and make it visible to other drivers

Examples

rear lamp on a car
It's illegal to drive at night without a functioning rear lamp.
Rain made the roads slippery, so he checked that every rear lamp was bright before setting off.
The left rear lamp on the car was not working and needed to be replaced.
