Boxing Day

proper noun
first weekday after Christmas
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑksɪŋ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɒksɪŋ deɪ / (BrE)
The first weekday after Christmas Day, celebrated as a public holiday in the UK and some other countries.

Examples

Boxing Day sales shopping
In the UK, Boxing Day is often spent with family or shopping during post-Christmas sales.
Many people in Canada watch sports or visit friends on Boxing Day.
