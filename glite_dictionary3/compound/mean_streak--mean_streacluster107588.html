mean streak

noun
tendency to be unkind or harsh
Pronunciation: / min strik / (AmE), / miːn striːk / (BrE)
a noticeable tendency in someone's character to be unkind, cruel, or harsh

Everyone liked Tom, but they warned me about his mean streak when he lost his temper.
Her mean streak comes out when she's feeling threatened.
