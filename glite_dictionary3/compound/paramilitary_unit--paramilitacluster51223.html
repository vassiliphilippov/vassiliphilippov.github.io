paramilitary unit

noun
group of civilians with a military structure
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / ˌparəˈmɪlɪtəri ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a group of civilians organized in a military style, often to operate alongside or instead of official military forces

Examples

armed paramilitary unit
A notorious paramilitary unit operated in the region for several years.
Journalists reported that the paramilitary unit wore uniforms similar to the national army.
The government deployed a paramilitary unit to assist police during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – militia: a military force raised from the civil population
  • antonym – regular army: the official, professional standing military force of a state
