Admission Day

noun
U.S. state admission holiday
Pronunciation: / ədˈmɪʃ.ən deɪ / (AmE), / ədˈmɪʃ.ən deɪ / (BrE)
In some U.S. states, a legal holiday commemorating the date the state was admitted to the Union.
