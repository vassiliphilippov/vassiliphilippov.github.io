last-gasp decision

noun
final or desperate decision
Pronunciation: / læst gæsp dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / lɑːst ɡɑːsp dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a decision made as a final or desperate effort, often at the last possible moment

Examples

make a last-gasp decision
After hours of debate, the team made a last-gasp decision to call a timeout.
He regretted making a last-gasp decision without considering all the options.
Their last-gasp decision paid off when the plan unexpectedly succeeded.
