in advance

adverb
before a given time or event
Pronunciation: / ɪn ədˈvæns / (AmE), / ɪn ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
before a particular time or event; ahead of time; in anticipation.

Examples

in advance of payment
I bought the concert tickets in advance to ensure we got good seats.
Please let us know in advance if you won't be able to attend the meeting.
You need to apply for a visa in advance if you want to travel there.
