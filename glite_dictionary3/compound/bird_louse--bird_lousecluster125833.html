bird louse

noun
parasitic insect on birds
Pronunciation: / bɝd laʊs / (AmE), / bɜːd laʊs / (BrE)
A small, wingless insect with biting mouthparts, usually parasitic on birds.

Examples

A bird louse can cause irritation and feather damage in affected birds.
