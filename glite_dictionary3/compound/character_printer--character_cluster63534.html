character printer

noun
printer outputting one character at a time
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪktər ˈprɪntər / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktə ˈprɪntə / (BrE)
a printer that prints one character at a time, rather than a full line or page

Examples

character printer prints letters
Early computing labs often used a character printer for basic document output.
The old character printer was much slower than modern laser printers.
Unlike a line printer, a character printer produces text one letter at a time.
