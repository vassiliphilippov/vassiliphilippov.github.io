smokescreen of success

noun
deceptive appearance of achievement
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkˌskriːn əv səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkˌskriːn əv səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
A misleading appearance or impression of achievement designed to hide the true situation.

Examples

smokescreen of success hides problems
Behind the smokescreen of success, the team was struggling with internal conflicts and missed deadlines.
The CEO's public announcements created a smokescreen of success that fooled most investors.
The awards and ceremonies acted as a smokescreen of success, masking deeper problems within the corporation.
