deviled egg

noun
hard-boiled egg with seasoned filled yolk
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəld ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɛvɪld ɛɡ / (BrE)
A hard-boiled egg that is cut in half, with the yolk mixed with mayonnaise and seasonings, then spooned or piped back into the white.

Examples

classic deviled egg appetizer
At Easter, we always make deviled eggs with the leftover hard-boiled eggs.
I like my deviled eggs with a touch of paprika on top.
She brought a plate of deviled eggs to the picnic, and they disappeared quickly.
