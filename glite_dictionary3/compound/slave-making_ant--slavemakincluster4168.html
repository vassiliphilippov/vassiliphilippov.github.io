slave-making ant

noun
ant that enslaves other ants
Pronunciation: / ˈsleɪv ˌmeɪkɪŋ ænt / (AmE), / ˈsleɪv ˌmeɪkɪŋ ænt / (BrE)
an ant species that invades colonies of other ants, capturing their young to be raised as workers in its own colony

Examples

slave-making ant invades colony
Researchers observed a colony of slave-making ants transporting captured young back to their nest.
Some species of slave-making ant rely entirely on their ability to capture workers from other colonies.
The slave-making ant raids neighboring nests to steal pupae and larvae.
