pedestrian bridge

noun
bridge for people on foot
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɛstriən brɪdʒ / (AmE), / pɪˈdɛstrɪən brɪdʒ / (BrE)
A bridge designed specifically for pedestrians to cross over obstacles such as roads or rivers.

Examples

cross a pedestrian bridge
A pedestrian bridge connects the parking lot to the entrance of the shopping mall.
The city constructed a new pedestrian bridge over the busy highway for safer crossing.
Tourists often stop on the pedestrian bridge to take photos of the river below.
