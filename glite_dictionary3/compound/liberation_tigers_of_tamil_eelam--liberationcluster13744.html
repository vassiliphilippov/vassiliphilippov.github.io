Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam

proper noun
Sri Lankan militant separatist group
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən ˈtaɪɡərz əv tæˈmɪl iːləˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən ˈtaɪɡəz əv tæˈmɪl iːləˈɛm / (BrE)
a militant organization based in Sri Lanka, also known as the Tamil Tigers, which fought for the creation of an independent Tamil state and was known for its use of guerrilla warfare and suicide bombings.
