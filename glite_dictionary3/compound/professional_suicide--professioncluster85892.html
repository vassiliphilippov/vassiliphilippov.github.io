professional suicide

noun
an action that ruins one's career
Pronunciation: / prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈsuːəsaɪd / (AmE), / prəˌfɛʃənəl ˈsjuːɪsaɪd / (BrE)
an action or decision that severely damages or ends one's career prospects.

Examples

professional suicide by quitting
Announcing his resignation so abruptly was considered professional suicide by his colleagues.
Refusing to adapt to new technology in the workplace can be professional suicide in today's job market.
She knew that criticizing her boss in public would be professional suicide, but she spoke up anyway.
