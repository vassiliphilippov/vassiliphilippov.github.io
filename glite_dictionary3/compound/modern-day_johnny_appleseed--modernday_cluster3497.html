modern-day Johnny Appleseed

noun
person who promotes tree planting or environmentalism
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdərn deɪ ˌdʒɑni ˈæpəlˌsid / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən deɪ ˌdʒɒni ˈæp(ə)lsiːd / (BrE)
a person who actively promotes environmentalism or the planting of trees, especially by encouraging others or planting many trees themselves
