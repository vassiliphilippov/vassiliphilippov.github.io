crème anglaise

noun
vanilla custard sauce for desserts
Pronunciation: / krɛm ɑn.ˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / kʁɛm ɑ̃.ˈɡlɛz / (BrE)
A thin, custard sauce flavored with vanilla or a liqueur, typically served with desserts.

Examples

creme anglaise dessert sauce
A drizzle of crème anglaise can make even a simple tart taste extraordinary.
She poured warm crème anglaise over the strawberries for an elegant finish.
The chef prepared a classic crème anglaise to serve with the chocolate cake.
