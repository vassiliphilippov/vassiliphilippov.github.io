sea bream

noun
flesh of certain edible saltwater fish
Pronunciation: / ˈsi ˌbrim / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌbriːm / (BrE)
the flesh of any of various saltwater fish of the family Sparidae or the family Bramidae, commonly eaten as food

grilled sea bream
