Sturm und Drang

noun
a period of upheaval or emotional turbulence
Pronunciation: / ˌʃtʊrm ʊn ˈdrɑːŋ / (AmE), / ˌʃtʊəm ʊn ˈdraŋ / (BrE)
A period or state of tumultuous upheaval, intense conflict, or emotional turbulence, especially in cultural, artistic, or political contexts.

Examples

teenage Sturm und Drang
Her adolescence was marked by Sturm und Drang as she struggled to find her identity.
Historians often refer to the 1960s as a decade of social Sturm und Drang in the West.
The company's restructuring led to a period of Sturm und Drang within the organization.
