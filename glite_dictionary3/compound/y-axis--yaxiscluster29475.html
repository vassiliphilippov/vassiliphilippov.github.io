y-axis

noun
vertical axis in a coordinate system
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪ ˌæksɪs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪ ˌæksɪs / (BrE)
the vertical axis in a plane coordinate system, usually representing the ordinate values

Examples

y-axis on a graph
Temperature changes are plotted along the y-axis in this chart.
The value on the y-axis shows the height of the plant in centimeters.
When plotting data on a graph, the y-axis is usually labeled with the dependent variable.
