inferior cerebral vein

noun
vein draining underside of the cerebral hemispheres
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪəriɚ səˈribəl veɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪəriə səˈriːbəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains the undersurface of the cerebral hemispheres and empties into the cavernous and transverse sinuses

Examples

inferior cerebral vein drains brain
Damage to an inferior cerebral vein can lead to venous infarction in the brain.
During surgery, the inferior cerebral vein must be carefully preserved to prevent complications.
The MRI scan showed a blockage in the inferior cerebral vein.
