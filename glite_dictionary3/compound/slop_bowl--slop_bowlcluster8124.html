slop bowl

noun
bowl for animal feed or waste
Pronunciation: / slɑp boʊl / (AmE), / slɒp bəʊl / (BrE)
a bowl used for holding animal feed, kitchen scraps, or waste, especially for pigs or in the context of waste disposal

Examples

slop bowl for kitchen scraps
After clearing the table, he emptied the scraps into the slop bowl.
She poured the leftover soup into the slop bowl for the pigs.
The old farmhouse kitchen had a large slop bowl by the door.
