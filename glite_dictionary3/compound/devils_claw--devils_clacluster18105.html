devil's claw

noun
African medicinal herb for inflammation
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəlz klɔ / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz klɔː / (BrE)
A southern African plant (Harpagophytum procumbens) or a preparation from its root, used as an herbal remedy to relieve inflammation and joint pain.

devil's claw herbal remedy
Herbalists often recommend devil's claw root to relieve arthritis pain and stiffness.

A recent trial found that topical devil's claw preparations reduced joint inflammation slightly in some patients.
