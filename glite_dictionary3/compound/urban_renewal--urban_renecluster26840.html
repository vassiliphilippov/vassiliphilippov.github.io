urban renewal

noun
redevelopment of city slum areas
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːbən rɪˈnuːəl / (AmE), / ˈɜːbən rɪˈnjuːəl / (BrE)
the process of clearing, rebuilding, and redeveloping areas of a city that are considered run-down or slums, often to improve housing and infrastructure

Examples

urban renewal project
After years of urban renewal, the downtown area became more attractive to businesses and tourists.
Many residents opposed the urban renewal plan because it threatened to displace longtime communities.
The city launched an urban renewal project to replace old buildings with modern housing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gentrification: the process of changing an area so that it is more appealing to people with money
  • antonym – urban decay: the process whereby a city falls into disrepair and dilapidation
