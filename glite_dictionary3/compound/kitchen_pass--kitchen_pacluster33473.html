kitchen pass

noun
permission to leave the kitchen or be excused from cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən pæs / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃən pɑːs / (BrE)
permission granted to temporarily leave the kitchen, often used in hospitality or joking contexts to refer to getting time off from cooking responsibilities.
