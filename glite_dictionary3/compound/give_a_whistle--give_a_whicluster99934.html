to give a whistle

verb
make a whistling sound
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ə ˈwɪsəl / (AmE), / ɡɪv ə ˈwɪsl̩ / (BrE)
to produce a whistling sound, especially by pursing the lips or using a device, often to attract attention

Examples
