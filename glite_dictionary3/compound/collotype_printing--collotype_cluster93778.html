collotype printing

noun
photomechanical printing process using a gelatin-coated plate
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːləˌtaɪp ˈprɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒləˌtaɪp ˈprɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a photomechanical printing process in which a glass plate coated with gelatin carries the image to be reproduced, capable of producing one or more colors
