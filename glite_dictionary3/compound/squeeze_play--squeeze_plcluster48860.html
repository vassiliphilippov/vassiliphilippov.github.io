squeeze play

noun
baseball scoring play with bunt
Pronunciation: / skwiz pleɪ /; / ˈskwiz pleɪ / (AmE), / skwiːz pleɪ /; / ˈskwiːz pleɪ / (BrE)
(in baseball) a play where a runner on third base tries to score as the batter bunts the ball
