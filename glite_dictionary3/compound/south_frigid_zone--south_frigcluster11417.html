South Frigid Zone

noun
region around the South Pole
Pronunciation: / saʊθ ˈfrɪdʒɪd zoʊn / (AmE), / saʊθ ˈfrɪdʒɪd zəʊn / (BrE)
the area of the Earth's surface surrounding the South Pole, including Antarctica and the nearby southern oceans, located within the Antarctic Circle.
