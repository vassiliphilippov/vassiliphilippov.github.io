embryoma of the kidney

noun
malignant kidney tumor in children
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm.briˈoʊ.mə əv ðə ˈkɪd.ni / (AmE), / ˌɛm.brɪˈəʊ.mə əv ðə ˈkɪd.ni / (BrE)
