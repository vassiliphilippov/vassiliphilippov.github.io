thunder mug

noun
container used as a chamber pot
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndɚ mʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈθʌndə mʌɡ / (BrE)
A portable container kept in the bedroom and used as a chamber pot for urination or defecation, especially before indoor plumbing.

Examples

thunder mug under bed
Before the advent of indoor plumbing, every bedroom had a thunder mug for nighttime use.
My grandmother would tell stories about using a thunder mug when she was a child.
The old farmhouse still had a thunder mug tucked under the bed for emergencies.
