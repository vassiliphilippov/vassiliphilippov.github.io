snot-nosed attitude

noun
rude or immature behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɑt ˌnoʊzd ˈæt̬.əˌtuːd / (AmE), / ˈsnɒt ˌnəʊzd ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
An impudent, rude, or immature manner or behavior, especially characteristic of children or adolescents.
