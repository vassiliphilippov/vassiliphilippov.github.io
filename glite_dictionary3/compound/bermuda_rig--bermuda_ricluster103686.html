Bermuda rig

noun
type of triangular sail rig for yachts
Pronunciation: / bərˈmjuːdə rɪg / (AmE), / bəˈmjuːdə rɪg / (BrE)
A sailing rig using triangular sails set fore and aft, typically used on yachts.
