dwarf willow

noun
a low-growing shrub willow of cold regions
Pronunciation: / dwɔrf ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
A small, low-growing willow shrub (Salix herbacea) found in boreal and alpine regions, characterized by creeping stems and bright green leaves.
