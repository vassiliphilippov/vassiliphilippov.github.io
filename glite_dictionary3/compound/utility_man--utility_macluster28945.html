utility man

noun
worker who fills in various roles
Pronunciation: / juˈtɪl.ə.ti mæn / (AmE), / juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti man / (BrE)
a man employed to perform a variety of tasks, especially one who can fill in for different roles as needed

Examples

utility man fixes problems
As a utility man, he was often called in to fix electrical or plumbing problems.
Companies appreciate having a skilled utility man who can handle unexpected jobs.
The coach relied on his utility man to cover any position during the game.
