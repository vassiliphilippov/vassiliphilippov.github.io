rum nose

noun
red, enlarged nose from alcohol abuse
Pronunciation: / rʌm noʊz / (AmE), / rʌm nəʊz / (BrE)
a reddish, enlarged nose with visible blood vessels, often linked to excessive alcohol consumption

Examples

People used to believe that a rum nose meant someone was a heavy drinker.
