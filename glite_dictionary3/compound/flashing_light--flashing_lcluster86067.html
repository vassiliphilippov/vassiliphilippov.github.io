flashing light

noun
a light that blinks intermittently
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃɪŋ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈflæʃɪŋ laɪt / (BrE)
a light that blinks or shines intermittently, often used as a warning or signal

Examples
