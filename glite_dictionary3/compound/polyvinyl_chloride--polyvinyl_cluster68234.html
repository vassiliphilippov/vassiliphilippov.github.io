polyvinyl chloride

noun
synthetic plastic polymer (PVC)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˌvaɪnəl ˈklɔːraɪd / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˌvaɪnɪl ˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
a synthetic plastic polymer made from vinyl chloride, widely used for making pipes, cable insulation, and many other products

Examples
