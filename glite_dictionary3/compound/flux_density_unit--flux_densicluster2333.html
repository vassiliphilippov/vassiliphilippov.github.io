flux density unit

noun
unit measuring flux per unit area
Pronunciation: / flʌks ˈdɛnsəti ˌjuːnɪt / (AmE), / flʌks ˈdɛnsəti ˌjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a unit used to measure the amount of flux passing through a unit area, especially in physics and engineering

flux density unit in physics
Magnetic sensors are calibrated using an appropriate flux density unit to ensure accuracy.
