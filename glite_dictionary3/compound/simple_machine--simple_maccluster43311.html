simple machine

noun
basic mechanical device that changes force
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəl məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a basic mechanical device, such as a lever or pulley, that changes the direction or magnitude of a force to help perform work

A lever is a common type of simple machine that makes lifting easier.
