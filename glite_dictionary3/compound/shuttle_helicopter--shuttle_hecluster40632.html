shuttle helicopter

noun
helicopter used on regular shuttle routes
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌtəl ˈhɛlɪˌkɑptɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtəl ˈhɛlɪˌkɒptə / (BrE)
a helicopter used to transport people or goods on a regular, back-and-forth route between two points

Examples

shuttle helicopter service
A shuttle helicopter was used to ferry guests from the airport to the island resort.
The company operates a shuttle helicopter service between the two cities for executives.
The oil rig relies on a shuttle helicopter for daily transportation of workers and supplies.
