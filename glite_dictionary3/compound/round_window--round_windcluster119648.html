round window

noun
membranous opening in the ear leading to the cochlea
Pronunciation: / raʊnd ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / raʊnd ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a small, membranous opening in the inner wall of the middle ear that leads into the cochlea

Examples

the round window of ear
Damage to the round window can affect hearing and balance.
Sound waves reach the round window after passing through the middle ear structures.
The surgeon carefully examined the round window during the ear operation.
