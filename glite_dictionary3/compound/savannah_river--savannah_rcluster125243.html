Savannah River

proper noun
river forming border between South Carolina and Georgia
Pronunciation: / səˈvænə ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / səˈvænə ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in the southeastern United States forming most of the border between South Carolina and Georgia, and flowing southeast into the Atlantic Ocean
