sexual health

noun
state of well-being relating to sexuality
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkʃuəl hɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈsɛkʃʊəl hɛlθ / (BrE)
A state of physical, emotional, mental, and social well-being in relation to sexuality, not merely the absence of disease or dysfunction.

Examples

sexual health education
Open discussions about sexual health help remove stigma and promote well-being.
Proper education can greatly improve your sexual health over a lifetime.
Regular check-ups are essential for maintaining good sexual health.
