mouth-to-mouth resuscitation

noun
emergency first aid breathing technique
Pronunciation: / ˌmaʊθ tə ˈmaʊθ rɪˌsʌsɪˈteɪʃən /; / ˌmaʊθ tə ˈmaʊθ rəˌsʌsɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaʊθ tə ˈmaʊθ rɪˌsʌsɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a first aid procedure in which a person breathes air into the lungs of someone who has stopped breathing, usually by direct mouth-to-mouth contact
