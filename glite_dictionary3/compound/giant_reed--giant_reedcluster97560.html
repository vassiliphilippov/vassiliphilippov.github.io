giant reed

noun
a tall grass with feathery flowers, found near water
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˌrid / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɪənt ˌriːd / (BrE)
a very tall perennial grass with jointed stems and large greyish-white feathery flower clusters, often found by riversides and in ditches
