secretary of state

noun
senior government official for foreign affairs
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkrəˌtɛri əv steɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɛkrɪtəri əv steɪt / (BrE)
the head of a government department concerned with foreign relations, especially the chief foreign affairs official in countries such as the United States and the United Kingdom
