black olive

noun
ripe, dark olive used in cooking
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈɑlɪv / (AmE), / blæk ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
a ripe olive that is dark in color and preserved in brine, oil, or by drying, commonly used in cooking and salads

Examples

sliced black olive
I prefer black olives to green ones because of their rich, mellow taste.
She added a handful of black olives to the salad for extra flavor.
The pizza was topped with mushrooms, peppers, and sliced black olives.
