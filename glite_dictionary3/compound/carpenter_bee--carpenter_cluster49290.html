carpenter bee

noun
a solitary bee that burrows into wood
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrpəntər biː / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpəntə biː / (BrE)
a large solitary bee that burrows into wood or plant stems to lay its eggs

Examples

carpenter bee drills wood
A carpenter bee can drill perfect holes in wooden fences to build its nest.
Gardeners often mistake the carpenter bee for a bumblebee because of its size and color.
I found several carpenter bees hovering near the decaying tree in my backyard.
