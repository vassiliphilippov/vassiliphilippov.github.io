shower glove

noun
mitt for exfoliating skin in the shower
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊər ɡlʌv / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə ɡlʌv / (BrE)
A mitt made of coarse material, used to wash and exfoliate the skin while bathing or showering.

Examples

use a shower glove to scrub
After applying soap, he scrubbed his back with a shower glove for a deep cleanse.
She uses a shower glove every day to keep her skin smooth and clean.
The shower glove is easy to rinse and dries quickly after use.
