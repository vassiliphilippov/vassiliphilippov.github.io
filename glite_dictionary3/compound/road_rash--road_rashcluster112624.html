road rash

noun
skin abrasion from falling on a hard surface
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊd ræʃ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd ræʃ / (BrE)
an area of scraped or abraded skin caused by falling or sliding on a hard surface, such as pavement

Examples

road rash after falling
He wore long sleeves to avoid getting road rash while skating.
Her road rash took several weeks to heal completely.
The nurse cleaned the road rash on his leg after the bicycle accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abrasion: an area of skin that has been scraped or worn away
