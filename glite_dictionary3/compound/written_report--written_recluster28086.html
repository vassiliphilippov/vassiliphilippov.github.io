written report

noun
document presenting findings in writing
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtən rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈrɪtən rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
a document that presents information or findings in written form, typically prepared by an individual or group after research or investigation

Examples

submit a written report
Every employee must file a written report following a workplace accident.
The committee submitted a detailed written report after concluding its investigation.
The consultant prepared a written report summarizing the project's progress and recommendations.
