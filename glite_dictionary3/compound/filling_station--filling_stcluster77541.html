filling station

noun
place selling fuel for vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪŋ ˌsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A place where fuel for motor vehicles is sold and often servicing is provided; a petrol or gas station.

Examples

at the filling station pump
Many modern filling stations also have convenience stores and restrooms.
The nearest filling station is five miles down the highway.
We stopped at a filling station to refuel the car during our road trip.
