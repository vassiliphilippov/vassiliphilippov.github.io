crimson wave

noun
vivid red sky at sunrise or sunset
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmzən weɪv / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmzən weɪv / (BrE)
a vivid red appearance in the sky, especially during sunrise or sunset, often evoking the image of a wave of the color crimson

Examples

a crimson wave at sunrise
A crimson wave heralded the arrival of the new day, bathing everything in warm light.
As the sun dipped below the hills, a crimson wave swept across the evening sky.
Photographers flocked to the beach to capture the crimson wave at dawn.
