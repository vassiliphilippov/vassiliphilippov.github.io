theoretical account

noun
hypothetical or conceptual explanation
Pronunciation: / θiˌɝːɪˈrɛtɪkəl əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / θɪˌɒrɪˈɛtɪkəl əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
an explanation or model that describes the structure or operation of something in hypothetical or conceptual terms, especially in academic or scientific contexts

Examples

a theoretical account of behavior
His theoretical account offers a framework for understanding social behavior in large groups.
The course begins with a theoretical account of economic development before exploring practical applications.
The research team proposed a new theoretical account of memory formation to explain their findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – model: a representation of a system or process used for explanation or prediction
  • synonym – theory: a system of ideas explaining something, based on general principles
