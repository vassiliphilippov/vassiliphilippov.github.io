stud finder

noun
device to locate wall studs
Pronunciation: / stʌd ˈfaɪndər / (AmE), / stʌd ˈfaɪndə / (BrE)
a device used to locate wooden or metal studs behind a wall, often by detecting nails or changes in density

Examples

use a stud finder on a wall
He used a stud finder to locate the beams before hanging the heavy mirror.
Make sure your stud finder is working correctly before you start drilling into the wall.
Most modern stud finders can detect both metal and wooden studs.
