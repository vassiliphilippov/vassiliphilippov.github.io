cake mold

noun
pan or container for baking cakes in shapes
Pronunciation: / keɪk moʊld / (AmE), / keɪk məʊld / (BrE)
a container or pan with a particular shape, used for baking cakes so that they take on that shape

Examples

cake mold for baking
Before baking, grease the cake mold to ensure the cake comes out easily.
She poured the batter into the cake mold to create a heart-shaped dessert.
The bakery has a collection of cake molds in many different designs.
