Jesus freak

noun
extremely enthusiastic Christian
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːzəs friːk / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːzəs friːk / (BrE)
A person who is extremely enthusiastic or outspoken about their Christian faith, often to the point of being perceived as excessive or zealous.

Examples

a Jesus freak at church
Being a Jesus freak, she attends church every Sunday and tells everyone about her faith.
During the festival, a group of Jesus freaks handed out pamphlets on the street corner.
Some colleagues consider him a Jesus freak because he constantly talks about religion at work.
