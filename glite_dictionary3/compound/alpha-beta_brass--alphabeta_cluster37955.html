alpha-beta brass

noun
strong brass alloy with high zinc
Pronunciation: / ˌælfə ˈbiːtə bræs / (AmE), / ˌælfə ˈbiːtə brɑːs / (BrE)
A type of brass containing more zinc than alpha brass, resulting in greater strength; used for castings and hot-worked products.

Examples

alpha-beta brass alloy parts
Alpha-beta brass alloys are commonly used in valves and marine hardware.
Many manufacturers prefer alpha-beta brass for its improved strength and suitability for hot-working.
The engineer specified alpha-beta brass for the casting due to its durability under stress.
