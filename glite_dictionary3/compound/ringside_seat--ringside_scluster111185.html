ringside seat

noun
front-row seat by a boxing or wrestling ring
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋ.saɪd sit /; / ˈrɪŋ.saɪd ˌsiːt / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ.saɪd siːt /; / ˈrɪŋ.saɪd ˌsiːt / (BrE)
a seat very close to the action at an event (especially a boxing or wrestling match), or a privileged position from which to observe something closely
