Mexican swamp cypress

noun
cypress tree native to Mexican highlands
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛksɪkən swɑmp ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪkən swɒmp ˈsaɪprəs / (BrE)
a large cypress tree (Taxodium mucronatum) native to the river valleys of the Mexican highlands, often found growing along streams and lakeshores
