flag waving

noun
display meant to arouse patriotism
Pronunciation: / flæɡ ˈweɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / flæɡ ˈweɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
An action, speech, or display meant to arouse or express strong patriotic feelings, often to rally support or distract from other issues.

Examples

political flag waving
Her speech was filled with flag waving but lacked real solutions to the country's problems.
Many politicians resort to flag waving when they want to rally public support.
The event turned into a spectacle of flag waving and patriotic songs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jingoism: extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive foreign policy
  • degree higher – nationalism: identification with and support for one's nation, often above others
  • degree lower – patriotism: love for or devotion to one's country
