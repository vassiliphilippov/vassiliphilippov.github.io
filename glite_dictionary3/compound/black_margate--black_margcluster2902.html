black margate

noun
a dusky grey food fish of the western Atlantic
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈmɑrˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / blæk ˈmɑːˌɡeɪt / (BrE)
a dusky grey fish (Anisotremus surinamensis) found in warm western Atlantic waters from Louisiana and Florida southward, valued as food
