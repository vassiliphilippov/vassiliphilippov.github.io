drama queen

noun
person who overreacts or exaggerates emotions
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑːmə kwiːn / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːmə kwiːn / (BrE)
a person who habitually overreacts to situations or exaggerates emotions for effect

Examples

Don't be such a drama queen—it's just a small scratch.
She was accused of being a drama queen when she started crying over the lost keys.
Whenever something minor goes wrong, he turns into a total drama queen.
