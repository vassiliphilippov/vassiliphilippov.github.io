parachute jumper

noun
person who jumps from aircraft with a parachute
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌʃut ˈdʒʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈpærəʃuːt ˈdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
a person who jumps from an aircraft using a parachute, either for sport or in the course of their work
