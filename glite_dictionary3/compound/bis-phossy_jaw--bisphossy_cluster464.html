bis-phossy jaw

noun
jawbone disease from phosphorus exposure
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪs ˈfɑːsi dʒɔ / (AmE), / ˌbɪs ˈfɒsi dʒɔː / (BrE)
a severe medical condition involving necrosis of the jawbone, caused by exposure to white phosphorus, historically affecting workers in the match industry
