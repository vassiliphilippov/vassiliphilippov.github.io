door-to-door

adjective
involving visits to each house
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔr tə ˈdɔr /; / ˌdɔr tə ˈdɔr / (AmE), / ˈdɔː tə ˈdɔː /; / ˌdɔː tə ˈdɔː / (BrE)
involving visiting each house in an area, especially for sales, campaigns, or inquiries
