picnic ham

noun
shoulder cut of pork, usually smoked
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪk.nɪk hæm / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.nɪk ham / (BrE)
a cut of pork from the shoulder of a hog, usually smoked and sometimes cured, often used as a less expensive alternative to traditional ham
