ground zero

noun
site directly below a bomb explosion
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd ˈzɪəɹoʊ /; / ˌɡraʊnd ˈzɪəroʊ / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd ˈzɪərəʊ /; / ˌɡraʊnd ˈzɪərəʊ / (BrE)
the exact location on the surface most affected by an explosion, especially a nuclear bomb
