regret rape

noun
claim of rape due to post-act regret
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡrɛt reɪp / (AmE), / rɪˈɡrɛt reɪp / (BrE)
(offensive) A situation where someone claims to have been raped after initially consenting to sex, often suggesting that their subsequent regret is mistaken for lack of consent.
