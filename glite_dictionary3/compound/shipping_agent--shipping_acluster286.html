shipping agent

noun
person managing shipments for shipowners
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪp.ɪŋ ˌeɪ.dʒənt / (AmE), / ˈʃɪp.ɪŋ ˌeɪ.dʒənt / (BrE)
a person or company responsible for handling shipments and logistics on behalf of a shipowner, including the arrangement of cargo, customs, and port services

Examples

shipping agent arranges cargo
As a shipping agent, Maria ensured that all documentation was in order before the ship departed.
If you have any questions about customs clearance, please contact the shipping agent assigned to your cargo.
The shipping agent coordinated the unloading of containers when the vessel arrived at the dock.
