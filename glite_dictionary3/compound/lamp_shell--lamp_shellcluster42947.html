lamp shell

noun
marine animal with a bivalve shell (brachiopod)
Pronunciation: / læmp ʃɛl / (AmE), / læmp ʃɛl / (BrE)
a marine animal with a bivalve shell and a pair of arms bearing tentacles for feeding; belonging to the phylum Brachiopoda
