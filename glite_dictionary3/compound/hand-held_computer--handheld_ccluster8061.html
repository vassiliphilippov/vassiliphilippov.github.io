hand-held computer

noun
portable computer small enough to hold or carry
Pronunciation: / ˌhændˈhɛld kəmˈpjuːtər / (AmE), / ˌhændˈhɛld kəmˈpjuːtə / (BrE)
a small, portable computer that can be held in one hand and carried easily, typically battery-powered and often small enough to fit in a pocket

Examples

The new hand-held computer model boasts a longer battery life and a touch screen interface.
