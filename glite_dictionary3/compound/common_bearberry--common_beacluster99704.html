common bearberry

noun
a mat-forming evergreen shrub with red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmən ˈberˌberi / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈbeəˌbɛri / (BrE)
an evergreen, mat-forming shrub found in North America and northern Eurasia; it has small white flowers, red berries, and leaves that turn red in autumn
