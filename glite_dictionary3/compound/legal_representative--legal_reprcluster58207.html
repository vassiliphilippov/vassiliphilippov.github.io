legal representative

noun
person authorized to act for another legally
Pronunciation: / ˈliːɡəl ˌrɛprɪˌzɛntəˈtɪv / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person who has the legal authority to act on behalf of another, such as through power of attorney or as the executor of a will

Examples

legal representative signs documents
As my father's legal representative, I had the authority to sign documents on his behalf.
She served as the legal representative for her client during the proceedings.
The court appointed a legal representative to manage the estate.

Related Terms

  • synonym – executor: person appointed to carry out the terms of a will
  • synonym – power of attorney: legal authorization to act on another's behalf
