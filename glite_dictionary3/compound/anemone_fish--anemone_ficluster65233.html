anemone fish

noun
a tropical fish living among sea anemones
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛməni fɪʃ / (AmE), / əˈnɛməni fɪʃ / (BrE)
a small brightly colored tropical marine fish that lives among the tentacles of sea anemones, with which it has a symbiotic relationship
