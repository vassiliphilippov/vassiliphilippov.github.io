ABO antibodies

noun
antibodies related to ABO blood groups
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪˌbiːˈoʊ ˈæn.tɪˌbɑː.diz / (AmE), / ˌeɪˌbiːˈəʊ ˈæn.tɪˌbɒd.iːz / (BrE)
Antibodies in the blood that react against antigens of the ABO blood group system, affecting blood compatibility.

Examples

ABO antibodies in blood
People with type B blood have ABO antibodies that will react against type A antigens.
The laboratory test detected the presence of ABO antibodies in the patient's serum.
Transfusion reactions can occur if ABO antibodies are not matched correctly in blood donation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ABO blood group: classification of human blood based on the presence or absence of A and B antigens
  • synonym – antibody: a protein produced by the immune system to identify and neutralize foreign substances
