single-breasted jacket

noun
jacket with a single row of buttons
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡl ˌbrɛstɪd ˈdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˌbrɛstɪd ˈdʒakɪt / (BrE)
A jacket designed so that the two sides of the front overlap just enough to allow a single row of buttons.

Examples

classic single-breasted jacket
He wore a navy single-breasted jacket to the interview, giving him a classic and professional look.
She prefers a single-breasted jacket because it's lighter and less bulky than the double-breasted style.
This season's collection features a modern take on the single-breasted jacket.

Related Terms

  • antonym – double-breasted jacket: jacket with wide, overlapping fronts and two parallel rows of buttons
