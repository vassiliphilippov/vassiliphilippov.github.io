kitchen sink regression

noun
regression affecting many aspects or variables
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk rɪˈɡrɛʃən / (BrE)
a software or statistical regression that affects many aspects or incorporates all possible variables, often leading to widespread problems or overcomplicated models
