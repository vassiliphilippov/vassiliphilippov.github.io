to hang a banner

verb
suspend and display a sign or decoration
Pronunciation: / hæŋ ə ˈbænər / (AmE), / hæŋ ə ˈbænə / (BrE)
to attach and display a large sign or decoration, typically by suspending it from above

Examples

to hang a banner over a stage
Before the rally began, organizers hung a banner across the stage to show their support.
Every year, we hang a banner on the porch to welcome our guests for the holiday party.
The students decided to hang a banner over the main entrance for graduation day.
