opinion poll

noun
survey of public opinion
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪnjən poʊl / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjən pəʊl / (BrE)
an activity in which a group of people are asked questions in order to find out what they think about a subject or issue

Examples

recent opinion poll
According to a recent opinion poll, most people favor stricter environmental regulations.
The latest opinion poll shows a sharp decline in support for the candidate.
The results of the opinion poll were published in all the major newspapers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – survey: an investigation of the opinions or experiences of a group of people, by asking them questions
