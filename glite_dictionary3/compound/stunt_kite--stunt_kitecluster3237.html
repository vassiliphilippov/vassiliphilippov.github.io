stunt kite

noun
maneuverable two-line kite for tricks
Pronunciation: / stʌnt kaɪt / (AmE), / stʌnt kaɪt / (BrE)
a type of kite that is highly maneuverable and controlled by two lines, allowing for aerobatic tricks and stunts

Examples

fly a stunt kite
A stunt kite allows the pilot to perform spins, dives, and other tricks with precision control.
She impressed the crowd by flying her stunt kite in a series of loops and sharp dives.
