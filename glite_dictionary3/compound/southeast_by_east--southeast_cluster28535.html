southeast by east

noun
compass point between southeast and east-southeast
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊθˌist baɪ ˈist / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˌiːst baɪ ˈiːst / (BrE)
the compass point that is one point east of southeast, equal to 123.75° clockwise from due north
