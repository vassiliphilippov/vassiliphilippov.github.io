Apple tax

noun
extra cost associated with Apple products
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəl ˌtæks / (AmE), / ˈæpəl ˌtæks / (BrE)
an informal term for the higher prices or additional costs associated with purchasing Apple products compared to similar alternatives
