supply officer

noun
military officer handling logistics and supplies
Pronunciation: / səˈplaɪ ˌɑfəsər / (AmE), / səˈplaɪ ˌɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a commissioned officer in the military responsible for matters relating to logistics, such as the supply of equipment, provisions, and materials
