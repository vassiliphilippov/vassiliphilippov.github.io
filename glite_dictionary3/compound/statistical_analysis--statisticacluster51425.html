statistical analysis

noun
the examination and interpretation of data
Pronunciation: / stəˈtɪstɪkəl əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəl əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the process of collecting, examining, and interpreting numerical data to discover patterns, trends, or relationships

Examples

statistical analysis of data
Accurate statistical analysis is essential for understanding complex scientific phenomena.
Modern business decisions often rely heavily on statistical analysis to guide their strategies.
The research team used statistical analysis to interpret the results of the experiment.
