crumb snatcher

noun
someone who sneakily takes leftover food
Pronunciation: / krʌm ˈsnætʃɚ / (AmE), / krʌm ˈsnætʃə / (BrE)
an informal term for someone, often a child or animal, who tries to take leftover food or snacks in a sneaky way
