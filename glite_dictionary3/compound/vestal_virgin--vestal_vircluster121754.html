vestal virgin

noun
priestess of Vesta in ancient Rome
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛstəl ˈvɜrdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɛstəl ˈvɜːdʒɪn / (BrE)
(in ancient Rome) a virgin priestess dedicated to the goddess Vesta, responsible for maintaining the sacred fire in her temple.
