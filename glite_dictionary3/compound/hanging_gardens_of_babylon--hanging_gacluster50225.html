Hanging Gardens of Babylon

proper noun
ancient terraced garden in Babylon
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋɪŋ ˈɡɑrdənz əv ˈbæbɪˌlɑn / (AmE), / ˈhæŋɪŋ ˈɡɑːdnz əv ˈbæbɪlən / (BrE)
