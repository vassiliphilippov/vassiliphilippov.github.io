lily-white

adjective
pure white color
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪli ˌwaɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪli ˌwaɪt / (BrE)
Of a pure, fresh, and unblemished white color.

Examples

lily-white dress
He painted the kitchen walls a lily-white to make the space feel open and clean.
Her wedding dress was lily-white, shimmering in the afternoon light.
The lily-white pages of the new book felt crisp beneath her fingers.

Related Terms

  • antonym – off-white: a white color with a slight tint
  • synonym – snow-white: pure white in color, like snow
