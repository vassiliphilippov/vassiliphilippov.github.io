note receivable

noun
written promise to receive money; promissory note asset
Pronunciation: / noʊt rɪˈsiːvəbl̩ / (AmE), / nəʊt rɪˈsiːvəbl̩ / (BrE)
A written promise to receive a specified sum of money at a certain time from another party; an asset created by a promissory note.

Examples

note receivable balance sheet
Auditors carefully reviewed each note receivable to ensure the terms were being met.
