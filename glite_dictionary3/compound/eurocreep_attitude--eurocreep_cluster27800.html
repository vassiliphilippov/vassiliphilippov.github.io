Eurocreep attitude

noun
negative view of European influence
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊroʊˌkriːp ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊˌkriːp ˈatɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a negative or suspicious perception of increasing European influence, especially as unwelcome or intrusive

Examples

Eurocreep attitude toward policy
Political analysts discussed the rise of the Eurocreep attitude in certain regions.

The spokesperson dismissed accusations of a Eurocreep attitude within the government.
