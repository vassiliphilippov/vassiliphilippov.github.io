south by east

noun
compass point: one point east of due south
Pronunciation: / saʊθ baɪ iːst / (AmE), / saʊθ baɪ iːst / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point (11°15′) east of due south.

Examples

south by east direction
The mountain lies almost exactly south by east from our current position.
The ship changed course to south by east, following the winds closely.
You will need to head south by east to reach the next harbor safely.
