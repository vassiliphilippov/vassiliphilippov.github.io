black morel

noun
a species of edible morel mushroom
Pronunciation: / blæk məˈrɛl / (AmE), / blæk məˈrɛl / (BrE)
A species of edible mushroom with a deeply pitted cap that is grey when young and turns black as it ages, with the fertile body attached to the stalk and little or no skirt.

Examples

wild black morel mushroom
A single black morel can fetch a high price at gourmet markets due to its rarity.

The chef sautéed fresh black morel mushrooms to bring out their earthy flavor.
