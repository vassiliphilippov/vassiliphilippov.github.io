nuclear option

noun
drastic last resort action
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.kli.ɚ ˈɑp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə ˈɒp.ʃən / (BrE)
An extreme or drastic course of action used as a last resort, especially when all other alternatives have failed.

Examples

the nuclear option decision
Facing mounting losses, the company considered closing its main plant as a nuclear option.
Some politicians argued that invoking the nuclear option would damage the institution's traditions.
The union leaders warned that striking was their nuclear option if talks broke down.

Related Terms

  • synonym – last resort: a final course of action when all else fails
