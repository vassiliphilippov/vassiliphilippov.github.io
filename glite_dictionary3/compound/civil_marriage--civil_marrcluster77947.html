civil marriage

noun
marriage by government official
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl ˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl ˈmærɪdʒ / (BrE)
a marriage ceremony performed and recorded by a government official rather than a religious authority

Examples

civil marriage ceremony
A civil marriage offers legal recognition without requiring a religious ceremony.
Some countries do not recognize religious ceremonies unless a civil marriage is also performed.
They decided to have a civil marriage at the city hall last summer.
