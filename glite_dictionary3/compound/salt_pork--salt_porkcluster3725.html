salt pork

noun
cured fatty pork meat
Pronunciation: / sɔlt pɔrk / (AmE), / sɒlt pɔːk / (BrE)
fatty pork from the back, sides, or belly of a pig, preserved by salting, often used in cooking or as a flavoring.
