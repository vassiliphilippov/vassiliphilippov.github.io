beige flag

noun
signal indicating a pause or truce
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪʒ flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪʒ flaɡ / (BrE)
a signal, typically a flag colored beige, used to indicate a pause or temporary halt, especially representing a truce
