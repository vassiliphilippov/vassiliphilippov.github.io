as if

conjunction
suggesting something; as though
Pronunciation: / æz ɪf / (AmE), / æz ɪf / (BrE)
in a way that suggests something is true or seems to be the case, usually to express comparison or supposition

Examples

smile as if surprised
He smiled as if nothing had happened, but I could tell he was upset.
She spoke as if she were the expert on the subject.
The dog tilted its head as if it understood every word.
