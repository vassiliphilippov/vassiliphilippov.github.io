full gainer

noun
dive with full backward somersault
Pronunciation: / fʊl ˈɡeɪnər / (AmE), / fʊl ˈɡeɪnə / (BrE)
A dive in which the diver propels the feet forward to complete a full backward somersault, entering the water feet first while facing away from the diving board.

Examples

to perform a full gainer
During the competition, she attempted a full gainer and received high marks from the judges.
He amazed the crowd by performing a flawless full gainer off the high dive.
Learning to do a full gainer requires strength, agility, and hours of practice.
