vena nasofrontalis

noun
a vein in the anterior part of the eye socket
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪnə ˌneɪsoʊfrʌnˈteɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˌviːnə ˌnæsəʊfrʌnˈtɑːlɪs / (BrE)
a vein located in the front-middle part of the eye socket, connecting the superior ophthalmic vein with the angular vein

Examples

vena nasofrontalis eye socket
An obstruction of the vena nasofrontalis can lead to complications involving the eye and surrounding tissues.
During surgery, care was taken to avoid damaging the vena nasofrontalis.
The vena nasofrontalis plays a crucial role in venous drainage of the orbit.

Related Terms

  • synonym – angular vein: a vein near the angle of the eye, connected to the facial vein
  • synonym – superior ophthalmic vein: a major vein draining the orbit (eye socket)
