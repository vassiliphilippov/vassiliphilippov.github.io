state secret

noun
government-classified confidential information
Pronunciation: / steɪt ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / steɪt ˈsiːkrɪt / (BrE)
information that a government classifies as confidential for reasons of national security or public interest

Examples

leak a state secret
Revealing a state secret can have serious legal consequences.

Unauthorized disclosure of a state secret is considered a major crime in many countries.
