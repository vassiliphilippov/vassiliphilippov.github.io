full cousin

noun
child of your aunt or uncle
Pronunciation: / fʊl ˈkʌzən / (AmE), / fʊl ˈkʌz(ə)n / (BrE)
a cousin who is the child of your aunt or uncle, sharing both grandparents in common rather than only one or none

Examples

full cousin shares grandparents
All of my full cousins grew up in the same town as me.
She invited her full cousin to the family reunion this summer.
Unlike a half cousin, a full cousin shares both sets of grandparents with you.
