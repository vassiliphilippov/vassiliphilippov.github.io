marrow squash

noun
elongated squash with dark green skin
Pronunciation: / ˈmæroʊ ˌskwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈmarəʊ ˌskwɒʃ / (BrE)
A type of squash plant grown for its elongated fruit with smooth dark green skin and pale flesh, commonly used as a vegetable.

Examples

fresh marrow squash salad

Stuffed marrow squash is a traditional dish in many European countries.
