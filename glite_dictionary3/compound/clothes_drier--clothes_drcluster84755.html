clothes drier

noun
machine for drying wet clothes
Pronunciation: / kloʊðz ˈdraɪər / (AmE), / kləʊðz ˈdraɪə / (BrE)
A machine or appliance used to dry clothes that are wet from washing, usually by circulating heated air.

Examples

clothes drier for laundry
After the wash cycle, she put the laundry into the clothes drier to finish the job.
Some people prefer to hang their clothes outside instead of using a clothes drier.
The clothes drier broke down during the winter, making it hard to keep up with laundry.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dryer: a machine that removes moisture from something
  • antonym – washing machine: machine that washes clothes
