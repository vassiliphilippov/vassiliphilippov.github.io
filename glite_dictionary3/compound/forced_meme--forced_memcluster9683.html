forced meme

noun
an artificially created or spread internet meme
Pronunciation: / fɔrst mim / (AmE), / fɔːst miːm / (BrE)
An internet meme that is deliberately promoted or feels artificial, often failing to gain genuine popularity.

Examples

spread a forced meme
After a week, the phrase felt like a forced meme rather than something genuinely funny.
People criticized the ad campaign for trying to create a forced meme instead of letting trends develop naturally.
That joke quickly became a forced meme when everyone started repeating it online.

Related Terms

  • antonym – organic meme: a meme that spreads naturally without deliberate promotion
