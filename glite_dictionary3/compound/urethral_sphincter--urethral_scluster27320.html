urethral sphincter

noun
muscle that controls urine flow
Pronunciation: / jʊˈriːθrəl ˈsfɪŋktɚ / (AmE), / jʊˈriːθrəl ˈsfɪŋktə / (BrE)
a muscle surrounding the urethra that contracts to control the release of urine from the bladder
