caudal fin

noun
tail fin of a fish or aquatic animal
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔdəl fɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdəl fɪn / (BrE)
the tail fin of fishes and some other aquatic vertebrates, used for propulsion and steering
