kola nut tree

noun
tree producing kola nuts (cola extract source)
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlə nʌt triː / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə nʌt triː / (BrE)
a tropical tree that bears large brown nuts containing caffeine and is the natural source of cola extract
