periodic event

noun
event recurring at intervals
Pronunciation: / pɪr.iˈɑd.ɪk ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / pɪə.rɪˈɒd.ɪk ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an event that occurs or recurs at regular intervals

Examples

monthly periodic event
A company meeting scheduled at the end of every month is considered a periodic event.
Many cultures have festivals that are celebrated as a periodic event each year.
The Olympic Games are a periodic event held every four years.
