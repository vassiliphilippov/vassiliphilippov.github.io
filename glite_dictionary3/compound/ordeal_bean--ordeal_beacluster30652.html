ordeal bean

noun
poisonous seed of the Calabar bean vine
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈdil bin / (AmE), / ɔːˈdiːl biːn / (BrE)
the highly poisonous dark brown seed of the Calabar bean vine, used traditionally in West African ordeals and as a source of the drug physostigmine
