sand dune

noun
hill or ridge of wind-blown sand
Pronunciation: / sænd duːn / (AmE), / sænd djuːn / (BrE)
a hill or ridge of sand formed by the wind, typically found in deserts or near bodies of water

Examples

climb a sand dune
On our trip to the beach, we rested on a large sand dune near the shore.
Shifting winds can reshape a sand dune over time.
The children climbed the sand dune and rolled down its sandy slope.
