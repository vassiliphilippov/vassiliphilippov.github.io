open-hearth process

noun
steelmaking process using an open-hearth furnace
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpənˈhɑrθ ˈproʊsɛs / (AmE), / ˌəʊpənˈhɑːθ ˈprəʊsɛs / (BrE)
