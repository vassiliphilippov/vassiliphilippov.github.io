puff of smoke

noun
a small cloud or burst of smoke
Pronunciation: / pʌf əv ˈsmoʊk / (AmE), / pʌf əv ˈsməʊk / (BrE)
a small, quick cloud or burst of smoke, often appearing suddenly and disappearing quickly

Examples

a puff of smoke rose
A single puff of smoke rose from the chimney and drifted into the sky.
The magician vanished in a puff of smoke during his grand finale.
With a sputter, the old engine gave out one last puff of smoke before stopping.
