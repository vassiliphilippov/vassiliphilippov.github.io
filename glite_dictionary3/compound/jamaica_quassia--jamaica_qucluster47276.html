Jamaica quassia

noun
West Indian tree yielding quassia drug
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈmeɪkə ˈkwɑːs.i.ə / (AmE), / dʒəˈmeɪkə ˈkwæs.i.ə / (BrE)
A West Indian tree (Picrasma excelsa) whose wood yields Jamaica quassia, used as a bitter tonic and insecticide.
