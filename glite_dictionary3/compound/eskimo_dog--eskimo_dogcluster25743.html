Eskimo dog

noun
heavy-coated Arctic sled dog
Pronunciation: / ˈɛskɪˌmoʊ dɔg / (AmE), / ˈɛskɪməʊ dɒg / (BrE)
A breed of heavy-coated Arctic sled dog, traditionally used for pulling sleds across snow and ice.

Examples

Eskimo dog pulls sled
Early explorers often relied on teams of Eskimo dogs to travel across snowy landscapes.
Her family has raised Eskimo dogs for generations, training them to pull heavy sleds over long distances.
The Eskimo dog is renowned for its strength and endurance in freezing Arctic conditions.
