robber frog

noun
a southwestern U.S. and Mexican frog with a bark-like call
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːbər frɑːɡ / (AmE), / ˈrɒbə frɒɡ / (BrE)
a small frog of the southwestern United States and Mexico, known for its bark-like call resembling a dog's bark

Examples

barking robber frog
At night, the distinctive call of the robber frog echoed through the desert canyon.
Many hikers were surprised to learn that the robber frog's croak sounds like a barking dog.
Researchers studied the habitat of the robber frog in the arid regions of northern Mexico.
