cow lily

noun
North American water lily with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / kaʊ ˈlɪli / (AmE), / kaʊ ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A common water lily of eastern and central North America, with broad leaves and globe-shaped yellow flowers, found in sluggish freshwater or slightly brackish water.

Examples

cow lily yellow flower
In early summer, the yellow blooms of the cow lily brighten up wetlands across the region.
The cow lily is often seen floating on the surface of quiet ponds and lakes.
We watched turtles resting on the broad leaves of a cow lily in the marsh.
