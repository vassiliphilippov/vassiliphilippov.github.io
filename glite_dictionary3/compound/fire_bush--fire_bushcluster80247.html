fire bush

noun
South American ornamental shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə bʊʃ / (BrE)
An evergreen South American shrub with showy trumpet-shaped orange flowers, cultivated as an ornamental or houseplant.

Examples

fire bush orange flowers
Gardeners often choose the fire bush for its striking orange flowers and easy maintenance.
In the winter garden, the fire bush remains lush and green when many other plants have shed their leaves.
The fire bush added a splash of vibrant color to the conservatory.
