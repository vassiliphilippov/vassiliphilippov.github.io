petrol line

noun
pipe supplying petrol to engine
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtroʊl laɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrəl laɪn / (BrE)
a pipe that carries petrol from a tank to the engine in a vehicle

Examples

petrol line leak
Always check the petrol line for cracks or wear during routine maintenance.
If the petrol line is blocked, the engine will not receive any fuel.
The mechanic discovered a leak in the petrol line that needed urgent repair.
