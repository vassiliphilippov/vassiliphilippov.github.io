Hindoostani language

noun
early form of Hindi spoken near Delhi
Pronunciation: / hɪnˌduːˈstɑːni ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / hɪnˌduːˈstɑːni ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an early form of Hindi spoken around Delhi, considered a historical basis of modern Hindi and Urdu
