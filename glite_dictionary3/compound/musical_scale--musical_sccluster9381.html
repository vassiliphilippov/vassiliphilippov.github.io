musical scale

noun
series of musical notes by pitch
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzɪkəl skeɪl / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪkəl skeɪl / (BrE)
A set of musical notes ordered by ascending or descending pitch according to a particular pattern, usually within an octave.
