red carpet

noun
special treatment or hospitality
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈkɑːrpɪt / (AmE), / rɛd ˈkɑːpɪt / (BrE)
special attention or hospitality given to an important guest; the treatment of someone as an honored visitor

Examples

receive the red carpet treatment
He was given the red carpet welcome wherever he went.
The actress received the red carpet treatment at the film premiere.
We rolled out the red carpet for our overseas guests.
