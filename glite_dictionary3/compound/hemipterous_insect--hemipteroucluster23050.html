hemipterous insect

noun
insect with sucking mouthparts (Hemiptera)
Pronunciation: / hɛˈmɪptɚəs ˈɪnsɛkt / (AmE), / hɛˈmɪptərəs ˈɪnsɛkt / (BrE)
an insect characterized by having sucking mouthparts and forewings that are thickened and leathery at the base, usually undergoing incomplete metamorphosis; member of the order Hemiptera

Examples

hemipterous insect with sucking mouthparts

The aphid is a well-known hemipterous insect that damages many garden plants.
