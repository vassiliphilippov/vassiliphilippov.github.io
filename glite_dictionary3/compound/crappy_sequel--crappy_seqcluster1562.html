crappy sequel

noun
poorly made follow-up film
Pronunciation: / ˈkræpi ˈsiːkwəl / (AmE), / ˈkræpi ˈsiːkwəl / (BrE)
An informal term for a sequel to a film that is considered to be of poor quality or disappointing compared to the original.

Examples

a crappy sequel movie
After enjoying the original, I had low expectations for the crappy sequel, and unfortunately I was right.
Critics agreed that the crappy sequel brought nothing new or interesting to the storyline.
Many fans were disappointed when the studio released a crappy sequel to their favorite action movie.
