hare and hounds

noun
outdoor running game with trail and pursuers
Pronunciation: / hɛr ənd haʊndz / (AmE), / heə ənd haʊndz / (BrE)
an outdoor game in which a group of runners (the hares) leave a trail for others (the hounds) to follow and try to catch them before the finish

Examples

play hare and hounds outdoors
At the picnic, the children played hare and hounds across the open fields.
The club organized a hare and hounds event, drawing participants from neighboring towns.
We enjoyed a lively session of hare and hounds on Saturday morning.
