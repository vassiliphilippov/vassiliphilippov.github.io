en famille

adverb
privately with one's family
Pronunciation: / ɑn faˈmij / (AmE), / ɑ̃ faˈmi / (BrE)
In a private and informal setting with one's family; at home, with only close family present.

Examples

dine en famille
The ambassador dined en famille after a long week of public engagements.
Their Sunday afternoons were spent en famille, enjoying quiet time together.
We usually celebrate birthdays en famille, without any guests.
