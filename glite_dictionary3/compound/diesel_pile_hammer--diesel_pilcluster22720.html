diesel pile hammer

noun
pile driver powered by diesel engine
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːzəl paɪl ˈhæmər / (AmE), / ˈdiːzəl paɪl ˈhamə / (BrE)
A pile driving device powered by a large two-stroke diesel engine, used in construction to drive piles into the ground.

Examples

operate a diesel pile hammer
Due to its power, the diesel pile hammer is often preferred for large-scale projects involving heavy foundations.
Operators must receive special training before operating a diesel pile hammer on site.
