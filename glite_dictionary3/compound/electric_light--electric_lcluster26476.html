electric light

noun
artificial light powered by electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk laɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk laɪt / (BrE)
A device that produces light using electricity, typically consisting of a glass enclosure and a heated wire filament.
