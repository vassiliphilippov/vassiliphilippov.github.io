pressure gauge

noun
instrument for measuring fluid pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃər ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure and display the pressure of a fluid (such as a gas or liquid) within a system
