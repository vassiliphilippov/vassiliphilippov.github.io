Midsummer Day

noun
quarter day around June 24 in England, Wales, and Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌsʌmɚ deɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌsʌmə deɪ / (BrE)
A traditional quarter day in England, Wales, and Ireland, celebrated around June 24, marking the middle of summer.

Midsummer Day celebrations
Contracts in some parts of England used to be renewed on Midsummer Day.
