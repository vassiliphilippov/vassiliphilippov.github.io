supraorbital vein

noun
vein draining the front of the scalp
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈɔrbɪtl veɪn / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈɔːbɪtl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the front of the scalp and unites with the supratrochlear vein to form the angular vein

Examples

supraorbital vein drains forehead
Blood from the forehead typically drains through the supraorbital vein into the angular vein.
Swelling of the supraorbital vein can sometimes cause discomfort above the eyes.
The supraorbital vein is often identified during surgical procedures involving the forehead.
