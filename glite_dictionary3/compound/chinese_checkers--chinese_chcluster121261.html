Chinese checkers

noun
board game with marbles on a star-shaped board
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪ.niz ˈtʃɛk.ərz / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪ.niːz ˈtʃɛk.əz / (BrE)
a board game played on a six-pointed star-shaped board in which each player tries to move a set of marbles from one point of the star to the opposite point

Examples

play Chinese checkers with friends
At the party, we set up a Chinese checkers tournament to entertain the guests.
