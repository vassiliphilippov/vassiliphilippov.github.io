electric typewriter

noun
typewriter powered by electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈtaɪpˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛk.trɪk ˈtaɪpˌraɪ.tər / (BrE)
A typewriter powered by an electric motor, making typing less effortful than on a manual typewriter.

Examples

old electric typewriter
Many offices replaced their old manual machines with electric typewriters in the 1960s.
She inherited a vintage electric typewriter from her grandfather.
The electric typewriter made typing long documents much easier.
