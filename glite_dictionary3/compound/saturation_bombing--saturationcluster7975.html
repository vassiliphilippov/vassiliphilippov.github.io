saturation bombing

noun
extensive, systematic area bombing
Pronunciation: / ˌsætʃəˈreɪʃən ˈbɑːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsætʃʊˈreɪʃən ˈbɒmɪŋ / (BrE)
A military strategy involving large-scale, systematic bombing aimed at destroying an entire area or target.

Examples

saturation bombing of a city
Military historians debated the effectiveness of saturation bombing compared to more precise methods.
The city was almost entirely destroyed by saturation bombing during the war.
The extensive use of saturation bombing had devastating consequences for civilians.

Related Terms

  • synonym – carpet bombing: large-scale bombing covering an entire area
  • antonym – precision bombing: targeted bombing of specific military objectives
