yoghurt-knitter

noun
person concerned with health food (humorous)
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊɡərt ˌnɪtər / (AmE), / ˈjɒɡət ˌnɪtə / (BrE)
a humorous or mildly mocking term for someone overly concerned with healthy eating, or who makes or enjoys health foods and snacks

Examples

health-conscious yoghurt-knitter
The yoghurt-knitter in our group suggested we all try a vegan lunch next week.
They called her a yoghurt-knitter because she always brought kale chips and chia pudding to work.
You don't have to be a yoghurt-knitter to enjoy homemade granola and herbal tea.
