edible-pod pea

noun
pea with edible pod
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdəbl pɑd pi / (AmE), / ˈɛdəbl pɒd piː / (BrE)
a variety of pea plant that produces peas with soft, thick, edible pods lacking the fibrous lining found in common peas

Examples

edible-pod pea plant
He grew several varieties of edible-pod peas in his backyard garden.
Stir-fries often include edible-pod peas for their sweet flavor and crunchy texture.
Unlike regular peas, edible-pod peas can be eaten whole, pod and all.
