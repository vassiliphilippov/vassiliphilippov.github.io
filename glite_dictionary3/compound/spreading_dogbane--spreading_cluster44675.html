spreading dogbane

noun
North American plant with pinkish flowers; used in folk medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɛdɪŋ ˈdɔɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdɪŋ ˈdɒɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a North American perennial plant (Apocynum androsaemifolium) with pinkish flowers in loose clusters, traditionally used in folk medicine for joint pain and inflammation
