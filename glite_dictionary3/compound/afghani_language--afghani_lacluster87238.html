Afghani language

noun
a language spoken in Afghanistan and Pakistan
Pronunciation: / æfˈɡɑːni ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / æfˈɡɑːni ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken in Afghanistan and Pakistan; often used to refer to either Pashto or Dari, the official languages of Afghanistan

Examples

Afghani language spoken in Afghanistan

The book provides a brief introduction to the Afghani language for travelers.
