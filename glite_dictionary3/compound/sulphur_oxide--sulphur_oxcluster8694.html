sulphur oxide

noun
chemical compound of sulphur and oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfɚ ˈɑkˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə ˈɒksaɪd / (BrE)
any of several chemical compounds consisting of sulphur and oxygen, notably sulphur dioxide and sulphur trioxide, which are important in chemistry and environmental science
