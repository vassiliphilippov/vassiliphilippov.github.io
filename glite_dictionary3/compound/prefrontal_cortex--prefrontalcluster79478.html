prefrontal cortex

noun
front part of the brain's frontal lobe
Pronunciation: / priˈfrʌntəl ˈkɔrtɛks / (AmE), / priˈfrʌntəl ˈkɔːtɛks / (BrE)
the front part of the frontal lobe of the brain, involved in complex cognitive behavior and decision making
