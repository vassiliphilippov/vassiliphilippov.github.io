sausage stuffer

noun
device for filling sausage casings
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɪdʒ ˈstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ ˈstʌfə / (BrE)
a device used to force ground meat into a casing to make sausages, typically operated by hand
