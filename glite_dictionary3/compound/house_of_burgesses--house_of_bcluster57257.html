House of Burgesses

proper noun
lower house of colonial Virginia legislature
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊs əv ˈbɜrdʒəsɪz / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs əv ˈbɜːdʒɪsɪz / (BrE)
