chaulmoogra tree

noun
East Indian tree used for medicinal oil
Pronunciation: / tʃɔlˈmuːɡrə triː / (AmE), / tʃɔːlˈmuːɡrə triː / (BrE)
An East Indian tree whose oily seeds are used to produce chaulmoogra oil, formerly utilized in treating leprosy.
