Chinese anise

noun
tree with aromatic star-shaped fruit, used as spice
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈniz ˈænɪs /; / ˈtʃaɪ.niz ˈæn.is / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz ˈænɪs /; / ˈtʃaɪ.niːz ˈæn.ɪs / (BrE)
a small tree native to China and Vietnam, and its star-shaped, anise-scented fruit used as a spice in cooking and traditional medicine
