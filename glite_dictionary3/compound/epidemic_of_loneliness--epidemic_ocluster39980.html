epidemic of loneliness

noun
widespread loneliness in a population
Pronunciation: / ɛˈpɪdəmɪk əv ˈloʊnlinəs / (AmE), / ɛˈpɪdəmɪk əv ˈləʊnlinəs / (BrE)
a situation in which large numbers of people in a society experience feelings of isolation and lack of meaningful social connections

Examples

an epidemic of loneliness in society
Governments are taking steps to address the epidemic of loneliness affecting millions of citizens.
Many health officials have described the current rise in isolation as an epidemic of loneliness.
Social media has ironically contributed to an epidemic of loneliness among younger generations.
