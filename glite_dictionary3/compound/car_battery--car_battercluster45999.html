car battery

noun
battery in a motor vehicle
Pronunciation: / kɑr ˈbætəri / (AmE), / kɑː ˈbætəri / (BrE)
A rechargeable battery, usually of the lead-acid type, used to start and power the electrical systems of a motor vehicle.

Examples

car battery replacement
It's important to check your car battery before going on a long road trip.
She jump-started her friend's car because the car battery had run out of power.
The mechanic said my car battery was completely dead and needed replacing.
