pole jumper

noun
athlete using a pole to jump over a high bar
Pronunciation: / poʊl ˈdʒʌmpər / (AmE), / pəʊl ˈdʒʌmpə / (BrE)
an athlete who jumps over a high crossbar using a long pole, especially in the sport of pole vault

Examples
