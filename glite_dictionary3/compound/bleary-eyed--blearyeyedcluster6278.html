bleary-eyed

adjective
having tired-looking or unfocused eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪəri aɪd / (AmE), / ˈblɪəri aɪd / (BrE)
having eyes that look tired, as from lack of sleep or exhaustion

Examples

bleary-eyed in the morning
He stumbled into the kitchen looking bleary-eyed after a long night of studying.
She gave him a bleary-eyed smile before heading back to bed.
The bleary-eyed commuters waited silently for the early morning train.
