personal cheque

noun
cheque from a personal bank account
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl tʃɛk / (BrE)
a cheque drawn against funds in an individual's personal account, not a business account

Examples

pay by personal cheque
