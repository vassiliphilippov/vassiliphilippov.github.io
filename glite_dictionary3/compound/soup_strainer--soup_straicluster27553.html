soup strainer

noun
kitchen tool for filtering soup or liquids
Pronunciation: / suːp ˈstreɪnər / (AmE), / suːp ˈstreɪnə / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil used to filter liquids or separate solids from liquids, especially when making or serving soup

Examples

soup strainer for broth
After boiling the pasta, she poured it through a soup strainer to drain the water.
He bought a stainless steel soup strainer for his new kitchen.
The chef reached for the soup strainer to remove the vegetables from the broth.
