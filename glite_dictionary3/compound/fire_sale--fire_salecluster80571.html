fire sale

noun
sale of goods at very low prices under duress
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ seɪl /; / ˈfaɪɚ ˌseɪl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə seɪl /; / ˈfaɪə ˌseɪl / (BrE)
a sale of goods at very low prices because the seller needs to sell quickly, often due to an emergency like bankruptcy or fire damage
