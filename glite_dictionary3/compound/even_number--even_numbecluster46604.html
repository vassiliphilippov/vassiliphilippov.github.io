even number

noun
number divisible by two
Pronunciation: / ˈivən ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈiːvən ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a whole number that is exactly divisible by two, such as 2, 4, 6, etc.

Examples

an even number of students
Please try to split the group into teams with an even number of players each.
The organizer requested an even number of tables for the room.
When you add two even numbers, the result is always even.
