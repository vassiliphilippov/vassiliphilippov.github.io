china stone

noun
type of white clay used in ceramics
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪnə stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnə stəʊn / (BrE)
a fine, usually white clay formed by the weathering of aluminous minerals, used in ceramics and as an absorbent or filler
