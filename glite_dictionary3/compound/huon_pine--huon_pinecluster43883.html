Huon pine

noun
Tasmanian timber tree with aromatic yellow wood
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuː.ɒn paɪn / (AmE), / ˈhjuː.ɒn paɪn / (BrE)
a large, slow-growing Tasmanian tree (Lagarostrobos franklinii) valued for its durable, yellowish, aromatic wood used in carving and shipbuilding
