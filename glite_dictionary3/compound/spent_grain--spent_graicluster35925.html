spent grain

noun
residue of malt/grains after brewing
Pronunciation: / spɛnt ɡreɪn / (AmE), / spɛnt ɡreɪn / (BrE)
the residue of malt and grains left after mashing and lautering during brewing, often used as animal feed.

Examples
