demand for explanation

noun
request for clarification or justification
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmænd fər ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːnd fə ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an insistence or request that someone clarify or justify what they have said or done

Examples

demand for explanation from management
A sudden demand for explanation from the board caught the manager off guard.
The senator faced a demand for explanation after her controversial comments at the conference.
When mistakes were discovered, there was an immediate demand for explanation from the shareholders.
