testament to

noun
evidence or proof of something
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstəmənt tu / (AmE), / ˈtɛstəmənt tʊ / (BrE)
evidence or proof that something exists or is true; something that shows clearly that a particular fact or quality is present.

Examples

testament to hard work
His quick recovery was a testament to the skill of the medical team.
The city's vibrant culture is a testament to its rich and diverse history.
The success of her company is a testament to her hard work and dedication.
