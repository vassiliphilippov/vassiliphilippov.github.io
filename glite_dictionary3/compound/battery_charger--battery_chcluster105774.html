battery charger

noun
device for charging rechargeable batteries
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəri ˌtʃɑrdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈbætəri ˌtʃɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a device that restores energy to a rechargeable battery by passing an electric current through it

Examples

battery charger for phones

The phone won't work until you connect it to the battery charger.
