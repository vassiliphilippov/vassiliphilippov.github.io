ice bag

noun
bag filled with ice for reducing swelling
Pronunciation: / aɪs bæɡ / (AmE), / aɪs bæɡ / (BrE)
a waterproof bag filled with ice, applied to the body to cool it or reduce swelling

Examples

ice bag for injury
He kept an ice bag on his knee to reduce the pain and swelling.
She pressed an ice bag against her swollen ankle after the injury.
The nurse gave him an ice bag to relieve his headache.
