sanghi community

noun
group supporting Hindu nationalist ideology in India
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːŋgi kəˈmjuːnəti / (AmE), / ˈsæŋgi kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a group of people associated with or supporting the Hindu nationalist ideology in India, especially those linked to the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) or similar organizations
