flower stalk

noun
erect stem bearing a flower
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊɚ stɑk / (AmE), / ˈflaʊə stɔːk / (BrE)
an upright, leafless stem bearing a flower or flower cluster, growing directly from the ground, as in a tulip

Examples

tall flower stalk
Each flower stalk produces a single, brightly colored bloom in spring.
She carefully trimmed the flower stalk to display the daffodil in a vase.
The tulip's flower stalk stood tall and straight above the green leaves.
