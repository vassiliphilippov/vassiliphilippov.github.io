whale louse

noun
marine parasite on whales
Pronunciation: / weɪl laʊs / (AmE), / weɪl laʊs / (BrE)
A small marine crustacean that lives as a parasite on whales and other cetaceans.

Examples

A magnified image revealed a whale louse clinging to the whale's fin.
Scientists collected several whale lice during studies of whale behavior.
