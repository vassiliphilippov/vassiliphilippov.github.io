Minoan civilisation

noun
Bronze Age culture of ancient Crete
Pronunciation: / mɪˈnoʊ.ən ˌsɪv.əl.əˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / mɪˈnəʊ.ən ˌsɪv.ɪ.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the Bronze Age culture that developed on Crete and flourished from about 3000 to 1100 BC, known for its advanced art, architecture, and writing systems
