jumping plant louse

noun
small leaping plant-feeding insect
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ plænt laʊs / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpɪŋ plɑːnt laʊs / (BrE)
a small, cicada-like insect with strong hind legs adapted for jumping, which feeds on the juices of plants

Examples

a jumping plant louse on leaves
A jumping plant louse can leap impressive distances despite its tiny size.
Scientists study the behavior of the jumping plant louse to learn about pest management.
The orchard was infested with jumping plant louse that damaged the fruit trees.
