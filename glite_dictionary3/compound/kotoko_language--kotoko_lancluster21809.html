Kotoko language

noun
Chadic language of the Kotoko people
Pronunciation: / koʊˈtoʊkoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəʊˈtəʊkəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken by the Kotoko people in areas south of Lake Chad, particularly along the lower Chari and Logone rivers
