public transport

noun
system of transport for the public
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈtrænspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈtrænspɔːt / (BrE)
a system or service of buses, trains, or similar vehicles that anyone can use, usually for a fee
