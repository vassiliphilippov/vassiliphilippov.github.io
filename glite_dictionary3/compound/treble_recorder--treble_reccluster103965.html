treble recorder

noun
medium-sized recorder instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛbəl rɪˈkoʊrdər / (AmE), / ˈtrɛbəl rɪˈkɔːdə / (BrE)
a medium-sized recorder, pitched higher than the tenor recorder and lower than the soprano, commonly used in music ensembles
