Bradley method of childbirth

noun
natural childbirth preparation method
Pronunciation: / ˈbrædli ˌmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldbɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈbrædli ˌmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldbɜːθ / (BrE)
