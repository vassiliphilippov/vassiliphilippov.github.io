church hat

noun
decorative hat worn for Sunday church services
Pronunciation: / tʃɝtʃ hæt / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ hæt / (BrE)
a decorative and elaborate hat traditionally worn by some women, especially Black women, for church services, particularly on Sundays

Examples

elegant church hat
Her grandmother always chose a different church hat for every special occasion.
Rows of colorful church hats filled the sanctuary each week.
She wore her favorite church hat to the Easter Sunday service.
