kept man

noun
man financially supported by a lover
Pronunciation: / kɛpt mæn / (AmE), / kɛpt man / (BrE)
A man who is financially supported by a woman with whom he has a romantic or sexual relationship, often outside of marriage.

Examples

a kept man by a wealthy woman
Despite his wealth, he was insulted by the suggestion that he was merely a kept man.
People whispered that the famous artist was her kept man, dependent on her fortune.
