altocumulus cloud

noun
cloud of rounded masses at mid-level
Pronunciation: / ˌæltoʊˈkjuːmjələs klaʊd / (AmE), / ˌæltəˈkjuːmjʊləs klaʊd / (BrE)
A type of white or gray cloud forming rounded masses or rolls at intermediate altitudes, typically between 2,000 and 7,000 meters (6,500 to 23,000 feet).
