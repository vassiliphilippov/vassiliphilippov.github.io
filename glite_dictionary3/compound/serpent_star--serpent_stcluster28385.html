serpent star

noun
marine animal with slender arms; brittle star
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrpənt stɑr / (AmE), / ˈsɜːpənt stɑː / (BrE)
a marine animal related to starfish, with slender, flexible arms extending from a small central disc; also called a brittle star
