blood sausage

noun
sausage made with animal blood
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈsɑsɪdʒ / (AmE), / blʌd ˈsɒsɪdʒ / (BrE)
A type of sausage made by cooking and mixing animal blood (usually pig's), fat, and other ingredients, typically having a dark color.

Examples

blood sausage with potatoes
