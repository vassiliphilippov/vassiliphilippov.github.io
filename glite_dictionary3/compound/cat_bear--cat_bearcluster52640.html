cat bear

noun
alternative name for the red panda
Pronunciation: / kæt ˈbɛr / (AmE), / kat ˈbɛə / (BrE)
An alternative name for the red panda, a small, reddish-brown, raccoon-like mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China.

Examples

a cat bear in the Himalayas
Although called a cat bear, this animal is more closely related to raccoons than to bears.
In the wild, the cat bear spends much of its time climbing trees and eating bamboo.
The cat bear is known for its striking reddish fur and bushy tail.
