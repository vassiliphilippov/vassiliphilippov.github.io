snow bank

noun
a mound or heap of snow
Pronunciation: / snoʊ bæŋk / (AmE), / snəʊ bæŋk / (BrE)
a mound or heap of snow, especially one formed by the wind or by snow being plowed to the side

Examples

a snow bank by the road
After the storm, a huge snow bank blocked the driveway.
Cars were parked beside the tall snow bank left by the plows.
The children climbed on the snow bank to build a fort.

Related Terms

  • synonym – snowdrift: a bank of snow heaped up by the wind
