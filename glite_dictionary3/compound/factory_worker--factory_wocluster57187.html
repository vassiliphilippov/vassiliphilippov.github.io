factory worker

noun
person employed in a factory
Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɚ ˌwɝː.kɚ / (AmE), / ˈfæk.təri ˌwɜː.kə / (BrE)
a person who is employed to work in a factory, usually performing physical or manual tasks as part of the manufacturing process
