bank of mum and dad

noun
informal: parental financial support
Pronunciation: / bæŋk əv mʌm ənd dæd / (AmE), / baŋk əv mʌm ənd dad / (BrE)
an informal term for parents who provide financial support, especially to their adult children for major expenses
