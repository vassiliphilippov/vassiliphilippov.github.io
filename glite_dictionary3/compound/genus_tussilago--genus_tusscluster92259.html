genus Tussilago

noun
genus of perennial herbs (coltsfoots)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs tʌsɪˈleɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs tʌsɪˈleɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of low, creeping yellow-flowered perennial herbs of north temperate regions, known as coltsfoots; sometimes includes related species classified in other genera.
