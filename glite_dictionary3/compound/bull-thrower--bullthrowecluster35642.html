bull-thrower

noun
aggressively blunt or outspoken person
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊl ˌθroʊər / (AmE), / ˈbʊl ˌθrəʊə / (BrE)
a person who is aggressively blunt or outspoken in discussions, often speaking at length or without restraint

Examples

outspoken bull-thrower
Despite her expertise, many in the team found her to be a bull-thrower who dominated every meeting.
The new manager has a reputation as a bull-thrower who always speaks his mind, regardless of others' opinions.
You don't have to be a bull-thrower to make your point; sometimes quiet confidence is more effective.
