salt flat

noun
level area covered with salt deposits
Pronunciation: / sɔlt flæt / (AmE), / sɒlt flæt / (BrE)
A level area of land covered with salt left after the evaporation of salt water.

Many migratory birds stop at the salt flats during their long journeys.
