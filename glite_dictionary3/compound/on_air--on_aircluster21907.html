on air

adjective
broadcasting live
Pronunciation: / ɑn ɛr / (AmE), / ɒn eə / (BrE)
(of a radio or television program or person) broadcasting live to the public.

Examples

show on air
Once the red light comes on, the show is on air.
The mistakes he made on air quickly went viral online.
The news anchor is on air every evening at six.

Related Terms

  • antonym – off air: not broadcasting live; not currently transmitted
