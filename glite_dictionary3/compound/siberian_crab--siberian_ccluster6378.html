Siberian crab

noun
Asian crab apple tree with small, tart fruit
Pronunciation: / sɪˈbɪriən kræb / (AmE), / sɪˈbɪəriən kræb / (BrE)
an Asian wild crab apple tree (Malus baccata) cultivated for its small, acidic, usually red fruit used in preserves
