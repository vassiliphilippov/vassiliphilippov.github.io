center bit

noun
drill bit with a central point and two cutters
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɚ bɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə bɪt / (BrE)
a type of drill bit with a sharp central point for guidance and two side cutters, used for boring holes in wood.

Examples

center bit for wood drilling
A center bit is ideal for guiding the drill straight through a workpiece.
The carpenter selected a center bit from his toolbox to start the hole precisely.
Using a center bit, she bored clean, accurate holes in the wooden board.
