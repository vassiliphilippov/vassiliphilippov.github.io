Balochi language

noun
an Iranian language of South and Central Asia
Pronunciation: / bəˈloʊtʃi ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / bəˈləʊtʃi ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken mainly in Pakistan, Iran, and Afghanistan, with communities in parts of the Persian Gulf and Russia.
