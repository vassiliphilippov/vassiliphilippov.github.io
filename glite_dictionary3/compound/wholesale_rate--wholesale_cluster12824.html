wholesale rate

noun
price per unit for bulk purchases
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlˌseɪl reɪt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊlseɪl reɪt / (BrE)
the price charged for goods when bought in large quantities, typically by a retailer or distributor rather than an end consumer

Examples

wholesale rate for bulk orders
Online platforms may allow small businesses to access the same wholesale rates as larger retailers.
Retail stores often buy clothing at the wholesale rate and sell them at higher retail prices.
The distributor offered a significant discount on the wholesale rate for large orders.

Related Terms

  • antonym – retail price: the price at which goods are sold to the final consumer
  • synonym – wholesale price: the price charged for goods in bulk (synonym for 'wholesale rate')
