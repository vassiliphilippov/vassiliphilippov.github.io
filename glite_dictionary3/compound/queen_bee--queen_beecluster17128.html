queen bee

noun
dominant woman in a group
Pronunciation: / kwiːn biː / (AmE), / kwiːn biː / (BrE)
a woman who is the dominant or most influential member of a group, often dictating social dynamics and decisions

Examples

the queen bee of school
He didn't like her queen bee attitude, always bossing people around.
In her high school, Jessica was always the queen bee, setting trends for everyone else.
The office queen bee quickly made it clear she was in charge of social events.

Related Terms

  • antonym – follower: a person who supports and is guided by another
  • synonym – leader: a person who leads or commands a group or organization
