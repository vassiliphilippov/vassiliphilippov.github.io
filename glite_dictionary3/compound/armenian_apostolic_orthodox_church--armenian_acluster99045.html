Armenian Apostolic Orthodox Church

proper noun
independent Armenian Christian church
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈminiən əˌpɑstəlɪk ˈɔrθədɑks tʃɜːrtʃ / (AmE), / ɑːˈmiːniən əˌpɒstəlɪk ˈɔːθədɒks tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
An independent Christian church established in Armenia around 300 CE, influenced by both Roman and Byzantine traditions.

Examples

Many Armenians attend services at the Armenian Apostolic Orthodox Church in their city.
The Armenian Apostolic Orthodox Church is one of the world's oldest Christian communities.
