tongue depressor

noun
medical instrument for pressing the tongue
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋ dɪˌprɛsər / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋ dɪˌprɛsə / (BrE)
A thin, flat medical instrument used to press down the tongue to allow examination of the mouth and throat.

Examples

wooden tongue depressor
As a child, I disliked the taste of the wooden tongue depressor during checkups.
Nurses ensure that each tongue depressor is sterile before use on patients.
