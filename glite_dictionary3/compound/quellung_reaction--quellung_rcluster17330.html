quellung reaction

noun
microbiology test detecting capsule swelling
Pronunciation: / ˈkvelʊŋ riˌækʃən / (AmE), / ˈkwɛlʊŋ rɪˈakʃən / (BrE)
a reaction in which the capsule of certain microorganisms swells visibly when treated with specific antibodies, used as a test for identification
