triple crème

noun
very rich, soft French cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpəl ˈkrem / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpl ˈkrem / (BrE)
A rich, fresh, soft French cheese containing at least 72% milk fat in its dry matter.

Examples

French triple creme cheese
At the market, I bought a wheel of French triple crème for our cheese board.
She served slices of triple crème cheese with fresh fruit as an appetizer.
The triple crème was so creamy and rich, it melted instantly on the tongue.
