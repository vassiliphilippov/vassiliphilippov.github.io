ten percent

noun
one tenth; 10% of a whole
Pronunciation: / tɛn pɚˈsɛnt / (AmE), / tɛn pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
One part in every ten, equal to 10% of a total amount.

Examples

ten percent of sales
Employees will receive a ten percent bonus if the company meets its targets.
Only ten percent of the respondents answered yes to the survey question.
The product price was reduced by ten percent during the sale.
