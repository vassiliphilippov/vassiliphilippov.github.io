Brazilian rosewood

noun
a Brazilian tree valued for its dark wood
Pronunciation: / brəˈzɪl.i.ən ˈroʊzˌwʊd / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl.i.ən ˈrəʊz.wʊd / (BrE)
a large Brazilian tree (Dalbergia nigra) valued for its hard, dark-colored wood streaked with black, used especially for furniture and musical instruments
