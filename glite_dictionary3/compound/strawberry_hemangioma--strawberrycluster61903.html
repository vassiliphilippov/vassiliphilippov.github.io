strawberry hemangioma

noun
red vascular birthmark in infants
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔːˌbɛri hɪˌmændʒiˈoʊmə / (AmE), / ˈstrɒˌbəri hɪˌmandʒiˈəʊmə / (BrE)
A congenital, bright red, raised vascular birthmark that resembles a strawberry and usually decreases in size during childhood.

Many strawberry hemangiomas do not require treatment unless they interfere with vision or breathing.
