Congo River

proper noun
major river in central Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːŋɡoʊ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡəʊ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A major river in central Africa, one of the world's longest, flowing through the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the Atlantic Ocean.
