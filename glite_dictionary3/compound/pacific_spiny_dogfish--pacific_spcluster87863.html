Pacific spiny dogfish

noun
small Pacific coast dogfish shark
Pronunciation: / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈspaɪni ˈdɔɡˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈspaɪni ˈdɒɡˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
