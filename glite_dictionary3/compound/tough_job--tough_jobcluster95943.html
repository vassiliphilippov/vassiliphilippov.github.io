tough job

noun
difficult or demanding task
Pronunciation: / tʌf dʒɑb / (AmE), / tʌf dʒɒb / (BrE)
a task or position that is difficult or demanding and requires great effort or endurance

Examples

a tough job to finish
Cleaning up after the big party was a tough job that lasted all morning.
It’s a tough job juggling work and school, but he manages to do both.
Landing that promotion will be a tough job, but she's determined to succeed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arduous task: a task requiring much effort, similar in meaning to 'tough job'
  • antonym – easy job: a task that is simple and requires little effort
