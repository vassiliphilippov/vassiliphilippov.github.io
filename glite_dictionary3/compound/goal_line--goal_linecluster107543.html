goal line

noun
line marking end of sports field
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊl laɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl laɪn / (BrE)
a line marking the end of the field or pitch, where the goal stands in sports such as football or soccer

Examples

cross the goal line
A defender stopped the ball just inches from the goal line.
The ball crossed the goal line, resulting in a point for the team.
The referee checked if the pass had gone over the goal line before making a decision.
