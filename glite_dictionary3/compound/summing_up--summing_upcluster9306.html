summing up

noun
final summary or conclusion
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈsʌmɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
a concluding summary of the main points, especially at the end of an argument or legal case

Examples

summing up the discussion
At the end of the meeting, she gave a brief summing up of the discussion.
The judge delivered his summing up before sending the jury out to deliberate.
The lawyer's summing up highlighted the weaknesses in the opposition's case.
