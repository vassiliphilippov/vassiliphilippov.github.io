pro-choice faction

noun
group supporting abortion rights
Pronunciation: / proʊ ˈtʃɔɪs ˈfækʃən / (AmE), / prəʊ ˈtʃɔɪs ˈfækʃən / (BrE)
a group of people who support the right of women to choose whether to have an abortion
