bureaucratic procedure

noun
needlessly complicated official process
Pronunciation: / bjʊˌɹɑː.kɹəˈtɪk pɹəˈsiː.dʒɚ / (AmE), / bjʊəˌɹɒk.rəˈtɪk prəˈsiː.dʒə / (BrE)
an official process or set of rules that is often unnecessarily complicated and time-consuming, typically associated with bureaucracy

Examples
