national monument

noun
historic structure or site protected by government
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənl ˈmɑnjuˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈnaʃənəl ˈmɒnjuːmənt / (BrE)
A structure or natural landmark of historic interest that has been set aside by a national government for preservation and public enjoyment.
