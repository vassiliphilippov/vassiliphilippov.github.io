North Germanic language

noun
Germanic language group of Scandinavia and Iceland
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ dʒərˈmænɪk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / nɔːθ dʒɜːˈmænɪk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the Germanic languages spoken primarily in Scandinavia and Iceland, such as Swedish, Danish, Norwegian, Icelandic, and Faroese
