Duchenne's muscular dystrophy

noun
a severe inherited muscle disease affecting boys
Pronunciation: / duːˈʃɛnz ˈmʌskjələr dɪsˈtrəfi / (AmE), / djʊˈʃɛnz ˈmʌskjʊlə dɪsˈtrəfi / (BrE)
a severe inherited type of muscular dystrophy that primarily affects boys, causing progressive muscle weakness; it is carried by females but affects only males due to X-linked recessive inheritance
