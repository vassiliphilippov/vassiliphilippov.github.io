European nuthatch

noun
Eurasian nuthatch songbird
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən ˈnʌtˌhætʃ / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈnʌtˌhætʃ / (BrE)
a small songbird of Eurasia, Sitta europaea, with blue-grey upperparts and a buff or white underside, known for climbing tree trunks and feeding on insects and seeds
