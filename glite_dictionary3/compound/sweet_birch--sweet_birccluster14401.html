sweet birch

noun
North American birch tree with spicy bark
Pronunciation: / swit bɜrtʃ / (AmE), / swiːt bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A North American birch tree (Betula lenta) with dark, spicy-scented bark used for oil and hard wood for furniture.
