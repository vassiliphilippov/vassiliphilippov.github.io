butternut tree

noun
North American walnut tree with edible nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtərˌnʌt tri / (AmE), / ˈbʌtənʌt triː / (BrE)
A North American walnut tree (Juglans cinerea) with light-brown wood and edible nuts, also used as a source of light-brown dye.

Examples

tall butternut tree
Native Americans traditionally used dye from the bark of the butternut tree for coloring fabrics.
The old butternut tree in our backyard provides ample shade during the summer.
We collected a basket of ripe nuts from the butternut tree last autumn.
