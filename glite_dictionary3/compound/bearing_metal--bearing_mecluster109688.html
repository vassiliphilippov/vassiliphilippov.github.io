bearing metal

noun
alloy for machine bearings
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪŋ ˌmɛtəl / (AmE), / ˈbeərɪŋ ˌmɛtəl / (BrE)
an alloy, often based on lead or tin, specifically used for making machine bearings due to its low friction and durability
