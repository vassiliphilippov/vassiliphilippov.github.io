trap-door spider

noun
spider that builds a silk-lined trapdoor nest
Pronunciation: / ˈtræp dɔr ˈspaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈtræp dɔː ˈspaɪdə / (BrE)
An American spider that constructs a silk-lined nest with a hinged, trapdoor-like lid.
