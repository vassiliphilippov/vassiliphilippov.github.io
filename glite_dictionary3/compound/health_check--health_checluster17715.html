health check

noun
diagnostic check for computer systems
Pronunciation: / hɛlθ tʃɛk / (AmE), / hɛlθ tʃɛk / (BrE)
A routine diagnostic test or inspection that checks the operational status, performance, and responsiveness of a computer system, service, or network.

Examples

system health check
The IT team runs a nightly health check to ensure all services remain responsive.
An automated health check alerted engineers when the database's response time doubled.
Before the product launch, we performed a full health check on every production server.

Related Terms

  • synonym – diagnostic: a test or procedure used to identify problems or measure performance
  • synonym – status check: a quick check to see whether a service or system is running correctly
