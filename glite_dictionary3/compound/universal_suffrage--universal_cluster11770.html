universal suffrage

noun
the right of all adults to vote
Pronunciation: / juːˈnɪvɚsəl ˈsʌfrɪdʒ / (AmE), / juːˈnɪvəsəl ˈsʌfrɪdʒ / (BrE)
The right of almost all adults to vote in political elections, regardless of sex, race, or wealth, except as disqualified by law.
