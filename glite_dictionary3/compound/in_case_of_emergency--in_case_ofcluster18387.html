in case of emergency

preposition
if an urgent situation occurs
Pronunciation: / ɪn keɪs əv ɪˈmɝːdʒənsi / (AmE), / ɪn keɪs əv ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi / (BrE)
if a serious or urgent situation happens; used to give instructions for actions to take when there is danger or an accident.

Examples

in case of emergency break glass
In case of emergency, break the glass to access the fire extinguisher.
Always keep your contact information updated in case of emergency.
The instructions say to call this number in case of emergency.
