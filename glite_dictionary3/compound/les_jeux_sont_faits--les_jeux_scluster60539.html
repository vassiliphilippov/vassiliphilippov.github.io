les jeux sont faits

x
the outcome is already decided
Pronunciation: / le ʒø sɔ̃ fɛ / (AmE), / le ʒø sɔ̃ fɛ / (BrE)
A French phrase used in English to mean that the decision is final or the outcome is already determined and cannot be changed.

Examples

les jeux sont faits decision final
"Les jeux sont faits, there's nothing more we can do now," he sighed, accepting the situation.
She whispered 'les jeux sont faits' as the contest began, knowing all preparations were complete.
When the final bid was in, the auctioneer declared, 'les jeux sont faits.'
