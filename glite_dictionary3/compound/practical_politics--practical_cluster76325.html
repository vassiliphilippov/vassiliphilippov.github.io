practical politics

noun
politics based on practical concerns, not theory
Pronunciation: / ˈpræk.tɪ.kəl ˈpɑ.lɪ.tɪks / (AmE), / ˈpræk.tɪ.kəl ˈpɒl.ɪ.tɪks / (BrE)
Politics concerned mainly with practical concerns and considerations, rather than with theories, morals, or ideologies.
