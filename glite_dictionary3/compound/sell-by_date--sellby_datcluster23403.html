sell-by date

noun
date marking latest sale of perishable goods
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl baɪ ˌdeɪt /; / ˈsɛl baɪ deɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛl baɪ ˌdeɪt /; / ˈsɛl baɪ deɪt / (BrE)
the date shown on food or other perishable products that tells you the last day it should be sold to ensure quality and freshness
