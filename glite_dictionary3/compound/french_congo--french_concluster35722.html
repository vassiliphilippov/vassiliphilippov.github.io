French Congo

proper noun
former name of the Republic of the Congo
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ ˈkɑŋɡoʊ / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈkɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
a former French colony in west-central Africa, now known as the Republic of the Congo, which gained independence in 1960.
