esprit de corps

noun
group spirit, morale, or shared pride
Pronunciation: / ɛsˌprit də ˈkɔr /; / ɛˌsprit də ˈkɔr / (AmE), / ɛsˌpriː də ˈkɔː /; / ɛˌspriː də ˈkɔː / (BrE)
the feeling of pride, loyalty, and unity shared by members of a group that motivates them to work together
