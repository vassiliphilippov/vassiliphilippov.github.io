Siberian spruce

noun
tall spruce tree of northern Europe and Asia
Pronunciation: / sɪˈbɪriən sprus / (AmE), / sɪˈbɪəriən spruːs / (BrE)
a tall spruce tree native to northern Europe and Asia, similar in appearance to the Norway spruce

Examples

tall Siberian spruce tree

Needles of the Siberian spruce are slightly longer than those of the Norway spruce.
The Siberian spruce can withstand extremely cold winters, making it a popular choice for reforestation projects in harsh climates.
