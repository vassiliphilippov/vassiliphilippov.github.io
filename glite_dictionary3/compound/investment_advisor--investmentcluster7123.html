investment advisor

noun
automated online investment service (robo-advisor)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛstmənt ədˈvaɪzər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛstmənt ədˈvaɪzə / (BrE)
(in finance/technology) a digital service or online platform that provides automated, algorithm-driven investment advice and portfolio management, often with little or no human involvement (also called a robo-advisor).

Examples

online investment advisor
Many beginners use a investment advisor online to automate portfolio allocation.
Their bank's investment advisor suggested a low-cost index fund portfolio via an app.
Before opening an account, she compared several investment advisor services and their annual fees.

Related Terms

  • synonym – financial advisor: a person or service that offers financial advice, including investments; the automated sense is a technology-driven type of this role
  • synonym – robo-advisor: an automated, algorithm-based service that gives investment advice and manages portfolios
