captive finance company

noun
manufacturer-owned finance company
Pronunciation: / ˈkæptɪv fəˈnæns ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈkæptɪv faɪˈnæns ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a finance company owned by a manufacturer and established to provide loans to dealers or consumers for purchasing the manufacturer's products

Examples
