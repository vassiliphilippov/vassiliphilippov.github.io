holding pattern

noun
flight path aircraft follows while awaiting landing clearance
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊldɪŋ ˌpætɚn /; / ˈhoʊldɪŋ ˌpætərn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊldɪŋ ˌpætn̩ / (BrE)
a flight path that an aircraft follows while waiting for permission to land
