black mustard

noun
Eurasian plant, source of mustard seeds
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈmʌstɚd / (AmE), / blæk ˈmʌstəd / (BrE)
a Eurasian annual plant (Brassica nigra) grown for its pungent seeds, used as a principal source of table mustard

Examples

black mustard seeds
Fields of black mustard bloom in late spring across the countryside.
Spicy black mustard seeds are often used in Indian cuisine.
