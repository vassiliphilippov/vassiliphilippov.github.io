repayment schedule

noun
plan for paying back money owed
Pronunciation: / riˈpeɪmənt ˈskɛdʒuːl / (AmE), / riˈpeɪmənt ˈʃɛdjuːl / (BrE)
an agreed plan that specifies the timing and amounts of payments required to pay back borrowed money

Examples

repayment schedule for a loan
A flexible repayment schedule made it easier for small businesses to manage their debts.
Before signing the contract, she carefully reviewed the repayment schedule to ensure she could afford the installments.
The bank provided a repayment schedule outlining monthly payments for the duration of the loan.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amortization schedule: table or plan showing each loan payment and breakdown of principal and interest
