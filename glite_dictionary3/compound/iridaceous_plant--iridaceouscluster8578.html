iridaceous plant

noun
bulbous plant of the Iridaceae family
Pronunciation: / ɪr.əˈdeɪ.ʃəs plænt / (AmE), / ɪr.ɪˈdeɪ.ʃəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
Any bulbous plant belonging to the family Iridaceae, including irises, crocuses, and gladioli.
