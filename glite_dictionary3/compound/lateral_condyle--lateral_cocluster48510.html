lateral condyle

noun
outer projection at lower femur
Pronunciation: / ˈlæt̬ərəl ˈkɑnˌdaɪl / (AmE), / ˈlætərəl ˈkɒndaɪl / (BrE)
the rounded protrusion on the outer side of the lower end of the femur, forming part of the knee joint

Examples

lateral condyle of femur
A fracture of the lateral condyle can affect knee stability.
During surgery, the doctor examined the lateral condyle for signs of damage.
The X-ray showed abnormal growth near the lateral condyle of the femur.
