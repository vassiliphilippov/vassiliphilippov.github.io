link whore

noun
someone who aggressively seeks web links for self-promotion
Pronunciation: / lɪŋk hɔɹ / (AmE), / lɪŋk hɔː / (BrE)
(slang, vulgar) A person who aggressively seeks backlinks for increased web traffic or self-promotion, often by spamming links online.

Examples

a link whore on forums
She admitted being a link whore during the early days of her blogging career.
Some users view him as a link whore because he posts the same article everywhere.
The forum moderators banned all link whores to reduce spam and improve discussion quality.
