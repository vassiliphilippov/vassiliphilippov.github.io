nihil obstat

noun
official Church declaration of no objection to publication
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.hɪl ˈɒb.stat, ˌnaɪ.hɪl ˈɑːb.stɑːt /; / ˌniːhɪl ˈɑːbstæt / (AmE), / ˌniː.hɪl ˈɒb.stat, ˌnaɪ.hɪl ˈɒb.stat /; / ˌniːhɪl ˈɒbstæt / (BrE)
an official statement (especially in the Roman Catholic Church) that a publication contains nothing objectionable to faith or morals
