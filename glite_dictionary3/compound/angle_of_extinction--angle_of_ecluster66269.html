angle of extinction

noun
rotation angle causing darkness in polarized light
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəl əv ɪkˈstɪŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡl əv ɪkˈstɪŋkʃən / (BrE)
the angle through which a crystal must be rotated from its axis before it appears maximally dark when viewed in polarized light
