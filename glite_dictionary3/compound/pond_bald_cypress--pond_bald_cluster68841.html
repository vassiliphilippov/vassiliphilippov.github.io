pond bald cypress

noun
a cypress tree of southeastern US swamps
Pronunciation: / pɑnd bɔld ˈsaɪprəs / (AmE), / pɒnd bɔːld ˈsɪprəs / (BrE)
a cypress tree (Taxodium ascendens) of the southeastern United States, with a swollen base, found in coastal swamps and flood-prone river areas
