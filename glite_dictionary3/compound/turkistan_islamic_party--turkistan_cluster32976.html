Turkistan Islamic Party

proper noun
Islamist militant group in Central Asia
Pronunciation: / tɜrˈkɪstæn aɪˈslæmɪk ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / tɜːˈkɪstæn ɪˈslæmɪk ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
