oil change

noun
replacing old oil in an engine
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɔɪl tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
the process of replacing the old, dirty oil in an engine with fresh, clean oil, typically as part of routine vehicle maintenance

Examples

oil change service
My car is due for an oil change this weekend.
The mechanic completed the oil change in less than thirty minutes.
You should get an oil change every 5,000 miles to keep your engine running smoothly.

Related Terms
