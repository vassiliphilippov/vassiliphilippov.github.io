junior varsity

noun
school or college team below varsity level
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuːniər ˈvɑːrsəti / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːniə ˈvɑːsəti / (BrE)
(in North American schools and colleges) a sports team representing a school or college that competes at a level below the main varsity team.

Examples

junior varsity basketball team
He made the junior varsity basketball team as a freshman and hopes to move up next year.
