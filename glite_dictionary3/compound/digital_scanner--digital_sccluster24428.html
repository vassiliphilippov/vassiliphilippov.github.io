digital scanner

noun
device converting images/documents to digital
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈskænər / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈskænə / (BrE)
an electronic device that creates a digital version of an image or document for input to a computer

Examples

digital scanner for documents
Every modern office is equipped with a digital scanner for document archiving.

You can use a digital scanner to make an electronic copy of a photograph.
