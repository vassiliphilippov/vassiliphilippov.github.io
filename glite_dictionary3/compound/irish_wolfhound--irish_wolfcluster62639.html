Irish wolfhound

noun
large Irish breed of hound
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈwʊlfˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ˈwʊlfˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
