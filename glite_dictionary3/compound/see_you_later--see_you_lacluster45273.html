see you later

x
informal farewell expression
Pronunciation: / si ju ˈleɪtɚ / (AmE), / siː juː ˈleɪtə / (BrE)
A casual way of saying goodbye, used when parting with someone, implying that you will meet again.

Examples

see you later at work
I'll finish the report and see you later at the meeting.
It was nice seeing you—see you later!
She waved and called out, 'see you later!' as she left the café.

  • synonym – goodbye: expression of farewell
  • degree lower – see you soon: farewell indicating expectation of meeting soon
