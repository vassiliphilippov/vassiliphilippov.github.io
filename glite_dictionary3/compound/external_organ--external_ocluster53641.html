external organ

noun
organ on or near body surface
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɝːnəl ˈɔːrɡən / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnəl ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
an organ located on or near the surface of the body

Examples

injured external organ
Injuries to an external organ are often easier to assess and treat than those to internal organs.
Some sensory receptors are part of external organs such as the eyes and ears.
The skin is considered the body's largest external organ.
