complementary medicine

noun
combining conventional and alternative medicine
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmpləˈmɛntəri ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntəri ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
A medical practice that combines conventional treatments with alternative or non-traditional therapies to support patient care.
