nomadic spirit

noun
urge or tendency to wander or explore
Pronunciation: / noʊˈmædɪk ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / nəʊˈmædɪk ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a tendency or strong desire to wander, travel, or explore new places rather than settle in one location

Examples

adventurous nomadic spirit
Despite having a stable job, his nomadic spirit kept him moving from one city to another.
Her nomadic spirit led her across continents in search of adventure.
Many artists are driven by a nomadic spirit that inspires their restless creativity.

Related Terms

  • antonym – homebody: a person who prefers staying at home
  • synonym – wanderlust: a strong desire to travel
