kill stealer

noun
player who takes another's kill in a game
Pronunciation: / kɪl stiːlɚ / (AmE), / kɪl stiːlə / (BrE)
a player in a multiplayer game who takes the final blow or credit for a kill that another player worked for

Examples

kill stealer in a team fight
He earned a reputation as a kill stealer after repeatedly taking the finishing blow.
Nobody likes a kill stealer in the team because it ruins the fun for everyone.
The game discourages kill stealers by rewarding teamwork instead of individual kills.
