fresh coffee

noun
newly brewed coffee
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ ˈkɑfi / (AmE), / frɛʃ ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
Coffee that has been freshly brewed and has not been sitting for long, often characterized by a strong aroma and full flavor.
