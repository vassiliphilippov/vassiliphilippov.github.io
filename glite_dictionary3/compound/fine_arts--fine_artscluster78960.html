fine arts

noun
visual arts for aesthetic purposes
Pronunciation: / faɪn ɑrts / (AmE), / faɪn ɑːts / (BrE)
Creative art forms such as painting, sculpture, and drawing, valued for their beauty and intellectual stimulation rather than practical function.
