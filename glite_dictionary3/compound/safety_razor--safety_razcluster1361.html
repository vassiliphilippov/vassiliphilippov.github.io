safety razor

noun
razor with a protective guard
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti ˌreɪzər / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti ˌreɪzə / (BrE)
a razor with a protective guard designed to minimize the risk of cutting the skin while shaving

Examples

use a safety razor carefully
A safety razor helps prevent accidental nicks when learning to shave.
He switched to a safety razor because it was gentler on his skin.
Many barbershops still offer shaves with a traditional safety razor.
