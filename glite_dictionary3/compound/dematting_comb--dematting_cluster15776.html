dematting comb

noun
comb for removing mats from pet fur
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmætɪŋ kʌm / (AmE), / dɪˈmætɪŋ kɒm / (BrE)
A special comb with sharp teeth designed to remove mats and tangles from an animal's coat.

Examples

dematting comb for pets
Before bathing your pet, gently run a dematting comb through their coat to remove any knots.
It's important to use a dematting comb regularly on long-haired cats to prevent painful tangles.
The groomer recommended a dematting comb for our dog's matted fur.
