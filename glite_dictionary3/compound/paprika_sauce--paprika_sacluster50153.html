paprika sauce

noun
creamy sauce with paprika and onion
Pronunciation: / pəˈprikə sɔs / (AmE), / pəˈpriːkə sɔːs / (BrE)
a creamy sauce made with sautéed chopped onion, paprika, and cream based on a velouté sauce

Examples

paprika sauce with chicken
She learned to make traditional paprika sauce from her Hungarian grandmother.
The chicken was topped with a rich paprika sauce that added a pleasant kick.
You can serve fish with a creamy paprika sauce for a flavorful dinner.
