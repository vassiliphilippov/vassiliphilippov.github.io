in the interest of

preposition
for the benefit of; for the sake of
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə ˈɪntrɪst əv / (AmE), / ɪn ði ˈɪntrəst ɒv / (BrE)
for the benefit or sake of (something or someone)

Examples

in the interest of safety
All decisions were made in the interest of public safety.
She skipped her vacation in the interest of finishing the project on time.
The company reduced costs in the interest of remaining competitive.
