mug book

noun
police file of criminal photos
Pronunciation: / mʌɡ bʊk / (AmE), / mʌɡ bʊk / (BrE)
a collection of photographs of known criminals kept by the police for identification purposes

Examples

police mug book
Police asked the witness to check the mug book for possible suspects.
The mug book contained hundreds of photos of previously arrested individuals.
The detective flipped through the mug book searching for a familiar face.
