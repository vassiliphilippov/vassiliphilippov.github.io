Stalinoid approach

noun
authoritarian leadership style like Stalin's
Pronunciation: / ˈstæ.lɪ.nɔɪd əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈstæ.lɪ.nɔɪd əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A style of leadership or management that employs authoritarian tactics and policies reminiscent of those used by Joseph Stalin.

Examples

Stalinoid approach to leadership
Critics accused the new manager of adopting a Stalinoid approach in running the company.
Many employees were uncomfortable with the Stalinoid approach the director employed to enforce discipline.
The Stalinoid approach to leadership leaves little room for creative input or dissent.
