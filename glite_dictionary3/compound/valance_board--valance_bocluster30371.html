valance board

noun
decorative board hiding curtain fixtures
Pronunciation: / ˈvæl.əns bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈvæl.əns bɔːd / (BrE)
a decorative board fixed at the top of a window to hide curtain fittings or hardware

valance board above window
