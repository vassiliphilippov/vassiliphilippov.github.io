herpes simplex encephalitis

noun
viral brain inflammation caused by herpes simplex
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːpiːz ˈsɪmˌplɛks ɪnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈhɜːpiːz ˈsɪmplɛks ɛnˌkɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A form of acute encephalitis caused by infection with herpes simplex virus type 1, typically affecting the temporal and frontal lobes of the brain.
