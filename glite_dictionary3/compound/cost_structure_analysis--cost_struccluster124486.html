cost structure analysis

noun
examination of production costs and profitability
Pronunciation: / kɑst ˈstrʌktʃɚ əˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / kɒst ˈstrʌktʃə əˈnæləsɪs / (BrE)
the process of examining all costs involved in producing a product to determine their behavior and potential profit margins

Examples

cost structure analysis for a company
Companies often rely on cost structure analysis to determine the best pricing strategies.
Effective cost structure analysis can reveal hidden expenses and improve overall profitability.
The finance team conducted a cost structure analysis before launching the new product line.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cost analysis: the process of reviewing and evaluating costs in general
  • synonym – profit analysis: the assessment of profitability in business operations
