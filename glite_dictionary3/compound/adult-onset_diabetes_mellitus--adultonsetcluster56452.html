adult-onset diabetes mellitus

noun
type of diabetes developing in adults
Pronunciation: / əˌdʌlt ˌɑnˌsɛt daɪəˌbitiz məˈlaɪtəs / (AmE), / əˌdʌlt ˌɒnˌsɛt daɪəˌbiːtiːz məˈlaɪtəs / (BrE)
A mild form of diabetes mellitus that develops gradually in adults, typically managed with diet and oral medication rather than insulin injections.
