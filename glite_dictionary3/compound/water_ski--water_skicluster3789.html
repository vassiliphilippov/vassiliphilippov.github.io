to water ski

verb
to ride on water skis
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌski / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌskiː / (BrE)
to glide across water on water skis, typically while being towed by a motorboat

Examples

to water ski behind a boat
I've never tried to water ski, but it looks like a lot of fun.
She learned to water ski last summer during their vacation at the lake.
They water skied for hours, enjoying the sunshine and warm water.
