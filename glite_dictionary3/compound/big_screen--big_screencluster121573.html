big screen

noun
large television or display
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ skrin / (AmE), / bɪɡ skriːn / (BrE)
a large display device, especially a television or monitor, used for watching films or video at home or in public spaces.

Examples

big screen TV
We bought a new big screen television for family movie nights this winter.
The hotel lounge had a big screen showing the live football match for guests.
They mounted a big screen monitor on the wall to stream movies during parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym – television: an electronic system for receiving and displaying visual images and sound; often used to watch programs or films at home.
