Japan wax

noun
yellow wax from sumac berries, used in polishes
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæn wæks / (AmE), / dʒəˈpan waks / (BrE)
a yellowish wax obtained from the berries of some sumac trees, used in making polishes, candles, and other products

Japan wax candles
