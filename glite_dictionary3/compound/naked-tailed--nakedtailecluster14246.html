naked-tailed

adjective
having a hairless tail
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkɪd teɪld / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd teɪld / (BrE)
(of an animal) having a tail that is hairless or lacking fur.

Examples

naked-tailed rat
Scientists observed a naked-tailed species of rat living in the dense rainforest.
The naked-tailed armadillo is well adapted to its environment in South America.
Unlike most squirrels, the naked-tailed mole rat has very little fur on its tail.
