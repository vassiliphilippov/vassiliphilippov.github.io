rot-resistant

adjective
not easily affected by rotting
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑt rɪˌzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈrɒt rɪˌzɪstənt / (BrE)
not easily damaged or destroyed by rotting; able to withstand decay caused by fungi or moisture.

Examples

rot-resistant wood
Cedar is a popular choice for outdoor furniture because it is rot-resistant.
Make sure to use rot-resistant materials when building in damp climates.
The deck was constructed with rot-resistant wood to ensure longevity.
