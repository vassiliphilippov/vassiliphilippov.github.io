smooth finish

noun
even, flawless surface
Pronunciation: / smuð ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / smuːð ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
a surface quality that is even, flat, and free of imperfections or roughness.

Examples

smooth finish on wood
His new razor left his face with a smooth finish every morning.
I prefer tiles with a smooth finish in the bathroom because they are easier to clean.
The craftsman polished the table to give it a smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • antonym – rough finish: a surface that is not smooth, with imperfections or texture
