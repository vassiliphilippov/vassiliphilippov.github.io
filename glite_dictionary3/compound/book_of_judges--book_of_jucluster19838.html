Book of Judges

proper noun
Old Testament book about Israel under the judges
Pronunciation: / bʊk əv ˈdʒʌdʒɪz / (AmE), / bʊk əv ˈdʒʌdʒɪz / (BrE)
a book of the Old Testament that recounts the history of the Israelites under the rule of judges before the establishment of monarchy
