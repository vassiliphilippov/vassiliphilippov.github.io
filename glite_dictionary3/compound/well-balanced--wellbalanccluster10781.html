well-balanced

adjective
mentally stable; emotionally healthy
Pronunciation: / wɛl ˈbælənst / (AmE), / wɛl ˈbælənsd / (BrE)
Having a stable and healthy mind, showing no signs of psychological disorder or emotional disturbance.

Examples

a well-balanced person
A well-balanced person is generally able to adapt to life's challenges.
Growing up in a supportive family helped him become well-balanced and resilient.
She is a well-balanced individual who handles stress with remarkable composure.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unstable: prone to psychological disturbance or emotional instability
  • synonym – well-adjusted: psychologically healthy and able to cope with the demands of everyday life
