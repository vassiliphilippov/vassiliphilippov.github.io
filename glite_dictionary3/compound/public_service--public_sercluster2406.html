public service

noun
service provided for the public good
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɝːvɪs /; / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
