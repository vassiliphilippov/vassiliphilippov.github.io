slop basin

noun
bowl for teacup dregs
Pronunciation: / slɑp ˈbeɪs.ən / (AmE), / slɒp ˈbeɪ.sən / (BrE)
a bowl used for emptying the dregs of teacups and coffee cups at the table, especially during tea service

Examples

slop basin for tea dregs
At afternoon tea, each table was equipped with a slop basin for guests to discreetly empty their cups.
She poured the cold tea dregs into the slop basin before requesting a fresh cup.
The antique slop basin completed her collection of Victorian tea set pieces.
