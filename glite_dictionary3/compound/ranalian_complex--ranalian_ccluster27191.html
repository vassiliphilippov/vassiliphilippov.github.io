ranalian complex

noun
primitive group of flowering plants (botany)
Pronunciation: / rəˈneɪliən ˈkɑmplɛks / (AmE), / rəˈneɪliən ˈkɒmplɛks / (BrE)
a group of primitive extant flowering plant families, characterized by well-developed perianths and apocarpous ovaries, formerly classified as a superorder including families such as Magnoliaceae and Ranunculaceae.
