stomach upset

noun
mild digestive disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmək ˌʌpˈsɛt / (AmE), / ˈstʌmək ˌʌpˈsɛt / (BrE)
A mild disorder of the digestive system marked by discomfort, nausea, or heartburn.

Examples

mild stomach upset
After eating the spicy curry, she had a stomach upset for the rest of the evening.
He missed work yesterday because of a mild stomach upset.
Travelers often experience a stomach upset when trying unfamiliar foods abroad.
