personal magnetism

noun
influential personal charm or appeal
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənl mægˈnɛtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl mæɡˈnɛtɪzəm / (BrE)
An attractive or compelling quality in a person that enables them to influence or charm others.
