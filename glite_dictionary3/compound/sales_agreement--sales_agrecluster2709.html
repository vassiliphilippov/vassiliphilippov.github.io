sales agreement

noun
legal contract for sale of property
Pronunciation: / seɪlz əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / seɪlz əˈɡriːmənt / (BrE)
a legal contract in which ownership of property is transferred from a seller to a buyer for an agreed price

Examples

sales agreement for a house
A copy of the sales agreement was sent to the buyer's lawyer for approval.
Both parties signed the sales agreement after negotiating the final price of the car.
The real estate agent reviewed the sales agreement with her clients before proceeding with the transaction.
