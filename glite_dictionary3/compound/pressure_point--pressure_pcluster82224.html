pressure point

noun
sensitive area of the body responsive to pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃər pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
An area on the body that is particularly sensitive to pressure, often used in massage, acupuncture, or martial arts to produce certain effects.

Examples

massage pressure point
During the exam, the doctor pressed a pressure point to check for tenderness.
Knowing where each pressure point is can help in both healing and self-defense techniques.
The therapist applied gentle pressure to a pressure point on my shoulder to relieve tension.
