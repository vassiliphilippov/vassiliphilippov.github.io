descriptive adjective

noun
adjective describing a noun's quality
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskrɪptɪv ˈædʒɪktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɪptɪv ˈadʒɪktɪv / (BrE)
An adjective that describes a quality, attribute, or characteristic of a noun, such as 'nervous' in 'a nervous person'.
