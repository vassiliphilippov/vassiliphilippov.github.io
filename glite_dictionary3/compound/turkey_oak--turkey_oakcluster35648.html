turkey oak

noun
deciduous European oak tree
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːki oʊk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki əʊk / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree of central and southern Europe and Asia Minor, with lance-shaped leaves with spiked lobes

Examples
