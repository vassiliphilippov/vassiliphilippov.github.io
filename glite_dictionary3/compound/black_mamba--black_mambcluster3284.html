black mamba

noun
nickname for Kobe Bryant
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈmæmbə / (AmE), / blæk ˈmambə / (BrE)
an informal, widely used nickname for the late American basketball player Kobe Bryant, referring to his speed, scoring ability, and competitive 'killer' instinct on the court.
