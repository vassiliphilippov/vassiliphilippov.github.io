snow plant

noun
bright red mountain plant found in western North America
Pronunciation: / snoʊ plænt / (AmE), / snəʊ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a fleshy, bright red flowering plant (Sarcodes sanguinea) native to mountain forests of western North America, often appearing while snow is still on the ground
