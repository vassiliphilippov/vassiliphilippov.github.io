corrugated fastener

noun
metal tool for joining wood
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəˌɡeɪtɪd ˈfæstənər / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.əˌɡeɪ.tɪd ˈfɑːs.tən.ə / (BrE)
a small strip of corrugated steel with sharp points on one side, used to join pieces of wood in rough carpentry

Examples

hammer in a corrugated fastener
Several corrugated fasteners were required to secure the pieces before the glue dried.
