half sister

noun
sister sharing one parent only
Pronunciation: / hæf ˈsɪstɚ / (AmE), / hɑːf ˈsɪstə / (BrE)
a sister who has either the same mother or the same father as you, but not both

Examples

half sister from another mother
Although they are half sisters, they are very close and talk every day.
My half sister and I have the same father but different mothers.
She visited her half sister for the holidays every summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sibling: a brother or sister; a person who shares at least one parent with another
  • degree higher – sister: a female sibling with both parents in common
  • antonym – stepsister: a sister by the remarriage of a parent, not related by blood
