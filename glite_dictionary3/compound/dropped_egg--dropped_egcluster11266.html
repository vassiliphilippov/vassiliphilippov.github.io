dropped egg

noun
egg cooked in simmering water (poached)
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑpt ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpt ɛɡ / (BrE)
an egg cooked by gently breaking it into simmering water, without its shell; a poached egg
