low-level

adjective
not intense; small in amount or degree
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ ˌlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈləʊ ˌlɛvəl / (BrE)
not intense; existing, occurring, or produced in small amounts or degrees

Examples
