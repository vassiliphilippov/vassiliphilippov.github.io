propoxyphene hydrochloride

noun
mildly narcotic pain-relief drug
Pronunciation: / proʊˌpɑksiˌfin ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (AmE), / prəˌpɒksɪˌfiːn ˌhaɪdrəˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
