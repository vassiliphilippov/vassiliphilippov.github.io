wheel tree

noun
Australian shade tree with showy red flowers
Pronunciation: / wil triː / (AmE), / wiːl triː / (BrE)
An eastern Australian tree (Stenocarpus sinuatus) widely grown for shade, with glossy leaves and circular clusters of bright red or orange-scarlet flowers.
