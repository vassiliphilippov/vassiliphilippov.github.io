steely-spined

adjective
showing strong determination or courage
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːli spaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈstiːli spaɪnd / (BrE)
(of a person or attitude) having a strong, determined character, showing firmness, courage, or resolve under pressure.

Examples

a steely-spined leader
Despite criticism, the steely-spined director never wavered in her vision for the project.
His steely-spined resolve inspired the whole team to keep going under pressure.
You need to be steely-spined to survive in the world of politics.

  • synonym – resolute: admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering
