mixed bud

noun
bud producing leaves and flowers
Pronunciation: / mɪkst bʌd / (AmE), / mɪkst bʌd / (BrE)
a bud that produces both leaves and flowers when it develops

Examples

mixed bud on a tree
Botanists observed that the mixed bud produced both shoots and blossoms in the spring.
