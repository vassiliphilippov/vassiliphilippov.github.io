food turner

noun
utensil for turning food while cooking
Pronunciation: / fuːd ˈtɜrnər / (AmE), / fuːd ˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil with a flat, flexible blade and a long handle, used for turning or serving food such as pancakes or eggs

Examples

food turner for pancakes
She used a food turner to flip the pancakes without breaking them.
The nonstick food turner was her favorite tool in the kitchen.
You can use a food turner to serve omelettes easily.
