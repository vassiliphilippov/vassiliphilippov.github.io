too expensive

adjective
costing more than can be afforded
Pronunciation: / tu ɪkˈspɛnsɪv / (AmE), / tuː ɪkˈspensɪv / (BrE)
costing more than one can afford; unreasonably high in price for someone's circumstances

Examples

too expensive for me
I wanted to buy the leather jacket, but it was simply too expensive.
Many families find organic groceries too expensive to purchase regularly.
The rent for that apartment is too expensive for most students in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym – affordable: inexpensive enough to be afforded; not expensive
  • synonym – expensive: costing a lot of money
