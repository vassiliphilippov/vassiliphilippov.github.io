springer spaniel

noun
breed of large spaniel dog
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋər ˈspæniəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋə ˈspæniəl / (BrE)
a breed of large spaniel with a wavy, silky coat, typically black or liver and white, known for its energy and ability in hunting and retrieving

Examples

friendly springer spaniel
A springer spaniel requires plenty of exercise and mental stimulation each day.
