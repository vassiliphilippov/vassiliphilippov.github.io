yellow-breasted chat

noun
North American songbird with yellow breast
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˌbrɛstɪd tʃæt / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌbrɛstɪd tʃat / (BrE)
a large North American songbird (Icteria virens) with olive-green upperparts and a bright yellow breast, known for its varied vocalizations and ability to mimic the songs of other birds
