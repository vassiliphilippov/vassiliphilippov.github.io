roly-poly pudding

noun
British dessert of rolled suet pastry with jam or fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊli ˈpoʊli ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌrəʊli ˈpəʊli ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
A traditional British dessert made from suet pastry spread with jam or fruit, rolled up, and baked or steamed.
