news article

noun
piece of writing reporting news
Pronunciation: / nuz ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / njuːz ˈɑːtɪkəl / (BrE)
a piece of writing in a newspaper or magazine that reports news events or information

Examples

read a news article
After the event, several news articles appeared explaining what happened.
She read a news article online about the latest advances in medicine.
The local newspaper published a detailed news article about the upcoming election.

Related Terms

  • antonym – editorial: a newspaper article expressing opinions of the editor or publisher
  • synonym – news report: a report or account of current events in the news media
