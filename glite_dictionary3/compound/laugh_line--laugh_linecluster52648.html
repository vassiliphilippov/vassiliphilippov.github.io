laugh line

noun
wrinkle near outer eye caused by smiling
Pronunciation: / læf laɪn / (AmE), / lɑːf laɪn / (BrE)
a wrinkle that appears at the outer corner of the eyes, typically caused by frequent smiling or laughing

Examples

laugh line around eyes
As she smiled, the laugh lines became more pronounced around her eyes.
Creams that claim to reduce the appearance of laugh lines are popular in the beauty industry.
He considered his laugh lines a sign of a happy life.
