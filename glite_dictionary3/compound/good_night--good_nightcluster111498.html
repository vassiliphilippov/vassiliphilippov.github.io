good night

interjection
expression used to wish a pleasant night
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈnaɪt / (BrE)
Used to express farewell or to wish someone a pleasant night, especially before parting or going to bed.

Examples

wish good night before bed
After the movie ended, everyone hugged and wished each other good night.
He sent a quick text to his friend saying good night before falling asleep.
She smiled and said, "good night!" as she closed the door.
