short-range

adjective
concerned with the near future
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt ˌreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
relating to a short period in the future; concerned with the near future or with things that will happen soon

Examples

short-range plan
Because of market volatility, the board developed both short-range and long-range strategies.
Our team's objectives are mostly short-range, focusing on what we can achieve this fiscal year.
The company announced a short-range plan to improve its customer service over the next six months.
