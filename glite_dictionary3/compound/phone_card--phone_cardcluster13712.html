phone card

noun
prepaid card for telephone calls
Pronunciation: / foʊn kɑrd / (AmE), / fəʊn kɑːd / (BrE)
A prepaid card used to pay for telephone calls, especially from public phones or when traveling.

Examples

phone card for calls
I bought a phone card from the convenience store.
Many people used phone cards before mobile phones became popular.
She used a phone card to call her family while traveling abroad.
