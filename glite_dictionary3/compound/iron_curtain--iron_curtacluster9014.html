iron curtain

noun
Cold War boundary of secrecy and censorship
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərn ˈkɜrtən /; / ˈaɪɚn ˈkɝːtn̩ / (AmE), / ˌaɪən ˈkɜːtn̩ /; / ˈaɪən ˈkɜːtn̩ / (BrE)
a barrier of secrecy and separation, especially the division between Eastern and Western Europe during the Cold War, or any impenetrable barrier between groups or ideologies
