health check

noun
medical examination to assess health
Pronunciation: / hɛlθ tʃɛk / (AmE), / hɛlθ tʃɛk / (BrE)
a thorough medical examination to assess a person's overall health, often including various tests depending on age and sex

Examples

annual health check
Before starting the training, he underwent a full health check at the local clinic.
It is advisable to have a health check every year to catch any potential problems early.
The company offers free health checks to all its employees as part of their wellness program.
