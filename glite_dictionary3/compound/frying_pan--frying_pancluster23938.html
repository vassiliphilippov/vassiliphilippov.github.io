frying pan

noun
shallow pan for frying food
Pronunciation: / ˈfraɪɪŋ ˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈfraɪɪŋ ˌpan / (BrE)
A shallow pan with a long handle, used for frying food.

Examples

frying pan for eggs
Always use a nonstick frying pan for making omelettes.
She heated the oil in the frying pan before adding the vegetables.
The handle of the frying pan became very hot during cooking.
