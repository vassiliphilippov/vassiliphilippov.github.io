golden groundsel

noun
weedy North American herb with golden-yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈɡraʊndsəl / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈɡraʊndsl̩ / (BrE)
a weedy herb native to the eastern United States and Texas, with golden-yellow flowers; sometimes considered invasive and occasionally classified in the genus Senecio
