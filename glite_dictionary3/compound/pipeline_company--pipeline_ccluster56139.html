pipeline company

noun
company providing data-pipeline services
Pronunciation: / c8pa6apccla6an cck8cmp59ni / (AmE), / c8pa6apla6an cck8cmp59ni / (BrE)
A company that designs, builds, hosts, or operates data pipelines and related services for moving, transforming, or integrating data (for example ETL or real-time streaming) rather than physical pipelines for oil or gas.

Examples

pipeline company manages data
The pipeline company specializes in real-time data integration across multiple cloud platforms and databases.
They hired a pipeline company to build ETL jobs and maintain streaming connectors for analytics.
Several startups became pipeline companies, offering managed data flows and transformation tools to enterprises.

Related Terms

  • synonym – data integration company: a company that provides tools or services to move and combine data from different sources
  • synonym – data pipeline: a set of processes that move and transform data from sources to destinations, often in ETL or streaming form
