heap of papers

noun
large, untidy pile of papers
Pronunciation: / hip əv ˈpeɪpɚz / (AmE), / hiːp əv ˈpeɪpəz / (BrE)
a large, untidy pile of papers lying together in a disorganized manner

Examples

messy heap of papers
He sighed as he stared at the heap of papers cluttering his desk.
She found her missing letter buried under a heap of papers.
There was a heap of papers on the floor after the meeting.
