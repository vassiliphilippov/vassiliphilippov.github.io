slant of sunlight

noun
shaft or angle of sunlight
Pronunciation: / slænt əv ˈsʌnˌlaɪt / (AmE), / slɑːnt əv ˈsʌn.laɪt / (BrE)
a shaft or angle of sunlight, especially as it falls or enters a space at an oblique angle

Examples

a slant of sunlight through window
A slant of sunlight lit up the dust motes swirling in the attic.
The living room was brightened by a golden slant of sunlight at dawn.
Through the half-open window, a slant of sunlight fell across the old wooden floor.
