call box

noun
public telephone booth
Pronunciation: / kɔl bɑks / (AmE), / kɔːl bɒks / (BrE)
a public booth equipped with a telephone for making calls, typically found on streets or highways

Examples

roadside call box
Many old call boxes have been removed due to the popularity of mobile phones.

The police officer directed her to use the call box by the main gate.
