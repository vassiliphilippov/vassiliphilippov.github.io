cloud grass

noun
a Spanish grass with feathery flower panicles
Pronunciation: / klaʊd ɡræs / (AmE), / klaʊd ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a Spanish grass (Agrostis nebulosa) with fine, feathery, cloud-like flower panicles, often grown for use in dried bouquets
