village bike

noun
communal or frequently used bicycle for local errands
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪlɪdʒ baɪk / (AmE), / ˈvɪlɪdʒ baɪk / (BrE)
a basic bicycle that is used frequently for short local trips or errands within a village or community

Examples

ride the village bike to shops
He unlocked the village bike and cycled to the bakery early every morning.
Residents often borrowed the village bike for errands across the small town.
The old village bike was well-worn but dependable for those quick trips to the store.

Related Terms

  • synonym – town bike: a bicycle used for casual riding around a town or city
