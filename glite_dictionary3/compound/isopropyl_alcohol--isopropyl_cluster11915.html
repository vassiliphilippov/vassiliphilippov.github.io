isopropyl alcohol

noun
liquid alcohol used as solvent and antiseptic
Pronunciation: / aɪ.səˈproʊ.pɪl ˈæl.kəˌhɑl / (AmE), / aɪ.səˈprəʊ.pɪl ˈæl.kəˌhɒl / (BrE)
a colorless, flammable liquid alcohol used chiefly as a solvent, antiseptic, and in antifreeze; also known as rubbing alcohol
