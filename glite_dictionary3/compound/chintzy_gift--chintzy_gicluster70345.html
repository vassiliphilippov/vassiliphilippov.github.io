chintzy gift

noun
cheap or poorly made gift
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪntsi ɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪntsi ɡɪft / (BrE)
A gift that is cheap or poorly made, often considered insubstantial or of low quality.

Examples

a chintzy gift for birthday
He was disappointed to receive a chintzy gift for his birthday instead of something meaningful.
No one wanted to admit they had brought the chintzy gift to the secret Santa exchange.
The company handed out chintzy gifts at the event that fell apart within days.
