fibre-optic transmission system

noun
communication system using fiber optic cables
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbər ˈɑptɪk trænˈsmɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə ˈɒptɪk trænzˈmɪʃən ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A communication system that transmits data or information using fiber optic cables, often at high speeds over long distances.

Examples

A fibre-optic transmission system is less susceptible to electromagnetic interference than traditional copper cables.

Related Terms

  • antonym – copper-wire transmission system: A communication system using copper wire cables instead of fiber optics.
  • synonym – fiber optics: The technology of transmitting light through thin, flexible fibers for communication.
