double eagle

noun
score of three under par on a hole (golf)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
(in golf) a score of three strokes under par on a single hole, also called an albatross.
