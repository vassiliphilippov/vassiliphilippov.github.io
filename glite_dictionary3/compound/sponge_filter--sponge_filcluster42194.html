sponge filter

noun
aquarium filter using synthetic sponge
Pronunciation: / spʌndʒ ˈfɪltɚ / (AmE), / spʌndʒ ˈfɪltə / (BrE)
A type of aquarium filter made of synthetic sponge, used to clean and aerate water by trapping debris while allowing beneficial bacteria to grow.

Examples

sponge filter for aquariums
I use a sponge filter in my fish tank because it's gentle on small fry.
Many aquarists choose a sponge filter for breeding tanks due to its biological filtration capability.
The sponge filter needs to be rinsed regularly to maintain water quality in the aquarium.
