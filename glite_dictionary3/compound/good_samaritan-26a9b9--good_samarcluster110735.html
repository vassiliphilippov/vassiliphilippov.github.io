good Samaritan

noun
a person who helps others in trouble
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd səˈmærɪtən / (AmE), / ɡʊd səˈmærɪtən / (BrE)
a person who generously helps others in difficulty or distress, often a stranger, without expecting a reward

Examples

a good Samaritan helps strangers
A good Samaritan paid for her groceries when she realized she had forgotten her wallet.
A good Samaritan stopped to assist the injured cyclist after the accident.
You don't have to be a good Samaritan to show a little kindness to strangers.
