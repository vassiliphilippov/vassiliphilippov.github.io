brake failure

noun
failure of a vehicle's brakes
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪk ˌfeɪljər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪk ˌfeɪliə / (BrE)
A situation in which a vehicle's brakes do not function properly and fail to stop the vehicle.

Examples

brake failure on the highway
He lost control because of sudden brake failure during the emergency stop.
Regular maintenance can reduce the risk of brake failure in older vehicles.
The accident was caused by brake failure on the downhill road.
