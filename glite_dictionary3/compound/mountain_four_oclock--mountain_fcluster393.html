mountain four o'clock

noun
leafy wildflower of the US Southwest
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən fɔr əˈklɑk / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn fɔːr əˈklɒk / (BrE)
A leafy wildflower with lavender-pink flowers that open in the evening and remain through the cool part of the next day, found in the mountains of southern Colorado to Arizona and into Mexico.
