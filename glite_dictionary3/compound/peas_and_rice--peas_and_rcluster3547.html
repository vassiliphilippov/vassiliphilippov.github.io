peas and rice

noun
a dish of peas cooked with rice
Pronunciation: / piz ənd raɪs / (AmE), / piːz ənd raɪs / (BrE)
A simple meal or dish consisting mainly of peas cooked with rice, common in various cuisines and often considered basic or unadorned.

Examples

peas and rice dish
After a long day at work, she made peas and rice for a quick dinner.
Although the restaurant offers gourmet dishes, I prefer the simplicity of peas and rice.
He grew up eating peas and rice as a staple meal.
