tongue fern

noun
an East Asian fern with tongue-shaped fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋ fɝn / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋ fɜːn / (BrE)
An East Asian fern with fronds shaped like tongues, sometimes classified in the genus Cyclophorus.
