paint-by-numbers style

noun
rigid, formulaic method or style
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪnt baɪ ˈnʌmbɚz staɪl / (AmE), / ˌpeɪnt baɪ ˈnʌmbəz staɪl / (BrE)
A rigid or formulaic method of doing something, especially in creative work, that allows little room for originality or imagination.

Examples

paint-by-numbers style writing
He wanted to break free from the paint-by-numbers style he had used early in his writing career.
Many pop songs today are written in a paint-by-numbers style to appeal to the widest audience.
The director's latest film was criticized for its paint-by-numbers style and lack of innovation.
