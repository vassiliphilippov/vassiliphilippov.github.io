hitting average

noun
baseball statistic for batter performance
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtɪŋ ˌævərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɪtɪŋ ˌævərɪdʒ / (BrE)
(Baseball) a measure of a batter's performance, calculated by dividing the number of base hits by the number of official times at bat.

Examples

high hitting average
A player's hitting average is often used to evaluate their skills at the plate.
His hitting average improved dramatically over the course of the season.
She set a team record with the highest hitting average last year.
