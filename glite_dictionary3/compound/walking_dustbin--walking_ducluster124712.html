walking dustbin

noun
person who eats messily or excessively
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkɪŋ ˈdʌstˌbɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ ˈdʌstbɪn / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who eats excessively or messily, especially junk food
