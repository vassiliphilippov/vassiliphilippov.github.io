boarding house

noun
private house providing lodging and meals
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪŋ ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A private house where lodgers pay to stay and are provided with meals.

Examples

boarding house with meals
After moving to the city, she found a boarding house that served breakfast and dinner.
The boarding house was known for its warm atmosphere and home-cooked meals.
They converted their old family home into a boarding house for students.
