schmucky attitude

noun
foolish or obnoxious attitude
Pronunciation: / ˈʃmʌki ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈʃmʌki ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
An attitude that is foolish, obnoxious, or contemptible in a way that is likely to irritate others.

Examples

show a schmucky attitude
