babe in arms

noun
very young child or naive person
Pronunciation: / beɪb ɪn ɑrmz / (AmE), / beɪb ɪn ɑːmz / (BrE)
A very young child who is being carried or, figuratively, a person who is very inexperienced or naive.

Examples

a babe in arms crying
During the evacuation, the woman clutched her babe in arms tightly.
Starting her new job, she felt like a babe in arms among seasoned professionals.
Their youngest is just a babe in arms, sleeping peacefully through the noisy party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – infant: a very young child or baby
  • synonym – novice: a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
  • antonym – veteran: a person with much experience in a particular field
