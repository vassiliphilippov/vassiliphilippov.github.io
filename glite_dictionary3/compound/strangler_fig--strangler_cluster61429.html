strangler fig

noun
tropical fig tree with strangling habit
Pronunciation: / ˈstræŋɡlər fɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈstræŋɡlə fɪɡ / (BrE)
a tropical fig tree that starts life on another tree and grows around it, often eventually killing the host tree
