ground squirrel

noun
burrowing rodent; crop pest
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd ˈskwɜrl /; / ˈɡraʊnd ˌskwɪrl / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd ˈskwɪrəl /; / ˈɡraʊnd ˌskwɪəl / (BrE)
a small, burrowing rodent found in North America and Eurasia, often with cheek pouches and stripes, that lives mainly on the ground
