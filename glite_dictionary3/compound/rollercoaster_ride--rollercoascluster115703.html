rollercoaster ride

noun
a period with many ups and downs
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlərˌkoʊstər raɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləˌkəʊstə raɪd / (BrE)
an experience or period marked by many sudden or extreme changes, especially in emotions or fortunes

Examples

emotional rollercoaster ride
Her emotions went on a rollercoaster ride during the final weeks before the exam results came out.
Last year was a real rollercoaster ride for the company, with profits soaring then plummeting several times.
The negotiations proved to be a rollercoaster ride from start to finish.
