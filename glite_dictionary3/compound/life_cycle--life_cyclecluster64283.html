life cycle

noun
the stages of growth and reproduction in an organism
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪkəl /; / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪkl /; / ˈlaɪf ˌsaɪ.kl̩ / (BrE)
the series of stages or changes an organism goes through from the beginning of its life, through development and reproduction, often repeating in new generations
