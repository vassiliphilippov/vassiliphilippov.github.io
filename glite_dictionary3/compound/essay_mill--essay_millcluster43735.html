essay mill

noun
company selling academic papers to students
Pronunciation: / ˈɛseɪ ˌmɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛseɪ ˌmɪl / (BrE)
A business or service that produces academic essays or papers to be submitted by students as their own work.

Examples

use an essay mill for homework
Many universities have strict policies against using an essay mill to complete coursework.
She was caught submitting a paper she bought from an essay mill.
Some students are tempted to turn to an essay mill during exam season.
