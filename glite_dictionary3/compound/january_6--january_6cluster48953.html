January 6

noun
the Jan 6, 2021 U.S. Capitol attack
Pronunciation: / \u02ccd\u0292\u00e6nju\u02c8\u025bri s\u026aks / (AmE), / \u02ccd\u0292an.ju\u02c8\u025b\u02d0.ri s\u026aks / (BrE)
(in political and media contexts) a label for the attack on the U.S. Capitol on January 6, 2021; used to refer to that event and its consequences.

Examples

January 6 Capitol attack
News reports compared the security failures during January 6 to other recent acts of political violence.
Survivors testified about the chaos they experienced inside the building on January 6 during congressional proceedings.
Many politicians still refer to January 6 when discussing threats to democratic institutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Capitol riot: the violent breach of the U.S. Capitol building on January 6, 2021
  • synonym – insurrection: an act or instance of rising in revolt against civil authority (often used in discussion of the January 6 event)
