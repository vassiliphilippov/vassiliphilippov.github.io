trade name

noun
name a product or service is marketed under
Pronunciation: / treɪd neɪm / (AmE), / treɪd neɪm / (BrE)
the name used by a company or manufacturer for a product or service, often for marketing purposes

Examples

trade name registration
Choosing a catchy trade name can help a new business attract customers.
Many products are sold under both a generic and a trade name.
The company protected its trade name through a trademark registration.

Related Terms

  • synonym – brand name: the name given to a product by the company that makes it
  • antonym – generic name: the standard or nonproprietary name for a product, especially pharmaceuticals
