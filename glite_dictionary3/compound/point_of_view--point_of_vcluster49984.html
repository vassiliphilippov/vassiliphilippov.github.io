point of view

noun
position from which something is viewed
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt əv vju / (AmE), / pɔɪnt əv vjuː / (BrE)
the position in space from which something is seen or observed

Examples

point of view of the statue
If you move to the left, you'll get a different point of view of the statue.
The director chose a high point of view to film the action scene.
The painting was created from a unique point of view near the water's edge.
