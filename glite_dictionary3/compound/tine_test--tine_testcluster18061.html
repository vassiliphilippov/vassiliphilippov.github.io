tine test

noun
diagnostic skin test for tuberculosis
Pronunciation: / taɪn tɛst / (AmE), / taɪn tɛst / (BrE)
a diagnostic test for tuberculosis in which a disk with several tines coated with tuberculin is used to puncture the skin; a reaction indicates past or present exposure to the tubercle bacillus

Examples

tine test for tuberculosis
A positive tine test result usually requires follow-up with more specific tests.
Because the tine test is less sensitive than the Mantoux test, it is used less frequently today.
The doctor decided to use the tine test as a quick screening tool for tuberculosis.
