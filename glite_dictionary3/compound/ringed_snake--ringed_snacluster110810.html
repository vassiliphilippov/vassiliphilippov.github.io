ringed snake

noun
European snake with yellow collar
Pronunciation: / rɪŋd sneɪk / (AmE), / rɪŋd sneɪk / (BrE)
a harmless European snake, typically grey with a bright yellow or white collar around its neck; common in England

Examples

a ringed snake in grass
