potty dance

noun
dance indicating need to use toilet
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑti dæns / (AmE), / ˈpɒti dɑːns / (BrE)
A playful or frantic movement, usually displayed by children, that indicates an urgent need to use the toilet.

Examples

do the potty dance
He was so focused on his potty dance that he almost missed the teacher's question.
The little girl began doing the potty dance as soon as she realized she needed to go.
Whenever their son does the potty dance, his parents know it's time for a bathroom break.
