jacket crown

noun
type of dental artificial crown
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækɪt kraʊn / (AmE), / ˈdʒakɪt kraʊn / (BrE)
a dental appliance consisting of an artificial crown, usually made of porcelain, fitted over a broken or decayed tooth

Examples

porcelain jacket crown
A jacket crown can improve both the function and appearance of a broken tooth.
Her front tooth was repaired with a porcelain jacket crown after the accident.
The dentist recommended a jacket crown to restore the damaged tooth.
