false vampire

noun
large insect-eating bat, not a true vampire bat
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈvæmpaɪər / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈvæmpaɪə / (BrE)
a large carnivorous bat found in the Old or New World, once mistakenly believed to suck blood but actually feeding on insects
