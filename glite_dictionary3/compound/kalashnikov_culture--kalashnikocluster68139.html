Kalashnikov culture

noun
social use of firearms to resolve disputes
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæʃ.nɪ.kɑv ˈkʌl.tʃər / (AmE), / kəˈlæʃ.nɪ.kɒf ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
a social environment or set of attitudes in which political disputes are resolved through the use of firearms, especially automatic rifles like the Kalashnikov

Examples

Kalashnikov culture dominates politics
Attempts to reduce Kalashnikov culture have faced strong resistance from armed militias.
