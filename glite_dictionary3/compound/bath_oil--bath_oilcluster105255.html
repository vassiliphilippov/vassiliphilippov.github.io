bath oil

noun
scented oil for use in bath water
Pronunciation: / bæθ ɔɪl / (AmE), / bɑːθ ɔɪl / (BrE)
a perfumed or scented oil designed to be added to bath water to moisturize and soften the skin

Examples

bath oil for soft skin
She poured a few drops of bath oil into the steaming water to soften her skin.
Some people use bath oil to hydrate and soothe dry skin after a long day.
The fragrance of lavender bath oil filled the bathroom as she relaxed.
