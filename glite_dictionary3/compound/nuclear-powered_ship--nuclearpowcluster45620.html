nuclear-powered ship

noun
ship propelled by nuclear reactor energy
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːkliɚ ˈpaʊɚd ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈnjuːkliə ˈpaʊəd ʃɪp / (BrE)
a ship that is propelled by energy produced from a nuclear reactor rather than conventional fuel

Examples

nuclear-powered ship travels far
A nuclear-powered ship can remain at sea much longer than conventional vessels.
