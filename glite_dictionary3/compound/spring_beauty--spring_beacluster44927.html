spring beauty

noun
North American flowering plant with edible corm
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
A small North American plant with a single pair of succulent leaves midway up the stem, a loose cluster of white, pink, or rose bowl-shaped flowers, and an edible corm.
