flight indicator

noun
gyroscope-based instrument for simulating horizon in flight
Pronunciation: / flaɪt ˈɪndɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / flaɪt ˈɪndɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
an aircraft instrument that provides the pilot with an artificial horizon to indicate attitude relative to the Earth's surface, based on a gyroscope

Examples

flight indicator shows attitude
Modern flight indicators are often digital rather than mechanical.
The flight indicator helped the pilot stay oriented during heavy clouds.
When the flight indicator malfunctioned, the co-pilot referenced backup instruments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – artificial horizon: instrument providing pilots with a visual horizon reference
  • synonym – attitude indicator: instrument showing aircraft orientation relative to the horizon
