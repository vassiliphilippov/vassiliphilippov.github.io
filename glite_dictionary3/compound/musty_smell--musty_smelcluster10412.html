musty smell

noun
stale, damp, or moldy odor
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsti smɛl / (AmE), / ˈmʌsti smɛl / (BrE)
an unpleasant odor caused by dampness, mold, or staleness, often found in closed or old spaces

Examples

old room with musty smell
After the rain, a musty smell lingered in the empty cabin for days.
She tried to air out the room to get rid of the musty smell.
The old wardrobe gave off a musty smell as soon as it was opened.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fresh smell: a clean, pleasant, and newly-scented odor
  • synonym – stale smell: an unpleasant, old or musty odor
