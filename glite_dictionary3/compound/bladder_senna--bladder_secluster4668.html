bladder senna

noun
European shrub with yellow flowers and inflated pods
Pronunciation: / ˈblædər ˈsɛnə / (AmE), / ˈblædə ˈsɛnə / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered European shrub, cultivated for its bright yellow flowers and large, inflated pods, and also used as a source of food for wildlife.
