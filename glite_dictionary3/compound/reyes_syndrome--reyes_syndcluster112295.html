Reye's syndrome

noun
rare childhood disease causing brain and liver problems
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪz ˌsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈraɪz ˌsɪn.drəʊm / (BrE)
a rare but serious condition affecting children, characterized by sudden brain swelling and liver dysfunction, usually following a viral infection
