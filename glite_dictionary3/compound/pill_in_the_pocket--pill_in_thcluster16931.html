pill in the pocket

noun
readily accessible medication for urgent use
Pronunciation: / pɪl ɪn ðə ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / pɪl ɪn ðə ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a method of keeping a medication, such as for heart rhythm problems, readily available to take immediately when symptoms occur, rather than on a fixed schedule
