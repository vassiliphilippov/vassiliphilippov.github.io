silver cord

noun
mother–child emotional bond
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ kɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə kɔːd / (BrE)
a strong emotional bond between a mother and her child

Examples

the silver cord of love
After her son left for college, she felt the silver cord stretch but never break.
Literature often portrays the silver cord as a symbol of maternal love and connection.
Some psychologists describe the silver cord as the invisible link connecting a mother and her child.
