black birch

noun
birch tree with reddish-brown bark
Pronunciation: / blæk bɝtʃ / (AmE), / blæk bɜːtʃ /; / blak bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
a birch tree native to eastern North America, with dark bark that produces aromatic oil and hard wood used for furniture
