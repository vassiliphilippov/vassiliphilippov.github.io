twilight zone

noun
surreal or confusing situation
Pronunciation: / ˈtwaɪˌlaɪt zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈtwaɪˌlaɪt zəʊn / (BrE)
a situation or place that is strange, mysterious, or difficult to understand, often one that feels surreal or confusing

Examples

strange twilight zone
After the announcement, the office entered a twilight zone of rumors and uncertainty.
The city at dawn felt like a twilight zone, empty and strangely silent.
When the rules changed without warning, we found ourselves in a twilight zone of confusion.
