to break dance

verb
perform break dance (street dance)
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌdæns / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌdɑːns / (BrE)
to perform an energetic style of street dance characterized by acrobatic and rhythmic movements, often to hip-hop music

Examples

to break dance on stage
A group of teenagers break danced during the halftime show.
Many people stopped to watch him break dancing on the sidewalk.
She learned to break dance by watching videos online.
