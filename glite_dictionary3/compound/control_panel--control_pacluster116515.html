control panel

noun
electrical device with switches and meters for control
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊl ˌpænəl / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊl ˌpænəl / (BrE)
An electrical device consisting of a flat surface that holds switches, dials, and meters used to control and monitor other electrical equipment.

Examples

main control panel switches
Operators monitored the temperature readings displayed on the control panel throughout the process.
She pressed a button on the control panel to start the machine.
The technician examined the control panel to find the cause of the malfunction.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dashboard: control panel in a vehicle, displaying instruments and gauges
  • synonym – instrument panel: panel displaying instruments and controls, especially in vehicles
