Barbados cherry

noun
tropical fruit rich in vitamin C
Pronunciation: / bɑɹˈbeɪdoʊs ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / bɑːˈbeɪdəʊs ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
an acid red or yellow fruit, similar in appearance to a cherry, from a tropical American shrub, noted for its high vitamin C content

Examples

fresh Barbados cherry fruit
Due to its high vitamin C content, the Barbados cherry is considered very nutritious.
The Barbados cherry is often used to make juices and jams because of its tangy flavor.
We planted a Barbados cherry shrub in our backyard to enjoy the fresh fruit.
