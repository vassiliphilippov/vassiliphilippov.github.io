forward pass

noun
ML computation propagating input through a model
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərd pæs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwəd pɑːs / (BrE)
(in machine learning) a computation in which input data are propagated through a model’s layers to produce outputs or predictions.

Examples

run a forward pass
During evaluation the forward pass computes output probabilities from the model's input.
The training loop performs a forward pass before computing the loss and gradients.
On complex networks, a single forward pass can take several seconds to complete.

Related Terms

  • antonym – backward pass: the computation, during training, that propagates gradients backward through a model to update parameters; the complementary step to a forward pass.
  • synonym – forward propagation: the process of passing input forward through a model to compute outputs; often used interchangeably with forward pass.
