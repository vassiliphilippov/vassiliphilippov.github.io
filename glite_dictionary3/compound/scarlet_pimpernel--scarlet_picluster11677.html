scarlet pimpernel

noun
herb with small scarlet, white, or purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑr.lət ˈpɪm.pərˌnɛl / (AmE), / ˈskɑː.lɪt ˈpɪm.pə.nɛl / (BrE)
a small annual herbaceous plant with scarlet, white, or purple flowers that typically close at the approach of rainy weather, native to Europe and widely naturalized elsewhere
