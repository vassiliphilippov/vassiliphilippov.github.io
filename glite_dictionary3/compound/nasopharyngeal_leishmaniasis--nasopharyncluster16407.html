nasopharyngeal leishmaniasis

noun
tropical disease affecting nose and throat membranes
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪsoʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl ˌliːʃməniˈaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌneɪsəʊfəˈrɪndʒɪəl ˌliːʃmənaɪˈəsɪs / (BrE)
a form of leishmaniasis in which sores are limited to the skin and mucous membranes of the nose and throat, typically found in Mexico and Central and South America
