eucerine anhydrous

noun
water-free ointment base for skin treatments
Pronunciation: / juːˈsɛrɪn ˌænˈhaɪdrəs / (AmE), / juːˈsɛrɪn ˌanˈhʌɪdrəs / (BrE)
