marble bones disease

noun
rare inherited disease with very dense bones
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrbəl boʊnz dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːbəl bəʊnz dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
an inherited disorder in which bones become unusually dense and hard, sometimes leading to the loss of bone marrow cavities
