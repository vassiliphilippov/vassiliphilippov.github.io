Capitol Hill

proper noun
site of U.S. Capitol and Congress
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpɪtəl ˈhɪl / (AmE), / ˌkapɪtəl ˈhɪl / (BrE)
The area in Washington, D.C., where the United States Capitol is located and where Congress meets; often used to refer to the U.S. Congress or its activities.
