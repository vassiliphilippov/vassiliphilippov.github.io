pad of paper

noun
sheets of paper bound together
Pronunciation: / pæd əv ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / pæd əv ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
a set of sheets of paper joined together along one edge, used for writing or drawing

Examples

pad of paper for notes
