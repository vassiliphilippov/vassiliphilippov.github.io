circular saw

noun
power saw with a rotating circular blade
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkjələr sɔ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə sɔː / (BrE)
a power-driven saw with a circular steel blade having teeth around its edge, used for cutting wood, metal, or other materials
