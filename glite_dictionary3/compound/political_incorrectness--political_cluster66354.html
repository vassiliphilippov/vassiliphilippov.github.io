political incorrectness

noun
use of language/actions likely to offend marginalized groups
Pronunciation: / pəˌlɪd.ɪ.kəl ˌɪn.kəˈrɛkt.nəs / (AmE), / pəˌlɪt.ɪ.kəl ˌɪn.kəˈrɛkt.nəs / (BrE)
the use of words or actions that are likely to offend or marginalize people from disadvantaged or discriminated groups, by not conforming to prevailing social norms of language and behavior

Examples

public political incorrectness
Her political incorrectness on social media led to significant public backlash.
Some defend political incorrectness as a challenge to censorship, while others see it as harmful.
The comedian was criticized for his political incorrectness during the show.

Related Terms

  • antonym – political correctness: the avoidance of forms of expression or action that are seen to exclude or marginalize disadvantaged social groups
