canebrake rattler

noun
variety of venomous rattlesnake (U.S. South)
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnˌbreɪk ˈræt.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪnˌbreɪk ˈræt.lə / (BrE)
A large venomous rattlesnake (Crotalus horridus atricaudatus) found in the southeastern United States, also known as the timber rattlesnake in that region.

a canebrake rattler snake
Farmers in the southern U.S. sometimes encounter canebrake rattlers in old cane fields.

While hiking in Georgia, we spotted a canebrake rattler sunning itself near the trail.
