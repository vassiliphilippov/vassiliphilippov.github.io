Read method of childbirth

noun
method of natural childbirth using relaxation and education
Pronunciation: / riːd ˈmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldˌbɜrθ / (AmE), / riːd ˈmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldˌbɜːθ / (BrE)
a method of natural childbirth that considers labor a normal process, emphasizing education, relaxation, and breathing techniques to reduce pain, which is seen mainly as psychological.
