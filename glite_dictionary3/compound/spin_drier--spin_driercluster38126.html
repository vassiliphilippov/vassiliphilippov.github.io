spin drier

noun
appliance that spins clothes dry
Pronunciation: / spɪn ˈdraɪər / (AmE), / spɪn ˈdraɪə / (BrE)
An electric appliance that removes water from clothes by spinning them rapidly.

Examples

spin drier for wet clothes
A spin drier can greatly reduce drying time compared to air-drying clothes.

He bought a compact spin drier for his small apartment.
