cobalt bloom

noun
hydrated cobalt arsenate mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌbɔlt bluːm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.bɒlt bluːm / (BrE)
a reddish mineral, hydrated cobalt arsenate, found in monoclinic crystals and used to color glass, usually found in cobalt- and arsenic-bearing veins
