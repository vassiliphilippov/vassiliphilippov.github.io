painted tongue

noun
Chilean herb with velvety, colorful flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntɪd tʌŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntɪd tʌŋ / (BrE)
A Chilean herb (Salpiglossis sinuata) with velvety, funnel-shaped yellowish or violet flowers, noted for long tongue-like styles at the corolla throat.
