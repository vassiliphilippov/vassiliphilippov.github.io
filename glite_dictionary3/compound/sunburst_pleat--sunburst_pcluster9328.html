sunburst pleat

noun
a pleat creating a flared, radiating effect
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌbɝst pliːt / (AmE), / ˈsʌnbɜːst pliːt / (BrE)
A knife pleat cut on the bias of fabric to create a flared, radiating effect, often used in skirts or dresses.
