vena palatina

noun
palate-draining vein
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə pæl.əˈtaɪ.nə / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə pæl.əˈtiː.nə / (BrE)
A vein that drains the region of the palate and empties into the facial vein.

Examples

vena palatina drains palate
During the dissection, the surgeon carefully identified the vena palatina.
Inflammation in the palate can sometimes affect the flow in the vena palatina.
The vena palatina plays a crucial role in draining blood from the palate area.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – facial vein: a vein that receives blood from the face, into which the vena palatina empties
