Achilles tendon

noun
tendon connecting calf to heel
Pronunciation: / əˈkɪliz ˈtɛndən / (AmE), / əˈkɪliːz ˈtɛndən / (BrE)
The strong tendon at the back of the ankle, connecting the calf muscles to the heel bone.

Examples

ruptured Achilles tendon
After months of rehabilitation, her Achilles tendon finally healed.
He tore his Achilles tendon during the race and required surgery.
The Achilles tendon is crucial for activities like walking, running, and jumping.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tendon: a flexible, strong band of tissue that connects muscle to bone
