tropic bird

noun
tropical seabird with long tail feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑpɪk bɝd / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪk bɜːd / (BrE)
a mostly white, web-footed tropical seabird, typically found far from land, known for its long tail feathers
