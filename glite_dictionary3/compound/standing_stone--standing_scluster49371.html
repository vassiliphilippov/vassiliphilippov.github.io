standing stone

noun
tall upright megalithic stone
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɪŋ stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ stəʊn / (BrE)
a tall upright stone, usually of prehistoric origin, erected alone or as part of a group, found especially in England and northern France

Examples

ancient standing stone
Archaeologists discovered a standing stone in the middle of the windswept field.
Many tourists visit Cornwall to see its famous standing stones.
