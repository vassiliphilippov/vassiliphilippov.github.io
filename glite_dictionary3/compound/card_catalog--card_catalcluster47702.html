card catalog

noun
library index with cards for items
Pronunciation: / kɑrd ˈkæt̬.əl.ɔg /; / kɑrd ˈkætəlˌɔg / (AmE), / kɑːd ˈkæt.əl.ɒg /; / kɑːd ˈkatəlɒg / (BrE)
a set of cards, usually in drawers, used in libraries to list and organize books or other items alphabetically
