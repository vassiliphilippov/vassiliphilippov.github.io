Mediterranean Sea

proper noun
large inland sea between Europe, Africa, and Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪtəˌreɪniən ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən ˈsiː / (BrE)
the large sea almost completely enclosed by Europe, Africa, and Asia, connecting to the Atlantic Ocean through the Strait of Gibraltar.

Examples

the Mediterranean Sea coast
Shipping routes through the Mediterranean Sea have been vital since ancient times.
