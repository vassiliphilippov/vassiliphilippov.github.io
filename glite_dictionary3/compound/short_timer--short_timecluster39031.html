short timer

noun
person finishing a commitment
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt ˈtaɪmɚ / (AmE), / ʃɔːt ˈtaɪmə / (BrE)
a person who is nearing the end of their period of employment, service, or commitment

Examples

a short timer at work
As a short timer, he didn't take on any new projects at work.
Because she was a short timer, her colleagues didn't expect her to attend the meetings.
Many short timers begin making plans for their next move before leaving.
