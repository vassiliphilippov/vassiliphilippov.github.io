selenious acid

noun
an acid derived from selenium
Pronunciation: / səˈliːniəs ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / sɪˈliːnɪəs ˈasɪd / (BrE)
a white or colorless, water-soluble solid acid (H₂SeO₃) formed by dissolving selenium dioxide in water, used in chemical analysis and synthesis.

Examples

selenious acid solution preparation
Selenious acid is used as an oxidizing agent in organic chemistry and in glass manufacturing.
Exposure to selenious acid should be handled with care due to its toxicity.
The laboratory technician prepared a solution of selenious acid for the analysis.
