plexus choroideus

noun
vascular tissue in brain ventricles
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛksəs kəˈrɔɪdiəs / (AmE), / ˈplɛksəs kəˈrɔɪdɪəs / (BrE)
A network of blood vessels in the cerebral ventricles that produces cerebrospinal fluid and regulates intraventricular pressure.
