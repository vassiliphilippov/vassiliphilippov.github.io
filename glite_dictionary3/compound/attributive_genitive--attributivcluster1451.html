attributive genitive

noun
genitive case used attributively
Pronunciation: / əˈtrɪbjətɪv ˈdʒɛnətɪv /; / əˈtrɪbjətɪv ˈdʒɛnɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈtrɪbjʊtɪv ˈdʒɛnɪtɪv / (BrE)
