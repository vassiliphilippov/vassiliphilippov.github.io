processed cheese

noun
cheese product made by blending cheeses
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑsɛst tʃiz / (AmE), / ˈprɒsɛst tʃiːz / (BrE)
cheese made by blending several types of cheese with emulsifiers and other ingredients to produce a uniform, shelf-stable product.
