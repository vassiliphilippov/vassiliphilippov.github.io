movie camera

noun
camera for recording moving images
Pronunciation: / ˈmuvi ˌkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi ˌkæmərə / (BrE)
a camera designed for taking a sequence of photographs on film or digitally, creating the illusion of motion when played back rapidly
