job interview

noun
formal meeting for employment selection
Pronunciation: / dʒɑb ˈɪntɚˌvju / (AmE), / dʒɒb ˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
a formal meeting in which an applicant is questioned to determine their suitability for a job

Examples

a job interview with manager
