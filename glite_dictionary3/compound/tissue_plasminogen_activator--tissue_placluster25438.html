tissue plasminogen activator

noun
protein/drug that breaks down blood clots
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪʃu ˌplæzməˌnoʊdʒən ˈæktəˌveɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtɪs.juː ˌplæz.mɪ.nəʊ.dʒən ˈæk.tɪ.veɪ.tə / (BrE)
