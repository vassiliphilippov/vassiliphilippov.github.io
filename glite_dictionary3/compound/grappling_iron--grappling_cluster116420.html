grappling iron

noun
tool with hooks for grasping
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræp.lɪŋ ˌaɪ.ɚn / (AmE), / ˈɡræp.lɪŋ ˌaɪ.ən / (BrE)
A tool with several hooks used for grasping or holding objects, often thrown with a rope.

Examples

grappling iron with rope
A grappling iron can help anchor boats to rocky shores.
He tossed the grappling iron onto the ledge and climbed up the rope.
The rescuers used a grappling iron to haul the debris out of the river.
