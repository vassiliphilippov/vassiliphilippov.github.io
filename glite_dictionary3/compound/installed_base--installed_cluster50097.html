installed base

noun
existing customers or units in operation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɔld beɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɔːld beɪs / (BrE)
the number of existing customers or units of a product currently in use by end users

Examples

large installed base
Expanding the installed base is crucial for future sales.
Software updates are often provided to the entire installed base of devices.
The company's installed base grew significantly last year.
