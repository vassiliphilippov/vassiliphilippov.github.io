salt plain

noun
flat land covered by salt deposits
Pronunciation: / sɔlt pleɪn / (AmE), / sɒlt pleɪn / (BrE)
A large, flat area of land covered with salt, left behind after the evaporation of a body of salt water.

Examples

vast salt plain
Explorers had to cross a treacherous salt plain before reaching the oasis.
Many migratory birds stop at the salt plain during their long journey south.
The vast salt plain stretched to the horizon, glittering under the midday sun.
