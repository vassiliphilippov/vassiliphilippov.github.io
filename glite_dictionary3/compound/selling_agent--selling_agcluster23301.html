selling agent

noun
person who sells for others on commission
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛlɪŋ ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person or company authorized to sell goods or property on behalf of another, usually for a commission
