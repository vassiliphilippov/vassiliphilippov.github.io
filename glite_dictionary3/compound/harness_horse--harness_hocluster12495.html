harness horse

noun
horse used to pull a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrnɪs hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːnɪs hɔːs / (BrE)
A horse trained and used for pulling a vehicle, such as a carriage or cart, especially in harness racing or for transport.

Examples

During the parade, a black harness horse pulled an old-fashioned carriage through the streets.
He owns several harness horses that he trains for competitions.
The harness horse trotted steadily down the country lane, pulling a small buggy.
