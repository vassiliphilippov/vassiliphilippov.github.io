breaker point

noun
contact in ignition distributor
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkər pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkə pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a movable contact in a vehicle's distributor, which opens and closes as the cam rotates, controlling the flow of current to the spark plugs

Examples

breaker point gap adjustment
If the breaker point gap is incorrect, the engine may misfire or run poorly.
Modern cars no longer use a breaker point system in their ignitions.
The mechanic checked the breaker point for signs of wear during the inspection.

Related Terms

  • synonym – contact breaker: a device that interrupts the flow of electrical current, especially in an ignition system
