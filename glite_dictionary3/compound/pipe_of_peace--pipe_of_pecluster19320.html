pipe of peace

noun
ceremonial Native American peace pipe
Pronunciation: / paɪp əv pis / (AmE), / paɪp əv piːs / (BrE)
a ceremonial, highly decorated pipe used by some Native American peoples and smoked on special occasions, especially as a symbol of peace or agreement
