McArdle's disease

noun
inherited muscle glycogen storage disease
Pronunciation: / məˈkɑrdlz dɪˌziz / (AmE), / məˈkɑːdlz dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
a rare inherited disorder characterized by abnormal accumulation of glycogen in skeletal muscle, leading to muscle weakness and cramping
