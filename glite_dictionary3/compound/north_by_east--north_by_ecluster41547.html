north by east

noun
compass point east of north
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ baɪ ist / (AmE), / nɔːθ baɪ iːst / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point east (clockwise) of due north, equal to 11°15′ east of due north.

Examples

north by east direction
Set your course to north by east to reach the island.
The ship turned slightly to north by east to avoid the rocks.
The wind was blowing steadily from north by east all morning.

  • degree lower – north: the compass direction corresponding to 0°
