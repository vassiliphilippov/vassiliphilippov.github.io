trump card

noun
a card in the declared trump suit
Pronunciation: / trʌmp kɑrd / (AmE), / trʌmp kɑːd / (BrE)
a playing card that belongs to the suit chosen as trumps in a particular game, which outranks the other suits

Examples

trump card in bridge
He played his trump card and won the round easily.
Holding the king as a trump card gave her a major advantage.
You must follow suit unless you have a trump card in your hand.
