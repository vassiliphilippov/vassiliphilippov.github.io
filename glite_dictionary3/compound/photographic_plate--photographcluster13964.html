photographic plate

noun
sheet for recording photographic images
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəˈɡræfɪk pleɪt / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəˈɡræfɪk pleɪt / (BrE)
A flat sheet of metal or glass coated with a light-sensitive material, used to record photographic images.

Examples

photographic plate for images
Astronomers once relied on the photographic plate to document images of distant stars.
She carefully removed the exposed photographic plate from the camera in the darkroom.
The old camera used a photographic plate instead of modern film.
