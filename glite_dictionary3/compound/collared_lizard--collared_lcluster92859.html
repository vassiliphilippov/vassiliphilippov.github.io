collared lizard

noun
a lizard with a collar-like neck marking
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlərd ˈlɪɡzɚd / (AmE), / ˈkɒləd ˈlɪɡzəd / (BrE)
a robust long-tailed lizard of central and western United States and northern Mexico, characterized by two dark bands around its neck resembling a collar
