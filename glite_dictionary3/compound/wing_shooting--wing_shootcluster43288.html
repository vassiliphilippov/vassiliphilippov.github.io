wing shooting

noun
shooting flying game birds
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ ˈʃuː.tɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪŋ ˈʃuː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of shooting game birds while they are flying.

Examples

wing shooting of birds
Many hunters travel to rural areas in the autumn for wing shooting of pheasants and grouse.
Proper safety measures are essential whenever you take part in wing shooting with others.
Wing shooting requires quick reflexes and precise aim as the birds fly overhead.
