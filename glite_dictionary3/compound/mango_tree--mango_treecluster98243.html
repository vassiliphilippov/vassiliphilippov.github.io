mango tree

noun
evergreen tree producing mango fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡoʊ tri / (AmE), / ˈmaŋɡəʊ triː / (BrE)
A large evergreen tropical tree (Mangifera indica) cultivated for its sweet, juicy, oval fruit called mango.
