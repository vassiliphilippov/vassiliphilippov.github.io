bounty hunter

noun
person who hunts for a reward
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnti ˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnti ˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person who pursues and captures or kills criminals, fugitives, or sometimes animals in order to get a reward
