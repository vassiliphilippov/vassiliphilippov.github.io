accidental injury

noun
unintentional physical harm
Pronunciation: / ˌæksɪˈdɛntəl ˈɪndʒəri / (AmE), / ˌæksɪˈdentəl ˈɪndʒəri / (BrE)
physical harm or damage to the body that occurs unexpectedly and is not caused intentionally

Examples

accidental injury at work
Children are taught safety rules to reduce the risk of accidental injury while playing outside.
He suffered a serious accidental injury while cooking dinner last night.
The company offers insurance to cover any accidental injury that might happen at work.

Related Terms
