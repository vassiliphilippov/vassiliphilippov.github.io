indigenous language

noun
a native language of a place
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪdʒɪnəs ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A language that is native to a particular place and has not been brought there from elsewhere.

Examples

speak an indigenous language
He grew up speaking an indigenous language in his home community.
Many indigenous languages are at risk of disappearing as younger generations shift to global languages.
The government is working to preserve the region's indigenous language through educational programs.
