solicitor general

noun
senior legal officer assisting the attorney general
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪsət̬ɚ ˈdʒɛnɚəl / (AmE), / səˈlɪsɪtə ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
a law officer appointed to assist the attorney general and often represents the government in court cases, especially appellate courts
