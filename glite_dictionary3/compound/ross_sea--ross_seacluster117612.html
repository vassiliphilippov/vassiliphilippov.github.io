Ross Sea

proper noun
sea in Antarctica
Pronunciation: / rɑs si / (AmE), / rɒs siː / (BrE)
an arm of the southern Pacific Ocean located along the coast of Antarctica, between Victoria Land and Marie Byrd Land

Examples

the Ross Sea in Antarctica
In 2016, the Ross Sea was designated as a marine protected area.
Scientists are studying the impact of climate change on the Ross Sea ecosystem.
The Ross Sea is home to a diverse range of Antarctic wildlife.
