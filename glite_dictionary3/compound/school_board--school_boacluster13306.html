school board

noun
governing body for local schools
Pronunciation: / skuːl bɔrd / (AmE), / skuːl bɔːd / (BrE)
a group of people elected or appointed to oversee the management and policies of local public schools

Examples

school board meeting
Each district elects a new school board every four years.
Parents voiced their concerns during the school board meeting last night.
The school board voted to increase funding for special education programs.
