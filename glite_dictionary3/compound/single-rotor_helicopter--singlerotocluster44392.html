single-rotor helicopter

noun
helicopter with one main rotor
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl ˈroʊtər ˈhɛlɪˌkɑptər / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˈrəʊtə ˈhɛlɪˌkɒptə / (BrE)
A helicopter with one main rotating blade assembly used for lift and control, typically with a tail rotor for stability.
