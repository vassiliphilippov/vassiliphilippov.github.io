sphenisciform seabird

noun
flightless seabird order, penguin
Pronunciation: / sfɪˈnɪsɪfɔrm ˈsiːbɝd / (AmE), / sfɪˈnɪsɪfɔːm ˈsiːbɜːd / (BrE)
A flightless seabird of the order Sphenisciformes, commonly known as a penguin, found mainly in cold southern oceans.
