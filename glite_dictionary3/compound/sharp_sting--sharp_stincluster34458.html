sharp sting

noun
sudden, intense pain like a prick
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrp stɪŋ / (AmE), / ʃɑːp stɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden, intense, and usually brief pain that feels as if caused by something pointed or pricking

Examples

a sharp sting of pain
A sharp sting of pain shot through his finger after the splinter pierced his skin.

There was a sharp sting when I touched the nettles in the garden.
