tragic ending

noun
very sad or disastrous ending
Pronunciation: / ˈtrædʒɪk ˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrædʒɪk ˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
An ending to a story, event, or situation that involves great sadness or disaster.

Examples

a tragic ending to a story
After all their struggles, the novel concluded with a tragic ending that nobody expected.
Many Greek myths are remembered for their tragic ending and moral lessons.
The film's tragic ending left the audience in tears.
