sebaceous cyst

noun
benign skin cyst filled with sebum
Pronunciation: / sɪˈbeɪʃəs sɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈbeɪʃəs sɪst / (BrE)
a common, benign cyst under the skin, filled with fatty material produced by a blocked sebaceous gland

Examples

sebaceous cyst under skin
A sebaceous cyst can sometimes become inflamed if the gland remains blocked.
She developed a small sebaceous cyst behind her ear that later needed treatment.
The doctor diagnosed the lump as a sebaceous cyst and recommended simple removal.

Related Terms
