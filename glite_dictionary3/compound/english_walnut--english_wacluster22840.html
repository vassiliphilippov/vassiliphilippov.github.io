English walnut

noun
nut from the Eurasian walnut tree, used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈwɔlnət / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈwɔːlnʌt / (BrE)
A nut produced by the Eurasian walnut tree, with a wrinkled two-lobed seed and a hard but relatively thin shell, widely used in cooking and baking.
