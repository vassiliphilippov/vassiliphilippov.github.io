pepper spray

noun
aerosol self-defense spray
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpɚ spreɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə spreɪ / (BrE)
A nonlethal aerosol spray containing a derivative of chili peppers, used to temporarily blind and incapacitate attackers or deter animals.

Examples

carry pepper spray for safety
Hikers in bear country are advised to bring pepper spray as a deterrent.
Many people carry pepper spray for personal safety when walking alone at night.
The police officer used pepper spray to subdue the aggressive man.
