pretty face

noun
an attractive person (often superficial)
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪti feɪs / (AmE), / ˈprɪti feɪs / (BrE)
An attractive person, especially one who is thought to have little else to offer beyond their looks.

Examples

Despite being known as a pretty face, she has an impressive academic record.
He was dismissed as a pretty face, but he proved his critics wrong by leading the project.
She is far more than just a pretty face in the corporate world.
