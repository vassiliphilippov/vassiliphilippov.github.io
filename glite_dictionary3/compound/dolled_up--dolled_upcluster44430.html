dolled up

adjective
dressed in fancy or formal clothing
Pronunciation: / dɑld ʌp / (AmE), / dɒld ʌp / (BrE)
Wearing fancy or formal clothing and often makeup, typically for a special occasion.

Examples

dolled up for a party
She got all dolled up for the company holiday party last night.
They were dolled up in their finest clothes for the wedding.
You don't need to get dolled up just to go to the supermarket.
