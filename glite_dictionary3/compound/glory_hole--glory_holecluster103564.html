glory hole

noun
nautical: small ship storage locker
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔri ˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri həʊl / (BrE)
a small storage locker at the stern of a boat or between decks of a ship, used for stowing equipment or supplies
