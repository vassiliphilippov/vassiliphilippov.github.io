satin bird

noun
Australian bird with striking male plumage
Pronunciation: / ˈsætən bɝd / (AmE), / ˈsætɪn bɜːd / (BrE)
A species of bird native to southeast Australia, the male of which is glossy violet-blue and the female light grey-green.
