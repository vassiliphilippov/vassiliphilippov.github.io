hawk nose

noun
downward-curved nose
Pronunciation: / hɑk noʊz / (AmE), / hɔːk nəʊz / (BrE)
A nose that is curved downward, resembling the beak of a hawk.

Examples

a hawk nose shape
Her hawk nose gave her face a striking, distinctive look.
The villain in the novel had a prominent hawk nose and piercing eyes.
With his hawk nose and sharp jawline, he was easily recognized in the crowd.
