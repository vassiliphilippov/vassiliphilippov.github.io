tall oat grass

noun
a Eurasian grass resembling oat, used for forage
Pronunciation: / tɔl oʊt ɡræs / (AmE), / tɔːl əʊt ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a coarse perennial Eurasian grass resembling oat, often found on roadsides, rough grassland, and hay meadows, and introduced in North America for forage
