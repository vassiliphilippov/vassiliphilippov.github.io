baking mold

noun
container for shaping baked goods
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪkɪŋ moʊld / (AmE), / ˈbeɪkɪŋ məʊld / (BrE)
A container, often with a distinctive shape, used to form and support baked goods such as cakes, breads, or pastries during baking.

Examples

baking mold for cake
After greasing the baking mold, he pressed the dough in for an even crust.
She poured the batter into the baking mold to create a perfectly shaped cake.
The bakery uses special baking molds to make their bread look unique.
