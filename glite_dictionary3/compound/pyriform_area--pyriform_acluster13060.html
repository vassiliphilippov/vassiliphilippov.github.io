pyriform area

noun
pear-shaped brain region in the rhinencephalon
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.rɪˌfɔrm ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈpɪ.rɪ.fɔːm ˈɛəriə / (BrE)
a pear-shaped region of the brain, located on either side in the rhinencephalon and involved in olfactory processing

Examples

the pyriform area of the brain
Damage to the pyriform area can affect the sense of smell.
Odors are processed in the pyriform area of the brain.
Researchers identified increased activity in the right pyriform area during olfactory tasks.
