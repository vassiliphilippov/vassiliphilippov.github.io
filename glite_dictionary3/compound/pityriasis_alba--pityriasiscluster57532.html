pityriasis alba

noun
skin condition with pale patches
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪt.iˈraɪ.əsɪs ˈælbə / (AmE), / ˌpɪt.ɪˈraɪ.əsɪs ˈal.bə / (BrE)
A common skin condition, usually in children or young adults, characterized by round patches of lighter, depigmented skin.
