library paste

noun
water-based adhesive for paper
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪˌbrɛri peɪst / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.brə.ri peɪst / (BrE)
a thick adhesive made from water and flour or starch, used especially for sticking paper and paperboard

Examples

library paste for paper crafts
She used library paste to mount the photograph in her scrapbook.
The librarian repaired the torn page with library paste and a steady hand.
Traditional library paste is often favored for its simplicity and archival quality.
