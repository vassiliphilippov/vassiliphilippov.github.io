garden balm

noun
lemon-scented perennial herb (lemon balm)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən bɑm / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˈbɑːm / (BrE)
a bushy perennial herb of the mint family, with small white or yellowish flowers and lemon-scented leaves, often grown in gardens and sometimes naturalized outside cultivation; also known as lemon balm
