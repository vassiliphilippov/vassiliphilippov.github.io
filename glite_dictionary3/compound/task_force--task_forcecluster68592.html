task force

noun
temporary military unit for a specific mission
Pronunciation: / tæsk fɔrs /; / tæsk fɔɹs / (AmE), / tɑːsk fɔːs / (BrE)
a group of people, often from different organizations or departments, formed temporarily to accomplish a specific task or solve a particular problem (can be military or non-military)
