slime mushroom

noun
a type of mushroom, genus Amanita
Pronunciation: / slaɪm ˈmʌʃˌruːm / (AmE), / slaɪm ˈmʌʃ.rʊm / (BrE)
A common name for a mushroom of the genus Amanita, some species of which are known for their slimy caps.

Examples

poisonous slime mushroom
Some species of slime mushroom are highly poisonous and should not be eaten.
The forest floor was dotted with slime mushrooms after the rain.
Under the shady trees grew a particularly large slime mushroom with a shiny, sticky cap.
