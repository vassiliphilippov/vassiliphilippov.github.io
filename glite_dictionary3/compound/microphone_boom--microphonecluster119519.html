microphone boom

noun
pole holding overhead microphone on set
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkrəˌfoʊn buːm / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəfəʊn buːm / (BrE)
a pole used to hold a microphone above a film or television set so it remains out of the camera's view
