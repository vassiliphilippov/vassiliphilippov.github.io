trash barrel

noun
large rubbish container
Pronunciation: / træʃ ˈbæɹəl / (AmE), / træʃ ˈbærəl / (BrE)
A large, cylindrical container for holding rubbish until it is collected.

Examples

public trash barrel
During the festival, volunteers emptied each trash barrel every few hours to avoid overflow.
He tossed his empty soda can into the trash barrel on the sidewalk.
The park was kept clean because every picnic table had a trash barrel nearby.
