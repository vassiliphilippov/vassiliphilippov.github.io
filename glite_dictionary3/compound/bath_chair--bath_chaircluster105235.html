bath chair

noun
historical wheelchair, usually pushed by attendant
Pronunciation: / bæθ tʃɛr / (AmE), / bɑːθ tʃɛː / (BrE)
a type of wheelchair, often with three or four wheels and a canopy, formerly used for transporting invalids, especially at spas or seaside resorts, and usually pushed by an attendant
