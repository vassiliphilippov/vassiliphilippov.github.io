trimmer arch

noun
arch supporting a hearth between floor trimmers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪmər ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪmə ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
an arch constructed between trimmers in a floor to support the weight of a hearth or fireplace opening

Examples

trimmer arch under fireplace
A mason explained how the trimmer arch distributes the fireplace's weight across the joists.
