blotch of ink

noun
an irregular spot of ink
Pronunciation: / blɑtʃ əv ɪŋk / (AmE), / blɒtʃ əv ɪŋk / (BrE)
a visible patch or irregular spot made by spilled or smudged ink

Examples

blotch of ink on paper
He tried to cover the blotch of ink on his shirt with his jacket.
She feared her essay would be unreadable after spilling a blotch of ink across the bottom.
The letter was ruined by a blotch of ink right in the middle of the page.
