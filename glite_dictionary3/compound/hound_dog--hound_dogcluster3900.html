hound dog

noun
a breed of hunting dog with drooping ears
Pronunciation: / haʊnd dɔg / (AmE), / haʊnd dɒg / (BrE)
A dog of any of several breeds used for hunting, typically characterized by large drooping ears and a keen sense of smell or sight.

Examples

a hound dog tracks animals
Many hunters value the hound dog for its extraordinary tracking ability.
The hound dog let out a long, mournful howl at dusk.
Their family owns a hound dog that loves to follow scents in the woods.
