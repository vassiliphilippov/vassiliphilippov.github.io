sauce verte

noun
herbed mayonnaise-based sauce
Pronunciation: / soʊs vɛrt / (AmE), / səʊs vɛət / (BrE)
A cold, creamy sauce made with mayonnaise, tarragon or dill, and chopped watercress and spinach or cucumber.

Examples

sauce verte with vegetables
Chilled vegetables with sauce verte make a perfect summer appetizer.

The chef recommended pairing the poached chicken with sauce verte for a fresh, herbal flavor.
