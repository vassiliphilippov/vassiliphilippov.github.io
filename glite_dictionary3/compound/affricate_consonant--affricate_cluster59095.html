affricate consonant

noun
speech sound combining stop and fricative
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.rɪˌkeɪt ˈkɑn.sə.nənt / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kət ˈkɒn.sə.nənt / (BrE)
a speech sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative, both sounds being articulated in the same place, such as the 'ch' in 'chair' or 'j' in 'joy'
