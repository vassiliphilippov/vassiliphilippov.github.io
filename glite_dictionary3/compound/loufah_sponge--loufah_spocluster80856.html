loufah sponge

noun
dried luffa fruit used as a sponge
Pronunciation: / ˈluːfə ˌspʌndʒ / (AmE), / ˈluːfə ˌspʌndʒ / (BrE)
the dried, fibrous skeleton of the fruit of a luffa plant, used as a natural sponge or strainer, especially for washing or scrubbing
