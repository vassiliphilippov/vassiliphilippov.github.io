glibertarian approach

noun
superficial libertarian philosophy
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlɪbərˈtɛriən əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌɡlɪbəˈtɛəriən əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A satirical or dismissive term for a political philosophy that superficially emphasizes liberty, often without serious consideration of the complexities involved.
