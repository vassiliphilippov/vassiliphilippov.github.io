serial murderer

noun
person who commits multiple separate murders
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəl ˈmɜrdərər / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl ˈmɜːdərə / (BrE)
a person who kills more than three people, usually one at a time, over a relatively short period

Examples

notorious serial murderer
A notorious serial murderer terrorized the city for months before being caught.
Documentaries often explore the psychology of a serial murderer to understand their motives.
The police suspected they were dealing with a serial murderer after finding similarities between the crimes.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – mass murderer: a person who kills several people at one time or in a short time frame
  • synonym – serial killer: a person who murders several people over a period, often with a similar method
  • degree higher – spree killer: a person who kills multiple people in a short period, often in different locations
