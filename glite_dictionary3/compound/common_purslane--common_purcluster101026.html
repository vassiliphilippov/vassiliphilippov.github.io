common purslane

noun
edible mat-forming herb with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.ən ˈpɜr.sleɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ən ˈpɜː.sleɪn / (BrE)
A low-growing, mat-forming herb with succulent leaves and small yellow flowers, often cultivated or found as a weed; its mildly sour leaves are edible and used in salads and cooked dishes, especially in Indian, Greek, and Middle Eastern cuisines.

Examples

common purslane salad leaves
