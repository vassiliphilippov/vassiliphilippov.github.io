gill arch

noun
bony/cartilaginous arch supporting fish gills
Pronunciation: / ɡɪl ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ɡɪl ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
one of a series of bony or cartilaginous arches on each side of the pharynx that support the gills in fishes and some aquatic amphibians
