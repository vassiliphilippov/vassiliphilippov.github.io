leaf bug

noun
small plant-feeding insect (Miridae)
Pronunciation: / liːf bʌɡ / (AmE), / liːf bʌɡ / (BrE)
a small, often brightly colored insect that feeds on plant juices, especially those belonging to the family Miridae
