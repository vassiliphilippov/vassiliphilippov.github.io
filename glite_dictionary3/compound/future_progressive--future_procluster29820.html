future progressive

noun
ongoing future verb tense
Pronunciation: / ˈfjutʃər prəˈɡrɛsɪv / (AmE), / ˈfjʊtʃə prəˈɡresɪv / (BrE)
A verb tense used to describe an action that will be ongoing at a particular time in the future.

Examples

future progressive tense
She will be studying abroad this time next year, using the future progressive tense.
The sentence 'They will be traveling all summer' uses the future progressive to indicate continuous activity.
When you arrive, I will be waiting for you at the station, expressed in the future progressive.
