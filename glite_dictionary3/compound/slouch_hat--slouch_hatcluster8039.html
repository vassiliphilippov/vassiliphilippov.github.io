slouch hat

noun
wide-brimmed soft felt hat
Pronunciation: / slaʊtʃ hæt / (AmE), / slaʊtʃ hæt / (BrE)
a soft felt hat with a wide, flexible brim that is often turned up on one side, traditionally worn as part of military or outdoor attire

Examples

wear a slouch hat
A slouch hat offers excellent protection from the sun during long hikes.
He wore a battered slouch hat during his travels across the outback.
The soldiers marched in uniform, each with a slouch hat angled to one side.
