benefit of clergy

noun
religious or legal sanction, esp. marriage
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnɪfɪt əv ˈklɜrdʒi / (AmE), / ˈbɛnɪfɪt əv ˈklɜːdʒi / (BrE)
formal or legal sanction by a religious ceremony, especially marriage; originally, an exemption from ordinary legal jurisdiction granted to clergymen.
