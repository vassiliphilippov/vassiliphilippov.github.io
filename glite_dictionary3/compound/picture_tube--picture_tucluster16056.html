picture tube

noun
cathode-ray tube in a television
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚ tuːb / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə tjuːb / (BrE)
a cathode-ray tube in a television that displays images by converting electrical signals into visible pictures on a screen
