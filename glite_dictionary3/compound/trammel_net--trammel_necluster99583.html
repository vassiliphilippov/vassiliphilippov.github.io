trammel net

noun
three-layered fishing net
Pronunciation: / ˈtræməl nɛt / (AmE), / ˈtræməl nɛt / (BrE)
a fishing net made of three layers, with two outer coarse mesh layers and a loose fine mesh inner layer, used to trap fish

Examples

trammel net catches fish
Marine biologists study the impact of trammel nets on local fish populations.
The fishermen lowered the trammel net into the water at dusk to catch mullet.
Using a trammel net, they were able to trap fish of various sizes efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gill net: a fishing net that catches fish by their gills
