lead sheet

noun
music sheet with melody, lyrics, chords
Pronunciation: / lid ʃit / (AmE), / liːd ʃiːt / (BrE)
A sheet of music showing only the melody, lyrics, and chord symbols for a song.

Examples

lead sheet for piano
If you can read a lead sheet, you can easily accompany most pop songs on the piano.
She wrote a lead sheet to share her new composition with other musicians.
The band played the song directly from the lead sheet during rehearsal.
