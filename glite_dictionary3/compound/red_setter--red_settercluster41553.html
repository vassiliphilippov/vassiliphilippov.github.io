red setter

noun
a breed of Irish dog with a red coat
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈsɛtər / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsɛtə / (BrE)
a breed of dog from Ireland with a chestnut-brown or mahogany-red coat, also known as the Irish Setter
