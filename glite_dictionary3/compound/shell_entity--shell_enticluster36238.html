shell entity

noun
inactive company with no assets or operations
Pronunciation: / ʃɛl ˈɛntəti / (AmE), / ʃɛl ˈɛntɪti / (BrE)
a company that exists only on paper, having no assets or active business operations, often used for financial maneuvers or to hold assets

Examples

offshore shell entity
Investors should be cautious when dealing with a shell entity as it might indicate potential fraud.
