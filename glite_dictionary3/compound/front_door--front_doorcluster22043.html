front door

noun
main entrance door of a building
Pronunciation: / frʌnt dɔɚ / (AmE), / frʌnt dɔː / (BrE)
The main door at the front entrance of a building, usually used as the primary way in or out.

Examples

open the front door
A package was left outside the front door this morning.
Please use the front door when you arrive for your appointment.
She left her keys in the front door when she came home last night.
