dead letter

noun
undeliverable mail
Pronunciation: / dɛd ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / dɛd ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a piece of mail that cannot be delivered to the recipient or returned to the sender

Examples

lost dead letter
After months of searching, her lost package was declared a dead letter.
Every year, thousands of dead letters accumulate due to incorrect addresses.
The post office has a special department for handling dead letters.
