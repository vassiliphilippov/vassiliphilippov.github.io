classic hemochromatosis

noun
inherited iron overload disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsɪk ˌhiːmoʊˌkroʊməˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˈklasɪk ˌhiːməʊˌkrəʊməˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
an inherited disorder causing excessive absorption and accumulation of iron in the body, often leading to organ damage
