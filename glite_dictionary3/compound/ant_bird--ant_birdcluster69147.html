ant bird

noun
South American bird that feeds on ants
Pronunciation: / ˈænt ˌbɝːd / (AmE), / ˈænt ˌbɜːd / (BrE)
any of several dull-colored South American birds that feed primarily on ants, often following swarms of army ants to catch prey disturbed by their movement.

Examples

ant bird follows ants
Many species of ant bird shadow army ant swarms to catch prey fleeing the ants.
The ant bird is often seen darting around the forest floor in search of insects.
We spotted a rare ant bird during our hike in the South American rainforest.
