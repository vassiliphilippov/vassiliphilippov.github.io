hat trick

noun
three scores by one player in a game
Pronunciation: / ˈhæt trɪk / (AmE), / ˈhæt trɪk / (BrE)
An act of achieving three successes of the same kind, especially three scores by one player in a single game or match, as in sports like cricket, hockey, or football.

Examples

score a hat trick
Achieving a hat trick is considered a significant milestone in football.
He scored a hat trick in last night's hockey game, thrilling the entire crowd.
She celebrated her third hat trick of the season with her teammates.
