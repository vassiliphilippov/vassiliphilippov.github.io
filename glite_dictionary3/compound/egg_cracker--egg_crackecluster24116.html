egg cracker

noun
kitchen tool for opening eggs
Pronunciation: / ɛɡ ˈkrækɚ / (AmE), / ɛɡ ˈkrækə / (BrE)
a kitchen tool or appliance used to break eggs and often to separate egg whites from yolks

Examples

egg cracker kitchen tool
Most people still prefer to crack eggs by hand rather than use an egg cracker.
She bought an egg cracker to help make breakfast quicker and less messy.
The egg cracker separates the yolk cleanly from the egg white every time.
