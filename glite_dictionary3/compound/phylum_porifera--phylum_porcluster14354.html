phylum Porifera

noun
biological phylum of sponges
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪləm pəˈrɪfərə / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪləm pɒˈrɪfərə / (BrE)
A major group (phylum) of simple aquatic animals, commonly called sponges, characterized by porous bodies and a lack of true tissues.
