Hudson Bay

proper noun
large inland sea in northern Canada
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌdsən beɪ / (AmE), / ˈhʌdsən beɪ / (BrE)
a large inland sea in northern Canada, connected to the Atlantic Ocean, and bordered by Ontario, Quebec, Manitoba, and Nunavut.

Examples

Hudson Bay freezes over
During winter, Hudson Bay is mostly covered with ice.
Many rivers, including the Nelson River, flow into Hudson Bay.
The coastline of Hudson Bay is famous for its polar bear population.
