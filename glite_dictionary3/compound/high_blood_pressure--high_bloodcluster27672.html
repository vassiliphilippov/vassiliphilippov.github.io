high blood pressure

noun
abnormally high blood pressure; hypertension
Pronunciation: / haɪ blʌd ˈprɛʃər / (AmE), / haɪ blʌd ˈprɛʃə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the force of the blood against artery walls is consistently higher than normal
