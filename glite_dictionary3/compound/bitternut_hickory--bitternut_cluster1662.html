bitternut hickory

noun
a North American hickory tree with bitter nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtərˌnʌt ˈhɪkəri / (AmE), / ˈbɪtənʌt ˈhɪkəri / (BrE)
a species of hickory tree found in eastern North America, recognized by its leaves with 7 or 9 leaflets and thin-shelled, very bitter nuts
