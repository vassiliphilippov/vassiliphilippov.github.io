three-banded armadillo

noun
South American armadillo with three bands
Pronunciation: / θri ˈbændɪd ˌɑːrməˈdɪloʊ / (AmE), / θriː ˈbændɪd ˌɑːmədɪləʊ / (BrE)
A small South American armadillo species distinguished by three movable bands of bony plates across its back.
