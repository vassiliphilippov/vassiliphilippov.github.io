mustard gas

noun
toxic chemical warfare agent
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstərd ˌɡæs / (AmE), / ˈmʌstəd ˌɡas / (BrE)
a toxic chemical warfare agent that causes severe blistering of the skin, eyes, and lungs, with no known antidote

Examples
