alarm system

noun
device for signaling danger or intrusion
Pronunciation: / əˈlɑrm ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / əˈlɑːm ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a device or set of devices that automatically signals the presence of danger, such as a fire or unauthorized entry

Examples

alarm system for security
Every home should have an alarm system in case of fire or burglary.
The museum installed a new alarm system to prevent thefts.
When the alarm system was triggered, the police responded within minutes.
