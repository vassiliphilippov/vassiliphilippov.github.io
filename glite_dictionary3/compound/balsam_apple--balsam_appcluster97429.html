balsam apple

noun
tropical vine with warty red or orange fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈbælsəm ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlsəm ˌæpəl / (BrE)
a tropical Old World climbing plant (Momordica balsamina) of the gourd family, with yellow flowers and red or orange warty fruit, sometimes cultivated for ornament or medicinal use.
