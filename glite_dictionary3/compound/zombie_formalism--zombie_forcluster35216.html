zombie formalism

noun
contemporary art style seen as superficial
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑːmbi ˈfɔːrməˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈzɒmbi ˈfɔːməlɪzəm / (BrE)
A movement in contemporary art characterized by superficial aesthetics and often criticized for lacking originality and depth.
