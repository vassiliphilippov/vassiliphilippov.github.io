Sam Browne belt

noun
leather belt with shoulder strap (uniform)
Pronunciation: / sæm braʊn bɛlt / (AmE), / sam braʊn belt / (BrE)
A leather belt supported by a strap passing over the right shoulder, used chiefly as part of a military or police uniform to support a sword or other equipment.
