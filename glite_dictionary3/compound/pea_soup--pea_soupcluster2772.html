pea soup

noun
thick soup made from dried peas
Pronunciation: / piː suːp / (AmE), / piː suːp / (BrE)
A thick soup made from dried peas, often pureed and sometimes flavored with ham or other vegetables.

Examples

homemade pea soup
My grandmother's recipe for pea soup has been passed down for generations.
On cold winter nights, nothing warms you up like a bowl of pea soup.
She added ham and carrots to the pea soup for extra flavor.
