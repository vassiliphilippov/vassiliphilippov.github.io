bubblegum bubble

noun
bubble made by blowing chewing gum
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌb.əlˌɡʌm ˈbʌb.əl / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əlˌɡʌm ˈbʌb.əl / (BrE)
A bubble formed by blowing air into a piece of chewing gum while it is in the mouth.

Examples

to blow a bubblegum bubble
A bubblegum bubble popped and stuck to my face during gym class.
She showed off by blowing a huge bubblegum bubble in front of her friends.
The contest was to see who could blow the largest bubblegum bubble.
