meadow goldenrod

noun
tall North American goldenrod with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊ ˈɡoʊldənˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˈɡəʊldənˌrɒd / (BrE)
