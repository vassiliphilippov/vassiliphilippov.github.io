low-rent housing

noun
affordable housing with low rent
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊˈrɛnt ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌləʊˈrɛnt ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
housing with relatively low rent, intended to be affordable for people with limited income

Examples

low-rent housing for families
Many families rely on low-rent housing to find a safe place to live within their budget.
The city council has increased the budget for low-rent housing projects to address the shortage of affordable homes.
The construction of new low-rent housing has helped reduce homelessness in urban areas.

Related Terms
