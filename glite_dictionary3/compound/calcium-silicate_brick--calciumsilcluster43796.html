calcium-silicate brick

noun
brick made from sand and lime
Pronunciation: / ˌkæl.si.əm ˈsɪl.ɪ.keɪt brɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæl.si.əm ˈsɪl.ɪ.keɪt brɪk / (BrE)
A type of brick made from a mixture of sand and slaked lime, rather than clay, usually light gray or off-white in color, used in construction.
