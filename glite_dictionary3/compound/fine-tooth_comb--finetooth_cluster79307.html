fine-tooth comb

noun
method of close, detailed examination
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪn tuθ koʊm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪn tuːθ kəʊm / (BrE)
A method or tool used to examine something very closely and thoroughly, often to find small details or errors.

Examples

go over with a fine-tooth comb

She went through his emails with a fine-tooth comb, searching for inconsistencies.
The report was checked with a fine-tooth comb to avoid any mistakes before submission.
