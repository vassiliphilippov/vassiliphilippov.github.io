Indian cherry

noun
North American shrub with black fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪən ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to eastern and central United States, noted for its golden-yellow foliage in autumn and black berrylike fruit.
