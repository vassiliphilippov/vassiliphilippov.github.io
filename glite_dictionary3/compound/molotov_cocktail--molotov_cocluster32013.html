Molotov cocktail

noun
homemade incendiary bomb
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːləˌtɔv ˈkɑːkteɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɒlətɒf ˈkɒkteɪl / (BrE)
a crude incendiary bomb made from a bottle filled with flammable liquid and fitted with a rag used as a wick, intended to be ignited and thrown

Examples

throw a Molotov cocktail
He was arrested for possessing a Molotov cocktail with the intent to use it unlawfully.
Protesters hurled a Molotov cocktail at the police barricade during the demonstration.
The soldiers discovered several homemade Molotov cocktails hidden in the abandoned building.
