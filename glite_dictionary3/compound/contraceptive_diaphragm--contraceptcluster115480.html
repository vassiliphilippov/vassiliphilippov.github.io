contraceptive diaphragm

noun
dome-shaped contraceptive device
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrəˈsɛptɪv ˈdaɪəˌfræm / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrəˈsɛptɪv ˈdaɪəfræm / (BrE)
a flexible, dome-shaped cup made of rubber or plastic, used as a contraceptive by covering the cervix to block sperm entry

Examples

insert contraceptive diaphragm
A contraceptive diaphragm should always be used with spermicide for maximum effectiveness.

The doctor explained how to properly insert and remove a contraceptive diaphragm.
