prix fixe

noun
fixed-price multi-course meal
Pronunciation: / pri ˈfiks / (AmE), / pri ˈfiːks / (BrE)
a meal offered in a restaurant at a fixed total price, usually with several courses and limited selection for each course

Examples

prix fixe menu
Many French bistros feature a prix fixe menu for special occasions.
The restaurant offers a prix fixe dinner, which includes an appetizer, entrée, and dessert.
We enjoyed a delicious prix fixe lunch during Restaurant Week downtown.
