acceptor RNA

noun
type of RNA involved in protein synthesis
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptɚ ɑrˌɛnˈeɪ / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptə ɑːˌɛnˈeɪ / (BrE)
a type of RNA molecule (tRNA) that attaches the appropriate amino acid to a growing protein chain during synthesis, according to genetic code instructions from mRNA
