knit stitch

noun
basic knitting stitch technique
Pronunciation: / nɪt stɪtʃ / (AmE), / nɪt stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a basic stitch in knitting made by inserting the needle through the front of the loop from the left-hand side

Examples
