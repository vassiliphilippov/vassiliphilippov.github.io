foreign direct investment

noun
investment in a foreign business or assets
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrən dɪˈrɛkt ɪnˈvɛstmənt /; / ˈfɔrən dəˈrɛkt ɪnˈvɛstmənt / (AmE), / ˈfɒrən dɪˈrɛkt ɪnˈvɛstmənt / (BrE)
an investment made by a company or individual in one country into business interests in another country, usually by owning or controlling a foreign company
