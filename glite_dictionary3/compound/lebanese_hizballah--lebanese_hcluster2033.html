Lebanese Hizballah

proper noun
Shiite Muslim political and militant group in Lebanon
Pronunciation: / lɛbəˌniz hɪz.bəˈlɑ / (AmE), / lɛbəˌniːz hɪz.bəˈlɑː / (BrE)
