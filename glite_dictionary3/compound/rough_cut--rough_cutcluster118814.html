rough cut

noun
initial edited version of a film
Pronunciation: / rʌf kʌt / (AmE), / rʌf kʌt / (BrE)
the first edited version of a film, consisting of preliminary sequences assembled before the final editing

Examples

rough cut of a movie
After editing the rough cut, the team discussed which scenes to trim or reshoot.
The director screened the rough cut for the producers before making further changes.
They were impressed by the rough cut, even though some scenes were unfinished.
