heart rate

noun
number of heartbeats per minute
Pronunciation: / hɑrt reɪt / (AmE), / hɑːt reɪt / (BrE)
the number of times the heart beats in a minute, often used as an indicator of health or fitness

Examples

check heart rate
During exercise, your heart rate increases to pump more blood.
She wore a smartwatch to monitor her heart rate throughout the day.
The doctor checked his heart rate after the stress test.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pulse: the rhythmic throbbing of arteries as blood is propelled through them, often felt at the wrist or neck
