mosquito hawk

noun
a predatory insect, usually a dragonfly
Pronunciation: / məˈskitoʊ hɔk / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ hɔːk / (BrE)
A large, slender-bodied insect with iridescent wings, known for preying on mosquitoes and similar insects; commonly refers to a dragonfly.

Examples

mosquito hawk flying over water
Gardeners welcome mosquito hawks because they help control the mosquito population.
I watched a mosquito hawk catch its prey in midair.
The pond was full of mosquito hawks darting above the water at dusk.

Related Terms
