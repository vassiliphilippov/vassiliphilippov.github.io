ground level

noun
height or surface of the ground
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊnd ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ɡraʊnd ˈlɛvəl / (BrE)
the height or surface of the ground on which something stands; the level of the ground outside a building or structure.
