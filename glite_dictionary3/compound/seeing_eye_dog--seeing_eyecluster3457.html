Seeing Eye dog

noun
guide dog for the blind (trademark)
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːɪŋ aɪ dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈsiːɪŋ aɪ dɒɡ / (BrE)
A dog trained to guide a blind or visually impaired person, originally a trademark term.

Examples

a Seeing Eye dog guides people
After losing her sight, she received a Seeing Eye dog that quickly became her constant companion.
Airlines allow passengers with a Seeing Eye dog to bring them into the cabin.
The man relied on his Seeing Eye dog to navigate the busy streets safely.
