hospital train

noun
train for transporting the sick or wounded
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːspɪtl treɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtl treɪn / (BrE)
a train equipped and used for transporting sick or wounded people, particularly soldiers, to medical facilities

Examples

hospital train transports soldiers
During the war, a hospital train arrived daily to carry wounded soldiers to safety.
Photographs show a hospital train stationed near the front lines, ready for casualties.
The Red Cross operated a hospital train equipped with beds and medical staff.
