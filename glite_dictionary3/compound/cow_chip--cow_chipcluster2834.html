cow chip

noun
piece of dried cow dung
Pronunciation: / kaʊ tʃɪp / (AmE), / kaʊ tʃɪp / (BrE)
a piece of dried dung from a cow, often found in fields and used historically as fuel or in rural games

Examples

dried cow chip
A single cow chip lay sun-drying in the middle of the dusty trail.
Farmers used to collect cow chips to burn as fuel during the winter months.
Tourists laughed as they watched the annual cow chip throwing contest in the small town.
