junction barrier

noun
device for connecting cables without plugs
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋkʃən ˌbæriɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋkʃən ˌbæriə / (BrE)
a device that connects two cables or wires directly, without the use of plugs

Examples
