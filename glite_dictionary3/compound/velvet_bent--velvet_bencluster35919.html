velvet bent

noun
a slender, narrow-leaved grass species
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlvət bɛnt / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt bɛnt / (BrE)
a common grass (Agrostis canina) with slender stems and narrow leaves, often used for lawns and golf courses
