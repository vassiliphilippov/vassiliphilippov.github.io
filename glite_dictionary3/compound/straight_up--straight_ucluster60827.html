straight up

adjective
served chilled without ice (bar term)
Pronunciation: / streɪt ʌp / (AmE), / streɪt ʌp / (BrE)
(of an alcoholic drink) shaken or stirred with ice but served without ice.

Examples

straight up martini
He always orders his whiskey straight up after a long day.
The bartender prepared her gin martini straight up, just as she requested.
Would you like your cocktail on the rocks or straight up?
