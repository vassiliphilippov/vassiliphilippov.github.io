innominate bone

noun
pelvic bone formed of ilium, ischium, and pubis
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɑmənət boʊn / (AmE), / ɪˈnɒmɪnət bəʊn / (BrE)
the large, flaring bone that forms one half of the pelvis, composed of the ilium, ischium, and pubis
