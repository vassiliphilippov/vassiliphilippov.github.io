electronic bike

noun
bicycle with an electric motor
Pronunciation: / əˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk baɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk baɪk / (BrE)
A bicycle equipped with an electric motor that can assist propulsion or operate independently from pedaling.

Examples

ride an electronic bike
Many commuters choose an electronic bike for an eco-friendly and efficient way to get to work.
She upgraded her old bicycle to an electronic bike to make tackling hills much easier.
Some cities have bike-sharing programs that include electronic bikes for longer or more challenging routes.
