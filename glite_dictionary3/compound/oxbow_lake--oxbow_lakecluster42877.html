oxbow lake

noun
crescent-shaped lake formed by cut-off river meander
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌboʊ leɪk / (AmE), / ˈɒks.bəʊ leɪk / (BrE)
a crescent-shaped lake formed when a meander from a river is cut off from the main channel
