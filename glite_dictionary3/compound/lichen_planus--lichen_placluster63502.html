lichen planus

noun
skin disease with purplish itchy bumps
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkən ˈpleɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪkən ˈpleɪnəs / (BrE)
a skin disease characterized by shiny, flat-topped, purplish, and usually itchy bumps, commonly appearing on the wrists, forearms, or thighs

Examples

lichen planus rash on skin
Patients with lichen planus often experience persistent itching and discomfort.
The dermatologist diagnosed the itchy rash on her arm as lichen planus.
Treatment for lichen planus may involve topical corticosteroids to relieve symptoms.
