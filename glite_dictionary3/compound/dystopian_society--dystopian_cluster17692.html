dystopian society

noun
an imaginary oppressive and nightmarish society
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtoʊpiən səˈsaɪəti / (AmE), / dɪsˈtəʊpɪən səˈsaɪəti / (BrE)
an imagined community or society that is undesirable or frightening, typically characterized by oppression, control, and lack of personal freedoms
