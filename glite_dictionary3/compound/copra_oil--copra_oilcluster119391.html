copra oil

noun
oil made from dried coconut kernel
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊprə ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɒprə ɔɪl / (BrE)
edible oil extracted from the dried kernel (copra) of coconuts, widely used in cooking and industry

Examples
