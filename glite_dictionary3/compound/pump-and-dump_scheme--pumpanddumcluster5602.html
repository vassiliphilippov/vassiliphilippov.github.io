pump-and-dump scheme

noun
illegal stock price manipulation scheme
Pronunciation: / pʌmp ənd dʌmp skiːm / (AmE), / pʌmp ənd dʌmp skiːm / (BrE)
an illegal practice of inflating the price of a stock by false or misleading statements in order to sell one's own shares at a profit, usually leaving other investors with losses

Examples

illegal pump-and-dump scheme
Authorities cracked down on a pump-and-dump scheme that defrauded hundreds of investors.
She lost thousands after falling victim to an online pump-and-dump scheme.
The financial regulator warned the public about the risks of pump-and-dump schemes.

  • synonym – insider trading: illegal trading based on non-public information
