pouter pigeon

noun
breed of pigeon with a puffed-out breast
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊtər ˈpɪdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈpaʊtə ˈpɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a breed of domestic pigeon known for inflating its crop, making its breast appear greatly enlarged
