vocational training

noun
instruction for a particular trade or job
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənəl ˈtreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
training that provides skills and education for a specific trade, profession, or occupation, usually emphasizing practical skills over academic theory

Examples

vocational training for electricians
Many high school students choose vocational training to learn practical job skills.
She completed her vocational training in automotive technology before starting work at the garage.
The government is investing in vocational training programs to reduce unemployment.
