walkie-talkie

noun
portable two-way radio
Pronunciation: / ˌwɔki ˈtɔki / (AmE), / ˌwɔːki ˈtɔːki / (BrE)
a small, portable, two-way radio that allows people to communicate wirelessly over short distances

Examples

use a walkie-talkie outdoors
During the hike, each group leader carried a walkie-talkie for emergencies.
She bought a pair of walkie-talkies for the camping trip so everyone could stay in touch.
The children used their walkie-talkies to talk across the yard while playing.
