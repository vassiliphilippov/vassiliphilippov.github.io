cardcastle

noun
structure made from playing cards
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdˌkæsəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdˌkɑːsəl / (BrE)
A fragile, stacked structure made by balancing playing cards against each other, often as a challenge or pastime.

Examples

build a cardcastle
He stared in dismay as his impressive cardcastle collapsed with the slightest touch.
She took great care while building her cardcastle, but a gust of wind brought it down.
The children competed to see who could make the tallest cardcastle before lunchtime.
