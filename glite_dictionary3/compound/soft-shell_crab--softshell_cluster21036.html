soft-shell crab

noun
recently molted edible crab with a soft shell
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔft ˌʃɛl kræb /; / ˈsɔft ʃɛl kræb / (AmE), / ˈsɒft ˌʃɛl kræb /; / ˈsɒft ʃɛl krab / (BrE)
a crab that has recently shed its old shell and has a soft, edible shell, often eaten whole as a delicacy
