junk sport

noun
trivial or low-quality sport/activity
Pronunciation: / dʒʌŋk spɔrt / (AmE), / dʒʌŋk spɔːt / (BrE)
A sport or activity regarded as trivial, lacking seriousness, or considered to be of low quality or value.

Examples

junk sport on TV
He argued that the network should spend less time on junk sport and more on real athletics.
Many critics dismiss competitive eating competitions as a junk sport rather than a legitimate contest.
The rise of televised wrestling led some traditionalists to complain about the popularity of junk sports.

Related Terms

  • synonym – junk food: food that has little nutritional value and is often high in fat, sugar, or salt
  • antonym – serious sport: sport considered serious and respectable
