Indian beech

noun
evergreen Asian tree with fragrant flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈbiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈbiːtʃ / (BrE)
An evergreen tree native to Asia with glossy, pinnate leaves and clusters of fragrant, creamy-white flowers, often grown as a shade tree.
