knee joint

noun
joint connecting thigh and lower leg
Pronunciation: / ˈniː ˌdʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈniː ˌdʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the joint in the human leg where the thigh meets the lower leg, protected at the front by the kneecap (patella)
