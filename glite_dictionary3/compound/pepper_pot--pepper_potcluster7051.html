pepper pot

noun
a spicy vegetable and tripe soup
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpər pɑt / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə pɒt / (BrE)
a thick soup made with vegetables and tripe, highly seasoned with pepper; often includes dumplings.

Caribbean pepper pot soup
