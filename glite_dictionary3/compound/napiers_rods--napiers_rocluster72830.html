Napier's rods

noun
mathematical calculation rods invented by John Napier
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪpiərz rɑdz / (AmE), / ˈneɪpɪəz rɒdz / (BrE)
a set of numbered rods invented by John Napier, formerly used as a manual aid for multiplication and division
