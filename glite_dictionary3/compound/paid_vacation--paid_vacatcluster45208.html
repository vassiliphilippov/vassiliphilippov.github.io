paid vacation

noun
time off work with pay
Pronunciation: / peɪd veɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / peɪd vəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A period of absence from work for rest or recreation during which an employee receives regular pay.

Examples

Some companies offer more paid vacation days as a benefit to long-term staff.
