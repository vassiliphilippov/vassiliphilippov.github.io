hay conditioner

noun
farm machine for drying hay faster
Pronunciation: / heɪ kənˈdɪʃənɚ / (AmE), / heɪ kənˈdɪʃənə / (BrE)
a farm machine that crushes or crimps newly cut hay to speed up and make more uniform the drying process
