tree house

noun
a house or platform built in a tree
Pronunciation: / ˈtriː ˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtriː ˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A small house or platform built in the branches of a tree, often used by children as a playhouse.

Examples

a tree house for kids
From her tree house, she could see the entire backyard and pretend she was in a castle.
The children spent their afternoons in the tree house they built with their father.
They invited their friends over for a sleepover in the tree house last summer.
