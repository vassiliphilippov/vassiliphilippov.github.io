figure eight

noun
skating movement tracing an 8 on ice
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚ eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə eɪt / (BrE)
a skating movement in which the skater traces the shape of the number 8 on the ice

Examples

to skate a figure eight
Practicing the figure eight helped him improve his balance and control.
She learned to execute a perfect figure eight during her first year of skating lessons.
The skater impressed the judges with a flawless figure eight on the rink.
