cable car

noun
vehicle on a cable railway
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪbəl kɑr / (AmE), / ˈkeɪbəl kɑː / (BrE)
a vehicle that carries passengers or goods on a cable railway, typically along a steep incline or suspended from a cable

Examples

ride a cable car
The cable car swung gently as it climbed toward the hillside village.
The city installed a new cable car system to improve transportation up the mountain.
Tourists enjoyed breathtaking views from the cable car ride across the bay.
