electric fan

noun
device for circulating air
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk fæn / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk fan / (BrE)
A device with rotating blades powered by an electric motor, used to circulate air.

Examples

electric fan cools room
During the summer, an electric fan makes sleeping much more comfortable.
He bought a new electric fan for his office to improve air circulation.
The room was hot, so she turned on the electric fan to cool down.
