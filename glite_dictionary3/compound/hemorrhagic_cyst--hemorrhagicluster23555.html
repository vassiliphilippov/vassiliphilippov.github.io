hemorrhagic cyst

noun
a cyst that contains blood
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛm.əˈrædʒ.ɪk sɪst / (AmE), / ˌhɛm.əˈrædʒ.ɪk sɪst / (BrE)
a cyst filled with or containing blood, often occurring as a result of bleeding within a cystic structure

Examples

hemorrhagic cyst on ovary
Many hemorrhagic cysts resolve without treatment, but some may cause abdominal pain.
The presence of a hemorrhagic cyst is often confirmed using imaging techniques such as MRI or ultrasound.
