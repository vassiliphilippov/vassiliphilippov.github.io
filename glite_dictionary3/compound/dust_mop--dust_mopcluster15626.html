dust mop

noun
long-handled dry floor cleaning tool
Pronunciation: / dʌst mɑp / (AmE), / dʌst mɒp / (BrE)
A long-handled cleaning tool with a soft, dry head used for removing dust from floors.

Examples

use a dust mop on floors
After moving the furniture, he noticed the floor needed a quick swipe with the dust mop.
She prefers using a dust mop because it picks up lint and debris without water.
The janitor used a dust mop to clean the hallway before students arrived.

Related Terms
