angry white man

noun
stereotype of a resentful white man
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡri waɪt mæn / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡri waɪt man / (BrE)
a stereotype of a white man, typically middle-aged or older, who is perceived as frustrated, resentful, or hostile, often in response to changes in social, political, or economic status.
