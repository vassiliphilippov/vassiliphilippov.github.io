landing field

noun
area for aircraft to take off/land
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪŋ fiːld / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ fiːld / (BrE)
an area of land prepared for aircraft to take off and land, typically smaller or less developed than an airport
