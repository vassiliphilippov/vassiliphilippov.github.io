bed wench

noun
derogatory term for promiscuous woman or female servant (historical)
Pronunciation: / bɛd wɛntʃ / (AmE), / bɛd wɛntʃ / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a woman considered promiscuous, or historically, a female servant in a medieval household, often with sexual connotations.

Examples

insulting bed wench remark
Calling her a bed wench was both insulting and inaccurate.
In medieval households, a bed wench was sometimes expected to attend to more than just the bedchamber.
The character was dismissed as a mere bed wench by the noblewomen.
