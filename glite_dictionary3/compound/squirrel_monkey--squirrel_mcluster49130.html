squirrel monkey

noun
small Central and South American monkey
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝːrəl ˌmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈskwɪrəl ˌmʌŋki / (BrE)
A small, active monkey native to Central and South America, with a long tail, greenish fur, and a black muzzle.

Examples

squirrel monkey in rainforest
At the zoo, children enjoyed watching the energetic squirrel monkeys play together.
Researchers observed a group of squirrel monkeys using their tails for balance as they moved through the canopy.
The squirrel monkey is known for its acrobatic leaps from tree to tree in the rainforest.
