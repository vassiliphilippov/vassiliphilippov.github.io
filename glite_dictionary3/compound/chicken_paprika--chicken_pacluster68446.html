chicken paprika

noun
chicken dish with paprika and sour cream
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən păˌpriːkə / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn pəˈpriːkə / (BrE)
A dish consisting of chicken simmered in broth with onions and paprika, often finished with sour cream.
