roulette wheel

noun
wheel used in the game of roulette
Pronunciation: / ruˈlɛt wiːl / (AmE), / ruːˈlɛt wiːl / (BrE)
A horizontal wheel with numbered slots, used in the game of roulette, on which bets are placed as to the slot in which a small ball will come to rest.

After several rounds, he noticed a pattern in the way the roulette wheel seemed to spin.
