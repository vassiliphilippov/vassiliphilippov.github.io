physical property

noun
measurable characteristic of matter (e.g., density, color)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈprɑːpərti / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˈprɒpəti / (BrE)
a characteristic of a material or substance that can be observed or measured without changing its composition, such as density, color, or melting point
