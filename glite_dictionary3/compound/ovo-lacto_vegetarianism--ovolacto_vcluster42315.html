ovo-lacto vegetarianism

noun
vegetarian diet with dairy and eggs
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvoʊˈlæktoʊ ˌvɛdʒəˈtɛriənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəʊˈlæktəʊ ˌvɛdʒɪˈtɛəriənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
A type of vegetarian diet that includes dairy products and eggs but excludes meat, fish, and poultry.

Examples

ovo-lacto vegetarianism includes eggs
Ovo-lacto vegetarianism allows for a wider variety of foods than veganism.
After much research, she adopted ovo-lacto vegetarianism as her preferred diet.
Many people choose ovo-lacto vegetarianism for both ethical and health reasons.
