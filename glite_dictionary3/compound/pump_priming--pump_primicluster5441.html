pump priming

noun
starting flow in a pump by adding water
Pronunciation: / pʌmp ˈpraɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / pʌmp ˈpraɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of introducing water into a pump to improve its seal and initiate water flow

Examples

pump priming with water
