Y2K compliant

adjective
able to process dates correctly from before and after the year 2000
Pronunciation: / waɪ tuː keɪ kəmˈplaɪənt / (AmE), / waɪ tuː keɪ kəmˈplaɪənt / (BrE)
(of computer systems or software) able to correctly process date and time information spanning the years before, during, and after the year 2000.

Examples

Y2K compliant computer system
Many businesses invested heavily to make their computers Y2K compliant in the late 1990s.
Our IT department confirmed the payroll system is fully Y2K compliant.
The bank upgraded its software to ensure all systems were Y2K compliant before 2000.

Related Terms

  • synonym – millennium bug: software error related to date handling in the year 2000
