negative attitude

noun
pessimistic or unconstructive outlook
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪv ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a pessimistic or unconstructive way of thinking or behaving, often expecting the worst or seeing the drawbacks in situations

Examples

show a negative attitude
A negative attitude can limit your opportunities at work.
Her negative attitude made it hard for her to enjoy the trip.
Try to replace your negative attitude with a more optimistic perspective.

Related Terms

  • antonym – optimism: hopefulness and confidence about the future
  • synonym – pessimism: the tendency to see the worst aspect of things
  • antonym – positive attitude: an optimistic or constructive way of thinking or behaving
