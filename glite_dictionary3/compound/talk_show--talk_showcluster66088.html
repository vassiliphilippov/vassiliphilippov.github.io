talk show

noun
TV or radio program with discussions and interviews
Pronunciation: / tɔk ʃoʊ / (AmE), / tɔːk ʃəʊ / (BrE)
A television or radio program in which well-known people discuss topics or answer questions from the audience.

Examples

popular talk show
Many celebrities share personal stories on late-night talk shows.
She was interviewed on a popular talk show last night.
The political candidate answered questions from callers during the live talk show.
