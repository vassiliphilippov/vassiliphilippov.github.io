German ivy

noun
succulent climbing houseplant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜɹmən ˈaɪvi / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˈaɪvi / (BrE)
A succulent, evergreen, twining plant native to South Africa with yellow flowers, grown mainly as a houseplant for its attractive foliage; sometimes classified in the genus Senecio.

Examples

German ivy houseplant
Gardeners sometimes confuse German ivy with English ivy, but they are different species.
Many people choose German ivy as an easy houseplant because of its trailing stems and lush green leaves.
She trained the German ivy to climb the trellis by her living room window.
