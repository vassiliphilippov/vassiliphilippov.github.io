ill turn

noun
an action with unintended harmful effect
Pronunciation: / ɪl tɝn / (AmE), / ɪl tɜːn / (BrE)
an action intended to help that results in harm or disadvantage

Examples

do an ill turn
Doing her homework for her turned out to be an ill turn when she was caught cheating.
He did his friend an ill turn by giving advice that led to trouble.
Sometimes, what you think is a favor can actually be an ill turn in disguise.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disservice: a harmful act; the opposite of a service
  • antonym – good turn: a helpful or beneficial action
