apetalous flower

noun
flower lacking petals
Pronunciation: / əˈpɛtələs ˈflaʊər / (AmE), / əˈpɛtələs ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
A flower that does not have petals.

Examples

an apetalous flower species
Botanists often study apetalous flowers to understand evolutionary adaptations in plants.
Some grasses bear apetalous flowers that rely on the wind rather than insects for pollination.
The willow tree produces small, inconspicuous apetalous flowers in the early spring.
