hypodermic syringe

noun
a syringe with a hypodermic needle for injections
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpəˈdɜrmɪk səˈrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌhɑɪpəˈdɜːmɪk səˈrɪndʒ / (BrE)
a syringe fitted with a hollow needle, used for injecting substances into or beneath the skin
