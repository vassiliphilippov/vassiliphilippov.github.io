mean trick

noun
cruel or unkind act
Pronunciation: / miːn trɪk / (AmE), / miːn trɪk / (BrE)
a cruel or unkind act that is intended to hurt or deceive someone

Examples

play a mean trick
He regretted playing such a mean trick on his unsuspecting brother.
It was a mean trick to embarrass her in front of everyone at the party.
Spreading that false rumor was a mean trick that caused a lot of pain.
