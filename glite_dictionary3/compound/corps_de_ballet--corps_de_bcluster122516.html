corps de ballet

noun
chorus group in a ballet company
Pronunciation: / kɔr də bæˈleɪ / (AmE), / kɔː də bæˈleɪ / (BrE)
The group of dancers in a ballet company who perform as a chorus, supporting the principal dancers.

Examples

corps de ballet dancers
Costume designers must create matching dresses for the entire corps de ballet.
She was promoted from the corps de ballet to a soloist after several seasons.
The corps de ballet moved in perfect unison during the performance.
