in detail

adverb
thoroughly; including all particulars
Pronunciation: / ɪn dɪˈteɪl / (AmE), / ɪn dɪˈteɪl /; / ɪn ˈdiːteɪl / (BrE)
thoroughly and including all important facts, features, or steps; not generally or superficially
