jungle cat

noun
medium-sized Asiatic wildcat species
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋɡəl ˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡ(ə)l ˌkat / (BrE)
a medium-sized wildcat (Felis chaus) native to Asia and northeastern Africa, typically found in wetlands and grasslands

Examples
