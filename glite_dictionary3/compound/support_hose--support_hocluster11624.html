support hose

noun
elastic stockings for vein support
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt hoʊz / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt həʊz / (BrE)
Elastic stockings designed to improve blood circulation and reduce pressure on the veins in the legs, often used to treat or prevent varicose veins.

Examples

support hose for legs
After her surgery, she wore support hose every day to aid circulation.
Many people find support hose helpful during long flights to reduce swelling in the legs.
My doctor recommended wearing support hose to help manage my varicose veins.
