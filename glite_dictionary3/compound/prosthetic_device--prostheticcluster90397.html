prosthetic device

noun
artificial replacement for a body part
Pronunciation: / prɑːsˈθɛtɪk dɪˈvaɪs / (AmE), / prɒsˈθɛtɪk dɪˈvaɪs / (BrE)
an artificial device that replaces a missing or damaged part of the body, such as a limb or tooth

Examples

prosthetic device for a leg
After the accident, he was fitted with a prosthetic device that allowed him to walk again.
Modern prosthetic devices are designed to closely mimic the function of natural limbs.
The dentist recommended a prosthetic device to replace her missing tooth.
