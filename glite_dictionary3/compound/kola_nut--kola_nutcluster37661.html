kola nut

noun
caffeinated seed used for cola flavoring
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊlə nʌt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlə nʌt / (BrE)
the brown, bitter seed of a tropical West African tree, containing caffeine and traditionally used as a stimulant and in the flavoring of cola drinks

Examples

chew a kola nut
Chewing a kola nut can help to combat fatigue due to its caffeine content.
In some West African cultures, sharing a kola nut is a sign of hospitality.
The company uses natural kola nuts in its beverage recipe instead of synthetic flavors.

  • synonym – cola: sweet carbonated drink flavored with kola nut extract or artificial substitute
