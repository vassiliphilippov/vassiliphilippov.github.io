brown onion sauce

noun
brown sauce with onions and wine or vinegar
Pronunciation: / braʊn ˈʌnjən sɔs / (AmE), / braʊn ˈʌnjən sɔːs / (BrE)
A brown sauce made with sautéed chopped onions, parsley, and dry white wine or vinegar, commonly served with meats or vegetables.
