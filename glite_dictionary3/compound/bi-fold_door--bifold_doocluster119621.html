bi-fold door

noun
door with two folding panels
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ foʊld dɔr / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ fəʊld dɔː / (BrE)
A door made of two panels that are hinged together and fold to one side when opened, typically used for closets or interior spaces.

Examples

bi-fold door for closet
She painted the bi-fold door white to match the trim in her hallway.
The bi-fold door makes it easy to access the entire closet without taking up too much space.
We decided to install a bi-fold door to save space in our small laundry room.
