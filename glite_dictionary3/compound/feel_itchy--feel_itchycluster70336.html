to feel itchy

verb
experience an itchy sensation
Pronunciation: / fil ˈɪtʃi / (AmE), / fiːl ˈɪtʃi / (BrE)
to experience an uncomfortable sensation on the skin that makes you want to scratch

Examples

to feel itchy after swimming
After swimming in the lake, I began to feel itchy on my arms and legs.
If you feel itchy after using the new soap, you might be allergic to it.
Wearing woolen clothes sometimes makes me feel itchy all over.
