Mount Ararat

proper noun
mountain in Turkey, ark's traditional landing site
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˈærəˌræt / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈærəræt / (BrE)
a mountain in eastern Turkey, traditionally considered the landing place of Noah's ark according to the Bible

Examples

Mount Ararat in Turkey
Armenians consider Mount Ararat a national symbol, even though it lies within modern Turkey.
