second-hand speech

noun
overheard conversation, especially on cellphones
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkəndˌhænd ˈspiːtʃ / (AmE), / ˌsɛkəndˌhænd ˈspiːtʃ / (BrE)
conversation or dialogue that is overheard, particularly when someone inadvertently hears others' cellphone conversations without participating in them.
