pinch hitter

noun
substitute batter in baseball
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪntʃ ˌhɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɪntʃ ˌhɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes someone else's place for a short time, especially a baseball player who bats instead of another player, or anyone who fills in for someone else temporarily
