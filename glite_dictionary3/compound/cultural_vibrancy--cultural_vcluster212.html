cultural vibrancy

noun
lively, dynamic cultural atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃɚəl ˈvaɪbrənsi / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈvaɪbrənsi / (BrE)
a lively and dynamic atmosphere or quality in the culture of a place, marked by active artistic and social life

Examples

city's cultural vibrancy
Efforts to support artists have enhanced the cultural vibrancy of the community.
Local festivals contribute greatly to the cultural vibrancy of the region.
The city's cultural vibrancy attracts visitors from all over the world.
