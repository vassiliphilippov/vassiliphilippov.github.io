poker face

noun
expressionless face giving nothing away
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊkɚ feɪs / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkə feɪs / (BrE)
an expressionless face that gives no indication of one's thoughts or feelings, especially to conceal one’s emotions or intentions

Examples

keep a poker face
Despite the shocking news, she maintained a poker face throughout the meeting.
During negotiations, having a poker face can be a valuable skill.
He practiced his poker face so no one could guess his real feelings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deadpan: a deliberately impassive or expressionless facial expression
  • antonym – tell: an unconscious action that gives away information in poker or other contexts
