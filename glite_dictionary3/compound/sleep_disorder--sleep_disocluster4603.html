sleep disorder

noun
medical condition affecting normal sleep
Pronunciation: / sliːp dɪˈsɔrdər / (AmE), / sliːp dɪˈsɔːdə / (BrE)
A medical condition that disturbs or disrupts a person's normal pattern of sleep.

Examples

sleep disorder diagnosis
A sleep disorder can seriously impact one's mood, energy, and overall health.
He was diagnosed with a sleep disorder after months of sleepless nights.
Treatments for a sleep disorder may include medication, therapy, or lifestyle changes.
