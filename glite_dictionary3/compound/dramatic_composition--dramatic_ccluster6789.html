dramatic composition

noun
a play or work meant for performance
Pronunciation: / drəˈmætɪk kəmˌpɑːzɪˈʃən / (AmE), / drəˈmatɪk kəmˌpɒzɪˈʃən / (BrE)
A literary work, such as a play, written to be performed by actors on stage, television, or in film.
