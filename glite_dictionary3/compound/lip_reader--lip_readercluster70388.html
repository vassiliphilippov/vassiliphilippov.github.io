lip reader

noun
person who deciphers speech by watching lips
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪp ˌriːdər / (AmE), / ˈlɪp ˌriːdə / (BrE)
a person who is able to understand speech by interpreting the movements of a speaker's lips

Examples

skilled lip reader
As a skilled lip reader, she could follow conversations even in noisy environments.
Despite his hearing loss, he participated fully thanks to his exceptional ability as a lip reader.
The courtroom provided a lip reader to help interpret the witness's testimony for the jury.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interpreter: a person who translates spoken language
  • synonym – lipreading: the skill or process of understanding speech by visually interpreting lip movements
