spice tree

noun
aromatic Pacific coast tree
Pronunciation: / spaɪs triː / (AmE), / spaɪs triː / (BrE)
A tree native to the Pacific coast with aromatic leaves, small flower clusters, and olivelike fruit, known for its hard, tough wood.

Examples

spice tree wood
Carpenters value the tough wood of the spice tree for building tools and furniture.
