genus Trichechus

noun
biological genus including manatees
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs trɪˈkiːkəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs trɪˈkiːkəs / (BrE)
the sole genus of the family Trichechidae, including the manatees, large aquatic herbivorous mammals of tropical rivers and coasts.
