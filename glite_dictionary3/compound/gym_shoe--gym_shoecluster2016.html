gym shoe

noun
light canvas athletic shoe
Pronunciation: / dʒɪm ʃu / (AmE), / dʒɪm ʃuː / (BrE)
A light-weight canvas shoe with a flexible rubber sole, typically worn for sports or physical exercise.
