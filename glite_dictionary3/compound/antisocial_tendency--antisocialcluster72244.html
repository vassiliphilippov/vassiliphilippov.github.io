antisocial tendency

noun
behavior contrary to social norms or social withdrawal
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈsoʊ.ʃəl ˈtɛn.dən.si / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈsəʊ.ʃəl ˈtɛn.dən.si / (BrE)
a pattern of behavior that opposes social norms or indicates avoidance of social interaction

Examples
