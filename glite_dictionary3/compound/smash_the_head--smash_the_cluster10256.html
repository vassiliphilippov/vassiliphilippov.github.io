to smash the head

verb
hit or attack the head violently
Pronunciation: / smæʃ ðə hɛd / (AmE), / smæʃ ðə hɛd / (BrE)
to violently strike or attack a person's head, often causing serious injury

Examples

to smash the head with a rock
In the novel, the villain threatens to smash the head of anyone who betrays him.
She warned him not to smash the head of the statue while moving it.
The attacker tried to smash the head of his enemy with a heavy object.
