common roundworm

noun
intestinal parasitic worm
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈraʊndˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈraʊndˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a type of intestinal parasitic worm (Ascaris lumbricoides) that infects humans and pigs, causing disease

Examples
