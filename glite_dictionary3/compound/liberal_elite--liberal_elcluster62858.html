liberal elite

noun
influential group with liberal views
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbɚəl ɪˈlit / (AmE), / ˈlɪb(ə)rəl ɪˈliːt / (BrE)
a group of influential people who hold liberal political views, often perceived as being socially and economically privileged

Examples

the liberal elite in politics
Many critics accuse the liberal elite of being out of touch with ordinary voters.
Some newspapers claim the liberal elite shapes public opinion on key social issues.
The debate was dominated by members of the liberal elite from top universities.
