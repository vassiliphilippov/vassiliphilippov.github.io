salmon trout

noun
flesh of migratory trout used as food
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmən traʊt / (AmE), / ˈsamən traʊt / (BrE)
The flesh of a marine trout that migrates from saltwater to freshwater, valued as food.

Examples

grilled salmon trout
Grilled salmon trout pairs perfectly with lemon and dill for a light summer meal.
The chef recommended the smoked salmon trout as an appetizer.
We enjoyed fresh salmon trout caught from the river that morning.
