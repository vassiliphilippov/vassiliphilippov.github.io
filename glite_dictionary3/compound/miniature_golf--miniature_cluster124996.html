miniature golf

noun
small-scale novelty golf game
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniətʃɚ ɡɑlf / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪətʃə ɡɒlf / (BrE)
A recreational game resembling regular golf but played on a small, artificial course with various obstacles, using only putters and golf balls.

Examples

play miniature golf with friends
On our vacation, we challenged each other to a friendly game of miniature golf.
The kids loved navigating the windmills and castles at the miniature golf course.
We decided to play a round of miniature golf at the local amusement park.
