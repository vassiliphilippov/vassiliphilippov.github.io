bishop pine

noun
Californian coastal pine tree species
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪʃəp paɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɪʃəp paɪn / (BrE)
A pine tree species (Pinus muricata) native to the northwestern coast of California, identified by its two or three needles per bundle.
