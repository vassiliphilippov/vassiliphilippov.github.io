smaller pectoral muscle

noun
chest muscle under the pectoralis major
Pronunciation: / smɔːlər pɛkˈtɔːrəl ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / smɔːlə pɛkˈtɔːrəl ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
a thin, triangular skeletal muscle located beneath the pectoralis major that draws the scapula downward or raises the ribs
