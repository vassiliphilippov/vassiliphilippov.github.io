Aegean culture

noun
prehistoric civilization of the Aegean region
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒiən ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / iːˈdʒiːən ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the prehistoric civilization that developed on the islands of the Aegean Sea and surrounding mainland areas, notably including the Minoan and Mycenaean cultures

Examples

ancient Aegean culture sites
Archaeologists have uncovered impressive palace complexes from the height of Aegean culture.
The pottery designs of Aegean culture strongly influenced later Greek art.
Trade played a central role in the development of Aegean culture across the region.
