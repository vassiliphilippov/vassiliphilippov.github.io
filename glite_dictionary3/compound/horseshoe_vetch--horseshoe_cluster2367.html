horseshoe vetch

noun
a European plant with yellow flowers and horseshoe-shaped pods
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌʃu vɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsʃuː vɛtʃ / (BrE)
a European perennial plant (Hippocrepis comosa) with yellow umbel-shaped flowers and flattened pods that separate into horseshoe-shaped segments
