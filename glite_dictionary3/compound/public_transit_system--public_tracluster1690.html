public transit system

noun
organized public transportation network
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈtræn.sɪt ˌsɪs.təm / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈtræn.zɪt ˌsɪs.təm / (BrE)
an organized network of transportation services, such as buses, trains, or subways, that is available for use by the general public

Examples

city public transit system
A reliable public transit system is essential for sustainable urban development.
Many commuters depend on the public transit system for their daily travel.
The city council plans to expand the public transit system to reduce traffic congestion.

Related Terms

  • antonym – private transport: transportation using one's own vehicles, not shared with the public
