wastepaper basket

noun
container for discarded paper
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌpeɪpər ˌbæskət / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌpeɪpə ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A container, typically with an open top, used for holding discarded paper and other small rubbish, especially in offices and homes.

Examples

wastepaper basket under desk
He tossed the crumpled memo into the wastepaper basket under his desk.
She missed the wastepaper basket when she tried to throw away a candy wrapper.
The office was tidy except for the overflowing wastepaper basket in the corner.
