Fibonacci number

noun
number in the Fibonacci sequence
Pronunciation: / ˌfiːbəˈnɑtʃi ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˌfɪbəˈnɑːtʃi ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
Any number in the sequence where each number is the sum of the previous two, starting with 0 and 1.
