pectoral sandpiper

noun
a species of sandpiper bird with a streaked breast
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛk.tɚ.əl ˈsændˌpaɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɛk.tər.əl ˈsændˌpaɪ.pə / (BrE)
A medium-sized migratory wading bird (Calidris melanotos) of North America and Asia, recognized for its streaked breast and the habit of inflating its chest during courtship displays.
