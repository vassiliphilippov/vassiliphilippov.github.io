New Year's Eve

noun
the last day of the year
Pronunciation: / nu ˈjɪrz ˈiv / (AmE), / nju ˈjɪəz ˈiːv / (BrE)
The last day of the year, especially the evening preceding New Year's Day, often marked with celebrations and festivities.

New Year's Eve party
Fireworks lit up the sky at midnight on New Year's Eve.
