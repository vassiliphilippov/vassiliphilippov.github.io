seismic event

noun
earth tremor or earthquake
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪzmɪk ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪzmɪk ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
a natural occurrence, such as an earthquake or tremor, caused by a sudden release of energy in the Earth's crust

Examples

a seismic event shakes ground
A minor seismic event was felt in the early morning hours but caused no damage.
Scientists recorded the seismic event using sensitive instruments placed throughout the region.
The seismic event shook the entire city, causing widespread concern.
