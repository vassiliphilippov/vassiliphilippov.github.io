genus Cycas

noun
type genus of Cycadaceae; tropical evergreen trees
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsaɪ.kəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈsaɪ.kəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Cycadaceae, consisting of widely distributed Old World evergreen tropical trees with pinnate leaves and columnar stems covered in the persistent bases of old leaves.

The genus Cycas is known for its distinctive columnar stem and feathery, pinnate leaves.
