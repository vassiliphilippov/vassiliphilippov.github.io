golden calf

noun
idol or object of undeserved worship
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊldən ˈkæf /; / ˈɡoʊldən kæf / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊldən ˈkɑːf /; / ˈɡəʊldən kɑːf / (BrE)
an object or thing that is worshipped or valued too much, especially something unworthy like money or material things (from the Bible story of the idol made by Aaron)
