Panama tree

noun
large deciduous tree native to Panama
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəmə ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈpanəmə ˌtriː / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree native to Panama, noted for its densely leafy crown and bare trunk, and believed to be the origin of the country's name

Examples

tall Panama tree
According to legend, the country was named after the Panama tree that once grew abundantly there.
Researchers studied the ecological role of the Panama tree in supporting local wildlife.
The huge Panama tree dominated the center of the village with its leafy crown.
