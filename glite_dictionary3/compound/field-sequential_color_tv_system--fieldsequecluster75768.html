field-sequential color TV system

noun
early color TV system using sequential primary color fields
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld səˈkwenʃəl ˈkʌlər ˌtiːˈviː ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈfiːld sɪˈkwenʃəl ˈkʌlə ˌtiːˈviː ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
an early system of color television in which images are transmitted by scanning successive fields in the three primary colors, typically red, green, and blue, in sequence.
