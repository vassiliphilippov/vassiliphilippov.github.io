whitetail jackrabbit

noun
large North American hare with white tail
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌteɪl ˈdʒækˌræbɪt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌteɪl ˈdʒækˌræbɪt / (BrE)
