two touch

noun
football rule: two touches before passing
Pronunciation: / tu tʌtʃ / (AmE), / tuː tʌtʃ / (BrE)
A form of football (soccer) play in which a player may touch the ball only twice before passing it to another player.

Examples

two touch football drill
During training, the coach encouraged us to play two touch to improve our passing speed.
He struggled at first with the two touch rule, but soon became much faster at moving the ball.
Professional teams often use two touch drills to develop quick decision-making on the field.
