cordate leaf

noun
heart-shaped leaf
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.deɪt lif / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.deɪt liːf / (BrE)
A leaf that is shaped like a heart, with a notch at the base and rounded lobes.

Examples

a cordate leaf shape
Botanists often describe the violet's foliage as having a cordate leaf arrangement.
Several houseplants have a distinctive cordate leaf shape that adds to their charm.
The linden tree's cordate leaf is a key feature for identification.
