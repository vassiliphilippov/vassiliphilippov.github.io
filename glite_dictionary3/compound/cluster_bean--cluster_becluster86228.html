cluster bean

noun
plant grown for edible pods and guar gum
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌstər ˌbiːn / (AmE), / ˈklʌstə ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a drought-tolerant plant grown for its edible pods and seeds, the latter used to produce guar gum as a thickener or stabilizer

Examples

cluster bean curry
Farmers in arid regions often grow cluster bean due to its drought resistance.
She cooked a traditional curry using fresh cluster bean pods from her garden.
The seeds of the cluster bean are processed to produce guar gum for the food industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym – guar: another name for cluster bean, especially when referring to the gum
