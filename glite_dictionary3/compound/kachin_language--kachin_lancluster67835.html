Kachin language

noun
Tibeto-Burman languages of northern Myanmar and nearby regions
Pronunciation: / kəˈtʃɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kəˈtʃɪn ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A group of Tibeto-Burman languages spoken mainly in the northernmost part of Myanmar (Burma) and in adjacent areas of China and India.

Examples

the Kachin language spoken in Myanmar
Learning the Kachin language can help connect with communities in the Kachin State.
She is conducting research on the Kachin language for her linguistics thesis.
The Kachin language is spoken by people living in northern Myanmar and parts of China.
