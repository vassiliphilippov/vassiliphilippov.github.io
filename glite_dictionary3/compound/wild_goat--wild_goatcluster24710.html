wild goat

noun
undomesticated goat
Pronunciation: / waɪld ɡoʊt / (AmE), / waɪld ɡəʊt / (BrE)
A goat that lives in the wild and has not been domesticated.

Examples

mountain wild goat
The wild goat population in the region has increased over the past decade.
Unlike domesticated breeds, a wild goat is adapted to survive in harsh, mountainous terrain.
We spotted a wild goat on the rocky hillside during our hike.

Related Terms

  • antonym – domestic goat: goat bred and kept by humans for milk, meat, or wool
