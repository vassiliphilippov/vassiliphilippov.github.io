serum albumin

noun
albumin protein in blood serum
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrəm ælˈbjumɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪərəm ælˈbjuːmɪn / (BrE)
a type of albumin protein found in blood serum, important for maintaining the osmotic pressure of the blood
