suboccipital muscle

noun
muscle beneath the occipital bone
Pronunciation: / sʌbˌɑkˈsɪpɪtəl ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / sʌbˌɒkˈsɪpɪtəl ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
Any of several small muscles located beneath the occipital bone at the base of the skull, involved in head movement and posture.
