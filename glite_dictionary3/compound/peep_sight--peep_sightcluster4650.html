peep sight

noun
rear aiming sight with a small aperture
Pronunciation: / pip saɪt / (AmE), / piːp saɪt / (BrE)
a rear gunsight with a small adjustable aperture through which the shooter aligns the front sight and the target

Examples

peep sight on a rifle
He adjusted the peep sight to improve his accuracy at the shooting range.
