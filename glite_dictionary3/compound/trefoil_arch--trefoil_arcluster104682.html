trefoil arch

noun
pointed arch with three rounded cusps
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːˌfɔɪl ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈtriːfɔɪl ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
A pointed arch having three cusps or rounded projections in the intrados, typically creating a cloverleaf shape.

Examples

trefoil arch in a cathedral
Many Gothic cathedrals feature the trefoil arch in their windows and doorways.
The entrance to the chapel is marked by a beautiful trefoil arch.
The restoration preserved the original trefoil arch above the altar.
