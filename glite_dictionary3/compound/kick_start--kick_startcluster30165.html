kick start

noun
foot-operated starter on a motorcycle
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪk stɑrt / (AmE), / ˈkɪk stɑːt / (BrE)
a lever on a motorcycle or similar vehicle that is operated with the foot to start the engine

Examples
