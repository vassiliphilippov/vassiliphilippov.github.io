delta iron

noun
high-temperature allotrope of iron
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛltə ˈaɪərn / (AmE), / ˈdɛltə ˈaɪən / (BrE)
an allotrope of iron that is stable at temperatures between 1403°C and 1532°C, just below its melting point.
