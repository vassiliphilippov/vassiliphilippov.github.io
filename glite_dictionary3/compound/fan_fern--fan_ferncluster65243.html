fan fern

noun
Australasian fern with fan-shaped fronds
Pronunciation: / fæn fɝn / (AmE), / fæn fɜːn / (BrE)
a large fern native to Australasia, characterized by fan-shaped, repeatedly forked fronds; sometimes classified in the genus Gleichenia

Examples

fan fern garden plant
The fan fern is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
The distinctive fronds of the fan fern make it easy to identify among other plants.
