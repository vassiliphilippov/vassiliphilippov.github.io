suction stop

noun
inward-drawn stop consonant
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌk.ʃən stɑp / (AmE), / ˈsʌk.ʃən stɒp / (BrE)
A stop consonant produced by drawing air inward into the mouth, as found in some African languages such as Bantu.
