Fanny Adams

noun
British slang for tinned meat
Pronunciation: / ˈfæni ˈædəmz / (AmE), / ˈfæni ˈædəmz / (BrE)
A British nautical slang term for tinned meat, originally used humorously or cynically to refer to food of dubious quality.

Examples

canned Fanny Adams
After weeks at sea, we grew tired of eating nothing but Fanny Adams from cans.
