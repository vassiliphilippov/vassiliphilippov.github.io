locust bean

noun
long pod of the carob tree, used as food
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəst ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəst ˌbiːn / (BrE)
the long pod of the carob tree, containing small beans and a sweet edible pulp that is used as animal feed and as a chocolate substitute

Examples

locust bean pod
Farmers frequently use dried locust beans as animal feed during the dry season.
Some health food stores sell locust bean powder as a chocolate substitute.
The extract from the locust bean is often used as a natural thickener in food products.
