gunny sack

noun
coarse burlap bag for goods
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌni sæk / (AmE), / ˈɡʌni sak / (BrE)
a large bag made of coarse burlap, typically used for transporting goods such as grain or potatoes

Examples

gunny sack of potatoes
Children raced down the hill sitting inside old gunny sacks.
He carried rice in a heavy gunny sack slung over his shoulder.
The potatoes were stored in a gunny sack in the barn.
