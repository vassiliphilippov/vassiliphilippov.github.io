spear thrower

noun
tool for throwing spears or darts
Pronunciation: / spɪr ˈθroʊər / (AmE), / spɪə ˈθrəʊə / (BrE)
A device, typically used in various indigenous and ancient societies, for propelling a spear or dart over a greater distance and with greater force.

Examples

ancient spear thrower
Archaeologists discovered an ancient spear thrower near the riverbank.
The museum's collection features a rare wooden spear thrower from prehistoric times.
Using a spear thrower allowed hunters to throw their darts much further than by hand.
