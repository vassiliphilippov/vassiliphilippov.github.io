white-rumped shrike

noun
a shrike species with a white rump
Pronunciation: / ˌwaɪt ˈrʌmpt ʃraɪk / (AmE), / ˌwaɪt ˈrʌmpt ʃraɪk / (BrE)
a species of shrike found in western North America, noted for its grey upperparts and white underparts, including a distinctive white rump; also known as a type of butcherbird
