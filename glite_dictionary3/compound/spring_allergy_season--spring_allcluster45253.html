spring allergy season

noun
time in spring with heightened pollen allergies
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈælɚdʒi ˈsizən / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈælədʒi ˈsiːzən / (BrE)
the time of year in spring when pollen levels are high, causing increased allergic reactions in sensitive individuals

Examples

If you suffer from allergies, the spring allergy season can be especially difficult.

Many people stock up on antihistamines before the spring allergy season begins.
