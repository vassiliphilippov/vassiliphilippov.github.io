Ku Klux Klan

proper noun
U.S. white supremacist secret society
Pronunciation: / ku klʌks klæn / (AmE), / kuː klʌks klæn / (BrE)
A secret society of white supremacists founded in the 19th-century United States, known for using terror and violence against Black people and other groups.
