finding of fact

noun
court or jury's decision on facts
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪndɪŋ əv ˈfækt / (AmE), / ˈfaɪndɪŋ əv ˈfakt / (BrE)
(Law) A decision made by a court or jury regarding the facts of a case, which may be used as the basis for a legal judgment.
