arty-crafty

adjective
pretentiously artistic; overly charming
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtiˈkræfti / (AmE), / ˌɑːtiˈkrɑːfti / (BrE)
Pretentiously or affectedly artistic; overly charming in a cloying way.

Examples

She finds the décor at that café a bit too arty-crafty for her taste.
The film was criticized as being arty-crafty rather than genuinely creative.
