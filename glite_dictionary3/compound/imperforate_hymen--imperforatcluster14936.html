imperforate hymen

noun
closed hymen preventing menstrual flow
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɝːfərət ˈhaɪmən / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːfərət ˈhaɪmən / (BrE)
a congenital condition in which the hymen is completely closed, preventing menstrual blood from leaving the vagina

Examples

congenital imperforate hymen
An imperforate hymen can cause abdominal pain in adolescent girls due to trapped menstrual blood.
Doctors usually diagnose an imperforate hymen during a physical examination when a girl has not started menstruating despite puberty.
Surgical intervention is required to treat an imperforate hymen and restore normal menstrual flow.
