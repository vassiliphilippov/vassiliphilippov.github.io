sheep sorrel

noun
a small wild plant with acid-tasting leaves
Pronunciation: / ʃip ˈsɔr.əl / (AmE), / ʃiːp ˈsɒr.əl / (BrE)
a small plant with arrow-shaped, pleasantly acid-tasting leaves, commonly found in dry places
