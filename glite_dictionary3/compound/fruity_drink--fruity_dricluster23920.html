fruity drink

noun
sweet beverage with fruit flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːti drɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈfruːti drɪŋk / (BrE)
a sweet beverage with a pronounced fruit flavor, often non-alcoholic and enjoyed especially by children or at parties

Examples
