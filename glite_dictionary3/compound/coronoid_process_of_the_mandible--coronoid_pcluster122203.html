coronoid process of the mandible

noun
projection on the upper mandible for muscle attachment
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊnɔɪd ˈprɑsɛs əv ðə mænˈdɪbəl / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊnɔɪd ˈprɒsɛs əv ðə mænˈdɪbəl / (BrE)
