one percent

noun
one part in a hundred
Pronunciation: / wʌn pərˈsɛnt / (AmE), / wʌn pəˈsɛnt / (BrE)
One part out of a hundred equal parts; 1%.

Examples

one percent of students
Only one percent of the population is affected by this rare disease.
Sales increased by one percent compared to last year, which is a modest improvement.
The solution contained just one percent salt by volume.
