capillary vessel

noun
smallest blood vessel connecting arterioles and venules
Pronunciation: / kəˈpɪləri ˈvɛsəl / (AmE), / kəˈpɪləri ˈvɛsəl / (BrE)
any of the smallest blood vessels that connect arterioles to venules, allowing exchange of substances between blood and tissues
