coloratura soprano

noun
high soprano skilled in ornate vocal music
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌl.ə.rəˈtu.rə səˈprɑː.noʊ / (AmE), / ˌkɒl.ə.rəˈtjʊə səˈprɑː.nəʊ / (BrE)
A soprano singer whose voice is agile and capable of performing elaborate ornamentation and rapid passages, particularly in coloratura music.

Examples

Maria auditioned for the role, showcasing her talent as a coloratura soprano.
