birther conspiracy

noun
conspiracy theory about a person's birthplace
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrθər kənˈspɪrəsi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθə kənˈspɪrəsi / (BrE)
a political conspiracy theory alleging that a person, often a public figure, was not born in the country they claim, especially used in relation to the eligibility for public office

Many people dismissed the birther conspiracy as unfounded and politically motivated.
