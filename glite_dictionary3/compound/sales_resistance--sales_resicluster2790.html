sales resistance

noun
customer reluctance to aggressive selling
Pronunciation: / seɪlz rɪˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / seɪlz rɪˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
the reluctance or opposition shown by potential customers toward aggressive or persuasive sales tactics

Examples

sales resistance from customers
Effective training can help salespeople overcome sales resistance from skeptical clients.
High-pressure tactics often increase sales resistance instead of boosting profits.
The company's new marketing strategy aimed to minimize sales resistance among first-time buyers.
