lap choly

noun
surgical removal of gall bladder via laparoscopy
Pronunciation: / læp ˈkoʊli / (AmE), / læp ˈkəʊli / (BrE)
An informal abbreviation for a laparoscopic cholecystectomy, the surgical removal of the gall bladder using small incisions and a laparoscope.
