hole card

noun
card dealt face down in poker
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊl kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊl kɑːd / (BrE)
(in poker) a playing card dealt face down to a player, not revealed until the showdown.

Examples

hole card face down
Each player receives one hole card and one card face up.
She won the hand with an ace as her hole card.
You should never reveal your hole card before the showdown.

Related Terms

  • antonym – community card: a card dealt face up in the center of the table, shared by all players
