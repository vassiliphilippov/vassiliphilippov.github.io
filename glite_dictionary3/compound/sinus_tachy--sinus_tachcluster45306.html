sinus tachy

noun
rapid heartbeat from the sinus node
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnəs ˈtæki / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnəs ˈtæki / (BrE)
An abnormally rapid heartbeat that originates from the sinus node, often used as an abbreviation for sinus tachycardia.

Examples

sinus tachy on ECG
Sinus tachy is sometimes a normal response to stress or fever.
Athletes can occasionally develop sinus tachy during intense exercise.
The doctor diagnosed her with sinus tachy after reviewing the ECG results.
