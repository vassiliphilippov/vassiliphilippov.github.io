faucial tonsil

noun
mass of lymphatic tissue in the throat
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔʃəl ˈtɑn.səl / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.ʃi.əl ˈtɒn.sɪl / (BrE)
either of two masses of lymphatic tissue, one on each side of the oral pharynx, commonly known as the palatine tonsils
