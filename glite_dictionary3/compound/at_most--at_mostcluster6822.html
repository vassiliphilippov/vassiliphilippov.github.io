at most

adverb
not more than; maximum limit
Pronunciation: / æt ˈmoʊst / (AmE), / ət ˈməʊst / (BrE)
not more than; used to indicate the maximum possible amount or number.

Examples

at most five minutes
The entire journey will take at most three hours by car.
The repairs should cost at most $500, according to the estimate.
You may bring at most two guests to the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym – at least: not less than; indicates a minimum limit
  • synonym – maximum: the greatest amount or degree possible or allowed
