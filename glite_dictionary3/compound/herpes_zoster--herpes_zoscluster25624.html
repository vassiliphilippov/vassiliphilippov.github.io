herpes zoster

noun
viral infection causing painful skin rash (shingles)
Pronunciation: / ˌhɜːrpiːz ˈzoʊstər / (AmE), / ˌhɜːpiːz ˈzɒstə / (BrE)
a viral infection characterized by painful skin eruptions along a nerve path, often accompanied by severe neuralgia; also known as shingles

Examples

herpes zoster skin rash
She was diagnosed with herpes zoster after developing a painful rash on her side.
The doctor explained that the nerve pain from herpes zoster can persist even after the rash disappears.
Treatment for herpes zoster can help reduce the risk of complications.
