sun dance

noun
Native American ceremonial dance at the solstice
Pronunciation: / sʌn dæns / (AmE), / sʌn dɑːns / (BrE)
a ceremonial dance performed by certain Native American peoples, traditionally held at the summer solstice as part of a ritual honoring the sun

Examples

sun dance ceremony
Each year, the tribe gathers for the sun dance to mark the start of summer.
Historians have studied the cultural significance of the sun dance among Plains tribes.
Participating in the sun dance requires weeks of preparation and spiritual fasting.
