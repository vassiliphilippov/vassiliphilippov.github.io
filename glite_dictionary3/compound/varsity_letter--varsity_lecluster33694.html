varsity letter

noun
award for sports achievement at school
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːrsəti ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈvɑːsəti ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
an award, usually in the form of a cloth letter, given to students for outstanding participation in a school's varsity sport or activity

Examples

varsity letter jacket
His jacket was decorated with three varsity letters from different athletic teams.
Many students work hard all year to earn a varsity letter in their favorite sport.
She was proud to receive her varsity letter after a successful basketball season.
