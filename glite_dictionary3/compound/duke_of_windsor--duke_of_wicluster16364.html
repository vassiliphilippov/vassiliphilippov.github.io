Duke of Windsor

proper noun
title of Edward VIII after abdication
Pronunciation: / duːk əv ˈwɪnzər / (AmE), / djuːk əv ˈwɪnzə / (BrE)
the title given to Edward VIII after his abdication as King of the United Kingdom in 1936, following his marriage to Wallis Simpson
