sick leave

noun
absence from work due to illness
Pronunciation: / sɪk liːv / (AmE), / sɪk liːv / (BrE)
a period of absence from work granted to an employee because of illness

Examples

on sick leave from work
Employees are entitled to ten days of sick leave per year.
He returned to work after a week of sick leave for surgery.
She was granted sick leave after coming down with the flu.
