Stalin Peak

proper noun
highest peak in the Pamir Mountains, Tajikistan
Pronunciation: / stɑːlɪn piːk / (AmE), / stɑːlɪn piːk / (BrE)
the highest mountain in the Pamir Mountains, located near the Chinese border in northeastern Tajikistan, rising to 24,590 feet (7,495 meters)
