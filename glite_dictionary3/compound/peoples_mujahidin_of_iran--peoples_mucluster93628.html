People's Mujahidin of Iran

proper noun
Iranian political organization with Marxist-Islamist ideology
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːpəlz muːˈdʒɑːhedin əv aɪˈrɑːn / (AmE), / ˈpiːplz mjuːˈhædɪn əv ɪˈrɑːn / (BrE)
