white cake

noun
pale cake made without egg yolks
Pronunciation: / waɪt keɪk / (AmE), / waɪt keɪk / (BrE)
A light, pale-colored cake made without egg yolks, typically using egg whites, flour, sugar, and butter.

Examples

slice of white cake

The recipe calls for egg whites only to achieve the perfect white cake texture.
