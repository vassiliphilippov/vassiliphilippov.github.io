toilet kit

noun
bag for carrying toiletries while traveling
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔɪlət kɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlət kɪt / (BrE)
a small bag or case used for carrying personal hygiene items, such as soap, toothbrush, and shaving supplies, especially while traveling

After losing his luggage, he was relieved to find a spare toilet kit at the airport shop.
