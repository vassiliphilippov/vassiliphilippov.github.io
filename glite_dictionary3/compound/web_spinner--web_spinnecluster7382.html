web spinner

noun
a slender insect that spins silken tunnels
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛb ˌspɪnər / (AmE), / ˈwɛb ˌspɪnə / (BrE)
A slender tropical insect that lives in colonies and spins silken tunnels for nesting, belonging to a small order called Embioptera.

Examples

tropical web spinner
Entomologists discovered a new species of web spinner in the Amazon rainforest.
The colony of web spinners constructed a series of interconnected silken tunnels along the tree branch.
Unlike spiders, web spinners use their front legs to produce silk for their tunnels.
