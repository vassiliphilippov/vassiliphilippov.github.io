surveillance system

noun
system for monitoring using cameras or devices
Pronunciation: / sɚˈveɪ.ləns ˈsɪs.təm / (AmE), / səˈveɪ.ləns ˈsɪs.təm / (BrE)
a system of cameras or other devices used to monitor individuals or groups, typically for security or observational purposes

Examples

surveillance system monitors buildings
Every entrance is covered by a surveillance system to ensure the safety of the employees.
Footage from the surveillance system was used as evidence during the trial.
The bank installed a new surveillance system to deter thieves and monitor activity inside the building.
