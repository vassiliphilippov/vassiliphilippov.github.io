white noise

noun
continuous noise with equal intensity across all frequencies
Pronunciation: / waɪt nɔɪs / (AmE), / waɪt nɔɪz / (BrE)
a steady or continuous sound containing all audible frequencies at equal intensity, often used to mask other sounds or help with sleep and concentration
