posterior temporal artery

noun
artery supplying back part of temporal lobe
Pronunciation: / pəˈstɪriɚ tɛmˈpɔrəl ˈɑrtəri / (AmE), / pɒˈstɪəriə tɛmˈpɔːrəl ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
A branch of the temporal artery that supplies blood to the posterior part of the cortex of the temporal lobe in the brain.
