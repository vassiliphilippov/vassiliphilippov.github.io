water wagon

noun
wagon for carrying and distributing water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ ˌwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌwæɡən / (BrE)
a wagon or vehicle designed for carrying and distributing water, especially for supplying troops, workers, or for sprinkling roads to control dust

Examples

a water wagon supplies roads
During the drought, the water wagon provided much-needed supplies to the isolated camp.
The old water wagon rumbled down the street, sprinkling the dusty road as it went.
Workers lined up at the water wagon to fill their canteens before returning to the fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym – water cart: a cart for carrying water, often used for similar purposes
