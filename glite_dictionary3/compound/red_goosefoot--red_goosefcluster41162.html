red goosefoot

noun
a Eurasian weed with reddish stems
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈguːsfʊt /; / ɹɛd ˈɡusˌfʊt / (AmE), / rɛd ˈɡuːsfʊt /; / ɹɛd ˈɡuːs.fʊt / (BrE)
