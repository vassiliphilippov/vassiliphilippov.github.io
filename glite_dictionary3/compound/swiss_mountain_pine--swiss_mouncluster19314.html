Swiss mountain pine

noun
shrubby pine tree of central Europe
Pronunciation: / swɪs ˈmaʊntn paɪn / (AmE), / swɪs ˈmaʊntən paɪn / (BrE)
A low, shrubby pine tree native to central Europe, having short, bright green needles in pairs of two; Pinus mugo.
