night rider

noun
member of secret vigilante group in post–Civil War U.S. South
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪt ˌraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt ˌraɪdə / (BrE)
a member of a secret, usually mounted, group in the southern United States after the Civil War, who engaged in acts of intimidation and revenge, often through violence or threats
