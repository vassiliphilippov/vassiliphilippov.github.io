apogee trajectory

noun
spacecraft's path at highest orbit point
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəˌdʒi trəˈdʒɛktəri / (AmE), / ˈapədʒi trəˈdʒɛktəri / (BrE)
the path followed by a spacecraft as it passes through the highest point in its orbit around a body
