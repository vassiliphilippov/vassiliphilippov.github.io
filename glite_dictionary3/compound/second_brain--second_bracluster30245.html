second brain

noun
tool or method aiding memory and organization
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd breɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd breɪn / (BrE)
a system, device, or method used to store information and assist with memory or tasks as an extension of the mind

Examples

digital second brain
For many people, a smartphone serves as a second brain that stores contacts, appointments, and ideas.
He relies on his laptop as a second brain to organize his busy schedule.
My digital notes have become a second brain for me, capturing everything I need to remember.
