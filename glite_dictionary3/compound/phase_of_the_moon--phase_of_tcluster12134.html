phase of the moon

noun
a recurring stage of the Moon's appearance
Pronunciation: / feɪz əv ðə mun / (AmE), / feɪz əv ðə muːn / (BrE)
a particular stage in the recurring cycle of appearances of the Moon, such as new moon, first quarter, or full moon

Examples

phase of the moon changes
During the full phase of the moon, the landscape appeared almost as bright as day.
Farmers used to track the phase of the moon to determine the best time for planting.
The phase of the moon affects how much light is visible at night.
