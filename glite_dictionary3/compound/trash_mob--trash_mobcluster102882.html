trash mob

noun
weak enemies in games
Pronunciation: / træʃ mɑb / (AmE), / træʃ mɒb / (BrE)
(in video games) a group or wave of weak, low‑level enemies that are easy to defeat and are typically not the main threat.

Examples

fight a trash mob in game
Before the boss fight, we had to clear a dozen weak trash mobs spawning from the room.
The raid leader told everyone to ignore the trash mob and focus on the patrol in the courtyard.
Grinding experience, she killed several trash mobs near the village until she reached level twenty.

Related Terms

  • synonym – minion: a weak or subordinate enemy or follower, often used to describe disposable foes in games
  • synonym – mook: slang for common, easily defeated enemies in video games or fiction
