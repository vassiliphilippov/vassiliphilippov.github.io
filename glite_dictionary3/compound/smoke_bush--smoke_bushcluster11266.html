smoke bush

noun
Australian shrub with white woolly flowers
Pronunciation: / smoʊk bʊʃ / (AmE), / sməʊk bʊʃ / (BrE)
any of several shrubs of the genus Conospermum, typically bearing panicles of mostly white, woolly flowers native to Australia
