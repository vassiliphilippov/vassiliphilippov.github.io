meadow pipit

noun
a small Eurasian songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊ ˈpɪpɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ˈpɪpɪt / (BrE)
a small brown-and-white songbird (Anthus pratensis) commonly found in northern and central Europe and Asia, known for its distinctive call and streaked plumage
