panic disorder

noun
mental health disorder with panic attacks
Pronunciation: / ˈpænɪk dɪˌsɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈpænɪk dɪˌsɔːdə / (BrE)
A mental health condition marked by recurrent, unexpected panic attacks, often accompanied by intense fear and physical symptoms.

Examples

panic disorder symptoms
People living with panic disorder may avoid places where they've had attacks before.
