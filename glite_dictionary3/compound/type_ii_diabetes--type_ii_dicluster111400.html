type II diabetes

noun
form of diabetes mellitus in adults
Pronunciation: / taɪp tuː ˌdaɪəˈbitiz / (AmE), / taɪp tuː ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz / (BrE)
a mild form of diabetes mellitus that develops mainly in adults and is typically managed by diet and medication rather than insulin injections

Examples

type II diabetes diagnosis
Advancements in medication have improved life for many living with type II diabetes.
My father was diagnosed with type II diabetes after years of unhealthy eating habits.
People with type II diabetes are often advised to exercise regularly to help control their blood sugar.
