kerching sound

noun
sound of coins or cash register
Pronunciation: / kərˈtʃɪŋ saʊnd / (AmE), / kəˈtʃɪŋ saʊnd / (BrE)
a sound resembling the clinking of coins or the ring of a cash register, typically associated with receiving money

Examples

the kerching sound of money
Every time they closed a sale, the satisfying kerching sound echoed through the busy store.
He paused for a moment, listening to the familiar kerching sound coming from the slot machine.
The shopkeeper smiled at the kerching sound as another customer made a purchase.
