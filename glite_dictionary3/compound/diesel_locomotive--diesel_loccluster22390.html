diesel locomotive

noun
locomotive powered by a diesel engine
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːzəl loʊˈkoʊmətɪv / (AmE), / ˈdiːzəl ləʊˈkəʊmətɪv / (BrE)
A railway locomotive powered by a diesel engine rather than by steam or electricity.

Examples

In the museum, you can see a restored diesel locomotive from the 1950s.
Many short-haul trains still rely on a diesel locomotive because some tracks are not electrified.
