yam bean

noun
edible Central American tuberous plant
Pronunciation: / jæm bin /; / jæm ˌbiːn / (AmE), / jæm biːn /; / jam ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a climbing or twining plant native to the Americas, grown for its large, edible tuberous roots and sometimes for other uses such as producing rotenone, oils, or enriching soil
