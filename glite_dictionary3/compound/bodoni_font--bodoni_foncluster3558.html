Bodoni font

noun
a high-contrast serif typeface by Bodoni
Pronunciation: / boʊˈdoʊni fɑnt / (AmE), / bəˈdəʊni fɒnt / (BrE)
a serif typeface with high contrast between thick and thin strokes, hairline serifs, and a regular, geometric shape, designed by Giambattista Bodoni in the late 18th century

Examples

Graphic designers often choose Bodoni font for projects that require a sense of sophistication.
