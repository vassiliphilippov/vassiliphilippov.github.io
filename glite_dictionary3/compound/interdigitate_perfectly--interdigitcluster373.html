to interdigitate perfectly

verb
to fit together seamlessly
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈdɪdʒɪˌteɪt ˈpɜrfɪktli / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdɪdʒɪteɪt ˈpɜːfɪktli / (BrE)
to fit together seamlessly, as the fingers of two hands might; to merge or combine parts so that they mesh exactly without gaps
