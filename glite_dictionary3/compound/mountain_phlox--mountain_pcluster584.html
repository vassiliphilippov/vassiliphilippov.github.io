mountain phlox

noun
a perennial flowering plant used as ground cover
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən floʊks / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn flɒks / (BrE)
a low, tufted perennial phlox with needle-like evergreen leaves and pink or white flowers, native to the United States and widely cultivated as a ground cover
