budget constraint

noun
limit on money that can be spent
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌdʒɪt kənˌstreɪnt / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪt kənˌstreɪnt / (BrE)
a limit on the amount of money that can be spent, often because of available financial resources
