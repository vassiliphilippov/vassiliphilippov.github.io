finishing school

noun
private school for girls, focusing on social and cultural training
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
a private school for girls that specializes in teaching social graces, cultural activities, and etiquette to prepare them for society

Examples
