fake book

noun
imitation book for display
Pronunciation: / feɪk bʊk / (AmE), / feɪk bʊk / (BrE)
an object made to look like a book, used as a decoration or to give the appearance of having a library

Examples

fake book on a shelf
She discovered that the beautiful leather-bound tomes were actually fake books hiding storage compartments.
The interior designer used fake books to give the office a scholarly atmosphere without buying real ones.
To impress guests, he filled the shelves with fake books that looked like antique volumes.
