snow angel

noun
shape made in snow by moving limbs
Pronunciation: / snoʊ ˈeɪndʒəl / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈeɪndʒəl / (BrE)
a shape made in the snow by lying on one's back and moving the arms and legs outward and inward to create the impression of an angel

Examples

make a snow angel
As a child, she loved making snow angels after every fresh snowfall.
He lay down in the yard and created a perfect snow angel beside his friends.
The field was dotted with dozens of snow angels from playful children.
