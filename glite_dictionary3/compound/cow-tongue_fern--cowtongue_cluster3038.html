cow-tongue fern

noun
type of fern with strap-shaped leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊ tʌŋ ˌfɝn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ tʌŋ ˌfɜːn / (BrE)
a fern with pale yellow-green, strap-shaped leaves, found as an epiphyte or on the ground from Florida to Uruguay
