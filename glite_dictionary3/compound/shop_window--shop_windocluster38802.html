shop window

noun
window at the front of a shop displaying goods
Pronunciation: / ʃɑp ˈwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ʃɒp ˈwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a window at the front of a shop, facing the street, used to display goods for sale

Examples

decorate the shop window
Children pressed their noses against the shop window, fascinated by the holiday decorations.
Every season, the shop window features a new collection of clothes and accessories.
The bakery arranged its cakes beautifully in the shop window to attract customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – display window: window of a shop displaying goods for sale
  • synonym – showcase: glass container used for displaying items in a shop
