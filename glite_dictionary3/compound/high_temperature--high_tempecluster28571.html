high temperature

noun
elevated body temperature; fever
Pronunciation: / haɪ ˈtɛmpɚətʃɚ / (AmE), / haɪ ˈtɛmpərətʃə / (BrE)
a body temperature that is above the normal range, usually indicating fever or illness

Examples

have a high temperature
If your child has a high temperature for more than three days, contact a doctor.
She stayed home from work because her high temperature made her feel weak and dizzy.
Taking acetaminophen can help reduce a high temperature during a cold or flu.
