credit system

noun
system allowing purchases on credit
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪt ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a system in which purchases can be made on credit, allowing payment to be deferred until a later date

Examples

credit system for shopping
After switching to a credit system, the company saw an increase in sales.
Many universities have a credit system to allow students flexibility in paying tuition.
The store operates on a credit system, allowing customers to pay for goods at a later date.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cash system: a system where purchases must be paid for at the time of transaction
  • synonym – installment plan: a system for paying for goods in parts over time
