eleventh cranial nerve

noun
accessory nerve; eleventh cranial nerve
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜrv / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛvənθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜːv / (BrE)
the accessory nerve; the eleventh cranial nerve that arises from cranial and spinal roots and controls certain neck and shoulder muscles
