Sango language

noun
trade language of Central Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːŋˌɡoʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsaŋɡəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a trade language widely spoken in the Central African Republic and parts of neighboring countries, especially Chad, serving as a lingua franca in the region.
