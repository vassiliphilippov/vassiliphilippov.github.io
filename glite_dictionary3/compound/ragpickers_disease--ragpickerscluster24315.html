ragpicker's disease

noun
a respiratory form of anthrax infection
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡˌpɪkərz dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈræɡˌpɪkəz dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
a form of anthrax infection contracted by inhaling dust that contains Bacillus anthracis, characterized by respiratory symptoms followed by severe cardiovascular collapse
