physical rehabilitation

noun
restoring physical abilities after injury or illness
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˌriːhəˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkəl ˌriːhəˌbɪlɪˈteɪʃn / (BrE)
the process of helping people regain or improve their physical abilities, especially after illness, injury, or disability

Examples

physical rehabilitation after injury
After his accident, Tom underwent months of physical rehabilitation to regain the ability to walk.
Innovations in physical rehabilitation have greatly improved recovery outcomes for patients with spinal injuries.
Many hospitals offer specialized programs in physical rehabilitation for stroke survivors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – physical therapy: treatment using physical methods like exercise and massage
  • synonym – rehabilitation: the action of restoring someone to health or normal life
