hemianopic scotoma

noun
blind spot in half the visual field
Pronunciation: / ˌhiː.mi.əˈnɑː.pɪk skoʊˈtoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˌhiː.mi.əˈnɒ.pɪk skəʊˈtəʊ.mə / (BrE)
