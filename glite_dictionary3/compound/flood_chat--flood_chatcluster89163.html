to flood chat

verb
overwhelm chat with excessive messages
Pronunciation: / flʌd tʃæt / (AmE), / flʌd tʃat / (BrE)
to overwhelm a chat or messaging service by sending an excessive number of messages, making it difficult for others to participate or read

Examples

to flood chat with messages
After the announcement, excited fans began to flood chat with emojis and gifs.
Please don't flood chat with repeated questions during the meeting.
The moderator warned users not to flood chat or they would be muted.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spam: irrelevant or unsolicited messages sent over the internet, often repeatedly
