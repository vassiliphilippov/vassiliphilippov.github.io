storage medium

noun
device or material for storing information
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrɪdʒ ˈmiːdiəm / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ ˈmiːdiəm / (BrE)
A device or material used to store digital or analog information, such as a hard drive, CD, or flash drive.

Examples

storage medium for files
Cloud services increasingly act as a remote storage medium for personal files.
The hard drive is a common storage medium used in most computers.
You should choose a storage medium with enough capacity for all your photos and videos.
