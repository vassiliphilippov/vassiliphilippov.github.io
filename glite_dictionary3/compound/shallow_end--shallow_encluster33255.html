shallow end

noun
part of a pool with little depth
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæloʊ ɛnd / (AmE), / ˈʃaləʊ end / (BrE)
the part of a swimming pool where the water is not deep, typically used by beginners or children

Examples

children in the shallow end
