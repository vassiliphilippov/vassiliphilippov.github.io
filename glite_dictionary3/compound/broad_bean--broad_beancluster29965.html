broad bean

noun
plant grown for large edible seeds
Pronunciation: / brɔd bin /; / brɔd ˈbin / (AmE), / brɔːd biːn /; / brɔːd ˈbiːn / (BrE)
an upright plant (Vicia faba) grown for its large, flat, edible seeds and sometimes used as animal fodder
