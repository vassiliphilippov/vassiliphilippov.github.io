stand mixer

noun
electric kitchen appliance for mixing
Pronunciation: / stænd ˈmɪksər / (AmE), / stænd ˈmɪksə / (BrE)
an electric kitchen appliance with a motor mounted on a stand, used for mixing, beating, or kneading ingredients

Examples

stand mixer for dough
A high-quality stand mixer can make baking much easier and more efficient.
