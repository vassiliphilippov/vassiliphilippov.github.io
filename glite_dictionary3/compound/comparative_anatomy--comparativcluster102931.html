comparative anatomy

noun
study of anatomical differences between species
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpɛrətɪv əˈnætəmi / (AmE), / kəmˌparətɪv əˈnatəmi / (BrE)
the scientific study of similarities and differences in the anatomical structures of different species of animals
