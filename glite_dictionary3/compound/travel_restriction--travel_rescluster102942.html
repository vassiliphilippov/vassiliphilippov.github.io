travel restriction

noun
limit placed on ability to travel
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəl rɪˈstrɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl rɪˈstrɪkʃən / (BrE)
an official or legal limit or control placed on the ability of people to travel from one place to another, often for health, security, or political reasons

Examples

COVID travel restriction
After the unrest, a travel restriction was imposed on movement within the city limits.
Due to the pandemic, several travel restrictions were implemented, affecting millions of people worldwide.
The government announced a new travel restriction for travelers from certain countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ban: an official or legal prohibition
  • synonym – restriction: a limiting condition or measure
  • synonym – travel permit: an official document authorizing travel
