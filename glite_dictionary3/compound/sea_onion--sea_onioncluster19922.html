sea onion

noun
Mediterranean plant with a medicinal bulb
Pronunciation: / si ˈʌn.jən / (AmE), / si ˈʌn.jən / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant with dense spikes of small white flowers, known for its bulb used in traditional medicine.
