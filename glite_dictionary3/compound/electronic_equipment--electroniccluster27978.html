electronic equipment

noun
devices using controlled electron flow
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ɪˈkwɪpmənt / (BrE)
Devices or apparatus that use the controlled movement of electrons, especially through gases, vacuums, or semiconductors, for their operation.
