feral woman

noun
a woman not socialized or raised by society
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrəl ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfɪərəl ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who has not been socialized, often having lived apart from human society and lacking typical social skills.

Examples

a feral woman in the wild
After years lost in the forest, she was considered a feral woman by the villagers.
Legends about a feral woman roaming the wilderness have fascinated people for generations.
The novel explores the life of a feral woman who struggles to adapt to modern society.

Related Terms

  • antonym – civilized woman: a woman who is socialized and conforms to societal norms
  • synonym – wild child: a child who has lived isolated from human contact
