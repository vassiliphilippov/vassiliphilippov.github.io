breathing space

noun
legal pause on debt enforcement
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːðɪŋ speɪs / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ speɪs / (BrE)
a legally protected period, provided by debt-relief schemes or statute, during which certain debt obligations are paused and most creditor enforcement actions are suspended.

Examples

legal breathing space for debts
The new law gives struggling households a twelve-week breathing space from creditor enforcement.
During the breathing space, lenders cannot start or continue most debt collection and enforcement actions.
He applied for a court-ordered breathing space to give him time to seek debt advice and restructure payments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – debt respite: a temporary relief period during which debt repayments or enforcement are paused
  • synonym – moratorium: a legally authorized temporary suspension of activity, such as enforcement or payments
