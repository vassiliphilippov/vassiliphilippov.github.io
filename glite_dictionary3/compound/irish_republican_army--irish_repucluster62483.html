Irish Republican Army

noun
Irish nationalist paramilitary group
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑːrmi / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪʃ rɪˈpʌblɪkən ˈɑːmi / (BrE)
a paramilitary organization of Irish nationalists that campaigned, often through violence, for the end of British rule in Northern Ireland and the reunification of Ireland
