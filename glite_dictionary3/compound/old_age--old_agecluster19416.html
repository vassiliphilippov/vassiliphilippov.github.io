old age

noun
later stage of life
Pronunciation: / oʊld eɪdʒ / (AmE), / əʊld eɪdʒ / (BrE)
The period of life when a person is elderly, typically after middle age, often associated with retirement and declining health or abilities.

Examples

old age brings wisdom
After retiring, she found new hobbies to enjoy in her old age.
Healthy habits in youth can have a positive impact during old age.
Many people look forward to spending their old age with family and friends.
