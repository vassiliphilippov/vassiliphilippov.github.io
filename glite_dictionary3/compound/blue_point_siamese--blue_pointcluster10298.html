blue point Siamese

noun
Siamese cat with bluish-grey points
Pronunciation: / bluː pɔɪnt saɪˈæmiz / (AmE), / bluː pɔɪnt saɪˈæmiːz / (BrE)
A Siamese cat variety with a pale bluish-cream body and dark bluish-grey points (ears, face, paws, and tail).
