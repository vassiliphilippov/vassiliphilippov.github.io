brand name

noun
name given to a product or service
Pronunciation: / brænd neɪm / (AmE), / brænd neɪm / (BrE)
The name given by a manufacturer or organization to a particular product or service to distinguish it from others.

Examples

brand name product
Coca-Cola is a globally recognized brand name.
Many people choose a product simply because of its brand name.
The company spent millions developing a brand name that would appeal to young consumers.
