field poppy

noun
annual European poppy with red flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˈpɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
an annual European poppy (Papaver rhoeas) with bright red flowers, commonly found in grain fields and often cultivated as an ornamental plant
