sprocket wheel

noun
toothed wheel for engaging a chain
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɑkɪt wil / (AmE), / ˈsprɒkɪt wiːl / (BrE)
A thin wheel with teeth that engage with a chain, commonly used to transmit rotary motion in bicycles, motorcycles, and machinery.

Examples

sprocket wheel on a bicycle
Maintenance of the sprocket wheel is essential for ensuring smooth operation of the conveyor system.
Most motorcycles use a sprocket wheel to drive the rear wheel via a chain.
The bicycle chain slipped off the sprocket wheel and had to be adjusted.
