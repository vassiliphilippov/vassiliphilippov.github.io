Old Nick

proper noun
the Devil; chief spirit of evil
Pronunciation: / oʊld nɪk / (AmE), / əʊld nɪk / (BrE)
a colloquial name for the Devil; the chief spirit of evil in Judeo-Christian and Islamic belief, regarded as the tempter of mankind and ruler of Hell

Examples

Old Nick tempts people
Folktales often describe Old Nick tempting people to stray from the path of virtue.
My grandmother used to warn us that Old Nick lurked in dark corners at night.
Some superstitions hold that meeting Old Nick in a dream is a bad omen.
