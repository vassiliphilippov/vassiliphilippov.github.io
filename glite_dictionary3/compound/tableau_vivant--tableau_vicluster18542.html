tableau vivant

noun
posed group representing a scene
Pronunciation: / ˌtæbloʊ viˈvɑn / (AmE), / ˌtabləʊ viˈvãː / (BrE)
A group of people artistically posed and often motionless to represent a scene or painting.

Examples

tableau vivant on stage
During the festival, participants formed a tableau vivant depicting a famous historical scene.
The actors remained perfectly still to create a striking tableau vivant at the end of the play.
The museum event included a tableau vivant recreating a classical painting.
