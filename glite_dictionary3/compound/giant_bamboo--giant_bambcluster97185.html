giant bamboo

noun
very large tropical bamboo plant
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt bæmˈbu / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt bamˈbuː / (BrE)
a very large tropical bamboo native to Southeast Asia, noted for its tough hollow stems that can resemble tree trunks
