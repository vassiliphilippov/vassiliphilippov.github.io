dark chocolate

noun
high-cocoa chocolate with little or no milk
Pronunciation: / dɑrk ˈtʃɑk.lət / (AmE), / dɑːk ˈtʃɒk.lət / (BrE)
a type of chocolate with a high percentage of cocoa solids, little or no milk, and less sugar than milk chocolate
