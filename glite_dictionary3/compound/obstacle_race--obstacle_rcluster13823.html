obstacle race

noun
race requiring competitors to overcome obstacles
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbstəkəl ˌreɪs / (AmE), / ˈɒbstəkl ˌreɪs / (BrE)
a race in which competitors must overcome various physical obstacles on the course

Examples

obstacle race at sports day
Jamie participated in the obstacle race at the annual school sports day.
The obstacle race included climbing walls, crawling under nets, and jumping over hurdles.
Training for an obstacle race requires both speed and agility.
