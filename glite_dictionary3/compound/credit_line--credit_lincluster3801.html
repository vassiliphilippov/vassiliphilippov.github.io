credit line

noun
line giving author's name in publication
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪt laɪn / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪt laɪn / (BrE)
a line in a newspaper, magazine, or other publication that gives the name of the writer of an article or story

Examples

Every photo in the exhibition had a credit line identifying the photographer.
