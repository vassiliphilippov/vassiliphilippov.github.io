unerring ability

noun
flawless capability; infallible skill
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɜrɪŋ əˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ʌnˈɜːrɪŋ əˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
an infallible or flawless capability to do something, without making mistakes or errors

Examples

unerring ability to spot mistakes
Her unerring ability to find typos made her an excellent editor.
His unerring ability to remember faces made him a valuable witness.
The detective's unerring ability to solve complex cases impressed everyone on the force.
