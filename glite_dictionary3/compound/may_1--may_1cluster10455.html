May 1

noun
first day of May, May Day festival or labor holiday
Pronunciation: / meɪ fɜrst / (AmE), / meɪ fɜːst / (BrE)
The first day of May, traditionally celebrated as May Day in many countries, marked by festivals celebrating spring or, in some countries, as a holiday honoring labor and workers.
