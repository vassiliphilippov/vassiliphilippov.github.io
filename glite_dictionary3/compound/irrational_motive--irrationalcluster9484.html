irrational motive

noun
motivation inconsistent with logic or reason
Pronunciation: / ɪˌræʃənəl ˈmoʊtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˌræʃənəl ˈməʊtɪv / (BrE)
a reason or motivation for doing something that does not follow logical or reasonable thought
