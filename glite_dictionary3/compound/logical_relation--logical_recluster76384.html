logical relation

noun
relation between propositions in logic
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːdʒɪkəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪkəl rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a relation between two or more propositions, such as implication, equivalence, or contradiction, that is determined by logic

logical relation between statements
