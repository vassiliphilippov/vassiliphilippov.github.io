anastigmatic lens

noun
lens correcting astigmatism
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˌstɪɡˈmætɪk lɛnz / (AmE), / ˌanəˌstɪɡˈmatɪk lɛnz / (BrE)
a lens designed to correct astigmatism by eliminating optical distortions, producing sharp images across the entire field of view

Examples

anastigmatic lens for sharp images
Optical engineers often use an anastigmatic lens in high-precision instruments to correct image distortions.
She replaced the standard lens with an anastigmatic lens to achieve sharper results when photographing distant objects.
The camera is equipped with an anastigmatic lens for crisp, distortion-free photographs.
