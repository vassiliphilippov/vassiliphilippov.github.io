hunting crop

noun
short whip used in horseback hunting
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntɪŋ krɑp / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ krɒp / (BrE)
a short whip with a thong at the end, used in hunting on horseback, often with a handle designed to open gates

Examples

hold a hunting crop
She skillfully used her hunting crop to open the gate without dismounting.
The rider carried a hunting crop as he guided his horse across the field.
Traditionally, a hunting crop is an essential accessory for fox hunting.
