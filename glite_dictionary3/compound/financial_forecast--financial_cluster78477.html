financial forecast

noun
estimate of future financial performance
Pronunciation: / fəˈnænʃəl ˈfɔrˌkæst / (AmE), / faɪˈnanʃəl ˈfɔːkɑːst / (BrE)
an estimate of a company's future financial position, income, expenses, and cash flow based on expected conditions and assumptions
