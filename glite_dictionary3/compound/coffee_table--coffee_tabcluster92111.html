coffee table

noun
low table for living rooms
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑfi ˌteɪbl / (AmE), / ˈkɒfi ˌteɪbl / (BrE)
A low table, typically placed in front of a sofa, used for holding drinks, magazines, and decorative items.

Examples

coffee table in the living room
A beautiful vase of fresh flowers sat in the center of the coffee table.
She placed the stack of magazines neatly on the coffee table in the living room.
We spilled juice all over the coffee table during the party last night.
