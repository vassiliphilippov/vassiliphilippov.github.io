mind reader

noun
AI or software that predicts intentions
Pronunciation: / \u02c8ma\u026and \u02ccri\u02d0d\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8ma\u026and \u02ccri\u02d0d\u0259 / (BrE)
(informal) A non-human agent (especially an AI system, app, or software) that predicts a person's thoughts, preferences, or intentions by analysing their data and behaviour.

Examples

AI mind reader app
My phone feels like a mind reader, suggesting restaurants before I even search online.
Advertisers claim their tools are mind readers, showing ads based on people’s browsing and purchase data.
The new smart assistant acted like a mind reader, scheduling exactly what I needed each day.

Related Terms

  • synonym – AI assistant: software that helps users by performing tasks and often predicting user needs
  • synonym – telepath: a person who is said to be able to read others' thoughts directly
