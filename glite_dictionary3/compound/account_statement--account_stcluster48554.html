account statement

noun
summary of recent financial transactions
Pronunciation: / əˈkaʊnt ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an official summary of recent financial transactions and the resulting balance in an account, usually sent by a bank or other financial institution

Examples

account statement from bank
I requested a printed copy of my account statement for my records.
Please review your account statement for any unauthorized transactions.
The bank sends my account statement at the beginning of each month.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bank statement: a printed record of the balance in a bank account and the amounts that have been paid into and withdrawn from it
