bur grass

noun
prickly grass of the genus Cenchrus
Pronunciation: / bɝ ˌɡræs / (AmE), / bɜː ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A type of grass from the genus Cenchrus, characterized by having spiny or prickly burs.

Examples
