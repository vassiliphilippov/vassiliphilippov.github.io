slender-winged

adjective
having narrow or thin wings
Pronunciation: / ˈslɛndɚ wɪŋd / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə wɪŋd / (BrE)
having narrow or thin wings; characterized by wings that are slim in proportion to their length.

Examples

slender-winged gull
Among the many species, the slender-winged gull is easily recognized by its narrow wings.
That slender-winged butterfly is rarely seen in this region.
The slender-winged dragonflies darted gracefully over the pond.
