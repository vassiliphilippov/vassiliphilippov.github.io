Red Cross

proper noun
international humanitarian organization
Pronunciation: / ɹɛd ˈkrɑs / (AmE), / ɹɛd ˈkrɒs / (BrE)
an international humanitarian organization that provides emergency assistance, disaster relief, and education, especially in situations of war and disaster

Examples

the Red Cross helps people
Many countries have their own Red Cross organizations that work together internationally.
She volunteers at the Red Cross every weekend, helping organize blood drives.
The Red Cross provided food and shelter to those affected by the earthquake.
