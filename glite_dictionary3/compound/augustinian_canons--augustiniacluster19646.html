Augustinian Canons

noun
religious order of priests under St. Augustine's rule
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈgʌstɪniən ˈkænənz / (AmE), / ɔːˈɡʌstɪnɪən ˈkanənz / (BrE)
a religious order of priests who live communally under the Rule of St. Augustine and share all property in common
