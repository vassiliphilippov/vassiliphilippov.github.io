Roman nose

noun
prominent, slightly curved nose
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊmən noʊz / (AmE), / ˈrəʊmən nəʊz / (BrE)
A nose with a prominent, slightly curved bridge, resembling profiles seen in ancient Roman statues.

Examples

a Roman nose profile
His Roman nose gave his profile a striking and noble appearance.
Many portraits of Roman emperors feature a distinct Roman nose.
She inherited her grandfather's Roman nose, which made her stand out in a crowd.
