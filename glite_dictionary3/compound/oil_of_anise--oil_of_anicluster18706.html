oil of anise

noun
essential oil from anise seeds
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl əv ˈænɪs / (AmE), / ɔɪl əv ˈænɪs / (BrE)
An essential oil obtained from the seeds of the anise plant, with a pungent, liquorice-like aroma, used in flavoring and aromatherapy.

Examples

oil of anise flavoring candy
Oil of anise is often used as a natural remedy for digestive discomfort.
The baker added a few drops of oil of anise to the cake batter for a unique flavor.
The scent of oil of anise filled the kitchen as the cookies baked.
