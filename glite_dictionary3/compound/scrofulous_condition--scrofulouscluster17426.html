scrofulous condition

noun
disease with swollen lymph nodes
Pronunciation: / ˈskroʊ.fjələs kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈskrɒf.jʊ.ləs kənˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by swollen or enlarged lymph nodes, especially in the neck, often as a result of tuberculosis infection

Examples

Related Terms
