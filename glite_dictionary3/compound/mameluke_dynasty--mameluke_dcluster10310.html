Mameluke dynasty

proper noun
historical ruling dynasty of Egypt and Syria
Pronunciation: / ˈmæməˌluk ˈdaɪnəsti / (AmE), / ˈmaməljuːk ˈdɪnəsti / (BrE)
a dynasty of former slave soldiers who ruled Egypt and Syria from the 13th to early 16th centuries

Examples
