low tide

noun
lowest sea level in the tidal cycle
Pronunciation: / loʊ taɪd / (AmE), / ləʊ taɪd / (BrE)
the time when the sea is at its lowest level due to the tide

Examples

low tide in the morning
Fishing boats were stranded on the sand flats at low tide.
The harbor is difficult to access during low tide because the water is too shallow.
We walked along the beach at low tide and found many seashells.
