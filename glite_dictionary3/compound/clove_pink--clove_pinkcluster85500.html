clove pink

noun
fragrant, clove-scented flowering plant
Pronunciation: / kloʊv pɪŋk / (AmE), / kləʊv pɪŋk / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant (Dianthus caryophyllus) with pink to purple-red, clove-scented, often double flowers; widely cultivated in various colors and varieties.

fragrant clove pink flowers
Horticulturists value the clove pink for its vibrant color and scent.
