sparrow hawk

noun
small Eurasian or North African hawk
Pronunciation: / ˈspæroʊ hɑk / (AmE), / ˈspærəʊ hɔːk / (BrE)
A small hawk found in Eurasia and northern Africa, known for preying on small birds and insects.
