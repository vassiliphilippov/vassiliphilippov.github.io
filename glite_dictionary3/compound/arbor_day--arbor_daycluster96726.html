Arbor Day

noun
annual tree-planting holiday
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrbər ˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɑːbə ˌdeɪ / (BrE)
A day set aside, typically each year, for planting trees and raising awareness about the importance of trees and forests.

Examples

Arbor Day tree planting
Many schools organize activities for Arbor Day, such as planting saplings on campus.
On Arbor Day, volunteers distributed free trees to promote reforestation efforts.
Our town hosts an Arbor Day festival every spring to encourage residents to plant new trees.
