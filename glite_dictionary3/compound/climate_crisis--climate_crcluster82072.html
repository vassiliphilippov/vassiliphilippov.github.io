climate crisis

noun
urgent environmental emergency caused by climate change
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmət ˌkraɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmət ˌkraɪsɪs / (BrE)
a situation in which significant and rapid changes to the climate cause serious and widespread environmental and social problems

Examples

global climate crisis
Governments worldwide are under pressure to address the climate crisis with effective policies.
Many young activists are calling for immediate action to combat the climate crisis.
The climate crisis has led to increased awareness of sustainability and renewable energy.
