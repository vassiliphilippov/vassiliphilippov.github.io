porphyritic rock

noun
igneous rock with large crystals in finer matrix
Pronunciation: / ˌpɔrfəˈrɪtɪk rɑk / (AmE), / ˌpɔːfɪˈrɪtɪk rɒk / (BrE)
An igneous rock that contains large crystals (phenocrysts) embedded in a finer-grained matrix or groundmass.
