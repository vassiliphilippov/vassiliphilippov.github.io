mastiff bat

noun
type of bat with folded ears and small wings
Pronunciation: / ˈmæs.tɪf bæt / (AmE), / ˈmæs.tɪf bæt / (BrE)
a soft-furred, chocolate-brown bat with folded ears and small wings, known for sometimes running along the ground

Examples

wild mastiff bat
Researchers observed a mastiff bat roosting in a cave near the river.
The mastiff bat is known to occasionally run on the ground instead of just flying.
Unlike many other bats, mastiff bats often have folded ears and small wings.
