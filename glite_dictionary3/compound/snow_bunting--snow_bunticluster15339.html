snow bunting

noun
small white Arctic songbird
Pronunciation: / snoʊ ˈbʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈbʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
a small Arctic songbird (Plectrophenax nivalis) with predominantly white plumage, often found in northern regions and tundra.

Examples

snow bunting in tundra
A flock of snow buntings landed on the icy shoreline, their white feathers blending with the snow.
Birdwatchers often travel north in winter to catch a glimpse of the elusive snow bunting.
The snow bunting breeds in the Arctic tundra and migrates south during the colder months.
