river otter

noun
North American aquatic mammal
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvɚ ˌɑtɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvə ˌɒtə / (BrE)
a semi-aquatic mammal of North America, known for its playful behavior and found along rivers and lakeshores
