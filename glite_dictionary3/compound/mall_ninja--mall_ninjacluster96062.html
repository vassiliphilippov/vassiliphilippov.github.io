mall ninja

noun
overconfident amateur in tactical gear
Pronunciation: / mɔl ˈnɪndʒə / (AmE), / mɔːl ˈnɪndʒə / (BrE)
a humorous or mocking term for someone who behaves as if they are highly skilled in tactical or stealthy combat, especially in the context of shopping malls or online forums, often showing an exaggerated or unrealistic sense of expertise

Examples

wannabe mall ninja
He was called a mall ninja after bragging about his collection of replica swords.
Several online forums poke fun at mall ninjas who boast about their imaginary missions.
The self-proclaimed mall ninja strutted through the store in full tactical gear.
