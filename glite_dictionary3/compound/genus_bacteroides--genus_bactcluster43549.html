genus Bacteroides

noun
genus of gut bacteria in Bacteroidaceae
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs bæk.təˈrɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs bæk.təˈrɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Bacteroidaceae, consisting of Gram-negative, rod-shaped, anaerobic bacteria that do not produce endospores or pigments and are commonly found in the gut of humans and animals.
