absentee ballot

noun
ballot cast by mail when absent
Pronunciation: / ˌæb.sənˈtiː ˈbæl.ət / (AmE), / ˌæb.sənˈtiː ˈbæl.ət / (BrE)
A ballot cast by someone who is unable to be present at the polling place, typically submitted by mail before election day.

Examples

mail an absentee ballot
Many states allow citizens living abroad to participate in elections by using an absentee ballot.
She mailed her absentee ballot two weeks before the election to ensure it would arrive on time.
Voters who cannot get to the polls on election day often request an absentee ballot.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mail-in ballot: a ballot submitted by mail, especially as an alternative to in-person voting
