fitted sheet

noun
sheet with elastic edges for a mattress
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪtɪd ʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪd ʃiːt / (BrE)
A bed sheet with elastic edges designed to fit snugly over a mattress.

Examples

fitted sheet on a bed
