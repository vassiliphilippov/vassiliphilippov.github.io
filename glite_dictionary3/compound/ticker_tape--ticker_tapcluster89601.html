ticker tape

noun
digital scrolling information display
Pronunciation: / t6ak59r te6ap / (AmE), / t6ak59 te6ap / (BrE)
a continuous, horizontally scrolling display (usually electronic) that shows real-time information such as stock prices, headlines, or scores.

Examples

scrolling ticker tape display
The financial channel ran a scrolling ticker tape showing live stock prices across the bottom.
During the broadcast, a bright ticker tape displayed breaking headlines and market updates.
She customized the website to include a compact ticker tape with sports scores and weather alerts.
