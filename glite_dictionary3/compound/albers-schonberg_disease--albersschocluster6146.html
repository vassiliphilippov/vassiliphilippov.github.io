Albers-Schonberg disease

noun
an inherited bone density disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈɑlbərz ˈʃoʊn.bɜrɡ dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈal.bəz ˈʃɒn.bɜːɡ dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
an inherited disorder marked by increased bone density, which in severe cases can result in the obliteration of the bone marrow cavity

Examples
