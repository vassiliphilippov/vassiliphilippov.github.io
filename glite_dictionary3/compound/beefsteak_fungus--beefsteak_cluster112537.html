beefsteak fungus

noun
a reddish edible fungus resembling raw meat
Pronunciation: / ˈbifˌsteɪk ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈbiːfsteɪk ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a type of edible fungus with a reddish, meat-like cap, commonly found in some regions and known for its resemblance to raw beef
