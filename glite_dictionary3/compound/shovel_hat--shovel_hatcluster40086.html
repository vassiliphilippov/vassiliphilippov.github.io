shovel hat

noun
traditional stiff clerical hat (Britain)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvəl hæt / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvəl hæt / (BrE)
a stiff, broad-brimmed hat with a rounded crown and a brim shaped like a shovel, traditionally worn by British clergymen or as a wide-brimmed sun hat in historical contexts
