Rorschach test

noun
psychological inkblot test
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔrʃɑk tɛst / (AmE), / ˈrɔːʃæk tɛst / (BrE)
a psychological test in which a person is shown inkblot images and asked to describe what they see, used to understand their thoughts, feelings, or personality
