southern charm

noun
appealing qualities of southern US culture
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌðərn tʃɑrm / (AmE), / ˈsʌðən tʃɑːm / (BrE)
the attractive and hospitable qualities considered typical of the southern United States, such as friendliness, warmth, and politeness

Examples

southern charm and hospitality
Her grandmother's southern charm made every guest feel instantly at home.
The town's southern charm was evident in its tree-lined streets and friendly community events.
Visitors often mention the southern charm they experience when traveling through Georgia and the Carolinas.
