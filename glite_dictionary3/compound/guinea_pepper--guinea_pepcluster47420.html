Guinea pepper

noun
West African plant and spice
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪni ˌpɛpər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪni ˌpɛpə / (BrE)
a West African plant (Aframomum melegueta) and its pungent seeds, used as a spice and in traditional medicine; also called grains of paradise
