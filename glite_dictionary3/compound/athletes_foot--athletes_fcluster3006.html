athlete's foot

noun
fungal infection of the feet
Pronunciation: / ˈæθˌlit͡s fʊt / (AmE), / ˈæθliːts fʊt / (BrE)
a fungal infection of the skin on the feet, especially between the toes, causing itching, scaling, and sometimes blistering

Examples

athlete's foot infection
Athlete's foot is a common problem among swimmers and athletes who use communal showers.
