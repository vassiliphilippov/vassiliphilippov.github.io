theropod dinosaur

noun
carnivorous dinosaur with strong hind legs
Pronunciation: / ˈθɛr.əˌpɑd ˈdaɪ.nəˌsɔr / (AmE), / ˈθɛr.ə.pɒd ˈdʌɪ.nə.sɔː / (BrE)
any of a group of mostly carnivorous dinosaurs from the Triassic to Cretaceous periods, characterized by short forelimbs and strong hind legs used for walking or running.
