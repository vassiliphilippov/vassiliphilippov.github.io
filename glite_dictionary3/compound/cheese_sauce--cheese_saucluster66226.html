cheese sauce

noun
sauce made with grated cheese
Pronunciation: / tʃiːz sɔs / (AmE), / tʃiːz sɔːs / (BrE)
A creamy white sauce made with grated cheese, typically used as a topping for dishes such as vegetables, pasta, or fish.

Examples

cheese sauce on pasta
A bowl of nachos isn't complete without a creamy cheese sauce for dipping.
She poured rich cheese sauce over the steamed broccoli for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for a homemade cheese sauce to be drizzled on the pasta.
