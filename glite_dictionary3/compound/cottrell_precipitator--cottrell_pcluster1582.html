Cottrell precipitator

noun
device for dust removal by electrostatic precipitation
Pronunciation: / kəˈtrɛl prɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪtər / (AmE), / kəˈtrɛl prɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪtə / (BrE)
an apparatus that removes dust particles from gases using electrostatic precipitation, especially in industrial processes
