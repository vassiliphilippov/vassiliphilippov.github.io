noseeum swarm

noun
group of tiny, biting insects
Pronunciation: / noʊˈsiəm swɔrm / (AmE), / nəʊˈsiːəm swɔːm / (BrE)
A group of very small, biting insects, often almost invisible, that gather in large numbers and can be very bothersome.

Examples

a noseeum swarm at dusk
A noseeum swarm appeared at dusk, forcing everyone to cover up with long sleeves.
I didn't notice the noseeum swarm until I started feeling the itchy bites.
We had to move indoors because a noseeum swarm made sitting outside impossible.
