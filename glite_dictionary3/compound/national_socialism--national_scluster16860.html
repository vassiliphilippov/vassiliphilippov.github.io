national socialism

noun
Nazi political ideology
Pronunciation: / ˌnæʃənəl ˈsoʊʃəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəl ˈsəʊʃəlɪzəm / (BrE)
A political ideology characterized by extreme nationalism, racism, dictatorial leadership, and expansionist aims, especially as practiced by the Nazi Party in Germany.
