high fashion

noun
exclusive, trend-setting clothing and style
Pronunciation: / haɪ ˈfæʃən / (AmE), / haɪ ˈfæʃən / (BrE)
the designing and making of exclusive, trend-setting clothing and accessories, often associated with top designers and luxury brands

Examples

high fashion runway show
Magazines closely follow the trends that emerge from the world of high fashion.
Paris remains a global center for high fashion collections and runway shows.
The designer is renowned for her work in high fashion and luxury eveningwear.
