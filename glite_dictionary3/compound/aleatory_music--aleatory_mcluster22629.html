aleatory music

noun
music involving elements of chance
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪliˌɛːtɔri ˈmjuzɪk / (AmE), / ˌælɪˈeɪtəri ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music in which some element of the composition is left to chance or to the performer's discretion during performance, often associated with certain experimental styles of the 20th century
