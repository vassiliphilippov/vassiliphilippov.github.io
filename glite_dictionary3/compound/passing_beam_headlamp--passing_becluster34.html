passing beam headlamp

noun
car headlamp with low/dipped beam
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ ˌbiːm ˈhɛdˌlæmp / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ ˌbiːm ˈhɛdˌlamp / (BrE)
A car headlamp that gives a dipped (low) beam to illuminate the road ahead without causing glare to other drivers.

Examples

passing beam headlamp replacement
She replaced her car’s passing beam headlamp after noticing it was not working properly.
The passing beam headlamp helps prevent glare for other drivers on the road.
You should switch to the passing beam headlamp when approaching oncoming traffic at night.
