anise seed

noun
liquorice-flavoured culinary seed
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪs siːd / (AmE), / ˈænɪs siːd / (BrE)
The small, liquorice-flavored seed of the anise plant, used in cooking, baking, and to flavour drinks and medicines.
