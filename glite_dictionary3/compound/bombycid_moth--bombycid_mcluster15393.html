bombycid moth

noun
Asian moth producing silk
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑmbɪsɪd mɑθ / (AmE), / ˈbɒmbɪsɪd mɒθ / (BrE)
A moderate-sized moth from Asia, whose larvae feed on mulberry leaves and produce silk.

Examples

bombycid moth produces silk
Farmers cultivate bombycid moths in controlled environments to harvest silk.
Larvae of the bombycid moth feed exclusively on mulberry leaves.
The bombycid moth is essential to the silk industry due to its larvae's silk-producing ability.
