spinal anaesthetic

noun
anaesthetic injected into the spine
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪnəl ænəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnəl ˌanəsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
an anaesthetic injected into the spine to induce numbness in the lower part of the body

Examples

receive a spinal anaesthetic
After receiving a spinal anaesthetic, the patient felt no sensation from the waist down.
Many women choose a spinal anaesthetic during childbirth for effective pain relief.
The surgeon recommended a spinal anaesthetic for the knee replacement surgery.
