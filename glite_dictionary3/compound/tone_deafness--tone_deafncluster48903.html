tone deafness

noun
insensitivity to social or emotional tone
Pronunciation: / to\u028an \u02c8d\u025bfn\u0259s / (AmE), / t\u0259\u028an \u02c8d\u025bfn\u0259s / (BrE)
Failure or inability to perceive, understand, or respond appropriately to the emotional tone, feelings, or social nuances in speech or situations, often causing insensitive or misaligned reactions.

Examples

tone deafness to feelings
Her tone deafness to colleagues' concerns damaged team morale during the crisis.
The politician's tone deafness about rising costs cost him voter support in the debate.
After the meeting she was criticized for tone deafness when praising budget cuts without empathy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – empathy: the ability to understand and share the feelings of others; opposite of being tone‑deaf socially
  • synonym – insensitivity: lack of awareness or concern for others' feelings or social context
