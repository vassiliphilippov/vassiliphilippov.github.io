conversation stopper

noun
remark ending conversation abruptly
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃən ˌstɑːpər / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən ˌstɒpə / (BrE)
a remark or statement that leaves others with no polite or easy way to respond
