smoking gun

noun
conclusive evidence of wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkɪŋ ˈɡʌn /; / ˈsmoʊkɪŋ ˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkɪŋ ˈɡʌn /; / ˈsməʊkɪŋ ˌɡʌn / (BrE)
a piece of evidence that clearly and indisputably proves someone has committed a crime or done something wrong
