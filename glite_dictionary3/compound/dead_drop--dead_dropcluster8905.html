dead drop

noun
secret location for covert information exchange
Pronunciation: / dɛd drɑp / (AmE), / dɛd drɒp / (BrE)
A secret location used for the covert exchange of information, especially between spies, so that neither party needs to meet in person.

Examples

dead drop location
Authorities discovered a dead drop in a hollow tree in the park.
The agent left the documents at the dead drop before sunrise.
Using a dead drop allowed the two spies to avoid ever meeting in person.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drop: a place where something is secretly left for another person to collect
