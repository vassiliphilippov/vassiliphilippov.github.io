peak season

noun
busiest, most expensive time for travel or business
Pronunciation: / pik ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / piːk ˈsiːzn̩ / (BrE)
the period when travel or business activity is at its highest and prices or rates are most expensive

Examples

peak season hotel prices
Hotel rooms are much more expensive during the peak season.
It's better to book flights early if you plan to travel in peak season.
Many attractions are crowded in peak season, so visiting off season can be more enjoyable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – high season: the period when activity or demand is at its highest (synonymous with 'peak season')
  • antonym – off season: the period when business activity or travel is lowest and prices are reduced
