resistance unit

noun
unit for measuring electrical resistance
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstəns ˈjunɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstəns ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
a unit used to measure electrical resistance, such as the ohm; the reciprocal of a unit of conductance
