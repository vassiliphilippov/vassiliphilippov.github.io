limited audit

noun
audit with restricted scope
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtɪd ˈɑːdɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪd ˈɔːdɪt / (BrE)
an audit with a scope restricted to specific periods or areas, such as certain accounts or transactions

Examples

limited audit of accounts
A limited audit can help identify issues in specific departments without the cost of a full review.
Due to budget constraints, the board approved a limited audit of the past six months.
The company underwent a limited audit focusing only on its international transactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – audit: a formal examination of financial accounts or records
  • antonym – full audit: an audit with no restrictions on scope
