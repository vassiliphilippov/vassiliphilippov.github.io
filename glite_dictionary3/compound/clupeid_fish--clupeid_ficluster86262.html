clupeid fish

noun
family of schooling food fishes (herrings, sardines, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈkluːpiɪd fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkluːpɪɪd fɪʃ / (BrE)
Any of a family of soft-finned schooling food fishes, such as herrings and sardines, typically found in the shallow waters of northern seas.
