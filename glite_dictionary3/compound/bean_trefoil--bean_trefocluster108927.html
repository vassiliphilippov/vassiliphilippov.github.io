bean trefoil

noun
shrub with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / bin ˈtrɔɪfɔɪl / (AmE), / biːn ˈtrɔɪfɔɪl / (BrE)
a shrub with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, producing backward curving seed pods; the leaves have a strong, unpleasant smell when crushed
