mother's boy

noun
boy/man overly attached to mother
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðərz bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəz bɔɪ / (BrE)
A boy or man who is excessively attached to his mother, sometimes regarded as lacking independence or traditional masculine interests.

Examples

a mother's boy at home
Everyone said Tom was a mother's boy because he called home every day.
Growing up as a mother's boy affected his confidence in social situations.
She worries her new boyfriend might be a mother's boy who can't make decisions on his own.
