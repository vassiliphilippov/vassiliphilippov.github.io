plain clothes detective

noun
detective in civilian clothes
Pronunciation: / pleɪn kloʊðz dɪˈtɛktɪv / (AmE), / pleɪn kləʊðz dɪˈtɛktɪv / (BrE)
a police detective who wears ordinary civilian clothing while on duty, rather than a uniform, to avoid drawing attention
