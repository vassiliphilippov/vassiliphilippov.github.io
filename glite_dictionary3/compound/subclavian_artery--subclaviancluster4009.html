subclavian artery

noun
artery supplying neck and arms
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈkleɪviən ˈɑːrˌtɛri / (AmE), / sʌbˈkleɪvɪən ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
either of two major arteries that carry blood to the neck, shoulders, and arms from the heart
