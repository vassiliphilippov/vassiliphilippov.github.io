horned lizard

noun
spiny lizard with hornlike head projections
Pronunciation: / hɔrnd ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
a small, spiny lizard found in western North America, with hornlike projections on its head and spiny scales on its body
