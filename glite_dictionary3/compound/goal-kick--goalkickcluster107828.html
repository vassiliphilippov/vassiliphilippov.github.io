goal-kick

noun
(rugby) attempt to score by kicking at goal
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊl kɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl kɪk / (BrE)
(in rugby) an attempt to score points by kicking the ball between the goalposts.
