sweet pepper

noun
mild, bell-shaped salad pepper
Pronunciation: / swit ˈpɛpɚ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
a plant that bears large, mild, thick-walled, usually bell-shaped fruits, commonly eaten raw or cooked as a vegetable

Examples

sweet pepper plant
A sweet pepper plant can produce dozens of bell-shaped fruits in a season.
She added chopped sweet pepper to the salad for extra color and crunch.
The recipe calls for both red and green sweet peppers.
