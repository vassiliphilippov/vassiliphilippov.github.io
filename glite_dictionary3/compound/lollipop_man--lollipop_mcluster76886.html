lollipop man

noun
man who helps children cross the road by a school
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːliˌpɑp mæn / (AmE), / ˈlɒl.i.pɒp mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to help children cross the road safely near a school, usually by holding up a circular sign to stop traffic

Examples

lollipop man helps children
Every morning, the lollipop man stands outside the school to keep the children safe.
The lollipop man raised his sign to stop the cars and let the children cross.
When the usual lollipop man was on holiday, a police officer filled in for him.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lollipop lady: woman employed to help children cross roads near schools
