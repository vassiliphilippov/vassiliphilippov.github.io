line of fate

noun
palm crease signifying destiny (palmistry)
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪn əv feɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪn əv feɪt / (BrE)
a crease on the palm, according to palmistry, believed to reveal information about a person's success or destiny

Examples

line of fate on palm
According to the palm reader, my line of fate suggests a prosperous career.
She traced the line of fate on his palm, predicting his future success.
Some people believe that the absence of a line of fate means a life influenced by external forces.
