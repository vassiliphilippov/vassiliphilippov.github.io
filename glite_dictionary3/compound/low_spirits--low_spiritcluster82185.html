low spirits

noun
mild sadness or depression
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˈspɪrɪts / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈspɪərɪts / (BrE)
a state of mild sadness or depression; a feeling of being slightly unhappy or downcast

Examples

in low spirits after failure
A rainy day often puts me in low spirits.
Despite his low spirits, he tried to remain hopeful about the future.
She was in low spirits after hearing the disappointing news.
