green monkey disease

noun
serious viral disease from green monkeys; caused by Marburg virus
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrin ˈmʌŋki dɪˌziz / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn ˈmʌŋki dɪˌziːz / (BrE)
a rare and often severe viral disease originally found in green monkeys, caused by the Marburg virus, which can be transmitted to humans and cause serious or fatal illness

Examples

green monkey disease outbreak
Laboratory workers developed symptoms of green monkey disease after accidental exposure to contaminated tissue.
