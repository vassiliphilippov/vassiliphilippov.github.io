single-reed instrument

noun
musical instrument with one vibrating reed
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl ˌɹid ˌɪn.strəˈmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˌriːd ˌɪn.strəˈmɛnt / (BrE)
A musical instrument that produces sound by sending air past a single reed, such as a clarinet or saxophone.
