home theatre

noun
system for movie-like experience at home
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈθiətɚ / (AmE), / həʊm ˈθɪətə / (BrE)
A system of audio and video equipment in the home designed to reproduce the experience of watching movies in a cinema.

Examples

home theatre system setup
Many electronics stores sell complete home theatre packages with surround sound speakers.
She invited friends over to watch the game on her new home theatre system.
We're upgrading our living room to add a home theatre for the best movie nights.
