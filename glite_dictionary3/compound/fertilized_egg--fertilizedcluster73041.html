fertilized egg

noun
egg joined with sperm, beginning development
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝː.t̬ɪ.laɪzd ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.tɪ.laɪzd ɛɡ / (BrE)
An egg that has been joined with a sperm cell and has begun the early stages of development into an embryo.
