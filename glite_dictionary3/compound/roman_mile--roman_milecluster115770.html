Roman mile

noun
ancient Roman unit of length
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.mən maɪl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.mən maɪl / (BrE)
an ancient Roman unit of length, approximately equal to 1,620 yards or 1,480 meters

Examples
