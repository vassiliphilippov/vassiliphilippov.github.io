Gulf of Ob

proper noun
inlet of Kara Sea in Siberia
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf əv ɑb / (AmE), / ɡʌlf əv ɒb / (BrE)
a large inlet of the Kara Sea located in western Siberia, Russia, formed by the estuary of the Ob River

Examples

the Gulf of Ob in Siberia
Fishing communities along the Gulf of Ob depend on its rich aquatic resources.
Several rivers empty their waters into the Gulf of Ob before reaching the Arctic Ocean.
The Gulf of Ob is covered by ice for most of the year.
