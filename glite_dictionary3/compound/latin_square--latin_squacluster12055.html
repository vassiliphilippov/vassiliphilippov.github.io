Latin square

noun
square matrix with unique symbols per row/column
Pronunciation: / ˈlæt̬.ɪn skwɛr / (AmE), / ˈlæt.ɪn skweə / (BrE)
a square array with n rows and n columns filled with n different symbols, arranged so that no symbol repeats in any row or column
