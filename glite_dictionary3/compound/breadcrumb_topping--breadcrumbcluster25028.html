breadcrumb topping

noun
breadcrumb layer used as a topping
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdˌkrʌm ˈtɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdkrʌm ˈtɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
A layer of breadcrumbs, sometimes mixed with butter or seasoning, used as a topping on dishes to add texture and flavor.
