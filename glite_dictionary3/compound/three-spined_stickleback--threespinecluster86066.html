three-spined stickleback

noun
small fish with three back spines
Pronunciation: / θriː spaɪnd ˈstɪklbæk / (AmE), / θriː spaɪnd ˈstɪklbæk / (BrE)
a small freshwater and coastal fish (Gasterosteus aculeatus) characterized by three sharp spines on its back, found in rivers and coastal waters in the Northern Hemisphere
