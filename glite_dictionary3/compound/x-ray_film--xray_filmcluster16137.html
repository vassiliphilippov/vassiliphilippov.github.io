X-ray film

noun
film for recording X-ray images
Pronunciation: / ˈɛks reɪ fɪlm / (AmE), / ˈɛks reɪ fɪlm / (BrE)
Photographic film that is specially designed to record images produced by X-rays, used in medical diagnosis and industrial testing.
