infrared therapy

noun
treatment using infrared radiation for pain relief
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.frəˈrɛd ˈθɛr.ə.pi / (AmE), / ˌɪn.frəˈrɛd ˈθɛr.ə.pi / (BrE)
a therapeutic treatment using infrared radiation to relieve pain and improve blood circulation in specific areas of the body

Examples

infrared therapy for pain relief

Some spas offer infrared therapy sessions for relaxation and improved circulation.
