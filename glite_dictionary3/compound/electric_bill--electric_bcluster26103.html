electric bill

noun
bill for electricity usage
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk bɪl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk bɪl / (BrE)
a bill showing the amount of money owed for electricity consumed over a period.

Examples

pay the electric bill
Please remember to pay the electric bill before the due date to avoid service interruption.
She split the electric bill with her roommate after moving in.
The electric bill was higher than usual last month due to the air conditioning.
