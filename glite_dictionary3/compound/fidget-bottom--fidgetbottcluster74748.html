fidget-bottom

noun
restless or fidgety person
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪdʒɪt ˌbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈfɪdʒɪt ˌbɒtəm / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is unable to sit still or is habitually restless
