to miss the update

verb
fail to receive latest information
Pronunciation: / mɪs ði ʌpˈdeɪt / (AmE), / mɪs ði ʌpˈdeɪt / (BrE)
to not receive or become aware of the most recent information or changes

Examples

miss the update from work
He apologized for providing the wrong information because he had missed the update from headquarters.
I didn't check my email yesterday and missed the update about the new schedule.
If you miss the update, please ask your manager for the latest instructions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – catch up: to become up to date after having fallen behind
  • synonym – miss out: fail to participate in something or benefit from it
