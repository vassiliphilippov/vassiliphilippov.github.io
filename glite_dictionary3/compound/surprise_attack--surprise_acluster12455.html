surprise attack

noun
attack made without warning
Pronunciation: / səˈpraɪz əˈtæk / (AmE), / səˈprʌɪz əˈtak / (BrE)
an attack that is launched without prior warning, intended to catch the target unprepared

Examples

launch a surprise attack
After hours of quiet, the pirates mounted a surprise attack on the passing ship.
The company's competitors staged a surprise attack by announcing their product early.
The enemy launched a surprise attack at dawn, catching the soldiers off guard.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ambush: a surprise attack made from a concealed position
