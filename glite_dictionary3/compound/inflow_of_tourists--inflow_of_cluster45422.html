inflow of tourists

noun
arrival or increase in tourists to a place
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌfloʊ əv ˈtʊr.ɪsts / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fləʊ əv ˈtʊə.rɪsts / (BrE)
an arrival or increase in the number of people visiting a place for tourism
