incompetent person

noun
person lacking necessary ability or skill
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑmpətənt ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒmpɪtənt ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who lacks the necessary ability, skill, or qualification to perform a task successfully

Examples

an incompetent person at work
In legal contexts, an incompetent person may have a guardian appointed to make decisions on their behalf.
She doesn't want to be seen as an incompetent person in front of her colleagues.
The department had to let go of the incompetent person who repeatedly made serious errors.
