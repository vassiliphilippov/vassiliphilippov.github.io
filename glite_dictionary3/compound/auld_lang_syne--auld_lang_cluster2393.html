auld lang syne

noun
traditional New Year's Eve song
Pronunciation: / ɔld læŋ saɪn / (AmE), / ɔːld læŋ saɪn / (BrE)
a traditional song, originally Scottish, sung to celebrate the past, especially at midnight on New Year's Eve

Examples

sing auld lang syne at midnight
At the stroke of midnight, everyone began singing auld lang syne and hugging each other.
Hearing auld lang syne always brings back fond memories of past holidays.
They played auld lang syne at the end of the party to mark the beginning of the new year.
