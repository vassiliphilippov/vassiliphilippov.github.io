Orange Group

noun
Protestant loyalist paramilitary group in Northern Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪndʒ ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ɡruːp / (BrE)
a Protestant loyalist paramilitary organization in Northern Ireland that opposes political settlements with Irish nationalists and attacks Catholic interests.
