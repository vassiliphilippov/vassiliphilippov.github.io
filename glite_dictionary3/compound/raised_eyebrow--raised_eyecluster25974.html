raised eyebrow

noun
gesture of surprise or skepticism
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪzd ˈaɪˌbraʊ / (AmE), / ˌreɪzd ˈaɪˌbraʊ / (BrE)
a facial expression or gesture showing surprise, skepticism, or disbelief, made by lifting one eyebrow

Examples

give a raised eyebrow
His story was so strange that it earned a raised eyebrow from everyone in the room.
She shot him a curious raised eyebrow when he arrived late for the meeting.
With a raised eyebrow, she challenged him to explain his sudden change of heart.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arched eyebrow: an eyebrow raised in an arch to show surprise or doubt
