trifid bur marigold

noun
Eurasian bur marigold species
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪ.fɪd bɜr ˈmɛr.ɪ.ˌɡoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtrɪ.fɪd bɜː ˈmær.ɪ.ɡəʊld / (BrE)
