alpha fetoprotein

noun
fetal protein linked to neural tube defects
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.fə ˌfiː.toʊˈproʊ.tiːn / (AmE), / ˈal.fə ˌfiː.təʊˈprəʊ.tiːn / (BrE)
a protein produced mainly by the fetal liver and yolk sac, whose elevated levels in a fetus may indicate neural tube defects such as spina bifida or anencephaly
