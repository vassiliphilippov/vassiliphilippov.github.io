Four Horsemen

noun
the four riders of the Apocalypse in the New Testament
Pronunciation: / fɔr ˈhɔrs.mən / (AmE), / fɔː ˈhɔːs.mən / (BrE)
(in the New Testament) the four riders symbolizing conquest, war, famine, and plague who are prophesied to appear at the end of the world.
