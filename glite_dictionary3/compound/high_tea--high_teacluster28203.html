high tea

noun
early evening meal with tea and food
Pronunciation: / haɪ tiː / (AmE), / haɪ tiː / (BrE)
a substantial meal eaten in the early evening, typically including tea and a variety of foods such as meats, bread, and cakes

Examples

high tea with sandwiches
She prepared sandwiches, scones, and cakes for high tea with her friends.
The family gathered for high tea after a long day of work and school.
Tourists enjoyed a traditional high tea in the quaint countryside inn.
