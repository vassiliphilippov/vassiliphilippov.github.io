hair dryer

noun
hand-held device for drying hair
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛr ˌdraɪər / (AmE), / ˈheə ˌdraɪə / (BrE)
a hand-held electrical device that blows warm air onto wet hair to dry and style it

Examples

use a hair dryer after shower
After his shower, he grabbed a hair dryer to style his hair.
She used the hair dryer to quickly dry her hair before leaving for work.
The hotel provided a hair dryer in every bathroom for guests' convenience.
