subnormal intelligence

noun
below-average cognitive ability
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈnɔrməl ɪnˈtɛlədʒəns / (AmE), / sʌbˈnɔːməl ɪnˈtelɪdʒəns / (BrE)
intelligence that is significantly below the average level, especially as measured by standard psychological tests

Examples

A diagnosis of subnormal intelligence often led to placement in special education classes.
