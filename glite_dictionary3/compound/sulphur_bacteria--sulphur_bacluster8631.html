sulphur bacteria

noun
bacteria involved in sulfur metabolism
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfər bækˈtɪəriə / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfə bækˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
Any bacteria of the genus Thiobacillus or other bacteria that oxidize or reduce sulfur compounds as part of their metabolism.
