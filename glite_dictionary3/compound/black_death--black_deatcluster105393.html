Black Death

noun
devastating bubonic plague epidemic (14th century)
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈdɛθ / (AmE), / blæk ˈdɛθ / (BrE)
the epidemic of bubonic plague that spread through Europe in the mid-14th century, killing millions of people
