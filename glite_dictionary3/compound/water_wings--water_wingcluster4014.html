water wings

noun
inflatable swimming aids for children
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər wɪŋz / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə wɪŋz / (BrE)
A pair of inflatable bags worn on the upper arms to help someone, especially a child, stay afloat while learning to swim.

Examples

Many parents use water wings to help their kids learn to swim safely.
