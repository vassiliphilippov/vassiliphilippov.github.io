ball-and-socket joint

noun
type of joint with spherical movement
Pronunciation: / bɔl ənd ˈsɑkɪt dʒɔɪnt /; / ˌbɔl ən ˈsɑkɪt dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / bɔːl ənd ˈsɒkɪt dʒɔɪnt /; / ˌbɔːl ən ˈsɒkɪt dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
a joint where the rounded end of one bone fits into a cup-like cavity of another bone, allowing movement in almost any direction (such as the hip or shoulder)

ball-and-socket joint in the hip
