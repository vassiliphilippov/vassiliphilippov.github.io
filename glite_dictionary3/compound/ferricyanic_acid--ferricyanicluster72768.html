ferricyanic acid

noun
a brown, unstable acid from ferricyanide
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛrɪsaɪˈænɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / ˌfɛrɪsaɪˈanɪk ˈasɪd / (BrE)
a brown, unstable acid formed by the action of acids on ferricyanides, chiefly known in solution and not isolated in pure form
