automatic toaster

noun
electric toaster with automatic pop-up function
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmætɪk ˈtoʊstər / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmatɪk ˈtəʊstə / (BrE)
An electric toaster in which bread slices are placed vertically in slots and automatically pop up when toasting is finished.

modern automatic toaster
