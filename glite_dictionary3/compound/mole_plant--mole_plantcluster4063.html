mole plant

noun
a poisonous plant (Euphorbia lathyris)
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊl plænt / (AmE), / ˈməʊl plɑːnt / (BrE)
a poisonous plant (Euphorbia lathyris) with upright stems and green leaves, sometimes grown in gardens and whose seeds yield a purgative oil
