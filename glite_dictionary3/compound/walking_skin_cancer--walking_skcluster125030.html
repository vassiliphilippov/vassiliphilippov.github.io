walking skin cancer

noun
person at high risk of skin cancer from sun or tanning
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkɪŋ skɪn ˈkænsɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkɪŋ skɪn ˈkænsə / (BrE)
(informal, humorous) A person considered at high risk of developing skin cancer due to excessive sun exposure or tanning.

Examples

walking skin cancer after tanning
After years of tanning beds, Mark jokes he's become a walking skin cancer.
Doctors warn that too much sun exposure can turn anyone into a walking skin cancer over time.
