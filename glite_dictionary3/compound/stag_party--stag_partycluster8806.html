stag party

noun
men-only pre-wedding celebration
Pronunciation: / stæɡ ˈpɑrti / (AmE), / stæɡ ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
A party held for a man who is about to get married, attended only by men, typically featuring entertainment and activities considered suitable for men.

Examples

stag party before wedding
He invited all his closest friends to his stag party before the wedding.
The stag party lasted all night and was full of surprises.
They hired a limousine for the stag party celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bachelor party: a party for a man about to marry, attended by his male friends
  • antonym – hen party: a party for a woman about to marry, attended by her female friends
