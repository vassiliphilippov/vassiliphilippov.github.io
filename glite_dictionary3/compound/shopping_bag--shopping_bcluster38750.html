shopping bag

noun
bag for carrying shopping
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːpɪŋ bæɡ / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpɪŋ bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag, usually made of plastic or strong paper and often with handles, used for carrying goods after shopping

Examples

carry groceries in a shopping bag
He forgot his reusable shopping bag and had to buy a new one at the checkout.
Plastic shopping bags have been banned in some cities to reduce waste.
She placed all the vegetables in a shopping bag before leaving the store.
