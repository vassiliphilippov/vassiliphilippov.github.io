team spirit

noun
group enthusiasm and motivation
Pronunciation: / tim ˈspɪrət /; / tiːm ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / tiːm ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a positive feeling of enthusiasm, loyalty, and support among members of a group that helps them work together towards shared goals
