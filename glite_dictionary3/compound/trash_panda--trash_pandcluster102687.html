trash panda

noun
humorous slang for raccoon
Pronunciation: / træʃ ˈpændə / (AmE), / træʃ ˈpændə / (BrE)
A raccoon, referred to humorously due to its habit of rummaging through garbage.

Examples

a curious trash panda
If you leave pet food outside, you might attract a curious trash panda.
Last night, a trash panda knocked over our recycling bin in search of food.
Some city dwellers have started to call raccoons trash pandas because of their bandit-like appearance.
