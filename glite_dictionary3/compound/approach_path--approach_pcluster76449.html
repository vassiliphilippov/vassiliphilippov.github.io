approach path

noun
final flight route to land an aircraft
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ pæθ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ pɑːθ / (BrE)
the route an aircraft follows when descending to land at an airport, especially the final part before touchdown

Examples

approach path for landing
Controllers guided the aircraft onto the correct approach path during heavy fog.
Obstructions near the approach path can pose serious risks for incoming flights.
The pilot aligned the plane perfectly with the approach path before landing.
