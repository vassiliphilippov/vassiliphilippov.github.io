ring rot bacteria

noun
bacteria causing ring rot in plants
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ rɑt bækˈtɪriə / (AmE), / rɪŋ rɒt bækˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
a group of bacteria that cause ring rot, a serious plant disease affecting tomatoes, potatoes, tobacco, and similar crops, resulting in brown rot of plant tissues
