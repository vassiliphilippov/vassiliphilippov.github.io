semaphore plant

noun
a tropical Asian shrub with moving leaflets
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛməˌfɔr plænt / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪˌfɔː plɑːnt / (BrE)
an erect tropical Asian shrub (Desmodium gyrans) whose small lateral leaflets move or rotate, especially in response to sunshine
