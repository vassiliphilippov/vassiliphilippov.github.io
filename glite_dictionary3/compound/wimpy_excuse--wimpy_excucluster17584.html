wimpy excuse

noun
weak or unconvincing excuse
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmpi ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ˈwɪmpi ɪksˈkjuːs / (BrE)
an excuse that is weak, unconvincing, or lacking in credibility

Examples

a wimpy excuse for being late
No one bought his wimpy excuse for missing the meeting; it sounded like he just didn't want to come.
She gave a wimpy excuse for being late, blaming the traffic when the roads were empty.
The teacher rolled her eyes at yet another wimpy excuse from a student about missing homework.

Related Terms

  • antonym – good excuse: an excuse that is reasonable or convincing
  • synonym – lame excuse: an excuse that is feeble or inadequate
