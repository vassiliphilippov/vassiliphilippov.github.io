compensatory time

noun
time off in exchange for overtime worked
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛnsəˌtɔri taɪm / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛnsət(ə)ri taɪm / (BrE)
Paid time off granted to an employee instead of extra pay for working overtime.

Examples
