no regrets

noun
acceptance of actions without remorse
Pronunciation: / noʊ rəˈɡrɛts / (AmE), / nəʊ rɪˈɡrɛts / (BrE)
used to express that one does not feel sorry or ashamed about something one has done

Examples

live with no regrets
After finishing the marathon, she felt exhausted but had no regrets about signing up.
He left his hometown to travel the world, saying he had no regrets.
He made a risky investment, but even after losing money, he insists he has no regrets.

Related Terms

  • antonym – regret: a feeling of sadness or disappointment over something that has happened or been done
  • antonym – remorse: deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
