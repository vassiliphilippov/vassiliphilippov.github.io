family Carcharhinidae

noun
family of requiem sharks (including tiger and soupfin sharks)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli kɑr.kəˈraɪ.nɪ.diː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli kɑː.kəˈraɪ.nɪ.diː / (BrE)
A large family of living sharks, found worldwide, especially in tropical waters, characterized by the absence of spines in the dorsal fin; includes requiem sharks such as tiger sharks and soupfin sharks.
