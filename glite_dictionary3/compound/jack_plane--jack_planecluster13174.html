jack plane

noun
carpenter's hand tool for smoothing wood
Pronunciation: / dʒæk pleɪn / (AmE), / dʒæk pleɪn / (BrE)
a carpenter's hand tool with a moderately long, flat sole, used for smoothing and shaping wood as an intermediate step during finishing

Examples

use a jack plane on wood
After planing with a jack plane, the wood was ready for finer tools.
He bought a vintage jack plane at the antique tool market.
The carpenter used a jack plane to smooth the rough surface of the tabletop.
