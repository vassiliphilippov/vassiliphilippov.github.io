root word

noun
basic word form without affixes
Pronunciation: / rut wɜrd / (AmE), / ruːt wɜːd / (BrE)
(Linguistics) The basic form of a word after all affixes such as prefixes or suffixes have been removed.

Examples

root word in vocabulary
Children learn how to find the root word to better understand vocabulary.
In the word 'unhappiness', the root word is 'happy'.
Many English words share a common root word from Latin or Greek.
