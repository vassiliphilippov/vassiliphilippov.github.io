takeover bid

noun
offer to buy control of a company
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkˌoʊvɚ bɪd / (AmE), / ˈteɪkˌəʊvə bɪd / (BrE)
an offer to buy enough shares in a company to gain control of it, especially as part of an acquisition

Examples
