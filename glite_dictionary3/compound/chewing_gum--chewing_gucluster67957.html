chewing gum

noun
sweetened substance for chewing
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃuːɪŋ ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːɪŋ ɡʌm / (BrE)
A sweetened, flavored preparation for chewing but not swallowing, typically made from chicle or synthetic rubber.

Examples

to chew chewing gum
Many people use chewing gum to freshen their breath.
