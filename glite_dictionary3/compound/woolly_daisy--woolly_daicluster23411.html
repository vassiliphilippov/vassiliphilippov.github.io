woolly daisy

noun
small woolly-leaved plant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊli ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈwʊli ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
A small annual plant of the southwestern United States, with grey woolly leaves and small golden-yellow flower heads, sometimes classified in the genus Eriophyllum.
