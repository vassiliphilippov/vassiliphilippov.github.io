minute steak

noun
thin steak cooked quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪt steɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt steɪk / (BrE)
A thin slice of steak that can be cooked quickly, usually by frying or grilling.

grilled minute steak
