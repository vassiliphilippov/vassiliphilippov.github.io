on a half shell

adjective
shellfish served in only one shell half
Pronunciation: / ɑn ə hæf ʃɛl / (AmE), / ɒn ə hɑːf ʃɛl / (BrE)
(of shellfish) served on the bottom shell only, without the top shell, typically as a dish or appetizer.
