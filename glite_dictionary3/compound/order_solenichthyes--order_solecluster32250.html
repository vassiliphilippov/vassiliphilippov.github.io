order Solenichthyes

noun
order of tropical marine fishes with tubular snouts
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdərˌ sɒləˈnɪkθiːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdəˌ sɒlɪˈnɪkθiːz / (BrE)
A small order of chiefly tropical marine fishes, including bellows fishes, shrimpfishes, cornetfishes, and pipefishes, all having a small mouth at the end of a tubular snout.
