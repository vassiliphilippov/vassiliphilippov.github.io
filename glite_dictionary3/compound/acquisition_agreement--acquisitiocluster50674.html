acquisition agreement

noun
contract for company acquisition or merger
Pronunciation: / əˈkwɪzɪʃən əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / əˌkwɪzɪʃ(ə)n əˈɡriːm(ə)nt / (BrE)
a legal contract outlining the terms and conditions under which one company acquires another company, typically as part of a merger or takeover

Examples

sign an acquisition agreement
Before the merger could proceed, both boards had to approve the acquisition agreement.
The acquisition agreement detailed the price and payment schedule for the transaction.
The companies finalized the acquisition agreement after months of negotiation and due diligence.

  • synonym – sale and purchase agreement: a contract for sale and transfer of a business or assets
