eye blink

noun
rapid reflex closure of the eyelid
Pronunciation: / aɪ blɪŋk / (AmE), / aɪ blɪŋk / (BrE)
an involuntary, rapid closing and opening of the eyelid, typically as a reflex to protect or moisten the eye

Examples

quick eye blink
A sudden gust of wind triggered an automatic eye blink.
Each eye blink helps to spread tears evenly over the surface of the eye.
The doctor measured the frequency of his eye blink during the examination.
