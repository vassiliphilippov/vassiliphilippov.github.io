slate roof

noun
roof covered with slate tiles
Pronunciation: / sleɪt ruːf / (AmE), / sleɪt ruːf / (BrE)
A roof that is covered with thin, flat pieces of slate stone, used as a durable and waterproof roofing material.

Examples

slate roof on a house
Heavy rain echoed across the slate roof during the storm.
The old cottage still has its original slate roof, which has lasted for over a century.
They decided to replace their asphalt shingles with a more traditional slate roof.
