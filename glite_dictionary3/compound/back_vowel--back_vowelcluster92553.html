back vowel

noun
vowel sound produced at the back of the mouth
Pronunciation: / bæk ˈvaʊəl / (AmE), / bak ˈvaʊəl / (BrE)
A vowel sound produced with the tongue positioned as far back as possible in the mouth, such as the 'u' in 'rule' or the 'o' in 'pole'.

Examples

Linguists classify sounds like 'o' in 'pole' as back vowels.
