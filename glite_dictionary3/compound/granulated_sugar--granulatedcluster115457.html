granulated sugar

noun
white sugar in small grains for cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræn.jəˌleɪ.t̬ɪd ˈʃʊɡ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡran.jʊ.leɪ.tɪd ˈʃʊɡ.ə / (BrE)
Sugar that has been processed into small, white grains, used especially in cooking and baking.

Examples

granulated sugar in baking
Most cookie recipes call for granulated sugar as the main sweetener.
Please add two cups of granulated sugar to the mixing bowl.
She prefers granulated sugar in her coffee rather than honey.
