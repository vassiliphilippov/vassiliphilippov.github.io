fish mousse

noun
light, creamy dish made with fish
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ muːs / (AmE), / fɪʃ muːs / (BrE)
A light, creamy dish made by blending cooked fish with eggs, cream, and seasonings, often served as a starter or appetizer.

Examples

fish mousse appetizer
At the party, guests enjoyed small portions of smoked salmon fish mousse on toast.
She prepared a light fish mousse for the dinner menu to impress her friends.
The chef served a delicate fish mousse with herb sauce as the appetizer.
