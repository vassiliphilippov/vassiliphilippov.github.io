shelter tent

noun
wedge-shaped floorless tent
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛltɚ tɛnt / (AmE), / ˈʃɛltə tɛnt / (BrE)
A small, wedge-shaped tent, usually without a floor or windows, used especially for temporary shelter outdoors.

Examples

set up a shelter tent
A basic shelter tent can be assembled quickly with just a few poles and stakes.
The soldiers slept in a shelter tent during their training exercises.
We set up a shelter tent to protect ourselves from the rain while camping.
