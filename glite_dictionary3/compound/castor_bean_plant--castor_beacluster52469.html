castor bean plant

noun
tropical shrub source of castor oil and ricin
Pronunciation: / ˈkæs.tɚ ˈbiːn ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.stə ˈbiːn ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
A large tropical shrub native to Africa and Asia, with large palmate leaves and spiny capsules containing seeds used to produce castor oil and ricin.
