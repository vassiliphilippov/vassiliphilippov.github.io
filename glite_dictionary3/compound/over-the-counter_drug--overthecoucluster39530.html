over-the-counter drug

noun
drug available without prescription
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚ ðə ˈkaʊntɚ ˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ðə ˈkaʊntə ˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
