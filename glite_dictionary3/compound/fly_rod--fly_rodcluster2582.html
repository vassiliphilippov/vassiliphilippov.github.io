fly rod

noun
rod for fly fishing
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪ ˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈflaɪ ˌrɒd / (BrE)
a long, flexible fishing rod designed specifically for fly fishing, used to cast lightweight flies

Examples

A high-quality fly rod can make a big difference when fly fishing for trout.

With her lightweight fly rod, she cast the line gently across the river.
