bone oil

noun
oily liquid from bones, lubricant
Pronunciation: / boʊn ɔɪl / (AmE), / bəʊn ɔɪl / (BrE)
an oily liquid made by heating or distilling animal bones, used in industry (such as lubricants, leather manufacturing, or sheep dips)
