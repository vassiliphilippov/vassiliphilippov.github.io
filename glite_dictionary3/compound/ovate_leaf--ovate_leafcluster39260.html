ovate leaf

noun
egg-shaped leaf, broadest at base
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.veɪt liːf / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.veɪt liːf / (BrE)
A leaf that is shaped like an egg, with the broader end at the base.
