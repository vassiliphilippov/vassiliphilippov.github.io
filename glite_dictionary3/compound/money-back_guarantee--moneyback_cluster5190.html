money-back guarantee

noun
promise to refund if not satisfied
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌni ˌbæk ˌɡærənˈti / (AmE), / ˈmʌni ˌbæk ˌɡærənˈtiː / (BrE)
a promise by a seller to return the full purchase price if the buyer is not satisfied with the product or service

Examples

money-back guarantee on products
He chose the service because it came with a 30-day money-back guarantee in case he changed his mind.
The company offers a money-back guarantee on all electronics if you are not completely satisfied.
With their money-back guarantee, you can shop with confidence and return the product if it doesn't meet your needs.
