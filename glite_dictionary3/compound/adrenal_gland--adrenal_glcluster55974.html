adrenal gland

noun
endocrine gland above the kidney
Pronunciation: / əˈdriːnəl ɡlænd / (AmE), / əˈdriːnəl ɡlænd / (BrE)
Either of a pair of endocrine glands located above the kidneys, which produce hormones such as adrenaline and cortisol.

Examples

A tumor in the adrenal gland can cause serious health issues if not treated.
