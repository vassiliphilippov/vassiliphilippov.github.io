Christmas Eve

noun
the day before Christmas
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪsməs ˈiv /; / ˈkrɪsməs iv / (AmE), / ˌkrɪsməs ˈiːv /; / ˈkrɪsməs iːv / (BrE)
the day or evening before Christmas Day (December 24th), celebrated with traditions and festivities
