sterile environment

noun
completely clean, germ-free environment
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛr.əl ɪnˈvaɪ.rən.mənt / (AmE), / ˈstɛr.aɪl ɪnˈvaɪ.rən.mənt / (BrE)
an environment that is completely clean and free from bacteria, germs, or other microorganisms

Examples

hospital sterile environment
During surgery, doctors work in a sterile environment to ensure patient safety.
Some plants require a sterile environment to grow successfully in tissue culture labs.
The laboratory must maintain a sterile environment to prevent contamination of samples.
