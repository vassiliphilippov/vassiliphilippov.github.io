Epistle of Jeremiah

noun
Apocryphal book ascribed to Jeremiah
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪsəl əv dʒəˈraɪmiə / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪsl əv dʒəˈraɪmiə / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book consisting of a letter ascribed to Jeremiah, addressing Jews exiled in Babylon and warning them against idolatry

Epistle of Jeremiah in the Bible
In some versions of the Bible, the Epistle of Jeremiah is included as a chapter of Baruch.
Scholars debate whether the Epistle of Jeremiah was originally written in Hebrew or Greek.
The Epistle of Jeremiah offers a sharp warning against the worship of idols.
