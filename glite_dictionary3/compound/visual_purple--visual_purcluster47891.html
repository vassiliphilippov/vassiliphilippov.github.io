visual purple

noun
pigment in the retina aiding dim light vision
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈpɝːpəl / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəl ˈpɜːpl / (BrE)
a reddish-purple pigment found in the retinal rods of vertebrates, that is sensitive to light and is involved in vision in dim light
