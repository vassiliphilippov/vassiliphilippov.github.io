a bit like

preposition
somewhat similar to
Pronunciation: / ə bɪt laɪk / (AmE), / ə bɪt laɪk / (BrE)
somewhat similar to; having some resemblance to (used to make a tentative comparison)

Examples

a bit like her haircut
Her new haircut looks a bit like the style she had in college.
The weather here is a bit like spring, even though it's the middle of winter.
This cake tastes a bit like the one my grandmother used to make.
