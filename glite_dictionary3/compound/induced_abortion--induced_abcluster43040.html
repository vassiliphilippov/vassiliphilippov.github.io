induced abortion

noun
deliberate termination of pregnancy
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdust əˈbɔrʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjuːst əˈbɔːʃən / (BrE)
The deliberate termination of a pregnancy through medical or surgical means.

Examples

to have an induced abortion
After much consideration, she decided to have an induced abortion for medical reasons.
Some countries have strict laws regarding induced abortion procedures.
The doctor discussed the risks and options associated with an induced abortion.

Related Terms

  • antonym – miscarriage: the spontaneous loss of a pregnancy, not deliberately induced
  • antonym – spontaneous abortion: an unintentional loss of pregnancy (miscarriage)
