fall guy

noun
person easily blamed or tricked
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔl ɡaɪ /; / fɔl ɡaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːl ɡaɪ /; / fɔːl ɡaɪ / (BrE)
a person who is blamed for something so that others can avoid blame or punishment; a scapegoat or dupe

Examples

the fall guy for mistakes
He was the fall guy in the whole operation, even though he was innocent.
The company used him as a fall guy to distract from their own mistakes.
They needed a fall guy to take the blame for the missing funds.
After the scandal broke, the company urgently needed a fall guy to divert attention from its executives.
He refused to be the fall guy for problems he didn't cause.
In the political crisis, she ended up as the fall guy despite having no real responsibility.
