Indian paint

noun
annual plant with greenish flowers and red fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən peɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən peɪnt / (BrE)
A European annual plant with clusters of greenish flowers, followed by red, pulpy, berrylike fruit; now naturalized in North America.

Examples

Indian paint red berries
Many gardens in North America feature Indian paint for its ornamental fruit.
The clusters of berries on the Indian paint turn bright red in autumn.
The naturalized Indian paint thrives in a variety of soil types.
