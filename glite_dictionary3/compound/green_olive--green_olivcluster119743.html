green olive

noun
olive picked unripe and pickled
Pronunciation: / ɡrin ˈɑlɪv / (AmE), / ɡriːn ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
an olive picked before ripening and pickled in brine, often served as a snack or in salads and sometimes stuffed with pimento

Examples

stuffed green olive
I prefer the salty flavor of a green olive over the milder black variety.
She always adds a green olive to her martini as a garnish.
The pizza was topped with peppers, onions, and sliced green olives.

Related Terms

  • antonym – black olive: an olive that is fully ripe and usually darker in color
