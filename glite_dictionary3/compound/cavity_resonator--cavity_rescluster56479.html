cavity resonator

noun
chamber for resonant wave oscillation
Pronunciation: / ˈkævəti ˈrɛzəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkævəti ˈrɛzəˌneɪtə / (BrE)
a hollow chamber with dimensions that allow resonant oscillation of electromagnetic or acoustic waves

Examples

cavity resonator in a laser
Lasers often use a cavity resonator to amplify light within the device.
Sound waves bounced repeatedly inside the cavity resonator, creating a clear resonance.
The cavity resonator is essential in many microwave and radio frequency applications.

  • synonym – resonator: a device or system that exhibits resonance or resonates in response to an external force
