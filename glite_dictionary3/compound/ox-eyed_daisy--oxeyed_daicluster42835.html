ox-eyed daisy

noun
a white-flowered Eurasian perennial plant
Pronunciation: / ɑks-aɪd ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ɒks-aɪd ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a tall perennial plant (Leucanthemum vulgare) native to Eurasia, with white flowers and a yellow center, widely naturalized in many regions.

Examples

wild ox-eyed daisy
Ox-eyed daisy is sometimes considered invasive in some areas because it spreads easily.
She picked a bouquet of ox-eyed daisies to brighten the kitchen.
The field was covered with blooming ox-eyed daisies in early summer.
