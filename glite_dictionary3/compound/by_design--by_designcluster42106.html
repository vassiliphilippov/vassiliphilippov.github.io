by design

adverb
intentionally; deliberately
Pronunciation: / baɪ dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / baɪ dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
Intentionally; as a result of planning or deliberate choice.

Examples

by design not by accident
He left the document on the desk by design, hoping someone would notice it.
The park was built by design to encourage interaction among residents.
The rooms are arranged by design to maximize natural light.

Related Terms

  • antonym – by accident: without intention; not deliberately
  • synonym – on purpose: intentionally; not by accident
