Begonia rex

noun
ornamental plant with patterned leaves
Pronunciation: / bəˈɡoʊniə rɛks / (AmE), / bɪˈɡəʊniə rɛks / (BrE)
A species of begonia, prized as an ornamental houseplant, with broad, rough-textured leaves patterned in silver, bronze, purple, and red-brown, and inconspicuous flowers.
