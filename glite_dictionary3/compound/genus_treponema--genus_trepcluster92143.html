genus Treponema

noun
bacterial genus (spirochetes; includes syphilis-causing species)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌtrɛpəˈniːmə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌtrɛpəˈniːmə / (BrE)
A genus of anaerobic spirochete bacteria, including species that are parasitic in warm-blooded animals and can cause diseases such as syphilis.
