cutaneous vein

noun
a superficial vein under the skin
Pronunciation: / kjuˈteɪniəs veɪn / (AmE), / kjuːˈteɪnɪəs veɪn / (BrE)
a vein located in the subcutaneous tissue that drains blood into deeper veins

Examples

cutaneous vein injection
