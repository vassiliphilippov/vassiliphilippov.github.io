knob-gobbler

noun
slang for a licking animal or foolish person
Pronunciation: / nɑb ˈɡɑblɚ / (AmE), / nɒb ˈɡɒblə / (BrE)
(slang) A dog or animal that excessively licks or nibbles; also, a silly or foolish person (often humorous or mildly pejorative).

Examples

silly knob-gobbler
Don't be such a knob-gobbler; think before you speak!
He laughed as the puppy, a real knob-gobbler, slobbered all over his shoes.
Whenever treats are out, my spaniel turns into a true knob-gobbler.
