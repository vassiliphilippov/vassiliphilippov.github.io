centre of immersion

noun
center of mass of submerged part of a vessel
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tər əv ɪˈmɜr.ʒən / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə əv ɪˈmɜːʃn / (BrE)
(physics) The center of mass of the immersed part of a ship or other floating object.
