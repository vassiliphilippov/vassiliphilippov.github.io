tab key

noun
key for indenting or tabulation on keyboard/typewriter
Pronunciation: / tæb kiː / (AmE), / tæb kiː / (BrE)
the key on a computer keyboard or typewriter that moves the cursor to the next tab stop, used for indenting or aligning text

Examples

press the tab key
On most keyboards, the tab key is located to the left of the Q key.
She accidentally hit the tab key and the whole text shifted to the right.
To indent the first line of your paragraph, just press the tab key.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tab: a key on a keyboard that moves the cursor forward to the next tab stop
