lobster stew

noun
stew with lobster in milk or cream
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑbstɚ stu / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə stjuː / (BrE)
A soup or stew made with diced lobster meat cooked in milk or cream, often seasoned and served hot.
