Chinese parasol tree

noun
ornamental Asian deciduous tree
Pronunciation: / ˈʧaɪˌniz ˈpæɹəˌsɑl ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪniːz ˈpærəsɒl ˌtriː / (BrE)
A deciduous tree (Firmiana simplex), native to Asia and grown as an ornamental in the southern United States, noted for its maple-like leaves and long clusters of yellow-green flowers followed by unusual leaflike pods.

shade from a Chinese parasol tree
