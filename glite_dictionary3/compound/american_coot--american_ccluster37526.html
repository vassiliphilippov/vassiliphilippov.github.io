American coot

noun
North American water bird species
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən kuːt / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən kuːt / (BrE)
a medium-sized water bird native to North America, with a slate-gray body, white bill, and lobed toes, often found in freshwater lakes and ponds
