spin doctor

noun
one who manipulates public perception, esp. in politics
Pronunciation: / spɪn ˈdɑktɚ / (AmE), / spɪn ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a person, especially in politics or public relations, who tries to control or manipulate how events or news are presented to influence public opinion
