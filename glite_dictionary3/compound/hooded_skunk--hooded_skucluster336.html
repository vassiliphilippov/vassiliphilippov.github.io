hooded skunk

noun
species of skunk with a distinctive white hood
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊdɪd skʌŋk / (AmE), / ˈhʊdɪd skʌŋk / (BrE)
a species of skunk (Mephitis macroura) found in Mexico and the southernmost parts of the southwestern United States, known for the prominent white ruff or hood on its neck and shoulders

Examples

a hooded skunk in Mexico
