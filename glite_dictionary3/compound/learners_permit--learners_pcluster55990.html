learner's permit

noun
provisional driving license for learners
Pronunciation: / ˈlɜrnərz pərˌmɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɜːnəz pəˌmɪt / (BrE)
an official document that allows a person to learn to drive a vehicle, usually under certain restrictions before obtaining a full license

Examples

learner's permit for driving
She passed the written test and now has her learner's permit.
With a learner's permit, new drivers must be accompanied by a licensed adult.
You must obtain a learner's permit before taking driving lessons with an instructor.
