balance and flexibility

noun
stability and adaptability of movement
Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.əns ənd flɛk.səˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns ən flɛk.sɪˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The ability to maintain physical stability while moving, and to adapt easily to different movements or positions.

Examples

Dancers rely on excellent balance and flexibility to perform challenging routines.
Regular stretching improves your balance and flexibility, reducing the risk of falls.
Yoga classes are designed to enhance balance and flexibility for people of all ages.
