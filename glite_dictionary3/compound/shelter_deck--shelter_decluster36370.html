shelter deck

noun
upper ship deck without overhead cover
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛltər dɛk / (AmE), / ˈʃɛltə dɛk / (BrE)
an upper deck of a ship that has no overhead protection from the weather but provides shelter to the deck below

Examples

on the shelter deck of a ship
Crew members huddled on the shelter deck during the storm, grateful for some protection from the wind.
The cargo was stored safely beneath the shelter deck to avoid exposure to rain.
The new design relocated lifeboats to the shelter deck for easier access in emergencies.
