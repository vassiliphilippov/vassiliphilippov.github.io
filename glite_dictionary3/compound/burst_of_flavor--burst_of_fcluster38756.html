burst of flavor

noun
sudden, intense taste sensation
Pronunciation: / bɝst əv ˈfleɪvɚ / (AmE), / bɜːst əv ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
a sudden, intense sensation of taste; a strong, unexpected flavor experienced in food or drink

Examples

enjoy a burst of flavor
Biting into the ripe cherry tomato released a burst of flavor in my mouth.
The fresh herbs provided a burst of flavor in the pasta dish.
This new hot sauce gives your tacos a surprising burst of flavor.
