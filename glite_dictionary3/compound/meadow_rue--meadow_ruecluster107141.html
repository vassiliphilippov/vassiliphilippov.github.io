meadow rue

noun
delicate perennial herb of the genus Thalictrum
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊ ruː / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊ ruː / (BrE)
any of several perennial herbs of the genus Thalictrum, with delicate, divided leaves and clusters of small purple or yellow flowers, commonly found in damp meadows and stream banks.
