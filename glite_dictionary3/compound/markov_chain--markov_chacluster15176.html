Markov chain

noun
stochastic process with discrete transitions
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑr.kɑf tʃeɪn /; / ˈmɑrˌkɔv tʃeɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.kɒf tʃeɪn /; / ˈmɑːkɒv tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a process or system where the next state depends only on the current state, not on the full history, usually at regular steps
