variety show

noun
entertainment program with mixed performances
Pronunciation: / vəˈraɪəti ˌʃoʊ / (AmE), / vəˈraɪəti ˌʃəʊ / (BrE)
a type of entertainment program consisting of a series of short, unrelated performances by different artists, such as songs, dances, skits, or magic acts

Examples

variety show on TV
Every year, our school organizes a variety show to display students’ talents.
She appeared on a popular television variety show in the 1970s.
The local theater hosted a variety show featuring comedians, singers, and dancers.
