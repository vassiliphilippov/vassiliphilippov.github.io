right of search

noun
legal right to search neutral ships in war
Pronunciation: / raɪt əv sɜrtʃ / (AmE), / raɪt əv sɜːtʃ / (BrE)
the right of a belligerent nation to stop and search neutral ships on the high seas during wartime
