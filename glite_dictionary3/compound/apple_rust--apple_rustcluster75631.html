apple rust

noun
plant disease causing spots on apples and pears
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.əl rʌst / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl rʌst / (BrE)
a plant disease caused by a rust fungus, resulting in orange or brown spots on apples, pears, and related trees.

Examples

apple rust spots on leaves
Applying a fungicide early in the spring can help control apple rust outbreaks.
Gardeners are encouraged to inspect their trees regularly for signs of apple rust.
The orchard suffered significant crop loss due to apple rust this season.
