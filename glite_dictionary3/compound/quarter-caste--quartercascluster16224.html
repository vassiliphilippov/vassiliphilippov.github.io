quarter-caste

noun
person of mixed caste heritage
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdərˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəˌkɑːst / (BrE)
a person with one grandparent belonging to a different racial or caste group, particularly within societies structured by caste

Examples

a quarter-caste child
As a quarter-caste, she often faced questions about her background.
He learned about his family's quarter-caste status from old documents.
The census counted a number of people identified as quarter-caste in the region.
