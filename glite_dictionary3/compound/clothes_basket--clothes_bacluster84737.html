clothes basket

noun
container for dirty or wet clothes
Pronunciation: / kloʊðz ˈbæskɪt / (AmE), / kləʊðz ˈbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
A large container, often with handles and made of wicker, plastic, or other materials, used for holding dirty laundry to be washed or wet clothes to be dried.

Examples

full clothes basket
After the clothes were dry, she carried them back upstairs in the clothes basket.
She tossed her dirty socks into the clothes basket before heading to the shower.
The overflowing clothes basket reminded him that it was time to do the laundry.
