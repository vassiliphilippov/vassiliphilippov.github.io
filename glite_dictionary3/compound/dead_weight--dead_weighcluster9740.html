dead weight

noun
a heavy or oppressive burden
Pronunciation: / dɛd weɪt / (AmE), / dɛd weɪt / (BrE)
a person, thing, or responsibility that is a burden and makes progress difficult, either physically (hard to move) or figuratively (not helpful or slowing things down)
