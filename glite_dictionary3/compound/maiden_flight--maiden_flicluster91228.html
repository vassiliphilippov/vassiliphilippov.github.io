maiden flight

noun
first flight of a new aircraft or vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdn flaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdn flaɪt / (BrE)
the first flight made by a new aircraft, spacecraft, or other vehicle.

Examples

successful maiden flight
Excitement filled the air as the rocket prepared for its maiden flight.
The company invited journalists to witness the maiden flight of their latest drone.
The new passenger jet successfully completed its maiden flight last week.
