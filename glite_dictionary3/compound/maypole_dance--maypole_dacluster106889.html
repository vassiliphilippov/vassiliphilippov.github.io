maypole dance

noun
traditional folk dance around a maypole
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪˌpoʊl dæns / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəʊl dɑːns / (BrE)
a traditional English folk dance performed around a tall pole decorated with flowers and ribbons, especially on May Day
