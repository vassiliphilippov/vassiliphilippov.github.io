oyster bed

noun
area where oysters are grown
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪstɚ bɛd / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə bɛd / (BrE)
an area, often on the sea floor or in a river, where oysters are artificially bred and grown for harvesting

Examples

natural oyster bed
A new oyster bed was established along the coastline to support local aquaculture.
Scientists monitor the water quality of the oyster bed to protect the developing shellfish.
The fishermen tended to the oyster bed every morning to ensure a healthy crop.
