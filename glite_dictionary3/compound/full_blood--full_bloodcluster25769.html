full blood

noun
person/animal of purebred descent
Pronunciation: / fʊl blʌd / (AmE), / fʊl blʌd / (BrE)
A person or animal descended from parents who are both of one pure breed or race.

Examples

full blood member
In some cultures, being a full blood member of a tribe carries special significance.
The horse is considered a full blood because both of its parents are registered thoroughbreds.
The kennel specializes in breeding full blood German Shepherds for show and work.
