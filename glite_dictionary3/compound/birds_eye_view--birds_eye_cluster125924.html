bird's eye view

noun
an elevated or comprehensive overview
Pronunciation: / bɝdz aɪ vju / (AmE), / bɜːdz aɪ vjuː / (BrE)
a view of a subject or scene from above, as if seen by a bird in flight; a general or comprehensive overview of a situation or topic

Examples

a bird's eye view of the city
A map provides a bird's eye view of the city, highlighting major landmarks.
From the mountain top, you get a bird's eye view of the entire valley.
The report gives a bird's eye view of the company's financial status.

  • synonym – overview: a general review or summary of a subject
