Serengeti Plain

proper noun
savanna region in northern Tanzania
Pronunciation: / sɛrənˈɡɛti pleɪn / (AmE), / sɛrənˈɡɛti pleɪn / (BrE)
a vast, open plain in northern Tanzania known for its rich wildlife and as the site of the annual migration of wildebeest and other animals
