yellow light

noun
traffic signal to proceed with caution
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛl.oʊ ˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ ˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a traffic signal that warns drivers to proceed with caution or prepare to stop because the signal is about to turn red
