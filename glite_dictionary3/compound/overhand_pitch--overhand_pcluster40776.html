overhand pitch

noun
baseball pitch thrown above the shoulder
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚˌhænd pɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌhænd pɪtʃ / (BrE)
a baseball pitch in which the pitcher throws the ball with the hand moving above the shoulder
