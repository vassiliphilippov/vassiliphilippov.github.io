to rewind time

verb
reverse recent events in a game
Pronunciation: / riˈwaɪnd taɪm / (AmE), / ˌriːˈwaɪnd taɪm / (BrE)
(in videogames) to reverse recent events in the game's timeline so actions can be undone and retried.

Examples

to rewind time in game
The game lets you rewind time to retry the risky leap without losing progress.
She rewound time and avoided the trap, which saved her run in the tournament.
Many speedrunners practice by rewinding time after mistakes to perfect their routes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – turn back the clock: to return to an earlier time or situation; used figuratively to describe reversing events.
  • synonym – undo: to reverse a previous action; in software or games, to cancel or revert a recent operation.
