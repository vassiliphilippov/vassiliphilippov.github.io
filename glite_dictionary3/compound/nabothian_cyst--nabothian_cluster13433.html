nabothian cyst

noun
mucus-filled cervical cyst
Pronunciation: / nəˈboʊθiən sɪst / (AmE), / nəˈbəʊθiən sɪst / (BrE)
A mucus-filled cyst that forms in the nabothian glands on the surface of the uterine cervix, usually benign and resulting from blockage of the gland's duct.

Examples

nabothian cyst on cervix
During the examination, the doctor explained that a nabothian cyst is a common and harmless finding.
She was relieved to learn that nabothian cysts are usually benign and do not cause any symptoms.
The ultrasound revealed a small nabothian cyst on her cervix, which required no treatment.
