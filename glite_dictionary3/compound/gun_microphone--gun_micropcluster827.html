gun microphone

noun
directional microphone with a long barrel
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn ˈmaɪ.kɹoʊˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ɡʌn ˈmaɪ.krəˌfəʊn / (BrE)
a highly directional microphone with a long, barrel-shaped design that allows for recording sounds from a distance by aiming it at the source
