nerve centre

noun
main place of control or coordination
Pronunciation: / nɝv ˈsɛntɚ / (AmE), / nɜːv ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
The main place from which an organization, activity, or system is controlled and where the most important decisions are made.

Examples

company nerve centre
London is considered the nerve centre of global finance.
The new hospital's nerve centre coordinates patient care across all departments.
The operations room serves as the nerve centre during a crisis.
