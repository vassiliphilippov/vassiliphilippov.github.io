prairie star

noun
a plant with pale pink or white flowers from North America
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛəri stɑr / (AmE), / ˈpreəri stɑː / (BrE)
a plant with mainly basal leaves and slender open racemes of white or pale pink flowers, native to prairies and open forests of northwestern United States, British Columbia, and Alberta
