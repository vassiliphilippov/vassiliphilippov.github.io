Schuller-Christian disease

noun
rare childhood inflammatory disease with skull lesions
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːlɚ ˈkrɪstʃən dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ˈʃʊlə ˈkrɪstʃən dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A rare inflammatory disease, mainly affecting young children, characterized by histiocytosis with disturbance of cholesterol metabolism, cystic lesions in the skull, and sometimes diabetes insipidus.
