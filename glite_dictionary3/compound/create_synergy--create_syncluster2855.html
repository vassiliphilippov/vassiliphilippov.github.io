to create synergy

verb
produce a greater combined effect through collaboration
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪt ˈsɪnɚdʒi / (AmE), / kriˈeɪt ˈsɪnədʒi / (BrE)
to produce a combined effect that is greater than the sum of individual efforts, especially through collaboration or teamwork

Examples

to create synergy in a team
By working together, the two companies were able to create synergy and dominate the market.
Effective communication helps teams create synergy and achieve outstanding results.
Our new partnership aims to create synergy between research and development.
