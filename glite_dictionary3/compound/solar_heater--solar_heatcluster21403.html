solar heater

noun
device that heats using solar energy
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
A device that uses energy from the sun to generate heat, typically for heating water or air.
