spidery handwriting

noun
thin, irregular handwriting
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdɚi ˈhændˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəri ˈhændˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
handwriting that is thin, irregular, and resembling the legs or movement of a spider

Examples

spidery handwriting on a letter
He recognized his grandmother’s spidery handwriting on the back of the old photograph.
Her spidery handwriting trailed across the page in wavering lines.
The letter was filled with spidery handwriting that was difficult to decipher.
