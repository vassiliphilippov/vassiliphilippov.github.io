cling film

noun
plastic film for wrapping food
Pronunciation: / klɪŋ fɪlm / (AmE), / klɪŋ fɪlm / (BrE)
a thin, transparent plastic film that clings to surfaces and itself, used for wrapping and preserving food

Examples

wrap food with cling film
He covered the salad bowl with cling film to keep it fresh overnight.
Make sure the cling film is sealed tightly around the dish before putting it in the refrigerator.
She wrapped the leftover cake in cling film to prevent it from drying out.
