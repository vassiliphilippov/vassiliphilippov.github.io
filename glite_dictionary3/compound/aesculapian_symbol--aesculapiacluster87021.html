Aesculapian symbol

noun
symbol of medicine and healing
Pronunciation: / ˌɛskjəˈleɪpiən ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / ˌiːskjʊˈleɪpiən ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
a symbol, typically the staff of Aesculapius, representing healing and medicine

Examples

Aesculapian symbol on hospitals
In ancient Greece, physicians displayed the Aesculapian symbol outside their places of healing.
Many people mistakenly confuse the caduceus with the Aesculapian symbol in medical contexts.
The Aesculapian symbol can be found on many medical association logos worldwide.
