state highway

noun
major road maintained by a state
Pronunciation: / steɪt ˈhaɪˌweɪ / (AmE), / steɪt ˈhaɪweɪ / (BrE)
A major public road maintained by a state government, designed for high-speed traffic.

Examples

busy state highway
Many small towns are connected by a network of state highways.
