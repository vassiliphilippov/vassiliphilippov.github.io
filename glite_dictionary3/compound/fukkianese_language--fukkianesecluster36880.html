Fukkianese language

noun
a Chinese language variety from Fujian province
Pronunciation: / ˌfʊkiəˈniːz ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌfʊkiəˈniːz ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the various forms of Chinese spoken in Fujian (Fukien) province and neighboring regions, also known as Hokkien or Min Nan.

Examples

Fukkianese language speakers
Many overseas Chinese communities speak Fukkianese language at home.
She is fluent in both Mandarin and Fukkianese language.
The shopkeeper answered my question in Fukkianese language, which I could barely understand.
