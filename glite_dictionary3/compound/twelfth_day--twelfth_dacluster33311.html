Twelfth day

noun
twelfth day after Christmas; Christian feast
Pronunciation: / twɛlfθ deɪ / (AmE), / twɛlfθ deɪ / (BrE)
The twelfth day after Christmas, often observed as the day commemorating the visit of the three wise men to the infant Jesus.

Examples

Twelfth day celebration
Children acted out the story of the wise men on the Twelfth day pageant.
Many people take down their Christmas decorations on the Twelfth day.
The church held a special service to celebrate the Twelfth day of Christmas.
