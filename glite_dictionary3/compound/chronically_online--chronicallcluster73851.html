chronically online

adjective
excessively online; overly absorbed in internet life
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɑnɪkli ɑnˈlaɪn / (AmE), / krəˈnɒnɪkli ɒnˈlaɪn / (BrE)
Spending an excessive or constant amount of time on the internet, often leading to social, emotional, or mental fatigue.

Examples

a chronically online person
Her chronically online lifestyle left her feeling disconnected from real-life interactions.
It's easy to become chronically online when your work and social life are both centered around the internet.
Many young people today are described as chronically online due to their constant internet use.

Related Terms

  • synonym – internet addict: a person addicted to the internet
  • antonym – offline: not connected to or using the internet
