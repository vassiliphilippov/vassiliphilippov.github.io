wage packet

noun
envelope with employee's wages
Pronunciation: / weɪdʒ ˈpækɪt / (AmE), / weɪdʒ ˈpakɪt / (BrE)
an envelope containing an employee's wages, typically given weekly

Examples

wage packet on payday
Every Friday, he eagerly opened his wage packet to see how much he had earned.
She saved part of her first wage packet to buy a new coat.
The workers complained when their wage packets were smaller than expected.
