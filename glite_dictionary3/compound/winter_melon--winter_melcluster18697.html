winter melon

noun
a type of large, pale-fleshed melon
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntɚ ˌmɛlən /; / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˌmɛl.ən / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˌmɛlən /; / ˈwɪn.tə ˌmɛl.ən / (BrE)
a large, round or oblong fruit with pale green or white flesh and a smooth rind, commonly used in Asian cooking; also, the plant that produces this fruit
