powder-post damage

noun
wood damage from powder-post beetles
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ poʊst ˈdæmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə pəʊst ˈdamɪdʒ / (BrE)
Damage to wood caused by infestation of powder-post beetles, resulting in fine, powdery dust and weakened structure.

Examples
