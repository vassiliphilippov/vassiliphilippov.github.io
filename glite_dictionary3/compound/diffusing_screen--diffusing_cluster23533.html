diffusing screen

noun
transparent filter that scatters and reduces light
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjuzɪŋ skrin / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːzɪŋ skriːn / (BrE)
a transparent device or filter that reduces and scatters light passing through it, often to soften illumination or reduce glare

Examples

diffusing screen for soft light
A diffusing screen can help eliminate unwanted shadows when lighting a subject.
Modern laboratories often equip microscopes with a diffusing screen for even illumination.
The photographer used a diffusing screen to soften the harsh sunlight during the outdoor shoot.

Related Terms

  • synonym – diffuser: a device that spreads or scatters light or sound
