tiger moth

noun
colorful moth; woolly bear larva
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡər mɒθ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə mɒθ / (BrE)
A medium-sized moth, often with brightly colored and patterned wings; its caterpillars are known as woolly bears.

Examples

tiger moth caterpillar
Several tiger moths fluttered around the porch light on a summer evening.
The tiger moth is famous for its striking orange-and-black wing patterns.
The caterpillar of the tiger moth is commonly called a woolly bear.
