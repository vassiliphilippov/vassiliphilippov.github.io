sampling station

noun
site for collecting samples or data
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmplɪŋ ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmplɪŋ ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
a location equipped for collecting samples or making observations for scientific or monitoring purposes

Examples

sampling station for air quality
Air pollution levels were measured at a sampling station near the busy highway.
Each sampling station was marked with a flag to make it easier to locate during the study.
The researchers visited each sampling station along the river to collect water quality data.

  • synonym – observation post: a place from which observations are made, especially for scientific or military purposes
