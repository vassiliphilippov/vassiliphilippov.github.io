youth-on-age

noun
a perennial herb that produces new plants on leaves
Pronunciation: / juθ ɑn eɪdʒ / (AmE), / juːθ ɒn eɪdʒ / (BrE)
A vigorous perennial herb, known for its erect racemes of flowers and the development of young plants where the leaf blade meets the leafstalk; commonly used as a name for certain Tolmiea species.

youth-on-age plant leaves
