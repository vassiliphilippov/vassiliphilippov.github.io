health food

noun
food believed to promote good health
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθ fuːd / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθ fuːd / (BrE)
food that is considered to be especially good for one's health, often because it is natural or has not been processed

Examples

health food store

The café offers a wide selection of health food options.
