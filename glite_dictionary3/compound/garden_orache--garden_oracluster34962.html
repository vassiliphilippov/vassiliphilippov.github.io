garden orache

noun
an edible leafy plant, also called mountain spinach
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən ˈɔːrətʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdən ˈɒrətʃ / (BrE)
An Asiatic annual plant (Atriplex hortensis) resembling spinach, often cultivated as a potherb and naturalized in Europe and North America.
