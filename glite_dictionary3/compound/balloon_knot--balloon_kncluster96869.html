balloon knot

noun
knot used to seal balloons
Pronunciation: / bəˈluːn nɑt / (AmE), / bəˈluːn nɒt / (BrE)
a knot made to securely seal the opening of an inflated balloon

Examples

balloon knot on a party balloon
He twisted the end and tied a balloon knot to keep the air inside.
She learned how to make a tight balloon knot for her party decorations.
Without a proper balloon knot, the helium quickly escaped from the balloon.
