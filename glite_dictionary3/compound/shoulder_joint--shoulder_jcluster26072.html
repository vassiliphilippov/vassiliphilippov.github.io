shoulder joint

noun
the joint connecting the upper arm and shoulder
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊldər dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
the ball-and-socket joint where the head of the humerus fits into a cavity of the scapula, allowing movement of the arm

Examples

painful shoulder joint
Arthritis can cause pain and stiffness in the shoulder joint.
