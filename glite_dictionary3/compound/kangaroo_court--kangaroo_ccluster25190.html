kangaroo court

noun
unofficial or sham court
Pronunciation: / ˌkæŋɡəˈru kɔrt /; / ˌkæŋɡəˈru ˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˌkæŋɡəˈruː kɔːt /; / ˌkæŋɡəˈruː ˌkɔːt / (BrE)
an unofficial or fake court or tribunal that ignores proper legal procedures and gives a biased or unfair decision
