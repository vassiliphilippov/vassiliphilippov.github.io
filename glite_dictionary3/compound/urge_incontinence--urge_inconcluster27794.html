urge incontinence

noun
loss of urine after sudden urge to urinate
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːdʒ ɪnˈkɑn.tɪ.nəns / (AmE), / ˈɜːdʒ ɪnˈkɒn.tɪ.nəns / (BrE)
A type of urinary incontinence characterized by a sudden, intense urge to urinate, followed by involuntary loss of urine, often caused by an overactive bladder muscle.

Examples

urge incontinence symptoms
Managing urge incontinence often involves bladder training and lifestyle changes.
Older adults are particularly susceptible to urge incontinence due to changes in bladder control.
The doctor diagnosed her with urge incontinence after she described her symptoms of frequent sudden urges to urinate.
