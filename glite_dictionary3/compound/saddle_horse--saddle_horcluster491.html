saddle horse

noun
horse kept for riding
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsædəl hɔːs / (BrE)
a horse, typically lightweight, that is bred or kept specifically for riding rather than for work or pulling loads

Examples

ride a saddle horse
After purchasing a new saddle horse, he spent weeks training it for competitions.
She preferred to ride her saddle horse through the countryside every morning.
The ranch keeps several saddle horses for guests to use on trail rides.

Related Terms

  • synonym – riding horse: a horse used for riding
  • antonym – workhorse: a horse used for work or pulling loads
