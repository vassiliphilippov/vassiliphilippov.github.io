cluster headache

noun
type of severe recurrent headache
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌstər ˌhɛdeɪk / (AmE), / ˈklʌstə ˌhɛdeɪk / (BrE)
A severe, recurring headache typically affecting one side of the head, often around the eye, and occurring in cyclical patterns or clusters.

Examples

severe cluster headache
He was diagnosed with cluster headache after experiencing excruciating pain around his eye every morning.
Many patients with cluster headache describe the pain as more intense than a migraine.
The neurologist recommended specific medications to help prevent cluster headache attacks.
