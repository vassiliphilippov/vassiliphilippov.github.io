head smut

noun
fungal disease affecting crop heads
Pronunciation: / hɛd smʌt / (AmE), / hɛd smʌt / (BrE)
a fungal disease of corn or sorghum in which the flowering head is replaced by a mass of black spores covered by a membrane

Examples

head smut infects corn
Proper crop rotation can help reduce the incidence of head smut in the field.
Researchers are working to breed resistant varieties to prevent head smut from damaging sorghum crops.
The farmer was concerned when he saw head smut developing on several ears of corn.
