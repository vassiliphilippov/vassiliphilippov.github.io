roller skate

noun
shoe with wheels for skating
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə skeɪt / (BrE)
A shoe fitted with small wheels arranged in pairs, used for skating on hard surfaces.

Examples

a pair of roller skates
His old roller skates were hidden in the attic for years.
She learned to roller skate at the local rink as a child.
Wearing her new roller skates, she glided smoothly across the pavement.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ice skate: shoe with a metal blade for skating on ice
  • synonym – rollerblade: boot with a single row of wheels used for skating
