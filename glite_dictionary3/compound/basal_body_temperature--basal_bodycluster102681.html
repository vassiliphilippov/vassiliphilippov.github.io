basal body temperature

noun
body temperature upon waking
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsəl ˈbɑdi ˌtɛmpɚˈtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪs(ə)l ˈbɒdi ˌtɛmp(ə)ˈtʃə / (BrE)
