potato beetle

noun
beetle that feeds on potato plants
Pronunciation: / pəˈteɪtoʊ ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / pəˈteɪtəʊ ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a black-and-yellow beetle that feeds on potato plants, especially in both its adult and larval stages

Examples
