genus Gasterophilus

noun
type genus of horse botflies
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌgæstəˈrɑfɪləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌɡastəˈrɒfɪləs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Gasterophilidae, comprising the horse botflies, which are parasitic insects that lay eggs on horses.
