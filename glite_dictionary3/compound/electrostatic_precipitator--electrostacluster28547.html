electrostatic precipitator

noun
device removing dust from gases by electric charge
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈstætɪk prɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk prɪˌsɪpɪˈteɪtə / (BrE)
