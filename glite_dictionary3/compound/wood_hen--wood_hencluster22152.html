wood hen

noun
flightless New Zealand bird (weka)
Pronunciation: / wʊd hɛn / (AmE), / wʊd hɛn / (BrE)
a flightless bird native to New Zealand, known for its bold and inquisitive behavior, with short wings each bearing a spur used in fighting
