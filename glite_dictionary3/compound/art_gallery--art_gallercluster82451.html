art gallery

noun
a place for exhibiting artworks
Pronunciation: / ɑrt ˈɡæləri / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈɡæləri / (BrE)
a place, either a building or an online space, where works of art are displayed for the public to view and often to purchase

Examples

visit an art gallery

The museum partnered with a virtual art gallery to host a digital exhibition during the festival.
Students curated a themed show in a popular online art gallery to practice exhibiting and selling work.

Related Terms

  • synonym – museum: a building where objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are exhibited
  • synonym – online gallery: a website or platform that displays and sells artworks
  • synonym – virtual exhibition: an exhibition of artworks presented in a digital or online format
