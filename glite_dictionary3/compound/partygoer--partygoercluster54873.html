partygoer

noun
person who attends parties
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrtiˌɡoʊər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtiˌɡəʊə / (BrE)

Examples

regular partygoer
As a frequent partygoer, she always knows where the best events are.
