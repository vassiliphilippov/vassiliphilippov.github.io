scale moss

noun
a type of moss-like liverwort
Pronunciation: / skeɪl mɑs / (AmE), / skeɪl mɒs / (BrE)
a moss-like liverwort with tiny, scalelike leaves, usually growing on other plants (epiphytic)

Examples

scale moss on tree bark
Researchers discovered a rare species of scale moss growing on the ancient oak trees.
The forest floor was covered with scale moss and other tiny plants.
Unlike true mosses, scale moss has scalelike leaves and often lives as an epiphyte.
