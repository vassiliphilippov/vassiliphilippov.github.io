railway car

noun
vehicle running on railway tracks
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪl.weɪ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ kɑː / (BrE)
A vehicle with wheels that runs on railway tracks, used for transporting passengers or goods.

Examples

The freight railway car was loaded with logs for delivery to the mill.
