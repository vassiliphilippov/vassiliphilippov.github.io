boysenberry bush

noun
hybrid bramble bearing boysenberries
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪzənˌbɛri bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪz(ə)nˌbɛri bʊʃ / (BrE)
A cultivated hybrid bramble, especially grown in California, that produces large dark wine-red fruit with a taste similar to raspberries.

Examples

boysenberry bush in the garden
Gardeners often train a boysenberry bush along a trellis for better support and sun exposure.
She pruned the old boysenberry bush to encourage new growth in the spring.
The boysenberry bush in our backyard produces fruit every summer.
