back-of-the-envelope

adjective
quick, informal calculation or plan
Pronunciation: / ˌbæk əv ði ɛnˌvəˈloʊp / (AmE), / ˌbæk əv ði ˈɒn.və.ləʊp / (BrE)
Done quickly and informally, without detailed analysis; used for rough calculations or plans.
