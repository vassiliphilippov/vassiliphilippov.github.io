adrenocorticotropic hormone

noun
hormone stimulating adrenal cortex
Pronunciation: / əˌdriːnoʊˌkɔːrtɪkoʊˈtrɑːpɪk ˈhɔːrmoʊn / (AmE), / əˌdriːnəʊˌkɔːtɪkəʊˈtrɒpɪk ˈhɔːməʊn / (BrE)
a hormone produced by the anterior pituitary gland that stimulates the adrenal cortex to secrete steroid hormones
