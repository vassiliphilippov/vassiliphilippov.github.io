monastic habit

noun
long garment worn by monks
Pronunciation: / məˈnæstɪk ˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / məˈnastɪk ˈhabɪt / (BrE)
a long, loose garment traditionally worn by monks in a monastery as a sign of their religious order
