Northern Rhodesia

proper noun
former British colony; now Zambia
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərn roʊˈdiːʒə / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən rəʊˈdiːzɪə / (BrE)
the former name of Zambia when it was a British protectorate in south-central Africa, prior to independence in 1964
