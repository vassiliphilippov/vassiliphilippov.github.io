suborder Brachyura

noun
group of crustaceans; true crabs
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˌɔrdər brəˈkjuːərə / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˌɔːdə brəˈkjʊərə / (BrE)
A group of crustaceans, including true crabs, characterized by a reduced abdomen folded against the underside of the body.
