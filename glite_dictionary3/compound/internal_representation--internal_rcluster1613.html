internal representation

noun
a mental idea or image of something
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɜrnəl ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːnəl ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a mental construction or image that represents external reality within the mind, often as an idea, image, or concept.
