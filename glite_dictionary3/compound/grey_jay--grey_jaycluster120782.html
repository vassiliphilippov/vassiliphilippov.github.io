grey jay

noun
jay of northern North America
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈdʒeɪ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈdʒeɪ / (BrE)
a jay of northern North America with a black-capped head and no crest, known for its bold behavior and tendency to steal food
