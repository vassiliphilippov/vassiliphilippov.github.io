morning dress

noun
men's formal daytime suit
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ ˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ ˌdrɛs / (BrE)
formal men's daytime attire consisting of a tailcoat, waistcoat, and striped trousers, typically worn at weddings and formal events

Examples
