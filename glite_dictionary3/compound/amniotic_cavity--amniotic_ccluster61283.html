amniotic cavity

noun
fluid-filled space around an embryo
Pronunciation: / ˌæmniˈɑtɪk ˈkævəti / (AmE), / ˌamniˈɒtɪk ˈkævəti / (BrE)
The fluid-filled space surrounding the developing embryo within the amniotic sac.

Examples

the amniotic cavity surrounds embryo
During pregnancy, the amniotic cavity gradually expands as the embryo grows.
The amniotic cavity provides a protected environment for the developing fetus.
Ultrasound scans can reveal abnormalities in the amniotic cavity early in gestation.
