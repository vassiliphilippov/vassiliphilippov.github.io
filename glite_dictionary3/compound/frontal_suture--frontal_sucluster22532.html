frontal suture

noun
suture between halves of the frontal bone
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.təl ˈsuː.tʃər / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.tl̩ ˈsuː.tjʊə / (BrE)
the suture between the two halves of the frontal bone of the skull, usually disappearing by age six

Examples

baby frontal suture
An X-ray revealed that the child's frontal suture was still visible.
In rare cases, the frontal suture persists into adulthood.
The pediatrician explained that the frontal suture closes by the age of six.
