cloud of smoke

noun
a large mass of smoke in the air
Pronunciation: / klaʊd əv smoʊk / (AmE), / klaʊd əv sməʊk / (BrE)
a large, visible mass of smoke in the air, often produced by fire or combustion

Examples

thick cloud of smoke
A sudden cloud of smoke made everyone cough and cover their faces.
The burning building sent a thick cloud of smoke billowing into the sky.
