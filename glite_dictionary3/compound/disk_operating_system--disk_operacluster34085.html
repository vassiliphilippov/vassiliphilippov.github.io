disk operating system

noun
operating system on a disk
Pronunciation: / dɪsk ˈɑpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / dɪsk ˈɒpəˌreɪtɪŋ ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
