French horn

noun
brass instrument with coiled tube and valves
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ hɔrn / (AmE), / frɛntʃ hɔːn / (BrE)
a brass musical instrument with a coiled conical tube and valves, producing a mellow, rounded tone
