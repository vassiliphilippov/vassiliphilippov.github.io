Great Smoky Mountains

proper noun
mountain range between North Carolina and Tennessee
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈsmoʊki ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈsməʊki ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range forming part of the Appalachians on the border between North Carolina and Tennessee, known for its misty appearance and rich biodiversity
