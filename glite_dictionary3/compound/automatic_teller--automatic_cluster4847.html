automatic teller

noun
cash-dispensing bank machine
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmætɪk ˈtɛlər / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk ˈtɛlə / (BrE)
A machine, typically located outside banks, that allows customers to withdraw cash and perform basic banking tasks using a coded card, without a human teller.

Examples

automatic teller machine
After losing her wallet, she reported her card stolen at the automatic teller.
He withdrew fifty dollars from the automatic teller late last night.
There was a long queue at the automatic teller during lunchtime.
