at worst

adverb
in the worst possible scenario
Pronunciation: / æt ˈwɜrst / (AmE), / at ˈwɜːst / (BrE)
in the least favorable or most negative situation; considering the most serious possible outcome

Examples

at worst in a crisis
At worst, we'll be delayed by an hour if the traffic is bad.
The project will cost $5,000 at worst, so we should be prepared for that expense.
You may have to start over, but at worst it's just a minor setback.

  • antonym – at best: in the best possible situation
