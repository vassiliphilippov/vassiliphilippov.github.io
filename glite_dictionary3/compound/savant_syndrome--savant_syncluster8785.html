savant syndrome

noun
condition where mental disability coincides with exceptional abilities
Pronunciation: / səˈvɑnt ˌsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / səˈvɑ̃ ˌsɪn.drəʊm / (BrE)
A rare condition in which a person with a mental disability, such as autism, exhibits exceptional skill or talent in a specific area, such as mathematics, music, or art.
