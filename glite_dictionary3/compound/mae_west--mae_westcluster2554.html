Mae West

noun
inflatable life jacket (WWII era)
Pronunciation: / meɪ wɛst / (AmE), / meɪ wɛst / (BrE)
a type of inflatable life jacket, especially one used by aircrew during World War II, so called because of its resemblance to the actress's figure

Examples
