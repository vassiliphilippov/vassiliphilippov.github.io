early-blooming

adjective
flowering early in the season
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrli ˈbluːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɜːli ˈbluːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) flowering during the early part of the growing season, especially in spring.

Examples

early-blooming flowers
Some early-blooming trees are vulnerable to late frosts.
The early-blooming tulips add a splash of color to the garden in March.
We planted several early-blooming perennials to ensure springtime blossoms.
