sun lamp

noun
lamp for ultraviolet light therapy or tanning
Pronunciation: / sʌn læmp / (AmE), / sʌn læmp / (BrE)
a lamp, often using a mercury-vapor bulb, that emits ultraviolet light and is used for medical treatment or cosmetic purposes such as tanning

Examples

use a sun lamp for tanning
After a few sessions under the sun lamp, her skin gained a noticeable tan.
He bought a sun lamp to help with winter blues during the darker months.
Many dermatologists recommend using a sun lamp to treat certain skin conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tanning lamp: lamp used to produce an artificial suntan
  • synonym – ultraviolet lamp: lamp that emits ultraviolet light
