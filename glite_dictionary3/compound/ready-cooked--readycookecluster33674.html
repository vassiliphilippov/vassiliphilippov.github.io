ready-cooked

adjective
cooked in advance and ready to eat or sell
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdi kʊkt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdi kʊkt / (BrE)
(of food) cooked in advance and ready to be sold or eaten without further preparation.

Examples

ready-cooked meal
Many restaurants now provide ready-cooked options for takeaway customers.
She bought some ready-cooked chicken to save time on dinner.
The supermarket offers a wide range of ready-cooked meals for busy families.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pre-cooked: cooked before packaging or sale
