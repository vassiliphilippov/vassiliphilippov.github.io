Pearl River

proper noun
river in southeast China
Pronunciation: / pɜrl ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / pɜːl ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in southeastern China that flows into the South China Sea, passing through Guangzhou and other cities

Examples
