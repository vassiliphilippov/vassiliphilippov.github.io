table of contents

noun
list of chapters or sections at the start of a book
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəl əv ˈkɑntɛnts / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl əv ˈkɒntɛnts / (BrE)
a list at the beginning of a book or document showing its chapters or sections and the pages on which they start

Examples

table of contents in a book
Every report should include a table of contents before the main sections.
Please refer to the table of contents for a list of included articles.
The table of contents made it easy to find each chapter in the textbook.
