pink family

noun
family of flowering plants (Caryophyllaceae)
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk ˈfæməli / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants, mostly herbs or subshrubs, typically having stems swollen at the nodes; includes carnations and campions (family Caryophyllaceae)
