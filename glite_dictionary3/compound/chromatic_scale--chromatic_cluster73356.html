chromatic scale

noun
12-note scale with all semitones in an octave
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈmætɪk skeɪl / (AmE), / krəʊˈmatɪk skeɪl / (BrE)
a musical scale consisting of twelve pitches, each a semitone above or below another, including all semitones within an octave
