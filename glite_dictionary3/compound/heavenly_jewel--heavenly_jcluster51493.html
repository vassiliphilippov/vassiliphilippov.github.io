Heavenly Jewel

noun
first deity of the Taoist Trinity
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvənli ˈdʒuːəl / (AmE), / ˈhɛv(ə)nli ˈdʒuːəl / (BrE)
In Taoism, the first of the three supreme deities known as the Taoist Trinity.
