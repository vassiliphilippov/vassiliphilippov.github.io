Baltic Sea

proper noun
a sea in northern Europe
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔltɪk ˌsi / (AmE), / ˈbɔːltɪk ˌsiː / (BrE)
an arm of the Atlantic Ocean in northern Europe, bordered by countries including Sweden, Finland, Russia, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Poland, Germany, and Denmark.

Examples

the Baltic Sea coast
Fishing is a major industry in the Baltic Sea region.
The Baltic Sea is bordered by several European countries, including Sweden and Finland.
The environmental health of the Baltic Sea is threatened by pollution and overfishing.
