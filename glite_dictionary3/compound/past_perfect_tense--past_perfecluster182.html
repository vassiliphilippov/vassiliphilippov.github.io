past perfect tense

noun
a verb tense for completed past actions
Pronunciation: / pæst ˈpɝː.fɪkt tɛns / (AmE), / pɑːst ˈpɜː.fɪkt tɛns / (BrE)
a verb tense used to express an action that was completed before another action or time in the past

Examples

past perfect tense in a sentence
'Had finished' in 'I had finished my homework before dinner' is an example of the past perfect tense.
English learners often struggle to use the past perfect tense correctly in conversation.
In the sentence 'She had left before I arrived,' the verb is in the past perfect tense.
