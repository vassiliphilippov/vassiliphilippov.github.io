house sparrow

noun
a small brown-and-grey European bird
Pronunciation: / haʊs ˈspɛroʊ / (AmE), / haʊs ˈspærəʊ / (BrE)
a small, hardy brown-and-grey bird (Passer domesticus) native to Europe and widely introduced elsewhere
