sulfonic acid

noun
organic acid with –SO₃H group
Pronunciation: / sʌlˈfɑnɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / sʌlˈfɒnɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
A class of organic acids derived from sulfuric acid, characterized by the presence of the –SO₃H group.
