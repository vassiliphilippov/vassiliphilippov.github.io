scalene triangle

noun
triangle with unequal sides
Pronunciation: / skeɪˈlin ˈtraɪæŋɡəl / (AmE), / skeɪˈliːn ˈtraɪæŋɡl / (BrE)
A triangle having all sides of different lengths and all angles of different measures.

Examples

a scalene triangle shape
In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the area of a scalene triangle.
No two angles in a scalene triangle are the same.
The sides of a scalene triangle all have different lengths.
