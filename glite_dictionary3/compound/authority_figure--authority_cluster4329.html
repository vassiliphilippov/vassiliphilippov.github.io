authority figure

noun
person with recognized authority or power
Pronunciation: / əˈθɔrəti ˌfɪɡjər / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒrəti ˌfɪɡə / (BrE)
a person who is regarded as having power or control, especially because of their position, expertise, or influence
