Lake Keuka

proper noun
Y-shaped glacial lake in New York State
Pronunciation: / leɪk ˈkjuːkə / (AmE), / leɪk ˈkjuːkə / (BrE)
A glacial lake in central New York State, distinguished as the only Y-shaped lake among the Finger Lakes.
