South Ossetia

proper noun
disputed territory in the South Caucasus
Pronunciation: / saʊθ oʊˈsiːtiə / (AmE), / saʊθ əʊˈsiːtiə / (BrE)
a region in the South Caucasus that declared independence from Georgia but is recognized by most countries as part of Georgia; considered a disputed territory
