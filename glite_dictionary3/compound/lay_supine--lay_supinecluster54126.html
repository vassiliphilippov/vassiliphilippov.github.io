to lay supine

verb
to lie on one's back, face upward
Pronunciation: / leɪ suˈpaɪn / (AmE), / leɪ sjuːˈpaɪn / (BrE)
to lie on one's back with the face or front upward

Examples

to lay supine on a mat
After the long hike, she lay supine on her yoga mat to rest.
He preferred to lay supine in the sun, letting the warmth wash over him.
The patient was instructed to lay supine on the examination table.
