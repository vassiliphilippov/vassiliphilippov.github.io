ion pump

noun
vacuum pump using ionization to remove gas
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.ɑn ˌpʌmp / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ɒn ˌpʌmp / (BrE)
a vacuum pump that removes gas by ionizing gas atoms or molecules and adsorbing them onto a metal surface
