kauri resin

noun
resin from New Zealand kauri trees
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊri ˈrɛzɪn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊəri ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
a pale, hard resin obtained from kauri trees of New Zealand, often found as a fossil and used in making varnishes and linoleum
