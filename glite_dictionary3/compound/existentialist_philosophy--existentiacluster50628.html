existentialist philosophy

noun
philosophical movement on individual freedom and responsibility
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˌzɪstɛnʃəˌlɪst fəˈlɑsəfi / (AmE), / ɛɡˌzɪstənʃəlɪst fəˈlɒsəfi / (BrE)
A philosophical movement of the 20th century, primarily in Europe, asserting that individuals are entirely free and responsible for creating their own meaning and values.
