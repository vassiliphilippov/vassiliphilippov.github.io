poorly designed

adjective
not well planned or constructed
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊrli dɪˈzaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈpɔːli dɪˈzaɪnd / (BrE)
not well planned, constructed, or arranged; lacking quality in design

Examples

poorly designed product
A poorly designed questionnaire can result in unreliable survey data.
Many customers complained that the app was poorly designed and difficult to use.
The bridge was poorly designed, leading to safety concerns after just a few years.
