kingwood tree

noun
Brazilian tree supplying decorative hardwood
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋ.wʊd triː / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋ.wʊd triː / (BrE)
A small Brazilian tree (Dalbergia cearensis) that produces hard, richly colored wood used especially in fine cabinetmaking.
