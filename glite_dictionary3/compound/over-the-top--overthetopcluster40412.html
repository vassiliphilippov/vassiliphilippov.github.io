over-the-top

adjective
excessive or exaggerated
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvər ðə ˈtɑp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvə ðə ˈtɒp / (BrE)

The film's special effects are sometimes over-the-top, but they're entertaining nonetheless.
