banded rattlesnake

noun
a venomous rattlesnake of eastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈbændɪd ˈrætlˌsneɪk / (AmE), / ˈbændɪd ˈrætlˌsneɪk / (BrE)
A venomous rattlesnake species with distinct dark bands, found in rugged regions of the eastern United States; also known as the timber rattlesnake.
