blazoned trail

noun
clearly marked path or trail
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪzənd treɪl / (AmE), / ˈbleɪzənd treɪl / (BrE)
A path, especially for walking or hiking, that is clearly marked with signs, paint, or other indicators.

Examples

blazoned trail through forest
A blazoned trail was marked with blue paint on the trees every few meters.
She preferred to stick to the blazoned trail rather than risk getting lost.
The hikers easily followed the blazoned trail through the dense woods.
