Shrove Tuesday

noun
the day before Lent in Christianity
Pronunciation: / ʃroʊv ˈtuːzdeɪ / (AmE), / ʃrəʊv ˈtjuːzdeɪ / (BrE)
The day immediately preceding Ash Wednesday, observed by Christians as the last day before Lent, often marked by confession and celebration, including the tradition of eating pancakes.

Examples

Shrove Tuesday pancake day
Shrove Tuesday is celebrated in some countries with parades and street festivals.
Many families gather to make pancakes on Shrove Tuesday.
Some people use Shrove Tuesday as a time to prepare spiritually for the start of Lent.
