red drum

noun
large edible fish found off US coast
Pronunciation: / rɛd drʌm / (AmE), / rɛd drʌm / (BrE)
a large edible fish (Sciaenops ocellatus) found along the Atlantic coast of the United States from Massachusetts to Mexico, valued both as a food fish and game fish

Examples

red drum fishing trip
Commercial fishermen have established regulations to protect the red drum population.
Many anglers prize the red drum for its fighting spirit and delicious taste.
