two-furrow plough

noun
plough with two moldboards
Pronunciation: / tuː ˈfɝːroʊ plaʊ / (AmE), / tuː ˈfʌrəʊ plaʊ / (BrE)
a plough equipped with two moldboards, allowing it to cut two furrows simultaneously
