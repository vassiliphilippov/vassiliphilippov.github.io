radio silence

noun
a period of no communication
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdioʊ ˈsaɪləns / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊ ˈsaɪləns / (BrE)
a period during which no communication or transmission occurs, especially after regular contact has stopped

Examples

radio silence from management
After the news broke, there was radio silence from the company for days.
Since our last conversation, she has maintained complete radio silence.
We waited anxiously during the radio silence between updates from the rescue team.
