shower mitt

noun
mitt used for washing the body
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊɚ mɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə mɪt / (BrE)
A mitt made of coarse material used for washing and scrubbing the body in the shower.

Examples

use a shower mitt to scrub
He bought a new shower mitt to replace his old one that had worn out.
She exfoliates her skin every morning using a shower mitt.
The shower mitt helps remove dead skin cells more effectively than a washcloth.
