civic center

noun
community building for social and cultural events
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvɪk ˌsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪk ˌsɛntə / (BrE)
A building or complex used as a focal point for social, cultural, or community activities within a town or city.
