writing assignment

noun
task requiring written work
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪŋ əˌsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ əˌsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
a task in which someone is required to write about a particular topic, often as part of a course or class

Examples

school writing assignment
For his college application, he had to complete a personal writing assignment.
My teacher gave us a challenging writing assignment on climate change.
She finished her writing assignment a day before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym – essay: a short piece of writing on a particular subject
  • synonym – homework: schoolwork assigned to be done outside the classroom
