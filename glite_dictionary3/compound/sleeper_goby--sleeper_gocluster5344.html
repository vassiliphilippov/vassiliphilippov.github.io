sleeper goby

noun
a tropical fish resembling a goby
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpər ˌɡoʊbi / (AmE), / ˈsliːpə ˌɡəʊbi / (BrE)
A tropical fish similar in appearance to a goby, known for resting quietly on the bottom in shallow water.
