smash hit

noun
extremely successful entertainment product
Pronunciation: / smæʃ hɪt / (AmE), / smæʃ hɪt / (BrE)
A song, movie, play, book, or other entertainment product that achieves overwhelming popular success and high sales.

Examples

smash hit movie
After the band's smash hit single, their popularity soared internationally.
Her debut novel quickly became a smash hit and topped bestseller lists around the world.
The movie was expected to do well, but no one predicted it would be such a smash hit.
