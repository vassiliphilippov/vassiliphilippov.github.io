pop feminism

noun
mainstream, popular-culture feminism
Pronunciation: / pɑp ˈfɛmɪnɪzəm / (AmE), / pɒp ˈfɛmɪnɪzəm / (BrE)
A contemporary form of feminism that is present in popular culture, often emphasizing mainstream ideas and accessible messaging about gender equality.
