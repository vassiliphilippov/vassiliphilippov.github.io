bell deck

noun
floor under bells in belfry
Pronunciation: / bɛl dɛk / (AmE), / bɛl dɛk / (BrE)
The floor located beneath the bells in an open belfry, usually providing support and access within a bell tower.

Examples

bell deck in a tower
Renovations included reinforcing the bell deck to support the newly cast bronze bells.
