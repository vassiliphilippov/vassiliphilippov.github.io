Trillium erectum

noun
a species of trillium with malodorous flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪliəm ɪˈrɛktəm / (AmE), / ˈtrɪlɪəm ɪˈrɛktəm / (BrE)
A species of trillium native to eastern North America, known for its malodorous pink to purple flowers and an astringent root traditionally used in folk medicine.
