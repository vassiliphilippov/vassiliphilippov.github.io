business card

noun
card with personal/business information
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪz.nəs kɑrd /; / ˈbɪznəs kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbɪz.nəs kɑːd /; / ˈbɪznəs kɑːd / (BrE)
a small printed card with a person's name, job title, company, and contact information, used for professional purposes

Examples
