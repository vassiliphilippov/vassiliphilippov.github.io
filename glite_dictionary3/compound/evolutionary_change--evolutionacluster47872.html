evolutionary change

noun
gradual development or transformation
Pronunciation: / ˌiː.vəˈluː.ʃəˌnɛr.i tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌiː.vəˈljuː.ʃən.ər.i tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a gradual process of development or transformation, especially through small, incremental steps over time

Examples

gradual evolutionary change
In biology, species experience evolutionary change over many generations.
Technological progress is often the result of evolutionary change rather than sudden breakthroughs.
