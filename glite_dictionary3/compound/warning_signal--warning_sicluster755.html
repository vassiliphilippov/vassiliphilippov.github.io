warning signal

noun
signal indicating danger or alert
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrnɪŋ ˌsɪɡnəl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnɪŋ ˌsɪɡnəl / (BrE)
a sound or visual signal that automatically indicates danger or requires urgent attention

Examples

loud warning signal
A loud horn acts as a warning signal on construction sites to alert workers of danger.
The fire alarm sounded as a warning signal when smoke was detected in the building.
The flashing lights serve as a warning signal for approaching trains at the crossing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alarm: a device giving warning of danger, especially by sound or signal
  • synonym – alert: an automatic notification of a particular problem or danger
