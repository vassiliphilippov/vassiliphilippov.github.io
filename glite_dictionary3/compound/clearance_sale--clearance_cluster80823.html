clearance sale

noun
sale to clear out stock
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrəns seɪl / (AmE), / ˈklɪə.rəns seɪl / (BrE)
an event at which goods are sold at reduced prices to clear out stock, typically at the end of a season or before closing a business
