soft-finned fish

noun
fish with soft, flexible fin rays (Malacopterygii)
Pronunciation: / sɔft fɪnd fɪʃ / (AmE), / sɒft fɪnd fɪʃ / (BrE)
A fish of the superorder Malacopterygii, characterized by having soft, flexible fin rays rather than spiny ones.
