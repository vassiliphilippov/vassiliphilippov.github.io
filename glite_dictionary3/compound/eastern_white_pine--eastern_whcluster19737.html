eastern white pine

noun
a tall timber pine of eastern North America
Pronunciation: / ˈistɚn waɪt paɪn / (AmE), / ˈiːstən waɪt paɪn / (BrE)
a tall pine tree native to eastern North America, valued for its timber and distinctive for its brown bark with longitudinal fissures when mature
