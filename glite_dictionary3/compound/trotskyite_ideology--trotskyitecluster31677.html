Trotskyite ideology

noun
political philosophy based on Leon Trotsky's ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑtskiˌaɪt aɪˈdɪɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˈtrɒtskiˌaɪt aɪˈdɪələdʒi / (BrE)
a political philosophy based on the ideas and theories of Leon Trotsky, particularly advocating permanent revolution and international socialism
