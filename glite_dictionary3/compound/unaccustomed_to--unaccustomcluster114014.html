unaccustomed to

adjective
not familiar or used to something
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈkʌstəmd tu / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈkʌstəmd tʊ / (BrE)
not familiar with something because you have not experienced it often or before; not habituated to

Examples

unaccustomed to early mornings
After years of late nights, Jane was unaccustomed to getting up early for work.
He felt nervous because he was unaccustomed to speaking in front of large crowds.
She was unaccustomed to the cold winters after moving from a tropical country.

  • antonym – used to: familiar with, habituated to
