glass cutter

noun
tool for cutting glass
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs ˈkʌtər / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a tool with a sharp wheel or blade used for scoring and cutting sheets of glass
