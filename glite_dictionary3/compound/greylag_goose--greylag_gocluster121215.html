greylag goose

noun
a species of grey wild goose of Europe
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪˌlæɡ ɡus / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌlæɡ ɡuːs / (BrE)
a common European wild goose with grey plumage, believed to be the ancestor of many domestic geese breeds
