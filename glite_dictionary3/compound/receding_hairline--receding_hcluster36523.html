receding hairline

noun
hair loss at the front of the scalp
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsidɪŋ ˈhɛrˌlaɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːdɪŋ ˈheə.laɪn / (BrE)
a gradual loss of hair at the front of the scalp, resulting in the hairline moving backward
