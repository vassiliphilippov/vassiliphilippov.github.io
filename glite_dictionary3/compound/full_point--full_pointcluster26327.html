full point

noun
punctuation mark (.) at sentence end
Pronunciation: / fʊl pɔɪnt / (AmE), / fʊl pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the punctuation mark (.) used to indicate the end of a declarative sentence or after abbreviations; called 'period' in American English.
