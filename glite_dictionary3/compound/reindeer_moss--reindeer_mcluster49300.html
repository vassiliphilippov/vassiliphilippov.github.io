reindeer moss

noun
a lichen eaten by reindeer and caribou
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌdɪr mɑs / (AmE), / ˈreɪndɪə mɒs / (BrE)
a greyish, branching lichen found in Arctic and some temperate regions, serving as the chief food for reindeer and caribou
