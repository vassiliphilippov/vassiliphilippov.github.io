second class

noun
not the highest quality; lower category
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkənd ˈklæs /; / ˈsɛkənd klæs / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈklɑːs /; / ˈsekənd klɑːs / (BrE)
a category or rank that is below the highest in quality or status
