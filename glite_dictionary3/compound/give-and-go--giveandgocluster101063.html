give-and-go

noun
basketball passing-and-cutting maneuver
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɪv ən ˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɡɪv ən ˈɡəʊ / (BrE)
A basketball maneuver in which one player passes the ball to a teammate and then runs toward the basket to receive a return pass.

Examples

basketball give-and-go play
In practice, we worked on improving our give-and-go techniques.
She scored after a quick give-and-go with her teammate near the basket.
The team executed a perfect give-and-go to break through the defense.
