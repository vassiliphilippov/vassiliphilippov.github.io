brain dead

adjective
irreversible total loss of brain function
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪn ˌdɛd / (AmE), / ˈbreɪn ˌdɛd / (BrE)
having an irreversible loss of all brain function, typically as indicated by a persistent flat electroencephalogram; clinically dead despite maintained body functions

Examples

brain dead patient
After the accident, doctors determined he was brain dead and recommended withdrawal of life support.
Once a person is declared brain dead, organ donation may become an option.
The patient's family struggled to accept the news that she was brain dead.
