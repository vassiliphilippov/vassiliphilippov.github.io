cat's eye

noun
gemstone showing a cat's eye effect
Pronunciation: / kæts aɪ / (AmE), / kæts aɪ / (BrE)
A gemstone, such as chrysoberyl or chalcedony, that exhibits a luminous band of reflected light resembling a cat’s eye when cut en cabochon.
