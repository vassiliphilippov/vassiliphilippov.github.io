bear hug

noun
wrestling hold with arms locked tightly
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛr ˌhʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈbeə ˌhʌɡ / (BrE)
A strong embrace in which one locks their arms tightly around another, especially as a wrestling hold.

Examples

give a bear hug
His bear hug was so strong it nearly took my breath away.
She gave her friend a bear hug after not seeing her for years.
The wrestler trapped his opponent in a bear hug and squeezed tightly.
