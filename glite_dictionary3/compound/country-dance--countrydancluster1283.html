country-dance

noun
traditional folk dance with couples in lines or sets
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri ˌdæns / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ˌdɑːns / (BrE)
A traditional folk dance in which couples are arranged in sets or lines, typically facing each other, and perform a series of figures to music.

Examples

a lively country-dance
At the party, everyone joined in a country-dance that lasted late into the night.
She learned the steps of a traditional English country-dance as part of her heritage.
The group performed a lively country-dance at the village festival.
