no-strings-attached

adjective
without conditions or obligations
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊ ˌstrɪŋz əˈtætʃt / (AmE), / ˌnəʊ ˌstrɪŋz əˈtætʃt / (BrE)
(of an agreement or relationship) having no special conditions or restrictions; not requiring any commitment or obligation.

Examples

no-strings-attached offer

They offered a no-strings-attached donation to the charity, asking for nothing in return.
