knowledge transfer

noun
sharing or transmission of knowledge
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑlɪdʒ ˈtrænsfɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɒlɪdʒ ˈtrɑːnsfə / (BrE)
the process of sharing or disseminating knowledge from one person, group, or organization to another

Examples

knowledge transfer between teams
Effective knowledge transfer is critical to maintaining expertise when employees leave a company.
Our training program aims to streamline knowledge transfer between new and experienced staff.
Universities often partner with industry to facilitate knowledge transfer through research collaborations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – knowledge sharing: the act of exchanging information or skills among people
