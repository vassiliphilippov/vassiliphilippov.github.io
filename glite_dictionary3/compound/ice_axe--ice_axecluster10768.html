ice axe

noun
mountaineering tool for climbing ice
Pronunciation: / aɪs ˌæks / (AmE), / aɪs ˌæks / (BrE)
A special tool with a pointed pick and an adze, used by mountaineers for cutting steps in ice and as an aid in climbing.

Examples

ice axe for climbing
Every climber packed an ice axe before setting out to cross the glacier.
He demonstrated the proper way to use an ice axe for self-arrest on a steep slope.
Her ice axe allowed her to cut secure footholds into the thick, frozen wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crampon: a metal plate with spikes, fastened to a boot for walking on ice
