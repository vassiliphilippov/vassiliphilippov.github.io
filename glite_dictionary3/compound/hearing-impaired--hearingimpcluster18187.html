hearing-impaired

adjective
having reduced or no hearing
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪrɪŋ ɪmˈpɛrd / (AmE), / ˈhɪə.rɪŋ ɪmˈpeəd / (BrE)
having reduced or limited ability to hear; experiencing partial or total hearing loss.
