interagency support

noun
assistance provided between government agencies
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˌeɪdʒənsi səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˌeɪdʒənsi səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
the provision of logistical or administrative assistance by one or more military services to other departments or agencies of a government

Examples
