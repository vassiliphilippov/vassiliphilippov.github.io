Broca's gyrus

noun
brain region for speech production
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkəz ˈdʒaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkəz ˈdʒʌɪrəs / (BrE)
the region of the brain in the left frontal lobe, usually responsible for speech production in most people

Examples

Broca's gyrus controls speech
Damage to the Broca's gyrus can result in difficulty forming coherent speech.

The surgeon carefully avoided the Broca's gyrus to preserve the patient's speech abilities.
