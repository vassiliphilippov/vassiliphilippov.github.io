Oriental Studies

noun
academic field studying Asian cultures and languages
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːriˈɛntəl ˈstʌdidz / (AmE), / ˌɔːrɪˈɛntəl ˈstʌdiz / (BrE)
the academic study of the languages, cultures, history, and societies of Asian regions, especially East and South Asia

Examples

degree in Oriental Studies
His research in Oriental Studies has contributed valuable insights into ancient Japanese culture.
She majored in Oriental Studies at university, focusing on the history of East Asia.
The department of Oriental Studies organizes lectures on Chinese language and literature every semester.
