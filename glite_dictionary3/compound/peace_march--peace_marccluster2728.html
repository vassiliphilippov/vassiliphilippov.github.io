peace march

noun
public protest march for peace
Pronunciation: / pis mɑrtʃ / (AmE), / piːs mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
an organized public march by a group of people protesting against war and calling for peace

Examples

join a peace march
Hundreds joined the peace march through the center of the city.
She carried a sign demanding nuclear disarmament at last year’s peace march.
The peace march was organized in response to growing tensions between the two countries.
