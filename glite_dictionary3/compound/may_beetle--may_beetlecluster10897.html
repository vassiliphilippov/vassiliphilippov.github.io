May beetle

noun
North American leaf-eating beetle found in late spring
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ ˌbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ ˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a large beetle, active in late spring, whose larvae feed on plant roots and can damage vegetation (found in North America and Europe)
