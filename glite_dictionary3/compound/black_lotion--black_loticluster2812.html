black lotion

noun
medical lotion with calomel and limewater
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈloʊʃən / (AmE), / blak ˈləʊʃən / (BrE)
a topical preparation of calomel and limewater formerly used to treat syphilitic sores and other skin conditions
