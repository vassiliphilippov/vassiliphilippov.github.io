blended whisky

noun
whisky made from a blend of whiskies or spirits
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛndɪd ˈwɪski / (AmE), / ˈblɛndɪd ˈwɪski / (BrE)
a whisky made by mixing two or more different whiskies or by combining whisky with neutral spirits

Examples
