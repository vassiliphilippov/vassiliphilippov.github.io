wood rabbit

noun
small rabbit native to North America
Pronunciation: / wʊd ˈræb.ɪt / (AmE), / wʊd ˈræb.ɪt / (BrE)
a small North American rabbit with greyish or brownish fur and a tail with a white underside, serving as a host for certain tick species
