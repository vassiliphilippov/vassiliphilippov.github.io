Tajiki language

noun
the Persian language of Tajikistan
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈdʒiːki ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / tɑːˈdʒiːki ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the variety of Persian spoken in Tajikistan, closely related to Farsi and used as an official language in Tajikistan

Examples

speak Tajiki language fluently
He grew up speaking Tajiki language at home with his family in Dushanbe.
She is fluent in Russian and Tajiki language after living in Tajikistan for five years.
The Tajiki language uses the Cyrillic alphabet, unlike Persian in Iran which uses Arabic script.
