one-third

noun
one of three equal parts; 1/3
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈθɝd / (AmE), / wʌn ˈθɜːd / (BrE)
each of three equal parts into which something can be divided; a third part or 1/3 of a whole.

Examples

one-third of the cake
One-third of the students did not attend the lecture.
He donated one-third of his earnings to charity.
The cake was divided into one-third portions for each child.
