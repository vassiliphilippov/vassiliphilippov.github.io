halftone engraving

noun
engraving for illustration reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfˌtoʊn ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfˌtəʊn ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
an engraving used for reproducing an illustration, especially by breaking up the image into small dots of varying size and spacing to simulate different tones

Examples

halftone engraving of a photo
He specialized in creating halftone engravings for scientific journals.
Many old books relied on halftone engravings to reproduce works of art.
The magazine featured a detailed halftone engraving to illustrate the new invention.
