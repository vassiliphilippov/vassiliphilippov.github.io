Clovis culture

noun
prehistoric North American culture
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊvɪs ˌkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈkləʊvɪs ˌkʌltʃə / (BrE)
A prehistoric Paleo-American culture in Central and North America, noted for characteristic fluted stone projectile points.

Examples

Clovis culture fluted points
Archaeologists unearthed dozens of fluted spear points linked to the Clovis culture.
Many experts consider the Clovis culture to be the earliest widespread human presence in the Americas.
