table-mountain pine

noun
a pine tree of the Appalachian uplands
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəl ˌmaʊntən paɪn / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl ˌmaʊntɪn paɪn / (BrE)
a small pine tree native to the upland regions of the eastern United States, especially the Appalachians, characterized by dark brown flaking bark and cones with thorn-tipped scales
