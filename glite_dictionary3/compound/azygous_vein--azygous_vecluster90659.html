azygous vein

noun
a vein draining the thoracic wall into the superior vena cava
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.zɪ.ɡəs veɪn / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.zɪ.ɡəs veɪn / (BrE)
a vein running up the right side of the thoracic vertebral column that drains blood from the thoracic and abdominal walls into the superior vena cava

Examples
