cluster bomblet

noun
small bomb released from a cluster bomb
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌstɚ ˈbɑm.lət / (AmE), / ˈklʌstə ˈbɒm.lət / (BrE)
one of the smaller explosive devices released from a cluster bomb, designed to disperse and detonate over a wide area
