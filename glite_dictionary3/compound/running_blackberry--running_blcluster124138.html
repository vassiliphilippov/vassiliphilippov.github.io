running blackberry

noun
trailing blackberry plant
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ ˈblækˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ ˈblækbəri / (BrE)
A type of blackberry bramble with long, trailing stems, found especially in North America.

Examples

running blackberry bush
She picked a basket of running blackberries from the edge of the forest.
The running blackberry thrives in sandy soils across parts of North America.
Unlike the upright variety, the running blackberry spreads quickly along the ground.
