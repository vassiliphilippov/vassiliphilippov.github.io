Arctic wolf

noun
a white-furred wolf of Arctic North America
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrktɪk wʊlf / (AmE), / ˈɑːktɪk wʊlf / (BrE)
A subspecies of wolf native to Arctic North America, characterized by white fur and a black-tipped tail.
