brier-wood

noun
wood from the briar root, used for pipes
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪər wʊd / (AmE), / ˈbraɪə wʊd / (BrE)
Wood from the hard, woody root of the briar (Erica arborea), often used to make tobacco pipes.
