private citizen

noun
ordinary person not in public office
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət ˈsɪtɪzən / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət ˈsɪtɪzən / (BrE)
an ordinary person who does not hold any official or public position in government or authority
