post chaise

noun
closed horse-drawn carriage (historical)
Pronunciation: / poʊst ʃeɪz / (AmE), / pəʊst ʃeɪz / (BrE)
A closed, four-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, formerly used for transporting passengers and mail.
