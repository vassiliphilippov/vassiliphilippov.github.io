multimodal bill of lading

noun
shipping document for multimodal transport
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈmoʊdəl bɪl əv ˈleɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈməʊdəl bɪl əv ˈleɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
a bill of lading that covers a shipment transported using at least two different modes of transportation (e.g., sea and rail) under a single contract

Examples

multimodal bill of lading shipment
A multimodal bill of lading simplifies the paperwork for goods moving through several transit methods.
Before accepting delivery, ensure that the multimodal bill of lading lists all relevant carriers.
The shipper requested a multimodal bill of lading to cover transportation by truck and ship.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bill of lading: a document issued by a carrier to acknowledge receipt of cargo for shipment
