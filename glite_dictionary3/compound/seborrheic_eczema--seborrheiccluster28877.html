seborrheic eczema

noun
chronic skin condition with greasy scales
Pronunciation: / sɛbəˌriɪk ˈɛk.sɪmə / (AmE), / sɛbəˌriːɪk ˈɛk.sɪmə / (BrE)
A chronic skin condition characterized by red, inflamed skin with greasy scales, typically occurring on the scalp, eyelids, or other areas rich in oil glands.
