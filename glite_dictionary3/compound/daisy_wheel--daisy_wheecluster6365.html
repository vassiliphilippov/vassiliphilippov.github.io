daisy wheel

noun
wheel-shaped printer element
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪzi ˌwiːl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪzi ˌwiːl / (BrE)
A wheel-shaped printing element with characters arranged around its edge, used in some typewriters and printers to produce text on paper.
