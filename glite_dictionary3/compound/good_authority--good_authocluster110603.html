good authority

noun
reliable source of information
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd əˈθɔrəti / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɔːˈθɒrəti / (BrE)
reliable or trustworthy source of information, especially based on the testimony of someone knowledgeable

Examples

I have it on good authority that the company will announce the merger next week.
She heard from good authority that the restaurant is closing soon.
