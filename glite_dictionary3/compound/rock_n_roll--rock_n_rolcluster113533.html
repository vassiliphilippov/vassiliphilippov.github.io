rock 'n' roll

noun
1950s-origin popular music genre
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑk ən ˈroʊl / (AmE), / ˌrɒk ən ˈrəʊl / (BrE)
A genre of popular music that originated in the 1950s, characterized by a blend of rhythm and blues and country and western influences, typically with a strong beat and simple chord progressions.
