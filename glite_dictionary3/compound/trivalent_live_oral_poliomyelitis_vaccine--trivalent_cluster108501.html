trivalent live oral poliomyelitis vaccine

noun
oral polio vaccine with three virus types
Pronunciation: / traɪˈveɪlənt laɪv ˈɔːrəl ˌpoʊlioʊˈmaɪəlɪtɪs vækˈsiːn / (AmE), / traɪˈveɪlənt lʌɪv ˈɔːrəl ˌpəʊlɪəʊˈmʌɪəlɪtɪs vakˈsiːn / (BrE)
