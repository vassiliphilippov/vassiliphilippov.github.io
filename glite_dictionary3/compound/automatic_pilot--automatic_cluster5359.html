automatic pilot

noun
device that steers vehicles automatically
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmæt̬ɪk ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
a device that automatically controls the steering of a ship, aircraft, or spacecraft to maintain a set course

Examples

automatic pilot controls plane
After takeoff, the captain switched to automatic pilot to keep the aircraft steady.
Most modern ships are equipped with an automatic pilot for ease of navigation.
The plane was able to cruise comfortably on automatic pilot during most of the flight.
