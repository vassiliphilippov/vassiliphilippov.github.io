snake charmer

noun
person who entertains by controlling snakes
Pronunciation: / sneɪk ˈtʃɑrmər / (AmE), / sneɪk ˈtʃɑːmə / (BrE)
a person who performs tricks or entertains people by appearing to control snakes, typically using music or movements
