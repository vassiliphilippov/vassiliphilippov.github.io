biogeographical region

noun
area defined by flora and fauna distribution
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊ.dʒioʊˈɡræf.ɪ.kəl ˈriː.dʒən / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊ.dʒiː.əˈɡræf.ɪ.kəl ˈriː.dʒən / (BrE)
an area of the Earth defined by the distribution patterns of its plant and animal species

Examples
