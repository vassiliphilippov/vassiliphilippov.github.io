packed cell volume

noun
ratio of red blood cells in blood (hematology)
Pronunciation: / ˈpækt sɛl ˈvɑl.jum / (AmE), / ˈpækt sɛl ˈvɒl.juːm / (BrE)
the ratio of the volume occupied by packed red blood cells to the volume of whole blood, measured by a hematocrit
