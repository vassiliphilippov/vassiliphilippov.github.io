12 o'clock

noun
noon or midnight; time when clock shows twelve
Pronunciation: / twɛlv əˈklɑk / (AmE), / twɛlv əˈklɒk / (BrE)
The time when the hour hand points to twelve on the clock; either noon (midday) or midnight.

Examples

12 o'clock midnight
Fireworks lit up the sky at 12 o'clock on New Year's Eve.
She usually eats lunch at 12 o'clock.
The train departs at 12 o'clock sharp every day.
