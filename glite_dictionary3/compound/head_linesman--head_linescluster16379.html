head linesman

noun
American football official on scrimmage line
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛd ˈlaɪnzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɛd ˈlaɪnzmən / (BrE)
an official in American football responsible for judging plays along the line of scrimmage and recording yardage gained or lost

Examples

the head linesman signals play
During the game, the head linesman kept a close eye on the line of scrimmage.
It is the duty of the head linesman to track the yardage gained or lost on each play.
The head linesman signaled a first down after the play.
