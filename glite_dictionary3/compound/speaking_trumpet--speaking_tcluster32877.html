speaking trumpet

noun
cone-shaped device for amplifying the voice
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːkɪŋ ˌtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈspiːkɪŋ ˌtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
a cone-shaped device used to amplify and direct the human voice, especially before the invention of electronic loudspeakers

Examples
