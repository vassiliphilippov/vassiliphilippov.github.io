baggage attendant

noun
person handling passengers' baggage
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡɪdʒ əˌtɛndənt / (AmE), / ˈbæɡɪdʒ əˈtɛndənt / (BrE)
an employee whose job is to handle or take care of passengers' baggage, especially at airports or train stations.

Examples

baggage attendant at airport
If you need assistance with your luggage, please ask a baggage attendant.
The baggage attendant carefully loaded the suitcases onto the cart.
The airport hired more baggage attendants during the busy holiday season.
