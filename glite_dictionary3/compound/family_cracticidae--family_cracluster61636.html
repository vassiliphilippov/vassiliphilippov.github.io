family Cracticidae

noun
Australian bird family (butcherbirds, currawongs, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli kræktɪˌdaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli kræktɪˌdaɪdiː / (BrE)
