white rust

noun
plant disease with white powdery fungus
Pronunciation: / waɪt rʌst / (AmE), / waɪt rʌst / (BrE)
a plant disease caused by certain fungi, characterized by a white powdery mass that appears on affected plant parts

Examples

white rust on leaves
