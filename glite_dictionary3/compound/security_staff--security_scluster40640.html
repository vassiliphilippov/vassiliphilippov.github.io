security staff

noun
employees responsible for organizational security
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊrəti stæf / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊərəti stɑːf / (BrE)
the group of employees in an organization responsible for protecting property, people, and information from theft, damage, or unauthorized access
