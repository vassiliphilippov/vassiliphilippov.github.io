twelfth cranial nerve

noun
cranial nerve controlling tongue muscles
Pronunciation: / twɛlfθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜrv / (AmE), / twɛlfθ ˈkreɪniəl nɜːv / (BrE)
the cranial nerve that supplies the muscles of the tongue, responsible for movement and control of the tongue

Examples

twelfth cranial nerve controls tongue
Damage to the twelfth cranial nerve can result in difficulty moving the tongue.
Tests showed impairment of the patient's twelfth cranial nerve function after the accident.
The surgeon took care to avoid the twelfth cranial nerve during the operation.
