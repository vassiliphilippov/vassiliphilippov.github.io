dramatic mezzo-soprano

noun
operatic mezzo-soprano voice type
Pronunciation: / drəˈmæt̬ɪk ˌmet.soʊ səˈprɑː.noʊ / (AmE), / drəˈmæt.ɪk ˌmet.səʊ səˈprɑː.nəʊ / (BrE)
