four-four time

noun
musical time signature with four beats per bar
Pronunciation: / fɔr fɔr taɪm / (AmE), / fɔː fɔː taɪm / (BrE)
a musical time signature in which each bar contains four beats, typically with a quarter note receiving one beat.
