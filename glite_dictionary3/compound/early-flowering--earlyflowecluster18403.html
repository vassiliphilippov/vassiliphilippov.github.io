early-flowering

adjective
blooming early in the season
Pronunciation: / ˈɜːrliˌflaʊərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɜːliˌflowərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) having flowers that bloom early in the season, especially in the spring.

Examples

early-flowering plant
Our early-flowering plants survived the last frost and started blooming in early spring.
Several early-flowering bulbs, such as snowdrops, provide vital food for pollinators.
The early-flowering cherry trees bring a splash of color to the garden in March.
