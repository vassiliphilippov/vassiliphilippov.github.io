dessert spoon

noun
spoon for eating desserts
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɜrt spuːn / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːt spuːn / (BrE)
A spoon larger than a teaspoon and smaller than a tablespoon, typically used for eating desserts.

Examples

dessert spoon for pudding
Please use the dessert spoon for your pudding, not the soup spoon.
She placed a dessert spoon next to each bowl of ice cream.
The recipe calls for one dessert spoon of sugar.
