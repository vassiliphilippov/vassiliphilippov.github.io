pack rat

noun
hoarding North American rodent
Pronunciation: / ˈpæk ˌræt / (AmE), / ˈpak ˌrat / (BrE)
a North American rodent of the genus Neotoma, known for its bushy tail and tendency to hoard food and objects

Examples

wild pack rat
Pack rats are attracted to shiny objects, sometimes carrying them back to their nests.
Hiking in the desert, we spotted a pack rat scurrying under a rock.
The pack rat builds elaborate nests from twigs and household items.
