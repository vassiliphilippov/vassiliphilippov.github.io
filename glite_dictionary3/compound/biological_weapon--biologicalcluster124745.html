biological weapon

noun
weapon that uses living organisms to harm
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl ˈwɛpən / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl ˈwɛpən / (BrE)
