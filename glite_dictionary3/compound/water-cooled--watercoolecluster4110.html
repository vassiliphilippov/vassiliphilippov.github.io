water-cooled

adjective
cooled using circulating water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚ kuːld / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə kuːld / (BrE)
(of an engine or machine) kept cool or designed to be kept cool by circulating water.

Examples

water-cooled engine
Many motorcycles feature water-cooled engines for more efficient temperature control.
The laboratory uses a water-cooled system to prevent overheating during experiments.
The new generator is water-cooled, making it quieter and more reliable than its air-cooled predecessor.

Related Terms

  • antonym – air-cooled: cooled by air rather than water
