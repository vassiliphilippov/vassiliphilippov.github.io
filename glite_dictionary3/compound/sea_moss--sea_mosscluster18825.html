sea moss

noun
a type of edible red algae
Pronunciation: / ˈsi ˌmɔs / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌmɒs / (BrE)
any of various red algae, especially those with rose to purple fronds, such as dulse or carrageen, often used as food or in traditional remedies.

Examples

dried sea moss for food
In coastal regions, sea moss is harvested and dried for culinary use.
Many people add sea moss to their smoothies for its nutrient content.
Traditional Irish recipes often include sea moss as a thickening agent.

Related Terms

  • synonym – carrageen: an edible red seaweed used to make carrageenan
  • synonym – dulse: an edible red seaweed with leathery fronds
