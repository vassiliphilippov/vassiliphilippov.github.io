woman of the house

noun
wife managing the household
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmən əv ðə haʊs / (AmE), / ˈwʊmən əv ðə haʊs / (BrE)
the wife who manages the household, especially while her husband earns the family income

Examples

woman of the house manages chores
As the woman of the house, she oversees the daily chores and keeps everything in order.
The woman of the house welcomed us warmly and offered us tea.
Traditionally, the woman of the house would organize family gatherings and celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lady of the house: female head or manager of a household
