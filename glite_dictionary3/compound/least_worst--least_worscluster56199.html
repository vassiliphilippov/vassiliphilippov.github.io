least worst

adjective
best of a set of bad choices
Pronunciation: / list wɜrst / (AmE), / liːst wɜːst / (BrE)
the best or least objectionable choice among a group of undesirable options

Examples

least worst option
Among all the candidates, she was considered the least worst choice by the committee.
The proposal was far from perfect but proved to be the least worst alternative available.
We had to choose the least worst route to avoid the heavy traffic and roadworks.
