to call someone a maggot

verb
insulting term implying worthlessness
Pronunciation: / kɔl ˈsʌmwʌn ə ˈmæɡət / (AmE), / kɔːl ˈsʌmwən ə ˈmæɡət / (BrE)
to insult someone by calling them 'maggot', implying they are worthless or lazy
