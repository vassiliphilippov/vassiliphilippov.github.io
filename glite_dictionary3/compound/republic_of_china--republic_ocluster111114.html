Republic of China

proper noun
government of Taiwan since 1949
Pronunciation: / rəˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈtʃaɪnə / (BrE)
the formal name for the government established on the island of Taiwan in 1949, after the Chinese Nationalists retreated from mainland China following the Communist victory

Examples

the Republic of China on Taiwan
After 1949, the Republic of China government relocated to the island of Taiwan.
International recognition of the Republic of China has declined since the 1970s.
The official name of Taiwan is the Republic of China.
