haute cuisine

noun
elaborate, high-quality cooking style
Pronunciation: / oʊt kwɪˈzin / (AmE), / əʊt kwɪˈziːn / (BrE)
A style of cooking that involves elaborate and skillful preparation of high-quality dishes, especially in the French tradition.

Examples

haute cuisine restaurant
He prefers casual comfort food to the formal dishes of haute cuisine.
Studying haute cuisine requires years of training in classic French cooking techniques.
The restaurant is famous for its haute cuisine and elegant atmosphere.

  • antonym – home cooking: food prepared simply at home, emphasizing comfort and tradition, not elaborate presentation
