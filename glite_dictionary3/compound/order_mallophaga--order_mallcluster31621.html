order Mallophaga

noun
former insect order; biting lice
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər məˈlɒfəɡə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə məˈlɒfəɡə / (BrE)
A former taxonomic order of insects comprising the biting lice, which are ectoparasites mainly of birds and some mammals.

Poultry farmers are concerned about infestations by lice from the order Mallophaga.
