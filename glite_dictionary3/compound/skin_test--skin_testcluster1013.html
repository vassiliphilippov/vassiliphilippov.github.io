skin test

noun
medical test for immunity or sensitivity using the skin
Pronunciation: / skɪn tɛst / (AmE), / skɪn tɛst / (BrE)
a medical test to determine immunity or sensitivity to a disease by introducing a small amount of a substance into or onto the skin

Examples

skin test for allergies
A skin test was performed to determine if she had been exposed to tuberculosis.
He developed a mild rash after the skin test, indicating possible sensitivity to the substance.
The doctor recommended a skin test to check for allergies before starting the medication.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allergy test: a test to identify substances that cause allergic reactions
  • synonym – patch test: a type of skin test used to identify substances that cause contact dermatitis
