egg roll

noun
Chinese-style fried filled pastry
Pronunciation: / ɛg roʊl / (AmE), / ɛg rəʊl / (BrE)
A Chinese-style dish consisting of chopped vegetables and meat wrapped in a thin pastry and deep-fried.

Examples

crispy egg roll
Many Chinese restaurants serve their own version of the egg roll.
She ordered an egg roll and wonton soup for lunch.
The egg roll was crispy and filled with fresh vegetables.
