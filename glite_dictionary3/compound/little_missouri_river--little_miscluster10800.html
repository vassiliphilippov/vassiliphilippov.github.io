Little Missouri River

proper noun
tributary river in the north-central United States
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪt̬l̩ məˈzʊri ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˌlɪtl məˈzʊəri ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
A river in the north-central United States that originates in northeastern Wyoming and flows through Montana and South Dakota before joining the Missouri River in North Dakota.
