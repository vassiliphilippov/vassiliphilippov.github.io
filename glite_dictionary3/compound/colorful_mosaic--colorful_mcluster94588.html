colorful mosaic

noun
diverse and vibrant arrangement or mixture
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlərfəl moʊˈzæɪ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈkʌləfəl məʊˈzæɪ.ɪk / (BrE)
A diverse and vibrant arrangement or mixture, often of people, objects, or elements, resembling the appearance of a multicolored mosaic.

Examples

a colorful mosaic of cultures
Her childhood memories are a colorful mosaic of sights, sounds, and emotions.
