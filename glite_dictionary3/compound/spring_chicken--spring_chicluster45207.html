spring chicken

noun
young, tender chicken
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈtʃɪk.ən / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈtʃɪk.ɪn / (BrE)
A young chicken, especially one that is tender and suitable for roasting.

Examples

roast spring chicken
At the market, the butcher claimed his spring chicken was the juiciest in town.
For Sunday dinner, we roasted a spring chicken with fresh herbs from the garden.
The chef recommended using a spring chicken for the recipe because of its tender meat.
