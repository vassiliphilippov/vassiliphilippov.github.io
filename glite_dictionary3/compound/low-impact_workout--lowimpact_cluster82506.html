low-impact workout

noun
gentle exercise easy on joints
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊ ˈɪmpækt ˈwɜːrkaʊt / (AmE), / ˌləʊ ˈɪmpakt ˈwɜːkaʊt / (BrE)
a form of exercise designed to minimize stress on the joints, involving gentle movements and reduced impact

Examples

low-impact workout for beginners
Many people recovering from injury prefer low-impact workouts to avoid stressing their bodies.
She switched to a low-impact workout routine to protect her knees from further injury.
Swimming and cycling are popular choices for those seeking a low-impact workout.

Related Terms

  • antonym – high-impact workout: a form of exercise that places greater stress on the joints due to more forceful or jarring movements
