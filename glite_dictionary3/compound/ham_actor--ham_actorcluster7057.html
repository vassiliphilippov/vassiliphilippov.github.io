ham actor

noun
an unskilled or exaggerated actor
Pronunciation: / hæm ˈæktər / (AmE), / hæm ˈæktə / (BrE)
An actor who overacts in an exaggerated or unskilled way, often lacking subtlety or realism.

Examples

a ham actor on stage
Some reviewers criticized him as a ham actor who always stole focus from his co-stars.
