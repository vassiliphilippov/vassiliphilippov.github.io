common gum cistus

noun
Mediterranean shrub with fragrant resin
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ɡʌm ˈsɪstəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ɡʌm ˈsɪstəs / (BrE)
a Mediterranean shrub (Cistus ladanifer) with white flowers and sticky stems and leaves that produce a fragrant resin used in perfumery, especially as a fixative
