clock pendulum

noun
swinging regulator in clocks
Pronunciation: / klɑk ˈpɛn(d)jələm / (AmE), / klɒk ˈpɛndjʊləm / (BrE)
a swinging rod or weight used in clocks to regulate the timing mechanism by its regular motion

Examples

clock pendulum swings
Dust on the clock pendulum can affect the clock's precision.
He carefully adjusted the clock pendulum to speed up the old wall clock.
The clock pendulum must swing freely for the grandfather clock to keep accurate time.
