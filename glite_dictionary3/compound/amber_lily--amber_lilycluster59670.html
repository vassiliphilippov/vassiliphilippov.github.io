amber lily

noun
plant with yellowish-orange flowers, southwestern US
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbɚ ˈlɪli / (AmE), / ˈæmbə ˈlɪli / (BrE)
a plant native to the southwestern United States with basal grasslike leaves and a narrow, open cluster of starlike yellowish-orange flowers on a leafless stalk; the only species of Anthericum found in North America
