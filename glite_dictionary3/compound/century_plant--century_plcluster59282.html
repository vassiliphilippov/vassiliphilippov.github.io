century plant

noun
tropical American plant with tall flower spikes
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tʃə.ri plænt / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tʃəri plɑːnt / (BrE)
A tropical American plant, especially Agave americana, with a rosette of fibrous, sword-shaped leaves and tall spikes of flowers, cultivated for ornament or fiber.
