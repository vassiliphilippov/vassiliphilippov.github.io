Emergency Alert System

noun
U.S. federal emergency broadcast system
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrdʒənsi əˈlɝt ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi əˈlɜːt ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a federal system in the United States that allows authorities to broadcast emergency warnings to the public via radio and television.
