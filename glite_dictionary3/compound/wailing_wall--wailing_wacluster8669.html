Wailing Wall

noun
sacred Jewish prayer site in Jerusalem
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪlɪŋ wɑl / (AmE), / ˈweɪlɪŋ wɔːl / (BrE)
a wall in Jerusalem considered sacred by Jews, believed to be the last remnant of the Second Temple, and used as a site for prayer and lamentation
