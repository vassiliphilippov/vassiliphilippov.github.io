frontal gyrus

noun
ridge on the frontal lobe of the brain
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.t̬l ˈdʒaɪ.rəs / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.tl ˈdʒaɪ.rəs / (BrE)
any of the ridges (convolutions) on the outer surface of the frontal lobe of the brain's cerebrum

Examples

left frontal gyrus
An MRI scan revealed an abnormality in the left frontal gyrus.
Damage to the superior frontal gyrus can affect decision-making and social behavior.
The neurosurgeon carefully mapped the frontal gyrus before proceeding with the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gyrus: a ridge or fold between two clefts on the cerebral surface of the brain
  • antonym – sulcus: a groove or furrow, especially on the surface of the brain
