athwart the road

preposition
across or obstructing the road
Pronunciation: / əˈθwɔrt ðə roʊd / (AmE), / əˈθwɔːt ðə rəʊd / (BrE)
Positioned or lying across the road, often obstructing passage.

Examples

athwart the road blocking traffic
A carriage was parked athwart the road, creating a sudden barrier for oncoming traffic.
A fallen branch lay athwart the road, making it impossible for cars to pass.
After the storm, several signs stood athwart the road, warning drivers of hazards ahead.

Related Terms

  • synonym – across the road: extending from one side of the road to the other
