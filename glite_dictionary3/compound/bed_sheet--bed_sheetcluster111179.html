bed sheet

noun
large rectangular bedding cloth
Pronunciation: / bɛd ʃit / (AmE), / bɛd ʃiːt / (BrE)
A large rectangular piece of cloth used to cover a mattress and as bedding, typically made of cotton or linen.

Examples

bed sheet on mattress
After spilling juice, he had to wash the stained bed sheet immediately.
She changed the bed sheet and put on a clean one before guests arrived.
The hotel provides fresh bed sheets every day for all its rooms.
