gut issue

noun
emotionally charged issue
Pronunciation: / ɡʌt ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ɡʌt ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
an issue or topic that provokes strong instinctive or emotional reactions, rather than rational or purely intellectual responses

Examples

political gut issue
Abortion has always been a gut issue for many voters on both sides of the debate.
Climate change became a gut issue after several devastating hurricanes hit the coast.
The candidate tried to turn economic policy into a gut issue that would mobilize passionate supporters.

Related Terms

  • synonym – contentious issue: an issue causing a lot of disagreement or heated debate
  • synonym – hot-button issue: an issue that evokes strong emotions or controversy
