to duck down

verb
lower oneself quickly (crouch)
Pronunciation: / dʌk daʊn / (AmE), / dʌk daʊn / (BrE)
to lower your body quickly, usually by bending your knees or crouching, often to avoid being seen or hit

Examples

to duck down quickly
As the car sped by, we quickly ducked down to avoid being seen.
She had to duck down to fit through the doorway.
The children ducked down behind the wall as the ball flew overhead.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crouch: to squat or bend down close to the ground
  • antonym – stand up: to rise to an upright position on the feet
