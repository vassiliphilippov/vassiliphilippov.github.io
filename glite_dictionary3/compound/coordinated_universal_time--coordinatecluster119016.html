coordinated universal time

noun
global standard time system
Pronunciation: / koʊˌɔrdəˌneɪtɪd juːˈnɪvərsəl taɪm / (AmE), / kəʊˌɔːdɪneɪtɪd juːˈnɪvəsəl taɪm / (BrE)
The standard time system based on Greenwich Mean Time, but updated with leap seconds to stay in sync with Earth's rotation; used as the basis for civil time worldwide.
