plant virus

noun
virus that infects plants
Pronunciation: / plænt ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / plɑːnt ˈvaɪrəs / (BrE)
A virus that infects plants, typically consisting of a single strand of RNA and causing various plant diseases.
