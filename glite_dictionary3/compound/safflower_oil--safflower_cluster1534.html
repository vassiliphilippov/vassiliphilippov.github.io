safflower oil

noun
oil from safflower seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈsæfˌlaʊɚ ɔɪl /; / ˈsæfˌlaʊ.ɚ ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈsæfˌlaʊə ɔɪl /; / ˈsæf.laʊ.ə ɔɪl / (BrE)
