plug fuse

noun
fuse that screws into a socket
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ fjuz / (AmE), / plʌɡ fjuːz / (BrE)
a type of electrical fuse with a threaded body that screws into a socket, used for circuit protection

Examples

replace a plug fuse
Before working on the wiring, she made sure to remove the plug fuse from its socket.
Many older homes still have plug fuses in their fuse boxes.
The electrician recommended replacing the old plug fuse with a modern circuit breaker.
