off-trail

adverb
away from established paths or routes
Pronunciation: / ˌɔfˈtreɪl / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈtreɪl / (BrE)
Not following an established or designated path or route, especially in outdoor activities such as hiking.

Examples

hiking off-trail
He spotted rare wildflowers while wandering off-trail in the national forest.
Hiking off-trail requires careful navigation and respect for the environment.
Some parks prohibit off-trail exploration to protect fragile ecosystems.
