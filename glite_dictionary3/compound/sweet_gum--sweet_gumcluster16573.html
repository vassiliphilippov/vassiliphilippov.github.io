sweet gum

noun
aromatic resin from sweet gum tree
Pronunciation: / swit ɡʌm / (AmE), / swiːt ɡʌm / (BrE)
an aromatic resin obtained from the sweet gum tree, used in medicine, perfumes, and as a chewing gum base

Examples

sweet gum resin
