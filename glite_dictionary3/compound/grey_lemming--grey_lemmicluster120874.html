grey lemming

noun
a species of small grey-furred lemming
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (BrE)
a species of small rodent found in parts of northern Europe and Asia, known for its grey fur; a type of Old World lemming

Examples
