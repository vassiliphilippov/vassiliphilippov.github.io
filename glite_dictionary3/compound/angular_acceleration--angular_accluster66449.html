angular acceleration

noun
rate of change of angular velocity
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡjələr ˌæk.sə.ləˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡjʊlə ˌæksələˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
The rate at which the angular velocity of a rotating object changes with time.

Examples

high angular acceleration
A figure skater increases her spin by generating high angular acceleration with her legs.
The car's tires started to slip when their angular acceleration became too great.
To calculate angular acceleration, you must measure how quickly the rotation speed changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – angular velocity: the rate of change of the angle of a rotating body
