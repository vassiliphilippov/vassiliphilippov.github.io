paternity leave

noun
leave for fathers after child's birth or adoption
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɝːnəti liːv /; / pəˈtɝː.nə.t̬i liːv / (AmE), / pəˈtɜːnəti liːv /; / pəˈtɜː.nə.ti liːv / (BrE)
a period of time off work that a father is allowed after the birth or adoption of his child, sometimes with pay
