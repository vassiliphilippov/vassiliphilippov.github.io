crepe de Chine

noun
thin, lightweight silk fabric
Pronunciation: / kreɪp də ʃin / (AmE), / krep də ʃiːn / (BrE)
A very thin, lightweight silk or silklike fabric with a slightly crinkled texture, often used in dresses and blouses.
