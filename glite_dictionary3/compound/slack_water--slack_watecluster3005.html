slack water

noun
period of still water at turn of the tide
Pronunciation: / ˈslæk ˌwɔtɚ /; / slæk ˈwɔt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈslæk ˌwɔːtə /; / slæk ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
a period or area in a tidal river or sea when the water is nearly still, usually at the turn of the tide
