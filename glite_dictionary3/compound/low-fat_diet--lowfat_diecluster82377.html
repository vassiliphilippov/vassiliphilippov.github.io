low-fat diet

noun
diet with reduced fat content
Pronunciation: / loʊ ˈfæt ˈdaɪ.ət / (AmE), / ləʊ ˈfæt ˈdaɪ.ət / (BrE)
a diet that restricts the intake of fat, emphasizing foods high in carbohydrates and protein instead

Examples

healthy low-fat diet
After his heart surgery, he switched to a low-fat diet to improve his health.
Many nutritionists recommend a low-fat diet for managing cholesterol levels.
Restaurants are increasingly offering low-fat diet options on their menus.

Related Terms

  • antonym – high-fat diet: a diet containing large amounts of fat
  • synonym – low-calorie diet: a diet with reduced calorie content, often for weight loss
