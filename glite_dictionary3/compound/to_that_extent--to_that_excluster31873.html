to that extent

adverb
to the specified degree or level
Pronunciation: / tə ðæt ɪkˈstɛnt / (AmE), / tə ðat ɪkˈstɛnt / (BrE)
to the degree or level mentioned; as much as is stated or implied.

Examples

trust to that extent

If you want to succeed to that extent, you must be prepared to work very hard.
She was angry, but I didn't think she would go to that extent to prove her point.
