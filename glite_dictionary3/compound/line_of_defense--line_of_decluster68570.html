line of defense

noun
organization or system for protection
Pronunciation: / laɪn əv dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / laɪn əv dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
an organization, system, or group that acts to protect against attack, danger, or threat

Examples

financial line of defense
For many people, savings accounts are their only line of defense in case of unexpected expenses.
The local fire department is our first line of defense against house fires.
Vaccination programs serve as an important line of defense against infectious diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym – defender: a person who defends something or someone
  • antonym – line of attack: the primary force or direction in attacking
