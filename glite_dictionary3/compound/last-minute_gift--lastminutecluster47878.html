last-minute gift

noun
a present bought at the last moment
Pronunciation: / læst ˈmɪnət ɡɪft / (AmE), / lɑːst ˈmɪnɪt ɡɪft / (BrE)
A gift bought or arranged just before it is needed, typically due to lack of preparation.

Examples

buy a last-minute gift
He rushed to the store to pick up a last-minute gift for his friend's birthday.
She was surprised and delighted by the thoughtful last-minute gift her colleague gave her.
Shopping malls are always crowded with people searching for last-minute gifts during the holiday season.
