automatic drive

noun
vehicle autonomous/self-driving mode
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˈmætɪk draɪv / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk draɪv / (BrE)
A vehicle mode or capability in which the car operates with minimal or no human control, using sensors and software to steer, accelerate, and brake.

Examples

automatic drive mode
During the test, the vehicle engaged automatic drive and maintained speed without driver input.
Consumers want cars with reliable automatic drive that can handle urban streets and parking.
Regulators require detailed safety data whenever an automatic drive system is tested on public roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym – autonomous driving: the technology and practice of vehicles driving themselves with little or no human intervention
  • synonym – self-driving: describing a vehicle that can operate without continuous human control
