terry cloth

noun
absorbent cotton fabric with loops
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛri klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈtɛri klɒθ / (BrE)
a thick cotton fabric with many small loops on both sides, making it very absorbent; commonly used for bath towels and bathrobes

Examples

terry cloth towel
After her shower, she wrapped herself in a soft terry cloth robe.
He bought new terry cloth hand towels for the guest room.
The hotel provides fresh terry cloth towels in every bathroom.
