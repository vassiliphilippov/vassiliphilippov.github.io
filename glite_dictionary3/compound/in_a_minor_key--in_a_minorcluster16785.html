having minor-key tonality (music)
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə ˈmaɪnɚ ki / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈmaɪnə kiː / (BrE)
(of music) having the tonality or character of a minor key, often producing a more subdued or somber mood.
