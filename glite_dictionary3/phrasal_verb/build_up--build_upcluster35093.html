to build up

verb
prepare for military confrontation
Pronunciation: / bɪld ʌp / (AmE), / bɪld ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare oneself or one's forces for a military confrontation, especially by increasing strength or resources

Examples

to build up military strength
Nations often build up their defenses in anticipation of a possible attack.
Tensions rose as both sides built up troops along the border.
The navy continued to build up its presence in the region throughout the summer.

  • synonym – mobilize: to prepare troops or resources for active service, especially in war
