to face death

verb
confront the prospect of dying
Pronunciation: / feɪs dɛθ / (AmE), / feɪs dɛθ / (BrE)
to confront the prospect or reality of dying, often with courage or composure

Examples

to face death bravely
After the accident, he had to face death for the first time in his life.
Despite her illness, she chose to face death without fear or regret.
Many soldiers face death on the battlefield every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym – meet one's end: to die, often used euphemistically
  • antonym – survive: to continue living after a dangerous situation
