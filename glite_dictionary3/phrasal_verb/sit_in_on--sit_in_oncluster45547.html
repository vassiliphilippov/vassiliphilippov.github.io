to sit in on

verb
attend as an observer
Pronunciation: / sɪt ɪn ɑn / (AmE), / sɪt ɪn ɒn / (BrE)
to attend a meeting, class, or event without taking an active role, often for observation or information

Examples

to sit in on a meeting
May I sit in on your class to learn more about the subject?
Our supervisor plans to sit in on the session but will not participate.
Several students sat in on the seminar out of curiosity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attend: to be present at an event, meeting, or function
