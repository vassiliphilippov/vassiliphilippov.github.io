to flump down

verb
sit or fall heavily and suddenly
Pronunciation: / flʌmp daʊn / (AmE), / flʌmp daʊn / (BrE)
to sit or fall heavily and suddenly, usually in a relaxed or exhausted manner

Examples

to flump down on a sofa
After a long day of hiking, she flumped down onto the soft grass.
He flumped down in the armchair, exhausted from work.
The dog ran inside and flumped down by the fireplace.
