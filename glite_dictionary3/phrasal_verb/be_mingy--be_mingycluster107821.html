to be mingy

verb
to act stingy or ungenerous
Pronunciation: / bi ˈmɪndʒi / (AmE), / bi ˈmɪndʒi / (BrE)
to be unwilling to give, spend, or share; to act in a stingy or ungenerous way

Examples

to be mingy with money
Don't be mingy; share your dessert with your friends.
If you are mingy with compliments, people may think you're unfriendly.
She didn't want to be mingy when it came to tipping the server.
