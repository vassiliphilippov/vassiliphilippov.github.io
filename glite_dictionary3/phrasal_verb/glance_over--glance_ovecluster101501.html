to glance over

verb
look at quickly or superficially
Pronunciation: / ɡlæns ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / ɡlɑːns ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to examine or look at something quickly or superficially without paying close attention

Examples

to glance over a report
Could you glance over my essay to see if there are any obvious mistakes?
He glanced over the contract and signed it without reading the details.
She glanced over the report before the meeting but didn't catch any errors.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inspect: examine something closely or carefully
  • synonym – scan: look at something quickly to get an overview
