to bed in the brakes

verb
condition new brakes for optimal use
Pronunciation: / bɛd ɪn ðə breɪks / (AmE), / bɛd ɪn ðə breɪks / (BrE)
to condition or run-in new brake components so they function optimally and safely

Examples

to bed in the brakes gently
