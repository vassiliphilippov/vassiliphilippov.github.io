to hike up

verb
pull up (clothing, etc.)
Pronunciation: / haɪk ʌp / (AmE), / haɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to pull something, especially a piece of clothing, up to a higher position

Examples

hike up your pants
During the hike, he stopped to hike up his backpack straps.
He quickly hiked up his jeans before stepping into the cold water.
She hiked up her skirt so she could climb over the fence.
