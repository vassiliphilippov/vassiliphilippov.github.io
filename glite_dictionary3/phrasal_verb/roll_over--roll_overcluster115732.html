to roll over

verb
turn by rolling
Pronunciation: / roʊl ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / rəʊl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to turn one's body or an object over by rolling

Examples

to roll over in bed
He tried to roll over in bed, but his arm was stuck under the blanket.
The car hit a patch of ice and began to roll over on the highway.
When commanded, the puppy would happily roll over for a treat.
