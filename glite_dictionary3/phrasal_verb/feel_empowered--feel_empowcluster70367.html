to feel empowered

verb
experience confidence and control
Pronunciation: / fil ɛmˈpaʊɚd / (AmE), / fiːl ɪmˈpaʊəd / (BrE)
to experience a strong sense of confidence, control, or strength, often as a result of support, encouragement, or increased rights

Examples

to feel empowered by support
After telling her story, he said he felt empowered to make changes in his life.
Many students feel empowered after participating in leadership workshops.
Volunteers often feel empowered when they see the impact of their work.
