to stay away

verb
to keep a distance from; to avoid
Pronunciation: / steɪ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / steɪ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
To keep a distance from someone or something; to avoid being near or involved with someone or something.

Examples

to stay away from trouble
She tried to stay away from negative people to protect her peace of mind.
The doctor advised him to stay away from junk food for better health.
You should stay away from the construction site because it is dangerous.

Related Terms

  • antonym – approach: to come near or nearer to something or someone
  • synonym – avoid: to keep away from or not do something
