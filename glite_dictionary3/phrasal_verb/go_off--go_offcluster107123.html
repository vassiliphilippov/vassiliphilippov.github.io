to go off

verb
(of a gun) to fire or discharge
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ɔf / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɒf / (BrE)
to be triggered, activated, or to start making a loud noise suddenly (such as an alarm, gun, or bomb)
