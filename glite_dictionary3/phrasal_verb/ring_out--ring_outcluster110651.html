to ring out

verb
sound loudly and clearly
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ aʊt / (AmE), / rɪŋ aʊt / (BrE)
to sound loudly and clearly, often for a sustained or sudden noise

Examples

ring out a loud sound
A single gunshot rang out in the silent night, startling everyone.
Laughter rang out from the playground as the children played.
The church bells rang out over the city, announcing the wedding.

Related Terms

  • synonym – echo: to be repeated or reverberated after the original sound
