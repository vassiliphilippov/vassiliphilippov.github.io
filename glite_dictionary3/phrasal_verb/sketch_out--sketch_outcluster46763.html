to sketch out

verb
give a brief outline or summary
Pronunciation: / skɛtʃ aʊt / (AmE), / skɛtʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to give a brief or general outline or summary of something without going into detail

Examples

to sketch out a plan
During the meeting, he sketched out the main points of the proposal.
She sketched out a plan for the project on a notepad.
The architect quickly sketched out her idea for the new building.
