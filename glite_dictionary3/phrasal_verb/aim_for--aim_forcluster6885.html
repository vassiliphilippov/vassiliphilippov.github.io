to aim for

verb
strive to achieve a goal
Pronunciation: / eɪm fɔr / (AmE), / eɪm fə / (BrE)
to strive to achieve something or to direct one's efforts towards a particular goal

Examples

to aim for success
She always aims for perfection in everything she does.
The company is aiming for a dramatic increase in sales this year.
We aimed for the highest score in the tournament.

Related Terms

  • antonym – avoid: to keep away from or not try to achieve
  • synonym – pursue: to follow or try to achieve something
