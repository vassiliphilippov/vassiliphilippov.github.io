to put hands up

verb
raise hands in surrender
Pronunciation: / pʊt hændz ʌp / (AmE), / pʊt hændz ʌp / (BrE)
to raise one's hands, typically as a sign of surrender or to show that one is not a threat

Examples

put hands up in surrender
During the demonstration, several people put their hands up in a gesture of peace.
If you ever get into trouble, just put your hands up and cooperate.
The suspect was ordered to put his hands up immediately.

Related Terms
