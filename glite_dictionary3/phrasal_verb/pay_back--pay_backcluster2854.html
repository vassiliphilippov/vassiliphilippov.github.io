to pay back

verb
to reciprocate or retaliate
Pronunciation: / peɪ bæk / (AmE), / peɪ bæk / (BrE)
to act in return for someone's behavior, often by giving similar treatment or recognition; to reciprocate or retaliate

Examples

to pay back a favor
He swore he would pay back anyone who had wronged him.
It took her years to finally pay back all the favors she had received.
She decided to pay back his kindness by helping him when he needed it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – revenge: to inflict harm in return for harm received
