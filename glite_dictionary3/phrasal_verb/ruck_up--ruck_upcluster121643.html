to ruck up

verb
gather belongings or get ready for action
Pronunciation: / rʌk ʌp / (AmE), / rʌk ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare for action or to gather one's belongings, especially quickly or in order to move on

Examples

to ruck up your things
It's almost time to ruck up and head out for the expedition.
She told everyone to ruck up their stuff before the campfire ended.
The soldiers quickly rucked up and moved to the next checkpoint.
