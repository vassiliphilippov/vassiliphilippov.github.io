to call out

verb
announce loudly (names, numbers, etc.)
Pronunciation: / kɔl aʊt / (AmE), / kɔːl aʊt / (BrE)
to say or announce something loudly so that people can hear, attract attention, or express emotion

Examples

call out a name

She called out each item on the list as she checked them off.
The teacher began to call out the students’ names for attendance.
He called out in surprise when he saw the results.
She called out his name, hoping he would hear her over the noise.
The teacher called out the answer so everyone could hear.
