to form bubbles

verb
produce bubbles and become foamy
Pronunciation: / fɔrm ˈbʌbəlz / (AmE), / fɔːm ˈbʌbəlz / (BrE)
to produce small round pockets of air or gas within a liquid or on its surface, making it bubbly or foamy

Examples

to form bubbles in water
The detergent made the water form bubbles when mixed vigorously.
The surface of the pond began to form bubbles as gas escaped from below.
When you shake the bottle, it will form bubbles on the top.

Related Terms

  • synonym – foam: to produce a mass of small bubbles in a liquid
  • synonym – froth: to form or produce a mass of small bubbles on the surface of a liquid
