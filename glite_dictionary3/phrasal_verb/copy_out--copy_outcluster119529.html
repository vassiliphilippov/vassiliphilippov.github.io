to copy out

verb
write or type something exactly as given
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑpi aʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɒpi aʊt / (BrE)
to write or type something exactly as it appears in another place, with great care to ensure accuracy

Examples

to copy out a passage
He copies out important documents to ensure he has a perfect record.
She carefully copied out the recipe from her grandmother’s notebook.
