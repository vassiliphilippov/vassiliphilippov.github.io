to brush down

verb
clean by brushing off dust or dirt
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ daʊn / (AmE), / brʌʃ daʊn / (BrE)
to clean a surface or object by brushing to remove dust, dirt, or debris

Examples

to brush down a horse
After riding, he took a few minutes to brush down his horse thoroughly.
Before visitors arrive, please brush down the hallway carpet to remove any debris.
She used a soft cloth to brush down the dusty shelves before placing the books on them.
