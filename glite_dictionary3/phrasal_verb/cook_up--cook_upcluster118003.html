to cook up

verb
prepare or make by mixing ingredients
Pronunciation: / kʊk ʌp / (AmE), / kʊk ʌp / (BrE)
to prepare or make (a dish or meal) by mixing ingredients, often quickly or inventively

Examples

to cook up a meal
He quickly cooked up some soup to warm everyone after the hike.
Let's cook up something special for dinner tonight.
She managed to cook up a delicious meal from just a few leftovers.
