to give out

verb
stop working or functioning (machine, body part)
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv aʊt / (AmE), / ɡɪv aʊt / (BrE)
(of a machine or body part) to stop working or functioning, usually after prolonged use or strain.

Examples

give out after hours
After running for hours, my old laptop finally gave out and wouldn't turn back on.
She collapsed when her legs gave out halfway through the marathon.
The town's water supply gave out during the drought, causing major problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – break down: (especially of a machine) to stop functioning
  • synonym – fail: (of a machine or part) to stop working properly
  • antonym – work: (of a machine or part) to perform its intended function
