to fuck up

verb
to mess up or ruin (vulgar)
Pronunciation: / fʌk ʌp / (AmE), / fʌk ʌp / (BrE)
to make a serious mistake or ruin something; to spoil or damage badly, often through carelessness or incompetence (vulgar/informal)

Examples

to fuck up a project
He totally fucked up the interview by forgetting the company's name.
If you fuck up this report again, the boss will be furious.
Try not to fuck up this time; it's your last chance.
If you fuck up this job, you'll have to explain it to the boss.
She was so nervous she thought she might fuck up her speech.
They really fucked up the project by missing the deadline.
