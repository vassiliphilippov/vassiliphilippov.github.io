to dope up

verb
administer drugs or narcotics to someone
Pronunciation: / doʊp ʌp / (AmE), / dəʊp ʌp / (BrE)
to give a narcotic or drug to someone, often to affect their performance or consciousness

Examples

to dope up an athlete
The coach decided to dope up the athletes to enhance their performance before the big race.
The prisoner claimed that he had been doped up without his consent.
They discovered the kidnappers had doped up the guard to make their escape easier.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drug: to administer a drug to someone, typically secretly
  • antonym – sober up: to become less intoxicated or drugged
