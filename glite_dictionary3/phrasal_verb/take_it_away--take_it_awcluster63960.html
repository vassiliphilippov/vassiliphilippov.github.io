to take it away

verb
remove or clear something unwanted
Pronunciation: / teɪk ɪt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / teɪk ɪt əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to remove or clear something, especially something regarded as unwanted or unsatisfactory.

Examples
