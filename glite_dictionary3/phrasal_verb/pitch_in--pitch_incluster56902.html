to pitch in

verb
contribute effort or resources
Pronunciation: / pɪtʃ ɪn / (AmE), / pɪtʃ ɪn / (BrE)
to join others in contributing work, effort, or resources towards a common goal or task

Examples

to pitch in with help
If everyone pitches in, the work will be done in no time.
The neighbors pitched in to help rebuild the storm-damaged fence.
They all decided to pitch in and buy a leaving gift for their colleague.
