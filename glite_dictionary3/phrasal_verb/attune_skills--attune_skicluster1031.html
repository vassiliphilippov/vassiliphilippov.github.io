to attune (skills)

verb
adapt or refine skills
Pronunciation: / əˈtun / (AmE), / əˈtjuːn / (BrE)
to adjust or adapt one's abilities to be suitable for a particular situation or task

Examples

to attune your skills
Coaches help athletes attune their skills for optimal performance.
She spent weeks to attune her skills to the new software.
You need to attune your skills if you want to succeed in this changing industry.
