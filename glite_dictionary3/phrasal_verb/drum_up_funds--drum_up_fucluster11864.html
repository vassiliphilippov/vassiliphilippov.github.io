to drum up funds

verb
to gather or raise money
Pronunciation: / drʌm ʌp fʌndz / (AmE), / drʌm ʌp fʌndz / (BrE)
to try to gather or raise money, especially by making an active effort

Examples

to drum up funds for charity
Community leaders are working hard to drum up funds for the renovation of the old library.
She spent weeks trying to drum up funds to cover her medical expenses.
The charity hopes to drum up funds for its new housing initiative through a series of events.
