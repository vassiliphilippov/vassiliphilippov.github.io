to slush around

verb
spill or splash liquid clumsily
Pronunciation: / slʌʃ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / slʌʃ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to spill or splash liquid copiously or clumsily, often making a mess

Examples

to slush around water
Be careful not to slush around that bucket of water on the floor.
He slushed around the paint, leaving drops everywhere in the room.
Please don't slush around hot coffee near the new carpet.
