to swing through

verb
make a brief visit or stop
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ θru / (AmE), / swɪŋ θruː / (BrE)
to visit a place briefly or make a short stop while on the way to somewhere else

Examples

to swing through the cafe
I’ll swing through the café after work for coffee.
Let’s swing through the park before heading to the concert.
We can swing through the store on our way home.
