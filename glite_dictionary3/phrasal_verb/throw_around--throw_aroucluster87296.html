to throw around

verb
use words or names carelessly
Pronunciation: / θroʊ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / θrəʊ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to mention or use (words, names, accusations, etc.) carelessly or frequently, especially without enough thought

Examples

throw around accusations
He tends to throw around accusations without any evidence.
The term 'genius' gets thrown around too easily these days.
You shouldn't throw around serious words like 'liar' unless you're sure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bandy about: to discuss or mention repeatedly or casually
