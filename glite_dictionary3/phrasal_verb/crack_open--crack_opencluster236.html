to crack open

verb
open forcefully, often a container
Pronunciation: / kræk ˈoʊ.pən / (AmE), / kræk ˈəʊ.pən / (BrE)
to open a container or object, often with some force or by splitting it, especially to access its contents

Examples

to crack open a bottle
After a long day, they sat down and cracked open a couple of beers.
He cracked open the old chest to see what was inside.
She grabbed a bottle and cracked open a cold soda on the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym – open: to make something not closed; to allow access
