to stink up

verb
cause bad smell in something
Pronunciation: / stɪŋk ʌp / (AmE), / stɪŋk ʌp / (BrE)
to cause (a place or thing) to smell very unpleasant

Examples

stink up the room
Don't stink up the kitchen with burnt popcorn again!
If you leave those shoes here, they'll stink up the whole room.
The garbage stunk up the entire apartment before it was taken out.
