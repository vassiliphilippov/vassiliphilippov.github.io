to ride away

verb
depart by riding (horse, bike, etc.)
Pronunciation: / raɪd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / raɪd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to leave a place by riding on a horse, bicycle, or motorcycle

Examples

to ride away on a horse
After saying goodbye, she rode away on her horse, disappearing into the distance.
He quickly rode away before anyone could stop him.
The thief rode away on a stolen bicycle as soon as the alarm sounded.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ride off: to leave by riding, especially to a distance
