to know about

verb
have information or awareness of something
Pronunciation: / noʊ əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / nəʊ əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to have information or knowledge concerning a particular subject, situation, or person

Examples

know about the news
Do you know about the changes to the schedule next week?
I don't know about that; it seems risky.
She doesn't know about the surprise party we're planning for her.
