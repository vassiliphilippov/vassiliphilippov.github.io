to fill up

verb
eat until full; eat to satiety
Pronunciation: / fɪl ʌp / (AmE), / fɪl ʌp / (BrE)
to eat until one is completely full or satisfied

Examples

to fill up on food
He filled up on pasta and couldn't eat another bite.
If you fill up on snacks, you won't be hungry for dinner.
Make sure you fill up at breakfast before the long hike.

Related Terms

  • antonym – go hungry: to not have enough to eat; to be hungry
  • synonym – stuff oneself: to eat so much that one is overfull
