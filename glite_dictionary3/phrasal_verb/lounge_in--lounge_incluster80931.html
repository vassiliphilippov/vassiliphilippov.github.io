to lounge in

verb
relax or spend time in a place
Pronunciation: / laʊndʒ ɪn / (AmE), / laʊndʒ ɪn / (BrE)
to relax comfortably or spend time in a place, often in a lazy or leisurely way

Examples

to lounge in a chair
After a long day, she likes to lounge in her favorite chair with a good book.
He was lounging in the lobby, waiting for his friends to arrive.
The cats always lounge in the sunniest spots of the house.
