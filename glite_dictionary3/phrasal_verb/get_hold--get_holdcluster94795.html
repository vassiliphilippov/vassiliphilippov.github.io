to get hold

verb
obtain or acquire something/someone
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt hoʊld / (AmE), / ɡɛt həʊld / (BrE)
to obtain something or someone, especially for a particular purpose; to manage to acquire or contact.

Examples

to get hold of someone
I finally managed to get hold of the manager after several phone calls.
It took him hours to get hold of a plumber on the weekend.
She tried to get hold of a copy of the book before her exam.
