to say goodbye

verb
to bid farewell or part ways
Pronunciation: / seɪ ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (AmE), / seɪ ɡʊdˈbaɪ / (BrE)
to bid farewell to someone; to express good wishes at parting.

Examples

to say goodbye to friends
Before leaving the party, she made sure to say goodbye to everyone.
He waved and said goodbye as the train pulled away from the station.
It's always difficult to say goodbye at the end of a wonderful holiday.
