to scroll through

verb
browse digital content by moving through it on a screen
Pronunciation: / skroʊl ˈθru / (AmE), / skrəʊl ˈθruː / (BrE)
to move repeatedly up, down, or across digital content on a screen, especially to browse or view information sequentially

Examples

to scroll through photos
He was scrolling through the document to find the information he needed.
I often scroll through my emails first thing in the morning to catch up on messages.
She likes to scroll through photos on her phone when she's bored.

Related Terms

  • synonym – browse: to look through information, especially on a digital platform, in a casual way
  • synonym – click through: to follow links from one web page or resource to another
