to zip over

verb
go quickly to a place
Pronunciation: / zɪp ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / zɪp ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to go quickly or travel swiftly to a place, often for a short visit

Examples

to zip over to a friend's house
Could you zip over to Mark's house and return his book?
I'll zip over to the office after lunch to drop off the documents.
She zipped over to the café on her scooter to meet her friends.
