to break into

verb
suddenly start expressing or doing something
Pronunciation: / breɪk ˈɪntu / (AmE), / breɪk ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to suddenly begin to express or perform something, such as talking, singing, or crying

Examples

to break into song
During the ceremony, the choir broke into song, filling the hall with music.
She suddenly broke into laughter during the meeting, startling everyone in the room.
The little boy broke into tears when he saw his lost puppy.
