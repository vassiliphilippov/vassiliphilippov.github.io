to cry out for help

verb
shout urgently for assistance
Pronunciation: / kraɪ aʊt fər hɛlp / (AmE), / kraɪ aʊt fə hɛlp / (BrE)
to shout loudly and urgently in order to seek assistance, especially in danger or distress

Examples

cry out for help loudly
No one heard him cry out for help during the thunderstorm.
She cried out for help when she saw the smoke coming from the kitchen.
When the boat started sinking, the passengers began to cry out for help.
