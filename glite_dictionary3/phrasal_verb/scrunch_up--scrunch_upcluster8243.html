to scrunch up

verb
to crouch down by bending the knees
Pronunciation: / skrʌntʃ ʌp / (AmE), / skrʌntʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to lower oneself into a crouched position by bending the knees, often bringing the body close to the ground

Examples

to scrunch up on the floor
He scrunched up on the floor to examine the piece of paper more closely.
The children scrunched up behind the bench to hide from their teacher.
When the rain started, they quickly scrunched up under the small umbrella.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crouch: to lower the body close to the ground by bending the knees
  • antonym – stand up: to be in an upright position on the feet
