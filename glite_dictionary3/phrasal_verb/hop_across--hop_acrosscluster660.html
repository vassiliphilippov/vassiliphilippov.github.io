to hop across

verb
move by jumping across something
Pronunciation: / hɑp əˈkrɔs / (AmE), / hɒp əˈkrɒs / (BrE)
to move from one side to another by jumping lightly on one foot or with short jumps

Examples

to hop across stepping stones
Children love to hop across the stepping stones in the park.
She watched the bird hop across the lawn, searching for worms.
The squirrel managed to hop across the narrow stream without getting wet.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jump across: to leap from one side to another
