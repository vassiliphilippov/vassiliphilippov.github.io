to set forth

verb
state or present formally
Pronunciation: / sɛt ˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / sɛt ˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
to state or present something clearly and formally, especially in writing or speech

Examples

to set forth an idea
She set forth her ideas during the meeting with great clarity.
The rules were set forth at the beginning of the course.
The terms of the contract are clearly set forth in this document.
