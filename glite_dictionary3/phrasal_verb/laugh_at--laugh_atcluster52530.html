to laugh at

verb
mock or ridicule someone or something
Pronunciation: / læf æt / (AmE), / lɑːf æt / (BrE)
to make fun of or ridicule someone or something in a mocking or scornful way

Examples

to laugh at mistakes

She tried not to laugh at his funny accent, but it was difficult.
The children began to laugh at his strange hat during recess.
