to send around

verb
to distribute or forward to others
Pronunciation: / sɛnd əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / sɛnd əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to distribute or forward (something) to a group of people, typically by sending copies to each of them

Examples

to send around an email
Each department head was asked to send around the updated policy document.
I'll send around the meeting agenda to everyone this afternoon.
She sent around an email inviting all her friends to the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – circulate: to pass or spread (something) among a group
  • synonym – distribute: to give out or deliver (something) to a number of people
