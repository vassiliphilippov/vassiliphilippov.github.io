to yap away

verb
talk incessantly in an annoying way
Pronunciation: / jæp əˈweɪ / (AmE), / jæp əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to talk continuously and in a tiresome or annoying way

Examples

to yap away all day
Even during the meeting, he couldn’t resist yapping away about trivial matters.
She tends to yap away on the phone every evening with her friends.
The kids would yap away for hours about their favorite cartoons.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chatter: to talk quickly and for a long time about unimportant things
  • synonym – jabber: to talk rapidly and excitedly but with little sense
