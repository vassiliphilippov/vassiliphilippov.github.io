to climb down

verb
descend from a height
Pronunciation: / klaɪm daʊn / (AmE), / klaɪm daʊn / (BrE)
to descend from a higher place by using the hands and feet, especially by means of a ladder, tree, or similar object

Examples

to climb down a ladder
She carefully climbed down the ladder after painting the top of the wall.
The cat got stuck in the tree and wouldn't climb down until we coaxed it.
We watched the hikers climb down the rocky slope as the sun was setting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – climb up: to go up something using hands and feet
  • synonym – descend: to move downward from a higher to a lower place
