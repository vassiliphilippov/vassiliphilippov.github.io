to scrunge together

verb
hastily gather or assemble
Pronunciation: / skrʌndʒ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / skrʌndʒ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to gather or assemble something quickly and with minimal resources, often in a rushed or makeshift manner

Examples

to scrunge together some money
Despite the late notice, he managed to scrunge together enough money for the rent.
She tried to scrunge together a last-minute meal when her friends arrived unexpectedly.
We had to scrunge together supplies for the impromptu picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scrape together: gather together with effort, especially money or resources
  • synonym – scrounge: seek to obtain something, typically money or food, at the expense or through the generosity of others
