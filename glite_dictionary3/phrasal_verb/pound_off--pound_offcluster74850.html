to pound off

verb
partition off into compartments
Pronunciation: / paʊnd ɑf / (AmE), / paʊnd ɒf / (BrE)
to partition (a body of water) off into separate compartments, especially using locks or barriers.

Examples

to pound off a section
After the flood, the team pounded off areas to prevent further damage.
Locks are used to pound off parts of a river for maintenance.
The engineers pounded off sections of the canal to control the water flow.
