to string someone along

verb
deceive or mislead someone over time
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ ˈsʌmwʌn əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / strɪŋ ˈsʌmwən əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to deceive or mislead someone over a period of time, especially by making them believe something false, often to gain time or personal advantage
