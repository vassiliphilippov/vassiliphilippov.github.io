to lick out the bowl

verb
use tongue to clean out a bowl
Pronunciation: / lɪk aʊt ðə boʊl / (AmE), / lɪk aʊt ðə bəʊl / (BrE)
to use one's tongue to remove and eat the remaining contents from a bowl, often after food preparation
