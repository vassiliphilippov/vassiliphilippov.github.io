to turn to

verb
seek help or guidance from
Pronunciation: / tɝn tu / (AmE), / tɜːn tuː / (BrE)
to go to someone or something for help, advice, or guidance

Examples

turn to someone for help

If you have a technical problem, you can turn to our support team for assistance.
When she needed advice, she would always turn to her older sister for guidance.
