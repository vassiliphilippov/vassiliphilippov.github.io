to be sick from

verb
feel nauseated due to a cause
Pronunciation: / bi sɪk frəm / (AmE), / bi sɪk frəm / (BrE)
to feel nauseated or vomit as a result of a specific cause, especially motion or something ingested

Examples

be sick from bad food

She felt like she would be sick from the smell of the strong chemicals.
Some people quickly are sick from long car rides on winding roads.
