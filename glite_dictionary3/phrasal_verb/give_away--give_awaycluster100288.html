to give away

verb
formally hand over bride at wedding
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ɡɪv əˈweɪ / (BrE)
(of a bride) to formally hand over to the bridegroom in marriage, traditionally performed by the father of the bride.

Examples

give away the bride
It was an emotional moment when her father gave away the bride.
Traditionally, the father gives away his daughter at the start of the ceremony.
When her brother gave her away, everyone in the audience was moved to tears.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hand over: to give something to someone else, often formally
