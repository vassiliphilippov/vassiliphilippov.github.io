to scratch off

verb
write quickly or hurriedly
Pronunciation: / skrætʃ ɔf / (AmE), / skrætʃ ɒf / (BrE)

Examples

I quickly scratched off a letter before leaving for the airport.
She scratched off a note to remind herself about the appointment.
