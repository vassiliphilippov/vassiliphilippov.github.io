to build up

verb
accumulate gradually
Pronunciation: / bɪld ʌp / (AmE), / bɪld ʌp / (BrE)
to gradually increase, accumulate, or make something stronger over time

Examples

to build up strength
A lot of stress tends to build up before big exams.
