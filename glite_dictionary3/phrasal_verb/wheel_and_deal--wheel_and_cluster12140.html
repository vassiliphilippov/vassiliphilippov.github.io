to wheel and deal

verb
to negotiate deals energetically
Pronunciation: / wiːl ənd diːl / (AmE), / wiːl ənd diːl / (BrE)
to negotiate, make deals, or trade energetically and often shrewdly to achieve personal advantage, especially in business or politics

You have to wheel and deal if you want to succeed in the stock market.
