to keep going

verb
continue without stopping
Pronunciation: / kip ˈɡoʊɪŋ / (AmE), / kiːp ˈɡəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
to continue to do something without stopping or being interrupted

Examples

to keep going despite problems

The bus broke down, but the driver managed to keep going until the next stop.
