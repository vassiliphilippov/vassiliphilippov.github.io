to turn back

verb
to force away or drive off
Pronunciation: / tɝn ˈbæk /; / tɝn bæk / (AmE), / tɜːn ˈbæk /; / tɜːn bæk / (BrE)
to stop or prevent someone or something from continuing, entering, or making progress
