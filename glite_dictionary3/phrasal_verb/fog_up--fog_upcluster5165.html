to fog up

verb
become covered with condensation
Pronunciation: / fɑɡ ʌp / (AmE), / fɒɡ ʌp / (BrE)
to become or cause to become covered with small drops of water, making it hard to see through

Examples

fog up the windows
If you don't turn on the defrost, your car windows will fog up.
The bathroom mirror fogged up after my hot shower.
Wearing a mask can make your glasses fog up in the cold weather.
