to veg out

verb
relax inactively; do nothing
Pronunciation: / vɛdʒ aʊt / (AmE), / vɛdʒ aʊt / (BrE)
to relax in a very passive way, often by doing nothing or watching television

Examples

to veg out on the sofa
After work, I love to veg out on the sofa with a good movie.
He spent the afternoon vegging out in front of the TV, not thinking about anything.
Sometimes you just need to veg out and do absolutely nothing all weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chill out: to relax completely or calm down
  • synonym – zone out: to lose concentration or become inattentive, often while relaxing
