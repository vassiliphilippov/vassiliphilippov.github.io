to take down

verb
demolish or dismantle a structure
Pronunciation: / teɪk daʊn / (AmE), / teɪk daʊn / (BrE)
to remove, dismantle, or demolish something that was fixed, displayed, or standing

Could you take down those curtains for washing?
They had to take down the tent before the rain started.
