to request a refund

verb
ask for money back after a purchase
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkwɛst ə rɪˈfʌnd / (AmE), / rɪˈkwɛst ə rɪˈfʌnd / (BrE)
to formally ask for the return of money paid for a product or service, usually because it was unsatisfactory or faulty
