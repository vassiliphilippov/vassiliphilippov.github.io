to brim over

verb
to overflow or spill over
Pronunciation: / brɪm ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / brɪm ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a liquid or emotion) to overflow or spill over the edge, usually because a container or person is full.

Examples

brim over with joy
His heart seemed to brim over with happiness at the news.
Tears began to brim over as she listened to the moving speech.
The cup was filled to the top and started to brim over.
