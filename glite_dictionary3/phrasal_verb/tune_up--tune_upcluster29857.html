to tune up

verb
adjust musical instrument's pitch
Pronunciation: / tun ʌp / (AmE), / tjuːn ʌp / (BrE)
to adjust the pitch or tone of a musical instrument so that it is in the correct or desired tuning

Examples

to tune up a guitar
Every guitarist knows it’s important to tune up before rehearsals begin.
He hired a professional to tune up his grand piano before the concert.
The cello was out of tune, so she stopped to tune up during the intermission.
