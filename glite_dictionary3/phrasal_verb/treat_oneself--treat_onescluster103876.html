to treat oneself

verb
indulge oneself, reward oneself
Pronunciation: / trit wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / triːt wʌnˈself / (BrE)
to do something special for oneself as a form of indulgence or reward

Examples

to treat oneself to dessert

You should treat yourself once in a while—you deserve it!
