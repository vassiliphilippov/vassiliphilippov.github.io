to paste up

verb
attach or display with adhesive
Pronunciation: / peɪst ʌp / (AmE), / peɪst ʌp / (BrE)
to attach or display (something) on a surface, especially by using adhesive

Examples

to paste up posters
He pasted up signs on all the doors to let people know about the meeting.
Let's paste up these posters around the school to advertise the concert.
Volunteers are needed to paste up flyers in the community center.
