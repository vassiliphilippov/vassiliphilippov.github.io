to wrap a bandage

verb
cover a wound with cloth
Pronunciation: / ræp ə ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ræp ə ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
to cover a wound or injured area with a strip of cloth for protection or support

Examples

wrap a bandage around an arm
After the accident, she quickly wrapped a bandage around his bleeding arm.
He had to wrap a bandage tightly to stop the swelling.
The doctor showed me how to wrap a bandage over my scraped knee.
