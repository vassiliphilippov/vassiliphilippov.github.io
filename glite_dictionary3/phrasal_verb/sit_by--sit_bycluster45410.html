to sit by

verb
remain inactive or indifferent
Pronunciation: / sɪt baɪ / (AmE), / sɪt baɪ / (BrE)
to remain inactive or indifferent while something happens, especially something bad or important

Examples

to sit by and watch
I couldn't just sit by and watch him get bullied at school.
Many people choose to sit by instead of speaking out against injustice.
She refused to sit by while her community struggled with pollution.
