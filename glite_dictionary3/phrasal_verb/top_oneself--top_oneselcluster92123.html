to top oneself

verb
surpass one's previous achievement
Pronunciation: / tɑp wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / tɒp wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to achieve something even better than one's previous accomplishment; to surpass one's own earlier efforts.
