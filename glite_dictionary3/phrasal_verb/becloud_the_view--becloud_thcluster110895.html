to becloud the view

verb
obscure or hide the view
Pronunciation: / bɪˈklaʊd ðə vju / (AmE), / bɪˈklaʊd ðə vjuː / (BrE)
to make it difficult or impossible to see something clearly; to obscure or hide the view

Examples

fog beclouds the view
Morning fog often beclouds the view from our kitchen window.
Tall buildings can sometimes becloud the view of beautiful sunsets in the city.
The thick smoke from the wildfire beclouded the view of the valley below.

Related Terms

  • synonym – block the view: to prevent someone from seeing something
  • synonym – obscure: to make something difficult to see or understand
