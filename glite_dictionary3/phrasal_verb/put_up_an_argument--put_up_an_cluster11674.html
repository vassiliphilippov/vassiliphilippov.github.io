to put up an argument

verb
present or express an argument
Pronunciation: / pʊt ʌp ən ˈɑːrɡjəmənt / (AmE), / pʊt ʌp ən ˈɑːɡjʊmənt / (BrE)
to present, express, or offer an argument, often in opposition or defense

Examples

put up an argument against criticism
Despite the criticism, she put up an argument for her design proposal.
He didn't put up an argument when asked to change teams.
They always put up an argument whenever new rules are introduced.
