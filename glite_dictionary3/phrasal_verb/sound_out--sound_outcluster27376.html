to sound out

verb
pronounce letters or syllables in sequence
Pronunciation: / saʊnd aʊt / (AmE), / saʊnd aʊt / (BrE)
to pronounce the sounds of letters or syllables in a word in order, typically to help read or spell it

Examples

to sound out a word
Can you sound out this word for me?
She sounded out every letter to help her read the sentence.
The teacher asked the children to sound out each syllable of the new word.
