to slide by

verb
(of time) to pass smoothly
Pronunciation: / slaɪd baɪ / (AmE), / slaɪd baɪ / (BrE)
(of time) to pass smoothly and often unnoticed.

Examples

hours slide by
As the hours slid by, he grew more anxious about the exam.
Sometimes years can slide by before we realize how much has changed.
The summer seemed to slide by without us even noticing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fly by: (of time) to seem to go by very quickly
  • synonym – pass by: to go past or elapse, especially of time
