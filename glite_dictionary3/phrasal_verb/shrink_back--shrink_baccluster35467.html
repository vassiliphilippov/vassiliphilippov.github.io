to shrink back

verb
withdraw from fear or disgust
Pronunciation: / ʃrɪŋk bæk / (AmE), / ʃrɪŋk bæk / (BrE)
to move away or withdraw from something because of fear or disgust

Examples

to shrink back in fear
He instinctively shrinks back from the open flame, remembering his childhood accident.
She shrank back when she saw the snake slithering toward her.
Whenever I mention spiders, she shrinks back in her chair.
