to go up

verb
be consumed or destroyed by fire
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ʌp / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ʌp / (BrE)
to be destroyed or consumed by fire; to burn completely.

Examples

go up in flames
Stacks of documents went up in smoke during the fire.
The entire cabin went up within minutes of the blaze starting.
The old barn went up in flames after the lightning strike.
