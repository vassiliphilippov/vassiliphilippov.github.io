to press out

verb
extract by pressing
Pronunciation: / prɛs aʊt / (AmE), / prɛs aʊt / (BrE)
to obtain (a liquid or other substance) from something by applying mechanical force

Examples

to press out juice
He pressed out the excess water from the spinach before cooking it.
She used a juicer to press out the fresh orange juice for breakfast.
They pressed out the oil from the seeds using heavy machinery.
