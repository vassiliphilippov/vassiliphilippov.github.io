to strike dumb

verb
render someone speechless with surprise or shock
Pronunciation: / straɪk dʌm / (AmE), / straɪk dʌm / (BrE)
to make someone unable to speak, usually because of surprise, shock, or fear

Examples

strike dumb with shock
He was struck dumb with disbelief when he heard the news.
Her unexpected question struck dumb everyone in the room.
The audience was struck dumb by the breathtaking performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – astound: to surprise or shock someone very much
  • antonym – find one's voice: to begin to speak or respond again after being silent, especially after shock
  • synonym – stun: to shock or surprise someone so much that they are temporarily unable to react
