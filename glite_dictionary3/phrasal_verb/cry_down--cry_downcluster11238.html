to cry down

verb
belittle, disparage
Pronunciation: / kraɪ daʊn / (AmE), / kraɪ daʊn / (BrE)
to publicly express a low opinion of someone or something; to belittle or disparage.

Examples

to cry down new ideas
Instead of offering help, he would rather cry down our ideas.
It's unfair to cry down someone's efforts just because you disagree with them.
The critics were quick to cry down the new policy before understanding it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – belittle: make someone or something seem unimportant
  • antonym – praise: express approval or admiration for someone or something
