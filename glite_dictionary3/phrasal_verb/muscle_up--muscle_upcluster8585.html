to muscle up

verb
perform a pull-up-to-dip movement
Pronunciation: / c8m8cs59l 8cp / (AmE), / c8m8csl 8cp / (BrE)
(in fitness/gymnastics) to pull oneself up and then push up over a bar or rings in one continuous movement (a pull-up followed by a dip).

Examples

to muscle up on a bar
She finally managed to muscle up onto the high bar after weeks of practice.
He muscled up from the rings to complete the full gymnastic movement.
At the CrossFit class, they practiced muscling up quickly between sets to build endurance.
