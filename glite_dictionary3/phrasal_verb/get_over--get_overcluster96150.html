to get over

verb
travel across or pass over
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to travel across or pass over something such as a distance, obstacle, or area

Examples

to get over a wall
It took us hours to get over the desert due to the heat.
The hikers managed to get over the mountain before nightfall.
We need to get over this river to reach the village.
