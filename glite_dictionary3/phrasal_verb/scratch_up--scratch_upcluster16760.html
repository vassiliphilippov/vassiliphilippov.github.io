to scratch up

verb
damage surface by scratching
Pronunciation: / skrætʃ ʌp / (AmE), / skrætʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to damage the surface of something by making small cuts or marks, typically by scraping or rubbing against it

Examples

to scratch up a surface
Be careful not to scratch up the table when you move it.
The cat managed to scratch up the new sofa with its claws.
Years of neglect had scratched up the old car's paint.
