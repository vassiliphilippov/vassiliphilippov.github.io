to plug in

verb
connect electrical device to power supply
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ ɪn / (AmE), / plʌɡ ɪn / (BrE)
to connect a piece of electrical equipment to a power supply by inserting its plug into a socket

Examples

to plug in a device
Before you use the blender, make sure to plug in the cord.
Can you plug in the lamp? It's too dark in here.
She forgot to plug in her phone last night, so the battery was dead this morning.

  • antonym – unplug: to disconnect electrical equipment from a power supply
