to go down

verb
(of a system/service) stop operating
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈdaʊn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈdaʊn / (BrE)
(of a system, service, or machine) to stop operating or functioning, especially temporarily.

Examples

go down during maintenance
If too many users try to access it, the system might go down again.
Our network went down unexpectedly during the storm.
The website will go down for maintenance tonight at midnight.

  • synonym – crash: (of a computer system) to stop working suddenly
