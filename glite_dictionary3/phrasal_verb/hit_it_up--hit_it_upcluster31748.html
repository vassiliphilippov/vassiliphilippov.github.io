to hit it up

verb
meet or socialize (informal)
Pronunciation: / hɪt ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / hɪt ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
(informal) To meet or socialize with someone, often for leisure or a friendly gathering.

Examples

hit it up at a bar
Let's hit it up at the new bar after work tonight.
They usually hit it up on Friday evenings with their friends.
We should hit it up sometime soon and catch up.
