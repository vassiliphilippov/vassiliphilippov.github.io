to turn out

verb
gather for a public event
Pronunciation: / tɝn aʊt / (AmE), / tɜːn aʊt / (BrE)
to come or gather for an event, especially in response to an invitation or announcement

Examples

turn out for an event
Despite the rain, a large crowd turned out for the outdoor concert.
Only a few students turned out for the early morning lecture.
Thousands of people turned out to see the royal parade.
A large crowd turned out for the opening ceremony.
Only a few people turned out despite the invitations sent to everyone.
We were surprised by how many guests turned out to support the charity event.
