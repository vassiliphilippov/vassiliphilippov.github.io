to conceive of

verb
to imagine or form an idea of
Pronunciation: / kənˈsiːv əv / (AmE), / kənˈsiːv əv / (BrE)
to form an idea or mental image of something; to imagine or think of something as possible or as true

Examples

to conceive of life elsewhere
Few people could conceive of life on another planet a century ago.
It's hard to conceive of a world without technology.
She could not conceive of him betraying her trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym – envision: to picture mentally, especially a future event
  • synonym – imagine: to form a mental image or concept of something
