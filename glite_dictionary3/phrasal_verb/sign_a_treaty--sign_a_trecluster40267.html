to sign a treaty

verb
to officially endorse a treaty
Pronunciation: / saɪn ə ˈtriti / (AmE), / saɪn ə ˈtriːti / (BrE)
to formally agree to and endorse a treaty by signing it, making its terms official

Examples

to sign a treaty officially
After months of negotiations, both countries agreed to sign a treaty on economic cooperation.
She attended the ceremony where the two governments signed a treaty of mutual defense.
The leaders will sign a treaty next week to end the long-standing border dispute.
