to draw together

verb
bring people closer emotionally
Pronunciation: / drɔ təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / drɔ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to bring people together, making them feel united in purpose or emotion

Examples

to draw together a group
Shared goals can draw together people from very different backgrounds.
The conflict ultimately drew together the two rival teams.
The tragedy drew together the entire community in support.

Related Terms

  • antonym – separate: to move apart or cause to move apart
