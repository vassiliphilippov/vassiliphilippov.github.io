to slick down

verb
make smooth and glossy
Pronunciation: / slɪk daʊn / (AmE), / slɪk daʊn / (BrE)
to make (hair or a surface) smooth and glossy by applying a substance or by stroking it flat

Examples

to slick down messy hair
He used gel to slick down his unruly hair before the interview.
She likes to slick down her baby hairs along her hairline for a polished look.
The rain helped slick down the dust on the road, making it look cleaner.
