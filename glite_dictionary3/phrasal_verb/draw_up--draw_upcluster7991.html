to draw up

verb
order or arrange in formation (esp. soldiers)
Pronunciation: / drɔː ʌp / (AmE), / drɔː ʌp / (BrE)
to form or arrange people or things, especially soldiers, into a particular order or formation

Examples

draw up the soldiers
After the signal, the cadets drew up quickly and waited for inspection.
The commander ordered the troops to draw up in a straight line.
The soldiers were drawn up in formation on the parade ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym – form up: to take up a particular formation, especially of soldiers
