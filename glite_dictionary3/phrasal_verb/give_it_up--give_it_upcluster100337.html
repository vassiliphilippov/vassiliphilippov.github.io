to give it up

verb
stop doing something; quit
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɪv ɪt ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˌɡɪv ɪt ˈʌp / (BrE)

Examples

to give it up for good

If you can't win the game, sometimes it's best to give it up and move on.
She found it hard to give it up even though everyone advised her to quit.
