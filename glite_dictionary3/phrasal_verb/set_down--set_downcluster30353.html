to set down

verb
land; come to rest (of vehicle, aircraft)
Pronunciation: / sɛt daʊn / (AmE), / sɛt daʊn / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, aircraft, etc.) to land or come to rest on the ground or another surface.

Examples

set down on the runway
Do not set down until you receive clearance from air traffic control.
The helicopter will set down in the clearing ahead.
The rescue boat set down safely after navigating the rough waters.
