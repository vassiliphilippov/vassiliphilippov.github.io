to feed upon

verb
be sustained or nourished by
Pronunciation: / fid əˈpɑn / (AmE), / fiːd əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to be sustained, nourished, or inspired by something, especially in an abstract or figurative sense

Examples

to feed upon new ideas
As a young scientist, he fed upon the research papers of great minds in his field.
Many artists feed upon personal experiences to create meaningful works.
She feeds upon the wisdom in ancient texts to guide her decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – feed on: to be nourished or supported by something, often used interchangeably
