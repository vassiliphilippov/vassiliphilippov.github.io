to jive on

verb
collaborate or agree on a topic
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪv ɑn / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪv ɒn / (BrE)
To collaborate with someone or come to an agreement on a particular topic or issue.

Examples

jive on the details
If we can jive on the details, this partnership could be a big success.
Let's sit down and see if we can jive on our goals before moving forward.
They just couldn't jive on the direction of the project, so they went their separate ways.
