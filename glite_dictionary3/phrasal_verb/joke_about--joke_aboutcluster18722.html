to joke about

verb
make humorous remarks about
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊk əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊk əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to make humorous or mocking remarks regarding someone or something, often not meant to be taken seriously

Examples

to joke about mistakes
He always jokes about his own mistakes so others won’t feel awkward.
It's not appropriate to joke about serious illnesses in front of patients.
They joked about the weather, even though they were trapped inside all day.

Related Terms

  • antonym – be serious about: to earnestly consider, not joke about something
  • synonym – make fun of: to laugh at or joke about someone or something
  • synonym – mock: to laugh at or make fun of someone or something
