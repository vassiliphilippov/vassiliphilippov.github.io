to rip it up

verb
tear into pieces; start over
Pronunciation: / rɪp ɪt ʌp / (AmE), / rɪp ɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to tear something into pieces, or to completely discard something and start over

Examples

to rip it up angrily
After reviewing her essay, the teacher suggested she rip it up and start from scratch.
He got so frustrated with the letter that he decided to rip it up and throw it away.
Instead of trying to fix the broken contract, the company chose to rip it up and negotiate a new one.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tear up: to tear something into small pieces
