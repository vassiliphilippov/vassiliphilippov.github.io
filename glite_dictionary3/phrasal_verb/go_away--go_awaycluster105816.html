to go away

verb
disappear; become unnoticeable
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to disappear or become no longer visible or noticeable

Examples

go away after rain
As the fog began to go away, the mountains became visible.
The pain will go away after a few days of rest.
The stain didn’t go away even after several washes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – appear: to become visible or noticeable
  • synonym – vanish: to disappear suddenly and completely
