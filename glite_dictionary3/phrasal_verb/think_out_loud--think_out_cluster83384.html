to think out loud

verb
express thoughts verbally as they occur
Pronunciation: / θɪŋk aʊt laʊd / (AmE), / θɪŋk aʊt laʊd / (BrE)
to express one's thoughts verbally while they are being formed, often without intending to address others directly

Examples

to think out loud during work
I often think out loud when trying to make difficult decisions.
Please don't mind me—I'm just thinking out loud about the weekend plans.
She began to think out loud as she worked through the problem on the board.

Related Terms

  • antonym – keep quiet: to not express one's thoughts aloud
  • synonym – muse: to think thoughtfully and sometimes speak one's thoughts
