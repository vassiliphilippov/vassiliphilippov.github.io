to call roll

verb
take attendance by reading names
Pronunciation: / kɔl roʊl / (AmE), / kɔːl rəʊl / (BrE)
to read aloud a list of names to check who is present, especially in a class or meeting

Examples

call roll in class
Please stay quiet while I call roll so we don't miss anyone.
