to pick up

verb
reflect or echo an aspect, often in color or style
Pronunciation: / pɪk ˈʌp / (AmE), / pɪk ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of a color, feature, or element) to reflect, echo, or highlight an aspect of something else, especially visually.
