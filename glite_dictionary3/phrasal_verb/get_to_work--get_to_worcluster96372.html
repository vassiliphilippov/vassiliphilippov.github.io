to get to work

verb
begin working or start tasks
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt tə ˈwɝk / (AmE), / ɡɛt tə ˈwɜːk / (BrE)
to begin working or start carrying out tasks, especially after a delay or interruption

Examples

to get to work on a project
After chatting for a while, we decided it was time to get to work on the project.
Once everyone arrived, they quickly got to work setting up the event.
She likes to get to work early so she can leave by five o'clock.
