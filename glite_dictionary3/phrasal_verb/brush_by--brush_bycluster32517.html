to brush by

verb
pass closely, often unnoticed
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ baɪ / (AmE), / brʌʃ baɪ / (BrE)
to move past someone or something very closely, often without pausing or attracting attention

Examples

to brush by a person
She tried to brush by her boss without being noticed.
The cat would often brush by my legs when it wanted food.
Tourists sometimes brush by the exhibits without really noticing them.
