to top off

verb
fill to the top; almost overflowing
Pronunciation: / tɑp ɔf / (AmE), / tɒp ɒf / (BrE)
to fill something to the top or to the point of almost overflowing

Examples

to top off a glass

She quickly topped off the tank so they wouldn't run out of gas.
Would you like me to top off your coffee before you leave?
