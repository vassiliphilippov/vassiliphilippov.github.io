to act as a liaison

verb
serve as an intermediary or connection
Pronunciation: / ækt æz ə liˈeɪzɑn / (AmE), / ækt æz ə liˈeɪzɒn / (BrE)
to serve as an intermediary or connection between two groups or parties, facilitating communication or cooperation

Examples

to act as a liaison between teams
During the merger, she acted as a liaison between the staff and management.
He is currently acting as a liaison to help both teams coordinate better.
Our consultant will act as a liaison with the local authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – liaison: a person who acts as a link between people or organizations
  • synonym – mediate: to intervene between people in a dispute in order to bring about an agreement
