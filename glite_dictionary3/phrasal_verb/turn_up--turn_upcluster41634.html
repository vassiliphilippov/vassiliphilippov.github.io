to turn up

verb
to arrive or appear unexpectedly
Pronunciation: / tɝn ʌp / (AmE), / tɜːn ʌp / (BrE)
to arrive or appear unexpectedly or after being lost or missing

Examples

turn up at a party
Despite the heavy rain, several volunteers managed to turn up to help with the clean-up.
He didn't expect his old friend to turn up at the party after so many years.
My missing notebook finally turned up under a pile of papers on my desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym – show up: to arrive, especially unexpectedly or after waiting
  • antonym – vanish: to disappear completely and suddenly
