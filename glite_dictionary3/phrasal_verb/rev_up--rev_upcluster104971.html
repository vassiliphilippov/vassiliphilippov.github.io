to rev up

verb
increase engine speed (RPM)
Pronunciation: / rɛv ʌp / (AmE), / rɛv ʌp / (BrE)
to increase the speed at which an engine operates by accelerating its revolutions per minute

Examples

to rev up the engine
He likes to rev up his car engine before driving off.
The mechanic instructed me to rev up the motorcycle to check the sound.
You could hear them revving up their engines at the starting line.
