to bust on

verb
criticize or tease, often playfully
Pronunciation: / bʌst ɑn / (AmE), / bʌst ɒn / (BrE)
to criticize or tease (someone), especially in a playful or informal way

Examples

My brother loves to bust on me whenever I make a silly mistake.
You can't just bust on people for trying something new.
