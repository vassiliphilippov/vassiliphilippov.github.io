to sleep together

verb
share a bed or sleep near someone
Pronunciation: / slip təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / sliːp təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to share a bed or sleep in the same space, typically referring to people sleeping in close proximity regardless of sexual activity

Examples

sleep together in one bed
After the storm, the whole family decided to sleep together in the living room for comfort.
During the camping trip, all the children sleep together in a large tent.
Some cultures expect newlyweds to sleep together from the first night of marriage.
