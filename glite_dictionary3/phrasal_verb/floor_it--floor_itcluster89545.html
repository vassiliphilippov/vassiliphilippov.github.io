to floor it

verb
accelerate a vehicle very quickly
Pronunciation: / flɔr ɪt / (AmE), / flɔː ɪt / (BrE)
to press the accelerator of a vehicle to the floor in order to drive as fast as possible; to accelerate quickly

Examples

to floor it on the highway
If we're going to catch the next train, you'd better floor it.
She floored it as soon as the traffic cleared.
When the police siren sounded behind him, he decided to floor it and speed away.
