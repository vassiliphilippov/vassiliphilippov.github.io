to make up

verb
tidy or arrange; prepare for use
Pronunciation: / meɪk ʌp / (AmE), / meɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to put something in order or tidy it, especially by arranging or preparing it for use

Examples

to make up a bed
Please make up the guest room before our visitors arrive.
She quickly made up the room so it looked neat for her interview.
The housekeeper will make up your bed every morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym – mess up: to make untidy or disordered
