to have a go at

verb
attempt something
Pronunciation: / hæv ə ɡoʊ æt / (AmE), / hæv ə ɡəʊ æt / (BrE)
to try to do something, especially something you have not done before

Examples

to have a go at skateboarding
After seeing the skateboarders, I wanted to have a go at it myself.
If you think you can fix it, why not have a go at repairing the old radio?
She's nervous about singing in public but decided to have a go at karaoke anyway.
