to cruise around

verb
drive or travel leisurely without a set route
Pronunciation: / kruːz əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / kruːz əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to drive or travel slowly and leisurely around an area, especially without a specific destination

Examples

to cruise around the city
He likes cruising around the city at night to relax after work.
They spent the afternoon cruising around town, enjoying the nice weather.
We were cruising around looking for a good spot to eat.
