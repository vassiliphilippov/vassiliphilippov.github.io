to sponge up

verb
absorb or soak up, like a sponge
Pronunciation: / spʌndʒ ʌp / (AmE), / spʌndʒ ʌp / (BrE)
to absorb or soak up a liquid or substance, especially using something absorbent, like a sponge

Examples

to sponge up spilled water
He used a cloth to sponge up the spilled paint before it dried.
She quickly grabbed a towel to sponge up the water on the floor.
The dog knocked over the juice and I had to sponge up the mess.
