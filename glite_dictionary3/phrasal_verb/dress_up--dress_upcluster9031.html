to dress up

verb
put ornamental coverings on animals
Pronunciation: / drɛs ʌp / (AmE), / drɛs ʌp / (BrE)
(of animals, especially horses) to put decorative coverings or ornaments on, often for a special occasion.

Examples

to dress up a horse
Several horses were dressed up in elaborate caparisons for the royal procession.
The stable hands will dress up the horses before the parade begins.
They dressed up the pony with colorful ribbons for the festival.
