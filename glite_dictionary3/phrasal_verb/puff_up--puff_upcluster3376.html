to puff up

verb
become proud, arrogant, or angry
Pronunciation: / pʌf ʌp / (AmE), / pʌf ʌp / (BrE)
to become filled with pride, arrogance, or anger, often showing this in one's appearance or behavior

Examples

he puffs up with pride
He tends to puff up when people compliment his work.
She puffed up with anger at the unfair accusation.
Whenever he talks about his promotion, he can't help but puff up.

Related Terms

  • antonym – deflate: to lose confidence or pride, often after being proud
  • synonym – swell with pride: to become very proud, often visibly
