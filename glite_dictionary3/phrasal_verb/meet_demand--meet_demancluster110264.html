to meet demand

verb
provide enough to satisfy demand
Pronunciation: / mit dɪˈmænd / (AmE), / miːt dɪˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
to supply enough of something to satisfy customers' or users' needs or requirements

Examples

meet demand for products
Farmers must work overtime to meet demand during the harvest season.
If we don't increase production, we won't meet demand this year.
The company launched a second factory to meet demand in overseas markets.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fall short of demand: fail to provide enough to meet demand
  • synonym – satisfy demand: fulfill the requirements or needs for something
