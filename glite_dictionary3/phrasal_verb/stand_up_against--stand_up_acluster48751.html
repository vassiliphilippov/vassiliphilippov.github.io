to stand up against

verb
resist or oppose
Pronunciation: / stænd ʌp əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / stand ʌp əˈɡɛnst / (BrE)

Examples

to stand up against injustice

Many people have stood up against discrimination in their communities.
She was the only one who stood up against the new company policy.
