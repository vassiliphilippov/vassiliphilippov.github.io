to stay on track

verb
remain focused on a goal
Pronunciation: / steɪ ɑn træk / (AmE), / steɪ ɒn træk / (BrE)
to continue focusing on a particular goal or objective without becoming distracted

Examples

to stay on track with work
Despite the distractions, she managed to stay on track with her studies.
If you want to finish the project on time, you need to stay on track.
We set regular reminders to help the team stay on track during the busy season.

  • synonym – stay focused: to keep one's attention on something
