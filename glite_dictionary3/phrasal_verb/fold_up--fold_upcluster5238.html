to fold up

verb
close down; cease trading
Pronunciation: / foʊld ʌp / (AmE), / fəʊld ʌp / (BrE)
to close down or stop operating, especially of a business, shop, or organization; to go out of business.

Examples

fold up a business
After years of losses, the family-owned shop had to fold up last winter.
When the startup failed to secure funding, investors feared it would fold up.
Many small restaurants folded up during the lockdown and never reopened.

Related Terms

  • synonym – close down: to stop operating or providing services, especially a business or institution
  • synonym – go out of business: to cease commercial operation because of financial failure or closure
