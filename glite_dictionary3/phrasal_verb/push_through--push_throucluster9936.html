to push through

verb
persevere despite obstacles
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ θru / (AmE), / pʊʃ θruː / (BrE)
to continue doing something or persevere despite obstacles, difficulty, or opposition

Examples

to push through difficulties
Despite feeling exhausted, she decided to push through and complete her shift.
Even when the weather turned bad, the team managed to push through and finish the race.
It can be hard to push through setbacks, but resilience often pays off.

Related Terms

  • antonym – give up: to stop trying; to quit
  • synonym – persevere: to continue steadily despite difficulty
