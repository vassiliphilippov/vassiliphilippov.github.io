to hash over

verb
discuss or reconsider in detail
Pronunciation: / hæʃ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / hæʃ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to discuss or review something in detail, especially by going over it again; to re-examine or reconsider thoroughly

Examples

hash over the details
After the meeting, they hashed over every detail of the proposal for hours.
The committee spent most of the afternoon hashing over the same old issues.
We don't need to hash over last year's problems again; let's focus on the future.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rehash: to talk about or present something again without much change
