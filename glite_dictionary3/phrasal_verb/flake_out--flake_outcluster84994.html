to flake out

verb
fall asleep suddenly; collapse from fatigue
Pronunciation: / fleɪk aʊt / (AmE), / fleɪk aʊt / (BrE)
to fall asleep, especially suddenly or due to tiredness

Examples

flake out on the couch
After the long hike, I completely flaked out on the couch.
He flaked out before midnight because he was so tired.
She tried to stay awake but flaked out during the movie.

Related Terms

  • synonym – doze: to sleep lightly or nap
  • synonym – nod off: to fall asleep, especially unintentionally
