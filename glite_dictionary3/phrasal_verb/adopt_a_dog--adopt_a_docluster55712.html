to adopt a dog

verb
to legally take a dog as a pet
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑpt ə dɔɡ / (AmE), / əˈdɒpt ə dɒɡ / (BrE)
to take a dog into one's home as a pet, especially through a legal or formal process, often from a shelter or rescue organization

Examples

to adopt a dog from a shelter
After months of convincing her parents, Lily finally adopted a dog for her birthday.
Many families choose to adopt a dog instead of buying one from a breeder.
We decided to adopt a dog from the local animal shelter last spring.
