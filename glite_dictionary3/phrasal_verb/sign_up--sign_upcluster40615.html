to sign up

verb
register to participate or join
Pronunciation: / saɪn ʌp / (AmE), / saɪn ʌp / (BrE)
to register or agree to join a group, activity, job, or service

Examples

to sign up for a class
Have you signed up for the school newsletter yet?
I decided to sign up for the photography workshop next weekend.
They encouraged employees to sign up for the new health insurance plan.
I decided to sign up for piano lessons after seeing the school advertisement.
Many athletes sign up for marathons months before the actual event.
She signed up as a volunteer at the local community center last year.

  • synonym – enroll: to officially register as a member of an institution or course
  • synonym – register: to officially sign up or enroll
  • antonym – withdraw: to remove oneself from participation or enrollment
