to feed back

verb
resubmit information to a system
Pronunciation: / fid bæk / (AmE), / fiːd bæk / (BrE)
to submit information or data again to a program or automatic system for further processing or analysis.

Examples

to feed back data
After making corrections, she had to feed back the updated file into the database.
Engineers will feed back the sensor data to improve the model's accuracy.
The system asks you to feed back your answer for further analysis.
