to keep back

verb
prevent the expression of emotion
Pronunciation: / kip bæk / (AmE), / kiːp bæk / (BrE)
to prevent oneself or someone else from expressing an emotion or reaction openly

Examples

to keep back tears
He couldn't keep back his frustration when he heard the news.
She tried to keep back her tears during the emotional speech.
They struggled to keep back their laughter at the joke.
