to bollix up

verb
make a mess of; spoil
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːlɪks ʌp / (AmE), / ˈbɒlɪks ʌp / (BrE)
to make a mess of; to spoil or ruin something through clumsiness or incompetence

Examples

to bollix up a plan
Don’t bollix up the presentation with last-minute changes.
I really bollixed up the project by missing the deadline.
The new manager has bollixed up the office filing system completely.

Related Terms

  • synonym – botch: to carry out a task badly or carelessly
  • antonym – fix: to repair or correct something that was messed up
  • synonym – screw up: to make a serious mistake or mess of something (informal)
