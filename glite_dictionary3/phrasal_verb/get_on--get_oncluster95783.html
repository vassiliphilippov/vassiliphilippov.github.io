to get on

verb
mount, especially a horse or bicycle
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ɑn / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɒn / (BrE)
to climb or mount onto the back of something, especially a horse, bicycle, or similar object.

Examples

to get on a bike

I watched him get on his bike and disappear down the street.
She quickly got on the horse and rode away.
