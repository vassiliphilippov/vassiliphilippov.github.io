to put down

verb
to euthanize an animal
Pronunciation: / pʊt daʊn / (AmE), / pʊt daʊn / (BrE)
to kill an animal, usually because it is old, sick, or suffering, in a way intended to minimize pain

Examples

to put down a sick animal
After the horse broke its leg, the owners agreed to have it put down.
It was a difficult decision, but they had to put down their elderly cat.
The vet advised that the dog should be put down to prevent further suffering.

Related Terms

  • synonym – euthanize: to deliberately end the life of an animal or person to relieve suffering
