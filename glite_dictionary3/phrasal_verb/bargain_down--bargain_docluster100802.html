to bargain down

verb
persuade to accept a lower price
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrɡən daʊn / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡən daʊn / (BrE)
to persuade someone to accept a lower price for something than was initially offered

Examples

to bargain down the price
He bargained down the car dealer by several hundred dollars before making the purchase.
I always try to bargain down street vendors when I travel abroad.
She managed to bargain down the price of the sofa at the flea market.
