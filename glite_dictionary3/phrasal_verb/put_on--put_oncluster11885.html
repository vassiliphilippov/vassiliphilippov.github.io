to put on

verb
gain weight
Pronunciation: / pʊt ɑn / (AmE), / pʊt ɒn / (BrE)
to gain (weight); to become heavier, especially by accumulating body fat

Examples

to put on weight
After recovering from illness, he put on ten pounds very quickly.
It's easy to put on weight if you eat late at night.
She noticed she had put on some weight over the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gain: to increase in weight
  • antonym – lose weight: to become lighter by reducing one's body weight
