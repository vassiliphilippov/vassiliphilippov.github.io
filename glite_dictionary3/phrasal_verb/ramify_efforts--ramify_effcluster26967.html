to ramify efforts

verb
expand or diversify actions
Pronunciation: / ˈræməˌfaɪ ˈɛfərts / (AmE), / ˈramɪˌfaɪ ˈɛfəts / (BrE)
to expand or diversify actions or initiatives to cover a wider area or set of objectives

Examples

to ramify efforts across regions
The organization plans to ramify efforts to address hunger in both urban and rural areas.
The school administration decided to ramify efforts by launching several community programs this year.
To succeed in the competitive market, we must ramify efforts across new product lines.

Related Terms
