to take one's eyes off

verb
stop looking at something or someone
Pronunciation: / teɪk wʌnz aɪz ɑf / (AmE), / teɪk wʌnz aɪz ɒf / (BrE)
to stop looking at something or someone; to cease gazing at something or someone, often because it is difficult to do so

Examples

to take your eyes off the screen
It's hard to take your eyes off a newborn baby when they're sleeping peacefully.
She warned her son not to take his eyes off the road while driving.
The performance was so captivating that I couldn't take my eyes off the stage.
