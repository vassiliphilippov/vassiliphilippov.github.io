to add to

verb
make something increase or intensify
Pronunciation: / æd tu / (AmE), / æd tʊ / (BrE)
to make something increase in number, amount, or degree; to intensify or enlarge something

Examples

to add to the problem
Rising costs add to the challenges faced by small businesses.
The recent delays only add to our frustration with the project.
Your support will add to the success of this campaign.

  • synonym – increase: to become or make greater in number, amount, or degree
