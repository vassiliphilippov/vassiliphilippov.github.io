to splash out

verb
spend money extravagantly
Pronunciation: / splæʃ aʊt / (AmE), / splæʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to spend a lot of money on something, especially in a way that is more extravagant than usual or necessary

Examples

to splash out on clothes
After getting her bonus, Emily splashed out on some new furniture for the house.
It's not often I splash out, but tonight calls for a celebration.
We decided to splash out and book a suite for our anniversary.
