to hit a sixer

verb
score six runs in cricket in one shot
Pronunciation: / hɪt ə ˈsɪksər / (AmE), / hɪt ə ˈsɪksə / (BrE)
(in cricket) to score six runs with a single shot by hitting the ball over the boundary without it touching the ground.
