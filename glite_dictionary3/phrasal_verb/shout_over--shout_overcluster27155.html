to shout over

verb
speak loudly to be heard over noise
Pronunciation: / ʃaʊt ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ʃaʊt ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to speak very loudly in order to be heard above other noise

Examples

to shout over loud music
During the party, we often had to shout over the loud music to talk.
He tried to shout over the wind, but no one could hear him.
The teacher had to shout over the sound of the construction outside the classroom.
