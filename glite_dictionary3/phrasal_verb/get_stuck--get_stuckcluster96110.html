to get stuck

verb
become unable to move or continue
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt stʌk / (AmE), / ɡɛt stʌk / (BrE)
to become unable to move or continue, usually because something is blocking or holding you back

Examples

get stuck in traffic
He tried to open the door, but it got stuck halfway.
If you get stuck on a problem, ask your teacher for help.
My car got stuck in the mud after the storm last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym – become unstuck: to become free after being stuck
  • synonym – bog down: to become or cause to become stuck or slowed down
