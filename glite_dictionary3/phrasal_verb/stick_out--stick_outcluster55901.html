to stick out

verb
be easily noticeable; attract attention
Pronunciation: / stɪk aʊt / (AmE), / stɪk aʊt / (BrE)
to be easily seen or attract attention because of being different or noticeable

Examples

stick out in a crowd
Her bright red coat made her stick out in the crowd.
His unconventional ideas always stick out during meetings.
The error in the report really stuck out to me.

  • synonym – stand out: to be very noticeable or distinct
