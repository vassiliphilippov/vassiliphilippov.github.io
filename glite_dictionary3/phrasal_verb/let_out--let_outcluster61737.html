to let out

verb
make a sound audibly
Pronunciation: / lɛt aʊt / (AmE), / lɛt aʊt / (BrE)
to express a sound, such as a cry, sigh, or other audible utterance, sometimes involuntarily or with strong feeling

Examples

let out a scream
He let out a deep breath, relieved the interview was over.
She couldn't help but let out a laugh at the joke.
The patient suddenly let out a loud groan during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – emit: to produce or discharge (a sound, noise, etc.)
  • synonym – utter: to say or make a sound with the voice
