to leave a mark

verb
have a lasting impact or influence
Pronunciation: / liv ə mɑrk / (AmE), / liːv ə mɑːk / (BrE)
to have a lasting impact or influence on someone or something, often in a noticeable way

Examples

to leave a mark on history
His innovative ideas left a mark on the industry that lasted for decades.
The teacher's encouragement really left a mark on her confidence.
Years of neglect had left a mark on the old house, making repairs necessary.

  • synonym – make an impact: to have a strong effect on someone or something
