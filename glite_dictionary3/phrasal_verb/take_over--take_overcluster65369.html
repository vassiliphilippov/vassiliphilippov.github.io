to take over

verb
assume another's expenses or debts
Pronunciation: / teɪk ˈoʊvər /; / teɪk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / teɪk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to assume responsibility for the expenses, debts, or obligations of another person or entity

Examples

to take over debts

Banks sometimes take over loans when smaller lenders go out of business.
