to fetch the mail

verb
retrieve email from a server
Pronunciation: / fɛtʃ ðə meɪl / (AmE), / fɛtʃ ðə meɪl / (BrE)
(in computing) to retrieve electronic mail from a mail server or service, typically by an email client or automated agent contacting the server and downloading messages.

Examples

to fetch the mail automatically
The mail client will fetch the mail every ten minutes to check for new messages.
After rebooting, her phone began to fetch the mail and show several unread emails.
The administrator scheduled a script to fetch the mail from the shared account each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – download: to transfer data from a remote system (such as a server) to a local device
  • synonym – retrieve: to get back or obtain data or files, especially from remote storage or a server
