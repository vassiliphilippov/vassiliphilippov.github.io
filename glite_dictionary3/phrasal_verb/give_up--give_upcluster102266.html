to give up

verb
(machine) stop functioning
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ʌp / (AmE), / ɡɪv ʌp / (BrE)
(of a machine or device) to stop functioning or cease to operate, usually after a period of use.

Examples

old machine gives up
After years of faithful service, the washing machine finally gave up last night.
My old laptop gave up in the middle of an important project.
The flashlight gave up just when we needed it during the power outage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – break down: (of a machine) to stop working
  • synonym – malfunction: fail to function properly (of a device)
