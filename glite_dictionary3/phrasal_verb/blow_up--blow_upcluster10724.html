to blow up

verb
to swell or become enlarged
Pronunciation: / bloʊ ʌp / (AmE), / bləʊ ʌp / (BrE)
to swell or cause something to swell or become larger, often suddenly

Examples

blow up an ankle
Her ankle started to blow up after she twisted it during the game.
If you store this balloon in the sun, it might blow up unexpectedly.
The cake will blow up in the oven if you add too much yeast.

Related Terms

  • antonym – shrink: to become smaller in size
  • synonym – swell: to increase in size, typically as a result of internal pressure
