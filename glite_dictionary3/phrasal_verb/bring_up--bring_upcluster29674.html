to bring up

verb
summon or conjure into existence
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ ʌp / (AmE), / brɪŋ ʌp / (BrE)
to summon into action or bring into existence, often as if by magic or supernatural means

Examples

bring up a storm
He claimed he could bring up a storm with just a few words.
The ancient ritual was said to bring up spirits from the underworld.
The magician tried to bring up an image of the lost city in the smoke.

  • synonym – summon: to call forth or evoke, especially by supernatural means
