to talk to oneself

verb
speak aloud when alone
Pronunciation: / tɑk tə wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / tɔːk tʊ wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to speak aloud when alone or not addressing others, often as a way of processing thoughts or feelings

Examples
