to tell stories

verb
narrate accounts or events
Pronunciation: / tɛl ˈstɔriz / (AmE), / tɛl ˈstɔːriz / (BrE)
to narrate accounts of events or experiences, either real or imaginary, to others

Examples

to tell stories to children
My grandfather used to tell stories about his childhood every night before bed.
She loves telling stories of her adventures abroad to her friends.
The children gathered around the campfire to tell stories under the stars.
