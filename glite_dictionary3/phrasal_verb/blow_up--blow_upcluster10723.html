to blow up

verb
fill with gas or air
Pronunciation: / bloʊ ʌp / (AmE), / bləʊ ʌp / (BrE)
To fill something, such as a balloon or tyre, with gas or air until it becomes larger or expands.

Examples

to blow up a balloon
Can you blow up these balloons for the party?
I forgot to blow up my air mattress before the guests arrived.
She used a pump to blow up her bicycle tyres.
