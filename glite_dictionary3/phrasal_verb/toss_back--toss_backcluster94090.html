to toss back

verb
move quickly backward, often head or hair
Pronunciation: / tɑs bæk / (AmE), / tɒs bæk / (BrE)
to move something backward quickly and lightly, often by throwing or jerking; especially used for moving one's head or hair back in a swift motion

Examples

He tossed back his head in laughter, unable to control his amusement.
She tossed back her hair and smiled at the camera.
The actress tossed back her head dramatically during the performance.
