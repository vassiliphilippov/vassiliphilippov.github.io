to get away

verb
escape from confinement
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to escape from a place where you are being held or confined, especially secretly or by force

Examples

to get away from prison
She tried to get away from her captors by climbing through a window.
