to hold up

verb
physically support; carry the weight of
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ʌp / (AmE), / həʊld ʌp / (BrE)
to support and carry the weight of something; to prevent something from falling by physically supporting it

Examples

hold up heavy books
Can this thin shelf really hold up all these heavy books?
The ancient columns still hold up the ruins after centuries.
The steel beams hold up the massive bridge deck.

Related Terms

  • synonym – support: to bear the weight of something and keep it upright
