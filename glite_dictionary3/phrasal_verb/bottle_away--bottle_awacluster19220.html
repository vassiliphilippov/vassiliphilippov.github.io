to bottle away

verb
suppress or hide emotions/memories
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑtəl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to consciously suppress or hide one's emotions, memories, or feelings rather than expressing or confronting them

Examples

bottle away your feelings
During stressful times, many people bottle away their fears instead of sharing them.
Rather than talking about his grief, he chose to bottle away his sorrow for years.
She regrets having bottled away her pain instead of seeking support.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bottle up: to restrain or repress emotions
  • antonym – express: to make known one's feelings or thoughts
