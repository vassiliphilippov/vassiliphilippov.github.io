to buckle down

verb
begin to work hard in earnest
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkəl daʊn /; / ˈbʌk.əl daʊn / (AmE), / ˈbʌkəl daʊn /; / ˈbʌk.əl daʊn / (BrE)
