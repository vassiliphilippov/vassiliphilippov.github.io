to strike up

verb
begin to play music or sing
Pronunciation: / straɪk ʌp / (AmE), / straɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to begin to play music or start singing, especially by a group or band

Examples

to strike up a song
As soon as the guests arrived, the band struck up a lively tune.
During the ceremony, the orchestra will strike up the national anthem.
Whenever he entered the room, the pianist would strike up his favorite song.

  • antonym – cease: to stop doing something; to bring to an end
