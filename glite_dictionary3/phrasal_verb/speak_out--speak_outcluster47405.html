to speak out

verb
express opinions openly and fearlessly
Pronunciation: / spiːk aʊt / (AmE), / spiːk aʊt / (BrE)
to express one's opinions or feelings openly and without fear, especially when it may be difficult to do so

Examples

to speak out against injustice
Many people are too afraid to speak out against injustice.
She decided to speak out, even though it was risky for her career.
The organization encourages victims to speak out about their experiences.
