to knock off

verb
write or produce quickly
Pronunciation: / nɑk ɑf / (AmE), / nɒk ɒf / (BrE)
to write or produce something quickly and with little effort

Examples

knock off three stories
He managed to knock off three short stories in one afternoon.
She can knock off a report in less than an hour when she's focused.
They asked me to knock off a quick summary before the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dash off: to write something quickly
  • synonym – scratch off: to write quickly and possibly carelessly
