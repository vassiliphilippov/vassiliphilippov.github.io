to care for

verb
look after or treat (someone ill or injured)
Pronunciation: / kɛr fɔr / (AmE), / keə fə / (BrE)
to look after someone who is sick or injured and provide necessary treatment or attention

Examples

to care for a sick person
After the accident, nurses worked tirelessly to care for the injured passengers.
He stayed home from work to care for his sick daughter.
Medical volunteers helped care for people affected by the hurricane.
