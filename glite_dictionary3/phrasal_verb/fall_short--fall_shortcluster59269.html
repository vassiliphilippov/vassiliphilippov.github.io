to fall short

verb
fail to reach a required standard
Pronunciation: / fɔl ʃɔrt / (AmE), / fɔːl ʃɔːt / (BrE)
to fail to reach the required or expected standard or amount

Examples

fall short of expectations
Despite all their efforts, the team's results still fall short of expectations.
If you fall short in your duties, there may be consequences.
The movie fell short of the critics' high standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fail: to not achieve something expected or desired
  • antonym – meet expectations: to satisfy or fulfill what is expected or required
