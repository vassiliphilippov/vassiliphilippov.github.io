to hand off

verb
transfer responsibility or control to someone else
Pronunciation: / hænd ɔf / (AmE), / hænd ɒf / (BrE)
to transfer responsibility, control, or an object to another person

Examples

to hand off a project
After launching the product, the team will hand off support to the customer service department.
I need to hand off the client files before I leave for vacation.
The coach decided to hand off leadership duties to her assistant.

Related Terms

  • synonym – delegate: to entrust a task or responsibility to another person
  • antonym – retain: to keep possession of something, not to give it to someone else
