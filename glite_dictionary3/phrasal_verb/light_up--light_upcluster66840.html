to light up

verb
begin to burn brightly
Pronunciation: / laɪt ʌp /; / laɪt ˈʌp / (AmE), / laɪt ʌp /; / laɪt ˈʌp / (BrE)
to make something start to burn or shine with light, or to become bright (including lighting a cigarette, fire, or turning on lights)
