to gas up

verb
hype or praise to boost confidence
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ʌp / (AmE), / ɡæs ʌp / (BrE)
(informal, slang) to praise, flatter, or hype someone in order to raise their confidence, enthusiasm, or mood.

Examples

to gas up a friend
Before the audition, the director gassed up the nervous singer with warm compliments.
He tried to gas up his friend before the presentation so they'd feel more confident.
Sports fans spent the evening gassing up the team on social media after the win.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hype up: to excite or promote someone or something to increase enthusiasm
  • synonym – pump up: to encourage or make someone feel more confident or energetic
