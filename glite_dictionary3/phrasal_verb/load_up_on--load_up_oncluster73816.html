to load up on

verb
gather or consume a lot of something
Pronunciation: / loʊd ʌp ɑn / (AmE), / ləʊd ʌp ɒn / (BrE)
to gather or obtain a large amount of something, or to consume something in large quantities

Examples

to load up on snacks
Before the trip, we should load up on snacks and water.
During flu season, I always load up on vitamin C.
Many people load up on sunscreen before going to the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stock up on: to buy or collect a large quantity of something for future use
