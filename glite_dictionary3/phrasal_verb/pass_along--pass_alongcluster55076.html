to pass along

verb
transmit or forward information or objects
Pronunciation: / pæs əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / pɑːs əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to convey information, news, or an object to someone else, often by relaying from one person to another

Examples

to pass along a message
If you hear anything important, please pass along the news to the rest of the team.
She promised to pass along my message to her supervisor.
When you receive the invitation, be sure to pass it along to anyone who's interested.

Related Terms

  • synonym – forward: to send something onward to another recipient
  • synonym – relay: to pass something on from one person to another
