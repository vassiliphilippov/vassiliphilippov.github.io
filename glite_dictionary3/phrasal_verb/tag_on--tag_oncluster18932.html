to tag on

verb
add or attach as an extra
Pronunciation: / tæɡ ɑn / (AmE), / tæɡ ɒn / (BrE)
to attach or add something, especially as an extra or afterthought

Examples

to tag on extra notes
She tagged on a few extra notes to her presentation after reviewing the feedback.
The editor decided to tag on an additional chapter at the end of the book.
They let us tag on our own questions at the end of the survey.

Related Terms

  • synonym – append: to add as a supplement or attachment
  • antonym – detach: to remove or separate something that is attached
