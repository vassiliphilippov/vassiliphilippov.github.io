to get into trouble

verb
encounter difficulties; face problems
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ˈɪntu ˈtrʌbəl / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈɪntu ˈtrʌbəl / (BrE)

Examples

She always seems to get into trouble no matter how careful she is.
Teenagers often get into trouble by breaking minor rules.
