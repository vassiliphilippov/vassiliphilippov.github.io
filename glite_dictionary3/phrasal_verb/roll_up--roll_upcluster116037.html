to roll up

verb
arrive, often by vehicle
Pronunciation: / roʊl ʌp / (AmE), / rəʊl ʌp / (BrE)
(informal) To arrive, especially in a vehicle or with noticeable presence.

Examples

The delivery truck rolled up right on time this morning.
They rolled up to the party in a bright red convertible.
