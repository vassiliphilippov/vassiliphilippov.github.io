to fall back

verb
rely on as a last resort
Pronunciation: / fɔl bæk / (AmE), / fɔːl bæk / (BrE)
to rely on or use something as a last resort when other options have failed

Examples

to fall back on savings
If he loses his job, he can fall back on his teaching credentials.
The company decided to fall back on traditional advertising when the online campaign proved ineffective.
When the primary plan failed, we had to fall back on our savings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – depend on: to rely on someone or something for support or help
  • synonym – resort to: to turn to or adopt (a course of action, especially an undesirable one) so as to resolve a difficult situation
