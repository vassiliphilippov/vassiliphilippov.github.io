to tread down

verb
crush or flatten by walking on
Pronunciation: / trɛd daʊn / (AmE), / trɛd daʊn / (BrE)
to crush or flatten something by walking on it repeatedly

Examples

to tread down grass

Please do not tread down the newly planted grass in the garden.
The farmers tread down the straw to prepare it for thatching.
