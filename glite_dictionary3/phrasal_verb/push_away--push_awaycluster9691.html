to push away

verb
reject or distance someone
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / pʊʃ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
To reject or distance oneself from someone, especially emotionally or socially.

Examples

push away friends
He realized he was pushing away people who genuinely cared about him.
She regretted having pushed away her friends during a difficult period in her life.
Whenever someone gets too close to him emotionally, he tends to push away.
