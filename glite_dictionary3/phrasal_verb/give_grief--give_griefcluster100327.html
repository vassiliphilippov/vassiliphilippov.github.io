to give grief

verb
criticize or complain about someone, often persistently
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ɡriːf / (AmE), / ɡɪv ɡriːf / (BrE)
to cause someone distress, trouble, or annoyance (by criticizing, complaining, or being difficult)
