to put up

verb
propose as a candidate
Pronunciation: / pʊt ʌp / (AmE), / pʊt ʌp / (BrE)
to propose someone as a candidate for a position or honor

Examples

put up a candidate
He was put up as a candidate for the board of directors.
The committee decided to put up her name for the award.
Their party plans to put up a representative in the local election.
