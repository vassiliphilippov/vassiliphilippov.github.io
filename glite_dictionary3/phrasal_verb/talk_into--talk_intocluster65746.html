to talk into

verb
persuade someone to do something
Pronunciation: / tɑk ˈɪntu / (AmE), / tɔːk ˈɪntuː / (BrE)

It took some effort, but we talked into accepting the new proposal.
She finally talked into joining the hiking club, even though he was hesitant at first.
