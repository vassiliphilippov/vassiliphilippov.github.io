to hold the line

verb
wait on the telephone line
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ðə laɪn / (AmE), / həʊld ðə laɪn / (BrE)
to keep a telephone line open and wait, usually while being connected or as someone retrieves information

Examples

hold the line during a call
Could you hold the line while I transfer your call to the manager?
Please hold the line; your call is very important to us.
She was asked to hold the line as the receptionist looked up her records.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hang on: to wait briefly, especially on the telephone
