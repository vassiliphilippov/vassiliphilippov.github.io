to buy a ticket

verb
purchase admission or entry
Pronunciation: / baɪ ə ˈtɪkət / (AmE), / baɪ ə ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
to purchase admission for an event, journey, or activity, typically by paying money

Examples

to buy a ticket for a concert
I arrived at the station early to buy a ticket before the morning rush.
She forgot to buy a ticket for the theater performance and it sold out.
You can buy a ticket online or at the venue's box office.
