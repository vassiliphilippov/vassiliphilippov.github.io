to adapt to

verb
become accustomed to new conditions
Pronunciation: / əˈdæpt tu / (AmE), / əˈdapt tʊ / (BrE)
to gradually adjust or become accustomed to a new situation, environment, or set of conditions

Examples

to adapt to new life
Children often adapt to changes in their routine more quickly than adults.
It may take some time to adapt to life in a new country.
The software is designed to adapt to the user's needs over time.
