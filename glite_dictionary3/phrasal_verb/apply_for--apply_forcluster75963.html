to apply for

verb
to submit a formal request (job, position, etc.)
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪ fɔr / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ fə / (BrE)
to formally request something, such as a job, scholarship, or permit, usually by completing an application or submitting required documents.

Examples

to apply for a job
He is planning to apply for the new marketing position next month.
Many international students apply for a visa before coming to the country.
She decided to apply for a research grant to fund her project.

  • synonym – apply: to make a formal request or seek something
