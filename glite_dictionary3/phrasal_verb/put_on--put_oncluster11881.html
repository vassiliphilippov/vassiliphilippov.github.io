to put on

verb
to dress yourself in clothes or accessories
Pronunciation: / pʊt ɑn / (AmE), / pʊt ɒn / (BrE)
to cover part of your body with an item of clothing or accessories; to dress yourself in a garment

Examples

to put on a jacket
He put on a tie for the job interview.
It was cold outside, so I put on my gloves.
She always puts on her coat before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym – take off: to remove clothing from your body
