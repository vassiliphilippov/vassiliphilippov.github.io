to put through

verb
connect by telephone
Pronunciation: / pʊt θru /; / pʊt θruː / (AmE), / pʊt θruː / (BrE)
to connect a telephone caller to the person or service they want to speak to

Examples

put through a call
Could you put through this call to Mr. Stevens, please?
I'll try to put through your call now; please hold the line.
The receptionist put through my call to the accounting department quickly.
After a short wait, the operator put through the connection to technical support.
Could you put through my call to Mr. Lee, please?
