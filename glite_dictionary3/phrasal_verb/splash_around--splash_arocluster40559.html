to splash around

verb
play energetically in water
Pronunciation: / splæʃ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / splæʃ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to play by moving about energetically in water and causing water to fly about

Examples

Ducks and geese splash around on the surface of the pond in the morning.
She loves to splash around in the puddles after it rains.
