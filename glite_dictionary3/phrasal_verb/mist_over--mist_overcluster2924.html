to mist over

verb
become covered with moisture or mist
Pronunciation: / mɪst ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / mɪst ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a surface) to become covered with a fine layer of moisture, making it difficult to see through.

Examples

mirror mists over
As she breathed on the mirror, it started to mist over.
The car windows began to mist over in the early morning chill.
The glass misted over quickly when we brought it out of the cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fog up: to become or cause to become covered with fog or steam
