to spark debate

verb
provoke discussion or controversy
Pronunciation: / spɑrk dɪˈbeɪt / (AmE), / spɑːk dɪˈbeɪt / (BrE)
to cause discussion or controversy to begin, especially on a particular topic

Examples

to spark debate online
Her comments on the subject quickly sparked debate among experts in the field.
The documentary has sparked debate regarding the ethics of genetic engineering.
The government's new policy has sparked debate across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym – provoke debate: to cause or incite debate
  • antonym – quell debate: to suppress or put an end to debate
