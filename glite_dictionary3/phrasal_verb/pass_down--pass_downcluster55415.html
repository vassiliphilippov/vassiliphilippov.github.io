to pass down

verb
transmit to later generations
Pronunciation: / pæs daʊn / (AmE), / pɑːs daʊn / (BrE)
to transmit or give something, such as traditions, beliefs, or possessions, from one generation to the next within a family or group

Examples

pass down family traditions
He passed down his knowledge of the craft to his apprentices.
Stories about our ancestors are passed down from parent to child.
These recipes have been passed down for generations in my family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hand down: to give or leave something to people who are younger or live after you
  • synonym – inherit: to receive (a quality, characteristic, or possession) from ancestors
