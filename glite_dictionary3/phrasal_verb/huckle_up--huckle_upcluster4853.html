to huckle up

verb
prepare oneself energetically
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌk.əl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈhʌk.əl ʌp / (BrE)
To prepare oneself or get ready for action with energy and determination.

Examples

to huckle up for work
After lunch, the team huckled up to tackle the big project.
If we want to win this game, we need to huckle up and focus.
She told everyone to huckle up before the deadline approached.
