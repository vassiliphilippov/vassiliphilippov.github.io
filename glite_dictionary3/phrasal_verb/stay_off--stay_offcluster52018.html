to stay off

verb
not enter or walk onto (a place)
Pronunciation: / steɪ ɔf / (AmE), / steɪ ɒf / (BrE)
to avoid entering or walking onto a particular place or surface

Examples

If you stay off the wet grass, it will dry faster.
