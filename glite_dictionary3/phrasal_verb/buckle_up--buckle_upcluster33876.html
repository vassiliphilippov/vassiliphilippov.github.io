to buckle up

verb
fasten seatbelt; prepare for difficulty
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌk.əl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.əl ʌp / (BrE)
to fasten a seatbelt for safety, or to get ready for a difficult or exciting experience

Examples

to buckle up your seatbelt
Before we start the journey, please buckle up for your own safety.
It's going to be a busy week at work, so get ready to buckle up.
The pilot told everyone to buckle up as turbulence was expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fasten: to close or secure something so that it won't open
  • antonym – unbuckle: to unfasten a seatbelt or belt
