to call up

verb
telephone someone
Pronunciation: / kɔl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
to telephone or try to contact someone by phone

Examples

to call up a friend
I'll call up Sarah later to see how she's doing.

After the interview, she called up her parents to share the news.
I meant to call up my sister last night but got too busy.
If you can't find the email, call up the office for help.
