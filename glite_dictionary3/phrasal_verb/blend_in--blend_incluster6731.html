to blend in

verb
combine ingredients evenly by mixing
Pronunciation: / blɛnd ɪn / (AmE), / blɛnd ɪn / (BrE)
to mix (something) with another substance so that the components are combined evenly

Examples

to blend in the eggs
After adding the flour, blend in the eggs until the batter is smooth.
Once the sauce is ready, blend in the cheese and stir well.
You should blend in the spices gently to avoid clumping.
