to bump into

verb
meet someone by chance
Pronunciation: / bʌmp ˈɪntu / (AmE), / bʌmp ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to meet someone by chance or unexpectedly, especially somewhere in public

Examples

bump into an old friend
I was surprised to bump into my old teacher at the grocery store.
She bumped into her childhood friend during the conference.
You never know who you might bump into while traveling abroad.
