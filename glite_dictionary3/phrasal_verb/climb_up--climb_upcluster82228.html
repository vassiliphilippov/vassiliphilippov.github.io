to climb up

verb
move upward with effort
Pronunciation: / klaɪm ʌp / (AmE), / klaɪm ʌp / (BrE)
to move or go upward, especially gradually or with effort, often by using one's hands and feet

Examples

to climb up a ladder
She had to climb up the stairs because the elevator was broken.
The children love to climb up the old oak tree in the park.
We watched the hikers climb up the steep mountain trail.
