to tump over

verb
to tip or overturn (Southern US)
Pronunciation: / tʌmp ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tʌmp ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to tip over or cause to tip over from an upright position; to overturn

Examples

tump over a canoe
Be careful or the canoe will tump over when you stand up.
I accidentally tumped over my coffee cup reaching for the phone.
The wind was so strong it made several trash bins tump over last night.
Be careful not to tump over the canoe when you get in.
He accidentally tumped over his soda while reaching for his phone.
The wind was so strong it tumped over several chairs on the porch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – knock over: to cause something to fall over
  • synonym – overturn: to turn upside down or cause to tip over
  • antonym – upright: in a vertical position, not tipped over
