to smooth down

verb
make less rough or difficult
Pronunciation: / smuð daʊn / (AmE), / smuːð daʊn / (BrE)
to make something less rough, uneven, or difficult by flattening, calming, or resolving it

Examples

to smooth down wrinkles
She used her hands to smooth down the wrinkles in the tablecloth.
The carpenter carefully smoothed down the wooden surface until it was perfectly even.
We need to smooth down any conflicts before the meeting ends.
