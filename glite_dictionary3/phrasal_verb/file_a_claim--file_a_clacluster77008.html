to file a claim

verb
submit a formal request for compensation
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪl ə kleɪm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪl ə kleɪm / (BrE)
to formally submit a request for payment or compensation, especially to an insurance company, after suffering a loss or damage

Examples

to file a claim for insurance
After the storm damaged his house, he immediately filed a claim with his insurance company.
The company helps customers file a claim for compensation after accidents.
You should file a claim if your luggage is lost during your flight.
