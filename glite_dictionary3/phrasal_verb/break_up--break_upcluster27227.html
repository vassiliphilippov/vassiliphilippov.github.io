to break up

verb
laugh uncontrollably
Pronunciation: / breɪk ˈʌp / (AmE), / breɪk ˈʌp / (BrE)
to begin laughing uncontrollably or very loudly, often in response to something funny

Examples

to break up laughing
Every time he tells that story, I can't help but break up laughing.
She completely broke up when she heard the hilarious voicemail.
The comedian's final joke made the entire audience break up for minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – compose oneself: to regain self-control after laughing or strong emotion
  • synonym – crack up: to laugh loudly and uncontrollably
