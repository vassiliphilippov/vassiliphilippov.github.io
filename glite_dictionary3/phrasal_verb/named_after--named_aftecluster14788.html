to named after

verb
given a name in honor of someone or something
Pronunciation: / neɪmd ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / neɪmd ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
to give someone or something the same name as another person or thing, usually to honor or show association with them

Examples

named after a famous person
Many streets are named after historical figures in this town.
My son is named after his grandfather.
The city was named after its founder by the early settlers.
