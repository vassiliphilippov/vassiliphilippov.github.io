to depart for

verb
leave for a destination
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑrt fɔr / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːt fə / (BrE)
to leave a place in order to go to a specified destination

Examples

to depart for Paris
She checked her ticket one last time before departing for her business trip.
The tour group departed for Paris as soon as everyone arrived at the station.
We will depart for Rome early tomorrow morning to avoid the traffic.
