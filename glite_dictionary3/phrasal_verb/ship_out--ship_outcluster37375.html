to ship out

verb
send goods by mail or freight
Pronunciation: / ʃɪp aʊt / (AmE), / ʃɪp aʊt / (BrE)
to send goods or items to customers or other recipients, usually by mail or freight

Examples

to ship out orders
Please ship out these parts to our overseas branch as soon as possible.
The company will ship out your order within two business days.
We need to ship out the holiday packages before the deadline.

  • antonym – receive: to get or be given something that was sent
