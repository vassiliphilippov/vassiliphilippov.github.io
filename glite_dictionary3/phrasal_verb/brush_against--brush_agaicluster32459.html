to brush against

verb
touch lightly in passing
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / brʌʃ əˈɡɛnst / (BrE)
to touch lightly or slightly as one passes by

Examples

As she hurried past, her coat brushed against the vase on the table.
