to brick over

verb
seal or cover with bricks
Pronunciation: / brɪk ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / brɪk ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to cover or seal up (an opening or area) with bricks, especially by building a wall across it

Examples

to brick over a doorway
The doorway was bricked over many years ago during renovations.
They decided to brick over the old fireplace to create more living space.
Workers will brick over the window to improve security.
