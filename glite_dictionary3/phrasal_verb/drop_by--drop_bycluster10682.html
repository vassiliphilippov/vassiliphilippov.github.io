to drop by

verb
visit informally without notice
Pronunciation: / drɑp baɪ / (AmE), / drɒp baɪ / (BrE)
to visit someone or somewhere briefly and informally, usually without planning ahead

Examples

to drop by a friend's house
Feel free to drop by my office anytime if you have questions.
I'm going to drop by the grocery store on my way home.
Yesterday, some old friends dropped by for an unexpected visit.
I'm going to drop by the office to pick up some papers.
She decided to drop by her friend's house on her way home.
Why don't you drop by my place after work for a coffee?

  • synonym – pop in: to visit briefly and informally
  • synonym – stop by: to visit briefly or informally
