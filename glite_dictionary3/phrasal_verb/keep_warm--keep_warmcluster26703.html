to keep warm

verb
maintain body heat, not get cold
Pronunciation: / kip wɔrm / (AmE), / kiːp wɔːm / (BrE)
to prevent yourself from becoming cold; to maintain body heat

Examples

They huddled by the fire to keep warm while camping.
