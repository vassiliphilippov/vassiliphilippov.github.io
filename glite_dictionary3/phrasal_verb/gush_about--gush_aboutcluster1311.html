to gush about

verb
talk enthusiastically about something
Pronunciation: / ɡʌʃ əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / ɡʌʃ əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to speak or write with excessive enthusiasm or praise about someone or something

Examples

to gush about a movie
He gushed about the meal for days after visiting the restaurant.
In her review, the critic gushed about the lead actor's incredible performance.
She couldn't stop gushing about her wonderful vacation to anyone who would listen.
