to wrap around

verb
enclose or cover by winding or folding
Pronunciation: / ræp əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ræp əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to enclose or cover something or someone completely by winding or folding a covering around it

Examples

to wrap around a blanket
She wrapped around a blanket to keep herself warm.
The scarf wraps around his neck twice for extra warmth.
Vines had wrapped around the old wooden fence.

  • antonym – unwrap: to remove a wrapping from something
