to toy with

verb
consider an idea without commitment
Pronunciation: / tɔɪ wɪð / (AmE), / tɔɪ wɪð / (BrE)
to consider or entertain (an idea or possibility) without making a firm decision

Examples

to toy with an idea
He was toying with several options before making his final decision.
I've been toying with the idea of starting my own business.
Sarah toyed with the possibility of moving abroad but never took action.

Related Terms

  • synonym – contemplate: to think about deeply or consider thoughtfully
  • synonym – entertain: to consider or allow yourself to think about an idea or possibility
