to average out

verb
calculate or become the average of several amounts
Pronunciation: / ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ aʊt / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ aʊt / (BrE)
to calculate or reach an average value after considering all amounts, especially over time or across cases
