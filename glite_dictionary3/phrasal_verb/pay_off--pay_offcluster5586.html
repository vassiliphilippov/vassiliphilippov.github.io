to pay off

verb
give money or reward to someone in return
Pronunciation: / peɪ ɔf / (AmE), / peɪ ɒf / (BrE)
to give someone money or another reward in return for work, help, or as compensation for something

Examples

to pay off workers

The manager agreed to pay off the staff for their overtime hours.
