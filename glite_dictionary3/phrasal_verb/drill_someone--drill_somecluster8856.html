to drill (someone)

verb
make someone practice intensively
Pronunciation: / drɪl ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / drɪl ˈsʌmwən / (BrE)
to make someone practice intensively and repeatedly in order to improve a skill

Examples

The language tutor regularly drilled us on our pronunciation skills.
