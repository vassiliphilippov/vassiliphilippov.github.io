to hop up

verb
jump or rise quickly
Pronunciation: / hɑp ʌp / (AmE), / hɒp ʌp / (BrE)
to jump or rise quickly, often with a light, sudden movement

Examples

to hop up from a chair
He hopped up from his chair as soon as he saw her enter the room.
If I hop up on this box, I can reach the top shelf.
