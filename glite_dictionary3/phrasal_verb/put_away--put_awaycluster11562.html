to put away

verb
to humanely euthanize an animal
Pronunciation: / pʊt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / pʊt əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to end the life of an animal humanely, especially by giving it an injection, in order to prevent further suffering

Examples

to put away a sick pet
After the diagnosis, the veterinarian recommended that we put away our elderly dog to end his suffering.
It was a hard decision, but they finally chose to put away the horse after its injury.
The shelter has a policy of only putting away animals when there are no other options left.
