to get down

verb
move down from a higher place
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt daʊn / (AmE), / ɡɛt daʊn / (BrE)
to move something or someone from a higher to a lower position

Examples

to get down a box
Could you get down that box from the top shelf for me?
He carefully got down the old painting and placed it on the table.
She asked her brother to get down the book she couldn't reach.
