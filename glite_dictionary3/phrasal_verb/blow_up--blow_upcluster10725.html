to blow up

verb
enlarge something, esp. an image
Pronunciation: / bloʊ ʌp / (AmE), / bləʊ ʌp / (BrE)
to make something larger, especially by enlarging a photograph or image.

Examples

to blow up a photo
Can you blow up this photo so everyone can see it?
She blows up small pictures before adding them to her reports.
The designer blew up the logo for use on the billboard.

  • synonym – enlarge: to make something bigger
  • antonym – reduce: to make something smaller
