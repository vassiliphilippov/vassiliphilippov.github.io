to spread over

verb
to cover by extending over
Pronunciation: / sprɛd ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / sprɛd ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to extend or form a layer or cover over something

Examples

to spread over a surface
A thick fog began to spread over the entire valley as night fell.
I watched icing spread over the warm cake, creating a glossy surface.
Lush green vines spread over the old stone wall in midsummer.
