to fill in

verb
to depict shade or shadow in art
Pronunciation: / fɪl ɪn / (AmE), / fɪl ɪn / (BrE)
to represent the effect of shade or shadow on (a drawing or painting) by adding lines, colors, or shading

Examples

to fill in a drawing
She filled in the sketch with soft shading to represent the evening light.
The artist carefully filled in the shadows to make the portrait look realistic.
You should fill in the background areas to create a sense of depth.

  • synonym – shade: to add shaded areas to a drawing
