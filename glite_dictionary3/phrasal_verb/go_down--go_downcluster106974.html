to go down

verb
(of the sun) set below the horizon
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ daʊn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ daʊn / (BrE)
(of the sun or another celestial body) to disappear below the horizon as it sets.

Examples

the sun goes down
It's always cooler once the sun has gone down.
The sky turned orange as the moon began to go down.
We watched the sun go down behind the mountains.
