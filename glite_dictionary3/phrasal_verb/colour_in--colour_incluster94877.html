to colour in

verb
add colour by filling (esp. in drawings)
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər ɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ɪn / (BrE)
to add colour to (a drawing, picture, or area) by filling it with crayons, paint, or similar materials.

Examples

to colour in a picture
He carefully coloured in the spaces between the lines to complete his drawing.
Many adults find colouring in to be a relaxing and enjoyable activity.
She asked the children to colour in the pictures in their books.

Related Terms

  • synonym – color in: add color to (American spelling)
  • synonym – colorize: to add color to black-and-white images or films
