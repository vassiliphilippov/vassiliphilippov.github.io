to rise up

verb
come to the surface (phrasal verb)
Pronunciation: / raɪz ʌp / (AmE), / raɪz ʌp / (BrE)
to move upwards and come to the surface of a liquid or other medium

Examples

rise up to the surface
After a few minutes, the sediment began to rise up from the bottom of the tank.
Bubbles rise up to the surface of the boiling water.
Oil droplets will rise up through the water and form a layer at the top.
