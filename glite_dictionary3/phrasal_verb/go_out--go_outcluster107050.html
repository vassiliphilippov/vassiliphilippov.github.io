to go out

verb
leave home to go somewhere
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈaʊt /; / ɡoʊ aʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈaʊt /; / ɡəʊ aʊt / (BrE)
to leave your home, a building, or a place, especially to do something social, for leisure, or as part of an activity or mission
