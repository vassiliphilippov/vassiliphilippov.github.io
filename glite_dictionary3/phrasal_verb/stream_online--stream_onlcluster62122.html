to stream online

verb
broadcast content live via the internet
Pronunciation: / strim ɑnˈlaɪn / (AmE), / striːm ɒnˈlaɪn / (BrE)
To broadcast audio, video, or other content live over the internet in real time.

Examples

to stream online a game
He started to stream online every weekend, attracting a large following.
Many artists stream online to reach their fans directly from home.
The conference will stream online for those who can't attend in person.
