to kit up

verb
provide with necessary equipment or gear
Pronunciation: / kɪt ʌp / (AmE), / kɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to supply or equip someone or oneself with a set of necessary articles, equipment, or tools

Examples

to kit up with gear
Make sure you kit up with all the safety gear before entering the workshop.
She kitted up the children with new raincoats and boots for the trip.
The climbers kitted up before starting their ascent.

  • synonym – outfit: to provide with necessary clothes or equipment
