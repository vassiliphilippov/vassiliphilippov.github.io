to put out

verb
administer anesthesia; render unconscious
Pronunciation: / pʊt aʊt / (AmE), / pʊt aʊt / (BrE)
to administer an anesthetic drug to someone so that they become unconscious, especially for surgery or a medical procedure

Examples

to put out a patient
Don't worry, the doctor will put out your child so she won't feel anything during the surgery.
The nurse will put out the patient before the procedure begins.
They had to put out Maria for her wisdom teeth removal.
