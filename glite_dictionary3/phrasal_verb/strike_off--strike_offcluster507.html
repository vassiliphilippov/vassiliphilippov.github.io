to strike off

verb
remove from a list or register
Pronunciation: / straɪk ɔf / (AmE), / straɪk ɒf / (BrE)
to remove (someone or something) from an official list or register

Examples

strike off the register
After several missed payments, his name was struck off the register.
Doctors can be struck off for serious professional misconduct.
The organization decided to strike off the company from its membership list.
