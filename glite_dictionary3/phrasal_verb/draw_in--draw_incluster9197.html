to draw in

verb
pull inward as by suction
Pronunciation: / drɔː ɪn / (AmE), / drɔː ɪn / (BrE)
to pull or cause to move inward as by suction or by taking in air

Examples

to draw in a breath
He drew in the fragrance of the flowers and smiled contentedly.
The vacuum cleaner draws in dust and dirt from the carpet.
When it's cold, you can draw in your breath and feel the icy air fill your lungs.
