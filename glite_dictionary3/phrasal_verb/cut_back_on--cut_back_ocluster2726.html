to cut back on

verb
reduce amount or use of something
Pronunciation: / kʌt bæk ɑn / (AmE), / kʌt bæk ɒn / (BrE)
to reduce or decrease the amount or use of something, especially to save money or improve health

Examples

The company had to cut back on spending due to declining profits.
