to hunch over

verb
to bend forward with rounded shoulders
Pronunciation: / hʌntʃ ˈoʊvɚ /; / hʌntʃ ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / hʌntʃ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to bend forward with your shoulders and upper back rounded, usually because of posture, tiredness, or concentration
