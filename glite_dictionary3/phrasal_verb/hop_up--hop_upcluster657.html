to hop up

verb
make (a machine) more powerful
Pronunciation: / hɑp ʌp / (AmE), / hɒp ʌp / (BrE)
to increase the power, speed, or effectiveness of something, especially a motor vehicle engine, by making modifications or improvements

Examples

to hop up a car engine
Enthusiasts often hop up their cars to win street races.
The mechanic managed to hop up the old engine for better performance.
They hopped up the system with a new turbocharger and better fuel injectors.
