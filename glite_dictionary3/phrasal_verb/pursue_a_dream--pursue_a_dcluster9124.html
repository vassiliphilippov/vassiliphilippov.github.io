to pursue a dream

verb
try to achieve an ambition
Pronunciation: / pərˈsuː ə driːm / (AmE), / pəˈsjuː ə driːm / (BrE)
to actively try to achieve or fulfill one's ambitions or aspirations

Examples

to pursue a dream career
After graduating from college, she moved to New York to pursue a dream of becoming a writer.
He left his corporate job to pursue a dream of opening his own restaurant.
Many people give up before they really pursue a dream that matters to them most.
