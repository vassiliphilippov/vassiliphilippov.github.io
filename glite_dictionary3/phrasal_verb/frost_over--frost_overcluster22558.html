to frost over

verb
become coated with frost
Pronunciation: / frɑst ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / frɒst ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a surface) to become covered with a thin layer of ice or frost.

Examples

frost over a window
If you don't keep the heat on, the windows will frost over during the night.
In freezing temperatures, car mirrors often frost over and need to be cleared before driving.
The windshield frosted over so quickly that I couldn't see the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ice up: (of a surface) become covered with ice
  • antonym – thaw: (of ice or frost) become liquid or melt
