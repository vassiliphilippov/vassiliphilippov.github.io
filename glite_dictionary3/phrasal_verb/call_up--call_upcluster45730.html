to call up

verb
bring forward for consideration
Pronunciation: / kɔl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
to bring something forward for discussion or consideration, especially in a formal setting such as a meeting or court

Examples

to call up an issue
At the meeting, she called up an old proposal for discussion.
The committee may call up any issue that requires urgent attention.
The judge decided to call up the case for immediate review.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bring up: to mention or introduce for discussion
