to make it to

verb
arrive at or attend an event
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪk ɪt tu / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk ɪt tuː / (BrE)
to succeed in arriving at or attending an event or place, often despite difficulties

Examples

to make it to the meeting
Despite the heavy traffic, she managed to make it to the interview on time.
I'm glad you could make it to my birthday party last night.
Unfortunately, I won't be able to make it to the meeting tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attend: to be present at an event
  • antonym – miss: to fail to attend or be present at something
