to put out

verb
publish or issue for public distribution
Pronunciation: / pʊt aʊt / (AmE), / pʊt aʊt / (BrE)
to prepare and issue (a book, magazine, announcement, etc.) for public distribution or sale

Examples

to put out a newspaper
Local journalists put out a daily newspaper for the community.
The author decided to put out her latest novel online for free.
The company will put out a new magazine next month.
