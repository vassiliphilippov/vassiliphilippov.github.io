to monkey around

verb
act aimlessly or idly
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to behave in a silly or aimless way, or to spend time doing unplanned activities with little purpose

Examples

to monkey around at work
Stop monkeying around and get to work on your project!
The kids were monkeying around in the backyard instead of doing their homework.
They spent the afternoon monkeying around with the new video game console.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fool around: to behave in a playful or silly way, often instead of doing something serious
  • synonym – mess about: to spend time doing things that are not important or serious
