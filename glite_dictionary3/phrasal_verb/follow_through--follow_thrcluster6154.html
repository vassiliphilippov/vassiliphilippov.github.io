to follow through

verb
complete an action to the end
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːloʊ θruː /; / ˈfɑː.loʊ ˌθruː / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ θruː /; / ˈfɒl.əʊ ˌθruː / (BrE)
to continue and complete an action, plan, or commitment; to finish what you start
