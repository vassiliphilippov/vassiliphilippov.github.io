to spin out

verb
prolong or extend (time, activity)
Pronunciation: / spɪn aʊt / (AmE), / spɪn aʊt / (BrE)

to spin out a story

The author spins out the story with unnecessary details.
The speaker tried to spin out his presentation to fill more time.
