to mope around

verb
move listlessly, often when unhappy
Pronunciation: / moʊp əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / məʊp əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to move around slowly and aimlessly, often because you are unhappy or bored

Examples

mope around the house
After getting the bad news, he just moped around the house all day.
If you keep moping around like this, you'll never start to feel better.
She didn't do much over the weekend except mope around and watch television.

Related Terms

  • antonym – liven up: to become more lively or energetic
  • synonym – mope: to move or act in a depressed way
