to hit pause

verb
temporarily stop or take a break
Pronunciation: / hɪt pɔz / (AmE), / hɪt pɔːz / (BrE)
to temporarily stop or take a break from an activity, often in order to consider, rest, or reassess before continuing

Examples

to hit pause on work
Before we continue, let's hit pause and review the agenda one more time.
She decided to hit pause on her studies after a stressful semester.
Sometimes you need to hit pause on your work to prevent burnout.
