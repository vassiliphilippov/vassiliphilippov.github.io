to build up

verb
develop (land or area); construct infrastructure
Pronunciation: / bɪld ʌp / (AmE), / bɪld ʌp / (BrE)
to develop land or an area by making it available or usable, especially by constructing buildings or infrastructure

Examples

to build up an area
Many coastal areas have rapidly built up over the last two decades.
Strict regulations help prevent developers from building up protected natural habitats.
The company plans to build up this vacant land into a shopping complex.

Related Terms

  • synonym – develop: to grow or cause to grow and become more mature, advanced, or elaborate
