to talk down to

verb
speak condescendingly to someone
Pronunciation: / tɔk daʊn tu / (AmE), / tɔːk daʊn tuː / (BrE)
to speak to someone in a way that shows you think they are less intelligent or important than you

Examples

talk down to a child
Instead of talking down to students, he encourages open discussion and questions.
Please don't talk down to me; I understand the situation perfectly well.
She hates it when her boss talks down to her during meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – belittle: to make someone or something seem unimportant
  • synonym – patronize: to treat someone with apparent kindness that betrays a feeling of superiority
