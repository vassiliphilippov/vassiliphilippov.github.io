to wake up

verb
stop sleeping; become awake
Pronunciation: / ˌweɪk ˈʌp /; / weɪk ʌp / (AmE), / ˌweɪk ˈʌp /; / weɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to stop sleeping and become awake, or to make someone else stop sleeping or become alert

Examples

to wake up early

The loud thunder woke up everyone in the house last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – awaken: to stop sleeping; to become aware or conscious
  • antonym – fall asleep: to begin to sleep
  • antonym – put to sleep: to cause someone to fall asleep
  • synonym – rouse: to wake someone from sleep or bring them to consciousness
