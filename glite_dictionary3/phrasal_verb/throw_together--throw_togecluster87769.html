to throw together

verb
make hastily or carelessly
Pronunciation: / θɹoʊ təˈɡɛðɚ /; / ˈθroʊ təˌɡɛðər / (AmE), / θrəʊ təˈɡɛðə /; / ˈθrəʊ təˌɡɛðə / (BrE)
to make or arrange something quickly and without much care or planning
