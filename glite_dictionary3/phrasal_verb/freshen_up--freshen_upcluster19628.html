to freshen up

verb
to wash or make oneself look refreshed
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛʃən ʌp / (AmE), / ˈfrɛʃən ʌp / (BrE)
to wash, tidy, or clean oneself to feel or appear more refreshed or presentable

Examples

to freshen up after travel

I like to freshen up before going out to dinner.
