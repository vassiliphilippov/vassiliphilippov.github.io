to go for

verb
be applicable or relevant
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ fər / (AmE), / ɡəʊ fə / (BrE)
to be applicable or relevant to someone or something

Examples

go for both groups
The regulations go for both employees and visitors.
The same principle goes for everyone involved in the project.
What goes for adults doesn't always go for children.
