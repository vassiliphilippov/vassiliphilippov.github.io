to conjure up

verb
bring into existence or to mind as if by magic
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑndʒər ʌp / (AmE), / ˈkʌndʒə ʌp / (BrE)
to bring something into existence or to mind, often as if by magic or with imagination

Examples

conjure up memories
Reading that poem always conjures up a sense of nostalgia in me.
The magician seemed to conjure up a rabbit out of thin air.
The smell of fresh bread can conjure up memories of childhood for many people.
