to go sour

verb
to deteriorate or decline
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈsaʊɚ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈsaʊə / (BrE)
to become unsuccessful, problematic, or to deteriorate or decline in condition or quality

Examples

go sour after a fight
Her mood tends to go sour whenever she receives bad news.
Negotiations may go sour if both sides refuse to compromise.
Their business partnership started to go sour after several disagreements.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deteriorate: to become progressively worse
  • antonym – improve: to become better or make something better
