to bang up

verb
damage or destroy violently
Pronunciation: / bæŋ ʌp / (AmE), / bæŋ ʌp / (BrE)

Examples

to bang up a car
Don't bang up your laptop by dropping it on the floor.
He really banged up his bicycle during the trick competition.
The car was badly banged up after the accident.
