to take to be

verb
consider or regard as
Pronunciation: / teɪk tə bi / (AmE), / teɪk tə bi / (BrE)
to regard or consider someone or something as being a particular thing; to assume or suppose to be.

Examples

take to be honest
I took her to be your sister when I first met her.
Many people take to be harmless what is actually quite dangerous.
She took him to be a doctor because of his white coat.
