to rally to support

verb
join together to provide help
Pronunciation: / ˈræli tə səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈræli tə səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
to come together as a group in order to provide help or assistance to someone or a cause

Examples

to rally to support victims
After the devastating storm, neighbors rallied to support those who had lost their homes.
Many volunteers rallied to support the fundraising event for cancer research.
The students rallied to support their classmate during his illness.
