to take home

verb
earn as salary or wage (after deductions)
Pronunciation: / teɪk hoʊm / (AmE), / teɪk həʊm / (BrE)
to earn a particular amount of money as a net salary or wage, especially after tax and deductions

Examples

to take home a salary
After taxes, she usually takes home around $2,500 a month.
He was offered a high salary, but he'll only take home about half after tax.
Most employees are surprised by how little they actually take home once all deductions are made.
