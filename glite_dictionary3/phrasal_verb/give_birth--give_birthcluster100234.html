to give birth

verb
to produce offspring
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv bɜrθ /; / ɡɪv ˈbɜrθ / (AmE), / ɡɪv bɜːθ /; / ɡɪv ˈbɜːθ / (BrE)
to produce a baby or young animal from the body; to deliver a child
