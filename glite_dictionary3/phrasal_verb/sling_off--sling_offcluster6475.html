to sling off

verb
remove or discard quickly, carelessly
Pronunciation: / slɪŋ ɔf / (AmE), / slɪŋ ɒf / (BrE)
to discard or remove something quickly, carelessly, or with little regard

Examples

sling off a jacket
After a long day, he would sling off his coat as soon as he got home.
She slung off her doubts and decided to take the risk.
The children sling off their backpacks the moment they step inside.
