to cover up

verb
hide or conceal the truth or something from view
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvər ʌp /; / ˈkʌvɚ ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə ʌp /; / ˈkʌvə ˌʌp / (BrE)
