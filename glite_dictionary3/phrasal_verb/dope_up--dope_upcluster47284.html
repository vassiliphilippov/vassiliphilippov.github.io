to dope up

verb
add dopants to a material
Pronunciation: / do\u028ap \u028cp / (AmE), / d\u0259\u028ap \u028cp / (BrE)
(in materials science and semiconductor fabrication) to introduce impurities (dopants) into a material or semiconductor to change its electrical or physical properties.

Examples

to dope up silicon
Researchers dope up silicon wafers with boron to create p-type regions for transistors.
To adjust carrier concentration, the engineer doped up the sample using ion implantation equipment.
During fabrication, technicians monitor how quickly they can dope up each wafer without causing damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – doping: the intentional introduction of impurities into a semiconductor to change its electrical properties
  • synonym – ion implantation: a technique for embedding dopant ions into a substrate to alter its conductivity
