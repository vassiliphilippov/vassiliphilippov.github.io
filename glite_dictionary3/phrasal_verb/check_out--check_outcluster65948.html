to check out

verb
process payment for items in a store
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk aʊt / (AmE), / tʃɛk aʊt / (BrE)
to record, total, and receive payment for items purchased, especially at a store or supermarket

Examples

check out groceries
After waiting in line, Sarah was ready to check out her items.
He learned how to check out customers quickly on his first day at work.
The cashier will check out your groceries at the register.
