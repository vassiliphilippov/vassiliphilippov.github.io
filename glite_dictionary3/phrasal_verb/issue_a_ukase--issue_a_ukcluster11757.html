to issue a ukase

verb
give an absolute order or decree
Pronunciation: / ˈɪʃu ə juˈkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈɪsjuː ə juˈkeɪs / (BrE)
to give or announce an authoritative or absolute order or decree

Examples

to issue a ukase demanding silence
He is known to issue a ukase the moment he senses any disorder.
The principal issued a ukase banning phones on school grounds.
When the CEO decided to issue a ukase, all company policies changed overnight.
