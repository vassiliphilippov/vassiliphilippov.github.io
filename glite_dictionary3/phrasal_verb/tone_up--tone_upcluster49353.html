to tone up

verb
improve skin tone or complexion
Pronunciation: / toʊn ʌp / (AmE), / təʊn ʌp / (BrE)
(in cosmetics) to improve the appearance, evenness, or firmness of the skin or complexion, often by using skincare products or treatments.

Examples

to tone up skin
She uses a nightly serum to tone up her skin and reduce uneven pigmentation.
After two weeks of exfoliating and masks, his complexion began toning up noticeably.
The clinic promised clients they'd see their skin toned up and brighter in one month.
