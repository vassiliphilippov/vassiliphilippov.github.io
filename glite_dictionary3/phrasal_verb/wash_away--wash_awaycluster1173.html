to wash away

verb
set aside as irrelevant
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / wɒʃ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to disregard or eliminate something as unimportant or irrelevant

Examples

to wash away worries
The joy of the festival helped residents wash away their worries and disagreements.
They tried to wash away all the criticism by shifting the topic to their achievements.
Time can wash away old grievances, allowing people to start anew.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dismiss: to decide that something or someone is not important and not worth considering
  • synonym – ignore: to deliberately pay no attention to something
