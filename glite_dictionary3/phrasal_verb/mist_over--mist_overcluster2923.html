to mist over

verb
become covered with mist or condensation
Pronunciation: / mɪst ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / mɪst ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of eyes) to fill with tears, making it hard to see clearly, usually because of emotion

Examples

eyes mist over
Her eyes misted over with tears as she listened to the speech.
The car windows quickly misted over on the cold morning.
The mirror misted over after a long, hot shower.
Her eyes misted over when she thought of her childhood home.
I watched as my friend’s eyes misted over during the heartfelt reunion.
The old man’s gaze misted over as he recalled his late wife.
