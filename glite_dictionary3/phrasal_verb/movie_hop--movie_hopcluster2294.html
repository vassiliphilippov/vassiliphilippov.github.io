to movie hop

verb
attend multiple movies on one cinema visit
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːvi hɑp / (AmE), / ˈmuːvi hɒp / (BrE)
to attend more than one movie in a single visit to a cinema, often by moving from one screening to another without paying for each

Examples

to movie hop at the cinema
After our first movie, we decided to movie hop and see the next big release.
It's easy to movie hop in a large multiplex without getting caught.
Teenagers sometimes movie hop to see three films for the price of one.

  • synonym – theater hop: to attend multiple shows at a theater in one outing
