to meet up

verb
to come together socially
Pronunciation: / mit ʌp / (AmE), / miːt ʌp / (BrE)
to come together or gather with others, often informally, usually for a social purpose

Examples

meet up with friends
After months apart, the team will finally meet up for a reunion.
Text me when you get there and we can meet up outside the cinema.
We plan to meet up with our friends at the new restaurant tonight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gather: to come together in a group
  • antonym – separate: to move apart or stop coming together as a group
