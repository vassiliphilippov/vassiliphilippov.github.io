to muster up

verb
gather or summon with effort
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstər ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmʌstə ʌp / (BrE)
to gather or summon (energy, courage, support, etc.), especially with effort

Examples

to muster up courage
He finally mustered up enough confidence to ask for a promotion.
It wasn't easy for him to muster up a smile after hearing the bad news.
She had to muster up the courage to speak in front of the large audience.
