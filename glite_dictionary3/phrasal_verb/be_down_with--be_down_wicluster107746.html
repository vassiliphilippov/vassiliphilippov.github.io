to be down with

verb
informal: agree with or support
Pronunciation: / bi daʊn wɪð / (AmE), / bi daʊn wɪð / (BrE)
(informal) To agree with or be supportive of something; to be in favor of something.

Examples

be down with the plan
A lot of people are down with the idea of working from home.
If you're down with this plan, let me know before tomorrow.
Not everyone in the group was down with changing the schedule so suddenly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agree with: to have the same opinion as; to support
  • antonym – oppose: to disapprove of and actively resist
