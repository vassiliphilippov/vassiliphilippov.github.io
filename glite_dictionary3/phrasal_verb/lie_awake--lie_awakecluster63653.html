to lie awake

verb
remain in bed without sleeping
Pronunciation: / laɪ əˈweɪk / (AmE), / laɪ əˈweɪk / (BrE)
to remain in bed without sleeping, especially when unable to fall asleep

Examples

I sometimes lie awake at night thinking about my future.
She tried to relax but ended up lying awake until dawn.
