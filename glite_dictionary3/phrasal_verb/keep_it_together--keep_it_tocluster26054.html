to keep it together

verb
remain calm and in control
Pronunciation: / kip ɪt təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / kiːp ɪt təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to remain calm and maintain emotional control, especially in a difficult situation

Examples

to keep it together under stress
Even though the situation was overwhelming, she managed to keep it together and finish the presentation.
He tried to keep it together for the sake of his family during the crisis.
It's hard to keep it together when everything seems to be falling apart around you.
