to lie in

verb
be in bed during childbirth or recovery
Pronunciation: / laɪ ɪn / (AmE), / laɪ ɪn / (BrE)
(of a woman) to be in bed during the period around childbirth, especially immediately after giving birth.

Examples

lie in after childbirth
During her recovery, she was expected to lie in and rest as much as possible.
In former times, women would lie in for several weeks after giving birth.
The tradition of having relatives visit while a mother lies in still survives in some cultures.
