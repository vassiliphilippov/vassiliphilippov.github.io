to pry into

verb
inquire into private matters intrusively
Pronunciation: / praɪ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / praɪ ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to try to find out private or personal information about someone, especially in a way that is annoying or intrusive

Examples

to pry into private life
Neighbors sometimes pry into each other's business out of curiosity.
Please don't pry into my family matters; I value my privacy.
Reporters were accused of trying to pry into the actor's private affairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – snoop: to secretly try to find out information about someone's private affairs
