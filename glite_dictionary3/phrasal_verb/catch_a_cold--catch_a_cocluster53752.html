to catch a cold

verb
become ill with a cold
Pronunciation: / kætʃ ə koʊld / (AmE), / katʃ ə kəʊld / (BrE)
to become ill with a cold, a mild viral infection that affects the nose and throat

Examples

to catch a cold after rain
He was worried that swimming in the rain would make him catch a cold.
If you don't wear a hat in winter, you might catch a cold.
She stayed home all week after she caught a cold.
