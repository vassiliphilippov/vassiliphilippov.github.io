to work through

verb
deal with thoroughly; process step by step
Pronunciation: / wɝk θru / (AmE), / wɜːk θruː / (BrE)
to deal with or apply oneself to something in a thorough or systematic way, often step by step

Examples

to work through a problem
He carefully worked through each item on the checklist before submitting his report.
Let's work through the next problem together to understand the steps involved.
She needed time to work through her emotions after the accident.
