to warm over

verb
reheat previously cooked food
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / wɔːm ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to heat food again after it has already been cooked

Examples

to warm over leftovers
I'll just warm over yesterday's stew for lunch since we're short on time.
She prefers not to warm over leftovers and makes fresh meals every day.
The pizza was cold, so he decided to warm over a few slices in the oven.
