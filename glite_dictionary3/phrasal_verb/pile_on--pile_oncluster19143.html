pile on

noun
mass collective criticism
Pronunciation: / paɪl ɑn / (AmE), / paɪl ɒn / (BrE)
a large, often rapid, collective attack of criticism, insults, or negative comments directed at someone (frequently online).

Examples

pile on of criticism
The post sparked a pile on of online criticism from thousands of commenters.
After her tweet, the politician faced a pile on that dominated social media for days.
Friends warned him about the pile on he would get if he posted such harsh comments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – backlash: a strong public reaction of criticism or disapproval, often against a person or action
  • synonym – online mob: a large group of internet users who collectively attack or harass someone online
