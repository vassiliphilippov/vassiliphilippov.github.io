to save the document

verb
to store a digital document
Pronunciation: / seɪv ðə ˈdɑkjəmənt / (AmE), / seɪv ðə ˈdɒkjumənt / (BrE)
to store a document in a computer's memory or disk, so that its contents are preserved for future use
