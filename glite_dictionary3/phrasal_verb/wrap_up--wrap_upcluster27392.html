to wrap up

verb
roll into a cylinder or tube
Pronunciation: / ræp ʌp / (AmE), / rap ʌp / (BrE)
to roll something into a cylinder or tube by folding or turning it around itself

Examples

to wrap up a poster
After the ceremony, staff wrapped up the banner and stored it away.
He carefully wrapped up the poster so it would fit in his bag.
She wrapped up the yoga mat tightly before leaving the studio.
