to subscribe to

verb
arrange to receive regularly (e.g. a publication or service)
Pronunciation: / səbˈskraɪb tu / (AmE), / səbˈskraɪb tʊ / (BrE)
to arrange to receive or obtain something regularly, such as a newspaper, magazine, or online service, usually by paying for it

Examples

to subscribe to a magazine
I subscribe to several magazines to stay informed about new trends.
More people now subscribe to streaming services than ever before.
She decided to subscribe to the newspaper for home delivery every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – enroll: to register or sign up for something regularly
  • antonym – unsubscribe: to cancel a subscription to something
