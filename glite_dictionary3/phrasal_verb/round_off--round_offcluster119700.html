to round off

verb
make something curved or rounded at the edge
Pronunciation: / raʊnd ɔf / (AmE), / raʊnd ɒf / (BrE)
to shape or finish something so that it has a smooth, curved, or rounded edge or surface

Examples

to round off sharp edges
Before painting, make sure to round off any rough splinters on the wooden rail.
He used sandpaper to round off the sharp corners of the table.
The sculptor carefully rounded off the edges of the statue for a softer look.
