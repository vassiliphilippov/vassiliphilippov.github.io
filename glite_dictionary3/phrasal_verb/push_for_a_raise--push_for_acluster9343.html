to push for a raise

verb
advocate for a salary increase
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ fər ə reɪz / (AmE), / pʊʃ fə ə reɪz / (BrE)
to advocate or actively seek an increase in salary or wages

Examples

to push for a raise at work
After completing several major projects, she decided to push for a raise during her annual review.
He spent weeks preparing evidence of his performance before finally pushing for a raise with his boss.
Many employees are hesitant to push for a raise even when they feel it's deserved.
