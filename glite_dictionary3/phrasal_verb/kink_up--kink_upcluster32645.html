to kink up

verb
curl up tightly; form kinks
Pronunciation: / kɪŋk ʌp / (AmE), / kɪŋk ʌp / (BrE)
(of something, especially hair or thread) to curl up tightly or form into kinks.

Examples

to kink up hair
Humidity can cause my hair to kink up after I've straightened it.
If the cord kinks up, it becomes difficult to use the headphones.
Watch out for rope that might kink up if it's not properly coiled.
