to step out on someone

verb
be unfaithful to a partner
Pronunciation: / stɛp aʊt ɑn ˈsʌmwʌn / (AmE), / stɛp aʊt ɒn ˈsʌmwʌn / (BrE)
(informal) to be unfaithful to one's romantic partner; to have a sexual or romantic relationship with someone outside a committed partnership.

Examples

to step out on a spouse
If you step out on your partner, it can destroy the trust in your relationship.
Rumors started after he was seen stepping out on his girlfriend with another woman.
She never expected her husband would step out on her after so many years of marriage.
