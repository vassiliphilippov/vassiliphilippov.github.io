to cut off

verb
suddenly stop or discontinue
Pronunciation: / kʌt ɑf / (AmE), / kʌt ɒf / (BrE)
to stop, interrupt, or block something suddenly, such as communication, progress, or access

Examples

to cut off the power

Our phone call was suddenly cut off due to poor reception.

A fallen tree cut off access to the village during the storm.
