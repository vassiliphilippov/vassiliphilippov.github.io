to clear the table

verb
remove items from the table after a meal
Pronunciation: / klɪr ðə ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / klɪə ðə ˈteɪbl̩ / (BrE)
to remove dishes and other items from the table at the end of a meal

Examples

clear the table after dinner
After everyone finished eating, he quickly cleared the table and put away the plates.
Could you please clear the table while I start washing the dishes?
It's polite to clear the table before serving dessert.
