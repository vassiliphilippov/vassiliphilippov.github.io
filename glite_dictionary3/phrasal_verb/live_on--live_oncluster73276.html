to live on

verb
continue to live; survive
Pronunciation: / lɪv ɑn / (AmE), / lɪv ɒn / (BrE)
to continue to live or survive, especially after a dangerous or difficult situation

Examples

to live on after danger
After the accident, he was grateful just to live on and see another day.
Despite the harsh conditions, the explorers managed to live on for weeks.
Her memory will live on in the hearts of those who loved her.
