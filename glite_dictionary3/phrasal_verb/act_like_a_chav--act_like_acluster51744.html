to act like a chav

verb
behave in a stereotypically chav manner
Pronunciation: / ækt laɪk ə tʃæv / (AmE), / ækt laɪk ə tʃæv / (BrE)
to behave in a way that is stereotypically associated with working-class youth, often characterized by flashy or brash behavior and speech, especially in British society
