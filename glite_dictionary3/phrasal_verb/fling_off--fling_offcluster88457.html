to fling off

verb
write quickly and easily
Pronunciation: / flɪŋ ɑf / (AmE), / flɪŋ ɒf / (BrE)
to write (something) quickly and without much effort

Examples

to fling off a quick note
He flung off a hasty apology note before rushing out the door.
She managed to fling off a quick email before leaving the office.
The author would often fling off short stories in a single afternoon.
