to wash up

verb
wash one's hands and face
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ ˈʌp / (AmE), / wɒʃ ˈʌp / (BrE)
to wash your hands and face, especially to clean up before eating or after getting dirty

Examples

wash up before dinner
He quickly washed up after working in the garden all afternoon.
Please wash up before you come to dinner.
The children must wash up when they come in from playing outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym – freshen up: to wash oneself quickly to look or feel clean and tidy
  • synonym – wash: to clean something using water
