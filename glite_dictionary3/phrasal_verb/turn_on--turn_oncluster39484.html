to turn on

verb
activate a device or machine
Pronunciation: / tɝn ɑn / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒn / (BrE)
to cause a machine or device to begin operating by pressing a button or flipping a switch

Examples

turn on the lights
Please turn on the lights when you enter the room.
She forgot to turn on the oven before baking the cake.
You can turn on the radio by pressing that button.

Related Terms

  • synonym – switch on: to activate a device by pressing a switch
  • antonym – turn off: to stop a machine or device from operating
