to come up with

verb
to produce or invent (an idea or solution)
Pronunciation: / kʌm ʌp wɪð / (AmE), / kʌm ʌp wɪð / (BrE)
to produce, provide, or think of something such as an idea, answer, plan, or thing that is needed
