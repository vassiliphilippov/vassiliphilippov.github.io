to cross oneself

verb
make the sign of the cross
Pronunciation: / krɑs wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / krɒs wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to make the sign of the cross on the body as a religious act, especially in the Christian tradition

Examples

to cross oneself before prayer
Before entering the church, she crossed herself and whispered a quick prayer.
He instinctively crossed himself when he heard the bad news.
Some people cross themselves whenever they pass a cemetery.
