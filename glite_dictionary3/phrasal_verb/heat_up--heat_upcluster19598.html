to heat up

verb
become or make more intense
Pronunciation: / hit ʌp / (AmE), / hiːt ʌp / (BrE)
to become or make something become more intense, active, or exciting

Examples

heat up competition
Competition between the two companies really heated up last year.
Tensions began to heat up as the debate progressed.
Things are heating up in the race for the championship.
