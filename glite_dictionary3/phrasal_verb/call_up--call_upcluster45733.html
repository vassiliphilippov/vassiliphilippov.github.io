to call up

verb
make available or bring to mind
Pronunciation: / kɔl ʌp / (AmE), / kɔːl ʌp / (BrE)
to make something (a resource, memory, information, etc.) available for use, either literally or figuratively

Examples

to call up data
A simple search can call up thousands of results from the database.
He calls up the file by clicking on the icon.
The smell of fresh bread called up memories of her grandmother’s kitchen.
