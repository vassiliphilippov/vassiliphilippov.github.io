to revolve around

verb
move in a circular path around something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑlv əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒlv əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to move in a circular path around a central point or object

Examples

revolve around a planet
Several moons revolve around Jupiter, forming a complex system.
The Earth revolves around the Sun in about 365 days.
The planets in our solar system all revolve around the Sun.
