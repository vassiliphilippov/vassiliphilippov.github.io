to wash off

verb
remove by washing with water or soap
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ ɔf / (AmE), / wɒʃ ɒf / (BrE)
to remove (something) from a surface by washing with water, soap, or another cleaning agent

Examples

to wash off dirt
After hiking, I needed to wash off the mud from my boots.
The chef washed off the vegetables under running water.
You should try to wash off the paint before it dries on your hands.
