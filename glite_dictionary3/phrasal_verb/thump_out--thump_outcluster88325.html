to thump out

verb
produce a rhythm by striking repeatedly
Pronunciation: / θʌmp aʊt / (AmE), / θʌmp aʊt / (BrE)
to play or produce (a rhythm, beat, or tune) forcefully, typically by striking repeatedly

Examples

He thumped out a steady rhythm on the drums during the rehearsal.
She thumped out the melody on the old piano with surprising energy.
The band thumped out their latest hit, filling the room with sound.
