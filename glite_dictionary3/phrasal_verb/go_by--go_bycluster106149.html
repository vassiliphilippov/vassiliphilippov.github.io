to go by

verb
follow or act in accordance with
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ baɪ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ baɪ / (BrE)
to follow or act in accordance with something such as a rule, principle, or standard

Examples

go by the rules
The judge promised to go by the law and make a fair ruling.
We always go by what the guidelines say when making decisions.
You should go by your instincts if you're unsure what to do.
