to make do

verb
manage with limited resources
Pronunciation: / meɪk duː / (AmE), / meɪk dʊ /; / meɪk duː / (BrE)
to manage or cope with what you have, even if it is not enough or not ideal

After losing his job, he learned to make do with much less money than before.
