to turf out

verb
expel or remove someone
Pronunciation: / tɝf aʊt / (AmE), / tɜːf aʊt / (BrE)
to force someone to leave a place or organization; to expel or eject someone

Examples

turf out unwanted guests
Security turfed out the protesters from the building.
The manager threatened to turf out anyone who broke the rules.
They turfed out the tenants for not paying their rent.
