to stick on

verb
attach or fasten onto a surface
Pronunciation: / stɪk ɑn / (AmE), / stɪk ɒn / (BrE)
to attach or fasten something onto the surface of something else, usually so that it stays in place

Examples

to stick on a label
Please stick on a stamp before sending the letter.
She stuck on the label to make sure her bag was identified.
We can stick on these decals to decorate the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym – affix: to attach or fasten to something
  • antonym – remove: to take something off a surface
