to hike up

verb
increase sharply, especially cost or rate
Pronunciation: / haɪk ʌp / (AmE), / haɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to increase (a price, rate, or amount) sharply or suddenly

Examples

hike up prices
After the announcement, the company hiked up its subscription prices overnight.
If you hike up the interest rates, fewer people may apply for loans.
The city plans to hike up parking fees next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – raise: to increase the amount or level of something
  • antonym – reduce: to make something smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
