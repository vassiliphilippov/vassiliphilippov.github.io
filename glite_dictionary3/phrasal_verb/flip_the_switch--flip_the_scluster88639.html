to flip the switch

verb
activate by moving a switch; change suddenly
Pronunciation: / flɪp ðə swɪtʃ / (AmE), / flɪp ðə swɪtʃ / (BrE)
to quickly activate or turn something on by moving a switch, or to change a situation suddenly and decisively

Examples

to flip the switch on a machine
He flipped the switch and the machine started humming immediately.
Sometimes a coach needs to flip the switch to change the team's energy.
When the room grew dark, she quickly flipped the switch and the lights came on.

Related Terms

  • synonym – turn on: to cause something to start working by switching it on
