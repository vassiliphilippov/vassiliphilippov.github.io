to throw back

verb
move or tilt something quickly backward
Pronunciation: / θroʊ bæk / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊ bæk / (BrE)
to move or tilt suddenly backwards, usually with a quick or light motion, often used in relation to the head or a drink

Examples

throw back a drink
He threw back his drink in one quick motion.
She threw back her head and laughed heartily.
The actor threw back his shoulders to strike a dramatic pose.
