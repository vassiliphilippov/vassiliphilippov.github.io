to bother me

verb
disturb or interrupt someone
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑðər mi / (AmE), / ˈbɒðə mi / (BrE)
to disturb or interrupt someone, often causing annoyance or distraction

Examples

bother me with noise
It really bothers me when people interrupt my phone calls.
Please do not bother me while I am trying to concentrate on this report.
She promised not to bother me with any more questions during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – annoy me: to make me feel slightly angry or irritated
  • antonym – leave me alone: to refrain from disturbing or interrupting me
