to keep to oneself

verb
not share thoughts or secrets
Pronunciation: / kip tə wʌnˈsɛlf /; / kip tu wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / kiːp tə wʌnˈsɛlf /; / kiːp tʊ wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
