to piddle away

verb
waste time idly
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdəl əˈweɪ /; / ˈpɪd.əl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪdəl əˈweɪ /; / ˈpɪd.əl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to waste time or resources by being idle, inefficient, or unproductive, especially on trivial things
