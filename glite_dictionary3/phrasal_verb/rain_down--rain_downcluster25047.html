to rain down

verb
fall or be sent down in large amounts
Pronunciation: / reɪn daʊn / (AmE), / reɪn daʊn / (BrE)
to fall or be sent down in large amounts, like rain, often from above

Examples

rain down ash
Ash and debris rained down from the volcano for hours.
Criticism continued to rain down from all sides after the decision was announced.
The confetti began to rain down on the celebrating crowd.
