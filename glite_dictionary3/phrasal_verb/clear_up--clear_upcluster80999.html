to clear up

verb
weather: become clear
Pronunciation: / klɪr ʌp / (AmE), / klɪə ʌp / (BrE)
(of weather) to become clear after being cloudy or rainy.

Examples

the sky clears up
It was raining all morning, but it finally cleared up around noon.
The sky will clear up by the afternoon, so we can have our picnic outside.
They waited at the café until the weather started clearing up.
