to peg down

verb
fasten or secure with pegs
Pronunciation: / pɛɡ daʊn / (AmE), / pɛɡ daʊn / (BrE)
to fasten or secure something, especially a tent or covering, to the ground using pegs or pins

Examples

to peg down a tent
She made sure to peg down each corner of the tarpaulin to keep it from blowing away.
The workers quickly pegged down the plastic sheets to protect the tools from rain.
We need to peg down the tent before the wind picks up.
