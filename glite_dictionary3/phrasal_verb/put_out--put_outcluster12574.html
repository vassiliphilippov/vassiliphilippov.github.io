to put out

verb
extend or hold out (part of the body)
Pronunciation: / pʊt aʊt / (AmE), / pʊt aʊt / (BrE)
to extend or hold something, especially a part of the body, away from yourself

Examples

put out your hand
He put out his foot to stop the door from closing.
Please put out your arm when the nurse asks you to.
She put out her hand to greet the visitor.
