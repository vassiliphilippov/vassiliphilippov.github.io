to jive about

verb
talk casually or informally
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪv əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / dʒaɪv əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to talk or discuss something casually, often in a lively or informal way

Examples

to jive about music
They spent the whole afternoon jiving about music and movies.
We sat around the kitchen table and jived about our favorite childhood memories.
Whenever they get together, they love to jive about their travel adventures.
