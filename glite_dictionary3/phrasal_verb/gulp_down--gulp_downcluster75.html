to gulp down

verb
eat or drink quickly and greedily
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlp daʊn / (AmE), / ɡʌlp daʊn / (BrE)
To eat or drink something quickly and often greedily, usually without pausing.

Examples

to gulp down a drink
I had to gulp down my breakfast because I was running late for work.
She gulped down a glass of water after the long run.
They gulped down their sandwiches before the meeting started.

Related Terms

  • synonym – wolf down: to eat something very quickly
