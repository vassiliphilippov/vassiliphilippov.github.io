to meet with

verb
receive a reaction or response
Pronunciation: / mit wɪð / (AmE), / miːt wɪð / (BrE)
to receive or encounter a particular reaction or response, especially one that is negative or critical

Examples

meet with criticism
Her suggestion to change the workflow was met with skepticism by the team.
His speech was met with enthusiastic applause at the ceremony.
The new policy has met with widespread opposition from local residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym – encounter: come across or experience (usually something, such as a reaction or obstacle)
  • synonym – receive: to be given or presented with something (such as a reaction)
