to slim down

verb
become thinner; lose weight
Pronunciation: / slɪm daʊn / (AmE), / slɪm daʊn / (BrE)
to become thinner by losing weight; to reduce one's body weight.

Examples

to slim down after eating
After his doctor’s advice, he started jogging to slim down and improve his health.
I managed to slim down by cutting out sugary drinks from my diet.
Many people try to slim down before summer by eating healthier and exercising regularly.

Related Terms

  • antonym – gain weight: to increase one's body weight
  • synonym – lose weight: to become lighter in body weight
