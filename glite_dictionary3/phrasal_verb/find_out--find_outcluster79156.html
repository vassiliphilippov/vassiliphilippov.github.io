to find out

verb
discover or establish through investigation
Pronunciation: / faɪnd aʊt / (AmE), / faɪnd aʊt / (BrE)
to discover or learn information, especially something that was not known before

Examples

to find out the truth

Related Terms

  • antonym – ignore: to deliberately pay no attention or remain unaware
