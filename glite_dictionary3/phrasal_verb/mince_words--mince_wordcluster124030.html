to mince words

verb
speak indirectly or with restraint
Pronunciation: / mɪns wɝdz / (AmE), / mɪns wɜːdz / (BrE)
to speak in a restrained or indirect way, especially to avoid being blunt or offending

Examples

to mince words about problems
He never minces words when discussing important issues with his team.
If you want the honest truth, don't expect him to mince words.
Rather than mincing words, she said exactly what everyone was thinking.
