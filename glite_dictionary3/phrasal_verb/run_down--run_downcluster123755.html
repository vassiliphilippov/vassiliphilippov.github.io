to run down

verb
hit and injure/kill with a vehicle
Pronunciation: / rʌn daʊn / (AmE), / rʌn daʊn / (BrE)
to injure or kill someone or something by striking and passing over them with a vehicle

Examples

to run down a pedestrian
She almost ran down a dog that darted into the road unexpectedly.
The cyclist was run down by a speeding car last night.
The driver failed to see the pedestrian and ran him down at the crosswalk.

Related Terms

  • synonym – knock over: to hit and cause to fall to the ground, especially with a vehicle
