to pull in

verb
(of a train) arrive at a station
Pronunciation: / pʊl ɪn / (AmE), / pʊl ɪn / (BrE)
(of a train) to arrive at a station and stop there.

Examples

pull in at the station
Passengers got ready as the commuter train pulled in late this morning.
The express train will pull in to Platform 3 shortly.
We watched the steam engine pull in at the small country station.
