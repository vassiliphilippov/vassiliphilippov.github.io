to carry to term

verb
complete a pregnancy to normal childbirth
Pronunciation: / ˈkæri tu tɚm / (AmE), / ˈkæri tuː tɜːm / (BrE)
to continue a pregnancy until the baby is fully developed and ready to be born, usually at the normal time for delivery

Examples

to carry to term a pregnancy
Despite medical risks, she chose to carry to term and gave birth to a healthy baby.
Doctors advised her that it might be difficult to carry to term after her surgery.
Many women with high-risk pregnancies hope to carry to term without complications.
