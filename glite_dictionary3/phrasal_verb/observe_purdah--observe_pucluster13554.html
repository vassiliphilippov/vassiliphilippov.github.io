to observe purdah

verb
follow practice of female seclusion for modesty
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɜːrv ˈpɜːrdɑː / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːv ˈpɜːdɑː / (BrE)
to follow the practice of female seclusion from public view for reasons of modesty, especially in certain Muslim and Hindu societies

Examples

to observe purdah at home
Many families expect their daughters to observe purdah beginning in adolescence.
She chose not to observe purdah after moving to the city.
Women in the village still observe purdah as part of their cultural traditions.
