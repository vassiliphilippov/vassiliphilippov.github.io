to stick about

verb
remain present or stay available
Pronunciation: / stɪk əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / stɪk əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
to remain in a place or stay available, typically for a short period

Examples

to stick about for a while
I decided to stick about for a bit in case he showed up.
She tends to stick about after meetings to chat with colleagues.
There's no reason to stick about if your work is finished.
