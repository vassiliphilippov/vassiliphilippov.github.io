to run out

verb
stop being valid (of documents)
Pronunciation: / rʌn aʊt / (AmE), / rʌn aʊt / (BrE)
(of a document, agreement, etc.) to stop being valid after a particular period has passed.

Examples

passport runs out
Check when your subscription is due to run out to avoid any interruptions.
My driver’s license will run out next year, so I need to renew it soon.
Their work visa ran out at the end of June, forcing them to leave the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expire: to come to the end of the period of validity
