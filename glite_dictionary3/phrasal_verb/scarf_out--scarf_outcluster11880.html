to scarf out

verb
eat greedily or overeat
Pronunciation: / skɑrf aʊt / (AmE), / skɑːf aʊt / (BrE)
(informal) To eat a large amount of food quickly or greedily; to overeat.

Examples

to scarf out pizza
Don't scarf out before the movie, or you'll get a stomach ache.
Every Thanksgiving, my brother tends to scarf out on turkey and stuffing.
The kids scarfed out on pizza and soda at the party.
