to stand back

verb
move or stay away from something or someone
Pronunciation: / stænd bæk /; / stænd ˈbæk / (AmE), / stænd bæk /; / stænd ˈbak / (BrE)
to move or stay at a distance from something or someone, often for safety, observation, or to avoid involvement

Examples

If you can't help, it's best to stand back and let the experts handle it.
