to put on

verb
add mileage to a vehicle's odometer
Pronunciation: / pʊt ɑn / (AmE), / pʊt ɒn / (BrE)
to cause a vehicle to record additional miles or kilometers on its odometer by driving it

Examples

to put on miles
She noticed that her old truck had put on another thousand miles since the last oil change.
The car hasn't put on many kilometers this year.
We put on nearly 500 miles during our road trip.
