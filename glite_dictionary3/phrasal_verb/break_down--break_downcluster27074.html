to break down

verb
analyze or separate into components
Pronunciation: / breɪk daʊn / (AmE), / breɪk daʊn / (BrE)
to separate something into its parts in order to analyze or understand it more easily

Examples

break down the problem
Let me break down the results so everyone can understand them.
Scientists can break down complex molecules into simpler elements for study.
The report will break down each expense category in detail.
