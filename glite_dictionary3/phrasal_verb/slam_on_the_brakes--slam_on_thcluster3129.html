to slam on the brakes

verb
suddenly and forcefully brake a vehicle
Pronunciation: / slæm ɑn ðə breɪks / (AmE), / slæm ɒn ðə breɪks / (BrE)
to apply the brakes of a vehicle suddenly and forcefully in order to stop quickly

Examples

slam on the brakes in traffic
If you slam on the brakes during rain, your car may skid.
She slammed on the brakes just in time to avoid hitting another car.
The driver had to slam on the brakes when a dog ran into the road.
