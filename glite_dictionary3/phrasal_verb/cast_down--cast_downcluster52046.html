to cast down

verb
make someone feel sad or discouraged
Pronunciation: / kæst daʊn / (AmE), / kɑːst daʊn / (BrE)
to make someone feel sad, discouraged, or disheartened

Examples

cast down his spirits
Her harsh criticism cast down his spirits for days.
I don't mean to cast down your enthusiasm, but the project faces many challenges.
The news of his failure cast down his entire family.
