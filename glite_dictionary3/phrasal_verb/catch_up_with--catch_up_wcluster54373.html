to catch up with

verb
make up missed work or progress
Pronunciation: / kætʃ ʌp wɪð / (AmE), / kætʃ ʌp wɪð / (BrE)
to do work or tasks that one has missed, especially after an absence, in order to reach the same level as others

Examples

to catch up with work
After missing a week of classes, I had to catch up with all my assignments.
It's important to catch up with the latest updates before the meeting.
She stayed late to catch up with the work she missed while she was sick.

  • synonym – make up: to do or complete something that was missed
