to peg away

verb
to work persistently at something
Pronunciation: / pɛg əˈweɪ / (AmE), / pɛg əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to work steadily and persistently, especially at something difficult or tedious

Examples

to peg away at work
Even when the work seemed endless, she continued to peg away until it was finished.
The team pegged away at the project despite repeated setbacks.
Writers must peg away every day if they want to complete a novel.
