to lounge around

verb
be lazy or idle
Pronunciation: / laʊndʒ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / laʊndʒ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to spend time being lazy or idle, usually in a relaxed way without doing anything productive

Examples

to lounge around at home
On Sundays, I like to lounge around and read magazines.
Stop lounging around and help me clean the house.
They spent the afternoon lounging around by the pool.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bum around: be lazy or idle, often moving from place to place
  • antonym – work: be engaged in productive activity
