to slap together

verb
assemble hastily and carelessly
Pronunciation: / slæp təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / slæp təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to make or assemble something quickly and carelessly, without attention to detail

Examples

to slap together a meal

They managed to slap together a basic website the night before the launch.
We can slap together a quick report if you need it by this afternoon.
