to let on

verb
reveal secret information
Pronunciation: / lɛt ɑn / (AmE), / lɛt ɒn / (BrE)
to reveal or disclose information that was meant to be kept secret

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym – conceal: to keep something secret; to prevent from being known
  • synonym – disclose: to make information known; especially something previously secret
  • synonym – divulge: to make known (private or sensitive information)
  • antonym – hide: to keep something secret or unknown
  • synonym – reveal: to make something known that was secret or hidden
