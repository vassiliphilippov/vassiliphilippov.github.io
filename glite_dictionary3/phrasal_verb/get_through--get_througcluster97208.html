to get through

verb
establish communication with
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ˈθruː / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈθruː / (BrE)
to succeed in establishing contact or communication with someone, especially by phone or other means

Examples

to get through to her on the phone

It took several attempts before he managed to get through to the customer service representative.
She finally got through to her parents after the storm knocked out the phone lines.
