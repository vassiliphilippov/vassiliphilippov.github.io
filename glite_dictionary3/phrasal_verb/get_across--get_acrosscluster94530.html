to get across

verb
become understood or clear
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt əˈkrɑs /; / ɡɛt əˈkrɔs / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈkrɒs / (BrE)
to communicate an idea or message successfully so that it is understood
