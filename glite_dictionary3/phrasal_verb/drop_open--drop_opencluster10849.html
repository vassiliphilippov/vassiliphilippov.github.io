to drop open

verb
fall open involuntarily (mouth or jaw)
Pronunciation: / drɑp ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / drɒp ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
(of a part of the body, especially the mouth or jaw) to fall open suddenly and involuntarily, typically as a result of surprise or shock.

Examples

drop open in surprise
Her jaw dropped open when she heard the shocking news.
My mouth dropped open as I read the final paragraph of the novel.
The child's mouth dropped open in amazement at the magician's trick.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gape: to open the mouth wide in surprise or wonder
