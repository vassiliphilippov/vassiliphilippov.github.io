to give notice

verb
formally inform someone
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv ˈnoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ɡɪv ˈnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
to formally inform someone of something, especially in a professional or official context

Examples

to give notice before leaving
Employees must give notice if they intend to leave the company.
She decided to give notice to her landlord before moving out.
You are required to give notice of any changes in your address.
