to muscle up

verb
gather strength or courage
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsəl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmʌsl ʌp / (BrE)
to gather strength, determination, or courage in preparation for a challenge

Examples

to muscle up for a challenge
Before the interview, she told herself to muscle up and remain confident.
It's time to muscle up and face your fears head-on.
They need to muscle up if they want to overcome these obstacles.
