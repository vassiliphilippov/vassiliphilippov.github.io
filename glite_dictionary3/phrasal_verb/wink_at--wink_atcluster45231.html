to wink at

verb
signal or flirt with a wink
Pronunciation: / wɪŋk æt / (AmE), / wɪŋk æt / (BrE)
to signal discreetly to someone or show playful interest by briefly closing one eye

Examples

to wink at a friend
During the conversation, he would often wink at her to make her laugh.
