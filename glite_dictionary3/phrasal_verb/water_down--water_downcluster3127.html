to water down

verb
make less strong or intense
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚ daʊn /; / ˈwɔtɚ daʊn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə daʊn / (BrE)
to make something less strong, intense, or effective, often by adding water or diluting it (can be used literally or figuratively)
