to shy away from

verb
avoid dealing with something unpleasant
Pronunciation: / ʃaɪ əˈweɪ frəm / (AmE), / ʃaɪ əˈweɪ frɒm / (BrE)
to avoid dealing with something difficult or unpleasant, usually because of fear or hesitation.

Examples

to shy away from hard work
He shied away from making any promises he couldn't keep.
Many people shy away from public speaking because it makes them nervous.
She tends to shy away from controversial topics during meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – avoid: to stay away from something
  • antonym – face up to: to confront or deal with something unpleasant
