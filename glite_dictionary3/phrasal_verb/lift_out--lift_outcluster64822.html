to lift out

verb
remove by picking up and taking out
Pronunciation: / lɪft aʊt / (AmE), / lɪft aʊt / (BrE)
to remove something by picking it up and taking it out, often carefully or with a tool such as a scoop.

Examples

to lift out a battery
Once the engine cooled, the mechanic lifted out the old battery and replaced it.
Please lift out the cake from the pan carefully to avoid breaking it.
The archaeologists lifted out the ancient vase using special tools.
