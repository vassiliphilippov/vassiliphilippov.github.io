to pan out

verb
wash dirt to separate gold/minerals
Pronunciation: / pæn aʊt / (AmE), / pæn aʊt / (BrE)
to wash earth or gravel in a pan to separate out gold or other precious minerals

Examples

to pan out gold
She spent hours panning out silt from the creek but found only a few flecks of gold.
The old prospector hoped that this riverbed would pan out enough gold to make him rich.
They learned how to pan out minerals using simple equipment at the mining museum.
