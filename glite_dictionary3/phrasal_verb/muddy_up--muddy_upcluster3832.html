to muddy up

verb
make dirty with mud
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdi ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmʌdi ʌp / (BrE)
to make something dirty by covering it or mixing it with mud

Examples

Don't muddy up your shoes by walking through that puddle.
