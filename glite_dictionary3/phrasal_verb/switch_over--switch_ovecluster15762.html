to switch over

verb
change from one thing to another
Pronunciation: / swɪtʃ ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / swɪtʃ ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to change from one system, method, or activity to another

Examples

to switch over to another channel
At midnight, the television channel will switch over to a new program.
It's easy to switch over from one account to another using the new software.
We decided to switch over to the backup generator during the power outage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – change over: to make a transition to a different system or method
  • antonym – switch back: to return to a previous system or state
