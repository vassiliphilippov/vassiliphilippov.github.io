to look bright

verb
appear promising or optimistic
Pronunciation: / lʊk braɪt / (AmE), / lʊk braɪt / (BrE)

Examples

As the sun rose over the city, everything suddenly looked bright and hopeful again.
Despite the recent setbacks, the company's future looks bright thanks to new investments.
