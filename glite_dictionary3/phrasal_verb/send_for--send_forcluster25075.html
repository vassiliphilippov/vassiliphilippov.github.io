to send for

verb
to request someone to come
Pronunciation: / sɛnd fɔr / (AmE), / sɛnd fə / (BrE)
to request or order someone or something to come to you, usually because they are needed

Examples

to send for a doctor
If you feel unwell, I will send for the doctor immediately.
She decided to send for her lawyer before answering any questions.
They had to send for help when their car broke down in the countryside.
She decided to send for her old diaries from storage.
The doctor told me to send for him if her condition got worse.
When the power failed, they had to send for an electrician immediately.

  • synonym – summon: to order someone to be present
