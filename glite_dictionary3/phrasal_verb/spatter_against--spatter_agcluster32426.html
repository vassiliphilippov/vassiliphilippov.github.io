to spatter against

verb
splash in small drops onto a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈspæt̬ɚ əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / ˈspætə əˈɡɛnst / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to splash or scatter in small drops onto a surface, often making spots or noise.

Examples

rain spatters against glass
Leaves and raindrops spattered against the kitchen window during the storm.
Paint accidentally spattered against the wall while he was cleaning his brush.
The muddy water began to spatter against the car door as they drove through the puddle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – splash against: to hit a surface and scatter liquid, making a sound
