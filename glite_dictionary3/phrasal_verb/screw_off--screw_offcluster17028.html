to screw off

verb
forcefully tell someone to go away; take time off
Pronunciation: / skru ɑf / (AmE), / skruː ɒf / (BrE)
(informal) Used to tell someone forcefully to go away or leave you alone; sometimes, to relax or take time off from work.
