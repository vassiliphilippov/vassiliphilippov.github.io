to zoom in

verb
focus closely or examine in detail
Pronunciation: / zum ɪn / (AmE), / zuːm ɪn / (BrE)
to examine something closely or focus attention on a particular part or detail

Examples

to zoom in on details
During the meeting, he zoomed in on the key issues that needed attention.
She zoomed in on the photograph to study the tiny details.
The documentary zooms in on the daily lives of the villagers.
