to come near

verb
almost do or experience something
Pronunciation: / kʌm nɪr / (AmE), / kʌm nɪə / (BrE)
to almost do or experience something; to be close to doing something without actually doing it

Examples

come near giving up
I came near to quitting my job last year when things got tough.
She came near to tears during the emotional speech.
The ball came near to hitting the window but missed by inches.
