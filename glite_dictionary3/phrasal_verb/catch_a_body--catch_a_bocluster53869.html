to catch a body

verb
find a dead body in a crime context
Pronunciation: / kætʃ ə ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / kæʧ ə ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
to find a dead person, especially in the context of a murder investigation; sometimes used as slang for being involved in or committing a killing

Examples

In many crime novels, the story begins when police catch a body under mysterious circumstances.
