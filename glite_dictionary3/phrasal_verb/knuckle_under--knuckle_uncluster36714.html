to knuckle under

verb
submit or yield reluctantly
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəl ˈʌndər / (AmE), / ˈnʌkəl ˈʌndə / (BrE)
to yield or submit to pressure or authority, especially unwillingly

Examples

to knuckle under pressure
After hours of arguing, she finally knuckled under and agreed to their conditions.
He always expects everyone to knuckle under to his authority, but I won't.
The workers refused to knuckle under to management's demands during the negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – give in: to stop resisting; to yield
  • antonym – stand firm: to refuse to yield or give way
