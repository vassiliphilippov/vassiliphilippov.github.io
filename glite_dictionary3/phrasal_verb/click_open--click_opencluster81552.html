to click open

verb
open with a clicking sound
Pronunciation: / klɪk ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / klɪk ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
to open something so that it makes a clicking sound

Examples

to click open a box
She clicked open the jewelry box and examined its contents.
The detective clicked open his briefcase to retrieve some papers.
With a quick twist, the key clicked open the old wooden chest.
