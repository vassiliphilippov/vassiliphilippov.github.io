to plank down

verb
drop or put down heavily
Pronunciation: / plæŋk daʊn / (AmE), / plæŋk daʊn / (BrE)
to put or drop something down heavily or carelessly, often with a loud sound

Examples

to plank down a box
He planked down the box on the table with a loud thud.
Tired after the journey, she planked down her suitcase in the hallway.
Without a word, he planked down the stack of books on my desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slam down: to put something down forcefully, usually making a noise
