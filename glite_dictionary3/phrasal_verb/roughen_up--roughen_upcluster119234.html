to roughen up

verb
make rougher or tougher
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfən ʌp / (AmE), / ˈrʌfən ʌp / (BrE)
to make something rougher or more uneven, or to make someone more aggressive or tougher

Examples

to roughen up a surface
Strong winds can quickly roughen up the surface of the usually calm pond.
The coach tried to roughen up the players to prepare them for the competition.
The new sandpaper will roughen up the wooden surface before painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – smooth out: make something smoother or less rough
  • synonym – toughen up: make someone or something stronger or more resilient
