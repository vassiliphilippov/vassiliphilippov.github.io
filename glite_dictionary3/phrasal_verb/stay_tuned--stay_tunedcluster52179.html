to stay tuned

verb
wait for further updates or information
Pronunciation: / steɪ tuːnd / (AmE), / steɪ tjuːnd / (BrE)
to remain attentive and wait for further information or updates, especially in broadcasts or announcements

Examples

stay tuned for updates
Be sure to stay tuned for our interview with the author after these messages.
If you want to learn the results, stay tuned until the end of the show.
The company asked investors to stay tuned for further developments later this week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – wait: to remain in a place or state until something happens
