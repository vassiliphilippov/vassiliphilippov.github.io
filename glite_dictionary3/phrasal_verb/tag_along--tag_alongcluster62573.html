to tag along

verb
go with someone, often uninvited
Pronunciation: / tæɡ əˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / tæɡ əˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to go somewhere with someone, especially when you are not specifically invited or needed

Examples

to tag along with friends
