to grow together

verb
to unite or merge by growing
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
(of plants, tissues, or objects) to join or unite by growing so closely that separate parts become one.

Examples

grow together into one
Over the years, his eyebrows began to grow together above his nose.
The broken bones will grow together if properly aligned and immobilized.
The two oak saplings eventually grew together, forming a single sturdy trunk.
