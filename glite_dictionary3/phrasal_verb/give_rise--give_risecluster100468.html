to give rise

verb
to cause or bring about
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv raɪz / (AmE), / ɡɪv raɪz / (BrE)
to cause something to happen or exist; to bring about or produce

Examples

to give rise to problems
Changing climates have given rise to new patterns of migration.
His reckless actions gave rise to many unforeseen problems.
The controversial remarks gave rise to a heated debate among the attendees.
