to warm up

verb
operate a machine until ready for use
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ʌp / (AmE), / wɔːm ʌp / (BrE)
to run a machine or engine until it reaches its normal working temperature

Examples

to warm up the engine
He let the generator warm up before switching on the equipment.
It's important to warm up the car on cold mornings to avoid engine problems.
They always warm up the truck for a few minutes before driving off.
