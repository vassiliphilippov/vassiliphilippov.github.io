to parcel out

verb
divide and distribute in portions
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑr.səl aʊt /; / ˌpɑːr.səl ˈaʊt /; / ˈpɑrsəl aʊt / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.səl aʊt /; / ˌpɑː.səl ˈaʊt /; / ˈpɑːsəl aʊt / (BrE)
