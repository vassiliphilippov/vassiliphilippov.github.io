to empty the tank

verb
remove all contents from a tank
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmpti ðə tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈɛmpti ðə taŋk / (BrE)
to remove all contents, especially liquid or fuel, from a tank or container until it is completely empty

Examples

empty the tank of fuel
Always empty the tank before attempting any maintenance on the engine.
She remembered to empty the tank after finishing the cleaning job.
The campers were told to empty the tank to avoid leaks during transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drain: to remove liquid from something until it is dry or empty
