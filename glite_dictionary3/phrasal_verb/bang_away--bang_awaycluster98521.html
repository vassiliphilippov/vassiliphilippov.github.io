to bang away

verb
play energetically or work persistently
Pronunciation: / bæŋ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / bæŋ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to play a musical instrument energetically or to work persistently and with force on something, often in a repetitive or noisy way

Examples

to bang away at the piano
He would often bang away at his keyboard late into the night.
She banged away at her novel until the first draft was finished.
The kids loved to bang away on the piano during music class.
