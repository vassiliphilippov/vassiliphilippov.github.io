to bounce around

verb
discuss ideas informally
Pronunciation: / baʊns əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / baʊns əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to discuss or exchange ideas or thoughts informally, often in a group setting

Examples

bounce around ideas
Let's bounce around some ideas before we make a final decision.
New proposals were bounced around until everyone agreed on a plan.
She loves to bounce around concepts with her colleagues at lunch.
