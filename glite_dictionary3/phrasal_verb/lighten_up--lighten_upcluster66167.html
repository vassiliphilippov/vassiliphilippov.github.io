to lighten up

verb
make more cheerful by brightening or adding color
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtn̩ ʌp /; / ˈlaɪtən ʌp / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtn ʌp /; / ˈlaɪtən ʌp / (BrE)
to make a place or thing brighter or lighter in color

Examples

lighten up a room
A few colorful pillows can really lighten up a dull living room.
She wanted to lighten up her office by painting the walls a soft blue.
We decided to lighten up the kitchen with yellow curtains and new paint.

As the sun rose, the sky began to lighten up over the mountains.
The walls will lighten up if you use a brighter shade of paint.
