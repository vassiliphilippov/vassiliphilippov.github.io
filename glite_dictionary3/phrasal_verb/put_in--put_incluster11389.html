to put in

verb
interrupt a conversation
Pronunciation: / pʊt ɪn / (AmE), / pʊt ɪn / (BrE)
to interrupt a conversation in order to make a comment or give input

Examples

to put in a comment
People kept trying to put in comments before I finished my story.
She waited politely before trying to put in her own question.
Whenever the discussion gets heated, John tends to put in his opinion.

  • synonym – cut in: to interrupt someone speaking
