to align with

verb
be in agreement or in harmony with
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪn wɪθ / (AmE), / əˈlaɪn wɪð / (BrE)
to be in agreement with or to adjust in order to match or support something

Examples

align with company values
His actions do not align with what he promised during the campaign.
The new policy must align with the company's core values to be effective.
We regularly update our curriculum to align with industry standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conform to: to comply with rules, standards, or laws
  • antonym – oppose: to be against or in disagreement with
