to brush down

verb
reprimand sharply
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ daʊn / (AmE), / brʌʃ daʊn / (BrE)
to reprimand someone, especially in a sharp or forceful manner

Examples

brush down an employee
After the mistake was found, the manager brushed down the junior staff in front of everyone.
He was brushed down by his coach for not following instructions during practice.
If you keep talking during the meeting, you'll just get brushed down again.

Related Terms

  • antonym – praise: to express approval or admiration
  • synonym – scold: to criticize angrily
  • synonym – tell off: to reprimand someone harshly
