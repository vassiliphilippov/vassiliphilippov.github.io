to boil over

verb
suddenly lose emotional control
Pronunciation: / bɔɪl ˈoʊvər / (AmE), / bɔɪl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to suddenly lose self-control and express strong emotion, especially anger

Examples

anger boils over
His anger boiled over when he discovered the mistake.
If you keep ignoring the problem, emotions are bound to boil over at some point.
She tried to stay calm, but her frustration eventually boiled over during the discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym – explode: to suddenly lose control and express anger violently
  • antonym – simmer down: to become less angry or emotional
