to soup up

verb
make more powerful; enhance performance (informal)
Pronunciation: / suːp ʌp / (AmE), / suːp ʌp / (BrE)
to modify (a machine or vehicle), especially to increase its power or performance

Examples

to soup up a car
He decided to soup up his motorcycle before the big race.
Many car enthusiasts love to soup up old engines in their garages.
The company souped up its laptop model by adding a faster processor.
