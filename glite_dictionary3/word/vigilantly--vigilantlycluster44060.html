vigilantly

adverb
in a watchful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪdʒələntli / (AmE), / ˈvɪdʒɪləntli / (BrE)
In a watchful and attentive manner, especially to detect danger or difficulties.

Examples

watch vigilantly for danger
Parents often supervise their children vigilantly while they play near the street.
She vigilantly checked each document for errors before submitting the final report.
The security guard watched vigilantly for any sign of trouble throughout the night.
