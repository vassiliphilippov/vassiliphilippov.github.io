oily

adjective
insincerely flattering or ingratiating
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪli / (AmE), / ˈɔɪli / (BrE)
excessively and unpleasantly smooth, flattering, or ingratiating in manner or speech; insincerely suave or obsequious.

Examples

oily smile
His oily compliments made everyone uncomfortable at the party.
She disliked his oily manner, finding it insincere and manipulative.
The politician gave an oily speech, clearly trying too hard to impress the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sincere: genuine, without pretense or insincerity
  • synonym – smarmy: obsequiously flattering or insincerely polite
  • synonym – unctuous: excessively flattering or insincere; having a smug, ingratiating manner
