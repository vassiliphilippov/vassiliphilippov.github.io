necrolysis

noun
breakdown of dead tissue
Pronunciation: / nɪˈkrɑlɪsɪs / (AmE), / nɛˈkrɒlɪsɪs / (BrE)
the disintegration and dissolution of dead tissue in the body, especially as a result of a disease process or burn

Examples

necrolysis of skin tissue
