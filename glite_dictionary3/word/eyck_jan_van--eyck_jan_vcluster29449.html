Eyck, Jan van

proper noun
Flemish painter; pioneer of oil painting
Pronunciation: / eɪk / (AmE), / eɪk / (BrE)
(1390–1441) Flemish painter who was a founder of the Flemish school of painting and pioneered modern techniques of oil painting.
