straight

adjective
alcoholic drink served without mixer
Pronunciation: / streɪt / (AmE), / streɪt / (BrE)
(of an alcoholic drink) served without water, ice, or any mixer; neat.

Examples

straight whiskey
He prefers his whiskey straight after a long day at work.
She ordered a bourbon straight, declining any ice or water.
Would you like your vodka straight or with a mixer?
