frothy

adjective
full of bubbles or foam
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑːθi / (AmE), / ˈfrɒθi / (BrE)
(of a liquid) containing many small bubbles; emitting or covered with foam or froth as from carbonation or fermentation.

Examples

frothy milk foam
After shaking the bottle, the soda became extremely frothy when opened.
She enjoyed a frothy cappuccino while reading her morning paper.
The waiter poured a glass of frothy beer for each guest at the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bubbly: full of bubbles; effervescent
  • synonym – foamy: full of or covered with foam
