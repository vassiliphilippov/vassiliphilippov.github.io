pan-loafy

adjective
having texture like pan-baked bread
Pronunciation: / pæn ˈloʊ.fi / (AmE), / pæn ˈləʊ.fi / (BrE)
having a texture similar to that of bread baked in a pan, typically soft and even

Examples

pan-loafy bread slices

This cake is wonderfully pan-loafy, with a soft and uniform crumb.
