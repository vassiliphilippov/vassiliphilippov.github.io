giantism

noun
abnormal largeness of stature
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪəntˌɪzəm /; / ˈdʒaɪənˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪəntɪz(ə)m /; / ˈdʒaɪəntɪzəm / (BrE)
a medical condition in which a person or animal grows much larger or taller than normal, usually because of too much growth hormone
