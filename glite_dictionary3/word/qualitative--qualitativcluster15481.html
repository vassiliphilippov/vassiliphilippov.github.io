qualitative

adjective
relating to qualities, not quantities
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑː.ləˌteɪ.tɪv /; / ˈkwɑː.ləˌteɪ.t̬ɪv / (AmE), / ˈkwɒl.ɪ.tə.tɪv / (BrE)
relating to the quality or qualities of something, not the quantity or amount

Examples

qualitative research methods

There was a significant qualitative difference between the two products.
This report focuses on qualitative analysis rather than numerical data.
