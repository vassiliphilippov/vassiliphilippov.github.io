fetich

noun
object believed to have magical powers
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfiːtɪʃ / (BrE)
A charm or object believed, especially by certain cultures or groups, to have magical powers or to embody a spirit.

Examples

lucky fetich
He wore a small fetich around his neck for good luck.
Many African tribes were once said to worship a fetich as a protective symbol.
The explorers were shown a curious fetich made from animal bones and feathers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fetish: an object believed to have magical powers; more common variant spelling
