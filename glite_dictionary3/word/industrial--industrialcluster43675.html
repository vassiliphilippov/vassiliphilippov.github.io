industrial

adjective
designed for heavy or commercial use
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl / (BrE)
(of goods or materials) designed to be durable and suitable for heavy use, especially in commercial or manufacturing settings.

Examples

industrial equipment
He chose industrial shelving for his garage because it could support heavy boxes.
The hotel installed industrial carpeting to withstand the constant foot traffic in the lobby.
Their kitchen is equipped with industrial appliances built for frequent, demanding use.

Related Terms

  • synonym – commercial: designed for business or trade use
  • antonym – domestic: intended for home use
