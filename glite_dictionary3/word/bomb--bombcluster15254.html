to bomb

verb
fail badly at something (informal)
Pronunciation: / bɑm / (AmE), / bɒm / (BrE)
to fail badly at something, especially an exam, test, or performance

Examples

to bomb an exam
Her presentation bombed and she was really embarrassed afterward.
I completely bombed the chemistry test despite studying all week.
If you don't prepare, you might bomb the final exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fail: to not succeed in passing an exam or achieving a goal
  • antonym – pass: to succeed in an exam or test
