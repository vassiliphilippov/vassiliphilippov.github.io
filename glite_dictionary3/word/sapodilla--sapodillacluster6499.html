sapodilla

noun
tropical fruit with sweet, brownish pulp
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpəˈdɪlə / (AmE), / ˌsapəˈdɪlə / (BrE)
A tropical fruit with rough brownish skin and very sweet, brownish pulp, native to Central America and the Caribbean.

Examples

ripe sapodilla fruit
He had never tasted a sapodilla before his trip to the Caribbean.
One sapodilla provides a rich source of fiber and natural sugar.
The fresh sapodilla she bought at the market was sweet and fragrant.
