criticalness

noun
urgency; state of being critical
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪtɪkəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪkəlnəs / (BrE)
a state or condition of being critical or of urgent importance; the quality of being at a point where decisive action is needed

Examples

the criticalness of a moment
During surgery, the doctors understood the criticalness of every decision they made.
He spoke about the criticalness of maintaining high safety standards in the plant.
The criticalness of the situation required immediate government intervention.
