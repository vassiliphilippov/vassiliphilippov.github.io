bungalow

noun
single-storey house
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋɡəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋɡələʊ / (BrE)
A low house having only one storey or, sometimes, upper rooms set in the roof, typically with a veranda.

Examples

cozy bungalow with veranda
The bungalow has a spacious veranda where we relaxed in the evenings.
They decided to retire in a small bungalow near the lake.
We spent our vacation in a cozy bungalow by the beach.
