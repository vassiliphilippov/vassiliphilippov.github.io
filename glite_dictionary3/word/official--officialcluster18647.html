official

adjective
recognized as the state or national church
Pronunciation: / əˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / əˈfɪʃəl / (BrE)
(of a church) recognized by the government as the established national or state institution.

Examples

official church
No other denomination had official status in the country until recent reforms.
The Anglican Church is the official church of England.
Unlike in some countries, the United States has no official church.
