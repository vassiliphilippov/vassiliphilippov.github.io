fake

noun
person who deceives by pretending
Pronunciation: / feɪk / (AmE), / feɪk / (BrE)
a person who pretends to be something they are not, often to deceive others

Examples

a fake doctor
Everyone thought he was a doctor, but he was just a fake.
She called him a fake after discovering his lies.
The art world is full of fakes who try to pass off imitations as originals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fraud: a person who is not what they pretend to be; a deceiver
  • antonym – genuine: real; actually what it is said to be; authentic
  • synonym – impostor: a person who pretends to be someone else to deceive
