to acclimate

verb
become used to a new climate or environment
Pronunciation: / əˈklɪ.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈæ.klɪ.meɪt / (BrE)
to adjust or become accustomed to a new climate, environment, or situation

Examples

to acclimate to new conditions
After moving to the mountains, it took her several weeks to acclimate to the altitude.
The athletes arrived early to acclimate before the competition began.
Workers need time to acclimate when transferring to a new country with extreme weather.
