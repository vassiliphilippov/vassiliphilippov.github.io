Venice

proper noun
city in northeastern Italy, known for its canals
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɛnɪs / (BrE)
A city in northeastern Italy, famous for its canals, built on islands in a lagoon and serving as the capital of the Veneto region.
