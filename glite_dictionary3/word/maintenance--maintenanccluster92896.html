maintenance

noun
act of sustaining life or subsistence
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪntənəns / (AmE), / ˈmeɪntənəns / (BrE)
the act of sustaining life by providing food or other means of subsistence

Examples

maintenance of life
His meager earnings were barely enough for the maintenance of his family.
The villagers depended on fish as their primary source of maintenance during the harsh winters.
Without adequate maintenance, many refugees faced hunger and disease.
