apology

noun
expression of regret or saying sorry
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / əˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
a statement or action in which you express regret or say sorry for having hurt or upset someone

Examples

make an apology
After their argument, she wrote a heartfelt apology to her friend.
He offered a sincere apology after breaking her favorite vase.
Please accept my apology for being late to the meeting.
