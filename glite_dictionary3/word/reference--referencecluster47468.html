to reference

verb
to mention or cite as a source
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛf.ɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˈrɛf.ər.əns / (BrE)
to mention, cite, or allude to something as a source of information or authority

Examples

to reference a source
In his speech, the professor referenced several influential studies from the past decade.
She carefully referenced all her sources at the end of her paper.
The article references important discoveries in the field of genetics.
During her talk, she referenced various historical events to illustrate her points.
He referenced the original document to support his argument.
The professor referenced several previous studies in her lecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cite: to quote as evidence or example; to mention as a reference
  • synonym – mention: to refer to something briefly or casually
