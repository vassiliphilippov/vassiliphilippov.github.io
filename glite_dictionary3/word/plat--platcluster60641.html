plat

noun
platinum trophy (gaming slang)
Pronunciation: / plæt / (AmE), / plæt / (BrE)
Informal (gaming). A platinum-level trophy or achievement awarded for completing all or nearly all of a game's objectives.

Examples

After months of challenges, she finally earned the plat for completing every side quest.
He posted a screenshot of his new plat on the forum to celebrate.
Many streamers plan long play sessions specifically to chase a plat on release day.
