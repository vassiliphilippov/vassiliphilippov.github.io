cracker

noun
thin, crisp wafer snack
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækər / (AmE), / ˈkrækə / (BrE)
A thin, crisp, unsweetened or semisweet wafer made of flour and water, often eaten as a snack.

Examples

eat a cracker snack
I packed some crackers in my lunch for a snack.
She served cheese and crackers at the party.
The soup came with a small packet of crackers on the side.
