acerbic

adjective
sharply critical or biting in tone
Pronunciation: / əˈsɜrbɪk / (AmE), / əˈsɜːbɪk / (BrE)
(of speech or writing) sharply critical, biting, or harsh in tone.

Examples

acerbic comment
He was known for his acerbic wit, often making biting jokes at others' expense.
Her acerbic comments during the meeting offended several colleagues.
The critic's acerbic review left the director visibly upset.

Related Terms

  • synonym – caustic: sarcastic in a scathing and bitter way
  • antonym – mild: not severe or harsh in tone
