economics

noun
study of production and distribution of goods and services
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪks, ˌikəˈnɑmɪks / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks, ˌɛkəˈnɒmɪks / (BrE)
The branch of social science concerned with the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services, and the management of resources.
