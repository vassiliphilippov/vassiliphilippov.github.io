pointless

adjective
having no purpose or use
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪnt.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪnt.ləs / (BrE)
Having no purpose or use; serving no useful purpose.

Examples

pointless argument
Arguing with him often feels pointless because he never listens to reason.
We decided to cancel the meeting since it seemed pointless to continue.
Writing more emails is pointless if they never reply.
