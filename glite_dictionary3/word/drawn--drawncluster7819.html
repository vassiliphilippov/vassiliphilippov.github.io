drawn

adjective
looking tired or strained (of a face)
Pronunciation: / drɔn / (AmE), / drɔːn / (BrE)
(of a person's face) looking tired, worried, or suffering, often noticeably pale or tense.

Examples

drawn face
After several nights without sleep, her face looked noticeably drawn.
He glanced up, his features drawn with worry over the upcoming exam.
The stress of the past few months had left him appearing pale and drawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym – careworn: appearing tired and unhappy because of prolonged worry
  • synonym – haggard: looking exhausted and unwell, especially from fatigue or worry
  • antonym – radiant: showing health and happiness
