Lophophora williamsii

noun
species of spineless, globe-shaped cactus (peyote)
Pronunciation: / loʊˌfɑːfərə wɪlˈjæmsiaɪ / (AmE), / ləʊˌfɒfərə wɪlˈjæmsiaɪ / (BrE)
