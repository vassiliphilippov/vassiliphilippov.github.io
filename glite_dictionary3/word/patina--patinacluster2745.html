patina

noun
greenish layer on metal from oxidation
Pronunciation: / pəˈtinə / (AmE), / ˈpætɪnə /; / pəˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a thin layer or film that forms on the surface of metals (like copper or bronze) or other materials over time, often giving an attractive or aged appearance
