decolonisation

noun
reforming institutions to remove colonial bias

the process of reforming curricula, museum displays, research, and other institutional practices to remove colonial biases and include historically marginalised perspectives.

Examples

decolonisation of education
Universities are pursuing decolonisation of the curriculum to include diverse voices and histories.
