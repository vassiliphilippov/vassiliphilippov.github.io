titre

noun
concentration determined by titration
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtiːtə / (BrE)
The concentration of a solution as determined by titration, typically expressed as the amount of a substance in a given volume of solution.

Examples

titre of a solution
After titration, the titre of the acid solution was found to be 0.1 mol/L.
Researchers monitored the titre levels to track the progress of the infection.
The antibody titre in the serum was measured using an immunoassay.
