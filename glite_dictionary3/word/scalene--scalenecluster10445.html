scalene

adjective
triangle with three unequal sides
Pronunciation: / skeɪˈliːn / (AmE), / skeɪˈliːn / (BrE)
(of a triangle) having all three sides of different lengths.

Examples

scalene triangle shape
In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the area of a scalene triangle.
The mathematician drew a scalene triangle on the board to illustrate his point.
Unlike an equilateral triangle, a scalene triangle has no equal sides or angles.

Related Terms

  • antonym – equilateral: triangle with all sides of equal length
  • antonym – isosceles: triangle with two sides of equal length
