hawala

noun
informal money transfer system
Pronunciation: / həˈwɑlə / (AmE), / həˈwɑːlə / (BrE)
an informal, trust-based system of transferring money, especially internationally, without physical movement of cash or bank records

Examples

hawala money transfer
Authorities are monitoring hawala networks as part of their anti-money-laundering efforts.
Many people in remote areas use the hawala system to send money home.
The hawala method relies on trust rather than formal banking channels.
