galago

noun
small nocturnal African primate (bush baby)
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈleɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəˈleɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
A small, agile, long-tailed nocturnal African primate with dense woolly fur, large eyes, and large ears, also known as a bush baby.
