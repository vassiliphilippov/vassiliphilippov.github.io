fundraiser

noun
person who solicits donations
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndˌreɪzər / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌreɪzə / (BrE)
a person who seeks to collect money for a cause, organization, or project, typically by organizing events or campaigns

Examples

experienced fundraiser
As a fundraiser, he organizes gala events to support medical research.
Sarah volunteered as a fundraiser for the local animal shelter this year.
The charity hired an experienced fundraiser to help increase donations.
