miliaria

noun
skin rash from blocked sweat ducts
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪliˈɛəriə / (AmE), / ˌmɪliˈɑːriə / (BrE)
A skin condition caused by blockage of the sweat ducts, resulting in small, itchy rashes, especially during high heat and humidity.

Examples

miliaria rash on skin
Infants are particularly susceptible to miliaria in warm, humid environments.
The doctor diagnosed her with miliaria after she developed a rash during the heatwave.
Wearing tight clothing in hot weather can increase your risk of miliaria.
