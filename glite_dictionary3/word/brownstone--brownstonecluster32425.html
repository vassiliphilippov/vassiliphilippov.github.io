brownstone

noun
a house built of reddish-brown sandstone
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnˌstəʊn / (BrE)
A townhouse or row house built of reddish-brown sandstone, especially common in northeastern US cities.

brownstone townhouse
