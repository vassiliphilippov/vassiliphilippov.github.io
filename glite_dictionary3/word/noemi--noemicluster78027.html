Noemi

proper noun
biblical mother-in-law of Ruth
Pronunciation: / noʊˈiːmi / (AmE), / nəʊˈiːmi / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the mother-in-law of Ruth, whose story is told in the Book of Ruth in the Old Testament.

Noemi in the Bible
Noemi's journey from hardship to hope is a central narrative in the Book of Ruth.
