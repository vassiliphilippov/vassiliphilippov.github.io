self-effacing

adjective
modest; not drawing attention to oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf əˈfeɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf ɪˈfeɪsɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

self-effacing attitude

The scientist's self-effacing attitude often led people to underestimate her groundbreaking research.
