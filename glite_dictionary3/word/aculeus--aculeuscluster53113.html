aculeus

noun
sharp-pointed anatomical structure; insect sting
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːliəs / (AmE), / əˈkjuːlɪəs / (BrE)
a sharp, pointed structure, especially the sting of a hymenopterous insect such as a bee or wasp
