mate

noun
fellow member of a team
Pronunciation: / meɪt / (AmE), / meɪt / (BrE)
a member of the same team, especially in sports; a teammate.

Examples

team mate
Every mate on the soccer team contributed to the win.
He celebrated the victory with his mates after the final whistle.
Jessica is a supportive mate who always encourages her teammates during practice.
