brittlebush

noun
fragrant southwestern US desert shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪtɫ̩ˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪt.əl.bʊʃ / (BrE)
a fragrant shrub native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, with brittle stems, blue-green leaves, and yellow flowers, valued for its resin used in incense, varnish, and traditional medicine
