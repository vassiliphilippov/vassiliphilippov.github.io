peba

noun
a nine-banded armadillo species
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛbə / (AmE), / ˈpiːbə / (BrE)
A species of armadillo with nine hinged bands of bony plates, found from Texas to Paraguay.

Examples

peba armadillo
Farmers in South America sometimes encounter the peba while working in the fields.
The peba is recognized by its nine distinctive bands of armor along its back.
The diet of the peba mainly consists of insects and small invertebrates.
