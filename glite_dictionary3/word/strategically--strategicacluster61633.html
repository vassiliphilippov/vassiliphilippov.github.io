strategically

adverb
in a manner relating to strategy
Pronunciation: / strəˈtɪdʒɪkli / (AmE), / strəˈtiːdʒɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is planned according to strategy.

Examples

strategically plan moves
She invested strategically to maximize long-term gains.
The company placed its offices strategically near key clients.
Troops were strategically positioned to defend the city.
