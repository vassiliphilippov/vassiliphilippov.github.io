vicegerent

noun
person acting as ruler's deputy
Pronunciation: / vaɪsˈdʒɛr.ənt / (AmE), / vaɪsˈdʒɛr.ənt / (BrE)
a person appointed by a ruler to act as an administrative deputy or representative, exercising authority on the ruler's behalf

Examples

vicegerent of the king
As vicegerent of the king, he held authority over the entire territory.
In the sultan's absence, the vicegerent was responsible for maintaining order.
The monarch appointed a trusted vicegerent to oversee the province during his absence.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deputy: a person appointed to assist or act for another
  • synonym – representative: someone who acts on behalf of another
