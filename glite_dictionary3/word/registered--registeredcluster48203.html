registered

adjective
officially documented (of a vehicle or boat)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstɚd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstəd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or vessel) officially recorded with the appropriate authorities and furnished with documents specifying ownership and other details.

Examples

registered vehicle
He showed the coast guard the registered documentation for his vessel.
Only registered boats are permitted to participate in the annual regatta.
The yacht is not allowed to leave the harbor until it is properly registered.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unregistered: not officially recorded or documented with authorities
