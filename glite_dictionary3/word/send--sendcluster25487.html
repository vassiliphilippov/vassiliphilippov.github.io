to send

verb
arrange delivery or transportation
Pronunciation: / sɛnd / (AmE), / sɛnd / (BrE)
to cause something (such as a message, object, or information) to go or be delivered to another place or person

Examples

to send a message

They are sending medical supplies to areas affected by the flood.
Please send the documents to my office as soon as possible.
She sent her children to school on the bus every morning.
We are sending an invitation to everyone in the department.
