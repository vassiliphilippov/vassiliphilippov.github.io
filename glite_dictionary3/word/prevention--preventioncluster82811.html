prevention

noun
the act of stopping something
Pronunciation: / prɪˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of stopping something from happening or arising

Examples

prevention of disease
Education plays a key role in the prevention of drug abuse among teenagers.
Regular handwashing is essential in the prevention of diseases.
The city has implemented strict measures for the prevention of crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym – avoidance: the act of keeping away from something
  • antonym – cure: the act of relieving or healing after an illness has occurred
