scruple

noun
unit of apothecary weight (20 grains)
Pronunciation: / ˈskruːpəl / (AmE), / ˈskruːpl / (BrE)
a unit of apothecary weight equal to 20 grains, used historically in pharmacy

Examples

a scruple of medicine
