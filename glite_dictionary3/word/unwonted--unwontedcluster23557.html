unwonted

adjective
unusual; not customary
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɔntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwəʊntɪd / (BrE)
(formal or literary) unusual; not customary or expected.

Examples

unwonted enthusiasm
He greeted us with unwonted enthusiasm, surprising everyone in the room.
The garden was filled with an unwonted stillness after the storm.
There was an unwonted tenderness in his voice when he spoke of his childhood.
