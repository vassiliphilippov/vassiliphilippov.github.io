concourse

noun
large public gathering area
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnˌkɔrs /; / ˈkɑːnˌkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋˌkɔːs /; / ˈkɒŋ.kɔːs / (BrE)
a large open area or hall in a public building, such as a station or airport, where people gather or move
