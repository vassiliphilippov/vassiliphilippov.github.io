Falstaff

proper noun
comic, dissolute Shakespearean character
Pronunciation: / ˈfæl.stæf / (AmE), / ˈfæl.stɑːf / (BrE)
A dissolute and humorous character in several plays by Shakespeare, known for his wit, boastfulness, and love of drink and mischief.
