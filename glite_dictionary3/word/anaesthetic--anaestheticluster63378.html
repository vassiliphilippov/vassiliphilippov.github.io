anaesthetic

adjective
causing loss of sensation
Pronunciation: / ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌænɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
causing a loss of sensation or consciousness, especially to prevent pain during a medical procedure.

Examples

anaesthetic cream
Before surgery, the area was treated with a local anaesthetic cream to numb the skin.
The dentist explained that the anaesthetic might leave your mouth numb for a few hours.
The patient was given an anaesthetic injection to ensure she felt no pain.
