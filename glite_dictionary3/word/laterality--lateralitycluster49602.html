laterality

noun
preference for one side of the body
Pronunciation: / ˌleɪtəˈrælɪti /; / ˌleɪtəˈræləti / (AmE), / ˌleɪtəˈrælɪti /; / ˌleɪtəˈraləti / (BrE)
the tendency for one side of the body or brain to be preferred, more developed, or specialized for certain functions (such as handedness or language ability)
