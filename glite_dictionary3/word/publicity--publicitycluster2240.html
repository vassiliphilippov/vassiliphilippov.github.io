publicity

noun
promotion or attention given to something
Pronunciation: / pʌbˈlɪsəti / (AmE), / pʌbˈlɪsəti / (BrE)
the activity or process of making something known to the public, especially in order to promote a product, person, cause, or idea

Examples

publicity campaign
The charity received a lot of publicity after the celebrity endorsement.
The company launched a major publicity campaign to improve its image.
The new law was introduced with minimal publicity, so many people were unaware of it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – advertising: the activity of producing advertisements
  • synonym – promotion: the activity of supporting or actively encouraging a cause, venture, or aim
  • antonym – secrecy: the action of keeping something secret or hidden
