midinette

noun
Parisian shopgirl or sales assistant
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːdiˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌmiːdɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
A young female sales assistant or shopgirl in Paris, especially in the early 20th century.
