to pick

verb
provoke or instigate (a fight, quarrel, etc.)
Pronunciation: / pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
to deliberately provoke or instigate (a fight, quarrel, etc.), often by challenging or annoying someone

Examples

to pick a fight
He likes to pick fights with strangers at bars.
She accused him of trying to pick an argument over nothing.
Stop trying to pick a quarrel every time you disagree.
