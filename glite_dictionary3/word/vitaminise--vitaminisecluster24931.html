to vitaminise

verb
add vitamins as a supplement
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈtæmənaɪz / (AmE), / vɪˈtæmɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to add vitamins to something, especially as a supplement to food

Examples

to vitaminise milk
After processing, the milk is often vitaminised before packaging.
Some breakfast cereals are vitaminised to improve their nutritional value.
The company decided to vitaminise its juice products to appeal to health-conscious consumers.
