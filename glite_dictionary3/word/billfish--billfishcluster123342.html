billfish

noun
predatory fish with long jaw and hard scales
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪlˌfɪʃ /; / ˈbɪl.fɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.fɪʃ /; / ˈbɪlˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
