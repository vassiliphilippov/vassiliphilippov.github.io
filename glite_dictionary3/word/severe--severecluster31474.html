severe

adjective
very strong or intense
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɪr /; / səˈvɪr / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪə / (BrE)
very strong, intense, or extremely bad or serious (used for pain, injury, weather, damage, etc.)
