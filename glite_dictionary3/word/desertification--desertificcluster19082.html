desertification

noun
conversion of land to desert
Pronunciation: / dəˌzɜrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌzɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.

Examples

desertification of farmland
Efforts to combat desertification include reforestation and improved irrigation methods.
Many scientists warn that poor land management will accelerate desertification in vulnerable regions.
The United Nations has launched initiatives to address desertification in Africa.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aridification: the process of becoming extremely dry or arid
