grim

adjective
unyielding; determined; relentless
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪm / (AmE), / ɡrɪm / (BrE)
Firm and unyielding; showing determination that cannot be appeased or changed.

Examples

grim determination
Despite the setbacks, his grim determination led him to succeed.
Her grim resolve made it clear she would not give in.
The company faced the grim necessity of laying off workers during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – inexorable: Impossible to stop or prevent; unyielding
  • synonym – relentless: Refusing to give up or lessen in intensity
  • antonym – yielding: Willing to give way or be influenced
