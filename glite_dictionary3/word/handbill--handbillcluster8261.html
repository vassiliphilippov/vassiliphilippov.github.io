handbill

noun
small printed public notice
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbɪl / (BrE)
a small printed notice, advertisement, or announcement distributed by hand to the public

Examples

handbill advertising sales
A handbill was placed on every car windshield in the parking lot.
He printed hundreds of handbills advertising his new restaurant.
The concert organizers distributed handbills to promote the event throughout the city.
