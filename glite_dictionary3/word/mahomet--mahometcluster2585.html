Mahomet

proper noun
historical English name for Muhammad, Prophet of Islam
Pronunciation: / məˈhoʊmət / (AmE), / məˈhɒmɪt / (BrE)
A historical English form of the name of the Prophet Muhammad (570–632), the founder of Islam and considered by Muslims as the last messenger of Allah.
