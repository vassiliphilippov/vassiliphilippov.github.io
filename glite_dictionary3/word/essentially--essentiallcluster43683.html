essentially

adverb
in essence; fundamentally
Pronunciation: / ɪˈsɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ɪˈsɛnʃəli / (BrE)
used to emphasize the basic, fundamental, or intrinsic nature of something; in essence or most important respects.
