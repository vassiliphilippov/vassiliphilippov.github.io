Pomo

noun
a member of a Native American people of northern California
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊməʊ / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people of northern California, living along the Russian River valley and nearby Pacific coast.
