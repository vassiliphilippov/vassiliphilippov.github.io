inbreeding

noun
mating of closely related individuals
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌbriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌbriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of mating between closely related individuals, often resulting in decreased genetic diversity

Examples

animal inbreeding
Conservationists warn that inbreeding can lead to genetic disorders in endangered species.
Strict policies are in place to prevent inbreeding among captive animals.
The population suffered from health problems due to excessive inbreeding.

Related Terms

  • antonym – outbreeding: the act of mating unrelated individuals; genetic mixing
