limited

adjective
having a specific or restricted scope
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪd / (BrE)
having a specific function, purpose, or scope, often restricted to a particular area or activity

Examples

limited role
He played a limited role in the negotiations, focusing only on legal matters.
Her authority within the company was limited to financial decisions only.
The scientists have limited access to the classified data for security reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym – restricted: confined within bounds; limited
  • antonym – unlimited: without any limits or restrictions
