to slog

verb
to hit hard with fist or bat
Pronunciation: / slɑg / (AmE), / slɒg / (BrE)
to strike heavily, especially with a fist or a bat

Examples

slog a ball
He slogged the ball out of the park with one powerful swing.
She slogged him on the shoulder to get his attention.
The boxer slogged his opponent in the jaw during the final round.
