Scutigeridae

noun
family of centipedes
Pronunciation: / skuːˌtɪdʒəˈrɪdiː / (AmE), / skjuːˌtɪdʒəˈrɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of centipedes, including the house centipede, characterized by long legs and fast movement.

Examples

Scutigeridae centipede family
Scientists studied the rapid movements characteristic of centipedes in the Scutigeridae family.
The family Scutigeridae includes the well-known house centipede found in many homes worldwide.
Unlike some other centipedes, members of the Scutigeridae are harmless to humans.
