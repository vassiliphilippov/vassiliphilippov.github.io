soymilk

noun
milk substitute made from soybeans
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔɪˌmɪlk / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪ.mɪlk / (BrE)
A milk-like beverage made from soybeans, used as a milk substitute in drinks and cooking.

Examples

soymilk in coffee
Many people who are lactose intolerant prefer soymilk in their coffee.
She used soymilk to make a vegan smoothie for breakfast.
The recipe calls for two cups of soymilk instead of regular milk.
