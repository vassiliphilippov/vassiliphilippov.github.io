ego

noun
inflated sense of self-importance
Pronunciation: / ˈiːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈiːɡəʊ / (BrE)
An inflated sense of self-importance or pride in one's own superiority over others.

Examples

big ego
Fame can inflate a person's ego to unhealthy levels.
Her ego prevented her from admitting she was wrong.
With a bruised ego, he struggled to handle the criticism.

Related Terms

  • synonym – egotism: Excessive reference to oneself or self-centeredness
  • antonym – humility: A modest view of one's own importance
