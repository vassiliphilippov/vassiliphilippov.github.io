stabiliser

noun
device or substance that ensures stability
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪbəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or substance used to make something stable or to maintain its stability, especially by preventing unwanted movement or change

Examples

stabiliser in paint
Some paints contain a stabiliser to prevent the colour from fading.
The airplane's stabiliser helps it maintain a steady flight path.
You should add a voltage stabiliser to protect electronic devices from power fluctuations.
