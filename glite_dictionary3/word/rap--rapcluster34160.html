to rap

verb
perform rap music
Pronunciation: / ræp / (AmE), / rap / (BrE)
to perform rap music by speaking lyrics rhythmically over a beat, often with rhyming verse

Examples

to rap lyrics
He started to rap at open-mic nights when he was just sixteen.
She rapped about her experiences growing up in the city.
The group will rap their new single at the concert tonight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – MC: to perform as a master of ceremonies or rapper, especially in hip-hop culture
  • antonym – sing: to make musical sounds with the voice, especially words with a set tune
