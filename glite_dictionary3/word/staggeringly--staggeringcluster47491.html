staggeringly

adverb
extremely surprisingly or greatly
Pronunciation: / ˈstæɡɚɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstæɡərɪŋli / (BrE)
To a degree that is extremely surprising, impressive, or hard to believe.

Examples

staggeringly high number

The company's new product was staggeringly successful, exceeding all expectations.
The cost of living in that city is staggeringly high compared to the national average.
