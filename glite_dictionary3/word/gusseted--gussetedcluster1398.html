gusseted

adjective
reinforced with gussets
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌs.ɪt.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡʌs.ɪt.ɪd / (BrE)
provided with or strengthened by one or more gussets, especially to reinforce joints or seams

Examples

gusseted plate joint
Engineers installed gusseted plates to reinforce the bridge joints.
Many high-end backpacks have gusseted pockets to expand for extra storage.
The new jeans are gusseted at the crotch for extra durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gusset: a piece of material sewn into a garment to strengthen or enlarge part of it
