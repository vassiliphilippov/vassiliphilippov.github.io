paradigmatic

adjective
relating to a grammatical paradigm or pattern
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.ə.dɪgˈmæt̬.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌpær.ə.dɪgˈmæt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or serving as a grammatical paradigm or model pattern, especially in linguistics.
