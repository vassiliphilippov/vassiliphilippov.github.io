mistily

adverb
in a vague or indistinct way
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstɪli / (AmE), / ˈmɪstɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is vague, indistinct, or lacking clear details.

Examples

mistily explain details
His explanation was so mistily worded that no one understood what he meant.
She recalled the events of her childhood mistily, unable to remember the exact details.
