Nelson

proper noun
British admiral, hero of Trafalgar
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛlsən / (AmE), / ˈnɛlsən / (BrE)
Horatio Nelson (1758–1805), an English admiral celebrated for his victories over the French fleets during the Napoleonic Wars, notably at the Battle of Trafalgar, where he was mortally wounded.
