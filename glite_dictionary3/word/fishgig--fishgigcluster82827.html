fishgig

noun
barbed fishing spear
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌɡɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌɡɪɡ / (BrE)
a tool consisting of a pole with a barbed point, used for catching fish by spearing

Examples

fishgig spears fish
He used a fishgig to catch dinner from the shallow stream.
The old shed still held his grandfather's rusty fishgig.
Traditional fishermen often prefer using a fishgig over a net in certain waters.
