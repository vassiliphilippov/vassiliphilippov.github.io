stature

noun
height of a standing person or animal
Pronunciation: / ˈstætʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈstætʃə / (BrE)
The height of a person or animal when standing upright.

Examples

tall stature
At six feet tall, his stature made him stand out in the crowd.
Giraffes are known for their impressive stature among animals of the African savanna.
The scientist measured the stature of each patient during the health survey.
