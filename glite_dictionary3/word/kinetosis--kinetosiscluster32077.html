kinetosis

noun
motion sickness caused by travel
Pronunciation: / kɪnəˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / kɪnɪˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition in which a person feels dizzy or nauseated due to the motion experienced while traveling in or on a moving vehicle; motion sickness
