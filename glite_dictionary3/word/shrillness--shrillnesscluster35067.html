shrillness

noun
piercing high-pitched sound quality
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃrɪlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having a loud, high-pitched, sharp, or piercing sound (especially unpleasant to the ear)
