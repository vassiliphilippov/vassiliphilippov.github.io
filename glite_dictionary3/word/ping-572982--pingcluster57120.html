ping

noun
sharp, high-pitched resonant sound
Pronunciation: / pɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪŋ / (BrE)
a short, sharp high-pitched resonant sound, such as that made by a sonar echo or a bullet striking metal

Examples

a loud ping sound
As the submarine detected another vessel, a loud ping echoed through the control room.
He heard the sharp ping of a bullet ricocheting off the metal gate.
The microwave ended with a familiar ping, signaling that the food was ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clang: a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym – ring: a clear resonant sound
  • antonym – thud: a dull sound, as of a heavy object falling
