isothermic

adjective
relating to equal or constant temperature
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈθɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈθɜːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or indicating equal or constant temperature, especially as shown by an isotherm.

Examples
