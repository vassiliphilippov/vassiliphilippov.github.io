inarguable

adjective
impossible to dispute or deny
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɑrɡjuəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈɑːɡjuəbl / (BrE)
impossible to deny or dispute; not open to argument

Examples

inarguable fact
His talent for music is inarguable, even among his critics.
It is inarguable that climate change poses significant risks to our planet.
The evidence she presented was simply inarguable and convinced everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym – debatable: open to discussion or argument
  • synonym – indisputable: impossible to question or doubt
