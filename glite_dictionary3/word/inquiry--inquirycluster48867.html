inquiry

noun
act of seeking knowledge or investigation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkwaɪəri /; / ɪnˈkwaɪəri, ˈɪŋkwɛri / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪəri /; / ɪnˈkwaɪəri, ˈɪŋkwəri / (BrE)
an act of seeking information or asking a question; a request for information or an investigation
