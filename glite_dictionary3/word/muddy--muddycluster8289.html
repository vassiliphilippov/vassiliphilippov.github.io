muddy

adjective
covered with mud; dirty
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdi / (AmE), / ˈmʌdi / (BrE)
covered with or full of mud; dirty and messy (especially ground or objects)

Examples

muddy boots
After the storm, the path was so muddy that it was hard to walk on.
She washed the muddy dog before letting it inside.
