gutlessness

noun
lack of courage or determination
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌt.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt.ləs.nəs / (BrE)
lack of courage or determination; the quality of being afraid to take risks or face difficulties

Examples

show gutlessness under pressure

Many critics blamed the government’s gutlessness for the failure to act decisively.
She overcame her gutlessness and finally confronted her fears.
