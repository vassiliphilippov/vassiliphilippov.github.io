bridgehead

noun
military defensive position at a bridge
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪdʒˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbrɪdʒˌhɛd / (BrE)
a military position in enemy territory, especially at the end of a bridge, used to secure a crossing and allow more troops or equipment to arrive
