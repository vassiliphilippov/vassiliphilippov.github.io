popularisation

noun
making something easy to understand and accessible
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑː.pjə.lɚ.əˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌpɑːpjələrəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒp.jʊ.lə.raɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌpɒpjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something easily understandable and accessible to the general public
