Dardanus

proper noun
founder of Troy in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrdənəs / (AmE), / ˈdɑːdənəs / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the legendary founder of Troy and ancestor of the Trojan kings.

Examples

Dardanus founder of Troy
According to legend, Dardanus sailed across the Aegean Sea to found Troy.
Many ancient texts describe Dardanus as the ancestor of Trojan royalty.
The myth of Dardanus links the origins of Troy to the gods.
