to freeze

verb
suddenly stop moving; become motionless
Pronunciation: / friːz / (AmE), / friːz / (BrE)
to stop moving or become unable to move, often suddenly and usually because of fear or surprise

Examples

to freeze with fear
If you see a bear in the woods, it's best to freeze and stay still.
She froze with her hand on the doorknob when she heard a strange noise.
When the teacher yelled, the students froze in their seats.

  • synonym – stop: to cease moving, operating, or acting
