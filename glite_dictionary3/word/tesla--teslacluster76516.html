tesla

noun
SI unit of magnetic flux density
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛs.lə / (AmE), / ˈtɛz.lə / (BrE)
the SI unit of magnetic flux density, equal to one weber per square meter

Examples

one tesla unit
A refrigerator magnet has a field of about five millitesla, much weaker than one tesla.
Physicists often use the tesla as the standard unit to describe magnetic fields.
The magnetic field inside the MRI machine measures several tesla in strength.
