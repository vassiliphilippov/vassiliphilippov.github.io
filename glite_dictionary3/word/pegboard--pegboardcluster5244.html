pegboard

noun
board with holes for pegs
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛɡˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpɛɡˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board with regularly spaced holes into which pegs can be fitted, typically used for organizing items or playing certain games

Examples

pegboard with tools
He mounted a new pegboard in the garage to help organize his sports equipment.
She hung her tools on the pegboard above the workbench for easy access.
The classroom used a pegboard to display students’ artwork and achievements.
