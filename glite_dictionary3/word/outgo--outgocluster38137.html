outgo

noun
money paid out; expenditure
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌgoʊ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌgəʊ / (BrE)
An amount of money spent or paid out; expenditure.

Examples

monthly outgo
Her monthly outgo far exceeds her income, creating financial difficulties.
The company's outgo has increased steadily over the past year.
We need to cut down our outgo if we want to save more money.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expenditure: the act of spending money; an amount spent
  • antonym – income: money received, especially on a regular basis
