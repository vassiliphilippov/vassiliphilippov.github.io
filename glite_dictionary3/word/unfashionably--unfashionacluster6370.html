unfashionably

adverb
in an unfashionable way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfæʃənəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfæʃnəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is not fashionable or not in accordance with current styles or trends.

Examples

unfashionably dressed
Although comfortable, his clothes were unfashionably outdated for the formal event.
She always wore her hair unfashionably, ignoring the latest styles.
The restaurant was decorated unfashionably, but the food was exceptional.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fashionably: in a fashionable manner
