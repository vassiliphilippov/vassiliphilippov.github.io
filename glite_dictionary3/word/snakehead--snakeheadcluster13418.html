snakehead

noun
organizer of illegal human smuggling
Pronunciation: / c8sne6akcch5bd / (AmE), / c8sne6akh5bd / (BrE)
(informal) a person who arranges or organizes the illegal entry or transport of people into a country, typically for profit.

a snakehead smuggles migrants

Investigators uncovered evidence showing that the snakehead coordinated travel, forged documents, and hidden routes.
