chili

noun
spicy stew with beef and beans
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪli / (BrE)
A spicy stew made chiefly of ground beef and chili peppers or chili powder, often with tomatoes and kidney beans.

Examples

bowl of chili
His homemade chili is famous for its rich flavor and tender beef.
She made a big pot of chili for the family dinner.
The cook-off winner had the spiciest chili I've ever tasted.
