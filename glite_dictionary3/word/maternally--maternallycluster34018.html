maternally

adverb
in a motherly manner
Pronunciation: / məˈtɝːnəli / (AmE), / məˈtɜːnəli / (BrE)
In a way that is characteristic of or appropriate to a mother; in a motherly manner.

Examples

maternally caring for children
He was maternally protective of his younger siblings after their parents left.
She cared for the orphaned kitten almost maternally, tending to its every need.
The teacher spoke maternally to her students, offering comfort and support.

Related Terms

  • synonym – motherly: of, relating to, or characteristic of a mother
  • antonym – paternally: in a way characteristic of a father; in a fatherly manner
