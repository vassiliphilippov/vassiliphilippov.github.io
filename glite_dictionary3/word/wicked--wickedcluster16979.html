wicked

adverb
informal intensifier meaning 'very'
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪkɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪd / (BrE)
Informal (especially in North American English) used before an adjective or adverb to mean ‘very’ or ‘extremely’.
