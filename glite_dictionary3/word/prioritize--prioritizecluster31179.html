to prioritize

verb
arrange by importance
Pronunciation: / praɪˈɔːrɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / praɪˈɒrɪtəʌɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or deal with things in order of importance or urgency

Examples

to prioritize important tasks
It's important to prioritize your own well-being when making decisions.
The team will prioritize customer feedback in the next project phase.
We need to prioritize the most urgent tasks before starting anything else.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rank: to assign a position or order to
