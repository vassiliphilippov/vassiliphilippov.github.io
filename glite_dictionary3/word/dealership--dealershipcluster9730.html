dealership

noun
authorized seller of goods or services
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːlərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈdiːləʃɪp / (BrE)
a business authorized to sell or distribute a company's products or services in a particular area, often specializing in vehicles

Examples

car dealership
He owns a successful dealership that sells both new and used vehicles.
She bought her car from the local dealership last summer.
The dealership is offering special discounts on new SUVs this month.

  • synonym – franchise: business authorized to operate under a company's brand and regulations
