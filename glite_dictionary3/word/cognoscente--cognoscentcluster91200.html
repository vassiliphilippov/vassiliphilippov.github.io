cognoscente

noun
expert with refined appreciation, esp. arts
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊn.jəˈʃɛnteɪ / (AmE), / ˌkɒɡ.nɒˈʃɛnteɪ / (BrE)
A person who has expert knowledge and keen appreciation of a particular field, especially in the fine arts.

Examples

art cognoscente
As a true cognoscente of jazz, she noticed subtle details in the performance that others missed.
Only a cognoscente would recognize the rare painting's historical significance.
The exhibition drew many cognoscenti eager to view the artist's latest works.

Related Terms

  • synonym – connoisseur: A person with expert knowledge or refined taste, especially in food, wine, or the arts
