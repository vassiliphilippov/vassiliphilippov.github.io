hawker

noun
person who sells goods in public places
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔkər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who travels from place to place selling goods, especially in the street or at markets

Examples

market hawker
A hawker shouted out deals for souvenirs at the entrance to the fair.
In some cities, hawkers line the streets selling newspapers and snacks to commuters.
The hawker offered fresh fruit from his cart to passersby in the busy market.

Related Terms

  • synonym – peddler: someone who travels about selling goods
  • synonym – vendor: a person selling something, especially in the street
