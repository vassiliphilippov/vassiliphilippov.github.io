plummy

adjective
having a rich, affectedly refined voice
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmi / (AmE), / ˈplʌmi / (BrE)
(of a voice or accent) having a rich, mellow sound, often thought to be affected or characteristic of the upper class.

Examples

plummy accent
He adopted a plummy tone whenever he wanted to impress his colleagues.
Her plummy voice stood out during the auditions for its depth and refinement.
The radio host spoke with a plummy accent that made every word sound important.

Related Terms

  • synonym – posh: (of an accent or voice) elegant or characteristic of the upper class
