to flash

verb
cover with thin metal (construction)
Pronunciation: / flæʃ / (AmE), / flæʃ / (BrE)
to protect or cover (a roof, joint, or structure) with a thin sheet of metal, especially to prevent water from entering

Examples

to flash a roof
Before installing the shingles, the workers made sure to flash the roof corners to prevent leaks.
It is important to flash around chimneys to ensure proper waterproofing.
The builder hired an expert to flash all exposed joints on the new addition.
