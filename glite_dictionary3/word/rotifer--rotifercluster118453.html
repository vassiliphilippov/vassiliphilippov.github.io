rotifer

noun
microscopic aquatic animal; plankton constituent
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.t̬ə.fɚ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.tɪ.fə / (BrE)
a microscopic aquatic animal with a ciliated, wheel-like organ used for feeding and locomotion, commonly found in freshwater plankton
