short

adjective
(phonetics) of brief duration in sound
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt / (BrE)
(of a speech sound or syllable) pronounced for a relatively brief duration; not lengthened.

Examples

short vowel
Linguists categorize the English 'i' in 'pit' as a short vowel.
Many English words contain both short and long vowel sounds.
The teacher explained that the vowel in 'pat' is a short sound.
