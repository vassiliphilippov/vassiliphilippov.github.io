clout

noun
short nail with a flat head
Pronunciation: / klaʊt / (AmE), / klaʊt / (BrE)
A short nail with a broad, flat head, used especially for attaching sheet metal to wood.

Examples

a clout nail
He found a rusty clout among the old tools in the shed.
The roofer hammered a clout into the tin to secure it to the frame.
You will need a special clout to fasten this sheet metal properly.
