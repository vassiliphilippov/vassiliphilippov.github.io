fantasy

noun
imagination divorced from reality
Pronunciation: / ˈfæn.tə.si / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tə.si / (BrE)
the faculty or activity of imagining things, especially things that are impossible or unlikely in reality

Examples

wild fantasy

He escapes the stresses of daily life through fantasy and storytelling.
