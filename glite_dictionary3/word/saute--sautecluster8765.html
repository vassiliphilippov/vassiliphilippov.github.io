to saute

verb
fry quickly in a little fat
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈteɪ / (AmE), / sɔːˈteɪ / (BrE)
To fry food quickly in a small amount of fat over relatively high heat.

Examples

to saute vegetables
First, saute the garlic and onions until they are golden brown.
She likes to saute her vegetables with olive oil for extra flavor.
The recipe says to saute the mushrooms for three minutes before adding the cream.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fry: to cook food in hot fat or oil, typically in a shallow pan
