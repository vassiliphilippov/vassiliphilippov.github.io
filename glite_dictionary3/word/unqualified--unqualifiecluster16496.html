unqualified

adjective
not limited or restricted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwɑːləˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɒlɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not limited or restricted; without reservation or qualification

Examples

unqualified support
His statement was an unqualified denial of any wrongdoing.
She received unqualified praise for her performance in the play.
The plan was met with unqualified support from the entire team.

Related Terms

  • synonym – absolute: not qualified or diminished in any way; total
  • antonym – conditional: subject to one or more conditions or requirements being met; made or granted on certain terms
