self-love

noun
excessive pride in oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈlʌv / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈlʌv / (BrE)
Excessive pride or admiration for oneself; an inordinate regard for one's own abilities or appearance.

Examples

excessive self-love pride
Her self-love made it difficult for her to accept any criticism, no matter how constructive.
The leader's self-love was evident in every speech he gave about his achievements.
Too much self-love can alienate friends and colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym – egotism: the practice of talking and thinking about oneself excessively because of an undue sense of self-importance
  • antonym – humility: a modest view of one's own importance; humbleness
