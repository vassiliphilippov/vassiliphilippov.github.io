creeper

noun
a plant that grows by creeping
Pronunciation: / ˈkripər / (AmE), / ˈkriːpə / (BrE)
a plant that grows along the ground, up walls, or over other surfaces by extending stems or runners

Examples

wall creeper plant
English ivy is a common creeper found in many gardens and parks.
The creeper quickly covered the old stone wall with its lush green leaves.
We planted a creeper along the fence to add some natural beauty to the yard.
