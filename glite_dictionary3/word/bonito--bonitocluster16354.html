bonito

noun
a food fish related to tuna, used in Japanese cuisine
Pronunciation: / bəˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / bəˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
a medium-sized marine fish of the mackerel family, related to tuna, found in warm seas
