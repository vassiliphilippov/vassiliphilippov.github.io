emergent

adjective
coming into existence
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɝːdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
In the process of coming into being or becoming important.

Examples

emergent technology
Several emergent trends are shaping the future of education.
The emergent company quickly gained a reputation for innovation.
The country is an emergent power in the technology industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym – established: having been in existence for a long time and therefore recognized and generally accepted
  • synonym – nascent: just coming into existence and beginning to display signs of future potential
