caroller

noun
singer of carols (esp. Christmas)
Pronunciation: / ˈkærələr / (AmE), / ˈkærələ / (BrE)
A person who sings carols, especially Christmas carols, usually as part of a group.

Examples

Christmas caroller
As caroller costumes go, theirs were especially festive and bright.
Every Christmas Eve, a group of carollers visits our neighborhood and fills the air with song.
The tradition of carollers singing door to door is still alive in many communities.
