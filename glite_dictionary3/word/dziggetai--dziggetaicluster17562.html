dziggetai

noun
Mongolian wild ass (animal)
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈɡɛtaɪ / (AmE), / dziˈɡɛtaɪ / (BrE)
A subspecies of the Asiatic wild ass native to Mongolia, known for its reddish-brown coat and white belly.
