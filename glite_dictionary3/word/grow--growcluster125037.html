to grow

verb
to become, or pass into a state
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊ / (AmE), / ɡrəʊ / (BrE)
to gradually become, or come to be, something; to pass into a particular state or condition

Examples

to grow tired
As the meeting continued, he grew impatient with the lack of progress.
She grew nervous before her big presentation.
The sky grew darker as the storm approached.

Related Terms

  • synonym – become: to begin to be; to undergo a change or development
  • antonym – remain: to continue to be in the same state
