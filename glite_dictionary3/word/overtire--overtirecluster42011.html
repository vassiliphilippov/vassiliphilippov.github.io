to overtire

verb
make excessively tired
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtaɪə / (BrE)
to make (someone) excessively tired; to exhaust through overexertion or lack of rest

Examples

to overtire a child
If you overtire the children with too many activities, they may get cranky.
It's important not to overtire your mind before an important exam.
She tried not to overtire herself while recovering from illness.
