ridgling

noun
male horse with undescended testicle(s)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdʒ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪd.lɪŋ / (BrE)
A male horse, especially a colt, in which one or both testicles have not descended into the scrotum.

Examples

young ridgling horse
Breeders may avoid using a ridgling for stud purposes due to fertility concerns.
During examination, the trainer realized one of the colts was actually a ridgling.
The vet diagnosed the young horse as a ridgling, explaining the lack of visible testicles.
