consignment

noun
batch of goods sent or delivered
Pronunciation: / kənˈsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / kənˈsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
a batch of goods that are sent or delivered, especially by a large vehicle such as a truck or ship, typically for sale or storage

Examples

consignment of goods
The truck arrived with a consignment of fresh produce from the farm.
They inspected the entire consignment before signing for delivery.
This consignment required special handling because of its fragile contents.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shipment: act of sending goods, or the goods sent
