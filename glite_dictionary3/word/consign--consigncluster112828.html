to consign

verb
give over for care or safekeeping
Pronunciation: / kənˈsaɪn / (AmE), / kənˈsaɪn / (BrE)
to give something over to another person, organization, or place for care, safekeeping, or custody

Examples

to consign valuables for safekeeping
Before the trip, please consign your valuables to the hotel safe.
He decided to consign his childhood toys to a trusted friend.
The old documents were consigned to the archives for preservation.
