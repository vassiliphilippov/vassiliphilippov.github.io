Phoronidea

noun
phylum of wormlike marine animals
Pronunciation: / foʊˌroʊnɪˈdiːə / (AmE), / fəˌrɒnɪˈdiːə / (BrE)
A small phylum of wormlike marine animals characterized by a tubular structure and a crown of tentacles surrounding the mouth.
