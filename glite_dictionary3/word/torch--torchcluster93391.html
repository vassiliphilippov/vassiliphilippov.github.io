torch

noun
small portable electric lamp
Pronunciation: / tɔrtʃ / (AmE), / tɔːtʃ / (BrE)
A small portable electric lamp powered by batteries, used for providing light; called a flashlight in American English.

Examples

torch with batteries
He always keeps a spare set of batteries for his torch in the kitchen drawer.
She grabbed her torch before heading into the dark basement.
When the power went out, we used a torch to find our way.
