largeness

noun
breadth or scope of understanding
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑrdʒ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːdʒ.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having a broad scope of understanding or an expansive mind

Examples

largeness of mind
He approached every issue with great largeness of mind, never getting bogged down in petty details.
The largeness of her understanding made her an invaluable advisor to many.
True leadership requires both vision and largeness of perspective.
