to assert

verb
to insist on one's opinions or rights
Pronunciation: / əˈsɝt /; / əˈsɜrt /; / əˈsɝːt / (AmE), / əˈsɜːt / (BrE)
to state or declare something confidently and firmly, or to insist on having your opinions or rights recognized

Related Terms

  • synonym – declare: to say something officially or emphatically
