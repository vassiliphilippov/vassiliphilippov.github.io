honkytonk

noun
cheap nightclub or bar
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑŋkiˌtɑŋk / (AmE), / ˈhɒŋkitɒŋk / (BrE)
a cheap or disreputable bar, nightclub, or dance hall, especially one that features country music and dancing

Examples

a lively honkytonk bar
Many classic American songs reference the lively atmosphere of a honkytonk.
She spent Friday evenings dancing at a local honkytonk with her friends.
The band played country music all night at the honkytonk downtown.

  • synonym – nightclub: an establishment for late-night entertainment, usually serving drinks and having music and dancing
