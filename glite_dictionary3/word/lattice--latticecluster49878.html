lattice

noun
small gridded opening for transactions
Pronunciation: / ˈlætɪs /; / ˈlæt̬.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈlætɪs /; / ˈlæt.ɪs / (BrE)
a structure made of strips of material crossing each other in a regular, crisscrossed pattern, often used as a framework or decoration
