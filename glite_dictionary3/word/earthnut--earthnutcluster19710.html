earthnut

noun
European plant with edible tubers
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈɜːθ.nʌt / (BrE)
a common European plant (Conopodium majus) with edible tubers that taste like roasted chestnuts

Examples

earthnut wild tuber
She tasted the roasted earthnut and was surprised by its nutty flavor.
The forager eagerly dug up an earthnut from the soft woodland soil.
Traditional recipes often use earthnuts as a flavorful substitute for chestnuts.
