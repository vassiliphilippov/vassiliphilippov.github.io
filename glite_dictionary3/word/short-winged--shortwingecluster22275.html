short-winged

adjective
having very short or undeveloped wings
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtˌwɪŋd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtˌwɪŋd / (BrE)
(especially of certain insects) having very short or undeveloped wings.

Examples

short-winged insect
Because it is short-winged, this species of moth cannot fly long distances.
Many cave-dwelling insects are short-winged or completely wingless.
