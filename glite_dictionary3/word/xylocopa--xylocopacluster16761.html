Xylocopa

noun
genus of carpenter bees
Pronunciation: / zaɪˈlɑkəpə / (AmE), / zaɪˈlɒkəpə / (BrE)
A genus of large bees commonly known as carpenter bees, noted for their habit of boring into wood to make nests.

Examples
