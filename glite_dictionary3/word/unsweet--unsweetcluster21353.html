unsweet

adjective
moderately dry (esp. of champagne)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswiːt / (AmE), / ʌnˈswiːt / (BrE)
(of champagne or other wines) moderately dry; not sweet in taste.

Examples

unsweet wine
I enjoy choosing a slightly unsweet wine for dinner parties.
The sommelier recommended an unsweet sparkling wine to accompany the seafood.
This vintage is an unsweet champagne ideal for those who prefer less sugar.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dry: containing little or no sweetness, especially of wine or champagne
  • antonym – sweet: having a sugary taste; not dry, especially of wine
