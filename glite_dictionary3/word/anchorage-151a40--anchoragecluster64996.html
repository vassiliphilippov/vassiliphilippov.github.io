anchorage

noun
fee for anchoring in a harbor
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæŋkərɪdʒ / (BrE)
a fee charged for the right to anchor a ship in a particular place, especially in a port or harbor

Examples
