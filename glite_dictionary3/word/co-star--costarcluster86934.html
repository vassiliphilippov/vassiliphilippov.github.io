co-star

noun
an actor with equal starring status
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ stɑr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ stɑː / (BrE)
one of two or more actors who have equal importance in a play, film, or television show

Examples

co-star in a movie
At the award ceremony, the director praised both co-stars for their performances.

The two actors are co-stars in the new romantic comedy.
