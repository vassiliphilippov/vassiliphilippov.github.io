arteria testicularis

noun
artery supplying the testicles
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪəriə tɛˌstɪkjʊˈlɛərɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə tɛˌstɪkjʊˈlæːrɪs / (BrE)
the artery that branches from the aorta to supply blood to the testicles

Examples
