venue

noun
place where an event occurs
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnju / (AmE), / ˈvɛnjuː / (BrE)
A place where an event or meeting takes place, especially a public one such as a concert, sports game, or conference.

Examples

venue for a concert
Due to the rain, the wedding venue was changed at the last minute.
The band will perform at a larger venue next year to accommodate more fans.
The conference organizers were searching for the perfect venue downtown.
