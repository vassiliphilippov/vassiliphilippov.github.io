to impanel

verb
enroll people, especially jurors, onto a panel
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpænəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpæn(ə)l /; / ɪmˈpænəl / (BrE)
to officially select and enroll people to serve as members of a jury
