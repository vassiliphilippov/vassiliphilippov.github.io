shoveler

noun
person who shovels for work
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌvələr / (AmE), / ˈʃʌvələ / (BrE)
a person whose job is to move, dig, or clear material using a shovel

Examples

construction shoveler
Each shoveler on the construction site wore protective gear while moving gravel.
He was employed as a shoveler in the coal mines during the winter.
The shoveler worked tirelessly to clear the driveway after the heavy snowfall.
