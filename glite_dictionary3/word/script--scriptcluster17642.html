script

noun
written text for a play, film, or broadcast
Pronunciation: / skrɪpt / (AmE), / skrɪpt / (BrE)
the written text of a play, film, or broadcast, used as a guide for actors or performers

Examples

script for a play

She wrote the entire script for the new television series herself.
The actors spent weeks memorizing their script before opening night.
