wigeon

noun
a freshwater duck of Eurasia and northern Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈwɪdʒən / (BrE)
A freshwater duck found in Eurasia and northern Africa, related to mallards and teals, known for its distinctive plumage and whistling call.
