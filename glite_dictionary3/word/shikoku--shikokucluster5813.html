Shikoku

proper noun
smallest main island of Japan
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈkoʊku / (AmE), / ʃɪˈkəʊku / (BrE)
the smallest of the four main islands of Japan, located to the south of Honshu and east of Kyushu, noted for its forests and mountains
