Wilhelm Grimm

proper noun
German folklorist and fairy tale collector
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪl.hɛlm ˈɡrɪm / (AmE), / ˈvɪl.hɛlm ˈɡrɪm / (BrE)
(1786–1859) The younger of the two German brothers known for collecting and publishing folk and fairy tales.
