sensuousness

noun
quality of pleasing the senses
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnʃuəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnʃuːəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being pleasing or appealing to the senses, especially in a physical or aesthetic way.

Examples

the sensuousness of art
She admired the sensuousness of the sculpture's curves and textures.
The sensuousness of the music transported the audience to another world.
There was a sensuousness to the food that made every bite memorable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sensuality: the enjoyment or pursuit of physical pleasure, particularly sexual pleasure
