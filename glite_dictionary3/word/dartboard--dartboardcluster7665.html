dartboard

noun
circular target board for darts
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrtˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdɑːtˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A circular board, typically of wood or cork, used as the target in the game of darts.

Examples

dartboard on the wall
He hung a dartboard on the wall in his garage for practice.
She scored a bullseye in the center of the dartboard.
The pub has a dartboard and organizes weekly tournaments.
