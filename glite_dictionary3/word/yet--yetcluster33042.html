yet

adverb
used after superlatives for emphasis
Pronunciation: / jɛt / (AmE), / jɛt / (BrE)
used after a superlative adjective or adverb to emphasize that something is the most or best so far

Examples

the best yet
It was the most successful concert yet for the band.
The test results are the highest yet recorded in the lab.
This is their darkest movie yet.
