discouragingly

adverb
in a discouraging manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskɝːrɪdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / dɪsˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm; in a way that discourages.

Examples

discouragingly low results
Enrollment numbers have dropped discouragingly since last year despite the school's efforts.
She looked at the unfinished work discouragingly, feeling overwhelmed by the amount left to do.
The team's performance declined discouragingly after the loss of their captain.

Related Terms

  • antonym – encouragingly: in a manner that gives support or confidence
