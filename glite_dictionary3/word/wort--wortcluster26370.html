wort

noun
unfermented liquid in brewing
Pronunciation: / wɝt / (AmE), / wɜːt / (BrE)
a sweet liquid extracted from malted grains, used in brewing before fermentation occurs

Examples

brewing wort liquid
A cloudy wort can affect the clarity of the finished beer.
After the grains are mashed, the resulting wort is transferred to the fermentation vessel.
