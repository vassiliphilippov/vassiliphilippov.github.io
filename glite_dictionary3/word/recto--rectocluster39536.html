recto

noun
right-hand page of an open book
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɛktəʊ / (BrE)
the right-hand page of an open book or the front of a single sheet in a printed work, usually bearing an odd page number

Examples

recto page illustration
On the recto page, you'll find a beautiful illustration of the cathedral.
Please refer to the recto of the next leaf for the continuation of the text.
The numbering begins on the recto and continues on each odd page.

  • antonym – verso: the left-hand page of an open book or the back of a printed sheet
