Leonardo

proper noun
Leonardo da Vinci, Italian Renaissance genius
Pronunciation: / liˈɑːrnəˌdoʊ / (AmE), / liˈɒːnədəʊ / (BrE)
Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519), Italian painter, sculptor, engineer, scientist, and architect, renowned as a preeminent genius of the Italian Renaissance.
