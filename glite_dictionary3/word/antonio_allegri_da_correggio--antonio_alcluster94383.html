Antonio Allegri da Correggio

proper noun
Italian Renaissance painter
Pronunciation: / ænˌtoʊniˌoʊ əˈlɛgri dɑ koʊˈreɪdʒioʊ / (AmE), / anˌtɔːnɪˌəʊ əˈlɛgri dɑ kəˈrɛdʒiəʊ / (BrE)
