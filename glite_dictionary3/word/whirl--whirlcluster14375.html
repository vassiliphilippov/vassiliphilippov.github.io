to whirl

verb
turn or move rapidly in circles
Pronunciation: / wɜrl / (AmE), / wɜːl / (BrE)
to turn or move rapidly in a circular or twisting motion

Examples

thoughts whirl quickly
She felt her thoughts whirl as she tried to absorb the news.
The children laughed as they whirled around on the playground carousel.
The wind began to whirl the fallen leaves across the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spin: to turn around and around quickly
  • antonym – still: not moving, motionless
  • synonym – twirl: to spin or cause to spin quickly
