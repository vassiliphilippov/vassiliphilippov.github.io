uncivilly

adverb
rudely; without courtesy
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪvəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪvəli / (BrE)
In a rude or impolite manner; without courtesy or respect.

Examples

to speak uncivilly to others
Children should be taught not to act uncivilly to their elders.
Despite her anger, she refused to behave uncivilly towards her colleagues.
He spoke uncivilly to the server when his order was wrong.
