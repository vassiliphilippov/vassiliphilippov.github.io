Masa

noun
a Chadic language group of Central Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːsə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːsə / (BrE)
an independent group of closely related Chadic languages spoken in the region between the Biu-Mandara and East Chadic language groups, primarily in Chad and Cameroon.
