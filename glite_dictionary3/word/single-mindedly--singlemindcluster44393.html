single-mindedly

adverb
with unwavering focus or determination
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋɡəlˈmaɪndɪdli / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡəlˈmaɪndɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows concentration on only one purpose, aim, or goal, without being distracted by other concerns.

Examples

single-mindedly pursue a goal
He single-mindedly focused on completing the project ahead of schedule.
She pursued her career single-mindedly, never allowing distractions to stand in her way.
The team worked single-mindedly towards winning the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym – determinedly: in a determined manner
  • synonym – single-minded: having or showing a single aim or purpose
