vascularization

noun
development of blood vessels in tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌvæskjələrɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvæskjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which body tissue develops blood vessels and capillaries
