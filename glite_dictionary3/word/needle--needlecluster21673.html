needle

noun
sharp pointed sewing implement
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈniːdl̩ / (BrE)
a small, slender, sharp-pointed implement, typically of steel, used for sewing, knitting, or similar purposes

Examples

sewing needle
Knitting with a fine needle requires steady hands.
She threaded the needle carefully before starting to sew the button.
The doctor used a sterile needle to give the injection.
