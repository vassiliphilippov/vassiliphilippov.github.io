flat

noun
musical symbol for a lower note
Pronunciation: / flæt / (AmE), / flæt / (BrE)
a symbol (♭) used in musical notation to indicate that the note should be played one half step lower than its natural pitch

Examples

music flat symbol
A flat lowers the pitch of the note by a semitone.
Make sure to play each flat exactly as written in the music.
The key signature contains two flats, indicating the piece is in B-flat major.
