one-dimensionality

noun
the property of having one dimension
Pronunciation: / ˌwʌn.dɪˌmɛnʃəˈnæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌwʌn.dɪˌmɛn.ʃəˈnæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality or state of having only one dimension, as in length, without width or height.
