localized

adjective
restricted to a specific location
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəlaɪzd / (BrE)
restricted or limited to a specific area or place

Examples

localized pain in shoulder

The infection remained localized in his left arm and did not spread.
