revival

noun
the act of bringing back into activity
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvaɪvəl / (AmE), / rɪˈvaɪvəl / (BrE)
an instance of something becoming active, important, or popular again after a period of decline

Examples

a revival of interest
The city saw a cultural revival as galleries and music venues opened downtown.
The theater company announced a revival of Shakespeare's play for the new season.
There has been a revival of interest in classic literature.

Related Terms

  • antonym – decline: the process of becoming weaker or less active
  • synonym – resurgence: an increase or revival after a period of little activity
