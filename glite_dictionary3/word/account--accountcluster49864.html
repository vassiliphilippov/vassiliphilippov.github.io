to account

verb
keep a record of; record transactions
Pronunciation: / əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
to keep a record of financial or other transactions, or to give a report or description of something

Examples

The witness was asked to account for what happened that night.
