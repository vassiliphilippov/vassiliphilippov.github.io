oca

noun
South American tuberous plant
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.kə / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.kə / (BrE)
A South American plant of the wood sorrel family, cultivated for its small edible tubers, which are a staple food in parts of the Andes.
