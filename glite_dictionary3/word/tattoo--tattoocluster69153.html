to tattoo

verb
mark the skin permanently with a design
Pronunciation: / təˈtuː / (AmE), / təˈtuː / (BrE)
to mark the skin with an indelible design by inserting pigment into the dermis

Examples

to tattoo a person
He has tattooed hundreds of people throughout his career.
She decided to tattoo a small butterfly on her ankle.
Some artists refuse to tattoo minors without parental consent.
