to harmonize

verb
bring into agreement or harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənaɪz / (BrE)
to bring things into agreement or make them compatible; to be suitable or pleasing together

to harmonize different ideas

The new policy aims to harmonize regulations across all EU countries.

The modern furniture does not harmonize with the old-fashioned décor of the room.
The plan must harmonize with the company's overall strategy to succeed.

The new manager helped harmonize the team members' diverse working styles.
