bouquet

noun
distinctive scent, especially of wine or perfume
Pronunciation: / boʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / buːˈkeɪ / (BrE)
a distinct and pleasant scent, especially the characteristic fragrance of wine or perfume

Examples

bouquet of wine
A complex bouquet filled the kitchen as the spices simmered.
She chose a perfume for its delicate floral bouquet.
The wine's bouquet was rich with notes of cherry and oak.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aroma: a distinctive, typically pleasant smell
  • synonym – odor: a particular smell, especially one that is distinctive
  • antonym – stench: a strong and very unpleasant smell
