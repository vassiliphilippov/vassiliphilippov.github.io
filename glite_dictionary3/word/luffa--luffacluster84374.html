luffa

noun
dried fruit fiber used as sponge
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌfə / (AmE), / ˈlʌfə / (BrE)
The dried fibrous part of the fruit of a plant of the genus Luffa, used as a natural sponge or strainer, especially for washing the body.

Examples

luffa sponge for bathing
After removing the seeds, the dried luffa can be cut into pieces for household cleaning.
Natural luffa sponges are often preferred over synthetic ones for exfoliating the skin.
She used a luffa to scrub her arms and back in the shower.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sponge: a porous material used for cleaning, often synthetic or from sea animals
