scatterbrain

noun
disorganized, forgetful person
Pronunciation: / ˈskæt̬ərˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈskætəˌbreɪn / (BrE)
a person who is forgetful, disorganized, or unable to concentrate

Examples

Don't leave the planning to him—he's too much of a scatterbrain.
I'm such a scatterbrain that I forgot my keys again this morning.
