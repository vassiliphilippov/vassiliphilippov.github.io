to whirlpool

verb
flow in a rapid, circular motion
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːlˌpuːl / (AmE), / ˈwɜːlˌpuːl / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to move or flow in a rapid, circular motion like that of a whirlpool.

Examples

to whirlpool down the drain
As the bath drained, I watched the last of the soap bubbles whirlpool away.
Colored dye was poured into the water so we could watch it whirlpool down the drain.
The river would whirlpool around the rocks after a heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym – eddy: to move in a circular motion, especially of water or air
