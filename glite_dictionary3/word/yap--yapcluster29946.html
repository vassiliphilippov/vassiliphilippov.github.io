to yap

verb
bark in a high-pitched tone
Pronunciation: / jæp / (AmE), / jæp / (BrE)
(of a dog) to bark in a sharp, high-pitched tone, typically in a repetitive or shrill manner.

Examples

puppies yap loudly
At night, the puppies would yap until their mother returned to the kennel.
The small dog began to yap loudly every time someone walked by the window.
The terrier yapped excitedly as it chased its tail in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym – yelp: to give a sharp, high-pitched bark or cry
