bandage

noun
material covering for an injury
Pronunciation: / ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
A piece of soft material used to cover and protect a wound or injured part of the body.

Examples

bandage on a wound
After the surgery, the nurse changed the bandage on her knee every day.
He wrapped a clean bandage around his injured hand to stop the bleeding.
Make sure the bandage is not too tight, or it might cut off circulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dressing: a piece of material applied to a wound for healing or protection
