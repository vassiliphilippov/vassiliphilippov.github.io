spokeswoman

noun
female representative or speaker
Pronunciation: / ˈspoʊksˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈspəʊksˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who speaks on behalf of a group, organization, or another person.

Examples

spokeswoman for the company
As spokeswoman for the school board, she addressed the concerns of parents and teachers.
During the press conference, the spokeswoman answered all the reporters’ questions clearly.
The company appointed a new spokeswoman to handle public relations.

Related Terms

  • antonym – spokesman: a man who speaks on behalf of others
  • synonym – spokesperson: a person who speaks on behalf of others, gender neutral
