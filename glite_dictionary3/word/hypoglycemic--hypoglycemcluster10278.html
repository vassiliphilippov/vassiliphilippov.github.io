hypoglycemic

adjective
relating to low blood sugar
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊɡlaɪˈsiːmɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəʊɡlʌɪˈsiːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or having hypoglycemia (an abnormally low level of glucose in the blood)

Examples

hypoglycemic patient
Doctors often prescribe hypoglycemic agents to manage diabetes.
She felt dizzy and faint due to her hypoglycemic condition.
The patient experienced a hypoglycemic episode after skipping breakfast.
