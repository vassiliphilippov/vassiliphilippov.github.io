herring

noun
edible flesh of a small fish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
a small, silver fish found in northern Atlantic and Pacific waters, often eaten as food (fresh, smoked, salted, or pickled)
