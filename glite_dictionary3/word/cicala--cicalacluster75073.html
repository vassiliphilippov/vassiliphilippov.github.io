cicala

noun
insect producing a loud, droning sound
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkɑːlə / (AmE), / sɪˈkɑːlə / (BrE)
A stout-bodied insect with large transparent wings, whose male produces a high-pitched droning sound; variant of cicada.

Examples

singing cicala
A single cicala can produce a remarkably loud noise, especially at dusk.
In Mediterranean countries, the cicala is associated with hot, sunny days.
The sound of the cicala filled the summer evening air.
