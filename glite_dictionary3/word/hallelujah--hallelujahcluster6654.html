hallelujah

noun
song or exclamation of praise to God
Pronunciation: / ˌhælɪˈluːjə / (AmE), / ˌhælɪˈluːjə / (BrE)
An exclamation or song expressing praise or thanks to God, often used in religious worship.

Examples

sing hallelujah loudly
A burst of hallelujah filled the church during the Easter service.
The choir sang a joyful hallelujah as the congregation stood in reverence.
With a loud hallelujah, he expressed his gratitude for the good news.
