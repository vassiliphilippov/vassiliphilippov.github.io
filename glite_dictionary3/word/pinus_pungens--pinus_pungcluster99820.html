Pinus pungens

noun
a pine tree species native to the eastern U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.nəs ˈpʌn.dʒɛnz / (AmE), / ˈpʌɪ.nəs ˈpʌn.dʒɛnz / (BrE)
A small pine tree native to the eastern United States (Appalachians), characterized by two needle-like leaves per bundle, dark brown flaking bark, and cones with thorn-tipped scales.
