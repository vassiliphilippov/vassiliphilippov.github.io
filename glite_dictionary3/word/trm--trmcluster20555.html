TRM

noun
UTC time a transmission is sent to a spacecraft
Pronunciation: / ti ɑr ɛm / (AmE), / ti ɑː ɛm / (BrE)
the Coordinated Universal Time at which a transmission is sent from Earth to a spacecraft or other celestial body.

Examples

TRM of a signal
All mission logs must record the exact TRM for each uplinked command.
Engineers compared the TRM of different transmissions to calculate the distance to the spacecraft.
The signal was sent at TRM 12:45:30, ensuring precise tracking of the probe's location.
