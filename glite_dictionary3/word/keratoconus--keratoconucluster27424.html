keratoconus

noun
eye condition where cornea bulges outward
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛrətoʊˈkoʊnəs / (AmE), / ˌkɛrətəˈkəʊnəs / (BrE)
A degenerative condition of the eye in which the cornea becomes progressively thinner and bulges into a cone-like shape, leading to vision distortion.
