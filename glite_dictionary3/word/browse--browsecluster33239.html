browse

noun
quick or casual reading
Pronunciation: / braʊz / (AmE), / braʊz / (BrE)
an act of reading quickly or casually, especially without a definite plan or focus

Examples

a quick browse
A browse of the magazine gave him some new recipe ideas.
She had a quick browse through the bookstore before meeting her friends.
The website allows users to have a browse and discover interesting articles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scan: to look through something quickly
  • synonym – skim: to read something quickly to get a general idea
