stereotypic

adjective
lacking originality or individuality
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛriəˈtɪpɪk / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəˈtɪpɪk / (BrE)
Lacking originality, spontaneity, or individuality; typical in a way that is uninspired or formulaic.

Examples

stereotypic behavior
Her speech was filled with stereotypic expressions rather than personal insights.
His interviews often sound stereotypic and rehearsed, lacking genuine spontaneity.
The movie relied on stereotypic characters instead of developing unique personalities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – original: fresh and not copied; creative
  • synonym – stereotyped: lacking originality or individuality; not spontaneous
