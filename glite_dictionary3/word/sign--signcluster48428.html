to sign

verb
put up or display signs (esp. along roads)
Pronunciation: / saɪn / (AmE), / saɪn / (BrE)
to put up or display signs to provide information or instructions, especially along roads or at places where guidance is needed

Examples

to sign a road
All hiking trails in the park are clearly signed to help visitors stay on the correct path.
Authorities will sign the new exit before the road opens to the public.
The city recently signed several dangerous intersections to improve road safety.
