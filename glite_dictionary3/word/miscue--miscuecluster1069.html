miscue

noun
minor unintended mistake
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌkju / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.kjuː / (BrE)
a minor and unintentional mistake, especially one made in speaking, writing, or performing an action

Examples

miscue during speech
His only miscue in the interview was forgetting one date, but overall he impressed the panel.
She laughed off the miscue when she called her colleague by the wrong name.
The speaker made a small miscue during his presentation but quickly recovered.
