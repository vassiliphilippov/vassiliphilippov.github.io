selfishness

noun
quality of being self-centered or unconcerned for others
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.fɪʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.fɪʃ.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being concerned only with one’s own advantage or welfare, with little or no regard for others.

Examples

show selfishness in teamwork
He was criticized for his selfishness during the group project, as he only cared about his own part.
Her selfishness was evident when she refused to share her food with anyone else.
Many friendships are ruined by selfishness and lack of empathy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – greed: an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or power
  • antonym – selflessness: the quality of caring more about other people than about yourself
