facelift

noun
superficial renovation or improvement
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪslɪft /; / ˈfeɪsˌlɪft / (AmE), / ˈfeɪslɪft / (BrE)
an improvement or renovation that makes something, especially a building, look newer or more attractive without major changes

Examples

facelift for building
Our office building is getting a facelift with a fresh coat of paint and new windows.
The company hoped that a product facelift would boost sales without changing its core features.
The historic theater received a much-needed facelift to restore its original charm.
After a quick facelift, the old café looked modern and welcoming again.
The company hopes that a major facelift of its software will attract new users.
The library is set to receive a much-needed facelift next spring.

  • synonym – renovation: the act of making changes and repairs to improve something
