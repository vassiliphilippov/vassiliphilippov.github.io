destination

noun
the place someone is going to
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛstəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the place to which someone or something is going or being sent, especially as the end of a journey

Examples

holiday destination
After a long flight, we finally reached our destination in Paris.
Make sure your luggage is labeled with your destination to avoid any confusion.
The train's final destination is the central station downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym – goal: an aim or desired result, often figuratively used for destination
  • antonym – origin: the place where something starts or begins, the opposite of destination
