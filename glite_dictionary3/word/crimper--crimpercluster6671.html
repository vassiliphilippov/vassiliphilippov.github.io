crimper

noun
device for curling hair
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmpɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmpə / (BrE)
a device consisting of a cylindrical tube around which hair is wound to curl it

Examples

hair crimper
I borrowed my sister's crimper to style my hair for the school dance.
She carefully placed each section of hair into the crimper before getting ready for her party.
Using a crimper can give your hair tight, bouncy curls in just a few minutes.

  • synonym – curler: a device for curling hair, often cylindrical
