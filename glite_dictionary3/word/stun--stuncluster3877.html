to stun

verb
to shock or greatly surprise
Pronunciation: / stʌn / (AmE), / stʌn / (BrE)
to shock or greatly surprise someone so that they are temporarily unable to react

Examples

to stun the crowd
Her unexpected resignation stunned the entire team.
I was stunned by the beauty of the landscape when I first saw it.
The sudden announcement stunned everyone in the office into silence.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amaze: to surprise greatly; fill with wonder
  • synonym – astound: to shock or greatly surprise
  • antonym – bore: to make someone feel uninterested or unsuprised
