invigorator

noun
something that gives energy or strength
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪɡəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives energy, strength, or renewed vitality

Examples

natural invigorator
A morning run acts as a natural invigorator that prepares me for the day's challenges.
Many athletes rely on coffee as an invigorator before training sessions.
The spa treatment was a true invigorator, leaving her feeling refreshed and renewed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – energizer: something that gives energy, especially as a product or device
  • antonym – enervator: something that weakens or drains energy
  • synonym – stimulant: a substance that raises levels of physiological or nervous activity
