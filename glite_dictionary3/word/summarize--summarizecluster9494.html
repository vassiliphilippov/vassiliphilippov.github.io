to summarize

verb
compute summary statistics
Pronunciation: / c8s8cm59ccra6az / (AmE), / c8s8cm59ra6az / (BrE)
(in statistics and data analysis) to compute and present concise numerical descriptions of a dataset, such as mean, median, counts, or standard deviation.

Examples

to summarize data
The software can summarize the dataset by reporting mean, median, and standard deviation.
After cleaning, she summarized each group's responses with counts and percentages for the report.
In R, the summary() function quickly summarizes numeric columns to show distribution and central tendency.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aggregate: to combine or compute values across records in a dataset to produce summary measures (e.g., sums, means).
  • synonym – describe: (in statistics) to provide numerical summaries that describe the main features of a dataset.
