sacred

adjective
devoted exclusively to something
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.krɪd / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.krɪd / (BrE)
devoted exclusively to a particular use, purpose, or person

Examples

sacred time
Every Sunday morning, an hour was sacred to family breakfast.
In the museum, one gallery is sacred to works by local artists.
The quiet room was sacred to her writing and no one else entered.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dedicated: exclusively allocated or set aside for a particular purpose
  • antonym – profane: not devoted to a special purpose; ordinary or secular
