velvetiness

noun
the quality of being velvety
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlvəti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvəti.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being velvety; the smooth, soft, or rich appearance, feel, or taste reminiscent of velvet.

Examples

the velvetiness of chocolate
She adored the velvetiness of the chocolate mousse, which melted on her tongue.
The velvetiness of the fabric made the dress especially luxurious to touch.
The wine's remarkable velvetiness distinguished it from others in the tasting.

  • antonym – roughness: the quality of being rough, not smooth
