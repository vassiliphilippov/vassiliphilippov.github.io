hookup

noun
device for utility connection
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈhʊk.ʌp / (BrE)
a device or arrangement that provides a connection between a power source or utility and a user, especially at a campsite or similar location

Examples

campsite hookup
Many parks provide only basic hookups, so plan accordingly.
The campsite offers an electrical hookup for each RV spot.
We need a water hookup if we're going to use the shower in the trailer.
