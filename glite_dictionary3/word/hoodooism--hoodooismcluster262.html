hoodooism

noun
folk religion/magical practice (Caribbean, US)
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːduːˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhuːduːˌɪzəm / (BrE)
a religion or set of magical practices rooted in African and Caribbean traditions, often associated with folk magic, witchcraft, and animistic beliefs, especially in Haiti and the southern United States

Examples

hoodooism rituals
He claimed that his good luck was the result of hoodooism practiced by his grandmother.
Many immigrants brought their traditional beliefs in hoodooism with them to the United States.
The novel explores the cultural significance of hoodooism in the southern states.
