satiation

noun
the state of being fully satisfied
Pronunciation: / ˌseɪʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌseɪsɪˈeɪʃən /; / ˌseɪʃɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the state of being fully satisfied, especially after eating or having a desire completely fulfilled
