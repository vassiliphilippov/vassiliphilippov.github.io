opposition

noun
person or group you compete against
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːpəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
the person or team you are competing against in a contest, game, or election

Examples

face strong opposition
Despite fierce opposition, the candidate won by a narrow margin.
She studied her opposition carefully before the chess tournament.
The team faced strong opposition in the championship game.

Related Terms

  • synonym – competitor: a person or group that competes against another
  • synonym – rival: a person competing with another for the same objective
  • antonym – supporter: a person who supports or is in favor of someone
