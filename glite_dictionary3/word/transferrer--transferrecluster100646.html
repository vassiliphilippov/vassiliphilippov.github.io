transferrer

noun
person who transfers something
Pronunciation: / trænˈsfɜrər / (AmE), / trɑːnsˈfɜːrə / (BrE)
a person or organization that transfers something to another, especially property, rights, or responsibility.

Examples

transferrer of property
As the transferrer of the property, she ensured all legal steps were taken.
Proper identification is required of the transferrer during the transaction.
The transferrer must sign the document before the title changes hands.

Related Terms

  • antonym – transferee: person to whom something is transferred
  • synonym – transferor: person who transfers title or rights, especially in legal contexts
