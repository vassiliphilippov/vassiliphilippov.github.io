feller

noun
person who cuts down trees
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛlər / (AmE), / ˈfelə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut down trees, especially as part of logging operations

Examples

skilled feller
Each feller must be trained to handle chainsaws safely and efficiently.
For years, he worked as a feller deep in the northern forests.
The feller skillfully brought down the old oak with a single swing of his axe.
