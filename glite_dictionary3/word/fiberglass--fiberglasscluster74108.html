fiberglass

noun
material of glass fibers in resin
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbərˌɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbəˌɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a strong, lightweight material made from glass fibers embedded in resin, used in construction and manufacturing

Examples

fiberglass boat hull
He repaired the cracked surfboard with a patch of fiberglass.
The boat's hull is made of fiberglass to ensure durability and lightness.
Workers wore protective gear while installing the new fiberglass insulation in the attic.
