Picea obovata

noun
spruce tree of northern Europe and Asia
Pronunciation: / paɪˌsiːə oʊˈboʊvətə / (AmE), / pɪˌsiːə əʊˈbəʊvətə / (BrE)
A tall spruce tree native to northern Europe and Asia, closely related to the Norway spruce.

Examples

Picea obovata forest tree
Botanists have found Picea obovata adaptable to various cold climates.
The cones of Picea obovata differ slightly from those of the Norway spruce.
