unconnected

adjective
lacking continuity or connection
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kəˈnɛk.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəˈnɛk.tɪd / (BrE)
lacking continuity, logical sequence, or orderly arrangement; not connected or related.

Examples

unconnected ideas
Her presentation was a series of unconnected ideas that puzzled the audience.
My unconnected thoughts made it hard to concentrate on the exam.
The novel's plot felt unconnected, with scenes jumping from one place to another without explanation.
