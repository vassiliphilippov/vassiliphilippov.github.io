nature

noun
a person's inherent temperament or character
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
the set of a person's fundamental emotional and intellectual qualities that determine their characteristic actions and reactions; temperament or character

Examples

gentle nature
His curious nature led him to explore new ideas and places.
It is in her nature to be generous and kind to everyone she meets.
You can't change someone's nature no matter how hard you try.
