cannon

noun
lower leg part of hoofed mammals
Pronunciation: / ˈkænən / (AmE), / ˈkænən / (BrE)
the lower part of the leg in hoofed mammals, extending from the hock to the fetlock, especially the large metacarpal or metatarsal bone

Examples

horse cannon bone
A thick cannon is often considered desirable in racehorses for added strength and support.
She carefully wrapped the cannon of each leg before the competition to prevent injury.
The horse injured its cannon during the race, requiring immediate veterinary attention.
