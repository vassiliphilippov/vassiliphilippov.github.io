to show

verb
to indicate or point out
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊ / (AmE), / ʃəʊ / (BrE)
to indicate a place, direction, person, or thing, either physically by pointing or figuratively by making it known

Examples

to show the way
Could you show me where the library is?
The guide showed us the way to the museum entrance.
With a quick gesture, she showed which book she wanted.
