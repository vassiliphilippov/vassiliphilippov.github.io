counter

noun
back part of a shoe or boot
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.tə / (BrE)
the piece of leather or stiff material forming the back part of a shoe or boot, typically providing support to the heel

shoe counter
