addle-brain

noun
confused or forgetful person
Pronunciation: / ˈædəlˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈædəlˌbreɪn / (BrE)
A person who is confused, mentally disoriented, or forgetful.

Examples

an addle-brain at work
After working for hours without a break, I felt like an addle-brain and couldn't focus on anything.
Don't be such an addle-brain; you left your keys in the fridge again!
She laughed and admitted she's a bit of an addle-brain when it comes to remembering appointments.
