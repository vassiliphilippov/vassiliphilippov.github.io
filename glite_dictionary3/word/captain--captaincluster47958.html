captain

noun
person commanding a merchant ship
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.tɪn /; / ˈkæp.tən / (AmE), / ˈkæp.tɪn / (BrE)
the person in command of a ship, aircraft, or airship (including merchant ships and airplanes)

Every captain must be trained to handle emergency situations at high altitudes.
