broadband

adjective
operating over a wide frequency range
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːd.bænd /; / ˈbrɔːdˌbænd / (BrE)
using or relating to a system that can send a lot of information quickly over a wide range of frequencies (especially for internet or communications)
