to bewilder

verb
confuse greatly; puzzle
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪldər / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldə / (BrE)
to confuse or puzzle someone greatly, making them unable to understand or think clearly

Examples

to bewilder a friend
Her sudden change in attitude completely bewildered him.
The complex instructions bewildered the students, leaving them unsure of what to do next.
The strange customs of the new country can bewilder visitors.
