nonphotosynthetic

adjective
not capable of photosynthesis
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌfoʊtoʊsɪnˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˌfəʊtəʊsɪnˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
(of an organism or tissue) not capable of carrying out photosynthesis.
