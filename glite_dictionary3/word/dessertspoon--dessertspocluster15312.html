dessertspoon

noun
unit of measure, about 10 ml
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɝːtˌspun / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːtˌspuːn / (BrE)
A measure of capacity equal to the amount held by a dessert spoon, typically about 10 millilitres or 2 teaspoons in volume.
