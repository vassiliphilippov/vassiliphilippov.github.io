galvanisation

noun
arousing stimulation or incitement to action
Pronunciation: / ˌɡælvəˌnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɡælvənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
Stimulation or shock that causes people to take lively or rapid action.

Examples

galvanisation for change
After months of inaction, the crisis served as a galvanisation for change.
Her words were a much-needed galvanisation that revived our enthusiasm for the project.
The unexpected speech acted as a galvanisation for the movement's supporters.

Related Terms

  • synonym – incitement: the act of urging one to action
  • synonym – stimulus: something that causes a reaction or activity
