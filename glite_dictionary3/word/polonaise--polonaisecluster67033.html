polonaise

noun
historical women's dress style
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊ.ləˈneɪz / (AmE), / ˌpɒl.əˈneɪz / (BrE)
A woman's dress with a tight-fitting bodice and an overskirt gathered up at the back to reveal a contrasting or colorful underskirt, popular in the late 18th century.
