under

adverb
in a lower position; beneath
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌndə / (BrE)
At a lower position, or beneath the surface of something; down below.

Examples

under a table
If there's an earthquake, you should get under a sturdy table.
The cat hid under quickly when it heard the thunder.
The hikers crawled under to escape the rain.
