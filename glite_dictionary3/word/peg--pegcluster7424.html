peg

noun
oar holder attached to a boat
Pronunciation: / pɛɡ / (AmE), / pɛɡ / (BrE)
a holder fixed to the side of a boat to keep an oar in place and serve as a pivot while rowing

Examples

oar peg holder
Each oar was slotted securely into a sturdy peg on the wooden skiff.
He repaired the loose peg before setting out onto the lake.
The broken peg made it impossible to row the boat efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rowlock: a swivel pivot for an oar on a boat
