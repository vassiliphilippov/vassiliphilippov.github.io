formatting

noun
arranging information by specified rules
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌmætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːmætɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrangement or organization of information according to a particular set of specifications, especially for computer processing or publication

Examples

formatting a document
Good formatting makes computer code clear to both machines and humans.
She spent hours on the formatting for her essay to meet the submission guidelines.
The formatting of the document ensures it is easy to read on any device.
