splashy

adjective
showy and attention-grabbing, often tasteless
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃi / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃi / (BrE)
Attracting attention by being bright, flashy, or showy, often in a way that lacks good taste.

Examples

splashy outfit
His splashy tie was the first thing everyone noticed when he walked into the room.
The magazine cover featured a splashy photo of the latest celebrity couple.
They threw a splashy party to impress their neighbors, complete with fireworks and a live band.

Related Terms

  • synonym – showy: drawing attention by being visually striking
  • antonym – understated: presented in a subtle and restrained manner
