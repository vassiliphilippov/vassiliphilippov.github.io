javelina

noun
wild pig-like mammal of the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˌhævəˈliːnə / (AmE), / ˌhævəˈliːnə / (BrE)
A wild, pig-like mammal with dark grey bristly hair and an indistinct white collar, found in semi-desert regions of Mexico and the southwestern United States.

Examples

wild javelina herd
Javelinas are common in the deserts of Arizona and Texas.

We spotted a javelina rooting around in the brush near the campsite.
