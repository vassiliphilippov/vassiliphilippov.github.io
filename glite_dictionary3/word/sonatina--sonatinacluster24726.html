sonatina

noun
short, simple sonata; musical composition
Pronunciation: / sɑːnəˈtiːnə / (AmE), / sɒn.əˈtiː.nə / (BrE)
A short and simple sonata, typically used as a term for a modest musical composition resembling a sonata but lighter or shorter in form.

Examples

sonatina for piano
He composed a sonatina for violin and piano, perfect for intermediate students.
