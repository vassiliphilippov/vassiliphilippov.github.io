suburbanite

noun
person living in a suburb
Pronunciation: / səˈbɜːrbənaɪt / (AmE), / səˈbɜːbənaɪt / (BrE)
a person who lives in a suburb, especially one who is thought to have the typical attitudes or lifestyle of suburban residents

Examples

typical suburbanite
Many suburbanites commute to the city for work every day.
She grew up as a suburbanite in a quiet neighborhood outside Chicago.
The newspaper article explored the changing lifestyles of modern suburbanites.
