topgallant

noun
a sail above a ship's topsail
Pronunciation: / tɑpˈɡælənt / (AmE), / tɒpˈɡæl(ə)nt / (BrE)
A sail set on the yard of a topgallant mast, above the topsail, on a square-rigged ship.

Examples

topgallant sail set
An experienced sailor knows when to reef the topgallant for safety.
During the race, they set both the topsail and the topgallant to catch more wind.
The crew hurried to lower the topgallant as the storm intensified.
