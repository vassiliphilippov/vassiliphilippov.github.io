Blissus leucopterus

noun
a black-and-white insect pest of cereal grasses
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪsəs luːˈkɑptərəs / (AmE), / ˈblɪsəs luːˈkɒptərəs / (BrE)
A small black-and-white true bug, also known as the chinch bug, that feeds on cereal grasses and can be a pest in agriculture.
