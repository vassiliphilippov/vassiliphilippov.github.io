woodcreeper

noun
Central/South American climbing bird
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌkriːpər / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌkriːpə / (BrE)
a Central or South American bird with a curved bill and stiff tail feathers, which climbs tree trunks like a woodpecker
