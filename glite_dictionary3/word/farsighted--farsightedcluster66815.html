farsighted

adjective
able to see distant objects clearly
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrˌsaɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɑːˌsaɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
able to see distant objects more clearly than those that are near; affected by hyperopia.

Examples

farsighted vision
Because he was farsighted, he could easily spot the sign on the distant building.
She has been farsighted since childhood and wears glasses for reading.
The optometrist explained that being farsighted means you have trouble seeing things up close.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nearsighted: able to see near objects more clearly than distant ones; affected by myopia
