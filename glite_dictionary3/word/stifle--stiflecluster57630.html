to stifle

verb
die or suffer from lack of oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈstaɪfəl / (BrE)
to prevent someone from breathing or to suffocate

Examples

to stifle breathing

The airtight room would stifle anyone who stayed there too long.
