backward

adjective
shy or retiring in behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈbækwərd / (AmE), / ˈbækwəd / (BrE)
(of a person or behavior) shy, reluctant, or retiring; not inclined to be forward or outgoing.

Examples

backward in social life
Despite his talents, he remained rather backward in social situations.
He plays the role of a backward admirer, hesitant to express his feelings.
She was too backward to speak up during the meeting, even when she disagreed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – forward: bold or assertive in manner
  • synonym – shy: nervous or timid in the company of others
