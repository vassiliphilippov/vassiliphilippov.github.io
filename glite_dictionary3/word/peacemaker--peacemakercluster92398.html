Peacemaker

noun
U.S. military belt-fed machine gun
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːsˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a belt-fed machine gun used by United States troops in World War II and the Korean War, capable of firing more than 500 rounds per minute
