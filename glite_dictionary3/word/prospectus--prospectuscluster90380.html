prospectus

noun
booklet outlining courses and information about an institution
Pronunciation: / prəˈspɛktəs / (AmE), / prəˈspɛktəs / (BrE)
a booklet or document providing details about an institution, especially a college or university, and its courses, facilities, and services

Examples

college prospectus
Before applying, she read through the prospectus to learn more about student life and facilities.
Most students request a prospectus to compare different colleges before making a decision.
The university sent me a prospectus outlining all its engineering courses.
