Copernicia alba

noun
South American wax-producing palm
Pronunciation: / koʊˌpɜrnɪˈʃiːə ˈælbə / (AmE), / kəʊˌpɜːnɪˈsiːə ˈalbə / (BrE)
A South American species of palm tree that produces a wax similar to carnauba wax.

Examples

Copernicia alba palm tree
