Leonardo da Vinci

proper noun
Italian Renaissance artist and polymath
Pronunciation: / liˌɑːrnədoʊ də ˈvɪntʃi / (AmE), / liˌɑːnɑːdəʊ də ˈvɪntʃi / (BrE)
(1452–1519) Italian artist, scientist, and engineer renowned as a leading figure of the Renaissance, famed for works such as the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper.
