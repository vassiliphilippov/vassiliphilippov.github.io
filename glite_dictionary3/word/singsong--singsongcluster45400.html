to singsong

verb
speak or chant in a musical, repetitive tone
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋˌsɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋˌsɒŋ / (BrE)
to speak, chant, or recite with a rhythmic, musical, and repetitive tone, often with little variation in pitch

Examples

to singsong a poem
He was singsonging the poem, making it sound almost like a lullaby.
She singsonged the nursery rhymes to soothe the children.
The teacher singsongs the alphabet as part of the lesson.
