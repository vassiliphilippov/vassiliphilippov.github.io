to aluminize

verb
to coat with a layer of aluminum
Pronunciation: / əˈluːmɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / əˈljuːmɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to coat or cover (a material or object) with a thin layer of aluminum, typically to protect against corrosion or to enhance reflective properties

Examples

to aluminize a surface
After they aluminized the telescope mirror, its reflectivity increased significantly.
Engineers often aluminize steel parts to protect them from rust and high temperatures.
The company decided to aluminize the roof panels to improve their durability and reflect heat.
