animalisation

noun
making people cruel or less human
Pronunciation: / ˌænɪmələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌænɪmələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making people cruel, brutal, or lacking normal human qualities

Examples

animalisation of people
His experiences in prison led to a gradual animalisation that alarmed his family and friends.
Many fear that constant exposure to violence results in the animalisation of society.
The animalisation of the soldiers during war was a cause for concern among their commanders.

Related Terms

  • synonym – brutalization: the process of making someone cruel or violent
  • antonym – humanization: the process of making something more humane
