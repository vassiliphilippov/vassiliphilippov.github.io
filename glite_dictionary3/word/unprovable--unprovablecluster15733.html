unprovable

adjective
impossible to prove
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈproʊvəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpruːvəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to prove or demonstrate as true or valid

Examples

unprovable claim
Her theory was interesting, but ultimately unprovable with the evidence available.
Some philosophical claims are inherently unprovable and cannot be verified.
The existence of parallel universes is currently considered unprovable by science.

Related Terms

  • antonym – provable: able to be proved or demonstrated as true
