relativization

noun
the act of making relative
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛlətɪvəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛlətɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something relative, or viewing it as relative rather than absolute.

Examples

relativization of truth
Critics argue that the relativization of truth undermines objective standards.
Einstein's theories contributed to the relativization of concepts like time and space.
The relativization of cultural values is often discussed in sociology classes.
