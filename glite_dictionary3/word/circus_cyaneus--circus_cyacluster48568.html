Circus cyaneus

noun
species of bird of prey (hen harrier)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkəs saɪˈæniəs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkəs saɪˈeɪniəs / (BrE)
A medium-sized bird of prey, also known as the hen harrier, found in North America and Europe, typically nesting in marshes and open fields.
