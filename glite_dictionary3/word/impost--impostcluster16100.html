impost

noun
base block of an arch
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈɪmpəʊst / (BrE)
the block or stone at the base of an arch, from which the curve of the arch rises

Examples

stone impost block
Damage to the impost weakened the entire structure of the ancient bridge.
Each side of the gateway featured a carved impost supporting the stone arch above.
