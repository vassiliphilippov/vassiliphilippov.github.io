barbeque

noun
outdoor meal with food cooked over an open fire
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː / (BrE)
a meal or gathering at which meat and other food are cooked out of doors on a rack over an open fire or hot coals, especially a whole animal roasted on a spit

Examples

family barbeque party
At the beach, they set up a barbeque and enjoyed grilled chicken and vegetables.
The annual barbeque featured a whole pig roasted over an open fire.
We invited our neighbors to a barbeque in the backyard last Saturday.
