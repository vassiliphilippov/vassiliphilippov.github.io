initiator

noun
person who starts an action or process
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɪʃieɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈnɪʃieɪtə / (BrE)
a person who begins or starts something, especially a process or course of action

Examples

project initiator
Clara was the main initiator of the new community garden project.
Every successful reform needs a determined initiator to get things started.
The initiator of the discussion encouraged others to share their opinions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – follower: a person who follows the lead or example of another
  • synonym – originator: a person who creates or starts something
