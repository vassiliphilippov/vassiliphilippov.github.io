closure

noun
the act of blocking or closing something
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
the act or process of closing or blocking something, especially a passage, road, or access point

Examples

closure of a road
Authorities announced the closure of all schools for safety reasons.
Due to the closure of several factories, unemployment in the area has increased.
The sudden closure of the main highway caused hours of traffic delays.
