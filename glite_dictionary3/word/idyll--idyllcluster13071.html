idyll

noun
a peaceful or romantic episode or scene
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.dəl / (AmE), / ˈɪ.dɪl / (BrE)
A period or experience of peaceful, happy, and picturesque charm, especially one that is characteristic of rural or romantic life.

Examples

romantic idyll
She looked back on those early days of marriage as an idyll of happiness and simplicity.
The summer they spent in the countryside was a perfect idyll to remember.
Their brief idyll ended when they returned to the city and their busy lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interlude: a period or event that comes between others and has a different character
