heavy-handed

adjective
unnecessarily forceful or domineering
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛviˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɛviˈhændɪd / (BrE)
using unnecessary force, harshness, or authority in handling situations or people

Examples

heavy-handed use of power
Her heavy-handed management style discouraged employees from sharing their ideas.
Many argue that censorship laws are too heavy-handed and restrict freedom of expression.
The police response to the protest was criticized as heavy-handed by many observers.
