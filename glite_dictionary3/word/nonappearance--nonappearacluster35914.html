nonappearance

noun
failure to appear, especially legally
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əˈpɪr.əns / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈpɪə.rəns / (BrE)
The failure to appear or be present, especially in a legal or official context.

Examples

nonappearance in court
A nonappearance by the witness delayed the trial.
His repeated nonappearance at meetings raised concerns about his commitment.
The defendant's nonappearance in court led to the issuance of a warrant.
