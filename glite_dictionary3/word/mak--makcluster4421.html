MAK

noun
terrorist group linked to al-Qaeda origins
Pronunciation: / ɛm eɪ keɪ / (AmE), / ɛm eɪ keɪ / (BrE)
a terrorist organization founded by Osama bin Laden in the 1980s to provide funding and recruit fighters for the Afghan conflict; its split led to the creation of al-Qaeda.

MAK organization
