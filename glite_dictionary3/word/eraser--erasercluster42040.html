eraser

noun
object for removing pencil marks
Pronunciation: / ɪˈreɪsər / (AmE), / ɪˈreɪsə / (BrE)
A small object, typically made of rubber or a similar material, used for removing marks made by pencil or, less commonly, ink.

Examples

eraser for pencil marks
I always keep an eraser in my pencil case.
She reached for her eraser to correct a mistake on her homework.
The artist carefully used an eraser to add highlights to the drawing.
