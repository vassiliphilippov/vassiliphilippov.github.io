semipublic

adjective
partly public in nature or access
Pronunciation: / sɛmiˈpʌblɪk / (AmE), / sɛmiˈpʌblɪk / (BrE)
partly but not fully open or accessible to the public; having some but not all qualities of a public institution or space

Examples

semipublic event

Their residence is situated in a semipublic courtyard, accessible only to a select group of neighbors.
