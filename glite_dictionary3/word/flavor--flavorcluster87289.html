to flavor

verb
to give taste to food or drink
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvər / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
to give a particular taste or quality to food or drink by adding seasoning or another substance

Examples

to flavor the soup
A squeeze of lemon can flavor the grilled fish beautifully.
She likes to flavor her soups with a pinch of paprika.
The chef flavored the sauce with fresh basil before serving.
