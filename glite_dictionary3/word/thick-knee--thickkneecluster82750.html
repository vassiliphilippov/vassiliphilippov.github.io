thick-knee

noun
a wading bird with a thickened knee joint
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪkˌni / (AmE), / ˈθɪk.niː / (BrE)
a large-headed, large-eyed nocturnal or crepuscular wading bird of the Old World and tropical America, noted for having a thickened knee joint

Examples

a thick-knee bird
A thick-knee called out in the darkness as we walked along the riverbank.
Several species of thick-knee inhabit the open grasslands of Africa and Asia.
The thick-knee is easily recognized by its big yellow eyes and swollen knee joint.
