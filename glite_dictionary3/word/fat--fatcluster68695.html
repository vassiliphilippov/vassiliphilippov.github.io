fat

adjective
containing or composed of fat
Pronunciation: / fæt / (AmE), / fæt / (BrE)
containing or having a lot of fat; having a large amount of excess flesh or body fat

Examples

fat food
Avocados are considered a healthy but fat food.
Nutritionists warn against eating too much fat meat.
The surgeon removed some fat tissue during the operation.
After months of indulging in desserts, he became noticeably fat.
Despite being fat, the cat was surprisingly agile.
The doctor advised her to lose weight because she was getting too fat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fatty: containing or composed of fat; greasy
  • antonym – lean: (of meat) containing little or no fat
  • degree higher – obese: very overweight, to the point of being unhealthy
  • degree lower – plump: having a full, rounded shape, slightly chubby
  • antonym – slim: thin in an attractive or healthy way
  • antonym – thin: having little flesh or fat on the body
