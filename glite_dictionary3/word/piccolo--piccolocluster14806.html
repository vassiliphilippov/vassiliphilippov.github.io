piccolo

noun
high-pitched small flute
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪk.əl.əʊ / (BrE)
a small flute with a very high pitch, sounding an octave higher than the standard flute

Examples

piccolo solo performance
A piccolo is often used to add brightness and sparkle to orchestral music.
She has been practicing the piccolo for her upcoming concert performance.
The orchestra featured a lively piccolo solo during the overture.
