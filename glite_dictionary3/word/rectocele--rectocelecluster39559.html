rectocele

noun
protrusion of rectum into vagina
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.toʊˌsiːl / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.təʊˌsiːl / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the rectum bulges or protrudes into the vagina, often due to weakened pelvic muscles, commonly after childbirth
