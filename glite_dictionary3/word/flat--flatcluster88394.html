flat

adjective
definite and unconditional (refusal, statement)
Pronunciation: / flæt / (AmE), / flæt / (BrE)
(of a statement or refusal) said in a definite and unconditional way, without any qualification or reservation.

Examples

flat refusal
Despite our offers, he gave a flat denial of any involvement.
Her boss gave a flat no when she asked for more time.
The request was met with a flat refusal from the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym – categorical: unambiguously explicit and direct
  • antonym – qualified: subject to reservations or conditions
