acidity

noun
sour or sharp taste
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪdəti / (AmE), / əˈsɪdəti / (BrE)
A sour or sharp taste present when something acidic is in the mouth.

Examples

acidity in fruit
Excessive acidity in fruit can make it taste unpleasantly sharp.
Some wines have a strong acidity that balances their sweetness.
The acidity of the lemonade made my mouth pucker.
