Batidaceae

noun
plant family containing saltworts
Pronunciation: / ˌbætəˈdeɪsiː / (AmE), / ˌbætɪˈdeɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
A family of plants coextensive with the genus Batis, commonly known as saltworts, found in saline environments.
