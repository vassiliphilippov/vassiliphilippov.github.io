to overarch

verb
form an arch over
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˈɑːtʃ / (BrE)
to form an arch over something; to span or cover with an arching shape

Examples

to overarch a bridge
A glass dome overarches the atrium, filling the space with natural light.
Tall trees overarch the pathway, creating a tunnel of green leaves overhead.
The ancient stone bridge overarches the narrow river below.
