humectant

noun
substance that retains moisture
Pronunciation: / hjuˈmɛktənt / (AmE), / hjuːˈmɛktənt / (BrE)
A substance added to products to help retain or preserve moisture.

Examples

humectant in lotion
Glycerin is a common humectant used in cosmetics and food products.
Many skincare products contain a humectant to help keep your skin hydrated.
The presence of a humectant prevents baked goods from drying out quickly.
