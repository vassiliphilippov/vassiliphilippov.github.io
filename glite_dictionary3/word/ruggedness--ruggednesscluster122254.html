ruggedness

noun
difficulty or demanding nature
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌɡɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being difficult, challenging, or demanding to do or accomplish

Examples

ruggedness of a task

The ruggedness of the mountain trail made the hike a real challenge for beginners.
