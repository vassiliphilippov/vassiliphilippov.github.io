buzzard

noun
a large European bird of prey
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌz.ɚd / (AmE), / ˈbʌz.əd / (BrE)
a large bird of prey found in Europe, belonging to the hawk family, typically with broad wings and a short tail

Examples

soaring buzzard
A buzzard soared above the fields, searching for its next meal.
During the hike, we spotted a buzzard perched on a fence post.
The conservation area is known for its large population of buzzards.
