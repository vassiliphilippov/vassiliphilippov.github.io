underweight

adjective
extremely thin; below healthy weight
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈweɪt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈweɪt / (BrE)
(of a person) extremely thin; below the normal or healthy weight.

Examples

underweight child
After his illness, he became noticeably underweight and weak.
Models are often criticized for appearing underweight on the runway.
The doctor said she was too underweight for her age and height.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bony: very thin, with bones that show easily
  • antonym – overweight: above the normal or healthy weight
  • synonym – skinny: very thin, especially in an unattractive or unhealthy way
