survivalist

noun
person preparing for major disaster
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪvəlɪst / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who prepares for the possibility of large-scale disasters or societal collapse by storing supplies and learning self-sufficiency skills

Examples

a survivalist stockpiles food
During the pandemic, the advice of survivalists became more widely sought after.
Many survivalists keep stocks of food and water in case of emergency.
The film follows a group of survivalists living off the grid after a disaster.

  • synonym – prepper: person actively preparing for disasters or emergencies
