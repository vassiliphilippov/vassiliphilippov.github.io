to commune

verb
receive Communion in the Catholic Church
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjun / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːn / (BrE)
(in the Catholic Church) to receive Communion; to partake in the sacrament of the Eucharist.

Examples

to commune the Eucharist
After preparing himself spiritually, he was ready to commune and receive the sacrament.
She chose to commune at the early morning mass on Sunday.
Those wishing to commune must first participate in the rite of confession.

Related Terms

  • synonym – communicate: to receive the Eucharist (archaic variant and synonym in religious sense)
