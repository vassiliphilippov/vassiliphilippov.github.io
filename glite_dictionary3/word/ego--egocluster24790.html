ego

noun
sense of self or identity
Pronunciation: / ˈiɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈiːɡəʊ / (BrE)
a person's sense of self; the conscious awareness of one's own identity.

Examples

personal ego
His enormous ego made it difficult for him to admit he was wrong.
Meditation can help you become more aware of your ego and its influence on your life.
Psychologists often discuss how the ego mediates between our instincts and social expectations.
