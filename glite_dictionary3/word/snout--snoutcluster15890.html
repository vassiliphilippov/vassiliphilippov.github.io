snout

noun
informal word for the nose
Pronunciation: / snaʊt / (AmE), / snaʊt / (BrE)
(informal) the human nose, especially when large or prominent.

Examples

big snout
He broke his snout in a football game last year.
He stuck his snout into everyone’s business, always prying.
She wrinkled her snout in disgust at the smell.
