apple

noun
round fruit with crisp, sweet or tart flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəl / (AmE), / ˈapəl / (BrE)
A round fruit with smooth red, yellow, or green skin, firm white flesh, and a crisp, sweet to tart taste, produced by the apple tree.

Examples

eat an apple
I like to snack on a crisp apple in the afternoon.
She brought a shiny red apple to school for her teacher.
We picked green apples from the tree in Grandma's yard.
