sickeningness

noun
extreme unpalatability; quality of being sickening
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkənɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪkənɪŋnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of causing a strong feeling of disgust, especially when referring to taste; extreme unpalatability.

Examples

the sickeningness of taste
She remembered the sickeningness of the medicine she was forced to drink as a child.
The sickeningness of the spoiled milk made him gag immediately.
The dish was ruined by the sickeningness of too much bitter gourd.
