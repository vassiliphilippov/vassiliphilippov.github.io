upstart

noun
gymnastic movement from inverted to upright position
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌstɑrt / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌstɑːt / (BrE)
a gymnastic movement in which the body starts with the legs above the upper body, then moves to an upright position by arching the back and swinging the legs downward while forcing the chest upright

Examples

gymnast performs an upstart
Her coach praised her technique during the upstart phase of the routine.
Mastering the upstart requires both strength and coordination.
The gymnast performed a flawless upstart on the uneven bars.
