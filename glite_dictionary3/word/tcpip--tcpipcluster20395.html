TCP/IP

noun
set of protocols for internet communication
Pronunciation: / ti si pi aɪ pi / (AmE), / tiː siː piː aɪ piː / (BrE)
a set of communication protocols used for interconnecting network devices on the internet, including the Transmission Control Protocol (TCP) and the Internet Protocol (IP)
