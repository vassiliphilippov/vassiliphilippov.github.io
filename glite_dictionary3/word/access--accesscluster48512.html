access

noun
the right to enter or use
Pronunciation: / ˈæksɛs /; / ˈækˌsɛs /; / ˈæk.sɛs / (AmE), / ˈæksɛs /; / ˈaksɛs /; / ˈæk.sɛs / (BrE)
