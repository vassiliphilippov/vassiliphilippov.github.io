inaugural

noun
formal speech at the start of an event, especially by a new president
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɔɡjərəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnɔːɡjərəl / (BrE)
a formal speech given at the beginning of an important event or ceremony, especially by a newly appointed leader, such as a president

Examples

inaugural speech
Her inaugural focused on themes of unity and hope.
People around the world tuned in to watch the televised inaugural.
The new president gave an inspiring inaugural that resonated with millions of people.

Related Terms

  • synonym – inaugural address: the formal speech given at an inauguration ceremony by someone beginning an official position
  • antonym – valedictory: a farewell speech, especially one given at the end of a career or event
