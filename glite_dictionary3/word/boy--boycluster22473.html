boy

noun
offensive term for Black man
Pronunciation: / bɔɪ / (AmE), / bɔɪ / (BrE)
(offensive) A disparaging term used, especially in the past, to address or refer to a Black man in a demeaning and racist manner.

Examples

call a man boy
Calling a grown man "boy" was a way to demean him, especially in a racist context.
He shouted, "Get out of my way, boy," using the term insultingly.
The word boy was used offensively by people in authority during segregation.
