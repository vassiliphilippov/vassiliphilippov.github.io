to deck

verb
knock someone down forcefully
Pronunciation: / dɛk / (AmE), / dɛk / (BrE)
(informal) to knock someone down forcefully, especially with a punch.

Examples

to deck a person
He was so angry that he nearly decked the man who insulted him.
One quick punch decked his opponent in the final round.
She threatened to deck anyone who tried to steal her bag.

Related Terms

  • synonym – floor: to knock someone to the ground (informal)
  • synonym – knock out: to make someone unconscious, especially by hitting them
