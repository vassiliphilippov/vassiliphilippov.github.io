vape

noun
electronic cigarette device
Pronunciation: / veɪp / (AmE), / veɪp / (BrE)
An electronic cigarette or device that produces a nicotine-containing vapor for inhalation.

Examples

a vape device
He always carries his vape with him instead of traditional cigarettes.
She bought a flavored vape to help her quit smoking tobacco.
The new regulations prohibit the use of a vape in public areas.
