to spice

verb
to add spices or herbs for flavor
Pronunciation: / spaɪs / (AmE), / spaɪs / (BrE)
to season food by adding herbs or spices to give it flavor.

Examples

to spice a dish
He spiced the curry generously, making it deliciously aromatic.
She likes to spice her soup with a pinch of cumin.
You can spice the chicken before roasting it for extra flavor.

  • synonym – season: add salt, herbs, or spices to food for flavoring
