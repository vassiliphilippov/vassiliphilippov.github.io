parity

noun
mathematical evenness or oddness
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəti / (AmE), / ˈpærəti / (BrE)
the property of an integer being even or odd, or the relationship between two numbers based on whether they are both even or both odd

Examples

number parity
Check the parity of these two numbers to see if they match.
In coding theory, parity is used to detect errors in binary sequences.
The parity of a number determines if it is even or odd.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – odd: an integer not divisible by two without remainder
