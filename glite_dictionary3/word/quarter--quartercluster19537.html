to quarter

verb
provide housing for troops
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrdɚ / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə / (BrE)
to provide accommodation or lodging for, especially for military personnel

Examples

to quarter soldiers
During the war, many families were required to quarter soldiers in their homes.
The commanding officer decided to quarter the troops in nearby villages for the winter.
The old barracks were used to quarter the regiment during training.

Related Terms

  • synonym – billet: to assign lodging to, especially soldiers
  • synonym – house: to provide living accommodation for someone
