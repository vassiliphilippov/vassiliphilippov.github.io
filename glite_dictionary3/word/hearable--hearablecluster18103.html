hearable

adjective
able to be heard; audible
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪrəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhɪərəbl / (BrE)
able to be heard or perceived by the ear; audible.

Examples

hearable sound
Due to the thick walls, no sound was hearable from outside the room.
She spoke in a soft but hearable tone so as not to startle the children.
The announcer's voice was barely hearable over the noise from the crowd.
