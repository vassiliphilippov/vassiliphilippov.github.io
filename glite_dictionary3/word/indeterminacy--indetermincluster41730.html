indeterminacy

noun
vagueness; lack of clear definition
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dɪˈtɜr.mə.nə.si / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈtɜː.mɪ.nə.si / (BrE)
the quality of being vague, uncertain, or not clearly defined or established

Examples

indeterminacy in meaning
Legal language can sometimes suffer from indeterminacy, resulting in disputes about meaning.
Modern art often embraces indeterminacy, leaving interpretation open to the viewer.
The indeterminacy of his answer made it difficult to know what he really meant.
