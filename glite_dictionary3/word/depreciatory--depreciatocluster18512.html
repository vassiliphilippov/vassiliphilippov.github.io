depreciatory

adjective
causing decrease in value
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpriʃ.i.əˌtɔr.i / (AmE), / dɪˈpriː.ʃi.ə.tər.i / (BrE)
tending to decrease or cause a decrease in value, especially in relation to currency or assets

Examples

depreciatory effect on prices
A series of depreciatory trends undermined the stock's long-term value.
Investors are wary of policies with potentially depreciatory impacts on market confidence.
The sudden drop in exports had a depreciatory effect on the national currency.
