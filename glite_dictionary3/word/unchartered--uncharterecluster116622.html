unchartered

adjective
not formally chartered or licensed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɑːr.tərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɑː.təd / (BrE)
Not having a charter; not formally established or licensed by a charter.

Examples

unchartered bank
As an unchartered bank, it cannot legally offer certain financial services.
