phycobilin

noun
pigment in red algae and cyanobacteria
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪkoʊˈbɪlɪn / (AmE), / ˌfɪkəʊˈbɪlɪn / (BrE)
a water-soluble pigment found in red algae and cyanobacteria, responsible for capturing light energy during photosynthesis

Examples

phycobilin pigment in algae
