flatly

adverb
in an unequivocal or definite manner
Pronunciation: / ˈflæt.li / (AmE), / ˈflæt.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows no doubt or qualification; without hesitation or softness

Examples

flatly refuse a request
He flatly refused to discuss the matter any further.
She flatly denied any involvement in the incident.
The committee flatly rejected the proposal as unrealistic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – categorically: in a way that is unambiguously explicit and direct
  • synonym – firmly: in a strong, determined, or unhesitating manner
  • antonym – hesitantly: in a tentative or uncertain manner, showing hesitation
