Europocentric

adjective
centered on Europe or Europeans
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊroʊpəˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəpəˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
Focusing on Europe and Europeans, often to the exclusion or detriment of other cultures or regions.

Examples

Europocentric view of history
Her analysis challenges the Europocentric assumptions that have long dominated the field of art history.
Some historians argue that traditional narratives are overly Europocentric and ignore important contributions from other cultures.
The curriculum has often been criticized for its Europocentric approach and limited attention to global perspectives.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Eurocentric: focusing on Europe or European values, often with a sense of superiority
