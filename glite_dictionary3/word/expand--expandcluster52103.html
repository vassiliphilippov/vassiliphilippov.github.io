to expand

verb
become larger in size or amount
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspand /; / ɪkˈspænd / (BrE)
to become or make something larger in size, number, amount, or scope; to grow or increase

Examples

to expand a business
Metal will expand when it is heated.
Our services have expanded dramatically over the last decade.
The company plans to expand into new markets next year.
Hot air causes the balloon to expand as it rises.
The company plans to expand its operations into new markets next year.
They decided to expand the kitchen to create more space for dining.
He hopes to expand his influence in the technology sector.
The company is planning to expand its operations into international markets.
With new investments, the university will expand its research program significantly.
