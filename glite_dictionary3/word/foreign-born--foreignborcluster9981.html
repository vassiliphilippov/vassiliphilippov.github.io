foreign-born

adjective
born in another country
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrənˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈfɒrənˌbɔːn / (BrE)
born in a country or area different from the one in which one currently lives

Examples

foreign-born citizens
Many foreign-born citizens bring valuable skills and perspectives to their new homes.
She is foreign-born, but has lived here for more than twenty years.
The city has a large foreign-born population from many different countries.
