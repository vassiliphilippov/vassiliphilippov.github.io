save

noun
sports play preventing a score
Pronunciation: / seɪv / (AmE), / seɪv / (BrE)
an act in sports where a player prevents the opposing team from scoring, especially by a goalkeeper or relief pitcher

Examples

a great save
Her quick reflexes resulted in an incredible save that kept the score tied.
The goalkeeper made a crucial save in the final seconds of the match.
The pitcher earned a save after holding the lead in the ninth inning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – block: an action that stops a ball or puck from going into the goal
  • antonym – goal: a point scored in sports by getting the ball or puck into the opposing team's net or area
