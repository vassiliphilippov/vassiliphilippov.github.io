sore

adjective
angry or resentful (informal)
Pronunciation: / sɔr / (AmE), / sɔː / (BrE)
angry, upset, or emotionally distressed, especially because of something that happened

Examples

sore feelings
After the argument, Tom remained sore for the rest of the evening.
He was still sore over losing the competition to his younger brother.
She gets sore whenever anyone criticizes her work, no matter how gently.
After her argument with her friend, she felt a sore sense of regret all day.
It was a sore disappointment to lose the championship after coming so close to victory.
The failure of the project was a sore point that no one wanted to discuss.
