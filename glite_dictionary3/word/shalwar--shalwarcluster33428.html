shalwar

noun
loose trousers from South Asia
Pronunciation: / ʃælˈwɑr / (AmE), / ʃælˈwɑː / (BrE)
A pair of loose, light trousers that are tight around the ankles, traditionally worn in South and Central Asia, especially by women with a kameez.

Examples

shalwar kameez outfit
Many women in Pakistan and India choose the shalwar as part of their everyday attire.
She wore a beautiful floral shalwar with a matching blue kameez.
Traditional shalwar are both comfortable and stylish during hot weather.
