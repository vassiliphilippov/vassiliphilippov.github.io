handmaiden

noun
female personal attendant or maid
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌmeɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌmeɪdn̩ / (BrE)
a female personal attendant or maid; a woman who serves another as an assistant

Examples

loyal handmaiden
Literature often features the devoted handmaiden as a symbol of loyalty and service.
She worked as a handmaiden in the noblewoman's household, performing a variety of tasks.
The queen's trusted handmaiden attended to her every need throughout the day.
