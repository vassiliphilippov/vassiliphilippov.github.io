incertitude

noun
state of uncertainty
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɝː.təˌtud / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜː.tɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
the state of being uncertain or lacking confidence about something

Examples

feeling of incertitude
During periods of great change, incertitude often affects people's decision-making.
He approached the negotiations with a sense of incertitude about the outcome.
Her voice revealed her incertitude about whether she should accept the offer.
