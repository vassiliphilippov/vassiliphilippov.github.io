staining

noun
act of marking with a stain
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsteɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of marking or discoloring a surface with a stain

Examples

staining wood surfaces
Proper staining of wood can enhance its natural beauty.
She was worried that the red wine would cause permanent staining on the tablecloth.
The staining of the carpet made it look old and dirty.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cleaning: the act of removing stains or dirt
  • synonym – spotting: the act of making spots or marks
