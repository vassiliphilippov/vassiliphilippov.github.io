Psalms

proper noun
book of religious poems in the Bible
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmz / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmz / (BrE)
a book of the Old Testament consisting of a collection of 150 religious poems or songs, traditionally attributed to King David
