candida

noun
genus of yeastlike fungi; can cause infection
Pronunciation: / ˈkændɪdə / (AmE), / ˈkændɪdə / (BrE)
a genus of yeastlike fungi, some species of which can cause infections in humans, especially in moist areas of the body
