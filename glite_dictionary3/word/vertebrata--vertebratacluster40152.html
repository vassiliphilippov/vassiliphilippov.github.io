Vertebrata

noun
animals with backbones (subphylum)
Pronunciation: / ˌvɝːtəˈbreɪtə /; / ˌvɜːrtəˈbreɪtə / (AmE), / ˌvɜːtɪˈbreɪtə / (BrE)
the group of animals that have a backbone, including fishes, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals (a major subphylum in biology)
