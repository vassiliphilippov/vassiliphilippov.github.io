undershirt

noun
collarless upper-body undergarment
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌʃɝt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
A collarless undergarment worn on the upper body, usually beneath a shirt.

Examples

undershirt under a shirt
Even in summer, he prefers to wear a undershirt under his dress shirt.
He put on a clean undershirt before dressing for work.
She bought a pack of white cotton undershirts for her husband.
