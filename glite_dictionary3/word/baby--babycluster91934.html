to baby

verb
treat with excessive indulgence or care
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
to treat someone with excessive care, indulgence, or attention, making things too easy for them

Examples

to baby the players
If you baby the players, they’ll never reach their full potential.
She loves her dog so much that she really babies him.
Stop babying your brother—he needs to learn to do things on his own.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mollycoddle: to protect someone too much and treat them as a baby
  • antonym – neglect: to give little or no attention or care to someone
  • synonym – pamper: to treat with excessive care or indulgence
