subversion

noun
undermining honesty or loyalty
Pronunciation: / səbˈvɜrʒən / (AmE), / səbˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
the act of undermining someone's honesty, loyalty, or moral integrity

Examples

subversion of morals
He feared the subversion of his core values after moving to the city.
Some critics argue that the film depicts the subversion of innocence through exposure to urban life.
The scandal was seen as a subversion of public trust in government institutions.
