yarn

noun
thread or strand used in textile crafts
Pronunciation: / jɑrn / (AmE), / jɑːn / (BrE)
A continuous strand of twisted fibers, such as cotton, silk, wool, or synthetic material, used for sewing, weaving, or knitting.

Examples

knit with yarn
After untangling the yarn, she started weaving a blanket.
She chose a bright red yarn to knit her new scarf.
The yarn is spun from natural wool and dyed in vibrant colors.
