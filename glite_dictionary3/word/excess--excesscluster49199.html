excess

adjective
more than necessary; surplus
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.sɛs / (AmE), / ˈɛk.sɛs / (BrE)
More than is necessary, permitted, or desirable; surplus.

Examples

excess weight
He tried to cut out excess calories from his diet.
The report was filled with excess information that distracted from the main points.
These containers are for collecting excess rainwater during storms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – extra: More than what is usual or necessary
  • antonym – insufficient: Not enough in amount or degree
  • synonym – surplus: An amount that is more than what is needed
