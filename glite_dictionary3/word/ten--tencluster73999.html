ten

noun
playing card with ten pips
Pronunciation: / tɛn / (AmE), / tɛn / (BrE)
A playing card with ten pips, being one of four such cards in a standard deck, one in each suit.

Examples

ten of hearts

I needed a ten to complete my straight in poker.
There are four tens in every standard deck of cards.
