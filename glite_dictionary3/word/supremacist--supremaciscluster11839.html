supremacist

noun
someone advocating group or racial superiority
Pronunciation: / səˈprɛməsɪst / (AmE), / sjuːˈprɛməsɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that one particular group, especially a racial group, is superior to all others and seeks to promote its dominance
