kaftan

noun
long, loose robe with sash
Pronunciation: / ˈkæf.tæn / (AmE), / ˈkæf.tæn /; / ˈkɑːf.tæn / (BrE)
a long, loose garment with wide sleeves, traditionally worn by men in the Middle East and North Africa, and also a loose-fitting dress for women inspired by this style
