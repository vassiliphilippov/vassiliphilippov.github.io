die

noun
small cube used in games or gambling
Pronunciation: / daɪ / (AmE), / daɪ / (BrE)
a small cube with faces marked from one to six dots, used in games of chance to generate random numbers

Examples

roll a die
Each die in the set is perfectly balanced to ensure fair play.
He rolled the die and hoped for a six to win the game.
When you lose a die under the table, the game can't continue.

  • synonym – dice: plural form; small cubes with numbered sides used in games
