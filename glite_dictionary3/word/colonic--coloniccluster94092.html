colonic

noun
intestinal flushing procedure
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑnɪk / (AmE), / kəˈlɒnɪk / (BrE)
a procedure in which water is introduced into the colon through the rectum to flush it out, often for purported health benefits

Examples

colonic treatment
A colonic should only be performed by a trained professional under sanitary conditions.
After her colonic, she said she felt lighter and more energetic.
Many spas now offer colonic treatments as part of their detox services.

  • synonym – enema: fluid injected into the rectum for cleaning or medication
