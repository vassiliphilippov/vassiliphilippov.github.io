to pick

verb
to gather or collect (plants, fruit, etc.)
Pronunciation: / pɪk / (AmE), / pɪk / (BrE)
to gather or collect something, especially fruit, flowers, or other plants, by removing them from where they grow

Examples

to pick apples
Every autumn, families come to pick apples at the orchard.
She likes to pick fresh flowers from her garden every morning.
We spent the afternoon picking wild strawberries in the meadow.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gather: to collect plants or other items from a place
  • synonym – harvest: to collect crops or fruit when they are ripe
