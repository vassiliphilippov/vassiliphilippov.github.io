Æthelred of Wessex

proper noun
King of Wessex (died 871); brother of Alfred the Great
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθəlˌrɛd əv ˈwɛsɛks / (AmE), / ˈɛθəlˌrɛd əv ˈwɛsɪks / (BrE)
(died 871) King of Wessex and Kent from 865 to 871, elder brother of Alfred the Great, remembered for leading defense against Danish invaders.
