mute

adjective
unable to speak due to deafness
Pronunciation: / mjuːt / (AmE), / mjuːt / (BrE)
(dated, sometimes offensive) Unable to speak, especially as a result of hereditary deafness.

Examples

mute child
He was born mute and learned to communicate using sign language.
In the past, children who were mute due to deafness often lacked educational support.
The school provided special resources for students who were mute from birth.

  • synonym – speechless: unable to speak; without speech
