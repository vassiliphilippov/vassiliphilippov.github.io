vision

noun
mystical supernatural experience or revelation
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a religious or mystical experience in which a supernatural being, event, or image is seen, often believed to convey a message or revelation

Examples

holy vision
During his meditation, he claimed to have had a vision of an angel bringing him comfort.
Many ancient prophets described their visions in sacred texts and parables.
The saint was known for experiencing profound visions that inspired her followers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – apparition: a ghostly or supernatural appearance
  • antonym – hallucination: an experience involving the apparent perception of something not present
