female

adjective
of the sex that can produce eggs or offspring
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl / (BrE)
of or relating to the sex that is capable of bearing offspring or producing eggs, especially in animals or plants.

Examples

female insects
Only the female insects lay eggs after mating in spring.
The female panda was carefully monitored during her pregnancy.
We planted a female holly tree to ensure it would bear berries.
