unhindered

adjective
not obstructed or interfered with
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɪndɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɪndəd / (BrE)
not obstructed, slowed, or interfered with; free to proceed without hindrance or restraint.

Examples

unhindered progress
Scientific progress should continue unhindered by unnecessary bureaucracy.
She moved unhindered through the crowd, reaching the exit quickly.
The river flowed unhindered through the valley after the dam was removed.
