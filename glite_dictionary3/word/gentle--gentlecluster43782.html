gentle

adjective
of noble birth; aristocratic
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.t̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛn.tl̩ / (BrE)
belonging to or characteristic of the nobility or aristocracy; of noble birth or breeding

Examples

gentle birth
He was considered gentle by birth, coming from a long line of aristocrats.
Her gentle manners reflected her noble upbringing.
The manor was reserved for the gentle families of the county.
