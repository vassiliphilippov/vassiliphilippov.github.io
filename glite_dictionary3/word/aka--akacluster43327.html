a.k.a.

adverb
abbreviation for 'also known as'
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˌkeɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌkeɪ ˈeɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate an alternative name or alias of a person or thing (abbreviation of 'also known as')

Examples

a.k.a. John Smith
She performs under the stage name "Starlight," a.k.a. Emily Dawson.
The author J.K. Rowling, a.k.a. Robert Galbraith, published several crime novels.
The suspect, a.k.a. "The Fox," was finally apprehended last night.
