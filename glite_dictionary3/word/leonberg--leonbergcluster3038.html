Leonberg

noun
large breed of dog, golden coat
Pronunciation: / ˈliːɒnˌbɜrɡ / (AmE), / ˈliːɒnˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
a large breed of dog, usually with a golden coat, developed by crossing a St Bernard and a Newfoundland.
