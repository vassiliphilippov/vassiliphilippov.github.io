topically

adverb
applied to a local area of the body
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːpɪkli / (AmE), / ˈtɒpɪkəli / (BrE)
in a way that is applied to a particular part of the body, rather than taken orally or injected

Examples

topically applied cream
Steroids can be administered topically for certain skin conditions.
The doctor recommended using the cream topically instead of taking medication orally.
This ointment should be applied topically to the affected area twice a day.

Related Terms

  • antonym – orally: by mouth, especially by swallowing
  • antonym – systemically: affecting the whole body, not just a local area
