to unbind

verb
untie or release from binding
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbaɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbaɪnd / (BrE)
to release from a binding or restraint; to untie or unfasten

Examples

to unbind a rope
After many years, the man was finally unbound from his restrictive obligations.
She quickly unbound the cords holding the package together.
They tried to unbind the rope from around the animal's legs.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bind: to tie or fasten something tightly
  • synonym – untie: to undo or loosen the tie or knot of something
