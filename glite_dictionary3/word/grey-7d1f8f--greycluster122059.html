grey

adjective
having grey or white hair; showing age
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ / (BrE)
(of a person or their hair) showing signs of age, especially having grey or white hair.

Examples

grey hair
Her once dark hair had become grey over the years.
His grey temples suggested a lifetime of wisdom and experience.
The old professor, with his grey beard, was respected by all the students.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hoary: having white or grey hair because of age
  • antonym – young: not showing signs of age
