Inocor

noun
drug for heart failure (trade name)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnəˌkɔr / (AmE), / ˈɪnəkɔː / (BrE)
the trade name of a drug used intravenously to treat heart failure by increasing the strength of heart muscle contractions
