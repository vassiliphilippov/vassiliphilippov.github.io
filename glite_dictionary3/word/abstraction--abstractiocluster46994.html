abstraction

noun
the act of withdrawing or removing
Pronunciation: / əbˈstrækʃən / (AmE), / əbˈstrækʃən / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing, removing, or taking something away from a larger whole

Examples

abstraction of water
The abstraction of water from the river has affected local wildlife.
The artist's work focuses on the abstraction of real-life objects into basic shapes.
There was a sudden abstraction of key personnel from the project team.

Related Terms

  • antonym – insertion: the act of putting something into something else
  • synonym – removal: the act of taking something away or off from the position occupied
