ascolichen

noun
lichen with ascomycete fungus
Pronunciation: / ˌæskoʊˈlaɪkɪn / (AmE), / ˌaskəʊˈlaɪkɪn / (BrE)
A lichen in which the fungal component belongs to the ascomycete group of fungi.

Examples

ascolichen species
Many ascolichens play a crucial role in breaking down rocks and forming soil.
The scientist carefully examined the ascolichen under the microscope to study its structure.
Unlike other lichens, an ascolichen contains an ascomycete as its fungal partner.
