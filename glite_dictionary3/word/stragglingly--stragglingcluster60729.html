stragglingly

adverb
in an irregular or scattered manner
Pronunciation: / ˈstræɡlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstræɡlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that is irregular, untidy, or scattered; in a way that spreads out unevenly.

Examples

to move stragglingly along
The parade moved stragglingly through the village as people joined at their own pace.
The wildflowers grew stragglingly along the edge of the abandoned path.
Vines climbed stragglingly up the fence, giving the garden a wild appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – irregularly: in an uneven or non-uniform manner
  • antonym – tidily: in a neat and orderly manner
