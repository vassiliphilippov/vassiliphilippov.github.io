foremother

noun
female ancestor
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌmʌðər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌmʌðə / (BrE)
a female ancestor, especially one who is an originator or founder of a family, community, or tradition

Examples

our foremothers in history
Many traditions have been passed down from our foremothers through the generations.
She researched her family tree and discovered a remarkable foremother who was a pioneering doctor.
The foremother of the movement fought tirelessly for women's rights.
