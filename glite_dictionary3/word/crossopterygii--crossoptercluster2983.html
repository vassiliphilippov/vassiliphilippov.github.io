Crossopterygii

noun
group of extinct lobe-finned fishes
Pronunciation: / krɒˌsɑptəˈrɪdʒiaɪ /; / krɑːsˌɑptəˈrɪdʒiːˌaɪ / (AmE), / krɒˌsɒptəˈrɪdʒɪaɪ /; / krɒsˌɒptəˈrɪdʒiːˌaɪ / (BrE)
