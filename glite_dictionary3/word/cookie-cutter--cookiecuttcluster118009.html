cookie-cutter

adjective
lacking individuality; identical in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊki ˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈkʊki ˌkʌtə / (BrE)
lacking originality or individuality; standardized, generic, or unimaginative

Examples

cookie-cutter houses

They wanted a more creative strategy, not another cookie-cutter plan.
