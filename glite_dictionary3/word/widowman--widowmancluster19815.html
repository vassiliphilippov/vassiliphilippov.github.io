widowman

noun
man whose wife has died
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪdoʊˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈwɪdəʊˌmæn / (BrE)
a man whose wife has died, especially one who has not remarried

Examples

old widowman
After his wife's passing, the lonely widowman kept mostly to himself in the old farmhouse.
In the village, people often offered support to the widowman after his great loss.
The story is about a widowman struggling to raise his children alone.
