slap

noun
a blow delivered with an open hand
Pronunciation: / slæp / (AmE), / slæp / (BrE)
a quick, sharp blow with the flat part of the hand or a flat object

Examples

a loud slap

She gave him a sharp slap on the cheek for being rude.
The sound of the slap echoed through the quiet room.
He received a stinging slap from his angry mother.
She gave him a slap across the face for his rude remark.
The sound of the slap echoed through the room.
