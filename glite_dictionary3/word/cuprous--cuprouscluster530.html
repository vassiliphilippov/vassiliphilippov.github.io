cuprous

adjective
containing copper in the +1 state
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːprəs / (AmE), / ˈkjuːprəs / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) containing copper with a valence of one; relating to copper in the +1 oxidation state.

Examples

cuprous oxide compound
Cuprous chloride is often used as a catalyst in organic synthesis.
Scientists studied the properties of various cuprous compounds in their experiment.
The formation of cuprous oxide occurs when copper reacts with oxygen at low temperatures.
