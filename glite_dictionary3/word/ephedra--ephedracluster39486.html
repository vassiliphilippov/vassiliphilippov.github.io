ephedra

noun
desert shrub with jointed stems and scalelike leaves
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛdrə / (AmE), / ɪˈfiːdrə / (BrE)
A genus of jointed, nearly leafless desert shrubs of the family Ephedraceae, having reduced, scalelike leaves and reddish fleshy seeds, often found in arid regions.
