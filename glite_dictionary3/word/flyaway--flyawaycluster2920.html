flyaway

adjective
loose or untidy (hair, clothing)
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪəˌweɪ / (BrE)
(of hair or clothing) loose, light, and tending to fly or move about instead of lying flat or staying in place.

Examples

flyaway hair
A gust of wind blew her flyaway hair across her face.
After running, her flyaway curls escaped from her ponytail.
He wore a flyaway scarf that fluttered in the breeze.
