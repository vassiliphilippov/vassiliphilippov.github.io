ethos

noun
distinctive spirit or values of a culture or era
Pronunciation: / ˈiːθɑs / (AmE), / ˈiːθɒs / (BrE)
The characteristic spirit, moral values, or guiding beliefs of a person, community, culture, or era.

Examples

ethos of a company
Many companies aim to cultivate an ethos of innovation and teamwork.
The ethos of the 1960s was characterized by a spirit of rebellion and desire for change.
The festival is shaped by the traditional ethos of the local community.
