Viminaria juncea

noun
Australian shrub with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / vɪməˈnɛəriə dʒuːnˈsiːə / (AmE), / vɪmɪˈnɑːrɪə dʒuːnˈsiːə / (BrE)
An Australian leafless shrub of the pea family, resembling broom and bearing small yellow flowers; commonly called the native broom.
