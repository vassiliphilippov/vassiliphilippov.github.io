to reclaim

verb
restore land or property to use
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkleɪm / (AmE), / rɪˈkleɪm / (BrE)
to make land or property usable again by improving or restoring it, often from a state of neglect or disuse

Examples

to reclaim farmland
Conservationists hope to reclaim parts of the wetland for native wildlife habitats.
Farmers worked together to reclaim the flood-damaged fields for agriculture.
The city launched a project to reclaim the abandoned railway yard and turn it into a park.
