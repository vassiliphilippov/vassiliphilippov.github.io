underdone

adjective
not sufficiently cooked
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈdʌn / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈdʌn / (BrE)
(of food) not cooked for long enough and therefore not ready to eat or lacking the desired doneness.

Examples

underdone steak
His omelet was underdone and still runny in the middle.
She prefers her vegetables slightly underdone for extra crunch.
The steak was underdone, so I sent it back to the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • antonym – overdone: (of food) cooked too much or for too long
