Tobit

noun
book of the Apocrypha; biblical story
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊbɪt / (AmE), / ˈtəʊbɪt / (BrE)
An Apocryphal book of the Bible, considered a popular novel for several centuries, telling the story of Tobit and his family.
