to hang

verb
to suspend meat to mature flavor
Pronunciation: / hæŋ / (AmE), / hæŋ / (BrE)
to suspend or attach something from above so that it is held up, displayed, or allowed to swing freely (such as a coat, picture, decoration, or meat)
