epistemologist

noun
specialist in the theory of knowledge
Pronunciation: / ɪˌpɪstəˈmɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɪˌpɪstəˈmɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in the theory of knowledge, especially in philosophical study of the nature, origin, and scope of knowledge

Examples

epistemologist studies knowledge
As an epistemologist, her research focuses on how we distinguish justified beliefs from opinions.
Many epistemologists have contributed to our understanding of how knowledge is acquired.
The philosophical debate was led by an epistemologist who challenged traditional notions of truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – epistemology: the theory of knowledge, especially regarding its methods, validity, and scope
