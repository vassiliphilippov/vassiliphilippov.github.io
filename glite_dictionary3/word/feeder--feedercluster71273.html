feeder

noun
animal being fattened for market
Pronunciation: / ˈfidər / (AmE), / ˈfiːdə / (BrE)
an animal, especially livestock, that is being fattened or is suitable for fattening for market

Examples

feeder cattle
The auction featured several pens of healthy feeder pigs.
The rancher bought a new group of feeders to raise for the fall sale.
Young cattle are often sold as feeders before being sent to feedlots.
