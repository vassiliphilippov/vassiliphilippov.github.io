to curry

verb
to groom an animal by brushing
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜːri / (AmE), / ˈkʌri / (BrE)
to groom an animal, especially by brushing its coat to give a neat appearance

Examples

to curry a horse
Grooms carefully curry their animals before a show to ensure they look their best.
Regularly currying your dog helps maintain a healthy and shiny coat.
She used a soft brush to curry the horse after riding.
