paraquat

noun
toxic herbicide chemical
Pronunciation: / ˈpærəˌkwɑt / (AmE), / ˈparəkwat / (BrE)
a poisonous yellow solid chemical used as a herbicide to control weeds

Examples

Exposure to paraquat can cause severe damage to the lungs and other organs.
Farmers often use paraquat to quickly clear fields of unwanted plants.
Improper handling of paraquat has led to several cases of poisoning in rural areas.
