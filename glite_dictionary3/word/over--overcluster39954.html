over

adjective
finished; concluded
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of an event or activity) having come or been brought to a conclusion; finished or ended.

The meeting is finally over, so we can all go home.
Their marriage was over after years of trying to make it work.
