teleworking

noun
working remotely using telecommunications
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of working from home or another location outside the traditional office, using telecommunications to communicate with the workplace.

Examples

teleworking from home
Due to the pandemic, teleworking became the default arrangement for most office workers.
Many employees have shifted to teleworking to reduce commuting time and increase flexibility.
The company provides all necessary tools for teleworking staff to stay connected and productive.
