funkiness

noun
quality of being original or quirky
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkinəs / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkinəs / (BrE)
The quality of being original, offbeat, unconventional, or eccentric, often with a distinctive or quirky style.

Examples

creative funkiness
He admired the band's funkiness and refusal to follow mainstream trends.
Her outfits always have a touch of funkiness that makes her stand out in the crowd.
The restaurant's decor combines elegance with just the right amount of funkiness.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conventionality: the quality of conforming to traditional standards
  • synonym – originality: the quality of being novel or unique
