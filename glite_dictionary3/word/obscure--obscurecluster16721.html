obscure

adjective
not easily noticed or attention-drawing
Pronunciation: / əbˈskjʊr / (AmE), / əbˈskjʊə / (BrE)
not easily noticed or attracting attention; inconspicuous or unremarkable.

Examples

obscure stain
An obscure stain on her shirt escaped everyone's attention during the meeting.
He wore an obscure badge that only a few dedicated fans could recognize.
The artist repaired the wall so skillfully that the obscure crack went unnoticed.
