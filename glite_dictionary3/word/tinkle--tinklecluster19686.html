to tinkle

verb
emit a high, light ringing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋ.kəl / (BrE)
to make or emit a light, high, ringing sound, like that of small bells or glass

Examples

to tinkle like glass
Coins tinkled as she dropped them into the glass jar.
I heard wind chimes tinkling outside the window as I fell asleep.
The little bell began to tinkle softly in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chime: to make a series of melodious ringing sounds, especially by bells
  • synonym – jingle: to make light ringing sounds, often repeatedly, like small metal objects knocking together
