shuttlecock

noun
badminton object with feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌt̬əlˌkɑk / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtəlkɒk / (BrE)
A light, cone-shaped object with a rounded end, typically made of cork or rubber and fitted with feathers or synthetic materials, that is struck back and forth in badminton and similar games.

Examples

hit a shuttlecock
After losing the shuttlecock in the bushes, the game had to pause until they found it.
Professional players often prefer feather shuttlecocks to those made of synthetic materials.
She hit the shuttlecock over the net with a swift flick of her wrist.

Related Terms

  • synonym – birdie: another term for shuttlecock, mainly North American
