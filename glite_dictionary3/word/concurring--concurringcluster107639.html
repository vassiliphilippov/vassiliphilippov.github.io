concurring

adjective
being of the same opinion
Pronunciation: / kənˈkɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈkɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of people or opinions) having the same opinion or agreeing with each other.

Examples

The judge wrote a concurring opinion supporting the court's decision.
Their concurring views helped resolve the debate quickly.
