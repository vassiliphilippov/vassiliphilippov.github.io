revers

noun
a turned-back lapel on a garment
Pronunciation: / rəˈvɛrz / (AmE), / rəˈvɛː / (BrE)
A lapel or turned-back part of a garment, especially on a woman's coat or dress, which shows the reverse side of the fabric.

Examples

revers on a coat
Designers often use contrasting fabrics on the revers to add visual interest to a garment.
She adjusted the revers of her jacket so that the patterned fabric was visible.
The coat featured elegant revers lined with silk, giving it a luxurious look.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lapel: the part on each side of a coat or jacket immediately below the collar that is folded back on either side of the front opening
