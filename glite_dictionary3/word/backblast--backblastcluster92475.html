backblast

noun
rearward blast from a weapon
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌblæst / (AmE), / ˈbækˌblɑːst / (BrE)
the blast of hot gases and debris directed backward from a recoilless weapon when it is fired

Examples

backblast from a rocket
Always check the area behind you for potential dangers before firing a rocket to avoid the backblast.
In confined spaces, the backblast from the launcher can cause serious injuries.
The soldier warned everyone to stand clear of the backblast zone during the exercise.
