sour

adjective
inaccurate musical pitch
Pronunciation: / saʊɚ / (AmE), / saʊə / (BrE)
(of a musical note or sound) not accurately pitched; out of tune.

Examples

sour note singing
His singing was sometimes sour, especially on the high notes.
The band sounded sour when their instruments weren't properly tuned.
The violinist played a sour note during her solo.

Related Terms

  • synonym – off-key: not having the correct musical pitch
  • antonym – tuneful: having a pleasant or correct musical pitch
