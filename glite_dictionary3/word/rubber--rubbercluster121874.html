rubber

noun
waterproof overshoe
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə / (BrE)
A waterproof overshoe made of rubber, worn over ordinary shoes to protect them from water or snow.

Examples

rubber boots
After the snowstorm, she wore rubber to keep her feet dry.
He put on his rubber before walking through the wet grass.
Please take off your rubber at the door to avoid tracking mud inside.
