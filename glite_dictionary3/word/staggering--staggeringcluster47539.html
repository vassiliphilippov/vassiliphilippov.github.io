staggering

adjective
extremely impressive or overwhelming
Pronunciation: / ˈstæɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstæɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
So great, impressive, or surprising as to shock or overwhelm.

Examples

staggering amount

The final cost of the new stadium was staggering and far exceeded initial estimates.
The statistics about global poverty rates are simply staggering.
