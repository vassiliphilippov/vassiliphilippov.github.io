mew

noun
the soft cry of a cat
Pronunciation: / mjuː / (AmE), / mjuː / (BrE)
a soft, high-pitched sound made by a cat, or any similar sound

Examples

a cat's mew
From behind the door, she heard a faint mew in the darkness.
The kitten let out a tiny mew as it searched for its mother.
The lost cat's constant mew made it easy for us to find her.

Related Terms

  • synonym – miaow: the characteristic crying sound of a cat
  • antonym – purr: a low, continuous, vibrating sound made by a cat
