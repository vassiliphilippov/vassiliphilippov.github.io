oesophagoscope

noun
instrument for examining the esophagus
Pronunciation: / iːˈsɑːfəɡəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ɪˌsɒfəˈɡəʊskəʊp / (BrE)
A medical instrument fitted with an optical device for examining the inside of the esophagus.

Examples

use an oesophagoscope to examine
A skilled doctor used an oesophagoscope to inspect the patient's esophagus for signs of disease.
Modern oesophagoscopes allow for both diagnostic and minor surgical procedures within the esophagus.
The oesophagoscope was carefully inserted to avoid causing discomfort to the patient.
