to propose

verb
suggest something for consideration
Pronunciation: / prəˈpoʊz / (AmE), / prəˈpəʊz / (BrE)
to suggest a plan, idea, or course of action for others to consider

Examples

to propose a plan
At the meeting, she proposed that the policy be revised to address new challenges.
He is proposing a new method to make the process more efficient.
Several experts proposed alternative solutions to the issue of climate change.
If you have a better solution, please propose it now.
She proposed that we take a break and resume work in the morning.
The committee will propose new guidelines at the next meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – oppose: to disagree with or resist a plan or suggestion
  • synonym – recommend: to suggest something as being good or suitable
  • synonym – suggest: to put forward an idea or plan for consideration
