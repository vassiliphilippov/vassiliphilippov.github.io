high-rise

noun
tall multistoried building
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌraɪz / (BrE)
a tall building with many floors, usually containing offices or apartments

Examples

modern high-rise apartment
Many modern high-rises use environmentally friendly building materials.
She bought an apartment on the twelfth floor of a high-rise.
The city skyline is dominated by a new high-rise near the river.
