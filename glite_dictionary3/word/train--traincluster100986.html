to train

verb
travel by train
Pronunciation: / treɪn / (AmE), / treɪn / (BrE)
to travel somewhere by train as a means of transport

Examples

to train across Europe
Many tourists train across Europe to enjoy the scenery.
She trained to Berlin last summer for a music festival.
We decided to train from Paris to Geneva instead of flying.
