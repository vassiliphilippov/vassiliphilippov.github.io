to legitimise

verb
make legal or officially acceptable
Pronunciation: / lɪˈdʒɪtəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / lɪˈdʒɪ.tɪ.maɪz / (BrE)

Examples

to legitimise same-sex marriage
New laws were introduced to legitimise same-sex marriage.
Some countries have moved to legitimise the use of medical marijuana.
The government decided to legitimise previously unregulated financial practices.
