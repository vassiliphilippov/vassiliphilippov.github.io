transistorised

adjective
equipped with transistors
Pronunciation: / trænˈzɪstɚˌaɪzd / (AmE), / trænˈzɪstəˌaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an electronic device) equipped with or using transistors instead of earlier electronic components such as vacuum tubes.

Examples

transistorised radio
Engineers were proud of their transistorised designs, which replaced bulky vacuum tubes.
The company released a new transistorised radio in the 1960s, revolutionising portable music.
This transistorised amplifier is much more reliable than earlier valve models.
