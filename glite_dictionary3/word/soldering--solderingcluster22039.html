soldering

noun
joining metals by melting solder
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːdərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒdərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of joining two or more metal objects together by melting and applying solder to the joint

Examples

soldering metal wires
During the workshop, students practiced soldering for the first time using copper pipes.
Proper soldering ensures a secure and reliable connection between the wires.
The technician was skilled at soldering tiny electronic components onto the circuit board.

Related Terms

  • synonym – brazing: joining metals with a filler metal with a lower melting point than the workpieces
  • synonym – welding: joining metals by melting their surfaces together
