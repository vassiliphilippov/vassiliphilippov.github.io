haystack

noun
large pile of hay
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌstæk / (AmE), / ˈheɪstak / (BrE)
A large, usually outdoor pile or stack of hay, typically shaped to shed water and used for storage.

Examples

a haystack in a field
After the harvest, the farmers built a huge haystack near the barn.
Looking for her lost earring in the haystack was nearly impossible.
The children played hide-and-seek around the old haystack in the field.
