hypodermic

noun
syringe with needle for injections
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ.pəˈdɝː.mɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.pəˈdɜː.mɪk / (BrE)
A medical syringe fitted with a hollow needle, used for injecting substances under the skin.

Examples

hypodermic needle
He reached into his bag for a hypodermic, preparing the injection with care.
Some hospitals now use disposable hypodermics to prevent contamination.
The nurse sterilized the hypodermic before administering the vaccine.
