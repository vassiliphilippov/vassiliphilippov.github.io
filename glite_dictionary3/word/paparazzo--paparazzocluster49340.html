paparazzo

noun
freelance celebrity photographer
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːpəˈrɑːtsoʊ / (AmE), / ˌpapəˈrattsəʊ / (BrE)
a freelance photographer who follows celebrities in order to take candid photographs for sale to newspapers or magazines

Examples

paparazzo chases celebrities
A paparazzo waited outside the restaurant, hoping to capture an exclusive photo of the actress.
As a seasoned paparazzo, he knew all the best places to spot famous people in Los Angeles.
The movie star sued a paparazzo for invasion of privacy after her picture appeared in a tabloid.
