acerbity

noun
sharp, sour taste
Pronunciation: / əˈsɝːbɪti / (AmE), / əˈsɜːbɪti / (BrE)
The quality of having a sharp, sour, or bitter taste.

Examples

acerbity of taste
Some green apples have a distinct acerbity that many people enjoy.
The acerbity of the lemon juice made my lips pucker.
The chef balanced the acerbity of the sauce with a touch of honey.

  • synonym – acidity: the quality of being acid or sour
  • antonym – sweetness: the quality of having a sweet taste
