Tulipa gesneriana

noun
a species of cultivated tulip
Pronunciation: / tuˈliːpə ˌɡɛz.nəˈraɪ.ə.nə / (AmE), / tjuːˈliːpə ˌɡɛz.nəˈraɪ.ə.nə / (BrE)
A species of tall, late-blooming tulip widely cultivated for its brightly colored flowers, considered the type species of garden tulips.
