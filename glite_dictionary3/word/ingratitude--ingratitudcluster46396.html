ingratitude

noun
lack of gratitude
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡrætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡrætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
Lack of gratitude; failure or refusal to show thanks or appreciation.

Examples

show ingratitude to parents
His ingratitude after all we did for him was deeply disappointing.
She regretted expressing her ingratitude so harshly to her parents.
The charity workers were discouraged by the ingratitude of some recipients.

  • synonym – thankfulness: the state of being thankful
