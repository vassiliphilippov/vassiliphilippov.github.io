to buy

verb
accept or believe as true (informal)
Pronunciation: / baɪ / (AmE), / baɪ / (BrE)
(informal) To accept or believe that something is true, especially when expressed as a claim or story.

Examples

to buy his story
I'm sorry, but I don't buy his explanation for missing the meeting.
Many people were skeptical and refused to buy the politician's promise.
She couldn't buy the idea that it was just an accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym – believe: to accept that something is true or real
  • antonym – doubt: to feel uncertain about something; to not believe
