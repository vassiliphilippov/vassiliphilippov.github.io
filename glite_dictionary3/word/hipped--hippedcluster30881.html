hipped

adjective
roof with slopes on all sides
Pronunciation: / hɪpt / (AmE), / hɪpt / (BrE)
(of a roof) having all sides sloping downwards to the walls, usually without vertical ends or gables.

Examples

hipped roof design
A hipped roof is often used in areas prone to strong winds.
Many bungalow designs include a hipped roof for added stability.
The old cottage features a hipped roof covered with slate tiles.
