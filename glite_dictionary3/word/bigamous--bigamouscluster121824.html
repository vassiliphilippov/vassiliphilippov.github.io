bigamous

adjective
relating to illegal second marriage
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡəməs / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡəməs / (BrE)
Relating to or involving the crime of marrying someone while already legally married to another person.

Examples

bigamous marriage
He was charged with entering into a bigamous marriage while his first wife was still alive.
The defendant pleaded guilty to maintaining a bigamous relationship for several years before being caught.
Their bigamous union was annulled when authorities discovered his previous marriage.

Related Terms

  • antonym – monogamous: having only one spouse at a time
  • degree higher – polygamous: having more than one spouse at the same time (may be legal in some places)
