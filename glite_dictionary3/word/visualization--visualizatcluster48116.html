visualization

noun
graphical representation of data
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪʒuələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɪʒuː(ə)laɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a graphical representation of data or information (such as charts, graphs, or dashboards) used to explore, analyze, or communicate patterns, relationships, or insights.

Examples

data visualization chart
The statistician created a visualization of the population data to reveal trends.
Their dashboard included an interactive visualization that allowed managers to explore monthly sales.
Students learned how to design effective visualizations that highlight key patterns without misleading readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chart: a visual display of information, often using bars, lines, or pies to show numerical data
  • synonym – data visualization: the practice and field concerned with creating graphical representations of data
