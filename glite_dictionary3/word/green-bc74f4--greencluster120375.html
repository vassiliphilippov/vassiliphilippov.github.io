green

adjective
naive or inexperienced person
Pronunciation: / ɡrin / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
(of a person) lacking experience or sophistication; naive and easily deceived or tricked.

Examples

green worker
As a recent graduate, she was still quite green in the workplace and made some rookie mistakes.
He’s too green to be left in charge of such an important project on his own.
When I first started investing, I was so green that I believed almost anything I was told.

Related Terms

  • antonym – experienced: having knowledge or skill from doing something for a long time
  • synonym – gullible: easily deceived or tricked
  • synonym – naive: showing lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment
