unprepossessing

adjective
not attractive or impressive
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌpriːpəˈzɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌpriːpəˈzɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
not attractive or impressive in appearance, making a neutral or unfavorable first impression.

Examples

an unprepossessing building
Despite its unprepossessing exterior, the restaurant serves excellent food.
His unprepossessing manner hid a sharp wit and a kind heart.
She found the unprepossessing house surprisingly cozy on the inside.
