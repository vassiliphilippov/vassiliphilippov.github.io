to infest

verb
overrun computer systems with malware
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛst / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛst / (BrE)
(of malware, bots, or other malicious programs) to spread through and overrun a computer system, network, or online space in large numbers, causing disruption, damage, or unauthorized access.

Examples

to infest a computer
After the phishing attack, hackers infested the company's network with ransomware overnight.
Security software failed to detect the botnet that began infesting hundreds of IoT devices last month.
When the USB drive was plugged in, malicious scripts started to infest her laptop immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym – compromise: to make a system vulnerable to or subject to unauthorized access
  • synonym – infect: to introduce harmful software or code into a computer system
