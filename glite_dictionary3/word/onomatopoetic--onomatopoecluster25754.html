onomatopoetic

adjective
relating to onomatopoeia (sound imitation)
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːnəˌmætəpoʊˈɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒnəˌmætəʊpəʊˈɛtɪk / (BrE)
(of a word) relating to or imitating natural sounds
