seasickness

noun
nausea from motion at sea
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌsɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌsɪknəs / (BrE)
A feeling of nausea and dizziness caused by the motion of a boat or ship on the water.

Examples

seasickness on a boat
Ginger tea is a common remedy for seasickness among sailors and passengers alike.
He suffered from seasickness throughout the entire cruise, barely leaving his cabin.
Her first sailing trip was ruined by a bout of seasickness that lasted hours.

  • synonym – motion sickness: nausea caused by movement, especially in a vehicle
