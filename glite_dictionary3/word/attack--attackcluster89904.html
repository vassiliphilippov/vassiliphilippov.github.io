to attack

verb
criticize strongly (in speech or writing)
Pronunciation: / əˈtæk / (AmE), / əˈtæk / (BrE)
to criticize or condemn someone or something vigorously in speech or writing

Examples

to attack an idea
His article attacks the government's economic policy with strong arguments.
The critics attacked the film's weak storyline and poor acting.
The senator publicly attacked his opponent for her voting record.

Related Terms

  • synonym – criticize: to express disapproval or find fault with
