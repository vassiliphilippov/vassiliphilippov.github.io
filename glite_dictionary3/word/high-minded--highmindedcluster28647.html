high-minded

adjective
having noble or lofty principles
Pronunciation: / haɪˈmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / haɪˈmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having strong moral principles or intellectual ideals; noble and lofty in thought or purpose.

Examples

high-minded ideals
Despite his reputation for being high-minded, he always remained approachable to his colleagues.
Her high-minded ideals sometimes made her seem removed from the practical realities of daily life.
The committee was praised for its high-minded approach to reforming the justice system.
