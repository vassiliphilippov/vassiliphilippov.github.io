hawker

noun
person who trains hawks for falconry
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔkər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who breeds and trains hawks, especially for the sport of falconry

Examples

hawker trains hawks
Every hawker must develop a strong bond with his or her bird to succeed.
She became a skilled hawker after years of studying the art of falconry.
