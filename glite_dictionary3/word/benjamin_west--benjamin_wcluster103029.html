Benjamin West

proper noun
English painter and Royal Academy president
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndʒəmɪn wɛst / (AmE), / ˈbɛndʒəmɪn wɛst / (BrE)
(1738–1820) An English painter born in America, known for serving as the second president of the Royal Academy and for his historical paintings.
