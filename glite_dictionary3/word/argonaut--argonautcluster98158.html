Argonaut

noun
hero who sailed with Jason for the Golden Fleece
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡəˌnɔt / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡənɔːt / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) one of the band of heroes who accompanied Jason in his quest to find the Golden Fleece.
