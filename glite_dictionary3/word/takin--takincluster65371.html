takin

noun
Himalayan goat-antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːkɪn / (AmE), / ˈteɪkɪn / (BrE)
A large, stout goat-antelope native to the eastern Himalayas, known for its thick, shaggy coat and curved horns.

Examples

wild takin herd
The takin is the national animal of Bhutan and features in local folklore.
The takin is well-adapted to cold mountainous environments in the eastern Himalayas.
Wildlife photographers often travel to Bhutan to capture images of the elusive takin.
