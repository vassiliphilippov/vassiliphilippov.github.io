spoonflower

noun
tropical plant with edible tubers
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːnˌflaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈspuːnˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A tropical American plant of the aroid family with edible tubers that are cooked and eaten like yams or potatoes.
