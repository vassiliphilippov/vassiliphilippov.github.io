to finger

verb
indicate fingering in music notation
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
to indicate which fingers should be used to play notes or chords on a musical score, especially for keyboard instruments

Examples
