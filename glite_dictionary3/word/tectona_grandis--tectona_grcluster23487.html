Tectona grandis

noun
species of tree yielding teak wood
Pronunciation: / tɛkˈtoʊnə ˈɡrændɪs / (AmE), / tɛkˈtəʊnə ˈɡrændɪs / (BrE)
A tall tropical tree native to South and Southeast Asia, cultivated for its hard, durable wood known as teak.
