unaffected

adjective
not changed; unchanged by influence or action
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈfɛk.tɪd /; / ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈfɛk.tɪd /; / ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪd / (BrE)
not changed, influenced, or affected by something; not showing any reaction or emotion
