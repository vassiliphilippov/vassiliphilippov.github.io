mantilla

noun
woman's silk or lace head scarf
Pronunciation: / mænˈtiːjə / (AmE), / mænˈtɪl.jə / (BrE)
a woman's scarf of silk or lace, worn over the head and shoulders, especially in Spain and South America

Examples

lace mantilla scarf
