surname

noun
family name; last name
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.neɪm / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.neɪm / (BrE)
A family name shared by members of a family, identifying them as distinct from others; usually inherited and placed after the given name in English-speaking cultures.
