familiarly

adverb
in an intimately familiar way
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪljɚli / (AmE), / fəˈmɪliəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows close acquaintance or familiarity; in an intimately familiar manner.

Examples

speak familiarly with friends
He was familiarly addressed by his childhood nickname at home.
She greeted the manager familiarly, having worked with him for years.
The author was familiarly known to her readers as 'Beth'.
