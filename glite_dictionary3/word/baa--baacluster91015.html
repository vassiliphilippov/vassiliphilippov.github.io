baa

noun
the cry made by sheep
Pronunciation: / bɑ / (AmE), / bɑː / (BrE)
the sound made by a sheep or lamb, typically written as 'baa' to represent the animal's cry

Examples

a sheep's baa
The children's book described the gentle baa of the newborn sheep.
The loud baa of a lamb echoed across the field.
We heard a distant baa as the farmer herded his flock.

  • synonym – bleat: the cry of a sheep, goat, or calf
