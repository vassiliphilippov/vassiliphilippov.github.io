to flyfish

verb
to fish with artificial flies
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌfɪʃ / (BrE)

Examples

Many anglers travel to remote rivers specifically to flyfish for trout and salmon.
She loves to flyfish in mountain streams every summer.
