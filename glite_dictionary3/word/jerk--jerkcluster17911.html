jerk

noun
Jamaican spicy seasoning and cooking style
Pronunciation: / dʒɜrk / (AmE), / dʒɜːk / (BrE)
(in cooking) a Jamaican style of seasoning and cooking, using a spicy dry rub or marinade and grilling or smoking meat or fish.

Examples

jerk chicken marinade
The restaurant's jerk chicken, marinated in a fiery spice rub, drew crowds nightly.
She learned to make authentic jerk at a cooking class during her trip to Jamaica.
For a smoky flavor, grill the pork over pimento wood after applying the jerk rub.

Related Terms

  • synonym – barbecue: a method of cooking meat over fire or smoke; similar cooking technique to jerk
  • synonym – rub: a mixture of spices applied to meat before cooking; similar to a jerk seasoning
