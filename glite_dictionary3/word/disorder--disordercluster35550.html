disorder

noun
disturbance of normal physical or mental function
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɔrdɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈsɔːdə / (BrE)
A physical or medical condition in which normal functioning of the body or mind is disturbed.

Examples

mental disorder diagnosed
He was diagnosed with a rare eating disorder that required special treatment.
Many sleep disorders can be managed with lifestyle changes.
The new drug is used to treat anxiety disorder in adults.

Related Terms

  • synonym – condition: state of health, often negative
  • synonym – disease: illness affecting the body or mind
  • antonym – health: the state of being well
