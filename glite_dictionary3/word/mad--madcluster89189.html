mad

adjective
very foolish or impractical
Pronunciation: / mæd / (AmE), / mad / (BrE)
(of ideas or actions) very foolish or lacking in good sense; wildly impractical or irrational.

Examples

mad idea
His friends thought it was mad to invest all his savings in such a risky project.
It was a mad plan to try to cross the desert without enough water.
She came up with a mad idea for turning the old bus into a flying machine.
