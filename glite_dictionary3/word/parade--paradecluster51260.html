to parade

verb
to display oneself showily
Pronunciation: / pəˈreɪd / (AmE), / pəˈreɪd / (BrE)
to walk or display oneself in a way that attracts attention or shows off, especially in an ostentatious manner

Examples

to parade new clothes
He enjoys parading his achievements at every opportunity.
She loves to parade her latest fashions in front of her friends.
The celebrity paraded down the red carpet, waving to the crowd.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hide: to conceal or keep from being seen
  • synonym – show off: to display oneself or something ostentatiously
