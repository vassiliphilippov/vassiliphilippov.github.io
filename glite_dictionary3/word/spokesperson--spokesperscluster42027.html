spokesperson

noun
person representing a group or organization
Pronunciation: / ˈspoʊksˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈspəʊksˌpɜːs(ə)n / (BrE)
A person who speaks as the representative of a group, organization, or another individual, presenting their views or policies to the public or media.

Examples

spokesperson for the company
During the press conference, the spokesperson clarified the organization's position on environmental issues.
Each department selected a spokesperson to address the concerns of their team members.
The company's spokesperson issued an official statement regarding the product recall.

Related Terms

  • synonym – advocate: a person who publicly supports a cause or policy
  • antonym – opponent: someone who is against or speaks in opposition
  • synonym – representative: a person chosen or appointed to act or speak for others
