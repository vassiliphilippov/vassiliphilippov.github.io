to flight

verb
to equip or decorate with feathers
Pronunciation: / flaɪt / (AmE), / flaɪt / (BrE)
to decorate or equip (especially an arrow) with feathers

Examples

to flight an arrow
She learned how to flight arrows for the local archery club.
The craftsman carefully flights each arrow before testing its accuracy.
Traditionally, hunters would flight their arrows using goose feathers.
