Brahmin

noun
mutated two-headed cattle (Fallout game)
Pronunciation: / br51d0m6an / (AmE), / br51d0m6an / (BrE)
(in the Fallout video game series) a large, mutated two-headed (or multi-headed) bovine creature kept as livestock and commonly encountered in the game's post-apocalyptic wasteland.
