acidic

adjective
having acid properties or a pH below 7
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪdɪk / (AmE), / əˈsɪdɪk / (BrE)
having the properties of an acid; containing acid or having a pH below 7.

Examples

acidic soil
Poor soil can become acidic, making it hard for some plants to grow.
The lemon juice tasted extremely acidic on my tongue.
The scientist measured the solution and confirmed it was highly acidic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acid: a substance with acidic properties
  • antonym – alkaline: having a pH greater than 7; opposite of acidic
