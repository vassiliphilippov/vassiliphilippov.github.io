cerulean

adjective
deep sky-blue color
Pronunciation: / səˈruːliən / (AmE), / sɪˈruːliən / (BrE)
Deep blue in color, resembling the clear sky on a bright day, sometimes with a purplish tint.

Examples

cerulean sky color
She wore a cerulean dress that matched the clear sky.
The artist painted the sea in a vivid cerulean that captivated everyone.
The mountain lake reflected the cerulean hue of an autumn afternoon.
