shortener

noun
service that shortens URLs
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u0283\u0254rt\u0259n\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0283\u0254\u02d0t\u0259n\u0259 / (BrE)
A service or tool that converts long web addresses (URLs) into shorter links, often to simplify sharing or to track clicks.

Examples

URL shortener tool
The platform offers a shortener that turns long URLs into short links for sharing.
She used several shorteners to compare link-tracking statistics across different social platforms.
Before posting, he checked the shortener's privacy settings to avoid exposing user data.
