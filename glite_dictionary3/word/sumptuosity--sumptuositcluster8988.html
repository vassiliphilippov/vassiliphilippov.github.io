sumptuosity

noun
luxurious or costly quality
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌmp.tʃuˈɑː.sɪ.ti / (AmE), / ˌsʌmp.tjʊˈɒs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being exceptionally luxurious, costly, or extravagant

Examples

sumptuosity of a palace
Guests were amazed at the sumptuosity of the wedding banquet.
He was criticized for the sumptuosity of his lifestyle during times of economic hardship.
The sumptuosity of the palace was evident in its gilded ceilings and marble halls.
