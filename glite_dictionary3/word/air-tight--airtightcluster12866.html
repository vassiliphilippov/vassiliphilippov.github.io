air-tight

adjective
completely sealed, not allowing air in or out
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈeəˌtaɪt / (BrE)
not allowing air or gas to pass in or out; completely sealed against air leakage

Examples

air-tight container
He packed his clothes in an air-tight bag to protect them from moisture.
Make sure the container is air-tight or the food will spoil quickly.
The scientist used an air-tight chamber to conduct the experiment safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hermetically sealed: completely airtight, especially so as to be impervious to air or gas
  • antonym – leaky: allowing liquid or gas to pass through unintentionally
