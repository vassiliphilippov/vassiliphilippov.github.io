ombudsman

noun
official investigating complaints against authority
Pronunciation: / ɑmˈbʊdzmən / (AmE), / ɒmˈbʊdzmən / (BrE)
an official appointed to investigate individuals' complaints against public authorities or organizations, especially in cases of maladministration

Examples

the ombudsman investigates complaints
If you feel you have been treated unfairly by a public authority, you can contact the ombudsman.
The ombudsman acts independently to ensure that government decisions are accountable and transparent.
The ombudsman launched an inquiry into the city's handling of citizen complaints.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mediator: a person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement
