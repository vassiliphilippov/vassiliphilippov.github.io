chastisement

noun
a reprimand or rebuke
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæstɪzˌmɛnt /; / ˈtʃæstaɪzmənt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːstɪzmənt /; / ˈtʃæstɪzmənt / (BrE)
the act of criticizing, rebuking, or scolding someone for a mistake or wrongdoing, especially as a form of correction or punishment
