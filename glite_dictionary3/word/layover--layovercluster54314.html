layover

noun
brief stop during a journey
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪˌoʊvər / (AmE), / ˈleɪˌəʊvə / (BrE)
a short stay between parts of a journey, especially when traveling by air.

Examples

airport layover
During the layover, she grabbed a coffee and checked her emails.
Their trip included a brief layover in Paris, giving them time to see the Eiffel Tower.
We had a three-hour layover in Chicago before catching our connecting flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stopover: a break in a journey with the intention of continuing later
