hutzpah

noun
shameless audacity; boldness
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊtspɑ / (AmE), / ˈhʊtspɑː / (BrE)
unusual boldness or audacity; shameless self-confidence or nerve, often in a way that is shocking or disrespectful

Examples

incredible hutzpah
It takes a lot of hutzpah to criticize your boss in front of everyone.
Only someone with real hutzpah would try to talk their way out of that situation.
She showed incredible hutzpah when she demanded a pay raise at her first meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – audacity: boldness or daring, often with disregard for normal restraints
  • synonym – insolence: rude or disrespectful boldness
