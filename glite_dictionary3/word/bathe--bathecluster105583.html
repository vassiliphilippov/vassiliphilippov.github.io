to bathe

verb
envelop or suffuse with light, liquid, etc.
Pronunciation: / beɪð / (AmE), / bɑːð / (BrE)
to suffuse, envelop, or cover something with a particular quality, substance, or light, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

bathed in light
Her face was bathed in tears after hearing the news.
The garden was bathed in the soft glow of the early morning sun.
The mountains were bathed in a golden light at sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym – immerse: to submerge or involve deeply
