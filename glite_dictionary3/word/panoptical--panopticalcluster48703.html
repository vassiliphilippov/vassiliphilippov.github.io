panoptical

adjective
all-encompassing or comprehensive in view
Pronunciation: / pænˈɑptɪkəl / (AmE), / pænˈɒptɪkəl / (BrE)
Providing a comprehensive or all-encompassing view; including everything visible in a single glance.

Examples

panoptical view of city
She created a panoptical map displaying every detail of the landscape.
The panoptical report presented an overview of all the company's operations.
The architect designed a panoptical observation deck that overlooks the entire city.
