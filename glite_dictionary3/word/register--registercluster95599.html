to register

verb
show a value on a measuring device
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstər / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
(of a gauge or instrument) to show or display a particular reading, measurement, or amount.

Examples

register a temperature
Her blood pressure monitor registered a sudden drop during the test.
The meter registers the amount of electricity consumed each month.
The pressure gauge registered a dangerously high level.

Related Terms

  • synonym – indicate: to point out or show, especially a measurement
  • synonym – show: to display or indicate something
