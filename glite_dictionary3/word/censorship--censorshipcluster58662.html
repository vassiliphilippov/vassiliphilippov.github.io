censorship

noun
suppression of information or content
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsərˌʃɪp /; / ˈsɛnsɚˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈsensəʃɪp /; / ˈsɛnsəʃɪp / (BrE)
the practice or system of removing, suppressing, or prohibiting parts of books, films, news, or other information that are considered objectionable or sensitive
