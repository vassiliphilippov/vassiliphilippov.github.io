predatory

adjective
hunting and eating other animals
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛdəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈprɛdətəri / (BrE)
(Of an animal) living by hunting and consuming other animals for food.

Examples

predatory animal hunts
A predatory fish stalked the smaller fish hiding among the rocks.
Many predatory animals have sharp claws and teeth for catching their prey.
Owls are predatory birds that hunt mainly at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – carnivorous: (Of an animal) feeding on other animals
