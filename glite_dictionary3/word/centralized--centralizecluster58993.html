centralized

adjective
under central control or authority
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈsɛntrəlaɪzd / (BrE)
Controlled, managed, or organized by a single central authority or brought together in one place.

Examples

centralized power structure
Disaster response is often more effective when centralized authority coordinates resources and personnel.
In a centralized government, power is concentrated in a national rather than a local administration.
The company adopted a centralized system to streamline its operations and increase efficiency.
