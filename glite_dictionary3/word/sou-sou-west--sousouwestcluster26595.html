sou'-sou'-west

noun
compass point between south and southwest
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊ sʊˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌsaʊ sʊˈwɛst / (BrE)
the compass point that lies halfway between south and southwest, corresponding to a bearing of 202.5 degrees
