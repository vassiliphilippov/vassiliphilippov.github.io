grain

noun
small particle of a substance
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪn / (AmE), / ɡreɪn / (BrE)
a small, hard particle of a substance, such as sand, salt, or sugar

Examples

a grain of sand
A grain of salt can make a big difference in the recipe.
Not a single grain of sugar was left in the jar.
She found a grain of sand stuck between her toes after leaving the beach.
