Lamellibranchia

noun
class of bivalve mollusks
Pronunciation: / ləˌmɛlɪˈbræŋkiə / (AmE), / ləˌmɛlɪˈbraŋkɪə /; / læˌmɛlɪˈbraŋkɪə / (BrE)
