iguanodon

noun
large herbivorous dinosaur of the Cretaceous period
Pronunciation: / ɪˈɡwɑːnəˌdɑn / (AmE), / ɪˈɡwɑːnədɒn / (BrE)
a large, herbivorous dinosaur of the early Cretaceous period, characterized by a long heavy tail and thumb spikes, formerly found in Europe and northern Africa
