to hole

verb
hit a ball into the hole (golf, etc.)
Pronunciation: / hoʊl / (AmE), / həʊl / (BrE)
(in golf or similar sports) to hit a ball into the hole, finishing the play for that hole.

Examples

to hole the ball
After several attempts, she finally holed the ball on the eighth green.
He holed his putt from twenty feet to win the tournament.
If you hole your shot from the bunker, it's an impressive achievement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sink: to hit a ball into a hole or pocket, especially in billiards or golf
