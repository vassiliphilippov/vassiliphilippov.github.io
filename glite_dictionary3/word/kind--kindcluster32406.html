kind

adjective
agreeable or beneficial (of conditions)
Pronunciation: / kaɪnd / (AmE), / kaɪnd / (BrE)
(of an environment or condition) agreeable or beneficial; conducive to comfort or well-being.

Examples

kind weather
Mountain weather can be unpredictable, but today it was unusually kind for our climb.
The cool sea breeze was especially kind to the hikers after their long trek.
This lotion is kind to sensitive skin, making it suitable for everyday use.
