ascetical

adjective
relating to rigorous self-discipline
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / əˈsɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by strict self-discipline and avoiding indulgence, especially for religious or spiritual reasons
