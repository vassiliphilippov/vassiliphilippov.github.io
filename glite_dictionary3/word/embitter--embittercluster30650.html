to embitter

verb
make resentful or bitter
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbɪtər / (AmE), / ɪmˈbɪtə / (BrE)
to make someone feel bitter or resentful

Examples

to embitter a person
The harsh policies embittered many people in the community.
The unfair criticism only served to embitter him further.
Years of rejection had embittered her perspective on life.
