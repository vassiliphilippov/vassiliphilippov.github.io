to advertize

verb
promote or publicize a product or service
Pronunciation: / ˈædvərˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈadvətaɪz / (BrE)
to make a product or service known to the public, especially in order to sell it; to promote through publicity

Examples

to advertize a product
If you want to attract more customers, you should advertize your services online.
The company plans to advertize its latest smartphones on television and social media.
They hired a marketing agency to advertize the new restaurant downtown.
