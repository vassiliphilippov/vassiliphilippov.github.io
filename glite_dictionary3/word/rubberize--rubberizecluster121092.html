to rubberize

verb
to coat with rubber
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌb.əˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.ə.raɪz / (BrE)
to coat or impregnate (a material) with rubber, especially to make it waterproof or more durable

Examples

Factories often rubberize containers to prevent leaks and corrosion.
The company will rubberize the soles of the shoes to improve their grip.
