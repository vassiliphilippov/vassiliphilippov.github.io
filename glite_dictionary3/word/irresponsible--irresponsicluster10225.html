irresponsible

adjective
not showing responsibility
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɪspənˈsɪbəl / (AmE), / ɪˌrɛspənˈsɪbəl / (BrE)
not thinking enough about the possible results of your actions, or not feeling responsible for what you do

Examples

irresponsible behavior
His irresponsible driving caused a major accident.
It was irresponsible of him to leave the children home alone.
She thinks it's irresponsible to spend money you don't have.

Related Terms

  • antonym – responsible: having an obligation to do something, or having control over or care for someone, as part of one's job or role
