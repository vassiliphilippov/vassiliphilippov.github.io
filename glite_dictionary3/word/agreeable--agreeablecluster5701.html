agreeable

adjective
in keeping with, compatible
Pronunciation: / əˈɡriəbəl / (AmE), / əˈɡriːəbl / (BrE)
(formal) in accordance with; consistent or compatible with something else.

Examples

agreeable with the rules
Her decisions were agreeable with the advice she received.
The company's new policy is agreeable with international regulations.
This interpretation is agreeable with the evidence presented.

Related Terms

  • synonym – concordant: in agreement or harmony with
  • synonym – conformable: compliant or consistent with something else
  • antonym – contrary: opposite in nature or in conflict with
