overbid

noun
bid higher than appropriate (in cards)
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈbɪd /; / ˌoʊvərˈbɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbɪd / (BrE)
a bid that is higher than the previous bid or higher than is reasonable, especially in auctions or card games

Examples
