self-hatred

noun
intense dislike of oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈheɪtrəd / (AmE), / ˌselfˈheɪtrɪd / (BrE)
Intense dislike or loathing of oneself or one's own actions, often accompanied by feelings of shame.

Examples

deep self-hatred
After years of bullying, he struggled with intense self-hatred.
Her self-hatred made it difficult for her to accept compliments from others.
Overcoming self-hatred requires patience and self-compassion.
