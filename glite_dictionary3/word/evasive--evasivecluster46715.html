evasive

adjective
avoiding danger or difficulty
Pronunciation: / ɪˈveɪsɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈveɪsɪv / (BrE)
tending to avoid or escape from difficulty or danger, particularly by quick or clever action

Examples

evasive action
Firefighters had to take evasive action as the building started to collapse.
She gave an evasive answer when asked about her involvement in the incident.
The pilot made an evasive maneuver to avoid the incoming missile.

  • synonym – elusive: difficult to find, catch, or achieve
