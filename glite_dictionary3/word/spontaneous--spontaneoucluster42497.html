spontaneous

adjective
happening without external cause or planning
Pronunciation: / spɑnˈteɪniəs / (AmE), / spɒnˈteɪniəs / (BrE)
happening or done naturally, without planning or being caused by something else

Examples

spontaneous decision
Her spontaneous decision to travel surprised everyone.

The scientist observed a spontaneous chemical reaction in the lab.
He gave a spontaneous speech that moved the entire audience.
Sometimes the best adventures happen when you make a spontaneous decision.
Their laughter was so spontaneous that everyone in the room joined in.
