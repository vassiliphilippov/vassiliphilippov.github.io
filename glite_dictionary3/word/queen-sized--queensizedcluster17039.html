queen-sized

adjective
of a standard bed size between double and king
Pronunciation: / ˈkwiːn saɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkwiːn saɪzd / (BrE)
(of a bed or mattress) having standard dimensions larger than a double but smaller than king-size.

Examples

queen-sized bed
She prefers a queen-sized bed because it's roomy without taking up too much space.
The hotel room included a spacious queen-sized bed with fresh linens.
They decided to buy a queen-sized mattress for their new apartment.
