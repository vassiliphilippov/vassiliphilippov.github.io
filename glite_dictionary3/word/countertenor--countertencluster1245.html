countertenor

adjective
having the highest male singing voice
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚˌtɛn.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.təˌtɛn.ə / (BrE)
(of a singing voice) describing or relating to the highest male voice, with a range above that of a tenor.

Examples

countertenor voice
He was cast as the lead due to his remarkable countertenor range.
His countertenor performance captivated the entire audience.
The composer wrote an aria specifically for a countertenor voice.
