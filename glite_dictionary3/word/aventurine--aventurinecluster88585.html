aventurine

noun
translucent, sparkly quartz mineral
Pronunciation: / əˈvɛntʃəˌrin / (AmE), / əˈvɛntjʊəriːn / (BrE)
a translucent variety of quartz containing small, reflective mineral inclusions, usually mica, giving it a sparkly or glistening appearance
