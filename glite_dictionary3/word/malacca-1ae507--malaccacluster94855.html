malacca

noun
stem of rattan palm used for canes, handles
Pronunciation: / məˈlækə / (AmE), / məˈlækə / (BrE)
the stem of a rattan palm, especially Calamus scipionum, used for making canes and umbrella handles

Examples

malacca cane stem
Authentic malacca is prized for its strength and light weight in walking sticks.
He walked with a malacca cane, which was both sturdy and elegant.
The umbrella had a handle made from polished malacca.
