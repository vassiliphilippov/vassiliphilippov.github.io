extinguisher

noun
device for putting out small fires
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃər / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃə / (BrE)
a portable device for putting out small fires, typically by ejecting a substance such as water, foam, or a chemical

Examples

extinguisher for fires
