raw

adjective
not cooked; uncooked (of food)
Pronunciation: / rɔ / (AmE), / rɔː / (BrE)
(of food) not cooked or heated for preparation; in a natural, uncooked state.

Examples

raw meat
Consuming raw eggs can pose a risk of foodborne illness.
She prefers to eat raw carrots as a healthy snack.
The fish was served raw in a traditional Japanese dish.

  • antonym – cooked: (of food) prepared by heating
