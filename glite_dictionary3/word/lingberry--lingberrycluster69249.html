lingberry

noun
northern shrub with edible red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋˌbɛri / (BrE)
A low, evergreen shrub found in northern temperate regions, bearing small red edible berries.
