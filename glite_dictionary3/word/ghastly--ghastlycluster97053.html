ghastly

adjective
horrifying; shockingly repellent
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæs.tli /; / ˈɡæsli / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːst.li /; / ˈɡɑːstli / (BrE)
causing horror, fear, or shock; very frightening or unpleasant, especially in a gruesome or supernatural way

Examples

ghastly crime scene
