cantonment

noun
temporary military camp or barracks
Pronunciation: / kænˈtoʊnmənt / (AmE), / kænˈtɒnmənt / (BrE)
a military camp or barracks where soldiers are temporarily housed, especially when specially built by an army

Examples

army cantonment area
During the training exercise, the soldiers stayed in a cantonment outside the city.
Medical facilities in the cantonment quickly handled any injuries among the soldiers.
The army built a large cantonment to house troops during the monsoon season.
