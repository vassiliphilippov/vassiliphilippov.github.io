couscous

noun
North African semolina pasta
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːskuːs / (AmE), / ˈkʊskuːs / (BrE)
a type of granular pasta made from crushed and steamed semolina, traditionally prepared in North Africa

Examples

fluffy couscous grains
I made a salad with roasted vegetables and couscous for dinner.
Moroccan cuisine often features spiced couscous served with lamb or chicken.
