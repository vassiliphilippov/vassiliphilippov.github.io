obsessivity

noun
extreme compulsiveness or preoccupation
Pronunciation: / əbˌsɛˈsɪvəti / (AmE), / ɒbˌsɛˈsɪvəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being extremely compulsive or preoccupied with a particular idea, activity, or subject
