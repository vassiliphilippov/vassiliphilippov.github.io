racism

noun
prejudice or discrimination based on race
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief that some races are superior to others, leading to discrimination or prejudice against people based on their race

Examples

fight racism in society
Many schools are working to educate students about the dangers of racism.
She spoke out against racism at the international conference.
The organization is committed to fighting racism in all its forms.
Instances of racism in the workplace can have serious consequences for employees and employers alike.
Open discussions about racism can help promote understanding and inclusivity.
The government has implemented new policies to combat racism in schools.

  • synonym – prejudice: preconceived opinion not based on reason or experience
