preferable

adjective
more desirable than another
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛrəbəl / (AmE), / prɪˈfɛrəbəl / (BrE)
more desirable or suitable than another; better or favored in comparison

Examples

preferable choice

For most commuters, living close to work is preferable to spending hours in traffic.
If possible, fresh ingredients are preferable to frozen ones in this recipe.
