mirid

noun
a plant bug of the Miridae family
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɪrɪd / (BrE)
A bug of the family Miridae, commonly known as a plant bug or leaf bug, many of which feed on plants.

Examples

mirid plant bug
A mirid is commonly found in gardens, feeding on the leaves of various plants.
Farmers monitor mirids because they can damage crops during the growing season.
The green mirid is known for sucking sap from cotton plants.
