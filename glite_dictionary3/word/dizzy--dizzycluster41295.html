dizzy

adjective
sensation of spinning or unsteadiness
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪzi / (AmE), / ˈdɪzi / (BrE)
having a sensation of spinning around and losing one's balance; likely to fall or feel faint

Examples

feel dizzy after spinning
After spinning around in circles, the children laughed and fell over, dizzy from the game.
Looking down from the tall tower made me feel a little dizzy and uneasy.
She stood up too quickly and suddenly felt dizzy, grabbing the table for support.

Related Terms

  • synonym – giddy: having a sensation of whirling and tendency to fall
  • synonym – vertiginous: causing or relating to a sensation of dizziness or whirling
  • synonym – woozy: feeling weak or unsteady, as if about to lose consciousness
