festschrift

noun
collection honoring a scholar
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛstˌʃrɪft / (AmE), / ˈfɛstˌʃrɪft / (BrE)
A collection of writings published in honor of a scholar, often marking a significant anniversary or retirement.

Examples

festschrift for a professor
Contributors to the festschrift included many of the scholar's former students and colleagues.
He was honored to have a festschrift compiled in recognition of his lifelong contributions to linguistics.
The university presented a festschrift to Professor Yang on the occasion of her retirement.
