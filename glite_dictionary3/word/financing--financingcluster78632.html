financing

noun
providing funds for business or projects
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪ.næn.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.næns.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of providing money for a particular purpose, especially for starting or running a business or project

Examples

financing a business
Government financing helped launch the startup's first product.
Home buyers often seek financing from banks to afford a mortgage.
The company secured financing for its new factory through a group of investors.
