to note

verb
write down for record
Pronunciation: / noʊt / (AmE), / nəʊt / (BrE)
to make a written record of something; to write something down for future reference

Examples

to note the address
He quickly noted the address so he wouldn't forget it.
Journalists note important facts in their notebooks as the event unfolds.
The secretary noted the key points during the meeting.

  • synonym – record: to set down in writing or other permanent form
