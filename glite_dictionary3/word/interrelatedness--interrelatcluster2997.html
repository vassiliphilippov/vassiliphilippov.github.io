interrelatedness

noun
state of being mutually connected
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərɪˈleɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌrɪˈleɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The state or condition of being closely connected and having mutual or reciprocal relationships with other things or parts.

Examples

interrelatedness of ideas
He emphasized the interrelatedness of all aspects of health, including physical and mental well-being.
Our ecosystem's stability depends on the interrelatedness of its plants, animals, and microorganisms.
The interrelatedness of these two scientific fields leads to many collaborative discoveries.
