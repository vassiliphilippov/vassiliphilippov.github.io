hoecake

noun
thin cornmeal cake, traditionally baked on a hoe
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈhəʊˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A thin, usually unleavened cake made from cornmeal, originally baked on the blade of a hoe over an open fire, especially in the southern United States.

Examples

fresh hoecake with honey
For breakfast, she served fresh hoecake with honey.
Pioneers would often cook a simple hoecake over the campfire during their travels.
The cookbook includes a traditional recipe for hoecake from the American South.
