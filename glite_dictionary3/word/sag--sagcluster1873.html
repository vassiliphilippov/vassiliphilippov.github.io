sag

noun
a dip or downward curve, especially from weight
Pronunciation: / sæɡ / (AmE), / sæɡ / (BrE)
a dip or downward curve in something, especially as a result of weight or weakness

Examples

sag in the mattress
Over time, the mattress developed a slight sag that made it uncomfortable.
The sag in the power line was more pronounced after the storm.
There was a noticeable sag in the middle of the old sofa.
