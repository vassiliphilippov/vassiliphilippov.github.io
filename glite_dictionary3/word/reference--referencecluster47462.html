reference

noun
a cited publication or passage
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛf.ɚ.əns /; / ˈrɛfɚəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛf.ər.əns /; / ˈrɛf.ərəns /; / ˈrɛfərəns / (BrE)
a mention, citation, or note about a source, publication, or information; the act of referring to something
