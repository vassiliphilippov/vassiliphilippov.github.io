ao dai

noun
traditional Vietnamese women's dress
Pronunciation: / aʊ ˈdaɪ / (AmE), / aʊ ˈdaɪ / (BrE)
A traditional Vietnamese women's dress consisting of a long-sleeved tunic with front and back panels, worn over trousers.
