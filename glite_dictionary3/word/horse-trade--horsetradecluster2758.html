to horse-trade

verb
negotiate with much bargaining
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrs ˌtreɪd /; / ˈhɔrsˌtreɪd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːs ˌtreɪd /; / ˈhɔːsˌtreɪd / (BrE)
