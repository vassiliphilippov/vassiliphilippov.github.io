hyperglycaemia

noun
abnormally high blood sugar
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərɡlaɪˈsiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəɡlʌɪˈsiːmɪə / (BrE)
An abnormally high level of glucose in the blood, typically associated with diabetes.

Examples
