cadastre

noun
official land ownership register
Pronunciation: / kəˈdæstər / (AmE), / kəˈdæstə / (BrE)
an official register of property showing the extent, value, and ownership of land, primarily for taxation purposes

Examples

national cadastre records
Every plot of land in the region is recorded in the official cadastre.
Lawyers consulted the cadastre before drawing up the sale agreement for the property.
The government updated the cadastre to reflect recent changes in land ownership.
