enteroceptor

noun
receptor sensing internal body stimuli
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntəroʊˈsɛptər / (AmE), / ˌɛntərəʊˈsɛptə / (BrE)
a sensory receptor that responds to stimuli originating inside the body, such as from internal organs or tissues
