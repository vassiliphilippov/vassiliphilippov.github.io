trailblazer

noun
person who marks trails with blazes
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪlˌbleɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlˌbleɪzə / (BrE)
a person who marks a trail through wilderness by making blazes or signs, especially on trees, to guide others

Examples

trailblazer marks trees
Centuries ago, it was common for a trailblazer to use an axe to create pathway signs.
Every team had a designated trailblazer responsible for guiding them through the dense forest.
The trailblazer carefully marked each tree to help hikers find their way.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pathfinder: someone who finds or makes a path through unexplored territory
