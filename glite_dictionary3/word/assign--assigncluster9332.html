to assign

verb
to transfer legal rights or interest
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪn / (AmE), / əˈsaɪn / (BrE)
to transfer legal rights or interest in property, typically to another person

Examples

to assign property
After inheriting the estate, he chose to assign his share to his sister.
She decided to assign her rights in the patent to a business partner.
The tenant may not assign the lease without the landlord’s written consent.
