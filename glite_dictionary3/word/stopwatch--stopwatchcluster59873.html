stopwatch

noun
a device for measuring elapsed time
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpˌwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈstɒpˌwɒtʃ / (BrE)
a small device used to measure the exact time taken for an event, such as a race, by starting and stopping it
