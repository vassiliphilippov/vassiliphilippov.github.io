unharmoniously

adverb
in a discordant or unharmonious manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.hɑːrˈmoʊ.ni.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.hɑːˈmɒ.ni.əs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks harmony; discordantly or without agreement.

Examples

to speak unharmoniously
During the debate, the panel members spoke unharmoniously, constantly interrupting each other.
The choir sang unharmoniously, making the piece hard to listen to.
The colors in the room were combined unharmoniously, creating a jarring visual effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym – discordantly: in a way that is harsh or lacks harmony
  • antonym – harmoniously: in a harmonious manner; with agreement or concord
