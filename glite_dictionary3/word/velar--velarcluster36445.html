velar

adjective
relating to the velum (soft palate)
Pronunciation: / ˈviːlər / (AmE), / ˈviːlə / (BrE)
relating to the velum, especially the soft palate or the part of the mouth forming velar sounds in speech

Examples

velar area anatomy
A velar fold can be observed in some anatomical conditions of the mouth.
Linguists distinguish velar sounds from other types of consonants based on where they are articulated.
The English 'k' is a velar consonant, pronounced with the back of the tongue against the soft palate.

Related Terms

  • synonym – palatal: of or relating to the palate, especially in speech sounds
