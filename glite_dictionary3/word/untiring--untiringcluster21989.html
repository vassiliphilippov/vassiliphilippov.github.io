untiring

adjective
persistent; not becoming tired
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtaɪərɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or effort) showing constant and energetic persistence; not becoming tired.

Examples

untiring effort
She was an untiring supporter of the charity, always willing to volunteer her time.
The scientist's untiring curiosity led to many important discoveries.
With untiring dedication, the team worked late into the night to finish the project.
