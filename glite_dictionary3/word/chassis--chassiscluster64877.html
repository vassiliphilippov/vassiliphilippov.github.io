chassis

noun
frame for electronic components
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæsi / (AmE), / ˈʃæsi / (BrE)
a metal frame that supports the circuit components in an electronic device

Examples

chassis of a computer
Dust had accumulated inside the chassis of the old television set.
The radio was repaired by removing the chassis and soldering new wires onto the circuit board.
The technician slid the chassis out of the amplifier to check for damaged components.
