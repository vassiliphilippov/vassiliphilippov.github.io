unseamed

adjective
having no seams
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsimd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːmd / (BrE)
(of a garment or object) having no seams; made in one piece or without seams.

Examples

unseamed container
Modern plastics can be molded into unseamed containers, avoiding traditional joints or glue.
She admired the unseamed design of the dress, which gave it a smooth, modern look.
The raincoat was entirely unseamed, keeping water from leaking through the fabric.

  • synonym – seamless: having no seams or joints; smooth and continuous
