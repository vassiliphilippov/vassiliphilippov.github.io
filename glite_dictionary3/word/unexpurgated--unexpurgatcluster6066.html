unexpurgated

adjective
not having material removed or censored
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛkspɚˌɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛkspəˌɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a text) not having had material deleted, especially material considered offensive or objectionable.
