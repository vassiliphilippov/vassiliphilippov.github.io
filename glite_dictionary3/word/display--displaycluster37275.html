display

noun
electronic device showing information visually
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈpleɪ / (AmE), / dɪsˈpleɪ / (BrE)
an electronic device that shows information visually, such as a computer screen, monitor, or digital panel

Examples

phone display
Modern smartphones are known for their vibrant displays with deep color and contrast.
She adjusted the brightness on the display to reduce eye strain while working.
The laptop has a high-resolution display that makes images look sharp and clear.
