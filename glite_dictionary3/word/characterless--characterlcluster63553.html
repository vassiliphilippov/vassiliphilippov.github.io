characterless

adjective
lacking distinctive qualities; uninteresting
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪktərləs / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktələs / (BrE)
lacking distinctive or individual qualities; dull and uninteresting.

Examples

a characterless speech
His speech was so characterless that no one remembered what he said.
She found the novel characterless and quickly lost interest in reading it.
The new office building is characterless and blends in with its surroundings.

Related Terms

  • antonym – distinctive: having a quality or characteristic that makes something different and easily noticed
  • synonym – nondescript: lacking distinctive or interesting features or characteristics
