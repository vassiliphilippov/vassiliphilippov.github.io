insensibly

adverb
without feeling or awareness
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛnsəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛnsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no feeling or awareness; in a numb or unfeeling manner.

Examples

insensibly walk past
She walked insensibly through the crowd, oblivious to the chaos around her.
The patient stared insensibly at the ceiling, not responding to the nurse's questions.
Unable to process the news, he reacted insensibly, his face devoid of emotion.
