unearned

adjective
not gained by effort or merit
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɝːnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɜːnd / (BrE)
not gained through one's own effort, merit, or service; acquired without deserving it or working for it

Examples

unearned income
He felt guilty about spending his unearned inheritance.
She received an unearned promotion because of her family connections.
The team won after scoring an unearned run in the final inning.
