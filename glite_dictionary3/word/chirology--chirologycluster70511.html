chirology

noun
palm reading to predict the future
Pronunciation: / kaɪˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / kaɪˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the practice of interpreting the lines on the palm of the hand to predict a person's future or character

Examples

chirology palm reading
Books on chirology often include detailed diagrams of the major palm lines and their meanings.
She became fascinated by chirology after visiting a fair where a palm reader analyzed her hand.
While many people view chirology as a pseudoscience, it has a long cultural history in many societies.
