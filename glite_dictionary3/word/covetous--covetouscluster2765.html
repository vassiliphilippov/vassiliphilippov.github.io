covetous

adjective
having a strong desire for others' possessions
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvətəs /; / ˈkʌv.ə.təs / (AmE), / ˈkʌvɪtəs /; / ˈkʌv.ɪ.təs / (BrE)
having a strong desire to possess something, especially something that belongs to someone else
