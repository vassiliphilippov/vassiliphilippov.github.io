to rerun

verb
perform or carry out again
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈrʌn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈrʌn / (BrE)
to cause to be performed, carried out, or enacted again; to repeat an action, process, or experiment.

to rerun an experiment
