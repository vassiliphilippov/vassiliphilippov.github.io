diamond

noun
precious gem; cut and polished diamond
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪmənd /; / ˈdaɪəmənd / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəmənd / (BrE)
a very hard, clear, and valuable stone made of pure carbon, used in jewelry and for cutting tools
