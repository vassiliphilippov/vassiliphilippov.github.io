verandah

noun
roofed porch along a house
Pronunciation: / vəˈrændə / (AmE), / vəˈrændə / (BrE)
a roofed platform along the outside of a house, typically level with the ground floor and sometimes partly enclosed.

Examples

verandah with chairs
She decorated her verandah with potted plants and comfortable chairs.
Their house has a wide verandah that wraps around three sides.
We sat on the verandah and watched the rain fall in the garden.
