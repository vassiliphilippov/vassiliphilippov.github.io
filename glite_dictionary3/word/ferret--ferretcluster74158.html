ferret

noun
domesticated mammal used for hunting rodents
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrət / (AmE), / ˈfɛrɪt /; / ˈfɛrət / (BrE)
a small mammal of the weasel family, often kept as a pet or used for hunting; includes both domesticated and wild types
