amphitheater

noun
sloping, tiered gallery for spectators
Pronunciation: / ˈæmfɪˌθiːətər /; / ˈæmfɪˌθiətɚ / (AmE), / ˈæmfɪˌθɪətə /; / ˈamfɪˌθɪətə / (BrE)
a large building or space with seats arranged in rising tiers around a central open area, used for performances, sports, or public events
