refrigerant

adjective
causing cooling or freezing
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɪdʒɚənt / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒərənt / (BrE)
(of a substance) causing cooling or freezing; capable of reducing temperature.
