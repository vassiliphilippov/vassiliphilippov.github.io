pimento

noun
mild, bell-shaped red pepper plant
Pronunciation: / pəˈmɛntoʊ / (AmE), / pɪˈmɛntəʊ / (BrE)
A plant (Capsicum annuum) bearing large, mild, thick-walled, usually bell-shaped red fruits used especially in salads and as stuffing.

Examples

pimento plant harvest
Farmers harvested the ripe red pimentos at the end of summer.
She prefers using pimento in her salads for its sweet, mild flavor.
The chef stuffed each pimento with a delicious cheese blend for the appetizer.
