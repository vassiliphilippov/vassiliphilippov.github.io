ubiquitousness

noun
state of being everywhere
Pronunciation: / juːˈbɪkwətəsnəs / (AmE), / juːˈbɪkwɪtəsnəs / (BrE)
The state of being present everywhere or seeming to be present everywhere at the same time.

Examples

ubiquitousness of technology
He was struck by the ubiquitousness of advertisements in the city.
The ubiquitousness of fast-food chains makes healthy eating more difficult.
The ubiquitousness of smartphones has changed the way we live and communicate.

  • synonym – ubiquity: the state of being everywhere at once
