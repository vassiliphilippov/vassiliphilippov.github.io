unscholarly

adjective
not scholarly; lacking academic rigor
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskɑːlərli / (AmE), / ʌnˈskɒləli / (BrE)
not exhibiting the qualities or standards of scholarship; lacking academic rigor or erudition.

Examples

unscholarly work
His unscholarly approach made the research paper difficult to take seriously.
Many critics dismissed the article as unscholarly and lacking in proper references.
She realized her notes were too unscholarly to include in the academic journal.
