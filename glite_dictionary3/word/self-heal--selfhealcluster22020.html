to self-heal

verb
recover without medical help
Pronunciation: / c8s5blfcchil / (AmE), / c8s5blfcchid0l / (BrE)
to recover from illness, injury, or emotional distress by natural processes, without external medical treatment or intervention.

Examples

to self-heal after injury
After months of fatigue, the injured athlete self-healed without surgery and returned to light training.
With rest and therapy, her small cuts began self-healing within days, surprising her doctor and family.
Many mild colds will self-heal as the immune system fights the virus over several days.
