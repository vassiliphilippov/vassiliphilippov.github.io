Benghazi

proper noun
city in northern Libya
Pronunciation: / bɛnˈɡɑzi / (AmE), / bɛnˈɡɑːzi / (BrE)
A port city in northern Libya, located on the Gulf of Sidra, formerly a joint capital of Libya with Tripoli.
