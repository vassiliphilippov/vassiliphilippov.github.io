expressiveness

noun
quality of conveying thought or feeling
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛsɪvnɪs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛsɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of effectively conveying thought or feeling through words, gestures, or artistic means

Examples

artistic expressiveness
He admired the expressiveness of the painting, which seemed to tell a story without words.
In literature, expressiveness often helps the reader connect with a character's emotions.
The expressiveness of her performance captivated the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expressive: having the ability to convey thought or feeling
