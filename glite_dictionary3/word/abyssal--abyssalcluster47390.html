abyssal

adjective
of the deep ocean (2,000–5,000 meters)
Pronunciation: / əˈbɪs.əl / (AmE), / əˈbɪs.əl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the depths of the ocean, typically between 2,000 and 5,000 meters below the surface
