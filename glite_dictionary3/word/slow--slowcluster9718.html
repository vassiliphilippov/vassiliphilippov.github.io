slow

adjective
not quick to understand; lacking mental sharpness
Pronunciation: / sloʊ / (AmE), / sləʊ / (BrE)
not quick to learn or understand things; lacking intellectual sharpness or mental acuity

Examples

slow learner
Although she was slow at first, she eventually grasped the main idea.
He was a bit slow when it came to understanding complex instructions.
Some teachers are especially patient with slow learners in the classroom.
