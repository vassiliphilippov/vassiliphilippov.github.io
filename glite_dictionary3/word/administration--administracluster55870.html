administration

noun
giving of medication
Pronunciation: / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of giving a drug or medication to a person or animal.

Examples

administration of medicine
Errors in the administration of insulin can be dangerous for patients with diabetes.
Proper administration of vaccines is essential to ensure their effectiveness.
