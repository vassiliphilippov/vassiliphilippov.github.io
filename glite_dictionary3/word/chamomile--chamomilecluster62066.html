chamomile

noun
herbal plant used for tea and medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməˌmaɪl / (AmE), / ˈkæməˌmiːl / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with feathery leaves and white, daisy-like flowers, often used to make herbal tea and in traditional medicine.

Examples

chamomile tea
Drinking chamomile tea before bed can help you relax and sleep better.
Some people use chamomile in homemade skin remedies because of its soothing properties.
The scent of chamomile filled the garden where the flowers were in full bloom.
