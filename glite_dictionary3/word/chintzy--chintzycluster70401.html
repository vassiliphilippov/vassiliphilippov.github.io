chintzy

adjective
of poor quality; cheap
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪntsi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪntzi / (BrE)
Of very poor quality, cheap or shoddily made; also, stingy or miserly.

Examples

chintzy souvenir shop
I don't want to buy that chintzy souvenir; it looks like it will fall apart in a week.
She resented the chintzy gifts her boss handed out every year.
The hotel room felt chintzy with its cheap decor and thin sheets.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cheap: inexpensive and often of low quality
  • antonym – luxurious: very comfortable and expensive; of high quality
  • synonym – shoddy: badly made or of poor quality
