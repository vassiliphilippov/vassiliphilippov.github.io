agreement

noun
grammatical correspondence of related words
Pronunciation: / əˈɡriːmənt / (AmE), / əˈɡriːm(ə)nt / (BrE)
(Grammar) the correspondence in form between related words, such as subject and verb or noun and adjective, according to rules of inflection.

Examples

agreement between words
Errors in agreement often make sentences sound awkward or ungrammatical.
In Spanish, noun-adjective agreement requires both words to match in gender and number.
Subject-verb agreement can be challenging for English learners.
