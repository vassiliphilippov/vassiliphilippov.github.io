PM

noun
private/direct online message
Pronunciation: / pi 5bm / (AmE), / pid0 em / (BrE)
a private message sent directly to another user on an online platform or forum; a direct message.

Examples

send a PM
If you have questions, please PM me the details after the meeting.
She received an unexpected PM from a recruiter offering a remote position.
The forum's rules say do not share private PM contents with other community members.

Related Terms

  • synonym – direct message: a message sent privately from one user to another on social platforms
  • antonym – public post: a message or post visible to all users on a platform; the opposite of a private message
