portrayal

noun
representation of a character by an actor
Pronunciation: / pɔːrˈtreɪ.əl / (AmE), / pɔːˈtreɪ.əl / (BrE)
the act or result of depicting, describing, or representing a person, character, or situation, especially in a work of art, literature, or performance (including acting)
