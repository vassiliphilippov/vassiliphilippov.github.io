hadj

noun
Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca
Pronunciation: / hɑdʒ / (AmE), / hædʒ / (BrE)
the Muslim pilgrimage to Mecca that every Muslim is expected to undertake at least once in their lifetime if able; one of the Five Pillars of Islam

Examples

the hadj to Mecca
Completing the hadj is considered a significant milestone in a Muslim's life.
He saved for years so he could make the hadj to Mecca with his family.
The government assists pilgrims traveling for the hadj each year.
