carefulness

noun
quality of being cautious
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkeə.f(ə)l.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being cautious and paying close attention to avoid mistakes, harm, or danger

Examples

with carefulness and attention
He handled the ancient vase with great carefulness, ensuring it was not damaged.
Her carefulness prevented the project from running into unexpected problems.
The scientist's carefulness was evident in the detailed notes and accurate results.
His carefulness in handling the fragile vase prevented any accidents.
Teachers appreciate students who show carefulness in their assignments.
The project was completed with great carefulness and precision.
