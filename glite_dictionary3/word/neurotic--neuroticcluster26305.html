neurotic

adjective
emotionally unstable or anxious (psychological)
Pronunciation: / nʊˈrɑːtɪk /; / njʊˈrɑtɪk / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
having or showing excessive anxiety, emotional instability, or symptoms of neurosis

Examples
