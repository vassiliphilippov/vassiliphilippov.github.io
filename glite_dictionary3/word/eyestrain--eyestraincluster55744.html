eyestrain

noun
tiredness or discomfort of the eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌstreɪn / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌstreɪn / (BrE)
tiredness or discomfort of the eyes caused by prolonged close work, reading, or screen use, often due to uncorrected vision problems

Examples

computer eyestrain
If you experience frequent eyestrain, you should have your eyes checked by a professional.
Long hours at the computer can cause eyestrain if you don't take regular breaks.
Reading in poor light may result in eyestrain and headaches.
