roller

noun
revolving cylinder
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a small cylinder or wheel that rolls, used to move, flatten, shape, or curl things (such as in cooking, hair styling, or on skates)

Examples

hair roller curler
He used a roller to flatten the dough on the kitchen counter.
She applied a fresh coat of paint to the wall with a paint roller.
The conveyor belt was supported by several metal rollers underneath.
After removing the last roller, her hair was full of tight waves.
My grandmother used to sleep with rollers to get perfect curls for the morning.
She put a roller in each section of her hair before going to bed.
I replaced the bent roller on my old roller skate with a new one.
The roller on my suitcase broke, making it difficult to pull.
The conveyor belt moved smoothly because each roller spun freely.

Related Terms

  • synonym – wheel: a circular object that revolves on an axle and forms part of a machine or vehicle
