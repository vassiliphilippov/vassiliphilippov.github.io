haji

noun
a Muslim who has made the pilgrimage to Mecca
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈdʒi / (AmE), / hɑːˈdʒi / (BrE)
A Muslim who has made the pilgrimage to Mecca; also used as a respectful title for such a person.

Examples

haji title of respect
In their community, the title haji is used out of great respect.
My grandfather is a haji, having completed his pilgrimage to Mecca many years ago.
The newly returned haji shared stories of his spiritual journey with the village.
