kelpie

noun
Australian sheepdog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛlpi / (AmE), / ˈkɛlpi / (BrE)
A breed of Australian sheepdog with pointed ears, known for its agility and intelligence, commonly used for herding livestock.

Examples

working kelpie dog
At the dog show, a sleek black kelpie impressed the judges with its agility.
Many ranchers consider the kelpie to be an indispensable working dog.
The farmer relied on his kelpie to round up the flock efficiently.
