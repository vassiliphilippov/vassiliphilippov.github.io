to contribute

verb
help to cause a particular result
Pronunciation: / kənˈtrɪbjut /; / kənˈtrɪbjuːt / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjuːt / (BrE)
to give or add something (such as money, help, ideas, or qualities) to help achieve a result or as part of a group effort
