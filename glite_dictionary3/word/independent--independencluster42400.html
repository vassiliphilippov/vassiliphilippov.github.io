independent

adjective
not controlled by a party or group
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
not controlled or influenced by others; free from outside authority or influence

Examples

an independent person
As an independent journalist, he avoids working for large media corporations.
She prefers to vote for an independent candidate rather than one affiliated with a major party.
The commission is designed to be independent from political influence.
His independent research led to groundbreaking discoveries in the field.
She has always valued being independent and making her own decisions.
The organization operates as an independent body free from government control.
