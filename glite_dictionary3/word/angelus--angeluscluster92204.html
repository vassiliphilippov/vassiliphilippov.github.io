Angelus

noun
Roman Catholic devotional prayer
Pronunciation: / ˈændʒələs / (AmE), / ˈandʒələs / (BrE)
A devotional prayer recited by Roman Catholics three times a day in memory of the Annunciation, often accompanied by the ringing of a bell.

Examples

the Angelus bell rings
Every evening at six o'clock, the church bells ring for the Angelus.
She paused her work at noon to say the Angelus along with her family.
The tradition of reciting the Angelus has been upheld in the village for generations.
