Henri Toulouse-Lautrec

proper noun
French painter of Montmartre nightlife
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈri tuːˌluːz loʊˈtrɛk / (AmE), / ɑ̃ʁi tu.luz lo.tʁɛk / (BrE)
(1864–1901) A French painter renowned for his vivid portrayals of Parisian nightlife, especially in the cafes and music halls of Montmartre.
