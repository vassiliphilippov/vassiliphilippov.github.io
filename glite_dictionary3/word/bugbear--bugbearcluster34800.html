bugbear

noun
cause of fear or irritation
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡˌbɛr / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡˌbɛə / (BrE)
A cause of obsessive fear, irritation, or anxiety; something that is strongly disliked or dreaded.

Examples

common bugbear
For many parents, screen time is the modern bugbear in family life.
Spelling errors are a particular bugbear of mine when reading essays.
The issue of taxes has long been a bugbear for small business owners.

  • synonym – pet peeve: something that especially irritates a person
