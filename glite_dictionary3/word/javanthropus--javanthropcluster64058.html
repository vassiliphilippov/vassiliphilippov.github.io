Javanthropus

noun
obsolete genus of early human (Solo man)
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈvænθrəpəs / (AmE), / dʒəˈvanθrəpəs / (BrE)
A former genus name for a primitive human, now classified as Homo soloensis (Solo man).
