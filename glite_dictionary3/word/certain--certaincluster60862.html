certain

adjective
definite but not specified
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.tn̩ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tən / (BrE)
Used to refer to a particular but unspecified person or thing that is known to the speaker or writer, or as definite but not named.

Examples

certain person arrived
A certain someone left this package at your door this morning.
She reserved certain seats near the window for her family.
You must meet certain requirements to qualify for the scholarship.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unspecified: not stated explicitly or identified
