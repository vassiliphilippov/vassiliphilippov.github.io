Hygrophorus kauffmanii

noun
a species of reddish-brown fungus
Pronunciation: / haɪˌɡrɑːfərəs kɔːfˈmænaɪ / (AmE), / haɪˌɡrɒfərəs kɔːfˈmænaɪ / (BrE)
A species of fungus with a slightly sticky cap; both the cap and gills are reddish brown, while the stalk is grey.
