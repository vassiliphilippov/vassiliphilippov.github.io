gliding

noun
flying a glider (sport/activity)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
The sport or activity of flying a glider, an unpowered aircraft, through the air.

Examples

gliding competition
He took up gliding as a hobby after retiring from his job.
Her love for gliding grew after her first solo flight in a sailplane.
The club offers gliding lessons for beginners every summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym – powered flight: flight using an aircraft with an engine for propulsion
  • synonym – soaring: flying at high altitude or for long periods, often by gliders using currents of rising air
