edge

noun
slight competitive advantage
Pronunciation: / ɛdʒ / (AmE), / ɛdʒ / (BrE)

edge over competitors
Innovative technology can provide a crucial edge in a rapidly changing market.
The team had a psychological edge thanks to their last-minute goal.
Years of experience gave her an edge over other candidates for the job.
