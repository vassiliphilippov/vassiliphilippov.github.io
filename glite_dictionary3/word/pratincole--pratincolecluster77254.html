pratincole

noun
Old World shorebird with pointed wings
Pronunciation: / ˈprætɪnˌkoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpratɪnkəʊl / (BrE)
An Old World shorebird with long, pointed wings and short legs, closely related to the coursers.
