particoloured

adjective
having several different colors in patches
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrtɪˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˌpɑːtɪˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having patches or sections of different, usually bright, colors.

Examples

particoloured kite
A particoloured kite fluttered brightly against the blue sky, catching everyone's attention.
The artist painted a particoloured mural on the school wall to make the playground more cheerful.
The clown wore a particoloured jacket with bold red, yellow, and green patches.

Related Terms

  • synonym – motley: made up of different colors; multicolored, especially in patches
  • synonym – piebald: having irregular patches of two colors, typically black and white
  • antonym – plain: having a single, unvarying color; not patterned or multicolored
