schmaltzy

adjective
excessively sentimental or emotional
Pronunciation: / ˈʃmɑltsi / (AmE), / ˈʃmɔːltzi / (BrE)
Excessively sentimental or emotional in a way that is considered insincere or exaggerated.

Examples

schmaltzy love story
He prefers realistic stories over schmaltzy romances.
Her schmaltzy speech about friendship sounded insincere to many listeners.
The movie's ending was so schmaltzy that it made the audience groan.

Related Terms

  • synonym – maudlin: self-pityingly or tearfully sentimental
  • antonym – restrained: not excessive or sentimental; showing self-control
  • synonym – sentimental: showing or expressing emotions, especially in an excessive way
