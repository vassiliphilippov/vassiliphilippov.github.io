Symbion pandora

noun
microscopic animal living on lobsters
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪm.bi.ən pænˈdɔːr.ə / (AmE), / ˌsɪm.bi.ən pænˈdɔː.rə / (BrE)
a microscopic species of marine animal, the only known member of Cycliophora, living symbiotically on lobsters and having a complex life cycle with both asexual and sexual reproduction
