winking

noun
rapid closing and opening of one eye
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the rapid closing and opening of one or both eyes, either as a signal, reflex, or reaction

Examples
