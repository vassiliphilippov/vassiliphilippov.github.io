bayonet

noun
blade fixed to a rifle as a weapon
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.ə.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.ə.nɪt / (BrE)
a blade that can be attached to the muzzle of a rifle or similar firearm and used as a weapon

Examples

bayonet on rifle
During the inspection, the sergeant checked that every weapon had a bayonet attached.
He displayed an antique bayonet from the 19th century in his collection.
The soldier fixed his bayonet to his rifle before the charge.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dagger: a short knife with a pointed and edged blade, used as a weapon
