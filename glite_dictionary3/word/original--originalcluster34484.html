original

adjective
new or first of its kind
Pronunciation: / əˈrɪdʒənəl / (AmE), / əˈrɪdʒɪnəl / (BrE)
(of ideas, works, or people) new, fresh, and different; not copied or derived from something else; existing as the first of its kind.

Examples

original idea
He always tries to find an original solution to every problem.
Her paintings are admired for their original style and vivid colors.
The company is known for its original approach to technology and design.
