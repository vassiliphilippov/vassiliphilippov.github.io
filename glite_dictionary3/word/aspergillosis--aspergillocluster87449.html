aspergillosis

noun
infection by Aspergillus fungus
Pronunciation: / ˌæspərˌdʒɪˈloʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌaspəˌdʒɪˈləʊsɪs / (BrE)
a disease or infection in humans caused by Aspergillus fungus, usually affecting the lungs, ears, skin, or other organs, especially in people with weakened immune systems
