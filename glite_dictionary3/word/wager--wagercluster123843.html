to wager

verb
assert confidently, as in a bet
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈweɪdʒə / (BrE)
to state confidently or assert that something will happen, as though making a bet

Examples

to wager he will
I wager he will be late, as usual.
She wagered that the project would finish ahead of schedule.
You can wager that this new policy will face criticism.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bet: to risk something, usually money, on the outcome of an event; to assert with confidence
