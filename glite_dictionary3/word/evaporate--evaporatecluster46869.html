to evaporate

verb
gradually disappear or fade away
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæpəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈvapəreɪt / (BrE)
(of an emotion, feeling, or condition) to gradually disappear or become less strong until it is gone

Examples

evaporate hope
As the years passed, her enthusiasm for the project began to evaporate.
Doubt can evaporate in the face of clear evidence.
Their excitement quickly evaporated when they heard the bad news.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disappear: to cease to be visible or exist
  • synonym – dissipate: to disappear or cause to disappear
