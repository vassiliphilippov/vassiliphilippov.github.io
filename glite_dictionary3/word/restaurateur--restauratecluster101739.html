restaurateur

noun
owner or manager of a restaurant
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛstəˌrɑːˈtɜr / (AmE), / ˌrɛstərəˈtɜː / (BrE)
A person who owns and manages a restaurant.

Examples

restaurateur runs a restaurant
After years as a chef, he decided to become a restaurateur and open his own bistro.
As a successful restaurateur, she opened three new restaurants in the city last year.
The restaurateur greeted each guest personally as they entered the dining room.
