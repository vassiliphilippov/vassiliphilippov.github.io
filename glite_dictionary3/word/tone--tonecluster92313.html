tone

noun
quality or character of someone's voice
Pronunciation: / toʊn / (AmE), / təʊn / (BrE)
the quality or character of a person's voice, especially as it expresses emotion or attitude

Examples

gentle tone voice
His soothing tone helped to calm the anxious child.
She replied in a sharp tone that took everyone by surprise.
You could tell by her tone that she was upset, even though she tried to hide it.
