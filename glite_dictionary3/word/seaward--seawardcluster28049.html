seaward

adjective
toward or facing the sea or coast
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːwərd / (AmE), / ˈsiːwəd / (BrE)
facing, directed toward, or situated in the direction of the sea or coast, away from inland areas.

Examples

seaward side
The balcony offers a stunning seaward view that captures the horizon.
The path took us along the seaward edge of the cliffs.
They built a seaward wall to protect the village from storms.

Related Terms

  • antonym – landward: facing or directed toward the land or inland
