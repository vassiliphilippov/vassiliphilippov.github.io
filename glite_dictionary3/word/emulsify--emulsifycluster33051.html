to emulsify

verb
mix into an emulsion
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmʌlsɪˌfaɪ /; / ɪˈmʌl.səˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪ /; / ɪˈmʌl.sɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to mix two liquids (such as oil and water) so they form a smooth, uniform mixture (an emulsion)

Examples
