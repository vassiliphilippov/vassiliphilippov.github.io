tart

adjective
sharp or harsh (of speech)
Pronunciation: / tɑrt / (AmE), / tɑːt / (BrE)
(of a remark or tone) marked by being sharp, biting, or harsh.

Examples

tart comment
He delivered a tart criticism of the proposed policy.
Her tart reply made everyone fall silent.
The debate became heated with increasingly tart exchanges.

Related Terms

  • synonym – biting: (of remarks) harshly critical or sarcastic
  • antonym – gentle: (of remarks) mild or kind, the opposite of harsh
  • synonym – sharp: (of remarks or tone) harsh or cutting
