intravenously

adverb
by injection into a vein
Pronunciation: / ɪn.trəˈvi.nəs.li / (AmE), / ɪn.trəˈviː.nəs.li / (BrE)
In a manner involving the injection or infusion of a substance directly into a vein.

Examples

given intravenously to patients
Fluids must be given intravenously when a patient is severely dehydrated.
Some chemotherapy treatments are delivered intravenously during hospital visits.
The patient was administered antibiotics intravenously to ensure rapid effect.
