uninhabited

adjective
having no people living there
Pronunciation: / ʌnɪnˈhæbɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnɪnˈhæbɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a place) having no people living there; deserted.

Examples

uninhabited island
After the evacuation, the village became completely uninhabited.
The uninhabited house stood silent at the end of the road.
They explored an uninhabited island in the Pacific.
