percipient

noun
a person who perceives through the senses
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɪpiənt / (AmE), / pəˈsɪpiənt / (BrE)
a person who perceives or becomes aware of things or events, especially through the senses

Examples

a skilled percipient
As a trained percipient, she noticed subtle changes in her surroundings that others missed.
In parapsychology, a percipient is someone who claims to receive information through extrasensory perception.
The study required each percipient to report their sensory experiences in detail.

  • synonym – observer: a person who watches or notices something
