orthopteran

noun
insect of order Orthoptera (e.g. grasshopper, cricket)
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθɑptɚən / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒptərən / (BrE)
an insect of the order Orthoptera, characterized by leathery forewings, membranous hind wings, and chewing mouthparts, including grasshoppers, crickets, and locusts.
