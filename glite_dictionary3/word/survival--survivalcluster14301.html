survival

noun
continuing to live or exist
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪ.vəl / (AmE), / səˈvaɪ.vəl / (BrE)
the state or fact of continuing to live or exist, especially in spite of difficult conditions

Examples

survival in harsh conditions
Access to clean water is essential for the survival of every community.
Her courage and determination were crucial to her survival in the wilderness.
The doctors were amazed by the patient's survival after such a serious accident.

Related Terms

  • antonym – death: the end of life; the cessation of existence
  • synonym – existence: the state of being or existing
