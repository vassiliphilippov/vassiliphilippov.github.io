blustery

adjective
aggressively loud or domineering
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌstəri / (AmE), / ˈblʌstəri / (BrE)
Aggressively loud and domineering in manner; apt to bully or browbeat others.

Examples

blustery speech
He tried to mask his uncertainty with a blustery display of confidence.
Her blustery speech drowned out any attempt at disagreement during the meeting.
The new manager's blustery style intimidated many of the staff at first.

  • synonym – bullying: using strength or influence to intimidate
  • antonym – gentle: kind and mild in manner
