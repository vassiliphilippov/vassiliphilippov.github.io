jabot

noun
decorative ruffle on front of blouse or shirt
Pronunciation: / ʒæˈboʊ / (AmE), / ʒaˈboʊ / (BrE)
A decorative ruffle or frill attached to the front of a woman's blouse or a man's shirt, often used in formal or historical clothing.

Examples

Her blouse was embellished with a delicate lace jabot.
