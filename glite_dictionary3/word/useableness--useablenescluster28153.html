useableness

noun
quality of being usable or useful
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːzəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈjuːzəbəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being capable of being used or of providing good service; usefulness.

useableness of a tool
Engineers consider the useableness of materials when designing products.
She doubted the useableness of the tool until she saw it in action.
The useableness of the new software became apparent when it saved the team hours of work.
