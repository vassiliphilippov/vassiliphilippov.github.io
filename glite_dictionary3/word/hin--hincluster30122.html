hin

noun
ancient Hebrew liquid measure (~1.5 gallons)
Pronunciation: / hɪn / (AmE), / hɪn / (BrE)
an ancient Hebrew unit of liquid measure, equal to about 1.5 gallons (5.7 liters)

Examples

hin of oil
