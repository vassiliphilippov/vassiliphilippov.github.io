fructification

noun
fruiting/reproductive organ in plants
Pronunciation: / ˌfrʌktɪfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfrʌktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the organ or part of a plant, especially in ferns and mosses, where fruit or reproductive bodies are produced

Examples

fructification in moss
Botanists studied the development of fructification in several moss species.
Each type of plant may display a distinct form of fructification.
The fructification of the fern was visible under the leaves as small clusters.
