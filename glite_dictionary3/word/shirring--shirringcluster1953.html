shirring

noun
method of baking eggs in a dish
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɜː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
A method of cooking eggs in which the eggs are baked in a shallow dish, usually with cream or butter.

Examples

shirring eggs with cream
Classic shirring involves baking eggs with cream and herbs for a delicate breakfast dish.
Some cookbooks recommend shirring eggs with a sprinkle of cheese on top.
The chef demonstrated shirring as a simple yet elegant way to prepare eggs.
