soma

noun
East Indian vine used for ritual drink
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊmə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊmə / (BrE)
A leafless vine from East India whose sour milky juice was formerly used to make an intoxicating drink, especially in ancient rituals.

Examples

soma plant juice
Researchers are still debating the exact identity of the soma plant.
Some texts describe the intoxicating effects of drinking soma.
The ancient priests prepared soma for the ceremonial sacrifice.
