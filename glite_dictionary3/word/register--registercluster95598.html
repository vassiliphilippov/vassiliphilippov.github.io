to register

verb
send by registered mail
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdʒɪstɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdʒɪstə / (BrE)
to send (a letter or package) by registered mail for secure delivery and tracking

Examples

to register a letter
He went to the post office to register the letter.
I need to register this package to ensure it arrives safely.
You should register important documents when sending them by post.
