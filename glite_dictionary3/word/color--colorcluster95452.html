color

adjective
showing images in natural colors
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
(of television, film, illustration, etc.) able to display or produce images in natural colors rather than only in black and white.

Examples

color television screen
The artist prefers working with color film over black-and-white.
The magazine includes several color illustrations of the city's landmarks.
We bought a new color television for the living room.

Related Terms

  • antonym – black-and-white: displaying images only in black, white, and shades of gray
