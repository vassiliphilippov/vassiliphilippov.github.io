petrifaction

noun
process of turning organic material into stone
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛtrɪˈfækʃən /; / ˌpɛtrəˈfækʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɛtrɪˈfækʃən / (BrE)
the process by which organic material (like plants) becomes fossilized and turns into stone, or the fossilized object created by this process
