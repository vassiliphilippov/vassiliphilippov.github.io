disforestation

noun
removal or clearing of forests
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsˌfɔrəˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsˌfɒrɪˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
the removal or clearing away of forests or trees, especially by deliberate action

Examples

disforestation of rainforests
Environmental groups are working to reduce disforestation in endangered rainforests.
The rapid disforestation of the area has led to severe soil erosion and loss of wildlife.
Unregulated logging often results in significant disforestation, threatening local ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deforestation: the action of clearing a wide area of trees
