careless

adjective
not paying attention; not careful
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkeə.ləs / (BrE)
not giving enough attention or concern; not careful

Examples

careless mistake
His careless driving caused a minor accident on the way to work.
She made a careless mistake on the test, costing her valuable points.
You should not be careless with other people's feelings.
He was careless of the risks, acting as if nothing could go wrong.
It was terribly careless of him to forget the safety instructions.
She drove with a careless disregard for the icy roads.

Related Terms

  • antonym – careful: giving close attention to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym – heedless: showing a reckless lack of care or attention
  • synonym – negligent: failing to take proper care in doing something
  • degree higher – reckless: showing a lack of concern for danger or consequences
  • synonym – thoughtless: not showing consideration for others or consequences
