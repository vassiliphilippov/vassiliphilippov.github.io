wispy

adjective
thin or faint; lacking clarity
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspi / (AmE), / ˈwɪspi / (BrE)
thin, faint, or lacking clarity or distinctness; not clearly defined or strong

Examples

wispy memories
As time passed, her memories of the event became increasingly wispy.
The artist captured only a wispy suggestion of the landscape in soft charcoal.
Through the fog, we could see only a wispy outline of the old house.
