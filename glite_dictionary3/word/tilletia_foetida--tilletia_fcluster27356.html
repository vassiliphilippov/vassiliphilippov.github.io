Tilletia foetida

noun
species of fungus causing wheat bunt
Pronunciation: / tɪˈlɛtiə foʊˈiːtɪdə / (AmE), / tɪˈlɛtiə fəʊˈɛtɪdə / (BrE)
A species of fungus that causes bunt disease in wheat, similar to Tilletia caries.
