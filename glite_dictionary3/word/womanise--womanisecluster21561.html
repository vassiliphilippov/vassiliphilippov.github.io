to womanise

verb
(of a man) pursue numerous amorous affairs
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənaɪz / (BrE)
(of a man) to have many amorous or sexual relationships with women, often considered as a habitual behavior.

Examples

to womanise with many women
He has been womanising for years, much to the dismay of his friends.
Rumors spread when the politician was caught womanising at exclusive parties.
Some novels romanticize the idea of a bachelor who loves to womanise.

Related Terms

  • synonym – womanizer: a man who habitually has many sexual relationships with women
