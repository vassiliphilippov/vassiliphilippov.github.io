cool

adjective
moderately low in temperature
Pronunciation: / kuːl / (AmE), / kuːl / (BrE)
At a temperature that is fairly low but not cold, often pleasantly so.

Examples

cool evening breeze
She wore a light sweater because the room felt cool in the morning.
The evening air was cool after a long, hot summer day.
We enjoyed some cool lemonade while sitting outside in the shade.
