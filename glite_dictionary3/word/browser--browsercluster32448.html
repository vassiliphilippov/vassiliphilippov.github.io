browser

noun
person browsing casually (esp. in a shop)
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊzɚ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊzə / (BrE)
a person who looks around casually without intending to buy or find anything specific, often in a shop or library

Examples

casual browser in a shop
Although I walked through the gallery as a browser, I ended up falling in love with a painting.
Librarians encourage even a casual browser to explore new sections of the library.
The store manager greeted every browser with a friendly smile, never pressuring anyone to buy.
