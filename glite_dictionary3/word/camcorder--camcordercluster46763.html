camcorder

noun
portable video camera and recorder device
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmˌkɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈkamˌkɔːdə / (BrE)
A portable electronic device that combines a video camera and a video recorder in one unit.

Examples

digital camcorder
Before smartphones, people often used a camcorder to capture family vacations.
He brought his camcorder to record the wedding ceremony.
The journalist used a camcorder to document the protest.
