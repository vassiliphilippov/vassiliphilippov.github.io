to click

verb
to suddenly become clear or understood
Pronunciation: / klɪk / (AmE), / klɪk / (BrE)
(of an idea or understanding) to suddenly become clear or fully understood.

Examples

idea clicks
After struggling with the concept all week, it finally clicked during the review session.
Sometimes it just takes a different explanation for things to click.
The solution clicked in her mind as she reread the instructions.
