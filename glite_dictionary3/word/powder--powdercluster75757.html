to powder

verb
make into a fine dry substance
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
to make (something) into a fine, dry substance by crushing or grinding it

Examples

to powder spices
Pharmaceutical companies often powder raw materials for easier formulation.
The artist carefully powdered the pigment before mixing it with oil.
The mill is used to powder spices for cooking.
