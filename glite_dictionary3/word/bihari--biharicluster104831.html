Bihari

noun
group of Indic languages spoken in Bihar
Pronunciation: / bɪˈhɑri / (AmE), / bɪˈhɑːri / (BrE)
a group of Indic languages spoken in the Indian state of Bihar and surrounding regions, and by communities in Pakistan and Bangladesh
