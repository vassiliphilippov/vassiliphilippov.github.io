scenically

adverb
in a way relating to scenery
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.nɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˈsiː.nɪ.kli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to stage scenery or presents a pleasing appearance, especially of a landscape

Examples

scenically located hill
Her house is scenically situated on top of a hill overlooking the valley.
The play was scenically spectacular, with impressive sets and lighting.
The route is scenically beautiful, winding through mountains and forests.
