to reignite

verb
ignite again; catch fire again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪɡˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪɡˈnaɪt / (BrE)
to ignite or catch fire again; to cause something to start burning once more

Examples

to reignite a fire
A spark from the nearby grill could reignite the pile of leaves if caution is not exercised.
Her passion for painting began to reignite after she visited the art gallery last summer.
The strong winds caused the embers to reignite after the fire was thought extinguished.
