Ceftin

proper noun
antibiotic medication (brand name)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛf.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛf.tɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for an antibiotic (cefuroxime axetil) used to treat various bacterial infections, including those of the lungs, throat, ears, and urinary tract.
