diabolically

adverb
in an extremely wicked or devilish way
Pronunciation: / daɪəˈbɑlɪkli / (AmE), / daɪəˈbɒlɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is extremely wicked or characteristic of a devil; with evil intent.

Examples

diabolically clever plan
She smiled diabolically as she revealed her devious plan.
The machine was designed diabolically to confuse anyone trying to use it.
The villain diabolically plotted to destroy the hero's reputation.
