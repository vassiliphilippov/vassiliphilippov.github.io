inhuman

adjective
not human in form or quality
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ɪnˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
not belonging to or resembling a human or human qualities; alien or strange in form or nature.

Examples

inhuman appearance
A strange, inhuman light flickered deep within the cavern.
The creature made an inhuman sound that sent chills down my spine.
The scream was so inhuman that everyone in the room fell silent.
