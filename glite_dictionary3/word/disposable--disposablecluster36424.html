disposable

noun
item designed for single use
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspoʊzəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈspəʊzəbəl / (BrE)
an item designed to be used once or only a few times before being thrown away

Examples

plastic disposables
At the party, we used paper disposables so that cleaning up would be easy.
More people are switching from plastic disposables to reusable products.
Single-use disposables create a significant environmental burden if not recycled properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – throwaway: something intended to be discarded after use
