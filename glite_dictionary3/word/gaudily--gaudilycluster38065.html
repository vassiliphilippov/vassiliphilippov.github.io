gaudily

adverb
in a tastelessly showy way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔːdɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːdɪli / (BrE)
in a showy and tastelessly bright or ornate manner

Examples

dress gaudily for a party
She dressed gaudily for the party, wearing neon colors and oversized jewelry.
The advertisement was gaudily designed, making it difficult to look at for long.
The living room was gaudily decorated with gold tinsel and flashing lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym – garishly: in a brightly and unpleasantly showy manner
