forgettable

adjective
easily forgotten; not memorable
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛɾəbl̩ / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛtəbl̩ / (BrE)
Easily forgotten; not memorable or noteworthy.

Examples

forgettable movie
Despite the hype, her performance was sadly forgettable.
Most of the items on the menu were unremarkable and entirely forgettable.
The movie was so forgettable that I can't recall any of the characters a week later.
