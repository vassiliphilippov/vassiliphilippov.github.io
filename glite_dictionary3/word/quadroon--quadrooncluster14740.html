quadroon

noun
person of one-quarter Black ancestry (archaic, offensive)
Pronunciation: / kwɑˈdruːn / (AmE), / kwɒˈdruːn / (BrE)
(historical, offensive) A person who has one Black grandparent and three White grandparents; a person of one-quarter Black ancestry.

Examples

a quadroon woman
He learned that his great-grandmother was classified as a quadroon under the old laws.
In nineteenth-century literature, the character was often described as a quadroon, reflecting period attitudes toward race.
The word quadroon is now considered outdated and offensive, but was once used in census records.
