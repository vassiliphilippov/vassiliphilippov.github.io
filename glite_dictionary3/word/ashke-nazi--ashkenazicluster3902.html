Ashke-Nazi

noun
offensive term targeting Jews or Ashkenazi Jews
Pronunciation: / ˌæʃkəˈnɑzi / (AmE), / ˌæʃkəˈnɑːzi / (BrE)
A highly offensive derogatory term used to insult Jews, especially those associated with Ashkenazi descent or certain political views.

Examples

call someone an Ashke-Nazi
He was deeply offended when someone called him an Ashke-Nazi online.
The term Ashke-Nazi has caused outrage in Jewish communities for its hateful connotations.
Using Ashke-Nazi in conversation is considered deeply offensive and unacceptable.
