CPI

noun
cost per app install
Pronunciation: / si pi a6a / (AmE), / sid0 pid0 a6a / (BrE)
In mobile advertising, the average cost to acquire a single installation of an app, calculated by dividing total campaign spending by the number of installs.

Examples

CPI for app installs
The team tracked CPI daily to see whether their ad spending led to affordable user growth.
Increasing competition raised the CPI, forcing managers to rethink their app marketing strategy.
They reduced bid prices until the campaign's CPI matched the predicted lifetime value per user.
