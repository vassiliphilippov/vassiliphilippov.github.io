to pump

verb
question persistently to get information
Pronunciation: / pʌmp / (AmE), / pʌmp / (BrE)
to question someone persistently and repeatedly in order to extract information

Examples

to pump someone for details
Reporters pumped the spokesman for an official comment, but he refused to answer.
The detective tried to pump the suspect for more details about the incident.
They kept pumping her with questions until she finally revealed the secret.

  • synonym – interrogate: to question formally and thoroughly
