scriptorium

noun
room in a monastery for writing manuscripts
Pronunciation: / skrɪpˈtɔriəm / (AmE), / skrɪpˈtɔːrɪəm / (BrE)
a room in a monastery set aside for writing or copying manuscripts, especially in the Middle Ages

Examples

monastery scriptorium
Many illuminated manuscripts were produced in the scriptorium of the abbey.
The discovery of a medieval scriptorium shed light on the monastery’s literary activity.
The monks gathered in the scriptorium each morning to copy ancient texts by hand.
