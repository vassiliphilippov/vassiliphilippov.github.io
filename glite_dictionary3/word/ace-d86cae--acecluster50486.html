to ace

verb
score a point with an unreturnable serve or shot
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
to serve or hit a ball in sports (like tennis or volleyball) so that the opponent cannot touch or return it, scoring a point directly

Examples

to ace a serve
Every time he aces a point, the crowd erupts in applause.
He aced the serve and won the match.
She managed to ace her opponent in the final game with a powerful serve.
He loves the feeling when he aces a tough competitor.
If you ace your rival on match point, you win in spectacular style.
She managed to ace her opponent with three powerful serves in a row.
