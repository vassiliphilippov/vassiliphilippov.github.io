Rhizopoda

noun
group of creeping protozoans
Pronunciation: / raɪˈzɑːpədə / (AmE), / raɪˈzɒpədə / (BrE)
a group of protozoans (single-celled organisms) that move and feed by extending parts of their body called pseudopodia, including amoebas and foraminifers
