supported

adjective
held up; weight borne from below
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrtɪd / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪd / (BrE)
(of an object or structure) held up or having its weight sustained, especially from below.

Examples

supported by metal brackets
Each shelf is supported by sturdy metal brackets underneath.
The old bridge is still supported by its original stone piers.
The statue remained supported by the pedestal even during the earthquake.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unsupported: not held up or having its weight borne
