week

noun
unit of work hours or days in a week
Pronunciation: / wik / (AmE), / wiːk / (BrE)
the number of hours or days that someone works in a calendar week, regarded as a unit of work time

Examples

full-time week
He recently switched to a part-time week to spend more time with his family.
Many employees work a standard 40-hour week in the office.
She prefers a shorter week with flexible hours.
