porbeagle

noun
species of shark in northern oceans
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrˌbiːgəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔːˌbiːgəl / (BrE)
a large, voracious shark (Lamna nasus) with a pointed snout, found in the northern Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.

Examples

a porbeagle shark
A mature porbeagle can grow up to nearly three meters in length.
Fishermen often encounter porbeagle sharks in the cold waters off the coast of Maine.
