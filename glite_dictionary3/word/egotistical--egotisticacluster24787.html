egotistical

adjective
excessively self-centered or conceited
Pronunciation: / ˌiːɡəˈtɪstɪkəl /; / ˌiɡəˈtɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəˈtɪstɪk(ə)l /; / ˌiːɡəˈtɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
having an exaggerated sense of self-importance; being excessively self-centered or conceited
