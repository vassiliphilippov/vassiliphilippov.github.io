bob

noun
weight on string, esp. in a pendulum
Pronunciation: / bɑb / (AmE), / bɒb / (BrE)
a heavy object, especially a metal ball, attached to a string or rod and allowed to swing, as in a pendulum

Examples

pendulum bob
He attached a metal bob to the end of the string to create a simple pendulum for his experiment.
The clock's pendulum swung back and forth, with the brass bob shining in the light.
The length and mass of the bob affect how quickly the pendulum swings.
