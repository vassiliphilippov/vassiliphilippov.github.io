haulier

noun
person or company transporting goods by road
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːliər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːliə / (BrE)
A person or company that transports goods by road for other people, usually as a business.

Examples

haulier of goods
After the company expanded, they became the region's largest haulier of refrigerated goods.
Many farmers hire a local haulier to transport their crops to market.
The haulier delivered the construction materials to the site early in the morning.
