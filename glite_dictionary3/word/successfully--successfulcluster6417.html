successfully

adverb
in a successful way
Pronunciation: / səkˈsɛs.fəli / (AmE), / səkˈsɛs.fəli / (BrE)

Examples

He negotiated the contract successfully, ensuring favorable terms for his company.
The new treatment was successfully tested on thousands of patients.
