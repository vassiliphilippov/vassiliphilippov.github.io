to gormandise

verb
eat greedily or excessively
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔrmənˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːm(ə)nˌdaɪz / (BrE)
To eat greedily or excessively; to overeat in an immoderate way.

Examples
