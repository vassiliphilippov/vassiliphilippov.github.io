unchangeable

adjective
not able to be changed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃeɪndʒəbl / (BrE)
not able to be changed; incapable of being altered or modified.

Examples

unchangeable rule
She believed that her fate was unchangeable, no matter what she tried.
Some traditions are considered unchangeable within certain cultures.
The rules for the contest are unchangeable once officially announced.

Related Terms

  • antonym – alterable: able to be changed or modified
  • synonym – immutable: unchanging over time or unable to be changed
