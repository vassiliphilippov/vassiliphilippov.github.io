Rhizobium

noun
genus of nitrogen-fixing bacteria in plant roots
Pronunciation: / raɪˈzoʊbiəm / (AmE), / raɪˈzəʊbɪəm /; / rɪˈzəʊbiəm / (BrE)
a genus of bacteria that live in the root nodules of leguminous plants and help fix nitrogen from the atmosphere
