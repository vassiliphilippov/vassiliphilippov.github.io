denaturised

adjective
altered from natural state
Pronunciation: / dɪˈneɪtʃɚaɪzd / (AmE), / dɪˈneɪtʃəraɪzd / (BrE)
(of a substance) having been changed so that its natural qualities or composition are altered, especially to make it unsuitable for consumption or original use.

Examples

denaturised alcohol
Because it is denaturised, this alcohol is not safe for drinking.
Many products contain denaturised ingredients to prevent misuse or accidental ingestion.
The laboratory uses denaturised alcohol as a solvent for chemical reactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – denatured: altered from the natural state, especially of alcohol
