gradual

adjective
happening slowly over time
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrædʒuəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrædʒuəl / (BrE)
happening or changing slowly and steadily over time; not sudden or steep

Examples

gradual change
A gradual incline made the hike manageable for beginners.
The recovery was gradual, with improvements seen over several months.

A gradual slope leads down to the riverbank, perfect for picnics.
The hill had a gradual incline, making the climb much easier.
Their driveway is gradual enough that it can be cleared easily after a snowfall.
