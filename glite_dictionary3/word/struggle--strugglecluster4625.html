struggle

noun
a serious clash or conflict between sides
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌɡ.əl / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡ.əl / (BrE)
a serious fight, conflict, or clash between people or groups, especially where both sides strongly oppose each other

Examples

the struggle for freedom
The struggle for independence united people from all walks of life.
The labor union's struggle with management lasted for several months.
There was a fierce struggle between the two factions for control of the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym – battle: a fight between opposing armed forces or groups
  • synonym – conflict: a serious disagreement or argument, often a prolonged one
  • antonym – peace: a state of tranquility or quiet, especially as the absence of war or conflict
