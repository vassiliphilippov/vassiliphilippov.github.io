Pico de Orizaba

proper noun
the highest mountain in Mexico
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːkoʊ deɪ ɔːˈriːzəbə / (AmE), / ˈpiːkəʊ deɪ ɒˈrɪzəbə / (BrE)
an extinct volcano in southern Mexico, situated between Mexico City and Veracruz; it is the highest peak in Mexico at 18,695 feet (5,636 meters).
