vainglorious

adjective
excessively proud, boastful
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪnˈɡlɔːriəs / (AmE), / ˌveɪnˈɡlɔːriəs / (BrE)
having or showing an excessively high opinion of one's own appearance, abilities, or worth; boastful or conceited.

Examples

vainglorious speech
His vainglorious speeches annoyed even his supporters in the audience.
She was too vainglorious to admit her mistakes to her colleagues.
The vainglorious king built monuments to celebrate his own achievements.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conceited: having an excessively high opinion of oneself
  • antonym – modest: having a humble or moderate view of one’s own importance
