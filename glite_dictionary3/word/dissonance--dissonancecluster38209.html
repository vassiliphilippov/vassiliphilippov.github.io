dissonance

noun
lack of agreement; conflict
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪs.ə.nəns / (AmE), / ˈdɪs.ə.nəns / (BrE)
A lack of agreement or harmony between people, opinions, or actions.

Examples

social dissonance
Political dissonance increased as the candidates presented opposing views.
The dissonance between his words and his actions made people question his sincerity.
There was obvious dissonance among the team members regarding the next steps for the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym – discord: disagreement or conflict between people
  • antonym – harmony: agreement; a state of peaceful coexistence
