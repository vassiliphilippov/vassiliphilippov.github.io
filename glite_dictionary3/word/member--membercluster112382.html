member

noun
organization belonging to another organization
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
an organization that officially belongs to another organization, such as a company, state, or institution forming part of an association, group, or alliance

Examples

member state
Canada is a member of the United Nations.
Each university in the consortium is a full member with voting rights.
The city became a member of the global cities coalition last year.
