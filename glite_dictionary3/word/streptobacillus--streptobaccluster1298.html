streptobacillus

noun
a rod-shaped Gram-negative bacterium
Pronunciation: / ˌstɹɛptə bəˈsɪləs / (AmE), / ˌstɹɛptə bəˈsɪləs / (BrE)
A rod-shaped Gram-negative bacterium, often forming chains, some species of which can cause diseases in humans and animals.
