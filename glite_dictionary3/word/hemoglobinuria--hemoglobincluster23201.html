hemoglobinuria

noun
hemoglobin in urine (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmoʊˌɡloʊbəˈnjʊriə / (AmE), / ˌhiːməʊˌɡləʊbɪˈnjʊəriə / (BrE)
The presence of hemoglobin in the urine, usually due to the breakdown of red blood cells.
