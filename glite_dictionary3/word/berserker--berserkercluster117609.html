berserker

noun
Norse warrior fighting in a frenzy
Pronunciation: / bərˈzɜrkər / (AmE), / bəˈzɜːkə / (BrE)
in Norse legend, a warrior who fought with wild, uncontrollable ferocity, often said to enter a trance-like rage before battle
