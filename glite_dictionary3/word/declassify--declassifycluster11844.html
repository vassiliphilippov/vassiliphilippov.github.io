to declassify

verb
remove classified status from information
Pronunciation: / ˌdiˈklæsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌdiːˈklæsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to officially remove the classification status from something, making it accessible to the public

Examples

to declassify secret documents
After much debate, the agency agreed to declassify the report for public release.
Journalists requested that the military declassify information about the historic mission.
The government decided to declassify several Cold War documents this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – classify: to assign something to a category, especially as secret or restricted information
