cream-colored

adjective
pale yellowish-white in color
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːm ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈkriːm ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a pale yellowish-white color similar to that of fresh cream

Examples

cream-colored cat
A cream-colored cat was sleeping in the sun by the window.
She bought a cream-colored sofa that matched her living room decor perfectly.
The bride wore a beautiful cream-colored dress made of silk.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ivory: white or yellowish-white color, resembling ivory
  • synonym – off-white: a white color with a slight tint of another color, often yellow or grey
  • degree higher – white: the color of milk or fresh snow, the palest color
  • degree higher – yellow: the color between green and orange in the spectrum, like that of lemon or gold
