kinky

adjective
hair: tightly curled or twisted
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋki / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋki / (BrE)
(of hair) having small tight curls or twists, often forming a dense texture.

Examples

kinky hair
After washing, Mara's kinky locks became even more defined and voluminous.
He prefers to wear his kinky hair natural rather than straightening it.
Her kinky hair always stood out in the crowd, framing her face with tight curls.
