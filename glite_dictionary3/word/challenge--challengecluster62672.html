challenge

noun
call to compete or fight
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæləndʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (BrE)
a call or invitation to compete, especially in a contest, fight, or game

Examples

a challenge to compete
His bold challenge to the authority of the leader surprised everyone in the room.
She accepted the challenge to play against the reigning chess champion.
The knight issued a challenge to his rival, demanding a duel at dawn.

Related Terms

  • antonym – acceptance: an act of agreeing to something offered, especially a challenge
  • synonym – contest: a competition or struggle between rivals
  • synonym – invitation: an act of inviting someone to participate
