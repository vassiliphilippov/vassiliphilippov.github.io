cured

adjective
rubber processed to improve properties
Pronunciation: / kjʊrd / (AmE), / kjʊəd / (BrE)
treated or preserved by a special process (such as drying, salting, chemical treatment, or aging), especially for materials, food, or plants

Examples

  • synonym – set: of cement, concrete, etc.: to become hard or solid
