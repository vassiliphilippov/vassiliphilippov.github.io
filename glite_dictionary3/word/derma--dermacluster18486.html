derma

noun
the inner layer of the skin
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜːrmə / (AmE), / ˈdɜːmə / (BrE)
the deep, vascular inner layer of the skin beneath the epidermis; also known as the dermis

Examples

derma under epidermis
Damage to the derma can result in scarring or impaired healing.
The derma provides strength and flexibility to the skin.
The tattoo needle deposits ink into the derma rather than the outer epidermis.
