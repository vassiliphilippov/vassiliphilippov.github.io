dispirited

adjective
lacking enthusiasm or hope
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɪrɪtɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈspɪrɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling or showing a lack of enthusiasm, hope, or spirit; downhearted or discouraged

Examples

dispirited team

She tried to cheer her dispirited friend with a kind message.
The team grew dispirited after losing three games in a row.
