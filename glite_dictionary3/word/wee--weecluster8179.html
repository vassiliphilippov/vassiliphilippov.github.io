wee

adjective
very early (as in 'wee hours')
Pronunciation: / wiː / (AmE), / wiː / (BrE)
(of time) occurring very early, especially soon after midnight or in the early morning.

Examples

wee hours of morning
I stayed up until the wee hours finishing my assignment.
She prefers to go jogging in the wee hours of the morning before the city wakes up.
The party lasted into the wee hours, long after midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – early: occurring before the usual or expected time
