alien

adjective
foreign; from another place or country
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪliən / (AmE), / ˈeɪliən / (BrE)
from another place, country, or very different in nature; foreign or unfamiliar

Examples

alien plants
She found the tropical plants in the greenhouse quite alien to her northern upbringing.
The ancient city was filled with alien customs that surprised the visitors.
The dish featured spices that were completely alien to the local cuisine.
Jealousy is alien to her nature; she is always happy for others.
The customs of the new country felt alien to him at first.
This approach is completely alien to our traditions and values.
