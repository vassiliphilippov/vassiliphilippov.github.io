Francis of Assisi

proper noun
Italian monk, founder of Franciscans, Catholic saint
Pronunciation: / ˈfræn.sɪs əv əˈsi.si / (AmE), / ˈfræn.sɪs əv əˈsiː.si / (BrE)
(c. 1181–1226) An Italian monk and preacher who founded the Franciscan order of friars and is venerated as a saint in the Roman Catholic Church.
