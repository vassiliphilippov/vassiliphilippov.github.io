motor

noun
a driving force or agent of change
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tə / (BrE)
a person, force, or thing that brings about movement, action, or change; a driving force

Examples

motor of change
Curiosity was the motor behind her lifelong pursuit of knowledge.
His passion became the motor that drove the entire project forward.
Innovation is often the key motor of economic development.
