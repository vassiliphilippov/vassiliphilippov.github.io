radiantly

adverb
in a bright, joyful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.di.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.di.ənt.li / (BrE)
In a bright, joyful, or glowing manner; emitting radiance or happiness.

Examples

radiantly smiled
He smiled radiantly as he accepted the award.
She beamed radiantly when she received the good news.
The garden glowed radiantly in the morning sunlight.
