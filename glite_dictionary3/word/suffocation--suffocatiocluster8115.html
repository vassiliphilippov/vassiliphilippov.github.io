suffocation

noun
killing by depriving of oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌfəˈkeɪʃn̩ /; / ˌsʌfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌfəˈkeɪʃn /; / ˌsʌfəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of being deprived of oxygen, causing difficulty in breathing or death
