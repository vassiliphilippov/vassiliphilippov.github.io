harmonisation

noun
alignment of rules or standards
Pronunciation: / \u02cch\u0251\u02d0rm\u0259n\u0259\u02c8ze\u026a\u0283\u0259n / (AmE), / \u02cch\u0251\u02d0m\u0259na\u026a\u02c8ze\u026a\u0283n / (BrE)
the process of making laws, regulations, standards, or technical specifications consistent across different organizations, jurisdictions, or systems.

Examples

harmonisation of laws
The harmonisation of data protection rules across the EU facilitated cross-border business.
Industry groups met to discuss the harmonisation of safety standards for medical devices.
Successful harmonisation between the states reduced duplicate testing and lowered company costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – harmonization: the American spelling of 'harmonisation'; the act of making rules or standards consistent
  • synonym – standardisation: the process of establishing common technical standards and specifications
