hypnotised

adjective
having attention fixated as if by a spell
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪp.nə.taɪzd / (AmE), / ˈhɪp.nə.taɪzd / (BrE)
having one's attention completely fixed, as if by a spell or under hypnosis

Examples

hypnotised by the show
I was hypnotised by the dancer's graceful movements and could not look away.
She sat hypnotised by the flickering flames in the fireplace, completely oblivious to her surroundings.
The children listened hypnotised as the magician told his mesmerizing story.
