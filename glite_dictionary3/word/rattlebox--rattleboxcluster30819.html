rattlebox

noun
plant with rattling seed pods (genus Crotalaria)
Pronunciation: / ˈrætlˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈrætlˌbɒks / (BrE)
Any of various plants of the genus Crotalaria, noted for their inflated pods that rattle when shaken; used as pasture and for green manure.

Examples

dried rattlebox pods
Children used to collect dried rattlebox pods and shake them like maracas.
Many farmers plant rattlebox to improve soil fertility as a green manure crop.
The inflated pods of the rattlebox make a distinct sound when the wind shakes them.
