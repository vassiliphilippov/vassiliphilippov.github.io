Cotula

noun
a genus of ground-covering herbs
Pronunciation: / koʊˈtuːlə / (AmE), / kəˈtjuːlə / (BrE)
a genus of herbaceous plants in the daisy family, found in many parts of the world and often used as ground cover

Examples

cotula ground cover
Cotula is often chosen as ground cover in landscaping due to its rapid growth and resilience.
Several species in the Cotula genus are valued for their ability to thrive in poor soils.
