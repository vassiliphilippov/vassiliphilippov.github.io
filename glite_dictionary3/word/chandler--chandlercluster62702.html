chandler

noun
seller of provisions and supplies
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃændlər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːn(d)lə / (BrE)
a person or business that sells provisions, supplies, or equipment, especially for ships.

Examples

chandler supplies ships
A skilled chandler ensured the fleet was fully equipped for the long voyage.
She inherited her family's chandler business, supplying local fishermen with everything they needed.
The ship's crew visited the chandler to stock up on food and tools before departure.
