to vulcanise

verb
undergo rubber vulcanization process
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlkəˌnaɪz /; / ˈvʌl.kə.naɪz / (AmE), / ˈvʌlkənaɪz /; / ˈvʌl.kə.naɪz / (BrE)
to treat rubber (or similar materials) with sulfur or other substances under heat to make it stronger and more elastic
