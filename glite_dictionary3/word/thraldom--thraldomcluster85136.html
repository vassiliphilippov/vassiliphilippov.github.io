thraldom

noun
state of being under another's control
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɔl.dəm / (AmE), / ˈθrɔːl.dəm / (BrE)
the state of being under the control of another person; complete subjugation or bondage

Examples

escape thraldom
Centuries of thraldom left lasting scars on the nation's identity.
He struggled to escape the thraldom imposed by his oppressors.
Many lived their entire lives in thraldom, never knowing freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bondage: the state of being a slave or completely controlled by something
  • antonym – freedom: the power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants; absence of subjugation
