harmoniser

noun
musician providing harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənaɪzə / (BrE)
a musician who sings or plays musical parts that form harmony with others in a group

Examples

harmoniser in a band
As a harmoniser, Mark adds depth and richness to every performance.
Sarah is a talented harmoniser in the school's vocal ensemble.
The band recruited a new harmoniser to improve their sound.
