to blather

verb
talk foolishly or meaninglessly
Pronunciation: / ˈblæðər / (AmE), / ˈblɑːðə / (BrE)
to talk at length in a foolish or meaningless way

Examples

to blather on endlessly
He tends to blather on about things he doesn't understand.
She was blathering nervously during the entire meeting.
Stop blathering and get to the point.

Related Terms

  • synonym – babble: to talk rapidly and continuously in a foolish way
  • synonym – prattle: to talk at length in a foolish or inconsequential way
