forgery

noun
criminal falsification of documents or objects
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrdʒɚi / (AmE), / ˈfɔːdʒəri / (BrE)
The crime of making a false document, signature, or imitation of an object with the intent to deceive or defraud.
