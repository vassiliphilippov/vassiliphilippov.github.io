to scent

verb
detect by smell
Pronunciation: / sɛnt / (AmE), / sɛnt / (BrE)
to detect or search for something by using the sense of smell, especially used about animals

Examples

to scent prey
Some animals can scent their prey from miles away.
The bear scented danger and moved cautiously through the woods.
The police dog quickly scented the missing person's trail in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym – smell: to perceive or detect the odor of something
  • synonym – sniff: to smell something by taking air in through the nose
