steeplechase

noun
horse race with obstacles
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːpəlˌtʃeɪs / (AmE), / ˈstiːpəlˌtʃeɪs / (BrE)
a race for horses in which competitors jump over fences, water jumps, and other obstacles along the course

Examples

horse steeplechase race
He trained his horse specifically for the challenging steeplechase course.
The annual steeplechase drew crowds from all over the region.
Winning the steeplechase is considered a major achievement in the equestrian world.
