astute

adjective
shrewd; clever at assessing situations
Pronunciation: / əˈstuːt / (AmE), / əˈstjuːt / (BrE)
having or showing a keen ability to accurately assess situations and turn this to one's advantage; shrewd and perceptive.

Examples

astute analysis
Her astute analysis of the situation impressed her colleagues.
Only an astute investor would spot such an opportunity in the market.
The manager made an astute observation that helped save the company money.

Related Terms

  • antonym – naive: lacking experience, wisdom, or judgment
  • synonym – shrewd: having or showing sharp powers of judgment; astute
