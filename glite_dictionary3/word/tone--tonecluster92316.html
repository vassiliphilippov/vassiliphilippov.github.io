tone

noun
a subtle variation in color
Pronunciation: / toʊn / (AmE), / təʊn / (BrE)
a slight variation or quality of a particular color, differing subtly from another color

Examples

light tone of blue
Interior designers often use different tones of the same color to add variety to a room.
She preferred a warmer tone of yellow for the kitchen walls.
The artist blended several tones of blue to create a sense of depth in the painting.
