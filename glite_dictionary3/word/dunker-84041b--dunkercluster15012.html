dunker

noun
a person who dips food before eating
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌŋkər / (AmE), / ˈdʌŋkə / (BrE)
a person who dips food into a drink or other liquid before eating it

Examples

dunker of cookies
As a habitual dunker, she never eats cookies without milk.
My grandfather is a true dunker, always dipping his biscuits into tea.
You could tell he was a dunker by the crumbs left in his coffee.
