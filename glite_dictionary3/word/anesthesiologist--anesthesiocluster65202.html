anesthesiologist

noun
doctor trained in anesthesia
Pronunciation: / ˌænəsˌθiːziˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌænəsˌθiːziˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a medical doctor who specializes in administering anesthesia and managing pain for patients during surgery and other procedures

Examples

anesthesiologist in surgery
Before surgery, the anesthesiologist explained what type of anesthesia would be used.
Residents in training often observe an anesthesiologist to learn the proper techniques.
The anesthesiologist monitored the patient's vital signs throughout the operation.
