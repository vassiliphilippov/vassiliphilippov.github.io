Sasquatch

noun
legendary hairy creature of North America
Pronunciation: / ˈsæskwɑːtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsæskwɒtʃ / (BrE)
a large, hairy, humanlike creature said to inhabit remote forests of the northwestern United States and Canada; also called Bigfoot.
