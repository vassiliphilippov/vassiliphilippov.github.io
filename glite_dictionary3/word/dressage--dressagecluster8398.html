dressage

noun
equestrian sport of horse maneuvers
Pronunciation: / drəˈsɑʒ / (AmE), / drɛˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
the art or sport of training a horse to perform precise movements in response to subtle commands from its rider

Examples

dressage competition
At the Olympics, the dressage routines displayed remarkable coordination between rider and horse.
She has been training her horse for dressage competitions throughout the year.
Years of practice are required to master the subtle cues necessary for successful dressage.
