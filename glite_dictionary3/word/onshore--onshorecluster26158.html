onshore

adjective
located on or near the land by the sea
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌʃɔr / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌʃɔː / (BrE)
Situated on or moving toward the land, especially along the coast; located on the land near the sea.

Examples

onshore swimming area
