tincture

noun
alcohol-based medicinal extract
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋktʃər / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋktʃə / (BrE)
A medicine made by dissolving a drug or plant extract in alcohol.

Examples

herbal tincture medicine
A few drops of the tincture should be diluted in water before taking it.
She purchased a tincture from the pharmacy to help her sleep.
The herbalist prescribed a tincture of echinacea for my cold symptoms.
