moonwort

noun
a small fern with crescent-shaped segments
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌwɝːt / (AmE), / ˈmuːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A small fern of the genus Botrychium, with crescent-shaped segments, found in America, Eurasia, and Australia.
