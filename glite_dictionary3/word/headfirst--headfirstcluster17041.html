headfirst

adverb
with the head foremost
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛdˈfɝst / (AmE), / ˌhɛdˈfɜːst / (BrE)
with the head leading or first, either physically (such as falling or diving) or when doing something quickly without thinking

Examples

to dive headfirst
Don't rush headfirst into big decisions without thinking them through.
She dived headfirst into the icy water, ignoring the cold.
The boy tumbled headfirst down the hill after tripping on a rock.
He tripped and fell headfirst onto the pavement, but only scraped his knee.
She dove headfirst into the cool lake without hesitation.
The children raced headfirst down the grassy hill, laughing as they rolled.
