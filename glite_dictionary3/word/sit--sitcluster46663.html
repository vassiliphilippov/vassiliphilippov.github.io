to sit

verb
to sit on and ride an animal
Pronunciation: / sɪt / (AmE), / sɪt / (BrE)
(of a person) to sit on and ride an animal, typically a horse, often while controlling its movements.

Examples

to sit a horse
He could sit a camel for hours during their desert journey.
She learned to sit a horse like a professional rider.
The instructor taught the children how to properly sit a pony.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ride: to be carried or supported by an animal or vehicle and travel in this way
