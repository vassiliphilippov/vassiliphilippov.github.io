to urbanise

verb
to impart urban habits or lifestyle
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.bə.naɪz / (AmE), / ˈɜː.bə.naɪz / (BrE)
to impart urban habits, ways of life, or responsibilities to a person, group, or thing

Examples

to urbanise young people
Educators aim to urbanise young students by exposing them to city culture and technology.
Efforts to urbanise the rural population have met both support and resistance.
Tourism can gradually urbanise wildlife as they adapt to human presence.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ruralize: to impart rural habits or ways of life
  • synonym – urbanize: alternative spelling, especially American English
