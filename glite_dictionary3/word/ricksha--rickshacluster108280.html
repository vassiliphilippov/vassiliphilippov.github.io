ricksha

noun
three-wheeled motorized taxi
Pronunciation: / c8r6ak8359 / (AmE), / c8r6ak8359 / (BrE)
A small three-wheeled motorized passenger vehicle used for short urban trips, often functioning as a cheap taxi (also called an auto rickshaw or tuk-tuk).

Examples

ride a ricksha taxi
In the hot afternoon we jumped into a noisy ricksha and drove through the crowded market.
The driver of the electric ricksha charged passengers by distance rather than by time.
Cities are experimenting with low-emission ricksha fleets to reduce traffic pollution in dense neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • synonym – auto rickshaw: a motorized three-wheeled vehicle used as a taxi, similar or identical in meaning
  • synonym – three-wheeler: a vehicle with three wheels, often used for short-distance urban transport
  • synonym – tuk-tuk: a small motorized three-wheeled vehicle used for passenger transport, especially in Southeast Asia
