to hunt

verb
pursue relentlessly
Pronunciation: / hʌnt / (AmE), / hʌnt / (BrE)
to pursue or chase someone or something relentlessly, often in order to capture, catch, or force them out

Examples

to hunt a suspect

The detectives continued to hunt the fugitive across several states.
Neighbors hunted the stray cats off their property.
The authorities hunted the protestors from the square at dawn.
The new regime hunted political opponents out of the country.
