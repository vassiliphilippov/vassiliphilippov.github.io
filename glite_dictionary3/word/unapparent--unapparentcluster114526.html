unapparent

adjective
not obvious or apparent
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈpærənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈpærənt / (BrE)
not easily seen, noticed, or understood; not readily apparent.

Examples

unapparent symptoms
Some medical conditions remain unapparent until symptoms suddenly appear.
The differences between the two versions were initially unapparent to most reviewers.
The real cause of the problem was unapparent for a long time.
