to miscarry

verb
suffer a miscarriage
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈkæri / (AmE), / mɪsˈkæri / (BrE)
to have a miscarriage; to lose a pregnancy before the fetus can survive outside the womb

Examples

to miscarry a baby
After several attempts, she sadly miscarried again last year.
Doctors explained that it is not uncommon for women to miscarry early in pregnancy.
She was devastated to learn that she would miscarry in her first trimester.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abort: to end a pregnancy early
  • antonym – conceive: to become pregnant
