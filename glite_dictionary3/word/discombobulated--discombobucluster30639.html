discombobulated

adjective
confused; thrown into disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəmˈbɑbjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəmˈbɒbjʊˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
confused and disconcerted; having one's composure disrupted

Examples

feel discombobulated after travel
After missing her flight, she felt completely discombobulated in the busy airport.
He was so discombobulated by the sudden news that he could hardly respond.
The professor appeared discombobulated when the projector suddenly stopped working during his lecture.
