concentricity

noun
the state of having a common center
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.sɛnˈtrɪs.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.sɛnˈtrɪs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
The quality or state of having the same center, especially of circles or spheres that share a common central point.

Examples

concentricity of circles
High concentricity is important when manufacturing bearings for rotating machinery.
In geometry class, we studied the concentricity of circles drawn within each other.
The mechanic measured the concentricity of the shafts to ensure they were correctly aligned.
