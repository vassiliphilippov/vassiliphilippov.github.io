capitate

adjective
abruptly enlarged and rounded at the tip
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪteɪt / (BrE)
(of a part or organ) abruptly enlarged and rounded at the tip; having a head-like, globular end.

Examples

capitate tip of stalk
Certain fungi develop a capitate structure at the end of their stalks.
The capitate gland is easily recognized by its swollen, rounded tip.
The flower cluster had a capitate form, resembling a small rounded head.
