Grace

proper noun
one of the three goddesses of charm and beauty in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪs / (AmE), / ɡreɪs / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) one of the three minor goddesses, known as the Graces, who personify beauty, charm, and gracefulness.
