caroler

noun
person who sings carols
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrələr / (AmE), / ˈkarələ / (BrE)
A person who sings carols, especially Christmas carols, often in a group and sometimes going from house to house.

Examples

Christmas caroler group
Every Christmas Eve, a group of carolers visits our neighborhood to sing festive songs.
The children dressed in warm clothes to join the carolers in the annual holiday parade.
We welcomed the carolers at our doorstep with hot chocolate and applause.
