prophylactic

adjective
preventing conception; contraceptive
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊfləˈklæktɪk / (AmE), / ˌprɒfɪˈlæktɪk / (BrE)
(of a drug, device, or method) capable of preventing conception or impregnation; contraceptive.

Examples

prophylactic method
Some herbal remedies are believed to have prophylactic effects against conception, but their efficacy is unproven.
The doctor recommended a prophylactic medication to help prevent unwanted pregnancy.
Using a prophylactic device is an effective method of birth control.
