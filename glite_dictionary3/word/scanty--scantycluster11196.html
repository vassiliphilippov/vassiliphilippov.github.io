scanty

adjective
revealing or insufficient clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈskæn.ti / (AmE), / ˈskæn.ti / (BrE)
(of clothing) barely sufficient to cover the body; revealing more of the body than is usual or considered appropriate.

Examples

scanty bikini outfit
She wore a scanty bikini to the pool, turning many heads.
Some schools have strict dress codes that prohibit overly scanty outfits.
The actor's scanty costume was considered too daring for television.

Related Terms

  • antonym – modest: (of clothing) not revealing or indecent
  • synonym – skimpy: (of clothing) very small and revealing
