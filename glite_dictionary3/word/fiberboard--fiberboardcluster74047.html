fiberboard

noun
wood-based building board
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbɚˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪ.bə.bɔːd / (BrE)
A building material made from wood chips or shavings that are bonded together with resin and compressed into rigid sheets.

Examples

fiberboard furniture panel
Many modern furniture pieces are constructed from fiberboard rather than solid wood.
The contractor chose fiberboard for the interior walls due to its affordability and insulating properties.
You should avoid getting fiberboard wet, as it can swell and lose its shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym – particleboard: a similar board made from wood particles and resin, compressed into sheets
