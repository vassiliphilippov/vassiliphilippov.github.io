nether

noun
video-game alternate dimension (Minecraft)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛðər / (AmE), / ˈnɛðə / (BrE)
(in video games) a separate, often dangerous alternate realm or dimension players can travel to; notably the fiery subterranean dimension in Minecraft.

Examples

the nether dimension
In Minecraft, players travel to the nether to mine glowstone and craft powerful potions.
She refused to enter the nether portal because the dimension looked too dangerous for beginners.
Many servers add special resources to the nether so factions can build bases and fight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dimension: a separate plane or realm of existence used in fiction and games
  • synonym – underworld: a fictional or mythological world beneath the surface, often dangerous or supernatural
