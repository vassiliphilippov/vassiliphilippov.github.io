pettifoggery

noun
petty or trivial quibbling
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛt̬iˈfɑːɡəri / (AmE), / ˌpɛtɪˈfɒɡəri / (BrE)
the practice of quibbling or arguing over trivial or insignificant matters, especially in a petty or underhanded way

Examples

pointless pettifoggery in meetings
Fed up with the pettifoggery of his colleagues, she called for a decision to be made.
His reputation for pettifoggery made him unpopular in board meetings.
The debate descended into pettifoggery over irrelevant technicalities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bickering: petty and trivial argument or squabble
  • antonym – fairness: the quality of being fair and reasonable
  • synonym – quibbling: the action of raising trivial objections
