to plank

verb
cook and serve on a wooden plank
Pronunciation: / plæŋk / (AmE), / plæŋk / (BrE)
to cook and serve (food, especially fish or meat) on a wooden plank, typically by baking or broiling

Examples

to plank salmon
At the restaurant, they planked the trout and served it with fresh herbs on top.
