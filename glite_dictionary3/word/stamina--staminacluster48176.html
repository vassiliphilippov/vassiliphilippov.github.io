stamina

noun
enduring strength and energy
Pronunciation: / ˈstæm.ə.nə / (AmE), / ˈstæm.ɪ.nə / (BrE)
The physical or mental strength that enables someone to sustain prolonged effort or activity without giving up or becoming exhausted.

Examples

build stamina for running
Running long distances requires a great deal of stamina.
She built up her stamina through months of rigorous training.
The team's stamina was tested during the grueling tournament.

Related Terms

  • synonym – endurance: the ability to withstand hardship or adversity over time
  • antonym – fatigue: extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion
  • synonym – vitality: the state of being strong and active; energy
