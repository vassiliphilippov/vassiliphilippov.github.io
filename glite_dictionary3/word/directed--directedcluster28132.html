directed

adjective
having a specified direction or purpose
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktɪd, daɪˈrɛktɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktɪd, daɪˈrɛktɪd / (BrE)
(often used in combination) having a specified or definite direction or purpose.

Examples

directed effort
Her actions seemed very directed and purposeful throughout the project.
The experiment used a directed flow of air to create specific conditions.
These animals display directed movement toward sources of food.

Related Terms

  • antonym – undirected: not having a specified or definite direction
