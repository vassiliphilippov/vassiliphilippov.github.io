worshiper

noun
person who takes part in worship
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who shows reverence and adoration for a deity or religious figure, especially in a formal religious setting

Examples

devout worshiper at church
As a devoted worshiper, he attended church every Sunday morning.
Hundreds of worshipers gathered at the temple to celebrate the festival.
In the quiet mosque, the worshipers knelt in silent prayer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – believer: a person who believes in religious faith or a particular doctrine
