rightish

adjective
slightly right-wing politically
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪʃ / (BrE)
Having or showing mild or moderate support for right-wing political views.

Examples

rightish political views
Although she holds some rightish opinions on economics, she is liberal on social issues.
His views are not strongly conservative, but they can be described as rightish.
The candidate's rightish platform appeals to voters who are dissatisfied with both extremes.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – centrist: holding moderate or neutral political views, neither left nor right
  • degree higher – right-wing: conservative or reactionary political views
