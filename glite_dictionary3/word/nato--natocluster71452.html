NATO

proper noun
international defense organization (North Atlantic Treaty Organization)
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪˌtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈneɪtəʊ / (BrE)
an international organization formed in 1949 for collective defense and security, comprising countries from North America and Europe

Examples

NATO military alliance
Membership in NATO is considered important for national security by many European countries.
Several countries joined NATO after the end of the Cold War.
The NATO summit discussed new strategies for collective defense.
