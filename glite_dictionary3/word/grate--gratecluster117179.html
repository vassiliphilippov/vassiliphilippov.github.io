to grate

verb
shred food using a rough surface
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt / (AmE), / ɡreɪt / (BrE)
to reduce food into small shreds or powder by rubbing it against a rough or sharp surface

Examples

to grate cheese
Nutmeg is often grated to release its flavor in desserts.
Please grate some cheese for the pasta.
She carefully grated a carrot to add to the salad.
