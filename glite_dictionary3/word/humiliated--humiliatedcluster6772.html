humiliated

adjective
made to feel ashamed or embarrassed
Pronunciation: / hjuˈmɪliˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / hjuˈmɪlɪeɪtɪd /; / hjuˈmɪlieɪtɪd / (BrE)
made to feel ashamed or embarrassed, especially because of a loss of dignity or pride

Examples
