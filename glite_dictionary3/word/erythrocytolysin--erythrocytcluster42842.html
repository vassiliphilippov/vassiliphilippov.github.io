erythrocytolysin

noun
substance that destroys red blood cells
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɪθ.roʊ.saɪˈtɑl.ɪ.sɪn / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪθ.rəʊ.saɪˈtɒl.ɪ.sɪn / (BrE)
