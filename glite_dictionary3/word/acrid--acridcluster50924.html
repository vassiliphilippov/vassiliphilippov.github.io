acrid

adjective
strong, unpleasant taste or smell
Pronunciation: / ˈækrɪd / (AmE), / ˈækrɪd / (BrE)
having a strong and unpleasantly sharp taste or smell; bitter or pungent.

Examples

acrid smoke
She wrinkled her nose at the acrid odor coming from the laboratory.
The acrid smoke from the fire irritated my eyes.
The soup had an acrid aftertaste that lingered unpleasantly.

Related Terms

  • antonym – mild: not strong or sharp in taste or smell
  • synonym – pungent: having a strong, sharp taste or smell
