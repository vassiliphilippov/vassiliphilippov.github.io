nonturbulent

adjective
not turbulent; having smooth flow
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtɜrbjələnt / (AmE), / nɒnˈtɜːbjʊlənt / (BrE)
(of a fluid) characterized by smooth, orderly flow without turbulence or disturbance.

Examples

nonturbulent air flow
Aircraft wings are engineered to minimize nonturbulent air movements during flight.
In laboratory experiments, researchers often prefer nonturbulent liquids for accurate measurements.
The pipe's design ensures a nonturbulent flow of water under normal conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – turbulent: characterized by disorder or irregular motion (especially of fluid)
