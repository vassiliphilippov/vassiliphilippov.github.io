PAYE

noun
British withholding tax system
Pronunciation: / pi eɪ waɪ i / (AmE), / piː eɪ waɪ iː / (BrE)
(in the UK) a system of collecting income tax directly from employees' wages or salaries by deduction before they are paid.

Examples

PAYE tax system
All employees in the UK are subject to PAYE on their earnings.
Many British workers have never known a system other than PAYE for their income tax.
Under the PAYE system, tax is deducted before you receive your paycheck.
