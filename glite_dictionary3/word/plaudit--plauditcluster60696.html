plaudit

noun
enthusiastic praise or approval
Pronunciation: / ˈplɔːdɪt / (AmE), / ˈplɔːdɪt / (BrE)
an expression of strong praise or approval; enthusiastic commendation

Examples

plaudits from critics
Despite initial skepticism, his invention eventually won him plaudits from the scientific community.
Her performance drew plaudits from the entire audience.
The director received plaudits from critics for her innovative film.
