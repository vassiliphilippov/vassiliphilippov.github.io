widower

noun
man whose wife has died
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪdoʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɪdəʊə / (BrE)
A man whose wife has died, especially one who has not remarried.

Examples

a widower lives alone
After his wife passed away last year, Mark became a widower.
He has lived alone as a widower for over a decade.
The support group welcomes both widowers and widows.
