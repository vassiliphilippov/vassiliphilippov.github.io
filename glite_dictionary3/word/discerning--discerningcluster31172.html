discerning

adjective
showing insight and good judgment
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈsɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
having or showing good judgment and the ability to notice and understand things, especially subtle details or differences

Examples

discerning eye for detail

She is a discerning reader who can recognize literary quality in any genre.
