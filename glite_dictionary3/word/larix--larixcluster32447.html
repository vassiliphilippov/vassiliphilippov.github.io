Larix

noun
genus of larch trees
Pronunciation: / ˈlær.ɪks / (AmE), / ˈlær.ɪks / (BrE)
A genus of deciduous coniferous trees known as larches, native to cooler regions of the Northern Hemisphere, with needle-like leaves that turn yellow and fall in autumn.
