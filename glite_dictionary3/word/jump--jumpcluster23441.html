to jump

verb
move or start suddenly in surprise or alarm
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp / (AmE), / dʒʌmp / (BrE)

Examples

to jump in surprise
He couldn't help but jump when the phone rang unexpectedly.
She jumped at the sudden bark of the dog nearby.
The children jumped in their seats when the fireworks went off.
