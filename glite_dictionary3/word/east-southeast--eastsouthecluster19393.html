east-southeast

noun
compass point between east and southeast
Pronunciation: / ist saʊθˈist / (AmE), / iːst saʊθˈiːst / (BrE)
the direction or compass point midway between east and southeast, corresponding to a 112.5° bearing from north

Examples

east-southeast direction
Mountains rise to the east-southeast of the valley, forming a natural barrier.
The ship changed course and headed east-southeast to avoid the storm.
They traveled east-southeast for several hours before reaching the coast.
