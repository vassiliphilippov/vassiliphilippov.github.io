impertinent

adjective
irrelevant; not pertinent
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɜrtənənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːtɪnənt / (BrE)
Not relevant or pertinent to the matter under consideration; irrelevant.

Examples

impertinent question in class
He brought up several impertinent details that had nothing to do with the discussion.
Most of the questions in the survey were impertinent to our actual concerns.
Please avoid making impertinent comments during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – irrelevant: not related to the matter at hand
  • antonym – pertinent: relevant to the matter under consideration
