inauguration

noun
beginning of a new system or operation
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɔɡjəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌnɔːɡjʊˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of beginning or introducing a new system, policy, or operation

Examples

inauguration of a new system
Management delayed the inauguration of the updated software platform until testing was complete.
The inauguration of the new recycling program brought excitement to the city council.
The hospital's inauguration of night-time emergency services greatly improved patient care.
