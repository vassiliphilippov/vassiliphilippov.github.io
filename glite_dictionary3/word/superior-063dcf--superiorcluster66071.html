superior

adjective
written above and to one side (typography)
Pronunciation: / suˈpɪriər / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
(of a written or printed character) placed above and to one side of another character.

Examples

superior letter
Copyright symbols sometimes include a superior letter to indicate year or owner.
Mathematical exponents are often written in superior form.
The number 2 in H₂O is typically printed as a superior figure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – superscript: a letter, figure, or symbol written or printed above the line
