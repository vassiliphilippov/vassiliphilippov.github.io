ungratifying

adjective
not giving satisfaction or reward
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡræt̬ɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡrætɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
not giving satisfaction, pleasure, or a sense of reward; unrewarding or unsatisfying.

Examples

ungratifying work experience
Despite his efforts, the job proved ungratifying and demoralizing over time.
She found the repetitive paperwork to be thoroughly ungratifying.
Volunteering at the shelter was sometimes hard, but it was never ungratifying.

Related Terms

  • antonym – gratifying: giving pleasure or satisfaction
  • synonym – thankless: not likely to be rewarded or appreciated
  • synonym – unsatisfying: not satisfying or fulfilling
