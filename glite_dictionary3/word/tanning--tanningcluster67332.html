tanning

noun
darkening of skin by ultraviolet light
Pronunciation: / ˈtænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtanɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which skin becomes darker in color after exposure to ultraviolet light, especially sunlight

Examples

sun tanning at beach
Prolonged tanning without protection can increase the risk of skin cancer.
She spent the afternoon at the beach, enjoying the tanning process.
They warned about the dangers of artificial tanning in salons.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bleaching: the process of making skin lighter in color
  • degree higher – sunburn: reddening of the skin caused by overexposure to sunlight
