Limicolae

noun
group of migratory shorebirds (taxonomic term)
Pronunciation: / lɪˈmɪkəˌli / (AmE), / lɪˈmɪkəˌli / (BrE)
a former taxonomic group used for migratory shorebirds, especially those now placed in the Charadrii
