arboreal

adjective
tree-like in form or structure
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈbɔriəl / (AmE), / ɑːˈbɔːrɪəl / (BrE)
having the form or branching structure of a tree; tree-like in appearance.

Examples

arboreal pattern
Geologists noted the arboreal patterns of mineral growth within the rock cavity.
Some sponges have an arboreal appearance, spreading branches like the limbs of a tree.
The arboreal structure of the coral made it resemble a miniature underwater forest.
