indiscretion

noun
lack of good judgment; mistake
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndəˈskrɛʃən /; / ˌɪndɪˈskrɛʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈskrɛʃən / (BrE)
a minor mistake or act showing a lack of good judgment or care, especially in speech or behavior
