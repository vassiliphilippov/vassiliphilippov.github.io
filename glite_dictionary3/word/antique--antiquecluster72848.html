antique

adjective
old and valuable due to age
Pronunciation: / ænˈtik / (AmE), / ænˈtiːk / (BrE)
(of an object) made in an earlier period and valued for its age, quality, or historical significance; very old or ancient

Examples

antique furniture piece
She collected antique jewelry that once belonged to her grandmother.
The museum displayed several antique clocks from the 18th century.
They restored their farmhouse with antique wooden beams and fixtures.
Many people collect antique coins because of their historical value.
The book was an antique volume, its pages yellowed with age.
The castle is filled with antique furniture dating back several centuries.
