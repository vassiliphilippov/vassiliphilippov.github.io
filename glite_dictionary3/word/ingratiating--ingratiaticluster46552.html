ingratiating

adjective
trying to please to gain favor
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡreɪʃiˌeɪtɪŋ /; / ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡreɪʃɪeɪtɪŋ /; / ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
behaving or acting in a way to gain someone's favor or approval, often in an insincere or overly eager manner
