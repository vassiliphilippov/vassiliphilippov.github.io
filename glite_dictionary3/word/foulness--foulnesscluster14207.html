foulness

noun
bad or unpleasant weather
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfaʊlnɪs / (BrE)
Very unpleasant or bad weather conditions.

Examples

foulness of the weather
Despite the foulness of last night's weather, the festival continued as planned.
The foulness of the storm kept everyone indoors for the entire day.
We trudged home through the foulness of wind and rain, soaked to the skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fairness: pleasant, mild weather conditions
  • synonym – nastiness: the unpleasantness or disagreeableness, especially of weather
