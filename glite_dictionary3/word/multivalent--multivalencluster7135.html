multivalent

adjective
association of multiple chromosomes in meiosis
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌl.tɪˈveɪ.lənt / (AmE), / ˌmʌl.tɪˈveɪ.lənt / (BrE)
(of chromosomes) involving the association of three or more homologous chromosomes during the first division of meiosis.

Examples

multivalent chromosomes pair
In certain plant species, multivalent chromosomes are observed during meiosis.
Researchers noted the presence of multivalent structures in the hybrid cells under microscopic examination.
The formation of multivalent associations can lead to complex genetic inheritance patterns.
