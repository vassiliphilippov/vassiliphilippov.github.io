venire

noun
jury pool; legal group for jury selection
Pronunciation: / vəˈnaɪri / (AmE), / vɪˈnɪəri / (BrE)
(Law) A group of people summoned to appear in court from whom a jury will be selected.

Examples

venire of jurors
Members of the venire waited in the courthouse lobby until their names were called.
Only a portion of the original venire was needed to form the final jury panel.
The judge instructed the venire about their responsibilities before jury selection began.
