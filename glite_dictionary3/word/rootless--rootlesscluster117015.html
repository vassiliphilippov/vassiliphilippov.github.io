rootless

adjective
without permanent home or ties
Pronunciation: / ˈrutləs / (AmE), / ˈruːtləs / (BrE)
having no permanent home, ties, or connection to a place or community; wandering or unsettled

Examples

a rootless traveler
After moving from city to city for years, she often felt rootless and alone.
He wrote a novel about a rootless traveler searching for meaning in life.
The rootless youth drifted from job to job without settling down.

Related Terms

  • antonym – settled: having a fixed home or established ties to a community
  • synonym – vagabond: wandering from place to place without a home or job
