untrodden

adjective
not walked on or traversed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtrɑd(ə)n / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrɒd(ə)n / (BrE)
(of land or a place) not having been walked on; never having been traversed or marked by footsteps.

Examples

untrodden path
She dreamed of exploring untrodden paths and discovering secret places.
The hikers ventured deep into the untrodden regions of the forest.
The snow lay thick and untrodden across the open field.

Related Terms

  • synonym – trackless: (of places) having no trails or paths
