Milton Friedman

proper noun
American economist, monetarism advocate
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪltən ˈfriːdmən / (AmE), / ˈmɪltən ˈfriːdmən / (BrE)
(1912–2006) A prominent American economist known for his advocacy of monetarism and opposition to government intervention in the economy.
