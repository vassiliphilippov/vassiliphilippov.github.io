Ruhollah Khomeini

proper noun
Iranian religious and political leader, led 1979 revolution
Pronunciation: / ruːˈhoʊlɑː xoʊˈmeɪni / (AmE), / ruːˈhɒlɑː xɒˈmeɪni / (BrE)
