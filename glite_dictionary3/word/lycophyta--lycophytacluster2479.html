Lycophyta

noun
division of vascular plants, including club mosses
Pronunciation: / laɪˈkɒf.ɪ.tə / (AmE), / lɪˈkɒf.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
a division or group of ancient vascular plants that includes the club mosses and their relatives, distinguished by small leaves and spore production

Examples

Lycophyta group of plants
Leaves of the Lycophyta are typically small and scale-like compared to those of true mosses.
Modern club mosses are classified within the Lycophyta division.
The Lycophyta were once dominant land plants during the Carboniferous period.
