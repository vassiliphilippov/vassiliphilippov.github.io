to decrescendo

verb
grow gradually quieter (music, verb)
Pronunciation: / ˌdi.krəˈʃɛn.doʊ / (AmE), / ˌdiː.krɪˈʃɛn.dəʊ / (BrE)
(of sound, especially in music) to gradually become quieter.

Examples

to decrescendo slowly
After the dramatic climax, the music slowly decrescendoed to a whisper.
As the storm passed, the thunder decrescendoed into gentle rumbles.
The choir decrescendoed beautifully at the end of the hymn.
