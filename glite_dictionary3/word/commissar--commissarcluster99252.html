commissar

noun
Communist Party political officer
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.mɪ.sɑr /; / ˈkɑːmɪˌsɑːr / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ɪ.sɑː /; / ˈkɒmɪsɑː / (BrE)
an official in a Communist government, especially in the former Soviet Union, responsible for political education, control, or enforcing party policy
