closure

noun
the act of closing down an institution or operation
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊʒər / (AmE), / ˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
the act or process of closing down an institution, business, or operation, especially permanently

Examples

closure of a business
After the school’s closure, students had to find places at other institutions.
The sudden closure of the factory affected hundreds of workers in the town.
They blamed the closure of local shops on rising rent and declining sales.

Related Terms

  • antonym – opening: the act of starting or resuming operations of an institution or business
  • synonym – shutdown: the process of ending operations, especially of a business or facility
