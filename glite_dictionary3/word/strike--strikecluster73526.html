strike

noun
all pins down in bowling with first ball
Pronunciation: / straɪk / (AmE), / straɪk / (BrE)
In tenpin bowling, an act of knocking down all ten pins with the first ball of a frame.

Examples

a strike in bowling
Getting a strike is the best possible outcome for each frame in bowling.
He finished the game with three strikes in the final frame.
She cheered when she rolled her first strike during the tournament.

Related Terms

  • antonym – spare: when all ten pins are knocked down in two tries in a frame, in bowling
