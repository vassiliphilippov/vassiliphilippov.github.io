Procavia capensis

noun
species of hyrax (rock hyrax)
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkeɪ.vi.ə kəˈpɛn.sɪs / (AmE), / prəˈkeɪ.vɪə kəˈpen.sɪs / (BrE)
