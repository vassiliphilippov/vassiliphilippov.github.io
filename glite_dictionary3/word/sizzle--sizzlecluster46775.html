sizzle

noun
hissing or crackling sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪzəl / (BrE)
a hissing or crackling sound, especially as of something frying or burning

Examples

the sizzle of bacon
The sizzle died down as the pan cooled.
The sizzle of bacon filled the kitchen every morning.
You could hear the sizzle from the hot grill across the patio.
