vapours

noun
state of depression (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪpərz / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəz / (BrE)
(Archaic) A depressed or melancholic emotional state; low spirits.

Examples

suffer vapours after loss
He was overcome by the vapours that lingered after his loss, barely speaking for days.
In old novels, ladies often suffered from the vapours and withdrew to their chambers.
Medical texts of the eighteenth century sometimes connected the vapours to imbalances in the body's humours.

  • synonym – blues: a feeling of sadness or depression
  • antonym – elation: joyful and proud; an uplifting feeling
  • synonym – melancholy: a feeling of deep sadness; depression
