Amorphophallus titanum

noun
tropical plant with large, smelly bloom
Pronunciation: / əˌmɔr.foʊˈfæl.əs taɪˈteɪ.nəm / (AmE), / əˌmɔː.fəˈfæl.əs tɪˈtɑː.nəm / (BrE)
A large, foul-smelling tropical plant native to Sumatra, known for producing one of the world's largest flowering structures and emitting a strong odor resembling rotting flesh.
