breakneck

adjective
dangerously fast; extremely rapid
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌnɛk / (BrE)
(especially of speed or movement) dangerously or extremely fast.

Examples

breakneck speed
The car raced down the highway at a breakneck speed, barely making the curve.
The cyclists descended the mountain at breakneck velocity, thrilling the crowd.
They completed the work at a breakneck pace to meet the tight deadline.
