to give

verb
to sacrifice one's life or loved ones
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv / (AmE), / ɡɪv / (BrE)
to suffer the loss of (particularly one's life or loved ones) for a cause or purpose

Examples

give his life
He gave his own life to save the villagers from the flood.
Many soldiers gave their lives in defense of their country.
She gave her son to the cause and never saw him again.
