confidentially

adverb
in a private or secret way
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.fɪˈdɛn.ʃə.li / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.fɪˈden.ʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that is meant to be private or secret, intended not to be shared with others

Examples

speak confidentially

The information was shared confidentially with the board members only.
