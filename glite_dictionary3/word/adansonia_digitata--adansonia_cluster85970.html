Adansonia digitata

noun
African baobab tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / əˌdænˈsoʊniə ˌdɪdʒɪˈteɪtə / (AmE), / əˌdanˈsəʊnɪə ˌdɪdʒɪˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
An African tree with a very thick trunk, known as the baobab, which produces a gourd-like fruit with edible pulp called monkey bread.
