to rat

verb
make hair fuller with a pad or roll
Pronunciation: / ræt / (AmE), / ræt / (BrE)
(of hair) to make look fuller by padding it with a roll or pad, or to use such a pad when arranging the hair.

Examples

to rat hair for volume
Many women in the 1950s would rat their hair for a bouffant look.
She learned how to rat her hair for special occasions from her grandmother.
To create the vintage style, she ratted her hair with a small pad to add volume.
