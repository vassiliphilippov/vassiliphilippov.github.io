theist

adjective
relating to belief in a god or gods
Pronunciation: / ˈθiɪst / (AmE), / ˈθiːɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of belief in the existence of a god or gods, especially as part of a religious doctrine.

Examples

theist beliefs
Certain theist traditions emphasize the personal nature of the divine.
His theist arguments were met with thoughtful responses from both believers and skeptics.
The debate was influenced by several theist perspectives on the origin of the universe.

Related Terms

  • antonym – atheist: not believing in the existence of any god or gods
  • synonym – deist: believing in a god who does not intervene in the universe
