history

noun
record or account of past events
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
an account or narrative of past events, especially those connected with a particular person, group, or period

Examples

history of a person
His lecture included the history of the city's famous landmarks.
She published a comprehensive history of the American Civil War.
The museum displays a detailed history of the local industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chronicle: a factual written account of important or historical events in the order of their occurrence
  • antonym – future: the time yet to come; events that will happen
  • synonym – story: a narrative, either true or fictitious, of past events
