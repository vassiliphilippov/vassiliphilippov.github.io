gauntry

noun
overhead supporting framework
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæntri / (AmE), / ˈɡæntri / (BrE)
a framework of steel bars supported on side structures, used to bridge over or around something, such as supporting railway signals or a traveling crane

Examples

steel gauntry structure
A row of lights was mounted on a massive gauntry above the railway tracks.
The engineer carefully inspected the gauntry before the crane began its operation.
Workers reinforced the old gauntry to support the new transmission cables.
