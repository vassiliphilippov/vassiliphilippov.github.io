liquid

adjective
in a state that flows easily; not solid or gas
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd / (BrE)
existing as or having the characteristics of a liquid; especially tending to flow rather than be solid or gaseous

Examples

liquid state
Ice cream melts and becomes liquid on a hot day.
Mercury is unique among metals because it is liquid at room temperature.
The medicine comes in liquid form and should be measured carefully.
