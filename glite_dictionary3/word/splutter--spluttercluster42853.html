splutter

noun
spitting or popping-sounding utterance
Pronunciation: / ˈsplʌtɚ / (AmE), / ˈsplʌtə / (BrE)
an utterance produced with spitting or popping sounds, especially when speaking angrily or in a flustered way

Examples

angry splutter
His angry splutter made everyone in the room fall silent.
The teacher was met with a confused splutter from the back of the class.
With a splutter of indignation, she tried to defend herself.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spit: to eject saliva forcibly from one's mouth
  • synonym – stammer: speak with involuntary pauses or repetitions
