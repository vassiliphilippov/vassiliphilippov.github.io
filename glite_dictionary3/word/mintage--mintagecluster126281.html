mintage

noun
number/process of creating digital tokens
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪn.tɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪn.tɪdʒ / (BrE)
the total number produced, or the process of creating, digital tokens or non-fungible tokens (NFTs) on a blockchain.
