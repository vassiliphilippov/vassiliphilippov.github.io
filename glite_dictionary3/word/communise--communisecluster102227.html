to communise

verb
make or become Communist
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmjəˌnaɪz /; / ˈkɑː.mjəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɒmjʊnaɪz /; / ˈkɒm.juː.naɪz / (BrE)
to make something follow Communist principles, especially by making it collectively or state owned
