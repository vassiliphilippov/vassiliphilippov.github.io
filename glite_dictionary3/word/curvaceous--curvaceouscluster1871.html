curvaceous

adjective
having an attractively curved body
Pronunciation: / kɜrˈveɪʃəs / (AmE), / kɜːˈveɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of a woman's body) having an attractively rounded shape with pronounced curves.

Examples

curvaceous figure
In the painting, the artist celebrated the beauty of a curvaceous goddess.
Many 1950s fashion models were admired for their curvaceous silhouettes.
The actress is known for her curvaceous figure, which she often emphasizes on the red carpet.
