sensualism

noun
the pursuit of sensual pleasures
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tʃu.əˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tjʊ.əˌlɪz.əm / (BrE)
the pursuit of or devotion to sensual pleasures; the doctrine that pleasure or sensations are the highest good

Examples

life of sensualism
He was often criticized for his sensualism and indulgence in worldly pleasures.
Her writing explores the tension between spiritual longing and sensualism.
Some philosophers have debated the merits of sensualism versus asceticism as a way of life.

Related Terms

  • antonym – asceticism: the practice of self-discipline and abstention from indulgence
  • synonym – hedonism: the pursuit of pleasure as the highest good
