Sauropterygia

noun
extinct order of marine reptiles
Pronunciation: / ˌsɔːroʊpˌtɛˈrɪdʒiə /; / sɔːroʊptəˈrɪdʒiə / (AmE), / ˌsɔːrəpˈtɛrɪdʒɪə /; / sɔːrəʊptəˈrɪdʒiə / (BrE)
an extinct order of marine reptiles (including plesiosaurs and nothosaurs) known for their long necks and flipper- or paddle-like limbs, adapted for life in water
