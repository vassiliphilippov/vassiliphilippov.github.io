to recycle

verb
process materials for reuse
Pronunciation: / riˈsaɪkl̩ / (AmE), / riˈsaɪkl̩ / (BrE)
to process used materials or objects so that they can be used again

Examples

to recycle plastic bottles
After the event, volunteers recycled all the empty water bottles.
Many cities encourage residents to recycle paper, plastic, and glass.
The company recycles old computers to reduce electronic waste.

Related Terms

  • antonym – discard: to throw something away
  • synonym – reuse: to use something again instead of throwing it away
