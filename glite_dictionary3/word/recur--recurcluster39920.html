to recur

verb
happen again; repeat
Pronunciation: / rəˈkɝː / (AmE), / rɪˈkɜː / (BrE)
to happen or occur again, especially at intervals or repeatedly

Examples

recur every year
Certain dreams tend to recur throughout my life.
If the pain recurs, please contact your doctor immediately.
The same issue recurred several times over the next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – occur: to happen; to take place
  • synonym – repeat: to do or occur again; to happen more than once
