freelance

adjective
self-employed; working independently
Pronunciation: / ˈfriˌlæns /; / ˈfriːˌlæns / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌlɑːns /; / ˈfriː.lɑːns / (BrE)
working for different organizations or clients on separate projects, rather than being permanently employed by one
