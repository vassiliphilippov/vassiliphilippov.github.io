dripless

adjective
not allowing drips or leaks
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪp.ləs / (AmE), / ˈdrɪp.ləs / (BrE)
Designed or constructed so as not to let drops of liquid fall or leak.

Examples

dripless candle
He prefers dripless candles because they don't leave wax stains on the table.
She installed a dripless faucet in the kitchen to reduce water waste.
The company launched a new dripless paint can that keeps your workspace cleaner.
