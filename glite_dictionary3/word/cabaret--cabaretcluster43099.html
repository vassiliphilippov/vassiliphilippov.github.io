cabaret

noun
entertainment venue with music and food
Pronunciation: / ˌkæbəˈreɪ /; / ˈkæbəˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈkæbəreɪ / (BrE)
a restaurant or nightclub where people watch live entertainment (such as music, dance, or comedy) while eating or drinking; or the entertainment performed there
