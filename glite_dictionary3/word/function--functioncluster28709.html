function

noun
mathematical relation with single-valued outputs
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
(Mathematics, science, or computing) a relationship or process where one thing depends on another, or a named part of a computer program that performs a specific task
