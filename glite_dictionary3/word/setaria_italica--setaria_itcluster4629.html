Setaria italica

noun
species of millet (foxtail millet)
Pronunciation: / səˈtɛəriə ɪˈtælɪkə / (AmE), / sɛˈtɑːrɪə ɪˈtælɪkə / (BrE)
A coarse, drought-resistant annual grass cultivated for its grain, hay, and forage, known as foxtail millet, grown mainly in Europe and Asia.
