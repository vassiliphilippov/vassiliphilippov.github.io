to swank

verb
show off ostentatiously
Pronunciation: / swæŋk / (AmE), / swæŋk / (BrE)
to display oneself or one's possessions in an ostentatious or pretentious way; to show off

Examples

to swank around town
He loves to swank around town in his luxury car.
She swanked her designer handbag at every opportunity.
They couldn't resist swanking about their recent holiday to Bali.
