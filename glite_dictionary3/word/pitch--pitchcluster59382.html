to pitch

verb
erect and fasten, as a tent
Pronunciation: / pɪtʃ / (AmE), / pɪtʃ / (BrE)
to erect and fasten something, especially a tent or other temporary structure

Examples

to pitch a tent
He pitched his tent near the river for the night.
They are pitching a large marquee for the wedding reception.
We need to pitch the tent before it gets dark.
