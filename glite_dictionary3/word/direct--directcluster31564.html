direct

adjective
having a proportional relationship (math/science)
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkt, daɪˈrɛkt /; / dɪˈrɛkt, daɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkt, daɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
used in technical or scientific contexts: (of electric current) flowing in one direction; (of celestial bodies) moving in a certain way; (of relationships) direct descent; (of speech) using exact words; (of quantities) changing together in the same direction
