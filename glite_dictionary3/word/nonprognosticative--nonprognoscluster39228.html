nonprognosticative

adjective
not predictive of the future
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.prɑɡˌnɑstəˈkeɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.prɒɡˌnɒstɪˈkeɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
Not offering or providing predictions about the future; not prognosticative.

Examples

nonprognosticative statement
A nonprognosticative statement simply describes the present situation without hinting at possible outcomes.
Her remarks remained nonprognosticative, focusing only on current issues without offering any predictions.
The report was deliberately nonprognosticative, avoiding any forecasts about market trends.
