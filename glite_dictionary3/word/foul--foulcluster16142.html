foul

adjective
not hit between foul lines (baseball)
Pronunciation: / faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
(of a baseball) not hit between the foul lines; landing or traveling outside the defined area of fair play.

Examples

foul ball
That pitch was clearly foul, landing outside the left field line.
The batter hit a foul ball deep into the stands.
The umpire called the play foul after examining where the ball landed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fair: (in baseball) hit between the foul lines; opposite of foul
