pirana

noun
South American carnivorous freshwater fish
Pronunciation: / pɪˈrɑːnə / (AmE), / pɪˈrɑːnə / (BrE)
A small, voracious carnivorous freshwater fish of South America that is known for attacking and consuming living animals.
