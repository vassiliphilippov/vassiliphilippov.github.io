matting

noun
border or background for a picture
Pronunciation: / ˈmætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmætɪŋ / (BrE)
material or a layer used as a border or background for a picture, especially in framing to provide contrast or protection.

Examples

matting for a picture frame
Custom matting can make artwork look more professional and preserve it longer.
He chose a black wood frame and cream matting for his diploma.
The gallery used a white matting to highlight the photograph’s colors.
