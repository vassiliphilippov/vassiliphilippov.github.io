atopognosis

noun
inability to localize touch (medical)
Pronunciation: / əˌtoʊpɑɡˈnoʊsɪs / (AmE), / əˌtəʊpɒɡˈnəʊsɪs / (BrE)
the inability to correctly identify the location on the body where a touch stimulus is applied, due to neurological impairment

Examples

atopognosis after brain injury
