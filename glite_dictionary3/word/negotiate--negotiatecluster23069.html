to negotiate

verb
agree on protocol/security parameters
Pronunciation: / nəˈɡoʊʃieɪt / (AmE), / nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt / (BrE)
(computing, networking, security) to automatically agree on shared protocol, configuration, or security parameters between systems during a handshake or connection setup.

Examples

to negotiate a connection
During the TLS handshake, the client and server negotiate a cipher suite and compression method.
The browser negotiated TLS 1.3 with the site, allowing faster secure connections than before.
Automated scripts are often negotiating fallback algorithms when the preferred protocols are unsupported.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agree: to reach the same decision or arrangement; to have the same opinion
  • synonym – handshake: a process by which two systems exchange information to establish a connection and parameters
