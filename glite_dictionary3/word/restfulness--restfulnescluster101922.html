restfulness

noun
the quality of being restful and calm
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛstfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈrɛstfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being restful; a feeling of peace and relaxation

Examples

deep restfulness
He decorated his room to enhance its restfulness after a long day at work.
She enjoyed the restfulness of the quiet countryside every summer.
The restfulness of the afternoon was broken only by the sound of birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – calmness: the state of being calm and peaceful
  • antonym – restlessness: the quality or state of being unable to rest or relax
