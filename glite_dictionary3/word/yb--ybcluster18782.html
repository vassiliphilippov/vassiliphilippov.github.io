YB

noun
abbreviation for yottabyte (10^24 bytes)
Pronunciation: / waɪ bi / (AmE), / waɪ bi / (BrE)
an abbreviation for yottabyte, a unit of digital information equal to one septillion (10^24) bytes or 1000 zettabytes.

Examples

1 YB of data
A petabyte is much smaller than a YB, which is almost unimaginably large.
Cloud providers are not yet offering services that can handle a YB of storage.
Storing a YB of data would require an enormous infrastructure.
