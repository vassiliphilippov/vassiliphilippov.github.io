whitehead

noun
small whitish acne bump
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌhɛd / (BrE)
a small, whitish bump on the skin caused by a blocked sebaceous gland, typically a type of acne lesion

Examples

a whitehead on skin
After using the new cleanser, the number of whiteheads on her forehead decreased.
Dermatologists often advise against squeezing a whitehead to avoid scarring.
He noticed a whitehead forming on his chin before the party.
