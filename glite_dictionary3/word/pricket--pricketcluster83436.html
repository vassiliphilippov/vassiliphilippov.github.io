pricket

noun
spike for holding a candle
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɪkɪt / (BrE)
a sharp metal spike or holder on which a candle is placed, typically used in candlesticks or candelabra.

Examples

pricket candle holder
A single taper burned on a pricket on the mantelpiece, casting long shadows across the room.
She polished the brass pricket until it gleamed in the candlelight.
The ancient church was lit by candles held upright on a sturdy pricket.
