antiferromagnetism

noun
magnetic arrangement with opposing spins
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.fɛr.oʊˈmæɡ.nəˌtɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌanti.fɛrəʊˈmaɡ.nɪ.tɪ.zəm / (BrE)
A phenomenon in which the magnetic moments of atoms or ions in a material are aligned in parallel but opposite directions, resulting in no net magnetization.
