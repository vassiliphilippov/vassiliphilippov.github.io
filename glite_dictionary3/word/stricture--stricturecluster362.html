stricture

noun
abnormal narrowing of a body passage
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈstrɪktʃə / (BrE)
an abnormal narrowing of a bodily canal or passageway, often due to disease or injury

Examples

esophageal stricture
A urethral stricture may cause urinary retention and discomfort.
Surgery was required to treat the intestinal stricture that was causing a blockage.
The patient was diagnosed with a stricture of the esophagus, making swallowing difficult.
