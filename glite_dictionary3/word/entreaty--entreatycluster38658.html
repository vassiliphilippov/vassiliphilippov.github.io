entreaty

noun
earnest or urgent request
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtriːti / (AmE), / ɪnˈtriːti / (BrE)
an earnest or urgent request, often made with deep emotion or seriousness

Examples

make an entreaty
Despite his desperate entreaty, the council refused to reopen the case.
She made an entreaty for understanding, hoping her friends would forgive her mistake.
Their entreaties for help were met with silence from the authorities.
