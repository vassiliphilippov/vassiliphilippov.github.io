Micmac

noun
member of a Canadian indigenous people
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪkˌmæk / (AmE), / ˈmɪk.mæk / (BrE)
A member of an Algonquian-speaking indigenous people native to the Maritime Provinces of Canada.

Examples

a Micmac person
A delegation of Micmac leaders attended the national conference on indigenous rights.
He is proud of his Micmac heritage and participates in community events.
