to immobilize

verb
(finance) convert assets to fixed capital
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz /; / ɪˈmoʊ.bəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊbəlaɪz /; / ɪˈməʊ.bɪ.laɪz /; / ɪˈməʊbɪˌlaɪz / (BrE)
(Finance/Economics) to make assets or capital unavailable, fixed, or inactive, so they cannot be used or transferred
