to perspire

verb
excrete sweat through the skin
Pronunciation: / pərˈspaɪr / (AmE), / pəˈspaɪə / (BrE)
to excrete sweat through the pores of the skin, especially as a result of heat, physical exertion, or stress

Examples

to perspire during exercise
After running for just a few minutes, I began to perspire heavily.
It was so hot outside that everyone started to perspire during the walk.
Many people perspire more when they are nervous or anxious.
