acceptance

noun
agreement to the terms of an offer (law)
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptəns / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptəns / (BrE)
(in contract law) Consent to the terms of an offer, usually by words or conduct, creating a binding contract.

Examples

A verbal acceptance is sometimes sufficient to finalize an agreement.
