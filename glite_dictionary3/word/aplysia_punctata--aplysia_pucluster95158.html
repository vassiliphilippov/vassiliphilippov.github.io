Aplysia punctata

noun
a species of sea hare (marine gastropod)
Pronunciation: / əˈplɪziə pʌŋkˈteɪtə / (AmE), / əˈplɪzɪə pʌŋkˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
A species of naked marine gastropod, also known as the sea hare, with a soft body, reduced internal shell, and two pairs of ear-like tentacles.
