wheel

noun
circular rotating part in vehicles or machines
Pronunciation: / wil / (AmE), / wiːl / (BrE)
A circular object that rotates on an axle and is used as a simple machine, typically forming part of vehicles and machinery to enable movement or facilitate work.

Examples

bicycle wheel
A bicycle cannot move properly if a wheel is bent or damaged.
The wheel was one of the most important inventions in human history.
The mechanic replaced the broken wheel on the cart before it could be used again.
