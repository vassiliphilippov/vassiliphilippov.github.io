Felis silvestris

noun
European wildcat species
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːlɪs sɪlˈvɛstrɪs / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪs sɪlˈvɛstrɪs / (BrE)
A bushy-tailed wildcat native to Europe, resembling the domestic cat, and considered the ancestor of the domestic cat.

Examples

Felis silvestris wildcat
