milled

adjective
shaped by milling machine
Pronunciation: / mɪld / (AmE), / mɪld / (BrE)
Shaped, cut, or finished by a milling machine or by a milling process (in manufacturing, metalwork, or woodworking).

Examples

milled metal parts
The engine components were milled from solid aluminum to meet strict dimensional tolerances.
The coin's edge was milled to prevent clipping and give it a textured finish.
She ordered a milled oak tabletop because she wanted precise, machine-cut grooves along its edges.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cut: removed or shaped by a cutting tool; a general term for material removal processes including milling.
  • synonym – machined: worked or shaped using machine tools; often used interchangeably with 'milled' in manufacturing.
