gassing

noun
deliberate poisoning with gas
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡasɪŋ / (BrE)
the deliberate act of poisoning a person or animal with gas, especially as a method of execution or chemical attack

Examples

gassing prisoners
The prisoners were victims of gassing during the war.
The report documented cases of gassing as a form of illegal execution.
There are strict international laws against the gassing of civilians.

  • synonym – poisoning: the act of causing harm or death by a toxic substance
