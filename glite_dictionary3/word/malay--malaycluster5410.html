Malay

noun
a member of the Malay ethnic group of Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪ / (AmE), / məˈleɪ / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group native to the northern Malay Peninsula, Malaysia, and parts of the western Malay Archipelago

Examples

a Malay person
Many Malays celebrate Hari Raya Aidilfitri together with their families.
She is a Malay from Penang, Malaysia.
The Malay people have a rich tradition of music and dance.
