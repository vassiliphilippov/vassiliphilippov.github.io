counsel

noun
advice or guidance
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.səl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl / (BrE)
advice or guidance given to someone, especially regarding a decision or future course of action.

Examples

wise counsel
She always seeks her father's counsel in difficult times.
The committee offered their counsel on the proposed changes.
Without wise counsel, he might have made a poor decision.
