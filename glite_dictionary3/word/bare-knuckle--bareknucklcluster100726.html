bare-knuckle

adjective
tough, unrestrained, rule-breaking
Pronunciation: / bɛr ˈnʌkəl / (AmE), / beə ˈnʌkl̩ / (BrE)
(of an activity or behavior) tough, unrestrained, and without regard for rules or conventions; conducted in a fiercely competitive or uncompromising way.

Examples

bare-knuckle business tactics
He built his empire through bare-knuckle business tactics that ignored traditional regulations.
The campaign took on a bare-knuckle tone as accusations and insults began to fly.
The debate turned into a bare-knuckle confrontation, with both sides refusing to compromise.
