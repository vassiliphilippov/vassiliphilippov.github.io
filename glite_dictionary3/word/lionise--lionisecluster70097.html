to lionise

verb
treat as a celebrity
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪ.əˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.ə.naɪz / (BrE)
to treat (someone) as a celebrity or assign great social importance to them

Examples

to lionise a famous person
He was lionised by the local press for his charitable work.
Many critics lionised the writer after her debut novel was published.
The scientist was lionised at international conferences.
