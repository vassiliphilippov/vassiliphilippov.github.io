to plump

verb
drop or sit down heavily
Pronunciation: / plʌmp / (AmE), / plʌmp / (BrE)
to set or drop something or oneself down heavily or suddenly, often with a noise

Examples

to plump down
She plumped herself onto the couch, exhausted from the long day.
The child plumped his backpack on the floor and ran outside to play.
With a sigh, he plumped into the armchair and closed his eyes.
