epicurism

noun
pursuit of sensual pleasure and fine living
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpɪkjəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪkjʊərɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the pursuit and enjoyment of fine food, drink, and sensual pleasure, especially as a way of life; devotion to luxurious living and refined tastes

Examples

love of epicurism in food
He was sometimes criticized for his epicurism rather than his intellectual achievements.
Her love for gourmet cuisine and fine wine was a clear expression of epicurism.
The lavish parties were marked by an atmosphere of epicurism and indulgence.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hedonism: the pursuit of pleasure as the highest good
