dive

noun
jump or plunge into water
Pronunciation: / daɪv / (AmE), / daɪv / (BrE)
A headlong plunge into water, especially from a diving board or similar structure.

Examples

a dive from board
His first dive was hesitant, but he quickly gained confidence.
She executed a perfect dive from the ten-meter platform.
The competition was won with a flawless dive in the final round.
