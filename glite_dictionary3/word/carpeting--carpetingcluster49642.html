carpeting

noun
floor covering made of thick fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pə.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
A floor covering made from a thick, heavy fabric, usually with a nap or pile, used to cover large areas of a floor.

Examples

carpeting the living room
After the flood, the ruined carpeting had to be removed.
She chose a soft gray carpeting to match the walls in the living room.
The new apartment came with plush carpeting in every room.
