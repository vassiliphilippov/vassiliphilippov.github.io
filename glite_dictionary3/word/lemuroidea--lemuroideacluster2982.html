Lemuroidea

noun
superfamily of lemurs and related primates
Pronunciation: / lɛˌmjʊəˈrɔɪdiə / (AmE), / lɛˌmjʊəˈrɔɪdiə / (BrE)
A former superfamily in primate classification that includes lemurs and related species, sometimes used as an alternative to or subdivision of Prosimii.

Examples

Lemuroidea classification group
Some zoological texts list Lemuroidea as a superfamily within the primate order.
