Ras Tafari

proper noun
Ethiopian emperor and Rastafarian figure
Pronunciation: / rɑs təˈfɑri / (AmE), / ræs təˈfɑːri / (BrE)
The regnal name of Emperor Haile Selassie I of Ethiopia (1892–1975), regarded as a messianic figure by Rastafarians.

Examples

Ras Tafari became emperor
During his reign, Ras Tafari worked to modernize Ethiopia and resist foreign invasion.
Many Rastafarians believe that Ras Tafari is the messiah foretold in their scriptures.
The influence of Ras Tafari can still be seen in Ethiopian culture today.
