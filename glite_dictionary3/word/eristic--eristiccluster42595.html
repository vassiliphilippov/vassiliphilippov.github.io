eristic

adjective
disputatious; inclined to argue for argument's sake
Pronunciation: / ɛˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
Given to arguing or disputing for its own sake, often using specious or deceptive reasoning.
