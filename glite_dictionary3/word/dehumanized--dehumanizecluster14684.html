dehumanized

adjective
lacking human qualities or dignity
Pronunciation: / diˈhjuːmənˌaɪzd / (AmE), / diˈhjuːmənˌaɪzd / (BrE)
Deprived of human qualities, personality, or dignity.

Examples

dehumanized by harsh treatment
After years in prison, he felt completely dehumanized by the experience.
Some critics argue that modern warfare has dehumanized soldiers, turning them into mere cogs in a machine.
The film tells the story of people who are dehumanized by a totalitarian regime.
