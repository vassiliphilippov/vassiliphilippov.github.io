prioress

noun
the head of a nunnery
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪ.ər.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈpraɪə.rəs / (BrE)
the female head or superior of a priory or a community of nuns

Examples

the prioress of a convent
As prioress, she was responsible for the spiritual and administrative welfare of the nunnery.
The prioress welcomed the new postulants to the convent with a gentle smile.
The villagers often sought advice from the wise prioress of the nearby priory.
