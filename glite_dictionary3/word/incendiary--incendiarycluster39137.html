incendiary

noun
person who sets property on fire illegally
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛndiəri / (BrE)
A person who illegally sets fire to property; an arsonist.

Examples

an incendiary criminal
Firefighters quickly extinguished the blaze started by an incendiary in the old factory.
The authorities are searching for the incendiary responsible for the recent string of arson attacks.
The police arrested the incendiary who caused the warehouse fire.
