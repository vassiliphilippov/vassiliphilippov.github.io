agave

noun
a tropical plant with fibrous leaves
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɑːveɪ / (AmE), / əˈɡɑːviː / (BrE)
A tropical American plant with a rosette of large, fibrous, sword-shaped leaves and tall flower spikes, some species grown for ornament or for their fiber.

Examples

agave plant leaves
An agave can thrive in hot, dry climates with little water.
The blue agave is used to make tequila in Mexico.
We planted an agave in the garden as a decorative feature.
