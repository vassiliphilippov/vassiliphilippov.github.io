knitted

adjective
made by knitting, not weaving
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈnɪtɪd / (BrE)
made by forming fabric from yarn or thread using a series of connected loops, rather than by weaving

Examples

knitted sweater
Many people prefer knitted hats in winter because they are warm and comfortable.
She wore a beautiful knitted scarf that her grandmother had made.
The baby was wrapped in a soft, hand-knitted blanket.
