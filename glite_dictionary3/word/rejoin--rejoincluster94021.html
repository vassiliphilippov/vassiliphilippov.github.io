to rejoin

verb
to join again after separation
Pronunciation: / riˈdʒɔɪn / (AmE), / riˈdʒɔɪn / (BrE)
to join something or someone again after being separated.

Examples

to rejoin the group
After years abroad, she finally decided to rejoin her family.
He plans to rejoin the team after recovering from his injury.
The rivers rejoin downstream to form a single larger flow.
