to thread

verb
remove hair by twisting thread around it
Pronunciation: / θrɛd / (AmE), / θrɛd / (BrE)
to remove facial hair, especially from the eyebrows, by using a thin cotton or polyester thread that is twisted and pulled over the unwanted hairs to remove them from the follicle

Examples

to thread eyebrows
Many people prefer to thread their upper lips because it's less harsh than waxing.
She has her eyebrows threaded every month at the beauty salon.
The beautician expertly threaded the fine hairs above his eyebrows.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pluck: to remove hair or feathers by pulling them out
  • synonym – wax: to remove hair by applying and pulling off wax
