cancelled

adjective
publicly ostracized on social media
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.səld / (AmE), / ˈkæn.səld / (BrE)
(informal) publicly criticised, ostracized, or boycotted—especially on social media—so that a person, group, or work loses support, reputation, or opportunities.

Examples

cancelled celebrity
After the scandal, the actor was publicly cancelled and lost several endorsement deals.
A tweet criticizing his remarks meant he quickly became cancelled by many followers.
Even old films are sometimes cancelled by groups who find their content offensive today.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boycott: to refuse to support or buy from someone as a protest
  • synonym – ostracize: to exclude or banish someone from a group or society
