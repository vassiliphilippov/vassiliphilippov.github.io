episodicity

noun
quality of occurring in episodes or sporadically
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪsoʊˈdɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪsəˈdɪsəti / (BrE)
The quality or state of occurring in separate episodes or at irregular intervals rather than continuously or at regular intervals.

Examples

the episodicity of events
Geologists studied the episodicity of volcanic eruptions over the past millennium.
Some diseases are characterized by a marked episodicity in their symptoms, appearing and disappearing unpredictably.
The episodicity of rainfall in this region makes agriculture challenging for local farmers.

Related Terms

  • antonym – periodicity: the quality of recurring at regular intervals
