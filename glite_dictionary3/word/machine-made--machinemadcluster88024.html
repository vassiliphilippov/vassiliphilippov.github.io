machine-made

adjective
produced by a machine, not by hand
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin meɪd / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn meɪd / (BrE)
(of a product) made with or by a machine, not by hand.

Examples

machine-made lace
She preferred machine-made lace because it was more affordable and easier to care for.
The factory increased its output of machine-made furniture last year.
These machine-made rugs are much less expensive than handmade ones.

  • antonym – handmade: made by hand rather than by machine
