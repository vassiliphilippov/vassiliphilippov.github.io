chromatography

noun
separation technique used in labs
Pronunciation: / kɹoʊˌmætəˈɡɹɑfɪ / (AmE), / kɹəˌmætəˈɡɹɑːfi / (BrE)
a laboratory technique for separating mixtures into their individual components by passing them through a medium in which the components move at different rates
