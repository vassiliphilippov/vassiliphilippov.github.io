Bogartian

adjective
in the style of Humphrey Bogart
Pronunciation: / boʊˈɡɑːrtiən / (AmE), / bəʊˈɡɑːtɪən / (BrE)
Of, relating to, or in the style of Humphrey Bogart, especially in reference to his manner or roles in film.

Examples

Bogartian style performance
His voice had a Bogartian rasp that brought old Hollywood to mind.
