federast

noun
derogatory term for a federalism supporter
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛdəˌræst / (AmE), / ˈfɛdəˌræst / (BrE)
A derogatory term, used especially to insult someone perceived as supporting federalism or a federation, often with a mocking or contemptuous intent.
