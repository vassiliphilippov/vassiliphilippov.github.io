circumspection

noun
careful consideration; caution
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrkəmˈspɛkʃən /; / ˌsɝː.kəmˈspɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmˈspɛkʃən /; / ˌsɜː.kəmˈspek.ʃən / (BrE)
the quality of being careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences before acting or speaking; prudence or caution
