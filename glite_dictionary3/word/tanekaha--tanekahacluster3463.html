tanekaha

noun
New Zealand celery pine tree
Pronunciation: / ˌtænəˈkɑː / (AmE), / ˌtanɪˈkɑː / (BrE)
a medium-tall New Zealand tree (Phyllocladus trichomanoides) known as the celery pine, with slender branches and thin, scale-like leaves
