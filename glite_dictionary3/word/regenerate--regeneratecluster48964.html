to regenerate

verb
to renew or restore; to undergo regeneration
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnəreɪt / (BrE)
to grow again or be renewed after being lost or damaged, especially of tissue, body parts, or cells
