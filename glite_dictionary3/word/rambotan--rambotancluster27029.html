rambotan

noun
tropical Southeast Asian fruit with soft spines
Pronunciation: / ˈræm.boʊ.tɑn / (AmE), / ˈræm.bə.tæn / (BrE)
A bright red, oval fruit from Southeast Asia, covered with soft spines and having a pleasantly acid taste.

Examples

fresh rambotan fruit
At the restaurant, the dessert included fresh rambotan served with coconut cream.
The children were curious about the spiky shell of the rambotan before tasting its sweet-tart flesh.
We bought a kilo of rambotan at the street market in Kuala Lumpur.
