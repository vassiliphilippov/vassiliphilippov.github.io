Euskadi ta Askatasuna

proper noun
armed Basque nationalist group (ETA)
Pronunciation: / eusˈkaði ta askaˈtasuna / (AmE), / eusˈkaði ta askaˈtasuna / (BrE)
an armed Basque nationalist organization founded in 1959 that sought independence for the Basque Country and carried out a campaign of violence, primarily in Spain, until its dissolution in 2018

Examples

Euskadi ta Askatasuna carried out attacks
After years of violence, Euskadi ta Askatasuna announced its final dissolution in 2018.
Many families were affected by the bombings claimed by Euskadi ta Askatasuna over the years.
The Spanish government spent decades trying to dismantle Euskadi ta Askatasuna and its network.
