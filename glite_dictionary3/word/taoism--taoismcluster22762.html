Taoism

noun
Chinese philosophical and religious tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊˌɪzəm /; / ˈdaʊ.ɪzəm, ˈtaʊ.ɪzəm /; / ˈdaʊˌɪzəm, ˈtaʊˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈtaʊˌɪzəm /; / ˈtaʊ.ɪzəm, ˈdaʊ.ɪzəm /; / ˈtaʊ.ɪzəm / (BrE)
a Chinese philosophical and religious tradition based on the teachings of Lao-tzu, emphasizing living in harmony with the Tao (the fundamental principle underlying the universe), and sometimes including practices like meditation, alchemy, and divination
