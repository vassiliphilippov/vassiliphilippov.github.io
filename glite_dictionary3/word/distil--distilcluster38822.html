to distil

verb
purify a liquid by distillation
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪl / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪl / (BrE)
to purify or extract a liquid by heating it to make vapor and then cooling it to collect the liquid, or to separate essential parts by this process
