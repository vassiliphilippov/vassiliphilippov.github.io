modest

adjective
not attracting sexual attention or offending decency
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɒdɪst / (BrE)
(of behavior, clothing, etc.) not intended to attract sexual attention or to offend sexual standards; avoiding indecency or impropriety.

Examples

modest clothing style
Despite the warm weather, his attire was always modest and respectful of local customs.
She chose a modest swimsuit that made her feel comfortable at the pool.
The school's dress code requires students to wear modest clothing that covers their shoulders and knees.

Related Terms

  • antonym – immodest: showing or seeking sexual attention; offending standards of decency
