unjointed

adjective
without joints or articulated segments
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
not having joints or jointed segments; lacking distinct points of articulation.

Examples

unjointed plant stem
She observed the unjointed stems of certain aquatic plants.
The artist depicted a creature with long, unjointed limbs to give it a surreal look.
The skeleton of the worm is unjointed, allowing it to move smoothly through soil.
