to caution

verb
warn or advise somebody about danger
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɔːʃən / (BrE)

Examples

to caution a friend
Parents often caution their children to be careful when crossing the street.
She cautioned her colleagues about the potential risks of the new procedure.
