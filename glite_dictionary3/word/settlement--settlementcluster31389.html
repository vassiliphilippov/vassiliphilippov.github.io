settlement

noun
the act of founding a colony
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt̬.əl.mənt /; / ˈsɛtl̩mənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛt.əl.mənt /; / ˈsɛtləmənt / (BrE)
a place where people establish a community and live together, or the act of creating such a community (including colonies and groups living abroad)
