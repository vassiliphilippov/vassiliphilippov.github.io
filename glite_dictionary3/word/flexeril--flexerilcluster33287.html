Flexeril

noun
brand name for a muscle relaxant drug
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛk.səˌrɪl / (AmE), / ˈflɛk.səˌrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for cyclobenzaprine, a muscle relaxant used to treat muscle spasms and acute musculoskeletal conditions.
