TAT

noun
projective psychological test with picture stories
Pronunciation: / ti æ ti / (AmE), / tiː æ tiː / (BrE)
a projective psychological test in which people create stories about ambiguous pictures to reveal aspects of their personality

Examples

TAT personality test
As part of the evaluation, each participant was asked to create a story for every image in the TAT.
Results from the TAT can provide insights into an individual's thoughts and emotions.
The psychologist administered the TAT to assess the patient's underlying motives and concerns.
