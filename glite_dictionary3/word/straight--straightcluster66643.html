straight

adjective
having no breaks; consecutive
Pronunciation: / streɪt / (AmE), / streɪt / (BrE)
(of a period of time or sequence) happening one after another or without interruption.

Examples

straight hours
He worked twelve straight hours without taking a break.
She read for three straight nights to finish the novel.
They have won six straight games this season.
