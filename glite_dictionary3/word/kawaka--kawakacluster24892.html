kawaka

noun
New Zealand timber tree similar to cypress
Pronunciation: / kəˈwɑːkə / (AmE), / kəˈwɑːkə / (BrE)
a New Zealand timber tree (Libocedrus plumosa) resembling the cypress, valued for its wood
