to center

verb
direct attention to something
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnɾɚ /; / ˈsɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə /; / ˈsɛn.tə / (BrE)
to focus attention or activity on something; to make something the main subject

Examples

to center your mind
It's important to center your energy when preparing for an important performance.

Her research has centered upon climate change and its effects.
Much of the discussion centered on ways to improve education.
The meeting centered around proposals for the new park.
