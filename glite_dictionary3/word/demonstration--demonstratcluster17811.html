demonstration

noun
military display to mislead or intimidate
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
a show of military force or activity intended to mislead or intimidate an opponent

Examples

military demonstration of force
That clever demonstration delayed the invasion by several days.
The enemy mistook our demonstration for a real attack and redeployed their troops.
The general ordered a demonstration along the river to distract enemy forces.

Related Terms

  • antonym – attack: an aggressive and direct military assault
  • synonym – feint: a deceptive or pretended blow, thrust, or other movement, especially in military or sports contexts
