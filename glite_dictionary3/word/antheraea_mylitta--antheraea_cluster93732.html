Antheraea mylitta

noun
silk-producing oriental moth species
Pronunciation: / ænˌθɪriə maɪˈlɪtə / (AmE), / ænˌθɪəriə mɪˈlɪtə / (BrE)
A species of oriental moth that produces brownish silk, known as tussar or tasar silk.

Examples

Antheraea mylitta silk moth
Antheraea mylitta is cultivated in India for its high-value tussar silk.
Research on Antheraea mylitta has helped improve traditional silk production methods.
The cocoons of Antheraea mylitta are collected for use in the silk industry.
