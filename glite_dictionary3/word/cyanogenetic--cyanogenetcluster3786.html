cyanogenetic

adjective
producing cyanide
Pronunciation: / saɪˌænədʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / saɪˌænədʒəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance) capable of producing cyanide, especially when metabolized or decomposed.

Examples
