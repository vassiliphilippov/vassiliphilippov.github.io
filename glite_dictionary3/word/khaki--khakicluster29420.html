khaki

noun
a sturdy yellowish-brown cloth
Pronunciation: / ˈkæki / (AmE), / ˈkɑːki / (BrE)
A sturdy, twilled fabric of yellowish-brown color, especially used in military uniforms.

Examples

khaki uniform pants
His trousers were made of durable khaki fabric.
She bought a new khaki jacket for hiking trips.
The soldiers wore khaki uniforms during the parade.
