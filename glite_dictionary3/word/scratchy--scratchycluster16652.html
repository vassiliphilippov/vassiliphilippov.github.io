scratchy

adjective
inconsistent or uneven in quality
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃi / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃi / (BrE)
(of a performance or result) inconsistent or uneven in quality or execution.

Examples

scratchy performance
Her recent work has been scratchy, showing flashes of brilliance mixed with careless mistakes.
His pitching was scratchy throughout the game, making it hard for the team to win.
The actor gave a scratchy performance on opening night, missing several lines.

Related Terms

  • antonym – consistent: always behaving or performing in a similar way
  • synonym – spotty: inconsistent in quality or performance
