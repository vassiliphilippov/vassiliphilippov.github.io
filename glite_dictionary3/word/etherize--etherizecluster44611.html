to etherize

verb
to anesthetize with ether
Pronunciation: / ˈiːθəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈiːθəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to administer ether to someone in order to induce anesthesia

Examples

to etherize a patient
Before anesthesia improved, it was common practice to etherize people undergoing major procedures.
In the 19th century, doctors would often etherize patients to perform painless surgery.
The surgeon decided to etherize the patient before beginning the operation.
