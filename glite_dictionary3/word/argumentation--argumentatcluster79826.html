argumentation

noun
systematic reasoning or discussion
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrgjəmenˈteɪʃən /; / ˌɑːrɡjəmenˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɑːgjʊmenˈteɪʃən /; / ˌɑːɡjʊmenˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of reasoning, discussing, or presenting arguments to support or oppose a point, especially to persuade others
