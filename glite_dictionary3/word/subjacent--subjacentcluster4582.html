subjacent

adjective
lying or situated below
Pronunciation: / səbˈdʒeɪsənt / (AmE), / sʌbˈdʒeɪsənt / (BrE)
situated directly below or underlying something else, especially in a physical or spatial sense.

Examples

subjacent rock layer
A protective layer covers the subjacent rock formation from erosion.
