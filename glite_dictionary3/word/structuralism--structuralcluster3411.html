structuralism

noun
anthropological theory of underlying structures
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌktʃɚəˌlɪzəm /; / ˈstrʌktʃərəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈstrʌktʃərəlɪzəm /; / ˈstrʌktʃərəlɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
a theory or approach in fields like anthropology, linguistics, or sociology that analyzes things in terms of underlying structures
