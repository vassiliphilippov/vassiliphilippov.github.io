wasted

adjective
extremely intoxicated (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈweɪstɪd / (BrE)
informal (slang) extremely intoxicated by alcohol or drugs; unable to act or think normally because of substance use.

Examples

wasted at parties
By midnight he was wasted after drinking too much at the crowded house party.
They got wasted at the concert and missed the last train home around one.
Her messages sent while wasted were embarrassing and she apologized the following morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drunk: having consumed too much alcohol and being impaired
  • antonym – sober: not under the influence of alcohol or drugs; clear-headed
