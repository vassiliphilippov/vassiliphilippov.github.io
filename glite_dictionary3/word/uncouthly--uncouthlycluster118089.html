uncouthly

adverb
in a rude or awkward manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkuːθli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkuːθli / (BrE)
In a rude or awkward manner; lacking refinement or grace.

Examples

to speak uncouthly
He spoke uncouthly during the interview, shocking his potential employers.
She uncouthly interrupted the formal dinner with loud jokes.
The tourists behaved uncouthly, ignoring the local customs and traditions.
