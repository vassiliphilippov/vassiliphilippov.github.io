petite

adjective
small and dainty in build (woman)
Pronunciation: / pəˈtit / (AmE), / pəˈtiːt / (BrE)
(of a woman or her figure) attractively small and dainty in build.

Examples

petite woman
Despite her petite stature, she has a commanding presence on stage.
In the modelling world, being petite is considered unique and appealing.
She wore a dress that accentuated her petite frame.

Related Terms

  • antonym – large: of considerable or relatively great size
  • synonym – small: of a size that is less than normal or usual
  • degree higher – tiny: extremely small
