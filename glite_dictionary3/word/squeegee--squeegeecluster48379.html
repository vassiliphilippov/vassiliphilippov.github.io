to squeegee

verb
to clean with a squeegee
Pronunciation: / ˈskwiːdʒi / (AmE), / ˈskwiːdʒi / (BrE)
to clean or wipe a surface, especially glass, using a squeegee.

Examples

to squeegee glass
After washing the car, she squeegeed the windows for a streak-free finish.
He carefully squeegeed the shower doors after every use to prevent water spots.
The workers squeegeed the rainwater off the stadium seats before the game started.
