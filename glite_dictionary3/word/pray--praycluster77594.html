to pray

verb
address a deity or worship in prayer
Pronunciation: / preɪ / (AmE), / preɪ / (BrE)
to address a deity, prophet, saint, or object of worship in words or thought, especially in order to ask for help or express thanks

Examples

to pray for help
Every night before bed, she would pray for her family's safety.
He knelt in the chapel to pray for forgiveness and strength.
Many people pray in times of trouble, seeking comfort and guidance.

  • synonym – worship: to show reverence and adoration for a deity
