videodisk

noun
optical disk for video playback
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪdioʊˌdɪsk / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊˌdɪsk / (BrE)
A digital recording, especially of a movie, stored on an optical disk and playable on a computer or television.
