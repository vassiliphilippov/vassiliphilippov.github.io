neckband

noun
tight-fitting necklace or ornamental band
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛkˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈnɛkˌbænd / (BrE)
A necklace or ornamental band that fits closely around the neck, especially as an article of jewelry.

Examples

pearl neckband
Pearls strung on a neckband can add elegance to any dress.
She wore a delicate velvet neckband to complete her evening outfit.
The bride chose a diamond-studded neckband for her wedding day.
