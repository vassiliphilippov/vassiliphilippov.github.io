rightmost

adjective
farthest to the right
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈraɪtməʊst / (BrE)
farthest to the right, situated at the extreme right position in a group or sequence.

Examples

the rightmost seat
Please stand in the rightmost lane when waiting for the subway.
The rightmost button on the toolbar is used to save your document.
The rightmost column contains the totals for each row.
