exulting

adjective
showing great joy and triumph
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋ / (BrE)
Feeling or showing great joy and triumph; rejoicing proudly, especially because of success.

Examples

exulting crowd cheers
His exulting teammates lifted the trophy above their heads after the final whistle.
She sent an exulting message to her friends when she got the news of her promotion.
The exulting crowd cheered wildly as the results were announced.
