uppishly

adverb
in a snobbish manner
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈʌpɪʃli / (BrE)
In a snobbish or arrogantly superior manner.

Examples

speak uppishly to others
He glanced uppishly at his classmates, convinced he was smarter than everyone else.
She spoke uppishly to the staff, expecting special treatment wherever she went.
The children turned their noses up uppishly at the simple meal that was served.
