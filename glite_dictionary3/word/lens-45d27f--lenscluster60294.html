lens

noun
FP abstraction for accessing/updating data
Pronunciation: / l5bnz / (AmE), / l5bnz / (BrE)
(in functional programming) a composable abstraction that focuses on a part of an immutable data structure, providing pure operations to get or set that part and returning updated structures without mutation.

Examples

data lens tool
The library provides a lens that lets you focus on a field inside nested JSON without copying.
By composing two lenses, she updated the deeply nested address street in one concise expression.
When testing, make sure the lens preserves immutability by returning a new structure after modification.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accessor: a function or method that retrieves a specific part of a data structure
  • synonym – optic: a general term in functional programming for composable data-access abstractions like lenses and prisms
