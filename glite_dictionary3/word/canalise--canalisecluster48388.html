to canalise

verb
to provide with a canal or canals
Pronunciation: / kəˈnælaɪz / (AmE), / kəˈnælaɪz / (BrE)
to provide (a city or area) with a canal or canals, often for transport, drainage, or irrigation purposes

Examples

to canalise a river
Efforts to canalise the river through the old town have improved transportation.
The engineers were hired to canalise the city to prevent future flooding.
Urban planners proposed to canalise the lowlands to boost local agriculture.

  • synonym – canalize: the American English spelling; same meaning
