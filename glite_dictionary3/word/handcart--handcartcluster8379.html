handcart

noun
small wheeled vehicle pushed by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd.kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈhænd.kɑːt / (BrE)
A small vehicle with wheels, designed to be pushed by hand, used for carrying goods.

Examples

push a handcart
After the storm, volunteers filled a handcart with debris from the park.
The street vendor loaded his handcart with fresh fruit every morning.
They used a handcart to transport the old books across campus.
