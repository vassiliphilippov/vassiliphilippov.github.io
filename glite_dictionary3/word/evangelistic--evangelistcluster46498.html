evangelistic

adjective
zealously enthusiastic for a cause
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæn.dʒəˈlɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌvan.dʒəˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Marked by strong or enthusiastic support for a cause, idea, or movement, often in a way that seeks to persuade others.

Examples

evangelistic support for change
His evangelistic enthusiasm for environmental issues often motivates others to get involved.
She spoke with such evangelistic passion that everyone in the room felt inspired.
The manager's evangelistic support of the new policy won over many skeptics.

Related Terms

  • synonym – zealous: showing great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause
