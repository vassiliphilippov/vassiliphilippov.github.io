interdepartmental

adjective
between departments
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərdɪˌpɑrtməntˈæl /; / ˌɪntərdɪˌpɑrtˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntədɪˌpɑːtmənˈtæl /; / ˌɪntədɪˌpɑːtˈmɛntl / (BrE)
existing or happening between two or more departments within an organization
