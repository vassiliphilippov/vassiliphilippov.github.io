stepsister

noun
daughter of a stepparent (not blood-related)
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpˌsɪstər / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌsɪstə / (BrE)
a girl or woman who is the daughter of one's stepparent, related to one by the marriage of a parent rather than by blood

Examples

younger stepsister
After the wedding, Emily had to adjust to life with a new stepsister.
Although we grew up together, my stepsister and I do not share any biological parents.
My stepsister and I became close friends after our parents got married.
