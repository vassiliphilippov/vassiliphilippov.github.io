availability

noun
being able to be used or obtained
Pronunciation: / əˌveɪləˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / əˌveɪləˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be used, obtained, or accessed when needed

Examples

availability of rooms
Please check the availability of rooms before making a reservation.
The availability of fresh water is crucial in this region.
We monitor the availability of technical support during weekends.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accessibility: the quality of being easy to reach, enter, or use
  • antonym – unavailability: the state of not being available
