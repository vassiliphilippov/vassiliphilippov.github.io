atavist

noun
organism showing ancestral traits
Pronunciation: / ˈæt̬əvɪst / (AmE), / ˈætəvɪst / (BrE)
an individual or organism that exhibits traits or characteristics typical of a more distant ancestral form, often due to genetic reversion.

Examples

an atavist in society
In literature, a character described as an atavist often struggles to fit into contemporary society.
Some scientists called the rare, tailed infant an atavist due to its ancestral feature.
The strange markings on the bird made it an atavist among its modern relatives.
