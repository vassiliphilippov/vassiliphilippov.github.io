Corydalus cornutus

noun
a species of large North American dobsonfly
Pronunciation: / kɔːˈraɪdələs kɔˈnjuːtəs / (AmE), / kɒˈraɪdələs kɔːˈnjuːtəs / (BrE)
A large North American dobsonfly, the male of which has long slender mandibles, with aquatic larvae often used as bait by anglers.
