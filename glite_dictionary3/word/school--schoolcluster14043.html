to school

verb
of fish, swim or gather in a group
Pronunciation: / skuːl / (AmE), / skuːl / (BrE)
(of fish) to swim or gather together in a large group.

Examples

fish school together
During the migration, many fish can be seen schooling together for protection.
The sardines school in great numbers near the coast.
We watched as thousands of tuna schooled around the reef.
