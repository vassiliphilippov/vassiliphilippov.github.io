sponge

noun
person who absorbs knowledge quickly
Pronunciation: / spʌndʒ / (AmE), / spʌndʒ / (BrE)
a person who is capable of learning new things quickly and absorbing information or skills readily

Examples

sponge for knowledge
As a new employee, she proved to be a sponge, quickly mastering every aspect of her job.
Children are often called sponges because they absorb new ideas and experiences rapidly.
He is a sponge when it comes to technology, always learning the latest advancements without effort.
