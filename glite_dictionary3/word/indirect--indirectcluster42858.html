indirect

adjective
not direct in manner or action
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dəˈrɛkt, ˌɪn.daɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈrɛkt, ˌɪndaɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
not done or stated in a straightforward way; not direct in manner, language, behavior, or action

Examples

indirect way of speaking
