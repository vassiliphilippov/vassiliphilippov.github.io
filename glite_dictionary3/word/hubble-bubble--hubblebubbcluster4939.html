hubble-bubble

noun
water pipe for smoking tobacco (hookah)
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌb.əl ˌbʌb.əl / (AmE), / ˈhʌb.əl ˌbʌb.əl / (BrE)
an oriental tobacco pipe with a long flexible tube, in which smoke is passed through water before being inhaled; also known as a hookah or narghile
