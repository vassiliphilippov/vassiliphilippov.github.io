foster

adjective
nurturing without blood or legal ties
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑstər / (AmE), / ˈfɒstə / (BrE)
(of a relationship or care) involving the raising or nurturing of a child by someone who is not a biological or legal parent.

Examples

foster family home
After living with a foster family for two years, he was reunited with his mother.
She has taken in several foster children over the past decade.
The organization helps place children in loving foster homes across the region.
