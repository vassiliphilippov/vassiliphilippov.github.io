mangled

adjective
badly torn or jagged, usually from injury
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡəld / (AmE), / ˈmæŋɡəld / (BrE)
(of an object or body part) having edges that are badly torn or jagged, typically as a result of injury or damage.

Examples

mangled body part

The mangled remains of the car were barely recognizable after the accident.
