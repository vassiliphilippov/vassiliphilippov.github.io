release

noun
the act of discharging something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlis / (AmE), / rɪˈliːs / (BrE)
the act or process of allowing a substance, energy, or other entity to escape or be discharged

release of gas

The new medication helps control the release of insulin in the body.
