to say

verb
to have specified wording (of a text)
Pronunciation: / seɪ / (AmE), / seɪ / (BrE)
to indicate or show information, especially in writing or on a device (such as a document, sign, or clock)

Examples

say on sign
The instructions say to press the green button when you are ready.
The letter said that my application was approved.
The sign on the gate says 'No Trespassing.'
My watch says it's almost three o'clock.
The sign says the restaurant opens at 8 AM.
What does the contract say about vacation days?

Related Terms

  • synonym – indicate: to show or point out
  • synonym – read: (of a text) to have a certain wording or be written in a particular way
  • synonym – show: to display or make visible
