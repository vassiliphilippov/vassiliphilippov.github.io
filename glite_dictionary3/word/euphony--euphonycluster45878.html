euphony

noun
pleasant, harmonious sound
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːfəni / (AmE), / ˈjuːfəni / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasing to the ear, especially through a harmonious combination of words or sounds

Examples

pleasant euphony in music
He found comfort in the euphony of birds singing outside his window.
Poets often strive for euphony in their verse to make it more pleasant to read aloud.
The euphony of the orchestra's performance captivated the audience throughout the evening.
