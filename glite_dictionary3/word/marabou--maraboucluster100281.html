marabou

noun
soft feathers used for trimming garments
Pronunciation: / ˈmærəˌbu / (AmE), / ˈmærəbuː / (BrE)
the soft, downy feathers of marabou storks, often used for trimming garments and accessories

Examples

soft marabou feathers
A soft marabou stole added a touch of glamour to her evening attire.

The dress was decorated with a marabou trim along the neckline.
