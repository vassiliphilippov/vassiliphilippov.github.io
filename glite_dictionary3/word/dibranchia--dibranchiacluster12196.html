Dibranchia

noun
group of cephalopods excluding nautiluses
Pronunciation: / daɪˈbræŋkiə / (AmE), / daɪˈbraŋkɪə /; / daɪˈbræŋkɪə / (BrE)
a major group (subclass) of cephalopods that includes octopuses, squids, and cuttlefish, but not the Nautilidae family
