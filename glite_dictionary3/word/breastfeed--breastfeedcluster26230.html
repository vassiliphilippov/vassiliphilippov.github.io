to breastfeed

verb
feed a baby with mother's breast milk
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstˌfid / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstˌfiːd / (BrE)
to feed (a baby) with milk directly from the mother's breast

Examples

to breastfeed a baby
He was breastfed as an infant before switching to bottle-feeding.
She decided to breastfeed her baby for the first six months.
Some workplaces provide spaces for mothers to breastfeed during the day.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bottle-feed: to feed (a baby) with a bottle rather than at the breast
  • synonym – nurse: to feed a baby at the breast
