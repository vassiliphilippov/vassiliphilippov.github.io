ludology

noun
study of games and gaming
Pronunciation: / luˈdɑlədʒi / (AmE), / luːˈdɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The study of games, especially video games, as a form of academic or theoretical inquiry.

Examples

ludology research papers
As ludology gains popularity, more universities are offering courses devoted to the analysis of video games.
She is completing her master's thesis in ludology with a focus on role-playing games.
The conference brought together leading scholars in ludology and interactive media.
