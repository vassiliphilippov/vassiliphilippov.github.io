Petrograd

proper noun
historical name for Saint Petersburg
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛtroʊˈɡrɑd / (AmE), / ˌpɛtrəˈɡrɑːd / (BrE)
The name of Saint Petersburg, Russia, used from 1914 to 1924; formerly the capital of Russia.
