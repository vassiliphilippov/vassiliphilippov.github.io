to powder

verb
apply powder to something
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdə / (BrE)
to apply a fine dry substance, such as cosmetic powder, to a surface, especially the skin or hair

Examples

to powder your face
He powdered the cake with sugar to finish the dessert.
In the past, men often powdered their wigs to achieve a distinguished look.
She quickly powdered her nose before the meeting.
