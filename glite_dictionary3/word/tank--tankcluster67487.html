to tank

verb
store in a tank
Pronunciation: / tæŋk / (AmE), / tæŋk / (BrE)
to store a liquid or substance in a tank by causing it to flow into the tank

Examples

to tank oil
After delivery, the newly harvested oil is immediately tanked for storage.
The company tanks the waste water before treating it further.
They tanked the chemicals to prevent spills during transport.
