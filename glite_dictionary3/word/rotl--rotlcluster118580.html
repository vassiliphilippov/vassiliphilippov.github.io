rotl

noun
unit of weight (1–5 pounds) in the Mediterranean region
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑtəl / (AmE), / ˈrɒtəl / (BrE)
a unit of weight used in some Muslim countries near the Mediterranean, varying from about one to five pounds

Examples

a rotl of wool
A rotl of wool varied in weight depending on the region.
In historical accounts, taxes were often calculated in rotls of grain.
The merchant measured out five rotls of spices for the customer.
