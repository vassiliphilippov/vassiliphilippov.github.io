to reprocess

verb
process again, especially for reuse
Pronunciation: / riˈprɑː.sɛs / (AmE), / riˈprəʊ.sɛs / (BrE)
to treat or process something again, especially in order to reuse or improve it

Examples

to reprocess waste materials
After collecting the data, researchers often reprocess it to correct errors or improve accuracy.
Some countries reprocess nuclear waste to extract usable materials.
The factory will reprocess the plastic bottles into new containers.
