Virgin

noun
the sixth sign of the zodiac (Virgo)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒɪn / (BrE)
the sixth sign of the zodiac, also known as Virgo, occurring when the sun is in this sign from about August 23 to September 22
