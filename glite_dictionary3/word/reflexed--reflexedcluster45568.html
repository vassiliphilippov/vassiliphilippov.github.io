reflexed

adjective
bent downward and outward (botanical)
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛkst / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛkst / (BrE)
(of leaves or other plant parts) bent downward and outward, usually at an angle greater than 90 degrees.

Examples

reflexed leaves
Some species display reflexed leaves to help shed water more effectively.
The botanist noted that the sepals were sharply reflexed away from the flower.
The orchid's reflexed petals give the flower a distinctive appearance.

  • synonym – recurved: curved or bent backward
