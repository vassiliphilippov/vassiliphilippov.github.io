chemist

noun
pharmacist (esp. in British English)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪst / (BrE)
a person qualified to prepare and sell medicinal drugs, especially in a pharmacy; a pharmacist

Examples

at the chemist counter
She picked up her prescription at the local chemist.
The chemist recommended an over-the-counter allergy medicine.
You can ask the chemist for advice on common health problems.
