mongrel

noun
derogatory term for irregular or mixed origin thing
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋɡrəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋɡrəl / (BrE)
A derogatory term for something of mixed origin that is irregular, inferior, or not genuine.

Examples

mongrel language mix
He considered the dialect a mongrel and preferred the traditional language instead.
Some critics dismissed the painting as a mongrel of several conflicting styles.
The chef's new menu is a mongrel of cuisines, lacking a clear identity.
