gallant

adjective
attentive and chivalrous to women
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlænt / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.ənt / (BrE)
(of a man) showing special attention, courtesy, or respect to women, in a chivalrous or courtly manner.

Examples

gallant gentleman behavior
He was always gallant, opening doors and offering his seat to women on the train.
She appreciated his gallant gesture of carrying her books across campus.
The gallant gentleman bowed deeply before the queen, impressing everyone with his courtesies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chivalrous: showing courage, courtesy, and attentiveness, especially towards women
  • antonym – ungallant: lacking in courtesy, especially towards women
