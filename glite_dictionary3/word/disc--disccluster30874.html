disc

noun
flat audio recording medium
Pronunciation: / dɪsk / (AmE), / dɪsk / (BrE)
A flat, circular object, especially one used for recording and reproducing sound, such as a phonograph record.

Examples

a music disc
Collectors often search for rare jazz discs from the 1950s.
She placed the disc carefully on the turntable and started the music.
The old phonograph could still play a disc with remarkable clarity.

  • synonym – record: a flat, circular device used to store and play recorded sound
