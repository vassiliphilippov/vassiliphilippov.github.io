impregnably

adverb
in an unassailable manner
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛɡ.nəb.li / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛɡ.nəb.li / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be overcome, captured, or defeated; in an unassailable manner.

Examples

impregnably defended position
She defended her position impregnably, leaving no room for doubt or counterargument.
The ancient fortress was impregnably situated atop the rocky hill, deterring all invaders.
The castle was impregnably built, with thick stone walls and a deep moat surrounding it.
