tightwad

noun
miserly person
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtwɑd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtwɒd / (BrE)
a person who is unwilling to spend money; a miser

Examples

stingy tightwad
Don't be a tightwad; treat yourself to a nice dinner once in a while.
Everyone called her a tightwad because she hated spending even a penny unnecessarily.
My uncle is such a tightwad he still uses coupons from the 1990s.

Related Terms

  • synonym – miser: a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible
  • antonym – spendthrift: a person who spends money carelessly
