abortionist

noun
person who performs abortions
Pronunciation: / əˈbɔrʃənɪst / (AmE), / əˈbɔːʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who performs abortions, especially illegally or without proper medical qualifications

Examples

illegal abortionist
Some women were forced to go to an abortionist because legal options were not available.
The police arrested an unlicensed abortionist operating out of a private clinic.
The word abortionist often carries a negative connotation in public debates.
