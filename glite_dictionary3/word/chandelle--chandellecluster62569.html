chandelle

noun
aerial climbing turn maneuver
Pronunciation: / ʃænˈdɛl / (AmE), / ʃanˈdɛl / (BrE)
an aircraft maneuver in which a sharp climbing turn is made to gain altitude while changing direction

Examples

a chandelle maneuver
Learning how to execute a chandelle is a requirement for advanced pilot training.
She used a sharp chandelle to evade the incoming aircraft.
The pilot performed a perfect chandelle during the air show, impressing the crowd.
