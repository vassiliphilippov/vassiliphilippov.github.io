high-handedness

noun
overbearing or domineering manner
Pronunciation: / haɪ ˈhændɪdnəs / (AmE), / haɪ ˈhændɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of acting in an overbearing, domineering, or arrogant manner, especially toward those considered inferior

Examples

government high-handedness
Complaints about government high-handedness have been increasing in recent months.
She resented his high-handedness in making all the decisions without consulting anyone.
The new manager's high-handedness quickly alienated many members of the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arrogance: overbearing pride or self-importance
  • antonym – humility: modest or low view of one's importance
