fork

noun
agricultural tool with prongs
Pronunciation: / fɔrk / (AmE), / fɔːk / (BrE)
a tool with a handle and two or more metal prongs, used for lifting, digging, or turning over soil or hay

Examples

garden fork
A hay fork is perfect for moving straw in the barn.
She leaned her old iron fork against the fence after digging up potatoes.
The farmer used a fork to turn over the compost pile.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pitchfork: a farm tool with a long handle and two or three long, curved prongs
