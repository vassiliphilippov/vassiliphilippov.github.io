clashing

adjective
sharply discordant or in strong conflict
Pronunciation: / ˈklæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
(of things) sharply and harshly discordant; conflicting strongly in appearance, sound, or interest.

Examples

clashing colors
Her outfit was ruined by the clashing patterns of her shirt and skirt.
The decorator avoided using clashing colors in the living room design.
There are often clashing opinions in a team brainstorming session.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conflicting: incompatible or at odds
  • antonym – harmonious: forming a pleasing or consistent whole
