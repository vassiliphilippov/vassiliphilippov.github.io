Ragusa

proper noun
former name for Dubrovnik, Croatia
Pronunciation: / rəˈɡuːsə / (AmE), / rəˈɡuːzə / (BrE)
the former name for Dubrovnik, a port city in southwestern Croatia on the Adriatic Sea, known for its historic old town and as a tourist destination
