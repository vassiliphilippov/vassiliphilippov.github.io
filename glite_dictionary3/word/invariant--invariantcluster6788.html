invariant

adjective
unchanging under transformation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛɹiənt /; / ɪnˈvɛriənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛːɹɪənt /; / ɪnˈvɛərɪənt / (BrE)
not changing; remaining constant or unchanged (in technical or general situations)
