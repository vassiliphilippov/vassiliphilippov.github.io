attitude

noun
deliberate pose or posture (art/theater)
Pronunciation: / ˈæ.t̬ɪ.tuːd /; / ˈæt̬.ə.tuːd / (AmE), / ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
a position or posture of the body, especially one taken for effect or to express a feeling

Examples

ballet attitude
During the performance, she held an elegant attitude that reflected classical ballet.
