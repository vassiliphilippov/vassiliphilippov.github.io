brain-fag

noun
mental exhaustion; mental fatigue
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪn ˌfæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪn ˌfæɡ / (BrE)
A state of mental exhaustion or fatigue that diminishes one's ability to think clearly or concentrate.
