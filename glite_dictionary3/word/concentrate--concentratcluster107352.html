to concentrate

verb
gather or come together
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsənˌtreɪt /; / ˈkɑn.sənˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsəntreɪt /; / ˈkɒn.sən.treɪt / (BrE)
to bring people, things, or resources together in one place or under one authority
