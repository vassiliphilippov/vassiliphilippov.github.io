Cornus

proper noun
genus of shrubs and small trees; dogwoods
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnəs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnəs / (BrE)
A genus of chiefly deciduous shrubs or small trees (dogwoods) native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, including dogwoods and cornels.
