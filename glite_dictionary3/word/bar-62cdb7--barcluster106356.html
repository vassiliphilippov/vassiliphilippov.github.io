bar

noun
unit of musical time in notation
Pronunciation: / bɑr / (AmE), / bɑː / (BrE)
A unit of musical time that contains a set number of beats, indicated by vertical lines on a musical staff.

Examples

musical bar
A drum solo typically fills one or two bars at the end of the song.
