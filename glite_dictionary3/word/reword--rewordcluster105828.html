to reword

verb
express in different words
Pronunciation: / riˈwɝd / (AmE), / riˈwɜːd / (BrE)
to express the same idea or message using different words

Examples

to reword a sentence
After reading the feedback, she decided to reword her introduction for clarity.
He reworded the instructions to avoid any confusion.
Please reword this sentence so that it's easier to understand.
