regard

noun
care or attention given to others
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
careful attention or consideration, especially towards others' needs or feelings.

Examples

regard for others
He accepted the job without any regard for the long hours it required.
She treated the children with special regard, ensuring their safety at all times.
We must act with regard for those less fortunate than ourselves.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disregard: lack of attention or care
  • synonym – heed: careful attention or notice
