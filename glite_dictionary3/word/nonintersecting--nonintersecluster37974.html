nonintersecting

adjective
never meeting or crossing (geometry)
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪntərˈsɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪntəˈsɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
(Of lines, planes, or surfaces) never meeting or crossing at any point.

Examples

nonintersecting lines
In geometry, nonintersecting circles never touch or cross at any point.
The architect designed nonintersecting beams to support the open structure.
