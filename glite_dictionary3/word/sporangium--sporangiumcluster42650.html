sporangium

noun
spore-producing structure
Pronunciation: / spəˈrændʒiəm / (AmE), / spəˈrandʒɪəm / (BrE)
a structure in which spores are produced, found in fungi, algae, and some plants

Examples

sporangium of a fungus
A sporangium often bursts open to disperse its contents into the air.
Each sporangium of the fern releases thousands of spores when mature.
The mold colony developed a visible sporangium at the tip of its stalk.
