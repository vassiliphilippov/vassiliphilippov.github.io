sympathetic

adjective
related to the sympathetic nervous system
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or controlled by the sympathetic nervous system, which is part of the autonomic nervous system and is involved in the body's response to stress or emergency situations.

Examples

sympathetic nervous system
Sympathetic stimulation causes an increase in heart rate and blood pressure.
Activation of sympathetic neurons prepares the body for a fight-or-flight response.
Damage to the sympathetic nerves can disrupt normal sweating patterns.
