pourboire

noun
small tip or gratuity
Pronunciation: / pʊərˈbwɑr / (AmE), / pʊəˈbwɑː / (BrE)
A small amount of money given as a tip, especially for services such as those provided by a waiter.

Examples

leave a pourboire for waiter
At the end of the meal, he left a generous pourboire for the waiter.
She slipped a pourboire into the bellhop's hand as thanks for carrying her luggage.
When traveling in France, it's customary to leave a small pourboire in cafés.
