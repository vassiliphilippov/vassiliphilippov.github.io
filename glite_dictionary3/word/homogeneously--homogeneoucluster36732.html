homogeneously

adverb
in a uniform or similar way
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈmoʊdʒəˌniəsli, həˈmɑːdʒɪˌneɪəsli / (AmE), / həˈmɒdʒɪnɪəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is uniform or similar in kind, nature, or composition throughout.

Examples

homogeneously mixed colors
The paintings are arranged homogeneously to create a sense of unity throughout the gallery.
The solution was mixed homogeneously so that every sample contained the same concentration of salt.
The team was composed homogeneously of experts from a single discipline.
