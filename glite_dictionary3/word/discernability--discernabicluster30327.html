discernability

noun
quality of being easy to perceive
Pronunciation: / dɪˌsɜrnəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / dɪˌsɜːnəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being easy to perceive or distinguish.

Examples

discernability of details
