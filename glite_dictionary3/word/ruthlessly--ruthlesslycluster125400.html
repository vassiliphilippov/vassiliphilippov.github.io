ruthlessly

adverb
in a harsh or merciless way
Pronunciation: / ˈruːθləsli / (AmE), / ˈruːθləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no pity or compassion for others; in a harsh or merciless manner.

Examples

ruthlessly crush rivals
Competition in the tech industry can be ruthlessly intense.
The company ruthlessly eliminated hundreds of jobs to cut costs.
The dictator ruthlessly suppressed any opposition to his regime.
