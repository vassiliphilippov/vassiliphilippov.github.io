delicate

adjective
precisely sensitive instrument or device
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlɪkət / (AmE), / ˈdɛlɪkət / (BrE)
(of an instrument or device) able to detect or respond to very small changes or differences with precision.

Examples

delicate instrument
Our lab is equipped with extremely delicate sensors to measure air quality changes.
The scientist used a delicate balance to weigh the chemical samples accurately.
This telescope is so delicate that it can detect the faintest light from distant stars.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sensitive: (of instruments) quick to detect or respond to slight changes, signals, or influences
