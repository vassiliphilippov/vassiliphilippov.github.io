Casanova

noun
man noted for many love affairs
Pronunciation: / ˌkæsəˈnoʊvə / (AmE), / ˌkæsəˈnəʊvə / (BrE)

Everyone at the office knew him to be a bit of a Casanova.
He had a reputation as a Casanova in his younger years.
