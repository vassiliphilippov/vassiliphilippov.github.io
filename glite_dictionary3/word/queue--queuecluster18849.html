queue

noun
computing: ordered list of tasks or messages
Pronunciation: / kjuː / (AmE), / kjuː / (BrE)
(in computing) an ordered list of tasks, jobs, or messages waiting to be processed or transmitted, with items typically handled in the order they arrive.

Examples

a queue of jobs
Before sending the file, make sure it enters the upload queue.
Messages are stored in a queue until the server is ready to handle them.
The print jobs were added to the queue and processed in order.

Related Terms

  • antonym – stack: (computing) a data structure in which the most recently added item is the first to be removed
