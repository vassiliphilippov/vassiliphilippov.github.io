to spume

verb
form or produce foam or froth
Pronunciation: / spjuːm / (AmE), / spjuːm / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to form or produce froth or foam; to become bubbly or frothy.

Examples

to spume from waves
Champagne overflowed, spuming from the bottle onto the floor.
Foam spumed around the rocks as the river tumbled downstream.
The waves began to spume as the storm intensified near the shore.

Related Terms

  • synonym – foam: to form a mass of small bubbles on the surface of a liquid
