chemical

noun
substance produced by or used in chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
A substance produced by or used in a chemical process, especially one created or manipulated for specific use.

Examples

toxic chemical
Always store chemicals in properly labeled containers away from food.
Many household cleaning products contain strong chemicals that can irritate your skin.
The laboratory tested the water for toxic chemicals after the spill.
