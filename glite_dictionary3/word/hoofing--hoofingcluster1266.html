hoofing

noun
dance emphasizing footwork
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʊfɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of dance in which the footwork and steps are more emphasized than gestures or postures, especially in tap dancing.

Examples

hoofing on stage
He started hoofing at the age of eight and quickly mastered complex routines.
She devoted herself to hoofing, practicing the intricate steps for hours each day.
The musical features classic hoofing in its vibrant tap dance numbers.
