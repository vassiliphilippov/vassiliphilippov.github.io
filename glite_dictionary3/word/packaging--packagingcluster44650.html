packaging

noun
presentation or promotion of something
Pronunciation: / ˈpækɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpækɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the way in which something, such as an idea, product, or person, is presented or promoted to the public

Examples

packaging of ideas
Politicians often focus on the packaging of their ideas to gain public support.
The company's clever packaging of its new service attracted many customers.
The success of the campaign was due to the creative packaging of a complex issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym – presentation: the way in which something is shown or arranged for others to see
  • synonym – promotion: the act of raising awareness or encouraging acceptance of something
