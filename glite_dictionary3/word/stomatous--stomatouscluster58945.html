stomatous

adjective
having or relating to plant stomata
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊ.mə.təs / (AmE), / ˈstəʊ.mə.təs / (BrE)
relating to or having stomata (tiny pores) on the surface of a plant organ.

Examples

stomatous plant leaves
A stomatous structure allows for transpiration and photosynthesis in plants.
Certain aquatic plants may lack a stomatous epidermis altogether.
The stomatous surface of the leaf aids in gas exchange.
