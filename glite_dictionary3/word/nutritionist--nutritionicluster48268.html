nutritionist

noun
expert in nutrition
Pronunciation: / nuˈtrɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / njuˈtrɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in the study of nutrition and provides advice on healthy eating

Examples

consult a nutritionist
After meeting with a nutritionist, she changed her eating habits to improve her health.
He worked with a nutritionist to prepare for the marathon.
The hospital hired a nutritionist to create meal plans for the patients.
