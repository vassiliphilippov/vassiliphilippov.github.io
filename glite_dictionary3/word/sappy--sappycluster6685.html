sappy

adjective
abundant in sap (plant/wood)
Pronunciation: / ˈsæpi / (AmE), / ˈsæpi / (BrE)
(of a plant, tree, or wood) containing a large amount of sap.

Examples

sappy pine wood
Freshly cut pine is typically quite sappy and can be difficult to burn.
The sappy maple trees are tapped each spring to make syrup.
We need to avoid using sappy kindling wood, as it creates too much smoke.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sapless: lacking sap; dry
