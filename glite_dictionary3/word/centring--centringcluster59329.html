centring

noun
the snap in American football
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.tɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(in American football) the act of putting the ball in play at the start of a down by snapping it between the legs to a back, usually the quarterback.

Examples

centring the football
A bad centring resulted in a fumble by the quarterback during the game.
Proper centring is critical to ensure a smooth start to each play.
The coach spent extra time practicing centring with the new center.

  • synonym – snap: the act of quickly passing the ball back to a teammate at the start of a play in American football
