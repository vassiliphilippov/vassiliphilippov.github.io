Gyromitra californica

noun
a California false morel fungus
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒaɪrəˈmaɪtrə ˌkæləˈfɔrnɪkə / (AmE), / ˌdʒaɪrəˈmaɪtrə ˌkælɪˈfɔːnɪkə / (BrE)
A species of false morel fungus, characterized by a brown, puffed-up fertile surface and a thick, fluted stalk, typically found under conifers in California.
