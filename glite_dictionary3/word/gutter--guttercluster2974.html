to gutter

verb
cut channels into a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌtər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌtə / (BrE)
(of water or other forces) to erode or cut channels or grooves into a surface.

Examples

to gutter a path
Over years, flowing water slowly guttered the stone path.
The melting ice guttered the pavement, leaving irregular grooves.
The strong winds and rain guttered deep channels into the hillside.
