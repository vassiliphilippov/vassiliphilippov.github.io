turning

noun
shaping with a lathe
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or activity of shaping an object on a lathe by rotating it against a cutting tool

Examples

turning on a lathe
During his apprenticeship, he learned the basics of turning and other woodworking techniques.
She specializes in the turning of intricate metal parts for machinery.
The delicate vase was created through careful turning on the lathe.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lathe: a machine for shaping wood, metal, or other material by means of a rotating drive
