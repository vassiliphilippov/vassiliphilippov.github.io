beastly

adjective
cruel or brutal; like a beast
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːstli / (AmE), / ˈbiːstli / (BrE)
Resembling a beast; lacking human sensibility or showing cruel or coarse qualities.

Examples

beastly behavior
He displayed a beastly disregard for the feelings of others, acting with shocking cruelty.
Her beastly behavior at the party shocked everyone present.
The prisoners were subjected to beastly treatment throughout their captivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bestial: lacking human sensibility; relating to a beast
  • synonym – brutish: resembling a brute; cruel or coarse
  • antonym – humane: showing compassion or benevolence
