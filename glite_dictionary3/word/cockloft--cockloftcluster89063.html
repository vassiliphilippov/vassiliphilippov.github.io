cockloft

noun
small loft or garret under a roof
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑk.lɔft / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.lɒft / (BrE)
A small loft or garret, especially one situated directly under the roof of a house.

Examples

old cockloft attic
The old manor's cockloft was cluttered with dusty trunks and forgotten relics.
The servants once slept in the cockloft above the main hall.
They converted the narrow cockloft into a cozy reading nook under the eaves.
