straddle

noun
the act of sitting or standing astride
Pronunciation: / ˈstrædəl / (AmE), / ˈstræd.əl / (BrE)
the act of sitting or standing with one leg on each side of something

Examples

a wide straddle
He performed a straddle before landing neatly on the mat.
She took a quick straddle of the fence to get to the other side.
The gymnast's straddle was perfect during her routine.
