survival

noun
something that continues to exist
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪvəl / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvəl / (BrE)
A person, thing, or practice that continues to exist from an earlier time.

Examples

survival from ancient times
Fossils are often described as survivals of ancient species.
Many idioms in language are survivals of old traditions.
This custom is a survival from pagan times.

  • synonym – remnant: a part or quantity that is left after the greater part has been used, removed, or destroyed
