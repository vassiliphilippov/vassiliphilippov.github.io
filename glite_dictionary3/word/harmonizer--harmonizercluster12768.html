harmonizer

noun
person who brings things into harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːməˌnaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who brings different people, ideas, or things into agreement or harmony; a mediator who resolves differences.

Examples

harmonizer in a group
As a harmonizer, his calm presence eased tensions between the rival teams.
Maria acted as the harmonizer during the meeting, ensuring everyone reached a fair compromise.
The project needed a harmonizer to align the goals of the different departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conciliator: a person who helps bring about agreement between two parties
  • synonym – mediator: a person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement
