SEbS

noun
compass point south of southeast
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs iː bi ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌɛs iː bi ˈɛs / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point south of southeast, or at an angle of 146 degrees and 15 minutes clockwise from due north.

Examples

SEbS direction
If you follow a SEbS course, you’ll reach the harbor by sunrise.
The ship changed direction to SEbS to avoid the approaching storm.
Weather reports indicated strong winds coming from the SEbS direction.
