clipper

noun
fast 19th-century sailing ship
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪpər / (AmE), / ˈklɪpə / (BrE)
a fast sailing ship with a sleek hull and tall masts, used in the 19th century, especially for long-distance cargo transport

Examples

old clipper ship
A beautifully restored clipper is now on display at the maritime museum.
