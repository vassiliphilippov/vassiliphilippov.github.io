tractor

noun
motor vehicle used in farming
Pronunciation: / ˈtræktər / (AmE), / ˈtraktə / (BrE)
A powerful motor vehicle with large rear wheels, used chiefly on farms for pulling machinery and trailers.

Examples

tractor plows field
A group of children watched as a red tractor hauled hay bales across the countryside.
The farmer used his tractor to plow the fields before planting the new crop.
