sash

noun
a band or strip of cloth worn as clothing
Pronunciation: / sæʃ / (AmE), / sæʃ / (BrE)
A long strip of cloth worn around the waist or over the shoulder, typically as part of an outfit or uniform, to fasten or decorate clothing.

Examples

sash over the shoulder
She wore a bright red sash over her dress at the ceremony.
The boy tied a blue sash tightly around his waist.
Traditional costumes often include a decorative sash to complete the look.
