unpromising

adjective
unlikely to succeed or be enjoyable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɑːmɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɒmɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
showing little or no likelihood of success or a positive outcome; unlikely to bring satisfaction or enjoyment

Examples

an unpromising start
Despite an unpromising start, the project eventually succeeded.
He accepted an unpromising role but turned it into a memorable performance.
The weather made our hike look rather unpromising at first.

Related Terms

  • antonym – promising: showing signs of future success or good results
