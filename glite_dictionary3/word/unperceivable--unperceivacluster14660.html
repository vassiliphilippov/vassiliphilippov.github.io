unperceivable

adjective
impossible to perceive
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.pɚˈsiː.və.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.pəˈsiː.və.bəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to perceive by the mind or senses.

Examples

unperceivable sound waves
For some animals, ultrasonic sounds are audible, but to humans they are unperceivable.
The movement of the glacier was so slow that it was unperceivable to the naked eye.
There was an unperceivable change in the tone of his voice during the conversation.
