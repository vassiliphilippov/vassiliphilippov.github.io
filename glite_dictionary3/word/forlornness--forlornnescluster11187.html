forlornness

noun
state of deep sadness or abandonment
Pronunciation: / fərˈlɔrn.nəs / (AmE), / fəˈlɔːn.nəs / (BrE)
A feeling of deep sadness, loneliness, or abandonment, especially when one has been left alone or neglected.

Examples

deep forlornness
He wrote poems filled with forlornness, reflecting on lost love and solitude.
The forlornness in her eyes was impossible to ignore after everyone had left.
The abandoned house seemed to radiate a sense of forlornness that made visitors uneasy.

  • synonym – despair: the complete loss or absence of hope
  • antonym – hopefulness: the state of being full of hope or optimism
  • synonym – melancholy: a deep, persistent sadness or gloom
