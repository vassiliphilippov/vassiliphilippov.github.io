disarray

noun
untidiness or disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪ / (BrE)
A state of untidiness or disorder, especially in clothing or appearance.

Examples

disarray of clothes
After the long journey, her hair was in complete disarray.
His tie was askew and his suit in disarray after the hectic morning.
The sudden announcement threw the staff into disarray.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mess: a dirty or untidy state
  • antonym – order: neat and organized condition
