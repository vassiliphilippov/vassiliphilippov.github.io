grey

noun
neutral color between black and white
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ / (BrE)
A neutral color between black and white, having no hue, often described as dull or intermediate in shade.

Examples

a grey color
He wore a grey suit to the interview.
Mist hung over the fields, turning everything a pale grey.
The walls were painted a soft grey to create a calming atmosphere.
