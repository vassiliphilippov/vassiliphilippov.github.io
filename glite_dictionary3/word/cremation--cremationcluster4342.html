cremation

noun
burning of a dead body to ashes
Pronunciation: / krɪˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / krɪˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the disposal of a dead body by burning it to ashes, typically as part of a funeral rite.

Examples

cremation ceremony
After the cremation, the ashes were scattered at sea according to his wishes.
Many cultures have adopted cremation as a common practice for dealing with the deceased.
The family chose cremation instead of a traditional burial.
