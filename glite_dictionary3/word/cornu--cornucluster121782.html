cornu

noun
horn-shaped anatomical structure
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.nu / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.njuː / (BrE)
(Anatomy) Any structure in the body that resembles a horn in shape.

Examples

uterine cornu
In some mammals, the uterus has two distinct cornua to accommodate multiple embryos.
Lesions can sometimes be found near the posterior cornu of the lateral ventricle.
The greater cornu of the hyoid bone provides support for the larynx.
