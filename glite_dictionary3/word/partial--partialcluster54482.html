partial

adjective
not complete; only a part
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːʃəl / (BrE)
existing or happening only in part; not complete or total

Examples

partial damage
After the storm, the museum suffered partial damage to its roof.

The committee received only a partial report and requested the remainder of the information.
