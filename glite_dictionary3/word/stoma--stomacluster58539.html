stoma

noun
tiny pore in a plant's leaf or stem
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˈstəʊ.mə / (BrE)
a tiny pore found on the surface of a leaf or stem, through which gases and water vapor can pass

Examples

stoma on a leaf
Each stoma can open or close in response to environmental conditions.
The stoma regulates the exchange of gases between the plant and its environment.
Water vapor exits the leaf mainly through the stoma during transpiration.

  • synonym – pore: a minute opening through which substances can pass
