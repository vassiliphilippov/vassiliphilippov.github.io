unvulcanized

adjective
not subjected to vulcanization (rubber)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvʌlkəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈvʌlkənaɪzd / (BrE)
(of rubber) not having been subjected to the process of vulcanization.

Examples

unvulcanized rubber sheet
Disposal of unvulcanized rubber must follow special guidelines due to its different chemical properties.
If left unvulcanized, the rubber remains soft and sticky, unsuitable for most industrial uses.
The manufacturer requires only unvulcanized rubber for this specific bonding process.

Related Terms

  • antonym – vulcanized: (of rubber) treated with sulfur and heat to improve elasticity and strength
