second

noun
formal support for a motion
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
a formal expression of support for a motion or proposal made by another person in a meeting or debate

Examples

give a second to a motion
At the annual meeting, she asked if anyone would give a second to the motion.
The committee waited for a second before voting on the proposal.
Without a second, the chairman could not proceed with the discussion.

  • synonym – support: formal backing of a proposal or idea
