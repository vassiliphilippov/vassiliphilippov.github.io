to give

verb
provide or offer food to someone
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv / (AmE), / ɡɪv / (BrE)
to hand, provide, or allow someone to have something; to transfer or offer something to another person (object, information, permission, etc.)

She was asked to give her honest opinion on the proposal.
