to Islamise

verb
convert to Islam
Pronunciation: / ˈɪsləˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɪsləmaɪz / (BrE)
to convert (a person, group, or place) to the religion, customs, or culture of Islam

Examples

to Islamise a country
During the 16th century, the empire sought to Islamise vast regions of its new territory.
Some rulers attempted to Islamise their populations through law and education reforms.
The policy was gradually put in place to Islamise public life and institutions.
