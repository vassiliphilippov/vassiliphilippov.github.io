unruffled

adjective
calm, smooth (of water)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrʌfəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈrʌfəld / (BrE)
(of water) not disturbed by wind or waves; smooth and calm.

Examples

After the storm passed, the pond returned to its usual unruffled state.
Early in the morning, the lake was perfectly unruffled, mirroring the sky above.
The fisherman preferred to row out only when the sea was unruffled and quiet.
