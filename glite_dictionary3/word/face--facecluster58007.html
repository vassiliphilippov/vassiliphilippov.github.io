to face

verb
cover the surface of something
Pronunciation: / feɪs / (AmE), / feɪs / (BrE)
to cover the front or surface of something, especially for decoration or protection

Examples

face a wall with stone
The old church was faced with limestone for a classic appearance.
They faced the kitchen cabinets with oak veneer to update the room.
Workers are facing the storefront with glass panels to modernize its look.
