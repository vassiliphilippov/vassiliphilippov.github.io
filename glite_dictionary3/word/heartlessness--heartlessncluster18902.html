heartlessness

noun
lack of compassion; cruelty
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtləsnɪs / (BrE)
a lack of compassion or concern for others; cruelty or callousness.

Examples

cold heartlessness
He regretted his earlier heartlessness and tried to make amends for his unkind words.
Her heartlessness shocked everyone at the meeting when she dismissed their concerns without hesitation.
Many critics have accused the policy of heartlessness toward the most vulnerable citizens.

Related Terms

  • antonym – compassion: sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings of others
  • synonym – cruelty: the quality of being cruel; causing pain or suffering
