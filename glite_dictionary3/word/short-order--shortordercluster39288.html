short-order

adjective
prepared quickly (food/cooking)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt ˌɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌɔːdə / (BrE)
(of food or cooking) able to be prepared and served quickly, typically in a diner or café.

Examples

short-order cook
He works as a short-order cook in a busy diner.
Many diners offer short-order meals like burgers and fries.
The café specializes in short-order breakfasts for people on the go.
