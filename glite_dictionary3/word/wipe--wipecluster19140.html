wipe

noun
act of rubbing or wiping
Pronunciation: / waɪp / (AmE), / waɪp / (BrE)
an act of cleaning or drying something by rubbing its surface, typically with a cloth, hand, or tissue

Examples

wipe with a tissue
After finishing the meal, he used a napkin to give his mouth a careful wipe.
He took a wipe at his glasses to clear the smudges.
She gave the table a quick wipe with a damp cloth.

  • synonym – rub: the act of moving something over a surface with pressure
