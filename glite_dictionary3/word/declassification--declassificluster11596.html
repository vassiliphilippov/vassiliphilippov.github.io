declassification

noun
removal of government classification restrictions
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdiːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which the government removes or reduces restrictions on a previously classified document, information, or weapon

Examples

declassification of documents
After months of debate, the committee approved the declassification of sensitive intelligence material.
The declassification of the files revealed new details about the historical incident.
The government announced the declassification of several Cold War documents last year.
