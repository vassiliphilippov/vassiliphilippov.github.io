roadman

noun
young, streetwise urban man (British slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdmæn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdmən / (BrE)
informal British: a young man associated with urban street culture, often seen as streetwise and identified by particular slang, clothing and attitudes.

Examples

urban roadman
A roadman explained the slang used by youths on the bus.
At the market, a young roadman wore a puffer jacket and expensive trainers.
Several roadmen gathered on the corner, talking loudly and nodding to each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym – streetwise: having the experience and knowledge to deal with life in an urban environment
  • synonym – urban youth: a young person who lives in a city and is often associated with contemporary city culture
