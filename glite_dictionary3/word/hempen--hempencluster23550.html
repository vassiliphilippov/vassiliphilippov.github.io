hempen

adjective
made of or like hemp fiber
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmpən / (AmE), / ˈhɛmpən / (BrE)
made of hemp or resembling fibers such as those used for making rope or cordage

Examples

The hempen fibers were strong enough to craft sturdy baskets.
