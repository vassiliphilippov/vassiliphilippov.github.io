pollyfish

noun
bright tropical fish with beaklike mouth
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑliˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɒliˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A brightly colored tropical fish with a parrotlike beak formed by fused teeth; also known as a parrotfish.

Examples

colorful pollyfish
Pollyfishes are known for their vibrant colors and distinctive beak-shaped mouths.
