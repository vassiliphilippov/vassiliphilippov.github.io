Alban Berg

proper noun
Austrian composer; twelve-tone music pioneer
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.bɑːn bɛrɡ / (AmE), / ˈal.bæn bɛːɡ / (BrE)
(1885–1935) An Austrian composer known for his works in the twelve-tone technique developed by Schoenberg, including operas such as 'Wozzeck' and 'Lulu'.
