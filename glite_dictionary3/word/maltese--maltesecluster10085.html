Maltese

noun
cat with bluish-grey fur
Pronunciation: / mɔlˈtiz / (AmE), / mɔːlˈtiːz / (BrE)
A domestic cat of any breed with short, bluish-grey fur, especially as the term is used in the United States.

Examples

Maltese cat
Many people think the Maltese is a specific breed, but it actually refers to the cat's color.
My neighbor adopted a beautiful Maltese that has striking blue-grey fur.
That shelter has several Maltese cats available for adoption this week.
