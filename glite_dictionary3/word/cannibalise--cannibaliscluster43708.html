to cannibalise

verb
take parts from to repair another
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.ə.bəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkæn.ɪ.bə.laɪz / (BrE)
to take parts from one item, especially a machine or vehicle, to use in repairing or creating another

Examples

to cannibalise an old car
The mechanic had to cannibalise an old car to fix the engine in my truck.
They cannibalised one jet to find spare parts for the rest of the fleet.
To keep our computers running, we often cannibalise memory from outdated machines.

Related Terms

  • synonym – repair: to restore something that is broken to good condition
  • synonym – salvage: to save parts from damaged items for reuse
