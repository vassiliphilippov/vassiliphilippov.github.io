sundress

noun
a light sleeveless summer dress
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌdrɛs / (BrE)
a light, loose-fitting sleeveless dress, typically worn in warm weather, with a wide neckline and thin shoulder straps that expose the arms and shoulders
