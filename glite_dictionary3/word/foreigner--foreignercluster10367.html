foreigner

noun
person from another country
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrənər / (AmE), / ˈfɒrənə / (BrE)
a person who comes from a different country or is not a citizen of the country where they are

Examples

foreigner living abroad
As a foreigner, she had to obtain a special permit to work.
He felt like a foreigner when he first moved to Japan.
Many foreigners visit the city every year to experience its culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alien: a person who is not a citizen of the country where they live
  • antonym – native: a person born in a specified place, especially one who has lived there all their life
