ESP

noun
supposed paranormal perception
Pronunciation: / ˌi ˌɛs ˈpi / (AmE), / ˌiː ˌɛs ˈpiː / (BrE)
the ability to gain information about objects, people, or events without using the known senses; extrasensory perception

Examples

ESP to read minds

Television shows about the paranormal often feature characters with ESP.
