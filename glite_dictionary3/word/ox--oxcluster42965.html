ox

noun
castrated adult male cattle used for work
Pronunciation: / ɑks / (AmE), / ɒks / (BrE)
an adult castrated male of the cattle species, especially Bos taurus, used as a draft animal

Examples

working ox
In many traditional societies, an ox is essential for heavy agricultural work.
The farmer used an ox to plow the field each spring.
The pair of oxen pulled the wagon through the muddy road.
