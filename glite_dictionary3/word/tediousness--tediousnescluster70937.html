tediousness

noun
state of being boring or monotonous
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːdiəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈtiːdiəsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being boring and wearisome, especially because of length or monotony.

Examples

tediousness of paperwork
He complained about the tediousness of filling out endless forms at the bank.
She found the tediousness of her daily routine increasingly hard to bear.
The tediousness of the lecture made it difficult for many students to stay awake.
