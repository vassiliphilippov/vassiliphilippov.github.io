tracing

noun
the act of copying by outlining
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪ.sɪŋ /; / ˈtreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪ.sɪŋ /; / ˈtreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making a copy or outline of a drawing or image by following its lines, or the drawing made in this way
