soundness

noun
prudent, sensible judgment
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndnəs / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being based on good judgment and reasoning; prudence and sensibility

Examples

soundness of judgment
Effective leadership depends on the soundness of long-term planning.
The soundness of his decisions has earned him respect among colleagues.
We questioned the soundness of her financial advice before investing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prudence: cautious and sensible behavior; good judgment
  • antonym – rashness: acting without careful consideration or good judgment
