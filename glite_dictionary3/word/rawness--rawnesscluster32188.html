rawness

noun
state of being crude or incomplete
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔnəs / (AmE), / ˈrɔːnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being crude, unfinished, or lacking refinement; incompleteness or imperfection

Examples

the rawness of an idea
Despite the rawness of the proposal, the committee saw potential in its innovative ideas.
Many readers are drawn to the rawness of autobiographies that reveal unfiltered emotions.
The critics noted the rawness of the artist's early work, pointing out its lack of polish.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crudeness: the quality of being in a natural or unrefined state
  • antonym – refinement: the process or result of improving or perfecting
