maharaja

noun
Indian Hindu prince or king of high rank
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːdʒə / (AmE), / ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːdʒə / (BrE)
A Hindu prince or king in India of high rank, especially one ranking above a raja.

Examples

the maharaja of India
British officials attended the coronation of the new maharaja.
The maharaja lived in a magnificent palace at the edge of the city.
The maharaja was known for his generosity and support of the local arts.
