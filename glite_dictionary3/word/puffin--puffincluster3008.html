puffin

noun
seabird with colorful bill
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌfɪn / (AmE), / ˈpʌfɪn / (BrE)
a northern seabird with a short neck and a brightly colored, laterally compressed bill, belonging to two genera

Examples

puffin with colorful beak
During our boat tour, we saw a colony of puffins nesting on the rocky cliffs.
Every spring, hundreds of puffins return to these islands to breed.
The puffin is easily recognized by its colorful beak and comical waddle.
