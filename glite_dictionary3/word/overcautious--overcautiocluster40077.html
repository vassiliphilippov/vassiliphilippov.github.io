overcautious

adjective
excessively cautious
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkɔʃəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkɔːʃəs / (BrE)
Excessively or unnecessarily cautious; taking more care than is needed out of fear of risk or error.

Examples

an overcautious person
Her overcautious approach meant their project progressed much slower than expected.
Sometimes people become overcautious after experiencing a minor accident.
The overcautious investor missed several good opportunities because he feared losing money.
