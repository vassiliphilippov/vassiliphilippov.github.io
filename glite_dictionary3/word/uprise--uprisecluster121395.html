to uprise

verb
rise to one's feet
Pronunciation: / əˈpraɪz / (AmE), / ʌpˈraɪz / (BrE)
to get up or stand up, especially from sitting or lying down

Examples

to uprise from a chair

Each morning in the monastery, the monks would uprise before dawn to begin their prayers.
She uprose quietly, careful not to wake the children still sleeping.
