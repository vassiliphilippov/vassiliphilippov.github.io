sanitised

adjective
made clean and sanitary
Pronunciation: / ˈsænəˌtaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈsanɪtʌɪzd / (BrE)
Made clean and free from dirt, bacteria, or other harmful substances, especially by using a cleaning agent.

Examples

sanitised hospital rooms
After the renovation, all the hospital rooms were thoroughly sanitised to ensure patient safety.
It is important that all kitchen surfaces are sanitised regularly to prevent the spread of germs.
The newly sanitised equipment was ready for use in the laboratory.
