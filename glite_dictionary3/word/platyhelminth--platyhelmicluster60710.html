platyhelminth

noun
flat-bodied worm of phylum Platyhelminthes
Pronunciation: / ˌplæt.i.hɛlˈmɪnθ / (AmE), / ˌplæt.ɪ.hɛlˈmɪnθ / (BrE)
A worm of the phylum Platyhelminthes, characterized by a flattened body, which may be parasitic or free-living.
