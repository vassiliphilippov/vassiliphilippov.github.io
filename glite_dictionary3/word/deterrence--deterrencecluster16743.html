deterrence

noun
the act of discouraging by warning
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɝːəns /; / dɪˈtɜɹəns /; / dəˈtɜɹəns / (AmE), / dɪˈtɛr.əns /; / dɪˈtɛɹəns /; / dɪˈtɛrəns / (BrE)
the act or process of discouraging someone from doing something by making them fear the possible consequences
