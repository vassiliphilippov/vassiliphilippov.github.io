preset

adjective
set in advance; prearranged
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌsɛt / (BrE)
(of a value, condition, or arrangement) set in advance or before use, typically by a manufacturer or in preparation for later use.

Examples

preset radio station
Each radio button is tuned to a preset station for convenience.
She followed the preset instructions to configure her new device.
The oven will heat to a preset temperature when you select the baking mode.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prearranged: arranged or decided before something happens
  • synonym – predetermined: established or decided in advance
