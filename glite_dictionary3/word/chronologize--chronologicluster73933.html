to chronologize

verb
establish the order of events in time
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɑlədʒaɪz / (AmE), / krəˈnɒlədʒaɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or establish the order of events in the sequence in which they occurred in time

The historian needed to chronologize the major events of the war to provide a clear timeline.
