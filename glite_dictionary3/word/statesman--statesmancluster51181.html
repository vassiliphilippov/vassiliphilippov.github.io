statesman

noun
respected political leader (male)
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪts.mən / (AmE), / ˈsteɪts.mən / (BrE)
A man who is a respected, experienced, and wise leader in national or international affairs, especially one who is involved in government or diplomacy.

Examples

respected statesman
He was honored as a visionary statesman who shaped his country's future.
Many hope the new senator will become a respected statesman in the years to come.
The former president is regarded as a statesman for his role in promoting peace talks.
