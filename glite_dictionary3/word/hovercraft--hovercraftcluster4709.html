hovercraft

noun
vehicle using air cushion to travel over surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌvɚˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈhʌvəkrɑːft / (BrE)
a vehicle that travels over water or land on a cushion of air produced by jets or fans

Examples

ride a hovercraft
A hovercraft can operate on beaches, marshes, and open water.
The military deployed a hovercraft to quickly cross the flooded area.
Tourists enjoyed the thrilling ride across the bay on a hovercraft.
