bum

noun
an idle or lazy person
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
a person who is lazy, avoids work, or has no permanent home or job (informal, sometimes insulting)

Examples

lazy bum
Don’t be such a bum—get up and find a job!
He spent most of his twenties as a bum, doing nothing all day.
Some people called him a bum, but he was just taking some time off.
He offered his sandwich to a bum sitting on the park bench.
Some people wrongly assume every bum chooses to live on the streets.
The old bum by the station told fascinating stories of his travels.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drifter: a person who moves from place to place without a permanent home or job
  • synonym – loafer: a person who is habitually lazy and does not work
  • synonym – slacker: a person who avoids work or effort
  • synonym – tramp: a person who travels from place to place on foot, usually doing odd jobs or begging
  • antonym – workaholic: a person who compulsively works long and hard
