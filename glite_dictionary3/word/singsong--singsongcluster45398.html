singsong

adjective
monotonous, chantlike speech
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋˌsɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋˌsɒŋ / (BrE)
spoken or pronounced with a monotonous, rhythmic, or chantlike cadence.

Examples

singsong voice
His explanation came out in a singsong tone, making it hard to take seriously.
She recited the poem in a singsong voice that put the children to sleep.
The tour guide spoke with a singsong rhythm that was oddly hypnotic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – monotone: speech or sound in a single, unvaried tone
