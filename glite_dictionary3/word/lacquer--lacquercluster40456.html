to lacquer

verb
to coat with lacquer
Pronunciation: / ˈlækər / (AmE), / ˈlækə / (BrE)
to cover or coat an object with a glossy, protective varnish called lacquer

Examples

to lacquer a surface
Artisans carefully lacquer decorative boxes to achieve a flawless finish.
It took several hours to lacquer all the shelves in the workshop.
She decided to lacquer the old wooden table to restore its shine.
