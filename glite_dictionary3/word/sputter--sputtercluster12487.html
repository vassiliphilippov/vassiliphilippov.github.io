sputter

noun
spattering or explosive noise
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌtər / (AmE), / ˈspʌtə / (BrE)
A series of explosive or spattering sounds, often made by an engine, fire, or similar source.

Examples

engine sputter
A quiet sputter came from the candle as it finally went out.
The sputter of the old car's engine indicated it was struggling to start.
We heard the sputter of fireworks echoing across the lake at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spatter: a series of short, sharp sounds typically made by liquid hitting a surface
