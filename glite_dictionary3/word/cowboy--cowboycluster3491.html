cowboy

noun
reckless or irresponsible person (esp. driver)
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ.bɔɪ / (BrE)
a person who is reckless or irresponsible, especially in driving vehicles

Examples

reckless cowboy driver
I don't trust that mechanic; he has a reputation for being a cowboy.
That new delivery driver is a real cowboy—he nearly hit three cars this morning.
The roads would be safer if there were fewer cowboys behind the wheel.
