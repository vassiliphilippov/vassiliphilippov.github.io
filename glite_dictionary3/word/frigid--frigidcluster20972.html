frigid

adjective
extremely cold
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪdʒɪd / (AmE), / ˈfrɪdʒɪd / (BrE)
Very cold in temperature; extremely cold.

Examples

frigid winter night
Despite his warm coat, the mountain air remained frigid all night.
She shivered as she dipped her toes into the frigid water of the lake.
The frigid wind made walking outside nearly impossible.
