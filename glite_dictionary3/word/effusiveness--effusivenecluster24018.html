effusiveness

noun
quality of being openly expressive or enthusiastic
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfjuzɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈfjuːzɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being openly expressive of emotions, especially in a friendly or enthusiastic way

Examples

warm effusiveness
Her effusiveness made everyone in the room feel immediately welcome.
Some people find too much effusiveness overwhelming, preferring a quieter approach.
The effusiveness of his greeting took her by surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym – enthusiasm: intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • antonym – reserve: a reluctance to show feelings or express emotions
