Carelian

noun
Finnic language of Karelia
Pronunciation: / kəˈriːliən / (AmE), / kəˈrɪːliən / (BrE)
the Finnic language spoken by the people of Karelia, a region in Russia and Finland

Examples

Carelian language
He can speak Finnish, Russian, and Carelian fluently.
Linguists are working to preserve Carelian among younger generations in both Russia and Finland.
The Carelian language has several dialects unique to different parts of the Karelia region.
