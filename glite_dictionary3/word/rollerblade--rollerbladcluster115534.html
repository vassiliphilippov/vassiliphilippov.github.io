Rollerblade

noun
type of in-line skate
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɚˌbleɪd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləbleɪd / (BrE)
A type of in-line skate with wheels arranged in a single line, originally a trademark but commonly used for any such skate.
