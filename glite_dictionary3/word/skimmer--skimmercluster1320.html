skimmer

noun
stiff straw hat with flat crown
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪmɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɪmə / (BrE)
a stiff hat made of straw with a flat crown and a flat brim, traditionally worn by men in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, especially in summer.

Examples

straw skimmer hat
He wore a crisp white suit and a skimmer to the garden party.
The band played as men in skimmer hats paraded along the riverside.
Vintage skimmers are now collector's items prized by fashion enthusiasts.
