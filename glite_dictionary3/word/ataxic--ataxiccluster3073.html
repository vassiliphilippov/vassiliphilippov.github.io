ataxic

adjective
lacking muscle coordination (medical)
Pronunciation: / əˈtæksɪk / (AmE), / əˈtæksɪk / (BrE)
lacking normal muscle coordination, especially as a result of a neurological disorder; marked by or affected with ataxia.

Examples

ataxic movements
Doctors noted several ataxic symptoms during the neurological examination.
Her movements became increasingly ataxic after the illness progressed.
The patient had an ataxic gait due to damage in his cerebellum.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ataxia: loss of full control of bodily movements
  • antonym – coordinated: having smooth, controlled movement; the opposite of lacking coordination
