bustle

noun
energetic or noisy activity
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌs.əl / (AmE), / ˈbʌs.əl / (BrE)
energetic or noisy activity and movement, often in a busy setting

Examples

morning bustle
In the morning bustle of the train station, she almost missed her train.
The bustle of the city can be overwhelming for some people.
There was a bustle of activity in the kitchen as dinner was being prepared.
