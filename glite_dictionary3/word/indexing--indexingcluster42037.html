indexing

noun
process of creating or applying an index
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌdɛksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪndɛksɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of classifying and providing an index to make items easier to find or retrieve

Examples

indexing of documents
Efficient indexing of files can save a company valuable time and resources.
Modern libraries rely on indexing to help readers locate information quickly.
The software automates the indexing of documents for faster search results.

  • synonym – cataloguing: the process of making a systematic list of items, usually with descriptive details
