greaseproof

adjective
resistant to grease or oil
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrisˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈɡriːsˌpruːf / (BrE)
resistant to penetration by grease, oil, or wax, especially referring to paper or packaging materials.

Examples

greaseproof paper sheets
I need some greaseproof sheets for baking cookies this afternoon.
It's best to wrap the cake in greaseproof paper to keep it fresh.
They lined the pans with greaseproof material to prevent sticking.
