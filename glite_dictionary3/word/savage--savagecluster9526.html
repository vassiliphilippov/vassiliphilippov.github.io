savage

noun
an (offensive) term for an uncivilized person
Pronunciation: / ˈsævɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsavɪdʒ / (BrE)
(offensive, archaic) A member of a people regarded as primitive or uncivilized.

Examples

a savage tribe
In old novels, indigenous people were often referred to as savages, which is offensive today.
Such language reflects outdated views that see others as mere savages.
The explorers wrongly described the local inhabitants as savages.
