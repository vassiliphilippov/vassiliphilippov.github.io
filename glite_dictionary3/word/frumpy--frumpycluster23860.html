frumpy

adjective
dowdy or old-fashioned, lacking style
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌm.pi / (AmE), / ˈfrʌm.pi / (BrE)
(of a person or their clothes) old-fashioned, unattractive, and lacking in style or neatness; dowdy.

frumpy clothes

The frumpy dress she wore was terribly out of fashion.
