lightness

noun
small weight; not heavy
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having little weight; the state of being comparatively light.

Examples

lightness of material
Engineers valued the lightness of aluminum for the aircraft's frame.
She admired the lightness of the summer dress, perfect for warm weather.
The lightness of the materials made the backpack easier to carry.

Related Terms

  • antonym – heaviness: the property of being heavy in weight
