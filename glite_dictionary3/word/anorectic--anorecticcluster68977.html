anorectic

noun
person with anorexia nervosa
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈrɛktɪk / (AmE), / ˌanəˈrɛktɪk / (BrE)
a person who suffers from anorexia nervosa, an eating disorder characterized by an abnormally low body weight and fear of gaining weight.

Examples

young anorectic
After months of counseling, the young anorectic began to regain a healthier outlook on food.
Family support is crucial for the recovery of an anorectic.
The clinic specializes in treating anorectic patients with a multidisciplinary approach.
