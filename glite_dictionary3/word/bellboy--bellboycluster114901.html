bellboy

noun
male hotel luggage and errand attendant
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛlˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɛl.bɔɪ / (BrE)
a male hotel employee who carries guests' luggage and runs errands for them

Examples

bellboy carries luggage
She tipped the bellboy for bringing extra towels to their suite.
The bellboy carried our suitcases to the hotel room and explained how the safe worked.
When we arrived, a bellboy greeted us at the entrance and offered to take our luggage.
