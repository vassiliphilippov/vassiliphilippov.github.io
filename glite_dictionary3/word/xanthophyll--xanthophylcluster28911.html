xanthophyll

noun
yellow plant pigment; a carotenoid
Pronunciation: / ˈzænθəˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˈzanθəˌfɪl / (BrE)
a yellow pigment (carotenoid) found in plants, animal fats, and egg yolks, responsible for yellow color in some plant tissues and involved in photosynthesis
