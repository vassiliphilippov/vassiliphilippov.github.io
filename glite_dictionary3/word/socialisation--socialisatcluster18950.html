socialisation

noun
conversion to collective or state ownership
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊʃələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsəʊʃəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of transferring private property or businesses to collective or government ownership, especially according to socialist principles

Examples

socialisation of companies
After the revolution, there was a rapid socialisation of all major industries.
Debates arose regarding the benefits and drawbacks of the socialisation of railways.
The socialisation of healthcare aimed to make services accessible to everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nationalization: the process of bringing an industry or assets under government control
  • antonym – privatization: the transfer of business, industry, or service from public to private ownership and control
