minter

noun
creator of blockchain tokens/NFTs
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪntər / (AmE), / ˈmɪntə / (BrE)
a person, organization, or service that creates (mints) new digital tokens, non-fungible tokens (NFTs), or other blockchain-based assets.

Examples

minter of NFTs
The NFT project's lead minter scheduled the token drop for next Tuesday evening.
Small artists often rely on platform features that let a minter set royalty percentages.
Several minters competed to create the rare token, spurring intense bidding after the auction.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mint (verb): to create or issue coins or digital tokens
  • synonym – token creator: a person or service that issues or creates digital tokens or NFTs on a blockchain
