Pandanales

noun
botanical order of flowering plants
Pronunciation: / ˌpændəˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌpandəˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of monocotyledonous flowering plants that includes the families Typhaceae, Sparganiaceae, and Pandanaceae.
