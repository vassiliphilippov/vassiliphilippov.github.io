wordily

adverb
in a verbose manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːdɪli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdɪli / (BrE)
In a verbose or overly detailed manner, using more words than necessary.

Examples

to speak wordily
He explained his position wordily, making the meeting much longer than expected.
She tends to respond wordily to even the simplest questions.
The instructions were written wordily and could have been much simpler.
