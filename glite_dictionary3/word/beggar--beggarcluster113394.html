to beggar

verb
reduce to poverty
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛɡər / (AmE), / ˈbɛɡə / (BrE)
To reduce someone to poverty; to make someone so poor that they have to beg for a living.

Examples

to beggar a family
Continuous war had beggared the once rich nation beyond recognition.
Such reckless spending could easily beggar even the wealthiest of individuals.
The collapse of the company beggared hundreds of workers and their families.

  • synonym – impoverish: to make someone poor
