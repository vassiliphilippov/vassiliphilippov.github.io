stoep

noun
a small front porch or steps
Pronunciation: / stuːp / (AmE), / stuːp / (BrE)
a small porch or set of steps at the front entrance of a house, especially in southern Africa.

Examples

sit on the stoep
Children often gather on the stoep to play games after school.
Her stoep was decorated with potted plants and a welcome mat.
We sat on the stoep enjoying the cool evening breeze.
