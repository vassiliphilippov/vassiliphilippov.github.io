unappetizingness

noun
quality of being unpleasant to eat
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈapɪtʌɪzɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unpleasant to eat or lacking appeal to the appetite

Examples

the unappetizingness of food
His experiment failed partly because of the unappetizingness of the test meal.
She was put off by the unappetizingness of the leftovers in the fridge.
The unappetizingness of the cafeteria food discouraged many students from eating lunch.
