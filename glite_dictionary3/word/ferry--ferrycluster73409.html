ferry

noun
transport service by boat or aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˈfɛri / (BrE)
a service or means of transporting people or goods across water or, less commonly, by air, usually at regular intervals.

Examples

ferry service schedule
During the storm, the ferry service was suspended for safety reasons.
The last ferry leaves the island at 7 p.m., so don't miss it.
They took a ferry across the river to reach the mainland.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bridge: a structure for crossing water or a gap, contrasting with ferry service
  • synonym – ferryboat: a vessel used for ferrying
