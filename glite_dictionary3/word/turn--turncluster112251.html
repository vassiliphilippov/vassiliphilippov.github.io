to turn

verb
go bad or spoil (food/drink)
Pronunciation: / tɝn / (AmE), / tɜːn / (BrE)
(of food or drink) to go bad or sour; to spoil.

Examples

milk turns sour
If you leave the milk out of the fridge, it will turn quickly.
The weather was so hot that the butter began to turn by midday.
This juice has turned; you shouldn't drink it anymore.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sour: to become sour or go off (of food/drink)
  • synonym – spoil: to become unfit for consumption or use
