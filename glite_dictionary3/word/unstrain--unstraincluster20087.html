to unstrain

verb
make relaxed or less tense
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstreɪn / (AmE), / ʌnˈstreɪn / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to feel relaxed or less tense

Examples

to unstrain your mind
A quiet evening at home helped to unstrain her nerves.
Deep breathing exercises can unstrain your body after a long day.
The massage therapist worked to unstrain his tense shoulders.

Related Terms

  • antonym – tense: to make or become tight or strained
