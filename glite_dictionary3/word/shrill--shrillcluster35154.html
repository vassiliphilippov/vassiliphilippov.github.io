to shrill

verb
make a high-pitched, piercing cry
Pronunciation: / ʃrɪl / (AmE), / ʃrɪl / (BrE)
to utter or make a high-pitched, piercing sound or cry.

Examples

to shrill loudly
A whistle shrilled across the field, signaling the end of the match.
She shrilled his name, desperate to get his attention.
The child suddenly shrilled in fright when she saw the dog.

  • synonym – scream: to make a loud, high-pitched cry
  • synonym – yell: to shout loudly
