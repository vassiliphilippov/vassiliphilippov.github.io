trigeminal

noun
the main sensory nerve of the face
Pronunciation: / traɪˈdʒɛmənəl / (AmE), / traɪˈdʒɛmɪnəl / (BrE)
the fifth cranial nerve responsible for sensation in the face and motor functions such as biting and chewing

Examples

trigeminal nerve pain
Neuralgia can sometimes affect the trigeminal nerve, causing severe facial pain.
The trigeminal system is essential for detecting facial sensations such as touch and temperature.
The dentist checked for any signs of trigeminal nerve damage during the exam.
