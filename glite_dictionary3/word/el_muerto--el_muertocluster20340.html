El Muerto

proper noun
Andean mountain on Argentina–Chile border
Pronunciation: / el ˈmwɛrtoʊ / (AmE), / el ˈmwɛətəʊ / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Andes on the border between Argentina and Chile, rising to 21,457 feet (6,544 meters)

Examples

El Muerto mountain peak
Climbers from around the world attempt to reach the summit of El Muerto each year.
