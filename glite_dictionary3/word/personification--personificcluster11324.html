personification

noun
representation of ideas as people
Pronunciation: / pɚˌsɑnəfəˈkeɪʃən /; / pərˌsɑːnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / pəˌsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of representing an idea, quality, or thing as if it were a person, especially in literature
