mousse

noun
foamy hair styling product
Pronunciation: / mus / (AmE), / muːs / (BrE)
A light, foamy substance dispensed from an aerosol can, used to style and give volume to hair.

Examples

hair mousse
She applied some mousse to her damp hair before blow-drying it.
This mousse holds my curls in place without making them stiff.
Use a volumizing mousse for a fuller hairstyle that lasts all day.

  • synonym – gel: a thick, jelly-like hair styling product
