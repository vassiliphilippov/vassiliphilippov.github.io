mown

adjective
cut grass or vegetation
Pronunciation: / moʊn / (AmE), / məʊn / (BrE)
(of grass or vegetation) cut down by a hand tool or machine.

Examples

mown grass
Please keep to the mown paths to avoid damaging wildflowers.
The field was covered in mown hay, ready for baling.
The lawn looked neat and tidy after being mown.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unmown: (of grass or vegetation) not cut down or left standing
