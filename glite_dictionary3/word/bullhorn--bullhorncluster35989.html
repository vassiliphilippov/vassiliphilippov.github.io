bullhorn

noun
portable loudspeaker with microphone
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a portable loudspeaker with an integrated microphone and amplifier, used to make one's voice louder in public spaces

Examples

bullhorn at a protest
Protesters shouted their demands through a bullhorn during the rally.
She forgot to bring the bullhorn to the outdoor event, making it hard to be heard.
The police officer addressed the crowd using a bullhorn so everyone could hear the instructions.
