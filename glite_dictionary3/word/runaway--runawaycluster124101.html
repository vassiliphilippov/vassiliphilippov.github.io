runaway

noun
someone who flees from a bad situation
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a person who has left a place or situation secretly and unexpectedly, often to escape trouble or difficulties

Examples

missing runaway child
After weeks of searching, her parents finally located the missing runaway.
Many runaways seek shelter in the city to escape difficult family situations.
The police found the young runaway hiding in a nearby train station.
