horn

noun
noisemaker for parties and games
Pronunciation: / hɔrn / (AmE), / hɔːn / (BrE)
a noisemaker, often used at parties or sporting events, that produces a loud sound when blown into

Examples

party horn
At midnight on New Year's Eve, everyone started making noise with a horn.
He bought a colorful horn to celebrate his team's victory.
The children took turns blowing the horn at the birthday party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – whistle: a device that emits a sound when blown, often used for signaling
