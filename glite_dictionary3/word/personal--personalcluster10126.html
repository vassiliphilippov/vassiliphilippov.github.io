personal

adjective
of or relating to one's body
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəl / (BrE)
relating to a person's body or physical self rather than to their mind, emotions, or social role.

Examples

personal hygiene
Good personal hygiene is important for preventing illness.
She keeps certain health matters strictly personal and private.
The patient needed help with his personal care after surgery.
