construct

noun
abstract theoretical idea or concept
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrʌkt / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkt / (BrE)
an abstract or theoretical idea, concept, or model that is inferred from and used to explain specific observations or data

Examples

a social construct
'Freedom' is a social construct that varies across cultures.
Intelligence is often considered a construct that cannot be directly observed but is inferred from tests.
The theory introduces several new constructs to explain human motivation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – concept: an idea or principle formed in the mind
  • synonym – hypothesis: a proposed explanation for a phenomenon
