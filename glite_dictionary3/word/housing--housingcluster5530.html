housing

noun
buildings where people live collectively
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
buildings or structures where people live, considered collectively; accommodation provided for people

Examples

affordable housing
Affordable housing is a major concern in big cities.
The government is planning to build new housing for low-income families.
There is an urgent need for student housing near the university.
