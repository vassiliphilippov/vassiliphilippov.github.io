to potter

verb
move or work in a leisurely way
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːtər /; / ˈpɑtər / (AmE), / ˈpɒtə / (BrE)
to move or work in a relaxed, unhurried, or aimless way, often doing small or unimportant tasks
