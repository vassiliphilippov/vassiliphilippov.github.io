pygmy

noun
an unusually small person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡmi / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmi / (BrE)
a person or thing that is much smaller than usual, typically by comparison with others of its kind or class

Examples

a pygmy animal
In the land of giants, he felt like a pygmy by comparison.
Scientists discovered a pygmy elephant on the remote island.
The species has a pygmy variant that rarely grows over 40 centimeters tall.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dwarf: a person of unusually short stature; something very small
  • antonym – giant: a person or thing of extremely large size
