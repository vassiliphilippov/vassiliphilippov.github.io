to choke

verb
fail under pressure (informal)
Pronunciation: / tʃoʊk / (AmE), / tʃəʊk / (BrE)
to fail to perform well in a critical situation because of nervousness or pressure

Examples

to choke under pressure
Despite her strong training, she choked during the finals and missed the last shot.
He usually plays well, but he choked in the championship match.
Many athletes fear they will choke when the pressure is on.

Related Terms

  • antonym – excel: to perform exceptionally well, especially under pressure
  • synonym – flop: to perform badly or fail completely, especially in a high-pressure situation
  • synonym – panic: become suddenly very anxious or fearful, especially so as to lose control
