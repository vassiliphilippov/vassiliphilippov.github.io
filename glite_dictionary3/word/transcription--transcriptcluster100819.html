transcription

noun
written or printed copy of spoken words
Pronunciation: / trænˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / trænˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
a written or printed version of spoken words, or something copied from one medium to another

Examples

transcription of speech
Accurate transcription ensures that spoken content can be preserved in written form.
I requested a transcription of the interview to review the details.
The court reporter provided a transcription of the witness's testimony for the records.

Related Terms

  • synonym – transcript: a written or printed version of something, especially a record of spoken words
