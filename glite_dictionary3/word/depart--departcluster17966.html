to depart

verb
leave an association or position
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑrt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːt / (BrE)
to leave or withdraw from an association, position, or place, especially formally or permanently

Examples

to depart a company
After ten years with the company, she decided to depart and pursue new opportunities.
He will depart from the board at the end of the month.
Several members departed the group after a disagreement about the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym – join: to become a member or part of an association; opposite action to depart
  • synonym – leave: to go away from somewhere or someone
  • synonym – resign: to leave a job or official position formally
