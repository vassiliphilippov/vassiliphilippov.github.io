to suit

verb
to be convenient or acceptable to
Pronunciation: / suːt / (AmE), / suːt /; / sjuːt / (BrE)
to be convenient, acceptable, or appropriate for someone; to fit someone's needs, wishes, or character

Examples

to suit your needs

It suited her nature to work independently rather than in a large team.
That color really suits you; you should wear it more often.
The quiet lifestyle of the countryside suits him much better than city life.
