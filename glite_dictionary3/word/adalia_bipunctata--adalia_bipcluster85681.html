Adalia bipunctata

noun
species of ladybird beetle (two-spot)
Pronunciation: / əˈdeɪliə baɪˌpʌŋkˈteɪtə / (AmE), / əˈdeɪliə bɪˌpʌŋkˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
A species of ladybird beetle with a red body and one black spot on each wing cover, commonly known as the two-spot ladybird.
