promenade

noun
square dance figure; couples march in a circle
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑːməˈneɪd, ˈprɑːməˌneɪd /; / ˌprɑː.məˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌprɒməˈnɑːd, ˈprɒməˌnɑːd /; / ˌprɒm.əˈnɑːd / (BrE)
a formal march or dance figure at a ball or in square dancing

Examples

square dance promenade
After completing the do-si-do, the couples moved smoothly into a promenade.
