to addle

verb
become rotten (esp. eggs)
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈæd.əl / (BrE)
(of eggs or other organic matter) to become rotten or putrid.

Examples

addle eggs
If eggs are kept too long, they may addle and smell unpleasant.
In the heat, food quickly starts to addle and attracts flies.
Neglected in the nest, the bird's eggs began to addle.
