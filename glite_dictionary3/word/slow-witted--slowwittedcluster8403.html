slow-witted

adjective
not quick to understand
Pronunciation: / sloʊˈwɪtɪd / (AmE), / sləʊˈwɪtɪd / (BrE)
not quick to understand or think; lacking mental sharpness or intelligence.

Examples

a slow-witted person
Despite his slow-witted manner, he was kind and generous to everyone he met.
Some found the movie's humor too slow-witted for their taste.
The detective grew frustrated with his slow-witted partner during the investigation.
