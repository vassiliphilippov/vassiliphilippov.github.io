tenter

noun
frame for stretching and drying cloth
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛntər / (AmE), / ˈtɛntə / (BrE)
a frame fitted with hooks for stretching and drying cloth after it has been woven and washed

Examples

tenter frame for cloth
Old mills often contained a tenter in their yard for finishing cloth.
The woolen fabric was carefully placed on the tenter to prevent shrinkage while drying.
Workers used the hooks of a tenter to keep the fabric taut during the drying process.
