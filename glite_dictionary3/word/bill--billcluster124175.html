bill

noun
the front brim of a cap
Pronunciation: / bɪl / (AmE), / bɪl / (BrE)
the projecting brim at the front of a cap or hat, designed to shade the eyes

Examples

bill of a cap
A sticker was still attached to the bill of his new cap.
He pulled down the bill of his cap and continued walking.
The bill of her baseball hat kept the sun out of her eyes.
