umbelliferous

adjective
relating to the carrot family of plants
Pronunciation: / ʌmˌbɛlɪˈfɛrəs / (AmE), / ʌmˌbɛlɪˈfɛrəs / (BrE)
(of a plant) belonging to or relating to the family Umbelliferae (Apiaceae), characterized by umbrella-shaped clusters of flowers.

Examples

umbelliferous plant family
Some herbs, such as parsley and dill, are umbelliferous plants.
The distinctive flower clusters make it easy to recognize umbelliferous species.
Wild carrot is one of the most widespread umbelliferous weeds in Europe.
