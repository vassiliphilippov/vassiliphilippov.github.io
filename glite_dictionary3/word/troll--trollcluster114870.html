to troll

verb
cause to move in a circle
Pronunciation: / troʊl / (AmE), / trəʊl / (BrE)
to cause to move round and round, especially in a playful or circular manner

Examples

to troll a ring
He trolled the metal ring around the garden, delighting his little brother.
She trolled the toy car in circles on the kitchen floor.
The children trolled their hoops along the path, laughing loudly.
