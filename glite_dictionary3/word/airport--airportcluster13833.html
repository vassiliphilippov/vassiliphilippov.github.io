airport

noun
airfield with passenger and cargo facilities
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpɔːt / (BrE)
A place where aircraft regularly take off and land, equipped with facilities such as control towers, hangars, and accommodations for passengers and cargo.

Examples

airport terminal
The city is planning to expand the airport to accommodate more international flights.
The plane landed safely at the airport despite the heavy fog.
We waited for her arrival in the main terminal of the airport.
