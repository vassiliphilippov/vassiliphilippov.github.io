arteria pancreatica

noun
artery supplying the pancreas
Pronunciation: / ɑːˌtɪriə pæŋkriˈætɪkə / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪəriə paŋkrɪˈatɪkə / (BrE)
A branch of the splenic artery that supplies blood to the pancreas.

Examples

arteria pancreatica supplies pancreas
Damage to the arteria pancreatica can compromise pancreatic function.
Surgeons must carefully avoid the arteria pancreatica during procedures on the pancreas.
The arteria pancreatica is essential for the vascularization of the pancreas.
