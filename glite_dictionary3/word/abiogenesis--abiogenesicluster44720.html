abiogenesis

noun
origin of life from nonliving matter
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.baɪ.oʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.baɪ.əʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the original development of living organisms from nonliving matter; spontaneous generation

Examples

abiogenesis of life
Early ideas about abiogenesis were eventually replaced by more evidence-based scientific theories.
Scientists have long debated whether abiogenesis is a plausible explanation for the origin of life on Earth.
