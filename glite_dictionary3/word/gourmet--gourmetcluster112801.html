gourmet

noun
person with refined taste in food and drink
Pronunciation: / ɡʊrˈmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊə.meɪ / (BrE)
a person who enjoys and has a refined taste in good food and drink

Examples

gourmet food lover
As a true gourmet, he travels the world trying out the best cuisine each country has to offer.
She is considered a gourmet because she can tell the difference between subtle flavors in every dish.
The restaurant was recommended by a famous gourmet who writes food columns.
