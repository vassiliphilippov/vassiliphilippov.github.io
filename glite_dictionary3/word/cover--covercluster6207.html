cover

noun
false identity for concealment
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
a false identity and background used to conceal a person's real purpose, especially for an undercover agent

Examples

cover identity
As a spy, he maintained a cover as an international businessman.
Her diplomatic role was only a cover for her intelligence work.
The police officer's cover was blown when a suspect recognized him.
