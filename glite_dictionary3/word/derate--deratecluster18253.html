to derate

verb
reduce rated capacity of equipment
Pronunciation: / diˈreɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈreɪt / (BrE)
to lower the maximum rated capacity or capability of electrical or mechanical equipment for safety or performance reasons

Examples

to derate equipment capacity
