improbable

adjective
unlikely to happen or be true
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɑːbəbl̩ /; / ɪmˈprɑːbəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɒbəbl̩ /; / ɪmˈprɒbəbəl / (BrE)
not likely to be true or to happen; very unlikely
