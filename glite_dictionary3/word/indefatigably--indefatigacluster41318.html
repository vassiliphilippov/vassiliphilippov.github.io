indefatigably

adverb
in a tireless or persistent manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈfatɪɡəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows no signs of tiring or giving up; with tireless energy.

Examples

work indefatigably for change
He campaigned indefatigably for environmental awareness throughout his life.
She indefatigably pursued her research despite many setbacks.
The volunteers worked indefatigably to help those in need after the disaster.
