anaesthetic

adjective
lacking sensation; insensible
Pronunciation: / ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌanɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
Lacking physical or emotional sensation; characterized by insensibility.

Examples

anaesthetic feeling
After the accident, his hand felt completely anaesthetic for several minutes.
He stared at the wall with an anaesthetic detachment, oblivious to the chaos around him.
The trauma left her anaesthetic to the pain that followed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insensible: lacking physical sensation or awareness
  • antonym – sensitive: quick to detect or respond to slight changes, signals, or influences
