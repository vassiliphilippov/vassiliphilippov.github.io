competence

noun
ability to do something well
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmpətəns / (AmE), / ˈkɒmpɪtəns / (BrE)
the ability to do something successfully or efficiently; the state of being adequately or well qualified in knowledge and skill

Examples

professional competence
Demonstrating linguistic competence is essential when applying for the translator position.
Her competence as an engineer was quickly recognized by her colleagues.
The company hired him because of his proven competence in project management.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ability: possession of the means or skills to do something
  • antonym – incompetence: lack of ability or qualification for a task
  • synonym – proficiency: advanced skill or expertise in a subject or activity
