naphthol

noun
a phenol derived from naphthalene
Pronunciation: / ˈnæfˌθɔl / (AmE), / ˈnæfˌθɒl / (BrE)
either of two crystalline phenol compounds derived from naphthalene, commonly used in dye manufacture and as chemical intermediates

Examples

naphthol dye production
1. Naphthol is used as an intermediate in the production of azo dyes.
2. There are two isomers of naphthol: alpha-naphthol and beta-naphthol.
3. The laboratory stocked both forms of naphthol for different chemical reactions.
