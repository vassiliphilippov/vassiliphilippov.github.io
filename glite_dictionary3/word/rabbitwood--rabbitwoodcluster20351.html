rabbitwood

noun
parasitic shrub of southeastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪtˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈræbɪtˌwʊd / (BrE)
A shrub native to the southeastern United States, parasitic on hemlock roots, with sparse spikes of greenish flowers and pulpy drupes.
