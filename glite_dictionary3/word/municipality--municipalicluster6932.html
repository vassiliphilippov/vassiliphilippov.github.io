municipality

noun
urban district with self-government
Pronunciation: / mjuˌnɪsɪˈpæləti /; / mjuːˌnɪsɪˈpæləti / (AmE), / mjuːˌnɪsɪˈpælɪti / (BrE)
a town or city with its own local government and the people or officials who run it
