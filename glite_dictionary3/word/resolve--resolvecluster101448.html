to resolve

verb
decide firmly or make a determination
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɑlv / (AmE), / rɪˈzɒlv / (BrE)
to make a firm decision about something; to decide firmly

Examples

to resolve to act
After much thought, she resolved to move to a new city and start anew.
At the start of the year, many people resolve to exercise more and eat healthier.
He resolved never to repeat the same mistake again in his career.
