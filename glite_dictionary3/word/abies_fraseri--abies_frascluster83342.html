Abies fraseri

noun
a species of fir tree from the southern Appalachians
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz ˈfreɪ.zəɹ.i / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.biː.iːz ˈfreɪ.zər.i / (BrE)
A small, fast-growing but short-lived species of fir native to the southern Appalachian Mountains, similar to balsam fir but with very short leaves.
