hardboard

noun
compressed wood fibre board
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdbɔːd / (BrE)
a cheap, rigid material made from compressed wood fibres and synthetic resin, used in building and furniture.

Examples

hardboard sheet
The shelves were made from hardboard and painted white to match the desk.
We used a sheet of hardboard to cover the hole in the wall temporarily.
You can easily cut hardboard with a sharp knife or saw for your project.
