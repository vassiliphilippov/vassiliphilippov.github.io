laguna

noun
body of water separated by sand or coral reef
Pronunciation: / ləˈɡuːnə / (AmE), / ləˈɡuːnə / (BrE)
a body of water separated from a larger body of water by a reef of sand or coral; a lagoon

Examples

laguna with coral reef
Many bird species make their home in the calm waters of the laguna.
The coral reef protected the shallow laguna from strong ocean currents.
We visited a beautiful laguna surrounded by lush mangroves during our trip to the coast.
