harmoniser

noun
person who brings things into harmony
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmənʌɪzə / (BrE)
a person who mediates to bring different things into harmonious agreement with each other

Examples

harmoniser of conflicts
As a skilled harmoniser, he managed to settle disputes in the team quickly.
She acted as the harmoniser during the negotiation, ensuring both sides reached a fair agreement.
Without a harmoniser, the project group remained divided over their approach.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arbitrator: a person who settles a dispute or brings about an agreement
  • synonym – mediator: a person who mediates between two parties
