tyiyn

noun
unit of currency in Kyrgyzstan
Pronunciation: / tiˈjin / (AmE), / tiˈjiːn / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Kyrgyzstan, equal to one one-hundredth of a som.

Examples

one tyiyn coin
During my trip to Kyrgyzstan, I collected a tyiyn as a souvenir.
Inflation made the value of the tyiyn practically negligible for daily transactions.
The price of a bus ticket is often listed in soms and tyiyns.
