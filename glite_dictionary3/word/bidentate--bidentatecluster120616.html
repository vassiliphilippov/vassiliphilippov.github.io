bidentate

adjective
having two toothlike projections
Pronunciation: / baɪˈdɛnteɪt / (AmE), / baɪˈdɛnteɪt / (BrE)
having two toothlike projections or points, each of which is itself toothed or notched.

Examples

bidentate ligand
Certain ligands in coordination chemistry are described as bidentate because they attach through two donor atoms.

The fossil had a bidentate jaw structure, making it unique among related species.
