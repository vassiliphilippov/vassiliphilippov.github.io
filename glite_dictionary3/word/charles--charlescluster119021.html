Charles

proper noun
eldest son of Elizabeth II, English monarch
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrlz / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz / (BrE)
the eldest son of Elizabeth II, former Prince of Wales, and heir to the English throne, born in 1948; became King Charles III in 2022.
