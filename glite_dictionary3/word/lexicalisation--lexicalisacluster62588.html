lexicalisation

noun
process of forming a word for a concept
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛksɪlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɛksɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming a word or phrase to express a particular concept or idea in a language

Examples

lexicalisation of a concept
Different languages vary in their rate of lexicalisation for certain concepts.
Metaphors often undergo lexicalisation and become part of everyday speech.
The lexicalisation of new technology terms is rapidly changing our vocabulary.
