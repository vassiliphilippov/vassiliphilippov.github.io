impishness

noun
playful or mischievous behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈɪmpɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being playful, mischievous, or inclined to make jokes or pranks in a lighthearted way

Examples

childlike impishness
A touch of impishness lightened the mood during the otherwise serious meeting.
He couldn't help but admire the impishness of the children as they played harmless tricks on each other.
Her impishness was evident in the playful glint in her eye as she hid his keys.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mischievousness: the quality of causing trouble or annoyance playfully
  • antonym – seriousness: the quality of being serious and earnest
