chequebook

noun
book of pre-printed cheques
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃekˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book containing pre-printed forms for writing cheques, issued by a bank to holders of checking accounts

Examples

chequebook from the bank
She ordered a new chequebook after running out of cheques in the old one.
The bank will send your chequebook within a few business days.
You should always keep your chequebook in a safe place when you’re not using it.
