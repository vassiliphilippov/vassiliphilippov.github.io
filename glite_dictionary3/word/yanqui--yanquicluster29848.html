yanqui

noun
an American, esp. a northerner (Latin American usage)
Pronunciation: / ˈjæŋki / (AmE), / ˈjaŋki / (BrE)
(especially in Latin America) an American, particularly one from the northern United States; sometimes used to refer to any person from the United States.

Examples

a yanqui tourist
Despite growing up in Argentina, he was sometimes mistaken for a yanqui by locals.
During the debate, the senator was labeled a yanqui due to his American views.
Tourists from Europe often don't realize the historical weight of the word yanqui in Latin America.
