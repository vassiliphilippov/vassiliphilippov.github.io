fall

noun
surrender or defeat (esp. in battle)
Pronunciation: / fɑl / (AmE), / fɔːl / (BrE)
the act of surrendering or being defeated, especially in a battle or siege, often under agreed conditions

Examples

fall of the city
After months of siege, the fall of the fortress was inevitable.
Historians debate the causes behind the fall of the empire.
The fall of the city marked the end of the rebellion.

Related Terms

  • synonym – capitulation: the act of surrendering, often with terms or conditions
  • antonym – victory: the defeat of an enemy or opponent
