repressive

adjective
restrictive; limiting freedom
Pronunciation: / rɪˈprɛsɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈprɛsɪv / (BrE)
restricting or controlling people's freedom or actions, often by force or strict rules

Examples

repressive government policies
Many organizations protested the repressive laws enacted last year.
The repressive atmosphere at the school discouraged creativity.
The country suffered under a repressive government that limited free speech.
Many people fled the repressive environment of their home country in search of freedom.
Strict and repressive policies often lead to unrest among the population.
The government imposed repressive measures to silence dissent and control the media.

  • synonym – oppressive: unjustly inflicting hardship and constraint
