just

adverb
absolutely; for emphasis
Pronunciation: / dʒʌst / (AmE), / dʒʌst / (BrE)
Used to emphasize the intensity or totality of a quality, action, or feeling; absolutely; simply.

Examples

just love this
I just love this song; it makes me smile every time I hear it.
This cake is just perfect—there's nothing I would change about it.
You have to see this movie; it's just amazing!
