necessary

adjective
unavoidably determined by prior circumstances
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛsəˌsɛri / (AmE), / ˈnɛsəsəri / (BrE)
required or essential, often because of circumstances; unavoidable or needed

Examples

necessary action
