unmanlike

adjective
lacking manly qualities
Pronunciation: / ˈʌnˌmæn.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈʌnˌmæn.laɪk / (BrE)
Not possessing qualities considered typical of a man; lacking manly traits.

Examples

unmanlike behavior
His actions were criticized as unmanlike by some of his peers.
Showing fear in battle was viewed as unmanlike in that era.
The novel depicts its hero as anything but unmanlike in the face of adversity.

Related Terms

  • antonym – manly: having qualities traditionally attributed to a man
