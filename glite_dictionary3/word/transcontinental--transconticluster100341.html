transcontinental

adjective
spanning or crossing a continent
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzˌkɑːn.tɪˈnen.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌtrænzˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.tl̩ / (BrE)
spanning, crossing, or extending across a continent; situated on or relating to the farther side of a continent.

Examples

transcontinental flight
