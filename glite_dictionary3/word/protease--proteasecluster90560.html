protease

noun
enzyme that breaks down proteins
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.tieɪz / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.ti.eɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme that breaks down proteins into smaller peptides or amino acids through proteolysis

Examples

digestive protease enzyme
A digestive protease helps to break down food proteins in the stomach.

Some bacteria secrete a protease that can degrade tissue proteins.
