speculation

noun
deep contemplation or philosophical thought
Pronunciation: / ˌspɛkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌspɛkjʊˈleɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)

Examples

The book is the result of years of speculation and reflection on human consciousness.
