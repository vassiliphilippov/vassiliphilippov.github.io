vulture

noun
large carrion-eating bird of prey
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈvʌltʃə / (BrE)
a large bird of prey with a bald head and weak claws that feeds mainly on carrion

Examples

a vulture circling overhead
A vulture soared high above the savannah, searching for its next meal.
After spotting a carcass, the vulture descended quickly to feed.
The documentary showed how vultures help keep ecosystems clean by eating dead animals.
