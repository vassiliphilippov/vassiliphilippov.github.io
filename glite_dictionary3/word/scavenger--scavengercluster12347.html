scavenger

noun
person who collects discarded items
Pronunciation: / ˈskævəndʒər / (AmE), / ˈskævɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person who searches through and collects items discarded by others, often for reuse or resale

Examples

scavenger collects trash
After the festival, a scavenger picked up bottles and cans left behind by revelers.
The scavenger spent hours sorting through the city's trash bins in search of reusable items.
Urban scavengers often find old electronics and sell them for parts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – collector: person who gathers things, especially as a hobby
  • synonym – forager: person or animal that searches widely for food or provisions
