fondant

noun
creamy sugar candy paste
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑndənt / (AmE), / ˈfɒndənt / (BrE)
A type of candy made from a thick, creamy sugar paste, often used as a filling or for coating cakes and sweets.

Examples

fondant icing roses
He decorated the cake with a layer of fondant shaped into roses.
She learned to make fondant from scratch for her homemade candies.
The center of each chocolate was filled with creamy fondant.
