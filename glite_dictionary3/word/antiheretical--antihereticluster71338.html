antiheretical

adjective
opposed to heresy
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.həˈrɛt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌæn.ti.həˈrɛt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
Opposed to heresy or to heretical beliefs; against doctrines considered to deviate from established religious teachings.

Examples

antiheretical writings
His writings reveal a strongly antiheretical stance against new sects.
