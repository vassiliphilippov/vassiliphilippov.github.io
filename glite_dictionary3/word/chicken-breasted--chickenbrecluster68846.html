chicken-breasted

adjective
having a chest with a protruding breastbone
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən ˌbrɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn ˌbrɛstɪd / (BrE)
having a chest deformity in which the breastbone protrudes outward, usually as a result of infantile rickets; also known as having a pigeon chest.

Examples

chicken-breasted child
Doctors described the chest deformity as chicken-breasted, requiring specialized physical therapy.
He noticed his chicken-breasted appearance in the mirror as his chest bone protruded.
The patient was chicken-breasted due to rickets in early childhood.
