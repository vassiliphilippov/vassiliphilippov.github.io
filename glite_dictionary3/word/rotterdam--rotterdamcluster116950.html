Rotterdam

proper noun
major city and port in the Netherlands
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːtərˌdæm / (AmE), / ˈrɒtədæm / (BrE)
the second largest city in the Netherlands, located in the western part of the country near the North Sea, known for its major port and modern architecture.
