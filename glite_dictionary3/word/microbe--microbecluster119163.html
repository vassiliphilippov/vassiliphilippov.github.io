microbe

noun
microscopic organism, e.g. a bacterium
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊb / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊb / (BrE)
a microscopic organism, especially one that can cause disease, such as a bacterium; not used technically in scientific classifications

Examples
