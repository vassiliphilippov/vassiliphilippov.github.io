venae labiales posteriores

noun
posterior veins from the labia majora
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnaɪ læˈbaɪəliːz pɒsˈtɪəriˌɔːriz / (AmE), / ˈviːnaɪ læˈbeɪliːz pɒsˈtɪəriˌɔːriːz / (BrE)
