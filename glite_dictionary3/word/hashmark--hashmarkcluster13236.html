hashmark

noun
insignia for years of service
Pronunciation: / ˈhæʃˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhæʃˌmɑːk / (BrE)
an insignia consisting of stripes or marks, worn on a uniform to show the number of years of service

Examples

hashmark on a uniform
Each hashmark on his jacket represents another three years of dedicated service.
She proudly added a new hashmark to her sleeve after a decade with the police force.
The sergeant's uniform displayed a hashmark for every five years he had served.
