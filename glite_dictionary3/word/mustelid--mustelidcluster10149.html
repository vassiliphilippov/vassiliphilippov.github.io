mustelid

noun
mammal of the weasel family
Pronunciation: / mʌˈstɛlɪd / (AmE), / ˈmʌstɪlɪd / (BrE)
a mammal of the weasel family (Mustelidae), which includes weasels, otters, badgers, and related animals, typically small, carnivorous, and fur-bearing.

Examples

mustelid family animals
Many mustelid species have long, slender bodies suited for hunting in tight spaces.
The mustelid family includes animals such as ferrets, weasels, and otters.
Wolverines are the largest land-dwelling members of the mustelid family.
