pouffe

noun
cushioned seat or footstool
Pronunciation: / puf / (AmE), / puːf / (BrE)
A thick, upholstered cushion, often cylindrical or cube-shaped, used as a seat or footstool.

Examples

sit on a pouffe
He rested his feet on the pouffe after a long day at work.
She sat on the pouffe in the corner while she tied her shoes.
The living room was decorated with a large rug and a blue pouffe.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ottoman: upholstered footstool or seat without a back or arms
