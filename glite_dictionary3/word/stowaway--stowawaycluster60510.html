stowaway

noun
person who travels secretly without paying
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊ.əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈstəʊ.əˌweɪ / (BrE)
a person who hides aboard a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle to travel secretly and without paying

Examples

hidden stowaway on ship
After days at sea, the stowaway finally revealed himself to the captain.
Security found a young stowaway on board the aircraft before takeoff.
The crew discovered a stowaway hiding in the cargo hold.
