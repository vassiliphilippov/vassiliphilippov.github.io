foetometry

noun
measurement of a fetus, especially the head
Pronunciation: / fiˈɒtəˌmɛtri / (AmE), / fɪˈɒtəmətri / (BrE)
the measurement of a fetus, especially the diameter of its head, typically to assess growth and development during pregnancy

Examples

foetometry during ultrasound
Foetometry is commonly performed during ultrasounds to track fetal development.
Accurate foetometry can help detect abnormalities in fetal growth before birth.
The doctor explained the results of the foetometry to the expectant parents.
