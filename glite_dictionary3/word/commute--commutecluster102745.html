commute

noun
regular journey between home and work
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjut / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːt / (BrE)
a regular journey between one's home and place of work, especially over some distance

Examples

long commute home
My daily commute takes about an hour each way by train.
She uses the time during her commute to catch up on reading.
