isolated

adjective
kept apart for health reasons
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.səˌleɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.sə.leɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
Kept apart from others, especially to prevent the spread of disease or infection.

Examples

isolated patient room
After the outbreak, several residents were isolated to stop the disease from spreading.
All isolated patients must remain in their rooms until cleared by a doctor.
The isolated area was strictly off-limits to unprotected personnel.

Related Terms

  • antonym – exposed: not isolated; at risk of contact with disease
  • synonym – quarantined: kept apart to prevent disease spread
