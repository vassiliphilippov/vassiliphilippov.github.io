impression

noun
digital ad display count
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
(in digital advertising and web analytics) a single instance of an online advertisement or piece of content being displayed to a user or on a page; used as a metric counting how often content is shown.

Examples

ad impression
The campaign delivered three million impressions across mobile apps during the first 48 hours.
Advertisers compare viewability and click-through rate in addition to raw impressions for performance.
Despite high impressions, the banner ad failed to drive meaningful engagement with the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – display: an instance of content being shown on a screen; often used to refer to an ad being presented to a user
  • synonym – reach: the number of unique users who see an ad or piece of content; related metric in advertising
