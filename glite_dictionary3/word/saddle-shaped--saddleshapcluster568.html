saddle-shaped

adjective
curved like a horse's saddle
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈsædəl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a form that curves upward at the edges and downward in the middle, resembling a horse's saddle.

Examples

saddle-shaped surface
Certain types of mathematical surfaces, like the hyperbolic paraboloid, are saddle-shaped.
The architect designed the roof with a distinctive saddle-shaped structure.
The mountain pass created a natural saddle-shaped depression between the peaks.
