time

noun
musical rhythm or meter
Pronunciation: / taɪm / (AmE), / taɪm / (BrE)
The measured or metrical rhythm in music, marked by a regular succession of beats or accents.

Examples

time signature in music
Each piece of music is written in a specific time, such as three-four or four-four.
The conductor clapped out the time so the choir could follow the rhythm more easily.
The students found it challenging to keep time during the fast section of the march.
