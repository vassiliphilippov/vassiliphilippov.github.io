underlay

noun
padding material beneath carpet or flooring
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌleɪ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəleɪ / (BrE)
A layer of material placed underneath a carpet, flooring, or other surface to provide cushioning or insulation.

Examples

underlay for carpet
Before installing the new carpet, the fitter laid down a thick underlay to add comfort and insulation.
Good quality underlay can help prolong the life of your flooring.
She chose a rubber underlay to reduce noise in her apartment.
