Votyak

noun
member of the Udmurt people of Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊtiˌæk / (AmE), / ˈvɒtɪak / (BrE)
a member of a Finno-Ugric-speaking people native to eastern European Russia, now more commonly known as the Udmurt.
