less

adverb
to a smaller degree or amount
Pronunciation: / lɛs / (AmE), / lɛs / (BrE)
to a smaller degree or extent; not as much as something else.

Examples

less often
He spends less time watching TV now than he used to.
The new phone weighs less than the previous model.
They offered less support than we expected.
