tetanic

adjective
relating to or causing tetanus
Pronunciation: / təˈtænɪk / (AmE), / tɪˈtænɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to or causing tetanus, or resembling the muscle spasms associated with tetanus

Examples

tetanic muscle spasms
A tetanic reaction can result in prolonged and rigid muscle spasms.
Certain toxins are known to produce tetanic symptoms in mammals.
The patient exhibited tetanic muscle contractions following the infection.
