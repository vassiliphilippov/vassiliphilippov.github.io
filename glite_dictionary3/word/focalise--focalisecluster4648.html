to focalise

verb
concentrate or localize in one place
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊkəˌlaɪz /; / ˈfoʊ.kə.laɪz / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkəlaɪz /; / ˈfəʊ.kə.laɪz / (BrE)
to focus or concentrate something, or become focused, either literally (like with a lens) or figuratively (like attention, ideas, or emotions)
