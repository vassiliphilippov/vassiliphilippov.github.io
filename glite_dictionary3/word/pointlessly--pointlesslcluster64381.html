pointlessly

adverb
without purpose or meaning
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪntlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪntləsli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks meaning, purpose, or usefulness.

Examples

walk pointlessly around
He wandered pointlessly through the empty corridors, unsure of where to go next.
She felt she was working pointlessly with no appreciation or clear results.
They argued pointlessly about old mistakes that could no longer be changed.

  • synonym – purposelessly: in a manner lacking purpose
