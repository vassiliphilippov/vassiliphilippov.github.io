Metatheria

noun
group of mammals including marsupials
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈθɪriə / (AmE), / ˌmiːtəˈθɪəriə /; / ˌmɛtəˈθɪərɪə / (BrE)
a group of mammals that includes all marsupials, whose young are born undeveloped and are often carried and fed in a pouch
