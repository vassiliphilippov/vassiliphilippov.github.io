flush

noun
poker hand of same-suit cards
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ / (AmE), / flʌʃ / (BrE)
A poker hand consisting of five cards of the same suit, not in sequence.

Examples

a flush in poker
He was excited when he realized he had a flush during the final round.
It's rare to see a flush beat a full house in poker.
She won the pot with a flush of hearts.
