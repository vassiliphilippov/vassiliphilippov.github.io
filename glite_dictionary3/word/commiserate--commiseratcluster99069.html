to commiserate

verb
express sympathy or compassion
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪz.əˌreɪt / (AmE), / kəˈmɪz.ə.reɪt / (BrE)
to feel or express sympathy or compassion for someone who is suffering

Examples

to commiserate with a friend
After hearing about her friend’s loss, she called to commiserate with him.
Colleagues gathered to commiserate over the recent layoffs at the office.
I went to commiserate with my neighbor when her dog ran away.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sympathize: to share or express feelings of pity or compassion
