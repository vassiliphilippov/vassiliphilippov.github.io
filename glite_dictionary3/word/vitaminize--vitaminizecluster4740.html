to vitaminize

verb
add vitamins to (something)
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.tə.məˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɪ.tə.mɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)

Examples

Food manufacturers often vitaminize processed products to help address nutritional deficiencies.
Many breakfast cereals are vitaminized to improve their nutritional value.
