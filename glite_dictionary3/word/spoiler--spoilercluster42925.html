spoiler

noun
person who reveals plot details
Pronunciation: / \u02c8sp\u0254\u026al\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8sp\u0254\u026al\u0259 / (BrE)
Someone who reveals important plot details, twists, or surprises about a book, film, game, or show, thereby reducing others' enjoyment.
