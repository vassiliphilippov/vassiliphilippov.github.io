to answer

verb
respond to a signal (door, phone, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːn.sə / (BrE)
to respond to a signal, such as a knock at the door or a ringing telephone, by opening the door or picking up the phone

Examples

to answer the phone
Could you please answer the door while I’m busy in the kitchen?
No one was home to answer when the deliveryman knocked.
She hurried to answer the telephone before it stopped ringing.
