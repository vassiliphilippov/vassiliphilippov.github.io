to arrest

verb
take into custody, as by police
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛst / (AmE), / əˈrɛst / (BrE)
to seize and take someone into custody, especially by legal authority

Examples

to arrest a suspect
If you resist, the officers will still arrest you and take you to the station.
She was arrested for driving without a valid license.
The police decided to arrest the man after reviewing the evidence.
