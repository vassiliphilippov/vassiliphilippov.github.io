Colchicum autumnale

noun
a European autumn-flowering plant (autumn crocus)
Pronunciation: / kolˈkɪkəm ɔːˈtʌmnəli / (AmE), / kɒlˈkɪkəm ɔːˈtʌmnəli / (BrE)
a bulbous autumn-flowering plant with white, purple, or lavender-and-white flowers, native to western and central Europe, also known as the autumn crocus
