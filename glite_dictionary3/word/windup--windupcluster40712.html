windup

adjective
operated by winding a mechanism
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪndˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌʌp / (BrE)
(of a toy or other device) operated by turning a key or similar mechanism to wind a spring inside.

Examples

windup toy robot
He gave his nephew a windup robot for his birthday.
One of her favorite childhood toys was a little windup duck that waddled across the floor.
The old-fashioned windup alarm clock rang loudly every morning.
