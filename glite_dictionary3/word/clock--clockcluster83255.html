to clock

verb
to measure or record time taken
Pronunciation: / klɑk / (AmE), / klɒk / (BrE)
to measure or record the time taken by someone or something, especially in a race or timed event

Examples

to clock a runner
Every swimmer was clocked precisely down to the hundredth of a second.
She clocked the runners' times during the marathon.
The car clocked a record speed on the track this afternoon.

  • synonym – time: to measure the duration of something
