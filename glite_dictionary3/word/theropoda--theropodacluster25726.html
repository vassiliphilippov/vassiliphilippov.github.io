Theropoda

noun
group of carnivorous saurischian dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / θəˈrɑːpədə / (AmE), / θɪˈrɒpədə / (BrE)
a group of carnivorous saurischian dinosaurs with short forelimbs, known from the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods
