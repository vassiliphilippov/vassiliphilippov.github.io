moulding

noun
decorative finishing strip
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative strip or surface used to finish or ornament the edge of a wall, ceiling, door, or piece of furniture
