tragicomedy

noun
literary work mixing tragedy and comedy
Pronunciation: / ˌtrædʒɪˈkɑːmədi / (AmE), / ˌtrædʒɪˈkɒmədi / (BrE)
a play, novel, or other literary work that combines elements of both tragedy and comedy
