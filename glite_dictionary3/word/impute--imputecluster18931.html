to impute

verb
estimate and fill missing data
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpjut / (AmE), / ɪmˈpjuːt / (BrE)
(in statistics and data analysis) to estimate and replace missing or incomplete values in a dataset using a statistical method.

Examples

to impute missing data
Researchers impute missing income responses to avoid bias in their national survey analysis.
The software imputes average values when a few entries in the dataset are blank.
By imputing missing test scores, teachers could compare student progress across terms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – estimate: to form an approximate judgment or calculation of a quantity or value
  • synonym – interpolate: to insert or calculate intermediate values within a series of known data points
