vertebrate

noun
animal with a backbone
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrtəˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪbrət / (BrE)
an animal with a backbone or spinal column, including mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and fishes

Examples

vertebrate animal group
A fish is a typical example of a vertebrate animal.
The fossil revealed the earliest known vertebrate from that geological period.
Unlike insects, all vertebrates have a well-developed nervous system.
