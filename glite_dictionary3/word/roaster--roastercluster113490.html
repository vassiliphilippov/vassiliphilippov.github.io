roaster

noun
pan for roasting food
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊstər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊstə / (BrE)
a large, usually deep pan or dish used for roasting food, especially meat, in an oven.

Examples

roaster pan in oven
After dinner, he washed the roaster and put it back in the cupboard.
She placed the turkey in the roaster and slid it into the oven.
The large roaster is perfect for preparing meals for a big family gathering.
