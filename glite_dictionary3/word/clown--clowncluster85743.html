to clown

verb
act playfully or foolishly
Pronunciation: / klaʊn / (AmE), / klaʊn / (BrE)
to act in a playful or silly way like a clown, often to entertain others

Examples

to clown around
He was clowning in class to make his friends laugh.
Stop clowning around and help me with this project.
The kids clowned for hours at the birthday party.

Related Terms

  • antonym – behave seriously: to act in a serious or earnest way
  • synonym – fool around: to behave in a silly or playful way
  • synonym – joke: to say or do things to make people laugh
