to snipe

verb
to shoot with precision from concealment
Pronunciation: / snaɪp / (AmE), / snaɪp / (BrE)
to shoot at someone or something from a concealed position, often with great precision

Examples

to snipe from cover
Gamers often use the term when they snipe their rivals from afar in online matches.
She managed to snipe the last target in the shooting competition with ease.
The soldier tried to snipe the enemy from his hidden vantage point.
