orthoepist

noun
expert in pronunciation reform
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθoʊəpɪst / (AmE), / ɔːˈθəʊəpɪst / (BrE)
A person who studies or is an expert in orthoepy, especially one who seeks to reform language pronunciation or spelling to better reflect pronunciation.
