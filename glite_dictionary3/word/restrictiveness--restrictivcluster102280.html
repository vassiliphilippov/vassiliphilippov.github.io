restrictiveness

noun
grammatical quality of limiting meaning
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstrɪktɪvnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈstrɪktɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of limiting or qualifying the meaning of another word or phrase, making it more specific

Examples

grammatical restrictiveness
In English grammar, restrictiveness helps clarify which noun the speaker is referring to.
Teachers often explain the restrictiveness of clauses to help students avoid ambiguity.
The restrictiveness of the adjective 'red' in 'red hat' narrows down the possible meanings.
