rumba

noun
syncopated music for the rumba dance
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmbə / (AmE), / ˈrʌmbə / (BrE)
A style of syncopated music in duple time, typically played for the rumba dance.

Examples

rumba music
She enjoys dancing to the vibrant beat of the rumba.
The band played lively rumba music all night at the festival.
Traditional rumba rhythms can be heard in many Latin American songs.
