Antennaria dioica

noun
species of low-growing perennial herb
Pronunciation: / ænˌtɛˈnɛr.i.ə daɪˈoʊ.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / anˌtɛnˈnaːrɪə daɪˈəʊ.ɪ.kə / (BrE)
A low-growing perennial herb with leaves covered in whitish down and clusters of small white flowers; commonly known as mountain everlasting or cat's-foot.
