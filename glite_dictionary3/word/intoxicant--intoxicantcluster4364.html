intoxicant

adjective
causing intoxication
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɑksɪkənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɒksɪkənt / (BrE)
(of a substance or drink) causing intoxication; making someone drunk or exhilarated.

Examples

intoxicant drink
Many countries strictly regulate the sale of intoxicant beverages.
The pub served several intoxicant liquors to its patrons.
You must be above a certain age to purchase any intoxicant drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intoxicating: causing someone to lose control due to alcohol or drugs
