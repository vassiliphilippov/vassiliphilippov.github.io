Ranunculus repens

noun
a creeping buttercup plant species
Pronunciation: / rəˈnʌŋkjʊləs ˈrɛpənz / (AmE), / rəˈnʌŋkjʊləs ˈrɛpɛnz / (BrE)
a perennial European herb with long creeping runners, commonly known as creeping buttercup, often found in moist grasslands and gardens

Examples

Ranunculus repens in lawns
Ranunculus repens often invades lawns and moist meadows, outcompeting native grasses.
Gardeners frequently struggle to control Ranunculus repens due to its rapid spread by runners.
