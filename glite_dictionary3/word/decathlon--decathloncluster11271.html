decathlon

noun
athletic competition of ten events
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkæθ.lɒn / (AmE), / dɪˈkaθ.lən / (BrE)
An athletic competition in which each contestant takes part in ten different track and field events.

Examples

Olympic decathlon event
