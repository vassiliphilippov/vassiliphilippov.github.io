womanly

adjective
having qualities traditionally associated with women
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənli / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənli / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with women, especially mature women, such as gentleness or compassion.

Examples

womanly qualities
His sister's womanly wisdom helped guide him through many difficult decisions.
She was admired for her womanly grace and compassion.
The author often wrote about womanly strength in her novels.
