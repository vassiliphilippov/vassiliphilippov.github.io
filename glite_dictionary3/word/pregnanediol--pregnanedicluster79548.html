pregnanediol

noun
compound found in women's urine, pregnancy indicator
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛɡ.nəˈniː.ɑl / (AmE), / ˌprɛɡ.nəˈniː.ɒl / (BrE)
a compound found in the urine of women during certain phases of the menstrual cycle and of pregnant women, used as an indicator of progesterone metabolism
