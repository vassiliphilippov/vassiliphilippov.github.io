deskbound

adjective
restricted to working at a desk
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛskˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈdɛskˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
Spending most of one's working time seated at a desk, especially rather than being physically active.

Examples

deskbound office worker
After years of working as a field engineer, he found the deskbound lifestyle challenging.
Many deskbound employees suffer from back pain due to sitting all day.
She prefers an active job because she's not suited to being deskbound.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sedentary: tending to spend much time seated; somewhat similar in meaning
