interoperable

adjective
able to work with other systems
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɑpərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˈɒpərəbl / (BrE)
(of systems or devices) able to exchange and make use of information with each other.

Examples

interoperable computer systems
Hospitals need interoperable medical records systems to share patient data securely.
Many smart home devices are interoperable and can be controlled from a single app.
The new software is fully interoperable with previous versions.
