Mutsuhito

proper noun
emperor of Japan (1867–1912); Emperor Meiji
Pronunciation: / muːtsuːˈhiːtoʊ / (AmE), / muːtsuːˈhiːtəʊ / (BrE)
the emperor of Japan from 1867 to 1912, known as Emperor Meiji, who led the country through a period of modernization
