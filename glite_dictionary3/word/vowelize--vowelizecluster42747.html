to vowelize

verb
pronounce or treat as a vowel
Pronunciation: / ˈvaʊəlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvaʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
to pronounce or treat (a sound or letter) as a vowel, especially by inserting a vowel sound or quality

Examples

to vowelize a letter
In some dialects, speakers often vowelize the l in 'bottle.'
Linguists study how liquids can be vowelized between consonants in certain languages.
She demonstrated how to vowelize a consonant when teaching phonetics.
