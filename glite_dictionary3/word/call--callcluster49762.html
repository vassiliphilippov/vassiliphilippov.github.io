to call

verb
lure by imitating animal sound
Pronunciation: / kɔl /; / kɑl / (AmE), / kɔːl / (BrE)
to shout, cry out, or make a loud sound to attract attention or express emotion; or (of an animal or bird) to make its characteristic sound
