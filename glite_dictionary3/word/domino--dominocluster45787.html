domino

noun
rectangular gaming tile with dots
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊməˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a small rectangular tile marked with zero to six dots on each of its two ends, used in playing the game of dominoes

Examples

play domino tiles
Each domino in the set is marked with a unique combination of dots.
He picked up a domino and placed it at the end of the row.
The children were entertained by making a tower out of each domino.
