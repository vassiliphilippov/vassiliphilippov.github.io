greylag

noun
a species of wild grey goose in Europe
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪˌlæɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪlæɡ / (BrE)
a common large grey wild goose of Europe, regarded as the ancestor of many domestic goose breeds
