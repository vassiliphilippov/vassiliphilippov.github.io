unsanded

adjective
not made smooth by sanding
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsændɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsændɪd / (BrE)
(of wood or a surface) not made smooth by sanding; not having been polished with sandpaper.

Examples

unsanded wood surface
Be careful with unsanded edges, as they can easily cause splinters.
He preferred to work with unsanded boards, allowing for a more rustic finish.
The table arrived unsanded, so the surface was still rough to the touch.
