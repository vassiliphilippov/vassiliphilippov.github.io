Haemoproteus

noun
genus of blood parasites in animals
Pronunciation: / hiː.məˈproʊ.ti.əs / (AmE), / hiː.məˈprəʊ.ti.əs / (BrE)
A genus of parasitic protozoans found in the blood of birds, reptiles, and amphibians, some of which can cause disease.
