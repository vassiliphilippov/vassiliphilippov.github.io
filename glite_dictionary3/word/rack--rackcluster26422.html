to rack

verb
torture on the rack
Pronunciation: / ræk / (AmE), / ræk / (BrE)
to torture someone by stretching them on the rack, a device formerly used for inflicting pain and extracting confessions.

Examples

to rack a prisoner
During the Inquisition, prisoners were often racked to force them to confess.
The captured spy was racked until he revealed his secrets.
Torturers racked their victims in the dungeons beneath the castle.
