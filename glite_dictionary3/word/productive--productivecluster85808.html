productive

adjective
producing large amounts; fertile
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʌktɪv / (AmE), / prəˈdʌktɪv / (BrE)
producing or capable of producing large amounts of something; fertile or fruitful.

Examples

productive land
After years of drought, the soil finally became productive again.
The region's productive farmland provides food for thousands of people.
This valley is known for its productive orchards and vineyards.

Related Terms

  • antonym – barren: not productive; unable to produce plants or crops
  • synonym – fertile: able to produce abundantly; highly productive, especially of land or soil
