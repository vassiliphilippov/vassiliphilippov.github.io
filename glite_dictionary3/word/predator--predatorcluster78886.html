predator

noun
one who preys on others for gain
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛdətər / (AmE), / ˈprɛdətə / (BrE)
A person or animal that attacks others, especially in order to steal or exploit them; someone who preys on others for personal gain.

Examples

financial predator
During the recession, ruthless financial predators exploited desperate homeowners.
Many online predators try to befriend children using fake profiles.
The serial predator was finally caught and brought to justice.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attacker: someone who attacks others, especially violently
  • antonym – prey: an animal that is hunted and killed by another for food; a person who is the victim of exploitation
  • antonym – victim: a person harmed, injured, or killed as a result of a crime, accident, or event
