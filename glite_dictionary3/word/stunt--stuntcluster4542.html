to stunt

verb
prevent normal growth or development
Pronunciation: / stʌnt / (AmE), / stʌnt / (BrE)
to prevent the normal growth or development of someone or something

Examples

stunt plant growth
Overcrowded roots in a pot will stunt the growth of plants.
Poor nutrition can stunt a child's physical and mental development.
The drought significantly stunted the harvest this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – encourage: to help or promote the development of someone or something
  • synonym – hinder: to make it difficult for someone or something to develop or succeed
