feedstock

noun
raw material for industrial process
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːdˌstɑk / (AmE), / ˈfiːdstɒk / (BrE)
raw material supplied to a process, especially in industry, to be converted into a finished product or energy

Examples

industrial feedstock supply
Crude oil is the primary feedstock for many petrochemical plants.
Switchgrass can be used as a feedstock in the production of biofuels.
The company is seeking a more sustainable feedstock for its manufacturing process.

  • synonym – raw material: basic material used to produce goods or energy
