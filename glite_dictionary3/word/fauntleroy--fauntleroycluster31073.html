Fauntleroy

noun
excessively polite and well-dressed boy
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔnt.ləˌrɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːnt.ləˌrɔɪ / (BrE)
A boy who is excessively polite, well-dressed, or affectedly refined, often used in a mildly disparaging way.

Examples

a Fauntleroy boy
At school, he was always called Fauntleroy because of his neat clothes and perfect manners.
