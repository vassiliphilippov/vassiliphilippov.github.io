to follow

verb
understand; grasp the meaning
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɒləʊ / (BrE)
to understand or grasp the meaning of what someone is saying or explaining

Examples

to follow an explanation
I'm not sure I follow what you mean—could you explain that again?
If you follow the reasoning, you will see why the answer is correct.
The textbook was so confusing that few students could follow it.
