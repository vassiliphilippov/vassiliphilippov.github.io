sweating

noun
the secretion of sweat through the skin
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswetɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which the sweat glands secrete a salty fluid through the skin, usually as a cooling mechanism

Examples

heavy sweating
He was nervous and noticeable sweating developed on his forehead.
Intense sweating can occur during strenuous physical activity.
Some medications may cause excessive sweating as a side effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dehydration: the process of losing more water than is taken in, often caused by excessive sweating
  • synonym – perspiration: the process of sweating
