to smut

verb
make obscene or indecent
Pronunciation: / smʌt / (AmE), / smʌt / (BrE)
to make (something) obscene or indecent, especially by adding vulgar or sexual content

Examples

to smut a story
Critics accused the film of smutting what was once an innocent story.
His attempts at humor only served to smut the conversation.
Some editors feel the new lyrics smut the original song.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sanitize: to make something less offensive or obscene
