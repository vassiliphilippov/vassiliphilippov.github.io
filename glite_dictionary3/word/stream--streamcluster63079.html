stream

noun
small natural flowing watercourse
Pronunciation: / striːm / (AmE), / striːm / (BrE)
a small, narrow, natural body of running water flowing on or under the earth's surface

Examples

mountain stream
A narrow stream of water ran through the valley, creating a peaceful atmosphere.
After the storm, the stream overflowed its banks and flooded the path.
The children spent the afternoon playing by the stream in the woods.
