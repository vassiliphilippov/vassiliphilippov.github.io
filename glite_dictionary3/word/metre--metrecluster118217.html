metre

noun
unit of length (SI)
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
the basic unit of length in the International System of Units, equal to 100 centimetres or approximately 39.37 inches

Examples

one hundred metres
He ran a distance of one hundred metres in record time.
The Olympic pool must be exactly 50 metres long.
The building stands over thirty metres tall.
