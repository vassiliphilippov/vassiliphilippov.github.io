imaginativeness

noun
creative ability to form new ideas
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmædʒɪˈneɪtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪˌmadʒɪˈneɪtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having or showing a creative ability to form new ideas, images, or concepts that are not present to the senses
