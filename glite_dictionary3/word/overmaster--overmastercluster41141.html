to overmaster

verb
to overcome by superior force
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈmæstɚ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmɑːstə / (BrE)
to overcome or subdue completely by superior force or influence

Examples

to overmaster strong feelings
His desire for revenge began to overmaster all rational thought.
She was suddenly overmastered by a wave of fear that left her speechless.
The soldiers overmastered the rebel forces after a fierce battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – overcome: to succeed in dealing with or gaining control of something
  • antonym – succumb: to stop resisting an opposing force; to yield
