arteria ascendens

noun
branch of artery supplying the ascending colon
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə əˈsɛndɛnz / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə əˈsɛndɛnz / (BrE)
The branch of the ileocolic artery that supplies blood to the ascending colon.
