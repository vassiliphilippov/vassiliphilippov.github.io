westmost

adjective
farthest to the west
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈwɛstməʊst / (BrE)
Located farthest to the west; most westerly.

Examples

the westmost point
Of all the villages, Oakham is the westmost.
The island is the westmost point in the archipelago.
They set up camp on the westmost hill to watch the sunset.

Related Terms

  • antonym – eastmost: farthest to the east
  • synonym – westernmost: farthest to the west; more common variant
