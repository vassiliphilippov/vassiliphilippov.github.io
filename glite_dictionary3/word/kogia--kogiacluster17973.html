Kogia

noun
a genus of small sperm whales
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdʒiə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdʒɪə / (BrE)
A genus of small sperm whales, including the pygmy sperm whale and dwarf sperm whale, found in deep oceans.
