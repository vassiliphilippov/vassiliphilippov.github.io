vanity

noun
worthlessness or futility
Pronunciation: / ˈvænəti / (AmE), / ˈvænɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being worthless, futile, or lacking real value or substance.

Examples

vanity of effort
He came to realize the vanity of his endless arguments.
Much of the work was exposed as mere vanity after the results were published.
She spoke about the vanity of worldly pursuits in her speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym – futility: the quality of being pointless or useless
  • synonym – worthlessness: the quality of lacking value or usefulness
