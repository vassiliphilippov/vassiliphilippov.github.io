absenteeism

noun
habitual absence from work or school
Pronunciation: / ˌæb.sənˈti.ɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ˌæb.sənˈtiː.ɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The practice of regularly staying away from work or school without good reason.

Examples

chronic absenteeism
Frequent absenteeism led to a decline in overall productivity at the factory.
Managers are concerned about the high rate of absenteeism among their staff.
The school is working on strategies to reduce student absenteeism.
