link

noun
element connecting to another part (computing)
Pronunciation: / lɪŋk / (AmE), / lɪŋk / (BrE)
(computing) an element in a program or web page that connects to another program, document, or location when activated.

Examples

click the link
Click the link to access the download page for the software update.
Each item in the table has a link that takes you to more details.
She added a link in her email to direct users to the support portal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hyperlink: a highlighted word or image that, when clicked, leads to another location or document on the internet
