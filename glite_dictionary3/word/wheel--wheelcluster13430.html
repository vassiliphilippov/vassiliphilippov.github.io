wheel

noun
roulette disc used in gambling games
Pronunciation: / wiːl / (AmE), / wiːl / (BrE)
the rotating disc with numbered slots used in the game of roulette and similar gambling games, on which bets are placed according to where the ball lands

Examples

roulette wheel
In some casinos, the wheel is checked frequently to ensure fair play.
She watched the wheel with anticipation, hoping the ball would land on her number.
The dealer spun the wheel as bets were placed around the table.
