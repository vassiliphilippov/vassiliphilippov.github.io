samizdat

noun
illegal distribution of banned literature
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmɪzˌdɑt / (AmE), / ˈsamɪzˌdat / (BrE)
the clandestine copying and distribution of literature that is officially banned, especially in the former Soviet Union.

Examples

samizdat literature
Authorities cracked down on anyone caught with samizdat material.
Many dissidents risked their lives producing samizdat in order to spread banned books and ideas.
The novel was first circulated as samizdat before being published abroad.
