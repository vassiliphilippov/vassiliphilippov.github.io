laboriousness

noun
the quality of requiring hard work
Pronunciation: / ləˈbɔriəsnəs / (AmE), / ləˈbɔːriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of requiring a great deal of effort or hard work

Examples

laboriousness of the task
Scientists often underestimate the laboriousness involved in collecting reliable field data.
She was praised for her patience in spite of the laboriousness of the process.
The laboriousness of the task discouraged many from volunteering.

Related Terms

  • synonym – difficulty: the state of being hard to do or overcome
  • antonym – ease: the state of being easy or requiring little effort
