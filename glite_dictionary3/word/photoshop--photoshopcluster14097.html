to photoshop

verb
digitally alter a photo or image
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtəˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʃɒp / (BrE)
to digitally alter a photograph or image, especially using Adobe Photoshop or similar software

Examples

to photoshop a picture
Can you photoshop me into that group photo as a joke?
He spent hours photoshopping his vacation pictures before sharing them online.
The magazine cover was heavily photoshopped to remove all imperfections.
