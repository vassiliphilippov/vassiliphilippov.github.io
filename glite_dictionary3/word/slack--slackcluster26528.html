slack

adjective
not strict or careful enough
Pronunciation: / slæk / (AmE), / slæk / (BrE)
not strict, careful, or rigorous; lacking the necessary attention or discipline.

Examples

slack discipline
Her slack attitude towards deadlines often caused delays on the team.
Teachers noticed that discipline had grown slack at the end of term.
The supervisor warned the workers not to be slack about following safety procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lax: lacking in strictness or discipline
  • antonym – strict: enforcing rules or discipline rigorously
