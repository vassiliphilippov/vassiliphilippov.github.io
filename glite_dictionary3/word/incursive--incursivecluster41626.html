incursive

adjective
relating to invasion or attack
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɝːsɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɜːsɪv / (BrE)
involving or characterized by invasion or aggressive attack

Examples

incursive military attack
Ancient history is filled with incursive expeditions into neighboring lands.
The country launched an incursive attack across its northern border.
The general warned of the dangers posed by incursive military actions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – defensive: intended to defend or protect, as opposed to attack
  • synonym – invasive: tending to spread aggressively or intrude, especially in military or medical context
