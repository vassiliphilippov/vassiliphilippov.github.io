coincidently

adverb
at the same time; simultaneously
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɪnsɪdəntli / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪnsɪdəntli / (BrE)
At the same time; in a way that is simultaneous or coinciding.

Examples

coincidently occur together
The two events happened coincidently on the very same evening.
Their birthdays occur coincidently every four years.
Three of us arrived at the station coincidently, despite leaving from different places.

Related Terms

  • antonym – separately: occurring apart or not together
  • synonym – simultaneously: occurring at the same time
