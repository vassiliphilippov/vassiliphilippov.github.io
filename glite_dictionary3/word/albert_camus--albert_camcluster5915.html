Albert Camus

proper noun
French novelist and philosopher (1913–1960)
Pronunciation: / ælˌbɛr kæˈmu / (AmE), / ælˌbɛː kaˈmyː / (BrE)
(1913–1960) French writer and philosopher, noted for exploring themes of absurdity and the human condition; author of works such as 'The Stranger' and 'The Myth of Sisyphus'.
