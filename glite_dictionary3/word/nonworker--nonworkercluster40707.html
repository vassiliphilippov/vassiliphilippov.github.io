nonworker

noun
person who does not work
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who does not work, especially one who does not have paid employment

Examples

unemployed nonworker
During the recession, the number of nonworkers in the country increased sharply.
She became a nonworker after her company downsized.
The benefits system provides assistance for nonworkers seeking employment.
