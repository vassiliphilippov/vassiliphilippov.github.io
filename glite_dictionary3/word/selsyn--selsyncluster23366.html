selsyn

noun
synchronous signal transmission system
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛl.sɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.sɪn / (BrE)
A system composed of a generator and a motor interconnected so that the motor replicates the generator's position; used to transmit angular position signals by electrical means.
