Carafate

noun
medicine for peptic ulcers
Pronunciation: / ˈkærəˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkærəˌfeɪt / (BrE)
A trademarked medicine in tablet form used to treat peptic ulcers by forming a protective coating over the ulcer site.
