DOA

x
abbreviation: dead on arrival
Pronunciation: / diː oʊ eɪ / (AmE), / diː əʊ eɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'dead on arrival', used to indicate that a person was already dead when brought to the hospital or emergency room.

Examples

DOA at the hospital
Although rushed to the ER, the patient was unfortunately pronounced DOA.
The chart was marked DOA, so no resuscitation was attempted.
The paramedics declared the accident victim DOA upon arrival at the hospital.
