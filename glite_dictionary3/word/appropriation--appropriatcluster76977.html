appropriation

noun
incorporation by joining or uniting
Pronunciation: / əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌprəʊprɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of incorporating something by joining or uniting it with something else

Examples

appropriation of culture
