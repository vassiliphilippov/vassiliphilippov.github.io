giving

noun
the act of imparting news or promises
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of imparting news, information, promises, or similar intangible things to others

Examples

giving of information

The giving of his word meant a lot to everyone in the room.
The ceremony included the formal giving of several important announcements.
