diametrically

adverb
completely; from opposite extremes
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪ.əˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdaɪ.əˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
(used with 'opposed' or similar) completely or directly, as from one extreme to another or from opposite ends of a diameter.

Examples

diametrically opposed views
The teams are positioned diametrically across from each other in the stadium.
The two politicians have diametrically opposed views on climate change.
Their opinions were diametrically different on nearly every issue.
