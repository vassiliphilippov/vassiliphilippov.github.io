cinch

noun
a card game similar to all fours
Pronunciation: / sɪntʃ / (AmE), / sɪntʃ / (BrE)
A trick-taking card game related to all fours, in which players bid for the right to choose trumps.

Examples

play cinch cards
He learned to play cinch from his grandparents, who played every weekend.
The rules of cinch require players to bid before naming a trump suit.
