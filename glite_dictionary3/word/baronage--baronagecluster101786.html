baronage

noun
the collective nobility or peers of a kingdom
Pronunciation: / ˈbær.ə.nɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbær.ənɪdʒ / (BrE)
all the barons or peers of a kingdom considered collectively; the nobility of a realm.
