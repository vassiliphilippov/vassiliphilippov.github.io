formula

noun
set of instructions for making something
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.mjə.lə / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.mjʊ.lə / (BrE)
A set of instructions or directions for making or preparing something, especially by following a specific procedure or list of ingredients.

Examples

secret formula for cooking
Her grandmother gave her a secret formula for baking the perfect bread.
The chemist followed the formula to create a new type of soap.
This formula for homemade cleaning spray is both effective and eco-friendly.
