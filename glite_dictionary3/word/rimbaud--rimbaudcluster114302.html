Rimbaud

proper noun
French poet; precursor to surrealism
Pronunciation: / ræmˈboʊ / (AmE), / ræmˈboʊ / (BrE)
(1854–1891) A French poet known for his influential and innovative poetry, considered a precursor to the surrealists.
