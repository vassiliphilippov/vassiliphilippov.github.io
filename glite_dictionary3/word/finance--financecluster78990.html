finance

noun
provision of funds or capital
Pronunciation: / fəˈnæns / (AmE), / ˈfaɪnɑːns / (BrE)
the commercial activity of providing funds or capital for businesses, projects, or individuals

Examples

business finance
Banks are the main providers of finance to small and medium-sized enterprises.
Government finance plays a crucial role in infrastructure development.
The company sought external finance to expand its operations.

Related Terms

  • antonym – debt: money owed or due
  • synonym – funding: the act of providing money for a particular purpose
  • synonym – investment: the action or process of investing money for profit
