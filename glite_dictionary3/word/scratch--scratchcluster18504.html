to scratch

verb
scrape to relieve itching
Pronunciation: / skrætʃ / (AmE), / skrætʃ / (BrE)
to scrape or rub a part of the body, typically with one's fingernails, in order to relieve itching

Examples

to scratch an itch
If you scratch too much, you might break the skin.
My cat likes to scratch behind her ears when she feels uncomfortable.
She tried not to scratch her arm, even though it was very itchy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rub: to move one's hand or an object back and forth on a surface
  • antonym – soothe: to gently calm or relieve discomfort
