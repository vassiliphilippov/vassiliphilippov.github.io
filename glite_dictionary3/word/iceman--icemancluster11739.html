iceman

noun
man who delivers ice
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈaɪs.mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to cut and deliver ice, especially before the widespread use of refrigerators

Examples

iceman delivers ice
As a child, he watched the iceman chip and carry ice through the neighborhood.
Many city residents relied on the iceman before electric refrigerators became common.
The iceman delivered large blocks of ice to our house every morning during the summer.
