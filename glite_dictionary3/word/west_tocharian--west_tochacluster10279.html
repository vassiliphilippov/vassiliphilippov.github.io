West Tocharian

noun
western dialect of Tocharian language
Pronunciation: / wɛst toʊˈkɛəriən / (AmE), / west təʊˈkɛəriən / (BrE)
the western dialect of the extinct Tocharian language, formerly spoken in the ancient cities of Kucha, Qarašähär, and Turpan in Central Asia
