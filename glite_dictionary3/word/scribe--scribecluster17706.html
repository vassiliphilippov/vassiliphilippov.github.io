to scribe

verb
mark a line with a pointed tool
Pronunciation: / skraɪb / (AmE), / skraɪb / (BrE)
to mark or score a line on a surface with a pointed instrument, especially in metalworking or carpentry, to guide cutting or machining

Examples

to scribe a line
Before cutting the glass, she carefully scribed a guideline to follow.
Scribing allows machinists to transfer precise measurements by scribing marks on the material.
The technician used a sharp tool to scribe a line across the metal plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym – etch: engrave a design on a surface
  • synonym – score: make a groove, line, or mark on a surface
