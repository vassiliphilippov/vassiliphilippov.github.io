guise

noun
false or deceptive appearance
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪz / (AmE), / ɡaɪz / (BrE)
an outward appearance or form, typically one that is false or intended to deceive

Examples

guise of a friend
He visited the company in the guise of a journalist to collect inside information.
Her kindness was only a guise to gain their trust and steal their secrets.
The thief entered the house in the guise of a deliveryman.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disguise: to change appearance to conceal identity
  • synonym – mask: something that conceals the true nature of a thing
  • synonym – pretense: a false display of feelings or intentions
  • antonym – reality: the state of things as they actually exist
