diffuser

noun
device that distributes sound waves evenly
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjuzər / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːzə / (BrE)
a device or structure that spreads sound waves evenly in a space, reducing echoes and improving acoustic quality

Examples

diffuser for sound
A well-placed diffuser can help eliminate echoes in an auditorium.
Acoustic engineers use a diffuser to distribute sound evenly throughout the space.
The recording studio installed a diffuser on the back wall to improve the sound quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym – absorber: a device that absorbs sound waves, reducing reverberation
  • synonym – baffle: a device used to control or direct the flow of sound or light
