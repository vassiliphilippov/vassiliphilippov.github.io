suburbanized

adjective
developed with or surrounded by suburbs
Pronunciation: / səˈbɝː.bəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / səˈbɜː.bəˌnaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an area or city) having been developed with or surrounded by suburbs; exhibiting the characteristics of suburbs.

Examples

suburbanized area
A highly suburbanized region often lacks a distinct city center.
The city's rapid growth has left it looking increasingly suburbanized in recent years.
Their neighborhood, once rural, is now completely suburbanized with shopping centers and schools.

Related Terms

  • antonym – rural: relating to the countryside rather than the town or city
  • degree higher – urbanized: (of an area) developed into a city or town
