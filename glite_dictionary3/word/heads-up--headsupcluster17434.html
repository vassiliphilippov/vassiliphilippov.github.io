heads-up

noun
advance warning or notice
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdzˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈhɛdzˌʌp / (BrE)
an advance warning or notice about something, often given informally

Examples

I appreciate the heads-up about the road closure before I left for work.
