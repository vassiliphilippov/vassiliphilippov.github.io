sleeper

noun
a tropical fish resembling a goby
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpər / (AmE), / ˈsliːpə / (BrE)
a small tropical fish resembling a goby, known for resting quietly on the bottom in shallow water

Examples

tropical sleeper fish
Biologists discovered a new species of sleeper in the shallow waters of the estuary.
The aquarium had a colorful sleeper resting motionlessly near the gravel.
Unlike many active fish, the sleeper often remains still at the bottom for hours.
