queasiness

noun
feeling of nausea or unease
Pronunciation: / ˈkwiz.i.nəs /; / ˈkwiziˌnɛs / (AmE), / ˈkwiːz.i.nəs /; / ˈkwiːzɪnəs / (BrE)
a feeling of discomfort or mild nausea in the stomach, sometimes with an urge to vomit or a general sense of unease
