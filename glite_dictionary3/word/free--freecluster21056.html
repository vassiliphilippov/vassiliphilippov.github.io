free

adjective
not held in slavery or servitude
Pronunciation: / friː / (AmE), / friː / (BrE)
not held, controlled, or limited; able to act, move, or be as one wishes; not in captivity or slavery

Examples

free man
After the Civil War, he was finally a free man.
Many nations fought for the right to make their citizens free.
She was born free and never experienced slavery.
After years of studying, she finally had a free afternoon to relax.
In a free society, people can express their opinions openly.
You are free to leave whenever you wish.
