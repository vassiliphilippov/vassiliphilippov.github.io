to retransmit

verb
send or broadcast again
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.trænˈsmɪt / (AmE), / ˌriː.trænzˈmɪt / (BrE)
to send or broadcast something again, especially after receiving it from another source

Examples

to retransmit a signal
After recording the speech, the broadcaster decided to retransmit it during prime time.
The station will retransmit the emergency signal to nearby towns.
They must retransmit the data if the first transmission fails.

Related Terms

  • synonym – transmit: to send or pass something from one place or person to another
