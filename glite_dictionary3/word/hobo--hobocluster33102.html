hobo

noun
travelling worker; itinerant laborer
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊˌboʊ /; / ˈhoʊboʊ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a person who travels from place to place, often homeless, looking for temporary work, especially by riding freight trains
