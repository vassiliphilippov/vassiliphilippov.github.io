luffa

noun
tropical plant; fruit used as sponge
Pronunciation: / ˈluːfə / (AmE), / ˈlʌfə / (BrE)
A tropical climbing plant of the gourd family, whose fibrous fruit is used as a sponge and whose young fruits are edible.

Examples

luffa plant in garden
Farmers in the region cultivate luffa for both food and natural sponges.
She uses a luffa to exfoliate her skin while showering.
The luffa gourd becomes fibrous when mature and is perfect for scrubbing dishes.
