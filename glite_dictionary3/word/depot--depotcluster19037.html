depot

noun
place for storing goods
Pronunciation: / ˈdiː.poʊ, dɪˈpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdep.əʊ / (BrE)
a place where large quantities of goods are stored before they are distributed or sold

Examples

goods depot
After the harvest, the crops were sent directly to the central depot for storage.
The company operates a large depot near the airport for its imported products.
The new buses will be delivered to the city’s main depot tomorrow morning.
