Kama

proper noun
Hindu god of love and desire
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmə / (BrE)
In Hinduism, the god of love and erotic desire.

Examples

Kama the love god
Devotees sometimes offer prayers to Kama for happiness and romance in life.
In Hindu mythology, Kama is often depicted wielding a bow made of sugarcane.
