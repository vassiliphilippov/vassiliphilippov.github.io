interruption

noun
act of stopping or breaking continuity
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
an act, event, or period when something is stopped or delayed, breaking the flow of an activity or process
