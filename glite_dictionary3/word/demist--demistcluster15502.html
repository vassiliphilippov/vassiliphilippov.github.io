to demist

verb
remove mist from a surface
Pronunciation: / diˈmɪst / (AmE), / diˈmɪst / (BrE)
to remove mist, condensation, or fog from a surface, especially a window or mirror

Examples

to demist a window
Please demist the car windows before we start driving.
She demisted her glasses with a gentle wipe.
The bathroom mirror automatically demists after a hot shower.

Related Terms

  • synonym – defog: to remove fog from a surface
  • antonym – fog up: to become covered with fog or mist
