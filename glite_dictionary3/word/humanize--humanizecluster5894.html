to humanize

verb
make more humane or civilize
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmənaɪz / (BrE)
to make (something) more humane, compassionate, or civilized; to give human qualities to something

Examples

to humanize the workplace
Efforts to humanize the workplace have included flexible hours and wellness initiatives.
Good leaders always strive to humanize their organizations by listening to employees' needs.
The mayor launched several programs to humanize the city's homeless shelters.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dehumanize: to deprive of positive human qualities
