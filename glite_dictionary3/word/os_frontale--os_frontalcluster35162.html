os frontale

noun
frontal bone of the skull
Pronunciation: / oʊs frɒnˈtæli / (AmE), / ɒs frɒnˈtɑːli / (BrE)
the large cranial bone that forms the front part of the skull and includes the upper part of the eye sockets (orbits)

Examples

os frontale fracture
A fracture to the os frontale can affect both the forehead and the upper part of the eye socket.
During the anatomy lecture, the professor pointed to the os frontale on the human skull model.
The os frontale plays a crucial role in protecting the brain and supporting the structure of the face.
