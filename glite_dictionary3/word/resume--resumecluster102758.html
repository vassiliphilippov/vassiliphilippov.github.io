resume

noun
short summary or overview
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzuːm / (AmE), / rɪˈzjuːm / (BrE)
A short account or summary of events, facts, or a subject, often used to provide an overview.

Examples

resume of events
A brief resume of recent events was included in the newsletter.
At the end of the meeting, she gave a resume of the proposals discussed so far.
The report began with a resume of the key findings from the past year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – overview: a general review or summary of a subject
  • synonym – summary: a brief statement of the main points
