alkyl

noun
organic radical group in chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.kɪl / (AmE), / ˈæl.kɪl / (BrE)
any of a group of univalent organic radicals with the general formula CnH2n+1, derived from aliphatic hydrocarbons by the removal of one hydrogen atom.
