Turkmen

noun
former Soviet republic; now Turkmenistan
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrkˌmɛn / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkmɛn / (BrE)
A former Soviet republic in Central Asia, located east of the Caspian Sea, now known as Turkmenistan.

Examples

Turkmen republic
After the collapse of the Soviet Union, the Turkmen republic gained independence as Turkmenistan.
During the 1980s, economic policies in the Turkmen region focused on cotton production.
The Turkmen SSR was established in 1925 as part of the Soviet Union.
