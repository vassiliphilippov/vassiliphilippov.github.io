Plantago

noun
genus of plantain family herbs
Pronunciation: / plænˈteɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / planˈteɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
A genus of small, mainly herbaceous plants in the plantain family (Plantaginaceae), found throughout the world.
