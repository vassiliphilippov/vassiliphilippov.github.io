inanimate

adjective
appearing lifeless or dead
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnænɪmət / (AmE), / ɪˈnænɪmət / (BrE)
appearing dead; not breathing or showing any sign of life.

Examples

inanimate body
After hours underwater, the diver was pulled out, inanimate and pale.
The doctor checked for a pulse but found the body completely inanimate.
The victim lay inanimate on the ground until help arrived.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – dead: no longer alive
  • synonym – lifeless: without life; dead or apparently dead
  • degree lower – unconscious: not awake and aware of and responding to one's environment
