arteria gastrica dextra

noun
artery supplying lower stomach (medical)
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə ɡæsˈtrɪkə ˈdɛkstrə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ɡæsˈtrɪkə ˈdɛkstrə / (BrE)
A branch of the hepatic artery that supplies blood to the pyloric portion of the stomach along the lesser curvature.
