moralisation

noun
the act of making more moral
Pronunciation: / ˌmɔːrələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒrələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something moral or more moral, often by introducing moral principles or values

Examples

the moralisation of society
Her lectures focused on the moralisation of contemporary art and culture.
Some critics argue that excessive moralisation can stifle creativity and open discussion.
The moralisation of media content often sparks debate about freedom of expression.
