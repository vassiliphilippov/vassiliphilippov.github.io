castor

noun
swiveling wheel on furniture or equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈkæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.stə / (BrE)
a small swiveling wheel fixed to the bottom of furniture, equipment, or machinery to make it easier to move

Examples

castor furniture wheel
The hospital bed is equipped with locking castors for safety and mobility.
The new office chairs glide smoothly thanks to the castors attached to their legs.
