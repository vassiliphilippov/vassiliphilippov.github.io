tongue

noun
muscular organ in the mouth
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ / (AmE), / tʌŋ / (BrE)
the muscular organ in the mouth, covered with mucous membrane, used for tasting, swallowing, and speaking

Examples

tongue in the mouth
Doctors use a depressor to examine a patient's tongue.
She burned her tongue on the hot soup.
The cat licked its fur with its rough tongue.
