goosander

noun
large fish-eating duck (common merganser)
Pronunciation: / ɡuˈsændər / (AmE), / ɡuːˈsændə / (BrE)
a large fish-eating diving duck (the common merganser, Mergus merganser) found in Europe and North America, with a long, slender, serrated bill
