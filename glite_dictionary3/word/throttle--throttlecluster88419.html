to throttle

verb
kill or strangle by squeezing the throat
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɑː.təl / (AmE), / ˈθrɒt.əl / (BrE)
to kill or try to kill someone by squeezing their throat and cutting off their air supply; to strangle

Examples

to throttle a person
Police reported that the suspect tried to throttle his coworker during the argument.
She felt his hands throttle her throat, making it impossible to breathe.
The villain attempted to throttle his victim in a fit of rage.

  • synonym – choke: block the airway, preventing breathing
  • synonym – strangle: kill by squeezing the throat
