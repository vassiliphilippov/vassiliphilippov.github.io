to mechanize

verb
make mechanical; use machines instead of people
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛkəˌnaɪz /; / ˈmɛk.əˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛkənaɪz /; / ˈmiː.kə.naɪz / (BrE)
to use machines or automatic devices instead of people to do work or tasks

Examples

to mechanize farm work
