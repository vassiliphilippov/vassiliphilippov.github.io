chickpea

noun
edible legume seed; garbanzo bean
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkˌpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.piː / (BrE)
a round, pale-yellow seed (also called garbanzo bean) used as food, especially in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern dishes
