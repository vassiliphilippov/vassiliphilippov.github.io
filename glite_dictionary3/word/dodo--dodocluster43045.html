dodo

noun
person with outdated style
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊˌdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.dəʊ / (BrE)
An old-fashioned person, or someone whose style or ideas are no longer fashionable.

Examples

old dodo
If you don’t learn to use new technology, you’ll end up a dodo at work.
People called him a dodo because he still wore bell-bottom jeans.
She was worried she might look like a dodo in her grandmother’s dress.
