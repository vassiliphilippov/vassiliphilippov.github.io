taskmaster

noun
person who imposes hard work
Pronunciation: / ˈtæskˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈtɑːsˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who imposes hard, continuous work on others, often in a strict or demanding way

Examples

strict taskmaster
She recognized the value of having a strict taskmaster to help her achieve her goals.
Some students found their coach inspiring, while others saw him as a relentless taskmaster.
The new manager proved to be a taskmaster, constantly pushing his team to meet tight deadlines.

Related Terms

  • synonym – martinet: a person who demands strict obedience
  • synonym – slave driver: a person who makes others work very hard
