raffishly

adverb
in a rakish, unconventional manner
Pronunciation: / ˈræfɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈræfɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner that is unconventional and slightly disreputable, but attractive or charming; with a rakish or careless flair.

Examples

dressed raffishly stylishly
He dressed raffishly for the party, ignoring all the formal dress codes.
She tipped her hat raffishly to one side before entering the room.
The artist smiled raffishly, charming everyone with his unconventional style.
