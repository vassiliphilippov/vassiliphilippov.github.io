repetitiveness

noun
quality of being repetitive
Pronunciation: / rɪˌpɛt.ɪˈtɪv.nəs / (AmE), / rɪˌpɛt.ɪˈtɪv.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being repetitive; the tendency to repeat the same thing, often leading to boredom or verboseness.

Examples

the repetitiveness of tasks
Her writing style suffers from repetitiveness, which can make her arguments less engaging.
Many critics complained about the repetitiveness in the artist's latest album.
The repetitiveness of the instructions made the meeting seem much longer than it actually was.
