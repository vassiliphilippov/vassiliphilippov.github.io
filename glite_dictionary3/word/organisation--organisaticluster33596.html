organisation

noun
systematic arrangement; act of organizing
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːr.ɡən.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔː.ɡə.naɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of arranging, structuring, or setting up people, things, or activities in a systematic way
