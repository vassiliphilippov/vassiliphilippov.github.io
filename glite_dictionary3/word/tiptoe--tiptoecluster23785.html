tiptoe

adverb
on one's toes; quietly or carefully
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpˌtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɪptəʊ / (BrE)
on the toes or as if on the toes, often in order to move quietly or reach something high

Examples

stand tiptoe to reach
He stood tiptoe to reach the top shelf.
She waited tiptoe outside the room, hoping not to wake the baby.
The cat crept tiptoe across the kitchen floor.
