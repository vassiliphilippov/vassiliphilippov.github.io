September

noun
ninth month of the year
Pronunciation: / sɛpˈtɛmbɚ / (AmE), / sɛpˈtɛmbə / (BrE)
The ninth month of the year, following August and preceding October.

Examples

in September every year
Her birthday is in September, so she always has a party as summer ends.
Leaves start to change color in September as autumn arrives.
The school year usually begins in early September in many countries.
