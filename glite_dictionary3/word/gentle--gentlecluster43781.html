gentle

adjective
having a moderate slope
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛn.təl / (AmE), / ˈdʒen.tl̩ / (BrE)
(of a slope) having a gradual or moderate incline; not steep.

Examples

gentle slope
Farmers prefer gentle slopes because they are easier to cultivate.
The path led up a gentle hill, making for an easy walk.
The road curved around the mountain in a gentle ascent.

Related Terms

  • antonym – steep: having a sharp or high degree of incline; opposite of gentle when referring to slopes
