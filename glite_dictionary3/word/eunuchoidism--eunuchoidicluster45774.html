eunuchoidism

noun
condition of lacking male sexual development
Pronunciation: / juːˈnʌkɔɪ̈ˌdɪzəm / (AmE), / juːˈnʌkɔɪdɪzəm / (BrE)
a medical condition in which a person, typically male, lacks typical male sexual development due to absent or non-functional testicles

Examples

eunuchoidism in males
He was diagnosed with eunuchoidism after failing to develop expected secondary sexual characteristics during adolescence.
Hormonal therapy is sometimes prescribed to treat individuals with eunuchoidism.
Symptoms of eunuchoidism may include reduced muscle mass and a lack of facial hair.

  • synonym – eunuchism: state or condition of being a eunuch, often due to removal or absence of testes
