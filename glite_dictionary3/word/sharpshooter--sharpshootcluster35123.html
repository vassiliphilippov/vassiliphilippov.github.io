sharpshooter

noun
expert marksman, skilled shooter
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrpˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who is very skilled at shooting or hitting a target accurately, especially with firearms or in sports
