Dimetrodon

noun
extinct reptile genus with a sail-like fin
Pronunciation: / daɪˈmɛtrədɒn / (AmE), / daɪˈmiːtrədɒn / (BrE)
A genus of extinct carnivorous synapsid reptiles from the Permian period, characterized by a large sail-like fin on its back.
