to dab

verb
touch or hit lightly
Pronunciation: / dæb / (AmE), / dæb / (BrE)
to touch something lightly and quickly, or to gently apply a small amount of something

Examples

to dab paint gently
He dabbed the paint onto the canvas using a small brush.
She dabbed her eyes with a tissue to wipe away the tears.
The nurse gently dabbed the wound with antiseptic.
He dabs a little perfume on his wrist before leaving for work.
She dabbed the wound with a clean cloth to stop the bleeding.
With a sponge, gently dab the paint onto the canvas to create texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pat: to touch lightly and repeatedly with a flat hand
  • antonym – smear: to spread or apply a substance carelessly or unevenly
  • synonym – tap: to strike lightly and quickly
