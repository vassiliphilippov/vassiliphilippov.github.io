signalisation

noun
making something conspicuous or clearly marked
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪɡnələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪɡnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something clearly noticeable or marking it in a conspicuous way, especially to convey information, warning, or instruction

Examples

signalisation of a road
Effective signalisation at the railway crossing has reduced the number of accidents.
The signalisation of the dangerous bend was improved by adding bright warning signs.
The city authorities invested in better road signalisation to increase safety for pedestrians.

Related Terms

  • synonym – signalling: the act of sending a signal or making something clear
