bumpkin

noun
unsophisticated or awkward rural person
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmp.kɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌmp.kɪn / (BrE)
an unsophisticated or socially awkward person, typically from the countryside

Examples

country bumpkin
Despite being called a bumpkin, she quickly adapted to life in the city.
He was treated like a bumpkin when he first moved to the big city.
They laughed at the bumpkin for his unfashionable clothes and lack of manners.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sophisticate: a person with much worldly experience and knowledge of fashion and culture
  • synonym – yokel: a person who is regarded as unsophisticated and rural
