no-no

noun
prohibited or frowned-upon thing
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.nəʊ / (BrE)
an action, behavior, or thing that is not allowed or is strongly discouraged

Examples

no-no at work

Wearing jeans to the formal dinner was considered a no-no.
