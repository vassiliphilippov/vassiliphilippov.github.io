to obstipate

verb
to constipate severely (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbstɪˌpeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒbstɪˌpeɪt / (BrE)
(of a disease or condition) to cause severe constipation.

Examples

to obstipate the bowels
Certain neurological disorders can obstipate patients, leading to significant discomfort.
Dietary changes alone will not necessarily obstipate an otherwise healthy individual.
The medication may obstipate some patients if not used carefully.
