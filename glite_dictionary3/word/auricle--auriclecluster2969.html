auricle

noun
outer visible part of the ear
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪkəl / (BrE)
the outer, visible, cartilaginous part of the ear that collects and directs sound waves into the ear canal

Examples

auricle of the ear
A strong wind made his auricle turn red and cold.
She wore a new earring on her left auricle to match her necklace.
The doctor examined the patient's auricle for signs of infection.
