grumose

adjective
having a lumpy or grainy texture
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːˌmoʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡruːməʊs / (BrE)
having a lumpy or grainy texture, especially as formed from a liquid turning into a semisolid or solid mass.
