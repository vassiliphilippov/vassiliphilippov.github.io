Prunus ilicifolia

noun
evergreen Californian shrub with spiny leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnəs ɪˌlɪsɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈpruːnəs ɪˌlɪsɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub or small tree native to California, with spiny, leathery leaves, white flowers, and edible plum-like fruit
