vulgar

adjective
conspicuously indecent or tasteless
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlɡər / (AmE), / ˈvʌlɡə / (BrE)
offensive or indecent in a conspicuous and tasteless way, especially in speech, appearance, or behavior

Examples

vulgar jokes told

The comedian's routine relied heavily on vulgar language and sexual innuendo.
