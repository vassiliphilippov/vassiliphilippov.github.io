ballet

noun
theatrical dance performance
Pronunciation: / bæˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˈbæleɪ / (BrE)
A theatrical dance performance in which a story is told through expressive and highly formalized movements set to music.

Examples

ballet dancer
She has been training in ballet since she was six years old.
The Moscow company performed the famous ballet 'Swan Lake' to a packed audience.
The school's annual ballet attracts dance enthusiasts from all over the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dance: an act of moving rhythmically to music, typically following a set sequence of steps
