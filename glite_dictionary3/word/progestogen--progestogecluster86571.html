progestogen

noun
steroid hormone group related to progesterone
Pronunciation: / proʊˈdʒɛstəˌdʒən / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛstədʒən / (BrE)
any of a group of steroid hormones, including progesterone, that promote characteristics and functions associated with pregnancy and the menstrual cycle

Examples

progestogen hormone therapy
Doctors may prescribe a progestogen to help regulate the menstrual cycle.
Many birth control pills contain a synthetic progestogen as an active ingredient.
Research has shown that progestogen therapy can reduce certain health risks in women.
