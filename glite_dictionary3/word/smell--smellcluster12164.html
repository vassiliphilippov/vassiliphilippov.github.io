smell

noun
atmosphere or feeling of a place or situation (figurative)
Pronunciation: / smɛl / (AmE), / smɛl / (BrE)
an atmosphere or feeling that surrounds a place, situation, or event, especially as sensed by intuition or impression rather than physical senses

Examples

smell of fear
It's hard to ignore the smell of tension that lingers in the workplace after layoffs.
The meeting had the smell of secrecy, and everyone seemed nervous.
There was a smell of excitement in the air as the concert was about to begin.

  • synonym – atmosphere: the feeling or mood that a place, situation, or event gives
