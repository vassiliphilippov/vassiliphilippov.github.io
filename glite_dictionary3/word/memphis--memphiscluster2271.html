Memphis

proper noun
ancient Egyptian city on the Nile
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛm.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɛm.fɪs / (BrE)
an ancient city of Egypt located on the Nile, south of modern-day Cairo, which served as the capital of the Old Kingdom.

Examples

ancient Memphis ruins
Archaeologists have discovered many artifacts in Memphis that reveal the city's ancient splendor.
During the Old Kingdom, Memphis was one of the most important urban centers in Egypt.
