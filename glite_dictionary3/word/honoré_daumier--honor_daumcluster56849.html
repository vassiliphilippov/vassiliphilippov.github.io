Honoré Daumier

proper noun
French painter and satirical printmaker
Pronunciation: / ɔˈnɔːreɪ doʊˈmjɛr / (AmE), / ɔːˈnɔːreɪ doʊˈmjɛə / (BrE)
(1808–1879) A French painter, sculptor, and printmaker known especially for his satirical lithographs commenting on 19th-century bourgeois society and politics.
