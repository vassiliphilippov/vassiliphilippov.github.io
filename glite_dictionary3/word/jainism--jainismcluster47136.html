Jainism

noun
Indian religion emphasizing nonviolence and asceticism
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪnɪzəm /; / ˈdʒaɪnɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
an ancient Indian religion that teaches nonviolence, asceticism, and the pursuit of spiritual liberation
