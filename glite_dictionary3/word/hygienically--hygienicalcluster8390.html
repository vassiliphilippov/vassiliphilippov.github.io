hygienically

adverb
in a clean and healthy way
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdʒiːnɪkli / (AmE), / haɪˈdʒiːnɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that promotes or maintains cleanliness and good health.

Examples

hygienically clean hands
All medical instruments must be handled hygienically to prevent infections.
Food should be prepared hygienically to ensure it is safe for consumption.
The kitchen was cleaned hygienically after every meal.
