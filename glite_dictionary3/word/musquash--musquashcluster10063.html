musquash

noun
semiaquatic North American rodent
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.kwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kwɒʃ / (BrE)
A large semiaquatic rodent native to North America, similar to a beaver, with dark glossy brown fur.
