jade-green

adjective
color between bluish green and yellowish green
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪd ˌɡrin / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪd ˌɡriːn / (BrE)
having a color resembling that of jade, typically varying from bluish green to yellowish green

The river glimmered with a mysterious jade-green hue under the morning sun.
