pinger

noun
sonar pulse-generating device
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋə / (BrE)
a device that generates pulses of sound for use in echo sounding, particularly in sonar equipment

Examples

underwater pinger device
Divers followed the signal of the pinger attached to the sunken aircraft's black box.
The research team checked the pinger regularly to ensure it was emitting strong acoustic pulses.
The vessel deployed a pinger to map the ocean floor using sonar technology.
