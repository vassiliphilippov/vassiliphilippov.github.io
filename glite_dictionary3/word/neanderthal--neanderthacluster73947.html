Neanderthal

adjective
relating to Neanderthal man or primitive
Pronunciation: / niˈæn.dərˌtɑl, niˈæn.dərˌθɔl / (AmE), / niˈɒn.dəˌtɑːl, niˈæn.dəˌθɑːl / (BrE)
relating to, belonging to, or resembling the extinct human species Neanderthal man; primitive or uncivilized (often capitalized).
