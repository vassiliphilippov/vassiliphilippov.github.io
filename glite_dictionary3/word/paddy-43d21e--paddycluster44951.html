paddy

noun
rice in the husk
Pronunciation: / ˈpædi / (AmE), / ˈpædi / (BrE)
rice in the husk, either still growing in the field or harvested but not yet milled

Examples

harvest paddy
Freshly threshed paddy is stored in large sacks to await milling.
The farmers harvested the paddy fields just before the rainy season began.
You can see paddy drying in the sun along the village roads.
