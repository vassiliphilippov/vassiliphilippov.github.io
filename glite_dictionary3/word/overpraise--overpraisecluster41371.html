overpraise

noun
excessive or undeserved praise
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈpreɪz / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpreɪz / (BrE)
excessive or inappropriate praise that is not fully deserved

Examples

teacher's overpraise
