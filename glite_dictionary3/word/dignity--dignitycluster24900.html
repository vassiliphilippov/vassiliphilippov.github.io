dignity

noun
worthiness of respect or honor
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪɡ.nə.ti /; / ˈdɪɡnəti / (AmE), / ˈdɪɡ.nɪ.ti /; / ˈdɪɡnəti / (BrE)
the quality or manner of being worthy of respect, honor, or self-respect; showing composure and seriousness
