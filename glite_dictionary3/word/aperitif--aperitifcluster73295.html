aperitif

noun
pre-meal alcoholic drink
Pronunciation: / əˈpɛr.əˌtif / (AmE), / əˈpɛr.ɪ.tiːf / (BrE)
An alcoholic drink served before a meal to stimulate the appetite.

Examples

aperitif before dinner
Many French restaurants offer a complimentary aperitif to guests.
She ordered an aperitif to start the evening in style.
We enjoyed a glass of aperitif on the terrace before dinner.
