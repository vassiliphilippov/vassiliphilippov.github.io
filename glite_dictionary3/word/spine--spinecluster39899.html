spine

noun
series of vertebrae; backbone
Pronunciation: / spaɪn / (AmE), / spaɪn / (BrE)
the series of vertebrae that forms the central supporting structure of the skeleton and protects the spinal cord

Examples

human spine
She injured her spine in a car accident, which required months of rehabilitation.
The human spine consists of thirty-three vertebrae stacked in a column.
X-rays revealed a fracture in his lower spine after the fall.
