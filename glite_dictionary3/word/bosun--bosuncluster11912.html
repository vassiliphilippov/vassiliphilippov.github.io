bo'sun

noun
ship's officer in charge of crew and equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊsən / (AmE), / ˈbəʊsən / (BrE)
a petty officer on a merchant ship responsible for supervising the work of the crew and maintaining the ship's equipment and fittings
