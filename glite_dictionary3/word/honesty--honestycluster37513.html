honesty

noun
plant with decorative, silvery seedpods
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnəsti / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪsti / (BrE)
A southeastern European plant (Lunaria annua) cultivated for its fragrant purplish flowers and distinctive round, flat, silver-white seedpods used for decoration.

Examples

honesty plant flowers
After the flowers fade, honesty produces translucent pods that resemble silver coins.
Many gardeners plant honesty for its striking appearance in both spring and autumn.
The dried seedpods of honesty add a delicate touch to floral arrangements.
