to address

verb
deal with a topic in speech, writing, or art
Pronunciation: / əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
to deal with or discuss a subject, problem, or issue

Examples

to address a problem
During the meeting, she addressed the topic of climate change in depth.
The film addresses the consequences of addiction through powerful storytelling.
The novel addresses important social issues such as poverty and injustice.
During the meeting, she addressed the issue of budget cuts directly.
The company plans to address customer complaints by improving its support services.
We need to address the environmental impact of our products before launching them.

  • synonym – tackle: to make a determined effort to deal with a difficult problem or task
