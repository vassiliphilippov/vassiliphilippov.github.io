smoke-dried

adjective
dried by exposure to smoke (usually food)
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkˌdraɪd / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkˌdraɪd / (BrE)
(especially of meat or fish) dried and preserved by exposure to wood smoke.
