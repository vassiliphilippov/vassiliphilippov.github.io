doohickey

noun
unspecified object; gadget
Pronunciation: / ˈduːˌhɪki / (AmE), / ˈduːˌhɪki / (BrE)
an object whose name you do not know or have forgotten; a gadget or thingamajig

Examples

Whenever I can't remember a tool's name, I just call it a doohickey.
