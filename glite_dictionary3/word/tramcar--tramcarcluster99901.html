tramcar

noun
electric rail vehicle for city transport
Pronunciation: / ˈtræmˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈtræmkɑː / (BrE)
a rail vehicle powered by electricity, used for passenger transport in urban areas, typically running on tracks along city streets

Examples

city tramcar
During our visit to Lisbon, we took a historic tramcar through the winding streets.
The city's new tramcar system makes commuting much easier for residents.
We watched as the bright yellow tramcar glided smoothly along the tracks downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym – streetcar: an electric vehicle running on city tracks
  • synonym – trolley: a wheeled vehicle that runs on tracks, especially in US cities
