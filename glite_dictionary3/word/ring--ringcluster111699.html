ring

noun
sound made by a bell or similar object
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪŋ / (BrE)
the sound produced by a bell, telephone, or similar object when struck or activated

Examples

ring of a bell
He heard the distant ring of the church bell early in the morning.
The sudden ring of the telephone startled her during the meeting.
There was a cheerful ring as the doorbell announced another visitor.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – clink: a short, sharp sound of metal being struck
  • synonym – peal: a loud repeated or reverberating sound of bells
  • synonym – toll: the sound of a large bell being struck slowly
