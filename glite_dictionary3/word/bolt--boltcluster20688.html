bolt

noun
a sudden, swift movement
Pronunciation: / boʊlt / (AmE), / bəʊlt / (BrE)
a sudden, swift movement or the act of moving very quickly and suddenly, especially to escape or reach something

Examples

a quick bolt forward
The rabbit made a sudden bolt across the field when it sensed danger.
When the alarm sounded, there was a bolt for the nearest exit.
With a bolt of energy, she raced down the street to catch the bus.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dash: a sudden, quick movement; a burst of speed
  • synonym – sprint: a short, fast run
