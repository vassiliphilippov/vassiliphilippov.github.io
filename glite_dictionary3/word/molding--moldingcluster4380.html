molding

noun
decorative strip for ornamentation or finishing
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative strip, usually of wood, plaster, or similar material, used for ornamentation or to cover transitions on walls, ceilings, or furniture
