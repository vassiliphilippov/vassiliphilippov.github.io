raglan

noun
coat or sweater with diagonal sleeves
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡlən / (AmE), / ˈræɡlən / (BrE)
a coat or sweater with sleeves that extend in one piece to the collar, leaving a diagonal seam from underarm to collarbone

Examples

raglan sleeve sweater
He wore a gray raglan overcoat during the chilly autumn evening.
She prefers sweaters with raglan sleeves for a more comfortable fit.
The new collection includes a stylish raglan made from soft wool.
