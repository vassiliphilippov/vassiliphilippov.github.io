hobbyhorse

noun
child's toy horse for riding
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːbiˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈhɒbiˌhɔːs / (BrE)
a child's toy consisting of a model of a horse mounted on rockers or a stick, which the child rides by straddling and pretending to ride a real horse

Examples

ride a hobbyhorse
As a child, she loved riding her hobbyhorse around the living room for hours.
He received a new hobbyhorse for his birthday and couldn't wait to show his friends.
The old-fashioned hobbyhorse was passed down through three generations in their family.
