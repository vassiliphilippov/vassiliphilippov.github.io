butt

noun
joint formed by joining ends or edges
Pronunciation: / bʌt / (AmE), / bʌt / (BrE)
a joint formed by fastening two ends or edges together without overlapping

Examples

butt joint boards
A butt is commonly used in woodworking where two boards are joined end to end.
The plumber used a butt to connect the two pieces of pipe securely.
Welders frequently create a butt to fuse metal surfaces without any overlap.

Related Terms

  • antonym – lap joint: a joint made by overlapping two pieces
