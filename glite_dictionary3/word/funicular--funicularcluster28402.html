funicular

adjective
operated by a cable
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɪkjələr / (AmE), / fjʊˈnɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
(of a railway or system) relating to or operated by a cable, typically one used to pull vehicles up a steep slope.

Examples

funicular cable system
A funicular system is often the best solution for transporting people up steep hills.
The funicular railway climbed the mountainside with ease thanks to its cable system.
The city invested in a funicular cable lift to connect the lower and upper districts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cable car: a vehicle moved by cables, often up inclines
