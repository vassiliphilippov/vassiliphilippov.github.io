logomania

noun
pathological excessive talking
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊɡəˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌlɒɡəˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
A pathological tendency to talk excessively and incoherently; excessive and uncontrollable talking.

Examples

uncontrollable logomania talking
Her logomania became apparent during the interview, where she barely paused for breath.
His logomania made meetings exhausting, as he could hardly stop talking for hours on end.
Some psychiatric disorders may include symptoms of logomania, with seemingly endless streams of words.

Related Terms

  • synonym – logorrhea: excessive and often incoherent talkativeness
