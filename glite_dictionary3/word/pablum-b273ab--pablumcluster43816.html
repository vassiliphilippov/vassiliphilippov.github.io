pablum

noun
worthless or oversimplified ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈpæbləm / (AmE), / ˈpabləm / (BrE)
ideas, writing, or statements that are bland, simplistic, and lacking in intellectual substance or originality; worthless or oversimplified ideas.
