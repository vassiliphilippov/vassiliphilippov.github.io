mufti

noun
Islamic religious law interpreter
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌf.ti / (AmE), / ˈmʌf.ti / (BrE)
an Islamic scholar who is qualified to interpret and expound on Muslim religious law

Examples

the mufti gives advice
As the highest religious authority, the mufti offers guidance on complex legal matters.
Many people sought the advice of the mufti before making important decisions.
The mufti was called upon to issue a ruling regarding the local community's dispute.
