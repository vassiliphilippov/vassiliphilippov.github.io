honeybells

noun
African shrub with yellow, honey-scented flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌbɛlz / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌbɛlz / (BrE)
An African shrub with creeping stems and slender, yellow, honey-scented flowers, sometimes produced singly or in pairs.
