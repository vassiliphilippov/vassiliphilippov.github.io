realization

noun
making something real or concrete
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɪəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of making something real or concrete, such as a plan, idea, or character

Examples

realization of a dream
His dream finally saw realization when the project was approved.
The realization of the plan required cooperation from every team member.
The successful launch marked the realization of years of engineering effort.
Her realization of the character brought the story to life for the audience.
The sculptor's work is a remarkable realization of ancient myths.
Through detailed models, the architect achieved a striking realization of his vision.
