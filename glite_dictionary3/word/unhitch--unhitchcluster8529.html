to unhitch

verb
to unfasten or detach
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɪtʃ / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɪtʃ / (BrE)
to unfasten or release something from a hitch or attachment

Examples

to unhitch a trailer
He quickly unhitched the trailer from the back of the truck.
Please unhitch the horses before we rest for lunch.
She unhitched her bike from the rack and rode away.

Related Terms

  • synonym – detach: to separate or remove from something
  • antonym – hitch: to fasten or attach with a hook or knot
