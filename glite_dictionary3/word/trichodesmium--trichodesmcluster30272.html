Trichodesmium

noun
genus of filamentous blue-green algae
Pronunciation: / trɪˌkoʊˈdɛzmiəm / (AmE), / trɪˌkəʊˈdɛzmɪəm / (BrE)
A genus of filamentous blue-green algae (cyanobacteria) found chiefly in tropical and subtropical ocean waters, known for forming large colonies and sometimes causing algal blooms.
