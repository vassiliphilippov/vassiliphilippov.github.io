Chukchi

noun
language of the Chukchi people
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʊk.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈtʃʊk.tʃi / (BrE)
the language of the Chukchi people, an indigenous and isolated language of unknown origin spoken in northeastern Siberia, noted for differences in pronunciation between men and women

Examples

the Chukchi language
Linguists have studied the Chukchi language for its unusual grammatical structures.

The Chukchi language features pronunciation differences based on the speaker's gender.
