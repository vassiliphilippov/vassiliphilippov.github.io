curling

noun
ice game using sliding stones
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
a game played on ice in which players slide heavy, handled stones toward a target area, aiming for precision and strategy

Examples

curling match
She joined a local curling club to learn the basics of the game.
The Winter Olympics always feature curling as one of the main sports.
The strategy in curling is often compared to chess on ice.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ice hockey: a fast contact sport played on ice with sticks and a puck
