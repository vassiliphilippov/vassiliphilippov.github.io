decentralized

adjective
distributed away from a central authority
Pronunciation: / diˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / diˈsɛntrəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an organization or system) having authority, power, or functions distributed away from a central location or authority to local or regional levels.

Examples

decentralized system design
A decentralized energy grid allows communities to generate and control their own electricity.
Many advocates believe a decentralized approach to education is more responsive to local needs.
The company adopted a decentralized management structure to give more autonomy to regional offices.

Related Terms

  • antonym – centralized: having concentration of authority or function at a single center or central authority
