sextillion

noun
a number: 1 followed by 21 (US) or 36 (UK) zeros
Pronunciation: / sɛkˈstɪljən / (AmE), / sɛkˈstɪljən / (BrE)
the number represented as 1 followed by 21 zeros in the American system (10^21), or 1 followed by 36 zeros in the British system (10^36)
