scan

noun
systematic examination or inspection
Pronunciation: / skæn / (AmE), / skæn / (BrE)
a systematic examination or inspection of an area, often using an instrument or device

Examples

scan of shelves
After a quick scan of the shelves, she found the book she was looking for.
Before boarding, airport security performed a scan of every passenger's bag.
The scientist conducted a detailed scan of the specimen under the microscope.
