to post

verb
ride rising and falling in rhythm with a trotting horse
Pronunciation: / poʊst / (AmE), / pəʊst / (BrE)
(of a rider) to rise and fall in the saddle in rhythm with a horse's trotting gait, especially in English or Western riding.

Examples

to post while riding
Instead of sitting the trot, he prefers posting to keep his balance.
Many riders post to the trot to make the ride smoother for both themselves and the horse.
She learned how to post during her first Western riding lesson.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sit: remain seated in the saddle while riding at a trot
