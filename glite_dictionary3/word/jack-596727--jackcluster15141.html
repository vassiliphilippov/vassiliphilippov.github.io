to jack

verb
lift with a mechanical jack
Pronunciation: / dʒæk / (AmE), / dʒæk / (BrE)
to lift or raise something, especially a vehicle, using a special mechanical device called a jack.

Examples

to jack up car
Before changing the tire, make sure to jack up the car securely.
She quickly jacked up the front wheels using a hydraulic jack.
The mechanic jacked the vehicle to inspect the undercarriage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hoist: to raise or lift, especially by mechanical means
  • antonym – lower: to move something to a lower position
