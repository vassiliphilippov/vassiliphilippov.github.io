hayrack

noun
frame for holding or transporting hay
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌræk / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌræk / (BrE)
a frame fitted to a wagon or cart to increase the capacity for carrying hay

Examples

hayrack on a wagon
The old farmer added a hayrack to his wagon to carry more feed during harvest.
They repaired the broken hayrack before heading out to collect the bales.
Without a hayrack, the load kept spilling onto the road as they transported the hay.
