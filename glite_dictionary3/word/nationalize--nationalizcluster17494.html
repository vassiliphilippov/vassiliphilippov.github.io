to nationalize

verb
bring under state ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnaʃ(ə)nəlaɪz / (BrE)
(of a government) to bring an industry, company, or asset under state control or ownership.

Examples

to nationalize an industry
Several industries were nationalized after the revolution to ensure public welfare.
The government decided to nationalize the failing railway system to improve its management.
There was a heated debate about whether to nationalize major energy companies.

Related Terms

  • antonym – privatize: to transfer ownership from the public sector to private individuals or organizations
