to mount

verb
(of male animals) copulate with
Pronunciation: / maʊnt / (AmE), / maʊnt / (BrE)
(of a male animal) to climb onto another animal for the purpose of copulation.

Examples

male mounts a female
During breeding season, the buck will attempt to mount several does.
The veterinarian observed as the stallion mounted the mare.
The zookeeper intervened when one chimpanzee tried to mount another.
