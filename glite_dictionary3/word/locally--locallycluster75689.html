locally

adverb
in a particular locality
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.kə.li / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.kəl.i / (BrE)
in a particular area, community, or locality rather than generally or nationally.

Examples

locally grown food
Decisions about the new park were made locally, not by the central government.
Many ingredients used in this dish are sourced locally.
The charity is funded locally through community events and donations.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – regionally: over a larger area than local, but less than national
