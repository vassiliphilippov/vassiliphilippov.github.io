to overfeed

verb
feed with too much food
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈfid / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈfiːd / (BrE)
to give someone or something more food than is necessary or healthy

Examples

to overfeed a pet
It is dangerous to overfeed fish, as leftover food pollutes the tank.
Many people tend to overfeed their pets, leading to obesity.
She realized she shouldn't overfeed the baby or it could get sick.
