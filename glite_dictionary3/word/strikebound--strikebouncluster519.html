strikebound

adjective
closed due to a strike
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of a business, service, etc.) closed or unable to operate because of a workers' strike.

strikebound factory
During the 1970s, strikebound industries frequently appeared in the headlines.
