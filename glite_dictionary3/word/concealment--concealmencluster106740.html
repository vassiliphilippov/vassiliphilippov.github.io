concealment

noun
the state of being hidden
Pronunciation: / kənˈsiːlmənt / (AmE), / kənˈsiːlmənt / (BrE)
The state of being hidden or kept out of sight.

Examples

total concealment
Effective concealment of the evidence made the investigation difficult.
She revealed her secret after years of strict concealment.
The burglar relied on the concealment of darkness to avoid detection.
