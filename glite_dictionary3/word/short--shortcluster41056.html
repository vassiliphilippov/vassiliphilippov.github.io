short

adjective
baked goods: crumbly or flaky
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt / (BrE)
(of baked goods) having a crumbly or flaky texture due to a high proportion of shortening or fat.

Examples

short pastry
Shortbread should be short and crumbly, not dense or chewy.
The chef recommended adding more butter to make the pastry extra short.
The pie had a wonderfully short crust that melted in your mouth.
