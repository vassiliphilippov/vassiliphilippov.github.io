to lose

verb
fail to get or obtain
Pronunciation: / luːz / (AmE), / luːz / (BrE)
to fail to get, obtain, or take advantage of something, especially an opportunity

Examples

to lose a chance
He realized too late that he would lose the grant if he didn't apply on time.
If you hesitate now, you might lose the offer.
She didn't want to lose her only chance at getting the job.

Related Terms

  • antonym – gain: obtain or secure (something desired or beneficial)
  • synonym – miss: fail to take advantage of or reach an opportunity
