vane

noun
rotating device showing wind direction
Pronunciation: / veɪn / (AmE), / veɪn / (BrE)
A flat or curved plate attached to an elevated structure, such as a roof, that rotates freely to indicate the direction from which the wind is blowing.

Examples

weather vane spins
Climbers noticed the rusted vane was still spinning at the top of the church steeple.
The vane turned rapidly as the storm approached, pointing north.
The old farmhouse had a metal vane on its roof shaped like a rooster.
