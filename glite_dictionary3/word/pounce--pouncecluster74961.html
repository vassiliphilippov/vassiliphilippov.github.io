pounce

noun
a sudden leap or attack
Pronunciation: / paʊns / (AmE), / paʊns / (BrE)
a sudden jump or movement to seize or attack, especially by an animal or in a figurative sense

Examples

a quick pounce forward
Investors waited for the perfect moment to make their pounce on the undervalued stocks.
The athlete made a pounce for the ball, stunning the opposing team.
With a swift pounce, the cat caught the mouse before it could escape.
