enamel

noun
paint with a hard, shiny finish
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnæməl / (AmE), / ɪˈnaməl /; / ɪˈnæməl / (BrE)
a hard, smooth, and often glossy coating or substance used for protection or decoration on metal, glass, pottery, or as a type of paint
