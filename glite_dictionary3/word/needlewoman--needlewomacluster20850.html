needlewoman

noun
woman who sews professionally
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdəlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈniːdlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who makes or mends clothes by sewing, especially as a profession.

Examples

skilled needlewoman
As a skilled needlewoman, she created beautiful gowns for her clients.
Her grandmother was a renowned needlewoman in her hometown.
The old book was embroidered by a talented needlewoman from the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym – seamstress: a woman whose occupation is sewing
  • synonym – tailor: a person who makes, repairs, or alters clothing professionally
