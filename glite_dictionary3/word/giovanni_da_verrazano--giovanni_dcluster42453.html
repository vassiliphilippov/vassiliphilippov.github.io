Giovanni da Verrazano

proper noun
Italian explorer of eastern North America
Pronunciation: / dʒiˈɑːvni dɑ vɛrəˈzɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / dʒiˈævni də ˌvɛrəˈzɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
(c. 1485–1528) An Italian explorer and navigator from Florence, known for charting the eastern coast of North America.
