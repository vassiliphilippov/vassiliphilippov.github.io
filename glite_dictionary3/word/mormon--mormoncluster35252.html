Mormon

proper noun
ancient prophet in Latter-Day Saint belief
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔr.mən / (AmE), / ˈmɔː.mən / (BrE)
(in the beliefs of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints) The ancient prophet whose writings form part of the Book of Mormon and were revealed to Joseph Smith.

Examples

Mormon the prophet
According to Latter-Day Saint scriptures, Mormon compiled the records of his people into what became the Book of Mormon.
Many missionaries explain the story of Mormon when introducing their faith to others.
The Church teaches that Mormon was a prophet and historian who lived in the ancient Americas.
