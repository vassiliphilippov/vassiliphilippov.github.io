pipeline

noun
pipe for transporting liquids or gases
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpaɪp.laɪn / (BrE)
A long pipe, typically underground, used to transport liquids or gases over long distances.

Examples

oil pipeline
A leak in the pipeline caused an environmental hazard.
A new gas pipeline is being constructed between the two countries.
The oil pipeline stretches for hundreds of miles across the desert.
