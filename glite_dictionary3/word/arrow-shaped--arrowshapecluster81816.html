arrow-shaped

adjective
shaped like an arrowhead
Pronunciation: / ˈæroʊ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈærəʊ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of an arrowhead, typically narrow and pointed, without flaring base lobes (especially of a leaf).

Examples

arrow-shaped leaf
Her drawing featured an arrow-shaped sign pointing towards the entrance.
Some aquatic plants have arrow-shaped blades, ideal for cutting through water currents.
The plant has arrow-shaped leaves that help it stand out in the garden.
