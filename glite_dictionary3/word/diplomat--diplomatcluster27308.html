diplomat

noun
official engaged in international negotiations
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpləˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈdɪpləmæt / (BrE)
an official person whose job is to represent their country abroad and manage international relations, especially through negotiation

Examples

career diplomat
After years of service, he became the senior diplomat at the embassy.
Being a successful diplomat requires tact, patience, and exceptional communication skills.
She worked as a diplomat and represented her country in international meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ambassador: a diplomatic official of the highest rank sent by one country as its long-term representative to another
