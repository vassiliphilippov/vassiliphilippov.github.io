such

adverb
to a great or extreme degree (emphatic use)
Pronunciation: / sʌtʃ / (AmE), / sʌtʃ / (BrE)
to so extreme a degree; used before a noun or noun phrase to emphasize its quality or extent.

Examples

such delicious food
I've never tasted such delicious food in my life.
She spoke with such enthusiasm that everyone wanted to join.
Why are you being such a perfectionist about this project?
