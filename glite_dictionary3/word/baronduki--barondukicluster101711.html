baronduki

noun
Siberian ground squirrel
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːrənˈduːki / (AmE), / ˌbærənˈduːki / (BrE)
A terrestrial squirrel native to Siberia, also known as the Siberian chipmunk, recognized for its striped back and burrowing habits.

Examples

wild baronduki burrow
Researchers observed how the baronduki collects nuts and seeds before winter.
The baronduki is a common sight in the forests of eastern Siberia.
Unlike other squirrels, the baronduki prefers living in underground burrows.
