cricket

noun
jumping insect that chirps
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪkət / (AmE), / ˈkrɪkɪt / (BrE)
a small jumping insect of the family Gryllidae, the male of which produces a characteristic chirping sound by rubbing its forewings together

Examples

hear cricket chirping
A cricket sang loudly outside my window all night.
Children tried to catch a cricket in the garden for their bug collection.
The male cricket produces sound by rubbing its wings together.
