to tap

verb
secretly listen to calls
Pronunciation: / tæp / (AmE), / tæp / (BrE)
to secretly listen to telephone or telegraph communications by connecting a device to the wire.

Examples

to tap a phone
It's illegal to tap someone's telephone without proper authorization.
The police decided to tap the suspect's phone to gather evidence.
They discovered that their business lines had been tapped for months.
