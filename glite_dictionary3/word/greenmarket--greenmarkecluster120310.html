greenmarket

noun
outdoor marketplace for farm products
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinˌmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
an open-air market where farm products, such as fresh fruits and vegetables, are sold directly by growers
