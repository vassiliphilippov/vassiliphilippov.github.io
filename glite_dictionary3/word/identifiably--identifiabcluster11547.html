identifiably

adverb
in an identifiable manner
Pronunciation: / aɪˌdɛntəˈfaɪəbli / (AmE), / aɪˌdɛntɪˈfaɪəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is able to be identified or recognized.

Examples

identifiably different accent
She spoke in a identifiably regional accent that set her apart from the others.
The two products are identifiably distinct despite their similar packaging.
The writing style is identifiably hers, with the same unique phrasing and tone.

Related Terms

  • synonym – identifiable: able to be recognized or distinguished
  • antonym – unidentifiably: in a manner that cannot be identified
