hommos

noun
Middle Eastern chickpea spread or dip
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌməs / (AmE), / ˈhʌm.əs / (BrE)
A thick Middle Eastern spread made from mashed chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic, typically served as a dip for pita bread.

Examples

hommos with pita bread
I love spreading hommos on my sandwiches for added flavor.
Many grocery stores offer different varieties of hommos with herbs or roasted peppers.
She served a bowl of hommos with warm pita at the party.
