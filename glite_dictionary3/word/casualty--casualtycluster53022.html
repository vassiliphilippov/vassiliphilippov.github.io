casualty

noun
fatal accident; accident causing death
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʒjəlti / (AmE), / ˈkæʒjuəlti / (BrE)
An accident or event that results in a person's death.

Examples

workplace casualty incident
The company was fined following a workplace casualty last year.
The motorway was closed for hours after a casualty involving a lorry and two cars.
There was only one casualty reported in the otherwise minor train derailment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fatality: a death resulting from an accident or disaster
