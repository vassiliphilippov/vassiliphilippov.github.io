hemolytic

adjective
causing destruction of red blood cells
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to, involving, or causing the destruction of red blood cells (hemolysis)

Examples

hemolytic reaction
A hemolytic reaction can sometimes occur after a blood transfusion if the blood types are not compatible.
