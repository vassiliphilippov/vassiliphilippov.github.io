semitransparent

adjective
partially transparent; lets some light through
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi.trænˈspɛrənt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmi.trænˈspærənt / (BrE)
allowing some light to pass through but not enough to see clearly through; partially transparent.

Examples

semitransparent curtains
The semitransparent curtains let in enough daylight to gently illuminate the room.
The artist painted with semitransparent layers of color to achieve a luminous appearance.
They used a semitransparent screen to create a dreamy, softened effect on stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym – opaque: not allowing light to pass through
  • synonym – translucent: allowing light to pass through diffusely
  • degree higher – transparent: allowing light to pass through so objects behind can be seen clearly
