nonassertive

adjective
not self-assertive; quiet or unforceful
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əˈsɜr.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈsɜː.tɪv / (BrE)
not inclined to assert oneself; not aggressively self-assured, though not necessarily lacking in confidence

Examples

nonassertive personality type
Despite being nonassertive, he led the team competently and earned their respect.
Her nonassertive style made her approachable and easy to work with.
The manager preferred employees who were nonassertive and quietly diligent.
