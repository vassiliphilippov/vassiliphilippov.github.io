folks

noun
people in general; group of people
Pronunciation: / foʊks / (AmE), / fəʊks / (BrE)
People in general, especially ordinary people or members of a community; often used informally to refer to a group of people.

Examples

friendly folks
Hey, folks, dinner is ready!
I met some friendly folks at the community event last night.
These folks have lived in the village for generations.
