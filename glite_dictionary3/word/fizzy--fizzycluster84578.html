fizzy

adjective
containing bubbles; effervescent
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzi / (AmE), / ˈfɪzi / (BrE)
(of a drink) containing bubbles of gas; effervescent or sparkling.

Examples

fizzy drink
After being left open overnight, the soda was no longer fizzy.
She prefers fizzy water to still water with her meal.
The children loved drinking fizzy lemonade at the party.
