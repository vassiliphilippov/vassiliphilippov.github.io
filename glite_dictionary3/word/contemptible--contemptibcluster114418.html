contemptible

adjective
deserving contempt or scorn
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛmptəbəl / (AmE), / kənˈtɛmptɪbəl / (BrE)
Deserving of contempt; deserving to be despised or regarded with scorn.

Examples

contemptible behavior
His contemptible behavior at the meeting shocked everyone present.
It was a contemptible attempt to deceive the public.
She found the way he treated his friends utterly contemptible.
