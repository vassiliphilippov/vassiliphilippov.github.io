literate

noun
a person who can read and write
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.ə.rət / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.ər.ət / (BrE)
A person who is able to read and write.

Examples

a literate person
As a literate, she was entrusted with keeping records for her village.
The school launched a program to help every child become a literate by the age of ten.
There are fewer literates in remote regions, highlighting the need for educational resources.
