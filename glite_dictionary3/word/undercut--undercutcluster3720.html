undercut

noun
tender loin meat cut
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkʌt / (BrE)
the tender meat from the loin on each side of the vertebral column, especially as used in cooking

Examples

beef undercut
