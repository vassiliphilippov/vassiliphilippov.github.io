vigor

noun
lively, imaginative style in writing or art
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪɡə / (BrE)
a quality in writing or art marked by imagination, liveliness, and intensity of style

Examples

vigor in writing
Critics admired the vigor of her writing, noting how it brought the story to life.
His speech was praised for its vigor and originality.
The author infuses her prose with such vigor that every page feels electric.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dullness: lack of lively or imaginative style in creative works
  • synonym – energy: vigorous and lively quality, especially in writing or art
