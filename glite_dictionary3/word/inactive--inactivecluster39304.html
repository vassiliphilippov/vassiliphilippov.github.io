inactive

adjective
not erupting (of volcanoes)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈæktɪv / (BrE)
(of a volcano or similar natural phenomenon) not erupting and not considered extinct.

Examples

inactive volcano
Even though the mountain looks peaceful, an inactive volcano can someday become active again.
Many islands were formed by now inactive volcanic activity.
The scientists determined that the inactive volcano had not erupted for over a century.

Related Terms

  • antonym – active: (of a volcano) currently erupting or having erupted recently
  • synonym – dormant: (of volcanoes) temporarily inactive but capable of becoming active again
  • antonym – extinct: (of a volcano) having no activity and not expected to erupt again
