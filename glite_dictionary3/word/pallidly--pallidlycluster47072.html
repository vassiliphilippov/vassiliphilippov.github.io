pallidly

adverb
in a lacking-vitality manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpælɪdli / (AmE), / ˈpælɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking in interest, energy, or vitality; without liveliness.

Examples

pallidly answer questions
She responded pallidly to the exciting news, barely showing any enthusiasm.
The actor delivered his lines pallidly, failing to capture the audience's attention.
The room was pallidly lit, making everyone feel lethargic and bored.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dully: in a dull manner; lacking liveliness
  • antonym – vividly: in a way that is full of life or vitality
