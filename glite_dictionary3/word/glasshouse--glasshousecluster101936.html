glasshouse

noun
building of glass for growing plants
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building with glass walls and a roof, used for growing and displaying plants in controlled conditions.
