Apollo

noun
class of Earth-crossing asteroid
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑloʊ / (AmE), / əˈpɒləʊ / (BrE)
an asteroid whose orbit around the Sun crosses the orbit of the Earth

Examples

an Apollo asteroid
An Apollo is classified by its semi-major axis being greater than Earth's and its path crossing Earth's orbit.
Scientists recently discovered a new Apollo that could pass close to Earth in the next decade.
The orbits of Apollo asteroids make them important objects to monitor for potential collisions.
