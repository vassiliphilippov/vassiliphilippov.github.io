batholitic

adjective
relating to a batholith
Pronunciation: / ˌbæθ.əˈlɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbæθ.əˈlɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a batholith, a large mass of intrusive igneous rock.

Examples

batholitic rock formation
A batholitic intrusion shaped much of the local landscape.
Geologists studied the batholitic granite exposed at the surface.
The region's batholitic rocks are evidence of intense geological activity millions of years ago.
