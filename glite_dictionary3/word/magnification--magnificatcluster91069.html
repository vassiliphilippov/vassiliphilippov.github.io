magnification

noun
enlarged photographic print
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡ.nə.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæɡ.nɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A photographic print that has been made larger than the original image or negative.

Examples

magnification of a photo
He ordered a magnification of the old family portrait for the living room.
The photographer displayed a magnification of the landscape in the gallery.
This magnification reveals more details than the original photograph.
