passing

adjective
not thorough; done hastily
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
Hasty and done without close attention; not thorough or detailed.

Examples

passing reference in speech
During his speech, he made a passing reference to the recent changes in policy.
He gave the document only a passing glance before moving on to the next task.
Maria showed nothing more than a passing interest in the topic during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cursory: performed rapidly and without thoroughness
  • synonym – superficial: not thorough or deep; lacking depth
