mince

noun
finely chopped or ground food, esp. meat
Pronunciation: / mɪns / (AmE), / mɪns / (BrE)
Food, especially meat, that has been cut or ground into very small pieces.

Examples

mince in spaghetti
The chef added a generous portion of mince to the sauce.
Vegetarian mince can be used instead of ground meat in many recipes.
We bought some beef mince for tonight's dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym – chunk: a thick, solid piece of something
  • synonym – ground meat: meat that has been ground or minced
