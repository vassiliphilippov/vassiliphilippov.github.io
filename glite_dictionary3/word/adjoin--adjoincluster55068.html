to adjoin

verb
to attach or be next to
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒɔɪn / (AmE), / əˈdʒɔɪn / (BrE)
to attach, add, or join something to another; to be next to or joined with

Examples

to adjoin documents
Please adjoin the relevant documents to your application form.
The backyard adjoins a small woodland area, offering beautiful views.
The new building will adjoin the existing library.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attach: to fasten or join one thing to another
  • antonym – detach: to separate or remove something from something else
