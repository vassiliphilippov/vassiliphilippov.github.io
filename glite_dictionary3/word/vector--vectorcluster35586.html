vector

noun
mathematical entity with magnitude and direction
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛk.tɚ /; / ˈvɛktɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɛk.tə /; / ˈvɛktə / (BrE)
a quantity in mathematics or physics that has both size (magnitude) and direction, often shown as an arrow
