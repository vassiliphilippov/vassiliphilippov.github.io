pinner

noun
user of Pinterest
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnər / (AmE), / ˈpɪnə / (BrE)
An internet user who saves or shares images, ideas, or links on the social platform Pinterest by 'pinning' them to virtual boards.

Examples

active pinner on Pinterest
She became a devoted pinner, saving fashion inspiration to themed boards.
As an experienced pinner, he knew how to create boards that attracted thousands of followers.
Many small businesses hire a pinner to manage their product images and link traffic to shops.
