Parasitaxus

noun
genus of parasitic yews
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.ə.sɪˈtæks.əs / (AmE), / ˌpær.ə.sɪˈtæks.əs / (BrE)
A genus of coniferous plants containing a single known species, Parasitaxus usta, a parasitic yew found in New Caledonia.

Examples
