waterbuck

noun
a large African antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌbʌk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəbʌk / (BrE)
a large African antelope (genus Kobus), typically found near water, with long, curved ridged horns in males and shaggy brownish-gray fur

Examples

a waterbuck near water
Waterbucks often stay close to water to escape predators.
The male waterbuck is easily recognized by its distinctive horns and white ring on its rump.
We spotted a waterbuck grazing near the riverbank during our safari.
