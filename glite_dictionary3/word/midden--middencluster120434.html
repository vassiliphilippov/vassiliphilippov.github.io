midden

noun
refuse or dung heap; archaeological dump
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdən /; / ˈmɪd.ən / (AmE), / ˈmɪd(ə)n /; / ˈmɪd.ɪn / (BrE)
a heap or deposit of waste material, such as dung, shells, bones, or other refuse, especially found at an archaeological site
