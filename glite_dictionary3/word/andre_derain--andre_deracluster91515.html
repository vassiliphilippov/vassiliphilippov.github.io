Andre Derain

proper noun
French painter, Fauvism movement
Pronunciation: / ɑnˈdreɪ dəˈræn / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈdʁe dəˈʁɛ̃ / (BrE)
(1880–1954) A French painter known as a leading exponent of Fauvism, recognized for his bold use of color and innovative style.
