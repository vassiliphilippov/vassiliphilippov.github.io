vena hemiazygos accessoria

noun
an accessory hemiazygos vein
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪ.nə ˌhiː.miˌæ.zɪˈɡɒs əkˈsɛs.ə.ri.ə / (AmE), / ˌveɪ.nə ˌhiː.miˈæ.zɪ.ɡɒs əkˈsɛs.ər.i.ə / (BrE)
a vein formed by the union of the 4th to 7th posterior intercostal veins, which empties into the azygos vein

Examples

the vena hemiazygos accessoria drains blood
A radiologist noted enlargement of the vena hemiazygos accessoria on the patient's chest scan.
Surgical procedures in the thoracic region require careful identification of the vena hemiazygos accessoria.
The vena hemiazygos accessoria drains blood from the upper left intercostal spaces into the azygos vein.
