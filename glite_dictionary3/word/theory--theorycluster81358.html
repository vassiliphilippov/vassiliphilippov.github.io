theory

noun
scientific explanation or system of principles
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪri, ˈθiəri / (AmE), / ˈθɪəri, ˈθɪəri / (BrE)
A well-substantiated explanation of some aspect of the natural world, based on an organized system of accepted knowledge and principles.

Examples

scientific theory
A scientific theory must be supported by evidence from repeated experiments and observations.
Einstein's relativity is a famous theory that fundamentally changed our understanding of space and time.
The evolution theory explains the diversity of species on Earth through natural selection.
