egotist

noun
a conceited, self-centered person
Pronunciation: / ˈiɡə.tɪst / (AmE), / ˈiːɡə.tɪst / (BrE)
a person who is excessively conceited or self-centered

Examples

selfish egotist
Despite his talents, Tom was regarded by many as an egotist.
Everyone in the office knows he is an egotist who always boasts about his achievements.
It's hard to work with an egotist because they rarely listen to others' ideas.
