ranitidine

noun
medication reducing stomach acid
Pronunciation: / rəˈnɪtəˌdin / (AmE), / rəˈnɪtɪdiːn / (BrE)
A medication that reduces stomach acid, used to treat peptic ulcers, gastritis, and acid reflux.
