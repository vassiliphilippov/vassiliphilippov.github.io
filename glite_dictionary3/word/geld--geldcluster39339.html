to geld

verb
castrate a male animal, especially a horse
Pronunciation: / ɡɛld / (AmE), / ɡɛld / (BrE)
to remove the testicles of a male animal, especially a horse, to make it unable to reproduce

Examples

to geld a horse
It is common practice to geld male horses that are not intended for breeding.
The farmer decided to geld the colt to make it easier to manage.
The veterinarian will geld the young stallion next week.
