strain

noun
muscle injury from overstretching
Pronunciation: / streɪn / (AmE), / streɪn / (BrE)
an injury to a muscle or tendon caused by overstretching or overuse, resulting in pain and swelling

Examples

muscle strain
After lifting the heavy box, she suffered a strain in her lower back.
Runners are often at risk of developing a strain from overtraining.
The doctor recommended rest and ice to treat the muscle strain.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sprain: injury to a ligament, not muscle
