plunker

noun
baseball hit that drops suddenly
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌŋkər / (AmE), / ˈplʌŋkə / (BrE)
(baseball) a hit in which the ball drops suddenly after being struck.

Examples

a plunker single
His only hit of the game was a plunker that just cleared the infield.
The batter surprised everyone with a clever plunker over second base.
The pitcher was frustrated after giving up a weak plunker into shallow right field.
