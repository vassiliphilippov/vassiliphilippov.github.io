relaxin

noun
hormone aiding labor in pregnancy
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlæksɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈlæksɪn / (BrE)
a hormone produced by the corpus luteum during late pregnancy that relaxes the pelvic ligaments and prepares the uterus for labor
