running

adjective
moving or flowing in a stream (of liquid)
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a liquid) moving or issuing in a continuous stream, especially as supplied by pipes or naturally flowing.

Examples

running water
Some remote villages still lack access to running water.
The cabin finally had running water after the plumbing was repaired.
They were grateful to have running hot water during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flowing: (of a liquid) moving in a steady and continuous way
