lift

noun
transportation by air
Pronunciation: / lɪft / (AmE), / lɪft / (BrE)
the transportation of people or goods by air, especially when other means are unavailable

Examples

emergency lift
During the flood, a lift was arranged to deliver supplies to the isolated area.
Rescue teams provided a lift to the stranded villagers by helicopter.
We received a lift to the hospital when the roads were blocked by snow.
