to attribute

verb
to assign as a cause or source
Pronunciation: / əˈtrɪbjut / (AmE), / əˈtrɪbjuːt / (BrE)
to say that something is caused by or belongs to a particular person or thing; to assign or classify

Examples

to attribute success
Many people attribute their success to hard work and persistence.
Scholars attribute this ancient text to an anonymous monk.
The museum attributed the painting to a famous Renaissance artist.
Some researchers attribute these findings to errors in the initial experiment design.
Taxonomists often attribute newly discovered plants to existing species based on their characteristics.
The committee will attribute patients to different categories according to their symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ascribe: to refer something to a supposed cause, source, or author
  • synonym – assign: to place something in a particular group or category
  • synonym – impute: to attribute (especially something undesirable) to someone
