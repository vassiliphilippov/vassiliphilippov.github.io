lucifer

noun
archaic term for a type of match
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsəfər / (AmE), / ˈljuːsɪfə / (BrE)
an old-fashioned type of match, consisting of a thin stick of wood or cardboard tipped with a combustible chemical, ignited by friction
