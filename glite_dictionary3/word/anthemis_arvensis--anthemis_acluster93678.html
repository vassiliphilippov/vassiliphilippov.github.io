Anthemis arvensis

noun
a white-flowered weed (corn chamomile)
Pronunciation: / ænˈθɛmɪs ɑːrˈvɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ænˈθɛmɪs ɑːˈvɛnsɪs / (BrE)
