bluff

adjective
direct and good-natured in manner
Pronunciation: / blʌf / (AmE), / blʌf / (BrE)
(of a person or manner) direct, outspoken, and good-natured, sometimes seeming abrupt but not unfriendly.

Examples

bluff manner
Despite his bluff exterior, he was sensitive to the feelings of others.
He greeted us with a bluff laugh and a firm handshake.
Her bluff manner made her easy to talk to, even if she sometimes seemed a bit abrupt.
