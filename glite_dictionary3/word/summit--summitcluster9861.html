summit

noun
highest point; top of a mountain or hill
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌmɪt / (BrE)
the highest point or top of something, especially a mountain or hill

Examples

summit of a mountain
Clouds drifted around the summit as the sun began to set.
From the summit, they could see miles of rolling landscape in every direction.
It took the hikers six hours to reach the summit of Mount Snowdon.

Related Terms

  • antonym – base: the lowest part or foundation of something; opposite of summit
  • synonym – peak: the pointed top of a mountain; the highest point of something
