Solomons

proper noun
group of islands in the southwestern Pacific Ocean
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑləmənz / (AmE), / ˈsɒləmənz / (BrE)
a group of islands in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, the northernmost of which belong to Papua New Guinea, while the remainder form the independent state of Solomon Islands within the Commonwealth.
