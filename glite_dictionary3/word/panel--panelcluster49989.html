panel

noun
control surface with electrical switches and meters
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəl / (AmE), / ˈpænəl / (BrE)
an electrical device with a flat surface containing switches, dials, and meters for controlling or monitoring other electrical equipment

Examples

The pilot adjusted several switches on the panel before takeoff.
