arriviste

noun
person newly risen in status, often unaccepted
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrəˈvist /; / æɹɪˈvist / (AmE), / ˌarɪˈviːst /; / aɹiˈviːst / (BrE)
a person who has recently gained wealth or social status and is not fully accepted by others, often seen as a social climber or lacking refinement
