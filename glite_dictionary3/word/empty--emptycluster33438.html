to empty

verb
to excrete or discharge contents from the body
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmpti / (AmE), / ˈɛmpti / (BrE)
(of a person or body part) to excrete or discharge its contents.

Examples

Some medications help the bowel empty more completely.
