planarian

noun
freshwater flatworm known for regeneration
Pronunciation: / pləˈner.i.ən / (AmE), / pləˈneə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a small, mostly freshwater flatworm, often studied for its remarkable ability to regenerate lost body parts
