unawareness

noun
lack of knowledge or attention
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈwɛr.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈweə.nəs / (BrE)
the state of not knowing about something or not noticing something

Examples

unawareness of danger
His unawareness of the deadlines caused him to miss several important meetings.
She acted with complete unawareness of the consequences of her words.
The driver’s unawareness of the slippery road conditions led to the accident.

Related Terms

  • antonym – awareness: knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • synonym – ignorance: lack of knowledge or awareness in general
