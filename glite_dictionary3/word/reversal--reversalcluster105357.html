reversal

noun
an event causing a setback
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrsəl / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːsəl / (BrE)
an unexpected or unfortunate event that hinders progress or causes a setback

Examples

unexpected reversal in progress
After several reversals, the team finally reached the finals.
Political reversal in the region has delayed the peace process.
The company suffered a major reversal when the deal fell through.

Related Terms

  • synonym – defeat: a loss or failure to achieve something hoped for
  • synonym – setback: a reversal in progress; a problem that delays or prevents progress
  • antonym – success: the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
