to canter

verb
to ride or move at a canter
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.tɚ /; / ˈkæn.tər /; / ˈkænər / (AmE), / ˈkæn.tə /; / ˈkæntə / (BrE)
to move at a canter, or to make a horse move at a canter (a pace between a trot and a gallop)
