susceptible

adjective
emotionally easily influenced
Pronunciation: / səˈsɛptəbəl /; / səˈsɛptəbl̩ / (AmE), / səˈsɛptəbl̩ / (BrE)
easily influenced, affected, or harmed by something (such as emotions, people, or situations)

Examples

susceptible to illness
