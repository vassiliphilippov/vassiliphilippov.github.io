aigrette

noun
plumed hat or jewelry ornament
Pronunciation: / eɪˈɡrɛt / (AmE), / eɪˈɡrɛt / (BrE)
A long plume, especially of egret feathers, worn as a decoration on a hat, or an ornament in the shape of a plume, often used in jewelry.

Examples

aigrette feather decoration
Her hat was adorned with a delicate white aigrette that fluttered in the breeze.
The bride wore a stunning aigrette in her hair, sparkling with diamonds.
The jeweler crafted a brooch in the form of an aigrette for the royal collection.
