Elizabethan

adjective
relating to the reign of Elizabeth I of England
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɪzəˈbiθən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪzəˈbiːθən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of the period when Elizabeth I was queen of England (1558–1603), or to the people, culture, or works of that time.

Examples

Elizabethan theatre style
Many buildings from the Elizabethan era still stand in England today.
She specializes in Elizabethan poetry and its historical context.
The Elizabethan theatre produced some of the greatest plays in English literature.
