rhythmic

adjective
having a regular pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪð.mɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɪð.mɪk / (BrE)
having a regular, repeated pattern of sounds or movements

Examples

rhythmic breathing
Her rhythmic breathing helped her relax before the race.
