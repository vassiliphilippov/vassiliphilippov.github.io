riffraffish

adjective
disreputable or low-class behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪfˌræfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɪfˌræfɪʃ / (BrE)
characteristic of or resembling riffraff; behaving in a way considered disreputable or low-class

Examples
