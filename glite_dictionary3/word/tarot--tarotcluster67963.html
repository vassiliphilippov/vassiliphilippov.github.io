tarot

noun
set of cards used for fortune-telling and games
Pronunciation: / təˈroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtærəʊ / (BrE)
a set of playing cards, typically numbering 78, used for fortune-telling and certain card games, featuring symbolic images representing virtues, vices, and other concepts

Examples

tarot card deck
A full tarot deck consists of seventy-eight cards with unique artwork.
Many people visit readers who specialize in interpreting the tarot.
She laid out the tarot cards to predict the future.
