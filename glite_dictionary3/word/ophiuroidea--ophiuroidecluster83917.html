Ophiuroidea

noun
class of echinoderms: brittle stars, basket stars
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.fi.jʊˈrɔɪ.di.ə /; / ˌoʊfiˌjʊəˈrɔɪdiə / (AmE), / ˌɒ.fɪ.jʊˈrɔɪ.dɪ.ə /; / ˌəʊfɪjʊəˈrɔɪdɪə / (BrE)
a class of echinoderms (marine animals) that includes brittle stars and basket stars, with a central disc and long, flexible arms
