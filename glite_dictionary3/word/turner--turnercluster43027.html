turner

noun
a gymnast in a turnverein
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
A gymnast or tumbler who is a member of a turnverein (a German gymnastic society).

Examples

turner in gymnastics club
As a skilled turner, he competed in several national gymnastic events organized by the local club.
Her great-grandfather was a prominent turner who promoted physical fitness in the community.
The city hosted a convention of turners from turnvereins across the country.
