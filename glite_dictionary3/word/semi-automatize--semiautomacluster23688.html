to semi-automatize

verb
make partly automatic
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi əˈtɑməˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˌsɛmi ɔːˈtɒmətaɪz / (BrE)
to make (a process or system) partly automatic, leaving some functions to be performed manually

Examples

to semi-automatize a process
It may be necessary to semi-automatize the manufacturing process, especially where full automation is too costly.
The company hopes to semi-automatize its data entry system to improve efficiency without losing oversight.
They decided to semi-automatize the workflow, automating routine steps but keeping critical decisions manual.
