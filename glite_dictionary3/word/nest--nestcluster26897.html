nest

noun
structure for animals' eggs or young
Pronunciation: / nɛst / (AmE), / nɛst / (BrE)
a structure built by birds or other animals to lay eggs or give birth and rear their young

Examples

bird nest
A mother bird carefully built her nest high up in the tree.
Each spring, rabbits create a new nest for their newborns in the tall grass.
The snake laid its eggs in a hidden nest beneath the rocks.
