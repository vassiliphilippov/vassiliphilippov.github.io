spacey

adjective
dazed or inattentive in manner
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsi / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsi / (BrE)
(of a person) appearing confused, dazed, or inattentive, as if under the influence of a drug or lost in thought.

Examples

spacey expression
His spacey look made me wonder if he was daydreaming or unwell.
I get really spacey if I don't get enough sleep at night.
She felt so spacey after taking the medication that she could barely focus on the conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – alert: quick to notice and react; attentive
  • synonym – spaced-out: dazed or inattentive as if under the influence of drugs
