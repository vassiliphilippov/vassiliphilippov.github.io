unchaste

adjective
not sexually chaste or pure
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃeɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃeɪst / (BrE)
not observing sexual chastity; lacking purity or modesty, especially in sexual conduct.

Examples

unchaste behavior
His critics accused him of leading an unchaste and extravagant lifestyle.
Rumors spread quickly about her supposedly unchaste behavior in the village.
The novel describes the tragic consequences of unchaste love in a strict society.
