subwoofer

noun
loudspeaker for low bass frequencies
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌwʊfɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌwʊfə / (BrE)
A loudspeaker designed to reproduce very low bass frequencies, typically used in audio systems to enhance deep sounds.

Examples

subwoofer for deep bass
He bought a new subwoofer to enhance his car's audio system.
The deep rumble from the subwoofer made the movie sound incredibly realistic.
Without a subwoofer, the music lacks powerful bass.
