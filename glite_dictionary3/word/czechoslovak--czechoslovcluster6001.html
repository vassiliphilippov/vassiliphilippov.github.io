Czechoslovak

noun
person from former Czechoslovakia
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɛkəsləˈvɑk / (AmE), / ˌtʃɛkəsləˈvak / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the former republic of Czechoslovakia, which existed in Central Europe from 1918 to 1992
