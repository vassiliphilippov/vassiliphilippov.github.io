trammel

noun
restraint or impediment to freedom
Pronunciation: / ˈtræməl / (AmE), / ˈtræməl / (BrE)
A restriction or impediment to freedom of action; something that confines, restrains, or limits movement or progress.

Examples

trammel of freedom
Fearing political trammels, she chose to work independently.
He broke free from the trammels of tradition and started his own business.
The new regulations acted as a trammel on innovation in the industry.

Related Terms

  • antonym – freedom: the power or right to act, speak, or think freely
  • synonym – hindrance: something that provides resistance, delay, or obstruction
  • synonym – restraint: something that limits or controls someone or something
