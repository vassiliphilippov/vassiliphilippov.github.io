plaza

noun
shopping center or mall
Pronunciation: / ˈplɑːzə / (AmE), / ˈplɑːzə / (BrE)
A shopping center consisting of a group of stores and restaurants, often landscaped and with parking, serving as a modern version of a marketplace.

Examples

plaza shopping center
On weekends, families like to stroll around the plaza and enjoy the outdoor cafes.
The plaza offers plenty of parking and a wide range of boutiques and restaurants.
The city built a new plaza with dozens of shops and food outlets near the highway.
