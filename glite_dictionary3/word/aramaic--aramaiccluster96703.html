Aramaic

noun
ancient Semitic language of southwestern Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌærəˈmeɪɪk / (AmE), / ˌærəˈmeɪɪk / (BrE)
a Semitic language originally spoken by the ancient Arameans, later used by various peoples in southwestern Asia and still spoken by some communities today
