fullness

noun
the state of being full
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being filled to capacity; the condition of being full

Examples

fullness after eating
After eating a large meal, she enjoyed a sense of fullness and satisfaction.
Spring brings the fullness of life back to the countryside.
The fullness of the container made it difficult to carry without spilling.

Related Terms

  • antonym – emptiness: the state of being empty
  • synonym – satiation: the feeling of being completely satisfied (especially with food)
