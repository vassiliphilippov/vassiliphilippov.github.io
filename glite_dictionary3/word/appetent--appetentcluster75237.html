appetent

adjective
having strong desire; eagerly desirous
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.ɪ.tənt / (AmE), / ˈap.ɪ.tənt / (BrE)
having a strong desire or longing for something; eagerly desirous.

Examples

appetent for success
Despite his calm appearance, the appetent student was determined to win the competition.
She cast an appetent glance at the pastries in the bakery window.
The company entered the market in an appetent pursuit of new opportunities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – avid: having or showing a keen interest or enthusiasm for something
  • synonym – eager: keen or enthusiastic, especially about something that is going to happen or about something you want to do
  • antonym – indifferent: having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned
