Pyridium

noun
analgesic drug for urinary tract infections
Pronunciation: / pɪˈrɪdiəm / (AmE), / pɪˈrɪdiəm / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for an analgesic drug (phenazopyridine) used to relieve pain and discomfort in urinary tract infections.
