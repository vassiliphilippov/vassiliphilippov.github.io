acerbity

noun
harshness or bitterness of manner
Pronunciation: / əˈsɝːbɪti /; / əˈsɝːbəti / (AmE), / əˈsɜːbɪti / (BrE)
harshness or bitterness in manner, tone, or speech

Examples
