indexation

noun
creating or maintaining an information index
Pronunciation: / cc6anccd5bkc8se6a8359n / (AmE), / cc6an.d5bkc8se6a.8359n / (BrE)
the process of creating, organizing, and maintaining an index of documents or data so that items can be found quickly during search or retrieval.

Examples

indexation of documents
The library implemented indexation of its archives to enable faster search.
Good metadata is essential for effective indexation of online research databases.
The team's first task was improving indexation so users could find documents by topic quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cataloguing: arranging and describing collection items so users can find them in a catalogue or database.
  • synonym – indexing: the act of creating an index for documents or data to support retrieval.
