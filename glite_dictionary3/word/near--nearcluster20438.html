near

adverb
almost; not quite
Pronunciation: / nɪr / (AmE), / nɪə / (BrE)
Almost; not quite or only just accomplished.

Examples

near collapse
By the end of the climb, I was near collapse from exhaustion.
She was so tired she was near falling asleep during the meeting.
The project is near completion, with only a few tasks remaining.
