Ltd.

noun
abbreviation for 'limited', a type of company
Pronunciation: / ˌɛlˌtiːˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌɛlˌtiːˈdiː / (BrE)
written abbreviation for 'limited', used after the name of a company that is organized to give its owners limited liability

Examples

company Ltd.
She is employed by Smith & Co. Ltd., a respected firm in the UK.
The documents need to state that the company is Ltd. to ensure legal protection for its owners.
When you see Ltd. after a company’s name, it means the shareholders’ liability is limited.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Inc.: abbreviation of 'incorporated', a US equivalent for companies with limited liability
  • synonym – limited company: a company whose owners have limited liability
