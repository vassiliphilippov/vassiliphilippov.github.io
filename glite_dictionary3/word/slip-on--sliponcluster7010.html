slip-on

noun
clothing/shoe easily put on or taken off
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪp ɑn / (AmE), / ˈslɪp ɒn / (BrE)
A piece of clothing or shoe designed to be easily put on and taken off without fastenings.

Examples

slip-on shoes
He prefers to wear slip-ons instead of laced shoes at home.
Her slip-ons are comfortable and perfect for travel.
The shop has a variety of slip-on sweaters for the winter season.
