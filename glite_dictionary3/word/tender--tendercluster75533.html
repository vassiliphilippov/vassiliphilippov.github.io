to tender

verb
make a formal offer or payment
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ /; / ˈtɛn.dɚ /; / ˈtɛndər / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə /; / ˈtɛn.də / (BrE)
to formally offer or present something, such as money, a proposal, or resignation, for acceptance
