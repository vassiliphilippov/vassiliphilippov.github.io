determinant

noun
decisive causal factor
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɝːmɪnənt / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnənt / (BrE)
a factor or element that decisively affects the nature or outcome of something

Examples

key determinant factor
Access to healthcare is a key determinant of overall well-being in many communities.
Social background remains a significant determinant of career opportunities in many countries.
The weather can act as an important determinant in agricultural success.

  • synonym – factor: something that contributes to a result
