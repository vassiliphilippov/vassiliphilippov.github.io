deceptiveness

noun
quality of being deceptive
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛptɪvnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛptɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of tending to deceive or give a false impression

Examples

deceptiveness of appearances
He warned us about the deceptiveness of appearances when judging people.
The deceptiveness of the advertisement led many people to buy a faulty product.
The magician's skill lies in the deceptiveness of his tricks.
