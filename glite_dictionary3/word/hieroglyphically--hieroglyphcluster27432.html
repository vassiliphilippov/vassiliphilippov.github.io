hieroglyphically

adverb
in the manner of hieroglyphs
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪrəˈɡlɪfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or resembling hieroglyphs; by means of or using hieroglyphs.

Examples

hieroglyphically written text
