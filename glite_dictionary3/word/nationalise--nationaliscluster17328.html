to nationalise

verb
bring under state ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəlaɪz / (BrE)
to bring a private industry or company under government ownership or control

Examples

to nationalise a company
Rising unemployment led the new administration to nationalise key energy companies.
Several industries were nationalised in the 20th century to protect public interests.
The government decided to nationalise the railway system after years of private mismanagement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expropriate: to take property from its owner for public use
  • antonym – privatise: to transfer from public to private ownership
