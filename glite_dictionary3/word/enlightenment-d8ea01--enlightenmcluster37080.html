enlightenment

noun
education leading to understanding
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈlaɪtənmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈlaɪtənmənt / (BrE)
The act of gaining knowledge and understanding, especially resulting from education or exposure to new ideas.

Examples

intellectual enlightenment
He viewed his years at university as a period of intellectual enlightenment.
Her trip abroad brought her a sense of enlightenment about different cultures and ways of thinking.
The spread of scientific knowledge led to an era of enlightenment in many parts of the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym – education: the process of receiving or giving systematic instruction
  • antonym – ignorance: lack of knowledge or awareness
