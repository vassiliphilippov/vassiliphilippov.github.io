Pentecostal

adjective
of or relating to Pentecostal churches or members
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛntəˈkɑstəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛntɪˈkɒstəl / (BrE)
relating to any of the Christian churches or movements that emphasize the work of the Holy Spirit and the direct experience of the presence of God, often through speaking in tongues

Examples

Pentecostal church service
Many Pentecostal believers emphasize the role of spiritual gifts in daily life.
She joined a Pentecostal church known for its lively worship services.
The Pentecostal movement spread rapidly throughout the twentieth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym – charismatic: of or relating to charismatic Christians or movements emphasizing gifts of the Spirit
