eruption

noun
sudden violent occurrence
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
a sudden, violent occurrence of something, usually undesirable or harmful

Examples

eruption of anger
His temper led to an eruption of anger during the meeting.
The sudden eruption of violence caught everyone off guard.
There was an eruption of protests in the city after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – explosion: a violent bursting with noise, especially a burst of energy or violence
  • synonym – outbreak: a sudden start of something unwelcome, such as war or disease
