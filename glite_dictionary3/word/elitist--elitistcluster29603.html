elitist

noun
person favoring rule by an elite
Pronunciation: / əˈliːtɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈliːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that a society or system should be led by an elite group of people

Examples

an elitist leader
As an elitist, he firmly believes that only the most capable individuals should govern.
Many critics described the politician as an elitist who was out of touch with ordinary citizens.
She was labeled an elitist for supporting policies that benefit only a select group.

Related Terms

  • antonym – populist: person who supports the rights of ordinary people against the elite
