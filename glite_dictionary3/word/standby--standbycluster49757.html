standby

adjective
kept ready for emergency use
Pronunciation: / ˈstændˌbaɪ / (AmE), / ˈstænd.bʌɪ / (BrE)
kept ready to be used if needed, especially in an emergency or when the usual option fails

Examples

standby crew ready
A standby crew was on site during the entire event.
The hospital has standby equipment for emergency situations.
We have a standby generator in case of power failure.
