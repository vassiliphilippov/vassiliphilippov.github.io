speakerphone

noun
telephone with microphone and loudspeaker
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːkərˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈspiːkəfəʊn / (BrE)
A telephone feature with a microphone and loudspeaker that allows conversations to be conducted without holding the handset, enabling several people to participate at once.

Examples

use the speakerphone feature
His voice echoed through the speakerphone, making it easy for the whole team to participate.
Please put the call on speakerphone so everyone can listen.
She answered the conference call using the speakerphone feature in her office.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hands-free: operated without the use of hands, often used for phones or devices
