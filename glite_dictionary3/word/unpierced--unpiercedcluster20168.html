unpierced

adjective
not pierced; without holes
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪrst / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪəst / (BrE)
not having been pierced; not penetrated by a hole or opening.

Examples

unpierced ears
Despite the trend, he kept his nose unpierced throughout school.
She preferred to wear clip-on earrings because her ears were unpierced.
The invitation specified that only unpierced envelopes would be accepted.
