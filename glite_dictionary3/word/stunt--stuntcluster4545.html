to stunt

verb
show off; flaunt (slang)
Pronunciation: / stʌnt / (AmE), / stʌnt / (BrE)
(slang) to show off or ostentatiously display wealth, status, abilities, or possessions, especially publicly or on social media.

Examples

to stunt with money
Celebrities love to stunt on social media with luxury cars and designer clothes.
He was stunting in front of his classmates after winning the scholarship award.
She stunted on her ex by posting photos from the expensive vacation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – downplay: to make something seem less important or to avoid drawing attention to it
  • synonym – flaunt: to display something (often money or success) so that people notice it
  • synonym – show off: to behave in a way intended to attract admiration or attention
