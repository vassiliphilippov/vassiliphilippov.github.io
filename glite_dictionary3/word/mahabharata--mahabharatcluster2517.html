Mahabharata

proper noun
ancient Indian epic poem
Pronunciation: / məˌhɑːbəˈrɑːtə / (AmE), / məˌhɑːbəˈrɑːtə / (BrE)
A sacred Sanskrit epic poem of ancient India, recounting the struggle between two groups of royal cousins and forming an important part of Hindu tradition.
