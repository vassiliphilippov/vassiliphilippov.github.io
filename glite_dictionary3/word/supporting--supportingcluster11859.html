supporting

adjective
capable of bearing a load; serving to support
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a structure or part) capable of bearing or holding up a load; serving to support.

Examples

supporting wall
Before demolishing a wall, make sure it is not a supporting structure.
The engineer confirmed that the supporting beams could handle the weight of the new equipment.
The renovation plans required reinforcing the supporting columns in the basement.
