chiliast

noun
believer in the coming millennium
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪliˌæst / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪæst / (BrE)
a person who believes in the coming of a future thousand-year age of peace and prosperity (the millennium), especially in a religious or doctrinal context
