banderillero

noun
bullfighter who plants darts in a bullfight
Pronunciation: / ˌbændəriˈjɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˌbændərɪˈleərəʊ / (BrE)
a bullfighter whose role is to stick decorated darts (banderillas) into the neck or shoulders of the bull during a bullfight
