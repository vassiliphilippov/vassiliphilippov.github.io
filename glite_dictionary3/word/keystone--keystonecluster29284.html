keystone

noun
central stone at top of an arch
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkiːstəʊn / (BrE)
the central, wedge-shaped stone at the apex of an arch or vault, locking the whole together

Examples

keystone in an arch
Architects admired the intricately carved keystone above the entrance.
The mason carefully placed the keystone to complete the archway.
Without the keystone, the entire structure would collapse.
