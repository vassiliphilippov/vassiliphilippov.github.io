outwardness

noun
quality of being outside or external
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtwɚdnəs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtwədnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being outside, or directed toward or relating to the exterior or external aspects

Examples

outwardness of appearance
Many philosophers debate the outwardness versus inwardness of human perception.
The outwardness of the design draws attention from all angles.
The garden's outwardness contrasts sharply with the intimacy of the indoor spaces.
