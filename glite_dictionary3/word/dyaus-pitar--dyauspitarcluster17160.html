Dyaus-pitar

proper noun
Hindu god of the sky
Pronunciation: / ˈdjaʊs pɪˌtɑr / (AmE), / ˈdjɑːʊs ˌpɪtɑː / (BrE)
In Hindu mythology, the god of the sky, regarded as a father figure and predecessor to later deities.

Examples

Dyaus-pitar in Vedic hymns
In the ancient Vedas, Dyaus-pitar is invoked as the primordial sky god.
