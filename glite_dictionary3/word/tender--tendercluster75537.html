tender

noun
official medium of payment
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛndə / (BrE)
an official medium of payment recognized by law for settling debts and transactions

Examples

legal tender
Checks are not legal tender, but they are widely accepted.
Foreign coins are not considered lawful tender in this country.
The shop only accepts cash as tender for its products.
