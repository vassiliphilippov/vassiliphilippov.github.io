Ixodes spinipalpis

noun
tick species transmitting Lyme disease
Pronunciation: / aɪˈzoʊdiːz spɪˌnɪpælˈpɪs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsəʊdiːz spɪˌnɪpalˈpɪs / (BrE)
