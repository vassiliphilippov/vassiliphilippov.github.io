particular

noun
specific fact or detail
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
a single fact or detail, especially as distinct from a general statement

Examples

every particular detail

We need to focus on the particular of how this policy will be implemented.
