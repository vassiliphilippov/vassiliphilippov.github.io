locative

noun
linguistic role indicating location
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkətɪv /; / ˈlɑːk.ə.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈlɒkətɪv /; / ˈlɒk.ə.tɪv / (BrE)
the grammatical case or role in language that shows where something happens or the location of an action or state
