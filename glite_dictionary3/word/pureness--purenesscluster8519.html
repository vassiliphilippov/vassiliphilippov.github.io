pureness

noun
state of being morally pure
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrnəs / (AmE), / ˈpjʊənəs / (BrE)
the quality of being morally pure, virtuous, or free from wrongdoing (can also refer to chastity, especially of women)

Examples

moral pureness
Many admired her pureness and unwavering commitment to honesty.
The pureness of his heart was evident in all his actions.
They viewed childhood as a time of innocence and pureness.
Certain traditional societies judged women harshly if their pureness was ever questioned.
Some ancient cultures placed a high value on a woman's pureness before marriage.
The heroine was admired throughout the novel for her unwavering pureness.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chastity: the state of being sexually pure or abstinent
  • antonym – impurity: lack of moral or sexual purity
  • synonym – purity: the quality of being morally or physically clean, pure
