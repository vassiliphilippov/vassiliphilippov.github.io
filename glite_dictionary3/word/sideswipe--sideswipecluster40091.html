sideswipe

noun
glancing blow to the side, especially of a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌswaɪp / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdswaɪp / (BrE)
a glancing blow or collision to the side of an object, especially the side of a vehicle

Examples

sideswipe accident
A sideswipe between two buses caused a traffic delay downtown.
The car suffered a sideswipe when the truck drifted into its lane.
There was only minor damage from the sideswipe in the parking lot.
