inwardness

noun
concern with inner self or values
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnwɚdnəs /; / ˈɪnwərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈɪnwədnɪs /; / ˈɪnwədnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being introspective, self-reflective, or deeply concerned with one's own thoughts and values
