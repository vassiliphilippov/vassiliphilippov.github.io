photomontage

noun
an image made by combining photographs
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊmɑnˈtɑʒ / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊmɒnˈtɑːʒ / (BrE)
a picture or image created by combining several photographs, often to produce a surreal or artistic effect

Examples

photomontage of famous faces
She spent hours creating a photomontage for her digital art project.
The exhibition featured a striking photomontage blending cityscapes with images of nature.
This magazine cover is actually a clever photomontage of several celebrity photos.
