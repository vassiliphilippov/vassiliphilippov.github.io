bonito

noun
flesh of a Pacific fish related to tuna
Pronunciation: / boʊˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / bəˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
the flesh of various Pacific food fishes of the genus Sarda, related to but smaller than tuna, used as food

grilled bonito fillet
