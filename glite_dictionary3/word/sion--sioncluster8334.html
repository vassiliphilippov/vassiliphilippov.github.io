Sion

proper noun
League of Legends undead champion
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.ən / (BrE)
(in video games) a named playable champion in the multiplayer game League of Legends, depicted as an undead, heavily armoured warrior known for high durability and crowd-control abilities.
