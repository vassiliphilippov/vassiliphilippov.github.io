attitude

noun
position/orientation of a vehicle in flight
Pronunciation: / ˈæt̬ɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
the orientation or position of an aircraft or spacecraft in relation to a specific reference point, such as the horizon or the direction of motion

Examples

aircraft attitude
Maintaining the correct attitude is critical when docking a spacecraft.
Sensors monitor the satellite's attitude to ensure it remains properly oriented.
The pilot adjusted the attitude of the jet to prepare for landing.
