staff

noun
plaster and hair building material
Pronunciation: / stæf / (AmE), / stɑːf / (BrE)
A building material made from plaster and hair, used for covering external surfaces of temporary structures or for decorative purposes.

Examples

staff material
Architects chose staff because it was lightweight and easy to mold into intricate designs.
During the exposition, ornate statues made of staff adorned the entrance to the main hall.
The temporary pavilion was constructed using staff for both the walls and columns.
