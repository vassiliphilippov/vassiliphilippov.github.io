Christian

adjective
relating to qualities or teachings of Jesus Christ
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
Showing qualities, values, or behavior associated with the teachings or spirit of Jesus Christ.

Examples

Christian values guide
Forgiveness is often described as a truly Christian virtue.
Her Christian charity toward strangers was admired by everyone.
They tried to act in a Christian manner even when it was difficult.
