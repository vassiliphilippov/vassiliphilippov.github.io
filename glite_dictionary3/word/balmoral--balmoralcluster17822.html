Balmoral

noun
traditional Scottish brimless cap
Pronunciation: / bælˈmɔr.əl / (AmE), / bælˈmɒr.əl / (BrE)
A traditional Scottish brimless cap, usually dark blue, with a flat top and often a plume on one side.

Examples

wear a Balmoral cap
He placed his Balmoral on the table before joining the dance.
The bagpiper wore a Balmoral with a traditional red plume.
Tourists often buy a Balmoral as a souvenir of their visit to Scotland.
