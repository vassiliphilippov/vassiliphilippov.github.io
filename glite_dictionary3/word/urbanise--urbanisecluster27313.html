to urbanise

verb
make more city-like or industrial
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.bə.naɪz / (AmE), / ˈɜː.bə.naɪz / (BrE)
make or become more like a city, usually by increasing buildings, industry, or population density

Examples

to urbanise a rural area
Once the area was urbanised, property values increased significantly.
Rapid migration can urbanise a region in just a few decades.
The government plans to urbanise several villages near the capital.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ruralise: make more rural or country-like
  • synonym – urbanize: alternative spelling, especially American English
