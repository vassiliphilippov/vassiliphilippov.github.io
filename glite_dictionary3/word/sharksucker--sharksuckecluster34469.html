sharksucker

noun
a fish that attaches to sharks
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrkˌsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːkˌsʌkə / (BrE)
a remora, a marine fish that attaches itself to sharks or other large fish using a sucker on top of its head
