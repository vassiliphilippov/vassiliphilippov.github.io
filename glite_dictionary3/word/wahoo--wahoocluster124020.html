wahoo

noun
large predatory marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːhuː / (AmE), / ˈwɑːhuː / (BrE)
a large, fast-moving predatory fish (Acanthocybium solandri) found in tropical and subtropical seas worldwide, valued as a food and game fish.

Examples

catch a wahoo fish
Anglers consider the wahoo one of the most thrilling fish to catch due to its speed.
