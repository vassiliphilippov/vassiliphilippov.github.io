balloon

noun
large bag filled with gas or heated air
Pronunciation: / bəˈlun / (AmE), / bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a bag filled with gas or air that floats, used for flying, decoration, or as a toy

Examples

colorful balloon decorations
She let go of the helium balloon, and it floated up into the sky.
The festival featured a giant balloon shaped like a dinosaur that attracted many visitors.
The hot air balloon drifted slowly over the countryside at sunrise.
He accidentally popped the balloon with a pin.
She tied a red balloon to each chair for the birthday party.
The children laughed as the balloon floated away into the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym – airship: powered aircraft that is kept buoyant by gas, typically rigid
  • synonym – blimp: nonrigid airship often used for advertising or observation
  • synonym – parachute: a device used to slow a person or object when falling, which resembles a balloon when inflated
