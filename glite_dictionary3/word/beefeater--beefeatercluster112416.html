beefeater

noun
ceremonial guard at the Tower of London
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːˌfiːtər / (AmE), / ˈbiːfiːtə / (BrE)
an officer in the ceremonial bodyguard of the British monarch, especially one of the Yeomen Warders of the Tower of London

Examples

a beefeater at the Tower
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a beefeater and wearing the famous uniform.
The beefeater led the tour group through the ancient halls of the Tower of London.
The beefeaters are known for their distinctive Tudor-style hats and traditional dress.
