policeman

noun
male member of police force
Pronunciation: / pəˈlis.mæn / (AmE), / pəˈliːs.mən / (BrE)
A male member of a police force whose job is to maintain law and order.

Examples

A policeman helped the lost child find his parents at the fair.
After the robbery, the policeman interviewed several witnesses.
The policeman directed traffic during the busy rush hour.
