to punt

verb
move a boat with a pole
Pronunciation: / pʌnt / (AmE), / pʌnt / (BrE)
to propel a flat-bottomed boat, especially on a river, by pushing against the riverbed with a long pole

Examples

to punt a boat
Every summer, tourists punt along the river in Cambridge.
She learned how to punt a boat during her trip to England.
The students took turns punting across the shallow water.
