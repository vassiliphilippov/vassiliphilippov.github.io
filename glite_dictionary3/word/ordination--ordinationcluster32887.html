ordination

noun
religious appointment ceremony
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrdnˈeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːr.dəˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːdɪˈneɪʃən /; / ˌɔː.dɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or ceremony of officially appointing someone to a religious office, such as a priest or minister
