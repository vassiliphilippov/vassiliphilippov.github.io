kitbag

noun
soldier's bag for personal gear
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈkɪt.bæɡ / (BrE)
a bag, typically a sturdy canvas bag, used by soldiers for carrying their clothing and equipment
