Westernization

noun
adoption of Western culture
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛstərnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌwɛstənʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adopting or being influenced by the cultural, social, or economic norms of Western countries

Examples

Westernization of culture
Some critics argue that Westernization has led to the loss of indigenous cultures.
The rapid Westernization of the city changed its traditional way of life.
Tourism played a significant role in the Westernization of the region.

Related Terms

  • antonym – Easternization: the adoption of Eastern cultural or social practices
  • synonym – modernization: the process of adapting something to modern needs or habits
