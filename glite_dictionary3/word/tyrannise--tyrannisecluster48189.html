to tyrannise

verb
to rule in a cruel and oppressive way
Pronunciation: / tɪˈrænəˌzaɪz /; / ˈtɪrəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / tɪˈranʌɪz /; / ˈtɪərənaɪz / (BrE)
to rule or use power over someone in a cruel and oppressive way, like a tyrant
