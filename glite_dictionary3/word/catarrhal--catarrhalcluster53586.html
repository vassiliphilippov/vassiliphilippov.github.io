catarrhal

adjective
relating to catarrh; mucous inflammation
Pronunciation: / kəˈtɑrəl / (AmE), / kəˈtarəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by catarrh, especially involving inflammation of mucous membranes with increased mucus secretion

Examples

catarrhal inflammation
A catarrhal cough often produces an excess of mucus in the throat.
The patient was diagnosed with a catarrhal inflammation of the nasal passages.
