to uppercase

verb
convert text to capital letters
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u028cp\u0259r\u02ccke\u026as / (AmE), / \u02c8\u028cp\u0259ke\u026as / (BrE)
to change letters in text so that they are all capital (large) letters; especially used in computing and typography to mean convert text to capitals.

Examples

to uppercase text
You can uppercase the selected text in the editor to standardize capitalization across the document.
Before exporting, she uppercased all headings so the PDF matched the brand guidelines.
The script automatically uppercases email fields to prevent case-sensitive matching errors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – capitalize: to make a letter or word begin with a capital letter; sometimes used interchangeably with making text uppercase.
  • antonym – lowercase: to change letters so they are small (non-capital) letters; opposite of making text uppercase.
