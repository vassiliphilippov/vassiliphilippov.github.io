keratoscleritis

noun
inflammation of cornea and sclera
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛr ə ˌtoʊ sklɛˈraɪ tɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɛr əʊ sklɛˈraɪ tɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of both the cornea and sclera of the eye

Examples

keratoscleritis with eye pain
Ophthalmologists monitor cases of keratoscleritis closely to prevent potential complications.
The patient was diagnosed with keratoscleritis after experiencing severe pain and blurred vision.
Treatment for keratoscleritis often includes corticosteroids to reduce inflammation.
