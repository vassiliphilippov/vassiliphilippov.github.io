bottom

noun
second half of an inning (baseball)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑt̬əm / (AmE), / ˈbɒtəm / (BrE)
(baseball) the second half of an inning, when the home team is at bat.

Examples

bottom of the ninth
In the bottom of the ninth, the home team made a dramatic comeback.
Let's see if they can hold the lead through the bottom of the eighth.
The Yankees scored three runs in the bottom of the seventh inning.
