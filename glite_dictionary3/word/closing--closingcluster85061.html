closing

noun
the act of shutting or ending something
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzɪŋ /; / ˈkloʊ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzɪŋ /; / ˈkləʊ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of shutting or bringing something to an end, or the final part of something (such as a door, business, event, or speech)
