to besot

verb
to stupefy with infatuation or drink
Pronunciation: / bɪˈsɑt / (AmE), / bɪˈsɒt / (BrE)
to make someone dull, confused, or stupefied, especially due to infatuation or intoxication

Examples

to besot with love
His mind was besotted by wine and he couldn't think straight.
She was so besotted with him that she ignored all his faults.
The young man was besotted by her charm, unable to see the truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – infatuate: to cause to be foolishly in love
