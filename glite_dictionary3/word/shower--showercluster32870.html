shower

noun
short period of rain or precipitation
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A short period of rain, snow, or other precipitation, usually of moderate intensity.

Examples

rain shower
The picnic guests ran for cover when a brief shower swept across the park.
There were a few light showers this morning but the afternoon was clear.
We decided not to go hiking because a sudden shower started pelting the area.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – downpour: a heavy and prolonged fall of rain
  • degree lower – drizzle: light rain with very small droplets
