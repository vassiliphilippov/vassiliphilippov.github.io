thirdhand

adjective
relating to tobacco-smoke residues (thirdhand smoke)
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u03b8\u025crd\u02cch\u00e6nd / (AmE), / \u02c8\u03b8\u025c\u02d0d\u02cch\u00e6nd / (BrE)
relating to or caused by residues from tobacco smoke that remain on surfaces, clothing, or dust and can lead to exposure after the smoke has cleared

Examples

thirdhand smoke exposure
Public health campaigns warn about thirdhand smoke exposure in homes and cars where smokers once lived.
Researchers are studying how thirdhand particles cling to children's toys and clothing after indoor smoking.
She asked the landlord to deep-clean the apartment because of lingering thirdhand odors from previous tenants.

Related Terms

  • synonym – passive smoking: involuntary inhalation of smoke from tobacco products used by others; often used interchangeably with secondhand smoke
  • degree lower – secondhand smoke: smoke inhaled involuntarily from someone else's smoking, or the smoke exhaled by a smoker
