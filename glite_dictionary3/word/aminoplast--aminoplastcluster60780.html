aminoplast

noun
type of synthetic resin used in adhesives and coatings
Pronunciation: / əˈminoʊˌplæst / (AmE), / əˈmiːnəʊˌplæst / (BrE)
a synthetic resin made from amino compounds, used as an adhesive and as a coating for paper and textiles
