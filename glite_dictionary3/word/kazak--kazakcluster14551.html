Kazak

noun
member of a Turkic people of Kazakhstan
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈzɑːk / (AmE), / ˈkæzæk / (BrE)
A member of a Turkic ethnic group native to Kazakhstan and parts of western Asia, traditionally Muslim.
