to mesh

verb
to work well together
Pronunciation: / mɛʃ / (AmE), / mɛʃ / (BrE)
to fit, engage, or work well together; to blend or coordinate effectively (mechanically or abstractly)

to mesh ideas smoothly

Their personalities mesh well, making them a strong team.
