to rise

verb
increase in volume, especially of dough
Pronunciation: / raɪz / (AmE), / raɪz / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially dough) to increase in volume, typically as a result of fermentation or another process causing expansion.

Examples

rise in the oven
Leave the bread in a warm place and watch the dough rise until it doubles in size.
The cake won't rise properly if the oven isn't hot enough.
When yeast is added, the batter will soon begin to rise and become light.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expand: to increase in size, volume, or amount
  • synonym – swell: to increase in size or volume, often gradually
