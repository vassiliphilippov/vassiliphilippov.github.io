to jingle

verb
make a light metallic ringing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
to make a light, ringing sound, typically one produced by small metallic objects striking each other

Examples

to jingle keys
I could hear the coins jingling at the bottom of her purse.
She nervously jingled her keys in her hand while waiting for the door to open.
The bells on the dog's collar jingled as it ran across the yard.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – clang: to make a loud, resonant, metallic sound
  • synonym – clink: to make a short, sharp, ringing sound like small metal objects striking each other
