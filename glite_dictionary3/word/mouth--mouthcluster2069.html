to mouth

verb
touch or handle with the mouth
Pronunciation: / maʊθ / (AmE), / maʊθ / (BrE)
to touch or handle with the mouth, especially gently, often without biting or eating

Examples

to mouth the bit
Horses sometimes mouth the bit while they are adjusting to a new bridle.
The baby reached out to mouth the colorful ring.
The puppy likes to mouth his toys before he chews on them.
