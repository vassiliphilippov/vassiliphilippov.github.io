shift

noun
key for typing uppercase letters
Pronunciation: / ʃɪft / (AmE), / ʃɪft / (BrE)
the key on a typewriter or computer keyboard that allows the user to type uppercase letters and other alternate characters

Examples

the shift key
Hold down the shift key while typing to capitalize a letter.
She accidentally pressed the shift key and everything turned into capital letters.
The shift key allows you to type special symbols above the numbers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – caps lock: the key that locks the keyboard in uppercase mode
