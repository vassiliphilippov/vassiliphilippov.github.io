isolated

adjective
separated from others
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
being or feeling separated from others; having little or no contact with other people or things

Examples

isolated from friends
After moving to a remote village, she often felt isolated from her friends and family.
He grew up in an isolated house at the edge of the forest.
Some animals live in isolated habitats, far from any human settlement.

  • antonym – connected: feeling joined or united with others
