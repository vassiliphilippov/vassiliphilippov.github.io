shoegazing

noun
alternative rock music subgenre
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃuːˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of alternative rock music characterized by heavy use of guitar effects, indistinct vocals, and dense, layered sounds.
