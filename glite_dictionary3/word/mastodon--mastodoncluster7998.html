Mastodon

noun
genus of extinct elephant-like mammals
Pronunciation: / ˈmæs.təˌdɑn / (AmE), / ˈmæs.təˌdɒn / (BrE)
A genus of extinct large, elephant-like mammals of the family Mammutidae, known commonly as mastodons, that lived from the Miocene to the Pleistocene epochs.
