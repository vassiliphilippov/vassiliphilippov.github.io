egotistically

adverb
in a self-centered manner
Pronunciation: / ˌiɡəˈtɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəˈtɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows an excessive sense of self-importance or self-centeredness.

Examples

to act egotistically
Even when proven wrong, he egotistically refused to admit his mistake.
His egotistically motivated decisions alienated many of his colleagues.
She spoke egotistically about her achievements at every opportunity.

Related Terms

  • antonym – altruistically: in a way that shows concern for the welfare of others
  • synonym – selfishly: in a way that shows concern only for one's own advantage
