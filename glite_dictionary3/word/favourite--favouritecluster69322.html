favourite

noun
competitor most likely to win
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
the competitor or team considered most likely to win a competition or event

Examples

the favourite to win
After his impressive record this season, he is the clear favourite to win the championship.
Although the favourite lost in the final round, she was gracious in defeat.
Many people were surprised when the favourite was eliminated in the first heat.

Related Terms

  • antonym – underdog: a competitor thought to have little chance of winning
