Haplopappus

noun
genus of American herbaceous plants or shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˌhæpləˈpæpəs / (AmE), / ˌhæpləˈpæpəs / (BrE)
A genus of North and South American perennial herbs or shrubs with yellow flowers, some species of which are sometimes included in other genera such as Hazardia.
