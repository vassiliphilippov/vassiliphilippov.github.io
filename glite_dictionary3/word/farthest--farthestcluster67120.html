farthest

adverb
greatest degree or extent
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrðɪst /; / ˈfɑrðəst / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðɪst / (BrE)
to or at the greatest distance or degree; most advanced or far in space, time, or progress

Examples

to go farthest in progress
He pushed the boundaries farthest with his bold theories.
Of all the candidates, she has gone farthest in her research on the subject.
The new policy takes environmental concerns farthest among all those proposed.
He ran the farthest of anyone in the race.
This telescope can see farthest into space, revealing distant galaxies.
We sat at the farthest table from the stage.
