proofreader

noun
person who checks and corrects written text
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːfˌriːdər / (AmE), / ˈpruːfˌriːdə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to read written or printed material and correct errors in spelling, grammar, or typesetting before final publication

Examples

skilled proofreader
Before the book went to print, a professional proofreader checked every page for errors.
He hired a proofreader to ensure his dissertation was free of grammatical mistakes.
She worked as a proofreader at the publishing house, catching minor mistakes others missed.

  • synonym – editor: person who prepares written material for publication
