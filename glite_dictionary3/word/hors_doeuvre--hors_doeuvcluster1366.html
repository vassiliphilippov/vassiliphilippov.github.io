hors d'oeuvre

noun
small appetizer before a meal
Pronunciation: / ɔːr ˈdɜːrv / (AmE), / ɔː dɜːv / (BrE)
A small dish served before the main part of a meal to stimulate the appetite.

Examples

hors d'oeuvre platter
She passed a tray of delicious hors d'oeuvres at the party.
The guests enjoyed shrimp as their favorite hors d'oeuvre.
We were served a selection of hors d'oeuvres before dinner.
