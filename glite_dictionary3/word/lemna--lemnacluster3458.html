Lemna

noun
genus of tiny aquatic floating plants (duckweed)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛm.nə /; / ˈlɛmnə / (AmE), / ˈlɛm.nə / (BrE)
a genus of very small, free-floating aquatic plants (duckweed) found on the surface of still or slow-moving freshwater, consisting of a leaflike frond with a single root
