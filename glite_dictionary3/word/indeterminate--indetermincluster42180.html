indeterminate

adjective
having no definite value (math)
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndəˈtɝːmənət / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈtɜːmɪnət / (BrE)
(of a quantity, value, or form in mathematics) having no definite or unique value, especially when an equation or expression does not yield a single solution.

Examples

indeterminate value in math
Dividing zero by zero is considered an indeterminate expression in calculus.
The limits resulted in an indeterminate value, so further analysis was needed.
When x equals zero, the expression becomes an indeterminate form.

Related Terms

  • synonym – undetermined: not decided or settled; not having a definite value or result
