GSA

noun
student LGBTQ+ support group
Pronunciation: / \u02ccd\u0292i\u02d0 \u025bs \u02c8e\u026a / (AmE), / \u02ccd\u0292i\u02d0 \u025bs \u02c8e\u026a / (BrE)
a student organisation (originally 'Gay–Straight Alliance', also 'Gender and Sexuality Alliance') that brings together LGBTQ+ students and straight allies to provide support, promote inclusion, and run awareness activities.

Examples

the GSA club
The campus GSA hosted a panel on LGBTQ+ rights and inclusion for incoming students.
After graduation he stayed in touch with friends he met through the local GSA.
School administrators approved the proposal to start a GSA to support queer students next fall.

Related Terms

  • synonym – LGBTQ+ club: an organised group at a school or university for LGBTQ+ students and allies
  • synonym – gay–straight alliance: a student group that supports LGBTQ+ students and fosters allyship
