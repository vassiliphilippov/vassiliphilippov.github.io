bent

adjective
determined; strongly resolved
Pronunciation: / bɛnt / (AmE), / bɛnt / (BrE)
determined to do something; having a strong and unwavering intention

Examples

bent on success
Despite criticism, he remained bent on finishing the project his way.
She was bent on proving everyone wrong, no matter how hard the challenge seemed.
The company is bent on achieving its sustainability goals by next year.

  • antonym – unwilling: not ready, eager, or prepared to do something
