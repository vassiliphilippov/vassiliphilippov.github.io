broken

adjective
(of animal) tamed or trained to obey
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊ.kən / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊ.kən / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a horse) tamed or trained to obey commands.

Examples

broken horse
Only a broken horse is suitable for a beginner to ride.
The mustang was fierce at first, but is now fully broken.
They refuse to sell any animal that isn't properly broken for riding.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unbroken: (of an animal) not tamed, not trained to obey
