cover

noun
military protective gunfire or bombardment
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvər / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
protective gunfire or bombardment intended to prevent the enemy from attacking or impeding one's own troops or movements

Examples

cover fire
Helicopters gave cover to the convoy as it moved through hostile territory.
The platoon advanced while their comrades provided cover from behind sandbags.
They waited for cover before attempting to cross the open field.

Related Terms

  • synonym – covering fire: gunfire intended to protect troops by preventing enemy action
