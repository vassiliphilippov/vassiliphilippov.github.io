engagement

noun
employment for a limited time (performers)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
an arrangement for performers or performing groups to be employed for a limited period, especially for a particular show or venue

Examples

engagement at the theater
She couldn't attend the party because she had a prior engagement at the theater.
The band has an engagement at the jazz club next Friday night.
Their tour included several one-night engagements across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym – booking: an advance arrangement to employ a performer or group
  • synonym – gig: a job or engagement for a musician or performer
