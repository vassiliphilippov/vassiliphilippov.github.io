leaker

noun
person who reveals confidential information
Pronunciation: / ˈliːkər / (AmE), / ˈliːkə / (BrE)
a person who secretly discloses confidential or sensitive information to the public or to unauthorized individuals

Examples

government leaker
After the government files were published, they began searching for the leaker.
Journalists often protect the identity of a leaker to encourage more disclosures.
The company suspected a leaker was responsible for the confidential plans appearing in the news.

Related Terms

  • synonym – whistleblower: a person who exposes secretive information or activity within an organization
