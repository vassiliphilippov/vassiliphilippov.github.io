tight

adjective
unwilling to spend money; mean (of a person)
Pronunciation: / taɪt / (AmE), / taɪt / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) unwilling to spend money; not generous; mean.

Examples

tight with money

My uncle is so tight he reuses tea bags to save a few pennies.
She's far too tight to buy anyone a drink when she goes out.
