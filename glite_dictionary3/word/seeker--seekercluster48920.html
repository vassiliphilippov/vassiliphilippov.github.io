seeker

noun
a person searching or pursuing something
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkər / (AmE), / ˈsiːkə / (BrE)
a person who is searching for or trying to obtain something, especially knowledge, truth, or a particular goal

Examples

seeker of truth
As a lifelong seeker of truth, she traveled the world to broaden her perspective.
He is a seeker of rare books, always searching old bookstores for hidden gems.
Many seekers come to the city with hopes of finding spiritual guidance.
