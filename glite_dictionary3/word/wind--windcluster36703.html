wind

noun
act of winding or twisting
Pronunciation: / waɪnd / (AmE), / waɪnd / (BrE)
an act of turning or twisting something, especially to wind up a mechanism such as a clock or toy

Examples

wind of a clock
After each wind of the clock, it would tick for hours.
He gave the toy a wind and it started playing music.
The music box needs a wind before it can play the tune.
