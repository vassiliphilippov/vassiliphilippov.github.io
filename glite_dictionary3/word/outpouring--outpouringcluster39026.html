outpouring

noun
a large flow or expression of something
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈpɔrɪŋ /; / ˈaʊtˌpɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / aʊtˈpɔːrɪŋ /; / ˈaʊtˌpɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden and strong expression of emotion, response, or words
