daygirl

noun
girl who is a day boarder at school
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
A girl who attends a boarding school as a day boarder, returning home each evening instead of staying in the dormitory.

Examples

daygirl at school
As a daygirl, she traveled to and from school every morning and evening.
The school provided separate common rooms for boarders and daygirls.
Unlike the full boarders, the daygirl was able to spend evenings with her family.
