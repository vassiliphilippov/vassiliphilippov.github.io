to blow

verb
boast or show off (informal)
Pronunciation: / bloʊ / (AmE), / bləʊ / (BrE)
(informal) To boast or show off; to talk with excessive pride about oneself or one's achievements.

Examples

to blow about success
He likes to blow about his achievements at every meeting.
She tends to blow whenever she gets a chance to talk about her promotion.
Stop blowing your own horn and let your work speak for itself.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boast: to talk with excessive pride about oneself or something one has done
  • antonym – humble: showing or having a modest or low estimate of one's importance
