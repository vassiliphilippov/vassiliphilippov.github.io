appeal

noun
quality of attraction or charm
Pronunciation: / əˈpil / (AmE), / əˈpiːl / (BrE)
a quality that makes someone or something attractive or interesting

Examples

appeal of music
Despite his unconventional style, the artist's appeal continues to grow worldwide.
The company's success is largely due to the global appeal of its products.
The new smartphone's sleek design gives it broad appeal among young professionals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allure: the quality of being attractive and interesting
  • synonym – charm: a quality that attracts or pleases, similar to appeal
  • antonym – repulsion: the quality of being unattractive or off-putting
