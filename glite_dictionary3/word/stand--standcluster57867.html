stand

noun
stop for a performance on a tour
Pronunciation: / stænd / (AmE), / stænd / (BrE)
a stop or engagement by a touring musical or theatrical group where a performance is given, often for a limited number of shows

Examples

stand at a theater
During their European tour, the group scheduled a five-night stand at the famous theater.
The band added another stand in Chicago due to high demand.
Their New York stand sold out within hours of tickets going on sale.

Related Terms

  • synonym – engagement: a scheduled performance or period of employment, especially for entertainers
  • synonym – gig: a single performance by a musician or group
