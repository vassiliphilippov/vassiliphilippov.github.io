motherlike

adjective
having qualities of a mother
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðərˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmʌðəlaɪk / (BrE)
Having qualities suggestive of or acting like a mother; kind, caring, or protective in a manner typical of a mother.

Her motherlike concern for the children made everyone feel at home.
