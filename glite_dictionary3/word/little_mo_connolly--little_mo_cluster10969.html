Little Mo Connolly

proper noun
American tennis champion (1934–1969)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.əl ˈmoʊ kəˈnɑː.li / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl ˈməʊ kəˈnɒl.i / (BrE)
(1934–1969) United States tennis player, the first woman to win the United States, British, French, and Australian championships in the same year (1953).
