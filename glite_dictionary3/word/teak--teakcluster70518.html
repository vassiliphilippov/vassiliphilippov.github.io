teak

noun
tropical tree; source of hard, durable wood
Pronunciation: / tik / (AmE), / tiːk / (BrE)
a tall tropical tree (Tectona grandis) native to South and Southeast Asia, valued for its hard, durable wood used in furniture and construction

Examples

tall teak tree
Most high-quality outdoor furniture is made from teak because of its resistance to weather.
The garden was shaded by a magnificent teak tree, its broad leaves rustling in the wind.
They imported teak from India to construct the ship's deck due to its durability.
