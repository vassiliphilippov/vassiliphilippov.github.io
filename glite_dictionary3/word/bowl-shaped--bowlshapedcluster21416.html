bowl-shaped

adjective
concave; shaped like a bowl
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊl ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbəʊl ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having a concave form with an open top, resembling the shape of a bowl.

Examples

bowl-shaped stadium
Engineers designed a bowl-shaped stadium to improve acoustics and visibility.
She admired the bowl-shaped flowers blooming in the garden.
The valley had a bowl-shaped depression that collected rainwater during storms.
