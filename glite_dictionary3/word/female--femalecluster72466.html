female

noun
an animal of the sex that produces eggs
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈfiːmeɪl / (BrE)
an animal, especially one of a species with two sexes, whose reproductive organs produce eggs or offspring and can be fertilized by a male

Examples

female animal
In many bird species, the female is less colorful than the male.
That lion is a female, as you can tell by the absence of a mane.
The female of the species usually lays eggs after mating.

  • antonym – male: an animal whose reproductive organs produce sperm
