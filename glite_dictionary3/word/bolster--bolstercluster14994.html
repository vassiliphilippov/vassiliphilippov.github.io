bolster

noun
long pillow for bed support or decoration
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊl.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊl.stə / (BrE)
A long, firm pillow, usually placed across a bed underneath the regular pillows for support or decoration.

Many traditional beds feature a cylindrical bolster for added comfort and style.
