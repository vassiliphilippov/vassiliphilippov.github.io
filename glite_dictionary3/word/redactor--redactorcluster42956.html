redactor

noun
automated redaction software
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdæktər / (AmE), / rɪˈdaktə / (BrE)
(in computing) a software program or automated system that removes, obscures, or masks sensitive information from documents, images, or datasets, often by applying rules or pattern recognition.

Examples

redactor removes data
Before publication, the legal team ran the documents through a redactor to hide client names.
The open-source redactor automatically blurred dates and Social Security numbers in the dataset.
After anonymization, auditors confirmed the redactor removed all personal identifiers from the file.

Related Terms

  • synonym – redact: to remove or obscure sensitive information from text or images.
  • synonym – redaction tool: software used to remove or obscure sensitive information from documents.
