contrary

adjective
opposed; completely different
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnˌtrɛri / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.trəri / (BrE)
opposed in nature, character, or purpose; completely different or inconsistent with something else

Examples

contrary opinion
Despite the evidence, she made a decision contrary to the facts.
His beliefs are contrary to accepted scientific understanding.
The new policy is contrary to our company's core values.
