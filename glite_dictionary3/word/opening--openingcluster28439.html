opening

noun
formal ceremony marking a beginning
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
a formal ceremony held to mark the beginning of something, such as a business, event, or building

Examples

the opening ceremony of a store
Hundreds of people were invited to the grand opening of the new community center.
The museum held a special opening attended by local dignitaries.
Tickets to the opening sold out within minutes.

  • synonym – inauguration: the formal introduction or beginning of a system, policy, or period
