awkwardness

noun
difficulty caused by size or shape
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːkwərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːkwədnəs / (BrE)
difficulty in carrying or managing something due to its large size, shape, or weight.

Examples

awkwardness of an object
Despite its beauty, the awkwardness of the sculpture made it hard to move without assistance.
The awkwardness of the large sofa made it difficult to carry upstairs.
They struggled with the awkwardness of the heavy box as they passed through the doorway.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unwieldiness: the fact of being difficult to handle because of size or shape
