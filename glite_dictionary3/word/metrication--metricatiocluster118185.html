metrication

noun
change to metric units
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtrɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛtrɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of changing from imperial units of measurement to the metric system

Examples

metrication of measurements
Despite official metrication, some people continue to use imperial units in their daily lives.
Many countries underwent metrication in the 1970s to standardize measurements in science and trade.
The process of metrication can be expensive, but it typically leads to greater efficiency.
