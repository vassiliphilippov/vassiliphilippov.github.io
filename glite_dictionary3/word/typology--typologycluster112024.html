typology

noun
systematic classification by type
Pronunciation: / taɪˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / taɪˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or systematic classification of things according to their general types or categories

Examples

typology of languages

The typology of ancient pottery helps archaeologists identify different cultures.
