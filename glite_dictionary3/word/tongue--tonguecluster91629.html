tongue

noun
animal tongue used as meat
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ / (AmE), / tʌŋ / (BrE)
The tongue of certain animals, especially cattle and sheep, prepared and eaten as food.

Examples

beef tongue dish
In some countries, tongue is considered a delicacy and is cooked with spices.
The chef served slices of pickled tongue as a starter.
We ordered beef tongue tacos at the market.
