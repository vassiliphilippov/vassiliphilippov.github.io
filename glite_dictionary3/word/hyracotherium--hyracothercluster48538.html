Hyracotherium

noun
extinct genus of early horses
Pronunciation: / haɪˌræsəˈθɪəriəm / (AmE), / haɪˌræsəˈθɪəriəm / (BrE)
an extinct genus of small, early horses that lived during the Eocene epoch, formerly called Eohippus
