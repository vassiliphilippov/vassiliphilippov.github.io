Priodontes giganteus

noun
species of giant armadillo
Pronunciation: / praɪˈɑːdɒntiz ʤaɪˈgæn(t)iəs / (AmE), / praɪˈɒdɒntiz dʒɪˈgæn(t)iːəs / (BrE)
A large species of armadillo native to South America, notable for its great size, reaching up to about three feet long excluding the tail.
