to dramatise

verb
exaggerate or make something seem theatrical
Pronunciation: / ˈdræməˌtaɪz /; / ˈdræm.ə.taɪz / (AmE), / ˈdræmətaɪz /; / ˈdræm.ə.taɪz / (BrE)
to make something seem more exciting, important, or serious than it really is by exaggerating or presenting it in a theatrical way
