atrazine

noun
synthetic herbicide for weed control
Pronunciation: / ˈætrəziːn / (AmE), / ˈætrəziːn / (BrE)
a synthetic herbicide derived from triazine, commonly used to control broadleaf and grassy weeds in crops such as corn and sugarcane
