ramblingly

adverb
in a digressive or wandering manner
Pronunciation: / ˈræm.blɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈræm.blɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a manner that is wandering, digressive, or lacking clear direction or focus

Examples

to speak ramblingly
During the interview, he answered ramblingly, making it hard to follow his train of thought.
Her letter was written ramblingly, jumping from one memory to another without much structure.
The professor spoke ramblingly about several unrelated topics before addressing the main question.

  • synonym – digressively: in a manner that departs from the main subject
