softening

noun
process of becoming softer
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑfənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒfənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming softer or making something less hard or firm

Examples

softening of soil
Recent rain caused a softening of the soil in the garden.
The softening of the clay made it easier to shape on the potter's wheel.
There was a noticeable softening in his voice as he started to apologize.
