Buchenwald

proper noun
Nazi concentration camp in central Germany
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːkənˌwɑld / (AmE), / ˈbuːkənˌvalt / (BrE)
A Nazi concentration camp for Jews and other prisoners during World War II, located in central Germany.
