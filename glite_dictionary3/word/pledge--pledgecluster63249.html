to pledge

verb
promise to donate money
Pronunciation: / plɛdʒ / (AmE), / plɛdʒ / (BrE)
to formally promise to give or pay a specified amount of money, especially regularly, to a cause or organization

Examples

to pledge money
After the disaster, thousands of people pledged monthly support to relief organizations.
She regularly pledges a portion of her earnings to environmental causes.
This year, I pledged fifty dollars to the local cancer charity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – contribute: to give (money, help, etc.) for a common purpose
  • synonym – donate: to give money or goods to help a person or organization
