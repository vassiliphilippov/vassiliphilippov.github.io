incidence

noun
the striking of a beam on a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnsɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈɪnsɪdəns / (BrE)
the striking of a ray or beam, especially of light, on a surface

Examples

incidence of light
At the boundary, the incidence of the sunlight was almost perpendicular to the glass pane.
The incidence of the laser beam on the prism determined how it would be refracted.
Understanding the angle of incidence is crucial in designing solar panels for maximum efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym – angle of incidence: the angle at which a ray or wave strikes a surface
