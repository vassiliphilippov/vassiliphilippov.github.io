to cross-fertilize

verb
cause to undergo cross-fertilization
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔs ˌfɜrtəˌlaɪz /; / krɑs ˈfɜrt̬əˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkrɒs ˌfəːtɪˌlaɪz /; / krɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to mix genetic material from different individuals or sources, especially to produce new varieties in plants or animals
