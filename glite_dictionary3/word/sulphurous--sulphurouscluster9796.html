sulphurous

adjective
containing or related to sulfur
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfərəs / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfjʊərəs / (BrE)
containing or relating to sulfur; derived from or characteristic of sulfur, especially in smell or composition.
