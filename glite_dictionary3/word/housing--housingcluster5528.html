housing

noun
protective casing for a mechanical part
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
a protective outer case or cover designed to contain or support a mechanical part or component

Examples

housing for machinery
A plastic housing was fitted over the wiring to prevent accidents.
He removed the metal housing to access the gears inside the machine.
The engine's housing protects its internal components from dust and moisture.
