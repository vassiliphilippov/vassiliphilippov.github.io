dispenser

noun
container releasing measured amounts
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɛnsɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈspensə / (BrE)
A container or device designed to release its contents in measured or prescribed amounts.

Examples

soap dispenser
A hand sanitizer dispenser is now installed at every doorway.
He refilled the soap dispenser in the bathroom.
The office has a water dispenser near the entrance for employees to use throughout the day.

  • synonym – distribute: to give out or deliver something to people
