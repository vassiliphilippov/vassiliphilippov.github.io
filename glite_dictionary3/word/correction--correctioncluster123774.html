correction

noun
decline in stock prices after increases
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / kəˈrɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a decline in stock market activity or prices following a period of increases, often seen as a natural adjustment

Examples

market correction occurs
Analysts warned that a correction was likely given the recent surge in stock prices.
Many investors view a correction as a healthy sign in an overheated market.
The stock market experienced a correction after months of record highs.
