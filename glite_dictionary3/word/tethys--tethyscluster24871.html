Tethys

proper noun
Titaness and sea goddess in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːθɪs / (AmE), / ˈtiːθɪs / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a Titaness and sea goddess, the wife of Oceanus.
