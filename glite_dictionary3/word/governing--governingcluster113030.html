governing

noun
the act of exercising authority
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌvərnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of controlling or directing according to rule, law, or authority

Examples

governing of a country
Effective governing depends on the fair enforcement of laws.
She studied public policy with a focus on the ethics of governing.
The governing of the country requires both wisdom and experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – administration: the process or activity of running a business, organization, etc.
  • synonym – government: the system or group of people governing an organized community
