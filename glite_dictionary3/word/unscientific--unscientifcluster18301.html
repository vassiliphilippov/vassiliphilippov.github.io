unscientific

adjective
not based on science
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.saɪˈɛn.tɪ.fɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.saɪˈɛn.tɪ.fɪk / (BrE)
not based on or consistent with the methods or principles of science.

Examples

unscientific method
His argument was dismissed as unscientific because it was not supported by any experiments or observations.
Relying on anecdotal evidence is considered unscientific by most researchers.
The study was criticized for its unscientific methods and lack of rigorous data analysis.
