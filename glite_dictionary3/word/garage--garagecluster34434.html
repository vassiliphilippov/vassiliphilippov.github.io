garage

noun
building for parking cars
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrɑʒ / (AmE), / ˈɡærɑːʒ / (BrE)
a building or part of a building used for parking and storing motor vehicles, especially cars.

Examples

garage for cars
He keeps his vintage cars in a large garage behind the house.
She pulled into the garage as it started to rain.
The apartment complex offers each resident a private garage.
