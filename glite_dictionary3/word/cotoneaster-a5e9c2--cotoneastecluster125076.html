cotoneaster

noun
a shrub with colourful foliage and small berries
Pronunciation: / koʊˈtoʊniˌæstər / (AmE), / kəˈtəʊniːˌæstə / (BrE)
a shrub of the genus Cotoneaster, with colourful autumn foliage, small white or pinkish flowers, and small red or black berries

Examples

cotoneaster bush
A row of cotoneaster bushes formed a natural hedge along the driveway.
