funnel

noun
conical tube or shape with wide and narrow ends
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌnəl /; / ˈfʌn.əl / (AmE), / ˈfʌnəl /; / ˈfʌn.əl / (BrE)
a tube or utensil with a wide top and narrow bottom, used for guiding liquid or powder into a small opening
