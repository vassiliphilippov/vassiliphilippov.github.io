nonflavored

adjective
without added flavoring
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈfleɪvɚd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈfleɪvəd / (BrE)
Not containing any added flavoring or flavor substances.

Examples

nonflavored yogurt
She prefers nonflavored yogurt so she can add her own fruit.
The medication is available in nonflavored tablets for those sensitive to artificial tastes.
When baking, many recipes call for nonflavored gelatin to avoid altering the dessert's taste.
