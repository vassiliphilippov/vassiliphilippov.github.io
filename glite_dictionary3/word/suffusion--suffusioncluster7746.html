suffusion

noun
gradual spreading of color or substance
Pronunciation: / səˈfjuːʒən / (AmE), / səˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
the gradual spreading or infusion of a color, liquid, or quality throughout something

Examples

suffusion of color
A gentle suffusion of pink covered the morning sky as the sun began to rise.
The suffusion of sadness in her eyes was unmistakable.
There was a suffusion of light that transformed the entire room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – infusion: the introduction or instilling of a substance into something
