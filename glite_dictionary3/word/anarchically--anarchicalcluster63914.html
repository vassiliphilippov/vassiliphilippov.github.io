anarchically

adverb
in a lawless or chaotic manner
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːrkɪkli / (AmE), / əˈnɑːkɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is lawless, lacking order, or governed by chaos or rebellion.

Examples

to act anarchically in crowds
As the crowd moved anarchically through the streets, it became difficult for police to maintain control.
Some students acted anarchically during the protest, ignoring all directions from supervisors.
The debate descended anarchically into shouting and confusion.
