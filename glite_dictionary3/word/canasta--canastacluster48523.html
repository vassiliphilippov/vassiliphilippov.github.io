canasta

noun
a card game similar to rummy
Pronunciation: / kəˈnæstə / (AmE), / kəˈnɑːstə / (BrE)
A card game of the rummy family, played with two decks and four jokers, in which players aim to form melds of cards of the same rank.

Examples

play canasta cards
At the club, several tables were set up for the weekly canasta tournament.
My grandmother taught me how to play canasta when I was a child.
