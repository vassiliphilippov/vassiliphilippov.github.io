bolt

noun
sliding bar in a lock
Pronunciation: / boʊlt / (AmE), / bəʊlt / (BrE)
the metal bar in a lock that slides into place to secure a door or window, operated by a key or handle.

Examples

a door bolt
He installed a new bolt to make the window more secure.
She turned the key and the bolt slid into place, locking the door securely.
The old house had a heavy bolt on the front gate for extra safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym – latch: a type of fastening used for keeping doors or gates closed
