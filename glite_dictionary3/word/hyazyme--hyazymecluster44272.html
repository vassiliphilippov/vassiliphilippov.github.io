Hyazyme

noun
enzyme trade name (hyaluronidase)
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪəˌzaɪm / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəˌzaɪm / (BrE)
A trade name for an enzyme (hyaluronidase) used to split hyaluronic acid, reducing viscosity and increasing tissue permeability.
