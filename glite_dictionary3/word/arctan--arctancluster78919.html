arctan

noun
inverse trigonometric function of tangent
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrkˌtæn / (AmE), / ˈɑːkˌtæn / (BrE)
the inverse trigonometric function of tangent; the angle whose tangent is a given number

Examples

arctan of a number
In trigonometry, the arctan of 1 is 45 degrees or π/4 radians.
To find the angle, take the arctan of the ratio of the opposite to adjacent sides.
