Pius XII

proper noun
Pope (1939–1958); WWII era
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪ.əs ˌtwɛlv / (AmE), / ˌpaɪ.əs ˌtwɛlv / (BrE)
(1876–1958) Pope of the Roman Catholic Church from 1939 to 1958, who maintained neutrality during World War II and was later criticized for not aiding persecuted Jews.
