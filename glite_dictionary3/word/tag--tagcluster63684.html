to tag

verb
mention or link someone on social media
Pronunciation: / tæɡ / (AmE), / tæɡ / (BrE)
(in social media) to mention or link a person or account in a post, comment, or photo so they are notified and the content is associated with them.

Examples

to tag a friend online
She tags her friend in the photo on Instagram to share the memory.
They tagged the conference speakers in the post so everyone could see their profiles.
I'm tagging all my classmates in the event invitation to make sure they get it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mention: to refer to or name someone in a post or message
  • synonym – notify: to give someone a message or alert about something; often automatic after a tag
