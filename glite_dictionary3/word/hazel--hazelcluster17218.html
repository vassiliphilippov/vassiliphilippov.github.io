hazel

adjective
light brown or yellowish-brown in color
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəl / (BrE)
of a light brown or yellowish-brown color, especially used to describe eyes or wood

Examples

hazel leaves
On his walk, he admired the hazel leaves turning yellow in the autumn breeze.
She has beautiful hazel eyes that seem to change color in the sunlight.
The table was made from hazel wood, giving it a warm, golden-brown appearance.
