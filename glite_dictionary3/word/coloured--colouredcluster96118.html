coloured

adjective
having color or a particular color
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlərd / (AmE), / ˈkʌləd / (BrE)
having color or a particular color; not colorless or transparent, sometimes used in combination (e.g., amber-coloured, brightly coloured).

Examples

coloured pencils
The artist preferred to use coloured pencils to bring her drawings to life.
The leaves turned beautifully coloured in shades of red and gold during the autumn.
We bought a set of coloured lights to decorate the garden for the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym – colorful: having bright or distinctive color
  • antonym – colorless: lacking any color
