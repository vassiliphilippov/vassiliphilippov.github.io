to excite

verb
produce a magnetic field in (device/component)
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsaɪt / (AmE), / ɪkˈsaɪt / (BrE)
to produce a magnetic field in (a device or component), especially by means of an electric current

Examples

to excite a magnet
Special circuits are used to excite the armature in this type of generator.
The operator can excite the electromagnet by switching on the power supply.
When an electric current is applied, it will excite the coil and generate a magnetic field.

  • synonym – energize: to supply with energy, often by electric current
