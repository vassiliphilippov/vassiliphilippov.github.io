granter

noun
person who grants or gives something
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræntər / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːntə / (BrE)
a person or organization that gives or formally transfers something, especially rights, property, or permission

Examples

granter of permission
A scholarship granter typically sets out certain eligibility requirements.
The granter of the license retains some control over how the rights are used.
The land was transferred from the granter to the new owner by deed.
