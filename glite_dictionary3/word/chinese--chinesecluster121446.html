Chinese

noun
a person from China
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈniz / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of China, including both the People's Republic of China and, historically, the Republic of China (Taiwan)
