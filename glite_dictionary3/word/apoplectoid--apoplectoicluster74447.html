apoplectoid

adjective
resembling apoplexy (stroke)
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈplɛktɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌapəˈplɛktɔɪd / (BrE)
resembling the symptoms or appearance of apoplexy (stroke), especially sudden loss of consciousness or paralysis
