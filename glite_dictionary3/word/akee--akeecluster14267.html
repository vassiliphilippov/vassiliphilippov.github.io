akee

noun
tropical fruit with toxic seeds and flesh
Pronunciation: / əˈkiː / (AmE), / əˈkiː / (BrE)
A red, pear-shaped tropical fruit native to West Africa, with poisonous seeds and flesh that is toxic unless properly ripened and cooked.
