moorfowl

noun
grouse of British upland moors
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊrˌfaʊl / (AmE), / ˈmɔːˌfaʊl / (BrE)
a reddish-brown grouse found in upland moors of Great Britain, especially the red grouse

Examples

moorfowl in the heather

Gamekeepers in Scotland work to protect the moorfowl population on the estates.
The moorfowl is well-camouflaged against the reddish grasses of the upland moors.
