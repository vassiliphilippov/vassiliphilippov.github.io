sententiously

adverb
in a terse, moralizing manner
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtɛnʃəsli / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɛnʃəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is concise and full of meaning, often in a moralizing or self-important manner

Examples

to speak sententiously
He spoke sententiously, eager to share his opinions with the audience.
She always responds sententiously when asked about ethical dilemmas.
The teacher nodded sententiously, making a point with every sentence.
