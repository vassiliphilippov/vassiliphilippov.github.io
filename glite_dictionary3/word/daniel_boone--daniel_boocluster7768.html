Daniel Boone

proper noun
famous American pioneer and explorer
Pronunciation: / ˈdænjəl buːn / (AmE), / ˈdanjəl buːn / (BrE)
(1734–1820) An American pioneer, explorer, and frontiersman, famous for his exploration and settlement of Kentucky.

Examples

Daniel Boone explored Kentucky
A statue of Daniel Boone stands in several American cities to commemorate his pioneering achievements.
Many stories have been told about Daniel Boone and his adventures on the American frontier.
Some history books highlight Daniel Boone as a symbol of courage and exploration.
