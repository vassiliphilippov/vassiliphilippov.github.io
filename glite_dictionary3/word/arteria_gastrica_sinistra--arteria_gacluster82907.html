arteria gastrica sinistra

noun
branch of celiac artery supplying stomach and esophagus
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə ɡæˈstrɪkə sɪˈnɪstrə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ɡæˈstrɪkə sɪˈnɪstrə / (BrE)
The left gastric artery; a branch of the celiac artery that supplies blood to the lesser curvature of the stomach and the abdominal portion of the esophagus.

Examples

An aneurysm in the arteria gastrica sinistra can lead to serious gastrointestinal complications.

The arteria gastrica sinistra is crucial for supplying blood to the upper part of the stomach.
