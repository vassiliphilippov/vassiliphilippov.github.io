touchline

noun
sideline in soccer or rugby
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃˌlaɪn / (BrE)
either of the two lines marking the sides of the field in soccer or rugby, outside of which the ball is out of play

Examples

along the touchline
He sprinted down the touchline before delivering a perfect cross.
Spectators lined the touchline to watch the exciting match.
The ball rolled across the touchline and out of play.
