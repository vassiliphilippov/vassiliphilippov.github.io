to splash

verb
mark with patches of contrasting color or texture
Pronunciation: / splæʃ / (AmE), / splæʃ / (BrE)
to mark or cover with patches of contrasting color or texture, often in an irregular or scattered manner

Examples

splash color on walls
In spring, the hillside is splashed with wildflowers of every color.
Late afternoon sunlight splashed the walls with golden patches.
The artist splashed the canvas with bold streaks of red and blue.
