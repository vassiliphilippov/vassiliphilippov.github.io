predaceous

adjective
hunting and killing for food
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdeɪʃəs / (AmE), / prɪˈdeɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of an animal) hunting and killing other animals for food; predatory.

Examples

predaceous beetle
Many beetle species are predaceous, feeding on other insects.
Some birds develop predaceous habits, preying on smaller animals.
The predaceous instincts of wolves make them effective hunters in the wild.
