sheng nu

noun
unmarried woman in China (usually educated, late twenties+)
Pronunciation: / ʃʌŋ ˈnuː / (AmE), / ʃɛŋ ˈnuː / (BrE)
(in China) an educated woman who remains unmarried by her late twenties or older, especially as regarded by society.
