striker

noun
naval trainee for a technical rating
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkə / (BrE)
a person in the navy who is receiving intensive training to qualify for a technical rating; especially in the U.S. Navy.

Examples

navy striker trainee
After enlisting, he became a striker and started training as an electronics technician.
Every new striker is assigned a mentor to guide them through their apprenticeship.
The fleet relies on dedicated strikers to fill vital technical positions.
