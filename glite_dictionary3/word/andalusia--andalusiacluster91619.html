Andalusia

proper noun
region in southern Spain
Pronunciation: / ˌændəˈluːziə / (AmE), / ˌandəˈluːziə / (BrE)
a region in southern Spain, known for its rich history, especially as a center of Moorish culture, and its location on both the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea
