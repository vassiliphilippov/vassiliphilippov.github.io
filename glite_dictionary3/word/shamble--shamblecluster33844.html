shamble

noun
a slow, dragging walk
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæmbəl / (AmE), / ˈʃæmbəl / (BrE)
a slow, awkward way of walking in which you drag your feet along the ground without lifting them fully

Examples

a tired shamble
