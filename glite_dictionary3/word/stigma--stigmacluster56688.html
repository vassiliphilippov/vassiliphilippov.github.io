stigma

noun
external opening in an arthropod's respiratory system
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪɡmə / (AmE), / ˈstɪɡmə / (BrE)
an external opening of the tracheal system in a terrestrial arthropod, allowing for the exchange of gases

Examples

insect stigma
Air enters the insect's body through special stigma on the sides of its thorax.
Damage to the stigma can interfere with the insect's breathing process.
The scientist located the stigma that led into the tracheal respiratory system.
