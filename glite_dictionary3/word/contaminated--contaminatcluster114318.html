contaminated

adjective
made impure or dirty by contact
Pronunciation: / kənˈtæmɪˌneɪtɪd /; / kənˈtæməˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
made impure, unsafe, or harmful by contact with something unclean or dangerous

Examples

contaminated water supply
