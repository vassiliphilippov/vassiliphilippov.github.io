tongueless

adjective
lacking a tongue
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋləs / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋləs / (BrE)
lacking a tongue; having no tongue

Examples

tongueless shoe
He wore tongueless shoes to make them easier to slip on and off.
The tongueless lizard adapted by using other parts of its mouth to catch prey.
The legend spoke of a tongueless ghost haunting the old castle.
