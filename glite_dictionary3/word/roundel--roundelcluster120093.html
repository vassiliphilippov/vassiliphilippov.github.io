roundel

noun
poetic form with repeated refrain
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndəl / (AmE), / ˈraʊndəl / (BrE)
A short poem consisting of three triplets with a refrain after the first and third, based on the French rondeau form.

Examples

write a roundel poem
In literature class, we studied the structure of the roundel and tried writing our own versions.
The roundel, with its recurring refrain, captivated the audience at the poetry reading.
The poet composed a roundel to express his feelings of longing and loss.
