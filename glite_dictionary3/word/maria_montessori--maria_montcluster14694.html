Maria Montessori

proper noun
Italian educator; developer of Montessori method
Pronunciation: / məˈriːə ˌmɒntəˈsɔːri / (AmE), / məˈriːə ˌmɒntɪˈsɔːri / (BrE)
(1870–1952) Italian educator who developed the Montessori method of education, emphasizing a child-centered, hands-on approach to learning.
