revolver

noun
a rotating door with several panels
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑlvər / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒlvə / (BrE)
a door consisting of several panels that rotate around a central axis, allowing people to enter or leave while minimizing airflow between inside and outside
