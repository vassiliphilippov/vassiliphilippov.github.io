organ

noun
reed keyboard instrument with bellows
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.ɡən /; / ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / ˈɔː.ɡən /; / ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
a large musical instrument with keyboards, in which sound is produced by air or electronically (such as a pipe organ or electronic organ)
