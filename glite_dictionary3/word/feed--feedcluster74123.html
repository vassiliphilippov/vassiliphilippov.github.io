feed

noun
stream of online content
Pronunciation: / fid / (AmE), / fiːd / (BrE)
A continuously updated list or stream of digital content (posts, images, updates) presented to a user on a website or in an app.

Examples

social media feed
Her feed showed travel updates from friends and bloggers every day as she scrolled through her phone.
The news app personalized my feed to prioritize local stories and breaking headlines.
Developers used an RSS feed to automatically send new articles to subscribers' inboxes each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stream: a continuous flow of digital content or data
  • synonym – timeline: a chronological list of posts or updates on a social platform
