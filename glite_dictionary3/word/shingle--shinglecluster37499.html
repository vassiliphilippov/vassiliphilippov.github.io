shingle

noun
a professional's signboard
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈʃɪŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
a small signboard displayed outside the office of a professional such as a lawyer or doctor, indicating their name and occupation

Examples

doctor's shingle sign
After passing the bar, she hung out her shingle and started her own practice.
Doctors in the past would often nail a shingle beside their door to attract patients.
When he retired, he took down his shingle after forty years of service.
