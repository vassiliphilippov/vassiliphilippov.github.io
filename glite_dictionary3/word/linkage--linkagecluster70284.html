linkage

noun
mechanical system transmitting power or motion
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkɪdʒ / (BrE)
a system of rods, springs, or pivots connected in such a way as to transmit power or motion in a machine or mechanism.

Examples
