unliveable

adjective
not suitable to live in
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪvəbəl / (BrE)
not suitable or safe to live in; unfit for habitation.

Examples

unliveable house
After the flood, the house became unliveable due to severe water damage.
The apartment was so cold and damp that it was considered unliveable by health inspectors.
Toxic fumes from the factory made the village unliveable for its residents.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inhabitable: suitable to live in
  • synonym – uninhabitable: not fit to live in
