praline

noun
nut-based sweet confection
Pronunciation: / ˈpreɪˌlin / (AmE), / ˈprɑːliːn / (BrE)
A sweet confection made from nuts and sugar syrup, often with butter, typically shaped into small, cookie-sized pieces.

chocolate praline
