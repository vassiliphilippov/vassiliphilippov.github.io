pepper

noun
tropical plant of the genus Capsicum
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpər /; / ˈpɛpɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
a plant of the Capsicum genus, or its hollow, fleshy fruit (such as bell peppers or chili peppers), used as a vegetable or spice
