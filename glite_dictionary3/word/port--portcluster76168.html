port

noun
hardware connection point on a device
Pronunciation: / pɔrt / (AmE), / pɔːt / (BrE)
a point on a computer or other electronic device where another device can be connected, especially for transferring data or power

Examples

USB port
Many modern laptops have a USB-C port for charging and data transfer.
Please plug the printer cable into the correct port on the back of your computer.
The external hard drive was not recognized because the port was damaged.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interface: a point where two systems or subjects meet and interact
  • synonym – socket: an opening or connection point for an electrical device
