naturalisation

noun
granting of citizenship to a foreigner
Pronunciation: / ˌnætʃərələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnatʃərəlʌɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the legal process by which someone who is not a citizen of a country is granted citizenship

Examples

naturalisation as citizen
After years of living in the country, she finally completed her naturalisation process.
