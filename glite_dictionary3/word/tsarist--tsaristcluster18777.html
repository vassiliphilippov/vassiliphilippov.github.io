tsarist

adjective
relating to a tsar or tsarist system
Pronunciation: / ˈzɑrɪst / (AmE), / ˈzɑːrɪst / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or supporting a tsar or the system of government ruled by a tsar, especially in Russia before 1917.
