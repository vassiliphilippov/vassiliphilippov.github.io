Cestida

noun
order of ribbon-like ctenophores
Pronunciation: / sɛsˈtaɪdə / (AmE), / sɛsˈtiːdə / (BrE)
A small order of ctenophores (marine invertebrates), characterized by ribbon-like bodies and short tentacles; includes one family.
