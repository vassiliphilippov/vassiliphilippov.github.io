Tiu

proper noun
Germanic god of war and sky
Pronunciation: / ˈtiu / (AmE), / ˈtiːuː / (BrE)
A god of war and the sky in Germanic mythology; the counterpart of the Norse god Tyr.

Examples

Tiu in mythology
In mythology, Tiu is associated with the sky and justice.
Some ancient Germanic tribes worshipped Tiu as a powerful protector in battle.
The name Tuesday is thought to derive from Tiu, reflecting his importance in early Germanic cultures.
