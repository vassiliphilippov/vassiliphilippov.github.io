sombreness

noun
seriousness or melancholy mood
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːm.bɚ.nəs /; / ˈsɑːmbrənɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɒm.brə.nəs /; / ˈsɒmbrənɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being serious, solemn, or sad; a feeling or manner of deep sadness or gravity
