Kiplingesque

adjective
in the style of Rudyard Kipling
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪplɪŋˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌkɪplɪŋˈɛsk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of the works or style of Rudyard Kipling, especially in subject matter, tone, or themes.

Examples

Kiplingesque adventure story
Critics praised the film's Kiplingesque themes of duty, empire, and boyhood bravery.

The author's latest novel features a Kiplingesque adventure set in colonial India.
