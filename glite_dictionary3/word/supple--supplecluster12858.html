to supple

verb
make pliant or flexible
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌp.əl / (AmE), / ˈsʌp.əl / (BrE)
to make something soft, pliant, or flexible, especially by use or treatment

Examples

to supple old boots
Constant use has supplied these saddles until they are soft and comfortable.
Daily conditioning will supple the leather straps and prevent cracking.
Oils can help to supple the fabric, making it easier to shape.

  • antonym – stiffen: to make or become stiff or rigid
