Metroxylon

noun
genus of Malayan palm trees
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtroʊˈzaɪlɒn / (AmE), / ˌmɛtrɒkˈsaɪlən / (BrE)
a genus of Malayan palm trees with pinnate leaves that flower and fructify once before dying, including the sago palm.
