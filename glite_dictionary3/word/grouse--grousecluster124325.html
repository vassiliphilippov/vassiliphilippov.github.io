grouse

noun
a plump-bodied game bird
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊs / (AmE), / ɡraʊs / (BrE)
a plump-bodied game bird with feathered legs and feet, found in the Northern Hemisphere and hunted for sport and food.

Examples

wild grouse
A grouse flushed from the underbrush as we walked through the forest.
During hunting season, several grouse were spotted near the moor.
The chef prepared a delicious dish of roasted grouse for the guests.
