mil

noun
unit, one thousandth of an inch
Pronunciation: / mɪl / (AmE), / mɪl / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to one thousandth of an inch, used especially to measure thickness of materials such as sheets and wire

Examples

20 mil thickness
A 20 mil coating will offer greater protection compared to a 10 mil coating.
The plastic sheeting is available in 6 mil and 10 mil thicknesses.
Wire insulation thickness is often measured in mil rather than millimeters.

Related Terms

  • synonym – millimeter: a metric unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter
