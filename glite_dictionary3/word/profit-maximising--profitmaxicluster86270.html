profit-maximising

adjective
aiming to maximise profit
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːfɪt ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪt ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person, action, or enterprise) seeking or aiming to make profits as great as possible.

Examples

profit-maximising company strategy
A profit-maximising firm will always adjust production to achieve the highest possible return.
Setting a profit-maximising price can sometimes reduce customer satisfaction in the long run.
The company's new strategy is strictly profit-maximising and focuses on increasing shareholder value.

Related Terms

  • antonym – loss-making: operating at a financial loss
  • synonym – profit-seeking: actively trying to gain monetary profit
