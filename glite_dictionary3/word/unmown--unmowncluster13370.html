unmown

adjective
not mowed (of grass or vegetation)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmoʊn / (AmE), / ʌnˈməʊn / (BrE)
(of grass or vegetation) not cut down with a hand tool or machine; not mowed.

Examples

unmown grass
After a month of rain, the garden remained unmown and wild.
The unmown grass in the field danced as the wind blew across it.
We couldn't use the park because the unmown lawn was too overgrown.

Related Terms

  • antonym – mown: cut down with a mower or hand tool (of grass/vegetation)
  • synonym – uncut: not trimmed, cut, or mowed
