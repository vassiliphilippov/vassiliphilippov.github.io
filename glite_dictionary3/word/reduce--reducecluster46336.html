to reduce

verb
make less complex or simpler
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdus / (AmE), / rɪˈdjuːs / (BrE)
to simplify, condense, or break something down into simpler or more basic parts

Examples

to reduce a problem
Engineers often reduce complex systems into manageable components before designing solutions.

The professor managed to reduce the complicated theory to a few key principles.

The equation was reduced to a simpler form by collecting like terms.
