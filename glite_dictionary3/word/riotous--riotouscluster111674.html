riotous

adjective
wild and unrestrained; morally uncontrolled
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪətəs / (AmE), / ˈraɪətəs / (BrE)
Behaving in a wild, unrestrained, or morally uncontrolled way; lacking restraint by social convention or morality.

Examples

riotous lifestyle
His riotous lifestyle eventually led to scandal and financial ruin.
The author’s memoir tells of his riotous youth spent in nightclubs across Europe.
The festival was marked by riotous celebrations that went long into the night, defying local curfews.

Related Terms

  • synonym – debauched: indulging in immoral or wild behavior
  • synonym – dissipated: indulging excessively in sensual pleasures; lacking moral restraint
  • antonym – temperate: showing moderation or self-restraint
