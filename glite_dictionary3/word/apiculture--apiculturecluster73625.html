apiculture

noun
the keeping and breeding of bees
Pronunciation: / ˈæpɪˌkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈeɪpɪˌkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the practice of keeping and breeding bees for the production of honey and other products

apiculture techniques
He studied apiculture to learn how to manage bee colonies efficiently.
Modern apiculture techniques have significantly increased honey yields around the world.
The region is renowned for its rich tradition of apiculture and natural honey production.
