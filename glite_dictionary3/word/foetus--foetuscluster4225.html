foetus

noun
unborn vertebrate in later development
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtəs / (AmE), / ˈfiːtəs / (BrE)
An unborn or unhatched vertebrate, particularly a human, in the later stages of development when the major structures are recognizable.

Examples

developing foetus
In some cultures, the protection of the foetus is a central ethical issue.
The doctor monitored the health of the foetus during each stage of the pregnancy.
Ultrasound scans can reveal the position and development of the foetus inside the womb.
