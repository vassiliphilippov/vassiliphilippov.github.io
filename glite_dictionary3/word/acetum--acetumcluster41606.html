acetum

noun
sour liquid; vinegar
Pronunciation: / əˈsiːtəm / (AmE), / əˈsiːtəm / (BrE)
A sour-tasting liquid, especially vinegar, produced by the oxidation of alcohol in wine or cider and used as a condiment or preservative.
