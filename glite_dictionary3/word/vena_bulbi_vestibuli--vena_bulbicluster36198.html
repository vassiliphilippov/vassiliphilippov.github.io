vena bulbi vestibuli

noun
vein of the vestibular bulb
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪ.nə ˈbʊl.baɪ ˌvɛs.tɪˈbjuː.laɪ / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə ˈbʊl.bɪ ˌvɛs.tɪˈbjuː.laɪ / (BrE)
