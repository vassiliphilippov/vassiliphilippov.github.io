contributing

adjective
helping to cause something; being partly responsible
Pronunciation: / kənˈtrɪbjutɪŋ / (AmE), / kənˈtrɪbjʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
Tending to help cause or bring about something; being partly responsible for a result.

Examples

contributing factor
Her efforts were contributing to the team's overall success.
Stress is one of the contributing causes of many health issues.
The storm was a contributing factor in the large number of power outages.
