DCI

noun
data center interconnect (network links)
Pronunciation: / di si ˈaɪ / (AmE), / diː siː ˈaɪ / (BrE)
Data Center Interconnect; high‑capacity networking links or technology used to connect and synchronise geographically separate data centers for data transfer, redundancy, and resource sharing.

Examples

DCI connects datacenters
The provider deployed DCI links to connect several regional data centers for redundancy.
Engineers upgraded the campus network to support higher-capacity DCI traffic between sites.
During the outage, they rerouted traffic across an alternate DCI path to restore services quickly.
