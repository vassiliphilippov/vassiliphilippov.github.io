birling

noun
log-rolling competition on water
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of balancing on and causing a log to spin rapidly in the water, typically as a competition

Examples

log birling contest
Hundreds gathered at the river to watch the annual birling championship.
Many consider birling both an exciting sport and an impressive display of agility.
She practiced birling every weekend to improve her balance and technique.
