trading

noun
buying and selling on financial markets
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of buying and selling securities, commodities, or goods on financial markets.

Examples

trading stocks online
He lost a lot of money in commodity trading last year.
Many people make a living through forex trading on international currency markets.
Stock trading has become much easier with the rise of online brokerages.

Related Terms

  • synonym – commerce: the activity of buying and selling, especially on a large scale
  • synonym – investment: the act of putting money into financial schemes, shares, or property with the expectation of achieving a profit
