enticement

noun
attractive quality that tempts or lures
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtaɪsmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪsmənt / (BrE)
something that attracts or tempts someone, or the act of attracting or tempting by offering a reward or benefit
