purple

noun
a color between red and blue
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜr.pəl / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl / (BrE)
a color intermediate between red and blue, typically associated with royalty or luxury

Examples

bright purple color
He chose purple for the walls to give the room a royal feel.
She wore a beautiful purple dress to the party.
The sky turned a deep shade of purple as the sun set.

  • degree lower – red: a primary color at one end of the spectrum next to orange and opposite violet
