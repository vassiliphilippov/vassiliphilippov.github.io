Westminster

proper noun
central London borough with major government sites
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstˌmɪnstər / (AmE), / ˈwɛsmɪnstə / (BrE)
a borough of central London on the River Thames, home to major government buildings including the Houses of Parliament, Buckingham Palace, and Westminster Abbey
