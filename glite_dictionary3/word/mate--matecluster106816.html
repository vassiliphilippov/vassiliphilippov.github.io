mate

noun
South American herbal drink
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.teɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.teɪ / (BrE)
A South American beverage made by steeping dried leaves of the yerba mate plant in hot water, traditionally served in a hollowed-out gourd and drunk through a metal straw.

Examples

drink mate
Drinking mate together is an important social ritual in several South American countries.
Many people in Argentina start their day with a cup of mate instead of coffee.
She offered her guests mate served in a traditional gourd with a metal straw.
