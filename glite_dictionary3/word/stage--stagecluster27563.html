stage

noun
raised platform for performances
Pronunciation: / steɪdʒ / (AmE), / steɪdʒ / (BrE)
a raised platform in a theatre or other venue where performers, speakers, or presenters stand and can be seen by an audience

Examples

theater stage
After her speech, she left the stage to a standing ovation.
The band walked onto the stage as the crowd cheered wildly.
Workers spent all day building the stage for the outdoor concert.
