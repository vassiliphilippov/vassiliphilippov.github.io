pottery

noun
the craft of making earthenware
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑː.t̬ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈpɒt.ər.i / (BrE)
the craft or activity of making objects such as pots, bowls, and vases from clay and hardening them by heat
