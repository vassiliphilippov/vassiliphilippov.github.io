William Herschel

proper noun
English astronomer; discovered Uranus and infrared radiation
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪljəm ˈhɜrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ˈhɜːʃəl / (BrE)
(1738–1822) English astronomer and composer, born in Germany, known for discovering the planet Uranus, cataloguing stars, and discovering infrared radiation.
