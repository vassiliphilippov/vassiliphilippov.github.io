expansive

adjective
impressive and ambitious in scale
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɑːnsɪv / (BrE)
(of behavior or actions) impressive and ambitious in scale or scope; grand or wide-ranging.

Examples

expansive plans
Her expansive vision for the company included launching products across three continents.
The billionaire was known for his expansive lifestyle and extravagant parties.
They embarked on an expansive renovation of the historic palace.
