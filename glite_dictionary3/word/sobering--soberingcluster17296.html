sobering

adjective
causing seriousness or thoughtfulness
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbərɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel more serious, thoughtful, or less cheerful, often as a result of realizing something unsettling or important.

Examples

sobering experience
Hearing about the risks involved was a sobering reminder to proceed with caution.
The film provided a sobering look at life in a war zone.
The statistics about climate change were truly sobering for the entire audience.
