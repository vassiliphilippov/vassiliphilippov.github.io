lip-synch

noun
social-media lip-synced video
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪp sɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈlɪp sɪŋk / (BrE)
a short recorded video in which someone lip-synches to a song or speech, typically created and shared on social media as part of a trend or for entertainment.

Examples

lip-synch video
Her latest lip-synch on TikTok quickly gained millions of views overnight.
Students filmed a creative lip-synch to the popular song for their school project last week.
She edited together several clips to make a funny lip-synch that the whole team shared.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clip: a short recorded segment of video or film, often shared online
  • synonym – lip-synch (verb): to move the lips in synchronization with recorded speech or singing
