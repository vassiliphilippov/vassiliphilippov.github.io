pedelec

noun
bicycle with pedal-assist electric motor
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːdəˌlɛk / (AmE), / ˈpiːdəlɛk / (BrE)
a bicycle equipped with an electric motor that assists the rider's pedaling effort
