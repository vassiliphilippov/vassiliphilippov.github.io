frontal

adjective
located at or relating to the front
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.t̬əl /; / ˈfrʌn.t̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.təl /; / ˈfrʌn.tl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or situated at the front part of something, including the forehead, skull, or a direct impact from the front

Examples
