Omar Khayyam

proper noun
Persian poet and mathematician (1048–1131)
Pronunciation: / oʊˌmɑːr kaɪˈjɑːm / (AmE), / əʊˌmɑː kaɪˈjɑːm / (BrE)
(c. 1048–1131) A Persian poet, mathematician, and astronomer, best known in the West for his poetic work 'The Rubáiyát', popularized by Edward FitzGerald's translation.
