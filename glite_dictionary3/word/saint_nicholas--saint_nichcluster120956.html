Saint Nicholas

proper noun
legendary patron saint of children; Christmas gift-bringer
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈnɪkələs / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈnɪkələs / (BrE)
A legendary Christian saint regarded as the patron saint of children and, in Western cultures, associated with bringing gifts to children at Christmas.

Examples

Saint Nicholas brings gifts
Every year, children look forward to the arrival of Saint Nicholas on Christmas Eve.
In many European countries, gifts are exchanged in honor of Saint Nicholas.
The story of Saint Nicholas has inspired countless holiday traditions around the world.
