hesitatingly

adverb
in a hesitant manner
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛzɪteɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhɛzɪteɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows hesitation or uncertainty.

Examples

to speak hesitatingly
After a long pause, the student hesitatingly explained his mistake.
He hesitatingly reached for the phone, afraid of what he might hear.
She answered hesitatingly, unsure of what to say next.
