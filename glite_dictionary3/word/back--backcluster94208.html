to back

verb
of wind, shift counterclockwise
Pronunciation: / bæk / (AmE), / bæk / (BrE)
(of the wind) to change direction in a counterclockwise direction, especially in meteorology.

Examples

wind backs north
During the storm, forecasters predicted the wind would back to the east.
If the wind backs, the weather often becomes unsettled along the coast.
Overnight, the wind began to back from southwest to south.
