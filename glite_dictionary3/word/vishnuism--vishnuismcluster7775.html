Vishnuism

noun
worship focused on the Hindu god Vishnu
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʃnuːˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈvɪʃnjuːˌɪzəm / (BrE)
the worship or religious tradition centered on Vishnu, one of the three principal gods of Hinduism
