autoplastic

adjective
relating to tissue repair using one's own tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊˈplæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˈplæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving autoplasty, especially the repair or reconstruction of tissue using tissue from the same individual

Examples

autoplastic tissue graft
In some cases of severe burns, autoplastic grafts are preferred over synthetic materials.
The research focuses on autoplastic reconstruction techniques to improve healing times.
The surgeon performed an autoplastic operation using the patient's own skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym – alloplastic: relating to the repair of tissue using material from outside the individual
