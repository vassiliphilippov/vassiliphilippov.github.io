catwalk

noun
narrow elevated walkway
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌwɔk / (AmE), / ˈkæt.wɔːk / (BrE)
a narrow, often elevated walkway, especially one high above the ground in a theater, between parts of a building, or along a bridge

Examples

high catwalk
During maintenance, the engineer accessed the pipes via a high catwalk above the machinery.
The stage crew moved the lights from the catwalk above the stage.
Workers must wear a safety harness when crossing the narrow catwalk over the factory floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – walkway: a passage or path for walking along, especially a raised one
