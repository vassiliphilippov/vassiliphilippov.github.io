ignitor

noun
device for lighting fuel or fires
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnaɪtər / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnaɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to ignite fuel, charges, or fires by producing a spark or flame

Examples

electric ignitor device
A gas stove may use an electric ignitor instead of matches for greater convenience.
Be sure to check that the barbecue ignitor works before starting to grill.
The technician replaced the faulty ignitor in the furnace to restore heat to the building.
