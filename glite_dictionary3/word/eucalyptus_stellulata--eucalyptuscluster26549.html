Eucalyptus stellulata

noun
species of eucalyptus tree
Pronunciation: / juːkəˈlɪptəs stɛl(j)uˈleɪtə / (AmE), / juːkəˈlɪptəs stɛl(j)ʊˈleɪtə / (BrE)
A small Australian eucalyptus tree with rough, dark bark at the base, known for producing a red kino gum.
