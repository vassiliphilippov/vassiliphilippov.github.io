perchloride

noun
chloride with unusually high chlorine content
Pronunciation: / pɚˈklɔːr.aɪd / (AmE), / pɜːˈklɔːr.aɪd / (BrE)
a chloride compound in which the proportion of chlorine is unusually high, especially compared to other chlorides of the same element.
