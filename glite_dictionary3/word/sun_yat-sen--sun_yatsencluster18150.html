Sun Yat-sen

proper noun
Chinese revolutionary and political leader
Pronunciation: / sʊn jæt ˈsɛn / (AmE), / sʌn jæt ˈsɛn / (BrE)
(1866–1925) A Chinese statesman and revolutionary leader who founded the Kuomintang and played a key role in overthrowing the Manchu dynasty, establishing the foundations for modern China.
