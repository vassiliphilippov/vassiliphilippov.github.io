to unravel

verb
disentangle or resolve something complicated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrævəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈrævəl / (BrE)
To separate or disentangle the threads of something; to solve or clarify something that is confusing or mysterious.

Examples

to unravel a mystery
Detectives worked tirelessly to unravel the secret behind the crime.
It took hours to unravel all the knots in the rope.
Sometimes, childhood memories unravel when you least expect them.

Related Terms

  • synonym – solve: to find an answer to a problem or mystery
  • synonym – untangle: to make something free from knots or twists
