shooter

noun
photographer or camera operator
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtə / (BrE)
(informal, journalism/media) a photographer or camera operator who records still images or video, especially on assignment.

Examples

shooter takes photos
The newsroom hired a skilled shooter to capture key moments of the event.
Freelance shooters often travel with lightweight gear to get story-ready video quickly.
During the protest a television shooter filmed interviews and crowds for the evening report.

Related Terms

  • synonym – camera operator: a professional who operates a video or film camera
  • synonym – photographer: a person who takes photographs, especially professionally
