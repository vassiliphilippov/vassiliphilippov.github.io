weepiness

noun
tendency to weep or cry
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːpinəs / (AmE), / ˈwiːpinɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being inclined to weep; a tendency to express sadness through tears

Examples

emotional weepiness
A sense of weepiness overcame her as she read the heartfelt letter.
He blamed his recent weepiness on the stressful week at work.
Her weepiness during the movie made everyone reach for tissues.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tearfulness: the state of being full of tears; an inclination to cry
