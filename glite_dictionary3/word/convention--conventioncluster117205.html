convention

noun
international agreement
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛn(t)ʃən / (AmE), / kənˈvɛn(t)ʃən / (BrE)
an international agreement between countries, especially one less formal than a treaty

Examples

convention on climate change
Several countries signed the convention to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
The Geneva Convention outlines protections for prisoners of war.
Under the new international convention, trade restrictions were lifted.

Related Terms

  • synonym – protocol: an official procedure or system of rules, sometimes meaning a formal international agreement
  • synonym – treaty: a formally concluded and ratified agreement between countries
