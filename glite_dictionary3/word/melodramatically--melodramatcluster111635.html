melodramatically

adverb
in an overly emotional or dramatic way
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛlədrəˈmætɪkli /; / ˌmɛl.ə.drəˈmæt.ɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌmɛlədrəˈmatɪkli /; / ˌmɛl.ə.drəˈmat.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
in an exaggeratedly emotional or dramatic way, often more than is realistic
