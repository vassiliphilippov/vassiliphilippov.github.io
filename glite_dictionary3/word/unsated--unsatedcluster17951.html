unsated

adjective
not satisfied; not sated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having satisfied one's desire or appetite; not sated.

Examples

He left the table with an unsated appetite, wishing there had been more to eat.

Their unsated ambition pushed them to achieve even more despite their earlier successes.
