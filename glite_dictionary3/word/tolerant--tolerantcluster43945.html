tolerant

adjective
able to endure adverse conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːlɚənt / (AmE), / ˈtɒlərənt / (BrE)
able to withstand adverse environmental conditions or physiological stress without harm

Examples

tolerant of cold weather
Some bacteria are highly tolerant of acidic environments.
The new crop variety is especially tolerant of drought conditions.
This type of grass is tolerant to heavy foot traffic and poor soil.
