abruptness

noun
quality of being sudden and unexpected
Pronunciation: / əˈbrʌptnəs / (AmE), / əˈbrʌptnəs / (BrE)
the quality of occurring suddenly and unexpectedly, often with little or no warning

Examples

abruptness of change
Her abruptness caught her colleagues off guard.
I was startled by the abruptness with which the meeting ended.
The abruptness of his departure left everyone in shock.
