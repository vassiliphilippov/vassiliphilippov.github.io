override

noun
manual control for bypassing automation
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌraɪd / (BrE)
a mechanism or control that allows a person to manually take control over or bypass the operation of an automatic device

Examples

manual override button
If the automatic doors get stuck, use the manual override.
The engineer engaged the override to stop the machine immediately.
The security system has an override in case of emergency.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bypass: a means of circumventing or ignoring a regular process
