to shift

verb
change or exchange roles or positions
Pronunciation: / ʃɪft / (AmE), / ʃɪft / (BrE)
to change, exchange, or transfer from one thing, state, quality, or focus to another

Examples

to shift focus
After an hour, the drivers shifted so the tired one could rest.
She shifted from leading the meeting to taking notes instead.

The conversation shifted from politics to sports.

She decided to shift her focus to a new career after many years in teaching.
