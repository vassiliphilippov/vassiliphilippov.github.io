appearance

noun
the act of coming into view
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
the act of becoming visible or present, especially in public or at an event

Examples

appearance on stage
Her appearance on stage was greeted with loud applause.

His first appearance on television marked the beginning of a long acting career.
The celebrity made an unexpected appearance at the charity event, delighting her fans.
