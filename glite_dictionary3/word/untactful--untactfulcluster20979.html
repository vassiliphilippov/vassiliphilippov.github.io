untactful

adjective
lacking tact or sensitivity
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæktfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaktfʊl / (BrE)
lacking sensitivity or consideration in dealing with others; not tactful.

Examples

untactful comment
Being untactful during sensitive negotiations can cause unnecessary conflict.
Her untactful remarks made the meeting uncomfortable for several colleagues.
It was rather untactful of him to criticize her cooking in front of everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym – tactful: showing sensitivity and skill in dealing with others
  • synonym – tactless: showing a lack of tact or sensitivity in dealing with others
