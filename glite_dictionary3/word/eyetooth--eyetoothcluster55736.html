eyetooth

noun
pointed tooth between incisors and premolars
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌtuθ / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌtuːθ / (BrE)
one of the four pointed teeth between the incisors and the premolars in each jaw; also called a canine tooth.
