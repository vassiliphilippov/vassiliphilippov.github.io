St. Martin

proper noun
French bishop and patron saint of France
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈmɑːrtən / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈmɑːtɪn / (BrE)
A French bishop of the 4th century, recognized as a patron saint of France, known for his acts of kindness and charity.
