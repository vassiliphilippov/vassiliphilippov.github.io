firehouse

noun
building for firefighters and equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A building where firefighters and firefighting equipment are stationed and from which they operate.

Examples

local firehouse
After the parade, the children were invited to tour the local firehouse and meet the firefighters.
Every morning, the crew at the firehouse checks and maintains their equipment to ensure it's ready for emergencies.
The new city firehouse was built near the school to improve response times.
