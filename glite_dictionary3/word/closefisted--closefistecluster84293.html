closefisted

adjective
unwilling to spend money
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊsˌfɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsˌfɪstɪd / (BrE)
unwilling to spend or give money; miserly

Examples

closefisted businessman
Despite his wealth, the old man remained closefisted and seldom donated to charity.
She is so closefisted that she refuses to tip waiters, no matter how good the service.
They accused the company of being closefisted with their employees' bonuses.

Related Terms

  • antonym – generous: willing to give more money or help than is usual or expected
  • synonym – stingy: unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous
