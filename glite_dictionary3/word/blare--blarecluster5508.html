to blare

verb
make a loud, harsh noise
Pronunciation: / blɛr / (AmE), / blɛə / (BrE)
to make a loud, harsh, and unpleasant noise

Examples

Music blared from the open windows of passing cars.

The loudspeaker blared announcements throughout the busy station.
Music blared from the loudspeakers all afternoon.
The alarm blared suddenly, startling everyone in the building.
The car horns blared in the traffic jam.
