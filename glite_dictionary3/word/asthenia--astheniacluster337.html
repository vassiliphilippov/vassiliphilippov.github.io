asthenia

noun
abnormal loss of strength (medical)
Pronunciation: / æsˈθiniə / (AmE), / æsˈθiːniə / (BrE)
A condition marked by abnormal weakness or loss of strength, especially as a symptom of disease.

Examples

chronic asthenia
Chronic asthenia can significantly impact a person's daily activities.
Doctors noted that her asthenia was likely due to an underlying neurological disorder.
The patient complained of sudden asthenia following the infection.
