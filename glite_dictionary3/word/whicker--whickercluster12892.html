to whicker

verb
(of a horse) make a gentle neigh or whinny
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪkər / (AmE), / ˈwɪkə / (BrE)
(of a horse) to make a soft, gentle neighing or whinnying sound.

Examples

to whicker softly
I heard the mares whickering to each other in the early morning.
She smiled as her pony whickered in recognition of her voice.
The horse began to whicker softly as I approached with an apple.

Related Terms

  • synonym – neigh: to make the loud, high sound of a horse
  • synonym – whinny: to make a gentle, high-pitched neigh, especially of a horse
