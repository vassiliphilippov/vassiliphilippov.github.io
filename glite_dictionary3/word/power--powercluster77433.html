power

noun
ability or capacity to do something
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊər / (AmE), / ˈpaʊə / (BrE)
the ability or capacity to do something or act in a particular way, especially mental or physical ability

Examples

mental power
He lacks the power to persuade people when he speaks.
Her power of concentration impressed all her teachers.
Studying daily helped improve his power of reasoning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ability: the possession of the means or skill to do something
  • antonym – incapacity: lack of power or ability
