publicity

noun
being open to public view
Pronunciation: / pʌbˈlɪsɪti / (AmE), / pʌbˈlɪsɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being open to public observation or access

Examples

publicity of records
Concerns were raised over the publicity of government meetings during the investigation.
The publicity of the proceedings guarantees that justice is seen to be done.
The judge emphasized the publicity of the trial to ensure transparency.

Related Terms

  • antonym – secrecy: the quality of being hidden or not open to the public
