librettist

noun
person who writes opera lyrics
Pronunciation: / lɪˈbrɛtɪst / (AmE), / lɪˈbrɛtɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes the words (libretto) for an opera or other long vocal work

Examples

librettist for an opera
As a librettist, she spent months crafting the perfect words for the production.
Many famous composers have relied on a skilled librettist to bring their music to life.
The celebrated librettist collaborated with the composer to create a memorable opera.
