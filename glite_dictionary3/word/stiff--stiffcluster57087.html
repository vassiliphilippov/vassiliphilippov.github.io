stiff

adjective
resolute or unwavering in attitude
Pronunciation: / stɪf / (AmE), / stɪf / (BrE)
(of a person or their attitude) marked by firm determination or resoluteness; not easily changed or shaken.

Examples

stiff resistance
Even under pressure, her stiff convictions never wavered.
His stiff determination inspired confidence in the whole team.
She maintained a stiff resolve despite the challenges she faced.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resolute: admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering
  • synonym – steady: firm in purpose or attachment; unwavering
  • antonym – wavering: becoming weaker; faltering or indecisive
