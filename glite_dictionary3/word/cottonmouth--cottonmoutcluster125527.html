cottonmouth

noun
venomous snake of southern US wetlands
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtənˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈkɒtənˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
A venomous, semiaquatic snake of the pit viper family, native to swamps and waterways in the southern United States.

Examples

cottonmouth snake
Be careful when fishing in these waters, as cottonmouths are common here.
The inside of a cottonmouth's mouth is bright white, which is how it got its name.
