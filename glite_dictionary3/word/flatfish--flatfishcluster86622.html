flatfish

noun
lean white flesh of flat marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈflætˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
The flesh of any of several thin-bodied, flat marine fish, typically served as white, lean fillets.

Examples

flatfish fillet dish
She prefers the mild flavor of flatfish over other kinds of seafood.
The restaurant's special tonight is grilled flatfish served with lemon butter sauce.
They carefully filleted the fresh flatfish for the chef to prepare.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flounder: a type of flat, edible fish from the same family
  • synonym – sole: another species of flat, edible fish often used in cooking
