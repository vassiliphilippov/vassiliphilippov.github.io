effervescence

noun
the process of bubbling as gas escapes
Pronunciation: / ˌɛfərˈvɛsəns / (AmE), / ˌɛfəˈvɛsəns / (BrE)
the process of bubbling or fizzing as gas escapes from a liquid, often seen as bubbles or foam

Examples

  • antonym – flatness: the absence of bubbles or fizz, especially in a beverage
