artistry

noun
superior creative skill or ability
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtɪstri / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪstri / (BrE)
a superior skill or ability in creating or performing something, especially in the arts, acquired through study, practice, or observation

Examples

musical artistry
Her artistry in painting brings every landscape to life with vivid emotion.
The pianist's artistry captivated the audience throughout the entire recital.
We were amazed by the artistry behind the intricate design of the stained glass windows.
