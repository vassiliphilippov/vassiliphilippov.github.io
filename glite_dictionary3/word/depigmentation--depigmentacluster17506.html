depigmentation

noun
loss of skin or hair pigmentation
Pronunciation: / diˌpɪɡmənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌpɪɡmɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the absence or loss of normal pigmentation in the skin or hair, resulting in lighter color

Examples

skin depigmentation
Some chemicals can cause depigmentation when they come into contact with the skin.
The scars on his arm showed clear depigmentation compared to the surrounding area.
Vitiligo is a condition that causes depigmentation of patches of skin.
