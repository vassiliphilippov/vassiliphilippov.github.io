suede

noun
soft leather with a napped surface
Pronunciation: / sweɪd / (AmE), / sweɪd / (BrE)
a type of soft leather with a napped surface, made from the underside of animal skin, typically used for clothing and accessories

Examples

suede leather jacket
Because suede is delicate, it can be easily stained by water.
She wore a stylish jacket made of suede to the party.
The suede shoes felt soft and luxurious to the touch.
