brushed

adjective
fabric with softened, raised surface
Pronunciation: / brʌʃt / (AmE), / brʌʃt / (BrE)
(of fabric) having a soft, raised surface created by brushing the fibers to make them smooth or fuzzy.

Examples

brushed cotton sheets
Brushed fabrics are often chosen for winter clothing because of their cozy feel.
She bought a jacket made of brushed cotton for extra warmth.
The blanket's brushed finish makes it soft to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – napped: (of fabric) having a soft, raised surface, typically achieved by brushing
