sealer

noun
material for coating and sealing surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlər / (AmE), / ˈsiːlə / (BrE)
a substance or material applied to a porous surface to form a hard, protective coating, often before painting or finishing

Examples

wood sealer
Before painting, apply a sealer to ensure the surface is smooth and protected.
She used a clear sealer to preserve the natural look of the wood table.
The contractor recommended a water-resistant sealer for the concrete patio.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sealant: a substance used to block the passage of fluids through the surface or joints or openings in materials
