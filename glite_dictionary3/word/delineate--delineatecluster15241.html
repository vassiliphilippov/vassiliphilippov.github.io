to delineate

verb
describe in detail
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɪniˌeɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪnieɪt / (BrE)
to describe or explain something in detail, clearly and precisely

Examples

to delineate a problem
In her memoir, she delineates the hardships she faced during her childhood.
The document delineates the new regulations for environmental protection.
The report delineates the steps involved in the project with remarkable clarity.
In his lecture, the professor delineated the concept of justice in various cultures.
She carefully delineated the main responsibilities for each team member.
The report aims to delineate the key differences between the two proposals.
