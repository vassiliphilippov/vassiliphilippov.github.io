smallness

noun
lack of generosity or pettiness
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɔlnəs / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːlnəs / (BrE)
a quality of being ungenerous or petty about trivial matters

Examples

smallness over money
Her smallness was apparent when she argued endlessly over minor expenses.
There was a certain smallness in his refusal to share credit with his colleagues.
You could detect smallness in the way she made every little favor seem like a burden.

Related Terms

  • antonym – generosity: the quality of being kind and generous
  • synonym – pettiness: concern with trivial or unimportant matters, especially in a mean way
