silver

noun
a soft white metallic element, Ag
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə / (BrE)
a soft, white, precious metallic element (symbol Ag) with the highest electrical and thermal conductivity of all metals, used in coins, jewelry, tableware, and photography
