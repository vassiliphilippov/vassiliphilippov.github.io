pharyngeal

noun
consonant produced in the pharynx
Pronunciation: / fəˈrɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / fəˈrɪndʒɪəl / (BrE)
A consonant sound produced with the root of the tongue against the pharynx (back of the throat).

Examples

pharyngeal consonant
Arabic contains several pharyngeal consonants not found in English.
Students practiced articulating a pharyngeal during their phonetics class.
The sound represented by 'ʕ' in the IPA is a voiced pharyngeal.
