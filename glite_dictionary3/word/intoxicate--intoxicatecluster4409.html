to intoxicate

verb
fill with excitement or happiness
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɑksɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɒksɪˌkeɪt / (BrE)
to fill someone with strong feelings of excitement, happiness, or optimism

Examples

to intoxicate with success
She was intoxicated by her sudden success and optimism.
The beauty of the landscape can intoxicate even the most jaded traveler.
The crowd's energy seemed to intoxicate the performers on stage.
