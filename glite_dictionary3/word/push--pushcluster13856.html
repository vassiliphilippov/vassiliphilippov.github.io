to push

verb
make forceful efforts during childbirth
Pronunciation: / pʊʃ / (AmE), / pʊʃ / (BrE)
(of a woman in labor) to make forceful muscular efforts, especially of the abdominal muscles, to help expel the baby during childbirth.

Examples

to push during labor
During labor, women are often encouraged to push when the time is right.
She gripped the bedrails tightly and began to push as instructed.
The nurse coached her to push with each contraction.
