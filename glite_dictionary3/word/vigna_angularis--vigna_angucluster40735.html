Vigna angularis

noun
species of bean cultivated in East Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪɡnə æŋˈɡjʊlərɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɪɡnə æŋˈɡjʊlərɪs / (BrE)
A bushy annual plant native to East Asia, widely cultivated for its edible seeds known as adzuki beans, used particularly in Chinese and Japanese cuisine.
