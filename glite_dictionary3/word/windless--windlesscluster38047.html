windless

adjective
without wind; calm (weather)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndləs / (AmE), / ˈwɪndləs / (BrE)
(of weather or a place) having no wind or very little wind.

Examples

windless night
Sailing is difficult in windless conditions, so the race was postponed.
The lake was perfectly still on that windless morning.
We enjoyed a quiet picnic in the windless park.
