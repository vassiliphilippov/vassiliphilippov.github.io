eulogium

noun
formal tribute or praise for the dead
Pronunciation: / juːˈloʊdʒiəm / (AmE), / juːˈləʊdʒiəm / (BrE)
A formal speech or written statement praising someone, especially a tribute for someone who has recently died.

Examples

moving eulogium
He wrote a touching eulogium for his childhood friend in the local newspaper.
Her eulogium of the late professor moved many of his former students to tears.
The family requested that a eulogium be read at the memorial service.
