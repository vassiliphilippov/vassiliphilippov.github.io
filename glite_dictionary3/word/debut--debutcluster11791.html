debut

noun
first appearance or introduction
Pronunciation: / deɪˈbju / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ.bju / (BrE)
the first public appearance or presentation of someone or something; the beginning of something new, especially in an official or formal way

Examples

debut performance
After months of rehearsals, the band finally made their debut at the music festival.
Her acting debut in the lead role was widely praised by critics.
The company's latest smartphone made its debut in stores last Friday.
