Charles II

proper noun
King of England, Scotland, and Ireland, 1630–1685
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrlz ðə ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːlz ðə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
