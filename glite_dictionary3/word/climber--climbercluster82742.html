climber

noun
earring that follows the earlobe
Pronunciation: / c8kla6am5a / (AmE), / c8kla6am59 / (BrE)
a type of earring that sits along and appears to 'climb' the earlobe, often following its curve.

Examples

ear climber earring
She wore a pair of silver climber earrings that traced the curve of her ear.
The designer created a delicate gold climber that follows the earlobe and ends in a tiny gem.
For her birthday he bought a pair of rose-gold climber earrings to complement her dress.
