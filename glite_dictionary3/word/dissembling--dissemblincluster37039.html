dissembling

noun
concealing true intentions by pretense
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛmblɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛmblɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of hiding your true motives, feelings, or beliefs by pretending or giving a false appearance; deception
