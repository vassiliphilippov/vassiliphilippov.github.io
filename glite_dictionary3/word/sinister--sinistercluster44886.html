sinister

adjective
suggestive of evil or harm
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnɪstər / (AmE), / ˈsɪnɪstə / (BrE)
suggesting that something evil, harmful, or dangerous is happening or will happen; giving a feeling of threat

Examples

sinister feeling

There was a sinister figure lurking in the shadows, making everyone uneasy.
Dark clouds gathered overhead, casting a sinister shadow on the town.
He gave her a sinister smile that sent chills down her spine.
The abandoned house had a sinister atmosphere that made everyone uneasy.
