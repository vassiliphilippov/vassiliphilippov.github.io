collision

noun
conflict of ideas or interests
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / kəˈlɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a situation in which two or more ideas, interests, or goals are in strong disagreement or conflict with each other

Examples

collision of ideas
The collision of their aspirations led to a prolonged negotiation.
The meeting revealed a collision of values between the traditionalists and the progressives.
There was an inevitable collision of priorities during the budget discussions.

  • synonym – conflict: serious disagreement or argument
