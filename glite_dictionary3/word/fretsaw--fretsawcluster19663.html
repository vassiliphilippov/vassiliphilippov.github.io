fretsaw

noun
fine-toothed saw for cutting curves
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛtˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈfrɛtˌsɔː / (BrE)
a fine-toothed saw with a narrow blade, used for cutting intricate curved patterns in wood or other materials

Examples

fretsaw detailed wood
A fretsaw is ideal for detailed woodworking projects requiring precise, curved lines.
The craftsman used a fretsaw to cut delicate curves in the wooden panel.
Using a fretsaw, she created intricate patterns for the jewelry box lid.
