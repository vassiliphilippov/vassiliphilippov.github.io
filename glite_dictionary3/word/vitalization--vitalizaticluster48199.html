vitalization

noun
the state of being filled with life or energy
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvaɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the state or process of being filled with life, energy, and vigor

Examples

vitalization of the body
After her long illness, she felt a gradual vitalization returning to her body.
The yoga retreat led to a profound sense of vitalization in all the participants.
Urban renewal projects can result in the vitalization of neglected neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • antonym – enervation: the process of being deprived of strength or energy
  • synonym – invigoration: the act of giving life or energy to something
  • synonym – revitalization: the action of bringing new life or energy to something
