shortness

noun
insufficiency or lack of something
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt.nəs / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt.nəs / (BrE)
the state or condition of lacking enough of something; insufficiency.

Examples

shortness of supplies
A sudden shortness of supplies disrupted the rescue effort.
The doctor noted a shortness of breath during his examination.
There is a shortness of skilled workers in the industry this year.
