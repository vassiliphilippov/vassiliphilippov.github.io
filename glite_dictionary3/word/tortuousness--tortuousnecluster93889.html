tortuousness

noun
quality of being twisted or winding
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtʃuəsnəs /; / ˈtɔr.tʃu.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃuəsnəs /; / ˈtɔː.tjʊ.əs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being full of twists and turns, or being winding and difficult to follow
