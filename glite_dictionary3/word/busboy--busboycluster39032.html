busboy

noun
male restaurant attendant (clears tables, assists waiters)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌsˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌs.bɔɪ / (BrE)
A male restaurant worker who clears tables, sets them, and assists waiters.

Examples

restaurant busboy
After the customers finished their dinner, the busboy quickly cleared the plates and wiped the table.
He worked part-time as a busboy while attending college in the evenings.
The busboy assisted the waiters by bringing water and resetting the tables between guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym – busser: restaurant worker who performs similar duties, gender-neutral
  • degree higher – waiter: person who serves food and drinks at a restaurant
