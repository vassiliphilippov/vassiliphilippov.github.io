to chuck

verb
pat or squeeze playfully (esp. under chin)
Pronunciation: / tʃʌk / (AmE), / tʃʌk / (BrE)
to pat or lightly squeeze someone fondly or playfully, especially under the chin

Examples

to chuck a chin

She smiled as her grandfather chucked her gently under the chin.
