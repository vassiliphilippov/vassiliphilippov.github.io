regional

adjective
of or relating to a region
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənl̩ /; / ˈriːdʒənl / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a particular region or area, not the whole country or world

Examples
