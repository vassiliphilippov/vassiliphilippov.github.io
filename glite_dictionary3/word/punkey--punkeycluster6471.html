punkey

noun
tiny bloodsucking insect; midge
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋki / (BrE)
A very small two-winged insect, such as a biting midge, that feeds on the blood of mammals, birds, and other insects.

Examples

a punkey bite
Many birds feed on punkeys as a source of protein.
Mosquitoes and punkeys made sitting outside unpleasant during the humid summer evenings.
The campers applied insect repellent to avoid bites from punkeys around the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym – midge: small, biting two-winged insect
