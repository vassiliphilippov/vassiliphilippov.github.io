unmoderated

adjective
not moderated; not made less extreme
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɑdəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɒdəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not made less extreme or moderate; not controlled to reduce intensity or severity.

Examples

unmoderated criticism

Unmoderated comments on internet forums can quickly become offensive or extreme.
