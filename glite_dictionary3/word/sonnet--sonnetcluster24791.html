sonnet

noun
14-line poem with fixed rhyme scheme
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑː.nət / (AmE), / ˈsɒn.ɪt / (BrE)
A poem of 14 lines with a fixed rhyme scheme, typically written in iambic pentameter.

Shakespearean sonnet
