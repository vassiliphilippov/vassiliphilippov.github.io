netball

noun
team sport with ball and hoop
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈnɛtbɔːl / (BrE)
A team sport played by two teams of seven players, in which the objective is to score points by throwing a ball through an opponent's raised hoop.

play netball matches
