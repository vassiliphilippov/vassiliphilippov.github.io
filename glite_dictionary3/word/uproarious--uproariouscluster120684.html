uproarious

adjective
very noisy or full of loud laughter
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈrɔriəs / (AmE), / ʌpˈrɔːrɪəs / (BrE)
Characterized by loud, uncontrollable, and often joyful noise or laughter.

Examples

uproarious party noise
A party became uproarious as the night went on and the music grew louder.
The uproarious cheers of the students could be heard from blocks away after their team's victory.
The comedian's jokes were so uproarious that the entire audience couldn't stop laughing.
