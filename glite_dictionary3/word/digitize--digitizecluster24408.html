to digitize

verb
convert into digital format
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to convert information or images into a digital form that can be processed by a computer

Examples

to digitize old photos
Many companies now digitize their records for easier storage and access.
The library decided to digitize its old manuscripts to preserve them for future generations.
You can easily digitize your family photos using a scanner.
