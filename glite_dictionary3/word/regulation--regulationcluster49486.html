regulation

adjective
conforming to official rules; standard
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛɡjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛɡjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
(of things) prescribed by or conforming to official rules or laws, especially in terms of standards or requirements.

Examples

regulation uniform
All soldiers were required to wear the regulation uniform during the ceremony.
Only regulation size basketballs are allowed in the tournament.
The office was equipped with regulation fire extinguishers in every room.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nonstandard: not conforming to the official or prescribed rules or norms
  • synonym – standard: established or accepted as a model of authority or excellence
