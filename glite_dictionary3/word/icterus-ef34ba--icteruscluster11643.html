icterus

noun
medical term for jaundice
Pronunciation: / ˈɪktərəs / (AmE), / ˈɪktərəs / (BrE)
yellowing of the skin and the whites of the eyes resulting from an accumulation of bile pigments in the blood; a medical symptom commonly known as jaundice

Examples

icterus in newborns
After a blood test confirmed elevated bilirubin, her icterus was diagnosed as hepatitis.
Chronic liver disease can manifest as icterus and abdominal pain.
The doctor noted the patient's icterus during the examination.
