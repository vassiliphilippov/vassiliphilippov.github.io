lanyard

noun
cord or strap worn for carrying items
Pronunciation: / ˈlæn.jɚd / (AmE), / ˈlæn.jəd / (BrE)
a cord or strap worn around the neck, shoulder, or wrist to carry small objects such as a knife, whistle, or identification card

Examples

lanyard for ID card
Security handed out a lanyard with each visitor's pass.
She wore her ID badge on a lanyard around her neck.
The keys were attached to a lanyard to prevent losing them at the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cord: a length of string or thick thread
