to receive

verb
to be given; to get (something)
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsiv / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːv / (BrE)
to get or be given something; to come into possession of something

Examples

to receive a gift
All guests will receive a complimentary drink upon arrival.
Did you receive my email yesterday, or should I resend it?
She was delighted to receive a promotion at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accept: to take willingly or agree to receive something offered
  • antonym – give: to hand over or provide something to someone else
