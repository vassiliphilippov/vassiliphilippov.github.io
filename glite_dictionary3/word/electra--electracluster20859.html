Electra

proper noun
daughter of Agamemnon in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrə / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrə / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) the daughter of Agamemnon and Clytemnestra who, with her brother Orestes, avenged her father's murder by helping kill her mother and her mother's lover.
