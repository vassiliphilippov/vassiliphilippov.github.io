slobber

noun
saliva dribbling from the mouth
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːbər / (AmE), / ˈslɒbə / (BrE)
saliva that drips or runs from the mouth, especially as a result of excitement or lack of control

Examples

dog slobber
She wiped the slobber off the dog's mouth after their walk.
The baby's chin was covered in slobber after playing with his toys.
There was a trail of slobber left on the couch cushion.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drool: saliva that flows from the mouth uncontrollably
