Midway

proper noun
naval battle in World War II (1942)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.weɪ / (BrE)
a decisive naval battle in World War II fought in June 1942, in which American forces defeated a Japanese fleet near the Midway Islands
