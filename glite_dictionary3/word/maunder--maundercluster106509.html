to maunder

verb
wander aimlessly
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔndər / (AmE), / ˈmɔːndə / (BrE)
to wander or move aimlessly and without clear purpose

Examples

to maunder through streets
As the afternoon faded, they maundered around the quiet streets with no real destination.
He would often maunder through the fields, lost in his thoughts.
The old dog maundered along the lane, stopping to sniff here and there.
