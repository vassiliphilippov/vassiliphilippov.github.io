cyclooxygenase-2

noun
enzyme linked to inflammation and pain
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkloʊˌɑksɪˌdʒiːneɪs tuː / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkləʊˌɒksɪˌdʒiːneɪs tuː / (BrE)
an enzyme involved in the production of prostaglandins, contributing to inflammation, pain, and fever

Examples

cyclooxygenase-2 enzyme activity
Excessive activity of cyclooxygenase-2 can lead to increased swelling and fever.
Researchers are studying drugs that selectively inhibit cyclooxygenase-2 to manage chronic pain.
Some painkillers work by blocking cyclooxygenase-2 and reducing inflammation.
