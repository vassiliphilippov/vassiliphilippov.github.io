Dracunculus medinensis

noun
a parasitic roundworm infecting humans
Pronunciation: / drəˌkʌŋkjələs ˌmɛdɪˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / drəˌkʌŋkjʊləs ˌmɛdɪˈnɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a parasitic roundworm found in India and Africa that infects humans and other vertebrates, living in the abdomen or beneath the skin
