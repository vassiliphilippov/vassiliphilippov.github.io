tutorial

noun
session of tuition with a tutor
Pronunciation: / tuˈtɔriəl, tjuˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / tjuːˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
a period of instruction given by a tutor to an individual or a small group, focusing on intensive learning or discussion

Examples

attend a tutorial session
Each student is expected to attend the weekly tutorial with their assigned tutor.
I found the small-group tutorial much more helpful than the large lecture sessions.
The professor scheduled an extra tutorial before the final exam to help us prepare.
