symmetric

adjective
having balanced, corresponding parts
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
having similarity or correspondence in size, shape, and relative position of parts on opposite sides of a dividing line or plane; exhibiting symmetry

Examples

symmetric pattern
If you fold the paper along the center, the pattern remains symmetric.
The artist prefers symmetric patterns because they appear balanced and pleasing to the eye.
The new building has a symmetric design with identical wings on both sides.

Related Terms

  • antonym – asymmetric: lacking symmetry or balance between parts
  • synonym – symmetrical: having or exhibiting symmetry; alternative form
