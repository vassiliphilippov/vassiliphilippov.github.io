jag

noun
sharp projection on an edge or surface
Pronunciation: / dʒæɡ / (AmE), / dʒæɡ / (BrE)
a sharp projection or point on an edge or surface, often resulting from a break or irregular cut

Examples

jag on metal
Be careful of the jag on that piece of metal; it could cut you.
He gripped a jag of stone to steady himself while climbing.
The glass broke, leaving a dangerous jag on the edge of the window.

Related Terms

  • synonym – notch: a V-shaped cut or indentation on an edge or surface
  • synonym – spur: a projecting point or sharp part on an object
