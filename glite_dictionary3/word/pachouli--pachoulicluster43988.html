pachouli

noun
fragrant tropical shrub; oil used in perfumes
Pronunciation: / pəˈtʃoʊli / (AmE), / pəˈtʃəʊli / (BrE)
A small East Indian shrub of the mint family with fragrant leaves, from which a strongly scented oil used in perfumes is obtained.

Examples

pachouli plant leaves
Many perfumes contain pachouli oil to add a rich, earthy scent.
She bought a bottle of pachouli essential oil at the herbal shop.
The pachouli plant is prized for its aromatic leaves used in traditional incense.
