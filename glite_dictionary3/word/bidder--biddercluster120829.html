bidder

noun
person making a bid (cards)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪdər / (AmE), / ˈbɪdə / (BrE)
A person who makes a bid, especially one who announces a bid during a card game.

Examples

bidder in bridge
During the bridge tournament, each bidder carefully considered their opening move.
In this round, the bidder called for hearts as the trump suit.
Sometimes the most experienced bidder will bluff to mislead the other players.

Related Terms

  • antonym – passer: someone who passes, especially in card games
  • synonym – player: someone who participates in a game
