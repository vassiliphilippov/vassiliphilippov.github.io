orientalism

noun
Asian-style customs or characteristics
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːriˈɛntəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɔːrɪˈɛntəlɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
characteristics, customs, or mannerisms regarded as typical of Asian civilizations, especially as represented in Western art, literature, or scholarship

Examples

orientalism in Western art
Critics have debated whether her novel's depiction of Asian cities amounts to engaging in orientalism.
Many 19th-century paintings are filled with orientalism in both style and subject matter.
The composer incorporated several orientalisms in the opera's music and staging.
