genteelness

noun
elegance and refinement of manner
Pronunciation: / dʒɛnˈtiːlnɪs / (AmE), / dʒɛnˈtiːlnɪs / (BrE)
Elegance and refinement of manner, speech, or appearance; the quality of being genteel.

Examples

air of genteelness
Despite his humble origins, he carried himself with remarkable genteelness throughout his career.
His genteelness was evident in every gesture and word he spoke at the dinner table.
She admired the quiet genteelness of her new neighbors, who always behaved with grace.
