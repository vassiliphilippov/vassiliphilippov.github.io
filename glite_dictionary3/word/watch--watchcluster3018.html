watch

noun
period of devotional wakefulness
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ / (AmE), / wɒtʃ / (BrE)
a period of staying awake, especially on the eve of a religious festival, for devotional purposes

Examples

prayer watch night
During the prayer watch, hymns were sung and candles lit as an act of devotion.
He participated in the annual Easter watch, staying awake in reflection and prayer.
The community held a watch in the church on Christmas Eve, praying until midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – vigil: a period of keeping awake during the time usually spent asleep, especially to keep watch or pray
