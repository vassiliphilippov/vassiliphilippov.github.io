to fold

verb
to cease operating (a business)
Pronunciation: / foʊld / (AmE), / fəʊld / (BrE)
(of a business, organization, etc.) to stop operating, typically because of financial failure.

Examples

company folds
After years of financial struggle, the company was forced to fold last spring.
If ticket sales don't improve, the festival might fold by the end of the year.
Several small bookstores have had to fold because of competition from online retailers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – close: to stop operating permanently (business, shop, etc.)
  • synonym – shut down: to cease business operations or stop working (especially machinery or business)
