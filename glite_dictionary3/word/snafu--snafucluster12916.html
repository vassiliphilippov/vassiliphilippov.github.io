snafu

adjective
in a state of confusion or chaos
Pronunciation: / snæˈfu / (AmE), / snæˈfuː / (BrE)
in a state of complete confusion or chaos, often as a result of an error or mismanagement

Examples

snafu situation
Our travel plans became a snafu after the airline canceled our flights without warning.
The project turned into a snafu when critical files were accidentally deleted.
Yesterday's meeting was a complete snafu, with overlapping agendas and technical problems.

  • synonym – mess: a state of disorder or confusion
