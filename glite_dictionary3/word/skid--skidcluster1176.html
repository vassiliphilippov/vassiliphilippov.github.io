to skid

verb
raise onto skids for support or transport
Pronunciation: / skɪd / (AmE), / skɪd / (BrE)
to lift or raise an object onto skids in order to support or move it more easily

Examples

to skid a container
The engineers used a crane to skid the container onto the wooden supports.
We had to skid the heavy machinery before it could be loaded onto the truck.
Workers skidded the large crate with metal rollers for easy transportation.
