maranta

noun
tropical plant genus with starchy roots
Pronunciation: / məˈræntə / (AmE), / məˈrɑːntə / (BrE)
Any of several tropical plants of the genus Maranta, known for their tuberous, starchy roots and large leaves, some of which are cultivated for arrowroot.

Examples

maranta plant leaves
Botanists collect different species of maranta for study and cultivation.
Native communities have traditionally used maranta as a food source and for medicinal purposes.
The roots of maranta are often processed to make arrowroot flour.
