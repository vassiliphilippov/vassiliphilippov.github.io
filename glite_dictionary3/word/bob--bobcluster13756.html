to bob

verb
remove or shorten an animal's tail
Pronunciation: / bɑb / (AmE), / bɒb / (BrE)
to remove or shorten the tail of an animal, especially as a practice with certain breeds

Examples

to bob a dog's tail
It is now illegal in many countries to bob a dog's tail without medical reasons.
The farmer decided to bob the tails of the young lambs for easier hygiene.
They traditionally bob the horses' tails for aesthetic purposes at the show.

  • synonym – dock: to cut short a part of an animal's tail
