three-mover

noun
chess problem solved in three moves
Pronunciation: / ˈθriˌmuːvər / (AmE), / ˈθriːˌmuːvə / (BrE)
A chess problem in which White is required to checkmate Black in no more than three moves, regardless of how Black defends.
