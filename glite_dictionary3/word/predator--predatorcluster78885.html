predator

noun
animal that hunts and eats others
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛd.ə.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈprɛd.ə.tə / (BrE)
an animal that lives by hunting and eating other animals

Examples

predator hunts prey
Lions are well-known predators that hunt in groups to catch their food.
Many birds of prey are skilled predators that rely on sharp eyesight and powerful talons.
The disappearance of top predators can upset the balance of an ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • antonym – prey: an animal hunted and eaten by another
