slashed

adjective
sharply reduced (of prices/rates)
Pronunciation: / slæʃt / (AmE), / slɑːʃt / (BrE)
(of rates or prices) reduced sharply, especially by a large amount.

Examples

slashed prices
Many airlines offered slashed fares to boost passenger numbers during the off-season.
Thanks to the slashed rent, she was finally able to move into an apartment in the city.
The store advertised slashed prices throughout the entire holiday weekend.
