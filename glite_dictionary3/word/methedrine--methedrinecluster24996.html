Methedrine

noun
trade name for methamphetamine (stimulant drug)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛθəˌdriːn / (AmE), / ˈmɛθɪdriːn / (BrE)
A trade name for methamphetamine, a drug used as a stimulant to the nervous system and as an appetite suppressant, usually in the form of its crystalline hydrochloride.
