response

noun
device's reaction to an input signal
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
the manner in which an electrical or mechanical device reacts to an input signal or a range of input signals

Examples

response to a signal
A rapid response to input signals is essential for high-fidelity audio equipment.
Engineers tested the response of the sensor to subtle voltage changes.
The speaker's response to low frequencies is impressive.
