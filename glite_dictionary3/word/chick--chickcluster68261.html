chick

noun
young bird, especially a baby chicken
Pronunciation: / tʃɪk / (AmE), / tʃɪk / (BrE)
A young bird, especially a newly hatched domestic chicken.

Examples

baby chick

We watched a chick hatch from its egg at the farm.
