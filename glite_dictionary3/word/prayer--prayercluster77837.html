prayer

noun
set text recited in prayer
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈpreə / (BrE)
a set or established text recited as part of a religious service or when praying

Examples

a prayer text
Each religion has its own unique prayer used in ceremonies.
He memorized a special prayer for his grandmother’s health.
The congregation recited the prayer together every Sunday morning.
