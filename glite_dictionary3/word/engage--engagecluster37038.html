to engage

verb
interact with users or content online
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
to interact with online content or users on digital platforms (for example by liking, commenting, sharing, or messaging), often to attract attention or provoke a response.

Examples

to engage with posts
The brand aims to engage users on Instagram by replying to comments and sharing stories.
She spends an hour each morning engaging with followers, answering messages and reposting fan art.
During the campaign, their posts engaged thousands of users who liked and shared the videos.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interact: to communicate or have an effect on each other; to take part in mutual actions
  • synonym – involve: to include or cause someone to take part in an activity or situation
