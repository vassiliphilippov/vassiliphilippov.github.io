subtropic

adjective
relating to regions near the tropics
Pronunciation: / səbˈtrɑːpɪk / (AmE), / sʌbˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the regions just north or south of the tropics, especially with warm or mild climates
