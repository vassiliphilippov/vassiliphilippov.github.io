green

noun
the color resembling grass
Pronunciation: / ɡrin / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
a color between blue and yellow on the spectrum, resembling the color of fresh grass

Examples

bright green color
She mixed blue and yellow paint to create the perfect green for her landscape.
The walls were painted a soft shade of green that brightened the whole room.
Traffic lights turn green to signal that cars may go.
