ropeway

noun
cable transport system with suspended carriers
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊpˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊp.weɪ / (BrE)
a system for transporting passengers or goods in carriers suspended from cables and supported by towers, typically over difficult terrain
