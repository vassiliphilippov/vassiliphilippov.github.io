magpie

noun
person who collects discarded items
Pronunciation: / ˈmæɡˌpaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmæɡ.paɪ / (BrE)
a person who habitually collects things, especially things that have been discarded by others

Examples

collecting magpie things
As a child, Sam was a little magpie, collecting marbles, bottle caps, and any shiny object he found.
He has a magpie's eye for finding treasures in other people's trash.
My aunt is a true magpie, always picking up old furniture from the curb.

Related Terms

  • synonym – collector: a person who collects things as a hobby or occupation
