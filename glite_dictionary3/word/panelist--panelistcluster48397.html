panelist

noun
member of a panel
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn.ə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈpæn.əl.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who is a member of a panel, especially one who takes part in a discussion or debate before an audience

As a panelist, she answered questions from both the moderator and the audience.
