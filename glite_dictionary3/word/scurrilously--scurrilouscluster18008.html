scurrilously

adverb
in an abusive or slanderous manner
Pronunciation: / ˈskɝː.ə.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈskɜː.rɪ.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is grossly or obscenely abusive or slanderous.

Examples

scurrilously attack a person
She scurrilously accused her rival of cheating during the debate.
That website scurrilously spread false rumors about the celebrity.
The politician was scurrilously attacked in the tabloid press for his personal life.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scurrilous: making or spreading scandalous claims about someone with the intention of damaging their reputation
