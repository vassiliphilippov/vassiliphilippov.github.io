dolman

noun
a woman's cloak with wide sleeves
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊlmən / (AmE), / ˈdɒlmən / (BrE)
a woman's loose cloak with wide, cape-like sleeves that are set into the body of the garment without a traditional armhole seam.

Examples

loose dolman coat
She draped her dolman over her shoulders before stepping outside.
The antique shop displayed a beautifully embroidered dolman from the Victorian era.
The fashion historian gave a presentation on the evolution of the dolman in women's outerwear.
