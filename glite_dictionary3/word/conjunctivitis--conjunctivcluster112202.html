conjunctivitis

noun
inflammation of the eye's conjunctiva
Pronunciation: / kənˌdʒʌŋk.təˈvaɪ.dəs / (AmE), / kənˌdʒʌŋk.tɪˈvaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the conjunctiva, the mucous membrane that covers the front of the eye and lines the inside of the eyelids, often causing redness and discomfort.

Examples

conjunctivitis symptoms appear
If your eyes are red and itchy, you may have conjunctivitis and should see a doctor.
Many students missed class due to an outbreak of conjunctivitis at the school.
Washing your hands frequently can help prevent the spread of conjunctivitis.
