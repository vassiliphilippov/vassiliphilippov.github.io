tremor

noun
involuntary shaking or vibration
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛmər /; / ˈtrɛmɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛmə / (BrE)
an involuntary shaking or quivering movement, such as in the body or voice, often caused by illness, fear, or nervousness

  • synonym – shiver: a slight shaking movement caused by cold, fear, or excitement
