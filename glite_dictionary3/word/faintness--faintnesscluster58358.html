faintness

noun
lack of courage or boldness
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪntnəs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪntnəs / (BrE)
lack of boldness or courage; timidity or weakness in spirit or purpose

Examples

faintness of heart
Her faintness of heart made it difficult for her to face challenges head-on.
In moments requiring leadership, faintness is often more damaging than outright failure.
The faintness of his resolve was clear when he backed out at the last minute.

Related Terms

  • antonym – courage: the ability to do something that frightens one
  • synonym – timidity: lack of courage or confidence
