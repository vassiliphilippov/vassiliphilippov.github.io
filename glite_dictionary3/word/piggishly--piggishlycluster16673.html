piggishly

adverb
in a pig-like or greedy manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner resembling a pig, especially greedily or gluttonously.

Examples

eat piggishly fast
He piggishly devoured the entire pizza before anyone else could have a slice.
She reached piggishly for more dessert, ignoring the looks from her friends.
The children grabbed the candies piggishly, not wanting to share.
