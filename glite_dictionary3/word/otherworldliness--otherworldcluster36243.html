otherworldliness

noun
quality of spiritual or mystical focus
Pronunciation: / ˌʌðərˈwɜrldli.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌðəˈwɜːldlinəs / (BrE)

Examples

The painting's otherworldliness lent the gallery an air of mystery and serenity.
