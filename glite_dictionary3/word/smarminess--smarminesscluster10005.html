smarminess

noun
insincere or smug earnestness
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑrmɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːmɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being excessively flattering, insincerely earnest, or smug in a way that is intended to impress or gain favor

Examples

fake smarminess in speech
He spoke to the audience with such smarminess that no one trusted his words.
Her smarminess made her coworkers feel uncomfortable during meetings.
The politician's speech was filled with smarminess and empty compliments.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sincerity: the quality of being honest and genuine
  • synonym – smarmy: excessively flattering or insincerely earnest
