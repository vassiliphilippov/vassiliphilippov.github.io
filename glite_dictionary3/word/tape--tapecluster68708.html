tape

noun
the finish line in a race
Pronunciation: / teɪp / (AmE), / teɪp / (BrE)
a strip, usually made of cloth or plastic, stretched across the finish line of a race to mark the end point; the finishing line in a foot race

Examples

finish line tape
Excitement filled the crowd as the athletes neared the tape at the end of the race.
He crossed the tape in record time, setting a new national best.
The runner surged forward and broke the tape just ahead of her rival.
