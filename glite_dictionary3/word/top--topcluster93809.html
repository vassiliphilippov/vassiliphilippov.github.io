top

noun
platform on a ship's mast
Pronunciation: / tɑp / (AmE), / tɒp / (BrE)
A platform surrounding the head of a lower mast on a sailing ship, providing a place for lookout or working with rigging.

Examples

ship's mast top
An enemy was spotted from the top of the mainmast.
During rough seas, standing watch in the top could be dangerous.
The sailor climbed up to the top to adjust the rigging.
