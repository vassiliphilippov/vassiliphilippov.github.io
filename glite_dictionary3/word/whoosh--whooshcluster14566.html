whoosh

noun
rushing sound of air or liquid
Pronunciation: / wuʃ / (AmE), / wuːʃ / (BrE)
a rushing sound made by a sudden movement of air, liquid, or another fluid

Examples

a loud whoosh sound
I heard a whoosh as the rocket shot into the sky.
The loud whoosh of the passing train startled the children on the platform.
There was a sudden whoosh when the air conditioning turned on.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – hiss: a sharp sibilant sound
  • synonym – swish: a soft rushing or swirling sound
