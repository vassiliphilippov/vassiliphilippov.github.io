tomboyish

adjective
like a tomboy; boisterous girl
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑm.bɔɪ.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtɒm.bɔɪ.ɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a girl) behaving in a way that is considered typical of boys, especially in being energetic, boisterous, or fond of physically active games.

Examples

tomboyish behavior at school
Dressed in jeans and a cap, she had a distinctly tomboyish appearance.
Her tomboyish ways made her popular with all the neighborhood kids.
She grew out of her tomboyish phase but still loves outdoor adventures.

  • antonym – girlish: characteristic of a girl; feminine
