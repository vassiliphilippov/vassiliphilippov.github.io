quintet

noun
group of five, esp. musicians
Pronunciation: / kwɪnˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɪnˈtɛt / (BrE)
a group of five musicians or singers who perform together

Examples

a quintet of musicians

Their string quintet won first prize in the competition.
Our school's music department formed a vocal quintet for the competition.
The jazz quintet played an impressive set at the concert hall last night.
The string quintet features two violins, two violas, and a cello.
