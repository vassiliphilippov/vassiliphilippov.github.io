imperiously

adverb
in a domineering or commanding manner
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəsli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəsli / (BrE)
In a commanding, domineering, or overbearing manner that shows a sense of superiority or authority.
