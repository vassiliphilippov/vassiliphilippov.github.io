disembodied

adjective
lacking a physical body
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɛmˈbɑːdid / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪmˈbɒdid / (BrE)
(of a spirit, voice, or idea) not having or appearing to be separated from a physical body.

Examples

disembodied voice
He felt a disembodied presence in the room, as if someone invisible was watching him.
She wrote a story about disembodied spirits wandering the ruins at midnight.
The disembodied voice echoed through the empty hallway, making everyone uneasy.
