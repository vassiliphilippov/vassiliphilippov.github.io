to powwow

verb
have a meeting or discussion
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊˌwaʊ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊˌwaʊ / (BrE)
to hold a conference or discussion, especially an informal meeting or consultation

Examples

to powwow about plans
Let’s powwow tomorrow to finalize the details.
The managers powwowed in the break room after the announcement.
We powwowed all afternoon but didn’t reach a decision.
