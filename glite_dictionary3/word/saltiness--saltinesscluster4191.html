saltiness

noun
the quality of being salty
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔl.ti.nəs /; / ˈsɔlti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːl.ti.nəs /; / ˈsɔːlti.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or degree of tasting salty; the presence of a salty flavor

Examples

saltiness of food
She complained about the saltiness of the potato chips.
Some cheeses are prized for their distinctive saltiness.
The saltiness of the soup overwhelmed the delicate flavors of the vegetables.
I love the saltiness of olives, especially in salads.
The saltiness of the chips made me quite thirsty.
The saltiness of the soup was overpowering, so I had to add more water.
