lip

noun
fleshy edge of the mouth
Pronunciation: / lɪp / (AmE), / lɪp / (BrE)
either of the two soft, fleshy edges that form the opening of the mouth in humans and many animals

Examples

chapped lip
He cut his lip while eating a sharp piece of bread.
She pressed her lip together to stop herself from laughing.
The child pouted, sticking out his bottom lip in disappointment.
