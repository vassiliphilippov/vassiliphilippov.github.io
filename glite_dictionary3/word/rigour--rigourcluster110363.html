rigour

noun
strictness or thoroughness
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡə / (BrE)
the strictness or thoroughness in attention to detail, standards, or accuracy

Examples

scientific rigour
His research was praised for its scientific rigour and meticulous methodology.
She questioned the rigour with which the experiment was conducted.
The rigour of the school's admission process ensures only dedicated students are accepted.

Related Terms

  • antonym – leniency: being less strict or showing tolerance
  • synonym – rigor: the strictness or severity (American spelling)
