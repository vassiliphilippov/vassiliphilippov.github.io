thermometrograph

noun
instrument recording temperature over time
Pronunciation: / θərˌmoʊˈmɛtrəˌɡræf / (AmE), / θɜːˌməʊˈmɛtrəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
An instrument that automatically records temperature changes over time on a graph.

Examples

thermometrograph records temperature
A thermometrograph can provide a detailed record of daily temperature variations.
The laboratory used a thermometrograph to continuously monitor temperature fluctuations.
Unlike a standard thermometer, a thermometrograph produces a graph tracking temperatures over extended periods.
