shapelessly

adverb
in a shapeless manner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪplɪsli / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpləsli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks a definite or distinct shape or form.

Examples

hang shapelessly over shoulders
Her hair fell shapelessly around her face after the long walk.
The dough spread shapelessly on the baking sheet before it was baked.
The old sweater hung shapelessly on the back of the chair.
