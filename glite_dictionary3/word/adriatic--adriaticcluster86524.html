Adriatic

noun
sea between Italy and Balkan Peninsula
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.driˈæt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌæd.riˈæt.ɪk / (BrE)
the sea forming an arm of the Mediterranean, lying between Italy on the west and Slovenia, Croatia, Montenegro, and Albania on the east

Examples

the Adriatic Sea
Sailboats are a common sight on the Adriatic during the summer months.
The Adriatic played a key role in trade between Italy and the Balkans.
The coast along the Adriatic is famous for its crystal-clear waters.
