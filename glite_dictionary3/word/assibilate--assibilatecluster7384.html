to assibilate

verb
change into a sibilant sound
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪbəˌleɪt / (AmE), / əˈsɪbɪleɪt / (BrE)
to change a sound, especially a consonant, into a sibilant (a hissing sound like 's' or 'z')

Examples
