Eragrostis abyssinica

noun
African cereal grass used for food and forage
Pronunciation: / ɛrəˌɡrɑstɪs əˌbɪsɪˈnɪkə / (AmE), / ɛrəˌɡrɒstɪs əˌbɪsɪˈnɪkə / (BrE)
an African grass cultivated for its edible seeds, producing white flour of good quality, and also used as forage and hay
