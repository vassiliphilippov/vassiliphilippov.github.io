unpasteurized

adjective
not pasteurized (untreated food/drink)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpæstʃəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɑːstjəraɪzd / (BrE)
(of a food or drink) not having undergone pasteurization, and therefore not treated to kill harmful microorganisms.

Examples

unpasteurized milk
Regulations restrict the sale of unpasteurized milk in many countries due to safety concerns.
Some people prefer unpasteurized apple cider for its natural flavor, despite a shorter shelf life.
The farm specializes in unpasteurized cheeses made from fresh local milk.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pasteurized: (of food or drink) having undergone pasteurization
