Lenin

proper noun
Russian revolutionary leader (1870–1924)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛnɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɛnɪn / (BrE)
(1870–1924) Russian revolutionary leader; founder of the Bolsheviks, architect of the Russian Revolution, and first head of the Soviet Union.
