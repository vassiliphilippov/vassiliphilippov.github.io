organizer

noun
electronic personal data device
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.ɡəˌnaɪ.zɚ / (AmE), / ˈɔː.ɡə.naɪ.zə / (BrE)
a small electronic device, similar to a hand-held computer, used for keeping personal information such as appointments, contacts, and notes

Examples

electronic organizer device
Before smartphones, many people used an electronic organizer for their schedules.
He relies on his organizer to keep track of appointments and phone numbers.
She set an alarm on her organizer to remind her of the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – personal digital assistant: a handheld electronic device that functions as a personal information manager
