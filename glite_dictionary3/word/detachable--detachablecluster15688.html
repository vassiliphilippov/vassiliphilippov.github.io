detachable

adjective
able to be separated without damage
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtætʃ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / dɪˈtætʃ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a part or accessory) designed to be removed or separated from something without causing damage.

Examples

detachable collar
He bought a sofa with detachable cushions, making cleaning much easier.
The backpack has a detachable rain cover for extra weather protection.
The lamp comes with a detachable cord for convenience during travel.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fixed: firmly attached and not intended to be removed
  • synonym – removable: able to be taken off or separated from something
