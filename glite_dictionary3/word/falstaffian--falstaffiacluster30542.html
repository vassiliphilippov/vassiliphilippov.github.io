Falstaffian

adjective
resembling Falstaff (jovial, fat, pleasure-loving)
Pronunciation: / fælˈstæfiən / (AmE), / fɔːlˈstæfiən / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of Falstaff, especially in being fat, jolly, and indulgent in pleasure or good humor.

Examples

Falstaffian humor
He possessed a Falstaffian appetite for food, drink, and good company.
Her Falstaffian humor and rotund figure made her the life of every party.
The mayor's Falstaffian laughter filled the banquet hall, delighting everyone around him.
