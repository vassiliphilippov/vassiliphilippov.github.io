fumigation

noun
disinfection using smoke or gas
Pronunciation: / ˌfjuːmɪˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfjuːmɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of disinfecting or purifying an area or object by exposing it to smoke or gas, usually to eliminate pests or pathogens

Examples

fumigation of a house
After the fumigation, it was safe for the workers to re-enter the building.
Experts recommend fumigation if you suspect termites in your home.
The warehouse underwent fumigation to eliminate any pests before storing the new shipment.

  • synonym – disinfection: removing or destroying harmful microorganisms
