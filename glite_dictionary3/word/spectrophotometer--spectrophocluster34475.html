spectrophotometer

noun
instrument for measuring light intensity across wavelengths
Pronunciation: / ˌspɛktroʊfəˈtɑmətər / (AmE), / ˌspɛktrəʊfəˈtɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure and compare the intensity of light at different wavelengths, especially for analyzing the properties of substances

Examples

spectrophotometer measures light
A spectrophotometer can measure absorbance at multiple wavelengths to identify different compounds.
Before running any tests, ensure the spectrophotometer is properly calibrated for accurate results.
The laboratory technician used a spectrophotometer to determine the protein concentration in the sample.
