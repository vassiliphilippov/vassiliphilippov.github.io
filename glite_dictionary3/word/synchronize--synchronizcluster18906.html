to synchronize

verb
make film sound match the action
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋ.krəˌnaɪz /; / ˈsɪŋkrəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.krə.naɪz /; / ˈsɪŋkrənaɪz / (BrE)
to make two or more things happen at the same time or in a coordinated way
