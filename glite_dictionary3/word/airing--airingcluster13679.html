airing

noun
a short outdoor walk or ride
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeərɪŋ / (BrE)
a short walk or ride taken outdoors for fresh air or exercise, often for one's health

Examples

take an airing outside

He took the dogs for an airing before dinner.
We went for an airing along the seafront to enjoy the breeze.
