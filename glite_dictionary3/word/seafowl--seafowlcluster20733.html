seafowl

noun
a seabird or marine bird
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌfaʊl / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌfaʊl / (BrE)
a bird that frequents coastal waters and the open ocean, such as gulls, pelicans, or albatrosses

Examples

wild seafowl
Fishermen often watch seafowl to locate schools of fish.
Several species of seafowl can be seen gliding over the waves at dawn.
The cliffs were alive with nesting seafowl each spring.
