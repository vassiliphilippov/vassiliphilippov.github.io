perusal

noun
careful reading or examination
Pronunciation: / pəˈɹuzəl / (AmE), / pəˈɹuːzəl / (BrE)
the act of reading or examining something carefully and thoroughly, especially to understand or remember it

Examples

perusal of a document
After a quick perusal of the menu, she decided to order the special.
He sat down with the document for a thorough perusal before signing.
The contract is complicated and deserves careful perusal by your lawyer.
