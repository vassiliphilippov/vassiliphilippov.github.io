undiscriminating

adjective
lacking discernment or good taste
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈskrɪm.ɪˌneɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈskrɪm.ɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
not having or showing good judgement or taste; failing to recognize differences or distinctions.

Examples

undiscriminating taste in food
He has an undiscriminating palate and enjoys almost any food.
The undiscriminating shopper bought everything on sale, regardless of quality.
Their undiscriminating acceptance of rumors often led them astray.
