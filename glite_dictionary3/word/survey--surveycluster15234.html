survey

noun
detailed inspection or examination
Pronunciation: / sɚˈveɪ / (AmE), / səˈveɪ / (BrE)
a detailed examination or inspection of something, often to assess its condition, quality, or value

Examples

survey of a building
After a detailed survey, the team reported several areas in need of urgent repair.
The engineer conducted a survey of the building's structural integrity before the renovations began.
The historical society ordered a survey of the old manuscripts to assess their condition.
After a brief survey of the audience, she began her lecture.
He made a careful survey of the old castle before entering.
The detective’s survey of the scene revealed several important clues.
