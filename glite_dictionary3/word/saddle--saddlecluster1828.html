saddle

noun
rear part of a fowl's back
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəl / (AmE), / ˈsædəl / (BrE)
the posterior part of the back of a domestic fowl, often referring to the area over the hips and base of the tail

Examples

chicken saddle
The color of the saddle can help identify different breeds of hens.
The rooster's saddle feathers are long and decorative.
When dressing a chicken, remove the saddle for certain recipes.
