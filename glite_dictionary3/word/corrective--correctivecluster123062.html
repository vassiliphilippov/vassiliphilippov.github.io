corrective

noun
device used to correct injury or disease
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛktɪv / (AmE), / kəˈrɛktɪv / (BrE)
a device or appliance used to treat an injury or disease by correcting a physical problem

Examples

corrective device
After the injury, he wore a special corrective on his knee for six months.
She was fitted with a corrective to help straighten her spine.
The dentist recommended a dental corrective for her misaligned teeth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – orthosis: an externally applied device used to modify the structural or functional characteristics of the neuromuscular and skeletal system
