acceptably

adverb
in an acceptable manner
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptəbli / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is good enough to be accepted, though not particularly impressive or outstanding.

Examples

acceptably clear explanation
His presentation was acceptably clear, but it could have been better organized.
She spoke acceptably in French, though she still made some mistakes.
The students performed acceptably on their final exams, meeting the basic requirements.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tolerably: in a manner that is sufficiently good; adequately
  • antonym – unacceptably: in a manner that is not acceptable
