headstone

noun
central stone at arch's top
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.stəʊn / (BrE)
the central stone at the top of an arch or vault, holding the structure together

Examples

headstone of an arch
Masons selected a perfectly shaped headstone for the ancient stone bridge.
The architect carefully placed the headstone to complete the archway.
Without a properly fitted headstone, the arch could collapse under its own weight.
