hygienic

adjective
promoting health by cleanliness
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / hʌɪˈdʒiːnɪk / (BrE)
Promoting or preserving health, especially by maintaining cleanliness and preventing disease.

Examples

hygienic practices
Doctors recommend hygienic practices such as frequent hand-washing and covering your mouth when sneezing.
It's important to keep your toothbrush in a hygienic container to prevent germs from spreading.
The restaurant's kitchen was praised for being exceptionally hygienic and well-maintained.
