Shinto

adjective
relating to the Japanese religion Shintoism
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪn.təʊ / (BrE)
relating to Shintoism, the indigenous religion of Japan that emphasizes ritual practices and a connection with kami (spirits).

Examples

Shinto shrine
A traditional wedding may include both Shinto and Western elements.
Many tourists visit Shinto shrines during the New Year to pray for good fortune.
The Shinto rituals performed at the shrine are centuries old.
