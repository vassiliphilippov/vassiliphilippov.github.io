primary

noun
preliminary election to select candidates
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪˌmɛri / (AmE), / ˈpraɪməri / (BrE)
an election held to select candidates or delegates for a subsequent main election, especially within a political party

Examples

primary election
She won the primary and will now compete in the general election.
The New Hampshire primary often sets the tone for the rest of the presidential race.
Voter turnout in the primary was higher than expected this year.
