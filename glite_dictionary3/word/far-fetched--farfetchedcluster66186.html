far-fetched

adjective
improbable, hard to believe
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑrˈfɛtʃt /; / ˈfɑr ˌfɛtʃt /; / ˈfɑrˌfɛtʃt / (AmE), / ˌfɑːˈfɛtʃt /; / ˌfɑː ˈfɛtʃt /; / ˈfɑːˌfɛtʃt / (BrE)
