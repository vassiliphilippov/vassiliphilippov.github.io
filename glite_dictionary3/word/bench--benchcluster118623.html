bench

noun
long seat for several people
Pronunciation: / bɛntʃ / (AmE), / bɛntʃ / (BrE)
A long, often hard seat for several people, typically made of wood or metal.

Examples

park bench
She placed her bag next to her on the stone bench outside the museum.
The players waited on the sidelines, sitting quietly on the bench.
We sat on the old wooden bench in the park and watched the sunset.
