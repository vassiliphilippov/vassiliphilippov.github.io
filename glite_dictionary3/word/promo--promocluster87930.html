promo

noun
advertisement or promotional message
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.məʊ / (BrE)
An advertisement or promotional message for a product, event, or cause, often broadcast or distributed for publicity.

Examples

promo for a show
I saw a promo for the new season of the show last night.
The radio station is running a promo for the concert next week.
They released a short promo to generate excitement for the upcoming event.

Related Terms

  • synonym – advertisement: a notice or announcement promoting a product, service, or event
  • synonym – trailer: a short promotional film for a movie
