defence

noun
sports team preventing opponent scoring
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
(in sports) the part of a team, or the team as a whole, that works to prevent the opposing side from scoring points or goals.

Examples

strong defence line
Our defence played exceptionally well in the second half of the match.
The coach praised the team's defence for holding off a strong attack.
This year, their defence has allowed fewer goals than any other team in the league.

Related Terms

  • antonym – offense: (in sports) the part of a team or the team as a whole that tries to score points or goals
