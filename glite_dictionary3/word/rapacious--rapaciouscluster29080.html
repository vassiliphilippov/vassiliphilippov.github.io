rapacious

adjective
predatory animal; catching live prey
Pronunciation: / rəˈpeɪʃəs / (AmE), / rəˈpeɪʃəs / (BrE)
(of animals) living by catching and eating other animals; predatory.

Examples

rapacious birds
Many rapacious species have adaptations that make them effective hunters.
The rapacious hawk circled above the field, searching for its next meal.
Wolves are known for their rapacious hunting instincts in the wild.
