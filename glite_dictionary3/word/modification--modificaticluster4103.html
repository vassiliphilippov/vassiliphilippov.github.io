modification

noun
the act of making changes or adjustments
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of changing something, especially slightly, to improve it or make it more suitable
