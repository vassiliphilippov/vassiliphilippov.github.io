to commend

verb
present as worthy or recommend
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɛnd / (AmE), / kəˈmɛnd / (BrE)
to present or recommend someone or something as worthy of attention, kindness, or confidence

Examples

to commend a friend for help
Her honesty and hard work commend her to everyone she meets.
I would like to commend this book to anyone interested in modern history.
The coach commended the team's effort after their hard-fought victory.

Related Terms

  • antonym – criticize: to express disapproval by pointing out faults
  • synonym – recommend: to suggest someone or something as suitable or worthy
