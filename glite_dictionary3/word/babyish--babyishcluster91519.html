babyish

adjective
resembling or characteristic of a baby
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi.ɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a baby, especially in being immature or childish.

Examples

babyish behavior
He spoke in a babyish voice to try to get sympathy.
Her babyish giggle made everyone in the room smile.
It's a bit babyish to throw a tantrum just because you didn't get your way.

Related Terms

  • synonym – childish: silly or immature, like a child
  • antonym – mature: showing the mental and emotional qualities of an adult
