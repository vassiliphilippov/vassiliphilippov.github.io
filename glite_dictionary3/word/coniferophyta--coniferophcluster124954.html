Coniferophyta

noun
a division of cone-bearing gymnosperms
Pronunciation: / kəˌnɪfəˈroʊfətə / (AmE), / kəˌnɪfəˈrɒfɪtə / (BrE)
a division of cone-bearing gymnosperm plants that includes the conifers, such as pines, spruces, and yews, as well as extinct related groups
