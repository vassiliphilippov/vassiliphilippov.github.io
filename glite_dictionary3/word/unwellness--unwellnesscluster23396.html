unwellness

noun
state of being unwell; illness
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛlnɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛlnɪs / (BrE)
A state of being unwell; impairment of normal physical health or function.

Examples

chronic unwellness
Chronic unwellness can significantly affect a person's quality of life.
He took the day off work due to a sudden unwellness that left him fatigued.
The doctor tried to pinpoint the source of her persistent unwellness.

  • synonym – illness: a disease or period of sickness affecting the body or mind
