projection

noun
representation of a figure on a plane
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the depiction of a figure or solid object on a plane as it would appear from a particular viewpoint

Examples

orthographic projection drawing
Engineers often use an orthographic projection to illustrate all sides of a machine part.
The architect created a projection of the building's shadow on the ground.
The map is a projection of the globe onto a flat surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym – elevation: a geometrical projection on a vertical plane
  • synonym – plan: a drawing showing the top view of an object or building
