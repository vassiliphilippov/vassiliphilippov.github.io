unhesitatingly

adverb
without hesitation or doubt
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛzəˌteɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛzɪteɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no hesitation or doubt; without pausing or showing uncertainty.

Examples

to answer unhesitatingly
He unhesitatingly agreed to help with the project despite his busy schedule.
She unhesitatingly accepted the job offer when it was made to her.
The firefighter unhesitatingly ran into the burning building to save the children.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hesitantly: in a hesitant or unsure manner
