nitrazepam

noun
a benzodiazepine sedative drug
Pronunciation: / nɪˈtræzəˌpæm / (AmE), / nɪˈtrazɪˌpam / (BrE)
a hypnotic and sedative drug of the benzodiazepine class, used for the short-term treatment of insomnia and certain seizures
