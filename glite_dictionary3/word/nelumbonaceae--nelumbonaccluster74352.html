Nelumbonaceae

noun
family of aquatic flowering plants (lotus)
Pronunciation: / nɪˌlʌmboʊˈneɪsiː / (AmE), / nɪˌlʌmbəʊˈneɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of aquatic flowering plants, commonly known as the lotus family, which in some classifications is considered separate from water lilies and contains the genus Nelumbo.

Botanists often distinguish Nelumbonaceae from other water lily families based on genetic differences.
