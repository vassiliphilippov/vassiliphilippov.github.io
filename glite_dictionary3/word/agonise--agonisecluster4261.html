to agonise

verb
suffer extreme pain or anguish
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈæɡənaɪz / (BrE)
to suffer extreme physical or mental pain or anguish

Examples

to agonise over a decision
He continued to agonise over the decision long after everyone else had moved on.
She agonised for hours before finally accepting the offer.
The patient was left to agonise in pain due to the delayed treatment.

  • synonym – suffer: to experience or feel pain or distress
  • synonym – writhe: to twist or squirm in pain or discomfort
