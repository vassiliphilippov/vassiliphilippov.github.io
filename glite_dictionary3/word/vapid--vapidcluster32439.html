vapid

adjective
lacking in taste or flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈvæpɪd / (AmE), / ˈvapɪd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) lacking taste or flavor; insipid or bland.

Examples

vapid drink
After boiling for too long, the soup became vapid and unappetizing.
I prefer strong coffee to the vapid tea they served at the meeting.
The vapid beer left a flat and disappointing impression on my palate.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bland: mild or lacking strong flavor
  • antonym – flavorful: full of flavor; tasty
  • synonym – insipid: lacking flavor; dull or uninteresting (of food or drink)
