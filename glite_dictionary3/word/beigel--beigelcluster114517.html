beigel

noun
Jewish glazed bread roll with hard crust
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪɡəl / (BrE)
A glazed, yeast-raised, doughnut-shaped bread roll with a hard crust, traditionally made in Jewish communities.
