to sterilise

verb
make infertile, especially by medical means
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛr.ə.laɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɛr.ɪ.laɪz / (BrE)
to make a person or animal unable to produce offspring, typically through a medical procedure

Examples

to sterilise a pet
She chose to sterilise her cat to prevent unwanted litters.
Some governments once tried to sterilise people against their will for eugenic reasons.
The clinic will sterilise the animals before offering them for adoption.
