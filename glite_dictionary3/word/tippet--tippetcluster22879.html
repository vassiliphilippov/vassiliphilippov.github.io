tippet

noun
fur shoulder cape with hanging ends
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪpɪt / (BrE)
a woman's long scarf or shoulder cape, especially one made of fur with hanging ends, sometimes using the whole pelt of an animal such as a fox or marten

Examples

tippet of fur
In old photographs, women are often seen wearing a fox tippet as part of their attire.
She draped her tippet over her shoulders before leaving the house.
The vintage shop had a rare mink tippet on display.
