improvisation

noun
spontaneous creation or performance
Pronunciation: / ˌɪm.prə.vəˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɪmprəvəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˌprɒv.ɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ɪmˌprɒvɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or art of creating or performing something spontaneously, without prior preparation, for example in music, speech, or writing
