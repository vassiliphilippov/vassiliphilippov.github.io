elevation

noun
height above sea level or reference point
Pronunciation: / ˌɛləˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the height of a place or object above sea level or the surrounding area; a raised area of land

Examples

high elevation
