incoherence

noun
lack of clarity or organization
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkoʊˈhɪrəns /; / ˌɪn.koʊˈhɪr.əns / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərəns /; / ˌɪn.kəʊˈhɪə.rəns / (BrE)
the quality of being unclear, disorganized, or lacking logical connection, especially in speech or writing
