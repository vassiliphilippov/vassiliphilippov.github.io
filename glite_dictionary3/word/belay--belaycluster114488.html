belay

noun
anchor point for a climber's rope
Pronunciation: / bəˈleɪ / (AmE), / bɪˈleɪ / (BrE)
a point or object to which a climber's rope is securely fastened during an ascent, providing safety support

Examples

belay anchor point
Before climbing further, she searched for a solid belay to secure her rope.
The guide checked each belay carefully for stability and safety.
We set up a belay on the ledge before attempting the next pitch.
