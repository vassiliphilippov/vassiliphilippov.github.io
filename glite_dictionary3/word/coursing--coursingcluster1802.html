coursing

noun
hunting game with sight-chasing dogs
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːsɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of hunting game animals, such as hares, with dogs that chase by sight rather than scent, especially using greyhounds

Examples

coursing with greyhounds
Greyhounds are often trained specifically for coursing competitions.
Many animal rights groups oppose coursing because of concerns for the welfare of the prey.
The tradition of coursing dates back several centuries in rural England.
