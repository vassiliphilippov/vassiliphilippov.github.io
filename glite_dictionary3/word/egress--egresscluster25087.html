to egress

verb
to go out or emerge
Pronunciation: / ˈiːˌɡrɛs / (AmE), / ˈiːɡrɛs / (BrE)
(formal) To go out or emerge from a place.

Examples

to egress a building
In case of emergency, all personnel should egress through the rear doors.
The crew will egress the spacecraft after it lands.
The firefighters managed to egress from the burning building safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym – emerge: to come into view, especially from concealment
  • synonym – exit: to go out or leave a place
