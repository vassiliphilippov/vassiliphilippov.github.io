to particularise

verb
describe in detail; be specific
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələˌraɪz / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪz / (BrE)
to state or describe something in detail; to be specific about something

Examples

to particularise details
In your application, please particularise any relevant work experience you have.
She asked the witness to particularise the events leading up to the incident.
The report does not particularise how the funds were allocated.

Related Terms

  • antonym – generalise: to make something more general and less specific
  • synonym – specify: to state something clearly and exactly
