doula

noun
a childbirth support person
Pronunciation: / ˈduːlə / (AmE), / ˈduːlə / (BrE)
A person, typically trained, who provides emotional and practical support to a woman during childbirth.

Examples

doula during childbirth
After training, she began working as a doula at a local hospital.
Many couples hire a doula to help them navigate the birth experience.
The doula provided encouragement and comfort throughout her labor.
