lovelorn

adjective
suffering from unrequited love
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvˌlɔrn / (AmE), / ˈlʌvˌlɔːn / (BrE)
Unhappy or sorrowful because of unrequited or lost love.

Examples

lovelorn teenager
He wandered the city like a lovelorn soul, haunted by memories of his lost romance.
She wrote poems late into the night, feeling lovelorn after their painful breakup.
The lovelorn character gazed out the window, hoping for her beloved to return.
