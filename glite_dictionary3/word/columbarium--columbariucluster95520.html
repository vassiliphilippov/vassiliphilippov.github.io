columbarium

noun
pigeon house or niche for urns
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːləmˈbɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌkɒləmˈbeəriəm / (BrE)
a structure with niches or compartments for the nesting of pigeons or doves, or for holding funeral urns
