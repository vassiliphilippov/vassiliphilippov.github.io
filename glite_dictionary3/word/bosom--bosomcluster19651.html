to bosom

verb
hold someone tightly in your arms
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊzəm / (AmE), / ˈbʊzəm / (BrE)
to hold someone tightly in your arms, especially as an expression of affection, comfort, or protection

She bosomed the frightened child, whispering soothing words to calm him.
