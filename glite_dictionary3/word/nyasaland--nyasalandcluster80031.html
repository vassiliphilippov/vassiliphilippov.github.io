Nyasaland

proper noun
former name of Malawi, African country
Pronunciation: / ˈnjɑːsəˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈnjɑːsəˌlænd / (BrE)
the former name of Malawi, a landlocked country in southern central Africa, which achieved independence from the United Kingdom in 1964
