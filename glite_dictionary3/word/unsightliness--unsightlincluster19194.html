unsightliness

noun
the quality of being ugly or unattractive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsaɪtlinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaɪtlinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unpleasant or unattractive in appearance; ugliness

Examples

urban unsightliness
Neighbors complained about the unsightliness caused by uncollected rubbish on the streets.
She tried to cover the unsightliness of the old scar with makeup.
The unsightliness of the abandoned building discouraged investors from buying the property.
