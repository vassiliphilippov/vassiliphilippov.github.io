Rama

proper noun
Hindu god, avatar of Vishnu
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːmə / (BrE)
in Hinduism, an incarnation (avatar) of the god Vishnu, known for his ideal qualities and revered as a deity; especially the hero of the epic Ramayana.

Examples

Rama in the Ramayana
Many Hindus regard Rama as the supreme example of virtue and duty.
The festival of Diwali is celebrated to honor Rama's return to Ayodhya after defeating Ravana.
The story of Rama and Sita forms the core of the epic Ramayana.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Ramayana: the ancient Indian epic centered on Rama
  • synonym – Vishnu: a major deity in Hinduism regarded as the preserver god
