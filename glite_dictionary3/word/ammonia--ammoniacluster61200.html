ammonia

noun
aqueous solution of ammonia gas
Pronunciation: / əˈmoʊniə / (AmE), / əˈməʊniə / (BrE)
A solution of ammonia gas in water, commonly used as a cleaning agent and in industry.

Examples

ammonia cleaning solution
Be careful when using ammonia as a cleaning product, as its fumes can be harmful.
Never combine ammonia with bleach, as the reaction produces dangerous gases.
The janitor mixed water with ammonia to clean the floor tiles more effectively.
