Alexandrian

adjective
relating to Alexander the Great or his empire
Pronunciation: / ˌælɪɡˈzændriən / (AmE), / ˌalɪɡˈzændrɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to Alexander the Great, his empire, or the period influenced by his rule.

Examples

Alexandrian culture
Her research focused on Alexandrian influences in early Mediterranean art.
Many cities were renamed during the Alexandrian period.
The Alexandrian conquest spread Greek culture throughout much of the ancient world.
