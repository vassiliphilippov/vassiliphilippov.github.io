blunderbuss

noun
indiscriminate, scattergun approach
Pronunciation: / \u02c8bl\u028cnd\u025a\u02ccb\u028cs / (AmE), / \u02c8bl\u028cnd\u0259b\u028cs / (BrE)
a person, method, or policy that is clumsy, indiscriminate, or lacking subtlety; a broad, scattergun approach to a problem or task.

Examples

blunderbuss approach
The consultant's blunderbuss strategy tried to fix every problem at once and pleased no one.
Faced with tight deadlines, they used a blunderbuss marketing plan that wasted resources quickly.
His speeches were verbal blunderbusses, hitting many targets but changing nothing in practice.

Related Terms

  • synonym – broad-brush: dealing with something in a general or undifferentiated way rather than precisely
  • synonym – scattergun: an approach that tries many things at once in an indiscriminate way
  • antonym – targeted approach: a precise, focused method that addresses specific issues directly
