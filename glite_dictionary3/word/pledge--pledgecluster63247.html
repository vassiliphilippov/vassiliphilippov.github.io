pledge

noun
item held as security for a debt
Pronunciation: / plɛdʒ / (AmE), / plɛdʒ / (BrE)
an item or deposit given as security for the fulfillment of a debt or obligation

Examples

pledge as security
He left his watch as a pledge for the money he borrowed.
The contract specified that the house would serve as a pledge for the debt.
The pawnshop kept her violin in pledge until she could repay the loan.

Related Terms

  • synonym – collateral: property or assets pledged by a borrower as security for a loan
