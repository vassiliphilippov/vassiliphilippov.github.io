enclosure

noun
protective housing for equipment
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkloʊʒər / (AmE), / ɪnˈkləʊʒə / (BrE)
a protective case, box, or cabinet that contains and shields electronic or electrical components or equipment from damage, dust, or moisture.

Examples

protective enclosure
The server's metal enclosure protects the internal components from dust.
They ordered a waterproof enclosure to keep the outdoor control panel safe during storms.
Technicians fitted additional cooling fans into several server enclosures to prevent overheating.

Related Terms

  • synonym – case: a container or covering used to protect an object, especially technical equipment
  • synonym – housing: a protective outer casing for a device or its parts
