to head

verb
remove the head of (animal/fish)
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
To remove the head of an animal or fish, especially as part of preparing food.

Examples

to head the fish
Before cooking, you should always head the shrimp to remove any unwanted parts.
Please head the salmon before you fillet it.
The chef taught us how to head and gut a fish quickly.
