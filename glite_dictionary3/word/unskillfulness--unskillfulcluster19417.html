unskillfulness

noun
lack of skill; incompetence
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskɪlfəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈskɪlfəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of lacking skill or competence in doing something.

Examples

unskillfulness at work
Because of her unskillfulness, the project missed several important deadlines.
His unskillfulness at repairing bikes led to more problems than solutions.
The chef's unskillfulness was obvious when the meal was burned.

Related Terms

  • synonym – incompetence: the inability to do something successfully
  • antonym – skillfulness: the quality of having skill or competence
