yoke

noun
fitted upper part of a garment
Pronunciation: / joʊk / (AmE), / jəʊk / (BrE)
A fitted piece of fabric forming the upper part of a garment, such as at the shoulders or hips, to which the main parts are attached.

Examples

yoke on shirt
A denim shirt with a double-stitched yoke is both sturdy and stylish.
Sew the sleeves to the yoke before attaching the main body of the blouse.
The dress featured a lace yoke that added an elegant touch to the neckline.
