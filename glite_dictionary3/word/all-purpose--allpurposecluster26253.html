all-purpose

adjective
suitable for many uses
Pronunciation: / ˌɔlˈpɜrpəs / (AmE), / ˌɔːlˈpɜːpəs / (BrE)
suitable for all types of use; not restricted to a particular function or purpose.

Examples

all-purpose cleaner
I bought an all-purpose tool that combines a knife, screwdriver, and scissors.
She prefers wearing all-purpose shoes that are comfortable for both work and casual outings.
This is an all-purpose cleaner suitable for kitchens and bathrooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – multi-purpose: having several different uses
  • antonym – specialized: designed for a particular purpose only
