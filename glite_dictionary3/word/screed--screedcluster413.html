screed

noun
level guide strip for plaster/concrete
Pronunciation: / skrid / (AmE), / skriːd / (BrE)
an accurately leveled strip of material applied to a wall or floor to guide the even application of plaster or concrete

Examples

screed of concrete
A metal screed can significantly improve the finish of a cement floor.
Before plastering the wall, a screed was fixed along its length for accuracy.
The builder placed a screed on the floor to ensure the concrete was level.

  • synonym – guide: something that serves to direct or indicate
