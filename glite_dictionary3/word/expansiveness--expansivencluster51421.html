expansiveness

noun
quality of being grand or expansive
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈspansɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being grand or extensive in scale, or having a tendency to expand or extend

Examples

expansiveness of vision
Her speech was marked by the expansiveness of her vision for the future.
The expansiveness of the desert made them feel small and insignificant.
The expansiveness of the mansion reflected the owner's wealth and ambition.

Related Terms

  • synonym – grandiosity: the quality of being grand or impressive in scale
  • antonym – modesty: the quality of being moderate or understated, opposite of grandeur
