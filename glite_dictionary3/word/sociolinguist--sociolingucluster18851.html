sociolinguist

noun
an expert in language and society
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsi.əʊˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies the relationship between language and society, focusing on how social and cultural factors affect the way language is used
