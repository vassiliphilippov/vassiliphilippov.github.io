silverweed

noun
perennial plant with silvery leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌwid / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəwiːd / (BrE)
A low-growing perennial plant with leaves that are silvery on the underside, found in northern parts of the United States, Europe, and Asia.

Examples

silverweed by the marsh
