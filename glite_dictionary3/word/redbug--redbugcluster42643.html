redbug

noun
larval mite causing skin irritation
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a small larval mite that feeds on the blood of humans and other vertebrates, often causing intense skin irritation

Examples

redbug bite
After hiking in the woods, she discovered several redbugs had bitten her ankles.
Farmers often take precautions to avoid redbug infestations in their fields.
Many campers complain about the itching caused by redbugs during the summer.
