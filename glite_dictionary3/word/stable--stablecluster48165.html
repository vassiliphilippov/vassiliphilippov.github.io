stable

adjective
not likely to change; maintaining equilibrium
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪ.bəl /; / ˈsteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈsteɪ.bəl /; / ˈsteɪbl /; / ˈsteɪbəl / (BrE)
not likely to change, fail, or move; steady, firm, or dependable

Related Terms

  • synonym – static: showing little or no change, especially in a way that is dull or uninteresting
  • synonym – steady: firmly fixed, constant, or unchanging
  • antonym – unstable: likely to change or fail; not firm or fixed
  • antonym – unsteady: not firm, likely to change or move
