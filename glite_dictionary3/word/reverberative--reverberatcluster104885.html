reverberative

adjective
having reverberation or resonance
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrbərˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːbərətɪv / (BrE)
(of a sound or space) characterized by or producing reverberation or resonance.

Examples

reverberative sound effect
He spoke with a reverberative voice that filled the hall.
The reverberative effect of the empty room made every sound seem louder.
The cathedral's reverberative acoustics enhance the choir's singing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resonant: (of sound) deep, clear, and continuing to sound or echo
