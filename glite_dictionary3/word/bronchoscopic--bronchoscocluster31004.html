bronchoscopic

adjective
relating to bronchoscopes or bronchoscopy
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɑŋ.kəˈskoʊ.pɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrɒŋ.kəˈskɒp.ɪk / (BrE)
