eudaimonia

noun
state of happiness or flourishing
Pronunciation: / juːˌdaɪməˈniːə / (AmE), / juːˌdaɪməˈniːə / (BrE)
a state of happiness, well-being, or flourishing, especially in the sense of fulfilling one's potential, as discussed in ancient Greek philosophy

The concept of eudaimonia emphasizes not just happiness, but living virtuously and realizing one's potential.
