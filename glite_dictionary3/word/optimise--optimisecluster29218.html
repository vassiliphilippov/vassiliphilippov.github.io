to optimise

verb
make as effective or useful as possible
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːptəˌmaɪz /; / ˈɑːptɪˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɒptɪmaɪz / (BrE)
to make something as effective or efficient as possible; to improve or adjust something to get the best result

Examples

to optimise a process
She is always looking for ways to optimise her daily routine.
The engine was carefully optimised for maximum efficiency.
We need to optimise our workflow to save time and resources.
She spent months trying to optimise the design for better performance.
The company hired an expert to optimise their supply chain processes.
We need to optimise this website to improve its loading speed.
