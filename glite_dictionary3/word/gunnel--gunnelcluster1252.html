gunnel

noun
upper edge of a boat's side
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnəl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnəl / (BrE)
The upper edge of the side of a boat or ship; the topmost plank or reinforcing band along the hull.

Examples

gunnel of a boat
He secured the oars underneath the gunnel before they started rowing.
The fisherman perched his rod on the gunnel as he waited for a bite.
When scrubbing down the sailboat, make sure to clean along the gunnel as well.
