ventriloquism

noun
the art of making one's voice seem to come from elsewhere
Pronunciation: / vɛnˈtrɪləkˌwɪzəm / (AmE), / vɛnˈtrɪlᵻkwɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
the art of projecting one's voice so that it seems to come from another source, especially from a dummy or puppet

Examples

ventriloquism with a puppet
His skill in ventriloquism made the audience believe the dummy was truly alive.
Many children's parties feature ventriloquism acts to entertain the guests.
She practiced ventriloquism for years before performing on stage with her puppet.
