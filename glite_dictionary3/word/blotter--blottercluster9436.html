blotter

noun
absorbent paper for drying ink
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈblɒtə / (BrE)
A piece of absorbent paper used to soak up excess ink from writing.

Examples

blotter paper sheet
She gently pressed a blotter on the letter to prevent the ink from smudging.
The antique desk came with an old-fashioned blotter resting on top.
