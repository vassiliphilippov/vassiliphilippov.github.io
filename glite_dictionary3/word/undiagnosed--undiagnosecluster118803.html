undiagnosed

adjective
not diagnosed; not yet identified
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈdaɪəɡˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈdaɪəɡnəʊzd / (BrE)
(of an illness, problem, or condition) not having been identified or recognized by diagnosis.

Examples

undiagnosed illness
Many cases of the rare disorder remain undiagnosed due to lack of awareness.
