attending

noun
focusing attention on something
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛn.dɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈtɛn.dɪŋ / (BrE)
The act or process of concentrating on specific features of the environment while ignoring others.

Examples

attending to details
Effective attending is essential for successful learning in noisy classrooms.
Mindful attending helps reduce stress and improve well-being.
The therapist practiced active attending to better understand her client's concerns.

Related Terms

  • synonym – attention: the mental focus on specific stimuli
  • antonym – distraction: a thing that prevents someone from giving full attention
