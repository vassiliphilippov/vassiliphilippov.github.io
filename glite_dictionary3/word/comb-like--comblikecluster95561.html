comb-like

adjective
resembling a comb in shape or structure
Pronunciation: / kɑmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / kɒmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a comb; having narrow, regular projections like the teeth of a comb

Examples

comb-like eyelashes
Her eyelashes were long and comb-like, fanning out gracefully from her eyelids.
The fossil revealed comb-like structures, suggesting it filtered food from the water.
The sea urchin has comb-like spines covering its body.
