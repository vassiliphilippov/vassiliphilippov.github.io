ashcan

noun
bin for rubbish and ashes
Pronunciation: / ˈæʃˌkæn / (AmE), / ˈæʃˌkan / (BrE)
A container for holding household rubbish, especially ashes, until it is collected.

Examples

ashcan for fireplace ashes
He emptied the contents of the fireplace into the ashcan behind the house.
Old newspapers and broken bottles filled the rusted ashcan in the alley.
The janitor wheeled the ashcan to the curb every Friday morning.
