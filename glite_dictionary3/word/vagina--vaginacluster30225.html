vagina

noun
canal in female reproductive system
Pronunciation: / vəˈdʒaɪnə / (AmE), / vəˈdʒaɪnə / (BrE)
the muscular, elastic canal in female mammals that extends from the external genitals to the uterus and forms part of the reproductive system

Examples

the vagina during childbirth
During childbirth, the fetus passes through the vagina to leave the mother's body.
Some infections can affect the vagina and cause discomfort.
The doctor explained how the vagina connects to the uterus.
