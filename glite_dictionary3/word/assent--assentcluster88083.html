assent

noun
agreement or approval
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛnt / (AmE), / əˈsɛnt / (BrE)
the act of agreeing to a statement, proposal, or idea, typically in a formal or official way

Examples

assent of parliament
Parliament's assent is required before the law can take effect.
She nodded in assent when asked if she agreed with the proposal.
Without their assent, we cannot move forward with the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acquiescence: reluctant acceptance without protest
  • synonym – consent: permission for something to happen or agreement to do something
  • antonym – dissent: the holding or expression of opinions that differ with those previously, commonly, or officially held
