naturalized

adjective
planted to appear wild or natural
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ.ər.ə.laɪzd / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ.ər.əl.aɪzd / (BrE)
(of plants) planted in a way that appears wild or as if growing naturally in the area.
