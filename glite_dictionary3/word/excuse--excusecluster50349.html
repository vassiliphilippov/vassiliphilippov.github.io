to excuse

verb
regard as justified; forgive a fault
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskjuz /; / ɪkˈskjus / (AmE), / ɪkˈskjuːz / (BrE)
to forgive, overlook, or make allowances for a fault, mistake, or bad behavior
