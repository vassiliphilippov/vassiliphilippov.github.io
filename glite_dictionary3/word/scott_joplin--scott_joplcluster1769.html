Scott Joplin

proper noun
American composer, 'King of Ragtime'
Pronunciation: / skɑt ˈdʒɑplɪn / (AmE), / skɒt ˈdʒɒplɪn / (BrE)
(1868–1917) an American composer and pianist known as the 'King of Ragtime', who was the first creator of ragtime music to systematically write down and publish his compositions.

Examples

Scott Joplin composed ragtime
Many consider Scott Joplin to be one of the most influential American composers of the early twentieth century.
My favorite ragtime piece was written by Scott Joplin.
The music of Scott Joplin experienced a major revival during the 1970s.
