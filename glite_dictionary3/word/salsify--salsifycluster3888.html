salsify

noun
a Mediterranean plant with edible root
Pronunciation: / ˈsælsəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsælsɪfi / (BrE)
A Mediterranean plant with long edible white roots and purple flowers, cultivated as a vegetable.
