handcuff

noun
locking restraint for wrist
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌkʌf / (AmE), / ˈhændˌkʌf / (BrE)
A metal fastening that can be locked around a person's wrist, typically used in pairs to restrain someone, especially by police.

Examples

police handcuff
After searching his pockets, she removed the handcuff and let him go.
He wore a single handcuff as a prop at the costume party.
The police officer quickly snapped a handcuff onto the suspect's wrist.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shackle: a device used to restrain the movement of a prisoner
