blowout

noun
sudden mechanical failure (e.g. burst tire)
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌaʊt / (BrE)
a sudden failure or rupture of a part or apparatus, especially when referring to a burst tire or similar mechanical malfunction

Examples

tire blowout
Power was lost in the building due to a blowout in the main electrical fuse.
The mechanic inspected the tire and confirmed that the blowout was caused by low air pressure.
While driving home, we experienced a blowout on the highway and had to pull over immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym – failure: the action or state of not functioning correctly
  • synonym – rupture: a break or burst suddenly
