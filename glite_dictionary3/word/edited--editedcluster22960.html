edited

adjective
improved or corrected by editing
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of text, film, etc.) improved or corrected through editing; having undergone revision or alteration to improve accuracy or quality.

Examples

edited article
Every edited article goes through several rounds of review before publication.
The edited manuscript was much clearer than the original draft.
This is the edited version of the documentary, which has been shortened for television.
