bilateral

adjective
having identical parts on both sides
Pronunciation: / baɪˈlæt̬.ɚ.əl / (AmE), / baɪˈlæt.ər.əl / (BrE)
Having identical parts on each side of a central axis; involving both sides equally.

Examples

bilateral symmetry
A leaf often displays bilateral symmetry along its central vein.
Many animals possess bilateral symmetry, which aids in movement and coordination.
The human body is bilateral in structure, with similar organs on both sides.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unilateral: involving or affecting only one side
