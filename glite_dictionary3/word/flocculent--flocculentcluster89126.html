flocculent

adjective
fluffy or woolly in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑːkjələnt / (AmE), / ˈflɒkjʊlənt / (BrE)
Having a soft, fluffy, or woolly appearance; resembling tufts of wool.

Examples

flocculent fur
She stroked the flocculent fur of the newborn lamb.
The flocculent clouds drifted lazily across the sky.
Under the microscope, the sample showed a flocculent texture.
