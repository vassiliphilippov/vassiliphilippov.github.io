indirect

adjective
not in a straight line or course
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dəˈrɛkt, ˌɪn.daɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˈrɛkt, ˌɪn.daɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
not going in a straight line or by the shortest route; not directly leading to a destination

Examples

indirect route home
