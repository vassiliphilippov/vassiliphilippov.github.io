circular

noun
printed advertisement or notice for wide distribution
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkjələr / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə / (BrE)
a printed advertisement or official notice intended for wide distribution, usually in the form of a leaflet or page

Examples

a circular flyer
Every household received a promotional circular advertising the latest discounts.
He threw the advertising circular into the recycling bin without reading it.
The company sent out a circular to inform customers of the new store opening.

Related Terms

  • synonym – brochure: a small booklet or pamphlet advertising or providing information
  • synonym – flyer: a small leaflet advertising an event or product
