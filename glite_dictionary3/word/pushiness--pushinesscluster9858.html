pushiness

noun
offensive assertiveness; aggressiveness
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃ.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being excessively assertive or overly forceful, often to the point of being offensive or annoying

Examples

her pushiness at work
Despite his pushiness, he rarely achieved his aims.
Her pushiness often puts off colleagues during meetings.
The pushiness of some salespeople can feel overwhelming.
