facile

adjective
performed with effortless skill
Pronunciation: / ˈfæ.səl / (AmE), / ˈfæ.sɑɪl / (BrE)
performing or achieved with ease; effortless or adroit, especially in a way that appears without difficulty

Examples

facile victory
Her facile handling of the negotiations impressed everyone in the boardroom.
The artist's facile brushwork gave the painting a lively, spontaneous quality.
With his facile touch, the pianist made even the hardest pieces sound simple.

Related Terms

  • synonym – effortless: achieved with little or no effort
  • antonym – labored: done with great effort and difficulty
