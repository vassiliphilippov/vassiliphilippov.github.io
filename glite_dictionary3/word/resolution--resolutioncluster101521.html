resolution

noun
reduction of inflammation or swelling (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈluʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
the disappearance or reduction of swelling or other signs of inflammation, especially in an organ such as the lung

Examples

resolution of swelling
Complete resolution of the swelling was noted after one week of treatment.
Early intervention can promote the resolution of inflammation in lung tissues.
The patient's fever subsided as the resolution of pneumonia became evident.

Related Terms

  • synonym – remission: a period during which symptoms of disease decrease or disappear
