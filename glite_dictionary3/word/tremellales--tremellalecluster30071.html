Tremellales

noun
an order of fungi with variable textures
Pronunciation: / trəˌmɛˈleɪliz /; / trɛˌmɛlˈeɪliːz / (AmE), / trɪˌmɛˈleɪliːz /; / trɛˌmɛlˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of fungi with fruiting bodies that are gelatinous, waxy, or horny in texture, most of which feed on decaying material
