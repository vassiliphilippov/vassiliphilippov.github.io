kilometre

noun
metric unit of length (1,000 meters)
Pronunciation: / kɪˈlɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to 1,000 meters, or approximately 0.62 miles

Examples

ten kilometres long
He ran a marathon, which is just over forty-two kilometres long.
Speed limits in many countries are measured in kilometres per hour.
The nearest town is five kilometres away from here.
