tonguefish

noun
a flatfish with a pointed tail
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋ.fɪʃ / (BrE)
A left-eyed marine flatfish with a pointed tail, found chiefly in warm seas, and of little commercial value.
