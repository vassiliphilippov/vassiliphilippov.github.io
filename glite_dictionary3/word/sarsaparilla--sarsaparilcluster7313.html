sarsaparilla

noun
a sweet, root-flavored soft drink
Pronunciation: / ˌsæs.pəˈrɪl.ə / (AmE), / ˌsɑː.spəˈrɪl.ə / (BrE)
a sweet, carbonated soft drink traditionally flavored with extract from sarsaparilla root, or sometimes with birch oil and sassafras.
