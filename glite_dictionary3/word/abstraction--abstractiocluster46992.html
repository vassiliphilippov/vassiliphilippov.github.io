abstraction

noun
a work of abstract art
Pronunciation: / æbˈstræk.ʃən / (AmE), / æbˈstræk.ʃən / (BrE)
a painting in which the representation of real objects is altered or omitted to focus on shapes, colors, and forms rather than realistic depiction

Examples

abstraction in painting
Critics praised the artist's use of texture and movement in his latest abstraction.
She bought a brightly colored abstraction to hang above her sofa.
The gallery featured several stunning abstractions alongside more traditional works.
