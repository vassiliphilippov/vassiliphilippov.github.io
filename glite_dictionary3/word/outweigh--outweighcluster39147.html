to outweigh

verb
be heavier than something else
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈweɪ / (AmE), / aʊtˈweɪ / (BrE)
(of one thing) to be heavier than (another thing).

Examples

outweigh an elephant
An elephant easily outweighs a horse.
Even with all the books removed, the box still outweighs the suitcase.
The large gold statue outweighs the small one by several kilograms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exceed: to go beyond in degree or amount
