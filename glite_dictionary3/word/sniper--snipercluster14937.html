sniper

noun
last-second auction bidder
Pronunciation: / c8sna6ap5a / (AmE), / c8sna6ap59 / (BrE)
(in online auctions) a person or software program that places a bid at the very last moment to try to win an item by preventing others from outbidding them.

Examples

auction sniper
He used an automated sniper to place the final bid on the vintage watch.
Several online snipers targeted the rare comic during the last thirty seconds of bidding.
The auction house warned that using a sniper might be frowned upon by some sellers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – auction sniping: the tactic of placing a bid in the final seconds of an online auction
  • synonym – bidder: a person who offers money for something at an auction
