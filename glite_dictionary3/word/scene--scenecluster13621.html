scene

noun
an incident or event (real or imaginary)
Pronunciation: / siːn / (AmE), / siːn / (BrE)
an incident, event, or happening, especially one that is emotionally significant or notable, whether real or imagined

Examples

dramatic scene

The accident was a shocking scene that no one could forget.
Their parting was a sad scene filled with tears and regret.
