blowtorch

noun
portable burner producing hot flame
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌtɔrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌtɔːtʃ / (BrE)
a portable device that mixes air and gas to produce a very hot flame, used for heating, welding, or soldering

Examples

use a blowtorch for welding
Always wear safety goggles when operating a blowtorch.
The artist shaped the glass using a small blowtorch.
The plumber used a blowtorch to solder the pipes together.

Related Terms

  • synonym – welding torch: device used for welding, typically producing higher temperatures
