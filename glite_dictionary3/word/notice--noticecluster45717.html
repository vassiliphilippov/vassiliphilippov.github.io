notice

noun
short critical review or appraisal
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtɪs / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtɪs / (BrE)
a short written or published review of an artistic performance, book, or event, usually offering a critical appraisal

Examples

positive notice review
After the exhibition, critics wrote glowing notices about the artist’s unique vision.
Her novel got mixed notices in the literary press, with some praising the style and others criticizing the plot.
The play received enthusiastic notices from several major newspapers.
