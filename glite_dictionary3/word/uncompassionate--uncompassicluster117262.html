uncompassionate

adjective
lacking compassion or sympathy
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmˈpæʃənət / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈpæʃənət / (BrE)
lacking compassion or sympathy for others; not showing concern for others' suffering.

Examples

uncompassionate response
Her uncompassionate attitude made it difficult for her to connect with her coworkers.
It was clear from his uncompassionate response that he did not care about the issue.
The manager's uncompassionate policies led to widespread dissatisfaction among employees.
