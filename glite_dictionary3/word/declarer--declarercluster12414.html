declarer

noun
person who makes a formal statement
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklɛɹɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈkleərə / (BrE)
a person who makes a statement or announces something as true, especially formally or publicly.

Examples

the declarer of results
As the official declarer of the findings, she held great responsibility.
In the debate, he took on the role of declarer for his team's position.
The declarer stood before the audience and announced the results with confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym – asserter: person who states something confidently
  • antonym – denier: person who denies something
