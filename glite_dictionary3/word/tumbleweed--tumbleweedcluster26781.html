tumbleweed

noun
wind-blown rolling plant of arid regions
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌmbəlˌwid /; / ˈtʌmbəlˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈtʌmbəlˌwiːd /; / ˈtʌmbəlwiːd / (BrE)
a plant, especially in dry regions, that breaks off from its roots when mature and is blown around by the wind as a rolling mass
