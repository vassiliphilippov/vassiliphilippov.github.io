to fertilise

verb
add fertilizer or nutrients to soil
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz / (BrE)
to add fertilizer or nutrients to soil to help plants grow

Examples

to fertilise the soil
Every spring, gardeners fertilise their lawns to ensure lush green grass.
The farmer decided to fertilise the field before planting new crops.
You should always fertilise houseplants carefully to avoid overfeeding them.
