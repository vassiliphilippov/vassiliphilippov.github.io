caparisoned

adjective
dressed in decorative coverings (esp. animal)
Pronunciation: / kəˈpærɪsənd / (AmE), / kəˈpærɪsənd / (BrE)
(of a horse or other animal) dressed in rich, decorative coverings or ornaments, especially for ceremony or display.

Examples

caparisoned horse in parade
In the festival, the elephant was caparisoned with gold and silk decorations.
Knight after knight rode in on caparisoned steeds that dazzled the crowds.
The king’s parade featured a line of caparisoned horses with gleaming harnesses.
