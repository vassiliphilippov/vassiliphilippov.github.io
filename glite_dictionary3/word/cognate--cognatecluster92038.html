cognate

adjective
related in origin or character
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːɡ.neɪt /; / ˈkɑːɡˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒɡ.neɪt / (BrE)
(of things, ideas, words, or languages) related by having the same origin or source
