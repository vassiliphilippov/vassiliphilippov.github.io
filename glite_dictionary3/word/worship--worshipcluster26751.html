worship

noun
deep love and admiration
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
a feeling of deep love, admiration, and respect, often towards a person or deity

Examples

worship of celebrities
Her worship of her mentor was evident in the way she spoke about him.
His worship of the artist bordered on obsession.
The fans expressed their worship for the actor by cheering loudly at every appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adoration: deep love and respect
  • antonym – disdain: the feeling of not respecting someone or something
  • synonym – reverence: profound respect and awe
