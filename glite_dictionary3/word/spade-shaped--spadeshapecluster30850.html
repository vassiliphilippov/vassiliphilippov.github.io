spade-shaped

adjective
having the shape of a spade
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪd ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈspeɪd ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a spade, especially resembling the pointed, broad blade of a spade tool or the symbol of the playing card suit.

Examples

spade-shaped leaf
Her pendant had a curious spade-shaped design that caught everyone's attention.
The leaf of the plant is distinctly spade-shaped, tapering to a point at the tip.
They installed a spade-shaped sign at the entrance to the garden.
