fingerstall

noun
protective sheath for a finger
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌstɔl / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəstɔːl / (BrE)
a protective sheath worn over a finger to guard against injury or infection, often used during medical procedures or manual work

Examples

fingerstall for protection
For extra grip and protection, she slipped a rubber fingerstall onto her thumb.
He wore a fingerstall on his index finger to protect a cut while working.
The nurse covered her finger with a fingerstall before changing the dressing.
