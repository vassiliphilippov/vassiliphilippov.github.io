goshawk

noun
large hawk used in falconry
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːsˌhɔk / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsˌhɔːk / (BrE)
A large hawk found in Eurasia and North America, often used in falconry, with broad wings and a long tail.
