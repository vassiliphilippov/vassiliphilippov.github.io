psychokinetic

adjective
moving objects by mental power
Pronunciation: / saɪˌkoʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / saɪˌkəʊkɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
Able to move objects by mental power or psychic force, without apparent physical means.
