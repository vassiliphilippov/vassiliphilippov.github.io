mustard

noun
pungent spice or condiment from mustard seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.tɚd / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.təd / (BrE)
a pungent yellow or brown powder or paste made from the seeds of a mustard plant, used as a spice or condiment

Examples

mustard on sandwiches
Could you please pass the mustard for my sandwich?
She added a spoonful of mustard to the salad dressing for extra flavor.
This recipe calls for both dry mustard and mustard seeds.
