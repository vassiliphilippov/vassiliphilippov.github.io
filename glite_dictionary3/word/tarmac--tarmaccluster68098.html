to tarmac

verb
surface with tarmacadam
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːr.mæk / (AmE), / ˈtɑː.mæk / (BrE)
To cover or surface (a road, path, etc.) with tarmacadam.

Examples

to tarmac a road
The company tarmacked the driveway to give it a smooth, durable finish.
They plan to tarmac the road leading to the new housing estate this summer.
Workers are tarmacking the parking lot to improve access for customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pave: to cover a surface, especially a road or path, with flat stones or bricks
