overreckoning

noun
an overestimation in calculation
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈrɛkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈrɛkənɪŋ / (BrE)
an act or result of calculating or estimating something as being larger or higher than it actually is

Examples

overreckoning the total cost
Her overreckoning of the project's costs led to budget cuts elsewhere.
Historical accounts were often influenced by an overreckoning of the army's size.
The company suffered losses due to an overreckoning of its future profits.

Related Terms

  • synonym – overestimation: the act of estimating something as greater than it is
  • antonym – underreckoning: an act or result of reckoning too low
