buster

noun
informal form of address for a man
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈbʌstə / (BrE)
(informal) A way of addressing a man, often to express irritation or warning, sometimes used for emphasis.

Examples

hey buster listen
Buster, you'd better watch where you're going next time.
Alright, buster, that's enough out of you.
Listen here, buster, you can't park your car there.
