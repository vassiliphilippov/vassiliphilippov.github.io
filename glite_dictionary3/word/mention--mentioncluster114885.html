to mention

verb
formally acknowledge or commend
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈmen.tʃən / (BrE)
to formally acknowledge or commend someone, especially in an official report or context for notable action or achievement

Examples

to mention a winner
At the award ceremony, the committee decided to mention her for her exceptional volunteer work.
Several soldiers were mentioned in the general's report for acts of bravery during the mission.
The coach made it a point to mention the team's unsung heroes in his speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym – commend: to praise or recommend someone officially
  • antonym – ignore: fail to pay attention to or acknowledge
