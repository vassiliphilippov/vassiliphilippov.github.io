defendable

adjective
able to be defended
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛndəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛndəbəl / (BrE)
Able to be defended against attack, criticism, or challenge.

Examples

defendable position
She did not think her actions were defendable in any way.
The city was situated on a hill, making it highly defendable during ancient times.
The lawyer argued that his client’s position was both reasonable and defendable.
