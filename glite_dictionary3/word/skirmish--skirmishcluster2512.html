skirmish

noun
short, minor fight or clash
Pronunciation: / ˈskɝː.mɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskɜː.mɪʃ / (BrE)
a brief and unplanned fight, especially between small groups or as part of a larger conflict

Examples

a skirmish between groups
A skirmish broke out between protesters and police near the city hall.
The two rival gangs had a brief skirmish before the police arrived.
There were several skirmishes along the border before the ceasefire was agreed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clash: a brief encounter or fight
  • degree higher – engagement: a battle or fight between armed forces
  • degree higher – war: a prolonged armed conflict
