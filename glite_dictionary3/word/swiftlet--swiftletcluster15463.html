swiftlet

noun
small Asian/Australasian bird; makes edible nests
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪft.lət / (AmE), / ˈswɪft.lɪt / (BrE)
a small swiftly flying bird of eastern Asia and Australasia, known for constructing edible nests used in bird's nest soup.

Examples

swiftlet edible nest
Many caves in Southeast Asia are protected due to their large swiftlet populations.
Scientists have studied the unique way swiftlets use echolocation to navigate in the dark.
