Truncocolumella citrina

noun
a species of yellow-orange fungus with a sterile column
Pronunciation: / truŋkoʊkəˈluːmə sɪˈtrinə / (AmE), / trʌŋkəʊkɒˈluːmɪlə sɪˈtraɪnə / (BrE)
a species of fungus with a round yellow to orange fruiting body, found on or just below the ground, distinguished by a sterile column extending into its spore-bearing tissue.
