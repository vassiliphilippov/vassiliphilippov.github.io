runner

noun
person who runs in races
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnɚ /; / ˈrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈrʌnə / (BrE)
a person who runs, especially for sport or exercise

Examples

marathon runner

Each runner wore a number pinned to their shirt during the race.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jogger: person who runs at a steady, moderate pace for exercise
  • antonym – jumper: an athlete who competes in jumping events (track and field)
  • synonym – sprinter: an athlete who runs short-distance races
  • antonym – walker: a person who travels on foot at a walking pace
