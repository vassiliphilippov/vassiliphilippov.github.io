sombrero

noun
hat with tall crown and broad brim
Pronunciation: / səmˈbrɛəɹoʊ / (AmE), / sɒmˈbrɛərəʊ / (BrE)
A broad-brimmed, tall-crowned hat, typically made of straw, traditionally worn in Mexico and the southwestern United States for protection from the sun.

Examples

wear a sombrero
A traditional Mexican sombrero features intricate designs woven into the straw.
He wore a large sombrero to keep the sun off his face during the festival.
The tourists bought colorful sombreros at the market as souvenirs.
