uppercase

adjective
written in capital letters
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpərˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈʌpəkeɪs / (BrE)
(of a letter) written or printed as a capital, as opposed to in small or lowercase letters.

Examples

uppercase letter
All the headings in the document are in uppercase.
If you write your name in uppercase, it will be easier to read.
Please enter your password using only uppercase letters.

Related Terms

  • antonym – lowercase: (of a letter) written or printed in small letters, not capitals
