loosely

adverb
in an open or not tightly packed way
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsli / (AmE), / ˈluːsli / (BrE)
In a way that is open in structure, not compact, or not tightly packed or connected.

Examples

loosely organized group
Several families formed a loosely organized community in the countryside.
She piled her hair up loosely and let a few strands fall around her face.
The fabric was loosely woven, making it light and airy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – tightly: in a way that is firmly fixed or closely packed
