Formica

noun
plastic laminate used for surfaces
Pronunciation: / fɔːrˈmaɪkə / (AmE), / fɔːˈmʌɪkə / (BrE)
A type of durable plastic laminate, often used as a surface for countertops and tables.

Examples

Formica kitchen countertop
He chose a dark woodgrain Formica for the new office desk.
Many retro diners feature tables topped with brightly colored Formica.
The kitchen countertops were finished with Formica for easy cleaning and durability.
