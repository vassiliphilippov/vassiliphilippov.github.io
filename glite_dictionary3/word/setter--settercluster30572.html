setter

noun
person who sets text for printing
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛtər / (AmE), / ˈsetə / (BrE)
a person who sets written material into type for printing

Examples

skilled setter at work
During the era of manual printing, a setter needed a great deal of precision and skill.
Modern publishing rarely uses a setter, as much of the work is now digital.
The setter worked late to prepare the newspaper for the next morning's edition.
