to overproduce

verb
produce too much of something
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚprəˈdus /; / ˌoʊvərprəˈdus / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəprəˈdjuːs / (BrE)
to produce more of something than is needed, wanted, or can be sold or used
