Rubus odoratus

noun
North American shrub with purple flowers and raspberry-like fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈruː.bəs oʊ.dəˈreɪ.təs / (AmE), / ˈruː.bəs əʊ.dəˈrɑː.təs / (BrE)
a shrub native to eastern North America, known for its showy rose to purplish flowers and red or orange thimble-shaped fruit; also called purple-flowering raspberry
