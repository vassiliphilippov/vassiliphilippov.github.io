pamperer

noun
person who excessively pampers others
Pronunciation: / ˈpæmpərər / (AmE), / ˈpæmpərə / (BrE)
a person who pampers or spoils someone by giving them excessive care or indulgence

Examples

overprotective pamperer
A good parent finds a balance between being a pamperer and setting boundaries.
She was known as a pamperer who gave her grandchildren everything they wanted.
Some people see their role as a pamperer to make their loved ones feel special.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spoiler: someone who spoils a person by overindulgence
