lining

noun
inner layer of a garment
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
a layer of material attached to the inside of something, such as a garment, bag, or shoe, for protection, comfort, or decoration
