to distemper

verb
paint with distemper paint
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtɛmpər / (AmE), / dɪsˈtɛmpə / (BrE)
to paint a surface with distemper paint, a traditional water-based paint

Examples

to distemper a ceiling
Before wallpapering, the craftsman distempered the ceiling for a smooth, matte effect.
Experts traditionally distemper the walls of historic buildings to match the original finish.
Many artists in the past preferred to distemper panels before applying delicate decorative details.
