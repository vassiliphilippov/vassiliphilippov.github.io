act

noun
short performance in a show
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / ækt / (BrE)
a short performance by an entertainer or group, or a major division of a play, opera, or ballet

Examples

a comedy act
Her comedy act had the whole audience laughing nonstop.
The magician ended his act with an impressive escape from a locked box.
They performed their dance act at the school talent show.
After the intermission, the orchestra began the second act of the ballet.
The audience erupted in applause at the end of the final act.
The first act of the play set the tone for the entire story.
