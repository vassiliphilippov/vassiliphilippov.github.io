vena spinalis

noun
veins draining the spinal cord
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪnə spaɪˈnælɪs / (AmE), / ˌviːnə spaɪˈneɪlɪs / (BrE)
Any of several veins that drain blood from the spinal cord.

Examples

vena spinalis injury
Damage to a vena spinalis can cause serious neurological symptoms.
During the surgery, the doctor carefully avoided injuring the vena spinalis.
The vena spinalis plays a crucial role in draining blood from the spinal cord.
