Ascension

noun
Christ's ascent to heaven after the Resurrection
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛnʃən / (AmE), / əˈsɛnʃən / (BrE)
(in Christian belief) the bodily rising of Jesus Christ into heaven on the 40th day after the Resurrection.

Examples

the Ascension of Jesus
Believers regard the Ascension as a key moment in the life of Jesus.
Many paintings depict the Ascension with Jesus rising above his disciples.
The church celebrates the Ascension forty days after Easter.
