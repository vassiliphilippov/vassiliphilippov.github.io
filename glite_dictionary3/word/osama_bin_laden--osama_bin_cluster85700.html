Osama bin Laden

proper noun
Arab terrorist leader, founder of al-Qaeda
Pronunciation: / oʊˈsɑːmə bɪn lɑːdən / (AmE), / əʊˈsɑːmə bɪn ˈlɑːd(ə)n / (BrE)
(1957–2011) An Arab terrorist who founded and led al-Qaeda, a militant Islamist organization responsible for large-scale terrorist attacks, including those on September 11, 2001.

Osama bin Laden led al-Qaeda
Osama bin Laden claimed responsibility for several major terrorist attacks during his leadership of al-Qaeda.
