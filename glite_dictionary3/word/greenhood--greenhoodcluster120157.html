greenhood

noun
a terrestrial orchid of the genus Pterostylis
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnhʊd / (BrE)
any of various terrestrial orchids of the genus Pterostylis, characterized by leaves in a basal rosette and green flowers with a hood-like dorsal sepal, often striped with purple, brown, or red.
