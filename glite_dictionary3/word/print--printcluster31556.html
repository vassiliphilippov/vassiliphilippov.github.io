to print

verb
write in separate, non-cursive letters
Pronunciation: / prɪnt / (AmE), / prɪnt / (BrE)
to write letters or words by hand using separate, upright characters rather than joined, cursive ones

Examples

to print letters clearly
All students must print their answers in block letters on the test sheet.
Please print your name clearly on the application form.
She taught her son to print before introducing him to cursive handwriting.
