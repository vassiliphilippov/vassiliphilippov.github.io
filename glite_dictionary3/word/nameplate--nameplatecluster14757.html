nameplate

noun
plate showing a name for identification
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪmˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈneɪmpleɪt / (BrE)
A flat, usually rectangular piece of metal, plastic, or other material that displays a person's name or the name of a company, typically fixed to a door, desk, or machine for identification.

Examples

nameplate on a door
Each machine in the factory has a nameplate listing its specifications.
She polished the nameplate on her desk every morning.
The manager's nameplate was fixed to the office door.
