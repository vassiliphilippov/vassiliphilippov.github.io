adhesive

adjective
able to stick or cling to surfaces
Pronunciation: / ədˈhisɪv / (AmE), / ədˈhiːsɪv / (BrE)
tending to stick to a surface or object; having the property of adhering

Examples

adhesive tape surface
Because the paint wasn't adhesive enough, it quickly peeled off the wall.
He wore an adhesive badge on his shirt during the event.
The adhesive quality of the tape makes it perfect for wrapping gifts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cohesive: tending to stick together as a unified whole
  • antonym – nonadhesive: not tending to stick or adhere
