hauncher

noun
machine for shaping board ends or edges
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔntʃər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːntʃə / (BrE)
a machine used to make shaped cuts on the end or edge of a board, especially in woodworking.

Examples

woodworking hauncher machine
Each hauncher in the workshop is maintained regularly to ensure accurate cutting.
