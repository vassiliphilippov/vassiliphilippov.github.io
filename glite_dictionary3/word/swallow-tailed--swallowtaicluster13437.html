swallow-tailed

adjective
having a forked tail like a swallow
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːloʊ teɪld / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊ teɪld / (BrE)
(especially of butterflies or birds) having a deeply forked or divided tail resembling that of a swallow.

Examples

swallow-tailed butterfly
Several species of swallow-tailed butterflies are known for their elegant, divided tails.
She admired the swallow-tailed coat pattern of the rare insect.
The swallow-tailed kite is a striking bird with a deeply forked tail that helps it swoop and glide.
