unarticulated

adjective
not having joints between segments
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɑːrˈtɪkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not composed of segments joined by joints; lacking articulations between the parts.

Examples

unarticulated limb
Some early fossils show unarticulated limbs, indicating primitive anatomical organization.
The worm's body is unarticulated, lacking any obvious divisions or segments.
Unlike insects, certain soft-bodied animals have an unarticulated structure.

  • antonym – articulated: having joints between segments
