erect

adjective
upright and straight in position
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
(of posture or position) upright and straight; not leaning or lying down.

Examples

erect posture
She kept her back erect throughout the yoga class, focusing on good posture.
The dog’s tail was erect, showing it was alert and possibly aggressive.
The guard stood erect at the entrance, watching everyone who passed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – prone: (of posture or position) lying flat, especially face downward
  • synonym – upright: (of posture or position) vertical; not leaning
