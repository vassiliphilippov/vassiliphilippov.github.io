to ride

verb
harass or nag persistently
Pronunciation: / raɪd / (AmE), / raɪd / (BrE)
to harass or criticize someone persistently, often in a nagging or annoying way

Examples

to ride a friend
His friends rode him all week after he forgot their meeting.
She gets frustrated when her colleagues ride her for every minor mistake.
Stop riding me about my messy room—I said I would clean it!

Related Terms

  • antonym – encourage: to give support or confidence to someone
  • synonym – nag: to annoy or irritate with persistent fault-finding or continuous urging
  • synonym – tease: to make fun of or annoy repeatedly
