to fling

verb
move suddenly and forcefully
Pronunciation: / flɪŋ / (AmE), / flɪŋ / (BrE)
to move or throw oneself suddenly and forcefully, often without control or restraint

Examples

to fling oneself into arms
After hearing the news, she flung herself into his arms.
He flung himself out of the chair and stormed out of the room.
The child flung himself across the bed in a fit of laughter.

  • synonym – hurl: to throw something forcefully
