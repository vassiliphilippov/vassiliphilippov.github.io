lancet-shaped

adjective
narrow and pointed like a lancet
Pronunciation: / ˈlæn.sət ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈlan.sɪt ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
narrow and tapering to a point, shaped like a lancet; especially used to describe leaves that are broadest in the middle and taper at both ends.

Examples

lancet-shaped leaf
The lancet-shaped blades made the sculpture appear elegant and dynamic.
The architect designed windows with a distinctive lancet-shaped arch for the chapel.
The plant's leaves are lancet-shaped and have sharply pointed tips.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lanceolate: shaped like a lance head, tapering to a point at each end; especially of leaves
