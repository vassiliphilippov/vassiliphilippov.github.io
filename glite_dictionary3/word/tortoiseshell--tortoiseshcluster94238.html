tortoiseshell

noun
cat with mottled black, cream, and orange fur
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrt̬əsˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtəsʃɛl / (BrE)
a cat with a coat marked by a mixture of black, cream, and yellow-orange patches

Examples

tortoiseshell cat
Her new cat is a beautiful tortoiseshell with striking orange and black fur.
Many tortoiseshell cats are known for their feisty and independent personalities.
The shelter has several tortoiseshell kittens available for adoption this weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym – calico: a cat with a tri-colored (white, black, and orange) coat, often with larger patches
