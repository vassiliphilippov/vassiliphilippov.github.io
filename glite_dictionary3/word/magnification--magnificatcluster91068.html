magnification

noun
ratio of image size to object size
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡnəfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌmæɡ.nɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌmaɡ.nɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of making something appear larger, especially using a lens, microscope, or other optical instrument
