showing

noun
presentation of a film or play
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when a film, play, or television programme is presented or screened to an audience

Examples

showing of a movie
The 7 p.m. showing of the film was completely sold out.
There will be a special showing of the documentary for students on Friday.
We missed the afternoon showing, so we had to wait until the evening.
