rough

adjective
having an uneven surface
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
having an uneven, irregular, or not smooth surface; not finished or polished

Examples

rough surface
Some towels are soft, but others feel quite rough on your skin.

The holly leaf is easily recognized by its rough edges that are sharply pointed and jagged.

They used a rough slab of marble as the foundation for the sculpture.
