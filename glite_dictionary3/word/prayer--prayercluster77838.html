prayer

noun
earnest or urgent request
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈpreə / (BrE)
an earnest or urgent request for something, especially from a person in authority or in a difficult situation

Examples

a prayer for help
Her prayer for understanding was met with silence.
The workers sent a prayer to management for better safety measures.
They filed a formal prayer to the court asking for leniency.

Related Terms

  • synonym – appeal: a serious or urgent request
  • degree higher – demand: an insistent and peremptory request, often not earnest
  • synonym – entreaty: an earnest or humble request
