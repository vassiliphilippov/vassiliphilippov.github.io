to snuffle

verb
to sniff inquiringly or noisily
Pronunciation: / ˈsnʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈsnʌfəl / (BrE)
to sniff or smell by breathing noisily through the nose, often in an inquisitive or searching manner

Examples

to snuffle for food
Bears often snuffle the ground for food as they wander through the forest.
She could hear him snuffling at the door, curious about what was inside.
The dog began to snuffle around the base of the tree, searching for hidden treats.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sniff: to draw air audibly through the nose to detect a smell
