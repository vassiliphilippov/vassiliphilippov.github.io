homespun

adjective
simple, rural; characteristic of country life
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌspʌn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌspʌn / (BrE)
simple and unsophisticated; characteristic of country life or people, especially in contrast to urban sophistication.

Examples

homespun wisdom
His homespun manners and honesty quickly won over the townspeople.
She preferred homespun advice to complicated psychological theories.
The author’s homespun wisdom appeals to those seeking simple truths.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rustic: having a simplicity and charm typical of the countryside
  • antonym – sophisticated: having, revealing, or involving a great deal of worldly experience and knowledge
