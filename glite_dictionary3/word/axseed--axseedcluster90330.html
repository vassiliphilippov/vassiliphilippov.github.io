axseed

noun
European herb with pink-and-white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈækˌsiːd / (AmE), / ˈaksˌiːd / (BrE)
A European herb resembling vetch, with umbels of pink-and-white flowers and sharp-angled pods, naturalized in the eastern United States.
