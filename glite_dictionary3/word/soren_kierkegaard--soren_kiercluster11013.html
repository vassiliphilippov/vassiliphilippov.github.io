Soren Kierkegaard

proper noun
Danish existentialist philosopher (1813–1855)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːrən ˈkɪrkəˌɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːrən ˈkɪəkəˌɡɑːd / (BrE)
(1813–1855) Danish philosopher regarded as a founder of existentialism, known for his exploration of faith, ethics, and individuality.
