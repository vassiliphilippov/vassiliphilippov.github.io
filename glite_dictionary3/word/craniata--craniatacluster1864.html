Craniata

noun
taxonomic group of animals with skulls
Pronunciation: / kreɪniˈeɪtə / (AmE), / krəˈnaɪətə / (BrE)
A major taxonomic group of animals comprising all vertebrates and their close relatives, characterized by the presence of a skull.
