Paul Cézanne

proper noun
French Post-Impressionist painter
Pronunciation: / pɔl seɪˈzæn / (AmE), / pɔːl seɪˈzan / (BrE)
(1839–1906) A French Post-Impressionist painter known for influencing the development of modern art by emphasizing the structural aspects of nature.
