banderilla

noun
decorative barbed dart used in bullfighting
Pronunciation: / ˌbændəˈriːjə / (AmE), / ˌbændəˈriːjə / (BrE)
a brightly decorated, barbed dart implanted in the neck or shoulders of a bull during a bullfight

Examples

banderilla in bullfighting
Spectators cheered as the banderilla was fixed with precision.
The matador skillfully placed each banderilla into the bull's shoulders.
Traditionally, two banderillas are used during this stage of the bullfight.
