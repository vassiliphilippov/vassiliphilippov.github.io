leucoma

noun
opaque white spot on the cornea
Pronunciation: / luˈkoʊmə / (AmE), / luːˈkəʊmə / (BrE)
an eye disease characterized by an opaque white spot on the cornea, often resulting from injury or infection

Examples

leucoma on cornea
A visible leucoma can significantly impair a person's vision.
Any trauma to the eye may develop into a leucoma if not treated properly.
The patient was diagnosed with a leucoma following a severe corneal infection.
