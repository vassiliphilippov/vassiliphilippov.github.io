unsportingly

adverb
in an unsportsmanlike way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɔrtɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɔːtɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that does not display fairness or respect in sporting behavior.

Examples

to act unsportingly after a game
He celebrated his victory unsportingly, taunting the defeated team.
She unsportingly distracted her opponent during the decisive point.
The player acted unsportingly by refusing to shake hands after the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sportingly: in a manner showing fairness and good sportsmanship
  • synonym – unsportsmanlike: not fair, generous, or polite in sporting behavior
