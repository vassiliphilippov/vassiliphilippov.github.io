gloomily

adverb
in a gloomy or pessimistic manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluː.mə.li / (AmE), / ˈɡluː.mɪ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses sadness, pessimism, or lack of hope.

Examples

look gloomily outside
He stared gloomily out the window as the rain poured down.
She gloomily predicted that the project would end in failure.
The old house sat gloomily at the end of the deserted street.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cheerfully: in a happy and optimistic manner
  • synonym – gloomy: having a dark, sad, or depressing quality
