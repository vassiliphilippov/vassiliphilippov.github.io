stuff

noun
quality or qualities needed for something
Pronunciation: / stʌf / (AmE), / stʌf / (BrE)
the essential quality or qualities needed for something, especially to succeed or be impressive

Examples

Curiosity is the very stuff of a good scientist.
Imagination is the stuff that dreams are made of.
The stuff of legends often comes from real historical events.
