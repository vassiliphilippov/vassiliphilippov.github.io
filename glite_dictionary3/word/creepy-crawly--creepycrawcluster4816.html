creepy-crawly

noun
small crawling creature (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈkripi ˌkrɔli /; / ˈkripi ˈkrɔli / (AmE), / ˈkriːpi ˌkrɔːli /; / ˈkriːpi ˈkrɔːli / (BrE)
an informal word for a small creature that crawls, such as an insect, worm, or spider, especially one that makes people feel uncomfortable or afraid

Examples

He hates camping because of all the creepy-crawlies that come out at night.
My daughter screamed when she saw a creepy-crawly in the bathtub.
