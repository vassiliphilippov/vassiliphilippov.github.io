gynecology

noun
branch of medicine for women's reproductive health
Pronunciation: / ˌɡaɪnəˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɡɪnɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine concerned with the health, diseases, and hygiene of the female reproductive system.

Examples

The hospital has an entire department dedicated to gynecology and reproductive care.
