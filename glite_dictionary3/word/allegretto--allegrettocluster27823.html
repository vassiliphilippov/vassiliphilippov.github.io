allegretto

noun
moderately quick musical tempo
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈɡrɛtoʊ /; / ˌælɪˈɡrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌalɪˈɡrɛtəʊ / (BrE)
(in music) a moderately quick tempo, faster than andante but slower than allegro; also, a piece or passage played at this speed
