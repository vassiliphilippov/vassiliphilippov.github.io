coquille

noun
seafood dish served in a scallop shell
Pronunciation: / koʊˈkiːl / (AmE), / kɒˈkiːl / (BrE)
A dish of seafood, often scallops, served in a scallop shell, typically with a creamy sauce and breadcrumbs.

Examples

coquille with scallops
He ordered a delicious coquille as a starter before the main course arrived.
