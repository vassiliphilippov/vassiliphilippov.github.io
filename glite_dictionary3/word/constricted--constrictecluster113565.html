constricted

adjective
narrowed or restricted; squeezed
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrɪktɪd / (AmE), / kənˈstrɪktɪd / (BrE)
made narrower or tighter by being squeezed or compressed; by extension, restricted or limited in scope or freedom.
