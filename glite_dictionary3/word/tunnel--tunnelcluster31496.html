tunnel

noun
virtual encrypted network path
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌnəl / (AmE), / ˈtʌnəl / (BrE)
a virtual, often encrypted, pathway that encapsulates and carries network traffic between two points (for example, in a VPN), allowing data to be transmitted securely or routed through intermediary systems.

Examples

VPN tunnel
The IT team established a secure tunnel so remote employees could access internal servers safely.
All data sent through the tunnel was encrypted to prevent interception on public Wi-Fi.
They configured the router to create a site-to-site tunnel between headquarters and the branch office.

Related Terms

  • synonym – VPN: a virtual private network that creates a secure, encrypted connection over the internet
  • synonym – secure connection: a network connection that protects data from interception or tampering, often using encryption
