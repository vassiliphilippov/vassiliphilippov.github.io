lodestar

noun
a star used as a navigational guide
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊdˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈləʊdˌstɑː / (BrE)
A star, especially the North Star, that is used as a guide by navigators or as a reference point in astronomy.

Examples

the lodestar in navigation
For centuries, the North Star has been humanity’s most trusted lodestar.
Sailors once relied on the lodestar to navigate the open seas.
The lodestar served as a beacon of hope on their long journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym – guide: something that directs or influences a course
  • synonym – polestar: the North Star, key navigational reference star
