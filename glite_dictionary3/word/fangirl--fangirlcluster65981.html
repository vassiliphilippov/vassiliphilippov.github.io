to fangirl

verb
act as an overenthusiastic devoted fan
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈfænˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
(informal) To behave in an overtly enthusiastic or obsessive way as a devoted fan, especially about celebrities, TV shows, or fictional characters.

Examples

to fangirl over a celebrity
Every time a new episode is released, fans fangirl on social media for hours.
She couldn't help but fangirl over her favorite singer when she saw him backstage.
They totally fangirled when they met the cast at Comic-Con.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fanboy: to behave as an excessively devoted male fan
