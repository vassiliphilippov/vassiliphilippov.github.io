to bull

verb
push or force through forcefully
Pronunciation: / bʊl / (AmE), / bʊl / (BrE)
to push, force, or move through something with determination or by brute strength

Examples

to bull through obstacles
Despite the obstacles, she bulled forward with her plan.
He bulled his way through the crowd to reach the exit.
They bulled through their demands during the negotiation.
