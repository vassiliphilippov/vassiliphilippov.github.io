monochromacy

noun
complete color blindness
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑː.nəˈkroʊ.mə.si / (AmE), / ˌmɒn.əˈkrəʊ.mə.si / (BrE)
the condition of complete color blindness, in which colors are distinguished only by their brightness
