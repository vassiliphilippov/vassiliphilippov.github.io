rigidly

adverb
in a stiff or strict manner
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdʒɪdli / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒɪdli / (BrE)
In a strict, stiff, or unbending manner; without flexibility or deviation.

Examples

rigidly follow rules
Despite objections, the company rigidly maintained its policies.
He stood rigidly at attention during the ceremony.
The teacher enforced the rules rigidly without exception.
