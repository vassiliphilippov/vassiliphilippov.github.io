Bodoni

noun
a serif typeface with thick and thin strokes
Pronunciation: / boʊˈdoʊni / (AmE), / bəˈdəʊni / (BrE)
a typeface characterized by regular shapes, thin hairline serifs, and pronounced thick vertical strokes, based on an 18th-century design by Gianbattista Bodoni

Examples

Bodoni typeface design
Printed invitations often use Bodoni to add a touch of sophistication.
The designer chose Bodoni for the magazine's title to give it a classic, elegant look.
The striking contrast in Bodoni makes it popular for both headlines and logos.
