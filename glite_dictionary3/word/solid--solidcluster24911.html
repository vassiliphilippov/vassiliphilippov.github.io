solid

adjective
not liquid or gas; firm and stable
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlɪd /; / ˈsɑː.lɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪd /; / ˈsɒl.ɪd / (BrE)
firm, hard, or having a definite shape; not liquid, gas, hollow, or soft; made of one material throughout
