to remain

verb
stay in a place; not leave
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmeɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪn / (BrE)
to stay in a place, state, or condition and not leave or change; to continue to exist after other things have changed or gone

Examples

Only two pieces of cake remain on the plate.
