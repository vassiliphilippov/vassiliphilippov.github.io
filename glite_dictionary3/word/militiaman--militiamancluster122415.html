militiaman

noun
male member of a militia
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪʃəmæn / (AmE), / mɪˈlɪʃəmən / (BrE)
a male member of a militia who serves, typically on a part-time basis, especially during emergencies.

Examples

armed militiaman
As a militiaman, he trained on weekends and returned to his regular job during the week.
During the unrest, every able-bodied militiaman was called to defend the town.
The local militiaman responded quickly when the alarm was raised in the village.
