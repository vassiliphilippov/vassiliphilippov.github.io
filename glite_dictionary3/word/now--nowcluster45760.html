now

adverb
used to preface a command or request
Pronunciation: / naʊ / (AmE), / naʊ / (BrE)
used to introduce a command, request, or new topic, or to get attention or add emphasis

Examples

Now pay attention
Now, don't forget to call your grandmother tonight.
Now, listen carefully to what I have to say.
Now, please take your seats so we can begin the meeting.
Now for something completely different: here’s the weather forecast.
Now, let’s move on to the next point on the agenda.
Now, what were you saying about your holiday plans?
