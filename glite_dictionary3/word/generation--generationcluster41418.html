generation

noun
stage of technological development
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a stage or period in the development or innovation of technology, especially as marked by a distinct set of features, capabilities, or design improvements

Examples

generation of smartphones
Each generation of gaming consoles brings significant improvements over the last.
The company has just released a new generation of electric cars with advanced features.
The first generation of mobile phones was much bulkier than the ones we have today.
