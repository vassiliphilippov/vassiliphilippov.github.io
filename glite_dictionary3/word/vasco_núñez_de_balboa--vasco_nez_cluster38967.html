Vasco Núñez de Balboa

proper noun
Spanish explorer; first European to see Pacific east shores
Pronunciation: / ˈvæsˌkoʊ ˈnuːnˌjez də bælˈboʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˈvæskəʊ ˈnjuːnjɛz də bælˈbəʊ.ə / (BrE)
(1475–1519) A Spanish explorer who crossed the Isthmus of Darien in 1513 and became the first European to see the eastern shores of the Pacific Ocean.

Examples

Vasco Nunez de Balboa crossed Panama
Many historians consider Vasco Núñez de Balboa a pivotal figure in the Age of Exploration.
Students learned about Vasco Núñez de Balboa and his journey across the Isthmus of Darien in history class.
The expedition led by Vasco Núñez de Balboa reached the Pacific Ocean in 1513.
