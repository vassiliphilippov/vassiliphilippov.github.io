cybernetic

adjective
relating to cybernetics
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪ.bɚˈnɛt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪ.bəˈnɛt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the science of cybernetics, especially the study of systems, control, and communication in living organisms and machines.

Examples

cybernetic control systems
Her thesis focuses on the cybernetic control systems within artificial intelligence.
In science fiction, cybernetic organisms often blur the line between human and machine.
The team is exploring the use of cybernetic enhancements to improve physical abilities.
