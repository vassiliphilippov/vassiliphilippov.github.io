blond

noun
light grayish yellow color
Pronunciation: / blɑnd / (AmE), / blɒnd / (BrE)
a light grayish yellow to near white color, often associated with hair, wood, or fabrics.

Examples

blond wood color
She chose a blond wood for the dining table to keep the look light and airy.
The artist mixed a touch of white to achieve the perfect shade of blond.
The walls were painted a soft blond that made the room feel brighter.
