psychedelic

adjective
characterized by vivid, hallucinatory mental states
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈdɛlɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈdɛlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or producing intense, vivid, and distorted perceptions or altered states of consciousness, often caused by certain drugs
