slipcover

noun
removable cloth cover for furniture
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpˌkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɪpˌkʌvə / (BrE)

Examples

slipcover for a sofa
Changing the slipcover is an easy way to update the room's look.
