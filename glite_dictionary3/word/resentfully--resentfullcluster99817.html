resentfully

adverb
in a resentful manner
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɛntfəli / (AmE), / rɪˈzɛntfəli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows bitterness or indignation at having been treated unfairly.

Examples

resentfully accept criticism
He resentfully handed over the last piece of cake to his younger brother.
She resentfully agreed to help her colleague despite feeling overlooked for the promotion.
The critic resentfully admitted that the play was better than anticipated.
