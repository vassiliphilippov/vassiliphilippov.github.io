to realise

verb
musically expand a figured bass
Pronunciation: / ˈriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɪə.laɪz / (BrE)
to expand or complete (a part in a piece of baroque music) by supplying the harmonies indicated in the figured bass

Examples

to realise a bass line
He carefully realised the part, adding imaginative chords to the original bass line.
Scholars often debate how best to realise the harmonies in early baroque works.
The continuo player was asked to realise the figured bass for tonight's performance.
