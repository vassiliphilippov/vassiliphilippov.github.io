kurta

noun
loose collarless shirt from South Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrtə / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtə / (BrE)
A loose, collarless shirt, typically knee-length, worn by men and women on the Indian subcontinent, often paired with a salwar, churidar, or pyjama.

Examples

traditional kurta outfit
He wore a crisp white kurta to the festival.
Many tourists buy a kurta as a souvenir when visiting India.
She paired her embroidered kurta with matching leggings for a traditional look.
