special

noun
featured dish or meal (restaurant)
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
a dish or meal that is featured and given particular prominence on a menu, usually for a limited time

Examples

special of the day
The restaurant offers a different special every Friday night.
They always check the special before ordering from the regular menu.
Today's special is grilled salmon with seasonal vegetables.
