to incrust

verb
decorate or cover lavishly
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkrʌst / (AmE), / ɪnˈkrʌst / (BrE)
to decorate or cover lavishly with a layer of something, especially gems or ornamental material

Examples

to incrust a dress with jewels
She incrusted her dress in jewels for the gala event.
The artisan incrusted the box with intricate patterns of mother-of-pearl.
The gold crown was incrusted with brilliant emeralds and rubies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – encrust: to cover or decorate with a hard layer or coating
