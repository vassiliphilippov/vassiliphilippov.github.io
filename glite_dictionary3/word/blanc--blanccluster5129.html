blanc

noun
white sauce of fat, broth, and vegetables
Pronunciation: / blɑːŋk / (AmE), / blɑːŋk / (BrE)
A white sauce made from fat, broth, and vegetables, used especially to cook or serve braised meat.

Examples

blanc sauce for meat
A traditional blanc gives the meat a delicate flavor and keeps it moist.
She learned to prepare blanc as part of her French cooking class.
The veal was simmered gently in a rich blanc until tender.
