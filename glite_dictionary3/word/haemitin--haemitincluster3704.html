haemitin

noun
red iron-containing pigment in blood
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːmɪtɪn / (AmE), / ˈhiːmɪtɪn / (BrE)
a complex red pigment containing iron, found in hemoglobin, which binds to oxygen in the blood
