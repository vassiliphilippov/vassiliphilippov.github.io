neo-baroque

noun
art or architecture imitating Baroque style
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊ bəˈroʊk / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊ bəˈrəʊk / (BrE)
A style in art or architecture that is inspired by or imitates the forms, motifs, and characteristics of 17th-century Baroque.
