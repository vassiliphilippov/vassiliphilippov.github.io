Ionian

noun
ancient Greek inhabitant of Attica or Ionia
Pronunciation: / aɪˈoʊniən / (AmE), / aɪˈəʊniən / (BrE)
a member of the ancient Greek people called the Ionians, known for their distinctive dialect and culture
