takeaway

noun
an act of gaining possession from the opponent (sports)
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈteɪkəˌweɪ / (BrE)
an act of gaining possession of the ball or puck from the opposing team, especially by intercepting a pass, in sports such as football or hockey

A crucial takeaway in the final minute secured their team's victory.
