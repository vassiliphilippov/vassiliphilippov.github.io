plastic

adjective
easily influenced or shaped
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪk /; / ˈplæs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk /; / ˈplɑː.stɪk / (BrE)
easily influenced or adaptable; able to change or be changed (especially of a person, mind, or character)
