to curtsy

verb
to bend the knees as a respectful greeting
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrtsi /; / ˈkɜːrtsi / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtsi / (BrE)
to lower your body slightly by bending your knees, usually as a polite greeting or sign of respect (especially by women or girls)
