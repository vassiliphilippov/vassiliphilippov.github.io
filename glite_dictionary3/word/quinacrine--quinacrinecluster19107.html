quinacrine

noun
a drug for worm infestations and malaria
Pronunciation: / kwɪˈnækrin / (AmE), / kwɪˈnækrɪn / (BrE)
a synthetic drug used to treat certain parasitic worm infestations and formerly used as an antimalarial medication
