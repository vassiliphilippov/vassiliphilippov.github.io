lacking

adjective
not present or missing
Pronunciation: / ˈlækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlækɪŋ / (BrE)
not present or not available; missing or absent

Examples

lacking any evidence
His enthusiasm was lacking during the important meeting.
Many essential details were lacking from the report.
The dish was tasty but lacking in spices.

Related Terms

  • synonym – absent: not present in a place, at an occasion, or as part of something
  • antonym – abundant: present in large quantities; more than enough
  • synonym – missing: not able to be found because it is not in its expected place
