fruitcake

noun
eccentric or crazy person (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈfruːtkeɪk / (BrE)
A person who behaves in a very eccentric, odd, or crazy way, often in a whimsical or unusual manner (informal, often humorous).

Examples

total fruitcake
Don't pay attention to her wild theories—she's harmless, just a bit of a fruitcake.
Everyone in the office thinks Harry's a fruitcake because of his bizarre sense of humor.
My uncle is a real fruitcake, always coming up with the weirdest inventions.

  • synonym – nutcase: an eccentric or crazy person
