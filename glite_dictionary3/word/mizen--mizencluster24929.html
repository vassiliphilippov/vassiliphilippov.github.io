mizen

noun
sail set on the mizzenmast
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪzən / (AmE), / ˈmɪzᵊn / (BrE)
A fore-and-aft sail set on the mizzenmast of a sailing vessel.

Examples

mizen sail set
During the storm, the mizen was torn but the other sails remained intact.
He adjusted the mizen for better balance while steering the ship.
The crew quickly hoisted the mizen to take advantage of the changing wind.
