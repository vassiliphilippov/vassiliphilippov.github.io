Menopon palladum

noun
a louse species parasitic on poultry
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛnəˈpɒn ˈpælədəm / (AmE), / ˌmɛnəˈpɒn ˈpalədəm / (BrE)
a species of louse that is parasitic on poultry, feeding on their skin and feathers

Examples

Menopon palladum infests chickens
