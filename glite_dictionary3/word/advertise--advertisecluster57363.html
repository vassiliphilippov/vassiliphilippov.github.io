to advertise

verb
promote a product or service
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.vərˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.taɪz / (BrE)
to make the public aware of a product, service, or event in order to encourage people to buy or use it

Examples

to advertise a product
Many businesses advertise special offers to attract new customers.
She was hired to advertise the concert on social media platforms.
The company plans to advertise the new product in national newspapers and magazines.
