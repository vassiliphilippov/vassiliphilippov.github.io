to objectify

verb
treat as an object or make impersonal
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to treat someone as an object or a thing, without regard to their personhood or individuality; to make something impersonal.

Examples

to objectify women
Many critics argue that certain advertisements objectify women and reduce them to mere products.
Some people worry that technology may objectify our relationships by removing emotional connections.
The film was accused of trying to objectify its characters instead of portraying them as complex individuals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – depersonalize: make impersonal or remove individuality
  • antonym – humanize: to make more human or emphasize human characteristics
