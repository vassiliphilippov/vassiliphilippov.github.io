fontanelle

noun
soft gap in an infant's skull
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑːn.təˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˌfɒn.təˈnɛl / (BrE)
a soft membranous gap between the bones of a baby's skull where bone formation is not complete, allowing for growth of the brain and skull

Examples

baby's fontanelle
A bulging fontanelle may be a sign of increased pressure in an infant's brain.
The anterior fontanelle usually closes by 18 months of age.
The doctor gently examined the baby's fontanelle during the checkup.
