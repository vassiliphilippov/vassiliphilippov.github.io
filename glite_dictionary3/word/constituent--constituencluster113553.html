constituent

noun
an element or component of a whole
Pronunciation: / kənˈstɪtʃuənt / (AmE), / kənˈstɪtjʊənt /; / kənˈstɪtjuənt / (BrE)
a part or component of something, especially one that is necessary for it to exist or function as a whole
