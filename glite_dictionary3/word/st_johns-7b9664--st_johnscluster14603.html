St. Johns

proper noun
major river in northeastern Florida
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒɒnz / (BrE)
A major river in northeastern Florida that flows north to Jacksonville and then east to the Atlantic Ocean.
