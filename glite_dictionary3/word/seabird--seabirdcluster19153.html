seabird

noun
a bird that lives near or on the sea
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈsiːbɜːd / (BrE)
a bird that lives near the sea or spends much of its life on or over the ocean, such as gulls, pelicans, or albatrosses

Examples

a seabird colony
Many seabirds nest on remote islands to avoid predators and human disturbance.
The photographer captured a stunning image of a seabird gliding above the crashing waves.
We watched a seabird dive into the water to catch its lunch.
