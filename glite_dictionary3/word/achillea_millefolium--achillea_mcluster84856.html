Achillea millefolium

noun
a herbaceous plant; yarrow
Pronunciation: / əˈkɪliə ˌmɪliˈfoʊliəm / (AmE), / əˈkɪliə ˌmɪlɪˈfəʊlɪəm / (BrE)
A Eurasian herb, commonly known as yarrow, with strong scent, feathery leaves, and small white or pink flowers, often found in meadows and naturalized in North America.
