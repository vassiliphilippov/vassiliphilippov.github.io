unbroken

adjective
not broken; whole and intact
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbroʊkən / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrəʊkən / (BrE)
not damaged or divided; whole and intact; in one piece.

Examples

unbroken vase
She treasured the antique vase, which was still unbroken after all these years.
The horse galloped across an unbroken field, free from fences or obstacles.
The windowpane was remarkable for remaining unbroken after the storm.

  • synonym – intact: remaining whole or undamaged
