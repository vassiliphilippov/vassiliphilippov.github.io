counterproposal

noun
alternative proposal made in response
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərprəˈpoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəprəˈpəʊzəl / (BrE)
A proposal made in response to and as an alternative to an earlier proposal.

Examples

make a counterproposal
During the negotiation, each side presented a counterproposal to address unresolved issues.
He reviewed her counterproposal before making a final decision on the contract.
