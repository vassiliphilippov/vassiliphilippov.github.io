to equilibrize

verb
bring into balance or equilibrium
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪl.əˌbraɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪl.ɪˌbraɪz / (BrE)
To bring into a state of balance or equilibrium.

Examples

to equilibrize the temperature
Modern air conditioning systems automatically equilibrize the temperature throughout the building.
The technician must equilibrize the pressure on both sides of the valve before proceeding.
To ensure fairness, the coach tried to equilibrize the teams based on skill level.
