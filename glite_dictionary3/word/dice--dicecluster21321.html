dice

noun
small cube used in games of chance
Pronunciation: / daɪs / (AmE), / daɪs / (BrE)
A small cube with its six faces marked with spots from 1 to 6, used in games of chance to generate random numbers.

Examples

roll the dice
A pair of loaded dice was discovered at the gambling table.
He rolled the dice to see who would go first in the game.
The game requires you to throw two dice and move your piece accordingly.

  • synonym – die: singular form for one cube used in games of chance
