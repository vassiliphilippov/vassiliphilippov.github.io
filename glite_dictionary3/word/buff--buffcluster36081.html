buff

noun
soft, thick, undyed leather
Pronunciation: / bʌf / (AmE), / bʌf / (BrE)
a soft, thick, undyed leather made from the skin of buffalo, oxen, or similar animals, often used for polishing or in military uniforms

Army officers traditionally wore gloves made of buff for formal occasions.
