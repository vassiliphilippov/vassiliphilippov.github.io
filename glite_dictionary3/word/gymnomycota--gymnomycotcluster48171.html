Gymnomycota

noun
group of slime molds (taxonomy)
Pronunciation: / dʒɪm.noʊ.maɪˈkoʊ.tə /; / dʒɪmˌnoʊmaɪˈkoʊtə / (AmE), / dʒɪm.nəʊ.maɪˈkəʊ.tə /; / dʒɪmˌnəʊmɪˈkəʊtə / (BrE)
a group or division of slime molds with a noncellular, multinucleate vegetative phase and a spore-producing stage, including Myxomycetes and Acrasiomycetes
