to fustigate

verb
to strike with a cudgel
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌstɪˌɡeɪt / (AmE), / ˈfʌstɪɡeɪt / (BrE)
to strike or beat with a cudgel or similar weapon

Examples

to fustigate an enemy
He warned the boys that further mischief would earn them a fustigating in the courtyard.
The rowdy crowd watched as the guard fustigated the thief with his club.
The villain threatened to fustigate anyone who dared approach him.

  • antonym – caress: to touch or stroke gently
  • synonym – cudgel: a short, thick stick used as a weapon
