Canadian

proper noun
a river in New Mexico, Texas, and Oklahoma
Pronunciation: / kəˈneɪdiən / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən / (BrE)
the Canadian River; a river rising in northeastern New Mexico, flowing east across the Texas panhandle and into Oklahoma, where it becomes a tributary of the Arkansas River
