respects

noun
formal expressions of esteem or courtesy
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛkts / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛkts / (BrE)
formal expressions or acts of esteem or courtesy, often as part of a visit or ceremony, especially in the phrase 'pay your respects'.

Examples

pay respects at a funeral

Many people came to the funeral to pay their respects to the late professor.
