agile

adjective
able to move quickly and easily
Pronunciation: / ˈædʒaɪl / (AmE), / ˈædʒaɪl / (BrE)
able to move quickly and easily; nimble or light-footed.

Examples

agile dancer
At 80 years old, her grandfather is still surprisingly agile.
Thanks to her agile movements, she dodged the falling branches with ease.
The gymnast was remarkably agile on the balance beam.

Related Terms

  • antonym – clumsy: awkward in movement or handling things
  • synonym – nimble: quick and light in movement or action
