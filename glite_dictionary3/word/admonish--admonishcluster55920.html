to admonish

verb
warn or advise firmly
Pronunciation: / ədˈmɑːnɪʃ /; / ædˈmɑːnɪʃ /; / ədˈmɑnɪʃ / (AmE), / ədˈmɒnɪʃ / (BrE)
to warn or tell someone firmly that they are doing something wrong, especially to help them improve or correct their behavior
