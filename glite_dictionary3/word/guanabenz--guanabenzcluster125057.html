guanabenz

noun
antihypertensive medication
Pronunciation: / ɡwɑnəˌbɛnz / (AmE), / ɡwænəˌbɛnz / (BrE)
An antihypertensive drug that lowers blood pressure by acting on the central nervous system; formerly marketed under the trade name Wytensin.
