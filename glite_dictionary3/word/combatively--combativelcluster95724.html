combatively

adverb
in an aggressive or fighting manner
Pronunciation: / kəmˈbætɪvli / (AmE), / kəmˈbætɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a readiness or eagerness to fight or argue

Examples

speak combatively
She answered his questions combatively, making it clear she would not be intimidated.
The two politicians debated combatively, each refusing to yield their position.
Throughout the meeting, he spoke combatively and challenged every suggestion.
