malahini

noun
newcomer to Hawaii
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːləˈhini / (AmE), / ˌmaːləˈhiːni / (BrE)
a person who is a newcomer to Hawaii, especially one who is not native to the islands

Examples

new malahini in Hawaii
As a malahini, she found the customs of Hawaii both fascinating and challenging.
Despite being a malahini, he quickly adapted to the Hawaiian way of life.
Local residents often welcome malahinis and teach them about island traditions.
