bulimia

noun
an eating disorder involving binge eating and compensatory behaviors
Pronunciation: / bjuˈlɪmiə / (AmE), / bjuˈlɪmɪə / (BrE)
a serious eating disorder characterized by episodes of excessive eating (binges) followed by attempts to avoid weight gain, such as vomiting, fasting, or excessive exercise

Examples

struggle with bulimia
Her struggle with bulimia began in her late teens, affecting her physical and emotional health.
Many support groups offer help to those suffering from bulimia and related eating disorders.
The doctor explained the long-term health risks associated with bulimia to her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anorexia: an eating disorder characterized by an abnormally low body weight and intense fear of gaining weight
