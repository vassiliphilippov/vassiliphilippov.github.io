foetology

noun
study of the fetus in the womb
Pronunciation: / fiːˈɒlədʒi / (AmE), / fiːˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medicine that studies the fetus within the uterus, focusing on development and health before birth.

Examples

foetology research center
Advancements in foetology have made prenatal diagnosis much more accurate.
She specialized in foetology to better understand fetal development and maternal health.
