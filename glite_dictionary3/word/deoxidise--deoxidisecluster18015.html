to deoxidise

verb
remove oxygen from a substance
Pronunciation: / diˈɑksɪdaɪz / (AmE), / diˈɒksɪdaɪz / (BrE)
to remove oxygen from a substance, especially chemically; to reduce or cause loss of oxygen

Examples

to deoxidise metal
Certain processes can deoxidise water to prevent corrosion.
Manufacturers often deoxidise steel to improve its quality.
The chemist used hydrogen to deoxidise the sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym – oxidise: to combine or become combined with oxygen
  • synonym – reduce: to cause a chemical reduction; to gain electrons or lose oxygen
