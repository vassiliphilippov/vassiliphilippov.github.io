landowner

noun
person who owns land
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈlændˌəʊnə / (BrE)
A person who owns land, especially large amounts of rural land.

Examples

wealthy landowner
As a major landowner, she influenced many decisions in the village.
Conflicts sometimes arise between landowners and tenants over land use rights.
The new landowner invested heavily in local agriculture.
