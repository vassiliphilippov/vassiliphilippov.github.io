feeling

noun
vague opinion or belief
Pronunciation: / ˈfilɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
an opinion or belief that is not based on certain knowledge or fact, often a vague or instinctive impression

Examples

feeling about something
His feelings about the company's decision were mostly positive.
I have a feeling that we will hear from her soon, even though she hasn't called.
She couldn't explain her feeling, but she trusted her instincts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – impression: an idea, feeling, or opinion about something or someone
