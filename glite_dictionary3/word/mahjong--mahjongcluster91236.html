mahjong

noun
Chinese tile-based game for four players
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːˈʒɔŋ / (AmE), / ˌmæˈʒɒŋ / (BrE)
A Chinese game for four players, played with 144 small tiles marked in different designs, where players aim to collect sets and pairs.

Examples

play mahjong with friends
Every Sunday, her family gathers to play mahjong for hours.
He learned the rules of mahjong while studying in Shanghai.
The sound of clattering mahjong tiles filled the apartment.
