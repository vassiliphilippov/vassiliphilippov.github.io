to chance

verb
happen accidentally or unintentionally
Pronunciation: / tʃæns / (AmE), / tʃɑːns / (BrE)
to happen to do something or find something by accident or without intention

Examples

to chance upon treasure
I chanced to find the letter while cleaning out the attic.
She chanced upon a rare book in the old shop.
We chanced to see a shooting star while walking home.
She chanced on an old photograph hidden behind the bookshelf.
We chanced upon a lovely little cafe while exploring the old town.
While walking in the forest, he chanced to find a rare wildflower.
