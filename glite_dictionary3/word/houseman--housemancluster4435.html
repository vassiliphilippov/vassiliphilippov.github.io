houseman

noun
medical graduate in hospital training
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsmən / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsmən / (BrE)
a medical graduate or advanced student undergoing supervised practical training in a hospital, often as an initial stage of postgraduate education

After finishing medical school, she worked as a houseman in a London teaching hospital.
Many housemen find the first year of clinical work both challenging and rewarding.
The responsibilities of a houseman include attending ward rounds and assisting senior doctors.
