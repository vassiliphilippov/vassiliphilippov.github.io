apportioning

noun
act of distributing or allocating
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔr.ʃən.ɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpɔː.ʃən.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of dividing and distributing something among people or groups, especially according to a plan or set of rules

Examples

apportioning resources fairly
Accurate apportioning of resources is essential during a crisis.
Debate arose over the fair apportioning of funds among the project teams.
The apportioning of responsibilities was clearly outlined in the contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allocation: the process of giving out resources or duties
  • synonym – distribution: the act of sharing something out among a number of recipients
