Philodendron

noun
genus of tropical climbing plants
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪləˈdɛndrən / (AmE), / ˌfɪləˈdɛndrən / (BrE)
A genus of tropical American climbing plants with smooth, shiny, evergreen leaves, often grown as ornamental houseplants.

Examples

Philodendron houseplant
Many species within the Philodendron genus thrive in low-light environments.
The large, glossy leaves of a Philodendron make it a popular choice for offices.
We added a Philodendron to the living room to bring more greenery indoors.
