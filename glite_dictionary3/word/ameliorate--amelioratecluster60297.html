to ameliorate

verb
make or become better; improve
Pronunciation: / əˈmiliəˌreɪt /; / əˈmiːliəˌreɪt / (AmE), / əˈmiːliəreɪt / (BrE)
to make something better or improve a bad situation

Examples

to ameliorate a problem
Despite their efforts, the reforms only partially ameliorated the economic crisis.
Effective communication can ameliorate conflicts in the workplace.
The new healthcare policy should ameliorate the living conditions of many citizens.
He sought to ameliorate the situation by offering a sincere apology.
International aid helped to ameliorate the crisis after the natural disaster.
The new policies are designed to ameliorate living conditions in the city.
