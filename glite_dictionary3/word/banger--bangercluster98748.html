banger

noun
energetic popular song (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈbæŋɡə / (BrE)
Informal/slang: a very popular, energetic, and highly catchy song or track, especially one played in clubs, on the radio, or online.

Examples

play a banger song
The DJ dropped a new banger and the dance floor instantly filled with people.
Streaming playlists labeled party are full of modern bangers that keep listeners energized all night.
Critics called the chorus a perfect banger because it was catchy and impossible to forget.
