florid

adjective
elaborately ornate or decorated
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrəd / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪd / (BrE)
Elaborately or excessively decorated, ornate, or showy, especially in style or language.

Examples

florid writing style

Their living room was decorated in a florid style, with intricate patterns everywhere.
