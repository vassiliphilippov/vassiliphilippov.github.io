to cover

verb
play a higher card than the previous
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə / (BrE)
(in card games) to play a card of a higher rank than the one just played by another player.

Examples

to cover a card
He couldn't cover the king, so he lost the trick.
If your opponent plays a ten, you can cover it with a jack or queen.
She always tries to cover my cards when we play bridge.
