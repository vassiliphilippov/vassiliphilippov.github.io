to toe

verb
move one's toes to a position or direction
Pronunciation: / toʊ / (AmE), / təʊ / (BrE)
to place or move the toes so that they are in a particular position or direction, especially while walking or standing

Examples

to toe inward
He always toes inwards when he's nervous.
Make sure you toe straight ahead during the race.
The dancer had to toe outwards for the routine.
