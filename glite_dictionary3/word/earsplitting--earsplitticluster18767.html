earsplitting

adjective
extremely loud; painfully loud
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌsplɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌsplɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely loud; so loud as to be painful or to cause temporary hearing loss.

Examples

earsplitting noise
She covered her ears to block out the earsplitting music at the concert.
The earsplitting crash of thunder made everyone jump in fright.
The construction site was filled with earsplitting noises all day long.

  • antonym – quiet: making little or no noise
