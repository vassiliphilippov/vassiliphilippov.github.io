rattling

noun
series of short, loud sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈræt̬.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈræt.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a rapid series of short, loud sounds, especially those heard in the chest in certain respiratory disorders

Examples

rattling chest sounds
A faint rattling could be heard through the stethoscope as she breathed.
The doctor detected a rattling in the patient's lungs during the examination.
The nurse listened carefully for any sign of rattling when monitoring his recovery.
