crispness

noun
visual sharpness or image clarity
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪspnəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪspnəs / (BrE)
the visual sharpness or perceived clarity of an image, video, or display, especially the distinctness of edges, fine detail, and overall image definition.

Examples

crispness of an image
The photographer praised the monitor's crispness when displaying high-resolution landscape images.
During the test, viewers reported the video's crispness improved noticeably after adjusting the bitrate.
Modern smartphones emphasize screen crispness to make text and icons appear sharper and clearer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – image clarity: the overall clearness and visibility of details in a picture or video
  • synonym – sharpness: the quality of having clearly defined edges and fine detail in an image
