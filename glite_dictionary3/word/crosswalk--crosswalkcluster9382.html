crosswalk

noun
marked crossing area for pedestrians
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔːsˌwɑk / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌwɔːk / (BrE)
a specially marked part of a road, usually at an intersection, where pedestrians are intended to cross safely

Examples

busy crosswalk
Always look both ways before stepping onto the crosswalk.
Drivers are required to stop for pedestrians in the crosswalk.
The new crosswalk made it much safer for children to reach the park.
