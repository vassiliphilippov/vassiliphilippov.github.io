freckle

noun
small brownish skin spot
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛk.əl / (AmE), / ˈfrɛk.əl / (BrE)
A small, light brown spot on the skin, often becoming more pronounced from exposure to sunlight.

Examples

freckle on skin
After a day in the sun, more freckles appeared on his cheeks.
His face was dotted with freckles from childhood.
She has a cute freckle on the tip of her nose.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blemish: a small mark or flaw on the skin
