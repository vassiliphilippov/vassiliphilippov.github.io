bondswoman

noun
female bound servant or slave
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑndzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbɒndzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is held in slavery or forced to serve another person without pay; a female slave or servant
