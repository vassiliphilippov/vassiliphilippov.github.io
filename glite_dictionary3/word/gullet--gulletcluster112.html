gullet

noun
the tube carrying food from mouth to stomach
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌl.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌl.ɪt / (BrE)
the tube that carries food from the mouth to the stomach; the esophagus

Examples

food down the gullet
He felt a sharp pain in his gullet after swallowing the fish bone.
She took a large gulp of water, feeling it rush down her gullet.
The food passes through the gullet on its way to the stomach.
