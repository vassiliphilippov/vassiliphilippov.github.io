charitably

adverb
in a kind and generous way
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃær.ɪ.t̬ə.bli / (AmE), / ˈtʃær.ɪ.tə.bli / (BrE)
In a kind and generous manner, showing charity or forgiveness toward others.

Examples

to judge charitably
Even when insulted, he tried to judge others charitably.
She spoke charitably about her rivals, despite their criticisms.
The teacher interpreted the student's late homework charitably, giving him the benefit of the doubt.

Related Terms

  • synonym – generously: in a generous manner, giving freely
  • antonym – harshly: in a cruel or severe way
