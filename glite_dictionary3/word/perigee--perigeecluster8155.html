perigee

noun
point closest to Earth in orbit
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛr.ɪ.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈpɛr.ɪ.dʒiː / (BrE)
the point in the orbit of a satellite or celestial object at which it is nearest to the Earth

Examples
