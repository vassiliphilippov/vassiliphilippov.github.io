dealing

noun
manner of conduct toward others
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the way in which a person behaves or conducts themselves when interacting with others, especially in matters of fairness or honesty

Examples

fair dealing with people
Her reputation for dealing with clients fairly earned her many loyal customers.
In all his dealing with staff, he was known for his kindness and integrity.
The company's honest dealing set it apart from its competitors.
