lifesaver

noun
ring-shaped life preserver
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌseɪvər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌseɪvə / (BrE)
a life preserver in the form of a ring made of buoyant material, used to rescue someone at risk of drowning

Examples

lifesaver ring on boat
Each boat is required by law to have at least one lifesaver on board.
She tossed a lifesaver to the man struggling in the lake.
The child grabbed the lifesaver that was thrown into the pool.

Related Terms

  • synonym – life preserver: a device designed to keep a person afloat in water
