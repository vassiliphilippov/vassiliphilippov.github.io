huckaback

noun
absorbent towel fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌk.əˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhʌk.ə.bæk / (BrE)
A coarse, absorbent cotton or linen fabric, typically used for making towels and similar items.

huckaback towel fabric
