rauwolfia

noun
medicinal shrub of the genus Rauwolfia
Pronunciation: / raʊˈwʊlfiə / (AmE), / raʊˈwʌlfiə / (BrE)
Any shrub or small tree of the genus Rauwolfia, having leaves in whorls and cymose flowers, whose roots yield substances used medicinally, especially as emetics, purgatives, or antihypertensives.

Examples

rauwolfia shrub leaves
Many antihypertensive drugs have been derived from rauwolfia plants.
The roots of rauwolfia are dried and processed for medicinal use.
Traditional healers have long used extracts of rauwolfia to treat various ailments.
