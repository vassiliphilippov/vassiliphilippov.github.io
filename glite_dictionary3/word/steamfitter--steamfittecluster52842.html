steamfitter

noun
worker installing pipes for heating/cooling systems
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːmˌfɪtər / (AmE), / ˈstiːmˌfɪtə / (BrE)
a skilled worker who installs and maintains pipes and equipment used for heating, ventilating, or refrigeration systems

Examples

steamfitter installs pipes
A steamfitter must be familiar with blueprints and complex piping systems.
After years of apprenticeship, he finally qualified as a steamfitter in the construction industry.
The hospital hired a steamfitter to upgrade their heating and cooling equipment.
