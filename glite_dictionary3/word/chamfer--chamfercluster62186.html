to chamfer

verb
cut a sloping edge on a material
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæmfər /; / ˈtʃæm.fər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːmfə /; / ˈtʃæm.fə / (BrE)
to cut or shape a sloping or beveled edge along the edge or corner of a material
