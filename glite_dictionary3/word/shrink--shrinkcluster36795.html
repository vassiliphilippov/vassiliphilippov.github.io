to shrink

verb
move back as from fear or pain
Pronunciation: / ʃrɪŋk / (AmE), / ʃrɪŋk / (BrE)
to move back or away suddenly, especially as a reaction to fear or pain

Examples

to shrink back quickly
He shrinks whenever someone tries to touch his injured shoulder.
She couldn't help but shrink away when the dog barked loudly.
The child shrunk from the injection, squeezing his mother's hand tightly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cringe: to move or bend one's body in fear or in a servile manner
  • synonym – flinch: to react physically by drawing back suddenly, as from pain or fear
