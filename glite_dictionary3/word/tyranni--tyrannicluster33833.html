Tyranni

noun
a suborder of birds (suboscines)
Pronunciation: / tɪˈræn.aɪ / (AmE), / tɪˈran.ɪ / (BrE)
A suborder of birds comprising the New World flycatchers, antbirds, ovenbirds, and woodhewers; also known as suboscines.
