postgraduate

noun
a student studying after a first degree
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈɡrædʒuət / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈɡrædʒuət / (BrE)
a student who is studying for a degree or other qualification after having completed their first degree

Examples

postgraduate student
After receiving her bachelor's degree, she became a postgraduate in environmental science.
He is a postgraduate looking to specialize in biomedical engineering.
Many postgraduate students are involved in groundbreaking research at the university.

Related Terms

  • antonym – undergraduate: a university student who has not yet received a first degree
