soapstone

noun
soft, compact mineral used for ornaments, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊpˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpstəʊn / (BrE)
a soft, heavy, compact mineral variety of talc with a soapy feel, used for hearths, tabletops, and ornaments
