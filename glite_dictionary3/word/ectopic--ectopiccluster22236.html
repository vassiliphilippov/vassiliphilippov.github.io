ectopic

adjective
occurring in an abnormal position
Pronunciation: / ɛkˈtɑpɪk / (AmE), / ɛkˈtɒpɪk / (BrE)
(of a body part or process) occurring in an abnormal position or place; displaced from its usual location.

Examples

An ectopic heartbeat can sometimes cause palpitations or irregular heart rhythms.
