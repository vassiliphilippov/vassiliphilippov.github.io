seafront

noun
area of a town by the sea
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.frʌnt / (AmE), / ˈsiː.frʌnt / (BrE)
the part of a town or city that is next to the sea, especially where people go for walks or where there are hotels and entertainment

Examples

walk along the seafront
Shops and cafes line the busy seafront during the summer months.
The hotel has a stunning view overlooking the seafront.
