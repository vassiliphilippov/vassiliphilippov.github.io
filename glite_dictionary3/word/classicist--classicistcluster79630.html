classicist

noun
advocate or practitioner of classicism
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈklæsɪsɪst / (BrE)
an artist or thinker who practices or supports classicism, especially in art, literature, or architecture

Examples

classicist painter
As a classicist, he rejects modern styles in favor of traditional forms and ideals.
Many classicists admire the balance and clarity found in Renaissance art.
She is a renowned classicist whose paintings evoke the harmony of ancient Greece.
