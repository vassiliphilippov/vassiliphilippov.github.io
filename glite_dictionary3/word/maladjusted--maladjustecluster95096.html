maladjusted

adjective
unable to adjust to life's demands
Pronunciation: / mæləˈdʒʌstɪd /; / ˌmæl.əˈdʒʌs.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌmaləˈdʒʌstɪd /; / ˌmal.əˈdʒʌs.tɪd / (BrE)
not able to cope well with social or emotional demands; having difficulty adapting to normal life (especially about people)

Examples

A maladjusted teenager may have trouble building lasting friendships.

The school provides counseling for maladjusted students who struggle to fit in.
