somesthesia

noun
sense of bodily sensation
Pronunciation: / səˈmiːsθiə /; / soʊmɛsˈθiːʒə / (AmE), / səʊˈmiːsθiə /; / səʊmɛsˈθiːzɪə / (BrE)
the ability to feel bodily sensations such as touch, pressure, pain, temperature, position, and feelings from internal organs
