leafless

adjective
having no leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈlifləs / (AmE), / ˈliːfləs / (BrE)
(of a plant or tree) having no leaves.

Examples

leafless tree
By late autumn, the trees in the park stood leafless and silent.
He drew a picture of a leafless tree to represent the cold season.
The leafless branches reached up to the gray winter sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bare: without the usual covering or leaves
  • antonym – leafy: having many leaves
