pimento

noun
sweet red pepper used in cooking
Pronunciation: / pɪˈmɛntoʊ / (AmE), / pɪˈmɛntəʊ / (BrE)
A fully ripened sweet red pepper, typically red in color, often used cooked or stuffed in foods such as olives and cheeses.

Examples

pimento stuffed olives
She stuffed the olives with pimento before serving them at the party.
Sliced pimento is often added to salads for color and sweetness.
The cheese spread had a mild pimento flavor that everyone enjoyed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bell pepper: a large, mild, sweet pepper, often green, red, or yellow
