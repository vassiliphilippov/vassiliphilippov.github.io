reddened

adjective
having become red, especially skin or eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdnd / (AmE), / ˈrɛd(ə)nd / (BrE)
(of skin or eyes) having become red, especially as a result of emotion, exertion, or irritation.

Examples

reddened eyes from crying
After a long day in the sun, his skin was noticeably reddened.
He avoided eye contact, embarrassed by his reddened face.
Her reddened cheeks showed that she had been crying for some time.
