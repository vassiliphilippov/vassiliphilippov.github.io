confrontation

noun
hostile opposition between groups
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.frənˈteɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkɑn.frənˈteɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkɑːnfrənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.frʌnˈteɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
a situation where people or groups are in conflict, opposition, or have a direct and often hostile disagreement
