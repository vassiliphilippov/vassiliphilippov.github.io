sneering

adjective
showing contempt; scornful
Pronunciation: / ˈsni.rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsniə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
Showing or expressing contempt or scorn, often through facial expression or tone of voice.

Examples

sneering smile
He gave her a sneering look that made her feel uncomfortable.
Her sneering remarks angered everyone in the room.
His sneering tone made it clear he did not respect their opinions.
