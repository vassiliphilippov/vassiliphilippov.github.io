Tyan Shan

proper noun
mountain range in Central Asia
Pronunciation: / tiˌæn ˈʃɑːn / (AmE), / tiˌæn ˈʃɑːn / (BrE)
a major mountain range in Central Asia, extending about 1,500 miles across several countries including China, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan.

Examples

the Tyan Shan mountains
Scientists have conducted important climate research in the Tyan Shan region.
The Tyan Shan mountains are known for their dramatic peaks and glaciers.
The scenery of the Tyan Shan range attracts hikers and nature lovers from around the world.
