treadmill

noun
exercise machine for walking or running in place
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛdˌmɪl / (AmE), / ˈtrɛd.mɪl / (BrE)
an exercise machine with a moving belt on which a person walks or runs in place

Examples

run on a treadmill
After injuring her knee, she started walking on the treadmill for rehabilitation.
He set the treadmill to a steep incline for a more challenging workout.
Many people prefer to use a treadmill at the gym when the weather is bad.

Related Terms

  • synonym – elliptical: an exercise machine for simulated stair climbing, walking, or running
