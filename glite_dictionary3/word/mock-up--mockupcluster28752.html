mock-up

noun
full-size model for study or testing
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːk ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmɒk ʌp / (BrE)
a full-size model or replica of something, built for study, testing, or display, often used in design or engineering

Examples

mock-up of a product
The architect showed us a full-size mock-up of the building's entrance.
The team created a mock-up of the new smartphone before starting production.
We presented a mock-up of the exhibition layout to the client for approval.

Related Terms

  • synonym – prototype: an original model on which something is patterned
