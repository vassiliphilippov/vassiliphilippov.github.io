memsahib

noun
European woman in colonial India
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛmˌsɑːhɪb / (AmE), / ˈmem.sɑː.hɪb / (BrE)
(historical, in South Asia) a term of respect for a European woman, especially the wife of a British official.
