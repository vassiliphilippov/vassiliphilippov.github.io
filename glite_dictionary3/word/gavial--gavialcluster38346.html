gavial

noun
large Indian crocodilian with long snout
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪ.vi.əl / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪ.vi.əl / (BrE)
A large fish-eating crocodilian native to India and nearby regions, with a long slender snout.

Examples

a gavial in a river
Scientists recently discovered a population of gavials in a remote stretch of river.
The gavial is easily recognized by its extremely long, narrow snout.
Unlike other crocodilians, the gavial feeds almost exclusively on fish.
