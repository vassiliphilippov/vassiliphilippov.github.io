Neobiotic

noun
antibiotic drug; intestinal antiseptic
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊbaɪˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊbaɪˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
A trademarked antibiotic obtained from an actinomycete, used as a sulfate form as an intestinal antiseptic during surgery.
