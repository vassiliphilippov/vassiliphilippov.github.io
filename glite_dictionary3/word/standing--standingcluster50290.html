standing

adjective
supported by a base; upright (not attached)
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an object) having a base that supports it in an upright position, not attached to a wall or ceiling.

Examples

standing fan
A standing fan can be moved from room to room as needed.
The exhibition featured a standing sculpture rather than one mounted on the wall.
We installed a standing lamp in the corner of the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mounted: fixed on a base for support or display
