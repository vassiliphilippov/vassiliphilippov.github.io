cerecloth

noun
waterproof waxed cloth (historical shroud)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪrˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈsɪə.klɒθ / (BrE)
A waterproof waxed cloth, especially one used in the past as a shroud for covering dead bodies.

Examples
