activity

noun
effectiveness of a substance in a reaction (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ækˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ækˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
(Chemistry) The effective concentration or capacity of a substance to participate in a chemical reaction, often differing from its actual concentration due to interactions.

Examples

chemical activity rate
A decrease in activity can indicate the presence of inhibitors in the reaction system.
Scientists measured the activity of the catalyst under different conditions.
The activity of the enzyme increases at higher temperatures.
