grovelling

adjective
excessively submissive or self-abasing
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːv(ə)lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒv(ə)lɪŋ / (BrE)
behaving in an excessively submissive way, showing an abject willingness to please or obey someone

Examples

grovelling apology
He offered a grovelling apology to his boss after the mistake was discovered.
Her grovelling attitude towards the manager made others uncomfortable.
The letter was filled with grovelling requests and desperate pleas for forgiveness.
