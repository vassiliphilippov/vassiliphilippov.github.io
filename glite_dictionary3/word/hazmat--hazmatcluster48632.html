HAZMAT

noun
abbreviation for hazardous material
Pronunciation: / ˈhæzˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈhæzˌmæt / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'hazardous material', used especially on warning signs to indicate the presence of dangerous substances
