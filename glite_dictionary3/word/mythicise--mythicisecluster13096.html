to mythicise

verb
interpret as a myth
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθɪˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪθɪsʌɪz / (BrE)
to make something into a myth or treat it as a myth; to give something mythical status

Examples

Scholars warn against attempts to mythicise the past, urging a more critical perspective.
Writers often mythicise historical figures to highlight their importance.
