cussedness

noun
obstinate contrariness or stubbornness
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌsɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈkʌsɪdnɪs / (BrE)
a stubborn, deliberately contrary, or disagreeable attitude; obstinate contrariness or willfulness

Examples

cussedness and stubbornness
His cussedness was legendary among those who tried to change his mind.
Out of pure cussedness, she refused to agree with any suggestion offered.
There was a certain cussedness in his refusal to ask for help even when he needed it.

  • synonym – contrariness: the state of being deliberately contrary
  • synonym – obstinacy: stubborn refusal to change one's opinion
