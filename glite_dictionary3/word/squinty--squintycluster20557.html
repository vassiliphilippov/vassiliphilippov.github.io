squinty

adjective
looking sideways with suspicion or envy
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɪn.ti / (AmE), / ˈskwɪn.ti / (BrE)
(of a glance or look) directed to one side, often with doubt, suspicion, or envy.

Examples

squinty glance
He gave me a squinty glance when I mentioned the missing money.
Her squinty eyes made it clear she didn't quite believe my story.
The cat watched the newcomer with a squinty suspicion from the corner of the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – askance: with an attitude or look of suspicion or disapproval
  • synonym – sidelong: looking or directed to one side
