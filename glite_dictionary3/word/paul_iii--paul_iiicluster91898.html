Paul III

proper noun
Italian pope (1534–1549), Counter-Reformation leader
Pronunciation: / pɔl ðə ˈθɜrd / (AmE), / pɔːl ði ˈθɜːd / (BrE)
(1468–1549) Italian pope from 1534 to 1549, notable for excommunicating Henry VIII, initiating the Council of Trent, and supporting the Counter-Reformation.
