vulgar

adjective
pertaining to the common people
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈvʌlɡə / (BrE)
relating to the common people or to ordinary, everyday language (not formal or refined)

Examples

vulgar language use

Some critics dismissed the poet's work as being too vulgar to be considered true art.
