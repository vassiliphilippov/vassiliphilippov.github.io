footage

noun
recorded film or video material
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtɪdʒ / (BrE)
film or video material that has been recorded, especially for use in movies, television, or news broadcasts.

Examples

raw footage
Documentary filmmakers often rely on archival footage to tell their stories.
She reviewed hours of surveillance footage to find the suspect.
The news station played dramatic footage of the rescue operation.
