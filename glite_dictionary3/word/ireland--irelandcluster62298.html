Ireland

proper noun
country in western Europe; Republic of Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərlənd / (AmE), / ˈaɪələnd / (BrE)
A country occupying most of the island of Ireland in the North Atlantic, west of Great Britain; officially known as the Republic of Ireland, it gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1921.

Examples

the country of Ireland
Many famous poets and writers have hailed from Ireland throughout history.
She traveled across Ireland to visit her family in Cork and Dublin.
The government of Ireland introduced new environmental policies last year.
