Niger

proper noun
country in West Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈniːʒə, naɪˈdʒɛə / (BrE)
a landlocked country in West Africa, largely covered by the Sahara Desert, which became independent from France in 1960

Examples

the country of Niger
French is the official language of Niger, reflecting its colonial history.
Much of Niger consists of arid desert, making agriculture especially difficult.
The president of Niger addressed the United Nations about climate change and security.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Nigeria: a neighboring country in West Africa, also named after the Niger River
