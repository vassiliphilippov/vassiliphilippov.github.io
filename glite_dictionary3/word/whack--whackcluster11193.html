to whack

verb
strike forcefully
Pronunciation: / wæk / (AmE), / wæk / (BrE)
to hit someone or something with a loud, forceful blow

Examples

to whack a ball
He whacked the ball over the fence with his bat.
Please don't whack the remote against the table; it might break.
The chef whacked the dough onto the table before kneading it.
