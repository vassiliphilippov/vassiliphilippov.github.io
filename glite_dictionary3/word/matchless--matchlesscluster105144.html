matchless

adjective
without equal; unrivaled
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃlɪs / (AmE), / ˈmatʃlɪs / (BrE)
so good that nothing or no one else is as good; without equal or rival

Examples

matchless beauty
His matchless performance earned him a standing ovation.
The artist was renowned for her matchless creativity and vision.
Visitors marveled at the matchless beauty of the mountain landscape.

  • antonym – ordinary: common, not exceptional or special
