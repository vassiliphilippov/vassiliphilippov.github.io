placative

adjective
meant to pacify or appease
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪkətɪv / (AmE), / ˈpleɪkətɪv / (BrE)
intended to pacify or appease, especially by making concessions or yielding to demands.

Examples

placative gesture
He made several placative gestures in an attempt to calm the angry crowd.
Her placative tone helped resolve the heated discussion peacefully.
The company issued a placative statement to address the workers' complaints.
