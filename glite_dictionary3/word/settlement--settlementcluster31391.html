settlement

noun
ending a business by paying off liabilities
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt̬.əl.mənt /; / ˈsɛtəlmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛt.əl.mənt /; / ˈsɛtəlmənt / (BrE)
the process of finalizing payments, trades, or liquidating assets in business or finance

Examples

financial settlement process
