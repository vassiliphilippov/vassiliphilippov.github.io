to itemise

verb
to list things separately
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪtəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈaɪtəmaɪz / (BrE)
to list or mention things separately, often with details for each item

Examples

to itemise each expense
Please itemise all your expenses for the trip in the report.
The accountant will itemise each deduction on your tax return this year.
They asked the contractor to itemise every cost in the proposal for transparency.
Please itemise each product on the invoice with its corresponding price.
She carefully itemised the damages caused during the move.
The accountant helped me itemise all of my business expenses for the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – enumerate: to mention (a number of things) one by one
  • synonym – itemize: to list or mention items separately (American spelling)
  • antonym – summarise: to present the main points without detailed listing
