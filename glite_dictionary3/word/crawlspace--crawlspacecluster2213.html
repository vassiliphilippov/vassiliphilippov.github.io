crawlspace

noun
low space beneath a building's floor
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔlˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈkrɔːlˌspeɪs / (BrE)
A narrow, unfinished space beneath the floor of a building, typically used to access wiring, plumbing, or ventilation.
