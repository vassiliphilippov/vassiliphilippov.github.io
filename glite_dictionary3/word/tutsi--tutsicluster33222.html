Tutsi

noun
member of an ethnic group in Rwanda and Burundi
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːtsi / (AmE), / ˈtʊtsi / (BrE)
a member of a people of Central Africa, primarily living in Rwanda and Burundi, traditionally speaking Bantu languages

Examples

Tutsi people of Rwanda
After the genocide, many Tutsi families returned to Rwanda from exile.
She studied the traditions of the Tutsi during her fieldwork in Burundi.
The documentary focused on the relationship between the Tutsi and Hutu communities.

  • antonym – Hutu: a member of a different major ethnic group in Rwanda and Burundi
