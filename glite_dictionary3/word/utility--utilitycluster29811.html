utility

noun
practical usefulness
Pronunciation: / juˈtɪl.ə.ti /; / juːˈtɪləti / (AmE), / juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti /; / juːˈtɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being useful or valuable; the usefulness or satisfaction something provides (including in economics)

Examples

utility value
Before buying a new car, consider the utility and reliability it offers.
Researchers debated the utility of the proposed method in real-world situations.
The utility of this new software has transformed the way we work.
Different individuals may assign different levels of utility to the same product.
In economics, utility is used to quantify the satisfaction people get from consuming goods.
Maximizing utility is a fundamental principle in consumer choice theory.
