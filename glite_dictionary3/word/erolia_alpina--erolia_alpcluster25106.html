Erolia alpina

noun
species of migratory sandpiper
Pronunciation: / ɛˈroʊliə ælˈpiːnə / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊliə ælˈpiːnə / (BrE)
A small sandpiper species that breeds in northern or Arctic regions and migrates to southern United States or Mediterranean areas for winter.
