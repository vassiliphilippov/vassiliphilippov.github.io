illness

noun
condition affecting health
Pronunciation: / ˈɪlnəs / (AmE), / ˈɪlnəs / (BrE)
A condition that affects the normal functioning of a body or mind, often marked by identifiable symptoms.

Examples

serious illness

Her illness kept her home from work for several days.
The doctor is trying to diagnose the cause of his mysterious illness.
