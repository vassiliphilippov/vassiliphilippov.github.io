brachycephalic

noun
person with a short, broad head
Pronunciation: / ˌbræki səˈfæl ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbræk ɪ səˈfæl ɪk / (BrE)
An adult whose head is short and broad in proportion to its length; a person with a brachycephalic skull.

Examples

brachycephalic person
Certain breeds of dogs, as well as some humans, may be classified as brachycephalic.
The anthropologist noted that the remains belonged to a brachycephalic individual.
The medical report described the patient as brachycephalic based on skull measurements.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dolichocephalic: an adult with a long, narrow head
