pedicab

noun
three-wheeled passenger vehicle powered by pedalling
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdɪˌkæb / (AmE), / ˈpiːdɪkæb / (BrE)
a small three-wheeled vehicle, typically propelled by pedalling, used for carrying passengers for hire

Examples

The tourists enjoyed a ride through the city in a pedicab.
