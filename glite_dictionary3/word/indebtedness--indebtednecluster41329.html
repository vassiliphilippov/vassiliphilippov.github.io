indebtedness

noun
the state of owing money
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the condition of owing money to someone else; the state of being in debt

Examples

heavy indebtedness
He managed to clear his indebtedness after years of careful budgeting.
Rising levels of indebtedness have become a concern for many households.
The company's indebtedness forced it to restructure its finances.

Related Terms
