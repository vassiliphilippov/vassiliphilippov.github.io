Saint Luke

proper noun
Apostle and author of the third Gospel
Pronunciation: / seɪnt luːk / (AmE), / seɪnt luːk / (BrE)
the Apostle of the New Testament closely associated with Saint Paul and traditionally regarded as the author of the third Gospel
