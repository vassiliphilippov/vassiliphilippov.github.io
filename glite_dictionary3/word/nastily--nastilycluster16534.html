nastily

adverb
in an unpleasant or spiteful way
Pronunciation: / ˈnæstɪli / (AmE), / ˈnɑːstɪli / (BrE)
In an unpleasant, spiteful, or ill-tempered manner.

Examples

to speak nastily
He nastily criticized her presentation in front of everyone.
She glared nastily at her rival before leaving the room.
The children were arguing nastily over who got the last cookie.
