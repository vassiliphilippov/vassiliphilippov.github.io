manhood

noun
state of being a man; manly qualities
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈman.hʊd /; / ˈmæn.hʊd / (BrE)
the state of being a man (not a boy), or the qualities traditionally associated with men such as courage or strength
