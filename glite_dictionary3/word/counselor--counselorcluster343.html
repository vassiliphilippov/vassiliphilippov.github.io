counselor

noun
person who gives advice or guidance
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.səl.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl.ə / (BrE)
a person trained to give advice and guidance about personal, social, or psychological problems.

Examples

school counselor
After her parents' divorce, she spoke to a counselor at school to help her cope.
As a counselor, he listens patiently and offers practical solutions to his clients' concerns.
The company provides access to a professional counselor for employees seeking support.

Related Terms

  • synonym – advisor: a person who gives advice in a particular field
  • synonym – therapist: a person trained to treat mental or psychological disorders
