sourpuss

noun
habitually sullen or grumpy person
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊərˌpʊs / (AmE), / ˈsaʊəpʊs / (BrE)
a person who is habitually sullen, gloomy, or grumpy

Examples

Don’t be such a sourpuss—try to enjoy the party for once.
My little brother turned into a real sourpuss after losing the game.
