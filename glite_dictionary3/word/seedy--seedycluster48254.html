seedy

adjective
sordid or disreputable; morally degraded
Pronunciation: / ˈsidi /; / ˈsiːdi / (AmE), / ˈsiːdi / (BrE)
(of a place or person) shabby, dirty, or run-down, and possibly connected to unpleasant or illegal activities

Examples
