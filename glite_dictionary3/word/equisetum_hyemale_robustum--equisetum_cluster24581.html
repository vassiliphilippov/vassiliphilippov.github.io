Equisetum hyemale robustum

noun
a subspecies of evergreen horsetail plant
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkwəˈsiːtəm haɪˈɛməli roʊˈbʌstəm / (AmE), / ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtəm hʌɪˈɛməli rə(ʊ)ˈbʌstəm / (BrE)
a subspecies of evergreen horsetail plant with rough-edged, erect stems, formerly used for scouring utensils because of its abrasive texture
