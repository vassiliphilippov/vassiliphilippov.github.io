speakeasy

noun
modern hidden or password bar
Pronunciation: / spi kizi / (AmE), / spi ki zi / (BrE)
A modern, often hidden or password-protected bar or lounge that deliberately evokes the look and atmosphere of Prohibition-era secret bars; usually legal and marketed as exclusive or themed.

The new cocktail lounge is a stylish speakeasy with a password entry.

She prefers quiet, hidden speakeasy venues that focus on craft cocktails and atmosphere.
