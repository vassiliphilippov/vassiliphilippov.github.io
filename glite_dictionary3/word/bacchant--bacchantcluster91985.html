bacchant

noun
drunken reveller, follower of Bacchus
Pronunciation: / bəˈkænt /; / ˈbækənt / (AmE), / ˈbækənt /; / ˈbakənt / (BrE)
a person who takes part in wild, drunken festivities or revelry, especially in the style of Bacchus
