dispute

noun
serious argument or disagreement
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspjut / (AmE), / dɪˈspjuːt / (BrE)
a serious argument or disagreement, especially about something important or lasting a long time

Examples

a dispute over money
His contract renewal has become the subject of much dispute among the board members.
The border dispute between the neighboring countries has lasted for decades.
The two companies are involved in a legal dispute over the rights to the patent.
The neighbors had a long-standing dispute over the boundary line between their properties.
The union entered into a dispute with management over pay and working conditions.
Their bitter dispute eventually led to a lawsuit.
