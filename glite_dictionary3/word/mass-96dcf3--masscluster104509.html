mass

noun
the ordinary people; the populace
Pronunciation: / mæs / (AmE), / mæs / (BrE)
the ordinary people in society, as opposed to the wealthy, powerful, or elite

Examples

the mass of people
Some artists try to create works that appeal to the mass rather than a select few.
The politician promised to represent the interests of the mass rather than the elite.
Throughout history, revolutions have often been led by the mass seeking change.

Related Terms

  • antonym – elite: a group that is superior in terms of ability or qualities to the rest of a group or society
  • synonym – populace: the ordinary people in a country or area
