to take

verb
require time, space, or effort
Pronunciation: / teɪk / (AmE), / teɪk / (BrE)
to require, need, or be able to hold or contain something (such as time, space, or capacity)

Examples

The theater only takes about 100 people, so book your tickets early.
Will this suitcase take all of my clothes for the trip?
