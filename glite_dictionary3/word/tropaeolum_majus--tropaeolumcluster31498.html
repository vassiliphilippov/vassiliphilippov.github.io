Tropaeolum majus

noun
a climbing plant with orange-yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / troʊˌpiːoʊˈluːm ˈmædʒəs / (AmE), / trəʊˌpiːəˈləʊləm ˈmædʒəs / (BrE)
