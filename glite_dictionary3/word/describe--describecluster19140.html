to describe

verb
to trace or mark out a shape or line
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskraɪb / (AmE), / dɪˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to mark out or trace the outline of (a shape, figure, or path) on a surface, especially in geometry or technical contexts

Examples

to describe a circle
As the stick moved through the sand, it described a winding path.
The architect described an arc to show the curve of the ceiling.
Using a compass, the student described a perfect circle on the paper.
