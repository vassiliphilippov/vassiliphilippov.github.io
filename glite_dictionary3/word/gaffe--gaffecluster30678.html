gaffe

noun
an embarrassing social or diplomatic mistake
Pronunciation: / ɡæf / (AmE), / ɡaf / (BrE)
an unintentional act or remark causing embarrassment to its originator; a social or diplomatic blunder

Examples

embarrassing gaffe at work
During the interview, he made a gaffe that offended several viewers.
She apologized immediately after realizing her gaffe at the formal dinner.
The politician's latest gaffe was widely reported in the media.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blunder: a stupid or careless mistake
  • antonym – compliment: a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym – faux pas: an embarrassing or tactless act or remark in a social situation
