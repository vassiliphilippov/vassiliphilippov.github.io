Sion

proper noun
hill in Jerusalem; name for Jerusalem
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.ən / (BrE)
A hill in Jerusalem that was originally a stronghold captured by King David, later the site of the Temple, and by extension a synonym for Jerusalem itself.

Examples

Jerusalem's Sion hill
In biblical poetry, Sion is frequently praised as the city of God.
Pilgrims traveled to Sion to see the ancient sites of Jerusalem.
The ruins on Sion remind visitors of the city's storied past.
