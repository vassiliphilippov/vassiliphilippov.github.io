arachnoid

noun
spider-like arthropod with eight legs
Pronunciation: / əˈræk.nɔɪd / (AmE), / əˈræk.nɔɪd / (BrE)
a member of the class of arthropods that includes spiders, scorpions, mites, and ticks, typically having eight legs and simple eyes
