impedimenta

noun
things that hinder progress
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɛdəˈmɛntə /; / ɪmˌpɛdəˈmɛnə / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɛdɪˈmɛntə / (BrE)
objects, equipment, or baggage that make movement or progress more difficult, especially when they are unnecessary or heavy
