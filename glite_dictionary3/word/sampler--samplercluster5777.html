sampler

noun
device or station for collecting samples
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmplɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmplə / (BrE)
a device or station used to collect samples or make measurements for analysis, especially in scientific or environmental studies

Examples

air sampler device
Researchers visited each site to maintain the water sampler and collect data.
The air quality sampler was installed on the rooftop to monitor pollution levels.
The laboratory uses an automated sampler to gather soil samples at regular intervals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sample: a small part or quantity intended to show what the whole is like
