there

adverb
in that matter or respect
Pronunciation: / ðɛr / (AmE), / ðeə / (BrE)
Used to refer to a particular matter, respect, or point just mentioned or under discussion.

Examples

good point there
He made a good point there about the importance of teamwork.
I think your analysis was excellent; I completely agree with you there.
She was right there—the project did need more funding.
