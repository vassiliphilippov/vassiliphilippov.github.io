Dentaria bulbifera

noun
a European bittercress plant species
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈtɛəri.ə ˌbʌl.bɪˈfɪr.ə / (AmE), / dɛnˈtɛːrɪə ˌbʌlbɪˈfɪərə / (BrE)
