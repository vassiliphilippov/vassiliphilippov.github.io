hocus-pocus

noun
meaningless magic chant
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊkəsˈpoʊkəs / (AmE), / ˌhəʊkəsˈpəʊkəs / (BrE)
A meaningless chant or incantation, especially used by magicians or in conjuring tricks.

Examples

hocus-pocus magic words
He muttered some hocus-pocus before producing a coin from behind my ear.
Many people dismissed the fortune teller's words as nothing but hocus-pocus.
The magician waved his wand and uttered hocus-pocus, astonishing the children with his trick.
