flyswat

noun
implement for killing insects
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌswɑt / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌswɒt / (BrE)
a handheld implement with a flat, often perforated, surface and a long handle, used for killing or swatting flies and other insects

Examples

flyswat a fly
He used a flyswat to chase the buzzing insect around the kitchen.
She grabbed the flyswat as soon as she saw a mosquito land on the windowsill.
The old flyswat hung on a hook by the back door.
