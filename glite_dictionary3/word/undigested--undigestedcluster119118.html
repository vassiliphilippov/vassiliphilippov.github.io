undigested

adjective
not digested or processed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈdʒɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈdʒɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of food or information) not processed, absorbed, or assimilated by the digestive system or the mind.
