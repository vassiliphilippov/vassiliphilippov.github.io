to ace

verb
to score a hole in one (in golf)
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
to hit a ball into the hole with a single stroke, especially in golf; to achieve a hole in one

Examples

to ace a hole
After acing the par-three, he celebrated with his friends.
He managed to ace the ninth hole during the tournament.
She hopes to ace at least one hole every season.
