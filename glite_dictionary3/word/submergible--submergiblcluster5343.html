submergible

adjective
able to be submerged or function underwater
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɝːdʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / səbˈmɜːdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be immersed in water or continue to function while underwater.

Examples

submergible pump
Engineers recommend only using submergible pumps for draining large swimming pools.
The new aquarium features a submergible lighting system to illuminate the tank from below.
Their research used a submergible camera to observe the coral reefs without disturbing marine life.
