to empathize

verb
understand another's feelings
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmpəˌθaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛmpəθaɪz / (BrE)
to understand and share another person's feelings or experiences

Examples

to empathize with others
As a counselor, she often empathizes with students facing academic challenges.
He tried to empathize by imagining what it must feel like to move to a new country.
It's important to empathize with people going through difficult times.
