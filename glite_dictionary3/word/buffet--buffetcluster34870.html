to buffet

verb
strike repeatedly and forcefully
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌf.ɪt /; / ˈbʌfɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʌf.ɪt /; / ˈbʌfɪt / (BrE)
to strike or hit something repeatedly and with force, especially by wind or waves
