outwards

adverb
toward the outside
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈaʊtwədz / (BrE)
in a direction toward the outside or away from a center or interior.

Examples

move outwards from center
She stretched her arms outwards to greet the morning sun.
The doors swung outwards, allowing the crowd to exit quickly.
The ripples spread outwards from the stone thrown into the pond.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inwards: toward the inside or center
  • synonym – outward: toward the outside (alternative spelling/usage)
