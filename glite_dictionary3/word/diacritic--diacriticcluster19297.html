diacritic

noun
mark added to a letter for pronunciation or distinction
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪ.əˈkrɪ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʌɪ.əˈkrɪ.tɪk / (BrE)
A sign or mark added to a letter to alter its pronunciation or to distinguish it in some way.

Examples

diacritic mark on a letter
Adding the correct diacritic is important when spelling words in French.
Some languages use a diacritic to distinguish vowel sounds.
