to square

verb
make something square or adjust to right angles
Pronunciation: / skwɛr / (AmE), / skweə /; / skwɛə / (BrE)
to make or check that something is straight, aligned, or forms a right angle

Examples

to square the edges
Before marking the wood, you need to square the edges with a file.
She used a ruler to square the paper before cutting it into smaller pieces.
The builder carefully squared the corners of the frame to ensure stability.
Before the photograph, she squared the picture frame on the wall.
He squared his shoulders and walked into the room with confidence.
The movers carefully squared the heavy boxes with the edge of the truck for easy loading.
