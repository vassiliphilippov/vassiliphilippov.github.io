healing

adjective
tending to cure; restorative
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
Having the quality of curing or restoring to health.

Examples

healing touch
Many people believe in the healing power of nature when recovering from illness.
She recommended a healing ointment for my skin irritation.
Yoga and meditation can have a healing effect on both mind and body.

Related Terms

  • synonym – curative: having healing or remedial properties
  • antonym – harmful: causing harm or damage; the opposite of healing
  • synonym – therapeutic: relating to the healing of disease
