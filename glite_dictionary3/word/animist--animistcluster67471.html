animist

adjective
relating to animism
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪmɪst / (AmE), / ˈænɪmɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of animism or its believers.

Examples

animist traditions
Some anthropologists study animist beliefs among indigenous peoples.
The animist worldview sees spirits present in all aspects of the natural world.
The traditional rituals of the tribe are deeply animist in nature.
