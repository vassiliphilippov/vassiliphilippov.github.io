Nasser

proper noun
large artificial lake in Egypt and Sudan
Pronunciation: / ˈnæsər / (AmE), / ˈnasə / (BrE)
a large artificial lake in southern Egypt and northern Sudan, created by the construction of the Aswan High Dam on the Nile River

Examples

Nasser Lake
Many Nile crocodiles can be found in the waters of Nasser.
The construction of the Aswan High Dam led to the creation of Nasser.
The villages near Nasser were relocated due to the flooding caused by the dam.
