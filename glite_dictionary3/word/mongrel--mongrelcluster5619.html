mongrel

noun
dog of mixed breed
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋɡrəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋɡrəl / (BrE)
A dog of mixed or indeterminate breed, often regarded as inferior.

Examples

friendly mongrel dog
Her favorite companion was a loyal mongrel with a patchy coat.
Some owners prefer a mongrel for its unique personality and hardiness.
The family adopted a mongrel from the animal shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mutt: an informal synonym meaning a dog of mixed breed
  • antonym – purebred: a dog of known and unmixed ancestry
