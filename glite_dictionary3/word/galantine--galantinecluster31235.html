galantine

noun
a cold, stuffed boned poultry dish
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlæntiːn / (AmE), / ɡəˈlæntiːn / (BrE)
A dish of boned poultry that is stuffed, cooked, covered with aspic, and served cold.

Examples

cold galantine dish
At the buffet table, the elaborately decorated galantine was the centerpiece.
