underbred

adjective
inferior or mixed breed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɚˈbrɛd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈbrɛd / (BrE)
of inferior or mixed breed; not purebred or lacking good breeding

Examples

underbred dog
Some breeders look down on underbred animals, but they can make wonderful pets.
The judge disqualified the underbred hound from the competition due to its ancestry.
The shelter took in several underbred puppies from the local area.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crossbred: produced by mating animals of different breeds or varieties
  • antonym – purebred: of an animal, bred from parents of the same breed or variety
