to feast

verb
eat sumptuously; join a banquet
Pronunciation: / fiːst / (AmE), / fiːst / (BrE)
to eat and drink sumptuously, often as part of a large meal or celebration

Examples

to feast on dishes
After the ceremony, the guests feasted on a variety of traditional dishes.
The soldiers feasted to celebrate their victory.
We feast every Thanksgiving with our extended family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – banquet: a formal large meal for many people, often in celebration
  • antonym – fast: to abstain from all or some kinds of food or drink
