sequoia

noun
wood of a giant California conifer
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkwoʊ.i.ə / (AmE), / sɪˈkwɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
The wood of either of two huge coniferous trees native to California, known for their great height and longevity.

Examples

sequoia wood
Conservation efforts now restrict the commercial harvest of sequoia in California.
Some furniture makers prize sequoia for its beautiful reddish color and grain.
The house was built using rare sequoia for the beams and paneling.

Related Terms

  • synonym – redwood: common name for several species of large coniferous trees, especially Sequoia sempervirens
