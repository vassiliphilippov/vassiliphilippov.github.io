housemaid

noun
female domestic worker in a household
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs.meɪd / (BrE)
A female domestic worker employed to do cleaning and other household tasks in someone's home.

Examples

a housemaid cleans rooms
She worked as a housemaid in London during her twenties.
The housemaid started her chores early every morning.
The wealthy family employed a housemaid to help with the laundry and cleaning.
