offspring

noun
product or result of something
Pronunciation: / ˈɑfˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɒfsprɪŋ / (BrE)
something that is produced as a result of a particular process, idea, or activity; a product or outcome.

Examples

offspring of ideas
Many modern genres of music are the creative offspring of earlier traditions.
The nation's constitution is the offspring of years of struggle and compromise.
This school was the offspring of a community effort to improve education.
