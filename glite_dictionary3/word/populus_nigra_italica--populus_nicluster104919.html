Populus nigra italica

noun
columnar black poplar cultivar; Lombardy poplar
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːpjʊləs ˈnaɪɡrə ɪˈtælɪkə / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊləs ˈnaɪɡrə ɪˈtælɪkə / (BrE)
A fastigiate (narrow, columnar) cultivar of black poplar, known for its tall, upright form and erect branches; commonly called the Lombardy poplar.

Populus nigra italica lines a driveway
