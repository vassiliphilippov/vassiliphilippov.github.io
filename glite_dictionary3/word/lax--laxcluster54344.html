lax

adjective
easily emptied (of bowels)
Pronunciation: / læks / (AmE), / læks / (BrE)
(of the bowels or similar organs) tending to empty easily or excessively.

Examples

lax bowels
Doctors may describe the bowels as lax when discussing gastrointestinal issues.
Some patients become lax after changes in their diet or medication.
The medication made her lax, leading to frequent trips to the bathroom.

Related Terms

  • antonym – constipated: unable to empty the bowels easily
