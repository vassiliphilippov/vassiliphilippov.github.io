ignis fatuus

noun
pale light over marshy ground
Pronunciation: / ˈɪɡ.nɪs ˈfætʃ.u.əs /; / ˌɪɡ.nɪs ˈfætʃ.u.əs / (AmE), / ˈɪɡ.nɪs ˈfæt.ju.əs /; / ˌɪɡ.nɪs ˈfætjʊ.əs / (BrE)
a pale, mysterious light seen at night over marshy ground (will-o'-the-wisp), or something that misleads people like an illusion or deceptive hope
