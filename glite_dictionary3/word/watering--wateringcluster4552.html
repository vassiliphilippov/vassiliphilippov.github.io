watering

noun
shedding tears; fluid from the eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.tər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of fluid (such as tears) flowing from the eyes, often involuntarily

Examples

watering eyes
Allergy season is marked by symptoms like watering and itchy eyes.
High pollen counts can lead to watering of the eyes and frequent sneezing.
The spicy food caused intense watering that blurred his vision for a moment.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dryness: lack of moisture; absence of tears or fluid in the eyes
  • synonym – tearing: shedding tears; the secretion of tears from the eyes
