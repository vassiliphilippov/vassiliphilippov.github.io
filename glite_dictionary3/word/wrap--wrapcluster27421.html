wrap

noun
a garment wrapped around the body
Pronunciation: / ræp / (AmE), / ræp / (BrE)
a piece of clothing, such as a shawl or cloak, that is wrapped or draped around the shoulders or body for warmth or style

Examples

silk wrap
A delicate lace wrap completed her elegant outfit.
She wore a silk wrap over her dress to keep warm.
The evening was chilly, so he offered her his wrap.
