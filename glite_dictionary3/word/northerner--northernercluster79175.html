Northerner

noun
person from the northern region
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðɚnɚ /; / ˈnɔrðərnər / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðənə / (BrE)
a person who comes from or lives in the northern part of a country, especially the north of England or the northern United States
