cony

noun
small mammal of rocky uplands (pika)
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊni / (AmE), / ˈkəʊni / (BrE)
a small, short-eared, burrowing mammal found in rocky uplands of Asia and western North America, also known as a pika

Examples

mountain cony chirps
During our hike, we spotted a cony chirping among the boulders.
Some species of cony are threatened by habitat loss.
The cony darted between the rocks to escape its predators.
