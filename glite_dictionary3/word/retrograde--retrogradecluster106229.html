to retrograde

verb
move backward or contrary to norm
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛtrəˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəˌɡreɪd /; / ˈrɛtrəʊˌɡreɪd /; / ˈrɛtrəʊɡreɪd / (BrE)
