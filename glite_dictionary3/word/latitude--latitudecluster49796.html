latitude

noun
imaginary line parallel to the equator
Pronunciation: / ˈlæ.t̬ɪˌtuːd /; / ˈlæt̬əˌtud / (AmE), / ˈlæt.ɪ.tjuːd /; / ˈlætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
the distance north or south of the equator, measured in degrees, or an imaginary line around the Earth showing this distance
