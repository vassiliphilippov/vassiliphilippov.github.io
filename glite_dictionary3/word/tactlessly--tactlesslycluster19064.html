tactlessly

adverb
in a tactless manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtækt.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈtækt.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of tact; without sensitivity when dealing with others or delicate situations.

Examples

speak tactlessly in public
He tactlessly pointed out everyone's mistakes during the meeting, which embarrassed his colleagues.
She tactlessly brought up the topic that everyone was trying to avoid.
The journalist tactlessly questioned the grieving family, causing them even more distress.

Related Terms

  • antonym – tactfully: in a manner showing sensitivity and care for others
  • synonym – tactless: showing a lack of sensitivity in dealing with others
