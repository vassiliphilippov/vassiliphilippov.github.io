unpunctual

adjective
not punctual; late
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpʌŋktʃuəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌŋktjʊəl / (BrE)
not doing something or arriving at the expected or agreed time; not punctual.

Examples

an unpunctual person
Clients expect their lawyers not to be unpunctual with deadlines.
His unpunctual arrival frustrated everyone waiting for the meeting.
