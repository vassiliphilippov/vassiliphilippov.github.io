pepsinogen

noun
inactive precursor of pepsin enzyme
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛpˈsɪnədʒən / (AmE), / ˌpɛpˈsɪnədʒən / (BrE)
an inactive precursor of the enzyme pepsin, produced by cells in the stomach lining and converted to pepsin by hydrochloric acid
