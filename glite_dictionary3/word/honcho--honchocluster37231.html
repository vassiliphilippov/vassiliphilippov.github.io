honcho

noun
person in charge; leader (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑntʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɒntʃəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is in charge or the leader of a group, organization, or activity

Examples

the honcho of a company
Everyone listened carefully when the honcho entered the room to give instructions.
She's the honcho of the project, so any questions should go to her.
The new honcho quickly made changes to boost team morale.
