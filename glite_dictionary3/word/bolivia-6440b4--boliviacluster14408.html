bolivia

noun
variation of canasta allowing melded sequences
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪviə / (AmE), / bəˈlɪviə / (BrE)
A variation of the card game canasta in which sequences (rather than just sets) can be melded.

Examples

bolivia card game
Have you ever tried bolivia with a larger group of players?
In bolivia, you can meld a run of cards in the same suit, unlike in standard canasta.
We played bolivia last night, and the new sequence rules made it more interesting.
