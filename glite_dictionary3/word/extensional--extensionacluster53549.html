extensional

adjective
specified by listing all members or cases
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnʃənəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃənəl / (BrE)
(of a definition or term) specified by listing all the individual objects or members it refers to, rather than by stating the properties they share.

Examples

extensional definition list
An extensional list of colors in the rainbow would simply name each one.
Some dictionaries use extensional approaches while others prefer intensional definitions.
The extensional definition of 'prime number less than 10' is '2, 3, 5, 7'.

Related Terms

  • antonym – intensional: defined by stating properties or characteristics, not by listing cases
