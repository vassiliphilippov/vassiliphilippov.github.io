glory

noun
halo or radiant light around a holy figure's head
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔri / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri / (BrE)
A halo or radiant light depicted around the head of a holy figure, especially in religious art, to signify sanctity.

Examples

glory around a saint
Artists often use gold leaf to depict the glory in religious portraits.
In medieval icons, the glory signifies the divine nature of the figure.
The painting featured a saint with a bright glory encircling her head.

Related Terms

  • synonym – halo: a circle of light around the head of a saint or holy person in art
