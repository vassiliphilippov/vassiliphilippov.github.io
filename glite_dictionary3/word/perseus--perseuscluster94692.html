Perseus

proper noun
a constellation in the northern sky
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜr.sjuːs / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.sjuːs / (BrE)
a conspicuous constellation in the Northern Hemisphere, situated between Auriga and Cassiopeia and crossed by the Milky Way
