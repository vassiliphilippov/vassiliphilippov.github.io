fomite

noun
object that transmits infection
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊˌmaɪt / (BrE)
An object or material, such as clothing, utensils, or furniture, that is likely to carry infection and transmit disease.

Examples

fomite on a doorknob
Careful cleaning of shared toys helps reduce the spread of germs by fomites.
Medical staff disinfect all possible fomites between patient visits.
The virus can survive on a fomite like a doorknob for several hours.
