dressing

noun
sauce for salads
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A sauce, typically either vinaigrette or creamy, served as an accompaniment for salads.

Examples

salad dressing bottle
Creamy Caesar dressing is my favorite for romaine lettuce salads.
He poured a light vinaigrette dressing over the fresh greens.
I like to add ranch dressing to my salad for extra flavor.
