Ponstel

noun
trademarked pain-relief drug (mefenamic acid)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑnˌstɛl / (AmE), / ˈpɒnˌstɛl / (BrE)
A trademarked nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (mefenamic acid) used to relieve mild pain, especially menstrual cramps.

Examples

Ponstel for period pain
