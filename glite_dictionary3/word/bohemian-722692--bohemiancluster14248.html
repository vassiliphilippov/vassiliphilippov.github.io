bohemian

adjective
unconventional in appearance or lifestyle
Pronunciation: / boʊˈhiːmiən / (AmE), / bəʊˈhiːmiən / (BrE)
Unconventional, especially in appearance or lifestyle; not following accepted standards of behavior or dress.

Examples

bohemian lifestyle
He admired her bohemian spirit and refusal to follow society's expectations.
She has always lived a bohemian lifestyle, dressing in bright clothes and traveling wherever she pleased.
Their apartment decor was distinctly bohemian, filled with art, books, and mismatched furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conventional: following accepted customs and proprieties
  • synonym – unconventional: not conforming to accepted customs or practices
