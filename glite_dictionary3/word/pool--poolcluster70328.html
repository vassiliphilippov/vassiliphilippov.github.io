pool

noun
a table game with cues and balls
Pronunciation: / pul / (AmE), / puːl / (BrE)
a game played on a rectangular table with six pockets, in which players use cues to hit balls into the pockets according to specific rules

Examples

pool table game
She is one of the top pool players in the city.
They spent the evening playing pool at the local bar.
We bought a new cue stick for our family pool table.
