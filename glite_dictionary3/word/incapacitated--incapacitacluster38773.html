incapacitated

adjective
unable to act or function; powerless
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kəˈpæs.ɪˌteɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kəˈpæs.ɪˌteɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
deprived of strength or power; unable to function or act normally, often due to illness or injury

Examples

incapacitated by illness
After the accident, he was incapacitated and required full-time care.
He was incapacitated by grief and could not attend the meeting.
The flu left her incapacitated for over a week, unable to get out of bed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – capable: having the ability or qualities necessary to do something
  • synonym – helpless: unable to take care of oneself or act independently
  • synonym – powerless: without power or ability to act
