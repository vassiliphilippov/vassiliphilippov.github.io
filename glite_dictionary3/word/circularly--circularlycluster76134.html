circularly

adverb
in a circular manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkjələrli / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊləli / (BrE)
In a way that is shaped like or moves in a circle; in a circular manner.

Examples

move circularly around
Fog swirled circularly above the lake as the morning sun rose.
The current flowed circularly around the drain, pulling leaves with it.
The dancers moved circularly around the maypole during the festival.
