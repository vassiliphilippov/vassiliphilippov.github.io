cowl

noun
metal covering over an engine
Pronunciation: / kaʊl / (AmE), / kaʊl / (BrE)
a protective covering, usually metal, that covers the engine of a vehicle, especially an aircraft

Examples

airplane cowl
After the accident, the cowl had to be replaced before the plane could fly again.
Older motorcycles often have a prominent cowl covering their engines for safety reasons.
The mechanic lifted the cowl to inspect the aircraft’s engine.
