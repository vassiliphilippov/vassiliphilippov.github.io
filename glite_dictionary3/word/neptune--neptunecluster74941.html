Neptune

proper noun
the eighth planet from the Sun
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛp.tun / (AmE), / ˈnɛp.tjuːn / (BrE)
the eighth planet from the Sun in the solar system, a large gaseous planet with a distinct blue color and faint rings of ice particles

Examples

planet Neptune
Astronomers study Neptune to understand the outer solar system.
It takes Neptune about 165 Earth years to orbit the Sun.
