parted

adjective
(of leaves) divided almost to the base
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a leaf or similar structure) having the edge cut or divided nearly to the base, forming distinct divisions or lobes.
