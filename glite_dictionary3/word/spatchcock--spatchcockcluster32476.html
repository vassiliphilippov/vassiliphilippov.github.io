spatchcock

noun
chicken or bird grilled after splitting along the back
Pronunciation: / ˈspætʃ.kɑk / (AmE), / ˈspætʃ.kɒk / (BrE)
A chicken or game bird split open along the back and grilled or roasted, usually soon after being killed.
