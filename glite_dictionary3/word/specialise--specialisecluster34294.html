to specialise

verb
focus on a specific area or activity
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃəlaɪz /; / ˈspɛʃəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəlaɪz / (BrE)
to focus on and become an expert in a particular area of work, study, or activity

to specialise in medicine

This company specialises in the restoration of historic buildings.

Our law firm specialises in environmental cases and sustainable business practices.
