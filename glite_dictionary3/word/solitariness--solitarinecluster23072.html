solitariness

noun
preference for being alone
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪtəˌrines /; / səˈlɪt̬əˌriːnəs / (AmE), / sɒˈlɪtərɪnɪs /; / səˈlɪtərinəs / (BrE)
the state of being alone, either by choice or circumstance; solitude

Examples
