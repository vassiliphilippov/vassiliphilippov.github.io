pyloric

adjective
relating to the pylorus of the stomach
Pronunciation: / paɪˈlɔrɪk / (AmE), / paɪˈlɒrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the pylorus, the opening between the stomach and the small intestine

Examples

pyloric sphincter muscle
A pyloric stenosis can cause severe vomiting in infants.
The surgeon carefully examined the pyloric region for any signs of obstruction.
Ulcers are sometimes found near the pyloric sphincter of the stomach.
