tiresome

adjective
causing boredom or annoyance
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪərsəm / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəsəm / (BrE)
Causing boredom, annoyance, or mental fatigue; uninteresting in a way that makes one feel weary.

Examples

tiresome task
His constant complaining became extremely tiresome after a while.
I find housework especially tiresome when I'm already tired from work.
The meeting was so tiresome that several people struggled to stay awake.

  • synonym – boring: not interesting; tedious or dull
  • antonym – stimulating: encouraging interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym – tedious: too long, slow, or dull; tiresome or monotonous
