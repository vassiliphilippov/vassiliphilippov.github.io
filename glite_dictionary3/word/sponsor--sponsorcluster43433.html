sponsor

noun
person or group supporting or funding something
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑnsər / (AmE), / ˈspɒnsə / (BrE)
a person or organization that supports, finances, or champions somebody or something, especially by providing funds or resources

Examples

sponsor company

The marathon would not have been possible without a generous sponsor to cover the expenses.
