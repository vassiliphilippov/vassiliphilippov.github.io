to fleet

verb
disappear or fade gradually
Pronunciation: / fliːt / (AmE), / fliːt / (BrE)
(literary or archaic) To disappear or pass away gradually; to fade slowly.

Examples

to fleet from memory

The pain in his chest fleeted overnight, much to his relief.
