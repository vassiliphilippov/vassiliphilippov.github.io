sangaree

noun
sweetened wine and citrus mixed drink
Pronunciation: / ˌsæŋɡəˈri / (AmE), / ˌsaŋɡəˈriː / (BrE)
A sweetened alcoholic drink made with red wine and orange or lemon juice, served with soda water.
