snag

noun
unexpected difficulty or obstacle
Pronunciation: / snæɡ / (AmE), / snæɡ / (BrE)
an unexpected difficulty or obstacle that causes a delay or problem

Examples

hit a snag
Everything was going smoothly until we encountered an unexpected snag.
The only snag in our plan was the lack of funding.
The project hit a snag when the supplier missed the deadline.
