evanescence

noun
gradual fading or vanishing
Pronunciation: / ˌɛvəˈnɛsəns / (AmE), / ˌiːvəˈnɛsəns / (BrE)
the process or quality of fading away or vanishing, especially from sight or memory

Examples

the evanescence of beauty
Poets often write about the evanescence of beauty and youth.
The evanescence of memories makes him cherish photographs even more.
The evanescence of the rainbow left the children in awe.

  • synonym – disappearance: the act of ceasing to be visible or to exist
