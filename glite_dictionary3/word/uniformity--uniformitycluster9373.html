uniformity

noun
lack of variation; sameness
Pronunciation: / ˌjunəˈfɔrmɪti /; / ˌjunɪˈfɔrmɪti / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈfɔːmɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being the same or consistent throughout; lack of variation or diversity
