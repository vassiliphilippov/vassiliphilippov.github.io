investigator

noun
a person who investigates
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛstəˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvestɪɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to examine facts or evidence in order to discover the truth about something, especially a crime or scientific matter

Examples

private investigator
As a scientific investigator, she was committed to finding the facts.
The police assigned an experienced investigator to the robbery.
The private investigator uncovered new evidence in the case.

Related Terms

  • synonym – detective: a person, especially a police officer, whose job is to investigate crimes
  • synonym – researcher: a person who carries out academic or scientific investigation
