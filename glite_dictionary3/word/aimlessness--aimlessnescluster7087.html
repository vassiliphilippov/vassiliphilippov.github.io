aimlessness

noun
lack of purpose or direction
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪmləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪmləsnɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having no clear purpose or direction

Examples

feeling of aimlessness
Her aimlessness made it difficult for her to commit to any long-term projects.
The film captures the aimlessness of youth during a long, uneventful summer.
There was a sense of aimlessness in his daily routine after he lost his job.

Related Terms

  • antonym – purposefulness: the quality of having a clear aim or purpose
