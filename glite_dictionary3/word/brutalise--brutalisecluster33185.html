to brutalise

verb
make cruel or insensitive
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbruːtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something cruel, violent, or insensitive, or to become this way (especially after exposure to violence or cruelty)
