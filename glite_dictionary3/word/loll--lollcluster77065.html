to loll

verb
hang or droop loosely
Pronunciation: / lɑl / (AmE), / lɒl / (BrE)
to hang, lean, or droop loosely or in a relaxed manner

Examples

loll out of a window
Long branches lolled over the edge of the old stone wall.
She sat back in the chair, her arms lolling lazily at her sides.
The dog's tongue lolled out of its mouth in the heat.
