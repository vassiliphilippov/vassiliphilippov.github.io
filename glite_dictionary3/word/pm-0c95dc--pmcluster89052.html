PM

adverb
between noon and midnight (12-hour clock)
Pronunciation: / pi ɛm / (AmE), / piː ɛm / (BrE)
used to indicate the time period between noon and midnight in the 12-hour clock system.

Examples

arrive 8 PM
She usually finishes work by 6 PM.
The meeting is scheduled for 3 PM in the conference room.
The train departs at 11:45 PM every night.

Related Terms

  • antonym – AM: used to indicate the time period between midnight and noon
