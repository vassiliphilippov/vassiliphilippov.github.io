bat

noun
a player's turn to hit in baseball
Pronunciation: / bæt / (AmE), / bæt / (BrE)
In baseball, a single turn by a player attempting to hit the ball when it is their opportunity to bat.

Examples

a bat in baseball
Each player gets a bat in every inning.
He injured his wrist during his first bat of the game.
She scored a home run on her third bat of the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym – at-bat: a player's turn batting in baseball, especially used in statistics
