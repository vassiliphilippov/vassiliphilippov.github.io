to crumb

verb
break into small crumbs
Pronunciation: / krʌm / (AmE), / krʌm / (BrE)
to break (something) into small pieces or crumbs

Examples

to crumb a cake
Carefully crumb the cake before adding the frosting.
She crumbed the bread for the casserole topping.
The chef crumbed the biscuits into the bowl.
