harmonization

noun
aligning rules or standards
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːrmənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhɑːmənɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making rules, standards, or procedures consistent across different organisations, jurisdictions, or systems so they work together.

Examples

harmonization of standards
The government's harmonization of product standards reduced cross-border costs for manufacturers.
Industry groups welcomed the harmonization of testing procedures across member states to simplify compliance.
Negotiators argued that harmonization of tax rules would prevent companies exploiting regulatory differences.

Related Terms

  • synonym – regulatory alignment: adjusting regulations so they are consistent across jurisdictions
  • synonym – standardization: the process of developing and implementing technical standards
