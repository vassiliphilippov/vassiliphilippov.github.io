housebreaker

noun
someone who breaks into a house to steal
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
A person who illegally enters another person's house with intent to steal; a burglar.

housebreaker steals valuables
