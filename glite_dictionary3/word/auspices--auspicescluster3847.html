auspices

noun
protection or support, especially by an authority
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːspɪsɪz / (AmE), / ˈɔːspɪsɪz / (BrE)
protection, support, or guidance, especially as given by an organization or authority; sponsorship.

Examples

under the auspices of an organization
Many research projects proceed under the auspices of academic institutions.
Thanks to the auspices of our local sponsors, the program was a success.
The event was organized under the auspices of the United Nations.

Related Terms

  • antonym – opposition: active resistance or dissent; contrast to support or sponsorship
  • synonym – patronage: the support or sponsorship provided by a patron
  • synonym – support: the act of helping or backing someone or something
