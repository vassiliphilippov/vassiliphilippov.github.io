Mentha piperita

noun
peppermint plant; aromatic herb
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛnθə paɪˈpɛrɪtə / (AmE), / ˌmɛnθə pɪˈpɛrɪtə / (BrE)
A herb with downy leaves and small purple or white flowers, known for producing a pungent oil used as a flavoring; commonly known as peppermint.

The essential oil extracted from Mentha piperita is widely used in candies and toothpaste.
