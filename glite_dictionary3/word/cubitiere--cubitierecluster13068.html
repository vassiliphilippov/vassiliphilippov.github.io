cubitiere

noun
elbow armor piece
Pronunciation: / kjuːˈbɪtiˌɛr / (AmE), / kjuːˈbɪtɪə / (BrE)
A piece of body armor designed to protect the elbow, used especially in medieval times.

Examples

cubitiere on armor
During the tournament, the cubitiere protected his arm from a heavy blow.
Medieval armorers would sometimes craft a cubitiere to match a full suit of plate armor.
The knight's cubitiere was intricately engraved with the family crest.
