shaded

adjective
protected from direct sunlight by shade
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪdɪd / (BrE)
partially or fully protected from direct sunlight by shade or shadow.

Examples

shaded garden area
Many plants thrive in shaded areas of the garden.
The street was lined with shaded walkways, making it pleasant for pedestrians.
We sat on a shaded bench to escape the hot afternoon sun.
