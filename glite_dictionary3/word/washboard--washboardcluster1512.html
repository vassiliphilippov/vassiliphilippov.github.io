washboard

noun
protective plank along a boat's side
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A broad plank or protective board along the side (gunwale) of a boat or ship, designed to prevent water from splashing over the side.

Examples

washboard on a boat
The sailor secured the washboard to prevent water from splashing onto the deck.
They replaced the old washboard before setting out on their next voyage.
Without a proper washboard, the fishing boat took on water during the storm.
