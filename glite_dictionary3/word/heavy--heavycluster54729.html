heavy

adjective
intense or powerful; forceful
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi / (BrE)
very intense, large, or great in amount, degree, or force (used for rain, losses, effort, activity, etc.)

Examples

heavy rain
Rescue teams struggled against the heavy rain to reach the flooded village.
The winds were so heavy that the trees began to sway dangerously.
There was heavy traffic on the highway after the accident.

They imposed heavy fines on businesses that violated the new environmental regulations.
