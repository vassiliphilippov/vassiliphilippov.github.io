gambit

noun
chess opening move involving a sacrifice
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæm.bɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡæm.bɪt / (BrE)
An opening move in chess in which a player sacrifices a minor piece to gain a strategic advantage.

Examples

chess gambit opening
He sacrificed his pawn with a daring gambit at the beginning of the game.
Her clever opening gambit took her opponent by surprise.
The King's gambit is a classic strategy favored by many aggressive players.
