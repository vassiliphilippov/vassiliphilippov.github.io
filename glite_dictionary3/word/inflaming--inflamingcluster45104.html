inflaming

noun
arousing strong emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of arousing strong or violent emotion in someone or a group

Examples

inflaming public anger
The politician's speech was criticized for its inflaming of public sentiment.
There was concern over the media's role in the inflaming of emotions during the crisis.
Unchecked rumors led to the inflaming of tensions between the two groups.
