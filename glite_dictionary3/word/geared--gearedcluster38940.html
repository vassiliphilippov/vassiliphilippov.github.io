geared

adjective
equipped with or using gears
Pronunciation: / ɡɪrd / (AmE), / ɡɪəd / (BrE)
equipped with gears or having gears engaged; designed or fitted to operate using gears.

Examples

geared bicycle
Modern bicycles are often geared for easier riding over hills.
The old clock tower is geared so that all its faces show the same time.
This machine is geared to provide different speeds for various tasks.
