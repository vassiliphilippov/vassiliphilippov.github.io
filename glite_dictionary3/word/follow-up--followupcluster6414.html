follow-up

noun
subsequent medical check after treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑː.loʊ.ʌp /; / ˈfɑloʊ ʌp / (AmE), / ˈfɒl.əʊ.ʌp /; / ˈfɒləʊ ʌp / (BrE)
a medical appointment or examination after initial treatment to monitor progress or recovery

Examples
