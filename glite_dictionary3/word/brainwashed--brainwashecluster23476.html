brainwashed

adjective
indoctrinated; forced to accept new beliefs
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnwɑːʃt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnwɒʃt / (BrE)
having had one's beliefs or attitudes forcibly changed through intensive indoctrination or manipulation

Examples

brainwashed by propaganda
After months of relentless propaganda, the citizens seemed completely brainwashed.
Critics argued that the show brainwashed viewers into buying unnecessary products.
Some cult members appeared visibly brainwashed and repeated slogans without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – indoctrinated: having had beliefs or values instilled systematically, usually without questioning
  • antonym – unbiased: not having or showing an undue or biased opinion
