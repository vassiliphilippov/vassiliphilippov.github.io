spoilage

noun
process or result of being spoiled
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈspɔɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the process or result of something (especially food) becoming spoiled, ruined, or unfit for use, usually because of decay or damage
