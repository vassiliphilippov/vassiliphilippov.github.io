self-stimulation

noun
mentally engaging activity done by oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf stɪˈmjuːleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf stɪˈmjʊːleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of engaging in activities by oneself to promote mental alertness, engagement, or focus

Examples

self-stimulation for focus
He uses music as a form of self-stimulation to help him concentrate on his work.
Regular self-stimulation through mindful exercises can enhance one's ability to pay attention.
Some people rely on puzzles and brainteasers for self-stimulation when they're feeling unfocused.
