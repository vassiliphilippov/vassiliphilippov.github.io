deceitfully

adverb
in a dishonest or misleading way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsiːtfəli / (AmE), / dɪˈsiːtfʊli / (BrE)
In a manner intended to mislead or deceive someone; dishonestly.

Examples

to act deceitfully
He deceitfully promised to repay the money, knowing that he never would.
She answered the questions deceitfully, withholding important information from the investigation.
The report was deceitfully edited to hide crucial errors.
