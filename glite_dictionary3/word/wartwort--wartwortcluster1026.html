wartwort

noun
European wildflower; believed to cure warts
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrt.wɜrt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːt.wɜːt / (BrE)
a European wildflower (mainly Euphorbia helioscopia) with flowers that turn toward the sun, traditionally believed to cure warts.

Examples

wartwort plant remedy
Folklore held that applying crushed wartwort leaves could help remove warts.
The wartwort grows in open fields throughout Europe and is easily recognized by its unusual flowers.
We noticed a patch of wartwort turning its blooms toward the midday sun.
