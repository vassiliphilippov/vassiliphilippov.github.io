inoculating

noun
act of administering a vaccine
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɑkjəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of introducing a vaccine or similar agent into the body to protect against disease by inducing immunity

Examples

to inoculate a patient
Medical professionals must follow strict hygiene protocols while inoculating patients.
Proper inoculating is essential during outbreaks to prevent the spread of disease.
The inoculating of hundreds of schoolchildren took place at the community center.
