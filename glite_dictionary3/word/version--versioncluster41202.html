version

noun
manual turning of a fetus in the uterus
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrʒən / (AmE), / ˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which a fetus is manually turned in the uterus to facilitate delivery

Examples

version procedure
Her obstetrician recommended a version procedure when the baby was found to be breech late in pregnancy.
Manual version can reduce the chance of a cesarean section in breech pregnancies.
