neencephalon

noun
cerebral cortex and related brain areas
Pronunciation: / niːˈɛn.səˌfæl.ɑn / (AmE), / niːˈɛn.sɪˌfæˌlɒn / (BrE)
the part of the brain with the most recent evolutionary origin, including the cerebral cortex and related areas

Examples

the neencephalon controls thinking
Damage to the neencephalon can result in impaired sensory perception and reasoning.
Researchers compared the structure of the neencephalon across several primate species.
The neencephalon is responsible for many higher cognitive functions in mammals.

  • antonym – paleencephalon: the part of the brain with older evolutionary origin
