burdock

noun
a plant with large leaves and burrs
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːˌdɑk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdɒk / (BrE)
a large biennial plant of the genus Arctium, native to Eurasia, with broad leaves and burrs that stick to clothing or fur
