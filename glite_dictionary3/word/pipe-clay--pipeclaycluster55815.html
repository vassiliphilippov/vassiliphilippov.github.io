to pipe-clay

verb
to clean or whiten with pipe-clay
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪp.kleɪ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪp.kleɪ / (BrE)
to whiten or clean (something, especially leather) using pipe-clay, a fine white clay

Examples

to pipe-clay leather boots
He carefully pipe-clayed his boots until they gleamed white.
In preparation for parade, the soldiers pipe-clayed their leather belts.
She spent the afternoon pipe-claying the old saddle for the historical reenactment.
