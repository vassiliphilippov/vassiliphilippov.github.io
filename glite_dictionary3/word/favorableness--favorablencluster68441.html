favorableness

noun
quality of being encouraging or advantageous
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvərəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərəbəlnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being likely to produce a good result; the state of being encouraging or advantageous.

Examples

favorableness of conditions
Investors assess the favorableness of market conditions before making decisions.
She was impressed by the favorableness of the reviews her book received.
The favorableness of the weather helped make the outdoor event a great success.
