Yajurveda

noun
Hindu sacred text of sacrificial formulas
Pronunciation: / jɑːˈdʒʊrˌveɪdə / (AmE), / jɑːˈdʒʊəˌveɪdə / (BrE)
one of the four Vedas, consisting of a collection of sacrificial formulas and prayers used in Hindu rituals

Examples

The Yajurveda is widely studied by scholars interested in the history of Hindu rituals.
