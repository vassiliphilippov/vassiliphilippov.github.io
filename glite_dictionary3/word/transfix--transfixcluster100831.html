to transfix

verb
make motionless with amazement or fear
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɪks / (AmE), / trænsˈfɪks / (BrE)
to cause someone to become motionless with amazement, terror, or awe

Examples

transfix with fear
He transfixed his opponents with a cold, unwavering stare.
She was transfixed by the beauty of the sunset, unable to look away.
The audience stood transfixed as the magician performed his final trick.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mesmerize: to hold someone's attention completely as if by magic
  • synonym – petrify: to make someone unable to move out of fear
