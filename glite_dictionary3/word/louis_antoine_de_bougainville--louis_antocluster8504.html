Louis Antoine de Bougainville

proper noun
French explorer and circumnavigator (1729–1811)
Pronunciation: / luːˈiː ˌænˈtwɑn də ˌbuːɡənˈviːl / (AmE), / lwi ˈɑ̃twan də buɡɛ̃vil / (BrE)
(1729–1811) A French explorer and admiral, noted for being the first Frenchman to circumnavigate the globe and for his contributions to scientific exploration.
