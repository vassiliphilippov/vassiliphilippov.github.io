to spill

verb
reveal secret or private information
Pronunciation: / spɪl / (AmE), / spɪl / (BrE)
to reveal secret, private, or confidential information, often unintentionally or under pressure

Examples

to spill the secret
If you know something important, now is the time to spill.
She accidentally spilled the surprise party plans to her friend.
Under pressure from reporters, the official spilled the details of the investigation.

  • synonym – disclose: to reveal or make known, especially information that was secret
