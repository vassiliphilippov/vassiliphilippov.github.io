Menominee

noun
a Native American tribe of Wisconsin
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːməˌni / (AmE), / mɪˈnɒmɪni / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people of Algonquian stock, living primarily on a reservation in central Wisconsin

Examples

a Menominee leader
A Menominee elder shared stories about the tribe's history at the community center.
Many members of the Menominee tribe live on a reservation in central Wisconsin.
The Menominee have maintained their traditions despite centuries of change.
