to round

verb
move around a curve or circle
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
to travel around or move along a curved or circular path

Examples

to round a corner
The path rounds the old oak tree at the center of the park.
The road rounds the lake before heading north again.
We rounded the corner and saw the house in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – circle: to move in a circle around something
  • antonym – straighten: to make or become straight rather than curved
