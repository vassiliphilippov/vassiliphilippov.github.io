sparkler

noun
hand-held firework emitting sparks
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrklɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklə / (BrE)
a small firework that burns slowly while emitting bright sparks, often held in the hand

Examples

hold a sparkler at night
Each child waved a sparkler during the evening celebration.
For her birthday party, they put a sparkler on top of the cake instead of a candle.
Please be careful not to touch the hot end of the sparkler after it burns out.

Related Terms

  • synonym – firework: an object containing explosive powder that makes a display of light or sound when lit
