escalator

noun
moving staircase for people
Pronunciation: / ˈɛskəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɛskəleɪtə / (BrE)
A moving staircase powered by a motor, designed to carry people between different floors or levels.

Examples

ride the escalator
Children should always hold the handrail when riding the escalator.
The escalator was out of service, so we had to use the stairs.
We took the escalator up to the second floor of the shopping mall.
