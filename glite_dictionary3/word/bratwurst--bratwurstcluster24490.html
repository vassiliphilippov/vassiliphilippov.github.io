bratwurst

noun
German pork sausage
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːtˌwɜrst / (AmE), / ˈbrætˌwɜːst / (BrE)
A type of German sausage made from pork, usually grilled or fried and typically served in a bread roll.

Examples

grilled bratwurst sausage
He ordered a bratwurst with sauerkraut and mustard at the German festival.
The smell of sizzling bratwurst filled the air at the food market.
We enjoyed grilled bratwurst at the summer barbecue.
