nigga

noun
offensive racial slur for Black people
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪɡə / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡə / (BrE)
A highly offensive term for a Black person, considered extremely insulting and derogatory, sometimes used informally within the Black community.

Examples

call someone a nigga
He was criticized for using the word nigga in his lyrics.
In some communities, nigga is used among friends, but it remains very controversial.
The use of nigga by people outside the Black community is considered deeply offensive.
