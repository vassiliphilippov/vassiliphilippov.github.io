bugleweed

noun
an aromatic herb native to eastern North America
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːɡlˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈbjuːɡlˌwiːd / (BrE)
a mildly narcotic and astringent aromatic herb with small whitish flowers, native to eastern North America, sometimes used in herbal medicine.

Examples
