bedraggled

adjective
untidy, dirty, and wet appearance
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdræɡəld / (AmE), / bɪˈdræɡəld / (BrE)
looking dirty, untidy, and often wet, as if after being in rain, mud, or neglected
