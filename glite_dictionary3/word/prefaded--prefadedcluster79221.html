prefaded

adjective
fabric or clothing made to look faded artificially
Pronunciation: / priˈfeɪdɪd / (AmE), / priˈfeɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of fabric or clothing) made to have a faded or worn appearance using artificial processes before being sold or worn.

Examples

prefaded jeans
I prefer prefaded shirts because they feel softer and more comfortable.
Manufacturers use special techniques to create a prefaded effect on denim.
These prefaded jeans give a vintage look right out of the store.
