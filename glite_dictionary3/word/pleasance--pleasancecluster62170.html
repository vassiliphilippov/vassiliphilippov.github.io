pleasance

noun
the feeling of pleasure or pleasantness
Pronunciation: / ˈpliːzəns / (AmE), / ˈpliːzəns / (BrE)
A feeling or state of pleasure or pleasantness; a sense of satisfaction or delight.

Examples

deep pleasance
After months of hard work, she basked in the pleasance of her well-earned accomplishment.
The tranquil garden filled him with a deep pleasance that words could not describe.
There is a quiet pleasance to sitting beside the fireplace on a cold night.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pain: physical or emotional suffering, the opposite of pleasure
  • synonym – pleasure: a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment
