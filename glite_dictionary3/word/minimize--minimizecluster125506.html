to minimize

verb
make as small as possible
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪn.ɪ.maɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪn.ɪ.maɪz / (BrE)
to make something as small or insignificant as possible

Examples

to minimize the risk
She wore sunscreen to minimize the risk of sunburn.
The company tries to minimize its impact on the environment.
We need to minimize costs if we want to stay competitive.

Related Terms

  • antonym – maximize: to make as large or great as possible
  • synonym – reduce: to make something smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
