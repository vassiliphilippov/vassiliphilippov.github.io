cheerlessness

noun
gloominess; absence of cheer
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪr.ləs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪə.ləs.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being gloomy, lacking happiness or hope
