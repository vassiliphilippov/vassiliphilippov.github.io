stiff-haired

adjective
having stiff or coarse hair
Pronunciation: / stɪf hɛrd / (AmE), / stɪf heəd / (BrE)
having hair that is stiff or coarse to the touch, not soft or flexible

Examples

stiff-haired dog
His beard became noticeably stiff-haired as he aged.
Some breeds are bred to have a stiff-haired muzzle.
The stiff-haired terrier was known for its wiry coat.
