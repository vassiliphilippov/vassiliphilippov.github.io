tonic

adjective
relating to normal muscle tone
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɒ.nɪk / (BrE)
(of muscles or tissue) relating to, producing, or characterized by normal tone or steady, continuous contraction.

Examples

tonic muscle contraction
A tonic muscle contraction helps maintain posture over long periods.
Some disorders can disrupt tonic activity, leading to muscle weakness or spasticity.
The physiologist observed a tonic reflex during the experiment.
