to gum

verb
to exude or produce gum (sticky substance)
Pronunciation: / ɡʌm / (AmE), / ɡʌm / (BrE)
(of a tree or plant) to exude or produce gum, a sticky substance.

Examples

trees gum sap
Eucalyptus trees gum from cracks in their bark during dry periods.
In the spring, certain species of acacia gum along the stems.
Some trees gum naturally when injured or exposed to harsh weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exude: to discharge (liquid or moisture) slowly
