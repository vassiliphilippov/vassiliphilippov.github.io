discord

noun
harsh mixture of sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsˌkɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdɪs.kɔːd / (BrE)
(literary) A harsh combination of sounds; lack of harmony in music.

Examples

musical discord
The composer skillfully used discord in the melody to create a sense of tension.
The sudden discord of shattering glass startled everyone in the quiet room.
Their instruments produced a discord that was jarring to the audience's ears.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cacophony: a harsh, discordant mixture of sounds
  • antonym – harmony: pleasing arrangement of sounds
