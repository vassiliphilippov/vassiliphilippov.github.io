formalized

adjective
made official or recognized
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrməˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməlaɪzd / (BrE)
Made official, legitimate, or recognized, especially through a defined process or by following required procedures.

Examples

formalized agreement
Her temporary appointment was finally formalized after months of review.
Our partnership became much stronger once it was formalized through a contract.
The company formalized its policies on remote work this year.
