Velociraptor

noun
genus of small carnivorous dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / vəˌlɑːsɪˈræptər / (AmE), / vɪˌlɒsɪˈræptə / (BrE)
A genus of small, fast-moving, carnivorous theropod dinosaurs from the Late Cretaceous period, known for their agility and sickle-shaped claws.
