enate

noun
relative on the mother's side
Pronunciation: / ˈiːneɪt / (AmE), / ˈiːneɪt / (BrE)
A person who is related through the mother's side of the family.

Examples

an enate cousin
Anthropologists often distinguish between agnates and enates when studying kinship systems.
In traditional societies, inheritance sometimes passed from an enate rather than an agnate.
