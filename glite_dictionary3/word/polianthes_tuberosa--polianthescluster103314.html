Polianthes tuberosa

noun
highly fragrant Mexican flowering plant
Pronunciation: / poʊliˈænθiːz tuːbəˈroʊsə / (AmE), / pɒliˈænθiːz tjuːbəˈrəʊsə / (BrE)
A tuberous Mexican plant with grasslike leaves, cultivated for its spikes of highly fragrant, waxy, white, lily-like flowers.
