savor

noun
distinctive taste or flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈseɪvə / (BrE)
a distinctive taste or flavor experience, especially from a savory food or condiment

Examples

savor of a dish
A pinch of salt can enhance the savor of almost any dish.
She closed her eyes to appreciate the full savor of the aged cheese.
The savor of the spicy sauce lingered on his tongue long after the meal.
