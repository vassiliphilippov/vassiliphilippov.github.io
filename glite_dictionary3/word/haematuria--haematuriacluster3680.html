haematuria

noun
presence of blood in the urine
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈtʊriə / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtjʊəriə / (BrE)
The presence of blood in the urine, often indicating a disease or disorder of the urinary tract.

Examples

haematuria in urine
He noticed haematuria after experiencing persistent back pain for several days.
Patients presenting with haematuria often undergo several diagnostic tests.
The doctor explained that haematuria could be a sign of a urinary tract infection.
