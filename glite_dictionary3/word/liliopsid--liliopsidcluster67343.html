liliopsid

noun
monocotyledonous flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪliˈɑpsɪd / (AmE), / ˌlɪliˈɒpsɪd / (BrE)
A monocotyledonous flowering plant; a plant whose stem grows by deposits on its inside, also known as a monocot.
