to gaze

verb
look steadily and intently
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪz / (AmE), / ɡeɪz / (BrE)
to look steadily and intently, especially in admiration, surprise, or thought

Examples

to gaze at stars
He stood silently and gazed into the distance, deep in contemplation.
She gazed out of the window, lost in thought.
The child gazed at the stars, fascinated by the night sky.
