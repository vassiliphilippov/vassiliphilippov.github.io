Francis Joseph

proper noun
Emperor of Austria and King of Hungary (1830–1916)
Pronunciation: / ˈfræn.sɪs ˈdʒoʊ.zəf / (AmE), / ˈfræn.sɪs ˈdʒəʊ.zɪf / (BrE)
(1830–1916) Emperor of Austria and King of Hungary, notable for his long reign and for his defeat by Napoleon III at the battle of Magenta.
