laureate

noun
person honored for outstanding achievements
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔriˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɒːrɪət / (BrE)
a person who has been honored for outstanding achievements, especially in the arts, literature, or sciences

Examples

Nobel laureate
Many laureates have gone on to influence future generations in their fields.
She became a laureate when she received the international poetry prize.
The Nobel laureate delivered an inspiring lecture on climate change.

Related Terms

  • antonym – loser: a person who does not win or is not honored
  • synonym – winner: a person who wins a prize or honor
