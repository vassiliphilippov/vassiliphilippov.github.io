touchiness

noun
easily irritated or offended
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃ.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ.ɪ.nɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Her touchiness made it difficult to discuss even minor issues with her.
