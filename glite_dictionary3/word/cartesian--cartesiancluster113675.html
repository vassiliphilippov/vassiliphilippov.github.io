Cartesian

adjective
relating to René Descartes or his philosophy
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈtiʒən / (AmE), / kɑːˈtiːzɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to the French philosopher René Descartes or his philosophy, especially his emphasis on logical analysis and deductive reasoning.

Examples

Cartesian philosophy
His ideas reflect a Cartesian understanding of mind and body.
Modern mathematics heavily relies on Cartesian coordinates and analytical methods.
The Cartesian approach emphasizes deductive reasoning in scientific inquiry.
