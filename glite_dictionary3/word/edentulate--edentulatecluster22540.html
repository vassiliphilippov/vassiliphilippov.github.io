edentulate

adjective
having few or no teeth
Pronunciation: / iˈdɛntʃəˌleɪt / (AmE), / iˈdɛntjʊlət / (BrE)
having few or no teeth; lacking most or all teeth, especially as a natural characteristic.

Examples

edentulate animal species
Certain armadillos are edentulate and depend on insects for food.
Many anteaters are edentulate, having evolved without the need for teeth.
The elderly patient was almost completely edentulate after years of dental problems.
