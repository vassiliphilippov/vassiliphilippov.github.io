mute

adjective
expressed without speech
Pronunciation: / mjut / (AmE), / mjuːt / (BrE)
expressed or conveyed without the use of speech; silent

Examples

mute protest
His mute protest was more powerful than words.
She gave him a mute glance full of understanding.
There was a mute plea for help in her eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – silent: making no sound; completely quiet
  • antonym – vocal: expressed using the voice or speech
  • synonym – wordless: without words; expressed without speaking
