remainder

noun
piece of leftover material or cloth
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmeɪndɚ /; / rɪˈmeɪn.dɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪndə /; / rɪˈmeɪn.də / (BrE)
the part or amount of something that is left after the rest has been used, removed, or taken away
