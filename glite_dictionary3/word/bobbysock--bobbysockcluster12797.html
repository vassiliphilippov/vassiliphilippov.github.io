bobbysock

noun
short ankle-length sock
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑbiˌsɑk / (AmE), / ˈbɒbiˌsɒk / (BrE)
A sock that reaches just above the ankle, typically worn with skirts or dresses, especially fashionable among teenage girls in the 1940s and 1950s.

Examples

bobbysock with skirt
She wore her favorite bobbysocks with a pleated skirt to the dance.
The store still sells bobbysocks for those seeking a retro look.
White bobbysocks and saddle shoes were once the height of teen fashion.
