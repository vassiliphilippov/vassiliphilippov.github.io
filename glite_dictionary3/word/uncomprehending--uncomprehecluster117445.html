uncomprehending

adjective
not understanding; lacking comprehension
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnkɒmprɪˈhɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
not understanding or showing a lack of understanding; unable to grasp meaning or significance.
