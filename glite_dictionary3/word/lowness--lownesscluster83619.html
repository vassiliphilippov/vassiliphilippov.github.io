lowness

noun
a state of feeling low in spirits
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊnəs / (BrE)
a state of feeling sad, depressed, or lacking in spirits

The film captures the lowness people feel during moments of loss and isolation.
