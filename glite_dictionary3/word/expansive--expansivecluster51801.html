expansive

adjective
able or likely to expand
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (BrE)
able or tending to expand, or characterized by expansion; capable of increasing in size, volume, or scope.

Examples

expansive soil
Certain soils become expansive when they absorb water, which can damage building foundations.
The balloon is made of an expansive material that stretches as it fills with air.
The scientist measured the expansive force created by the heated gas.
