dialogue

noun
formal discussion to reach agreement
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌlɔg / (AmE), / ˈdʌɪəlɒɡ / (BrE)
a formal discussion between two or more groups or countries, especially with the aim of resolving differences or reaching an agreement

Examples

dialogue between groups
After months of tension, the two companies began a dialogue to explore possible collaboration.
International dialogue is essential for maintaining peace between neighboring countries.
The leaders agreed to open a dialogue to address their territorial dispute.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conflict: a serious disagreement or argument
  • synonym – negotiation: discussion aimed at reaching an agreement
  • synonym – talks: formal discussions, especially between countries or groups
