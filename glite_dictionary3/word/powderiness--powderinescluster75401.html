powderiness

noun
quality of being powdery or fine-textured
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊdəri nəs / (AmE), / ˈpaʊdərɪ nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being powdery or having a very fine, dust-like texture

Examples

fine powderiness
Bakers often prefer the powderiness of finely milled flour for delicate pastries.

The powderiness of the snow made it perfect for skiing down the mountain.
