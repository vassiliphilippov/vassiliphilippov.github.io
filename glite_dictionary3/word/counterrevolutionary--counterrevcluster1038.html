counterrevolutionary

noun
person opposing a previous revolution
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˌrɛvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
a person who seeks to overturn or reverse the changes introduced by a previous revolution
