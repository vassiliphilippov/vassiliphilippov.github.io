Rhone-Alpes

proper noun
region of eastern France
Pronunciation: / roʊn ˈælpz / (AmE), / rəʊn ˈælpz / (BrE)
a former administrative region of eastern France, known for its mountainous terrain and major rivers including the Rhone, Saone, and Isere.
