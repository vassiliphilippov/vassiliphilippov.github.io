fast

adjective
(of clocks) showing time ahead of correct time
Pronunciation: / fæst / (AmE), / fɑːst / (BrE)
(of a clock or watch) showing a time later than the correct time.

Examples

fast clock
I missed my meeting because my watch was fast by ten minutes.
If your alarm clock is fast, you might get up too early every day.
The kitchen clock is fast, so dinner will be ready earlier than expected.
