to overcast

verb
to cover with clouds
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈkæst / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəkɑːst / (BrE)
to cover with clouds; to make cloudy or gloomy

Examples

to overcast the sky
Darkness overcast the fields as the storm approached.
Rain clouds often overcast the city in early spring.
Thick fog quickly overcast the morning sky, hiding the sun from view.

  • synonym – cloud: to cover with or become covered with clouds
