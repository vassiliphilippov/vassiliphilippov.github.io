antiviral

adjective
inhibiting or stopping viruses
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈvaɪ.rəl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈvaɪ.rəl / (BrE)
(of a substance, action, or drug) inhibiting or stopping the growth and reproduction of viruses.
