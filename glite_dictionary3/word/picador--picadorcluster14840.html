picador

noun
bullfighter who pierces bull with lance
Pronunciation: / ˌpikəˈdɔr / (AmE), / ˌpɪkəˈdɔː / (BrE)
a horseman in a bullfight who pricks the bull with a lance to weaken and provoke it at the start of the fight

Examples

picador on horseback
During the bullfight, the picador's careful lance strokes were met with boos from the crowd.
Each picador relies on the strength and training of his horse to stay safe in the arena.
The picador rode into the ring, poised to deliver the first strike to the bull.
