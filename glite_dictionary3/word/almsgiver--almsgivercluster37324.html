almsgiver

noun
person who gives alms
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlmzˌɡɪvər / (AmE), / ˈɑːlmzˌɡɪvə / (BrE)
a person who gives money, food, or other help to people in need, especially as a charitable act

Examples
