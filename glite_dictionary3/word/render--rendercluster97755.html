render

noun
wall coating material for masonry
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛndɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛndə / (BrE)
a mixture similar to stucco used as a coating for exterior masonry walls to provide a smooth or textured finish

Examples

colored render finish
Architects often choose colored render to give buildings a modern appearance.
Damage to the render can allow moisture to seep into the masonry beneath.
The brick house was finished with a layer of render to protect it from the weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stucco: plaster used for coating wall surfaces or molding into architectural decorations
