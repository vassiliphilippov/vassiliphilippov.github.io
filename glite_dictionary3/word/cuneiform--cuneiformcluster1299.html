cuneiform

adjective
of wedge-shaped bones in anatomy
Pronunciation: / kjuˈniəˌfɔrm /; / ˈkjuːniəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / kjuːˈniːɪfɔːm / (BrE)
shaped like a wedge, especially relating to certain bones in anatomy (such as in the foot or wrist)
