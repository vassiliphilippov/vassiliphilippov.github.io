Lonchocarpus

noun
genus of tropical American shrubs and trees
Pronunciation: / lɒnˈkoʊkɑːrpəs /; / ˌlɑntʃoʊˈkɑːrpəs / (AmE), / lɒnˈkəʊkɑːpəs /; / ˌlɒntʃəˈkɑːpəs / (BrE)
a genus of tropical American shrubs and trees with pinnate leaves and red or white flowers, some species of which are used in traditional fishing, as sources of rotenone, or for medicinal purposes
