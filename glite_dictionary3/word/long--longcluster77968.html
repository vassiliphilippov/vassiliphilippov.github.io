long

adjective
involving high risk or low probability (esp. of odds)
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ / (AmE), / lɒŋ / (BrE)
(of odds or a chance) involving a high degree of risk or a low probability of success.

Examples

long odds
Few bettors were willing to back the horse at such long odds.
He won big by making a risky bet at long odds.
Investing in that startup was a long shot, but it paid off.

Related Terms

  • antonym – short: (of odds) having a high probability; the antonym in gambling contexts
