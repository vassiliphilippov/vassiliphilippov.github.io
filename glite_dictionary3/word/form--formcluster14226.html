form

noun
mold for shaping poured concrete
Pronunciation: / fɔrm / (AmE), / fɔːm / (BrE)
a mold or frame used to give shape to poured concrete until it hardens

Examples

concrete form frame
Before pouring the driveway, the workers carefully built a form to hold the wet concrete in place.
Once the concrete had set, they removed the form to reveal the new wall.
The carpenter checked that the form was level before they started filling it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mold: a hollow container used to give shape to molten or hot liquid material
