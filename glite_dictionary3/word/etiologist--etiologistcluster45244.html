etiologist

noun
specialist in causes of diseases
Pronunciation: / ˌiːtiˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a medical specialist who studies the causes or origins of diseases

Examples

etiologist studies diseases
As an etiologist, Dr. Lee specializes in researching the causes of autoimmune disorders.
Hospitals often consult an etiologist when facing unexplained outbreaks of illness.
The etiologist presented his findings on the origins of the rare disease at the conference.
