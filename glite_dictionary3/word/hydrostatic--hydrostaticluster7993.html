hydrostatic

adjective
about fluids at rest or their pressure
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈstætɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəʊˈstatɪk / (BrE)
relating to fluids at rest or the pressures exerted or transmitted by such fluids

Examples

hydrostatic pressure measurement
A hydrostatic balance is used to determine fluid densities with high accuracy.
Scientists measured the hydrostatic pressure at various depths in the tank.
The dam is designed to withstand significant hydrostatic forces from the reservoir behind it.
