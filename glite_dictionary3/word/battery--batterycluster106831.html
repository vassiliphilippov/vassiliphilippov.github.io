battery

noun
group of related things used together
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˈbætəˌri / (BrE)
a group of similar items or tests arranged or used together for a common purpose

Examples

battery of tests
The candidate faced a battery of difficult questions during the interview.
The lawyer prepared a battery of documents for the court hearing.
The research required a battery of laboratory tests to confirm the diagnosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – series: a number of things that are similar or related, arranged or happening one after the other
