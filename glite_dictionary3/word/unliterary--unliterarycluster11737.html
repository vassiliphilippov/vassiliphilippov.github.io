unliterary

adjective
not having a literary style; plain in expression
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪt̬əˌrɛri / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪtərəri / (BrE)
Not showing the style, affectation, or pretension typical of literary writing; plain or straightforward in manner or expression.

Examples

unliterary style
He preferred an unliterary simplicity in his storytelling.
Her comments had an unliterary directness that surprised the audience.
The review praised her unliterary style for its honesty and lack of pretension.
