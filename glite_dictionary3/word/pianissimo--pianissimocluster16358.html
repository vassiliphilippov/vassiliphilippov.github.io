pianissimo

adjective
very soft (music direction)
Pronunciation: / piˌænɪˈsɪmoʊ / (AmE), / piˌanɪˈsɪməʊ / (BrE)
(in music) very soft or quiet; a direction to play or sing very softly.

Examples

pianissimo dynamic level
Her voice dropped to a pianissimo to convey a sense of intimacy in the song.
Please begin the piece pianissimo and gradually increase the volume.
The violinist played the final notes pianissimo, creating a moment of delicate beauty.
