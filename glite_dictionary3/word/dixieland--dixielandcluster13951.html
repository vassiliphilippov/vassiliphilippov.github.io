Dixieland

proper noun
the southern states of the former Confederacy
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪksiˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈdɪksiˌlænd / (BrE)
the southern states of the United States, especially those that seceded to form the Confederacy during the Civil War (1861–1865)
