adobo

noun
a marinated dish, often served with rice
Pronunciation: / əˈdoʊboʊ / (AmE), / əˈdəʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a dish of meat, fish, or vegetables marinated and cooked in a seasoned sauce, typically served with rice; especially common in Filipino and Latin American cuisines

Examples

Filipino adobo dish
At the buffet, they served both beef adobo and vegetarian options for the guests.
I ordered the chicken adobo at the Filipino restaurant, and it was delicious.
My grandmother taught me how to make her special pork adobo recipe from scratch.
