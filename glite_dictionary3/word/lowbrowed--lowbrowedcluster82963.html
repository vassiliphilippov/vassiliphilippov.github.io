lowbrowed

adjective
not intellectual or cultured
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌbraʊd / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌbraʊd / (BrE)
(of a person or their tastes) lacking intellectual, cultural, or artistic sophistication; not cultivated.

Examples

lowbrowed comedy show
He always preferred lowbrowed entertainment over high art and literature.
Her lowbrowed sense of humor made her popular at parties.
The critic dismissed the movie as too lowbrowed to warrant serious discussion.
