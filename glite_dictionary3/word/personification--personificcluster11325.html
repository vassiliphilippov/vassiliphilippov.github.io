personification

noun
a person embodying a quality or idea
Pronunciation: / pərˌsɑːnəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / pəˌsɒnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who embodies or represents a particular quality, idea, or characteristic

Examples

personification of courage
For many, Gandhi was the personification of peace and nonviolence.
She is the very personification of kindness and generosity.
The coach was seen as the personification of determination during the championship.
