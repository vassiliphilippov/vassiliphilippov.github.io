nebulous

adjective
vague or ill-defined; lacking clear form
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛb.jə.ləs /; / ˈnɛbjələs / (AmE), / ˈnɛb.jʊ.ləs /; / ˈnɛbjʊləs / (BrE)
not clear, vague, or lacking definite shape or form (used for ideas, explanations, memories, etc.)

Examples

nebulous idea
Her explanation for the missing money was strangely nebulous.
The details of the plan remain nebulous and subject to change.
There was only a nebulous distinction between the two proposals.
Her memories of childhood are nebulous and fragmented.
The committee rejected the proposal because of its nebulous objectives.
They gave only nebulous instructions, which confused the whole team.

  • synonym – vague: not clearly expressed or defined
