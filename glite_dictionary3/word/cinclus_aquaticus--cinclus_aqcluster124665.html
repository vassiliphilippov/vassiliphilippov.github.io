Cinclus aquaticus

noun
scientific name for the European dipper bird
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋ.kləs əˈkwæt.ɪ.kəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.klʊs əˈkwæt.ɪ.kəs / (BrE)
