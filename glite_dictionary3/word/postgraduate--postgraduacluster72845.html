postgraduate

adjective
relating to education after a bachelor's degree
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈɡrædʒuət / (AmE), / pəʊstˈɡrædʒuət / (BrE)
relating to studies undertaken after earning a bachelor's degree, especially at a university or college

Examples

postgraduate course
After completing my bachelor's, I'm considering postgraduate studies abroad.
He is enrolled in a postgraduate program in molecular biology.
The university offers several postgraduate courses in law and engineering.
