spaced-out

adjective
confused or dazed; inattentive
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪst aʊt /; / ˌspeɪstˈaʊt / (AmE), / ˈspeɪst aʊt /; / ˌspeɪstˈaʊt / (BrE)
confused, dazed, or not fully aware of what is happening, as if under the influence of drugs or very tired
