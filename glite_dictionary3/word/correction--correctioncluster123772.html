correction

noun
something replacing an error
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛkʃən /; / kəˈrɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / kəˈrɛkʃən /; / kəˈrɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of fixing, remedying, or making something right after a mistake or error (including in documents, technical, medical, or scientific contexts)
