sterility

noun
inability to produce offspring
Pronunciation: / stəˈrɪləti / (AmE), / stəˈrɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state or condition of being unable to produce offspring or conceive children

Examples

sterility in women
After years of trying to have a baby, they were devastated to learn about her sterility.
Scientists are studying the causes of male sterility to develop better treatments.
The disease can cause sterility in both men and women if left untreated.
