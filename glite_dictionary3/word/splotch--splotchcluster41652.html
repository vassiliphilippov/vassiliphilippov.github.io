to splotch

verb
to mark with blotches or spots
Pronunciation: / splɑtʃ / (AmE), / splɒtʃ / (BrE)
to mark or cover with irregular spots or blotches, typically of color or liquid

Examples

to splotch paint
He splotched blue ink across the paper, leaving messy marks everywhere.
She accidentally splotched paint on her new jeans while working on the mural.
The children splotched mud all over the clean kitchen floor after playing outside.
