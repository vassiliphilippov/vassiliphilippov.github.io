feverishly

adverb
with intense excitement or agitation
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːvɚɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈfiːvərɪʃli / (BrE)
In a way that shows intense excitement, energy, or activity, often as if suffering from a fever.

Examples

to work feverishly
He packed his suitcase feverishly, barely noticing what he was throwing in.
She typed feverishly on her laptop to meet the deadline.
The rescue workers searched feverishly for survivors amidst the rubble.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calmly: in a relaxed or peaceful manner
  • synonym – frantically: in a hurried, disordered, or panic-stricken way
