Marya Sklodowska

proper noun
birth name of Marie Curie, Nobel-winning chemist
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑriə skloˈdɔfska / (AmE), / ˈmɑːriə sklɒˈdɒfska / (BrE)
The birth name of Marie Curie (1867–1934), a French chemist and physicist born in Poland, celebrated for her pioneering research on radioactivity and for being awarded two Nobel Prizes.
