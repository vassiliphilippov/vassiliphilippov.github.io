seasoning

noun
adding spices or flavorings to food
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of adding salt, herbs, spices, or other flavorings to food to enhance its taste

Examples

to add seasoning
Proper seasoning can turn a simple meal into something delicious.
She pays close attention to the seasoning when preparing her dishes.
The seasoning of the soup made it much more flavorful.
