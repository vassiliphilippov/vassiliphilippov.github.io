headlong

adjective, adverb
at great speed; recklessly
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɛd.lɒŋ /; / ˈhɛdlɒŋ / (BrE)
very quickly and without thinking carefully; in a hasty or reckless way
