to retroflex

verb
to pronounce with tongue curled back
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛtroʊˌflɛks / (AmE), / ˈrɛtrəʊˌflɛks / (BrE)
(of a consonant) to pronounce or articulate with the tongue curled back towards the palate.

Examples

to retroflex a consonant
Children learning Hindi often learn to retroflex consonants from an early age.
Many speakers of South Asian languages retroflex certain consonants that English does not.
The linguist demonstrated how to retroflex the 't' sound for students.
