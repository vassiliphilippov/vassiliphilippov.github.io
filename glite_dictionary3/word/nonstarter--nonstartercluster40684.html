nonstarter

noun
a consistently unsuccessful person
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstɑrtər / (AmE), / nɒnˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a person who is consistently unsuccessful, especially one who fails to succeed or be considered seriously

Examples

political nonstarter
After three unsuccessful campaigns, some labeled the candidate a political nonstarter.
Because she never finished her assignments, her teacher called her a nonstarter.
Despite his enthusiasm, many considered him a nonstarter in the competition.

  • synonym – loser: a person who is habitually unsuccessful or defeated
