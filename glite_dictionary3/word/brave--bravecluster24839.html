to brave

verb
face with courage
Pronunciation: / breɪv / (AmE), / breɪv / (BrE)
to face or deal with something dangerous, difficult, or unpleasant with courage

Examples

to brave danger
Despite her fear, she braved the dark alley to get home quickly.
He braved the freezing weather to take his dog for a walk.
Many journalists brave dangerous situations to report the news firsthand.
