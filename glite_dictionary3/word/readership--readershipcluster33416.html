readership

noun
audience of a publication
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈriːdəʃɪp / (BrE)
the group of people who read a particular newspaper, magazine, book, or other publication

Examples

readership of a newspaper
That newspaper appeals to an older readership interested in local news.
The author hoped her new novel would attract a wider readership.
The magazine's readership has grown significantly over the past year.
