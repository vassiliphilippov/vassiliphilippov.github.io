Adrianople

proper noun
historic city in northwestern Turkey
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪdriəˈnoʊpəl / (AmE), / ˌeɪdrɪəˈnəʊpəl / (BrE)
A historic city in northwestern Turkey, formerly known as a Thracian town, rebuilt and renamed by the Roman Emperor Hadrian; now known as Edirne.
