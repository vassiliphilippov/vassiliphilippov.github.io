Nikolai Ivanovich Bukharin

proper noun
Russian Bolshevik revolutionary and theorist
Pronunciation: / nɪˈkoʊlaɪ ˌivaˈnoʊvɪtʃ buˈkɑrɪn / (AmE), / nɪˈkɒlaɪ ˌivəˈnəʊvɪtʃ bʊˈkɑːrɪn / (BrE)
(1888–1938) A prominent Bolshevik leader in Russia, known for advocating gradual collectivization of agriculture and later executed during Stalin's purges.
