injectiveness

noun
property of being an injection in mathematics
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɛktɪvnɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛktɪvnɪs / (BrE)
(mathematics) The property of a function or mapping being an injection, where each element of the range corresponds to at most one element of the domain.
