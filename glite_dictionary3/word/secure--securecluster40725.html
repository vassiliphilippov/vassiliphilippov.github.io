to secure

verb
guarantee payment of a debt or obligation
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
to guarantee or make certain of the payment of a debt or obligation, often by providing collateral or surety

Examples

to secure a loan
She agreed to secure the account by providing a personal guarantee for payment.
The company secured its debts with valuable assets to reassure investors.
The loan was secured with his house as collateral, ensuring the lender would be paid back.

Related Terms

  • synonym – guarantee: to promise or ensure the fulfillment of something, especially payment
