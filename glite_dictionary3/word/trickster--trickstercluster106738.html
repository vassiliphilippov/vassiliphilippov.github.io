trickster

noun
someone who plays tricks or pranks
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪk.stɚ /; / ˈtrɪkstɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪk.stə /; / ˈtrɪkstə / (BrE)
a person who deceives, cheats, or plays tricks on others, often for fun or personal gain
