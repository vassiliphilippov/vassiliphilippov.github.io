thallophytic

adjective
relating to thallophytes (non-vascular plants)
Pronunciation: / ˌθæləˈfɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌθaləˈfɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of thallophytes (a group of non-vascular plants such as algae, fungi, and lichens)

Examples

thallophytic algae group
Ferns differ from thallophytic groups because they possess vascular tissue.
Many thallophytic plants reproduce through spores instead of seeds.
The scientist specialized in thallophytic organisms such as algae and fungi.

Related Terms

  • synonym – thallophyte: a simple plant, such as algae, fungi, and lichens, lacking true stems, roots, or leaves
