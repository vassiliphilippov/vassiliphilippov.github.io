deconstructionist

adjective
relating to deconstructionism
Pronunciation: / diˌkʌnstrʌkʃəˌnɪst / (AmE), / diˌkʌnstrʌkʃəˌnɪst / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of deconstructionism, a method of critical analysis of texts that questions traditional assumptions about certainty, identity, and truth

Examples

deconstructionist theory
Her deconstructionist approach to literature emphasized the instability of language.
Many art historians apply deconstructionist theories to reinterpret traditional works.
The professor delivered a deconstructionist critique of classic philosophy.
