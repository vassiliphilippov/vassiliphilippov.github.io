mechanism

noun
natural structure working like a machine
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛkəˌnɪzəm /; / ˈmɛk.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmɛkənɪz(ə)m /; / ˈmɛk.ə.nɪ.zəm /; / ˈmɛkənɪzəm / (BrE)
