to recount

verb
to narrate or describe in detail
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / rɪˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
to tell someone about something, especially something that you have experienced, in detail; to narrate or describe the facts of an event

Examples

to recount a story
During the interview, she recounted every detail of the accident.
He recounted his adventures in the Amazon to an eager audience.
The witness recounts the events of that night in court.

Related Terms

  • synonym – describe: to give an account in words of someone or something
  • synonym – narrate: to tell a story or describe a series of events
