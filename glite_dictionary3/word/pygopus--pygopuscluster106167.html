Pygopus

noun
genus of legless lizards
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪɡəpəs / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡəpəs / (BrE)
a genus of snake-like, limbless or nearly limbless lizards in the family Pygopodidae, with no forelimbs and only small hind limbs
