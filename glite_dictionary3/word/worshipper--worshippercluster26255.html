worshipper

noun
person who admires excessively
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrʃɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪpə / (BrE)
a person who admires someone or something excessively, often unable to see their faults

Examples

worshipper of celebrities
As a devoted worshipper of her favorite author, she never saw any flaws in his work.
His friends accused him of being a blind worshipper who refused to acknowledge the leader's mistakes.
You shouldn't be a worshipper of celebrities—everyone has faults.

Related Terms

  • synonym – admirer: a person who admires someone or something
  • antonym – critic: a person who points out faults or deficiencies
