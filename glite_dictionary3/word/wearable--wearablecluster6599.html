wearable

noun
item designed to be worn on the body
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛr.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈweə.rə.bəl / (BrE)
an item or covering designed to be worn on the body, especially clothing or electronic devices integrated into clothing

Examples

smart wearable device
Many tech companies are launching new wearables that monitor your health and fitness.
Smart wearables have become popular among athletes for tracking their performance.
