translator

noun
machine-translation software
Pronunciation: / trænsˈleɪtər / (AmE), / trænsˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a software program or online service that automatically translates text or speech from one human language into another, often in real time.

Examples

online translator app
The travel app's built-in translator converted our conversation into English instantly during the market visit.
Real-time translators are becoming essential for international teams working across different time zones and languages.
The conference provided a simultaneous translator so attendees could follow talks in several languages.

Related Terms

  • antonym – human translator: a person who translates written or spoken language from one language into another.
  • synonym – machine translation: the automatic translation of text or speech by computer software.
