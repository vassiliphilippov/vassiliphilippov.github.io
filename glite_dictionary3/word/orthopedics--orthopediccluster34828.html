orthopedics

noun
medical specialty for bones and joints
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˈpidɪks / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈpiːdɪks / (BrE)
the branch of medicine dealing with the prevention or correction of disorders of the bones, joints, and associated muscles and ligaments
