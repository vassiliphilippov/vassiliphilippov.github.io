to triplicate

verb
make three copies
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪplɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrɪplɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to make three copies of something; to reproduce threefold

Examples

to triplicate a document
Please triplicate this document before the meeting.
The assistant triplicated the files for each department's review.
We need to triplicate the forms for administrative purposes.
