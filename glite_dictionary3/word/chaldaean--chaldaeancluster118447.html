Chaldaean

noun
inhabitant of ancient Chaldea
Pronunciation: / kælˈdiːən / (AmE), / kælˈdiːən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of ancient Chaldea, a region in southern Babylonia (modern-day Iraq)
