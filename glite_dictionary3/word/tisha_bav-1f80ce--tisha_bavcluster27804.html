Tisha b'Av

proper noun
major Jewish fast day of mourning
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːʃə bəˈɑv / (AmE), / ˈtiːʃə bəˈɑːv / (BrE)
a major Jewish fast day commemorating the destruction of the First and Second Temples in Jerusalem, observed with mourning and fasting

Examples

Tisha b'Av fast day
Jews traditionally fast and refrain from celebration on Tisha b'Av.
Many synagogues hold special services on Tisha b'Av to mourn the destruction of the Temple.
Some communities read the Book of Lamentations aloud during Tisha b'Av observances.
