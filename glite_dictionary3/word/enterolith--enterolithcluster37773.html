enterolith

noun
intestinal stone or calculus
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.tə.roʊ.lɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tə.rəʊ.lɪθ / (BrE)
A stone-like mass (calculus) formed in the intestines.

Examples

intestinal enterolith removal
An enterolith may require surgical intervention if it becomes too large or obstructive.
Chronic digestive issues can sometimes lead to the formation of an enterolith.
