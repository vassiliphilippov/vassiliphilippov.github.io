territorial

adjective
relating to a territory or area
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈtɔriəl /; / ˌtɛr.əˈtɔr.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌtɛrɪˈtɔːriəl /; / ˌtɛr.ɪˈtɔː.ri.əl / (BrE)
relating to or connected with a particular territory, area, or the territory of a state or ruler
