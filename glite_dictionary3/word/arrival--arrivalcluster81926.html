arrival

noun
achievement of a goal or objective
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪvəl / (AmE), / əˈraɪvəl / (BrE)
the achievement of a goal or objective, especially after considerable effort or anticipation

Examples

arrival of success
Her arrival as a leading expert in the field was the result of years of dedication.
The company's arrival at international prominence was marked by its groundbreaking new product.
Winning the award signified his arrival in the world of literature.
