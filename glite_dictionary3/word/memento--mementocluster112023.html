memento

noun
object kept as a reminder
Pronunciation: / məˈmɛnˌtoʊ / (AmE), / məˈmɛntəʊ / (BrE)
An object kept as a reminder or souvenir of a person or event.

Examples

memento from childhood
He wore his grandfather's watch as a memento of their time together.
She kept the old postcard as a memento of her trip to Paris.
The team gave their coach a signed football as a memento of the season.

  • synonym – souvenir: an object kept as a reminder of a place, event, or person
