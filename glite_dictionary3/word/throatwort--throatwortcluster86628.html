throatwort

noun
European bellflower used medicinally
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊtˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊtˌwɜːt / (BrE)
A European bellflower with blue-purple to lilac flowers, formerly used to treat sore throats.
