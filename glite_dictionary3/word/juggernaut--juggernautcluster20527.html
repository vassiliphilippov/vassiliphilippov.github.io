juggernaut

noun
an overwhelming, crushing force
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡɚˌnɔt /; / ˈdʒʌɡərˌnɔt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡənɔːt / (BrE)
a massive, powerful, and unstoppable force or institution that crushes anything in its way

Examples

The movie was a box office juggernaut, dominating ticket sales for months.
The new tech company quickly became a juggernaut in the industry, eclipsing all competitors.
No one could stand in the way of the political juggernaut sweeping the nation.
The film franchise grew into a financial juggernaut unmatched by any other studio.
The new smartphone launch quickly became a juggernaut in the tech industry.
