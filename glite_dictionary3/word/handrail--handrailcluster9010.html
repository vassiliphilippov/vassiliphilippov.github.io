handrail

noun
a rail for support on stairs or balconies
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌreɪl / (AmE), / ˈhændreɪl / (BrE)
a rail fixed alongside a staircase, balcony, or other area, designed to be held by the hand for support or to prevent falling

Examples

handrail on stairs
A new handrail was installed on the balcony to improve safety.
She grasped the handrail tightly as she climbed the slippery stairs.
The old man leaned on the handrail for support while descending.

Related Terms

  • synonym – banister: the structure formed by the handrail and its supporting posts on a staircase
