to honor

verb
accept a check or payment instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnɚ / (AmE), / ˈɒnə / (BrE)
(of a bank or business) to accept or pay the amount of a check, bill, or other payment instrument when presented.

Examples

honor a check
If the signature is missing, the company may refuse to honor the draft.
The bank will honor this check if you present it today.
We always honor valid payment orders from our clients.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dishonor: refuse to accept or pay a check, bill, etc.
