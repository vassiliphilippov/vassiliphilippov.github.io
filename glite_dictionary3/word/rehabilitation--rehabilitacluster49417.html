rehabilitation

noun
restoration to normal life or society
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌriː.həˌbɪl.ɪˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of helping someone to return to normal life or to a useful place in society, especially after imprisonment, illness, or addiction

Examples

rehabilitation after prison
After his release from prison, he entered a rehabilitation program to help him adjust to society.
Effective rehabilitation is crucial for patients recovering from serious injuries or addiction.
The government invested in rehabilitation centers to reduce recidivism and support social reintegration.

  • synonym – recovery: the process of becoming healthy after an illness or injury
