emission

noun
a substance released into the environment
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
a substance, such as gas or radiation, that is released into the air or environment

Examples

car emissions problem
Car emissions contribute significantly to air pollution in the city.
Scientists are monitoring the emissions from the power plant to ensure compliance with safety standards.
The factory was fined for its excessive emissions of harmful chemicals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pollutant: a substance that pollutes, especially one released into the environment
