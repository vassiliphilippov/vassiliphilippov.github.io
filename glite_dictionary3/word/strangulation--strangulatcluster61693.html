strangulation

noun
stopping breathing by throat compression
Pronunciation: / ˌstræŋɡjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌstraŋɡjʊˈleɪʃ(ə)n /; / ˌstræŋɡjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or condition of stopping someone from breathing by squeezing or compressing the neck
