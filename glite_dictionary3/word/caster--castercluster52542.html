caster

noun
pivoting wheel for moving furniture or equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstə / (BrE)
a small wheel, usually mounted on a swivel, attached to the bottom of furniture, equipment, or machinery to make it easier to move.
