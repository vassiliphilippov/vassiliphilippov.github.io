pyjama

noun
loose-fitting sleepwear; jacket and trousers
Pronunciation: / pəˈdʒæmə / (AmE), / pɪˈdʒɑːmə / (BrE)
loose-fitting clothes consisting of a jacket top and trousers, worn for sleeping or lounging, usually in the plural form

Examples

pyjamas for sleeping
He changed into his pyjamas before going to bed.
She bought a new pair of silk pyjamas for the trip.
The children wore matching pyjamas at the sleepover.
