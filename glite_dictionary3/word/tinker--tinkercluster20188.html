tinker

noun
itinerant repairer of metal utensils
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋkɚ /; / ˈtɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who repairs, adjusts, or experiments with things, especially in a casual or amateur way (historically, a traveling repairer of pots and pans)
