senescent

adjective
growing old or aging
Pronunciation: / səˈnɛsənt / (AmE), / sɪˈniːsənt / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) growing old; showing signs of aging or deterioration due to age.

Examples

senescent trees
Even the most robust trees eventually become senescent and stop producing new leaves.

The senescent population in many countries is creating challenges for healthcare systems.
