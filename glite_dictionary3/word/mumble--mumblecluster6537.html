mumble

noun
soft, indistinct utterance
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌmbəl / (BrE)
a soft, indistinct utterance that is difficult to understand

Examples

a quiet mumble
He responded with a barely audible mumble that left everyone confused.
Her nervous mumble made it hard to catch what she was saying.
The teacher asked him to repeat his answer because his mumble was unclear.

Related Terms

  • synonym – murmur: a low, continuous, indistinct sound; synonym for quiet utterance
  • synonym – mutter: to speak or say something in a low or barely audible voice; also a synonym for indistinct speech
