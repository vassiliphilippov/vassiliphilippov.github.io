co-optation

noun
the act of appointing summarily
Pronunciation: / koʊ.ɑpˈteɪ.ʃən /; / koʊˌɑpˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / kəʊ.ɒpˈteɪ.ʃən /; / kəʊˌɒpˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of appointing someone to a group or position, often by decision of existing members instead of a general election or usual process
