to rap

verb
strike or hit sharply
Pronunciation: / ræp / (AmE), / ræp / (BrE)
to hit or knock quickly and lightly, making a short sound

Examples

to rap the table
He rapped his knuckles sharply against the window to call the dog.
She rapped on the door and waited for a response.
The teacher rapped the table with a ruler to get everyone's attention.
He rapped lightly at the door, not wanting to wake the whole house.
She began to rap her nails on the counter while waiting.
The rain rapped steadily on the tin roof all night long.
