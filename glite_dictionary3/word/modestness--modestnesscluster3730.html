modestness

noun
lack of vanity or conceit
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑː.dɪst.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɒd.ɪst.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being free from vanity or conceit; humility or lack of self-importance

Examples

modestness in attitude

Her modestness earned her the respect of colleagues who appreciated her humility.
People admired her modestness in accepting praise without boasting.
