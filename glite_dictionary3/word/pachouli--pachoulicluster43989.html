pachouli

noun
perfume from the patchouli plant
Pronunciation: / pəˈtʃoʊli / (AmE), / pəˈtʃəʊli / (BrE)
A strong, heavy perfume made from the oil of the patchouli plant.

Examples

pachouli perfume scent
She dabbed a little pachouli on her wrists before leaving the house.
Some people find the smell of pachouli too strong, while others love its earthy aroma.
The shop was filled with the unmistakable scent of pachouli.
