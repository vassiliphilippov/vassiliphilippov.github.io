to abrase

verb
wear away by rubbing or scraping
Pronunciation: / əˈbreɪs / (AmE), / əˈbreɪz / (BrE)
to wear away the surface of something by rubbing or scraping; to erode.

Examples

to abrase a surface
Repeated washing can abrase delicate fabrics, causing them to thin over time.
The technician used a fine grit paper to abrase the metal before painting.
Years of wind and sand have slowly abrased the surface of the ancient monument.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abrade: to wear away or rub off through friction
  • synonym – erode: to gradually wear away
