pedestrian

noun
person walking on foot
Pronunciation: / pəˈdɛstriən / (AmE), / pəˈdɛstriən / (BrE)

Examples

During rush hour, hundreds of pedestrians fill the sidewalks downtown.
The city has built new crosswalks to keep pedestrians safe.
