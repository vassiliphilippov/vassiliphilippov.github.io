bolide

noun
especially bright, sometimes exploding meteor
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊ.laɪd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.laɪd / (BrE)
A very bright meteor that may explode in the atmosphere.

Examples

a bolide streaking sky
Astronomers observed a bolide streaking across the night sky, followed by a brilliant flash.
Cameras captured footage of a bolide illuminating the sky before it disintegrated.
The sudden explosion of the bolide startled people in several nearby towns.
