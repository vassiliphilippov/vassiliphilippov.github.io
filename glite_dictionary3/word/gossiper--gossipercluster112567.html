gossiper

noun
person who often gossips
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːsəpər / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsɪpə / (BrE)
a person who habitually talks about the private affairs of others, often spreading rumors or personal information

Examples

office gossiper
Being called a gossiper hurt her feelings, but it made her reflect on her behavior.

The gossiper in the group always manages to find out everyone's secrets.
