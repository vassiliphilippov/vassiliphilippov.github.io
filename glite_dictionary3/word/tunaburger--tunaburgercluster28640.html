tunaburger

noun
hamburger-style sandwich with tuna patty
Pronunciation: / ˈtuː.nəˌbɝː.ɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈtjuː.nəˌbɜː.ɡə / (BrE)
A sandwich similar to a hamburger, made with a cooked tuna patty instead of beef, typically served in a bun with various toppings.
