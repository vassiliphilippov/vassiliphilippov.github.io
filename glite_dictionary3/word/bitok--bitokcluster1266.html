bitok

noun
Russian meat patty dish
Pronunciation: / bɪˈtɔk / (AmE), / bɪˈtɒk / (BrE)
A Russian dish made of small patties of ground meat, often mixed with onions, bread, and milk, and typically served with a sour cream sauce.

Examples

homemade bitok patties
