bonkers

adjective
informal; crazy, mad
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑŋkərz / (AmE), / ˈbɒŋkəz / (BrE)
(informal) Crazy, mad, or behaving in a very strange way.

Examples

go bonkers after stress
After working nonstop for days, she felt like she was going bonkers.
His plan to swim across the icy river seemed completely bonkers to everyone.
Sometimes my cat goes absolutely bonkers and runs around the house for no reason.
