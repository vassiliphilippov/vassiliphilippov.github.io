elephant

noun
symbol of the U.S. Republican Party
Pronunciation: / ˈɛl.ə.fənt / (AmE), / ˈɛl.ɪ.fənt / (BrE)
the symbol of the Republican Party in the United States, originating from political cartoons by Thomas Nast in 1874.
