heterologous

adjective
from a different species (biology/medicine)
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtəˈrɑləɡəs / (AmE), / ˌhɛtəˈrɒləɡəs / (BrE)
(of tissue, cells, organs, or molecules) coming from a different species or having a different origin or structure
