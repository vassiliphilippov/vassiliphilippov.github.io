Pinus monticola

noun
a tall western North American pine tree
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnəs mɑnˈtaɪkələ / (AmE), / ˈpɪnəs mɒnˈtɪkələ / (BrE)
a tall pine tree native to western North America, with stout blue-green needles and grey-brown bark that develops rectangular plates as it matures
