neopallium

noun
part of the cerebral cortex
Pronunciation: / niː.oʊˈpæli.əm / (AmE), / niː.əʊˈpælɪ.əm / (BrE)
the most recently evolved part of the cerebral cortex, involved in higher brain functions such as sensory perception, cognition, and motor commands
