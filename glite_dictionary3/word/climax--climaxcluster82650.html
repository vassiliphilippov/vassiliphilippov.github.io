climax

noun
most severe stage of a disease
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪ.mæks / (AmE), / ˈklaɪ.maks / (BrE)
the most intense or severe stage of a disease, when symptoms are at their peak

Examples

climax of illness
After several days of worsening symptoms, the patient reached the climax of the illness.
Doctors monitored her closely as she approached the climax of the disease.
The fever persisted until the climax, after which her condition gradually improved.
