wafer

noun
a thin, crisp cake or cookie
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪfər / (AmE), / ˈweɪfə / (BrE)
a very thin, crisp cake or cookie, often sweet and sometimes used in desserts or as a garnish

Examples

chocolate wafer cookie
He enjoyed his coffee with a vanilla wafer on the side.
She placed a wafer on top of the ice cream sundae for decoration.
The bakery sold chocolate-filled wafers that were light and crunchy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – biscuit: a small baked unleavened cake, typically crisp, sometimes soft (Brit.)
  • synonym – cookie: a small sweet baked treat, crisp or chewy (Am.)
