uninjured

adjective
not harmed or wounded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɪndʒɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɪndʒəd / (BrE)
not harmed or wounded; not having suffered physical or mental injury.

Examples

remain uninjured after accident
Despite the fall, he was completely uninjured and ready to continue the hike.
Miraculously, the child was found uninjured after being lost in the forest for hours.
The passengers escaped the accident uninjured, thanks to their seat belts.
