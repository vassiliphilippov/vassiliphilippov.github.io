incoming

adjective
about to take office; successor
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
about to assume a position or office, especially as a successor to another person

Examples

incoming president
Preparations are underway for the transition to the incoming administration.
The incoming president addressed the nation after the election results were announced.
The board met to introduce the incoming director to the staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym – outgoing: leaving or relinquishing a position or office
