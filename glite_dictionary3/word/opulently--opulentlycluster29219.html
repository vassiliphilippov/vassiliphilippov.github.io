opulently

adverb
in a rich or luxurious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.pjə.lənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɒp.jʊ.lənt.li / (BrE)

Examples

to live opulently

The dining room was opulently decorated with gold-plated furniture and sparkling chandeliers.
The hotel lobby is opulently furnished to impress its wealthy guests.
