acousticophobia

noun
fear of sounds or noise
Pronunciation: / əˌkuːstɪkoʊˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / əˌkuːstɪkəʊˈfəʊbɪə / (BrE)
A morbid or pathological fear of sounds, including noises or even one's own voice.

Examples

severe acousticophobia symptoms
Her acousticophobia made it difficult for her to participate in group conversations.
People with acousticophobia may avoid social situations to escape exposure to sounds or their own voice.
The patient was diagnosed with acousticophobia after developing severe anxiety around everyday noises.
