caffeinism

noun
caffeine poisoning; excessive caffeine intoxication
Pronunciation: / kæˈfiːnɪzəm / (AmE), / kəˈfiːnɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
A condition of poisoning or intoxication resulting from excessive consumption of caffeine-containing products.

Examples

severe caffeinism symptoms
Doctors diagnosed her with caffeinism after she complained of anxiety and palpitations.
Heavy coffee drinkers may experience caffeinism if they suddenly increase their intake.
Symptoms of caffeinism can include restlessness, insomnia, and rapid heartbeat.
