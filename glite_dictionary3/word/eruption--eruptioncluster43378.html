eruption

noun
sudden appearance of a symptom, often on the skin
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
an instance of something, especially a symptom or skin condition, suddenly appearing and becoming visible on the surface

Examples

skin eruption
After exposure to the allergen, a red eruption appeared on his arms.
Dermatologists are trained to diagnose the cause of a sudden skin eruption.
The patient developed an itchy eruption after taking the new medication.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rash: an area of red spots on the skin, often due to illness or irritation
