to sick

verb
to vomit (British, informal)
Pronunciation: / sɪk / (AmE), / sɪk / (BrE)
(British, informal) to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth; to vomit.

Examples

to sick on the floor
He drank too much and then sicked all over the floor.
I had to pull over because I thought I was going to sick in the car.
The child felt ill and sicked after eating too much cake.
