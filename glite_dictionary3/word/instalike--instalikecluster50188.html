to instalike

verb
instantly like something on social media
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɪnstəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
to instantly show approval for something on social media, especially by clicking a 'like' button soon after it is posted

Examples

to instalike a photo
He instaliked the announcement and then commented to show his support.
It's flattering when someone instalikes your story the moment it goes live.
She always instalikes her friends' photos within seconds of them being posted.
