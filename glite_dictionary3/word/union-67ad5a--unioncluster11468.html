union

noun
organization of employees formed for collective bargaining
Pronunciation: / ˈjun.jən / (AmE), / ˈjuː.njən / (BrE)
an organization of workers formed to protect and further their rights and interests through collective bargaining with employers

Examples

union of workers
All new employees are required to join the union before they start work.
He served as president of the teachers' union for four years.
The union negotiated better pay and benefits for its members last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – labor union: an organization of workers formed to advocate for their rights and interests
  • synonym – trade union: an organized association of workers in a trade, group of trades, or profession
