Señora

noun
Spanish title for a married woman
Pronunciation: / seɪˈnjɔɾə / (AmE), / seɪˈnjɔːrə / (BrE)
a Spanish title or form of address for a married woman; equivalent to 'Mrs.' or 'madam'
