numberplate

noun
vehicle plate showing registration number
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌmbɚˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbəˌpleɪt / (BrE)
A plate fixed to the front and back of a motor vehicle, displaying its registration number.

Examples

numberplate on a car
European numberplates often look different from those in the United States.
She reported the stolen vehicle by giving the police its numberplate.
The car's numberplate was dirty and difficult to read.
