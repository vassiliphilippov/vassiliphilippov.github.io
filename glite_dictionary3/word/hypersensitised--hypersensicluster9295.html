hypersensitised

adjective
abnormally sensitive (esp. to allergens)
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ.pɚˈsɛn.sɪ.taɪzd / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.pəˈsɛn.sɪ.taɪzd / (BrE)
having an abnormally increased sensitivity, especially to an allergen or other specific factor

Examples

hypersensitised to pollen
After the vaccine, some patients became hypersensitised to common environmental allergens.
Because of his condition, he was hypersensitised to even the smallest traces of pollen in the air.
Her skin became hypersensitised after repeated exposure to the new detergent.

Related Terms

  • synonym – allergic: having an allergy or sensitivity to substances
  • antonym – desensitised: having reduced sensitivity
