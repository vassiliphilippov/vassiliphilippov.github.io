Muhammad

proper noun
the prophet and founder of Islam
Pronunciation: / muˈhɑːməd / (AmE), / mʊˈhɑːməd / (BrE)
The Arab prophet (570–632) who, according to Islam, was the last messenger of Allah and the founder of Islam.

Examples

Muhammad the Prophet
Historians regard Muhammad as one of the most influential figures in world history.
Many Muslims travel to Mecca to honor the Prophet Muhammad.
The teachings of Muhammad are recorded in the Hadith and the Qur'an.
