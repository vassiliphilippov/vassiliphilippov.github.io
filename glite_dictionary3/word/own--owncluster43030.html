own

adjective
emphasizing possession (adj.)
Pronunciation: / oʊn / (AmE), / əʊn / (BrE)
used after a possessive to emphasize that something belongs to a particular person or thing and not to anyone else.

Examples

her own room
Every child needs their own room.
He always brings his own lunch to work.
She wanted a place to call her own.

  • synonym – personal: referring or relating to a particular person
