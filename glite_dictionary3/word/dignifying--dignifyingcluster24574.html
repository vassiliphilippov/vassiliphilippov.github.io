dignifying

adjective
conferring dignity or honor
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪɡnɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪɡnɪˌfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
making something or someone appear worthy of dignity or honor; conferring a sense of respect or nobility.

Examples

dignifying gesture
The artist's moving tribute had a dignifying effect on the ceremony.
The old architecture lends a dignifying atmosphere to the entire neighborhood.
There is something dignifying about standing up for your values.
