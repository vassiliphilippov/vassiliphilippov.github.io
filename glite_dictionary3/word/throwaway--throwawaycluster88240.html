throwaway

noun
casual or lightly spoken remark
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊ.əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊ.əˌweɪ / (BrE)
a remark or comment spoken casually and without emphasis, often not intended to be taken seriously

Examples

a throwaway comment
He made a throwaway about the weather, but it stuck with me all day.
Her funniest line was actually a throwaway that the audience loved.
Most of his best jokes seemed like throwaways, delivered so casually you might miss them.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aside: a remark made in passing, often not intended for all to hear
