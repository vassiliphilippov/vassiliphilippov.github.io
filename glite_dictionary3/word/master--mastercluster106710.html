master

noun
officer in command of a merchant ship
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstə / (BrE)
an officer who is licensed to command a merchant ship

Examples

master of ship
After a thorough inspection, the master decided it was safe to set sail.
He has worked his way up from seaman to master of the vessel.
The master is responsible for the safety of the crew and cargo.
