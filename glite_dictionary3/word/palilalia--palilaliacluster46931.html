palilalia

noun
pathological repetition of words
Pronunciation: / ˌpæləˈleɪliə / (AmE), / ˌpalɪˈleɪlɪə / (BrE)
A medical condition in which a person involuntarily and rapidly repeats words or phrases, especially their own speech.

Palilalia is commonly seen in some neurological disorders, including Tourette syndrome.
