to qualify

verb
become fit or eligible; meet requirements
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑlɪˌfaɪ /; / ˈkwɑːləˌfaɪ /; / ˈkwɑl.ɪ.faɪ / (AmE), / ˈkwɒlɪfaɪ /; / ˈkwɒl.ɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to have or show the necessary skills, experience, or qualities to be allowed to do something or be chosen for something
