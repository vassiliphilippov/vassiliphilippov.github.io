equipping

noun
the act of supplying equipment
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of providing or supplying with equipment, especially weapons or other necessities for a particular purpose

Examples

equipping an expedition
He oversaw the equipping of the expedition with the latest survival gear.
The equipping of the hospital with modern technology took several months.
The army's rapid equipping with new rifles made a noticeable difference on the battlefield.
