VA

noun
virtual assistant (remote or AI helper)
Pronunciation: / viː eɪ / (AmE), / viː eɪ / (BrE)
abbreviation for virtual assistant: a person or software agent who performs administrative or other tasks remotely, often for multiple clients.

Examples

hire a VA
I hired a VA to handle customer inquiries and calendar management remotely.
Many small businesses rely on a VA to manage emails, scheduling, and social media posts.
After hours, their AI-powered VA can draft replies and prioritize urgent messages automatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym – assistant: a person or tool that helps with tasks; a broader term including in-person and virtual helpers
  • synonym – virtual assistant: a remote worker or software tool that performs tasks like scheduling, email management, and customer support
