resolve

noun
formal decision made by vote at a meeting
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɑlv / (AmE), / rɪˈzɒlv / (BrE)
a formal statement of a decision or opinion agreed upon by a meeting or assembly, usually reached by voting

Examples

committee resolve
After much discussion, the committee adopted a resolve supporting the new policy.
The board passed a resolve to increase funding for the program.
The council's resolve was recorded in the official minutes of the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resolution: a formal statement of a decision or intention by an official group
