to breast

verb
to push through or reach with the chest
Pronunciation: / brɛst / (AmE), / brɛst / (BrE)
to move forward with the chest or to reach the top of something

Examples

to breast a hill
As he reached the summit, he proudly breasted the final ridge.
The runner breasted the tape just ahead of her rivals.
They breasted the steep hill despite strong winds.
Once we breast the final hill, we will see the valley spread out below.
The climbers breasted the ridge just before dawn, exhausted but exhilarated by the view.
The hikers paused to catch their breath after breasting the steep incline.
