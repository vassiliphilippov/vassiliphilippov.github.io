grace

noun
prayer before a meal
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪs / (AmE), / ɡreɪs / (BrE)
a short prayer of thanks said before or after a meal

Examples

say grace before dinner
At Thanksgiving, everyone joined hands while Grandpa said grace.
Before dinner, the family always pauses to say grace.
Would you like to lead us in grace this evening?
