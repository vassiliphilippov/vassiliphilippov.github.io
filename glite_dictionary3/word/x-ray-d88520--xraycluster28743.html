to x-ray

verb
to take an x-ray photograph (verb)
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksˌreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛks.reɪ /; / ˈɛksˌreɪ / (BrE)
to examine or photograph the inside of something (especially the body) using x-rays, usually for medical reasons
