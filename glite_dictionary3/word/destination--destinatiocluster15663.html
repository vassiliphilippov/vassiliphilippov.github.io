destination

noun
the goal or purpose aimed for
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛstəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the ultimate goal or purpose that someone intends to achieve or that something is meant to accomplish

Examples

final destination
For her, becoming a doctor was the destination that justified all her hard work.
In the pursuit of knowledge, the journey is often as important as the destination.
Our strategy needs to be clear about the destination we want to reach as a company.
