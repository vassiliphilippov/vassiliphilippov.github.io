ignoramus

noun
an ignorant person
Pronunciation: / ˌɪɡnəˈreɪməs / (AmE), / ˌɪɡnəˈreɪməs / (BrE)
a person who is ignorant or lacks knowledge in a particular subject or in general

Examples

complete ignoramus
Only an ignoramus would believe such obvious misinformation.

Though he was called an ignoramus, he proved everyone wrong by solving the problem.
