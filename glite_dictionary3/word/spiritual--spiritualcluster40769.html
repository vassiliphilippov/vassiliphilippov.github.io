spiritual

adjective
relating to the spirit or soul
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting the human spirit or soul rather than material or physical things

Examples

spiritual growth
Her spiritual beliefs guided her through difficult times.
Many people seek spiritual fulfillment through meditation or prayer.
The retreat offered both physical and spiritual renewal.

Related Terms

  • antonym – material: relating to physical objects rather than the spirit
  • synonym – religious: relating to religion or the belief in a higher power
