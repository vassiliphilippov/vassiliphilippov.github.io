stint

noun
a small American sandpiper
Pronunciation: / stɪnt / (AmE), / stɪnt / (BrE)
a very small sandpiper, especially the smallest species found in North America

Examples

a stint wading
During migration, flocks of stints can be seen resting on the beaches.
The stint foraged for insects along the muddy shoreline.
The smallest stint species breeds in northern Alaska and Siberia.
