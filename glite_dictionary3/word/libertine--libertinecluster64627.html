libertine

noun
morally unrestrained person
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbərˌtin / (AmE), / ˈlɪb.ə.tiːn / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who behaves without moral principles or a sense of responsibility, particularly in sexual matters

Examples

a libertine man
His reputation as a libertine was well known throughout the city.
She warned her daughter about falling for a notorious libertine.
The novel's protagonist is a charming libertine who disregards society's rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym – profligate: a person who is recklessly extravagant or wasteful in the use of resources; also dissolute
  • antonym – puritan: a person with strict moral beliefs, especially about sexual behavior
