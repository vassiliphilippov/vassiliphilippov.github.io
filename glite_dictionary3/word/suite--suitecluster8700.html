suite

noun
matching set of furniture
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
a set of matching furniture pieces designed to be used together, typically for a particular room

Examples

suite of furniture
She chose a dining suite that matched the style of her home.
The bedroom suite includes a bed, two nightstands, and a wardrobe.
We bought a new living room suite with a sofa and two armchairs.
