iontotherapy

noun
therapy using electric current to deliver ions
Pronunciation: / aɪˌɑntoʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / aɪˌɒntəˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a medical treatment that uses an electric current to introduce medicinal ions into bodily tissues through the skin
