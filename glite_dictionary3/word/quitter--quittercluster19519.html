quitter

noun
person who gives up easily
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkwɪtə / (BrE)
a person who gives up easily or does not finish something

Examples

a quitter in sports
Even when things got tough, she refused to be a quitter.
He was labeled a quitter after he dropped out of the competition halfway.
Nobody likes a quitter, so try to see the project through to the end.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resigner: a person who gives up, especially by resigning
