relief

noun
military freeing of a besieged place
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlif / (AmE), / rɪˈliːf / (BrE)
the act of freeing a place, especially a city or town, from siege or enemy encirclement, typically by military action

Examples

relief of a city
During the war, the relief of the fortress was celebrated as a turning point.
He wrote a report on the relief of the town after weeks of blockade.
The army advanced quickly to bring relief to the besieged city.
