calliope

noun
musical instrument of steam whistles
Pronunciation: / kəˈlaɪəpi / (AmE), / kəˈlʌɪəpi / (BrE)
A musical instrument with a series of steam whistles played from a keyboard, often used in circuses and fairs.

Examples

calliope music at a fair
A vintage calliope was on display at the fair, delighting children with its cheerful tunes.
He learned to play the calliope for performances on the historic riverboat.
The sound of the calliope filled the air as the circus parade passed through town.
