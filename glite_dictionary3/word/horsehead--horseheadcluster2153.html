horsehead

noun
a type of silvery, flat-bodied marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌhɛd / (BrE)
any of several silvery marine fishes with very flat bodies, especially of the genus Eques, distinguished by their slender, compressed forms and prevalent in warm seas.

horsehead fish species
