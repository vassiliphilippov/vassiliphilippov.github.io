miasmic

adjective
full of vapor or miasma
Pronunciation: / miˈæzmɪk /; / maɪˈæzmɪk / (AmE), / mɪˈazmɪk / (BrE)
filled with or resembling a thick, unpleasant, and unhealthy vapor or smell, often caused by pollution or decay
