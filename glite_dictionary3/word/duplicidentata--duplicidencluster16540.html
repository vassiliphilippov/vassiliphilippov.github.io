Duplicidentata

noun
former rodent suborder for lagomorphs
Pronunciation: / ˌdjuːplɪsɪdɛnˈteɪtə / (AmE), / ˌdjuːplɪsɪdɛnˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
an artificial former suborder of rodents, now synonymous with the order Lagomorpha, comprising gnawing mammals such as rabbits, hares, and pikas

Modern taxonomy has replaced Duplicidentata with the order Lagomorpha for rabbits and hares.
