hyperidrosis

noun
excessive sweating disorder
Pronunciation: / haɪˌpɜrɪˈdroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpərɪˈdrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
abnormally excessive sweating of the body, often unrelated to heat or exercise

Examples

hyperidrosis of the hands
Hyperidrosis can affect the hands, feet, underarms, or face, leading to discomfort in daily life.
Patients with hyperidrosis often feel embarrassed by their constant sweating.
Treatment options for hyperidrosis can include prescription antiperspirants and even surgery in severe cases.
