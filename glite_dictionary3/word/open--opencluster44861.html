open

noun
sports tournament open to all competitors
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
A sports tournament in which both professionals and amateurs are allowed to compete.

Examples

the French Open
Many tennis fans look forward to watching the French Open every spring.

The US Open attracts players from all over the world, both amateur and professional.
