Chiron

proper noun
wise centaur in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪˌrɒn / (AmE), / ˈkaɪ.rən / (BrE)
A wise and learned centaur in Greek mythology who tutored many famous heroes, including Achilles, Asclepius, Hercules, and Jason.

Examples

Chiron teaches heroes
According to legend, Chiron was known for his kindness and medical knowledge.
Many heroes, such as Hercules and Achilles, were trained by Chiron in their youth.
Stories of Chiron often highlight the contrast between his wisdom and the wild nature of other centaurs.
