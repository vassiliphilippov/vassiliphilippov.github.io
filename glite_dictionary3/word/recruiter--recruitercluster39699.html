recruiter

noun
person who enlists for military service
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkruːtər / (AmE), / rɪˈkruːtə / (BrE)
an official whose job is to enlist people for military service

Examples

recruiter enlists soldiers
A recruiter visited our college campus to discuss opportunities in the air force.
After passing the entrance exam, she received a call from a military recruiter.
The recruiter explained the benefits of joining the army to the high school students.

Related Terms

  • synonym – enlistment officer: an official whose job involves enlisting personnel, especially for military service
