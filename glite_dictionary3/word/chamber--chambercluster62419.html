chamber

noun
an enclosed space, natural or artificial
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪmbər /; / ˈtʃeɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪmbə / (BrE)
an enclosed space, natural or artificial, inside something larger, such as a cave, a part of the body, or a structure

Examples
