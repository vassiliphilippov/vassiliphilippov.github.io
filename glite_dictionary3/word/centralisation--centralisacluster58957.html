centralisation

noun
the act of concentrating control in a center
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of bringing control, power, or activities under a single central authority or location
