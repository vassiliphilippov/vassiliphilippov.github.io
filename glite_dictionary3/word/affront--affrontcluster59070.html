affront

noun
deliberate act or remark causing offense
Pronunciation: / əˈfrʌnt / (AmE), / əˈfrʌnt / (BrE)
a deliberate act or remark that causes offense or shows a lack of respect

Examples

a personal affront
Her refusal to shake hands was seen as a personal affront by the other guest.
Ignoring the invitation altogether was a clear affront to tradition.
Many considered his comments an affront to the community's values.

Related Terms

  • antonym – compliment: a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym – insult: a remark or action that is rude or offensive
