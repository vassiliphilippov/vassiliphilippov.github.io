HND

noun
vocational diploma (mainly UK)
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪtʃ ˌɛn ˈdi / (AmE), / ˌeɪtʃ ˌɛn ˈdiː / (BrE)
a vocational diploma awarded in the UK and some other countries, typically after two years of study, that prepares students for a particular career or further study toward a degree

Examples

HND in business
After completing her HND in business administration, she found a job with a large advertising firm.
He enrolled in an HND course in engineering at the local technical college.
Many students with an HND can enter the final year of a bachelor's degree program.
