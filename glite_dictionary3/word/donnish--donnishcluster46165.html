donnish

adjective
scholarly or pedantic; academic-like
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑː.nɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɒn.ɪʃ / (BrE)
Behaving in a way that is characteristic of an academic, especially in being overly scholarly or pedantic.

Examples

donnish attitude
His donnish manner sometimes intimidated students who preferred a more informal approach.
She offered a donnish critique of the film, focusing on obscure narrative techniques.
The meeting took on a donnish tone, with participants quoting academic studies and rare texts.
