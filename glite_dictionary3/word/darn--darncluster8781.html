darn

noun
a sewn repair in fabric
Pronunciation: / dɑrn / (AmE), / dɑːn / (BrE)
A repair made by sewing over a worn or torn hole, especially in a garment.

Examples

a neat darn
Her skillful darn was almost invisible, blending in with the sock's original pattern.
I made a careful darn in the elbow of my favorite sweater.
The old scarf was full of darn after years of use and repair.
