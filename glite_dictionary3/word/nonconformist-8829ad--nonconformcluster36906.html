nonconformist

noun
person who does not conform
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈfɔrmɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːmɪst / (BrE)
a person who does not conform to established customs, attitudes, or standards of conduct

Examples

a nonconformist thinker
As a lifelong nonconformist, she often challenged the status quo in her community.
His nonconformist attitude made him both admired and misunderstood by his peers.
Many artists are proud to be called nonconformists for their unique perspectives.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conformist: person who conforms to established customs or standards
  • synonym – rebel: person who resists authority or control
