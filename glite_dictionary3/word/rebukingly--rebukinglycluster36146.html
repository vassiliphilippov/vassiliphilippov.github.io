rebukingly

adverb
in a rebuking manner
Pronunciation: / rɪˈbjuːkɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈbjuːkɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner showing disapproval or criticism; in a way that expresses a rebuke.

Examples

look rebukingly at someone
He shook his head rebukingly after seeing the mess in the kitchen.
She looked at him rebukingly when he interrupted the meeting.
The teacher spoke rebukingly to the students who were chatting in class.
