luxuriation

noun
enjoyment of luxury or pleasure
Pronunciation: / lʌɡˌzjʊriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / lʌɡˌzjʊəriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of taking great pleasure, ease, or comfort, especially in luxury

Examples

luxuriation in comfort
After weeks of hard work, she allowed herself a weekend of luxuriation at a spa.
His luxuriation in fine food and wine was well-known among his friends.
The novel describes the luxuriation of its characters in the opulent surroundings of the palace.

Related Terms

  • antonym – asceticism: severe self-discipline and avoidance of all forms of indulgence
  • synonym – luxury: great comfort, especially as provided by expensive and beautiful things
