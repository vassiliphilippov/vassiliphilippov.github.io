nonsynchronous

adjective
not occurring at the same time
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs / (BrE)
not occurring at the same time or not operating in unison with something else.

Examples

nonsynchronous schedules
Because their schedules were nonsynchronous, they struggled to find time to meet.
The system failed due to the nonsynchronous timing of its components.
The two machines have nonsynchronous operations, which can lead to communication problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym – asynchronous: not happening or operating at the same time
  • antonym – synchronous: happening or operating at the same time
