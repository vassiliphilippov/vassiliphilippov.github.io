pretense

noun
false claim or appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌtɛns, prɪˈtɛns /; / ˈpriːˌtɛns /; / ˈpriː.tɛns / (AmE), / ˈpriːtɛns, prɪˈtɛns /; / ˈpriː.tɛns /; / ˈpriː.təns /; / ˈpriːtɛns / (BrE)
an attempt to make something that is not true appear true; a false claim, display, or reason used to deceive or hide the real situation
