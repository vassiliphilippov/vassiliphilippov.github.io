direction

noun
formal instruction or order
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkʃən, daɪˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃən, daɪˈrɛkʃən /; / dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
an official instruction, order, or set of instructions telling someone what to do or how to do something

Examples
