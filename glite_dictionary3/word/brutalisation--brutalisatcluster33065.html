brutalisation

noun
treating someone with extreme cruelty
Pronunciation: / ˌbruːtəlɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbruːtələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbruːtəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbruːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbruːtələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of treating someone with extreme cruelty or violence, or making someone cruel or insensitive, often by exposing them to violence
