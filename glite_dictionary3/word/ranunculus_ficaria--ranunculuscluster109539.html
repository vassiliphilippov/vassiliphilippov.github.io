Ranunculus ficaria

noun
European perennial herb with yellow flowers; lesser celandine
Pronunciation: / rəˈnʌnkjələs fɪˈkɛəriə / (AmE), / rəˈnʌŋkjʊləs fɪˈkeəriə / (BrE)
a perennial herb native to Europe, with heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers, sometimes called lesser celandine; its tuberous roots have been used medicinally to relieve piles
