pycnogonid

noun
marine arthropod (sea spider)
Pronunciation: / pɪkˈnɑɡənɪd / (AmE), / pɪkˈnɒɡənɪd / (BrE)
any of a group of small marine arthropods, also known as sea spiders, characterized by long, slender legs and small bodies
