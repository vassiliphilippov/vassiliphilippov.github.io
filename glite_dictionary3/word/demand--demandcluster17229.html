demand

noun
desire and ability to buy goods or services
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmænd / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
the desire and ability of people to buy goods and services at given prices

Examples

demand for products
After the advertising campaign, the demand for their services doubled.
Rising incomes tend to increase the demand for new technology products.
When prices go up, the demand for that item usually falls.

Related Terms

  • antonym – supply: the amount of a commodity available for use or purchase
