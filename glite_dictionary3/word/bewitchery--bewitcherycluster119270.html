bewitchery

noun
captivating or enchanting charm
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪtʃəri / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪtʃəri / (BrE)
The quality of captivating or enchanting charm that attracts and fascinates others.

her bewitchery charms

There was a certain bewitchery in his smile that made people instantly trust him.
