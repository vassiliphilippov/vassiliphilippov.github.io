to sallow

verb
make appear yellowish or unhealthy
Pronunciation: / ˈsæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsaləʊ / (BrE)
to make (someone or something) look yellowish or unhealthy, especially in the face

Examples

Her face was sallowed by months of worry and sleepless nights.
Illness and confinement began to sallow the complexion of the entire family.
