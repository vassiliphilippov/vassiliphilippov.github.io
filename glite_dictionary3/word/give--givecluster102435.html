to give

verb
dedicate or devote entirely to a cause or person
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv / (AmE), / ɡɪv / (BrE)
to dedicate, devote, or contribute (time, effort, money, attention, etc.) to someone or something

Examples

give time
After her retirement, she decided to give her time to volunteering at the hospital.
He chose to give himself entirely to scientific research and discovery.
Many artists give their entire lives to perfecting their craft.
He proudly said he had already given at the office when asked for another donation.
Many employees give to local charities during the holiday season.
We encourage all members of the community to give generously to support the museum.
It's important to give priority to your health above all else.
Please give your full attention to the following instructions.
You should give careful thought to your future career plans.

  • synonym – devote: to give all or most of one's time or resources to (a purpose or person)

  • antonym – withhold: to refuse to give or dedicate something
