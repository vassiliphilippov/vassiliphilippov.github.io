leg

noun
animal limb used as food
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ / (AmE), / lɛɡ / (BrE)
the part of an animal's limb used as food, especially the thigh or hind limb, often prepared and eaten as meat

Examples

leg of lamb
I roasted a leg of lamb with rosemary and garlic for dinner.
She bought a leg of venison from the butcher for the holiday meal.
The restaurant is famous for its grilled chicken legs.
