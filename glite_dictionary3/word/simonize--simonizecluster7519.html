to Simonize

verb
polish a vehicle with wax
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪməˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪmənaɪz / (BrE)
to polish and treat (a motor vehicle's surface) with wax, especially using a commercial product, to achieve a shiny, protective finish

After she Simonized the motorcycle, the paintwork looked brand new.
