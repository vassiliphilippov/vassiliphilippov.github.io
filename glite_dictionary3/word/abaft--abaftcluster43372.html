abaft

adverb
toward the rear of a ship/aircraft
Pronunciation: / əˈbæft / (AmE), / əˈbɑːft / (BrE)
towards or at the rear (stern) of a ship or tail of an aircraft

Examples

abaft the ship
Passengers must not go abaft the safety barrier during takeoff.
The damaged section of the ship was abaft the engine room.
The lifeboats are located abaft the main mast.
