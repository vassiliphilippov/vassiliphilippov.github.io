rough-textured

adjective
having a coarse or uneven surface
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌf ˌtɛkstʃɚd / (AmE), / ˈrʌf ˌtɛkstʃəd / (BrE)
having a surface that feels uneven, coarse, or irregular to the touch

Examples

rough-textured fabric
She chose a rough-textured fabric for the pillows to add contrast to the sofa.
The rough-textured bark of the tree made it easy for the child to climb.
The walls of the old cottage were rough-textured and painted white.
