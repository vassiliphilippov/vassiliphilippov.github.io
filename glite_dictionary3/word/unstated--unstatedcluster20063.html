unstated

adjective
not expressed explicitly
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪtɪd / (BrE)
not expressed or made explicit in words; not stated openly or directly.

Examples

unstated feelings
Her disappointment was unstated, but I could sense it in her silence.
The company's unstated policy discouraged employees from taking long vacations.
There seemed to be an unstated rule that everyone arrived early to meetings.

Related Terms

  • antonym – explicit: stated clearly and in detail
  • synonym – implicit: suggested though not directly expressed
