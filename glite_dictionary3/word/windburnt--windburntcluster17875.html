windburnt

adjective
affected by skin irritation from wind
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌbɝnt / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌbɜːnt / (BrE)
Having skin reddened or irritated as a result of prolonged exposure to the wind.

Examples

windburnt cheeks
After a day sailing on the lake, his cheeks were windburnt and sore.
She returned from the hike with a windburnt face and chapped lips.
You should protect your skin in cold climates to avoid getting windburnt.
