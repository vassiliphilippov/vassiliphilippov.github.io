to twist

verb
move with a contorted or writhing motion
Pronunciation: / twɪst / (AmE), / twɪst / (BrE)
to move or turn the body or a part of it with a writhing or contorted motion, often as if struggling

Examples

The dog would twist its body whenever anyone tried to pick it up.
