denaturized

adjective
changed in nature, esp. chemically
Pronunciation: / diˈneɪtʃəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / diˈneɪtjəˌraɪzd / (BrE)
(of a substance) changed in nature or natural quality, especially by chemical process; a variant form of 'denatured'.

Examples

denaturized food products
Some food products are denaturized during processing to alter their properties.
The laboratory used denaturized alcohol for cleaning the equipment.
The scientist explained that the protein had been denaturized by heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – denatured: (of a substance) changed so as to lose its natural qualities
