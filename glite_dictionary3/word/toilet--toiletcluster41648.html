toilet

noun
state of ruin or failure (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔɪlət / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt / (BrE)
(informal) A state or situation in which something has been ruined, lost, or wasted, especially regarding money, effort, or reputation.
