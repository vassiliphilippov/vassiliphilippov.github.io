to remind

verb
prompt by suggesting forgotten words
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmaɪnd /; / ɹɪˈmaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈmaɪnd /; / ɹɪˈmaɪnd / (BrE)
to help someone remember something, for example by prompting them or making them think of something they need to do or have forgotten
