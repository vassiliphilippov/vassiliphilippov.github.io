pack

noun
therapeutic sheet or blanket
Pronunciation: / pæk / (AmE), / pæk / (BrE)
a sheet or blanket, either dry or wet, wrapped around the body for therapeutic purposes, especially to relieve pain or reduce fever

Examples

hot pack therapy
A hot pack can help relax sore muscles after exercise.
She wrapped a wet pack around his leg to relieve the swelling.
The nurse applied a cold pack to reduce the patient's fever.

Related Terms

  • synonym – compress: a pad of material applied to part of the body to relieve inflammation or stop bleeding
