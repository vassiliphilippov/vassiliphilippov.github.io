generalization

noun
formulating general concepts from specific cases
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛn(ə)rəlɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌdʒɛn(ə)rələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌdʒɛn(ə)rələˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the process of forming general concepts or principles by identifying common features from specific instances
