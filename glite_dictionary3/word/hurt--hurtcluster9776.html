hurt

adjective
damaged; impaired (of objects or value)
Pronunciation: / hɝːt / (AmE), / hɜːt / (BrE)
(of an object or its value) damaged or impaired in some way.

hurt reputation

The car's resale price was hurt after the accident.
The company's reputation was hurt by the data breach.
