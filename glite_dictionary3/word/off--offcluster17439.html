off

adjective
cancelled; no longer scheduled
Pronunciation: / ɑf / (AmE), / ɒf / (BrE)
(of an event or arrangement) cancelled or no longer scheduled to take place.

Examples

off due to rain
All flights are off until the storm passes.
She was disappointed when her birthday party was suddenly off.
The match is off because of the weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym – on: (of an event) scheduled or due to happen as planned
