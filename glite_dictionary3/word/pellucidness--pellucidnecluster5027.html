pellucidness

noun
quality of being transparent or clear
Pronunciation: / pəˈluːsɪdnəs / (AmE), / pɛˈluːsɪdnəs / (BrE)
The quality of allowing light to pass through with minimal scattering or distortion; clarity or transparency.

Examples

pellucidness of glass
Artists often admire the pellucidness of fine glass objects.
Scientists measured the pellucidness of the water to assess its purity.
The pellucidness of the crystal allowed us to see every detail inside.
