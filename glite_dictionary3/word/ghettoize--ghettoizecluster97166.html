to ghettoize

verb
confine or isolate in a segregated area
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛtoʊˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡɛtəʊˌaɪz / (BrE)
to confine or isolate a group of people to a segregated area, especially one that is impoverished or marginalized; to treat or make a group feel isolated or marginalized

Examples

to ghettoize a community
Many activists argue that such policies will ghettoize minority communities.
She worries that labeling certain classes as 'remedial' will ghettoize students from less privileged backgrounds.
The refugees were ghettoized on the outskirts of the capital without access to proper services.

Related Terms

  • synonym – segregate: to separate groups from each other based on race, class, or another factor
