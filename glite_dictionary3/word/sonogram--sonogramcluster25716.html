sonogram

noun
ultrasound-produced internal body image
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑː.nəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈsɒn.əˌɡræm / (BrE)
an image of the inside of the body produced by ultrasound, often used to examine a fetus or internal organs

Examples

sonogram of a baby
A sonogram can help diagnose problems with internal organs.
She keeps a framed sonogram of her first child on her desk.
The doctor showed us the baby's sonogram during the appointment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ultrasound: sound waves with frequencies above human hearing, used for imaging organs
