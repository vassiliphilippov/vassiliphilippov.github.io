Sedum rosea

noun
Eurasian mountain succulent plant
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdəm roʊˈziːə / (AmE), / ˈsiːdəm ˈrəʊziə / (BrE)
A Eurasian mountain plant with succulent, pink-tipped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers, also known as roseroot.
