upwardly

adverb
in an upward direction or toward improvement
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpwərdli / (AmE), / ˈʌpwədli / (BrE)
In a direction from a lower to a higher position, or in a way that involves improvement or higher status.

Examples

upwardly mobile
Property prices have been rising upwardly since the start of the year.
She has been moving upwardly in her company, earning several promotions in just a few years.
The results indicate an upwardly mobile trend among the younger population.
