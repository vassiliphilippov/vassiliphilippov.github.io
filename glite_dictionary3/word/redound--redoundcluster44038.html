to redound

verb
to contribute to a result, often as benefit or disadvantage
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdaʊnd / (AmE), / rɪˈdaʊnd / (BrE)
to result in or contribute to a particular consequence, especially as an advantage or disadvantage to someone
