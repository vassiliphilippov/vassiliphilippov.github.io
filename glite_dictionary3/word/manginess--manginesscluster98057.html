manginess

noun
shabby or dirty condition; shabbiness
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪndʒinəs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪndʒinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being shabby, dirty, or in poor condition, especially as a result of wearing threadbare or filthy clothing

Examples

manginess of old clothes
Despite the manginess of the old sofa, it was still comfortable to sit on.
She was embarrassed by the manginess of her coat, but could not afford a new one.
The manginess of his clothes made him look older than his years.
