light-handedly

adverb
gently or delicately
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪtˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˌlaɪtˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is delicate, gentle, or not forceful.

Examples

to handle light-handedly
He adjusted the settings light-handedly, making sure each change was subtle and precise.
She brushed the dust from the old painting light-handedly, careful not to cause any damage.
The coach praised the player for handling criticism light-handedly and staying composed.
