Galileo Galilei

proper noun
Italian astronomer and scientist (1564–1642)
Pronunciation: / ɡəˌliːlioʊ ɡæləˈleɪi / (AmE), / ɡaˌliːliəʊ ɡalɪˈleɪi / (BrE)
(1564–1642) Italian astronomer, physicist, and mathematician, known for pioneering use of the telescope in astronomy and major contributions to science.
