skunkweed

noun
a foul-smelling Rocky Mountain herb
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌŋkˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋkˌwiːd / (BrE)
A tall herb native to the Rocky Mountains, known for its sticky leaves and unpleasant odor.
