to proclaim

verb
announce officially or publicly
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkleɪm / (AmE), / prəˈkleɪm / (BrE)
to announce or state something officially or publicly, especially in a formal or emphatic way

Examples

to proclaim victory

They proclaimed him emperor in a lavish public ceremony.
