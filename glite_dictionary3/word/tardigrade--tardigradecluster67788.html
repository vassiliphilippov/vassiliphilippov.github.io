tardigrade

noun
microscopic water-dwelling animal (phylum Tardigrada)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːr.dɪˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɑː.dɪ.ɡreɪd / (BrE)
a minute water-dwelling animal of the phylum Tardigrada, also known as a water bear, noted for its extreme survival abilities
