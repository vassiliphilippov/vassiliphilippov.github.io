impossibleness

noun
state of being impossible
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɑːsəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɒsəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being impossible; the incapability of existing or occurring.

Examples

the impossibleness of success
He spoke at length on the impossibleness of such a theory ever being proven.
She smiled at the impossibleness of fitting the sofa through the narrow doorway.
The utter impossibleness of the task deterred even the bravest volunteers.
