to dismember

verb
cut off the limbs of a body
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / dɪsˈmembə / (BrE)
to cut or pull off the limbs of a person or animal; to divide a body into separate parts

Examples

to dismember a body
Forensic experts suspect that the victim was dismembered after death.
The butcher skillfully dismembered the animal for meat processing.
The tiger dismembered the tourist in a matter of minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – butcher: to kill and cut up an animal or person, especially in a violent way
  • synonym – mutilate: to inflict serious damage on a body, especially by cutting or tearing
