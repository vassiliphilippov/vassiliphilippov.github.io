to chelate

verb
form a stable ring compound with a metal ion (chemistry, verb)
Pronunciation: / ˈkiː.leɪt / (AmE), / ˈkiː.leɪt / (BrE)
(of a substance) to combine with a metal ion to form a stable ring-shaped compound, especially in a chemical reaction.

Examples

to chelate a metal
Some drugs are designed to chelate excess iron in the bloodstream.
The ligand will chelate the metal ion to form a highly stable compound.
When EDTA is added, it chelates calcium ions, effectively removing them from solution.
