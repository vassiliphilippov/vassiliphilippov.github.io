to toll

verb
ring a bell slowly (often for a death)
Pronunciation: / toʊl / (AmE), / təʊl / (BrE)
(of a bell) to ring slowly, especially as a signal or sign, often for a death or at a solemn event.

Examples

to toll a bell
A single bell tolled solemnly during the funeral procession.
Every evening, the cathedral bell tolls to signal the end of the day.
The church bells tolled at midnight, signaling the passing of the old year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ring: to make a bell produce sound by striking it
