Racine

proper noun
French tragedian and advocate of Jansenism
Pronunciation: / ræˈsiːn / (AmE), / raˈsiːn / (BrE)
(1639–1699) A French dramatist and advocate of Jansenism, known for his influential tragedies based on Greek and Roman themes.
