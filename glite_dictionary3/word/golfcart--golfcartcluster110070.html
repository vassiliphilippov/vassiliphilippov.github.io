golfcart

noun
small vehicle for golfers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑlf.kɑrt / (AmE), / ˈɡɒlf.kɑːt / (BrE)
a small motor vehicle designed to carry golfers and their equipment around a golf course

Examples

drive a golfcart
He loaded the clubs into the back of the golfcart before driving to the next hole.
The battery on our golfcart ran out halfway through the game.
They rented a golfcart to get around the course more easily.
