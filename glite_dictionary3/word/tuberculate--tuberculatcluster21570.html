tuberculate

adjective
having small rounded bumps
Pronunciation: / tuˈbɜːrkjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / tjuːˈbɜː.kjʊ.lət / (BrE)
(of a surface or organism) covered with small, rounded projections (tubercles).

Examples

tuberculate skin surface
Certain species of cactus display a tuberculate surface instead of smooth ribs.
The frog’s skin is distinctly tuberculate, giving it a rough texture to the touch.
Under the microscope, the pollen grains appeared highly tuberculate and irregular.
