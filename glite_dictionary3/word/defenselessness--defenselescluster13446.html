defenselessness

noun
state of being unable to defend oneself
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛnsləsnəs / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsləsnɪs / (BrE)
The state of being unable to protect oneself from harm or attack.

Examples

child's defenselessness
He was touched by the child's obvious defenselessness and decided to help.
Her defenselessness in the unfamiliar city made her cautious at night.
The documentary showed the defenselessness of the small animals against predators.

Related Terms

  • antonym – invulnerability: the quality of being impossible to harm or damage
  • synonym – vulnerability: the quality of being easily hurt or attacked
