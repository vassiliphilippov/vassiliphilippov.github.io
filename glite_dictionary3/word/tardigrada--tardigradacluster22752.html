Tardigrada

noun
phylum of microscopic aquatic invertebrates
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑrdɪˈɡreɪdə /; / tɑːrˈdɪɡrədə / (AmE), / ˌtɑːdɪˈɡreɪdə /; / tɑːˈdɪɡrədə / (BrE)
a phylum of tiny, water-dwelling animals with four pairs of legs, known for surviving extreme conditions; also called water bears or moss piglets
