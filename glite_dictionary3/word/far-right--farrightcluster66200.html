far-right

adjective
at the extreme right (positional)
Pronunciation: / fɑr ˈraɪt / (AmE), / fɑː ˈraɪt / (BrE)
Situated at or toward the extreme right side of something (position rather than political orientation).

Examples

far-right corner
Move the label to the far-right corner so it doesn't cover the main text.
Place the navigation menu on the far-right side of the header for better balance.
The teacher pointed to the far-right column where students should input their answers.
