Malcolmia maritima

noun
a coastal flowering herb; Virginia stock
Pronunciation: / mælˈkoʊlmiə məˈrɪtɪmə / (AmE), / mælˈkəʊlmɪə məˈrɪtɪmə / (BrE)
an erect branching herb native to the coasts of southwestern Greece and southern Albania, cultivated for its fragrant clusters of white, pink, red, or lilac flowers; also known as Virginia stock.
