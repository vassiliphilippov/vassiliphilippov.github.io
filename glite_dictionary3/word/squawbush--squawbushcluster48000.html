squawbush

noun
deciduous shrub native to California
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɑːˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈskwɔːˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub native to California, known for its unpleasantly scented, usually trifoliate leaves, and edible fruit.
