mallet

noun
long-handled sports implement for hitting a ball
Pronunciation: / ˈmælɪt / (AmE), / ˈmalɪt / (BrE)
a long-handled sports implement with a heavy head, used to hit a ball in games such as polo or croquet

Examples

mallet for croquet
In croquet, each player uses a mallet to strike the ball through the hoops.
She bought a new mallet before joining the club's weekend match.
The polo player's mallet swung gracefully as he aimed at the ball.
