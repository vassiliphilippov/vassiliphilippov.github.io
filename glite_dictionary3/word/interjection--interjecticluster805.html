interjection

noun
abrupt exclamatory word or phrase
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈdʒɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdʒɛkʃən / (BrE)
a word or phrase used as an abrupt exclamation expressing emotion, such as surprise, pain, or joy
