exoticness

noun
quality of being exotic
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɑːtɪknəs / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪknɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being exotic; unusual or strikingly different in appearance, character, or effect.

Examples

the exoticness of flowers
She admired the exoticness of the rare flowers in the botanical garden.
The exoticness of the remote island attracted adventurous travelers from around the world.
The chef's dishes had a certain exoticness that set them apart from traditional cuisine.
