dwojniak

noun
Polish mead made with equal honey and water
Pronunciation: / dvɔˈɲjak / (AmE), / dvɔˈɲjak / (BrE)
A traditional Polish mead made using equal parts of honey and water, resulting in a sweet, strong beverage.
