Santa Maria del Tule

proper noun
town in Mexico, famous for giant Montezuma cypress
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːntə məˈriːə dɛl ˈtuːleɪ / (AmE), / ˌsæntə məˈriːə dɛl ˈtuːleɪ / (BrE)
A town in southeastern Mexico near Oaxaca, known for the Ahuehuete, a giant Montezuma cypress tree, considered one of the largest and oldest trees in the world.
