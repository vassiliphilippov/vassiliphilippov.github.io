shinbone

noun
the larger of the two lower leg bones
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪnˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃɪnbəʊn / (BrE)
the inner and thicker of the two bones between the knee and ankle in the human leg; the tibia

Examples

break a shinbone
A strong kick to the shinbone can be very painful.
She bruised her shinbone when she bumped into the coffee table.
The X-ray showed a fracture in his shinbone.
