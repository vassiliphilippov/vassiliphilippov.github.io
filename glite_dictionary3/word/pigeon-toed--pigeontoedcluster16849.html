pigeon-toed

adjective
having feet that turn inward
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒ.ən toʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒ.ɪn təʊd / (BrE)
(of a person) having feet that turn inward while walking or standing.

Examples

pigeon-toed child walks
As a child, she was slightly pigeon-toed and often tripped over her own feet.
His pigeon-toed walk was noticeable on the soccer field.
Many children who are pigeon-toed outgrow the condition as they develop.
