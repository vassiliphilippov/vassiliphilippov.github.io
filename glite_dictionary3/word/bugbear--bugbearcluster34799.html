bugbear

noun
imaginary monster used to scare children
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡˌbɛr / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡˌbɛə / (BrE)
An imaginary creature or monster, especially one used to frighten children.

Examples

childhood bugbear
Stories of the bugbear have been told for centuries to frighten misbehaving children.
The old folktale described a bugbear lurking in the dark woods outside the village.
When she refused to eat her vegetables, her nanny warned that the bugbear would come for her at night.
