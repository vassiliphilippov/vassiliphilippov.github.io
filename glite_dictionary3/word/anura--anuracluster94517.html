Anura

noun
order of amphibians: frogs and toads
Pronunciation: / əˈnʊrə / (AmE), / əˈnjʊərə / (BrE)
A taxonomic order of amphibians that comprises frogs, toads, and related species, typically characterized by the absence of a tail in adults.
