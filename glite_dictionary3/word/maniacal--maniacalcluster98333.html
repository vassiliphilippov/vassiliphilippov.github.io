maniacal

adjective
wildly frenzied or uncontrolled
Pronunciation: / məˈnaɪ.ək.əl / (AmE), / məˈnaɪ.ək.əl / (BrE)
behaving in an extremely wild, uncontrolled, or frenzied way, resembling that of a maniac

Examples

maniacal laughter
He laughed with a maniacal gleam in his eyes, startling everyone in the room.
Her maniacal energy kept the team going long after midnight.
The villain's maniacal plan nearly destroyed the city.
