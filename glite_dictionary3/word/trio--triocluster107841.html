trio

noun
a set of three people or things
Pronunciation: / ˈtriː.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈtriː.əʊ / (BrE)
A set or group of three people or things considered as a unit.

Examples

trio of friends
A trio of judges will decide the winner of the competition.
She arranged the flowers in a trio of small vases on the table.
The siblings bought a trio of matching bracelets during their holiday.
