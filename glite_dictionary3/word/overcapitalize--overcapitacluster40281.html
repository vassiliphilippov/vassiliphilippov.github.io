to overcapitalize

verb
to provide or record excessive capital in a business
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪz /; / ˌoʊvɚˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkæpɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to provide, record, or estimate more capital or value for a business or property than it is really worth, often leading to financial problems
