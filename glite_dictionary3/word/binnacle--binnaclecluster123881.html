binnacle

noun
housing for a ship's compass
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪn.ə.kəl / (AmE), / ˈbɪn.ə.kəl / (BrE)
a built, nonmagnetic housing for a ship's compass, typically located in front of the helm

Examples

binnacle compass housing
A sudden jolt sent the charts tumbling near the binnacle at the helm.
Before setting sail, the captain always checked the binnacle for accuracy.
The ship's binnacle was polished brass and housed the main compass.
