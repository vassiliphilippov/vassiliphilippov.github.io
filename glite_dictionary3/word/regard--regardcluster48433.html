regard

noun
a particular aspect or detail
Pronunciation: / rəˈɡɑrd / (AmE), / rɪˈɡɑːd / (BrE)
a particular aspect or detail; a point, especially used in the phrase 'in this/that regard' to mean 'in this/that respect'

Examples

in this regard
In this regard, the study provides new insights into the problem.
There is little difference between the two companies in that regard.
With regard to safety, the airline has an excellent record.
