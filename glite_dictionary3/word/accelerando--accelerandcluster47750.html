accelerando

adjective, adverb
music: gradually increasing in tempo
Pronunciation: / əkˌsɛləˈrɑndoʊ /; / əˌtʃɛləˈrɑndoʊ / (AmE), / akˌsɛləˈrandəʊ /; / aksɛlɛˈrandəʊ / (BrE)
(in music) with a gradual increase in speed or tempo; to be played faster and faster
