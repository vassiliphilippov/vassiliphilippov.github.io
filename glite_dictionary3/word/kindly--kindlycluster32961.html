kindly

adjective
pleasant or agreeable (of weather or things)
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪndli / (AmE), / ˈkaɪndli / (BrE)
(of weather or environments) pleasant and agreeable; producing positive or comfortable feelings.

Examples

The island's kindly climate attracted tourists all year round.
We enjoyed the kindly sunshine during our afternoon walk.
