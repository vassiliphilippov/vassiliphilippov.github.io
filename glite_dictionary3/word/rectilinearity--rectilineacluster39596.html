rectilinearity

noun
the state of being straight or rectilinear
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛktəˌlɪniˈærəti / (AmE), / ˌrɛktɪˌlɪniˈærɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being rectilinear; straightness, especially in reference to lines or surfaces.

Examples

rectilinearity of lines
Physics experiments often assume the rectilinearity of light rays for simplicity.
The rectilinearity of the building's design gave it a modern, minimalist appearance.
The artist broke the rectilinearity of the grid pattern with bold, diagonal lines.
