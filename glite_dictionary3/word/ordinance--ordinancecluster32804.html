ordinance

noun
city or local government law
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrdɪnəns /; / ˈɔrdɪnəns / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪnəns / (BrE)
an official rule, law, or order made by a government or authority, especially a local government

Examples

It is illegal to smoke in public parks under local ordinances.
