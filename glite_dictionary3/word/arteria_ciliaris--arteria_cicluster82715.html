arteria ciliaris

noun
artery of the eye's choroid
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə sɪˈlaɪərɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə sɪlɪˈeərɪs / (BrE)
any of several arteries that supply blood to the choroid coat of the eye.

Examples

arteria ciliaris supplies eye
Damage to the arteria ciliaris can lead to vision problems due to disrupted blood supply.
Ophthalmologists carefully examine the arteria ciliaris during certain eye surgeries.
The arteria ciliaris plays an essential role in nourishing the choroid of the eye.
