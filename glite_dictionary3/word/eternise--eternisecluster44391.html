to eternise

verb
make eternal or famous forever
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtɜrnaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈtɜːnaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something last forever in memory, fame, or existence

Examples

to eternise a memory
She attempted to eternise her memories in the pages of her diary.
The grand monument was built to eternise the founder's name.
The poet hoped his verses would eternise the hero's deeds.
