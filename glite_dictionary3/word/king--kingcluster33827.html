king

noun
a promoted piece in checkers
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ / (AmE), / kɪŋ / (BrE)
In checkers, a piece that reaches the opponent's first row and is promoted, allowing it to move both forward and backward.

Examples

checkers king promoted
After reaching the last row, her checker became a king and gained new abilities.
Once a king is made, it can move both forwards and backwards across the board.
You must crown your king by placing another piece on top of it.
