short-fetched

adjective
improbable, implausible
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrt ˌfɛtʃt / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːt ˌfɛtʃt / (BrE)
(of an idea or explanation) improbable and unlikely to be true; implausible.

Examples
