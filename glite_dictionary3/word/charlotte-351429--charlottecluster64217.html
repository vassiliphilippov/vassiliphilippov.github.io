charlotte

noun
dessert with cake lining and fruit or cream filling
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːrˈlɑːt / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈlɒt / (BrE)
A dessert made by lining a mold with cake, bread, or biscuits and filling it with fruit, custard, or whipped cream.

Examples

strawberry charlotte dessert
For dessert, she served a delicious charlotte filled with fresh strawberries and cream.
He was impressed by how elegantly the charlotte held its shape after being unmolded.
The restaurant's signature dish is a warm apple charlotte topped with vanilla sauce.
