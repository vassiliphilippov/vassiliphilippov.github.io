counterrevolution

noun
a movement to reverse a prior revolution
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərˌrɛvəˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˌrɛvəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
A political movement or revolution aimed at reversing the changes brought about by a previous revolution.
