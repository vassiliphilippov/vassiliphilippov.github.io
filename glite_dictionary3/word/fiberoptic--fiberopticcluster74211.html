fiberoptic

adjective
relating to fiber optics technology
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbɚˈɑptɪk / (AmE), / ˌfaɪbərˈɒptɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or using thin flexible fibers of glass or other transparent solids to transmit light signals, usually for telecommunications or medical instruments.

Examples

fiberoptic cable system
A fiberoptic camera was inserted to inspect the interior of the pipeline.
Surgeons often use fiberoptic cables in endoscopic procedures to improve visibility inside the body.
The hospital upgraded its internet connection to a fiberoptic network for faster data transfer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – optical fiber: a thread-like fiber capable of transmitting light, used in fiber optics
