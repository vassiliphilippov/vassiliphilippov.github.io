equilateral

adjective
having all sides of equal length
Pronunciation: / ˌikwəˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl / (BrE)
(of a shape or figure) having all sides of equal length.

Examples

equilateral triangle
An equilateral triangle has three equal sides and three equal angles.
Each face of a regular tetrahedron is an equilateral triangle.
The architect designed an equilateral courtyard for symmetry and balance.

Related Terms

  • antonym – isosceles: having two sides of equal length (of a triangle)
  • antonym – scalene: having all sides of different lengths (of a triangle)
