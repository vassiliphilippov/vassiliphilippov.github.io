mnemonic

noun
cryptocurrency seed phrase
Pronunciation: / nɪˈmɑːnɪk / (AmE), / nɪˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)
(in computing, especially cryptocurrency) a sequence of words used as a seed phrase to back up and recover access to a digital wallet.

Examples

mnemonic phrase wallet
Be sure to write down your twelve-word mnemonic and store it in a safe place.
The wallet app will display a new mnemonic when you create a backup for your account.
If you lose your mnemonic, you may permanently lose access to the funds in the wallet.

Related Terms

  • synonym – recovery phrase: an alternate name for the ordered words used to recover wallet access
  • synonym – seed phrase: a sequence of words used to back up and restore a cryptocurrency wallet
