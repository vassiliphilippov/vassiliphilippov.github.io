mate

noun
officer below captain on a ship
Pronunciation: / meɪt / (AmE), / meɪt / (BrE)
an officer on a commercial ship who is ranked below the master and has responsibilities for navigation and the crew

Examples

chief mate
After several years as third mate, she was promoted to chief mate.
If the master is away, the mate assumes command of the vessel.
The mate supervised the deck crew during the cargo loading.
