tour

noun
journey visiting several places
Pronunciation: / tʊr / (AmE), / tʊə / (BrE)
a journey or trip in which one visits several places, often returning to the starting point, especially for pleasure, study, or business

Examples

tour of Europe
Her European tour lasted for three weeks and included six countries.
The city offers a bike tour that covers all the major attractions.
We went on a guided tour of the ancient castle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – circuit: a journey or route all the way around a particular place or area
  • synonym – excursion: a short journey or trip made for pleasure
