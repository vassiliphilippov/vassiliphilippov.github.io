behavioristic

adjective
relating to behaviorism
Pronunciation: / bəˌheɪvjəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / bɪˌheɪvjəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of behaviorism, a theory of learning that emphasizes observable behaviors over internal mental processes.

Examples

behavioristic approach to learning
His teaching style is distinctly behavioristic, emphasizing reinforcement and punishment.
Many early psychologists favored behavioristic methods over introspective ones.
The study was conducted using a behavioristic approach, focusing solely on observable actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – behavioral: relating to behavior, especially in psychology
  • antonym – cognitive: relating to mental processes such as thinking and memory
