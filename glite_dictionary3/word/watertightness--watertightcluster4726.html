watertightness

noun
condition of being impermeable to water
Pronunciation: / ˌwɔːtərˈtaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˌwɔːtəˈtaɪtnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being made so that water cannot enter or escape.

Examples

watertightness of a container
Engineers tested the watertightness of the new roof after heavy rainfall.
Proper sealing will improve the watertightness of your outdoor storage container.
The watertightness of the hull is crucial for the safety of the submarine.
