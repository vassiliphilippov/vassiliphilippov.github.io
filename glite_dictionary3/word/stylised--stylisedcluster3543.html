stylised

adjective
artificially styled, not natural
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪləˌzd / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlʌɪzd / (BrE)
presented or created according to a particular style rather than expressing things as they really are; not natural or realistic.

Examples

stylised art
She prefers stylised illustrations to realistic drawings in children's books.
The film's stylised action scenes gave it a unique visual identity.
The play was performed in a highly stylised manner, emphasizing gesture over realism.

Related Terms

  • antonym – naturalistic: depicting things in a natural or realistic way
  • synonym – stylized: presented according to a particular style; not natural (US spelling)
