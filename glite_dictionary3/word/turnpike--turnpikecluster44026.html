turnpike

noun
toll expressway
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnˌpaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnpaɪk / (BrE)
a main road, especially an expressway, on which a toll is collected for use

Examples

the turnpike toll road
The new turnpike has reduced travel time between the two cities considerably.
Traffic on the turnpike was heavy during the holiday weekend.
We paid five dollars to use the turnpike on our way to Boston.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expressway: a major highway designed for fast traffic
  • antonym – freeway: a highway without tolls
