angelfish

noun
a type of flat-bodied shark
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəlˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəlˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A type of shark with a broad, flat body and winglike pectoral fins, belonging to the family Squatinidae, also known as angel sharks.

Examples

large angelfish shark
Divers spotted a rare angelfish near the edge of the reef yesterday.
The angelfish glided over the sandy ocean floor, almost hidden by its flat body.
Unlike most sharks, an angelfish lies flat on the seabed, waiting to ambush its prey.
