to ebonise

verb
make wood look like ebony
Pronunciation: / ˈɛbəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛbənʌɪz / (BrE)
to stain wood or another material black so that it resembles ebony

Examples

to ebonise wood
He is carefully ebonising the picture frame to create a bold contrast with the artwork.
Many 19th-century cabinets were ebonised to mimic the luxurious look of real ebony.
The craftsman decided to ebonise the chair legs to match the rest of the furniture.
