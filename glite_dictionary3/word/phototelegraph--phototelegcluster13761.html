phototelegraph

noun
device for transmitting images by telegraph
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈtɛləˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈtɛlɪɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a device used for transmitting photographs or images over a distance by telegraphy.
