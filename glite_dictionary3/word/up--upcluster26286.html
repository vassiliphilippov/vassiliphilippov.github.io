up

adjective
functioning (of a computer or system)
Pronunciation: / ʌp / (AmE), / ʌp / (BrE)
(of computers or systems) functioning or operating normally; not down or out of service.

Examples

up and running
If the network isn't up, you won't be able to access your email.
