Mahagauri

proper noun
Hindu goddess of purity; form of Devi
Pronunciation: / mɑːhəˈɡɔːri / (AmE), / mɑːhəˈɡɔːri / (BrE)
In Hinduism, a goddess representing purity and auspiciousness, known as a benevolent form of Devi and revered for her shining beauty.
