paraphrasis

noun
rewording for clarification
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈfreɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈfreɪsɪs / (BrE)
The act of rewording or restating something, usually for the purpose of clarification.

Examples

A careful paraphrasis can reveal underlying assumptions in the original statement.
