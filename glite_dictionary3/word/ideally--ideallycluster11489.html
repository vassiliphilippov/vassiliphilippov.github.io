ideally

adverb
in the best possible way; as desired
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiəli / (AmE), / aɪˈdɪəli / (BrE)
In the best possible way; as one would wish; under perfect circumstances.

Examples

ideally in every case
Ideally, every child should have access to quality education.
Ideally, the process will be completed by the end of the week.
We should meet at noon, but ideally a bit earlier if possible.

  • antonym – practically: in a practical manner; realistically
