flush

noun
reddening of the face from emotion
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ / (AmE), / flʌʃ / (BrE)
a red or rosy color on the face or skin, or a sudden feeling of heat or emotion that causes this

Examples

a flush of anger

He tried to hide the flush that revealed his embarrassment.

She noticed the warm flush on her face as she stepped in from the cold.
