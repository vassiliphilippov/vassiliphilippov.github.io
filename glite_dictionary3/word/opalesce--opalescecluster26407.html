to opalesce

verb
show colors like an opal
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.pəˈlɛs / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.pəˈlɛs / (BrE)
to display or reflect a play of colors like an opal; to show an iridescent or shimmering appearance
