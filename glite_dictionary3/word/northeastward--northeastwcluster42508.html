northeastward

noun
compass point between north and east
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˈistwərd / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːstwəd / (BrE)
The direction or point on the compass that is midway between north and east, at an angle of 45 degrees.

Examples

northeastward direction
Birds migrate along a northeastward route in the spring.
The ship anchored at a point due northeastward of the lighthouse.
They traveled in a northeastward direction to reach the coast.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – east-northeast: the compass point between north and east (ENE, 67.5°)
  • degree lower – north-northeast: the compass point between north and northeast (NNE, 22.5°)
  • synonym – northeast: the compass point between north and east (NE, 45°)
