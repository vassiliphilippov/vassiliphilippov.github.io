rocket

noun
firework sent high into the air
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
a firework or device that is propelled high into the air by burning fuel, often producing a visual or audible display, or used for rescue operations
