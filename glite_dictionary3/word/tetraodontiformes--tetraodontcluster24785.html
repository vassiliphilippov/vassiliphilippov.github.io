Tetraodontiformes

noun
an order of fishes including puffers and boxfishes
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛt̬.ɹi.oʊˌdɑn.tɪˈfɔːr.miːz / (AmE), / ˌtɛt.ɹi.əʊˌdɒn.tɪˈfɔː.miːz / (BrE)
an order of mainly marine fishes including puffers, triggerfishes, boxfishes, and ocean sunfishes, characterized by their unique body shapes and spiny skins
