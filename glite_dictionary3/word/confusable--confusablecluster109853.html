confusable

adjective
easily mistaken for something else
Pronunciation: / kənˈfjuːzəbl̩ / (AmE), / kənˈfjuːzəbl̩ / (BrE)
so similar to something else that it is easily mistaken for it or confused with it

Examples

confusable medication bottles
Several of the medication bottles look confusable, so you should always double-check the label.
Some commonly confusable pairs of words are 'affect' and 'effect'.
The two species are so confusable that even experienced birdwatchers sometimes mix them up.

Related Terms

  • antonym – distinct: clearly different or easy to distinguish
  • synonym – mistakable: able to be mistaken for something else
