piperazine

noun
medication for treating certain worm infestations
Pronunciation: / paɪˈpɛrəˌzin / (AmE), / paɪˈpɛrəziːn / (BrE)
a synthetic compound used as a medicine to treat infestations by roundworms or pinworms

Examples

piperazine deworming medicine
Some over-the-counter deworming medications contain piperazine as the main active ingredient.
The doctor prescribed piperazine to eliminate the pinworms in the child's intestines.
Veterinarians often administer piperazine to pets for the treatment of roundworm infections.
