pantry

noun
small room for storing food
Pronunciation: / ˈpæntri / (AmE), / ˈpæntri / (BrE)
a small room or cupboard in a house where food, dishes, and other provisions are stored

Examples

well-stocked pantry
Every Sunday, he restocks the pantry with fresh groceries.
She keeps all the canned goods neatly organized in the pantry.
The pantry was empty except for some pasta and rice.
