smoothness

noun
quality of being free from errors or interruptions
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuð.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsmuːð.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being free from errors, interruptions, or obstacles; seamlessness in operation or appearance.

Examples

smoothness of operation
She was impressed by the smoothness of his speech, without a single pause or mistake.
The smoothness of the new software update made using the app a pleasant experience.
The train's smoothness over the tracks was remarkable compared to the old models.

Related Terms

  • synonym – regularity: the quality of being even or uniform; regular occurrence
  • antonym – roughness: the quality of being rough, not smooth
