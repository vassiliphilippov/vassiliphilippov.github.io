Phylloporus boletinoides

noun
a species of brown-capped fungus
Pronunciation: / fɪˌloʊpərəs boʊˌlɛtəˈnɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / fɪˌlɒpərəs bəʊˌlɛtəˈnɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
