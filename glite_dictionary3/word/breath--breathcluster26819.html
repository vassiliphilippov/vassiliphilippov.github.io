breath

noun
slight movement of air
Pronunciation: / brɛθ / (AmE), / brɛθ / (BrE)
a slight movement of air; a very small amount or trace of wind

Examples

breath of wind
Early in the morning, a faint breath of wind rippled across the lake.
Not a breath stirred among the trees that night.
The room was so stuffy, there wasn't a breath of air coming through the window.
