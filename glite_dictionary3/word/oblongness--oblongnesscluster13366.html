oblongness

noun
the state of being oblong in shape
Pronunciation: / əˈblɔŋ.nəs / (AmE), / əˈblɒŋ.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being oblong in shape, especially resembling a rectangle with unequal adjacent sides
