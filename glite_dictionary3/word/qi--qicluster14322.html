qi

noun
vital energy in Chinese philosophy and medicine
Pronunciation: / tʃi / (AmE), / tʃi / (BrE)
In Chinese philosophy and traditional medicine, the circulating vital energy believed to be present in all living things, whose balance is considered essential for health and well-being.

Examples

balance qi energy
Many martial arts disciplines emphasize the importance of controlling one's qi.
She practiced tai chi to strengthen her qi and improve her well-being.
Traditional Chinese medicine teaches that qi must flow freely for good health.
