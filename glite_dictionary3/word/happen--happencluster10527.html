to happen

verb
do or be by chance
Pronunciation: / ˈhæpən / (AmE), / ˈhæpən / (BrE)
to do or be something by chance, without planning or intention

Examples

happen to know
Do you happen to have a pen I can borrow?
I happen to know the answer, if you'd like some help.
She happened to meet an old friend at the supermarket.

Related Terms

  • synonym – occur: to happen or take place, especially unexpectedly
