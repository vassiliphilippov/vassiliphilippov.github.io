takoyaki

noun
Japanese octopus snack ball
Pronunciation: / tɑːkoʊˈjɑːki / (AmE), / takəʊˈjaki / (BrE)
A Japanese snack consisting of small ball-shaped pieces of batter filled with diced octopus and other ingredients, cooked in a special pan.

Examples

hot takoyaki balls
During the festival, we enjoyed several plates of takoyaki from the street stalls.
Many people top their takoyaki with bonito flakes and a sweet, savory sauce.
She learned how to make takoyaki using the traditional pan and fresh ingredients.
