rhinion

noun
tip at end of nasal bone suture
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪniən / (AmE), / ˈraɪnɪən / (BrE)
the anterior tip at the end of the suture of the nasal bones, located at the lower end of the nasal bridge

Examples

broken rhinion
A small fracture was detected near the patient's rhinion after the accident.
Anatomists use the rhinion as a reference point in nasal measurements.
The surgeon marked the rhinion to guide the nasal reconstruction procedure.
