Babel

proper noun
biblical tower, site of language confusion
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbl / (BrE)
the tower mentioned in the Book of Genesis which Noah's descendants attempted to build to reach heaven, but whose construction was halted when God confused their language
