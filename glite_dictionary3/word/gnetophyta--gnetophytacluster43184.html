Gnetophyta

noun
a division of gymnosperm plants
Pronunciation: / nɛˈtɑfə.tə / (AmE), / ˌgniːtəˈfaɪtə / (BrE)
A division of gymnospermous flowering plants, considered an evolutionary link between conifers and angiosperms; classified variously as a class (Gnetopsida) or subdivision (Gnetophyta or Gnetophytina).
