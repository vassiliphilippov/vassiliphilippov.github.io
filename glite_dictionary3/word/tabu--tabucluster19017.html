tabu

adjective
forbidden by custom or religion
Pronunciation: / təˈbu / (AmE), / təˈbuː / (BrE)
forbidden to be used or touched, especially due to social or religious custom, particularly in South Pacific cultures

Examples

tabu foods
In traditional ceremonies, certain foods are tabu for women to eat.
It was considered tabu for outsiders to enter the sacred grove.
The islanders explained that touching the artifact was strictly tabu.

Related Terms

  • synonym – taboo: forbidden by social custom or religious practice
