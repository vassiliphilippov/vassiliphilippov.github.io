trollop

noun
disparaging term for an untidy or promiscuous woman
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːləp /; / ˈtrɑː.ləp / (AmE), / ˈtrɒləp /; / ˈtrɒl.əp / (BrE)
a woman who is considered dirty, untidy, or sexually promiscuous (used as an insult)
