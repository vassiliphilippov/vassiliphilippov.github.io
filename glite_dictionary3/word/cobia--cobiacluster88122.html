cobia

noun
large tropical marine food and game fish
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.bi.ə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.bi.ə / (BrE)
a large tropical marine fish (Rachycentron canadum) with a long dark stripe on each side, valued as a food and game fish and found in coastal and open waters worldwide
