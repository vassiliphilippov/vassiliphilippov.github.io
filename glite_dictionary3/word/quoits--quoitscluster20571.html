quoits

noun
a game of throwing rings at a stake
Pronunciation: / kwɔɪts / (AmE), / kwɔɪts / (BrE)
a traditional game in which players throw rings, typically made of iron or rope, at a stake in the ground, aiming to encircle it.

Examples

play quoits outdoors
Every summer, the village holds a quoits tournament on the green.
She learned to play quoits from her grandfather, who was a local champion.
The rules of quoits are simple, but mastering the game requires skill and precision.
