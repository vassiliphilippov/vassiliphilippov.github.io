to smart

verb
feel or cause sharp pain
Pronunciation: / smɑrt / (AmE), / smɑːt / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) to feel or cause a sharp, stinging pain.

Examples

to smart from pain
Her knee still smarts where she bumped it against the table.
My eyes smarted from the smoke in the room.
The salt water made the small cut on his hand smart even more.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sting: to cause a sharp or burning pain
