simnel

noun
fruitcake with almond paste for Easter or Lent
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmnəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪmnəl / (BrE)
a light fruitcake, often covered with marzipan, traditionally eaten at Lent or Easter in Britain
