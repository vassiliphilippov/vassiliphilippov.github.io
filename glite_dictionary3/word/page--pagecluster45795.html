to page

verb
contact by pager or announcement
Pronunciation: / peɪdʒ / (AmE), / peɪdʒ / (BrE)
to contact someone, especially by using a pager or by announcing their name over a public address system

Examples

to page a doctor
Please page me as soon as the package arrives at reception.
Security was paging a lost child over the loudspeaker.
The hospital staff will page Dr. Smith if there's an emergency.

Related Terms

  • synonym – summon: to call someone to appear or be present
