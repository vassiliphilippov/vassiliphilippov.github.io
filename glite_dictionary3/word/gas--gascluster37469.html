to gas

verb
attack using poisonous gas
Pronunciation: / ɡæs / (AmE), / ɡas / (BrE)
to attack or kill with poisonous gas; to subject (people or animals) to gas fumes, especially as a weapon or method of execution

Examples

to gas a room
After accidentally mixing chemicals, the workers feared that they might gas themselves if they weren't careful.
Authorities warned that the criminals intended to gas the building as part of their escape plan.
During the war, soldiers were terrified that enemy forces would gas the trenches overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – asphyxiate: to kill by depriving of oxygen
  • synonym – poison: to administer a substance that causes illness or death
