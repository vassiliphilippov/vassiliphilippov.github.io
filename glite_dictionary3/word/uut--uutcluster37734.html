Uut

noun
former symbol for element 113 (nihonium)
Pronunciation: / juː ˈjuː tiː / (AmE), / juː ˈjuː tiː / (BrE)
The former chemical symbol for ununtrium, a radioactive transuranic element with atomic number 113. Now known as nihonium (Nh).
