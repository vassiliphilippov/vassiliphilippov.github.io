dacryocystitis

noun
inflammation of the lacrimal sac
Pronunciation: / ˌdækri.oʊˌsɪsˈtaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌdakrɪəʊsɪsˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the lacrimal sac, often causing pain, swelling, and obstruction of the tear drainage into the nose
