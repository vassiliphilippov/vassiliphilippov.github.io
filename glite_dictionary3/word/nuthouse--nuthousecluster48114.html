nuthouse

noun
slang term for mental hospital
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌt.haʊs / (AmE), / ˈnʌt.haʊs / (BrE)
(slang, often offensive) A mental hospital; an institution for people with mental illnesses, especially used derogatorily.

Examples

sent to the nuthouse
After his erratic behavior, his family threatened to send him to the nuthouse.
She joked that her job was so stressful, she might end up in a nuthouse someday.
Some people still use the outdated term nuthouse to refer to mental health facilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insane asylum: a hospital for people with serious mental disorders
  • synonym – psychiatric hospital: medical hospital specializing in mental illness
