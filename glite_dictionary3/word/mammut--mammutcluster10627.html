Mammut

noun
genus of extinct mastodons
Pronunciation: / ˈmæm.uːt / (AmE), / ˈmæm.uːt / (BrE)
a genus of extinct large mammals, commonly known as mastodons, that lived from the late Miocene to the late Pleistocene
