athlete

noun
person trained to compete in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈæθˌlit / (AmE), / ˈæθliːt / (BrE)
a person who is trained or skilled in sports, especially one who competes in organized events

Examples

professional athlete
He was honored as the most outstanding athlete of the year at the awards ceremony.
Many professional athletes train daily to improve their performance.
She has been an athlete since her teens, excelling in track and field.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nonathlete: person who does not train or compete in sports
  • synonym – sportsman: person who plays sport, typically male
