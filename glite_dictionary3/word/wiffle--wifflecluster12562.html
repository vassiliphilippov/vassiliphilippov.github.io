Wiffle

noun
light plastic ball with cutouts (trademark)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪfəl / (BrE)
A trademarked name for a lightweight, hollow plastic ball with cutouts, commonly used for play and in informal versions of baseball.

Examples

Wiffle ball game
His favorite childhood toy was a Wiffle because it was safe to use indoors.
She brought out a new Wiffle for the kids to play with at the picnic.
We played a game of backyard baseball using a Wiffle instead of a real ball.
