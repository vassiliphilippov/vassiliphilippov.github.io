crystallization

noun
process of forming crystals
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪstəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

crystallization of salt

The crystallization of salt from seawater is a common example of this process.
