wig

noun
artificial covering of hair for the head
Pronunciation: / wɪɡ / (AmE), / wɪɡ / (BrE)
An artificial covering of hair for all or most of the head, typically made from real or synthetic hair and worn to conceal baldness or for theatrical or fashion purposes.

Examples

wear a wig
In the 18th century, white powdered wigs were a symbol of status.
She wore a wig to cover her thinning hair after chemotherapy.
The actor put on a curly wig for his role in the play.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hairpiece: a small or partial wig used to cover a section of the head
