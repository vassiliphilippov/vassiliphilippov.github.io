metastasis

noun
spread of disease, especially cancer, within the body
Pronunciation: / məˈtæstəsɪs / (AmE), / məˈtastəsɪs / (BrE)
the spread of a disease, especially cancer, from one part of the body to another

Examples

metastasis of cancer
After several years, the cancer showed signs of metastasis to the lungs.
Doctors monitored her closely for possible metastasis beyond the original tumor site.
The prognosis worsened once metastasis was detected in the lymph nodes.

  • synonym – metastatic: pertaining to or characteristic of metastasis
