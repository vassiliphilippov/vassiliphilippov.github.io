antacid

noun
substance to reduce stomach acidity
Pronunciation: / ænˈtæsɪd / (AmE), / antˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a substance that neutralizes stomach acid, used to relieve indigestion and heartburn

Examples

take an antacid tablet
After eating a spicy meal, he took an antacid to soothe his upset stomach.
Many people keep antacids at home for occasional heartburn or indigestion.
The doctor recommended an antacid to help manage her symptoms of acid reflux.
