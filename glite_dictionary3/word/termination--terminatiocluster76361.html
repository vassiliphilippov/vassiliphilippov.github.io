termination

noun
the end point of something
Pronunciation: / tɝː.məˈneɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / tɜː.mɪˈneɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the final point or boundary at which something ends; the end or extremity of an object or process

Examples

termination point

Each nerve fiber ends at a synaptic termination in the muscle.
The termination of the railway line is at the city’s western edge.
