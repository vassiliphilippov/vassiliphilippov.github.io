Jim Henson

proper noun
creator of the Muppets, puppeteer
Pronunciation: / dʒɪm ˈhɛnsən / (AmE), / dʒɪm ˈhɛnsən / (BrE)
(1936–1990) An American puppeteer, animator, and filmmaker best known for creating the Muppets, influential in television and film puppetry.

Jim Henson created the Muppets
Jim Henson revolutionized children's television with characters like Kermit the Frog and Miss Piggy.
