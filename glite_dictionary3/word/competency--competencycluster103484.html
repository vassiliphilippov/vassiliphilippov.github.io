competency

noun
the quality of being adequately qualified
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːm.pə.tən.si / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pɪ.tən.si / (BrE)
The ability to do something successfully or efficiently; the quality of being adequately or well qualified physically and intellectually.

Examples

professional competency
His technical competency impressed everyone at the job interview.
Leadership competency is essential for managers in today's organizations.
The course is designed to improve students' competency in written communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym – competence: the ability to do something successfully or efficiently
  • antonym – incompetence: lack of ability or qualification to do something
