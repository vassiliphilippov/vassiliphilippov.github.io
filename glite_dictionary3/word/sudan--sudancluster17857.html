Sudan

proper noun
region of northern Africa south of the Sahara
Pronunciation: / suːˈdæn / (AmE), / suːˈdɑːn / (BrE)
a vast region of northern Africa, south of the Sahara, extending from the Atlantic Ocean to the Red Sea

Examples

the Sudan region
Archaeological research in the Sudan has revealed important insights into early African civilizations.
Rainfall is more abundant in the Sudan than in the arid deserts to its north.
The Sahel forms a transition between the Sahara and the Sudan region.
