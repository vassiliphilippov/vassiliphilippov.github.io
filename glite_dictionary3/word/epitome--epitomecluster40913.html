epitome

noun
brief summary or abstract
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪtəmi / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪtəmi / (BrE)
A brief summary or abstract, especially of a written work.

Examples

epitome of the story

The editor provided an epitome of the lengthy report for quick reference.
This handbook is an epitome of the subject for busy readers.
