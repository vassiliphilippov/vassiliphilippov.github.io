to submit

verb
formally apply for consideration
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɪt / (AmE), / səbˈmɪt / (BrE)
to formally present or hand over something (such as a document, idea, or request) for consideration, judgment, or approval

Examples

to submit an application
All proposals must be submitted electronically to the funding agency.
Each candidate is required to submit a resume and cover letter with their job application.
She decided to submit her application for the scholarship before the deadline.
Counsel will submit a written report to the court.
May I submit that this contract is invalid?
She submitted her recommendations to the committee for review.
Applicants must submit their forms by the end of the month.
Please submit any complaints in writing to the office.
She will submit her resignation to the board tomorrow morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – delegate: to assign responsibility or decision-making to another person
  • synonym – offer: to present something for acceptance or rejection
  • synonym – present: to give something formally to someone in authority
  • synonym – propose: to put forward an idea or suggestion
  • synonym – relegate: to assign to another person, especially a lower position or responsibility
  • synonym – render: to formally deliver or hand over something, often required by law or duty
  • antonym – withhold: to refuse to give something that is due or desired
