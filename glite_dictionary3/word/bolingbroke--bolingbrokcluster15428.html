Bolingbroke

proper noun
Henry IV of England (king 1399–1413)
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlɪŋˌbroʊk / (AmE), / ˈbəʊlɪŋˌbrʊk / (BrE)
the title used for Henry IV, the first Lancastrian king of England (1399–1413), who deposed Richard II and suppressed several rebellions.

Bolingbroke was crowned King Henry IV after seizing the throne from Richard II.
