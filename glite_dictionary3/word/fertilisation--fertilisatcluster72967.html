fertilisation

noun
enriching soil with fertilizer or manure
Pronunciation: / ˌfɝːtəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɜːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making soil or land more fertile, especially by applying fertilizer or manure

Examples
