bottlenose

noun
type of dolphin with rounded forehead
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑt.əlˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.əl.nəʊz / (BrE)
a dolphin with a rounded forehead and a well-developed beak, found mainly in the northern Atlantic and Mediterranean.

Examples

bottlenose dolphin swimming
A bottlenose leaped playfully out of the water near our boat.
Scientists tracked a pod of bottlenose dolphins along the Mediterranean coast.
The bottlenose is easily recognized by its rounded forehead and prominent beak.
