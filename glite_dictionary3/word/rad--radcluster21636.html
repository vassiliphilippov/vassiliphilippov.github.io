rad

noun
unit of absorbed ionizing radiation
Pronunciation: / ræd / (AmE), / ræd / (BrE)
a unit of absorbed ionizing radiation equal to the absorption of 100 ergs per gram of material

Examples

100 rad absorbed
Older research papers often report radiation in rad units rather than grays.
Scientists measured the exposure in rads after the nuclear accident.
The patient received a dose of 50 rads during the treatment.
