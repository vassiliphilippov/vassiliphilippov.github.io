hypertext

noun
electronically linked computer text
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.pərˌtɛkst / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.pəˌtɛkst / (BrE)
Computer text or documents in which sections are linked electronically so that a user can easily move from one part to another or between documents.

Examples

hypertext links
By clicking on highlighted words in the hypertext, you can quickly access related information.
Educational websites often use hypertext to help students navigate between topics seamlessly.
The world wide web is based on the concept of hypertext linking documents together.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hyperlink: a reference from one hypertext document to another or to another part of the same document
