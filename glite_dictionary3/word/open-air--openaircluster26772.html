open-air

adjective
outdoors; not under cover
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊpənˈɛr / (AmE), / ˌəʊpənˈeə / (BrE)
(of an activity or place) taking place or located outdoors, not under cover.

Examples

open-air concert
Many people prefer open-air dining during the warm summer evenings.
The concert was held in an open-air amphitheater surrounded by trees.
We enjoyed an open-air market full of local crafts and produce.
