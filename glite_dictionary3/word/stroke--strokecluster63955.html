to stroke

verb
touch gently and repeatedly
Pronunciation: / stroʊk / (AmE), / strəʊk / (BrE)
to move your hand gently and repeatedly over a surface, especially in a comforting or soothing way

Examples

to stroke a cat
As a child, I often stroked the petals of flowers in my grandmother’s garden.
He stroked her hair to calm her before the test.
She gently stroked the cat’s fur until it purred happily.
