audiovisual

noun
material using sight and sound
Pronunciation: / ˌɔdioʊˈvɪʒuəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːdiəʊˈvɪʒjuəl / (BrE)
a material or device that uses both sight and sound to present information, especially for teaching or communication purposes

Examples

audiovisual display
Modern museums often rely on audiovisual displays to provide interactive experiences for visitors.
The teacher supplemented her lecture with several audiovisual materials to engage the students.
We need to check the audiovisual before the conference begins to ensure everything works.
