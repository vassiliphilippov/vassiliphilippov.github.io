cachectic

adjective
relating to or affected by cachexia
Pronunciation: / kəˈkɛktɪk / (AmE), / kəˈkɛktɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by cachexia, characterized by general physical wasting and malnutrition.

Examples

cachectic patient
A cachectic appearance is a common sign of advanced cancer in many patients.
The patient appeared cachectic, with pronounced muscle wasting and frail limbs.
Years of chronic illness had left him looking severely cachectic and malnourished.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cachexia: general physical wasting and malnutrition, usually as a result of chronic disease
