jowly

adjective
having sagging flesh under chin or jaw
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaʊli / (AmE), / ˈdʒaʊli / (BrE)
(of a person) having sagging or prominent folds of flesh beneath the chin or lower jaw.

Examples

jowly face
His jowly cheeks shook when he laughed heartily.
She noticed her reflection becoming more jowly as she grew older.
The elderly man's jowly face gave him a kindly, paternal appearance.
