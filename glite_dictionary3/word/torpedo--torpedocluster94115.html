to torpedo

verb
attack with torpedoes
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / tɔːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
to attack or hit something with torpedoes, typically in a military context

Examples

to torpedo enemy ship
Several ships were torpedoed off the coast during the conflict.
