to root

verb
dig with the snout (animal, esp. pig)
Pronunciation: / rut / (AmE), / ruːt / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially a pig) to dig or search in the ground with the snout.

Examples

to root in soil
The animal was rooting around the garden, digging up vegetables with its snout.
The pig rooted under the fence in search of food.
Wild boars often root through the soil to find insects and roots.
