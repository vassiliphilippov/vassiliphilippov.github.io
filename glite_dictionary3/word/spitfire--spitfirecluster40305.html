spitfire

noun
quick-tempered, fiery person (often female)
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪtˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɪtˌfaɪə / (BrE)
A person, especially a girl or woman, who is very emotional and quick to become angry.

Examples

a real spitfire woman
Despite her small size, Maria can be quite the spitfire when provoked.
She was known as a spitfire in the office, never afraid to speak her mind.
The main character is a real spitfire, always passionate and quick to defend her friends.
