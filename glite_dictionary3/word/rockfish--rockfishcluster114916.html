rockfish

noun
edible flesh of certain fish caught among rocks
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
the lean, edible flesh of any of various market fish that are caught among rocks, valued for food
