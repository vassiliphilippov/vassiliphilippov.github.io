charge d'affaires

noun
temporary head diplomat at an embassy
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrdʒ dəfɛrz / (AmE), / ʃɑːʒ dəˈfeəz / (BrE)
a diplomat who temporarily acts as head of an embassy in the absence of the ambassador

Examples

charge d'affaires leads embassy
A charge d'affaires attended the official ceremony in the ambassador's place.
After the resignation, the charge d'affaires managed all diplomatic communications with the host country.
The embassy was headed by a charge d'affaires during the ambassador's absence.
