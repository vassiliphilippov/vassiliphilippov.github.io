unvaried

adjective
lacking variety or change
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɛr.iɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈveə.riɪd / (BrE)
Not showing or having any variety; lacking in change or diversity.

Examples

unvaried daily routine
Her daily routine was so unvaried that she began to feel bored and restless.
The landscape remained unvaried throughout the long drive across the plains.
The meal was unvaried, with the same dishes served every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym – monotonous: dull and repetitive, lacking in variety
  • antonym – varied: showing variety; not always the same
