uncongenial

adjective
incapable of being grafted (plants)
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kənˈdʒiː.ni.əl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kənˈdʒiː.ni.əl / (BrE)
(of plant stock or scions) incapable of being successfully grafted together due to incompatibility.

Examples

uncongenial plant stock
Because the rootstock and scion were uncongenial, the tree did not thrive.
The failure of the graft was due to uncongenial stock and scion.
These two varieties are uncongenial and cannot be grafted successfully.
