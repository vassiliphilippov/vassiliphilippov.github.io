to incense

verb
make very angry
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛns / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛns / (BrE)

Examples

to incense a crowd
His arrogant attitude can incense his colleagues at times.
It would incense the public if the truth were revealed.
The unfair accusation seemed to incense her even more.
