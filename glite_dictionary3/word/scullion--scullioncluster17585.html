scullion

noun
low-ranking kitchen servant
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌliən / (AmE), / ˈskʌlɪən / (BrE)
a servant employed to do menial tasks in a kitchen, such as washing dishes and cleaning

Examples

kitchen scullion
He began his career as a humble scullion in the royal kitchens.
The young scullion scrubbed pots and pans long after the others had gone to bed.
Years ago, a scullion would be responsible for all the dirty kitchen work.
