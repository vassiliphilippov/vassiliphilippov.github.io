to return

verb
to yield or produce a profit/result
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
(of an investment, business, or asset) to produce or yield (a profit, result, or amount).

Examples

to return a profit

The investment returned a healthy profit last year.
This savings account returns a modest interest annually.
