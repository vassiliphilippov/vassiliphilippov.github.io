funky

adjective
having an unpleasant odor
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋki / (BrE)
Having an unpleasant or offensive smell.

Examples

funky smell
After days without cleaning, the locker room got a funky smell that was hard to ignore.
Something in the trash is starting to smell really funky—we should take it out.
The old refrigerator had a funky odor that made us cringe every time we opened it.
