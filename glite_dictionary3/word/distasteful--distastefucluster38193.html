distasteful

adjective
unpleasant in taste or smell
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈteɪstfəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈteɪstfəl / (BrE)
having an unpleasant taste or smell; not pleasing to the senses of taste or smell.

Examples

distasteful smell
Her expression showed that she considered the cheese's odor distasteful.
I found the medicine quite distasteful due to its bitter flavor.
The leftovers had become distasteful and had to be thrown away.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unpalatable: not pleasant to taste; unpleasant in flavor
