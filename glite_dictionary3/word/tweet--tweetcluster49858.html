to tweet

verb
make a high-pitched bird sound
Pronunciation: / twiːt / (AmE), / twiːt / (BrE)
(of a small bird) to make a short, high-pitched, chirping sound.

Examples

to tweet in the morning
Every morning, the robins tweet outside my window as the sun rises.
In the quiet forest, a sparrow suddenly tweeted from a nearby branch.
We watched as the canary tweeted cheerfully in its cage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chirp: make a short, sharp sound, especially by a bird or insect
