evangelism

noun
zealous preaching of the Christian gospel
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæn.dʒəˌlɪ.zəm / (AmE), / ɪˈvan.dʒə.lɪ.zəm / (BrE)
Zealous preaching or enthusiastic advocacy of the Christian gospel, especially with the aim of conversion.

Examples

Christian evangelism
His commitment to evangelism led him to travel and preach in many countries.
Many churches hold special events to promote evangelism in their communities.
The rise of television expanded the reach of modern evangelism across the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym – proselytism: the practice of trying to convert people to one's faith or beliefs
