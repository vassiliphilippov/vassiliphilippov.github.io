nolle prosequi

noun
legal notice to drop prosecution
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑli ˈprɑsɪˌkwaɪ /; / ˌnɒli proʊˈsiːkwaɪ / (AmE), / ˌnɒli ˈprɒsɪˌkwaɪ /; / ˌnɒlɛ ˈprɒsɪkwʌɪ / (BrE)
a formal legal notice or order stating that prosecution will not be pursued any further
