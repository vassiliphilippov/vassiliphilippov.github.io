polysyllabic

adjective
having many syllables
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪsɪˈlæbɪk /; / ˌpɑːliˌsɪˈlæbɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪsɪˈlæbɪk / (BrE)
(of a word) having more than one syllable; or (of speech or writing) using long or complex words
