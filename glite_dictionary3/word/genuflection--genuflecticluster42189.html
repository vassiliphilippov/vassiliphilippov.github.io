genuflection

noun
act of bending knees in worship or respect
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛnjəˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛnjʊˈflɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of bending one or both knees as a sign of worship or respect, especially in a religious context

Examples

genuflection before altar
Her respectful genuflection impressed everyone during the ceremony.
In some churches, genuflection is required as an act of reverence upon entering.
The congregation responded with a solemn genuflection before the altar.
