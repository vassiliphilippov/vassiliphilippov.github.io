workshop

noun
short training course for a small group
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːk.ʃɑːp / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk.ʃɒp / (BrE)
a short and intensive educational session or course for a small group, focusing on practical skills or problem solving.

Examples

workshop on writing skills
During the workshop, participants developed solutions to real-world business problems.
She attended a workshop on creative writing last weekend.
The company organized a workshop to improve teamwork among employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym – lecture: an educational talk to an audience, especially as part of a class
  • synonym – seminar: a meeting for discussion or training, often academic
