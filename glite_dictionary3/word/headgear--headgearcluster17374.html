headgear

noun
horse's harness for the head
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈhɛdɡɪə / (BrE)
the parts of a horse's harness or equipment that fit around its head, such as the bridle and halter

Examples

horse headgear
Properly fitted headgear is essential for both the safety and control of the horse.
She replaced the worn leather straps of her horse's headgear with new ones.
The groom carefully checked the headgear before leading the horse out of the stable.
