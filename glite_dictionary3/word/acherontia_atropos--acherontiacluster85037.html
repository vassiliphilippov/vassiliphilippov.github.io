Acherontia atropos

noun
European moth with skull-like markings
Pronunciation: / əˈkɛr.ən.ti.ə ˈæ.trə.pɒs / (AmE), / əˈkɛr.ən.ti.ə ˈæ.trə.pɒs / (BrE)
