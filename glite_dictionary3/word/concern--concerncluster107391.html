to concern

verb
be relevant or related to
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝn / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːn / (BrE)
to be relevant or related to someone or something; to have to do with

Examples

concern the project
My responsibilities mainly concern the financial aspects of the project.
The information in the report concerns several departments within the company.
This issue does not concern you at all, so there's no need to get involved.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irrelevant: not related to the subject being considered
  • synonym – pertain: to relate to; to have to do with
  • synonym – refer: to relate to or be connected with
