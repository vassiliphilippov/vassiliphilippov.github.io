imbibition

noun
the act of absorbing or drinking liquids
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌbɪˈbɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˌbɪˈbɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or act of absorbing or taking in liquid, especially by living tissues or materials; also, the act of drinking

Examples

imbibition of wine
During the tasting, the imbibition of various wines was carefully paced.
Rapid imbibition of fluids can sometimes lead to discomfort or bloating.
The imbibition of water is essential for seed germination in plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym – absorption: the process by which one thing absorbs or is absorbed by another
  • synonym – drinking: the act of consuming liquid, especially alcoholic drink
