ghostliness

noun
quality of being eerie and ghost-like
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊstlinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstlinəs / (BrE)
The quality of being mysterious, eerily strange, or resembling a ghost, often inspiring fear.

Examples

strange ghostliness
A pale mist hung over the graveyard, adding to its ghostliness.
Her voice had a ghostliness that made everyone uneasy.
The ghostliness of the abandoned house sent chills down my spine.
