to renege

verb
fail to keep a promise or commitment
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnɛɡ / (AmE), / rɪˈniːɡ / (BrE)
to go back on a promise, agreement, or commitment; to fail to fulfill an obligation

Examples

to renege on a promise
He decided not to renege on his commitment to support the project.
If you renege on your word, people will be reluctant to trust you again.
The company was criticized for reneging on several of its contractual obligations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – break one's word: to fail to keep a promise
  • antonym – fulfill: to carry out or keep a promise, agreement, or obligation
