gross

adjective
total before deductions
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊs / (AmE), / ɡrəʊs / (BrE)
(of an amount) total, before any deductions such as taxes or expenses have been made.

Examples

gross amount
Always calculate the gross amount before considering deductions.
Her gross salary is higher than her net pay.
The company's gross earnings increased by 10% this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – net: amount after deductions are made
