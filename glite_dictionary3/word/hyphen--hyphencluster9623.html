to hyphen

verb
join with a hyphen
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪfən / (AmE), / ˈhaɪfən / (BrE)
to separate or join words or parts of words with a hyphen.

Examples

to hyphen two words
Editors often hyphen compound adjectives to avoid confusion.
If you hyphen the two names, the meaning becomes clearer.
She was unsure whether to hyphen 'well known' in her essay.
