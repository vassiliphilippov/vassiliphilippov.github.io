nonobservance

noun
failure to follow a law or rule
Pronunciation: / nɑn.əbˈzɝː.vəns / (AmE), / nɒn.əbˈzɜː.vəns / (BrE)
The failure to comply with or adhere to a law, custom, rule, or practice.

Examples

nonobservance of rules
His frequent nonobservance of company policies eventually resulted in disciplinary action.
Religious leaders expressed concern over the nonobservance of traditional customs among the youth.
The nonobservance of safety regulations led to a serious workplace accident.
