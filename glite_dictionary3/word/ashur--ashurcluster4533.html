Ashur

proper noun
ancient Assyrian city on the Tigris in Iraq
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːʃʊr / (AmE), / ˈaʃʊə / (BrE)
an ancient Assyrian city on the Tigris River, which served as the traditional capital of Assyria, located just south of present-day Mosul in Iraq
