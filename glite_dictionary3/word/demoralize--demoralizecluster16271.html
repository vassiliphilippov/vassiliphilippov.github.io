to demoralize

verb
make someone lose confidence or morale
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɔrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒr.ə.laɪz /; / dɪˈmɒrəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone lose confidence, hope, or morale; to discourage or lower someone's spirits

Examples
