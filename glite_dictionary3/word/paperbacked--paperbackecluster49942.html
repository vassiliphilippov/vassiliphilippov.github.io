paperbacked

adjective
having a paper/flexible cover (of books)
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌbækt / (BrE)
(of books) having a flexible binding made of thick paper or cardstock; bound as a paperback.

Examples

paperbacked book edition
In the library, the paperbacked copies are usually found on a separate shelf.
She prefers paperbacked novels because they are lightweight and easy to carry around.
The paperbacked edition is much cheaper than the hardcover version.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hardbacked: (of books) having a stiff, hard cover
  • synonym – paperback: a book with a flexible paper cover
