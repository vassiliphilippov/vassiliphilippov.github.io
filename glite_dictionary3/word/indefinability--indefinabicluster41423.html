indefinability

noun
quality of being impossible to define
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dɪˌfaɪ.nəˈbɪl.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˌfaɪ.nəˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
