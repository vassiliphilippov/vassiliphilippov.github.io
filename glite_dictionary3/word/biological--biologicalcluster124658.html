biological

adjective
genetically related (of parent or child)
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.əˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
related by blood; connected through genetic relationship rather than by adoption or marriage.

Examples

biological parent
Although raised by adoptive parents, he wanted to know his biological roots.
He met his biological mother for the first time as an adult.
The court awarded custody to the biological father.
