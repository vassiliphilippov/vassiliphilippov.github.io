to blink

verb
shine or flash intermittently (light)
Pronunciation: / blɪŋk / (AmE), / blɪŋk / (BrE)
(of a light) to shine with a quick, intermittent gleam; to flash on and off repeatedly.

Examples

to blink lights
Red and blue lights blinked in the darkness as the police car approached.
The distant lighthouse began to blink as night fell.
The warning light will blink rapidly if there is a problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flash: shine with a sudden bright light
  • synonym – flicker: burn or shine with an unsteady or wavering light
