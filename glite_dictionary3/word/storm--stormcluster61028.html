storm

noun
violent weather with strong winds and rain
Pronunciation: / stɔrm / (AmE), / stɔːm / (BrE)
a violent weather condition with strong winds, heavy rain, and often thunder and lightning

Examples

violent storm
After the storm passed, the streets were flooded and several trees had fallen.
The storm knocked out electricity across the entire coastal city.
We watched lightning flash in the distance as the storm rolled closer.
