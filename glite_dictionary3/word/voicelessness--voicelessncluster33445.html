voicelessness

noun
absence of vocal cord vibration in speech sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪslɪsnəs /; / ˈvɔɪsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪslɪsnɪs /; / ˈvɔɪsləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of speech sounds being produced without vibration of the vocal cords (phonetics)
