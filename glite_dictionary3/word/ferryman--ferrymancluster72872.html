ferryman

noun
man who operates a ferry
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfɛrɪmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to operate a ferry, especially one who transports people across a river or other body of water

Examples

The ferryman rowed us safely across the wide river.
