Photostat

noun
duplicating machine for quick photographic copies
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtəˌstæt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊstæt / (BrE)
a type of duplicating machine that produces quick positive or negative photographic copies directly onto specially prepared paper

Examples

Photostat machine copy
Before modern copiers, a Photostat was the standard equipment in many law offices.
The old office still had a working Photostat that employees used for important document copies.
They preserved the contract by making a Photostat of the original document.
