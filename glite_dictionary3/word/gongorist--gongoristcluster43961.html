Gongorist

noun
practitioner of Góngora's ornate style
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊŋɡərɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡɒŋɡərɪst / (BrE)
A writer or poet who adopts the elaborate, ornate style characteristic of the Spanish poet Luis de Góngora.

Examples

famous Gongorist poet
Her early works show the influence of Gongorist aesthetics in both form and language.
Many critics found the Gongorist approach unnecessarily convoluted and obscure.
The poet, once a fierce Gongorist, eventually adopted a simpler and more direct style.
