gopherwood

noun
type of deciduous tree, biblical wood
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊfərˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊfəˌwʊd / (BrE)
a small deciduous tree with round-topped crown, showy white terminal flowers, and heavy hardwood used for yellow dye; also known from biblical references as the wood used to build Noah's Ark
