menopause

noun
end of menstruation in women
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnəˌpɔz / (AmE), / ˈmɛnəˌpɔːz / (BrE)
the period in a woman's life when menstruation permanently stops, typically occurring in middle age

Examples

menopause symptoms
After reaching menopause, she scheduled regular health check-ups to monitor her well-being.
Early menopause can sometimes be triggered by medical treatments or genetic factors.
Many women experience hot flashes and mood changes during menopause.
