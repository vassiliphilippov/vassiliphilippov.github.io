tutorial

adjective
relating to tutors or instruction sessions
Pronunciation: / tuˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / tjuːˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to a tutor, tutoring, or instructional sessions designed to teach a particular subject or skill

Examples

tutorial support
Many universities offer tutorial support for first-year students.
She attended several tutorial classes to prepare for her exams.
The professor held a tutorial session to clarify difficult topics.

  • synonym – instructional: intended to teach or instruct
