unoriented

adjective
lacking direction or clear goal
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɔriˌɛntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɔːrɪəntɪd / (BrE)
not having a definite direction, goal, or sense of orientation; lacking an established position or plan.

Examples

feel unoriented after travel
After getting off the subway, he felt completely unoriented and lost his sense of direction.
The new employee was unoriented during her first week at the large company.
Their research remained largely unoriented until they set a clear objective.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disoriented: confused as to time or place; out of touch
  • antonym – oriented: having a definite position or direction
