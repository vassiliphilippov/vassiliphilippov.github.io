dumbfounding

adjective
astonishing; bewildering
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
Astonishing or bewildering; causing someone to be speechless with surprise or amazement.

Examples

dumbfounding news
Her dumbfounding discovery changed the course of the investigation entirely.
It was a dumbfounding coincidence to meet an old friend in such a remote place.
The magician's final trick was so dumbfounding that the audience sat in stunned silence.
