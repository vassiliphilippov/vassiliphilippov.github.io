pre-orientation

noun
university program for new students before semester
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌɔːriənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌɒriənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
A program of specialized activities organized by a college or university to introduce and acclimate new students before the official start of the academic semester.

Examples

pre-orientation program for students
Many colleges offer pre-orientation programs for students with special interests or needs.
She attended a pre-orientation session to learn more about campus resources.
The university's pre-orientation week helped me make friends before classes started.
