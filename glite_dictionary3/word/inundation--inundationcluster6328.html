inundation

noun
overwhelming quantity or flood
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.ʌnˈdeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ʌnˈdeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
An overwhelming number or amount of things occurring at once.

Examples

inundation of messages
During the sale, there was an inundation of customers at the store.
Social media saw an inundation of comments following the celebrity's announcement.
The company received an inundation of job applications after posting the new position online.
