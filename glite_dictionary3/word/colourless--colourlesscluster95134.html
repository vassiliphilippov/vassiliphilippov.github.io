colourless

adjective
lacking color; dull
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.ə.ləs / (BrE)
lacking color or brightness; dull or weak in color.

Examples

colourless liquid
After hours underwater, the fish appeared almost colourless in the dim light.
She wore a colourless dress that blended in with the grey sky.
The walls in that room were completely colourless, making the space feel dull and empty.

Related Terms

  • antonym – colorful: full of color; bright and varied in color
  • synonym – colorless: lacking color or brightness (American spelling)
