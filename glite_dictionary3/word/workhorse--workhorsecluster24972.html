workhorse

noun
dependable machine under heavy use
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌhɔrs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌhɔːs / (BrE)
A machine or device that operates reliably under heavy or continuous use, often performing the main or most demanding tasks.

Examples

reliable workhorse machine
For decades, the Boeing 747 was the workhorse of international air travel.
The diesel generator has become a workhorse for many remote construction sites.
This server is the workhorse of our computer lab, running day and night without failure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mainstay: a chief support or main element
  • synonym – workhorse: a person who works hard and reliably
