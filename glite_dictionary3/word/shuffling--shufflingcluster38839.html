shuffling

noun
the act of mixing playing cards
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌflɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌflɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of mixing playing cards by moving them about to change their order, especially before dealing

Examples

shuffling the cards
After a quick shuffling of the deck, the dealer distributed the cards to each player.
He made sure the shuffling was thorough to ensure fair play.
The magician's impressive shuffling amazed the audience.
