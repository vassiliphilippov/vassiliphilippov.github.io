praesidium

noun
executive committee in socialist nations
Pronunciation: / preɪˈsɪdiəm / (AmE), / priˈsɪdiəm / (BrE)
In some socialist countries, a permanent executive committee that exercises the powers of a larger legislative body when it is not in session.

Examples

the praesidium meeting
A new chairperson was elected to oversee the praesidium's activities for the coming year.
During parliamentary recess, all major decisions were handled by the praesidium.
The praesidium met late into the night to address the economic crisis.
