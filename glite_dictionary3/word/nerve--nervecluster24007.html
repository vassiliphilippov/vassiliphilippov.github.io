nerve

noun
courage or boldness
Pronunciation: / nɝːv / (AmE), / nɜːv / (BrE)
courage or boldness in difficulty or danger; the ability to continue despite fear or adversity.

Examples

have the nerve to try
I wish I had the nerve to ask him out.
It took a lot of nerve to jump off the highest diving board.
She found the nerve to speak up for herself in the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bravery: courageous behavior or character
  • synonym – courage: the ability to do something that frightens one
  • antonym – timidity: lack of courage or confidence
