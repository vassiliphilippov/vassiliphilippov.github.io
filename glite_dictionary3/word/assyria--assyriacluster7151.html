Assyria

proper noun
ancient Mesopotamian kingdom
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪriə / (AmE), / əˈsɪəriə / (BrE)
an ancient kingdom and empire in northern Mesopotamia, located largely in present-day Iraq, which flourished from the 25th century BCE until its fall in the 7th century BCE.
