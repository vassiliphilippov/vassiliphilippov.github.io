rehabilitative

adjective
helping restore health or normal life
Pronunciation: / riˌhæbɪˈleɪtɪv / (AmE), / riːˌhæbɪˈleɪtətɪv /; / riˌhæbɪˈleɪtətɪv / (BrE)
relating to the process or intention of helping someone return to health, normal life, or good condition after illness, injury, addiction, or imprisonment
