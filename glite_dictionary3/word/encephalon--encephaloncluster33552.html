encephalon

noun
the brain as part of the nervous system
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈsɛf.əˌlɑn / (AmE), / ɛnˈsɛf.əˌlɒn / (BrE)
the part of the central nervous system comprising the brain, contained within the skull and continuous with the spinal cord

Examples

human encephalon
In medical imaging, damage to the encephalon can reveal neurological disorders.
The encephalon is responsible for integrating and processing sensory information in higher animals.
The spinal cord transmits signals between the body and the encephalon.
