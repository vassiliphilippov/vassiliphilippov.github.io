agape

adjective
mouth wide open in wonder or surprise
Pronunciation: / əˈɡeɪp / (AmE), / əˈɡeɪp / (BrE)
with the mouth wide open, typically in wonder or amazement

Examples

stood agape in wonder
He stood agape in the museum, entranced by the ancient works of art.
She listened agape to the incredible news that she had won the lottery.
The children stared agape at the magician's disappearing trick.
