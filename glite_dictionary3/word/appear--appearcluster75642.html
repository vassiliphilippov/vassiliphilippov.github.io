to appear

verb
formally present oneself (in court or before authority)
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪr / (AmE), / əˈpɪə / (BrE)
to present oneself formally, especially before a court or official authority

Examples

appear before the judge
All three witnesses will appear before the judge next Tuesday.
Failure to appear in court can lead to serious legal consequences.
He was required to appear at the immigration office with his documents.
