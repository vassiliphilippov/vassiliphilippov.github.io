teleconferencing

noun
holding conferences by telecommunications
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛləˈkɑːn.fɚən.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈkɒn.fər.əns.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The use of telecommunications equipment, such as video or audio links, to hold a conference between people in different locations.
