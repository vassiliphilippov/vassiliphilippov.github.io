hindquarter

noun
back half of a side of meat
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪndˌkwɔrtər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪndˌkwɔːtə / (BrE)
The back half of a side of an animal, especially as cut and prepared for meat.

Examples

a hindquarter of beef
After the deer was butchered, the hindquarter was made into steaks and roasts.
Farmers often sell the hindquarter separately from the forequarter to butchers.
The chef selected a hindquarter of beef for the roast dinner.
