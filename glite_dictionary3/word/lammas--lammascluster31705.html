Lammas

noun
Christian harvest festival (August 1)
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm əs /; / ˈlæməs / (AmE), / ˈlæm əs /; / ˈlæməs / (BrE)
a festival held on or around August 1 to mark the beginning of the harvest, traditionally observed in Christian (England and Scotland) and modern neopagan (Wiccan, Pagan) traditions
