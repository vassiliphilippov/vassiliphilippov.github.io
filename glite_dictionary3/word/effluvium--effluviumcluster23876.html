effluvium

noun
foul-smelling vapor or outflow
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfluviəm / (AmE), / ɪˈfluːviəm / (BrE)
a foul-smelling outflow or vapor, especially a gaseous waste

A strong effluvium seeped from the chemical plant, making it hard to breathe nearby.

The stagnant pond gave off an effluvium that made the locals avoid it in summer.
