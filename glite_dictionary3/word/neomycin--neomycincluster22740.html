neomycin

noun
an antibiotic used in medicine and surgery
Pronunciation: / niː.oʊˈmaɪ.sɪn / (AmE), / niː.əʊˈmaɪ.sɪn / (BrE)
an antibiotic produced by certain actinomycetes, used to treat infections and as an intestinal antiseptic, especially in surgery

Examples

neomycin antibiotic treatment
Before surgery, patients are sometimes given neomycin as an intestinal antiseptic.
Some people may develop a skin reaction when using ointments containing neomycin.
The doctor prescribed neomycin to treat the patient's bacterial infection.
