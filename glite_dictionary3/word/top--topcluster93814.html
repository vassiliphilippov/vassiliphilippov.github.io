to top

verb
finish or cover the top (of something)
Pronunciation: / tɑp / (AmE), / tɒp / (BrE)
to provide with a top or to finish the top part of something, especially a structure

Examples

to top a cake
Each pillar was topped by an ornate marble ball.
The monument was topped with a gleaming bronze statue.
They topped the cake with a generous layer of frosting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cap: to cover or close the top or end of something
