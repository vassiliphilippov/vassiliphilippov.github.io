specialiser

noun
person expert in one field
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in and concentrates on a particular occupation or branch of learning

Examples

specialiser in medicine
As a marketing specialiser, she focuses on social media strategies for brands.
Many companies prefer to hire a specialiser rather than a generalist for technical roles.
The hospital employed a specialiser in infectious diseases to oversee the department.
