mixer

noun
non-alcoholic drink used with alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪksər / (AmE), / ˈmɪksə / (BrE)
a non-alcoholic beverage, such as club soda or fruit juice, added to alcoholic drinks to dilute or flavor them

Examples

fruit juice mixer
She asked for a mixer to go with her vodka.
Tonic water is a popular mixer for gin.
We need more mixers for the cocktail party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chaser: a mild drink taken after a strong alcoholic one
