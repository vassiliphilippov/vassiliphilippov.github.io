Plotinus

proper noun
Roman philosopher, founder of Neoplatonism
Pronunciation: / ploʊˈtaɪnəs / (AmE), / plɒˈtaɪnəs / (BrE)
(c. 205–270) A Roman philosopher, born in Egypt, who was the leading figure of Neoplatonism.
