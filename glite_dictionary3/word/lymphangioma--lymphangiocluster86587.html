lymphangioma

noun
benign tumor of lymphatic vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪmfæn.dʒiˈoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˌlɪmfæn.dʒiˈəʊ.mə / (BrE)
A benign tumor made up of an abnormal mass of lymphatic vessels.

Examples

lymphangioma in children
A lymphangioma is typically detected in early childhood during routine examinations.
The infant was diagnosed with a lymphangioma on her neck.
Treatment for a lymphangioma may involve surgical removal if it causes complications.
