New Forest

proper noun
national park and woodland in southern England
Pronunciation: / nuː ˈfɔrɪst / (AmE), / njuː ˈfɒrɪst / (BrE)
an area of woodland and heath in southern England, originally designated as royal hunting grounds in the 11th century, now a national park known for its ponies
