to negotiate

verb
discuss to reach an agreement
Pronunciation: / nəˈɡoʊʃieɪt / (AmE), / nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪt / (BrE)
to try to reach an agreement by formally discussing something, especially in business or politics

Examples

to negotiate an agreement
She helped negotiate a better contract for the athletes.
The company will negotiate with unions to prevent a strike.
We need to negotiate the terms of the agreement before signing.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bargain: to discuss to reach a mutually acceptable price or agreement
  • synonym – mediate: to intervene to settle differences in a dispute
  • antonym – refuse: to decline to negotiate or accept terms
