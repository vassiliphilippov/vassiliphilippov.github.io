blusher

noun
edible mushroom that reddens when touched
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌʃər / (AmE), / ˈblʌʃə / (BrE)
a yellowish edible mushroom (agaric) that typically turns red when touched, commonly found in woodland areas

Examples

wild blusher mushroom
Before consuming a wild mushroom, be sure it is a genuine blusher and not a similar toxic species.
The distinctive feature of the blusher is its red staining when handled or cut.
We found several blushers growing beneath the oak trees in the autumn woods.
