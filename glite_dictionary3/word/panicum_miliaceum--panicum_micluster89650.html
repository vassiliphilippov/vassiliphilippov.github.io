Panicum miliaceum

noun
species of millet grown for grain or forage
Pronunciation: / ˈpænɪkəm ˌmɪliˈeɪsiəm / (AmE), / ˈpænɪkəm ˌmɪlɪˈeɪsɪəm / (BrE)
