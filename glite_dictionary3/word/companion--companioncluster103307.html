companion

noun
a paid person who accompanies another
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjən / (AmE), / kəmˈpæn.jən /; / kəmˈpanjən / (BrE)
a person who is with you to provide company, care, or friendship (such as a friend, assistant, or travel partner)
