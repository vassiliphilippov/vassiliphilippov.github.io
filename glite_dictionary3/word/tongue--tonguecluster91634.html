to tongue

verb
use the tongue to play notes on a wind instrument
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ / (AmE), / tʌŋ / (BrE)
to use the tongue to articulate notes when playing a wind instrument, by interrupting or modifying the airflow

Examples

to tongue notes
It is important to learn how to tongue properly when playing scales on the flute.
Some beginners struggle to tongue rapidly during fast passages.
The clarinetist was careful to tongue each note for clarity.
