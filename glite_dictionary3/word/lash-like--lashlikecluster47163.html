lash-like

adjective
resembling a whip or lash
Pronunciation: / ˈlæʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlæʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having or resembling the shape or motion of a lash or whip; especially used to describe threadlike, flexible structures such as flagella.
