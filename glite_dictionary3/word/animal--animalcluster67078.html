animal

adjective
relating to physical appetites or passions
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪməl / (AmE), / ˈænɪməl / (BrE)
relating to physical appetites and passions rather than intellect or reason; sensual or carnal

Examples

animal desire
Food provided her with a purely animal pleasure that had little to do with nutrition.
He tried to resist his animal instincts and act rationally.
There was an animal intensity in their rivalry that surprised everyone.
