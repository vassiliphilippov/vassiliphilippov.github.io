loutishness

noun
rude, boorish behavior or manner
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊtɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈlaʊtɪʃnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being uncouth, rude, or aggressively boorish in behavior.

Examples

his loutishness at parties
His constant loutishness made him unpopular with his neighbors.
She was shocked by the loutishness displayed at the football match.
The concert was spoiled by the loutishness of a few in the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boorishness: rude or insensitive behavior; lack of manners
  • antonym – civility: formal politeness and courtesy in behavior or speech
