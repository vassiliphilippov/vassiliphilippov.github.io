St. Brigid

proper noun
Irish patron saint and abbess (c. 453–523)
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈbrɪdʒɪd / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈbrɪdʒɪd / (BrE)
An Irish abbess and one of the patron saints of Ireland, renowned for her piety and leadership; lived circa 453–523.
