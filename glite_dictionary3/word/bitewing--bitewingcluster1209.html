bitewing

noun
dental X-ray film held by teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪtˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪtˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
A dental X-ray film or radiograph designed to be held between the teeth, used for detecting cavities and bone loss.
