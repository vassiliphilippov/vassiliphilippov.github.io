to plug

verb
actively promote or advertise
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ / (AmE), / plʌɡ / (BrE)
to promote or advertise something by publicly praising its qualities, especially in order to sell or popularize it

Examples

to plug a new book
Celebrities often plug various products on social media platforms.
He tried to plug his cousin's restaurant during the interview.
The author will plug her new book on several talk shows this month.

  • synonym – promote: to support or actively encourage a product or cause
