to confabulate

verb
talk together; confer
Pronunciation: / kənˈfæbjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / kənˈfæbjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to talk together or discuss something, especially in order to make a decision; to confer

Examples

to confabulate about plans
The committee will confabulate before reaching a final decision.
The doctors confabulated about the best treatment for the patient.
We should confabulate with all stakeholders to ensure everyone's concerns are addressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – confer: to have discussions; exchange opinions
  • synonym – consult: to seek advice or exchange views with others
