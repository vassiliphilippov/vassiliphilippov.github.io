nationalism

noun
belief in national superiority
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəlɪzəm /; / ˈnæʃənəˌlɪzəm /; / ˈnæʃ.ə.nəˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəlɪzəm /; / ˈnaʃənəlɪzəm /; / ˈnæʃ(ə)nəlɪz(ə)m /; / ˈnæʃ.ən.əl.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
the belief or strong feeling that your own nation is more important than others, often including the desire for independence or acting in your nation's own interests
