counter

adjective
opposing; resistant
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
(used only before a noun) done in opposition or response to something; opposing or resisting.

Examples

counter argument
She made a counter argument during the debate to challenge his point.
The company launched a counter campaign in response to the negative publicity.
The government introduced counter measures to address the growing threat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – opposing: acting in opposition
  • antonym – supportive: giving support or encouragement
