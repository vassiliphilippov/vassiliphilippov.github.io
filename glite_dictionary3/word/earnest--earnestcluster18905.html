earnest

noun
deposit or pledge for a contract
Pronunciation: / ˈɝː.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈɜː.nɪst / (BrE)
a sum of money or valuable item given by one party to another as a pledge to bind a contract or agreement

Examples

earnest money
As an earnest, she gave him five gold coins to secure the land deal.
The earnest was returned when the contract was fulfilled.
They handed over a small earnest to demonstrate their commitment to the agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deposit: a sum of money paid as an initial part of a larger payment or as security for an agreement
  • synonym – down payment: an initial payment made when something is bought on credit
