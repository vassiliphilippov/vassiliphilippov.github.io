self-assertive

adjective
confidently forceful; assertive
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf əˈsɜrtɪv /; / ˌsɛlf əˈsɝtɪv / (AmE), / ˌself əˈsɜːtɪv /; / ˌsɛlf əˈsɜːtɪv / (BrE)
