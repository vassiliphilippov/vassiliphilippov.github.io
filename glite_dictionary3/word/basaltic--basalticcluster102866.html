basaltic

adjective
relating to basalt (volcanic rock)
Pronunciation: / bəˈsɔltɪk / (AmE), / bəˈsɔːltɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, made of, or containing basalt, a dark, fine-grained volcanic rock.

Examples

basaltic rock formation
Geologists studied the basaltic composition of the soil in the region.
The island's cliffs are formed from layers of basaltic rock.
Volcanoes in Hawaii frequently erupt basaltic lava flows.
