to touch

verb
consume (especially food or drink)
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ / (AmE), / tʌtʃ / (BrE)
to avoid or refuse to deal with something or someone; or to refuse to eat or drink something

Examples

to touch your dinner
He was so upset that he wouldn't touch his dinner.
I haven't touched a cigarette in ten years.
She rarely touches alcohol these days.
Most banks won't touch customers with a poor credit history.
No one would touch that risky business venture after the scandal.
She wouldn't touch politics, even if she were paid.

Related Terms

  • synonym – approach: to come near or deal with (often used figuratively)
  • synonym – drink: to take liquid into the mouth and swallow it
  • synonym – eat: to put food into the mouth, chew, and swallow it
  • synonym – handle: to deal with or manage a situation or person
