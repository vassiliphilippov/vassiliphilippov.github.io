generalisation

noun
process of forming general concepts
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛn(ə)rəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌdʒɛnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛn(ə)rələˈzeɪʃ(ə)n /; / ˌdʒɛnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming general ideas or principles by identifying what is common among specific cases
