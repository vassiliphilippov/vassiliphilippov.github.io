Richard the Lionheart

proper noun
King of England, Third Crusade leader
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃərd ðə ˈlaɪənˌhɑrt / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ðə ˈlaɪənhɑːt / (BrE)
(1157–1199) King of England from 1189 to 1199, noted for his role as a leader in the Third Crusade and for his reputation as a warrior.
