transgender

adjective
relating to a different gender identity from birth sex
Pronunciation: / trænzˈdʒɛndɚ / (AmE), / trænzˈdʒɛndə / (BrE)
describing a person whose gender identity differs from the sex they were assigned at birth, or relating to such people or experiences

Examples

transgender rights movement
Many countries are updating laws to better protect transgender individuals from discrimination.
She is a proud transgender woman who works to support others in her community.
The company has a policy supporting transgender employees and their rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cisgender: identifying with the gender assigned at birth
