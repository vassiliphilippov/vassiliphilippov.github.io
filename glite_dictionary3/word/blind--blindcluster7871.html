blind

noun
window covering to block light or view
Pronunciation: / blaɪnd / (AmE), / blaɪnd / (BrE)
A screen or covering, especially for a window, used to block light or vision or to provide privacy.

Examples

blind for windows
She pulled the blind down to keep the sunlight out of her room.
Some people prefer wooden blinds instead of curtains in their homes.
The office installed new blinds for more privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – curtain: A piece of cloth hung to cover a window or divide a room
