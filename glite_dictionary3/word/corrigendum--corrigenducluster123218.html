corrigendum

noun
a printer's error needing correction
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrɪˈdʒɛndəm / (AmE), / ˌkɒrɪˈdʒɛndəm / (BrE)
an error in a printed work that should be corrected, usually noted after publication

Examples

corrigendum in a book
A corrigendum was printed in the following issue to clarify the dates.
Readers should consult the corrigenda for corrections to the original text.
The publisher issued a corrigendum to address a typographical mistake on page three.

Related Terms

  • synonym – erratum: an error in printing or writing, especially one noted in a published list
