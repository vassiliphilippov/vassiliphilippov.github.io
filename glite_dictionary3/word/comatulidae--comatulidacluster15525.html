Comatulidae

noun
former family of crinoids (feather stars)
Pronunciation: / koʊˌmætjʊˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / kəˌmatjʊˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
a former taxonomic family of free-swimming crinoids, now considered synonymous with Antedonidae

Examples

Comatulidae family classification
In older scientific literature, some species of feather stars were assigned to the family Comatulidae.
Researchers examined fossil records attributed to Comatulidae to better understand ancient marine biodiversity.
The classification of Comatulidae has largely been replaced by Antedonidae in recent taxonomic revisions.
