grail

noun
an ultimate goal or object of pursuit
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪl / (AmE), / ɡreɪl / (BrE)
an ultimate goal or object of a prolonged and difficult search or pursuit

Examples

scientific grail discovery
Finding a cure for cancer remains the medical grail for researchers worldwide.
For many scientists, a theory that unifies gravity and quantum mechanics is the grail of modern physics.
For years, the perfect recipe was his personal grail, always just out of reach.
