criminalisation

noun
the act of making something illegal by law
Pronunciation: / krɪm.ə.nə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / krɪm.ɪ.nə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of making an activity or behavior illegal through legislation

Examples

the criminalisation of drugs
Many activists argue against the criminalisation of homelessness, calling instead for supportive measures.
Recent years have seen the criminalisation of activities that were previously considered socially acceptable.
The criminalisation of certain drugs remains a topic of heated debate around the world.
