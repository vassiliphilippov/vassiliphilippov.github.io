Job

noun
person showing patient endurance in suffering
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊb / (AmE), / dʒəʊb / (BrE)
a person who patiently bears great suffering or misfortune without complaining, referring to the biblical figure noted for his endurance

Examples

a Job in hardship
Despite his hardships, he remained a Job, never complaining about his misfortunes.
She is a real Job, enduring so much pain with quiet strength.
Sometimes you need to be a Job in tough times, showing patience and resilience.

  • synonym – martyr: a person who suffers greatly, often for a cause or belief
