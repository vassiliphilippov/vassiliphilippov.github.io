transgendered

noun
person who has had a sex change operation
Pronunciation: / trænzˈdʒɛndɚd / (AmE), / trænzˈdʒɛndəd / (BrE)
a person who has undergone a sex reassignment operation to change from one gender to another

Examples

transgendered person
Discrimination against transgendered persons is still a concern in many countries.
She supports organizations that advocate for transgendered people and their families.
The rights of transgendered individuals are increasingly being recognized worldwide.

Related Terms
