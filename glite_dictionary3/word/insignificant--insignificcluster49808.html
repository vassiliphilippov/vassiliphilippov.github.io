insignificant

adjective
unimportant; too small to matter
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.sɪɡˈnɪf.ɪ.kənt /; / ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.sɪɡˈnɪf.ɪ.kənt /; / ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt / (BrE)
too small or unimportant to be worth considering; having little or no importance, influence, or value
