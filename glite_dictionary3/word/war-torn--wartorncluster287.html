war-torn

adjective
damaged by war
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔr tɔrn / (AmE), / ˈwɔː tɔːn / (BrE)
(of a place) severely damaged or ruined by the effects of war.

Examples

war-torn city
After years of conflict, the war-torn city lay in ruins.
Humanitarian aid is desperately needed in the war-torn region.
Many refugees fled their war-torn homeland to seek safety elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym – devastated: completely destroyed or ruined
  • antonym – peaceful: free from disturbance and war
