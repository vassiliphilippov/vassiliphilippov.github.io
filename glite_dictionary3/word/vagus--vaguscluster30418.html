vagus

noun
the tenth cranial nerve; controls internal organs
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪɡəs / (AmE), / ˈveɪɡəs / (BrE)
the tenth cranial nerve (vagus nerve), which controls the heart, lungs, digestive tract, and other organs in the chest and abdomen
