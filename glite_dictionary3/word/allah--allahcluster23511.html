Allah

proper noun
the Islamic name for God
Pronunciation: / əˈlɑː / (AmE), / əˈlɑː / (BrE)
The name of God in Islam, used by Muslims and Arabic-speaking Christians and Jews.

Examples

Allah is great

Many Muslims pray to Allah five times a day facing Mecca.
