aging

noun
process of growing older
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of growing older and showing the effects of advancing age in living organisms.

Examples

natural aging process
Aging is a natural part of life that affects every living organism.
Many people try to maintain an active lifestyle to delay the signs of aging.
Research seeks ways to slow the effects of aging on the human body.
