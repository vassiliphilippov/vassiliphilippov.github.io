constructive-metabolic

adjective
of or relating to anabolism
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrʌktɪv ˌmɛtəˈbɑlɪk / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌktɪv ˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to anabolism, the metabolic process involving the building up of complex molecules in living organisms.
