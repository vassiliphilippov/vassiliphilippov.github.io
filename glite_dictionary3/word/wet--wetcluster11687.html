to wet

verb
make something or someone wet
Pronunciation: / wɛt / (AmE), / wɛt / (BrE)
to make something or someone wet by covering or soaking it with liquid

Examples

to wet your hair
He wetted his hair under the faucet before combing it.
Please wet the sponge before using it to clean the counter.
You should wet your hands before handling the dough.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dry: to remove moisture, making something not wet
  • synonym – soak: to make or become thoroughly wet by immersing in liquid
