squint-eyed

adjective
affected by strabismus (misaligned eyes)
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɪnt.aɪd / (AmE), / ˈskwɪnt.aɪd / (BrE)
Having eyes affected by strabismus; having eyes that do not look in the same direction at the same time.

Examples

squint-eyed child
She felt self-conscious about her squint-eyed look, especially in photographs.
Some people are born squint-eyed and may choose corrective surgery.
The artist depicted the character as squint-eyed to emphasize his unique appearance.
