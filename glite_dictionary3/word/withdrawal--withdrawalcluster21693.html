withdrawal

noun
ending drug use
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈdrɔː.əl / (AmE), / wɪðˈdrɔː.əl / (BrE)
the act of stopping the use of a drug, especially after becoming dependent on it

Examples

withdrawal symptoms
After ten years of addiction, his withdrawal from heroin required medical supervision.
She went through a difficult withdrawal period after quitting cigarettes.
Sudden withdrawal from certain medications can cause severe symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abstinence: the practice of deliberately avoiding something, especially alcohol or drugs
