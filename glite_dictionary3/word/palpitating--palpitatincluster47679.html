palpitating

adjective
trembling or throbbing rapidly
Pronunciation: / ˈpælpɪˌteɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpælpɪteɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
moving or trembling with a slight, rapid motion, often from excitement, fear, or exertion.

Examples

palpitating hands
His palpitating hands revealed just how nervous he was before the interview.
She could feel her palpitating heart as she waited for the test results.
The wounded bird lay on the ground, its small chest palpitating with each breath.

Related Terms

  • synonym – quivering: trembling or shaking with a slight, rapid motion
  • synonym – throbbing: beating with a strong, regular rhythm
