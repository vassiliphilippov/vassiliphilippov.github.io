faery

noun
magical, humanlike creature in folklore
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri / (AmE), / ˈfeəri / (BrE)
A small, often humanlike creature in folklore, believed to possess magical powers and a playful nature; a fairy.

Examples

tiny faery creature
In the moonlit garden, a delicate faery danced among the flowers.
Legends say that a faery can grant wishes to those deemed worthy.
