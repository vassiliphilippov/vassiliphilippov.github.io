T. S. Eliot

proper noun
British-American poet and Nobel laureate
Pronunciation: / ti ɛs ˈɛliət / (AmE), / tiː ɛs ˈɛliət / (BrE)
(1888–1965) a British poet, playwright, and literary critic, born in the United States, known for works such as 'The Waste Land' and for his contribution to modernist poetry.
