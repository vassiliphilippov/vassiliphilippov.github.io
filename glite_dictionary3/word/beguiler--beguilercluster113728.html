beguiler

noun
a deceiver or charmer
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡaɪlər / (AmE), / bɪˈɡaɪlə / (BrE)
a person who deceives or charms someone, often by leading them to believe something that is untrue

Examples

cunning beguiler
Beware of the beguiler who promises more than he can deliver.
She called him a beguiler for the way he twisted the truth.
The master beguiler convinced everyone with his charming stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deceiver: a person who causes someone to believe something that is not true
  • antonym – honest person: someone who is truthful and does not deceive others
  • synonym – trickster: a person who deceives or cheats people
