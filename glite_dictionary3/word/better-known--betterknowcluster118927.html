better-known

adjective
more widely known or famous
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛtərˈnoʊn / (AmE), / ˌbɛtəˈnəʊn / (BrE)
more widely known or familiar than the other person or thing being compared; more famous or recognized.

Examples

better-known author
Although he wrote several novels, his better-known works are in poetry.
Of the two movies, the first is far better-known around the world.
The scientist is better-known for her environmental activism than for her academic papers.
