fare

noun
payment for public transportation
Pronunciation: / fɛr / (AmE), / feə / (BrE)
the money paid for a journey on public transport such as a bus, train, or taxi

Examples

bus fare
Please have the correct fare ready before you board the train.
She handed the taxi driver the fare at the end of the ride.
The bus fare increased last month, making commuting more expensive.
