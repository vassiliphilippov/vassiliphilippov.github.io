vena nasalis externa

noun
vein of the external nose
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnə nəˈseɪlɪs ɛkˈstɜːrnə / (AmE), / ˈviːnə nəˈseɪlɪs ɛkˈstɜːnə / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the external nose and empties into the angular or facial vein

Examples

vena nasalis externa of nose
During rhinoplasty, care must be taken to avoid damaging the vena nasalis externa.
Inflammation of the vena nasalis externa can lead to localized swelling on the nose.
The vena nasalis externa drains directly into the angular vein in most individuals.
