print

noun
picture/design made from an engraving
Pronunciation: / prɪnt / (AmE), / prɪnt / (BrE)
a picture or photograph produced by pressing paper against an inked surface or from a photographic negative or digital image

  • antonym – painting: an image made with paint, not printing
