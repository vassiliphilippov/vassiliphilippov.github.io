twiddle

noun
series of idle twists or turns
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪd.əl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪd.əl / (BrE)
a series of small, usually idle, twists or turns, often made absentmindedly with the fingers

Examples

nervous twiddle of fingers
His nervous twiddle of the pen made it clear he was anxious.
She gave the key a quick twiddle before unlocking the door.
With a twiddle of her hair, she seemed lost in thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fidget: small restless movements, usually with the hands or feet
