lithesome

adjective
gracefully thin and supple
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪθsəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪθsəm / (BrE)
(of a person or their movements) gracefully thin, supple, and able to move with ease.

Examples

lithesome dancer
The cat stretched in a lithesome manner, almost appearing boneless.
The dancer's lithesome movements captivated the entire audience.
With a lithesome figure, she glided effortlessly across the stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lithe: thin, supple, and graceful (especially of the body)
