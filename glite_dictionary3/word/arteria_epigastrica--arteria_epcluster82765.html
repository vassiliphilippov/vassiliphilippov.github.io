arteria epigastrica

noun
artery supplying front of abdomen
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɛriə ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪkə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪkə / (BrE)
Any of three arteries that supply the anterior wall of the abdomen.

Examples

arteria epigastrica supplies abdomen

The arteria epigastrica provides important blood supply to the abdominal muscles.
