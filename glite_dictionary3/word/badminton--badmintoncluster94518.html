badminton

noun
a sport played with rackets and a shuttlecock
Pronunciation: / ˈbæd.mɪn.tən / (AmE), / ˈbæd.mɪn.tən / (BrE)
A game played on a court in which players use light, long-handled rackets to hit a shuttlecock back and forth over a net.

Examples

play badminton matches
She is the captain of the university badminton team.
The school offers badminton as part of its physical education program.
We played a friendly match of badminton in the backyard yesterday.
