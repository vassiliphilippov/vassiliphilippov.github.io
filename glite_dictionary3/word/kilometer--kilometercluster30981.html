kilometer

noun
metric unit of length (1,000 metres)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / kɪˈlɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to 1,000 metres or approximately 0.62 miles

Examples

run five kilometers
It is about ten kilometers from my house to the city center.
The marathon course is over forty-two kilometers long.
They measured the distance and found it was exactly one kilometer.
