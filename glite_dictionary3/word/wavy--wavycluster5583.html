wavy

adjective
having a series of smooth curves
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvi /; / ˈweɪ.vi / (AmE), / ˈweɪvi /; / ˈweɪ.vi / (BrE)
having a series of smooth curves or undulations; not straight or flat (especially used for hair, but also for other things)
