eggnog

noun
sweet alcoholic drink with eggs and milk
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡˌnɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈɛɡ.nɒɡ / (BrE)
a rich, sweet drink made with milk or cream, beaten eggs, sugar, and usually alcohol, often served at festive occasions

Examples

homemade eggnog
Every Christmas, my grandmother makes a batch of homemade eggnog for the family.
She brought a jug of eggnog to the holiday party, and it was gone in minutes.
Some people like to add a sprinkle of nutmeg on top of their eggnog.
