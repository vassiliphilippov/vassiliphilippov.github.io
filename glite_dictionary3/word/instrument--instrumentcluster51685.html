instrument

noun
device requiring skill for use
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.strə.mənt / (BrE)
A tool or device designed for a specific function that requires skill to use, especially in scientific, musical, or technical work.

Examples

scientific instrument
Learning to play a musical instrument takes years of practice and dedication.
The scientists used a precise instrument to measure the chemical reaction.
The surgeon picked up a delicate instrument to begin the operation.
