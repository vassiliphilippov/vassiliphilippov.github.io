muzzle

noun
end of a gun barrel
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌzl̩ / (BrE)
the open circular end of a gun barrel from which the bullet or projectile exits

Examples

muzzle of a gun
Always keep the muzzle of the gun pointed in a safe direction.
