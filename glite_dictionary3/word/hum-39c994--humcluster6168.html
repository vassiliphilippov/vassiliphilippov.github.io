to hum

verb
be busy and noisy with activity
Pronunciation: / hʌm / (AmE), / hʌm / (BrE)
(of a place) to be full of activity and noise, often from people working or moving about.

Examples

hum with activity
By midday, the market was humming as customers arrived in large numbers.
The office was humming with activity on Monday morning.
The restaurant hummed with conversation and laughter all evening.

  • synonym – buzz: to be busy and full of lively activity and noise
