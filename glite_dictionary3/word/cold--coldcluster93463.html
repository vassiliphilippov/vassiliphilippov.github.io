cold

noun
a mild viral respiratory infection
Pronunciation: / koʊld / (AmE), / kəʊld / (BrE)
a common viral infection of the nose and upper respiratory tract, causing symptoms such as a runny nose, sneezing, and sore throat

Examples

catch a cold
It's normal to catch a cold during the winter months.
Many people treat a cold with rest and plenty of fluids.
She missed two days of school because she had a bad cold.
