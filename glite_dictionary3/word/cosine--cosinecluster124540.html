cosine

noun
vector similarity measure
Pronunciation: / c8ko8a.sa6an / (AmE), / c8k598a.sa6an / (BrE)
A measure of similarity between two non-zero vectors equal to their dot product divided by the product of their magnitudes; commonly used in information retrieval and machine learning.

Examples

cosine similarity score
The recommender system uses cosine to measure similarity between user preference vectors.
To compare documents, the analyst calculated the cosine of their TF-IDF vectors.
During training, the model maximised the cosine between positive item and user vectors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cosine similarity: the similarity measure defined as the cosine of the angle between two vectors; often used to compare documents or embeddings
