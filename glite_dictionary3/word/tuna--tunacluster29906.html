tuna

noun
a New Zealand freshwater eel
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːnə / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnə / (BrE)
an eel found in New Zealand rivers and lakes, especially species important in Māori culture

Examples

to catch tuna
The Māori traditionally catch tuna during the annual migration season.
This conservation program aims to protect the habitat of native tuna in New Zealand.
We saw several tuna swimming in the shallow river near the village.
