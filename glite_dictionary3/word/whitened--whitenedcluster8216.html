whitened

adjective
turned white in color, often with age
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtənd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtənd / (BrE)
(of hair or other things) having become white in color, especially as a result of age or bleaching.

Examples

whitened hair
Her once black hair is now whitened by the passing years.
Streaks of whitened hair appeared at her temples as she aged.
The old man's whitened beard made him look wise and venerable.
