have-not

noun
a poor person; someone lacking wealth or possessions
Pronunciation: / ˌhævˈnɑt / (AmE), / ˌhævˈnɒt / (BrE)
a person who is poor or lacks wealth, especially in contrast to those who are well-off

Examples

The economic divide between the have-nots and the wealthy has widened in recent years.
