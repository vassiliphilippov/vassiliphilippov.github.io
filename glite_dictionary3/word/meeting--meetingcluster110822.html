meeting

noun
gathering for a purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈmitɪŋ /; / ˈmiː.t̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪŋ /; / ˈmiː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
an occasion when people come together for a particular purpose, especially to discuss something (can be formal or informal)

Examples

business meeting
Do you have time for a quick meeting this afternoon?
She arranged a meeting with her lawyer to discuss the contract.
The weekly staff meeting is scheduled for 10 a.m. every Monday.
After the meeting, everyone felt more informed about the changes.
She scheduled a meeting with her team to discuss the new project.
The annual board meeting will take place next Thursday.
