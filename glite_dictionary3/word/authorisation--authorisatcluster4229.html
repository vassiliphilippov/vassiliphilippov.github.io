authorisation

noun
official permission or approval
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːθərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
official permission or approval to do something

Examples

manager grants authorisation
The manager must grant authorisation before you proceed with the purchase.
We received authorisation to access the restricted area.
Without proper authorisation, entry is strictly prohibited.
All transactions must have prior authorisation from the finance department.
She received formal authorisation to proceed with the project.
The manager's authorisation is required before you can access those files.
