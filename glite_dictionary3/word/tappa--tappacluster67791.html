tappa

noun
fibrous bark used for bark cloth
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːpə / (AmE), / ˈtæpə / (BrE)
a paper-like cloth made in the Pacific Islands by beating the inner bark of certain trees, or the bark itself used for this purpose

Examples

tappa cloth making
