to wheelbarrow

verb
to move using a wheelbarrow
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlˌbæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlˌbærəʊ / (BrE)
to transport or move something using a wheelbarrow.

Examples

to wheelbarrow heavy bags
Can you wheelbarrow these bags of soil over to the flowerbed?
He wheelbarrowed the heavy stones from the garden to the shed.
She wheelbarrowed mulch all afternoon during the garden project.
