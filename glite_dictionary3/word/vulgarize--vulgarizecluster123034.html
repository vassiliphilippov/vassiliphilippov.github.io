to vulgarize

verb
make more accessible or less refined for the public
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlɡjəˌraɪz /; / ˈvʌlɡəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈvʌlɡəˌraɪz /; / ˈvʌlɡəraɪz / (BrE)
to make something less refined, sophisticated, or of lower quality, often to appeal to the general public
