to stump

verb
remove tree stumps from land
Pronunciation: / stʌmp / (AmE), / stʌmp / (BrE)
to remove tree stumps from a piece of land, often to prepare it for cultivation or construction

Examples

to stump a field
After logging, they decided to stump the area to clear it for development.
It took several days to stump the entire property using heavy machinery.
The farmer hired workers to stump the field before planting new crops.
