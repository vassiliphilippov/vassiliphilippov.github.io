milker

noun
automatic milking machine
Pronunciation: / c8m6alk59r / (AmE), / c8m6alk59 / (BrE)
a machine or robotic system that automatically milks cows, extracting and collecting milk without manual milking.

Examples

automatic milker
The dairy upgraded to a robotic milker to automate daytime milking.
A technician serviced the new milker before the herd arrived that morning.
Small family farms often share a portable milker to reduce equipment costs.
