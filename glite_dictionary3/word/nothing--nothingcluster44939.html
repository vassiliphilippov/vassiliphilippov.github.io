nothing

noun
not anything; zero, none
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ / (BrE)
not anything; no single thing; a quantity of no importance or zero value.

Examples

nothing to show
After all that effort, we ended up with nothing to show for it.
She said nothing during the entire meeting.
There is nothing in the box—it's completely empty.
