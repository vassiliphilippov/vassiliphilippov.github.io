Jurassic

adjective
of the Jurassic geological period
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈræsɪk / (AmE), / dʒʊəˈræsɪk / (BrE)
relating to the second period of the Mesozoic Era, characterized by the dominance of dinosaurs and the appearance of the first birds.

Examples

Jurassic fossils
Many fossils discovered in the region date back to Jurassic times.
The Jurassic period saw a tremendous increase in the diversity of dinosaurs.
The landscape is dotted with Jurassic limestone formations.

  • degree lower – Triassic: earlier period of the Mesozoic Era
