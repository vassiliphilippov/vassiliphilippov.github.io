divulgence

noun
act of disclosing secrets or private matters
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvʌldʒəns / (AmE), / dɪˈvʌl.dʒəns / (BrE)
the act of making something known that was previously secret or private

Examples

divulgence of secrets
His unexpected divulgence of company secrets shocked his colleagues.
She regretted the divulgence of personal details during the interview.
The divulgence of classified documents caused a national scandal.
