to bench

verb
place an animal on display at a show
Pronunciation: / bɛntʃ / (AmE), / bɛntʃ / (BrE)
to place (an animal, especially a dog) on display or in a designated area at an exhibition or competition for judging

Examples

to bench a dog (show)
All participants are asked to bench their pets in the main exhibition hall.
At the annual competition, owners bench their dogs for the judges.
You must bench your spaniel before the show starts at noon.
