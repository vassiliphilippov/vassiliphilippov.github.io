unrhythmic

adjective
lacking regular rhythm
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪð.mɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪð.mɪk / (BrE)
not having a regular or consistent rhythm; irregular in beat or accent

Examples

unrhythmic dance steps
His steps were so unrhythmic that he tripped over his own feet.
The dancer’s movements were unrhythmic and difficult to follow.
The poem felt disjointed because of its unrhythmic meter.

Related Terms

  • antonym – rhythmic: having a regular repeated pattern of sounds or movements
