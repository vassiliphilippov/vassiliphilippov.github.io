setter

noun
a breed of hunting dog
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛtə / (BrE)
a breed of long-haired dog once trained to crouch when finding game, but now trained to indicate its location by standing still and pointing

Examples

hunting setter dog
During the hunt, the setter froze and pointed to alert the hunters to nearby game.
She adopted a beautiful Irish setter from the local animal shelter.
The setter moved gracefully through the tall grass, nose to the ground.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pointer: a breed of hunting dog that indicates game by pointing
