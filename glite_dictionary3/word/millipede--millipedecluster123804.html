millipede

noun
arthropod with many legs
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌpid /; / ˈmɪləˌpid / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪpiːd /; / ˈmɪlɪˌpiːd / (BrE)
a small, elongated arthropod with a long, segmented body, most segments having two pairs of legs

Examples
