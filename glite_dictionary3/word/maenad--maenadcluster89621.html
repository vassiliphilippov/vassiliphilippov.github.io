maenad

noun
female follower of Dionysus in Greek myth
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnæd / (AmE), / ˈmiːnad / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a female follower of Dionysus, often depicted as frenzied or ecstatic; also, a woman who behaves in a wild or ecstatic way
