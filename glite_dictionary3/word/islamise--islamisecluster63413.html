to Islamise

verb
make conform to Islamic law
Pronunciation: / ˈɪsləˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɪsləmaɪz / (BrE)
to cause something, such as laws or customs, to conform to Islamic law or principles

Examples

to Islamise laws
Certain groups hope to Islamise social practices in accordance with religious teachings.
The government attempted to Islamise the education system by revising school curricula.
There was debate over plans to Islamise local dietary regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Christianise: cause to conform to Christian principles or beliefs
  • antonym – secularise: make something less influenced by religion; remove religious influence
