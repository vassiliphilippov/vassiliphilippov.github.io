remote

adjective
far from populated areas; isolated
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmoʊt / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊt / (BrE)
far away from cities or people; distant in space

Examples

remote village
Medical supplies are difficult to deliver to such remote communities.
The expedition set out toward a remote region only a few had ever visited.
They live in a remote mountain village with no paved roads.
Astronomers study the light from remote galaxies to understand the universe's origins.
The village is located in a remote part of the country, miles from the nearest town.
They dreamed of traveling to a remote island with no signs of civilization.
