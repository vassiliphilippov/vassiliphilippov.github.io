buffalo

noun
large wild ox-like animal of Africa and Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌfələʊ / (BrE)
a large wild animal of the cattle family with horns and a heavy body, found in parts of Africa and Asia, such as the water buffalo and Cape buffalo.

Examples

water buffalo farm
A herd of buffalo grazed near the riverbank at sunrise.
The water buffalo is often used as a draft animal in Southeast Asia.
Unlike the American bison, the African Cape buffalo is much more aggressive.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bison: a large, shaggy-haired wild ox, native to North America and Europe
