to individualise

verb
make or treat as individual
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəˌlaɪz /; / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəlaɪz /; / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something unique or tailored to a particular person; to give something a distinctive or personal character
