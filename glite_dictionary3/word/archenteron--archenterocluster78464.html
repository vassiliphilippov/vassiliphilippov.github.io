archenteron

noun
central embryonic cavity forming the gut
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈkɛntəˌrɑn / (AmE), / ɑːˈkɛntərɒn / (BrE)
the central cavity within the gastrula stage of an embryo, which develops into the intestinal or digestive cavity
