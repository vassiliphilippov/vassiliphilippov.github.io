cream

noun
the fatty part of milk
Pronunciation: / kriːm / (AmE), / kriːm / (BrE)
the thick, fatty part of milk that rises to the top and is used in cooking or as a food

Examples

cream in coffee
She poured a little cream into her coffee.
The dessert was topped with fresh strawberries and cream.
The recipe calls for a cup of heavy cream.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – milk: the liquid produced by the mammary glands of mammals, especially cows, used as food
