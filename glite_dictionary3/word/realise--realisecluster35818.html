to realise

verb
become fully aware or understand
Pronunciation: / ˈriːəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɪəlaɪz / (BrE)
to become fully aware of or understand something clearly

Examples

to realise a mistake

She finally realised the truth about her friend's intentions.
