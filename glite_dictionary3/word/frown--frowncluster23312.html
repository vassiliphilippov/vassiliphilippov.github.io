frown

noun
facial expression of displeasure
Pronunciation: / fraʊn / (AmE), / fraʊn / (BrE)
A facial expression made by bringing the eyebrows together and wrinkling the forehead, showing dislike, displeasure, or concern.

Examples

frown lines
A deep frown appeared on his face when he heard the news.
She gave him a puzzled frown before answering the question.
The teacher's frown made the students fall silent.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scowl: an angry or bad-tempered expression
  • antonym – smile: a pleased, kind, or amused facial expression
