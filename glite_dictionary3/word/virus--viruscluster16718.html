virus

noun
malicious self-replicating computer program
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a computer program that can copy itself and often causes harm to files or other programs on the same computer

Examples

computer virus spreads
He installed new security software to protect his computer from a virus.
Opening email attachments from unknown senders might expose your system to a virus.
The company's network was infected by a virus that deleted important files.

Related Terms

  • synonym – malware: software designed to harm, exploit, or otherwise compromise computers or networks
