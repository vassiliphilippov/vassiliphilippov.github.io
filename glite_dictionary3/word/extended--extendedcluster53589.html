extended

adjective
lasting longer than usual; prolonged
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛndɪd / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛndɪd / (BrE)
lasting longer than expected or usual; tediously prolonged in time or duration.

Examples

extended stay
Due to the storm, their vacation involved an extended stay at the hotel.
He grew impatient with the extended negotiations over the contract.
The meeting turned into an extended discussion that lasted for hours.
