nib

noun
writing point of a pen
Pronunciation: / nɪb / (AmE), / nɪb / (BrE)
the pointed metal or plastic part at the end of a pen that touches the paper when you write

Examples

nib of a pen
Calligraphers often use pens with different nibs to achieve varied line thickness.
He replaced the nib of his fountain pen after it became worn out.
Make sure the nib is clean before you begin writing.
