third

noun
third base in baseball
Pronunciation: / θɝd / (AmE), / θɜːd / (BrE)
(in baseball) the base that must be touched third by a base runner, usually referred to as 'third base'.

Examples

reach third base
After a strong hit, she advanced to third without hesitation.
He slid into third just ahead of the throw.
The coach signaled to the runner at third to head home.

Related Terms

  • synonym – third base: the third of four bases on a baseball field, which a player must touch in sequence to score a run
