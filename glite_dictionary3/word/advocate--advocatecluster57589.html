advocate

noun
a person who supports or argues for a cause
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.və.keɪt / (AmE), / ˈæd.və.kət / (BrE)
a person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy

Examples

advocate for change
As an advocate of equal rights, he spoke at the rally.
She has always been a passionate advocate for environmental protection.
The organization is seeking an advocate to help represent the needs of local children.

Related Terms

  • antonym – opponent: a person who disagrees with or fights against a cause
  • synonym – proponent: a person who puts forward an idea or proposal
