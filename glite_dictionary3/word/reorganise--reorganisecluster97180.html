to reorganise

verb
arrange or organize again
Pronunciation: / riˈɔr.ɡə.naɪz /; / riˈɔrɡəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / riˈɔː.ɡə.naɪz /; / riːˈɔːɡənaɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or organize something again in a new or different way
