to toss

verb
to mix lightly, especially food
Pronunciation: / tɑs / (AmE), / tɒs / (BrE)
to mix or agitate lightly by lifting and turning, especially with reference to food such as salad

Examples

to toss a salad
Before grilling, he quickly tossed the vegetables in a spicy marinade.
She likes to toss the salad with olive oil and lemon juice before serving.
The chef is tossing the pasta to make sure it's evenly coated with sauce.
