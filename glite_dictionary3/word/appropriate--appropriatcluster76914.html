to appropriate

verb
take cultural elements without permission
Pronunciation: / 59c8pro8apricce6at / (AmE), / 59c8pr598apr6ae6at / (BrE)
to take or use elements (for example styles, symbols, or ideas) from another culture or group for one's own purposes, often without permission, acknowledgement, or understanding, and sometimes causing offence.

Examples

to appropriate cultural symbols
The designer chose to appropriate traditional motifs from various cultures for their runway show.
Critics argued that the musician appropriated sacred symbols without understanding their cultural meaning.
Companies must avoid advertising that appropriate indigenous art for profit without permission.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cultural appropriation: the adoption or use of elements of one culture by members of another culture, often without permission or understanding
  • synonym – steal: to take something that belongs to someone else without permission; sometimes used as a stronger synonym when appropriation is illicit or exploitative
