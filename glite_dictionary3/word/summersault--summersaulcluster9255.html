summersault

noun
acrobatic flip where feet roll over head
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmərˌsɔlt / (AmE), / ˈsʌməˌsɔːlt / (BrE)
An acrobatic movement in which a person turns forward or backward in a complete revolution, bringing the feet over the head and landing on the feet.

Examples

do a summersault
Children laughed as they practiced summersaults on the soft grass.
He tried to do a summersault but landed awkwardly on his back.
The gymnast performed a flawless summersault during her floor routine.
