Laredo

proper noun
city in southern Texas
Pronunciation: / ləˈreɪdoʊ / (AmE), / ləˈreɪdəʊ / (BrE)
A city in southern Texas, situated on the north bank of the Rio Grande, known for its role as a major port of entry between the United States and Mexico.
