to breast

verb
face or confront directly
Pronunciation: / brɛst / (AmE), / brɛst / (BrE)
to confront or meet something, especially a difficulty or obstacle, directly and with determination

Examples

to breast a challenge
Despite the force of the wind, he breasted the storm and reached the shore safely.
She was determined to breast every challenge that lay ahead of her.
The hikers breasted the mountain pass before sunset, pressing forward despite exhaustion.
