naprapath

noun
therapist practicing naprapathy
Pronunciation: / ˈnæp.rəˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈnap.rəˌpæθ / (BrE)
A therapist trained and qualified to practice naprapathy, a system of treatment involving manual manipulation of connective tissues.

naprapath treats pain
After injuring his back, he visited a naprapath for treatment.
Her sister became a naprapath specializing in connective tissue therapies.
The clinic employs both chiropractors and a certified naprapath.
