Ostyak

noun
member of a Siberian Ugrian people
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːstiˌæk / (AmE), / ˈɒstiːˌak / (BrE)
a member of a nomadic Ugrian people traditionally living in northwestern Siberia, east of the Ural Mountains

Examples

an Ostyak person
Anthropologists have studied Ostyak traditions and beliefs for over a century.
Many Ostyak people now prefer to be known as Khanty, reflecting their own name for themselves.
The Ostyak have a long history of fishing and reindeer herding in Siberia.
