to radicalize

verb
make more extreme in views
Pronunciation: / ˈrædɪkəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrædɪkəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone adopt more extreme social or political views or policies

Examples

to radicalize young people
He was radicalized after reading articles about political injustice.
The group hopes to radicalize young people through social media campaigns.
Years of oppressive policies helped radicalize the population against the government.
