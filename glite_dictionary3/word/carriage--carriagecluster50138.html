carriage

noun
baby stroller or pram
Pronunciation: / ˈkærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪdʒ / (BrE)
a small vehicle with four wheels in which a baby or young child is pushed around

Examples

baby carriage
Please fold up the carriage before getting on the bus.
The old-fashioned carriage had large wheels and a canopy.
We took the baby for a walk in his carriage around the neighborhood.
