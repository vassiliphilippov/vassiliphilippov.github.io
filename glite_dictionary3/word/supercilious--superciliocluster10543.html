supercilious

adjective
arrogantly superior or disdainful
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈsɪliəs /; / ˌsuː.pɚˈsɪl.i.əs / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈsɪlɪəs /; / ˌsuː.pəˈsɪl.i.əs / (BrE)
behaving or looking as though you think you are better than other people; showing arrogant disdain or haughtiness
