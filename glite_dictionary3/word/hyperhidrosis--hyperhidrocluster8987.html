hyperhidrosis

noun
excessive sweating disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərhaɪˈdroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəhʌɪˈdrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by excessive and profuse sweating, often more than what is needed to regulate body temperature.

hyperhidrosis treatment options
Many people with hyperhidrosis feel self-conscious about their condition in social situations.
