generically

adverb
without a brand or trademark name
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnɛrɪkli / (AmE), / dʒəˈnɛrɪkli / (BrE)
Without referring to a specific brand name or trademark, usually in relation to products or pharmaceuticals.

Examples

generically labeled medicine
Customers often choose products that are marketed generically to save money.
Many medicines are sold generically after their patent expires.
The company decided to package their cereal generically rather than creating a branded label.
