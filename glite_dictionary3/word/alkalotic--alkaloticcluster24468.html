alkalotic

adjective
characterized by or tending toward alkalosis
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.kəˈlɑ.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌæl.kəˈlɒ.tɪk / (BrE)
(of a person, tissue, or condition) characterized by or tending toward alkalosis (an excess of base or decrease of acid in the body).

Examples

alkalotic blood sample
Blood gas analysis revealed an alkalotic state due to excessive vomiting.
Doctors monitor for alkalotic imbalances to prevent complications in critical care patients.
The patient became alkalotic after prolonged hyperventilation.
