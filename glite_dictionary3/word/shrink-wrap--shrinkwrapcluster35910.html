shrink-wrap

noun
license or terms bundled with a product
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u0283r\u026a\u014bk \u02ccr\u00e6p / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0283r\u026a\u014bk \u02ccr\u00e6p / (BrE)
a set of license terms or a contract provided with a product (especially software) that is intended to become binding when the package is opened or the product is used.

Examples

shrink-wrap license agreement
The company enclosed a shrink-wrap agreement that users must accept before opening the software package.
Many customers ignore the shrink-wrap terms, not realizing they restrict liability and require arbitration for disputes.
A recent court case found the shrink-wrap license unenforceable because it was not reasonably communicated before purchase.

Related Terms

  • synonym – end-user license agreement: a contract specifying how a user may use software and what restrictions apply
  • synonym – terms of use: the rules and conditions that govern a person's use of a product or service
