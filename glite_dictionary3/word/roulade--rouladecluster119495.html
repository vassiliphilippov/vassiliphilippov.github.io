roulade

noun
meat dish rolled around filling
Pronunciation: / ruːˈlɑd / (AmE), / ruːˈlɑːd / (BrE)
A dish consisting of a thin slice of meat rolled around a filling and cooked.

Examples

beef roulade dish
Making a beef roulade requires some skill in rolling the meat around the flavorful filling.
She served a chicken roulade at the holiday party, impressing all the guests.
The chef prepared a delicious roulade stuffed with spinach and cheese for dinner.
