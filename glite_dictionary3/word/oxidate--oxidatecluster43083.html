to oxidate

verb
to combine with oxygen; convert to an oxide
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒksɪdeɪt / (BrE)
to combine with oxygen or to add oxygen to something, especially as a chemical reaction
