four-dimensional

adjective
relating to four dimensions
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔr.dɪˈmɛn.ʃə.nəl / (AmE), / ˌfɔː.dɪˈmɛn.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
Involving or relating to four dimensions, especially including time as the fourth dimension in addition to the three spatial dimensions.

Examples

four-dimensional space-time
In physics, time is often considered the fourth component in a four-dimensional model.
Some computer games allow players to interact in a four-dimensional environment.
The theory describes the universe as a four-dimensional space-time continuum.
