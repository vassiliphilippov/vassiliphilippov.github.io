Cristoforo Colombo

proper noun
Italian name for Christopher Columbus, explorer
Pronunciation: / krisˌtoʊfəroʊ koʊˈlʌmboʊ / (AmE), / krɪˌstɒfəroʊ kəˈlʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
The Italian name for Christopher Columbus (1451–1506), the Genoese navigator and explorer who completed four voyages across the Atlantic Ocean under the Spanish crown, opening the way for the widespread European exploration of the Americas.
