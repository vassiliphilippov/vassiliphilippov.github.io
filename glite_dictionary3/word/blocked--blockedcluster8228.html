blocked

adjective
obstructed; closed off
Pronunciation: / blɑkt / (AmE), / blɒkt / (BrE)
completely obstructed or closed off so that movement or flow is prevented

Examples

blocked nose
Her nose was so blocked that she could hardly breathe through it.
Several blocked pipes caused flooding in the basement.
The highway was blocked by fallen trees after the storm.
Due to construction, the street remains blocked to all traffic until further notice.
Several blocked intersections caused long delays for commuters this morning.
The main road was blocked by a fallen tree after the storm.

  • antonym – open: not closed or blocked; allowing passage
  • synonym – plugged: obstructed so that nothing can pass through
  • antonym – unblocked: not obstructed or closed off; clear
