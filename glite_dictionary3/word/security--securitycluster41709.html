security

noun
the state of being safe from danger
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊr.ə.ti /; / sɪˈkjʊrɪti /; / sɪˈkjʊrəti / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti /; / sɪˈkjʊərəti /; / sɪˈkjʊərɪti / (BrE)
