antivenene

noun
antitoxin for animal venom
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈvɛn.iːn / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈvɛniːn / (BrE)
A medical substance that neutralizes the effects of venom from snake, insect, or other animal bites.
