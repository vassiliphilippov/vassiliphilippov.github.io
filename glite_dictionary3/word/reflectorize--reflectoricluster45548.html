to reflectorize

verb
add reflectors or reflective coating
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛktəˌraɪz / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛktəraɪz / (BrE)
to provide (a surface or object) with reflectors or a reflective coating, especially for increasing visibility and safety

Examples

to reflectorize a bicycle
All bicycles must be reflectorized under the new safety regulations.
The construction crew decided to reflectorize the signs to enhance nighttime visibility.
They spent the afternoon reflectorizing the school's parking lot for the annual festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym – retroreflect: to reflect light directly back to its source
