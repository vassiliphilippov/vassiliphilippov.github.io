hopeful

adjective
promising; likely to succeed
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊpfəl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpfʊl /; / ˈhəʊpfəl / (BrE)
showing or feeling hope; having a positive expectation about the future or outcome
