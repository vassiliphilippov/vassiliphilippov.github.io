dazzling

adjective
shining very brightly; intensely bright
Pronunciation: / ˈdæzlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdæzlɪŋ / (BrE)
shining with a very bright or intense light, making it difficult to see

Examples

dazzling light
She was momentarily blinded by the dazzling headlights of the oncoming car.
The dazzling sunlight reflected off the snow, making it hard to see.
The ice sparkled with a dazzling brilliance under the winter sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blinding: so bright it makes vision difficult
  • antonym – dim: not shining brightly or clearly
  • synonym – glaring: giving out a strong, harsh light
