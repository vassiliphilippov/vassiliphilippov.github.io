dewberry

noun
a trailing bramble with blackberry-like fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈduːˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈdjuːˌbɛri / (BrE)
a trailing bramble plant of the rose family, especially of North America, bearing edible black or dark purple berries similar to blackberries

Examples

dewberry bush
She gathered a basket full of ripe dewberries on her morning walk.
The dewberry has a sweet, tangy flavor that is perfect for making jam.
We found a patch of dewberry bushes near the riverbank.
