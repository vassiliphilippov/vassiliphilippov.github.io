exchange

noun
telecommunications facility connecting telephone lines
Pronunciation: / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
a building or facility where telephone lines are connected so that calls can be made between telephones

Examples

telephone exchange
Calls in this area are routed through the local exchange just outside town.
Technicians spent the morning repairing faults at the regional exchange.
The company's old exchange was decommissioned after the network upgrade.
