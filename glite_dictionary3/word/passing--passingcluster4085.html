passing

adjective
relating to advancing the ball by throwing
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
(in sports) relating to or involving the act of advancing the ball by throwing it, especially as part of a team's offensive strategy.

Examples

passing game strategy
The team is known for its strong passing game.
Their passing offense allowed them to score several touchdowns last season.
With an improved passing attack, they have become serious contenders for the championship.
