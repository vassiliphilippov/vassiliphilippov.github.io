finisher

noun
baseball relief pitcher who closes games
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a relief pitcher who is brought in to protect a lead in the last inning or two of the game.

Examples

finisher saves game
After years as a starting pitcher, he transitioned into the role of finisher for the team.
Every championship team relies on a dependable finisher to close out tight games.
The manager called on his trusted finisher to secure the win in the ninth inning.
