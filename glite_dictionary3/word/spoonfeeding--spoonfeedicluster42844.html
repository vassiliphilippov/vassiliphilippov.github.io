spoonfeeding

noun
feeding someone using a spoon
Pronunciation: / ˈspuːnˌfiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspuːnˌfiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of feeding someone, especially a baby, with a spoon.

Examples

spoonfeeding a baby
