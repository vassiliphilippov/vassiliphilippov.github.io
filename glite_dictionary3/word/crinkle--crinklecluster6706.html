crinkle

noun
slight fold or wrinkle in a surface
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪŋkəl / (BrE)
a small fold, wrinkle, or crease, especially in a surface such as skin or fabric.

Examples

a crinkle in fabric
After sitting in his pocket, the letter had developed a noticeable crinkle.
She laughed, creating a crinkle at the edge of her eyes.
The old photograph showed a deep crinkle in the corner where it had been folded.
