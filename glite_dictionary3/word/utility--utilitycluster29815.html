utility

adjective
usable but inferior grade of beef
Pronunciation: / juˈtɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / juːˈtɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
(of beef) of a usable but inferior grade, lower in quality than standard.

Examples

utility beef
Despite being utility grade, the beef was still safe and suitable for consumption.
The processor only purchases utility grade meat for their budget line.
This cut was classified as utility beef, so it was used for ground products.
