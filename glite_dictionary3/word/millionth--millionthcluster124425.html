millionth

adjective
ordinal number: position one millionth
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪljənθ / (AmE), / ˈmɪljənθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number corresponding to one million in a sequence; the position marked as number one million.

Examples

millionth time

Their company celebrated its millionth sale with a big party.
