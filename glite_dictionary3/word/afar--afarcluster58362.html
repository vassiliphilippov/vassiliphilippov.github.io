afar

adverb
at or from a great distance
Pronunciation: / əˈfɑr / (AmE), / əˈfɑː / (BrE)
(old-fashioned) at, from, or to a great distance; far away.

Examples

afar from home
The ancient castle could be seen afar on the misty hillside.
The sound of the festival music drifted afar across the valley.
Travelers brought news afar from distant lands.

Related Terms

  • synonym – far: at, to, or by a great distance; not near
