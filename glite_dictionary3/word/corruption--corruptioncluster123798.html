corruption

noun
moral depravity or moral decay
Pronunciation: / kəˈrʌpʃən /; / kəˈɹʌpʃən / (AmE), / kəˈrʌpʃən /; / kəˈɹʌpʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of being morally bad or losing good values; making someone or something immoral
