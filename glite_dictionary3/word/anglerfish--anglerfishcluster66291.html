anglerfish

noun
predatory fish with lure on head
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡlərˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡləˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
A type of fish with a large mouth and a wormlike growth on its head used for luring prey.
