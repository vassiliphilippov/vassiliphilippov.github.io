faulty

adjective
containing errors; defective
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔlti / (AmE), / ˈfɔːlti / (BrE)
containing mistakes or defects; not working correctly or not following rules or standards.

Examples

faulty reasoning
His argument was dismissed for being based on faulty reasoning.
She returned the faulty charger that failed to power her laptop.
The machine has been recalled because it was found to be faulty and unsafe.

Related Terms

  • synonym – defective: having a fault or defect; imperfect
  • antonym – flawless: having no defects or faults
