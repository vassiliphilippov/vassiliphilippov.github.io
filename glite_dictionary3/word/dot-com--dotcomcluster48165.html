dot-com

adjective
relating to Internet companies
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑt kɑm / (AmE), / ˈdɒt kɒm / (BrE)
relating to a business that operates mainly or entirely on the Internet, especially referring to those started during the late 1990s Internet boom.

Examples

dot-com company
After the dot-com bust, many startups struggled to survive in a changed marketplace.
She works for a successful dot-com advertising agency.
The city attracted countless dot-com entrepreneurs during the tech boom.
