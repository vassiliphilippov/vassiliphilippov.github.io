to void

verb
to empty or clear something of contents
Pronunciation: / vɔɪd / (AmE), / vɔɪd / (BrE)
to empty or clear a place or container of its contents or occupants

Examples

to void a room
After the guests left, the staff voided the banquet hall of all decorations.
The police voided the building when the fire alarm went off.
The technician carefully voided the tank before refilling it with a new solution.
