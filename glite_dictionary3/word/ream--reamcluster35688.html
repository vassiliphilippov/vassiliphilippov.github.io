ream

noun
a quantity of paper (480 or 500 sheets)
Pronunciation: / rim / (AmE), / riːm / (BrE)
a standard quantity of paper, usually consisting of 480 or 500 sheets (20 quires)

Examples

a ream of paper
She used almost a full ream printing the draft of her thesis.
The contract was so long it filled an entire ream of paper.
