compromise

noun
security breach; data exposure
Pronunciation: / c8k51d0mpr59ccma6az / (AmE), / c8k52m.pr59.ma6az / (BrE)
(in information security) an incident in which systems, accounts, or data are accessed, exposed, or altered without authorization, reducing confidentiality or integrity.

Examples

data compromise incident
The company announced a compromise that exposed millions of customer records to hackers.
After detecting unusual activity, the team investigated the compromise to identify affected accounts.
Regulatory fines followed when auditors confirmed a compromise of the database's encryption keys.
