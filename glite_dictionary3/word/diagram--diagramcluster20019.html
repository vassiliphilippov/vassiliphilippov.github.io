to diagram

verb
to make a schematic drawing
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪ.əˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.əɡræm / (BrE)
to represent something by means of a diagram, especially to make a schematic or technical drawing that shows how it works or is constructed

Examples

to diagram a process
After reviewing the process, we diagrammed each step to identify potential improvements.
She will diagram the molecular structure for the class tomorrow.
The engineer diagrammed the new circuit on the whiteboard.

Related Terms

  • synonym – illustrate: to make something clear by drawing or explaining with pictures
