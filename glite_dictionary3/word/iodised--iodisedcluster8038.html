iodised

adjective
treated or mixed with iodine
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.əˌdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ə.daɪzd / (BrE)
(of a substance) treated or mixed with iodine, especially to prevent iodine deficiency.

Examples

iodised salt
Most table salt today is iodised to help prevent thyroid problems in the population.
She checked the label to confirm the product was iodised before purchasing it.
The government initiated a program to provide iodised salt in rural areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym – iodized: American spelling: treated or mixed with iodine
  • antonym – plain: not containing added iodine
