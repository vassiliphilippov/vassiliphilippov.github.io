hyperbetalipoproteinemia

noun
genetic disorder with high beta-lipoproteins and cholesterol
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ.pɚˌbiː.tə.lɪp.oʊˌproʊ.tiː.niːˈmiː.ə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.pəˌbiː.tə.lɪp.əʊˌprəʊ.tiː.niːˈmiː.ə / (BrE)
a genetic disorder characterized by abnormally high levels of beta-lipoproteins and cholesterol in the blood, often leading to early atherosclerosis
