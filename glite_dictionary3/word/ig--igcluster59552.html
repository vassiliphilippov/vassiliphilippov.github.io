Ig

noun
antibody protein (immunology abbreviation)
Pronunciation: / aɪ dʒi / (AmE), / aɪ dʒi / (BrE)
A class of proteins produced by lymph tissue in vertebrates, functioning as antibodies in the immune response; short for immunoglobulin.
