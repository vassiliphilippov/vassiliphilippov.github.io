sufferable

adjective
able to be endured
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌf.ɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈsʌf.ər.ə.bəl / (BrE)
Able to be endured, tolerated, or put up with, though unpleasant.

Examples

sufferable pain
She found the pain sufferable after taking the medication prescribed by her doctor.
The harsh winters in the region are barely sufferable for those unaccustomed to the cold.
While the noise was annoying, it was still sufferable compared to the previous construction work.

Related Terms

  • antonym – insufferable: impossible to endure; intolerable
