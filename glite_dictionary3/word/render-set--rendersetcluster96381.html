to render-set

verb
plaster a wall with two coats
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛndɚ ˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈrɛndə ˌsɛt / (BrE)
to cover a surface, especially a wall, with two coats of plaster for a smooth finish

Examples

to render-set a wall
Before painting, it is important to render-set any rough interior surfaces.
The builders render-set the exterior walls to protect them from the weather.
They spent the weekend render-setting the new extension to achieve a flawless finish.
