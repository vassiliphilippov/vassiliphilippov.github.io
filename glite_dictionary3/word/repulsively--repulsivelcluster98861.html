repulsively

adverb
in an offensive or disgusting way
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌlsɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌlsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that causes disgust or strong dislike; offensively or abhorrently.

Examples

to speak repulsively
He spoke repulsively about his coworkers, making everyone uncomfortable.
Some villains are written to behave repulsively to highlight their evil nature.
The kitchen was so dirty and repulsively smelly that we refused to eat there.

Related Terms

  • antonym – attractively: in a way that is pleasing or attractive
  • synonym – disgustingly: in a way that causes disgust
