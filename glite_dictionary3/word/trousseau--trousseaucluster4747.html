trousseau

noun
bride's clothes and linens for marriage
Pronunciation: / truːˈsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtruːsəʊ / (BrE)
The clothes, accessories, and linens collected by a bride for her marriage.

Examples

trousseau for a bride
Her mother helped her pack the trousseau before the wedding ceremony.
The antique chest was filled with her grandmother’s trousseau.
The bride spent months preparing her trousseau with beautiful dresses and fine linens.
