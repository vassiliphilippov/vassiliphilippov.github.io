to coalesce

verb
to fuse or unite into one whole
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊəˈlɛs /; / ˌkoʊ.əˈlɛs / (AmE), / ˌkəʊəˈlɛs /; / ˌkəʊ.əˈlɛs / (BrE)
