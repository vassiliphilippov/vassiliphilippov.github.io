Al Qanoon

proper noun
Pakistani extremist militant coalition group (2002-)
Pronunciation: / æl kɑˈnuːn / (AmE), / æl kɑˈnuːn / (BrE)
A terrorist organization formed in Pakistan in 2002 as a coalition of extremist militant groups including Lashkar-e-Taiba, Lashkar-e-Jhangvi, Jaish-e-Muhammad, and elements of al-Qaeda.
