alpaca

noun
South American mammal valued for its wool
Pronunciation: / ælˈpækə / (AmE), / ælˈpækə / (BrE)
a domesticated South American mammal with long silky fleece, related to the llama and valued for its wool

Examples

wild alpaca herds
Many travelers visit Peru to see wild alpaca herds grazing in the Andes.
She wore a sweater made from pure alpaca.
The farm raises alpacas for their luxurious wool.
