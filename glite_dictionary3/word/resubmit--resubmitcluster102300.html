to resubmit

verb
submit something again
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.səˈmɪt / (AmE), / ˌriː.səˈmɪt / (BrE)
to submit (something) again, especially after correction or revision, or when previous submission failed or was rejected

Examples

to resubmit a form
After revising the report, she decided to resubmit it to her manager for approval.
If your application is incomplete, you will need to resubmit it with all the required documents.
The system allows users to resubmit their forms in case of technical errors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – submit: to present or propose something for consideration or judgment
