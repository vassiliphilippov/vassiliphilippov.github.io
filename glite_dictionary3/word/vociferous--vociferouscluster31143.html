vociferous

adjective
loud and forceful, especially in protest
Pronunciation: / voʊˈsɪfərəs / (AmE), / vəʊˈsɪfərəs / (BrE)
expressing opinions or feelings loudly and forcefully; characterized by strong vocal outcry or protest

Examples

vociferous protest
A vociferous opponent of the new policy spoke for nearly an hour at the meeting.
Despite his vociferous objections, the decision had already been made.
The protesters grew increasingly vociferous as their demands were ignored by officials.
