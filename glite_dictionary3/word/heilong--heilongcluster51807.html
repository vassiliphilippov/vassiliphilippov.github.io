Heilong

proper noun
a river between China and Russia
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌlɒŋ / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌlɒŋ / (BrE)
a river in Asia forming part of the border between China and Russia, flowing into the Sea of Okhotsk
