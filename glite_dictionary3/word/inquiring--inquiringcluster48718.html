inquiring

adjective
eager to learn or know
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkwaɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
showing an interest in learning new things or in finding out information; given to inquiry.

Examples

inquiring mind
During the interview, her inquiring questions impressed the panel.
His inquiring nature led him to travel to distant countries in search of knowledge.
She has an inquiring mind that always wants to understand how things work.
