regretful

adjective
feeling or expressing regret
Pronunciation: / rɪˈɡrɛtfəl / (AmE), / rɪˈɡrɛtfʊl / (BrE)
feeling or showing regret or sorrow for something that has happened or been done, especially when wishing it had been different.

Examples

regretful apology
He sounded regretful when he apologized for missing the meeting.
Looking back, he felt regretful about his harsh words.
She gave him a regretful smile before leaving the party early.

Related Terms

  • synonym – remorseful: feeling deep regret and guilt for a wrongdoing
  • antonym – unrepentant: not feeling or showing regret about one's actions or attitudes
