invisibility

noun
the state of being invisible
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvɪzəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪnˌvɪzəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being impossible to see or not visible to the eye.

Examples

social invisibility
For many years, the plight of the homeless was treated with invisibility by society.
The cloak gave her the gift of invisibility, letting her walk unseen among her enemies.
The wizard used a spell to grant himself invisibility during the duel.

Related Terms

  • synonym – transparency: the quality of allowing light to pass through so that objects behind can be seen clearly
  • antonym – visibility: the state of being able to be seen
