attendee

noun
person attending an event or meeting
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛnˌdi / (AmE), / əˈtɛndi / (BrE)
a person who is present at and participates in an event, meeting, or gathering

Examples

conference attendee
As an attendee, you will have access to exclusive workshops and networking events.
Each attendee received a welcome package at the conference registration desk.
The seminar was a success, drawing over a hundred attendees from the region.
