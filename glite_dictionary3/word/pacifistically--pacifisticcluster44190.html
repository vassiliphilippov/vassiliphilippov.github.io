pacifistically

adverb
in a pacifistic manner
Pronunciation: / ˌpæsɪˈfɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpæsɪˈfɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by or supporting pacifism; in a pacifistic way.

Examples

to speak pacifistically
He argued pacifistically for solutions to the conflict.
She always responded pacifistically to even the most heated criticism.
The protest was organized pacifistically, with no intention of confrontation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pacifistic: opposed to war or violence; favoring peaceful solutions
