bouffant

noun
puffy, rounded women's hairstyle
Pronunciation: / buːˈfɑnt / (AmE), / buːˈfɒnt / (BrE)
A woman's hairstyle in which the hair is styled to give a rounded, puffy appearance, often with backcombing or teasing for volume.

Examples

bouffant hairstyle
A stylist created a retro bouffant for the fashion show.
She wore her hair in a dramatic bouffant for the wedding.
The actress became famous for her signature bouffant in the 1960s.
