hinny

noun
offspring of a male horse and a female donkey
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪni / (AmE), / ˈhɪni / (BrE)
A hybrid animal that is the offspring of a male horse and a female donkey (jenny); usually sterile.

Examples

hinny offspring animal
A hinny has a gentler disposition than a mule.
Breeding a male horse with a female donkey results in a hinny.
The farmer explained the differences between a hinny and a mule to the children.
