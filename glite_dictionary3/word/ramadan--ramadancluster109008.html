Ramadan

noun
Muslim month of fasting
Pronunciation: / ˈræməˌdɑn / (AmE), / ˈræmədæn / (BrE)
the ninth month of the Islamic calendar, when Muslims fast from sunrise to sunset and focus on prayer and reflection
