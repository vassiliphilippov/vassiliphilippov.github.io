reentrant

adjective
pointing inward (angle, surface)
Pronunciation: / riˈæntrənt / (AmE), / riˈæntrənt / (BrE)
(of an angle or surface) pointing inward or projecting inward, especially forming an internal angle of less than 180 degrees.

Examples

reentrant corner shape
In geometry, some polygons have reentrant corners that indent towards the interior.
The architect designed a courtyard with several reentrant angles to create private spaces.
The building's reentrant façade gave it a unique and jagged appearance.
