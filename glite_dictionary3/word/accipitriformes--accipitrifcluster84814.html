Accipitriformes

noun
order of birds including hawks and eagles
Pronunciation: / ækˌsɪpɪtrɪˈfɔːrmiːz /; / ækˌsɪpɪtrəˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / ækˌsɪpɪtrɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
an order of birds that includes most diurnal birds of prey, such as hawks, eagles, vultures, and kites; sometimes considered an alternative name for Falconiformes
