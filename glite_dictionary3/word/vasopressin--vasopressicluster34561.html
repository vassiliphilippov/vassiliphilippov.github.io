vasopressin

noun
hormone regulating blood pressure and urine output
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪsoʊˈprɛsɪn /; / ccve6aso8ac8pr5bs6an / (AmE), / ˌveɪsəˈprɛsɪn /; / ccve6as59c8pr5bs6an / (BrE)
