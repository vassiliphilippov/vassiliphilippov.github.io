machinist

noun
person skilled with machine tools
Pronunciation: / məˈʃinɪst / (AmE), / məˈʃiːnɪst / (BrE)
a person skilled in operating and maintaining machine tools, especially in a workshop or factory

Examples

factory machinist
A skilled machinist can turn a block of metal into a precision part.
After years of training, he became a highly respected machinist in the factory.
The machinist carefully calibrated the lathe before beginning the new project.
