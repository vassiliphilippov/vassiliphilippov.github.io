consolidation

noun
the act of uniting things into one
Pronunciation: / kənˌsɑː.lɪˈdeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / kənˌsɒl.ɪˈdeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of combining two or more things into a single, more effective or coherent whole

Examples

consolidation of companies
Following the consolidation of the schools, students shared new resources and facilities.
His role was crucial in the consolidation of the two rival companies.
The consolidation of several departments led to a more streamlined organization.

Related Terms

  • antonym – division: the act of separating something into parts
  • synonym – merger: the combination of two things, especially companies, into one
  • synonym – unification: the process of making or becoming a single unit
