intimidation

noun
the feeling of being intimidated
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌtɪməˈdeɪʃən /; / ɪnˌtɪmɪˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɪmɪˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
the feeling of being afraid, timid, or discouraged because of someone else's actions, power, or status

Examples
