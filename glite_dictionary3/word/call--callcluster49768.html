to call

verb
(of a ship/bus/train) stop at a place
Pronunciation: / kɔl / (AmE), / kɔːl / (BrE)
(of a ship, bus, or train) to stop at a place, especially as part of a scheduled route.

Examples

the train calls at stations
Our train will call at several small towns before reaching the city.
The cruise ship will call at Naples on its way to Greece.
The ferry does not call at this island during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bypass: to go past or skip a place instead of stopping
  • synonym – stop: to cease movement or progress, or to make a halt
