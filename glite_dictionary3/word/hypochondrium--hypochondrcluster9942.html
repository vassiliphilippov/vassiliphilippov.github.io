hypochondrium

noun
upper region of the abdomen below the ribs
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ.poʊˈkɑn.dri.əm / (AmE), / ˌhɪ.pəˈkɒn.dri.əm / (BrE)
the upper part of the abdomen on either side, just below the lowest ribs and beside the epigastrium

Examples

right hypochondrium pain
Pain in the right hypochondrium often indicates a problem with the gallbladder.
The doctor pressed gently on his hypochondrium to check for any tenderness.
The left hypochondrium contains parts of the stomach and the spleen.
