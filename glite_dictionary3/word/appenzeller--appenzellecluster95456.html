Appenzeller

noun
Swiss mountain dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˌæpənˈzɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˌapənˈzɛlə / (BrE)
A medium-sized breed of Swiss dog, one of the four native Swiss mountain dog breeds, known for its herding and guarding abilities.

Examples

Appenzeller mountain dog
Many Swiss families value the Appenzeller for its loyalty and alertness.
She adopted an Appenzeller to help herd her cattle in the countryside.
The Appenzeller excels as a farm dog due to its intelligence and energy.
