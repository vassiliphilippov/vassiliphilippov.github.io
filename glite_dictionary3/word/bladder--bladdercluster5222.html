bladder

noun
a sac filled with air or gas
Pronunciation: / ˈblædɚ / (AmE), / ˈblædə / (BrE)
a thin, flexible sac, typically made of animal skin, rubber, or synthetic material, that can be filled with air or gas

Examples

inflatable bladder
Many fish have a swim bladder that helps them control buoyancy.
The old-fashioned whoopee cushion contains a simple air bladder that makes a funny noise.
The rubber bladder inside the football keeps it inflated.

  • synonym – balloon: a flexible bag that can be inflated with air or gas
