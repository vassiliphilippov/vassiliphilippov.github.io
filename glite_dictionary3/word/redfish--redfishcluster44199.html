redfish

noun
male salmon after spawning
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɛd.fɪʃ / (BrE)
A male salmon that has recently spawned, typically exhibiting a reddish coloration.

Examples

spawning redfish salmon
After spawning, the male salmon turns into a redfish and can be easily spotted in the river.
Fishermen say it's rare to catch a redfish at this time of year.
The biologist identified the brightly colored redfish swimming upstream.
