ribald

adjective
coarse or vulgar humor
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪb.əld / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.əld / (BrE)
referring to speech, behavior, or humor that is coarse, vulgar, or indecent, especially in a humorous way

Examples

ribald jokes
His ribald jokes had everyone in the room laughing and blushing at the same time.
She whispered a ribald comment that made her friends burst out laughing.
The comedian's ribald humor wasn't suitable for a family audience, but many adults enjoyed it.
