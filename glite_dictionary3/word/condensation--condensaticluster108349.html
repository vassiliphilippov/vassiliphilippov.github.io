condensation

noun
shortened version of a written work
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.dənˈseɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dɛnˈseɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a shortened version of a written work, created by omitting less important details while retaining the main ideas

Examples

condensation of text
His essay is a brilliant condensation of the original research paper, focusing on the main findings.
She read only the condensation of the lengthy report to save time.
The magazine published a condensation of the bestselling novel for its monthly issue.
