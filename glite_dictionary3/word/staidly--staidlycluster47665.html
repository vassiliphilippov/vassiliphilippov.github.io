staidly

adverb
in a serious and respectable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪdli / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdli / (BrE)
In a serious, quiet, and respectable manner; not showing excitement or emotion.

Examples

to speak staidly
He nodded staidly and waited his turn to speak.
She staidly declined the invitation to the wild party.
They handled the ceremonial duty staidly, showing great composure.
