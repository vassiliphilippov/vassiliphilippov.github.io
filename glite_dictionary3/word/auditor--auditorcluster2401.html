auditor

noun
person who examines financial accounts
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪtə / (BrE)
a qualified person who officially examines and verifies the financial accounts and records of an organization

Examples

auditor checks accounts
