vocaliser

noun
device or software that converts text to speech
Pronunciation: / \u02c8vo\u028a.k\u0259.la\u026a.z\u025a / (AmE), / \u02c8v\u0259\u028a.k\u0259.la\u026a.z\u0259 / (BrE)
a device or computer program that converts written text or digital input into spoken words (text-to-speech).
