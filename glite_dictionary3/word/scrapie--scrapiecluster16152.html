scrapie

noun
fatal nervous disease of sheep
Pronunciation: / ˈskreɪpi / (AmE), / ˈskreɪpi / (BrE)
A fatal disease affecting sheep, marked by chronic itching, loss of muscular control, and progressive degeneration of the central nervous system.

Examples

scrapie in sheep
Researchers are studying new ways to prevent scrapie from spreading in sheep populations.
Symptoms of scrapie include intense itching and difficulties with movement.
The farmer was devastated when several sheep in his flock were diagnosed with scrapie.
