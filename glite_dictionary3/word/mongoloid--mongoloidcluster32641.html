Mongoloid

adjective
dated term for people of central/eastern Asian origin
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑŋɡəˌlɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋɡəlɔɪd / (BrE)
dated, offensive: relating to a former anthropological classification comprising peoples of central and eastern Asia and certain other groups, now considered inappropriate and obsolete.

Examples

Mongoloid racial group
Early anthropological texts used the word Mongoloid to describe various Asian populations.
Researchers have abandoned the concept of Mongoloid as it lacks scientific basis.
The term Mongoloid is now considered offensive in reference to racial classification.
