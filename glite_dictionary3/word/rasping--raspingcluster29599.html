rasping

adjective
unpleasantly harsh or grating in sound
Pronunciation: / ˈræspɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɑːspɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound) unpleasantly harsh or grating.

Examples

rasping voice
His rasping voice made the announcement even more alarming.
She was disturbed by the rasping noise of metal against metal.
The old man's cough was deep and rasping, echoing through the silent room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – grating: having a harsh and unpleasant sound
  • antonym – smooth: (of a sound) pleasant and free from harshness or rough edges
