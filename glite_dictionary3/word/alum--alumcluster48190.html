alum

noun
a white crystalline double sulfate of aluminum
Pronunciation: / ˈæləm / (AmE), / ˈaləm / (BrE)
a white or colorless crystalline chemical (a double sulfate of aluminum and another element, such as potassium or ammonium), used in dyeing, pickling, and as an astringent
