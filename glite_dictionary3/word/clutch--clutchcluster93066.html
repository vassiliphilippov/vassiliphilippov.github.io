clutch

noun
small, strapless handbag
Pronunciation: / klʌtʃ / (AmE), / klʌtʃ / (BrE)
A small, flat, strapless women's purse designed to be carried in the hand.

Examples

clutch bag
A stylish clutch is perfect for formal events where a larger bag would be cumbersome.
She chose a black clutch to complement her evening dress.
The bride carried a white satin clutch with her bouquet.
