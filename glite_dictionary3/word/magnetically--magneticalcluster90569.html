magnetically

adverb
in a way suggestive of magnetism or strong attraction
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that relates to or resembles magnetism, especially as though having an irresistible attraction.

Examples

magnetically attract people
Iron filings lined up magnetically along the pattern of the field.
She smiled magnetically, captivating everyone in the room.
The two artists were magnetically drawn to each other's creative energy.
