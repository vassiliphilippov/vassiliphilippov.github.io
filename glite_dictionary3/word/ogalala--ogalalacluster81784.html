Ogalala

noun
Siouan language of the Oglala people
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊɡəˈlɑːlə / (AmE), / ˌəʊɡəˈlɑːlə / (BrE)
a Siouan language spoken by the Oglala people, a subgroup of the Lakota

Examples

Ogalala language
Few people today are fluent speakers of Ogalala due to the decline in use among the younger generation.
Linguists have documented Ogalala as part of their efforts to revitalize endangered indigenous languages.
The Ogalala language preserves many cultural stories and traditions unique to the Oglala tribe.
