unfilled

adjective
not yet completed or satisfied
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪld / (BrE)
(of orders, positions, or requests) not yet completed, satisfied, or provided; remaining outstanding.

Examples

unfilled job position
Due to the labor shortage, many job openings remain unfilled this year.
Her prescription is currently unfilled at the pharmacy due to a supply delay.
There are still several unfilled orders waiting to be processed in the system.
