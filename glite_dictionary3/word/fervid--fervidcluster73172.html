fervid

adjective
extremely hot; burning (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝː.vɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.vɪd / (BrE)
(archaic) Extremely hot, burning, or glowing.

Examples

The travelers sought shelter from the fervid sun that blazed overhead.
