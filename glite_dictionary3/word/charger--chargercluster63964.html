charger

noun
device for recharging batteries
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrdʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a device used for supplying electric power to recharge a battery, especially in electronic devices

Examples

phone charger
I forgot my charger at home and now my phone is dead.
Many hotels now provide USB chargers in their rooms for guests.
She bought a new charger for her laptop after the old one broke.
