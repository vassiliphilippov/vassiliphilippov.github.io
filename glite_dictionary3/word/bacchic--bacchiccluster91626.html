bacchic

adjective
relating to drunken revelry
Pronunciation: / ˈbæk.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈbak.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to riotously drunken revelry or festivities, especially those reminiscent of Bacchus, the Roman god of wine
