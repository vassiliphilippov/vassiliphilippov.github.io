interpellation

noun
formal demand for official explanation in parliament
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˌpɛˈleɪʃən /; / ˌɪntərpəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌpɛˈleɪʃən /; / ˌɪntəpəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a formal act of questioning or interrupting, especially in a parliament or official meeting, to demand an explanation or raise an objection
