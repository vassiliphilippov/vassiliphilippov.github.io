Asclepiadaceae

noun
family of herbs and shrubs with milky sap
Pronunciation: / əˌskliːpiəˈdeɪsi.iː /; / əˌskliːpiəˈdeɪsiːˌiː / (AmE), / əˌskliːpɪəˈdeɪsɪ.iː /; / əˌskliːpɪəˈdeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of tropical and subtropical herbs and shrubs, most with milky sap, formerly its own family but now usually included in Apocynaceae
