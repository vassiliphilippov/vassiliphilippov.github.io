vapid

adjective
lacking liveliness or interest
Pronunciation: / ˈvæpɪd / (AmE), / ˈvapɪd / (BrE)
lacking interest, liveliness, or spirit; dull or insipid.

Examples

vapid conversation
Many critics found the novel vapid, with little to engage the reader.
She gave a vapid smile, betraying her lack of genuine interest.
The movie was so vapid that I struggled to stay awake until the end.
