hash mark

noun
service insignia on a uniform
Pronunciation: / hæʃ mɑrk / (AmE), / hæʃ mɑːk / (BrE)
a badge or insignia, often in the form of a short line or stripe, worn on a uniform to represent years of service, typically in the military or police

Examples

hash mark on a uniform
After twenty years, he wore four hash marks on his sleeve.
Each hash mark on the uniform represents three years of service.
The officer proudly pointed to the hash marks that showed his years of dedication.
