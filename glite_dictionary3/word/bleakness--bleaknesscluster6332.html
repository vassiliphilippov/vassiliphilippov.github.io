bleakness

noun
the state of being bleak or desolate
Pronunciation: / ˈbliːknəs / (AmE), / ˈbliːknɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being bleak, especially a cold, empty, and desolate atmosphere or feeling

Examples

winter bleakness
He tried to lighten the bleakness of their situation with a joke.
The bleakness of the winter landscape made her feel lonely.
There was a palpable bleakness in the abandoned factory.
