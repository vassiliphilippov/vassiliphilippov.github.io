Turfan

noun
ancient Tocharian dialect of Turpan region
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrˌfæn / (AmE), / ˈtɜːfæn / (BrE)
an extinct dialect of the Tocharian language, formerly spoken in the region around Qarašähär and Turpan in Central Asia
