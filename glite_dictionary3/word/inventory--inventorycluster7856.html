inventory

noun
itemized list of goods or supplies on hand
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.vənˌtɔɹ.i /; / ˈɪnvənˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈɪn.vən.tə.ri /; / ˈɪnvəntri /; / ˈɪn.vən.tər.i / (BrE)
all the goods, materials, or items a business has, or the complete list of them (often with details of quantity and value)
