tablet

noun
portable touchscreen computer
Pronunciation: / ˈtæb.lət / (AmE), / ˈtæb.lɪt / (BrE)
a small, portable computer that has a touchscreen and is operated primarily by touch or a stylus, rather than a physical keyboard

Examples

use a tablet computer
Many students now bring a tablet to class instead of a laptop.
She uses her tablet to read books and watch movies while traveling.
The new tablet features a larger display and improved battery life.

Related Terms

  • synonym – laptop: a portable computer with a keyboard and screen attached in a clamshell design
