wallpaper

noun
decorative paper for covering walls
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔlˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a paper, often with a decorative pattern, used to cover and decorate the interior walls of a room or building

Examples

wallpaper with flowers
She chose a floral wallpaper pattern for the bedroom walls.
The old wallpaper was peeling off after years of humidity.
We decided to put up new wallpaper in the living room to brighten it up.

Related Terms

  • synonym – paint: colored liquid applied to walls for decoration or protection
  • synonym – paneling: decorative wooden or other material panels for interior walls
