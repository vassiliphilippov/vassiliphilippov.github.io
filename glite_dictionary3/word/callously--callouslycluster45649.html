callously

adverb
in a cruel or unfeeling way
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.əs.li / (AmE), / ˈkæl.əs.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a cruel disregard for others' feelings; unfeelingly or heartlessly.

Examples

to callously ignore pain
Developers callously destroyed the historic building without consulting the local community.
She callously ignored his plea for help, turning her back as he struggled.
The dictator callously dismissed the suffering of his people during the speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym – callous: showing or having an insensitive and cruel disregard for others
  • antonym – compassionately: in a way that shows sympathy and concern for others
