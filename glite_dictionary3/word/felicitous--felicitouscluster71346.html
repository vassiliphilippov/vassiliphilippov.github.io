felicitous

adjective
marked by good fortune
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪsɪtəs / (AmE), / fəˈlɪsɪtəs / (BrE)
marked by good fortune; resulting in or characterized by happiness and success

Examples

felicitous outcome
She considered herself to have led a felicitous life, full of unexpected joys.
Thanks to some felicitous investments, he retired earlier than he expected.
Their meeting at the café was a felicitous coincidence that changed everything.
