retroversion

noun
backward tilting of a body part
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊˈvɜrʒən / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˈvɜːʃən / (BrE)
the backward turning or tilting of an organ or body part, especially the uterus

Examples

retroversion of the uterus
Doctors explained that retroversion can sometimes cause discomfort but is often harmless.
In some cases, retroversion of the pelvic organs may go unnoticed for years.
The ultrasound revealed a retroversion of the uterus that required careful monitoring.

  • antonym – anteversion: forward tilting of an organ or body part
