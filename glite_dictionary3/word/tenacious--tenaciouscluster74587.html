tenacious

adjective
having a strong memory
Pronunciation: / təˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / tɪˈneɪʃəs / (BrE)
Having a strong ability to remember things; highly retentive.

Examples

tenacious memory
A detective needs a tenacious memory to keep track of countless facts and clues.
Her tenacious memory allowed her to recall details that everyone else had forgotten.
With his tenacious mind, he mastered several languages with ease.
