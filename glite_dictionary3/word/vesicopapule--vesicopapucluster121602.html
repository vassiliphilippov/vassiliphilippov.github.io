vesicopapule

noun
skin lesion turning into a blister
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛsɪkoʊˈpæpjul / (AmE), / ˌvɛsɪkəʊˈpæpjuːl / (BrE)
A small, raised skin lesion (papule) that develops into a blister filled with fluid.

Examples

itchy vesicopapule
A vesicopapule may indicate an early stage of certain viral or allergic skin conditions.
After a day, the initial papule evolved into a vesicopapule, becoming more pronounced and fluid-filled.
The patient presented with several vesicopapules on her forearm after contact with the plant.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blister: a small bubble on the skin filled with serum
  • synonym – papule: a small, raised, solid pimple or swelling on the skin
