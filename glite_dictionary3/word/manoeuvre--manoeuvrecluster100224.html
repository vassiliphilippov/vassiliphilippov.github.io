manoeuvre

noun
a skilful or careful movement
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːvər / (AmE), / məˈnuːvə / (BrE)
a movement or series of moves that requires skill and care, often performed deliberately and with dexterity

Examples

careful manoeuvre
Her quick manoeuvre prevented the glass from falling off the table.
It took a tricky manoeuvre to park the car in such a tight space.
The pilot executed a difficult manoeuvre during the air show.
