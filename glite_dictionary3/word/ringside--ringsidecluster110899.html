ringside

noun
seats next to a boxing or wrestling ring
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋsʌɪd / (BrE)
the area or seats immediately next to a boxing or wrestling ring, offering an unobstructed view of the action

Examples

ringside seat at boxing
Journalists watched from ringside as the tension in the ring mounted.
She managed to get a ringside seat for the championship fight.
The crowd cheered loudly from ringside during the intense final match.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ringside seat: a seat in the first row beside a ring; best view of a match
