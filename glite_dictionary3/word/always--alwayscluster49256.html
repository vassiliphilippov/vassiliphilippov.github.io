always

adverb
at any time; in any case
Pronunciation: / ˈɔlˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈɔːl.weɪz / (BrE)
At any time or in any event; used to suggest a possible action or to express an option.

Examples

always ask for help
If you can't solve the problem, you can always ask for help.
When in doubt, you should always prioritize safety over speed.
You always have the option to change your mind later.

  • antonym – never: at no time; not ever
