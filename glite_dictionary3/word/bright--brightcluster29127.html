bright

adjective
promising or hopeful (of future/prospects)
Pronunciation: / braɪt / (AmE), / braɪt / (BrE)
(of the future, a prospect, or a period) likely to be successful, hopeful, or impressive

The ceremony was marked by bright colors and elaborate costumes.
