readiness

noun
prompt willingness or eagerness
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛd.i.nəs /; / ˈrɛdinəs / (AmE), / ˈrɛd.i.nɪs /; / ˈrɛdinɪs / (BrE)
willingness or preparedness to do something; being ready and eager for action
