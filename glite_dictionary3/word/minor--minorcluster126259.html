to minor

verb
to study as a secondary subject in college
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
to study a subject as a secondary focus at a college or university, in addition to a major subject

Examples

to minor in biology
After choosing to minor in French, she spent a semester studying in Paris.
He plans to minor in environmental science while majoring in engineering.
Many students minor in business to complement their main field of study.
