whisper

noun
soft, rustling or faint noise
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspər / (AmE), / ˈwɪspə / (BrE)
a soft, rustling or faint noise, like the sound of silk or leaves moving in the wind

Examples

a whisper of wind
A faint whisper of wind brushed across the lake.
The whisper of leaves in the evening quieted his mind.
There was a whisper of silk as she entered the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rustle: a soft, muffled crackling sound like dry leaves or paper
