notorious

adjective
famous for something bad
Pronunciation: / noʊˈtɔːriəs / (AmE), / nəˈtɔːriəs / (BrE)
famous or well known for something bad or negative; having a bad reputation

Examples

notorious criminal
He is a notorious con artist who deceived hundreds of people.
The city became notorious for its high crime rate in the 1990s.
The restaurant is notorious for its terrible service and rude staff.
