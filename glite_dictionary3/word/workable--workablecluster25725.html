workable

adjective
capable of being done or put into practice
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be done or put into practice successfully with the means or conditions available

We need to develop a workable schedule that fits everyone's needs.
