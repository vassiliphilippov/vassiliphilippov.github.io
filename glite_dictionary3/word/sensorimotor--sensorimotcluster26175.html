sensorimotor

adjective
involving both sensory and motor processes
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛnsəriˈmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˌsensəriˈməʊtə / (BrE)
relating to the combined functions or interaction of the sensory and motor processes of an organism

Examples

sensorimotor skills development
Damage to sensorimotor pathways can affect both movement and sense perception.
Infants go through a sensorimotor stage where they learn by interacting with their environment.
The therapy focused on improving the child's sensorimotor coordination.
