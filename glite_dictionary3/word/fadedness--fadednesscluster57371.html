fadedness

noun
the state of being faded
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈfeɪdɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having lost some of its original color or intensity.

Examples

the fadedness of fabric
Over time, the fadedness of the curtains became more noticeable in the sunlight.
She preferred the fadedness of vintage clothing to brand-new items.
The fadedness of the old photograph made the details hard to see.
