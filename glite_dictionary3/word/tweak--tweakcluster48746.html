to tweak

verb
make small adjustments
Pronunciation: / twiːk / (AmE), / twiːk / (BrE)
to make small adjustments or improvements to something, often to achieve the best result

Examples

to tweak a recipe
She decided to tweak the recipe by adding a pinch of cinnamon.
The sound engineer spent an hour tweaking the microphone settings for the perfect tone.
You may need to tweak your resume before you send out more applications.
