hard

adjective
excessively indulging, especially in alcohol
Pronunciation: / hɑrd / (AmE), / hɑːd / (BrE)
(of a person) tending to indulge excessively in bodily appetites, especially drinking alcohol to excess.

hard drinker

His uncle was known as a hard drinker throughout the small town.
