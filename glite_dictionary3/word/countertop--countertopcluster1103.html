countertop

noun
flat surface of a counter
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntɚˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌtɒp / (BrE)
the flat working surface on top of a counter, especially in a kitchen or bathroom

Examples

kitchen countertop
Please don't place hot pans directly on the countertop.
She cleaned the countertop after preparing dinner.
The kitchen countertop was made of polished granite.
