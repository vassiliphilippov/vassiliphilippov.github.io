Gentianopsis thermalis

noun
Rocky Mountain blue-flowered fringed gentian
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛn(t)ʃi.əˈnɑp.sɪs θərˈmeɪ.lɪs / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛn(t)ʃɪ.əˈnɒp.sɪs θəˈmɑː.lɪs / (BrE)
