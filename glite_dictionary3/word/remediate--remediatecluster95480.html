to remediate

verb
to correct or fix a problem
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmiːdieɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈmiːdieɪt / (BrE)
to correct or improve something that is wrong or deficient, especially in an official, systematic, or technical way

Examples

to remediate a problem
New software was installed to remediate the system's security vulnerabilities.
The company took action to remediate the safety hazards identified during the inspection.
The school is working to remediate gaps in students' math skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rectify: to put right; correct
  • synonym – remedy: to set right (a wrong), especially by making improvements
