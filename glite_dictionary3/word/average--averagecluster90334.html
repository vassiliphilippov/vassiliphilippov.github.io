average

noun
the mean or typical value in data
Pronunciation: / ˈæv.ɚ.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæv.ər.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
a number expressing the central or typical value in a set of data, calculated by dividing the total of all values by their number; the mean

Examples

average of numbers
The city's temperature average in July is 28°C.
The class average on the exam was higher than expected.
To find the average, add up all the scores and divide by the number of students.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mean: the sum of values divided by their number; the arithmetic mean
  • antonym – median: the value separating the higher half from the lower half of a data set
  • antonym – mode: the value that appears most frequently in a data set
