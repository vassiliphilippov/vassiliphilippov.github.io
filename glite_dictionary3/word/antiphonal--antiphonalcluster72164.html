antiphonal

adjective
characterized by alternating responses
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪf.ə.nəl / (AmE), / ænˈtɪf.ə.nəl / (BrE)
describing music, singing, or speech with alternating responses, especially between two groups or in church services
