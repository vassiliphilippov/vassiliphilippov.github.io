kaffir

noun
offensive slur for Black African (esp. South Africa)
Pronunciation: / ˈkæfər / (AmE), / ˈkæfə / (BrE)
An extremely offensive and insulting term for a Black African, especially used in South Africa; now regarded as a racial slur and not used in polite speech or writing.

Examples

He was shocked to hear someone use the word kaffir in a conversation.
