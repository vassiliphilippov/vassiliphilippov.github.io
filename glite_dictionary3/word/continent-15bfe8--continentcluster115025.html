continent

noun
large landmass on Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.t̬ɪ.nənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɪ.nənt / (BrE)
one of the main continuous expanses of land on the earth, such as Africa, Asia, or Europe.

Examples

African continent
Africa is a continent rich in diverse cultures and ecosystems.
Australia is both a country and a continent.
The explorers dreamed of traveling across the entire continent by horseback.
