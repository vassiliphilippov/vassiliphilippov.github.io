Aleve

noun
brand of pain-relief drug (naproxen)
Pronunciation: / əˈliv / (AmE), / əˈliːv / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for naproxen, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain and reduce inflammation.

Examples

take Aleve for pain
I took Aleve to help with my headache and it started working within an hour.
The doctor recommended Aleve for managing my joint pain after I sprained my ankle.
You should not mix Aleve with other anti-inflammatory medications without your pharmacist's advice.
