lotto

noun
a game of chance with numbered cards and balls
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɒtəʊ / (BrE)
a game of chance in which numbered balls are drawn at random and players mark the corresponding numbers on their cards, hoping to complete a winning pattern

Examples

play lotto weekly
My grandparents play lotto every Saturday, hoping to win the jackpot.
She marked off the numbers on her lotto card as they were called out.
The charity organized a lotto night to raise funds for their new project.

  • synonym – bingo: a similar game where matching numbers are marked on cards
