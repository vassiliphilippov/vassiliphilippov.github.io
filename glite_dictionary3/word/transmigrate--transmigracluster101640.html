to transmigrate

verb
move and settle in another country or region
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmaɪɡreɪt / (AmE), / tranzˈmaɪɡreɪt / (BrE)
to move from one country or region to another and settle there

Examples

to transmigrate to another country
After decades of unrest, the family decided to transmigrate to a new continent in search of peace.
Several ancient communities would transmigrate each generation, adapting to different lands as needed.
The monks chose to transmigrate to a distant region to establish their new monastery.
