Mitella diphylla

noun
a woodland plant of northeastern North America
Pronunciation: / maɪˈtɛlə dɪˈfɪlə / (AmE), / mɪˈtɛlə dɪˈfɪlə / (BrE)
A North American woodland plant of the saxifrage family, usually with two opposite leaves on upright stems ending in racemes of small white flowers; commonly known as miterwort.
