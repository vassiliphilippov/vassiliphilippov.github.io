per centum

noun
proportion per hundred; percent
Pronunciation: / pɚ ˈsɛn.təm / (AmE), / pə ˈsɛn.təm / (BrE)
A proportion or rate in relation to a whole, especially one calculated per hundred; percent.

Examples

per centum rate
He paid a commission of ten per centum on the sale.
The interest was calculated at six per centum per annum.
The law capped increases to five per centum annually.

Related Terms

  • synonym – percent: one part in every hundred
