musculus pectoralis

noun
large chest muscle
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌs.kjʊ.ləs ˌpɛk.təˈreɪ.lɪs / (AmE), / ˌmʌs.kjʊ.ləs ˌpɛk.təˈreɪ.lɪs / (BrE)
either of two large muscles located on the chest, connecting the front walls of the chest with the bones of the upper arm and shoulder

Examples

musculus pectoralis muscle group
