coxa

noun
the hip joint (femur-acetabulum connection)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːk.sə / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.sə / (BrE)
the ball-and-socket joint formed between the head of the femur and the acetabulum in the hip region
