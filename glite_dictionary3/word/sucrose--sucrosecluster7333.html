sucrose

noun
a common type of sugar; sweetening agent
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːkˌroʊs / (AmE), / ˈsjuːkrəʊs / (BrE)
a type of sugar found in many plants and commonly used as a sweetening agent; chemically, a disaccharide composed of glucose and fructose
