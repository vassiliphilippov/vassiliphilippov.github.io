nihilism

noun
doctrine advocating destruction of systems
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪ.əˌlɪ.zəm /; / ˈnaɪ.əˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈniː.ɪˌlɪ.zəm /; / ˈnɪɪ.lɪ.zəm /; / ˈnɪ.ɪ.lɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the belief that rejects all established authority, values, and meaning in life; the idea that nothing has real existence or purpose
