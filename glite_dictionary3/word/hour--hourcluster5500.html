hour

noun
sixty-minute period of time
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈaʊə / (BrE)
A period of time equal to sixty minutes or one twenty-fourth of a day.

Examples

one hour later
She needs to finish her homework within the next hour.
The movie lasted almost three hours and kept everyone entertained.
