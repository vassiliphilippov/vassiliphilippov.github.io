short

adjective
having little length
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt / (BrE)
measuring a small distance from end to end or not tall in height; not long or not tall

Examples

short hair
He decided to cut his hair short for the summer.
The model wore a short dress that ended above her knees.
The rope was too short to reach the ground.
Among all her siblings, Maria was always the short one in family photos.
Despite being short, he made a big impression on everyone he met.
The short chimney barely reached above the roofline.
