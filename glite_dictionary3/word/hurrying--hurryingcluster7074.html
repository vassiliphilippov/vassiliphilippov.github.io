hurrying

noun
the act of moving quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜːriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʌriɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving or acting quickly, often because of urgency

Examples

hurrying to work
After a morning of hurrying, he finally sat down to rest.
She was hurrying to catch the last train home.
Their hurrying down the hallway caused quite a commotion.
