truancy

noun
absent from school without permission
Pronunciation: / ˈtruː.ən.si / (AmE), / ˈtruː.ən.si / (BrE)
The act or practice of staying away from school without permission.

Examples

truancy from school
Her unexplained truancy led to a meeting with the principal and her parents.
Increased truancy rates prompted the district to hire more attendance officers.
The school takes truancy very seriously and notifies parents when students are absent without a valid excuse.
