Micah

proper noun
Hebrew prophet (8th century BC)
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkə / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkə / (BrE)
A minor Hebrew prophet of the 8th century BC, known for prophesying the destruction and later restoration of Jerusalem and Samaria.
