Vaccaria hispanica

noun
a European flowering plant, also called cowherb
Pronunciation: / vækˈkɛəriə hɪˈspænɪkə / (AmE), / vækˈkeəriə hɪˈspanɪkə / (BrE)
A European annual plant with pale rose-colored flowers, often found in grainfields and sometimes cultivated as an ornamental flower; also known as cowherb or soapwort.
