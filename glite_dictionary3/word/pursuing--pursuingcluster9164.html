pursuing

adjective
chasing or following (someone or something)
Pronunciation: / pərˈsuːɪŋ / (AmE), / pəˈsjuːɪŋ / (BrE)
following someone or something in order to catch, overtake, or accompany them.

Examples

pursuing police car

The pursuing team never gave up hope of catching the leader.
The detective ran after the pursuing suspect through the crowded market.
