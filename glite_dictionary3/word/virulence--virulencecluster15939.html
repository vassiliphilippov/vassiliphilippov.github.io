virulence

noun
extreme hostility or harmfulness
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪr.jə.ləns / (AmE), / ˈvɪə.rʊ.ləns / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely hostile or harmful in attitude, expression, or action

Examples

virulence of criticism
Online debates can quickly take on a tone of virulence if not moderated.
She was surprised by the virulence of the criticism directed at her proposal.
The virulence of his response shocked everyone at the meeting.
