Dermochelyidae

noun
family of large marine turtles (leatherbacks)
Pronunciation: / dɜr.moʊˈki.li.ɪ.di / (AmE), / dɜː.məʊˈkiː.lɪ.aɪ.diː / (BrE)
A family of large marine turtles known as leatherback sea turtles, distinguished by their leathery shells and wide distribution in warm oceans.
