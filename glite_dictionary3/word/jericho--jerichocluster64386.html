Jericho

proper noun
ancient city near the Dead Sea, biblical reference
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛrɪˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛrɪkəʊ / (BrE)
An ancient city in the West Bank near the northern end of the Dead Sea, noted in the Old Testament as the first city taken by the Israelites under Joshua.
