colobus

noun
African arboreal monkey with silky fur
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊləbəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒləbəs / (BrE)
an arboreal monkey native to western and central Africa, noted for its long silky fur and reduced thumbs

Examples

colobus monkey group
Researchers observed a group of colobus feeding on young leaves in the forest canopy.
Several colobus watched us quietly from the treetops.
