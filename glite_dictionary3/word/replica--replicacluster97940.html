replica

noun
exact or close copy of something
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛplɪkə / (AmE), / ˈrɛplɪkə / (BrE)
an exact or close copy of something, especially a work of art or an object, made by the original maker or someone else

Examples

replica painting
He bought a replica jersey of his favorite football player.
The museum displayed a replica of the ancient statue.
This watch is a replica and not the original antique.

Related Terms

  • synonym – copy: something made to look exactly like another thing
  • antonym – original: the first or actual version from which copies are made
