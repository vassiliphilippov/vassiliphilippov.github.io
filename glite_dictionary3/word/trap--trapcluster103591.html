trap

noun
subgenre of hip-hop
Pronunciation: / træp / (AmE), / træp / (BrE)
a subgenre of hip-hop, originating in the Southern United States, characterized by sparse, ominous rhythms, heavy 808 bass, and lyrical themes often referencing life in the 'trap' (drug-selling environments).

Examples

trap music
The club played classic trap tracks that defined the genre.
Many producers in Atlanta helped shape modern trap with sparse beats and booming 808s.
She prefers listening to trap late at night because of its dark, hypnotic rhythms.
