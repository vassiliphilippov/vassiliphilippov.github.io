Americanization

noun
assimilation into American culture
Pronunciation: / əˌmɛrɪkənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌmɛrɪkənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which a person or group adopts the customs, values, and behaviors of American culture

Examples

Americanization of culture
Critics argue that Americanization can erode local customs and languages.
Many immigrants underwent Americanization programs upon their arrival in the early twentieth century.
The Americanization of traditional foods has changed how many cuisines are prepared in the United States.
