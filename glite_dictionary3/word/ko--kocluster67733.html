KO

noun
a knockout blow in combat sports
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkeɪˈəʊ / (BrE)
a blow in boxing or other combat sports that knocks an opponent unconscious, resulting in an immediate win

Examples

KO punch
He suffered his first KO after an unexpected uppercut.
Her stunning KO was the highlight of the evening.
The boxer scored a KO in the seventh round to win the match.
