Kanchenjunga

proper noun
mountain on Nepal-India border, third highest in the world
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntʃənˈdʒʌŋɡə / (AmE), / ˌkæn.tʃənˈdʒʌŋ.ɡə / (BrE)
the third highest mountain in the world, located in the Himalayas on the border between Nepal and the Indian state of Sikkim; elevation 28,208 feet (8,586 meters)
