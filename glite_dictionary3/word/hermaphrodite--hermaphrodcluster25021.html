hermaphrodite

noun
organism with both male and female sexual traits
Pronunciation: / hərˈmæfɹəˌdaɪt / (AmE), / hɜːˈmæf.rə.daɪt / (BrE)
a person, animal, or plant that has both male and female reproductive organs or sexual characteristics
