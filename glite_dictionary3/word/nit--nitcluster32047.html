nit

noun
unit of luminance (brightness)
Pronunciation: / nɪt / (AmE), / nɪt / (BrE)
a unit of luminance equal to one candela per square meter, used to measure the brightness of a surface

Examples

nit brightness level
A monitor with 300 nits is considered quite suitable for office use.
Many modern smartphones advertise their screen brightness in nits.
The display can reach a brightness of 1000 nits for better visibility in sunlight.

  • synonym – lumen: unit of luminous flux
