sandpit

noun
pit for digging sand
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈsæn.pɪt / (BrE)
A large excavation in sandy ground from which sand is extracted, often for industrial or construction purposes.

Examples

industrial sandpit
A new sandpit was opened near the river to supply construction materials for the city.
After heavy rain, the sandpit partially filled with water, creating a temporary pond.
The workers extracted tons of sand from the sandpit every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym – quarry: an open excavation from which stone or minerals are extracted
