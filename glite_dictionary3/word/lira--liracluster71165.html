lira

noun
former currency of Malta
Pronunciation: / ˈliːrə / (AmE), / ˈlɪərə / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Malta, equal to 100 cents, used before the adoption of the euro.

Examples

Maltese lira banknote
He exchanged his liri for euros before leaving Malta.
Many older price tags can still be found showing amounts in lira.
The Maltese lira was replaced by the euro in 2008.
