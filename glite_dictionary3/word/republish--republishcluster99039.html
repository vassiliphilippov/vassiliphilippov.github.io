to republish

verb
publish again
Pronunciation: / riˈpʌblɪʃ / (AmE), / riˈpʌblɪʃ / (BrE)
to publish (a work or information) again, especially after revision or in a different form or edition

Examples

to republish a book
After new evidence was discovered, the researchers had to republish their findings.
The magazine decided to republish the article in its anniversary issue.
The novel was so popular that the publisher chose to republish it with additional content.

Related Terms

  • synonym – publish: to make a document or work available to the public
