hallelujah

interjection
exclamation of relief or joy
Pronunciation: / ˌhælɪˈluːjə / (AmE), / ˌhælɪˈluːjə / (BrE)
Used as an exclamation to express strong relief, joy, or triumph; often used outside religious worship.

Examples

shout hallelujah happily
After months of waiting, she cried hallelujah when the visa finally arrived.
When the power came back on, the whole office shouted hallelujah and cheered loudly.
The doctor smiled and said, 'hallelujah,' after running the successful test on the patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hooray: an exclamation of happiness or approval
  • synonym – hurrah: an exclamation expressing joy, approval, or triumph
