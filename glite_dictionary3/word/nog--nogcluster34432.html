nog

noun
wooden block in masonry wall for fixing joinery
Pronunciation: / nɑɡ / (AmE), / nɒɡ / (BrE)
a wooden block built into a masonry wall to provide a surface for fixing joinery or nails

Examples

nog in a wall
Proper placement of each nog ensures that shelves can be securely fastened later.
