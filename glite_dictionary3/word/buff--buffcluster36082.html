to buff

verb
polish to make shiny
Pronunciation: / bʌf / (AmE), / bʌf / (BrE)
to polish a surface by rubbing it, in order to make it smooth or shiny

Examples

to buff a surface
After applying the wax, she began buffing her car to achieve a high shine.
He used a soft cloth to buff the silverware until it gleamed.
The janitor buffed the marble floors for the special event.

Related Terms

  • synonym – polish: to make the surface of something smooth and shiny by rubbing it
  • antonym – tarnish: to make or become less bright or shiny
