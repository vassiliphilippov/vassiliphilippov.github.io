Tevet

noun
month in the Hebrew calendar
Pronunciation: / teɪˈvɛt / (AmE), / teɪˈvɛt / (BrE)
the fourth month of the civil year and the tenth month of the ecclesiastical year in the Hebrew calendar, occurring in December and January
