Marcus Junius Brutus the Younger

proper noun
Roman statesman, Caesar's assassin
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrkəs ˈdʒuːniəs ˈbruːtəs ði ˈjʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkəs ˈdʒuːnɪəs ˈbruːtəs ði ˈjʌŋɡə / (BrE)
(85–42 BC) A Roman statesman famous for leading, along with Cassius, the conspiracy to assassinate Julius Caesar.
