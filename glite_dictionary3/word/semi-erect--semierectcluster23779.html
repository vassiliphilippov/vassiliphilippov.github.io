semi-erect

adjective
partly upright (of plants)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi ɪˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmi ɪˈrɛkt / (BrE)
(of a plant) partly upright in position, neither fully erect nor completely prostrate.
