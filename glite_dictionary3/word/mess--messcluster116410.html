mess

noun
large number or amount (informal US)
Pronunciation: / mɛs / (AmE), / mɛs / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly US) A large number or amount of something, especially when phrased as 'a mess of.'

Examples

a mess of clothes
She has a mess of old clothes in her attic.
There was a mess of people waiting outside the concert hall.
We picked a mess of strawberries from the garden this morning.

  • synonym – lot: a large amount or number of people or things
  • synonym – slew: a large number or amount, especially used informally
