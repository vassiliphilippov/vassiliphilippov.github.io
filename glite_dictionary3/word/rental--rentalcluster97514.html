rental

noun
the act of paying for use of something
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn.təl / (AmE), / ˈrɛn.təl / (BrE)
the act of paying for the temporary use of something, such as property, a vehicle, or equipment.

Examples

rental service
Bike rental is available near the train station for commuters.
She paid a weekly rental fee for the car while on vacation.
The rental of the apartment includes utilities and internet access.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lease: a contract granting use or occupation of property for a specified period in exchange for rent
  • antonym – purchase: the act of buying something and gaining ownership
