director

noun
member of a board governing an organization
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
A person who is a member of a board of directors and is responsible for helping to govern or manage an organization, company, or institution.

Examples

company director
Several directors attended the annual general meeting to discuss corporate strategy.
She was appointed as a director on the company's board last year.
The director voted against the proposal during the board meeting.
