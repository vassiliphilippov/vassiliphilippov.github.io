taxicab

noun
car for hire with a driver
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksɪˌkæb / (AmE), / ˈtæksɪkab / (BrE)
A car for hire with a driver, paid to transport passengers to their chosen destination.

Examples

ride a taxicab
She hailed a taxicab outside the airport terminal after her flight arrived.
The city's taxicab drivers went on strike to demand better working conditions.
We took a taxicab across town when it started to rain heavily.
