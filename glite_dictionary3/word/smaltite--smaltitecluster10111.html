smaltite

noun
a mineral of cobalt arsenide, source of cobalt and nickel
Pronunciation: / ˈsmæl.taɪt / (AmE), / ˈsmɔːl.taɪt / (BrE)
a grey mineral consisting chiefly of cobalt arsenide with some nickel and iron, used as an ore of cobalt and nickel
