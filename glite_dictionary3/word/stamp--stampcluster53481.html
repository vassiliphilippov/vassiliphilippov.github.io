stamp

noun
a printed or engraved symbol or mark
Pronunciation: / stæmp / (AmE), / stæmp / (BrE)
a symbol or mark created by printing or engraving, especially one used to indicate ownership, approval, or to authenticate something

Examples

passport stamp mark
Every passport entry had a new stamp indicating the border crossing.
She loves collecting vintage postage stamps from different countries.
The official document bore an embossed stamp as proof of authenticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – imprint: a mark or impression made by pressure
  • synonym – seal: a device or substance used to join two things together and to prevent them from coming apart or to indicate authenticity
