to enjoy

verb
have or benefit from something
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪ / (BrE)
to have or benefit from something, especially a right, privilege, or advantage

Examples

Employees enjoy certain health benefits as part of their contract.
Many retirees enjoy a steady income from pensions.

The company enjoyed strong growth during the last quarter.
