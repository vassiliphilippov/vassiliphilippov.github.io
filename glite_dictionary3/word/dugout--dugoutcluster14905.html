dugout

noun
shelter dug into the ground
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌɡˌaʊt /; / ˈdʌɡ.aʊt / (AmE), / ˈdʌɡ.aʊt / (BrE)
a shelter or dwelling dug into the ground, often covered with earth, used for protection or living (including prehistoric, military, or animal shelters)
