collector

noun
person who collects payments as a job
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛktɚ / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect payments, such as rent or taxes, from others

Examples

collector of taxes
A collector may represent the city or a private landlord when collecting payments.
She asked the collector for a receipt after paying her taxes.
The collector visited each property at the end of the month to gather the rent.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – landlord: person who rents out property and often collects rent
  • synonym – tax collector: a person whose job is to collect taxes
