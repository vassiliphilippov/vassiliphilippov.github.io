to hibernate

verb
be in a dormant or inactive state
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪbɚˌneɪt /; / ˈhaɪbərˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪbəneɪt /; / ˈhaɪbəneɪt / (BrE)
for an animal to spend a long period in a deep sleep or inactive state, usually during winter
