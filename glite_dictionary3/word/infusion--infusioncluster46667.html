infusion

noun
the act of introducing a new quality
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfjuʒən / (AmE), / ɪnˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
the act of introducing a new quality, element, or influence into something

Examples

infusion of new ideas
A generous infusion of funding saved the project from failure.
The infusion of fresh ideas helped the company grow rapidly.
The festival got a new energy thanks to the infusion of young volunteers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – injection: the act of introducing something new, especially forcefully
  • antonym – removal: the act of taking something away
