to costume

verb
dress or supply with a costume
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːstuːm /; / ˈkɑːstuːm, kɑːˈstuːm / (AmE), / ˈkɒstjuːm /; / ˈkɒstjuːm, kɒˈstjuːm / (BrE)
