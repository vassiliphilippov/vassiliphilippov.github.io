attosecond

noun
one quintillionth of a second
Pronunciation: / ˈætoʊˌsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈatəʊˌsɛkənd / (BrE)
A unit of time equal to one quintillionth (10⁻¹⁸) of a second.

Examples

one attosecond duration
An attosecond is to a second what a second is to the age of the universe.
Physicists use attoseconds to measure the movement of electrons in atoms.
The laser produced pulses as short as a single attosecond.
