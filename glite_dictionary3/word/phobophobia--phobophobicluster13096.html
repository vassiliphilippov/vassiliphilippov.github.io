phobophobia

noun
fear of developing a phobia
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊbəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌfəʊbəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
an abnormal or irrational fear of developing a phobia

Examples

phobophobia symptoms
His constant worry about developing another irrational fear suggested he suffered from phobophobia.
Some patients with anxiety disorders also experience phobophobia, fearing the onset of new phobias.
Therapists sometimes encounter phobophobia in clients who are aware of their vulnerability to anxiety.
