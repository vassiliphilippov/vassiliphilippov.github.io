to murder

verb
to kill deliberately and unlawfully
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːdər / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdə / (BrE)
to kill someone deliberately and with premeditation, especially when it is unlawful

Examples

to murder a victim
He plotted for weeks before finally murdering his business partner.
Investigators believe she was murdered because she knew too much about the crime.
The mafia boss ordered his rivals to be murdered during the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – assassinate: to kill an important person for political or religious reasons
  • antonym – save: to prevent the death of someone
  • synonym – slaughter: to kill in a violent or brutal way
