anestrus

noun
period of sexual inactivity in female mammals
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛstrəs / (AmE), / ˈænɪstrəs / (BrE)
a phase in the reproductive cycle of some female mammals during which they are sexually inactive and do not display estrus behavior

Examples

anestrus period in animals
During anestrus, the mare shows no signs of reproductive behavior and hormonal activity is low.
Seasonal breeders often experience anestrus when environmental conditions are unfavorable for raising offspring.
The veterinarian explained that the dog was in anestrus and therefore not receptive to mating.
