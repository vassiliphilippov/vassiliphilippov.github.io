vernacular

noun
language of a specific group or region
Pronunciation: / vərˈnækjələr / (AmE), / vəˈnakjʊlə /; / vəˈnækjʊlə / (BrE)
the language or dialect spoken by ordinary people in a particular country, region, or group, especially as distinct from the literary or official language
