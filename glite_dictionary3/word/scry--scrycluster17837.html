to scry

verb
look at top cards in a deck (card games)
Pronunciation: / skraɪ / (AmE), / skraɪ / (BrE)
(in card games) to look at the top one or more cards of your deck and decide whether to keep them on top, put them on the bottom, or otherwise reorder them according to the game's rules.

Examples

to scry top cards
In the tournament, she decided to scry the top two cards before drawing her next card.
He scried the top card and placed it on the bottom of his deck to avoid a bad draw.
During cleanup, players are scrying to improve their chances by arranging their next draws carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym – peek: to look quickly or secretly at something; similar in meaning when used for looking at top cards.
