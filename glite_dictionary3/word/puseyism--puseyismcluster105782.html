Puseyism

noun
principles of the Oxford Movement (Anglicanism)
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuziˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpjuːziˌɪzəm / (BrE)
The principles and doctrines of the Oxford Movement within the Church of England, as advocated by Edward Bouverie Pusey and expounded in the 'Tracts for the Times'.
