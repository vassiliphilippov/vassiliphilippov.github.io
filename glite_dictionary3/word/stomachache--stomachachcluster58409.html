stomachache

noun
pain in the stomach or abdomen
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌməkˌeɪk / (AmE), / ˈstʌməkˌeɪk / (BrE)
A pain or uncomfortable feeling in the stomach or abdomen.

Examples

stomachache after eating
A mild stomachache is often relieved by resting or drinking warm tea.
After eating the questionable shrimp, he developed a sharp stomachache within an hour.
She couldn't concentrate in class because of a severe stomachache.
