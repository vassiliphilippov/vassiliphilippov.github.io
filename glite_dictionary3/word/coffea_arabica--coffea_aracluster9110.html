Coffea arabica

noun
main species of coffee plant
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːfi ə əˈræbɪkə / (AmE), / ˌkɒfi ə əˈrabɪkə / (BrE)
A species of coffee plant native to northeastern Africa, widely cultivated for its seeds, which produce most of the world's coffee.
