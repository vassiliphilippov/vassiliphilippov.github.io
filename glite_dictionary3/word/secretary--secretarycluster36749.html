secretary

noun
administrative assistant
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkrəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɛkrətəri / (BrE)
a person employed to assist with correspondence, record-keeping, and other administrative tasks for an individual or organization

Examples

secretary answers calls
Each department has a secretary to handle paperwork and phone calls.
The secretary scheduled the meeting and sent out invitations to all participants.
When I called the office, the secretary answered and transferred me to my manager.
