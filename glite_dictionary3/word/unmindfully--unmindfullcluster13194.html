unmindfully

adverb
in a careless or thoughtless way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmaɪndfəlli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌɪndfəli / (BrE)
In a careless or thoughtless manner; without paying attention or consideration.

Examples

to act unmindfully
He often speaks unmindfully, unaware of how his words affect others.
She unmindfully tossed her keys onto the table and missed entirely.
Walking unmindfully into the street, she almost caused an accident.
