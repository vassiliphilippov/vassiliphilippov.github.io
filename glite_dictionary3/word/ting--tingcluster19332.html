ting

noun
light clear metallic sound
Pronunciation: / tɪŋ / (AmE), / tɪŋ / (BrE)
a light, clear, metallic sound, like that made by a small bell or glass

Examples

a ting from a bell
A gentle ting signaled that the elevator had arrived.
She heard the ting of a spoon tapping against a teacup.
The tiny bell made a soft ting as it swayed in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – clang: a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym – ping: a short, sharp, high-pitched sound
