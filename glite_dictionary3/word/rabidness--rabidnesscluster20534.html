rabidness

noun
extreme excitement or enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈræbɪdnɪs / (BrE)
A state of extreme or unrestrained excitement, enthusiasm, or passion.

Examples

rabidness for football
His rabidness for the sport inspired everyone around him to work harder.
The rabidness of the crowd at the concert made it an unforgettable experience.
The critics remarked on the rabidness of her devotion to the cause.
