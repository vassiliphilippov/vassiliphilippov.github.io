elemental

noun
fantasy spirit embodying an element
Pronunciation: / \u02cc\u025bl\u0259\u02c8m\u025bnt\u0259l / (AmE), / \u02cc\u025bl\u026a\u02c8m\u025bnt\u0259l / (BrE)
A supernatural being or spirit that embodies or represents one of the classical elements (fire, water, earth, air), often appearing in fantasy fiction, games, or folklore.

Examples

summon an elemental spirit
The sorcerer summoned an elemental of fire to wage war against his foes.
During the ritual, she bound a water elemental to guard the temple's entrance at night.
Legends say the forest is watched by invisible elementals that protect the trees and streams.
