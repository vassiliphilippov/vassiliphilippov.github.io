cony

noun
old term for a rabbit
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊni / (AmE), / ˈkəʊni / (BrE)
an old term for a rabbit, especially the European rabbit, or any of several small burrowing mammals with long ears and short tails

Examples

wild cony meat
In medieval recipes, a cony was a popular meat for noble feasts.
