dentin

noun
hard tissue beneath tooth enamel
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛn.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɛntɪn /; / ˈdɛn.tɪn / (BrE)
the hard, dense tissue beneath the enamel that forms most of a tooth
