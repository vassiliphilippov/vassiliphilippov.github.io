guereza

noun
an African colobus monkey species
Pronunciation: / ɡwəˈrɛzə / (AmE), / ɡwəˈriːzə / (BrE)
a large African colobus monkey (Colobus guereza) with a black coat, white fringes along the sides, and often a white tail tuft

Examples

guereza monkey
During the safari, we spotted a guereza leaping gracefully through the treetops.
Researchers observed the feeding habits of the guereza in its natural habitat.
The guereza is known for its striking black and white fur and bushy white tail.
