to dally

verb
flirt without serious intentions
Pronunciation: / ˈdæli / (AmE), / ˈdæli / (BrE)
to talk or behave flirtatiously without serious intentions

Examples

to dally with someone
He tends to dally with his colleagues at office parties, but never takes it further.
If you dally with everyone you meet, people may question your intentions.
She accused him of trying to dally with her feelings instead of being sincere.
