Russia

proper noun
former empire in eastern Europe and northern Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃə / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃə / (BrE)
a former empire in eastern Europe and northern Asia, ruled from the 14th century with Moscow as its capital, reaching its height under Peter the Great and Catherine the Great, and overthrown by the 1917 revolution
