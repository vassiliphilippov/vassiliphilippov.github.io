claw-shaped

adjective
resembling the shape of a claw
Pronunciation: / klɔː ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / klɔː ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape that resembles a claw, often curved and pointed.

Examples

claw-shaped hook
