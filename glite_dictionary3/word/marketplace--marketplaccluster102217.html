marketplace

noun
place where goods are bought and sold
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkətˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪtˌpleɪs / (BrE)
an open area or building in a town where people gather to buy and sell goods, especially regularly or in large numbers

Examples

busy marketplace
Every Saturday, the town’s marketplace is filled with stalls selling fresh produce and handmade crafts.
The old marketplace has been renovated and now hosts a lively farmers’ market.
Tourists often gather in the central marketplace to sample local foods and buy souvenirs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – market: an open space where a market is held; the act of buying and selling
