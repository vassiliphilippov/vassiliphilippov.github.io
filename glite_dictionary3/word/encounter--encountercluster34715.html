to encounter

verb
to experience or face (something)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkaʊntər / (AmE), / ɪnˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
to experience or be faced with something, especially difficulties or problems

Examples

to encounter difficulties
During her travels, she often encountered unexpected challenges along the way.
The company encountered significant resistance when proposing new policies.
You may encounter some difficulties during the installation process, but support is available.
During the trip, we encountered some unexpected difficulties with the weather.
She encountered many obstacles while trying to start her own business.
The scientists often encounter technical challenges during their research.
