rate

noun
amount charged or paid per unit
Pronunciation: / reɪt / (AmE), / reɪt / (BrE)
an amount of a charge or payment calculated in relation to a particular standard, such as time, quantity, or unit

Examples

hourly rate
Electricity is billed at a higher rate during peak hours.
He was surprised by the low hourly rate for the consultation.
The hotel offers a special rate for weekend stays.

  • synonym – fee: a fixed charge or payment for a service
  • synonym – tariff: a schedule of charges or rates
