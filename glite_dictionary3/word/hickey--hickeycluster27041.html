hickey

noun
mark on skin from kissing or sucking
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪki / (AmE), / ˈhɪki / (BrE)
A temporary reddish mark or bruise on the skin caused by kissing or sucking, usually as a display of affection.

a hickey on the neck

The hickey faded after a few days without leaving a scar.
