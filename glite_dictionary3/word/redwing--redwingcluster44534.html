redwing

noun
small European thrush with reddish flanks
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
a small European thrush (Turdus iliacus) with reddish flanks and underwing, commonly found in woodland and hedgerows

Examples

flock of redwings migrating
During migration, large flocks of redwings can be seen across northern Europe.
The distinctive call of the redwing is often heard at dusk in rural areas.
We spotted a redwing feeding among the autumn leaves.
