to smoke

verb
inhale and exhale tobacco smoke
Pronunciation: / smoʊk / (AmE), / sməʊk / (BrE)
to inhale and exhale the smoke of burning tobacco or another substance, especially from a cigarette, cigar, or pipe

Examples

to smoke a cigarette
Children are often warned about the dangers of smoking at a young age.
He promised his doctor that he would never smoke again.
It is illegal to smoke in most restaurants and public places now.
