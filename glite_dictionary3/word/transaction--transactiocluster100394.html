transaction

noun
blockchain record of value transfer
Pronunciation: / trænˈzækʃən / (AmE), / trænˈzækʃən /; / trænˈzækʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a recorded operation in computing or digital systems, especially in blockchain or cryptocurrencies, where value or information is transferred and stored as data
