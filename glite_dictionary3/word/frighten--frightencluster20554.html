to frighten

verb
to make someone feel afraid
Pronunciation: / ˈfraɪtən / (AmE), / ˈfraɪtn / (BrE)
to make someone feel afraid; to cause fear in someone

Examples

to frighten a child
He wears a costume to frighten people on Halloween.
Sudden noises easily frighten my dog.
The loud thunder frightened the children hiding under the table.
