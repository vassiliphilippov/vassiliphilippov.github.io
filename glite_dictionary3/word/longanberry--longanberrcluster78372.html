longanberry

noun
small Asian fruit similar to lychee
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋənˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋənˌbɛri / (BrE)
A small, round, sweet fruit native to Asia, similar to the lychee but with a more translucent flesh and a brownish shell.
