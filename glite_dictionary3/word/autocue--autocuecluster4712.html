autocue

noun
prompting device for speakers
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtəˌkjuː / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəˌkjuː / (BrE)
a device used for displaying the script or text to a speaker or presenter, typically in television or broadcasting.

Examples

autocue script display
After the autocue malfunctioned, she had to remember her lines from memory.
The newsreader glanced at the autocue to stay on script during the broadcast.
The studio upgraded its autocue system for smoother live shows.

Related Terms

  • synonym – teleprompter: a device that displays scripts for speakers (mainly American use)
