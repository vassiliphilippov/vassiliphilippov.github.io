to snuffle

verb
breathe noisily through nose
Pronunciation: / ˈsnʌf.əl /; / ˈsnʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈsnʌf.əl /; / ˈsnʌfəl / (BrE)
to breathe noisily through a blocked or congested nose, often because of illness, allergies, or crying
