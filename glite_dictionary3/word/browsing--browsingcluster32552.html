browsing

noun
casual or superficial reading or looking
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of looking through written material or goods in a casual or superficial way, without a specific aim.

Examples

browsing in a shop
A little casual browsing often leads to unexpected discoveries.
He enjoys browsing magazines while waiting for his appointment.
She spent the afternoon browsing through the bookstore without buying anything.

Related Terms

  • antonym – perusing: reading carefully or thoroughly
  • synonym – scanning: looking over quickly or superficially
