ravigote

noun
sauce seasoned with herbs and capers
Pronunciation: / ˌræ.viˈɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˌravɪˈɡəʊt / (BrE)
A sauce made from velouté or vinaigrette and seasoned with chopped chervil, chives, tarragon, shallots, and capers, served as an accompaniment to meat, fish, or vegetables.

Examples

ravigote sauce recipe
At the French bistro, my favorite starter was the chicken terrine with ravigote.
She prepared a classic ravigote using fresh tarragon and shallots from her garden.
The chef served the poached salmon with a tangy ravigote sauce on the side.
