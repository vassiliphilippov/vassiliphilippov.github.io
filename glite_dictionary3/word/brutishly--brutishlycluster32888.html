brutishly

adverb
in a cruel, beast-like manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːtɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈbruːtɪʃli / (BrE)
In a cruel or savage manner; like a brute or beast.

Examples

to behave brutishly
He brutishly interrupted the conversation, ignoring everyone's feelings.
The dictator ruled brutishly, showing no mercy to his opponents.
The prisoners were brutishly beaten by their captors.

Related Terms

  • antonym – gently: in a mild, kind, or tender manner
  • synonym – savagely: in a fierce, violent, or uncontrolled manner
