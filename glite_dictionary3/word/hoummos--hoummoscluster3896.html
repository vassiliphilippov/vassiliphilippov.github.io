hoummos

noun
spread made with chickpeas and tahini
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːməs / (AmE), / ˈhʊm.ɒs / (BrE)
A thick, creamy spread made from mashed chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic, commonly eaten as a dip or spread, especially in Middle Eastern cuisine.
