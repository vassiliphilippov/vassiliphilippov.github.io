pharyngeal

adjective
relating to the pharynx (throat)
Pronunciation: / færɪnˈdʒiəl / (AmE), / fɑːrɪnˈdʒiːəl / (BrE)
Relating to or situated in the pharynx (the part of the throat behind the mouth and nasal cavity).

Examples
