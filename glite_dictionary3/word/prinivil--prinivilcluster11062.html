Prinivil

noun
antihypertensive drug, trade name
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnɪvɪl / (AmE), / ˈprɪnɪvɪl / (BrE)
A trade name for lisinopril, an ACE inhibitor used to treat high blood pressure and after heart attacks.

Examples
