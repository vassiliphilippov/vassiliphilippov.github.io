defiant

adjective
openly resisting authority or opposition
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfaɪənt / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪənt / (BrE)
showing open resistance or bold disobedience to authority or an opposing force

Examples

defiant attitude
Despite warnings, he remained defiant and refused to leave the building.
Her defiant stance inspired others to challenge the unfair rules.
The child gave his mother a defiant look when told to clean his room.
