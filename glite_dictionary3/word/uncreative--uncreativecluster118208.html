uncreative

adjective
not creative; lacking originality
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkrieɪˈtɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kriːˈeɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
lacking originality or the ability to create new ideas; not inventive or imaginative.

Examples

an uncreative solution
Her approach to problem-solving was considered uncreative by her colleagues.
Many people found the movie plot painfully uncreative and predictable.
The ad campaign was dull and uncreative, failing to catch anyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym – creative: having the ability to create; imaginative
