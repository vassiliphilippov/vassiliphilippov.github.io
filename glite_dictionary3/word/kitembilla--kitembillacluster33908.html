kitembilla

noun
tropical shrub with edible, gooseberry-flavored fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪtɛmˈbɪlə / (AmE), / ˌkɪtɛmˈbɪlə / (BrE)
A small, spiny shrub native to Sri Lanka and India, cultivated for its maroon-purple fruit with sweet, purple pulp tasting like gooseberries.

Examples

kitembilla shrub
Farmers in southern India often harvest kitembilla during the late summer season.

The sweet pulp of the kitembilla is sometimes used to make traditional jams and jellies.
