shortish

adjective
somewhat short
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtɪʃ / (BrE)
fairly short; somewhat short in length or duration.

Examples

a shortish jacket
He wore a shortish jacket that barely reached his waist.
Her hair is shortish—not quite a bob, but close.
The meeting was shortish, lasting only about half an hour instead of the usual hour.
