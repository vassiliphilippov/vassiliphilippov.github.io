longlegs

noun
a type of wading bird (stilt)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌlɛɡz / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌlɛɡz / (BrE)
a long-legged, three-toed black-and-white wading bird found in inland ponds, marshes, or brackish lagoons; also known as a stilt
