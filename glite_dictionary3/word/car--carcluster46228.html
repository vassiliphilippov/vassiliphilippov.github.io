car

noun
vehicle on a cable railway
Pronunciation: / kɑr / (AmE), / kɑː / (BrE)
a compartment or vehicle that carries people or goods in a transport system, such as a train, cable railway, airship, or elevator

Examples

train car
The old mining car carried loads of ore down the hillside.
Tourists stood in line to board the car for a scenic ride to the summit.
We took the car up the steep mountain slope using the cable railway.
During the flight, the crew remained in the car of the airship.
Engineers designed a reinforced car to improve safety on the new airship.
The car was equipped with observation windows and navigation controls.
A new subway car was added to increase capacity during rush hour.
The train consisted of several passenger cars and a dining car.
Three cars had jumped the rails during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cable car: a vehicle that moves on rails and is propelled by a continuously moving cable
  • synonym – coach: passenger compartment of a train
  • synonym – elevator: a platform or compartment used for raising and lowering people or goods
  • degree lower – freight car: railroad car designed for carrying goods
  • synonym – gondola: the compartment attached to an airship or balloon that carries people and equipment
  • degree higher – locomotive: engine that pulls railroad cars
