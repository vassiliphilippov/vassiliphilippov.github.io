feature

noun
main film in a cinema program
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈfiːtʃə / (BrE)
the main full-length film in a cinema or movie theater program, as distinguished from short films or previews

Examples

main feature film
After the feature ended, the audience applauded enthusiastically.
The feature tonight is an award-winning documentary.
We arrived early to catch both the short and the feature.
