straddle

noun
gymnastic movement with legs apart
Pronunciation: / ˈstræd.əl / (AmE), / ˈstræd.əl / (BrE)
a gymnastic movement in which the legs are extended on either side of the parallel bars, with the body supported by the arms

Examples

a straddle on bars
His routine included a challenging straddle followed by a smooth dismount.
She performed a perfect straddle on the parallel bars during her routine.
The coach demonstrated how to do a straddle safely without losing balance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – split: a position or movement in gymnastics or dance in which the legs are extended in opposite directions
