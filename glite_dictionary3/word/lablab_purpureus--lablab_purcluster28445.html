Lablab purpureus

noun
climbing plant with edible pods and purple flowers
Pronunciation: / læbˌlæb pɝːˈpjʊəriəs / (AmE), / læbˌlæb pjʊəˈpjʊəriəs / (BrE)
a perennial climbing plant of the pea family, native to the Old World tropics, with trifoliate leaves, fragrant purple flowers, and edible maroon pods; cultivated as an ornamental and vegetable, especially in the Indian subcontinent
