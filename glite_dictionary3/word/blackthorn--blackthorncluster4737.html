blackthorn

noun
thorny Eurasian shrub with plumlike fruits
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈblækˌθɔːn / (BrE)
a thorny Eurasian shrub (Prunus spinosa) with small white flowers and bluish-black, plumlike fruits, often used for making sloe gin

Examples

blackthorn hedge
Farmers often planted blackthorn hedges as natural barriers between fields.
She picked ripe sloes from the blackthorn bushes to make homemade jam.
The dense thickets of blackthorn provided shelter for birds during winter.
