solfeggio

noun
singing exercise using musical syllables
Pronunciation: / sɒlˈfɛdʒ.i.oʊ /; / sɔlˈfɛdʒioʊ / (AmE), / sɒlˈfɛdʒ.i.əʊ /; / sɒlˈfɛdʒiəʊ / (BrE)
a way of teaching or practicing singing by using the syllables do, re, mi, fa, sol, la, and ti to represent notes in a scale
