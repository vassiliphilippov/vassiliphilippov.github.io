Merops

proper noun
type genus of bee-eaters (Meropidae)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛr.ɑps / (AmE), / ˈmɪər.ɒps / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Meropidae, comprising the bee-eaters, a group of brightly colored, insectivorous birds found in Africa, Eurasia, and Australia.
