to reinvent

verb
create or change something again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈvɛnt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈvɛnt / (BrE)
to create or change something again in a new or improved way, especially to make it seem new or adapted for a new purpose

She managed to reinvent the traditional recipe with modern ingredients.
The band tried to reinvent their music to appeal to a younger audience.
