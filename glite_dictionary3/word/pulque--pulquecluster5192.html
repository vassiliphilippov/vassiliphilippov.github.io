pulque

noun
fermented Mexican drink from agave
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlkeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpʊlkeɪ / (BrE)
A traditional Mexican alcoholic beverage made from the fermented juice of certain agave plants, especially the maguey.

Examples

glass of pulque
Many locals stopped at the roadside stand for a glass of pulque after a long day.
She was curious to try pulque during her trip to Oaxaca.
The history of pulque dates back to pre-Columbian times in central Mexico.
