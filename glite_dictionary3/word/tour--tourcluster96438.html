tour

noun
period of military service at a post
Pronunciation: / tʊr / (AmE), / tʊə / (BrE)
a period of time spent doing a particular job or duty, especially in the military or as part of a work shift

Examples

tour of duty
After his second tour overseas, he received a promotion.
He just finished a tour in Afghanistan and is now back home.
Many soldiers complete more than one tour during their military careers.
After a long tour on night duty, she was finally able to go home.
During my tour at the hospital, I met many new patients.
His third tour in the navy lasted six months before he was rotated out.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deployment: being stationed or assigned to a military position or area
  • synonym – rotation: a tour of duty followed by reassignment or relief
  • synonym – shift: a scheduled period of work, especially in jobs that require coverage around the clock
