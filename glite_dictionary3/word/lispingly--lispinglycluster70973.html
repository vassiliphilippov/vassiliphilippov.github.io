lispingly

adverb
in a manner showing a lisp
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪspɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɪspɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lisp; with a lisp.

Examples

speak lispingly
He answered lispingly, making it hard for others to understand him clearly.
She spoke lispingly, her words soft and sibilant.
The child recited the poem lispingly, delighting the audience with her innocent charm.
