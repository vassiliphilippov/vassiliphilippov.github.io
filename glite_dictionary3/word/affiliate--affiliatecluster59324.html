affiliate

noun
a connected subordinate organization or member
Pronunciation: / əˈfɪliˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈfɪlieɪt /; / əˈfɪlɪeɪt / (BrE)
a person or organization officially connected to a larger group or company

Examples

affiliate organization
Our local branch is an affiliate of a much larger international association.
She works for an affiliate of the national charity organization.
The television network has an affiliate in nearly every major city.
As an affiliate, the organization follows the guidelines set by the parent group but manages its own affairs.
Several local TV stations are affiliates of the national broadcasting network.
The company operates through a number of overseas affiliates to reach international markets.
