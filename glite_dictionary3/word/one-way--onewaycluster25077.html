one-way

adjective
allowing movement in one direction only
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌnˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈwʌnˌweɪ / (BrE)
allowing movement or travel in only one direction; not allowing return by the same route.

Examples

one-way street
Be careful not to drive the wrong way down a one-way street.
He bought a one-way ticket to Paris and didn't plan to return.
The city council decided to make Main Street a one-way road to help with traffic flow.

Related Terms

  • antonym – two-way: allowing movement or communication in both directions
