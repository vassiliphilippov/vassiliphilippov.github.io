dacitic

adjective
relating to dacite rock
Pronunciation: / dəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / dəˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of dacite, a light-colored volcanic rock consisting mainly of plagioclase and quartz.

dacitic lava flow
