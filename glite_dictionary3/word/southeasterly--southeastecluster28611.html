southeasterly

adjective
facing or toward the southeast
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊθˈistərli / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈiːstəli / (BrE)
Situated in or oriented toward the southeast.

Examples

southeasterly direction faces
