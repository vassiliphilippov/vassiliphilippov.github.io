to aspirate

verb
pronounce with a breathy sound (phonetics)
Pronunciation: / ˈæ.spəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈæ.spɪˌreɪt / (BrE)
to pronounce (a speech sound, especially a stop) with an audible breath, as in the initial 'p' in 'pat'

Examples

to aspirate a consonant
In Hindi, certain consonants are always aspirated for proper pronunciation.
Linguists study how and when children begin to aspirate different sounds.
When teaching English pronunciation, she reminds students to aspirate the initial 't' in 'top'.
