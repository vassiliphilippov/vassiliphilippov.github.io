tetchy

adjective
easily irritated or annoyed
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛtʃi / (AmE), / ˈtɛtʃi / (BrE)
Easily irritated or annoyed; touchy or prone to being upset by small things.

Examples

tetchy mood
My boss has been especially tetchy this week due to all the deadlines.
The tetchy old man shouted at the children for making too much noise.
Try not to bother her this morning; she seems a bit tetchy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calm: not easily upset or excited
  • synonym – irritable: easily annoyed, showing a quick temper
  • synonym – touchy: easily upset or offended; sensitive
