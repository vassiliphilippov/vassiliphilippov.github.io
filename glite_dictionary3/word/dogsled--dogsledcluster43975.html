dogsled

noun
sled pulled by dogs
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌslɛd / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌslɛd / (BrE)
a sled that is pulled by dogs, typically used for travel or transport over snow and ice

Examples
