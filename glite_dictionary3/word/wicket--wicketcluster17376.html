wicket

noun
croquet arch; croquet equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɪkɪt / (BrE)
a small arch made of wire that forms part of the course in croquet and through which the ball must pass

Examples

croquet wicket
Each player aimed to guide the ball through the wicket with precision.
The wicket was slightly bent after yesterday's match.
You must hit your ball through every wicket to complete the course.
