air-dried

adjective
dried by unheated air
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌdraɪd / (AmE), / ˈeəˌdraɪd / (BrE)
dried by exposure to unheated air rather than by artificial heat.

Examples

air-dried wood
Make sure the wood is properly air-dried before using it for furniture.
She prefers air-dried clothes because they feel fresher and softer.
The air-dried herbs retain more of their natural flavor than those dried with heat.
