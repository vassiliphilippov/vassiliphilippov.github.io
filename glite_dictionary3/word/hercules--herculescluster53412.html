Hercules

proper noun
mythical hero known for strength and labors
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝːkjəˌliz / (AmE), / ˈhɜːkjʊˌliːz / (BrE)
a hero in classical mythology renowned for his extraordinary strength and for performing twelve challenging labors to attain immortality
