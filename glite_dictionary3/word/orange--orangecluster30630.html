orange

noun
color between red and yellow
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɹəndʒ, ˈɔɹɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈɒɹɪndʒ / (BrE)
A color that is between red and yellow in the spectrum, resembling the hue of ripe oranges.

Examples

bright orange color

The leaves turned a beautiful shade of orange in autumn.
