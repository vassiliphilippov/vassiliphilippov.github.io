scofflaw

noun
habitual lawbreaker
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔfˌlɔ / (AmE), / ˈskɒfˌlɔː / (BrE)
a person who habitually disregards or flouts the law, especially by failing to answer court summonses or comply with regulations

Examples

Authorities launched a crackdown on scofflaws who ignore court-ordered fines.
