corruption

noun
dishonest or fraudulent conduct
Pronunciation: / kəˈrʌpʃən /; / kəˈɹʌpʃən / (AmE), / kəˈrʌpʃən /; / kəˈɹʌpʃən / (BrE)
dishonest or illegal behavior by people in power, especially involving bribery or abuse of authority
