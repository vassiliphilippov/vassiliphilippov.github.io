absolution

noun
formal forgiveness of sins
Pronunciation: / ˌæb.səˈluː.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæb.səˈljuː.ʃən /; / ˌæb.səˈluː.ʃən / (BrE)
the formal act of forgiving someone for their sins, especially in the Christian Church, as pronounced by a priest
