Tibur

proper noun
ancient town near Rome, now Tivoli
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪ.bər / (AmE), / ˈtiː.bʊər / (BrE)
The ancient name for a town east of Rome, now known as Tivoli, noted for its waterfalls and as a summer resort during the Roman Empire.

Examples

Tibur near Rome
Many poets of antiquity celebrated the scenic beauty of Tibur.
The villa at Tibur was a favored retreat for Roman emperors.
Today, tourists visit the waterfalls near Tibur, now the modern town of Tivoli.
