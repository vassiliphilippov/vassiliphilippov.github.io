sheepshearing

noun
festival for sheep shearing
Pronunciation: / ˈʃipˌʃɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpˌʃɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
A traditional festival held when sheep are sheared, often featuring celebrations, competitions, and gatherings.

Examples

sheepshearing festival
During the sheepshearing, locals demonstrate traditional shearing techniques and celebrate with music and food.
Many rural communities preserve their heritage by organizing a sheepshearing as a community event.
The village hosts an annual sheepshearing each summer, drawing visitors from across the region.
