to drag

verb
move/select items on a screen by holding and dragging
Pronunciation: / dræɡ / (AmE), / dræɡ / (BrE)
to move an object or select items on a computer screen by holding down a mouse button or pressing on a touch screen while moving the pointer or finger

Examples

to drag an icon
He dragged the icon to the trash bin to delete the document.
To resize the window, simply drag one of its corners with your mouse.
You can drag a file into the folder to move it.

Related Terms

  • antonym – drop: to release an object after dragging it on a computer screen
