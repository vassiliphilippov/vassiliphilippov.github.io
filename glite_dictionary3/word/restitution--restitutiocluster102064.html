restitution

noun
restoring to original state
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛstəˈtuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛstɪˈtjuːʃən / (BrE)
the act of making up for loss or damage by returning something to its owner, restoring it to its original state, or paying compensation
