to snooze

verb
delay or silence an alert
Pronunciation: / snuːz / (AmE), / snuːz / (BrE)
to silence or delay an alarm, notification, or reminder temporarily by using a device's snooze feature.

Examples

to snooze an alarm
She decided to snooze the meeting notification until after lunch to focus.
He snoozed his alarm three times before finally getting out of bed.
Their app allows users to keep snoozing alerts for a chosen period on Fridays.

Related Terms

  • synonym – postpone: to put something off to a later time
  • synonym – silence: to stop something (such as a device) from making noise
