SVM

noun
machine learning classification method
Pronunciation: / ɛs vi ˈɛm / (AmE), / ɛs vi ˈɛm / (BrE)
a supervised machine learning method that constructs hyperplanes in a high-dimensional space to classify data or perform regression

Many researchers use SVM to distinguish between different types of cancer cells in their studies.
