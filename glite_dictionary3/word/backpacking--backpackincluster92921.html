backpacking

noun
carrying equipment or traveling with a backpack
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌpækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌpækɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or practice of carrying goods or equipment in a backpack; also, travel or hiking while carrying supplies in a backpack

Examples

backpacking across Europe
He spent the summer backpacking across the Appalachian Trail with only basic supplies.
Proper backpacking requires planning, especially when traveling through remote areas.
The backpacking of heavy scientific equipment was necessary for the mountain expedition.
