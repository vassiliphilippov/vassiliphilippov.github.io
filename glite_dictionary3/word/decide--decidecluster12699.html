to decide

verb
settle or resolve conclusively
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to bring a disagreement or contest to an end by making a final judgment or choice; to settle conclusively

Examples

decide the case
After hours of debate, the jury finally decided the case.
Sometimes it's difficult for a judge to decide disputes that involve family members.
The committee will decide the outcome of the appeal next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adjudicate: to make a formal judgment or decision about a problem or disputed matter
  • antonym – reopen: to begin again after being closed or resolved
  • synonym – settle: to resolve or bring to an end a dispute or uncertainty
