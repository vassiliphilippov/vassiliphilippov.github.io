dystopian

adjective
characterized by great misery or oppression
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtoʊpiən /; / dɪˈstoʊpiən / (AmE), / dɪsˈtəʊpiən /; / dɪsˈtəʊpɪən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a society or place that is extremely bad, oppressive, or miserable, often imagined or in the future
