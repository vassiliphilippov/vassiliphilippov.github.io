vehicle

noun
object transmitting infection
Pronunciation: / ˈviːɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a substance or object that acts as a medium to carry, transmit, or distribute something (such as a drug, pigment, or infectious agent)

Examples

vehicle for medicine
Contaminated towels acted as a vehicle for the spread of the disease in the hospital.
Dishes not properly cleaned may become a vehicle of infection in restaurants.
Money can serve as a vehicle for transmitting bacteria between people.
In this ointment, an oil-based vehicle is used to carry the medication into the skin.
The choice of vehicle can affect how evenly the pigment spreads on the surface.
Water often serves as the vehicle for dyes in solutions used for scientific experiments.
