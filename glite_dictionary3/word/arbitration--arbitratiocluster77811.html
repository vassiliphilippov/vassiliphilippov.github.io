arbitration

noun
settlement of a dispute by an impartial decision
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃən /; / ˌɑːr.bɪˈtreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɑːbɪˈtreɪʃən /; / ˌɑː.bɪˈtreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of settling a dispute by having an impartial person or panel make a decision that is usually binding on all parties involved
