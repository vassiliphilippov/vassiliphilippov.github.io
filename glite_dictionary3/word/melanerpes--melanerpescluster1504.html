Melanerpes

noun
a genus of woodpeckers
Pronunciation: / mɛˈlænərˌpiːz / (AmE), / mɛˈlanəˌpiːz / (BrE)
A genus of woodpeckers found in the New World, especially in North and South America, characterized by distinctive patterns and colors.
