porticoed

adjective
having one or more porticoes
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrtɪˌkoʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtɪˌkəʊd / (BrE)
(of a building or structure) having one or more porticoes, typically with free-standing columns at the ends or sides.

Examples

porticoed entrance
Guests walked through the porticoed entrance, admiring the tall marble columns on either side.
The architect designed a porticoed facade to give the building a sense of grandeur.
The museum is housed in a porticoed neoclassical building overlooking the river.
