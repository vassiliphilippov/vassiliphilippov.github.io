Henry I

proper noun
King of England, 1100–1135
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛnri ðə ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˌhɛnri ði ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
(1068–1135) King of England from 1100 to 1135, the youngest son of William the Conqueror, known for conquering Normandy in 1106.
