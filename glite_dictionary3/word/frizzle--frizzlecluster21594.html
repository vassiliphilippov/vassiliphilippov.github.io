to frizzle

verb
to curl hair tightly
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzəl / (BrE)
to form hair or other material into tight curls or twists, often using heat or chemicals

Examples

to frizzle hair ends
