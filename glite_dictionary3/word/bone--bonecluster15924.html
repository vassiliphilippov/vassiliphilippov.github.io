to bone

verb
remove bones from meat or fish
Pronunciation: / boʊn / (AmE), / bəʊn / (BrE)
to remove the bones from (meat, fish, or poultry) before cooking or serving

Examples

to bone a chicken
It takes skill to bone a chicken without tearing the meat.
She asked the butcher to bone the leg of lamb for the meal.
The chef carefully boned the fish before baking it.

  • synonym – debone: to remove the bones from meat, fish, or poultry
