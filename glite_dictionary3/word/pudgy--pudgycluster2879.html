pudgy

adjective
slightly fat and short; plump
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdʒi / (AmE), / ˈpʌdʒi / (BrE)
(of a person or part of the body) slightly fat in a way that is not unattractive; short and plump.

Examples

pudgy fingers
He has pudgy fingers that make playing the piano a challenge.
The toddler's pudgy cheeks turned rosy when she smiled.
Their new puppy is adorable and a bit pudgy from all the treats.
