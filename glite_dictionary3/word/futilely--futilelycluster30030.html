futilely

adverb
in a useless or ineffectual way
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtəl.li, ˈfjuːtaɪl.li / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.taɪl.li / (BrE)
In a way that has no useful result; uselessly or ineffectually.

Examples

to try futilely
He argued futilely against the company's decision to close the factory.
She futilely tried to make him change his mind, but he refused to listen.
The protesters shouted futilely as the demolition crew began their work.

Related Terms

  • antonym – efficiently: in a way that achieves maximum productivity
  • synonym – ineffectively: in a way that does not produce the desired result
