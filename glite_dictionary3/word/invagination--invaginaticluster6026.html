invagination

noun
folding in to form a pocket
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌvædʒəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌvadʒɪˈneɪʃən /; / ˌɪnˌvædʒɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of a layer folding inward to form a pouch, pocket, or cavity, especially in biological structures
