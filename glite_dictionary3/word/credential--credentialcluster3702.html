credential

noun
document proving qualifications or status
Pronunciation: / krəˈdɛnʃəl / (AmE), / krɪˈdɛnʃəl / (BrE)
an official document that proves a person's qualifications, identity, or status

Examples

credential check
Applicants must submit their academic credentials with their application forms.
Her impressive credentials made her the top choice for the position.
The doctor displayed his credentials on the wall of his office.
