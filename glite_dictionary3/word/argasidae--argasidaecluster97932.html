Argasidae

noun
family of soft-bodied ticks
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈɡæsɪdi /; / ˌɑːrɡəˈsaɪdiː / (AmE), / ɑːˈɡæsɪdiː /; / ˌɑːɡəˈsiːdiː / (BrE)
a family of soft-bodied ticks with flexible or leathery bodies and no hard dorsal shield, often parasites of birds and mammals
