invalidating

adjective
causing something to be invalid or untrue
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvælɪˌdeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvælɪdeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
making something invalid or showing it to be untrue or not legally accepted.

Examples

invalidating evidence
An invalidating statement can undermine trust in scientific research.
Her constant criticism had an invalidating effect on his confidence.
The court issued an invalidating decision that rendered the law unenforceable.
