feat

noun
impressive achievement or act
Pronunciation: / fit / (AmE), / fiːt / (BrE)
an impressive or notable act or achievement, especially one requiring skill, courage, or strength

Examples

amazing feat
Climbing the mountain in winter was no ordinary feat.
The bridge is considered an engineering feat of its time.
Winning the championship was an incredible feat for the young athlete.
