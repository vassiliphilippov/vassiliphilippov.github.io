unpasteurised

adjective
not pasteurized (especially food or drink)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpæstʃəraɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɑːstjəraɪzd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) not having undergone pasteurization; not sterilized by heat treatment to destroy harmful microorganisms.

Examples

unpasteurised milk
Drinking unpasteurised milk can pose health risks due to potential bacteria.
Some health food stores sell unpasteurised juices for those seeking raw alternatives.
The cheese is made from unpasteurised milk, giving it a distinctive flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pasteurized: having undergone pasteurization
