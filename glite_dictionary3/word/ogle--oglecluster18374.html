to ogle

verb
look at with sexual interest
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊɡəl / (AmE), / ˈəʊɡl̩ / (BrE)
to look at someone in a way that shows strong sexual attraction or interest, often in a way that is considered inappropriate

Examples

to ogle a pretty girl
He couldn't help but ogle the attractive model at the party.
She caught him ogling her from across the room, which made her uncomfortable.
Tourists ogled the paintings in the museum, admiring every detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym – leer: to look at someone in an unpleasant, sexually interested way
  • synonym – stare: to look at someone or something for a long time
