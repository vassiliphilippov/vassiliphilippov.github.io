saddlecloth

noun
cloth under a horse's saddle
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəlˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈsædəlˌklɒθ / (BrE)
A thick cloth placed under a saddle on a horse's back to protect the horse and absorb sweat.

Examples

saddlecloth under saddle
After the ride, he removed the sweaty saddlecloth and hung it up to dry.
She placed a clean saddlecloth on the horse before securing the saddle.
The ornate saddlecloth was embroidered with gold thread for the parade.

  • synonym – pad: a piece of material placed beneath something for protection or comfort
