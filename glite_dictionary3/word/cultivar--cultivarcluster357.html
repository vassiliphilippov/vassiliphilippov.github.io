cultivar

noun
a plant variety produced by selective cultivation
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌl.təˌvɑr / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.tɪˌvɑː / (BrE)
A plant variety that has been produced and maintained by selective breeding and cultivation, and is distinguished by specific characteristics.

Examples

Roses are often sold under the name of a specific cultivar selected for color and hardiness.
The new cultivar of apple was praised for its crisp texture and sweet flavor.
