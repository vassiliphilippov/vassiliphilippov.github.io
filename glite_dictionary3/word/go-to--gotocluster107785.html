go-to

adjective
reliably preferred choice or resource
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊ tu /; / ɡoʊ tu / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊ tuː /; / ɡəʊ tuː / (BrE)
describing someone or something as the most reliable, trusted, or preferred option for a particular purpose or situation
