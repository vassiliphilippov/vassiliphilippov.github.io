squeeze

noun
the act of forcing through a narrow space
Pronunciation: / skwiz / (AmE), / skwiːz / (BrE)
an act of forcing oneself or something through a narrow or restricted space

Examples

squeeze in an elevator
It was a real squeeze to fit everyone into the tiny elevator.
She managed to get out, but it was quite a squeeze.
We made it under the fence, but it was a tight squeeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crush: the act of pressing or squeezing something with force
  • synonym – jam: an overcrowded or blocked situation, especially in traffic or a narrow space
