to assuage

verb
to satisfy or quench (thirst, desire)
Pronunciation: / əˈsweɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈsweɪdʒ / (BrE)
to satisfy a desire or need, such as thirst; to relieve or quench

Examples

to assuage thirst
He hoped the clear springwater would assuage his parched throat.
No amount of soda could assuage his thirst after running the marathon.
The cool lemonade assuaged her thirst after a long, hot walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym – quench: to satisfy (thirst) by drinking; to put out
