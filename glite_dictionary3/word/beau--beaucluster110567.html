beau

noun
fashionable man; a dandy
Pronunciation: / boʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ / (BrE)
a man who is very concerned with his dress and appearance; a fashionable or dandyish man

Examples

a stylish beau
A group of beaux gathered at the ball, each competing for attention.
He was a real beau, always dressed in the latest fashions.
The beau admired his reflection before heading out for the evening.
