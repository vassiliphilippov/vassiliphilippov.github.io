skimming

noun
concealment of income for tax evasion
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of failing to declare income in order to avoid paying taxes on it

Examples

skimming cash earnings
Auditors found evidence of skimming at multiple locations of the business.
