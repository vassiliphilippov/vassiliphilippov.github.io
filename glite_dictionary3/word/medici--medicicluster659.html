Medici

proper noun
powerful Florentine family; Renaissance patrons
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪtʃi / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪtʃi / (BrE)
an aristocratic Italian family of merchants and bankers who were powerful rulers of Florence during the 15th century and prominent patrons of the Renaissance.
