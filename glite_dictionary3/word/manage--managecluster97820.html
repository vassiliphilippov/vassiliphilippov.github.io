to manage

verb
be in charge of; supervise
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒ / (BrE)
to be in charge of, direct, or supervise a person, group, business, or project

Examples

to manage a team
He will manage the event to make sure everything runs smoothly.
It's not easy to manage a team with so many different personalities.
She was hired to manage the new branch and train the staff.

Related Terms

  • antonym – neglect: to fail to take proper care or responsibility for
  • synonym – oversee: to watch over and direct work or activity
  • synonym – supervise: to watch and direct the work of others
