prefabrication

noun
factory-made building sections for assembly
Pronunciation: / ˌprifæbrɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of manufacturing building sections in a factory so they can be efficiently assembled on site

Examples

prefabrication of houses
Advancements in prefabrication have improved the quality and speed of building large-scale infrastructure.
Many modern homes are built using prefabrication to reduce construction time and costs.
The company specializes in the prefabrication of modular office units for quick installation.
