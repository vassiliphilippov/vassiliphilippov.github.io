Triatoma

noun
genus of kissing bugs (insects)
Pronunciation: / traɪˈætəmə / (AmE), / trʌɪˈatəmə / (BrE)
A genus of blood-sucking insects commonly called kissing bugs or conenoses, some species of which are known to transmit Chagas disease.
