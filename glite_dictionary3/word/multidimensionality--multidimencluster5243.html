multidimensionality

noun
the state of having many aspects
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌlti.daɪˌmɛnʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪ.daɪˌmɛnʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of having many different aspects, dimensions, or facets.

Examples

multidimensionality of intelligence
Researchers explored the multidimensionality of intelligence rather than viewing it as a single trait.
She admired the multidimensionality in his art, which revealed new meanings upon each viewing.
The multidimensionality of the project made it both fascinating and challenging to all involved.
