Cajanus

noun
genus of tropical shrubby plants
Pronunciation: / kəˈdʒeɪnəs / (AmE), / kəˈdʒeɪnəs / (BrE)
A genus of erect, densely branched shrubby perennial plants native to the Old World tropics, which includes the pigeon pea; widely naturalized in other warm regions.
