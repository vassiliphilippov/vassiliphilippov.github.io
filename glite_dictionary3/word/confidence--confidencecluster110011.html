confidence

noun
numerical measure of certainty (statistics/AI)
Pronunciation: / c8k51d0n.f6a.d59ns / (AmE), / c8k52n.f6a.d59ns / (BrE)
a numerical measure (often given as a probability, score, or percentage) that expresses how certain a statistical method, model, or test is about a conclusion, prediction, or estimate.

Examples

confidence score result
The classifier returned a confidence score of 0.92 for the predicted label.
Her medical test produced a confidence of 95% that the tumor was benign.
Researchers reported low confidence in the survey results due to the small sample size.

Related Terms

  • synonym – certainty: the state of being sure about something; the higher the confidence, the greater the certainty
  • synonym – probability: a numerical measure of how likely an event or outcome is to occur; often used interchangeably with confidence in model outputs
