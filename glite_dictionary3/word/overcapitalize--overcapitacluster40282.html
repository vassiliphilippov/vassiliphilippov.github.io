to overcapitalize

verb
use too many capital letters
Pronunciation: / \u02cco\u028av\u0259r\u02c8k\u00e6p\u026at\u0259\u02ccla\u026az / (AmE), / \u02cc\u0259\u028av\u0259\u02c8k\u00e6p\u026at\u0259la\u026az / (BrE)
to use capital letters too much in writing, for example writing many words in uppercase or using ALL CAPS for emphasis or to indicate shouting.

Examples

to overcapitalize a headline
Many users overcapitalize headlines on social media to grab attention, but it can seem aggressive.
She accidentally overcapitalized the email subject line, making the message look like shouting.
Avoid overcapitalizing words in formal writing, since excessive capitals reduce readability and appear unprofessional.

Related Terms

  • synonym – write in all caps: to type or write words entirely in uppercase letters, often used for emphasis or to indicate shouting
