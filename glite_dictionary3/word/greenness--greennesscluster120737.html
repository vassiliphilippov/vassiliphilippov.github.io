greenness

noun
unripe state (of fruit/vegetables)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrin.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn.nəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of not being ripe, especially in fruit or vegetables

Examples

fruit's greenness
Due to its greenness, the fruit tasted sour and was difficult to digest.

The greenness of the bananas meant they were not yet ready to eat.
