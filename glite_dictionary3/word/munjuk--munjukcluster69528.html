Munjuk

noun
a Chadic language of Cameroon
Pronunciation: / ˈmʊn.dʒʊk / (AmE), / ˈmʌn.dʒʊk / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken south of Lake Chad, especially in the Far North Region of Cameroon, particularly in the Mayo-Danay division and Maga subdivision
