inevitable

noun
unavoidable event or outcome
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛvətəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛvɪtəbl / (BrE)
An event or outcome that cannot be avoided or prevented.

Examples

accept the inevitable
After months of tension, the workers accepted the inevitable and went on strike.
She faced the inevitable with courage and dignity.
The collapse of the old bridge was simply inevitable given its poor condition.

Related Terms

  • antonym – avoidable: able to be avoided or prevented
  • synonym – certainty: something that is certain to happen
