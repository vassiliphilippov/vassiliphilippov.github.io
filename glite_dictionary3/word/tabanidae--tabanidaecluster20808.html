Tabanidae

noun
family of horseflies
Pronunciation: / təˈbænɪˌdiː / (AmE), / təˈbanɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, biting flies known commonly as horseflies and deer flies, characterized by their stout bodies and bloodsucking habits.
