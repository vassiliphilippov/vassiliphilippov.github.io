Perisoreus canadensis

noun
North American jay species, also known as Canada jay
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrɪˈsɔːriəs ˌkænəˈdɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈsɔːrɪəs ˌkænəˈdɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a species of jay native to northern North America, known for its black-capped head, lack of a crest, and noted boldness in pilfering food
