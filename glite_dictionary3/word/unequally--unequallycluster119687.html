unequally

adverb
in an unequal manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈiːkwəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːkwəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not equal, fair, or uniform.

Examples

unequally distributed resources
Opportunities are still distributed unequally in many parts of society.
Students from different backgrounds are sometimes treated unequally in the classroom.
The inheritance was divided unequally among the siblings, causing resentment.
