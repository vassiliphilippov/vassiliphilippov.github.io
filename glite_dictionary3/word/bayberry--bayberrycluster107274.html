bayberry

noun
West Indian tree, source of bay rum
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbəri / (BrE)
A tropical West Indian tree (Pimenta racemosa), the leaves of which are the source of bay rum.

Examples

bayberry leaves oil
Local farmers harvest bayberries for their aromatic oils.
The bayberry thrives in the warm climates of the Caribbean.
The leaves of the bayberry are distilled to produce bay rum.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bay rum: a fragrant liquid used in cosmetics, prepared from the leaves of the bayberry tree
