coward

noun
person who lacks courage
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊərd / (AmE), / ˈkaʊəd / (BrE)
a person who lacks courage or is excessively afraid to face danger, difficulty, or pain

Examples

call him a coward
He was branded a coward for refusing to join the fight.
No one wanted to be called a coward in front of the others.
Sometimes being careful doesn't make you a coward; it just makes you wise.
