to regularise

verb
apply regularization to a model
Pronunciation: / \u02c8r\u025b\u0261j\u0259l\u0259r\u02cca\u026az / (AmE), / \u02c8r\u025b\u0261j\u028al\u0259\u02cca\u026az / (BrE)
(in statistics and machine learning) to apply regularization methods or penalties (for example L1 or L2 penalties, dropout) to a model or its parameters in order to limit complexity and reduce overfitting.

Examples

to regularise a model
To avoid overfitting, the team decided to regularise the neural network with an L2 penalty.
She regularised the regression model by adding dropout and L1 penalties before validation.
During training they prevented memorization by regularising parameters and early-stopping based on validation loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym – regularization: the process of adding penalties or constraints to a model to prevent overfitting
  • synonym – regularize: apply regularization (American spelling)
