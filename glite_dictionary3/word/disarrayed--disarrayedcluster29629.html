disarrayed

adjective
in disorder; untidy
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪd / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪd / (BrE)
(of something) untidy or thrown into disorder; in a state of disarray.

Examples

disarrayed hair
She quickly straightened her disarrayed hair before the video call.
The documents on his desk were disarrayed after the hurried search.
The room looked disarrayed after the children finished playing.
