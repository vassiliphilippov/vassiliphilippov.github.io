hooliganism

noun
violent or destructive behavior in groups
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːlɪˌɡænɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhuːlɪɡənɪzəm / (BrE)
violent, rowdy, or destructive behavior, especially by young people in groups.

Examples

football hooliganism
Acts of hooliganism disrupted the football match and led to several arrests.

The city council is taking measures to prevent hooliganism during public events.
