perforation

noun
line of small holes for easy tearing
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝːfəˈreɪʃən /; / ˌpɝː.fəˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɜːfəˈreɪʃən /; / ˌpɜː.fəˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a hole or a row of small holes made in a material (such as paper), especially to make tearing easier, or the act of making such holes
