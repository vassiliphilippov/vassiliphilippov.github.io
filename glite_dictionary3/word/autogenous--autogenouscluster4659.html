autogenous

adjective
originating from within (especially the body)
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈtɑːdʒənəs / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒdʒənəs / (BrE)
Originating or produced from within; occurring naturally within the body or organism rather than being caused by external factors.

Examples

autogenous healing process
Certain bacteria can produce autogenous spores under stress conditions.
