window

noun
opportune or optimal period of time
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.doʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.dəʊ / (BrE)
a period of time when a particular action can be taken or is most likely to succeed

Examples

window of opportunity
The treatment must be given within a specific window to be effective.
There was only a short window of time to submit the application.
We need to act quickly before the window of opportunity closes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – opportunity: a time or set of circumstances that makes it possible to do something
