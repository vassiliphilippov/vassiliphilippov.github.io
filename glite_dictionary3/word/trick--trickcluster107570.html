to trick

verb
deceive or mislead someone
Pronunciation: / trɪk / (AmE), / trɪk / (BrE)
to deceive or mislead someone by using cunning or deceitful methods, often to gain an advantage

Examples

to trick a friend
Don't trick people just to get what you want; it's dishonest.
He was tricked by a fake email that looked official.
She tried to trick her brother into doing her chores for her.
