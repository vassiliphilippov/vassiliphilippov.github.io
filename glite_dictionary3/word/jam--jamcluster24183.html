jam

noun
sweet preserve made from fruit and sugar
Pronunciation: / dʒæm / (AmE), / dʒam / (BrE)
a sweet preserve made by boiling crushed fruit with sugar, used especially as a spread for bread or toast

Examples

strawberry jam
She spread strawberry jam on her toast every morning.
The homemade jam was delicious and full of fruit pieces.
They bought several jars of apricot jam at the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jelly: a sweet, clear preserve made from fruit juice and sugar
  • synonym – marmalade: a preserve made from citrus fruit, especially oranges, with pieces of peel
