carpel

noun
female reproductive organ of a flower
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pəl / (BrE)
the female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of an ovary, a stigma, and typically a style, and forming part of or a simple pistil
