moderato

adjective
moderate tempo in music
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑdərˈɑtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɒdəˈrɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
(in music) at a moderate tempo; to be played neither too fast nor too slow.

Examples

moderato tempo
He asked the orchestra to maintain a moderato pace throughout the piece.
The pianist shifted from allegro to moderato for a calmer mood.
The third movement should be played moderato as indicated in the score.
