overfeeding

noun
excessive feeding; giving too much food
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈfiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈfiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving someone or something more food than is necessary or healthy

Examples

overfeeding pets
Overfeeding your pets can lead to obesity and various health problems.
Parents should avoid overfeeding their children, as it can contribute to unhealthy weight gain.
The farmer realized that overfeeding the animals was wasting resources.
