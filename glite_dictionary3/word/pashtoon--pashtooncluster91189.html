Pashtoon

noun
member of an Afghan-Pakistani ethnic group
Pronunciation: / pʌʃˈtuːn / (AmE), / pʌʃˈtuːn / (BrE)
a member of a mountain-dwelling ethnic group native mainly to eastern Afghanistan and northwestern Pakistan, known for their distinct language and culture

a Pashtoon community
