twitter

noun
high-pitched birds' chirping
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪdər / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtə / (BrE)
a series of light, rapid, high-pitched sounds, especially the chirping of small birds

Examples

a twitter of sparrows
A sudden twitter in the hedge made her stop and listen.
The forest was full of the twitter of birds at dawn.
We woke up to the gentle twitter of sparrows outside our window.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chirp: a short, sharp sound made by small birds or insects
  • synonym – warble: a continuous, trilling or quavering sound made by a bird
