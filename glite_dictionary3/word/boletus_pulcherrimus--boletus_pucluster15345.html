Boletus pulcherrimus

noun
a poisonous bolete mushroom species
Pronunciation: / boʊˈliːtəs pʊlˈkɛrɪməs / (AmE), / bəʊˈliːtəs pʊlˈkɛrɪməs / (BrE)
a species of poisonous bolete mushroom with a brown cap, scarlet pores, and a thick netted stalk, found in North America
