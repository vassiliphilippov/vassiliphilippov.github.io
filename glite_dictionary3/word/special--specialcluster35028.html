special

adjective
designed for a particular purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃ.əl /; / ˈspɛʃəl / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃ.əl /; / ˈspɛʃəl / (BrE)
different from what is usual or ordinary; unique, specific, or intended for a particular purpose, person, or occasion; sometimes more important or better than usual
