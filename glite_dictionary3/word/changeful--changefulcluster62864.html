changeful

adjective
prone to frequent change
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪn(d)ʒ.fəl / (BrE)
Likely to change; having a tendency to undergo frequent or marked alteration.
