trochanter

noun
bony prominence on the femur
Pronunciation: / troʊˈkæntər / (AmE), / trəʊˈkæntə / (BrE)
a bony prominence near the upper end of the femur to which muscles are attached

Examples

trochanter of femur
Muscles such as the gluteus medius are attached to the greater trochanter.
Pain around the trochanter can indicate bursitis or muscle strain.
The surgeon explained that the fracture was just below the trochanter of the femur.
