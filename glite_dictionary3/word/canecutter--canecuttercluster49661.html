canecutter

noun
swamp rabbit of southeastern U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪnˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a large swamp rabbit found in the southeastern United States, especially in swamps and lowlands.

Examples

canecutter in swamps
