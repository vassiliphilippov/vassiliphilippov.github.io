less

particle
comparative particle for smaller amount or degree
Pronunciation: / lɛs / (AmE), / lɛs / (BrE)
Used with adjectives and adverbs to form the comparative and indicate a smaller amount, degree, or extent.

Examples

less expensive clothes
For a healthier option, choose meals that are less salty.
If you leave earlier, you'll be less likely to hit traffic.
The hike was much less difficult than I expected.
