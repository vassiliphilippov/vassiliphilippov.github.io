transshipment

noun
transfer of goods between conveyances
Pronunciation: / trænˈʃɪpmənt / (AmE), / trænzˈʃɪpmənt / (BrE)
the action or process of transferring goods from one ship, vehicle, or conveyance to another during their journey to the final destination

Examples

transshipment of cargo
Many containers are handled for transshipment at major logistics hubs around the world.
The transshipment of goods at the port significantly increased trade efficiency.
The company specializes in the transshipment of perishable goods across continents.
