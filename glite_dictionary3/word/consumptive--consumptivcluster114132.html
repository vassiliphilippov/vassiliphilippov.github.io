consumptive

adjective
having or related to tuberculosis
Pronunciation: / kənˈsʌmptɪv / (AmE), / kənˈsʌmptɪv / (BrE)
afflicted with or related to pulmonary tuberculosis (historically called consumption)

Examples

consumptive illness
Many Victorian novels feature tragic heroines who are described as consumptive.
She developed a consumptive cough that worried her family greatly.
The sanitarium was filled with consumptive patients hoping for a cure in the mountain air.
