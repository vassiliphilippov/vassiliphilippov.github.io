mamba

noun
a venomous African snake
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːmbə / (AmE), / ˈmambə / (BrE)
a large, highly venomous, slender-bodied snake found in Central and southern Africa, known for its speed and often fatal bite

Examples

deadly mamba snake
A single bite from a mamba can be fatal without immediate medical treatment.
The black mamba is considered one of the world's deadliest snakes.
The green mamba is less aggressive than its black counterpart, but still highly venomous.
