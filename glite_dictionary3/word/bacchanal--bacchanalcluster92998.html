bacchanal

noun
wild, drunken revelry
Pronunciation: / ˌbækəˈnæl / (AmE), / ˌbakəˈnal / (BrE)
a wild and drunken celebration, often marked by excessive drinking and sexual activity

Examples

wild bacchanal party
News of the politician's attendance at a wild bacchanal shocked the public.
The ancient Romans were known to hold secret bacchanals in honor of their wine god.
The film depicts a lavish bacchanal where all social rules are cast aside.
