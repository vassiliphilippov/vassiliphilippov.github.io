rickrack

noun
zigzag-edged decorative braid or ribbon
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪkˌræk / (AmE), / ˈrɪkˌrak / (BrE)
a narrow braid or ribbon with a zigzag pattern, used as a decorative edging or trimming on clothing or fabrics

Examples

rickrack trim on clothes
She sewed rickrack along the hem of the skirt for extra decoration.

Vintage dresses often feature rickrack as a cheerful embellishment.
