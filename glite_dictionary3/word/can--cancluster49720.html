to can

verb
to dismiss from a job (informal, US)
Pronunciation: / kæn / (AmE), / kæn / (BrE)
(informal, chiefly US) to dismiss someone from their job; to fire or discharge from employment.

Examples

to can an employee
After the mistake, management decided to can the entire marketing team.
If you keep showing up late, they're going to can you.
She was canned for violating company policy last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dismiss: to remove someone from their job
  • synonym – fire: to dismiss someone from their job, especially suddenly
  • antonym – hire: to employ someone for a job
