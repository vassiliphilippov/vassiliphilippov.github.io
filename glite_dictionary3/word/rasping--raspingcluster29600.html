rasping

noun
harsh, grating vocal sound
Pronunciation: / ˈræspɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɑːspɪŋ / (BrE)
A harsh, grating sound, especially of a person's voice when speaking in an irritated or rough tone.

Examples

a rasping noise
He answered with a rasping that revealed his irritation.
Her rasping made everyone in the room turn to look at her.
The teacher's rasping carried over the noise of the classroom.
