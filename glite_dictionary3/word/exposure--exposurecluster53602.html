exposure

noun
disclosure of something secret
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊʒə / (BrE)
the act of making something known or visible, especially something secret or hidden

Examples

exposure of secrets
After the exposure of the company's illegal activities, several employees were fired.
The exposure of the politician's financial records shocked the public.
They were worried that the exposure of their plans would ruin the deal.
His sudden exposure as the author of the anonymous letters surprised the entire community.
The exposure of private details in the media can be very distressing.
The exposure of the company's financial mismanagement led to a public outcry.

  • synonym – disclosure: the act of making something known or public; revelation
  • synonym – revelation: the act of making something known or revealed
