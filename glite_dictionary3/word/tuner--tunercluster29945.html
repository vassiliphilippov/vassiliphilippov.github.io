tuner

noun
person who tunes musical instruments
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːnər / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnə / (BrE)
a person who adjusts a musical instrument so that it produces the correct pitch or tone

Examples

piano tuner
After the concert, the violin tuner made some quick adjustments backstage.
She trained as a tuner and now works for a famous orchestra.
The piano tuner visited our house every six months to keep the instrument sounding perfect.
