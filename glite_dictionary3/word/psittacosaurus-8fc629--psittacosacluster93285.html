psittacosaurus

noun
a primitive hornless dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / sɪˌtækəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / sɪˌtækəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a small, primitive genus of dinosaur from the Early Cretaceous period, distinguished by a beak-like jaw, long hind limbs, and a rudimentary frill, but lacking horns.

fossil psittacosaurus
