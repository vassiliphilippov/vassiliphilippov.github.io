cervicitis

noun
inflammation of the uterine cervix
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜr.vɪˈsaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɜː.vɪˈsaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the cervix of the uterus, often caused by infection

Examples

cervicitis from infection
