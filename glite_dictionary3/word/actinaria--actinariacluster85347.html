Actinaria

noun
order of sea anemones
Pronunciation: / ækˈtɪnəriə / (AmE), / ækˈtɪnəriə / (BrE)
An order of marine animals, commonly known as sea anemones, characterized by their colorful, fleshy bodies and numerous tentacles.
