megaton

noun
unit of explosive force (one million tons)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛgəˌtʌn /; / ˈmɛɡəˌtʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɛgətʌn /; / ˈmɛɡətʌn / (BrE)
a unit for measuring explosive power, equal to the energy released by one million tons of TNT (often used for nuclear weapons)
