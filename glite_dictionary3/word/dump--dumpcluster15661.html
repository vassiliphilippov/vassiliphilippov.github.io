to dump

verb
end a relationship abruptly
Pronunciation: / dʌmp / (AmE), / dʌmp / (BrE)
to end a relationship with someone suddenly and without consideration for their feelings; to dismiss someone unceremoniously

Examples

to dump a boyfriend
After three years together, she dumped him by text message with no explanation.
He felt terrible when his girlfriend suddenly dumped him for someone else.
The manager dumped half the team after the disappointing season.

  • synonym – dismiss: to remove someone from a job or position
  • degree lower – dump: to throw away or get rid of something unwanted
  • synonym – fire: to dismiss someone from employment
