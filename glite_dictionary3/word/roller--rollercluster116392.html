roller

noun
pigeon that somersaults in flight
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a pigeon breed known for performing backward somersaults in flight or on the ground

Examples

performing roller tricks
Enthusiasts gather every year to watch the rollers show off their aerial acrobatics.
She bred a new line of roller pigeons known for their spectacular flips.
The Birmingham roller is famous for its performances during pigeon competitions.
