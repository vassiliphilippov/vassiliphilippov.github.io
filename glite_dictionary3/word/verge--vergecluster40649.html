to verge

verb
to approach or come close to (something)
Pronunciation: / vɝdʒ / (AmE), / vɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to come very close to or almost be something, especially something bad or extreme

Examples

to verge on disaster
Her excitement verged on hysteria as the concert began.
His reckless comments verged on disrespectful.
The situation verges on chaos whenever the manager is away.
