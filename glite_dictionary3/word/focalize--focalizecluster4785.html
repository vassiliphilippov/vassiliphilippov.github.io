to focalize

verb
to become or make focused
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊkəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkəlaɪz / (BrE)
to bring something into focus, especially by adjusting a lens or making an image clear

to focalize an image

The camera lens will focalize the image automatically.

The photographer needed to focalize the image before taking the picture.
