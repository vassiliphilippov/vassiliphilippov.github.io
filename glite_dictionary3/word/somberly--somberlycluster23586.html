somberly

adverb
in a serious or solemn manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmbɚli / (AmE), / ˈsɒmbəli / (BrE)

Examples

to speak somberly

She somberly delivered the news to the team.
The crowd listened somberly as the names of the victims were read aloud.
