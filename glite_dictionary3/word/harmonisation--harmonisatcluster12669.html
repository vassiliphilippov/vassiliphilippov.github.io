harmonisation

noun
singing together in harmony
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːrmənəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌhɑrmənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhɑːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌhɑːmənaɪˈzeɪʃn / (BrE)
the act of arranging or performing music so that different parts sound good together in harmony
