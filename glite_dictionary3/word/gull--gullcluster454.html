gull

noun
a gullible or easily deceived person
Pronunciation: / ɡʌl / (AmE), / ɡʌl / (BrE)
a person who is easily deceived or tricked; someone who is gullible and easy to take advantage of

Examples

a gull for scams
Don't be such a gull; you can't believe everything you hear on the internet.
The clever con artist quickly identified a gull among the tourists.
They took him for a gull and tricked him out of his savings.
