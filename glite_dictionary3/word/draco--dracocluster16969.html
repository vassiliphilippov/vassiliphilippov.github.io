Draco

proper noun
Athenian lawmaker known for harsh laws
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdreɪkəʊ / (BrE)
An Athenian lawmaker of the 7th century BC, known for creating a harsh legal code that prescribed death for many offenses.
