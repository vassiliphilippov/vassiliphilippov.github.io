devil-may-care

adjective
cheerfully irresponsible; carefree
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛvəl meɪ ˈkɛr / (AmE), / ˌdɛvəl meɪ ˈkeə /; / ˌdɛvəl meɪ ˈkɛə / (BrE)
carefree and unconcerned about consequences; behaving in a free-spirited or reckless way
