broken-down

adjective
in very poor or dilapidated condition
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkəndaʊn /; / ˈbroʊkən daʊn / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkəndaʊn /; / ˈbrəʊkən daʊn / (BrE)
in very poor condition or not working, especially because of age or neglect (often used for machines, vehicles, or buildings)
