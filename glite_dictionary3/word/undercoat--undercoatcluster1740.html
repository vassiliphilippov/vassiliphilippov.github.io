undercoat

noun
protective coating under a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌkoʊt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkəʊt / (BrE)
a protective layer of tar or rubberlike material applied to the underside of a motor vehicle to prevent corrosion

Examples

undercoat car protection
It's important to check if your vehicle already has an undercoat before scheduling maintenance.
Regularly renewing the undercoat helps prevent rust and prolongs the vehicle's life.
The mechanic applied a fresh undercoat to protect the car during the winter months.
