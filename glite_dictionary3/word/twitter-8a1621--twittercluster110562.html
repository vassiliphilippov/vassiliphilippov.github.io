to twitter

verb
make high-pitched bird sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtwɪtə / (BrE)
(of a bird) to make a series of short, high-pitched sounds; to chirp or trill.

Examples

birds twitter in trees
Outside the window, dozens of swallows were twittering excitedly.
Sparrows twittered on the fence as the sun rose.
The birds twitter merrily in the trees each morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chirp: to make a series of short, high-pitched sounds (as a bird or insect)
  • synonym – warble: to sing softly and with a succession of constantly changing notes
