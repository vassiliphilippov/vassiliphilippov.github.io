pantomimist

noun
actor performing using gestures only
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn.tə.mɪ.mɪst / (AmE), / ˈpæn.tə.mɪ.mɪst / (BrE)
an actor who performs and conveys meaning using gesture and facial expression without spoken words
