impression

noun
dental imprint for modeling teeth/gums
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛʃən / (BrE)
(Dentistry) An imprint taken of the teeth and gums in wax or plaster to create a model for dental work.

Examples

dental impression
Accurate impressions are essential for creating well-fitting dental appliances.
Once the impression had set, the technician used it to produce a custom mouthguard.
The dentist made an impression of her teeth before fitting the crown.
