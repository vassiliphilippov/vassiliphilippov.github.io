nonsense

adjective
having no intelligible meaning
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌsɛns / (AmE), / ˈnɒnsəns / (BrE)
(of words or sounds) having no intelligible meaning; meaningless or senseless.

Examples

nonsense words invented
Children often invent nonsense words while playing games.
His answer was just a nonsense jumble that left us confused.
The poem was filled with nonsense syllables that nobody could interpret.
