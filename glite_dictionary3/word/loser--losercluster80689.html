loser

noun
person who consistently fails
Pronunciation: / ˈluːzər / (AmE), / ˈluːzə / (BrE)
a person who is habitually unsuccessful or considered a failure, often used informally or as an insult

She refuses to think of herself as a loser, despite several setbacks.
