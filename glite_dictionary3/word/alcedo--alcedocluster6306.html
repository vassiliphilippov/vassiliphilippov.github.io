Alcedo

noun
genus of kingfishers (birds)
Pronunciation: / ælˈsiːdoʊ / (AmE), / ælˈsiːdəʊ / (BrE)
The type genus of the kingfisher family (Alcedinidae), comprising certain species of kingfishers, including the common kingfisher.
