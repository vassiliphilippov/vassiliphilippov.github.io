matted

adjective
not glossy; not reflecting light
Pronunciation: / ˈmætɪd / (AmE), / ˈmætɪd / (BrE)
(of a surface) not shiny or glossy; not reflecting light.

Examples

matted surface finish
A matted finish on the photograph helped reduce glare from overhead lights.
She chose matted wall paint rather than a glossy one for a more modern look.
The artist preferred matted paper to give the drawing a subtle, muted appearance.
