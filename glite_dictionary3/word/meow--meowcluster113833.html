meow

noun
cat's vocal sound
Pronunciation: / miˈaʊ / (AmE), / mɪˈaʊ / (BrE)
the sound that a cat makes, or any sound resembling it

Examples

loud meow

Her ringtone is a realistic meow that always confuses her friends.
The loud meow from the kitchen told me the cat was hungry.
