death

noun
end or destruction of something
Pronunciation: / dɛθ / (AmE), / dɛθ / (BrE)
The end or destruction of something; the time when something ceases to exist or operate.

Examples

death of tradition
Her resignation signaled the death of the old leadership style.
The scandal marked the death of the politician's career.
