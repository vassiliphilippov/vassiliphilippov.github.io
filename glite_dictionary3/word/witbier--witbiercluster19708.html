witbier

noun
Belgian-style wheat beer
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪt.bɪr / (AmE), / ˈvɪt.bɪə / (BrE)
A pale, cloudy wheat beer of Belgian origin, typically brewed with barley, wheat, and spices such as coriander and orange peel.
