uncommitted

adjective
not busy or already engaged
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not busy or occupied; not already engaged or pledged to another task or activity.

Examples

uncommitted staff members
All employees were assigned tasks except for a few uncommitted staff members.
Because her time was uncommitted, she agreed to help organize the event.
I have two uncommitted hours this afternoon if you want to meet.
