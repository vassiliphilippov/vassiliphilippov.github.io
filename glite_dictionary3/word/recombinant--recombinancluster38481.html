recombinant

adjective
produced by DNA recombination
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑːmbɪnənt / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒmbɪnənt / (BrE)
relating to or resulting from the combination of genetic material from different sources, especially as achieved by genetic engineering

Examples

recombinant DNA technology
A recombinant vaccine was developed using advanced genetic techniques.
Scientists studied the effects of recombinant DNA technology on crop improvement.
The production of recombinant proteins has revolutionized pharmaceutical research.
