unstoppered

adjective
having the stopper removed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑpərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɒpəd / (BrE)
(of a container) having had its stopper removed.

Examples

unstoppered bottle
She left the unstoppered bottle of perfume on her dresser overnight.
The scent of vinegar filled the air from the unstoppered jar.
Wine began to oxidize after the unstoppered jug was forgotten on the counter.
