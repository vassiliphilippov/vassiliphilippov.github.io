programme

noun
event schedule booklet
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
a printed or written booklet that gives details of the events, performers, and schedule at a theatrical, sporting, or similar event

Examples

programme booklet details
Please check the programme for details about tonight's musical performances.
The theater handed out a programme to each attendee before the show began.
Without a programme, it's hard to know when each speaker is scheduled.
