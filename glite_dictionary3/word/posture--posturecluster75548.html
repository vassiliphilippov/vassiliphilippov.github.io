posture

noun
adopted mental attitude or stance
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːstʃər / (AmE), / ˈpɒstʃə / (BrE)
a particular mental attitude or approach deliberately adopted towards something, often for a specific purpose

Examples

posture on an issue
His political posture shifted as public opinion changed.
She criticized the government's posture on climate change as too passive.
The company adopted a tough posture towards negotiations with the union.

Related Terms

  • synonym – approach: a way of dealing with something
  • synonym – attitude: a settled way of thinking or feeling about something
  • synonym – stance: a publicly stated opinion or attitude
