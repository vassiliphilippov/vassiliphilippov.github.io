Geronimo

proper noun
famous Apache leader (1829–1909)
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈrɑːnɪˌmoʊ / (AmE), / dʒəˈrɒnɪməʊ / (BrE)
(1829–1909) A prominent Apache leader who led resistance against U.S. and Mexican forces and opposed the confinement of his people to reservations.
