over

adverb
throughout a period of time
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
Used to indicate that something happens during the whole period of a stated time.

Examples

over the years
Attendance increased steadily over the last five years.
She completed the assignment over the weekend.
They worked over the summer to finish the project.
