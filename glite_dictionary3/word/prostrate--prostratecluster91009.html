prostrate

adjective
lying stretched out on the ground
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.streɪt / (AmE), / ˈprɒs.treɪt / (BrE)
lying stretched out on the ground, especially face downward, often as a sign of exhaustion, submission, or adoration
