branded

adjective
marked with a brand name or trademark
Pronunciation: / ˈbrændɪd / (AmE), / ˈbrændɪd / (BrE)
(of goods) marked or labeled with a distinctive name or symbol, usually indicating exclusive rights or trademark.

Examples

branded clothing line
Many consumers prefer branded products because they trust the quality.
Some stores sell both branded and generic items to cater to different customers.
The market for branded clothing has grown rapidly in recent years.
