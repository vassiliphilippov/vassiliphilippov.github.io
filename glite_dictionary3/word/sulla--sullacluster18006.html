Sulla

proper noun
Roman general and dictator (138–78 BC)
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlə / (AmE), / ˈsʌlə / (BrE)
(138–78 BC) A Roman general and dictator, known for his military campaigns and leadership during the late Roman Republic.
