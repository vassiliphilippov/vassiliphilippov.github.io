LGB

noun
laser-guided bomb (military term)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛl.dʒiːˈbiː / (AmE), / ˌɛl.dʒiːˈbiː / (BrE)
abbreviation for a laser-guided bomb, a type of smart bomb that seeks and follows the laser light reflected from its designated target in order to correct and guide its descent.

Examples

LGB strike target
Cloud cover can interfere with the effectiveness of an LGB during an air raid.
Modern warfare often relies on LGBs for precision strikes against high-value targets.
The fighter jet launched an LGB to accurately hit the bunker.
