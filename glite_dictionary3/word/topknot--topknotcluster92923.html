topknot

noun
decorative knot or tuft on the head
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑpˌnɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɒp.nɒt / (BrE)
a knot or tuft of hair or feathers arranged on the top of the head, often as a decorative feature

Examples

hair in a topknot
He pulled his long hair into a topknot before heading to practice.
Some birds have a distinctive topknot of feathers on their heads.
The dancer wore a bright red ribbon around her topknot.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bun: a hairstyle in which the hair is drawn back and twisted into a round coil at the back or top of the head
