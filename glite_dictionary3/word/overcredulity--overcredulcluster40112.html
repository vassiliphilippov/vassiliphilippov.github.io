overcredulity

noun
excessive willingness to believe
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərkrəˈduːləti / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkrɪˈdjuːlɪti / (BrE)
Excessive readiness or willingness to believe things; too much credulity.

Examples

danger of overcredulity
He regretted his overcredulity after believing so many unfounded rumors.
Her overcredulity made her an easy target for scams and manipulative people.
The journalist warned against overcredulity when reading sensational news reports online.
