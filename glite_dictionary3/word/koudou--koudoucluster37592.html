koudou

noun
African spiral-horned antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːduː / (AmE), / ˈkuːduː / (BrE)
either of two large African antelopes with long, twisted horns and distinctive stripes, native to the bush country of Africa

a koudou antelope
