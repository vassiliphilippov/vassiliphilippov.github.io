tenderness

noun
pain on touching a body part
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛndɚnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛndənəs / (BrE)
pain felt when an area of the body is touched, often indicating inflammation or sensitivity

Examples

tenderness in muscles
She complained of tenderness in her throat when swallowing.
Swelling and tenderness around the wound suggested a possible infection.
The doctor checked for tenderness in his abdomen during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sensitivity: state of being easily affected or hurt, especially to touch
  • synonym – soreness: pain or discomfort in a part of the body
