glaring

adjective
shining intensely; very bright
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlerɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡleərɪŋ / (BrE)
shining with a very strong or dazzling light; intensely bright or blinding

Examples

glaring sunlight
Snow on the mountain reflected a glaring light that made it hard to keep my eyes open.
The glaring sun forced us to shield our eyes as we walked outside.
The headlights were so glaring that I had to pull over until I could see clearly again.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blinding: making it difficult to see because of strong light
  • synonym – dazzling: extremely bright and hard to look at
  • antonym – dim: not shining brightly or clearly
