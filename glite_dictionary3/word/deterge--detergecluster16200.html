to deterge

verb
cleanse by washing off impurities
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɜrdʒ / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to cleanse or wash away impurities, especially by removing foul matter from the body; chiefly used in medical contexts
