stout-stemmed

adjective
having a thick, strong stem (of a plant)
Pronunciation: / staʊt stɛmd / (AmE), / staʊt stɛmd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having a thick and strong stem.

Examples

Gardeners prefer stout-stemmed varieties for cut flowers because they are less likely to droop.
