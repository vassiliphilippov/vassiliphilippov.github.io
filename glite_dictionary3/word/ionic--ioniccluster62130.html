Ionic

noun
Ancient Greek dialect of Ionia and Attica
Pronunciation: / aɪˈɑnɪk / (AmE), / aɪˈɒnɪk / (BrE)
the form of Ancient Greek spoken and written in the region of Ionia and, with some variation, in Attica and Athens

Ionic dialect studies
