Anne Hutchinson

proper noun
American religious dissenter and colonist
Pronunciation: / æn ˈhʌtʃɪnsən / (AmE), / æn ˈhʌtʃɪnsən / (BrE)
(1591–1643) American colonist and religious leader born in England, known for her role in the Antinomian Controversy and banishment from the Massachusetts Bay Colony for her religious beliefs.

Anne Hutchinson challenged authority
