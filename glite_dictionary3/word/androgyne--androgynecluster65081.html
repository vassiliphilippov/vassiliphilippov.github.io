androgyne

noun
person with both male and female sexual characteristics
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.drəˌdʒaɪn / (AmE), / ˈan.drə.dʒʌɪn / (BrE)
a person who has both male and female sexual characteristics and organs, making gender assignment at birth ambiguous
