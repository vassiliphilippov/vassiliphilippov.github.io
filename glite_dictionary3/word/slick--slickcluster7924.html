slick

noun
large trowel for smoothing surfaces
Pronunciation: / slɪk / (AmE), / slɪk / (BrE)
A type of large, flat-bladed trowel used by masons or bricklayers to make surfaces smooth or slick.

Examples

mason's slick
Careful use of a slick gives the wall a professional, polished appearance.
He accidentally dropped his slick while working on the brickwork.
The mason reached for the slick to finish smoothing the fresh cement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – trowel: a small hand tool with a flat blade used for spreading and smoothing mortar or plaster
