acidimetry

noun
analysis of bases using standard acid solutions
Pronunciation: / ˌæsɪˈdɪmɪtri / (AmE), / ˌasɪˈdɪmɪtri / (BrE)
the process of determining the amount of a base in a substance by measuring with a standard acid solution

Examples

acidimetry in chemical analysis
Accurate acidimetry requires careful preparation of the standard acid solutions.
Students in the chemistry lab learned how to use acidimetry for quantitative analysis.
The technician performed acidimetry to determine the concentration of the base in the solution.
