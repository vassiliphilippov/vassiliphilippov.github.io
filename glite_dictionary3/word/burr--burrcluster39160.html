burr

noun
prickly seed vessel or fruit
Pronunciation: / bɜr / (AmE), / bɜː / (BrE)
a rough or prickly seed vessel or fruit that clings to animal fur or clothing

Examples

prickly burr seed
As we hiked through the woods, I picked a burr off my sock nearly every mile.
Some plants develop a burr to help their seeds spread by attaching to animals.
The dog came back from the field with several burrs stuck in its fur.
