to read

verb
look at and understand written text
Pronunciation: / ɹiːd /; / riːd / (AmE), / riːd / (BrE)
to look at and understand the meaning of written or printed words or symbols (silently or in a particular language)

Examples

to read a book
He reads the newspaper every morning with his coffee.
I read the instructions carefully before assembling the furniture.
She likes to read novels before going to sleep.
It took him years to read Japanese newspapers with ease.
Many children learn to read English at an early age.
She can read both Russian and French fluently.

  • synonym – peruse: to read carefully or thoroughly
  • synonym – scan: to look through written material quickly
  • synonym – understand: to grasp the meaning of something
