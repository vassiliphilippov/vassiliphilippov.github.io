aristocratic

adjective
of or relating to the aristocracy
Pronunciation: / ˌærəstəˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌærɪstəˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
belonging to or typical of the aristocracy or people of high social rank; having manners or qualities associated with the nobility

Examples

aristocratic family background
His aristocratic bearing set him apart at the informal gathering.
She was raised in an aristocratic family with a long history and strict traditions.
The mansion was filled with aristocratic portraits, fine furniture, and rare books.
