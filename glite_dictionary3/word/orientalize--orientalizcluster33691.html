to orientalize

verb
make more Asian in character or style
Pronunciation: / ɔːrˈjɛntəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˌɔːrɪˈɛntəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something have characteristics associated with the cultures of East Asia or Asia more broadly

Examples

to orientalize a room
She decided to orientalize her garden by planting cherry blossoms and adding a koi pond.
Some artists in the 19th century tried to orientalize their work to appeal to Western fascination with Asia.
The designer worked to orientalize the room by adding bamboo screens and lanterns.

Related Terms

  • antonym – westernize: to make something have characteristics associated with Western cultures
