preserves

noun
sweet spread made from fruit and sugar
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɝːvz / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːvz / (BrE)
A type of sweet spread made by cooking fruit with sugar, often with fruit pieces remaining, and used as a condiment or spread.

Examples

preserves on toast
She spread homemade preserves on her toast every morning.
The farm stand sells a variety of berry preserves and local honey.
Would you like some strawberry preserves with your scone?
