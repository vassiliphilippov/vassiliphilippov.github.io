ha-ha

noun
landscape ditch with one vertical wall
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑ hɑ / (AmE), / ˈhɑː hɑː / (BrE)
a ditch with one side as a vertical retaining wall, used in gardens or parks to divide land or mark a boundary without blocking the view
