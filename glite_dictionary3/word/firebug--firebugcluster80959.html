firebug

noun
person who sets fires illegally
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a person who illegally sets fire to property; an arsonist

Examples

to catch a firebug
As a child, he was fascinated by flames, but as an adult, he became a notorious firebug.
Police are searching for the firebug responsible for a string of arson attacks in the neighborhood.
The abandoned warehouse was destroyed by a firebug last night.
