thoughtfulness

noun
kind and considerate regard for others
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔt.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.fəl.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being kind and considerate of others' needs and feelings, or an act that shows this

Examples

thoughtfulness toward others
A moment of thoughtfulness can make someone's entire day better.
His thoughtfulness was evident in the little gifts he brought for everyone.
She was touched by the thoughtfulness of her friends during her illness.
He thanked her for her thoughtfulness in remembering his birthday.
Her thoughtfulness in bringing soup when I was sick meant a great deal to me.
The thoughtfulness of his gesture made her smile.
