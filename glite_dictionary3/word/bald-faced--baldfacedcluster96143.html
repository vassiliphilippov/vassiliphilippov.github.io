bald-faced

adjective
having white markings on the face (animal)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔld ˌfeɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɔːld ˌfeɪst / (BrE)
(of an animal) having white markings on the face, especially as a distinct patch or blaze.

Examples

bald-faced Hereford cattle
That rancher breeds bald-faced Hereford cattle known for their distinctive facial markings.
The horse stood out with its striking bald-faced appearance.
We admired the elegant, bald-faced mare grazing in the field.
