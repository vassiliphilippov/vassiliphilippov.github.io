plowshare

noun
cutting blade of a plow
Pronunciation: / ˈplaʊˌʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈplaʊˌʃeə / (BrE)
the main cutting blade of a plow, designed to slice through and loosen the top layer of soil

Examples
