uninventive

adjective
lacking originality or creativity
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈvɛntɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvɛntɪv / (BrE)
lacking originality or creative ability; not inventive or imaginative.

Examples

uninventive approach
He was known for his uninventive style, sticking closely to old techniques.
Her approach to solving the problem was disappointingly uninventive.
The film received criticism for its uninventive plot and flat characters.
