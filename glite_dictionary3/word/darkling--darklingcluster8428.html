darkling

adjective
happening in the dark or night (poetic)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːrklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːklɪŋ / (BrE)
(poetic) Occurring in the dark or at night; happening during darkness.

Examples

darkling night sky
A darkling silence fell over the quiet, sleeping city.
The poet wrote of darkling woods where shadows danced.
They set off on a darkling adventure, guided only by the moon.
