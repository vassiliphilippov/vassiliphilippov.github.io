cooperativeness

noun
willingness to cooperate
Pronunciation: / kəˈɑːpərətɪvnəs / (AmE), / kəʊˈɒpərətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to work with others or act together for a common purpose

Examples

team cooperativeness
Her cooperativeness made the entire project much easier to complete.
The team's cooperativeness impressed the organizers of the event.
We value cooperativeness as an essential quality in our employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym – collaboration: the act of working with someone to produce or create something
  • antonym – uncooperativeness: the quality of being unwilling to work with others
