redemption

noun
exchanging loyalty points for rewards
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdɛmpʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈdɛmpʃən / (BrE)
the act of exchanging loyalty points, reward credits, vouchers, or similar accumulated rewards for goods, services, discounts, or other benefits.

Examples

redemption of points
After saving enough miles, she requested a flight redemption to use her frequent-flyer rewards.
The app shows available items for redemption when customers reach the required points threshold.
Points from the supermarket card can be used for gift-card redemption at self-service machines.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exchange: to give something and receive something else in return
  • synonym – redeem: to exchange something (such as a coupon or points) for goods, services, or value
