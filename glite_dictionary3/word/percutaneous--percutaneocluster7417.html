percutaneous

adjective
performed or applied through the skin
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝː.kjuːˈteɪ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.kjʊˈteɪ.nɪ.əs / (BrE)
(of a procedure or medication) performed or applied through the unbroken skin, without incision.

Examples

percutaneous injection procedure
Nicotine is commonly administered via percutaneous patches that adhere to the skin.
Recent advances allow for percutaneous procedures that minimize recovery time compared to traditional surgery.
The doctor recommended a percutaneous injection to deliver the medication directly into the bloodstream.
