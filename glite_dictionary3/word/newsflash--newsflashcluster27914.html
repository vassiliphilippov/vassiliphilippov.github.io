newsflash

noun
short, sudden news announcement
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌflæʃ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌflæʃ / (BrE)
a short and sudden news announcement reporting recent or important events

Examples

breaking newsflash
A newsflash interrupted the television show to report breaking news about the earthquake.
Everyone in the office stopped to listen to the latest newsflash on the financial crisis.
The radio played a newsflash about the sudden resignation of the prime minister.
