visualization

noun
forming a mental image
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪʒuələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɪʒuː(ə)laɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act or process of forming a mental image of something, especially as an aid to understanding or achieving a goal

Examples

mental visualization exercise
Good visualization can help manage anxiety by picturing calm and peaceful scenes.
Many athletes use visualization techniques to enhance their performance before a competition.
Through visualization, he imagined himself successfully giving the big presentation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – hallucination: an experience of seeing something that is not present outside the mind
  • synonym – imagination: the ability to form new ideas or images in the mind
