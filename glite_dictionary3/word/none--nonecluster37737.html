none

pronoun
not any; no one
Pronunciation: / nʌn / (AmE), / nʌn / (BrE)
not any; not one of a group of people or things; no person or thing.

Examples

none of them
None of the students completed the assignment by the deadline.
Of all the options available, none appealed to her.
When asked how many tickets were left, he replied that none remained.

  • antonym – some: an unspecified amount or number of people or things
