roadless

adjective
without roads or pathways
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdləs / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdləs / (BrE)
(of an area or place) without roads; having no roads or constructed pathways.

Examples

roadless wilderness area
Much of the national park remains roadless and untouched by development.
The proposal would protect millions of acres of roadless wilderness from logging.
They trekked across a roadless expanse to reach the remote village.
