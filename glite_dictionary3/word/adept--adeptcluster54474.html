adept

noun
highly skilled person
Pronunciation: / əˈdɛpt / (AmE), / əˈdɛpt / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled or proficient in a particular activity or field

Examples

an adept at chess
As an adept in the kitchen, he can prepare gourmet meals with ease.
She is an adept at solving complex puzzles that stump others.
The journalist interviewed an adept in artificial intelligence for the article.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expert: a person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • antonym – novice: a person new to a field or activity, lacking experience
