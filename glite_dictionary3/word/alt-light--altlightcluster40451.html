alt-light

noun
right-wing subculture, distinct from 'alt-right'
Pronunciation: / ɑltˈlaɪt / (AmE), / ɒltˈlaɪt / (BrE)
A loosely defined political grouping or subculture characterized by right-leaning views that reject overtly extremist or white nationalist positions associated with the 'alt-right'.
