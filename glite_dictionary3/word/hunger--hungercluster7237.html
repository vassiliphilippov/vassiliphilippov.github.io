to hunger

verb
to strongly crave or desire
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡə / (BrE)
to have a strong craving or great desire for something, especially food or success

Examples

to hunger for success
As the days passed, he hungered for news from home.
Many people hunger for success in their chosen fields.
She hungered for adventure and new experiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crave: to have an intense desire for something
  • synonym – yearn: to have an intense feeling of longing for something
