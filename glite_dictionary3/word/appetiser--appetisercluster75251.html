appetiser

noun
small dish served before a meal
Pronunciation: / ˈæpɪˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈæpɪtaɪzə / (BrE)
A small dish or drink served before the main part of a meal to stimulate the appetite.

Examples

light appetiser before meal
Each guest was served a light appetiser to start the dinner.
Stuffed mushrooms are my favourite appetiser at this restaurant.
We ordered a delicious appetiser before the main course arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym – appetizer: small dish served before a meal (American spelling)
  • antonym – dessert: sweet course served at the end of a meal
  • antonym – main course: the principal dish of a meal
