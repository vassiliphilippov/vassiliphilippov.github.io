two-sided

adjective
having two sides or aspects
Pronunciation: / ˈtuˌsaɪdəd /; / ˈtuː ˌsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˌtuːˈsaɪdɪd /; / ˈtuː ˌsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
having two sides or surfaces, or able to be used on both sides (such as tape, fabric, or objects)
