eccentrically

adverb
in a way not centered; off-center
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛntrɪkli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛntrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is not symmetrically positioned with respect to the center; off-center or away from the central axis.

Examples

rotates eccentrically
The gear rotates eccentrically when the shaft is misaligned.
The painting was hung eccentrically on the wall, giving the room a quirky look.
The wheel spun eccentrically, causing a noticeable wobble.

Related Terms

  • antonym – symmetrically: in a symmetrical, centered manner
