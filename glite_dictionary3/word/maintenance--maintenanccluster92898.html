maintenance

noun
court-ordered support for a former spouse
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪntənəns / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnt(ə)nəns / (BrE)
money that a court orders one person to pay regularly to their former spouse after separation or divorce, to support them financially.

Examples

court-ordered maintenance
After the divorce, he was required to pay maintenance to his ex-wife every month.
She relies on maintenance payments to help cover the household expenses.
The couple went to court to determine the amount of maintenance owed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alimony: court-ordered payment from one spouse to another after divorce or separation
  • synonym – child support: court-ordered payments to support a child after parents separate
