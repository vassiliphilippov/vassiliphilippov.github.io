bearer

noun
person carrying a coffin at a funeral
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrər / (AmE), / ˈbeə.rə /; / ˈbeərə / (BrE)
a person who carries or presents something, such as a message, an object, or as part of a ceremony

No one likes to be the bearer of bad tidings, but someone must deliver the message.
The bearer of this letter is authorized to collect the parcel on my behalf.
