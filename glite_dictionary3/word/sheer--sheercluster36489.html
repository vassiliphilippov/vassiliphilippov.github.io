sheer

adjective
pure, not mixed with anything else
Pronunciation: / ʃɪr / (AmE), / ʃɪə / (BrE)
(of a substance or thing) not mixed with anything else; pure or unadulterated.

Examples

sheer luck

The cave was illuminated by sheer sunlight streaming through the opening.
