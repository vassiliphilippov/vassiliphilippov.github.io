reunion

noun
gathering of former associates
Pronunciation: / riˈjunjən / (AmE), / riˈjuːniən / (BrE)
A gathering or party of people who were previously associated, such as classmates or family members, who meet after a period of separation.

Examples

family reunion party
Our high school reunion was a wonderful opportunity to reconnect with old friends.
She traveled across the country to attend her family reunion last summer.
The college alumni planned a reunion to celebrate the university's centenary.

  • synonym – reunite: to come together again after being apart
