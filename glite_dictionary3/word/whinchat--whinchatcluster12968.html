whinchat

noun
European songbird of grassy meadows
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tʃæt / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tʃæt / (BrE)
A small brown-and-buff European songbird found in grassy meadows, known for its distinctive call.

The whinchat is less common in cities than in rural grasslands.
