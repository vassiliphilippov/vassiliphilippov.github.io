authorization

noun
the right or power to make decisions
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌɔːθərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
official permission, approval, or power to do something
