clotbur

noun
Eurasian plant with burrs
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑtˌbɜr / (AmE), / ˈklɒtˌbɜː / (BrE)
a biennial herb native to temperate Eurasia, with a stout taproot and burr-like seed heads that stick to animals or clothing
