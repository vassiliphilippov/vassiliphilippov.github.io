spidery

adjective
resembling a spider or its web
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdəri / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəri / (BrE)
thin, delicate, or having an appearance resembling that of a spider or its web.

Examples

spidery handwriting
Long, spidery cracks ran across the old plaster ceiling.
She wrote her signature in a tiny, spidery script across the page.
The plant's spidery leaves gave it a delicate appearance.
