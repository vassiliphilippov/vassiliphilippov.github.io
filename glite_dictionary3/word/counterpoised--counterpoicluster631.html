counterpoised

adjective
balanced by an opposing weight
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚˌpɔɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.təˌpɔɪzd / (BrE)
brought into balance or equilibrium by an opposing weight or force

Examples

counterpoised by opposing force
Political power in the country was counterpoised by the influence of the judiciary.
The sculpture was carefully counterpoised on a narrow pedestal, so it appeared almost to float.
The team’s strengths were counterpoised by a few notable weaknesses.
