to enrich

verb
improve or enhance the quality of something
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈrɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪnˈrɪtʃ / (BrE)
to improve the quality of something, often by adding something to it

Examples

to enrich your knowledge
Her travels have greatly enriched her perspective on the world.
Reading widely can enrich your vocabulary and expand your knowledge.
They use special fertilizers to enrich the soil before planting crops.

Related Terms

  • antonym – degrade: to lower the quality of something
  • synonym – improve: to make something better
