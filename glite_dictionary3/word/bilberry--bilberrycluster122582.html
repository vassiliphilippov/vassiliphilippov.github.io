bilberry

noun
edible dark blue berry or its shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪlˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.bəri / (BrE)
a small, dark blue edible berry, or the low-growing shrub that produces it, found especially in Europe and North America and similar to a blueberry
