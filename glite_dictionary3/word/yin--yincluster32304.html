yin

noun
dark, feminine force in Chinese philosophy
Pronunciation: / jɪn / (AmE), / jɪn / (BrE)
(in Chinese philosophy) the dark, passive, and feminine principle of the universe, which interacts with yang to maintain harmony in the cosmos.
