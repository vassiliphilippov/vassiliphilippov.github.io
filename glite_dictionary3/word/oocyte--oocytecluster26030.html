oocyte

noun
immature female egg cell
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.ə.saɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.ə.saɪt / (BrE)
A female cell in an ovary that may develop into an ovum after completing two meiotic divisions.

Examples

human oocyte
Genetic mutations in the oocyte can affect fertility in mammals.
Scientists observed the development of a human oocyte under the microscope.
The oocyte matures inside the ovary before it is released during ovulation.
