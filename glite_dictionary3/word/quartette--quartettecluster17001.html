quartette

noun
group of four people or things
Pronunciation: / kwɔrˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɔːˈtɛt / (BrE)
a set of four people or things considered as a single unit, especially in music or art

Examples

quartette of chairs
He bought a quartette of matching chairs for the dining room.
The museum displayed a quartette of paintings by the same artist.
The renowned string quartette performed at the concert hall last night.

  • synonym – quartet: a group or set of four, especially musicians or singers
