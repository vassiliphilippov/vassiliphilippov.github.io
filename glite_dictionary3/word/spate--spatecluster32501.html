spate

noun
a large number occurring quickly
Pronunciation: / speɪt / (AmE), / speɪt / (BrE)
a large number of similar things or events occurring in quick succession, especially unpleasant ones

Examples

a spate of problems
A spate of new restaurants has opened downtown this month.
After the election, there was a spate of resignations across the company.
The city has experienced a spate of burglaries in recent weeks.

  • synonym – rush: a sudden large number or influx
