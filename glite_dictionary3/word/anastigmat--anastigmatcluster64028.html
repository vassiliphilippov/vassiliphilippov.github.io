anastigmat

noun
astigmatism-correcting compound lens
Pronunciation: / əˈnæstɪɡˌmæt / (AmE), / əˈnastɪɡmat / (BrE)
A compound lens or lens system designed to correct astigmatism, producing nearly point images.

Examples

anastigmat camera lens
An anastigmat is essential in many professional microscopes to ensure clarity.
Many early photographic lenses suffered from distortion until the invention of the anastigmat.
The camera was fitted with an anastigmat to achieve sharper images.
