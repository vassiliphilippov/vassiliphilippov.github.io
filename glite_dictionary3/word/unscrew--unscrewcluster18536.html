to unscrew

verb
loosen or remove by turning
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskru / (AmE), / ʌnˈskruː / (BrE)
to loosen or remove something by turning it, especially something that is screwed in (like a lid, cap, or fastener)

Examples

to unscrew a jar lid

Before replacing the bulb, make sure to unscrew the old one carefully.
