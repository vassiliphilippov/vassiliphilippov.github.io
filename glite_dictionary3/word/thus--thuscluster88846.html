thus

noun
aromatic resin used in worship or fumigation
Pronunciation: / ðʌs / (AmE), / ðʌs / (BrE)
an aromatic gum resin obtained from various Arabian or East African trees, formerly used in religious ceremonies, embalming, and fumigation; also known as frankincense

Examples

burn thus incense
Boxes of rare thus were imported from distant lands for the royal ceremony.
In ancient temples, priests would burn thus as an offering to the gods.
The embalming process included the use of thus to preserve the body.
