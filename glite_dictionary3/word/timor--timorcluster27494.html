Timor

proper noun
island in Indonesia/Malay Archipelago
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːmɔr / (AmE), / ˈtiːmɔː / (BrE)
an island in southeastern Asia, lying in the Malay Archipelago; the largest and easternmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands, divided between Timor-Leste (East Timor) and Indonesia.

After visiting Bali, she continued her travels eastward to Timor.
