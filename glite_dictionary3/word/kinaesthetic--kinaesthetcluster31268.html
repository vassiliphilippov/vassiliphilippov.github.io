kinaesthetic

adjective
relating to the sense of movement
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪnəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɪnɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the perception of body movements, position, or muscular tension; connected with the sense of physical movement
