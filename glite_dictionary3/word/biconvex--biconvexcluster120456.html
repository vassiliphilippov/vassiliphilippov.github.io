biconvex

adjective
convex on both sides
Pronunciation: / baɪˈkɑnvɛks / (AmE), / baɪˈkɒnvɛks / (BrE)
having a surface that is convex on both sides, like the shape of a lens or lentil.

Examples

biconvex lens shape
Microscopes often use a biconvex glass to magnify tiny objects.
The biconvex lens focuses light rays to a single point.
The seed was biconvex in shape, resembling a small lentil.
