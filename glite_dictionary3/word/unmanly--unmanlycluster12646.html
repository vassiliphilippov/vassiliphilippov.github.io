unmanly

adjective
not having masculine qualities
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmanli / (BrE)
not having or showing qualities like strength, courage, or resolve that are traditionally considered masculine; weak or cowardly

Examples

an unmanly act
Calling him unmanly for refusing to fight is both unfair and outdated.
He felt his fear was unmanly and tried to hide it from his friends.
Some people still consider crying in public to be unmanly, though attitudes are changing.
His unmanly behavior during the crisis disappointed his friends.
She dismissed his fears as unmanly and urged him to be braver.
The film critic described the hero's retreat as cowardly and unmanly.
