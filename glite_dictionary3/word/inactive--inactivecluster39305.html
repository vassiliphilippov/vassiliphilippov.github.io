inactive

adjective
not progressing (medical condition)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈæktɪv / (BrE)
(of a disease or medical condition) not progressing or increasing, or progressing very slowly.

Examples

inactive disease
After treatment, her hepatitis became inactive, greatly reducing her symptoms.
Doctors monitor chronic illnesses to ensure they stay inactive and do not worsen.
The patient's cancer has remained inactive for several years, showing no signs of growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym – active: progressing or increasing (of a disease)
