clavicle

noun
bone connecting shoulder blade and sternum
Pronunciation: / ˈklævɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈklævɪkəl / (BrE)
A long bone that serves as a strut between the shoulder blade and the sternum.

Examples

broken clavicle
Doctors examined the patient's clavicle for signs of injury.
He fractured his clavicle while playing football.
The clavicle is commonly known as the collarbone.
