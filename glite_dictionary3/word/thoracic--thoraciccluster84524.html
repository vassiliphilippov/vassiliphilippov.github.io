thoracic

adjective
relating to the chest or thorax
Pronunciation: / θɔˈræsɪk / (AmE), / θəˈræsɪk / (BrE)
relating to the chest or thorax, especially the part of the body between the neck and the abdomen in humans and other animals.

Examples

thoracic vertebra
A thoracic vertebra is one of the twelve bones making up the central part of the spine.
He experienced severe pain in the thoracic region after the accident.
The surgeon performed a thoracic procedure to repair the patient's lungs.
