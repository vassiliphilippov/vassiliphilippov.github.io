paramilitary

noun
civilian group organized like a military force
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈmɪlɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌparəˈmɪlɪtəri / (BrE)
a group of civilians organized in a military style, often to supplement or act in place of a regular army

Examples

paramilitary group
Several armed paramilitary groups operate in remote areas outside government control.
The government accused the paramilitary of destabilizing the region through illicit activities.
The peace process stalled after a resurgence of violence by paramilitaries.

Related Terms

  • antonym – army: the official armed forces of a nation
  • synonym – militia: a military force raised from the civilian population to supplement a regular army
