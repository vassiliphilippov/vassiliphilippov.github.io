aglow

adjective
softly bright or glowing
Pronunciation: / əˈɡloʊ / (AmE), / əˈɡləʊ / (BrE)
shining with a soft, bright, or radiant light; illuminated with a gentle glow

Examples

aglow with light
Her face was aglow with excitement on the night of the celebration.
The city streets are aglow during the annual festival of lights.
The windows of the cottage were aglow with the warm light of candles.
