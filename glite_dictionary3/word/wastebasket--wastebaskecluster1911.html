wastebasket

noun
container for rubbish or waste paper
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌbæskət / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a container with an open top, used for holding paper and other small items of rubbish

Examples

wastebasket by the desk
He tossed the crumpled paper into the wastebasket by his desk.
Please empty your wastebasket before leaving for the day.
The office wastebasket was overflowing with old documents.
