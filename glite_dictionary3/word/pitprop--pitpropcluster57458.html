pitprop

noun
wooden support in a mine
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtˌprɑp / (AmE), / ˈpɪt.prɒp / (BrE)
A wooden support used to hold up the roof of a mine to prevent collapse.

Examples

pitprop supports roof
After years underground, the old pitprop showed signs of decay and needed replacing.
Safety regulations require each pitprop to be checked regularly for damage.
