complementary

adjective
serving to complete or enhance
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmpləˈmɛn.tə.ri / (AmE), / ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛn.təri / (BrE)
acting as or providing something that completes or enhances something else

Examples

complementary skills
The new software is complementary to our existing tools, not a replacement.
Their personalities are complementary, making them an effective team.
These two complementary skills are essential to running a successful business.

Related Terms

  • synonym – supplementary: providing an addition or supplement rather than a completion
