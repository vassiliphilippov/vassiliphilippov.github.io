sherd

noun
broken fragment of pottery or similar artifact
Pronunciation: / ʃɝd / (AmE), / ʃɜːd / (BrE)
A broken fragment of pottery or other brittle artifact, especially one found in archaeological contexts.

Examples

ancient sherd discovery
A single sherd can sometimes reveal important clues about past civilizations.
During the excavation, the archaeologists found a sherd with ancient markings on it.
