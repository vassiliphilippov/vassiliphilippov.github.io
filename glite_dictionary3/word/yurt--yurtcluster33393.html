yurt

noun
portable circular tent dwelling
Pronunciation: / jɝt / (AmE), / jɜːt / (BrE)
A circular, portable tent with a domed roof, traditionally used by nomadic peoples of Central Asia.

Examples

traditional yurt home
After the festival, we dismantled the yurt and packed it away.
Many tourists stayed in a yurt to experience traditional Mongolian culture.
The nomads set up their yurt on the grassy steppe for the summer months.
