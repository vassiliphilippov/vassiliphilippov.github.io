Kadikoy

proper noun
district in Istanbul, Turkey
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːdiˈkɔɪ / (AmE), / ˌkædɪˈkɔɪ / (BrE)
A district in Istanbul, Turkey, formerly a separate town on the Bosporus and historically known as the site of the Council of Chalcedon.
