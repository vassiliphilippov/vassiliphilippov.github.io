yardstick

noun
three-foot measuring stick
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrdˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdˌstɪk / (BrE)
A measuring stick or tape, usually three feet (one yard) long, used for measuring length.

Examples

yardstick for measuring
He keeps a yardstick in his workshop to measure fabric accurately.
Our teacher asked us to bring a yardstick for the math project.
The carpenter measured the board with a yardstick before cutting it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – meter stick: a measuring stick one meter long, used for measuring length
