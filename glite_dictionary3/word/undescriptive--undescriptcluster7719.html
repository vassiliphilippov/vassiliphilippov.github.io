undescriptive

adjective
not giving a clear description
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪsˈkrɪptɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪsˈkrɪptɪv / (BrE)
not giving a clear or detailed description; failing to describe something effectively.

Examples

undescriptive writing style
His writing was so undescriptive that readers struggled to visualize the scene.
She criticized the painting's title as undescriptive, offering no insight into the artwork.
The report was rather undescriptive and lacking in specific details.

  • antonym – descriptive: giving a clear, detailed description
