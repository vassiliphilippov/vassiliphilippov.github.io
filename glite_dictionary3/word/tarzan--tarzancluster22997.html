Tarzan

proper noun
man raised by apes; literary character
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑrˌzæn / (AmE), / ˈtɑːzæn / (BrE)
A fictional character, a man raised by apes in the African jungle and known as the hero of a series of novels by Edgar Rice Burroughs.

Examples

Tarzan swings vines
Many people associate the wild jungle with the image of Tarzan leaping from tree to tree.
The actor played Tarzan in the latest big-budget adaptation of the story.
When I was a child, I loved reading about Tarzan swinging through the jungle on vines.
