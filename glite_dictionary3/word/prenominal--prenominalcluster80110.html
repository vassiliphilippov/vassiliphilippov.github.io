prenominal

adjective
adjective placed before a noun
Pronunciation: / priˈnɑːmɪnl̩ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈnɒmɪnl̩ / (BrE)
(of an adjective) placed before the noun it modifies.

Examples

prenominal adjective position
In the phrase 'a beautiful painting,' 'beautiful' is used in the prenominal position.
Some languages do not use prenominal adjectives as frequently as English.
The prenominal use of adjectives can sometimes change their meaning compared to when used after the noun.
