washer

noun
flat disc used to prevent leakage
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃər / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃə / (BrE)
A flat disc, typically of metal, rubber, or plastic, placed between two surfaces to prevent leakage, distribute pressure, or reduce vibration.

Examples

metal washer ring
A rubber washer provides a watertight seal in the pipe joint.
Before tightening the bolt, make sure to place a washer between the surfaces.
The plumber replaced the worn washer to stop the faucet from dripping.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gasket: a seal of rubber or other material used to prevent leakage
