survival

noun
continuing to exist by adaptation
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪvəl / (AmE), / səˈvaɪvəl / (BrE)
the process by which organisms that are best adapted to their environment continue to live and reproduce, often used in the context of evolution

Examples

survival of the fittest
Her research focuses on the survival of endangered animals in changing habitats.
Natural survival favors the strongest and most flexible species.
The theory of evolution explains why survival depends on adaptation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – extinction: the process in which a species ceases to exist
