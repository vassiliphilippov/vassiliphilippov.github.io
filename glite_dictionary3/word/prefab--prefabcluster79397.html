prefab

noun
a prefabricated building
Pronunciation: / ˈpriˌfæb / (AmE), / ˈpriːfæb / (BrE)
a building that is manufactured in sections and then assembled on site; a prefabricated structure

Examples

a prefab building
After the tornado, the community was rebuilt with modern prefabs.
Many schools installed temporary prefabs to accommodate more students.
The family lived in a prefab for several years after the war.
