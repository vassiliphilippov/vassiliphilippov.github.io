unfavorableness

noun
quality of being unfavorable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪvərəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪvərəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of not being favorable, encouraging, or indicative of success

Examples

unfavorableness of conditions
Investors were concerned about the unfavorableness of the market conditions this quarter.
She was discouraged by the unfavorableness of his response to her proposal.
The unfavorableness of the weather forced the organizers to postpone the event.

  • antonym – favorable: showing approval or encouragement; positive
