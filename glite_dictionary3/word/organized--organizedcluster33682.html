organized

adjective
belonging to a labor union
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡənaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a worker, group, or workplace) belonging to or formed into a labor union.

Examples

organized labor union
An organized shop must follow the rules agreed upon by the labor union.
Many organized workers participated in the strike to demand better wages.
The company has both organized and non-organized employees on its staff.
