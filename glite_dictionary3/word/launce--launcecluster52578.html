launce

noun
small silvery eel-like fish
Pronunciation: / lɑns / (AmE), / lɔːns / (BrE)
A very small silvery eel-like fish that forms schools and burrows into sandy beaches, especially found in coastal waters.

Examples

launce as bait
Fishermen sometimes use launce as bait when angling for larger fish.
Schools of launce can often be seen darting through the shallow waters near the shore.
The seabirds swooped down to catch launce as they emerged from the sand.
