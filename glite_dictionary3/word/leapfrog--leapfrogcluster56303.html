leapfrog

noun
children's game involving jumping over others
Pronunciation: / ˈliːpˌfrɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈliːpˌfrɒɡ / (BrE)
a children's game in which players take turns jumping over one another after each has assumed a crouched position
