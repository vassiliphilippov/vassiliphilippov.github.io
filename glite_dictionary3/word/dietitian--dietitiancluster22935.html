dietitian

noun
person specializing in diet and nutrition
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
A person whose job is to give advice about diet and nutrition.

Examples

dietitian gives advice
After being diagnosed with diabetes, he met with a dietitian to adjust his diet.
Hospitals often employ a dietitian to ensure patients receive proper nutrition.
She consulted a dietitian for advice on her meal plan.

  • synonym – nutritionist: a person who is an expert in nutrition
