to gag

verb
to retch or choke involuntarily
Pronunciation: / ɡæɡ / (AmE), / ɡæɡ /; / ɡaɡ / (BrE)
to choke, retch, or have a strong reaction in the throat, often because something is touching the back of the mouth or blocking the airway
