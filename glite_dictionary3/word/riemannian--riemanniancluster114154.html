Riemannian

adjective
relating to Riemann's geometry
Pronunciation: / riˈmɑniən / (AmE), / riˈmɑːniən / (BrE)
relating to the type of non-Euclidean geometry developed by Bernhard Riemann, especially as it concerns curved spaces.

Examples

Riemannian geometry research
Her research focuses on Riemannian manifolds in differential geometry.
Many concepts in physics are described using Riemannian geometry.
The Riemannian metric measures the distance between points on a curved surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym – Euclidean: relating to Euclid's geometry, based on flat space
