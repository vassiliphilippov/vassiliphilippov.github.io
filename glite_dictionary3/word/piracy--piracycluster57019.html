piracy

noun
armed robbery or hijacking of ships or aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrəsi / (AmE), / ˈpaɪrəsi / (BrE)
the act of attacking and taking control of a ship or aircraft by force, especially at sea, usually to steal from it

Examples

sea piracy
He specialized in investigating cases of piracy on international waters.
Modern piracy threatens shipping routes in several parts of the world.
The cargo ship fell victim to piracy off the coast of Somalia.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hijacking: taking control of a vehicle, especially an aircraft, illegally by force
