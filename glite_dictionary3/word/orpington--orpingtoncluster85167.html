Orpington

noun
a large English breed of chicken
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrpɪŋtən / (AmE), / ˈɔːpɪŋtən / (BrE)
A large breed of domestic chicken originating from England, known for its white skin and soft, profuse feathers.

Examples

Orpington chicken breed
Many people choose Orpington chickens for their docile temperament and attractive appearance.
She won first prize for her champion buff Orpington at the poultry show.
The farm raises both Orpington and Sussex chickens for their meat and eggs.
