chalkboard

noun
board for writing with chalk
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɔkˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɔːkˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A large, usually rectangular board with a smooth, dark surface, used for writing or drawing on with chalk, commonly found in classrooms.

Examples

write on the chalkboard
He wrote the day's assignments on the chalkboard every morning.
Please clean the chalkboard before the next class begins.
The teacher drew a diagram on the chalkboard to explain the concept.
