rotation

noun
mathematical transformation involving turning around a point
Pronunciation: / roʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / rəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
(Mathematics) A transformation that turns a figure or coordinate system around a fixed point, usually the origin, by a certain angle.
