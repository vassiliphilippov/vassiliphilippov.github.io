thickly

adverb
in a poorly articulated, unclear manner
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪkli / (AmE), / ˈθɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is difficult to understand, as if the speaker's tongue is too heavy or swollen; with poor articulation

Examples

to speak thickly
After the surgery, he could only speak thickly for a few hours.
His words came out thickly, slurred by exhaustion.
She muttered thickly, her words hard to understand through her tears.
