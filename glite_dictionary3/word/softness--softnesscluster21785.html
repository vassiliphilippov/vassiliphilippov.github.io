softness

noun
effeminacy (derogatory, especially in men)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɒftnɪs / (BrE)
(derogatory, of a man) the quality of being effeminate or lacking traditional masculine traits.

Examples

softness in a man
He feared that showing emotion would be mistaken for softness by his peers.
In that era, many viewed artistic interests as a sign of softness in men.
The general criticized the softness he perceived among the younger officers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – effeminacy: the quality of having characteristics regarded as typical of women; unmanliness
  • antonym – masculinity: possession of qualities traditionally associated with men
