virtuous

adjective
morally good or righteous
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːtʃuəs / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtʃuəs / (BrE)
having or showing high moral standards; morally good or righteous.

Examples

virtuous leader
He was respected as a virtuous leader who always tried to do what was right.
It is not always easy to remain virtuous when faced with difficult choices.
The novel's virtuous heroine sacrifices her happiness for the good of others.
