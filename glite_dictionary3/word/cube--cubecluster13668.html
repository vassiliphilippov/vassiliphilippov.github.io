to cube

verb
cut into cubes
Pronunciation: / kjuːb / (AmE), / kjuːb / (BrE)
to cut (food or other substances) into pieces with six equal square faces; to cut into cubes

Examples

to cube potatoes
After you cube the cheese, serve it on a platter with crackers.
She cubed the potatoes before adding them to the stew.
The recipe says to cube the chicken breast evenly.
