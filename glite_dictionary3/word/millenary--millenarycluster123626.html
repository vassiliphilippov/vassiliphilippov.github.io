millenary

adjective
relating to the doctrine of a millennium
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪləˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪnəri / (BrE)
relating to the doctrine or belief in a coming millennium, especially a period of a thousand years of peace and righteousness foretold in some religious traditions

Examples

millenary religious belief
His millenary views made him an influential figure in apocalyptic theology debates.
Some early Christian sects held millenary beliefs concerning the imminent arrival of a thousand-year reign of Christ.
The book explores millenary expectations in various religious movements throughout history.

Related Terms

  • synonym – millennial: relating to a thousand-year period, especially a millennial age; or (noun) a person reaching adulthood in the early 21st century
