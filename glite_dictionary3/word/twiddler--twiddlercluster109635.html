twiddler

noun
person who fidgets or manipulates objects
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪd.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪd.lə / (BrE)
a person who manipulates or fidgets with something, especially with their fingers, often in a nervous or unconscious manner

Examples

nervous twiddler
As a habitual twiddler, she always found herself playing with her pen during meetings.
He's a notorious twiddler, always twisting his hair absent-mindedly.
The nervous twiddler couldn't stop fiddling with his ring while waiting for the results.
