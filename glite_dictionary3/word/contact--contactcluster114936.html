to contact

verb
touch physically; make physical connection
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tækt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tækt / (BrE)
to be in or come into direct physical connection with something or someone

Examples

to contact a surface
As the two surfaces contact, friction generates heat.
Do not allow the acid to contact your skin.
