stand-up

noun
brief daily team meeting
Pronunciation: / ˈstændˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈstandˌʌp / (BrE)
a short meeting in which team members stand and give brief status updates, often held daily in agile or software teams.

Examples

daily stand-up meeting
The team holds a quick stand-up every morning to review today's tasks.
During the remote stand-up, each developer gave a brief update on their progress.
They try to keep the stand-up under fifteen minutes so people stay focused.

Related Terms

  • synonym – meeting: an assembly of people for discussion or decision-making; a stand-up is a short, focused meeting.
  • synonym – scrum: a framework for agile project management that commonly includes daily stand-up meetings.
