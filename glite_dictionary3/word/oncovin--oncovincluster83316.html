Oncovin

noun
trademarked name for cancer drug vincristine
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːnkoʊˌvɪn / (AmE), / ˈɒŋkəʊˌvɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked name for vincristine, an antineoplastic drug derived from the periwinkle plant, used especially to treat cancers of the lymphatic system.

Examples

Oncovin chemotherapy treatment
Many lymphomas are effectively managed with a combination of drugs, including Oncovin.
The doctor prescribed Oncovin as part of the chemotherapy regimen.
Treatment with Oncovin is known to cause some side effects such as neuropathy.
