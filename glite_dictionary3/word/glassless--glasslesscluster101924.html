glassless

adjective
without glass (windows, doors, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæs.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːs.ləs / (BrE)
(of windows, doors, etc.) having no glass; not furnished with glass.

Examples

glassless window frame
Due to the storm, several rooms were left with glassless panes that let in rain and debris.
The abandoned house had glassless windows through which the wind howled.
They installed temporary boards in the glassless frames while they waited for repairs.
