self-sacrificing

adjective
willing to give up own interests for others
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈsækrɪˌfaɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌselfˈsækrɪfaɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
Willing to give up one's own interests, needs, or wishes for the sake of others.

Examples

self-sacrificing mother
His self-sacrificing nature made him a beloved leader among his peers.
She is self-sacrificing, always putting her family's needs before her own.
The nurse's self-sacrificing attitude inspired everyone in the hospital.

Related Terms

  • synonym – altruistic: showing a disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • antonym – selfish: lacking consideration for others; concerned chiefly with one's own personal profit or pleasure
