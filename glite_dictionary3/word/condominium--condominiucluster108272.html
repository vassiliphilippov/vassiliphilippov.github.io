condominium

noun
privately owned apartment/unit in shared building
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn dəˈmɪni əm /; / ˌkɑːn.dəˈmɪn.i.əm / (AmE), / ˌkɒn dəˈmɪni əm /; / ˌkɒn.dəˈmɪn.i.əm / (BrE)
an apartment or unit in a building where each residence is individually owned, and common areas are shared with other owners

Examples

modern condominium building

The building is managed by a condominium association that oversees the shared spaces.
They bought a new condominium near the city center for their retirement.
