rotundity

noun
fullness or richness of sound
Pronunciation: / roʊˈtʌndəti / (AmE), / rəʊˈtʌndəti / (BrE)
the quality of being full or rich in tone; resonance or roundness of sound

Examples

rotundity of voice
Her speech was marked by a pleasing rotundity that captivated the audience.
The rotundity of his voice filled the concert hall with warmth.
The cello's rotundity contrasted beautifully with the violin's sharpness.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resonance: the quality in a sound of being deep, full, and reverberating
  • antonym – thinness: lacking fullness or richness in sound
