docudrama

noun
film or TV program dramatizing real events
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑk.juˌdrɑː.mə / (AmE), / ˈdɒk.juˌdrɑː.mə / (BrE)
A film or television program that presents factual events with dramatized elements, often using actors to portray real people.

Examples

docudrama about history
She prefers watching a docudrama to a purely fictional thriller because it feels more realistic.
The docudrama aimed to accurately depict the events leading up to the disaster.
The network aired a docudrama about the famous court case last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – documentary: a film or program presenting facts objectively, without dramatization
