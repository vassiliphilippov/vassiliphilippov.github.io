apron

noun
paved area at an airport for aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪprən / (AmE), / ˈeɪprən / (BrE)
a paved or hard-surfaced area at an airport where aircraft are parked, loaded, or unloaded

Examples

airport apron
Maintenance crews were working on the jet parked at the apron.
Passengers are often transferred by bus to the apron where smaller planes await.
The plane waited on the apron until it was cleared for departure.

Related Terms

  • antonym – runway: long strip where aircraft take off and land at an airport
  • synonym – taxiway: path connecting runways with terminals or aprons at an airport
