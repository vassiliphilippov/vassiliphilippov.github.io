to hiccough

verb
to make a sudden involuntary hiccup sound
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈhɪk.ʌp / (BrE)
to make a sudden, involuntary spasm of the diaphragm and a characteristic sound in the throat; to hiccup

Examples

to hiccough after eating
After drinking cold water too quickly, Mark hiccoughed loudly in front of his friends.
Every time she laughed too hard, she would suddenly hiccough uncontrollably.
He tried to hold his breath to stop himself from hiccoughing during the meeting.
