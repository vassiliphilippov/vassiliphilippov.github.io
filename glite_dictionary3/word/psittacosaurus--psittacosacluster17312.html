Psittacosaurus

noun
a genus of primitive horned dinosaur
Pronunciation: / sɪˌtækəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / sɪˌtækəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a genus of small, herbivorous, beaked dinosaurs that lived during the Early Cretaceous period and are considered the most primitive horned dinosaurs.
