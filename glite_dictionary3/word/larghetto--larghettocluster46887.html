larghetto

adjective
somewhat slow musical tempo
Pronunciation: / lɑːrˈɡɛtoʊ / (AmE), / lɑːˈɡɛt.əʊ / (BrE)
(in music) at a tempo that is slightly faster than largo and slower than andante; broadly and rather slowly.
