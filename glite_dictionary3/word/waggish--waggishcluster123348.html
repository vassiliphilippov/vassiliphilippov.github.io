waggish

adjective
humorous; playful; joking
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈwæɡɪʃ / (BrE)
humorous in a playful or mischievous way; fond of making jokes.

Examples

waggish sense of humor
His waggish remarks lightened the mood of the meeting.
She gave a waggish grin after playing a harmless prank on her friend.
The author's waggish storytelling kept the audience entertained throughout the evening.
