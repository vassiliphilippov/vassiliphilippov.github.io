semirigid

adjective
partly rigid; partially kept in shape by internal structure and gas pressure
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈrɪdʒɪd /; / ˌsɛm.iˈrɪdʒ.ɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈrɪdʒɪd /; / ˌsɛ.mɪˈrɪdʒ.ɪd / (BrE)
partly but not fully rigid; having some stiffness or support but not completely inflexible
