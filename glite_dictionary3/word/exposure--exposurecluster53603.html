exposure

noun
act of subjecting to an experience or influence
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊʒə / (BrE)
the experience of being subjected to something, such as an influence, environment, risk, or harm

Examples

repeated exposure to danger

Teachers worry about the exposure of students to online misinformation.
The exposure of young children to violent media has raised concerns among parents and educators.
His exposure to criticism made him more resilient over time.
Public exposure of the company's secrets left them open to lawsuits.
The refugees faced constant exposure to danger in the war zone.
