tendosynovitis

noun
inflammation of a tendon sheath
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛndoʊˌsaɪnəˈvaɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌtɛndəʊˌsɪnəʊˈvaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a tendon and its surrounding sheath, often causing pain and restricted movement.

Examples

tendosynovitis in the wrist
Overuse of the thumb can lead to tendosynovitis, especially among frequent smartphone users.
The patient was diagnosed with tendosynovitis after complaining of wrist pain and swelling.
Treatment of tendosynovitis may include rest, anti-inflammatory medication, and physical therapy.
