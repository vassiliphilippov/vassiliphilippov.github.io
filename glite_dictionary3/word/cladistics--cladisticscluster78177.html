cladistics

noun
classification by evolutionary relationships
Pronunciation: / kləˈdɪstɪks / (AmE), / kləˈdɪstɪks / (BrE)
a method of classifying organisms based on the analysis of evolutionary relationships, as represented by the branching of cladograms

Examples

cladistics in biology
Cladistics has become a fundamental tool in modern evolutionary biology.
By using cladistics, scientists can better understand how different species are related.
The course introduced students to cladistics and the construction of cladograms.
