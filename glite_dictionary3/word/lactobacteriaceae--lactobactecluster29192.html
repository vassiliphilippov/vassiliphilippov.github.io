Lactobacteriaceae

noun
family of lactic acid bacteria
Pronunciation: / ˌlæktoʊˌbæktəˈriːəsiː /; / ˌlæktoʊˌbæktɪriˈeɪsiˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˌlaktəʊˌbaktəˈrɪːəsiː /; / ˌlaktəʊˌbaktɪrɪˈeɪsiaɪ / (BrE)
