shaft

noun
vertical passageway in a building
Pronunciation: / ʃæft / (AmE), / ʃɑːft / (BrE)
a long, narrow, usually vertical passage or tunnel, especially in a building or underground (such as for an elevator or a mine)

Examples

elevator shaft
The elevator was stuck in the shaft between the fourth and fifth floors.
They installed lighting along the shaft to make maintenance easier.
Workers inspected the shaft for any signs of structural damage.
A new ventilation shaft was constructed to improve airflow in the mine.
Engineers inspected the shaft for signs of structural weakness after the earthquake.
The miners descended deep into the shaft to reach the underground seam.
