5-membered

adjective
having a five-membered ring (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / faɪvˈmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / faɪvˈmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of five atoms or groups.
