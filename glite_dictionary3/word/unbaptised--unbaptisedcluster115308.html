unbaptised

adjective
not having received baptism
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbæp.taɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbæp.tʌɪzd / (BrE)
Not having undergone the Christian ceremony of baptism.

Examples

unbaptised child
During the ceremony, prayers were offered for the souls of the unbaptised.
He grew up in a largely Catholic town as one of the few unbaptised children.
The baby remained unbaptised due to the family's religious beliefs.
