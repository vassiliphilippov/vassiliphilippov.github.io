Hachiman

proper noun
Shinto god of war
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑː.tʃiˌmɑːn / (AmE), / ˈha.tʃiˌman / (BrE)
A major Shinto god of war and divination, venerated as the protector of Japan and its people.
