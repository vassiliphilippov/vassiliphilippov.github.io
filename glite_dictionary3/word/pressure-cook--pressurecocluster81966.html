to pressure-cook

verb
cook food with a pressure cooker
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃɚ kʊk / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃə kʊk / (BrE)
to cook food in a pressure cooker, using steam under high pressure to reduce cooking time

Examples

to pressure-cook beans
I usually pressure-cook beans to save time in the kitchen.
She is pressure-cooking a stew for tonight's dinner.
Vegetables can be pressure-cooked to preserve their nutrients and flavor.
