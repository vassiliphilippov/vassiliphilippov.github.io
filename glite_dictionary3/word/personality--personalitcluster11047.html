personality

noun
the distinctive qualities of a person
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜːrsəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the combination of qualities and characteristics that form an individual's distinctive character, including behavioral, emotional, and mental traits

Examples

unique personality traits
Despite identical appearances, the twins have very different personality traits.
He was hired for the job as much for his engaging personality as for his skills.
Her personality is warm and outgoing, making her easy to talk to.
