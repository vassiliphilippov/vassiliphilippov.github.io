polysomy

noun
having extra chromosomes in cells
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪsəmi / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪsəmi / (BrE)
A condition in which an organism has one or more extra chromosomes in its somatic cells, leading to a genetic abnormality.
