pit

noun
hard stone inside certain fruits
Pronunciation: / pɪt / (AmE), / pɪt / (BrE)
the hard, woody inner stone of some fruits, such as peaches, plums, cherries, or olives, that contains the seed

Examples

pit of a peach
Before baking the pie, remove the pits from all the peaches.
She bit into the cherry and accidentally cracked the pit with her tooth.
The olive pit is usually discarded after eating the fruit.

  • synonym – stone: the hard, seed-containing part inside some fruits, such as peaches or cherries
