persuasion

noun
the act of convincing someone
Pronunciation: / pərˈsweɪʒən / (AmE), / pəˈsweɪʒn / (BrE)
the act of convincing someone to do or believe something through reasoning or argument

Examples

use persuasion skills
Advertising often uses subtle persuasion to influence consumer choices.
Her skill in persuasion helped her win the debate against more experienced opponents.
It took some persuasion, but he finally agreed to attend the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – convincing: the action of making someone believe something
  • antonym – dissuasion: the act of persuading someone not to do something
