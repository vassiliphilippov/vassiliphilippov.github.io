Sequoiadendron giganteum

noun
species of giant evergreen tree (giant sequoia)
Pronunciation: / səˌkwɔɪ.əˈdɛndrən ʤaɪˈgiː.tʃi.əm / (AmE), / sɪˌkwɔɪ.əˈdɛndrən dʒɪˈgiː.ti.əm / (BrE)
