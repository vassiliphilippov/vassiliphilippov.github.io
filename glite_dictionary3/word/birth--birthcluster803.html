to birth

verb
to give birth; to bring into being
Pronunciation: / bɝːθ / (AmE), / bɜːθ / (BrE)
to give birth to; to cause (a baby or other young) to be born

Examples

to birth a child
After hours of labor, she finally birthed a healthy baby girl.
Some animals are able to birth their young without assistance.
The midwife birthed three babies during her shift last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deliver: to assist in giving birth, especially as a medical professional
  • synonym – give birth: to bear a child; to bring forth offspring
