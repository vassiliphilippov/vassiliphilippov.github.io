Sphyrna tiburo

noun
species of small hammerhead shark (bonnethead)
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪrnə tɪˈbjuːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈsfɪənə tɪˈbjuːrəʊ / (BrE)
