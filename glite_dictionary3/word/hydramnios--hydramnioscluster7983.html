hydramnios

noun
excess amniotic fluid in pregnancy
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdræmni.əs / (AmE), / hɪˈdræmnɪɒs / (BrE)
A medical condition in pregnancy characterized by the accumulation of excess amniotic fluid in the amniotic sac.

Examples

hydramnios during pregnancy
Hydramnios can lead to complications for both mother and baby if not properly managed.
Doctors often investigate the underlying causes when hydramnios is diagnosed during pregnancy.
The ultrasound revealed that the patient was suffering from hydramnios, requiring close monitoring.
