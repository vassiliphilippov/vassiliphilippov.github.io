siesta

noun
early afternoon nap
Pronunciation: / siˈɛstə / (AmE), / siˈɛstə / (BrE)
a short nap or rest taken in the early afternoon, especially in hot countries

Examples

afternoon siesta
After lunch, many people in Spain enjoy a relaxing siesta before returning to work.
She found that a brief siesta helped restore her energy in the afternoon.
The hotel encouraged guests to take a siesta during the hottest part of the day.
