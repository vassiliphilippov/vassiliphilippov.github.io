to dub

verb
give a nickname
Pronunciation: / dʌb / (AmE), / dʌb / (BrE)
to give an unofficial name or nickname to someone or something

Examples

to dub a new nickname
Fans dubbed the team 'The Invincibles' after their unbeaten season.
He was dubbed 'Mr. Fix-It' because he always solved our problems.
The press dubbed her the 'Queen of Pop' after the release of her new album.
