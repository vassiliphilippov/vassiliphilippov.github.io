tube

noun
anatomical hollow cylindrical body structure
Pronunciation: / tuːb / (AmE), / tjuːb / (BrE)
a long, hollow, cylindrical object used as a container, passage, or conduit for liquids, gases, or other materials (including in the body, plants, or general use)
