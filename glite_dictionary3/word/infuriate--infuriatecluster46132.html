to infuriate

verb
make extremely angry
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfjʊriˌeɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfjʊərieɪt / (BrE)
to make someone extremely angry; to enrage

Examples

to infuriate a customer
Delays in the delivery process can infuriate even the most patient customers.
Her constant interruptions began to infuriate everyone in the meeting.
It would infuriate him if he found out you lied.
