haematoidin

noun
orange-yellow pigment from hemoglobin
Pronunciation: / hiːˌmætɔɪˈdɪn / (AmE), / hiːˌmatɔɪˈdɪn / (BrE)
an orange-yellow pigment formed from hemoglobin, occurring in tissues and contributing to the yellow coloration seen in jaundice

Jaundice results when haematoidin and related pigments accumulate in the bloodstream.
