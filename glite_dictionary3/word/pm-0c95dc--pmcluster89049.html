PM

noun
phase modulation of a carrier wave
Pronunciation: / piː ɛm / (AmE), / piː ɛm / (BrE)
a method of modulating a radio signal by varying the phase of the carrier wave

Examples

PM signal strength
Engineers tested both PM and FM to determine which gave clearer audio transmission.
In digital communications, PM is often combined with other modulation techniques.
The radio uses PM to improve its resistance to signal interference.
