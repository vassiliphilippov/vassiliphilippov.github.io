somatic

adjective
of the body, not the mind
Pronunciation: / səˈmætɪk / (AmE), / səˈmatɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the body, especially as distinct from the mind or spirit.

Examples

somatic symptoms
Chronic stress can sometimes result in somatic pain, such as headaches or stomachaches.
The doctor explained that her somatic symptoms had no clear physical cause.
They studied the somatic effects of the medication on muscle tissue.
