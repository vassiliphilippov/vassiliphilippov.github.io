to disseminate

verb
spread widely (information, ideas, etc.)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛmɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to spread information, ideas, or beliefs widely so that it reaches many people

Examples

to disseminate information
It is important to disseminate research findings through scholarly journals and conferences.
The government used leaflets to disseminate safety instructions during the emergency.
The organization aims to disseminate accurate information about climate change to the public.
