dehumidifier

noun
appliance for removing moisture from air
Pronunciation: / di.hjuːˈmɪd.əˌfaɪ.ər / (AmE), / diː.hjuːˈmɪd.ɪ.faɪ.ə / (BrE)
A device that removes moisture from the air to improve indoor comfort.

Examples

use a dehumidifier in a room
After a heavy rain, the dehumidifier helped dry out the basement.
Running a dehumidifier made the air much more comfortable during the humid summer.
We put a dehumidifier in the laundry room to prevent mold growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym – humidifier: a device that adds moisture to the air
