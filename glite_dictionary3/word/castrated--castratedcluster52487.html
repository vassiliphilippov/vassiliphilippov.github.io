castrated

adjective
deprived of sexual capacity or attributes
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈkæstreɪtɪd / (BrE)
having been deprived of the power to reproduce or sexual attributes, especially by removal of the testes or ovaries.
