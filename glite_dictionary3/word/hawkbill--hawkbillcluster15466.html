hawkbill

noun
species of sea turtle with a hawk-like beak
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔkˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɔːkˌbɪl / (BrE)
a tropical sea turtle with a hawk-like beak, valued for its shell and considered a source of food

Examples

hawkbill sea turtle

The hawkbill is prized for its beautiful tortoiseshell, which has been used in jewelry for centuries.
The sharp, hooked beak of the hawkbill helps it feed on sponges and anemones.
