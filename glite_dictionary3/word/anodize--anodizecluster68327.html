to anodize

verb
apply an oxide coating to metal
Pronunciation: / ˈænəˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈanəˌdʌɪz / (BrE)
to coat (a metal) with a protective or decorative oxide layer by means of an electrolytic process

Examples

to anodize aluminum parts
After we anodized the parts, they developed a durable, colored finish.
Many bicycles feature anodized rims for greater strength and style.
The company will anodize the aluminum panels to prevent corrosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym – electroplate: to coat a metal using an electric current
