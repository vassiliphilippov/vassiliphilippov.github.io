smiling

noun
act or expression of smiling
Pronunciation: / ˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsmaɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
A facial expression in which the corners of the mouth are turned upward, typically indicating pleasure, amusement, or friendliness.

Examples

a smiling face
Her smiling made everyone in the room feel more comfortable.
The photograph captured his genuine smiling as he greeted old friends.
There was constant smiling and laughter during the celebration.

Related Terms

  • antonym – frown: A facial expression of displeasure or concern in which the corners of the mouth are turned downward
  • synonym – grin: A broad smile, often showing the teeth
