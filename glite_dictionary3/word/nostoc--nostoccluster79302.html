Nostoc

noun
genus of freshwater blue-green algae
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑstɑk / (AmE), / ˈnɒstɒk / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater blue-green algae that form colonies, often found in moist environments; type genus of the family Nostocaceae.
