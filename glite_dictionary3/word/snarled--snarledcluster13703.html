snarled

adjective
tangled in knots or snarls
Pronunciation: / snɑrld / (AmE), / snɑːld / (BrE)
tangled or twisted together in a disorderly mass, especially in knots or snarls.

Examples

snarled wires
He struggled to untie the snarled fishing lines in his tackle box.
Her hair became snarled after a windy walk along the beach.
The snarled wires behind the television were difficult to separate.
