bushman

noun
man living in the Australian bush
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃmən / (BrE)
a man who lives in or is native to the bush country of Australia, especially one who is adept at surviving in the wild

Examples

skilled bushman
As a skilled bushman, he could survive for weeks with only basic supplies.
