taxiway

noun
aerial corridor for drones
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksiˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtæksiˌweɪ / (BrE)
a designated low-altitude aerial corridor or route used by unmanned aerial vehicles (drones) and urban air taxis to move between takeoff, landing or delivery points.

Examples

drone taxiway
City officials published maps showing new drone taxiway corridors above industrial zones for deliveries.
Operators must program their eVTOLs to follow designated taxiway routes during low-altitude flights over the city.
Emergency services rehearsed redirecting drones away from a blocked taxiway during an urban air mobility test.

Related Terms

  • synonym – airway: a defined route in the air used by aircraft; similar in function but usually at higher altitudes
  • synonym – drone corridor: a planned route or area of airspace reserved for drone operations
