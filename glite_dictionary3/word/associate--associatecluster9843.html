to associate

verb
make a mental or logical connection
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsieɪt / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsieɪt / (BrE)
to make a connection in the mind between two or more things; to link logically or causally

Examples

to associate ideas
It is difficult to associate his calm demeanor with the intense rumors about him.
Many people associate chocolate with feelings of happiness and comfort.
Scientists often associate smoking with increased health risks.
