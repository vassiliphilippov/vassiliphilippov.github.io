to Europeanize

verb
make more European in character or customs
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊˈpiːəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊˈpiːənaɪz / (BrE)
to make something more like European customs, culture, or ideas

Examples

to Europeanize local culture
His desire to Europeanize traditional art sparked controversy among cultural purists.
Many local restaurants started to Europeanize their menus to attract international tourists.
The government sought to Europeanize the country's institutions after joining the European Union.

Related Terms

  • synonym – westernize: to make more like Western (especially Western European or American) culture
