petchary

noun
a migratory kingbird species
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtʃəri / (AmE), / ˈpɛtʃəri / (BrE)
A kingbird native to the southeastern United States that migrates to tropical America; similar to but larger than the eastern kingbird.

Examples

a petchary bird
Birdwatchers sometimes travel to Florida just to catch a glimpse of the rare petchary.
The petchary can often be seen perched atop telephone wires during migration season.
Unlike the eastern kingbird, the petchary winters much farther south in the tropics.
