to examine

verb
look at or question closely
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzæmɪn /; / ɪɡˈzæm.ɪn / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzæmɪn /; / ɪɡˈzæm.ɪn / (BrE)
to look at, study, or analyze something or someone carefully and thoroughly in order to find out information or detect problems
