horsetail

noun
joint-stemmed, perennial plant (Equisetum)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrˌsteɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsteɪl / (BrE)
a perennial plant with hollow, jointed stems and narrow, toothlike leaves, of the genus Equisetum, spreading by creeping rhizomes and often considered weedy

Examples

horsetail plant species
Fossils show that horsetail plants have existed since the time of the dinosaurs.
She identified the unusual plant as a species of horsetail by its hollow stem and segmented appearance.
The field was overrun with horsetail, making it difficult to cultivate crops.
