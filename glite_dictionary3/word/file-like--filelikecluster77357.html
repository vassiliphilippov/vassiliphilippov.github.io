file-like

adjective
resembling a file
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪlˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪl.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a file, especially in shape, appearance, or function.

Examples

file-like tool
