mainmast

noun
the principal mast of a sailing ship
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnˌmæst / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnmɑːst / (BrE)
the principal mast of a sailing ship, especially when there are two or more masts

Examples

climb the mainmast
Sailors climbed the mainmast to adjust the sails during the storm.
The captain stood near the mainmast, surveying the open sea.
The hurricane snapped the mainmast in two, leaving the ship helpless.
