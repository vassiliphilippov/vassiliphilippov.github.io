to review

verb
look back on; recall events
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvju / (AmE), / rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
to look back on or think about something that happened in the past, especially to assess or remember it

Examples

to review your life
After the ceremony, she reviewed her achievements with a sense of pride.
He often reviews the past year at the beginning of each New Year.
Sitting by the fire, they reviewed the events of their long friendship.

Related Terms

  • synonym – recall: to bring a memory of something into your mind
