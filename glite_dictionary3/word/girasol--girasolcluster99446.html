girasol

noun
a plant grown for edible tubers, Jerusalem artichoke
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪrəˌsɒl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪərəˌsɒl / (BrE)
A tall perennial plant (Helianthus tuberosus) with hairy stems and leaves, widely cultivated for its edible, knobby tubers; also known as Jerusalem artichoke.
