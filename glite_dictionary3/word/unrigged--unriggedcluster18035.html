unrigged

adjective
not manipulated; fair
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪɡd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪɡd / (BrE)
Not rigged or manipulated; fair and free from tampering, especially of elections, votes, or decision-making processes.

Examples

unrigged election results
Opposition leaders insisted the upcoming vote must be unrigged to restore public confidence in the system.
Activists campaigned for transparent procedures so the hiring process would remain unrigged and merit-based.
After the recount, officials declared the election unrigged, rejecting allegations of tampering.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fair: free from bias, dishonesty, or injustice
  • antonym – rigged: manipulated or fixed (an outcome) dishonestly
