to excavate

verb
to recover by digging
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.sə.veɪt /; / ˈɛkskəˌveɪt /; / ˈɛk.səˌveɪt / (AmE), / ˈɛk.sə.veɪt /; / ˈɛkskəveɪt /; / ˈɛk.skəˌveɪt / (BrE)
