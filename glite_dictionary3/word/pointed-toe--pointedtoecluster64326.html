pointed-toe

adjective
having a pointed toe
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪn.tɪd ˌtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪn.tɪd ˌtəʊ / (BrE)
(of a shoe or similar item) having a toe that comes to a point rather than being rounded or square.
