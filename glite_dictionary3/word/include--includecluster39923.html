to include

verb
to have as part of a whole
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluːd /; / ɪnˈklud / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːd / (BrE)
to have or consider someone or something as part of a group, set, or whole; to contain or include as a member or part
