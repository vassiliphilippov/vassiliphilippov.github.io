to devilise

verb
make devilish; turn into a devil
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvɪˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdɛvɪlaɪz / (BrE)
To turn into a devil or make devilish; to imbue with devilish qualities.

Examples

to devilise a person
His heart was devilised by years of bitterness and betrayal.
Literature often devilises its villains to heighten the contrast with its heroes.
The horrors of battle seemed to devilise even the most gentle souls.

Related Terms

  • synonym – demonize: to portray as wicked or demonic
