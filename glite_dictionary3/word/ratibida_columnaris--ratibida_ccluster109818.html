Ratibida columnaris

noun
a coneflower native to North America
Pronunciation: / rəˈtɪbɪdə kəˈlʌmnərɪs / (AmE), / rəˈtɪbɪdə kəˈlɒmnərɪs / (BrE)
A species of coneflower with flower heads resembling a Mexican hat, having a tall red-brown disk and drooping yellow or yellow and red-brown petals; native to the Great Plains and foothills of the Rocky Mountains.
