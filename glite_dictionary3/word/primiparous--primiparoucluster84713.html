primiparous

adjective
having given birth only once
Pronunciation: / praɪˈmɪpərəs / (AmE), / prɪˈmɪpərəs / (BrE)
(of a woman) having given birth to one child only; relating to a first childbirth.

Examples

primiparous mother
As a primiparous patient, she required additional reassurance during labor.
Her experiences as a primiparous mother were both joyful and challenging.
The study compared primiparous and multiparous women after delivery.

Related Terms

  • antonym – multiparous: having given birth more than once
  • antonym – nulliparous: having never given birth
