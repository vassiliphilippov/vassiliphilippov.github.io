Jew-ish

adjective
partly identifying with Judaism or Jewish culture
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːˌɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːˌɪʃ / (BrE)
Describing a person who partially identifies with Judaism, or whose culture is influenced by Jewish traditions, but who does not fully observe or belong to traditional Jewish religious or ethnic categories.

Examples

Jew-ish identity
He identifies as Jew-ish because he celebrates some holidays but doesn't follow the religion strictly.
Many of her childhood traditions were Jew-ish, mixing Hanukkah with other cultural influences.
My friends say my cooking is Jew-ish, since I love bagels but don't keep kosher.
