agent

noun
business intermediary; buys or sells for others
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒənt /; / ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒənt /; / ˈeɪ.dʒənt / (BrE)
a person who acts or represents another, especially in business, legal, official, or law enforcement matters

Examples

  • synonym – officer: person holding a position of authority in an organization or government, especially law enforcement
  • synonym – representative: a person chosen or appointed to act or speak for others
  • antonym – suspect: person thought to be guilty of a crime (opposite role in the context of investigation)
