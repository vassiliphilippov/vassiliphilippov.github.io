to glamourize

verb
make glamorous or attractive
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæmərˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡlæməraɪz / (BrE)
to make something seem glamorous or more attractive than it really is

Examples

to glamourize a lifestyle
Certain magazines tend to glamourize unrealistic body images.
The film has been criticized for glamourizing violence.
They tried to glamourize the life of a criminal, ignoring the consequences.
