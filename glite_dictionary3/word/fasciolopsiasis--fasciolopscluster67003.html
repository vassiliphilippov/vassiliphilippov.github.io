fasciolopsiasis

noun
infection by Fasciolopsis buski fluke
Pronunciation: / fæʃiˌoʊlɑpˈsaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / fæʃɪˌɒlɒpˈsaɪəsɪs / (BrE)
A parasitic infection caused by infestation with the large intestinal fluke Fasciolopsis buski, common in parts of eastern Asia.
