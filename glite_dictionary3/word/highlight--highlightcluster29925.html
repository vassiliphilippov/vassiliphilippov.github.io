to highlight

verb
apply cosmetic highlighter to the face
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.laɪt / (BrE)
to apply a cosmetic highlighter to parts of the face, such as the cheeks or eyebrows, to make them more prominent or luminous

Examples

to highlight cheekbones
Before going out, she carefully highlighted her face with a shimmery powder.
Many makeup tutorials show how to highlight the bridge of your nose and the area above your brows.
She likes to highlight her cheekbones for a radiant glow.
