nimbly

adverb
in a quick and agile manner
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪmbli / (AmE), / ˈnɪmbli / (BrE)
in a quick, light, and agile manner; moving with ease and skill

Examples

jump nimbly over hurdles
She nimbly dodged the obstacles in her path during the race.
The cat nimbly jumped from the sofa to the windowsill.
The gymnast moved nimbly across the balance beam, impressing the judges.
