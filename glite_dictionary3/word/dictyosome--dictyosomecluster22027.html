dictyosome

noun
structure in animal cell cytoplasm (Golgi complex)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪktioʊˌsoʊm / (AmE), / ˈdɪktɪəˌsəʊm / (BrE)
a netlike structure in the cytoplasm of animal cells, especially those producing secretions, functioning as part of the Golgi apparatus
