yawl

noun
two-masted sailing boat
Pronunciation: / jɔl / (AmE), / jɔːl / (BrE)
A sailing boat with two masts, the smaller mizzen mast located behind the rudderpost.

Examples

classic yawl sailboat
He restored an old wooden yawl and entered it in the regatta.
The yawl sailed gracefully across the bay in the early morning light.
Their family spent summers cruising the coastline in a sturdy yawl.
