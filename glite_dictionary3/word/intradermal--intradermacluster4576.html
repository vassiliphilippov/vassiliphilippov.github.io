intradermal

adjective
between the layers of the skin
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈdɜrməl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈdɜːməl / (BrE)
Administered or occurring within or between the layers of the skin, especially referring to an injection.

Examples

intradermal injection
An intradermal patch test can help diagnose certain skin sensitivities.
The doctor administered an intradermal injection to test for allergies.
Tuberculosis screening often uses the intradermal route for the Mantoux test.
