topography

noun
study or description of land surface features
Pronunciation: / təˈpɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / təˈpɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the detailed study or description of the surface features and shape of an area or region, including its mountains, valleys, and other physical characteristics

Examples

study topography
Changes in river flow can significantly alter the topography over time.
Satellite images help scientists understand the topography of remote regions.
The topography of the island includes mountains, valleys, and numerous streams.

Related Terms

  • synonym – relief: the variation in elevation and slope of the land surface
  • synonym – terrain: the physical features of a stretch of land
