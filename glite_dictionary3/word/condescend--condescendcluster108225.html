to condescend

verb
act as if lowering oneself; show superiority
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːndəˈsend /; / ˌkɑn.dɪˈsɛnd / (AmE), / ˌkɒndɪˈsend /; / ˌkɒn.dɪˈsɛnd / (BrE)
to do something that you think is beneath you or below your dignity
