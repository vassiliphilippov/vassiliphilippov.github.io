unshakably

adverb
in an unwavering, determined manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃeɪkəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪkəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that cannot be shaken, weakened, or changed; with unwavering determination or resolve.

Examples

unshakably calm under pressure
He remained unshakably calm throughout the entire crisis.
She unshakably believed in her friend's innocence, despite all the rumors.
Their commitment to each other was unshakably strong, even in difficult times.
