imperialism

noun
extension of a country's power over others
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a policy or practice by which a country extends its power and influence over other countries, especially by force, colonization, or political and economic control

Related Terms

  • antonym – anti-imperialism: opposition to imperialism
  • synonym – colonialism: the practice or policy of acquiring full or partial political control over another country, occupying it with settlers, and exploiting it economically
  • antonym – nationalism: loyalty and devotion to a nation; opposing policy to imperialism
