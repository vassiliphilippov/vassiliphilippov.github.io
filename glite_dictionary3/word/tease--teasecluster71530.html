tease

noun
seductive woman who exploits men's attention
Pronunciation: / tiz / (AmE), / tiːz / (BrE)
a woman who deliberately attracts men's attention and affection but does not intend to reciprocate, often using her appeal to manipulate or exploit them

Examples

a flirty tease
Everyone knows she's a real tease, enjoying attention from several men at once.
He felt frustrated because she acted like a tease all night but never gave him her number.
Some people unfairly label a confident woman as a tease just because she dresses attractively.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flirt: a person who behaves as if attracted to others but is not serious
