clear

adjective
not in contact with; away from danger or obstruction
Pronunciation: / klɪr / (AmE), / klɪə / (BrE)
not in contact with, close to, or connected with something, especially something dangerous or unwanted.

Examples

clear of danger
After hours of driving, we were clear of the traffic jam.
Ensure the runway is clear of debris before landing.
The hikers finally got clear of the thick forest by noon.

Related Terms

  • antonym – caught: trapped or unable to move away from something
  • synonym – free: not under the control or in the power of another; not physically obstructed or in contact
  • synonym – safe: protected from or not exposed to danger or risk
