skydiver

noun
person who parachutes from aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌdaɪvər / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌdaɪvə / (BrE)
a person who jumps from an aircraft and performs freefall maneuvers before deploying a parachute

Examples

experienced skydiver
As a beginner skydiver, she found her first solo jump exhilarating.
During the show, each skydiver formed part of an impressive aerial formation.
The skydiver leapt from the plane and executed a perfect midair spin.
