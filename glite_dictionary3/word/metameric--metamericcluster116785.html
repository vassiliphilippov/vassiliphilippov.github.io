metameric

adjective
having body divided into segments
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈmɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈmɛrɪk / (BrE)
(of an organism) having the body divided into a series of similar segments or metameres, as in earthworms or lobsters.
