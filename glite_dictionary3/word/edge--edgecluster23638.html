to edge

verb
move slowly and carefully
Pronunciation: / ɛdʒ / (AmE), / ɛdʒ / (BrE)
to move slowly and carefully in a particular direction, often by small amounts

Examples

to edge toward the door
He edged toward the door, trying to escape the conversation.
She edged closer to the exit, hoping not to draw attention.
The cat edged along the narrow fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym – creep: move slowly and carefully so as not to be noticed
  • antonym – rush: move with great speed
