pursy

adjective
breathing with difficulty
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsi / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsi / (BrE)
Breathing with difficulty; short-winded, especially as a result of being overweight or unhealthy.

Examples

a pursy old man
After climbing the stairs, the pursy gentleman paused to catch his breath.
She gave a pursy gasp after the strenuous walk across the field.
The old dog grew pursy with age and could no longer run as before.
