corundum

noun
very hard mineral, aluminum oxide
Pronunciation: / kəˈrʌndəm / (AmE), / kəˈrʌndəm / (BrE)
A very hard mineral, aluminum oxide in crystalline form, used as an abrasive and as the basis for gemstones such as ruby and sapphire.
