plague

noun
swarm of harmful insects
Pronunciation: / pleɪɡ / (AmE), / pleɪɡ / (BrE)
A large and destructive swarm of insects, especially when they attack crops or plants.

Examples

plague of locusts
A plague of beetles made it impossible to save the fruit trees.
Farmers feared another plague would ruin the year's harvest.
The crops were destroyed by a plague of locusts that swept through the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym – infestation: the state of being invaded or overrun by pests
