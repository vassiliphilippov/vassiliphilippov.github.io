to pull

verb
remove feathers from poultry
Pronunciation: / pʊl / (AmE), / pʊl / (BrE)
(of poultry) to remove the feathers from a bird, such as a chicken, as part of preparation for cooking.

Examples

to pull a chicken
She learned how to pull poultry as part of her kitchen duties.
The chef began to pull the chicken before seasoning it.
Traditionally, you would pull the capon by hand after scalding it.
