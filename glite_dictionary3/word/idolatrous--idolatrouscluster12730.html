idolatrous

adjective
relating to idolatry
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɑːlətrəs / (AmE), / aɪˈdɒlətrəs / (BrE)
showing or relating to excessive devotion or worship, especially of idols or in a figurative sense

Examples

idolatrous worship practices
Critics described the luxurious rituals of the palace as dangerously idolatrous.
Many reformers denounced the practice as idolatrous and sought to abolish it.
The ancient tribe was known for its idolatrous ceremonies and sacred images.
He warned against idolatrous worship of material possessions, urging a simpler way of life.
Some critics accuse fans of having an idolatrous attachment to celebrity culture.
The crowd gave an idolatrous reception to the pop singer, screaming and waving in adoration.
