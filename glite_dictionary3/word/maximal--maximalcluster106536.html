maximal

adjective
as great as possible; highest degree
Pronunciation: / ˈmæk.sɪ.məl / (AmE), / ˈmæk.sɪ.məl / (BrE)
as great or complete as possible; the highest or greatest possible in degree, amount, or extent

Examples

maximal effort
The athlete's training program was designed to achieve maximal performance on race day.
This treatment offers maximal benefit with minimal risk to the patient.
We measured the maximal length the material could stretch before breaking.

Related Terms

  • antonym – minimal: as small or slight as possible; the opposite of maximal
