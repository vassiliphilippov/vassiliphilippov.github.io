eve

noun
latter part of the day
Pronunciation: / iv / (AmE), / iːv / (BrE)
the latter part of the day, from late afternoon until nightfall; evening or twilight

Examples

quiet eve
During the cool eve, the garden came alive with the sounds of crickets.
The golden hues of the eve painted the sky with breathtaking colors.
We sat by the fireplace and talked quietly throughout the eve.
