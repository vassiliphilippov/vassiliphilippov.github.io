hand-hold

noun
support to grip physically or guidance for beginners
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌhoʊld / (AmE), / ˈhændˌhəʊld / (BrE)
something that can be gripped with the hand for support, especially while climbing; also, a source of guidance or support for beginners.

Examples

hand-hold for support
For new users, the simple interface acts as a hand-hold in navigating the system.
She searched the rock face for a secure hand-hold before pulling herself up.
The instructor's clear explanations were a helpful hand-hold for the class.
