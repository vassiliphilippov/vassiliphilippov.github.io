informally

adverb
in a colloquial or casual way
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɔrməli / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːməli / (BrE)
in a way that uses colloquial or everyday language rather than formal language

Examples

speak informally with friends
He greeted everyone informally, using first names and jokes.
She explained the rules informally, making everyone feel at ease.
The new policy was informally announced at the company picnic before the official email went out.

Related Terms

  • antonym – formally: in a way that follows established conventions or formal language
