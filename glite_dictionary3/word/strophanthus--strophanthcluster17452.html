Strophanthus

noun
genus of tropical Asian and African plants
Pronunciation: / stroʊˈfænθəs / (AmE), / strəʊˈfænθəs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical shrubs, woody vines, and small trees native to Asia and Africa, some species of which are known for their toxic seeds used as arrow poisons.
