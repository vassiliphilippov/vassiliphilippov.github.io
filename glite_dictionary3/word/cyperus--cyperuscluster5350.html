Cyperus

noun
genus of grasslike sedge plants
Pronunciation: / saɪˈpɪrəs / (AmE), / saɪˈpɪərəs / (BrE)
A genus of grasslike, rhizomatous herbs in the sedge family (Cyperaceae), found throughout most regions of the world except very cold areas.
