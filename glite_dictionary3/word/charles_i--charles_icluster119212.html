Charles I

proper noun
King of the Franks and Holy Roman Emperor (742–814)
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃɑrlz ðə ˈfɜrst / (AmE), / ˌtʃɑːlz ðə ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
(742–814) King of the Franks and Holy Roman Emperor, notable for uniting much of Western Europe during the early Middle Ages; also known as Charlemagne.
