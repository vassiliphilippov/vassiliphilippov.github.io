novelisation

noun
making a novel from another work
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːvəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒvələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adapting a film, television show, or other work into the form of a novel

Examples

novelisation of a movie
