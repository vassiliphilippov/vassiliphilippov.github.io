tammy

noun
Scottish woolen cap
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmi / (AmE), / ˈtæmi / (BrE)
A round, flat woolen cap of Scottish origin, typically with a pompom in the center, also known as a tam o'shanter.

He wore a traditional tartan tammy when visiting the Scottish Highlands.
