graining

noun
a wood-like texture or pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
a texture or pattern resembling the grain of wood, often produced artificially to imitate wood surfaces
