due

adjective
owed and payable (money/debt)
Pronunciation: / duː / (AmE), / djuː / (BrE)
(of a sum of money or obligation) owed and payable, or something that is deserved or appropriate

Examples

due on payment
No interest will be charged if the bill is paid as soon as it becomes due.
The rent is due on the first day of each month.
Your final payment will be due at the end of the year.
In due course, the matter will be reviewed by the board.
The company was accused of not taking due care to protect consumer data.
With all due respect, I must disagree with your opinion.

Related Terms

  • synonym – appropriate: suitable or proper in the circumstances
  • synonym – deserved: rightfully earned or merited
  • antonym – overdue: (of a payment) past the expected or required time of payment
  • synonym – payable: (of money) required to be paid
  • antonym – undue: not appropriate or not deserved
