to interleave

verb
insert blank pages in a book
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈliv /; / ˌɪntɚˈliv / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈliːv / (BrE)
to insert or arrange things (such as pages, sheets, or layers) between other things in an alternating or layered way
