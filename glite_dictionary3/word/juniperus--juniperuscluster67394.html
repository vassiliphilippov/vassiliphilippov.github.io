Juniperus

noun
genus of juniper trees and shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuːnɪˈpɛrəs / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːnɪˈpɛːrəs / (BrE)
A genus of evergreen coniferous trees and shrubs commonly known as junipers, characterized by needle-like or scale-like leaves and berry-like cones.
