vividness

noun
quality of being clear and intense
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪvɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈvɪvɪdnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being strikingly clear, intense, or full of life and detail, especially in appearance, description, or imagination

Examples

vividness of imagination
Readers admired the vividness of the author's descriptions, which brought each scene to life.
The vividness of her memories made the past feel almost real again.
The artist used bold colors to enhance the vividness of his paintings.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dullness: lack of vividness or clarity
  • synonym – intensity: the quality of being intense
