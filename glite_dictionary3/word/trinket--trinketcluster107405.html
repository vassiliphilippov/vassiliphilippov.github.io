trinket

noun
small inexpensive ornament or jewelry
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪŋkət / (AmE), / ˈtrɪŋkɪt / (BrE)
a small decorative object or piece of jewelry that is inexpensive and often considered to have little real value

Examples

pretty trinket
He gave his friend a tiny trinket as a keepsake from his travels.
She wore a shining trinket around her neck, though it held no real value.
The market stalls were filled with colorful trinkets and souvenirs for tourists.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bauble: cheap, showy piece of jewelry
  • antonym – valuable: something of great value, especially jewelry
