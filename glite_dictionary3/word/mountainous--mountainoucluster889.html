mountainous

adjective
containing many mountains
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊn.tən.əs /; / ˈmaʊntənəs / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪ.nəs /; / ˈmaʊntɪnəs / (BrE)
(of land or a region) having many mountains or being very hilly or craggy
