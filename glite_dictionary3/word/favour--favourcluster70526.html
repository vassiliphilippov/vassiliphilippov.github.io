favour

noun
small gift for party guests
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvə / (BrE)
A small gift given to a guest at a party, especially as a souvenir to thank them for attending.

Examples

party favour
At the dinner, every place setting had a small favour tied with a ribbon.
Each wedding guest received a lovely favour as a token of appreciation.
The children took home colourful favours from the birthday party.
