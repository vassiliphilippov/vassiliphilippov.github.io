bookmobile

noun
mobile library vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊk.moʊˌbiːl / (AmE), / ˈbʊk.məʊˌbiːl / (BrE)
a large vehicle equipped with shelves of books and used as a mobile library, typically serving communities without easy access to a library building

Examples

the bookmobile visits town
Children eagerly waited for the bookmobile to arrive at the school parking lot.
The city sends a bookmobile to rural communities every week, bringing books to people who can't visit the library.
The library's old bookmobile was replaced with a modern, eco-friendly vehicle.
