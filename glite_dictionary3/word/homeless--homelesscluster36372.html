homeless

adjective
without a home or security
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊm.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm.ləs / (BrE)
Without a home; lacking permanent shelter, stability, or security, either physically or in a figurative or spiritual sense.

Examples

feel homeless inside
After losing his job, Mark felt completely homeless in the bustling city.
She sometimes described herself as homeless in spirit, searching for a place to belong.
The charity provides support and shelter to homeless families during the winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – destitute: without the basic necessities of life
  • antonym – housed: having a home or shelter
