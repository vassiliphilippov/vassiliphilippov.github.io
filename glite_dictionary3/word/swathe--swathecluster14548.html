to swathe

verb
wrap or envelop in cloth or coverings
Pronunciation: / sweɪð / (AmE), / swɒð, sweɪð / (BrE)
to wrap or envelop completely, especially in cloth or bandages

Examples

to swathe a wound
He swathed his injured arm with a clean bandage.
The nurse gently swathed the newborn in a soft blanket.
Thick fog swathed the harbor, hiding the ships from view.
