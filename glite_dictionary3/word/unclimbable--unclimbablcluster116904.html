unclimbable

adjective
impossible to climb
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklaɪməbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈklaɪməbəl /; / ʌnˈklaɪməbl / (BrE)
impossible to climb or go up, usually because it is too smooth, steep, or difficult

Examples
