viscose

noun
cellulose-based material for making rayon
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪsˌkoʊs / (AmE), / ˈvɪs.kəʊs / (BrE)
a cellulose-based material made by treating cellulose with caustic soda and carbon disulfide, used in the production of rayon and films

Examples

viscose solution process
Manufacturers often blend viscose with other fibers to improve texture and durability.
She prefers dresses made of viscose for their lightweight feel and breathability.
The shirt was made from soft viscose, which draped beautifully.
