to refuse

verb
elude or resist explanation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfjuz / (AmE), / rɪˈfjuːz / (BrE)
to elude or resist something, especially in a way that is difficult to explain or understand

Examples

refuse easy explanation
Her motives for leaving refuse easy interpretation by anyone who knew her.
The data refuses a straightforward analysis, confounding even the best scientists.
The mystery of the lost painting continues to refuse explanation, leaving experts puzzled.
