authorisation

noun
the power to give orders or make decisions
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the power or right to give orders, make decisions, or enforce obedience

Examples

authorisation to give orders
Only high-ranking officers have the authorisation to approve such requests.
Without proper authorisation, employees cannot enter the restricted area.
You need authorisation from your manager before accessing these documents.
