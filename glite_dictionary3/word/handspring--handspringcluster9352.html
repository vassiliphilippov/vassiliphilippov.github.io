handspring

noun
an acrobatic movement from feet to hands and back
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌsprɪŋ / (BrE)
An acrobatic movement in which a person goes from a standing position onto their hands and then pushes off to land back on their feet.

Examples

do a handspring
After lots of practice, he finally managed to land his first handspring.
She performed a perfect handspring during her gymnastics routine.
The coach demonstrated a handspring on the mat for the students.
