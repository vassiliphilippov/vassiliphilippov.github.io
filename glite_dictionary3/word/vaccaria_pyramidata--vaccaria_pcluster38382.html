Vaccaria pyramidata

noun
annual plant with pale rose flowers
Pronunciation: / vəˈkɛəriə ˌpɪr.əˈmɛɪ.də / (AmE), / vakˈkeəriə ˌpɪr.əˈmeɪ.də / (BrE)
A European annual plant with pale rose-colored flowers, grown ornamentally or found as a weed in grain fields; also introduced in North America and sometimes classified as a soapwort.
