pilot

noun
trial program for a TV/radio series
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
a single episode of a proposed television or radio series, produced to demonstrate the series' concept and attract sponsors or network approval

Examples

TV pilot episode
After watching the pilot, the producers decided to move forward with a full season.
Many great TV shows never make it past the pilot stage.
The network commissioned a pilot to test the new sitcom concept.
