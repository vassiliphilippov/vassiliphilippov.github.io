peremptory

adjective
insisting on immediate obedience; not allowing refusal
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛmptəri /; / pəˈrɛmp.tɔːr.i /; / pəˈrɛmp.tə.ri / (AmE), / pəˈrɛmptəri /; / pəˈrɛmp.təri / (BrE)
insisting on immediate obedience or action, often in a bossy or domineering way, leaving no opportunity for refusal
