smoother

noun
power tool for sanding wood
Pronunciation: / ˈsmuðər / (AmE), / ˈsmuːðə / (BrE)
a power tool designed for sanding wood, typically using an endless loop of sandpaper moved at high speed by an electric motor

Examples

electric smoother tool
He used a smoother to finish the surface of the wooden table.
Modern smoothers make it easier to achieve a fine finish on woodwork.
The carpenter replaced the sandpaper belt on his electric smoother.

  • synonym – sander: a power tool used for smoothing or polishing surfaces by abrasion with sandpaper
