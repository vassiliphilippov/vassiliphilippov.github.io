to twitch

verb
move or toss sharply; turn over
Pronunciation: / twɪtʃ / (AmE), / twɪtʃ / (BrE)
to move or toss something with a sudden, sharp motion that causes it to turn over in the air

Examples

to twitch a hat
He twitched his hat off, flipping it high above his head.
She twitched the pancake into the air with a practiced flick of her wrist.
The fisherman twitched the line sharply, causing the lure to tumble.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flip: to turn over with a sudden, quick movement
  • synonym – jerk: to move or cause to move with a sudden quick motion
