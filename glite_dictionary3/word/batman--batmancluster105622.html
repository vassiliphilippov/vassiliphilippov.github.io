batman

noun
military servant assigned to an officer
Pronunciation: / ˈbætmən / (AmE), / ˈbætmən / (BrE)
a male servant in the British military assigned to assist a commissioned officer with personal and domestic affairs

Examples

batman assists officer
During the First World War, a batman often carried messages and managed supplies for his superior.
The officer called his batman to help prepare his uniform for inspection.
Though the role is now outdated, stories of the loyal batman still appear in soldiers' memoirs.
