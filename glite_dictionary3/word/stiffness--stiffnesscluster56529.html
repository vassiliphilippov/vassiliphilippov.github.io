stiffness

noun
lack of ease or elegance in manner
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪfnəs / (AmE), / ˈstɪfnəs / (BrE)
a lack of ease, elegance, or naturalness in manner or movement, often caused by embarrassment or discomfort

Examples

stiffness in manner
A certain stiffness in his greeting suggested he felt uncomfortable.
He tried to hide his stiffness at the formal dinner, but it was obvious to everyone.
Her stiffness during the interview betrayed her nervousness.

Related Terms

  • synonym – awkwardness: the quality of being awkward or clumsy in manner
  • antonym – gracefulness: the quality of being graceful and elegant
