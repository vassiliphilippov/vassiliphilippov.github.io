to lend

verb
impart a quality or character
Pronunciation: / lɛnd / (AmE), / lɛnd / (BrE)
(figurative) To give or impart a particular quality, characteristic, or atmosphere to something.

Examples

lend importance
Her calm voice lent an air of dignity to the ceremony.
His humor lends warmth to the team's meetings.
The old paintings lend the room a sense of history.
