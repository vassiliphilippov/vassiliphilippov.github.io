pathos

noun
quality that evokes pity or sadness
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪθoʊs /; / ˈpeɪ.θɑs /; / ˈpeɪˌθɑs / (AmE), / ˈpæθɒs /; / ˈpeɪ.θɒs / (BrE)
a quality in art, writing, speech, or situations that makes people feel pity, sadness, or deep emotion

Related Terms

  • antonym – bathos: (in literature or speech) an effect of anticlimax created by unintentional lapse in mood from the sublime to the trivial
  • antonym – callousness: lack of empathy or compassion for others
  • synonym – emotion: a strong feeling such as love, anger, or joy
  • synonym – empathy: the ability to understand and share another person's feelings
  • synonym – pity: a feeling of sorrow for someone's misfortune
