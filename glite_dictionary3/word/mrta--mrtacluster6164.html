MRTA

noun
Peruvian Marxist guerrilla group (1980s–1990s)
Pronunciation: / ɛm ɑr ti eɪ / (AmE), / ɛm ɑː tiː eɪ / (BrE)
a Marxist-Leninist guerrilla group in Peru, active mainly in the 1980s and 1990s, known for its efforts to overthrow the Peruvian government.
