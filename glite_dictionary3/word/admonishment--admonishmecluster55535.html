admonishment

noun
a firm warning or rebuke
Pronunciation: / ædˈmɑːnɪʃmənt / (AmE), / ədˈmɒnɪʃmənt / (BrE)
a firm warning or expression of disapproval, especially given with the intention of correcting behavior

Examples

receive an admonishment
After the admonishment from her teacher, she resolved to focus more in class.
He accepted the admonishment quietly, knowing he needed to improve his performance.
The manager's admonishment made it clear that tardiness would not be tolerated.

  • synonym – rebuke: an expression of sharp disapproval or criticism
