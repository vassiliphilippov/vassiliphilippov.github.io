level

adjective
steady and unvarying (esp. voice/manner)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈlɛv(ə)l / (BrE)
not changing much and not showing sudden swings or variations; steady or even, especially in tone, mood, or manner.

Examples

level voice
Despite the bad news, she spoke in a level voice, hiding all emotion.
He kept a level head during the crisis and didn't panic.
Her level gaze made it clear that she expected an honest answer.
