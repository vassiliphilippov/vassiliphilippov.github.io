fitting

noun
standardized accessory for systems
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a small part or accessory, often standardized, used to connect or assemble components in a larger system, such as a pipe, electrical, or mechanical system

Examples

pipe fitting connector
All electrical fittings must be installed according to safety regulations.
She ordered several brass fittings to complete the restoration of the antique lamp.
The plumber replaced the old pipe fitting to stop the leak.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fixture: an object fixed in position, often permanently, in a building or system
