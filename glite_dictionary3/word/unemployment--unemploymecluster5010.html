unemployment

noun
state of being without work
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈplɔɪmənt / (BrE)
The state of not having a paid job, especially when actively seeking work.

Examples

high unemployment rate
Graduates often worry about facing unemployment after finishing university.
Rising unemployment has become a major concern for the government in recent years.
The company closed several factories, resulting in increased unemployment in the region.
