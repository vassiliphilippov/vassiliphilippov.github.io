hematocrit

noun
proportion of red blood cells in blood
Pronunciation: / hɪˈmætəkrɪt / (AmE), / hɪˈmatəkrɪt / (BrE)
the proportion of the volume of red blood cells to the total volume of blood, usually expressed as a percentage

Examples

hematocrit level in blood
After the transfusion, her hematocrit increased to a healthy range.
Doctors routinely check the hematocrit level during annual physicals.
The patient's hematocrit was lower than normal, indicating possible anemia.
