to airfreight

verb
send cargo by air transport
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌfreɪt / (AmE), / ˈeəˌfreɪt / (BrE)
to transport (goods or cargo) by aircraft; to send by air.

Examples

to airfreight goods overseas
Due to the tight deadline, we decided to airfreight the components directly from Germany.
The company regularly airfreights fresh flowers to customers around the world.
They airfreighted the medical supplies to the remote region to save time.

Related Terms

  • synonym – airlift: to transport supplies or people by air, especially in emergency situations
  • antonym – sea freight: to transport goods by sea, as opposed to by air
  • synonym – ship: to send goods by any form of transport
