inflexibleness

noun
lack of physical flexibility
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɛksəbəlnɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɛksɪb(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking physical flexibility; rigidity or stiffness

Examples

physical inflexibleness
His inflexibleness made it difficult for him to touch his toes during the workout.
Physical therapy helped her overcome the inflexibleness in her joints.
The inflexibleness of the old wooden board caused it to snap easily.
