Diapsida

noun
major reptile group (zoology)
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæpsɪdə /; / daɪˈæpsədə / (AmE), / daɪˈapsɪdə / (BrE)
a major group of reptiles (excluding turtles) with two openings in the skull, now usually divided into Lepidosauria and Archosauria
