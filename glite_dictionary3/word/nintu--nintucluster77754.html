Nintu

proper noun
Mesopotamian goddess of birth
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪntu / (AmE), / ˈnɪntuː / (BrE)
A Mesopotamian goddess worshipped as the 'Lady of Birth', also known as Ninkhursag.

Examples

Nintu goddess of birth
Archaeological findings reference Nintu in inscriptions dedicated to fertility.
In ancient texts, Nintu is revered as the creator of mankind.
Priests offered prayers to Nintu during childbirth rituals in Mesopotamia.
