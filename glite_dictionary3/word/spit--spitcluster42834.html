to spit

verb
pierce with a skewer for cooking
Pronunciation: / spɪt / (AmE), / spɪt / (BrE)
to pierce or thread (meat or other food) with a skewer or spit for cooking, especially over a fire

Examples

to spit meat for cooking
Before roasting, the chef will spit the lamb and season it with herbs.
Large chunks of meat are often spitted over an open flame.
They spitted pieces of chicken for the barbecue.
