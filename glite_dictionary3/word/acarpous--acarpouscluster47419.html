acarpous

adjective
not producing fruit (of plants)
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑr.pəs / (AmE), / əˈkɑː.pəs / (BrE)
(of a plant) not producing fruit; barren.

Examples

acarpous plant species
Botanists examined the acarpous branches to determine the cause of their barrenness.
Many cactus species are acarpous and only flower without producing any fruit.
The acarpous plant failed to yield any fruit throughout the growing season.

Related Terms

  • synonym – barren: unable to produce fruit or offspring
  • antonym – fertile: (of a plant) able to produce fruit or offspring
