to hoof

verb
walk, especially quickly (informal)
Pronunciation: / hʊf / (AmE), / hʊf / (BrE)
(informal) To walk, especially to do so quickly or on foot, often implying a long or energetic journey.

Examples

to hoof it home
After missing the bus, we had to hoof it all the way home.
If you want to save money, you can hoof it to the train station instead of taking a taxi.
They decided to hoof it across town to get some exercise.

Related Terms

  • synonym – walk: to move at a regular pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
