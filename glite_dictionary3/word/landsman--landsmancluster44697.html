landsman

noun
inexperienced male sailor
Pronunciation: / ˈlændzmən / (AmE), / ˈlændzmən / (BrE)
a man who is inexperienced at sea, especially a sailor on his first voyage

Examples

new landsman on ship
Every landsman must learn quickly if he hopes to become a true sailor.
On his first day aboard the ship, the landsman struggled to keep his balance on the deck.
The seasoned sailors teased the landsman for not knowing the names of the ropes.
