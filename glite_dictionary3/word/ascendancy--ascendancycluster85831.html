ascendancy

noun
state of having power or control
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛn.dən.si / (AmE), / əˈsɛn.dən.si / (BrE)
the state of having power or influence over others; domination or control

Examples

ascendancy over rivals
His company's ascendancy in the tech industry was undisputed for years.
She struggled to maintain her ascendancy within the organization amid increasing competition.
The political party rose to ascendancy after winning the election by a landslide.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dominance: the state of being dominant or having power over others
  • antonym – subordination: the state of being lower in rank or position
