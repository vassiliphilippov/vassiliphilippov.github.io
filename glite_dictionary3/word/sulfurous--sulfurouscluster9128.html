sulfurous

adjective
oppressively hot and humid (air or atmosphere)
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlfɚəs / (AmE), / ˈsʌlfjʊərəs / (BrE)
(of air or atmosphere) characterized by oppressive heat and humidity.

Examples

sulfurous air
A sulfurous haze settled over the city after the afternoon thunderstorm.
The air outside was sulfurous and heavy, making it difficult to breathe.
We struggled through the sulfurous heat of midsummer as we hiked up the hill.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stifling: (of air or atmosphere) very hot and making breathing difficult
  • synonym – sultry: (of air or weather) hot and humid; oppressive
