doorknocker

noun
decorative device for knocking on a door
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrˌnɑkər / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌnɒkə / (BrE)
a decorative metal object attached by a hinge to a door, used for knocking to announce a visitor's presence

Examples

doorknocker on a door
He rapped twice with the doorknocker before waiting for someone to answer.
She lifted the heavy doorknocker and let it fall with a loud clang.
The old brass doorknocker was shaped like a lion's head.
