thoughtlessly

adverb
without careful consideration
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔt.ləs.li / (AmE), / ˈθɔːt.ləs.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of careful thought or concern for others

Examples

to speak thoughtlessly
He thoughtlessly interrupted the meeting, annoying everyone present.
Leaving the lights on all night is not only wasteful but also thoughtlessly inconsiderate.
She spoke thoughtlessly and later regretted her harsh words.
He thoughtlessly interrupted her while she was speaking.
She thoughtlessly tossed the letter in the trash without reading it.
The children thoughtlessly left their toys all over the floor.
