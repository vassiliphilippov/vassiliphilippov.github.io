to allow

verb
deduct as a discount or trade-in
Pronunciation: / əˈlaʊ / (AmE), / əˈlaʊ / (BrE)
to deduct or give a specified sum as a discount or trade-in value, especially when accepting something in exchange

Examples

allow a discount
The dealership allowed us a generous sum for our old car as part of the new purchase.
The shop allowed me $100 on my old phone when I traded it in.
They allowed a 10% discount for early payment of the invoice.
