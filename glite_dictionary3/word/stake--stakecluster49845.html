to stake

verb
lock crypto tokens to earn rewards
Pronunciation: / steɪk / (AmE), / steɪk / (BrE)
(in blockchain and cryptocurrency) to lock or commit cryptocurrency tokens to a network or protocol in order to support operations (for example, validating transactions) and earn rewards.

Examples

to stake cryptocurrency
Many investors choose to stake their cryptocurrencies to earn passive income from network rewards.
She staked her ETH on a validator and began receiving small daily interest payments.
Before participating, read the white paper to understand how you will stake tokens safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym – delegate: to assign or entrust tokens to a validator or staking pool in some proof-of-stake systems.
  • synonym – lock (up): to make assets temporarily unavailable for withdrawal, for instance to participate in a protocol.
