Mohican

noun
the Algonquian language of the Mohican
Pronunciation: / moʊˈhiːkən / (AmE), / məʊˈhiːkən / (BrE)
the now-extinct Algonquian language formerly spoken by the Mohican people of the northeastern United States

Examples
