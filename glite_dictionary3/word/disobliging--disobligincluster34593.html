disobliging

adjective
deliberately unhelpful; unaccommodating
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.əˈblaɪ.dʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈblɪdʒ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
deliberately unhelpful or unwilling to do favors for others; unaccommodating.
