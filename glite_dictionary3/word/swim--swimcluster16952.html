to swim

verb
be covered/submerged in liquid
Pronunciation: / swɪm / (AmE), / swɪm / (BrE)
(of an object) to be covered with or submerged in a liquid, especially abundantly.

Examples

swim in sauce
His pancakes arrived swimming in syrup, just the way he liked them.
The pasta was swimming in creamy sauce, almost too much to eat.
The vegetables were swimming in a pool of oil.
