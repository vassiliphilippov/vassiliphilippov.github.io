gestation

noun
development of an idea or plan
Pronunciation: / dʒɛˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / dʒɛˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of developing an idea or plan in the mind before it becomes fully formed

Examples

gestation of ideas
His novel went through a long period of gestation before he finally started writing.
The gestation of that business idea began during her college years.
The gestation of the project took several months before it was ready to launch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – incubation: the process of developing something, especially an idea, before it is ready
