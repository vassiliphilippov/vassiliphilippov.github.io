amnios

noun
innermost membranous sac around an embryo
Pronunciation: / ˈæmniˌɑs / (AmE), / ˈamniɒs / (BrE)
the thin innermost membranous sac that encloses the developing embryo of reptiles, birds, and mammals

Examples

the amnios surrounds embryo
A rupture of the amnios can lead to complications during pregnancy.
Scientists studied the amnios to understand embryonic development in birds.
The amnios provides a protective environment for the developing fetus.
