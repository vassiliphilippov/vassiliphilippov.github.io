untruthfully

adverb
in an untruthful or dishonest manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtruθfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈtruːθfəli / (BrE)
in a manner that is not truthful; in a way that involves lying or being dishonest

Examples

to answer untruthfully
He answered the question untruthfully to avoid getting into trouble.
She untruthfully claimed she had completed the assignment on time.
The witness spoke untruthfully during her testimony in court.
