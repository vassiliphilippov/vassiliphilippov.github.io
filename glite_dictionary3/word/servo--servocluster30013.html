servo

noun
automatic control mechanism with feedback
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːr.voʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vəʊ / (BrE)
A control mechanism that uses a small input to regulate a much larger output of power, often incorporating a feedback system to maintain stability.

Examples

servo motor system
A malfunction in the servo caused the control surfaces to stop responding properly.
He connected the servo to the control board to automate the door system.
The servo in the robot arm ensures precise movement and positioning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – servomechanism: automatic device that uses feedback to operate a mechanism
