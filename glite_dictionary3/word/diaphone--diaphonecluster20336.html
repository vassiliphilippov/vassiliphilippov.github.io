diaphone

noun
two-tone foghorn
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəfəʊn / (BrE)
a type of foghorn that produces a signal consisting of two distinct tones, used especially as a maritime warning device

Examples

diaphone fog signal
As the fog rolled in, the deep blast of the diaphone echoed across the harbor.
Maritime authorities activated the diaphone during poor visibility to ensure the safety of passing vessels.
The lighthouse was equipped with a powerful diaphone to warn ships during foggy nights.
