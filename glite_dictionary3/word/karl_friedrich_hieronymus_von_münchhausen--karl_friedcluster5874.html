Karl Friedrich Hieronymus von Münchhausen

proper noun
German nobleman known for exaggerated stories
Pronunciation: / kɑrl ˈfriːdərɪk hiˌɛrəˈnoʊməs vɑn ˈmʊnchaʊzən / (AmE), / kɑːl ˈfriːdərɪk hiːəˈrɒnɪməs fɒn ˈmʊnchaʊzən / (BrE)
(1720–1797) A German nobleman famous for telling wildly exaggerated stories about his adventures as a soldier and hunter; his name has become associated with any extravagant tale or tall story.
