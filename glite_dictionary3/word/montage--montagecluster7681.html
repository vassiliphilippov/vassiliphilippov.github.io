montage

noun
art composed from assembled images or paper pieces
Pronunciation: / mɑnˈtɑʒ / (AmE), / mɒnˈtɑːʒ / (BrE)
an artistic composition made by assembling various pieces, such as photographs or paper, into a unified image

Examples

montage of photos
She created a colorful montage to capture her memories from the trip.
The art exhibit featured a striking montage of vintage photographs and magazine clippings.
The film began with a rapid montage of city scenes and bustling crowds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – collage: an artistic composition made by gluing items onto a surface
