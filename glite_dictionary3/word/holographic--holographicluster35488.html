holographic

adjective
written in the author's own hand
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːləˈɡræfɪk /; / ˌhoʊ.ləˈɡræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɒləˈɡræfɪk /; / ˌhɒl.əˈɡræf.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a document) written entirely by hand by the person whose name is on it

Examples

holographic will
