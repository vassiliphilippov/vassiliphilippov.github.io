clannishly

adverb
in an exclusive, group-focused manner
Pronunciation: / ˈklæn.ɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˈklæn.ɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is characteristic of or resembling a close-knit group, especially with an exclusive or inward-looking attitude.

Examples

act clannishly at school
She wished her classmates would stop interacting so clannishly and include everyone more often.
Some neighborhoods are known to act clannishly, keeping outsiders at a distance.
The new team members felt unwelcome because the established staff behaved clannishly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clannish: showing strong loyalty to one's own group and often excluding others
