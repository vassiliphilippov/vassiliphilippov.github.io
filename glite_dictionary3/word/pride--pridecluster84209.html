pride

noun
source of happiness or satisfaction
Pronunciation: / praɪd / (AmE), / praɪd / (BrE)
a person or thing that gives great happiness or satisfaction, often because of a personal connection or achievement

Examples

pride and joy
Her son has always been her biggest pride since he became a doctor.
The school became the pride of the community after its recent achievements.
Winning the award was a moment of pride for the entire family.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disgrace: a person or thing causing shame or loss of respect
  • synonym – joy: a feeling of great pleasure and happiness
