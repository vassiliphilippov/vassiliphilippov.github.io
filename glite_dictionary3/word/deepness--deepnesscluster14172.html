deepness

noun
quality of low, voluminous pitch
Pronunciation: / ˈdip.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdiːp.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of having a low, loud, and resonant pitch, especially in a sound or voice

Examples

deepness of voice
She was impressed by the deepness of the bass notes during the concert.
The deepness of his voice startled everyone in the room.
The deepness of the rumbling thunder added to the storm's intensity.
