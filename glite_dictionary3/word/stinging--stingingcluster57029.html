stinging

adjective
harsh or hurtful in tone
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
(of speech or remarks) harsh, hurtful, or critical in tone or character; intended to wound or criticize sharply.

Examples

stinging criticism
He regretted his stinging words as soon as he saw her reaction.
Her stinging sarcasm left everyone in the room silent.
The politician responded with a stinging rebuke to his critics.

Related Terms

  • synonym – caustic: (of language) able to hurt or criticize severely
  • synonym – cutting: (of a remark) intending to wound or offend
  • antonym – gentle: showing a kind, mild, or tender quality
