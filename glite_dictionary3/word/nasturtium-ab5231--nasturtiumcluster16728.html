nasturtium

noun
a plant with brightly colored flowers
Pronunciation: / nəˈstɝrʃəm /; / nəˈstɝːʃəm / (AmE), / nəˈstɜːʃəm / (BrE)
a plant with round leaves and brightly colored flowers, often grown as an ornamental and also used as an edible herb in salads and cooking
