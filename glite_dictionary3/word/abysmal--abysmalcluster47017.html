abysmal

adjective
extremely bad or severe
Pronunciation: / əˈbɪzməl / (AmE), / əˈbɪzməl / (BrE)
Extremely bad or severe; immeasurably great or appalling in extent, degree, or quality.

Examples

abysmal performance
His lack of knowledge about basic history facts was truly abysmal.
The living conditions in the camp were described as abysmal by aid workers.
The team delivered an abysmal performance that disappointed both fans and critics.

  • antonym – excellent: extremely good; of the highest quality
