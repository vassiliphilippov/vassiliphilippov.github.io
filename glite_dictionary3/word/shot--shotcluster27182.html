shot

noun
informal photograph
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt / (AmE), / ʃɒt / (BrE)
an informal photograph or a single continuous sequence in a film or video

Examples

take a shot with camera
He showed me a shot he took at last night's party.
She posted a candid shot of her friends on social media.
That's a great shot of you and your dog in the park.
That dramatic shot was filmed in a single take without any cuts.
The director asked for another shot to capture a better angle.
The opening shot of the movie shows the city skyline at dawn.

  • synonym – snapshot: an informal photograph taken quickly, especially without formal posing
  • synonym – take: an attempt or version of filming a particular scene or shot in a movie
