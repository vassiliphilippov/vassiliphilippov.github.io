teatime

noun
afternoon meal with tea and light food
Pronunciation: / ˈtiˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈtiːtaɪm / (BrE)
the time in the afternoon when tea and a light meal of sandwiches or cakes is traditionally served, especially in Britain
