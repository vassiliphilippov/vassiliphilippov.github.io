impulsively

adverb
in a sudden or unplanned way
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpʌlsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpʌlsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is driven by sudden urges or desires, without careful thought or planning.

Examples

to act impulsively
He bought the car impulsively, without comparing prices or thinking it through.
Mark quit his job impulsively after a heated argument with his boss.
Sometimes she speaks impulsively and then apologizes for her bluntness.

Related Terms

  • antonym – deliberately: with careful thought and intention
  • synonym – rashly: without careful consideration for consequences
