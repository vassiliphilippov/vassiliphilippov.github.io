to hog-tie

verb
to tie someone's limbs together tightly
Pronunciation: / hɑɡ taɪ /; / ˈhɑɡ.taɪ /; / hɑːɡ taɪ / (AmE), / hɒɡ taɪ /; / ˈhɒɡ.taɪ / (BrE)
to tie together the hands and feet (or all four legs) of a person or animal so that movement is very restricted
