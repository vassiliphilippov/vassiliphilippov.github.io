to pilot

verb
to navigate and direct a vehicle's course
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət / (BrE)
to operate or navigate a vehicle, especially an aircraft or a ship

Examples

to pilot a plane

A skilled crew member can pilot the helicopter in difficult weather conditions.
Captain Smith will pilot the plane across the Atlantic tonight.
She learned how to pilot a small aircraft during her training.
