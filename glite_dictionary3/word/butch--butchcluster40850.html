butch

adjective
showing strong masculine traits
Pronunciation: / bʊtʃ / (AmE), / bʊtʃ / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a man) having or displaying traditionally masculine traits in appearance or manner.

Examples

butch male behavior
He tried to sound more butch to fit in with the football team.
Jake always acts so butch around his friends to impress them.
Wearing leather jackets made them look even more butch than usual.
