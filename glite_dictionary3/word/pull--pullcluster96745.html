pull

noun
muscle or ligament strain injury
Pronunciation: / pʊl / (AmE), / pʊl / (BrE)
an injury caused by the overstretching or straining of a muscle or ligament

Examples

a thigh pull
After sprinting, he felt a pull in his thigh and had to stop running.
If you feel a pull during exercise, it is best to rest the muscle.
She missed the rest of the season due to a hamstring pull.
