Adapa

proper noun
Babylonian demigod; first man
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑːpə / (AmE), / əˈdɑːpə / (BrE)
In Babylonian mythology, a demigod and the first man, sometimes identified with Adam.

Examples

Adapa in Babylonian myth
In ancient texts, Adapa is described as a wise man favored by the god Ea.
Some scholars compare Adapa to the biblical Adam due to their similar roles in creation stories.
The myth of Adapa explains the origin of human mortality in Babylonian legend.
