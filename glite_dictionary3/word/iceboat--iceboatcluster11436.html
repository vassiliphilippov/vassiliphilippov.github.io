iceboat

noun
ship or boat for breaking ice
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈaɪs.bəʊt / (BrE)
a boat or ship with a reinforced bow designed for breaking through ice-covered waters and keeping navigation channels open

Examples

iceboat breaks ice
During harsh winters, an iceboat is essential for keeping trade routes accessible in the Arctic.
The iceboat cleared a path through the frozen harbor, allowing other vessels to follow safely.
The crew prepared the iceboat for its journey across the icy river.
