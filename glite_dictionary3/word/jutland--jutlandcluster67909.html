Jutland

proper noun
World War I naval battle off Denmark, 1916
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌtˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌtlənd / (BrE)
a major naval battle of World War I (1916) fought between British and German fleets off the coast of Denmark, notable for its indecisive outcome
