constraint

noun
physical restriction or limitation
Pronunciation: / kənˈstreɪnt / (AmE), / kənˈstreɪnt / (BrE)
the state of being restricted or held back, especially physically, by something that limits movement or freedom

Examples

physical constraint
Despite the constraint of handcuffs, he managed to stand up and move around.
Legal constraints prevented him from leaving the building until the investigation was complete.
The patient wore a special vest as a constraint to prevent accidental falls.

Related Terms

  • synonym – restraint: the action of holding something back or limiting it; also refers to physical restriction
