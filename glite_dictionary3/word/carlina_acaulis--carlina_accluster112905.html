Carlina acaulis

noun
stemless alpine perennial plant with large flowers
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈlaɪnə əˈkɔːlɪs / (AmE), / kɑːˈlɪnə əˈkɔːlɪs / (BrE)
A stemless perennial plant of the daisy family, native to alpine regions of southern and eastern Europe, with large silvery-white or purplish-brown flowers and spiny, hairy leaves.
