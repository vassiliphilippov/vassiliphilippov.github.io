electrocardiographic

adjective
related to electrocardiograph or ECG recording
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌkɑrdiəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌkɑːdiəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by an electrocardiograph, or to the recording of heart activity via electrical signals

Examples

electrocardiographic test results
Her electrocardiographic results showed normal sinus rhythm throughout the examination.
Researchers analyzed the electrocardiographic patterns to detect irregular heart rhythms.
The doctor reviewed the patient's electrocardiographic data before making a diagnosis.
