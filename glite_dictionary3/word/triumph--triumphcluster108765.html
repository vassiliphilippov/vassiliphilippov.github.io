triumph

noun
great joy from victory or achievement
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪəmf / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.əmf / (BrE)
a great feeling of joy or satisfaction that comes from a victory or achievement

Examples

a triumph of joy
Her smile radiated triumph as she accepted the award on stage.
The army marched through the city in a spirit of triumph after the battle.
Winning the championship was a moment of pure triumph for the whole team.

Related Terms

  • antonym – defeat: loss in a contest or battle
  • synonym – victory: defeat of an opponent or success in a struggle
