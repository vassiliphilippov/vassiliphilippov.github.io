acrid

adjective
harsh or bitter in tone
Pronunciation: / ˈækrɪd / (AmE), / ˈækrɪd / (BrE)
(of language or tone) harsh, bitter, or caustic; expressing criticism or hostility in an unpleasantly sharp way.

Examples

acrid remark
He answered the question with an acrid tone that surprised everyone in the room.
Her acrid comments during the meeting offended several colleagues.
The review was acrid, sparing no criticism of the film's flaws.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acerbic: (of speech or tone) sharp and forthright
  • synonym – caustic: (of remarks) severely critical or sarcastic
  • antonym – gentle: (of tone or language) mild or kind
