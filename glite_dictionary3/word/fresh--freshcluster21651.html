fresh

adjective
not salty (of water)
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ / (AmE), / frɛʃ / (BrE)
(of water) not containing salt; not derived from the sea or an ocean.

Examples

fresh water
After a week at sea, the sailors longed for a glass of fresh water.
Many fish species cannot survive outside of fresh water environments.
The lake provides a supply of fresh water for the entire city.

Related Terms

  • antonym – saltwater: water that contains a significant concentration of salt, esp. sea water
