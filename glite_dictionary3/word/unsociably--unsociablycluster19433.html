unsociably

adverb
in an unsociable manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsoʊʃəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsəʊʃəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is not sociable; in a way that avoids the company of others or social interaction.

Examples

to act unsociably at dinner
During dinner, he ate unsociably, barely speaking to anyone at the table.
She always sits unsociably in the corner at parties, rarely joining the conversation.
The team worked unsociably, each member keeping to themselves.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sociably: in a sociable manner; in a way that enjoys or seeks company
