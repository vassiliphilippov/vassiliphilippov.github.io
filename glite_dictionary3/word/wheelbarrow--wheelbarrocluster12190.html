wheelbarrow

noun
small cart with one wheel, for carrying loads
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlˌbæroʊ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlˌbærəʊ / (BrE)
a small cart with one wheel at the front and two long handles at the back, used for carrying small loads, especially in a garden or on a building site

Examples

garden wheelbarrow
He used a wheelbarrow to move soil from the garden to the compost heap.
She loaded the bricks into the wheelbarrow before wheeling them across the yard.
The old wheelbarrow had a flat tire and couldn't be pushed easily.
