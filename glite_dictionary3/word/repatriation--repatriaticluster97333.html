repatriation

noun
return to one's home country
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌpeɪtriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌpætriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of returning someone or something to their own country

Examples

repatriation of citizens
After years abroad, the diplomat's repatriation was celebrated with a formal ceremony.
The government organized the repatriation of citizens stranded overseas during the crisis.
The museum oversaw the repatriation of several ancient artifacts to their country of origin.
