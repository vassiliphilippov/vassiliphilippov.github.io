to trust

verb
confidently hope or expect something
Pronunciation: / trʌst / (AmE), / trʌst / (BrE)
to believe or confidently hope that something will happen or be the case; to expect and wish for a particular outcome

Examples

to trust your journey
Trust you had a pleasant journey and found everything to your satisfaction.
I trust you will consider my request favorably and respond soon.
We trust that the event will be a great success for the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expect: to regard something as likely to happen
  • synonym – hope: to desire with expectation of fulfillment
