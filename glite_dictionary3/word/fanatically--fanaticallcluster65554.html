fanatically

adverb
with extreme enthusiasm or zeal
Pronunciation: / fəˈnætɪkli / (AmE), / fəˈnætɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows excessive enthusiasm or extreme devotion to a cause, idea, or activity.

Examples

fanatically support a team
He fanatically collected rare stamps, dedicating hours to his hobby every week.
She fanatically supported her favorite team, never missing a single match.
The group fanatically defended their beliefs despite widespread criticism.
