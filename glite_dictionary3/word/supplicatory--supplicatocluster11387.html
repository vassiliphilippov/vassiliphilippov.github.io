supplicatory

adjective
expressing humble entreaty or prayer
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌplɪkəˌtɔri / (AmE), / səˈplɪkətəri / (BrE)
expressing or conveying a humble and earnest request or prayer; entreating in a supplicant manner

Examples

supplicatory prayer
His supplicatory tone made everyone in the room pay attention to his plea.
She raised her hands in a supplicatory gesture, hoping for mercy from the judges.
The letter was written in a supplicatory style, expressing humble requests for aid.
