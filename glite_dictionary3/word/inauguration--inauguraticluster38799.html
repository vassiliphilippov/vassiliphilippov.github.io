inauguration

noun
formal ceremony of induction into office
Pronunciation: / ɪˌnɔɡjəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌnɔːɡjʊˈreɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
a formal ceremony to mark the beginning of a leader's term or the opening of something important, especially the official induction of someone into office

Examples

presidential inauguration ceremony
Her speech at the inauguration set the tone for her presidency.
The inauguration of the new mayor drew a large crowd from across the city.
The museum will hold its official inauguration next weekend.
