poking

noun
sharp, quick jab or thrust
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
A sharp or quick hand gesture, especially resembling a jab or thrust with a finger or an object.

Examples

poking with a finger
A gentle poking was all it took to get their attention.
He silenced his friend with a swift poking of the finger.
The child’s constant poking annoyed everyone at the dinner table.
