aromatic

adjective
containing benzene rings (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌærəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
(Chemistry) Containing or relating to one or more benzene rings; having the structure or properties characteristic of benzene or its derivatives.

Examples

aromatic compound
Benzene is one of the most basic aromatic substances found in organic chemistry.
Many dyes are derived from aromatic compounds and are used in industry.
The laboratory synthesized an aromatic hydrocarbon as part of the experiment.
