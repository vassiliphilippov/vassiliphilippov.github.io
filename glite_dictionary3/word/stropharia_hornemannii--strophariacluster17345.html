Stropharia hornemannii

noun
species of large gilled mushroom
Pronunciation: / stroʊˈfɛəriə hɔr.nəˈmæn.i.aɪ / (AmE), / strɒˈfɛəriə hɔː.nɪˈmæn.ɪ.aɪ / (BrE)
A large gilled mushroom with a slimy purple or olive cap, purple-black gills as it matures, and a long stalk marked with remnants of a white sheath up to a ring.
