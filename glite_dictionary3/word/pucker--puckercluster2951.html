pucker

noun
an uneven fold or wrinkle in fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌkɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌkə / (BrE)
an irregular fold or wrinkle in an otherwise smooth surface, especially in cloth or fabric

Examples

a pucker in fabric
A faint pucker ran across the smooth tablecloth after it was washed.
She noticed a small pucker near the seam of her new dress.
The tailor carefully ironed out every pucker from the suit jacket.
