to convalesce

verb
recover after illness or injury
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛs / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈlɛs / (BrE)
to spend time recovering after an illness, injury, or medical treatment, gradually regaining health and strength

Examples

to convalesce at home
After being discharged from the hospital, he needed several weeks to convalesce at home.
Doctors advised that he convalesce slowly to regain his full strength.
She decided to convalesce by the seaside, where the air was fresh and clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym – recover: to return to a normal state of health or strength
  • antonym – relapse: to fall again into illness after partial recovery
