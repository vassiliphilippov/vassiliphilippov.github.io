modelling

noun
making preliminary sculptures for finished works
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːd(ə)lɪŋ /; / ˈmɑːdəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒd(ə)lɪŋ /; / ˈmɒdəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of creating or shaping a sculpture or artwork from a pliable material such as clay or wax, or the resulting artwork
