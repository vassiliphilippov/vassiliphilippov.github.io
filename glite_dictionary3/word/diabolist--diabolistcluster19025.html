diabolist

noun
person who worships Satan
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæbəlɪst / (AmE), / dɪˈæbəlɪst / (BrE)
A person who worships or supports Satan or is an adherent of Satanism.

Examples

a diabolist worships Satan
Accusations of being a diabolist were common during the witch trials in Europe.
He was fascinated by stories of diabolists and their forbidden rituals.
The novel's antagonist is a notorious diabolist who leads a secret cult.
