hogweed

noun
tall plant with thick stems and flower clusters
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡˌwid / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌwiːd / (BrE)
A tall, coarse plant with thick stems and clusters of white or purple flowers, sometimes considered invasive.

Examples

giant hogweed plant
Gardeners must wear gloves when removing hogweed because its sap can irritate the skin.
Giant hogweed is known for growing rapidly along riverbanks.
