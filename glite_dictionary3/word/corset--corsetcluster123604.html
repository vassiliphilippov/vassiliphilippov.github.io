corset

noun
close-fitting women's foundation garment
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.sɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.sɪt / (BrE)
a close-fitting undergarment worn by women to shape and support the torso, often extending from below the bust to the hips

Examples

wear a corset
Modern designers sometimes incorporate the corset style into evening wear.
She laced the corset tightly before putting on her dress for the costume party.
Victorian women often wore a corset to achieve a fashionable silhouette.
