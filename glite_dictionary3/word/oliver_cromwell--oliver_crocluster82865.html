Oliver Cromwell

proper noun
English general and statesman (1599–1658)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪvər ˈkrɒmwɛl / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪvə ˈkrɒmwəl / (BrE)
(1599–1658) An English general and statesman who led the parliamentary forces during the English Civil War and later ruled as Lord Protector of England.
