lockup

noun
fixed logo and wordmark arrangement
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈlɒkˌʌp / (BrE)
a fixed graphic arrangement of a logo, wordmark, and other brand elements designed to be used together as a single unit.

Examples

brand lockup design
The design guide specifies a single lockup for the logo and brand name on all materials.
Before printing, the marketing team reviewed every lockup to ensure consistent spacing and color use.
The agency provided vertical and horizontal lockup versions to fit different layout constraints.

Related Terms

  • synonym – logo: a symbol or design adopted by an organization to identify its products or brand
  • synonym – wordmark: a distinctively styled text treatment of a brand or company name used as a visual identifier
