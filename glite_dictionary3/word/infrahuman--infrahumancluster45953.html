infrahuman

adjective
evolutionarily below humans
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfɹəˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˌɪnfɹəˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
(of animals or organisms) belonging to a group that is evolutionarily below humans; less evolved than humans.

Examples

infrahuman species group
Certain diseases only affect infrahuman species and not humans.
Many biologists study the behavior of infrahuman primates to gain insights into human evolution.
The cognitive abilities of infrahuman animals are often compared to those of humans in psychological research.
