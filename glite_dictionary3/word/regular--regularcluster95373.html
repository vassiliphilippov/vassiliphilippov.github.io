regular

noun
frequent or habitual visitor
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
a person who frequently visits or is a consistent member or follower of a place, group, or organization

Examples

regular customer
Every morning, the regular sat at the same table by the window.
She quickly became a regular at the local coffee shop, where everyone knew her name.
The bartender greeted each regular with a friendly nod as they walked in.
As a regular, she was trusted with important tasks within the committee.
He is known as a party regular, always showing up for meetings and rallies.
The club's regulars played a crucial role in organizing the annual fundraiser.
