elegance

noun
simplicity and ingenious effectiveness
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlɪɡəns / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪɡəns / (BrE)
the quality of being simple, ingenious, and effective, especially in the solution of a problem in science or mathematics

Examples

elegance in design
He admired the elegance of the code, which solved the problem in just a few lines.
The elegance of her mathematical proof impressed the entire faculty.
The scientist was praised for the elegance of his experiment's design.
