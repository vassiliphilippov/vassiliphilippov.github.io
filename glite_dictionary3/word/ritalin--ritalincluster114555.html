Ritalin

noun
central nervous system stimulant drug
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtəˌlɪn / (AmE), / ˈrɪtəlɪn / (BrE)
A trademarked drug used as a central nervous system stimulant, commonly prescribed to treat attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and narcolepsy.
