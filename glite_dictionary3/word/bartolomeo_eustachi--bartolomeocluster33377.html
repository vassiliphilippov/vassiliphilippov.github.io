Bartolomeo Eustachi

proper noun
Italian anatomist, father of modern anatomy
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑrtəloʊˈmeɪoʊ juːˈstæki / (AmE), / ˌbɑːtələˈmeɪəʊ juːˈstæki / (BrE)
(1520–1574) An Italian anatomist known as a father of modern anatomy, noted for his detailed descriptions of the ear and the heart.
