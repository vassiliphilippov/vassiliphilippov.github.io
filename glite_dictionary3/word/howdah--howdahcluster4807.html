howdah

noun
seat for riding on an elephant or camel
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊdə / (AmE), / ˈhaʊdə / (BrE)
a (usually canopied) seat or platform used for riding on the back of a camel or elephant, especially in South and Southeast Asia.

Examples

howdah on an elephant
Carved ivory panels decorated the ancient howdah in the museum display.
The prince watched the parade from the golden howdah atop the elephant.
Tourists enjoyed a majestic view from the howdah during the festival procession.
