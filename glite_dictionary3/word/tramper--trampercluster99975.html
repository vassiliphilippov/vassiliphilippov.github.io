tramper

noun
person who walks noisily or stamps
Pronunciation: / ˈtræmpər / (AmE), / ˈtræmpə / (BrE)
a person who walks with a heavy, noisy step, often stamping on the ground

Examples

noisy tramper upstairs
As a child, she was such a tramper that her footsteps shook the hallway.
The tramper made the floorboards creak with every step.
The noisy tramper could be heard long before he entered the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stomper: someone who stomps (walks with forceful, noisy steps)
  • antonym – tiptoer: someone who walks on tiptoe, moving quietly
