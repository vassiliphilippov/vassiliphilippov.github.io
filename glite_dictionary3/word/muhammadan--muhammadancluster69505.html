Muhammadan

noun
archaic term for a Muslim
Pronunciation: / məˈhæmədən / (AmE), / mʊˈhæmədən / (BrE)
an archaic term for a follower of the prophet Muhammad; a Muslim (now considered outdated and sometimes offensive)
