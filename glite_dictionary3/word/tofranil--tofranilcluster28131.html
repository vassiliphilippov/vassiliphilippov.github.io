Tofranil

noun
antidepressant drug (trademark name)
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊfrənɪl / (AmE), / ˈtəʊfrənɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked drug name for imipramine, a tricyclic antidepressant used to treat clinical depression and sometimes other conditions.

Examples

take Tofranil tablets
Tofranil is sometimes used to treat enuresis in children as well as depression.
After months of therapy, her doctor prescribed Tofranil to help manage her depression.
Some patients experience drowsiness when taking Tofranil for the first time.
