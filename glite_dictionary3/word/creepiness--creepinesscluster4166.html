creepiness

noun
unpleasant feeling of unease
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːpinəs / (AmE), / ˈkriːpinɪs / (BrE)
an unpleasant feeling of discomfort or unease, often likened to the sensation of insects crawling on the skin

Examples

strange creepiness
She couldn't shake the creepiness she felt after watching the horror movie alone.
The creepiness of the old abandoned house made everyone uneasy.
There was a distinct creepiness in the way the photograph seemed to follow your gaze.

Related Terms

  • antonym – comfort: a state of physical or mental well-being, free from unease
  • synonym – uneasiness: a feeling of discomfort or anxiety
