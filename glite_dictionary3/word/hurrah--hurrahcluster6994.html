hurrah

noun
a cheer of joy or approval
Pronunciation: / həˈrɑ / (AmE), / hʊˈrɑː / (BrE)
a loud shout of joy or approval, typically given to celebrate a victory or success

Examples

a loud hurrah
Let's give the departing captain a big hurrah to show our appreciation.
The announcement of the new policy was met with a chorus of hurrah from the audience.
The crowd erupted in a loud hurrah as the team scored the winning goal.

Related Terms

  • antonym – boo: a shout to express disapproval or contempt
  • synonym – hooray: a shout of joy or approval, variant spelling
