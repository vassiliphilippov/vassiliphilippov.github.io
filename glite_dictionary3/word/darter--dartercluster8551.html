darter

noun
fish-eating bird with a long neck
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrtər / (AmE), / ˈdɑːtə / (BrE)
A fish-eating bird of warm inland waters, having a long flexible neck and a slender, sharp-pointed bill; also called anhinga or snakebird.

Examples

darter bird fishing
A darter catches its prey with its sharp, pointed bill.
The darter spread its wings to dry after diving for fish.
We spotted a darter gliding gracefully across the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anhinga: another name for darter, a fish-eating bird with a long neck
  • synonym – snakebird: common name for darter (bird) due to snake-like neck
