chestnut

noun
a reddish-brown horse
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
A horse with a dark golden-brown or reddish-brown coat.

Examples

chestnut horse
Several chestnuts grazed peacefully in the sunlit meadow.
She always preferred riding a chestnut over other horses for their calm temperament.
The winner of the race was a beautiful chestnut with a gleaming coat.
