skim

adjective
milk with cream removed
Pronunciation: / skɪm / (AmE), / skɪm / (BrE)
(Of milk or a milk product) having had the cream removed.

Examples

skim milk
She prefers to drink skim milk in her coffee.
Some recipes call for skim yogurt to reduce fat content.
The cafeteria only serves skim milk in small cartons.

Related Terms

  • synonym – skimmed: (Of milk) same as 'skim' in British English; having had the cream removed.
  • antonym – whole: (Of milk) containing all its natural fat; not skimmed.
