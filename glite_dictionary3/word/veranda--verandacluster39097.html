veranda

noun
roofed porch along outside of a building
Pronunciation: / vəˈrændə / (AmE), / vəˈrɑːndə / (BrE)
A roofed platform along the outside of a house, level with the ground floor and often partly enclosed.

Examples

shady veranda
In summer, guests often gather on the shady veranda to escape the heat.
The house was famous for its wide veranda wrapped around three sides.
We enjoyed our tea while sitting on the veranda overlooking the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym – balcony: a platform projecting from the wall of a building, typically above the ground floor
  • synonym – patio: a paved outdoor area adjoining a house
  • synonym – porch: a covered shelter projecting in front of the entrance of a building
