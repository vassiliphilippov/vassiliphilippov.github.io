bulimic

noun
person with bulimia (eating disorder)
Pronunciation: / buˈlɪmɪk, bəˈliːmɪk / (AmE), / bjuːˈlɪmɪk, bəˈliːmɪk / (BrE)
a person who suffers from bulimia, an eating disorder characterized by episodes of overeating followed by fasting or purging

Examples

a bulimic person
As a teenager, she battled for years as a bulimic before seeking help.
Experts say it's important to understand the psychological challenges faced by every bulimic.
The support group provided a safe space for each bulimic to share their experiences and struggles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anorexic: a person suffering from anorexia nervosa
  • synonym – bulimia: an eating disorder with bouts of overeating and purging
  • antonym – healthy: not suffering from an illness, well
