favorite

noun
person likely to win; expected winner
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪvərət / (AmE), / ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
A person or competitor who is considered most likely to win a competition or contest.

Examples

the favorite to win
After his impressive record this season, he is the clear favorite to win the tournament.
Despite being the favorite, she remained humble and focused throughout the competition.
The horse named Thunder was the favorite according to most betting odds.

Related Terms

  • antonym – underdog: a competitor thought unlikely to win
