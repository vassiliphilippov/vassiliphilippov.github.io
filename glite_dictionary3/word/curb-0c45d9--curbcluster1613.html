curb

noun
the act of restraining or limiting
Pronunciation: / kɝb / (AmE), / kɜːb / (BrE)
the act of restraining or limiting something, especially to prevent excess or uncontrolled behavior.

Examples

curb on spending
A strict diet can act as a curb to unhealthy eating habits.
The government has introduced measures to put a curb on pollution.
The new law places a curb on excessive executive bonuses.

  • synonym – limit: a restriction or boundary
  • synonym – restraint: the action of keeping something under control
