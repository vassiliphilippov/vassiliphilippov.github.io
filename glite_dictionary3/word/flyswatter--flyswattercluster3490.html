flyswatter

noun
device for killing flies and insects
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌswɑtər / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌswɒtə / (BrE)
A device with a flat mesh or plastic surface and a handle, used for killing flies and other insects.

Examples

flyswatter kills flies
He missed the mosquito with the flyswatter, and it escaped into another room.
She grabbed the flyswatter to get rid of the buzzing fly in the kitchen.
We keep a flyswatter next to the window during the summer months.
