carotene

noun
yellow or orange plant pigment
Pronunciation: / ˈkærətiːn /; / ˈkær.əˌtin / (AmE), / ˈkærətiːn /; / ˈkær.əˌtiːn / (BrE)
a yellow or orange pigment found in plants (especially carrots) that the body can use to make vitamin A
