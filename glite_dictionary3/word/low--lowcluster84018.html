low

adjective
of low rank or status
Pronunciation: / loʊ / (AmE), / ləʊ / (BrE)
of low or inferior rank, status, or quality; humble or modest in position or standing; unrefined or lacking sophistication

Examples

low social status
Despite his low rank, he performed his duties with utmost dedication.
He was born into a low social class and worked hard to succeed.
The workers in the low positions often received little recognition.
Critics described the movie's jokes as low and lacking refinement.
Some people enjoy low comedy, while others find it distasteful.
The play was criticized for its low humor and unsophisticated jokes.

  • synonym – crude: lacking in culture, refinement, or taste
  • antonym – high: of elevated rank or status
  • synonym – humble: of low social, administrative, or political rank
  • synonym – modest: not having or showing a high opinion of oneself or one's status
  • antonym – refined: cultured and with good taste; the opposite of low in this sense
