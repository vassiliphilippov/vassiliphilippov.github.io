quango

noun
semi-independent government-funded organization
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːŋɡoʊ /; / ˈkwɑːŋˌgoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkwæŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
an organization that is funded by the government but operates independently and is not a direct part of government administration
