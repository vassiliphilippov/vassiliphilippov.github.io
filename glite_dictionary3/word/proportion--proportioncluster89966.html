proportion

noun
relationship between part and whole (fraction)
Pronunciation: / pɹəˈpɔɹʃən / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃən / (BrE)
the relationship of one part to another or to the whole, expressed as a quotient or fraction

Examples

proportion of students
A small proportion of the population voted in the last election.
Only a tiny proportion of the budget is spent on education.
The proportion of sugar to flour is important in this recipe.
