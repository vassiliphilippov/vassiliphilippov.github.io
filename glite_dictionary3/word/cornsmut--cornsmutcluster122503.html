cornsmut

noun
fungal disease of maize
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌsmʌt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌsmʌt / (BrE)
a fungal disease affecting maize (Indian corn), caused by Ustilago maydis, resulting in swollen, blackish growths on the ears and kernels

Examples

cornsmut infection in maize
Scientists are researching ways to prevent cornsmut in commercial crops.
The farmer was dismayed to find cornsmut spreading through his maize field.
The swollen, dark growths on the corn were a clear sign of cornsmut.
