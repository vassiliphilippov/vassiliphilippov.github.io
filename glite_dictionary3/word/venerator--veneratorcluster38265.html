venerator

noun
person who reveres or deeply respects
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈvɛnəˌreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who regards someone or something with deep respect or reverence

Examples

venerator of tradition
As a committed venerator of classical music, he attends every major concert in the city.
She was known as a venerator of ancient traditions, valuing rituals passed down through generations.
The shrine attracted many venerators from around the region who came to pay their respects.

Related Terms

  • antonym – despiser: a person who shows contempt or disdain
  • synonym – venerate: to regard with great respect; to revere
  • synonym – worshipper: a person who shows religious reverence for a deity or sacred thing
