pectoral

noun
ornament worn on the chest
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛk.tɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˈpɛk.tər.əl / (BrE)
An ornament or piece of jewelry designed to be worn on the chest, often of ceremonial or symbolic significance.

Examples

gold pectoral necklace
Priests in certain religious ceremonies would don a jeweled pectoral over their robes.
The ancient Egyptian king was buried wearing a magnificent pectoral of gold and precious stones.
The museum displayed a collection of Bronze Age pectorals found at the archaeological site.
