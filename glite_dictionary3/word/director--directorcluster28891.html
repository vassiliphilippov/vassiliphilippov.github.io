director

noun
person who leads a musical group
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
a person who leads and guides a group of musicians, such as a choir, band, or orchestra, especially during rehearsals and performances

Examples

choir director
As a talented director, she brought out the best in every musician onstage.
Orchestra members rely on their director for guidance during both rehearsal and performance.
The director signaled the choir to begin the opening hymn.
