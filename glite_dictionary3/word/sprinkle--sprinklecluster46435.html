to sprinkle

verb
to rain lightly
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋkəl / (BrE)
(of rain) to fall in small, light drops; to rain gently or lightly.

Examples

to sprinkle rain
Don't worry, it's just sprinkling; we won't get too wet.
It began to sprinkle just as we were leaving the house.
Only a few drops fell when it started to sprinkle in the afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drizzle: to rain lightly with very small drops
  • antonym – pour: to rain very heavily
