Prunus dasycarpa

noun
hybrid apricot tree of Asia with purple fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnəs ˌdæsɪˈkɑːrpə / (AmE), / ˈpruːnəs ˌdæsɪˈkɑːpə / (BrE)
