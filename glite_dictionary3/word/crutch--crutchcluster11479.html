crutch

noun
support used for walking by disabled people
Pronunciation: / krʌtʃ / (AmE), / krʌtʃ / (BrE)
a support made of wood or metal that fits under a person's armpit and is used to aid walking if they have difficulty using one or both legs

Examples

use crutch to walk
After his surgery, Mark had to use crutches to walk around the house.
She leaned heavily on her crutch as she crossed the parking lot.
The nurse showed her how to properly adjust the height of the crutch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cane: a walking stick used for support while walking
