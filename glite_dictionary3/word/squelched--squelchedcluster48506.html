squelched

adjective
subdued or suppressed
Pronunciation: / skwɛltʃt / (AmE), / skwɛltʃt / (BrE)
subdued, ended, or suppressed; prevented from continuing or growing

Examples

squelched rumors quickly
Rumors of a merger were squelched before they could spread through the company.
The committee's dissent was squelched during the heated meeting.
The protest was quickly squelched by the city's authorities.
