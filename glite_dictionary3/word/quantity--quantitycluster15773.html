quantity

noun
amount or number of something
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːn.t̬ə.ti /; / ˈkwɑːn.t̬ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˈkwɒn.tɪ.ti / (BrE)
an amount or number of something that can be measured or counted

Examples

large quantity of water
Only a small quantity of water remained in the pitcher after the party.

We have a large quantity of fresh fruit available this season.
A certain quantity of patience is required when dealing with young children.
He stored a good quantity of wood for the winter.
They bought a large quantity of fresh fruit at the market.
