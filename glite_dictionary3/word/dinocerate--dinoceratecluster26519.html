dinocerate

noun
extinct ungulate mammal of Dinocerata
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪnəˈsɛrət / (AmE), / ˌdɪnəˈsɪərət / (BrE)
a member of an extinct order of large, herbivorous ungulate mammals known as Dinocerata, which lived during the Paleocene and Eocene epochs
