capital

noun
an uppercase (large) letter
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.ɪ.təl / (AmE), / ˈkæp.ɪ.təl / (BrE)
a large letter of the alphabet used as the first letter of proper names and sometimes for emphasis; an uppercase letter

Examples

write a capital
Each sentence must begin with a capital letter.
She wrote her name in bold capitals at the top of the form.
The word 'London' starts with a capital because it is a proper noun.
