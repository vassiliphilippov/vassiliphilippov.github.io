Churchill

proper noun
British prime minister and leader during WWII
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɝːtʃɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃɪl / (BrE)
(1874–1965) British prime minister, orator, and writer, known for his leadership during World War II and for receiving the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1953.
