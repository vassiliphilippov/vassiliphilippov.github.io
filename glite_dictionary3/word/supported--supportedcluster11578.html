supported

adjective
maintained by aid or assistance
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrtɪd / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪd / (BrE)
sustained or maintained by aid, help, or backing rather than by direct physical means.

Examples

supported by government grants
Her research project was supported through government grants.
The local shelter is supported by generous donations from the community.
This news outlet is supported solely by its subscribers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – backed: having support, especially financial or moral
  • antonym – unsupported: not sustained or maintained by aid; lacking support
