to set

verb
restore bone or part to normal position
Pronunciation: / sɛt / (AmE), / sɛt / (BrE)
to put a broken bone or dislocated part back into its normal position, or to adjust a device

Please set the alarm clock for 6 a.m. before you go to bed.
