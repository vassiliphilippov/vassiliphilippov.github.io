to tackle

verb
put a harness on an animal
Pronunciation: / ˈtækəl / (AmE), / ˈtækəl / (BrE)
to put a harness on (an animal, especially a horse or ox) in preparation for work or pulling a vehicle

Examples

to tackle a horse
Have you finished tackling the oxen for the day's work?
The farmer stopped to tackle the horses before setting off down the road.
They quickly tackled the mule to the cart at sunrise.
