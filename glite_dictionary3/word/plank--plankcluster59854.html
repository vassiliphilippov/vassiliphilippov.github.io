plank

noun
policy element in a political platform
Pronunciation: / plæŋk / (AmE), / plæŋk / (BrE)
a specific policy or principle that is part of the official platform of a political party or organization

Examples

key plank of policy
Healthcare reform was a central plank of the candidate's election platform.
Tax reduction remains a major plank of their policy agenda.
The party added an environmental plank to appeal to younger voters.
