reputably

adverb
in a reputable or respected manner
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛpjətəbli / (AmE), / ˈrɛpjʊtəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is considered reputable; in a way that is respected and regarded as trustworthy or of good character.

Examples

reputably run company
She invested only in reputably established businesses with a track record of integrity.
The charity is reputably run and has won several awards for transparency.
This company is reputably known for treating its employees fairly and ethically.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disreputably: in a disreputable or shameful manner
  • synonym – respectably: in a respectable manner
