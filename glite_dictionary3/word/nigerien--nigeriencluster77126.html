Nigerien

noun
person from Niger (West Africa)
Pronunciation: / niˈʒɛriən / (AmE), / nɪˈdʒɪəriən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Niger, a country in West Africa.

Examples

a Nigerien citizen
Many Nigeriens speak French as a result of the country's colonial history.
She interviewed a Nigerien farmer about the impacts of climate change on local agriculture.
The Nigerien delegation arrived at the United Nations summit this morning.
