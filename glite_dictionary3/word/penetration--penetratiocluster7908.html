penetration

noun
deep understanding or insight
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛn.əˈtreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɛn.ɪˈtreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the ability to perceive, understand, or interpret something clearly and deeply; insight.

Examples

penetration of a problem
Her penetration into the motives behind the decision impressed everyone on the team.
The professor's penetration revealed truths that had been overlooked for decades.
With remarkable penetration, she quickly understood the complex situation.

  • synonym – perceptiveness: the quality of having or showing insight or good judgment
  • antonym – superficiality: lack of depth of knowledge or insight
