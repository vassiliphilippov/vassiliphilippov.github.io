to etch

verb
make stand out or clearly defined
Pronunciation: / ɛtʃ / (AmE), / ɛtʃ / (BrE)
to make something stand out, be clearly defined, or sharply visible, often in a way that is strongly impressed or memorable

Examples

etched in memory
Her expression was etched with sorrow as she listened to the news.
The silhouette of the mountains was etched against the sunrise.
Those painful memories are etched in my mind forever.
