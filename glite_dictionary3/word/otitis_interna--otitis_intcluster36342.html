otitis interna

noun
inflammation of the inner ear
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtaɪtɪs ɪnˈtɜrnə / (AmE), / əʊˈtaɪtɪs ɪnˈtɜːnə / (BrE)
Inflammation of the inner ear, often causing symptoms such as vertigo and vomiting.

Examples

otitis interna causes vertigo
The patient was diagnosed with otitis interna after experiencing severe dizziness and nausea.
Untreated otitis interna can lead to permanent hearing loss in some cases.
Veterinarians frequently encounter otitis interna in dogs presenting with head tilts or balance issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym – labyrinthitis: inflammation of the labyrinth (inner ear) causing similar symptoms
