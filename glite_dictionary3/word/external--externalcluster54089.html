external

adjective
outward or superficial; on the surface only
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɝːnəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜː.nəl / (BrE)
concerned with or relating to outward appearance rather than inner nature; superficial or on the surface only.

Examples

external appearance
His external calm hid the turmoil he felt inside.
She has an external concern for how others see her.
The changes were only external and did not affect the company's culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – superficial: existing or occurring at or on the surface; not deep or thorough
