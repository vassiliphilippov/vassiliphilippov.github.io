thriftless

adjective
careless with money or resources
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɪftləs / (AmE), / ˈθrɪftləs / (BrE)
Careless with money or resources; lacking thrift or economy.

Examples

thriftless spending habits
Despite his large inheritance, his thriftless habits quickly led him into debt.
She accused her brother of being thriftless with the family fortune.
The thriftless spending in the organization prompted a financial review.

Related Terms

  • antonym – thrifty: using money and other resources carefully
  • synonym – wasteful: using resources carelessly, opposite of thrifty
