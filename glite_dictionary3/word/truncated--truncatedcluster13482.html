truncated

adjective
cut short; shortened in duration or extent
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌŋˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈtrʌŋkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
Shortened by having a part cut off or by being reduced in duration or extent.

Examples

truncated speech
He gave a truncated version of his speech because of time constraints.
The meeting was truncated due to technical difficulties.
Their vacation plans were truncated by an unexpected emergency.
