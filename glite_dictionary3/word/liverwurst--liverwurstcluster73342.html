liverwurst

noun
sausage made from ground liver
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvərˌwɜrst / (AmE), / ˈlɪvəwɜːst / (BrE)
A type of sausage made from ground liver and seasoned meat, often spreadable and served cold.

Examples

slice of liverwurst
At the deli, he ordered a sandwich with liverwurst and onions.

Some people enjoy the strong flavor of liverwurst more than other types of sausage.
