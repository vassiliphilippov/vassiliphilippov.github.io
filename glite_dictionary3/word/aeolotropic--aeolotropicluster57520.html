aeolotropic

adjective
having properties that vary with direction
Pronunciation: / iˌɑlɵˈtrɑpɪk / (AmE), / iˌɒlɵˈtrɒpɪk / (BrE)
(of a material or property) having different values when measured along different axes; exhibiting physical properties that vary with direction.
