calculous

adjective
relating to bodily stones (calculi)
Pronunciation: / ˈkælkjələs / (AmE), / ˈkalkjʊləs / (BrE)
relating to or caused by a calculus (stone) or calculi in the body, such as in the kidneys or gallbladder

Examples

calculous gallbladder disease
Recurring calculous nephritis can lead to chronic kidney problems if untreated.
She presented with acute pain due to a calculous obstruction in the urinary tract.
The patient was diagnosed with a calculous gallbladder after the ultrasound revealed several stones.

  • synonym – calculus: a hard stone-like concretion formed in the body
