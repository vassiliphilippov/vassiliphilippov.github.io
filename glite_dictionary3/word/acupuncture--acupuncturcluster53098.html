acupuncture

noun
pain relief method using needles
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.jəˌpʌŋk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈæk.juˌpʌŋk.tʃə / (BrE)
A medical treatment for relieving pain or treating disease by inserting thin needles into specific points on the skin.

Examples

acupuncture treatment session
He felt nervous before his first acupuncture session but soon relaxed.
She decided to try acupuncture to relieve her chronic back pain.
