matting

noun
coarse fabric used as covering
Pronunciation: / ˈmæ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmæt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a coarse fabric, often made from straw or hemp, used as a covering for floors or other surfaces

Examples

floor matting material
The floor of the hut was covered with matting made from woven reeds.
The museum displayed antique hemp matting as part of its exhibit on traditional crafts.
We rolled out the straw matting to keep the room cool during summer.
