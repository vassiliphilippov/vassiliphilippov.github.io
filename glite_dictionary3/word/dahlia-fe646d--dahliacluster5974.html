dahlia

noun
a flowering plant with showy blooms
Pronunciation: / ˈdæliə, ˈdeɪliə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːliə, ˈdeɪliə / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Dahlia, native to Mexico and Central America, grown for its tuberous roots and large, colorful, rayed flower heads

Examples

bright dahlia flowers
Many gardeners prize the dahlia for its striking variety of shapes and colors.
