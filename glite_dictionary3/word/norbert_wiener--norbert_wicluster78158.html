Norbert Wiener

proper noun
US mathematician, founder of cybernetics
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrbərt ˈwiːnər / (AmE), / ˈnɔːbət ˈwiːnə / (BrE)
(1894–1964) United States mathematician noted as the founder of cybernetics, the scientific study of control and communication in animals and machines.

Norbert Wiener founded cybernetics
Norbert Wiener made pioneering contributions to the development of cybernetics in the mid-20th century.
