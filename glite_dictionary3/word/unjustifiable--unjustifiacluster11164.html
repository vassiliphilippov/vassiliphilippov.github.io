unjustifiable

adjective
not able to be justified or excused
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dʒʌˈstɪ.faɪ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dʒʌˈstɪ.fɪ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be shown to be right, reasonable, or acceptable; impossible to justify or excuse

Examples

unjustifiable behavior
His behavior at the meeting was completely unjustifiable and shocked everyone present.
It is unjustifiable to spend so much money on things we do not need.
The judge ruled that the company's actions were unjustifiable under the law.

Related Terms

  • synonym – inexcusable: impossible to excuse or pardon
  • antonym – justifiable: able to be shown to be right or reasonable
