bromidic

adjective
trite or unoriginal; given to clichés
Pronunciation: / broʊˈmɪdɪk / (AmE), / brəʊˈmɪdɪk / (BrE)
lacking originality or freshness; dull, trite, or unoriginal, especially because of overuse
