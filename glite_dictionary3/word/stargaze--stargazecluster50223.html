to stargaze

verb
to observe stars in the sky
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrˌɡeɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɑːɡeɪz / (BrE)
to look at and study the stars in the night sky, often for enjoyment or scientific interest

Examples

to stargaze with a telescope
Every summer, he would stargaze from the roof with his telescope.
Last night, we stargazed for hours and spotted several constellations.
We lay on the grass to stargaze after the sun went down.

  • synonym – starwatch: to observe stars, usually with intent
