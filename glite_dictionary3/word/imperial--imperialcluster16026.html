imperial

noun
small tufted chin beard
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl / (BrE)
A small, tufted beard on the chin, similar to that worn by Emperor Napoleon III.

Examples

an imperial beard
Growing an imperial requires careful grooming to keep it neat.
He styled his beard into an imperial for the costume party.
The portrait depicted Napoleon III with his signature imperial.
