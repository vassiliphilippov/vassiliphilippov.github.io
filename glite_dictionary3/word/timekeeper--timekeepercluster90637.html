timekeeper

noun
person who records employees' work hours
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmˌkipər / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person, usually a clerk, responsible for recording the hours worked by employees or workers

Examples

company timekeeper
As a timekeeper, she checked the daily log sheets and calculated overtime.
The timekeeper ensured that all employees' hours were accurately recorded each week.
The factory hired a new timekeeper to improve payroll accuracy.
