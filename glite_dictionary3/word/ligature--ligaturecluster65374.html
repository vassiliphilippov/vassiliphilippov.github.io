ligature

noun
thing used for tying or binding
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪɡətʃər /; / ˈlɪɡjətʃər / (AmE), / ˈlɪɡətʃə /; / ˈlɪɡjətʃə / (BrE)
something (such as a thread, wire, or cord) used for tying or binding things together, especially in surgery to tie blood vessels or tissues
