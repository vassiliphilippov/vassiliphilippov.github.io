congratulation

noun
expression of praise for success or good fortune
Pronunciation: / kənˌɡrætʃəˈleɪʃən /; / kənˌɡrætʃ.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / kənˌɡrætjʊˈleɪʃən /; / kənˌɡratjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or words of expressing praise or good wishes to someone for their success or special occasion
