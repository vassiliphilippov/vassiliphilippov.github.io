Nordic

noun
the Scandinavian and Icelandic language family
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrdɪk / (AmE), / ˈnɔːdɪk / (BrE)
the northern family of Germanic languages spoken in Scandinavia and Iceland, including Danish, Swedish, Norwegian, Icelandic, and Faroese

Examples

Nordic language family
Icelandic, Swedish, and Danish all belong to the Nordic group of languages.
Old Nordic manuscripts have been invaluable to linguists studying the origins of Scandinavian languages.
The Nordic language family shares many features with other Germanic languages.
