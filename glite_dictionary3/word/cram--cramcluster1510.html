to cram

verb
to pack tightly or completely
Pronunciation: / kræm / (AmE), / kræm / (BrE)
to fill something completely by forcing things or people into it tightly

Examples

cram clothes in
She managed to cram all her clothes into a single suitcase.

Tourists tried to cram onto the last bus leaving the station.
