endomorph

noun
person with a rounded, soft body type
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndoʊˌmɔrf / (AmE), / ˈɛndəʊˌmɔːf / (BrE)
a person with a soft, rounded body shape, typically with a higher proportion of body fat and a tendency to gain weight easily

Examples

an endomorph body type
As an endomorph, he found it more difficult to lose weight than his friends.
Nutrition plans can be tailored to suit endomorph body types for better results.
The fitness coach discussed the unique challenges endomorphs often face in maintaining a healthy weight.
