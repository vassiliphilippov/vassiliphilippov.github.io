sparkling

adjective
(of drink) containing bubbles of gas
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklɪŋ / (BrE)
(of wine or water) containing bubbles of gas, either naturally or added artificially.

Examples

sparkling water bottle
Many people prefer sparkling wine for celebrations and special occasions.
She ordered a glass of sparkling water with lemon at the restaurant.
Would you like your water still or sparkling?
