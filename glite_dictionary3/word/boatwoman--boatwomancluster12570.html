boatwoman

noun
female boat driver or passenger
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who drives, navigates, or rides in a boat, especially as an occupation or notable activity.

Examples

expert boatwoman at the oars
During the regatta, a skilled boatwoman took first place in the women's race.
The boatwoman guided her passengers across the wide river with practiced ease.
We hired a local boatwoman to show us the hidden lagoons along the coast.
