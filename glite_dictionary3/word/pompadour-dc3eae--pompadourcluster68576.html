to pompadour

verb
to arrange hair in a pompadour style
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːmpəˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈpɒmpədɔː / (BrE)
to style hair in a pompadour, arranging it high over the forehead and often swept upward or back

Examples

to pompadour hair
He carefully pompadoured her hair, creating a stylish and dramatic look.
In the 1950s, many women would pompadour their hair to emulate movie stars.
She asked the stylist to pompadour her hair for the party.
