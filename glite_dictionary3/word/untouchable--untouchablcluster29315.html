untouchable

adjective
impossible to obtain or reach
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʌtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʌtʃəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to obtain or reach; not accessible or attainable.

Examples

untouchable goal
After years of mining, the gold deep in the mountains became untouchable to all but the bravest explorers.
Due to its remote location, the ancient temple remains largely untouchable by tourists.
The company has set goals that are so high, they seem untouchable to most employees.
