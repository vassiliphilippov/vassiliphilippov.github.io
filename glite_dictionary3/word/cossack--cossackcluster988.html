Cossack

noun
member of a Slavic military people of Russia and Ukraine
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːˌsæk / (AmE), / ˈkɒs.ak / (BrE)
a member of a group of predominantly East Slavic peoples, especially in southern Russia and Ukraine, historically known for their military skill and horsemanship

Examples

Cossack horseman
During the parade, men dressed as Cossacks performed traditional dances and demonstrated their horsemanship.
