abstractor

noun
person who makes abstracts or summaries
Pronunciation: / əbˈstræk.tɚ / (AmE), / əbˈstræk.tə / (BrE)
a person who makes abstracts or summarizes information, especially by preparing a concise summary of a document

Examples

abstractor of documents
As an abstractor of medical journals, her job is to distill complex studies into brief reports.
Every legal firm employs an abstractor to review property records and summarize key details.
The abstractor provided a concise summary of the lengthy research article.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abridger: a person who produces an abridged version of a text
  • synonym – summarizer: a person or thing that summarizes text or information
