perceiver

noun
device or software perception module
Pronunciation: / p59rc8sid0v59r / (AmE), / p59rc8sid0v59 / (BrE)
(in computing and robotics) a device or software component that collects, fuses, and interprets sensory data from the environment as part of a perception system.

Examples

robot perceiver
The robot's perceiver fused camera and LiDAR data to map its surroundings.
Multiple perceivers on the drone provided overlapping views for robust obstacle detection.
Our navigation stack replaced the old module with a learning-based perceiver last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym – perception system: a combination of hardware and software that processes and interprets sensory data to understand the environment.
  • synonym – sensor: a device that detects or measures a physical property and records, indicates, or responds to it.
