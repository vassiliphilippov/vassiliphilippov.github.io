naphazoline

noun
vasoconstrictor drug for nasal and eye relief
Pronunciation: / nəˈfæzəliːn / (AmE), / nəˈfæzəliːn / (BrE)
A drug used as a vasoconstrictor in nasal sprays to relieve nasal congestion and in eyedrops to treat redness and irritation.

Examples

naphazoline nasal spray
My doctor recommended naphazoline eyedrops for my irritated eyes.
This nasal spray contains naphazoline to help reduce swelling and congestion.
Use medications with naphazoline cautiously, as overuse can cause rebound congestion.
