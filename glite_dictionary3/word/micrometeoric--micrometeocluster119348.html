micrometeoric

adjective
relating to micrometeoroids
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊmiːtiˈɔɹɪk / (AmE), / ˌmʌɪkrəʊmiːtɪˈɒrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to micrometeoroids, the tiny particles of matter from space that reach or interact with Earth's atmosphere or surface.

Examples

micrometeoric dust particles
Geologists analyzed the micrometeoric dust found in sedimentary rocks to trace ancient cosmic events.
Scientists studied the micrometeoric particles collected from Antarctic ice cores to learn about Earth's atmosphere.
The spacecraft was protected by a layer designed to withstand micrometeoric impacts during its journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym – micrometeoroid: a very small meteoroid, typically less than a millimeter in size
