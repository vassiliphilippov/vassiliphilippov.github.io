Cryptobranchus

noun
genus of large aquatic salamanders
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪptoʊˈbræŋkəs / (AmE), / ˌkrɪptəˈbraŋkəs /; / ˌkrɪptəʊˈbraŋkəs / (BrE)
