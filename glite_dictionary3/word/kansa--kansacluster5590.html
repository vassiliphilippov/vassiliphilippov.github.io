Kansa

noun
a member of a Native American people of Kansas
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.sə / (AmE), / ˈkæn.sə / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people of the Siouan language family, originally living in the Kansas river valley in Kansas.

Examples

a Kansa person
Descendants of the Kansa still live in parts of Oklahoma today.
The Kansa once inhabited large areas along the Kansas River.
The traditions of the Kansa people are preserved through tribal ceremonies and storytelling.
