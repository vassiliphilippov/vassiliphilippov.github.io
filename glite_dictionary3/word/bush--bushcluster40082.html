bush

noun
area of dense stunted vegetation
Pronunciation: / bʊʃ / (AmE), / bʊʃ / (BrE)
an area of wild or dense vegetation, especially in remote or uncultivated regions (often called 'the bush' in Australia, Africa, or Canada)

Examples

the bush wilderness
After hiking through the bush, we emerged scratched and covered in dust.
Many animals make their homes in the bush far from human settlements.

Learning to survive in the bush can be challenging for the unprepared.
Many explorers have ventured deep into the bush in search of rare wildlife.
They spent the summer camping in the rugged bush of northern Canada.
