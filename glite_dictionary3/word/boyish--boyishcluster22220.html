boyish

adjective
like or characteristic of a young boy
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ.ɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.ɪʃ / (BrE)
(of a person, appearance, or behavior) having qualities or characteristics typical of a young boy, such as innocence, playfulness, or charm.

Examples

boyish charm
Even in his forties, he retained a boyish charm that people found endearing.
His boyish enthusiasm made the whole team feel younger and more energetic.
She smiled a boyish grin after winning the game.
