to cowl

verb
cover with or as with a cowl
Pronunciation: / kaʊl / (AmE), / kaʊl / (BrE)
to cover with or as if with a cowl; to wrap or envelop in a covering, especially around the head.

Examples

to cowl the head
Mist cowled the mountain peaks, hiding them from view.
She cowled her head with a dark shawl before stepping into the cold night.
The statue was cowled in heavy cloth for protection during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cloak: to cover or conceal with a cloak
  • antonym – uncover: to remove a cover or not be covered
