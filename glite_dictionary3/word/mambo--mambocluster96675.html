mambo

noun
Latin American dance style
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈmambəʊ / (BrE)
A lively Latin American dance style, similar in rhythm to the rumba, often performed to the music of the same name.

Examples

lively mambo dance
Her favorite dance is the energetic mambo because of its lively steps.
The club featured a live band playing classic mambo rhythms all night.
They learned to mambo at a dance class in Havana.
