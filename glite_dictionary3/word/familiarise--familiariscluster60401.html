to familiarise

verb
make acquainted or conversant
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪliəˌraɪz / (AmE), / fəˈmɪlɪəraɪz / (BrE)
to make (someone or oneself) acquainted or conversant with something; to help become familiar with a subject, place, or process

Examples

to familiarise students with rules
Teachers often familiarise students with safety procedures before a lab experiment.
The training program helped me familiarise myself with the company's software.
You should familiarise yourself with the area before traveling alone.
