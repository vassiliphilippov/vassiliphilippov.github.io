Scorpio

noun
eighth sign of the zodiac
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔr.pi.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈskɔː.pi.əʊ / (BrE)
The eighth sign of the zodiac, covering approximately October 23 to November 21, traditionally symbolized by a scorpion.

Examples

Scorpio zodiac sign
My birthday is in late October, so my zodiac sign is Scorpio.
People born under Scorpio are said to be passionate and determined.
