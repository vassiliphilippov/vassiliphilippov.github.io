costusroot

noun
fragrant root used in perfumery and preservation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑstəsˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈkɒstəsˌruːt / (BrE)
A fragrant root of an annual herb (Saussurea costus) native to the eastern Himalayas, used for its volatile oil in perfumery and preserving furs.

Examples

costusroot oil perfume
Many traditional medicines incorporate costusroot for its aromatic and supposed healing properties.
Preservers once used costusroot to protect valuable furs from moth damage.
The essential oil distilled from costusroot is prized in fine perfumery.
