focus

noun
maximum clarity or distinctness
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.kəs /; / ˈfoʊkəs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.kəs /; / ˈfəʊkəs / (BrE)
the state of being clear and sharp, either in an image or in an idea or situation

Examples

focus of idea

Her face was completely out of focus in the photograph.
Please adjust the lens to achieve the best focus for the microscope slide.
