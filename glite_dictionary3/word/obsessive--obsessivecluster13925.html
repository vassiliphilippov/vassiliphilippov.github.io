obsessive

noun
person with an obsession
Pronunciation: / əbˈsɛsɪv / (AmE), / əbˈsɛsɪv / (BrE)
a person who is preoccupied or excessively concerned with a particular idea, activity, or thing

Examples

an obsessive collector
As an obsessive, he spent hours organizing his book collection alphabetically every week.
Her friends teased her for being an obsessive about cleanliness and tidiness.
Some obsessives devote their lives to collecting rare stamps or coins.
