vena portae

noun
vein carrying blood to the liver
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪnə ˈpɔːrtaɪ / (AmE), / ˈviːnə ˈpɔːteɪ / (BrE)
a short vein that carries blood from the digestive organs to the liver
