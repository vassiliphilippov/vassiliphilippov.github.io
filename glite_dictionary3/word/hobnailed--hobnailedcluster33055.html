hobnailed

adjective
wearing boots with hobnails
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑbˌneɪld / (AmE), / ˈhɒbˌneɪld / (BrE)
wearing or characterized by the use of heavy boots with hobnails.

Examples

hobnailed boots
He appeared at the door, a hobnailed figure clad in thick wool and leather.
In the muddy field, hobnailed soldiers trudged wearily toward their camp.
The workers clomped into the room, their hobnailed boots echoing on the wooden floor.

  • synonym – hobnail: a short, thick-headed nail used to reinforce the soles of boots
