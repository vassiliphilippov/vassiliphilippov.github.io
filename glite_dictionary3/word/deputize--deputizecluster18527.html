to deputize

verb
act as a substitute
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛpjəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈdɛpjʊtaɪz / (BrE)
to act as a substitute for another person, especially in an official capacity

Examples

to deputize at work

She will deputize for the principal at tomorrow's assembly.
