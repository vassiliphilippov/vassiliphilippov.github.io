cat

noun
a small domesticated or wild feline mammal
Pronunciation: / kæt / (AmE), / kæt / (BrE)
a small domesticated animal with soft fur and retractable claws, kept as a pet or for catching mice; also, any wild animal of the cat family such as lions or tigers

Examples

cat chases mice
After adopting a cat, Lena found her home much livelier and more comforting.
Children laughed as the playful cat chased a ball of yarn across the floor.
The cat jumped gracefully onto the windowsill to watch the birds outside.
The African lion is a magnificent cat living on the savannah.
The tiger is the largest cat found in Asia.
Unlike domestic pets, most cats in this category are fierce predators.
