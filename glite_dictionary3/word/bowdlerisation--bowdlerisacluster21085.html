bowdlerisation

noun
removal of objectionable content from texts
Pronunciation: / ˌbaʊdlɚaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbaʊdlɚəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbaʊdləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of removing or changing parts of a text that are considered offensive or improper, often making the text less strong or authentic
