tap

noun
the act of intercepting calls
Pronunciation: / tæp / (AmE), / tæp / (BrE)
an act of secretly listening to or recording someone's telephone or telegraph communications, usually for information-gathering purposes

Examples

phone tap
Detectives obtained a warrant before installing the tap on the suspect's line.
News of the government's illegal tap caused a scandal.
The police placed a tap on his phone to gather evidence for the investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – wiretap: the act or device for secretly listening to people's phone calls
