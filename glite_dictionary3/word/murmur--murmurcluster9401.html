murmur

noun
low, continuous indistinct sound
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝː.mɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.mə / (BrE)
a low, continuous, indistinct sound, often made by people speaking quietly or by soft movement

Examples

a murmur of voices
A gentle murmur drifted through the audience as the speaker approached the podium.
She could hear the soft murmur of voices from the next room.
The forest was filled with the murmur of leaves rustling in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym – babble: continuous low, murmuring sound, especially of water or indistinct speech
  • antonym – roar: loud, deep, continuous sound
  • synonym – whisper: soft and low spoken sound
