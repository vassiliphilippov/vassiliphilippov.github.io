bang

noun
hair covering the forehead
Pronunciation: / bæŋ / (AmE), / bæŋ / (BrE)
a section of hair at the front of the head that is cut short and hangs over the forehead

Examples

cut bangs short
After her haircut, the bangs made her look much younger.
His bangs kept falling into his eyes during the game.
She decided to get bangs before the new school year started.
