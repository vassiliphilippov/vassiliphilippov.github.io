texture

noun
distinctive character or quality
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstʃə / (BrE)
the distinctive character or quality of something, especially as perceived by the senses or imagination

Examples

cultural texture
She admired the rich cultural texture found in ancient traditions.
The texture of city life inspired the novel's plot and themes.
