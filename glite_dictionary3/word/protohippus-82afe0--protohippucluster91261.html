protohippus

noun
extinct Pliocene horse genus
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtoʊˈhɪpəs / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈhɪpəs / (BrE)
an extinct genus of horse from the Pliocene epoch, about the size of a donkey
