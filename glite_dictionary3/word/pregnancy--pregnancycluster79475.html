pregnancy

noun
the state of being pregnant
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛɡnənsi / (AmE), / ˈprɛɡnənsi / (BrE)
the state of carrying a developing fetus within the uterus from conception to birth

Examples

healthy pregnancy
Regular check-ups are important throughout a healthy pregnancy.
Smoking during pregnancy can pose serious health risks to the baby.
The news of her pregnancy brought great joy to the entire family.
