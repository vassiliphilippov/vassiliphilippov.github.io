heaviness

noun
quality of being heavy; great weight
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvinəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛvinɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being heavy; great weight compared to other things

Examples

the heaviness of metal
Engineers must account for the heaviness of materials used in construction.
She was surprised by the heaviness of the winter coat when she picked it up.
The heaviness of the box made it difficult to lift on his own.

Related Terms

  • antonym – lightness: the quality of being light in weight
  • synonym – weight: the amount that something weighs
