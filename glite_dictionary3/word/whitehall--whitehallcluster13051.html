Whitehall

proper noun
main government street in London; British government
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌhɔl / (AmE), / ˈwaɪthɔːl / (BrE)
a wide street in London, running from Trafalgar Square to the Houses of Parliament, noted as the site of many government offices; also used to refer to the British government administration collectively

Examples

Whitehall in London
Many departments are headquartered along Whitehall in central London.
Senior officials at Whitehall declined to comment on the new policy.
The decision is being reviewed by Whitehall before it goes to Parliament.
