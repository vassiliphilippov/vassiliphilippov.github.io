subway

noun
underground pedestrian passage
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.weɪ / (BrE)
An underground tunnel or passage designed for pedestrians to cross beneath a road or railway.

Examples

pedestrian subway tunnel
In London, you often need to use a subway to reach the other side of the street.
The city installed a new subway so pedestrians could safely cross the busy highway.
We took shelter in a subway during the sudden thunderstorm.
