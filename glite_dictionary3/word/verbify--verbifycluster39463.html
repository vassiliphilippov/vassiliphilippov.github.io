to verbify

verb
make into a verb
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrbəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːbɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to turn a noun or another part of speech into a verb, especially by creating a new verbal form

Examples

to verbify a noun
Linguists sometimes verbify nouns to illustrate changes in language.
Students often try to verbify technical terms in casual speech.
The word 'Google' was quickly verbified as people began to 'Google' things online.
