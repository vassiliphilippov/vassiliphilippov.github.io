pretending

noun
the act of giving a false appearance
Pronunciation: / prɪˈtɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / prɪˈtɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of behaving in a way that is intended to make others believe something that is not true.

Examples

pretending games
Children spend hours in pretending, creating imaginary worlds and characters.
His pretending was so convincing that everyone believed his story.
She grew tired of his constant pretending and wanted the truth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deception: the act of deceiving someone
  • synonym – pretense: an attempt to make something that is not the case appear true
