litterbug

noun
person who drops litter in public places
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtərˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtəˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a person who carelessly drops litter in public places instead of disposing of it properly

Examples

a litterbug drops trash
If you see a litterbug, report them to keep our community clean.
Local authorities have launched a campaign to catch and fine every litterbug in the city.
The park was filled with rubbish left behind by a careless litterbug.
