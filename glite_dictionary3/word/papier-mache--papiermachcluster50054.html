papier-mache

noun
moldable material made of paper pulp and glue
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪpiər məˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˌpæpi.eɪ məˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
a material made from paper pulp mixed with glue or paste, which can be molded when wet and becomes hard when dry, often used for making decorative objects

Examples

papier-mache mask
After it dried overnight, the papier-mache bowl was ready to be painted.
She crafted a mask out of papier-mache for the school play.
The artist specializes in making colorful papier-mache sculptures.
