phenyltoloxamine

noun
antihistamine drug for coughs and congestion
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛnɪltoʊˈlɒksəˌmiːn / (AmE), / ˌfiːnɪltɒˈlɒksəmiːn / (BrE)
An antihistamine drug used in combination with other medications to treat symptoms such as coughing and nasal congestion.
