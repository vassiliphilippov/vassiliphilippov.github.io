hayrack

noun
rack for holding hay for livestock
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌræk / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌræk / (BrE)
a rack used for holding hay so that livestock can feed from it

Examples

hayrack for feeding animals
A wooden hayrack stood in the middle of the pasture, piled high with fresh hay.
Goats gathered around the hayrack, munching on the sweet-smelling grass.
The farmer filled the hayrack before bringing the cows into the barn.
