dicky

noun
small folding rear seat in old cars
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪki / (AmE), / ˈdɪki / (BrE)
a small third seat that folds out at the back of an old-fashioned two-seater car, usually used for occasional passengers

Examples

dicky car seat
Few modern cars have a dicky, making them a charming feature of classic automobiles.
He proudly showed his friends the dicky in his restored vintage roadster.
The children squeezed into the dicky for the short drive to the park.
