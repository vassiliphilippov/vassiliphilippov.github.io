conveniently

adverb
in a suitable or easy way
Pronunciation: / kənˈviːniəntli / (AmE), / kənˈviːniəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is easy, suitable, or fitting for a particular purpose.

Examples

conveniently placed downtown
Her office is conveniently close to public transportation.
The hotel is conveniently located near the airport.
You can conveniently order groceries online and have them delivered to your door.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inconveniently: in a way that is not easy or suitable
