geographer

noun
expert in geography
Pronunciation: / dʒiˈɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / dʒiˈɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who studies or is an expert in geography, especially the physical features of the earth and its atmosphere

Examples

geographer studies maps
As a geographer, she has traveled the world to study different landscapes and climates.
Early explorers frequently consulted a geographer before embarking on their journeys.
The work of a geographer often involves using maps and satellite data to analyze Earth's surface.
