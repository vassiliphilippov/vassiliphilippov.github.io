harpooner

noun
person who uses a harpoon
Pronunciation: / hɑːrˈpunər / (AmE), / hɑːˈpuːnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to throw or use a harpoon, especially in hunting whales or large sea animals

harpooner hunts whales
Each harpooner played a vital role in the whaling crew's success.
