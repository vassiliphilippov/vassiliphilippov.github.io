chiliasm

noun
belief in Christ's thousand-year reign
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪliˌæzəm / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪˌazəm / (BrE)
the belief in a thousand-year reign of Christ on earth, as prophesied in the Book of Revelation
