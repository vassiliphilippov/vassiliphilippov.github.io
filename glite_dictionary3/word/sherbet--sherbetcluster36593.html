sherbet

noun
frozen dessert with fruit juice and dairy
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜrbət / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːbət / (BrE)
A sweet, frozen dessert made from fruit juice, sugar, and sometimes milk, egg white, or gelatin, giving it a smooth texture.

Examples

lemon sherbet dessert
For dessert, he served lemon sherbet in chilled glass bowls.
She prefers sherbet over ice cream because it's less creamy but still sweet.
The raspberry sherbet was light and refreshing on a hot summer day.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – ice cream: a frozen dessert made with cream, sugar, and flavoring, containing more dairy than sherbet
  • degree lower – sorbet: a frozen dessert made with sweetened fruit juice or puree, but without dairy
