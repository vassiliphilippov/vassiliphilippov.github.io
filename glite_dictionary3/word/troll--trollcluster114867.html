troll

noun
fishing by drawing a baited line through water
Pronunciation: / troʊl / (AmE), / trəʊl / (BrE)
A method of fishing by drawing a baited line or lure slowly through the water behind a moving boat.

Examples

troll fishing method
During our fishing trip, we decided to troll along the river’s edge to attract more fish.
He prefers to troll for bass in the early morning when the lake is calm.
Some anglers use electronic motors to troll slowly and maintain the perfect lure speed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – trawl: to fish with a cone-shaped net dragged through the water
