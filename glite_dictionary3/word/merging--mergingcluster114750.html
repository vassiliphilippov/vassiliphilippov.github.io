merging

adjective
joining or combining into one
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
coming together or joining as one, especially of separate parts, ideas, or entities; flowing together.

Examples

merging cultures
Her ideas are the result of a merging of Eastern and Western philosophies.
The merging of the two rivers creates a powerful current.
The technology industry is constantly merging different platforms to offer better solutions.
