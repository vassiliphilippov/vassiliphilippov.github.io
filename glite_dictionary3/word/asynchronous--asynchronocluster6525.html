asynchronous

adjective
not occurring at the same time
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪŋkrənəs / (AmE), / əˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs /; / əˈsɪŋkrənəs / (BrE)
not happening, existing, or moving at the same time; not requiring things to be synchronized (used generally and in digital communication)
