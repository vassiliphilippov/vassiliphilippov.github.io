express

adjective, adverb
by special fast service (esp. mail)
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs / (BrE)
operating or sent quickly, with few or no stops (used for transport or delivery); or by a special fast service
