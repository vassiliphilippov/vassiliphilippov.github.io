rich

adjective
(of food) containing much fat, egg, or sugar
Pronunciation: / rɪtʃ / (AmE), / rɪtʃ / (BrE)
(of food or drink) containing a lot of fat, sugar, or flavor; heavy, strong, or hard to digest
