earshot

noun
the range where a sound can be heard
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈɪəʃɒt / (BrE)
The range or distance within which a sound, especially a voice, can be heard.

Examples

within earshot of voices
Make sure you stay within earshot in case we need to call you.
The announcer's voice could be heard from anywhere within earshot of the stadium.
They whispered secrets just out of earshot of their teacher.

Related Terms

  • antonym – out of earshot: too far away to hear
