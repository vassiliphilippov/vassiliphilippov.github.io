knower

noun
person possessing knowledge
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊər / (AmE), / ˈnəʊə / (BrE)
a person who knows, perceives, or has knowledge or understanding of something

Examples

knower of secrets
As a knower of ancient languages, she was highly respected among her peers.
Each knower interprets experience in a unique way.
The philosopher explored the relationship between the knower and what is known.
