chink

noun
short light metallic sound
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋk / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk / (BrE)
a short, light, ringing metallic sound

Examples

chink of keys
A faint chink could be heard as the keys brushed against each other.
She heard the chink of coins being dropped into the jar.
The chink of glasses echoed through the quiet room.
