Microhylidae

noun
a family of narrow-mouthed frogs
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈhɪl.ɪ.diː / (AmE), / ˌmɪ.krəʊˈhɪl.ɪ.diː / (BrE)
