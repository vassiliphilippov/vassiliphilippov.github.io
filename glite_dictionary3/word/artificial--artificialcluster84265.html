artificial

adjective
made by humans, not natural
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl /; / ˌɑːr.t̬əˈfɪʃ.əl / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈfɪʃəl /; / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl / (BrE)
made or produced by humans rather than occurring naturally, especially as a copy of something natural

Examples
