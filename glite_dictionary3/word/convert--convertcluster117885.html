to convert

verb
exchange or replace with another of the same category
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝːt / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːt / (BrE)
to exchange or replace something with another thing of the same or a related kind, especially currency, units, or forms

Examples

to convert euros to dollars
I asked the bank to convert my euros into dollars for my trip.
The company plans to convert its assets into shares for easier trading.
You need to convert these measurements from inches to centimeters before baking.
