clear

adjective
free from restrictions or qualifications
Pronunciation: / klɪr / (AmE), / klɪə / (BrE)
free from restrictions, doubts, or qualifications; definite or unambiguous.

Examples

clear answer
It was a clear decision, with no conditions attached to the offer.
She was declared the clear winner after all the ballots were counted.
The judge gave him a clear bill of health, meaning he passed the examination with no issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unqualified: not limited or restricted; unconditional
