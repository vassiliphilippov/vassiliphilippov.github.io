to signify

verb
express or indicate by words or signals
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nɪ.faɪ /; / ˈsɪɡ.nəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to be a sign of, mean, or make something known (by words, actions, or signals)
