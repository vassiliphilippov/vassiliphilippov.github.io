specialisation

noun
focus or area of expertise in a field
Pronunciation: / ˌspɛʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌspɛʃəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌspɛʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a specific area of study, work, or skill that someone focuses on, or the process of becoming an expert in it

Her specialisation in marine biology required years of study and research.
