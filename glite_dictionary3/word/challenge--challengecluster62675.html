to challenge

verb
invite to compete or prove
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪndʒ / (BrE)
to invite someone to take part in a contest or competition, or to prove or justify something

Examples

to challenge a rival
He challenged his opponent to a rematch after the narrow defeat.
She challenged the champion to a friendly tennis match.
The company challenges you to find a better deal anywhere else.

Related Terms

  • antonym – accept: agree to engage in a challenge
  • synonym – contest: attempt to win in competition with others
