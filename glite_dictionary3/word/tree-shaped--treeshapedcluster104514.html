tree-shaped

adjective
resembling the shape or branching of a tree
Pronunciation: / ˈtriˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a form or structure resembling that of a tree, especially with a main trunk and branches spreading out.
