commination

noun
Anglican prayer or service proclaiming God's anger at sinners
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a service or prayer in the Anglican Church, especially read on Ash Wednesday, warning of God's anger against sinners and pronouncing curses upon unrepentant people

commination service prayer
During the commination, the congregation hears solemn warnings against sin and unrepentance.
