pliant

adjective
adaptable; flexible in attitude or behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈplaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈplaɪənt / (BrE)
able to adjust readily to different conditions; easily adaptable or flexible in attitude or behavior

Examples

pliant attitude
He admired her pliant approach to solving problems, always willing to try new ideas.
Her pliant nature made her a favorite among team leaders who valued adaptability.
The rules were intentionally pliant to accommodate various situations.
