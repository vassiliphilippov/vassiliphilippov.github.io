uncannily

adverb
in a strange or mysterious way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkænəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkanɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is strange or mysterious, especially in making you feel uneasy or surprised.

Examples

uncannily accurate result
Her predictions about the market turned out to be uncannily accurate.
The child was uncannily good at remembering people's names after hearing them just once.
