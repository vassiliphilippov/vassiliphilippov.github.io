specimen

noun
sample of tissue or fluid for testing
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛsəmɪn / (AmE), / ˈspɛsɪmɪn / (BrE)
a small sample of tissue, blood, urine, or other material taken from a person or animal for medical examination or testing

Examples

urine specimen sample

The doctor asked for a tissue specimen to help diagnose the illness.
