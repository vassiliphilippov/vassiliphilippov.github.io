Nypa

noun
genus of mangrove palm trees
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪpə / (AmE), / ˈniːpə / (BrE)
A monotypic genus of palm trees found in tidal mangrove forests of Australasia, known for the species Nypa fruticans.
