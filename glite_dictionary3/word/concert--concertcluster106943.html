concert

noun
musical performance by musicians or singers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnsɚt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsət / (BrE)
a performance of music by musicians or singers, usually given in public and not involving theatrical staging

concert ticket

Tickets for the band's concert sold out within minutes.
