antipyretic

adjective
reducing or preventing fever
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌpaɪˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌpaɪˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) preventing or reducing fever.
