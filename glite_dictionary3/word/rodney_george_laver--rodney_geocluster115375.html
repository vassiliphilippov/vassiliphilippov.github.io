Rodney George Laver

proper noun
Australian tennis champion, Grand Slam winner
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑdni ˈdʒɔrdʒ ˈleɪvər / (AmE), / ˈrɒdni ˈdʒɔːdʒ ˈleɪvə / (BrE)
