lacrimator

noun
tear-producing gas or substance
Pronunciation: / ˈlækrəˌmeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈlak.rɪ.meɪ.tər / (BrE)
A gas or substance that causes the eyes to produce tears, especially used for crowd control without causing lasting harm.
