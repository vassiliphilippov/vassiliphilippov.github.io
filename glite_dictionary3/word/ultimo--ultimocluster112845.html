ultimo

adjective
of the previous month
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltɪˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪməʊ / (BrE)
of or relating to the previous month; used to refer to a date in the month immediately before the current one, especially in formal or business correspondence.

Examples

ultimo invoice date
His account was settled on the 15th ultimo as noted in the records.
Please refer to my letter dated the 22nd ultimo for more details.
The payment was due on the 30th ultimo, but has not yet been received.

Related Terms

  • antonym – instant: of or relating to the current month (used in correspondence)
  • antonym – proximo: of or relating to the next month (used in correspondence)
