AWOL

adjective
absent without official leave
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪˌwɑl / (AmE), / ˈeɪˌwɒl / (BrE)
absent from duty or one's post without official permission, especially in a military context

Examples

AWOL team member
If a team member goes AWOL, the project can quickly fall behind schedule.
Several employees went AWOL during the busiest week of the year.
The soldier was reported AWOL after failing to return from leave.

  • synonym – truant: absent from a place, especially school, without permission
