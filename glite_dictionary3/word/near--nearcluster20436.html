near

adjective
close in distance, time, or relation
Pronunciation: / nɪr / (AmE), / nɪə / (BrE)
close in distance, time, degree, or relation; not far away in space, time, quality, or circumstances.

Examples

near the school
Our house is near the school, making it easy for the children to walk every day.
The project is near completion and should be finished by next week.
