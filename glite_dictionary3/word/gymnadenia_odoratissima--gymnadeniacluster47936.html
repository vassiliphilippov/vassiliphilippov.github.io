Gymnadenia odoratissima

noun
a species of orchid
Pronunciation: / dʒɪm.nəˈdiː.ni.ə oʊˌdoʊ.rəˈtɪs.ɪ.mə / (AmE), / dʒɪm.nəˈdiː.ni.ə əʊˌdəʊ.rəˈtɪs.ɪ.mə / (BrE)
A species of orchid similar to Gymnadenia conopsea, with smaller flowers on shorter stems and much shorter spurs.
