washy

adjective
thin or watery, lacking flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃi / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃi / (BrE)
(of a liquid, especially a drink) thin, watery, or lacking in strength or flavor.

Examples

washy tea
After adding too much milk, the tea had a rather washy flavor.
He complained about the washy coffee served at the meeting.
The soup tasted surprisingly washy, as if it had been watered down.

Related Terms

  • antonym – strong: (of a drink) having a lot of flavor or strength
  • synonym – watery: (of a drink) containing too much water, lacking in strength or flavor
  • synonym – weak: (of a liquid, especially a drink) lacking strength or flavor
