intern

noun
medical graduate in practical training
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.tɝn / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɜːn / (BrE)
a graduate or advanced student in medicine who is gaining supervised practical experience in a hospital or clinic before obtaining full qualification

Examples

medical intern position
As an intern, she was responsible for monitoring patients during her hospital rotations.
Every intern must work overnight shifts as part of their training.
He completed his year as an intern before starting his residency.
