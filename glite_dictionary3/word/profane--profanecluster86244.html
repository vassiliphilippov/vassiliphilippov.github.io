profane

adjective
irreverent toward sacred things
Pronunciation: / proʊˈfeɪn /; / prəˈfeɪn / (AmE), / prəˈfeɪn / (BrE)
showing a lack of respect for sacred things; irreverent, blasphemous, or using obscene language

Examples

profane language
Entering the temple with muddy boots was viewed as a profane act by the locals.
Some critics considered the artist's work profane for its use of religious imagery.
The comedian's profane jokes offended several members of the audience.
She was scolded for using profane language in the classroom.
Some people find certain song lyrics too profane for radio play.
The comedian's profane jokes drew both laughter and criticism from the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blasphemous: showing disrespect to God or sacred things
  • synonym – obscene: offensive to moral principles; indecent
  • antonym – sacred: regarded with reverence and respect, often due to religious significance
