Excellency

noun
formal title for dignitaries
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛlənsi / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛlənsi / (BrE)
a title used to address or refer to high-ranking officials such as ambassadors, governors, or other dignitaries, usually preceded by 'Your', 'His', or 'Her'.

Examples

Your Excellency the ambassador
Please address the letter to Her Excellency the Governor.
The ambassador was referred to as Excellency throughout the ceremony.
We were honored to meet Excellency at the embassy reception.
