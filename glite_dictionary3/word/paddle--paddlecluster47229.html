to paddle

verb
stir with a paddle
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpæd.əl / (BrE)
to stir or mix something with a paddle, typically a flat-bladed implement

Examples

to paddle dough
Before adding more flour, she paddled the dough to work out any lumps.
He used a long stick to paddle the mixture until it became smooth.
The chef paddled the thick soup for several minutes to prevent it from burning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stir: to mix a liquid or other substance by moving an object such as a spoon in it
