flipper

noun
swimming aid worn on the foot
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪpər / (AmE), / ˈflɪpə / (BrE)
A flat, paddle-like shoe worn on the foot to aid swimming, especially underwater.

Examples

swimming flippers
He packed his snorkel, mask, and flippers for the trip.
She put on her flippers before diving into the ocean.
The instructor recommended using flippers to swim more efficiently.
