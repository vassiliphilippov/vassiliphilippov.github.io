logwood

noun
hard wood yielding dye from a tropical tree
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔɡˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡˌwʊd / (BrE)
the very hard brown to brownish-red heartwood of a tropical American tree, used especially for preparing a purplish-red dye
