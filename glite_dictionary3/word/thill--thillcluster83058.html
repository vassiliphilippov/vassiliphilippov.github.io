thill

noun
one of the shafts of a cart or carriage
Pronunciation: / θɪl / (AmE), / θɪl / (BrE)
either of the pair of shafts on a cart or carriage that extend alongside the animal pulling it, attaching the vehicle to the animal.

Examples

thill of a cart
The horse was harnessed between the thills of the cart.
The old wagon's thill was broken and needed repair.
They replaced the worn-out thill with a sturdy new wooden shaft.
