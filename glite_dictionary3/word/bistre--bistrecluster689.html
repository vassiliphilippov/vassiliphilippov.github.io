bistre

noun
brownish-yellow pigment from wood soot
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪstər / (AmE), / ˈbɪstə / (BrE)
a brownish-yellow pigment made from the soot of burned wood, used especially in drawing and watercolor painting

Examples
