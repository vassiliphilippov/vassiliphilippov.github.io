patchy

adjective
irregular or uneven in quality or texture
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃi / (AmE), / ˈpætʃi / (BrE)
Not of the same quality, appearance, or texture throughout; irregular or uneven in quality, texture, or coverage.

Examples

patchy grass
After the drought, the grass on the lawn grew back in a patchy manner.
Her knowledge of the subject is patchy despite her years of study.
The doctor's explanation was patchy and left the patient confused.

Related Terms

  • antonym – smooth: having an even and regular surface or consistency
  • synonym – uneven: not uniform or equal in quality or surface
