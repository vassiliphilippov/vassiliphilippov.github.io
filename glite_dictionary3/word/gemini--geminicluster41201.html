Gemini

proper noun
constellation and zodiac sign
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛmɪˌnaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmɪni / (BrE)
a constellation and zodiac sign in the Northern Hemisphere, located between Taurus and Cancer on the ecliptic

Examples

Gemini constellation
Many astrologers believe that people born under Gemini have lively personalities.
The constellation Gemini is easily visible in the night sky during winter.
The two brightest stars in Gemini are called Castor and Pollux.
