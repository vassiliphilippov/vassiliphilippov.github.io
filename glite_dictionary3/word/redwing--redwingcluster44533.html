redwing

noun
North American blackbird with wing patches
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
A North American blackbird (Agelaius phoeniceus) with distinctive scarlet and yellow patches on its wings, often found in wetlands and open fields.

Examples

male redwing in wetlands
A male redwing flashed its brilliant scarlet shoulder patches while defending its territory.
The distinctive call of the redwing echoed through the marsh at sunrise.
We spotted several redwings perched among the cattails during our walk.
