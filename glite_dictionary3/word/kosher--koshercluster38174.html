kosher

noun
food prepared according to Jewish law
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊʃər / (AmE), / ˈkəʊʃə / (BrE)
Food that complies with Jewish dietary laws.

Examples

kosher meat products
During Passover, they only eat kosher foods at home.
Is this chicken kosher, or was it not prepared according to the rules?
The restaurant offers a full menu of kosher dishes certified by local authorities.
