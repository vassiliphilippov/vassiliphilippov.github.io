redemption

noun
repayment of debt or repurchase of securities
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdɛmpʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈdɛmpʃən / (BrE)
the act of repaying or recovering the principal amount of a debt or security, especially by paying off a loan or repurchasing issued shares or bonds

Examples

redemption of bonds
Early redemption of the loan saved the business thousands in interest payments.
Shareholders were notified about the redemption of preferred stock later this year.
The company announced the redemption of its outstanding bonds ahead of schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym – purchase: the act of buying something, especially in finance or business
  • synonym – repayment: the act of paying back money previously borrowed
