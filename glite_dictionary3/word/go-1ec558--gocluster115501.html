go

noun
an attempt or try
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ / (BrE)
an attempt or try at doing something, often brief or informal; a period or turn during which someone is engaged in an activity, after which another person will take over

Examples

She gave it another go before asking for help.

Everyone gets a go at playing the game before the winner is decided.
It's your go now, so take over the controls.
