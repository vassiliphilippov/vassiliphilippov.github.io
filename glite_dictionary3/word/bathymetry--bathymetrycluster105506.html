bathymetry

noun
measurement of ocean depths
Pronunciation: / bəˈθɪmətri / (AmE), / bəˈθɪmətri / (BrE)
the science of measuring the depths and mapping the topography of oceans, seas, or other large bodies of water.

Examples

ocean bathymetry survey
Advances in sonar technology have greatly improved bathymetry in deep-sea research.
Detailed bathymetry maps are crucial for safe navigation and marine construction projects.
Scientists use bathymetry to identify underwater mountains and trenches.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hydrography: the scientific description and analysis of bodies of water
