Surnia ulula

noun
grey-and-white diurnal owl of northern regions
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrniə juːˈluːlə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːnɪə juːˈluːlə / (BrE)
A grey-and-white diurnal, hawk-like owl found in northern regions of the Northern Hemisphere, also known as the Northern Hawk Owl.
