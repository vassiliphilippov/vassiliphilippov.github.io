thermograph

noun
temperature-recording instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈθɝːməˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈθɜːməˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
an instrument that automatically records temperature changes over time on a graph

Examples

thermograph temperature chart
Engineers installed a thermograph in the greenhouse to track daily temperature changes.
The thermograph produced a continuous record of the patient's body temperature throughout the night.
The laboratory used a thermograph to monitor temperature fluctuations during the experiment.
