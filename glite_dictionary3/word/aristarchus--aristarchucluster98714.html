Aristarchus

proper noun
ancient Greek grammarian and commentator
Pronunciation: / ˌærɪˈstɑrkəs / (AmE), / ˌærɪˈstɑːkəs / (BrE)
an ancient Greek grammarian (c. 217–145 BC) known for his influential commentaries on the Iliad and the Odyssey
