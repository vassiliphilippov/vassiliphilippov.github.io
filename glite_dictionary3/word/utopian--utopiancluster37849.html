Utopian

adjective
relating to a perfect or ideal society
Pronunciation: / juːˈtoʊ.pi.ən / (AmE), / juːˈtəʊ.pi.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to an ideal or perfect society; resembling a utopia.

Examples

Utopian society
He outlined a Utopian vision for the future of education in his speech.
Many critics argue that her proposals are too Utopian to be practical.
The novel describes a Utopian society where everyone lives in harmony.

Related Terms

  • synonym – idealistic: believing in or aiming for perfection
  • antonym – realistic: accepting things as they are; pragmatic
