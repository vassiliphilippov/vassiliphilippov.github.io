lemma

noun
bract enclosing a grass floret
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛmə / (AmE), / ˈlɛmə / (BrE)
the lower and broader of the two bracts (glumes) that enclose the floret in grasses (family Gramineae)

Examples

remove the lemma (plant)
Botanists carefully removed the lemma to study the seed structure beneath.
Each spikelet consists of a pair of glumes at the base and a fertile lemma above.
The outer lemma and inner palea together protect the reproductive organs of the grass floret.
