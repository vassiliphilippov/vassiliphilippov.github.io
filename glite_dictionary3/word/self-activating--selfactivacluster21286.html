self-activating

adjective
operating automatically by itself
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈæktɪˌveɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈæktɪveɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a device or system) designed to activate or operate automatically by itself without external input.

Examples

self-activating sprinkler system
Installing a self-activating sprinkler system can provide extra safety in case of fire.
New cars often come equipped with self-activating headlights for driving in low light conditions.
The laboratory uses a self-activating alarm to ensure no dangerous gases go undetected.

  • synonym – automatic: operating by itself with little or no direct human control
