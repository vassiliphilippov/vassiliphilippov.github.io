mistflower

noun
a flowering plant with blue flower heads
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈmɪstˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the central and southeastern United States and the West Indies, with large, showy heads of clear blue flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Eupatorium.

mistflower blooms
