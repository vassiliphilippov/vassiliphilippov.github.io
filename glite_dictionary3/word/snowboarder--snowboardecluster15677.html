snowboarder

noun
person who rides a snowboard
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌbɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌbɔːdə / (BrE)
a person who rides a snowboard down snow-covered slopes as a sport or pastime

Examples

He is an experienced snowboarder who competes in international events.
