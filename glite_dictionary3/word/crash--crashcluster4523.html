to crash

verb
enter uninvited (informal)
Pronunciation: / kræʃ / (AmE), / kræʃ / (BrE)
(informal) To attend an event or enter a place without being invited or without permission.

Examples

to crash a party
He tried to crash the celebrity after-party but was stopped at the door.
Teenagers were caught attempting to crash the exclusive concert without tickets.
We decided to crash the wedding because we weren't on the guest list.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gatecrash: to attend an event without an invitation
