perpetuity

noun
the state of lasting forever
Pronunciation: / pərˌpɛtʃuˈɪti / (AmE), / pəˌpɛtjuːˈɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of lasting forever or for an indefinite period without end

Examples

perpetuity of love
He doubted the perpetuity of their friendship, fearing it might someday end.
Many believe the monuments were built to stand in perpetuity.
The trust fund was designed to last in perpetuity for future generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – eternity: infinite or unending time
  • antonym – transience: the state of lasting only for a short time
