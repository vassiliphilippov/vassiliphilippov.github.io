quincentenary

noun
500th anniversary or celebration
Pronunciation: / kwɪn.sɛnˈtɛn.ə.ri / (AmE), / kwɪn.senˈtiː.nər.i / (BrE)
The 500th anniversary of a significant event, or the celebration of such an anniversary.

Examples

quincentenary of founding
A special medal was commissioned for the quincentenary of the university.
In 1992, Spain celebrated the quincentenary of Columbus's voyage to the Americas.
The city hosted a quincentenary festival to mark 500 years since its founding.
