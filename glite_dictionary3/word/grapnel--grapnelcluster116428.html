grapnel

noun
a small anchor with several flukes
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræpnəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrapnəl /; / ˈɡræpnəl / (BrE)
a small anchor or hook with several prongs, used for anchoring boats or for grasping and holding things, often thrown with a rope
