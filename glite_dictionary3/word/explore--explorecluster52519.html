to explore

verb
investigate or examine in detail
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsplɔr / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɔː / (BrE)
to examine, study, or investigate a subject, idea, or possibility in detail

Examples

to explore an idea
She spent the afternoon exploring the different theories presented in the lecture.
The book explores the reasons behind the rise in crime rates.
We need to explore all possible solutions before making a decision.
The documentary aims to explore the impact of climate change on Arctic wildlife.
The scientists decided to explore the origins of the mysterious phenomenon.
We will explore several approaches to solving this complex problem in our research.
