arteria pudenda

noun
artery supplying the external genital organs
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə pjuːˈdɛndə / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə pjuːˈdɛndə / (BrE)
Either of a group of arteries that supply blood to the external genital organs in humans.

Examples
