fibre

noun
thread-like structure used in textiles
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə / (BrE)
a slender, thread-like structure of a substance that can be spun into yarn or used to make textiles

Examples

cotton fibre
Cotton fibre is commonly used in the textile industry to manufacture clothing.
