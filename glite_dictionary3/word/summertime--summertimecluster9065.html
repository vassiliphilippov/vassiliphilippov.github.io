summertime

noun
the period during summer
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɚˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈsʌmətaɪm / (BrE)
the period of the year when it is summer, typically characterized by warm or hot weather

Examples

hot summertime days
During summertime, the days are long and the nights are warm.
Many families go on vacation during summertime when the children are out of school.
The town holds an annual festival every summertime to celebrate the season.
