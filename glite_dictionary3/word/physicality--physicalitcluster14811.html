physicality

noun
embodied presence or tactile engagement
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzɪˈkælɪti / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
The quality or aspect of a performance, artwork, or interface that emphasises bodily presence, movement, or tactile interaction—the sense of embodiment or tactile engagement.

Examples

physicality in performance
The VR exhibit emphasized physicality, demanding full-body movement to explore the environment.
Contemporary dance companies prize the performers' raw physicality over elaborate sets or costumes.
The game's haptic suit adds a layer of physicality that makes battles feel dangerously real.
