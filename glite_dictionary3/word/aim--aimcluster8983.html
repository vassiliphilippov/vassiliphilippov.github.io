to aim

verb
intend or plan to do something
Pronunciation: / eɪm / (AmE), / eɪm / (BrE)
to plan, intend, or have a goal to achieve something

Examples

to aim for success
He aimed to improve his health by exercising regularly.
She aims to finish her degree next year.
We aim to provide excellent customer service at all times.
Many students aim for academic excellence throughout their university studies.
She aims to become the youngest person ever to climb Mount Everest.
The company aims to revolutionize the way we use technology.
