martingale

noun
spar beneath the bowsprit of a sailboat
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtɪŋˌɡeɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtɪŋɡeɪl / (BrE)
A spar fixed under the bowsprit of a sailboat to counteract upward pull and maintain rigging tension.

Examples

martingale under bowsprit
A strong martingale is essential for maintaining the integrity of the bowsprit in rough waters.
