tastelessness

noun
the state of having no flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪstləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈteɪstləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having no flavor

Examples

tastelessness of food
He complained about the tastelessness of hospital food after his surgery.
The tastelessness of the dish disappointed everyone at the dinner party.
You could tell something was missing from the soup because of its tastelessness.

Related Terms

  • antonym – flavorfulness: the quality of having a strong or interesting flavor
  • synonym – insipidity: lack of flavor or zest
