Prunus avium

noun
Eurasian tree, source of sweet cherry fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnəs ˈeɪvi.əm / (AmE), / ˈpruːnəs ˈeɪvɪəm / (BrE)
a large Eurasian tree species that bears small, dark, bitter fruit in the wild and sweet, edible fruit when cultivated; also known as the wild cherry or sweet cherry.
