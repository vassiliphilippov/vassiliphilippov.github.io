barbet

noun
a type of tropical bird with a stout bill
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrˌbɛt / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪt / (BrE)
A small, brightly colored tropical bird with a stout bill and short, weak wings, found mainly in Asia, Africa, and Central and South America.

Examples

colorful barbet bird
A barbet perched on the branch, displaying its colorful plumage.
The dense forest is home to the rare red-and-yellow barbet.
We spotted several species of barbets during our trip to the tropics.
