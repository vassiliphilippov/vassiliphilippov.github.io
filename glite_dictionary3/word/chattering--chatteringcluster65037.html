chattering

noun
rapid repeated noise from a machine
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæt̬ərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃætərɪŋ / (BrE)
a rapid, repeated series of noises, especially made by the moving parts of a machine

Examples

machine chattering
He was concerned by the chattering from the computer's fan every time it was turned on.
Reducing chattering in the machine will improve both its performance and its lifespan.
The constant chattering of the gears made it clear that the engine needed maintenance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rattling: a series of short, sharp noises, often from something shaking or vibrating
