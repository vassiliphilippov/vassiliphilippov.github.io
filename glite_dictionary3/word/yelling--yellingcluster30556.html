yelling

noun
loud, inarticulate cry; act of shouting
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of uttering a loud, inarticulate cry, typically as an expression of pain, excitement, or anger.

Examples

loud yelling in class
He was scolded for his constant yelling during class.
The yelling from the playground could be heard down the street.
Their argument ended in loud yelling that attracted everyone's attention.
