gauss

noun
unit of magnetic flux density
Pronunciation: / ɡaʊs / (AmE), / ɡaʊs / (BrE)
a unit of magnetic flux density equal to one maxwell per square centimeter

Examples

1000 gauss magnet
Laboratory magnets can produce fields exceeding several thousand gauss.
The Earth's magnetic field is about 0.5 gauss at the surface.
The intensity of the magnetic field was measured in gauss.
