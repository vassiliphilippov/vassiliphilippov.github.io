barrow

noun
quantity carried in a barrow
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˈbærəʊ / (BrE)
The amount or quantity that can be carried in a single barrow.

Examples

The price was set per barrow of potatoes sold at the market.
