counter

noun
quick, witty or critical reply
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.tə / (BrE)
a quick reply to a question or remark, especially one that is witty or critical

Examples

witty counter
He offered a clever counter to her sarcastic comment.
Her counter left everyone in the room speechless.
The politician's counter was both witty and disarming.

Related Terms

  • synonym – rejoinder: a quick reply, especially a sharp or witty one
  • synonym – retort: a sharp, quick, or witty reply
  • synonym – riposte: a quick, clever reply to an insult or criticism
