glacial

adjective
extremely cold
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈɡleɪsɪəl / (BrE)
Extremely cold; resembling ice or glaciers in temperature.

Examples

glacial wind
Despite the glacial air in the conference room, the meeting dragged on for hours.
She hurried across the glacial street to escape the biting cold.
The glacial wind cut through his coat and made him shiver uncontrollably.
