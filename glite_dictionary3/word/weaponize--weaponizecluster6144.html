to weaponize

verb
to convert for use as a weapon
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛpəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈwɛpənaɪz / (BrE)
to adapt or use something as a weapon or for military purposes

Examples

to weaponize information
Social media platforms can be weaponized to spread misinformation during elections.
Some countries fear their rivals may weaponize new scientific discoveries.
The regime was accused of weaponizing food supplies as a means of control.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disarm: to take weapons away or remove the military potential
  • synonym – militarize: to give a military character to something
