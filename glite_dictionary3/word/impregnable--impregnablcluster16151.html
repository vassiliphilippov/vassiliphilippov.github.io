impregnable

adjective
unable to be overcome or attacked
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛɡnəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛɡnəbl /; / ɪmˈprɛɡnəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to be captured, defeated, or overcome; cannot be attacked or harmed

Examples

impregnable fortress
Despite numerous attempts, the hackers found the new security system impregnable.
Her confidence in her decision seemed impregnable, no matter what others said.
The castle was considered impregnable due to its thick walls and strategic location.
His impregnable self-confidence inspired everyone around him to take on new challenges.
The fortress of her beliefs was impregnable, regardless of criticism or doubt.
The lawyer presented an impregnable argument that could not be easily disputed.
