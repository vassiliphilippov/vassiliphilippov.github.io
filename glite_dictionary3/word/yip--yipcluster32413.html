yip

noun
sharp, high-pitched animal cry
Pronunciation: / jɪp / (AmE), / jɪp / (BrE)
a sharp, high-pitched cry or bark, especially made by a dog or similar animal

Examples

a loud yip
Each excited yip from the dogs made the children laugh.
The puppy let out a sudden yip when it was startled by the noise.
We heard the yip of a fox echoing through the forest at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – yelp: a short, sharp cry, especially of pain or alarm
