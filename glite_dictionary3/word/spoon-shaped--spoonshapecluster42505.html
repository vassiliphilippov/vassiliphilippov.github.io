spoon-shaped

adjective
resembling the shape of a spoon
Pronunciation: / ˈspun ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈspuːn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape that resembles that of a spoon, typically rounded and concave.

Examples

spoon-shaped bowl
Her soup was served in a spoon-shaped bowl that fit perfectly in her hand.
The fossil had a distinct spoon-shaped beak, unusual for its species.
The leaves of this plant are spoon-shaped and gather water efficiently.
