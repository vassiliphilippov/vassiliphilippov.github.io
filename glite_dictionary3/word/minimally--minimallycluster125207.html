minimally

adverb
to a minimal degree
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnəməli / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməli / (BrE)
To the smallest possible extent or degree; only very slightly.

Examples

minimally changed settings
He moved the lever minimally to adjust the angle just a bit.
The patient was minimally conscious after the procedure, responding only to loud noises.
This solution will only minimally affect the overall performance of the system.

Related Terms

  • antonym – maximally: to the greatest possible degree
  • synonym – slightly: to a small degree or extent
