TIA

noun
internet initialism 'This Is Africa' (informal)
Pronunciation: / tiː aɪ ˈeɪ / (AmE), / tiː aɪ ˈeɪ / (BrE)
An informal internet initialism for 'This Is Africa', used in captions or comments to suggest that an image, event, or situation reflects perceived characteristics or stereotypes associated with Africa.

Examples

TIA meme caption
They posted a photo of the crowded market with the caption TIA to suggest everyday chaos.
Seeing the power cut, someone commented TIA, meaning 'This is Africa', which sparked debate.
Journalists warned that using TIA in headlines can reinforce unfair stereotypes about the continent.
