fodder

noun
data used to train AI models
Pronunciation: / \u02c8f\u0251\u02d0.d\u025a / (AmE), / \u02c8f\u0252d.\u0259 / (BrE)
(often figurative; in computing and media) material, especially large amounts of text, images, or other data, used to train, fine-tune, or test machine-learning models.

Examples

fodder for AI
Public posts online are increasingly fodder for AI training models.
Researchers warned that biased social media content often becomes fodder for flawed algorithms.
The company scraped millions of comments as fodder to fine-tune their language model.

Related Terms

  • synonym – training data: data used to train machine-learning models
