forewoman

noun
female leader of a jury
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is the leader or spokesperson of a jury

Examples

forewoman of the jury
As forewoman, it was her responsibility to communicate the jury's decision to the judge.
She was appointed forewoman after a unanimous vote by the rest of the jurors.
The forewoman announced the verdict clearly and confidently in front of the court.

Related Terms

  • synonym – foreman: a man who is the leader or spokesperson of a jury
  • synonym – foreperson: the leader or spokesperson of a jury (gender-neutral)
