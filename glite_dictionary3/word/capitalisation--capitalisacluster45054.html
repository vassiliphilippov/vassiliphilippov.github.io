capitalisation

noun
estimation of a company's value
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkapɪtəlʌɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the total value of a company's shares or assets as estimated in financial terms; the process of estimating such value

Examples

capitalisation of a company
Accurately determining the capitalisation of a business is essential for attracting investors.
Changes in the economic climate can greatly affect a firm's capitalisation.
The company's capitalisation reached over $10 billion after its successful IPO.
