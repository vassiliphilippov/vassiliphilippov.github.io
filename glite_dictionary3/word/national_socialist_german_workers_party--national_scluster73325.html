National Socialist German Workers' Party

noun
German political party (Nazi Party) 1919–1945
Pronunciation: / ˌnæʃənəl soʊˈʃɪlɪst ˈdʒɜːrmən ˈwɜːrkərz ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˌnæʃənəl səʊˈʃɪlɪst ˈdʒɜːmən ˈwɜːkəz ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
the political party founded in Germany in 1919, led by Adolf Hitler from 1921, which ruled Germany as a dictatorship from 1933 to 1945; also known as the Nazi Party
