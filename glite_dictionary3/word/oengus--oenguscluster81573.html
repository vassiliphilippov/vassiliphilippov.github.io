Oengus

proper noun
Celtic god of love and beauty
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪnɡəs / (AmE), / ˈeɪŋɡəs / (BrE)
In Irish mythology, a Celtic god of love and beauty, regarded as the patron deity of young men and women.
