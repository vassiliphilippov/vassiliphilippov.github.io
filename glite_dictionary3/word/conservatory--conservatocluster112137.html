conservatory

noun
glass building for growing plants
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝː.vəˌtɔːr.i / (AmE), / kənˈsɜː.və.tər.i / (BrE)
A glass building or room, often attached to a house, used for growing and displaying plants.

Examples

conservatory with plants
Several rare orchids are grown in the Victorian conservatory at the botanical gardens.
She designed a beautiful conservatory that overlooked the garden and provided year-round greenery.
The family spent their afternoons relaxing in the conservatory among the tropical plants and flowers.
