life

noun
vitality or liveliness in action/expression
Pronunciation: / laɪf / (AmE), / laɪf / (BrE)
animation, liveliness, or vitality in action or expression, often used to describe energy or spiritedness imparted to something.

The director wanted the actors to bring more life to their performances.
