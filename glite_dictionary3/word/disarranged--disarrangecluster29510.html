disarranged

adjective
not in order; disturbed arrangement
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪndʒd / (BrE)
(of things) not in order; having the arrangement disturbed or untidy.

Examples

disarranged furniture
After the earthquake, the furniture was left disarranged throughout the room.
Her disarranged hair suggested she had just woken up.
The papers on his desk were completely disarranged after the wind blew through the window.
