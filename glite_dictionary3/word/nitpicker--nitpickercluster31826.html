nitpicker

noun
person who criticizes minor details
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪtˌpɪkər / (AmE), / ˈnɪtˌpɪkə / (BrE)
a person who finds fault with small or trivial details, often unnecessarily

Examples

a nitpicker at work
My boss is such a nitpicker that he points out the tiniest mistakes in every report.
Stop being a nitpicker and focus on the bigger picture.
Working with a nitpicker can make even simple tasks feel exhausting.
