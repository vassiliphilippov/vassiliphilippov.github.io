progressive

adjective
favouring reform or progress
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪv / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪv / (BrE)
favoring or promoting social reform or new, modern ideas, especially in politics or education

Examples

progressive social ideas
