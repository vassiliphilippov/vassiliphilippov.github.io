to note

verb
mention or remark upon
Pronunciation: / noʊt / (AmE), / nəʊt / (BrE)
to mention or point out something; to remark or observe

Examples

to note a fact
He noted in passing that the results were better than expected.
In her report, she noted the progress made by each student.
The chairman noted that attendance had improved significantly this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ignore: to deliberately pay no attention to
  • synonym – mention: to refer briefly to something
  • synonym – observe: to say or mention as a comment
