to deny

verb
refuse to allow someone to have
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnaɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈnaɪ / (BrE)
to refuse to give, allow, or grant something to someone

Examples

deny a request
Her parents deny her sweets to protect her health.
The company cannot deny employees their legal rights.
They chose to deny him access to the confidential documents.
She denied them entry into the private event.
Some parents deny their children access to certain websites.
The board decided to deny his request for additional funding.

Related Terms

  • antonym – allow: to let someone have or do something
  • antonym – grant: to agree to give or allow something requested
  • antonym – permit: to officially allow someone to do or have something
  • synonym – refuse: to say no to a request
