pusher

noun
small wheeled baby carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃər / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃə / (BrE)
a small vehicle with four wheels in which a baby or small child is pushed around, especially by an adult

Examples

baby pusher
Don't forget to fold the pusher before putting it in the car boot.
She placed her son in the pusher and went for a walk in the park.
The twins shared a double pusher on their outings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pram: a large, traditional baby carriage, British English
  • synonym – stroller: a small carriage for pushing a baby or young child, US English
