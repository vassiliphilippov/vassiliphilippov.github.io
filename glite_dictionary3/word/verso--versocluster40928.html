verso

noun
reverse side of a coin or medal
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɜːsəʊ / (BrE)
the side of a coin or medal that does not bear the principal design, usually opposite the obverse

Examples

verso of a coin
Collectors sometimes prefer the verso of the medal for its rare markings.
The verso of the coin was carefully examined for authenticity.
The commemorative coin features a portrait on the obverse and a national emblem on the verso.

Related Terms

  • antonym – obverse: the principal or front side of a coin or medal
  • synonym – reverse: the opposite side of a coin or medal, also called verso
