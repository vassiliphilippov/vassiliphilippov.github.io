hawthorn

noun
a spring-flowering shrub or small tree (Crataegus)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌθɔːn / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub of the genus Crataegus, with white or pink spring flowers and red fruits, often found in hedgerows
