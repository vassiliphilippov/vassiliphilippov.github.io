futurism

noun
belief in the primacy of the future
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃərɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or attitude that the purpose or meaning of life is to be found in the future rather than the past or present

Examples

modern futurism ideas
Critics of futurism claim it risks neglecting valuable lessons from the past.
His worldview was shaped by futurism, leading him to always focus on what could be achieved next.
Many philosophers of futurism argue that society should prioritize long-term goals over immediate concerns.
