refuter

noun
person who disproves an argument
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfjuːtər / (AmE), / rɪˈfjuːtə / (BrE)
a person who disproves a statement or argument by offering contrary evidence or reasoning

Examples

refuter of claims
As a skilled refuter, she exposed the flaws in her opponent's logic during the debate.
The discussion panel included a noted refuter who challenged widely held beliefs with new data.
Throughout history, every major scientific breakthrough has faced a determined refuter.
