heathfowl

noun
large northern European grouse (black grouse)
Pronunciation: / ˈhiθˌfaʊl / (AmE), / ˈhiːθˌfaʊl / (BrE)
A large northern European grouse with black plumage and a lyre-shaped tail, also known as the black grouse.
