to bray

verb
crush or grind to powder
Pronunciation: / breɪ / (AmE), / breɪ / (BrE)
to crush or grind something into small pieces or powder, especially using a mortar and pestle

Examples

to bray herbs
Ancient apothecaries would bray medicinal roots for their remedies.
The cook began to bray the spices in a heavy mortar.
You should bray the dried herbs thoroughly before adding them to the mixture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crush: to press something so hard that it breaks or changes shape
  • synonym – grind: to reduce something to small particles or powder by crushing it
