kabbalism

noun
Jewish mystical tradition; secret theological doctrine
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːbəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkæbəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
The mystical and esoteric system of interpretation originating in Jewish tradition known as Kabbalah; in extended use, adherence to secret or arcane theological doctrines.
