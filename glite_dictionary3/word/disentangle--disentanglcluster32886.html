to disentangle

verb
free from entanglement or involvement
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡl̩ /; / ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡəl / (BrE)
to free someone or something from a complicated or difficult situation
