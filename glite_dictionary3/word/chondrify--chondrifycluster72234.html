to chondrify

verb
become cartilage
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːndrɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkɒndrɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
(of tissue) to become or be transformed into cartilage.

Examples

to chondrify into cartilage
During development, some skeletal elements chondrify before ossifying.
If the tissue fails to chondrify, it may result in skeletal abnormalities.
The cells chondrify rapidly during the early stages of limb formation.
