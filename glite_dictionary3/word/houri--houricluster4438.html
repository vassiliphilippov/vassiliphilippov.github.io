houri

noun
beautiful maiden in Islamic paradise
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊri / (AmE), / ˈhʊəri / (BrE)
(in Islam) a beautiful, dark-eyed maiden who, according to the Quran, will accompany the faithful in Paradise.

Examples

a houri in paradise
According to the story, each believer is promised a houri in the afterlife as a reward for faith.
In Islamic tradition, a houri is often depicted as a symbol of heavenly beauty.
The poet described her as lovely as a houri from ancient tales.
