to trespass

verb
enter land without permission
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛs.pæs /; / ˈtrɛspæs / (AmE), / ˈtrɛs.pəs /; / ˈtrɛspɑːs / (BrE)
to enter someone's land or property without permission or legal right

Examples
