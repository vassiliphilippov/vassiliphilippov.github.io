heroically

adverb
in a heroic or brave manner
Pronunciation: / həˈroʊɪkli / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great courage or bravery, like a hero.

Examples

to act heroically in danger
Despite the overwhelming odds, she fought heroically until the very end.
He endured his illness heroically, never once complaining about his suffering.
The firefighter acted heroically when he rescued the family from the burning building.

  • antonym – cowardly: in a cowardly manner, lacking courage
