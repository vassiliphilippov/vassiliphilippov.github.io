tuck

noun
compact body position in sports
Pronunciation: / tʌk / (AmE), / tʌk / (BrE)
a position in sports such as diving or skiing where the knees are bent and drawn toward the chest, with the body compacted tightly

Examples

tuck position
He held a tight tuck while spinning in the air.
In freestyle skiing, an aerodynamic tuck helps reduce wind resistance.
The diver performed a flawless tuck during her somersault.
