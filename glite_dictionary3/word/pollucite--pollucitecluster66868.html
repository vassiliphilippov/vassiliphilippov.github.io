pollucite

noun
rare mineral; main source of cesium
Pronunciation: / pəˈluːsaɪt / (AmE), / pɒˈluːsaɪt / (BrE)
a rare mineral consisting mainly of hydrated cesium aluminum silicate, and the principal ore of cesium
