curable

adjective
able to be cured or healed
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊrəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkjʊərəbəl / (BrE)
(of a disease or condition) able to be cured; capable of being healed or remedied.

Examples

curable disease
Many forms of cancer are now considered curable with early detection and proper treatment.
The doctor assured her that the infection was curable with a course of antibiotics.
While the condition can be serious, it is usually curable if treated promptly.

Related Terms

  • antonym – incurable: unable to be cured or healed
  • synonym – treatable: able to be treated, especially medically
