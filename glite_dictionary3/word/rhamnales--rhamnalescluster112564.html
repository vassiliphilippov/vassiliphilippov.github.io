Rhamnales

noun
former plant order including families like Rhamnaceae and Vitaceae
Pronunciation: / ræmˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / ræmˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
A former order of dicotyledonous flowering plants that included the families Elaeagnaceae, Leeaceae, Rhamnaceae, and Vitaceae.

Examples

Rhamnales plant families
Botanists used to recognize Rhamnales as a distinct order, though this classification is no longer widely used.
Many species formerly placed in the Rhamnales are now classified under different orders.
The Rhamnales included families of plants such as the grapevines and buckthorns.
