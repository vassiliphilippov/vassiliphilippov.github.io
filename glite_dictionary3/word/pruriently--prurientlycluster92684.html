pruriently

adverb
in a lewd or lustful way
Pronunciation: / ˈprʊriəntli / (AmE), / ˈprʊərɪəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows an excessive interest in sexual matters; in a lewd or lustful manner.

Examples

look pruriently at photos

Some tabloids write pruriently about celebrities' private lives to attract more readers.
The novel was criticized for describing events pruriently, focusing too much on explicit details.
