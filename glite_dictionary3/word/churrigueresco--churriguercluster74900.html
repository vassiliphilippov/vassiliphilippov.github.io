churrigueresco

adjective
highly ornate architectural style
Pronunciation: / tʃuːˌriːɡəˈrɛskoʊ / (AmE), / tʃʊəˌrɪɡəˈrɛskəʊ / (BrE)
(of architecture) characterized by very elaborate and symmetrical ornamentation, in the style of the Spanish Churrigueresque movement.
