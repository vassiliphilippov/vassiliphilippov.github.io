mRNA

noun
RNA molecule carrying genetic information
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm ɑːr ɛn ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛm ɑː ˈrɛn eɪ / (BrE)
a type of RNA that carries genetic information from DNA to the ribosome, where proteins are synthesized
