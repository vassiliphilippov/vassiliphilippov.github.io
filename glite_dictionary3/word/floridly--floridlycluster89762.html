floridly

adverb
in an excessively ornate way
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔr.ɪd.li / (AmE), / ˈflɒr.ɪd.li / (BrE)
In a florid manner; in a way that is excessively elaborate, ornate, or showy.

Examples

floridly written speech
He floridly decorated his speech, making even mundane topics sound grandiose.
The author wrote her descriptions floridly, filling each paragraph with elaborate metaphors and vivid imagery.
The hall was floridly adorned, with ornate gold trim and lavish tapestries covering every wall.
