millenarism

noun
belief in a future Christian millennium
Pronunciation: / mɪˈlɛnəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / mɪˈlɛnərɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in a coming Christian millennium—a thousand-year period of holiness and happiness on earth, especially as described in the Book of Revelation.

Examples

Early Protestant groups often embraced millenarism as part of their religious reforms.

The rise of millenarism has often coincided with periods of social upheaval.
