oliguria

noun
abnormally low urine output
Pronunciation: / ˌɑlɪˈgjʊriə /; / ˌɑːlɪˈɡjʊriə / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪˈgjʊəriə /; / ˌɒlɪˈɡjʊəriə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the body produces an abnormally small amount of urine

Examples
