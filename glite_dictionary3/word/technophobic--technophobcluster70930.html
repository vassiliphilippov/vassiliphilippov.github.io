technophobic

adjective
afraid of or resistant to technology
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛk.nəˈfoʊ.bɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛk.nəˈfəʊ.bɪk / (BrE)
Having or showing a fear or dislike of advanced technology, especially computers and modern devices.

Examples

technophobic attitude
Despite being technophobic, he eventually learned to use a smartphone for daily tasks.
Some schools are introducing special programs for technophobic students to help them adapt to digital learning.
The new software intimidated her so much that she felt downright technophobic.
