inbred

adjective
produced by inbreeding
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌbrɛd / (BrE)
produced by inbreeding; resulting from the mating of closely related individuals over generations.

Examples

inbred animals
Certain purebred animals are often highly inbred to maintain specific traits.
The inbred mice showed increased susceptibility to disease compared to the control group.
The researchers studied the effects of an inbred population on genetic diversity.

Related Terms

  • antonym – outbred: not produced by inbreeding; from unrelated individuals
