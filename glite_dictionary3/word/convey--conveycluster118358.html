to convey

verb
transfer property title or ownership
Pronunciation: / kənˈveɪ / (AmE), / kənˈveɪ / (BrE)
to transfer legal title or ownership of property from one person to another

Examples

convey property
Her will stated that she wished to convey the land to her children.
The deed was signed last week to officially convey ownership.
The seller agreed to convey the property to the new owner upon completion of the payment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – transfer: to move ownership from one person or entity to another
