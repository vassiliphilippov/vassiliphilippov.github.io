daredevil

noun
reckless risk-taker
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrˌdɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛəˌdɛvəl / (BrE)
a person who enjoys taking dangerous risks and acts recklessly or impulsively

Examples

a daredevil stunt
Despite warnings, she showed her daredevil side by climbing the cliff without ropes.
His parents worried about his daredevil stunts on his skateboard.
The daredevil performed a backflip on his motorcycle over five cars.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adventurer: a person who enjoys or seeks adventure
  • antonym – coward: a person who lacks courage or is excessively afraid of danger
