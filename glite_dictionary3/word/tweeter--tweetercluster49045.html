tweeter

noun
Twitter user
Pronunciation: / c8twit5a / (AmE), / c8twid0t59 / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who posts messages (tweets) on the social media platform Twitter; a Twitter user.

Examples

active tweeter online
The politician became a famous tweeter after joining the platform and gaining followers.
As a tweeter, she uses the site to share quick news updates and opinions every morning.
Local businesses hire influencers who are active tweeters to promote their new products online.
