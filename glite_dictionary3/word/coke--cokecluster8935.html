Coke

noun
trademarked cola soft drink
Pronunciation: / koʊk / (AmE), / kəʊk / (BrE)
A trademarked carbonated soft drink with a cola flavor, produced by The Coca-Cola Company; commonly used to refer to any cola-flavored soft drink.
