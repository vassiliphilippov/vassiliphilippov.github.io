venomously

adverb
in a very spiteful or hostile manner
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛnəməsli / (AmE), / ˈvɛnəməsli / (BrE)
In a manner expressing intense malice or spite; with extreme bitterness or hostility.

Examples

to speak venomously

She spoke venomously about her former business partner during the interview.
The debate quickly turned personal, with each side responding more venomously than before.
