nonstick

adjective
designed to prevent food from sticking
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈstɪk / (BrE)
(of surfaces, especially of cookware) designed to prevent food from sticking during cooking.

Examples

nonstick frying pan
Always use wooden utensils with nonstick pans to avoid damaging the coating.
She prefers to cook pancakes in a nonstick skillet for easy flipping.
The nonstick surface makes cleanup much faster after cooking meals.
