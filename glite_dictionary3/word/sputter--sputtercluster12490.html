to sputter

verb
remove atoms from a surface via energetic bombardment
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈspʌtə / (BrE)
to subject (a material or surface) to a process in which atoms are ejected, typically by bombardment with energetic particles

Examples

to sputter a surface
Certain techniques sputter thin films onto microchips for electronic manufacturing.
Solar wind can sputter atoms off the surfaces of airless planetary bodies.
The scientist used a plasma beam to sputter gold atoms from the target surface.
