Pedionomus torquatus

noun
small Australian plains bird (plains-wanderer)
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdiəˈnoʊməs tɔrˈkwætəs / (AmE), / ˌpiːdiəˈnəʊməs tɔːˈkwɑːtəs / (BrE)
a small, ground-dwelling Australian bird resembling a button quail, sometimes classified as a wading bird but typically found on plains; also known as the plains-wanderer
