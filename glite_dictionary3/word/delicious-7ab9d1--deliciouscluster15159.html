delicious

adjective
highly pleasing or delightful
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪʃ.əs / (BrE)
Extremely pleasing or delightful, especially in a way that entertains or brings joy.

delicious joke
