Scincidae

noun
family of lizards known as skinks
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.sɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.sɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of lizards commonly known as skinks, characterized by their smooth, shiny scales and often elongated bodies.
