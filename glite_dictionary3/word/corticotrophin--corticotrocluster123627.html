corticotrophin

noun
pituitary hormone stimulating the adrenal cortex
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrtɪkəˈtroʊfɪn / (AmE), / ˌkɔːtɪkəˈtrəʊfɪn / (BrE)
a hormone produced by the anterior pituitary gland that stimulates the adrenal cortex to release corticosteroids
