panduriform

adjective
shaped like a fiddle; leaf with rounded ends and constricted middle
Pronunciation: / pænˈdʊrəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / panˈdjʊərɪfɔːm / (BrE)
(of a leaf) having rounded ends with a narrowed, constricted center; shaped like a fiddle.

Examples

panduriform leaf shape
Botanists use the term panduriform to describe leaves with a fiddle-shaped outline.
She identified the species by its panduriform leaves, which resemble a violin.
The leaves of this plant are panduriform, giving them a distinctive appearance.
