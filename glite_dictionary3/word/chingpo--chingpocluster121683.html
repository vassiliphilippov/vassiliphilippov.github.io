Chingpo

noun
a Kachinic language
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋˈpoʊ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋˈpəʊ / (BrE)
A member of the Kachinic group of languages, spoken by the Chingpo (Jingpo) people in parts of Myanmar (Burma) and neighboring regions.

Chingpo language study
Researchers are documenting the grammar and vocabulary of Chingpo for linguistic studies.
