incarnate

adjective
embodied in bodily or physical form
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑrnət /; / ɪnˈkɑrˌneɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɑːnət /; / ɪnˈkɑː.nət / (BrE)
(of a spirit, quality, or deity) embodied or made real in bodily form; given physical shape or substance
