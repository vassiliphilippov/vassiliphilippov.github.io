crescendo

adjective
gradually increasing in loudness (music)
Pronunciation: / krəˈʃɛndoʊ / (AmE), / krɛˈʃɛndəʊ / (BrE)
(of music) gradually increasing in loudness or intensity.

Examples

crescendo passage
As the music swelled in a crescendo, the dancers moved faster across the stage.
The movement ended with a dramatic crescendo that filled the concert hall with sound.
The orchestra played the passage with a crescendo that built excitement in the audience.

  • antonym – diminuendo: gradually decreasing in loudness
