contribution

noun
individual effort toward a result
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
the effort or part played by a person in achieving a result or advancing a project

Examples

contribution to success
Every team member's contribution is essential for the project's overall success.
His contribution in the meeting helped clarify the main issues and led to productive solutions.
She was praised for her significant contribution to the charity's successful fundraising campaign.
