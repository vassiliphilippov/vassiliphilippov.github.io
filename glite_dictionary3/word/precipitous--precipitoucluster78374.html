precipitous

adjective
dangerously steep (of slope, drop, etc.)
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪpɪtəs / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪpɪtəs / (BrE)
(of a slope, drop, or rise) extremely steep and often dangerous.

Examples

precipitous cliff face
A precipitous drop in the path made hiking treacherous during the rain.
The trail to the summit was blocked by a precipitous cliff face.
They carefully descended the precipitous mountain road as thick fog rolled in.

Related Terms

  • synonym – steep: having a sharp inclination or slope
