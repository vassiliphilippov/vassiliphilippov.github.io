academicism

noun
strict adherence to academic conventions
Pronunciation: / əˈkædəˌsɪzəm /; / əˈkæd.əˌsɪ.zəm / (AmE), / əˈkadəmɪsɪz(ə)m /; / əˈkæd.əm.ɪ.sɪ.zəm / (BrE)
an emphasis on strict academic rules or conventions, often criticized for limiting creativity or originality

Examples

academicism in research methods
