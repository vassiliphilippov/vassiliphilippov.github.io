to buzz

verb
call someone with a buzzer
Pronunciation: / bʌz / (AmE), / bʌz / (BrE)
to call someone by operating a buzzer, especially to request their attention or admittance

Examples

to buzz the door
If you need assistance, just buzz the attendant from your room.
Please buzz me when you arrive at the front door.
