compressible

adjective
able to be compressed
Pronunciation: / kəmˈprɛsəbəl / (AmE), / kəmˈprɛsɪbəl /; / kəmˈprɛsəbəl / (BrE)
able to be compressed or made smaller in size or volume, especially by pressure (used for materials like gases or fluids)
