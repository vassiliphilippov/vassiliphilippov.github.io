sawwort

noun
European dye-producing plant
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːwɔːt / (BrE)
A European perennial plant with serrated leaves that are used to produce a yellow dye.

Examples

sawwort yellow dye
Botanists discovered a rare species of sawwort in the alpine meadow.
