boundary

noun
greatest or furthest limit of something
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊndɛri / (AmE), / ˈbaʊndəri / (BrE)
the greatest or furthest limit or degree that something can reach

Examples

boundary of ability
She refused to push herself beyond the boundary of her endurance.
The discussion tested the boundary of what was considered acceptable.
There is a clear boundary between professional and personal life for him.
