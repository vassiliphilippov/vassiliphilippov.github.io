footer

noun
length or height in feet (in combination)
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtər / (AmE), / ˈfʊtə / (BrE)
(used only in combination) a measure indicating the height or length of something in feet; used especially in phrases such as 'six-footer' or '40-footer'.

Examples

a 20-footer putt
Everyone cheered when she made the difficult 25-footer putt on the final hole.
He played basketball with a teammate who was a towering six-footer.
The museum just acquired a rare 50-footer for its collection of historic yachts.
