studbook

noun
official record of pedigrees of purebred animals
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈstʌd.bʊk / (BrE)
An official record listing the pedigree of purebred animals, especially horses, kept for breeding purposes.

Examples

studbook records pedigree
Breeders must consult the studbook before pairing animals to avoid close inbreeding.
The Thoroughbred studbook contains detailed records of every registered horse's ancestry.
The zoo maintains a global studbook for endangered species to manage breeding programs.
