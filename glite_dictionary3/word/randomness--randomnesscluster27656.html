randomness

noun
quality of being random or unpredictable
Pronunciation: / ˈrændəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈrændəmnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lacking any predictable order or plan; the state of being random

Examples

pure randomness
Her life seemed guided less by careful planning and more by pure randomness.
Scientists simulate randomness in experiments to test probability theories.
The randomness of the lottery makes it impossible to predict the winning numbers.
