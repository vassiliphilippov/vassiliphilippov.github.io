to insert

verb
to include something casually or unobtrusively
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɝt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːt / (BrE)
to introduce or include something, especially casually or unobtrusively, into a conversation, text, or situation

Examples

to insert a comment
He tried to insert a few words about his project during the meeting.
She managed to insert a clever joke into the conversation without anyone noticing.
The author would often insert personal anecdotes subtly into her essays.
