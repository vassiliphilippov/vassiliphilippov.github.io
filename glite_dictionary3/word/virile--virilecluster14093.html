virile

adjective
sexually capable (male)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪrɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɪərʌɪl / (BrE)
(of a male) capable of sexual intercourse; having the physical capability to copulate.

Examples

virile young man
Despite his graying hair, he remained a virile and energetic man.
Many ancient sculptures celebrated the virile qualities of the male form.
The doctor confirmed that he was still quite virile for his age.

Related Terms

  • antonym – impotent: unable to copulate or achieve erection
