headgear

noun
clothing or coverings for the head
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌɡɪə / (BrE)
anything worn on the head, especially as clothing or for protection, decoration, or ceremonial purposes.

Examples

protective headgear
Many athletes use special headgear to protect themselves during competitions.
She chose elegant headgear for the royal wedding.
The safety regulations require all workers to wear headgear while on site.
