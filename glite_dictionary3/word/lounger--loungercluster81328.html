lounger

noun
reclining armchair
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊndʒər / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒə / (BrE)
a comfortable armchair whose back can be lowered and footrest raised to allow the sitter to recline

Examples

reclining lounger chair
After a long day at work, she relaxed in the lounger and watched television.
He spent the afternoon reading in his favorite lounger by the window.
The living room was furnished with a large lounger and a matching footstool.
