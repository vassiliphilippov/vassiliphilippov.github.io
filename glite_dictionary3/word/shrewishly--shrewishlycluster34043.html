shrewishly

adverb
in a bad-tempered, nagging manner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃruːɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈʃruːɪʃli / (BrE)
In a way that is bad-tempered, nagging, or scolding, like a shrew.

Examples

to speak shrewishly
She spoke shrewishly to her husband when he forgot their anniversary.
The boss shrewishly criticized every minor mistake in the report.
Whenever she lost her temper, she would react shrewishly towards the children.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shrewish: bad-tempered and aggressively assertive (typically used of a woman)
