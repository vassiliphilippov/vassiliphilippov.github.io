redstart

noun
small North American warbler with orange markings
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌstɑrt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌstɑːt / (BrE)
a small flycatching warbler found in eastern North America, the male being marked with bright orange on its sides, wings, and tail.

Examples

American redstart male
