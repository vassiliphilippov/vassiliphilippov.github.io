mesomorphy

noun
muscular, big-boned body type
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛzəˈmɔrfi / (AmE), / ˌmɛzəˈmɔːfi / (BrE)
The condition or quality of having a muscular and well-built, big-boned body type, typically classified as mesomorphic in somatotype theory.

Examples

natural mesomorphy
His natural mesomorphy gave him an advantage in most sports.
Thanks to her pronounced mesomorphy, she built muscle easily during training.
The study compared athletic performance among individuals with varying degrees of mesomorphy.
