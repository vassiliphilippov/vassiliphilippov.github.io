transoceanic

adjective
across or spanning an ocean
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænsoʊˈʃænɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrænzəʊˈsiːænɪk / (BrE)
crossing, spanning, or situated on the other side of an ocean; relating to or involving travel or communication between continents across an ocean

Examples

transoceanic flight route
The airline introduced a new transoceanic flight route between Asia and North America.
The ship's sturdy construction made it ideal for transoceanic voyages.
Undersea cables enable transoceanic communication between continents.
