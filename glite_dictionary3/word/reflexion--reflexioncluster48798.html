reflexion

noun
nonverbal sign or manifestation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛkʃən / (BrE)
A sign or manifestation of a condition or feeling, not expressed verbally.

Examples

reflexion of mood
A student's messy desk can be a reflexion of their current mood.
Her smile was a reflexion of her genuine happiness.
The quietness in the room was a reflexion of the solemn occasion.
