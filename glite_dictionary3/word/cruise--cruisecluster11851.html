cruise

noun
ocean trip for pleasure
Pronunciation: / kruːz / (AmE), / kruːz / (BrE)
a journey on a large ship taken for pleasure, usually visiting several places

Examples

a cruise holiday
During the cruise, they visited several islands and explored new cities.
The couple enjoyed a luxury cruise for their anniversary.
They are going on a cruise to the Caribbean next month.

  • synonym – expedition: a journey with a particular purpose, especially that of exploration
  • synonym – voyage: a long journey by sea or in space
