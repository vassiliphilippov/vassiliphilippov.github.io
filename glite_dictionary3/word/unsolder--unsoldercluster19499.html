to unsolder

verb
remove solder from a joint
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɑdɚ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɒdə / (BrE)
to remove solder from a joined object, especially in electronic or metalworking connections

Examples

to unsolder a wire
Be careful not to damage the board when you unsolder the connections.
She unsoldered the wires so she could replace the faulty switch.
To repair the circuit, he had to unsolder the damaged component first.
