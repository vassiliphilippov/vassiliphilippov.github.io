to cipher

verb
convert text into code
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪfə / (BrE)
to convert ordinary language into a code, especially to keep a message secret

Examples

to cipher a message
All sensitive documents were ciphered before being sent electronically.
Can you cipher this message so that only the team understands it?
She spent hours ciphering secret codes for the club's activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – decipher: to convert code back into ordinary language
  • synonym – encode: to convert information into a different form, often for secrecy or storage
