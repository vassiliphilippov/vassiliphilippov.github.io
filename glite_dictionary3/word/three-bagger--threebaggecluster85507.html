three-bagger

noun
base hit reaching third base (baseball)
Pronunciation: / ˈθri ˌbæɡər / (AmE), / ˈθriː ˌbaɡə / (BrE)
(Baseball) A base hit on which the batter safely reaches third base; a triple.

Examples

hit a three-bagger
He has recorded more three-baggers this season than anyone else on the roster.
She managed a three-bagger that drove in two runs for her team.
The crowd cheered when he hit a three-bagger in the bottom of the seventh inning.
