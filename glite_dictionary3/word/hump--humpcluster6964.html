to hump

verb
to arch or round the back (bend forward)
Pronunciation: / hʌmp / (AmE), / hʌmp / (BrE)
to arch or round your back by bending forward and drawing the shoulders forward

Examples

hump your shoulders
He humped his shoulders against the cold as he walked down the street.
She humped over her desk, concentrating on the paperwork.
The cat humped its back when it saw the dog approaching.
