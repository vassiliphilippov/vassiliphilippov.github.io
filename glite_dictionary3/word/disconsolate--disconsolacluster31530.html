disconsolate

adjective
unable to be comforted; deeply sad
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkɑnsələt /; / dɪsˈkɑːnsələt / (AmE), / dɪsˈkɒnsələt / (BrE)
very sad or making others feel very sad; unable to be comforted
