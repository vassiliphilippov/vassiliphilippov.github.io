suspension

noun
temporary removal from privilege or position
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛnʃən / (AmE), / səˈspɛnʃən / (BrE)
the temporary removal of someone from a privilege, office, or activity as a penalty or pending investigation.

Examples

suspension from school
After several warnings, his suspension from the club was inevitable.
The company announced the suspension of the employee pending an internal review.
The student received a suspension from school for violating the code of conduct.
