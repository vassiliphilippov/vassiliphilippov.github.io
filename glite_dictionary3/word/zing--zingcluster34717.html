zing

noun
high-pitched buzzing or humming sound
Pronunciation: / zɪŋ / (AmE), / zɪŋ / (BrE)
a brief high-pitched buzzing or humming sound, especially one that is sharp or vibrant

Examples

a zing sound
A zing followed as the arrow shot through the air.
She heard the zing of a mosquito fly past her ear.
The zing of the bell echoed through the hall.
