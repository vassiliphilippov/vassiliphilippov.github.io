takeout

noun
prepared food to eat elsewhere
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈteɪkˌaʊt / (BrE)
food that is prepared and sold by a restaurant or store to be eaten elsewhere

Examples

order takeout food
After a long day, ordering takeout is the easiest option.
She brought some Chinese takeout to the office for lunch.
We decided to get takeout instead of cooking dinner at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym – takeaway: food bought at a restaurant or shop to be eaten elsewhere (British English)
