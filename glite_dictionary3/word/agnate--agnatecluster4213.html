agnate

adjective
related through the father's side
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡ.neɪt / (AmE), / ˈaɡ.neɪt / (BrE)
Related through the male line or on the father's side of the family.

Examples

agnate uncle
A agnate uncle is one related to you through your father's family.
In traditional societies, property often passes down the agnate line.
Only his agnate relatives were included in the inheritance according to the old law.
