ermine

noun
small weasel-like mammal in white winter coat
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrmɪn / (AmE), / ˈɜːmɪn / (BrE)
a small mammal of the weasel family, especially when in its white winter coat; also, its white fur, used historically for trimming garments.

Examples

wild ermine in snow
In winter, the ermine blends in perfectly with the snow-covered landscape.
The royal robe was trimmed with luxurious ermine.
We spotted an ermine darting across the snowy field during our hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stoat: a small mammal related to the weasel, whose white-coat phase is called ermine
