watchstrap

noun
band for attaching a wristwatch to the wrist
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːtʃˌstræp / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌstræp / (BrE)
a band made of cloth, leather, or metal links, attached to a wristwatch and worn around the wrist
