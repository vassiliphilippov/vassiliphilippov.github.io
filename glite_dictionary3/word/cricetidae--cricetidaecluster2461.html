Cricetidae

noun
family of small New World rodents
Pronunciation: / kraɪˈsiːtɪˌdi /; / kraɪˈsiːtɪˌdiː / (AmE), / kraɪˈsiːtɪdiː /; / kraɪˈsiːtɪˌdiː / (BrE)
