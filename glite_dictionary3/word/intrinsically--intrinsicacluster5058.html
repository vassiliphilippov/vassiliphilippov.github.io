intrinsically

adverb
in an inherent or essential manner
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtrɪnzɪkli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtrɪnsɪkli / (BrE)
in an essential or inherent way; with respect to the true nature of something

Examples

intrinsically valuable
Gold is intrinsically valuable because of its scarcity and physical properties.
Some people believe that art is intrinsically connected to human expression.
The task isn't intrinsically difficult, but it does require patience.
