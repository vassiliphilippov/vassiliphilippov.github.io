Saint Bernard

noun
large Swiss Alpine rescue dog breed
Pronunciation: / seɪnt bərˈnɑrd / (AmE), / seɪnt bəˈnɑːd / (BrE)
a large, strong breed of dog originally from the Swiss Alps, known for its thick coat and use in mountain rescue work
