to spiritise

verb
imbue with a spirit
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to imbue or infuse with a spirit; to invest with spiritual qualities

Examples

to spiritise an object
Ancient rituals were believed to spiritise ordinary objects, giving them special power.
The novelist sought to spiritise the mundane world of her characters with magic realism.
The painting seemed to spiritise the landscape, imbuing it with a sense of the supernatural.
