hallux

noun
the big toe; innermost and largest toe
Pronunciation: / ˈhæləks / (AmE), / ˈhalʌks / (BrE)
the innermost and largest toe of the foot, commonly known as the big toe, especially in humans

Examples

hallux fracture
A fracture of the hallux can make walking extremely painful.
In certain birds, the hallux is adapted for perching and grasping branches.
The surgeon examined the patient's hallux for signs of inflammation before proceeding.
