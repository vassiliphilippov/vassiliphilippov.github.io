crumpet

noun
thick soft cake with porous texture
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌmp.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈkrʌm.pɪt / (BrE)
A thick, soft, and round cake with a porous texture, usually cooked on a griddle and often eaten toasted with butter.

Examples

toasted crumpet with butter
He put the crumpet on the griddle until it turned golden brown.
She enjoyed a hot crumpet with butter and jam for breakfast.
The bakery sells fresh crumpets every morning.
