Shih Tzu

noun
small, long-haired Chinese dog breed
Pronunciation: / ʃiˈtsu / (AmE), / ʃiː ˈtsuː / (BrE)
a small, long-haired breed of dog originating in China, with a short muzzle and a distinctive flowing coat
