kinesthesia

noun
sense of body movement and position
Pronunciation: / kɪnəsˈθiʒə /; / ˌkɪnəˈsθiʒə / (AmE), / kɪnɛsˈθiːziə /; / ˌkɪnɪsˈθiːziə / (BrE)
the sense that allows you to feel the position and movement of your muscles and joints
