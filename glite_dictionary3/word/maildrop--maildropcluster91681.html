maildrop

noun
receptacle for depositing mail
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪl.drɑp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪl.drɒp / (BrE)
A container or designated place where mail can be deposited for collection.

Examples

maildrop for letters
Apartment residents use a communal maildrop to send outgoing mail.
He slipped the letter into the maildrop outside the post office.
The building has a secure maildrop for tenants to deposit important documents.
