unlawfully

adverb
illegally; not in conformity with the law
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɔfli / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɔːfli / (BrE)
In a manner that does not conform to the law; illegally.

Examples

unlawfully enter a building
He was accused of unlawfully entering the building without permission.
The company was fined for unlawfully dumping waste in the river.
They discovered the documents had been unlawfully obtained.

Related Terms

  • synonym – illegally: in a way that is against the law
  • antonym – lawfully: in a way that is allowed by law
