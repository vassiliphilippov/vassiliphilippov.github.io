Centropus phasianinus

proper noun
Australian bird with a long tail (pheasant coucal)
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtroʊpəs fejziˈænɪnəs / (AmE), / sɛnˈtrəʊpəs fɛziˈænɪnəs / (BrE)
A species of Australian bird in the cuckoo family, notable for its long, pheasant-like tail; commonly known as the pheasant coucal.
