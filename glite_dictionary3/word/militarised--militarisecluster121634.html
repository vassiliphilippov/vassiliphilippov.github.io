militarised

adjective
equipped with military arms
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd / (BrE)
Equipped with military arms or given a military character; subject to military control or influence.

Examples

militarised zone
After the coup, the government established a militarised zone around the capital.
Many protesters opposed the militarised response by the police to peaceful demonstrations.
The border has become increasingly militarised in recent years due to security concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym – demilitarised: having all military forces or installations removed
