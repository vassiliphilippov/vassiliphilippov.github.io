jacket

noun
metal shell casing for ammunition
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækət /; / ˈdʒækɪt /; / ˈdʒæk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒækɪt /; / ˈdʒakɪt /; / ˈdʒæk.ɪt / (BrE)
an outer covering or casing placed around something to protect or improve its appearance, including for objects like bullets, teeth, DVDs, or potatoes
