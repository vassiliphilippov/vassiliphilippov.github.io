fingerboard

noun
part of a stringed musical instrument's neck
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌbɔrd /; / ˈfɪŋɡɚˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌbɔːd /; / ˈfɪŋɡəbɔːd / (BrE)
the flat or narrow strip on a musical instrument (especially stringed instruments) where the fingers press to play different notes
