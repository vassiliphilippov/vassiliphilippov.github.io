to catfish

verb
deceive with a fake online identity (esp. romantically)
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkat.fɪʃ / (BrE)
To deceive someone by pretending to be another person online, often by using a fake identity to pursue a romantic relationship.

Examples

to catfish strangers
He discovered he had been catfished when the person he was chatting with online refused to video call.
Many people have been catfished on dating apps by someone using fake photos.
She warned her friends about the dangers of catfishing after her own experience online.

Related Terms

  • synonym – imposter: a person who pretends to be someone else to deceive others
  • synonym – scam: a dishonest scheme or fraud, especially on the internet
