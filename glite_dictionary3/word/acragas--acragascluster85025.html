Acragas

proper noun
ancient Greek city in Sicily
Pronunciation: / ˈækrəˌɡæs / (AmE), / ˈakrəɡas / (BrE)
an ancient town in southwestern Sicily, near the coast, known for its six Greek temples; modern-day Agrigento.
