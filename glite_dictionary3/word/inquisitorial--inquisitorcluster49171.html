inquisitorial

adjective
relating to judicial investigation by a judge
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌkwɪzəˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ɪnˌkwɪzɪˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to official or judicial investigations, especially where an authority actively investigates the facts
