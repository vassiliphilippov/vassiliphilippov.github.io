digitalin

noun
cardiac stimulant from foxglove
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪdʒɪˈteɪlɪn / (AmE), / ˌdɪdʒɪˈteɪlɪn / (BrE)
a powerful cardiac stimulant extracted from the foxglove plant, used especially to treat certain heart conditions
