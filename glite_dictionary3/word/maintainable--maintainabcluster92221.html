maintainable

adjective
able to be maintained
Pronunciation: / meɪnˈteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / meɪnˈteɪnəbəl / (BrE)
able to be maintained or kept in a specified state or condition, especially with reasonable effort.

Examples

maintainable vehicle
A maintainable vehicle reduces the cost of repairs and keeps it running longer.
The old software was barely maintainable due to its complex codebase.
They designed the garden to be easily maintainable throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unmaintainable: not able to be maintained
