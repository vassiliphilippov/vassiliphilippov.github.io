solidly

adverb
completely or unanimously, as a group
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑlɪdli / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows complete agreement or unity; as a single undivided group or whole.

Examples

solidly behind the leader
During the election, the workers supported the candidate solidly.
The board voted solidly in favor of the new policy.
The community stood solidly behind the mayor after the crisis.

  • synonym – unanimously: with everyone agreeing completely
