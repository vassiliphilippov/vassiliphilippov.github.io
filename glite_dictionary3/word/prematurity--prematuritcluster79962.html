prematurity

noun
state of being premature, often in birth
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːməˈtʃʊrəti / (AmE), / ˌpriːməˈtjʊərɪti / (BrE)
The state of occurring or being born before the expected or usual time, especially referring to birth before the normal gestation period.

Examples

prematurity of birth
Doctors closely monitor infants with prematurity for potential health complications.
The prematurity of his decision surprised everyone in the meeting.
The prematurity of the baby's birth required advanced medical care.
