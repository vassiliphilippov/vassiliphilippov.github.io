rampant

adjective
uncontrolled; widespread (usually negative)
Pronunciation: / ˈræmpənt / (AmE), / ˈræmpənt / (BrE)
(of something unwelcome or unpleasant) existing or spreading unchecked and often out of control.

Examples

rampant crime
Crime was rampant in the city during the late 1980s.
Rumors about the company's financial troubles were rampant throughout the office.
The garden was overgrown, with weeds rampant among the flowers.

Related Terms

  • antonym – controlled: under restraint or kept within limits
  • synonym – unchecked: not restrained or controlled
