resounding

adjective
loud and echoing (of sound)
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzaʊndɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈzaʊndɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound) loud and deep, characterized by resonance; echoing or reverberating.

Examples

resounding cheer
A resounding cheer echoed across the stadium as the team scored.
Her voice was resounding enough to be heard at the back of the auditorium.
The hall was filled with the resounding sound of applause after the speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym – resonant: deep, clear, and continuing to sound or ring
