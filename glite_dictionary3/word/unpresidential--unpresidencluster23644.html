unpresidential

adjective
not suitable for a president
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prɛz.ɪˈdɛn.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɛz.ɪˈdɛn.ʃəl / (BrE)
(of behavior or actions) not characteristic of or suitable for a president; lacking the dignity, decorum, or appropriateness expected of a president.

Examples

Critics argued that the candidate's tweets were often unpresidential in tone and content.
