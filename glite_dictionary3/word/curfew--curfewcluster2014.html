curfew

noun
official restriction on going outdoors after a set time
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜr.fju / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.fjuː / (BrE)
an official order requiring people to remain indoors or off the streets after a specified time, usually issued for security or safety reasons

Examples

city curfew order
After the riot, the police enforced a strict curfew throughout the downtown area.
Teens often complain about their parents setting an early curfew on weekends.
The city imposed a curfew starting at 8 p.m. to ensure public safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lockdown: a state of isolation or restricted access instituted for safety or security
