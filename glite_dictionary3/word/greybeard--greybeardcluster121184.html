greybeard

noun
17th-century stoneware jug with a long neck
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪˌbɪrd / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌbɪːd / (BrE)
A stoneware drinking jug with a long neck, typically decorated with a caricature of Cardinal Bellarmine, used in the 17th century.
