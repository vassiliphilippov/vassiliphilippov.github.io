choked

adjective
blocked or obstructed
Pronunciation: / tʃoʊkt / (AmE), / tʃəʊkt / (BrE)
blocked or heavily obstructed, especially so that nothing can pass through; clogged up.

Examples

choked with leaves
A drain choked with leaves can easily overflow during heavy rain.
He noticed the machine had stopped working because the filter was choked with dust.
The highway was choked with rush hour traffic this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clogged: blocked with an accumulation of material
