meeting

noun
coming together; merging
Pronunciation: / ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of coming together or merging, especially of ideas, groups, or minds

Examples

meeting of minds
The meeting of different cultures can create something entirely new.
The meeting of the two rivers forms a beautiful delta.
There was a true meeting of minds during their discussions.
