Segway

noun
self-balancing two-wheeled transporter
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ / (BrE)
A trademarked, self-balancing two-wheeled personal transporter that operates by detecting the rider's movements.

Examples

ride a Segway downtown
He commutes to work on his Segway, enjoying the convenience and speed.
She lost her balance while riding a Segway for the first time.
Tourists often rent a Segway to explore the city center with a guide.
