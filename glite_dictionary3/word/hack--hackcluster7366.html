to hack

verb
kick on the shins (esp. in sports)
Pronunciation: / hæk / (AmE), / hæk / (BrE)
to kick someone on the shins, often deliberately, especially in sports such as football

Examples

to hack a player
He was furious after being hacked on the shins by the opposing player.
If you hack someone in football, you could be given a yellow card.
The defender was penalized for hacking his opponent during the match.
