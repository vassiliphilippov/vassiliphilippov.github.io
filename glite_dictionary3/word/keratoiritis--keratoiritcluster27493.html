keratoiritis

noun
inflammation of cornea and iris
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛrətoʊaɪˈraɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌkɛrətəʊaɪˈraɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of both the cornea and the iris of the eye.

Examples

keratoiritis affects vision
Symptoms of keratoiritis often include blurred vision, eye discomfort, and sensitivity to light.
The doctor diagnosed her with keratoiritis after examining the redness and pain in her eye.
Untreated keratoiritis can lead to vision problems if not managed promptly.
