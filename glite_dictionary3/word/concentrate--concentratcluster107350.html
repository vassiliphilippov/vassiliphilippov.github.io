to concentrate

verb
to cook until liquid is reduced
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt /; / ˈkɑnsənˌtreɪt /; / ˈkɑːn.sənˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnsəntreɪt /; / ˈkɒn.sənˌtreɪt / (BrE)
