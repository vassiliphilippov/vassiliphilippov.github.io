unfertilized

adjective
not made fertile; not fertilized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɝːtəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɜːtɪlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an egg, soil, etc.) not having been made fertile, especially not having undergone fertilization.

Examples

unfertilized egg
Most flowers produce unfertilized seeds if pollination does not occur.
She studied the difference between fertilized and unfertilized samples under the microscope.
The chicken laid an unfertilized egg, so it will never develop into a chick.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fertilized: made fertile, especially by union with sperm or by adding nutrients
