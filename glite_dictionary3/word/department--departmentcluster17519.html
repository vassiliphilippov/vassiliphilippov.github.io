department

noun
division of an organization
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑrtmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
A specialized division within a large organization such as a business, university, or government, responsible for a particular area of work.

Examples

department of sales
Each department in the company submits a monthly report.
She works in the department of Biology at the university.
The customer service department handles all the complaints and inquiries.
