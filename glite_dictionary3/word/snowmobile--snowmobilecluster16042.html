to snowmobile

verb
to ride a snowmobile
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊmoʊˌbiːl / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊməʊˌbiːl / (BrE)
to ride or travel by snowmobile, especially over snow-covered terrain

Examples

to snowmobile across fields
He spends every weekend snowmobiling with his friends in the mountains.
Last winter, she snowmobiled through the forest during her trip to Canada.
We decided to snowmobile across the frozen lake early in the morning.
