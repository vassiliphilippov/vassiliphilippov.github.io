atomisation

noun
separation into fine particles or droplets
Pronunciation: / ˌæt̬əˌmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌætəmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
The process of breaking something into very fine particles or droplets, often by spraying or dispersing.

Examples

atomisation of paint droplets
Advances in atomisation technology have improved paint spraying methods.
Modern inhalers rely on the atomisation of medication for effective delivery to the lungs.
The atomisation of the fuel ensures thorough combustion.
