irritating

adjective
causing annoyance or discomfort
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrɪˌteɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
causing annoyance, anger, or mild discomfort; making someone feel annoyed or impatient.

Examples

irritating noise
I found the loud buzzing of the fly especially irritating while I was trying to read.
She develops an irritating cough whenever she stays in dusty rooms.
Wearing wool directly on my skin can be irritating during the summer.

  • antonym – soothing: having a calming or comforting effect
