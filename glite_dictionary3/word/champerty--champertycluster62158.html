champerty

noun
illegal agreement to share lawsuit profits
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæmpɚti / (AmE), / ˈtʃæmpəti / (BrE)
an illegal or unethical agreement in which a person, often a lawyer, agrees to finance someone else's lawsuit in return for a share of the proceeds if the case is won

Examples

illegal champerty agreement
Some jurisdictions have strict laws against champerty to prevent corrupt legal practices.
The court ruled that their arrangement amounted to champerty and declared it void.
The lawyer was disbarred after being found guilty of champerty in several cases.

  • synonym – maintenance: improper support for another's lawsuit
