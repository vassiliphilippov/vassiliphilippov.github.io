threader

noun
tool for threading ribbon through loops
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɛdər / (AmE), / ˈθrɛdə / (BrE)
a blunt needle or tool used for threading ribbon, elastic, or cord through loops or hems in fabric

Examples

threader for ribbon
She used a threader to pull the ribbon through the drawstring casing.
The threader made sewing her costume much easier.
Without a threader, it would have taken much longer to thread the elastic.

Related Terms

  • synonym – needle: a small, slender, pointed implement for sewing or piercing
