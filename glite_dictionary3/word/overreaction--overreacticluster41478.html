overreaction

noun
an excessive or inappropriate response
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərriˈækʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərriˈækʃən / (BrE)
an excessive or inappropriate response, especially one involving strong emotions or behavior

Examples

an overreaction to criticism
Her angry response was an obvious overreaction to a minor mistake.
Many people believe the company's decision to recall the product was an overreaction.
The manager apologized for his overreaction after hearing the full story.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exaggeration: a statement that makes something seem greater or more intense than it is
  • antonym – underreaction: an insufficient or too weak response
