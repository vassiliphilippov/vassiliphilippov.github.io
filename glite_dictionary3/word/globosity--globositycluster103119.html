globosity

noun
roundness or sphericity
Pronunciation: / ɡləˈbɑsɪti / (AmE), / ɡləˈbɒsɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being spherical or nearly spherical in shape.

Examples

globosity of a fruit
Botanists often compare the globosity of seeds when identifying plant species.
Scientists measured the globosity of the newly discovered asteroid to determine its origin.
The globosity of the fruit makes it easy to distinguish from other types.
