to spam

verb
send unsolicited or junk email
Pronunciation: / spæm / (AmE), / spæm / (BrE)
To send unsolicited or unwanted messages, especially email, to a large number of recipients.

Examples

to spam inboxes
Many users reported that their inboxes had been spammed overnight.
Marketing companies often spam thousands of people with unsolicited advertisements.
Please do not spam the forum with repeated messages or irrelevant links.
