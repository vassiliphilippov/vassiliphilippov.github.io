immersion

noun
deep mental involvement or absorption
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːʃən / (BrE)
deep mental involvement or absorption in something, especially when attention is fully focused

Examples

deep immersion in work
Her immersion in the project was so total that she often forgot to eat.
Language immersion is considered one of the most effective ways to learn a new tongue.
The virtual reality experience provided a sense of complete immersion in an imaginary world.

Related Terms

  • synonym – absorption: deep mental involvement in something
  • antonym – distraction: lack of concentration or focus
