introvert

noun
a person who prefers solitude to socializing
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntrəˌvɜrt / (AmE), / ˈɪntrəˌvɜːt / (BrE)
a person whose personality is characterized by inward focus, preferring solitary activities and often feeling drained by social interaction

Examples

quiet introvert
Although she is an introvert, Maria enjoys spending time with a few close friends.
As an introvert, he finds large parties overwhelming and prefers quiet evenings at home.
Many introverts excel in roles that require deep concentration and minimal social interaction.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – ambivert: a person whose personality has a balance of introvert and extrovert features
  • antonym – extrovert: a person who is outgoing and energized by social interaction
