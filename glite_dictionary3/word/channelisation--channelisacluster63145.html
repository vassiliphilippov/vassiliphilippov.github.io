channelisation

noun
management through specified channels
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃæn.ə.ləˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌtʃæn.ə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of managing information, activities, or resources through specified channels of communication or distribution

Examples

channelisation of information
Efficient channelisation of information is critical to avoiding misunderstandings in large organizations.
Proper channelisation of complaints ensures that issues are addressed quickly by the right department.
The channelisation of funds helped streamline the charity's operations.
