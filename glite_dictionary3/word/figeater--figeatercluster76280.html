figeater

noun
large green beetle of southern US
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡˌiːtər / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡˌiːtə / (BrE)
a large, greenish beetle (Cotinis mutabilis) found in the southern United States, often seen feeding on fruit such as figs and grapes
