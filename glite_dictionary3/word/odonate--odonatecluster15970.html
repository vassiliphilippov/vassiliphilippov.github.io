odonate

noun
predatory aquatic insect; dragonfly or damselfly
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊdəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈəʊdəneɪt / (BrE)
A large predatory aquatic insect with two pairs of membranous wings, belonging to the order Odonata, which includes dragonflies and damselflies.

Examples

colorful odonate flying
Many odonates are indicators of good water quality in their habitats.

The wetland is home to several species of odonate, including both dragonflies and damselflies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – damselfly: a slender-bodied odonate insect with wings held together above the body when at rest
  • synonym – dragonfly: a type of odonate insect with a long body and two pairs of strong transparent wings
