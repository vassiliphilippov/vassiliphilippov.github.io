to escallop

verb
bake food with sauce and breadcrumbs
Pronunciation: / ɪˈskɑl.əp / (AmE), / ɪˈskæl.əp / (BrE)
To bake (food) in a sauce, often with milk, and usually with breadcrumbs on top.

Examples

to escallop potatoes
My grandmother loves to escallop potatoes for Sunday dinners.
The chef escalloped the vegetables, adding a golden breadcrumb topping before baking.
We decided to escallop some fish with a creamy dill sauce.
