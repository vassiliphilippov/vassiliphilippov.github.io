borscht

noun
Eastern European beet soup
Pronunciation: / bɔrʃt / (AmE), / bɔːʃt / (BrE)
A soup of Eastern European origin, made chiefly from beets and often served with sour cream.

Examples

hot borscht soup
In winter, my grandmother always made hot borscht to warm us up.
She served a bowl of borscht garnished with a dollop of sour cream.
The restaurant specializes in traditional dishes like borscht and pierogi.
