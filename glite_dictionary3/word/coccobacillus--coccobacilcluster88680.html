coccobacillus

noun
bacterium intermediate in shape between coccus and bacillus
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑkoʊbəˈsɪləs / (AmE), / ˌkɒkəʊbəˈsɪləs / (BrE)
a bacterium with a shape intermediate between spherical (coccus) and rod-shaped (bacillus); a very short rodlike bacterium
