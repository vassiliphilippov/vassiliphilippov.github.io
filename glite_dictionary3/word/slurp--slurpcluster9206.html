to slurp

verb
eat or drink with a loud noise
Pronunciation: / slɜrp / (AmE), / slɜːp / (BrE)
to eat or drink something with a loud sucking noise

Examples

to slurp soup
He loudly slurped his noodles, drawing curious looks from nearby diners.
Please don't slurp your soup at the table—it's considered rude.
She slurped the milkshake through her straw with obvious enjoyment.
