wriggler

noun
restless, fidgety person (esp. child)
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪɡlər / (AmE), / ˈrɪɡlə / (BrE)
a person, especially a child, who cannot keep still and is always moving or fidgeting

Examples

restless wriggler child
Despite our best efforts, the little wriggler wouldn't sit still for the family photo.
Teachers sometimes find it challenging to manage a classroom with more than one wriggler.
The toddler is such a wriggler during storytime that it's hard to keep him in one place.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fidget: a person who fidgets or cannot keep still
  • synonym – wiggler: a person or thing that wiggles or moves restlessly
