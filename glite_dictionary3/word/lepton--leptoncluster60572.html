lepton

noun
former Greek fractional coin/unit
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛp.tɑn / (AmE), / ˈlɛp.tɒn / (BrE)
A small coin and former fractional currency unit in Greece, with 100 lepta equaling one drachma.
