graphologist

noun
handwriting analysis specialist
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈfɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɡrəˈfɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A specialist who studies handwriting in order to infer a person's character or psychological traits.

Examples

graphologist analyzes handwriting
A graphologist can sometimes detect signs of nervousness in a person's signature.
The graphologist concluded that the writer was confident and outgoing based on the slant of the letters.
The company hired a graphologist to analyze job applicants' handwriting samples.
