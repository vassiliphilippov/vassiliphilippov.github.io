domestic

noun
servant doing household work
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɛstɪk / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪk / (BrE)
a servant employed to do household tasks such as cleaning and cooking

Examples

a domestic worker
After years of working as a domestic, she finally found a job with better pay.
Many large houses in the nineteenth century employed several domestics.
The domestic was responsible for cleaning and preparing meals in the home.
