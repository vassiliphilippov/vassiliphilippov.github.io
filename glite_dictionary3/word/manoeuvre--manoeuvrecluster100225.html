manoeuvre

noun
military training exercise
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːvər / (AmE), / məˈnuːvə / (BrE)
A military training exercise designed to test or demonstrate tactical skills and coordination.

Examples

military manoeuvre
During their annual manoeuvre, the soldiers practiced setting up defensive positions in the field.
Large-scale manoeuvres were held near the border to test military readiness.
The army participated in joint manoeuvres with allied forces last spring.
