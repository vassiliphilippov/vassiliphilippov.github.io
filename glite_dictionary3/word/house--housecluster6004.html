house

noun
people living together; a family or household
Pronunciation: / haʊs / (AmE), / haʊs / (BrE)
A social unit consisting of people who live together, such as a family or household.

Examples

a house of four people
After dinner, the house gathered in the living room to watch television.
He grew up in a house full of laughter and siblings.
The entire house waited eagerly for the news.

  • synonym – household: a group of people, often a family, who live together
