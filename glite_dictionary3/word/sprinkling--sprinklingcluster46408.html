sprinkling

noun
the act of scattering drops of liquid
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of scattering drops of liquid, especially water, over a surface or object

Examples

a gentle sprinkling of water
A gentle sprinkling of rain refreshed the dry earth.
The priest performed a sprinkling of holy water during the ceremony.
There was a sprinkling of water on the leaves after the morning dew.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – dousing: the act of wetting thoroughly
  • synonym – spray: a fine stream of liquid dispersed in small drops
