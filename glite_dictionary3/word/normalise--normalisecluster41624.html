to normalise

verb
make conform to a standard or norm
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːrməlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈnɔːməlaɪz / (BrE)
to make normal or bring into conformity with a standard or norm

Examples

to normalise relations
The two countries agreed to normalise diplomatic relations after years of conflict.
They hope to normalise trade between the regions by removing unnecessary restrictions.
We should normalise the data before analyzing the results.

Related Terms

  • antonym – abnormalize: to make abnormal; to cause to deviate from the normal
  • synonym – regularize: to make regular or conform to rules
  • synonym – standardize: to make things conform to a standard
