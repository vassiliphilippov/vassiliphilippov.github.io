primordium

noun
organ or tissue at earliest developmental stage
Pronunciation: / praɪˈmɔrdiəm / (AmE), / prɪˈmɔːdiəm / (BrE)
an organ or tissue in its earliest stage of development, serving as the foundation for subsequent growth or differentiation

Examples

primordium of a leaf
Damage to the floral primordium can prevent the proper formation of flowers.
