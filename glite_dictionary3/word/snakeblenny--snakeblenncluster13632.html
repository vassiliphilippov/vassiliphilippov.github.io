snakeblenny

noun
a long, slender fish of cold northern waters
Pronunciation: / ˈsneɪkˌblɛni / (AmE), / ˈsneɪkˌblɛni / (BrE)
a small, elongated fish of cold northern waters, especially the Arctic and northern Atlantic, known for its snake-like appearance

Examples

a snakeblenny fish
