Heliozoa

noun
a group of freshwater protozoa
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːliəˈzoʊə /; / ˌhiːli.oʊˈzoʊ.ə / (AmE), / ˌhiːlɪəˈzəʊə /; / ˌhiːlɪəˈzəʊ.ə / (BrE)
a group of mostly freshwater single-celled protozoa with spherical bodies and long, radiating pseudopodia, often called 'sun animalcules'
