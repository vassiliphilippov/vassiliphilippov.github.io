centre

noun
moderate political position or group
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn(t)ər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
a group of people or individuals with moderate political views, neither left-wing nor right-wing; centrists

Examples

the political centre
Debate often focuses on the extremes, while the centre is overlooked.
Many voters in the election identified themselves as members of the centre.
The new policy aims to attract support from the political centre.

Related Terms

  • synonym – centrist: a person with moderate political beliefs; not aligned with the left or right
  • antonym – left: the group or position supporting progressive or socialist policies
  • antonym – right: the group or position supporting conservative policies
