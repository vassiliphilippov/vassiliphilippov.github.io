to lasso

verb
catch with a lasso
Pronunciation: / læˈsoʊ / (AmE), / læˈsəʊ / (BrE)
to catch an animal by throwing a lasso around it

Examples

to lasso a cow
During the show, he lassoed three cattle in less than a minute.
She tried to lasso the wild pony, but it kept dodging the rope.
The cowboy managed to lasso the stray calf before it ran off into the hills.
