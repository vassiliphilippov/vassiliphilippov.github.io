dithyrambic

adjective
in the style of a dithyramb; passionate or wild
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪθɪˈræmbɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɪθɪˈræmbɪk / (BrE)
written or performed in the style of a dithyramb, often marked by passionate, impetuous, or wild expression.
