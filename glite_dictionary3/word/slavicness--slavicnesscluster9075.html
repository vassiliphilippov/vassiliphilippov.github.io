Slavicness

noun
the state of being Slavic
Pronunciation: / ˈslævɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈslævɪknɪs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being Slavic, or possessing characteristics typical of Slavic people or cultures.

Examples

distinct Slavicness in culture
He felt an undeniable Slavicness in the architecture of the village.
Her research explored the Slavicness of folk traditions in Eastern Europe.
Many novels celebrate a deep sense of Slavicness through their language and customs.
