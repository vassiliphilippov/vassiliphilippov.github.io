to display

verb
to show or make visible
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
to show something or make it visible or apparent, especially so it can be seen by others

Examples

to display emotions
He tried not to display his disappointment when he heard the news.
She likes to display her collection of rare stamps in a glass case.
The museum will display the artist's paintings in a special exhibition next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exhibit: to publicly show for people to look at
  • antonym – hide: to prevent from being seen; to conceal
