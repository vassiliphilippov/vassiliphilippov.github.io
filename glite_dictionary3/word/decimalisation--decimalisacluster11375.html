decimalisation

noun
conversion to a decimal system
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛsɪməlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛsɪməlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of converting a system, especially currency or measurement, to a decimal-based system

Examples

decimalisation of currency
