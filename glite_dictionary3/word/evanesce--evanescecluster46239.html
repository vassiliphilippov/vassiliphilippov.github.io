to evanesce

verb
disappear gradually
Pronunciation: / ˌɛvəˈnɛs / (AmE), / ˌiːvəˈnɛs / (BrE)
to disappear gradually; to fade away or vanish slowly

Examples

to evanesce into mist
Her worries seemed to evanesce with each reassuring word he spoke.
Over time, the mist over the lake began to evanesce as the sun rose higher.
The colors in the old painting slowly evanesced, leaving only faint outlines.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fade: to gradually disappear or become less distinct
  • synonym – vanish: to disappear suddenly or completely
