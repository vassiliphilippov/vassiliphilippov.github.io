to shepherd

verb
tend or guide sheep or goats
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛpərd / (AmE), / ˈʃɛpəd / (BrE)
to tend, guide, or look after sheep or goats as a shepherd does

Examples

to shepherd a flock
He used to shepherd his flock across the hillside every morning.
She spent her childhood shepherding sheep with her father.
The villagers have shepherded goats in this region for generations.

  • synonym – herd: to look after animals, especially grazing animals
