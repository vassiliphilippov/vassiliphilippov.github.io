Chinaman

noun
left-arm unorthodox spin delivery in cricket
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪnəˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnəmən / (BrE)
(cricket) a ball bowled by a left-handed bowler to a right-handed batsman that spins from off to leg, typically a left-arm unorthodox spin delivery.

Examples

Chinaman spin bowling
His specialty is the Chinaman, which has puzzled many top batsmen.
Left-arm bowlers often practice the Chinaman to add variety to their attack.
The bowler surprised the batsman with a clever Chinaman that spun sharply.
