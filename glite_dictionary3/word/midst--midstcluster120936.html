midst

noun
the middle part, surrounded by other things
Pronunciation: / mɪdst / (AmE), / mɪdst / (BrE)
the middle part or position of a group, place, or series of things or people, often implying being surrounded by them

Examples

in the midst of friends
He stood in the midst of his friends, smiling broadly.
She found herself in the midst of a heated debate.
The garden was a peaceful spot in the midst of the busy city.
