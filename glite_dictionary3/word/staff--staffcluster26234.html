to staff

verb
provide with employees or workers
Pronunciation: / stæf / (AmE), / stɑːf / (BrE)
to provide workers for a place or to work as an employee in a place

Examples

to staff a store
During the holidays, extra workers are staffed at the customer service desk.
Sometimes, smaller branches are staffed by only one or two people.
The hospital is well staffed with experienced nurses and doctors.
A team of security guards staffs the building 24 hours a day.
During the holidays, extra employees are hired to staff the customer service counters.
Our volunteers staff the help desk every Saturday morning.
