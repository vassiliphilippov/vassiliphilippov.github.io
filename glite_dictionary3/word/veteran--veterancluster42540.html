veteran

noun
an experienced or long-serving person
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛt̬.ɚ.ən / (AmE), / ˈvɛt.ər.ən / (BrE)
a person with long experience in a particular activity or field, especially one who has served in the armed forces

Examples

veteran teacher
As a veteran teacher, she is respected by her colleagues for her deep experience.
The job requires a veteran in project management to handle its complexity.
The retired general is a veteran of many campaigns.

Related Terms

  • synonym – expert: a person who is highly skilled or knowledgeable in a particular area
  • antonym – novice: a person new to a field or activity; a beginner
