soft

adjective
mild and pleasant (esp. weather, sounds, light)
Pronunciation: / sɔft / (AmE), / sɒft / (BrE)
(of weather, sounds, light, etc.) mild and pleasant; not harsh or intense.

Examples

soft breeze
A soft breeze drifted through the open window, making the curtains flutter.
The soft light at sunrise made everything look calm and inviting.
We enjoyed a soft spring morning, perfect for sitting outside with coffee.
