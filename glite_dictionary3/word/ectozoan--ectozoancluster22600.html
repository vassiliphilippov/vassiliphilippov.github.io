ectozoan

adjective
relating to organisms living on animals' surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.təˈzoʊ.ən / (AmE), / ˌɛk.təˈzəʊ.ən / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of epizoa; living on the external surface of an animal.

Examples

ectozoan species found
Researchers identified several ectozoan species living on the whale's skin.
Some parasites are strictly ectozoan and never invade the host's internal tissues.
The fish was infested with ectozoan creatures clinging to its scales.

  • synonym – epizoic: Living on the exterior of an animal
