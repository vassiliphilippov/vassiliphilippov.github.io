terminally

adverb
in the final stage; at the end
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.mə.nəl.i / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.mɪ.nəl.i / (BrE)
In a way that relates to an end or a final stage, especially of an illness or process.

Examples

terminally ill patient
His injuries were terminally severe after the accident.
She was terminally bored by the endless meeting.
The patient was diagnosed as terminally ill with cancer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fatally: in a way that causes death
  • antonym – temporarily: for a limited period of time
