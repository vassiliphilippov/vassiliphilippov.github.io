toast

noun
celebrated person, center of admiration
Pronunciation: / toʊst / (AmE), / təʊst / (BrE)
A person who is celebrated and receives much acclaim and attention, especially in a particular place or group.

Examples

toast of the town
After her performance on Broadway, she became the toast of the theater world.
He was the toast of the party, with everyone eager to meet him.
The young entrepreneur is now the toast of Silicon Valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym – celebrity: a famous person, especially in entertainment or sports
  • synonym – idol: a person who is greatly admired or revered
