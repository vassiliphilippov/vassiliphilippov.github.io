to miter

verb
to fit pieces together at an angle (miter joint)
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪtɚ /; / ˈmaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪtə / (BrE)
to cut or join two pieces (usually wood or metal) at an angle so they fit together to form a miter joint
