Cucumis melo inodorus

noun
group of muskmelon varieties with non-musky aroma
Pronunciation: / kjuːˈkjuːmɪs ˈmiːloʊ ɪˈnɑːdərəs / (AmE), / kjuːˈkjuːmɪs ˈmiːləʊ ɪˈnɒdərəs / (BrE)
A group of muskmelon varieties having smooth, white rinds and white or greenish flesh that lacks the typical musky aroma.
