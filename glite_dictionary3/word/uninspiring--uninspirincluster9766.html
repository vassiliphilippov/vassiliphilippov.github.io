uninspiring

adjective
not exciting or interesting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈspaɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈspaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
Not causing or deserving enthusiasm or interest; dull or unexciting.

Examples

an uninspiring speech
She gave an uninspiring speech that failed to motivate the audience.
The movie's plot was so uninspiring that I lost interest halfway through.
The office was filled with dull, uninspiring decor.
