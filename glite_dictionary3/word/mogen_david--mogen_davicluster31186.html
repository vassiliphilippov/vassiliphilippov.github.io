Mogen David

noun
six-pointed star; symbol of Judaism
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊɡən dəˈviːd / (AmE), / ˈməʊɡən dəˈviːd / (BrE)
A six-pointed star formed by two interlaced equilateral triangles, used as a symbol of Judaism; also known as the Star of David.
