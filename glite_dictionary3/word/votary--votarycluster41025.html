votary

noun
person bound by vows, often religious
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊ.tə.ri /; / ˈvoʊtəˌri / (AmE), / ˈvəʊ.təri /; / ˈvəʊtəɹi / (BrE)
a person who is devoted to a religion or worship, often bound by vows or participating in rituals
