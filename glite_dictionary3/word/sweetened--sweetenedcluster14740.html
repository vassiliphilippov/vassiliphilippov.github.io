sweetened

adjective
with added sugar or sweetener
Pronunciation: / ˈswiːtənd / (AmE), / ˈswiːtənd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) having had sugar or a sweetener added to make it taste sweet.

Examples

sweetened drink
Always check if the juice is sweetened or contains added sugars.
She prefers her tea sweetened with honey instead of sugar.
The sweetened yogurt was more popular among children.
