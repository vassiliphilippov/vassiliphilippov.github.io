scope

noun
electronic instrument for visualizing electrical signals
Pronunciation: / skoʊp / (AmE), / skəʊp / (BrE)
a device or instrument used to see, magnify, or measure things, such as a telescope, rifle scope, or electronic measuring tool

Examples

digital scope
Modern laboratories often have at least one digital scope for troubleshooting electronic equipment.
She adjusted the scope to get a clearer view of the waveform on the screen.
The technician used a scope to check the signal output from the circuit.
Astronomers use a powerful scope to observe distant planets.
She adjusted the scope on her rifle to improve her aim.
The hunter peered through the scope to spot deer on the far hill.

  • synonym – oscilloscope: an instrument for displaying, measuring, and analyzing electronic signals
