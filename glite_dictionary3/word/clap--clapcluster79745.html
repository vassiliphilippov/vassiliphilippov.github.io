clap

noun
a sudden loud noise
Pronunciation: / klæp / (AmE), / klæp / (BrE)
a sudden, sharp, or loud noise, especially one made by thunder, an explosion, or two objects striking each other

Examples

clap of thunder
A deafening clap of thunder shook the house during the storm.
The fireworks ended with a final, echoing clap that startled everyone.
We jumped in surprise at the sudden clap outside the window.
A sudden clap of thunder startled everyone at the picnic.
She could hear the clap of the judge's gavel echo in the courtroom.
There was a loud clap as the two pieces of wood struck together.
