preventative

adjective
serving to prevent or hinder
Pronunciation: / prɪˈvɛntətɪv / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛntətɪv / (BrE)
intended to stop something undesirable, especially disease or harm, from happening

Examples

preventative health care
Regular exercise can be a preventative measure against many health problems.
The clinic offers preventative services such as vaccinations and screenings.
Wearing sunscreen is a preventative step to avoid sunburn and skin damage.
Applying sunscreen is a simple preventative measure against skin cancer.
Many doctors recommend regular preventative screenings to detect diseases early.
The hospital invests heavily in preventative healthcare programs for the community.
