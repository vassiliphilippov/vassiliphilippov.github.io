foil

noun
light fencing sword
Pronunciation: / fɔɪl / (AmE), / fɔɪl / (BrE)
a light, thin, flexible sword with a blunted tip, used in the sport of fencing

Examples

fencing foil sword
He won the gold medal in the men's foil event at the Olympics.
She practiced her thrusts and parries with her fencing foil for an hour every day.
The foil is lighter and more flexible than the épée.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sabre: a fencing sword with a curved blade, used in a different discipline
  • synonym – épée: a heavier fencing sword with a stiffer blade and a larger guard
