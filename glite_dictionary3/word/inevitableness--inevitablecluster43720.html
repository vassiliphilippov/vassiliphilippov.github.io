inevitableness

noun
the quality of being unavoidable
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛvɪtəblnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛvɪtəblnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unavoidable or certain to happen

Examples

the inevitableness of change
He accepted the inevitableness of defeat with quiet dignity.
Some people are frightened by the inevitableness of aging and time passing.
The inevitableness of change is a theme found in many philosophical works.
