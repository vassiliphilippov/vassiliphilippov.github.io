to plaster

verb
cover a wound with a therapeutic substance
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstə / (BrE)
to cover (a wound or sore) with a therapeutic substance or medical dressing

Examples

to plaster a wound
After the fall, he plastered ointment onto his scraped knee.
Old remedies often involved plastering herbal pastes on injuries.
The nurse quickly plastered the wound to prevent infection.
