hopefully

adverb
expressing hope for something
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊpfəli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊpfəli / (BrE)
used to express the hope that something will happen or be the case

Examples

Hopefully the rain stops
Hopefully the rain will stop before the ceremony starts.
Hopefully, we can resolve this issue by tomorrow morning.
She looked at the test results hopefully, wishing for good news.

Related Terms

  • synonym – perhaps: possibly, but not certainly
  • antonym – unfortunately: used to introduce a disappointing or regrettable fact
