tagalong

noun
person who follows others persistently
Pronunciation: / ˈtæɡ.əˌlɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæɡ.əˌlɒŋ / (BrE)
a person who persistently follows or accompanies others, often in an unwanted or annoying way

Examples

Sarah didn't mind her little brother at first, but after a while he became a real tagalong.
The group grew tired of the tagalong who kept interrupting their private conversations.
