sorehead

noun
someone easily annoyed or resentful
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who is easily annoyed or resentful, especially over petty things or perceived slights

Examples

a sorehead after losing
After losing the argument, he acted like a real sorehead and left in a huff.
Don't be such a sorehead; it's just a game and everyone is trying to have fun.
His coworkers try to avoid the sorehead in the office, as he's always complaining about something.

  • synonym – grump: someone who is habitually irritable or complaining
  • synonym – sore loser: someone who complains or sulks after losing
