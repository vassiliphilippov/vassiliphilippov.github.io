caulk

noun
waterproof filler or sealant for joints
Pronunciation: / kɑk / (AmE), / kɔːk / (BrE)
a waterproof material used to fill and seal joints or seams in buildings or other structures to prevent leakage

Examples

caulk for windows
Before painting, make sure to fill any cracks with caulk for a smooth finish.
The plumber applied caulk around the bathtub to prevent water from seeping into the wall.
We used a silicone caulk to seal the window frames during the renovation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sealant: a substance used to block the passage of fluids through the surface or joints
