transcription

noun
copying DNA into mRNA (genetics)
Pronunciation: / trænˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / trænˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
(Genetics) The process by which the genetic information in a DNA sequence is copied into messenger RNA (mRNA).
