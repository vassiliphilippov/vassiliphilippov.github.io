reminder

noun
something that prompts remembering
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmaɪndər /; / ɹɪˈmaɪndɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈmaɪndə /; / ɹɪˈmaɪndə / (BrE)
something (such as a note, message, event, or object) that helps you remember to do something or not forget
