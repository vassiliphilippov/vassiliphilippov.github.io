amortisation

noun
gradual repayment of a debt
Pronunciation: / əˌmɔːrtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌmɔːtɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of gradually reducing a debt or the value of an asset by making regular payments or spreading costs over a period of time
