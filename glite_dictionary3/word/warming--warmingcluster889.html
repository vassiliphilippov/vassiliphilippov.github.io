warming

adjective
producing heat sensation on body
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel warm or comfortable, either by producing heat or giving a sense of warmth

Examples

warming pad
He applied a warming pad to his back to relieve the pain.
Some ointments use chili extracts for their warming properties.
This cream has a warming effect on sore muscles and helps ease tension.
A cup of warming tea can be very comforting in winter.
She sat by a warming fire after her walk in the snow.
The warming soup was just what I needed on a cold evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym – calefacient: producing warmth when applied to the body
  • antonym – cooling: causing something to become cooler
