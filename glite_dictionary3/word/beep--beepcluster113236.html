to beep

verb
call or alert by pager
Pronunciation: / bip / (AmE), / biːp / (BrE)
to call, summon, or alert someone by sending a signal from a beeper or pager

Examples

to beep a doctor
Doctors were often beeped in the middle of the night for emergencies.
He beeped his assistant when he arrived at the airport.
If you need me during the meeting, just beep me.

  • synonym – page: to contact someone via a pager or public announcement
