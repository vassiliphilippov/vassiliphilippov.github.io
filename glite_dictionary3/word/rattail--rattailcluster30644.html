rattail

noun
deep-sea fish with tapering tail
Pronunciation: / ˈræˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈræt.eɪl / (BrE)
A deep-sea fish with a large head, a tapering body, and a long, slender tail, also known as grenadier.

Examples

rattail fish species
Scientists study the rattail to learn about life in the ocean's depths.
