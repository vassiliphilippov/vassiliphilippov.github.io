spike

noun
sharp-pointed holding device
Pronunciation: / spaɪk / (AmE), / spaɪk / (BrE)
a holding device consisting of a rigid, sharp-pointed object that holds items when they are impaled on it

Examples

paper spike
She impaled the order slip onto the spike so it wouldn’t get lost.
The desk spike was full of discarded notes and messages.
The receipts were collected on a spike next to the cash register.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spindle: a thin, pointed rod used for holding papers
