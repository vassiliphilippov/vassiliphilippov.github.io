hypovolemic

adjective
relating to decreased blood volume
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊvəˈlimɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəʊvəˈliːmɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or caused by a decrease in the volume of circulating blood in the body.

Examples

hypovolemic shock
A hypovolemic state can lead to shock if not treated promptly.
Physicians monitor fluid levels carefully to prevent hypovolemic complications after surgery.
The patient was diagnosed as hypovolemic following severe blood loss after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hypovolemia: an abnormally low volume of blood in the body
