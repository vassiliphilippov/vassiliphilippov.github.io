pre-eminently

adverb
with superiority or distinction
Pronunciation: / priˈɛmɪnəntli / (AmE), / priˈɛmɪnəntli / (BrE)
To a degree that is superior to all others; in a way that shows distinction or prominence above others.

Examples

pre-eminently talented leader
She is pre-eminently qualified for the position, possessing experience far beyond her peers.

This novel stands out as pre-eminently original among the author's works.
