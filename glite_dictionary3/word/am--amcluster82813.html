A.M.

adverb
before noon (time)
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˈɛm / (BrE)
used to refer to the time between midnight and noon; before 12 o'clock in the day.

Examples

at 7 A.M. sharp
My train leaves at 7 A.M. tomorrow morning.
She usually has her first cup of coffee by 8 A.M..
The meeting is scheduled for 10 A.M. sharp, so please be on time.
