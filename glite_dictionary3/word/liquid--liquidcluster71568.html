liquid

adjective
clear and bright (often of eyes, air, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd / (BrE)
(especially of eyes, voice, or air) clear, bright, and shining, often with a smooth or flowing quality.

Examples

liquid light
Early morning brought a liquid clarity to the mountain air.
She looked at him with liquid brown eyes that sparkled in the sunlight.
The singer's liquid soprano voice captivated the entire audience.
