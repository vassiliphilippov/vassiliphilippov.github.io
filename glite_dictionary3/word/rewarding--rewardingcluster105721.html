rewarding

adjective
giving satisfaction or fulfillment
Pronunciation: / rɪˈwɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈwɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an activity or experience) giving you satisfaction, pleasure, or a sense of achievement.

Examples

a rewarding experience
Completing the marathon was physically demanding but deeply rewarding.
Many people choose teaching because they consider it a rewarding career.
She found working with children to be a truly rewarding experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fulfilling: making you feel happy and satisfied because you are doing something important or useful
  • antonym – unsatisfying: not giving satisfaction or pleasure
