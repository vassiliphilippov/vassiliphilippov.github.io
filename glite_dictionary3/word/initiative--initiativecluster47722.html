initiative

noun
first step in a process
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɪʃətɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈnɪʃətɪv / (BrE)
the first step or action in a series that sets something in motion

Examples

launch an initiative
Her suggestion was the initiative that spurred a series of important reforms.
Investment in education was the government's first initiative after the election.
The organization launched a new initiative to promote sustainable farming in the region.
