dissimilar

adjective
not similar; different
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɪmələr / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪmɪlə / (BrE)
not like each other; different in appearance, nature, or quality

Examples

dissimilar opinions
Her views on education are quite dissimilar from those of her colleagues.
The results from the two experiments were surprisingly dissimilar.
The two cultures have dissimilar traditions and customs.
The twins had dissimilar interests and pursued completely different careers.
The two paintings are strikingly dissimilar in style and composition.
Their houses are so dissimilar that you wouldn't guess they're on the same street.
