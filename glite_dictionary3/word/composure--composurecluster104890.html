composure

noun
calmness and self-control
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpoʊʒər / (AmE), / kəmˈpəʊʒə / (BrE)
The state of being calm and in control of oneself, especially in a difficult situation.

Examples

keep composure under pressure
Despite the chaos around her, she maintained her composure and calmly explained the situation.
During the heated debate, his composure set him apart from the other participants.
She lost her composure briefly when she heard the unexpected news.

Related Terms

  • antonym – agitation: a state of anxiety or nervous excitement
  • synonym – equanimity: mental calmness and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation
