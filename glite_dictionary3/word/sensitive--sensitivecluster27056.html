sensitive

adjective
relating to classified or security matters
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsətɪv / (BrE)
Relating to classified information or matters affecting national security, requiring protection from unauthorized disclosure.

Examples

sensitive government documents
Access to sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel only.
Leaks of sensitive data could threaten public safety and national interests.
The committee is responsible for reviewing sensitive national security documents.
