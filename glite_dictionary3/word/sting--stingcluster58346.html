to sting

verb
to impose a disadvantage or charge on someone
Pronunciation: / stɪŋ / (AmE), / stɪŋ / (BrE)
to cause someone to suffer a financial or other disadvantage, often unexpectedly or unfairly

Examples

to sting a customer
I felt cheated when the company stung me with hidden charges.
She got stung with a hefty late payment penalty.
The hotel stung us with additional fees at checkout.
