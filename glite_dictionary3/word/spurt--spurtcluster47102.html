spurt

noun
sudden discharge or gush
Pronunciation: / spɝt / (AmE), / spɜːt / (BrE)
a sudden, forceful discharge or gush of liquid or other substance

Examples

spurt of water
A spurt of water shot out when he turned on the hose.
Blood emerged in a brief spurt from the small wound.
The ketchup came out in a sudden spurt and splattered everywhere.
