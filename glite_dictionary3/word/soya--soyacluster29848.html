soya

noun
protein-rich food from soybeans
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
A protein-rich food product made from soybeans, widely used as an alternative to animal protein in foods.

Examples

soya milk drink
Soya milk is a popular alternative to dairy milk for people with lactose intolerance.
Many vegetarians use soya as a substitute for meat in their diets.
The farm produces large quantities of soya each year for export.

Related Terms

  • synonym – soybean: the bean from which soya products are derived
