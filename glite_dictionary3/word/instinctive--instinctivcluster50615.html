instinctive

adjective
done automatically without conscious thought
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɪŋktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɪŋktɪv / (BrE)
(of a feeling or action) done automatically and without conscious thought, as if prompted by instinct.

Examples

instinctive reaction
He gave an instinctive smile when he saw an old friend approaching.
Her instinctive reaction was to shield her child from harm.
It was instinctive for her to reach out and catch the falling glass.

Related Terms

  • synonym – automatic: (of an action) done unconsciously or by habit
  • antonym – deliberate: done consciously and intentionally
