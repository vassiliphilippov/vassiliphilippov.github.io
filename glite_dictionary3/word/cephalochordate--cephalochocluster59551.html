cephalochordate

noun
marine animal with a notochord
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛfəloʊˈkɔrˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɛfələʊˈkɔːdeɪt / (BrE)
A small, fish-like marine animal of the subphylum Cephalochordata, having a notochord instead of a true spinal column.

Examples

cephalochordate species
Lancelets are a type of cephalochordate found in shallow marine environments.
Scientists study cephalochordates to understand early vertebrate evolution.
The notochord in a cephalochordate runs the entire length of its body.
