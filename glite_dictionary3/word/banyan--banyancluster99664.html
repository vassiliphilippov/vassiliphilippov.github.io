banyan

noun
East Indian tree with aerial roots; Ficus benghalensis
Pronunciation: / ˈbæn.jən / (AmE), / ˈbæn.jən / (BrE)
A large East Indian fig tree (Ficus benghalensis) that produces aerial roots which grow down into the soil to form additional trunks.

Examples

giant banyan tree
A banyan can spread across a large area as its aerial roots become new trunks.
Many pilgrims gathered under the ancient banyan for shelter during the monsoon.
The enormous banyan tree in the village square provides shade for everyone.
