technical

adjective
related to practical/scientific subjects
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl /; / ˈtɛknɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛk.nɪ.kəl /; / ˈtɛknɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to specialized knowledge, skills, or practical application, especially in science, engineering, technology, or requiring specific expertise; also, conforming to precise rules or involving technical analysis in finance
