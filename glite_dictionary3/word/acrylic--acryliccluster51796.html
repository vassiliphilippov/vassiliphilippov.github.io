acrylic

noun
synthetic resin or fiber
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɪlɪk / (AmE), / əˈkrɪlɪk / (BrE)
a synthetic material (resin, plastic, or fiber) made from acrylic compounds, used in paints, plastics, coatings, and adhesives
