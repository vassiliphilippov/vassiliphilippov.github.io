sloped

adjective
inclined or slanting in direction
Pronunciation: / sloʊpt / (AmE), / sləʊpt / (BrE)
(of a surface or line) having a slanting or inclined direction, not level or straight.

Examples

sloped surface
Place the painting on a sloped surface for a better viewing angle.
The roof was sloped to allow rainwater to runoff easily.
They struggled up the sloped path leading to the cottage.

Related Terms

  • antonym – level: having a flat, even surface; not sloped
  • synonym – sloping: inclined from a horizontal or vertical position; slanting
