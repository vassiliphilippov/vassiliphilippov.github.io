rhinoscope

noun
instrument for examining nasal passages
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪnəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈraɪnəskəʊp / (BrE)
a medical instrument with a mirror, designed to examine the nasal passages and nasopharynx

Examples

use a rhinoscope for examination
A rhinoscope is essential for thorough ear, nose, and throat examinations.
The doctor inserted a rhinoscope to get a clear view of the patient's nasal cavity.
Using a rhinoscope, the specialist was able to diagnose a blockage deep in the nose.
