to inflect

verb
change the form of a word grammatically
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɛkt / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɛkt / (BrE)
(of a word) to change its form according to grammatical rules, such as for tense, case, or number.

Examples

inflect a verb form
In English, nouns rarely inflect for gender.
Some languages inflect almost every word depending on its grammatical function.
When you inflect a verb in Spanish, you must match the subject.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conjugate: to inflect a verb, especially to indicate tense, person, or number
  • synonym – decline: to inflect a noun, pronoun, or adjective, especially for case, number, or gender
