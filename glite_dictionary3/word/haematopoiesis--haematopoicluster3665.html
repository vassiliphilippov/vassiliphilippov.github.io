haematopoiesis

noun
formation of blood cells
Pronunciation: / hiˌmætəpoʊˈiːsɪs / (AmE), / hiːˌmatəʊpɔɪˈiːsɪs / (BrE)
The process by which blood cells are formed in the living body, especially in the bone marrow.
