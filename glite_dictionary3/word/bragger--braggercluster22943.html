bragger

noun
boastful person
Pronunciation: / ˈbræɡər / (AmE), / ˈbræɡə / (BrE)
A person who boasts or talks proudly about their achievements, possessions, or abilities, often excessively.

Examples

loud bragger
Despite his talent, he managed not to become a bragger about his achievements.
No one likes a bragger who constantly talks about their own success.
She was called a bragger after bragging about her promotion to everyone in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boaster: a person who boasts
  • antonym – modest person: a person who does not boast or show off
