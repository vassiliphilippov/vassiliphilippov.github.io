Vayu

proper noun
Hindu god of wind
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːjuː / (AmE), / ˈvɑːjuː / (BrE)
In Hinduism, the god of wind, regarded as an important deity in the Vedic tradition.

Vayu the wind god
