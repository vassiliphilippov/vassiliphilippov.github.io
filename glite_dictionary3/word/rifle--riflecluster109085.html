rifle

noun
long-barreled shoulder firearm
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəl / (BrE)
a gun with a long barrel and a spiral-grooved bore, designed to be fired from the shoulder

Examples

hunting rifle
He cleaned his rifle carefully after returning from the hunt.
She learned to shoot a rifle at the firing range.
The soldier slung his rifle over his shoulder as he marched.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gun: a weapon that shoots bullets or other projectiles
  • antonym – pistol: a small firearm designed to be held in one hand
