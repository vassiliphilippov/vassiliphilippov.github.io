discharge

noun
release or pouring out of a fluid
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒ /; / ˈdɪs.tʃɑrdʒ / (AmE), / dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒ /; / ˈdɪs.tʃɑːdʒ / (BrE)
a substance or material that is released or comes out from something, especially as a liquid, gas, or waste
