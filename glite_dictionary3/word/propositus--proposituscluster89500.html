propositus

noun
person chiefly concerned in a case or event
Pronunciation: / proʊˈpɑzɪtəs / (AmE), / prəʊˈpɒzɪtəs / (BrE)
the person immediately affected by or central to a particular action, event, or case, especially in law or genetics

Examples

the propositus in a family
