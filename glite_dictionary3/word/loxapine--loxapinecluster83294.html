loxapine

noun
antipsychotic drug for schizophrenia
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑksəˌpiːn / (AmE), / ˈlɒksəpiːn / (BrE)
A tranquilizer used, especially under the trade name Loxitane, to treat schizophrenia and other psychiatric disorders.
