ridiculously

adverb
so as to seem absurd or laughable
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdɪkjʊləsli / (AmE), / rɪˈdɪkjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely unreasonable, absurd, or deserving of laughter.

Examples

ridiculously expensive price
He arrived ridiculously early for the interview, nearly an hour before it started.
The cake was ridiculously large for such a small birthday party.
The price of the designer handbag was ridiculously high, yet people still lined up to buy it.

Related Terms

  • synonym – absurdly: in a ridiculous or unreasonable way
  • antonym – seriously: in a sincere or earnest way, opposite of laughably
