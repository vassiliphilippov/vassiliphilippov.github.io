nominal

adjective
named for a specific person
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːmɪnəl /; / ˈnɑː.mə.nəl / (AmE), / ˈnɒmɪnəl /; / ˈnɒm.ɪ.nəl / (BrE)
existing in name only, or named but not real or actual; having a title or status without the real power or substance
