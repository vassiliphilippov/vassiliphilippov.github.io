hand-held

adjective
small enough to be used in the hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhænd hɛld / (AmE), / ˈhænd hɛld / (BrE)
(of a device or object) small and light enough to be operated while held in the hand.

Examples

hand-held device
A hand-held mixer is perfect for small baking tasks.
He bought a hand-held GPS unit for hiking.
Using a hand-held scanner makes digitizing receipts much quicker.

Related Terms

  • synonym – portable: Able to be easily carried or moved, especially because of being a lighter and smaller version than usual.
