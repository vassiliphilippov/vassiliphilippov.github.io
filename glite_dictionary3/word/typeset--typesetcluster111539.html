to typeset

verb
prepare text for printing
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪpˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌsɛt / (BrE)
to arrange text and images on a page in preparation for printing or publishing, usually using a particular font, size, and layout

Examples

to typeset a book
He typeset the entire document himself using traditional methods.
Her first novel was professionally typeset before publication.
The magazine is typeset using a modern digital process.
