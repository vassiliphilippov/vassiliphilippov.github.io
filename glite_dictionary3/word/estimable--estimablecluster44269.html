estimable

adjective
able to be estimated or computed
Pronunciation: / ˈɛstɪməbəl / (AmE), / ˈɛstɪməb(ə)l / (BrE)
able to be estimated or calculated; possible to compute or assess the value, amount, or extent of something

Examples

estimable amount
Although the exact impact is uncertain, the potential costs are estimable within a reasonable range.
The company's total assets are estimable based on its most recent financial statements.
The risks involved in the project are not precisely known but are generally estimable.
