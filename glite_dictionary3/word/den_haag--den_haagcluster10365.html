Den Haag

proper noun
city in the Netherlands; seat of government
Pronunciation: / dɛn ˈhɑx / (AmE), / dɛn ˈhɑːx / (BrE)
The Dutch name for The Hague, the seat of government and royal residence in the Netherlands, and home to the International Court of Justice.
