KGB

proper noun
Soviet Union security and intelligence agency
Pronunciation: / keɪ dʒiː ˈbiː / (AmE), / keɪ dʒiː ˈbiː / (BrE)
the main security and intelligence agency of the Soviet Union, responsible for state security, intelligence, and secret police activities until 1991
