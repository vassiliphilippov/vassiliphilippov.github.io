imaging

noun
medical process of producing internal body pictures
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
The process of producing pictures of the interior of the body, especially for medical examination or diagnosis.

Examples

medical imaging scan
Advances in medical imaging have improved the early detection of many diseases.
The doctor ordered magnetic resonance imaging to get a clearer view of the brain.
Ultrasound imaging is frequently used during pregnancy to monitor fetal development.

Related Terms

  • synonym – X-ray: a type of imaging using X-rays to view inside the body
  • synonym – scanning: the act of using medical equipment to produce images inside the body
