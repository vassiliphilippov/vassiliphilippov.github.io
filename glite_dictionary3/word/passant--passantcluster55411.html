passant

adjective
heraldic: walking with right foreleg raised
Pronunciation: / pəˈsænt / (AmE), / pəˈsɑːnt / (BrE)
(Heraldry) depicted in a walking position with the right foreleg raised and the other three on the ground, used especially for animals on coats of arms.

Examples

lion passant on shield
A heraldic leopard passant appears on the shield, denoting vigilance and bravery.
In the family crest, the lion is shown passant, symbolizing courage in action.
The arms display three boars passant on a silver field.
