rockabilly

noun
1950s-inspired subculture and aesthetic
Pronunciation: / \u02c8r\u0251\u02d0k.\u0259\u02ccb\u026al.i / (AmE), / \u02c8r\u0252k.\u0259\u02ccb\u026al.i / (BrE)
a revivalist subculture and retro aesthetic inspired by 1950s rock and roll and country music, especially evident in vintage clothing, hairstyles, dancing, and events.
