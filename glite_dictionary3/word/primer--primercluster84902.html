primer

noun
cosmetic base applied before makeup
Pronunciation: / c8pra6am59r / (AmE), / c8pr6am59 / (BrE)
a cosmetic product applied to the skin before makeup to create a smoother surface, reduce shine, and help foundation and other cosmetics last longer.

Examples

primer under makeup
She applied a silicone-based primer before the foundation for a flawless look.
The makeup artist recommended a mattifying primer to reduce shine under stage lights.
At the counter, he asked which primer would work best for acne-prone skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – makeup base: a product applied before foundation to create an even surface and improve makeup wear
