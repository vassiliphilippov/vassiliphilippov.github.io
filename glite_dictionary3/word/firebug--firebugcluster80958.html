firebug

noun
brightly colored insect, especially Pyrrhocoris apterus
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəbʌɡ / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored bug, especially Pyrrhocoris apterus, often found in warm regions and known for its vivid red and black markings

Examples

firebug on a leaf
A firebug can be recognized by its striking red and black body.
