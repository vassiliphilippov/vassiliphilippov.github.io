impersonal

adjective
not influenced by personal feelings
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɜrsənəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɜːsənəl / (BrE)
not influenced by personal feelings or opinions; neutral or unbiased

Examples

impersonal tone
An impersonal tone is expected in most academic writing.
She tried to remain impersonal during the debate to avoid offending anyone.
The manager gave impersonal feedback, focusing only on the facts rather than emotions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – biased: prejudiced for or against something
  • synonym – neutral: not supporting either side; impartial
