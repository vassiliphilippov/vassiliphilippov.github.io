frijolillo

noun
poisonous shrub with blue flowers and red beans
Pronunciation: / fri.hoʊˈli.joʊ / (AmE), / fri.hɒˈliː.jəʊ / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree with pinnate leaves that are poisonous to livestock, bearing dense clusters of fragrant blue flowers and red beans.
