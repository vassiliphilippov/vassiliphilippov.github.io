to pay

verb
bear consequences for an action
Pronunciation: / peɪ / (AmE), / peɪ / (BrE)
to suffer the consequences or bear a penalty for an action, especially as punishment or retribution

Examples

pay the price
He cheated on the exam and now he must pay the price.
If you break the law, you will have to pay for your actions.
She knew she would pay for her mistakes eventually.

Related Terms

  • synonym – atone: to make amends for a wrongdoing or error
  • synonym – suffer: to experience something unpleasant as a result of an action
