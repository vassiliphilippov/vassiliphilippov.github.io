bullfighting

noun
the spectacle of fighting bulls for entertainment
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌfaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌfaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The traditional spectacle in which a person fights and often kills a bull as a public entertainment, especially popular in Spain and some Latin American countries.

Examples

bullfighting in Spain
Bullfighting is considered a traditional art form in Spain.
Many tourists visit Spain to witness the spectacle of bullfighting during festival seasons.
The debate over whether bullfighting should be banned continues to divide opinions.
