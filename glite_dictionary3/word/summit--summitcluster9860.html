summit

noun
meeting of heads of governments
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌm.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌm.ɪt / (BrE)
an official meeting or conference attended by the leaders of governments, especially to discuss important issues

Examples

summit meeting of leaders
Next month’s peace summit will bring together presidents from across the continent.
The summit ended with an agreement on climate action between the countries.
World leaders gathered at the G7 summit to discuss the global economy.

  • synonym – conference: a formal meeting for discussion
  • synonym – summitry: the practice of holding summits
