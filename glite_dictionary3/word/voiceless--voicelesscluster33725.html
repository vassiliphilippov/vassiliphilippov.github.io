voiceless

adjective
lacking vocal cord vibration (phonetics)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪs.ləs / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪs.ləs /; / ˈvɔɪs.lɪs / (BrE)
(of a sound, especially in speech) produced without vibration of the vocal cords
