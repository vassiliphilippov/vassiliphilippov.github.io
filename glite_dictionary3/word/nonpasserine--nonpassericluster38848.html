nonpasserine

adjective
not a perching bird (non-passerine)
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpæsəˌraɪn / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpæsəraɪn / (BrE)
relating to birds that are not perching birds, especially those not belonging to the order Passeriformes

nonpasserine bird species
Owls, penguins, and hummingbirds are considered nonpasserine species within the bird classification system.

Unlike most songbirds, eagles are nonpasserine and have different anatomical features.
