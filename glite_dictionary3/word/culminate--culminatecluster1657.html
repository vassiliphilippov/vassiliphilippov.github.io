to culminate

verb
rise to or form a summit
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlmɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlmɪneɪt / (BrE)
(of an object or structure) to rise to or form a highest point or summit.

Examples

culminate in a point
Her elaborate hairstyle culminated in a dramatic twist at the crown.
The ancient temple culminates in a magnificent dome.
The mountain range culminates in a snow-capped peak.
