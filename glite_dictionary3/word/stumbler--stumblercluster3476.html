stumbler

noun
person who trips or nearly falls
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmblər / (AmE), / ˈstʌmblə / (BrE)
a person who trips or nearly falls while walking or running

Examples

clumsy stumbler
As he clumsily shuffled through the forest, he was the very image of a stumbler.
Every school trip seems to have at least one stumbler trailing behind the group.
The marathon's rocky path turned even seasoned athletes into stumblers.
