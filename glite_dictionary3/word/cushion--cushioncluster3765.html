cushion

noun
layer of air under a hovercraft
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃən / (BrE)
a device or layer that absorbs shocks or reduces impact, such as in machinery, vehicles, or as a layer of air

Examples

air cushion layer
A thin cushion beneath the vehicle allows it to traverse both land and water.
