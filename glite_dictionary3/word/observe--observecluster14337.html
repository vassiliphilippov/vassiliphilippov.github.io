to observe

verb
celebrate or honor a holiday or rite
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝv / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːv / (BrE)
to celebrate or honor (a holiday, festival, or religious rite) by following customary practices or ceremonies

Examples

to observe a holiday
Jews around the world observe Passover with ritual meals and readings.
Many families observe Thanksgiving by gathering for a special meal.
We observe Independence Day each year with fireworks and parades.

Related Terms

  • synonym – celebrate: to acknowledge an occasion with festivities or ceremonies
  • antonym – ignore: to pay no attention to; disregard customs or expected rites
