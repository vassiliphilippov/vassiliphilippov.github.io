metatarsal

adjective
relating to the metatarsus (foot bones)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈtɑrsəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈtɑːsəl / (BrE)
of or relating to the metatarsus, the group of bones in the foot between the ankle and the toes.
