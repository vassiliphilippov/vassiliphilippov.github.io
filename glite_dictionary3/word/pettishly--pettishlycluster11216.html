pettishly

adverb
in an irritable or petulant manner
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛt.ɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˈpɛt.ɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a petulant or irritable manner, especially over something minor; with childishly bad-tempered behavior.

Examples

react pettishly to criticism
He pushed his chair back pettishly, annoyed by the interruption.
She replied pettishly when asked to wait a few more minutes.
The child pettishly refused to eat his vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym – petulantly: in a sulky, irritable manner
