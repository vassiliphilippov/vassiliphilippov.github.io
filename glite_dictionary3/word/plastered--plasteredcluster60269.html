plastered

adjective
very drunk; intoxicated
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɚd / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstəd / (BrE)
Very drunk; heavily intoxicated by alcohol.

Examples

plastered after drinking
He was completely plastered after the party last night.
I don't remember anything from that night because I was so plastered.
They got plastered celebrating their team's big win.
