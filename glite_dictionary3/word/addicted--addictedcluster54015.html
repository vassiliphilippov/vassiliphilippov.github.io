addicted

adjective
dependent on a substance or activity
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪktəd / (AmE), / əˈdɪktɪd / (BrE)
physically or mentally dependent on a habit-forming substance or activity, often unable to stop using it or doing it

Examples

addicted to drugs
After her injury, she worried about becoming addicted to painkillers.
He became addicted to video games and found it hard to concentrate on his studies.
Many people are addicted to caffeine without realizing it.

  • synonym – dependent: unable to do without something regularly consumed or engaged in
