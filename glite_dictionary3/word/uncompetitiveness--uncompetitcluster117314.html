uncompetitiveness

noun
lack of ability to compete effectively
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.kəmˈpɛt.ə.tɪv.nəs / (AmE), / ˌʌn.kəmˈpɛt.ɪt.ɪv.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being not able to compete successfully with others.

Examples

Analysts attribute the company's losses to its growing uncompetitiveness in the global marketplace.
