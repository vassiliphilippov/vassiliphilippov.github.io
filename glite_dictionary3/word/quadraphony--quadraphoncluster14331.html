quadraphony

noun
four-channel sound recording system
Pronunciation: / kwəˈdræfəni / (AmE), / kwəˈdræfəni / (BrE)
a system of sound recording or reproduction that uses four separate channels to create a more immersive listening experience than traditional stereo

Examples

quadraphony sound system
During the 1970s, quadraphony was promoted as a major advancement over traditional stereo.
Some home theater systems can simulate quadraphony using modern surround sound technology.
The album was originally released in quadraphony, providing a unique listening experience with sound coming from all directions.
