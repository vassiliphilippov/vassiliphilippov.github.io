welfarist

adjective
relating to a welfare state
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlˌfɛrɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɛl.fə.rɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or supporting the principles of a welfare state, where the government plays a significant role in the protection and promotion of the economic and social well-being of its citizens.

Examples

welfarist policies
His views are distinctly welfarist, focusing on government intervention to reduce inequality.
Some critics argue that welfarist policies may discourage personal responsibility.
The politician promoted several welfarist reforms to expand access to healthcare.

Related Terms

  • synonym – welfare state: a social system in which the government provides for the citizens' needs
