ingenuousness

noun
openness or frankness
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɛnjuəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛnjuːəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being open, honest, and innocent, without deceit

Examples

childlike ingenuousness

His ingenuousness made others feel at ease, as he always spoke sincerely.
