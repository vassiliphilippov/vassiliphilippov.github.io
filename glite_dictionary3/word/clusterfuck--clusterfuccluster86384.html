to clusterfuck

verb
cause a chaotic failure (vulgar)
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌstɚˌfʌk / (AmE), / ˈklʌstəˌfʌk / (BrE)
to cause or experience a complete, disastrously disorganized failure; to make something chaotic or turn it into a mess (vulgar, slang).

Examples

to clusterfuck a project
If management ignores the warnings, the launch will clusterfuck the whole customer experience by Friday.
Their rushed decisions clusterfucked the project, leaving the team to fix critical errors overnight.
Everyone joked that the new policy is just clusterfucking our attempts to improve morale.

Related Terms

  • synonym – botch: to carry out a task badly or carelessly, causing failure
  • antonym – fix: to repair or correct a problem; opposite of causing a failure
  • synonym – mess up: to make a mistake or cause disorder; to spoil something
