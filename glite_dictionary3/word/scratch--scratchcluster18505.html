scratch

noun
harsh noise made by scraping
Pronunciation: / skrætʃ / (AmE), / skrætʃ / (BrE)
a harsh, rasping noise produced by scraping or rubbing

Examples

scratch of branches
She could hear the scratch of branches brushing against the window.
The old record player made a faint scratch as the needle moved.
The only sound in the room was the scratch of his pen on paper.
