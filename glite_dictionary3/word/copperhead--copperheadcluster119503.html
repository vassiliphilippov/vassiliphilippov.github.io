copperhead

noun
a venomous pit viper of the eastern US
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɒpəhɛd / (BrE)
a venomous, coppery-brown pit viper (Agkistrodon contortrix) found in upland regions of the eastern United States
