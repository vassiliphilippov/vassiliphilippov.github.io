to render

verb
cause to become
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛn.də / (BrE)
to cause someone or something to be in a particular state or condition

Examples

to render something useless
Her carelessness rendered the contract invalid.
The faulty wiring rendered the machine useless.
The news rendered him speechless with shock.
