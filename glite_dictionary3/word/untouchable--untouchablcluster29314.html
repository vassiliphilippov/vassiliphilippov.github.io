untouchable

adjective
not allowed to be touched
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʌtʃ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʌtʃ.ə.bəl / (BrE)
not allowed or permitted to be touched; forbidden to the touch.

Examples

untouchable objects
In the art gallery, all paintings are untouchable to prevent damage.
Many items in the old library are untouchable due to their fragile condition.
The rare vase in the exhibit is strictly untouchable for visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – inaccessible: not able to be reached or entered
  • antonym – permissible: allowed or permitted
