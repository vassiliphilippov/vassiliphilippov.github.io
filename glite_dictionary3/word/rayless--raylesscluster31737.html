rayless

adjective
lacking rays or ray flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪləs / (AmE), / ˈreɪləs / (BrE)
(of plants or flowers) lacking rays or ray flowers; having no radiating parts.

Examples

rayless flowers
Certain species in the daisy family produce rayless flowers, containing only disk florets.
The rayless sunflower is distinguished by its absence of petal-like rays.
The botanist noted the rayless appearance of the newly discovered plant.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ray: a radiating part or structure, especially in flowers
