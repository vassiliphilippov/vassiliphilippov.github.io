tartaric

adjective
relating to tartar or tartaric acid
Pronunciation: / tɑrˈtærɪk / (AmE), / tɑːˈtærɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or derived from tartar, especially tartaric acid, a compound found in many plants and used in food and industry.

Examples

tartaric acid powder
Baking powder often contains tartaric acid as one of its main ingredients.
Many grapes are naturally high in tartaric acid.
The winemaker explained that tartaric crystals can sometimes form in bottled wine.
