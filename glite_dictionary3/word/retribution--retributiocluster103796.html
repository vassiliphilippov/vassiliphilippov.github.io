retribution

noun
punishment or vengeance for wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtrɪˈbjuːʃən /; / ˌrɛtrəˈbjuːʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrɪˈbjuːʃən / (BrE)
punishment or penalty given as deserved payback for wrongdoing, often seen as morally right or coming from a higher power
