hexestrol

noun
synthetic estrogen compound (medicine)
Pronunciation: / hɛkˈsɛstrɔl / (AmE), / hɛkˈsɛstrɒl / (BrE)
a synthetic estrogen compound used in medicine to treat menstrual irregularities, menopausal symptoms, and formerly as a contraceptive

Examples

hexestrol tablets for menopause
Hexestrol was once prescribed to regulate menstrual cycles in women.
Doctors used hexestrol in hormone therapy to relieve menopausal symptoms before safer alternatives were developed.
Research shows that prolonged use of hexestrol can have serious side effects.
