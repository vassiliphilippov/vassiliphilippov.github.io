to rubberneck

verb
stare curiously or intrusively
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbərˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrʌbəˌnɛk / (BrE)
To look or stare with great curiosity, especially at something unusual or interesting, often in a way that slows progress or is considered intrusive.

Examples

to rubberneck at crashes
Drivers tend to rubberneck during major accidents, causing further traffic delays.
Please don't rubberneck when passing the construction site; it's unsafe.
Several tourists stopped to rubberneck at the car crash by the roadside.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gawk: to stare openly and stupidly
  • antonym – ignore: to pay no attention
  • synonym – ogle: to stare at in a lecherous manner
