groundkeeper

noun
person maintaining estate or sports grounds
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person employed to care for and maintain the grounds of an estate, park, or sports field

Examples

groundkeeper at a park
A skilled groundkeeper ensures the park remains clean and beautiful throughout the year.
During the summer, the groundkeeper mows the lawns and waters the flower beds regularly.
The groundkeeper arrived early each day to prepare the cricket pitch for play.
