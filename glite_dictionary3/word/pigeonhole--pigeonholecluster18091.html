to pigeonhole

verb
classify unfairly; stereotype
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒᵊnˌhoʊl /; / ˈpɪdʒənˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒᵊnhəʊl /; / ˈpɪdʒ.ən.həʊl / (BrE)
to unfairly classify or label someone or something as belonging to a particular category
