ankle

noun
joint between foot and leg
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈaŋkl̩ / (BrE)
the joint connecting the foot with the leg, where the tibia and fibula meet the talus

Examples

sprained ankle
Doctors examined the X-ray and confirmed that his ankle was not broken.
She twisted her ankle while hiking on the rocky trail.
The soccer player wore a brace to support her injured ankle.
