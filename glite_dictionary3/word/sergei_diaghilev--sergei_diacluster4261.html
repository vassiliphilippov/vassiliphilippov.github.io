Sergei Diaghilev

proper noun
Russian ballet impresario, founder of the Ballets Russes
Pronunciation: / sərˈɡeɪ diˈɑːɡəlɛv / (AmE), / səˈɡeɪ dɪˈɑːɡɪlɛv / (BrE)
(1872–1929) Russian ballet impresario best known for founding the Ballets Russes and introducing Russian ballet to Western Europe.

Examples

Sergei Diaghilev led ballet
