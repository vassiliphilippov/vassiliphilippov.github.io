folacin

noun
B vitamin vital for cell growth
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊləsɪn / (AmE), / ˈfəʊləsɪn / (BrE)
a B vitamin (also known as folic acid) that is essential for cell growth and reproduction

Examples

folacin supplement
A deficiency of folacin can cause anemia and other health problems.
Leafy green vegetables are a good dietary source of folacin.
Many prenatal vitamins contain folacin to support fetal development.

Related Terms

  • synonym – folic acid: a form of water-soluble B vitamin important for metabolism and cell division
