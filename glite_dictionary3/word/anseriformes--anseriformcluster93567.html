Anseriformes

noun
order of birds—ducks, geese, swans, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.sə.rəˈfɔr.miːz /; / ˌænsərəˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / ˌan.sə.rɪˈfɔː.miːz /; / ˌænsərɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
an order of birds that includes ducks, geese, swans, and screamers, characterized by webbed feet and a broad, flat bill
