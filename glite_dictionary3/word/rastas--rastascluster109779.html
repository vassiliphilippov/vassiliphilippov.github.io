Rastas

noun
members of Rastafarian movement
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːstəz / (AmE), / ˈrɑːstəz / (BrE)
Members of the Rastafarian religious and social movement originating in Jamaica, known for wearing dreadlocks and associated with reggae music and marijuana use.

The philosophy and style of Rastas have influenced global music and youth culture.
