to bear

verb
to yield or produce (interest, returns)
Pronunciation: / bɛr / (AmE), / bɛə / (BrE)
(of an account or investment) to yield or produce (especially with reference to interest, dividends, or returns).

Examples

bear interest
My savings account bears a moderate amount of interest each year.
The old certificate no longer bears interest after its maturity date.
This type of bond does not bear any interest payments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pay: to provide a return or reward, especially a financial one
  • synonym – yield: to produce or provide (a natural, agricultural, or industrial product)
