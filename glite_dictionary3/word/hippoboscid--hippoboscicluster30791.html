hippoboscid

noun
parasitic bloodsucking fly
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪpoʊˈbɑsɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəˈbɒsɪd / (BrE)
a bloodsucking fly of the family Hippoboscidae, parasitic on birds and mammals

Examples

parasitic hippoboscid fly
