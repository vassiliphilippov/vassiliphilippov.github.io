printout

noun
computer-produced printed document
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnˌtaʊt / (AmE), / ˈprɪntaʊt / (BrE)
a document or page produced by a computer printer, showing information in printed form

Examples

printout of results
After submitting the form online, a confirmation printout was generated for records.
She handed the manager a printout of the latest sales figures.
The report was difficult to read on screen, so he made a printout.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hard copy: a physical print of digital information
  • antonym – soft copy: an electronic version of a document, not printed
