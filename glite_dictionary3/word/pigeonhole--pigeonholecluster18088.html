pigeonhole

noun
small compartment for storage or sorting
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒənˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒɪnhəʊl / (BrE)
a small compartment, typically one of a set, used for sorting or storing papers, letters, or small items

Examples

staff room pigeonhole
Each staff member has a pigeonhole in the staff room for internal mail.
Her letters accumulated in the dusty pigeonhole at the front desk.
The manager placed the documents in the appropriate pigeonhole for filing.
