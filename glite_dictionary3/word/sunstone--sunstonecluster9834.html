sunstone

noun
a glittering mineral, often feldspar or quartz
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsʌnstəʊn / (BrE)
a translucent variety of feldspar or quartz, typically spangled with tiny reflections from inclusions of mica or other minerals, giving it a glittering appearance
