Harold Kroto

proper noun
British chemist and fullerene discoverer
Pronunciation: / ˈhærəld ˈkroʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhæɹ.əld ˈkrəʊ.təʊ / (BrE)
(1939–2016) A British chemist who, along with Robert Curl and Richard Smalley, discovered fullerenes and helped pioneer a new field in chemistry.
