coyote

noun
person who smuggles migrants into the US
Pronunciation: / kaɪˈoʊti / (AmE), / kaɪˈəʊti / (BrE)
a person who smuggles illegal immigrants across the border into the United States, especially from Mexico

Examples

coyote smuggler
Many migrants pay a coyote large sums of money for the dangerous journey north.
The authorities arrested a coyote who was transporting people across the border.
The newspaper reported that coyotes often abandon people in the desert.

Related Terms

  • synonym – smuggler: a person who illegally transports people or goods across borders
