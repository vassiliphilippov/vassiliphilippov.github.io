rattan

noun
material from climbing palms for furniture and weaving
Pronunciation: / rəˈtæn / (AmE), / rəˈtan / (BrE)
a climbing palm with long, slender, flexible stems, or the stems themselves, used for making furniture, baskets, and similar items

Examples

rattan furniture
Many baskets in the market were woven using strong rattan stems.
The chair was made from rattan and looked elegant in the sunroom.
They bought a beautiful rattan sofa for their garden terrace.
After the storm, he repaired the patio furniture with fresh rattan.
Baskets made from rattan are both lightweight and durable.
The craftsman skillfully wove the rattan into an elegant chair.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cane: the stem of certain plants, used for weaving or furniture
  • synonym – wicker: pliable twigs, typically of willow, used for weaving baskets and furniture
