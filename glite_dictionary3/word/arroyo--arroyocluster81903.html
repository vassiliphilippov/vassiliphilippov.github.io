arroyo

noun
dry stream bed; small watercourse
Pronunciation: / əˈroʊ.joʊ / (AmE), / əˈrɔɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
A small steep-sided watercourse or stream, typically one that is dry except during heavy rain.

Examples

dry arroyo bed
After the storm, water rushed through the usually dry arroyo at the edge of town.
During summer, the arroyo is often just a winding stretch of sand and rocks.
Hikers crossed a narrow arroyo before reaching the canyon walls.
