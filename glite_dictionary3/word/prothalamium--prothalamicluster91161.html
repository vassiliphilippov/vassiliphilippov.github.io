prothalamium

noun
song celebrating a marriage
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊθəˈleɪmiəm / (AmE), / ˌprəʊθəˈleɪmiəm / (BrE)

The poet composed a prothalamium for the couple’s upcoming wedding.
