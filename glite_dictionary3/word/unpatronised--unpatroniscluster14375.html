unpatronised

adjective
not supported or frequented
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpeɪtrəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpat.rə.naɪzd / (BrE)
Having received little or no patronage; not frequented or supported by clients or customers.

Examples

unpatronised theater
Many theaters in rural areas are largely unpatronised during the winter season.
The shop was unpatronised by locals after it raised its prices.
The small cafe remained unpatronised despite its excellent pastries.
