immovably

adverb
in a way that cannot be moved
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmuːvəbli / (AmE), / ɪˈmuːvəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that cannot be moved; firmly fixed or motionless.

Examples

immovably fixed in place
Despite the strong winds, the statue stood immovably in the center of the park.
She sat immovably at her desk, refusing to leave until her work was done.
The ancient tree remained immovably rooted after the storm passed.
