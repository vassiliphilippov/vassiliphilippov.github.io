myringoplasty

noun
surgical repair of a perforated eardrum
Pronunciation: / mɪˌrɪŋɡoʊˈplæsti / (AmE), / mɪˌrɪŋɡəʊˈplæsti / (BrE)
a surgical operation to repair a perforated eardrum using a tissue graft

Examples

myringoplasty surgery on ear
A successful myringoplasty can significantly reduce the risk of recurring ear infections.
The patient underwent a myringoplasty to restore hearing in his left ear after suffering an injury.
The surgeon explained that the myringoplasty procedure would use a small tissue graft from behind the ear.
