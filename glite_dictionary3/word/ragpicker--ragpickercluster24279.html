ragpicker

noun
person who collects rags for resale
Pronunciation: / ˈrægˌpɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈræɡˌpɪkə / (BrE)
A person who collects rags and other refuse from garbage or public places, often for resale.

Examples

He wrote a novel about a ragpicker who dreams of a better life.
In nineteenth-century Paris, a ragpicker was a common sight on city streets.
