refreshed

adjective
updated after reload (digital content)
Pronunciation: / r\u026a\u02c8fr\u025b\u0283t / (AmE), / r\u026a\u02c8fr\u025b\u0283t / (BrE)
Updated or reloaded (of a web page, app, data, or interface), showing new or current information after a refresh or reload action.

Examples

refreshed web page
After the bug fix, the refreshed website showed the corrected prices and new product images.
She noticed the app displayed a refreshed news feed with the latest headlines after reopening it.
The designer submitted the refreshed interface mockups to demonstrate the updated color scheme and icons.

  • synonym – updated: made current or brought up to date
