fondu

noun
dish of melted cheese or chocolate for dipping
Pronunciation: / fɒnˈduː / (AmE), / fɒnˈduː / (BrE)
A hot dish of melted cheese or chocolate, served in a communal pot for dipping bread, fruit, or vegetables.

Examples

fondu with melted cheese
