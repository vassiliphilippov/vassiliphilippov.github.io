selflessly

adverb
in an altruistic or unselfish way
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlflɪsli / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows concern for the needs and wishes of others rather than one's own; in an altruistic manner.

Examples

to act selflessly
He selflessly stepped aside to let his colleague take credit for the project.
She selflessly donated her time every weekend to help at the animal shelter.
Their leader selflessly put the team's needs above her own throughout the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – altruistically: in a selfless or unselfish manner
  • antonym – selfishly: in a way that is concerned only with one’s own advantage
