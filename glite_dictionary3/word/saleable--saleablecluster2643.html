saleable

adjective
able to be sold
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˈseɪləbəl / (BrE)
Able to be sold or suitable for sale.

Examples

saleable goods
After extensive repairs, the old house finally became saleable.
Most of the artwork in the gallery is saleable at reasonable prices.
The farmer sorted the apples into saleable and unsaleable batches before going to market.
