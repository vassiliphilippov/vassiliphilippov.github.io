Adonis

proper noun
beautiful youth in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / əˈdoʊnɪs / (AmE), / əˈdəʊnɪs / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a handsome youth loved by Aphrodite and Persephone, noted for his beauty and tragic fate.
