grayly

adverb
in a gray manner; with gray appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is gray in color or appearance.

Examples

clouds grayly cover
Mist grayly veiled the mountains in the early morning light.
Old photographs grayly line the walls of the dusty hallway.
The sky was grayly overcast as the storm approached.
