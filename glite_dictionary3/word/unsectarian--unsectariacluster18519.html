unsectarian

adjective
not limited to any sect, school, or party
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.sɛkˈtɛr.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌʌn.sɛkˈteə.ri.ən / (BrE)
Not limited to or associated with a particular sect, school, or political party; impartial or inclusive of all groups.

Examples

unsectarian approach
His writing displayed an unsectarian outlook, avoiding bias toward any particular ideology.
She admired the unsectarian approach of the organization, which promoted unity and respect among diverse groups.
The university has an unsectarian policy, welcoming students from all religious and cultural backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nonsectarian: not limited to a particular religious sect or group
  • antonym – sectarian: relating to or characteristic of a sect, often narrow-minded
