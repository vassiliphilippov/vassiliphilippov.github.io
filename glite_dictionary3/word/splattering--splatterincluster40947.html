splattering

noun
explosive spattering noise
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæt̬.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsplæt.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a noise produced by something spattering or sputtering suddenly and explosively

Examples

loud splattering sound
An abrupt splattering echoed from the kitchen as the oil hit the hot pan.
The splattering of rain against the window kept him awake all night.
We heard a loud splattering from the pipes when the heating turned on.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spatter: a short burst of noise or liquid striking a surface
