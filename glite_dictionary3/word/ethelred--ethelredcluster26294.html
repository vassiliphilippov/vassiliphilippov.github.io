Ethelred

proper noun
King of England, 10th–11th century
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθəlˌrɛd / (AmE), / ˈiːθəlˌrɛd / (BrE)
(c. 968–1016) King of England, known as Ethelred the Unready, who reigned from 978 to 1016 and struggled to defend England against Danish invasions.

Examples

Ethelred the Unready
Ethelred ascended the English throne in 978 after the murder of his half-brother.
Many historians criticize Ethelred for his inability to unify the country against the Danish threat.
The reign of Ethelred was marked by repeated Viking invasions and political instability.
