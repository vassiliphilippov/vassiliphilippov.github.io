shadbush

noun
North American tree/shrub with white flowers and edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈʃadˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
A North American tree or shrub of the genus Amelanchier, with showy white flowers and edible blue-black or purplish fruit.

Examples

shadbush berries
Birds are attracted to the berries of the shadbush throughout the summer.
He planted a shadbush in his garden for its beautiful flowers and edible fruit.
The shadbush is often the first tree to bloom in the spring forest.
