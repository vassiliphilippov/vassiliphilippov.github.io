to source

verb
obtain from a particular supplier or place
Pronunciation: / sɔrs / (AmE), / sɔːs / (BrE)
to obtain goods, materials, or information from a particular place, person, or company, especially for business purposes

Examples

to source materials
Many manufacturers source their raw materials from overseas.
The company sourced its components through a new supplier last year.
We are currently sourcing eco-friendly fabrics for our new product line.

  • synonym – procure: to obtain something, especially with effort or care
