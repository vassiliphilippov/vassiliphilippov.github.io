cryogen

noun
liquid used for ultra-low-temperature refrigeration
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪ.oʊˌdʒɛn / (AmE), / ˈkraɪ.əʊ.dʒən / (BrE)
a substance, especially a liquid, that boils at below -160°C and is used for refrigeration at extremely low temperatures
