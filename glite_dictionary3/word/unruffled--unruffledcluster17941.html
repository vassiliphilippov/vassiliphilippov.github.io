unruffled

adjective
calm and composed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrʌfəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈrʌfəld / (BrE)
Calm and not easily upset, disturbed, or agitated; remaining composed under pressure.

Examples

unruffled under pressure
Despite the chaos around her, she remained unruffled and focused on her work.
He answered the difficult questions in an unruffled manner, impressing the interviewers.
The team’s coach appeared unruffled even when his players made mistakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – agitated: feeling or appearing troubled or nervous
  • synonym – calm: not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
