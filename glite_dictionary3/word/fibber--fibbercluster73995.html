fibber

noun
person who tells small lies
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪbər / (AmE), / ˈfɪbə / (BrE)
A person who tells lies, especially trivial or harmless ones.

Examples

little fibber
Don't be such a fibber; we all know you didn't finish your homework.
My little brother is a frequent fibber when it comes to eating vegetables.
