reconnaissance

noun
military or exploratory survey for information
Pronunciation: / rəˈkɑːnəˌsɑːns / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒnɪsəns / (BrE)
the act of obtaining information about an enemy or area, especially in preparation for military action; an exploratory survey or investigation

Examples

reconnaissance mission
On their reconnaissance mission, the team gathered vital intelligence.
Satellite images were used for reconnaissance of the disaster area.
The army conducted a reconnaissance to locate enemy positions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – scouting: the act of gathering information, especially for military or survey purposes
  • synonym – survey: a detailed examination or inspection, especially as a means of gathering information
