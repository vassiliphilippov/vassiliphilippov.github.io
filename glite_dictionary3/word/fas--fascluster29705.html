FAS

noun
fetal alcohol syndrome; birth defects from alcohol during pregnancy
Pronunciation: / ˌɛf.eɪˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌɛf.eɪˈɛs / (BrE)
a congenital condition caused by prenatal exposure to alcohol, leading to physical deformities, facial abnormalities, and cognitive impairment
