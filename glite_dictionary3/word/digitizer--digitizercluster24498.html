digitizer

noun
device converting analogue to digital signals
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzə / (BrE)
a device that converts analogue signals into digital signals for use by computers or other digital equipment

Examples

digitizer converts signals
Many touchscreen devices use a digitizer to detect finger or stylus input.
The digitizer converts the artist's drawings into digital files for computer editing.
The engineer replaced the faulty digitizer to restore accurate signal processing.
