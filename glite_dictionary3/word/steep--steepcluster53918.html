to steep

verb
devote oneself fully to something
Pronunciation: / stiːp / (AmE), / stiːp / (BrE)

Examples

to steep oneself in music
After retiring, she steeped herself in the world of classical music.
During the summer, I steeped myself in learning new languages.
He steeped himself in historical research for years before publishing his book.
