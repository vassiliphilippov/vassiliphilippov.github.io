Jan Hendrix Oort

proper noun
Dutch astronomer; proposed the Oort cloud
Pronunciation: / jɑn ˈhɛn.drɪks ˈɔrt / (AmE), / jan ˈhɛn.drɪks ˈɔːt / (BrE)
(1900–1992) A Dutch astronomer renowned for proving that the Milky Way galaxy rotates and for proposing the existence of the Oort cloud surrounding the solar system.

Jan Hendrix Oort studied galaxies
