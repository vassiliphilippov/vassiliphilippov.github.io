pruritus

noun
severe itching of the skin
Pronunciation: / pruˈraɪtəs / (AmE), / pruːˈraɪtəs / (BrE)
A severe itching sensation of the skin, often caused by allergies, infections, or underlying medical conditions.

Examples

severe pruritus episode
Allergic reactions are a common reason for pruritus in children.
Certain medications can cause pruritus as a side effect.
The patient complained of persistent pruritus that affected her sleep.
