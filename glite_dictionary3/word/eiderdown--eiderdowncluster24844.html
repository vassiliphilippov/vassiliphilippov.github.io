eiderdown

noun
soft quilt filled with eider duck down
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪdərˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈaɪdədaʊn / (BrE)
A soft quilt filled with the down feathers of the eider duck, often used as a warm bed covering.

Examples

soft eiderdown quilt
She snuggled under the eiderdown to keep warm on the cold winter night.
The antique eiderdown was beautifully embroidered and very soft to the touch.
We replaced our old blanket with a luxurious eiderdown for extra comfort.
