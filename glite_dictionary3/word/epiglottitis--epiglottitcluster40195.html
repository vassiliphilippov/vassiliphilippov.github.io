epiglottitis

noun
inflammation of the epiglottis
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪɡlɑˈtaɪt̬ɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪɡlɒˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
A serious medical condition in which the epiglottis becomes inflamed, often causing fever, severe sore throat, and difficulty in swallowing.
