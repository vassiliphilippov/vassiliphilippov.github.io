to render

verb
melt fat to purify it
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛn.də / (BrE)
to melt fat or lard in order to separate and remove impurities

Examples

to render fat
In old recipes, cooks would render lard before adding it to pie crusts.
The chef used a low flame to render the bacon, collecting the clear fat for cooking.
To make traditional soap, you must first render animal fat to remove any impurities.
