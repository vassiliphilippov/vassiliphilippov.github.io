crinkleroot

noun
North American herb with pungent roots
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪŋkləˌrut / (AmE), / ˈkrɪŋkləˌruːt / (BrE)
a North American herb (Cardamine diphylla) with pungent, scaly or toothed roots, often found in moist woods
