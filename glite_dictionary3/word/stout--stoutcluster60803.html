stout

adjective
having rugged physical strength
Pronunciation: / staʊt / (AmE), / staʊt / (BrE)
(of a person) having strong and rugged physical strength; tough and able to withstand fatigue or hardship.

Examples

stout worker
Only stout men could carry the supplies across the rough terrain.
The stout sailor braved the stormy seas without hesitation.
The team recruited several stout workers for the physically demanding job.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sturdy: strong and solidly built; robust
  • antonym – weak: lacking physical strength or robustness
