talk

noun
gossip or rumor, idle conversation
Pronunciation: / tɔk / (AmE), / tɔːk / (BrE)
gossip or rumors, especially when spread informally or without evidence; idle conversation about others.

Examples

idle talk
Ignore the talk; most of it is just baseless rumors.
She quickly became the subject of talk in the small town.
There has been a lot of talk about the new manager's private life.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gossip: casual or unconstrained conversation about others, typically involving details not confirmed as true
  • synonym – rumor: a currently circulating story or report of uncertain or doubtful truth
