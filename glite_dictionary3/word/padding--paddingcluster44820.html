padding

noun
soft material for filling or protection
Pronunciation: / ˈpædɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpædɪŋ / (BrE)
soft or resilient material used to fill, give shape, protect, or add comfort to something

Examples

padding in shoes
Boxers wear gloves with thick padding to protect their hands and opponents.
She added some padding to the seat for extra comfort during long drives.
The winter jacket contains extra padding to keep you warm in cold weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lining: a layer of material on the inside surface for comfort or protection
  • synonym – stuffing: material used to fill cushions or soft objects
