to offer

verb
make available for sale
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːfər /; / ˈɑː.fɚ /; / ˈɔfər /; / ˈɑfər / (AmE), / ˈɒfə /; / ˈɒf.ə /; / ˈɒf.ər / (BrE)
to make something available, provide, or present something for someone to accept, use, or consider (including for sale, use, opportunity, or as a gesture)

Examples

The supermarket is offering fresh strawberries at a reduced price this week.
They offered the limited-edition poster for sale at the concert.
During the sale, the store offers significant discounts on many items.
She offered her assistance to anyone who needed help with the project.

She offered us some snacks when we arrived at her house.
The company offered him a generous salary package.
They offer free shipping on all online orders.
