Harriet Tubman

proper noun
American abolitionist and Underground Railroad conductor
Pronunciation: / ˈhæriət ˈtʌbmən / (AmE), / ˈhæriət ˈtʌbmən / (BrE)
(1820–1913) An American abolitionist who escaped slavery and became a leading figure in helping others to freedom via the Underground Railroad.
