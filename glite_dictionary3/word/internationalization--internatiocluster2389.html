internationalization

noun
bringing under international control
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of bringing something under international control, influence, or management

Examples

internationalization of policy
Debates over the internationalization of environmental policy have grown in recent decades.
One consequence of the agreement was the internationalization of the disputed territory.
The conflict resulted in the partial internationalization of the region's administration.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nationalization: the process of bringing something under national control
