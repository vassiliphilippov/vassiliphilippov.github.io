to solemnize

verb
perform or observe with dignity
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːləmˌnaɪz /; / ˈsɑː.ləmˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɒləmˌnaɪz /; / ˈsɒl.əm.naɪz / (BrE)
