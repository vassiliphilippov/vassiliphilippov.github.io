to whip

verb
beat or stir rapidly (esp. food)
Pronunciation: / wɪp / (AmE), / wɪp / (BrE)
to beat or stir (eggs, cream, etc.) vigorously, especially with a whisk, to incorporate air or achieve a desired consistency

Examples

to whip cream
Beat the cream until it starts to whip and forms soft peaks.
If you whip the ingredients too long, the mixture may curdle.
She used a wire whisk to whip the egg whites vigorously.

Related Terms

  • synonym – whisk: to beat or stir rapidly with a light, swift movement, especially with a whisk
