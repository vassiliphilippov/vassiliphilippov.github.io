uncertain

adjective
lacking confidence or assurance
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɝːtən / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːtn / (BrE)
lacking confidence or assurance; not having clear certainty about oneself or something

Examples

uncertain steps
He walked with uncertain steps, glancing nervously around the room.
Her uncertain smile revealed her lack of confidence.
She gave an uncertain answer, not sure if it was correct.
