ricksha

noun
two-wheeled cart pulled by a person
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪkʃə / (AmE), / ˈrɪkʃə / (BrE)
A small two-wheeled passenger cart pulled by a person, typically used for transport in some Asian countries.

Examples

pull a ricksha
He worked tirelessly as a ricksha puller to support his family.
