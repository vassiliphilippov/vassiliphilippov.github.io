dial

noun
rotating numbered disc on a telephone
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəl / (BrE)
A disc on a telephone, usually marked with numbers, that is rotated a set distance to select each digit when making a call.

Examples

phone dial
He slowly turned the dial to enter the phone number.
My grandmother's house still has a phone with a rotary dial.
Old telephones have a circular dial instead of buttons.

Related Terms

  • antonym – keypad: a set of buttons for entering numbers on a telephone
