unapproachable

adjective
impossible or very hard to reach
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈproʊ.tʃə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈprəʊ.tʃə.bəl / (BrE)
impossible or very difficult to reach or access; located or situated so as not to be easily approached

Examples

unapproachable mountain peak

The summit remained unapproachable until better climbing equipment was invented.
