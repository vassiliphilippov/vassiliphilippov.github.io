precentor

noun
musical director of a choir
Pronunciation: / priˈsɛntər / (AmE), / prɪˈsɛntə / (BrE)
the person who leads and directs the singing of a choir, especially in a church or cathedral

Examples

precentor leads choir
After years of service, the cathedral appointed a new precentor to oversee the music program.
As precentor, she was responsible for selecting the hymns every Sunday.
The precentor led the choir through a complex arrangement for the Easter service.
