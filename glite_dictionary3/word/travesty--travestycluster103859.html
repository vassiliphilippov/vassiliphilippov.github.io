travesty

noun
humorous imitation; distorted version
Pronunciation: / ˈtræv.ɪ.sti /; / ˈtræv.ə.sti / (AmE), / ˈtræv.ɪ.sti /; / ˈtræv.ə.sti / (BrE)
a work, performance, or representation that humorously imitates and distorts something; a grotesque or absurd parody or misrepresentation
