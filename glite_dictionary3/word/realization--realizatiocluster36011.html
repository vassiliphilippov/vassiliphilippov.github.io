realization

noun
completed/enriched musical composition by another
Pronunciation: / ˌriələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌriːələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɪəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of completing or elaborating a musical composition, often by adding harmonies, instrumentation, or finishing unfinished sections
