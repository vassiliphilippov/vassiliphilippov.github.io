Dachau

proper noun
Nazi concentration camp near Munich
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːkaʊ / (AmE), / ˈdaːkaʊ / (BrE)
A Nazi concentration camp for Jews and other prisoners, established in 1933 near Munich in southern Germany.
