to ace

verb
succeed easily or with great success
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
to succeed very easily or do extremely well at something, especially a test or challenge

Examples

to ace a test
He always seems to ace his presentations, making them look effortless.
If you want to ace the interview, you need to prepare your answers thoroughly.
She studied hard and managed to ace the biology exam.
He was so well prepared that he aced every challenge the course threw at him.
If you study hard, you can ace the interview tomorrow.
She aced her final exam and received the highest grade in the class.
