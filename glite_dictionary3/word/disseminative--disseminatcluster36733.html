disseminative

adjective
spreading by diffusion
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛm.əˌneɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛm.ɪ.nə.tɪv / (BrE)

Examples

disseminative effect
Education can be a disseminative force, spreading knowledge throughout a community.
Her research focused on the disseminative mechanisms involved in plant pollination.
The disseminative properties of the virus made it particularly dangerous in crowded areas.
