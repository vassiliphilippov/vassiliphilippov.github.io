defence

noun
justification or support of an act or belief
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛns / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛns / (BrE)
an argument, statement, or explanation made to justify or support actions, beliefs, or opinions

She gave an eloquent defence of her research when questioned by the committee.
The manager's defence of the team's poor performance was well received by the press.
