untravelled

adjective
not having been travelled over
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrævəld / (BrE)
(of a place or route) not having been travelled over or through by people.

Examples

untravelled road
Much of the island remains untravelled territory even today.
She dreamed of exploring untravelled regions of the world.
The hikers took an untravelled path through the dense forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym – untrodden: not walked on; not having been stepped on
