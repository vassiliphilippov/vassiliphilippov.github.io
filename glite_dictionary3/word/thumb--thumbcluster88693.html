to thumb

verb
turn pages quickly (e.g., in a book)
Pronunciation: / θʌm / (AmE), / θʌm / (BrE)
to turn the pages of a book or a document quickly, typically only glancing at the contents

Examples

to thumb through pages
He thumbed through the magazine while waiting for his appointment.
I often thumb through cookbooks for dinner ideas.
She quickly thumbed through her notes before the exam began.

  • synonym – leaf: to turn pages quickly without careful reading
