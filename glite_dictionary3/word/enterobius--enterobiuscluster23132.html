Enterobius

noun
genus of pinworms (parasitic worms)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛntəˈroʊbiəs / (AmE), / ˌɛntəˈrəʊbiəs / (BrE)
a genus of small parasitic worms (pinworms) that infect the intestines of humans and sometimes other animals
