Godwin Austen

proper noun
second highest mountain (K2) in the Karakoram Range
Pronunciation: / ˈgɑd.wɪn ˈɔː.stɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.wɪn ˈɔː.stɪn / (BrE)
