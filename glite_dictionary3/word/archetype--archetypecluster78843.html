archetype

noun
EHR clinical data template
Pronunciation: / c851d0rk6accta6ap / (AmE), / c851d0k6ata6ap / (BrE)
(in health informatics) a formal, reusable model or template for representing a clinical concept in an electronic health record, used to ensure consistent data encoding and interoperability between systems.

Examples

medical archetype template
Hospitals adopted a standard archetype to model patient data in their EHR system.
The clinical team reviewed the archetype to ensure vital signs were recorded consistently across records.
Developers published a new archetype for allergy information to improve data sharing between hospitals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – schema: a formal definition of the structure and rules for storing and validating data
  • synonym – template: a predefined structure used to organise or represent information consistently
