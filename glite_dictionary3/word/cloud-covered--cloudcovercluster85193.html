cloud-covered

adjective
covered with clouds
Pronunciation: / klaʊdˌkʌvɚd / (AmE), / klaʊdˌkʌvəd / (BrE)
(of the sky, weather, or a surface) covered with or obscured by clouds.

Examples

cloud-covered sky
A cloud-covered sky made the afternoon seem gloomy and gray.
From the plane, the city below was cloud-covered and invisible to us.
The mountaintop remained cloud-covered throughout our hike, hiding the view.
