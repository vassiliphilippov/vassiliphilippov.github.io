roentgenogram

noun
X-ray image for medical diagnosis
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛntɡənˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈrɛntɡənəˌɡræm / (BrE)
an image produced by exposing photographic film or a digital sensor to X-rays, used to examine the internal structures of the body for medical diagnosis

Examples

roentgenogram of the chest
A chest roentgenogram revealed abnormalities in the lungs.
Interpreting a roentgenogram requires specialized training in radiology.
The doctor examined the patient's roentgenogram to assess the extent of the bone fracture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – radiograph: an image produced on a sensitive plate or film by X-rays, gamma rays, or similar radiation
  • synonym – x-ray: an image produced using X-rays, commonly for medical examination
