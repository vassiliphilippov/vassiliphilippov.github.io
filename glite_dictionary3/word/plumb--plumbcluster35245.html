to plumb

verb
examine or explore in depth
Pronunciation: / plʌm / (AmE), / plʌm / (BrE)
to examine or explore something thoroughly and in great depth

Examples

to plumb the mystery
Scientists have plumbed the mysteries of the ocean for decades.
She set out to plumb the reasons behind his sudden disappearance.
The novel plumbs the depths of human emotion with remarkable insight.
