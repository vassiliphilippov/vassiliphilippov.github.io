tessellation

noun
pattern of shapes fitted together
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛsəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtɛsɪˈleɪʃən /; / ˌtɛsɪˈleɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an arrangement or pattern of shapes closely fitted together without gaps or overlaps, or the process of covering a surface with such a pattern (especially in art, tiling, or mosaics)
