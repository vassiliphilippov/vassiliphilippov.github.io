baiting

noun
harassment of a tethered animal
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of harassing or tormenting a tethered animal, often for sport or entertainment

Examples

baiting a bull
Animal rights activists protest against the baiting of bulls and other tethered creatures.
Laws were enacted to prevent the baiting of animals for sport.
The cruel practice of baiting bears was once considered a popular form of entertainment.
