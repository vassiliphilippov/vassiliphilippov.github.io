to boost

verb
push or lift up from below or behind
Pronunciation: / bust / (AmE), / buːst / (BrE)
to push or lift someone or something up from below or behind

Examples

to boost a friend
He knelt down so his teammate could boost himself up to grab the ledge.
She boosted her friend over the fence during the obstacle course.
We had to boost the child onto the climbing wall because he was too short.
