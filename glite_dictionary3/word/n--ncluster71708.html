N

noun
chemical symbol for nitrogen
Pronunciation: / ɛn / (AmE), / ɛn / (BrE)
The chemical symbol for nitrogen, a colorless, odorless, tasteless inert gas that makes up about 78 percent of Earth's atmosphere and is essential to all living things.
