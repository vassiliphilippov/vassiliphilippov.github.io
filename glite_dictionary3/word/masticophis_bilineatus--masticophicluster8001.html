Masticophis bilineatus

noun
species of snake native to the US southwest
Pronunciation: / ˌmæstɪˈkoʊfɪs baɪˌlɪniˈeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌmæstɪˈkəʊfɪs baɪˌlɪniːˈɑːtəs / (BrE)
A species of snake, also known as the Sonoran whipsnake, found in the southwestern United States; it lives both on the ground and in trees.

Masticophis bilineatus climbs trees
