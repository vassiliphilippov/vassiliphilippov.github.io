amphiboly

noun
ambiguous grammatical construction
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbəli / (AmE), / amˈfɪbəli / (BrE)
A grammatical construction in a sentence that creates ambiguity, leading to more than one possible interpretation.

Examples

Writers should try to avoid amphiboly in order to express their ideas more clearly.
