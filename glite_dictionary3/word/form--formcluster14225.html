form

noun
life-size dummy for displaying clothes
Pronunciation: / fɔrm / (AmE), / fɔːm / (BrE)
a life-size dummy, often shaped like a human torso, used to display or fit clothes in shops or by tailors

Examples

dress a form
Each store window featured a form dressed in the latest fashions.
She bought a new form to help her create custom-fit jackets in her studio.
The designer adjusted the dress carefully on the form before the show.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mannequin: a life-sized model of the human body used for displaying clothes
