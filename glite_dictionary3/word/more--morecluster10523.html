more

particle
used to form the comparative
Pronunciation: / mɔr / (AmE), / mɔː / (BrE)
Used with adjectives and adverbs to form the comparative, indicating a greater degree or amount.

Examples

more quickly
He finished his homework more quickly than I did.
The mountain is more beautiful in the spring.
This new story is more exciting than the old one.
