patroness

noun
female patron or sponsor
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪtrənɛs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtrənɪs / (BrE)
a woman who gives financial or other support to a person, organization, or cause, or the wife of a patron

Examples

patroness of the arts
As the patroness of several charities, she was known for her generosity.
The celebrated artist thanked her patroness for funding the new exhibit.
The festival was organized under the guidance of its long-time patroness.

  • synonym – patron: a person who gives support, especially financial, to an artist, organization, or cause
