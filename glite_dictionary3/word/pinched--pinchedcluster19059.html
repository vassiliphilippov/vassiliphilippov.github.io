pinched

adjective
squeezed or pressed tightly (body part)
Pronunciation: / pɪnʧt / (AmE), / pɪnʧt / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) squeezed or pressed so tightly as to be uncomfortable or cause pain.

Examples

pinched toes
After hiking all day, my pinched feet were begging for relief.
He wore a hat that left a pinched red mark on his forehead.
Her toes felt pinched after hours in those narrow shoes.

  • synonym – compressed: pressed or squeezed into a smaller space
