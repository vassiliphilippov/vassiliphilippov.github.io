tricolour

noun
flag with three colored stripes
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈtrɪkʌlə / (BrE)
A flag with three bands or stripes of different colors, especially the national flag of France.

Examples

national tricolour flag
He waved the Irish tricolour proudly during the parade.
Many countries adopted a tricolour as a symbol of national unity and liberty.
The French tricolour was first raised in 1794 during the Revolution.
