proteolytic

adjective
relating to the breakdown of proteins
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtiəˈlɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtiəˈlɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or causing the breakdown of proteins by enzymatic action.

Examples

proteolytic enzyme action
Certain enzymes exhibit proteolytic activity, enabling them to break down dietary proteins in the stomach.
Researchers studied the proteolytic enzymes to develop new treatments for digestive disorders.
The proteolytic process is crucial in cell metabolism and recycling of cellular components.
