woolen

noun
fabric made from sheep's wool
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlən / (AmE), / ˈwʊlən / (BrE)
A fabric made from wool, especially the short, soft hairs of sheep; often used for clothing and blankets.

Examples

soft woolen blanket
She wrapped herself in a thick woolen to stay warm during the cold night.
The tailor recommended a high-quality woolen for making winter suits.
Washing a woolen in hot water can cause it to shrink.
