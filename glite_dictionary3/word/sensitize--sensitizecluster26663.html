to sensitize

verb
make sensitive or aware
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪz /; / ˈsɛn.sə.taɪz /; / ˈsɛn.səˌtaɪz /; / ˈsɛn.sɪˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsɪtaɪz /; / ˈsɛn.sɪ.taɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something sensitive or responsive to something, or more aware of it (such as a drug, allergen, or issue)
