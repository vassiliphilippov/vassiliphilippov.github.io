Cipro

noun
antibiotic brand for bacterial infections
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪproʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɪprəʊ / (BrE)
A brand name for the antibiotic ciprofloxacin, used to treat serious bacterial infections of the skin, respiratory tract, urinary tract, bones, or joints.

Examples

take Cipro tablets
After being diagnosed with pneumonia, she started taking Cipro as part of her treatment.
My doctor prescribed Cipro for my severe urinary tract infection.
Some bacteria have developed resistance to Cipro, making infections harder to treat.
