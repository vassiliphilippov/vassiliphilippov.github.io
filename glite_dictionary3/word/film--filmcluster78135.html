film

noun
thin plastic sheet for wrapping or covering
Pronunciation: / fɪlm / (AmE), / fɪlm / (BrE)
a thin layer or sheet of material, such as plastic or oil, covering a surface

Examples

a film of oil
A thin film protected the new device from scratches during shipping.
Before painting, remove the plastic film from the surface of the product.
She covered the leftovers with a layer of film to keep them fresh.
A film of oil floated on the surface of the water.
A thin film of ice covered the pond after the cold night.
She wiped away the film of dust from the window sill.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coating: a layer of a substance covering a surface
  • antonym – foil: a thin sheet of metal, used for wrapping food
  • synonym – layer: a sheet, quantity, or thickness of material covering a surface or lying between two things
  • synonym – plastic wrap: a thin, clingy plastic film used in food storage
