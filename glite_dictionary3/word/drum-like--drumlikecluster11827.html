drum-like

adjective
resembling a drum in shape
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌm ˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdrʌm ˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Shaped like or resembling a drum, especially in having a cylindrical form with flat ends.

Examples

drum-like shape
She noticed the drum-like appearance of the new water tank.
The massive, drum-like structure dominated the town square.
The tower's drum-like base was made of reinforced concrete.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cylindrical: having the shape of a cylinder
