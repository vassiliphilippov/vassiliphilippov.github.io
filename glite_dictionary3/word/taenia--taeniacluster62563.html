taenia

noun
genus of parasitic tapeworms
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːniə / (AmE), / ˈtiːniə / (BrE)
a genus of parasitic tapeworms, some species of which infect humans, often using pigs as intermediate hosts

Taenia species are responsible for many cases of tapeworm infections in humans worldwide.
