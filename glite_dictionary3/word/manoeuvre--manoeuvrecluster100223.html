manoeuvre

noun
a tactical action to evade an opponent
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːvər /; / məˈnuːvɚ / (AmE), / məˈnuːvə /; / məˈnjuːvə / (BrE)
an action or movement designed to achieve an advantage or specific aim, especially in a tactical, strategic, or competitive situation
