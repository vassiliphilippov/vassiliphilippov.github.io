nonpareil

noun
someone or something without equal
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.pəˈrɛl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pəˈrɛl / (BrE)
a person or thing that is unrivaled or has no equal; a model of excellence or perfection

Examples

nonpareil of talent
His skills as a pianist are truly nonpareil, setting a standard for others to follow.
She is the nonpareil of kindness in our community, admired by everyone who knows her.
The chef’s dessert was a nonpareil, unmatched by anything I've tasted before.

Related Terms

  • antonym – peer: a person who is equal to another in abilities or qualifications
