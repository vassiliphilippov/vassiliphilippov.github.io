kafir

noun
offensive term for Black Africans (South Africa)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːfɪr / (AmE), / ˈkæfɪə / (BrE)
An offensive and insulting term for a Black African, used mainly in South Africa; considered highly offensive and racist.
