burned-out

adjective
no longer working due to heat or friction
Pronunciation: / bɝnd aʊt / (AmE), / bɜːnd aʊt / (BrE)
(of a device or component) no longer functioning because excessive heat or friction has caused damage.

Examples

burned-out lightbulb
After years of use, the television ended up with a burned-out picture tube.
She replaced the burned-out bulb in the living room lamp.
The burned-out motor made it impossible to start the machine.
