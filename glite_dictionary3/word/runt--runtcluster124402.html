runt

noun
small, weak person (disparaging)
Pronunciation: / rʌnt / (AmE), / rʌnt / (BrE)
A person who is unusually small or weak, especially in a way that invites derision or contempt.

Examples

runt of the class
At school, he was often bullied for being the runt of the class.
She may be small, but don’t call her a runt; she's tough as nails.
The taller kids laughed and called him a runt, which hurt his feelings.
