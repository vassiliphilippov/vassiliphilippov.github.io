atomization

noun
breaking into fine particles or droplets
Pronunciation: / ˌædəməˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌætəməˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of breaking something into very fine particles or droplets

Examples

atomization of liquid
Spraying paint involves the atomization of liquid into tiny droplets.
The atomization of fuel improves combustion efficiency in modern engines.
The process of atomization is crucial in the manufacture of metal powders.
