addend

noun
number added to another
Pronunciation: / ˈædˌɛnd / (AmE), / ˈadɛnd / (BrE)
a number that is added to another in a mathematical operation of addition

Examples

addend in sum
In the equation 5 + 3 = 8, both 5 and 3 are addends.
The sum of the addends gives you the total answer.
