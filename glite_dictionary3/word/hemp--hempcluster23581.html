hemp

noun
plant of the genus Cannabis; fiber source
Pronunciation: / hɛmp / (AmE), / hɛmp / (BrE)
A tall, coarse, annual plant of the genus Cannabis, grown for its tough fiber, seeds, and sometimes for the production of narcotic drugs.

Examples

hemp plant
In some countries, hemp is also regulated due to its relationship with cannabis.
Industrial hemp is valued for its strong, natural fibers.
The farmer grew hemp for use in making ropes and textiles.

  • synonym – marijuana: a psychoactive drug from the Cannabis plant
