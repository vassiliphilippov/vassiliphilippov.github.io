to thatch

verb
cover with straw or reeds as a roof
Pronunciation: / θætʃ / (AmE), / θætʃ / (BrE)
to cover a roof or structure with thatch (a layer of straw, reeds, or similar material) to form a roof or covering.

Examples

to thatch a roof
Local craftsmen were hired to thatch the new cottages in the village.
Many traditional houses in the region are still thatched by hand every few decades.
They decided to thatch the hut using reeds collected from the nearby marsh.

  • synonym – roof: to cover or provide with a roof
