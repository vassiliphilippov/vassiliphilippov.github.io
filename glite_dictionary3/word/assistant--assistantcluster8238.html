assistant

noun
digital or AI helper
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / əˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
a digital or AI-powered software agent that performs tasks, answers questions, or provides information for a user.

Examples

virtual assistant app
She asked her assistant to schedule the meeting using the AI calendar.
The company's new assistant answers customer queries by combining knowledge and natural-language understanding.
Before the trip, we taught the assistant our preferences to provide personalized recommendations.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chatbot: a program that simulates conversation with users, often to answer questions or provide support
  • synonym – virtual assistant: software that performs tasks or services for a user, often using AI or automation
