Nicaragua

proper noun
country in Central America
Pronunciation: / ˌnɪkəˈrɑːɡwə / (AmE), / ˌnɪkəˈræɡjuə / (BrE)
A country in Central America, bordered by Honduras to the north and Costa Rica to the south, which became independent from Spain in 1821.
