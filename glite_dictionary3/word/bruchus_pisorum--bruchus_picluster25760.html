Bruchus pisorum

noun
pea weevil; beetle harmful to pea plants
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːkəs paɪˈsɔrəm / (AmE), / ˈbruːkəs paɪˈsɔːrəm / (BrE)
a species of beetle whose larvae live in and feed on the seeds of the pea plant, commonly known as the pea weevil.

Examples

Bruchus pisorum damages peas
Scientists are studying ways to control Bruchus pisorum without using chemical pesticides.
The infestation of Bruchus pisorum caused significant damage to the farmer’s pea crop.
The larvae of Bruchus pisorum develop inside the pea seeds, making detection difficult.
