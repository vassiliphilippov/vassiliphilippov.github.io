while

noun
a short period of time
Pronunciation: / waɪl / (AmE), / waɪl / (BrE)
a period of time of indeterminate but usually short length, marked by some action or condition

Examples

a short while
I’ll be back in a while, so please wait here.
She sat and read for a while before dinner was ready.
We haven’t seen each other for quite a while.
