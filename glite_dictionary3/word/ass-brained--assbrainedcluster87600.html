ass-brained

adjective
very foolish; lacking intelligence
Pronunciation: / æs breɪnd / (AmE), / æs breɪnd / (BrE)
Very foolish or showing a lack of intelligence; acting in a silly or thoughtless way.

Examples

an ass-brained idea
Don't be so ass-brained—think before you act next time.
His ass-brained plan was doomed to fail from the start.
I couldn't believe how ass-brained their excuse sounded in court.
