rachischisis

noun
congenital vertebral defect
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪkaɪˈskɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˌrakɪˈskɪsɪs / (BrE)
a congenital defect in which one or more vertebrae are malformed, sometimes resulting in exposed spinal cord tissue
