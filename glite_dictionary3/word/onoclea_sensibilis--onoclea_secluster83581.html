Onoclea sensibilis

noun
a species of fern (sensitive fern)
Pronunciation: / ˌɑː.nəˈkliː.ə sɛnˈsɪb.ɪ.lɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒ.nəˈkliː.ə sɛnˈsɪb.ɪ.lɪs / (BrE)
