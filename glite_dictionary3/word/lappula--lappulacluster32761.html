Lappula

noun
a genus of plants called stickweed or beggar's lice
Pronunciation: / ləˈpjuːlə / (AmE), / ləˈpjuːlə / (BrE)
a genus of plants in the borage family (Boraginaceae), commonly called stickweed or beggar's lice, with small prickly fruits or seeds that stick to clothing or fur
