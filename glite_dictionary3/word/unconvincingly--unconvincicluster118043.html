unconvincingly

adverb
in an unconvincing manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈvɪnsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈvɪnsɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that fails to persuade or convince someone.

Examples

smile unconvincingly
He smiled unconvincingly when asked about his whereabouts last night.
She tried to explain her lateness unconvincingly, failing to justify the delay.
The witness spoke unconvincingly, and the jury remained doubtful of his testimony.
