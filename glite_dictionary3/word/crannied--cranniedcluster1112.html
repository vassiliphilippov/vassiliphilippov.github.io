crannied

adjective
having small cracks or crevices
Pronunciation: / ˈkrænid / (AmE), / ˈkrænid / (BrE)
(of a surface) having small cracks or crevices; marked by the presence of crannies.

Examples

crannied stone wall
He ran his hand along the crannied surface of the ancient steps.
Moss grew in every crannied corner of the abandoned courtyard.
The old castle had a crannied stone wall that let in beams of light.
