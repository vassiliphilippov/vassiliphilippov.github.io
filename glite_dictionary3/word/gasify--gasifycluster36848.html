to gasify

verb
convert into gas
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡæsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to turn or cause to turn into gas, especially by the application of heat or in a chemical process

Examples

to gasify solid waste
At high temperatures, the organic material will rapidly gasify and release energy.
The waste can be gasified to produce a clean fuel for power generation.
To reduce emissions, factories sometimes gasify coal instead of burning it directly.
