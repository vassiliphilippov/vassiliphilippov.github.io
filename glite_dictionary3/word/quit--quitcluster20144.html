to quit

verb
stop doing something; cease
Pronunciation: / kwɪt / (AmE), / kwɪt / (BrE)
to stop doing or give up something, such as a habit, activity, or pursuit

Examples

to quit smoking

Please quit making so much noise while I’m on the phone.
You should quit worrying about things you can’t control.
After years of smoking, he finally decided to quit for the sake of his health.
Many people find it hard to quit unhealthy habits once they've begun.
She wanted to quit her job and pursue her passion for painting.
