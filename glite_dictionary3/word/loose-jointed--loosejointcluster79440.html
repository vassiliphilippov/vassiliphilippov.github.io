loose-jointed

adjective
loosely articulated or constructed
Pronunciation: / ˈluːs ˌdʒɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˈluːs ˌdʒɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
Loosely articulated or constructed; not firmly joined together.

Examples

loose-jointed toy robot
After years of play, the toy robot became loose-jointed and wobbly.
Her writing style was somewhat loose-jointed, lacking clear structure or flow.
The old sofa was loose-jointed and sagged whenever anyone sat on it.
