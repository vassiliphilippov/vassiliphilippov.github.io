signer

noun
person using sign language
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnə / (BrE)
a person who uses sign language to communicate, especially someone who translates spoken language into sign language for the deaf

Examples

signer translates speech
A signer stood beside the stage, making the concert accessible to everyone.
She became a skilled signer after taking several courses in American Sign Language.
The signer at the conference interpreted every speech for the deaf audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interpreter: a person who translates spoken language into another language, including sign language
