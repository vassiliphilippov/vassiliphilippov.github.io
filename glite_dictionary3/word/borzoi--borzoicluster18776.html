borzoi

noun
large Russian sight hound dog
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔr.zɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɔː.zɔɪ / (BrE)
A large, slender breed of dog with a silky coat, originally bred in Russia for hunting wolves by sight rather than scent.

Examples

a borzoi dog
At the dog show, a borzoi took first place in the hound group.
She adopted a retired borzoi from a local rescue organization.
The borzoi is known for its graceful elegance and gentle nature.
