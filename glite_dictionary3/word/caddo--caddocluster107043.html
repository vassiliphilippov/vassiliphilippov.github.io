Caddo

noun
Native American people of the southern US
Pronunciation: / ˈkædoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkædəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a group of Native American peoples historically inhabiting areas of the southern United States, especially in what are now Louisiana, Arkansas, Texas, and Oklahoma
