window

noun
opening in a wall or screen
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndoʊ /; / ˈwɪn.doʊ /; / ˈwɪnˌdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəʊ /; / ˈwɪn.dəʊ / (BrE)
an opening or transparent panel in a wall, roof, vehicle, or object that lets in light, air, or allows you to see through
