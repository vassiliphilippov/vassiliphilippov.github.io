alto

adjective
second highest instrument in a group
Pronunciation: / ˈæltoʊ / (AmE), / ˈaltəʊ / (BrE)
(of a musical instrument) designating the second highest member of a family of similar instruments.

Examples

alto clarinet
He switched from the alto clarinet to the tenor clarinet last year.
She plays the alto saxophone in the jazz band.
The ensemble included an alto recorder to add depth to the harmony.
