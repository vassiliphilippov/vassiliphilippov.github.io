corpulent

adjective
excessively fat; bulky
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrpjələnt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpjʊlənt / (BrE)
(of a person) having a large, bulky body; excessively fat.

Examples

corpulent man
Despite his corpulent appearance, he was surprisingly agile on the dance floor.
Doctors advised the corpulent patient to adopt a healthier lifestyle.
The corpulent gentleman struggled to squeeze into the theater seat.
