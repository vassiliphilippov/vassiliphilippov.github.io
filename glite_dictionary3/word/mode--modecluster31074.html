mode

noun
grammatical category expressing speaker's attitude
Pronunciation: / moʊd / (AmE), / məʊd / (BrE)
(Grammar) a category of verb forms that expresses the manner in which an action or state is conceived by the speaker, such as indicative, subjunctive, or imperative.

Examples

subjunctive mode
In French, each verb tense has a specific mode that affects its meaning.
The subjunctive mode is rarely used in everyday English.
Verbs can be inflected to show the mode of the sentence, such as imperative or indicative.
