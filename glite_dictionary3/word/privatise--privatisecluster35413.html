to privatise

verb
transfer to private ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪvətʌɪz / (BrE)
to transfer a business, industry, or service from public (governmental) to private ownership and control

Examples

to privatise a company
Many workers were worried about job security when the company was privatised.
Some argue that it is not always beneficial to privatise public services.
The government decided to privatise several major utilities to encourage competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nationalise: transfer from private to government ownership
  • synonym – privatize: transfer from public to private ownership (spelling variant, mainly US)
  • synonym – publicise: to make public, share with the public (different meaning but similar form)
