Oenanthe aquatica

noun
European poisonous herb (water dropwort)
Pronunciation: / oʊˈɛnænθi əˈkwɑːtɪkə / (AmE), / əʊˈɛnænθi ɔːˈkwætɪkə / (BrE)
A European poisonous herb with fibrous roots, commonly found in wet places; also known as water dropwort.
