clearance

noun
official permission to proceed
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪr.əns / (AmE), / ˈklɪə.rəns / (BrE)
official authorization or permission to proceed, especially to enter or leave a restricted area or to take a particular action

Examples

customs clearance process
Customs can only release the goods after proper clearance has been granted.
The pilot waited for clearance from air traffic control before beginning the takeoff.
We need security clearance to access the restricted area.

Related Terms

  • synonym – authorization: official permission or approval
  • synonym – permit: an official document giving someone authorization to do something
  • antonym – refusal: denial of permission to do something
