to scull

verb
propel a boat with oars (sculls)
Pronunciation: / skʌl / (AmE), / skʌl / (BrE)
to propel a boat using one or two oars, especially by moving them from side to side

Examples

to scull a boat
He learned how to scull by watching experienced rowers.
She expertly sculled the narrow boat down the river.
We took turns sculling as we crossed the calm lake.
