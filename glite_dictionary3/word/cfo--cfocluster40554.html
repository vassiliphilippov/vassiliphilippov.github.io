CFO

noun
humorous title for person in charge of office fun
Pronunciation: / si \u025bf o\u028a / (AmE), / si\u02d0 \u025bf \u0259\u028a / (BrE)
informal, humorous title for a person (often in a team or small company) responsible for organizing social activities, boosting morale, or managing workplace culture rather than finances.

Examples

CFO plans parties
The startup appointed a CFO to organize weekly game nights and surprise treats for staff.
As their CFO, Jenna planned team retreats, birthday celebrations, and morale workshops every quarter.
Nobody expected the company's CFO to lead morning yoga and free pizza Fridays.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Chief Fun Officer: a joking or informal title for someone who organizes social events and morale activities
  • synonym – Chief Happiness Officer: an informal title for someone focused on employee wellbeing and workplace culture
