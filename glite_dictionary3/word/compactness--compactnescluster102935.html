compactness

noun
the state of being closely packed
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpæktnəs /; / kəmˈpækt.nəs / (AmE), / kəmˈpæktnəs /; / kəmˈpaktnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being closely and neatly packed together; the state of being dense or crowded together
