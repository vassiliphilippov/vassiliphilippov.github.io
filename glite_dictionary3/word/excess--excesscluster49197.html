excess

noun
state of being more than needed or allowed
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛs, ˈɛkˌsɛs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛs, ˈɛk.ses / (BrE)
an amount that is more than necessary, permitted, or desirable; too much of something

Examples

in excess of limits
