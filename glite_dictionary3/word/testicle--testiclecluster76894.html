testicle

noun
male reproductive gland
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪkəl / (BrE)
Either of the two oval organs in males that produce sperm and secrete male sex hormones.

Examples

swollen testicle
Injury to a testicle can be extremely painful and may require medical attention.
One testicle is sometimes lower than the other, which is completely normal.
