spammer

noun
person who sends unwanted emails
Pronunciation: / ˈspæmər / (AmE), / ˈspæmə / (BrE)
a person who sends unsolicited or unwanted emails, especially in large quantities

Examples

email spammer
As a spammer, he sent thousands of unwanted advertisements every day.
Many internet users receive daily messages from at least one persistent spammer.
The company installed filters to block any emails from a known spammer.
