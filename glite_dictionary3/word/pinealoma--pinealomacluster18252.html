pinealoma

noun
tumor of the pineal gland
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪniəˈloʊmə / (AmE), / ˌpɪniːəˈləʊmə / (BrE)
A tumor arising from the pineal gland in the brain.

Examples

A pinealoma may lead to hormonal disturbances if the pineal gland's function is affected.
Surgical removal of a pinealoma can be complex due to its location in the brain.
