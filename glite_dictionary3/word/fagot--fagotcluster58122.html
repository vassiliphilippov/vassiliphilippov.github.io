fagot

noun
bundle of sticks or branches
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡət / (AmE), / ˈfæɡət / (BrE)
a bundle of sticks or branches bound together, usually for use as fuel

Examples

a fagot of sticks
In medieval times, a fagot was often used to kindle large fires.
