to mind

verb
be in charge of; look after
Pronunciation: / maɪnd / (AmE), / maɪnd / (BrE)
to be in charge of or take care of someone or something; to deal with or look after

Examples

to mind the children
Could you mind the babies while I go shopping?
He was asked to mind the shop while the manager was away.
Would you mind my luggage for a moment?

Related Terms

  • synonym – look after: to take care of; to be responsible for someone or something
  • antonym – neglect: to fail to care for something properly
