hydrant

noun
above-ground water outlet pipe
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdrənt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrənt / (BrE)
a pipe with a valve and spout, usually above ground, for drawing water from a main, especially for fighting fires
