trifler

noun
one who treats things unseriously
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪflər / (AmE), / ˈtraɪflə / (BrE)
a person who treats serious matters in a trivial or unserious way; someone who behaves without seriousness

Examples

careless trifler
His colleagues viewed him as a trifler, always joking when they needed seriousness.
No trifler would endure such hardships for the sake of a hobby.
She dismissed him as a mere trifler who could not be trusted with important tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dabbler: a person who engages in an activity in a superficial way
  • antonym – serious person: someone who behaves earnestly and sincerely
