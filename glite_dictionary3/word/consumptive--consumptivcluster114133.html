consumptive

noun
person with pulmonary tuberculosis
Pronunciation: / kənˈsʌmptɪv / (AmE), / kənˈsʌmptɪv / (BrE)
a person suffering from pulmonary tuberculosis, especially as a historical or literary term

Examples

a consumptive patient
He was a frail consumptive, coughing persistently and struggling to regain his health.
In many Victorian novels, the tragic fate of the consumptive heroine is a common theme.
The sanatorium provided special care for each consumptive admitted in the early 1900s.
