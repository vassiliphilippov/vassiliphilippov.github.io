crackpot

noun
eccentric or crazy person
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈkrækˌpɒt / (BrE)
a person who is considered eccentric, unconventional, or somewhat crazy, especially in their ideas or behavior

The internet is full of crackpot theories about aliens and conspiracies.
