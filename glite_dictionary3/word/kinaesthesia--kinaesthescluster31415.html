kinaesthesia

noun
sense of body movement and position
Pronunciation: / ˌkɪn.əsˈθiː.ʒə /; / kɪn.iˈθiː.ʒə / (AmE), / ˌkɪn.ɪsˈθiː.zi.ə /; / kɪn.iˈθiːz.i.ə / (BrE)
