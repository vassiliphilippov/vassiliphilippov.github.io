Richard Adolf Zsigmondy

proper noun
Austrian-born German chemist, Nobel laureate
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃərd ˈædɑlf ˈtsiːɡˌmɔndi / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃəd ˈadɒlf ˈtsiːɡˌmɒndi / (BrE)
(1865–1929) An Austrian-born German chemist renowned for his research on colloidal solutions, for which he was awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1925.
