aesthetically

adverb
in a way relating to beauty
Pronunciation: / ɛˈsθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / iːsˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty; tastefully.

Examples

aesthetically pleasing design
She decorated her garden aesthetically, choosing plants that complemented each other.
The film was aesthetically stunning, with breathtaking visuals throughout.
The room was aesthetically designed to create a sense of calm.
