roughdried

adjective
laundry dried but not ironed
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfˌdraɪd / (AmE), / ˈrʌfˌdraɪd / (BrE)
(of laundry) dried after washing but not ironed or finished.

Examples

roughdried laundry
