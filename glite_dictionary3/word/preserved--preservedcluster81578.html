preserved

adjective
kept in original or good condition
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɝvd / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːvd / (BrE)
kept in its original state or in good condition, often by preventing decay, damage, or change (especially by special treatment)

Examples

preserved fruit
