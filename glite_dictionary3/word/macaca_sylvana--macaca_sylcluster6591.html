Macaca sylvana

noun
a tailless macaque of North Africa and Gibraltar
Pronunciation: / məˈkɑːkə sɪlˈvænə / (AmE), / məˈkɑːkə sɪlˈvɑːnə / (BrE)
A species of tailless macaque found on rocky cliffs and in forests in northwestern Africa and Gibraltar, also known as the Barbary macaque.
