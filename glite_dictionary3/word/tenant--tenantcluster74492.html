tenant

noun
client organization in multi‑tenant cloud
Pronunciation: / \u02c8t\u025bn\u0259nt / (AmE), / \u02c8t\u025bn\u0259nt / (BrE)
(in computing, especially cloud or SaaS) a client organization or logical customer account that shares the same software or infrastructure with others but has its data and settings kept separate.

Examples

tenant uses software
In cloud software, a tenant is an organization that shares infrastructure but keeps its data isolated from others.
Our SaaS platform gives each tenant a separate database and access controls to protect customer data.
After onboarding, the IT team configured the new tenant with custom settings and security policies.
