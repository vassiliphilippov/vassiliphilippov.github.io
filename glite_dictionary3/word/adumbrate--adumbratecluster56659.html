to adumbrate

verb
describe or outline briefly
Pronunciation: / ˈæd.əmˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ˈad.ʌm.breɪt / (BrE)
to describe or outline something briefly or in a general way; to give a summary of the main points.

Examples

to adumbrate main points
He attempted to adumbrate his proposal before the details were finalized.
The historian sought to adumbrate the causes of the conflict in her lecture.
The introduction will adumbrate the topics covered in the rest of the book.

Related Terms

  • synonym – outline: give the main features or general idea of something
  • synonym – summarize: give a brief statement of the main points
