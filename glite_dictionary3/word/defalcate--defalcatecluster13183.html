to defalcate

verb
to embezzle funds entrusted to one
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfælk.eɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈfæl.keɪt / (BrE)
to embezzle or misappropriate funds or property entrusted to one's care

Examples

to defalcate company funds
Anyone who attempts to defalcate public money commits a serious crime.
Auditors discovered she had defalcated a significant sum over several years.
The manager was charged when he tried to defalcate funds from the company account.
