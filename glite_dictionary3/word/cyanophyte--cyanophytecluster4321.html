cyanophyte

adjective
relating to cyanobacteria
Pronunciation: / saɪˈænəˌfaɪt / (AmE), / saɪˈanəˌfaɪt / (BrE)
relating to or caused by photosynthetic bacteria of the class Cyanobacteria.

Examples

cyanophyte bacteria
Researchers are studying cyanophyte pigments for use in biotechnology.
Some cyanophyte toxins are harmful to both wildlife and humans.
The rapid growth of cyanophyte blooms can threaten aquatic ecosystems.
