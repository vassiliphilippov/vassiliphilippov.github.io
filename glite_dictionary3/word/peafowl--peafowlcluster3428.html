peafowl

noun
large ornamental Asian pheasant
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːˌfaʊl / (AmE), / ˈpiːˌfaʊl / (BrE)
A very large, terrestrial pheasant native to South and Southeast Asia, known for its ornamental value and vividly colored plumage, especially in males.

Examples

peafowl with bright feathers
Many zoos keep peafowl for visitors to admire their exquisite plumage.
The majestic peafowl strutted across the palace gardens, displaying its vibrant tail.
Wild peafowl can sometimes be seen in rural parts of India and Sri Lanka.

Related Terms

  • synonym – peacock: the male peafowl, noted for its colorful tail
  • synonym – peahen: the female peafowl, usually brown or grey
