sawbill

noun
a fish-eating duck with a serrated bill
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːˌbɪl / (BrE)
A large diving duck with a slender, hooked bill that has serrated edges, adapted for catching fish; typically refers to merganser species.
