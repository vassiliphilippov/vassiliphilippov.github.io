sloppy

adjective
loose-fitting (especially clothing)
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːpi / (AmE), / ˈslɒpi / (BrE)
(of clothing) loose-fitting or hanging loosely, not closely fitting the body.

Examples

sloppy clothes
His clothes looked a bit sloppy, but he felt entirely comfortable.
She prefers to wear sloppy sweaters on cold evenings at home.
The fashion trend this season includes wide, sloppy pants and oversized shirts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – baggy: (of clothing) loose and hanging in folds
  • antonym – tight: closely fitting the body
