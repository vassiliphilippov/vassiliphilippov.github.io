nonautonomous

adjective
not self-governing; under external control
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔnˌɔˈtɑnəməs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɔːˈtɒnəməs / (BrE)
(of people or political bodies) not having the power to govern themselves; controlled by outside forces.

Examples

nonautonomous region
Minority groups feared a nonautonomous status within the proposed federation.
Some territories are nonautonomous and rely heavily on external administration.
The region remained nonautonomous under colonial rule for decades.
