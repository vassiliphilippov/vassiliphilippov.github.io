response

noun
reply to a question or criticism
Pronunciation: / rəˈspɑns /; / rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
something that is said, written, or done as a reply or reaction to a question, situation, or event

Examples

response to a question
He gave a thoughtful response when asked about his future plans.
The company issued a detailed response to the allegations.
We are still awaiting her response to our invitation.
A thoughtful response can keep the conversation engaging and productive.
He waited for her response before continuing the discussion.
Her quick response to the question impressed the panel of judges.
He was disappointed by the hostile response to his honest remarks.
Her proposal received a surprisingly enthusiastic response from the committee.
The celebrity did not expect the lukewarm response from fans at the event.
