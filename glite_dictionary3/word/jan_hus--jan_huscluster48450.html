Jan Hus

proper noun
Czech religious reformer (1372–1415)
Pronunciation: / jɑn hus / (AmE), / jæn hʌs / (BrE)
(c. 1372–1415) A Czech religious reformer who criticized the Catholic Church and was executed for heresy; considered a precursor to the Protestant Reformation.

Examples

Jan Hus religious reformer
Jan Hus is remembered as a national hero in the Czech Republic for his role in religious reform.
