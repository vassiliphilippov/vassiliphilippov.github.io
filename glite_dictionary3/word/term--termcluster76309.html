term

noun
a fixed or limited period of time
Pronunciation: / tɝm / (AmE), / tɜːm / (BrE)
a fixed or limited period of time during which something, such as a school session, political office, or prison sentence, lasts

Examples

school term ends

The judge gave him a five-year term in prison for the crime.
The president’s term of office will end next year.
