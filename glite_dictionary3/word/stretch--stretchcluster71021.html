stretch

noun
capacity to be stretched; elasticity
Pronunciation: / strɛtʃ / (AmE), / strɛtʃ / (BrE)
the ability of a material or object to be stretched without breaking; elasticity

Examples

stretch in fabric
Athletic wear often contains materials with extra stretch for greater flexibility during exercise.
Because of its high stretch, this rubber band can hold thick stacks of paper together.
The fabric has good stretch, making the shirt comfortable to wear.
