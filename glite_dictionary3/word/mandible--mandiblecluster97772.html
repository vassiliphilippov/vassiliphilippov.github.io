mandible

noun
lower jawbone in vertebrates
Pronunciation: / ˈmændəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmændɪbəl / (BrE)
the lower jawbone in vertebrates, especially the bone that is hinged so the mouth can open and close

Examples

human mandible
A broken mandible often requires surgery to heal properly.
The human mandible supports the lower teeth and plays a key role in chewing.
The lion's powerful mandible helps it grasp and kill its prey.
