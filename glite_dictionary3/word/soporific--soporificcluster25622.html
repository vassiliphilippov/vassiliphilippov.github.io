soporific

adjective
inducing sleepiness or mental dullness
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑpəˈrɪfɪk /; / sɑːpəˈrɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɒpəˈrɪfɪk /; / sɒpəˈrɪfɪk / (BrE)
causing sleepiness or making someone feel sleepy or drowsy

Examples

soporific effect

This medication can have a soporific effect if taken in large doses.
