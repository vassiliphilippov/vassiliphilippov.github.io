internuncio

noun
minor papal diplomatic representative
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈnʌnsiˌoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnʌnsɪəʊ / (BrE)
A diplomatic representative of the Pope in the Roman Catholic Church, ranking below a nuncio.

Examples

Papal internuncio appointment
As internuncio, he mediated between the Vatican and the local government.
The newly appointed internuncio was welcomed by clergy and dignitaries alike.
The role of an internuncio is less prominent than that of a nuncio but remains important in church diplomacy.
