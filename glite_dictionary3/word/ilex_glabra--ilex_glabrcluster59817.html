Ilex glabra

noun
a holly species native to eastern North America
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪlɛks ˈɡleɪbrə / (AmE), / ˈaɪlɛks ˈɡleɪbrə / (BrE)
An evergreen holly native to eastern North America, with oblong leathery leaves and small black berries, commonly known as inkberry.
