to pose

verb
assume a posture, especially for art or photos
Pronunciation: / poʊz / (AmE), / pəʊz / (BrE)
to assume a particular position or posture, especially for an artist, photographer, or modeler

Examples

to pose for a photo
He had to pose very still for the sculpture to be accurate.
Several students volunteered to pose for the college's promotional photos.
The artist asked her to pose with a thoughtful expression for the portrait.

Related Terms

  • synonym – model: to sit or stand for an artist or photographer to create an image
