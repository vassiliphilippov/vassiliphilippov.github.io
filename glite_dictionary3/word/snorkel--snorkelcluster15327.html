to snorkel

verb
to swim or dive with a snorkel
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɔrkəl / (AmE), / ˈsnɔːkəl / (BrE)
to swim or dive just below the surface of water while breathing through a snorkel

Examples

to snorkel in water
Many tourists snorkel in these clear waters every summer.
She snorkeled for hours, watching colorful fish pass by beneath her.
We plan to snorkel along the coral reef this afternoon.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – dive: to jump or plunge into water, especially head first
  • synonym – scuba dive: to swim underwater using self-contained breathing apparatus
