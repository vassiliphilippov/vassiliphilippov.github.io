victualler

noun
supplier of food or provisions
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪtələr / (AmE), / ˈvɪtələ / (BrE)
a person or company that supplies food and other provisions, especially to an army or a ship

Examples

victualler supplies ships
Centuries ago, being a victualler was a vital trade in port towns.
During the campaign, the army depended on a victualler for fresh bread and meat.
The victualler delivered barrels of salted pork to the navy ship every week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – purveyor: a person or company that supplies goods, especially food
