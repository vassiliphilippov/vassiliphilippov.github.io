cyclicity

noun
the quality of occurring in cycles
Pronunciation: / saɪˈklɪs.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / sɪˈklɪs.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of recurring in cycles or at regular intervals

Examples

economic cyclicity
His research focuses on the cyclicity present in economic growth patterns.
Scientists observed the cyclicity of sunspot activity over decades.
The cyclicity of the seasons shapes agricultural activities around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym – recurrence: the act of recurring or happening again
