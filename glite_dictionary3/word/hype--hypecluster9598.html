to hype

verb
promote in an exaggerated way
Pronunciation: / haɪp / (AmE), / haɪp / (BrE)
to promote or publicize something in an exaggerated or misleading way, often generating more excitement than is justified

Examples

to hype a new product
Critics accused the studio of hyping the movie far beyond its actual quality.
He hyped his skills during the interview, which made him seem more qualified than he really was.
The company decided to hype the new smartphone with flashy ads and celebrity endorsements.

  • synonym – promote: encourage or support the publicity of something
