hair-splitting

noun
making overly fine distinctions
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌsplɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌsplɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making overly fine or trivial distinctions, especially in argument or analysis

Examples

endless hair-splitting arguments

I wish people would stop hair-splitting and focus on the real issues.
The meeting deteriorated into hair-splitting about insignificant points.
