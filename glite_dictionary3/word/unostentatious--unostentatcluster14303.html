unostentatious

adjective
not showy; modest or restrained
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌɑːstɛnˈteɪʃəs /; / ˌʌn.ˌɑː.stɛnˈteɪ.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃəs /; / ˌʌn.ˌɒs.tɛnˈteɪ.ʃəs / (BrE)
not showy, pretentious, or designed to attract attention; modest, simple, or restrained
