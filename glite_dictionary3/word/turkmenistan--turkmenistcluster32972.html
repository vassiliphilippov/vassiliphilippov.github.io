Turkmenistan

proper noun
country in Central Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌtɝːkmɛnɪˈstæn / (AmE), / ˌtɜːkmɛnɪˈstɑːn / (BrE)
A country in Central Asia, east of the Caspian Sea, north of Iran, and south of Kazakhstan; formerly a Soviet republic (1925–1991).
