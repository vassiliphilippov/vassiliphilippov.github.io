complaint

noun
medical condition or disorder
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpleɪnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪnt / (BrE)
a medical condition, disorder, or illness, especially one that persists or recurs; a cause for expressing discomfort or distress

Examples

Arthritis is a common complaint among older adults.
