to constipate

verb
cause to be constipated
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.stəˌpeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.stɪ.peɪt / (BrE)
to cause someone to become unable to easily pass stools or to become constipated

Examples

to constipate the body
Eating too much cheese may constipate you if you're not careful.
Some medications can constipate patients, so doctors often recommend a high-fiber diet.
The lack of water in his diet began to constipate him after a few days.
