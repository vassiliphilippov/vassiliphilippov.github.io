Bedouin

noun
member of a nomadic Arab tribe
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛduɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.u.ɪn / (BrE)
a member of a traditionally nomadic Arab tribe, especially found in the deserts of North Africa, Arabia, and the Middle East

Examples

Bedouin tribe
A group of Bedouin set up tents at the edge of the oasis.
Many Bedouin today still maintain some aspects of their traditional nomadic lifestyle.
The Bedouin have adapted their way of life to survive in harsh desert environments.
