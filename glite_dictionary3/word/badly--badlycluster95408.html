badly

adverb
with distress or emotional upset
Pronunciation: / ˈbædli / (AmE), / ˈbadli / (BrE)
In a way that shows great distress, resentment, regret, or an emotional reaction.

Examples

to take badly
After hearing the news, she cried badly for hours.
He took the criticism badly and refused to speak to anyone.
The children reacted badly when their pet disappeared.
