mittelschmerz

noun
ovulation-related abdominal pain
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪtl̩ˌʃmɜrts / (AmE), / ˈmɪtl̩ˌʃmɜːts / (BrE)
A pain in the lower abdomen, typically experienced in the area of the ovary at the time of ovulation, midway through the menstrual cycle.

Examples

mittelschmerz during ovulation
