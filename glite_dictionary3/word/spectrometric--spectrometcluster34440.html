spectrometric

adjective
relating to spectrometry
Pronunciation: / ˌspɛktrəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌspɛktrəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or involving spectrometry, the measurement of spectra produced when matter interacts with or emits electromagnetic radiation.

Examples

spectrometric analysis
Accurate spectrometric measurements are essential in modern chemical research.
She published her findings after conducting spectrometric experiments on various minerals.
The spectrometric analysis revealed the chemical composition of the unknown sample.
