to proofread

verb
check written work for errors
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːfˌriːd / (AmE), / ˈpruːfˌriːd / (BrE)
to read written material and correct mistakes in spelling, grammar, or typing before it is printed or published

Examples

to proofread a document
Before the article goes to print, someone needs to proofread it for accuracy.
I always proofread my essays before submitting them to avoid simple mistakes.
She proofread the report carefully to ensure there were no typos or grammatical errors.
