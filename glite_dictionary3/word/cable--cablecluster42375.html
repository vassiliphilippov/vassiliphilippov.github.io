to cable

verb
send a message by telegraph or cable
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.bəl / (BrE)
to send a message by telegraph or submarine cable; to telegraph.

Examples

to cable news
He cabled his congratulations to the winning team from overseas.
She promised to cable news of her safe arrival as soon as she reached London.
We will cable you the results as soon as we receive them.
