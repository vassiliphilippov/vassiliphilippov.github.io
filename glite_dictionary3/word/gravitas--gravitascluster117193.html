gravitas

noun
seriousness; dignified presence
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrævɪˌtɑs / (AmE), / ˈɡravɪtas / (BrE)
seriousness and dignity in manner or appearance; a formal and respectable presence.

Examples

gravitas of a leader
Despite his youth, he carried himself with the gravitas of a seasoned leader.
She delivered her speech with remarkable gravitas, impressing everyone in the audience.
The judge's gravitas lent authority to the courtroom proceedings.
