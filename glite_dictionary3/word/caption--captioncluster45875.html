to caption

verb
add a title or explanation to an image
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpʃən / (AmE), / ˈkæpʃən / (BrE)
to provide a short title or explanation for a photograph, drawing, or other illustration

Examples

to caption a photo
She carefully captioned each photo in her travel album to remember the details.
The editor asked me to caption all the images in the article before publication.
We spent all afternoon captioning pictures for the company’s new website.
