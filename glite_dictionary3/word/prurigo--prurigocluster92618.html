prurigo

noun
chronic itchy skin disease
Pronunciation: / pruˈraɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / pruˈriːɡəʊ / (BrE)
A chronic inflammatory disease of the skin characterized by itchy, blister-capped papules.

Examples

chronic prurigo rash
Chronic prurigo can significantly affect a person's quality of life due to the intense itching.
Effective treatment for prurigo often includes topical steroids and antihistamines.
The patient was diagnosed with prurigo after presenting with persistent itchy bumps on her skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – eczema: an inflammatory skin disorder causing itchy, red, and scaly patches
