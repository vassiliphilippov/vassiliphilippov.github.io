trunk

noun
main stem of a tree
Pronunciation: / trʌŋk / (AmE), / trʌŋk / (BrE)
the main woody stem of a tree, typically covered with bark and supporting the branches and leaves

Examples

tree trunk
Moss and fungi grew along the base of the trunk.
The logger measured the trunk before preparing to cut it for lumber.
The old oak's trunk was thick and deeply grooved with age.
