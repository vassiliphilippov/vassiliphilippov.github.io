infielder

noun
baseball/softball infield player
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌfiːldər / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌfiːldə / (BrE)
A player who occupies a defensive position in the infield in baseball or softball.

Examples

baseball infielder
Many infielders are known for their quick reflexes and strong arms.
She started her career as an infielder before switching to the outfield.
