hob

noun
troublesome folklore creature
Pronunciation: / hɑb / (AmE), / hɒb / (BrE)
in folklore, a small, mischievous or troublesome fairy or goblin

Examples

According to the old tales, a hob would hide in the kitchen and play tricks on the family.
Folklore describes a hob as both a nuisance and sometimes a reluctant helper.
