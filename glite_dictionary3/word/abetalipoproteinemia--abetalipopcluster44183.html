abetalipoproteinemia

noun
rare inherited fat metabolism disorder
Pronunciation: / əˌbiːtəˌlaɪpoʊˌproʊtiˈniːmiə / (AmE), / əˌbiːtəˌlɪpəʊˌprəʊtiˈniːmiə / (BrE)
A rare inherited disorder of fat metabolism resulting in a severe deficiency of beta-lipoproteins, abnormal red blood cells (acanthocytes), and very low cholesterol levels.

Examples

abetalipoproteinemia affects fat
Doctors must monitor cholesterol levels closely when treating abetalipoproteinemia.
Genetic counseling was recommended for families affected by abetalipoproteinemia.
The patient was diagnosed with abetalipoproteinemia after showing symptoms of fat malabsorption and anemia.
