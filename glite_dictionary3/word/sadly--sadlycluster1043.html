sadly

adverb
in an unfortunate way
Pronunciation: / ˈsædli / (AmE), / ˈsadli / (BrE)
unfortunately; used to express regret or disappointment about a situation

Examples

sadly true

The event was sadly underfunded, leading to many cancellations.
The playground was sadly neglected by the local council.
