constructively

adverb
in a helpful or productive way
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrʌktɪvli / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is useful, helpful, or intended to improve or develop something.

Examples

to discuss constructively
Even in disagreement, it's important to communicate constructively to reach a positive outcome.
She offered her feedback constructively, focusing on solutions rather than just pointing out problems.
The teacher encouraged the students to criticize each other's work constructively during the workshop.

Related Terms

  • antonym – destructively: in a way that causes damage or harm
