phrenological

adjective
relating to phrenology (skull shape and personality)
Pronunciation: / frɛnəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / frɛnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of phrenology, the pseudoscientific study of skull shape as an indicator of mental faculties and character

Examples

phrenological theory
He gave a phrenological analysis of the politician's character, relying on the shape of his skull.
Museum exhibits include several phrenological busts once used to demonstrate this outdated science.
The phrenological approach has been thoroughly discredited, yet it fascinated 19th-century thinkers.
