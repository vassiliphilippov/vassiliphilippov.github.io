unconsecrated

adjective
not made holy or dedicated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈkɑːnsəˌkreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈkɒnsɪˌkreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been made holy or officially dedicated for religious use; not consecrated.

Examples

unconsecrated ground
He refused to use the unconsecrated bread in the ceremony.
The coffin was buried in an unconsecrated part of the cemetery.
The old chapel stands on unconsecrated ground.
