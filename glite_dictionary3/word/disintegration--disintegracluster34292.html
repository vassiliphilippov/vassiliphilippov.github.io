disintegration

noun
process of losing organization
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˌtɪɡrəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˌtɪɡrəˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of losing cohesion or organization within a system or structure

Examples

disintegration of society
After the scandal, there was a noticeable disintegration of trust among the team members.
Scientists warned about the possible disintegration of the ecosystem due to pollution.
The sudden collapse of leadership caused a slow disintegration of the organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fragmentation: the process of breaking into small separate parts
  • antonym – integration: the process of combining or incorporating into a whole
