multi-coloured

adjective
having many different colours
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkʌləd / (BrE)
Having sections or patches in two or more different, often bright, colours.

Examples

multi-coloured balloons
Children love multi-coloured balloons at birthday parties.
The artist wore a multi-coloured scarf during the festival.
The garden was full of multi-coloured flowers in early spring.
