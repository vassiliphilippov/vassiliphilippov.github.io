stonechat

noun
a European songbird with a reddish-brown breast
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊnˌtʃæt / (AmE), / ˈstəʊnˌtʃæt / (BrE)
a small European songbird with black plumage and a reddish-brown breast, commonly found in open country

Examples

stonechat singing on bush
A stonechat perched on the fence, flicking its tail in the early morning sun.
The call of the stonechat sounds like two stones being tapped together.
We spotted a male stonechat with its distinctive black head and rusty breast.
