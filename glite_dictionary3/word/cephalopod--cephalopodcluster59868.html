cephalopod

noun
marine mollusk with tentacles (e.g., octopus, squid)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛfələˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈsɛfələˌpɒd / (BrE)
A marine mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, characterized by a well-developed head, large eyes, and tentacles with suckers, such as an octopus or squid.
