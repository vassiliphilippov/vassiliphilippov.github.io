tropicbird

noun
tropical white seabird with long tail
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑːpɪkˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkˌbɜːd / (BrE)
A mostly white tropical seabird with webbed feet and long tail streamers, often found far from land.

Examples

soaring tropicbird over ocean
A tropicbird can travel great distances across the open ocean in search of food.
Several tropicbirds were spotted nesting on the remote island's cliffside.
The tropicbird soared gracefully above the turquoise waves, its long tail streaming behind.
