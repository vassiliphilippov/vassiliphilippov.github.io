Alces alces

noun
scientific name for moose/European elk
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.siːz ˈæl.siːz / (AmE), / ˈæl.siːz ˈæl.siːz / (BrE)
the scientific name for a large northern deer species with broad, flattened antlers in males; known as the moose in North America and the elk in Europe
