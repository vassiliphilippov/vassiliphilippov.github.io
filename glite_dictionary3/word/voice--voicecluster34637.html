voice

noun
a sound resembling a vocal utterance
Pronunciation: / vɔɪs / (AmE), / vɔɪs / (BrE)
a sound or characteristic noise made by something that is not human, or a computer-generated speaking sound

Examples

voice of the bell
Each bell in the cathedral had its own distinct voice in the morning air.
I listened to the voice of the river rushing past the rocks.
The voice of the thunder filled the night sky.
The smart speaker's voice sounded natural and reassuring during the morning routine.
She changed the navigation system's voice to a male-sounding option for clearer directions.
Developers tested a new voice for the app to improve accessibility for blind users.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sound: a particular auditory impression; an audible noise
  • synonym – speech synthesis: the process or technology of generating artificial speech from text or phonetic input
  • synonym – text-to-speech: technology that converts written text into spoken audio using synthesized voices
