Mandarin

noun
the official standard form of Chinese in China
Pronunciation: / ˈmændərɪn / (AmE), / ˈmændərɪn / (BrE)
the official standard form of Chinese, based on the Beijing dialect, and spoken as the national language of China

Examples

Mandarin language class
Many schools now offer Mandarin classes to help students learn the official language of China.
She is fluent in Mandarin and often acts as a translator during business meetings.
Watching Chinese films in Mandarin has improved my listening skills considerably.
