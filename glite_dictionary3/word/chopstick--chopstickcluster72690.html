chopstick

noun
slender eating stick used in pairs
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑp.stɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒp.stɪk / (BrE)
one of a pair of slender sticks, usually made of wood, plastic, or metal, used as an eating utensil, especially in East Asian cultures.

Examples

use chopsticks to eat
He struggled to pick up the slippery noodles with his chopsticks.
I bought a beautiful pair of lacquered chopsticks as a souvenir from China.
Many people in Japan use chopsticks instead of forks and knives.
