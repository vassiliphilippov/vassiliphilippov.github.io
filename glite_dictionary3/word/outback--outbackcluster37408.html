outback

noun
remote inland regions of Australia
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈaʊtbæk / (BrE)
the remote and sparsely populated inland regions of Australia, especially the bush country far from urban centers

Examples

the outback of Australia
Life in the outback can be harsh due to its extreme climate and isolation.
Many people dream of exploring the vast outback on an adventurous road trip.
She worked at a cattle station deep in the Australian outback.
