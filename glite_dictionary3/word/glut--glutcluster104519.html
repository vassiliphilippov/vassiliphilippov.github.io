to glut

verb
eat or drink to excess
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌt / (AmE), / ɡlʌt / (BrE)
to eat or consume food or drink in excess; to overindulge oneself

Examples

to glut on food
After the long hike, they glutted on the feast laid out before them.
He would often glut himself on sweets during the holidays.
It is unwise to glut on rich foods if you want to remain healthy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gorge: to eat a large amount greedily
  • antonym – starve: to suffer or die from lack of food
  • synonym – stuff: to eat until one is full or beyond
