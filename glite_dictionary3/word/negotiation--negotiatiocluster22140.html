negotiation

noun
process of reaching an agreement by discussion
Pronunciation: / nəˌgoʊʃiˈeɪʃən /; / nɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / nɪˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of discussing something in order to reach an agreement, especially in business or politics
