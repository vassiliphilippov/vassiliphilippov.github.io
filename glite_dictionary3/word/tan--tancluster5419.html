to tan

verb
become brown or darkened by the sun
Pronunciation: / tæn / (AmE), / tæn / (BrE)
(of a person's skin) to become brown or darker after exposure to the sun or wind.

Examples

to tan at the beach
If you tan too long, you risk damaging your skin.
Many people tan easily in the summer sun.
She spent the afternoon at the beach to tan for her vacation.
