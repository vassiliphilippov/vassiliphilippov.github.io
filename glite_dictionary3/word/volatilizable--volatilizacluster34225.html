volatilizable

adjective
capable of being volatilized
Pronunciation: / voʊˈlætəˌlaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / vəˈlatɪˌlaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
(of a substance) capable of being converted into a vapor or gas, especially by the action of heat.

Examples

volatilizable chemical compound
Certain chemicals in the laboratory are volatilizable when heated.
Only volatilizable substances are measured by this particular test.
The volatilizable components of the sample must be handled with care to prevent loss.
