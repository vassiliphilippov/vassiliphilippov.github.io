eyehole

noun
small hole for looking through
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a small hole in a door, mask, or other object through which one can look or peep

Examples

eyehole in a door
She looked through the eyehole in her mask during the masquerade ball.
Steam escaped from the eyehole in the oven as the bread baked.
The old wooden door had an eyehole at eye level so you could see visitors.
