orienting

adjective
providing direction or orientation
Pronunciation: / ˈɔriˌɛntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪəntɪŋ / (BrE)
serving to guide or determine direction, position, or awareness, especially in relation to a reference system; providing orientation

Examples

orienting signal
Her orienting instincts helped her quickly find her way in the unfamiliar city.
The animal made an orienting movement before deciding which direction to travel.
The company organized an orienting seminar to help new staff adjust to the workplace culture.
