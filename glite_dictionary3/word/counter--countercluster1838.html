counter

noun
a person who counts items or people
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.tɚ /; / ˈkaʊntɚ / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.tə /; / ˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
a person or device that counts things, or a register in computing that stores a count
