to tell

verb
narrate or recount
Pronunciation: / tɛl / (AmE), / tɛl / (BrE)
to narrate or give a detailed account of something, especially in spoken words

Examples

to tell a story
Can you tell us what happened at the meeting?
Grandpa told me an exciting tale before bedtime.
She loves to tell stories about her childhood adventures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – describe: to give an account in words of someone or something, including all the relevant characteristics
  • synonym – recount: to tell someone about something, especially something experienced
  • synonym – relate: to give a spoken or written account of something
