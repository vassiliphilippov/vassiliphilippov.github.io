loot

noun
virtual items or in-game currency
Pronunciation: / luːt / (AmE), / luːt / (BrE)
(in gaming and online contexts) virtual items, equipment, or currency obtained as rewards, drops, or prizes, often from defeated enemies, chests, or events.

Examples

game loot
After the boss fight, the chest contained rare loot for the party.
She traded some valuable loot for in-game currency to buy a new weapon.
Players spent hours farming the dungeon to collect enough loot to craft armor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drop: an item or reward that appears after defeating an enemy or opening a container in a game
  • synonym – reward: a prize or item given for completing a task, objective, or challenge
