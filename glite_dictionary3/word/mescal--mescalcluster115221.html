mescal

noun
Mexican liquor from agave
Pronunciation: / məˈskɑl / (AmE), / mɛˈskæl / (BrE)
a colorless Mexican liquor distilled from the fermented juices of agave plants, especially the century plant

Examples

mescal liquor shot
After dinner, we sampled a shot of mescal served with a slice of orange.
He bought a bottle of artisanal mescal as a souvenir from his trip to Mexico.
The region is famous for producing high-quality mescal from local agave plants.
