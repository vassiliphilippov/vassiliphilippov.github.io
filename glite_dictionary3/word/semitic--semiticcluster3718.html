Semitic

adjective
relating to Semitic languages
Pronunciation: / səˈmɪtɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈmɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Semitic peoples, their languages, or cultures (such as Hebrew, Arabic, and Amharic)
