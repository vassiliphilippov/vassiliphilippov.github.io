hindshank

noun
cut of meat from back leg
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪndˌʃæŋk / (AmE), / ˈhaɪndˌʃæŋk / (BrE)
a cut of meat taken from the upper part of the rear leg of an animal, especially cattle or lamb
