NLP

noun
natural language processing (computing)
Pronunciation: / ɛn ɛl pi / (AmE), / ɛn ɛl pi / (BrE)
the field of computer science and linguistics concerned with the interaction between computers and human (natural) languages, especially the programming of computers to process and analyze large amounts of natural language data
