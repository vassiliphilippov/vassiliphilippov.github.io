matte

noun
having a dull, non-glossy surface
Pronunciation: / mæt / (AmE), / mæt / (BrE)
A surface quality that lacks shine, gloss, or reflective highlights; a dull finish.

Examples

matte finish
She preferred matte lipstick because it looked more natural than gloss.
The photograph was printed with a matte finish to reduce glare.
The walls were painted in a matte light gray, giving the room a modern look.

Related Terms

  • antonym – glossy: having a shiny or reflective surface
  • degree higher – satin: having a surface sheen between matte and glossy
