to bum

verb
ask for and get something for free
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
to ask for and obtain something for free, typically in a casual or informal way

Examples

to bum a ride
Can I bum a ride home from you tonight?
He managed to bum a cigarette off a passerby.
Stop trying to bum money every time we meet.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mooch: to ask for and receive something for free
  • synonym – scrounge: to seek to obtain something, typically by stealth or persuasion, without paying for it
