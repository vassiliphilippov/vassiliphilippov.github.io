to reappear

verb
to appear again after absence
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.əˈpɪr / (AmE), / ˌriː.əˈpɪə / (BrE)
to appear again after having disappeared or been absent for a period of time

Examples

to reappear after absence
After months of silence, the missing cat suddenly reappeared on their doorstep.
Every year, the rare flower reappears in the spring.
The symptoms may reappear if the treatment is stopped too soon.
