latchkey

noun
key for opening a latch or door
Pronunciation: / ˈlætʃˌki / (AmE), / ˈlatʃkiː / (BrE)
A key used to open a latch, especially one for opening an outside door.

Examples

latchkey for the door
Having lost her latchkey, Sarah had to wait outside until someone arrived.
He hung his latchkey on a hook by the door as soon as he got home.
In old houses, the latchkey was often large and made of sturdy iron.
