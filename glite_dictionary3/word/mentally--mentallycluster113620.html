mentally

adverb
in the mind; relating to mental processes
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
In the mind; with respect to the intellect or mental processes rather than the body.

Examples

mentally tired
After the long exam, I felt completely mentally exhausted.
He is strong mentally even though he struggles physically.
She prepared mentally for the interview by practicing answers in her head.
