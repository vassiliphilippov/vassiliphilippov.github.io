demotion

noun
online reduction of visibility or ranking
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
the reduction of a post's, account's, or feature's visibility, ranking, or prominence on an online platform by algorithms or moderation.

Examples

demotion of a post
After the controversy, the journalist complained that the platform had applied a demotion to his articles.
Algorithm changes caused a sudden demotion of many small creators, leaving their videos with far fewer views.
Moderators warned against manipulating tags to avoid a demotion that would push content out of the trending feed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – promotion: the act of increasing the visibility or ranking of content or an account
  • synonym – shadowban: the practice of making a user's content less visible or hidden without explicitly notifying them
