polyneuritis

noun
inflammation of multiple peripheral nerves
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˌnʊˈraɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪnjʊəˈraɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation affecting many or all of the peripheral nerves, often resulting in weakness or sensory changes.

Examples

polyneuritis affects nerves
Her symptoms of muscle weakness and pain were attributed to polyneuritis by the neurologist.
Some infections, like leprosy, can lead to polyneuritis affecting multiple parts of the body.
The patient was diagnosed with polyneuritis after experiencing numbness and tingling in his limbs.
