overcrossing

noun
bridge for pedestrians over a road or railway
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkrɔsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkrɒsɪŋ / (BrE)
A bridge or passage built over a road or railway, designed for use by pedestrians.

Examples

Plans for the new shopping center include an overcrossing connecting both sides of the street.
