frill

noun
decorative pleated strip of material
Pronunciation: / frɪl / (AmE), / frɪl / (BrE)
a strip of fabric, often pleated or gathered, used as a decorative edging or trimming on clothing or other items

Examples

lace frill
She added a lace frill to the edge of the curtains.
The blouse was decorated with pink frill on the sleeves.
The dress had a delicate frill around the neckline.
