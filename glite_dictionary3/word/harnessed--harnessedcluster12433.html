harnessed

adjective
controlled and put to use
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrnɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɑːnɪst / (BrE)
(of a natural force or resource) brought under control and put to use for a practical purpose.

Examples

harnessed solar power
Scientists have found new ways to utilize harnessed solar power for heating homes.
The harnessed energy of the wind is used to generate electricity.
The harnessed potential of the river revolutionized the region's agriculture.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unharnessed: not controlled or utilized; left untamed or unused
