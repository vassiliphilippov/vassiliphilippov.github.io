evangelicalism

noun
Protestant Christian movement stressing personal faith
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæn.dʒəˈlɪ.kə.lɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪˌvan.dʒɪˈlɪk.ə.lɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
A branch of Protestant Christianity emphasizing the importance of personal conversion, faith in Jesus Christ for salvation, and the authority of the Bible.
