Rethuglican

noun
derogatory term for a Republican
Pronunciation: / ɹɪˈθʌɡ.lɪ.kən / (AmE), / ɹɪˈθʌɡ.lɪ.kən / (BrE)
(slang, derogatory) A pejorative term for a member or candidate of the Republican Party, implying thuggish behavior or attitudes.

a Rethuglican politician
He refused to vote for any Rethuglican after the recent scandals.
