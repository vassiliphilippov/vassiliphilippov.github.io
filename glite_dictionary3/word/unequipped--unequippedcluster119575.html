unequipped

adjective
not having necessary equipment or ability
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.ɪˈkwɪpt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.ɪˈkwɪpt / (BrE)
not having the necessary equipment, preparation, or ability for a particular purpose or task.

Examples

unequipped for the job
He was completely unequipped for the challenges of climbing the mountain alone.
Many recent graduates feel unequipped to enter the competitive job market.
Their tiny boat was unequipped for such a violent storm.
