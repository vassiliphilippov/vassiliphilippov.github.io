Pridnestrovie

proper noun
self-declared unrecognized state in Eastern Europe
Pronunciation: / prɪdˌnɛstroʊˈviː / (AmE), / prɪdˌnɛstrəʊˈviː / (BrE)
A self-declared, unrecognized state in Eastern Europe that has declared independence from Moldova, also known as Transnistria.

Examples

Pridnestrovie declared independence
After the collapse of the Soviet Union, Pridnestrovie declared independence from Moldova.

Many residents of Pridnestrovie retain close ties to both Moldova and Russia.
