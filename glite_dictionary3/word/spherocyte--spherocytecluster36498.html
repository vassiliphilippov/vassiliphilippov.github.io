spherocyte

noun
abnormal spherical red blood cell
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪrəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsfɪərəˌsaɪt / (BrE)
an abnormal red blood cell that is spherical in shape rather than the normal disc shape

Examples

Patients with autoimmune hemolytic anemia may develop spherocytes as part of the disease process.

The laboratory test revealed the presence of spherocytes among the red blood cells.
