to recreate

verb
make again or produce a close copy
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.kriˈeɪt /; / ˌriːkriˈeɪt / (AmE), / ˌriː.kriˈeɪt /; / ˌriːkrɪˈeɪt / (BrE)
