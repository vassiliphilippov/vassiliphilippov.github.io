eosinophilia

noun
high eosinophil count in blood
Pronunciation: / iˌoʊsɪnəˈfɪliə / (AmE), / iˌəʊsɪnəˈfɪliə / (BrE)
an abnormally high number of eosinophils in the blood, often occurring as a symptom of allergic conditions or certain infections
