polymetry

noun
simultaneous use of different metres in music
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪmətri / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪmɪtri / (BrE)
The use of two or more different metres at the same time in a piece of music.

Examples

polymetry in music
African drumming often features polymetry, with several meters interacting in a single performance.
By experimenting with polymetry, the band produced unique and unexpected rhythmic textures in their latest album.
The composer employs polymetry to create complex layers of sound in the orchestral score.
