non-conducting

adjective
not able to conduct heat, electricity, or sound
Pronunciation: / nɑn kənˈdʌktɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒn kənˈdʌktɪŋ / (BrE)
not able to conduct heat, electricity, or sound.

Examples

non-conducting material
Because rubber is non-conducting, it is used as an insulating material for electric wires.
Glass is typically non-conducting, making it a good choice for windows in high-voltage equipment.
The walls are made of a non-conducting substance to minimize heat loss.
