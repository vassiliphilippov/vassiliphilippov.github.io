unafraid

adjective
not afraid; fearless
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈfreɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈfreɪd / (BrE)
not feeling fear or worry; not afraid, even when facing danger, difficulty, or criticism
