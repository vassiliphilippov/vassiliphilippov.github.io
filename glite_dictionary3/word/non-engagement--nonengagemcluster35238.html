non-engagement

noun
lack of participation in group activities
Pronunciation: / nɑn ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / nɒn ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing from or not participating in the activities of a group or organization

Examples

non-engagement in meetings
Human resources developed new strategies to address employee non-engagement in company events.
Persistent non-engagement with the team's projects led to a decline in group performance.
The school is concerned about the non-engagement of some students during class discussions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disengagement: the act of withdrawing from involvement or participation
  • antonym – participation: the action of taking part in something
