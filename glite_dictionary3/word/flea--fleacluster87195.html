flea

noun
small jumping parasitic insect
Pronunciation: / fliː / (AmE), / fliː / (BrE)
a small, wingless insect that jumps and feeds on the blood of mammals and birds, often found on pets like cats and dogs

Examples

flea on a dog
She felt a tiny bite and spotted a flea jumping on her ankle.
The family dog brought a flea infestation into the house after playing in the yard.
Veterinarians recommend regular treatments to protect pets from flea bites.
My dog started scratching because he had a flea infestation.
She found a flea on her cat's ear during grooming.
We had to treat the house to get rid of every last flea.

Related Terms

  • synonym – louse: small parasitic insect infesting humans and animals
  • synonym – tick: a small parasitic arachnid that feeds on the blood of mammals, birds, and sometimes reptiles and amphibians
