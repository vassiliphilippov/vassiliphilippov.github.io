dustpan

noun
receptacle for sweeping up dust
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈdʌstpæn / (BrE)
A flat container with a short handle, used to collect dust and dirt swept from the floor.

Examples

dustpan and brush
After sweeping, he emptied the debris into the dustpan before throwing it away.
She used a dustpan to collect the piles of dust from the floor.
The plastic dustpan cracked when it was dropped on the tiles.
