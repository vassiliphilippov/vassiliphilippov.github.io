throwaway

noun
advertisement for mass distribution
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊəˌweɪ / (BrE)
an advertisement, typically printed on paper and intended for mass distribution, often designed to be discarded after reading

Examples

throwaway flyer
I found a throwaway for a new pizza place in my mailbox this morning.
Local businesses often rely on throwaways to reach as many potential customers as possible.
The company distributed thousands of throwaways in the neighborhood to announce the grand opening.

Related Terms

  • synonym – circular: an advertisement or notice distributed to many people
  • synonym – flyer: a small handbill advertising an event or product
