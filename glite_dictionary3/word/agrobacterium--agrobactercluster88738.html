Agrobacterium

noun
genus of rod-shaped plant-pathogenic bacteria
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡroʊbækˈtɪriəm / (AmE), / ˌaɡrəʊbækˈtɪəriəm / (BrE)
a genus of small, motile, rod-shaped bacteria found in soil that reduce nitrates and can cause tumor-like growths (galls) on the stems of plants
