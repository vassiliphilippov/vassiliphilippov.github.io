intersex

noun
person with both male and female biological traits
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌsɛks / (AmE), / ˈɪntəsɛks / (BrE)
A person born with reproductive or sexual anatomy that does not fit typical definitions of male or female.

Examples

an intersex person
Some intersex individuals choose not to undergo any medical intervention.
The hospital provided special counseling for parents of an intersex child.
The organization advocates for the rights of intersex people worldwide.
