bullpucky

noun
nonsense; lies (informal slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌpʌki / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌpʌki / (BrE)
nonsense or untrue talk; lies, especially used to express disbelief or dismissal.

Examples

total bullpucky
Don’t listen to him; everything he says is bullpucky.
I knew right away that his excuse was just bullpucky.
The politician dismissed the allegations as complete bullpucky.
