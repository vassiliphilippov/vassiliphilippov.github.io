Saint Brigid

proper noun
Irish patron saint and abbess
Pronunciation: / ˌseɪnt ˈbrɪdʒɪd / (AmE), / ˌseɪnt ˈbrɪdɪd / (BrE)
An Irish abbess and one of the patron saints of Ireland, known for her piety and founding of several convents in the 5th and 6th centuries.
