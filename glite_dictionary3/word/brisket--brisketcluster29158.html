brisket

noun
cut of meat from the breast or lower chest
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪskɪt / (AmE), / ˈbrɪskɪt / (BrE)
a cut of meat from the breast or lower chest, especially of beef, often used for slow cooking or barbecuing

Examples

slow-cooked brisket
Jewish holidays often include a traditional brisket dish.
She slow-cooked the brisket until it was tender and flavorful.
The menu features smoked brisket served with barbecue sauce.
