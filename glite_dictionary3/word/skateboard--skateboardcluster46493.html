skateboard

noun
a board with wheels for riding
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪtˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈskeɪtˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a flat board mounted on wheels, ridden while standing or crouching, and propelled by pushing with one foot

Examples

ride a skateboard
He learned how to ride a skateboard when he was ten years old.
Many teenagers spend hours practicing tricks on their skateboards at the park.
The skateboard rolled down the hill after she lost her balance.
