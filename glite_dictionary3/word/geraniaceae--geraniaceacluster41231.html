Geraniaceae

noun
family of herbaceous flowering plants (geranium family)
Pronunciation: / dʒəˌræn.iˈeɪ.si.i / (AmE), / dʒəˌræn.iˈeɪ.siː.iː / (BrE)
A family of chiefly herbaceous flowering plants, commonly known as the geranium family, including the genus Geranium.
