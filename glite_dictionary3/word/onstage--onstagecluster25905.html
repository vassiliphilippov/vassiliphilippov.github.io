onstage

adjective, adverb
appearing or taking place on a stage
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌsteɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌsteɪdʒ / (BrE)
on or onto the stage, or happening on the part of a stage visible to the audience (as part of a performance)

Examples
