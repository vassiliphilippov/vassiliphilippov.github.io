federally

adverb
by or relating to the federal government
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛdɚəli / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or by the federal government, especially of a country composed of states or provinces.

Examples

federally funded program
The new law is federally funded to ensure equal educational opportunities.
This drug is federally approved for medical use in all states.
Wildlife in the park is federally protected under several conservation acts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – federal: relating to a system of government in which several states form a unity but remain independent in internal affairs
