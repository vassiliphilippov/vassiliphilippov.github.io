stoic

noun
person who is indifferent to emotions
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈstəʊ.ɪk / (BrE)
a person who shows little or no emotion, especially when enduring pain or hardship

Examples

a stoic in trouble
Despite the chaos around him, the stoic remained calm and collected.
She faced the difficult news like a true stoic, without shedding a tear.
The stoic in our group always handles stress better than anyone else.

Related Terms

  • antonym – emotional: showing strong feelings
  • synonym – emotionless: lacking emotion or feeling
