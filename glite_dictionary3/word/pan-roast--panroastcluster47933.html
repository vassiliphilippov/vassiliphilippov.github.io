to pan-roast

verb
roast food in a pan
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn ˌroʊst / (AmE), / ˈpæn rəʊst / (BrE)
to cook food, especially meat, by roasting it in a pan on the stove or in the oven instead of using a spit or open fire

Examples

to pan-roast chicken
Heat the skillet before you pan-roast the chicken breasts for the best results.
She prefers to pan-roast vegetables to enhance their natural sweetness.
The chef expertly pan-roasted the duck, giving it a crispy skin and tender meat.

  • synonym – roast: to cook food, especially meat, using dry heat
