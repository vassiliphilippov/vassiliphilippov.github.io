conger

noun
large scaleless marine eel
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡə / (BrE)
a large, dark-colored, scaleless marine eel found in temperate and tropical coastal waters, some species of which are used for food.
