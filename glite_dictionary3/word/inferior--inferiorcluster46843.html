inferior

adjective
printed or written below and to one side
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪəriə / (BrE)
(of written or printed text) placed below and to one side of another character, especially in mathematical or scientific notation.

Examples

inferior symbol
In chemical formulas, the number of atoms of each element is usually written as an inferior symbol.
Please write the variable indices as inferior letters, just below the main symbol.
The mathematician explained the use of inferior numbers in tensor notation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – subscript: a character written below and to one side of another character, as in mathematics or science
