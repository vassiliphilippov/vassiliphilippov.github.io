bosom

noun
environment of close affection and protection
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊzəm / (AmE), / ˈbʊzəm / (BrE)
a close, affectionate, and protective acceptance or environment, especially one providing comfort or security

Examples

bosom of the family
After a long absence, he found comfort in the bosom of his family.
Many children seek safety and understanding in the bosom of their community.
She was raised in the bosom of a loving household.
