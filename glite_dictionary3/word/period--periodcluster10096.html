period

noun
monthly menstrual discharge (women)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪriəd / (AmE), / ˈpɪəriəd / (BrE)
the monthly discharge of blood from the uterus in nonpregnant women, typically occurring from puberty to menopause

Examples

Her period was late this month, so she made an appointment with her doctor.
Many women experience cramps and mood swings during their period.

Related Terms

  • antonym – menopause: the time in a woman's life when menstruation permanently stops
  • synonym – menses: the monthly flow of blood from the uterus
  • synonym – menstruation: the process of the monthly discharge of blood from the uterus
