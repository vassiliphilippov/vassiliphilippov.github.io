trace

noun
drawing copied over a transparent sheet
Pronunciation: / treɪs / (AmE), / treɪs / (BrE)
a drawing made by placing a semitransparent sheet over an original image and carefully copying its lines

Examples

trace drawing
A trace allows students to learn by copying complex patterns.
She handed me a trace of the map so I could study the route.
The artist made a trace of the design to preserve the details.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tracing: the act or process of copying drawing lines; also the resulting drawing
