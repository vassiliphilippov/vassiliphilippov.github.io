camphoraceous

adjective
having the smell or properties of camphor
Pronunciation: / ˌkæmfəˈreɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌkamfəˈreɪʃəs / (BrE)
having the characteristic smell or properties of camphor; resembling camphor.

camphoraceous smell

The ointment has a camphoraceous aroma that helps clear nasal passages.
