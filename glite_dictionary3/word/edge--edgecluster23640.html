to edge

verb
to share a boundary or border with
Pronunciation: / ɛdʒ / (AmE), / ɛdʒ / (BrE)
(of a place or area) to be next to or share a border with another.

Examples

edges the river
Our property edges the river, providing beautiful views year-round.
The dense forest edges the highway for many miles.
The national park edges several small towns to the east.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adjoin: to be adjacent to; to share a boundary
  • synonym – border: to be next to or share a boundary with (as a verb)
