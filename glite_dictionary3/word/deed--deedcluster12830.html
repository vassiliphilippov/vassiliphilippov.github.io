deed

noun
legal property document
Pronunciation: / did / (AmE), / diːd / (BrE)
a legal document that is signed and delivered, especially relating to the ownership or transfer of property

Examples

deed of property
Before you can sell the land, you must locate the deed.
She received the deed to the house after the sale was completed.
The lawyer examined the deed to verify the rightful owner.
