aubergine

noun
plant and its edible fruit (eggplant)
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊbərˌʒiːn /; / ˈoʊbərˌʒin / (AmE), / ˈəʊbəʒiːn / (BrE)
a plant with large, glossy, edible fruit (usually dark purple) used as a vegetable; also called eggplant
