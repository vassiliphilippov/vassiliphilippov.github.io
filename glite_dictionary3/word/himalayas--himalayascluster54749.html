Himalayas

proper noun
mountain range in South Asia
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪməˈleɪəz / (AmE), / ˌhɪməˈleɪəz / (BrE)
a vast mountain range in South Asia, stretching along the border between India and Tibet, containing the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest
