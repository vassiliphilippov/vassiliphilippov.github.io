spar

noun
type of cleavable mineral, e.g., calcite or feldspar
Pronunciation: / spɑr / (AmE), / spɑː / (BrE)
any of several nonmetallic minerals, such as calcite or feldspar, that are light in color, transparent or translucent, and cleavable.
