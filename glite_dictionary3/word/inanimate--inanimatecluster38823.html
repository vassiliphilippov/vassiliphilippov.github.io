inanimate

adjective
not alive; not animate
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnænɪmət / (AmE), / ɪˈnænɪmət / (BrE)
not alive; lacking the qualities of living things such as movement or consciousness.

Examples

inanimate object
During the demonstration, the magician seemed to bring the inanimate statue to life.
She arranged the inanimate objects on the shelf with great care.
The scientist explained how inanimate matter could never possess consciousness.

  • antonym – animate: having life; alive
