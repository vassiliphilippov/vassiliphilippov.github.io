os pisiforme

noun
small wrist bone in the carpus
Pronunciation: / oʊs paɪˈsɪfɔːrmi / (AmE), / ɒs pɪˈsɪfɔːmi / (BrE)
A small, pea-shaped bone in the wrist that articulates only with the triquetral bone.

Examples

os pisiforme fracture
Fractures of the os pisiforme are rare compared to other carpal bones.
The os pisiforme can be palpated on the ulnar side of the wrist.
The os pisiforme is considered a sesamoid bone within the human wrist.
