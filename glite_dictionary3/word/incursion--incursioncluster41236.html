incursion

noun
sudden entry into a place or domain
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɜrʒən / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɜːʃən / (BrE)
an act of entering a place or domain, especially in a way that is sudden or unwanted, often in large numbers

Examples

incursion of tourists
Environmentalists warn of the incursion of invasive species into the local ecosystem.
The incursion of foreign companies changed the landscape of the technology industry.
The new regulations were seen as an incursion into personal freedom.

Related Terms

  • synonym – invasion: an instance of entering a place, especially by force or in large numbers
