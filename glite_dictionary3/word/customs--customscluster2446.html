customs

noun
taxes or duties on imports/exports
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəmz / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəmz / (BrE)
the taxes or duties collected by a government on imported or exported goods

Examples

customs duties payment
Smuggling is often an attempt to avoid paying customs on valuable items.
The government increased customs on imported electronics to protect local industries.
Travellers must declare goods and pay any required customs before leaving the airport.
