dock

noun
a weedy plant (genus Rumex) with long taproots
Pronunciation: / dɑk / (AmE), / dɒk / (BrE)
any of several coarse weedy plants of the genus Rumex, with long taproots, sometimes used as leafy greens or in folk medicine

Examples

dock plant leaves
Some herbalists use dock root to make traditional remedies.
The fields behind the house were overrun with wild dock plants.
Young dock leaves can be cooked and eaten as a green vegetable.
