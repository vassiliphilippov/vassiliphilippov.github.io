renunciative

adjective
characterized by renunciation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈnʌnsɪətɪv / (BrE)
Characterized by or showing renunciation; tending to give up or reject something, especially desires or personal interests.

Examples

renunciative lifestyle
Adopting a renunciative approach, he gave up social media to find inner peace.
Her renunciative attitude was evident when she declined the luxurious comforts offered to her.
The monk's lifestyle is highly renunciative, focused on rejecting worldly pleasures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ascetic: characterized by severe self-discipline and abstention
  • antonym – self-indulgent: characterized by giving in to one's own desires
