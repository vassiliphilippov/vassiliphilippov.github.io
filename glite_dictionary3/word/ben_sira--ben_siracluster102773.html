Ben Sira

noun
book of maxims in the Apocrypha
Pronunciation: / bɛn ˈsi.rə / (AmE), / bɛn ˈsiː.rə / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book of wisdom teachings, also known as Sirach or Ecclesiasticus, resembling Proverbs in style and content
