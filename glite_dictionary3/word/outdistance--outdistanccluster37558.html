to outdistance

verb
go much farther ahead; surpass
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈdɪstəns / (AmE), / aʊtˈdɪstəns / (BrE)
to go much farther ahead of others, especially in a competition or race

Examples

to outdistance the competition
He trained hard so he could outdistance everyone in the marathon.
She managed to outdistance her competitors during the final lap.
The company's new product quickly outdistanced its rivals in sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym – surpass: to be greater than; to exceed
