straggler

noun
someone who lags behind a group
Pronunciation: / ˈstræɡlər / (AmE), / ˈstræɡlə / (BrE)
a person or animal that falls behind the main group or moves away from it, especially when traveling together

Examples

a straggler in a race
After the marathon, only a few stragglers crossed the finish line.
The guide kept stopping to wait for a straggler who was far behind the group.
The teacher made sure no straggler was left behind during the field trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym – laggard: someone who lags behind; a slow person
  • antonym – leader: a person who leads or is at the front of a group
