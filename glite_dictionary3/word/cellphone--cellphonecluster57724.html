cellphone

noun
mobile telephone device
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈsɛlˌfəʊn / (BrE)
A portable telephone that connects to a network via radio signals and can be used in different locations within a coverage area.

Examples

cellphone rings loudly
Most teenagers use their cellphone to access social media and send messages.
Please silence your cellphone before entering the theater.
She forgot her cellphone at home and couldn't call anyone all day.
