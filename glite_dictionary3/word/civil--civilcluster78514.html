civil

adjective
officially recognized divisions of time
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl / (BrE)
(of periods or divisions of time) officially recognized by law or by common usage, as opposed to astronomical or religious.

Examples

civil calendar
A civil day is measured from midnight to midnight, regardless of sunrise or sunset.
The civil calendar differs from the religious calendar in some countries.
The government follows the civil year for all official matters.
