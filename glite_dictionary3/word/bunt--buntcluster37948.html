to bunt

verb
hit a baseball lightly for a short distance
Pronunciation: / bʌnt / (AmE), / bʌnt / (BrE)
to hit a baseball lightly so that it travels a short distance, usually as a deliberate play

Examples

to bunt the ball
He tried to bunt the ball to get on base.
The coach signaled her to bunt during the next play.
They practiced how to bunt perfectly during training.

Related Terms

  • antonym – swing: to hit a baseball with a full swing, the usual way
