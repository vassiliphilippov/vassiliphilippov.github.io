lobbyism

noun
practice of influencing legislation or officials
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑː.biˌɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈlɒb.i.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
the organized practice of attempting to influence decisions made by officials, especially legislators, on behalf of a special interest group
