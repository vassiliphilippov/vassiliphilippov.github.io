parachuting

noun
the sport of descending by parachute
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛɹəˌʃuːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpærəˌʃuːtɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity or sport of descending to the ground from an aircraft using a parachute.

Examples

to try parachuting from a plane
