to fix

verb
about to do something (American informal)
Pronunciation: / fɪks / (AmE), / fɪks / (BrE)
(especially American English, informal) to be about to do or intending to do something, often used with 'to' followed by a verb.

Examples

fix to leave
Are you fixing to leave, or do you have time to talk?
I'm fixing to call him as soon as I finish my work.
She said she's fixing to move out next month.
