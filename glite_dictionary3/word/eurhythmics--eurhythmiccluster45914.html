eurhythmics

noun
teaching music through rhythmic movement
Pronunciation: / jʊˈrɪðmɪks / (AmE), / jʊəˈrɪðmɪks / (BrE)
a method of teaching musical understanding through harmonious bodily movements that interpret the rhythm of musical compositions
