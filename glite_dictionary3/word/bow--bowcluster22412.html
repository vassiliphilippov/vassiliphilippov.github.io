bow

noun
a gesture of respect or greeting by bending forward
Pronunciation: / baʊ / (AmE), / baʊ / (BrE)
an act of bending the head or upper body forward as a sign of respect, greeting, reverence, or submission

Examples

give a bow
He gave a deep bow before the queen.
She greeted her elders with a traditional bow.
With a polite bow, the conductor acknowledged the applause.

Related Terms

  • synonym – curtsy: a formal gesture of respect or greeting made by women, involving bending the knees
  • degree lower – nod: a small movement of the head downward and upward, often as a greeting or sign of agreement
