terracotta

noun
brownish-red earthenware material
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈkɑtə / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈkɒtə / (BrE)
A hard, unglazed, brownish-red earthenware, used especially for pottery, tiles, and architectural decoration.

Examples

terracotta pot
A collection of terracotta pots lined the garden wall, filled with herbs and flowers.
She chose terracotta tiles for the patio to give it a warm, rustic feel.
The museum featured several ancient terracotta statues discovered in India.
