to trace

verb
copy a drawing by drawing over lines
Pronunciation: / treɪs / (AmE), / treɪs / (BrE)
to copy a drawing or design by drawing over its lines, or to draw lines following or outlining a shape

Examples

to trace a picture
Children often trace pictures to practice their drawing skills.
He traced the design using a thin sheet of tracing paper.
She carefully traced the flower onto a new sheet of paper.
He traced the map with his finger, following the path they would take.
She carefully traced the outline of the maple leaf onto her notebook.
The artist traced a series of elegant lines across the canvas to form the sketch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – copy: to make an identical version of something
  • synonym – draw: to make marks or a picture with a pen or pencil
  • synonym – sketch: to make a quick drawing
