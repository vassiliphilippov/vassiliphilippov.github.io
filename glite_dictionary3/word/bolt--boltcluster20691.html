to bolt

verb
to lock with a bolt
Pronunciation: / boʊlt / (AmE), / bəʊlt / (BrE)
to fasten or lock (a door, window, etc.) using a bolt

Examples

to bolt the door
He always makes sure to bolt the gate at night for extra security.
Please bolt the door before you leave the house.
She quickly bolted the window when she heard footsteps outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unbolt: to unlock or open by removing a bolt
