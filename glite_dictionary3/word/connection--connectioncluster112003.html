connection

noun
influential person you know
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / kəˈnɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
a person whom you know and who can help you, especially because they are influential or well-placed; often used in the plural.

Examples

business connections
His family’s political connections opened many doors for him.
She was able to get the job thanks to her connections in the industry.
Without the right connections, it can be difficult to succeed in this field.
