maverick

noun
an unbranded stray animal, especially a calf
Pronunciation: / ˈmæv.ɚ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˈmæv.ər.ɪk / (BrE)
an unbranded animal, especially a stray calf, on the open range that is not marked as belonging to any owner

Examples

unbranded maverick calf
A maverick was often claimed by the first cowboy who managed to brand it.
Many mavericks wandered across the open plains, unclaimed by any owner.
The ranchers searched for mavericks to add to their herds during the spring roundup.
