herdsman

noun
man who tends and drives livestock herds
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrdzmən / (AmE), / ˈhɜːdzmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to look after and drive a herd of animals, especially cattle or sheep

Examples

herdsman with cattle
As a young boy, he dreamed of becoming a herdsman like his father.
Local villagers often rely on the herdsman to protect their livestock from predators.
The herdsman led the cattle to the river at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cowboy: a man who herds and tends cattle, especially in the US
  • synonym – shepherd: a person who tends and rears sheep
