amoebiasis

noun
disease caused by amoeba infection
Pronunciation: / əˌmiːbiˈaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / əˌmiːbɪˈeɪsɪs / (BrE)
A disease caused by infection with amoebas, especially Entamoeba histolytica, typically affecting the intestines.

Examples

amoebiasis infection symptoms
Symptoms of amoebiasis often include abdominal pain and diarrhea.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with amoebiasis after tests detected the parasite.
Travelers are sometimes at risk of contracting amoebiasis in areas with poor sanitation.
