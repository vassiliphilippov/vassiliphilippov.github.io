to read

verb
say written words aloud
Pronunciation: / rid / (AmE), / riːd / (BrE)
to look at written or printed words and say them aloud so that others can hear

Examples

to read a story aloud
At the ceremony, she will read the official document to the audience.
Please read the instructions before beginning the exam.
