Colaptes chrysoides

noun
woodpecker species of the southwestern US
Pronunciation: / koʊˈlæptiːz ˌkrɪsiˈɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / kəʊˈlæptiːz ˌkrɪsiˈɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
A species of woodpecker found in the southwestern United States, similar to the yellow-shafted flicker but without the red neck marking.

Unlike the yellow-shafted flicker, the Colaptes chrysoides lacks a red mark on its neck.
