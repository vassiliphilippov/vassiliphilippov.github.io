tautly

adverb
in a tight, stretched manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔtli / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtli / (BrE)
In a manner that is stretched or held tightly; without any slack.

Examples

hold tautly a rope
He held the fishing line tautly, waiting for a bite.
She wrapped the bandage tautly around his arm to stop the bleeding.
The wires were stretched tautly between the poles.

Related Terms

  • antonym – loosely: in a loose or slack manner
