culm

noun
stem of a grass or grass-like plant
Pronunciation: / kʌlm / (AmE), / kʌlm / (BrE)
the stem or stalk of grasses and grass-like plants, especially those of the family Gramineae (grasses)

Examples

culm of bamboo
After the harvest, only the culms remained in the rice field.
Different species of grass can be identified by the structure of their culm.
The botanist carefully examined the culm of the bamboo for signs of disease.
