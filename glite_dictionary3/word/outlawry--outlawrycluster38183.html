outlawry

noun
state of being an outlaw; act of outlawing
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌlɔːri / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌlɑːri / (BrE)
the state of being an outlaw or the act of declaring someone an outlaw, especially as punishment for criminal activity; defiance of the law or legal prohibition
