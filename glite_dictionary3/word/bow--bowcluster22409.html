bow

noun
a weapon for shooting arrows
Pronunciation: / boʊ / (AmE), / bəʊ / (BrE)
a weapon for shooting arrows, consisting of a curved piece of resilient wood with a taut string attached to its ends, used to propel arrows when drawn and released

Examples

archery bow
Ancient warriors often relied on the bow for hunting and battle.
She learned how to make a simple bow from flexible wood and string.
The archer expertly drew his bow and released an arrow at the target.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arrow: a slender pointed shaft shot from a bow
  • synonym – crossbow: a weapon for shooting arrows, consisting of a bow mounted on a stock that shoots projectiles called bolts
