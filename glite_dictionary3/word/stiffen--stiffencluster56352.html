to stiffen

verb
make or become stiff
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪfən / (AmE), / ˈstɪfən / (BrE)
to make or become physically stiff, rigid, or less flexible (such as a material, body part, or object)

Examples

to stiffen a shirt collar
