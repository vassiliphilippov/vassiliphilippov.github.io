civilian

adjective
not military or police; civil
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɪljən / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪljən / (BrE)
relating to civil life or to people who are not members of the armed forces or the police.

Examples

civilian clothes
He decided to return to civilian life after serving in the navy.
Only civilian vehicles are allowed beyond this checkpoint.
The city is under civilian control after many years of military rule.
