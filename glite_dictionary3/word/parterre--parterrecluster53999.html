parterre

noun
rear main floor seating in a theater
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈtɛr / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɛə / (BrE)
the part of a theater auditorium at the rear of the main floor, typically beneath the balconies, used for seating

Examples

theater parterre seats
During the intermission, people mingled in the aisles near the parterre.
Our tickets placed us in the parterre, offering a clear view of the stage.
The parterre often sells out quickly because of its excellent acoustics.
