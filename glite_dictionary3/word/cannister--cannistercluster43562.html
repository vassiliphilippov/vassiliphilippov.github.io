cannister

noun
metal container for dry foods
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.ɪ.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæn.ɪ.stə / (BrE)
A metal container used for storing dry foods such as tea or flour; an alternative spelling of 'canister'.

Examples

cannister of tea
I found an old cannister full of coffee beans in the pantry.
She kept the tea leaves fresh by storing them in a tightly sealed cannister.
The flour was stored in a large metal cannister on the kitchen counter.
