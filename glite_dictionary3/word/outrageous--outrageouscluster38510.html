outrageous

adjective
shockingly excessive or unreasonable
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈreɪdʒəs / (AmE), / aʊtˈreɪdʒəs / (BrE)
shockingly excessive or beyond what is considered reasonable or acceptable

Examples

outrageous price
It's outrageous to expect anyone to pay that much for a concert ticket.
Some celebrities are known for their outrageous spending habits.
The company charged an outrageous fee for late payments.
