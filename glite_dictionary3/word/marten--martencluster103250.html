marten

noun
small agile mammal of the weasel family
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːr.tən / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.tən / (BrE)
A small, slender-bodied, agile mammal of the weasel family, typically found in forests of the Northern Hemisphere.

Examples

wild marten
A marten darted swiftly across the forest floor and disappeared among the pine trees.
The fur of the marten has long been prized for making luxurious garments.
The researchers set up cameras to track the movements of the elusive American marten in the wild.
