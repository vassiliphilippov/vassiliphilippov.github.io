hardcore

adjective
extremely explicit (often sexual)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌkɔr / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌkɔː / (BrE)
(of entertainment or material) extremely explicit and graphic, especially in depicting sexual activity; not intended to be mild or censored.

Examples

hardcore adult movies

Many websites clearly label content as hardcore to warn viewers about explicit material.
The film was classified as hardcore and could not be shown in mainstream cinemas.
