carpal

noun
bone of the wrist
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr.pəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pəl / (BrE)
any of the eight small bones forming the wrist in primates

Examples

carpal bone fracture
An X-ray revealed a fracture in the carpal bone of her left hand.
Athletes are sometimes at risk for injuries to the carpal region after a fall.
The carpal bones allow for the wrist’s flexibility and range of motion.
