Physalis pubescens

noun
annual plant with yellow berries in husk
Pronunciation: / fɪˈseɪlɪs pjuˈbɛsəns / (AmE), / fɪˈseɪlɪs pjuˈbɛsns / (BrE)
A species of annual plant native to the Americas, having round, fleshy yellow berries enclosed in a bladder-like husk; grown for decoration or fruit.
