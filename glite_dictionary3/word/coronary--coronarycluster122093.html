coronary

adjective
relating to the arteries of the heart
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrəˌneri / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.ə.nər.i / (BrE)
Relating to or denoting the arteries that surround and supply the heart, or resembling a crown in position or arrangement.

Examples

coronary artery
After his heart attack, he underwent a coronary bypass procedure.
Plaque buildup in the coronary vessels can seriously affect heart function.
The surgeon examined the coronary arteries for any signs of blockage.
