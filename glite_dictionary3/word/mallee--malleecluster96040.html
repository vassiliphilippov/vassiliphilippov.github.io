mallee

noun
type of low-growing Australian eucalyptus
Pronunciation: / ˈmæli / (AmE), / ˈmæli / (BrE)
A low-growing, bushy Australian eucalyptus tree, typically with several slender stems arising from a woody base.

Examples

Australian mallee tree
