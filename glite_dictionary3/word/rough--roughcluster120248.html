rough

noun
golf: long grass beside the fairway
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
in golf, the area on a course bordering the fairway where the grass is kept longer and is more difficult to play from

Examples

golf rough
His ball landed in the rough, making the next shot much harder.
She managed to hit the ball out of the rough and onto the green.
The rough on this course is particularly thick this season.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fairway: the closely mown part of a golf course between tee and green
