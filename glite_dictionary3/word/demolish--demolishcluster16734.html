to demolish

verb
eat quickly and completely
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːlɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒlɪʃ / (BrE)
to eat food quickly and completely, especially with great enthusiasm or hunger

Examples

to demolish a burger
She demolished a giant burger after running the marathon.
The kids demolished their ice creams in record time.
They demolished the cake before anyone else had a chance to try it.
