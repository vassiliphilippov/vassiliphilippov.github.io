STD

noun
short-term disability benefits
Pronunciation: / cc5bscctid0c8did0 / (AmE), / cc5bscctid0c8did0 / (BrE)
Abbreviation for short-term disability: employer-provided insurance or benefits that pay partial income when an employee cannot work for a short period because of illness or injury.

Examples

STD insurance claim
Her employer approved STD benefits to cover her two months off.
He enrolled in his company's STD plan as soon as he broke his wrist.
Before surgery she checked whether her STD would begin on the first day of leave.

Related Terms

  • antonym – LTD: long-term disability insurance, which provides income replacement for extended or permanent inability to work
  • synonym – short-term disability: insurance or benefits providing partial income for a limited period when an employee cannot work due to illness or injury
