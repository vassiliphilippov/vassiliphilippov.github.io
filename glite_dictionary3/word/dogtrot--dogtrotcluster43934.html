dogtrot

noun
a steady, easy trot
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌtrɑt / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌtrɒt / (BrE)
A steady, easy trot similar to the gait of a dog.

Examples

steady dogtrot pace
The horse settled into a comfortable dogtrot as they crossed the open field.
They moved at a dogtrot to avoid getting tired too quickly.
With his pack bouncing on his back, he continued his hike at a steady dogtrot.
