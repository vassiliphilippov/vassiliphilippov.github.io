unfathomed

adjective
not measured; very deep or mysterious
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfæðəmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfæðəmd / (BrE)
not measured or explored for its depth; too deep or mysterious to be understood or known.

unfathomed depths

The explorers were captivated by the unfathomed depths of the ocean.
