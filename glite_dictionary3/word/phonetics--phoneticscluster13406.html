phonetics

noun
study of speech sounds
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɛtɪks / (AmE), / fəˈnɛtɪks / (BrE)
the study of the physical sounds of human speech, including their production, transmission, and perception

Examples

study phonetics
A basic knowledge of phonetics is essential for language teachers and linguists.
She studied phonetics at university to better understand how humans produce speech sounds.
The phonetics of a language can reveal a lot about its history and evolution.
