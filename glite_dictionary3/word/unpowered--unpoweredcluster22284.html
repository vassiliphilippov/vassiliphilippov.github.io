unpowered

adjective
not supplied with power
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpaʊərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpaʊəd / (BrE)
not provided with or using a source of power, especially mechanical or electrical power.

Examples

unpowered vehicle
Electric trains can be moved through stations as unpowered carriages during maintenance.
The glider remained unpowered throughout its entire flight, relying solely on air currents.
The inventor built a small unpowered boat to test his new hull design.
