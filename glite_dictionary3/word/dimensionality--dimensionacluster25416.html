dimensionality

noun
property relating to number of dimensions
Pronunciation: / dəˌmɛnʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / dɪˌmenʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or property of having a particular number of dimensions, especially in space or mathematics

Examples

spatial dimensionality
Artists often play with dimensionality to create a sense of depth in their paintings.
Reducing the dimensionality of data can simplify complex analyses in statistics and machine learning.
The dimensionality of our universe is commonly described as three spatial dimensions.
