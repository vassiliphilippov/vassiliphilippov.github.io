Penstemon cyananthus

noun
a North American plant with blue-violet flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛnstɪmən saɪˈænənˌθəs / (AmE), / ˌpɛnstɪmən sʌɪˈanənθəs / (BrE)
a species of erect perennial plant with blue-violet flowers arranged in rings near the stem tips, native to Idaho, Utah, and Wyoming
