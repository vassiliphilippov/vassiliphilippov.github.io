Balder

proper noun
Norse god of light and peace
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔldər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːldə / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the god of light and peace, known for his beauty and kindness; the son of Odin and Frigg and the husband of Nanna.

Examples

Balder the god
In the ancient myths, Balder was cherished by all the gods for his gentle spirit.
Many poets have celebrated the tragic story of Balder and his untimely death.
The legend says that after Balder was slain, the entire world mourned his passing.
