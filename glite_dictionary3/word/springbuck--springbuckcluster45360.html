springbuck

noun
South African gazelle known for leaping
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋˌbʌk / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋˌbʌk / (BrE)
A gazelle of southern Africa noted for its habit of leaping high into the air, also known as the springbok.

Examples

springbuck leaps high
During our safari, we spotted a herd of springbuck near the river.
The springbuck is famous for its high, playful jumps called pronking.
The springbuck leaped gracefully across the grassy plain.
