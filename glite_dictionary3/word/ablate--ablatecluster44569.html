to ablate

verb
remove by erosion or vaporization
Pronunciation: / əˈbleɪt / (AmE), / əˈbleɪt / (BrE)
to remove material from the surface of something by erosion, melting, vaporization, or other processes

Examples

to ablate a surface
During re-entry, the spacecraft's heat shield began to ablate due to intense friction.
The surgeon used a laser to ablate the tumor tissue with precision.
Wind and water gradually ablate the mountain's rocky surface over centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym – erode: to gradually wear away the surface of something
