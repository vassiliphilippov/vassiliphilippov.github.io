puncture

noun
the act of piercing or perforating
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋktʃə / (BrE)
the act of making a hole in something with a sharp object

Examples

a puncture wound
A tire puncture can cause a sudden loss of pressure while driving.
The doctor made a small puncture in the patient's skin to draw blood.
There was evidence of a single puncture in the balloon, causing it to deflate rapidly.

  • synonym – perforation: the action of making a hole through something
