to cast

verb
to deposit (e.g. a vote or ballot)
Pronunciation: / kæst / (AmE), / kɑːst / (BrE)
to formally deposit or submit something, especially a vote or ballot, in an official way

Examples

to cast a vote
After much thought, she went to the polling station to cast her ballot.
All eligible citizens are encouraged to cast a vote in the upcoming election.
You must show identification before you are allowed to cast your vote.

  • synonym – submit: to formally present or deliver something for consideration or judgement
