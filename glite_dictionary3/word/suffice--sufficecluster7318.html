to suffice

verb
be enough; be sufficient
Pronunciation: / səˈfaɪs / (AmE), / səˈfaɪs / (BrE)
to be enough or adequate for a particular purpose or need

Examples

to suffice for a need
A simple explanation will suffice for most people.
A smile sometimes suffices to brighten someone's day.
One cup of sugar should suffice for this recipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym – be enough: to have sufficient quantity or quality; to be adequate
  • antonym – be insufficient: to not be enough or adequate
