cosmopolite

noun
sophisticated world traveler
Pronunciation: / kɑːzˈmɑː.pə.laɪt / (AmE), / kɒzˈmɒ.pə.laɪt / (BrE)
a person who is sophisticated and at ease in many different countries and cultures, often as a result of extensive travel or experience abroad

Examples

worldly cosmopolite
As a true cosmopolite, she felt comfortable dining in any city around the globe.
His cosmopolite perspective enriched every discussion on international affairs.
The author, a celebrated cosmopolite, drew inspiration from his travels through Asia and Europe.
