minute

noun
distance measured by travel time in minutes
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnət / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
A distance that can be covered in a specified number of minutes, measured by the time it takes to travel, rather than by units of length.

Examples

minutes walk home
It's only about ten minutes to his house if you take the shortcut.
The hotel is just five minutes from the city center by car.
We found a nice restaurant just a few minutes away from the museum.
