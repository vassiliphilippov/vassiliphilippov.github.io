bragging

noun
boastful talk or display
Pronunciation: / ˈbræɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbræɡɪŋ / (BrE)
an act or instance of talking with excessive pride and self-satisfaction about one's achievements, possessions, or abilities

Examples

bragging after victory
After the win, there was a lot of bragging in the locker room.
He tried to impress us with his bragging, but it only made him look insecure.
Her constant bragging about her new car made everyone uncomfortable.
