photolithography

noun
printing process using photographic plates
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊlɪˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊlɪˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a process of printing or creating patterns using plates or masks made from photographic images, especially in lithography and semiconductor manufacturing

Examples

advanced photolithography process
