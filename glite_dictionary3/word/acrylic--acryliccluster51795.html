acrylic

noun
synthetic fabric made from polyacrylonitrile
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɪlɪk / (AmE), / əˈkrɪlɪk / (BrE)
a synthetic fabric made from polyacrylonitrile, often used in clothing and textiles as a lightweight, warm alternative to wool

Examples

acrylic sweater
Many artists prefer wool over acrylic for winter wear, but the latter is more affordable.
The blanket's acrylic fibers make it lightweight and warm.
This sweater is made from acrylic, so it's both soft and easy to wash.
