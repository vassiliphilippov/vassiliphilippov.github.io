playday

noun
a day set aside for play or recreation
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪ.deɪ / (BrE)
A day or a period set aside for play, recreation, or diversion from regular work or studies.

Examples

The school organizes a playday every spring to let students relax and participate in games.
