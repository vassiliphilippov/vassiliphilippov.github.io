uncertainness

noun
the state of being uncertain or in doubt
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɝːtənnɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːtənnɪs / (BrE)
the state of being unsure or not knowing; the condition of being doubtful or dependent on chance.

Examples

uncertainness about the future
His uncertainness about the future made it difficult for him to make long-term plans.
The company's uncertainness regarding market trends led to cautious investment strategies.
There was a sense of uncertainness in the air as they awaited the final decision.
