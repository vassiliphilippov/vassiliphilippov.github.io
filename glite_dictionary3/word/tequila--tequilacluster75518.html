tequila

noun
Mexican spirit from agave
Pronunciation: / təˈkiːlə / (AmE), / tɛˈkiːlə / (BrE)
A Mexican alcoholic drink made from the fermented and distilled juice of the agave plant.

Examples

shot of tequila
Authentic tequila is made from blue agave plants in Mexico.
She ordered a shot of tequila with lime and salt at the bar.
The party guests enjoyed cocktails made with tequila and fresh fruit juice.

  • synonym – mezcal: Mexican spirit also made from agave, but usually with a stronger flavor
