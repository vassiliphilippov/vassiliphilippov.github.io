to fag

verb
act as a servant for senior boys (British schools)
Pronunciation: / fæg / (AmE), / fæɡ / (BrE)
(historical, chiefly British) to act as a servant for senior boys in a British public school, especially as part of a traditional system.

Examples

to fag for seniors
At his new school, every first-year boy had to fag for an older student.
He resented having to fag in the evenings, polishing boots and running errands.
The tradition of fagging was eventually abolished due to changing attitudes toward hierarchy in schools.
