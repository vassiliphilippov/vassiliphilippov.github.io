piles

noun
swollen anal veins; hemorrhoids
Pronunciation: / paɪlz / (AmE), / paɪlz / (BrE)
Swollen and inflamed veins in and around the anus and lower rectum, causing discomfort and bleeding; also known as hemorrhoids.

Examples

suffer from piles
A high-fiber diet can help prevent piles from developing.

Many people experience piles at some point in their lives, especially as they age.
