tease

noun
an act of mocking or provoking someone
Pronunciation: / tiz / (AmE), / tiːz / (BrE)
an act of irritating or mocking someone, often playfully or persistently, especially by making jokes or provoking them

Examples

a gentle tease
He pretended to ignore the tease from his classmates during lunch.
Her brother's constant tease about her messy hair made her laugh.
Sometimes, a gentle tease among friends can strengthen their bond.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mockery: the act of making fun of someone or something
  • synonym – taunt: an insulting or mocking remark intended to provoke
