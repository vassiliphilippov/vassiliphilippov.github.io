Erysiphe

proper noun
genus of powdery mildew fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌɛr.ɪˈsaɪ.fi / (AmE), / ˌɛr.ɪˈsɪ.fi / (BrE)
A genus of fungi in the family Erysiphaceae, known as powdery mildews, which are plant pathogens causing powdery mildew disease.
