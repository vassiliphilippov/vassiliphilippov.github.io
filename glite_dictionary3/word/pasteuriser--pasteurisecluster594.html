pasteuriser

noun
machine for pasteurizing, especially milk
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstʃəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstjʊəˌraɪzə / (BrE)
a machine or apparatus used to pasteurize substances, especially milk, by heating them to a specific temperature to destroy harmful microorganisms

Examples

pasteuriser for milk
A faulty pasteuriser may allow harmful microbes to survive in milk.
Before bottling, the juice is heated in the pasteuriser to eliminate any bacteria.
The dairy invested in a new pasteuriser to improve the safety of its milk products.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pasteurization: the process of heating to destroy microorganisms in food or drink
  • synonym – pasteurizer: machine for pasteurizing substances, chiefly milk; American spelling
