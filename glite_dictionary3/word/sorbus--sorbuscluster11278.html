Sorbus

noun
a genus of trees and shrubs in the Rosaceae family
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrbəs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːbəs / (BrE)
a genus of deciduous trees and shrubs in the family Rosaceae, including rowans and whitebeams, with feathered leaves and clusters of small fruits

Examples

Sorbus tree fruit
Botanists have classified mountain ash under the genus Sorbus due to shared fruit and leaf structures.
Several species within the Sorbus genus are commonly cultivated as ornamental trees for their attractive berries.
The leaves of Sorbus trees are characterized by their feather-like appearance and bright autumn colors.
