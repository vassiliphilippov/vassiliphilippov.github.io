fainthearted

adjective
lacking courage or determination
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪntˌhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪnthɑːtɪd / (BrE)
lacking courage, boldness, or determination; easily discouraged or afraid to take risks

Examples

a fainthearted person
He dismissed the plan as something only the fainthearted would shy away from.
She was too fainthearted to try skydiving with her friends.
The mountain trail is not for the fainthearted, as it requires nerves of steel.

Related Terms

  • antonym – brave: showing courage and determination
  • synonym – timid: showing a lack of courage or confidence
