exanthem

noun
disease-related skin rash or eruption
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˈzænθəm, ɪɡˈzænθəm / (AmE), / ɛɡˈzænθəm, ɪɡˈzænθəm / (BrE)
a widespread skin rash or eruption that appears as a symptom of a disease, especially in children
