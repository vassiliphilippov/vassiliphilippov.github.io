uptown

adjective
in or of the upper part of a town
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌtaʊn / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌtaʊn / (BrE)
Located in or relating to the upper part of a town or city, often considered more fashionable or residential.

Examples

uptown apartment
Many artists moved into the uptown area as rents in the city center increased.
The uptown shopping district attracts people from all over the city.
They live in an uptown neighborhood known for its tree-lined streets and historic homes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – downtown: in or relating to the central business district of a city
