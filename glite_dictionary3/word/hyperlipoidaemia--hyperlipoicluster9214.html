hyperlipoidaemia

noun
abnormally high blood lipid levels
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌlɪpoʊaɪˈdiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəlɪpɔɪˈdiːmiə / (BrE)
