poor

noun
people with little money or possessions
Pronunciation: / pʊr / (AmE), / pɔː / (BrE)
(the poor) people who have little money or few possessions, considered as a group.

Examples

help the poor
Government programs aim to support the poor in both urban and rural areas.
Many charities focus their efforts on helping the poor during the winter months.
Throughout history, the poor have often struggled for better living conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – needy: people lacking basic necessities, considered as a group
  • antonym – rich: people who have a lot of money, considered as a group
