platyrrhine

adjective
having broad or widely separated nostrils
Pronunciation: / ˈplætɪˌraɪn / (AmE), / ˈplætɪˌraɪn / (BrE)
relating to New World monkeys with nostrils that are far apart, or to people with broad, flat noses.

Examples

Certain indigenous groups are described as having platyrrhine nasal features in anthropological studies.

The scientist studied platyrrhine monkeys in the rainforests of South America.
