loose-fitting

adjective
not tight; hanging loosely
Pronunciation: / ˈluːs ˌfɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈluːs ˌfɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of clothing) not fitting closely to the body; hanging loosely.

Examples

loose-fitting shirt
He prefers loose-fitting clothes in the summer for better comfort.
She bought a loose-fitting jacket to layer over her sweaters.
The dress code suggests wearing loose-fitting attire for the yoga class.

Related Terms

  • synonym – baggy: (of clothing) loose and hanging loosely on the body
  • antonym – tight-fitting: (of clothing) fitting the body closely
