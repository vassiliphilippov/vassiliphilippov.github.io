saponaceous

adjective
soapy or soap-like
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpəˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌsapəˈneɪsiəs / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of soap, especially in being slippery or soapy to the touch

Examples

saponaceous film
