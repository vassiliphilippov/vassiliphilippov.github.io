passageway

noun
narrow connecting corridor or hall
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪdʒˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪdʒweɪ /; / ˈpæsɪdʒˌweɪ / (BrE)
a long, narrow space (such as a corridor or hallway) that connects one room or place to another
