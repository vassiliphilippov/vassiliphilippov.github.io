to programme

verb
arrange or plan the events of something
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
to arrange or plan the activities or events for a particular occasion

Examples

to programme an event

She was asked to programme the annual music festival.
