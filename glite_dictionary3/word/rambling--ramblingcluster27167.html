rambling

adjective
spreading irregularly; sprawling
Pronunciation: / ˈræmblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈræmblɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an object or structure) spreading out in different directions or distributed irregularly; sprawling or straggling.

Examples

rambling old house

Wild roses grew in rambling clusters along the garden fence.
