to solarize

verb
to reverse tones in photography by light exposure
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊləˌraɪz /; / ˈsoʊləraɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɒləraɪz /; / ˈsəʊləraɪz / (BrE)
to cause a photographic image to change or become distorted by exposing it to light or too much sunlight (including reversing tones or overexposing)
