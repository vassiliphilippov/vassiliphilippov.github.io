ritualist

noun
person expert in rites and ceremonies
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃuəlɪst /; / ˈrɪtʃʊəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in, studies, or strictly follows rituals or ceremonies, especially in religion
