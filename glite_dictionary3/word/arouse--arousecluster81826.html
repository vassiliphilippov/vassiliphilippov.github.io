to arouse

verb
summon or bring into existence, often magically
Pronunciation: / əˈraʊz / (AmE), / əˈraʊz / (BrE)
to summon into action or bring into existence, often as if by magic or a supernatural force

Examples

to arouse a spirit
Legends claim some shaman could arouse storms with a single chant.
The author hoped to arouse a sense of wonder in his audience, almost as if by magic.
The ritual was said to arouse ancient spirits who would answer the villagers' questions.

  • synonym – evoke: to bring a feeling, memory, or image into the mind; to call forth
