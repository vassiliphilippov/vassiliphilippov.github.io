passing

adjective
brief; lasting a short time
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ / (BrE)
Lasting only for a short period; brief or fleeting.

Examples

passing moment of regret
He felt a passing pang of regret as he watched his friends leave.
Her interest in jazz was just a passing phase, but now she listens every day.
In the passing years, the old house slowly fell into disrepair.
