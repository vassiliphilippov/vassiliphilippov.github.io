bronze

noun
low competitive rank (gaming)
Pronunciation: / brɑnz / (AmE), / brɒnz / (BrE)
a rank or tier in competitive games and online ranking systems, typically indicating a lower or third-place level (below silver and gold).

Examples

bronze rank
She climbed to bronze in the online ladder after winning several placement matches last season.
Players in bronze often get matched with others learning the game or just starting competitive play.
The tournament awarded small rewards to anyone who reached bronze or higher during the season.
