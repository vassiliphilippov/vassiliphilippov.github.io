sipunculid

noun
small unsegmented marine worm
Pronunciation: / sɪˈpʌŋkjəlɪd / (AmE), / sɪˈpʌŋkjʊlɪd / (BrE)
a small unsegmented marine worm of the phylum Sipuncula, which retracts its anterior portion into the body when disturbed, giving the appearance of a peanut

Examples

marine sipunculid worm
A sipunculid resembles a peanut when it retracts its front end into its body.
Marine biologists discovered several sipunculids burrowed in the sandy seabed.
The sipunculid quickly withdrew its head when touched by the diver.
