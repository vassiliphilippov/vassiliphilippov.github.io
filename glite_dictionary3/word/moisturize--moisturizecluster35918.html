to moisturize

verb
make or keep something moist
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔɪstʃəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɔɪstʃəraɪz / (BrE)
to make something slightly wet or add moisture to it, especially to improve its condition or prevent dryness

Examples

to moisturize your skin
It's important to moisturize your skin every day, especially in dry weather.
Many people use a humidifier to moisturize the air in their homes during winter.
Remember to moisturize after washing your hands to prevent dryness.

Related Terms

  • synonym – humidify: to increase the amount of moisture in the air
