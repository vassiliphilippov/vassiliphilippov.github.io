sociolinguistic

adjective
relating to language and society
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the study of language in its social context, especially how language varies and changes in different social groups.

Examples

sociolinguistic research methods
A sociolinguistic approach can explain why language differs between age groups.
Researchers conducted a sociolinguistic survey to investigate language use in urban areas.
The sociolinguistic patterns of the community reveal much about its history and identity.
