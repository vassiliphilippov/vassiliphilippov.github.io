to blood

verb
smear with blood in a ritual
Pronunciation: / blʌd / (AmE), / blʌd / (BrE)
to smear with blood, especially as part of an initiation ceremony or ritual, such as after a hunt

Examples

to blood a hunter
It was traditional to blood young hunters once they made their first kill.
The chief gently blooded the boy's cheeks to mark his initiation into the group.
The hunters blooded the new member after his first successful deer hunt.
