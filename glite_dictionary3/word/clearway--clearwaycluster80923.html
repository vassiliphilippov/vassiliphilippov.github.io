clearway

noun
road where stopping is prohibited
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈklɪəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a road or part of a road where stopping and parking are not allowed, except in emergencies or for breakdowns

Examples

Parking is strictly forbidden on a clearway during peak hours.
