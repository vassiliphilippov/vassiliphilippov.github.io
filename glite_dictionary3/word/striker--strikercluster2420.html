striker

noun
employee participating in a labor strike
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkə / (BrE)
an employee who is taking part in a strike against their employer

Examples

striker on strike
After a week, most strikers returned to their jobs when a new contract was signed.
Management hired temporary workers to replace the strikers.
The union's strikers demanded improved working conditions and higher pay.
