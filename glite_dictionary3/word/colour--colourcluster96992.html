colour

noun
timbre of a musical sound
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
The quality of a musical sound that distinguishes different types of sound production, instruments, or voices; timbre.

Examples

colour of a voice
Critics praised the pianist for achieving a rich colour throughout her performance.
Each instrument brings its own colour to a musical composition.
The new violin added a warmer colour to the orchestra's overall sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym – timbre: the character or quality of a musical sound distinct from its pitch and intensity
