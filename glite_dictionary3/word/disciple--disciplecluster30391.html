disciple

noun
follower of a teacher or doctrine
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪpəl / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪpəl / (BrE)
a person who believes in and helps to spread the teachings of another, especially a religious or philosophical leader

disciple of Jesus

The young man became a disciple of the famous guru, eager to learn everything he could.
Throughout history, many disciples have worked tirelessly to spread their teacher's message.
