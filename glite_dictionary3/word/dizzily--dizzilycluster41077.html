dizzily

adverb
in a dizzy or light-headed manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪzɪli / (AmE), / ˈdɪzɪli / (BrE)
In a manner characterized by dizziness or feeling light-headed, often with a sensation of spinning or unsteadiness.

Examples

walk dizzily after spinning
After standing up too quickly, she swayed dizzily and grabbed the back of a chair.
Birds swooped dizzily overhead as the storm winds picked up.
The rollercoaster ended, and he staggered dizzily onto the platform.
