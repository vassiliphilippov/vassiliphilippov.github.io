prostration

noun
act of lying face down, often in reverence
Pronunciation: / prɑːˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / prɒˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of lying stretched out on the ground, especially as a sign of submission or worship

Examples

religious prostration
Acts of prostration are common in many religious traditions as a mark of respect.
During the ceremony, the monks performed a full prostration before the altar.
The student's prostration before the teacher showed great humility.
