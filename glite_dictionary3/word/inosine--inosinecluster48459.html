inosine

noun
a nucleoside formed from adenosine
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.oʊˌsin / (AmE), / ˈɪn.əʊˌsiːn / (BrE)
a nucleoside formed by the deamination of adenosine, used in biological research and sometimes in medical contexts such as organ transplantation
