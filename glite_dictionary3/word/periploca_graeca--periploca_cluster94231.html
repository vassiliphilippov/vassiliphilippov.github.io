Periploca graeca

noun
a climbing shrub with dark leaves and poisonous yellow fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛr.ɪˌploʊ.kə ˈɡriː.kə / (AmE), / ˌpɛr.ɪˌpləʊ.kə ˈɡriː.kə / (BrE)
A deciduous climbing shrub with dark, glossy leaves, ill-scented but interesting flowers, and poisonous yellow fruits, native to southeastern Europe and Asia Minor, often grown for ornamental purposes.
