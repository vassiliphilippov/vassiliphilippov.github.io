blowup

noun
an emotional outburst
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌʌp / (BrE)
A sudden and uncontrolled outburst of emotion, especially anger.

Examples

angry blowup
After several small arguments, he finally had a blowup and stormed out.
Her unexpected blowup at the meeting shocked everyone in the room.
There was a serious blowup between the two friends over the misunderstanding.
