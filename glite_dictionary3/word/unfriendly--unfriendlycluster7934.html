unfriendly

adjective
harsh or inhospitable (environment, conditions)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfrɛndli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfrɛndli / (BrE)
(of an environment or conditions) not suitable for life, growth, or development; harsh or inhospitable.

Examples

unfriendly environment
A sudden drop in temperature created unfriendly conditions for the seedlings.
Scientists study how certain plants can survive in unfriendly climates.
The Sahara Desert is an unfriendly environment for most forms of life.

Related Terms

  • antonym – congenial: pleasant or suitable (as an environment)
  • synonym – hostile: very harsh or antagonistic to life or growth
