to tabulate

verb
arrange data in a table
Pronunciation: / ˈtæbjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈtæbjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to arrange information or data in a table or systematic list, usually with columns and rows

Examples

to tabulate survey results
After collecting the survey responses, she tabulated the data to look for trends.
Please tabulate the expenses for each department before the meeting.
The researchers tabulate their results in an easy-to-read chart.

Related Terms

  • synonym – list: to record or enumerate items in a sequence
  • synonym – organize: to arrange systematically
