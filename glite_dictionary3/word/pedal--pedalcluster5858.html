pedal

noun
foot-operated lever
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛdəl / (AmE), / ˈpɛdəl / (BrE)
a lever that is pressed with the foot to control a mechanism, such as on a bicycle, piano, or vehicle

Examples

pedal on a bicycle
Press the pedal gently to accelerate the car.
She adjusted the pedal height on her bicycle for a more comfortable ride.
The piano's soft pedal creates a much quieter sound.
