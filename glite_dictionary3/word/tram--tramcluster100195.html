tram

noun
electric passenger vehicle on tracks
Pronunciation: / træm / (AmE), / træm / (BrE)
A passenger vehicle that runs on tracks, typically along urban streets, and is powered by electricity.

Examples

city tram ride
A new tram line is being built to reduce traffic congestion.
She caught the tram to work every morning instead of driving.
The city has an extensive tram network connecting all major neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bus: a road vehicle for transporting many passengers, usually on scheduled routes
  • synonym – streetcar: an electric passenger vehicle running on rails in city streets
  • synonym – trolley: an electric vehicle that runs on rails, usually in a city (North American usage)
