wintry

adjective
relating to or typical of winter
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntri / (AmE), / ˈwɪntri / (BrE)
Characteristic of or relating to winter; cold, bleak, or typical of winter.

Examples

wintry weather
Her cheeks were red from the wintry air as she entered the house.
The wintry sunlight barely warmed the frozen ground.
We walked through the wintry landscape, bundled up against the biting wind.

Related Terms

  • antonym – summery: Characteristic of or relating to summer
  • synonym – wintery: Variant spelling of wintry, less common
