lateness

noun
the state of being late
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈleɪtnəs / (BrE)
The fact or quality of being late or arriving after the expected or usual time.

Examples

the lateness of the hour
Despite the lateness of the hour, the meeting continued for another hour.
His repeated lateness eventually led to a warning from his boss.
We apologize for the lateness of this response and appreciate your patience.
