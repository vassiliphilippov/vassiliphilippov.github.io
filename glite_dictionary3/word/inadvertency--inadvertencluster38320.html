inadvertency

noun
inattentiveness; neglect of responsibility
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnədˈvɝːtəntsi / (AmE), / ˌɪnədˈvɜːtəntsi / (BrE)
the quality of being inattentive or careless, especially by failing to fulfill duties or responsibilities

Examples

inadvertency in duties
His repeated inadvertency at work eventually led to serious mistakes.

The accident resulted from inadvertency rather than deliberate misconduct.
