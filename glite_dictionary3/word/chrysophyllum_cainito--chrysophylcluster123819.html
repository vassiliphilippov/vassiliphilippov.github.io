Chrysophyllum cainito

noun
tropical tree with edible star-shaped fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪsoʊˈfɪləm kaɪˈniːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkrɪsəˈfɪləm kaɪˈniːtəʊ / (BrE)
an evergreen tree native to the West Indies and Central America, known for its edible purple fruit that is star-shaped in cross section and has leaves with golden silky undersides
