Laertes

proper noun
father of Odysseus in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / leɪˈɜrtiz / (AmE), / leɪˈɜːtiːz / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the father of Odysseus and a king of Ithaca.

Examples

Laertes king of Ithaca
Greek myths often depict Laertes as a symbol of patience and endurance.
