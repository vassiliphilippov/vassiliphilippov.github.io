Psilophyton

noun
a genus of early Paleozoic herbaceous plants
Pronunciation: / saɪ.loʊˈfaɪ.tɒn / (AmE), / sɪ.ləˈfaɪ.tɒn / (BrE)
a genus of small, wiry, herbaceous plants of the Paleozoic era with underground rhizomes and apical sporangia, considered the type genus of the family Psilophytaceae
