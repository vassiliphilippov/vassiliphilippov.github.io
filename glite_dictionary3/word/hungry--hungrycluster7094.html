hungry

adjective
feeling the need to eat
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌŋɡri / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡri / (BrE)
feeling a need or desire to eat; experiencing the sensation of hunger

Examples

hungry for food
After a long hike, everyone felt hungry and ready for a big meal.
I skipped breakfast this morning, so I'm really hungry now.
The hungry children rushed to the dining hall as soon as the bell rang.

Related Terms

  • antonym – full: having eaten enough; not hungry
  • degree lower – peckish: slightly hungry (British informal)
  • degree higher – starving: very hungry; suffering from extreme hunger
