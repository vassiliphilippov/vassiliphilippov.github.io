environmentally

adverb
in relation to the environment
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌvaɪɚənˈmɛntəli / (AmE), / ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the natural world and the impact of human activity on its condition.

Examples

environmentally friendly products
Companies are striving to become more environmentally responsible in their operations.
Switching to renewable energy sources is an environmentally beneficial decision.
The building was designed with environmentally friendly materials to reduce its carbon footprint.
