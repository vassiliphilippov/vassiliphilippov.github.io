Cyanophyceae

noun
class of blue-green algae (photosynthetic bacteria)
Pronunciation: / saɪˌænəˈfaɪsiː.i /; / saɪəˌnoʊfaɪˈsiː.i / (AmE), / saɪˌanəʊˈfɪsiː.iː /; / saɪˌænə(ʊ)faɪˈsiː.iː / (BrE)
a class of photosynthetic bacteria, commonly called blue-green algae, found in freshwater and marine environments and characterized by the presence of chlorophyll a and phycobilins
