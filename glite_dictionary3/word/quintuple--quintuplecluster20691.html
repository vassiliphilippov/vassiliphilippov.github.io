to quintuple

verb
increase fivefold
Pronunciation: / kwɪnˈtʌpəl / (AmE), / kwɪnˈtjuːpəl / (BrE)
to increase or make something increase to five times its original amount or size

Examples

to quintuple your money
If you invest wisely, your money could quintuple within a few years.
Sales of the product are expected to quintuple if the new campaign is successful.
The city's population quintupled over the last decade due to rapid urbanization.
