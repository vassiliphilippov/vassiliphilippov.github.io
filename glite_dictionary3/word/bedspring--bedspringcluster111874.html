bedspring

noun
spring supporting a mattress in a bed
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛdˌsprɪŋ / (BrE)
a spring or one of several springs used to support a mattress in a bed; usually found in the base or frame of the bed

Examples

bedspring poking through
I could feel the bedsprings poking through after years of use.
She replaced the broken bedspring to make the mattress more comfortable.
The old bedspring creaked every time I turned over in bed.
