fathom

noun
unit of water depth (six feet)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæð.əm /; / ˈfæðəm / (AmE), / ˈfað.əm / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to six feet (about 1.83 meters), mainly used to measure the depth of water
