leather

noun
animal skin treated for use as material
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛðər / (AmE), / ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
the skin of an animal, made smooth and flexible by removing the hair and tanning, used for making shoes, bags, clothes, etc.

Examples

leather jacket
He polished his leather boots before the meeting.
Many luxury handbags are crafted from genuine leather.
The jacket was made from high-quality leather and felt very soft to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hide: the skin of an animal, especially when tanned or dressed
