remoteness

noun
state of being remote or distant
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmoʊtnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊtnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being far away in distance, time, or relation; the condition of being remote.

Examples

physical remoteness
She felt a certain remoteness from her old friends after moving abroad.
The remoteness of the mountain village makes it difficult to access in winter.
The judge considered the remoteness of the defendant's actions when making his decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym – closeness: State of being near in space, time, or relation; opposite of remoteness
  • synonym – distance: Amount of space between two points; separation in space
