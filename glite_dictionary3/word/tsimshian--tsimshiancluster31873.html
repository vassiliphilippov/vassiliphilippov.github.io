Tsimshian

noun
Penutian language of the Tsimshian people
Pronunciation: / tsɪmˈʃiːən / (AmE), / tsɪmˈʃiːən / (BrE)
the language of the Tsimshian people, belonging to the Penutian language family and spoken in parts of British Columbia and Alaska
