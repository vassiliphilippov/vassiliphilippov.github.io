height

noun
distance from head to foot (person)
Pronunciation: / haɪt / (AmE), / haɪt / (BrE)
the measurement of how tall someone or something is, from base to top

Examples

height of a person
Doctors measure children's height regularly to monitor their growth.
He was proud of his son's height compared to other boys his age.
Her height is 170 centimeters, which is considered above average for women.
Please write your height on the medical form in centimeters or inches.
The height of the mountain was over 2,000 meters above sea level.
They measured the height of the building before installing the new windows.

Related Terms

  • antonym – depth: the distance from the top down to the bottom
  • antonym – length: the measurement or extent of something from end to end
  • synonym – tallness: the quality or state of being tall, especially in a human or animal
