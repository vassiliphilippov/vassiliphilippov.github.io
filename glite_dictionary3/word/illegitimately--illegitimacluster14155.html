illegitimately

adverb
in an unlawful or improper manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪləˈdʒɪtəmətli / (AmE), / ˌɪləˈdʒɪtɪmətli / (BrE)
in a manner that is not in accordance with law, rules, or accepted standards; unlawfully or improperly

Examples

illegitimately gain money
He was accused of acquiring property illegitimately during his tenure in office.
Profits gained illegitimately are subject to legal penalties.
The candidate was removed from the race after being found to have illegitimately altered official documents.

Related Terms
