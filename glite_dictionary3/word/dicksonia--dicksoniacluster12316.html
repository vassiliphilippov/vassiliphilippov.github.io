Dicksonia

noun
genus of tree ferns from Australasia
Pronunciation: / dɪkˈsoʊniə / (AmE), / dɪkˈsəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of tree ferns native to temperate regions of Australasia, with divided fronds and usually marginal sori, sometimes classified in the family Cyatheaceae
