fortification

noun
the art of building military defenses
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrt̬əfɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌfɔrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɔːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or science of building military defenses, or the defensive structures themselves (such as walls or moats) to protect against attack
