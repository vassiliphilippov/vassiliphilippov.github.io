coarse

adjective
rough in texture; large particles
Pronunciation: / kɔrs / (AmE), / kɔːs / (BrE)
Having a rough texture or composed of relatively large particles; not fine or smooth.

Examples

coarse sand grains
She sifted out the coarse grains from the flour.
The rug felt coarse under my bare feet.
We used coarse sand to improve drainage in the garden soil.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fine: of textures that are smooth or composed of small particles
