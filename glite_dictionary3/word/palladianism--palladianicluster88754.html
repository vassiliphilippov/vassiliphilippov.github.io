Palladianism

noun
architectural style inspired by Palladio
Pronunciation: / pəˈleɪdiənɪzəm / (AmE), / pəˈleɪdɪənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
an architectural style based on the principles and designs of Andrea Palladio, characterized by symmetry, classical forms, and proportion.
