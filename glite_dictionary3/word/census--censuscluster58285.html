to census

verb
to conduct an official population count
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsəs / (BrE)
to conduct an official count or survey of a population, typically recording various details of individuals.

Examples

to census wildlife
A team of researchers is censusing plant species in the region to monitor biodiversity.
Biologists censused the bird population in the wetlands last spring.
The government decided to census the remote villages to update national records.

Related Terms

  • synonym – survey: to systematically collect information from a group
