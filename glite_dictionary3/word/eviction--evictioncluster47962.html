eviction

noun
legal removal from property
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɪk.ʃən /; / ɪˈvɪkʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈvɪk.ʃən /; / ɪˈvɪkʃən / (BrE)
the act of forcing someone to leave a property they are renting or occupying, usually by a landlord
