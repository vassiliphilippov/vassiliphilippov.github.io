bundle

noun
a collection of items tied together
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌn.dl̩ /; / ˈbʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.dl̩ /; / ˈbʌndl̩ /; / ˈbʌnd(ə)l / (BrE)
a group or collection of things tied, wrapped, or packaged together (including a set of software programs)

Examples
