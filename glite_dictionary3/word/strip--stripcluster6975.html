to strip

verb
damage the thread of a screw
Pronunciation: / strɪp / (AmE), / strɪp / (BrE)
to damage or wear away the thread of a screw so that it can no longer be properly fastened or removed with a screwdriver

Examples

to strip a screw
Be careful not to strip the screw when assembling the furniture.
If you use the wrong screwdriver, you might strip the head of the screw.
She accidentally stripped the screw, making it difficult to remove.
