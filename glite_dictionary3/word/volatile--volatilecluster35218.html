volatile

adjective
inconstant or changeable in feelings
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːlətl / (AmE), / ˈvɒlətʌɪl / (BrE)
(of a person or their feelings) liable to sudden, unpredictable changes in affection or attachments; inconstant or fickle.

Examples

volatile personality traits
His volatile nature meant he was affectionate one moment, then distant the next.
Sarah's volatile feelings made it hard for her friends to predict her reactions.
The relationship ended because of her partner's volatile attachments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fickle: having changeable or unstable affections or loyalties
  • antonym – steadfast: firm and unwavering in purpose or loyalty
