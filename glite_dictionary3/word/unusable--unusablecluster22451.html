unusable

adjective
not fit or able to be used
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjuːzəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈjuːzəbl / (BrE)
not fit or suitable for use; incapable of being used.

Examples

unusable equipment
Because the file was corrupted, it became unusable for our project.
Much of the storm-damaged furniture was deemed unusable by the movers.
The old printer was completely unusable after the ink spilled inside.
