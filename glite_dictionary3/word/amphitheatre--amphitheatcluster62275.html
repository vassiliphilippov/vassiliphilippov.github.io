amphitheatre

noun
large round or oval building for spectacles
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.fəˌθi.ə.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈam.fɪˌθɪə.tə /; / ˈam.fɪˌθiː.ə.tə / (BrE)
a building or area with rising, tiered seats for spectators, used for performances, sports, lectures, or demonstrations
