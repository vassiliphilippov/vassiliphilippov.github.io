zumbooruck

noun
camel-mounted artillery used in Asian armies
Pronunciation: / zʊmˈbuːrək / (AmE), / zʊmˈbʊərʌk / (BrE)
a camel mounted with a swiveling gun, used as mobile artillery in early modern Arab, Afghan, Persian, and Indian armies.
