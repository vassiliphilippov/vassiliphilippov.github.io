enlightenment

noun
spiritual liberation from cycle of rebirth (Eastern religions)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈlaɪtənmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈlaɪtənmənt / (BrE)
(Hinduism and Buddhism) A state of perfect knowledge or spiritual insight that frees a person from the cycle of rebirth, characterized by the extinction of desire and suffering and the dissolution of individual consciousness.

Examples

spiritual enlightenment
After many years of study and practice, he finally achieved enlightenment.
Many Buddhists spend their lives seeking enlightenment through meditation and self-discipline.
Some believe that enlightenment brings freedom from suffering and the end of the cycle of rebirth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – awakening: a realization or coming to spiritual understanding, especially in Buddhism
  • synonym – nirvana: a transcendent state free from suffering and the cycle of rebirth in Buddhism
