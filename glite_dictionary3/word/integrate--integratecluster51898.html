to integrate

verb
combine parts to form a whole
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.təˌɡreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.ɡreɪt / (BrE)
to combine one thing with another to form a whole, or to make something become part of a larger group or system

Examples

to integrate new ideas
He worked hard to integrate his ideas with those of his colleagues.
It's important to integrate new employees into the team quickly and smoothly.
The company plans to integrate several smaller units into one large division.

  • synonym – incorporate: combine or include as part of a whole
