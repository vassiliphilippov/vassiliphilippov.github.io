bilge

noun
water collected in a ship's lowest part
Pronunciation: / bɪldʒ / (AmE), / bɪldʒ / (BrE)
water that collects in the lowest part of a ship's hull, often from leaks or internal spillage

Examples

bilge water smell
A strong odor was coming from the bilge, alerting the captain to a possible leak.
Routine maintenance includes checking for excess water in the bilge of the vessel.
The crew pumped the bilge to prevent water from accumulating in the lower decks.
