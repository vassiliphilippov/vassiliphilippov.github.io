Oliver Hazard Perry

proper noun
U.S. naval officer, War of 1812 hero
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪvər ˈhæzərd ˈpɛri / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪvə ˈhæzəd ˈpɛri / (BrE)
(1785–1819) A United States naval officer known for leading the fleet that defeated the British at the Battle of Lake Erie during the War of 1812.
