congregation

noun
the act of gathering together
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːŋɡrəˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of gathering or coming together, especially in a group.

Examples

the congregation of people
Birds formed a large congregation on the wire as dusk approached.
During the festival, there was a notable congregation of residents in the main square.
The sudden congregation of people in the park surprised the local authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – assembly: the action of gathering together in one place for a common purpose
