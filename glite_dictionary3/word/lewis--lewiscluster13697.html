Lewis

proper noun
American explorer and leader of the Lewis and Clark expedition
Pronunciation: / ˈluːɪs / (AmE), / ˈluːɪs / (BrE)
(Meriwether Lewis) United States explorer and soldier who led the expedition from St. Louis to the mouth of the Columbia River (1774–1809).
