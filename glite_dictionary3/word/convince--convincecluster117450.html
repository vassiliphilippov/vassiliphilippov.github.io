to convince

verb
persuade someone of the truth
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɪns / (AmE), / kənˈvɪns / (BrE)
to cause someone to believe that something is true or to persuade someone to do something

Examples

to convince a friend
It took me several hours to convince her to join the team.
The lawyer tried to convince the jury of his client's innocence.
You will have to convince me that your plan is safe before I agree.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dissuade: to persuade someone not to take a particular course of action
  • synonym – persuade: to cause someone to do or believe something by reasoning or urging
