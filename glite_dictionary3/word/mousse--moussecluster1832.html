mousse

noun
light, creamy whipped dessert
Pronunciation: / mus / (AmE), / muːs / (BrE)
a rich, airy dessert made by whipping egg whites and cream, often flavored with chocolate, fruit, or other ingredients

Examples

chocolate mousse
He learned to make lemon mousse in his cooking class.
She served a delicious chocolate mousse for dessert.
The restaurant offers raspberry mousse with fresh mint on top.
