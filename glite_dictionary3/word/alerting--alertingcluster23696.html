alerting

noun
state of readiness to respond
Pronunciation: / əˈlɝːtɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈlɜːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the state of being ready to respond quickly to a stimulus or situation

Examples

alerting the mind
Caffeine consumption has been shown to improve alerting in many sleep-deprived individuals.
During overnight shifts, alerting is crucial for air traffic controllers to maintain safety.
Researchers measured participants' alerting using reaction time to sudden signals.
