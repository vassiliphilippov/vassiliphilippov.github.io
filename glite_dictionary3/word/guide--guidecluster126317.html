to guide

verb
direct or motivate toward a goal
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪd / (AmE), / ɡaɪd / (BrE)
to influence, direct, or motivate someone or something toward a particular goal or course of action

Examples

to guide decisions
His conscience will always guide him to do the right thing.
She tried to guide her son towards a fulfilling career.
The manager's vision continued to guide the company through tough times.

Related Terms

  • antonym – mislead: to lead or guide in the wrong direction
