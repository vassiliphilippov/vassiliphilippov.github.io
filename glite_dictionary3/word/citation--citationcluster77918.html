citation

noun
official summons to appear in court
Pronunciation: / saɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / saɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an official summons requiring a person to appear in court or at a legal proceeding

Examples

court citation notice
Failure to respond to the citation could result in a warrant for her arrest.
