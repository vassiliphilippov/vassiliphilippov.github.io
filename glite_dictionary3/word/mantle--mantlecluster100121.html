mantle

noun
shelf above a fireplace
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈmæn.tl̩ / (BrE)
A shelf that projects from the wall above a fireplace, usually serving as a decorative frame.

Examples

mantle above fireplace
A clock sat in the center of the old stone mantle.
He decorated the mantle with garlands and candles for the holidays.
She placed the family photos carefully on the mantle above the fireplace.
