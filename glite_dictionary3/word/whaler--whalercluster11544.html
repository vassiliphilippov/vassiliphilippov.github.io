whaler

noun
a person who hunts whales
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈweɪlə / (BrE)
A person who works on a ship that hunts and kills whales for commercial purposes.

Examples

experienced whaler
As a young man, he dreamed of becoming a whaler and exploring distant oceans.
He spent twenty years as a whaler in the icy waters of the Arctic.
The old whaler told stories of his adventures at sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym – whaleman: a person who hunts whales, especially in the past
