kilogram

noun
SI unit of mass; 1,000 grams
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪləˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈkɪləɡræm / (BrE)
The basic unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI), equal to one thousand grams.

Examples

one kilogram weight
A newborn panda typically weighs only about 0.1 kilogram.
She lost three kilograms after changing her diet.
The sack of rice weighs five kilograms.
