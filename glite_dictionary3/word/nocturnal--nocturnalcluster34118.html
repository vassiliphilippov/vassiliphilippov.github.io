nocturnal

adjective
active or happening at night
Pronunciation: / nɑkˈtɝnəl /; / nɑkˈtɜrnəl / (AmE), / nɒkˈtɜːnəl / (BrE)
active, happening, or existing during the night rather than the day

Examples

nocturnal animals hunt
