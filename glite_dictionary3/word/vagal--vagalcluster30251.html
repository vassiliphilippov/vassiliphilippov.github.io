vagal

adjective
relating to the vagus nerve
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈveɪɡəl / (BrE)
Relating to the vagus nerve, which is a cranial nerve involved in controlling functions of the heart, lungs, and digestive tract.

Examples

vagal nerve activity
A sudden vagal stimulation can cause a drop in heart rate.
Certain digestive issues may be linked to vagal nerve dysfunction.
The doctor noted a strong vagal response during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – vagus nerve: the tenth cranial nerve, extending from the brainstem to the abdomen
