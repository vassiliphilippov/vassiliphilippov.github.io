service

noun
an act of help or assistance
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrvɪs /; / ˈsɝːvɪs /; / ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs /; / ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
an act of help, assistance, or work done for someone, especially as a commercial offering
