apiculturist

noun
person who keeps bees for honey
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪpɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (AmE), / ˌeɪpɪˈkʌltʃərɪst / (BrE)
A person who keeps and raises bees, especially for the production of honey.

Examples

apiculturist manages bees
As an experienced apiculturist, she frequently educates others on the importance of bees to the environment.
The apiculturist inspected the hives each morning to ensure the bees were healthy.
The local apiculturist supplies fresh honey to markets throughout the region.
