coloration

noun
appearance or pattern of colors
Pronunciation: / ˌkʌləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkʌləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
The appearance or pattern of colors on the surface of something, especially as it affects its overall look.

Examples

natural coloration
Her cheeks had a rosy coloration after the brisk walk in the cold air.
Scientists studied the coloration patterns of tropical fish to understand their behavior.
The vivid coloration of the butterfly helps it to ward off predators.
