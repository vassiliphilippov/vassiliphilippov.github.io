stay

noun
interruption pause; period of inactivity
Pronunciation: / steɪ / (AmE), / steɪ / (BrE)
a period or state of inactivity or pause following an interruption

Examples

brief stay
The rain caused a brief stay in the proceedings.
The referee called for a stay to address the player's injury.
There was a sudden stay in the conversation when the announcement was made.

  • synonym – halt: a temporary stop or interruption
  • synonym – pause: a temporary stop or break in an activity
  • antonym – resumption: the act of beginning again after an interruption
