salinity

noun
amount of salt in a solution
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪnəti / (AmE), / səˈlɪnɪti / (BrE)
the amount of salt present in a solution, especially in water or soil

Examples

salinity of seawater
Irrigating with high salinity water can damage crops over time.
Scientists measured the salinity of the lake to track environmental changes.
The salinity of ocean water affects marine life in various ways.
