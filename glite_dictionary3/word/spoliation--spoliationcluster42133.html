spoliation

noun
the act of taking by force; plundering
Pronunciation: / ˌspoʊliˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌspəʊliˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of taking goods or property by force, especially wrongfully or illegally; the act of plundering or despoiling
