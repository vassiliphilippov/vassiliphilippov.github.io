seppuku

noun
ritual samurai suicide by self-disembowelment
Pronunciation: / sɛˈpuːkuː / (AmE), / sɛˈpuːkuː / (BrE)
an act of ritual suicide by self-disembowelment with a sword, historically practiced by samurai in traditional Japanese society

Examples

commit seppuku honorably
Historians have documented several famous cases of seppuku during the Edo period.
Many films portray seppuku as an act of honor in feudal Japan.
The disgraced samurai chose to commit seppuku rather than face execution.
