tolerance

noun
permissible range of variation
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːlərəns / (AmE), / ˈtɒlərəns / (BrE)
the allowable amount of variation of a specified quantity, especially in technical or scientific contexts

Examples

tight tolerance
Engineers must consider the tolerance of each component during assembly.
