unassuming

adjective
modest; not arrogant or pretentious
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈsumɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈsjuːmɪŋ / (BrE)
not pretentious, boastful, or arrogant; modest and humble in manner or appearance

Examples

an unassuming person
Despite his achievements, he remained an unassuming person who rarely talked about his success.
Her unassuming manner made everyone feel comfortable around her.
The house looked unassuming from the outside, but inside it was beautifully decorated.

  • antonym – arrogant: having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance
  • synonym – humble: having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
  • synonym – modest: having a moderate or humble opinion of oneself
