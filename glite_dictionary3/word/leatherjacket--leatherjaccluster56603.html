leatherjacket

noun
larva of certain crane flies
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛðɚˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
the tough-skinned larva of certain crane flies, often found in soil and known to damage grass roots

Examples

soil leatherjacket larva
