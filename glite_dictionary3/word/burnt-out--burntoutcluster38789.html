burnt-out

adjective
damaged and inoperative due to heat or friction
Pronunciation: / bɝnt aʊt / (AmE), / bɜːnt aʊt / (BrE)
No longer working or functioning because of damage caused by heat or friction.

Examples

burnt-out engine
After years of use, the burnt-out motor failed to start.
The burnt-out fuse needed to be replaced before the lamp would work again.
We had to remove the burnt-out bulb and put in a new one.
