foulness

noun
wickedness or moral corruption
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfaʊlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely wicked or immoral; moral corruption or depravity

Examples

foulness of his actions

Many were shocked by the foulness revealed in the official investigation.
The novel explores the foulness that can grow in the absence of compassion.
