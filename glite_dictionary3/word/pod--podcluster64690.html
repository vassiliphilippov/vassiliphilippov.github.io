pod

noun
group of aquatic mammals
Pronunciation: / pɑd / (AmE), / pɒd / (BrE)
a group of aquatic mammals such as whales or dolphins that swim together

Examples

pod of dolphins
A pod of dolphins followed the boat for several miles.
Each pod of whales has its own unique set of calls.
Scientists observed a large pod of orcas off the coast of Alaska.
