to develop

verb
map a 3D surface onto a plane (geometry)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləp / (BrE)
(geometry) To create a representation of a three-dimensional surface on a plane without stretching or distorting the surface.

Examples

to develop a surface
In geometry, to develop a cylinder means to lay out its curved surface as a rectangle.
The engineer showed how to develop the shape of a cone onto flat paper.
To create a map projection, you must develop the globe's surface without stretching it.
