to delight

verb
to take great pleasure in
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlaɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈlaɪt / (BrE)
to take great pleasure or joy in something; to enjoy something greatly

Examples

to delight in music
He delights in sharing stories about his travels around the world.
Many people delight in a warm cup of tea on a cold day.
She delights in teaching children and seeing their progress.

Related Terms

  • synonym – enjoy: to take pleasure in; find joy in something
  • antonym – loathe: to feel intense dislike or disgust for
