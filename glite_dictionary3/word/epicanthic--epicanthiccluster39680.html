epicanthic

adjective
relating to the epicanthus fold
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈkænθɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkanθɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the epicanthus, especially referring to the skin fold of the upper eyelid covering the inner corner of the eye.
