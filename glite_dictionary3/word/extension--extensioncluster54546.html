extension

noun
additional telephone on the same line
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛnʃən / (BrE)
an additional telephone set connected to the same telephone line, allowing multiple phones to use the same number.

Examples

extension phone line
Every office has at least one extension connected to the central line.
He answered the call from the extension in his bedroom.
Please do not use the extension while someone is talking on the main phone.
