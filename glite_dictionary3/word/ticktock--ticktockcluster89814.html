to ticktock

verb
make a ticking sound like a clock
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪk.tɑk / (AmE), / ˈtɪk.tɒk / (BrE)
to make a repeated sound like that of a clock ticking

Examples

to ticktock all night
As midnight approached, the silence was broken only by a watch that ticktocked on the bedside table.
In the quiet classroom, the timer ticktocking on the teacher’s desk made everyone nervous.
The old wall clock ticktocks steadily in the hallway, marking every second.
