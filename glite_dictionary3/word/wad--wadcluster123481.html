to wad

verb
to pack tightly to capacity
Pronunciation: / wɑd / (AmE), / wɒd / (BrE)
to compress, pack, or roll something into a tight, soft mass

Examples

to wad paper together

Don’t wad your clothes; fold them neatly instead.
He wadded the letter and tossed it in the trash.
