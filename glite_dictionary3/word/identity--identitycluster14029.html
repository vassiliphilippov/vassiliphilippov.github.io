identity

noun
online representation and credentials
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɛntəti / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪti / (BrE)
In computing and online services, the set of data and credentials that represent a person or organization digitally and that are used to authenticate, identify, or personalize access to systems and services.

Examples

online identity
The company verifies each user's identity with two-factor authentication before allowing payments.
Her social media identity connects profile photos, posts and the apps she uses for work.
Developers designed a secure identity system to manage access rights across multiple services.
