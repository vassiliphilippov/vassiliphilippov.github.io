stream

noun
live online broadcast
Pronunciation: / strid0m / (AmE), / strid0m / (BrE)
a live audio or video broadcast transmitted over the Internet in real time, available to watch as it happens.

Examples

live stream online
Millions tuned in to watch the stream of the game on their phones and smart TVs.
She scheduled a Q&A after the webinar's stream to answer audience questions in real time.
Podcasters often offer a separate subscriber-only stream with behind-the-scenes interviews each week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – broadcast: to transmit a program or signal to a wide audience; in this context, transmission over the Internet
  • synonym – livestream: a live video or audio broadcast transmitted over the Internet
