to milk

verb
to extract milk from an animal
Pronunciation: / mɪlk / (AmE), / mɪlk / (BrE)
to draw milk from the udder of a female mammal, especially a cow or goat

Examples

to milk a cow
After washing his hands, he began to milk the animals for the day's supply.
Every morning, the farmer goes to milk the cows in the barn.
She learned how to milk a goat during her visit to the farm.
