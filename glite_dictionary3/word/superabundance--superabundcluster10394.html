superabundance

noun
an excessive amount; surplus
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərəˈbʌndəns / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərəˈbʌndəns / (BrE)
an extremely large or excessive quantity of something; more than is needed or appropriate

Examples

a superabundance of food
The garden produced a superabundance of tomatoes this year, far more than the family could eat.
The report criticized the superabundance of unnecessary details in the proposal.
There was a superabundance of decorations, making the room look cluttered rather than festive.
