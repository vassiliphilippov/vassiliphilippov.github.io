pig-headedly

adverb
in a stubborn, obstinate way
Pronunciation: / pɪɡ ˈhɛdɪdli / (AmE), / pɪɡ ˈhɛdɪdli / (BrE)
In a stubborn and unreasonably obstinate manner.

Examples

pig-headedly refuse advice
He pig-headedly refused to listen to any suggestions, no matter how sensible they were.
She pig-headedly stuck to her original plan despite clear evidence it would not work.
They pig-headedly ignored the advice of the experts and went ahead with their own idea.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stubbornly: in a stubborn or unyielding manner
