stanchion

noun
vertical support post or rod
Pronunciation: / ˈstæn.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈstan.tʃən / (BrE)
a vertical post or rod used as a support, especially in buildings, fences, or railings

Examples

stanchion supports railing
A metal stanchion holds up the handrail along the staircase.

The workers installed new stanchions to reinforce the safety barrier.
