renouncement

noun
act of formally giving something up
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnaʊnsmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈnaʊnsmənt / (BrE)
a formal statement or act of giving up a claim, right, or possession

Examples

renouncement of citizenship
Her renouncement of her title surprised the entire royal family.
His renouncement of violence marked a turning point in the negotiations.
The contract required a formal renouncement of any previous agreements.
