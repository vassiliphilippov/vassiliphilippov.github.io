to confer

verb
discuss or consult with others
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɝː / (AmE), / kənˈfɜː / (BrE)
to discuss something with others or consult, especially in order to make a decision

Examples

to confer with colleagues
Before making changes, I prefer to confer with my colleagues.
The committee will confer again before announcing its decision.
The doctors conferred privately to discuss the best course of treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – consult: to seek advice or discuss with someone before making a decision
  • synonym – deliberate: to discuss or consider carefully
