varnisher

noun
person who applies varnish
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑrnɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈvɑːnɪʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to apply a finishing coat of varnish to surfaces, especially wood

Examples

varnisher finishes wood
After the table was sanded, a skilled varnisher applied the final glossy coat.
Each varnisher must ensure the finish is even and free of bubbles.
The museum hired a varnisher to restore several antique cabinets this spring.
