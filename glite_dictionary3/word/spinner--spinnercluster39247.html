spinner

noun
graphical loading indicator (UI)
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnər / (AmE), / ˈspɪnə / (BrE)
a small animated graphic in a computer or mobile interface that rotates or moves to show that a program, page, or process is loading or working

Examples

loading spinner
The site displays a blue spinner while the page loads.
Designers replaced the static icon with a subtle spinner to show background processing.
Users complained the endless spinner meant the app had frozen and needed a restart.

Related Terms

  • synonym – loading indicator: a graphical element that shows a system is loading or processing
  • synonym – progress bar: a horizontal bar that fills to show the progress of a task; an alternative visual indicator
  • synonym – throbber: an informal term for an animated loading icon used in web and application interfaces
