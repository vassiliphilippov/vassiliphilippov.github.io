tic-tac-toe

noun
paper-and-pencil game with Xs and Os
Pronunciation: / ˌtɪk tæk ˈtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtɪk tæk ˈtəʊ / (BrE)
A game played by two people, who take turns marking Xs and Os in a 3×3 grid, aiming to place three of their marks in a row, column, or diagonal.

Examples

play tic-tac-toe with friends
After drawing the grid, she challenged her brother to a quick game of tic-tac-toe.
It only takes a few minutes to learn how to play tic-tac-toe.
The children spent the afternoon playing tic-tac-toe on scraps of paper.
