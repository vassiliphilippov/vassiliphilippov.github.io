to think

verb
to consider or reflect
Pronunciation: / θɪŋk / (AmE), / θɪŋk / (BrE)
to use your mind to consider, reason, reflect, or solve problems

Examples

to think carefully

You should think carefully before you answer such a question.

Infants gradually learn to think as their brains develop.
