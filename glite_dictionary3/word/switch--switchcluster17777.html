switch

noun
electrical circuit control device
Pronunciation: / swɪtʃ / (AmE), / swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a device for making, breaking, or changing the connections in an electrical circuit

Examples

light switch
Please make sure the power switch is off before replacing the bulb.
She flipped the switch to turn on the lights in the room.
The engineer installed a safety switch to prevent electrical accidents.

Related Terms

  • synonym – circuit breaker: a device that interrupts the flow of electricity in a circuit as a safety measure
  • synonym – toggle: a type of switch operated by a lever or button
