peaty

adjective
containing or resembling peat
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːti / (AmE), / ˈpiːti / (BrE)
(of soil, land, or a substance) containing, resembling, or characteristic of peat.

Examples

peaty soil

The whisky had a distinct peaty flavor that lingered on the palate.
They hiked across the peaty moorlands despite the morning mist.
