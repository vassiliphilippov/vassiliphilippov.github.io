cutlet

noun
thin slice of meat, usually fried
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌt.lət / (AmE), / ˈkʌt.lɪt / (BrE)
A small, thin slice of meat, especially veal, usually breaded and fried or broiled.

Examples

breaded cutlet
After dipping the pork cutlet in breadcrumbs, she fried it until golden brown.
He ordered a chicken cutlet sandwich for lunch.
The restaurant is famous for its tender veal cutlet served with lemon and capers.
