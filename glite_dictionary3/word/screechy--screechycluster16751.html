screechy

adjective
having a high, harsh sound
Pronunciation: / ˈskritʃi / (AmE), / ˈskriːtʃi / (BrE)
having or making a high-pitched, harsh, and unpleasant sound, like a scream or a rusty hinge.

Examples

screechy voice
Her voice became screechy when she was upset.
I can't stand the screechy sound of chalk on the blackboard.
The old door opened with a screechy noise that echoed through the hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym – raspy: having a rough or harsh sound
  • synonym – shrill: having a high-pitched and piercing sound
