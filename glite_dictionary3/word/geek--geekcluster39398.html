geek

noun
carnival performer doing shocking acts
Pronunciation: / ɡik / (AmE), / ɡiːk / (BrE)
(historical) A carnival performer who performs bizarre or repulsive acts, such as biting the heads off live animals.

Examples

carnival geek act
In early twentieth-century sideshows, a geek was expected to shock and disgust the audience.
She was horrified at the spectacle performed by the carnival geek.
The old circus had a geek whose only act was biting the heads off chickens.
