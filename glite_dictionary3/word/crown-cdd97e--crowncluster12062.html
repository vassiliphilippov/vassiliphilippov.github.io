to crown

verb
fit a tooth with an artificial cover
Pronunciation: / kraʊn / (AmE), / kraʊn / (BrE)
to fit a tooth with an artificial covering, usually made of enamel or metal, to restore its shape, strength, or appearance

Examples

to crown a tooth
Many patients feel nervous before getting a tooth crowned.
She had her broken molar crowned to restore its appearance and function.
The dentist will crown your tooth after the root canal procedure.
