socialite

noun
fashionable, socially prominent person
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪt / (BrE)
A person who is well-known in fashionable society and regularly attends social events.

Examples

socialite attends parties
As a well-connected socialite, he was invited to every important event in the city.
The magazine featured a socialite known for her glamorous parties and extravagant lifestyle.
The young socialite spent her evenings attending charity balls and art openings.

  • antonym – nobody: a person of no importance or influence
