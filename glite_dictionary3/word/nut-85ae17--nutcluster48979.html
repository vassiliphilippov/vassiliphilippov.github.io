nut

noun
large hard-shelled edible seed
Pronunciation: / nʌt / (AmE), / nʌt / (BrE)
a usually large, hard-shelled seed that is edible and grows within a hard outer casing

Examples

crack a nut
Almonds are technically seeds but are commonly considered a nut.
He cracked open the nut to eat the fresh kernel inside.
Squirrels gather and store nuts for the winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – seed: a plant's unit of reproduction, capable of developing into another plant
