Cinchona lancifolia

noun
Colombian tree; source of Cartagena bark
Pronunciation: / sɪŋˈkoʊnə ˌlænsɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / sɪŋˈkəʊnə ˌlansɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
A tree native to Colombia, known as a source of Cartagena bark, which is a type of cinchona bark used for medicinal purposes.
