Plassey

proper noun
site of 1757 battle in Bengal, India
Pronunciation: / ˈplɑːsi / (AmE), / ˈplɑːsi / (BrE)
The site of a decisive battle in 1757 in Bengal, India, where British forces under Robert Clive defeated Siraj-ud-Daula, establishing British supremacy in Bengal.

Battle of Plassey
A monument now stands at the site of Plassey to commemorate the historic conflict.
Historians often discuss how Plassey led to two centuries of British rule in India.
The Battle of Plassey marked a turning point in Indian and British history.
