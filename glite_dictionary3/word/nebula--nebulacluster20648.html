nebula

noun
faint corneal spot (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛbjələ / (AmE), / ˈnɛbjʊlə / (BrE)
In pathology, a faint, cloudy spot on the cornea of the eye.

Examples

nebula on cornea
After the injury, a small nebula appeared on the patient's cornea.
Long-standing infections sometimes result in a nebula that causes blurred sight.
The ophthalmologist identified a nebula that could affect her vision if left untreated.
