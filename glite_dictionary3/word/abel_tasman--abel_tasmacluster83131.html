Abel Tasman

proper noun
Dutch navigator; first European visitor to Tasmania and New Zealand
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪbəl ˈtæzmən / (AmE), / ˌeɪbəl ˈtæzmən / (BrE)
(1603–1659) Dutch navigator who was the first known European to reach Tasmania and New Zealand.

Examples

Abel Tasman explored seas
Abel Tasman made significant voyages that led to the European discovery of Tasmania and New Zealand.
