to tink

verb
make a high, ringing sound
Pronunciation: / tɪŋk / (AmE), / tɪŋk / (BrE)
to make or emit a short, high-pitched ringing sound, similar to the sound of a small bell

Examples

tink like a bell
A tiny bell tinked, announcing the arrival of a new customer.
Every time she passed by, her silver bracelets would tink softly against each other.
The delicate glass wind chimes tink in the gentle breeze outside the window.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – clang: to make a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym – tinkle: to make a light, clear ringing sound
