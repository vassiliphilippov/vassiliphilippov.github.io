Costanoan

noun
Native American people of coastal California
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑstəˈnoʊən / (AmE), / ˌkɒstəˈnəʊən / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people historically inhabiting the coastal region of California between Monterey and San Francisco Bay
