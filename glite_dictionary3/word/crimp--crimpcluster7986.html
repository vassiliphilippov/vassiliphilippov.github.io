to crimp

verb
curl or wave tightly (esp. hair)
Pronunciation: / krɪmp / (AmE), / krɪmp / (BrE)
to curl tightly or make into waves, especially by pressing or folding

Examples

to crimp hair
She loves to crimp her hair before going out to parties.

You can crimp fabric to add some texture to your project.
