facile

adjective
fluent and effective in expression
Pronunciation: / ˈfæsl / (AmE), / ˈfæs.aɪl / (BrE)
able to express oneself easily, clearly, and effectively, especially in speech or writing; fluent or eloquent.

Examples

facile speaker
Her facile manner of speaking won over the audience immediately.
The politician delivered a facile speech that captivated the entire room.
With his facile tongue, he could persuade almost anyone to see his point of view.

Related Terms

  • synonym – eloquent: fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing
  • synonym – fluent: able to express oneself easily and articulately
