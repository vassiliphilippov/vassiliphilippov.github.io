anomaly

noun
deviation from the norm
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːməli /; / əˈnɑː.mə.li / (AmE), / əˈnɒməli /; / əˈnɒm.ə.li / (BrE)
something or someone that is different from what is normal, standard, or expected
