twink

noun
low-level character with high-level gear
Pronunciation: / twɪŋk / (AmE), / twɪŋk / (BrE)
(in online gaming) a low-level player character that has been equipped or supplied with high-level gear or resources to gain an advantage at that level.

Examples

twink with rare gear
Players complained that the new patch removed several twinks from low-level battlegrounds.
He created a twink on purpose, equipping a level five character with endgame armor.
Guild rules forbid bringing a twink to starter raids because they upset game balance.
