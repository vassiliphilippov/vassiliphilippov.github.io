gasbag

noun
person who talks a lot, usually boringly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈɡasˌbaɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who talks a lot, especially about trivial or uninteresting topics

Examples

old gasbag at work
Everyone tries to avoid sitting next to the office gasbag at lunch.
He didn’t want to come across as a gasbag during the meeting, so he kept his comments brief.
Uncle Jim can be a real gasbag at family gatherings, going on about politics for hours.
