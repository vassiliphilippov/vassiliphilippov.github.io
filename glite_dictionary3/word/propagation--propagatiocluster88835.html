propagation

noun
producing offspring or multiplying
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑː.pəˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɒp.əˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of producing offspring or increasing in number, especially by reproduction or multiplication

Examples

propagation of plants
In biology class, we studied the propagation of bacteria under different conditions.
Successful propagation of livestock is vital for sustainable agriculture.
The propagation of certain plants can be achieved by taking cuttings from the parent.
