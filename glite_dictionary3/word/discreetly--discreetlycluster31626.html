discreetly

adverb
in a careful and unobtrusive way
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskritli / (AmE), / dɪˈskriːtli / (BrE)
In a way that shows carefulness and self-restraint, so as not to attract attention or cause embarrassment.

Examples

discreetly leave a party
He moved discreetly through the crowded room, avoiding unwanted attention.
Julia slipped out of the meeting discreetly so no one would notice her absence.
They handled the negotiations discreetly to prevent rumors from spreading.

Related Terms

  • antonym – openly: in a way that is not secret or hidden
  • synonym – secretly: in a way that is meant to be hidden from others
