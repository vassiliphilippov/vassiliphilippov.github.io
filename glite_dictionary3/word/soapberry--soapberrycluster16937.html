soapberry

noun
tree or fruit yielding natural soap
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊpˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpˌbɛri / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Sapindus, or its fruit, which contains saponin and is used as a natural soap
