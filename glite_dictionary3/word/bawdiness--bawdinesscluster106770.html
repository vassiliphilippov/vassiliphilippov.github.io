bawdiness

noun
indecency; obscene humor or behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːdinəs / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being indecent or obscene, especially in speech or humor

Examples

comic bawdiness

Victorian literature often shied away from bawdiness in favor of more restrained expression.
