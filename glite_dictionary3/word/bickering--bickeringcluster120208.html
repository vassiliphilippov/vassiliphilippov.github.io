bickering

noun
petty argument or quarrel
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪkərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪkərɪŋ / (BrE)
continuous arguments or quarrels over unimportant matters, especially in a petty or irritating way.

Examples

endless bickering over chores
Despite their bickering, the two friends always made up quickly.
The constant bickering between siblings drove their parents crazy.
There was so much bickering in the meeting that nothing got accomplished.

Related Terms

  • antonym – harmony: agreement or concord among people
  • synonym – quarrel: an angry argument or disagreement, typically between people who are usually on good terms
