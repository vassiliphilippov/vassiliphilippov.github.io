exploration

noun
systematic search or investigation
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkspləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛkspləˈreɪʃən /; / ˌɛksplɔːˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of searching, examining, or investigating something thoroughly and systematically, including careful consideration of a subject

Examples

scientific exploration
Scientific exploration of the deep ocean has revealed many new species in recent years.
The exploration of the unknown jungle took several weeks and required careful planning.
Their exploration of the ancient ruins provided valuable insights into the past.
Any exploration of complex topics requires patience and critical thinking.
Her book offers a deep exploration of human relationships and identity.
The committee began a thorough exploration of the ethics involved in genetic testing.
