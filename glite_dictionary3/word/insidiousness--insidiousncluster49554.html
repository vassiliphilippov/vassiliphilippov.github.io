insidiousness

noun
subtle or gradual harmfulness
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪdi.əs.nəs /; / ɪnˈsɪdiəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪd.i.əs.nəs /; / ɪnˈsɪdiəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being harmful in a gradual, subtle, or sneaky way, often not noticed at first

Examples
