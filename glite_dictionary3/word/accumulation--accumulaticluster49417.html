accumulation

noun
a collection of things gathered together
Pronunciation: / əˌkjumjəˈleɪʃən /; / əˌkjuːmjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌkjuːmjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of collecting or gathering things over time
