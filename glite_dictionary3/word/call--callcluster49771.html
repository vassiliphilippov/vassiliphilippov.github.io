call

noun
poker/card game action: matching a bet
Pronunciation: / kɔl / (AmE), / kɔːl / (BrE)
an act in a card game, such as poker, where a player matches the current bet to stay in the game rather than raising or folding

Examples

a call in poker
After several raises, the next player responded with a call.
During the poker game, he made a call to match the previous bet.
If you don't want to raise or fold, you can simply call.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fold: in poker, to withdraw from a hand and forfeit the round
  • antonym – raise: in poker, to increase the amount of the current bet
