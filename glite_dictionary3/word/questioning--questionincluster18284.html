questioning

adjective
expressing doubt or uncertainty
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛs.tʃə.nɪŋ /; / ˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwɛs.tʃən.ɪŋ /; / ˈkwɛstʃənɪŋ /; / ˈkwɛstʃ(ə)nɪŋ / (BrE)
