Pandanus tectorius

noun
species of screw pine native to the Pacific
Pronunciation: / ˌpændənəs tɛkˈtɔriəs / (AmE), / ˌpandənəs tɛkˈtɔːrɪəs / (BrE)
A species of screw pine native to coastal regions of the Pacific Islands, also known as the Polynesian screw pine, cultivated for its edible fruit and fibrous leaves.
