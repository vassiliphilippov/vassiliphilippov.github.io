floating

adjective
suspended or staying on a liquid's surface
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
staying on the surface of a liquid without sinking or being suspended in a liquid

Examples

floating boat
Rescuers found the boat floating several miles off the coast after the storm.
Several floating leaves gathered at the edge of the pond.
The floating icebergs made navigation through the waters difficult for the crew.

Related Terms

  • antonym – sinking: going below the surface of a liquid
