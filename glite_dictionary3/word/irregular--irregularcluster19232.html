irregular

adjective
not meeting manufacturing standard; imperfect
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrɛɡjələr / (AmE), / ɪˈrɛɡjʊlə / (BrE)
(of a manufactured product) failing to meet a standard due to a minor imperfection, often sold at a reduced price.

Examples

irregular product
Although the shirt was labeled irregular, the flaw was barely noticeable.
The store offers irregular clothing at significant discounts.
These shoes are marked as irregular because of a slight color mismatch.
