torn

adjective
jagged or ragged from being ripped
Pronunciation: / tɔrn / (AmE), / tɔːn / (BrE)
Having edges that are jagged or ragged from being ripped or injured.

Examples

torn jeans
She covered her torn sleeve with a scarf to hide the damage.
The child's jeans were torn at the knee after he fell on the pavement.
The flag hung by the gate was torn and faded from years of wind and sun.

  • synonym – ripped: torn apart or split
