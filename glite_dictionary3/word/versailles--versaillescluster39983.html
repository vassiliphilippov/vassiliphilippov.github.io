Versailles

proper noun
grand palace near Paris, royal residence
Pronunciation: / vərˈsaɪ / (AmE), / vɛəˈsaɪ / (BrE)
a grand palace and former royal residence built in the 17th century for Louis XIV, located southwest of Paris near the city of Versailles
