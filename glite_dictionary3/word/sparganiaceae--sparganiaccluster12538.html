Sparganiaceae

noun
former family of aquatic plants (bur-reeds)
Pronunciation: / spɑːrˌɡæniˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / spɑːˌɡaniˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
a former family of aquatic flowering plants, now generally included in Typhaceae, coextensive with the genus Sparganium (bur-reeds)

Examples

Sparganiaceae plant family
Botanists once recognized Sparganiaceae as a distinct family before advances in plant taxonomy led to its merger.
Species formerly placed in Sparganiaceae are commonly known as bur-reeds and grow in wet habitats worldwide.
