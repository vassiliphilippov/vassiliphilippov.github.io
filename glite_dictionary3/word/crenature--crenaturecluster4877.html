crenature

noun
rounded notch or curve along an edge
Pronunciation: / krɪˈneɪtʃər / (AmE), / krɪˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a rounded projection or notch along the edge of an object, such as a leaf, shell, or cell, characterized by a series of curves or indentations

Examples

crenature of a leaf
The crenature along the shell's edge made it easy to distinguish from similar species.
The crenature of a maple leaf is often used to identify the species.
Under the microscope, the crenature of the red blood cells was clearly visible.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crenation: the formation of rounded projections or notches, especially in biological cells
