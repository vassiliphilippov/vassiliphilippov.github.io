self-discipline

noun
ability to control one's impulses or desires
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈdɪsəplɪn / (AmE), / ˌselfˈdɪsəplɪn /; / ˌsɛlfˈdɪsɪplɪn / (BrE)
the ability to control your own behavior and feelings, especially when it is difficult or tempting not to

Examples

She attributes her success to her self-discipline and determination.
Without self-discipline, it's easy to be distracted from your long-term goals.

Training for a marathon takes a lot of self-discipline and commitment.
With enough self-discipline, you can stick to your goals even when it's difficult.
