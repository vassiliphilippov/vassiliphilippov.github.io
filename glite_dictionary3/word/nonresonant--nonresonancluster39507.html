nonresonant

adjective
not producing resonance
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈrɛzənənt / (AmE), / nɒnˈrɛzənənt / (BrE)
Not producing or exhibiting resonance; lacking reverberation or the tendency to vibrate sympathetically.

Examples

nonresonant tone
She preferred a nonresonant tone in her recording booth for clearer vocals.
The studio was designed with nonresonant walls to minimize unwanted echoes.
This material is highly nonresonant, making it suitable for soundproofing applications.
