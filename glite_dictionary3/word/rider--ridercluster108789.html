rider

noun
person traveling as passenger
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.də / (BrE)
a person who travels in a vehicle, such as a bus, car, train, or boat, but is not operating it

Examples

rider in a car
Carpooling apps match drivers with riders heading in the same direction.
Each rider must pay the fare before boarding the train.
The bus was crowded, and every rider had to hold on tightly.

  • antonym – driver: person who operates or controls a vehicle
  • synonym – passenger: a person who is traveling in a vehicle but not operating it
