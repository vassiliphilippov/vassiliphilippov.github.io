to clank

verb
make a loud metallic sound
Pronunciation: / klæŋk / (AmE), / klæŋk / (BrE)
(of something made of metal) to make a loud, sharp, metallic sound, or to move making such a sound.

Examples

to clank loudly
As the knight entered, his armor clanked with every step.
She heard chains clanking in the basement during the night.
The old elevator would clank loudly as it reached each floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clang: make a loud, resonant, metallic sound
  • synonym – clatter: make a continuous rattling sound
  • antonym – thud: to make a dull sound as of a heavy object falling
