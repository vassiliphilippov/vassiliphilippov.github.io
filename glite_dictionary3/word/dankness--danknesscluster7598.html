dankness

noun
unpleasant dampness and cold
Pronunciation: / ˈdæŋknəs / (AmE), / ˈdaŋknəs / (BrE)
The quality of being unpleasantly damp and cold.

Examples

cold dankness inside
After the rain, the dankness in the old house could be felt everywhere.
She shivered in the dankness that seeped through the walls each winter.
The dankness of the cellar made it an unpleasant place to stay for long.
