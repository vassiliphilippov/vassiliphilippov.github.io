alecost

noun
Eurasian herb used for flavoring and potpourri
Pronunciation: / ˈæliˌkɔst / (AmE), / ˈæliˌkɒst / (BrE)
A Eurasian perennial herb (Tanacetum balsamita) with a scent resembling tansy, yellow buttonlike flowers, and leaves used as a salad green, flavoring, or for potpourri.
