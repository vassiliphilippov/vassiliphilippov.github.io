to ruffle

verb
to mix into a random order
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈrʌfəl / (BrE)
to mix things together so as to create a random order or arrangement, especially by stirring, tossing, or shuffling

Examples

to ruffle the cards
Before dealing, she ruffled the cards thoroughly to ensure fairness.
He ruffled the deck a few times, then passed it to his opponent.
It's important to ruffle the tickets before drawing a winner.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shuffle: to mix up the order of items, especially playing cards
