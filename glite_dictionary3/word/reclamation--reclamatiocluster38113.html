reclamation

noun
rescuing and restoring to the right course
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkləˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkləˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of rescuing someone from moral error or wrongdoing and restoring them to the right path

Examples

reclamation of criminals
A major goal of the program is the reclamation of individuals who have strayed from the law.
The reclamation of former criminals often requires patience and community support.
The charity was dedicated to the reclamation of troubled youth through guidance and education.
