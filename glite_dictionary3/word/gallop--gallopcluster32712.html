gallop

noun
fast gait of a horse
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæl.əp / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.əp / (BrE)
A fast running gait of a horse or other quadruped, in which all four feet leave the ground simultaneously during each stride.

Examples

full gallop speed
She watched the herd move across the plain at a full gallop.
Suddenly, the sound of hoofbeats at a gallop echoed through the valley.
The horse broke into a gallop as it approached the finish line.
