Hoder

proper noun
blind god in Norse mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊ.dər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊ.də / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, a blind god who, deceived by Loki, killed his brother Balder with a shaft of mistletoe.
