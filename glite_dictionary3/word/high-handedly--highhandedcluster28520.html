high-handedly

adverb
in a domineering or overbearing manner
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˌhaɪˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is domineering or shows a disregard for others' opinions or rights; in an overbearing or autocratic manner.

Examples

high-handedly ignore complaints
He high-handedly dismissed their concerns as unimportant.
She imposed her decisions high-handedly, without considering her team's input.
The manager acted high-handedly, refusing to listen to any objections.

  • synonym – arrogantly: in a manner showing an exaggerated sense of one's own importance; synonym of high-handedly
  • antonym – considerately: in a thoughtful and respectful manner; antonym of high-handedly
