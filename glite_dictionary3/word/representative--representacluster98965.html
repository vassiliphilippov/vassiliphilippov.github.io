representative

adjective
of a government by elected officials
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
Relating to a system of government in which citizens exercise power through elected officials who act on their behalf.

Examples

representative government system
A representative democracy allows people to have a voice through their chosen officials.
The constitution guarantees a representative form of government for all states.
The country adopted a representative system where citizens elect their leaders.
