disarmament

noun
reduction of military forces or weapons
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɑrməmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈzɑːməmənt / (BrE)
The act of reducing or eliminating military forces or weapons, especially as part of an agreement between countries.

Examples

nuclear disarmament
International talks on disarmament have taken place for decades.
Many people argue that disarmament is essential for lasting peace.
The treaty calls for the gradual disarmament of both countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym – armament: the process of equipping with weapons or military supplies
