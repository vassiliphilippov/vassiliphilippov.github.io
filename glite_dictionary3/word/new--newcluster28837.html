new

adjective
not used; in original condition
Pronunciation: / nu / (AmE), / njuː / (BrE)
Not showing signs of use, wear, or exposure; in original, unused condition.

Examples

new car
She drove a new car that had never been on the road before.
The book was so clean, it appeared almost new.
The furniture in the showroom looked new and untouched.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unused: not previously used or worn
  • antonym – worn: showing signs of use, especially damage or fading
