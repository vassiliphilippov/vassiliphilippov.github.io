pride

noun
excessive self-esteem (a deadly sin)
Pronunciation: / praɪd / (AmE), / praɪd / (BrE)
Excessive and unreasonable self-esteem or arrogance, regarded as one of the seven deadly sins.

Examples

deadly pride
According to Christian tradition, pride is considered the worst of the seven deadly sins.
His pride blinded him to his own faults, leading to his downfall.
She warned that unchecked pride could ruin even the most virtuous person.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arrogance: an attitude of superiority manifested in an overbearing manner
  • antonym – humility: modesty or a lack of excessive pride
