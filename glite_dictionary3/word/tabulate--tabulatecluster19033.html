to tabulate

verb
to shape with a flat surface
Pronunciation: / ˈtæbjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈtæbjʊleɪt / (BrE)
(technical) To shape or cut (something) so that it has one or more flat surfaces.

Examples

to tabulate a crystal
Geologists examined the crystals tabulated by natural processes in the cave.
Over centuries, the river tabulated the rocks, giving them smooth, flat sides.
The stones were carefully tabulated to ensure they would fit together in the wall.
