tout

noun
person who sells betting advice
Pronunciation: / taʊt / (AmE), / taʊt / (BrE)
a person who sells tips or advice, especially about betting or gambling, typically at a racetrack

Examples

tout sells tips
He was banned from the track for acting as a tout without a license.
Many consider the advice of a tout to be unreliable at best.
The tout offered secret tips on which horse would win the next race.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tipster: person who gives or sells advice about betting or gambling
