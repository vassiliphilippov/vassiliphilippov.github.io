honey

noun
sweet liquid made by bees
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌni / (AmE), / ˈhʌni / (BrE)
A sweet, thick, yellowish liquid made by bees from the nectar of flowers, used as food.

Examples

honey on toast
Drizzle some honey over the pancakes before serving.
She likes to put honey in her tea instead of sugar.
The bees worked hard to fill the hive with honey.

  • synonym – syrup: a thick sweet liquid made from sugar and water, often used in cooking
