pyx

noun
container for Eucharist wafers
Pronunciation: / pɪks / (AmE), / pɪks / (BrE)
a container, usually made of precious metal, used in churches to hold the consecrated wafers of the Eucharist

Examples

pyx for wafers
During the service, the priest placed the consecrated wafers in a pyx for later distribution to the sick.
She opened the ornate pyx to show the hosts inside before the procession.
The small gold pyx was carefully carried to the hospital by the minister.
