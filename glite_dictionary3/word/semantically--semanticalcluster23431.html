semantically

adverb
in terms of meaning
Pronunciation: / səˈmæn.tɪ.kli / (AmE), / sɪˈmæn.tɪ.kli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the meaning of words, phrases, or sentences

Examples

semantically related words
That word is semantically ambiguous and could be interpreted in several ways.
The two sentences are semantically equivalent, even though they are structured differently.
This passage is semantically rich, offering multiple layers of meaning.
