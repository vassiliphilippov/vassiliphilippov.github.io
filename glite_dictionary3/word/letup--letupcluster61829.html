letup

noun
pause or reduction in activity
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈlɛtˌʌp / (BrE)
a pause or reduction in intensity, activity, or pressure; a lull

Examples

letup in pressure
The manager was hoping for a letup in customer complaints.
There was finally a letup in the heavy rain during the afternoon.
We worked for ten hours without a letup.
