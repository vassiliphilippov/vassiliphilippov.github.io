summarisation

noun
the act of making a summary
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌmərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌmərʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of expressing the main points of something briefly and clearly; the process of making a summary

Examples

summarisation of a text
Accurate summarisation helps readers quickly grasp complex information.
Her summarisation of the article highlighted only the most important facts.
The teacher asked for a summarisation of each chapter before the exam.
