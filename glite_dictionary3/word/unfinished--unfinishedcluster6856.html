unfinished

adjective
not completed or finished
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
not completed; not brought to a finished or final state

Examples

unfinished homework
He left the table covered with unfinished work when he went home.
Her novel was published posthumously, although it was unfinished.
The building remained unfinished for over a decade because of funding issues.
She turned in an unfinished painting for the competition.
The renovations remain unfinished after several months of work.
There is still some unfinished homework on the table.
