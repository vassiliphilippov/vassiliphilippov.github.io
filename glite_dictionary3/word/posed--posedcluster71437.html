posed

adjective
carefully arranged for a photograph or painting
Pronunciation: / poʊzd / (AmE), / pəʊzd / (BrE)
(of a person or group) arranged or positioned carefully for a photograph or painting, typically not in a natural way.

Examples

posed for a photo
All the children were posed with big smiles for the camera.
The family photo was carefully posed in front of the fireplace.
The painting depicted several women posed elegantly in period costumes.

  • antonym – spontaneous: not posed or arranged; natural or unplanned
