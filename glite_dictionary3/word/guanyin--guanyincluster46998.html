Guanyin

proper noun
Buddhist Goddess of Mercy
Pronunciation: / ˈgwɑnˌjɪn /; / ɡwɑnˈjin / (AmE), / ˈgwæn.jɪn /; / ɡwænˈjɪn / (BrE)
