overeating

noun
eating too much food
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈriːtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of eating more food than is necessary or healthy.

Examples

holiday overeating
Doctors warn that overeating can increase the risk of serious health problems.
He tried to control his overeating by following a meal plan.
Her overeating during the holidays led to several extra pounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gluttony: habitual greedy or excessive eating
  • antonym – moderation: avoiding excess or extremes, especially in eating or drinking
