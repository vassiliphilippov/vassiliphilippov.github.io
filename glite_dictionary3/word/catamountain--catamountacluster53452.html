catamountain

noun
European wildcat; ancestor of the domestic cat
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈmaʊntɪn / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈmaʊntɪn / (BrE)
a bushy-tailed wildcat of Europe resembling the domestic cat, regarded as the ancestor of the domestic cat
