proportionality

noun
constant ratio between quantities
Pronunciation: / prəˌpɔrʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / prəˌpɔːʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of two quantities having a constant ratio

Examples

mathematical proportionality
Direct proportionality means that as one variable increases, the other does as well.
In mathematics, proportionality is fundamental to understanding similar triangles.
The scientist examined the proportionality between the two chemical substances.

  • synonym – proportion: the relationship of one part to another or to the whole
