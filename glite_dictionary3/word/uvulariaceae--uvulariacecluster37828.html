Uvulariaceae

noun
former plant family, now split
Pronunciation: / juːˌvjuːlɛriˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / juːˌvjuːlɛriˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
a former family of flowering plants once included in some classification systems under the lily family, now split among several families including the autumn crocus and lily families
