rhythmicity

noun
quality of having rhythm in music
Pronunciation: / ɹɪðˈmɪsɪti / (AmE), / ɹɪθˈmɪsɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of having rhythm, especially as imparted by accents and durations of notes in music
