longicorn

noun
beetle with very long antennae
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋ.ɪ.kɔrn / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋ.ɪ.kɔːn / (BrE)
A long-bodied beetle distinguished by very long antennae, especially those of the family Cerambycidae.

The longicorn is easily recognized by its extremely long antennae, often longer than its body.
