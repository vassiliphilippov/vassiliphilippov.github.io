trait

noun
distinctive personal quality or feature
Pronunciation: / treɪt / (AmE), / treɪt / (BrE)
a particular quality or characteristic of someone’s personality or nature that distinguishes them from others

Examples

personality trait
Her creativity is a trait admired by her colleagues.
Honesty is a valuable trait in any friendship.
Patience is not a trait that comes easily to everyone.
