Viscount Nelson

proper noun
English admiral, defeated French at Trafalgar
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪˌkaʊnt ˈnɛlsən / (AmE), / ˈvaɪkaʊnt ˈnɛlsən / (BrE)
A title given to Horatio Nelson, the English admiral who defeated Napoleon's French fleets and died at the Battle of Trafalgar.
