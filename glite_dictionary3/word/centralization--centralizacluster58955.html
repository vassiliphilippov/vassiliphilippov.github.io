centralization

noun
the act of consolidating control centrally
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛntrələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsentrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of bringing control, power, or activities under a single central authority or organization
