spirit

noun
supernatural being or ghost
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
A supernatural and incorporeal being that can sometimes appear or make itself known to humans, such as a ghost or apparition.

Examples

evil spirit
Many believe that the old house is haunted by a spirit who appears at midnight.
She claimed she could communicate with the spirits of the dead during séances.
Some cultures leave offerings to appease wandering spirits in their midst.
