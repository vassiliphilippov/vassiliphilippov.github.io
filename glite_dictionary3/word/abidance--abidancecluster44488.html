abidance

noun
the act of conforming to rules or standards
Pronunciation: / əˈbaɪdəns / (AmE), / əˈbaɪdəns / (BrE)
the act of following or conforming to rules, laws, or accepted standards

Examples

abidance by rules
Corporate abidance with environmental laws is now closely monitored.
She appreciated his abidance by the rules during the competition.
The school's strict abidance to safety regulations has kept students secure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – compliance: the act of obeying a rule or law
  • antonym – noncompliance: failure or refusal to conform to rules or standards
