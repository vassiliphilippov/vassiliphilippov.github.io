flippantly

adverb
in a disrespectful or unserious manner
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪpəntli / (AmE), / ˈflɪpəntli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of respect or seriousness; lightly and often inappropriately.

Examples

answer flippantly
He responded flippantly to the important question, making everyone uncomfortable.
She flippantly dismissed the suggestion without giving it any real thought.
You shouldn't speak so flippantly about matters that affect people's lives.

Related Terms

  • antonym – seriously: in a serious manner; with earnestness
