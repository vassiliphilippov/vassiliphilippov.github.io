to sputter

verb
spit explosively; eject with popping sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌtɚ / (AmE), / ˈspʌtə / (BrE)
To spit or eject something with explosive or popping sounds, often uncontrollably or irregularly.

Examples

to sputter water
She coughed and sputtered after accidentally swallowing some water.
The candle flame began to sputter as the wind blew through the open window.
The old car sputtered to a halt on the side of the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spit: to force saliva or another substance out of the mouth
