to ring

verb
attach an identifying ring to (a bird, etc.)
Pronunciation: / rɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪŋ / (BrE)
to attach a small identifying ring, typically to the leg of a bird, for research or tracking purposes

Examples

to ring a bird
Each bird was carefully ringed before being released back into the wild.
Scientists ring thousands of migratory birds each year to track their movements.
Volunteers helped ring the geese as part of the conservation project.
