authorisation

noun
document granting official permission
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
an official document giving permission or authority to do something

Examples

show authorisation document
Entry is forbidden unless you show a valid authorisation at the gate.
The inspector presented his authorisation before entering the premises.
You will need an authorisation from your manager to access this area.
