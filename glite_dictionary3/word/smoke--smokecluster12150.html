smoke

noun
the act of smoking
Pronunciation: / smoʊk / (AmE), / sməʊk / (BrE)
the act of inhaling and exhaling the fumes of burning tobacco or other substances, especially as a habit or social activity

Examples

have a smoke
He gave up having a smoke every morning as part of his new healthy routine.
Many offices have designated areas where employees can have a smoke.
She stepped outside for a quick smoke during her lunch break.
