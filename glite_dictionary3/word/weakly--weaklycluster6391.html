weakly

adverb
in a feeble or frail manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwikli / (AmE), / ˈwiːkli / (BrE)
in a way that is not strong, forceful, or confident

Examples

to speak weakly
He spoke weakly, barely audible over the noise of the crowd.
She smiled weakly after her illness, unable to stand for long.
The injured bird flapped its wings weakly, struggling to take flight.
He protested weakly, not really wanting to argue.
She smiled weakly as she tried to get out of bed.
The light shone weakly through the thick curtains.
