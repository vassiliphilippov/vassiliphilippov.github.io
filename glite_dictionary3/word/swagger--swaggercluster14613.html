swagger

noun
a proud, confident walk
Pronunciation: / ˈswæɡər / (AmE), / ˈswæɡə / (BrE)
A proud, confident, and sometimes arrogant manner of walking.

Examples

a confident swagger
He entered the room with a noticeable swagger, drawing everyone's attention.
Her confident swagger made it clear she was in charge.
There was a touch of swagger in his steps after winning the award.

Related Terms

  • synonym – strut: to walk in a proud, stiff way
  • degree lower – walk: to move at a regular pace by lifting and setting down each foot in turn
