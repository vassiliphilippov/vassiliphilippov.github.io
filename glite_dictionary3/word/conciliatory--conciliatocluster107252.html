conciliatory

adjective
intended to pacify or reconcile
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪliəˌtɔri / (AmE), / kənˈsɪliətəri /; / kənˈsɪlɪətəri / (BrE)
intended or likely to calm, pacify, or make peace; showing a willingness to ease tension or make concessions
