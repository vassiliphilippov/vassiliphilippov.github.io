eyeball

noun
the round part of the eye
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌbɔːl / (BrE)
the round part of the eye of a vertebrate, contained within the eye socket

Examples

human eyeball
A speck of dust got into her eyeball, making it water.
Some animals have much larger eyeballs relative to their head size.
The doctor examined his eyeball for any signs of infection.
