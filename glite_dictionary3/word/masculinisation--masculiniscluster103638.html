masculinisation

noun
abnormal development of male traits in a female
Pronunciation: / ˌmæskjəlɪnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæskjʊlɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the abnormal development of male sexual characteristics in a female, usually due to hormone therapies or endocrine disorders

Examples

masculinisation of women
Prolonged use of anabolic steroids can lead to masculinisation in women.
Researchers studied the impact of hormone exposure on the masculinisation of female rats.
The doctor explained that adrenal gland disorders might result in masculinisation during puberty.
