megestrol

noun
synthetic hormonal drug, used in cancer treatment
Pronunciation: / məˈdʒɛstrɔl / (AmE), / mɪˈdʒɛstrɒl / (BrE)
a synthetic hormonal drug used especially to treat certain cancers such as endometrial carcinoma and to stimulate appetite in specific medical conditions
