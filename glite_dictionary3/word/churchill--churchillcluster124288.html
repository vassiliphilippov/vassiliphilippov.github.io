Churchill

proper noun
Canadian town on Hudson Bay
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɝːtʃɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃɪl / (BrE)
a town in northern Manitoba, Canada, on Hudson Bay, known as an important port for shipping grain and for its polar bear population.
