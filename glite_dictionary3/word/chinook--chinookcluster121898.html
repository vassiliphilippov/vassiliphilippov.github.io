Chinook

noun
Indigenous language of the Chinook people
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈnʊk / (AmE), / tʃɪˈnʊk / (BrE)
the language of the Chinook people, belonging to the Penutian family, traditionally spoken in the Pacific Northwest of North America

the Chinook language
