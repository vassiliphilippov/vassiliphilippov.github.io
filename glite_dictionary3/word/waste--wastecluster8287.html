waste

noun
barren or uninhabited wilderness
Pronunciation: / weɪst / (AmE), / weɪst / (BrE)
a large area of land that is barren, empty, and not suitable for cultivation or habitation, often considered wild or desolate

Examples

desert waste land
Centuries ago, this region was a desolate waste with little sign of life.
Legends speak of treasure hidden deep within the trackless waste of the northern plains.
The explorers struggled to cross the waste that stretched to the horizon in every direction.

Related Terms

  • synonym – barren: land too poor to produce much or any vegetation
  • antonym – oasis: a fertile spot in a desert
  • synonym – wilderness: an uncultivated, uninhabited, and inhospitable region
