screamer

noun
South American aquatic bird with trumpeting call
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːmər / (AmE), / ˈskriːmə / (BrE)
A large, gooselike aquatic bird of South America with a harsh, trumpeting call, belonging to the family Anhimidae.
