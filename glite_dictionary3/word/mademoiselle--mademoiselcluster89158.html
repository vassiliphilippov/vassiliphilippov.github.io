mademoiselle

noun
a small silvery drumfish
Pronunciation: / ˌmædəmwɑˈzɛl / (AmE), / ˌmadəmwɑˈzɛl / (BrE)
a small silvery drumfish (Leiostomus xanthurus) found along the coasts of the United States from New York to Mexico, often mistaken for white perch
