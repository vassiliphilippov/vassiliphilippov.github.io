jook-sing

noun
person of Chinese descent raised outside Chinese culture
Pronunciation: / dʒʊkˈsɪŋ / (AmE), / dʒʊkˈsɪŋ / (BrE)
(offensive, sometimes derogatory) A person of Chinese descent who is raised outside Chinese culture, often perceived as lacking knowledge of Chinese language or traditions.
