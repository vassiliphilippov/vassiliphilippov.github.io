briquet

noun
block of compressed fuel
Pronunciation: / brɪˈkɛt / (AmE), / brɪˈkɛt / (BrE)
A block made from compressed charcoal or coal dust, used as fuel.

Examples

briquet for grilling
Because of its low smoke, a briquet is often used for indoor heating.
He placed a couple of briquets under the grill before starting to cook.
The campfire was fueled by a single briquet that burned steadily for hours.
