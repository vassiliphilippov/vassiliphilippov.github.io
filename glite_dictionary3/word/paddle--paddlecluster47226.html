paddle

noun
blade on a paddle wheel or water wheel
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpæd.əl / (BrE)
a flat, broad blade attached to the rim of a paddle wheel or water wheel that moves water or is moved by water to provide propulsion or generate power

Examples

paddle on a water wheel
A broken paddle on the old water wheel reduced its efficiency.
Engineers inspected each paddle to ensure the water wheel would function properly.
The paddles of the steamboat's wheel churned the water as it moved down the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blade: flat part of an implement or machine used for cutting, propelling, or moving
