alienated

adjective
feeling isolated or estranged
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪliəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈeɪliəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
feeling isolated or estranged from other people, a group, or society

Examples

alienated from friends
After moving to a new city, Sarah felt alienated from her old friends.
His harsh words left her feeling completely alienated from the family.
Many teenagers feel alienated when they have no one to talk to about their problems.
After moving to a new city, she often felt alienated from her classmates.
He described himself as alienated and disconnected in the large, impersonal city.
Many workers became alienated by the changes in company culture.
