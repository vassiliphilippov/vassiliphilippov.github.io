manageably

adverb
in a manageable way
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒəbli / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is easy to manage or deal with.

Examples

explained steps manageably
She explained the project steps manageably, so everyone understood what to do.
The trail is manageably steep, making it suitable for most hikers.
The workload was divided manageably among the staff to avoid stress.
