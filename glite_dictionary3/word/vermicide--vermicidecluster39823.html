vermicide

noun
substance that kills worms
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrmɪˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈvɜːmɪsaɪd / (BrE)
A substance or agent that kills worms, especially those parasitizing the intestines.

Examples

vermicide tablet for worms
Most vermicides are available in tablet or liquid form for animal use.
Proper dosing of a vermicide is essential to avoid harming healthy tissue.
The doctor prescribed a vermicide to treat the patient’s intestinal infection.
