transmitter

noun
person who sends a message
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmɪtɚ / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a person who sends or conveys a message to another; the sender in a communication process

Examples

message transmitter
A successful transmitter tailors information to the needs of the audience.
In any conversation, the transmitter carefully chooses words to ensure the message is clear.
The misunderstanding occurred because the transmitter failed to provide enough context.
