breezily

adverb
in a cheerful or casual way
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːzɪli / (AmE), / ˈbriːzɪli / (BrE)
In a cheerful, casual, and confident manner; without concern or anxiety.

Examples

to smile breezily
He walked breezily into the interview room, full of confidence.
She answered breezily, showing no sign of stress despite the difficult questions.
They discussed the project breezily, as if there were no deadlines approaching.

Related Terms

  • synonym – breezy: cheerful, relaxed, and unconcerned
