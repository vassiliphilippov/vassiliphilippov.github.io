asymptotically

adverb
in a way that approaches an asymptote
Pronunciation: / ˌæs.ɪmpˈtɑ.tɪk.li / (AmE), / ˌæs.ɪmpˈtɒ.tɪk.li / (BrE)
In a way that approaches a given value or line (an asymptote) more and more closely, usually without ever reaching it, especially in mathematics and science.

Examples
