platen

noun
flat plate in a press or printer
Pronunciation: / ˈplætn̩ / (AmE), / ˈplætən / (BrE)
the flat plate in a printing press or similar machine that presses paper or other material against a surface to create an impression

Examples

platen presses paper
An old letterpress uses a platen to press paper onto the inked type.
The rubber covering on the platen needed replacement after years of use.
The technician carefully adjusted the platen before starting the printing process.
