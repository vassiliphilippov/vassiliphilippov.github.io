mate

noun
South American holly; leaves for tea-like drink
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːteɪ / (AmE), / ˈmæteɪ / (BrE)
A South American holly tree (Ilex paraguariensis), whose dried leaves are used to make a stimulating beverage similar to tea.

Examples

yerba mate
Many people in Argentina start their day with a cup of mate instead of coffee.
She bought a pouch of mate while exploring a local market in Uruguay.
The dried leaves of mate are steeped in hot water to create a strong beverage.
