to advance

verb
pay money before due
Pronunciation: / ədˈvæns / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
to pay (money) to someone before it is due or before work is completed

Examples

to advance money
Can you advance me some money until payday?
She asked her employer to advance her salary for the upcoming holiday.
The company agreed to advance payment for the project.
