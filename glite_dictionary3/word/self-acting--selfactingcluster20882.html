self-acting

adjective
operating automatically
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈæktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈæktɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a machine or process) operating automatically without the need for human intervention.

Examples

self-acting machine
Early textile mills used self-acting spinning machines to increase efficiency.
The self-acting valve shuts off the water supply in case of an emergency.
This self-acting mechanism eliminates the need for constant supervision.

Related Terms

  • synonym – automatic: working by itself with little or no direct human control
  • antonym – manual: operated or done by hand rather than automatically
