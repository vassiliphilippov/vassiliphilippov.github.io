darkness

noun
state of ignorance or unenlightenment
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːrknəs / (AmE), / ˈdɑːknəs / (BrE)
A state of ignorance or lack of knowledge and understanding; unenlightenment.

Examples

darkness of ignorance
His inspiring words helped dispel the darkness of my confusion.
Superstition often thrives in conditions of darkness and fear.
The new discoveries lifted the community out of intellectual darkness.

Related Terms

  • antonym – enlightenment: the state of having knowledge or understanding (esp. as opposed to ignorance)
  • synonym – ignorance: lack of knowledge or awareness
