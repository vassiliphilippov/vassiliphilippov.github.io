Fumimaro Konoe

proper noun
Japanese statesman and wartime prime minister
Pronunciation: / fuːmiˈmɑːroʊ koʊˈnoʊeɪ / (AmE), / fuːmiˈmɑːrəʊ kəˈnəʊeɪ / (BrE)
