to wear

verb
(of materials) last and stay usable
Pronunciation: / wɛr / (AmE), / wɛə / (BrE)
(of a material or product) to last and remain in good condition through use over time.

Examples

wear well
The paint on the walls did not wear as well as expected.
These shoes wear well even after years of daily use.
Wool carpets wear better than synthetic ones in busy areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym – deteriorate: to become worse over time or through use
  • synonym – endure: to last for a long time; to continue to exist
