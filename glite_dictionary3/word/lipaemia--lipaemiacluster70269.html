lipaemia

noun
excess lipids in the blood
Pronunciation: / lɪˈpiːmiə / (AmE), / lɪˈpiːmiə / (BrE)
the presence of an abnormally high concentration of lipids in the blood

Examples

lipaemia in blood test
Patients with uncontrolled diabetes are more likely to develop lipaemia.
Severe lipaemia can increase the risk of cardiovascular disease if left untreated.
The doctor ordered a blood test to check for lipaemia after noticing elevated cholesterol levels.
