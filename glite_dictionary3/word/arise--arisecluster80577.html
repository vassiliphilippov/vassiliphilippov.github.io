to arise

verb
move upward (smoke, mist, etc.)
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪz / (AmE), / əˈraɪz / (BrE)
(of smoke, mist, etc.) to move or go upward into the air.

Examples

arise above smoke
As the sun warmed the field, a gentle mist began to arise above the grass.
Early morning fog arose from the river, shrouding the valley in mystery.
Thick smoke could be seen arising from the distant volcano after the eruption.

  • synonym – rise: to move upward; to go up
