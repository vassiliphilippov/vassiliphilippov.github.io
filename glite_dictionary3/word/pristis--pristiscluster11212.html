Pristis

noun
genus of sawfishes
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪstɪs / (AmE), / ˈprɪstɪs / (BrE)
A genus of large cartilaginous fishes known as sawfishes, characterized by their long, flattened rostrum edged with teeth.
