insensitive

adjective
not responsive to physical stimuli
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛnsətɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛnsɪtɪv / (BrE)
not responsive to physical stimuli; not affected or changed by something such as touch, heat, or light.

Examples

insensitive to pain
Some bacteria are insensitive to certain antibiotics, making infections harder to treat.
The device is insensitive to minor vibrations, ensuring accurate measurements.
This material is insensitive to changes in temperature and remains stable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unresponsive: not reacting to stimuli or influences
