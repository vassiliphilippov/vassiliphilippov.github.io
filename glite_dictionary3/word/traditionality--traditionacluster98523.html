traditionality

noun
strict adherence to tradition
Pronunciation: / trəˌdɪʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / trəˌdɪʃəˈnæləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of adhering strictly to traditional methods or teachings

Examples

strong traditionality
Her art exhibits both creativity and a certain traditionality in its techniques.
Some communities value traditionality over innovation when it comes to education methods.
The traditionality of the ceremony impressed foreign visitors with its strong sense of cultural identity.

Related Terms

  • antonym – modernity: the quality or state of being modern
  • synonym – traditionalism: the upholding or maintenance of tradition, especially so as to resist change
