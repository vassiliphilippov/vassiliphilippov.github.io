trick

noun
round of cards in a card game
Pronunciation: / trɪk / (AmE), / trɪk / (BrE)
In card games, a round in which each player plays one card, and the player with the highest card wins.

Examples

win a trick in cards
Each player tries to take as many tricks as possible during the game.
If you cannot follow suit, you cannot win the trick.
She won the first trick with her ace of spades.
