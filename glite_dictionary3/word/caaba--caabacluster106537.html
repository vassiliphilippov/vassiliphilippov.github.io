Caaba

noun
holy Islamic shrine in Mecca
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːbə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbə / (BrE)
The sacred stone building in Mecca that is the most important shrine in Islam; Muslims face toward it when praying.

Examples

the Caaba in Mecca
Muslims around the world face the Caaba when they perform their daily prayers.
The Caaba is draped in a black cloth known as the kiswah during the annual Hajj.
Thousands of pilgrims travel to Mecca each year to visit the Caaba.
