gawker

noun
person who stares foolishly or openly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔːkər / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who stares openly and often foolishly or rudely, especially at something unusual or interesting

Examples

curious gawker
A crowd of gawkers gathered around the accident, blocking the road.
She disliked being treated like an object by passing gawkers at the zoo.
The police dispersed the gawkers who had stopped to watch the fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bystander: a person who is present at an event but does not participate
  • synonym – onlooker: a person who watches something without taking part
  • synonym – spectator: a person who watches an event
