Lucrezia Borgia

proper noun
Italian noblewoman and Renaissance patron
Pronunciation: / luːˈkreɪtsiə ˈbɔrdʒə / (AmE), / luːˈkreɪtsiə ˈbɔːdʒiə / (BrE)
