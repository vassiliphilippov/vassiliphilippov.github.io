slicker

noun
waterproof raincoat, often treated cotton
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪkər / (AmE), / ˈslɪkə / (BrE)
a raincoat, especially one made of cotton fabric treated with oil or pigment to make it waterproof.

Examples

yellow slicker
Children splashed through puddles in bright slickers after the downpour.
She wore her yellow slicker as the storm rolled in.
The fisherman pulled on his old slicker before heading out to sea.
