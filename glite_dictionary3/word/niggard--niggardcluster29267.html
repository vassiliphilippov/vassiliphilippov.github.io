niggard

noun
mean or miserly person
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪɡərd / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡəd / (BrE)
An excessively mean or miserly person; someone who is unwilling to give or spend.

Examples

a niggard with money
Despite his wealth, he was known in town as a notorious niggard.
No one invited the niggard to the charity banquet, knowing he would never contribute.
The old niggard refused to donate a penny to the poor.

Related Terms

  • synonym – miser: a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible
  • antonym – spendthrift: a person who spends money carelessly or extravagantly
