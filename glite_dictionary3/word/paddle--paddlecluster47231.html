to paddle

verb
to swim by moving hands or feet, like a dog
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpæd.əl / (BrE)
to move through water with short movements of the hands or feet, especially in a manner resembling a dog swimming

Examples

to paddle with feet
She paddled across the stream without getting completely wet.
The children paddled in the shallow end of the pool, laughing as they moved their arms and legs.
The puppy fell into the pond and began to paddle instinctively to stay afloat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dog-paddle: to swim by moving arms and legs like a dog
