bellbottom

adjective
trousers flared at the bottom
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛlˌbɑːtəm / (AmE), / ˈbɛlˌbɒtəm / (BrE)
(of trousers) having legs that become wider from the knees downward; flaring at the bottom.

The vintage shop specializes in bellbottom trousers from the 1970s.
