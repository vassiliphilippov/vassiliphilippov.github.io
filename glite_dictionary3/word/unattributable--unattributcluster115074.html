unattributable

adjective
not able to be attributed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈtrɪbjətəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtrɪbjʊtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be attributed to a particular source, author, or cause.

Examples

unattributable quote
