course

noun
part of a meal served at one time
Pronunciation: / kɔrs / (AmE), / kɔːs / (BrE)
one of the separate parts of a meal served separately, such as the starter, main dish, or dessert

Examples

three-course meal
A traditional French meal might include several courses.
For dessert, the final course was a rich chocolate cake.
They served the soup as the first course of the dinner.
