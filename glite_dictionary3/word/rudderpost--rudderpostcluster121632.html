rudderpost

noun
vertical post for rudder pivot
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌdərˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈrʌdəˌpəʊst / (BrE)
a vertical post at the forward edge of a rudder, about which the rudder pivots

Examples

rudderpost of a boat
A strong rudderpost is essential for safe and responsive steering in rough waters.
During repairs, they discovered damage where the rudderpost connected to the hull.
The shipbuilder inspected the rudderpost for any signs of corrosion.
