murmuring

adjective
making a low, continuous sound
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜrmərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːm(ə)rɪŋ / (BrE)
making a low, continuous, and indistinct sound, like soft voices or gentle waves

Examples

murmuring voices
He heard murmuring voices in the quiet library, barely above a whisper.
In the forest, a murmuring wind passed gently through the tall trees.
The murmuring river soothed her as she walked along its banks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – susurrant: making a soft, whispering or rustling sound
