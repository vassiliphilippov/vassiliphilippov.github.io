centner

noun
unit of weight (100 kilograms)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnt.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛnt.nə / (BrE)
a unit of weight equal to 100 kilograms, used chiefly in some European countries.

Examples

a centner of potatoes
Farmers recorded their potato harvest in centners per hectare.
The shipment weighed five centners and required special handling.
Wheat was sold by the centner in several Eastern European markets.
