witch

noun
female Wiccan practitioner
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃ / (AmE), / wɪtʃ / (BrE)
A female believer or practitioner of Wicca, a modern pagan religion involving nature-based rituals and beliefs.

Examples

modern witch
Many young women today identify as a witch and practice modern pagan traditions.
She considers herself a witch and celebrates the rituals of Wicca.
The local witch led the group in a ceremony honoring the solstice.
