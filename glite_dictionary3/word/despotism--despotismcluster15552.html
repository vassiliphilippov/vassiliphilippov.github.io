despotism

noun
absolute rule by a dictator
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛspəˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdɛspətɪz(ə)m /; / ˈdɛspətɪzəm / (BrE)
a system of government or rule where one person or group has absolute power, often used in a cruel or oppressive way
