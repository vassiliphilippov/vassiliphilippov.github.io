teredo

noun
marine mollusk (shipworm genus)
Pronunciation: / təˈriːdoʊ / (AmE), / təˈriːdəʊ / (BrE)
A marine bivalve mollusk of the genus Teredo, commonly known as a shipworm, which bores into and often destroys submerged wood.
