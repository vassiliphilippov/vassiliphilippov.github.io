splotch

noun
irregularly shaped spot or mark
Pronunciation: / splɑtʃ / (AmE), / splɒtʃ / (BrE)
a large, irregularly shaped mark or spot, especially one of color or dirt

Examples

ink splotch

There was a bright red splotch on his shirt where the tomato sauce had landed.
