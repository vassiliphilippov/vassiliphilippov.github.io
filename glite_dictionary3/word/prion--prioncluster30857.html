prion

noun
infectious protein particle (microbiology)
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪ.ɑn / (AmE), / ˈpriː.ɒn / (BrE)
a type of infectious protein particle, lacking nucleic acid, believed to cause certain degenerative diseases of the nervous system
