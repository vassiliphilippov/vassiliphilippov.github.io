mackerel

noun
a type of marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈmæk.ɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˈmæk.ər.əl / (BrE)
A marine fish of the family Scombridae, known for its streamlined body and commonly used as food.

Examples

school of mackerel
Mackerel are known for swimming in large schools in the open ocean.
Grilled mackerel with lemon is a popular dish in many coastal regions.
The fisherman proudly displayed a large mackerel he had just caught.
