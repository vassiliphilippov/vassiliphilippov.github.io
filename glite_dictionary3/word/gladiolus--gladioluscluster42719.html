Gladiolus

noun
genus of flowering plants (gladiolas)
Pronunciation: / ɡlædˈiːələs / (AmE), / ˌɡlæd.iˈəʊ.ləs / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the iris family, known for their tall spikes of brightly colored flowers, commonly called gladiolas.
