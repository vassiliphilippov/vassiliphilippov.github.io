bairn

noun
child (Scottish/Northern English)
Pronunciation: / bɛrn / (AmE), / bɛːn / (BrE)
(chiefly Scottish and Northern English) a child; son or daughter.

Examples

young bairn
During the festival, every bairn in the village joined the parade.
He moved to the city when he was just a bairn.
Her grandmother told stories to the youngest bairn by the fire.

Related Terms

  • antonym – adult: a fully grown person
  • synonym – child: a young human being below the age of puberty
