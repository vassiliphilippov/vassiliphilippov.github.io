consistency

noun
firmness or thickness of a substance
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪstənsi / (AmE), / kənˈsɪstənsi / (BrE)
the quality or state of being firm, thick, or cohesive and retaining shape, especially in a substance

Examples

consistency of dough
For best results, cookie dough should have a thick consistency that is easy to shape.
The chef tested the consistency of the sauce before serving it to the guests.
This paint is known for its smooth consistency, making it perfect for detailed work.

  • synonym – density: the degree of compactness of a substance
  • antonym – fluidity: the quality of being able to flow easily
  • synonym – texture: the feel and appearance of a surface or substance
