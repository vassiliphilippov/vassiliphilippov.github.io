eastbound

adjective
going toward the east
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈiːstˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle, route, or movement) going or heading toward the east.

Examples

eastbound traffic
Heavy traffic was reported on the eastbound lanes of the highway this morning.
