meter

noun
measuring instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtɚ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure and display the amount, degree, or quantity of something, such as electricity, gas, or speed

Examples

electricity meter
A parking meter only accepts coins for payment.
Please check the water meter when you move into your new apartment.
The electricity meter showed a significant increase in usage this month.
