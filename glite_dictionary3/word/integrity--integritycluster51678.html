integrity

noun
honesty; strong moral principles
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛɡrəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛɡrəti / (BrE)
the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles

Examples

personal integrity
A company known for its integrity attracts loyal customers and talented employees.
He refused to compromise his integrity for personal gain.
The judge was respected for her integrity and commitment to justice.
