inclusive

adjective
including everything or stated limits
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluːsɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːsɪv / (BrE)
including everything or everyone, and especially including the limits or endpoints stated.

Examples

inclusive of all people
Please state the exact dates, from May 1 to May 7 inclusive.
The rent is inclusive of all utilities, so you won’t have extra bills.
Their museum pass has an inclusive price that covers all exhibits.
