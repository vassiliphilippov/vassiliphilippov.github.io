intermeshed

adjective
fitted parts working together
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmɛʃt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɛʃt / (BrE)
(of toothed parts or gears) interlocked and working together by fitting into each other.

Examples

intermeshed gears
Each engine drives a pair of intermeshed gears that turn the main rotor.
The designer ensured that the intermeshed rotors would never collide during operation.
You can see the intermeshed cogs spinning inside the transparent clock case.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disengaged: not interlocked or not working together (of gears)
  • synonym – meshed: (of gears) engaged or fitted together
