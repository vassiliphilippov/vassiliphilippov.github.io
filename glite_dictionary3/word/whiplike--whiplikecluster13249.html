whiplike

adjective
resembling a whip
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪp.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪp.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a whip, usually long, thin, and flexible.

Examples

whiplike tail
Her hair flew out in whiplike strands as she spun around.
Some bacteria move using a single whiplike flagellum.
The lizard used its whiplike tail to defend itself against predators.
