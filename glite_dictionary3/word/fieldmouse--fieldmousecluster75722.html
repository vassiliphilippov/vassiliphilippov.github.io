fieldmouse

noun
a small nocturnal Old World mouse
Pronunciation: / ˈfildˌmaʊs / (AmE), / ˈfiːldˌmaʊs / (BrE)
A small nocturnal mouse of the genus Apodemus commonly found in woods, fields, and gardens in the Old World.

Examples

a fieldmouse in grass
After dusk, a fieldmouse crept out of its burrow to forage in the garden.
Owls often hunt for fieldmice at the edge of woods.
The fieldmouse scurried quickly through the tall grass, searching for seeds to eat.
