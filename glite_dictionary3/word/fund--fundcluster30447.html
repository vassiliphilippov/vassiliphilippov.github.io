to fund

verb
provide money to pay debts or interest
Pronunciation: / fʌnd / (AmE), / fʌnd / (BrE)
to provide or save money for a particular purpose, project, or expense

Examples

to fund a project
Governments often fund infrastructure improvements through the sale of bonds.

  • synonym – save: to keep money for future use instead of spending it
  • antonym – spend: to use money to pay for something
  • synonym – sponsor: to support something financially, especially an event or activity
