balancing

noun
making things correspond or agree
Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.ən.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbæl.ənsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making two or more things correspond or be in agreement, especially by comparing and adjusting them

Examples

balancing accounts
Proper balancing of accounts is essential for any business.
She spent the afternoon balancing her expenses with her bank statement.
The accountant reviewed the balancing of the two sets of figures for discrepancies.
