neuter

noun
grammatical gender: neither masculine nor feminine
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtə / (BrE)
a grammatical gender denoting words that are neither masculine nor feminine, usually referring to inanimate objects or abstract concepts
