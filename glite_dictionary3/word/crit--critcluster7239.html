crit

noun
short-course bicycle race
Pronunciation: / krɪt / (AmE), / krɪt / (BrE)
a bicycle race held on a short course, typically less than 5 km (3 miles) in length

Examples

win a crit race
Many professional cyclists started their careers in crits across Europe.
She trained hard for the local crit in her city.
The crit course featured sharp corners and fast sprints.
