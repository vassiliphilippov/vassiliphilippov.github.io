rivalrous

adjective
eager to compete or surpass others
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪvəlrəs / (AmE), / ˈraɪvəlrəs / (BrE)
having a strong desire to compete with or surpass others; characterized by rivalry.

Examples

rivalrous competition
The siblings' rivalrous relationship pushed them to achieve more than they might have alone.
The two teams maintained a rivalrous attitude throughout the competition, each striving to outdo the other.
There was a rivalrous energy in the office that encouraged employees to exceed their goals.
