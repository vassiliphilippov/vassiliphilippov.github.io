luster

noun
a soft shine or glow
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌstɚ / (AmE), / ˈlʌstə / (BrE)
a gentle sheen or soft glow, especially that of a surface that reflects light

Examples

silver's luster
After polishing the silverware, its original luster was restored.
The diamond's luster made it particularly attractive to collectors.
The pearl's soft luster set it apart from the common stones.
