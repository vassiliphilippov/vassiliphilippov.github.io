decent

adjective
appropriate or proper for a context
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːsənt / (AmE), / ˈdiːsənt / (BrE)
appropriate or proper for a particular person, occasion, or context; conforming to what is suitable or fitting

Examples

decent burial
It is only right to give her a decent burial surrounded by her loved ones.
The host ensured that every guest received a decent welcome upon arrival.
Wearing formal clothing was considered decent for the solemn ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fitting: suitable or appropriate; right for a particular purpose or occasion
  • antonym – improper: not suitable or appropriate for the circumstances
