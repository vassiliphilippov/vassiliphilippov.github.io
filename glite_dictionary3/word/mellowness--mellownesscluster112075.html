mellowness

noun
pleasant, relaxed mood or character
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛloʊnəs /; / ˈmɛl.oʊ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɛlənəs /; / ˈmɛl.əʊ.nəs / (BrE)
a state of relaxed good humor, kindness, or enjoyment, often resulting from maturity or a relaxed mood
