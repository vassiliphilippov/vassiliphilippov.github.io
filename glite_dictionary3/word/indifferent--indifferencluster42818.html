indifferent

adjective
impartial; unbiased
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪf.ɚ.ənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪf.ər.ənt / (BrE)
not favoring one side or person over another; impartial or unbiased.

Examples

indifferent judge
It is crucial that the judge remains indifferent throughout the entire trial.
Journalists are expected to report the news in an indifferent manner.
The administrator was praised for her indifferent handling of the dispute.
