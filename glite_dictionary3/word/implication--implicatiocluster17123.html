implication

noun
involvement, especially suggesting guilt
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpləˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌɪm.pləˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌɪm.plɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the fact of being involved or connected to something, especially something wrong or illegal

Examples

Despite his denial, the evidence suggested his implication in the scheme.
