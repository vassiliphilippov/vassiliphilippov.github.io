commercial

adjective
related to commerce or business
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɝː.ʃəl /; / kəˈmɝːʃəl / (AmE), / kəˈmɜː.ʃəl /; / kəˈmɜːʃəl / (BrE)
related to business, commerce, or making a profit (including goods or activities intended for business use or suitable for sale)
