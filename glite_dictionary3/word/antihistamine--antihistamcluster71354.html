antihistamine

noun
drug for treating allergies and colds
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈhɪstəˌmin / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈhɪ.stə.miːn / (BrE)
a drug that counteracts the effects of histamine, used especially to treat allergies and colds

Examples

take an antihistamine tablet
Before you travel, pack an antihistamine in case of unexpected allergic reactions.
People often use an antihistamine to reduce itching after a mosquito bite.
The doctor recommended taking an antihistamine to relieve my hay fever symptoms.
