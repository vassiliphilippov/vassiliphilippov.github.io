burly

adjective
large, muscular in build
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːli / (AmE), / ˈbɜːli / (BrE)
(of a person) large and strong, with a heavy, muscular build.

Examples

burly man
Despite his burly appearance, he had a gentle and kind personality.
Several burly firefighters carried heavy equipment up the stairs during the emergency.
The burly security guard easily restrained the unruly fan at the concert.

  • synonym – husky: strong and heavily built (of a person)
  • antonym – slender: thin, slim in build
  • synonym – strapping: tall and strong; robust
