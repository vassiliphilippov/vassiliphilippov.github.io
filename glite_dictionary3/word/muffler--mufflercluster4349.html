muffler

noun
scarf worn around the neck
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌf.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌf.lə / (BrE)
A scarf or wrap worn around the neck to keep it warm.

Examples

wool muffler scarf
During winter, a thick muffler is essential to stay warm.
He wrapped his woolen muffler snugly around his neck before stepping outside.
She wore a blue muffler to protect herself from the chilly wind.
