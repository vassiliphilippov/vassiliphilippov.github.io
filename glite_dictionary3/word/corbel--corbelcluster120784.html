to corbel

verb
to support or furnish with a corbel
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɔːbəl / (BrE)
to support or furnish with a corbel, especially in architecture; to project (a structure or part) by means of a corbel.

Examples

to corbel a ledge
Stone blocks were carefully corbelled to create the decorative ledge above the doorway.
The ancient temple's roof was corbelled out over the entrance for additional shade.
The architect decided to corbel the balcony, giving it an elegant, projecting look.

Related Terms

  • synonym – support: to bear the weight of something; to hold up
