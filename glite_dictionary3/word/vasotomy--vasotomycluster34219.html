vasotomy

noun
surgical incision of vas deferens
Pronunciation: / vəˈsɑtəmi / (AmE), / vəˈsɒtəmi / (BrE)
A surgical incision into or division of the vas deferens.

Examples

vasotomy procedure
A vasotomy is sometimes required to access blocked seminal ducts.
Recovery after a vasotomy usually involves minimal discomfort.
