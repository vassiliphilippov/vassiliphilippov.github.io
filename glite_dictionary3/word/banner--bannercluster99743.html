banner

noun
full-width newspaper headline
Pronunciation: / ˈbænər / (AmE), / ˈbænə / (BrE)
a newspaper headline that runs across the full width of a page, often used for important or dramatic stories

Examples

banner headline
Readers were drawn in by the striking banner at the top of the newspaper.
The editor decided to use a large banner for the major story of the day.
The front page featured a banner announcing the historic peace agreement.
