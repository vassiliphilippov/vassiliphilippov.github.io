to impose

verb
to order and collect (tax, fine etc.)
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpoʊz / (AmE), / ɪmˈpəʊz / (BrE)
to officially order that a rule, tax, fine, or punishment must be paid or obeyed, and to collect it

Examples

to impose a fine
Local authorities have the power to impose penalties on businesses that violate health regulations.
The government decided to impose a new tax on luxury goods starting next year.
The university can impose fines on students who damage library property.

Related Terms

  • synonym – levy: to impose and collect (a tax, fee, fine, etc.)
