float

noun
tool for smoothing plaster or cement
Pronunciation: / floʊt / (AmE), / fləʊt / (BrE)
a hand tool with a flat face, used for smoothing and finishing the surface of plaster, cement, or stucco

Examples

plaster float tool
After applying the final coat of plaster, the mason used a float to create a perfectly smooth surface.
Proper use of a float ensures that stucco walls have an even, attractive texture.
The worker selected a wooden float to finish the edges of the wet cement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – trowel: a small hand tool with a flat blade, used for applying and shaping plaster, cement, or mortar
