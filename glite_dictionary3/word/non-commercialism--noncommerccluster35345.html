non-commercialism

noun
rejection of commercial activity
Pronunciation: / nɑn kəˈmɜr.ʃəˌlɪz.əm / (AmE), / nɒn kəˈmɜː.ʃəl.ɪ.zəm / (BrE)
The principle or practice of not being involved in commercial activities or enterprises.

Examples
