to pace

verb
measure distance by stepping
Pronunciation: / peɪs / (AmE), / peɪs / (BrE)
to measure a distance by counting the number of steps taken while walking

Examples

to pace a distance
She paces the garden to mark where the new fence will go.
The surveyor paced the length of the field to estimate its size.
We paced out the distance between the two buildings before digging.
