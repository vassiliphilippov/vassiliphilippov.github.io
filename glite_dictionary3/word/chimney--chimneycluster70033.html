chimney

noun
glass tube on an oil lamp
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmni / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmni / (BrE)
a glass tube or enclosure surrounding the flame of an oil lamp to protect it and improve combustion

Examples

lamp chimney glass
He cleaned the chimney of the oil lamp to ensure a brighter flame.
Make sure the chimney is placed carefully over the wick before lighting the lamp.
The old lantern still had its original chimney despite years of use.
