hazy

adjective
indistinct or blurred in outline
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzi / (AmE), / ˈheɪzi / (BrE)
not clear or distinct; blurred or obscured in outline or detail

Examples

hazy childhood memories
Her memories of childhood were hazy, like a dream half remembered.
On hot summer afternoons, the mountains appear hazy in the distance.
The photograph was so hazy that we couldn't make out anyone's face.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blurry: lacking clear definition; making it difficult to see details
  • antonym – clear: easy to see or distinguish; not blurred
