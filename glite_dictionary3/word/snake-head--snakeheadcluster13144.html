snake-head

noun
illegal people smuggler (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈsneɪkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈsneɪkˌhɛd / (BrE)
(informal) a person who illegally arranges for other people to be smuggled across borders, especially the leader of an organized smuggling group.

Examples

illegal snake-head leader
Police arrested the alleged snake-head who had organized dozens of illegal crossings last year.
Journalists reported that the gang's snake-head negotiated payments and travel documents for migrants.
Survivors described how smugglers tricked them and handed them over to ruthless snake-heads.
