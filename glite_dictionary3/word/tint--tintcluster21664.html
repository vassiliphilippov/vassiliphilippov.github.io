tint

noun
slight variation of a color
Pronunciation: / tɪnt / (AmE), / tɪnt / (BrE)
A slight variation or degree of a particular color; a shade that differs only a little from another.

Examples

a pale tint
Light passing through the stained-glass window gave the room a warm tint.
Lipsticks come in every tint imaginable, from soft pink to deep plum.
The artist added a pale tint of blue to the sky in his painting.
