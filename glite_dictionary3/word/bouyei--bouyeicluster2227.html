Bouyei

noun
member of the Bouyei people
Pronunciation: / boʊˈjeɪ / (AmE), / bəʊˈjeɪ / (BrE)
A member of the Bouyei (Buyi) ethnic group, a Tai-speaking people native to Guizhou and nearby provinces of China.

Examples

Bouyei ethnic group
A Bouyei elder showed us traditional embroidery patterns passed down through her family.
The Bouyei community in Guizhou holds an annual festival with music and dance.
Researchers interviewed three young Bouyei about language transmission and cultural identity.
