to rustle

verb
make a soft crackling sound
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌs.əl / (AmE), / ˈrʌs.əl / (BrE)
to make or cause to make a soft, dry, crackling sound

Examples

to rustle leaves
As he walked, his trousers rustled softly against each other.
She heard someone rustling papers in the office next door.
The autumn leaves rustle as the wind sweeps through the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym – crackle: make a series of small, sharp sounds
  • degree lower – whisper: speak very quietly
