extravagance

noun
excessive or wasteful spending
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstrævəɡəns / (AmE), / ɪkˈstrævəɡəns / (BrE)
the act or quality of spending money in an excessive or wasteful way

Examples

financial extravagance
Critics blame government extravagance for the rise in taxes.
He regretted his youthful extravagance once he saw his empty bank account.
The family's extravagance soon led them into debt.
His extravagance quickly depleted the family fortune.
Learning to avoid extravagance can help you save money and manage your budget better.
The company's extravagance on luxury offices raised questions about its priorities.

Related Terms

  • antonym – frugality: careful use of resources, avoiding waste
  • synonym – profligacy: reckless extravagance or wastefulness
  • synonym – wastefulness: the quality of using more than is needed
