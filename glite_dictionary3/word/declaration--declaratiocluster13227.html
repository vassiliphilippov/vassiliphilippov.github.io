declaration

noun
final bid in contract bridge
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
In contract bridge, the final bid that establishes the contract and determines the number of tricks the bidder must make.
