exposure

noun
vulnerability to weather conditions
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊʒə / (BrE)
the condition of being unprotected from severe weather or environmental conditions, such as wind, rain, cold, or heat

Examples

death from exposure
Many casualties of the disaster were found to have died due to exposure.
Prolonged exposure to extreme cold can cause frostbite or hypothermia.
The hikers suffered from exposure after getting lost in the mountains overnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym – protection: the state of being kept safe from harm or danger, especially from the weather
