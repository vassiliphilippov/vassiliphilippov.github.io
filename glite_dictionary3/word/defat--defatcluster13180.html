to defat

verb
remove fat from something
Pronunciation: / diˈfæt / (AmE), / diˈfæt / (BrE)
to remove fat from something, especially from food or a substance during processing

Examples

to defat meat
After roasting the chicken, she used a spoon to defat the gravy.
Food manufacturers often defat products to make low-fat versions for consumers.
The laboratory technician carefully defatted the tissue sample before analysis.
