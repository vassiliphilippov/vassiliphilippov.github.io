lam

noun
sudden escape, especially from police
Pronunciation: / lӕm / (AmE), / lam / (BrE)
a rapid escape from a place, especially to avoid arrest or capture

Examples

on the lam after crime
After the robbery, the suspects were on the lam for days before being caught.
Following the scandal, she disappeared and was rumored to be on the lam in another country.
The notorious gangster went on the lam to avoid prosecution.

Related Terms

  • antonym – capture: the act of being caught or arrested
  • synonym – getaway: an escape, especially after committing a crime
