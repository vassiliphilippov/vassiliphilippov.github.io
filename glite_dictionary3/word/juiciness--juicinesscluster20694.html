juiciness

noun
the quality of being juicy
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːsi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːsi.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of containing a lot of juice and being appetizing, especially in food.

Examples

the juiciness of steak
Her favorite burgers are known for their incredible juiciness and flavor.
Proper cooking can enhance the juiciness of chicken breast.
The juiciness of the peach made it perfect for a summer snack.
