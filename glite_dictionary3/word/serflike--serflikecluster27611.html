serflike

adjective
like a serf; servile or oppressed
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝf.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɜːf.laɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of a serf; showing the qualities of someone in servitude or subjugation.

Examples

serflike obedience
Decades after emancipation, villagers still lived a serflike existence under their landlords.
Her serflike obedience annoyed her more independent colleagues.
The workers endured serflike conditions, with long hours and little pay.
