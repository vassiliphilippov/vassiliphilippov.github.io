distortion

noun
image defect from imperfect optics
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɔrʃən /; / dɪˈstɔɹʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈstɔːʃən / (BrE)
a change in the appearance, shape, or form of something so that it is no longer accurate or true to its original
