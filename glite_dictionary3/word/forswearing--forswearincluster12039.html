forswearing

noun
act of formally giving up something
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈswɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːˈswɛərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of formally renouncing, giving up, or surrendering a right, possession, or privilege

Examples
