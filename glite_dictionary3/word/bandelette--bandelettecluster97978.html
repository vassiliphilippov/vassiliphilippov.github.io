bandelette

noun
narrow ring-shaped molding on column
Pronunciation: / bæn.dəˈlɛt / (AmE), / bænd.ɪˈlɛt / (BrE)
A narrow molding forming a ring, typically found at the top of a column in classical architecture.

bandelette on a column
