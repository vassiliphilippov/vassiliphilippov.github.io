troubled

adjective
financially distressed; at risk of default
Pronunciation: / c8tr8cb59ld / (AmE), / c8tr8cb59ld / (BrE)
Of a company, bank, asset, or market: in serious financial difficulty, suffering losses or declining credit quality and at increased risk of insolvency or default.

Examples

troubled company
The troubled lender announced it would seek rescue funds after mounting losses.
Investors sold shares in the troubled bank following the unexpected downgrade.
Regulators moved quickly to stabilize the troubled market and restore depositor confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym – distressed: (finance) suffering significant financial problems or loss of value
