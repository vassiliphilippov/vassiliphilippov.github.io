to possess

verb
have (a quality or knowledge)
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛs / (AmE), / pəˈzɛs / (BrE)
to have or own something, such as an object, quality, skill, or attribute

Examples

She possesses exceptional musical talent and dedication.
This candidate possesses the experience needed for the position.
Despite her young age, she possessed remarkable confidence and independence.
He possesses a deep knowledge of classical music and history.
The wealthy collector possessed several rare and valuable paintings.
