shawl

noun
large cloth worn over shoulders or head
Pronunciation: / ʃɑl / (AmE), / ʃɔːl / (BrE)
a large piece of cloth, usually rectangular or square, worn around the shoulders or head, especially by women, for warmth or as a decoration.

Examples

shawl over shoulders
He bought a handmade shawl from the local market as a souvenir.
She wrapped a silk shawl around her shoulders as the evening grew colder.
The old woman wore a colorful shawl over her head during winter.
