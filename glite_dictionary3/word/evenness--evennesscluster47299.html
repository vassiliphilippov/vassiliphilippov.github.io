evenness

noun
quality of being balanced or uniform
Pronunciation: / ˈiːvənnəs / (AmE), / ˈiːv(ə)nnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being level, balanced, or consistent in degree, amount, or character

Examples

evenness of tone
He admired the evenness of her tone throughout the debate.
Her calm and evenness in stressful situations inspired confidence in the team.
The evenness of the surface made the floor safe for walking.

Related Terms

  • synonym – balance: a state where different elements are equal or in the correct proportions
  • antonym – unevenness: the quality of being not level or not equal
  • synonym – uniformity: the quality of being uniform or the same throughout
