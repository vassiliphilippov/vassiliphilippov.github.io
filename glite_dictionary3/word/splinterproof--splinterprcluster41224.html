splinterproof

adjective
resistant to splintering
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɪntɚˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈsplɪntəˌpruːf / (BrE)
Designed to resist splintering or breaking into sharp fragments.

Examples

splinterproof glass
Cyclists wore splinterproof goggles to protect their eyes from debris on the track.
