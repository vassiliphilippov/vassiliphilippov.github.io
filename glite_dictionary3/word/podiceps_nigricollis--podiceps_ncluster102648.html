Podiceps nigricollis

noun
species of grebe (black-necked grebe)
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊ.dɪˈsɛps nɪˈɡrɪk.əlɪs / (AmE), / ˌpəʊ.dɪˈsɛps nɪˈɡrɪk.ə.lɪs / (BrE)
A species of small grebe with yellow ear tufts and a black neck, found in Eurasia, southern Africa, and western United States; commonly known as the black-necked grebe.
