synesthetic

adjective
involving multiple senses, as in synesthesia
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəˈsθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪnɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or experiencing synesthesia; involving the combination of two or more senses.

Examples

synesthetic experience
Her poetry is rich in synesthetic metaphors that combine sight, sound, and touch.
Some people have a synesthetic perception of music, seeing colors when they hear certain notes.
The artist created a synesthetic experience, blending sounds with vibrant visuals.
