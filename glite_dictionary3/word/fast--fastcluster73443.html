fast

adjective
photographic lens/film: allows short exposures
Pronunciation: / fæst / (AmE), / fɑːst / (BrE)
(of a photographic lens or film) allowing short exposure times by admitting more light or being more sensitive.

Examples

fast lens
I replaced my old lens with a fast one to capture clearer images indoors.
Photographers often choose fast film for action or sports photography.
This is a fast lens, perfect for shooting in low light conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – slow: (of a photographic lens or film) requiring longer exposure due to less light admitted or sensitivity
