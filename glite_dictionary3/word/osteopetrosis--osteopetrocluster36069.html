osteopetrosis

noun
rare inherited bone disorder with increased density
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstioʊpəˈtroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊpəˈtrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A rare inherited disorder characterized by abnormally increased bone density, which can lead to obliteration of the bone marrow cavity and other complications.

Examples

diagnose osteopetrosis early
Researchers are studying genetic mutations responsible for osteopetrosis in hopes of finding a cure.
The diagnosis of osteopetrosis was confirmed by X-ray imaging showing unusually dense bones.
Treatment options for osteopetrosis are limited, often requiring careful management of symptoms.
