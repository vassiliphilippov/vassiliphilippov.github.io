premiership

noun
office or term of a premier
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːmiərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈpriːmɪəʃɪp / (BrE)
the office or term of a premier or prime minister; the period during which someone serves as premier

Examples

his premiership ended

During his premiership, the country underwent significant reform and modernization.
Her premiership lasted for more than a decade, making her one of the longest-serving leaders in the nation's history.
