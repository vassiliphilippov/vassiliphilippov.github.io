to lift

verb
to remove scalp and hair by scalping
Pronunciation: / lɪft / (AmE), / lɪft / (BrE)
to remove someone's scalp, often together with the hair, usually as a form of violence or as practiced by some Native American groups in history

Examples

to lift a scalp
