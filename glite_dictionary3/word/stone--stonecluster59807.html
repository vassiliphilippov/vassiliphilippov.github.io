stone

noun
crystalline mineral used as a gemstone
Pronunciation: / stoʊn / (AmE), / stəʊn / (BrE)

Examples

a diamond stone

She admired the deep blue stone in her engagement ring.
The necklace was adorned with a sparkling stone set in gold.
