to caress

verb
touch gently with affection
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛs / (AmE), / kəˈrɛs / (BrE)
to touch or stroke someone or something gently in a loving or affectionate way

Examples

to caress a cheek
A warm breeze caressed his skin as he stood by the open window.
He gently caressed her hair as they watched the sunset together.
She caressed the old photograph, lost in memories of her childhood.
