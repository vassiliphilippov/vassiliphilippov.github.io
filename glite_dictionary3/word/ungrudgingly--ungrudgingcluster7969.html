ungrudgingly

adverb
willingly and without resentment
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
In a generous or willing manner, without reluctance or resentment.

Examples

to help ungrudgingly
He accepted his responsibilities ungrudgingly, never complaining about extra tasks.
She ungrudgingly donated her time to help those in need every weekend.
They ungrudgingly shared their knowledge to ensure the project's success.
