condensate

noun
product formed by condensation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.dənˌseɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.dən.seɪt / (BrE)
a liquid or solid that forms when a gas or vapor cools and changes state, such as water droplets on a cold surface
