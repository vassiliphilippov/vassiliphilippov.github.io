to regift

verb
give a received gift to someone else
Pronunciation: / riˈɡɪft / (AmE), / riːˈɡɪft / (BrE)
to give a present that one has received to someone else instead of keeping it or using it

Examples

to regift an unwanted present
He wondered if it was rude to regift something he didn't like.
Many people regift unwanted holiday presents rather than letting them go to waste.
She decided to regift the candle she received at the office party.
