subconsciously

adverb
without conscious awareness
Pronunciation: / səbˈkɑn.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒn.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is influenced by or originates in the subconscious mind, without conscious awareness.

Examples

subconsciously make choices
He subconsciously tapped his foot to the music without even realizing it.
Many of our decisions are made subconsciously rather than through deliberate thought.
She subconsciously avoided mentioning his name in conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – consciously: with full awareness or intention
