curricular

adjective
relating to a curriculum
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɪkjələr / (AmE), / kəˈrɪkjələ / (BrE)
relating to an academic curriculum or course of study in a school or university.

Examples

curricular activities
The committee focuses on improving curricular standards across all grade levels.
The school decided to revise its curricular offerings to include more technology courses.
There are several curricular requirements that students must fulfill before graduation.
