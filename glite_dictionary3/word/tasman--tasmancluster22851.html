Tasman

proper noun
Dutch explorer who discovered Tasmania and New Zealand
Pronunciation: / ˈtæzmən / (AmE), / ˈtaz.mən / (BrE)
Abel Tasman (1603–1659), a Dutch explorer and navigator, known as the first European to reach Tasmania and New Zealand.

Examples

Tasman explored Australia
Abel Tasman mapped parts of Australia and New Zealand during his voyages in the 17th century.
Historians credit Tasman with being the first European to reach Tasmania.
The Tasman Sea is named after the Dutch navigator Abel Tasman.
