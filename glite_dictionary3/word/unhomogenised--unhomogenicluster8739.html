unhomogenised

adjective
not homogenized (esp. milk)
Pronunciation: / ʌn.hoʊˈmɑdʒəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌn.hɒˈmɒdʒ.ɪ.naɪzd / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially milk) not having been homogenized; not subjected to a process to make it uniform throughout.

Examples

unhomogenised milk bottle
Some people prefer unhomogenised milk because of its natural taste and cream layer.
The dairy offered both homogenised and unhomogenised varieties for customers to choose from.
You can see the cream rising to the top in a bottle of unhomogenised milk.

Related Terms

  • antonym – homogenised: having undergone homogenization; made uniform throughout
