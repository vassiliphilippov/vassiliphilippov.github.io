homoeopathic

adjective
relating to homeopathy
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmiəˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmiəˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
relating to or based on the practice of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine in which diseases are treated with highly diluted substances that produce symptoms similar to the disease in healthy people.

Examples

homoeopathic medicine
She works at a clinic that specializes in homoeopathic treatments.
Some patients prefer homoeopathic remedies over conventional drugs.
There is ongoing debate about the effectiveness of homoeopathic medicine.

Related Terms

  • antonym – allopathic: relating to conventional (mainstream) medical practice; often contrasted with homeopathic
