leadership

noun
the group of leaders of a group or organization
Pronunciation: / ˈliːdərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈliːdəʃɪp / (BrE)
the group of people who direct or manage an organization, country, or other collective entity

Examples

company leadership
After the election, a new leadership took charge of the organization.
Many employees disagree with the decisions of the current leadership.
The leadership has called for a new strategy in response to the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – administration: the group responsible for running an organization or government
  • synonym – management: the people who manage an organization or business
  • antonym – rank and file: the ordinary members of an organization as opposed to its leaders
