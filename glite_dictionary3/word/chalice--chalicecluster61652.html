chalice

noun
ceremonial cup, especially for Eucharist
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃælɪs / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪs / (BrE)
a large, often ornate cup with a stem and base, used for drinking wine, especially during the Christian Eucharist or other religious ceremonies
