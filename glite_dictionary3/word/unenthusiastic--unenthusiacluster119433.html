unenthusiastic

adjective
not excited or interested
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˌθuziˈæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪk / (BrE)
not showing excitement, interest, or eagerness for something

Examples

unenthusiastic response
He gave an unenthusiastic response to the invitation, making it clear he wasn't interested.
Her unenthusiastic applause barely rose above a polite patter.
The students appeared unenthusiastic about the new changes to the timetable.

  • antonym – enthusiastic: showing intense and eager enjoyment or interest
