booby

noun
tropical seabird with bright bill or feet
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːbi / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi / (BrE)
a large tropical seabird of the gannet family, noted for its brightly colored bill or feet, found chiefly in warm ocean regions

Examples

booby bird
The booby dived gracefully into the sea to catch fish for its chicks.
The blue-footed booby is famous for its strikingly colored feet and humorous courtship dance.
We spotted a flock of boobies nesting on the rocky island during our boat tour.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gannet: a large seabird related to the booby, with predominantly white plumage
