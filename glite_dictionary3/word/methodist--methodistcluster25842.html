Methodist

adjective
relating to the Methodist church or its beliefs
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛθədɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛθədɪst / (BrE)
of or relating to the branch of Protestant Christianity that follows the teachings of John and Charles Wesley.

Examples

Methodist church service
She attended a renowned Methodist seminary to complete her theological studies.
The charitable spirit is a key aspect of Methodist belief.
The church has a strong Methodist tradition that shapes its community services.
