mucin

noun
substance in mucus protecting body surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːsɪn / (AmE), / ˈmjuːsɪn / (BrE)
a nitrogenous substance found in mucous secretions that serves as a lubricant protecting body surfaces
