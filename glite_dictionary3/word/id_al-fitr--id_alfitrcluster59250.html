Id al-Fitr

noun
festival marking the end of Ramadan
Pronunciation: / ˌiːd æl ˈfɪtər / (AmE), / ˌiːd æl ˈfɪtə / (BrE)
the Muslim festival marking the end of Ramadan, celebrated with communal prayers and feasting

Examples

Id al-Fitr celebrations
Muslims around the world gather to celebrate Id al-Fitr after a month of fasting.

The streets were filled with joy and laughter during Id al-Fitr celebrations.
