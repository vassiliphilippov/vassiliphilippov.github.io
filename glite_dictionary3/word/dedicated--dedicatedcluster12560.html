dedicated

adjective
set apart for a sacred or special purpose
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdəˌkeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪd / (BrE)
solemnly devoted or set apart for a particular purpose, especially a sacred or commemorative one

Examples

dedicated space for worship
A special room is dedicated as a place for quiet reflection and prayer.
Many churches are dedicated to specific saints or events in religious history.
The newly built memorial was dedicated to honor the fallen soldiers.
