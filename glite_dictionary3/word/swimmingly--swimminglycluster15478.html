swimmingly

adverb
smoothly; without difficulties
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈswɪmɪŋli / (BrE)
In a smooth and successful way, without any problems or difficulties.

Examples

everything goes swimmingly
Despite technical challenges, the entire operation went swimmingly.
Everything was progressing swimmingly until an unexpected problem arose.
The launch of the product proceeded swimmingly, to everyone's relief.
