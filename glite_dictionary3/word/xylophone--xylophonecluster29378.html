xylophone

noun
musical instrument with wooden bars struck by mallets
Pronunciation: / ˈzaɪ.ləˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈzaɪ.lə.fəʊn / (BrE)
a musical instrument consisting of a series of wooden bars of graduated length, played by striking with small mallets to produce notes of a chromatic scale

Examples

play the xylophone
A set of colorful xylophone bars was a popular toy in the nursery.
She learned to play the xylophone in her elementary school band.
The musician performed an intricate melody on the xylophone during the concert.
