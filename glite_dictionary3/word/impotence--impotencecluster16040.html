impotence

noun
weakness; lack of power
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpətəns / (AmE), / ˈɪmpətəns / (BrE)
The state of lacking power or strength; weakness or feebleness.

Examples

political impotence
She was angered by the sense of impotence that came with being ignored.
The impotence of the opposition became clear after the vote.
The government’s impotence in dealing with corruption frustrated the public.

Related Terms

  • synonym – powerlessness: lack of ability or means to act
  • antonym – strength: the quality of being strong
