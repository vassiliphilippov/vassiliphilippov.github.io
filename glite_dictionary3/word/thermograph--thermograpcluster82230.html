thermograph

noun
infrared device for mapping body temperature
Pronunciation: / ˈθɝːməˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈθɜːməˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a medical instrument that uses infrared imaging to detect and record temperature variations on the surface of the body

Examples

medical thermograph scan
A thermograph can help detect abnormal heat patterns that may indicate underlying health issues.
Advancements in thermograph technology have improved non-invasive diagnostic methods in medicine.
The doctor used a thermograph to assess temperature differences in the patient's limbs.
