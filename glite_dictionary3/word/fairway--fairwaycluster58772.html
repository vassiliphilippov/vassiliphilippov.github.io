fairway

noun
golf course area between tee and green
Pronunciation: / ˈferˌweɪ /; / ˈfɛrˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈfeəˌweɪ /; / ˈfeə.weɪ / (BrE)
the part of a golf course between the tee and the green where the grass is cut short and is easy to play from
