Anastomus

noun
genus of storks called openbills
Pronunciation: / əˈnæs.tə.məs /; / əˈnæstəˌmoʊs / (AmE), / əˈnastəməs /; / əˈnæstəˌməs / (BrE)
a genus of large wading birds called openbills, found in Asia and Africa, with distinctive bills that do not close completely
