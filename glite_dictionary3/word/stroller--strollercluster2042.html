stroller

noun
pushchair for babies or young children
Pronunciation: / ˈstroʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˈstrəʊlə / (BrE)
a small, folding vehicle with four wheels in which a baby or young child can be pushed around while seated

Examples

baby stroller
Parents pushed their children in strollers along the busy sidewalk.
She put her baby in the stroller and went for a walk in the park.
The stroller folds easily and fits in the trunk of the car.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pram: a baby carriage, especially in British English
  • synonym – pushchair: a small chair on wheels for pushing a baby or young child
