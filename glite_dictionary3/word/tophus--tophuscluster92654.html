tophus

noun
uric acid deposit in gout
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.fəs / (AmE), / ˈtɒf.əs / (BrE)
a deposit of uric acid crystals, especially around a joint or in the ear, typically occurring in advanced gout

Examples
