intoned

adjective
spoken in a chant-like monotone
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtoʊnd / (AmE), / ɪnˈtəʊnd / (BrE)
spoken or sung with a monotonous or chant-like cadence or rhythm, as in chanting.

Examples

intoned reply
His intoned response made it difficult to tell how he really felt.
She spoke the poem in an intoned manner, making it sound like a ritual chant.
The intoned prayers echoed through the silent hall, calming everyone present.
