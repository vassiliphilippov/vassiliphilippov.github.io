impenetrable

adjective
impossible to understand
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbl / (BrE)
extremely difficult or impossible to understand; incomprehensible.

Examples

impenetrable explanation
For outsiders, the technical discussion remained largely impenetrable.
Her writing style is so dense and impenetrable that few people finish her books.
The report was filled with impenetrable legal language that confused most readers.
