florescence

noun
period of blossoming or flowering
Pronunciation: / flɔˈrɛsəns / (AmE), / flɔːˈrɛsəns / (BrE)
the process or period of blossoming; the time during which flowers bloom or a plant produces blossoms

Examples

florescence of spring flowers
Botanists studied the florescence of rare alpine plants in the region.
During spring, the garden was a spectacle of colors in full florescence.
The cherry trees reached their peak florescence in early April, attracting crowds to the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bloom: the period or process when a plant produces flowers
  • antonym – wither: to fade, shrivel, or lose freshness (of flowers/plants)
