hindbrain

noun
posterior part of the brain
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪndˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪndˌbreɪn / (BrE)
the posterior part of the brain, comprising the cerebellum, pons, and medulla oblongata

Examples

the hindbrain controls movement
Cerebellum and brainstem are both major components of the hindbrain.
Damage to the hindbrain can affect balance and coordination.
The hindbrain develops early in embryonic growth.
