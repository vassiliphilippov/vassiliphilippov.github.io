stew

noun
dish of meat or fish cooked with vegetables
Pronunciation: / stuː / (AmE), / stjuː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of meat or fish and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid.

Examples

beef stew
On cold days, nothing is better than a hot stew.
She served a delicious beef stew for dinner.
The aroma of the bubbling stew filled the kitchen.
