alphabet

noun
set of letters used in writing a language
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.fəˌbɛt / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bɛt / (BrE)
A set of letters or symbols in a fixed order, used to represent the basic sounds of a language in writing.

Examples

the alphabet song
Children often learn the alphabet before they start reading words.
The Cyrillic alphabet is used in Russian and several other languages.
The English alphabet has twenty-six letters.
