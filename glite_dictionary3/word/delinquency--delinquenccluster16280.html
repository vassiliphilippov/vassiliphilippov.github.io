delinquency

noun
neglectful or careless behavior
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi / (BrE)
a tendency to neglect duties or responsibilities; negligent or careless behavior

Examples

delinquency at work
A pattern of delinquency among the staff led to a decline in productivity.
Repeated delinquency at work resulted in a formal warning from management.
The student's delinquency in completing assignments concerned his parents and teachers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – carelessness: lack of attention or concern
  • antonym – diligence: careful and persistent effort or work
  • synonym – negligence: failure to take proper care in doing something
