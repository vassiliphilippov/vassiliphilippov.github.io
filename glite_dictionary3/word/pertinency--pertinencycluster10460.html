pertinency

noun
relevance or applicability
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrtənənsi / (AmE), / ˈpɜːtɪnənsi / (BrE)
the quality or state of being relevant or applicable to a particular matter

Examples

pertinency to the topic
Her comments lacked pertinency to the discussion at hand.
The lawyer questioned the pertinency of the witness's remarks.
We must assess the pertinency of each piece of evidence presented.

Related Terms

  • antonym – irrelevance: lack of relevance or applicability
  • synonym – pertinence: the quality of being relevant to a particular matter
