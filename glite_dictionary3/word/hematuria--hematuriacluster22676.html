hematuria

noun
blood in the urine (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈtʊriə / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtjʊəriə / (BrE)
the presence of blood in the urine, often indicating disease or injury of the urinary tract

Examples
