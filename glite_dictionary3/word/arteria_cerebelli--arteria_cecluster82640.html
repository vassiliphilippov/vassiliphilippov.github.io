arteria cerebelli

noun
artery supplying the cerebellum
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪriə ˌsɛrəˈbɛlaɪ / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə ˌsɛrəˈbɛlaɪ / (BrE)
an artery that supplies blood to the cerebellum in the brain

Examples
