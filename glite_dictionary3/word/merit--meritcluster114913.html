to merit

verb
to be worthy or deserve something
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛrɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛrɪt / (BrE)
to be worthy of or deserve something, especially praise or reward

Examples

to merit praise
His actions merit careful consideration before any conclusion is drawn.
Such outstanding service merits recognition from the entire community.
Your proposal merits further discussion by the board.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deserve: to be worthy of something, especially a reward or punishment
