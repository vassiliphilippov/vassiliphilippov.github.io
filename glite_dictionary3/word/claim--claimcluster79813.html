claim

noun
assertion of something as true
Pronunciation: / kleɪm / (AmE), / kleɪm / (BrE)
a statement that something is true, typically without providing proof or evidence

Examples

claim of truth
Many people challenge his claim that the story is based on true events.
She made a claim about seeing a rare bird in her backyard.
The company issued a claim that their product was the safest on the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accusation: a charge or claim that someone has done something illegal or wrong
  • synonym – assertion: a confident and forceful statement of fact or belief
  • antonym – denial: a statement that something is not true
