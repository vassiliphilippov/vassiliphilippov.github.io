tailspin

noun
emotional collapse or confusion
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlˌspɪn / (AmE), / ˈteɪlˌspɪn / (BrE)
A state of rapidly deteriorating emotional control, often resulting in confusion or collapse.

Examples

in a tailspin emotionally
After losing his job, Tom went into a tailspin and struggled to cope for months.
The news sent her emotions into a tailspin, leaving her unable to focus at work.
Their sudden breakup threw him into a deep tailspin of anxiety and uncertainty.

Related Terms

  • synonym – breakdown: a sudden collapse in mental health or emotional functioning
  • synonym – panic: a sudden overwhelming fear, often producing irrational behavior
  • antonym – stability: the state of being steady and not easily disturbed emotionally
