coincidental

adjective
happening by chance at the same time
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.ɪnˈsɪd.ən.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.ɪnˈsɪd.ən.tl̩ / (BrE)
happening by chance at the same time as something else, without being planned or connected

Examples

coincidental timing
It was coincidental that both companies launched their products on the same day.
Our meeting at the airport was completely coincidental and took us both by surprise.
The timing of their arrivals was purely coincidental and not planned.

Related Terms

  • antonym – intentional: done on purpose, not by chance
  • synonym – simultaneous: occurring at the same time
