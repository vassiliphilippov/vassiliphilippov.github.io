semiotics

noun
study of signs and symbols
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈɑtɪks / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈɒtɪks / (BrE)
the study of signs and symbols and their use or interpretation, especially as a system of communication

Examples

study of semiotics
His research in semiotics explores the meaning behind visual symbols in art.
She studied semiotics to better understand how advertising communicates with consumers.
The course covers key concepts in semiotics and their applications in media analysis.
