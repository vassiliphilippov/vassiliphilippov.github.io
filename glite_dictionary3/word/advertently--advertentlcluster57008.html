advertently

adverb
deliberately; intentionally
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɜrtəntli / (AmE), / ədˈvɜːtəntli / (BrE)
in a careful and deliberate manner; intentionally

Examples

to act advertently
She advertently ignored the distractions to focus on her work.
The information was advertently released to clarify the misunderstanding.
The manager advertently addressed each concern raised during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inadvertently: in an unintentional or careless manner
