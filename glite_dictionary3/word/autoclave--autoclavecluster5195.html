to autoclave

verb
to sterilize using an autoclave
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtoʊˌkleɪv / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊkleɪv / (BrE)
to subject something to the action of an autoclave, especially for sterilization by steam under pressure

Examples

to autoclave instruments
After cleaning the glassware, she autoclaved it to ensure it was sterile.
Laboratory technicians routinely autoclave surgical instruments to eliminate all microorganisms.
The hospital staff were trained to autoclave waste materials before disposal.
