sticktight

noun
parasitic insect, esp. affecting chickens
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkˌtaɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɪk.tʌɪt / (BrE)
A small parasitic insect, especially a type of flea, that attaches itself firmly to the skin of animals or birds, particularly chickens.

Examples

sticktight fleas on chickens
A heavy burden of sticktights can weaken young chicks and cause health problems.
The farmer checked his chickens for sticktights after noticing signs of irritation.
Veterinarians recommend treating poultry houses to prevent sticktight infestations.

  • synonym – flea: a small wingless jumping insect that feeds on blood
