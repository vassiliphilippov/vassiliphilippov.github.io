jambeau

noun
armor for the lower leg
Pronunciation: / ʒæmˈboʊ / (AmE), / ʒamˈbəʊ / (BrE)
A piece of armor made to protect the lower leg below the knee.

Examples

jambeau on a knight
During the reenactment, she carefully strapped the jambeaux over her boots.
The knight wore a jambeau on each leg to shield himself from sword blows.
Without a proper jambeau, a warrior’s legs were vulnerable in battle.
