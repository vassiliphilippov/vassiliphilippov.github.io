telegram

noun
message transmitted by telegraph
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪɡræm / (BrE)
a message sent by telegraph and delivered in written or printed form, especially prominent before electronic communication became widespread

Examples

send a telegram
In the early 20th century, urgent news was often delivered by telegram.
She received a telegram announcing the birth of her niece.
The soldier sent a telegram home from overseas to reassure his family.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cablegram: a message sent by submarine cable, similar to a telegram
  • antonym – letter: a written, typically mailed, communication sent between people
