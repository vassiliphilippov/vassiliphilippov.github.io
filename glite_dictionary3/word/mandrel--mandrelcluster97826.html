mandrel

noun
rotating shaft for supporting parts
Pronunciation: / ˈmændrəl / (AmE), / ˈmændrəl / (BrE)
a shaft or spindle, often rotating, used to support or serve as an axis for larger rotating parts in machines or tools

Examples

mandrel holds cylinder
The engineer explained how the mandrel supports the rotating cylinder.
The rotating disc is mounted on a mandrel during machining.
When replacing the wheel, be sure the mandrel is clean and smooth.
