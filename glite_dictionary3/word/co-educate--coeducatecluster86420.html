to co-educate

verb
educate both sexes together
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɛdʒəˌkeɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈɛdjʊkeɪt / (BrE)
to educate boys and girls or men and women together in the same classes or institution

Examples

to co-educate students
Many schools now co-educate boys and girls in all subjects.
Some private academies still do not co-educate their pupils.
The decision to co-educate students was controversial at first but proved successful.
