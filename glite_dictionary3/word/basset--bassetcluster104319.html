basset

noun
a short-legged breed of hound dog
Pronunciation: / ˈbæsɪt / (AmE), / ˈbæsɪt / (BrE)
a breed of short-legged, smooth-haired hound with long ears, originally bred for hunting.

basset hound dog
At the dog show, the basset drew attention with its long, droopy ears and soulful eyes.
