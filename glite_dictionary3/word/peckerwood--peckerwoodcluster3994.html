peckerwood

noun
another term for woodpecker (bird)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛkərˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɛkəˌwʊd / (BrE)
A bird with strong claws and a stiff tail adapted for climbing, and a hard, chisel-like bill for boring into wood for insects; another term for a woodpecker.

Examples

peckerwood pecking tree
A peckerwood drilled into the old oak tree looking for insects.
The forest was filled with the sounds of peckerwoods tapping on the trunks.
We spotted a colorful peckerwood while hiking in the woods.
