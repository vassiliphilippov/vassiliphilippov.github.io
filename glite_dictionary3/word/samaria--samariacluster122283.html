Samaria

proper noun
ancient city, former capital of northern Israel
Pronunciation: / səˈmɛriə / (AmE), / səˈmeəriə / (BrE)
an ancient city in central Palestine, established in the 9th century BC as the capital of the northern kingdom of Israel; its site is now in northwestern Jordan
