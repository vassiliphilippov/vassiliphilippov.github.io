trainbandsman

noun
member of an early English militia
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪnbændzmən / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnbændzmən / (BrE)
a male member of a trainband, a local militia unit, especially in England during the 16th to 18th centuries.

Examples

English trainbandsman uniform
He dressed in the uniform of a proud trainbandsman for the parade.
Many a trainbandsman was called upon to defend the town during the uprising.
The diary mentions a trainbandsman drilling with his fellows in the village square.
