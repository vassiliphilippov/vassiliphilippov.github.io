to alphabetize

verb
to provide with an alphabet
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.fə.bəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈæl.fə.bə.taɪz / (BrE)
To provide (a language or group) with an alphabet; to create or introduce a writing system based on an alphabet.

Examples

to alphabetize a language
After years of study, she helped alphabetize the local language by developing a suitable script.
Linguists worked to alphabetize the indigenous language, making it easier to read and write.
The missionaries sought to alphabetize the spoken dialects they encountered.
