unequivocalness

noun
the quality of being unambiguous or clear
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkəlnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clear and not open to more than one interpretation; unambiguity

Examples

unequivocalness of answer

The unequivocalness of the contract terms protected both parties from misunderstanding.
