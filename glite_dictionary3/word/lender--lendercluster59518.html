lender

noun
person or organization lending money
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛndər / (AmE), / ˈlɛndə / (BrE)
a person or organization that lends money, especially as a business

Examples

lender bank
Banks act as lenders when they provide mortgages to homebuyers.
If you miss a payment, the lender may charge additional interest.
The lender approved her home loan application after reviewing her credit score.

Related Terms

  • antonym – borrower: a person or organization that receives money from a lender with the intention to repay
  • synonym – creditor: a person or organization to whom money is owed; often used synonymously with 'lender'
