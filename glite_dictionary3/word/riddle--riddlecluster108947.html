to riddle

verb
separate using a sieve
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdəl / (AmE), / ˈrɪd.əl / (BrE)
to separate or sift (grain from chaff, etc.) using a riddle (a type of sieve)

Examples

to riddle grain
After threshing, the workers would riddle the seed through a large sieve.
Farmers used to riddle the harvested grain to remove the chaff before storage.
She carefully riddled the flour to make it finer for baking.
