redshank

noun
a wading bird with red legs
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌʃæŋk / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌʃæŋk / (BrE)
a common wading bird of the sandpiper family, found in Eurasia, with long red legs and a straight bill

Examples

a redshank wading
