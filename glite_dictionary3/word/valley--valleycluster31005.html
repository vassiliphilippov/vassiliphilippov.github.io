valley

noun
low area between hills or mountains
Pronunciation: / ˈvæli / (AmE), / ˈvæli / (BrE)
A low area of land between hills or mountains, often with a river running through it.

Examples

mountain valley
Many farms are located in the fertile valley where the soil is rich.
The valley was surrounded by high, snow-capped mountains on all sides.
They hiked down into the lush valley to explore the river below.
