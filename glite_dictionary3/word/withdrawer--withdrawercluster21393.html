withdrawer

noun
someone who withdraws permission or approval
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈdrɔɚ / (AmE), / wɪðˈdrɔːə / (BrE)
a person or authority who withdraws, especially one who formally takes back permission or approval

Examples

withdrawer of approval
As the withdrawer of approval, the committee must provide justifications in written form.
In the contract, the withdrawer is responsible for notifying all parties of any cancellations.
The licensing board, as the withdrawer of his permit, explained the reasons for their decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym – withdraw: to take back or remove something previously granted
