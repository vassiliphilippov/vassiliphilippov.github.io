potted

adjective
grown in a pot (of plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɒtɪd / (BrE)
(of a plant) planted or grown in a pot rather than in the ground.

Examples

potted plant
A row of potted herbs lined the kitchen counter, ready to be used in cooking.
She placed the potted fern on the windowsill to catch the morning sun.
They bought several potted flowers to brighten up the apartment balcony.
