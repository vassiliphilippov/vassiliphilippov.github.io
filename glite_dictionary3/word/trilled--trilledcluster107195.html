trilled

adjective
produced with a rapid vibration; with a trill
Pronunciation: / trɪld / (AmE), / trɪld / (BrE)
(of a speech sound) articulated with a rapid vibration of the tongue or vocal cords, as in the Spanish 'r'.

Examples

trilled r sound
Certain languages use a trilled sound where English would have a simple 'r'.
He struggled to make the trilled consonant in Spanish class.
The Italian singer pronounced every trilled 'r' perfectly in her performance.
