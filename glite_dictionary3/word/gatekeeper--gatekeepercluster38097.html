gatekeeper

noun
person who guards an entrance
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to guard or control access to a gate or entrance

Examples

gatekeeper at the entrance
A uniformed gatekeeper stood at the main entrance, ready to answer visitors' questions.
As the gatekeeper of the estate, he was responsible for monitoring all who entered and left.
The gatekeeper checked everyone's identification before allowing them onto the premises.

  • synonym – guard: a person who protects or watches over something
