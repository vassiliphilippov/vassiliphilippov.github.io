return

noun
income or profit from investment
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
the profit or income generated from an investment, property, or business transaction

Examples

return on investment
Investors are seeking higher returns from overseas markets.
She calculated the return on her property investment before selling it.
The average return on government bonds has fallen in recent years.

Related Terms

  • antonym – loss: an instance of losing, especially money or value; opposite of profit or return
  • synonym – yield: the full amount of an agricultural or industrial product; also used for investment income
