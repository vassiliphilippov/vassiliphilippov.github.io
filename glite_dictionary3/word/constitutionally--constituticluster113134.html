constitutionally

adverb
according to the constitution (law)
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.stɪˈtuː.ʃən.əl.i / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.stɪˈtjuː.ʃən.əl.i / (BrE)
In a way that is allowed or required by a constitution, especially a political constitution.

Examples

constitutionally allowed change
Such a change is not constitutionally possible without a national referendum.
The board acted constitutionally in making its decision.
The court decided the law was not constitutionally valid.
