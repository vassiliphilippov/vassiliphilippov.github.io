to encounter

verb
compete against someone
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkaʊntɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkaʊntə / (BrE)
to meet and compete against an opponent in a sport, game, or battle

Examples

to encounter a rival
She hopes to encounter her rival in the championship round.
The two teams will encounter each other in the final match.
They have encountered some of the best players in the league this season.
