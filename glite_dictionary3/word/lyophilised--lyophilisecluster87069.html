lyophilised

adjective
dried by freeze-drying
Pronunciation: / laɪˈɑːfəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / laɪˈɒfɪlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of tissue, blood, serum, or other biological substances) dried by freezing and removing moisture in a high vacuum.

Examples

lyophilised blood sample
Scientists use lyophilised blood samples for analysis in many experiments.
The lab technician carefully rehydrated the lyophilised tissue for further testing.
The vaccine must be kept lyophilised until it is ready for use.

Related Terms
