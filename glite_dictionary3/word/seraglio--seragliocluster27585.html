seraglio

noun
living quarters for women in a Muslim household (historical)
Pronunciation: / səˈrɑːljoʊ / (AmE), / sɪˈrɑːlɪəʊ / (BrE)
the part of a Muslim household, especially in former times, reserved for wives, concubines, and female relatives; a harem.

Examples

the seraglio of a palace
Intrigue and luxury were common stories about life inside the seraglio.
The sultan's wives lived in the seraglio for much of their lives.
Tourists visited the restored seraglio in the old palace complex.

Related Terms

  • synonym – harem: the separate part of a Muslim household reserved for wives and female servants
