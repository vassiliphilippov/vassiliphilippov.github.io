recital

noun
public performance of memorized material
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsaɪ.t̬əl / (AmE), / rɪˈsaɪ.təl / (BrE)
a public performance in which someone recites or repeats something memorized, such as a poem or speech

Examples

recital of a poem
Her flawless recital of the speech impressed both judges and audience.
The school held a poetry recital where students performed their favorite works.
We attended a dramatic recital at the local community theater last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – performance: an act of performing a play, concert, or other form of entertainment
  • synonym – reading: an event at which something is read aloud to an audience
