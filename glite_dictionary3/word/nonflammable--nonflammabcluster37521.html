nonflammable

adjective
not able to catch fire or burn
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈflæməbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈflaməbəl / (BrE)
Not able to catch fire or burn easily; impossible to ignite.

Examples

nonflammable material
Most laboratory benches are made of nonflammable materials for safety reasons.
Store nonflammable liquids separately from flammable chemicals to prevent accidents.
The new insulation is completely nonflammable and meets all fire safety standards.

Related Terms

  • antonym – flammable: able to catch fire easily
  • antonym – inflammable: easily set on fire, same as flammable
