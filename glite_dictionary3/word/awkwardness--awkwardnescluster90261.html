awkwardness

noun
embarrassing or uncomfortable situation
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkwərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːkwədnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being embarrassing, uncomfortable, or lacking in social grace.

Examples

social awkwardness

The silence grew, amplifying the awkwardness between the two friends.
There was a noticeable awkwardness in the room after he made his comment.
