oversized

adjective
larger than normal or expected
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌsaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌsaɪzd / (BrE)
larger than the usual or expected size; too large for its purpose or type.

Examples

an oversized sweater
She wore an oversized sweater that hung loosely on her frame.
The artist created an oversized sculpture for the public square.
The company delivered an oversized package that couldn't fit through the door.

Related Terms

  • antonym – undersized: smaller than the usual or expected size
