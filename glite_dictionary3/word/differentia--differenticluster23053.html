differentia

noun
distinguishing characteristic, esp. in taxonomy
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪfəˈrɛn(t)ʃiə / (AmE), / ˌdɪfəˈrɛn(t)ʃɪə / (BrE)
a distinguishing characteristic, especially one that separates one species from others within the same genus

Examples

differentia of a species
Biologists use differentia to classify organisms more precisely.
