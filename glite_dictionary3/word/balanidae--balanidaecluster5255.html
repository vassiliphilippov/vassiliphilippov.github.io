Balanidae

noun
family of stalkless barnacles
Pronunciation: / bəˈlænɪˌdiː / (AmE), / bəˈlanɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of stalkless barnacles found in marine environments, characterized by their conical shells and attachment to hard surfaces.
