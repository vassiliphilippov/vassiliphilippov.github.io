faded

adjective
intoxicated; under the influence (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈfeɪdɪd / (BrE)
(slang, informal) intoxicated or under the influence of drugs or alcohol; noticeably affected by substances.

Examples

faded after drinking
After three shots and a few beers he looked completely faded and stumbled to the sofa.
Her friends helped the faded guest into a taxi after the house party ended late.
The concert photos showed the lead singer clearly faded during the second half of the set.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drunk: intoxicated by alcohol; often used synonymously with 'faded' when alcohol is involved
  • synonym – high: under the influence of drugs; commonly used as a slang synonym of 'faded' in some contexts
  • antonym – sober: not under the influence of alcohol or drugs; opposite of 'faded' in this slang sense
