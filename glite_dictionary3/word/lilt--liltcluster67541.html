to lilt

verb
speak or sing with a rhythmic swing
Pronunciation: / lɪlt / (AmE), / lɪlt / (BrE)
to speak, sing, or play in a light, rhythmic, and graceful manner; to articulate with a lively or musical rhythm

Examples

to lilt a song
He would lilt every sentence, making even ordinary words sound musical.
Her voice began to lilt as she recited the poem to the children.
The singer lilted the melody, giving it a playful, uplifting quality.
