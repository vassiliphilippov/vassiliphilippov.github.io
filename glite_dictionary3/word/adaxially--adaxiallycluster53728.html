adaxially

adverb
facing toward the axis (botany, zoology)
Pronunciation: / əˈdæksiəli / (AmE), / əˈdæksiəli / (BrE)
In a manner facing toward the axis or central line of a structure, especially in botany and zoology.

Examples

adaxially facing the stem
In some species, the flowers open adaxially toward the stem.
The epidermal cells are arranged adaxially to maximize sunlight absorption.
The leaves are hairy only adaxially, giving them a distinctive texture.
