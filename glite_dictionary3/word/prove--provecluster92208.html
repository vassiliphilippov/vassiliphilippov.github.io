to prove

verb
take a trial impression (printing)
Pronunciation: / pruv / (AmE), / pruːv / (BrE)
(printing) to take a trial impression of a block, plate, or type before the final printing.

Examples

to prove a plate
It is common practice to prove the type to check for any defects.
The printer decided to prove the plate before running the full batch.
They proved a block to ensure the design appeared as expected.
