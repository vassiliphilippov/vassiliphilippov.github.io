wallpaper

noun
digital background image
Pronunciation: / \u02c8w\u0254l\u02ccpe\u026ap\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8w\u0254\u02d0l\u02ccpe\u026ap\u0259 / (BrE)

Examples

phone wallpaper image
She set a serene beach photo as her wallpaper on her phone.
