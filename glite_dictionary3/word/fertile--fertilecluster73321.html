fertile

adjective
highly productive or creative (mind or imagination)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrt̬l / (AmE), / ˈfɜːtaɪl / (BrE)
(of a person's mind or imagination) highly productive or creative; capable of generating many new ideas.

Examples

fertile imagination
During the meeting, his fertile mind generated dozens of innovative solutions.
The author's fertile imagination led to the creation of an entire fictional world.
The period was a fertile time for artistic and literary experimentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fecund: intellectually productive or creative
  • synonym – prolific: producing many works or results; highly productive (person or mind)
