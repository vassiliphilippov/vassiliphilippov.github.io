to develop

verb
to cultivate skills through training or teaching
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləp / (BrE)
to help a person or group grow, improve, or acquire skills or abilities through teaching, training, or guidance

Examples

to develop young talent
Mentorship programs can develop future leaders within the company.
Teachers work hard to develop students' critical thinking skills.
The school aims to develop talented musicians through an intensive program.

  • synonym – educate: to give intellectual, moral, or social instruction to someone
  • antonym – neglect: to fail to care for or develop someone or something
