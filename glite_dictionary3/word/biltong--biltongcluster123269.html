biltong

noun
dried, cured meat (southern African snack)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪl.tɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.tɒŋ / (BrE)
lean dried meat that has been salted and spiced, typically cut into strips, and air-dried, especially popular in southern Africa.
