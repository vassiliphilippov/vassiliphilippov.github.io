to steam

verb
clean using steam
Pronunciation: / stiːm / (AmE), / stiːm / (BrE)
to clean something using steam, especially by applying hot steam to remove dirt or stains

Examples

to steam a carpet
She steamed the upholstered sofa to remove allergens and dust.
The technician steamed the curtains to refresh them before hanging.
We need to steam the carpets to get rid of all the stains.
