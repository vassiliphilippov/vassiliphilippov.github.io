NEbE

noun
compass point; one point east of northeast
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.iː.biːˈiː / (AmE), / ˌɛn.iː.biːˈiː / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point east of northeast; precisely halfway between northeast and east-northeast, at 56.25° on the compass.
