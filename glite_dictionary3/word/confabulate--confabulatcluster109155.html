to confabulate

verb
to chat informally
Pronunciation: / kənˈfæbjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / kənˈfabjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to engage in informal conversation or chat, especially about unimportant matters

Examples

to confabulate with friends

She enjoys confabulating with friends on a leisurely afternoon.
