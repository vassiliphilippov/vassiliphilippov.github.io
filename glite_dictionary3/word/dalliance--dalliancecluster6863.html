dalliance

noun
playful or idle delay
Pronunciation: / ˈdæliəns / (AmE), / ˈdalɪəns / (BrE)
a period or act of spending time in a playful or idle way instead of working; frivolous delay

Examples

dalliance with social media
Her dalliance with social media cost her several hours each day when she should have been studying.
Instead of getting to work, he indulged in a brief dalliance by the window, watching the rain.
The manager warned the staff against dalliance during office hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym – idleness: the state of being inactive or avoiding work
  • synonym – procrastination: the act of delaying or postponing something
