vaulting

adjective
excessive or overreaching (esp. ambition)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
(especially of ambition or pride) excessive or overreaching; showing excessive self-confidence or striving to reach very high goals.

vaulting ambition

Her vaulting pride made her unpopular with her colleagues.
The leader's vaulting ambition ultimately led to his downfall.
