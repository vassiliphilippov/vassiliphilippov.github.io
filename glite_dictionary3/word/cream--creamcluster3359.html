to cream

verb
beat to make creamy (esp. in cooking)
Pronunciation: / kriːm / (AmE), / kriːm / (BrE)
to make a food creamy by beating it, especially butter or butter with sugar

Examples

to cream butter
First, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
She creamed the cheese before adding it to the mixture.
To make the cake batter, you need to cream the eggs and butter.
