to whiff

verb
strike out in baseball by missing the pitch
Pronunciation: / wɪf / (AmE), / wɪf / (BrE)
(in baseball) to strike out by swinging and missing the pitch charged as the third strike.

Examples

to whiff at baseball
He has a tendency to whiff under pressure, striking out at key moments.
She whiffed three times in the game, missing every third pitch she faced.
The batter whiffed on the final pitch and the game was over.

Related Terms

  • synonym – strike out: (in baseball) to be put out by failing to hit three pitches
