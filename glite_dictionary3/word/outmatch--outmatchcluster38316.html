to outmatch

verb
surpass; do better than
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈmætʃ / (AmE), / aʊtˈmatʃ / (BrE)
to surpass or do better than someone or something; to exceed in performance or ability

Examples

to outmatch a champion
Despite his experience, he could not outmatch the champion in the final round.
If you want to outmatch your rival, you'll need to work even harder.
Their innovative design outmatched all the competitors at the convention.

Related Terms

  • synonym – outdo: to do better than; to exceed in performance
  • synonym – outperform: to perform better than someone or something
  • synonym – surpass: to be better than; to go beyond in achievement or quality
  • antonym – underperform: to perform less well than expected or less well than others
