dishonourably

adverb
in a manner lacking honour
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɑːnərəbli / (AmE), / dɪsˈɒnərəbli / (BrE)
In a way that causes or shows loss of honour or integrity.

Examples

to act dishonourably in business
He behaved dishonourably towards his friends when he betrayed their trust.
She was dishonourably discharged from the military after the incident was investigated.
The public official acted dishonourably in accepting bribes while in office.

Related Terms

  • synonym – disgracefully: in a way that causes shame or disgrace
  • antonym – honourably: in a manner showing honour or integrity
