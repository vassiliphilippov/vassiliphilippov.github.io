flyweight

noun
weight class for lightest fighters in sports
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌweɪt / (BrE)
a weight division in boxing and other combat sports for very light competitors (usually up to about 112 or 115 pounds), or a competitor in this division
