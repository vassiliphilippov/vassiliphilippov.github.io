saltlike

adjective
resembling a chemical salt
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔltˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having characteristics that resemble those of a chemical salt, especially a compound formed by replacing hydrogen in an acid with a metal.

Examples

saltlike crust forms
After evaporation, a saltlike crust formed along the shore of the lake.
Some chemical reactions produce saltlike residues that accumulate in the container.
The mineral had a saltlike appearance, with a crystalline structure.
