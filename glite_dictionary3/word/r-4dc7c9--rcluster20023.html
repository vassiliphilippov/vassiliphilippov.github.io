noun
radius in geometry
Pronunciation: / ɑr / (AmE), / ɑː / (BrE)
(in geometry) the length of a line segment from the center to the circumference of a circle or sphere; the radius.

Examples

r of a circle
If the r of a sphere is 5 cm, its diameter is 10 cm.
The distance r is essential for calculating the circumference.
The formula for the area of a circle is π times r squared.
