foundation

noun
organization supported by an endowment
Pronunciation: / faʊnˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organization that is established to provide money for a particular purpose, often supported by an endowment

Examples

charity foundation
Several scholarships are funded each year by the university's alumni foundation.
She received a grant from a local foundation to start her research project.
The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation supports global health and education initiatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym – charity: an organization set up to provide help and raise money for those in need
  • synonym – trust: an arrangement where funds are managed by an organization for a specific purpose
