Enbrel

noun
trade name for a medication treating autoimmune disorders
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.brəl / (AmE), / ˈɛn.brəl / (BrE)
a trade name for a genetically engineered medication (etanercept) used to treat rheumatoid arthritis and other autoimmune conditions by inhibiting tumor necrosis factor (TNF)
