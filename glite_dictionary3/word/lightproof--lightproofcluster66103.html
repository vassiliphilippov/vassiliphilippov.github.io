lightproof

adjective
impervious to light
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌpruːf / (BrE)
Not allowing light to pass through; impervious to light.

Examples

lightproof container
Laboratory equipment is often housed in lightproof cabinets to maintain strict lighting conditions.
The photographer stored her film in a lightproof bag to protect it from exposure.
You should keep these chemicals in a lightproof container to avoid spoilage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – opaque: not able to be seen through; not transparent
  • antonym – transparent: allowing light to pass through so that objects behind can be seen clearly
