scienter

adverb
knowingly, in law
Pronunciation: / saɪˈɛntər / (AmE), / saɪˈɛntə / (BrE)
(Law) Deliberately or knowingly, especially with knowledge of the wrongfulness of an act.

Examples

with scienter intent
Proof of scienter is required to establish liability under the securities laws.
The court found that the company acted with scienter when it misled investors.
Without evidence of scienter, the defendant could not be convicted of fraud.
