burka

noun
full-body garment worn by Muslim women
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜr.kə / (AmE), / ˈbɜː.kə / (BrE)
A loose, usually full-body garment worn by some Muslim women, typically covering the entire body and face with only a mesh or veiled opening for the eyes.

Examples

wear a burka
Debates about the burka have sparked controversy in many countries.
Many styles of burka feature a mesh screen over the eyes.
She wore a burka during her trip to the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hijab: a headscarf covering the hair, worn by Muslim women
  • synonym – niqab: a veil covering the face, worn by some Muslim women
