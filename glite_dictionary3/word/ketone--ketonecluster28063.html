ketone

noun
organic compound with carbonyl group
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːtoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkiːtəʊn / (BrE)
a compound belonging to a class of organic substances containing a carbonyl group bonded to two hydrocarbon groups

Examples

a ketone compound
Acetone is the simplest ketone and is commonly used as a solvent.
Many fragrances contain ketone compounds to enhance their scent.
The presence of ketones in urine may indicate diabetes or fasting.
