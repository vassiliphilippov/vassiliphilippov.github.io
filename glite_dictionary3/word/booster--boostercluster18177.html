booster

noun
device that amplifies a signal
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːstər / (AmE), / ˈbuːstə / (BrE)
a device that increases or restores the strength of an electrical signal, especially in communications systems

Examples

booster signal device
A booster helps eliminate signal loss over long cable runs in a building.
Engineers recommended using a booster to improve the clarity of the television transmission.
The radio signal was too weak until we installed a booster on the antenna.
