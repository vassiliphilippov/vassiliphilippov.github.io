homespun

noun
coarse, loosely woven fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌspʌn / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌspʌn / (BrE)
a coarse, loosely woven fabric, originally made from yarn spun at home

Examples

coarse homespun cloth
A piece of coarse homespun was draped over the rustic table.
