get

noun
an unlikely return of a difficult shot (in sport)
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt / (AmE), / ɡɛt / (BrE)
a return in a game, such as tennis or squash, of a shot that seemed impossible to reach and would normally score a point for the opponent

Examples

She made a stunning get on match point, keeping herself in the game.
That was the best get I've seen all tournament, saving a sure point.
