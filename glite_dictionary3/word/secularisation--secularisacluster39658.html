secularisation

noun
transfer from church to civil ownership
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkjələraɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsɛkjəlɚɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛkjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something less religious or moving it from religious to non-religious (civil) control or influence
