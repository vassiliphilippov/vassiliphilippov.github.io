clinic

noun
training or instruction session
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪk / (BrE)
a meeting at which people receive advice, training, or remedial instruction in a particular subject or activity

Examples

clinic for writing
She attended a writing clinic to get feedback on her short stories.
The football team held a special clinic to help new players improve their skills.
The music school offers a summer clinic for aspiring jazz musicians.

  • synonym – workshop: a meeting for intensive study or training in a subject
