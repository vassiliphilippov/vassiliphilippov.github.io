to export

verb
send goods abroad for sale
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɔrt / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɔːt / (BrE)
to send goods or services to another country for sale or trade

Examples

to export goods overseas
Last year, the company exported over a million tons of steel to Asia.
Many countries export agricultural products to generate foreign income.
They are planning to export their software to new international markets next year.
