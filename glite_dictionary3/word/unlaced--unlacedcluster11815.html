unlaced

adjective
with laces not tied
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈleɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪst / (BrE)
(of footwear or clothing) with the laces not tied or fastened.

Examples

unlaced boots
He tripped over his unlaced boots while hurrying out the door.
Her unlaced corset hung loosely around her waist.
The children left their unlaced sneakers by the entrance after playing outside.
