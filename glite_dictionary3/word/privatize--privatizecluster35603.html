to privatize

verb
transfer from public to private ownership
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪ.vəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪ.və.tʌɪz / (BrE)
to transfer a business, industry, or service from public (government) ownership or control to private ownership or control

Examples

to privatize a company
Several countries have privatized their water utilities over the last decade.
The debate about whether to privatize healthcare continues among policymakers.
The government decided to privatize the national airline to improve efficiency.
