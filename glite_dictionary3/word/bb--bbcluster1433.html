BB

noun
texting term of endearment
Pronunciation: / ˈbiː ˈbiː / (AmE), / ˈbiː ˈbiː / (BrE)
Informal abbreviation used in messages or speech as a term of endearment for a loved one, short for 'baby' or 'babe'.

Examples

text BB sweetly
She sent a late-night message to BB, asking if he was still awake for a call.
When he greeted his girlfriend he mouthed 'good morning' and texted BB a heart emoji.
Parents sometimes call their infant BB in private messages, an affectionate shorthand between partners.
