dependant

noun
a person financially supported by another
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
a person who relies on another, especially for financial support, such as a child or spouse living with the provider

Examples

family dependant
After losing his job, Mark struggled to provide for his dependants.
Many employees receive tax benefits if they have dependants living at home.
The insurance policy covers the dependants of the main applicant.

  • synonym – dependent: alternative spelling, chiefly US English
