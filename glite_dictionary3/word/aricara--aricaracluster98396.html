Aricara

noun
member of a Native American people (Dakotas)
Pronunciation: / ˌærɪˈkɑːrə / (AmE), / ˌærɪˈkɑːrə / (BrE)
A member of a Native American people, historically living in the Dakotas west of the Missouri River.

Examples
