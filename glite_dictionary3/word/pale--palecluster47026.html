pale

noun
a wooden strip forming part of a fence
Pronunciation: / peɪl / (AmE), / peɪl / (BrE)
a wooden stake or strip used as an upright part of a fence; one of a series making up a paling

Examples

wooden pale fence

He mended the broken pale on the garden fence.
The fence was missing a pale, letting the rabbits through.
