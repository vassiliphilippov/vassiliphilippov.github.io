arrowroot

noun
edible starch from arrowroot plant
Pronunciation: / ˈæroʊˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈærəʊˌruːt / (BrE)
a starch obtained from the root of the arrowroot plant, used as a thickening agent in cooking
