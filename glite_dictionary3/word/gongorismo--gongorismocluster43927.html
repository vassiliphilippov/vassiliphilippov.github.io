Gongorismo

noun
ornate Spanish poetic style
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊŋɡəˈrizmoʊ / (AmE), / ɡɒŋɡəˈriːzməʊ / (BrE)
An elaborate and esoteric style of writing in Spanish poetry, characterized by Latinate vocabulary, complex syntax, and classical allusions, associated with the poet Luis de Góngora.

The influence of Gongorismo is evident in the highly stylized language of several Spanish Golden Age poets.
