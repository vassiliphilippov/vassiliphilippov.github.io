unfertilised

adjective
not having been fertilized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɜrtəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfəːtɪlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of an egg or ovum) not having been fertilized by a male's sperm.

Examples

unfertilised egg
Chickens can lay unfertilised eggs even without a rooster present.
It is safe to eat an unfertilised egg, as it does not contain a developing embryo.
The biologist studied the difference between fertilised and unfertilised eggs under the microscope.
