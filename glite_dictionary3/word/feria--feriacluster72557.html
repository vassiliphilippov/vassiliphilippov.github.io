feria

noun
local festival or fair (Spanish-speaking regions)
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪriə, feɾˈi.a / (AmE), / ˈfɛːrɪə, feˈɾi.a / (BrE)
(in Spanish-speaking regions) a local festival or religious fair, typically held in honor of a patron saint.

Examples

local feria festival
Every year, the city hosts a lively feria with music, dancing, and traditional food.
Stalls lined the streets during the feria, offering handmade crafts and local delicacies.
Tourists flock to the small town for its annual feria celebrating the local patron saint.
