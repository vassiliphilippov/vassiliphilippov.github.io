Newfoundland

noun
large dog breed, strong swimmer
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːfəndlænd / (AmE), / ˈnjuːfəndlənd / (BrE)
a large, strong breed of dog with a thick, usually black coat, originally developed in Newfoundland and known for its intelligence and swimming ability

Examples

Newfoundland dog breed
