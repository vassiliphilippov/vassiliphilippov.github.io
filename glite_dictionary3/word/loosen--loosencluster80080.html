to loosen

verb
make less tight or secure
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsən /; / ˈlusən / (AmE), / ˈluː.sən /; / ˈluːsən / (BrE)
to make something less tight or more relaxed, or to become less tight or more relaxed (such as shoelaces, knots, or grip)
