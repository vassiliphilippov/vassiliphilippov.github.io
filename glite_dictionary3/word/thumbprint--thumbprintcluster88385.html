thumbprint

noun
impression made by the thumb
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌmˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈθʌm.prɪnt / (BrE)
a mark or impression made by the pad of a thumb, especially as a means of identification

Examples

thumbprint on paper
Each child made a unique painting using only their thumbprint on the card.
She pressed her thumbprint onto the ink pad for the security check.
The detective lifted a clear thumbprint from the doorknob at the scene of the crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fingerprint: an impression or mark made by a finger
