venter

noun
person who expresses opinions or grievances
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntər / (AmE), / ˈvɛntə / (BrE)
a person who expresses or gives vent to personal opinions or grievances

Examples

persistent venter
As the meeting dragged on, the most persistent venter in the room aired yet another complaint.
Social media gives every venter a chance to voice their grievances to the world.
Sometimes a venter simply wants someone to listen, not to offer solutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – complainer: a person who expresses dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym – grumbler: a person who complains about something in a bad-tempered way
  • antonym – optimist: a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future (opposite attitude)
