methapyrilene

noun
antihistamine drug (formerly used)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛθəˈpɪrɪlin / (AmE), / ˌmiːθəˈpɪrɪliːn / (BrE)
An antihistamine drug formerly used to treat allergic conditions such as rhinitis, dermatitis, or itching (pruritus).

Examples
