trochlearis

noun
the fourth cranial nerve of the eye
Pronunciation: / troʊˌkliːəˈrɪs / (AmE), / trɒkˌlɪəˈrɪs / (BrE)
either of the two fourth cranial nerves that control the superior oblique muscles of the eyes
