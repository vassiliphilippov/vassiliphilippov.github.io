araroba

noun
bitter yellow medicinal powder for skin diseases
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrəˈroʊbə / (AmE), / ˌarəˈrəʊbə / (BrE)
a bitter yellow powder obtained from the heartwood of certain South American trees, used in the treatment of skin diseases such as psoriasis and eczema.

Examples
