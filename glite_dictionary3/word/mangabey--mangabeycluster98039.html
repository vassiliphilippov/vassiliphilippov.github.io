mangabey

noun
large, agile African monkey
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡəˌbeɪ / (AmE), / ˈmaŋɡəbeɪ / (BrE)
a large, agile, tree-dwelling monkey of the genus Cercocebus or Lophocebus, distinguished by long limbs, a long tail, and often white upper eyelids, native to Africa

Examples

wild mangabey monkey
Researchers observed the behavior patterns of the mangabeys in the wild.
The mangabey swung gracefully between the branches in its forest habitat.
The white eyelids of a mangabey are one of its most distinguishing features.
