obscure

adjective
difficult to find or discover
Pronunciation: / əbˈskjʊr / (AmE), / əbˈskjʊə / (BrE)
difficult to find or located in a remote or hard-to-access place

Examples

obscure village
Many rare species live in obscure areas of the rainforest, far from human settlements.
The entrance to the cave was so obscure that most hikers passed it by without noticing.
We finally found his obscure workshop hidden behind several old warehouses.
Many important discoveries have taken place in seemingly obscure corners of the world.
She was born in an obscure mountain village far from the main roads.
The artist retreated to an obscure community where few outsiders ever visited.
