disposable

adjective
intended for single or short-term use
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspoʊzəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈspəʊzəbəl / (BrE)
intended to be used once or for a short time and then thrown away

Examples

disposable gloves
Please remember to put your disposable gloves in the bin after use.
She prefers using disposable contact lenses when she travels.
The hotel provided disposable razors for its guests.

  • antonym – reusable: able to be used more than once
