grazier

noun
person raising cattle or sheep
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪʒər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪzɪə / (BrE)
a person who raises and grazes cattle or sheep for commercial purposes

Examples

grazier manages sheep
Many graziers in Australia manage vast areas of land devoted to livestock.
The grazier checked his sheep every morning to ensure they were healthy and well-fed.
The drought forced the grazier to sell some of his cattle at a loss.
