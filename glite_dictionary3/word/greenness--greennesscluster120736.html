greenness

noun
the quality of being green in color
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrin.nəs / (AmE), / ˈɡriːn.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being green in color, especially resembling the color of growing grass

Examples

fresh greenness
After weeks of drought, the steady rain brought back the greenness to the fields.
She admired the deep greenness of the emerald in her ring.
The greenness of the newly sprouted leaves signaled the arrival of spring.
