frappe

noun
a thick milkshake with ice cream
Pronunciation: / ˈfræp / (AmE), / ˈfræpeɪ / (BrE)
A thick milkshake made with ice cream, especially as served in New England, USA.

Examples

chocolate frappe milkshake
In New England, a frappe always contains ice cream, unlike a regular milkshake.
She ordered a chocolate frappe to cool off on the hot afternoon.
The diner is famous for its thick and creamy frappes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – milkshake: a cold drink made of milk, a sweet flavouring such as fruit or chocolate, and usually ice cream, mixed together until it is thick and creamy
