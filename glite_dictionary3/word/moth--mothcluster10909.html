moth

noun
nocturnal insect related to the butterfly
Pronunciation: / mɑθ / (AmE), / mɒθ / (BrE)
an insect related to the butterfly, typically active at dusk or night, with a stout body and feathery or hairlike antennae.

Examples

moth flying at night
A moth fluttered around the porch light, drawn by the glow.
She found a moth with delicate wings resting on her windowsill one evening.
Some species of moth are known to damage clothes by eating natural fibers.
