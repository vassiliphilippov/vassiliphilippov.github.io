whydah

noun
African weaverbird with long tail feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪdə / (AmE), / ˈwɪdə / (BrE)
A small African bird of the weaverbird family, typically with mainly black plumage and, in males, long tail feathers during the breeding season.
