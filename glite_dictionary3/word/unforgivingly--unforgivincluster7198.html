unforgivingly

adverb
in an unforgiving or harsh manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.fərˈɡɪv.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fəˈɡɪv.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is not willing to forgive; harshly and without mercy.

Examples

to speak unforgivingly
He spoke unforgivingly about the mistakes made by his team.
She handled criticism unforgivingly, refusing to give anyone a second chance.
The teacher unforgivingly deducted points for every error, no matter how small.

Related Terms

  • antonym – forgivingly: in a way that shows forgiveness
  • synonym – mercilessly: in a way that shows no mercy
