zettabyte

noun
unit of digital information (2^70 bytes)
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛtəˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈzɛtəˌbaɪt / (BrE)
a unit of digital information equal to about a sextillion (1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000) bytes; used to measure extremely large amounts of data
