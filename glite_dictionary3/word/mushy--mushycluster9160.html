mushy

adjective
soft and pulpy in texture
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌʃi / (AmE), / ˈmʌʃi / (BrE)
soft and pulpy in texture; having the consistency of mush.

Examples

mushy vegetables
After boiling the vegetables too long, they turned out mushy and unappetizing.
She doesn't like her oatmeal too mushy and prefers it with some texture.
The bananas became mushy after sitting out in the heat all day.

Related Terms

  • antonym – firm: solid or resistant to pressure
  • synonym – soft: not hard or firm to the touch
