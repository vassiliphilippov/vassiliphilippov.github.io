highbrowed

adjective
highly cultured or intellectual
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌbraʊd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌbraʊd / (BrE)
having or showing highly cultured, intellectual, or educated tastes and interests; sometimes used with a hint of pretension

Examples
