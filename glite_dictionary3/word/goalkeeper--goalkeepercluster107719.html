goalkeeper

noun
player who guards the goal in sport
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊlˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊlˌkiːpə / (BrE)
the player in sports such as soccer, hockey, or lacrosse whose main role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring by guarding the goal
