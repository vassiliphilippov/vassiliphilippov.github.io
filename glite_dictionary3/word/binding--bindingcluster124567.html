binding

noun
device securing a ski boot to a ski
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
a device fixed to a ski to hold the boot in place, releasing it in case of a fall

Examples

ski binding device
Modern ski bindings are designed for both safety and performance.
Please check that your binding is properly adjusted before you go skiing.
The bindings released automatically when she fell, preventing injury.
