knuckles

noun
metal weapon worn over knuckles
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlz / (AmE), / ˈnʌkl̩z / (BrE)
A small metal weapon designed to be worn over the knuckles on the back of the hand, used to enhance the force of a punch.
