one-sided

adjective
not reversible; has only one usable side
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / wʌn ˈsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of an object or material) not reversible or capable of having either side exposed; intended to be used with only one particular side facing out.

Examples

one-sided poster
Our new banners are one-sided, making them lighter and more cost-effective for indoor events.
The invitation was printed on one-sided glossy paper, with a blank reverse.
These jackets are one-sided, so you can't turn them inside out to wear them differently.
