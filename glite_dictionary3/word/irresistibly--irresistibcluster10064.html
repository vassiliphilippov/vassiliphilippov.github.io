irresistibly

adverb
in an impossible-to-resist way
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is impossible to resist or refuse.

Examples

smile irresistibly
He smiled irresistibly, making it impossible for her to stay angry.
She found herself irresistibly drawn to the beautiful music.
The aroma of freshly baked bread was irresistibly tempting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – irresistible: impossible to resist
  • antonym – resistibly: in a way that can be resisted
