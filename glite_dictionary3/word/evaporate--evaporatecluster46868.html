to evaporate

verb
remove liquid by vaporization
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæpəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈvapəreɪt / (BrE)
to change from a liquid (or solid) into vapor, especially by heating, or to cause this to happen

Scientists evaporate the solvent to isolate the solid compound.

If you leave a glass of water in the sun, it will evaporate quickly.
