Ruscaceae

noun
a family of flowering plants (botany)
Pronunciation: / ruːˈskeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ruːˈskeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants formerly recognized in some classification systems, often treated as a subfamily of Liliaceae; not widely accepted in modern taxonomy
