to distinguish

verb
to make different or special
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ / (BrE)
to make someone or something stand out or be notable because of a special quality

Examples

distinguishes her as leader
Her willingness to listen distinguishes her as an outstanding manager.
Integrity is what distinguishes a true leader from the rest.
The delicate flavor distinguishes this wine from all the others.
He became distinguished in his field by working harder than anyone else.
Her elegant style truly distinguishes her from other designers in the industry.
The bright red door distinguishes their house from every other one on the street.

Related Terms

  • antonym – blend in: not to stand out; to appear the same as surroundings
  • synonym – differentiate: to recognize or show the difference between things
