milkweed

noun
plant with milky sap and tufted seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkwid / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkwiːd / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Asclepias, found mainly in North America, with milky sap and pods that split open to release seeds with silky tufts

Examples

milkweed plant
Monarch butterflies lay their eggs exclusively on milkweed plants.
The silky tufts of the milkweed seeds drifted through the autumn air.
We found a patch of milkweed growing along the edge of the field.
