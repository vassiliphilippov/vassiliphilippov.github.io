carboxyl

noun
the –COOH group in organic acids
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈbɑksəl / (AmE), / kɑːˈbɒksɪl / (BrE)
the univalent group –COOH, present in and characteristic of organic acids

Examples

carboxyl group
A carboxyl group is responsible for the acidic properties of many organic compounds.
