peppermint

noun
pale mint-green colour
Pronunciation: / \u02c8p\u025bp\u0259r\u02ccm\u026ant / (AmE), / \u02c8p\u025bp\u0259m\u026ant / (BrE)
a pale, cool green colour resembling the leaves of the peppermint plant, used in design, fashion, and decor.

Examples

peppermint green color
They painted the kitchen cabinets a soft peppermint to give the space a fresh, airy feel.
For the café's branding, she chose peppermint accents that complemented the warm wood tables.
The interior designer suggested a peppermint throw pillow to brighten the neutral sofa.
