cylinder

noun
engine chamber for a piston
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪl.ɪn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.ɪn.də / (BrE)
A chamber in an engine or other machine in which a piston moves back and forth.

Examples

an engine cylinder
The car has a four-cylinder engine that offers efficient performance.
The mechanic checked inside the cylinder for signs of damage.
When the cylinder is worn out, the piston may not move smoothly.
