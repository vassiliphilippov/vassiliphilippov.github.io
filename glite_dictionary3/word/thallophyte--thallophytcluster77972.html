thallophyte

noun
simple organism without true stems, roots, or leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈθæləˌfaɪt / (AmE), / ˈθaləˌfʌɪt / (BrE)
a member of a group of simple organisms, such as algae, fungi, and lichens, that lack true stems, roots, or leaves

Examples

thallophyte like algae
Fungi are often classified as a type of thallophyte because they lack true roots, stems, and leaves.
