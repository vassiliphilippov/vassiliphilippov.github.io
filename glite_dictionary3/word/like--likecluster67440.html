like

adjective
resembling or similar to
Pronunciation: / laɪk / (AmE), / laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to something; having qualities in common with someone or something else; often used in compound adjectives.

Examples

childlike innocence
He has a childlike innocence that endears him to everyone.
Her voice had a like tone to her mother's.
The two buildings have a remarkably like appearance from a distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – similar: having a resemblance in appearance or character
  • antonym – unlike: different from; not similar to
