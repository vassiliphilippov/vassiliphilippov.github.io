gyre

noun
spiral or circular formation
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪə / (BrE)
a circular or spiral formation, especially one made up of a series of concentric circles as seen in patterns of leaves or petals

Examples

a swirling gyre
A tornado forms a violent gyre of wind, churning everything in its path.
The artist drew a graceful gyre of lines on the paper, mimicking the pattern of a rose's petals.
The leaves were arranged in a perfect gyre around the stem, creating a striking spiral effect.
