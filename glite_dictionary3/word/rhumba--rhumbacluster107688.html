to rhumba

verb
to dance the rhumba
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmbə / (AmE), / ˈrʌmbə / (BrE)
to perform the rhumba, a ballroom dance of Cuban origin characterized by a slow rhythm and sensuous movements.
