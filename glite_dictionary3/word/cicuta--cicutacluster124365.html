Cicuta

noun
genus of poisonous plants (water hemlock)
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjuːtə / (AmE), / sɪˈkjuːtə / (BrE)
a small genus of highly poisonous perennial herbs, known as water hemlocks, with tuberous roots
