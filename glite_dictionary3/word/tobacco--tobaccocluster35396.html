tobacco

noun
dried leaves for smoking or chewing
Pronunciation: / təˈbækoʊ / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ / (BrE)
The dried and processed leaves of the tobacco plant, used for smoking, chewing, or sniffing.

Examples

smoking tobacco
Chewing tobacco can be just as addictive as smoking it.

The farm specializes in growing tobacco for commercial sale.
