blastoma

noun
tumor of immature cells
Pronunciation: / blæˈstoʊmə / (AmE), / blɑːˈstəʊmə / (BrE)
A tumor composed of immature, undifferentiated cells, often arising in embryonic tissues.

Examples

blastoma tumor diagnosis
Certain types of cancer in children, such as neuroblastoma and retinoblastoma, are classified as blastomas.
The doctor diagnosed the child with a blastoma after reviewing the biopsy results.
Treatment options for a blastoma may include surgery, chemotherapy, or radiation therapy, depending on its location and stage.
