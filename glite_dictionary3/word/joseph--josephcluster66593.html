Joseph

proper noun
husband of Mary; foster father of Jesus
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊzəf / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊzɪf / (BrE)
(in the New Testament) the husband of Mary and, in Christian belief, the foster father of Jesus.

Examples

Joseph husband of Mary
Christians honor Joseph for his faith and devotion to his family.
In the nativity story, Joseph travels with Mary to Bethlehem for the census.
Many works of art depict Joseph as a caring and protective father figure.
