shelter

noun
temporary accommodation for the homeless
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛl.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɛl.tə / (BrE)
a building or place that provides temporary accommodation and protection, especially for homeless or displaced people

Examples

a homeless shelter
After losing his job, he spent several nights in a homeless shelter.
The city opened an emergency shelter for families displaced by the flood.
Volunteers serve meals to residents at the local women's shelter.
