Alpha Geminorum

proper noun
bright multiple star in Gemini (Castor)
Pronunciation: / ˌælfə dʒɛˈmɪnərəm / (AmE), / ˌalfə dʒɛˈmɪnərəm / (BrE)
the second brightest star in the constellation Gemini, a multiple star system with six components, also known as Castor
