to express

verb
send by rapid or special messenger service
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛs / (BrE)
to send (a letter, package, etc.) by rapid transport or a special messenger service

Examples

to express a package
She expressed the documents to the headquarters for urgent processing.
The company expressed the samples to their client using an overnight courier.
We need to express this package if we want it to arrive by tomorrow.
