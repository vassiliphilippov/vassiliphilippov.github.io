plug

noun
device in an engine that ignites fuel
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ / (AmE), / plʌɡ / (BrE)
an electrical device fitted into the cylinder head of an internal-combustion engine that ignites the fuel by means of an electric spark

Examples

spark plug
Each cylinder of the engine contains a plug to ignite the air-fuel mixture.
If a plug is worn out, the engine may misfire or run poorly.
The mechanic replaced the faulty plug to ensure the engine started smoothly.
