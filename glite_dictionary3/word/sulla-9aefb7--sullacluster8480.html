sulla

noun
Mediterranean forage plant; source of honey
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌlə / (AmE), / ˈsʌlə / (BrE)
A perennial plant of southern Europe, cultivated as forage and valued for its nectar-rich pink flowers, which are important for honey production.
