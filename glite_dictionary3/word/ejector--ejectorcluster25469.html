ejector

noun
device that draws fluid using a jet
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device or mechanism that expels or moves something, such as a pump or a part of a machine

Examples

ejector pump system
A ejector pump can move fluids without moving parts in contact with the liquid.
Technicians installed a new ejector to improve the efficiency of the cooling system.
The steam ejector was used to create a vacuum in the condenser.
A broken ejector can prevent a gun from working reliably.
After firing, the ejector automatically pushes out the empty shell from the chamber.
The ejector failed to expel the spent casing, causing a jam in the rifle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – extractor: a device in a firearm that withdraws the cartridge case from the chamber
  • synonym – injector: device that introduces fluid into something, often under pressure
