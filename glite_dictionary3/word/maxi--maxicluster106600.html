maxi

noun
long skirt or dress, below calf
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksi / (AmE), / ˈmæksi / (BrE)
a long skirt or dress that reaches below the calf, often to the ankles

During the 1970s, the maxi became a popular fashion statement among young women.

The new boutique is featuring a line of elegant maxis for the summer season.
