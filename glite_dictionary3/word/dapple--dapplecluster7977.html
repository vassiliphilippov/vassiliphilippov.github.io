dapple

noun
a small contrasting spot or patch
Pronunciation: / ˈdæpəl / (AmE), / ˈdæpəl / (BrE)
a small area of color that is different from the color around it, often seen as a spot or patch

Examples

dapple of color
A few dapples of color brightened the painter’s otherwise monochrome canvas.
In the morning light, dapples of sunlight danced across the forest floor.
The horse’s coat was covered with dapples of grey and white.

Related Terms

  • synonym – patch: a small area differing from what's around it
  • synonym – spot: a small round or roundish mark, differing in color or texture
