leucorrhea

noun
vaginal discharge (medical)
Pronunciation: / luː.kəˈriː.ə / (AmE), / luː.kəˈriː.ə / (BrE)
a white or yellowish discharge of mucus from the vagina, especially when abnormal or indicative of infection
