dame

noun
woman of rank or refinement
Pronunciation: / deɪm / (AmE), / deɪm / (BrE)
a woman of rank, dignity, or refinement, often used as a title for certain women of honor or distinction

Examples

a titled dame

She was honored as a dame for her contributions to the arts.
The elegant dame arrived at the gala accompanied by two attendants.
