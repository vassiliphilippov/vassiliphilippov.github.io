verticilliosis

noun
plant disease caused by Verticillium fungi
Pronunciation: / vɜrtɪˌsɪliˈoʊsɪs / (AmE), / vɜːtɪˌsɪliˈəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A plant disease causing wilting, caused by fungi of the genus Verticillium.

Examples

verticilliosis in tomatoes
Symptoms of verticilliosis are frequently mistaken for other types of plant wilts.
The spread of verticilliosis can devastate entire crops if not controlled.
Tomato plants suffering from verticilliosis often show yellowing leaves and stunted growth.
