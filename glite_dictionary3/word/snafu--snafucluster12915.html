snafu

noun
chaotic or confused situation
Pronunciation: / snæˈfu / (AmE), / snæˈfuː / (BrE)
A chaotic or confused situation; a mess.

Examples

a big snafu
After the new software update, the entire system became a snafu and no one could work.
The team's meeting schedule turned into a snafu when no one showed up at the right time.
There was a major snafu at the airport when the computers stopped working.
