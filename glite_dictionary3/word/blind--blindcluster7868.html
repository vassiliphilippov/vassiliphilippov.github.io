blind

adjective
unable to see
Pronunciation: / blaɪnd / (AmE), / blaɪnd / (BrE)
Unable to see because of injury, disease, or a congenital condition.

Examples

blind since birth
Despite being blind since birth, she has learned to read Braille fluently.
He raised money to help blind children get access to special education.
The school offers support for blind and visually impaired students.
