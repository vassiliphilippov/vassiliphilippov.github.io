one-step

adjective
done in a single step; single-stage
Pronunciation: / \u02c8w\u028cn.st\u025bp / (AmE), / \u02c8w\u028cn.st\u025bp / (BrE)
done or carried out in a single stage or operation; requiring only one main step.

one-step process
