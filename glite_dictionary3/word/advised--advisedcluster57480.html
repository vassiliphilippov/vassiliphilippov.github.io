advised

adjective
informed; notified
Pronunciation: / ədˈvaɪzd / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzd / (BrE)
having been informed or notified about something; aware due to receiving advice or information.

Examples

advised of changes
All relevant parties were advised before the meeting began.
Please keep me advised of any new developments.
You will be advised of the results once the review is complete.

Related Terms

  • synonym – informed: having knowledge or information about something
  • antonym – unadvised: not having received advice or information
