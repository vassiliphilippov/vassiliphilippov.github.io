to externalise

verb
make external or manifest
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɝː.nə.laɪz /; / ɪkˈstɝrnəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜː.nə.laɪz /; / ɪkˈstɜːnəlaɪz / (BrE)
