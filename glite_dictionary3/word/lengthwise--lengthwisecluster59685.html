lengthwise

adverb
in the direction of the length
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛŋθˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlɛŋθˌwaɪz /; / ˈlɛŋθwʌɪz / (BrE)
in the direction of the length or longest side of something
