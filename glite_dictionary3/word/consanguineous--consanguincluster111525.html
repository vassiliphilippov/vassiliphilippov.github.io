consanguineous

adjective
related by blood
Pronunciation: / kɑn.sæŋˈɡwɪ.ni.əs / (AmE), / kɒn.sæŋˈɡwɪː.ni.əs / (BrE)
(of people) relating to or descended from the same ancestor; related by blood.

Examples
