DiaBeta

proper noun
brand name for an oral antidiabetic drug
Pronunciation: / daɪəˈbeɪtə / (AmE), / daɪəˈbiːtə / (BrE)
A trademarked name for glyburide, an oral antidiabetic drug that stimulates the release of insulin from the pancreas.
