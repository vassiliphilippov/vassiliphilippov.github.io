soja

noun
annual plant; soybean
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.dʒə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.dʒə / (BrE)
an annual bushy plant native to Asia, cultivated for its oil-rich seeds and used in food, forage, and soil improvement; also known as the soybean
