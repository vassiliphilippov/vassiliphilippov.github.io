NSC

noun
abbreviation for National Security Council (US government committee)
Pronunciation: / ɛn ɛs si / (AmE), / ɛn ɛs si / (BrE)
an abbreviation for National Security Council, a committee in the executive branch of government that advises the president on foreign, military, and national security matters
