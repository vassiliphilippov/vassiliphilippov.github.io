Filipino

noun
official language of the Philippines
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪləˈpinoʊ / (AmE), / ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnəʊ / (BrE)
the official language of the Philippines, based primarily on Tagalog and incorporating words from other Philippine languages

Examples

Filipino language
