ridiculous

adjective
absurd or laughable; deserving scorn
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdɪkjələs / (AmE), / rɪˈdɪkjʊləs / (BrE)
very silly, unreasonable, or absurd, so much that it deserves to be laughed at or not taken seriously
