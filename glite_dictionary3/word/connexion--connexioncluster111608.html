connexion

noun
association or linking of ideas or events
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛkʃən / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of bringing ideas or events together in memory or imagination; an association or link between concepts.

Examples

memory connexion
His essay explores the connexion of childhood memories with adult behavior.
In psychology, the connexion between stimulus and response is fundamental.
She made a sudden connexion between her dream and the events of the previous day.
