tombola

noun
raffle or lottery drawing with prizes
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːm.bə.lə / (AmE), / ˈtɒm.bə.lə / (BrE)
A type of lottery in which numbered tickets are drawn at random from a revolving drum, with each ticket corresponding to a prize.

Examples

tombola prize draw
I bought several tickets for the tombola at the school fair, hoping to win a prize.
The tombola drum spun noisily as volunteers drew the winning numbers.
The charity organized a tombola to raise funds for the new community center.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lottery: a means of raising money by selling numbered tickets and giving prizes to the holders of numbers drawn at random
  • synonym – raffle: a lottery in which people buy tickets to win a prize
