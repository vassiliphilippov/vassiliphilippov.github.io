dentate

adjective
having toothlike projections at the margin
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛn.teɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.teɪt / (BrE)
having toothlike or serrated projections along the edge, especially used in biology and botany to describe leaves or anatomical structures.
