lightweight

noun
weight class between featherweight and welterweight
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt.weɪt /; / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt /; / ˈlaɪtweɪt / (BrE)
a weight class in sports like boxing or wrestling for competitors who are heavier than featherweights but lighter than welterweights, or a person who competes in this class
