elkhound

noun
Norwegian breed of hunting dog
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlkˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈɛlkˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
A breed of compact, medium-sized dog with a heavy grey coat, developed in Norway for hunting elk.
