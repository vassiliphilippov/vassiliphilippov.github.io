Johann Strauss II

proper noun
Austrian composer of waltzes, the 'waltz king'
Pronunciation: / joʊˌhɑn ˈstraʊs səˈkənd / (AmE), / jəʊˌhan ˈstraʊs səˈkənd / (BrE)
(1825–1899) An Austrian composer known as the 'waltz king', famous for his many waltzes and dance music, and the son of Johann Strauss the Elder.
