dresser

noun
person who dresses in a certain way
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsə / (BrE)
a person who dresses in a specified style, especially one who pays particular attention to their clothing and appearance

Examples

smart dresser
He's a meticulous dresser, never seen with a wrinkle in his shirt.
She's always been an elegant dresser, admired for her taste.
The party was filled with stylish dressers showing off the latest trends.
