Egyptologist

noun
scholar of ancient Egypt
Pronunciation: / iːˌdʒɪpˈtɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / iːˌdʒɪpˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A scholar or archaeologist who specializes in the study of ancient Egypt and its antiquities.

Examples

Egyptologist studies tombs
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an Egyptologist and uncovering ancient tombs in Egypt.
Dr. Carter was a renowned Egyptologist who led expeditions in the Valley of the Kings.
The museum invited an Egyptologist to deliver a lecture on the mysteries of the pyramids.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Egyptology: the study of the history and culture of ancient Egypt
  • synonym – archaeologist: a person who studies human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites
