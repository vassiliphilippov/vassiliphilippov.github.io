scrapbook

noun
album for clippings and photos
Pronunciation: / ˈskræpˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈskrapˌbʊk / (BrE)
a book or album in which clippings, photographs, or other memorabilia are collected and preserved, often arranged creatively.

Examples

scrapbook of photos
After the trip, he created a scrapbook filled with ticket stubs and postcards.
She spent the afternoon adding old family photos to her scrapbook.
The children proudly showed their summer memories in a colorful scrapbook.
