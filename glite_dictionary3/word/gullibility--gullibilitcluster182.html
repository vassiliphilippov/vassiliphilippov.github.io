gullibility

noun
tendency to be easily deceived
Pronunciation: / ˌɡʌləˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the tendency to believe things too readily and therefore to be easily deceived or tricked
