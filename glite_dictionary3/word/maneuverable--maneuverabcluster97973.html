maneuverable

adjective
easy to move or steer
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːvərəbəl / (AmE), / məˈnjuːvərəbl / (BrE)
Able to be easily moved or steered in different directions or positions.

Examples

maneuverable vehicle
A maneuverable wheelchair is essential for navigating crowded areas.
Thanks to its compact design, the drone is highly maneuverable indoors.
The new car is much more maneuverable in tight city streets than the old model.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agile: able to move quickly and easily
  • antonym – unwieldy: difficult to move or control because of size or shape
