paradise

noun
place of eternal happiness (heaven)
Pronunciation: / ˈpæɹəˌdaɪs / (AmE), / ˈpærədaɪs / (BrE)
(in some religions) the place where good people go after death; heaven.

Examples

paradise after death
Believers hope to enter paradise after a life of virtue and faith.
Some religions describe paradise as a place of peace, light, and eternal happiness.
The idea of paradise has inspired countless works of art and literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym – heaven: the dwelling place of God and the angels; a state of eternal happiness
  • antonym – hell: the place or state of punishment for the wicked after death
