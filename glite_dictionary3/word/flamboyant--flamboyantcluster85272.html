flamboyant

noun
a tropical tree with scarlet or orange flowers
Pronunciation: / flæmˈbɔɪənt / (AmE), / flamˈbɔɪənt / (BrE)
A tropical tree (Delonix regia), native to Madagascar, widely grown for its large and vivid scarlet or orange flower clusters.

Examples

a flamboyant tree
A single flamboyant can transform a plain landscape into a vibrant spectacle during flowering season.
Many streets in the city are lined with flamboyant trees, creating a canopy of red and orange flowers.
The garden was dominated by a magnificent flamboyant in full bloom.
