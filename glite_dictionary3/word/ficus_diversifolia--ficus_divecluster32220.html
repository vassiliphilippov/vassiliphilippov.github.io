Ficus diversifolia

noun
species of houseplant with mistletoe-like leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪkəs daɪˌvɜrsɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˈfiːkəs dɪˌvɜːsɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
A shrub or small tree often grown as a houseplant, with leaves resembling those of mistletoe.

Examples

Ficus diversifolia houseplant
I bought a Ficus diversifolia at the nursery to brighten up my office.
Many people keep a Ficus diversifolia in their living rooms because of its attractive foliage.
The leaves of Ficus diversifolia are often mistaken for those of mistletoe.
