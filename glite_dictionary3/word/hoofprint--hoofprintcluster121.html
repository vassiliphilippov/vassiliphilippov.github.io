hoofprint

noun
impression made by an animal's hoof
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊfˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈhʊfˌprɪnt / (BrE)
a visible mark or impression left on a surface by the hoof of an animal

Examples

hoofprint in mud
She knelt down to examine the hoofprint in the soft earth.
The muddy path was scattered with fresh hoofprints from passing deer.
The trail of hoofprints led straight to the riverbank.
