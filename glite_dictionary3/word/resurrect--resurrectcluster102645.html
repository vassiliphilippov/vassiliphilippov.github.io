to resurrect

verb
bring back into use
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkt / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkt / (BrE)
to bring something back into use, practice, or attention after it has been forgotten or inactive

to resurrect old ideas
