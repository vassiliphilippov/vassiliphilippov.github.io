cortisone

noun
a steroid hormone used to treat inflammation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtɪˌzoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtɪzəʊn / (BrE)
a steroid hormone produced by the adrenal cortex, used medically to treat inflammation and other conditions

Examples
