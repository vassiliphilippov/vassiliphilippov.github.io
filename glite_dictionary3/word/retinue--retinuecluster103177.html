retinue

noun
group accompanying an important person
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛt.ən.ju / (AmE), / ˈrɛt.ɪ.njuː / (BrE)
A group of assistants, advisers, or others accompanying an important person.

Examples

retinue of bodyguards
A large retinue followed the movie star throughout the festival.
During the state visit, the president and his retinue toured the historic site.
The queen arrived with her retinue of ladies-in-waiting and advisers.
