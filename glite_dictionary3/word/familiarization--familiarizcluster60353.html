familiarization

noun
the act of becoming familiar with something
Pronunciation: / fəˌmɪljəɹəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / fəˌmɪliəɹʌɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the process of learning about something so that you become comfortable or knowledgeable with it

Examples

familiarization with new equipment
His quick familiarization with the software impressed his manager.
Proper familiarization with emergency procedures is required for all employees.
The training program included a familiarization tour of the new facility.

Related Terms

  • synonym – acquaintance: knowledge or experience of something; personal knowledge
  • antonym – ignorance: lack of knowledge or information
