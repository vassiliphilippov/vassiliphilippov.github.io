correlation

noun
statistical relationship between variables
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃən /; / ˌkɔːr.əˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒr.əˈleɪ.ʃən /; / ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a relationship or connection between two or more things, especially when changes in one are associated with changes in another (often used in statistics)
