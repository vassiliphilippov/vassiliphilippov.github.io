dab

noun
a light or quick touch
Pronunciation: / dæb / (AmE), / dab /; / dæb / (BrE)
a light or quick touch, or a small amount of something (like cream or butter) applied gently

Examples

dab of cream
Apply a small dab of cream to the affected area.
He gave the painting a gentle dab with his brush.
She removed her tears with a quick dab of her handkerchief.
He added a dab of butter to the pan before cooking the eggs.
She put a dab of cream on her finger and gently applied it to the cut.
There was just a dab of sauce on the edge of his plate.

  • synonym – smear: a small amount of a substance that has been spread on a surface
  • synonym – splash: a small quantity of liquid thrown or falling onto a surface
  • synonym – tap: a light touch or gentle knock
