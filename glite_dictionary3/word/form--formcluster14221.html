form

noun
class or group of students in a school
Pronunciation: / fɔrm / (AmE), / fɔːm / (BrE)
a group of students in a school who are taught together and usually move up through the grades as a unit, especially in British schools

Examples

school form group
Our form teacher organized a field trip for the whole group.
She has been with the same form since Year 7.
The school assigned each new student to a form based on their age.
