unattractive

adjective
not arousing interest or appeal
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈtræk.tɪv / (BrE)
not arousing interest or appeal; lacking qualities that make something desirable or engaging.

Examples

an unattractive offer
An unattractive investment failed to capture the attention of potential buyers.
Many found the proposal unattractive because it offered little innovation or excitement.
The job offer was unattractive due to its low salary and lack of benefits.
