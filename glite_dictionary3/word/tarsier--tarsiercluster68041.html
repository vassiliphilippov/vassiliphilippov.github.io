tarsier

noun
small nocturnal primate of Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːr.si.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɑː.sɪ.ə / (BrE)
a small nocturnal tree-dwelling primate native to Southeast Asia, especially Indonesia and the Philippines, with large eyes and long fingers, feeding only on insects and small animals

Examples

A tarsier can turn its head almost 180 degrees to spot prey.
