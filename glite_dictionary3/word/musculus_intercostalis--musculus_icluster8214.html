musculus intercostalis

noun
muscle between the ribs
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.kjəl.əs ˌɪn.tərˈkoʊ.stæl.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.ləs ˌɪn.təˈkɒs.tæl.ɪs / (BrE)
