frigid

adjective
emotionally cold or unfriendly
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪdʒɪd / (AmE), / ˈfrɪdʒɪd / (BrE)
lacking warmth or friendliness; cold and unwelcoming in manner or attitude

Examples

The frigid tone of the meeting made everyone uncomfortable.
