nonchalant

adjective
calm, unconcerned
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnʃəˈlɑnt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnʃəˈlɑːnt / (BrE)
behaving in a calm, relaxed way that shows you are not worried or anxious; showing a lack of concern or enthusiasm

Examples

nonchalant attitude
Despite the chaos around him, he remained nonchalant and composed.
His nonchalant attitude made it seem as if he didn't care about the outcome at all.
She gave a nonchalant shrug when asked about the results of the test.

Related Terms

  • synonym – casual: not showing much care or thought; unconcerned
  • antonym – concerned: worried or upset about something
  • synonym – insouciant: showing a lack of concern or anxiety
