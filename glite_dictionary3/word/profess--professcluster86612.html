to profess

verb
declare belief or allegiance openly
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛs / (AmE), / prəˈfɛs / (BrE)
to declare, claim, or admit something openly, especially feelings, beliefs, or innocence, often when it may not be true

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym – affirm: to state something firmly and publicly
  • synonym – confess: to admit to a fault or crime
  • synonym – declare: to state something in a formal or emphatic way
  • antonym – deny: to state that something is not true or refuse to accept it
  • synonym – pretend: to behave as if something is true when it is not
