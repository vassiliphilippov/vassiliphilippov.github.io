formality

noun
an established procedure or social custom
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈmæl.ə.ti /; / fɔrˈmæləti / (AmE), / fɔːˈmæl.ə.ti /; / fɔːˈmælɪti / (BrE)
an established procedure, action, or requirement that follows rules, customs, or etiquette, often done out of tradition or convention
