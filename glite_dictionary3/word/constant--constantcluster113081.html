constant

adjective
firm and unwavering; steadfast
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnstənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒnstənt / (BrE)
remaining firm and unwavering in purpose, loyalty, or affection; steadfast.

Examples

constant support
Despite criticism, she was constant in her beliefs and never wavered.
His constant support made a real difference to the team.
She remained a constant friend through all the difficult times in my life.

  • antonym – fickle: changing frequently, especially regarding loyalties or affections
  • synonym – steadfast: firm and unwavering in commitment or loyalty
