fumble

noun
sports mistake dropping the ball
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈfʌmbəl / (BrE)
In sports, an act of accidentally dropping or failing to hold onto the ball, especially in American football.

Examples

costly fumble
After recovering the fumble, the defense quickly turned the tide.
His first career touchdown came after he scooped up a fumble on the field.
The quarterback's fumble in the last quarter cost his team the game.
