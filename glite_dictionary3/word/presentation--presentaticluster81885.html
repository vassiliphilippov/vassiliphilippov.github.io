presentation

noun
position of fetus during birth (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
(in obstetrics) The position of the fetus in the uterus in relation to the birth canal, especially as it affects the process of birth.

Examples

breech presentation
A breech presentation may require special delivery procedures.
Doctors determined the baby's presentation using an ultrasound scan.
The midwife explained the importance of fetal presentation during labor.
