to skate

verb
move wearing skates
Pronunciation: / skeɪt / (AmE), / skeɪt / (BrE)
to move smoothly over a surface wearing skates, especially on ice or roller skates

Examples

to skate on ice
Every winter, children love to skate on the frozen lake near their town.
He learned to skate backwards after several months of practice.
They decided to skate together at the new indoor rink.

  • synonym – rollerblade: to skate using in-line skates (Rollerblades)
