softness

noun
quiet or gentle quality of a sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɒftnəs / (BrE)
the quality of a sound being quiet, gentle, and lacking in loudness or harshness

Examples

softness of her voice
The softness of her whisper made the room feel calm and peaceful.
The softness of the music was perfect for a relaxing evening.
You could hear the softness in the way the piano was played during the lullaby.

Related Terms

  • synonym – gentleness: the quality of being gentle or mild, especially in sound or manner
  • antonym – loudness: the quality of being loud, especially in sound
