teaspoonful

noun
amount a teaspoon holds
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːˌspuːnfʊl / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌspuːnfʊl / (BrE)
An amount equal to what a teaspoon can hold, often used as a measurement in cooking and medicine.

Examples

a teaspoonful of sugar
Add one teaspoonful of sugar to your tea and stir well.
She takes a teaspoonful of medicine every morning after breakfast.
The recipe calls for a teaspoonful of salt and a pinch of pepper.
