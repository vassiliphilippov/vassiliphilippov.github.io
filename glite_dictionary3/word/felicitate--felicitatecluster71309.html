to felicitate

verb
formally express congratulations
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪsɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / fɪˈlɪsɪteɪt / (BrE)
to formally express good wishes or congratulate someone for an achievement or occasion

Examples

to felicitate a winner
The ambassador was felicitated by his peers on receiving the international award.
The university officials felicitated the top students with medals and certificates during the ceremony.
We gathered to felicitate her on the grand success of her latest novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym – congratulate: express pleasure at someone's achievement
