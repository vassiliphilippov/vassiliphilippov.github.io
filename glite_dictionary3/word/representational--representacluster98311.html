representational

adjective
depicting things as they appear, especially in art
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
(especially in art) depicting objects, figures, or scenes in a way that is recognizable and realistically reflects appearance.

Examples

representational painting style
She prefers representational art to abstract works because of its clarity.
The museum features mainly representational paintings from the nineteenth century.
Their new exhibition focuses on representational images of everyday urban life.

Related Terms

  • antonym – abstract: not representing recognizable objects, especially in art
  • synonym – figurative: representing forms that are recognizably derived from real object sources, especially in art
