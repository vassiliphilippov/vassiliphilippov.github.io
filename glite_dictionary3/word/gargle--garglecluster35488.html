gargle

noun
sound produced while gargling
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːɡl / (BrE)
a bubbling or gurgling sound made while bathing the throat with a liquid

Examples

gargle sound effect
He was startled by the strange gargle coming from the sink late at night.
The gargle echoed in the bathroom as she rinsed her mouth after brushing her teeth.
The child's attempts at singing turned into a playful gargle of laughter.
