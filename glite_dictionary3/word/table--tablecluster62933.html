to table

verb
postpone a proposal or discussion
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈteɪb(ə)l / (BrE)
to postpone or delay consideration of (something), typically a proposal or motion, until a later time

Examples

to table a proposal
Due to recent developments, the board chose to table the proposal.
Let's table this discussion and return to it at the end of the meeting.
The committee decided to table the vote until next week.

Related Terms

  • antonym – advance: to move (something) forward in time; do earlier
  • synonym – postpone: to delay something to a later time
