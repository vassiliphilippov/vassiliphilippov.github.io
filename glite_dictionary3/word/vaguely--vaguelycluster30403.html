vaguely

adverb
unclearly or indistinctly
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪɡli / (AmE), / ˈveɪɡli / (BrE)
In a way that is not clear, definite, or detailed.

Examples

vaguely remember something
His face looks vaguely familiar, but I can't place him.
She could vaguely remember her childhood in the old house.
The outline of the island was vaguely visible in the mist.
