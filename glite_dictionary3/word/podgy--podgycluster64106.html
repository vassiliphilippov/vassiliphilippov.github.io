podgy

adjective
slightly fat and short (person)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑdʒi / (AmE), / ˈpɒdʒi / (BrE)
(of a person or their body) slightly fat and short, often in a way that is seen as unattractive or cute.

Examples

podgy fingers
His podgy fingers struggled to reach all the piano keys at once.
She smiled as she squeezed her cat’s podgy belly.
The little boy was podgy but full of energy as he played in the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym – plump: slightly fat in a pleasant way
  • antonym – slim: slender, thin (especially in an attractive way)
