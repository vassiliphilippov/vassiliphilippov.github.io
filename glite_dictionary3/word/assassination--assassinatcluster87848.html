assassination

noun
attack on someone's reputation
Pronunciation: / əˌsæsɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌsæsɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of deliberately attempting to damage someone's reputation, especially through false or malicious statements

Examples

assassination of character
Her reputation never fully recovered after the vicious assassination carried out by her political rivals.
The documentary exposes the methods used in the assassination of public figures' reputations.
The newspaper was accused of engaging in assassination of the senator's character.

Related Terms

  • synonym – defamation: the act of damaging someone's good reputation; slander or libel
  • synonym – slander: the action or crime of making a false spoken statement damaging to a person's reputation
