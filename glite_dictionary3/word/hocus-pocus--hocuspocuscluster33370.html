hocus-pocus

noun
deceptive or misleading talk/activity
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊkəsˈpoʊkəs / (AmE), / ˌhəʊkəsˈpəʊkəs / (BrE)
deceptive or meaningless talk or activity, especially intended to trick or mislead people (often by making something seem mysterious or magical)
