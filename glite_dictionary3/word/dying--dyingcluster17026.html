dying

noun
the end of something; termination
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
The end or termination of something, especially hopes, ideas, or periods.

Examples

the dying days
During the dying of the old year, people reflected on past mistakes.
She felt a dying of hope as more bad news arrived.
The dying of optimism in the community was felt by everyone after the announcement.
