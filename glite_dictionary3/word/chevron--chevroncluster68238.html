chevron

noun
V-shaped UI icon indicating navigation/expand
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛvrən / (AmE), / ˈʃɛvrən / (BrE)
(in user interfaces) a small V-shaped icon or mark used on-screen to indicate navigation direction, a drop-down control, or expandable/collapsible content.

Examples

menu chevron icon
Tap the chevron to reveal the hidden details in the menu.
The app uses small right-pointing chevrons to show which items lead to subpages.
Designers chose a subtle down-facing chevron to indicate collapsible sections on the page.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drop-down arrow: an arrow-shaped icon that shows a menu or list can be expanded
  • synonym – indicator: a symbol or icon that shows status, direction, or available actions in an interface
