Shetland

proper noun
group of islands north of Scotland
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛtlənd / (AmE), / ˈʃɛtlənd / (BrE)
an archipelago of about 100 islands in the North Atlantic Ocean, situated to the north of mainland Scotland
