Vaccaria

noun
seeds used in acupressure
Pronunciation: / vækˈkɛəriə / (AmE), / vakˈkeə.riə / (BrE)
The small, dried seeds of the Vaccaria plant used in acupressure or auricular acupuncture as tiny stimulants applied to acupuncture points.

Examples

Vaccaria seeds therapy
The therapist taped a tiny packet of dried Vaccaria to my ear to relieve tension.
At the clinic, they recommended placing a single Vaccaria on each acupuncture point for two days.
You can buy sterile adhesive patches preloaded with Vaccaria at many acupuncture supply shops.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ear seed: a small seed or bead applied to the ear to stimulate auricular acupuncture points
