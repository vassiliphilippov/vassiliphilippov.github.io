Leuwenhoek

proper noun
Dutch pioneer microscopist, first described microbes
Pronunciation: / ˈluːvənˌhʊk / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪvənˌhʊk / (BrE)
Antonie van Leuwenhoek (1632–1723), Dutch pioneer microscopist known for first describing microbes, spermatozoa, and blood corpuscles.

Examples

Leuwenhoek discovered microbes
Many textbooks credit Leuwenhoek as the father of microbiology.
