to catheterize

verb
insert a catheter into a body part
Pronunciation: / ˈkæθəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈkæθətəˌraɪz / (BrE)
to insert a catheter into a body part, especially to allow the passage or drainage of fluids

Examples

to catheterize the bladder
Doctors sometimes need to catheterize the bladder in cases of urinary retention.
She was taught how to catheterize patients safely during her clinical training.
