protractor

noun
angle-measuring instrument
Pronunciation: / proʊˈtræktər / (AmE), / prəˈtræktə / (BrE)
a flat, semicircular or circular instrument for measuring or drawing angles, typically marked in degrees

Examples

protractor for measuring angles
Before drawing the diagram, she carefully positioned her protractor on the paper.
Engineers often use a protractor to ensure accurate measurements of angles in their sketches.
The math teacher explained how to use a protractor to measure the angle of the triangle.
