William Schwenk Gilbert

proper noun
English librettist and dramatist, collaborator with Sullivan
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪljəm ʃwɛŋk ˈgɪlbɚt / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ʃwɛŋk ˈɡɪlbət / (BrE)
