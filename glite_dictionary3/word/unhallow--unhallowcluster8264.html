to unhallow

verb
remove the sacredness from
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhæloʊ / (AmE), / ʌnˈhaləʊ / (BrE)
to remove the consecration or sacredness from (a person or an object); to make no longer holy

Examples

to unhallow a church
According to legend, the ground was unhallowed before the burial took place.
Some rituals are performed to unhallow sacred objects that are no longer in use.
The temple was unhallowed after being converted into a marketplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym – desecrate: to treat (a sacred thing or place) with violent disrespect
