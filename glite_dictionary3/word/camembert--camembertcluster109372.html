Camembert

noun
soft creamy French cheese
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməmˌbɛrt / (AmE), / ˈkaməmˌbɛː / (BrE)
a rich, soft, creamy French cheese with an edible white rind, traditionally made from cow's milk

Examples

slice of Camembert

The creamy texture of Camembert makes it perfect for baking as an appetizer.
