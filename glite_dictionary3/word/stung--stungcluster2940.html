stung

adjective
emotionally hurt or provoked to anger
Pronunciation: / stʌŋ / (AmE), / stʌŋ / (BrE)
feeling hurt, offended, or provoked to anger as a result of someone's words or actions

Examples

felt stung by criticism
After being left out of the meeting, he was visibly stung.
His words left me stung and unsure of how to respond.
She felt stung by his harsh criticism and left the room quietly.

  • synonym – irritated: feeling slight anger or annoyance
