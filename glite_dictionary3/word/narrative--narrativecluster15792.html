narrative

noun
an account or story of events
Pronunciation: / ˈnærətɪv / (AmE), / ˈnærətɪv / (BrE)
a spoken or written account of connected events; a story, especially one that is detailed or imaginative

Examples

a narrative story
Her new novel weaves a complex narrative that spans three generations.
The documentary offered a powerful narrative of the community’s struggle with change.
The film’s narrative shifts between past and present to deepen the story.

Related Terms

  • synonym – account: a report or description of an event or experience
  • synonym – story: an account of imaginary or real people and events told for entertainment
