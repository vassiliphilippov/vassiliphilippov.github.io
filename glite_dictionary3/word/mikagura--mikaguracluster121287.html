mikagura

noun
formal Shinto ritual dance
Pronunciation: / miˈkɑːɡuːrə / (AmE), / miˈkɑːɡʊrə / (BrE)
a type of formal Shinto ritual dance, performed especially at imperial and major shrine ceremonies in Japan

Examples

mikagura ritual dance
During the annual festival, the priests performed the mikagura to honor the deities.
Scholars studied the history and significance of mikagura in Japanese imperial ceremonies.
The mikagura is characterized by its solemn and highly formalized movements.
