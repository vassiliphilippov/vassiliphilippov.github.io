to coinsure

verb
participate in coinsurance as an insurer
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɪnʃʊr / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪnʃɔː / (BrE)
to participate in a coinsurance arrangement, especially as one of multiple insurers sharing the risk of a policy

Examples

to coinsure a policy
After a risk assessment, the firms were willing to coinsure the investment project.
Several companies decided to coinsure the new high-risk policy together.
The banks agreed to coinsure the property against natural disasters.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insure: to provide or obtain insurance coverage
  • synonym – underwrite: to assume risk and guarantee insurance coverage
