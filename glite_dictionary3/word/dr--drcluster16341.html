Dr.

noun
title for someone with a doctorate
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑktər / (AmE), / ˈdɒktə / (BrE)
a title used before the name of a person who has obtained a doctorate, such as a Ph.D.

Examples

Dr. Lee teaches
Dr. Lee will be giving the keynote speech at the conference on Saturday.
After earning her Ph.D., she officially became Dr. Smith.
Both Dr. Patel and Dr. Johnson contributed to the research article.
