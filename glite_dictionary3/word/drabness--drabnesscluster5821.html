drabness

noun
dullness; lack of style or color
Pronunciation: / ˈdræbnəs / (AmE), / ˈdræbnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being dull, lacking color or stylishness.

Examples

drabness of clothing
He tried to add some color to offset the drabness of his office suit.
The city streets lost their winter drabness when the flowers began to bloom.
There was a certain drabness to the apartment that made it feel uninviting.
