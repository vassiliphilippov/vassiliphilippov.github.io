remote

noun
device for controlling a machine from a distance
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmoʊt / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊt / (BrE)
a device that is used to control a machine or apparatus, such as a television, from a distance

Examples

TV remote
Can you hand me the remote?
She bought a universal remote to operate all her devices.
The remote isn't working, so I can't change the channel.
