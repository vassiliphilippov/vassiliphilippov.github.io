Ruminantia

noun
suborder of cud-chewing mammals
Pronunciation: / ruːˌmɪniˈænʃiə /; / ˌruːmɪˈnænʃiə / (AmE), / ruːˌmɪniˈantɪə /; / ˌruːmɪˈnantɪə / (BrE)
a group (suborder) of mammals that chew cud, including cattle, sheep, goats, antelopes, deer, giraffes, and similar animals
