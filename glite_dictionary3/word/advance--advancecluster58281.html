to advance

verb
give a promotion
Pronunciation: / ədˈvæns / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
to give someone a promotion or assign them to a higher position in an organization

Examples

to advance an employee
After years of dedication, she was finally advanced to the role of director.
He was advanced within the company for his outstanding performance.
Many employees hope to advance quickly in their careers by taking on extra responsibilities.
