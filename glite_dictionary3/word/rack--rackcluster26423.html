to rack

verb
adjust with a rack and pinion
Pronunciation: / ræk / (AmE), / ræk / (BrE)
to move or adjust (a camera, lens, or device) using a rack and pinion mechanism

Examples

to rack the lens
Before filming started, the operator began racking the lens to achieve the desired perspective.
She carefully racked the camera to shift the focal plane for the next shot.
The technician needed to rack the microscope slide into perfect focus.
