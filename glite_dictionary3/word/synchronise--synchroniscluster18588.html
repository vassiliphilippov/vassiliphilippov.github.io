to synchronise

verb
make film sound match action
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋkrəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.krə.naɪz / (BrE)
to make the sound in a motion picture exactly match the movement and actions on the screen

Examples

to synchronise sound
It can take hours to synchronise the dialogue with the actors' lip movements in a movie.
Technicians worked to synchronise the new soundtrack with the remastered film footage.
You must synchronise the audio carefully so that every sound matches the on-screen action.
