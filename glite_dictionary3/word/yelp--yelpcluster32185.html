yelp

noun
a sharp, high-pitched cry
Pronunciation: / jɛlp / (AmE), / jɛlp / (BrE)
a short, sharp, high-pitched cry or bark, especially made by a dog or in pain or excitement

Examples

a yelp of pain
A loud yelp echoed through the park as the dog chased a squirrel.
Her yelp of surprise startled everyone in the room.
The puppy let out a sudden yelp when its tail was stepped on.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bark: the sharp sound made by a dog
  • synonym – howl: a long, loud, doleful cry uttered by a dog or wolf
