magnolia

noun
tree with large fragrant flowers
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnoʊ.li.ə / (AmE), / mæɡˈnəʊ.li.ə / (BrE)
a tree or shrub of the genus Magnolia, known for their large, fragrant flowers and longevity

Examples

magnolia tree blossoms

Every spring, the magnolia fills the garden with a sweet, distinctive fragrance.
