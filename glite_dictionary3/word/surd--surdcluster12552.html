surd

adjective
voiceless speech sound (phonetics)
Pronunciation: / sɜrd / (AmE), / sɜːd / (BrE)
(of a speech sound) produced without vibration of the vocal cords; voiceless.

Examples

surd speech sound
In phonetics, 'k' is classified as a surd sound, unlike 'g'.
Linguists study both surd and sonant consonants to understand speech patterns.
The letter 'p' is a surd consonant in English.
