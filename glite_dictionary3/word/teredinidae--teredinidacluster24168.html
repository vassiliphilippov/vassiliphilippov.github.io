Teredinidae

noun
family of shipworm mollusks
Pronunciation: / təˌrɛdəˈnɪdiː / (AmE), / təˌriːdɪˈnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine mollusks known as shipworms that bore into and destroy submerged wood, such as piers and ships.
