pinniped

noun
aquatic mammal with flippers, e.g. seal
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnɪˌpɛd /; / ˈpɪn.əˌpɛd /; / ˈpɪnəˌpɛd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnɪˌpɛd /; / ˈpɪn.ɪˌpɛd / (BrE)
