doting

adjective
excessively loving or indulgent
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdəʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely fond of someone and often showing excessive love and attention, sometimes to a foolish degree.

Examples

doting parent
He was a doting husband, always attentive and caring toward his wife.
Her doting parents rarely refused her anything, no matter how unreasonable her requests.
The doting grandmother spent hours baking cookies for her grandchildren every weekend.
