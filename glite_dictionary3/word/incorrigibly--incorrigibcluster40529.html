incorrigibly

adverb
in an uncorrectable manner
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɔr.ɪ.dʒə.bli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒr.ɪ.dʒə.bli / (BrE)
In a way that is impossible to correct, reform, or improve.

Examples

incorrigibly optimistic
He remained incorrigibly optimistic despite repeated setbacks.
She is incorrigibly late to every meeting, no matter how important.
The child giggled incorrigibly through the entire lesson.
