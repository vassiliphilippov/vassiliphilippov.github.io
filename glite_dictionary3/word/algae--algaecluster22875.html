algae

noun
aquatic plant-like organisms
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.dʒi / (AmE), / ˈæl.dʒiː / (BrE)
Simple plant-like organisms, mostly aquatic, that contain chlorophyll but lack true stems, roots, and leaves.
