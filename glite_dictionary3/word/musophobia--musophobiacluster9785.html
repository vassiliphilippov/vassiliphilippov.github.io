musophobia

noun
morbid fear of mice
Pronunciation: / ˌmjuːsəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌmjuːsəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
An abnormal or morbid fear of mice.

Examples

musophobia causes panic
Because of his musophobia, he avoided old houses where he thought mice might be present.
His severe musophobia made it impossible for him to visit friends who kept pet mice.
The psychologist diagnosed her with musophobia after repeated panic attacks at the sight of rodents.

Related Terms
