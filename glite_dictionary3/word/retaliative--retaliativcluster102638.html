retaliative

adjective
of or relating to retaliation or retribution
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtæliˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈtæliətɪv / (BrE)
Relating to or having the nature of retaliation or retribution; intended as a response to wrongdoing or injury.

Examples

retaliative action
His actions were clearly retaliative, intending to settle old scores.
Some argue that retaliative violence only perpetuates the cycle of conflict.
The country imposed retaliative tariffs after the trade dispute escalated.

Related Terms

  • synonym – retaliatory: relating to or characterized by retaliation
  • synonym – retributive: of or relating to retribution
