scorper

noun
engraving tool for scooping broad areas
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔrpər / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpə / (BrE)
A tool with a curved blade used by engravers to scoop out broad areas of wood or metal.

Examples

scorper for engraving
