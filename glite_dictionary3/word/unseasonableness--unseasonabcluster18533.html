unseasonableness

noun
occurrence at an inappropriate time
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsiːzənəblnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːzənəblnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of occurring at an unsuitable or inappropriate time

Examples

unseasonableness of a request
He complained about the unseasonableness of her request, as it came just before midnight.
The unseasonableness of the storm disrupted all their outdoor plans.
The farmer worried about the unseasonableness of the late frost affecting his crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unseasonable: not occurring at a suitable or usual time
