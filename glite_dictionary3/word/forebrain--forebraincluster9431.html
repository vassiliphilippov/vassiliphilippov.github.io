forebrain

noun
anterior part of the brain
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌbreɪn / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌbreɪn / (BrE)
the anterior part of the brain, developing from the front part of the neural tube, and responsible for higher brain functions

Examples

human forebrain
During embryonic development, the forebrain forms from the anterior part of the neural tube.
Many higher cognitive functions are processed in the forebrain.
The damage to his forebrain affected his memory and reasoning abilities.
