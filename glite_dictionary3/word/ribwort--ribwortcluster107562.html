ribwort

noun
narrow-leaved plantain species
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈrɪbwəːt / (BrE)
an Old World plantain (Plantago lanceolata) with long, narrow ribbed leaves, widely found in temperate regions

Examples

ribwort plant leaves
Children used to play games using the tall flower stalks of ribwort in the countryside.
Some herbal remedies use ribwort for its soothing properties in teas and poultices.
The field was dotted with ribwort, easily recognized by its slender, ribbed leaves.
