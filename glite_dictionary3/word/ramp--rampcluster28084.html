ramp

noun
North American wild onion
Pronunciation: / ræmp / (AmE), / ræmp / (BrE)
A North American wild onion (Allium tricoccum) with broad leaves, a slender bulb, and a strong garlic-like odor and flavor, often used as a vegetable.

Examples

wild ramps salad
Because of their short season, ramps are highly prized by chefs and home cooks alike.
In spring, foragers search the woods for ramps to add to seasonal dishes.
The restaurant featured a special salad made with fresh ramps and roasted potatoes.
