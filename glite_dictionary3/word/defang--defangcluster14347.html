to defang

verb
remove the fangs from an animal
Pronunciation: / diˈfæŋ / (AmE), / diˈfæŋ / (BrE)
to remove the fangs from an animal, especially a snake or other venomous creature

Examples

to defang a snake
After the procedure, the veterinarian confirmed that the snake had been successfully defanged.
Some zoos defang their snakes to ensure the safety of handlers and visitors.
The wildlife expert carefully defanged the cobra before the demonstration.
