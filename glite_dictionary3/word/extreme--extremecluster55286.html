extreme

adjective
farthest or most distant
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstrim / (AmE), / ɪkˈstriːm / (BrE)
farthest or most distant from the center or from a given point; at the outermost limit.

Examples

extreme edge
She placed the fence at the extreme boundary of her property.
The marathon ended at the extreme southern tip of the city.
They built a house on the extreme edge of the forest, far from neighbors.

Related Terms

  • antonym – central: situated at the center
  • synonym – outermost: farthest from the center or a point
