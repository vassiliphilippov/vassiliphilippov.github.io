continental

adjective
relating to a continent
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntəl /; / ˌkɑːn.tənˈen.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˌkɒntɪˈnɛntəl /; / ˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.təl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a continent, especially the mainland part as distinct from surrounding islands or regions
