equal

noun
person of the same rank or status
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkwəl / (AmE), / ˈiːkwəl / (BrE)
a person or thing considered to be the same in status, rights, or abilities as another

Examples

an equal in ability
Among her equals, she was known for her fairness and honesty.
He refused to acknowledge anyone as his equal in the competition.
She always treated her employees as equals, regardless of their positions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inferior: a person of lower rank or status
  • synonym – peer: a person who is equal to another in abilities, qualifications, age, background, and social status
  • antonym – superior: a person of higher rank or status
