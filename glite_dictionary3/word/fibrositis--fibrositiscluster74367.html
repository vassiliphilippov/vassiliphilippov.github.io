fibrositis

noun
inflammation of fibrous tissue, esp. in muscles
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbrəˈsaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəʊˈsaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of white fibrous tissue, especially muscle sheaths, often causing pain and stiffness
