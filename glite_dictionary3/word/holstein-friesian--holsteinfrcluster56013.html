Holstein-Friesian

noun
breed of dairy cattle from Holland
Pronunciation: / hoʊlˌstaɪn friːˈziːən / (AmE), / hɒlˌstaɪn friːˈziːən / (BrE)
a breed of large dairy cattle, originally from northern Holland, distinguished by black-and-white markings and known for high milk production

Examples

Holstein-Friesian dairy cow
A prize-winning Holstein-Friesian was exhibited at the county agricultural show this year.
Many modern dairy farms prefer Holstein-Friesian cows for their efficient production and adaptability.
The Holstein-Friesian is renowned for its impressive milk yield in commercial dairy farming.
