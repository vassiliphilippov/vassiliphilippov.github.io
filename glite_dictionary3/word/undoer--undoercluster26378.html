undoer

noun
person who undoes or opens things
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈduːər / (AmE), / ʌnˈduːə / (BrE)
a person who undoes, unfastens, unwraps, or opens something

Examples

undoer of knots

Children can be surprisingly talented undoers of shoe laces and ribbons.
The magician was a skilled undoer of knots and locks.
