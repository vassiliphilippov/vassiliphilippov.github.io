crushing

adjective
devastating; overwhelmingly destructive
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Causing overwhelming distress or destruction; devastating in effect, often used of emotional or physical blows.

Examples

crushing defeat
He received the crushing news of the company's bankruptcy yesterday.
Her confidence was dealt a crushing blow when she was not selected for the role.
The team suffered a crushing defeat in the final minutes of the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym – devastating: causing severe shock, distress, or destruction
  • antonym – mild: not severe or strong; gentle or moderate
