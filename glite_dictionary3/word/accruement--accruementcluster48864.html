accruement

noun
the act of accumulating or being added
Pronunciation: / əˈkruːmənt / (AmE), / əˈkruːmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of accumulating or being added to over time, especially in financial contexts

Examples

accruement of interest
Regular savings led to the gradual accruement of a sizeable nest egg.
The accruement of interest on the account was calculated monthly.
The company monitored the accruement of benefits over the five-year period.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accrual: the process of accumulating or the accumulated amount
