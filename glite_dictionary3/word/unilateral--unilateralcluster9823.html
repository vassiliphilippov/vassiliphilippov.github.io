unilateral

adjective
involving one side or party
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪˈlætərəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈlætərəl / (BrE)
involving, occurring on, or affecting only one side, part, or party

Examples

unilateral decision
A unilateral ceasefire was declared by one side of the conflict.
The company made a unilateral decision without consulting the other stakeholders.
The patient suffered from unilateral hearing loss in his right ear.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bilateral: involving two sides or parties
