to open

verb
unfold or spread out
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.pən /; / ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pən /; / ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
to move from a closed to an open state; to make something not closed, covered, or blocked

The balcony doors open into the living room, letting in plenty of light.
