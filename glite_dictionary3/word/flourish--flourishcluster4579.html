flourish

noun
lively brass fanfare or musical passage
Pronunciation: / ˈflɝːɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈflʌrɪʃ / (BrE)
a short, lively musical passage played on brass instruments, especially as a ceremonial or dramatic announcement

Examples

trumpet flourish
As the curtain rose, a bright musical flourish echoed through the theater.
The ceremony began with a flourish that captured everyone's attention.
The king's arrival was announced with a flourish of trumpets.
