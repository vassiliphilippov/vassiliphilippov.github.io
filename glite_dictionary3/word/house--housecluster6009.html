house

noun
gambling establishment management
Pronunciation: / haʊs / (AmE), / haʊs / (BrE)
the organization or management running a gambling establishment, such as a casino, which supervises games and usually takes a share of gamblers' wagers

Examples

the house wins bets
It's important to remember that the house takes a percentage of every bet placed.
The house always has an advantage in every game offered to players.
When you win a big jackpot, the house pays your prize according to the rules.
