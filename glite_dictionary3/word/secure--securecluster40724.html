to secure

verb
fasten or fix firmly
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
to fasten or fix firmly so that something cannot move or be taken away

Examples

to secure the door
Please secure all windows before leaving the house.
She secured the necklace clasp behind her neck.
The cargo was secured with strong ropes to prevent it from shifting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fasten: to attach firmly or join securely
  • antonym – loosen: to make less tight or fixed
