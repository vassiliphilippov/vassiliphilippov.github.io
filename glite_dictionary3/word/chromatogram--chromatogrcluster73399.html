chromatogram

noun
record showing separation in chromatography
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈmætəˌɡræm / (AmE), / krəˈmatəɡram / (BrE)
a visual record or output showing the separation of components in a mixture as detected by chromatography

Examples

chromatogram shows results
A peak appeared on the chromatogram at a retention time of ten minutes.
High-performance liquid chromatography produces a detailed chromatogram with clearly separated peaks.
The scientist analyzed the chromatogram to identify the different substances present in the sample.
