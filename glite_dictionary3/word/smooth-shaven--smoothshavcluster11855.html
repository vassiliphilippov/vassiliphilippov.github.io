smooth-shaven

adjective
closely shaved, smooth-faced
Pronunciation: / ˌsmuðˈʃeɪvən / (AmE), / ˌsmuːðˈʃeɪvən / (BrE)
(Of a man's face) having been very closely shaved so that the skin feels smooth.

Examples

After years of wearing a beard, he decided to go smooth-shaven for the new job.
