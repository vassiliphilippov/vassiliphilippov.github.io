to relay

verb
control by means of a relay
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌleɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪˌleɪ / (BrE)
to control or operate (a device or signal) by means of a relay, especially an electrical one

Examples

to relay a signal
A backup system is relaying signals in case of main system failure.
Modern traffic lights are often relayed to control the flow of vehicles.
The engineer designed the circuit to relay the power automatically.
