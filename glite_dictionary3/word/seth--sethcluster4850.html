Seth

proper noun
third son of Adam and Eve in the Bible
Pronunciation: / sɛθ / (AmE), / sɛθ / (BrE)
(in the Bible) the third son of Adam and Eve, given by God in place of Abel.

Examples

Bible story of Seth
According to Genesis, Seth was born after the death of Abel.
Genealogies in the Bible often trace human ancestry through Seth.
Many biblical scholars study the descendants of Seth to understand early human history.
