appendectomy

noun
surgical removal of the appendix
Pronunciation: / əˌpɛnˈdɛktəmi / (AmE), / əˌpɛnˈdɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove the vermiform appendix, usually as a treatment for appendicitis

Examples

appendectomy surgery
Doctors performed an emergency appendectomy after she developed acute appendicitis.
He recovered quickly following his appendectomy and returned to work within a week.
The surgeon explained the risks and benefits of an appendectomy before the procedure.
