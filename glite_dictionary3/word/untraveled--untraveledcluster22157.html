untraveled

adjective
not traveled over; not visited
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtrævəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtrævəld / (BrE)
(of a road, path, or area) not having been traveled over or through; not visited or explored.

Examples

untraveled road
Many explorers are drawn to the mysteries of untraveled areas.
The book describes adventures on untraveled roads far from civilization.
We decided to hike along the untraveled trail through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym – untrodden: not having been walked on or trampled
