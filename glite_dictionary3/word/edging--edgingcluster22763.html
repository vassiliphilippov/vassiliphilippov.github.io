edging

noun
decorative border or trim along an edge
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
a border or trim made by placing material along the edge of something, such as a fringe on clothing or a rug, to provide a finished look or decorative effect

Examples

edging on fabric
Mary sewed a colorful edging onto the pillow covers to match her curtains.
The rug’s silk edging was beginning to fray after years of use.
The tablecloth had a beautiful lace edging that made it look elegant.

  • synonym – fringe: a decorative border of hanging threads, cords, or strips
