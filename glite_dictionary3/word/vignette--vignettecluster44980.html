vignette

noun
photo with fading edges
Pronunciation: / vɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / vɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
a photograph whose edges fade out gradually to the background instead of forming a clear border

Examples

vignette photo effect
I applied a black and white vignette to my portrait, making the center stand out.
Professional photographers sometimes use a vignette to give images a nostalgic or classic look.
The old wedding vignette on the mantelpiece has soft, faded edges that add to its charm.
