Dona

noun
Spanish courtesy title for women
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊ.nə / (AmE), / ˈdəʊ.nə / (BrE)
a Spanish courtesy title used before the first name of a woman, equivalent to 'Mrs.' or 'Lady'

Examples

History books often mention Dona Ana de Mendoza.
