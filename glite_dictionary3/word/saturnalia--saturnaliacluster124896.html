Saturnalia

noun
orgiastic festival in ancient Rome
Pronunciation: / ˌsætəˈneɪliə / (AmE), / ˌsatəˈneɪlɪə / (BrE)
An ancient Roman festival held in honor of the god Saturn, characterized by feasting, revelry, and a temporary overturning of social norms.
