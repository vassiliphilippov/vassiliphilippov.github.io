undercut

noun
a cut made underneath to remove material
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌkʌt /; / ˈʌndɚˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkʌt / (BrE)
a cut made underneath an object or surface to remove material, or the material removed by such a cut (especially in carpentry, mining, or metalworking)
