thrashing

noun
a crushing defeat
Pronunciation: / ˈθræʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθræʃɪŋ / (BrE)
an overwhelming or decisive defeat, especially in a competition or contest

Examples

The team suffered a humiliating thrashing in the final match of the season.
Their rivals gave them a real thrashing on the basketball court last night.
