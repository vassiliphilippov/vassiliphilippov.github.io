intifadah

noun
Palestinian uprising against Israeli control
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɪˈfɑːdə / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪˈfɑːdə / (BrE)
An uprising by Palestinian Arabs in the Gaza Strip and the West Bank against Israeli control, especially those occurring in the late 1980s and again in 2000.

Examples

the intifadah uprising
Discussions about peace are often framed in the context of the intifadah movements.
Many international observers visited the West Bank during the second intifadah.
The first intifadah began in 1987 and had a major impact on regional politics.
