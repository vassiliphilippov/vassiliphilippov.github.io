foehn

noun
warm, dry Alpine wind
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪˌɒn / (AmE), / ˈfəːn / (BrE)
A warm, dry wind that blows down the northern slopes of the Alps, often causing a rapid rise in temperature and decrease in humidity.

Examples

strong foehn wind
Many locals attribute sudden changes in mood or health to the arrival of the foehn.
The foehn wind swept through the valley, melting the snow in just a few hours.
Yesterday’s foehn brought unusually high temperatures for this time of year.
