lambert

noun
unit of luminance (cgs system)
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmbɚt / (AmE), / ˈlæmbət / (BrE)
a unit of luminance in the centimeter-gram-second system, equal to the brightness of a perfectly diffusing surface emitting or reflecting one lumen per square centimeter

Examples

screen brightness in lamberts
High-quality displays are often measured in lamberts to assess their brightness.
The device registered a luminance of five lamberts on the screen.
The engineer converted the brightness from lamberts to candelas per square meter.
