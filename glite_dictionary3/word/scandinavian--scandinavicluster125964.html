Scandinavian

noun
northern Germanic language group
Pronunciation: / ˌskændəˈneɪviən / (AmE), / ˌskændɪˈneɪviən / (BrE)
the group of northern Germanic languages spoken in Scandinavia and Iceland, including Danish, Norwegian, Swedish, Icelandic, and Faroese
