pseudonym

noun
fictitious name used as an alias
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːdəˌnɪm / (AmE), / ˈsjuːdənɪm / (BrE)
A fictitious name, especially one used by an author or performer instead of their real name.

Examples

pseudonym for an author
His real identity was hidden behind the pseudonym he used in all his works.
Many authors choose to publish under a pseudonym to maintain their privacy.
The singer adopted a pseudonym early in her career.

Related Terms

  • synonym – alias: a false or assumed identity
  • antonym – real name: a person's true or legal name
