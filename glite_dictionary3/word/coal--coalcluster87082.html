coal

noun
a glowing piece from a fire
Pronunciation: / koʊl / (AmE), / kəʊl / (BrE)
a piece of wood or coal left from a fire that glows or smolders with heat

Examples

hot coal from fire
A spark from a burning coal set the old newspaper alight.
He picked up a hot coal with a pair of tongs to light his pipe.
The campfire had died down, leaving only a single glowing coal in the ashes.
