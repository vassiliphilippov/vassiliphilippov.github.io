to stay

verb
remain temporarily in a place
Pronunciation: / steɪ / (AmE), / steɪ / (BrE)
to remain in a place, state, or situation and not leave or change, including temporarily living or visiting somewhere, or persevering during difficulty

Examples

to stay in a hotel

The painting stayed untouched for decades.
The weather will stay cold throughout the week.
