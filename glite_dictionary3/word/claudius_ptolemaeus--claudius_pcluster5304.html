Claudius Ptolemaeus

proper noun
2nd-century Alexandrian astronomer and mathematician
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔːdiəs toʊˌlɛmˈiːəs / (AmE), / ˈklɔːdɪəs təʊlɪˈmeɪəs / (BrE)
An Alexandrian astronomer and mathematician of the 2nd century who proposed the geocentric model of the universe, influential until the Renaissance.
