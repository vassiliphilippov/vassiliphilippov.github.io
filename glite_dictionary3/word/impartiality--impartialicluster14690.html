impartiality

noun
fairness; lack of bias
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpɑːrʃiˈæləti / (AmE), / ɪmˌpɑːʃɪˈælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of treating all parties or sides equally and without bias

Examples

impartiality in decisions
A judge must demonstrate complete impartiality when hearing a case.
Her impartiality was praised by both parties involved in the dispute.
Journalistic impartiality is essential for maintaining public trust.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bias: a tendency to favor one side over another, often unfairly
  • synonym – fairness: the quality of making judgments that are free from discrimination
