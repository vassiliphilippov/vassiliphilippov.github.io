PR

noun
promotion to create goodwill; public relations
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːˈɑːr / (AmE), / ˌpiːˈɑː / (BrE)
the activity or process of promoting a person, organization, or product to create goodwill and manage public perception; public relations

Examples

PR campaign launch
After the scandal, they launched a PR campaign to regain customer trust.
Good PR can make a significant difference to a brand's reputation.
The company hired a new PR firm to improve its public image.

Related Terms

  • synonym – public relations: the practice of managing the spread of information between an individual or organization and the public
