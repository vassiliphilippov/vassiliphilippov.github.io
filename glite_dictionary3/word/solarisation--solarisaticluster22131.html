solarisation

noun
adoption of solar power
Pronunciation: / ccso8al59r59c8ze6a8359n / (AmE), / ccs598al59ra6ac8ze6a8359n / (BrE)
the process of adopting or integrating solar power into a system, infrastructure, or community, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

Examples

solarisation of energy systems
The island's electricity grid is undergoing solarisation, replacing diesel generators with solar-powered microgrids.
The city's ambitious solarisation plan aims to supply public buildings with rooftop panels within five years.
Small farmers welcomed the solarisation of irrigation pumps, which cut fuel costs and reduced outages.

Related Terms

  • synonym – renewable energy adoption: the broader process of switching from fossil fuels to renewable energy sources, including solar power.
  • synonym – solarization: Alternative spelling often used in American English; the same meaning of adopting solar power.
