landing

noun
structure for boarding or unloading from boats
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ / (BrE)
a platform or structure at the edge of water where boats can load or unload people or goods

Examples

boat landing
Fresh supplies were carried from the ship to the shore via the landing.
The boat docked at the landing to let passengers off.
We waited by the landing until the ferry arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dock: a structure where vessels may be moored, loaded, or unloaded
  • synonym – pier: a platform extending from the shore into the water, used as a landing place for boats
