firewall

noun
network security system in computing
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪrˌwɑl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌwɔːl / (BrE)
(computing) a security system, often comprised of hardware and software, that restricts unauthorized access to or from a private computer network.

Examples

firewall protects network
A strong firewall is essential to protect company data from cyberattacks.
Make sure your firewall is always active when connecting to public Wi-Fi networks.
The IT department updated the firewall to block new types of malware.
