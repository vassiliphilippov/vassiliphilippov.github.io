Ostrogoth

noun
member of the eastern Goths; historical group
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːstrəˌɡɑθ / (AmE), / ˈɒstrəˌɡɒθ / (BrE)
a member of the eastern branch of the Goths, a Germanic people who established a kingdom in northern Italy around 500 AD
