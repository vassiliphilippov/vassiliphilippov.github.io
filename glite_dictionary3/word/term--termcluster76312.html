term

noun
end of pregnancy; due birth period
Pronunciation: / tɝːm / (AmE), / tɜːm / (BrE)
the end of the normal period of pregnancy, when a baby is due to be born

Examples

at term birth
Babies born at term generally have better health outcomes than those born prematurely.
She carried all three of her children to term without any complications.
The doctor explained that the pregnancy had reached term and labor could begin any day.
