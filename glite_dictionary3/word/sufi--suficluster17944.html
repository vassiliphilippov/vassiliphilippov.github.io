Sufi

noun
Muslim follower of Islamic mysticism (Sufism)
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːfi / (AmE), / ˈsuːfi / (BrE)
A Muslim who follows Sufism, the mystical tradition within Islam focused on seeking direct experience of God.
