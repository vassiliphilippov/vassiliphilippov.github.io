ayah

noun
Indian nursemaid or nanny
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.ə / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.ə / (BrE)
(in India) a woman employed as a nursemaid or nanny, especially one of South Asian heritage attending children in a European household.

Examples

ayah cares for children
Her ayah taught her to speak Hindi as well as English.
Stories of loyal ayahs are often found in colonial literature.
The British family hired an ayah to help care for their young children during their stay in India.
