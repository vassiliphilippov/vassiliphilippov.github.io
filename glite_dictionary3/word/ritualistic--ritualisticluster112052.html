ritualistic

adjective
relating to rituals or ceremonies
Pronunciation: / ˌrɪtʃuəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌrɪtʃuəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the practice or observance of rituals or ceremonies, often in a formal or prescribed manner.

Examples

ritualistic ceremony
He found comfort in the ritualistic repetition of his morning routine.
The organization has a ritualistic process for welcoming new members.
The tribe performed a ritualistic dance each year to celebrate the harvest.

Related Terms

  • antonym – spontaneous: occurring naturally without planning or ritual
