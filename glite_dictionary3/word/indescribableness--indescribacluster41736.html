indescribableness

noun
state of being impossible to describe
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being impossible or extremely difficult to describe in words.

The sheer indescribableness of the scene left everyone in awe.
