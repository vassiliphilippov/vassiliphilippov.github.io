quipu

noun
ancient Inca record-keeping device
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːpuː / (AmE), / ˈkiːpuː / (BrE)
An ancient Inca device for recording information, consisting of a main cord with variously colored knotted strings attached, used for keeping records and performing calculations.
