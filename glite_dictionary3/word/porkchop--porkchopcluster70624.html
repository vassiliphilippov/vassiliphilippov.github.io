porkchop

noun
chop of pork with bone
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrkˌtʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈpɔːkˌtʃɒp / (BrE)
A thick slice of pork, usually including a rib or part of a vertebra, cut from a pig and cooked as food.
