workbag

noun
bag for holding work materials, esp. sewing
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a bag for carrying implements and materials needed for work, especially sewing tools and supplies.

Examples

workbag with sewing tools
Every evening, she kept her embroidery projects neatly tucked away in a floral workbag.
She reached into her workbag for a needle and thread to repair the tear in her shirt.
The old wooden workbag belonged to her grandmother and was filled with vintage sewing tools.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sewing kit: a set of tools and supplies for sewing, often in a container
