stickweed

noun
plant with seeds that cling to fabric or fur
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈstɪk.wiːd / (BrE)
any of various herbaceous plants whose seeds attach themselves to clothing or animal fur by means of hooks or bristles

Examples

stickweed seeds on clothes
After walking through the field, I found several stickweed seeds stuck to my jeans.
Gardeners often remove stickweed to prevent its seeds from spreading everywhere.
The dog's fur was covered in tiny green stickweed burs after its walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym – burdock: a plant whose burs cling to fur and clothing
