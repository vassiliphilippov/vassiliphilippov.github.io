chrysalis

noun
pupa stage of moth or butterfly
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs.ə.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.ə.lɪs / (BrE)
the pupa stage of a moth or butterfly, enclosed in a hardened outer casing, during which it undergoes transformation from larva to adult

Examples

butterfly chrysalis
During the chrysalis stage, the butterfly undergoes a remarkable transformation inside the casing.
The caterpillar spun a silk cocoon and soon became a chrysalis hanging from the branch.
We watched as the chrysalis slowly darkened in color, signifying the butterfly was nearly ready to emerge.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pupa: an insect in the inactive immature stage between larva and adult
