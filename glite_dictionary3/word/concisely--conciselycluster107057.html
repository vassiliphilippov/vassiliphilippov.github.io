concisely

adverb
in a brief and clear manner
Pronunciation: / kənˈsaɪsli / (AmE), / kənˈsaɪsli / (BrE)
In a way that is clear and brief, using only a few words; without unnecessary detail.

Examples

to explain concisely
Please answer each question concisely to save time during the interview.
She explained the instructions concisely, making sure everyone understood the process quickly.
The report summarized the year’s events concisely and avoided any unnecessary information.

Related Terms

  • synonym – briefly: using only a few words; for a short time
  • antonym – elaborately: in a detailed and complicated way
