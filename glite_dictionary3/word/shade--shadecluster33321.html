shade

noun
a haunting mental image or memory
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪd / (AmE), / ʃeɪd / (BrE)
a memory or impression of something from the past that lingers and haunts the mind, often causing unease

Examples

a shade from the past
As he walked through the empty house, a shade from his childhood memories crept into his mind.
Even after so many years, the shade of that terrible night lingered in his thoughts.
She tried to forget, but the shade of her past failure haunted every decision she made.
