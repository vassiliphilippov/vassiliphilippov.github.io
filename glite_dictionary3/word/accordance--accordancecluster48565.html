accordance

noun
granting of rights or privileges
Pronunciation: / əˈkɔrdəns / (AmE), / əˈkɔːdəns / (BrE)
the act of granting or bestowing rights or privileges to someone, especially in a formal or legal context

Examples

accordance with the law
Citizenship was granted in accordance with the new laws.
He protested the accordance of special privileges to certain groups.
The accordance of voting rights marked a significant milestone in the country's development.

Related Terms

  • synonym – grant: to give or bestow something formally, especially rights or privileges
