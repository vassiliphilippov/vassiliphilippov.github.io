perseverance

noun
persistent determination
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsəˈvɪrəns /; / ˌpɜːrsəˈvɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsɪˈvɪərəns / (BrE)
the quality of continuing to try or remain determined, even when things are difficult or take a long time
