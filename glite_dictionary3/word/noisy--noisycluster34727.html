noisy

adjective
full of loud or unpleasant sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɪzi / (AmE), / ˈnɔɪzi / (BrE)
full of or characterized by loud or unpleasant sounds; making a lot of noise.

Examples

noisy party
Our neighbors have a noisy party every weekend.
She prefers a quiet workspace to a noisy office.
The noisy construction outside made it hard to concentrate.
