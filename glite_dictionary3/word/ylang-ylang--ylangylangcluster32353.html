ylang-ylang

noun
tropical tree with aromatic flowers
Pronunciation: / iˈlæŋ iˈlæŋ / (AmE), / ɪˌlæŋ ˈilæŋ / (BrE)
An evergreen tropical Asian tree with fragrant greenish-yellow flowers that yield an aromatic oil, commonly used in perfumes and as an ornamental plant.

Examples

ylang-ylang tree flowers
Gardeners in tropical regions often plant ylang-ylang as a decorative tree.
The oil extracted from ylang-ylang flowers is highly valued in aromatherapy.
The perfume had a sweet, exotic scent thanks to the addition of ylang-ylang oil.
