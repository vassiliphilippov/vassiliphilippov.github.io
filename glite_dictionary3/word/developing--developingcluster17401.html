developing

noun
processing film to make images visible
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of treating photosensitive material, such as photographic film, with chemicals to make an image visible

Examples

developing film
Advances in technology have made developing photographs much faster and more convenient.
Proper developing is crucial to achieving high-quality photographic prints.
The photographer spent hours in the darkroom developing his rolls of film.
