enrollment

noun
the act of enrolling
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈroʊlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈrəʊlmənt / (BrE)
the act of officially joining or registering for a course, school, or organization

Examples

enrollment in a course
Enrollment in the university has increased dramatically this year.
Online enrollment allows students to sign up for classes from home.
The school requires proof of residency at the time of enrollment.
