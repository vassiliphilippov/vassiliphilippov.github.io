castrato

noun
male singer castrated to keep high voice
Pronunciation: / kæsˈtrɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / kæsˈtrɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
a male singer who was castrated before puberty to preserve a high vocal range, typically soprano or alto
