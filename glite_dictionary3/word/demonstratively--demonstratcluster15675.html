demonstratively

adverb
in an expressive, emotional manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑnstrətɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒnstrətɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that clearly shows feelings or emotions, often openly and with expressive gestures.

Examples

speak demonstratively with gestures
He spoke demonstratively about his achievements, using grand gestures.
She hugged her friend demonstratively in front of everyone at the airport.
The actor bowed demonstratively after receiving a standing ovation.
