keen

adjective
intense; sharp (of feelings, sensations)
Pronunciation: / kin / (AmE), / kiːn / (BrE)
intense or sharply felt (of feelings, sensations, pain, or wind)

Examples

The knife gave him a keen pain in his finger as he cut himself.

She shivered as a keen chill swept through the old house.
The keen wind made it painful to walk along the shore.
