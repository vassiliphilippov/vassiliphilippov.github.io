weft

noun
yarn woven across the warp in weaving
Pronunciation: / wɛft / (AmE), / wɛft / (BrE)
The yarn that is woven across the warp threads in weaving to form fabric.

Examples

weft threads in fabric
Each row of weft gives the fabric its unique design and texture.
She carefully guided the weft over and under the warp threads to make the scarf.
The colorful pattern was created by alternating threads in the weft.
