harpoon

noun
barbed spear used for hunting large fish or whales
Pronunciation: / hɑrˈpun / (AmE), / hɑːˈpuːn / (BrE)
a long spear with a barbed, pointed end that is thrown or fired at large fish or whales to catch them, usually with a rope attached

Examples

harpoon a whale
After throwing the harpoon, he quickly pulled on the rope to secure the fish.
Modern harpoons are sometimes launched from cannons instead of being thrown by hand.
The crew used a harpoon to catch the giant whale in the icy waters.

Related Terms

  • synonym – spear: a weapon with a pointed tip, used for thrusting; general term for pole weapons
