to Europeanise

verb
bring under European institution influence
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊˈpiːəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊˈpiːənaɪz / (BrE)
to make something more European in character, customs, ideas, or by bringing it under the influence or policies of European institutions (especially the European Union)
