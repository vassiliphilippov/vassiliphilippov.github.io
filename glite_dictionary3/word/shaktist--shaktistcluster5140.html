Shaktist

noun
worshipper of Shakti in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːktɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃaktɪst / (BrE)
A follower or worshipper of Shakti, the supreme goddess in Shaktism, a major tradition within Hinduism.

Examples

devout Shaktist worshipper
As a devoted Shaktist, she participated in rituals honoring the goddess Durga.
He studied the scriptures important to Shaktist philosophy and worship.
The temple welcomes both traditional Hindus and Shaktists alike for the annual festival.

Related Terms
