plectrum

noun
device for plucking instrument strings
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛk.trəm / (AmE), / ˈplɛk.trəm / (BrE)
A small thin piece, usually of plastic, metal, or other material, used to pluck or strum the strings of a musical instrument such as a guitar.
