young

adjective
inexperienced; not tried or tested
Pronunciation: / jʌŋ / (AmE), / jʌŋ / (BrE)
(of a person) lacking experience or skill in a particular activity or field.

She was still young in the profession, but eager to learn.
The young assistant made a few mistakes during his first week.
