to ruggedize

verb
make durable for tough conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌɡ.ɪ.daɪz / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡ.ɪ.daɪz / (BrE)
to make a product or device robust and able to withstand rough, harsh, or demanding conditions

Examples

to ruggedize a device
Many smartphones have been ruggedized to survive drops and extreme weather.
The manufacturer decided to ruggedize the laptop for use by military personnel.
They plan to ruggedize the cameras so they can be used in industrial settings.
