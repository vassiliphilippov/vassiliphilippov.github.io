transference

noun
the act of transferring something
Pronunciation: / trænˈsfɜr.əns /; / trænsˈfɝː.əns / (AmE), / trɑːnsˈfɜː.rəns /; / trænsˈfɜː.rəns / (BrE)
the act of moving or transferring something (such as data, rights, or ownership) from one place, form, or person to another
