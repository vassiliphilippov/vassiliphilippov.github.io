seal

noun
fastener for tight closure
Pronunciation: / siːl / (AmE), / siːl / (BrE)
a device, material, or layer used to close something tightly or to protect a surface from air, liquid, or moisture

Examples

airtight seal

The airtight seal keeps food fresh for longer.
The engineer checked the seal on the pipe to ensure there were no leaks.

Before installing the tiles, a seal should be applied to the surface.
The painter recommended using a waterproof seal on the brick walls.
