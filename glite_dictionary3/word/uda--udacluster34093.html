UDA

noun
Ulster Protestant paramilitary group in Northern Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː diː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌjuː diː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Ulster Defence Association, a Protestant paramilitary group in Northern Ireland active mainly during the Troubles and associated with acts of violence and criminal activity

Examples

the UDA group
Several members of the UDA were arrested during a raid on illegal arms caches.
The UDA was implicated in a number of attacks during the Troubles in Northern Ireland.
