robotic

adjective
relating to robots; mechanical or automated
Pronunciation: / roʊˈbɑtɪk / (AmE), / rəʊˈbɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a robot, especially in being mechanical or automated.

Examples

robotic arm movement
Her movements were stiff and robotic, as if she were performing a rehearsed routine.
Many households now use robotic vacuum cleaners to keep their floors clean.
The factory relies on robotic arms to assemble cars quickly and accurately.

Related Terms

  • synonym – automated: operated automatically, especially by machines or computers
  • synonym – mechanical: operated by or relating to a machine or machinery
