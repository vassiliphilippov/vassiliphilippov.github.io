aesthetician

noun
person trained in beauty treatments
Pronunciation: / ˌɛsˌθɛˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌiːsˌθɛˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a person trained to give beauty treatments such as facials, manicures, and skin care.

Examples

aesthetician gives facials
After her facial, she thanked the aesthetician for making her skin look radiant.
She decided to become an aesthetician after developing an interest in beauty treatments.
The aesthetician recommended a new skincare routine during my visit to the spa.
