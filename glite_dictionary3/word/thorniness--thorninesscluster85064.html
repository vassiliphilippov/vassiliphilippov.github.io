thorniness

noun
the state of being covered in thorns
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔrni.nəs / (AmE), / ˈθɔː.ni.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being covered with sharp thorns or spines

Examples

thorniness of the bush
The thorniness of the bushes made it difficult to pick the berries without gloves.
The thorniness of the plant protects it from most herbivores.
We underestimated the thorniness of the path and got several scratches along the way.
