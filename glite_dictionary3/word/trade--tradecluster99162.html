trade

noun
commercial exchange of goods and services
Pronunciation: / treɪd / (AmE), / treɪd / (BrE)
the business or activity of buying and selling goods and services, especially between countries or companies; the amount of business a shop or company does

Examples

international trade
Restrictions on trade can affect the global economy significantly.
The company's success is largely due to its strong focus on international trade.
Venice was once a major hub of trade between Europe and Asia.
Bad weather kept much of the usual trade away from the market.
Holiday sales brought extra trade to local stores.
The café relies on lunchtime trade from nearby offices.
