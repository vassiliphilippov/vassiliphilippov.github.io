stiff

adjective
rigidly formal in manner or style
Pronunciation: / stɪf / (AmE), / stɪf / (BrE)
(of manner, tone, or style) rigidly formal or lacking in natural ease or warmth.

Examples

stiff formal style
His speech came across as stiff and rehearsed rather than genuine.
I found the writing style of the report a bit too stiff for casual readers.
The atmosphere at the dinner party was uncomfortably stiff.
