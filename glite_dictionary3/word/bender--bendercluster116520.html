bender

noun
extended drinking spree or party
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛn.də / (BrE)
an extended period of heavy drinking or lively partying, often involving excessive alcohol consumption

Examples

go on a bender
After losing his job, he went on a bender that lasted the entire weekend.
She regretted the bender she had with her friends after waking up with a headache.
Their wild bender made for some unbelievable stories the next day.
