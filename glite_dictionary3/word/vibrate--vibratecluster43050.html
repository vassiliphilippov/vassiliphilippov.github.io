to vibrate

verb
move back and forth rapidly
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪˌbreɪt / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.breɪt / (BrE)
to move or swing back and forth or from side to side in a regular, rapid motion

Examples

vibrate the phone
If you pluck the guitar string, you can see it vibrate from side to side.
The bridge began to vibrate slightly as the heavy truck drove across it.
When the phone is on silent, it will vibrate to alert you of new messages.

Related Terms

  • synonym – oscillate: to move or swing back and forth at a regular speed
  • synonym – quiver: to shake with a slight, rapid motion
