cordless

adjective
without a cord; battery-powered
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːd.ləs / (BrE)
(of an electrical device) operating without a cord attached, usually powered by a battery.

Examples

cordless phone
He replaced his old drill with a modern cordless model for convenience.
She prefers a cordless vacuum cleaner because it's easier to move around the house.
The new cordless headphones allow you to listen to music anywhere without tangled wires.

Related Terms

  • antonym – corded: (of devices) equipped with a cord
  • synonym – wireless: (of devices) operating without wires or cables
