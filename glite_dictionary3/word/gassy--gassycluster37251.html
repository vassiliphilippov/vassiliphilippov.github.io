gassy

adjective
having excess intestinal gas
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsi / (AmE), / ˈɡasi / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) suffering from or affected by excessive gas in the stomach or intestines.

Examples

gassy stomach pain
After eating beans, she often feels gassy and uncomfortable.
He avoided carbonated drinks because they made him gassy.
The pediatrician suggested remedies for a gassy baby.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flatulent: suffering from or marked by an accumulation of gas in the alimentary canal
