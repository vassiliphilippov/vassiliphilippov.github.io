thrombolysis

noun
medical breakdown of blood clots
Pronunciation: / θrɑːmˈbɑːləsɪs / (AmE), / θrɒmˈbɒlɪsɪs / (BrE)
the dissolution or breaking down of blood clots, especially by medical treatment

Examples

thrombolysis treatment for clots
