os hyoideum

noun
U-shaped bone at base of tongue
Pronunciation: / ɑs haɪˈoʊɪdiəm / (AmE), / ɒs hʌɪˈəʊɪdɪəm / (BrE)
the U-shaped bone at the base of the tongue that supports the tongue and its muscles
