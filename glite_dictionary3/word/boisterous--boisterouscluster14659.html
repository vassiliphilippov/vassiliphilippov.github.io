boisterous

adjective
noisy and energetic
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ.stɚ.əs /; / ˈbɔɪstərəs / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.stər.əs /; / ˈbɔɪstərəs / (BrE)
loud, energetic, and lively in a way that is often noisy or lacking restraint (used for people, crowds, or behavior)

Examples

boisterous crowd
Her boisterous laughter could be heard throughout the house.
The boisterous crowd cheered loudly as the team scored the winning goal.
The party became more boisterous as the night went on.
A boisterous crowd filled the stadium, cheering loudly for their team.
His boisterous laughter could be heard from across the street.
The children grew increasingly boisterous as the birthday party went on.

  • synonym – rambunctious: boisterous and difficult to control

  • synonym – unruly: lacking discipline or restraint
