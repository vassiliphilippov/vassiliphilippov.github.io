shambling

noun
slow, dragging way of walking
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæmblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃæmblɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of walking with slow, dragging steps without lifting the feet properly.

Examples

slow shambling walk
His shambling was so pronounced that strangers often offered him a seat.
