Vichy

proper noun
collaborationist French regime (1940–44)
Pronunciation: / ˈviːʃi / (AmE), / ˈviːʃi / (BrE)
The regime of France that collaborated with Nazi Germany during the Second World War, based in the city of Vichy from 1940 to 1944.
