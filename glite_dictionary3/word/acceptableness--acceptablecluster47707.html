acceptableness

noun
the quality of being acceptable
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛptəblnəs / (AmE), / əkˈsɛptəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being acceptable; satisfactoriness according to approved standards.

Examples

acceptableness of behavior
Some questioned the acceptableness of the comedian's jokes in such a formal setting.
The acceptableness of his proposal was discussed at length during the meeting.
