obtusely

adverb
in a stupid or insensitive way
Pronunciation: / əbˈtusli / (AmE), / əbˈtjuːsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is slow to understand, insensitive, or lacking sharpness of intellect; stupidly or insensitively.

Examples

obtusely ignore advice
He obtusely misunderstood the instructions, causing unnecessary confusion.
She obtusely ignored the obvious hints that something was wrong.
The manager responded obtusely to the team's concerns, missing the real issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bluntly: in a direct and uncompromising way, sometimes insensitively
  • antonym – sensitively: in a manner showing awareness and consideration for others' feelings
