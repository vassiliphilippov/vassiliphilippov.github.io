mingy

adjective
unwilling to give or share; stingy
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪndʒi / (AmE), / ˈmɪndʒi / (BrE)
(informal) unwilling to give or share; mean or stingy, especially with money or resources.

Examples

mingy with money
Don't be so mingy with the snacks—there's plenty to go around.
Her mingy attitude made her unpopular among her colleagues.
That's a mingy tip for such excellent service.

Related Terms

  • antonym – generous: willing to give more of something than is strictly necessary or expected
  • synonym – mean: unwilling to give or share; not generous
  • synonym – stingy: unwilling to spend or give; reluctant to part with resources
