twin

adjective
being two identical or matching
Pronunciation: / twɪn / (AmE), / twɪn / (BrE)
(of two things) exactly alike or forming a pair; matching in appearance, size, or function.

Examples

twin beds
She bought a pair of twin lamps for her bedside tables.
The house is designed with twin bedrooms on either side of the hallway.
They installed twin turbochargers to boost the car's performance.
