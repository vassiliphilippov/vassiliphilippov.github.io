chestnut

noun
wood from chestnut trees
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛs.nət / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
the wood obtained from any of various chestnut trees of the genus Castanea, valued for its durability and appearance

Examples

chestnut wood
Carpenters often use chestnut for its resistance to decay.
