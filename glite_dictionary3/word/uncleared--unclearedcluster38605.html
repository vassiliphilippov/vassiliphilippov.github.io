uncleared

adjective
not cleared of obstructions
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklɪrd / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪəd / (BrE)
not cleared of objects, obstructions, or unwanted material; remaining covered or blocked

Examples

uncleared land
Much of the forest is still uncleared, providing a habitat for wildlife.
Several vehicles became stuck on the uncleared roads during the blizzard.
The hiking trail remained uncleared after the storm, making it difficult to walk through.
