guenon

noun
small African monkey (Cercopithecus)
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈnoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡweɪnɒn / (BrE)
A small, slender African monkey with long hind limbs and tail, and long hair around the face, belonging to the genus Cercopithecus.

Examples

wild guenon monkey
A young guenon clung tightly to its mother's back as they searched for fruit.
Researchers observed a troop of guenons moving through the dense forest canopy.
The guenon is easily recognized by its distinctive facial hair.
