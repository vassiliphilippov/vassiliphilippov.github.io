disappearance

noun
the act of leaving without explanation
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
the act of leaving secretly or without explanation, so that one's whereabouts become unknown

Examples

mysterious disappearance
Her unexplained disappearance sparked a massive search across the region.
The sudden disappearance of the famous artist left everyone puzzled.
There was great concern after the disappearance of the funds from the bank account.

Related Terms

  • antonym – appearance: the act of becoming visible or present
  • synonym – vanishing: the act of suddenly going out of sight or existence
