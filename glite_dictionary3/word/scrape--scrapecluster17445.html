scrape

noun
harsh noise from scraping
Pronunciation: / skreɪp / (AmE), / skreɪp / (BrE)
a harsh, grating noise made by scraping one object against another

Examples

scrape of metal
She was startled by the harsh scrape of metal against concrete.
The scrape of his chair on the floor echoed through the quiet room.
The scrape of violin bows distracted her during the concert.
