worship

noun
religious devotion or ceremonies
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɜːʃɪp / (BrE)
the activity of showing reverence and adoration for a deity; religious devotion or ceremonies

Examples

worship service music
For many, music is an important part of worship.
Freedom of worship is a fundamental human right.
The church holds regular worship services every Sunday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adoration: deep love and respect, especially for a deity
  • antonym – blasphemy: the act or offense of speaking sacrilegiously about God or sacred things
  • synonym – veneration: great respect or reverence, often in a religious context
