Cepheus

proper noun
constellation in the northern sky
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːfiəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːfiəs / (BrE)
a faint constellation in the northern sky near Cassiopeia and the pole star, named after a king in Greek mythology.
