voluptuous

adjective
characterized by luxury and sensual pleasure
Pronunciation: / vəˈlʌptʃuəs / (AmE), / vəˈlʌptjʊəs / (BrE)
Providing or characterized by luxury and sensual pleasure; giving gratification to the senses.

Examples

voluptuous luxury
She reclined in a voluptuous armchair, savoring the rich textures and scents of the room.
The dining room was decorated in a voluptuous style, with deep red velvet curtains and gold accents.
The hotel suite offered a voluptuous sense of comfort, from the silk sheets to the gourmet chocolates.

Related Terms

  • antonym – austere: severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance; having no luxuries
  • synonym – luxurious: comfortable and expensive; providing luxury
