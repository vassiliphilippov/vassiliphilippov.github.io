fireman

noun
male firefighter
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəmæn / (BrE)
A man whose job is to put out fires and rescue people from dangerous situations, especially as a member of a fire department.

Examples

fireman fighting fires
A retired fireman spoke to children at the local school about fire safety.
Several firemen worked together to control the wildfire in the forest.
The fireman bravely entered the burning building to rescue the trapped family.
