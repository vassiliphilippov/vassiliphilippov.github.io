moonshell

noun
marine snail with smooth, rounded shell
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈmuːnʃɛl / (BrE)
A marine snail with a smooth, rounded shell that forms a short spire, belonging to the family Naticidae.

Examples

moonshell on the beach
Collectors prize the moonshell for its glossy surface and nearly perfect roundness.
The beach was littered with shells, including several moonshells and conch shells.
The diver found a beautiful moonshell buried in the sand near the coral reef.

Related Terms
