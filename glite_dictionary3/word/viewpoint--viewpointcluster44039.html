viewpoint

noun
a particular perspective or point of view
Pronunciation: / ˈvjuːˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈvjuːˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
a particular way of thinking about or considering something; a perspective or point of view

Examples

different viewpoint
From his viewpoint, the project was a complete success.
Her viewpoint on climate change differs from that of her colleagues.
It is important to consider different viewpoints when making decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – perspective: a way of thinking about something, similar in meaning to viewpoint
  • synonym – point of view: an opinion, attitude, or judgment, especially in relation to how something is considered
