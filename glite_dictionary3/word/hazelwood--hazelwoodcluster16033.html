hazelwood

noun
reddish-brown wood from the sweet gum tree
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪzəlˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈheɪzəlˌwʊd / (BrE)
A reddish-brown wood from the heartwood of the sweet gum tree, used especially in making furniture.

Examples

hazelwood furniture
Antique chairs made of hazelwood are prized by collectors for their warm tones.
She admired the smooth finish and rich grain of the hazelwood table.
The craftsman chose hazelwood for its durability and appealing color.
