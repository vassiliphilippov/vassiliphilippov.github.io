Phaethon

proper noun
genus of seabirds (tropicbirds)
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪ.əˌθɑn / (AmE), / ˈfiː.ə.θən / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Phaethontidae, comprising the tropicbirds, which are seabirds found in tropical oceans.
