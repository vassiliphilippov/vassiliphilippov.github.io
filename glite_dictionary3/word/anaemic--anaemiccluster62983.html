anaemic

adjective
lacking healthy red blood cells (medical)
Pronunciation: / əˈniːmɪk / (AmE), / əˈniːmɪk / (BrE)
suffering from anemia or relating to anemia, a condition in which there is a deficiency of red cells or hemoglobin in the blood.

Examples

anaemic blood test
After months of feeling tired, the doctor told her she was anaemic and needed more iron.
Her anaemic appearance worried her friends and family.
The patient’s anaemic condition required careful monitoring by the medical team.
