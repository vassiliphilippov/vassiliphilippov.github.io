equable

adjective
calm and even-tempered
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkwəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɛkwəb(ə)l / (BrE)
(of a person) calm and even-tempered; not easily disturbed or angered.

Examples

equable personality
Despite the chaos in the office, he remained remarkably equable throughout the day.
His equable temperament makes him a great mediator in arguments.
She has such an equable nature that even stressful news doesn't fluster her.

  • antonym – irritable: easily annoyed or angered
  • synonym – placid: not easily upset or excited; calm
