lipstick

noun
colored cosmetic for lips
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪp.stɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɪp.stɪk / (BrE)
A colored cosmetic applied to the lips to enhance or change their color.

Examples

red lipstick
He noticed a faint mark of red lipstick on the cup.
She reapplied her lipstick before stepping out of the car.
The store carries every shade of lipstick you can imagine.
