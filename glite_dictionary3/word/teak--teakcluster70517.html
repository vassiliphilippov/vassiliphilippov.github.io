teak

noun
hard, durable wood from the teak tree
Pronunciation: / tik / (AmE), / tiːk / (BrE)
A hard, strong, and durable yellowish-brown wood from the teak tree, prized for its resistance to insects and warping, used in furniture, shipbuilding, and construction.

Examples

teak wood furniture
Antique furniture made from teak can last for generations with minimal care.
The deck was crafted from solid teak, giving the yacht both beauty and durability.
We chose teak for our garden table because it withstands the weather so well.
