Oncorhynchus nerka

noun
a species of red-fleshed Pacific salmon
Pronunciation: / ɑnkəˈrɪŋkəs ˈnɜrkə / (AmE), / ɒŋkɒˈrɪŋkəs ˈnɜːkə / (BrE)
A species of small salmon with red flesh, native to rivers and tributaries of the northern Pacific, valued as food; adults die after spawning.
