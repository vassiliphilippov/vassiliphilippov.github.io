sorghum

noun
a genus of cereal grasses; edible grain
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔr.ɡəm / (AmE), / ˈsɔː.ɡəm / (BrE)
a tall grass grown in warm regions for its edible grain, animal feed, and to make syrup
