unarticulated

adjective
not spoken in clear words or syllables
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɑːrˈtɪkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of sounds) uttered without the use of normal words or syllables; not clearly pronounced or expressed in speech.

Examples

unarticulated sound
His response was an unarticulated grunt of frustration.
She made an unarticulated sound, unable to express how she felt.
The child's cry was unarticulated, filled with emotion but no real words.

Related Terms

  • antonym – articulate: able to express oneself clearly or to pronounce words clearly
