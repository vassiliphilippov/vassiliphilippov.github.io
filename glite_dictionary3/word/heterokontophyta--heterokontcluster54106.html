Heterokontophyta

noun
a group of algae with unequal flagella
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˌkɑːnˈtɑːfɪtə /; / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˌkɑːntəˈfaɪtə / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊˌkɒnˈtɒfɪtə /; / ˌhɛtəˌrɒntəˈfaɪtə / (BrE)
a group or division of algae with chlorophyll a (and often c) and flagella of unequal lengths; sometimes used instead of 'Chrysophyta'
