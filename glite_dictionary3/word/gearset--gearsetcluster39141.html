gearset

noun
set of virtual equipment (gaming)
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u0261\u026ar\u02ccs\u025bt / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0261\u026a\u0259s\u025bt / (BrE)
a predefined set of virtual equipment or items that a video-game character equips to optimize performance, role, or statistics.

Examples

video game gearset
He switched to a DPS gearset for the boss fight.
Raid leaders recommend bringing multiple gearsets to adapt to different encounters.
She quickly swapped her gearset between quests to maximize her healing output.
