to detoxify

verb
treat to remove drugs or alcohol
Pronunciation: / diˈtɑksɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / diˈtɒksɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to remove toxic or harmful substances from a person or thing, especially to make it safe or healthy
