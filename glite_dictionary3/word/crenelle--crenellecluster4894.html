crenelle

noun
rounded projection or notch along an edge
Pronunciation: / krəˈnɛl / (AmE), / krəˈnɛl / (BrE)
a rounded projection or the notch between such projections, formed by curves along the edge of an object, such as a leaf, shell, or cell

Examples

crenelle on a leaf
The leaf's edge was decorated with a series of crenelles that gave it a distinctive appearance.
The seamstress added delicate crenelles to the border of the fabric to create a decorative effect.
Under the microscope, the cell membrane showed several crenelles resulting from shrinkage in a hypertonic solution.
