blip

noun
mark on radar showing detected object
Pronunciation: / blɪp / (AmE), / blɪp / (BrE)
a small spot of light or mark that appears on a radar screen, representing the position of a detected object

Examples

blip on radar
Every ship appeared as a separate blip on the radar.
The pilot saw a mysterious blip appear on his radar screen.
When the weather changed, more blips began showing up on the monitor.
