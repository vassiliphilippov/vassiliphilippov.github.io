to behold

verb
see or observe, especially something impressive
Pronunciation: / bɪˈhoʊld / (AmE), / bɪˈhəʊld / (BrE)
to see or observe (someone or something), especially of remarkable or impressive nature

Examples

to behold a wonder
As the curtain rose, the audience beheld a spectacular scene of dancers and lights.
If you behold a rare bird in the forest, count yourself lucky.
We stood atop the hill to behold the breathtaking view at sunrise.

  • synonym – see: to perceive with the eyes
