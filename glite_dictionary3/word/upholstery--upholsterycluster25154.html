upholstery

noun
padded covering of furniture
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈhoʊlstəri / (AmE), / ʌpˈhəʊlstəri / (BrE)
the soft, padded covering, including fabric, padding, springs, and webbing, on furniture such as chairs and sofas

Examples

upholstery fabric
Cleaning the upholstery regularly helps to maintain the furniture's appearance.
He chose a deep blue fabric for the new upholstery of his sofa.
The upholstery on the old armchair was worn and torn in several places.
