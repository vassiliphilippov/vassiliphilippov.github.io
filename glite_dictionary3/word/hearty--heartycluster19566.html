hearty

adjective
providing abundant nourishment (of food)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrti / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti / (BrE)
(of food) providing abundant nourishment; satisfying and filling.

Examples

hearty meal
After hours of hard work, the farmers gathered for a hearty meal together.
The inn serves hearty soups that are perfect for a cold winter's day.
We enjoyed a hearty breakfast before setting out on our hike.

Related Terms

  • antonym – light: of a meal: small, easy to digest, not filling
  • synonym – substantial: of a meal: large in amount, filling and satisfying
