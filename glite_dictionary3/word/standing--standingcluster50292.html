standing

noun
status or reputation within a group
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
the status, reputation, or position of a person or group within a particular context, such as society or a profession

Examples

high standing

The club requires all members to be in good standing to participate in events.
