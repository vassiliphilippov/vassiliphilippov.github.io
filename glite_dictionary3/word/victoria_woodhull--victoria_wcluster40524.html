Victoria Woodhull

proper noun
U.S. women's rights activist and presidential candidate
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔːriə ˈwʊdhʌl / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːriə ˈwʊdhʌl / (BrE)
