to reflate

verb
inflate again
Pronunciation: / riˈfleɪt / (AmE), / riˈfleɪt / (BrE)
to inflate something again; to cause something to become filled with air or gas after deflation

Examples

to reflate a tire
After the tire went flat, the mechanic had to reflate it before we could continue driving.
If you accidentally let out too much air, it's easy to reflate the balloon.
Sarah used a pump to reflate the air mattress before the guests arrived.

  • synonym – inflate: to fill with air or gas
