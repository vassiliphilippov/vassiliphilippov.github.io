to decline

verb
to refuse politely or formally
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈklaɪn / (BrE)
to refuse something, especially politely or formally

Examples

to decline an offer
Despite the generous offer, he politely declined to participate in the project.
She declined the invitation to the gala, saying she had other plans.
When asked to speak at the event, she declined with sincere apologies.

Related Terms

  • antonym – accept: to agree to take something offered
  • synonym – refuse: to say no to an offer or request
