disarray

noun
mental confusion or disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪ / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.əˈreɪ / (BrE)
A state of mental confusion or lack of clear and orderly thought or behavior.

Examples

mental disarray
After losing his job, he fell into a state of emotional disarray.
Her mind was in complete disarray after hearing the unexpected news.
The team's sudden change in strategy left the players in mental disarray.

Related Terms

  • antonym – clarity: the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • synonym – confusion: a lack of clarity or certainty in thought or behavior
