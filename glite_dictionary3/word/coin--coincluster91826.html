coin

noun
digital currency unit (cryptocurrency)
Pronunciation: / kɔɪn / (AmE), / kɔɪn / (BrE)
a unit of digital currency native to a blockchain; a non-physical token representing value or a medium of exchange in a cryptocurrency system.

Examples

earn digital coins
My wallet app shows how many coins I own and their current market value.
Traders moved to buy coin after the exchange listed a new stable token last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cryptocurrency: a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography and operates on a decentralized ledger.
  • synonym – token: a digital asset representing value or utility on a blockchain; often distinguished from a blockchain's native coin.
