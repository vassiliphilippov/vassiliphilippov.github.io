round

adverb
throughout a period; all the time
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
(adverb) At all times during a stated period; throughout, from beginning to end.

Examples

round the year
The city is lively round the year with festivals and events.
The shop is open round the clock for customers' convenience.
They need care round the year, not just in summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – all the time: continuously; always, without interruption
  • synonym – throughout: in or during the whole of (a period of time)
