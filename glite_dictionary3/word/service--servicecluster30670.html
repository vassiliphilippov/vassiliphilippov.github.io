to service

verb
provide use or benefit for
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
(of a facility, system, or utility) to provide use or benefit for; to supply or meet the needs of a specified area or group.

Examples

to service an area
The new water tower will service the entire eastern district.
This line of buses services downtown and the surrounding neighborhoods.
Two backup generators service the hospital in case of a power outage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – serve: to be of service to; to furnish or supply with something needed
