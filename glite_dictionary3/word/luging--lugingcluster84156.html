luging

noun
sport of riding a luge sled
Pronunciation: / ˈluːʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈluːʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of riding a luge, a small sled ridden supine and feet first, typically down an ice track

Examples

luging down ice
He has been luging competitively for over five years.
Olympic athletes train for years to perfect their luging skills.
The thrill of luging down an icy track attracts many winter sports enthusiasts.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – bobsledding: the sport of racing down an ice track in a bobsled
  • synonym – luge: a small, light toboggan for one or two people, ridden supine and feet first
