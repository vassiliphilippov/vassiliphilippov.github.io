cheapjack

noun
peddler of inferior goods
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃipˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːpdʒak / (BrE)
a person who sells shoddy or inferior goods at low prices, often in a showy or aggressive manner

Examples

cheapjack seller
Many buyers learned to be wary of cheapjacks and their questionable merchandise.
She dismissed him as a cheapjack with nothing of value to offer.
The cheapjack at the fair tried to sell me a watch that broke after one day.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hawker: a person who travels selling goods in the street
  • degree higher – merchant: someone who buys and sells goods, especially in large quantities
  • synonym – pedlar: a person who sells goods from place to place
