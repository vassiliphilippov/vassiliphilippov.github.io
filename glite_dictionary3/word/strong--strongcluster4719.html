strong

adjective
intense or noticeable; not faint
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ / (AmE), / strɒŋ / (BrE)
very intense, powerful, or having a strong effect (sensation, quality, wind, chemical, etc.)

Examples

strong smell
A strong wind rattled the windows all night.
The medicine has a strong taste that some people find unpleasant.
There was a strong smell of coffee in the kitchen.
Be careful with that strong disinfectant—it can irritate your skin.
He poured a strong cup of coffee to wake himself up in the morning.
This medicine is very strong, so take only the prescribed dose.
