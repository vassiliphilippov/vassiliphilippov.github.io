Herrerasaurus

noun
primitive Triassic theropod dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / hɛˌrɛrəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / hɛˌrɛərəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a primitive genus of theropod dinosaur found in Argentina from the early Triassic period, named after the rancher who discovered it.
