spring

noun
a quick, light leap or jump
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden, light jump or leap forward or upward, often made with energy or enthusiasm

The cat made a quick spring onto the windowsill to catch the sunlight.
