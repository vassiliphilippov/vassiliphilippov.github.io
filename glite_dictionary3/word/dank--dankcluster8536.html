dank

adjective
unpleasantly damp and chilly
Pronunciation: / dæŋk / (AmE), / dæŋk / (BrE)
(of an atmosphere or place) unpleasantly damp, cool, and often musty.

Examples

dank basement room
After days of rain, a dank smell filled the old cottage.
The basement was cold and dank, making it uncomfortable to stay there for long.
We walked through a dank tunnel that dripped with moisture from above.
