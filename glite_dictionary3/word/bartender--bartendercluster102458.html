bartender

noun
person who serves drinks at a bar
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrˌtɛndər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːˌtɛndə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to mix and serve alcoholic drinks at a bar.

The bartender recommended a unique cocktail that I had never tried before.
