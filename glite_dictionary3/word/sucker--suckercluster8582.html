sucker

noun
hard candy on a stick
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkər / (AmE), / ˈsʌkə / (BrE)
A piece of hard candy mounted on a small stick, intended to be sucked or licked.

Examples

cherry sucker candy
After dinner, the children each received a cherry-flavored sucker as a treat.
He unwrapped a grape sucker and popped it into his mouth while studying.
The store sells suckers in dozens of different shapes and colors.
