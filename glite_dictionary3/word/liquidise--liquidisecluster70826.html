to liquidise

verb
make or become liquid
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪdaɪz / (BrE)
to make or become liquid, especially by heating or blending

Examples

to liquidise vegetables
He liquidised the mixture to ensure all the ingredients were fully combined.
The machine can liquidise fruits in seconds, making it easy to prepare smoothies.
To make the soup creamy, simply liquidise the vegetables with a hand blender.
