volitionally

adverb
in a manner involving will or choice
Pronunciation: / voʊˈlɪʃənəli / (AmE), / vəˈlɪʃənəli / (BrE)
In a manner involving the use of one's will or making a conscious choice.

Examples

to act volitionally
Deciding volitionally to leave the company, he pursued his lifelong dream instead.
She apologized volitionally, rather than under pressure from her colleagues.
The patient signed the consent form volitionally, understanding all the risks involved.

Related Terms

  • antonym – involuntarily: in a manner not controlled by the will
  • synonym – willingly: in a willing or voluntary manner
