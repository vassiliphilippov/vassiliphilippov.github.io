wheeziness

noun
high-pitched breathing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːzi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈwiːzi.nəs / (BrE)
the condition of producing high-pitched, whistling sounds when breathing, typically due to narrowed or obstructed airways

Examples

chest wheeziness
Her asthma attacks were often preceded by a period of mild wheeziness.
If you experience persistent wheeziness, you should seek medical attention.
