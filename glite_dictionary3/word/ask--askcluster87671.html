to ask

verb
demand a price or condition
Pronunciation: / æsk / (AmE), / ɑːsk / (BrE)
to request or demand a particular price or condition for something, especially when selling or negotiating

Examples

ask a price
The artist is asking $500 for her latest painting, but she might accept less.
They are asking strict conditions before agreeing to the deal.
When selling old books, he usually asks a fair price based on their condition.

Related Terms

  • synonym – demand: to ask for forcefully or with insistence
  • antonym – offer: to present or propose (the opposite role in a transaction)
