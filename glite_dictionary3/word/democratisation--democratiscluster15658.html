democratisation

noun
making something more democratic
Pronunciation: / dɪˌmɑːkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making a system, organization, or country more democratic or open to participation by all people

Examples

democratisation of society
Education plays a vital role in the democratisation of society.
Technological advances have contributed to the democratisation of information worldwide.
The recent democratisation of the country has led to freer elections and more public participation.

Related Terms

  • antonym – authoritarianism: the enforcement of strict obedience at the expense of personal freedom
  • synonym – democracy: a system of government by the whole population; democratic system
