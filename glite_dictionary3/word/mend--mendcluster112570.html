to mend

verb
to repair something damaged or broken
Pronunciation: / mɛnd / (AmE), / mɛnd / (BrE)
to repair something that is broken or damaged by fixing what is torn or replacing a part

Examples

to mend a broken chair
After dropping her necklace, she took it to a jeweler to mend the broken clasp.
Can you mend this old chair, or is it beyond repair?
He managed to mend the torn sleeve on his shirt in just a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym – break: to cause something to separate into pieces or stop working
  • synonym – fix: to restore something that is broken to working order
  • synonym – repair: to restore something to a good condition after damage
