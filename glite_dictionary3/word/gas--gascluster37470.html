gas

noun
unit of computation and transaction fee in blockchain
Pronunciation: / ɡæs / (AmE), / ɡæs / (BrE)
(in blockchain technology) a unit that measures the computational work required to execute a transaction or smart contract; paid as a fee (often called a gas fee) to miners or validators.

Examples

gas fee blockchain
Miners prioritize transactions with higher gas fees to ensure faster processing.
She adjusted the gas price to speed up confirmation of her Ethereum transfer.
The smart contract required more gas than expected to complete execution on chain.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fee: a charge paid for a service; in this context, payment for processing a blockchain transaction
  • synonym – transaction fee: a payment made to include and process a transaction on a blockchain
