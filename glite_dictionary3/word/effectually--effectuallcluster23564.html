effectually

adverb
in an effectual manner
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛktʃuəli / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛktjʊəli / (BrE)
In an effectual manner; producing the intended result or desired effect.

Examples

effectually solve a problem
Her arguments effectually silenced any further opposition.
The new law effectually banned the use of plastic bags in the city.
This policy effectually discouraged employees from arriving late to work.

Related Terms

  • synonym – effectively: in an effective manner; successfully producing the intended result
