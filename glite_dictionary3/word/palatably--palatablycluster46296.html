palatably

adverb
in a pleasant or acceptable way
Pronunciation: / ˈpælətəbli / (AmE), / ˈpælətəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is pleasant to the taste or acceptable to the mind or feelings

Examples

The book explains complex theories palatably, making them accessible to a wider audience.
The chef prepared the vegetables so palatably that even the children enjoyed them.
