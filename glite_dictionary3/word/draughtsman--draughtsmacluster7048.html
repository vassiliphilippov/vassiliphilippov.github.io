draughtsman

noun
person who draws technical plans
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftsmən / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːf(t)smən / (BrE)
a person skilled at drawing plans or technical diagrams, especially of buildings or machines

Examples

draughtsman drawing plans
As a mechanical draughtsman, he creates detailed schematics for new machines.

The company hired a talented draughtsman to prepare the architectural blueprints.
