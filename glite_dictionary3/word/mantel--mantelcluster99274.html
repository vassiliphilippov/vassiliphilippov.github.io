mantel

noun
shelf above a fireplace
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.təl / (AmE), / ˈmæn.tl̩ / (BrE)
A shelf above a fireplace, often projecting from the wall and sometimes ornately decorated.

Examples

mantel above fireplace
A clock and two candlesticks decorated the old stone mantel.
He hung his Christmas stocking on the mantel every year.
She placed the family photos on the mantel above the fireplace.
