cane

noun
flexible plant stem (e.g. bamboo, rattan)
Pronunciation: / keɪn / (AmE), / keɪn / (BrE)
the strong, slender, often flexible stem of certain plants such as bamboo, reed, rattan, or sugar cane, used for making furniture, baskets, and other items

Examples

cane furniture
Baskets made from cane are lightweight and durable.
She used a piece of cane to support the tomato plants in her garden.
The chair was woven from strips of cane harvested from the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym – reed: tall, slender-leaved plant of wetlands; also used for its stem
