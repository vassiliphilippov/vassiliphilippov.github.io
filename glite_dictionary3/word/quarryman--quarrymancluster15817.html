quarryman

noun
male worker in a quarry
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈkwɒrɪˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to extract stone, slate, or other materials from a quarry

Examples

quarryman working stone
After years as a quarryman, he knew all the dangers of the job.
His father was a quarryman who spent decades working in the local limestone pits.
The experienced quarryman skillfully cut blocks of granite from the rock face.
