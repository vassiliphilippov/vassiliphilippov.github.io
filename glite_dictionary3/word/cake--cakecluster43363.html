cake

noun
sweet baked food
Pronunciation: / keɪk / (AmE), / keɪk / (BrE)
a sweet baked food made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and fat, often with added flavoring, and usually cooked in an oven

Examples

birthday cake
For her birthday, she baked a chocolate cake with strawberry icing.
The wedding cake was beautifully decorated with fresh flowers and pearls.
Would you like a slice of cake with your tea?
