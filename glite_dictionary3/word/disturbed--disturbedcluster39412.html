disturbed

adjective
afflicted by anxiety or emotional trouble
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɝːbd / (AmE), / dɪˈstɜːbd / (BrE)
feeling or showing emotional or mental trouble, such as anxiety, uneasiness, or mental health problems
