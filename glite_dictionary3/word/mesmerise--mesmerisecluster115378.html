to mesmerise

verb
hold attention as if by magic
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛzməˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛzməraɪz / (BrE)
to attract and hold the attention of someone as if by magic or hypnotic power

Examples

to mesmerise an audience
Children were mesmerised by the bright colors and fast movements of the parade.
His deep voice seemed to mesmerise everyone in the room.
The magician managed to mesmerise the entire crowd with her performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bore: to make someone feel uninterested or dull; the opposite of captivate
  • synonym – captivate: to attract and hold the interest and attention of; charm
  • synonym – fascinate: to attract the strong attention and interest of; charm
