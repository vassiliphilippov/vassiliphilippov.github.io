sarape

noun
Mexican brightly colored shawl or blanket
Pronunciation: / səˈrɑːpeɪ / (AmE), / səˈrɑːpeɪ / (BrE)
A long, brightly colored woolen shawl or blanket, often with fringed ends, traditionally worn by Mexican men.
