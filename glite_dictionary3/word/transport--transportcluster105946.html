to transport

verb
to enrapture; carry away with emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈtrænspɔrt, trænzˈpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtrænspɔːt, trænzˈpɔːt / (BrE)
to cause someone to feel extreme joy or rapture; to carry away with strong emotion

Examples

to transport with delight
She was transported with joy by the news of her success.
The beauty of the painting transports visitors every time they see it.
The sublime music seemed to transport him to another world.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bore: to cause someone to feel uninterested or dull
  • synonym – enrapture: to fill with great delight; to captivate completely
