single

adjective
uniform or universal; not varying
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
applying to all cases or parts without variation; uniform or universal.

Examples

single system rule
The country adopted a single system of taxation across all regions.
There is a single health policy that covers all employees.
We need a single set of guidelines for every department.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unified: made into or formed as a unit
  • antonym – varied: differing in some way; not uniform
