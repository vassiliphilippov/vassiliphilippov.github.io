lonesome

adjective
single and isolated; the only one
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnsəm / (AmE), / ˈləʊns(ə)m / (BrE)
Being the only one; single and isolated from others.

Examples

a lonesome road
A lonesome ship drifted far from the busy ports.
He spent the night beside a lonesome road, miles from any town.
In the garden stood a single, lonesome rose.
