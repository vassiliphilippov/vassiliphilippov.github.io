involved

adjective
connected by participation or association
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
connected with something by taking part or by being associated or affected

Examples

involved in a project
Everyone involved in the project received a thank-you note.
No one involved was available for comment after the meeting.
The involved parties met to resolve the dispute.
