Halloween

noun
a festival observed on October 31
Pronunciation: / ˌhæloʊˈwiːn / (AmE), / ˌhæləʊˈiːn / (BrE)
The evening of October 31, especially celebrated by children in costumes who go door to door for treats and by playing pranks.

Examples

Halloween costumes
Every year, children dress up in costumes and go trick-or-treating on Halloween.
She loves watching scary movies with her friends on Halloween night.
The neighbors decorated their house with pumpkins and skeletons for Halloween.
