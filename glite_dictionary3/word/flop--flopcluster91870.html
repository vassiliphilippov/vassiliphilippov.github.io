to flop

verb
fail completely; be unsuccessful
Pronunciation: / flɑp / (AmE), / flɒp / (BrE)
(of a plan, event, or performance) to fail completely or be unsuccessful.

Examples

to flop at the box office
Despite the advertising campaign, the movie completely flopped at the box office.
His attempt at stand-up comedy flopped and the audience remained silent.
The new product flopped in the market, selling far less than expected.
