Carpocapsa pomonella

noun
species of moth, apple pest
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrpəˈkæpsə ˌpoʊməˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˌkɑːpəˈkæpsə ˌpɒməˈnɛlə / (BrE)
A small grey moth whose larvae are common pests in apples and walnuts, also known as the codling moth.
