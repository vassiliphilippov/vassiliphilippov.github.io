underdressed

adjective
wearing clothes too informally
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈdrɛst / (BrE)
Wearing clothes that are too informal or inadequate for a particular occasion.

Examples

underdressed at a party
He felt underdressed when he arrived at the formal dinner in jeans.
Most guests were in evening wear, making me worry I was underdressed.
You shouldn't be underdressed for a job interview; appearance matters.
