Dorothy Dix

proper noun
American advice columnist's pen name (1870–1951)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔr.ə.θi dɪks / (AmE), / ˈdɒr.ə.θi dɪks / (BrE)
The pen name of Elizabeth Meriwether Gilmer (1870–1951), an American journalist known for her widely syndicated advice column for people seeking help with personal and romantic problems.
