promotional

adjective
relating to advancement or promotion
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊʃənəl / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the advancement of someone to a higher position or rank; connected with promotion within an organization or system.

Examples

promotional exam
He took a promotional exam last year to qualify for a higher position.
Only employees with at least two years of service are eligible for promotional opportunities.
The department announced several promotional openings for senior staff members.

Related Terms

  • synonym – promotion: the act of raising someone to a higher position or rank
