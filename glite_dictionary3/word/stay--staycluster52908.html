to stay

verb
fasten or support with stays
Pronunciation: / steɪ / (AmE), / steɪ / (BrE)
to fasten or support with stays (such as ropes, wires, or braces) to provide strength or stability

Examples

to stay a structure
Engineers will stay the bridge supports to ensure they can handle the increased load.
He carefully stayed the mast with thick ropes before the storm arrived.
The builders had to stay the old wall with metal braces to prevent it from collapsing.
