to destalinise

verb
remove influence of Stalinism
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɑːləˌnaɪz / (AmE), / dɪˈstɑːlɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to remove the influence, practices, or policies associated with Stalinism, especially in a government or society

Examples

to destalinise a country
After the leader's death, the new government promised to destalinise the country's institutions.
Historians debate how thoroughly the nation actually destalinised in the years following Stalin's rule.
Many monuments were removed as part of efforts to destalinise the capital city.

Related Terms

  • antonym – stalinise: to impose the policies or characteristics of Stalinism
