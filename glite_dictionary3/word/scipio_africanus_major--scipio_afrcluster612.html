Scipio Africanus Major

proper noun
notable Roman general, defeated Hannibal at Zama
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪp.i.oʊ ˌæf.rɪˈkeɪ.nəs ˈmeɪ.dʒər / (AmE), / ˈsɪp.i.əʊ ˌæf.rɪˈkeɪ.nəs ˈmeɪ.dʒə / (BrE)
(circa 237–183 BC) A prominent Roman general who led the invasion of Carthage during the Second Punic War and defeated Hannibal at the Battle of Zama.
