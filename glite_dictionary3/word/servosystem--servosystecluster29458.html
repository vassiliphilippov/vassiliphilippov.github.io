servosystem

noun
automatic control system with feedback
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrvoʊˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvəʊˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system in which a small mechanical input is used to control a larger output, often incorporating negative feedback to maintain accuracy.

Examples

automatic servosystem control
A breakdown in the servosystem can result in loss of automation and accuracy.
Modern aircraft use multiple servosystems to adjust flight surfaces.
The robotic arm relies on a servosystem for precise movement control.
