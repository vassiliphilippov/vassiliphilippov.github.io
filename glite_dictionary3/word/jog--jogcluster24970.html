to jog

verb
to align the edges of a paper stack
Pronunciation: / dʒɑɡ / (AmE), / dʒɒɡ / (BrE)
(in printing or document handling) to align or even up the edges of a stack of paper by lightly shaking or tapping it.

Examples

to jog the papers
After printing, please jog the stack to ensure all sheets are aligned.
Before binding, the assistant will jog the papers to line up the edges neatly.
The operator paused to jog the pages before placing them in the copier.
