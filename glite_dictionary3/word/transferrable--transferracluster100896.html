transferrable

adjective
able to be transferred or moved
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɝːəbəl / (AmE), / trænsˈfɜːrəbl / (BrE)
able to be transferred or moved from one place, person, or situation to another.

Examples

transferrable job skills
Many skills learned in this job are transferrable to other industries.
Ownership of the software license is transferrable under certain conditions.
The airline ticket is transferrable, so you may give it to someone else if you can't travel.

Related Terms

  • antonym – nontransferable: not able to be transferred or moved
  • synonym – transferable: also able to be transferred or moved; more common variant spelling
