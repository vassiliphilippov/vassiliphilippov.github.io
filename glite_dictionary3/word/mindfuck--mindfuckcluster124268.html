mindfuck

noun
something that confuses or unsettles the mind
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪndˌfʌk / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndˌfʌk / (BrE)
A situation, event, or experience that deliberately confuses, manipulates, or destabilizes someone's perception or understanding.

Examples

a real mindfuck experience
Reading his novel can be a real mindfuck, as it constantly challenges your perception of reality.
That plot twist at the end was a complete mindfuck and left everyone shocked.
The movie was such a mindfuck that I left the theater feeling totally disoriented.
