Bedloe's Island

proper noun
former name of Liberty Island in New York Bay
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdloʊz ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈbɛdləʊz ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
the former name of Liberty Island, an island in New York Bay southwest of Manhattan, now the site of the Statue of Liberty
