chalcid

noun
tiny parasitic wasp; pest controller
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.sɪd / (AmE), / ˈkalsɪd / (BrE)
A tiny wasp-like insect whose larvae are parasitic on the eggs or larvae of other insects, often helping to control insect pests.

Examples

chalcid wasp parasite
Scientists studied the life cycle of a chalcid to understand its parasitic behavior.
Several species of chalcid help reduce populations of crop-damaging insects.
The chalcid is often used in agriculture for natural pest control.
