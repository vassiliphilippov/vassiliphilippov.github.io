to block

verb
to shape over a block (garment, hat)
Pronunciation: / blɑk / (AmE), / blɒk / (BrE)
to shape or mold (a hat, garment, etc.) by stretching or pressing over a block or form

Examples

to block a sweater
After washing the sweater, she carefully blocked it to restore its original size.
Professionals often block garments before finishing them to achieve sharp lines and crisp folds.
The milliner will block the felt to create the perfect shape for the hat.
