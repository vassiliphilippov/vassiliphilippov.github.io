polish

noun
a substance for making surfaces shiny
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪʃ / (BrE)
a substance used to make a surface smooth and shiny by rubbing, often applied to shoes, furniture, or cars

Examples

shoe polish
She applied a layer of polish to her shoes to make them shine.
The car polish removed all the small scratches and made the paint look new.
Use a furniture polish to restore the table's glossy finish.

  • synonym – wax: a solid substance used to polish or protect surfaces
