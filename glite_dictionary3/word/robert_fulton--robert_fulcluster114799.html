Robert Fulton

proper noun
American inventor; steamboat pioneer
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊbərt ˈfʊltən / (AmE), / ˈrəʊbət ˈfʊltən / (BrE)
(1765–1815) An American inventor known for designing the first commercially successful steamboat and the first steam-powered warship.
