Anasa tristis

noun
species of North American squash bug
Pronunciation: / əˈnæsə ˈtrɪstɪs / (AmE), / əˈnæsə ˈtrɪstɪs / (BrE)
A large, dark-colored North American bug of the squash bug family that feeds on the sap of plants in the gourd family, often considered an agricultural pest.
