Christopher Marlowe

proper noun
English poet and playwright (1564–1593)
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪstəfɚ ˈmɑrloʊ / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstəfə ˈmɑːləʊ / (BrE)
(1564–1593) An English poet and playwright noted for introducing blank verse to English drama, and known for works such as "Doctor Faustus."
