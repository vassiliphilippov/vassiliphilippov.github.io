ho-hum

adjective
dull or boring; uninteresting
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊˌhʌm / (AmE), / ˈhəʊˌhʌm / (BrE)
so dull or uninteresting as to cause boredom or mental weariness; monotonous or tedious.

Examples

ho-hum attitude
After hearing the same speech for the third time, the audience found it rather ho-hum.
Her presentation was criticized for being ho-hum and lacking new ideas.
The film was so ho-hum that several people left before it ended.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dull: lacking interest or excitement; boring
  • antonym – exciting: causing great enthusiasm or eagerness
