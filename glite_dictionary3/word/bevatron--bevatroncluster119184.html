bevatron

noun
high-energy particle accelerator
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛvəˌtrɑn / (AmE), / ˈbiːvəˌtrɒn / (BrE)
a type of cyclotron designed to accelerate protons to energies of several billion electron volts.
