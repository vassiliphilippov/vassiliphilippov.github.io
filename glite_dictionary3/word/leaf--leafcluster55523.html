leaf

noun
sheet of paper in a book or manuscript
Pronunciation: / lif / (AmE), / liːf / (BrE)
a single sheet of paper in a book or manuscript, especially one with writing or printing on it

Examples

leaf of a book
A torn leaf was missing from the back of the old book.
She carefully turned the delicate leaf of the ancient manuscript.
The librarian found several loose leaves inside the cover.
