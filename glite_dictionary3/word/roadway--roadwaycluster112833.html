roadway

noun
part of a road vehicles travel on
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd.weɪ / (BrE)
the part of a road over which vehicles travel; the area designated for vehicular traffic as opposed to sidewalks or verges

Examples

busy roadway
Heavy rain caused flooding across the roadway, making it impassable for cars.
The city plans to widen the roadway to ease traffic congestion.
Workers repaired the damaged roadway over the weekend.

  • synonym – road: a wide way leading from one place to another
  • antonym – sidewalk: path along the side of a road for pedestrians
