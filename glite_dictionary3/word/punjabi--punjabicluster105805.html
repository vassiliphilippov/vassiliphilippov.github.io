Punjabi

noun
a person from Punjab (Pakistan or India)
Pronunciation: / pʌnˈdʒɑːbi / (AmE), / pʌnˈdʒɑːbi / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the Punjab region of Pakistan or India, or a member of the Punjabi people

Punjabi people
