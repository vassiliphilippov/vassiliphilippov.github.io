catechu

noun
an East Indian tree used for dye and tanning
Pronunciation: / ˈkætɪˌtʃu / (AmE), / ˈkætɪˌtjuː / (BrE)
an East Indian tree (Acacia catechu) known for its extract used in dyes, tanning, and medicine
