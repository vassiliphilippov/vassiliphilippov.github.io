to cry

verb
demand urgent attention or action
Pronunciation: / kraɪ / (AmE), / kraɪ / (BrE)
(of a situation or problem) to demand immediate attention or action.

Examples

cry out for help
The injustice in the community cries out for reform.
The urgent need for more housing cries to be addressed by the authorities.
This neglected garden really cries for some care and attention.
