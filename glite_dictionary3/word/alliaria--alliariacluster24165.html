Alliaria

proper noun
plant genus including garlic mustard
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪ.əˌrɪə / (AmE), / əˈlɪə.ri.ə / (BrE)
a genus of herbs in the family Brassicaceae, characterized by broad leaves, white flowers, and long seed pods (siliques), including the commonly known garlic mustard.
