white

noun
the clear part of an egg
Pronunciation: / waɪt / (AmE), / waɪt / (BrE)
the clear, viscous substance surrounding the yolk of an egg, consisting mainly of albumin dissolved in water; egg white

Examples

white of an egg
Separating the white from the yolk can be a bit tricky at first.
She likes to beat the white of the egg until it's stiff for baking.
The recipe calls for two whites and one yolk.
