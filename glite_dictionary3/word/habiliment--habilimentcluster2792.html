habiliment

noun
formal or distinctive clothing
Pronunciation: / həˈbɪləmənt / (AmE), / həˈbɪlɪmənt / (BrE)
(formal or archaic) an article of clothing; garments worn on the body, especially of a particular kind or for a particular occasion.

Examples

festive habiliments
During the festival, villagers donned their most colorful habiliments to take part in the parade.
He arrived in the habiliment of a medieval knight, complete with armor and plume.
The ceremonial habiliment of the judges was rich and elaborate.
