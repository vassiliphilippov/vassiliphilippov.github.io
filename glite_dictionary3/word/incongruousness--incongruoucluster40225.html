incongruousness

noun
state of being unsuitable or out of place
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑŋɡruəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒŋɡruːəsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being out of place, unsuitable, or not in harmony with the surroundings or situation.

Examples

incongruousness of style
Critics pointed out the incongruousness in the film's mood and setting.
She was struck by the incongruousness of the cheerful music during the somber scene.
The incongruousness of his casual clothes at the formal event was impossible to ignore.

Related Terms

  • antonym – congruity: the quality of being in agreement or harmony
  • synonym – incongruity: the state of being incongruous or out of keeping
