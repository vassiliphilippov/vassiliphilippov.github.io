bender

noun
curveball pitch in baseball (North American slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛndə / (BrE)
(North American, informal) A pitch in baseball that is thrown with spin, causing it to curve as it approaches the batter; a curveball.
