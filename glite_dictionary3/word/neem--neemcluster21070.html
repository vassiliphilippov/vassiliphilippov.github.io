neem

noun
tropical medicinal tree of South Asia
Pronunciation: / nim / (AmE), / niːm / (BrE)
a large semi-evergreen tree (Azadirachta indica) native to the Indian subcontinent, known for its medicinal bark, aromatic seeds, and gum; widely used in traditional medicine and agriculture

Examples

neem tree leaves
Farmers often use oil extracted from neem seeds as a natural pesticide.
Many traditional medicines incorporate neem bark due to its reputed healing properties.
The neem tree is celebrated for its ability to purify the air and provide shade in rural villages.
