graze

noun
superficial skin abrasion
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪz / (AmE), / ɡreɪz / (BrE)
A slight injury where the surface of the skin is scraped or rubbed off.

Examples

graze on the elbow
After playing football on the pavement, he had a graze on his elbow.
She fell off her bike and ended up with a graze on her knee.
The nurse cleaned the graze and covered it with a bandage.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cut: An injury made by something sharp
  • synonym – scrape: A scratch or abrasion on the skin
