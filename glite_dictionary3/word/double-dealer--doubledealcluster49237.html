double-dealer

noun
a deceitful or hypocritical person
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəlˌdiːlər / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əlˌdiː.lə / (BrE)
a person who pretends to have certain beliefs or feelings while secretly acting in a different or opposite way; a hypocrite or deceiver

Examples

a double-dealer at work
Everyone was shocked to learn he was a double-dealer who had been spying for the rival company.
In politics, it's hard to tell who is a true friend and who is a double-dealer.
She warned me not to trust him, calling him the office double-dealer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hypocrite: a person who pretends to have virtues, beliefs, principles, etc., that they do not actually possess
