argument

noun
summary of a literary work's plot or subject
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡjəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡjʊmənt / (BrE)
a brief summary of the plot or subject of a literary work, such as a poem, play, or novel, often appearing at the beginning of the text

Examples

argument before story
A short argument precedes the novel, providing readers with background information.
The edition of the play contains an argument before each act, summarizing the action.
The epic poem includes an argument that outlines its major themes.
