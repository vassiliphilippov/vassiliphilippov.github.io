Christopher Carson

proper noun
American frontiersman and Civil War general
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəfər ˈkɑrsən / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəfə ˈkɑːsən / (BrE)
(1809–1868) United States frontiersman, guide of Fremont's expeditions in the 1840s, and Union general in the American Civil War.

Christopher Carson explored the West
As a guide, Christopher Carson was instrumental in opening up the American West to settlement.
