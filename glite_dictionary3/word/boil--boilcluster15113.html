boil

noun
painful, pus-filled sore on the skin
Pronunciation: / bɔɪl / (AmE), / bɔɪl / (BrE)
a painful, swollen area on the skin that is caused by a bacterial infection and filled with pus

Examples

painful boil on skin
Poor hygiene can sometimes cause a boil to develop on the skin.
She had a large boil on her arm that needed medical treatment.
The doctor lanced the boil to drain the pus and reduce the pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abscess: a swollen area within body tissue containing pus
