vindication

noun
proof of justification or correctness
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪndəˈkeɪʃən /; / ˌvɪndɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɪndɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or result of being proven right or justified, especially after being doubted or criticized
