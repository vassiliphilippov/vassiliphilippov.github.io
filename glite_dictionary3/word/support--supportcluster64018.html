to support

verb
bear the weight of; keep upright
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
to bear the weight of something or keep it upright and prevent it from falling

Examples

to support a building
Bookshelves must be strong enough to support the weight of all those books.
He used a cane to support his injured leg while walking.
The pillars support the entire structure of the bridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hold up: to keep something from falling; to support physically
  • synonym – sustain: to support or bear the weight of
