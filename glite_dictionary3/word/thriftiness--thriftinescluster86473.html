thriftiness

noun
carefulness with money or resources
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɪfti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈθrɪfti.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of using money and resources carefully and not wastefully; frugality.

Examples

thriftiness with money
He was admired for his thriftiness and wise financial decisions.
Her thriftiness allowed her to save enough money to buy a house.
The company's thriftiness helped it survive during tough economic times.
