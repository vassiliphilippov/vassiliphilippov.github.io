native

noun
indigenous plant or animal
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tɪv / (BrE)
A plant or animal species that is indigenous to a particular region or environment, not introduced from elsewhere.

Examples

native plant species
Biologists are working to protect natives from invasive species.
The oak tree is a native of this region and thrives in its climate.
This national park is home to many native plants that flourish in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym – endemic: a species that is native to and restricted to a certain place
  • antonym – exotic: a species not native but introduced to a region
  • antonym – non-native: a plant or animal that is introduced from elsewhere
