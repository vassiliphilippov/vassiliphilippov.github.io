master

noun
head of a school
Pronunciation: / ˈmæs.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.stə / (BrE)
the presiding officer or head of a school, especially in traditional or private institutions

Examples

master of school
After years of service, she was appointed master of the respected preparatory school.
During the tour, the master explained the school's long-standing traditions to the visitors.
The master of the college addressed the students during the annual ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym – headmaster: principal or head teacher, especially in a private school
  • synonym – principal: the person in charge of a school
