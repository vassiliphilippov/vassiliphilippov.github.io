to hearken

verb
to listen (literary, archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrkən / (AmE), / ˈhɑːkən / (BrE)
to listen carefully to something, especially as a command; used mainly in formal, literary, or archaic contexts.

Examples

to hearken to advice
Hearken to my words, for they are spoken in wisdom.
If you hearken to the wind, you may learn secrets untold.
The people were asked to hearken unto the king's commands.

Related Terms

  • synonym – listen: to give attention with the ear; to hear
