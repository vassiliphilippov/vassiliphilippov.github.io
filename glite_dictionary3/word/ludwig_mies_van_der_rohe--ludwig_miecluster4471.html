Ludwig Mies van der Rohe

proper noun
German-American modernist architect (1886–1969)
Pronunciation: / ˈluːdvɪɡ ˈmiːs væn dər ˈroʊə / (AmE), / ˈluːdvɪɡ ˈmiːs van də ˈrəːə / (BrE)
(1886–1969) German-born American architect known for designing unornamented steel frame and glass skyscrapers, influential in modernist architecture.
