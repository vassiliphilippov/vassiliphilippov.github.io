action

noun
a lawsuit or judicial proceeding
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈæk.ʃən / (BrE)
A legal process in which one party sues another in court to enforce or protect a right, prevent a wrong, or seek justice for a wrong suffered.

Examples

bring an action
He decided to bring an action in court to recover the damages.
Several actions were pending before the judge at the district court.
The company filed an action against its competitor for patent infringement.

  • synonym – lawsuit: a claim or dispute brought before a court of law
