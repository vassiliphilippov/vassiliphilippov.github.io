Sinhala

noun
Indo-Aryan language of Sri Lanka
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈhɑːlə / (AmE), / sɪnˈhɑːlə / (BrE)
the main Indo-Aryan language spoken by the majority of people in Sri Lanka

Examples

Sinhala language course
He took a course to improve his Sinhala language skills before traveling to Colombo.
Many Sri Lankan children learn to read and write in Sinhala at an early age.
The official documents in Sri Lanka are often printed in both Sinhala and Tamil.
