awestruck

adjective
filled with awe and wonder
Pronunciation: / ˈɔː.strʌk / (AmE), / ˈɔː.strʌk / (BrE)
filled with a feeling of awe; having a sense of reverence, respect, and wonder, often mixed with fear or admiration.

Examples

awestruck by beauty
He stood awestruck in front of the magnificent cathedral, barely able to speak.
Her awestruck expression showed how deeply she respected and admired the artist's talent.
The children gazed in awestruck silence at the fireworks lighting up the night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amazed: greatly surprised or astonished
  • antonym – unimpressed: not feeling admiration or respect
