do

noun
first note of the major scale (solfège)
Pronunciation: / doʊ / (AmE), / dəʊ / (BrE)
The syllable naming the first and tonic note of any major scale in solmization (solfège).

Examples

do in music

The scale begins with do, followed by re, mi, and so on.
