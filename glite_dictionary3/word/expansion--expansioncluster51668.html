expansion

noun
software or game add-on pack
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspæn.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈspæn.ʃən / (BrE)
a package of additional content or features for a video game or software application that extends the original product’s functionality, areas, or storyline.

Examples

game expansion
The RPG's latest expansion added new quests and an extra map.
Players complained the expansion required a paid subscription to access its premium missions.
After installing the expansion, the game introduced a new continent with dozens of side quests.

Related Terms

  • synonym – DLC: downloadable content for a video game, often paid, that adds features or content
  • synonym – add-on: additional software or content that extends the functionality of a program or game
