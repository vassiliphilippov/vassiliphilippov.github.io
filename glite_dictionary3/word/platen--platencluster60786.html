platen

noun
flat supporting plate in machinery
Pronunciation: / ˈplætn̩ / (AmE), / ˈplæt.ən / (BrE)
a flat plate or surface in a machine, especially one that holds or supports an item being worked on, such as the work table of a machine tool

Examples

platen supports workpiece
Before starting the task, they cleaned the platen of the machine thoroughly.
The engineer adjusted the platen to ensure the metal sheet was properly aligned.
The printing press uses a heavy iron platen to press paper against the type.
