return

noun
reciprocal action or response
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtɝn /; / rɪˈtɜrn / (AmE), / rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
an action or response given back in exchange or as a reply

Examples

return for help
In return for her kindness, he offered to drive her to the airport.
She baked him a cake in return for his help with the project.
They sent gifts as a return gesture after receiving our invitation.
He was known for his quick return whenever someone challenged him.
Her clever return left everyone in the room speechless.
The politician's sharp return silenced his critics during the debate.
