harvestman

noun
long-legged, spiderlike arachnid
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrvɪstmən / (AmE), / ˈhɑːvɪstmən / (BrE)
A spiderlike arachnid with a small, rounded body and very long, thin legs, belonging to the order Opiliones.

Examples

A harvestman scurried across the garden path, its delicate legs barely touching the ground.
Children often mistake a harvestman for a spider, but they are actually different types of arachnids.
We spotted several harvestmen clinging to the wooden fence after the rain.
