to trump

verb
proclaim with fanfare
Pronunciation: / trʌmp / (AmE), / trʌmp / (BrE)
to proclaim or announce something with or as if with a fanfare; to promote or publicize loudly or extravagantly

Examples

to trump a success
The company trumped its new product launch with an elaborate press event.
The mayor trumped his achievements during the election campaign.
The museum trumped the opening of its latest exhibition with banners and music.

Related Terms

  • synonym – announce: to make something known publicly or officially
  • synonym – publicize: to make something widely known; to promote
