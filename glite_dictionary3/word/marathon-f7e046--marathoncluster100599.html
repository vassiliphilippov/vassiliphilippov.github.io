marathon

noun
long-distance running race
Pronunciation: / ˈmærəˌθɑn / (AmE), / ˈmærəθən / (BrE)
A long-distance running race with an official distance of 26 miles 385 yards (42.195 kilometers).

Examples

Running a marathon requires months of training and dedication.
She completed her first marathon in under four hours.
The city hosts an annual marathon that attracts runners from around the world.
