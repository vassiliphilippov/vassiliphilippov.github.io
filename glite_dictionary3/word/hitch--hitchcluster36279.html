hitch

noun
period of time spent in military service
Pronunciation: / hɪtʃ / (AmE), / hɪtʃ / (BrE)
a period of time spent in the military or a particular job, especially in the armed forces.

Examples

a hitch in the Army
After a four-year hitch in the Army, he returned home to start a business.
During my hitch overseas, I learned valuable skills that still help me today.
He completed his first hitch in the Navy before enrolling in college.
