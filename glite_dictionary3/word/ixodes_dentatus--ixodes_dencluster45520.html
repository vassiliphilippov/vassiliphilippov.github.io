Ixodes dentatus

noun
a tick species that transmits Lyme disease
Pronunciation: / aɪˈzoʊdiːz dɛnˈteɪtəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsəʊdiːz dɛnˈteɪtəs / (BrE)
a species of hard-bodied tick that transmits the Lyme disease spirochete to animals such as cottontail rabbits and wood rats
