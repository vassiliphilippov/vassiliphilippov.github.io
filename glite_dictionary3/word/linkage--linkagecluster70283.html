linkage

noun
association or connection between things
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋ.kɪdʒ / (BrE)
a connection or association between things, or the act of joining things together

Examples

linkage between ideas
Scientists are studying the linkage between air pollution and respiratory diseases.
The report highlighted the linkage of economic growth with environmental sustainability.
There is a strong linkage between education and income in this region.
Geneticists study the linkage of certain traits inherited together.
The new treaty established closer linkage between the two countries.
There is a strong linkage between exercise and improved mental health.
