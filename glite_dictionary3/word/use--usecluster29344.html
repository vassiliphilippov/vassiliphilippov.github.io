to use

verb
consume or exhaust resources
Pronunciation: / juːs / (AmE), / juːz / (BrE)
to consume or expend a resource or material until none is left

Examples

to use all resources
After several hours, we had used all the firewood we brought for the campfire.
The factory uses a huge amount of water each day in its operations.
Try not to use all the printer paper in one week.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conserve: to use as little as possible to avoid waste
  • synonym – consume: to use up or eat up resources completely
  • synonym – expend: to use up, especially of money or resources
