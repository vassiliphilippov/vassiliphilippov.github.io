Philemon

proper noun
hospitable countryman in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / fəˈlimən / (AmE), / fɪˈliːmɒn / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a simple countryman who, along with his wife Baucis, showed hospitality to Zeus and Hermes when they visited earth in disguise.
