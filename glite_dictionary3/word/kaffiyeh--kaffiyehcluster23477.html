kaffiyeh

noun
traditional Arab headscarf
Pronunciation: / kəˈfiːə / (AmE), / kəˈfiːə / (BrE)
A traditional Arab headdress, usually a square cloth folded into a triangle and worn over the head, often secured with a cord called an agal.

Examples

wear a kaffiyeh scarf
He wore a white kaffiyeh draped loosely over his shoulders.
Many people recognize the kaffiyeh as a symbol of Middle Eastern heritage.
The black-and-white patterned kaffiyeh shielded him from the sun.
