imitation

noun
theory in art: accurate representation
Pronunciation: / ˌɪməˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
(in art or aesthetics) The doctrine that artistic representations should closely and accurately reproduce nature or human behavior.

Some philosophers argue that imitation is the highest aim of artistic creation.
