unseasoned

adjective
(of food) without salt or spices
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsizənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːzənd / (BrE)
(of food) not having salt, spices, or other flavoring added.

Examples

unseasoned chicken breast
He served unseasoned vegetables to highlight their natural flavor.
The chicken was left unseasoned, which disappointed many diners.
The soup tasted bland because it was unseasoned.

Related Terms

  • antonym – seasoned: (of food) having salt, spices, or other flavorings added
