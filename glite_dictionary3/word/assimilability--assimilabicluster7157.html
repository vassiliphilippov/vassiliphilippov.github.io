assimilability

noun
ability to be assimilated (as nourishment)
Pronunciation: / əˌsɪməˌləbɪlɪti / (AmE), / əˌsɪmɪləˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being capable of being absorbed and utilized, especially as nourishment.
