second

noun
the person or thing next after the first
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛk.ənd / (AmE), / ˈsɛk.ənd / (BrE)
the person or thing that comes next after the first in a sequence, competition, or order

Examples

be the second to arrive
He was the second to arrive at the meeting.
She finished a close second in the race.
The school won second place in the competition.
