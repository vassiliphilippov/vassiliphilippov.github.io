crowned

adjective
having a crown or crown-like feature
Pronunciation: / ˈkraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkraʊnd / (BrE)
having a crown, or something resembling a crown, on top; often used in combination to describe something topped or adorned as with a crown.

Examples

crowned head
She wore a crowned tiara at the gala event.
The city was overlooked by a hill crowned with ancient ruins.
The statue is crowned with a wreath symbolizing victory.

Related Terms

  • antonym – uncrowned: not having a crown; not formally invested with a crown
