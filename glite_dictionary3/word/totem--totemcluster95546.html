totem

noun
magical object in fiction/games
Pronunciation: / \u02c8to\u028at\u0259m / (AmE), / \u02c8t\u0259\u028at\u0259m / (BrE)
(in fiction and gaming) an object believed to have magical powers or that grants abilities or bonuses to a character when used or equipped.

Examples

magic totem item
In the game, the ancient totem increased each player's magic power for ten minutes.
She placed a carved totem on the altar to protect the village from storms.
They collected several totems that granted new abilities when equipped during battles.

Related Terms

  • synonym – amulet: an object believed to have protective or magical powers
  • synonym – talisman: an object thought to bring good luck or magical influence
