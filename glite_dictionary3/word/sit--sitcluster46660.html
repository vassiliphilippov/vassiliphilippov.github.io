to sit

verb
pose for an artist or photographer
Pronunciation: / sɪt / (AmE), / sɪt / (BrE)
to assume a posture, especially for an artist or photographer, typically by remaining still for a portrait or similar artistic work

Examples

to sit for a portrait
He was invited to sit as the subject of a famous sculpture.
Many writers sit for photographs to be used in book jackets.
She agreed to sit for a portrait in the artist's studio.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pose: to assume a particular attitude or position, especially for artistic purposes
