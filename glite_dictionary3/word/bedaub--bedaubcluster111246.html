to bedaub

verb
smear or cover thickly
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdɔb / (AmE), / bɪˈdɔːb / (BrE)
to smear or cover a surface thickly, especially with something sticky or messy

Examples

to bedaub a face
He bedaubed his face with camouflage paint before the game.
Her apron was bedaubed with flour and chocolate from baking all afternoon.
The children bedaubed the walls with colorful paint during art class.

Related Terms

  • antonym – clean: to remove dirt or substances from a surface
  • synonym – smear: to spread a substance, especially in a messy way
