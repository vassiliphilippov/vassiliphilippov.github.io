yard

noun
railway area for trains and cars
Pronunciation: / jɑrd / (AmE), / jɑːd / (BrE)
an area with a network of railway tracks and sidings used for storing, sorting, or maintaining train cars and engines.

Examples

railway yard operations
A new maintenance yard was constructed to handle increased rail traffic.
The freight train was waiting in the yard for several hours before being dispatched.
Workers inspected the locomotives in the railway yard every morning.
