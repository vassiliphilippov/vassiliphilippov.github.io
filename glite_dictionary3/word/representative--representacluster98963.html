representative

noun
person acting on behalf of others
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprəˈzɛntətɪv /; / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person who acts or speaks on behalf of others, especially in an official capacity
