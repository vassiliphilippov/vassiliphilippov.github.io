applier

noun
person who submits an application
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪ.ər / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person who requests or seeks something, such as a job, assistance, or admission, by submitting an application

Examples

job applier
Each applier must submit their documents before the application deadline.
The applier should have relevant experience and strong communication skills.
The company received more than a hundred appliers for the single position.
