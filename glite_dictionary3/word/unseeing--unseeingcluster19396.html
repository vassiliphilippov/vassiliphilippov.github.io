unseeing

adjective
not consciously observing; not perceiving
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsiːɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːɪŋ / (BrE)
not consciously noticing or registering what is before one's eyes; not observing or perceiving

Examples

unseeing stare
His unseeing eyes stared out the window as he replayed the news in his mind.
She wandered the street with an unseeing gaze, lost in her thoughts.
The child sat unseeing at the breakfast table, too tired to notice anything around her.

  • synonym – blind: unable to see; lacking sight
