latchet

noun
leather strap for fastening footwear
Pronunciation: / ˈlætʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈlatʃɪt / (BrE)
a narrow leather strap or thong, especially one used to fasten a sandal or shoe to the foot

Examples

latchet on a sandal
Ancient Roman footwear often featured a simple leather latchet to secure the shoe.
He repaired his sandal by replacing the broken latchet.
She loosened the latchet on her shoe after a long day.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lace: a cord or leather strip passed through eyelets or hooks to fasten a shoe
