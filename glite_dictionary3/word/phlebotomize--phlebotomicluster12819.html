to phlebotomize

verb
draw blood from a vein
Pronunciation: / fləˈbɑtəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / flɪˈbɒtəˌmaɪz / (BrE)
to draw blood from a person's vein for medical purposes

Examples

to phlebotomize a patient

The nurse will phlebotomize you to obtain a blood sample for the lab tests.
Years ago, doctors would frequently phlebotomize patients as a treatment for many diseases.
