dour

adjective
sternly determined; unyielding
Pronunciation: / daʊr / (AmE), / ˈdʊə ˈdaʊə / (BrE)
Showing a stern and persistent determination; stubbornly unyielding in attitude or action.

Examples

dour determination
Despite countless setbacks, his dour commitment to the cause never wavered.
Her dour refusal to negotiate surprised everyone in the room.
The dour judge was unmoved by their emotional pleas for mercy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stubborn: unwilling to change one's attitude or position
  • synonym – tenacious: holding firmly to something; persistent
  • antonym – yielding: inclined to give in; not stubborn or firm
