request

noun
an act of asking for something
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkwɛst / (AmE), / rɪˈkwɛst / (BrE)
an act of asking for something, especially in a polite or formal way; a formal message or petition

Examples

make a request
At her request, the meeting was postponed until next week.
He made a formal request for additional funding.
The waiter responded quickly to our request for more water.
She submitted an official request to access the documents.
The committee denied his request for more funding.
You must put the request in writing to the managing director.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – demand: an insistent and forceful request
  • antonym – offer: an act of presenting something for acceptance or rejection
  • synonym – petition: a formal written request, typically one signed by many people
  • degree higher – plea: an emotional or urgent request
  • degree lower – suggestion: an act of suggesting rather than formally requesting
