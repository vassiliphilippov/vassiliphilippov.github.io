chalet

noun
mountain house with sloping roof; Swiss style cottage
Pronunciation: / ʃæˈleɪ / (AmE), / ʃæˈleɪ / (BrE)
A wooden house or cottage with a sloping roof and wide eaves, typically found in Switzerland or built in that style, often used by holidaymakers in mountainous regions.

Examples

Swiss chalet style
The village is filled with wooden chalets decorated with colorful flower boxes.
They designed their new home to resemble a traditional Swiss chalet.
We stayed in a cozy chalet overlooking the alpine valley during our ski holiday.
