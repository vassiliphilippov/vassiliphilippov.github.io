to glass

verb
put in a glass container
Pronunciation: / ɡlæs / (AmE), / ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
to put or place (something) in a glass container, such as a bottle or jar, typically for storage or preservation

Examples

to glass the jam
After preparing the jam, she carefully glassed it for long-term storage in the cellar.
The company glasses fresh juice to preserve its flavor and quality.
They are glassing the honey today to get it ready for sale at the market.
