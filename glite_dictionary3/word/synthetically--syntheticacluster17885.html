synthetically

adverb
in an artificial or synthesized way
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / sɪnˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
by means of synthesis; in a way that involves artificial or chemical production rather than occurring naturally.

Examples

synthetically produced drugs
Insulin can now be produced synthetically for diabetic patients worldwide.
Many fragrances used in perfumes are created synthetically rather than extracted from natural sources.
These materials are not found in nature and must be manufactured synthetically in laboratories.
