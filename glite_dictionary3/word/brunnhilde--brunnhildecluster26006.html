Brunnhilde

proper noun
mythical Valkyrie and queen in German legend
Pronunciation: / brʊnˈhɪldə / (AmE), / brʊnˈhɪldə / (BrE)
A Valkyrie in Norse legend and a queen in the German epic Nibelungenlied, known for her love of Siegfried, whom she had killed after deceit and then took her own life.

Brunnhilde the Valkyrie queen
