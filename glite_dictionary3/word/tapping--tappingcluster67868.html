tapping

noun
sound of light blows or knocks
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæpɪŋ / (BrE)
a light, repeated sound made by gentle blows or knocks

Examples

soft tapping sound
A faint tapping came from the other side of the wall.
He recognized the tapping of rain on the roof during the night.
The tapping at the window woke her from a deep sleep.
