Arabian

noun
a breed of horse from Arabia
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (BrE)
a breed of horse known for its spirited, graceful, and intelligent nature, originally from the Arabian Peninsula

Examples

ride an Arabian horse
Many horse shows feature an Arabian class to celebrate the breed's elegance.
She rode her favorite Arabian across the open fields at dusk.
The Arabian is prized all over the world for its endurance and beauty.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Arabian horse: a horse of the Arabian breed
  • antonym – thoroughbred: a breed of horse developed for racing, different from Arabian
