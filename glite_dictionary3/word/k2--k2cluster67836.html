K2

proper noun
the world's second-highest mountain
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪ ˈtu / (AmE), / ˌkeɪ ˈtuː / (BrE)
a mountain in the Karakoram Range on the border between Pakistan and China, recognized as the world's second-highest peak at 8,611 meters (28,251 feet).

An expedition to K2 requires careful planning and expert mountaineering skills.
