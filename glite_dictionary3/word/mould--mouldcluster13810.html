to mould

verb
to become covered with mould
Pronunciation: / moʊld / (AmE), / məʊld / (BrE)
(of food or organic matter) to become covered with mould due to humidity or age; to spoil as a result of developing mould.

Examples

to mould old bread
Don't let the old fruit mould in the bowl.
If you leave bread out in the humid air, it will soon mould.
The books moulded after being stored in the damp basement for months.
