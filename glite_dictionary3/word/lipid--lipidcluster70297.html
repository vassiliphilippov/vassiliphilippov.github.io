lipid

noun
fat-like organic substance in cells
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪp.ɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪp.ɪd / (BrE)
any of a group of oily organic compounds that are insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents, and are essential structural components of living cells
