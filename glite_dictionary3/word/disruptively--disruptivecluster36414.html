disruptively

adverb
in a disruptive manner
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈrʌptɪvli / (AmE), / dɪsˈrʌptɪvli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes disruption or disturbance.

Examples

to speak disruptively
She was asked to leave the meeting after shouting disruptively at her colleagues.
Some innovations can be introduced disruptively, challenging the status quo in the industry.
The students behaved disruptively during the assembly, making it difficult for the speaker to continue.

Related Terms

  • antonym – constructively: in a helpful or productive manner
