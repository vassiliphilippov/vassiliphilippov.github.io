aiglet

noun
decorative tag or tip on a cord, often on uniforms
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪɡlət / (AmE), / ˈeɪɡlɪt / (BrE)
a decorative metal tag or tip used to fasten or adorn a cord or braid, often found on the shoulder of military uniforms
