Oceanic

noun
eastern subfamily of Malayo-Polynesian languages
Pronunciation: / oʊˈʃænɪk / (AmE), / əʊˈsiːənɪk / (BrE)
a group of languages constituting the eastern subfamily of the Malayo-Polynesian family, spoken in the islands of the Pacific Ocean
