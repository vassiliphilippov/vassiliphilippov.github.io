to shag

verb
to perform the shag dance
Pronunciation: / ʃæɡ / (AmE), / ʃaɡ / (BrE)
To dance the shag, a lively partner dance characterized by quick steps and associated with swing music, particularly in the United States.

Examples

to shag at parties
Every summer, festival-goers gather to shag to live beach music.
I've never learned how to shag, but it looks like fun.
They decided to shag all night at the beach club's swing event.
