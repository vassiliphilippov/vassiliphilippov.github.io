Jean Arp

proper noun
Alsatian Dadaist artist and poet
Pronunciation: / ʒɑn ɑrp / (AmE), / ʒɑ̃ aʁp / (BrE)
(1887–1966) Alsatian artist and poet, cofounder of Dadaism in Zurich, known for his abstract organic sculptures.
