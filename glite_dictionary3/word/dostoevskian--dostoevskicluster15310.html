Dostoevskian

adjective
relating to Dostoevsky or his style
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑːstoʊˈjɛfskiən / (AmE), / ˌdɒstɔɪˈɛfskiən / (BrE)
Of or relating to Feodor Dostoevsky, his works, or his distinctive literary style, especially characterized by psychological depth, exploration of moral dilemmas, and intense human suffering.

Examples

Dostoevskian novel themes
Critics noted the Dostoevskian atmosphere, marked by philosophical debates and suffering.
His characters often endure Dostoevskian struggles with morality and inner conflict.
The novel was praised for its Dostoevskian exploration of guilt and redemption.
