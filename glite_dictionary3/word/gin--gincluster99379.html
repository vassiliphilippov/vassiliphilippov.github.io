gin

noun
a snare or trap for small animals
Pronunciation: / dʒɪn / (AmE), / dʒɪn / (BrE)
a trap for catching birds or small animals, often consisting of a slip noose

Examples

set a gin trap
He found an old gin tangled in the bushes behind the shed.
Small mammals are often caught using a simple gin with a slip noose.
