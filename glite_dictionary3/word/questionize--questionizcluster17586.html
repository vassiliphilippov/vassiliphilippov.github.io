to questionize

verb
to critically challenge or examine beliefs or ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛstʃəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkwɛstʃəˌnaɪz / (BrE)

She often questionizes company policies she disagrees with.
