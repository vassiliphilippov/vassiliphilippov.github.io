cascara

noun
coffee cherry husks; tea-like drink
Pronunciation: / kæsˈkɑːrə / (AmE), / kæˈskɑːrə / (BrE)
the dried husks (skins) of coffee cherries, or a tea-like beverage brewed from those husks.

Examples

cascara coffee tea
She ordered a glass of cascara, a tea-like drink brewed from coffee cherry husks.
At the café, he tried cascara for the first time and enjoyed its sweet, fruity flavor.
Local roasters sell dried cascara to brew at home like an herbal tea.
