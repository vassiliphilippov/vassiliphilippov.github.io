disconnection

noun
state of being disconnected
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəˈnɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəˈnɛkʃ(ə)n /; / ˌdɪskəˈnɛkʃən / (BrE)
the state or act of being separated or breaking a connection between things

Examples

power disconnection
Some people experience a sense of disconnection from their work or community.
The faulty cable caused the disconnection of the internet service.
There was a sudden disconnection in the power supply during the storm.

Internet disconnection can be frustrating during important online meetings.
She felt an emotional disconnection from her coworkers after moving to a different department.
