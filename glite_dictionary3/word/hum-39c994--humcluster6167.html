to hum

verb
make a low steady sound
Pronunciation: / hʌm / (AmE), / hʌm / (BrE)
to make a low, continuous, steady sound (like a machine, bee, or background noise)

Examples

hum like machines
As you entered the room, you could hear the computers humming in the background.
Everything in the house was silent except for the refrigerator, which continued to hum.
The old air conditioner would always hum softly throughout the night.
A distant machine hummed throughout the night, lulling him to sleep.
Bees hum busily in the garden during spring.
The refrigerator began to hum as soon as it was plugged in.
