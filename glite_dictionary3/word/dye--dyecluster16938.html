to dye

verb
apply color to a specimen for study
Pronunciation: / daɪ / (AmE), / daɪ / (BrE)
to apply a coloring substance to a specimen, especially for microscopic study

Examples

to dye microscope slides
Histologists commonly dye tissue slices before examining them for abnormalities.
Special solutions are used to dye bacteria so that their structures can be observed clearly.
