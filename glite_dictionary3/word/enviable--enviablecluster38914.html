enviable

adjective
deserving to be envied
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnviəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɛnviəb(ə)l / (BrE)
Deserving to be desired or envied; likely to cause envy.

Examples

enviable position
He found himself in the enviable position of being able to choose between two great job offers.
Her enviable success inspired both admiration and jealousy among her peers.
She enjoyed an enviable reputation for her intelligence and charm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – desirable: worth having or wanting
  • antonym – unenviable: not desirable or likely to cause envy
