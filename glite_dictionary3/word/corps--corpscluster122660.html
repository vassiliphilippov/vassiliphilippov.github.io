corps

noun
organized group of people with a shared activity
Pronunciation: / kɔːr / (AmE), / kɔː / (BrE)
a group of people engaged in a particular activity or working together as an organized body, especially within a profession or organization

Examples

press corps

She was honored to join the diplomatic corps after years of training.
The corps of engineers worked tirelessly on the new highway project.
