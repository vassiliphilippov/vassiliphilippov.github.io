to salve

verb
apply a healing ointment
Pronunciation: / sæv / (AmE), / sɑːv / (BrE)
to apply a salve or ointment to (a wound or area), usually to promote healing or soothe pain

Examples

to salve a wound
After the rash appeared, she immediately salved her skin to soothe the irritation.
Ancient remedies often involved salving wounds with herbal ointments.
The nurse carefully salved the child's scraped knee to prevent infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anoint: to smear or rub with oil or an oily substance, especially for ritual purposes or healing
  • degree higher – heal: to make or become healthy or whole again
