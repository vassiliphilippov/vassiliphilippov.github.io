fierce

adjective
intense and violent in force or energy
Pronunciation: / fɪrs / (AmE), / fɪəs / (BrE)
having or showing strong intensity, aggression, force, or passion (in people, emotions, weather, or competition)

Examples

fierce competition

A fierce storm disrupted flights across the region.
The argument erupted into a fierce shouting match.
