to devein

verb
remove the vein from a shrimp
Pronunciation: / diˈveɪn / (AmE), / diːˈveɪn / (BrE)
to remove the dark dorsal vein from a shrimp before cooking

Examples

to devein a shrimp
After peeling, he started deveining the prawns as instructed.
Be sure to devein the shrimp before adding them to the stir-fry.
She patiently deveined each shrimp for the seafood salad.
