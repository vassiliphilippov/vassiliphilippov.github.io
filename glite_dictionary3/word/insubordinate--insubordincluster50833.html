insubordinate

adjective
refusing to obey authority
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnsəˈbɔrdnət / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəˈbɔːdɪnət /; / ˌɪnsəˈbɔːdnət / (BrE)
refusing to obey orders or show respect for someone in authority; disobedient
