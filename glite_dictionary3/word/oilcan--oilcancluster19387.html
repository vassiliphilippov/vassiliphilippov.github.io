oilcan

noun
container for applying oil to machinery
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪlˌkæn / (AmE), / ˈɔɪlˌkan / (BrE)
A container with a long, narrow spout used for storing and applying oil to machinery and other mechanical parts.

Examples

oilcan for machines
After filling the oilcan, she went around oiling each squeaky wheel.
He kept an old oilcan on the workshop shelf for greasing the engine parts.
The mechanic carefully used a oilcan to lubricate the machine's hinges.
