dressing

noun
fertilizer or manure applied to soil
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A substance, such as fertilizer or manure, applied to soil to make it more fertile.

Examples

soil dressing fertilizer
After sowing, a quick dressing of fertilizer can greatly boost plant growth.
Gardeners often add compost as a dressing for their vegetable beds.
The wheat crop responded well to the top dressing of manure applied in early spring.
