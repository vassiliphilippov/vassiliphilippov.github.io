currant

noun
small dried seedless raisin used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝː.ənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌr.ənt / (BrE)
a small dried seedless raisin, especially of a variety grown in the Mediterranean region and California, used in cooking and baking

Examples

dried currant pieces
She sprinkled a handful of currants over her oatmeal for extra sweetness.
The recipe calls for soaked currants to provide a burst of tartness.
Traditional scones often include dried currants for added flavor.
