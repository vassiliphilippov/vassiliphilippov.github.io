gollop

noun
large mouthful or spoonful of food
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑl.əp / (AmE), / ˈɡɒl.əp / (BrE)
a large mouthful, spoonful, or serving of food or drink, taken quickly or greedily

Examples

gollop of stew
After hiking for hours, they each enjoyed a hearty gollop of stew.
He finished the milk in one big gollop.
She took a gollop of tea before rushing out the door.
