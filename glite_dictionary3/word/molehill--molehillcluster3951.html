molehill

noun
mound of earth made by a mole
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊlˌhɪl / (AmE), / ˈməʊlˌhɪl / (BrE)
A small mound of earth raised on the surface of the ground by a mole burrowing underneath.

Examples

a molehill in the grass
Gardeners often remove every molehill to keep their lawns neat.
The molehill looked tiny compared to the nearby anthill.
We found a fresh molehill in the middle of the lawn this morning.
