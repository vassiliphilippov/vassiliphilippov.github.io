misbehaviour

noun
improper or immoral behaviour
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
Improper, unacceptable, or immoral behaviour.

Examples

The teacher was frustrated by the constant misbehaviour in the classroom.
