to interpret

verb
explain or clarify meaning
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɚprət /; / ɪnˈtɝːprət / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprət /; / ɪnˈtɜːprɪt / (BrE)
to explain or decide the meaning of something, such as words, actions, or events

Examples
