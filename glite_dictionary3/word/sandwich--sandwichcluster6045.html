sandwich

noun
bread slices with a filling
Pronunciation: / ˈsændwɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsænwɪdʒ / (BrE)
Two or more slices of bread with a filling such as meat, cheese, or vegetables between them, eaten as a snack or meal.

Examples

a sandwich with cheese
He made a delicious sandwich with turkey and cheese for lunch.
She quickly packed a ham sandwich for her picnic.
Would you like a sandwich or a salad for your meal?
