to fluoridise

verb
to treat with fluoride
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrɪˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈflʊərɪdaɪz / (BrE)
to treat with fluoride, especially to add fluoride to water or teeth to prevent decay

Examples

to fluoridise drinking water
Dental professionals may recommend products that fluoridise children's teeth.
Some communities debate whether to fluoridise their drinking water.
The city council voted to fluoridise the public water supply last year.
