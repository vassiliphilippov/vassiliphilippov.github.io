slam

noun
insulting or critical remark
Pronunciation: / slæm / (AmE), / slæm / (BrE)
an insulting or aggressive remark intended to hurt or criticize someone

Examples

a slam against rivals
He couldn't resist delivering a slam when asked about his rival.
Her latest slam against her colleague caused quite a stir in the office.
The comedian's routine was full of clever slams at politicians.
