Chou En-lai

proper noun
Chinese communist leader and premier (1898–1976)
Pronunciation: / ʧoʊ ɛnˈlaɪ / (AmE), / ʧəʊ ɛnˈlaɪ / (BrE)
(1898–1976) Chinese revolutionary and leading communist statesman who served as premier of the People's Republic of China from 1949 to 1976.
