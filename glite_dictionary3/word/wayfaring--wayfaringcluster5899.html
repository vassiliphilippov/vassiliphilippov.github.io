wayfaring

adjective
traveling, especially on foot
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪˌfɛr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪˌfeə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
traveling, especially by walking from place to place

Examples

wayfaring traveler
During her youth, she lived a wayfaring life, exploring distant lands on foot.
The wayfaring monk wandered from village to village, sharing his wisdom along the road.
They welcomed the wayfaring travelers who arrived at their cottage, offering them food and shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym – nomadic: moving from place to place without a fixed home
  • synonym – peripatetic: traveling from place to place, especially for work
