gravelly

adjective
harsh or grating (voice or sound)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrævəli / (AmE), / ˈɡravəli / (BrE)
(of a voice or sound) unpleasantly harsh or grating.

Examples

gravelly voice sound
His gravelly laugh echoed through the quiet room, making everyone turn their heads.
She spoke with a gravelly tone after years of smoking.
The old man's gravelly voice made him sound intimidating to the children.
