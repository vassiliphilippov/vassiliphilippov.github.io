transcriber

noun
person who writes down spoken material
Pronunciation: / trænˈskaɪbɚ /; / trænˈskraɪbər /; / trænˈskaɪbər / (AmE), / trænˈskraɪbə /; / trænˈskaɪbə / (BrE)
a person who converts spoken words or text into written form (including making transcripts, phonetic notation, or rewriting in another script)

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym – scribe: a person who copies written documents
  • synonym – transcriptionist: person who transcribes speech to text, especially in professional settings
  • synonym – translator: a person who translates text into another language
