snappy

adjective
briskly cold and invigorating (weather)
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpi / (AmE), / ˈsnæpi / (BrE)
(of weather or air) briskly cold in a way that feels invigorating and refreshing.

The air was snappy this morning, perfect for a brisk walk.
