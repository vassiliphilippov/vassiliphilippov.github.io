foul

adjective
unfair or against the rules
Pronunciation: / faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
violating accepted standards or rules, especially in sports or competition; unfair or unsporting.

Examples

foul tactics
His foul tactics in business quickly earned him a bad reputation.
The player was penalized for making a foul tackle during the match.
The referee called a foul play when the runner was tripped intentionally.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fair: in accordance with rules or standards; legitimate
