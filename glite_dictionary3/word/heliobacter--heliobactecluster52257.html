Heliobacter

noun
genus of bacteria found in gastric mucosa
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːli.oʊˈbæktər / (AmE), / ˌhiːlɪ.əʊˈbæktə /; / ˌhiːli.əˈbæktə / (BrE)
a genus of bacteria with a helical or curved shape, found in the stomach lining of primates, including humans
