fangirl

noun
female fan, highly enthusiastic (esp. pop culture)
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈfænˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
A female fan who is extremely enthusiastic and sometimes obsessively interested in a particular person, group, or field, especially in entertainment or pop culture.

Examples

dedicated fangirl of a band
As a proud fangirl, she collects every piece of merchandise related to the band.
She became a total fangirl when she met her favorite actor at the convention.
The online forum is filled with fangirl debates about the latest comic book adaptation.
