firewall

noun
fire-resistant barrier in buildings or vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪrˌwɔl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəwɔːl / (BrE)
A fireproof or fire-resistant wall designed to prevent the spread of fire within a building or vehicle.

Examples

firewall between rooms
After the accident, inspectors checked the firewall to make sure it had not been damaged.
The building code requires a firewall between the garage and the living space.
The engineers installed a steel firewall to protect the cabin from engine fires.
