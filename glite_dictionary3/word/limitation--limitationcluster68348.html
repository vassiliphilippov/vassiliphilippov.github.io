limitation

noun
the state of being limited or restricted
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪməˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the condition or quality of being limited or restricted; a constraint or shortcoming

Examples

physical limitation
Despite his talent, his physical limitation kept him from competing at the highest level.
The new law imposes several limitations on what businesses are allowed to do.
There are some technical limitations that we need to consider before proceeding with the project.
