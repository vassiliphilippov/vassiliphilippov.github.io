second-string

adjective
replacement or reserve (team context)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd ˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsɛkənd ˈstrɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or player) belonging to a team as a replacement or substitute, not among the regular members.

Examples

second-string player
Despite being a second-string player, she scored the winning goal.
He was promoted from the second-string to the starting lineup after the injury.
The second-string quarterback showed impressive skills during practice.

Related Terms

  • antonym – first-string: regular or starting member of a team
