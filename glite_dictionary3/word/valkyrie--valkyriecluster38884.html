Valkyrie

noun
maiden in Norse myth who leads slain heroes to Valhalla
Pronunciation: / vælˈkɪri / (AmE), / vælˈkɪəri / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) one of Odin's maidens who chooses those to be slain in battle and leads them to Valhalla.

Examples

Valkyrie chooses warriors
In the ancient saga, a Valkyrie appeared on the battlefield to guide fallen warriors to Valhalla.
Legends describe each Valkyrie as both a fierce fighter and a mystical escort for heroic souls.
The opera featured a powerful scene in which the Valkyrie flew through the stormy skies.
