statement

noun
official record of account transactions
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an official document showing credits and debits in an account, typically sent by a bank or other financial institution

Examples

bank statement

She noticed an error on her credit card statement and contacted the bank immediately.
