throat

noun
passage in neck to stomach and lungs
Pronunciation: / θroʊt / (AmE), / θrəʊt / (BrE)
the passage in the front part of the neck (in humans or animals) through which food and air pass

Examples

sore throat
He cleared his throat before beginning his speech.
Her throat was sore from coughing all night.
She wore a scarf around her throat to keep warm during the winter.
A cat's throat vibrates when it purrs.
The bird had a distinctive red patch on its throat.
The veterinarian checked the dog's throat for signs of infection.
