sodding

adjective
intensifier expressing annoyance or emphasis
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɒdɪŋ / (BrE)
(especially British, informal) Used as an intensifier to express annoyance or emphasis, often with mild irritation; sometimes considered mildly vulgar.
