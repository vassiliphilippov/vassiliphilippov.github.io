forester

noun
person trained in managing forests
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.ə.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɒr.ɪ.stə / (BrE)

Examples

forester manages forests
After studying environmental science, she became a forester in a national park.
The forester explained the importance of sustainable management to protect wildlife and resources.
The forester oversaw the replanting of trees after the forest fire.
