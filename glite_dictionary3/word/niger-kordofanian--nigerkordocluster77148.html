Niger-Kordofanian

noun
major sub-Saharan African language family
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪdʒər kɔrˌdəˈfeɪniən / (AmE), / ˌniːdʒə ˌkɔːdəˈfeɪniən / (BrE)
a major family of languages spoken in sub-Saharan Africa, including most languages south of the Sahara; many are tonal, though some, like Swahili or Fula, are not

Examples

Niger-Kordofanian languages group

Research into Niger-Kordofanian languages has greatly expanded our understanding of African linguistics.
The Niger-Kordofanian languages are spoken across a vast region of sub-Saharan Africa.
