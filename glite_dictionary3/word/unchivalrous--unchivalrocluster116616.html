unchivalrous

adjective
not courteous or gentlemanly
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɪvəlrəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɪvəlrəs / (BrE)
showing a lack of courtesy or gentlemanly behavior; discourteous or ungallant.

Examples

unchivalrous behavior
His unchivalrous remarks offended several people at the dinner table.
It was unchivalrous of him to ignore her request for help.
Such unchivalrous behavior is frowned upon in polite society.

Related Terms

  • antonym – chivalrous: polite, courteous, especially toward women; gallant
  • synonym – discourteous: rude or lacking courtesy
