Anobiidae

noun
family of wood-boring beetles
Pronunciation: / əˌnoʊˈbaɪɪˌdiː /; / əˌnoʊ.biˈɪd.iː / (AmE), / aˌnəʊˈbɪɪdiː /; / əˌnəʊ.bɪˈɪd.iː / (BrE)
