well-worn

adjective
showing signs of much use
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛlˈwɔrn / (AmE), / ˌwɛlˈwɔːn / (BrE)
showing clear signs of heavy use or handling, often from being used many times

Examples

well-worn jacket
His well-worn jacket had several patches and faded colors.
She picked up her well-worn copy of the novel and started reading again.
The well-worn path through the forest showed that many people had traveled there.
