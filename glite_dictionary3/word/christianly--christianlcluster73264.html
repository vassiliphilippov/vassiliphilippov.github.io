christianly

adjective
in a manner befitting a Christian
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstʃənli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃənli / (BrE)
In a manner that is characteristic of or appropriate to a Christian; befitting a Christian.

Examples

christianly behavior
His actions reflected his christianly values, even in difficult situations.
She always treats others in a christianly way, showing kindness and forgiveness.
They responded to criticism with a christianly patience and humility.
