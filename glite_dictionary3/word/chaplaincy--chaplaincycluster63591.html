chaplaincy

noun
position or office of a chaplain
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæplənsi / (AmE), / ˈtʃæplənsi / (BrE)
the office, position, or period of service of a chaplain, especially within an institution such as a school, hospital, prison, or military organization

Examples

chaplaincy at a hospital
His chaplaincy in the military lasted for over a decade.
She completed her training and began her chaplaincy at the local hospital.
The college offers a chaplaincy program to support students' spiritual needs.
