misanthropist

noun
person who dislikes humankind
Pronunciation: / mɪˈsænθrəpɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈsænθrəpɪst / (BrE)
A person who dislikes or distrusts other people or humankind in general.

Examples

reclusive misanthropist
After years of disappointment, she became something of a misanthropist.
Despite his reputation as a misanthropist, he cared deeply for animals.
The misanthropist avoided social gatherings and preferred solitude.

Related Terms

  • synonym – misanthropy: a dislike or distrust of humankind
  • antonym – philanthropist: a person who seeks to promote the welfare of others
