West African

noun
languages of the far western West Africa region
Pronunciation: / wɛst ˈæfrɪkən / (AmE), / west ˈæfrɪkən / (BrE)
a group of languages spoken in the far western region of West Africa, including languages such as Yoruba and Wolof.
