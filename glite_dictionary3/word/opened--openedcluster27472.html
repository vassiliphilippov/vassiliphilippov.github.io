opened

adjective
not closed (of mouth or eyes)
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənd / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənd / (BrE)
(of the mouth or eyes) not closed; in a state of being apart or uncovered.

Examples

opened eyes wide
His opened mouth suggested he was about to speak but hesitated.
She stared at him with her opened eyes, wide with surprise.
The baby slept with her lips slightly opened.
