snuff

noun
act of inhaling an odor
Pronunciation: / snʌf / (AmE), / snʌf / (BrE)
the act of sensing or inhaling an odor through the nose

Examples

a snuff of air
He took a cautious snuff to check for any gas leak.
The dog gave a quick snuff at the stranger before barking.
With a soft snuff, she detected the aroma of fresh bread.
