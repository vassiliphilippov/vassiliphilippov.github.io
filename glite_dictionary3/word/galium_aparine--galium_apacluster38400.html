Galium aparine

noun
a prickly-stemmed annual plant species
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪliəm əˈpɛrɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡalɪəm əˈparɪni / (BrE)
A species of annual plant in the bedstraw family, with stems covered in curved prickles, found in North America, Europe, and Asia; also known as cleavers or goosegrass.
