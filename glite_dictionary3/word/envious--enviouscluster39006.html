envious

adjective
feeling jealousy for others' advantages
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnviəs / (AmE), / ˈɛnviəs / (BrE)
feeling or showing a desire for what someone else has; jealous of another's advantages or possessions

Examples

to feel envious of friends
Looking at the beautiful garden, he couldn't help but feel envious.
She was envious of her colleague's promotion and new responsibilities.
Their luxurious lifestyle made many people envious of their success.

Related Terms

  • synonym – covetous: having or showing a great desire to possess something, typically something belonging to someone else
  • antonym – generous: showing a readiness to give more of something, especially money, than is strictly necessary or expected
  • synonym – jealous: feeling or showing envy of someone or their achievements and advantages
