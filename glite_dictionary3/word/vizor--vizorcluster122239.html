vizor

noun
projecting brim to shade the eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.zər / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.zə / (BrE)
A projecting brim or shield on a cap, helmet, or similar headwear designed to shade or protect the eyes.

Examples

vizor on a cap
He lowered the vizor of his cap as the sun grew stronger.
My old cycling hat had a small vizor that helped block the glare.
The football player's helmet included a tinted vizor for extra eye protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym – visor: projecting front part on a cap or helmet to protect the eyes
