cordially

adverb
in a warm and friendly manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdʒəli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdiəli / (BrE)
In a warm, friendly, or sincere manner; with heartfelt friendliness or goodwill.

Examples

cordially welcome guests
She cordially greeted each guest as they arrived at the party.
The two leaders shook hands cordially at the press conference.
We were cordially invited to the opening ceremony.
