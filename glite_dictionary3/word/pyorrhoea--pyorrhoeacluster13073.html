pyorrhoea

noun
discharge of pus, especially from gums
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪəˈrioʊə /; / paɪəˈriə / (AmE), / ˌpaɪəˈriːə /; / ˌpaɪ.əˈriː.ə / (BrE)
a serious gum disease that causes pus to discharge from the gums (also called chronic periodontitis)
