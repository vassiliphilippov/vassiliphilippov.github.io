nonsteroidal

adjective
not containing steroids
Pronunciation: / nɒnˈstɛrɔɪdəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈstɪərəʊdəl / (BrE)
not containing steroids or not having the effects of steroid hormones.

Examples

nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
Doctors often prescribe nonsteroidal medications to reduce inflammation without the side effects of steroids.
She was advised to take a nonsteroidal drug for her chronic pain.
Some nonsteroidal therapies are preferred for patients sensitive to steroids.
