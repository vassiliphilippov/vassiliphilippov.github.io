to gamble

verb
take a risk in hopes of success
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæmbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡæmbəl / (BrE)
to take a risk in the hope of a favorable or advantageous outcome, especially in situations involving uncertainty

Examples

to gamble on success
Every time you make a bold career change, you gamble with your future.
Investing in a startup can be a risky move, but many entrepreneurs are willing to gamble on success.
She decided to gamble her savings on a new business idea.
