drafting

noun
writing a preliminary version
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of preparing a preliminary version of a piece of writing, usually to be revised and improved later

Examples

drafting an essay
During drafting, try not to worry about making everything perfect.
Good drafting is essential before submitting any important report.
The novelist spent weeks drafting the first chapters of her new book.

Related Terms

  • synonym – editing: the process of making changes and corrections to improve a text
  • degree higher – finalizing: the process of completing and approving a final version
  • synonym – revision: the process of making changes to an earlier draft to improve it
