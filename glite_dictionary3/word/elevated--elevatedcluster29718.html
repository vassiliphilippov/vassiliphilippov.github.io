elevated

noun
overhead urban railway line
Pronunciation: / ˌɛləˈveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪˈveɪtɪd / (BrE)
(North American) an elevated railway, especially one powered by electricity that runs on a track above street level.
