sinopia

noun
red ocher pigment
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnoʊpiə / (AmE), / sɪˈnəʊpiə / (BrE)
A reddish-brown natural earth pigment, consisting of red ocher, formerly used for underdrawings and frescoes.
