Galbulidae

noun
family of birds (jacamars)
Pronunciation: / gælˈbjuːlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / gælˈbjuːlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of tropical American birds known as jacamars, characterized by their slender bodies and long, sharp bills.

The Galbulidae are well known for their iridescent plumage and insect-eating habits.
