tandoor

noun
traditional clay oven from South Asia
Pronunciation: / tænˈdʊr / (AmE), / tænˈdʊə / (BrE)
a cylindrical clay oven, traditionally used in northern India and Pakistan for baking bread and roasting meats

Examples

tandoor oven bread
