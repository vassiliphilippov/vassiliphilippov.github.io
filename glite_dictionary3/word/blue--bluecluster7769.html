blue

adjective
of noble or aristocratic descent
Pronunciation: / bluː / (AmE), / bluː / (BrE)
(of a person or family) belonging to or characteristic of the nobility; aristocratic.

Examples

His blue lineage was a source of pride in the small town.
They belonged to a blue family with a long history of wealth and power.
