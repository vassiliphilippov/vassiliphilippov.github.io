athwart

adverb
at an oblique angle; diagonally
Pronunciation: / əˈθwɔrt / (AmE), / əˈθwɔːt / (BrE)
at an oblique or slanting angle; diagonally across something

Examples

light athwart a room
A beam of sunlight shot athwart the dusty attic, illuminating hidden corners.
She cast her scarf athwart her shoulders before stepping outside.
The curtain fell athwart the doorway, blocking the fading light from the hall.

  • synonym – aslant: at a slant; obliquely
