framboesia

noun
infectious tropical disease with skin lesions
Pronunciation: / fræmˈbiːziə / (AmE), / fræmˈbiːzɪə / (BrE)
An infectious tropical disease, similar to syphilis in its early stages, characterized by red skin eruptions and ulcerating lesions.

Examples

treat framboesia infection
Doctors diagnosed the patient with framboesia after noticing the characteristic red skin eruptions.
Early treatment is crucial to prevent complications from framboesia.
The spread of framboesia in tropical regions remains a concern for public health authorities.
