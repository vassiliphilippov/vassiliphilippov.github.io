cryoscope

noun
instrument for measuring freezing points
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈkraɪəskəʊp / (BrE)
An instrument used to measure the freezing and melting points of liquids.

Examples

cryoscope checks freezing
A malfunction in the cryoscope led to inaccurate freezing point readings.
Scientists used a cryoscope to record the temperature at which the solution solidified.
The cryoscope is commonly used in laboratories to determine the purity of milk.
