conditioner

noun
substance used to soften hair or clothes
Pronunciation: / kənˈdɪʃənər / (AmE), / kənˈdɪʃənə / (BrE)
a substance added during washing, especially of hair or clothes, to make them softer, smoother, or in better condition

Examples

hair conditioner
After shampooing her hair, she applied conditioner to make it soft and shiny.
He forgot to buy conditioner, so his hair felt dry after washing.
Some people use fabric conditioner to give their clothes a fresh scent and smooth feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym – shampoo: cleansing substance for hair
  • synonym – softener: substance used to make things softer, especially in laundry
