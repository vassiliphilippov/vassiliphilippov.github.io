metrification

noun
adoption of the metric system
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtrəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adopting the metric system of measurement in place of imperial or other systems

Examples

metrification of measurements
Businesses faced challenges during the metrification process but eventually adapted.
Opponents of metrification argued in favor of keeping traditional units of measurement.
The government's metrification of road signs was completed over several years.

  • synonym – metrication: the process of adopting the metric system
