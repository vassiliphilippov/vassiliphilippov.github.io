exogamy

noun
marriage outside one's group or tribe
Pronunciation: / ɪgˈzɑɡəmi / (AmE), / ɛkˈsɒɡəmi / (BrE)
The custom or law of marrying outside one's social group, clan, or tribe.

Examples

exogamy in marriage customs
Anthropologists study exogamy to understand patterns of social organization.
Many indigenous cultures traditionally practiced exogamy to strengthen alliances between clans.
