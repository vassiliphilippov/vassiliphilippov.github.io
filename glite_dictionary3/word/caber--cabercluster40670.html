caber

noun
wooden pole used in Scottish Highland games
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪbər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪbə / (BrE)
a heavy, trimmed wooden pole, especially the trunk of a young tree, that is tossed as a test of strength in Scottish Highland games
