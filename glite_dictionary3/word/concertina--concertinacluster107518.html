concertina

noun
free-reed musical instrument
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsərˈtiːnə / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsəˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a small musical instrument similar to an accordion, played by pressing its ends together to force air through reeds and producing musical notes

Examples

play the concertina
He played the concertina at the local folk festival.
She bought an antique concertina from the market last weekend.
The haunting sound of the concertina filled the quiet room.
