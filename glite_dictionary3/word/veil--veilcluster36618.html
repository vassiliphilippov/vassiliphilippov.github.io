veil

noun
cloth covering for head and face
Pronunciation: / veɪl / (AmE), / veɪl / (BrE)
a piece of cloth worn to cover the head and face, often for religious, ceremonial, or privacy reasons

Examples

wedding veil
In some cultures, women choose to wear a veil for religious reasons.
She lifted her veil to greet the guests after the ceremony.
The bride wore a delicate lace veil over her face.
