cosy

adjective
warm and friendly atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊzi / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi / (BrE)
comfortable, warm, and pleasant; giving a feeling of comfort and friendliness (especially of a place or atmosphere)

Examples

cosy room
Her apartment has a cosy atmosphere with soft lighting and warm colors.
They had a cosy family gathering by the fire on a cold evening.
We spent the afternoon in a cosy café, enjoying coffee and conversation.
She wrapped a blanket around herself and settled into a cosy armchair.
The little café on the corner felt cosy and inviting during the winter storm.
We spent the evening reading books in a cosy cottage by the sea.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cold: lacking warmth or friendliness
  • synonym – comfortable: providing physical ease or relaxation
  • synonym – snug: warm, comfortable, and protected, often in a small space
