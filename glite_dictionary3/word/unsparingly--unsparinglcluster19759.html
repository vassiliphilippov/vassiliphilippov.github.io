unsparingly

adverb
in a relentless or harsh manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɛːrɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no restraint, mercy, or moderation; relentlessly or with harshness.

Examples

to criticize unsparingly
He criticized the government's response unsparingly, leaving no aspect unexamined.
She judged her own performance unsparingly, determined to learn from every mistake.
The editor spoke unsparingly of the novel's weaknesses in her review.

Related Terms

  • antonym – gently: in a kind or mild manner
  • synonym – harshly: in a severe or unkind manner
