stiltedly

adverb
in a stiff or unnatural way
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪltɪdli / (AmE), / ˈstɪltɪdli / (BrE)
In a stiff, formal, and unnatural manner.

Examples

to speak stiltedly
He spoke stiltedly during the ceremony, betraying his nervousness.
She greeted her former colleague stiltedly, unsure of what to say.
Their conversation proceeded stiltedly, lacking the warmth of old friendship.
