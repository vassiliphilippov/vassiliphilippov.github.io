gumshield

noun
mouthguard for sports
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌʃiːld / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmʃiːld / (BrE)
A protective device worn over the teeth and gums to reduce injury during contact sports, especially boxing.
