bogie

noun
unidentified or possibly enemy aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɡi / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɡi / (BrE)
an aircraft of unknown identity, often considered possibly hostile in military or aviation contexts

Examples

enemy bogie detected
