chokecherry

noun
small, sour berry from the chokecherry tree
Pronunciation: / ˈt͡ʃoʊkˌt͡ʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊkˌtʃɛri / (BrE)
The small, sour, dark red or black fruit of the North American chokecherry tree, often used in jams, jellies, and preserves.

Examples

ripe chokecherry fruit
Birds love to feast on ripe chokecherry in late summer.

The jam was made from wild chokecherry collected in the northern woods.
