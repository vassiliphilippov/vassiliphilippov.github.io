subcontractor

noun
person or company hired by a main contractor
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbkənˈtræktər / (AmE), / ˌsʌbkənˈtræktə / (BrE)
a person or company hired by a primary contractor to carry out specific tasks or services as part of a larger project

Examples

subcontractor on a project
Each subcontractor must comply with the safety regulations on the site.
Payment will be issued directly to the subcontractor upon completion of their task.
The main contractor hired a subcontractor to handle the electrical work.
