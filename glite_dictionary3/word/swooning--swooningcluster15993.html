swooning

adjective
feeling faint or dizzy
Pronunciation: / ˈswu.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswuː.nɪŋ / (BrE)
Feeling weak and likely to lose consciousness; dizzy or faint.

Examples

swooning from heat
After running in the heat, she was swooning and needed to sit down.
He stood swooning at the sight of blood, gripping the back of a chair for support.
The crowd was swooning from exhaustion after hours in the sun.
