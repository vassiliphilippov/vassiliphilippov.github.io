Hominoidea

noun
superfamily of apes and humans
Pronunciation: / hoʊˌmɪniˈoɪdiə / (AmE), / hɒˌmɪniˈɔɪdiə / (BrE)
a biological superfamily of primates that includes all apes and humans, but not monkeys

Examples

Hominoidea superfamily group
Gorillas, chimpanzees, and humans all belong to the Hominoidea superfamily.
The evolutionary relationships among Hominoidea have been the subject of extensive scientific research.
Unlike monkeys, members of Hominoidea do not possess tails.

Related Terms
