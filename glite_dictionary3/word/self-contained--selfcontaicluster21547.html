self-contained

adjective
complete and independent unit
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf kənˈteɪnd / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf kənˈteɪnd / (BrE)
complete and independent; not needing or relying on anything else to function or exist

Examples

self-contained apartment unit

The community was designed to be self-contained, including schools, shops, and parks.
The new apartment is self-contained, with its own kitchen and bathroom.
