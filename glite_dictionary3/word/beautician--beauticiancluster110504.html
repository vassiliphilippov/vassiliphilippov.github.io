beautician

noun
person giving beauty treatments
Pronunciation: / bjuˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / bjuːˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to give beauty treatments such as hair styling, skincare, and cosmetic services, typically in a salon

Examples

beautician at a salon
Many people visit a beautician before important events to look their best.
She has worked as a beautician at the local salon for over ten years.
The beautician recommended a new facial treatment for my skin type.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cosmetologist: a person trained to provide cosmetic treatments to hair, skin, and nails
