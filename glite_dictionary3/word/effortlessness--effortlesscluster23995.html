effortlessness

noun
quality of requiring little effort
Pronunciation: / ˈɛfərtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɛfətləsnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being easy to do or requiring little or no effort.

Examples

natural effortlessness
He spoke with a effortlessness that made the language seem easy to learn.
Her dance had such effortlessness that it looked almost magical.
The effortlessness of his running style impressed the entire team.
