to interpenetrate

verb
penetrate or blend into each other
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈpɛnəˌtreɪt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈpɛnɪtreɪt / (BrE)
to mix, pass into, or spread through each other so that the parts are combined or interlocked
