wirelessness

noun
state of having no wires or cables
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪr.ləs.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə.ləs.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being wireless; absence of wires or cables, especially in communication or electronic systems.

Examples

modern wirelessness
Many modern offices prioritize wirelessness to create more flexible and tidy workspaces.
Some people doubt the true wirelessness of systems that still require occasional wired charging.
The wirelessness of new headphones makes them extremely convenient for exercising.
