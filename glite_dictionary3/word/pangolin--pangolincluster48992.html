pangolin

noun
scaly mammal that eats ants and termites
Pronunciation: / ˈpæŋɡəlɪn / (AmE), / ˈpæŋɡəlɪn / (BrE)
A nocturnal mammal found in Africa and Asia, covered with overlapping horny scales and having a long snout used to feed on ants and termites.

Examples

pangolin eats ants
Researchers are working to protect the pangolin from illegal wildlife trade.
