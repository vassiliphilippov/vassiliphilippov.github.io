Nisan

noun
month in the Hebrew calendar (March–April)
Pronunciation: / ˈniːsɑn / (AmE), / ˈniːsɑn / (BrE)
the first month of the ecclesiastical year and the seventh month of the civil year in the Hebrew calendar, occurring in March and April

Examples

Nisan in the Hebrew calendar
Jewish tradition holds that Nisan marks the anniversary of the Exodus from Egypt.
Passover begins on the fifteenth day of Nisan in the Hebrew calendar.
The month of Nisan usually falls in March or April on the Gregorian calendar.
