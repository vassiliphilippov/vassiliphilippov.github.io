to discern

verb
detect or recognize with the senses
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɜrn / (AmE), / dɪˈsɜːn / (BrE)
to detect or recognize something, especially with difficulty, by using the senses

Examples

to discern a shape
In the thick fog, it was nearly impossible to discern the outline of the building.
She could just discern a faint sound coming from the alleyway.
With his keen eyesight, he was able to discern the tiny movements of the butterfly.
