progeria

noun
rare genetic disorder causing premature aging in children
Pronunciation: / proʊˈdʒɪəriə / (AmE), / prəʊˈdʒɪəriə / (BrE)
a rare genetic disorder in children characterized by symptoms of rapid aging, such as grey hair, wrinkled skin, and a stooped posture
