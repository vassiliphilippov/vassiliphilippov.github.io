splash

noun
patch of bright colour
Pronunciation: / splæʃ / (AmE), / splæʃ / (BrE)
a small area or patch of color, or a small quantity of liquid

Examples

a splash of color
A splash of yellow flowers brightened up the garden.
Her outfit needed a splash of color to make it less plain.
The painter added a splash of bright red to the canvas.
Could you add a splash of milk to my coffee, please?
The recipe calls for just a splash of vanilla extract.
There was a splash of mud on her new shoes after the rain.
