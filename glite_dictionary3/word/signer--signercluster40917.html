signer

noun
a person who signs a document
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnə / (BrE)
a person who signs a document and is thereby bound by its contents

Examples

signer of contract
Each signer must present identification before their signature is accepted.
The signer of the contract agreed to all the listed terms and conditions.
The document requires a signer who is at least eighteen years old.

Related Terms

  • synonym – subscriber: a person who signs a document, especially a contract or official paper
  • synonym – witness: a person who observes the signing of a document and attests to it
