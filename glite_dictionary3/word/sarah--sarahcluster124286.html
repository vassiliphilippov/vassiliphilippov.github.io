Sarah

proper noun
wife of Abraham in the Old Testament
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛrə / (AmE), / ˈseərə / (BrE)
(In the Old Testament) the wife of Abraham and mother of Isaac, regarded as a matriarch in Judaism, Christianity, and Islam.
