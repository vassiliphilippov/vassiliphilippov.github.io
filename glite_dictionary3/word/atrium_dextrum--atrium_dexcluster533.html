atrium dextrum

noun
right upper chamber of the heart
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.tri.əm ˈdɛk.strəm / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.tri.əm ˈdɛk.strəm / (BrE)
the right upper chamber of the heart that receives blood from the venae cavae and coronary sinus
