dispiritedness

noun
a feeling of being discouraged or downhearted
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɪrɪtɪdnɪs / (AmE), / dɪˈspɪərɪtɪdnɪs / (BrE)
a state of feeling discouraged or lacking in enthusiasm and hope

Examples

deep dispiritedness

There was a sense of dispiritedness among the team after their defeat.
