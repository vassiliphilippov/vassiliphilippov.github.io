to compile

verb
put together information or materials
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpaɪl / (AmE), / kəmˈpaɪl / (BrE)
to collect and organize information, documents, or materials from different sources into a list, book, or report

Examples

to compile a report

She was asked to compile a detailed report using data from various departments.
The editor will compile the articles into a comprehensive magazine issue.
He compiled a list of local restaurants for tourists.
She spent years compiling data for her research project.
The editor will compile the essays into a single volume.
