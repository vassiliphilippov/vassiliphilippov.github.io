unruliness

noun
disobedience; lack of discipline
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈruːlinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈruːlɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being difficult to control or manage; disobedience and lack of discipline

Examples

child's unruliness
His persistent unruliness led to frequent detentions.
Teachers often struggle with unruliness in large classrooms.
The unruliness of the crowd made it difficult for security to maintain order.

Related Terms

  • antonym – discipline: the practice of training people to obey rules; controlled behavior
  • synonym – disobedience: refusal or failure to obey rules or someone in authority
