undesirous

adjective
not having desire; lacking desire
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪərəs / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪərəʊs / (BrE)
not having or feeling any desire for something; lacking desire

Examples

undesirous of change
Despite the tempting offer, she remained undesirous of any change in her routine.
He seemed completely undesirous of fame or recognition throughout his career.
The committee was undesirous to pursue the proposal any further.

Related Terms

  • antonym – desirous: having or showing desire; wanting something
  • synonym – indifferent: having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned
