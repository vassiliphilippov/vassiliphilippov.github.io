pivot

noun
person around whom a group maneuvers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvət / (AmE), / ˈpɪvət / (BrE)
the person in a formation or rank around whom others turn or maneuver

Examples

During the parade, the officer acted as the pivot for the entire formation.
In the drill, the pivot remained stationary while the rest of the line wheeled around.
The pivot plays a crucial role in the coordinated movement of the marching band.
