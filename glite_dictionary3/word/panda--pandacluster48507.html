panda

noun
large black-and-white mammal of China
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn.də / (AmE), / ˈpæn.də / (BrE)
A large, black-and-white bear-like mammal native to bamboo forests in China and Tibet, known for its distinctive coloring and diet of bamboo.

Examples

giant panda bamboo
Many zoos around the world have pandas as one of their main attractions.
Scientists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered panda.
The panda spends most of its day eating bamboo in the forests of China.
