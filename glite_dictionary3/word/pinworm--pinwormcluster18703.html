pinworm

noun
parasitic worm infesting intestines
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌwɝm / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a small, threadlike parasitic worm that infests the intestines and rectum, especially in children

Examples

pinworm infection
After her daughter started itching at night, the doctor diagnosed a pinworm infection.
Parents should wash bedding regularly to prevent the spread of pinworms among children.
The child was given medicine to eliminate the pinworm infestation.
