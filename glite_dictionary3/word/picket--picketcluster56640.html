picket

noun
stake or strip forming a fence
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪkɪt / (BrE)
a pointed or flat-topped stake or strip of wood, metal, or plastic fixed upright in a series to form a fence or enclosure

Examples

a picket fence
A white picket fence surrounded the cottage, giving it a classic, charming look.
Children poked their fingers between the pickets to watch the puppies on the other side.
He replaced several broken pickets after the storm damaged the backyard fence.
