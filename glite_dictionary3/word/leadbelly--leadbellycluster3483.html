Leadbelly

proper noun
famous American folk and blues singer
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdˌbɛli / (AmE), / ˈlɛdˌbɛli / (BrE)
The nickname of Huddie Ledbetter (1885–1949), a United States folk and blues singer and composer known for his powerful vocals and vast repertoire.
