GHRF

noun
hormone stimulating growth hormone release
Pronunciation: / dʒiː eɪtʃ ɑːr ɛf / (AmE), / dʒiː eɪtʃ ɑːr ɛf / (BrE)
a hormone that stimulates the anterior pituitary gland to release growth hormone
