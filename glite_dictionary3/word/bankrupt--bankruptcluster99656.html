bankrupt

noun
insolvent person or business
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋ.krʌpt / (AmE), / ˈbaŋ.krʌpt / (BrE)
a person or organization that is unable to pay their debts and has been legally declared insolvent

Examples

declared bankrupt in court
After the failed investment, Tom was declared a bankrupt by the court.
As a bankrupt, she lost control over her assets to pay off her creditors.
The company became a bankrupt following years of financial losses.

Related Terms

  • synonym – debtor: a person who owes money
  • antonym – solvent: having enough money to pay one's debts
