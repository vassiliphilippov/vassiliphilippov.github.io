severeness

noun
quality of being plain or strict
Pronunciation: / sɪˈvɪrnəs / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪənɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being very plain, simple, or lacking ornament; austerity or strictness in appearance or style.

Examples

architectural severeness
Critics admired the severeness and lack of embellishment in the artist's later works.
Despite the severeness of her dress, she carried herself with elegance.
The severeness of the building's design gave it a solemn, imposing quality.
