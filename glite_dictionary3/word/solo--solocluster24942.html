solo

noun
a flight with only the pilot on board
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a flight in which a pilot flies an aircraft alone, without any other person on board

Examples

solo flight
Every pilot remembers their very first solo as a milestone in their training.
She completed her first solo last week and received her pilot's license.
The instructor watched from the ground during his student's solo.
