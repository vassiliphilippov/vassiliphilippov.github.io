Anguis fragilis

noun
a legless European lizard (slowworm)
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡwɪs frəˈdʒɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡwɪs frəˈdʒɪlɪs / (BrE)
A small, burrowing, legless European lizard with small eyes, commonly known as the slowworm, and often mistakenly thought to be blind.
