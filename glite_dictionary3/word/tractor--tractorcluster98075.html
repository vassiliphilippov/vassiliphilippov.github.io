tractor

noun
truck cab that pulls trailers
Pronunciation: / ˈtræktər / (AmE), / ˈtræktə / (BrE)
A motor vehicle with a cab but no cargo body, designed for pulling large trailers, especially in trucking and freight transport.

Examples

tractor pulls trailer
A fleet of tractors arrived at the distribution center early this morning.
Drivers must be specially licensed to operate a tractor with a semi-trailer attached.
The tractor pulled a load of cars across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lorry: a large, heavy motor vehicle for transporting goods (primarily British)
  • synonym – semi-trailer: a trailer that is attached to and pulled by a tractor unit
