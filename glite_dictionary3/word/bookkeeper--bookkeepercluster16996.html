bookkeeper

noun
person handling business accounts
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to record the financial transactions and accounts of a business or organization

Examples

bookkeeper manages accounts
As a bookkeeper, she is responsible for tracking all income and expenses.
The company's bookkeeper ensures that all financial records are accurate and up to date.
They hired a new bookkeeper to manage their growing business transactions.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accountant: a person whose job is to prepare and inspect financial records
