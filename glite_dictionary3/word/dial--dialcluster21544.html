dial

noun
face of a clock or watch
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.əl /; / ˈdaɪəl / (BrE)
the flat, graduated face or circular display of a clock, watch, or measuring instrument that shows values such as time, speed, or pressure
