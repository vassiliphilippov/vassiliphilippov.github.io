piscary

noun
a place for catching and processing fish
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪsˌkɛri / (AmE), / ˈpɪs.kəri / (BrE)
A place, such as a fishery, where fish are caught, processed, and sometimes sold.

Examples

piscary by the river
He found employment at the bustling piscary on the coast, learning about both fishing and processing.
Local laws strictly regulate the use and maintenance of a piscary to preserve fish stocks.
The family owned an old piscary by the river, which provided the village with fresh fish.
