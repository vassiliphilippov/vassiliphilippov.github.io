no-see-um

noun
tiny biting midge or fly
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊˈsiː.əm / (AmE), / ˌnəʊˈsiː.əm / (BrE)
A very small biting midge or fly, especially one that feeds on the blood of mammals and birds.
