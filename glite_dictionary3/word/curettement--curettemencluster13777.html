curettement

noun
removal of tissue by scraping with a curette
Pronunciation: / kjʊˈrɛtmənt / (AmE), / kjʊəˈrɛtmənt / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which tissue or growths are removed from a bodily cavity by scraping with a curette

Examples

curettement of uterus
After the curettement, the woman's recovery was swift and uncomplicated.
The doctor recommended a curettement to remove abnormal tissue from the patient's uterus.
The pathologist examined the material obtained from the curettement under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym – curettage: the process of scraping tissue from a body cavity, especially with a curette
