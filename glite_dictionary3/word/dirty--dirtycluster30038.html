dirty

adjective
manuscript with many corrections or alterations
Pronunciation: / ˈdɝti / (AmE), / ˈdɜːti / (BrE)
(of a manuscript or document) marked or defaced with numerous corrections, edits, or alterations.

Examples

dirty draft
After several rounds of revisions, the dirty copy was almost unreadable.
Please send me a dirty manuscript so I can check your suggested edits.
The editor returned the dirty draft covered in red ink corrections.
