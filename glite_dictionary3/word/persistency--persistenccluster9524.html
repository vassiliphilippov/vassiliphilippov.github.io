persistency

noun
persistent determination; perseverance
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɪstənsi / (AmE), / pəˈsɪstənsi / (BrE)
the quality of continuing firmly or obstinately in a course of action in spite of difficulty or opposition; persistent determination

Examples

strong persistency
Her persistency helped her overcome numerous obstacles in her career.
The team's persistency paid off with a hard-earned victory.
Without persistency, it is easy to give up when challenges arise.
