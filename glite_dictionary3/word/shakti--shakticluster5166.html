Shakti

noun
divine female energy in Hinduism
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːkti / (AmE), / ˈʃakti / (BrE)
In Hinduism, the divine female energy or generative force, personified as the wife of Shiva and a benevolent form of Devi.
