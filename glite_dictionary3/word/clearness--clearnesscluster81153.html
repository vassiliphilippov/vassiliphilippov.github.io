clearness

noun
the quality of being clear or easy to understand
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrnəs / (AmE), / ˈklɪənəs / (BrE)
The quality of being easy to perceive, understand, or interpret; the state of being clear.

Examples

clearness of explanation
He admired the clearness of the mountain air during his morning hike.
The clearness of her explanation made the concept much easier to grasp.
The report was praised for its clearness and concise language.
