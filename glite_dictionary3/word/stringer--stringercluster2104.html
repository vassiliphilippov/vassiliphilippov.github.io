stringer

noun
member of a ranked sports team squad
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈstrɪŋə / (BrE)
a member of a sports team, especially in reference to their ranking in the squad, such as a first or second string player

Examples

first stringer player
As a first stringer, she started every game of the season.
He was promoted from a second stringer to the starting lineup after his impressive performance.
The coach rotated several stringers to keep the team fresh during the tournament.
