gravestone

noun
stone marker for a grave
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪvˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪv.stəʊn / (BrE)
a stone slab or marker placed at a grave, usually bearing an inscription in memory of the person buried there

Examples

gravestone inscription
After the ceremony, they placed flowers next to the gravestone of their loved one.
Each gravestone in the cemetery tells a unique story from the past.
The old gravestone had weathered inscriptions that were hard to read.
