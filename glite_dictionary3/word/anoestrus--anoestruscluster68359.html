anoestrus

noun
sexual inactivity period in female mammals
Pronunciation: / ˌænoʊˈɛstrəs / (AmE), / ˌanəʊˈɛstrəs / (BrE)
A period of sexual inactivity in female nonhuman mammals between two periods of estrus.

Examples

anoestrus period in animals
During anoestrus, the female remains sexually inactive and is not receptive to mating.
Environmental factors can sometimes prolong the duration of anoestrus in certain species.
Veterinarians monitor hormone levels to determine the transition out of anoestrus.
