god

noun
supernatural being worshipped as a deity
Pronunciation: / ɡɑd / (AmE), / ɡɒd / (BrE)
A being believed to have supernatural powers, worshipped as controlling some part of the world or personifying a force, especially in polytheistic religions.

Examples

ancient god
Ancient Greeks believed that each aspect of life was watched over by a different god.
He prayed to the river god for a safe journey.
Many cultures have a god of war who is worshipped before battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deity: a god or goddess; a divine being
  • synonym – goddess: a female deity or god
