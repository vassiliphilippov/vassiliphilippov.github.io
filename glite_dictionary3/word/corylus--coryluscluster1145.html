Corylus

noun
genus of hazel trees and shrubs
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.ɪ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒr.ɪ.ləs / (BrE)
a genus of deciduous shrubs or small trees, commonly known as hazels, that produce edible nuts and have separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
