round

noun
regular route for duty, esp. police or sentry
Pronunciation: / raʊnd / (AmE), / raʊnd / (BrE)
a regular set of visits, a route, or a circuit that is followed, especially as part of one's job or for passing information

Examples

make a round

The security guard finished his round before returning to the office.

The story is making the rounds in political circles.
As part of his rounds, the doctor visits each patient in the ward.
The nurse completed her morning rounds before taking a break.
The security guard will begin his nightly rounds at midnight.

Related Terms
