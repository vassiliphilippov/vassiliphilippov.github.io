madness

noun
unrestrained excitement or enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / ˈmædnɪs / (AmE), / ˈmædnɪs / (BrE)
Unrestrained excitement or enthusiasm, often to an intense or excessive degree.

Examples

The crowd was swept up in the madness of the festival, dancing until dawn.
There was a madness in her devotion to the project that inspired everyone around her.
