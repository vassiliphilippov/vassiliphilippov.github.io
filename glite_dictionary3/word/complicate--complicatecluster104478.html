to complicate

verb
make more complex or difficult
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːm.pləˌkeɪt /; / ˈkɑːm.plɪ.keɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.plɪ.keɪt / (BrE)
to make something more complex or difficult than it was before
