crossing

noun
intersection point of two paths or lines
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsɪŋ /; / ˈkrɑsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsɪŋ / (BrE)
a place where two roads, paths, or lines cross, or where you can go from one side to the other (such as an intersection, marked crossing, or shallow river crossing)

  • antonym – barrier: an obstacle that prevents crossing

  • synonym – footbridge: a bridge designed for pedestrians to cross over
  • synonym – ford: a shallow place in a river or stream allowing one to walk or drive across
