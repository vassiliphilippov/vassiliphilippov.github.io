minicab

noun
a small car used as a taxi
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌkæb / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪkab / (BrE)
a small car used as a taxi, especially one that is booked in advance rather than hailed on the street

Examples

book a minicab
The minicab arrived promptly outside her house at eight o'clock.
Unlike a traditional taxi, a minicab must be pre-booked by phone or app.
We called a minicab to take us home after the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym – taxicab: a car licensed to transport passengers in return for payment of a fare
