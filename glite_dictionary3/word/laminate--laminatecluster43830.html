to laminate

verb
cover with a thin layer
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.ə.neɪt / (AmE), / ˈlæm.ɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
to cover or overlay with a thin layer of material, typically for protection or strength

Examples

to laminate a card
After printing my ID card, I had it laminated for extra durability.
The teacher asked the students to laminate their artwork before displaying it in the hallway.
We decided to laminate the kitchen countertops to protect them from stains.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coat: to cover with a layer of a substance
  • synonym – overlay: to cover the surface with a coating, layer, or sheet
