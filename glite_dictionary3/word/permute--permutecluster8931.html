to permute

verb
change the order of
Pronunciation: / pərˈmjut / (AmE), / pəˈmjuːt / (BrE)
to change the order or arrangement of a set of things

Examples

to permute a list
The program will randomly permute the sequence each time it runs.
To solve the puzzle, simply permute the tiles until they are in order.
You can permute the letters of the word to create new words.
