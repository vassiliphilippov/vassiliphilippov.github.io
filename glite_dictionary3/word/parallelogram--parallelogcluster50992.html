parallelogram

noun
a quadrilateral with parallel, equal sides
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛrəˈlɛloʊˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˌparəˈlɛləɡram / (BrE)
a quadrilateral with opposite sides that are both parallel and equal in length

Examples

parallelogram with equal sides
A rectangle is a special type of parallelogram where all angles are right angles.
She was asked to calculate the area of the parallelogram drawn on the board.
The opposite sides of a parallelogram are always equal in both length and direction.
