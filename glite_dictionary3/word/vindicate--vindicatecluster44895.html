to vindicate

verb
maintain or uphold, especially rights or claims
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪn.dɪˌkeɪt /; / ˈvɪndɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / ˈvɪn.dɪ.keɪt /; / ˈvɪndɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to show or prove that someone or something is right, justified, or not guilty, especially after being doubted or criticized

The new evidence helped to vindicate her claim of innocence.

  • antonym – disprove: to prove that something is false or incorrect
