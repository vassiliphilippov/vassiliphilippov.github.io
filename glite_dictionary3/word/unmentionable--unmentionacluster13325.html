unmentionable

adjective
not suitable to discuss; taboo
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɛnʃənəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛnʃənəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of something) unsuitable or forbidden as a topic of conversation; taboo or not to be discussed.

Examples

unmentionable topic
Certain unmentionable words were censored from the broadcast.
The politician avoided any unmentionable topics during the debate.
They whispered about past scandals, careful not to mention anything truly unmentionable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – taboo: prohibited or restricted by social custom
