Berberis vulgaris

noun
a European shrub with red berries (common barberry)
Pronunciation: / ˌbɜrbərɪs vʌlˈɡɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˌbɜːbərɪs vʌlˈɡɛːrɪs / (BrE)
An upright deciduous shrub native to Europe, with clusters of red, juicy berries, widely naturalized in the United States; also known as common barberry.
