to breathe

verb
draw air in and out of lungs
Pronunciation: / briːð / (AmE), / briːð / (BrE)
To draw air into and expel it from the lungs.

Examples

to breathe deeply
After running up the hill, he paused to breathe deeply and catch his breath.
Doctors told her to try to breathe slowly during the test.
