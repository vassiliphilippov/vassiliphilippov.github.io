impregnability

noun
the quality of being impossible to overcome
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌprɛɡ.nəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ɪmˌprɛɡ.nəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being strong enough to resist or withstand attack; invulnerability.

Examples

impregnability of a fortress
Over time, the myth of the castle's impregnability was finally shattered.
The impregnability of the fortress gave its defenders a sense of security.
They boasted about the impregnability of their new security system.

Related Terms

  • synonym – invulnerability: the quality of being impossible to harm or damage
  • antonym – vulnerability: the quality of being exposed to the possibility of being attacked or harmed
