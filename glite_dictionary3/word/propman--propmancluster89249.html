propman

noun
male stage crew member in charge of props
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑpˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈprɒpˌman / (BrE)
a male member of a theatre stage crew who is responsible for managing and arranging properties (props) used in a production

Examples

propman arranges props
As a propman, he worked behind the scenes to prepare all the stage accessories.
During rehearsals, the propman quickly repaired a broken chair for the next scene.
The propman made sure every item was in place before the curtain rose.

Related Terms

  • synonym – props master: person in charge of props in a theatre production, regardless of gender
