pulverised

adjective
reduced to fine particles
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlvəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpʌlvəraɪzd / (BrE)
reduced to fine particles by crushing, grinding, or pounding.

Examples

pulverised stone powder
After hours of grinding, the ore became a pulverised powder.
Pulverised sugar dissolves faster than regular granulated sugar in beverages.
The chef added some pulverised garlic to the sauce for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym – granulated: formed into grains or granules; not finely divided
  • synonym – powdered: reduced to powder form; very finely ground
