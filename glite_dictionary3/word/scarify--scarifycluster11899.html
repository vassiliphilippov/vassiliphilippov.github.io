to scarify

verb
break up the surface of soil
Pronunciation: / ˈsker.əˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈskær.ɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
To break up the surface of (soil) to improve its condition for planting or aeration.

Examples

to scarify the soil
Farmers often scarify the soil before planting new crops.
The gardener used a tool to scarify the compacted earth.
To encourage grass growth, you should scarify your lawn in early spring.
