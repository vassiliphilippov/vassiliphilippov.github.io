pioneer

noun
person who initiates new developments
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌpaɪəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who is among the first to develop or apply new methods, areas of knowledge, or activities

Examples

pioneer of science
Many pioneers faced ridicule for their unconventional ideas.
Marie Curie was a true pioneer in the field of radioactivity.
The company became a pioneer of renewable energy technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym – innovator: a person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products
  • synonym – trailblazer: a person who is the first to do something or go somewhere
