palmitin

noun
an ester of glycerol and palmitic acid
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.mɪ.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈpæl.mɪ.tɪn / (BrE)
an ester formed from glycerol and palmitic acid, commonly found in animal and vegetable fats
