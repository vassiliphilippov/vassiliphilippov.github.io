grist

noun
grain to be ground or already ground
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪst / (AmE), / ɡrɪst / (BrE)
Grain that is to be ground or has been ground, especially for making flour.

Examples

grist for the mill
After a good harvest, there was enough grist to last through the winter.
Small farmers brought their grist to the local watermill.
The miller prepared the grist for tomorrow's flour production.
