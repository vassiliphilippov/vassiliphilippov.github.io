chantlike

adjective
resembling chanting in sound or rhythm
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːntˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having a monotonous, rhythmic, or musical quality similar to chanting.

Examples

chantlike voice

The leader's chantlike instructions echoed through the hall.
The monks' voices had a chantlike rhythm as they recited their prayers.
