cartridge

noun
ammunition case for firearms
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A metal or plastic case containing a charge and a bullet or shot, used as ammunition in firearms.
