lampooner

noun
person who mocks through satire or imitation
Pronunciation: / læmˈpunər / (AmE), / læmˈpuːnə / (BrE)
a person who mocks or satirizes others, especially by imitating their literary or musical style for comic effect

Examples

lampooner mocks politicians
During the festival, a local lampooner performed a hilarious musical skit mocking politicians.
She became known as a sharp lampooner after her satirical essays went viral online.
The clever lampooner delighted audiences with his biting parodies of famous speeches.
