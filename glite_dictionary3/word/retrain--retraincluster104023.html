to retrain

verb
train a machine-learning model again
Pronunciation: / riˈtreɪn / (AmE), / riːˈtreɪn / (BrE)
(in computing, especially machine learning) to train a model again or update it with new data in order to improve its performance or adapt it to changed conditions.

Examples

to retrain a computer model
The company decided to retrain the recommendation system on recent user interactions.
After collecting more labeled data, they retrained the fraud detection model to reduce false positives.
The startup is retraining its chatbot weekly to incorporate users' new phrases and slang.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fine-tune: to make small adjustments to a model or system, typically by training on specific data to improve performance
  • synonym – update: to change something to make it more current or accurate; in computing, to apply new data or parameters
