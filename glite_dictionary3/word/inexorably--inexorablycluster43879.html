inexorably

adverb
relentlessly or unchangeably
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɛksərəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈɛksərəblɪ / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be stopped, changed, or persuaded; relentlessly.

Examples

inexorably move forward
History moved inexorably toward a turning point.
She felt the deadline approaching inexorably, no matter how hard she worked.
The disease progressed inexorably, despite all medical efforts to halt it.
