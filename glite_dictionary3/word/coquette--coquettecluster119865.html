coquette

noun
a flirtatious or teasing woman
Pronunciation: / koʊˈkɛt / (AmE), / kɒˈkɛt / (BrE)
a woman who flirts or behaves in a teasingly seductive way to attract attention or admiration

Examples

a coquette at parties
He suspected she was a coquette when she winked at him from across the room.
She was known as a coquette, charming everyone at the party with her playful smiles.
The writer described the character as a clever coquette who always got her way.

  • synonym – flirt: a person who behaves as if they are attracted to someone, without serious intent
