anaemic

adjective
lacking energy or strength
Pronunciation: / əˈniːmɪk / (AmE), / əˈniːmɪk / (BrE)
lacking in energy, strength, or vitality; weak or feeble

Examples

anaemic performance
After a week of illness, her performance at work was noticeably anaemic.
His argument for the proposal was anaemic and failed to persuade the board.
The play received anaemic reviews from the critics, who found it slow and unconvincing.
