tailboard

noun
gate at the rear of a vehicle for loading
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈteɪlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a hinged or removable board or gate at the rear of a vehicle, especially a truck or van, that can be lowered or removed to facilitate loading or unloading

Examples

tailboard of a truck
Before unloading the boxes, he lowered the tailboard of the truck.
Make sure the tailboard is securely fastened before driving away.
The workers dropped the tailboard to access the cargo area.
