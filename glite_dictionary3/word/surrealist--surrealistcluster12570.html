surrealist

noun
artist of the surrealism movement
Pronunciation: / səˈriəlɪst / (AmE), / səˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
an artist or writer who is a member of, or identified with, the surrealism movement, characterized by the expression of the unconscious mind and dream-like imagery

Examples

surrealist painter
As a surrealist, she sought to represent the irrational in her art.
Many surrealists were fascinated by dreams and the workings of the subconscious.
The Spanish painter Salvador Dalí is perhaps the most famous surrealist of all time.
