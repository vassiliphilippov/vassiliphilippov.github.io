to metricise

verb
apply performance metrics
Pronunciation: / \u02c8m\u025btr\u026a\u02ccsa\u026az / (AmE), / \u02c8m\u025btr\u026as\u028c\u026az / (BrE)
(in management, data, or organisational contexts) to introduce, adopt, or apply measurable indicators (metrics) to evaluate or monitor the performance of a process, service, or activity.

Examples

to metricise performance data
The city began to metricise its public services, tracking response times and satisfaction scores.
After the audit, the department metricised every workflow to measure efficiency and reduce delays.
Teams are metricising their campaigns now, using clear KPIs to report weekly progress.

Related Terms

  • synonym – measure: to calculate or assess the size, amount, or degree of something
  • synonym – metricize: apply measurable metrics (American spelling)
  • synonym – quantify: to express or measure the quantity of something
