unreverberant

adjective
not echoing or reverberating
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈvɜr.bɚ.ənt / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈvɜː.bər.ənt / (BrE)
(of a space or material) not causing or having a tendency to produce echoes or reverberations.

Examples

unreverberant room
Carpets and drapes can create an unreverberant environment, making it easier to focus on conversation.
Her voice sounded flat in the unreverberant conference room, lacking any echo.
The studio was designed to be completely unreverberant for accurate sound recording.
