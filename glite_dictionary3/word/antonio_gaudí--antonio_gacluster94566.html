Antonio Gaudí

proper noun
Spanish Art Nouveau architect (1852–1926)
Pronunciation: / ænˌtoʊni.oʊ gaʊˈdi /; / ænˈtoʊni.oʊ ɡaʊˈdi / (AmE), / anˌtɒni.əʊ gaʊˈdiː /; / anˈtɔːnjəʊ ɡaʊˈdiː / (BrE)
