gallantry

noun
heroic bravery, especially in battle
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləntri / (AmE), / ˈɡæləntri / (BrE)
heroic or exceptional courage and bravery, especially in dangerous or difficult situations such as battle

Examples

display gallantry in battle
Her gallantry in the face of overwhelming odds inspired everyone around her.
The leader's gallantry became legendary among his comrades.
The soldier was awarded a medal for his gallantry during the fierce battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bravery: courageous behavior or character
  • antonym – cowardice: lack of bravery
  • synonym – valor: great courage in the face of danger, especially in battle
