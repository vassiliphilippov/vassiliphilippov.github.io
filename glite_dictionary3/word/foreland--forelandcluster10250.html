foreland

noun
land at a forward edge or margin
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌlænd /; / ˈfɔːrˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.lənd / (BrE)
an area of land that sticks out from the coast or forms the forward edge of something, especially into the sea
