to postpose

verb
to place after another sentence element
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈpoʊz / (AmE), / pəʊstˈpəʊz / (BrE)
(of a word, especially a grammatical element) to place after another constituent in a sentence.

Examples

to postpose a particle
It is common in Turkish to postpose particles for emphasis.
Some languages postpose adjectives rather than placing them before nouns.
The syntax rules specify whether to postpose certain modifiers in the sentence.

Related Terms

  • antonym – prepose: to place (a word or phrase) before another constituent in a sentence
