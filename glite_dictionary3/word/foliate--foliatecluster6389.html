to foliate

verb
decorate with leaves or leaf designs
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.li.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.li.eɪt / (BrE)
to decorate or ornament with leaves or leaf-shaped designs

Examples

to foliate a page
Medieval manuscripts were often beautifully foliated and illustrated by hand.
The artisan skillfully foliated the edges of the silver bowl with intricate leaf patterns.
