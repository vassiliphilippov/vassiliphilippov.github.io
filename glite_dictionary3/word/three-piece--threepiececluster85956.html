three-piece

adjective
consisting of three matching parts (usually clothing)
Pronunciation: / θri pis / (AmE), / θriː piːs / (BrE)
(of clothing) consisting of three matching parts, typically referring to a suit with jacket, trousers, and waistcoat.

Examples

three-piece suit
For the interview, she preferred a three-piece outfit for a more polished appearance.
He wore a classic three-piece suit to the wedding, looking exceptionally sharp.
The three-piece design includes a jacket, matching trousers, and a waistcoat.
