re-experiencing

noun
undergoing a prior experience again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɪkˌspɪriənsiŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɪkˌspɪəriənsiŋ / (BrE)
the act of undergoing a previous experience again, especially in memory or emotion

Examples

re-experiencing past emotions
Films sometimes evoke a powerful re-experiencing of childhood emotions in viewers.
She described the vivid re-experiencing of her first day at school as if it were yesterday.
The patient reported frequent re-experiencing of traumatic events during therapy.
