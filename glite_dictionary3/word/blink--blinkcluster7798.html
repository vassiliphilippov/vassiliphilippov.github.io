to blink

verb
suppress tears or emotion by blinking
Pronunciation: / blɪŋk / (AmE), / blɪŋk / (BrE)
to try to prevent or suppress (tears or emotion) by blinking.

Examples

to blink back tears
He tried to blink back his emotions during the speech.
She managed to blink away her tears before anyone noticed.
She quickly blinked away any sign of sadness and smiled.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hold back: to restrain or suppress (especially emotion or tears)
