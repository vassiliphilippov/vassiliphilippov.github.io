opaque

adjective
not allowing light to pass through
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpeɪk / (AmE), / əʊˈpeɪk / (BrE)
not letting light pass through; not transparent or translucent

Examples

opaque glass window
The bathroom window was made of opaque glass to ensure privacy.
The liquid turned opaque after adding the chemical, making it impossible to see through.
Thick clouds made the sky completely opaque, blocking out the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym – translucent: allowing some light to pass through but not detailed shapes
  • antonym – transparent: allowing light to pass through so that objects behind can be seen clearly
