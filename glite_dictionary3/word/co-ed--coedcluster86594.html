co-ed

adjective
attended by both sexes
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌɛd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌɛd / (BrE)
Attended by members of both sexes; open to both male and female participants.

Examples

co-ed sports team
Many sports clubs are now co-ed to encourage participation from everyone.
The university recently became a co-ed institution after years of single-sex education.
They live in a co-ed dormitory, sharing common spaces with both men and women.

Related Terms

  • synonym – coeducational: relating to education for both sexes together
  • antonym – single-sex: attended by members of only one sex
