to ideate

verb
form an idea or concept in the mind
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ.di.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈaɪ.di.eɪt / (BrE)
to form an idea, image, or concept of something in the mind; to imagine or conceive.

Examples

to ideate new ideas
Designers often ideate new concepts before beginning the development process.
She was asked to ideate solutions to improve customer satisfaction.
The team spent an hour ideating possible campaign strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – conceive: to form an idea or concept in the mind
  • synonym – imagine: to form a mental image of something not present
