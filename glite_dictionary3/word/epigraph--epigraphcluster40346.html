epigraph

noun
an engraved inscription
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpɪˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈɛpɪɡrɑːf / (BrE)
an inscription engraved on a building, statue, or coin, especially as a dedication or memorial

Examples

epigraph on a monument
A marble epigraph commemorates those who contributed to the building's construction.
Each old coin was carefully examined for its unique epigraph by the historian.
The ancient temple's entrance bore an epigraph in Latin honoring the gods.
