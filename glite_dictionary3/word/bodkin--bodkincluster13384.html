bodkin

noun
blunt needle for threading ribbon
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑd.kɪn /; / ˈbɑːdkɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɒd.kɪn /; / ˈbɒdkɪn / (BrE)
a small tool used in sewing for threading ribbon, tape, or cord through fabric, or for making holes in fabric or leather
