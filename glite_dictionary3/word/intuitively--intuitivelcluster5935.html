intuitively

adverb
in a manner based on intuition
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtuː.ɪ.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ɪnˈtjuː.ɪ.tɪv.li / (BrE)
in a way that is based on intuition or instinctive understanding rather than conscious reasoning

Examples

act intuitively
He navigated the unfamiliar city intuitively, trusting his sense of direction.
Many people intuitively know when something is wrong, even without evidence.
She intuitively understood his feelings despite his silence.
