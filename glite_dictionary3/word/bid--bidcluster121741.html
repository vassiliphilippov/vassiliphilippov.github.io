to bid

verb
declare intent, especially in card games
Pronunciation: / bɪd / (AmE), / bɪd / (BrE)
to make a statement of intent or demand, especially by declaring the value or suit in a card game such as bridge

Examples

to bid three hearts
He decided to bid three hearts, surprising his partner.
It's your turn to bid in the next round of bridge.
Players must bid before any cards are played.

Related Terms

  • synonym – offer: an act of stating a value in a game or auction
  • antonym – pass: to decline to make a bid in a card game
