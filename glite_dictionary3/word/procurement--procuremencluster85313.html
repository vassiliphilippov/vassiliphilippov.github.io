procurement

noun
the act of obtaining goods or services
Pronunciation: / prəˈkjʊrmənt / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊəmənt / (BrE)
the process of obtaining or acquiring goods, services, or supplies, especially for business or government purposes

Examples
