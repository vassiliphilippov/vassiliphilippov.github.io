footfall

noun
sound of a step while walking
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌfɔl / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌfɔːl / (BrE)
The sound made by a person's footsteps as they walk.

Examples

soft footfall in the hall
He listened carefully, trying to distinguish each footfall behind him in the hall.
The soft footfall of the child was barely audible on the carpet.
The sudden footfall on the stairs startled her.
