yoghourt

noun
fermented milk food; variant of 'yogurt'
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊ.ɡɚt / (AmE), / ˈjɒɡ.ət / (BrE)
A semi-solid food made from milk fermented by bacteria, resulting in a tart, creamy texture; also spelled 'yogurt'.
