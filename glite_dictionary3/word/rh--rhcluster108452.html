RH

noun
abbreviation for releasing hormone
Pronunciation: / ɑr eɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɑː ɒtʃ / (BrE)
An abbreviation for 'releasing hormone', any of several hormones produced in the hypothalamus that stimulate the release of specific hormones from the anterior pituitary gland.
