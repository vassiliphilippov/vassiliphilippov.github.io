oleaginous

adjective
excessively ingratiating; oily in manner
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊliˈædʒɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊliˈædʒɪnəs / (BrE)
excessively and unpleasantly suave, ingratiating, or insincere in manner or speech; overly eager to please, often in a way that is perceived as oily or smarmy.

Examples

oleaginous manner
His oleaginous attempts to win favor with the boss were obvious to everyone.
She found his oleaginous manner insincere and repulsive, despite his polite words.
The politician's oleaginous compliments made the audience roll their eyes in disbelief.
