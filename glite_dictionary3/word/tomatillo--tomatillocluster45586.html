tomatillo

noun
small edible husked fruit, like a tomato
Pronunciation: / toʊməˈtiːjoʊ /; / toʊ.məˈtiː.joʊ /; / toʊˌmɑːˈtiːjoʊ / (AmE), / təˈmætɪəʊ /; / təˈmæt.ɪ.jəʊ /; / təˌmætˈiːjəʊ / (BrE)
a small, round, edible fruit (usually green or purple) with a papery husk, used in Mexican cooking; also, the plant that produces this fruit
