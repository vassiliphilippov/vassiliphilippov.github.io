Lincocin

noun
trade name for lincomycin antibiotic
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋkəʊsɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkəʊsɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for lincomycin, an antibiotic obtained from Streptomyces bacteria, used to treat infections resistant to penicillin.
