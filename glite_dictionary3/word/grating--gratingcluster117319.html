grating

adjective
harsh, unpleasant sound
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a sound or voice) unpleasantly harsh or jarring to the ears.

Examples

grating voice sound
Her laugh had a grating quality that set his teeth on edge.
His grating voice made it difficult to listen to the announcement.
The grating sound of metal scraping against metal was intolerable.

Related Terms

  • synonym – harsh: unpleasantly rough or jarring to the senses
  • antonym – mellifluous: pleasantly smooth and musical to hear
