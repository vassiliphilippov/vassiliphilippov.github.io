mum

adjective
not speaking; silent
Pronunciation: / mʌm / (AmE), / mʌm / (BrE)
not speaking; remaining silent, especially when expected to speak

Examples

stay mum under pressure
He stayed mum about the incident despite repeated questions.
Please keep mum about the surprise party until Friday.
She remained mum during the entire meeting, offering no comments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – silent: not making any sound; not speaking
