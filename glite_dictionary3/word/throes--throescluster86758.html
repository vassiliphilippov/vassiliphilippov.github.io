throes

noun
intense pain or struggle
Pronunciation: / θroʊz / (AmE), / θrəʊz / (BrE)
intense or violent pain and suffering, often occurring during a difficult or final stage of something

Examples

in the throes of grief
She was in the throes of grief after hearing the news.
The country is still in the throes of economic crisis.
Witnesses described the animal's final throes before it succumbed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agony: extreme physical or mental suffering
  • antonym – ecstasy: an overwhelming feeling of joy; opposite in emotional intensity to pain
