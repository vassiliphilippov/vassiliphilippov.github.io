astringent

adjective
having a constricting or tightening effect
Pronunciation: / əˈstrɪndʒənt / (AmE), / əˈstrɪndʒənt / (BrE)
having the property of drawing together or constricting soft tissue, often used to describe substances that tighten skin or stop bleeding

Examples

astringent skin lotion
The doctor applied an astringent to stop the minor bleeding.
This astringent lotion helps tighten the pores of your skin.
Witch hazel is known for its astringent properties in treating oily skin.
