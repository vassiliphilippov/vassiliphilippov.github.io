princely

adjective
impressively large or luxurious; grand
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnsli / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsli / (BrE)
(of a sum, amount, or thing) impressively large or luxurious; characteristic of wealth and grandeur.

Examples

princely sum
She received a princely sum for her memoirs.
The company awarded him a princely bonus for his outstanding performance.
They live in a princely mansion overlooking the bay.

Related Terms

  • antonym – modest: not large or elaborate; relatively moderate
