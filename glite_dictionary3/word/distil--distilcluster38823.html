to distil

verb
condense from vapor to liquid
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪl / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪl / (BrE)
to form or produce liquid drops, especially by condensation

Examples

distil into drops

Rain forms when water vapor in the atmosphere cools and distils into droplets.
