yawning

adjective
mouth open, showing boredom or tiredness
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or group) having the mouth wide open, typically indicating boredom, tiredness, or sleepiness.
