busybody

noun
a meddling person
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪziˌbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈbɪziˌbɒdi / (BrE)
a person who is too interested in and meddles in other people's private affairs

Examples

nosy busybody
Nobody likes a busybody poking around in their business.
She didn't appreciate the busybody at work spreading rumors about her private life.
The neighborhood busybody couldn't resist asking about the new family moving in.
