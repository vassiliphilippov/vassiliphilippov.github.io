communication

noun
a message conveyed between people or groups
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a message or information that is conveyed from one person or group to another

Examples

official communication letter
Please read this important communication from the human resources department.
The manager issued a communication to all staff regarding the new policy.
They received an urgent communication from their overseas partners.

Related Terms

  • synonym – message: a verbal, written, or recorded communication sent to or left for a recipient
