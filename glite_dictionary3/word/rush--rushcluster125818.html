rush

noun
attempt to advance ball by running (American football)
Pronunciation: / rʌʃ / (AmE), / rʌʃ / (BrE)
(in American football) an attempt to advance the ball by running with it, especially into the defensive line.

Examples

a rush attempt
He led the league in yards gained from rush attempts.
The running back made a spectacular rush down the field.
Their defense managed to stop the rush at the goal line.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pass: (American football) an attempt to advance the ball by throwing it
