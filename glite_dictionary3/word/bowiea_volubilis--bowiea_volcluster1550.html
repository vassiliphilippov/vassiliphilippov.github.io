Bowiea volubilis

noun
South African ornamental plant with green stems and bulbs
Pronunciation: / boʊˈiːə vəˈluːbɪlɪs / (AmE), / bəʊˈiːə vəˈluːbɪlɪs / (BrE)
A much-branched, leafless, twining South African plant cultivated as an ornamental for its bright green stems growing from large aboveground bulbs.
