umbrella

noun
something that unites or includes related items
Pronunciation: / əmˈbrɛlə / (AmE), / ʌmˈbrɛlə / (BrE)
A thing that unites or includes a group of related things or organizations.

Examples

umbrella organization
Environmental regulations fall under the broad umbrella of public health policies.
Many non-profit groups work under the umbrella of the national charity organization.
The company acts as an umbrella for several small start-ups in different industries.
