broad

adjective
obvious or lacking subtlety
Pronunciation: / brɔd / (AmE), / brɔːd / (BrE)
(of a remark or action) lacking subtlety; obvious or easily understood.

Examples

broad hint
He gave us a broad hint that the meeting was over.
Her broad smile made her true feelings obvious to everyone.
The politician made a broad statement about the economy, leaving no room for ambiguity.
