to dump

verb
to knock down forcefully (esp. in sports)
Pronunciation: / dʌmp / (AmE), / dʌmp / (BrE)
to knock someone down with force, often in a physical confrontation or in sports

Examples

to dump an opponent
He dumps his rivals with impressive force in every match.
She was dumped by a strong tackle during the game.
The wrestler dumped his opponent onto the mat with ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym – deck: to knock someone down, especially with a punch
  • synonym – floor: to knock someone to the ground
  • antonym – lift: to raise to a higher position
