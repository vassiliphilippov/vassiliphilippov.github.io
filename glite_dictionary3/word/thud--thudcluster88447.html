thud

noun
dull, heavy impact sound
Pronunciation: / θʌd / (AmE), / θʌd / (BrE)
a dull, heavy sound made by the impact of a heavy object

Examples

thud of a book
A heavy book landed on the floor with a soft thud.
She fell to the ground with a thud as the crowd gasped.
The door closed with a loud thud behind him.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – bang: a loud, sudden noise, often made by something hitting a surface
  • degree lower – tap: a light or gentle sound of a slight blow
  • synonym – thump: a heavy dull sound, often from something hitting a surface
