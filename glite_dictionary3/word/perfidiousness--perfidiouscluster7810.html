perfidiousness

noun
betrayal; treacherousness
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɪdiəsnəs / (AmE), / pəˈfɪdiəsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being disloyal or treacherous; betrayal of trust or faith.

History is replete with tales of political perfidiousness that changed the fate of nations.
