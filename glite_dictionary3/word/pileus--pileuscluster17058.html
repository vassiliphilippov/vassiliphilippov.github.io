pileus

noun
cap of a mushroom or similar fungus
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪliəs / (AmE), / ˈpɪliːəs / (BrE)
the umbrella- or cone-shaped cap forming the top of a stalked fleshy fungus such as a mushroom

Examples

pileus of a mushroom
Raindrops collected on the pileus, creating tiny pools on the mushroom cap.
Scientists measured the diameter of the pileus to classify the different fungus species.
The pileus of the mushroom was broad and bright red, standing out in the forest floor.
