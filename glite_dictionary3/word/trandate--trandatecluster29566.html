Trandate

noun
trade name for a blood pressure medication
Pronunciation: / ˈtrænˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtræn.deɪt / (BrE)
A trade name for labetalol, an antihypertensive drug that blocks alpha and beta-adrenergic receptors to lower blood pressure.
