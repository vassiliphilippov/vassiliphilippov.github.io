minder

noun
person who looks after babies or children
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪndər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndə / (BrE)
A person who looks after babies or young children, especially in their own home while the parents are working.

Examples

minder for children
Finding a reliable minder gives parents peace of mind during the workday.
She works as a minder for several families in her neighborhood.
The couple hired a minder to care for their infant while they were at work.
