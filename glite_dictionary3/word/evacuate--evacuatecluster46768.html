to evacuate

verb
discharge waste matter from the body
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvækjuˌeɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈvækjueɪt / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) to excrete or discharge waste matter from the body, especially the bowels.

Examples

to evacuate bowels
Certain medications may make it difficult for you to evacuate regularly.
Doctors advised him to evacuate his bowels before the procedure.
The patient was unable to evacuate his bowels without assistance.
