to sling

verb
support in a sling (body part)
Pronunciation: / slɪŋ / (AmE), / slɪŋ / (BrE)
to support, carry, or immobilize (an arm or other part of the body) in a sling, typically to aid healing after injury

Examples

to sling an injured arm
After breaking her collarbone, the doctor slung her arm to prevent further injury.
He slinged his injured wrist before heading to the clinic.
The nurse gently slinged the boy's arm to keep it stable.
