sambuca

noun
Italian liqueur flavored with licorice
Pronunciation: / sæmˈbjuːkə / (AmE), / sæmˈbuːkə / (BrE)
An Italian liqueur made from elderberries or other fruits and flavored with licorice.

Examples

glass of sambuca
After dinner, he ordered a glass of sambuca as a digestif.
She served sambuca with three coffee beans floating on top, an Italian tradition.
The strong licorice flavor of sambuca makes it popular in many cocktails.
