smothered

adjective
restrained or suppressed
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌðɚd / (AmE), / ˈsmʌðəd / (BrE)
(of a sound, action, or feeling) restrained, suppressed, or prevented from being fully expressed or released.

Examples

smothered cry
A smothered sob escaped her as she struggled to remain calm.
He let out a smothered laugh, trying not to disturb the others in the room.
She covered her mouth to hide a smothered yawn during the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stifled: restrained, held back, or suppressed
  • synonym – suppressed: prevented from being expressed or revealed
  • antonym – unrestrained: not restrained or held back; free and uncontrolled
