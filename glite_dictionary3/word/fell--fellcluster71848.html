to fell

verb
sew by folding and stitching fabric edges
Pronunciation: / fɛl / (AmE), / fɛl / (BrE)
to sew a seam by folding the edges of the fabric and stitching them down so that the raw edges are enclosed

Examples

to fell fabric edges
After sewing the fabric together, he felled the edges for extra strength.
She carefully felled the seam to give the shirt a neat, durable finish.
The instructions say to fell the seams for a professional appearance.
