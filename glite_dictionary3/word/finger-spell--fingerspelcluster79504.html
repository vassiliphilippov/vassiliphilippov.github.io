to finger-spell

verb
spell words with hand signs
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌspɛl / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəˌspɛl / (BrE)
to communicate a word by spelling it out letter by letter using the manual alphabet, especially in sign language.

Examples

to finger-spell a word
Deaf children learn to finger-spell new vocabulary before they use full signs.
During the lesson, the teacher asked the students to finger-spell their names.
When he forgot the sign for a word, he would finger-spell it slowly.
