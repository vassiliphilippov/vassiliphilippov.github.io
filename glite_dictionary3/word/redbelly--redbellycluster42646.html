redbelly

noun
freshwater turtle with red underside markings
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌbɛli / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbɛli / (BrE)
A freshwater turtle, found in Chesapeake Bay tributaries, known for red markings on the lower shell.

Examples

a redbelly turtle
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the endangered redbelly.
The redbelly is easily recognized by the bright red markings on its lower shell.
We spotted a redbelly basking on a log in the Chesapeake Bay area.
