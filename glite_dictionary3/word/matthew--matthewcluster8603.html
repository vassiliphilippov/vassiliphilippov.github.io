Matthew

proper noun
one of Jesus's apostles; Gospel author
Pronunciation: / ˈmæθ.ju / (AmE), / ˈmæθ.juː / (BrE)
(in the New Testament) one of Jesus's twelve apostles, traditionally regarded as the author of the first Gospel.
