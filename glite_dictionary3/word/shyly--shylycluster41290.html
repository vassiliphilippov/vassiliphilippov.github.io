shyly

adverb
in a shy or timid manner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪ.li / (BrE)
In a shy, timid, or bashful manner.

Examples

smile shyly

She shyly accepted the compliment, hardly meeting his gaze.
The child looked at the stranger and smiled shyly.
