appointment

noun
a person officially chosen for a post
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔɪntmənt / (AmE), / əˈpɔɪntmənt / (BrE)
a job or position to which someone is officially assigned, the act of assigning someone to such a position, or the person who is appointed

Examples

His appointment as ambassador was announced last week.

She received an appointment as a professor at the university.
A government appointment often requires approval from several officials.
Her appointment to the committee surprised many colleagues.
The board's appointment of the new director was announced yesterday.
