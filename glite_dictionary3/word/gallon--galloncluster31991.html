gallon

noun
British unit of capacity (approx. 4.545 liters)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæl.ən /; / ˈɡælən / (AmE), / ˈɡæl.ən /; / ˈɡælən / (BrE)
a unit for measuring liquid volume, used in the UK and US (the exact amount is different in each country)
