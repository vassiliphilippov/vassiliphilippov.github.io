sailor

noun
member of a ship's crew
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlər / (AmE), / ˈseɪlə / (BrE)
a person who works on a ship, especially as a member of the crew (including both civilian and navy ships)

Many sailors participated in the rescue mission during the storm.
The young sailor stood proudly on the deck of the destroyer.
