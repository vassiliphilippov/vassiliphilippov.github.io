handline

noun
fishing line managed by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈhændˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a fishing line operated and managed chiefly by hand rather than with a rod or reel

Examples

fish with a handline
Fishermen often use a handline to catch fish from small boats.
He skillfully pulled in the trout using his handline.
The handline was easy to carry and perfect for shallow waters.
