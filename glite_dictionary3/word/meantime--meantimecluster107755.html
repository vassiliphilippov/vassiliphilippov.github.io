meantime

adverb
during the intervening time
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːnˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈmiːnˌtaɪm / (BrE)
During the time between two events; in the intervening period.

Examples

in the meantime wait
Dinner will be ready soon. Meantime, why don't you set the table?
His flight was delayed; in the meantime, he read a book at the airport.
She was waiting for the results and, in the meantime, kept herself occupied with other projects.

  • synonym – meanwhile: at the same time; during the intervening time
