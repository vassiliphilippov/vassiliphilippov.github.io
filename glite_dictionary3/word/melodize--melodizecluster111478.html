to melodize

verb
compose or supply a melody
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛləˌdaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛlədaɪz / (BrE)
to provide or compose a melody for something, especially in music

Examples

to melodize a poem
She melodized the lines, turning them into a beautiful song.
Some musicians are able to melodize even the simplest lyrics spontaneously.
The composer decided to melodize the poem for a new choral piece.
