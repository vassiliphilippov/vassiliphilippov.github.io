waiting

noun
the act of remaining inactive while expecting something
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of remaining inactive in one place while expecting something to happen or someone to arrive.

Examples

long waiting period
During the storm, the waiting for news felt endless.
He grew restless after hours of waiting for a reply to his message.
The long waiting tested everyone's patience at the crowded train station.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anticipation: expectation or waiting for something to happen
  • synonym – delay: the period during which something is delayed
