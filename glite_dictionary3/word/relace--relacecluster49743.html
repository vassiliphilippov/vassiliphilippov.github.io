to relace

verb
lace again; thread laces anew
Pronunciation: / riˈleɪs / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪs / (BrE)
to lace (something) again, especially by threading laces through eyelets or holes once more

Examples

to relace old shoes
After getting the new shoelaces, she decided to relace her sneakers for a fresh look.
Every few weeks, I relace my running shoes to make sure they fit securely.
The hiker stopped to relace his boots before continuing up the trail.
