nonspeaking

adjective
not involving speech or spoken lines
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
not capable of or not involving spoken words or speech; used especially of roles, situations, or people where no speaking occurs.

Examples

nonspeaking role
Many background actors in films have nonspeaking parts requiring only physical presence.

The child is nonspeaking but communicates effectively using sign language.
