faultfinding

adjective
inclined to criticize others
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔltˌfaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːltˌfaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
always criticizing or finding mistakes in others or things

Examples
