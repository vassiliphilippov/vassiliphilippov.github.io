boo

noun
cry to express disapproval
Pronunciation: / buː / (AmE), / buː / (BrE)
a loud noise or cry made to show disapproval or contempt, especially from a group of people at a public event

Examples

loud boo from crowd
He let out a loud boo when the speaker made a controversial remark.
Several boos could be heard from the back of the theater when the movie ended abruptly.
The crowd erupted in boos as the referee announced his decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym – cheer: a shout of approval or encouragement
