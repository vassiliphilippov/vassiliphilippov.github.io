draughts

noun
board game similar to checkers
Pronunciation: / dræfts / (AmE), / drɑːfts / (BrE)
A board game for two players in which each has 12 pieces that are moved diagonally on a checkerboard, with the aim of capturing the opponent's pieces by jumping over them.

Examples

play draughts with friends
Many regard draughts as a game of skill and strategy, not just luck.

We spent the rainy afternoon playing draughts by the fireplace.
