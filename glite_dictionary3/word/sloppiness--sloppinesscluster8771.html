sloppiness

noun
excessive sentimental emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːpi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈslɒp.i.nəs / (BrE)
excessive or insincere emotion, especially in a sentimental or maudlin way

Examples

emotional sloppiness
Critics accused the book of descending into emotional sloppiness near the end.
I found the movie's sloppiness distracting rather than moving.
She couldn't bear the sloppiness of the overly sentimental speech.
