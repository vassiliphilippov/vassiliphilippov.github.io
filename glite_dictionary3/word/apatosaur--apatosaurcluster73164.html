apatosaur

noun
large herbivorous Jurassic dinosaur
Pronunciation: / əˈpætəˌsɔr / (AmE), / əˈpætəsɔː / (BrE)
A very large, long-necked, herbivorous dinosaur that lived in North America during the late Jurassic period.

Examples

giant apatosaur skeleton
Children often confuse the apatosaur with the brontosaurus when learning about dinosaurs.
Scientists discovered a nearly complete apatosaur skeleton in Wyoming last year.
The apatosaur was one of the largest land animals to ever walk the Earth.

Related Terms

  • synonym – brontosaurus: another large, long-necked dinosaur of the Jurassic period, closely related to apatosaur
