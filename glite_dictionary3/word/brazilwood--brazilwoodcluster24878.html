brazilwood

noun
tropical tree with red wood used for dye and cabinetry
Pronunciation: / brəˈzɪlˌwʊd / (AmE), / brəˈzɪl.wʊd / (BrE)
a tropical tree with a prickly trunk, whose dense red wood is used for producing red dye and for fine cabinetry
