division

noun
arithmetic operation to find how many times one number fits into another
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / dɪˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
The process of finding how many times one number is contained within another; the arithmetic operation that is the inverse of multiplication.
