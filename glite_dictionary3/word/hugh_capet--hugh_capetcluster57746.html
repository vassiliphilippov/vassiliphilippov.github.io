Hugh Capet

proper noun
first Capetian king of France, 987-996
Pronunciation: / hjuː kæˈpeɪ / (AmE), / hjuː ˈkæpɛt / (BrE)
King of France from 987 to 996 and founder of the Capetian dynasty.

Examples

Hugh Capet founded dynasty
Hugh Capet established the Capetian dynasty, which would rule France for centuries.
After the death of the last Carolingian king, Hugh Capet was elected king of France in 987.
Many historians regard Hugh Capet's reign as a turning point in French history.
