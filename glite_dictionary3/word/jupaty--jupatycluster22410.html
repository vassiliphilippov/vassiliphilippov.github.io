jupaty

noun
tall Brazilian feather palm
Pronunciation: / huːˈpɑːti / (AmE), / huːˈpæti / (BrE)
a tall Brazilian feather palm with a terminal crown of very large, leathery, pinnately divided leaves, whose long, strong stems are used for structural purposes
