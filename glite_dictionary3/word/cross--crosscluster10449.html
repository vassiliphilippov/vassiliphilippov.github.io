to cross

verb
fold or arrange to form a cross
Pronunciation: / krɑs / (AmE), / krɒs / (BrE)
to place or fold one thing over another so as to form a cross; to arrange parts so they intersect

Examples

to cross your arms
He tends to cross his fingers for luck during exams.
It's more comfortable for me to cross my legs while reading.
She politely crossed her ankles while sitting down.
