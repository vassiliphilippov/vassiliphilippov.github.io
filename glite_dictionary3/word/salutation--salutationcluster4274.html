salutation

noun
formal greeting or gesture of goodwill
Pronunciation: / ˌsæl.jəˈteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsæl.jʊˈteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a formal gesture or statement made to greet, show respect, or express goodwill to someone
