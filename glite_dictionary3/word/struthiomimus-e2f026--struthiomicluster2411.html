struthiomimus

noun
a small, ostrich-like dinosaur
Pronunciation: / struːθioʊˈmaɪməs / (AmE), / struːθiəʊˈmʌɪməs / (BrE)
a small, lightly built, toothless dinosaur of the late Cretaceous period that resembled an ostrich in size and proportions
