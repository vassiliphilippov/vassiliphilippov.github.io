Polemoniales

noun
former plant order including Polemoniaceae and others
Pronunciation: / poʊˌlɛməniˈeɪliˌiz / (AmE), / pəʊˌlɛməniˈeɪlɪiːz / (BrE)
a former order of flowering plants that included families such as Polemoniaceae, Solanaceae, Boraginaceae, Labiatae, Lentibulariaceae, and Pedaliaceae; in some classifications, it also included Scrophulariales.
