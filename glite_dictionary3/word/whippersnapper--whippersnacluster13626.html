whippersnapper

noun
cheeky, presumptuous young person
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪpərˌsnæpər / (AmE), / ˈwɪpəˌsnæpə / (BrE)
a young person who is regarded as presumptuous, cheeky, or lacking in respect

Examples
