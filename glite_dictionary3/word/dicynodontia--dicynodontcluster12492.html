Dicynodontia

noun
an extinct order of tusked therapsids
Pronunciation: / daɪˌsaɪnəˈdɒntiə / (AmE), / daɪˌsɪnəˈdɒntɪə /; / daɪˌsaɪnɒnˈtɪə / (BrE)
