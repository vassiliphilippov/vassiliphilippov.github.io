Hyalospongiae

noun
class of sponges with six-rayed siliceous spicules
Pronunciation: / haɪˌæloʊˈspʌndʒiˌi /; / haɪ.ə.ləˈspɒn.dʒi.i / (AmE), / haɪˌaləʊˈspʌndʒɪˌiː /; / haɪ.ə.ləˈspɒn.dʒiː / (BrE)
