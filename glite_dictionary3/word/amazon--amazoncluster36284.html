Amazon

noun
female warrior of Greek myth
Pronunciation: / ˈæm.əˌzɑn / (AmE), / ˈæm.ə.zən / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a member of a legendary race of women warriors from Scythia, reputed for their strength, bravery, and archery skills.

Examples

fierce Amazon warrior
Ancient Greek art often depicted an Amazon in battle attire, wielding a bow and shield.
She commanded her troops with the courage of an Amazon from the old epics.
The myth of the Amazon has inspired countless stories about powerful women in history and legend.
