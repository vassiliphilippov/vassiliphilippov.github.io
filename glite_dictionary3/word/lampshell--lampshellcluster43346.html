lampshell

noun
marine animal with bivalve shell (brachiopod)
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmpˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈlampˌʃɛl / (BrE)
a marine animal with a bivalve shell and a pair of arms bearing tentacles for capturing food, commonly found worldwide; also known as a brachiopod

Fossilized lampshells can be found in many layers of sedimentary rock.
