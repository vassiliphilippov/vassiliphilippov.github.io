stunted

adjective
smaller or less developed than normal
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌntɪd / (AmE), / ˈstʌntɪd / (BrE)
smaller or less developed than normal, usually due to poor growth or unfavorable conditions

Examples

stunted growth
Children in the region often suffer from stunted growth due to poor nutrition.
The stunted trees on the rocky hillside struggled to survive against the cold winds.
The prolonged drought left many of the crops stunted and unable to produce fruit.
