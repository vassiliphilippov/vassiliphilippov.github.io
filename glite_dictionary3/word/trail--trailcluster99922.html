trail

noun
a rough path through wild country
Pronunciation: / treɪl / (AmE), / treɪl / (BrE)
a rough path or track made through wild or hilly country, often for walking, hiking, or riding

Examples

hiking trail

The mountain trail is steep in some places but offers breathtaking views.
We followed the trail through the forest until we reached the lake.
