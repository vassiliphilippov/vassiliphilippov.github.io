longbow

noun
tall, powerful wooden bow
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋˌbəʊ / (BrE)
a tall, powerful wooden bow, typically about 5–6 feet long, drawn by hand and used especially in medieval England for archery and warfare

Examples

longbow archer
Archers trained for years to fully master the longbow.
He learned how to craft a longbow using traditional methods.
The English longbow was crucial in many medieval battles.
