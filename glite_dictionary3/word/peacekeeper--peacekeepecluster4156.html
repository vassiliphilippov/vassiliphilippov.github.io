peacekeeper

noun
a person who keeps or enforces peace
Pronunciation: / ˈpisˌkipər / (AmE), / ˈpiːsˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person who keeps or enforces peace, often by mediating conflicts or maintaining order

Examples

group peacekeeper
As the peacekeeper in our group, she always calms arguments before they escalate.
He acted as a peacekeeper during the heated debate.
UN peacekeepers were sent to the region to help restore order.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mediator: a person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement
