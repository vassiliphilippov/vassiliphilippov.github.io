stake

noun
financial or legal share or interest
Pronunciation: / steɪk / (AmE), / steɪk / (BrE)
a share or interest in a business, project, or enterprise, especially a financial one; a legal right or claim to part of something

Examples

stake in a company
He has no stake in the outcome of the negotiations.
Several investors bought a stake in the new restaurant.
She owns a significant stake in the tech company.

Related Terms

  • synonym – interest: a legal share or involvement in something, especially financially
