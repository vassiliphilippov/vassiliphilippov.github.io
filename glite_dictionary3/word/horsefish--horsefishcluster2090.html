horsefish

noun
a type of flat-bodied marine fish
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a name for several species of silvery marine fish with very flat bodies, often used for certain pipefish or sea horses

Examples

a horsefish swimming
Biologists are studying the horsefish and its adaptation to life in shallow coastal habitats.
Some fishermen mistake the horsefish for a small seahorse due to its unusual body shape.
The horsefish glided gracefully through the clear waters of the reef.
