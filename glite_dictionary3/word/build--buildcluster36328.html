build

noun
character configuration in games
Pronunciation: / bɪld / (AmE), / bɪld / (BrE)
a player's chosen configuration of a game character — the combination of abilities, equipment, and stat allocations.

Examples

character build
Many players favor a high-damage build for end-game dungeons in raids.
He changed his character's build to focus on defense before the boss fight.
Online guides show several viable build options for players who prefer ranged combat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – loadout: the set of equipment or weapons a player selects for a character
