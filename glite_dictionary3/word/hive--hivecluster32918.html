to hive

verb
to store or accumulate like bees
Pronunciation: / haɪv / (AmE), / haɪv / (BrE)
to store or collect something, especially in a manner similar to bees storing honey and pollen; to accumulate and keep for future use

Examples

to hive supplies
After the harvest, the farmer carefully hived the honey for the winter months.
She hived away valuable information in her notebooks over the years.
The research team hived their findings in a secure digital archive.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hoard: to collect and keep large amounts of something, often secretly
