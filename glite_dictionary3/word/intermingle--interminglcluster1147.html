to intermingle

verb
mix or blend together
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɪŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
to mix or blend together, especially so that the components become difficult to distinguish

Examples

to intermingle cultures
At the party, guests from different backgrounds intermingled freely.
The cultures began to intermingle after years of peaceful contact.
The music and conversation intermingled in the crowded café, creating a lively atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blend: mix together thoroughly
  • synonym – combine: mix or join together to form a whole
  • antonym – separate: to move or be apart, not mixed
