basso rilievo

noun
shallow sculptural relief style
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːsoʊ rɪˈljɛvoʊ / (AmE), / ˌbæsəʊ rɪˈljɛvəʊ / (BrE)
a type of sculptural relief in which the forms project only slightly from the background and none are completely undercut
