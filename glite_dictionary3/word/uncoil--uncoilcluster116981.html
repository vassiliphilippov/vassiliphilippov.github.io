to uncoil

verb
to unwind; to straighten from a coil
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɔɪl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔɪl / (BrE)
to straighten or unwind something that is coiled or curled

Examples

to uncoil a snake
As the snake sensed danger, it started to uncoil and get ready to move.
He carefully began to uncoil the hose to water the garden.
She watched the rope slowly uncoil from the spool as the boat drifted away.

Related Terms

  • antonym – coil: to wind into loops or spirals
