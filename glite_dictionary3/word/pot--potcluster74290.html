pot

noun
container for defecation/urination (chamber pot)
Pronunciation: / pɑt / (AmE), / pɒt / (BrE)
a container, typically a round one, used as a plumbing fixture for defecation and urination, especially before indoor toilets became widespread

Examples

chamber pot
Before the advent of indoor plumbing, most households had a pot in each bedroom.
In the middle of the night, she reached under her bed for the pot.
The antique pot was decorated with faded blue flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chamber pot: a bowl kept in a bedroom and used as a toilet, especially in the past
