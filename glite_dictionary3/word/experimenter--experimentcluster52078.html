experimenter

noun
person who carries out experiments
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛr.əˌmɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛr.ɪ.men.tə / (BrE)
a person who conducts scientific tests or investigations to discover something or test a hypothesis

Examples

scientific experimenter
As an experimenter, she always ensured her tests were fair and unbiased.
The experimenter explained the procedure to the participants before beginning the trial.
The experimenter recorded the results carefully to avoid any errors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – researcher: a person who carries out academic or scientific inquiry
  • antonym – subject: a person or animal that is part of an experiment, not conducting it
