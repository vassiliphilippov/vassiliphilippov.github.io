disorganised

adjective
lacking order or organization
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / dɪsˈɔːɡənaɪzd / (BrE)
Lacking order, structure, or proper arrangement; not methodical or systematic.

Examples

disorganised desk
He is often criticised for his disorganised approach to managing his time.
Her desk was so disorganised that she could never find anything when she needed it.
The disorganised process led to repeated mistakes in the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chaotic: completely disordered and unpredictable
  • antonym – organized: arranged in a systematic or structured way
