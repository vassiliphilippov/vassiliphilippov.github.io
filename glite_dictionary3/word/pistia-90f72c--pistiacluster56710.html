pistia

noun
floating aquatic plant (water lettuce)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪstiə / (AmE), / ˈpɪstɪə / (BrE)
A floating aquatic plant of the genus Pistia, with a rosette of soft, wedge-shaped leaves, commonly known as water lettuce and often found as a weed in rivers and lakes of warm regions.

Examples

pistia water lettuce
Local communities often struggle to control the rapid spread of pistia in their waterways.

The pond was covered in pistia, making the surface look lush and green.
