valvular

adjective
relating to valves
Pronunciation: / ˈvælvjələr / (AmE), / ˈvalvjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to, resembling, or functioning by means of a valve or valves.

Examples

valvular heart disease
A valvular mechanism ensures that blood flows in only one direction within the veins.
Certain diseases affect the valvular function of the heart, restricting blood flow.
The surgeon diagnosed a valvular heart defect that required immediate attention.
