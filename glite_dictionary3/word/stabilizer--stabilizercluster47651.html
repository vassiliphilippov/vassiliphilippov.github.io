stabilizer

noun
device or substance for steadiness
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪbəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
a device or substance that makes or keeps something steady or stable

Examples

stabilizer bar on machine
Adding a chemical stabilizer helps keep the mixture uniform over time.
Many photographers use a tripod as a stabilizer to prevent their cameras from shaking.
The aircraft's stabilizer was damaged during the flight, resulting in instability.
