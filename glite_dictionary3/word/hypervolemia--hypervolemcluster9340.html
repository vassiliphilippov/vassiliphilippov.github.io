hypervolemia

noun
increased blood volume (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpɚvoʊˈlimiə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəvəʊˈliːmɪə / (BrE)
an abnormally increased volume of circulating blood in the body

Examples

hypervolemia in patients
