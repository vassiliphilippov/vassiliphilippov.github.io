surveyor

noun
person who conducts a statistical survey
Pronunciation: / sərˈveɪər / (AmE), / səˈveɪə / (BrE)
a person who conducts a statistical survey, typically gathering and analyzing data from a sample of people

Examples

surveyor collects data
A team of surveyors analyzed the collected data for emerging trends in the population.
Each surveyor visited fifty households to ask about voting intentions.
The company hired a surveyor to collect responses for their annual customer satisfaction study.
