neoromanticism

noun
revival movement of Romanticism in art and literature
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.oʊ.roʊˈmæn.tɪ.sɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊ.rəʊˈmæn.tɪ.sɪ.zəm / (BrE)
