to tabularise

verb
arrange in tabular form
Pronunciation: / təˈbjuləˌraɪz / (AmE), / təˈbjuːləraɪz / (BrE)
to arrange or present (data, information, etc.) in a table or tabular form

Examples

to tabularise data
After collecting the survey data, she carefully tabularised her findings in several charts.
The researcher decided to tabularise the results for easier comparison.
We need to tabularise these statistics before presenting them to the committee.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tabularize: arrange or enter in tabular form (American spelling)
