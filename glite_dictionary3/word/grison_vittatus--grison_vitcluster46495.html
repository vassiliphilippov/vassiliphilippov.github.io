Grison vittatus

noun
weasel-like carnivore of Central and South America
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪsən vɪˈteɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪsən vɪˈteɪtəs / (BrE)
A carnivorous mammal of Central and South America, resembling a weasel, with a greyish-white back and dark underparts.
