hypermedia

noun
multimedia system with linked information
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ.pɚˈmiː.di.ə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.pəˈmiː.di.ə / (BrE)
a multimedia system in which items of information are interconnected and can be accessed nonlinearly, often by following links between them

Examples

interactive hypermedia system
Advancements in hypermedia have transformed how information is organized and accessed online.
Educational tools built with hypermedia allow students to interact with videos, texts, and images in one platform.
The new website uses hypermedia to let users easily explore related topics by clicking on links.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hypertext: text system where pieces are linked electronically
