backboard

noun
supportive board for the back
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board placed behind or beneath someone or something to provide support, especially for the back

Examples

on a backboard stretcher
Doctors often use a backboard to prevent spinal injury during emergency situations.
Paramedics carefully lifted the patient onto the backboard before moving her to the ambulance.
