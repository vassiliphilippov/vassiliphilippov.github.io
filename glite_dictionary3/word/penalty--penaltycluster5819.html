penalty

noun
payment for breaking a contract
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnəlti / (AmE), / ˈpɛnəlti / (BrE)
a punishment, disadvantage, or negative consequence for breaking a law, rule, or agreement (can include paying money or other forms of punishment)

Breaking the rules can result in a severe penalty.

  • synonym – punishment: a penalty imposed for doing something wrong, especially legally or morally
  • antonym – reward: something given in recognition of service, effort, or achievement; the opposite of penalty
