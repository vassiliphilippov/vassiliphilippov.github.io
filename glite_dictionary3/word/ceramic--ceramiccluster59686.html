ceramic

adjective
made of ceramic material
Pronunciation: / səˈræmɪk / (AmE), / səˈræmɪk / (BrE)
made of, relating to, or consisting of ceramic material such as pottery or porcelain

Examples

ceramic vase
The collection includes a rare ceramic vase from the eighteenth century.
The company specializes in ceramic products for industrial applications.
We installed elegant ceramic tiles in the kitchen.
