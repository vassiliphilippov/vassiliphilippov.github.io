laciniate

adjective
having finely slashed or jagged edges (esp. in botany)
Pronunciation: / ləˈsɪniˌeɪt / (AmE), / ləˈsɪniːət / (BrE)
(of a leaf or other organ) having edges that are irregularly and finely slashed or cut into narrow, pointed lobes.

Examples

laciniate leaf edges
Botanists often note when petals are laciniate in formal descriptions.
The fern has laciniate leaves that give it a delicate appearance.
The margin of the seaweed is laciniate, adding to its intricate shape.
