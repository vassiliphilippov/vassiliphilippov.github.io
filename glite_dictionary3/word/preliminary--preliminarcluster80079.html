preliminary

adjective
preceding or preparatory; introductory
Pronunciation: / prɪˈlɪməˌnɛri / (AmE), / prɪˈlɪmɪnəri / (BrE)
Occurring before or in preparation for something more important; introductory.

Examples

preliminary meeting discussion
The committee held a preliminary meeting to outline the main topics.
These are just preliminary results; the final report will be available next month.
We conducted a preliminary survey before launching the full study.

Related Terms

  • antonym – concluding: serving to end or finalize
  • synonym – introductory: serving as an introduction; preliminary
