superior

adjective
(astronomy) orbit beyond Earth's
Pronunciation: / suˈpɪriɚ / (AmE), / suːˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
(of a planet) having an orbit farther from the Sun than that of the Earth.

Examples

superior planet
In astronomy, Jupiter is categorized as a superior planet.
Mars is considered a superior planet because it orbits the Sun beyond Earth.
The distinction between superior and inferior planets depends on their orbits relative to Earth's.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inferior: (of a planet) having an orbit closer to the Sun than Earth's orbit
