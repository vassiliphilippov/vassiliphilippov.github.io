carpophore

noun
slender stalk supporting a carpel (botany)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr.pəˌfɔr / (AmE), / kɑːˈpɒ.fɔː / (BrE)
(Botany) A slender stalk that supports or acts as an axis for a carpel or fruit segment in certain plants.

Examples

carpophore of a flower
Botanists examined the carpophore to identify the species of the herbarium specimen.
