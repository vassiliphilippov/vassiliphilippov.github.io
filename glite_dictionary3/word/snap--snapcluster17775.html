snap

noun
informal photograph
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
an informal photograph, often taken quickly, or a short photo/video message sent in the Snapchat app

Examples

a snap picture
He captured a candid snap of his friends laughing at the picnic.
She always takes a snap whenever she sees a beautiful sunset.
The album was full of old snaps from their vacation in Italy.
She sent me a quick snap from the party on Snapchat.
His snap showed the crowded kitchen and lasted only a few seconds.
They exchanged playful snaps all evening instead of sending long messages.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Snapchat message: a photo or video sent using the Snapchat app that usually disappears after viewing
  • synonym – snapshot: an informal photograph taken quickly
