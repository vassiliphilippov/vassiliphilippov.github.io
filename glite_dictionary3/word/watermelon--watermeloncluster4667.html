watermelon

noun
a large, juicy fruit with green rind and red flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtərˌmɛlən /; / ˈwɔtərˌmɛlən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌmɛlən / (BrE)
a large, round or oval fruit with a hard green rind and sweet, juicy red or sometimes yellowish flesh, usually with many black seeds
