backstage

noun
area of a theater out of audience view
Pronunciation: / bækˈsteɪdʒ / (AmE), / bækˈsteɪdʒ / (BrE)
the part of a theater or performance venue that is out of sight of the audience, where preparations and other activities occur

Examples
