Samuel Houston

proper noun
U.S. leader in Texas independence, 1793–1863
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmjuəl ˈhjuːstən / (AmE), / ˈsæmjuːəl ˈhjuːstən / (BrE)
(1793–1863) United States politician and military leader known for his role in securing Texas's independence from Mexico and for making Texas part of the United States.

Samuel Houston led Texas
