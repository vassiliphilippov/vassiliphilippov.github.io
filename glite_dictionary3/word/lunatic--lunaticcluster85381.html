lunatic

noun
reckless or irresponsible person
Pronunciation: / ˈluːnəˌtɪk / (AmE), / ˈluːnətɪk / (BrE)
a person who behaves in a wild, reckless, or irresponsible way

Examples

drive like a lunatic
He drove through the city like a lunatic, ignoring every traffic light.
Only a lunatic would try to climb that mountain without proper equipment.
People looked at her as if she were a lunatic when she started shouting in the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym – madman: a person who behaves in a wildly foolish way
  • synonym – maniac: an extremely reckless or dangerous person
  • antonym – sane person: a person showing sound judgment or rational behavior
