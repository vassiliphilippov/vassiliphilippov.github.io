next

adjective
the following day of the week after the current one
Pronunciation: / nɛkst / (AmE), / nɛkst / (BrE)
(of a day of the week) the day occurring after the present instance of that day; the following (for example, 'next Saturday' means the Saturday after this one).

Examples

next Friday
I'm busy this Friday, but I'm free next Friday.
The meeting is scheduled for next Tuesday at 10 a.m.
We always visit our grandparents next Sunday after church.

Related Terms

  • antonym – this: referring to the current or upcoming day in a week
