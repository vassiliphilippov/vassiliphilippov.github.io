snacker

noun
someone who snacks between meals
Pronunciation: / ˈsnækər / (AmE), / ˈsnækə / (BrE)
a person who regularly eats snacks between meals or eats lightly instead of full meals

Examples

frequent snacker
As a habitual snacker, she always keeps granola bars in her bag.
He tried to stop being a snacker to improve his diet.
Late-night snackers often find it hard to avoid eating unhealthy foods.
