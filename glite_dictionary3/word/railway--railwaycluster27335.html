railway

noun
track for trains
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪlˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈreɪl.weɪ / (BrE)
A track made of steel rails along which trains run; a system of tracks and related infrastructure for train transportation.

Examples

railway track
A new railway would boost trade and travel between the two cities.
Maintenance crews work overnight to repair the damaged railway tracks.
The children were fascinated by the old railway running through the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym – railroad: A line of tracks built for train travel, especially used in American English
