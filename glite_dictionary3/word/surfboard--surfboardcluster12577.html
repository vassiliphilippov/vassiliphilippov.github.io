surfboard

noun
buoyant board used for surfing
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːf.bɔːrd / (AmE), / ˈsɜːf.bɔːd / (BrE)
a long, narrow, buoyant board used for riding on the surface of waves in surfing

Examples

ride a surfboard
After his lesson, he proudly balanced on his surfboard for a few seconds.
Carrying a bright yellow surfboard, she ran across the beach toward the water.
He waxed his surfboard before heading out to catch the morning waves.
