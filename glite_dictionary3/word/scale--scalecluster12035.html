scale

noun
a thin flake of dead skin
Pronunciation: / skeɪl / (AmE), / skeɪl / (BrE)
a thin, flat, rigid plate or flake forming part of the outer covering of animals (like fish or reptiles), plants, or as a protective layer (such as metal armor), or a flake of dead skin
