heel

noun
back/bottom part of a shoe or boot
Pronunciation: / hil / (AmE), / hiːl / (BrE)
the part of a shoe or boot that forms the back and bottom portion, supporting and elevating the rear of the foot

Examples

high heel
My new boots have a sturdy heel that adds some height.
She slipped and the heel of her shoe broke off.
The cobbler repaired the worn-down heel on my favorite pair of shoes.
