malignancy

noun
intense evil or ill will
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪɡ.nən.si / (AmE), / məˈlɪɡ.nən.si / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely harmful, evil, or having intense ill will toward others

Examples

hidden malignancy
He was shocked by the sheer malignancy of her words.
Rumors spread with a malignancy that poisoned the entire community.
The novel explores the malignancy that can fester within a seemingly perfect family.
