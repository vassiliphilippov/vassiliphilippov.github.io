armoury

noun
collection of resources or strategies
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrˌmɛri / (AmE), / ˈɑːməri / (BrE)
A collection of resources, skills, or strategies available for use, especially as metaphorical tools to achieve something.

Examples

armoury of skills
He drew upon his armoury of persuasive techniques to win the debate.
She considered patience one of the greatest tools in her armoury as a teacher.
The company's vast armoury of patents gives it an advantage over competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arsenal: a collection of weapons or resources for a particular purpose
