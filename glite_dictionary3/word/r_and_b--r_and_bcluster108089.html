R and B

noun
popular music genre blending blues and jazz
Pronunciation: / ɑr ənd biː / (AmE), / ɑː ənd biː / (BrE)
a style of popular music developed in the United States by Black musicians, combining elements of blues and jazz, and an important precursor to rock and roll
