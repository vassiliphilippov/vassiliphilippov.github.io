chestnut

adjective
golden to reddish brown (hair, fur, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt / (BrE)
(of hair, feathers, or animals) having a golden brown to reddish brown color.

Examples

chestnut hair
His chestnut hair curled softly around his face.
She admired the horse's shiny chestnut coat under the sunlight.
The bird had a distinctive chestnut patch on its wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym – auburn: of a reddish-brown color, especially hair
  • degree higher – mahogany: a reddish-brown color (often of wood or hair)
  • degree lower – tan: a light brown to brownish orange color
