deployment

noun
distribution of forces or resources for action
Pronunciation: / dɪˈplɔɪmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈplɔɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of positioning or arranging forces, resources, or equipment in readiness for action or work, especially in military or organizational contexts

Examples

deployment of troops
Successful deployment of new technology depends on careful planning and training.
The deployment of additional troops to the border was completed within a week.
The general ordered the deployment of all available units in response to the crisis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mobilization: the act of assembling and preparing troops or resources for active service
