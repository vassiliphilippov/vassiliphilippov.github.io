bold

adjective
clear and distinct in appearance
Pronunciation: / boʊld / (AmE), / bəʊld / (BrE)
(of lines, shapes, or designs) clear, distinct, and easily noticeable; standing out strongly from its surroundings.

Examples

bold colors
She prefers bold patterns when decorating her apartment.
The artist used bold lines to define the shape of the figure.
The title was printed in bold letters at the top of the page.

  • antonym – unobtrusive: not attracting attention; blending in
