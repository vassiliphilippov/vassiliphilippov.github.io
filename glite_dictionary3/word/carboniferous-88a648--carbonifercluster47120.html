carboniferous

adjective
of the Carboniferous geologic period
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrbəˈnɪfərəs / (AmE), / ˌkɑːbənˈɪfərəs / (BrE)
relating to or denoting the Carboniferous period, a geologic era known for its extensive coal-forming forests and deposits

Examples

carboniferous period fossils
