alienating

adjective
causing someone to feel isolated
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪliəˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeɪliəneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
making someone feel isolated, excluded, or unfriendly towards others.

Examples

alienating attitude
Her alienating behavior during meetings made collaboration difficult.
His dismissive attitude had an alienating effect on the team.
The company's new policies were seen as alienating to long-term employees.
