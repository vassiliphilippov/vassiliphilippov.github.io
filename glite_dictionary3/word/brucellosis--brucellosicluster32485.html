brucellosis

noun
infectious disease from animals, causes fever
Pronunciation: / ˌbruːsəˈloʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌbruːsɪˈləʊsɪs /; / ˌbruːsəˈləʊsɪs / (BrE)
an infectious bacterial disease of animals (such as cattle and sheep) that can be transmitted to humans, causing fever and other symptoms
