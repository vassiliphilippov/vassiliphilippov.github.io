Bombacaceae

noun
family of tropical trees with woolly seeds
Pronunciation: / bɑmˌbækəˈsiːi /; / ˌbɑːmbəˈkeɪsiː / (AmE), / bɒmˌbækəˈsiːi /; / ˌbɒmbəˈkeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of tropical trees with large dry or fleshy fruit, usually containing woolly seeds (including the kapok and baobab trees)
