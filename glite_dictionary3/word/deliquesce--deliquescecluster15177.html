to deliquesce

verb
become liquid by absorbing moisture
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛs / (AmE), / ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛs / (BrE)
to become liquid by absorbing moisture from the air, or (of organic matter) to turn to liquid, especially through decay
