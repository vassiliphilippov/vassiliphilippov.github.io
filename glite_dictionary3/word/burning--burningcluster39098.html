burning

noun
the process of combustion
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of a substance reacting with oxygen to produce heat and light; combustion

Examples

burning of wood
During the burning of wood, energy is released as heat and light.
Proper ventilation is crucial during the burning of candles to avoid smoke buildup.
The burning of fossil fuels is a major source of air pollution.
