zipper

noun
a fastener with interlocking teeth and a slider
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪpər / (AmE), / ˈzɪpə / (BrE)
a fastening device made of two toothed strips and a sliding piece that locks them together, used especially in clothing and bags

Examples

zipper on a jacket
Make sure the zipper is closed before you wash the hoodie.
She struggled with the zipper on her suitcase at the airport.
The zipper on my jacket broke during the hike.

Related Terms

  • antonym – button: a small disc or knob sewn onto clothing for fastening
  • synonym – fastener: a device used to close or join things, especially clothing
