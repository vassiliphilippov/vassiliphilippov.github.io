frambesia

noun
infectious tropical disease with skin lesions
Pronunciation: / fræmˈbiːziə / (AmE), / fræmˈbiːziə / (BrE)
An infectious tropical disease resembling syphilis in its early stages, marked by red skin eruptions and ulcerating lesions.

Examples

tropical frambesia disease
Early treatment of frambesia can prevent complications and long-term disability.
Outbreaks of frambesia have declined due to improved public health measures.
The doctors suspected frambesia after seeing the patient's characteristic skin eruptions.
