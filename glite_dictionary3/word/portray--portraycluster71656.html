to portray

verb
describe or represent in words
Pronunciation: / pɔːrˈtreɪ / (AmE), / pɔːˈtreɪ / (BrE)
To describe or represent someone or something in words; to depict in speech or writing.

Examples

to portray a person in writing
Reviewers praised how the novel portrays rural life with honesty and compassion.
The biography portrays the politician as a complex and misunderstood figure.
The documentary portrays the struggles of immigrants trying to build a new life.
