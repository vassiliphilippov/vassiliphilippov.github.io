proceedings

noun
official meeting record
Pronunciation: / prəˈsiːdɪŋz / (AmE), / prəˈsiːdɪŋz / (BrE)
an official written record of what took place at a meeting or conference

Examples

proceedings of a conference
A copy of the proceedings is kept on file for future reference.
She referred to the proceedings to confirm the committee's decision on the project.
The proceedings from last week's board meeting were distributed to all the members.
