choke

noun
valve for carburetor air flow control
Pronunciation: / tʃoʊk / (AmE), / tʃəʊk / (BrE)
a device that reduces or blocks the flow of air, liquid, or electricity in a machine or circuit (such as a valve or electrical component)

Examples

a choke valve
A faulty choke can make the engine difficult to start.

If your audio system hums, adding a choke to the power line might solve the problem.
The technician installed a choke to eliminate unwanted interference in the circuit.
