turning

noun
waste shaving from a lathe
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a small piece or shaving of material, such as metal or wood, produced as waste when shaping an object on a lathe.

Examples

metal turning shavings
After finishing the project, she swept away the wood turnings scattered around the bench.
Brass turnings are collected and sold for recycling at the factory.
The floor of the workshop was covered with metal turnings from the lathe.
