AU

noun
Apple audio plugin format
Pronunciation: / cce6a 8ju / (AmE), / cce6a 8jud0 / (BrE)
a plugin format (Audio Units) used on Apple platforms for audio instruments and effects, loadable in compatible digital audio workstations.

Examples

AU plugin format
The DAW now loads native AU plugins, expanding available audio effects and processing options.
She exported the instrument as an AU so it would work in Logic Pro on her Mac.
To test compatibility, the studio loaded the synth both as an AU and a VST plugin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Audio Unit: Apple's audio plugin specification for instruments and effects.
