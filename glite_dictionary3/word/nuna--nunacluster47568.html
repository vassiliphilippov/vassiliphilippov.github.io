nuna

noun
Korean term for older female (by a male)
Pronunciation: / ˈnuː.nɑ / (AmE), / ˈnuː.nɑ / (BrE)
(in Korean culture) a term used by a male to address or refer to an older female, especially an older sister or close older woman.

Examples

call her nuna
In Korean dramas, you’ll often hear a younger male call an older girl nuna.
Jiho always called his friend’s sister nuna out of respect.
My nuna helps me with my homework whenever I ask.
