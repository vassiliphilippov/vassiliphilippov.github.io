toad

noun
stout, warty-skinned amphibian
Pronunciation: / toʊd / (AmE), / təʊd / (BrE)
A tailless, stout-bodied amphibian, typically with dry, warty skin and long hind limbs, found in both aquatic and terrestrial environments.

Examples

a toad hops
A toad hopped across the garden path as I approached.
Children are often fascinated by the bumpy skin of a toad.
Many species of toad can be found in both wet and dry habitats.

Related Terms

  • synonym – frog: a tailless amphibian, often with smooth skin and long legs for leaping
