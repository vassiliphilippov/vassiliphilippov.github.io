ignorantly

adverb
in an ignorant manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɪɡ.nɚ.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈɪɡ.nərənt.li / (BrE)
In a manner that shows a lack of knowledge, understanding, or awareness.

Examples

to speak ignorantly
He spoke ignorantly about the subject, having done no research at all.
Many people act ignorantly when faced with unfamiliar cultures.
She ignorantly dismissed the warnings, unaware of the potential danger.

Related Terms

  • antonym – knowledgeably: in a knowledgeable manner; with show of knowledge or awareness
