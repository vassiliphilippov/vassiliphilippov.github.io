gluttonously

adverb
in an excessively greedy way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlʌt̬.nəs.li / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌt.nəs.li / (BrE)
In a gluttonous or excessively greedy manner, especially when eating or drinking.

Examples

eat gluttonously at dinner
After fasting all day, she ate gluttonously at the buffet.
He devoured the chocolate cake gluttonously, barely pausing to breathe.
The children grabbed the candies gluttonously, stuffing their pockets before anyone noticed.
