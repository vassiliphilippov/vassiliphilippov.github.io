oneirism

noun
dreamlike mental state while awake
Pronunciation: / oʊˈnaɪrɪzəm / (AmE), / əʊˈnaɪərɪzəm / (BrE)
a mental state characterized by dreamlike experiences or hallucinations while awake, often resembling daydreaming

Examples

experience oneirism episodes
His oneirism made it difficult for him to distinguish reality from fantasy.
Periods of oneirism can occur during episodes of extreme fatigue or illness.
The patient exhibited signs of oneirism after taking the medication.

  • synonym – daydreaming: a series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present
  • degree higher – lucid dreaming: a dream during which the dreamer is aware that they are dreaming
