to slur

verb
speak insultingly or disparagingly
Pronunciation: / slɝː / (AmE), / slɜː / (BrE)
to speak about someone in an insulting or disparaging way; to make a derogatory or offensive remark about someone

Examples

to slur a rival's name
He was accused of trying to slur his opponent's reputation during the debate.
It is wrong to slur people based on their background or beliefs.
The article appeared to slur several members of the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym – insult: to speak or treat with disrespect or scorn
  • antonym – praise: to express warm approval or admiration of someone
