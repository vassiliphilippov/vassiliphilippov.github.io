Max Muller

proper noun
British philologist and Sanskrit scholar
Pronunciation: / mæks ˈmʊlər / (AmE), / mæks ˈmʊlə / (BrE)
(1823–1900) A British philologist and Orientalist, born in Germany, renowned for his pioneering work in the study of Sanskrit and comparative religion.
