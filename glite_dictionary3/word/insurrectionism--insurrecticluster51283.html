insurrectionism

noun
advocacy of revolt against authority
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənɪzəm / (BrE)
The principle or advocacy of revolt against established authority or government.

Examples

insurrectionism against government
He wrote a treatise defending insurrectionism as a moral response to injustice.
The organization's beliefs were rooted in insurrectionism and opposition to tyranny.
Throughout history, insurrectionism has often been met with harsh governmental crackdowns.
