storage

noun
place where goods are kept
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstɔːrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a place or facility where goods, materials, or equipment are kept until needed; a depository for goods

Examples

storage unit rental
After moving, they put all their extra furniture into storage.
Seasonal decorations are kept in storage until they are needed.
The company uses a secure storage facility to keep its inventory safe.
