lightening

noun
descent of uterus before childbirth
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtn̩ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnɪŋ / (BrE)
the descent of the uterus into the pelvic cavity in late pregnancy, when the fetus moves lower in preparation for birth

Examples

pregnancy lightening
After lightening, breathing often becomes easier for the expectant mother.
Some women notice lightening several weeks before labor begins.
The doctor explained that lightening is a normal part of late pregnancy.
