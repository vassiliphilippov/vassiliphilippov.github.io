rockweed

noun
type of brown seaweed on coastal rocks
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌwiːd / (BrE)
a coarse brown seaweed that grows on rocks exposed at low tide, commonly found along seashores

Examples
