ilang-ilang

noun
tropical Asian tree with aromatic flowers
Pronunciation: / iˌlɑŋ iˈlɑŋ / (AmE), / iˌlæŋ iˈlæŋ / (BrE)
An evergreen tropical Asian tree (Cananga odorata) with strongly scented greenish-yellow flowers that yield a valuable essential oil, grown widely as an ornamental plant in the tropics.
