clostridia

noun
rod-shaped bacteria of the genus Clostridium
Pronunciation: / klɑˈstrɪdiə / (AmE), / klɒˈstrɪdiə / (BrE)
Rod-shaped bacteria of the genus Clostridium, often swollen at the center by an endospore and known for causing various diseases in humans and animals.
