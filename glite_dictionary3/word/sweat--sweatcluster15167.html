sweat

noun
moisture condensing on a cold surface
Pronunciation: / swɛt / (AmE), / swɛt / (BrE)
condensation of moisture appearing on a cold surface, resembling perspiration

Examples

sweat on glass
Drops of sweat formed quickly on the surface of the cold bottle.
He wiped the sweat off his lemonade glass before taking a sip.
The iced tea glass was covered in sweat on the humid porch.
