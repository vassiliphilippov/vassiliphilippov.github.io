P.M.

noun
time between noon and midnight
Pronunciation: / ˌpiː ˈɛm / (AmE), / ˌpiː ˈɛm / (BrE)
The period of time from noon until midnight, used to indicate time in the 12-hour clock system.

Examples

P.M. opening hours
Most stores close by 8 P.M. on weekdays.
Please submit your assignments before 11:59 P.M. tonight to be considered on time.
The meeting is scheduled for 3:00 P.M. in the conference room.
