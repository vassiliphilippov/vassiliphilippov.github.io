to bestride

verb
sit or stand with a leg on either side
Pronunciation: / bɪˈstraɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈstraɪd / (BrE)
to sit or stand with a leg on either side of (something); to get up on or mount by straddling

Examples

to bestride a horse
He bestrided his horse with practiced ease.
The conqueror would bestride his new territory proudly.
The statue in the town square bestrides the main entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym – mount: to get up on or climb (something, especially an animal or bicycle)
