novice

noun
person new in a religious order
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑvɪs / (AmE), / ˈnɒvɪs / (BrE)
a person who has entered a religious order and is preparing to take final vows, but has not yet done so

Examples

novice nun
As a novice, she learned the traditions and rules of the convent.
Before taking her vows, she spent a year living as a novice.
The novice attended daily prayers with the rest of the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym – probationer: a person undergoing trial or probation, especially in a religious context
