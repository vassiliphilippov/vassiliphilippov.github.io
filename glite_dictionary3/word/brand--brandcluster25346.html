brand

noun
mark burned into skin for identification
Pronunciation: / brænd / (AmE), / brænd / (BrE)
a mark burned into the skin or a mark (literal or symbolic) that shows disgrace, infamy, or identification

Examples

brand on skin
As a criminal, he was marked with a brand for life.
The cattle each bore a distinctive brand on their flank.
The old photographs showed the brand left by the master's iron.
After his betrayal, he carried the brand of a traitor for the rest of his life.
In ancient societies, criminals sometimes bore a physical brand as a sign of their offense.
The scandal left a permanent brand on the family name.
