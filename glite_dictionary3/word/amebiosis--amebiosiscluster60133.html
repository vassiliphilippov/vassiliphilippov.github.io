amebiosis

noun
infection by disease-causing amoeba
Pronunciation: / əˌmiːbiˈoʊsɪs / (AmE), / əˌmiːbiˈəʊsɪs / (BrE)
an infection caused by disease-producing amoebae, typically of the genus Entamoeba

Examples

amebiosis infection
Proper sanitation can help prevent outbreaks of amebiosis in communities.
