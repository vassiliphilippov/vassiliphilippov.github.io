pocket

noun
opening on billiard table for balls
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
An opening at the corner or side of a billiard or snooker table into which balls are struck.

Examples

billiard pocket
A missed shot caused the cue ball to drop into the nearest pocket.
He aimed carefully and sent the ball straight into the corner pocket.
The black ball hovered near the side pocket throughout the game.
