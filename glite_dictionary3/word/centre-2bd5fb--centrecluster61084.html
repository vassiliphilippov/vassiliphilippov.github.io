to centre

verb
focus attention or effort
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnɚ /; / ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
to focus attention or activity on something; to make something the main point

Examples

to centre your thoughts
Please centre your thoughts on the task at hand.
She tried to centre her energy on finishing her degree.
The discussion centred on the proposed changes to the policy.
His research centres on climate change and its global effects.
The discussion centred around ways to improve public transport.
The story centres on a young woman's journey of self-discovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym – center: To focus on or make the main subject or activity (American spelling)
  • synonym – focus: direct attention or effort on something
