galvanizer

noun
person who inspires others into action
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡal.və.naɪ.zə / (BrE)
a person who stimulates and excites others to take action, especially as a leader or motivator

Examples

Her talent as a galvanizer helped transform the struggling community group into a thriving organization.

The coach was known as a galvanizer who always brought out the best in his team.
