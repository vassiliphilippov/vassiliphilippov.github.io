tatty

adjective
tastelessly showy or gaudy
Pronunciation: / ˈtæti / (AmE), / ˈtæti / (BrE)
Tastelessly showy, flashy, or gaudy, often implying poor quality or cheapness.

Examples

tatty party costume
She wore a tatty costume covered in sequins and gaudy colors to the party.
The living room was filled with tatty decorations that clashed loudly with the walls.
The shop window was crowded with tatty jewelry and flashy trinkets.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flashy: attracting attention by being bright, ostentatious, or showy
  • synonym – gaudy: extravagantly bright or showy, typically so as to be tasteless
  • antonym – understated: presented or expressed in a subtle or restrained way
