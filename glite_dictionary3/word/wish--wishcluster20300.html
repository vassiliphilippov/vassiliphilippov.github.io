wish

noun
polite expression of goodwill
Pronunciation: / wɪʃ / (AmE), / wɪʃ / (BrE)
(usually plural) a polite expression of hope or desire for someone's happiness, success, or well-being.

Examples

send wishes
Please send my warmest wishes to your family.
She sent her best wishes in the card for a speedy recovery.
The newlyweds received countless wishes for happiness and prosperity.
