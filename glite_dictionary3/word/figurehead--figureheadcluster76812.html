figurehead

noun
leader in name only, front person
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡəˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person who holds a nominal position of leadership but has no real power, often used as a front for questionable or illegal activities

Examples

company figurehead leader
The accused mob boss used a figurehead to hide his involvement in the business.
The president was merely a figurehead while real decisions were made behind the scenes.
They appointed her as CEO, but everyone knew she was just a figurehead for the board.
