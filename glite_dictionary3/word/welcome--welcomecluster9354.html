to welcome

verb
to receive or accept gladly
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəm / (BrE)

Examples

to welcome advice

She always welcomes opportunities to learn new skills.
The new policy was welcomed by community leaders.
