expectantly

adverb
in an eagerly waiting manner
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛktəntli / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛktəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you are eagerly waiting for something to happen or for someone to do something

Examples

look expectantly at someone
Children gathered expectantly around the table, eager for the cake to be served.
She waited expectantly for the announcement to begin.
The audience looked expectantly toward the stage as the lights dimmed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hopefully: in a hopeful manner; with expectation that something desired will happen
