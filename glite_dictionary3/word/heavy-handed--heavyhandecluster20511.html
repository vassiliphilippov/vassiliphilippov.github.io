heavy-handed

adjective
clumsy; lacking manual skill
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛvi ˈhændɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɛvi ˈhændɪd / (BrE)
clumsy or lacking skill, especially in using the hands or performing physical tasks

Examples

heavy-handed handling of tools
Despite his heavy-handed efforts, the delicate vase slipped and shattered.
Her heavy-handed approach to painting left smudges all over the canvas.
The heavy-handed apprentice dropped several tools during the repair job.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clumsy: awkward in movement or handling things
  • antonym – deft: skillful and quick in movement
