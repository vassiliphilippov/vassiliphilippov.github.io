Alca torda

noun
a species of northern Atlantic auk (razorbill)
Pronunciation: / ˈælkə ˈtɔrdə / (AmE), / ˈælkə ˈtɔːdə / (BrE)
A species of auk found in the northern Atlantic, recognized by its black-and-white plumage and sharply compressed bill; also known as the razorbill.
