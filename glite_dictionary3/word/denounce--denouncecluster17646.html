to denounce

verb
inform against; betray someone to authorities
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnaʊns / (AmE), / dɪˈnaʊns / (BrE)
to publicly give information about someone, especially to authorities, often exposing them to punishment

Examples

to denounce a criminal
After being pressured by the authorities, he finally denounced his former accomplice.
During the interrogation, the suspect denounced several members of the group to the police.
She refused to denounce her colleagues despite repeated threats.

Related Terms

  • synonym – betray: to give information about someone that puts them at risk, especially to authorities
  • synonym – expose: to make known something hidden, especially wrongdoing
  • antonym – protect: to keep someone safe from harm or exposure
