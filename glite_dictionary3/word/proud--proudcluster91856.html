proud

adjective
feeling pleasure or satisfaction in achievement
Pronunciation: / praʊd / (AmE), / praʊd / (BrE)
feeling deep pleasure, satisfaction, or self-respect as a result of one's own achievements, qualities, or possessions, or those of someone with whom one is closely associated.

Examples

proud of achievements
He felt proud to represent his community at the national conference.
Her parents were extremely proud when she graduated with honors.
Winning the championship was a proud moment for the whole team.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – arrogant: having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • antonym – ashamed: feeling embarrassed or guilty due to one's actions or characteristics
  • degree lower – modest: having a moderate or humble view of one's abilities or achievements
