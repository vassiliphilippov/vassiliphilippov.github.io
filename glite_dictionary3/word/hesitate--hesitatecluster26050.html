to hesitate

verb
pause briefly before acting or speaking
Pronunciation: / ˈhezɪˌteɪt /; / ˈhɛzɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈhezɪteɪt /; / ˈhɛzɪteɪt / (BrE)
