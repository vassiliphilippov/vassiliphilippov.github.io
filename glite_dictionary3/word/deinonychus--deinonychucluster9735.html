Deinonychus

noun
a wolf-sized bipedal dinosaur with a large claw
Pronunciation: / daɪˈnɑnɪkəs /; / daɪˈnɑːnɪkəs / (AmE), / daɪˈnɒnɪkəs / (BrE)
a swift, bipedal carnivorous dinosaur from the Cretaceous period, known for its large curved claw on each hind foot
