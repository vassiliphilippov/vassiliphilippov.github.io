to exhaust

verb
expel a gas or substance from a system
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔst / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːst / (BrE)
to expel (a substance, especially gas or vapour) from a system, such as an engine or plant

Examples

to exhaust gas
After the reaction, the chamber exhausts the remaining steam through a vent.
The factory was fined for exhausting pollutants without proper treatment.
The new filtration system reduces the amount of toxic gases exhausted into the atmosphere.
