purulency

noun
formation or presence of pus
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊr.jələn.si / (AmE), / ˈpjʊə.rʊl.ən.si / (BrE)
The presence or formation of pus, typically as a symptom of infection.

Examples

purulency in a wound
Chronic sinus infections may lead to purulency and discomfort in the affected area.
Post-operative purulency requires immediate medical attention to prevent further complications.
The doctor noted significant purulency in the wound, indicating a serious infection.
