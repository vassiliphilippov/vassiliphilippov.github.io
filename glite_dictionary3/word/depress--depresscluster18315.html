to depress

verb
press down; push a button or key downwards
Pronunciation: / dɪˈprɛs / (AmE), / dɪˈprɛs / (BrE)
to press down or to push something, especially a button or key, downwards

Examples

to depress a button
Please depress the red button to reset the machine.
The elevator won't move until you depress the floor selection button.
You need to depress the clutch pedal before shifting gears.
