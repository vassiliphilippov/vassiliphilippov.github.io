gratified

adjective
feeling pleased after getting what is desired
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræt.əˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡrat.ɪ.faɪd / (BrE)
feeling pleased and satisfied because you have received what you wanted or expected

Examples

gratified by praise
He looked gratified when his efforts were finally acknowledged.
She felt gratified by the praise she received from her teachers.
The company was gratified to see an increase in customer satisfaction.

Related Terms

  • antonym – disappointed: unhappy because something you hoped for did not happen
  • synonym – satisfied: pleased because something has happened in the way that you want
