to balance

verb
be in a steady position; be in equilibrium
Pronunciation: / ˈbæl.əns /; / ˈbæləns / (AmE), / ˈbæl.əns /; / ˈbæləns / (BrE)
to remain or be held in a steady position without falling; to keep something steady

Examples

She balances her laptop on her knees while working on the train.
