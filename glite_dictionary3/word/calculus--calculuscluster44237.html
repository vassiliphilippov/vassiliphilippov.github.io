calculus

noun
deposit on teeth and gums (tartar)
Pronunciation: / ˈkælkjələs / (AmE), / ˈkalkjʊləs / (BrE)
a hard, yellowish or whitish deposit that forms on the teeth and gums, commonly known as tartar

Examples

remove calculus buildup
After removing the calculus, her teeth looked much cleaner.
Regular brushing and flossing can help prevent the build-up of dental calculus.
The dentist found a layer of calculus on his lower teeth during the check-up.
