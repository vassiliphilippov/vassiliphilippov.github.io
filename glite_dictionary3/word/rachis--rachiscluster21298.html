rachis

noun
main axis of a compound leaf or inflorescence
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.kɪs / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.kɪs / (BrE)
the main axis or shaft of a compound leaf or a compound inflorescence in plants

Examples

rachis of a fern
Many ferns have a long rachis from which the leaflets grow symmetrically.
The rachis of the flower cluster was thick and sturdy, holding numerous blossoms.
The feather's central rachis provides support for its barbs and barbules.
