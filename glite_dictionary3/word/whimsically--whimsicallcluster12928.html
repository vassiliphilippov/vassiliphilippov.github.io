whimsically

adverb
in a fanciful or playful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmzɪkli / (AmE), / ˈwɪmzɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is fanciful, playful, or characterized by whimsy.

Examples

to dress whimsically
She whimsically decided to wear mismatched socks to the party, just for fun.
The children decorated their cupcakes whimsically, using sprinkles, candies, and little umbrellas.
The garden was whimsically arranged with brightly colored sculptures hidden among the plants.
