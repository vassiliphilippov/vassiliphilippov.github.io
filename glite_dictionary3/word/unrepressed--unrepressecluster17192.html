unrepressed

adjective
not restrained or suppressed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈprɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈprɛst / (BrE)
(of feelings, impulses, etc.) not restrained, suppressed, or kept under control.

Examples

unrepressed joy
His unrepressed enthusiasm made the whole team feel excited about the project.
She spoke with unrepressed honesty, never hiding her true feelings from anyone.
The therapist encouraged the expression of unrepressed emotions during the session.

Related Terms

  • antonym – repressed: restrained or held back (feelings, impulses, etc.)
