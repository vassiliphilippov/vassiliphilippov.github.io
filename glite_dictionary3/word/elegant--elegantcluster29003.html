elegant

adjective
graceful and simple in movement or design
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlɪgənt / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪgənt / (BrE)
displaying effortless beauty, simplicity, and grace in movement or execution

Examples

elegant solution
He proposed an elegant solution to the complex mathematical problem, impressing the panel of judges.
The elegant dancer captivated everyone with her smooth and flowing movements.
The lines of the building's design are elegant, achieving both simplicity and beauty.
