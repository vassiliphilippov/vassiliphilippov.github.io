Novocaine

noun
local anesthetic (trade name for procaine)
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊ.vəˌkeɪn / (AmE), / ˈnəʊ.vəˌkeɪn / (BrE)
A brand name for procaine hydrochloride, a local anesthetic used especially in dentistry.
