riveter

noun
worker who fastens metals with rivets
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvətər / (AmE), / ˈrɪvɪtə / (BrE)
a worker who fastens pieces of metal together by inserting and hammering rivets

Examples

skilled riveter
As a riveter, she worked on shipbuilding during the war.
The factory hired a skilled riveter to assemble the steel beams.
The job required every riveter to wear safety equipment.
