shine

noun
brightness or glow of light
Pronunciation: / ʃaɪn / (AmE), / ʃaɪn / (BrE)
brightness or radiance produced by light; the quality of being bright and reflecting or emitting light.

Examples

shine on shoes
His shoes had a remarkable shine after they were polished.
The car lost its shine after many years of use.
There was a beautiful shine on her hair in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dullness: the lack of brightness or shine
  • synonym – glow: a steady radiance of light or heat
