toe

noun
one of the digits of the foot
Pronunciation: / toʊ / (AmE), / təʊ / (BrE)
one of the five small parts at the end of each foot in humans and many animals; a digit of the foot.

Examples

toe injury
Each toe helps you keep your balance while walking or running.
She stubbed her toe on the edge of the table and winced in pain.
The child counted the toes on her foot to make sure there were five.
