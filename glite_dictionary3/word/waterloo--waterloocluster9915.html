Waterloo

proper noun
battle in 1815 that ended Napoleon's rule
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬.ɚˌluː / (AmE), / ˌwɔː.təˈluː / (BrE)
the decisive battle fought on 18 June 1815 near Waterloo in Belgium, where British and Prussian forces defeated Napoleon Bonaparte's French army, effectively ending his rule as Emperor of the French

Examples

Waterloo battle 1815
Many historians consider Waterloo to be a turning point in European history.
The Battle of Waterloo marked the end of Napoleon's empire.
Tourists often visit the site of Waterloo to learn about the famous battle.
