hyalin

noun
glassy substance in cartilage and tissues
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪəlɪn / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪəlɪn / (BrE)
a glassy, translucent substance occurring in hyaline cartilage and certain tissues, especially under pathological conditions
