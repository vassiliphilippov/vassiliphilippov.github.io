nascent

adjective
just coming into existence
Pronunciation: / ˈnæsənt / (AmE), / ˈnasənt / (BrE)
(especially of a process or organization) just coming into existence and beginning to display signs of future potential.

Examples

nascent technology
Researchers observed the nascent stages of the disease through careful monitoring.
The nascent industry quickly attracted investments from around the world.
The politician supported several nascent movements for social reform.
