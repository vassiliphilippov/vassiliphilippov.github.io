Asteraceae

noun
plant family including daisies and sunflowers
Pronunciation: / ˌæstɚˈeɪsiˌi /; / ˌæstəˈreɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌæstəˈreɪsiːˌiː /; / ˌæstəˈreɪsiːiː / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants, including daisies, sunflowers, asters, and dandelions, characterized by composite flower heads made up of many small florets
