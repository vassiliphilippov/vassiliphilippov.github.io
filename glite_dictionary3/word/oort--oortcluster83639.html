Oort

proper noun
Dutch astronomer (1900–1992)
Pronunciation: / ɔrt / (AmE), / ɔːt / (BrE)
(Jan Hendrik) Oort (1900–1992), Dutch astronomer renowned for proving that the galaxy is rotating and for proposing the existence of the Oort cloud.
