proprioceptive

adjective
relating to the body's sense of position or movement
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.pri.oʊˈsɛp.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.pri.əˈsɛp.tɪv / (BrE)
relating to proprioception, the body's ability to sense its position and movement in space

Examples

proprioceptive sense
Children with certain developmental disorders may have difficulty processing proprioceptive information.
Good proprioceptive awareness is essential for athletes to maintain balance and coordination during complex movements.
