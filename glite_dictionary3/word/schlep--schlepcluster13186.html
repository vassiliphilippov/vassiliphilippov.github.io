to schlep

verb
carry or drag with effort
Pronunciation: / ʃlɛp / (AmE), / ʃlɛp / (BrE)
to carry or drag something with effort, especially something heavy or awkward

Examples

to schlep heavy bags
After the picnic, we schlepped our chairs and coolers back to the car.
I had to schlep all my groceries home from the store.
She didn't want to schlep her suitcase up the long flight of stairs.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drag: to pull something along the ground with effort
  • synonym – lug: to carry or pull something heavy with effort
