Pembroke

noun
a breed of corgi dog with a short tail
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛmˌbroʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɛm.brʊk / (BrE)
a breed of corgi that is smaller, has straight legs, pointed ears, and a short tail, officially known as the Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Examples

Pembroke Welsh corgi
