benni

noun
East Indian plant yielding sesame
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛni / (AmE), / ˈbɛni / (BrE)
an East Indian annual plant (Sesamum indicum) cultivated for its seeds, which are used to produce sesame oil and as food

Examples

benni seeds
Farmers harvested benni seeds to produce oil used in cooking.
The benni plant thrives in warm, well-drained soils.
