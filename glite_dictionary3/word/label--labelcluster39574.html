label

noun
identifying tag attached to an object
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bəl / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bəl / (BrE)
a piece of paper, cloth, or other material that is attached to an object to give information about it

Examples

label on a bottle
Each bottle had a label with the product name and expiration date.
Please check the label for washing instructions before cleaning the shirt.
The price on the label was much lower than I expected.
