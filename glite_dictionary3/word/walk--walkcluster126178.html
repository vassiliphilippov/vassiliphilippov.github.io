to walk

verb
make an animal walk by leading it
Pronunciation: / wɑk / (AmE), / wɔːk / (BrE)
to make an animal, especially a dog or horse, walk by leading, exercising, or accompanying it on foot

Examples

to walk the dog
He promised to walk the horse around the paddock after breakfast.
Please remember to walk your dog before leaving for work.
She volunteers to walk the dogs at the animal shelter every weekend.
