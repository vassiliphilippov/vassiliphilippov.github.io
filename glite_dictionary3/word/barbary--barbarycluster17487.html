Barbary

proper noun
historical region in North Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbəri / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbəri / (BrE)
a historical region of northern Africa along the Mediterranean coast, stretching from Egypt to Gibraltar, known from the 16th to 19th centuries as a base for pirates

Examples

Barbary coast region
European ships often feared attacks when passing near the Barbary coast during the 17th century.
The Barbary region was notorious for its pirate activity and slave raids.
