Augustan

adjective
of the era of Roman Emperor Augustus
Pronunciation: / ɔˈɡʌstən / (AmE), / ɔːˈɡʌstən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the period of the Roman Emperor Augustus, especially in terms of its art, literature, or culture
