to suburbanize

verb
make or become more like a suburb
Pronunciation: / səˈbɝː.bə.naɪz /; / səˈbɝː.bəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / səˈbɜː.bə.naɪz / (BrE)
to make an area more like a suburb, usually by building houses and infrastructure typical of suburbs
