sunbeam

noun
a ray of sunlight
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌbiːm / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.biːm / (BrE)
a ray of sunlight, especially one that is visible as it passes through the air

Examples

a sunbeam through clouds
A single sunbeam broke through the clouds and lit up the garden.
She watched dust dance in the sunbeam streaming through the window.
The cat lay sleepily in a warm sunbeam on the floor.
