Cassia

noun
genus of leguminous plants
Pronunciation: / ˈkæsiə, ˈkæʃə / (AmE), / ˈkasiə, ˈkaʃə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae, which includes several species often classified as members of the genus Senna or Chamaecrista.
