footloose

adjective
free, unrestricted; not tied down
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌlus / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌluːs / (BrE)
free to go or do as one pleases; not tied down by responsibilities or commitments

Examples

Many people envy the footloose lifestyle of digital nomads who work from anywhere.
