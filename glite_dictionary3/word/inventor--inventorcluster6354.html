inventor

noun
person who creates something new
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛntər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛntə / (BrE)
a person who creates or designs something that has not existed before

Examples

inventor of the telephone
Many great inventors faced failure before achieving success.
The inventor received a patent for her new device.
Thomas Edison is often cited as a famous inventor of the light bulb.
