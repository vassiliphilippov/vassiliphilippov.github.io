to hue

verb
to become colored
Pronunciation: / hjuː / (AmE), / hjuː / (BrE)
(of an object or surface) to take on a particular color or become colored.

Examples

to hue red

The autumn leaves hued into shades of gold and red as the season changed.
The sky hued with purple at dusk, signaling the end of the day.
