nasopharyngeal

adjective
relating to the nasopharynx
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪzoʊˌfærɪnˈdʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌneɪzəʊˌfærɪnˈdʒiːəl / (BrE)
relating to the nasopharynx, the part of the pharynx situated behind the nasal cavity and above the soft palate.

Examples

nasopharyngeal cancer
A nasopharyngeal carcinoma is a rare type of cancer that originates in the upper part of the throat.
A nasopharyngeal swab is often used for diagnosing respiratory infections.
The doctor examined the nasopharyngeal area for any signs of inflammation.
