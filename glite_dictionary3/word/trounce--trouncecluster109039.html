to trounce

verb
defeat thoroughly in competition
Pronunciation: / traʊns / (AmE), / traʊns / (BrE)
to defeat an opponent decisively or thoroughly, especially in a competition, contest, or conflict

Examples

to trounce a rival team
Our school trounced last year’s champions in the basketball tournament.
She trounced her opponent in the final debate.
The team trounced their rivals with a score of 6–0.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beat: to win against someone in a game, competition, or argument
  • synonym – defeat: win a victory over someone in a contest or conflict
  • antonym – lose: to fail to win; to be defeated
