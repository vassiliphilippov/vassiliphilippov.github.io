turnout

noun
widened part of a road for passing or parking
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnˌaʊt / (BrE)
a part of a road that has been widened to allow vehicles to pass or park

Examples

roadside turnout area
The scenic route has several turnouts for drivers to stop and take pictures.
There was a turnout just ahead where we could let the faster vehicles pass.
We pulled into a turnout to admire the mountain view.

  • synonym – lay-by: a place at the side of a road where vehicles may pull off the road and stop
  • synonym – pull-off: an area at the side of a road where vehicles can stop temporarily
