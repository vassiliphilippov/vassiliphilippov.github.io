hazmat

noun
dangerous material or substance
Pronunciation: / ˈhæzˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈhæzˌmat / (BrE)
Dangerous substances, especially those that are harmful to health or the environment, often referred to in transportation and safety regulations.

Examples

hazmat team arrives
A hazmat team was called in after the chemical leak was discovered.
The train was carrying several hazmat containers, so emergency crews responded quickly.
The workers wore special suits to protect themselves from the hazmat at the spill site.
