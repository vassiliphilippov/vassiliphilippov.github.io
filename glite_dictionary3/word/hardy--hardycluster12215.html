hardy

adjective
courageous; not easily frightened
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑr.di / (AmE), / ˈhɑː.di / (BrE)
willing to take risks and undeterred by fear or intimidation; brave and courageous.

Examples

hardy adventurer
Despite the threats, she remained hardy and stood up for what she believed in.
Only the most hardy climbers attempt to reach the mountain's summit in winter.
The hardy volunteers braved the harsh weather to complete the rescue mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intrepid: fearless; showing great courage
  • antonym – timid: showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
