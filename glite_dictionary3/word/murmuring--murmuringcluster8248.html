murmuring

noun
low, indistinct complaint
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːmərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːmərɪŋ / (BrE)
a complaint or expression of dissatisfaction uttered in a low and indistinct tone

Examples

murmuring of protest
Despite the official's reassurances, a quiet murmuring of discontent spread through the crowd.
His suggestion was met with a murmuring of disagreement from several members.
There was a murmuring among the audience when the decision was announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym – complaint: an expression of dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym – grumble: to complain about something in a quiet but angry way
