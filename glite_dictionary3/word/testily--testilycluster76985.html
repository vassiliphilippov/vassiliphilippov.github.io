testily

adverb
in an irritable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstɪli / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪli / (BrE)
In a way that shows irritation or impatience; in a petulant or irritable manner.

Examples

replied testily
"I already told you," he replied testily, his patience wearing thin.
He snapped testily at the reporter when asked another question.
She answered testily, clearly annoyed by the interruption.
