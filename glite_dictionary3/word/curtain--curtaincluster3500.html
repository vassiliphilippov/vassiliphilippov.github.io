curtain

noun
hanging cloth for window or division
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝː.tn̩ / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.tən / (BrE)
A piece of hanging cloth, often used to cover a window or divide a room, that can be opened or closed to block light or provide privacy.

Examples

close the curtain at night
Heavy velvet curtains kept the room dark and quiet.
She pulled the curtain aside to let in some morning sunlight.
We hung a thick curtain between the two areas for extra privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – drape: a long curtain or piece of fabric for covering or decorating a surface, especially in American English
