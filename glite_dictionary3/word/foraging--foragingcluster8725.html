foraging

noun
searching for food or provisions
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.ɪ.dʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɒr.ɪ.dʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of searching widely for food or provisions, especially in the wild

Examples

foraging for wild food
As winter approached, foraging became increasingly difficult for the birds.
Some communities still rely on foraging as their main source of food.
The bears spent most of the day foraging for berries in the forest.
