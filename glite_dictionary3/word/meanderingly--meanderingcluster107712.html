meanderingly

adverb
in a winding, indirect way
Pronunciation: / miˈæn.dɚ.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / miˈæn.dər.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a winding or indirect manner, with many turns or curves.

Examples

to speak meanderingly
He spoke meanderingly, drifting from one topic to another without any clear direction.
The path of the conversation moved meanderingly through memories and forgotten dreams.
The road stretched meanderingly across the plain, avoiding every hill it could.
