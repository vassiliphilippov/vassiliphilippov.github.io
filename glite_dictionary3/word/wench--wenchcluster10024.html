wench

noun
young woman (dated, informal, possibly offensive)
Pronunciation: / wɛntʃ / (AmE), / wɛntʃ / (BrE)
(dated, sometimes humorous or offensive) A young woman, especially one of low social status; formerly used as an informal or disparaging term.
