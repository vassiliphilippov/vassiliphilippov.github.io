padauk

noun
a reddish hardwood from padauk trees
Pronunciation: / pəˈdʊk / (AmE), / pəˈdɑːk / (BrE)
A dense, reddish hardwood obtained from padauk trees, often used in decorative cabinetwork and furniture making.
