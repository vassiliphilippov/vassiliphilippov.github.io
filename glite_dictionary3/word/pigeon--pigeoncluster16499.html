pigeon

noun
plump-bodied bird, often domesticated
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈpɪdʒ.ɪn / (BrE)
a bird of the family Columbidae, typically with a plump body, short legs, and a small head, found both in the wild and as domesticated varieties

Examples

pigeon in the city
A flock of pigeons gathered in the city square, searching for crumbs.
Several pigeons perched on the window ledge, cooing softly.
She keeps a white pigeon as a pet in her backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dove: a bird closely related to and sometimes synonymous with a pigeon, usually smaller and with a cooing call
