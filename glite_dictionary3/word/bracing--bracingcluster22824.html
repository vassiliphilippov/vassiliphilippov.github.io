bracing

noun
structural support for stiffening frameworks
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
a structural component used to strengthen or stiffen a framework or structure

Examples

steel bracing
Steel bracing was installed to reinforce the building's frame against earthquakes.
The engineer recommended adding extra bracing to stabilize the roof during the renovation.
Without proper bracing, the wooden structure would be too weak to support the upper floors.
