duplicability

noun
ability to be duplicated
Pronunciation: / ˌduːplɪkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌdjuːplɪkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being able to be duplicated or reproduced.

Examples

duplicability of results
Concerns were raised about the duplicability of the findings in different laboratories.
Technological improvements have greatly increased the duplicability of high-quality prints.
The duplicability of these experiments is essential for validating the results.
