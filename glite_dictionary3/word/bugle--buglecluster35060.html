to bugle

verb
to play a bugle instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbjuːɡl̩ / (BrE)
to play a sound or signal on a bugle, a simple brass instrument without valves

Examples

to bugle a call
As the band advanced, a musician bugled a stirring tune to rally the troops.
During the ceremony, he stood aside and quietly bugled taps in memory of the fallen.
The soldier bugled the morning reveille at sunrise.
