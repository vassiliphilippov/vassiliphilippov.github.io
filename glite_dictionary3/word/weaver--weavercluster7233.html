weaver

noun
person who weaves fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːvər / (AmE), / ˈwiːvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make fabric by weaving threads together

Examples

skilled weaver
My grandmother was a talented weaver who sold her fabrics at the market.
The weaver skillfully created beautiful patterns using colorful threads.
Traditional villages often have a weaver who supplies cloth for the whole community.

Related Terms

  • synonym – loom operator: a person who operates a loom to weave fabric
  • antonym – tailor: a person who makes or repairs clothes, especially suits and jackets
