snappishly

adverb
in an irritable or curt manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæp.ɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˈsnæp.ɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a rude, curt, or irritable manner; showing annoyance or impatience in speech or behavior.

Examples

reply snappishly to questions
If you continue to answer snappishly, people may stop listening to you.
She replied snappishly when questioned about her plans.
The manager spoke snappishly to the staff after the mistake was made.
