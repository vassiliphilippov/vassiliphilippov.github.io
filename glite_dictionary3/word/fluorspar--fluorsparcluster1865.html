fluorspar

noun
a mineral; chief source of fluorine
Pronunciation: / ˈflʊrˌspɑr / (AmE), / ˈflɔːˌspɑː / (BrE)
A mineral consisting of calcium fluoride that is often fluorescent under ultraviolet light and is the chief source of fluorine.
