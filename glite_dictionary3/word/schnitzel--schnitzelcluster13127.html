schnitzel

noun
breaded, fried meat cutlet
Pronunciation: / ˈʃnɪtsəl / (AmE), / ˈʃnɪtsl̩ / (BrE)
A dish consisting of a thin slice of meat, usually veal or pork, that is breaded and deep-fried.

Examples

crispy schnitzel meal
She learned how to make homemade schnitzel using her grandmother's recipe.
The chef recommends serving schnitzel with a slice of lemon and potato salad.
We ordered a traditional Wiener schnitzel for dinner at the Austrian restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cutlet: a small piece of meat, often breaded and fried
