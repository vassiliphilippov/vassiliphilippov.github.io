vena auricularis

noun
vein serving the ear region
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪnə ɔˌrɪkjəˈlɛrɪs / (AmE), / ˌviːnə ɔːˌrɪkjʊˈlɑːrɪs / (BrE)
Either of two veins that serve the ear region.

Examples

vena auricularis supplies ear
An obstruction in the vena auricularis can lead to swelling of the external ear.
During the surgery, the surgeon carefully identified the vena auricularis to prevent unnecessary bleeding.
The vena auricularis is responsible for draining blood from the outer ear region.
