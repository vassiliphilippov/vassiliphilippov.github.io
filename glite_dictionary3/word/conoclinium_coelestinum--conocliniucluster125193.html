Conoclinium coelestinum

noun
perennial plant with blue flowers, also called blue mistflower
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnəˈklɪniəm ˌsiːlɛsˈtaɪnəm / (AmE), / ˌkɒnəˈklɪnɪəm ˌsiːlɛsˈtiːnəm / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant with large, showy heads of clear blue flowers, native to the central and southeastern United States and the West Indies; also known as blue mistflower or wild ageratum.
