to mill

verb
produce ridges on an edge, esp. coins
Pronunciation: / mɪl / (AmE), / mɪl / (BrE)
to produce a series of ridges or grooves around the edge of an object, especially a coin, usually for identification or decoration.

Examples

to mill a coin
Collectors can tell if a coin has been milled along the edge.
Newly milled coins have uniform ridges that help prevent counterfeiting.
The process to mill the edges of coins was introduced for added security.
