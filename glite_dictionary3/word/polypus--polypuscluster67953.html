polypus

noun
small growth on mucous membrane
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑlɪpəs / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪpəs / (BrE)
a small, abnormal growth on the surface of a mucous membrane, typically having a vascular structure

Examples

polypus in the colon
A polypus in the colon can sometimes lead to more serious health concerns if left untreated.
Doctors discovered a small polypus growing in her throat during the examination.
The surgeon removed a polypus from the patient's nasal cavity.
