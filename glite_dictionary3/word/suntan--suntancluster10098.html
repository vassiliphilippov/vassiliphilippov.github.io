suntan

noun
browning of skin from sun exposure
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌtæn / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.tæn / (BrE)
A browning or darkening of the skin caused by exposure to sunlight.

Examples

suntan after vacation
After a week at the beach, she had a noticeable suntan on her arms and face.
His golden suntan made it clear he had spent the summer outdoors.
Many people use sunscreen to prevent getting a suntan or sunburn.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tan: a golden-brown color of the skin due to sun exposure
