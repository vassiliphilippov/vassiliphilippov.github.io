to recover

verb
reuse materials from waste products
Pronunciation: / rəˈkʌvər / (AmE), / rɪˈkʌvə / (BrE)
to obtain usable materials from waste products and reuse them, typically through recycling or reprocessing

Examples

to recover useful materials
Eggshells and coffee grounds can be recovered as compost rather than ending up in a landfill.
Many modern facilities are designed to recover metals like aluminum and copper from electronic waste.
The company implements processes to recover plastics from discarded packaging for further use.
