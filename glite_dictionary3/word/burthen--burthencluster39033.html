burthen

noun
archaic spelling of 'burden'
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːðən / (AmE), / ˈbɜːθ(ə)n / (BrE)
(Archaic) An alternative spelling of 'burden'; a load, responsibility, or something carried, figuratively or literally.

Examples

heavy burthen
He laid down his burthen after a long and difficult journey.
Poets sometimes use the word burthen instead of 'burden' in old verses.
The burthen of leadership often weighs heavily on those in power.

Related Terms

  • synonym – burden: a load, especially a heavy one; responsibility
