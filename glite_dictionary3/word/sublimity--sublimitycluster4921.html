sublimity

noun
nobility or grandeur in thought or style
Pronunciation: / səˈblɪməti / (AmE), / sʌˈblɪmɪti / (BrE)
nobility or grandeur in thought, feeling, or style; the quality of being sublime

Examples

emotional sublimity
In his writing, he aimed for sublimity rather than mere eloquence.
She was moved by the sublimity of his selfless actions.
The sublimity of the mountain landscape left the hikers speechless.
