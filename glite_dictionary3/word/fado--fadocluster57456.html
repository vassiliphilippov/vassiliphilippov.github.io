fado

noun
Portuguese folk music genre
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːdəʊ / (BrE)
A style of Portuguese folk music characterized by mournful tunes and lyrics, often about longing, love, or loss.

Lisbon is famous for its traditional fado houses where musicians sing about love and sorrow.
