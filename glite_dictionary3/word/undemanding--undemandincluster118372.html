undemanding

adjective
not requiring much effort or skill
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈmæn.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈmɑːn.dɪŋ / (BrE)
not needing much effort, skill, or patience; easy to do or satisfy.

Examples

undemanding job
Despite her busy schedule, she preferred an undemanding hobby like knitting in the evenings.
He chose an undemanding route for their walk, making it easy for everyone to participate.
The role was fairly undemanding, but it gave her time to focus on her studies.
