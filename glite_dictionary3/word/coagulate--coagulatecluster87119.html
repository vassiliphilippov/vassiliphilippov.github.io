to coagulate

verb
change a liquid to a thickened or solid state
Pronunciation: / koʊˈæɡjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈæɡjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to change, or cause to change, from a liquid to a thickened or solid state, usually by a chemical process
