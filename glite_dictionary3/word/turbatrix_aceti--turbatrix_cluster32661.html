Turbatrix aceti

noun
a nematode worm found in vinegar
Pronunciation: / tɝːˈbætrɪks əˈsiːtaɪ / (AmE), / tɜːˈbætrɪks əˈsiːtaɪ / (BrE)
a minute nematode worm, commonly known as the vinegar eel, that feeds on microorganisms in fermenting substances such as vinegar

Examples

Turbatrix aceti in vinegar
Turbatrix aceti are often found in unfiltered vinegar, feeding on bacteria and yeast.
