torpedo

noun
railway warning explosive device
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈpidoʊ /; / tɔrˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / tɔːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
a small explosive device used as a warning, to increase oil/gas flow, or as a firework that explodes with a loud noise
