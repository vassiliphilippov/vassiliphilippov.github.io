semiweekly

noun
periodical issued twice a week
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈwiːkli / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈwiːkli / (BrE)
A periodical published twice each week.

Examples

a semiweekly magazine
Publishing a semiweekly requires a dedicated editorial team to meet the tight schedule.
Subscribers looked forward to each issue of the semiweekly for community updates.
The local news was distributed in a semiweekly that arrived every Tuesday and Friday.
