epicranium

noun
soft tissues covering the skull
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈkreɪniəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkreɪniəm / (BrE)
the muscle, aponeurosis, and skin covering the upper part of the skull (cranium)

Examples

the epicranium of the skull
