solidness

noun
the quality of being solid or reliable
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑlɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being solid, stable, or reliable, especially in a financial, factual, or moral sense.

Examples

solidness of character

The solidness of the company's financial position reassured the investors.
The jury was convinced by the solidness of the evidence presented in court.
