interconnected

adjective
mutually connected
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkəˈnɛktɪd / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkəˈnɛktɪd / (BrE)
having parts or things connected with each other so that they affect or depend on each other

The new software allows all the devices to be interconnected for better efficiency.
