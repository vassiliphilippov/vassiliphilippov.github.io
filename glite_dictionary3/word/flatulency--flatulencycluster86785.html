flatulency

noun
excessive gas in the digestive tract
Pronunciation: / ˈflætʃə-lən-si / (AmE), / ˈflætjʊ-lən-si / (BrE)
the state of having excessive gas in the digestive tract, often leading to discomfort or bloating

Examples

flatulency after eating beans
A diet high in fiber can sometimes increase flatulency in some individuals.
She sought medical advice to deal with persistent flatulency.
The patient complained of recurring flatulency after meals.

Related Terms

  • synonym – flatulence: the accumulation of gas in the alimentary canal
