germ

noun
origin or initial inspiration for later work
Pronunciation: / dʒɝm / (AmE), / dʒɜːm / (BrE)
an initial idea or element that inspires or leads to later development; the origin of something

Examples

germ of an idea
She had only a germ of an idea when she started, but it grew into a major project.
That conversation planted the germ for what would become their new business.
The germ of his novel came from a story he heard as a child.
