groveler

noun
person who acts in an excessively humble or submissive way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːvələr / (AmE), / ˈɡrɒvələ / (BrE)
a person who behaves in an excessively humble or submissive way, often to gain favor or avoid punishment

Examples

a groveler at work
He acted like a complete groveler in front of his boss, agreeing with everything.
Nobody respects a groveler who always puts themselves down to please others.
Tired of being called a groveler, she finally stood up for herself at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym – self-respecting person: someone who maintains dignity and self-respect
  • synonym – sycophant: a person who acts obsequiously toward someone important to gain advantage
  • synonym – toady: a person who behaves obsequiously to someone important
