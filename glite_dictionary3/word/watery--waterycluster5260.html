watery

adjective
wet with water or secreted moisture
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtəri /; / ˈwɑːtəri /; / ˈwɑːt̬əri / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəri / (BrE)
containing, covered with, or resembling water; wet or having a watery appearance
