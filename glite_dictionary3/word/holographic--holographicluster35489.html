holographic

adjective
relating to holography or holograms
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊ.ləˈɡræf.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɒl.əˈɡræf.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using holography or holograms; involving the creation or display of three-dimensional images using laser light

Examples

holographic image
Her presentation included a holographic projection of the solar system, fascinating the audience.
Holographic ticketing, using holographic images for authentication, is becoming more widespread in event security.
The museum featured a holographic display of ancient artifacts that appeared three-dimensional to visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym – hologram: a three-dimensional image formed by the interference of light beams from a laser or other coherent light source
  • synonym – holography: the science and practice of making holograms
