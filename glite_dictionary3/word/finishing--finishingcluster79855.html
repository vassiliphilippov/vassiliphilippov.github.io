finishing

noun
decorative or protective surface coating
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative texture or layer applied to a surface to give it a particular appearance or protection

Examples

finishing coat
He added a layer of protective finishing to the metal sculpture.
The finishing on the kitchen cabinets resists both stains and scratches.
The wooden table features a glossy finishing that highlights the natural grain.
