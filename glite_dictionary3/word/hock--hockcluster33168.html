to hock

verb
disable by cutting the hock (animal)
Pronunciation: / hɑk / (AmE), / hɒk / (BrE)
to disable an animal, especially a horse, by cutting the tendons at the hock joint

Examples

to hock a horse
Deliberately hocking a working animal is now illegal in many countries.
Farmers in the past sometimes hocked livestock as a punishment for straying.
The cruel poachers hocked the horses so they could not escape.
