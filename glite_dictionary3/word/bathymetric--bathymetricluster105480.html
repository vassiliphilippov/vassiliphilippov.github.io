bathymetric

adjective
relating to measuring water depths
Pronunciation: / ˌbæθɪˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌbæθɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of the depths of oceans, seas, or other large bodies of water

Examples

bathymetric survey
A bathymetric survey was conducted to determine the average depth of the ocean trench.
The researchers used bathymetric data to map the underwater terrain of the lake.
These bathymetric charts help sailors avoid underwater hazards.
