dresser

noun
a chest of drawers for clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛs.ɚ /; / ˈdrɛsər / (AmE), / ˈdrɛs.ə /; / ˈdrɛsə / (BrE)
a piece of furniture with drawers, sometimes with a mirror, used for storing clothes and sometimes for dressing or applying makeup

Examples

She kept her jewelry neatly arranged on the dresser in her bedroom.
The old oak dresser had a large mirror and several drawers for cosmetics.
