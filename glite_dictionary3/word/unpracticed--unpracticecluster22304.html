unpracticed

adjective
without practice or experience
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpræktɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈpræktɪst / (BrE)
not having had much or any practice; lacking experience or skill in something.

Examples

an unpracticed driver
As an unpracticed driver, he was nervous about taking the highway for the first time.
Her unpracticed hands struggled to tie the intricate knots required.
The pianist was noticeably unpracticed after a long break from performing.
