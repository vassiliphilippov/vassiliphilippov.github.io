to trash

verb
to criticize harshly
Pronunciation: / træʃ / (AmE), / træʃ / (BrE)
to criticize something or someone very severely, often unfairly or with strong disapproval

Examples

to trash an idea
He felt hurt when his friends trashed his ideas without even considering them.
Some reviewers trashed the movie for its weak plot and poor acting.
The company was trashed online after the latest product failed to meet expectations.

Related Terms

  • antonym – praise: to express approval or admiration
  • synonym – slam: to criticize harshly or fiercely
