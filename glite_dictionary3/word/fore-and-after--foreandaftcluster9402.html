fore-and-after

noun
sailing vessel with fore-and-aft rig
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔr.əndˈæf.tər / (AmE), / ˌfɔː.rəndˈɑːf.tə / (BrE)
A sailing vessel rigged with sails set along the line of the keel rather than across it (i.e., equipped with a fore-and-aft rig).
