candidate

noun
person considered for a position or honor
Pronunciation: / ˈkændəˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkændɪdət / (BrE)
a person who is being considered for a position, office, honor, or prize

Examples

candidate for a job
After careful consideration, the committee chose three candidates for the award.
Each candidate must submit a resume and cover letter before the deadline.
She was a strong candidate for the position of team leader.

Related Terms

  • synonym – applicant: a person who applies for something, especially a job
  • antonym – incumbent: the current holder of an office or position
  • synonym – nominee: a person who has been nominated for an office or award
