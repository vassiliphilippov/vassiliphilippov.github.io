Epimedium grandiflorum

noun
a creeping plant used as ground cover
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈmidiəm ˌɡrændɪˈflɔrəm / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈmiːdiəm ˌɡrændɪˈflɔːrəm / (BrE)
A species of slow-growing creeping plant, often with semi-evergreen leaves on erect wiry stems, commonly used as ground cover in gardens.
