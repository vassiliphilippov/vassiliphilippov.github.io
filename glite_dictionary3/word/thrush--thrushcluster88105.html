thrush

noun
oral fungal infection
Pronunciation: / θrʌʃ / (AmE), / θrʌʃ / (BrE)
A fungal infection of the mouth, especially common in infants or people with weakened immune systems, causing whitish patches and discomfort.

Examples

oral thrush infection
Antifungal medication is often prescribed to treat thrush in infants and adults.
Debilitated patients are especially prone to developing thrush due to their compromised immune systems.
The pediatrician diagnosed the baby with thrush after examining the white patches inside her mouth.
