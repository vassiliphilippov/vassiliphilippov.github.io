peach

noun
a soft, sweet stone fruit
Pronunciation: / pitʃ / (AmE), / piːtʃ / (BrE)
a round, soft fruit with sweet yellow or white flesh, a slightly furry skin, and a stone in the center, eaten fresh or used in cooking
