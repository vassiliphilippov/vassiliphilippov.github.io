acyclovir

noun
antiviral drug for herpes treatment
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪkləˌvɪr / (AmE), / əˈsɪkləˌvɪə / (BrE)
an antiviral drug used chiefly to treat herpes infections; it relieves symptoms but does not cure the disease

Examples

acyclovir tablets
Doctors often prescribe acyclovir to patients experiencing outbreaks of genital herpes.
The pharmacist explained how to take acyclovir tablets for maximum effectiveness.
You should start taking acyclovir as soon as you notice symptoms to help reduce discomfort.
