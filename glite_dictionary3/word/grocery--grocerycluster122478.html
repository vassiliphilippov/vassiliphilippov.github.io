grocery

noun
food and household items sold by a grocer
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊsəri / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsəri / (BrE)
an item of food or other goods sold by a grocer, usually referring to items bought for household use, especially foodstuffs

Examples

buy groceries
He carried the bags of groceries up three flights of stairs.
She stopped at the supermarket to pick up some groceries for dinner.
The rising cost of groceries has made it harder for many families to budget.
