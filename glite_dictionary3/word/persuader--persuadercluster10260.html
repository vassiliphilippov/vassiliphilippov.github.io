persuader

noun
person who tries to convince others
Pronunciation: / pərˈsweɪdər / (AmE), / pəˈsweɪdə / (BrE)
a person who tries to convince others to do or believe something

Examples

skilled persuader
Every good salesperson is a natural persuader who knows how to close a deal.
She was always a skillful persuader, easily convincing her friends to join her projects.
The talented persuader managed to win everyone over to his side during the debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym – convincer: a person who convinces others; synonym for persuader
  • antonym – dissuader: a person who persuades someone not to do something
