soundtrack

noun
recorded music and sound for a film
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊnˌtræk / (AmE), / ˈsaʊnˌtræk / (BrE)
the recorded music, speech, and sound effects that accompany a film, or the narrow strip on a film where this sound is stored
