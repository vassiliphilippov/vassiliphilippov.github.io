communicable

adjective
(of disease) able to be transmitted by infection
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunɪkəbəl / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnɪkəbl / (BrE)
(of a disease) able to be transmitted from one person or organism to another, usually by infection.

Examples

communicable disease
Measles is a highly communicable disease that can spread quickly in unvaccinated populations.
Some illnesses are not communicable and cannot be passed from person to person.
The hospital has strict procedures for handling patients with communicable infections.

Related Terms

  • synonym – contagious: (of a disease) spread by contact; infectious.
  • synonym – infectious: (of a disease) liable to be transmitted to people, organisms, etc.; contagious.
  • antonym – noncommunicable: (of a disease) not transmissible directly from one person to another.
