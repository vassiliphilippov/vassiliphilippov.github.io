to patronize

verb
to sponsor or support
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪtrəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpætrənaɪz / (BrE)
to support or sponsor an organization, activity, or person, especially by providing funding or encouragement

Examples

to patronize the arts
Several wealthy donors patronized the museum, allowing it to expand its exhibits.
She has chosen to patronize various youth scholarship programs in her community.
The company decided to patronize the local arts festival this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sponsor: to support or finance a person, organization, or activity
