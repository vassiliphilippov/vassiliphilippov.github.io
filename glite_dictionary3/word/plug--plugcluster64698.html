plug

noun
blatant promotional mention
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ / (AmE), / plʌɡ / (BrE)
an act of blatant or sensational promotion, especially in media or advertising

Examples

a plug for a product
It's common for musicians to include a plug for their latest album at concerts.
She gave her new book a quick plug during the interview.
The actor casually dropped a plug for his upcoming film on the talk show.

Related Terms

  • synonym – promotion: the activity of promoting something (such as a product or event)
