rhapsodically

adverb
in an ecstatic or enthusiastic manner
Pronunciation: / ræpˈsɒdɪkli / (AmE), / ræpˈsɒdɪkli / (BrE)
In an ecstatic, enthusiastic, or highly emotional manner.

Examples

to speak rhapsodically
He described the sunset rhapsodically, unable to hide his excitement.
She spoke rhapsodically about her experiences traveling the world.
The audience cheered rhapsodically after the final performance.
