terrorisation

noun
the act of causing fear
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəraɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌtɛrərɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of deliberately causing fear, especially through threats or violence (sometimes for political purposes)
