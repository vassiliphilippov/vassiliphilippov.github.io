Sphaerobolaceae

noun
family of fungi ejecting spherical gleba
Pronunciation: / sfɪroʊboʊˈleɪsiˌi / (AmE), / sfɪərəʊbəʊˈleɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
A family of fungi in which the nearly spherical spore mass (gleba) is forcibly ejected at maturity; contains a single genus.
