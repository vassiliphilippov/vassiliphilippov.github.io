seafaring

noun
the work or occupation of a sailor
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌfɛrɪŋ /; / ˈsiˌfɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌfeə.rɪŋ /; / ˈsiːˌfeərɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or occupation of traveling or working by sea, especially as a sailor
