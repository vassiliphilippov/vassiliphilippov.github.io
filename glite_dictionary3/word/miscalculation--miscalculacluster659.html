miscalculation

noun
an error in calculation or judgment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌkælkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌkælkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
An error or mistake made in calculating or judging a numerical or strategic situation.

Examples

serious miscalculation
A small miscalculation in the budget caused major financial problems for the company.
Due to a miscalculation, the rocket missed its intended orbit.
Her miscalculation of the opponent's strength led to a quick defeat.
