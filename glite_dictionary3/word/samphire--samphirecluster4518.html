samphire

noun
succulent coastal plant, used in glass-making
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈsæmfaɪə / (BrE)
a fleshy coastal plant with succulent stems and small scale-like leaves, found in salt marshes and formerly used in glass-making

Examples

fresh samphire salad
