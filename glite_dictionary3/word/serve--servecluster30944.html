to serve

verb
spend time in prison as punishment
Pronunciation: / sɝv / (AmE), / sɜːv / (BrE)
to spend a period of time in prison or in a labor camp as a punishment for a crime

Examples

to serve a sentence
After being convicted, she began to serve her sentence immediately.
He will serve ten years in prison for his crimes.
Many inmates hope to serve only part of their time with good behavior.
