agateware

noun
veined, mottled pottery resembling agate
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡətˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈæɡətˌwɛə / (BrE)
Pottery with a veined and mottled appearance designed to resemble agate, often made by combining clays of different colors.

Examples

agateware pottery bowl
Potters achieve the distinctive appearance of agateware by layering different colored clays.
