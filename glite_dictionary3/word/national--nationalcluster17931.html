national

noun
citizen or subject of a nation
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃ.ən.əl / (AmE), / ˈnaʃ.ən.əl / (BrE)
a person who is a citizen or subject of a particular nation, especially in relation to legal or immigration status

Examples

foreign national
Foreign nationals must have a valid visa to work in this country.
He was arrested along with several other French nationals.
The embassy provides services to its nationals who are living abroad.

Related Terms

  • antonym – alien: a person who is not a citizen or national of a country
  • synonym – citizen: a legally recognized subject or member of a state or nation
