agreeable

adjective
willing to agree or consent
Pronunciation: / əˈɡri.ə.bəl / (AmE), / əˈɡriː.ə.bəl / (BrE)
willing to agree with or accept something; ready to give consent.

Examples

agreeable to a plan
If you are agreeable to the terms, please sign the contract.
My boss seemed agreeable to changing the project deadline.
She was agreeable to the idea of meeting next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – consenting: willing to agree or allow something
  • antonym – unwilling: not willing to do something or allow something to happen
