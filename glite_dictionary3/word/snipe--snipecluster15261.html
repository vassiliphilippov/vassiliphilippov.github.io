to snipe

verb
place a last‑second bid in an online auction
Pronunciation: / snaɪp / (AmE), / snaɪp / (BrE)
to place a bid in an online auction at the last possible moment (often automatically) in order to win by preventing others from responding.

Examples

to snipe an auction
She used a bidding bot to snipe the auction at the last second.
He successfully sniped the rare poster with a five‑dollar bid one second before closing.
Many collectors prefer sniping because it prevents rivals from reacting before the auction ends.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bid: to make an offer of money at an auction
  • synonym – outbid: to offer a higher bid than someone else
