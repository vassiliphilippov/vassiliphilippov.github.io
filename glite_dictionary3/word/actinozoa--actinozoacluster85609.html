Actinozoa

noun
class of marine animals, includes corals and anemones
Pronunciation: / ˌæktɪnoʊˈzoʊə /; / ˌæktɪnəˈzoʊə / (AmE), / ˌæktɪnəʊˈzəʊə /; / ˌæktɪnəˈzəʊə / (BrE)
a large class of sedentary marine animals, including sea anemones and corals, that do not have a medusoid (jellyfish-like) stage
