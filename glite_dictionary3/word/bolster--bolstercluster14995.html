to bolster

verb
add padding or support
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊl.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊl.stə / (BrE)
to add padding or support to something, making it more comfortable or secure

Examples

to bolster a seat
She used extra foam to bolster the cushions on the old sofa.
The carpenter decided to bolster the seat of the chair to make it more comfortable.
They bolstered their camping mattress for a better night’s sleep.
