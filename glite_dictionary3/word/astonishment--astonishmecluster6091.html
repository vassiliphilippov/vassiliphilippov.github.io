astonishment

noun
great surprise or amazement
Pronunciation: / əˈstɑnɪʃmənt / (AmE), / əˈstɒnɪʃmənt / (BrE)

Examples

in astonishment at news
She stared at him in astonishment when he revealed the news.
The magician's trick left the entire audience in astonishment.
To her astonishment, the long-lost painting was found in the attic.
