hemoglobinemia

noun
excess hemoglobin in blood plasma
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˌɡloʊbɪˈniːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˌɡləʊbɪˈniːmiə / (BrE)
The presence of an abnormally high concentration of hemoglobin in the blood plasma.

Examples

hemoglobinemia after surgery
