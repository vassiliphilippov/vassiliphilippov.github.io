paddle

noun
flat bat used for hitting balls in games
Pronunciation: / ˈpæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpæd.əl / (BrE)
a small, usually wooden, bat with a flat surface used to hit balls in games such as table tennis or paddleball

Examples

paddle for table tennis
He bought a new paddle for his favorite paddleball game.
She picked up her paddle before starting the table tennis match.
The children played with wooden paddles and a rubber ball in the backyard.
