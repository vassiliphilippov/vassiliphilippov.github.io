rationally

adverb
in a logical and reasonable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈræʃənəli / (AmE), / ˈræʃənəli / (BrE)
In a logical and reasonable way, based on facts or clear thinking.

Examples

to think rationally
Despite her anger, she responded rationally to the criticism.
He approached the problem rationally, weighing all the evidence before acting.
She tried to rationally explain her decision to the team.
