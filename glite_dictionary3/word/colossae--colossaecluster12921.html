Colossae

proper noun
ancient city in Phrygia, Asia Minor
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑːsi.i / (AmE), / kəˈlɒsi.i / (BrE)
an ancient city in southwestern Phrygia in Asia Minor, known as the site of an early Christian church addressed by Paul's Epistle to the Colossians

Archaeologists have long studied the ruins of Colossae to learn more about ancient Phrygian culture.
