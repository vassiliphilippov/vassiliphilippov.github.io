etui

noun
small ornamental case or bag for small articles
Pronunciation: / eɪˈtwi / (AmE), / eɪˈtwiː / (BrE)
A small ornamental case or bag, often used by women to carry small articles such as needles, cosmetics, or sewing materials.

Examples

etui for sewing tools
She kept her embroidery scissors in a delicate silk etui on her dressing table.
The museum displayed a 19th-century ivory etui decorated with tiny paintings.
Victorian ladies often carried an etui containing needles, thread, and other sewing tools.
