undesirable

noun
an unwanted or objectionable person
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪərəbəl / (BrE)
a person who is considered unwanted, unpleasant, or objectionable in a particular place or group.

Examples

an undesirable person

Some clubs have strict door policies to keep undesirables away.
The police were criticized for targeting so-called undesirables in the city center.
