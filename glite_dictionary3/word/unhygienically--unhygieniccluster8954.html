unhygienically

adverb
in an unsanitary or unclean manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnhaɪˈdʒiːnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌʌnhaɪˈdʒiːnɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is not clean or sanitary; in a manner likely to cause or spread illness or infection.

Examples

to prepare food unhygienically
Food was prepared unhygienically in the basement kitchen, leading to several cases of food poisoning.
Many street vendors are criticized for selling snacks that are cooked unhygienically.
The hospital was fined after it was found that medical instruments were being handled unhygienically.

Related Terms

  • synonym – unhygienic: not clean or sanitary; likely to cause infection
