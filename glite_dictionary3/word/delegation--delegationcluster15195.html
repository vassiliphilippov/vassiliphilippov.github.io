delegation

noun
group of representatives
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛl.əˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛl.ɪˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a group of people chosen or appointed to represent others, especially at a conference or meeting

Examples

a delegation attends meeting
A French delegation attended the peace talks in Geneva.
Each country sent a delegation to participate in the summit.
