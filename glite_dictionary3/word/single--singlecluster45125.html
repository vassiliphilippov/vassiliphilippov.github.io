single

adjective
concentrated or undivided (devotion, attention, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl / (BrE)
(of attention, devotion, feeling, etc.) concentrated on one object or objective; undivided; exclusive.

Examples

single focus goal
His affection for his childhood home remained single throughout his life.
She pursued her goal with single devotion, never allowing distractions to interfere.
The teacher gave single attention to each student during the session.

Related Terms

  • synonym – exclusive: restricted to a single object or person; not shared
  • synonym – undivided: not separated or shared; whole or entire (especially in reference to attention or feeling)
