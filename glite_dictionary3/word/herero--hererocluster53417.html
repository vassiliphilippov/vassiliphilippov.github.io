Herero

noun
a member of a Bantu ethnic group in southwestern Africa
Pronunciation: / həˈrɛroʊ / (AmE), / həˈrɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu-speaking pastoral people living mainly in Namibia, Botswana, and Angola

Herero people
Many Herero live in the central region of Namibia.
