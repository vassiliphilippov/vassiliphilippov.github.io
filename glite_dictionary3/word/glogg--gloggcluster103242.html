glogg

noun
Scandinavian spiced hot punch
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌɡ / (AmE), / ɡlɒɡ / (BrE)
A Scandinavian spiced punch made with claret and aquavit, often including spices, raisins, orange peel, and sugar, served hot.
