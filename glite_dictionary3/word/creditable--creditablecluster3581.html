creditable

adjective
worthy of praise or approval
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪtəbəl / (BrE)
deserving praise or approval, though not necessarily outstanding or exceptional.

Examples

creditable performance
Although the project was late, the team's effort was still creditable given the challenges.
Her performance in the play was creditable, even if not outstanding.
The company made a creditable attempt to reduce waste this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – commendable: deserving praise or recognition
  • antonym – discreditable: deserving shame or criticism; bringing disrepute
