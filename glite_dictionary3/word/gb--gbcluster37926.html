GB

noun
unit of digital information (gigabyte)
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːˈbiː /; / dʒiː biː / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːˈbiː /; / dʒiː biː / (BrE)
a unit of digital information storage equal to about one billion bytes; abbreviation for gigabyte
