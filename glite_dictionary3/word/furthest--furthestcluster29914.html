furthest

adjective
most distant in space, time, or order
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːðəst / (AmE), / ˈfɜːðɪst / (BrE)
at or to the greatest distance in space, time, or order; most remote or advanced

Examples

furthest planet away
Of all the planets, Pluto is the furthest from the Sun.
She reached the furthest point of the trail before turning back.
This is the furthest we have ever traveled on our annual road trips.
