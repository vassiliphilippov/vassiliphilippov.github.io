refueling

noun
the act of supplying or taking on fuel
Pronunciation: / riˈfjulɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈfjuːəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of supplying fuel to something, especially a vehicle or machine, or taking on fuel oneself

Examples

refueling a car
Emergency refueling operations were set up after the storm disrupted supply lines.
The airplane required refueling before it could continue its journey.
They scheduled a quick refueling stop at the nearest gas station.
