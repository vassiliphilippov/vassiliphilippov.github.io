to key

verb
to provide with a key
Pronunciation: / ki / (AmE), / kiː / (BrE)
to provide (a place or lock) with a key; to supply someone with the key(s) to something

Examples

to key a lock
After losing his keys, he had his house keyed again for security.
Our new office was keyed to match the master key system.
The landlord keyed every apartment after the renovation.
