frigidness

noun
the state of being very cold
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪdʒ.ɪd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfrɪdʒ.ɪd.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being very cold; lack of warmth or heat

Examples

winter frigidness
She shivered at the frigidness in the room after the heater broke.
The frigidness of the Arctic night can be life-threatening without proper clothing.
The frigidness of the wind made it impossible to stay outside for long.
