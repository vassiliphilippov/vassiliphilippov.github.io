reel

noun
device for winding fishing line
Pronunciation: / ril / (AmE), / riːl / (BrE)
a cylindrical device used for winding up or letting out fishing line, film, thread, or other flexible material

Examples

fishing reel device
He attached the new reel to his fishing rod before heading to the lake.
She carefully wound the fishing line back onto the reel after a long day.
The line tangled in the reel made it difficult to cast properly.
He bought a new reel of thread for his sewing machine.
She wound the fishing line tightly onto the reel before casting her rod.
The old film reel was stored in a metal canister in the archive.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bobbin: a small cylinder used to wind thread or wire, especially in sewing or weaving machines
  • synonym – spool: a cylindrical device onto which thread, wire, or other material is wound
