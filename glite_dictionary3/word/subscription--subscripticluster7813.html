subscription

noun
payment for periodical issues
Pronunciation: / səbˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / səbˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
a payment or arrangement to receive regular access to a publication, service, or digital content

Examples

subscription to a magazine
His subscription expired last week, so he missed the latest issue.
She renewed her subscription to the magazine for another year.
The newspaper offers a monthly subscription that includes weekend deliveries.
She renewed her subscription to the streaming platform for another year.
The company's new app offers a weekly trial before users choose a subscription plan.
Many people cancel a subscription when the monthly fee becomes too expensive.
