rhinoscopy

noun
medical examination of nasal passages
Pronunciation: / raɪˈnɑːskəpi / (AmE), / raɪˈnɒskəpi / (BrE)
a medical examination of the nasal passages, typically using a rhinoscope

Examples

rhinoscopy procedure
A rhinoscopy can help detect polyps, tumors, or other abnormalities in the nasal passages.
After the injury, a rhinoscopy revealed a small tear inside her nasal cavity.
The doctor performed a rhinoscopy to diagnose the patient's persistent nasal congestion.
