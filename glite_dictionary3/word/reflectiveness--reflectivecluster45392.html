reflectiveness

noun
quality of thoughtful contemplation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛktɪvnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛktɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being thoughtful, especially in a quiet and contemplative manner

Examples

deep reflectiveness
Her reflectiveness became apparent as she silently pondered the meaning of the poem.
Periods of reflectiveness often help people make better decisions.
The teacher admired his student's reflectiveness during class discussions.

  • synonym – thoughtfulness: the quality of being considerate or reflective
