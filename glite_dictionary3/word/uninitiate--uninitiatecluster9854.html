uninitiate

adjective
lacking experience or initiation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈnɪʃiət / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈnɪʃiət / (BrE)
lacking experience or knowledge in a particular subject or activity; not having been introduced to something.

Examples

uninitiate members
The ancient ritual baffled the uninitiate in the audience.
This guide is intended to help the uninitiate explore the basics of wine tasting.
To the uninitiate, quantum physics can seem impossibly complex.
