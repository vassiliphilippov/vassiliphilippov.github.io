puff

adjective
gathered to bulge outward (in garments)
Pronunciation: / pʌf / (AmE), / pʌf / (BrE)
(of a part of a garment) gathered or shaped so as to bulge or protrude outward for fullness and decorative effect.

Examples

puff sleeves dress
She wore a dress with puff sleeves for the costume party.
The designer added puff shoulders to give the blouse a vintage look.
Those puff cuffs make the shirt look more elegant and playful.
