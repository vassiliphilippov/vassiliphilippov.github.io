piaster

noun
monetary unit; 1/100 lira (Turkey)
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪˌæstər /; / piˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈpɪəstə /; / piˈæstə / (BrE)
a small unit of money, equal to one hundredth of a lira or pound, used in several countries such as Turkey, Egypt, Lebanon, Sudan, and Syria
