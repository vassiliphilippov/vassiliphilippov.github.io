mho

noun
unit of electrical conductance
Pronunciation: / moʊ / (AmE), / məʊ / (BrE)
A unit of electrical conductance equal to the reciprocal of an ohm.

Examples

one mho of conductance
Although the mho has largely been replaced by the siemens, it is still found in older texts.
One mho is the conductance value when one ampere flows with a potential difference of one volt.
The conductance of the circuit was measured as 5 mhos.
