stifle

noun
joint in quadrupeds, like the knee
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈstaɪf(ə)l / (BrE)
the joint between the femur and tibia in a quadruped animal, corresponding to the human knee

Examples

dog's stifle joint
A torn ligament in the dog's stifle may require surgery to heal properly.
Lameness in the hind leg is often caused by problems in the stifle joint.
The veterinarian examined the horse's stifle carefully for signs of swelling.
