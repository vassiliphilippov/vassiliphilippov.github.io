permit

noun
a large game fish of the West Indies
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.mɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.mɪt / (BrE)
a large game fish (Trachinotus falcatus) found in the western Atlantic Ocean, particularly around the West Indies, popular with sport fishers.
