sweet

adjective
having a high sugar content (wine)
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
having a sugary taste; tasting of sugar or something similar (including food, drink, or wine)

Examples

sweet chocolate cake

Related Terms

  • antonym – bitter: having a sharp, often unpleasant taste; not sweet
  • antonym – dry: (of wines) lacking sweetness; having no or little residual sugar
  • antonym – savory: not sweet; salty or spicy in taste
  • antonym – sour: having an acidic taste; not sweet
  • synonym – sugary: containing or tasting like sugar
  • antonym – unsweetened: (of food or drink) not having added sugar or sweetener
