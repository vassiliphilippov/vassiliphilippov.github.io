furthest

adverb
to the greatest degree or extent
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːðɪst / (AmE), / ˈfɜːðɪst / (BrE)
to the greatest degree or extent; at or to the most advanced stage, especially in abstract or figurative contexts

Examples

furthest from normal
His artwork is furthest removed from anything conventional or traditional.
Of all the competitors, Marie ran furthest into the dense forest before turning back.
This proposal takes our research furthest in exploring the impact of new technologies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – farthest: to the greatest distance (often used for physical space)
  • antonym – nearest: closest in distance or relation
