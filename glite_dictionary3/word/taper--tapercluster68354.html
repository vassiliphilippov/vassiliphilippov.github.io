to taper

verb
diminish or become smaller gradually
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈteɪpə / (BrE)
to become gradually smaller, fewer, or less; to decrease gradually in size, amount, or intensity

Examples

taper off attendance
Attendance at the event began to taper as the evening wore on.
Her interest in the project gradually tapered over the months.
The pain should taper off after you start the new medication.

Related Terms

  • synonym – diminish: to decrease or make smaller or less
  • antonym – increase: to become or make greater in size, amount, or degree
