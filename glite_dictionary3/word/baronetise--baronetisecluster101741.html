to baronetise

verb
make someone a baronet
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrənˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbærənətaɪz / (BrE)
to confer the rank or title of baronet upon someone

Examples

to baronetise a person
After years of public service, he was finally baronetised by the crown.
It was rare for someone outside the nobility to be baronetised in those days.
