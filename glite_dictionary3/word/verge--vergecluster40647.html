verge

noun
grass border by a road
Pronunciation: / vɝdʒ / (AmE), / vɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a grass strip or border of land along the edge of a road, path, or pavement

Examples

roadside verge grass
After the rain, the verge beside the motorway was dotted with wildflowers.
Parking on the grassy verge is not permitted in this area.
The council decided to plant flowers along the verge to brighten up the street.
