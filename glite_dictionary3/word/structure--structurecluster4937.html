structure

noun
anatomical part of an organism
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌktʃər / (AmE), / ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
a particular complex part of a living organism, defined by its construction and arrangement of tissues or bones

Examples

bone structure
Doctors examined the muscle structure to assess the injury.
Good bone structure contributes to a balanced facial appearance.
The leaf's internal structure allows it to perform photosynthesis efficiently.
