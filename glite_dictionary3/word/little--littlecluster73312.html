little

adjective
(of a voice) faint or soft
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l̩ / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl / (BrE)
(of a voice) faint or soft; not loud or strong.

Examples

a little voice
A little sigh escaped his lips, barely audible in the quiet.
She heard a little voice whispering from the next room.
The child spoke in such a little voice that no one noticed her at first.
