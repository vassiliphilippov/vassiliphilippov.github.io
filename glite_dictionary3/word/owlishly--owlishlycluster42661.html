owlishly

adverb
in an owl-like manner
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊ.lɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˈaʊ.lɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that resembles or suggests an owl, especially by being solemn, wise, or watchful in appearance or behavior.

Examples

look owlishly serious
He nodded owlishly during the meeting, giving everyone the impression he was thinking deeply.
She peered owlishly at the document, trying to decipher the handwriting.
The professor looked owlishly over his glasses at the noisy students.

  • synonym – wisely: in a wise manner
