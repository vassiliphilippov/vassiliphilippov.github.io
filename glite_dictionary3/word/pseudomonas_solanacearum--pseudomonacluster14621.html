Pseudomonas solanacearum

noun
bacterium causing brown rot in crops
Pronunciation: / suːdəˈmoʊnəs ˌsoʊləneɪˈserəm / (AmE), / sjuːdəˈməʊnəs ˌsɒlənəˈseərəm / (BrE)
a species of bacterium that causes brown rot in crops such as tomatoes, potatoes, and tobacco

Examples

Pseudomonas solanacearum infects potatoes
