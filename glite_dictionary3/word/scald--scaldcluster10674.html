to scald

verb
heat liquid to just below boiling
Pronunciation: / skɔld / (AmE), / skɔːld / (BrE)
to heat a liquid, especially milk, to near boiling point without actually boiling it

Examples

to scald milk
Carefully scald the milk before adding it to the recipe.
The chef instructed the apprentice to scald the cream gently over a low flame.
You should scald the water to sterilize the bottles.
