semiautomatic

noun
a pistol that reloads automatically with each shot
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌɔtəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌɔːtəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
a pistol that automatically reloads after each shot but requires a separate pull of the trigger for each round fired

Examples

semiautomatic pistol
He prefers carrying a semiautomatic for its quick reloading feature and reliability.
The new law restricts the sale of semiautomatics to civilians in the state.
The officer drew his semiautomatic and ordered the suspect to put his hands up.

Related Terms
