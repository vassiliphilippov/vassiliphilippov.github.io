potpourri

noun
medley of musical pieces
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊpʊˈri / (AmE), / ˌpɒpʊˈri / (BrE)
a musical composition made up of a collection of different songs or musical pieces, often taken from various sources and arranged as a continuous sequence

Examples

potpourri of melodies
She arranged a potpourri for piano using melodies from different musicals.
The orchestra performed a potpourri of popular movie themes at the concert.
Their latest album ends with a lively potpourri combining folk and jazz tunes.
