blowup

noun
violent explosion or release of energy
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊ.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈbləʊ.ʌp / (BrE)
an explosion or a sudden and violent release of energy, especially as a result of a chemical or nuclear reaction

Examples

nuclear blowup
Scientists are studying the effects of a nuclear blowup on the environment.
The chemical plant experienced a sudden blowup after a leak went undetected.
The factory was destroyed in a massive blowup last year.
