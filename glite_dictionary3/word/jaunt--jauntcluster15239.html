to jaunt

verb
go on a short pleasure trip
Pronunciation: / dʒɑnt / (AmE), / dʒɑːnt / (BrE)
to go on a short journey or excursion for pleasure

Examples

to jaunt around town
Last spring, she jaunted through the countryside, stopping at every small village.
They often jaunt around Europe during the summer holidays.
We decided to jaunt off to the seaside for the weekend.

Related Terms

  • antonym – commute: to travel regularly to and from work
  • synonym – excursion: a short journey or trip for pleasure
