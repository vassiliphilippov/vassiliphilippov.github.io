electrotherapy

noun
therapeutic use of electric currents
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the medical treatment of diseases or injuries by applying electric currents to the body

Examples

electrotherapy for pain relief
After her injury, the doctor recommended electrotherapy to speed up her recovery.
Many clinics now offer electrotherapy to help patients with chronic muscle pain.
Some forms of electrotherapy are used to treat certain types of paralysis.
