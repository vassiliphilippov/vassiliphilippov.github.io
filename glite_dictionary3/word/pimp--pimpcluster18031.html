to pimp

verb
decorate or upgrade ostentatiously
Pronunciation: / pɪmp / (AmE), / pɪmp / (BrE)
informal slang: to decorate, modify, or upgrade something (especially a vehicle or gadget) in a showy or ostentatious way.

Examples

to pimp a car
They decided to pimp their car with neon underglow and an eye-catching decal for the show.
She pimped her laptop with a colorful skin and a brighter keyboard backlight.
At the event, the volunteers were pimping an old bicycle to make it look like a custom build.

Related Terms

  • synonym – customize: to change something to suit a particular person or purpose
  • synonym – trick out: informal: to add attractive or impressive features to something
