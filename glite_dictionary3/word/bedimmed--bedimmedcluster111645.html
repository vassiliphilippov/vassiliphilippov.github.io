bedimmed

adjective
made dim or obscured
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdɪmd / (AmE), / bɪˈdɪmd / (BrE)
(literary) Made dim, less bright, or indistinct; obscured.

Examples

bedimmed by tears
Her vision was bedimmed by tears.
The moon appeared bedimmed behind the thick mist.
The old painting's colors were bedimmed by decades of dust and sunlight.
