esthetically

adverb
in a way relating to beauty or taste
Pronunciation: / ɛˈsθɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ɪsˈθɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is concerned with beauty or the appreciation of beauty; in a tasteful or artistic manner.

Examples

esthetically pleasing design
She prefers to arrange her living space esthetically with an emphasis on harmony and balance.
The artwork was esthetically arranged to catch the viewers' eyes as they entered the gallery.
The interior of the restaurant has been decorated esthetically to create a welcoming atmosphere.
