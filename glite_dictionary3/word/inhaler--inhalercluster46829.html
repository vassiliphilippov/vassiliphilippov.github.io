inhaler

noun
device for inhaling medicated vapor
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈheɪlər / (AmE), / ɪnˈheɪlə / (BrE)
a device that releases a medicated vapor for inhalation, often used to relieve nasal congestion or treat respiratory conditions

Examples

inhaler for asthma
He always carries his inhaler in case he starts feeling congested.
If you have a cold, an inhaler can provide relief from nasal stuffiness.
The doctor prescribed an inhaler to help manage her asthma symptoms.
