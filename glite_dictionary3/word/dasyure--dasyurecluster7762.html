dasyure

noun
carnivorous marsupial of Australia/New Guinea
Pronunciation: / ˈdæziˌjʊr / (AmE), / ˈdæsɪjʊə / (BrE)
Any of several small, carnivorous marsupials of the family Dasyuridae, native to Australia and New Guinea, somewhat resembling martens.

Examples

dasyure hunting prey
Despite its small size, a dasyure can be quite ferocious when threatened.
Scientists observed a dasyure hunting insects in the rainforest canopy.
The spotted-tailed dasyure is one of Australia's most efficient predators.
