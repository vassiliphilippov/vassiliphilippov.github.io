Djakarta

proper noun
former spelling of Jakarta, Indonesian capital
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈkɑrtə / (AmE), / dʒəˈkɑːtə / (BrE)
An older spelling of Jakarta, the capital and largest city of Indonesia located on the island of Java.
