sheer

adjective
complete; utter; absolute (for emphasis)
Pronunciation: / ʃɪr / (AmE), / ʃɪə / (BrE)
used to emphasize the large amount, quality, or degree of something; complete and without restriction or qualification

Examples

sheer determination
Her sheer determination inspired everyone who knew her.
It was sheer luck that he found his lost keys in the park.
The mountain presented a sheer challenge to even the most experienced climbers.

Related Terms

  • synonym – absolute: complete; total; not limited by any conditions
  • antonym – partial: existing only in part; not total
  • synonym – utter: complete; total; used to emphasize something negative or strong
