Luxemburg

proper noun
country in northwestern Europe
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌksəmˌbɜrɡ / (AmE), / ˈlʌksəmˌbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A country in northwestern Europe, officially the Grand Duchy of Luxemburg, bordered by Belgium, France, and Germany; known for its status as an international financial center.

Examples

Luxemburg borders France
After completing her studies, she was offered a banking job in Luxemburg.
Many international corporations have their European headquarters in Luxemburg.
The small nation of Luxemburg is famous for its medieval castles and scenic countryside.
