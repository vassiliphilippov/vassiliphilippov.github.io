distant

adjective
not closely related or connected
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈdɪstənt / (BrE)
(of relationships or similarity) not closely related or connected; far apart in kinship, relevance, or resemblance.

Examples

distant relative
He was introduced as a distant relative who lived abroad.
The issue was only of distant relevance to our discussion.
There is only a distant resemblance between her painting and his style.

Related Terms

  • antonym – close: closely related or connected
  • synonym – remote: not closely connected; distant in relationship or relevance
