to rope

verb
fasten or tie with a rope
Pronunciation: / roʊp / (AmE), / rəʊp / (BrE)
to fasten, tie, or secure something with a rope

Examples

to rope off area
Please rope off the area so that no one enters by mistake.
The climbers roped themselves to each other before starting the ascent.
They roped the boxes together to keep them from shifting during the move.
