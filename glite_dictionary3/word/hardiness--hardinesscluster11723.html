hardiness

noun
willingness to take risks or face danger
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrdinəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to undertake things that involve risk or danger; boldness or courage

Examples

hardiness to take risks
Few leaders possess the hardiness required to make such difficult decisions in times of crisis.
His hardiness was evident when he volunteered for the perilous expedition.
The hardiness of the mountain climbers impressed everyone back at base camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym – boldness: willingness to take risks; confidence and courage
  • antonym – timidity: lack of courage or confidence; shyness
