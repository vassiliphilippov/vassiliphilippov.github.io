Avon

proper noun
former county in southwestern England
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪvɒn / (AmE), / ˈeɪvən / (BrE)
A former county in southwestern England, named after the river Avon, that existed from 1974 to 1996.

Avon county
Many historical documents still reference Avon as the county for local towns.
