truckler

noun
person who acts servilely
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌk.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrʌk.lə / (BrE)
a person who acts in a servile or excessively deferential manner towards others, often lacking self-respect

a truckler to authority
He was seen as a truckler, always agreeing with the boss to avoid trouble.
No one respected the truckler in the office, who always bowed to authority.
Rather than stand up for himself, he acted as a truckler to those in power.
