Sambo

noun
offensive Black caricature in literature/culture
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈsæm.bəʊ / (BrE)
(historical, offensive) A stock character in literature and culture typified by exaggeratedly stereotypical features, often used to caricature Black people. The term and character are now considered highly offensive and racist.
