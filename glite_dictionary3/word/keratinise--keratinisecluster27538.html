to keratinise

verb
to become impregnated with keratin
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛr.ə.təˌnaɪz /; / ˈkɛr.ə.tɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɛr.ə.tɪˌnaɪz /; / kəˈræt.ɪ.naɪz / (BrE)
(of tissue or cells) to become or make hard and tough by producing and accumulating keratin
