waster

noun
person who wastes resources
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstər / (AmE), / ˈweɪstə / (BrE)
a person who spends or uses resources, time, or money in a wasteful or self-indulgent way

Examples

lazy waster
Jake is such a waster; he spends all his money on things he doesn’t need.
She was tired of being called a waster by her hard-working family.
The company cannot afford to hire another waster while trying to cut costs.

Related Terms

  • antonym – conserver: a person who uses resources carefully and avoids waste
  • synonym – spendthrift: a person who spends money in an extravagant, irresponsible way
