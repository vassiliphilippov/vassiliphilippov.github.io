to patronage

verb
to act as a patron or sponsor
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪtrənˌeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪtrənɪdʒ / (BrE)
(formal) To support, sponsor, or act as a patron to a person, group, or activity.

Examples

to patronage local artists
During the festival, the council agreed to patronage art programs for local schools.
The organization hoped the celebrities would patronage their fundraising events.
The wealthy family chose to patronage several promising young artists in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym – sponsor: to support financially or through resources
  • synonym – support: to give assistance or backing
