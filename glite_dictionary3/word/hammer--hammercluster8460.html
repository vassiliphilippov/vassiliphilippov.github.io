hammer

noun
forceful, repeated blow or pounding
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmər / (AmE), / ˈhamə / (BrE)
a heavy, repeated blow or the act of pounding; a forceful impact, especially one delivered repeatedly

Examples

a hammer at door
A sudden hammer at the door startled everyone in the house.
She woke to the hammer of rain against her window.
The boxer felt the hammer of his opponent's punches throughout the round.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – tap: a light or gentle blow
