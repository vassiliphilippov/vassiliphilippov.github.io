Fenrir

proper noun
giant wolf in Norse mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛnˌrɪr / (AmE), / ˈfɛnˌrɪə / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) a monstrous wolf, the offspring of Loki, who is prophesied to kill Odin during Ragnarök.
