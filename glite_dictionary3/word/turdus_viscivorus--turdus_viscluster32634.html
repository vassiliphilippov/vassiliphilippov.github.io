Turdus viscivorus

noun
large European thrush (mistle thrush)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrdəs vɪˈsɪvərəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːdəs vɪˈsɪvərəs / (BrE)
A large European thrush that feeds primarily on mistletoe berries, also known as the mistle thrush.
