splashiness

noun
showy or ostentatious quality
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being showy, flashy, or ostentatious, often lacking in elegance or subtlety

Examples

colorful splashiness
He dressed with a splashiness that made him stand out at every party.
Some critics felt the design's splashiness detracted from its true elegance.
The splashiness of the event overshadowed its intended message of charity.

Related Terms

  • antonym – modesty: the quality of being unassuming or moderate
  • synonym – ostentation: showy display intended to impress
