to compost

verb
to convert material into compost
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.poʊst / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.pəʊst / (BrE)
to convert (organic matter) into compost, typically by allowing it to decompose under controlled conditions

Examples

to compost food waste
Gardeners often compost leaves and grass clippings in the autumn.
Many households now compost their food scraps to reduce waste.
You should compost organic debris instead of throwing it away.
