wide-cut

adjective
having extra width or fullness (of clothing)
Pronunciation: / waɪd ˈkʌt / (AmE), / waɪd ˈkʌt / (BrE)
(of clothing) designed with extra width or fullness; having ample fabric to create a loose or generous fit.

Examples

wide-cut jeans
Wide-cut jeans became popular again in the early 2000s.
She prefers wide-cut trousers for comfort and style.
The designer showcased a wide-cut coat in the new collection.
