to dilate

verb
elaborate or expand on a subject
Pronunciation: / daɪˈleɪt / (AmE), / daɪˈleɪt / (BrE)
to speak or write at length or in detail about a subject; to elaborate or expand upon a topic

Examples

to dilate on a topic
During the lecture, the professor began to dilate on the importance of critical thinking.
She was asked to dilate further on her initial comments during the interview.
The author likes to dilate upon historical events in great detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym – elaborate: to add details or expand on a subject
  • synonym – expand: to give more details, to make something more comprehensive
  • antonym – summarize: to state briefly or in summary form
