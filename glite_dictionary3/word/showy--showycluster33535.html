showy

adjective
conspicuously bright or ostentatious
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ.i / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ.i / (BrE)
having a bright, elaborate, or ostentatious appearance that attracts attention, often in a way that is excessive or meant to impress
