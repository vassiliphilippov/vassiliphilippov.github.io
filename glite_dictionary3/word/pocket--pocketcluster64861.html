pocket

noun
anatomical saclike structure in animals
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑkɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt / (BrE)
a saclike anatomical structure in certain animals, such as the pouch of a marsupial or a storage area in a gopher's cheek

Examples

cheek pocket
