lah

noun
sixth note in solfège (music)
Pronunciation: / lɑ / (AmE), / lɑː / (BrE)
the syllable used to name the sixth note (submediant) of a major or minor scale in solmization (as in solfège)

Examples

lah in solfege scale
In solfège training, lah represents the sixth note of the scale.
She sang the melody up to lah and then came back down.
The sequence do, re, mi, fa, so, lah, ti is used to teach pitch.
