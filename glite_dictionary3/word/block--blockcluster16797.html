block

noun
act of obstructing movement
Pronunciation: / blɑk / (AmE), / blɒk / (BrE)
an act of obstructing or stopping someone's movements, especially in sports or physical activities

Examples

defensive block
A sudden block by the goalie prevented the team from gaining the lead.
She received a penalty for making an illegal block during the game.
The defender made a crucial block to stop the forward from scoring.
