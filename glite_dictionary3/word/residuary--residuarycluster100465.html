residuary

adjective
entitled to remainder of an estate
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪdʒuˌɛri / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪdjʊəri /; / rɪˈzɪdjuəri / (BrE)
relating to what is left over, especially the part of an estate that remains after debts and specific gifts are paid
