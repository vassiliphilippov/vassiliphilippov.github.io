unprofessional

adjective
not showing professional standards
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈfɛʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈfɛʃənəl / (BrE)
not showing the skill, standards, or behavior expected of a qualified person in a profession

Examples

unprofessional behavior at work
His unprofessional attitude cost him the promotion.
It is unprofessional to share confidential client information without permission.
The manager considered her lateness and excuses to be very unprofessional.
