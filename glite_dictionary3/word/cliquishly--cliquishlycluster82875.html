cliquishly

adverb
in a manner typical of a clique
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪkɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈkliːkɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner characteristic of a clique; showing exclusion or exclusivity toward outsiders.

Examples

act cliquishly at school
At work, her colleagues sometimes treat her cliquishly, refusing to share important information.
Some children acted cliquishly on the playground, making others feel left out.
The group behaved cliquishly, ignoring everyone who wasn't part of their circle.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cliquish: tending to form cliques and exclude others
