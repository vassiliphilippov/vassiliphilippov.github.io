T-shirt

noun
casual short-sleeved shirt
Pronunciation: / ˈtiː ʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˈtiː ʃɜːt / (BrE)
A casual short-sleeved shirt with no collar, typically made of cotton and having a simple, close-fitting design.

Examples

white T-shirt
After running, she changed into a clean T-shirt to cool down.
He wore a plain white T-shirt and blue jeans to the party.
The store sells T-shirts in every color imaginable.
