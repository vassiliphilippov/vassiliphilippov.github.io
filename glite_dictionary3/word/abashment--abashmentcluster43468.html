abashment

noun
feeling of embarrassment or shame
Pronunciation: / əˈbæʃmənt / (AmE), / əˈbæʃmənt / (BrE)
A feeling of embarrassment or self-conscious discomfort, especially caused by modesty or shame.

Examples

deep abashment
A moment of abashment passed over her face before she answered the question.
He spoke with abashment after making the embarrassing mistake in front of the class.
Her sudden abashment was obvious when she realized everyone was looking at her.
