engagement

noun
active participation in a group
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of actively participating in the activities of a group

Examples

engagement in class activities
Parental engagement in school events is crucial for student success.
The company measures employee engagement to monitor satisfaction and productivity.
The new project sparked higher engagement among team members during meetings.

  • synonym – involvement: the fact or condition of being involved with or participating in something
  • synonym – participation: the act of taking part in something
  • antonym – withdrawal: the process of ceasing to participate in an activity
