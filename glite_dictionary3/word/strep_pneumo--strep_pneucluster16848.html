Strep pneumo

noun
abbreviation for Streptococcus pneumoniae bacterium
Pronunciation: / strɛp ˈnuːmoʊ / (AmE), / strɛp ˈnjuːməʊ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for Streptococcus pneumoniae, a type of bacteria that can cause pneumonia and other respiratory infections
