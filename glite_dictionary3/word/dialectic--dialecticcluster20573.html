dialectic

noun
contradiction or interplay of opposing ideas
Pronunciation: / daɪəˈlɛktɪk / (AmE), / daɪəˈlɛktɪk / (BrE)
a method or process in philosophy where opposing ideas or arguments are discussed to reach a new understanding or truth
