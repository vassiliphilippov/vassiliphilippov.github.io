plainness

noun
clarity; the state of being easy to understand
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnnəs / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to understand or free from ambiguity; clarity in expression or appearance

Examples

plainness of instructions
He admired the plainness with which the instructions were written.
The plainness of her speech made her arguments easy for everyone to follow.
The document's plainness helped prevent any misunderstanding among team members.
