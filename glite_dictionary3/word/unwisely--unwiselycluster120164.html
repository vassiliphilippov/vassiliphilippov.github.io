unwisely

adverb
in a foolish or imprudent way
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwaɪzli / (AmE), / ʌnˈwaɪzli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of good sense or judgment.

Examples

to act unwisely
He unwisely spent all his savings on a single risky investment.
Many people unwisely ignore the safety instructions when using power tools.
She unwisely trusted someone she barely knew with her personal information.

Related Terms

  • synonym – foolishly: in a way that lacks good sense or judgment
  • antonym – wisely: in a way that shows good sense or judgment
