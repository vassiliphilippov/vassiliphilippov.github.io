vault

noun
underground burial chamber
Pronunciation: / vɔlt / (AmE), / vɔːlt / (BrE)
an underground room or chamber used for burial, typically constructed to house the remains of several people

Examples

family vault
After centuries, the royal vault remains untouched by grave robbers.
The family was interred in a stone vault beneath the old cathedral.
They discovered ancient treasures hidden in the burial vault during the excavation.
