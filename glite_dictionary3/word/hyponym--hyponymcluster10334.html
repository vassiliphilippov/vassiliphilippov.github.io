hyponym

noun
a more specific word
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpəˌnɪm / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪpənɪm / (BrE)
A word whose meaning is included within that of another, more general word.

Examples

hyponym of 'animal'
'Scarlet' is a hyponym of 'red', meaning it is a specific type of red.
In the category of vehicles, 'car' is a hyponym of 'vehicle'.
Knowing the hyponym helps you understand the precise meaning in a sentence.
