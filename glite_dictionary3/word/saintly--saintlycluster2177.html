saintly

adjective
like a saint; very virtuous or kind
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪntli / (AmE), / ˈseɪntli / (BrE)
Very good, kind, or virtuous; resembling or characteristic of a saint.

Examples

saintly devotion
He is admired for his saintly devotion to helping the poor.
Her saintly patience with the children impressed everyone at the school.
The nurse cared for her patients with a saintly kindness.
