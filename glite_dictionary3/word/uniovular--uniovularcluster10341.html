uniovular

adjective
having a single ovule
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːniˈɑːvjələr / (AmE), / ˌjuːniˈɒvjʊlə / (BrE)
(of an ovary or organism) having a single ovule; producing or originating from a single ovule.

Identical twins are often described as uniovular because they develop from a single ovum.
