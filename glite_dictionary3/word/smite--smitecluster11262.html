to smite

verb
to strike or inflict pain, often as punishment
Pronunciation: / smaɪt / (AmE), / smaɪt / (BrE)
to inflict a heavy blow or to cause pain or suffering, especially as a punishment or by a supernatural force

Examples

to smite with punishment

In ancient tales, heroes were often smitten by the wrath of the gods.
The goddess threatened to smite those who disobeyed her commands.
