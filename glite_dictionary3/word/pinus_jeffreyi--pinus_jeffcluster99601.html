Pinus jeffreyi

noun
a pine tree native to western North America
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.nəs ˈdʒɛf.ri.aɪ / (AmE), / ˈpiː.nəs ˈdʒɛf.ri.aɪ / (BrE)
A tall pine tree native to western North America, with long blue-green needles in clusters of three and elongated cones; sometimes considered a variety of ponderosa pine.
