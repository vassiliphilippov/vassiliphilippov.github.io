Monarda didyma

noun
aromatic North American herb; bee balm
Pronunciation: / moʊˈnɑːrdə dɪˈdɪmə / (AmE), / məˈnɑːdə dɪˈdɪmə / (BrE)
A perennial aromatic herb of eastern North America with dense showy heads of variously colored tubular flowers; also known as bee balm or bergamot.
