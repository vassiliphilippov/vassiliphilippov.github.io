samba

noun
canasta card game variant
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmbə / (AmE), / ˈsæmbə / (BrE)
A form of canasta played with three decks of cards and six jokers, allowing for more cards to be grouped in melds.

Examples

samba card game
Samba is more challenging than standard canasta because there are more cards to manage.
After learning canasta, he quickly picked up the rules of samba.
We spent most of the weekend playing samba with friends.
