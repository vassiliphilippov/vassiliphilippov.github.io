gumdrop

noun
a sugar-coated jellied candy
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌmˌdrɑp / (AmE), / ˈɡʌmˌdrɒp / (BrE)
A small, soft, jellied candy coated with sugar crystals, typically brightly colored and fruit-flavored.

Examples

colorful gumdrop candy
Children eagerly picked out their favorite flavors of gumdrops at the fair.
She offered me a red gumdrop from the candy jar on her desk.
The cake was decorated with colorful gumdrops around the edge.
