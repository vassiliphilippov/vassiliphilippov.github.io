to brighten

verb
become clear or less cloudy (weather/sky)
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪtn̩ / (BrE)
To become clear or less cloudy, especially with reference to the weather or sky.

Examples

the sky brightens after rain
After an hour of rain, the clouds began to brighten and the sun peeked through.
As the storm passed, the sky started to brighten, bringing hope for a pleasant day.
The weather is expected to brighten in the afternoon, with clear skies by evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clear: (of the sky or weather) to become free of clouds or fog
