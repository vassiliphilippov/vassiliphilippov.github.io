demonstrability

noun
capability of being demonstrated or proved
Pronunciation: / dɪˌmɑnstrəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒnstrəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to be demonstrated or proved logically

Examples

demonstrability of a claim
Her argument lacked the necessary demonstrability to convince the panel.
Mathematical statements often vary in their demonstrability.
The demonstrability of the theory was questioned by several scientists.

Related Terms

  • synonym – demonstrate: to show or prove something clearly
  • antonym – indemonstrable: not capable of being demonstrated or proved
