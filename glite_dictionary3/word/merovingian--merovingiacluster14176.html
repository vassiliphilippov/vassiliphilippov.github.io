Merovingian

adjective
relating to the Merovingian dynasty
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛrəˈvɪndʒiən / (AmE), / ˌmɛrəˈvɪndʒɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Merovingian dynasty, a Frankish ruling family in Gaul from the 5th to 8th centuries.

Examples

Merovingian dynasty period
