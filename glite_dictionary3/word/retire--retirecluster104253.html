to retire

verb
move back; withdraw
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪr /; / rɪˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈtʌɪə /; / rɪˈtaɪə / (BrE)
to move away or withdraw from a place, meeting, or group, often to go somewhere private or quiet

Examples

At the conclusion of dinner, the ladies retired to the drawing room.
