taxiway

noun
paved airport path for taxiing planes
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksiˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈtæksiˌweɪ / (BrE)
a paved strip at an airport used by aircraft to move between the runway and the parking area, hangars, or terminal

Examples

airport taxiway
Maintenance vehicles were seen driving on the taxiway early this morning.
Pilots must receive clearance before entering an active taxiway.
The plane left the runway and proceeded along the taxiway to the terminal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – runway: a long, flat surface where aircraft take off and land
