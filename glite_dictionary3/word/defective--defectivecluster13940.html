defective

adjective
having a fault or imperfection
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)
not working properly or having a fault; imperfect or faulty in operation, function, or structure

Examples

defective product
Inspectors removed the defective parts from the assembly line to prevent future malfunctions.
She received a defective product and immediately contacted customer service for a replacement.
The defective phone would not turn on despite several attempts to charge it.
A defective battery can cause the device to stop working unexpectedly.
Many defective items were discovered during the quality control inspection.
The product was returned because it was defective and did not operate as advertised.
