ICSH

noun
hormone stimulating sex cell activity
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ ˌsi ˈɛɪtʃ ˌɛs ˈeɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˌaɪ ˌsiː ˈɛtʃ ˌɛs ˈɛtʃ / (BrE)
