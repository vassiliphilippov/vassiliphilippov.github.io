lasciviously

adverb
in a sexually offensive or lewd manner
Pronunciation: / ləˈsɪviəsli / (AmE), / ləˈsɪviəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows overt or offensive sexual desire.

Examples

lasciviously winked
He winked lasciviously at her from across the room, making her feel uncomfortable.

The villain grinned lasciviously as he revealed his sinister intentions.
