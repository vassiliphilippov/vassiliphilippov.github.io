zoopsia

noun
hallucination of seeing animals
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈɑpsiə / (AmE), / zəʊˈɒpsiə / (BrE)
A visual hallucination involving the perception of animals, sometimes occurring in mental disorders such as delirium tremens.

Examples

zoopsia with animals
Doctors recognized the zoopsia as a symptom of his underlying neurological disorder.
During the night, her zoopsia made her believe that insects were crawling on the walls.
The patient reported experiencing zoopsia during his withdrawal episode.
