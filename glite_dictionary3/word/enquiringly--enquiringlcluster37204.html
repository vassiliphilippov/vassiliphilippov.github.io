enquiringly

adverb
in an inquisitive manner
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that expresses curiosity or asks for information; inquisitively.

Examples

look enquiringly at someone
He raised his hand enquiringly, unsure if he was allowed to speak.
She glanced enquiringly at her professor, hoping for a hint about the assignment.
The child looked enquiringly at his mother when he heard the unfamiliar word.
