pseudohermaphrodite

noun
person with mismatched internal and external sex organs
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊhɚˈmæfrəˌdaɪt / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊhɜːˈmæfrədaɪt / (BrE)
a person whose external genitalia are of one sex but whose internal reproductive organs are of the other sex; not a true hermaphrodite, as there is no ambiguity of external sex at birth

Examples

male pseudohermaphrodite
In older medical texts, the term pseudohermaphrodite is often used to describe certain intersex conditions.
Researchers now prefer more precise terminology than pseudohermaphrodite when referring to disorders of sexual development.
The doctor explained that the patient was a pseudohermaphrodite, possessing male testes but female external genitalia.
