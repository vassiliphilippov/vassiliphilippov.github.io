photocoagulator

noun
surgical laser instrument for photocoagulation
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊkoʊˈæɡjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊkəʊˈæɡjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
