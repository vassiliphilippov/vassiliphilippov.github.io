Pilea involucrata

noun
species of Central/South American nettle with velvety leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪliə ˌɪnˌvɑlˈjʊkrətə / (AmE), / ˈpaɪliə ˌɪnˌvɒlˈjuːkrətə / (BrE)
A species of low, stingless nettle native to Central and South America, with velvety brownish-green toothed leaves and clusters of small green flowers.
