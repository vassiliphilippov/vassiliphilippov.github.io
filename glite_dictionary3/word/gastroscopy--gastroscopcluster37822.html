gastroscopy

noun
examination of the stomach with a gastroscope
Pronunciation: / ɡæˈstrɑːskəpi / (AmE), / ɡæˈstrɒskəpi / (BrE)
A medical procedure in which the inside of the stomach is examined using a gastroscope passed through the esophagus.

Examples

have a gastroscopy procedure
After the gastroscopy, the patient was able to go home the same day.
During the gastroscopy, the specialist looked for signs of ulcers or inflammation.
The doctor recommended a gastroscopy to investigate her persistent stomach pain.
