to hone

verb
make a skill or ability perfect
Pronunciation: / hoʊn / (AmE), / həʊn / (BrE)
to improve a skill or ability so that it becomes as effective or perfect as possible

Examples

to hone skills
Athletes hone their abilities through dedicated practice and rigorous training.
Over the years, he managed to hone his negotiation skills to a fine point.
She took extra lessons to hone her French before moving to Paris.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dull: to make less sharp or effective
  • synonym – improve: make or become better
  • synonym – polish: improve or perfect a skill through practice
