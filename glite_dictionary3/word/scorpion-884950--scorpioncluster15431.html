scorpion

noun
venomous tailed arachnid
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔr.pi.ən / (AmE), / ˈskɔː.pi.ən / (BrE)
A predatory arachnid found in warm, dry regions, with a long, segmented tail ending in a venomous stinger.

Examples

desert scorpion
A desert scorpion hid beneath the rocks to avoid the midday sun.
She watched a scorpion scuttle quickly across the sandy path.
The sting of a scorpion can be dangerous, especially to children.
