to hinder

verb
to make progress more difficult
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪndər /; / ˈhɪndɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɪndə / (BrE)
to make it difficult for something to happen or for someone to do something; to obstruct or be an obstacle
