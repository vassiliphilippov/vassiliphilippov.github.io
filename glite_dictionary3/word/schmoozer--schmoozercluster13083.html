schmoozer

noun
a person skilled at charming conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈʃmuːzər / (AmE), / ˈʃmuːzə / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at engaging in informal, charming, and often persuasive conversation, especially to gain favor or advantage

Examples

office schmoozer
As a politician, she was a natural schmoozer who built strong relationships quickly.
Everyone loved talking to the office schmoozer at company parties.
He's a real schmoozer—always knowing just what to say to get what he wants.

  • synonym – flatterer: someone who praises others excessively to gain favor
