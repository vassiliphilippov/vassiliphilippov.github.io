to accrue

verb
to accumulate or increase over time
Pronunciation: / əˈkru / (AmE), / əˈkruː / (BrE)
to increase or accumulate over time, especially as a result of regular additions

Examples

to accrue benefits
Benefits will accrue to employees who stay with the company longer.
Interest will accrue on your savings account monthly.
Over the years, a considerable sum has accrued in her retirement fund.

Related Terms

  • synonym – accumulate: to gather or build up gradually
  • antonym – diminish: to become less, decrease
