to signalise

verb
provide with traffic signals
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡnəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəlaɪz / (BrE)
to provide (a road, intersection, etc.) with traffic signals

Examples

to signalise a junction

Plans are underway to signalise the routes leading to the new shopping centre.
