Frans Hals

proper noun
Dutch painter known for lively portraits
Pronunciation: / frænz hælz / (AmE), / frænz hælz / (BrE)
(c. 1582–1666) A Dutch painter known for his lively and humorous portraits and genre scenes, prominent in the Dutch Golden Age.
