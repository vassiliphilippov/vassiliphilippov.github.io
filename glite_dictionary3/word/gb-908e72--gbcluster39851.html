Gb

noun
gigabit (1,000,000,000 bits, abbrev.)
Pronunciation: / dʒiː biː / (AmE), / dʒiː biː / (BrE)
abbreviation for gigabit, a unit of information equal to 1,000,000,000 (10^9) bits

Examples

Gb internet speed
Downloading a movie might take only a few seconds with a 5 Gb connection.
Network devices today often support data transfers measured in Gb rather than megabits.
The new fiber optic service offers speeds up to 1 Gb per second.
