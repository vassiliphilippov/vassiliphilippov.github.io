picometre

noun
metric unit; one trillionth of a meter
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkoʊˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəˌmiːtə / (BrE)
a metric unit of length equal to one trillionth of a meter (10⁻¹² metres)

Examples

one picometre long
Some atomic radii are as small as 50 picometres.
The bond length between certain atoms is less than 100 picometres.
The wavelength of X-rays can be measured in picometres.
