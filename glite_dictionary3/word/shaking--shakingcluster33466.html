shaking

noun
a shaky or trembling motion
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
a quick, repeated, or trembling movement back and forth or up and down; the act or result of shaking
