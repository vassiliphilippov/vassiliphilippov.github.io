gatekeeper

noun
person controlling access
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a person or organization that controls access to something or someone

Examples

gatekeeper of information
An effective gatekeeper balances security with openness to new ideas.
In many industries, editors serve as gatekeepers to publication.
The gatekeeper insisted that only authorized personnel could enter the premises.

Related Terms

  • antonym – barrier: something that blocks access; contrasting concept
  • synonym – doorkeeper: someone who watches an entrance; similar role
