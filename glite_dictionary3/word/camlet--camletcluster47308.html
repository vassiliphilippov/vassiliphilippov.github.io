camlet

noun
garment made of camlet fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmlət / (AmE), / ˈkamlɪt /; / ˈkæmlɪt / (BrE)
a rich woven fabric, originally made of silk and camel's hair (sometimes wool or blends), used for garments and upholstery; also, a garment made from this fabric
