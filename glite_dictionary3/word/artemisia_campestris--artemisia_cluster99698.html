Artemisia campestris

noun
a species of European wormwood plant
Pronunciation: / ɑrtɪˈmɪziə kæmˈpɛstrɪs / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈmɪzɪə kæmˈpɛstrɪs / (BrE)
