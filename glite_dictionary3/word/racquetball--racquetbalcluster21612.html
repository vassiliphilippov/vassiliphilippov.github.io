racquetball

noun
indoor game with rackets and a rubber ball
Pronunciation: / ˈrækɪtˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈrækɪtˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A game played with short-handled rackets and a hollow rubber ball on an indoor court with walls, similar to handball.

Examples

play racquetball indoors
After a strenuous racquetball match, they relaxed in the clubhouse.
He enjoys playing racquetball twice a week at the local gym.
The racquetball court was fully booked for the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym – squash: a similar indoor racket sport played on a four-walled court
