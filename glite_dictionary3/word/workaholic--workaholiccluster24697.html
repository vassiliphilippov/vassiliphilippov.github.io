workaholic

noun
person obsessed with working
Pronunciation: / ˌwɝːkəˈhɑːlɪk / (AmE), / ˌwɜːkəˈhɒlɪk / (BrE)
a person who compulsively works a lot and finds it difficult to stop working

Examples

a workaholic boss
Being a workaholic can negatively affect your relationships and health.
My father is a true workaholic and rarely takes any vacations.
The company appreciates his dedication, but his workaholic tendency worries his friends.
