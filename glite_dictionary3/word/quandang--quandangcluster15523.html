quandang

noun
Australian tree with edible fruit and seed
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑn.dæŋ / (AmE), / ˈkwɒn.dæŋ / (BrE)
An Australian tree of the genus Santalum, especially Santalum acuminatum, which bears edible fruit and a nutlike seed.
