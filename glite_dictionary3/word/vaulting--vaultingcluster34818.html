vaulting

noun
a horse's light leap
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
A light leap performed by a horse in which both hind legs leave the ground before the forelegs come down.

Examples

horse's vaulting leap
During the event, the stallion's graceful vaulting drew a round of applause.
She trained her mare to perfect its vaulting for dressage competitions.
The horse's vaulting over the low hurdle impressed the judges.

  • synonym – leap: a long or high jump
