preserver

noun
buoyant rescue device
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɜrvər / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːvə / (BrE)
a buoyant belt or jacket designed to keep a person afloat and prevent drowning

Examples

preserver belt on boat
