Costanoan

noun
Native American language of central California
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑstəˈnoʊən / (AmE), / ˌkɒstəˈnəʊən / (BrE)
a member of a group of related Penutian languages formerly spoken by the Costanoan people of coastal central California.

Examples

the Costanoan language
Linguists are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of Costanoan.
Several words in English are derived from Costanoan place-names in California.
The last fluent speaker of Costanoan passed away in the twentieth century.
