quarto

noun
book size: four leaves per sheet
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːrtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkwɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
A book size in which each sheet of paper is folded twice to form four leaves or eight pages.

Examples

a quarto book
He preferred the portable size of a quarto to the larger folio volumes.
The library's archives feature several historical works printed in quarto format.
The rare book collection included a Shakespearean quarto from the 17th century.
