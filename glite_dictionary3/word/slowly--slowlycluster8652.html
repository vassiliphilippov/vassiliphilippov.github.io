slowly

adverb
at a low speed; not quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊli / (AmE), / ˈsləʊli / (BrE)
In a gradual way; at a low speed or with little rapidity.

Examples

move slowly
Please drive slowly through the school zone for safety.
She walked slowly down the path, enjoying the morning air.
The story unfolds slowly, allowing readers to savor every detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym – quickly: at a fast speed; rapidly
  • synonym – slow: not quickly; at a low speed (occasionally used adverbially, esp. in informal contexts)
