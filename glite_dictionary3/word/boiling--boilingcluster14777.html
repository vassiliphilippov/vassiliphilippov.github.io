boiling

noun
application of heat to turn liquid into gas
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of heating a liquid until it changes into a gas, especially by reaching its boiling point

Examples

boiling water
During boiling, bubbles of steam form throughout the liquid.
The boiling of water is a key step in cooking pasta.
They observed the boiling of various liquids under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • antonym – condensation: the process by which a gas turns into a liquid
  • degree lower – evaporation: the process of a liquid turning into vapor (not necessarily at boiling point)
  • synonym – vaporization: the change of a substance from liquid or solid to vapor
