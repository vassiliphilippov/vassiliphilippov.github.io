cut

noun
refusal to recognize someone
Pronunciation: / kʌt / (AmE), / kʌt / (BrE)
an act of deliberately refusing to acknowledge or greet someone you know, especially as a social slight

Examples

cut at party
After their argument, his former colleague greeted him with a deliberate cut at the party.
Receiving a public cut from an old friend can be deeply hurtful.
She gave him a cold cut in the hallway, pretending not to see him.
