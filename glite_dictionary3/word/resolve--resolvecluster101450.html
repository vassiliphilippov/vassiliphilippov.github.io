to resolve

verb
cause to disperse into solution; dissolve
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɑlv / (AmE), / rɪˈzɒlv / (BrE)
to cause (a substance) to disperse or break up so that it becomes part of a solution; to dissolve

Examples

to resolve a substance
Heating can help resolve the sugar more quickly in the solution.
Stir the mixture gently until all the salt has resolved in the water.
The chemist managed to resolve the compound into its component ions by adding acid.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dissolve: to mix with a liquid so as to form a solution
