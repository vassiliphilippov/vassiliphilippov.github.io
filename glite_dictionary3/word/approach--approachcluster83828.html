approach

noun
tentative proposal or suggestion
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a tentative proposal or suggestion made to someone, often to elicit a reaction or to begin negotiations or a relationship

Examples

approach to a company
After several subtle approaches, he finally asked her out to dinner.
She politely declined his approach at the party.
The company made an approach to the rival firm with a merger proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym – advance: a proposal or move made towards someone, especially of a romantic kind
