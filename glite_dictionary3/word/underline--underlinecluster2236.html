to underline

verb
draw a line underneath for emphasis
Pronunciation: / ˈʌn.dɚˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈʌn.dəˌlaɪn / (BrE)
to draw a line or lines under a word or sentence to emphasize it or indicate its importance

Examples

to underline a word
He underlined her name twice to make sure he wouldn't forget.
Please underline the key words in each sentence.
