wash

noun
dry streambed in arid regions
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ / (AmE), / wɒʃ / (BrE)
a dry channel or bed of a stream that only carries water after heavy rain, especially in arid regions

Examples

dry river wash
After the storm, water rushed through the usually dry wash at the bottom of the canyon.
Flash floods are common in this wash during the summer monsoon season.
We hiked along the wash, searching for animal tracks left in the sandy bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym – arroyo: a steep-sided gully or channel in arid regions, usually dry except after rain
