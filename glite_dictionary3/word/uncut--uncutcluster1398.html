uncut

adjective
not shortened; complete version
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌt / (BrE)
(of films, books, etc.) not shortened or edited; presented in its complete, original form.

Examples

uncut movie version
I prefer reading the uncut edition of the novel to get the full story.
The director's uncut version includes scenes that were left out in theatres.
This special release features the uncut concert as it was originally performed.

Related Terms

  • antonym – abridged: shortened by omitting parts, especially in books or texts
