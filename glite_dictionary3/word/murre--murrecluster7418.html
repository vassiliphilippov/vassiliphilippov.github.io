murre

noun
a northern black-and-white diving seabird
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜr / (AmE), / ˈmʌr / (BrE)
A black-and-white diving seabird of northern seas, belonging to the genus Uria.

Examples

a murre dives
A single murre can dive to remarkable depths in search of fish.
As the boat approached, several murres took off and flew low over the water.
The cliffs were covered with thousands of nesting murres during the summer.
