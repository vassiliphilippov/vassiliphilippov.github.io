patch

noun
temporary electrical or communications connection
Pronunciation: / pætʃ / (AmE), / pætʃ / (BrE)
a temporary connection in an electrical or communications system, made to address a fault or allow for testing or modification

Examples

patch cable connection
During the call, a patch was used to connect the lines temporarily.
The technician installed a patch to bypass the damaged circuit.
They applied a quick patch to restore service until a permanent fix could be made.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jumper: a short wire used to make a temporary connection
