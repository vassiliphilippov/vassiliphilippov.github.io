presentational

adjective
relating to presentation
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌprɛzənˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with the act of presenting or a presentation, especially in psychology or philosophy.

presentational style
