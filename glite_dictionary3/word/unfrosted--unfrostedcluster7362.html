unfrosted

adjective
not having a frosted coating
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfrɔstɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfrɒstɪd / (BrE)
(of glass or a similar material) lacking a frosted or opaque coating, remaining clear or transparent.

Examples

unfrosted glass window
Most decorative vases in the shop were unfrosted, giving them a glossy, transparent appearance.
The kitchen had unfrosted glass windows to let in more light.
They bought a box of unfrosted light bulbs for the living room ceiling.

Related Terms

  • antonym – frosted: having a frosted coating; rendered opaque or translucent
