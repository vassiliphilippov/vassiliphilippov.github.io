cardioid

noun
heart-shaped mathematical curve
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑr.diˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.di.ɔɪd / (BrE)
a heart-shaped curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle that rolls around another circle of the same radius
