Ligustrum japonicum

noun
evergreen shrub native to Japan and Korea
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡʌstrəm dʒəˈpoʊnɪkəm / (AmE), / lɪˈɡʌstrəm dʒæˈpɒnɪkəm / (BrE)
