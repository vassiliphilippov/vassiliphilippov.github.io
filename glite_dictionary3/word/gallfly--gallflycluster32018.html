gallfly

noun
small wasp that induces plant galls
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡalˌflʌɪ /; / ˈɡal.flaɪ /; / ˈɡɔːlˌflaɪ / (BrE)
