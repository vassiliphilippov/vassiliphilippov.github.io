euphonium

noun
brass wind instrument, tenor tuba
Pronunciation: / juˈfoʊniəm / (AmE), / juːˈfəʊniəm / (BrE)
A brass wind instrument with a wide conical bore, resembling a small tuba, that plays in the tenor range of the tuba family.

Examples

play the euphonium in band
Sam is learning to read bass clef music for his euphonium lessons.
She played the euphonium in the marching band throughout high school.
The rich, mellow sound of the euphonium stood out during the orchestra's performance.
