to add

verb
to say further as a remark
Pronunciation: / æd / (AmE), / æd / (BrE)
to say something further as a remark or comment, often when joining in or continuing a conversation

Examples

to add a comment
"I think we should leave soon," she added quietly.
After the main announcement, the spokesperson added a few additional details.
He added that he would be back before midnight.
