to ride

verb
keep a pedal partially engaged
Pronunciation: / raɪd / (AmE), / raɪd / (BrE)
To keep a vehicle control partially engaged by lightly pressing a pedal, especially the clutch or brake, with the foot.

Examples

to ride the brake
He kept riding the pedal, which caused problems with the engine.
If you ride the clutch too much, you'll wear it out quickly.
New drivers are often told not to ride the brakes going downhill.
