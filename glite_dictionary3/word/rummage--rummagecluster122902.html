to rummage

verb
search hurriedly and without order
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈrʌmɪdʒ / (BrE)
to search through something in a hurried or disorderly way, especially by moving things around

Examples

to rummage in a bag
He rummaged around in the drawer until he found a pen.
She began to rummage through her bag, looking for her keys.
The children rummaged in the attic for old toys.

Related Terms

  • synonym – search: to try to find something by looking or otherwise seeking carefully
