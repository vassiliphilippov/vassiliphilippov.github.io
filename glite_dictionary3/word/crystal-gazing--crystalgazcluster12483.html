crystal-gazing

noun
fortune telling with a crystal ball; idle speculation
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəl ˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəl ˌɡeɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of predicting the future by looking into a crystal ball; also, engaging in speculation or theorizing without taking practical action

Examples

crystal-gazing about the future
His business plan consisted mainly of crystal-gazing, not concrete steps.
Some people dismiss economic forecasts as nothing more than crystal-gazing.
The old woman was famous for her crystal-gazing abilities at the county fair.

  • synonym – fortune-telling: the art or practice of predicting the future
