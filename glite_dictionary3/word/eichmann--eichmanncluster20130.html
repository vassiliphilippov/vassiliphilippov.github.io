Eichmann

proper noun
Nazi official in charge of Holocaust deportations
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪkmən / (AmE), / ˈaɪkmən / (BrE)
(1906–1962) An Austrian Nazi official responsible for organizing and overseeing the transport of Jews to concentration camps during the Holocaust.
