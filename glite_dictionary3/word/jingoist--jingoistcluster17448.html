jingoist

noun
extremely nationalistic, warlike person
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪst / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪŋɡəʊɪst / (BrE)
A person who expresses extreme patriotism and supports aggressive or warlike foreign policy.

Examples

a jingoist politician
Far from being a jingoist, she advocated for peaceful diplomatic solutions to conflicts.
He dismissed criticisms of the country's policies as the ramblings of jingoist fanatics.
The politician was accused of being a jingoist for his constant calls for military action.
