leiomyosarcoma

noun
malignant tumor of smooth muscle tissue
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪ.oʊˌmaɪ.oʊsɑrˈkoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌlaɪ.əʊ.maɪ.əʊ.sɑːˈkəʊ.mə / (BrE)
A rare malignant tumor of smooth muscle tissue, most commonly found in the digestive tract, uterus, bladder, or prostate.

Examples

leiomyosarcoma in the uterus
A leiomyosarcoma in the uterus can cause abnormal bleeding and pain.
Surgical removal is the primary treatment for leiomyosarcoma in many cases.
The patient was diagnosed with a rare form of leiomyosarcoma affecting the bladder.
