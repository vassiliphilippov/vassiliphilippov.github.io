resistant

adjective
opposing or defiant of authority
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪstənt / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪstənt / (BrE)
disposed to or engaging in active opposition or defiance, especially against established authority

Examples

resistant to authority
Her resistant attitude often led to conflict with supervisors at work.
Some groups remain resistant to changes in government policy, staging protests and marches.
The students became increasingly resistant to the new regulations imposed by the school administration.

Related Terms

  • antonym – compliant: willing to agree with or obey rules or authority
  • synonym – rebellious: refusing to obey authority, rules, or tradition
