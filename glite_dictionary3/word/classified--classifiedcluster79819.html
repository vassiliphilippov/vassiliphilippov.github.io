classified

noun
small newspaper or magazine advertisement
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
a small advertisement placed in a newspaper or magazine, grouped with similar ads and typically offering goods or services for sale or seeking to make contact

Examples

classified ad section
He placed a classified in the local newspaper to sell his car.
Many people still find jobs by reading the classified section every Sunday.
The classified was brief but generated a lot of interest from potential buyers.
