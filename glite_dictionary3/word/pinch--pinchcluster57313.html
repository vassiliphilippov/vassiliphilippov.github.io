pinch

noun
minor injury from squeezing body part
Pronunciation: / pɪntʃ / (AmE), / pɪntʃ / (BrE)
a small, sharp squeeze with the fingers, or an injury caused by squeezing

Examples

a sharp pinch
A small pinch on my arm left a red mark for hours.
Be careful not to get a pinch when handling that machinery.
She got a pinch on her finger while closing the door.
Add a pinch of salt to the recipe.
I felt a sharp pinch when the nurse took blood.
She gave her brother a playful pinch on the arm.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bruise: an injury appearing as an area of discolored skin
  • synonym – nip: a small, quick squeeze or bite, especially with the fingers or teeth
