pledge

noun
solemn promise or commitment
Pronunciation: / plɛdʒ / (AmE), / plɛdʒ / (BrE)
a solemn promise or commitment to do, give, or refrain from doing something

Examples

make a pledge
Before taking office, she made a pledge to serve with honesty and integrity.
Every club member must sign a pledge not to share confidential information.
He gave a pledge to donate ten percent of his winnings to charity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – oath: a solemn promise regarding one's future action or behavior
  • synonym – promise: a declaration that one will do or refrain from doing something
  • synonym – vow: a solemn promise, often with religious overtones
