amorphous

adjective
having no clear structure or organization
Pronunciation: / əˈmɔr.fəs /; / əˈmɔrfəs /; / əˈmɔɹfəs / (AmE), / əˈmɔː.fəs /; / əˈmɔːfəs / (BrE)
lacking clear structure, organization, or boundaries; vague or badly organized, especially in explanations, groups, or systems
