buckle

noun
belt or strap fastener
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌk.əl / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.əl / (BrE)
A clasp or fastener, typically made of metal or plastic, used to join together the ends of a belt, strap, or similar item, often featuring a movable prong.

Examples

buckle on a belt
He adjusted the buckle on his belt before stepping on stage.
Her new shoes have a shiny silver buckle on each strap.
The strap wouldn't stay tight because the buckle was broken.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clasp: a device with interlocking parts used for fastening things together
