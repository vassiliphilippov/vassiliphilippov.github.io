unreachable

adjective
impossible or extremely difficult to reach
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈritʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈriːtʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to get to or reach, especially because of location or situation

Examples

unreachable mountain peak
Because of the rising floodwaters, the island became temporarily unreachable.
For many, a college education once seemed financially unreachable.
The cabin was so far up the mountain that it felt completely unreachable in winter.
