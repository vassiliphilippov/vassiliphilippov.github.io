frugally

adverb
in a way that avoids waste
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːɡəli / (AmE), / ˈfruːɡəli / (BrE)
In a way that avoids waste by using resources sparingly or economically.

Examples

live frugally
During the drought, villagers used water frugally to make it last longer.
He cooked frugally to save both money and ingredients.
She always spends money frugally and avoids unnecessary purchases.

Related Terms

  • synonym – economically: in a manner that uses resources carefully or sparingly
  • antonym – lavishly: in a very generous or extravagant way
