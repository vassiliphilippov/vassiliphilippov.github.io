scrupulousness

noun
strict attention to detail; moral carefulness
Pronunciation: / ˈskruːpjələsnəs / (AmE), / ˈskruːpjʊləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very careful, paying attention to details, and having high moral standards

Examples

extreme scrupulousness in work
His scrupulousness made him an excellent proofreader, as he caught even the smallest errors.
Many admired her scrupulousness in following ethical guidelines in every situation.
The scrupulousness of the audit team ensured that no irregularities were overlooked.
Good judges are known for their fairness and scrupulousness in interpreting the law.
Her scrupulousness in reporting ensured that every fact was checked twice before publication.
The scientist's scrupulousness was evident in her precise and careful experiments.
