Bhagavad-Gita

noun
sacred Hindu scripture and philosophical text
Pronunciation: / ˌbɑːɡəvəd ˈɡiːtɑː / (AmE), / ˌbʌɡəvəd ˈɡiːtə / (BrE)
a Hindu sacred text, part of the Mahabharata, presenting a spiritual dialogue between Krishna and Arjuna on duty, righteousness, and the meaning of life
