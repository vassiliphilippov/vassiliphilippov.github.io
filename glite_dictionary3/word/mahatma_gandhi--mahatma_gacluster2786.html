Mahatma Gandhi

proper noun
Indian independence leader, 1869–1948
Pronunciation: / məˈhɑtmə ˈɡɑndi / (AmE), / məˈhɑːtmə ˈɡandi / (BrE)
(1869–1948) Indian political and spiritual leader who led the country's struggle for independence from British rule through nonviolent resistance.
