solvent

noun
liquid that dissolves other substances
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlvənt / (AmE), / ˈsɒlvənt / (BrE)
a liquid that is able to dissolve other substances, forming a solution

Examples

solvent dissolves paint
Acetone is a powerful solvent often used to clean laboratory equipment.
The paint solvent quickly dissolved the stubborn stain on the brush.
Water is a common solvent used in chemistry laboratories.

Related Terms

  • antonym – solute: the substance dissolved in a solvent to form a solution
  • synonym – solution: a mixture formed when a solute is dissolved in a solvent
