straight

noun
straight section of a road or track
Pronunciation: / streɪt / (AmE), / streɪt / (BrE)
a section of a road, track, or course that is not curved or bent; a straight segment, especially in racing contexts

Examples

the straight track
On this straight, drivers often reach their maximum speed.
Spectators gathered along the straight to watch the cars racing side by side.
The car picked up speed as it entered the straight before the finish line.
