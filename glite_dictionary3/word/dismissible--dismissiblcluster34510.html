dismissible

adjective
subject to dismissal
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈmɪsəbəl / (AmE), / dɪsˈmɪsɪbəl / (BrE)
Able to be dismissed; liable or subject to dismissal.

Examples

dismissible employee
All probationary employees are dismissible at any time without cause.
The board considered the director's action dismissible under company policy.
The judge ruled that the claim was dismissible under the current law.
