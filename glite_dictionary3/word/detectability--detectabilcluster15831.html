detectability

noun
state of being able to be detected
Pronunciation: / dɪˌtɛktəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌtɛktəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being able to be detected or noticed.

Examples

detectability of a signal
Camouflage is used to reduce the detectability of military vehicles in combat.
Improved instruments have increased the detectability of faint signals from space.
The detectability of the virus depends on the sensitivity of the testing method.

  • synonym – visibility: the state of being able to be seen
