planking

noun
work of covering with planks
Pronunciation: / ˈplæŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈplæŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or work of covering an area, such as a floor or the sides of a ship, with planks of wood

Examples

floor planking
Proper planking ensures the floor remains stable and even.
Restoring the old house required extensive planking of the damaged floorboards.
The carpenters finished the planking of the ship's hull last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym – decking: the process of covering a surface with planks, especially as a floor or outdoor feature
