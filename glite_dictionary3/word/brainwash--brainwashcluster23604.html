to brainwash

verb
forcibly indoctrinate someone with new beliefs
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnˌwɑʃ /; / ˈbreɪnwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnwɒʃ /; / ˈbreɪnˌwɒʃ / (BrE)
