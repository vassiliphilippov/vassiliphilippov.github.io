metasequoia

noun
a large deciduous tree from China, often ornamental
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtə sɪˈkwɔɪə / (AmE), / ˌmiːtə sɪˈkwɔɪə / (BrE)
A large, fast-growing deciduous tree native to China, with flat bright-green leaves and small globular cones, often grown as an ornamental and once known only from fossils.
