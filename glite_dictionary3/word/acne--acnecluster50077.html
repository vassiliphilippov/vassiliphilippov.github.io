acne

noun
skin condition causing pimples and lesions
Pronunciation: / ˈækni / (AmE), / ˈækni / (BrE)
A common skin condition in which the hair follicles become inflamed, causing pimples, blackheads, and other lesions, especially on the face, chest, and back.

Examples

acne on the face
Many teenagers struggle with acne during puberty.
Persistent acne can sometimes affect a person's confidence.
She tried several creams to reduce her acne outbreaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pimple: a small inflamed elevation of the skin, usually related to acne
