loner

noun
a person who prefers to be alone
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnər / (AmE), / ˈləʊnə / (BrE)
a person who prefers to be alone and avoids the company or assistance of others

Examples

quiet loner
After moving to the city, she became more of a loner and spent less time with friends.
He was always known as a loner who kept to himself.
Some people mistakenly think that being a loner means being unhappy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – extrovert: a person who is outgoing and socially confident
  • synonym – introvert: a person predominantly concerned with their own thoughts and feelings rather than with external things
