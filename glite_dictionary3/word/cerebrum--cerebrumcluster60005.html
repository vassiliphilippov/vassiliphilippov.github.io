cerebrum

noun
largest part of the brain; responsible for thought and movement
Pronunciation: / səˈriːbrəm / (AmE), / səˈriːbrəm / (BrE)
the largest part of the brain, located in the front part of the skull, consisting of two hemispheres and responsible for thought, voluntary movement, sensation, and consciousness
