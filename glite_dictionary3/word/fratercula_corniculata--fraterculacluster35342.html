Fratercula corniculata

noun
a puffin species of the northern Pacific
Pronunciation: / frəˈtɛrkjʊlə kɔrnɪˈkjuleɪtə / (AmE), / frəˈtɜːkjʊlə kɔːnɪˈkjʊːleɪtə / (BrE)
A species of puffin found in the northern Pacific, known as the horned puffin, notable for the fleshy black "horns" above its eyes.
