amusement

noun
feeling of delight at being entertained
Pronunciation: / əˈmjuzmənt / (AmE), / əˈmjuːzmənt / (BrE)
A feeling of delight or enjoyment caused by something entertaining or funny.

Examples

amusement at jokes
His jokes were a source of endless amusement to his friends.
She watched the children's performance with great amusement.
The look of surprise on his face caused much amusement among the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym – boredom: the feeling of being bored or uninterested
  • synonym – enjoyment: pleasure or satisfaction gained from something
