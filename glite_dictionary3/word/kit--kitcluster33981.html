to kit

verb
to supply with equipment or tools
Pronunciation: / kɪt / (AmE), / kɪt / (BrE)
to supply someone or something with a set of necessary articles, tools, or equipment

Examples

to kit a team
Each student will be kitted with a laptop on the first day.
The team was kitted out with brand new uniforms for the season.
We need to kit the expedition with proper camping gear before departure.

  • synonym – equip: to provide with necessary items for a particular purpose
