obstreperousness

noun
noisy defiance; unruliness
Pronunciation: / əbˈstrɛpɚəsnəs / (AmE), / əbˈstrɛpərəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being noisy and difficult to control; stubborn defiance marked by loud or unruly behavior.

Examples
