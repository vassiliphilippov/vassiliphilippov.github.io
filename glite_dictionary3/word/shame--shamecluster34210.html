to shame

verb
surpass or outdo greatly
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪm / (AmE), / ʃeɪm / (BrE)
To surpass or outdo (someone or something) to such a degree that they appear inferior or inadequate.

Examples

to shame a rival
Her brilliant performance shamed all her rivals at the talent show.
The new runner shamed the competition by finishing a full minute ahead.
The team's victory last night shamed their previous best by a huge margin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – outdo: to do better than someone else
  • synonym – surpass: to be better or greater than (someone or something)
