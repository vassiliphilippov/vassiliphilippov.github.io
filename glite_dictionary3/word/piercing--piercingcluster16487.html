piercing

adjective
keenly perceptive or insightful
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪr.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪə.sɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person's intelligence, observation, or remark) showing the ability to notice and understand things quickly and deeply; perceptive or insightful.

Examples

piercing insight
Her piercing analysis quickly exposed the flaws in the argument.
The reporter's piercing questions left the politician visibly uncomfortable.
With a piercing gaze, she seemed to see right through his excuses.

Related Terms

  • antonym – blunt: not perceptive or lacking sharpness in understanding
  • synonym – incisive: clear-thinking and able to express ideas sharply
  • synonym – penetrating: showing an ability to understand things deeply
