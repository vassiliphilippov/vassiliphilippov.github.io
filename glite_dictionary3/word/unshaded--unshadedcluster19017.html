unshaded

adjective
not having shading or shadows (esp. in drawings)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃeɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of a drawing or picture) lacking areas of shadow or shading; not depicted with shadow.

Examples

unshaded drawing
Children often create unshaded sketches before learning how to draw shadows and depth.
The artist preferred using unshaded lines to create a clean, minimalist effect.
This comic strip is drawn in a completely unshaded style, giving it a bold appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym – shaded: (of a picture) with shadowed or shaded areas
  • synonym – shadowless: lacking or free from shadows
