swayback

adjective
having a sagging or curved spine
Pronunciation: / ˈsweɪˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsweɪˌbak / (BrE)
having an abnormal downward curvature or sagging of the spine, especially in horses or other animals

Examples

swayback horse
Certain breeds are more prone to becoming swayback as they age.
The old mare was clearly swayback after years of hard work on the farm.
Veterinarians examined the horse and diagnosed it as swayback.

Related Terms

  • synonym – lordosis: excessive inward curvature of the spine, especially in humans
