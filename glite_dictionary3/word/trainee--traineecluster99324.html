trainee

noun
person undergoing training
Pronunciation: / treɪˈni / (AmE), / treɪˈniː / (BrE)
a person who is being taught the skills needed for a particular job or activity

Examples

new trainee arrives
All trainees must complete a safety course before using the equipment.
As a trainee chef, he learned a lot from experienced staff in the kitchen.
The new trainee started her first day on the job today.

Related Terms

  • synonym – apprentice: a person learning a trade from a skilled employer
  • antonym – trainer: a person who teaches or coaches skills
