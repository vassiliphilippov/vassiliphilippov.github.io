dolmen

noun
type of prehistoric stone tomb
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊl.mən / (AmE), / ˈdɒl.mən / (BrE)
a prehistoric structure consisting of two or more large upright stones supporting a flat horizontal capstone, typically used as a tomb

Examples

ancient dolmen tomb
Archaeologists recently uncovered a new dolmen in the rural countryside.
The ancient dolmen stood silently in the grassy field, its massive stones weathered by centuries.
