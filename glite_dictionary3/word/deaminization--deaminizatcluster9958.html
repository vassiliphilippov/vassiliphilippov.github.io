deaminization

noun
removal of amino group from a compound
Pronunciation: / diˌæmɪnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌamɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the removal of an amino group from an amino acid or another amino compound

Examples

deaminization of amino acids
