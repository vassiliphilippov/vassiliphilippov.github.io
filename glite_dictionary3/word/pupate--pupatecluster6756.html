to pupate

verb
to develop into a pupa (biology)
Pronunciation: / ˈpjuː.peɪt / (AmE), / ˈpjuː.peɪt / (BrE)
(of an insect larva) to develop or transform into a pupa as part of its life cycle.

Examples

to pupate into a pupa
After several days of feeding, the caterpillars pupate and form chrysalises.
It takes about a week for mosquito larvae to pupate in warm temperatures.
Many beetle larvae pupate underground to protect themselves during metamorphosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym – metamorphose: to change form, especially as during an insect's development
