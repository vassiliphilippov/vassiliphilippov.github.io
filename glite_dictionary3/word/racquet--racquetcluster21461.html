racquet

noun
sports implement for hitting a ball or shuttlecock
Pronunciation: / ˈrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈrakɪt / (BrE)
A sports implement with a handle and an oval frame strung with cords, used to hit a ball or shuttlecock in games such as tennis, squash, or badminton.

Examples

tennis racquet
His badminton racquet snapped during the final match.
It takes practice to control the racquet and improve your serve.
She bought a new racquet for her tennis lessons.
