purchase

noun
means of gaining a firm hold or advantage
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.tʃəs /; / ˈpɜrt͡ʃəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.tʃəs /; / ˈpɜːtʃəs / (BrE)
the ability to gain a firm hold or an advantage, especially by applying pressure or effort; a means of exerting influence or control in a situation

Examples
