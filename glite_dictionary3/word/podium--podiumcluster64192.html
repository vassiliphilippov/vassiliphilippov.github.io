podium

noun
raised platform for speaker or awardee
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊdiəm / (AmE), / ˈpəʊdiəm / (BrE)
A small raised platform on which a person stands to be seen by an audience, often when giving a speech or receiving an award.

Examples

stand on the podium
Officials placed the medals on the podium before the ceremony began.
She approached the podium to deliver her acceptance speech.
The winner stood proudly on the podium as the crowd cheered.

Related Terms

  • synonym – platform: a flat, raised level surface used for people or things to stand on
