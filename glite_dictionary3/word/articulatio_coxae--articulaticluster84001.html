articulatio coxae

noun
the anatomical hip joint
Pronunciation: / ɑrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃioʊ ˈkoʊksaɪ / (AmE), / ɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃɪəʊ ˈkɒksiː / (BrE)
the ball-and-socket joint formed between the head of the femur and the acetabulum of the pelvis; also known as the hip joint

Damage to the articulatio coxae can result in limited mobility and pain.
