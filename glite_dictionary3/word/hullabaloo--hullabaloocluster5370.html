hullabaloo

noun
loud commotion or fuss
Pronunciation: / ˌhʌləbəˈlu / (AmE), / ˌhʌləbəˈluː / (BrE)
a commotion or noisy fuss, especially one made by a group of people in protest or excitement

Examples

loud hullabaloo
Despite the hullabaloo in the street, she remained calm and focused on her work.
The children created a hullabaloo when the magician arrived at the party.
There was quite a hullabaloo when the decision was announced.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calm: a state of peace and quiet, the opposite of disturbance
  • synonym – commotion: a state of confused and noisy disturbance
  • synonym – uproar: a loud and impassioned noise or disturbance
