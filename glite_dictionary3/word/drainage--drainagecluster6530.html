drainage

noun
the removal of liquid, usually by channels
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪnɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdreɪnɪdʒ / (BrE)
the process of removing liquid from a place or object, typically by allowing it to flow away through channels or holes

Examples

drainage system
