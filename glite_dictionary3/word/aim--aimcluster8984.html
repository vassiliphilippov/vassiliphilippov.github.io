to aim

verb
design for a specific audience
Pronunciation: / eɪm / (AmE), / eɪm / (BrE)
to direct something (such as a remark, product, or action) toward a particular person, group, or purpose

Examples

to aim a message

The workshop was aimed at improving communication among employees.

The remarks were clearly aimed at the opposition party.

What exactly are you aiming at with that suggestion?
