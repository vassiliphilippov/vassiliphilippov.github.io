sardonic

adjective
scornfully mocking or cynical
Pronunciation: / sɑrˈdɑnɪk / (AmE), / sɑːˈdɒnɪk / (BrE)
Displaying a cynical, mocking, or scornful sense of humor; disdainfully or ironically humorous.

Examples

sardonic smile
He gave a sardonic smile as he dismissed their concerns.
Her sardonic comments made everyone laugh, although some found them a bit harsh.
The critic's sardonic review stung the director more than he cared to admit.
