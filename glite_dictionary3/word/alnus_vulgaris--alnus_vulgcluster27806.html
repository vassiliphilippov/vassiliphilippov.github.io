Alnus vulgaris

noun
species of alder tree (common alder)
Pronunciation: / ˈælnəs vʌlˈɡɛərɪs / (AmE), / ˈal.nəs vʌlˈɡɛər.ɪs / (BrE)
A species of alder tree native to Europe and western Asia, with brown-black bark, woody catkins, and leaves hairy beneath; also known as the common alder.
