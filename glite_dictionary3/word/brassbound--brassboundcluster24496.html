brassbound

adjective
having brass fittings or trim
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of an object) having trim or fittings made of brass, especially as decoration or reinforcement.

Examples

brassbound chest
The captain stored valuable documents in a brassbound chest for extra security.
The old mansion had a huge, brassbound door at its entrance.
We admired the brassbound trunk that belonged to her grandfather.
