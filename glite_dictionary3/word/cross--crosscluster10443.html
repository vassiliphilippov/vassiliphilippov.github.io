cross

noun
Christian symbol; crucifixion representation
Pronunciation: / krɑs / (AmE), / krɒs / (BrE)
a structure or symbol consisting of an upright and a crosspiece, especially as a symbol of Christianity; also, a burden or serious difficulty one must endure
