knot

noun
a fastening made by tying rope or string
Pronunciation: / nɑt / (AmE), / nɒt / (BrE)
a fastening made by tying a piece of rope, string, or other material upon itself or to another object

Examples

Scouts learn how to tie different types of knot for camping.
