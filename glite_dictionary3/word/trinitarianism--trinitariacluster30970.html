Trinitarianism

noun
Christian doctrine of the Trinity
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪnɪˈtɛriənˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌtrɪnɪˈtɛːrɪənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
The Christian doctrine that emphasizes belief in the Trinity, the unity of Father, Son, and Holy Spirit as three persons in one God.
