sedan

noun
type of passenger car with a closed body
Pronunciation: / sɪˈdæn / (AmE), / sɪˈdæn / (BrE)
a passenger car with a closed body, separate trunk, and typically front and rear seats and two or four doors

Examples

comfortable sedan car
He prefers driving a sedan because of its comfort and ample seating space.
The sedan easily accommodated their family of five for the weekend trip.
They purchased a new sedan with four doors and a spacious trunk.
