pilferage

noun
theft of small items or amounts
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlfərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɪlfərɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or practice of stealing small amounts or minor items, especially repeatedly

Examples

pilferage of goods
