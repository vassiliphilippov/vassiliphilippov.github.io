Fuji

proper noun
highest mountain in Japan; extinct volcano
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːdʒi / (AmE), / ˈfuːdʒi / (BrE)
an extinct volcano in south-central Honshu, Japan, known for its symmetrical snow-capped peak and as the country's highest mountain; a sacred site and popular destination for climbers and pilgrims
