barrel

noun
tube of a gun through which bullets travel
Pronunciation: / ˈbærəl / (AmE), / ˈbærəl / (BrE)
The long, cylindrical part of a firearm through which the bullet travels when the gun is fired.

Examples

gun barrel
A longer barrel can improve the accuracy of a firearm.
He cleaned the barrel of his rifle after the hunting trip.
The barrel was so hot after firing that you couldn't touch it.
