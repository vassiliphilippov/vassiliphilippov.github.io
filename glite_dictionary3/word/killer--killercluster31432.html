killer

noun
very difficult or exhausting task or situation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪlər / (AmE), / ˈkɪlə / (BrE)
(informal) A task or situation that is extremely difficult, exhausting, or challenging to handle.

Examples

a killer exam
That final exam was a real killer this year.
The hiking trail is beautiful, but the last mile is a killer.
This week’s schedule is a killer—I barely have time to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym – challenge: something difficult that tests abilities
  • antonym – walkover: a task that is very easy to do
