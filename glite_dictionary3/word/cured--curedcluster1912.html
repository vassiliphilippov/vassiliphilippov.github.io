cured

adjective
freed from illness or injury
Pronunciation: / kjʊrd / (AmE), / kjʊəd / (BrE)
no longer suffering from an illness, injury, or medical condition; restored to health.

Examples

cured of disease
After months of treatment, the doctors declared him cured of the infection.
She feels grateful every day for being cured after such a long illness.
The dog was finally cured and ready to return home.
