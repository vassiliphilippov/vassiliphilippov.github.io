to aspirate

verb
remove by suction
Pronunciation: / ˈæspəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈaspɪreɪt / (BrE)
to remove a fluid or substance from a body or container by means of suction

Examples

to aspirate fluid
Doctors may aspirate pus from an abscess using a syringe.
Technicians routinely aspirate samples in the laboratory to prepare them for analysis.
The nurse had to aspirate fluid from the patient's lungs during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym – suction: the act or process of removing something by sucking it out
