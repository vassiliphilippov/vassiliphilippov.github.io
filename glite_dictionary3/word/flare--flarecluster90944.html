flare

noun
bright light device for warning or identification
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
a device that produces a bright light, used for warning, illumination, or identification, especially in emergency situations

Examples

an emergency flare signal
A red emergency flare lit up the night sky above the stranded hikers.
Each lifeboat was equipped with a flare in case of distress.
The sailors ignited a flare to signal their location to the rescue helicopter.
