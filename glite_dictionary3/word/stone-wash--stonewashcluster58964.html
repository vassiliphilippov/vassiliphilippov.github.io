to stone-wash

verb
wash fabric with stones for a worn look
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊn.wɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈstəʊn.wɒʃ / (BrE)
to wash (fabric or clothing) with stones to produce a faded, worn appearance

Examples

to stone-wash jeans
Many denim manufacturers stone-wash their jeans to give them a vintage look.

They plan to stone-wash all the jackets in the new clothing line before release.
