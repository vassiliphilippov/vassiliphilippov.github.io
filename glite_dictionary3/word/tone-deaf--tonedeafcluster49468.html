tone-deaf

adjective
unable to distinguish musical pitch
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊnˌdɛf / (AmE), / ˈtəʊnˌdɛf / (BrE)
Unable to perceive or reproduce differences in musical pitch accurately; unable to appreciate music.

Examples

tone-deaf singer
Although he loves singing, Jake is completely tone-deaf and can't carry a tune.
Even if you're tone-deaf, you can still enjoy listening to music.
Some children are naturally talented at music, while others seem almost tone-deaf.

Related Terms

  • synonym – tuneless: lacking musical ability or unable to carry a tune
