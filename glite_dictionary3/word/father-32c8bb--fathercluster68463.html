to father

verb
be the male parent of (offspring)
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑðɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðə / (BrE)
to be the male parent of a child; to produce (offspring) by reproduction

Examples

to father a child

Some men father children but do not take part in raising them.
The scientist hopes to father a new generation of disease-resistant crops.
