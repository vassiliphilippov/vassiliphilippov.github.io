PFLP-GC

proper noun
Palestinian militant organization (abbreviation)
Pronunciation: / pi ɛf ɛl pi dʒi si / (AmE), / piː ɛf ɛl piː dʒiː siː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine – General Command, a Marxist–Leninist militant organization active mainly in the Middle East and responsible for several attacks in Western Europe.
