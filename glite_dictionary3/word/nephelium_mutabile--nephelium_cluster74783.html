Nephelium mutabile

noun
East Indian fruit tree; close to rambutan
Pronunciation: / nɪˈfiliəm mjuˈteɪbəl / (AmE), / nɪˈfiːliəm mjuːˈteɪbəli / (BrE)
An East Indian fruit tree of the soapberry family, bearing fruit similar to but sweeter than that of the rambutan.
