plume

noun
a feather-like shape or mass (e.g. smoke, grass)
Pronunciation: / plum / (AmE), / pluːm / (BrE)
a long, thin shape or mass that spreads out in the air like a feather, such as smoke, steam, or grass heads

Examples

plume of smoke
A plume of steam drifted above the hot spring, visible for miles.
A thick plume of smoke rose from the burning building.
The grass waved in the wind, its golden plumes glowing in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym – feather: a light, soft growth on the body of birds
