Anwar el-Sadat

proper noun
Egyptian president and Nobel Peace Prize laureate
Pronunciation: / ˈænwɑr ɛl səˈdɑt / (AmE), / ˈænwɑː ɛl səˈdæt / (BrE)
(1918–1981) Egyptian statesman who served as president of Egypt and was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize for negotiating a peace treaty with Israel.
