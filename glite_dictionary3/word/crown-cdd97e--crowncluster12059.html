crown

noun
artificial dental covering for a tooth
Pronunciation: / kraʊn / (AmE), / kraʊn / (BrE)
a dental appliance consisting of an artificial covering fitted over a broken or decayed tooth

Examples

dental crown fitting
After his accident, he needed a crown to restore his front tooth.
Getting a crown can help protect a tooth after a root canal.
The dentist matched the color of her crown to her natural teeth.
