clump

noun
heavy, dull sound (impact)
Pronunciation: / klʌmp / (AmE), / klʌmp / (BrE)
a heavy, dull sound produced by the impact of heavy objects or footsteps.

Examples

a clump on wood
There was a loud clump as the box fell onto the floor.
We heard the clump of boots echoing down the hallway.
With a clump, the heavy book landed on the table.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – bang: a sudden loud noise
  • synonym – thud: a dull sound, as of a heavy object striking a surface
