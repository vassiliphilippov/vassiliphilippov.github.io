fit

adjective
suitable or appropriate
Pronunciation: / fɪt / (AmE), / fɪt / (BrE)
suitable or of the right quality for a particular purpose or person.

Examples

fit for use
Only water that is fit for human consumption should be used.
She believes he is fit for the role of manager.
The document was not fit to be shown to the public.

Related Terms

  • synonym – suitable: right or appropriate for a particular person, purpose, or situation
  • antonym – unfit: not suitable or proper for a particular purpose or person
