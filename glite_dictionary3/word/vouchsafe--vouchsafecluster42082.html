to vouchsafe

verb
grant or give in a condescending way
Pronunciation: / ˌvaʊtʃˈseɪf / (AmE), / ˌvaʊtʃˈseɪf / (BrE)
to grant or give something to someone in a gracious, condescending, or reluctant manner

Examples

to vouchsafe an answer
She rarely vouchsafes her opinions to others.
The king did not vouchsafe a reply to the messenger's request.
Would you vouchsafe me an explanation of your decision?

Related Terms

  • antonym – deny: to refuse to give or grant something
  • synonym – grant: to give or allow someone something, usually formally
