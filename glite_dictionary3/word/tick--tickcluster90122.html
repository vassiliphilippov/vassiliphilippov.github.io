tick

noun
mark made to show completion or selection
Pronunciation: / tɪk / (AmE), / tɪk / (BrE)
a mark (✓) made to show something has been noted, selected, or completed, especially in lists or checkboxes

Examples

tick a box
Make sure you add a tick beside each item you have packed.
Place a tick in the box if you agree to the terms.
The teacher put a tick next to every correct answer.

Related Terms

  • synonym – check mark: a mark (✓) made to indicate agreement or completion, mainly in American English
