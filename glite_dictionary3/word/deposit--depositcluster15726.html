deposit

noun
layer of matter left by nature
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑzɪt /; / dɪˈpɑːzɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈpɒzɪt / (BrE)
a layer or mass of material (such as minerals, sediment, or organic matter) that has accumulated naturally
