alleviation

noun
the act of making pain or suffering less severe
Pronunciation: / əˌliːviˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌliːvɪˈeɪʃən /; / əˌliːviˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something less severe or the feeling of relief when something bad is reduced
