stack

noun
large industrial chimney
Pronunciation: / stæk / (AmE), / stak / (BrE)
a tall, large chimney, especially one on an industrial building, through which combustion gases and smoke are released

Examples

factory stack smoke
After the explosion, the old brick stack was left leaning dangerously.
New environmental regulations require filters to be installed on every stack to reduce emissions.
The factory's stack released a plume of steam into the cold morning air.

Related Terms

  • synonym – chimney: a structure for venting smoke or gases from a fire, furnace, or stove
  • synonym – smokestack: a tall chimney on a factory or ship for discharging smoke
