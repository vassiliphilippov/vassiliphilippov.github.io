tribute

noun
payment extorted as protection money
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪb.juːt /; / ˈtrɪbˌjut / (AmE), / ˈtrɪb.juːt / (BrE)
a payment made under threat or as a sign of dependence, often to avoid harm or as a form of submission
