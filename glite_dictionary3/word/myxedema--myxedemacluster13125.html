myxedema

noun
severe hypothyroid condition with swelling
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪk.sɪˈdiː.mə / (AmE), / ˌmɪk.sɪˈdiː.mə / (BrE)
A severe form of hypothyroidism characterized by dry skin, swelling around the lips and nose, and mental deterioration.

Examples

myxedema symptoms appear
Doctors noted signs of myxedema in individuals with longstanding thyroid deficiencies.
The patient was diagnosed with myxedema after presenting with facial swelling and dry skin.
Untreated myxedema can lead to serious mental deterioration if not managed promptly.
