morocco

noun
high-quality goatskin leather
Pronunciation: / məˈrɑkoʊ / (AmE), / məˈrɒkəʊ / (BrE)
A soft, durable leather made from goatskin, typically with a pebble-grained finish, used especially for making shoes and book bindings.
