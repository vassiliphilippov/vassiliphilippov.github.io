resurgent

adjective
increasing or reviving again
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɜːrdʒənt / (AmE), / rɪˈsɜːdʒənt / (BrE)
increasing or reviving after a period of little activity, popularity, or occurrence

Examples

resurgent interest
After years of decline, the city experienced a resurgent interest in urban renewal projects.
The party benefited from resurgent nationalism during the election campaign.
There has been resurgent enthusiasm for traditional crafts among young designers.
