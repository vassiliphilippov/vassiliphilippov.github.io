squash

noun
edible gourd fruit eaten as a vegetable
Pronunciation: / skwɑʃ / (AmE), / skwɒʃ / (BrE)
an edible vegetable (or the plant it grows on) from the gourd family, such as zucchini, pumpkin, or butternut squash
