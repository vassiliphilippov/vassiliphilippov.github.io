dryer

noun
machine that removes moisture
Pronunciation: / ˈdraɪ.ər / (AmE), / ˈdraɪ.ə / (BrE)
An electric or gas-powered appliance used for removing moisture from clothes or other items, typically by blowing heated air.

Examples

dryer for clothes
After washing the towels, I put them in the dryer to dry.
Our dryer broke down, so now we have to hang the clothes outside.
The new dryer uses sensors to save energy and prevent overheating.
