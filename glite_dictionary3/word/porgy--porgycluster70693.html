porgy

noun
edible fish of the southern Atlantic coast
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrɡi / (AmE), / ˈpɔːɡi / (BrE)
The lean flesh of a fish found in warm waters along the southern Atlantic coast of the United States, often used as food.

Examples

grilled porgy fillet

Many local restaurants serve grilled porgy as a specialty dish.
The mild flavor of porgy makes it popular in Southern seafood recipes.
