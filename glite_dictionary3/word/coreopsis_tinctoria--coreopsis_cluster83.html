Coreopsis tinctoria

noun
North American flowering annual; 'plains coreopsis'
Pronunciation: / kɔːˈɹiː.əp.sɪs tɪŋkˈtɔɹ.i.ə / (AmE), / kɒːˈriː.əp.sɪs tɪŋkˈtɔː.rɪ.ə / (BrE)
