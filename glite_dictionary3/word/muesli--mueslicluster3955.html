muesli

noun
breakfast cereal of oats, fruit, and nuts
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzli / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzli / (BrE)
A mixture of raw rolled oats, grains, dried fruit, seeds, and nuts, typically eaten with milk for breakfast.

Examples

bowl of muesli
For a healthy start to the day, she often chooses muesli with fresh berries and yogurt.
He bought a box of muesli at the supermarket to try a new breakfast routine.
Some people prefer their muesli soaked overnight for a softer texture.
