Dipteryx odorata

noun
tropical tree; source of tonka beans
Pronunciation: / dɪpˈtɛrɪks oʊˈdɔrətə / (AmE), / dɪpˈtɛrɪks əʊˈdɔːrətə / (BrE)
A tall tropical South American tree whose fragrant black seeds, known as tonka beans, are used for flavoring.
