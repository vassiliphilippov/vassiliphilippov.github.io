to aromatise

verb
fill with a pleasant smell
Pronunciation: / əˈroʊməˌtaɪz / (AmE), / əˈrɒmətaɪz / (BrE)
to fill or impregnate with a pleasant smell or aroma

Examples

to aromatise a room
A gentle breeze aromatised the room with the scent of lavender.
The chef likes to aromatise his dishes with fresh herbs.
You can aromatise oils by infusing them with citrus peel or spices.
