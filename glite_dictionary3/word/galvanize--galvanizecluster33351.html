to galvanize

verb
stimulate into action
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælvəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡælvənaɪz / (BrE)
to shock or excite someone into taking action

Examples

to galvanize a team
Her determination galvanized the team, leading to a remarkable comeback victory.
The discovery of fraud galvanized investors to take immediate legal action.
The president's speech managed to galvanize the entire nation into demanding reform.

Related Terms

  • synonym – incite: to encourage someone to act, especially in a forceful way
  • synonym – motivate: to provide someone with a reason for doing something
