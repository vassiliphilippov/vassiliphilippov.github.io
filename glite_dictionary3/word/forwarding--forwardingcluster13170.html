forwarding

noun
the act of sending on to another destination
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwədɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of sending something on to another destination, especially mail or communications

Examples

forwarding of emails
Automatic forwarding of emails can save you a lot of time.
The forwarding of important documents was handled by the office staff.
The company charges a fee for the forwarding of parcels to overseas locations.

  • synonym – transmission: the act of sending or conveying from one person or place to another
