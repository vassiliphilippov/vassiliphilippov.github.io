dreamless

adjective
without dreams (of sleep)
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmləs / (AmE), / ˈdriːmləs / (BrE)
(of sleep) not disturbed by dreams; occurring without dreaming.

Examples

dreamless sleep
After days of anxiety, he finally enjoyed a dreamless rest.
He fell into a dreamless sleep after his long journey.
The medication left her feeling calm and dreamless throughout the night.

Related Terms
