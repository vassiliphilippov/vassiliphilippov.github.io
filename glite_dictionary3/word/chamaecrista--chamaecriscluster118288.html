Chamaecrista

noun
genus of tropical plants with sensitive leaves
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪmiːˈkrɪstə /; / ˌkæmiˈkrɪstə / (AmE), / ˌkamɪˈkrɪstə /; / ˌkæmiˈkrɪstə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical herbs or subshrubs in the legume family, known for sensitive leaves and seed pods that burst open suddenly; some species were formerly classified as Cassia
