crustaceous

adjective
relating to crustaceans (crabs, lobsters, etc.)
Pronunciation: / krəˈsteɪʃəs / (AmE), / krʌˈsteɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of crustaceans, a class of mostly aquatic arthropods such as crabs and lobsters
