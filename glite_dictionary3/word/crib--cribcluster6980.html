crib

noun
cards discarded in cribbage
Pronunciation: / krɪb / (AmE), / krɪb / (BrE)
(in cribbage) the set of cards discarded by players, which count toward the dealer's score.

Examples

crib cards discarded
He discarded a pair of fives into the crib, hoping for a high score.
In cribbage, having a good crib can make all the difference in a close game.
The dealer picked up the crib and began to count the points.
