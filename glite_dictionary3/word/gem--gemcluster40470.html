gem

noun
virtual in‑game currency or collectible
Pronunciation: / dʒem / (AmE), / dʒem / (BrE)
In video games and mobile apps, a virtual item or unit of in‑game currency that players earn or buy to unlock features, buy items, or speed progress.

Examples

collect gems in game
Players can buy gems to speed up building and unlock premium characters.
She earned a rare gem after completing the daily quest and used it for a skin.
Developers often sell bundles of gems so players can purchase extra lives or special items.

Related Terms

  • synonym – in‑game currency: virtual money used within a game to purchase items or services
  • synonym – token: a unit or object representing value or access within a system
  • synonym – virtual currency: a digital unit of value used in online platforms and games
