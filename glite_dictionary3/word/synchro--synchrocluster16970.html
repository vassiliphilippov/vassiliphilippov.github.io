synchro

noun
synchronizing generator-motor system
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋkrəʊ / (BrE)
a system consisting of an electrical generator and a motor so connected that the motor reproduces the generator's movements; used for precise synchronization

Examples

synchro control device
Engineers installed a synchro for accurate position feedback in the control panel.
The ship's navigation used a synchro system to relay compass information to the bridge.
Training materials explained how a synchro converts mechanical motion to electrical signals.
