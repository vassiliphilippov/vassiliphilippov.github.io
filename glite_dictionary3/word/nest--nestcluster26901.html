nest

noun
criminals, spies, or similar group in one place
Pronunciation: / nɛst / (AmE), / nɛst / (BrE)
a secret or protected place where a group of people (such as criminals, spies, or weapons) are hidden or operate

The troops were ordered to destroy the machine-gun nest before moving forward.
They located a sniper nest hidden among the trees.
