province

noun
a person's area of responsibility or expertise
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.vɪns / (AmE), / ˈprɒv.ɪns / (BrE)
an area of responsibility, activity, or knowledge that is considered to belong to a particular person, organization, or group

Examples

province of science
Debate about ethics is the province of philosophers and theologians.
It is not within my province to approve such expenditures.
Organizing the details of the event was her province and she handled everything beautifully.

  • synonym – domain: an area of knowledge or activity
  • synonym – responsibility: the state or fact of having a duty to deal with something
  • synonym – sphere: an area of activity or interest
