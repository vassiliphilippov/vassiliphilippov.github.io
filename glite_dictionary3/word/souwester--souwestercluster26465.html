sou'wester

noun
waterproof hat with long back brim
Pronunciation: / saʊˈwɛstər / (AmE), / saʊˈwɛstə / (BrE)
a waterproof hat with a wide, slanting brim, longer at the back than the front, worn especially by sailors in wet weather

Examples

rain sou'wester hat
Old photographs show children playing on the beach in oilskins and sou'westers.
She wore a bright yellow sou'wester to keep her dry during the storm.
The fisherman pulled his sou'wester tighter as the rain began to pour.
