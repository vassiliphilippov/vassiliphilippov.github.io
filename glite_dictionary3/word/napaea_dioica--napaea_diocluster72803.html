Napaea dioica

noun
tall American herb with palmate leaves and white flowers
Pronunciation: / nəˈpiː.ə daɪˈoʊ.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / nəˈpiː.ə daɪˈəʊ.ɪ.kə / (BrE)
