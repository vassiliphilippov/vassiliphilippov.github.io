hemiplegic

noun
person paralyzed on one side
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmiˈpliːdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛmiˈpliːdʒɪk / (BrE)
a person who is paralyzed on one side of the body due to hemiplegia

Examples

a hemiplegic patient
As a hemiplegic, she required assistance with daily activities after her stroke.
Support groups can provide valuable resources for the family of a hemiplegic.
The physical therapist specializes in working with hemiplegic patients to improve their mobility.
