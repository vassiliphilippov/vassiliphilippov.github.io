unappetizing

adjective
not appealing in taste, smell, or look
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈæpəˌtaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈapɪtʌɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
Not appealing in appearance, smell, or taste; not likely to make you want to eat it.

Examples

unappetizing food
Despite its unappetizing color, the soup actually tasted quite good.
The cafeteria served an unappetizing stew that most students avoided.
The leftovers looked unappetizing after sitting in the fridge for a week.
