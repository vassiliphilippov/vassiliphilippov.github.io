sweepstakes

noun
lottery where pooled money is the prize
Pronunciation: / ˈswipˌsteɪks / (AmE), / ˈswiːpsteɪks / (BrE)
a competition, especially one in which money is paid to enter and the entire prize consists of the funds collected from all the participants

Examples

sweepstakes entry form
Many people enter sweepstakes each year, hoping to become wealthy overnight.
She won the grand prize in a national sweepstakes and used the money to travel the world.
The charity organized a sweepstakes to raise funds, with all proceeds going to the winner.

  • synonym – lottery: a contest in which winners are chosen by chance
