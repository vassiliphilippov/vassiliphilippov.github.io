micropyle

noun
tiny opening in ovule wall
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.kroʊˌpaɪl / (AmE), / ˈmʌɪ.krəʊ.paɪl / (BrE)
a minute opening in the wall of an ovule, through which the pollen tube enters during fertilization

Examples

micropyle of an ovule
Damage to the micropyle can prevent successful fertilization in many flowering plants.
Scientists observed the location of the micropyle under a microscope to study plant reproduction.
The pollen tube enters the ovule through the micropyle during the process of fertilization.
