crayfish

noun
freshwater crustacean resembling a small lobster
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪ.fɪʃ /; / ˈkreɪˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.fɪʃ / (BrE)
a small freshwater crustacean with a hard shell and pincers, resembling a small lobster, found in rivers and streams
