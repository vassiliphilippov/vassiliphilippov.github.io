to unpin

verb
remove pins from something
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpɪn / (AmE), / ʌnˈpɪn / (BrE)
to remove pins from something; to unfasten by taking out the pins

Examples

to unpin a badge
After the ceremony, he unpinned the medal from his uniform and placed it in a box.
Please make sure to unpin all the decorations before we pack everything away.
She carefully unpinned the note from the bulletin board.

Related Terms

  • antonym – pin: to fasten or attach with a pin
