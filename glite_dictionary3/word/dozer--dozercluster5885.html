dozer

noun
large earth-moving tractor with blade
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊzər / (AmE), / ˈdəʊzə / (BrE)
A large, powerful tractor equipped with a broad blade at the front for moving earth or flattening ground, especially on construction sites.

Examples

dozer moves earth
He operated the dozer with skill, leveling the ground perfectly.
Several dozers lined up along the edge of the field, ready for the day's work.
The construction crew used a dozer to clear the site for the new building.
