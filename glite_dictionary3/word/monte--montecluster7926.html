monte

noun
a Spanish card gambling game
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːn.teɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɒn.teɪ / (BrE)
A gambling card game of Spanish origin in which players bet that one of three face-up cards will be matched before the others as cards are dealt from the pack.
