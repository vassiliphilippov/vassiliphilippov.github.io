stutterer

noun
person who stutters while speaking
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌt̬.ɚ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈstʌt.ər.ə / (BrE)
a person who speaks with involuntary pauses and repetitions, typically as a result of a speech disorder

Examples

child stutterer
As a child, he was a shy stutterer who avoided speaking in class.
Emily overcame her challenges as a stutterer through therapy and perseverance.
The support group allowed each stutterer to share their experiences openly.
