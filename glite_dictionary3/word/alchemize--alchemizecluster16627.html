to alchemize

verb
transform as if by alchemy
Pronunciation: / ˈælkəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈælkɪˌmaɪz / (BrE)
To transform or change something, especially in a mysterious or magical way, as if by alchemy.

Examples

to alchemize simple things
Some artists attempt to alchemize everyday experiences into profound works of art.
The ancient wizard claimed he could alchemize base metals into gold.
The chef hoped to alchemize the simple ingredients into a memorable dish.
