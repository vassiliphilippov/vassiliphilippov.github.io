judgement

noun
court document explaining a legal decision
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌdʒmənt / (BrE)
a legal decision or official document stating the result of a court case

Examples

court judgement
A copy of the final judgement was sent to all parties involved in the case.
In his written judgement, the judge explained the reasons behind the verdict.
The court issued its judgement after several days of testimony and deliberation.
A written judgement was issued and filed in the records of the case.
The court delivered its judgement after reviewing all the evidence presented by both sides.
The defendant awaited the judgement that would determine his fate.
