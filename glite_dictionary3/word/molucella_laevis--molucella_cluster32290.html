Molucella laevis

noun
an aromatic plant with bell-shaped calyces
Pronunciation: / moʊˌluːˈsɛlə ˈliːvɪs / (AmE), / məˌlʊˈsɛlə ˈliːvɪs / (BrE)
An aromatic annual plant with tall stems, bearing small whitish flowers surrounded by conspicuous bell-shaped calyces, commonly called 'Bells of Ireland'.
