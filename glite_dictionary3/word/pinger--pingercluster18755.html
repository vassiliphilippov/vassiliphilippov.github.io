pinger

noun
sender of pings/notifications
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋə / (BrE)
a person, program, or service that sends short messages or notifications (pings) to check status or prompt a response.

Examples

app pinger notifications
The app uses a pinger to nudge inactive users with short reminder notifications every morning.
Our monitoring system includes two pingers that check server response times and alert us if slow.
After no reply to his email, she sent a gentle pinger — a quick message asking for a response.

Related Terms

  • synonym – notifier: a tool or person that sends alerts or notifications
  • synonym – ping: to send a short message or signal to check availability or get attention
