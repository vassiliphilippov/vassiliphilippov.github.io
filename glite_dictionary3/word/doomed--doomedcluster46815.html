doomed

noun
those destined to die soon
Pronunciation: / ˈduːmd / (AmE), / ˈduːmd / (BrE)
people who are destined or fated to die soon, often as a result of unavoidable circumstances.

Examples

the doomed soldiers

In the final act, the playwright focuses on the doomed and their sense of resignation.
