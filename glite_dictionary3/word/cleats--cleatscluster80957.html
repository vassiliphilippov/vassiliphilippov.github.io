cleats

noun
sports shoes with projections on the soles
Pronunciation: / klits / (AmE), / kliːts / (BrE)
sports shoes with projections (studs or spikes) on the soles to provide better grip on soft or slippery ground

Examples

cleats for soccer
His old cleats were worn down, so he bought a new pair for the season.
The soccer team laced up their cleats before heading onto the field.
You must wear cleats on wet grass to avoid slipping during the game.
