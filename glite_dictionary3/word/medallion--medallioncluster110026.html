medallion

noun
a large commemorative or award medal
Pronunciation: / məˈdæliən / (AmE), / mɪˈdalɪən / (BrE)
a large medal, often given as an award for winning a championship or to commemorate a significant event

Examples

gold medallion award
At the ceremony, each winner was presented with a commemorative medallion.
She wore the gold medallion she had received after winning the marathon.
The medallion hangs proudly on his wall, marking his athletic achievements.

Related Terms

  • synonym – medal: a flat piece of metal given as an award for achievement
