Trinidad

proper noun
Caribbean island off Venezuela
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪnɪˌdæd / (AmE), / ˈtrɪnɪdæd / (BrE)
an island in the southern Caribbean, just off the northeastern coast of Venezuela, forming part of the nation of Trinidad and Tobago.
