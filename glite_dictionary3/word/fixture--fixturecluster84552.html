fixture

noun
a regular presence in a place
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪkstʃə / (BrE)
a person who is regularly seen in a particular place; a regular patron or habitual presence

Examples

a fixture at the bar
After years of going to the same coffee shop, she became a fixture there.
Everyone in the neighborhood knows him—he's a fixture at the local basketball court.
The old man is a fixture at the corner bar, always sitting on his favorite stool.

Related Terms

  • synonym – habitue: a regular patron of a place
  • synonym – regular: a person who frequents a place often
