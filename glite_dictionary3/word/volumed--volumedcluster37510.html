volumed

adjective
rising or formed in rounded masses
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːlmd / (AmE), / ˈvɒlmd / (BrE)
(of smoke, cloud, or similar substances) formed or rising in rounded or billowing masses.

Examples

volumed smoke clouds
He coughed, struggling to see through the volumed haze filling the hallway.
The fire sent volumed smoke spiraling into the sky.
Thick, volumed clouds gathered above the mountains before the storm.
