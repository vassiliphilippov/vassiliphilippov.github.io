lowering

adjective
darkened or threatening, especially of the sky
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaʊ.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(of the sky or weather) darkened by clouds, threatening, or gloomy.

Examples

lowering sky
A lowering afternoon cast a shadow over the deserted park.
The lowering sky threatened to break into a storm at any moment.
The hikers hurried home as the lowering clouds gathered above.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bright: giving out or reflecting much light; shining
  • synonym – gloomy: dark or poorly lit, especially so as to appear depressing or frightening
