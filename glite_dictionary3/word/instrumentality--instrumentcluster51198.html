instrumentality

noun
means or device for achieving an end
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnstrəˌmɛnˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˌɪnstrʊmənˈtælɪti /; / ˌɪnstrjuːmənˈtælɪti / (BrE)
a means, tool, or the quality of being a means or agent used to achieve a particular purpose

Examples
