pear-shaped

adjective
shaped like a pear, tapered at one end
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛr ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpeə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a rounded shape that is wider at the bottom and tapers toward the top, like a pear.

Examples

pear-shaped diamond ring
She chose a pear-shaped diamond for her engagement ring.
The fruit was easily identified by its pear-shaped outline.
The vase has a delicate pear-shaped body with a narrow neck.
