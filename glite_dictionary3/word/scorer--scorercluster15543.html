scorer

noun
official who records scores in a game
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔrər / (AmE), / ˈskɔːrə / (BrE)
an official responsible for recording the score during the progress of a game or sporting event

Examples

scorer at a match
The scorer announced the final results once the game ended.
The cricket scorer tallied the runs after every over.
Without an official scorer, mistakes in the score can easily occur during the match.
