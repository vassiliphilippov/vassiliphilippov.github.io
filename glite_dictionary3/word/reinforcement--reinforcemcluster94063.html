reinforcement

noun
device or material for added strength
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrsmənt /; / ˌriːɪnˈfɔrsmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈfɔːsmənt / (BrE)
something (such as a device, material, extra troops, or supplies) that is added to make something stronger or more effective

Examples

metal reinforcement bars
