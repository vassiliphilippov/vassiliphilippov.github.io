joyless

adjective
lacking happiness or pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪlɪs / (BrE)
Not experiencing, showing, or causing any happiness or pleasure.

Examples

joyless face

They described the ceremony as a cold and joyless affair.
