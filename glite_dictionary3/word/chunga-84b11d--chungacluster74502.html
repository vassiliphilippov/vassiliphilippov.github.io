chunga

noun
South American Cariama bird
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃuŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈtʃuŋɡə / (BrE)
A large, long-legged, crested bird native to South America, especially Argentina, known as the Cariama; scientifically Chunga burmeisteri.
