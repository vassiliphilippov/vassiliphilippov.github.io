gallinule

noun
small aquatic bird of the genus Gallinula
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælɪˌnul / (AmE), / ˈɡalɪnjuːl / (BrE)
a small aquatic bird of the genus Gallinula, distinguished by a frontal shield and resembling a domestic hen; found in marshy habitats

Examples

gallinule in wetlands
