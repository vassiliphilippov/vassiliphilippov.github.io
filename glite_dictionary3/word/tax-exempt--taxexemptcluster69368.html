tax-exempt

noun
security not subject to tax
Pronunciation: / tæks ɪgˈzɛmpt / (AmE), / tæks ɪgˈzɛmpt / (BrE)
A financial security, such as a bond, whose income is not subject to taxation by federal, state, or local authorities.

Examples

tax-exempt bond
