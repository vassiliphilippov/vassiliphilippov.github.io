Cosimo the Elder

proper noun
Italian financier and statesman (1389–1464)
Pronunciation: / ˈkəʊzɪmoʊ ði ˈɛldər / (AmE), / ˈkɒzɪməʊ ði ˈɛldə / (BrE)
(1389–1464) An Italian financier and statesman who played a key role in the rise of the Medici family and was influential in Florence's political and artistic life.
