tinea unguium

noun
fungal infection of the nails
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪniə ʌŋˈɡwiəm / (AmE), / ˈtɪniə ʌŋˈɡwɪəm / (BrE)
A fungal infection of the nails, especially the toenails.

Examples

tinea unguium infection
People with diabetes are more susceptible to developing tinea unguium.
The patient was diagnosed with tinea unguium after presenting with thickened, discolored toenails.
Treatment for tinea unguium often requires antifungal medication for several months.
