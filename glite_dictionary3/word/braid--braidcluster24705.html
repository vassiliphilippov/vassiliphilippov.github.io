to braid

verb
form by intertwining strands
Pronunciation: / breɪd / (AmE), / breɪd / (BrE)
to interweave three or more strands (such as hair, thread, or dough) to form a braid

Examples

to braid bread dough
If you braid the dough before baking, it creates a beautiful pattern on the bread.
She braided her daughter's hair every morning before school.
The artisan skillfully braided colorful threads to make bracelets for tourists.
He helped his daughter braid friendship bracelets for her friends.
She likes to braid her hair every morning before school.
The stylist carefully braided the intricate designs into my hair.
