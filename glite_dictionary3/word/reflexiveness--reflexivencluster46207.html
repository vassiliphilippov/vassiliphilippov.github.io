reflexiveness

noun
property of reflexive pronouns relating to their subject
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛksɪvnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛksɪvnɪs / (BrE)
the grammatical property or relation in which a reflexive pronoun refers back to its own subject or antecedent
