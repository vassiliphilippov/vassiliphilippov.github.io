to entitle

verb
give the right to
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtaɪtəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪtəl / (BrE)
to give someone the right to do or have something

Examples

to entitle someone to benefits
Holding a valid ticket entitles you to enter the event.
Membership in the club entitles you to use all the facilities.
The new benefits policy entitles employees to extra vacation days.

Related Terms

  • synonym – authorize: to give official permission for something
  • antonym – disqualify: to take away someone's right to do something
