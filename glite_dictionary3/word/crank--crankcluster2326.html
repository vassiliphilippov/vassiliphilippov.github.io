to crank

verb
bend into the shape of a crank
Pronunciation: / kræŋk / (AmE), / kræŋk / (BrE)
to bend or form into the shape of a crank or an angled piece

Examples

to crank a metal rod
Please crank the wire at this point until it forms an L-shape.
The metal rod must be cranked at the end to fit the apparatus.
Workers cranked the pipe to create an efficient handle shape.
