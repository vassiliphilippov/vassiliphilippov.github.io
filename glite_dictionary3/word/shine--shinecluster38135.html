to shine

verb
(of face) appear with bright color
Pronunciation: / ʃaɪn / (AmE), / ʃaɪn / (BrE)
(of a person's face or manner) to appear bright, healthy, or happy, often as a result of emotion or well-being

Examples

shine with happiness
After jogging, her cheeks shone with a rosy flush.
His face shined with excitement as he heard the good news.
The children's faces shone red from playing outside in the cold.
He shone with happiness after receiving the award.
Her whole face seemed to shine when she heard the good news.
Their eyes shone with excitement at the prospect of going on vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beam: smile radiantly; express happiness clearly with one's face
  • synonym – glow: (of a person's face) to radiate warmth or color, often from health, exertion, or emotion
  • synonym – radiate: clearly emit a feeling, quality, or emotion through one's expression or manner
