reactor

noun
device for controlled nuclear reactions
Pronunciation: / riˈæktər / (AmE), / riˈæktə / (BrE)
A device in which a controlled nuclear reaction is maintained and used to produce energy or artificial elements.

Examples

nuclear reactor
After the accident, operations at the reactor were suspended for several months.
Scientists at the facility monitor the reactor around the clock for safety.
The city is building a new reactor to supply clean energy to households.
