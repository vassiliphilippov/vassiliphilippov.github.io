Giotto di Bondone

proper noun
Florentine painter and early art innovator
Pronunciation: / dʒiˈoʊtoʊ di bɒnˈdoʊneɪ / (AmE), / dʒɪˈɒtəʊ di bɒnˈdəʊneɪ / (BrE)
(1267–1337) Italian painter from Florence who pioneered a more naturalistic style, greatly influencing pre-Renaissance art.

Examples

Giotto di Bondone painted frescoes
Art historians praise Giotto di Bondone for his revolutionary use of perspective and emotion.

The frescoes by Giotto di Bondone in the Arena Chapel are considered masterpieces of Western art.
