scorching

adjective
extremely hot or parching
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔrtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
Very hot; burning or parching due to heat.

Examples

scorching sun
Her face was red after standing outside in the scorching weather.
The scorching sun forced everyone to seek shade by noon.
We took a walk despite the scorching afternoon heat.
