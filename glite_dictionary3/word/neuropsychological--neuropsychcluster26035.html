neuropsychological

adjective
relating to neuropsychology
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˌsaɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or concerned with neuropsychology, especially with how brain function affects psychological processes and behavior.

Examples

neuropsychological assessment
A neuropsychological assessment can help determine how a neurological disorder is affecting cognitive function.
Researchers found a neuropsychological correlation between memory loss and damage to specific brain regions.
The patient underwent a series of neuropsychological tests following her brain injury.
