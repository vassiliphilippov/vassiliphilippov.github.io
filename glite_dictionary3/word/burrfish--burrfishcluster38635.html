burrfish

noun
spiny fish with rigid spines
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, spiny fish of the family Diodontidae, with rigid, flattened spines that can be raised for defense

Examples

spiny burrfish
During our dive, we spotted a burrfish hiding among the corals.
The burrfish can inflate its body when threatened by predators.
Unlike porcupinefish, the burrfish's spines are always visible and upright.
