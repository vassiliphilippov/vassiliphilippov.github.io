to think

verb
recall from memory
Pronunciation: / θɪŋk / (AmE), / θɪŋk / (BrE)

Examples

to think of a name

I can't think what her last name was right now.
She tried hard to think of the answer, but nothing came to mind.

  • antonym – forget: to fail to remember; to be unable to recall
