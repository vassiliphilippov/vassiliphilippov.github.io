fennel

noun
feathery herb leaves used for seasoning
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛnəl / (AmE), / ˈfɛnəl / (BrE)
The feathery leaves of the fennel plant, used as a seasoning in cooking.

Examples

chopped fennel leaves
She sprinkled chopped fennel over the roasted vegetables before serving.
The recipe calls for fresh fennel to add a subtle, sweet flavor to the salad.
You can substitute parsley if you don't have any fennel leaves for the fish dish.
