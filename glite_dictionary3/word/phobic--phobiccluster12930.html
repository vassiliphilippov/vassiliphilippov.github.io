phobic

adjective
affected by irrational fear
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Suffering from or relating to an irrational fear of something.

Examples

phobic about spiders
As a child, he was phobic of the dark and refused to sleep alone.
She became phobic about flying after a turbulent flight last year.
Some people are phobic of dogs even if they've never had a bad experience.

  • synonym – phobia: an irrational fear of a particular thing or situation
