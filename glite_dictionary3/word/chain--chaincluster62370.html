chain

noun
group of businesses under one company
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn / (AmE), / tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a group of similar stores, restaurants, hotels, or other businesses owned or managed by the same company

Examples

chain store
Many fast food chains offer similar menus around the world.
She prefers to shop at local stores rather than big chains.
The hotel is part of a luxury chain found in major cities worldwide.
