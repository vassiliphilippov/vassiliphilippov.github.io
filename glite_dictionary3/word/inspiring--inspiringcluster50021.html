inspiring

adjective
stimulating enthusiasm or emotion
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspaɪɹɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈspaɪəɹɪŋ / (BrE)
Having the effect of stimulating enthusiasm, creativity, or a positive emotional response in others.

Examples

an inspiring story
Her dedication to helping others is truly inspiring.
The coach gave an inspiring speech that lifted everyone's spirits before the game.
We visited an inspiring exhibition that made us see the world differently.

Related Terms

  • synonym – motivating: causing someone to want to do something; providing a reason to act
  • antonym – uninspiring: not stimulating or exciting; dull
