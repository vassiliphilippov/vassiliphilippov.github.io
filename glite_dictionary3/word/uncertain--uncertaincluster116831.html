uncertain

adjective
ambiguous; not clearly defined
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɜrtən / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɜːtn / (BrE)
not clearly defined or decided; ambiguous or vague, especially when clarity is lacking

Examples

uncertain answer
He gave an uncertain reply, leaving everyone confused about his intentions.
Her reasons for leaving were deliberately uncertain, causing much speculation.
The outcome of the negotiations remains uncertain due to the lack of agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ambiguous: open to more than one interpretation; not having one clear meaning
  • antonym – clear: easy to perceive, understand, or interpret
