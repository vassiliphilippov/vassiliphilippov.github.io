unfitness

noun
state of poor physical condition
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtnɪs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtnɪs / (BrE)
the state of being in poor physical condition and lacking fitness, especially as a result of inactivity or unhealthy living

Examples

physical unfitness
Decades of inactivity contributed to his overall unfitness.
His prolonged period of unfitness made even a short walk exhausting.
The team lost the match due to the unfitness of several key players.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fitness: the condition of being physically healthy and strong
