aldose

noun
a sugar containing an aldehyde group
Pronunciation: / ˈælˌdoʊs / (AmE), / ˈæl.dəʊs / (BrE)
A monosaccharide sugar that contains an aldehyde group at the end of its carbon chain, or exists in a hemiacetal form.
