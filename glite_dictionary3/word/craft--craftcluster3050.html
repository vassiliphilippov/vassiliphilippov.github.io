craft

noun
vehicle for water, air, or space travel
Pronunciation: / kræft / (AmE), / krɑːft / (BrE)
a vehicle designed for navigation on water, in air, or through outer space, such as a boat, ship, or spacecraft

Examples

space craft
Each craft in the fleet was equipped with advanced communication devices.
Scientists are testing a new craft designed for travel to Mars.
The rescue craft approached the sinking ship quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – aircraft: a vehicle capable of flight, such as an airplane or helicopter
  • synonym – vessel: a ship or large boat, especially one used on water
