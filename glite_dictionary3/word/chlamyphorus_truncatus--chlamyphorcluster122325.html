Chlamyphorus truncatus

noun
species of small Argentine armadillo
Pronunciation: / klæˌmɪfəˌrəs trʌŋˈkeɪtəs / (AmE), / klæˌmɪfəˌrəs trʌŋˈkeɪtəs / (BrE)
A very small species of armadillo native to Argentina, characterized by pale silky hair and pink plates on its head and neck, commonly known as the pink fairy armadillo.
