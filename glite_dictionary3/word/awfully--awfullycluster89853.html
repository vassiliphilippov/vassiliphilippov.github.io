awfully

adverb
to a very great degree; very
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːfli, ˈɑːfli / (AmE), / ˈɔːfli / (BrE)
To a very great or excessive degree; used to add emphasis.

Examples

awfully sorry about
I'm awfully sorry for the mistake.
It was awfully nice of you to help.
She looked awfully tired after her long journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dreadfully: used as an intensifier for emphasis
  • antonym – slightly: to a small degree; the opposite in intensity
  • synonym – terribly: used as an intensifier meaning 'very' or 'extremely'
