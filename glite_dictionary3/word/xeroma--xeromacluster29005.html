xeroma

noun
dryness of the eye's conjunctiva and cornea
Pronunciation: / ziˈroʊmə / (AmE), / zɪəˈrəʊmə / (BrE)
Abnormal dryness of the conjunctiva and cornea of the eye, often caused by vitamin A deficiency.

Examples

xeroma of the eye
Prolonged lack of tear production can result in xeroma.
The patient was diagnosed with xeroma after complaining of persistent eye discomfort.
Vitamin A supplementation helped improve her symptoms of xeroma.
