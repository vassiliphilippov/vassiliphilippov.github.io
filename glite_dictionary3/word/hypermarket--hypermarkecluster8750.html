hypermarket

noun
very large self-service store
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpərˌmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪpəmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
A very large self-service store offering a wide variety of goods, usually located on the outskirts of a town or city.

Examples

shop at a hypermarket
Many people drive to the hypermarket for its wide selection and low prices.
The new hypermarket sells everything from groceries to televisions under one roof.
We did all our shopping at the hypermarket outside town.
