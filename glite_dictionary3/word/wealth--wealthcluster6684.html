wealth

noun
great abundance of something
Pronunciation: / wɛlθ / (AmE), / wɛlθ / (BrE)
A large amount or great abundance of something valuable or desirable.

Examples

wealth of knowledge
She brought a wealth of experience to her new role at the company.
The museum offers a wealth of information about the region's history.
There is a wealth of opportunities for those willing to look.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abundance: a very large quantity of something
  • antonym – scarcity: the state of being in short supply; shortage
