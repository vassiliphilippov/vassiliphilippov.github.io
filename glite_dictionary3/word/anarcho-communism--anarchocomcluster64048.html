anarcho-communism

noun
anarchist form of communism
Pronunciation: / ænˌɑrkoʊˈkɑmjəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ænˌɑːkəʊˈkɒmjʊˌnɪzəm / (BrE)
A political theory that combines anarchism and communism, advocating the abolition of the state, capitalism, wages, and private property, and supporting common ownership, direct democracy, and voluntary associations.
