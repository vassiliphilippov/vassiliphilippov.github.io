frictionless

adjective
without friction; smooth, offering no resistance
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪkʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈfrɪkʃ(ə)nləs / (BrE)
lacking friction; having a surface or characteristic that causes no resistance to movement when in contact with another object

Examples

frictionless surface
A frictionless environment is an ideal scenario that cannot be achieved in reality.
Scientists try to create frictionless surfaces for use in advanced machinery.
The puck slid almost frictionless over the smooth ice.
