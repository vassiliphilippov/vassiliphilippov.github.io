equinox

noun
time of equal day and night
Pronunciation: / ˈikwəˌnɑks /; / ˈiː.kwɪ.nɑks / (AmE), / ˈiːkwɪnɒks /; / ˈiː.kwɪ.nɒks / (BrE)
either of the two times each year when the sun crosses the equator and day and night are about the same length
