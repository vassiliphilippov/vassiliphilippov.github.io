Chagatai

noun
medieval Turkic literary language of central Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːgətaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːgətaɪ / (BrE)
a Turkic literary language used in central Asia during the medieval and early modern periods, named after Chagatai, son of Genghis Khan
