cowgirl

noun
female ranch worker; woman cowboy
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌɡɝl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who works with cattle and performs traditional cowboy skills, such as riding and roping, either on a ranch or in rodeos
