nonfissile

adjective
not capable of nuclear fission
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfɪsaɪl / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɪsaɪl / (BrE)
(of a material) not capable of sustaining a chain reaction by nuclear fission.

Examples

nonfissile nuclear material
Nonfissile materials are often used as diluents in nuclear reactors.
Natural uranium contains a large proportion of nonfissile isotopes.
Thorium-232 is considered a nonfissile material under normal reactor conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – fissile: capable of sustaining a chain reaction by nuclear fission
