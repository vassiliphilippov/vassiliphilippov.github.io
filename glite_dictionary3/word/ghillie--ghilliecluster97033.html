ghillie

noun
soft shoe with decorative lacing
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪli / (BrE)
A light shoe without a tongue, featuring decorative lacing up the instep, commonly worn for Scottish dancing.

Examples

ghillie dance shoes
She bought a new pair of ghillies before joining the Scottish dance troupe.
The unique crisscross lacing on ghillies distinguishes them from other types of dance shoes.
Traditional Highland dance requires dancers to wear ghillies that are tightly laced around the feet.
