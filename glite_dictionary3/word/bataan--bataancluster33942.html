Bataan

proper noun
World War II battle site in the Philippines
Pronunciation: / bəˈtæn, bəˈtɑn / (AmE), / bəˈtæn, bəˈtɑːn / (BrE)
a peninsula and island in the Philippines, famous as the site of a major battle and siege during World War II, where United States forces surrendered to Japan in 1942 before the area was recaptured in 1945.

Examples

Bataan Peninsula battle
After its liberation in 1945, Bataan became a symbol of heroism and sacrifice.
Many Americans remember Bataan as the site of a tragic World War II defeat.
The soldiers endured unimaginable hardship during the siege of Bataan.
