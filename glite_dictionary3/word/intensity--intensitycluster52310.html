intensity

noun
degree of strength or force
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛnsəti / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛnsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being intense; the degree or strength of something such as feeling, activity, or light

Examples

emotional intensity

The intensity of the storm surprised even veteran meteorologists.
This workout increases the intensity of your exercise gradually over time.
