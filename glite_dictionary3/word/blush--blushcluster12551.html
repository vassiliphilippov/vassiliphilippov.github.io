blush

noun
cosmetic for coloring cheeks
Pronunciation: / blʌʃ / (AmE), / blʌʃ / (BrE)
A pink or red powder or cream applied to the cheeks to give a healthy or rosy appearance.

Examples

blush powder makeup
For a natural look, try using cream blush instead of powder.
She applied a touch of blush to her cheeks before leaving the house.
The soft pink blush complemented her skin tone perfectly.
