bactericidal

adjective
capable of killing bacteria
Pronunciation: / bækˌtɪrɪˈsaɪdəl / (AmE), / bækˌtɪərɪˈsaɪdəl / (BrE)
(of a substance) able to kill bacteria or inhibit their growth.

Examples

bactericidal spray
Regular cleaning with bactericidal sprays reduces the risk of contamination in hospitals.
Some antibiotics are bactericidal, while others only inhibit bacterial growth.
The doctor prescribed a bactericidal ointment to prevent infection after the surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym – bacteriostatic: inhibiting the growth of bacteria without destroying them
