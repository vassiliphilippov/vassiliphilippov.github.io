chutzpah

noun
shameless audacity or boldness
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊtspɑ / (AmE), / ˈxʊtspɑː / (BrE)
shameless audacity; impudence or boldness that is often admired or criticized

Examples

incredible chutzpah
Her chutzpah both impressed and shocked those around her.
It takes real chutzpah to start a business with no experience at all.
Nobody else had the chutzpah to question the director’s decision so openly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – audacity: extreme boldness or daring, often with an arrogant disregard for safety or conventions
  • synonym – nerve: boldness in behavior or speech, especially in difficult situations
  • antonym – timidity: lack of courage or confidence
