Nimitz

proper noun
U.S. admiral, WWII Pacific fleet commander
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪmɪts / (AmE), / ˈnɪmɪts / (BrE)
(1885–1966) United States admiral who commanded the Pacific fleet during World War II and played a key role in the defeat of the Japanese navy.
