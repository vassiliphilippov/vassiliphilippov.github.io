unavailing

adjective
ineffective; futile; producing no effect
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈveɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈveɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
not producing the desired result; ineffective or futile.

Examples

unavailing efforts
All her efforts to change his mind proved unavailing.
His protests were unavailing, and the decision remained unchanged.
They made an unavailing attempt to save the old building from demolition.

Related Terms

  • antonym – effective: successful in producing a desired or intended result
  • synonym – futile: incapable of producing any useful result; pointless
  • synonym – ineffectual: not producing the intended effect
