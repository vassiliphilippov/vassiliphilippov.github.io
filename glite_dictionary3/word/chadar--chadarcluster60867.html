chadar

noun
cloth used as women's head covering or shawl
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːdər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːdə / (BrE)
a large piece of cloth worn by Muslim and Hindu women as a head covering, veil, or shawl, typically draped over the body for modesty

Examples

She draped her chadar over her head before leaving the house.
The colorful chadar matched her traditional attire perfectly.
Visitors to the shrine are requested to wear a chadar as a sign of respect.
