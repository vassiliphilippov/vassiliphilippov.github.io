abuser

noun
person who treats others cruelly or harmfully
Pronunciation: / əˈbjuːzər / (AmE), / əˈbjuːzə / (BrE)
A person who treats another person or a thing in a cruel, violent, or harmful way, especially regularly or habitually.

Examples

child abuser
Many children struggle to trust adults after living with an abuser.
The court ordered the abuser to attend mandatory counseling sessions.
The organization provides support for survivors of abusers.
