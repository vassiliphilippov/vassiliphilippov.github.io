to exhaust

verb
make extremely tired
Pronunciation: / ɪgˈzɔst / (AmE), / ɪgˈzɔːst / (BrE)
to make someone very tired; to use up someone's physical or mental energy completely

Examples

to exhaust yourself
If you work too hard without breaks, you will exhaust yourself.
Taking care of three kids can exhaust even the most energetic parent.
The long hike through the mountains really exhausted me.

Related Terms

  • antonym – invigorate: to give strength or energy to someone
  • synonym – tire: to reduce someone's strength or energy
