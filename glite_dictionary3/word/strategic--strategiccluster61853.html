strategic

adjective
relating to strategy or planning
Pronunciation: / strəˈtidʒɪk /; / strəˈtiːdʒɪk / (AmE), / strəˈtiːdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or important for planning and achieving long-term goals or success, especially as part of a plan or strategy

Examples

strategic decision making
She took a strategic approach to solving the complex problem.
The company made several strategic acquisitions to expand its market share.
The location of the new factory is strategic for reaching international customers.
During the game, her strategic moves gave her a clear advantage.
The army carried out a strategic withdrawal to regroup its forces.
The company made a strategic decision to enter the Asian market.
