graffito

noun
an inscription or drawing on walls; singular of graffiti
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈfitoʊ / (AmE), / ɡrəˈfiːtəʊ / (BrE)
a drawing or inscription scratched or written on a wall or other surface, often as a form of expression or decoration, especially regarded as a singular instance of graffiti

Examples

graffito on a wall
A single graffito was discovered during the excavation of the site.
One graffito here depicts a ship, carved centuries ago by a passing traveler.
The ancient graffito on the temple wall provides clues about daily life in Rome.
