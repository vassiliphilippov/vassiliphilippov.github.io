pinwheel

noun
wheel with pins at right angles around rim
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌwiːl / (AmE), / ˈpɪn.wiːl / (BrE)
a wheel with many pins set at right angles around its rim, often used in games or as a mechanical component

Examples

game pinwheel spins
He spun the pinwheel quickly, watching the pins blur together.
The game used a pinwheel to randomly select tasks for players.
When the pinwheel stopped spinning, the indicator pointed to the winning number.
