bath

noun
act of washing the body in water
Pronunciation: / bæθ / (AmE), / bɑːθ / (BrE)
an act of washing the body by immersing oneself in water, typically in a bathtub

Examples

take a bath
I like to take a relaxing bath after a long day at work.
She drew a hot bath and added some lavender oil to the water.
The children splashed water everywhere during their evening bath.

Related Terms

  • antonym – shower: an act of washing oneself with a spray of water
