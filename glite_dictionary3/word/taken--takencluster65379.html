taken

adjective
suddenly affected (illness, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkən / (AmE), / ˈteɪkən / (BrE)
(of a person) suddenly affected by a specified condition, especially illness; used in phrases such as 'be taken ill' or 'be taken drunk'.

Examples

be taken ill
He is sometimes taken drunk after just one or two drinks.
She was suddenly taken ill at the party and had to go home early.
You may be taken ill without any warning, so always be prepared.

Related Terms

  • synonym – stricken: suddenly affected by something, especially an unpleasant condition
