fulfillment

noun
processing and shipping orders
Pronunciation: / fəlˈfɪlmənt / (AmE), / fəlˈfɪlmənt / (BrE)
The process in commerce of receiving, processing, packing, and shipping customer orders (order fulfillment).

Examples

fulfillment center
The retailer outsourced its fulfillment to a third-party provider to speed delivery.
Our startup built a local fulfillment hub to process online orders faster and cut costs.
During the holiday rush, warehouse staff struggled to keep up with fulfillment for thousands of orders.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fulfillment center: a warehouse where orders are received, packed, and shipped
  • synonym – logistics: the planning and control of movement and storage of goods
  • synonym – order processing: handling and preparing customer orders for shipment
