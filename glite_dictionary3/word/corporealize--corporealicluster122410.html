to corporealize

verb
make abstract things physical
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈpɔriəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / kɔːˈpɔːrɪəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something abstract, such as a feeling or idea, tangible or physical; to give physical form to something nonphysical

Examples

to corporealize an idea
Philosophers debate whether it is possible to corporealize abstract concepts like love or justice.
The artist hoped to corporealize her emotions through sculpture.
The dance performance aimed to corporealize the theme of transformation on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym – embody: to give a tangible or visible form to an idea or feeling
  • synonym – materialize: to cause something to appear in physical form
