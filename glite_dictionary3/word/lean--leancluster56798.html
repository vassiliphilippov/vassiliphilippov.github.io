lean

noun
slang narcotic drink
Pronunciation: / liːn / (AmE), / liːn / (BrE)
(slang) a recreational, narcotic drink made by mixing codeine-containing cough syrup (often with promethazine) with a soft drink and sometimes candy.

Examples

drink lean (slang)
The rapper admitted he enjoyed lean during late-night studio sessions.
Doctors warned that drinking lean can cause dangerous breathing problems and other serious health risks.
Police found empty bottles of cough syrup used to make lean at the suspect's apartment last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym – purple drank: a recreational beverage made from cough syrup mixed with soda and sometimes candy; the same phenomenon referred to as 'lean'.
  • synonym – sizzurp: informal name for a drink made from codeine cough syrup mixed with soft drinks; used synonymously with 'lean'.
