bookend

noun
support for books on a shelf
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌɛnd / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌɛnd / (BrE)
an object placed at the end of a row of books to keep them upright on a shelf or table

Examples

bookend on shelf
She placed a heavy bookend at the end of the shelf to keep the novels upright.
The silver bookend was a gift from his grandmother.
Without a bookend, the books kept falling over.
