to brail

verb
haul fish aboard with nets
Pronunciation: / breɪl / (AmE), / breɪl / (BrE)
to haul fish aboard (a boat) using brails (nets or ropes)

Examples

to brail fish aboard
The fishermen skillfully brailed the catch onto the deck using large nets.
They needed extra help to brail the heavy load of tuna aboard the ship.
We watched as the crew brailed hundreds of sardines at sunrise.
