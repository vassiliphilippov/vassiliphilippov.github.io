inactive

adjective
not involved in military operations
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈæktɪv / (BrE)
(of a military unit or personnel) not involved in active military operations or not currently serving.

Examples

inactive soldier
Many soldiers remain inactive during times of peace.
