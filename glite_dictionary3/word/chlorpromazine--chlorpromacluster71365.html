chlorpromazine

noun
antipsychotic and sedative drug
Pronunciation: / klɔrˈproʊməˌziːn / (AmE), / klɔːˈprəʊməziːn / (BrE)
a drug used as an antipsychotic, sedative, and tranquilizer, especially in the treatment of schizophrenia and other mental disorders

Examples
