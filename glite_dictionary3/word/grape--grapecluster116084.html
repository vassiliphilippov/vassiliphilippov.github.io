grape

noun
a small edible berry from a vine
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪp / (AmE), / ɡreɪp / (BrE)
a small, round, smooth-skinned fruit (usually green or purple) that grows in clusters on vines and is eaten as food or used to make wine
