tired

adjective
feeling a need to rest; lacking energy
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
feeling a need to rest or sleep because of a lack of strength or energy

Examples

feel tired after work
After a long day at work, she felt completely tired and ready for bed.
I am too tired to go out tonight, so I'll stay in and rest.
The tired children fell asleep in the car on the way home.
