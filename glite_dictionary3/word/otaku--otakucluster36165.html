otaku

noun
fan of anime/manga or Japanese pop culture
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtɑːkuː / (AmE), / əʊˈtɑːkuː / (BrE)
A person who is very enthusiastic about anime and manga, often with deep knowledge or intense interest in Japanese pop culture.

Examples

anime otaku
At the comic convention, many otaku cosplayed as their favorite characters.
His room is filled with manga books and collectibles because he is an otaku.
She considers herself an otaku and spends hours watching new anime series.
