Cinderella

noun
overlooked woman who attains sudden success
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪndəˈrɛlə / (AmE), / ˌsɪndəˈrɛlə / (BrE)
A woman whose talents or worth have been overlooked but who achieves sudden success and recognition.

Examples

the Cinderella of sports
After years of hard work, she became the Cinderella of the tech world, finally gaining the recognition she deserved.
He referred to the artist as a modern Cinderella whose talent was ignored until her sudden breakthrough.
The small research team turned out to be the Cinderella story of the industry, winning the prestigious award.
