sir

noun
formal address for a man
Pronunciation: / sɝː / (AmE), / sɜː / (BrE)
A respectful or formal term of address for a man, especially one whose name is unknown.

Examples

excuse me, sir
Sir, could you please help me with this form?
Excuse me, sir, you dropped your wallet.
May I take your order, sir?
