implausible

adjective
not believable or reasonable
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplɔzəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɔːzəbl / (BrE)
not seeming reasonable or likely; difficult to believe.

Examples

implausible excuse
Her explanation for being late was completely implausible to everyone in the meeting.
It is implausible that he finished the entire project in just one night.
The movie's plot was so implausible that the audience couldn't take it seriously.

Related Terms

  • antonym – plausible: seeming reasonable or probable
  • synonym – unbelievable: not able to be believed; unlikely to be true
