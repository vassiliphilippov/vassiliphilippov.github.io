rawness

noun
lack of experience or skill
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔːnəs / (AmE), / ˈrɔːnəs / (BrE)
the state of lacking experience, knowledge, or skill; inexperience.

Examples

rawness in a beginner
Due to her rawness, she made several predictable mistakes in her first job.
The rawness of the new recruits was evident on their first day.
The team's rawness showed in their confused response to the sudden problem.

  • synonym – inexperience: lack of knowledge or skill from practice
