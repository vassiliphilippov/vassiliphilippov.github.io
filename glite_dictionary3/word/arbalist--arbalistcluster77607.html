arbalist

noun
type of crossbow with a steel bow
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈɑːbəlɪst / (BrE)
A type of crossbow, especially a heavy one with a steel bow, used in the late Middle Ages.

Examples

heavy arbalist weapon
He demonstrated how the arbalist could launch bolts with remarkable force.
The museum's medieval collection includes a rare arbalist from the fifteenth century.
Unlike a simple bow, the arbalist required a winch to draw the steel bow.
