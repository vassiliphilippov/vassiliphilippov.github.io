bagel

noun
ring-shaped bread roll
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪɡl̩ / (BrE)
a dense, ring-shaped bread roll made from yeast dough, typically boiled and then baked, with a chewy interior and a crisp, shiny crust

Examples
