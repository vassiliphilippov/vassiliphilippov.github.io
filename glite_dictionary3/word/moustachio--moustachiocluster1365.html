moustachio

noun
large bushy moustache
Pronunciation: / muˈstæʃioʊ / (AmE), / mʊˈstæʃiəʊ / (BrE)
A large, bushy moustache, sometimes with hair growing down the sides of the mouth.

Examples

bushy moustachio
He takes great pride in grooming his bushy moustachio every morning.
His impressive moustachio curled dramatically at the ends, making him stand out in a crowd.
The villain twirled his thick moustachio while plotting his next move.
