transplanter

noun
machine for transplanting seedlings
Pronunciation: / trænsˈplæn.tɚ / (AmE), / trænsˈplæn.tə / (BrE)
a machine (often used in agriculture) that automatically or semi-automatically moves and plants seedlings or young plants into soil or growing beds.

Examples

transplanter plants seedlings
The farm installed a new transplanter to speed up rice planting across the flooded paddies.
Modern transplanters can plant thousands of seedlings per hour with minimal human supervision.
Engineers tested the prototype transplanter in varied soil types before the harvest season.

Related Terms

  • synonym – planter: a machine or device used to place seeds or seedlings into the soil; related agricultural equipment.
  • synonym – rice transplanter: a specialized transplanter used to set rice seedlings in paddies, commonly used in mechanized rice farming.
