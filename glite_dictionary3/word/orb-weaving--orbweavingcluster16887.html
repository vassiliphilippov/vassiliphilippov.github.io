orb-weaving

adjective
weaving circular spider webs
Pronunciation: / ɔrb ˈwiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔːb ˈwiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a spider) weaving circular, wheel-shaped webs, typical of the family Araneidae.

Examples

orb-weaving spider webs
Among arachnids, orb-weaving species are known for their beautifully symmetrical webs.
Researchers studied the behavior of several orb-weaving spiders in different environments.
The orb-weaving spider spun an intricate web in the garden overnight.

  • synonym – web-spinning: producing webs (as a spider does); especially spinning webs of silk
