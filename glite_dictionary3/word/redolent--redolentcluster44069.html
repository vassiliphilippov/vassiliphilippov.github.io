redolent

adjective
suggestive or reminiscent of something
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdələnt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdələnt / (BrE)
strongly suggestive or reminiscent of something; serving to bring something to mind

Examples

redolent of memories
Her speech was redolent of hope and resilience.
That film is redolent of classic Hollywood glamour.
The old house was redolent of childhood memories.

Related Terms

  • synonym – evocative: bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind
  • antonym – forgetful: not likely to recall something; failing to bring something to mind
  • synonym – reminiscent: tending to remind one of something
