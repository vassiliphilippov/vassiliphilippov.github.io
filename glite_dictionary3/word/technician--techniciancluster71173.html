technician

noun
person skilled in a technique
Pronunciation: / tɛkˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / tɛkˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled in a particular intellectual or artistic technique

Examples

Although he is a great artist, critics say he is more of a technician than a visionary.

The laboratory hired a technician known for his skill in delicate procedures.
