to jig

verb
to dance a lively, leaping dance
Pronunciation: / dʒɪɡ / (AmE), / dʒɪɡ / (BrE)
to dance a lively dance with quick, leaping and kicking motions, especially to traditional music

Examples

to jig around
After hearing the lively music, she couldn't help but jig around the room.
He was jigging to the cheerful rhythm of the fiddle when I arrived.
