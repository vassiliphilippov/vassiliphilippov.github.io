solo

noun
composition or passage for one performer
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a musical composition or passage designed to be performed by a single voice or instrument, sometimes with accompaniment

Examples

solo performance
Each member of the band played a short solo during the concert.
She composed a beautiful violin solo for her recital.
The pianist's breathtaking solo brought the entire audience to their feet.
