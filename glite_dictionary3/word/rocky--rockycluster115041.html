rocky

adjective
marked by jolts or uneven movement
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːki / (AmE), / ˈrɒki / (BrE)
causing or marked by frequent jolts or irregular movements; unsteady or uneven

Examples

rocky boat ride
The boat ride became rocky as soon as the waves picked up.
Their journey through the mountains was rocky, with constant jolts along the narrow trails.
We took a rocky drive down the old rural road in his truck.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bumpy: marked by frequent bumps or jolts
  • antonym – smooth: not having abrupt variations in movement or surface
