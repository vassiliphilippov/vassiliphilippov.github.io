Valium

noun
trade name for a tranquilizer (diazepam)
Pronunciation: / ˈvæliəm / (AmE), / ˈval.i.əm / (BrE)
The trade name for a drug (diazepam) used as a tranquilizer to relieve anxiety, relax muscles, and treat certain seizure disorders.
