Chelydridae

noun
family of freshwater snapping turtles
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪdrəˌdi / (AmE), / kɛˈlɪdrɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of large freshwater turtles that includes the genera Chelydra and Macrochelys, commonly known as snapping turtles.
