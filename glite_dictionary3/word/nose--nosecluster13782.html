to nose

verb
defeat narrowly (verb)
Pronunciation: / noʊz / (AmE), / nəʊz / (BrE)
to defeat or edge out someone or something by a very small margin, especially in a race or competition.

Examples

to nose ahead
She just nosed past her opponent in the spelling bee to take first place.
The athlete nosed his rival at the finish line by just a fraction of a second.
Their team nosed ahead in the final minutes to win the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym – edge: to narrowly defeat someone in a contest
  • degree higher – trounce: to defeat decisively or by a large margin
