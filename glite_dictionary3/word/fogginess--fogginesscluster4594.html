fogginess

noun
unclear or indistinct quality
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːɡinəs / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡinəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being unclear, indistinct, or lacking sharp outlines

Examples

mental fogginess
A sense of mental fogginess made concentrating on the lecture difficult.
She peered through the fogginess that had settled over the city streets.
The fogginess of the photograph made it hard to recognize anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blurriness: the quality of being blurred or indistinct
  • antonym – clarity: the quality of being clear and easily understood or seen
