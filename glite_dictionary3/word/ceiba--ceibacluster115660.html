Ceiba

noun
genus of tropical American trees
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪbə / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical American trees with palmately compound leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers, including the kapok tree

Examples

giant Ceiba tree
A mature Ceiba can grow to a considerable height, dominating the rainforest canopy.
Many species in the Ceiba genus produce seed pods filled with silky fibers.
The Ceiba tree is considered sacred in many indigenous cultures of Central America.
