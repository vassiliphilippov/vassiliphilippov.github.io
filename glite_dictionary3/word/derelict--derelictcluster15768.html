derelict

noun
homeless and jobless person
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrəˌlɪkt / (AmE), / ˈdɛrɪˌlɪkt / (BrE)
a person without a home, job, or property, often neglected by society

Examples

homeless derelict man
He offered a warm meal to a derelict he met near the station.
Society too often ignores the plight of the modern derelict.
The city created new shelters to support every derelict living on the streets.
