profaned

adjective
treated without respect; made unholy
Pronunciation: / prəˈfeɪnd / (AmE), / prəˈfeɪnd / (BrE)
treated with a lack of respect or as not sacred, especially something religious or holy

Examples

profaned holy place
Many considered it offensive when the monument was profaned by graffiti.
The ancient temple was profaned by vandals who carved their names into the stones.
The sacred traditions were profaned by careless behavior during the ceremony.
