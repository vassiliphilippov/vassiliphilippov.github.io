beady

adjective
small, shiny, and round (esp. eyes)
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdi / (AmE), / ˈbiːdi / (BrE)
(of eyes or objects) small, round, and shining, especially suggesting watchfulness or intensity.

Examples

beady eyes
Her beady little eyes darted around the room, missing nothing.
She noticed his beady eyes watching her from the shadows.
The crow cocked its head, its beady gaze unblinking and intense.
