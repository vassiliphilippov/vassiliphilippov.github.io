outboard

noun
motorboat with an outboard motor
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtbɔːd / (BrE)
an outboard motor (a motor attached to the outside of a boat at the back), or a small boat with such a motor
