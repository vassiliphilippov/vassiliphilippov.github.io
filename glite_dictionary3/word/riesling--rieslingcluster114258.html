Riesling

noun
type of white wine; Rhine grape variety
Pronunciation: / ˈriːzlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈriːzlɪŋ / (BrE)
A fragrant dry or sweet white wine made from a variety of grape grown especially in the Rhine valley and similar regions.
