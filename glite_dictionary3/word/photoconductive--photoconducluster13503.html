photoconductive

adjective
responding to light with increased conductivity
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊ.t̬oʊ.kənˈdʌk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌfəʊ.təʊ.kənˈdʌk.tɪv / (BrE)
Relating to or exhibiting increased electrical conductivity as a result of exposure to light.
