flare

noun
reddened area of skin spreading from a focus
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
a sudden recurrence or worsening of symptoms or a reddening of the skin due to irritation or disease

Stress can trigger a flare in people with chronic skin conditions.
