to coincide

verb
to be the same or to agree
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd / (AmE), / ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to be the same in position, nature, or time; to correspond exactly or agree

Examples

coincide with an opinion
My opinion coincides completely with yours on this issue.
The ceremony coincided with her birthday, making the occasion extra special.
Their holiday plans coincide with ours this year, so we'll all be away at the same time.

Related Terms

  • synonym – agree: to have the same opinion or to be consistent
  • antonym – differ: to be unlike or not the same as
