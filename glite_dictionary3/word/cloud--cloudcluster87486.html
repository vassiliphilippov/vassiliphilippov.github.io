cloud

noun
internet-based computing services
Pronunciation: / klaʊd / (AmE), / klaʊd / (BrE)
(computing) a network of remote servers and services accessed over the Internet that provide storage, processing, and other computing resources on demand.

Examples

store data in the cloud
Many startups store data in the cloud to scale resources on demand.
Please save your photos to the cloud so you can access them from any device.
The company moved backups to the cloud after a trusted provider offered better redundancy.

Related Terms

  • synonym – cloud computing: the delivery of computing services (servers, storage, databases, networking, software) over the Internet.
  • synonym – data center: a facility that houses computer systems and associated components used to provide computing resources.
  • antonym – local storage: data storage kept on a user's own device rather than on remote servers.
