editing

noun
preparation and revision of material
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of preparing written, visual, or audio material for publication or presentation by correcting, arranging, or revising it to an acceptable form

Examples

editing a manuscript
She spent several hours editing her novel before submitting it to the publisher.
The documentary won an award for outstanding editing.
Video editing requires both technical skill and creativity.

Related Terms

  • synonym – proofreading: the act of checking for errors in text before publication
  • synonym – revision: the act of revising or preparing a manuscript for publication
