aggro

noun
enemy attention in games
Pronunciation: / ˈæɡroʊ / (AmE), / ˈæɡrəʊ / (BrE)
(gaming) hostile attention or threat from one or more enemies; the state of being the target of enemy attacks or AI focus.

Examples

aggro from enemies
In raids, players often manage aggro to control enemy focus and protect teammates.
The healer lost aggro for a moment and the boss hit them hard before recovering.
Tanks use threat abilities to build aggro quickly at the start of an encounter.
