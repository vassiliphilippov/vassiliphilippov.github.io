uncousinly

adjective
not befitting a cousin
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌzɪnli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌzɪnli / (BrE)
Not showing qualities or behavior expected of a cousin; not befitting a cousin.

Examples

uncousinly behavior
Despite their argument, he regretted his uncousinly remarks and apologized the next day.
His uncousinly attitude made family gatherings uncomfortable for everyone.
She thought it was rather uncousinly of him to ignore her at the party.
