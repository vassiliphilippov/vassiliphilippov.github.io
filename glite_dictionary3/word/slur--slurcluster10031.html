to slur

verb
to speak indistinctly
Pronunciation: / slɜr / (AmE), / slɜː / (BrE)
to speak words indistinctly so that they run together and are difficult to understand

Examples

to slur your words
After the third glass of wine, he began to slur his words noticeably.
The actor deliberately slurred his lines to portray drunkenness on stage.
Tired and barely awake, she would sometimes slur her speech in late-night calls.

Related Terms

  • antonym – enunciate: to pronounce words clearly and distinctly
  • synonym – mumble: to speak quietly and not clearly enough for the words to be understood
