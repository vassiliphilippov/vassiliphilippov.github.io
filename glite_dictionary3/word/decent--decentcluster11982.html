decent

adjective
socially/morally acceptable; respectable
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːsənt / (AmE), / ˈdiːsənt / (BrE)
Socially or morally acceptable; conforming to recognized standards of good behavior or propriety.

Examples

decent person
Despite his mistakes, people still consider him a decent man at heart.
It's important to treat others in a decent and respectful manner.
She comes from a decent family and was raised with strong values.

Related Terms

  • antonym – improper: not conforming to accepted standards of behavior
  • synonym – respectable: regarded by society to be good, proper, or correct
