unlabelled

adjective
not having a label or tag
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈleɪbəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪbəld / (BrE)
not having a label or tag attached; without identification by means of a label.

Examples

unlabelled package
Airport staff may discard unlabelled luggage for security reasons.
All the unlabelled boxes were set aside for inspection.
The chemicals in the cabinet were unlabelled, making them potentially dangerous.
