influentially

adverb
in an influential manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌɪnflʊˈɛnʃəli / (BrE)
In a way that has great influence or power to affect others' actions, opinions, or events.

Examples

act influentially in politics
Many figures acted influentially in shaping the country's modern policies.
She wrote influentially about social change and justice.
The leader spoke influentially at the conference, inspiring many to take action.

Related Terms

  • antonym – ineffectively: in a way that lacks influence or effect
  • synonym – powerfully: in a powerful manner
