objectively

adverb
in an unbiased or impartial way
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪvli / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is not influenced by personal feelings or opinions; with objectivity.

Examples

judge objectively
It is important to assess each situation objectively rather than relying on emotions.
She tried to look at her performance objectively to find ways to improve.
The report analyzes the data objectively, avoiding any personal bias.
