infrequency

noun
rarity; seldom occurrence
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfriːkwənsi / (AmE), / ɪnˈfriːkwənsi / (BrE)
The state or quality of being rare or seldom occurring.

Examples

infrequency of events
Because of the infrequency of buses at night, we decided to take a taxi home.
The infrequency of earthquakes in this region makes it relatively safe for building tall structures.
The infrequency of rain during the summer can lead to drought conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym – frequency: the rate at which something occurs
  • synonym – rarity: the state of being rare
  • synonym – scarcity: the state of being scarce or in short supply
