Sahaptino

noun
member of an Indigenous people of the Pacific Northwest
Pronunciation: / səˈhæptɪnoʊ / (AmE), / səˈhæptɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a North American Indigenous people who lived in Oregon along the Columbia River and its tributaries in Washington and northern Idaho.

Examples

Sahaptino people of Oregon
A group of Sahaptino hunters followed the salmon migration along the river.
Many artifacts found in the region have been traced back to the Sahaptino people.
The traditions of the Sahaptino are still celebrated by their descendants today.
