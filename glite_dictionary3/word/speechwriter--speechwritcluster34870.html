speechwriter

noun
person who writes speeches for others
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːtʃˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈspiːtʃˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to write speeches for others, especially politicians or public figures, to deliver

Examples

speechwriter for a president
As a speechwriter, he must capture the speaker's personality and message perfectly.
She worked as a speechwriter for the president during his term in office.
The company's CEO hired a professional speechwriter to help craft her keynote address.
