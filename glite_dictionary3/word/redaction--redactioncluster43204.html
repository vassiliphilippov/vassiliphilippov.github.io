redaction

noun
editing or preparing text for publication
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdækʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈdækʃ(ə)n /; / rɪˈdækʃən / (BrE)
the process of editing or preparing text for publication or official use, often by arranging, revising, or removing content
