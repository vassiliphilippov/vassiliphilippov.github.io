to touch

verb
be equal to in quality or ability
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ / (AmE), / tʌtʃ / (BrE)
to be equal to (someone or something) in quality, achievement, or ability; to match

to touch her skill

His new novel doesn't touch the success of his earlier works.
No other student in the class can touch her in mathematics.
