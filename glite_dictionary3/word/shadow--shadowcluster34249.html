shadow

noun
slight indication or trace
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊ / (BrE)

Examples

His apology lacked even the shadow of sincerity.
Not a shadow of evidence was found at the scene of the crime.
There wasn't even a shadow of doubt in her mind about the decision.
