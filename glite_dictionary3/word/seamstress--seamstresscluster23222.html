seamstress

noun
a woman who sews dresses professionally
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːm.strəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːm.strɪs / (BrE)
a woman whose occupation is making or mending dresses or other clothes by sewing

Examples

seamstress sewing dresses
Her grandmother worked as a seamstress in a busy garment factory.
Many young women learned to be a seamstress as a means to support their families.
The seamstress skillfully repaired the torn hem on my favorite dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dressmaker: a person who makes custom dresses, usually for women
  • synonym – tailor: a person who makes or alters clothes, especially suits and men's clothing
