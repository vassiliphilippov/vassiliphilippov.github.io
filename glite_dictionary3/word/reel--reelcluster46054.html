reel

noun
short social-media video
Pronunciation: / ril / (AmE), / riːl / (BrE)
A short, usually vertical video clip created for social media platforms for sharing and viewing, typically under a minute long.

Examples

Instagram reel video
She posted a funny reel on Instagram that quickly gained thousands of views.
Brands now use short reels to show products and reach younger audiences on social platforms.
