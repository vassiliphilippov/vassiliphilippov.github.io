tenaciously

adverb
with stubborn determination
Pronunciation: / təˈneɪʃəsli / (AmE), / tɪˈneɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a determined and persistent manner; with unyielding firmness or perseverance.

Examples

to fight tenaciously
Despite repeated failures, she worked tenaciously toward her goal.
He defended his beliefs tenaciously in every discussion.
The weed clung tenaciously to the cracks in the pavement.
