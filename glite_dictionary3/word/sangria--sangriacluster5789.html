sangria

noun
red wine punch with fruit and juice
Pronunciation: / sæŋˈɡriːə / (AmE), / sæŋˈɡriːə / (BrE)
A cold drink made from red wine mixed with fruit juice, pieces of fruit, and sometimes soda water.

Examples

glass of sangria
Many Spanish restaurants offer different varieties of sangria on their menu.
On a hot summer day, a pitcher of sangria is the perfect refreshment.
She added oranges and lemons to the sangria before serving it to her guests.
