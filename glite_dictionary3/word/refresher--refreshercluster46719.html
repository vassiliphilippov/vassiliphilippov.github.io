refresher

noun
course to update knowledge or skills
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɛʃər / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃə / (BrE)
a course that reviews and updates knowledge or skills for people who have not kept up with recent developments

Examples

a refresher course
I took a refresher on CPR because I hadn't practiced it in years.
Teachers are encouraged to attend a refresher every few years to stay current.
The company offered a refresher to help employees learn the latest safety protocols.

Related Terms

  • synonym – refresher course: a course to review and update existing knowledge in a subject
