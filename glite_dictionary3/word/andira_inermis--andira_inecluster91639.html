Andira inermis

noun
tropical tree with toxic bark and medicinal uses
Pronunciation: / ænˈdaɪrə ɪˈnɜːrmɪs / (AmE), / ænˈdaɪərə ɪˈnɜːmɪs / (BrE)
A tropical tree with shaggy, unpleasant-smelling, toxic bark, known for its strong durable wood, whose bark and seeds are used as a purgative, vermifuge, and narcotic.
