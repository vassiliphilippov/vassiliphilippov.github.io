austral

noun
former currency unit of Argentina
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊstrəl / (AmE), / ˈaʊstrəl / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Argentina, equal to 100 centavos, used from 1985 to 1991

Examples

Argentine austral currency
