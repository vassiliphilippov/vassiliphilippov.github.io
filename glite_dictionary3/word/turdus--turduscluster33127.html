Turdus

proper noun
type genus of thrushes (Turdidae)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːdəs / (AmE), / ˈtɜːdəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Turdidae, comprising thrushes such as the song thrush and the blackbird.

Turdus genus thrushes
Many European thrushes belong to the Turdus genus.
