cystocele

noun
hernia of the bladder into the vagina
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəˌsil / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəsiːl / (BrE)
A hernia in which the urinary bladder protrudes through the wall of the vagina, sometimes occurring after childbirth.

Examples

cystocele after childbirth
A cystocele can cause discomfort and urinary problems if left untreated.
Surgical repair is one treatment option for a severe cystocele.
Women who have had multiple pregnancies may be at increased risk of developing a cystocele.
