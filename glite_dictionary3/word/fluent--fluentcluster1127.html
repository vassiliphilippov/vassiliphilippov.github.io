fluent

adjective
able to express oneself easily and clearly
Pronunciation: / ˈfluənt / (AmE), / ˈfluːənt / (BrE)
able to express oneself easily and clearly, especially in speaking or writing a language.

Examples

fluent performance
After years of practice, Julia gave a fluent performance on the piano.
His fluent presentation impressed everyone at the conference.
She is fluent in both Spanish and French, making her an excellent translator.

Related Terms

  • synonym – articulate: able to express ideas clearly and effectively in speech or writing
  • antonym – halting: showing hesitation or lack of fluency in speech
