to ramp

verb
furnish or equip with a ramp
Pronunciation: / ræmp / (AmE), / ræmp / (BrE)
to equip or furnish (a place or structure) with a ramp, especially for accessibility or movement between levels

Examples

to ramp a building
During renovations, they ramped the old theater to comply with safety regulations.
The government plans to ramp public buildings to ensure full accessibility for everyone.
The school recently ramped all its entrances to improve wheelchair access.
