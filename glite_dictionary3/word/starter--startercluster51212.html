starter

noun
fermentation culture for bread, cheese, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrtər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a culture of yeast or bacteria used to begin the fermentation or souring process in making bread, cheese, or butter

Examples

bread starter
Before baking the bread, you need to feed the starter so it's active.
She keeps a jar of sourdough starter in her refrigerator for weekly baking.
Traditional cheese-making often begins with a carefully cultivated starter culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym – leaven: a substance (usually yeast) that promotes fermentation in dough or liquid
  • synonym – sourdough: a type of bread made with a fermented starter
