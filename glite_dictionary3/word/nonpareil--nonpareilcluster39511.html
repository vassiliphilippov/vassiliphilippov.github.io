nonpareil

noun
decorative sugar bead for confections
Pronunciation: / nɑn.pəˈrɛl / (AmE), / nɒn.pəˈrɛl / (BrE)
A small, round, colored sugar bead used as a decoration on candies, cookies, and other sweets.

Examples

colorful nonpareil sprinkles
Children love candies covered in chocolate and rolled in nonpareils.
She sprinkled nonpareils on top of the cupcakes for a festive look.
The baker decorated the cookies with colorful nonpareils before baking them.
