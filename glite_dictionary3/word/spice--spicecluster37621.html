to spice

verb
make more interesting or exciting
Pronunciation: / spaɪs / (AmE), / spaɪs / (BrE)
To make something more interesting, exciting, or lively; to enliven.

Examples

to spice a story
The director wanted to spice the play with more humorous scenes.
They decided to spice their routine by trying a new restaurant every week.
You can spice up your outfit with some colorful accessories.
