interview

noun
formal meeting with an important person
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.t̬ɚˌvju /; / ˈɪntərˌvju / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tə.vjuː /; / ˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
a formal meeting or conversation in which someone is asked questions, often for a job, information, or consultation

Examples

job interview
After months of requests, the scientist finally secured an interview with the Nobel laureate.
The ambassador was granted an interview with the president to discuss foreign policy.
The mayor scheduled an interview for citizens who wanted to present their concerns directly.
After the controversial match, the coach gave a brief interview to the press.
She prepared thoroughly for her interview with the renowned scientist.
The journalist conducted an interview with the author about her new book.

Related Terms

  • synonym – audience: a formal meeting with a person of rank or power
  • synonym – consultation: a meeting to seek advice or discuss something
  • synonym – interrogation: formal, often aggressive questioning, especially by police
