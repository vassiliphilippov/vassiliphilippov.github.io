sloe

noun
small, tart, dark purple fruit of a wild plum tree
Pronunciation: / sloʊ / (AmE), / sləʊ / (BrE)
a small, dark purple, sour fruit from the blackthorn bush (Prunus spinosa), often used in preserves and drinks like sloe gin
