NAC

noun
North Atlantic Council (NATO decision-making body)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn eɪ ˈsi / (AmE), / ˌɛn eɪ ˈsi / (BrE)
abbreviation for North Atlantic Council, the main decision-making body of NATO, consisting of representatives from all member countries.

Examples

NAC meeting
All major NATO policy decisions are made by the NAC.
Delegates from each member country participate in the NAC meetings.
The NAC will meet tomorrow to discuss the latest security issues affecting the alliance.
