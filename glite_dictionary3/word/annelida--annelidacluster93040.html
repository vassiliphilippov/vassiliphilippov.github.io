Annelida

noun
phylum of segmented worms
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈlɪdə / (AmE), / ˌanɪˈlɪdə / (BrE)
a phylum of invertebrate animals that includes segmented worms such as earthworms, leeches, and related species, characterized by ring-like segments along their bodies
