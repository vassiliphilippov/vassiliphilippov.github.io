vermin

noun
harmful small animals or insects (pests)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrmɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɜːmɪn / (BrE)
Small animals or insects that are harmful or troublesome, especially those that carry disease or cause damage, such as rats or cockroaches.

Examples

vermin like rats
Authorities launched a campaign to control vermin in the city.
Cereals must be stored carefully to protect them from vermin.
The farmer set traps to keep vermin out of the barn.

  • synonym – pest: an insect or small animal harmful to crops or property
