spawner

noun
female fish during spawning period
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːnər / (AmE), / ˈspɔːnə / (BrE)
A female fish during the period when it is spawning.

Examples

spawner lays eggs
A healthy habitat is essential for every spawner to lay her eggs safely.
During the spring, biologists monitor each spawner to estimate the salmon population.
The river is closed to fishing to protect returning spawners during the breeding season.
