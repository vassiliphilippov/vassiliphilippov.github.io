zebu

noun
domesticated humped ox
Pronunciation: / ˈziːbu / (AmE), / ˈziːbuː / (BrE)
a domesticated ox of South Asian origin, with a humped back, large dewlap, and long horns, often used as a draft animal

Examples

a zebu with a hump
A zebu can be recognized by the characteristic hump on its shoulders and long, curved horns.
In India, zebus are prized for their endurance and ability to work in intense heat.
