precaution

noun
measure taken to prevent harm
Pronunciation: / prɪˈkɔʃən / (AmE), / prɪˈkɔːʃən / (BrE)
an action or measure taken in advance to prevent harm or danger

Examples

precaution against theft
As a precaution, she always locks her door before going to bed.
Fire drills are conducted as a precaution in case of emergencies.
Travelers are advised to take basic precautions to avoid illness when visiting tropical countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym – risk: the possibility of danger or loss
  • synonym – safeguard: something that serves as protection or defense
