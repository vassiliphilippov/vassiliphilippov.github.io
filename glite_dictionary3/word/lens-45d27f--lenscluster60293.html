lens

noun
metaphorical way of seeing or understanding
Pronunciation: / lɛnz / (AmE), / lɛnz / (BrE)
a way of considering or thinking about something; a means of seeing or understanding an issue, often used metaphorically.

Examples

lens for understanding
Education can act as a lens that shapes our view of the world.
If you look at the data through the lens of economics, the trends become clearer.
The author uses a feminist lens to interpret historical events.
