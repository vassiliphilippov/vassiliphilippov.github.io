wetness

noun
the quality or state of being wet
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛtnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɛtnəs / (BrE)
the state or condition of being wet; the quality of containing or being covered by a liquid, especially water

Examples

wetness of the grass
After the storm, the wetness of the grass made it uncomfortable to sit outside.
She checked the wetness of the paint before touching the wall.
The wetness on the roads caused several cars to slip and slide.
