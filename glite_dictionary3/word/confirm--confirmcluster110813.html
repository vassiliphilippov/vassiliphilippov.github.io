to confirm

verb
formally approve for a position
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɝːm / (AmE), / kənˈfɜːm / (BrE)
to formally approve someone for a position, especially through an official process or vote

Examples

to confirm an appointment
It took several months for the committee to confirm her appointment as ambassador.
The Senate voted to confirm the nominee after a lengthy debate.
The board must confirm the president's selection before the new manager can start.

Related Terms

  • synonym – approve: to officially agree to or accept
  • antonym – reject: to refuse to accept or approve
