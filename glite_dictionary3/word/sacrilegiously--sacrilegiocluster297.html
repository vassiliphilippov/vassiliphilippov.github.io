sacrilegiously

adverb
in a disrespectful manner toward sacred things
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrɪˈlɪdʒəsli / (AmE), / ˌsakrɪˈlɪdʒəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows disrespect or irreverence toward something sacred.

Examples

to speak sacrilegiously
He spoke sacrilegiously about the holy traditions, causing outrage among the congregation.
The artifact was treated sacrilegiously by the careless tourists.
The mural in the ancient temple was sacrilegiously defaced during the night.
