conscious

adjective
aware; noticing or recognizing something
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃəs / (BrE)
aware of and able to perceive or notice something; having realization or recognition (especially followed by 'of').

Examples

conscious of danger
As a new driver, Mia was always conscious of the rules of the road.
He seemed barely conscious of the people around him at the party.
She was fully conscious of her responsibilities at work.
