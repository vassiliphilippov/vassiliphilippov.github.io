to collapse

verb
fold or close up (object)
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæps / (AmE), / kəˈlæps / (BrE)
to fold or close up something, especially to make it smaller or more compact

Examples

to collapse a table
After the picnic, she collapsed the folding table and put it in the car.
He collapses the ladder to store it in the small closet.
Please collapse your strollers before boarding the bus.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fold: to bend something, especially so that one part covers another
