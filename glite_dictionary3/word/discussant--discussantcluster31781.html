discussant

noun
participant in a formal discussion or panel
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskʌs.ənt / (AmE), / dɪˈskʌs.ənt / (BrE)
a person who takes part in a formal discussion, especially as a panelist or respondent in a conference or academic setting

Examples

conference discussant
As a discussant, her role was to stimulate further debate and raise insightful questions.
At the academic conference, each discussant provided valuable feedback on the presented papers.
The panel included a discussant who summarized and critiqued the main arguments after each talk.

  • synonym – panelist: a member of a panel discussion
  • synonym – participant: a person who takes part in something
