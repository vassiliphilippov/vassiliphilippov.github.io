to swipe

verb
make a touchscreen gesture
Pronunciation: / swaɪp / (AmE), / swaɪp / (BrE)
to move a finger or stylus across a touchscreen or trackpad to cause an action such as navigate, unlock, delete, or change content.

Examples

to swipe the screen
To clear emails, she swiped right and archived several messages without opening them.
When the phone locked, he swiped up on the screen to enter his passcode.
Many apps let you swipe left to delete items or reveal extra options quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym – slide: to move smoothly across a surface; in touch interfaces, to move content or pages by dragging a finger.
  • synonym – tap: to touch a screen briefly with a finger or stylus to select or activate something.
