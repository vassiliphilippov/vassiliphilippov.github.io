Ivan III Vasilievich

proper noun
Russian Grand Duke and state unifier (1440–1505)
Pronunciation: / aɪˈvɑn ði ˈθɜrd væˌsɪl.jəˈvɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈiːvən ðə ˈθɜːd ˌvæsɪliˈevɪtʃ / (BrE)
(1440–1505) Grand Duke of Muscovy, known for uniting Russian lands and reducing Tatar influence, laying the foundations for the Russian state.

Ivan III Vasilievich united Russia
