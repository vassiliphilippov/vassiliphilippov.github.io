hail

noun
a rapid outpouring of objects
Pronunciation: / heɪl / (AmE), / heɪl / (BrE)
a large number of objects or things being thrown or falling through the air at the same time, often in a forceful or rapid way

Examples

a hail of bullets
A hail of questions rained down on the speaker during the press conference.
The army advanced through a hail of bullets.
The protesters were met by a hail of stones from the angry crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym – barrage: a concentrated outpouring, as of words or blows
  • synonym – volley: a number of bullets, arrows, or other projectiles discharged at one time
