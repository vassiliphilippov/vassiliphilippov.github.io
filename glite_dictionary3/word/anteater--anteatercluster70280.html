anteater

noun
small Australian marsupial; termite eater
Pronunciation: / ˈæntˌitər /; / ˈænˌtiːtər / (AmE), / ˈæntiːtə /; / ˈænˌtiːtə / (BrE)
a small Australian mammal with a long snout and claws, feeding mainly on ants or termites; includes the numbat and the spiny anteater (echidna)
