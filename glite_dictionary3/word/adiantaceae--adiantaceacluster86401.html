Adiantaceae

noun
a family of ferns in some classifications
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪdiˌænˈteɪsi.i /; / ˌæd.i.ænˈteɪ.si.iː / (AmE), / ˌadɪ anˈtiːsiːiː /; / ˌæd.i.ænˈteɪ.siː / (BrE)
