amphibian

noun
aircraft for land and water operations
Pronunciation: / æmˈfɪbiən / (AmE), / æmˈfɪbiən / (BrE)
an aircraft that is designed to take off from and land on both water and land

Examples

amphibian aircraft landing
Pilots must take a special course to operate an amphibian safely.
The Coast Guard uses an amphibian to patrol remote islands and coastal areas.
The new amphibian features advanced technology for smooth water landings.
