tail

noun
reverse side of a coin (opposite heads)
Pronunciation: / teɪl / (AmE), / teɪl / (BrE)
(usually tails) The side of a coin opposite the one bearing the image of a person's head.

Examples

tails on a coin
He flipped the coin and it landed on tails.
Let's see who goes first—call heads or tails.
