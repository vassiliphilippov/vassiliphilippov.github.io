thump

noun
heavy dull sound from impact
Pronunciation: / θʌmp / (AmE), / θʌmp / (BrE)
a heavy, dull sound or a heavy blow

Examples

a loud thump
I heard a sudden thump coming from the attic.
The ball landed with a thump on the roof of the car.
There was a loud thump as the box hit the floor.
He landed a solid thump on his opponent's shoulder during the match.
She gave the table a thump to get everyone's attention.
There was a loud thump as the book fell to the floor.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – bang: a sudden loud hit or impact
  • synonym – punch: a blow with the fist
  • degree lower – tap: a light or gentle hit
  • synonym – thud: a dull sound, as of a heavy object striking the ground
