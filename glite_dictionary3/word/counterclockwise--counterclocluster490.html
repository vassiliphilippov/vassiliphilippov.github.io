counterclockwise

adjective, adverb
in the opposite direction to a clock's hands
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntɚˈklɑkˌwaɪz /; / ˌkaʊntərˈklɑkwaɪz / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntəˈklɒkˌwaɪz /; / ˌkaʊntəˈklɒkwaɪz / (BrE)
in or moving in a direction opposite to the way the hands of a clock move

Examples

turn counterclockwise slowly

To unscrew the lid, turn it counterclockwise until it comes off.

Turn the screw counterclockwise to loosen it.
