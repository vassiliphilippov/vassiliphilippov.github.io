uncultivated

adjective
not prepared for growing crops (of land)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌltɪˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of land or fields) not prepared or used for growing crops.

Examples

uncultivated land
Much of the region is covered with uncultivated land and dense forest.
The farmer left the field uncultivated for several years to restore its fertility.
Wildflowers flourished in the uncultivated meadow behind the house.

  • antonym – cultivated: land prepared for crops
