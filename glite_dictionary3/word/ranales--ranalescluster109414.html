Ranales

noun
historical order of flowering plants
Pronunciation: / ræˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / ræˈneɪliːz /; / rəˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
a former order of flowering plants that included families such as Ranunculaceae, Magnoliaceae, and Lauraceae; now mostly obsolete in modern plant classification
