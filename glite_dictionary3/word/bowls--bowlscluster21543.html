bowls

noun
lawn game using biased wooden balls
Pronunciation: / boʊlz / (AmE), / bəʊlz / (BrE)
A lawn game in which players roll biased wooden balls so that they stop as close as possible to a smaller ball called a 'jack.'

Examples

play bowls outdoors
