contralto

adjective
having the lowest female vocal range
Pronunciation: / kənˈtræltoʊ / (AmE), / kənˈtræl.təʊ / (BrE)
(of a voice or singer) having the lowest range of the female voice.

Examples

contralto singing style
Critics praised her unique, contralto timbre, which stood out among the cast.
Her contralto voice lent a rich depth to the choir's performance.
The lead character was portrayed by a singer with a powerful contralto tone.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – mezzo-soprano: the female voice between soprano and contralto
  • antonym – soprano: the highest female singing voice
