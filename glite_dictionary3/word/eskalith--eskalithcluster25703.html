Eskalith

noun
drug for bipolar disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈɛskəˌlɪθ / (AmE), / ˈɛskəˌlɪθ / (BrE)
A trademarked drug containing lithium carbonate, used chiefly to treat bipolar disorder and certain types of depression.
