toggle

noun
two-position electrical switch
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑɡəl /; / ˈtɑɡl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtɒɡəl /; / ˈtɒɡl̩ / (BrE)
a physical switch or fastener, such as a hinged switch for devices or a rod used to fasten clothing, ropes, or sails

Examples
