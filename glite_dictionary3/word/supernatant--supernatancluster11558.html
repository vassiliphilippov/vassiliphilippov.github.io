supernatant

adjective
floating above sediment (esp. liquid)
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pərˈneɪ.tənt / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈneɪ.tənt / (BrE)
(of a liquid) floating on the surface above a sediment or precipitate.

Examples

supernatant liquid layer
After centrifugation, the supernatant liquid was carefully poured off.
Skim the supernatant layer to separate it from the solid residue.
The technician examined the supernatant for clarity before proceeding.
