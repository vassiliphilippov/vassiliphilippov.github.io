dosimetry

noun
measurement of absorbed radiation dose
Pronunciation: / doʊˈsɪmətri / (AmE), / dəʊˈsɪmətri / (BrE)
the measurement and calculation of the amount of radiation absorbed by an object or person, especially for medical or safety purposes

Examples

medical dosimetry
Accurate dosimetry is essential for ensuring patient safety during radiation therapy.
Advances in dosimetry have improved the effectiveness of cancer treatments.
He specialized in dosimetry to monitor exposure levels in nuclear facilities.
