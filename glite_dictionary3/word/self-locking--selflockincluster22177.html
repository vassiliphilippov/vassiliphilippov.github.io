self-locking

adjective
locking automatically when closed
Pronunciation: / sɛlf ˈlɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / sɛlf ˈlɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a mechanism or device) locking automatically when closed, without the need for separate action.

Examples

self-locking door mechanism
Many modern suitcases feature self-locking zippers for added protection during travel.
The self-locking gate ensures security by automatically fastening when closed.
The technician praised the self-locking mechanism for its reliability and ease of use.
