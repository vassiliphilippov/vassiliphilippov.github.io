reconciler

noun
person who tries to bring peace
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.ənˌsaɪ.lər / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.ənˌsaɪ.lə / (BrE)
a person who seeks to bring about agreement or restore friendly relations between others

Examples

a skilled reconciler
As a skilled reconciler, she helped the two countries reach a peaceful agreement.
He saw himself as a reconciler, always seeking to heal divisions within the group.
The community respected him as a reconciler during times of disagreement.
