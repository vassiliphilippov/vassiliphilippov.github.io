cover

noun
natural envelope or protective layer
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvɚ /; / ˈkʌvər /; / ˈkʌv.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə /; / ˈkʌv.ə / (BrE)
something that covers, protects, or hides another thing (such as a lid, blanket, book cover, or natural layer)

Examples

cover of snow
After the storm, the ground was left with only a thin cover of snow.
