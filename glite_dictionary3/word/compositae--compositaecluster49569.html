Compositae

noun
family of flowering plants with composite flower heads
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑzɪˌti /; / kəmˈpoʊzəˌtiː / (AmE), / kɒmˈpɒzɪti /; / kəmˈpɒzɪtiː / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants with heads made up of many small flowers, including daisies, asters, sunflowers, dandelions, and marigolds; also called Asteraceae
