anomalously

adverb
in an abnormal or unusual way
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑː.mə.ləs.li / (AmE), / əˈnɒm.ə.ləs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected.

Examples

to act anomalously
She behaved anomalously at the meeting, unlike her usual calm self.
The device functioned anomalously under extreme conditions, surprising the engineers.
Their results were reported anomalously compared to previous studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym – anomalous: deviating from the norm; irregular
  • antonym – normally: in a usual or expected way
