immunocompromised

adjective
having a weakened or impaired immune system
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmjunəˈkɑmprəˌmaɪzd / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəˈkɒmprəmaɪzd / (BrE)
having a weakened or impaired immune system, making it harder to resist infections and diseases

Examples

immunocompromised patient
Cancer treatments can leave patients immunocompromised and more susceptible to infections.
During flu season, immunocompromised people are advised to take extra precautions.
The hospital has special units for immunocompromised children to reduce their risk of illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym – immunodeficient: having a deficient or incomplete immune system
