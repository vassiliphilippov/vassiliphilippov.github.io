to verbalize

verb
convert into a verb
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈvɜːbəlaɪz / (BrE)
to convert (a noun or other part of speech) into a verb; to form a verb from another word

Examples

to verbalize a noun
Linguists often verbalize new technology terms to describe emerging actions.
Many English nouns have been verbalized over time, such as 'to process' from 'process'.
The word 'email' was quickly verbalized after its initial use as a noun.
