to amalgamate

verb
to combine or unite into one
Pronunciation: / əˈmæl.ɡəˌmeɪt / (AmE), / əˈmal.ɡə.meɪt / (BrE)
to combine or unite to form one structure, organization, or substance

Examples

to amalgamate two companies
Several smaller groups were amalgamated into one large organization last year.
The chef plans to amalgamate traditional flavors with modern techniques in his new restaurant.
The two companies decided to amalgamate their operations to increase efficiency.

Related Terms

  • antonym – separate: to move or be moved apart, not combined
