to salaam

verb
greet with a salaam gesture
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑm / (AmE), / səˈlɑːm / (BrE)
to greet someone with a salaam, a gesture of respect or greeting commonly used in Muslim cultures, typically involving a low bow with the right palm placed on the forehead.

Examples

to salaam an elder
During the ceremony, he respectfully salaamed before the elders.
She salaamed to show her gratitude and respect after receiving the award.
The ambassador salaamed upon entering the room to greet his hosts.

Related Terms

  • synonym – bow: to bend the upper part of the body as a gesture of respect or greeting
