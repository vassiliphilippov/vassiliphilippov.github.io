amberfish

noun
a warm-water amber-colored carangid fish
Pronunciation: / ˈæmbɚˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈæmbəˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
any of several warm-water carangid fish with amber to coppery coloring and a forked tail, found chiefly in tropical and subtropical seas.

fresh amberfish fillet
