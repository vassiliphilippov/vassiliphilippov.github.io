technician

noun
person trained in a technical skill
Pronunciation: / tɛkˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / tɛkˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
a person whose job involves special training or skill in a specific technical process or activity

Examples

technician repairs equipment
As a laboratory technician, she ensures that all tests are performed accurately.
Every technician on the team specializes in a different type of software installation.
The hospital hired a new technician to maintain the medical equipment.
