emersion

noun
the act of emerging or coming out
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrʒən / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːʃən / (BrE)
the act of coming into view or reappearing after being hidden or concealed (especially after an eclipse in astronomy)
