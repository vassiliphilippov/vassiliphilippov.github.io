nonbelligerent

adjective
not participating in a war
Pronunciation: / nɑn.bəˈlɪdʒ.ɚ.ənt / (AmE), / nɒn.bəˈlɪdʒ.ər.ənt / (BrE)
(of a country or group) not engaged in active warfare; not participating directly in armed conflict.
