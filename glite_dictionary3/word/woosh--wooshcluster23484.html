to woosh

verb
move quickly with a rushing sound
Pronunciation: / wuʃ / (AmE), / wuːʃ / (BrE)
to move very quickly, making a rushing or sibilant sound

Examples

to woosh past
As she opened the window, a cold breeze wooshed into the room.
The rocket wooshed upwards, leaving a trail of smoke in its wake.
The train wooshed past the platform, startling the waiting passengers.

  • synonym – zoom: to move or travel very quickly
