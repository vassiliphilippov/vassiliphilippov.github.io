boxer

noun
a breed of medium-sized, strong dog
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑksər / (AmE), / ˈbɒksə / (BrE)
a breed of medium-sized, strong, short-haired dog with a brindle or fawn coat and a square-jawed muzzle, originally developed in Germany

Examples

boxer dog breed
My family adopted a young boxer from the animal shelter last year.
She takes her boxer for a long walk every morning before work.
The boxer is known for its playful nature and boundless energy.
