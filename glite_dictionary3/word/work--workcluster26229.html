to work

verb
to cause to ferment
Pronunciation: / wɝːk / (AmE), / wɜːk / (BrE)
to cause (a substance, especially an alcoholic liquid) to undergo fermentation

Examples

to work the wine
It is important to carefully work the mixture to produce a fine champagne.
The yeast will work the must until all the sugar is gone.
They work the cider in large barrels each autumn.
