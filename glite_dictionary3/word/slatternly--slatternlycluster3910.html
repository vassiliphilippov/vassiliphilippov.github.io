slatternly

adjective
dirty and untidy (usually of a woman)
Pronunciation: / ˈslætərnli / (AmE), / ˈslætənli / (BrE)
(of a woman or her appearance) dirty and untidy; showing a lack of care for one's habits or appearance; slovenly or promiscuous.

Examples

slatternly woman
His mother warned him not to keep slatternly company.
She was criticized for her slatternly habits and lack of personal hygiene.
The room had a slatternly appearance, with clothes strewn everywhere.
