departure

noun
deviation from the norm or standard
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɑrtʃɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈpɑːtʃə / (BrE)

Examples

departure from tradition
Her latest novel is a significant departure from her earlier work.
The new policy represents a major departure from traditional practices.
This modern design is quite a departure from the classic style we usually see.
