concretion

noun
formation of a stonelike mass in the body
Pronunciation: / kənˈkriːʃən / (AmE), / kənˈkriːʃən /; / kɒnˈkriːʃən / (BrE)
a hard lump or stonelike mass formed by the accumulation of mineral salts, especially in a body organ such as the kidneys or bladder
