non-binary

noun
person not exclusively male or female
Pronunciation: / n\u0251n\u02c8ba\u026a\u02ccn\u025bri / (AmE), / n\u0252n\u02c8ba\u026an\u0259ri / (BrE)
a person whose gender identity does not fit within the traditional categories of male or female.

Examples

a non-binary individual
The conference included several non-binary speakers who discussed workplace inclusion and rights.
A close friend came out as non-binary and asked to be referred to by they/them pronouns.
University records now allow students to identify as non-binary on their official enrollment forms.
