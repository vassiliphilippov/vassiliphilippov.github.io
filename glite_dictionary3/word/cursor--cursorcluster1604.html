cursor

noun
screen indicator in computing
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝː.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.sə / (BrE)
a movable indicator on a computer screen that shows where the next action will occur, such as where text will be entered or a command selected.

Examples

move the cursor
She lost track of the cursor while editing the spreadsheet.
The blinking cursor indicates where you can start typing in the document.
You can use your mouse to move the cursor to the desired location on the screen.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pointer: device or on-screen symbol for indicating a position; often shaped like an arrow
