to rant

verb
speak angrily or loudly at length
Pronunciation: / rænt / (AmE), / rɑːnt / (BrE)
to speak or shout at length in an angry, impassioned, or loud way

Examples

to rant loudly
He was ranting on social media about the latest political scandal.
She tends to rant when she's frustrated with work.
The coach began to rant about the team's poor performance during halftime.
