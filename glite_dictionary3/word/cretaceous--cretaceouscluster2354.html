Cretaceous

adjective
relating to the last period of the Mesozoic era
Pronunciation: / krɪˈteɪʃəs / (AmE), / krɪˈteɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to the third and last period of the Mesozoic era, characterized by the dominance of dinosaurs and the appearance of flowering plants

Examples

Cretaceous period fossils
Flowering plants began to flourish in the Cretaceous era.
Some of the most famous dinosaurs lived during the Cretaceous period.
The Cretaceous fossils found in this region are remarkably well preserved.
