Dolichos lignosus

noun
South African vine with edible pods
Pronunciation: / doʊˈlaɪkoʊs lɪgˈnoʊsəs / (AmE), / dəˈlɪkəs lɪgˈnəʊsəs / (BrE)
A South African evergreen, partly woody vine cultivated for its clusters of rosy-purple flowers and edible bean-like pods; also used as green manure.
