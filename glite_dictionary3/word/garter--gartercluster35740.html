garter

noun
elastic band for holding up stockings or sleeves
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrtər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːtə / (BrE)
A band, typically elastic, worn around the leg to hold up a stocking, or around the arm to keep a sleeve in place.

Examples

garter belt
In Victorian times, a garter was an essential accessory for women.
She adjusted her garter before putting on her dress.
The bride wore a blue garter under her wedding gown for good luck.
