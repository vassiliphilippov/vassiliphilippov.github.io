teaser

noun
worker who prepares wool fibers
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːzər / (AmE), / ˈtiːzə / (BrE)
a person who prepares wool for spinning by separating and loosening the fibers, traditionally by hand or with special tools.

Examples

wool teaser worker
In the past, every spinning workshop relied on a skilled teaser to ensure the quality of the wool.
My grandfather once worked as a teaser in the Yorkshire wool mills.
The teaser carefully separated the tangled wool fibers before they were spun into yarn.
