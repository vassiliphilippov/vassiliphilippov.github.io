Acular

noun
trade name for an anti-inflammatory drug
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːlər / (AmE), / əˈkjuːlə / (BrE)
A trade name for ketorolac, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used especially as an eye drop to relieve itching or inflammation.
