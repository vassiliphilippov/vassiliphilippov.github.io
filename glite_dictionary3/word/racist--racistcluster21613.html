racist

adjective
discriminatory based on race
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈreɪsɪst / (BrE)
showing prejudice, discrimination, or belief that one race is superior to others

Examples

racist comment
A racist attitude can cause deep harm to communities.
His comments were widely criticized as racist and unacceptable.
The company adopted strict policies against racist behavior in the workplace.
His boss was fired for making several racist comments in a meeting.
Many people rallied against the use of racist language in the media.
The new law was criticized as being blatantly racist by civil rights groups.
