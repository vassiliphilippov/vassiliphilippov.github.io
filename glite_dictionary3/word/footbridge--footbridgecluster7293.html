footbridge

noun
bridge for pedestrians
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌbrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌbrɪdʒ / (BrE)
A narrow bridge designed for people to walk across, not for vehicles.

Examples

cross a footbridge
A new footbridge was built to connect the two campuses.
During the flood, the footbridge was the only safe way across the stream.
We crossed the river using the old footbridge near the park.
