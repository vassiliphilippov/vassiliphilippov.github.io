microphone

noun
sound-to-electrical signal device
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪ.kɹəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmaɪ.krə.fəʊn / (BrE)
A device that converts sound waves, especially speech or music, into electrical signals for recording or amplification.

Examples

use a microphone
Please speak directly into the microphone so that your voice is recorded properly.
She adjusted the microphone before starting her speech to ensure everyone could hear her clearly.
The band uses a wireless microphone for their lead singer during live performances.

  • synonym – megaphone: a device for amplifying and directing the voice
