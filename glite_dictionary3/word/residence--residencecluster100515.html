residence

noun
a place where one lives
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛzɪdəns / (BrE)
a place where someone lives for a significant period; a home or dwelling.

Examples

family residence
After moving to the city, she changed her residence to be closer to work.
He maintains several residences in different countries for business reasons.
The university requires students to provide proof of residence when they enroll.

Related Terms

  • synonym – abode: a place of residence; a house or home
  • synonym – home: the place where one lives permanently, especially as a member of a family or household
  • antonym – workplace: a place where one works (as opposed to where one lives)
