to signalize

verb
communicate by signals or signs
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nə.laɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nə.laɪz / (BrE)
to communicate or convey information using signals or signs, especially non-verbally

Examples

to signalize agreement
During the noisy meeting, she signalized her agreement with a quick nod.
He signalized the driver by waving a red flag at the intersection.
In the field, scouts often signalize important discoveries using hand gestures.

Related Terms

  • synonym – signal: to communicate with a gesture, action, or device
