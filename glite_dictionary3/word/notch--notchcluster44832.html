notch

noun
mountain pass; low point in a ridge
Pronunciation: / nɑtʃ / (AmE), / nɒtʃ / (BrE)
a narrow pass or opening in a range of mountains that is lower than the surrounding peaks

Examples

mountain notch pass
A chilled wind whipped through the notch between the two tallest peaks.
Several roads converge at the notch, offering access to the national park.
The hikers crossed the narrow notch before dusk fell over the valley.
