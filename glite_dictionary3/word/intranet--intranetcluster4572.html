intranet

noun
private internal computer network
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.trəˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.trə.net / (BrE)
a private computer network within an organization, using Internet protocols and accessible only to its members

Examples

company intranet
Access to the intranet is restricted to members of the organization.
Our company uses an intranet to share documents and announcements with employees.
You can find the HR forms on the intranet portal.
