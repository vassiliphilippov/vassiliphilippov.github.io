leeward

adjective
on or toward the sheltered side from wind
Pronunciation: / ˈliːwɚd / (AmE), / ˈliːwəd / (BrE)
Situated on or toward the side sheltered from the wind; facing away from the direction from which the wind is blowing.

Examples

leeward side protected
The campsite was set up in a leeward position for protection from the weather.
They anchored the boat on the leeward side of the bay to escape the strong winds.
Vegetation grows more densely on the leeward side of the mountain.

Related Terms

  • antonym – windward: on or toward the side facing the wind
