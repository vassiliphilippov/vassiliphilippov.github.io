ball

noun
rounded body part or mass
Pronunciation: / bɔl / (AmE), / bɔːl / (BrE)
a rounded mass or area, especially a rounded part of the body such as at the base of the thumb or the sole of the foot

Examples

ball of foot
He developed a callus on the ball of his foot after running.
She massaged the ball at the base of her thumb to ease tension.
The cat pressed its paw, resting the ball of its hand on my arm.
