earthbound

adjective
confined to the earth
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːθˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
Confined to the earth; unable to fly or leave the ground.

Examples

earthbound birds
Her earthbound concerns kept her too busy to dream of traveling to space.
The earthbound creatures gazed up at the sky, longing for the freedom of flight.
The novel explores both earthbound realities and imaginative flights of fancy.
