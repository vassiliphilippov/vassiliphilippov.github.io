to connect

verb
be scheduled for continuous travel (transport)
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛkt / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt / (BrE)
(of transportation services) to be scheduled so as to provide a continuous journey for passengers, typically by allowing direct transfers from one service to another.

Examples

connect flights easily
Do these flights connect directly or will I have a layover?
Our ferry will connect with the morning coach to London.
The last bus doesn't connect with the midnight train, so you'll have to wait.

Related Terms

  • synonym – transfer: to move from one vehicle or service to another during travel
