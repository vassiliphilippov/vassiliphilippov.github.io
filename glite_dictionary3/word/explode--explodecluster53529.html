to explode

verb
release a stop consonant with a burst of air
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsploʊd / (AmE), / ɪkˈspləʊd / (BrE)
(of a stop consonant) to be released with a burst of air pressure, as in the pronunciation of sounds like /p/, /t/, and /k/.

Examples

explode a consonant
In some dialects, speakers do not fully explode final stop consonants.
Linguists analyze how stop consonants explode at the release phase of articulation.
When pronouncing the word 'pat,' make sure to explode the 'p' sound clearly.
