to listen

verb
pay close attention; take notice of
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪs.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɪs.ən / (BrE)
to pay close attention to or take notice of something, especially advice or warnings

Examples

to listen to advice
He refused to listen to the warnings, which led to trouble later.
If you listen carefully to your teachers, you will learn much more.
You should listen to your doctor's advice if you want to recover quickly.
