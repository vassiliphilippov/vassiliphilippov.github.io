to cutinize

verb
become or turn into cutin (in plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːtəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkjuːtɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)
(of plant cells or tissues) to convert into or become impregnated with cutin.

Examples

to cutinize plant cells
Researchers observed that some tissues failed to cutinize properly in mutant plants.

Under certain conditions, leaves may cutinize more extensively to adapt to dry climates.
