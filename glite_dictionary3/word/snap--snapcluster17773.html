snap

noun
stringless, easily broken green bean
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
A tender variety of green bean without strings, which easily breaks into pieces; commonly used as a vegetable.

Examples

fresh snaps beans
I prefer snaps over string beans because they are easier to prepare.
She added fresh snaps to the salad for extra crunch.
The farmer brought a basket of snaps to the market this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym – green bean: a young, tender, unripe bean pod eaten as a vegetable
  • synonym – snap bean: an edible pod of some varieties of beans, also known as string bean
