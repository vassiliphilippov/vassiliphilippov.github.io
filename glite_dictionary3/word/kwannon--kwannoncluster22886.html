Kwannon

proper noun
Japanese Buddhist goddess of mercy
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːn ən / (AmE), / ˈkwæn ən / (BrE)
The Japanese name for the Buddhist bodhisattva of compassion, corresponding to the Chinese Kuan Yin.
