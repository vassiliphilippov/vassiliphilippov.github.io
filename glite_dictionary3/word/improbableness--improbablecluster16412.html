improbableness

noun
the quality of being improbable
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɑːbəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɒbəbəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being improbable; the condition of being unlikely to happen or true.

Examples

improbableness of success
Scientists questioned the improbableness of two such rare events occurring together.
She was fascinated by the improbableness of winning against such overwhelming odds.
The sheer improbableness of the story made it difficult for anyone to believe.
