shadowy

adjective
difficult to see or unclear
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊi / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊi / (BrE)
difficult to see, understand, or define; vague or insubstantial

Examples

shadowy figure in history

The distant figure appeared shadowy and indistinct in the fading light.
