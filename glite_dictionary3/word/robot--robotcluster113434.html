robot

noun
machine capable of automatic actions
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.bɑt / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.bɒt / (BrE)
A machine, especially one programmable by a computer, capable of carrying out a complex series of actions automatically.

Examples

industrial robot
A robot can be programmed to perform repetitive tasks with precision.
In the movie, the robot developed feelings and befriended a young child.
The factory uses robots to assemble cars on its production line.

Related Terms

  • synonym – android: robot with a human appearance
  • synonym – automaton: mechanical device that performs a set of instructions
  • antonym – human: person as opposed to a robot
