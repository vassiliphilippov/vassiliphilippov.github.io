hodometer

noun
instrument measuring traveled distance
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈdɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / həʊˈdɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the distance traveled by a vehicle; an odometer

Examples

hodometer reading distance
Early surveyors relied on a hodometer to calculate the length of roads.
He examined the hodometer to record exactly how far the carriage had traveled.
The mechanical hodometer on the old truck still worked after decades of use.
