hydrocephalic

adjective
relating to hydrocephalus
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəsɪˈfælɪk / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪdrə(ʊ)sɪˈfalɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by hydrocephalus, a condition characterized by an abnormal accumulation of fluid in the brain.
