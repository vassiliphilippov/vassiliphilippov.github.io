cycloolefin

noun
cyclic hydrocarbon with double bonds, non-aromatic
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkloʊˈoʊləˌfɪn / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪkləʊˈəʊlɪfɪn / (BrE)
