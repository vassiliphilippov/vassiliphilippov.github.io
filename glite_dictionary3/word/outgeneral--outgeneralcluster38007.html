to outgeneral

verb
defeat by superior strategy
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / aʊtˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)

The young commander managed to outgeneral his more experienced rival in the decisive battle.
