tiredness

noun
temporary lack of energy or strength
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪədnɪs / (BrE)
A state of being tired; a temporary lack of strength or energy resulting from physical or mental effort.

Examples

feel tiredness after work
After running the marathon, a wave of tiredness washed over him.
She struggled to stay awake during the meeting because of her overwhelming tiredness.
The long hours of studying led to a deep sense of tiredness by midnight.

  • synonym – fatigue: extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion
