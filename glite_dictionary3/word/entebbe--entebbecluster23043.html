Entebbe

proper noun
town in Uganda, site of 1976 hostage rescue
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtɛbi / (AmE), / ɛnˈtɛbi / (BrE)
A town in southern Uganda on the shores of Lake Victoria, known for its international airport and as the site of a 1976 hostage rescue by Israeli commandos.
