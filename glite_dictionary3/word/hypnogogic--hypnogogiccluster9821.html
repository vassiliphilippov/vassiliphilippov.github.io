hypnogogic

adjective
relating to the state before falling asleep
Pronunciation: / hɪp.nəˈɡɑ.dʒɪk / (AmE), / hɪp.nəˈɡɒ.dʒɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the state immediately before falling asleep; inducing sleep or characteristic of drowsiness experienced prior to sleep.

Examples

hypnogogic state before sleep
Many people experience vivid hypnogogic imagery while drifting off to sleep.
Some artists draw inspiration from the hypnogogic sensations that occur just before sleep.
The patient's hypnogogic state was accompanied by brief auditory hallucinations.

Related Terms
