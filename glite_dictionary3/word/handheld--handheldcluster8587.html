handheld

adjective
designed to be used while held in the hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌhɛld / (AmE), / ˈhænd.hɛld / (BrE)
(of a device) small and light enough to be operated while being held in the hand.

Examples

handheld device
Many people now read the news on their handheld devices.
The inspector used a handheld scanner to check barcodes on the products.
The photographer switched to a handheld camera for greater flexibility.
