pseudobulb

noun
bulblike stem part in some orchids
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːdoʊˌbʌlb / (AmE), / ˈsjuːdəʊˌbʌlb / (BrE)
a thickened, bulblike part of the stem in some orchids, used for water and nutrient storage

Examples

orchid pseudobulb
Each pseudobulb can produce one or more leaves and sometimes flowers.
Orchid growers often check the pseudobulb for signs of dehydration.
The pseudobulb helps the plant survive periods of drought.
