boxer

noun
person who packs items into boxes
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑksər / (AmE), / ˈbɒksə / (BrE)
a workman whose job is to pack items into containers, especially boxes.

Examples

boxer packs goods
A team of boxers worked late to get all the holiday orders shipped on time.
She found a summer job as a boxer at the local distribution center.
The boxer carefully packed the fragile goods into sturdy cardboard boxes.
