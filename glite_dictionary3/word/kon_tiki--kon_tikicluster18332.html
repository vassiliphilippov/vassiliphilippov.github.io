Kon Tiki

noun
light balsa raft; Heyerdahl's 1947 raft
Pronunciation: / kɑn ˈtiːki / (AmE), / kɒn ˈtiːki / (BrE)
a light raft made of balsa wood, especially one modeled after or referring to the raft used by Thor Heyerdahl in his 1947 Pacific expedition
