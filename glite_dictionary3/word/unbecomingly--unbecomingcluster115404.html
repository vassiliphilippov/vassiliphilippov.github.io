unbecomingly

adverb
in an inappropriate or unsuitable manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.bɪˈkʌm.ɪŋ.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.bɪˈkʌm.ɪŋ.li / (BrE)
In a way that is not appropriate, proper, or suitable; lacking decorum.

Examples

unbecomingly loud at dinner
He laughed unbecomingly at a serious moment, embarrassing his friends.
She spoke unbecomingly at the formal event, drawing disapproving looks from the audience.
The student behaved unbecomingly during the ceremony, ignoring the rules of decorum.

Related Terms

  • antonym – decorously: in a proper, dignified, or socially acceptable manner
  • synonym – unbecoming: not fitting or appropriate; unattractive (adjective form)
