Ascaris

noun
genus of parasitic roundworms
Pronunciation: / ˈæskərɪs / (AmE), / ˈæskərɪs / (BrE)
A genus of large, parasitic roundworms with a three-lipped mouth, especially known for infecting the intestines of humans and animals.
