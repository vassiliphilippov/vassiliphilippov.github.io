Niger

proper noun
major river in West Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈniːdʒə / (BrE)
A major river in West Africa that flows through several countries and empties into the Gulf of Guinea in the South Atlantic Ocean.

Examples

the Niger river
Fishermen rely on the Niger for their daily catch.
The Niger is crucial for agriculture in Mali and Niger.
The annual flooding of the Niger creates fertile land for thousands of farmers.
