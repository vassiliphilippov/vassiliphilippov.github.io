flatness

noun
the state of being flat; two-dimensionality
Pronunciation: / ˈflætnəs / (AmE), / ˈflætnəs / (BrE)
the condition or quality of having two dimensions, especially lacking in height or depth; the state of being flat

Examples

the flatness of land
Satellite images revealed the remarkable flatness of the region, with hardly any hills or valleys.
Scientists measured the flatness of the surface to ensure it would work for the experiment.
The flatness of the drawing made it appear almost abstract to the viewers.

Related Terms

  • antonym – roughness: the quality of being uneven or irregular
  • synonym – smoothness: the quality of having an even or regular surface
