Constantinople

proper noun
former name of Istanbul, Turkey
Pronunciation: / kɒnˌstæn.tɪˈnoʊ.pəl / (AmE), / kɒn.stæn.tɪˈnəʊ.pəl / (BrE)
the former name of Istanbul, historically the capital of the Byzantine Empire, founded by Constantine I in the fourth century

Examples
