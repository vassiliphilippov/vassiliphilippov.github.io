Mahonia

noun
a genus of evergreen shrubs and small trees
Pronunciation: / məˈhoʊniə / (AmE), / məˈhəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen shrubs and small trees with spiny or pinnate leaves and yellow flowers, native to North and Central America and Asia
