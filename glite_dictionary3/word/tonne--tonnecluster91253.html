tonne

noun
unit of weight (1,000 kg)
Pronunciation: / tʌn / (AmE), / tɒn / (BrE)
a unit of weight equal to 1,000 kilograms, used mainly outside the United States

Examples

one tonne of steel
Global carbon emissions are measured in millions of tonnes each year.
One tonne of steel can be recycled to make new products.
The truck was carrying over three tonnes of gravel to the construction site.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ton: a unit of weight equal to 2,000 pounds (US) or 2,240 pounds (UK)
