installment

noun
regular part-payment of a debt
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɑlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɑːlmənt / (BrE)
a sum of money paid as part of a series of payments for something, especially a debt, usually at regular intervals

Examples

monthly installment payment
Each month, she paid an installment on her student loans.
The car was finally his after he made the last installment.
You can buy the sofa now and pay in twelve easy installments.

  • synonym – payment: the act of paying money
