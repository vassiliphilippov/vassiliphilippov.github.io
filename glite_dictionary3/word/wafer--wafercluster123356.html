wafer

noun
adhesive seal for letters
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪfər / (AmE), / ˈweɪfə / (BrE)
a small, thin disk of adhesive paste used to seal letters or documents

Examples

wafer seal on envelope
He pressed a red wafer onto the envelope to seal it closed.
In the 18th century, letters were often sealed with an adhesive wafer rather than wax.
She collected antique wafer seals from old family correspondence.
