world

noun
earthly life, not the afterlife
Pronunciation: / wɜrld / (AmE), / wɜːld / (BrE)
the concerns and affairs of earthly life, as distinct from spiritual or heavenly matters

Examples

pleasures of the world
Many religious teachings urge believers not to be distracted by the pleasures of the world.
She renounced the world and devoted herself to spiritual contemplation.
Some faiths stress a complete separation from the world in order to achieve enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • antonym – afterlife: life after death, a spiritual existence after earthly life
  • antonym – heaven: the place or state regarded as the home of God and the angels, regarded as distinct from the material world
