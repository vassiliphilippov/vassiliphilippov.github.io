friendzone

noun
state of unreciprocated romantic interest
Pronunciation: / c8fr5bndcczo8an / (AmE), / c8fr5bndccz598an / (BrE)
Informal. the situation in which one person has romantic or sexual feelings for another but is seen only as a friend, so the feelings are not reciprocated.

Examples

stuck in the friendzone
Being stuck in the friendzone made him avoid dating for months afterward.
They joked about the friendzone after she introduced him as a 'great friend'.
Therapy helped her accept life in the friendzone and move on to new relationships.
