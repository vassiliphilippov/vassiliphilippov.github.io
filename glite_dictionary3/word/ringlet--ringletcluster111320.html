ringlet

noun
small circular formation of concentric circles
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋlət / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a small circular band or a round formation made up of concentric circles, such as those seen in leaves or flower petals

Examples

ringlet of water
Several ringlets appeared on the surface of the pond after the raindrop fell.
The fern displayed tiny ringlets at the tip of each new frond.
The rose's petals formed a delicate ringlet around the center.

Related Terms

  • synonym – circle: a round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from the center
