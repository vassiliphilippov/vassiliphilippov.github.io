hyperlipaemia

noun
excess lipids in the blood
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərlɪˈpiːmiə / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəliˈpiːmɪə / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by an abnormally high concentration of lipids in the blood.
