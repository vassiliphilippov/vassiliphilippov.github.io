Culex

noun
genus of mosquitoes
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːlɛks / (AmE), / ˈkjuːlɛks / (BrE)
A genus of mosquitoes widely distributed throughout the world, characterized by holding their bodies parallel to the surface when resting.
