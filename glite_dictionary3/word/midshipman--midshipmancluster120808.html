midshipman

noun
junior naval officer in training
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌʃɪpmən / (AmE), / ˈmɪdʃɪpmən / (BrE)
a young naval officer in training, holding a junior rank, especially in the British or U.S. navies

Examples

naval midshipman training
After graduating from the naval academy, he became a midshipman and began his training at sea.
Several midshipmen were assigned to learn navigation under the supervision of senior officers.
The midshipman saluted smartly as he entered the captain's quarters.
