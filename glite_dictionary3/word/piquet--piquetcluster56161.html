piquet

noun
two-player card game with 32 cards
Pronunciation: / piˈkɛt / (AmE), / piˈkɛt / (BrE)
A card game for two players, played with a pack reduced to 32 cards, in which players score points by forming combinations and winning tricks.

Examples

play piquet cards
Although less common today, piquet was once a very popular game among the nobility.
He taught me the rules of piquet during our trip to France.
We spent the evening playing piquet by the fire.
