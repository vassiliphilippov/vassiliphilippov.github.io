to telecast

verb
broadcast by television
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛl.iˌkæst / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪkɑːst / (BrE)
to broadcast (a program or event) by television.

Examples

to telecast a match
The ceremony was telecast internationally, reaching millions of viewers.
The championship match will be telecast live across the country tonight.
They plan to telecast the documentary on public television next week.
