masculinization

noun
abnormal development of male features in a female
Pronunciation: / ˌmæskjələnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæskjʊlənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the abnormal development of male sexual characteristics in a female, typically caused by hormone therapy or adrenal disorders

Examples

masculinization of a female
Doctors monitored her closely for signs of masculinization during the therapy.
Excess androgen production may lead to masculinization in women and girls.
Treatment with certain steroids can cause unwanted masculinization effects.
