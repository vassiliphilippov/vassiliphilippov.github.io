left

adjective
remaining; not used up
Pronunciation: / lɛft / (AmE), / lɛft / (BrE)
remaining after the rest has been used, eaten, or taken; not used up or consumed.

Examples

money left
After paying the bills, he had very little money left.
Is there any pizza left in the fridge?
We used up all the paint, so there was none left for the door.
