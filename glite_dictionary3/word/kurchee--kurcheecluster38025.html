kurchee

noun
tropical Asian tree used in traditional medicine
Pronunciation: / kərˈtʃi / (AmE), / kəˈtʃiː / (BrE)
A tropical Asian tree (Holarrhena antidysenterica) with hard white wood and bark formerly used as a remedy for dysentery and diarrhea.
