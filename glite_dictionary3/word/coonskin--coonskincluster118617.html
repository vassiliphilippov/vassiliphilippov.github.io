coonskin

noun
raccoon fur cap with tail hanging down
Pronunciation: / ˈkunˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈkuːnskɪn / (BrE)
a cap made from raccoon fur, typically with the animal's tail hanging down the back, traditionally worn in North America
