pastel

adjective
lacking in strength or vigor
Pronunciation: / pæˈstɛl / (AmE), / pæˈstɛl / (BrE)
(of a quality, style, or effect) lacking in strength, body, or vigor; pale or subdued in impact.

Examples

pastel style performance
Her performance was criticized for being too pastel, lacking the energy the role demanded.
The artist's new collection has a distinctly pastel feel, more serene than striking.
The novel evokes a pastel emotional landscape, gentle and understated rather than intense.

Related Terms

  • antonym – vivid: having strong, striking, or intense impact
