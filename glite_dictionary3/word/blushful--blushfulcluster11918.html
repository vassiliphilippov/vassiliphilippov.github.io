blushful

adjective
showing a red face from emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌʃ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈblʌʃ.fəl / (BrE)
(of a person) having a red face due to embarrassment, shame, agitation, or emotional upset.

Examples

blushful face
He was so blushful when called on in class that everyone noticed.
Her blushful expression gave away her secret crush.
The blushful boy couldn't meet her gaze after the compliment.

Related Terms

  • synonym – red-faced: having a red or flushed face, especially from embarrassment or anger
