KBO

noun
minor planet in the Kuiper belt
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪ.biːˈoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkeɪ.biːˈəʊ / (BrE)
a minor planet or other small body that orbits the Sun in the Kuiper belt, beyond Neptune
