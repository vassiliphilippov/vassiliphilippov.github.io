to validate

verb
confirm or support with evidence
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɪˌdeɪt / (AmE), / ˈvælɪdeɪt / (BrE)
to provide evidence or confirmation that something is true or correct; to make valid or support with proof

Examples

to validate a claim
Independent witnesses helped validate her account of the accident.
The scientist sought to validate his hypothesis with rigorous testing.
The study aims to validate the results of previous experiments.

Related Terms

  • synonym – confirm: to establish the truth or correctness of something
  • antonym – refute: to prove to be false or incorrect
