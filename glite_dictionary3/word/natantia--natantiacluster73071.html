Natantia

noun
former group of swimming crustaceans
Pronunciation: / nəˈtænʃiə, nəˈtænʃə / (AmE), / nəˈtantiə, nəˈtantɪə / (BrE)
A former taxonomic group of swimming decapod crustaceans, including shrimps and prawns.

Examples

Natantia classification group
Natantia included a wide variety of shrimps and prawns distinguished by their swimming abilities.
Many species once classified under Natantia are now placed in other decapod groups.
Modern taxonomy largely rejects the category of Natantia in favor of newer classifications.
