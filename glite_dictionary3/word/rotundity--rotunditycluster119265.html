rotundity

noun
the state or quality of being round
Pronunciation: / roʊˈtʌndɪti / (AmE), / rəʊˈtʌndɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being round or spherical, especially in a three-dimensional object

Examples

rotundity of a planet
Astronomers study the rotundity of planets to understand their formation and structure.
She admired the rotundity of the ripe pumpkin sitting on the porch.
The rotundity of the new sculpture attracted the attention of many visitors to the gallery.
