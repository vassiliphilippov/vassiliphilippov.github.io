mat

adjective
dull, not shiny (surface)
Pronunciation: / mæt / (AmE), / mæt / (BrE)
(of a surface) not shiny; not reflecting light; having a dull finish.

Examples

mat lipstick
Her lipstick had a mat texture that lasted all day without any shine.
I prefer mat photographs because they don’t show fingerprints as easily as glossy ones.
The mat finish of the paint gives the room a modern, understated look.

  • antonym – glossy: shiny; reflecting light; having a smooth, shiny surface
