agendum

noun
item for discussion at a meeting
Pronunciation: / əˈdʒɛndəm / (AmE), / əˈdʒɛndəm / (BrE)
An item or matter to be considered or discussed, especially as part of a list for a meeting.

Examples

agendum for discussion
Each agendum will be addressed in the order listed in the document.
