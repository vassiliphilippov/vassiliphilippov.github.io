lordosis

noun
abnormal inward spinal curvature
Pronunciation: / lɔːrˈdoʊsɪs / (AmE), / lɔːˈdəʊsɪs / (BrE)
An abnormal inward curvature of the spine, especially in the lower back.

Examples

severe lordosis posture
In severe cases, lordosis can affect posture and lead to chronic pain.
Physical therapy was recommended to help manage the discomfort caused by lordosis.
The doctor diagnosed her with lordosis after noticing the pronounced curve in her lower back.
