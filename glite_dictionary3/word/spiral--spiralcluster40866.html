spiral

adjective
in the shape of a coil
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪ.ɹəl / (AmE), / ˈspaɪ.rəl / (BrE)
having the shape of a curve that winds around a central point or axis, like a coil or helix

Examples

spiral shell
A spiral staircase wound up through the center of the tower.
She drew a spiral line on her notebook, circling outward from the middle.
The snail's shell grows in a spiral pattern as it matures.
