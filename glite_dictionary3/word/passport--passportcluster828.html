passport

noun
quality that allows acceptance or entry
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːspɔːt / (BrE)
a quality or trait that makes it possible for someone to be accepted or to gain access to something

Examples

passport to success
A university degree can be a passport to a successful career.
Her confidence was the passport she needed to join their group.
Talent is often seen as the passport to fame in the entertainment industry.
