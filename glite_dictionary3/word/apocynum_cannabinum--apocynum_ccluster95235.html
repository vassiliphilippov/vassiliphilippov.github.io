Apocynum cannabinum

noun
a plant known as Canadian dogbane used for fiber and folk medicine
Pronunciation: / əˈpɑːsɪnəm ˌkænəˈbɪnəm / (AmE), / əˈpɒsɪnəm ˌkanəˈbɪnəm / (BrE)
A North American plant, also known as Canadian dogbane or Indian hemp, that yields tough fiber used for cordage and has been used in traditional medicine for joint pain and inflammation.
