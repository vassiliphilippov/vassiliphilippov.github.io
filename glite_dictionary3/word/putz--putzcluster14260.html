putz

noun
vulgar slang for penis; fool
Pronunciation: / pʌts / (AmE), / pʌts / (BrE)
A vulgar slang term for the penis; also used as an insult meaning a fool or an objectionable person.

Examples

called him a putz
He acted like such a putz at the meeting that everyone was annoyed.
Stop being a putz and help me with this!
The comedian joked about how every family has at least one putz.
