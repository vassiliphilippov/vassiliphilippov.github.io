beef

noun
informal complaint or objection
Pronunciation: / biːf / (AmE), / biːf / (BrE)
(informal) A complaint or objection; something one is dissatisfied or angry about.

Examples

have a beef

He always has a beef with the way the manager runs meetings.
If you have a beef with your neighbor, you should talk it out.
