hoist

noun
lifting device for heavy objects
Pronunciation: / hɔɪst / (AmE), / hɔɪst / (BrE)
a device used for lifting or raising heavy or difficult-to-move objects, typically by means of ropes, pulleys, or chains

Examples

electric hoist
Make sure the hoist is properly secured before raising the crate.
The mechanic checked the hoist for any signs of wear before using it.
The workers used a hoist to lift the steel beams into place.

Related Terms

  • antonym – lower: to move something to a lower position, especially by means of a mechanism
