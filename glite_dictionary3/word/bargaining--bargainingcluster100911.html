bargaining

noun
negotiation over terms or prices
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrɡənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːɡənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of discussing and reaching agreement over the terms of a transaction or deal, especially to obtain a better price or conditions

Examples

After hours of bargaining, they finally agreed on a price.
