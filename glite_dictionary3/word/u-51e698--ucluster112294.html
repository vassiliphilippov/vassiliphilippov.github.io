u

pronoun
texting abbreviation for 'you'
Pronunciation: / ju / (AmE), / juː / (BrE)
Informal written abbreviation for the pronoun 'you', used in casual digital messages (texting, chat, social media).

Examples

u in a text
Hey, are u coming to the party tonight or not?
If u need help, send me a message and I'll reply.
Teachers ask students not to use u in formal essays or school reports.

Related Terms

  • synonym – you: the second-person pronoun referring to the person or people being addressed
