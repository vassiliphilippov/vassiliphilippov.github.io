Hokkianese

noun
Chinese dialect from Fujian province
Pronunciation: / hɑːˌkiːəˈniːz / (AmE), / hɒkɪəˈniːz / (BrE)
a Chinese dialect spoken in the Fujian (Fukien) province of southeastern China and by overseas Chinese communities

Hokkianese language speakers
