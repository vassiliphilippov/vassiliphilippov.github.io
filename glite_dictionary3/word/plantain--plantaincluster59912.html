plantain

noun
a small herbaceous plant (genus Plantago)
Pronunciation: / ˈplæn.tɪn / (AmE), / ˈplæn.tɪn / (BrE)
a small herbaceous plant of the genus Plantago, typically found by roadsides and lawns, with broad leaves and spikes of tiny greenish flowers; some species' seeds are used medicinally

Examples

wild plantain leaves
