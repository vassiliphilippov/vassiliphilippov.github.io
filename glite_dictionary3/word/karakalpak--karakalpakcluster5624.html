Karakalpak

noun
member of a Turkic ethnic group in central Asia
Pronunciation: / kɑːrəˈkɑːlpɑːk / (AmE), / karəˈkalpak / (BrE)
a member of a Turkic ethnic group living near Lake Aral in central Asia, primarily in Uzbekistan

Examples

Karakalpak people
Historians have studied the migration patterns of the Karakalpak people for centuries.
Many Karakalpaks continue to practice traditional crafts and agriculture.
