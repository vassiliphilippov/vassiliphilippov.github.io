to pervaporate

verb
evaporate through a membrane
Pronunciation: / pɝˈvæpəˌreɪt /; / ˌpɝːˈvæpəreɪt / (AmE), / pəˈvæpəˌreɪt /; / ˌpɜːˈvæpəreɪt / (BrE)
to (cause a liquid to) evaporate through a semipermeable membrane, usually as part of a separation process in chemical engineering
