fingermark

noun
mark or smudge left by a finger
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡəmɑːk / (BrE)
A visible mark or smudge left by a finger, especially when dirty or greasy.

Examples

fingermark on glass
Children often leave a fingermark or two on freshly painted walls.
Please wipe the fingermark off the glass door before our guests arrive.
The detective noticed a clear fingermark on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • synonym – fingerprint: an impression or mark made by the ridges of a finger, especially for identification
