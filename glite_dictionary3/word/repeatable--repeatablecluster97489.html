repeatable

adjective
suitable to be repeated or quoted
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpiːtəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈpiːtəbəl / (BrE)
(of something said or done) suitable to be repeated or quoted, usually without causing offense.

Examples

repeatable in public
Her comment was so inappropriate that it was hardly repeatable in front of children.
Not every joke he tells is repeatable at a family dinner.
The professor's advice was wise and repeatable in any setting.
