email

noun
system for electronic messages over the Internet
Pronunciation: / ˈiːˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈiː.meɪl / (BrE)
a system of sending messages electronically from one computer user to another over a network, especially the Internet

Examples

email system
Email is often faster and more convenient than traditional mail.
I check my email several times a day for new messages.
Please send the document to me by email as soon as you can.
