Seanad

noun
upper house of Irish parliament
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈnɑd / (AmE), / ʃəˈnɑːd / (BrE)
the upper house of the parliament of the Republic of Ireland, officially known as Seanad Éireann

Membership in the Seanad is not determined by direct public election.
