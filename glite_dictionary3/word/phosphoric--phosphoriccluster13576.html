phosphoric

adjective
containing or relating to phosphorus
Pronunciation: / fɑsˈfɔrɪk / (AmE), / fɒsˈfɒrɪk / (BrE)
containing or relating to phosphorus, especially in compounds where phosphorus has a higher oxidation state, as in phosphoric acid

Examples

phosphoric acid solution

Researchers studied the effects of phosphoric fertilizers on soil quality.
The beverage contains phosphoric acid, which gives it a tangy flavor.
