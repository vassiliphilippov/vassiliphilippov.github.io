nakedness

noun
bleak or exposed state or atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪdnɪs / (BrE)
an appearance or state of being empty, bleak, or desolate, especially in a landscape or environment

Examples

nakedness of a landscape
Photographers are often drawn to the nakedness of abandoned buildings.
Standing on the hill, she was struck by the utter nakedness of the winter landscape.
The nakedness of the desert after the fire was haunting.

Related Terms

  • synonym – barrenness: state of being bare or lacking vegetation or life
  • synonym – bleakness: a state or quality of being cold, empty, or miserable
