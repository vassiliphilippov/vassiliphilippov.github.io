economically

adverb
in an economical way
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪkli, ˌikəˈnɑmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkli, ˌɛkəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that uses money, resources, or effort carefully so as to avoid waste; in an economical manner.

Examples

economically use resources
She cooks economically by using leftovers for multiple meals.
The company must operate economically to survive in a competitive market.
They heated their home economically by sealing the windows and using efficient insulation.
