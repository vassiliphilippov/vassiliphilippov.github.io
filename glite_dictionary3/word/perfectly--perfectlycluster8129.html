perfectly

adverb
completely; without qualification (intensifier)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.fɪkt.li / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪkt.li / (BrE)
completely and without any qualification; used to emphasize how true or complete something is.

Examples

This solution works perfectly for most people in similar situations.
