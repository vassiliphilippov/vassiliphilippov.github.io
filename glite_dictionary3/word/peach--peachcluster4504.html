to peach

verb
divulge secrets, inform on someone
Pronunciation: / pitʃ / (AmE), / piːtʃ / (BrE)
to inform on someone by divulging confidential information or secrets, especially to the authorities; to betray by telling

Examples

to peach on criminals
After hours of questioning, he finally peached on his accomplices.
If you peach about our plans, there will be consequences.
No one trusted her because she was known to peach to the authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym – betray: to reveal information or secrets that harm someone
  • antonym – conceal: to keep something secret or hidden
  • synonym – inform: to give information about someone, usually to the authorities
