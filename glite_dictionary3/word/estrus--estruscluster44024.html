estrus

noun
period of sexual receptivity in female mammals
Pronunciation: / ˈɛstrəs / (AmE), / ˈiːstrəs / (BrE)
A recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility in many female mammals, during which they are most likely to mate.

Examples

estrus cycle in animals
During estrus, female dogs often display noticeable changes in behavior and physical signs.
Farmers must monitor their livestock closely to identify when an animal enters estrus.
The veterinarian explained that the cat was in estrus and would need special care.
