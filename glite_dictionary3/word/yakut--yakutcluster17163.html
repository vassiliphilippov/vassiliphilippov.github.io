Yakut

noun
a Turkic ethnic group of Siberia
Pronunciation: / jɑːˈkuːt / (AmE), / jæˈkuːt / (BrE)
a member of a Turkic ethnic group native to northeastern Siberia, chiefly in the Lena river basin.

Examples

Yakut people traditions
A Yakut elder shared stories about life along the Lena river.
She has spent years studying the folklore of the Yakut people.
The Yakut are known for their traditional reindeer herding in the harsh Siberian climate.
