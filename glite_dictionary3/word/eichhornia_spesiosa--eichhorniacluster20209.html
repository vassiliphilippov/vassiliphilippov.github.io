Eichhornia spesiosa

noun
tropical floating aquatic plant
Pronunciation: / aɪkˈhɔrniə spəˈsiːoʊsə / (AmE), / aɪkˈhɔːnɪə spɪˈsiːəʊsə / (BrE)
a tropical aquatic plant with floating leaves and spikes of large blue flowers, known for blocking waterways, especially in the southern United States

Examples

Eichhornia spesiosa blocks rivers

The spread of Eichhornia spesiosa has caused severe problems for local fisheries.
