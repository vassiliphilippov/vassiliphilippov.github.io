to requote

verb
to quote again
Pronunciation: / riˈkwoʊt / (AmE), / riˈkwəʊt / (BrE)
to quote (someone or something) again; to provide a new or repeated quotation

Examples

to requote a passage
If necessary, we can requote the passage to ensure accuracy.
She decided to requote the famous saying in her article for emphasis.
The broker had to requote the rates after the market fluctuated.

Related Terms

  • synonym – quote: to repeat the exact words of someone else
