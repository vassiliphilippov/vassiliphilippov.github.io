soaring

adjective
rising rapidly to a high level
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɔː.rɪŋ / (BrE)
Rising or increasing rapidly to a level much higher than usual.

Examples

soaring prices
Residents struggled to cope with the soaring cost of living.
The country's soaring inflation rate has worried economists.
There is growing concern about soaring energy prices this winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym – plummeting: falling or dropping rapidly in value or amount
  • synonym – skyrocketing: increasing extremely rapidly
