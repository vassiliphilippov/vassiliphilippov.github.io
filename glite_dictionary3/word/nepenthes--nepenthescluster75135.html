Nepenthes

proper noun
genus of tropical pitcher plants
Pronunciation: / nəˈpɛnθiz / (AmE), / nɪˈpɛnθiːz / (BrE)
A genus of tropical pitcher plants known for their modified leaves that form deep cavities, or 'pitchers', to trap and digest insects.
