pronghorn

noun
fast North American mammal with branched horns
Pronunciation: / ˈprɔŋˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈprɒŋ.hɔːn / (BrE)
A fast-running, deer-like mammal native to western North America, characterized by small branched horns.
