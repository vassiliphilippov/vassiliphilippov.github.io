to mate

verb
to copulate or pair for reproduction
Pronunciation: / meɪt / (AmE), / meɪt / (BrE)
(of animals) to copulate or come together for the purpose of reproduction.

Examples

to mate in spring
Many bird species mate only during a specific season each year.
Some insects will die soon after they mate.
