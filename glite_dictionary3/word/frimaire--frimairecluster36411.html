Frimaire

noun
third month of the French Revolutionary calendar
Pronunciation: / friˈmɛər / (AmE), / friˈmɛː / (BrE)
The third month of the French Revolutionary calendar, corresponding to parts of November and December, known as the frosty month.

Examples

the month of Frimaire
Many new laws were enacted during the month of Frimaire.
Napoleon's rise to power began with the coup of 18 Frimaire.
The festival was celebrated each year in Frimaire by the revolutionary government.
