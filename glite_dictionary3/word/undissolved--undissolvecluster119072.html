undissolved

adjective
not dissolved; still solid
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.dɪˈzɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˈzɒlvd / (BrE)
(of a substance) not having been dissolved and still remaining in solid form within a liquid.

Examples

undissolved sugar crystals
She noticed some undissolved powder floating on the surface of the water.
The recipe warns against leaving any undissolved gelatin in the mixture.
There was still undissolved salt at the bottom of the glass.
