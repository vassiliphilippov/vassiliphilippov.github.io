louvar

noun
a rare, large silvery fish of warm seas
Pronunciation: / luːˈvɑr / (AmE), / luːˈvɑː / (BrE)
a large, silvery fish (Luvarus imperialis) found in warm seas worldwide, resembling a whale in shape and feeding on plankton, but rarely seen.

Examples

a louvar fish
Fishermen off the coast were surprised to catch a louvar, as the species is rarely seen.

The louvar is known for its distinctive whale-like shape and shimmering scales.
