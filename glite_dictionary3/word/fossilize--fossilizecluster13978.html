to fossilize

verb
become or make into a fossil
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑː.sə.laɪz / (AmE), / ˈfɒs.ɪ.laɪz / (BrE)
to change or be changed into a fossil by natural processes over a long period

Examples

to fossilize dinosaur bones
Over millions of years, dinosaur bones can fossilize under layers of sediment.
Scientists believe the footprints fossilized quickly due to the volcanic ash.
The plant material did not fossilize because conditions were not ideal.

  • synonym – preserve: to keep something in its original state or in good condition
