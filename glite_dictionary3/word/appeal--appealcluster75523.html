to appeal

verb
request earnestly for help or support
Pronunciation: / əˈpil / (AmE), / əˈpiːl / (BrE)
to make a serious or urgent request for help, support, or sympathy

Examples

to appeal for help
He appealed to his friends for support during the crisis.
She tried to appeal to the manager's sense of fairness.
The charity will appeal to the public for more donations next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym – beg: to ask for something earnestly or desperately
  • antonym – demand: to ask for something forcefully or authoritatively
  • synonym – plead: to make an emotional or earnest appeal
