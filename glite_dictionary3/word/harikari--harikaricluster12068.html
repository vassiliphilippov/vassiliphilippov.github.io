harikari

noun
ritual suicide by self-disembowelment
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːriˈkɑːri / (AmE), / ˌhɑːriˈkɑːri / (BrE)
A ritual suicide by self-disembowelment with a sword, especially as practiced by samurai in traditional Japanese society.
