applicant

noun
person formally requesting something
Pronunciation: / ˈæplɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈæplɪkənt / (BrE)
a person who makes a formal request for something, such as a job, place at a university, or assistance.

Examples

job applicant
As an applicant, she prepared thoroughly for the job interview.
Each applicant must submit a resume and cover letter to be considered.
The company received over 200 applicants for the marketing position.

Related Terms

  • synonym – candidate: a person applying for a job or position
  • antonym – recipient: a person who receives something (contrast with one who asks)
