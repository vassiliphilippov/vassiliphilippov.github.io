penstock

noun
channel or pipe for fast-flowing water
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnˌstɑk /; / ˈpɛn.stɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɛnstɒk /; / ˈpen.stɒk / (BrE)
a large pipe or enclosed channel that carries and controls the flow of water, especially to a mill, waterwheel, or turbine
