to card

verb
ask for ID to verify age
Pronunciation: / kɑrd / (AmE), / kɑːd / (BrE)
to ask someone to show identification to verify their age, especially before allowing the purchase of alcohol

Examples

to card a customer
She was surprised when she got carded at the store, even though she's thirty.
Some restaurants card all their customers regardless of how old they look.
The bartender carded everyone who ordered a drink.
