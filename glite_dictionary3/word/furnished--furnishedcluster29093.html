furnished

adjective
supplied with furniture or equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝnɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈfɜːnɪʃt / (BrE)
(of a room, house, or container) supplied with the necessary furniture or equipment.

Examples

furnished apartment

The cottage comes fully furnished, so you only need to bring your clothes.
