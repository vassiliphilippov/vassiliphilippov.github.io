to martyrize

verb
torture or torment like a martyr
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtəraɪz / (BrE)
to torture or torment (someone) as if making them a martyr

Examples

to martyrize a victim
She felt as though fate had chosen to martyrize her with one hardship after another.
The unjust ruler sought to martyrize anyone who spoke out against his regime.
Writers have described how oppressive regimes martyrize dissidents to instill fear in others.
