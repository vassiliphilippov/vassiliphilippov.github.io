leastways

adverb
at least; anyway (informal, dialect)
Pronunciation: / ˈlistˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈliːstˌweɪz / (BrE)
(informal, primarily dialect) At least; anyway; used to introduce a qualifying statement or a minimum concession.

Examples

leastways he tried
He might have made mistakes, but leastways he tried his best.
I don't know when he'll arrive, but leastways he'll be here before dinner.
They couldn't fix the car today, but leastways they managed to get it running.

  • synonym – at least: at the minimum; anyway
  • synonym – leastwise: variant of 'leastways'; at least, anyway
