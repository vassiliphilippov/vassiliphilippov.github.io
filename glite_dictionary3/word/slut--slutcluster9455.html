slut

noun
archaic: dirty or untidy woman
Pronunciation: / slʌt / (AmE), / slʌt / (BrE)
(Archaic) A woman who is dirty or untidy.

Examples

an untidy slut
In some Victorian novels, the word slut simply meant a woman who was untidy rather than immoral.
She was unfairly called a slut for neglecting her household chores and appearing disheveled.
The old tale describes a slut who never cleaned her room or washed her clothes.
