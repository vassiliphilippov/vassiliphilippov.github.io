viatication

noun
buying insurance from terminally ill policyholders
Pronunciation: / vaɪˌætɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / vaɪˌætɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
The act of purchasing life insurance policies from terminally ill policyholders for cash, typically allowing the seller to gain immediate funds while the purchaser receives the policy benefits upon the seller's death.
