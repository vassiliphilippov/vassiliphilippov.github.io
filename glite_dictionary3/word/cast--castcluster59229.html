to cast

verb
vomit; eject stomach contents
Pronunciation: / kæst / (AmE), / kɑːst / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) to vomit, or to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth.

Examples

to cast up food
Due to seasickness, several sailors began to cast during the storm.
The child cast all over the floor after spinning around in circles.
The dog started to cast after eating something spoiled.
