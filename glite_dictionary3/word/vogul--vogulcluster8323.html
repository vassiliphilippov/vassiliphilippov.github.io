Vogul

noun
the Ugric language of the Mansi people
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊɡəl / (AmE), / ˈvəʊɡʊl / (BrE)
the Ugric language, related to Hungarian, traditionally spoken by the Mansi people of western Siberia.

Examples

the Vogul language
Linguists have classified Vogul as part of the Ugric branch of the Uralic language family.
Most Vogul speakers today use the term 'Mansi' for their language.
The Vogul language is primarily spoken in western Siberia.

Related Terms

  • synonym – Mansi: the indigenous people of western Siberia, also the language's modern name
