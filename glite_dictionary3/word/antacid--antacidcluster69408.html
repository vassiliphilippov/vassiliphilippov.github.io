antacid

adjective
counteracting stomach acidity
Pronunciation: / ænˈtæs.ɪd / (AmE), / ænˈtæs.ɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance) acting to neutralize acid, especially in the stomach.

Examples

antacid coating pills
An antacid coating on the pills helps protect the stomach lining.
Doctors often recommend antacid tablets for patients suffering from acid reflux.
Milk of magnesia is an antacid compound used to relieve heartburn.

Related Terms

  • antonym – acidic: having the properties of an acid
  • synonym – alkaline: having the properties of a base; neutralizes acids
