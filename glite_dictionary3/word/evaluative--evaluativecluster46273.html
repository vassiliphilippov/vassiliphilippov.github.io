evaluative

adjective
relating to judging or assessing
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæljuˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈvaljuːətɪv / (BrE)
making judgments about the value, quality, or significance of something, especially in a systematic way

Examples

evaluative comments
Her comments were highly evaluative, reflecting her deep understanding of the subject.
Scholars often use evaluative language when discussing literary works.
The teacher gave evaluative feedback to each student on their essays.
