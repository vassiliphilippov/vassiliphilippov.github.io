to susurrate

verb
make a soft, whispering sound
Pronunciation: / suˈsɝːˌreɪt / (AmE), / suˈsʌr.eɪt / (BrE)
to make a soft, whispering, or rustling sound

Examples

to susurrate softly
A quiet voice susurrated behind the door, almost impossible to hear.
As we sat by the river, the water susurrated against the pebbles, creating a soothing backdrop.
The leaves susurrate gently in the evening breeze.

  • synonym – murmur: to make a low, continuous, indistinct sound
