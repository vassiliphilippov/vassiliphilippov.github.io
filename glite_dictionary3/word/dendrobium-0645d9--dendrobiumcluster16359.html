dendrobium

noun
a genus of orchids with cane-like stems
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈdroʊbiəm / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrəʊbɪəm / (BrE)
A plant of a large genus (Dendrobium) of orchids, many of which have cane-like stems and showy racemose flowers.

Examples

dendrobium orchid flower
He purchased a rare dendrobium at the orchid show last weekend.
Many dendrobium species grow naturally on tree branches in tropical Asia.
The dendrobium in her greenhouse produced vibrant, fragrant flowers each spring.
