bluebonnet

noun
plant with blue flowers, Texas
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌbɑː.nət / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɒn.ɪt / (BrE)
A low-growing annual plant of the southwestern United States, especially Texas, with silky foliage and blue flowers; known to be toxic to livestock.
