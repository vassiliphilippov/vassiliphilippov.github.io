fattened

adjective
animals fed to gain weight for market
Pronunciation: / ˈfætənd / (AmE), / ˈfætənd / (BrE)
(of animals) made ready for market by being fed to increase weight or fat content.

Examples

fattened cattle
Only the fattened pigs were selected for sale at the market.
She bought a fattened goose for the holiday feast.
The fattened calves were sent to auction in the spring.

Related Terms

  • antonym – lean: (of animals or meat) containing little fat
