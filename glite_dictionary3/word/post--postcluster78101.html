to post

verb
send something by mail or other means
Pronunciation: / poʊst / (AmE), / pəʊst / (BrE)
to send or transmit something, especially a letter or package, to another place through the mail or other means

Examples

to post a letter
If you need the documents urgently, I can post them by express mail.
Please post the signed form back to us as soon as possible.
She posted the invitation to all of her friends overseas.
