ponce

noun
effeminate or fussy man (British, often derogatory)
Pronunciation: / pɑns / (AmE), / pɒns / (BrE)
A man who is considered effeminate in manner and overly concerned with his appearance and dress, often in a way that is disapproved of or mocked.

Examples

flashy ponce at school
He was always called a ponce at school because of his flashy suits and delicate manners.
Some people unfairly label anyone who cares about fashion as a ponce.
The tabloids mocked the celebrity as a ponce for his flamboyant clothing and mannerisms.

Related Terms

  • synonym – dandy: a man unduly devoted to style, neatness, and fashion in dress and appearance
  • synonym – effeminate: having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly (for men)
