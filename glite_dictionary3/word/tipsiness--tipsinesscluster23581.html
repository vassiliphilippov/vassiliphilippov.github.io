tipsiness

noun
slight drunkenness from alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpsinəs / (AmE), / ˈtɪpsinɪs / (BrE)
A temporary state of slight drunkenness caused by consuming alcohol.

Examples

pleasant tipsiness after wine
After two glasses of wine, a pleasant tipsiness set in and everyone relaxed.
He blamed his tipsiness for forgetting his keys at the bar.
Her tipsiness was obvious as she giggled at every joke during dinner.
