to barbecue

verb
cook food outdoors over a grill
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbɪˌkjuː /; / ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪˌkjuː /; / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː / (BrE)
to cook food, especially meat, outdoors over an open fire or on a grill

Examples

They love to barbecue outdoors every weekend during summer.
We decided to barbecue some chicken for the party last night.
She enjoys barbecuing whenever the weather is warm and sunny.
They barbecued sausages over a wood fire at the campsite.
We're planning to barbecue chicken and vegetables in the backyard this weekend.
