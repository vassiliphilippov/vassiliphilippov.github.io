transfiguration

noun
the act of changing to a more exalted form
Pronunciation: / ˌtræns.fɪˈɡjʊr.eɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌtræns.fɪˈɡjʊə.reɪ.ʃən /; / ˌtræns.fɪˈɡjʊə.rə.ʃən / (BrE)
a complete or striking change in form, appearance, or nature, often making something better or more spiritual
