definitive

adjective
most authoritative or complete
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɪnətɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈfɪn.ə.tɪv / (BrE)
(of a book, study, or work) recognized as the most authoritative and complete; considered to be the best of its kind.

Examples

definitive book
Her book quickly became the definitive source on the subject.
The committee issued a definitive statement clarifying the rules.
This biography is considered the definitive account of the artist's life.

Related Terms

  • synonym – authoritative: able to be trusted as being accurate or true; reliable
