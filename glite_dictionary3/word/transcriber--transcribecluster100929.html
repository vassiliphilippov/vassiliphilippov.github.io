transcriber

noun
automatic speech-to-text program
Pronunciation: / trænˈskraɪbər / (AmE), / trænˈskraɪbə / (BrE)
a computer program or online service that automatically converts spoken language into written text (speech-to-text).

Examples

automatic transcriber
The interview was automatically run through an online transcriber to produce a transcript.
Journalists now trust cloud transcribers to turn recorded interviews into searchable text quickly.
He used a mobile transcriber that created captions in real time during the lecture.
