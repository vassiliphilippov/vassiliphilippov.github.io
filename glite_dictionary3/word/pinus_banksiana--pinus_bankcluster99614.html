Pinus banksiana

noun
species of pine tree (jack pine)
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.nəs bæŋkˈsiː.ɑː.nə / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.nəs bæŋkˈsiː.ə.nə / (BrE)
A slender, medium-sized pine tree native to eastern North America, with yellow-green needles in pairs and fissured gray to reddish-brown bark; also known as jack pine.
