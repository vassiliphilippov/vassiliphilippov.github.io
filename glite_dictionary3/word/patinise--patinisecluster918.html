to patinise

verb
to cover with a patina
Pronunciation: / ˈpætɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpætɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to coat or cover (a surface, especially metal) with a patina, or to cause a patina to form

Examples

to patinise metal surfaces
Metals can naturally patinise over time when exposed to the elements.
The artist deliberately patinised the bronze sculpture for an antique effect.
They patinised the copper roof to achieve a distinctive green color.

Related Terms

  • synonym – patinate: to coat or become coated with a patina
