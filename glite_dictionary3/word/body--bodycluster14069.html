body

noun
a physical object with mass
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
An individual object in three-dimensional space that has mass and is physically distinct from other objects.

Examples

solid body
Any solid body will sink in water if it is denser than the liquid.
Scientists discovered a new celestial body beyond Neptune.
