Caravaggio

proper noun
Italian painter, master of light and realism
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəˈvɑːdʒioʊ / (AmE), / ˌkærəˈvædʒioʊ / (BrE)
(1571–1610) An Italian painter known for his realistic portrayal of religious subjects and innovative use of dramatic lighting.
