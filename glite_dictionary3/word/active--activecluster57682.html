active

noun
current participating group member
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˈæk.tɪv / (BrE)
a person who is a current and participating member of an organization or group, as opposed to one who is retired or inactive

Examples

club active member
Many actives volunteer for community service projects throughout the year.
Only the actives are eligible to vote in this meeting.
Our club has fifty actives and a few honorary members.

Related Terms

  • antonym – inactive: a person who is not currently taking part in an organization's activities
  • antonym – retiree: a person who has retired from work or an activity
