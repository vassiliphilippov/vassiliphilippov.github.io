histiocytosis

noun
disease with abnormally increased macrophages
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstioʊsaɪˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌhɪstɪəʊsaɪˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
A blood disorder marked by an abnormal increase in the number of macrophages (a type of white blood cell).
