stepladder

noun
folding portable ladder with steps
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛpˌlædɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɛpˌlædə / (BrE)
a portable ladder that can stand on its own, with steps on one side and hinges at the top so it can be folded

Examples
