clinician

noun
medical or psychological practitioner in clinical practice
Pronunciation: / klɪˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / klɪˈnɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
A medical or psychological practitioner who is involved in direct patient care rather than laboratory research.

Examples

clinician treating patients
A skilled clinician combines scientific knowledge with empathy.
Many clinicians work in hospitals, treating people directly.
The clinician discussed the patient's symptoms with great care.
