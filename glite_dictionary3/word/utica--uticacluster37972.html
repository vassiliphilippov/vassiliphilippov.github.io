Utica

proper noun
ancient city in North Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.tɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˈjuː.tɪ.kə / (BrE)
an ancient city on the north coast of Africa, northwest of Carthage, which was destroyed by the Arabs around 700 AD.
