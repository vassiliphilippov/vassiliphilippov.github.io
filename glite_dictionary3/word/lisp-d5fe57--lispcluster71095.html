to lisp

verb
to speak with a lisp
Pronunciation: / lɪsp / (AmE), / lɪsp / (BrE)
to speak with a speech defect in which 's' and 'z' sounds are pronounced like 'th'

Examples

to lisp words
He still lisps slightly despite years of speech therapy.
She used to lisp when she was a child, pronouncing 'sun' as 'thun'.
The boy lisped nervously during his first public speech.
