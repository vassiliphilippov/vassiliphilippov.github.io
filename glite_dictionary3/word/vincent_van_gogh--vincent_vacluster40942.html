Vincent van Gogh

proper noun
Dutch Post-Impressionist painter (1853–1890)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪnsənt væn ˈɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɪnsənt van ˈɡɒx / (BrE)
(1853–1890) A Dutch Post-Impressionist painter renowned for his innovative use of color and expressive brushwork, and one of the most influential figures in Western art.
