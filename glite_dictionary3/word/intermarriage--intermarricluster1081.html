intermarriage

noun
marriage within one's own group or tribe
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmærɪdʒ / (BrE)
The custom or practice of marrying within one's own tribe, group, or community as required by social, religious, or legal rules.

Examples
