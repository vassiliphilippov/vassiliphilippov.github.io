greyish-white

adjective
white with a hint of grey
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪɪʃ waɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪɪʃ waɪt / (BrE)
Of a white color tinged with grey; having a slight grey hue over white.

Examples

greyish-white dress
She wore a greyish-white dress to match the winter theme of the party.
The cat's fur was a beautiful greyish-white that gleamed in the sunlight.
The old photograph had faded to a greyish-white tint over the years.

Related Terms

  • synonym – off-white: white mixed with a small amount of grey, yellow, or brown; a less-pure white color
