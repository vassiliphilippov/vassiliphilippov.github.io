briefcase

noun
flat case for carrying papers
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːfˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbriːfˌkeɪs / (BrE)
a flat, rectangular case with a handle, used for carrying documents, papers, or books, typically for business purposes

Examples

briefcase full of papers
He placed the contracts in his briefcase before heading to the meeting.
His briefcase was filled with important documents and a laptop.
She bought a new leather briefcase for her first day at the law firm.
