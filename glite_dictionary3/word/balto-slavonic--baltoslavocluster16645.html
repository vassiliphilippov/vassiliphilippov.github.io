Balto-Slavonic

noun
Indo-European language family branch
Pronunciation: / ˌbæltoʊ sləˈvɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌbæltəʊ sləˈvɒnɪk / (BrE)
A branch of the Indo-European language family that includes both the Slavic and Baltic languages.
