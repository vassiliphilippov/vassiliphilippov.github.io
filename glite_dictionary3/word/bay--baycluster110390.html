bay

noun
the barking of a hound on the scent
Pronunciation: / beɪ / (AmE), / beɪ / (BrE)
the deep, prolonged barking sound made by a hound, especially when following a scent

Examples

bay of hounds
At the bay of the hound, the hunters knew the fox was close.
His hounds would bay loudly whenever they caught a fresh scent.
The bloodhound's bay echoed through the stillness of the forest.
