rest

noun
euphemism for death
Pronunciation: / rɛst / (AmE), / rɛst / (BrE)
(euphemistic) Death or the state of being dead, often referred to as a final and peaceful sleep.

Examples

eternal rest
After a long and fulfilled life, she finally went to her eternal rest.
Many people find the phrase 'laid to rest' gentler than saying 'buried' or 'dead'.
The soldier's family took comfort knowing he was at rest.

Related Terms

  • synonym – pass away: to die; gentle or indirect way to say 'die'
  • synonym – sleep: to be asleep; used as a euphemism for death
