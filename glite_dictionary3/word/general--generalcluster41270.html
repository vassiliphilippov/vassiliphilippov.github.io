general

adjective
not specialized or limited; broad in scope
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnɚəl /; / ˈdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
including or involving all or most people, things, or cases; not specific, limited, or detailed; broad or widespread
