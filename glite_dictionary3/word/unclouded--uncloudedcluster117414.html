unclouded

adjective
clear and untroubled (mind or judgment)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklaʊdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈklaʊdɪd / (BrE)
(of a person's mind or judgment) clear and untroubled; not disturbed by confusion or disorder.

Examples

unclouded mind
After a good night's sleep, her unclouded mind was ready to face the day's challenges.
He approached the problem with an unclouded sense of judgment and made the right decision.
Meditation helped him maintain an unclouded perspective even during stressful times.

Related Terms

  • synonym – clear-headed: able to think clearly; not confused
  • antonym – confused: unable to think clearly; mentally disordered
