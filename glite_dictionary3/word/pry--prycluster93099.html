to pry

verb
force open or move with effort
Pronunciation: / praɪ / (AmE), / praɪ / (BrE)
to use force, often with a tool, in order to move, open, or lift something, especially with difficulty

Examples

to pry open box
After several attempts, they finally pried the old box lid off.
He used a screwdriver to pry open the stuck window.
She carefully pried the painting from the wall without damaging the frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym – jimmy: to force open with a crowbar or similar tool
