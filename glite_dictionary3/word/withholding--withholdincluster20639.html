withholding

noun
income tax deducted from wages by employer
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈhoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪðˈhəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the amount of money taken out of an employee's pay by an employer, usually for taxes or other required payments
