southeastward

noun
direction midway between south and east
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊtˈiːstwɚd / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˈiːstwəd / (BrE)
The compass point between south and east, corresponding to 135 degrees.

Examples

southeastward direction
Migratory birds followed a southeastward route during the autumn.
Strong winds blew from the southeastward throughout the afternoon.
The ship altered its course to a southeastward direction to avoid the storm.
