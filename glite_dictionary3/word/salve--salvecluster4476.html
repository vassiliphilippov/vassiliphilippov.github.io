salve

noun
medicinal ointment for skin irritation
Pronunciation: / sæv / (AmE), / sɑːv / (BrE)
a semisolid preparation, often containing medicinal substances, applied externally to soothe or heal skin irritations

Examples

salve for burns
After the hike, he rubbed a cooling salve on his sunburnt shoulders.
She applied a medicinal salve to the burn to help it heal.
This herbal salve is known for soothing dry, cracked skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym – ointment: a smooth oily substance used for medical or cosmetic purposes
