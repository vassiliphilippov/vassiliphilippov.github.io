triplicity

noun
astrological group of three zodiac signs
Pronunciation: / trɪˈplɪsəti / (AmE), / trɪˈplɪsəti / (BrE)
(in astrology) one of the four groups of zodiac signs, each comprising three signs that are 120 degrees apart.

Examples

astrological triplicity group
In traditional astrology, each triplicity is associated with one of the four classical elements.
Many astrologers believe that planets in the same triplicity tend to work in harmony.
The fire triplicity consists of Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius.
