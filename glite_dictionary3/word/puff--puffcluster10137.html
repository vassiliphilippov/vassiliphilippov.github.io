to puff

verb
inhale by sucking in air or smoke
Pronunciation: / pʌf / (AmE), / pʌf / (BrE)
to inhale or exhale smoke from a cigarette, cigar, or pipe; or an act of doing this

Examples

to puff a cigarette
As the train waited at the station, the old man puffed on his pipe thoughtfully.
He puffed hard, drawing cold morning air deep into his lungs.
She took a moment to puff on her cigarette before answering.
He puffed on his cigar, sending rings of smoke into the air.
She lit a cigarette and puffed contentedly in the garden.
The old man puffed his pipe while reading the morning newspaper.

  • synonym – inhale: to breathe in air, smoke, or vapor
