autonomy

noun
personal independence; self-government
Pronunciation: / əˈtɑnəmi / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒnəmi / (BrE)
the ability to make your own decisions independently; personal independence or self-government

Examples

personal autonomy
After years of strict rules, he finally experienced the autonomy he had always desired.
Many employees value autonomy in the workplace and prefer to set their own schedules.
The teenager wanted more autonomy in making her own choices about her future.

Related Terms

  • antonym – dependence: the state of relying on or being controlled by someone or something else
  • synonym – independence: the state of being independent, especially personal self-reliance
