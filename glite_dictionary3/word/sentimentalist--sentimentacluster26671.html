sentimentalist

noun
person given to excessive sentimentality
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛn.təˈmɛn.təl.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɪˈmɛn.təl.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who is prone to excessive emotional feelings or sentimentality

Examples

hopeless sentimentalist
Although he called himself a realist, many saw him as more of a sentimentalist at heart.

The author was criticized as a sentimentalist for his overly emotional writing style.
