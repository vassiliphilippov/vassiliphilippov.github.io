megasporophyll

noun
leaf that bears female sporangia (botany)
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡəˈspɔrəˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˌmiːɡəˈspɒrəfɪl / (BrE)
a leaf-like structure in non-flowering plants that bears only megasporangia (spore cases that produce female spores)

Examples

megasporophyll in a cone
Botanists observed the arrangement of megasporophylls in various gymnosperm species.
Each megasporophyll produces megaspores that develop into the female gametophyte.
The female cones of cycads are composed of tightly packed megasporophylls.
