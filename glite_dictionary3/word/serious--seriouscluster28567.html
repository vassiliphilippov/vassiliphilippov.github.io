serious

adjective
threatening great harm; dangerous or severe
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəs / (BrE)
causing fear or worry by threatening great harm or having dangerous consequences

Examples

serious injury
After the accident, he was rushed to hospital with a serious head injury.
Doctors say she is in serious condition but expected to recover.
This is a serious matter—if we don't act quickly, people could get hurt.
