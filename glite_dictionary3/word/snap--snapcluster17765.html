snap

noun
sudden breaking with a sharp noise
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
an instance of something breaking suddenly and sharply, often with a noise

Examples

the snap of a stick
She heard the snap of the pencil as it cracked in two.
The sudden snap of the wire startled everyone in the room.
There was a loud snap as the branch broke under his weight.
