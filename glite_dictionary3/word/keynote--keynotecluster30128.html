to keynote

verb
deliver the main address at an event
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌnoʊt / (AmE), / ˈkiːˌnəʊt / (BrE)
to deliver the main address at a conference or event, usually setting the theme or underlying tone.

Examples

to keynote a conference
Every year a renowned figure is chosen to keynote the international summit.
He will keynote the ceremony, sharing his insights on innovation.
She was invited to keynote the technology conference last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym – address: a formal speech delivered to an audience
  • synonym – lecture: an educational talk to an audience
