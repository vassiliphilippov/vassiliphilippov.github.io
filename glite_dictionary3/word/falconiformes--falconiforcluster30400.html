Falconiformes

noun
order of diurnal birds of prey
Pronunciation: / fælˌkoʊnɪˈfɔrmiːz /; / ˌfælkənəˈfɔːrmiːz / (AmE), / falˌkəʊnɪˈfɔːmiːz /; / ˌfælkənɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
an order of mainly daytime birds of prey, including falcons, hawks, eagles, ospreys, caracaras, and vultures, known for their hooked beaks and strong talons
