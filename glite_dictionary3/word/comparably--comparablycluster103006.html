comparably

adverb
in a way that can be compared
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛrəbli / (AmE), / kəmˈpærəbli / (BrE)
In a manner or to a degree that can be compared with something else; similarly or equivalently.

Examples

comparably skilled workers
Her skills are comparably advanced to those of her colleagues in the department.
The prices of these two smartphones are comparably affordable, making them both attractive choices.
The results for Group B were comparably impressive, matching those of Group A.

Related Terms

  • antonym – incomparably: in a way that cannot be compared or is beyond comparison
  • synonym – similarly: in a similar way
