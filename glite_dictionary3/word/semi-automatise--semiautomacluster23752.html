to semi-automatise

verb
make (a process) semiautomatic
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi ˈɔtəˌmætaɪz / (AmE), / ˌsɛmi ɔːˈtɒmətaɪz / (BrE)
to make (a process or system) semiautomatic, i.e., to automate part but not all of its operations

Examples

to semi-automatise a process
Is it possible to semi-automatise the data entry task to reduce errors but keep some human oversight?
The company chose to semi-automatise its customer support to balance efficiency with a personal touch.
We're planning to semi-automatise the order processing system next month.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – automatise: make automatic
  • degree lower – manualize: make manual or performed by hand
