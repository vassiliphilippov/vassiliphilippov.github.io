irregardless

adverb
nonstandard term for 'regardless'
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrəˈɡɑːrdləs / (AmE), / ˌɪrəˈɡɑːdləs / (BrE)
informal, nonstandard term meaning 'regardless'; used to emphasize that something is true without regard to other considerations, though often considered incorrect.

Examples

irregardless of the facts
Irregardless of what others think, he decided to follow his plan.
People often use irregardless even though it is considered nonstandard.
She said she would attend the event irregardless of the weather forecast.

Related Terms

  • synonym – regardless: without paying attention to the current situation
