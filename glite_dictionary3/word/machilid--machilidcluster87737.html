machilid

noun
type of jumping, wingless insect
Pronunciation: / məˈkɪlɪd / (AmE), / məˈkɪlɪd / (BrE)
A wingless insect of the family Machilidae, often found in dark, moist environments and known for making erratic leaps when disturbed.
