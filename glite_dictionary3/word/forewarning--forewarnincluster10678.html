forewarning

noun
advance warning about a future event
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈwɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːˈwɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
an advance warning or caution about a future event, especially something undesirable

Examples

early forewarning
His sense of unease served as a kind of forewarning before the incident occurred.
The thunder was a forewarning of the approaching storm.
Without any forewarning, the fire alarm suddenly began to ring.
