petit bourgeois

noun
member of the lower middle class
Pronunciation: / pəˌti bʊrˈʒwɑ /; / pəˌtiː bʊrˈʒwɑː / (AmE), / pəˌti bʊəˈʒwɑː /; / ˌpeti bʊəˈʒwɑː / (BrE)
a member of the lower middle class, especially someone seen as conventional, materialistic, or a small business owner

Examples

a petit bourgeois shop owner

Many revolutions viewed the petit bourgeois as wavering between the working class and the elite.
The petit bourgeois of the town tended to vote conservatively in local elections.
