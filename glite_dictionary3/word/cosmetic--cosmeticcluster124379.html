cosmetic

noun
beauty product for the body
Pronunciation: / kɑzˈmɛtɪk / (AmE), / kɒzˈmɛtɪk / (BrE)
A substance or preparation applied to the body, especially the face, to improve or change its appearance.

Examples

cosmetic products
Many people use at least one cosmetic daily, such as lotion or lipstick.
She always keeps her cosmetic bag with her for quick touch-ups.
The new law requires all cosmetic products to be tested for safety.
