Phyllidae

noun
family of leaf insects
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfɪlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of insects commonly known as leaf insects, characterized by their broad, flat bodies that resemble leaves, aiding in camouflage.
