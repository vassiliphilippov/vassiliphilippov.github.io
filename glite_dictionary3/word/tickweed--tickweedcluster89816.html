tickweed

noun
a plant of the genus Coreopsis with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkˌwid / (AmE), / ˈtɪkˌwiːd / (BrE)
Any of numerous plants of the genus Coreopsis, noted for their showy, usually yellow, daisylike flowers blooming over extended periods, native to North and South America.
