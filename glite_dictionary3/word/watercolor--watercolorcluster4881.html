watercolor

noun
paint with water-soluble pigments
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚˌkʌlɚ /; / ˈwɔː.t̬ɚˌkʌl.ɚ /; / ˈwɔt̬ɚˌkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkʌlə /; / ˈwɔː.təˌkʌl.ə /; / ˈwɔːtəkʌlə / (BrE)
a type of paint in which the pigments are suspended in water, used especially for art on paper
