to signpost

verb
mark with a signpost
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnpəʊst / (BrE)
to mark a place, path, or route with a signpost for direction or information

Examples

to signpost a path
If the route isn't signposted, hikers might easily take a wrong turn.
The hiking trail was carefully signposted to prevent anyone from getting lost.
Volunteers spent the afternoon signposting the new cycle path through the park.
