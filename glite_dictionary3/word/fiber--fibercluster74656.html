fiber

noun
threadlike substance for yarn or fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbər /; / ˈfaɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə /; / ˈfaɪ.bə / (BrE)
a thin, threadlike material or substance, natural or manufactured, used to make fabric, textiles, or as a material substitute
