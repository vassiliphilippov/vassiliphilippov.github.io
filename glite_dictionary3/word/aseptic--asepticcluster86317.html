aseptic

adjective
free from pathogenic microorganisms
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛptɪk / (AmE), / əˈsɛptɪk / (BrE)
Free from harmful microorganisms; using methods to prevent infection by microorganisms.

Examples

aseptic surgical tools
All surgical tools must be aseptic before an operation.
Modern laboratories maintain an aseptic environment for sensitive experiments.
Nurses used aseptic techniques to minimize the risk of infection.
