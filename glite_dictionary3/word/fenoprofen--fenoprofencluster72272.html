fenoprofen

noun
NSAID for treating pain and inflammation
Pronunciation: / fɛˈnoʊprəfən / (AmE), / fɛˈnɒprəfən / (BrE)
A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain and inflammation, especially in arthritis and related disorders.

Examples

fenoprofen for joint pain
Fenoprofen is less commonly used than ibuprofen but is effective for inflammatory conditions.
