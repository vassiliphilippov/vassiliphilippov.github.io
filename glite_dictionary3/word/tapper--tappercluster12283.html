tapper

noun
worker who cuts screw threads with a tap
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpər / (AmE), / ˈtæpə / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to use a tap to cut screw threads inside holes in metal or other materials

Examples

skilled tapper in metalwork
