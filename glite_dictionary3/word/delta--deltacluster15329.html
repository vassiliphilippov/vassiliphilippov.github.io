delta

noun
triangular river mouth landform
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛl.tə / (AmE), / ˈdɛl.tə / (BrE)
An area of low, flat land shaped like a triangle, formed where a river splits and spreads out as it enters a larger body of water, typically depositing sediment.

Examples

delta river land
The delta is home to a wide variety of wildlife and plant species.
The city is located at the southern edge of the delta.
The fertile delta supported many farming communities along the river.
