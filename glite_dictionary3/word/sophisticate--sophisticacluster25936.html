sophisticate

noun
worldly-wise, cultured person
Pronunciation: / səˈfɪstɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / səˈfɪstɪkeɪt / (BrE)
A person who is knowledgeable, refined, and experienced in the ways of the world, especially in matters of culture or fashion.

Examples

a true sophisticate
As a true sophisticate, she navigated the art world with ease and confidence.
His reputation as a sophisticate preceded him wherever he traveled.
The party was attended by every known sophisticate in the city.
