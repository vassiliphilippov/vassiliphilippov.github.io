usefully

adverb
in a useful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈjusfəli / (AmE), / ˈjuːsfəli / (BrE)
In a useful or advantageous manner; in such a way as to achieve a desired purpose.

Examples

usefully spend time
Extra storage space can always be usefully utilized in a small apartment.
Her time was usefully spent learning new skills during the holidays.
The funds were usefully invested in community projects.
