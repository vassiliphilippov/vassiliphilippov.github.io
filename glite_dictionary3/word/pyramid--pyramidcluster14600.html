to pyramid

verb
arrange or build in the shape of a pyramid
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪr.əˌmɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɪə.rə.mɪd / (BrE)
to arrange or build up in the shape or structure of a pyramid, typically increasing in levels or layers.

Examples

to pyramid cans
At the event, volunteers pyramided the canned goods into a massive display for charity.
The costs tend to pyramid as more features are added to the original plan.
They began to pyramid the chairs for storage, stacking each layer smaller than the one below.
