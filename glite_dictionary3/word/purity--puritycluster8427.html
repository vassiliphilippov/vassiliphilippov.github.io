purity

noun
state of being unmixed or unadulterated
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrəti / (AmE), / ˈpjʊərəti / (BrE)
the state of being free from any contamination, adulteration, or mixture; being completely unmixed or unadulterated

Examples

chemical purity
Scientists are striving to improve the purity of the chemical compound.
She admired the purity of the snow, untouched by footsteps.
The purity of the water was tested before it was deemed safe to drink.
