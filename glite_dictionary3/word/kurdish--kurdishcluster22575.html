Kurdish

noun
Iranian language spoken by Kurds
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜːrdɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɜːdɪʃ / (BrE)
an Iranian language spoken by the Kurdish people, primarily in Turkey, Iran, Iraq, Syria, and surrounding regions
