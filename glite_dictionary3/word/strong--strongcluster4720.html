strong

adjective
high-alcohol drink (distilled)
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ / (AmE), / strɒŋ / (BrE)
(of an alcoholic drink) containing a high percentage of alcohol, typically one that is distilled rather than fermented.

Examples

strong drink
She prefers strong liquors over beer or wine when she goes out.
Some cocktails are made with a mix of strong spirits and fruit juice.
This is a strong whiskey with an alcohol content of 40 percent.
