homebody

noun
person who prefers to stay at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbɒdi / (BrE)
a person who prefers to stay at home rather than go out socially or travel

Examples

quiet homebody life
Although her friends love traveling, Emily is happy being a homebody and hosting dinner at her place.
During winter, Mark becomes even more of a homebody and rarely accepts invitations out.
I've always been a homebody, preferring a quiet night with a book to a loud party.

Related Terms

  • antonym – socialite: person who is frequently seen at social events
  • synonym – stay-at-home: person who does not go out much; prefers to remain at home
