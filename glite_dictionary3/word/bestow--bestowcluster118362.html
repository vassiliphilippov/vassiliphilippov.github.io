to bestow

verb
impart a quality or characteristic
Pronunciation: / bɪˈstoʊ / (AmE), / bɪˈstəʊ / (BrE)
to give or impart a quality, characteristic, or property to someone or something

Examples

to bestow a quality
Her kindness bestows a sense of comfort on everyone she meets.
His leadership bestowed confidence on the young team.
The old building bestows a charm on the neighborhood that new constructions lack.

  • synonym – grant: to give or bestow (a quality, right, or asset)
