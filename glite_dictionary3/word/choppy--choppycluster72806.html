choppy

adjective
rough with small waves (sea/water)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpi / (BrE)
(of water or the sea) rough and having small waves.

Examples

choppy sea waves
Strong winds made the usually calm lake quite choppy today.
The boat rocked in the choppy water, making some passengers uneasy.
We decided to postpone our swim because the ocean looked too choppy.

Related Terms

  • antonym – calm: (of water or the sea) free from waves or disturbance
