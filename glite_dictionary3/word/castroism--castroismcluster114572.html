Castroism

noun
Cuban communism under Fidel Castro
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstroʊˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkæstrəʊˌɪzəm / (BrE)
the political and economic policies and practices associated with Fidel Castro's regime in Cuba, characterized by a form of communism.
