tamandua

noun
Central/South American small anteater
Pronunciation: / təˈmænduə / (AmE), / təˈmændjʊə /; / təˈmandjʊə / (BrE)
a small anteater from Central and South America, with a prehensile tail and four large claws, that eats mainly insects
