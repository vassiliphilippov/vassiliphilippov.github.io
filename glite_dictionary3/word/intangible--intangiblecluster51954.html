intangible

adjective
not easily defined or grasped
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtændʒəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtændʒɪbəl /; / ɪnˈtandʒɪb(ə)l / (BrE)
not having a physical presence; unable to be touched or clearly defined (such as feelings, qualities, or ideas)
