extinct

adjective
permanently inactive (of volcano)
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɪŋkt / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɪŋkt / (BrE)
no longer active or functioning; having stopped working, burning, or erupting (used for volcanoes, fires, lights, etc.)
