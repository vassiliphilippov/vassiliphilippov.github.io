ulcerated

adjective
affected by an ulcer or open sore
Pronunciation: / ˈʌlsəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌlsəˌreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of tissue or surface) affected by an ulcer or ulcers; having open sores.

Examples

ulcerated skin tissue
Doctors observed ulcerated lesions on the lining of his stomach.
Her foot was painful and ulcerated due to poor circulation.
The wound became ulcerated after several weeks without proper treatment.
