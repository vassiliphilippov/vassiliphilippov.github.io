dipsomania

noun
uncontrollable craving for alcohol
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪpsoʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌdɪpsəˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
a persistent, uncontrollable craving for alcoholic drinks, especially in excessive amounts
