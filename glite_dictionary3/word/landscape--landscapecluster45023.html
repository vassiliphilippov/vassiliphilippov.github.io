landscape

noun
an expanse of scenery you can see at once
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈlan(d)skeɪp / (BrE)
an area of land that can be seen in a single view, especially one that is beautiful or has a particular appearance

Examples

beautiful landscape
She painted the stunning landscape she saw from her window every morning.
The mountain landscape was breathtaking as the sun rose over the peaks.
Tourists stopped to admire the picturesque landscape stretching out below the hill.

Related Terms

  • degree lower – cityscape: a view or image of a city
  • degree higher – panorama: an unbroken view of the whole region surrounding an observer
  • synonym – scenery: the natural features of a landscape considered in terms of their appearance
  • synonym – view: what can be seen from a particular place
