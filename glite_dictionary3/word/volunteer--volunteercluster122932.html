volunteer

adjective
working or serving without pay
Pronunciation: / ˌvɑlənˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌvɒlənˈtɪə / (BrE)
(of work or a worker) undertaken or performed without payment; unpaid.

Examples

volunteer work experience
Most of the staff at the community center are volunteer workers who contribute their time for free.
Our museum relies on a team of volunteer guides to offer tours to visitors.
The volunteer firefighters responded quickly to the emergency call.

Related Terms

  • antonym – paid: receiving payment for work done
  • synonym – unpaid: not receiving payment for work done
