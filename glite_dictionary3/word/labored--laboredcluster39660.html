labored

adjective
done with effort; lacking ease
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbɚd /; / ˈleɪ.bɚd / (AmE), / ˈleɪbəd /; / ˈleɪ.bəd / (BrE)
not natural, smooth, or easy; showing effort, strain, or difficulty (used for writing, speech, movement, breathing, etc.)

Examples
