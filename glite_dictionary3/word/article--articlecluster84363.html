article

noun
individual item or object (e.g., product, piece)
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪkl / (BrE)
an individual item or object, especially one of a particular kind or category, often as manufactured or for sale

Examples

article of clothing
Each article on the shelf was carefully labeled and priced.
She packed every article of clothing she owned for the trip.
The museum displayed a rare article of ancient pottery.
