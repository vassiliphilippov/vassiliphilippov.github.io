to fence

verb
deal in stolen goods
Pronunciation: / fɛns / (AmE), / fɛns / (BrE)
to buy, receive, or deal in stolen goods in order to sell them to others

Examples

to fence stolen items
He was arrested after trying to fence a stash of stolen jewelry.
She didn't steal the cars herself, but she did fence them for a profit.
The gang used a legitimate business as a front to fence electronics.
