jury

noun
group judging a competition
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʊri / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊəri / (BrE)
A group of people chosen to judge and decide the results of a competition.

Examples

jury of experts
A panel of experts served as the jury for the science fair.
Each artwork was carefully evaluated by the jury before the winners were chosen.
The jury awarded first prize to a talented young pianist.
