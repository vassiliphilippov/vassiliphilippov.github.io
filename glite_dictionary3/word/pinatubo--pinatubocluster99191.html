Pinatubo

proper noun
volcano in the Philippines
Pronunciation: / piːnəˈtuːboʊ / (AmE), / piːnəˈtuːbəʊ / (BrE)
a volcano in Luzon, northwest of Manila in the Philippines, known for its major eruption in 1991 after centuries of dormancy
