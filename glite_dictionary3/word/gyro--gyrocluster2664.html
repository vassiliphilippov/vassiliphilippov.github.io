gyro

noun
Greek sandwich with meat in pita bread
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪroʊ / (AmE), / ˈjɪərəʊ / (BrE)
A Greek sandwich consisting of sliced roast meat, onion, and tomato, usually served in pita bread.

Examples

eat a gyro sandwich
I ordered a gyro with extra tzatziki sauce for lunch.
She tried making a homemade gyro, complete with pita and fresh vegetables.
The restaurant serves the best lamb gyro in town.
