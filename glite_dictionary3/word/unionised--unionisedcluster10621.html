unionised

adjective
belonging to a labor union
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːniəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈjuːniəˌnaɪzd / (BrE)
(of a person, group, or workplace) having membership in or being formed into a labor union.

Examples

unionised workers
Most of the factory staff are unionised and participate in collective bargaining.
Several unionised teachers joined the strike to protest working conditions.
The unionised sector of the workforce has greater job security than non-unionised workers.
