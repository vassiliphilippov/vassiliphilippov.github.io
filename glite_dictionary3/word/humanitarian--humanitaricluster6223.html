humanitarian

noun
person who promotes human welfare
Pronunciation: / hjuˌmænɪˈtɛriən /; / hjuˌmænəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / hjuːˌmænɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
a person who works to promote human welfare, compassion, and help others, especially through charity or social reform
