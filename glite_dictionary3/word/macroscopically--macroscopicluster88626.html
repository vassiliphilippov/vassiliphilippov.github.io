macroscopically

adverb
without a microscope, visible to the naked eye
Pronunciation: / ˌmæk.rəˈskɑː.pɪ.kli / (AmE), / ˌmæk.rəˈskɒ.pɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a way that is visible to the naked eye; without using a microscope.

Examples

macroscopically visible features
The defect was not visible macroscopically, requiring further analysis.
The samples were first assessed macroscopically before being prepared for microscopic study.
The tissue appeared normal macroscopically, but microscopic examination revealed abnormalities.
