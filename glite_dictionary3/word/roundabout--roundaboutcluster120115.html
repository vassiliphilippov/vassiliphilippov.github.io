roundabout

noun
rotating amusement ride for children
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndəˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈraʊndəˌbaʊt / (BrE)
a large, rotating machine with seats, often shaped like horses or animals, on which children ride for amusement, especially at fairs or amusement parks

Examples

ride a roundabout
Dozens of kids queued up to get a seat on the colorful roundabout in the park.
She promised her son a ride on the roundabout after he finished his ice cream.
The children laughed excitedly as the roundabout spun faster under the bright lights of the fairground.
