envelope

noun
paper container for letters or documents
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.vəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈɛn.və.ləʊp / (BrE)
a flat, usually rectangular paper or plastic container used to enclose a letter, document, or small object
