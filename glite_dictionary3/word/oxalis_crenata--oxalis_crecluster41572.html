Oxalis crenata

noun
Andean tuber-producing wood sorrel
Pronunciation: / oʊˈzælɪs krəˈneɪtə / (AmE), / ɒkˈsælɪs krɛˈnɑːtə / (BrE)
A species of South American wood sorrel cultivated for its edible tubers, often used as a food crop in the Andes.
