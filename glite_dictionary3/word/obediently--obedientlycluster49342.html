obediently

adverb
in an obedient manner
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbiːdiəntli / (AmE), / əˈbiːdiəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows willingness to obey or follow instructions or authority

Examples

to listen obediently
She obediently handed over her phone when asked by her father.
The children sat obediently at their desks, waiting for the teacher's instructions.
The dog followed its owner's command obediently every time.
