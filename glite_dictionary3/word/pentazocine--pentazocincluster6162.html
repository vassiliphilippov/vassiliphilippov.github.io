pentazocine

noun
synthetic analgesic drug
Pronunciation: / pɛnˈtæzəˌsin / (AmE), / pɛnˈtazəsiːn / (BrE)
A synthetic analgesic drug used to relieve moderate to severe pain, less addictive than morphine.
