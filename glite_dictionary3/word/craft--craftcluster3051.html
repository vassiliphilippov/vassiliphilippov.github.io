craft

noun
cunning or deceitful skill
Pronunciation: / kræft / (AmE), / krɑːft / (BrE)
skill in achieving one's aims by deceit or cunning; shrewdness, especially when used for deception.

Examples

political craft
He relied on his craft to manipulate people into agreeing with his plans.
People began to mistrust her because of the craft she displayed in her business dealings.
The politician was admired for his craft in negotiating complicated deals.
