Wagner

proper noun
German composer of operas (1813–1883)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːɡnər / (AmE), / ˈvɑːɡnə / (BrE)
(1813–1883) A German composer known for his operas and for creating the concept of the musical drama, integrating music, drama, and spectacle.
