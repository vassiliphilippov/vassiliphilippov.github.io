bolus

noun
small rounded mass, esp. chewed food
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊləs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊləs / (BrE)
a small rounded mass of a substance, especially chewed food at the moment of swallowing

Examples

chewed bolus of food
After chewing thoroughly, the food formed a bolus ready to be swallowed.
Each bolus passes from the mouth to the esophagus during eating.
The technician analyzed a bolus of chewed bread under the microscope.
