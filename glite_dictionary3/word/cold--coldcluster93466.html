cold

adjective
lacking the warmth of life (dead)
Pronunciation: / koʊld / (AmE), / kəʊld / (BrE)
Having lost the warmth of life; dead.

Examples

found cold
After the accident, he was found cold by the roadside before help arrived.
She lay cold and still, having passed away peacefully during the night.
The phrase 'cold in his grave' refers to someone who is already cold, or dead.
