arm

noun
part of a chair or sofa supporting the arm
Pronunciation: / ɑrm / (AmE), / ɑːm / (BrE)
a part of an object, piece of furniture, or machine that sticks out and is similar in shape or function to a human arm, such as the arm of a chair or a machine

Examples

arm of a chair
He leaned back, placing both hands on the arm of his armchair.
She rested her elbow on the arm of the sofa as she read her book.
The antique chair has beautifully carved wooden arms on both sides.
He adjusted the arm of the turntable before playing the record.
One arm of the chandelier was missing after the move.
The arm of the river curved gently around the fields.
