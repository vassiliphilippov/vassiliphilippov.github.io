scoop

noun
bucket/shovel part of excavating machinery
Pronunciation: / skuːp / (AmE), / skuːp / (BrE)
a tool or utensil with a curved bowl, or the bucket part of a machine, used for digging, serving, or moving things like ice cream, liquids, or soil
