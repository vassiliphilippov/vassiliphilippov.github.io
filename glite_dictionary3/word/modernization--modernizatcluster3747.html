modernization

noun
updated or modern version of something
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːdərnəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɒdənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
A modern version or adaptation of something, especially a literary or artistic work, updated to fit contemporary styles or ideas.

Examples

modernization of a story
Critics praised the modernization for making the themes accessible to today's audience.
The director's modernization of the classic play brought new energy to the story.
This modernization features updated language and contemporary costumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym – adaptation: a version revised for a new use or audience
