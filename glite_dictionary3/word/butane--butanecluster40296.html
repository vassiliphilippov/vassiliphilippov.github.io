butane

noun
a flammable hydrocarbon gas used as fuel
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːteɪn / (AmE), / ˈbjuːteɪn / (BrE)
a flammable hydrocarbon gas, C4H10, found in natural gas and petroleum, used as a fuel and in the manufacture of rubber and other chemicals
