Xanthomonas

noun
genus of yellow-pigmented bacteria
Pronunciation: / zænˌθoʊˈmoʊnəs / (AmE), / zanˌθəˈməʊnəs / (BrE)
a genus of bacteria that produce a yellow pigment (not soluble in water), similar to Pseudomonas, and known to affect plants
