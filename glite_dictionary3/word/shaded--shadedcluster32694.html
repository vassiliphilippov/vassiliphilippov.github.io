shaded

adjective
with shadow gradations (art)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of a picture or drawing) having areas rendered with gradations of shadow to suggest depth or volume.

Examples

shaded drawing details
In the portrait, the shaded areas add depth and realism to the face.
She gently blended the pencil marks to create shaded regions in her sketch.
The artist skillfully used shaded lines to give the drawing a three-dimensional effect.
