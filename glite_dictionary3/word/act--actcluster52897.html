to act

verb
perform duties or fill a role
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / ækt / (BrE)
to perform a particular function or discharge duties, especially temporarily or in place of someone else

Examples

to act as manager
He agreed to act in a temporary capacity until a replacement was found.
In his absence, I will act as the chair of the meeting.
She was asked to act as manager while her supervisor was away.

Related Terms

  • synonym – function: to perform an official function or duty
  • synonym – serve: to fulfill a role or function, especially officially
