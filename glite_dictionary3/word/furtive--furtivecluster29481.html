furtive

adjective
secretive and sly
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːtɪv / (AmE), / ˈfɜːtɪv / (BrE)
trying to avoid being noticed, often because of guilt or fear of getting into trouble; secretive or sly

Examples

furtive glance
His furtive movements made everyone suspicious of his intentions.
She gave him a furtive smile when no one was looking.
The cat cast a furtive glance at the open door before sneaking outside.
He made a furtive attempt to slip out of the room unnoticed.
She cast a furtive glance over her shoulder, worried she was being followed.
The student's furtive movements made the teacher suspicious of cheating.
