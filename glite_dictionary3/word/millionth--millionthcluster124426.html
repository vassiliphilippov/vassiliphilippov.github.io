millionth

noun
the 1,000,000th item in a sequence
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪljənθ / (AmE), / ˈmɪljənθ / (BrE)
the position of someone or something that is number 1,000,000 in a sequence; the item in a series that comes after nine hundred and ninety-nine thousand, nine hundred and ninety-nine others

Examples

millionth follower
He never imagined he would become the millionth follower on her social media page.
She proudly accepted the award for being the millionth customer at the store.
The millionth visitor was greeted with confetti and cheers.
