plastered

adjective
walls coated with plaster
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstərd / (AmE), / ˈplɑːstəd / (BrE)
(of walls) covered with a smooth layer of plaster, typically in preparation for painting or decoration.

Examples

plastered wall surface
A plastered surface provides a solid base for wallpaper.
After renovation, the once rough stone walls were beautifully plastered.
The living room had freshly plastered walls, ready for painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym – unplastered: (of walls) not covered with plaster
