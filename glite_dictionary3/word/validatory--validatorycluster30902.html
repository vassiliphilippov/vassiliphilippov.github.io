validatory

adjective
providing confirmation or validation
Pronunciation: / ˈvælɪdəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈvælɪdəˌtɔːri / (BrE)
serving to confirm or corroborate the validity or truth of something; providing validation

Examples

validatory evidence
Additional test results acted as validatory data for the initial findings.
Rigorous peer review serves a validatory function in academic publishing.
The researcher provided validatory evidence to support her hypothesis.

Related Terms

  • antonym – invalidating: making something invalid; disproving
  • synonym – validating: serving to make something valid; confirming
