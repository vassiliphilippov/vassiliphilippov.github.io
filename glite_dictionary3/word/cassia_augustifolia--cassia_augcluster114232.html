Cassia augustifolia

proper noun
plant used medicinally; senna
Pronunciation: / ˌkæsiə ɔːˌgʌstəˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˌkæsɪə ɔːˌgʌstɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
an erect shrub with tawny yellow flowers, whose dried leaves are used medicinally as a cathartic; also known as senna
