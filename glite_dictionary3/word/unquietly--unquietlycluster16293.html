unquietly

adverb
in a restless or agitated manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwaɪətli / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwaɪətli / (BrE)
In a restless, agitated, or disturbed manner; not calmly.

Examples

to sleep unquietly
He slept unquietly that night, troubled by worries he could not name.
She paced unquietly across the room, unable to settle her thoughts.
The waters churned unquietly beneath the stormy sky.
