inhalation

noun
the act of breathing in
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnhəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnhəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of breathing in air or other gases into the lungs

Examples

deep inhalation of air
Deep inhalation of fresh air can help calm your nerves.
She felt better after a long, slow inhalation followed by a steady exhale.
The doctor warned that inhalation of toxic fumes could be dangerous.

Related Terms

  • antonym – exhalation: the act of breathing out air from the lungs
  • synonym – inspire: to draw air into the lungs
