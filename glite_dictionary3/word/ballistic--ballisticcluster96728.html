ballistic

adjective
relating to motion under momentum and gravity
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / bəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the motion of objects moving under their own momentum and the force of gravity, especially after being projected or released

Examples

ballistic missile path
After separation from the rocket, the capsule entered a ballistic trajectory back to Earth.
The engineers are analyzing the ballistic properties of the missile's flight path.
The study focused on the ballistic motion of fragments ejected during the explosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym – projectile: an object thrown or fired, especially as a weapon
