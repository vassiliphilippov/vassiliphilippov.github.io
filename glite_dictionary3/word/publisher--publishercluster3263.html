publisher

noun
person or company issuing books, music, etc.
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or company that prepares, issues, owns, or manages books, newspapers, magazines, or other works for public sale or distribution
