unbeneficed

adjective
not holding a church benefice
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɛnɪfɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛnɪfɪst / (BrE)
(of a member of the clergy) not holding a benefice; having no church office or endowed position.

Examples

unbeneficed clergyman
He took temporary work as an unbeneficed curate in the countryside.
Several unbeneficed clergymen attended the diocesan meeting.
The young priest remained unbeneficed despite many years of service in the parish.
