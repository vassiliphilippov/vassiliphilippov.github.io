refreshment

noun
snacks and drinks served at events
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɛʃmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɛʃmənt / (BrE)
snacks and drinks, especially those served as a light meal or during a break at events or meetings.

Examples

refreshment table snacks
A table near the entrance was covered with refreshment for the travelers.
Light refreshment will be provided after the ceremony.
They set out tea and refreshments for the guests in the garden.

Related Terms

  • degree higher – meal: an occasion when people eat food, especially breakfast, lunch, or dinner
  • synonym – snack: a small amount of food eaten between meals
