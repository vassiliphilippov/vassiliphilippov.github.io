hard

adjective
strong or distilled alcoholic drink
Pronunciation: / hɑrd / (AmE), / hɑːd / (BrE)
(of an alcoholic drink) strong, containing a high percentage of alcohol and being distilled rather than fermented.

Examples

hard liquor
Many people prefer hard liquor like whiskey or vodka over beer.
She poured herself a glass of hard cider at the party.
The bar serves a variety of hard spirits, including rum, gin, and tequila.

Related Terms

  • antonym – soft: (of a drink) not alcoholic or low in alcohol
