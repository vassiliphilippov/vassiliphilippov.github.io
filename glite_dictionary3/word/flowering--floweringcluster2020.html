flowering

noun
the peak or development stage, especially culturally
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
The development or reaching of a peak, especially in a culture, art, or intellectual activity; a period of full maturity or achievement.

Examples

flowering of culture
The Renaissance was known as the flowering of European art and culture.
The city experienced a flowering of creativity during the period of prosperity.
We have witnessed the flowering of his poetic talent over the years.

Related Terms

  • synonym – blossoming: The process of coming into full development or fruition.
  • antonym – decline: A gradual decrease or loss in strength or quality; the opposite state after a peak.
