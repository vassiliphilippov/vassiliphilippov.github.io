furze

noun
a spiny, flowering shrub (gorse)
Pronunciation: / fɝz / (AmE), / fɜːz / (BrE)
a spiny, evergreen shrub with yellow flowers, common in western Europe; another name for gorse (Ulex europaeus)

Examples

yellow furze bush
Cattle sometimes graze on furze when other food is scarce.

The sharp thorns of furze can make walking through the hedges quite difficult.
