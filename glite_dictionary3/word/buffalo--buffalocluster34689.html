buffalo

noun
large North American wild bison
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌf.əˌloʊ /; / ˈbʌfəˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌf.ə.ləʊ /; / ˈbʌfələʊ / (BrE)
a large, shaggy-haired wild animal native to North America (also called bison), or its meat used as food
