Sorbus sitchensis

noun
a mountain ash species native to western North America
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrbəs sɪtˈʃɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɔːbəs sɪtˈʃɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A species of mountain ash native to the western coast of North America, characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of white flowers and orange-red berries.
