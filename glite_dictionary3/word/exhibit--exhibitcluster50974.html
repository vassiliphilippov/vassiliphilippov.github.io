exhibit

noun
object displayed publicly
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɪbɪt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɪbɪt / (BrE)
an object or collection of objects displayed for the public, especially in a museum, gallery, or exhibition

Examples

museum exhibit
Each exhibit in the science fair demonstrated a unique experiment.
The dinosaur skeleton is the most popular exhibit at the natural history museum.
The museum's new exhibit attracts thousands of visitors every month.
