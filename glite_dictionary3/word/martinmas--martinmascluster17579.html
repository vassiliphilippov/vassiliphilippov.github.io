Martinmas

noun
feast day of Saint Martin (Nov. 11); Scottish quarter day
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtɪnˌmæs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtɪnməs / (BrE)
The feast day of Saint Martin, observed on November 11th, formerly one of the quarter days in Scotland.

Examples

Martinmas feast day
Rents were once paid on Martinmas, one of the old Scottish quarter days.
Several European countries still observe special customs for Martinmas each year.
The farmers gathered for the traditional Martinmas celebrations every November 11th.
