methylenedioxymethamphetamine

noun
a synthetic stimulant and psychoactive drug
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛθɪliˌnaɪˌɒksiˌmɛθəmˈfɛtəmiːn / (AmE), / ˌmiːθɪˌliːniˌdɪɒksiˌmɛθəmˈfɛtəmiːn / (BrE)
a synthetic stimulant and psychoactive drug, known for producing feelings of increased energy, pleasure, and emotional closeness; it is used recreationally and was formerly used in psychotherapy
