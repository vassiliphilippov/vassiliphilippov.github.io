waterscape

noun
a painting or scene featuring water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːtərˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌskeɪp / (BrE)
a painting, drawing, or other artistic representation of a scene featuring a large expanse of water, such as the sea, a lake, or a river
