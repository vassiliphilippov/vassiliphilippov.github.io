consciously

adverb
deliberately; with awareness
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
In a deliberate and intentional way; with full awareness of what one is doing.

Examples

consciously make a choice
He consciously tried to keep his voice calm during the conversation.
Many people consciously choose healthier foods when they learn more about nutrition.
She consciously avoided making eye contact with her former boss at the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym – intentionally: in a deliberate, planned manner
  • antonym – unconsciously: without being aware
