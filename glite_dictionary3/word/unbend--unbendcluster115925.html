to unbend

verb
to detach a sail from a spar or stay
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɛnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛnd / (BrE)
(Nautical) To unfasten a sail from a spar or a stay.

Examples

to unbend a sail
After docking, they began to unbend the sails for maintenance.
It's important to unbend the jib carefully to avoid damage to the fabric.
The crew were ordered to unbend the mainsail before the storm hit.
